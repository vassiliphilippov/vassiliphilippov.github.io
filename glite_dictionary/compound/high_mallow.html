high mallow

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
a perennial herb with rosy-purple flowers, native to the Old World and introduced to the United States
a perennial herb with rosy-purple flowers

Examples

The high mallow adds vibrant color to cottage gardens with its rosy-purple blooms.
Many herbalists value high mallow for its soothing properties when used in teas or infusions.
You can spot high mallow growing wild along roadsides in some parts of the United States.
