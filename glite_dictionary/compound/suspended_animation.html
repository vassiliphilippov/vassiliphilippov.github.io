suspended animation

1. NOUN

/ səˈspɛndɪd ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛndɪd ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a temporary stopping of vital bodily functions, making a person or animal appear dead, usually without permanent harm
temporary cessation of vital functions
medical

Examples

After nearly drowning, the boy was found in a state of suspended animation until doctors revived him.
Some animals enter suspended animation during winter to survive harsh conditions with minimal bodily functions.
The movie explored the idea of putting astronauts into suspended animation for long interstellar journeys.
