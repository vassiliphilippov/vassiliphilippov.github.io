cam stroke

1. NOUN

/ kæm stɹoʊk / (AmE), / kæm strəʊk / (BrE)
The maximum movement or distance traveled by a pivoted or reciprocating part as controlled by a cam.
maximum movement governed by a cam

Examples

The engineer measured the cam stroke to ensure proper valve timing in the engine.
An incorrect cam stroke can lead to inefficient machine operation.
Adjusting the cam stroke improved the accuracy of the automatic feeder.
