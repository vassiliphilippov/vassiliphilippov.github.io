decorative element

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkɚˌeɪtɪv ˈɛləmənt / (AmE), / ˈdɛkərətɪv ˈɛlɪmənt / (BrE)
an item or feature added to something to enhance its appearance or aesthetic appeal
an item used for decoration or embellishment

Examples

The architect added a carved wood decorative element to the entrance of the building.
Each cake was topped with a unique decorative element made from sugar.
She selected a vase as a decorative element for her living room shelf.

Synset Examples

Sandcorn is often used as a decorative element in gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornament - an object used to decorate something
  • Synonym: embellishment - a decorative detail or feature added to something
