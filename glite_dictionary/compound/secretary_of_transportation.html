Secretary of Transportation

1. NOUN

/ sɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i əv ˌtræn.spɚˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.rə.təri əv ˌtræn.spɔːˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the head of the Department of Transportation in the United States government, responsible for overseeing transportation policies and regulations
head of U.S. Department of Transportation
american

Examples

The president nominated her as the new Secretary of Transportation last year.
As Secretary of Transportation, he implemented several new highway safety regulations.
The Secretary of Transportation announced a major investment in public transit infrastructure.
