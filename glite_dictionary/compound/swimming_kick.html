swimming kick

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ kɪk / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ kɪk / (BrE)
a movement of the legs, especially in a repeated or rhythmic manner, to propel the body forward or upward in swimming
leg movement in swimming

Examples

The flutter swimming kick is commonly used in freestyle events.
She practiced her swimming kick to improve her speed in the pool.
Proper technique in the swimming kick helps swimmers move more efficiently through the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kick - a movement of striking out with the foot or feet
