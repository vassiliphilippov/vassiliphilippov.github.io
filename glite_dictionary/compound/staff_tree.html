staff tree

1. NOUN

/ stæf tri / (AmE), / stɑːf triː / (BrE)
A small tree or climbing shrub of the genus Celastrus, known for its brightly colored fruits and twining growth.
tree/shrub of genus Celastrus

Examples

The bright berries of the staff tree make it popular in autumn gardens.
Gardeners should be cautious, as some varieties of staff tree can spread aggressively.
A mature staff tree can climb along fences and trellises, creating dense foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bittersweet - alternative name for several Celastrus species
