freshwater limpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃˌwɔtər ˈlɪmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃˌwɔːtə ˈlɪmpɪt / (BrE)
A small, cone-shaped freshwater snail that resembles a marine limpet, found living and feeding on freshwater plants.
minute conical freshwater snail resembling a limpet

Examples

The freshwater limpet attaches itself to river stones and aquatic plants.
Certain species of freshwater limpet are sensitive indicators of water pollution.
Researchers observed that the freshwater limpets helped keep algae levels under control in the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limpet - a type of aquatic snail with a conical shell, found on rocks in marine environments
