athletic competition

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk ˌkɑmpəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈθlɛtɪk ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a contest in which athletes compete against each other in sports or physical activities
contest between athletes

Examples

The city will host an international athletic competition this summer.
He trained for months to prepare for the annual athletic competition.
Many students participated in the school's first athletic competition of the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tournament - a series of contests between teams or competitors
  • Synonym: match - a sports contest between two teams or players
