fern rhapis

1. NOUN

/ fɝn ˈræpɪs / (AmE), / fɜːn ˈræpɪs / (BrE)
A small, graceful palm with reedlike stems, whose leaf bases are covered with loose, coarse fibers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant.
a type of small ornamental palm

Examples

The fern rhapis is a popular choice for indoor decoration due to its elegant appearance.
She placed a pot of fern rhapis near the window to brighten up the living room.
Botanical gardens often feature fern rhapis among their exotic palm collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady palm - another name for the fern rhapis, especially in horticulture
