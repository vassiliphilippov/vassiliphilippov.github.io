scalie friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪli frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈskeɪli frɛnd / (BrE)
a friend who identifies with or feels a strong connection to reptilian or lizard-like traits, often within subcultures such as the furry community
friend who identifies with reptilian traits
slang

Examples

My scalie friend designed a costume with realistic lizard scales for the convention.
Every summer, our group of furry and scalie friends gathers to share artwork and stories.
As a scalie friend, Kevin collects reptile figurines and draws dragons in his spare time.

Synset Examples

Her scalie friend loves to show off his tattoos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furry friend - a friend who identifies with or admires animal (typically mammal) traits

Etymology

From 'scalie', slang for someone interested in or identifying with reptiles, from 'scale' + '-ie'.
