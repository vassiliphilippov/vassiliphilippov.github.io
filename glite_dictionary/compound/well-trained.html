well-trained

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈtreɪnd / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈtreɪnd / (BrE)
Having received thorough instruction or preparation in a skill or task.
thoroughly taught or prepared

Examples

The well-trained staff handled the emergency quickly and efficiently.
We adopted a well-trained dog from the animal shelter.
Only well-trained athletes can complete such a demanding marathon.

Synset Examples

The whoremistress had her girls well-trained for the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trained - having been taught a particular skill or proficiency
