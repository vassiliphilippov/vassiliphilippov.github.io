wristwatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪstˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪstˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
A small watch that is worn on a strap around the wrist.
watch worn on the wrist

Examples

She glanced at her wristwatch to check the time before leaving.
He received a gold wristwatch as a retirement gift from his colleagues.
During the meeting, I noticed his elegant wristwatch matched his suit perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece carried or worn for telling time

Etymology

Formed from 'wrist' + 'watch', indicating a watch worn on the wrist.
