pitch contour

1. NOUN

/ pɪtʃ ˈkɑnˌtʊr / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ˈkɒn.tʊə / (BrE)
the pattern of rising and falling pitch in spoken language or music
pattern of voice or note pitch rise and fall

Examples

In Mandarin, each syllable has a distinct pitch contour that affects the word's meaning.
The melody's pitch contour rises dramatically in the chorus, creating excitement.
She studied the pitch contour of different English intonations for her linguistics research.
