news event

1. NOUN

/ nuz ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / njuːz ɪˈvent / (BrE)
an event considered newsworthy or of interest to the media and public
an event that is newsworthy

Examples

The journalists quickly traveled to cover the news event as it unfolded.
Social media played a major role in spreading information about the news event.
After the news event, officials held a press conference to answer questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: story - an account of a news event or situation
