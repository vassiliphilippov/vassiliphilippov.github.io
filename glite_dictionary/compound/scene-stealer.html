scene-stealer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsin ˌstilər / (AmE), / ˈsiːn ˌstiːlə / (BrE)
an actor or performer who draws more attention than others in the same scene, often unintentionally outshining them
person who attracts more attention than others

Examples

The child actor was a real scene-stealer in the movie, captivating the audience with every line.
Despite being in a minor role, her performance made her the scene-stealer of the play.
The puppy became an unexpected scene-stealer during the photoshoot.

Synset Examples

babies are natural scene-stealers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showstopper - a performer or act receiving enthusiastic applause or attention
