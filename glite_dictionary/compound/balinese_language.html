Balinese language

1. NOUN

/ bəˈliːniːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bəˈliːniːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Austronesian language spoken by the people of Bali, Indonesia
language spoken in Bali, Indonesia

Examples

The Balinese language is still widely spoken in villages across the island.
She is fluent in both Indonesian and Balinese language.
Researchers documented several traditional stories told in Balinese language during their fieldwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indonesian language - the official language of Indonesia
  • Degree Lower: Javanese language - the Austronesian language spoken on the island of Java

Etymology

From Bali, the Indonesian island, and English 'language'.
