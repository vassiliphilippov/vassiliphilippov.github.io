brain disease

1. NOUN

/ breɪn dɪˈziz / (AmE), / breɪn dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
any disorder or illness that affects the brain, impairing its function or structure
disease affecting the brain
medical

Examples

Scientists are researching new treatments for brain disease to improve patient outcomes.
Alzheimer's is a type of brain disease that leads to memory loss and cognitive decline.
Early diagnosis of a brain disease can significantly improve the chances of successful treatment.
