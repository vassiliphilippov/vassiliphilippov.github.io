fifth base

1. NOUN

/ fɪfθ beɪs / (AmE), / fɪfθ beɪs / (BrE)
(slang) A euphemism for full sexual intercourse or advanced sexual activity, typically the final stage in a series of escalating intimate acts, as referenced in the baseball metaphor for dating and relationships.
slang term for sexual intercourse
slang informal

Examples

Some people debate what exactly counts as fifth base in a relationship.
They laughed about how fifth base was never even discussed in high school.
After months together, the couple decided they were ready for fifth base.

Synset Examples

At the party, they joked about going for fifth base.
After months of dating, they finally reached fifth base.
They laughed when someone asked if they had hit fifth base yet.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first base - the first stage of physical intimacy, usually kissing
  • Degree Lower: second base - the second stage of physical intimacy, usually touching above the waist
  • Degree Lower: third base - the third stage of physical intimacy, usually touching below the waist
  • Synonym: home run - a colloquial term for sexual intercourse (final base in the metaphor)

Etymology

Formed by extending the metaphor of 'base' in baseball, where each 'base' signifies increasing levels of intimacy.
