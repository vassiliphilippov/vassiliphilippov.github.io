sand snake

1. NOUN

/ sænd sneɪk / (AmE), / sand sneɪk / (BrE)
a small North American burrowing snake that lives in sandy habitats
a small burrowing North American snake

Examples

The sand snake spends most of its time buried beneath loose desert soils.
Scientists observed a sand snake emerging to hunt insects at dusk.
Children were fascinated by the small sand snake they discovered under a rock.
