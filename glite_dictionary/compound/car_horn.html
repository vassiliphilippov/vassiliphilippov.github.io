car horn

1. NOUN

/ kɑr hɔrn / (AmE), / kɑː hɔːn / (BrE)
a device in a car that emits a loud sound for warning or attracting attention
device in cars that makes a warning sound

Examples

She pressed the car horn when the cyclist swerved in front of her.
A broken car horn can make driving in the city difficult.
The loud sound of a car horn startled the pedestrians.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn - a device that makes a loud warning sound
