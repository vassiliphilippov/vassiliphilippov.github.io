strawberry tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri ˌtriː / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree (Arbutus unedo) native to southern Europe and Ireland, with white flowers and edible but bland scarlet berries
evergreen tree with scarlet edible berries

Examples

The fruits of the strawberry tree are bright red but surprisingly bland in flavor.
A mature strawberry tree can be an attractive addition to a Mediterranean garden.
She tasted a few strawberry tree berries while hiking through southern Spain.
