pineapple juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪˌnæpəl dʒus / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnæpl dʒuːs / (BrE)
the sweet liquid obtained from pineapples, often consumed as a drink or used in cooking
juice made from pineapples

Examples

I ordered a glass of pineapple juice with my breakfast.
You can use pineapple juice to tenderize meat in certain recipes.
She mixed some pineapple juice into the cocktail for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange juice - juice made from oranges
  • Synonym: grapefruit juice - juice made from grapefruits
