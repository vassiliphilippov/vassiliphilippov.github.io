fishtail palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌteɪl pɑm / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌteɪl pɑːm / (BrE)
an attractive East Indian palm tree with distinctive bipinnate, fishtail-shaped leaves, often grown as an ornamental plant
type of palm tree with fishtail-shaped leaves

Examples

The fishtail palm is popular in landscaping for its unique foliage.
Gardeners appreciate the beauty of the fishtail palm in tropical gardens.
The leaves of the fishtail palm resemble a fish's tail, giving the tree its name.
