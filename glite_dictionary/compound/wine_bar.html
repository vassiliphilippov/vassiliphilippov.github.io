wine bar

1. NOUN

/ waɪn bɑr / (AmE), / waɪn bɑː / (BrE)
a bar or establishment where wine is the main alcoholic beverage served, often offering a range of wines by the glass or bottle
bar serving mainly wine

Examples

They spent the evening at a chic wine bar sampling reds from around the world.
A new wine bar has opened downtown, featuring local vineyards.
She prefers to meet friends at a wine bar rather than a noisy nightclub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar - establishment where alcoholic drinks are served
