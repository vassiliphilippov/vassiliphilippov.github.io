kai apple

1. NOUN

/ kaɪ ˈæpəl / (AmE), / kaɪ ˈap(ə)l / (BrE)
a South African fruit with an apricot-like scent and flavor, often used for pickles and preserves
South African fruit like apricot

Examples

The homemade jam was made from kai apple harvested in the local orchard.
She bought fresh kai apples at the market to make preserves.
The tangy flavor of kai apple pairs well with spicy dishes.
