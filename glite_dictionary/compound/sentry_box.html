sentry box

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntri bɑks / (AmE), / ˈsɛntri bɒks / (BrE)
a small shelter with an open front, used to protect a sentry from the weather
small shelter for a sentry

Examples

The guard stood in the sentry box at the entrance to the palace.
During the storm, the soldier took shelter in his sentry box.
The castle’s sentry box was freshly painted and overlooked the drawbridge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guardhouse - building for guards or sentries
