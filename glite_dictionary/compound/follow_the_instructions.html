to follow the instructions

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑloʊ ðə ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ ði ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (BrE)
to do what the directions or guidelines tell you to do
do as the directions say

Examples

Please follow the instructions on the package to ensure safe usage.
He did not follow the instructions, which led to a critical error.
If you follow the instructions exactly, the experiment should work as described.

Synset Examples

Make sure to follow the prescription instructions carefully.
It's a simple task; just follow the instructions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to obey - to do what someone in authority tells you to do
  • Antonym: ignore the instructions - to not pay attention to or disregard directions
