commodities exchange

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɑdətiz ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdɪtiz ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
an organized market where raw materials and primary products are bought and sold, typically for future delivery
market for trading raw materials

Examples

The Chicago Board of Trade is a famous commodities exchange where grain and other goods are traded.
She started her career as a broker at a leading commodities exchange.
Oil and precious metals prices are determined by market activity at the commodities exchange.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - a marketplace for buying and selling stocks and shares
