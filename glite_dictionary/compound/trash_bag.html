trash bag

1. NOUN

/ træʃ bæɡ / (AmE), / træʃ bæɡ / (BrE)
a strong plastic bag used for collecting and disposing of waste
plastic bag for waste
american

Examples

She tied up the trash bag before taking it outside to the bin.
Could you please grab a new trash bag from under the sink?
The torn trash bag spilled its contents all over the driveway.

Synset Examples

I need a trash bag for this mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bin bag - a plastic bag for holding rubbish (chiefly UK)
  • Synonym: garbage bag - a plastic bag used for collecting waste (chiefly North America)
