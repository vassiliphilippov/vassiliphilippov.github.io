white slaver

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈsleɪvər / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsleɪvə / (BrE)
a person who forces women into prostitution, especially as part of an organized operation
person forcing women into prostitution

Examples

Police arrested a notorious white slaver who was running an illicit trafficking ring.
The story exposed the horrors faced by women at the hands of a white slaver in the city.
Authorities have increased efforts to eliminate networks operated by white slavers across the region.
