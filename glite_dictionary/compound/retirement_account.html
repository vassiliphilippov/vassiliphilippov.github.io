retirement account

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈtaɪərmənt əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a financial account or plan in which money is saved to be used after one retires from work
account for post-retirement savings

Examples

She decided to deposit extra funds into her retirement account every month.
A 401(k) is a popular type of retirement account in the United States.
It's important to start a retirement account early to ensure a comfortable future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pension - regular payment made during retirement
  • Synonym: 401(k) - a type of retirement savings plan in the US
  • Synonym: savings account - bank account for saving money
