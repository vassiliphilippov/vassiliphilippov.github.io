Kazak language

1. NOUN

/ kɑˈzɑk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈzæk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken primarily by the Kazakh people in Kazakhstan and surrounding regions
the Turkic language of Kazakhstan

Examples

Many students are learning Kazak language to connect with their heritage.
The grammar rules of the Kazak language differ from those of Russian.
She translated documents from English into Kazak language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kazakh - the Turkic language spoken by Kazakh people; alternative spelling

Etymology

From Russian 'казахский язык' (kazakhsky yazyk), via Kazakh 'қазақ тілі' (qazaq tili).
