toby jug

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.bi dʒʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.bi dʒʌɡ / (BrE)
A large mug or jug in the shape of a stout man, typically wearing a three-cornered hat, used primarily for serving or drinking beer.
mug shaped like a stout man

Examples

The collector proudly displayed a rare toby jug on the mantelpiece.
She poured ale from an old ceramic toby jug at the village fair.
His grandfather’s toby jug became a cherished family heirloom.

Etymology

Named after the character Toby Fillpot from 18th-century English drinking culture.
