lawn furniture

1. NOUN

/ lɔn ˈfɜrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / lɔːn ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
chairs, tables, and other furniture designed for use outdoors on a lawn or in a garden
outdoor furniture for lawns and gardens

Examples

We bought new lawn furniture to enjoy summer meals outside.
The lawn furniture was arranged in a circle for the garden party.
After the storm, she had to pick up pieces of lawn furniture that had blown across the yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patio furniture - outdoor furniture designed for use on patios
