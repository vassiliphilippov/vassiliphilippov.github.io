Golden Week

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən wiːk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən wiːk / (BrE)
a holiday period in Japan that includes several public holidays in late April and early May, often marked by travel and festivals
Japanese holiday period with multiple public holidays

Examples

Many people take vacations during Golden Week in Japan.
Hotels can be fully booked during Golden Week because of the crowds.
Some companies close their offices for all of Golden Week each year.

Synset Examples

We’re planning a trip during Golden Week.
Many festivals occur during Japan's Golden Week.
Golden Week is the perfect time for travel.
