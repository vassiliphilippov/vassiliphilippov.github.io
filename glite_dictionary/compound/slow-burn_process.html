slow-burn process

1. NOUN

/ ˈsloʊ ˌbɜrn ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ ˌbɜːn ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a process or development that unfolds gradually over an extended period of time
process that develops gradually

Examples

Building trust within a team is often a slow-burn process that requires patience and consistent effort.
The novel succeeds because of its slow-burn process of building tension and deepening character relationships.
Her recovery from the injury was a slow-burn process, taking months of therapy and persistence.

Synset Examples

It's a slow-burn process, but the results are worth it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradual process - a process that happens slowly over time
  • Antonym: rapid process - a process that happens quickly
