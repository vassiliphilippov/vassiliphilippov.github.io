local fishmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a fish seller or shop located near where one lives or works
nearby fish seller or shop

Examples

Every Saturday morning, I visit the local fishmonger for the freshest catch.
The local fishmonger always recommends the best seafood recipes to his customers.
She prefers to buy prawns from the local fishmonger rather than the supermarket.

Synset Examples

I bought some fresh salmon from the local fishmonger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishmonger - a person or shop that sells fish and seafood
