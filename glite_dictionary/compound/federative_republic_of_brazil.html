Federative Republic of Brazil

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɛdərətɪv rɪˈpʌblɪk əv brəˈzɪl / (AmE), / ˌfɛdərətɪv rɪˈpʌblɪk əv brəˈzɪl / (BrE)
The official name of Brazil, the largest country in South America and the world's largest Portuguese-speaking nation.
official name of Brazil, South American country

Examples

The president of the Federative Republic of Brazil addressed the United Nations general assembly.
International agreements often refer to the Federative Republic of Brazil in their formal texts.
Many official documents list the country's name as the Federative Republic of Brazil rather than simply Brazil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brazil - the country commonly known as Brazil in South America
