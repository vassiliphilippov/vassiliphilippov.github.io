gastro outbreak

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæstroʊ ˈaʊtˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrəʊ ˈaʊtbreɪk / (BrE)
an occurrence of widespread gastrointestinal illness, often caused by infection or contaminated food or water
occurrence of stomach illness

Examples

The school closed temporarily after a gastro outbreak affected dozens of students.
A gastro outbreak at the hotel led to numerous guests falling ill over the weekend.
Authorities warned the public about a recent gastro outbreak linked to contaminated water.

Synset Examples

They canceled the picnic because of a gastro outbreak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outbreak - a sudden occurrence of something unwelcome, such as disease

Etymology

Shortened form of 'gastroenteritis' plus 'outbreak'.
