pace lap

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪs læp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪs læp / (BrE)
the introductory lap in a car race, run at a controlled speed to prepare the cars for the start of the race
introductory lap in a car race

Examples

The drivers completed the pace lap before lining up for the official start.
A crash occurred during the pace lap, delaying the beginning of the race.
Fans cheered as the cars made their way through the pace lap behind the safety car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formation lap - the lap before the beginning of a race, often synonymous with pace lap
