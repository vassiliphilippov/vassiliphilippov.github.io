heavy heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi hɑːt / (BrE)
a feeling of deep sadness or sorrow; a state of being emotionally heavy or burdened, especially due to trouble or loss.
feeling of sadness or sorrow

Examples

She left her childhood home with a heavy heart, knowing she might never return.
With a heavy heart, he announced the company's closure to his employees.
I write this letter with a heavy heart to express my condolences for your loss.

Synset Examples

He had a heavy heart after the news.

She accepted the news with a heavy heart.
She left the party with a heavy heart.
She will depart with a heavy heart tonight.
He said goodbye with a heavy heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadness - the emotion of being sad or sorrowful
  • Antonym: joy - the emotion of great happiness
