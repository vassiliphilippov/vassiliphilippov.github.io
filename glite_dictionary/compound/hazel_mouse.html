hazel mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪzəl maʊs / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl maʊs / (BrE)
a small, agile rodent of the dormouse family, typically found in Europe and known for its bushy tail and nocturnal habits
a type of dormouse rodent

Examples

The hazel mouse is often difficult to spot due to its nocturnal lifestyle.
Researchers tagged several hazel mice to monitor their movement in the forest.
The hazel mouse builds its nest high in the branches for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormouse - a small nocturnal rodent related to the hazel mouse
