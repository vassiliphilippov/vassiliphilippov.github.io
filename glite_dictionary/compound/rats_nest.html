rat's nest

1. NOUN

/ ræts nɛst / (AmE), / ræts nɛst / (BrE)
a place or situation that is extremely messy, disorganized, or cluttered
messy or cluttered place or situation

Examples

I found a rat's nest of cables behind the television and had to untangle everything.
The kitchen turned into a rat's nest after the party last night.
Trying to fix the old computer, he discovered a rat's nest of wires inside the case.

Synset Examples

I cleaned up the rat's nest in the attic yesterday.
His desk is a complete rat's nest of papers.
The garage was a rat's nest of old tools and junk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a dirty or untidy state or place
  • Synonym: clutter - a collection of things lying about in an untidy mass
