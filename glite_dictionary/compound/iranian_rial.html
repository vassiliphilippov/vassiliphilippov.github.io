Iranian rial

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈreɪniən riˈɑl / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪniən riˈɑːl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Iran
currency of Iran

Examples

The exchange rate for the Iranian rial changes rapidly due to economic conditions.
She exchanged her dollars for Iranian rials before traveling to Tehran.
The government announced a new security feature for the Iranian rial bills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rial - unit of currency used in several countries

Etymology

From Persian 'rial', deriving from Spanish 'real' (royal), referring to a historical coin.
