Cape Froward

1. PROPN

/ keɪp ˈfɹaʊərd / (AmE), / keɪp ˈfɹaʊəd / (BrE)
the southernmost point on the mainland of South America, located on the Strait of Magellan in southern Chile
southernmost point of mainland South America

Examples

The expedition reached Cape Froward after several weeks of challenging travel through Patagonia.
Storms are frequent near Cape Froward, making it a difficult area for navigation.
Cape Froward is often cited in geography textbooks as the southern tip of mainland South America.
