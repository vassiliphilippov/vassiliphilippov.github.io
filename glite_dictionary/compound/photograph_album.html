photograph album

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊ.t̬ə.ˌgræf ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.tə.ˌgrɑːf ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
a book with blank pages used to collect and display photographs
book for storing photographs

Examples

She placed her favorite family pictures in a large photograph album on the living room shelf.
Looking through the old photograph album brought back many happy memories of childhood vacations.
Each page of the photograph album was carefully labeled with names and dates.
