vintage postage stamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ ˈpoʊstɪdʒ stæmp / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ ˈpəʊstɪdʒ stæmp / (BrE)
a postage stamp that is old, typically rare, and often collected for its historical or philatelic value
old collectible postage stamp

Examples

She displayed her most valuable vintage postage stamp in a glass-covered case.
Collectors gathered to exchange rare vintage postage stamps at the annual fair.
He carefully removed a vintage postage stamp from an old envelope.

Synset Examples

The letter required a vintage postage stamp for delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commemorative stamp - a stamp issued to honor or commemorate a specific event or person
