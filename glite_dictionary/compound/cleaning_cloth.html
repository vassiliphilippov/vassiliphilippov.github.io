cleaning cloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliː.nɪŋ klɔːθ / (AmE), / ˈkliː.nɪŋ klɒθ / (BrE)
a piece of cloth used for wiping or cleaning surfaces, especially to remove grease, oil, or dirt
cloth for wiping or cleaning surfaces

Examples

She grabbed a cleaning cloth to wipe down the greasy stove.
Always use a fresh cleaning cloth when polishing your car's surface.
The mechanic keeps a cleaning cloth handy for wiping tools after every use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rag - a piece of old cloth used for cleaning or wiping
