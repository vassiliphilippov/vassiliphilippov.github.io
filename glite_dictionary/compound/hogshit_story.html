hogshit story

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔɡˌʃɪt ˈstɔːɹi / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌʃɪt ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
A story that is absurd, unbelievable, or clearly false.
absurd or unbelievable tale
informal vulgar

Examples

He told me a hogshit story about how he once fought a bear with his bare hands.
Everyone knew it was just another hogshit story meant to impress the new students.
Don't waste my time with that hogshit story; tell me what really happened.

Synset Examples

I can't believe you fell for that hogshit story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tall tale - an exaggerated or fanciful story
