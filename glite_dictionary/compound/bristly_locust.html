bristly locust

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪsli ˈloʊkəst / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsli ˈləʊkəst / (BrE)
a large shrub or small tree of eastern North America, with bristly stems and clusters of pink flowers
shrubs or small trees with bristly stems and pink flowers

Examples

The bristly locust is often planted along roadsides for erosion control.
We admired the bright pink blooms of the bristly locust during our hike.
A dense thicket of bristly locust blocked the edge of the old field.
