extra flair

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkstrə flɛr / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə fleə / (BrE)
an added touch of style, elegance, or originality that enhances the overall appearance or impression of something
additional style or elegance

Examples

Her outfit had extra flair thanks to the bright scarf she wore.
The chef added edible flowers to the dish for extra flair.
Decorating the invitations with gold foil gave them extra flair.

Synset Examples

She added a frill to her dress for extra flair.
He added a sparklepire to his artwork for extra flair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flair - distinctive and stylish quality
