cute chungus

1. NOUN

/ kjuːt ˈtʃʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / kjuːt ˈtʃʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A slang term for a cute, chubby character, especially in internet memes and popular culture.
slang for a cute, chubby character
slang

Examples

The new video game character is such a cute chungus that fans instantly fell in love.
Everyone wanted the stuffed animal because it looked just like a cute chungus.
She drew a cute chungus for her friend's birthday card, making everyone smile.

Synset Examples

The plush toy was designed as a cute chungus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chungus - a slang term for a large, chubby character, especially in memes

Etymology

Formed from "cute" (pleasing or endearing) and "chungus" (internet meme term for a large, chubby character, origin in online culture).
