knock-down

1. ADJ

/ nɑk daʊn / (AmE), / nɒk daʊn / (BrE)
(of a blow or force) powerful enough to knock someone or something to the ground or to overwhelm.
powerful enough to knock down or overwhelm

Examples

The boxer delivered a knock-down punch that ended the match quickly.
The storm hit with knock-down force, causing significant damage across the region.
The prosecutor's knock-down argument left the defense speechless.

Synset Examples

a knock-down blow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overpowering - extremely strong or intense; overwhelming
  • Antonym: mild - not strong or powerful; gentle
