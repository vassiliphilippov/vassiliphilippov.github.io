touch screen

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌtʃ ˌskrin / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ ˌskriːn / (BrE)
A computer or device display that enables the user to interact by touching areas on the screen.
interactive display operated by touch

Examples

Most modern smartphones use a touch screen for navigation and input.
She wiped the touch screen with a cloth to remove the fingerprints.
The new ATM features a large touch screen for customer convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display - an electronic device for displaying images or information
