accommodation endorser

1. NOUN

/ əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən ɪnˈdɔrsər / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən ɪnˈdɔːsə / (BrE)
a person who endorses a promissory note solely as a favor to the borrower, receiving no compensation or benefit
person who endorses a note as a favor, no benefit
legal

Examples

The bank required an accommodation endorser before approving the loan.
As an accommodation endorser, she took on risk without any personal benefit.
He agreed to become an accommodation endorser to help his friend secure financing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accommodation party - a person who signs a negotiable instrument to help another party, without receiving value
