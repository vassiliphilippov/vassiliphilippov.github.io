total nutsucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬əl ˈnʌtˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈnʌtˌsʌkə / (BrE)
A vulgar and offensive term used to express strong disappointment with something or someone; a derogatory insult for a person or thing considered deeply unsatisfactory.
vulgar insult for something disappointing
vulgar offensive slang

Examples

He dismissed the movie as a total nutsucker after seeing it on opening night.
Many fans felt that the game’s ending was a total nutsucker.
After waiting hours for the concert, the sound system failure was a total nutsucker.

Synset Examples

He called the new policy a total nutsucker.
