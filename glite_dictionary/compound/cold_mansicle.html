cold mansicle

1. NOUN

/ koʊld ˈmænˌsɪkəl / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈmænˌsɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A frozen treat similar to a popsicle.
frozen treat like a popsicle
informal

Examples

After mowing the lawn, he enjoyed a cold mansicle to cool down.
The novelty of a cold mansicle drew a crowd at the summer festival.
On hot days, nothing beats relaxing with a cold mansicle in hand.

Synset Examples

A cold mansicle was the perfect treat after running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: popsicle - a flavored frozen treat on a stick

Etymology

Blend of 'man' and 'popsicle', suggesting a popsicle for adults.
