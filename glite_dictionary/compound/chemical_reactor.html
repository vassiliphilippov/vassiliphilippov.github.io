chemical reactor

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl riˈæktər / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl riˈæktə / (BrE)
a vessel or device designed to contain and control chemical reactions
apparatus for chemical reactions

Examples

The chemical reactor must be carefully monitored to ensure safety during the process.
Modern chemical reactors are designed to maximize efficiency and minimize waste.
She specializes in the design of industrial chemical reactors for large-scale production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reactor - an apparatus used to initiate and control a reaction
