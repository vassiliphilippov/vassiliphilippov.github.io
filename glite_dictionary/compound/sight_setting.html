sight setting

1. NOUN

/ saɪt ˈsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / saɪt ˈsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
the adjustment of a gun's sight for elevation and windage to accurately target at a specific range
adjustment of a gun's sight for accuracy

Examples

Before firing, the marksman carefully checked the sight setting on his rifle.
Different sight settings are required when shooting at targets at varying distances.
Accurate sight setting is crucial for long-range shooting competitions.
