hockey league

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːki liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɒki liːɡ / (BrE)
an organized association of hockey teams that compete against each other according to a set schedule and rules
an association of hockey teams

Examples

After joining the national hockey league, the team saw a marked improvement in its performance.
The local youth hockey league organizes tournaments every winter.
Many players dream of making it to a professional hockey league one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: league - an association of sports teams or clubs formed to compete against each other
