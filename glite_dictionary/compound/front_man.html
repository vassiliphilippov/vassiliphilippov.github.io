front man

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt mæn / (AmE), / frʌnt mæn / (BrE)
a man who serves as a cover or figurehead for the real leaders of a dubious or illegal activity
man acting as a cover for illegal activities

Examples

The authorities suspected he was just a front man for the criminal organization.
The company hired a front man to pose as its CEO while the real owner stayed hidden.
Many believe the politician is only a front man for powerful interests operating behind the scenes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: figurehead - a person with apparent but no real authority, used by others as a cover
