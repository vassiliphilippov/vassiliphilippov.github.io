red bird's eye

1. NOUN

/ ɹɛd bɝdz aɪ / (AmE), / ɹɛd bɜːdz aɪ / (BrE)
A European biennial plant of the catchfly family, with red or pink flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Lychnis.
European plant with red or pink flowers
