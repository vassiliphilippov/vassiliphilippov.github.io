per annum

1. ADV

/ pɚ ˈænəm / (AmE), / pə ˈænəm / (BrE)
For each year; annually, especially with reference to amounts of money or occurrences.
for each year; annually

Examples

He received a salary of $75,000 per annum at his new job.
The subscription costs $20 per annum, payable each January.
We expect sales to increase by 7% per annum over the next decade.

Synset Examples

he earned $100,000 per annum
we issue six volumes per annum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annually - happening every year
  • Degree Lower: per month - for each month

Etymology

From Latin 'per annum' meaning 'by the year'.
