middle-age spread

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪd.əl.eɪdʒ ˈsprɛd / (AmE), / ˌmɪd.əl.eɪdʒ ˈsprɛd / (BrE)
an increase in body weight, especially around the waist, that commonly occurs during middle age
weight gain in middle age

Examples

He noticed that his middle-age spread was starting to show despite his regular workouts.
Many people struggle with middle-age spread as their metabolism slows down.
A healthy diet and exercise can help prevent middle-age spread.
