shotgun mike

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn ˌmaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtɡʌn ˌmaɪk / (BrE)
a highly directional microphone with a long, narrow barrel, designed to capture sound from a specific area, often from a distance
highly directional microphone

Examples

The sound technician positioned the shotgun mike to capture the actors' voices clearly from across the set.
Using a shotgun mike, the crew recorded outdoor interviews without picking up much background noise.
Because of the long barrel, a shotgun mike is ideal for recording dialogue in noisy environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microphone - device that converts sound waves into electrical signals

Etymology

From 'shotgun' (referring to its barrel shape) and 'mike', a shortened form of 'microphone'.
