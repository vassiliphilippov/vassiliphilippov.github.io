apple-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈæpəl ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the smell or fragrance of apples
smelling of apples

Examples

The apple-scented candle filled the room with a fresh, fruity aroma.
She wore an apple-scented perfume that reminded him of autumn orchards.
The store sells a variety of apple-scented bath products.
