rootsy music

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːtsi ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈruːtsi ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music that draws on traditional or folk influences, often noted for its authentic and earthy quality
music with traditional or folk influences

Examples

He has always been drawn to rootsy music that blends country and blues.
The festival featured several artists known for their rootsy music style.
Critics praised the album's blend of modern rock with rootsy music influences.

Synset Examples

He prefers rootsy music that tells real stories.
The band has a rootsy sound that captivates audiences.
Her rootsy style reflects her deep connection to her heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk music - traditional music of the people
  • Synonym: roots music - music based on traditional genres like blues, country, and folk

Etymology

From 'roots' (in reference to origins or traditions) plus '-y' (forming adjectives), used in American popular music criticism.
