oriental spruce

1. NOUN

/ ɔːrˈiːən.tl̩ sprus / (AmE), / ɔːˈriː.ənt.l̩ spruːs / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to the Caucasus and Asia Minor, often grown as an ornamental, with drooping branchlets
evergreen tree native to the Caucasus and Asia Minor

Examples

The oriental spruce is prized by gardeners for its gracefully weeping branches.
We planted an oriental spruce in the backyard for its dense, dark green foliage.
Unlike other species, the oriental spruce thrives in colder climates and acidic soils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spruce - a type of evergreen coniferous tree
