maiden over

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdn ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
an over in cricket during which no runs are scored
cricket: an over with no runs scored

Examples

The bowler delivered a brilliant maiden over at the start of the match.
It's rare to see so many maiden overs in a T20 game.
Her accuracy earned her three maiden overs in the first session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: over - a set of six legal deliveries bowled by a player in cricket

Etymology

From 'maiden', meaning 'unspoiled' or 'unused', referring to the bowler not conceding runs.
