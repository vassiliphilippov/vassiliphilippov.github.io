tulip bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlɪp bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪp bɛd / (BrE)
A section of ground prepared for growing tulips, usually arranged as a distinct display in a garden.
flowerbed for tulips

Examples

Each spring, the tulip bed in our front yard bursts with vibrant colors.
They spent the afternoon weeding the tulip bed to keep it looking tidy.
A neatly arranged tulip bed can be the centerpiece of any garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowerbed - A prepared area of soil for growing flowers
