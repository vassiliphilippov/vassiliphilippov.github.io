fast comer

1. NOUN

/ fæst ˈkʌmər / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who attains success or recognition very quickly compared to others
someone who succeeds quickly

Examples

He was considered a fast comer in finance, rising to a managerial role within a year.
As a fast comer in politics, she quickly won the support of influential figures.
Despite being new to the company, she's already known as a fast comer for her quick promotions.

Synset Examples

He's a fast comer in the tech industry, gaining respect quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast mover - a person or company that acts or progresses quickly
  • Antonym: late bloomer - someone who achieves success or maturity later than usual
