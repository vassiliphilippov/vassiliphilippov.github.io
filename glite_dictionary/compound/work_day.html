work day

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːk ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A day on which work is usually done, especially as part of a normal schedule of employment.
a day when work is done

Examples

After a long work day, she likes to relax with a good book.
My typical work day starts at 9 a.m. and ends at 5 p.m.
They decided to extend the work day by one hour to meet the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: day off - a day when one does not work
