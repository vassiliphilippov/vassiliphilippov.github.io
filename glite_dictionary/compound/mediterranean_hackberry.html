Mediterranean hackberry

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈhækbɛri / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈhakbəri / (BrE)
A bright green deciduous shade tree native to southern Europe, known for its dense foliage.
deciduous shade tree of southern Europe

Examples

The Mediterranean hackberry thrives in warm regions with mild winters.
Gardeners value the Mediterranean hackberry for its shade and resilience.
In southern France, the Mediterranean hackberry is a common sight along country roads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hackberry - a tree of the Celtis genus, known for its hard wood and edible berries
