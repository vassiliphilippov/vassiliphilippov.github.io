over nine thousand

1. X

/ ˈoʊvɚ naɪn ˈθaʊzənd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə naɪn ˈθaʊzənd / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) An expression used to indicate an extremely high quantity, degree, or level, often in exaggeration.
extremely large amount (hyperbolic, informal)
informal

Examples

People keep talking about the new movie's budget, claiming it's over nine thousand dollars.
When she saw the final exam, her stress levels went over nine thousand.
The complaints about the new policy are over nine thousand by now.

Synset Examples

The excitement in the room was over nine thousand.
His strength is over nine thousand, they say.

Etymology

Popularized as an internet meme originating from the Japanese anime 'Dragon Ball Z', where the phrase was used in a dramatic context.
