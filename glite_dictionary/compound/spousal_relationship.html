spousal relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaʊzəl rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈspaʊzəl rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
the legally recognized relationship between two people who are married to each other
relationship between married spouses

Examples

Their spousal relationship has lasted more than thirty years, filled with trust and support.
Financial matters can put strain on a spousal relationship if not handled openly and honestly.
Many couples attend counseling to help strengthen their spousal relationship.

Synset Examples

a long and happy marriage
God bless this union

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage - the legal union of two people as partners in a personal relationship

Etymology

From 'spousal', deriving from Latin 'sponsalis' (betrothal), and 'relationship'.
