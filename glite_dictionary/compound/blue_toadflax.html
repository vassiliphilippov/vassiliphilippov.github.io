blue toadflax

1. NOUN

/ blu toʊdˌflæks / (AmE), / bluː təʊdˌflæks / (BrE)
A North American plant with slender spikes (racemes) of blue-violet flowers, commonly found in fields and along roadsides.
North American plant with blue-violet flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with blue toadflax swaying in the gentle breeze.
She identified the wildflower as blue toadflax during her hike in the countryside.
Gardeners appreciate blue toadflax for its delicate blue-violet blooms in late spring.
