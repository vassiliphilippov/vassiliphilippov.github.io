rya rug

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪ.ə ˌrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.ə ˌrʌɡ / (BrE)
a thick, shaggy rug of Scandinavian origin, traditionally made in Sweden and often characterized by long pile and colorful patterns
thick Scandinavian shag rug

Examples

The antique rya rug in the living room added both color and warmth to the space.
She bought a handwoven rya rug as a centerpiece for her Swedish-inspired decor.
A traditional rya rug can be recognized by its long, soft pile and geometric designs.

Etymology

From Swedish 'rya', the name of the traditional Scandinavian rug.
