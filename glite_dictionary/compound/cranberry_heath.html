cranberry heath

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrænˌbɛri hiːθ / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri hiːθ / (BrE)
A small prostrate or ascending shrub with scarlet flowers and succulent fruit resembling cranberries, sometimes classified in the genus Styphelia.
a shrub with cranberry-like fruit

Examples

The cranberry heath thrives in sandy soils and bears edible, berry-like fruits.
Scientists recently discovered new medicinal properties in the fruits produced by the cranberry heath.
Hikers admired the vibrant scarlet flowers of the cranberry heath along the trail.
