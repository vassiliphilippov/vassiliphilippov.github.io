delivery boy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɪvəri bɔɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man employed to deliver goods to customers, often by bicycle or motorbike
male employed to deliver goods

Examples

The delivery boy arrived promptly with our pizza.
She thanked the delivery boy for bringing the package to her door.
After school, he worked as a delivery boy to earn extra money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery man - a man employed to deliver goods to customers
  • Synonym: courier - a person employed to deliver messages or packages
  • Synonym: delivery girl - a girl or young woman employed to deliver goods
