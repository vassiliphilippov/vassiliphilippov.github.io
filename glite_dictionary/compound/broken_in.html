broken in

1. ADJ

/ ˈbroʊkən ɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən ɪn / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a horse) tamed or trained to obey commands or accept being ridden.
tamed or trained (esp. a horse)

Examples

The broken in pony was gentle enough for children to ride.
After several weeks of work, the horse was finally broken in.
These broken in animals no longer react fearfully to human contact.

Synset Examples

a horse broken to the saddle
this old nag is well broken in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tame - domesticated or trained, not wild
  • Antonym: untrained - not trained or tamed; wild or inexperienced
