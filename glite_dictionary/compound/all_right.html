all right

1. ADV

/ ɑl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
used to express agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment, often at the beginning of a sentence
expression of agreement or acknowledgment

Examples

All right, let’s get started with the meeting.
All right, I’ll come with you.
All right, I see what you mean now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: okay - used to express agreement or acceptance

2. ADV

/ ɔl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
in a satisfactory or acceptable manner; adequately
satisfactorily; adequately

Examples

Despite feeling nervous, she performed all right in the interview.
The cake turned out all right, even though I forgot an ingredient.
His ankle hurt during the game, but he managed to play all right anyway.
