Caliphate State

1. PROPN

/ ˈkælɪfeɪt steɪt / (AmE), / ˈkælɪfeɪt steɪt / (BrE)
a Turkish terrorist organization of fundamentalist Muslims, associated with al-Qaeda, that operates in Germany and seeks to overthrow the Turkish government to establish an Islamic nation modeled on Iran
Turkish Islamist terrorist group in Germany

Examples

The German authorities increased their surveillance of the Caliphate State after new threats emerged.
Members of the Caliphate State were arrested on charges of plotting attacks against Turkish officials.
The international community has condemned the violent ideology promoted by the Caliphate State.

Etymology

From 'caliphate' (Islamic state led by a caliph) and 'state' (organized political community).
