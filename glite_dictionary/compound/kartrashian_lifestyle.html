Kartrashian lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈtræʃiən ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / kɑːˈtræʃiən ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A lifestyle characterized by conspicuous excess, materialism, and pursuit of fame, often associated with the Kardashian family.
extravagant, fame-focused lifestyle
informal slang

Examples

She joked about her Kartrashian lifestyle during dinner.
The influencer's new mansion perfectly reflects her Kartrashian lifestyle.
Critics say that show promotes a Kartrashian lifestyle to teenagers.

Synset Examples

She joked about her Kartrashian lifestyle during dinner.
That reality show is all about the Kartrashian lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kardashian lifestyle - Lifestyle associated with the Kardashian family's fame and excess

Etymology

Blend of 'Kardashian' (American celebrity family) and 'trash', used pejoratively.
