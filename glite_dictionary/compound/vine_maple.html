vine maple

1. NOUN

/ vaɪn ˈmeɪ.pəl / (AmE), / vaɪn ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
a small maple tree of northwestern North America, with arching stems that can root and form dense thickets
a maple tree native to northwestern North America

Examples

The vine maple is common in the forests of the Pacific Northwest.
We planted a vine maple to add autumn color to our garden.
The thickets of vine maple provided shelter for many small animals.
