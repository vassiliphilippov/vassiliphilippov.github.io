telephone bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn bɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌfəʊn bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the amount of money owed for telephone service during a particular period
statement of charges for phone service

Examples

I was surprised by how high my telephone bill was this month.
She called the company to dispute a charge on her telephone bill.
Many people now pay their telephone bill online instead of by mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility bill - a statement showing charges for electricity, water, gas, or other utilities
  • Synonym: telephone bill - statement of charges for telephone service
