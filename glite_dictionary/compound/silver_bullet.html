silver bullet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˈbʊlət / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
A simple and seemingly magical solution to a complex or difficult problem.
simple solution to a complex problem

Examples

Many believe technology is the silver bullet that will fix education.
There is no silver bullet for solving complex global challenges.
The committee warned that a silver bullet does not exist for economic recovery.

Synset Examples

Many believe technology is the silver bullet for education.
There is no silver bullet for solving complex problems.
Finding a silver bullet solution often leads to disappointment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panacea - a universal remedy for all problems or diseases
  • Synonym: cure-all - a solution that cures everything

Etymology

Metaphor from folklore, where a silver bullet was believed to be the only weapon effective against supernatural beings such as werewolves.
