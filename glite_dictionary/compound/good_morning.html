good morning

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
A polite expression used to greet someone in the morning or to wish them well at the beginning of the day.
morning greeting

Examples

Every day when he arrived at the office, he smiled and said good morning to his colleagues.
She greeted her neighbors with a cheerful good morning as she walked her dog.
A quick good morning can brighten someone's entire day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good afternoon - a conventional greeting used in the afternoon
  • Synonym: good evening - a conventional greeting used in the evening
  • Antonym: good night - a conventional expression used when parting or going to bed at night
