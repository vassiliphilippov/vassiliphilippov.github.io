Discovery Day

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈskʌvəri deɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌvəri deɪ / (BrE)
a holiday in some countries commemorating the discovery of the Americas by Christopher Columbus
public holiday marking Columbus's discovery of America

Examples

Many schools and offices are closed on Discovery Day each year.
The parade celebrating Discovery Day attracts thousands of people.
Some historians debate the importance of Discovery Day in modern times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Columbus Day - a holiday in the United States commemorating Christopher Columbus's landing in the Americas
