studio couch

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdioʊ kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdiəʊ kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
an upholstered couch designed to be easily converted into a double bed
convertible couch-bed

Examples

We bought a studio couch for the guest room because it saves space and doubles as a bed.
The studio couch in the living area easily converts into a comfortable bed for overnight visitors.
She prefers a studio couch in her small apartment to keep the room flexible and stylish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa bed - a sofa that can be converted into a bed
  • Synonym: futon - a padded mattress, often foldable, used as a bed or couch
  • Degree Lower: couch - a long, upholstered seat for several people
