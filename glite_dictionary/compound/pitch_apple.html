pitch apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃ ˌæp.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃ ˌæp.əl / (BrE)
a tropical American tree (Clusia rosea) with thick, leathery leaves and solitary white or rose-colored flowers
tropical American tree with white or rose flowers

Examples

The pitch apple is often grown as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
Visitors admired the pitch apple for its beautiful, waxy flowers and broad green leaves.
The large leathery leaves of the pitch apple make it ideal for providing dense shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clusia - genus of tropical American trees and shrubs, including pitch apple
