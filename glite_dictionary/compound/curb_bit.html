curb bit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɝb ˌbɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɜːb ˌbɪt / (BrE)
A type of horse's bit with an attached chain or strap, designed to exert pressure on the horse's chin to aid in control.
horse's bit with chain or strap for control

Examples

The rider adjusted the curb bit on the horse before entering the show ring.
Some horses respond better to a curb bit than to a snaffle bit.
The curb bit provides additional leverage, making it easier to control strong-willed horses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bit - metal mouthpiece for controlling a horse
