brass buttons

1. NOUN

/ bræs ˈbʌtənz / (AmE), / brɑːs ˈbʌtənz / (BrE)
A South African herb (Cotula coronopifolia) with small, golden-yellow, globe-shaped flower heads, cultivated as an ornamental plant.
ornamental plant with yellow globe-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden was brightened by a patch of brass buttons near the pond.
Landscapers often use brass buttons for ground cover in coastal gardens.
I planted brass buttons along the walkway, and now yellow blooms appear every spring.
