domestic science

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The study of the theory and practice of homemaking, including cooking, cleaning, sewing, and household management, taught as a subject in schools.
subject on homemaking skills

Examples

She decided to take domestic science in high school to learn more about cooking and nutrition.
In the past, domestic science was a required subject for all female students.
The domestic science lab was fully equipped with stoves and sewing machines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: home economics - the study of managing a household and its resources
