dead end job

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ɛnd dʒɑb / (AmE), / dɛd end dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job with no chance of progress or promotion
job with no advancement opportunities

Examples

After ten years in a dead end job, he decided to change careers and go back to school.
Many young people worry about getting stuck in a dead end job with no future.
She left her dead end job at the factory to pursue her dream of opening a bakery.

Synset Examples

He felt stuck in a dead end job with no promotions.
She felt stuck in a dead-end junk job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: career - a job or series of jobs with opportunities for advancement
