feeling daiquiried

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈdaɪkəriːd / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈdaɪkərɪd / (BrE)
slightly drunk or tipsy after drinking daiquiris
slightly drunk from daiquiris
informal

Examples

After two cocktails, she was clearly feeling daiquiried and started to laugh at everything.
I get feeling daiquiried pretty quickly because they're so sweet and go down easily.
He texted me to say he was feeling daiquiried and might be home late.

Synset Examples

Feeling daiquiried, she decided to take a nap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tipsy - slightly drunk
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol

Etymology

From 'daiquiri', a cocktail named after Daiquirí, a town in Cuba, with '-ed' forming an adjective.
