Javanese language

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɑːvəˈniz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌdʒɑːvəˈniːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Austronesian language spoken mainly on the island of Java in Indonesia.
language spoken in Java, Indonesia

Examples

More than eighty million people speak Javanese language as their native tongue.
He is fluent in both Indonesian and Javanese language.
The Javanese language has a rich literary tradition dating back centuries.

Etymology

From Java (the island) + -ese (denoting nationality or language).
