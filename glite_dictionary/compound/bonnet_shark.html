bonnet shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑnɪt ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbɒnɪt ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a small, harmless hammerhead shark with a spade-shaped head, commonly found in bays and estuaries
a small hammerhead shark species

Examples

The bonnet shark is often seen swimming above sandy bottoms in shallow bays.
Unlike larger hammerheads, the bonnet shark poses no threat to humans.
Researchers tagged several bonnet sharks to monitor their movement patterns in coastal waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammerhead shark - a family of sharks with a distinctive hammer-shaped head
