hurdle race

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɝːdəl reɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɜːdəl reɪs / (BrE)
A running race in which athletes must jump over a series of barriers called hurdles.
race involving jumping over hurdles

Examples

The men's 110-meter hurdle race was the highlight of the track meet.
She trained for months to prepare for her first hurdle race.
Falling at the last hurdle cost him the hurdle race victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steeplechase - a race in which competitors jump fences and ditches
