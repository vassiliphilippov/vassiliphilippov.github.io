genus Radyera

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ræˈdɪərə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ræˈdɪərə / (BrE)
A small genus of flowering shrubs native to the Southern Hemisphere, including bush hibiscus species.
genus of shrubs; bush hibiscus

Examples

The genus Radyera comprises several shrubs commonly known as bush hibiscus.
Botanists recently discovered a new species within the genus Radyera in Australia.
Gardeners value plants from the genus Radyera for their attractive hibiscus-like flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bush hibiscus - a common name for plants in the genus Radyera
