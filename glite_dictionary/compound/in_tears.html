in tears

1. X

/ ɪn tɪrz / (AmE), / ɪn tɪəz / (BrE)
crying or overcome with emotion such that one is weeping
crying; weeping

Examples

By the time the movie ended, she was in tears.
He left the room in tears after hearing the sad news.
Their heartfelt speech had the whole family in tears.

Synset Examples

The play was a tragedy that left the audience in tears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crying - shedding tears as an expression of emotion
  • Synonym: sobbing - crying noisily, making loud convulsive gasps
