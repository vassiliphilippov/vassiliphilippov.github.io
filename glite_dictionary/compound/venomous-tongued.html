venomous-tongued

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛnəməs tʌŋd / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəməs tʌŋd / (BrE)
(of a person or speech) characterized by harsh, spiteful, or caustic comments or language.
having harsh or spiteful speech

Examples

Her venomous-tongued remarks created tension at the meeting.
The columnist was known for his venomous-tongued reviews of new restaurants.
Despite her venomous-tongued reputation, she could be surprisingly kind in private.

Synset Examples

Her venomous-tongued comments hurt many feelings.
Being venomous-tongued can lead to isolation.
The venomous-tongued critic didn't hold back in her review.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp-tongued - speaking in a harsh or critical way
  • Antonym: sweet-tongued - speaking in a gentle or flattering manner
