beer o'clock

1. NOUN

/ bɪr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / bɪə əˈklɒk / (BrE)
an informal time, typically after work or on a social occasion, regarded as appropriate for drinking beer or relaxing
time appropriate for drinking beer
informal

Examples

It’s only three in the afternoon, but after that long meeting, it feels like beer o'clock.
Once the sun comes out on Friday, everyone agrees it’s beer o'clock.
Do you want to join us in the garden when it’s beer o'clock later?

Synset Examples

After work, it’s always beer o'clock for us.
He declared it was beer o'clock and poured himself a drink.
It's finally beer o'clock; let's head to the bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine o'clock - a time considered appropriate for drinking wine
