fettuccine Alfredo

1. NOUN

/ fɛtəˌtʃini ælˈfreɪdoʊ / (AmE), / fɛtʊˌtʃiːni ælˈfriːdəʊ / (BrE)
An Italian pasta dish made with fettuccine served in a rich cream and cheese sauce.
pasta with creamy cheese sauce

Examples

I ordered fettuccine Alfredo at the Italian restaurant last night.
Her favorite comfort food is homemade fettuccine Alfredo with extra Parmesan cheese.
Many people add grilled chicken or broccoli to their fettuccine Alfredo for a heartier meal.

Etymology

From Italian, literally 'Alfredo's fettuccine', named after restaurateur Alfredo di Lelio.
