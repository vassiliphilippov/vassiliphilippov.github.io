talent management

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæl.ənt ˈmæn.ədʒ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈtælənt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the systematic process of attracting, developing, and retaining skilled employees within an organization
strategies for managing employee talent

Examples

Effective talent management can give companies a competitive edge in their industry.
Many organizations invest in talent management programs to develop their best employees.
Modern talent management involves using technology to identify and train future leaders.

Synset Examples

Effective talent management is crucial for company growth.
Their talent management strategy has proven successful.
She specializes in talent management for tech startups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human resources - the department responsible for managing employee relations and personnel
  • Synonym: personnel management - administration of employees and workforce matters
