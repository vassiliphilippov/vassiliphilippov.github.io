adhesive bandage

1. NOUN

/ ədˈhisɪv ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪv ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
A small strip of fabric or plastic with a sticky coating and a pad, used to cover minor wounds or cuts.
strip to cover minor wounds

Examples

She covered the small cut on her finger with an adhesive bandage.
Do you have an adhesive bandage in your first aid kit?
The nurse cleaned the scrape and applied an adhesive bandage to protect it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band-aid - a brand name often used generically for adhesive bandage
  • Synonym: plaster - british term for a small adhesive bandage
