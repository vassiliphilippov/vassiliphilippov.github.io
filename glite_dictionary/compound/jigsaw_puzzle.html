jigsaw puzzle

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪɡˌsɔ pʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡsɔ ˈpʌzl̩ / (BrE)
A puzzle consisting of a picture that has been cut into many interlocking pieces, which must be fitted together to re-form the complete image.
picture puzzle with interlocking pieces

Examples

My grandmother loves spending weekends working on a jigsaw puzzle at her kitchen table.
The jigsaw puzzle was missing one piece, so the picture was never complete.
Solving a difficult jigsaw puzzle can be a relaxing way to spend an afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - a game or problem designed to test ingenuity or knowledge

Etymology

From 'jigsaw', a type of saw used to cut the puzzles, and 'puzzle'.
