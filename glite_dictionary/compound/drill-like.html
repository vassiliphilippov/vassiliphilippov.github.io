drill-like

1. ADJ

/ drɪl laɪk / (AmE), / drɪl laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a drill, especially in appearance or operation
resembling a drill

Examples

The device's drill-like attachment made it ideal for boring precise holes in wood.
He used a drill-like motion to quickly mix the ingredients.
The insect's drill-like mouthparts allow it to bore into hard tree bark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - a tool or machine with a pointed end for making holes
