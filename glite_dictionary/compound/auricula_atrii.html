auricula atrii

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə ˈeɪtraɪ / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə ˈaːtrii / (BrE)
A small conical pouch projecting from the upper front part of each atrium of the heart.
pouch on atrium of heart
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully examined the auricula atrii during the heart procedure.
Blood clots can sometimes form in the auricula atrii, increasing the risk of stroke.
A detailed scan revealed an abnormality in the left auricula atrii.

Etymology

From Latin: 'auricula' meaning little ear, and 'atrii' meaning of the atrium.
