science museum

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪəns mjuˈziəm / (AmE), / ˈsaɪəns mjuˈzɪəm / (BrE)
a museum dedicated to collecting, preserving, and displaying objects of scientific significance and interest
museum for scientific exhibits and education

Examples

The new science museum features hands-on experiments for children of all ages.
On our school trip, we visited a science museum to learn about space exploration.
Many cities have a science museum that inspires visitors to explore scientific discoveries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: museum - a building where objects of historical, artistic, or scientific interest are exhibited
