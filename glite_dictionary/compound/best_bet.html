best bet

1. NOUN

/ bɛst bɛt / (AmE), / bɛst bɛt / (BrE)
the most reliable, advisable, or likely option for success
most reliable option; best choice

Examples

If you're looking for authentic cuisine, the local market is your best bet.
For a quick answer to your question, the internet is your best bet.
When it comes to travel insurance, reading online reviews is usually your best bet.

Synset Examples

Investing in stocks is often the best bet for growth.
Taking the bus is your best bet for getting there.
If you're unsure, asking a local is your best bet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe bet - an option that is very likely to succeed or be correct
  • Antonym: long shot - an option that is unlikely to succeed
