fudge sauce

1. NOUN

/ fʌdʒ sɔs / (AmE), / fʌdʒ sɔːs / (BrE)
a thick, rich chocolate sauce, typically served hot as a dessert topping
thick hot chocolate dessert sauce

Examples

She poured warm fudge sauce over her ice cream for an extra treat.
The brownies were topped with a generous amount of fudge sauce.
Would you like some fudge sauce on your banana split?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate sauce - a sweet sauce made from chocolate, used as a dessert topping
