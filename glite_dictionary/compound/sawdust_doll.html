sawdust doll

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔːˌdʌst dɑl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌdʌst dɒl / (BrE)
A doll stuffed with sawdust, typically made from cloth or similar materials and popular in earlier times.
a doll filled with sawdust

Examples

The old trunk in the attic contained a delicate sawdust doll from her childhood.
After years of play, the sawdust doll had a few seams that needed patching.
She cherished the handmade sawdust doll passed down from her grandmother.
