cross street

1. NOUN

/ krɔs strit / (AmE), / krɒs striːt / (BrE)
a street that intersects a main street, usually at a right angle and extends on both sides of it
street intersecting a main street

Examples

The shop is located on the corner of Main Avenue and the cross street.
Can you tell me which cross street is closest to the subway entrance?
There is a traffic light at every cross street along this boulevard.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: main street - the principal street of a town, often the main shopping street
