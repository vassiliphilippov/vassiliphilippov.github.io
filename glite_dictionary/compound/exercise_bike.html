exercise bike

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksərˌsaɪz baɪk / (AmE), / ˈeksəsaɪz baɪk / (BrE)
a stationary device designed for indoor cycling exercise, resembling a regular bicycle without wheels
stationary indoor cycling device

Examples

After work, she rode her exercise bike for thirty minutes to stay fit.
The gym has a row of exercise bikes for cardio workouts.
He bought an exercise bike to use during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationary bike - a device for indoor cycling exercise
