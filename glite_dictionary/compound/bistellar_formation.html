bistellar formation

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈstɛlər fərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / bɪˈstelə fəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a particular type of astronomical shape or configuration involving two or more stars
a shape formed by two or more stars

Examples

The bistellar formation in the sky was breathtaking.
Astronomers mapped the movement of the bistellar formation over several nights.
The guide pointed out a rare bistellar formation through the observatory telescope.

Synset Examples

The bistellar formation in the sky was breathtaking.

Etymology

From Latin 'bis' (twice) and 'stella' (star), referring to arrangements involving stars.
