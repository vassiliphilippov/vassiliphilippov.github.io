rump steak

1. NOUN

/ rʌmp steɪk / (AmE), / rʌmp steɪk / (BrE)
a steak cut from the rump of a cow, typically a tender and flavorful cut of beef
steak cut from the rump of beef

Examples

I ordered a rump steak with a side of vegetables at the restaurant.
She prefers her rump steak cooked medium rare for extra tenderness.
The chef recommended marinating the rump steak overnight for the best flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sirloin steak - a steak cut from the sirloin, another section of beef
