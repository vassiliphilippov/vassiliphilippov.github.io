medical institution

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌɪnstɪˈtuʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
an organization or facility where medical care, treatment, or research is provided
organization providing medical care or research

Examples

The town's largest medical institution recently expanded its emergency department to better serve patients.
Research at the medical institution has led to groundbreaking advances in cancer treatment.
She hopes to work at a medical institution that specializes in pediatric care after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospital - institution providing medical and surgical treatment and nursing care
  • Synonym: clinic - medical facility for outpatient care
