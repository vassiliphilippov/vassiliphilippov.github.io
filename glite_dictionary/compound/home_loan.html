home loan

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm loʊn / (AmE), / həʊm ləʊn / (BrE)
A loan, usually from a bank or other financial institution, that is secured by the value of a borrower's home, typically used to purchase or refinance real estate.
loan secured by a borrower's home

Examples

The couple applied for a home loan to buy their first house.
Due to her excellent credit score, she qualified for a lower interest home loan.
Many banks offer online calculators to estimate your monthly payments on a home loan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortgage - a legal agreement in which a person borrows money to buy property, especially a house, and pays back the loan over a period of years
