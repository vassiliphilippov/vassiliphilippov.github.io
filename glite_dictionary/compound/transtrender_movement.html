transtrender movement

1. NOUN

/ trænˈstrɛndər ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / trænˈstrɛndə ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a movement that advocates for the freedom of individuals to express gender identity as they choose, regardless of societal expectations or biological sex
movement for gender identity expression rights

Examples

The transtrender movement has sparked debate within the LGBTQ+ community.
Many young people find comfort and acceptance in the transtrender movement.
Critics misunderstand the goals of the transtrender movement, which centers on freedom of gender expression.

Synset Examples

The transtrender movement encourages authenticity and self-expression.

Etymology

From 'transtrender' (blend of 'transgender' and 'trend'), and 'movement'.
