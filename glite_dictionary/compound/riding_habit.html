riding habit

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌhæbɪt / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌhæbɪt / (BrE)
a long skirt and jacket, sometimes with a hat, traditionally worn by women for horseback riding
attire worn by women for horseback riding

Examples

In the portrait, she wore a tailored riding habit and held a crop.
Her elegant riding habit made her stand out during the hunt.
Victorian women often commissioned custom-made riding habits for equestrian events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: habit - distinctive clothing worn for a particular purpose or occupation
