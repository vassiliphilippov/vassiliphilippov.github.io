purplish-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːplɪʃ ˈblæk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ ˈblæk / (BrE)
Having a black color with a tinge or shade of purple.
black with a hint of purple

Examples

The velvet curtains were a deep purplish-black that added drama to the room.
Her hair appeared almost purplish-black in the dim evening light.
The artist mixed shades until he achieved the perfect purplish-black tone for the painting.
