inflow of new ideas

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌfloʊ əv nu aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌfləʊ əv njuː aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
the arrival or introduction of fresh and original concepts or thoughts
arrival of fresh concepts or thoughts

Examples

The inflow of new ideas helped the company stay ahead of its competitors.
Universities benefit from the inflow of new ideas brought by international students.
A constant inflow of new ideas is essential for innovation in technology firms.

Synset Examples

The inflow of new ideas revitalized the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influx of ideas - a large arrival of ideas
  • Antonym: outflow of ideas - the movement of ideas out of a group or entity
