whining Willy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnɪŋ ˈwɪli / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnɪŋ ˈwɪli / (BrE)
a nickname for a person who constantly complains or finds fault, especially in a tiresome, habitual way
a habitual complainer (nickname)
informal

Examples

I don't want to sound like a whining Willy, but this noise really bothers me.
Stop being such a whining Willy and try to look on the bright side for once.
At every meeting, there's always one whining Willy who complains about everything.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows him as whining Willy for his complaints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complainer - a person who complains frequently or habitually
  • Synonym: grumbler - someone who grumbles or complains often
