to hide someone's excitement

1. VERB

/ haɪd sʌmwʌnz ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / haɪd sʌmwʌnz ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
to deliberately prevent others from noticing one's feelings of joy or enthusiasm
conceal feelings of excitement

Examples

Even though he had won the award, he managed to hide his excitement during the ceremony.
She couldn't hide her excitement when she saw the surprise party waiting for her.
They tried to hide their excitement as the results were announced, but their smiles gave them away.

Synset Examples

He tried to hide his excitement when she walked in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to conceal - to keep something secret; to hide
