cat fancier

1. NOUN

/ kæt ˈfænsiər / (AmE), / kæt ˈfænsiə / (BrE)
a person who breeds and takes a keen interest in the care and appreciation of cats, often as a hobby
person who breeds and cares for cats

Examples

The annual show attracted many renowned cat fanciers from across the country.
As a dedicated cat fancier, she knows the history of nearly every breed.
Several cat fanciers contributed to the development of this rare breed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat lover - a person who especially likes or loves cats
