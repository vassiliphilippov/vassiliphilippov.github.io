meat market

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / miːt ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A shop where meat and poultry, and sometimes fish, are sold.
shop selling meat and poultry

Examples

I stopped by the meat market to buy some fresh chicken for dinner.
The old meat market on Main Street has been owned by the same family for generations.
Many shoppers prefer the local meat market over the supermarket for quality beef.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butcher - a person who sells or slaughters meat
  • Synonym: fishmonger - a shop selling fish; a fish seller
