party woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑɹti ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a female member of a political party who unwaveringly supports and follows the party's policies and leadership
female loyalist in a political party

Examples

She is known as a committed party woman who never questions the leadership's decisions.
The local branch relied on every party woman to mobilize voters during the campaign.
As a party woman, she always puts the organization's needs above her own.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party man - a male member of a political party who strictly follows the party line
