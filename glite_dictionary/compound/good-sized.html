good-sized

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd saɪzd / (AmE), / ɡʊd saɪzd / (BrE)
of a fairly large or adequate size; sufficiently big for a particular purpose
fairly large in size

Examples

We need a good-sized room for the meeting to fit everyone comfortably.
They have a good-sized backyard where the children can play.
She adopted a good-sized dog from the shelter last week.

Synset Examples

We need a good-sized room for the meeting.
She bought a good-sized TV for her living room.
The garden is good-sized for a barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: large - of considerable or relatively great size
  • Antonym: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
