finger-lickin' good

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌlɪkɪn ˈɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌlɪkɪn ˈɡʊd / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely tasty or delicious; so good that you want to lick your fingers after eating.
extremely tasty or delicious
informal

Examples

The barbecue ribs were so finger-lickin' good that we ordered a second plate.
Her chocolate cake is always finger-lickin' good and disappears in minutes.
We spent all afternoon cooking, but the meal turned out finger-lickin' good.

Synset Examples
