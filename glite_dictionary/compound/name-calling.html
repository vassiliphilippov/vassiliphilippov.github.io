name-calling

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪm ˌkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈneɪm ˌkɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of insulting someone by using derogatory or abusive names
verbal abuse by insulting names

Examples

The political debate turned ugly with constant name-calling from both sides.
Teachers discourage name-calling to create a respectful classroom environment.
She walked away from the argument to avoid further name-calling.

Synset Examples

Avoiding name-calling like Jewtard promotes healthier discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - a disrespectful or scornful remark or act
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
