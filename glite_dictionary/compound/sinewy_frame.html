sinewy frame

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnju.i freɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɪnjuː.i freɪm / (BrE)
A lean and muscular body; a physique characterized by prominent muscles and little body fat.
lean, muscular physique

Examples

After years of hard labor, his sinewy frame spoke of strength and endurance.
She admired the dancer's sinewy frame as he moved across the stage.
His sinewy frame made him appear taller and more athletic than he really was.

Synset Examples

He had a sinewy frame from years of rock climbing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular build - a physique with well-developed muscles
  • Degree Lower: slender frame - a physique that is thin and slight
