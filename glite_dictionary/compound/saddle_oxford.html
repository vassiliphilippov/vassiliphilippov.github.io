saddle oxford

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæd.əl ˈɑːk.sfərd / (AmE), / ˈsæd.əl ˈɒk.sfəd / (BrE)
a type of low-heeled Oxford shoe with a distinctive contrasting band, known as a saddle, across the instep
Oxford shoe with contrasting saddle band

Examples

She wore her favorite saddle oxford shoes to the dance.
The school's uniform policy requires black and white saddle oxford footwear.
Vintage saddle oxford styles have made a comeback in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxford - a type of lace-up shoe with a low heel
