miniature schnauzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniətʃɚ ˈʃnaʊzɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪnətʃə ˈʃnaʊzə / (BrE)
a small breed of schnauzer dog, known for its wiry coat and bushy eyebrows and beard
a small breed of schnauzer dog

Examples

The miniature schnauzer is known for its intelligence and strong personality.
Her family adopted a playful miniature schnauzer from the local animal shelter.
At the dog show, the miniature schnauzer impressed the judges with its neat grooming and lively gait.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: schnauzer - a breed of dog with a distinctive beard and eyebrows

Etymology

From German *Schnauzer* (snouter, moustached) and English *miniature*.
