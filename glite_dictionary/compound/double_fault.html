double fault

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl fɔlt / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl fɔːlt / (BrE)
in tennis, the act of making two successive serving faults, resulting in the loss of the point
two serving faults in tennis

Examples

She lost the game after committing a double fault on the final point.
His second serve was just outside the line, resulting in a double fault.
It's frustrating to lose a critical point because of a double fault.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fault - an unsuccessful attempt to serve the ball in tennis
