hotep conference

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛp ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛp ˈkɒn.fər.əns / (BrE)
A conference that focuses on Afrocentric themes, often relating to Black identity, heritage, and culture.
Afrocentric-themed conference

Examples

She attended a hotep conference to connect with like-minded individuals in the Black community.
The annual hotep conference featured workshops on African history and spirituality.
Scholars gathered at the hotep conference to discuss Afrocentric approaches to education.

Synset Examples

She attended a hotep conference to learn more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Afrocentrism - An approach to history and culture focused on African perspectives

Etymology

Formed from 'hotep,' an ancient Egyptian word meaning 'to be at peace,' used in Afrocentric contexts, plus 'conference.'
