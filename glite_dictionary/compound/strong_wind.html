strong wind

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ wɪnd / (AmE), / strɒŋ wɪnd / (BrE)
a wind that blows with great force or intensity
a wind of high force or intensity

Examples

During the storm, the trees bent dangerously under the strong wind.
Sailing can be challenging in strong wind conditions.
The weather forecast warned of strong wind throughout the afternoon.

Synset Examples

The skyscraper swayed gently in the strong winds.
The monsoon season brings heavy rains and strong winds.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gale - a very strong wind
  • Degree Lower: breeze - a light and gentle wind
