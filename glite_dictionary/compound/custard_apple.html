custard apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstɚd ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd ˌæpəl / (BrE)
a tropical fruit with sweet, soft, edible pulp, produced by trees of the genus Annona
sweet tropical fruit of Annona tree

Examples

The farmer showed us how to eat a ripe custard apple straight from the tree.
I bought some delicious custard apples at the local market.
The creamy texture of custard apple makes it popular in desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherimoya - a similar tropical fruit from Annona cherimola, sometimes called custard apple

2. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstərd ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd ˌæpəl / (BrE)
any of several tropical American trees of the genus Annona, or their soft, edible fruit with sweet, creamy pulp
tropical fruit tree; sweet edible fruit

Examples

The custard apple is prized for its sweet and creamy flesh.
Farmers in the region export large quantities of custard apples every year.
She made a delicious smoothie using fresh custard apple pulp.
