traditional dance

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənl dæns / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl dɑːns / (BrE)
a dance that is characteristic of and passed down within a particular cultural or ethnic group
dance belonging to a cultural tradition

Examples

At the festival, the performers wore vibrant costumes for the traditional dance.
Each region in the country has its own unique traditional dance that tells a story.
The children learned a traditional dance from their grandparents to celebrate the holiday.

Synset Examples

They performed a traditional dance inspired by Sambo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk dance - a traditional dance, often from rural or community origins
