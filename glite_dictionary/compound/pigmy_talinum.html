pigmy talinum

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡmi təˈlaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi təˈlaɪnəm / (BrE)
A low-growing plant native to the southwestern United States, with dense narrow succulent leaves and fairly large deep pink flowers that grow close to the ground.
southwestern US plant with pink ground-level flowers

Examples

The garden featured the delicate pigmy talinum nestled among the rocks.
After the rains, clusters of pigmy talinum appeared, dotting the landscape with pink blooms.
We identified the succulent leaves as belonging to a pigmy talinum during our desert hike.
