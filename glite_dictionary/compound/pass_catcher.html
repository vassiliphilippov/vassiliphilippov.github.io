pass catcher

1. NOUN

/ pæs ˈkætʃər / (AmE), / pɑːs ˈkætʃə / (BrE)
a football player who catches or is intended to catch a forward pass, such as a wide receiver or tight end
football player who catches passes

Examples

The team's top pass catcher finished the season with over 1,200 receiving yards.
Tight ends are often valued not only for blocking but also as reliable pass catchers.
The coach praised the rookie pass catcher for his agility and hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receiver - a player who catches passes in football
