kindness of strangers

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaɪndnəs əv ˈstreɪn.dʒɚz / (AmE), / ˈkaɪndnəs əv ˈstreɪn.dʒəz / (BrE)
help or compassion received from people who are not known to you
help from unknown people

Examples

She survived her journey across the country thanks to the kindness of strangers.
In difficult times, the kindness of strangers can make all the difference.
He was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers when he lost his wallet.

Synset Examples

The orphan found solace in the kindness of strangers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
