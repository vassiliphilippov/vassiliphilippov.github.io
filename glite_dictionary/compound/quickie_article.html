quickie article

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪki ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki ˈɑːtɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a short and informal article, often produced quickly and with minimal research or depth
brief, informal article
informal

Examples

She submitted a quickie article to the local newspaper before the deadline.
Many online magazines rely on quickie articles to keep their content fresh.
He dashed off a quickie article about the event, knowing it wasn't his best work.

Synset Examples

He wrote a quickie article for the blog.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: feature article - a detailed and in-depth article
  • Antonym: report - a neutral, formal written account

Etymology

From 'quickie' (something done rapidly) + 'article'.
