Black Muslim

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk ˈmʌzləm / (AmE), / ˌblak ˈmʊzlɪm / (BrE)
a member of the Nation of Islam, a religious and cultural movement among African Americans in the United States
member of the Nation of Islam
american

Examples

Malcolm X was a prominent Black Muslim and a key figure in the civil rights movement.
The Black Muslim community has played a significant role in shaping African American culture and history.
He converted to Islam and became a Black Muslim after being inspired by their teachings.
