back saw

1. NOUN

/ bæk sɔ / (AmE), / bæk sɔː / (BrE)
A handsaw with a reinforced, stiffened upper edge, typically used for precise woodworking cuts.
handsaw with reinforced upper edge

Examples

A back saw is ideal for making precise cuts in woodworking projects.
He used a back saw to carefully trim the molding for a perfect fit.
The carpenter recommended a back saw because of its sturdy reinforced spine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenon saw - a type of back saw used in woodworking for cutting tenons
