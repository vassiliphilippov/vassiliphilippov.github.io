Republic of Iceland

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈaɪslənd / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈaɪslənd / (BrE)
the official name for Iceland, an island country in the North Atlantic that became independent from Denmark in 1944
formal name for the country of Iceland

Examples

The Republic of Iceland is known for its dramatic landscapes and geothermal activity.
In 1944, the Republic of Iceland declared full independence from Denmark.
The president of the Republic of Iceland addressed the nation in a televised speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iceland - an island country in the North Atlantic; also known as the Republic of Iceland
