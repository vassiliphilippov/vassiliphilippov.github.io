starter motor

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑːr.tɚ ˌmoʊ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɑː.tə ˌməʊ.tə / (BrE)
an electric motor that is used to start an internal combustion engine, especially in vehicles
electric motor used to start an engine

Examples

The mechanic said the starter motor needed to be replaced due to wear.
When I turned the key, I only heard a clicking noise from the starter motor.
Modern cars often use a compact starter motor to ignite the engine quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starter - device for starting an engine or system
