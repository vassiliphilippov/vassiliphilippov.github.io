Empire State of the South

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛmˌpaɪɚ steɪt əv ðə saʊθ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpaɪə steɪt əv ðə saʊθ / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Georgia, especially in reference to its economic and cultural prominence in the southeastern United States
nickname for the U.S. state of Georgia

Examples

Atlanta, located in the Empire State of the South, is a major business and cultural hub.
Georgia is often called the Empire State of the South due to its historical significance and growth.
Many tourists visit the Empire State of the South for its diverse landscapes and rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Georgia - a state in the southeastern United States, known as the 'Empire State of the South'

Etymology

Modelled after the nickname 'Empire State' for New York, denoting Georgia's ambition and economic status in the South.
