avoirdupois unit

1. NOUN

/ ˌævɚdəˈpwɑɪz ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˌævədəˈpɔɪz ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
any of the units of weight used in the avoirdupois system, such as the pound or ounce, chiefly for measuring mass in the imperial and US customary systems.
unit of weight in the avoirdupois system

Examples

The pound is the most commonly used avoirdupois unit in the United States.
Grain is the smallest avoirdupois unit still in use in some measurements.
She learned to convert between metric grams and each avoirdupois unit during her science class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pound - unit of weight in the avoirdupois system
  • Synonym: ounce - smaller unit of weight in the avoirdupois system

Etymology

From Middle French 'avoir de pois' (goods of weight), originally referring to goods sold by weight.
