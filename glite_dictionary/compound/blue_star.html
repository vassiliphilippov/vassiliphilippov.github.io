blue star

1. NOUN

/ bluː stɑr / (AmE), / bluː stɑː / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with clusters of blue, star-shaped flowers, commonly found in southeastern United States.
plant with blue, star-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming blue star during the spring.
She chose blue star for its attractive blue flowers and easy maintenance.
Native to the Southeast, blue star adds color and texture to perennial borders.
