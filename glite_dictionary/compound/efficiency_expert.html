efficiency expert

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfɪʃənsi ˌɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ɪˈfɪʃənsi ˌɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to analyze business operations and suggest ways to increase productivity and reduce waste
specialist in improving business efficiency

Examples

The company hired an efficiency expert to streamline its manufacturing process.
After a thorough review, the efficiency expert recommended several workflow changes.
Many businesses rely on an efficiency expert to identify areas where costs can be reduced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultant - a person who provides expert advice professionally
