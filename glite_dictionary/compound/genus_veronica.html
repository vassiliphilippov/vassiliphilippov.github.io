genus Veronica

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs vəˈrɒn.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs vɛˈrɒn.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering herbs, commonly known as speedwells, with pink, white, blue, or purple flowers
a genus of flowering herbs (speedwell)

Examples

The genus Veronica contains hundreds of species known for their colorful flowers.
Gardeners often plant members of the genus Veronica for their attractive blooms and ground cover abilities.
Recent studies have reclassified several species within the genus Veronica based on genetic evidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedwell - common name for plants of the genus Veronica

Etymology

From Latin 'Veronica', the name of the plant genus.
