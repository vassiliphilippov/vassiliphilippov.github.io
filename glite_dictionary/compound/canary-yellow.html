canary-yellow

1. ADJ

/ kəˈnɛri ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / kəˈneəri ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
Of a light to moderate yellow color, resembling that of a canary bird.
light yellow shade, like a canary

Examples

She wore a canary-yellow dress to the wedding, which immediately caught everyone's eye.
The kitchen walls were painted a cheerful canary-yellow to brighten up the space.
He bought a canary-yellow bike so he would be easily seen by motorists.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - a bright color between green and orange on the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: lemon-yellow - a bright, pale yellow shade like that of a lemon
  • Degree Higher: golden-yellow - a deeper or more intense shade of yellow, like gold

Etymology

Named after the canary bird, known for its vibrant yellow plumage.
