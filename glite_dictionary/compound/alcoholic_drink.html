alcoholic drink

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.kəˈhɑ.lɪk ˈdrɪŋk / (AmE), / ˌæl.kəˈhɒ.lɪk ˈdrɪŋk / (BrE)
A drink that contains alcohol, such as beer, wine, or spirits.
drink containing alcohol

Examples

Wine is a popular alcoholic drink often served with dinner.
She ordered a soft drink because she does not consume alcoholic drinks.
Beer is the most widely consumed alcoholic drink in many countries.

Synset Examples

alcohol (or drink) ruined him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liquor - strong alcoholic drink
  • Antonym: non-alcoholic drink - a drink that does not contain alcohol
