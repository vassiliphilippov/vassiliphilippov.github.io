greyish-blue

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
A shade of blue that has a noticeable hint of grey.
bluish color with grey tint

Examples

The sky at dusk often turns a beautiful greyish-blue shade.
She chose a greyish-blue paint for her bedroom to create a calming atmosphere.
His eyes were an unusual greyish-blue, making them stand out in a crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluish-grey - a shade of grey with a hint of blue
