hairy lip fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛri lɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈheəri lɪp fɜːn / (BrE)
a small North American evergreen fern with densely woolly stipes and lower frond surfaces
a small North American evergreen fern

Examples

The hairy lip fern thrives in rocky, shaded habitats across the eastern United States.
Botanists studied the woolly texture of the hairy lip fern's fronds under a microscope.
I spotted a cluster of hairy lip ferns growing along the trail during my hike.
