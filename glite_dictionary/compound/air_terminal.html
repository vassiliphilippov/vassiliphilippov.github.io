air terminal

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˈtɝː.mɪ.nəl / (AmE), / eə ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəl / (BrE)
A building or facility at an airport where passengers or air cargo are processed before departure or after arrival.
airport building for passengers or cargo

Examples

The new air terminal can handle over ten million passengers a year.
We arrived early at the air terminal to check in our luggage.
The city's air terminal was recently upgraded with better amenities for travelers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airport terminal - building at an airport for ticketing, baggage, and boarding
