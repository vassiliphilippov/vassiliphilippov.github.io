young person

1. NOUN

/ jʌŋ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / jʌŋ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a child or adolescent; someone who is not yet an adult
child or adolescent

Examples

A young person should always feel safe at home and in school.
The club provides activities and support for any young person in the community.
It's often difficult for a young person to find their place in the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child - a young human being below the age of puberty or below the legal age of majority
  • Antonym: adult - a fully grown or developed person
  • Degree Higher: teenager - a person aged between 13 and 19 years
