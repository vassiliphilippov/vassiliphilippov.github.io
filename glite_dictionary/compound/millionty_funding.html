millionty funding

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪl.jən.ti ˈfʌn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.jən.ti ˈfʌn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
An informal, exaggerated term for a very large amount of funding, typically an unspecified but huge sum.
very large amount of funding (informal, exaggerated)
informal

Examples

The small company surprised everyone by announcing a millionty funding round last quarter.
After months of pitches, the app finally landed millionty funding to expand globally.
She joked that unless they received millionty funding, the project would never be finished.

Synset Examples

The startup secured a millionty funding from investors.

Etymology

Formed by combining 'million' with the mock-numeral suffix '-ty', humorously implying an enormous sum.
