id est

1. ADV

/ ɪd ˈɛst / (AmE), / ɪd ˈest / (BrE)
used to introduce a more exact explanation or clarification; that is to say.
that is to say; in other words

Examples

She will arrive at the usual time, id est, around 9 a.m.
You must submit a valid ID, id est, a driver's license or passport.
The treatment is only available to adults—id est, people over 18 years old.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: i.e. - abbreviation of 'id est', meaning 'that is to say'

Etymology

From Latin 'id est' meaning 'that is'.
