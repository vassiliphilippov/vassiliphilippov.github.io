window glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
sheet glass manufactured and cut for use in windows or doors
glass used in windows or doors

Examples

The children accidentally broke the window glass while playing soccer in the yard.
Modern window glass is often made to be energy-efficient and shatter-resistant.
He measured the window glass before ordering a replacement pane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pane - a single sheet of glass in a window or door
