mirror carp

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrər kɑrp / (AmE), / ˈmɪrə kɑːp / (BrE)
a variety of domestic carp with a few large, shiny scales, bred for ornamental or food purposes
variety of domestic carp with large shiny scales

Examples

The pond was stocked with several impressive mirror carp that glistened in the sunlight.
Anglers prize the mirror carp for its distinct appearance and fighting spirit.
As a child, she loved to watch the mirror carp swimming just beneath the water's surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carp - a large freshwater fish, commonly kept for food or ornamental purposes
