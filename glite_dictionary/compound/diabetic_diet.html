diabetic diet

1. NOUN

/ ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
A diet specially designed to help control blood sugar levels and manage the symptoms of diabetes.
diet for managing diabetes

Examples

After being diagnosed with diabetes, Mark started following a diabetic diet recommended by his doctor.
A diabetic diet often limits sugar and refined carbohydrates to help control blood glucose levels.
The hospital provides patients with a special diabetic diet to meet their nutritional needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diet - a regulated selection of foods
