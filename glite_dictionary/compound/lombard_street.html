Lombard Street

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɑːmbɑrd ˌstriːt / (AmE), / ˈlɒmbɑːd ˌstriːt / (BrE)
a street in central London traditionally known as the centre of London's banking industry
street in London, historical banking centre
british

Examples

Many of the oldest banks in London have their offices on Lombard Street.
Lombard Street has long been regarded as the heart of the British banking industry.
He worked as a clerk for a major firm based in Lombard Street during the 19th century.

Etymology

Named after the Lombard bankers from northern Italy who settled there in the Middle Ages.
