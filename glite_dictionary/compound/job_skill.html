job skill

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb skɪl / (AmE), / dʒɒb skɪl / (BrE)
a skill or ability required to perform a particular job or be employable
a skill needed for employment

Examples

Communication is an important job skill that employers look for in candidates.
Learning new job skills can help you advance your career.
The training program focuses on developing job skills for young adults.

Synset Examples

Reeducation can be crucial for improving job skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job - a paid position of regular employment
  • Synonym: skill - the ability to do something well; expertise
