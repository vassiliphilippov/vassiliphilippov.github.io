potato pancake

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈpænkeɪk / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈpænkeɪk / (BrE)
a round, flat cake made from grated potato mixed with egg and a little flour, fried until crisp
fried cake of grated potato and egg

Examples

She made delicious potato pancakes for breakfast using leftover potatoes.
Traditional potato pancakes are often served with applesauce or sour cream.
At the festival, visitors lined up to try the famous potato pancake recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latke - a traditional Jewish potato pancake
  • Synonym: hash brown - a fried patty of grated or shredded potato
