spit curl

1. NOUN

/ spɪt kɜrl / (AmE), / spɪt kɜːl / (BrE)
A small spiral curl of hair stuck flat to the forehead or cheek, typically using saliva or styling product.
small curl plastered to forehead or cheek

Examples

The actress wore a spit curl on her cheek as part of her vintage hairstyle.
In old movies, leading ladies often had a single spit curl on their forehead.
She carefully styled a spit curl to complete her retro look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiss curl - a lock of hair shaped to lie flat against the face, especially on the forehead or cheek

Etymology

From 'spit' (saliva), referring to the method originally used to stick the curl.
