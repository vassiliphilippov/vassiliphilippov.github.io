speech balloon

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ bəˈluːn / (AmE), / spiːtʃ bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a graphic element in cartoons or comics, usually a rounded outline, that contains the words spoken by a character
cartoon text bubble

Examples

The artist added a speech balloon to show what the character was saying.
In many comics, a speech balloon indicates dialogue or thoughts.
Kids had fun creating their own speech balloons in the comic workshop.

Synset Examples

Speech bubbles are used not only to include a character's words, but also emotions, voice inflections and unspecified language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speech bubble - a rounded shape containing text that represents speech, especially in comics
