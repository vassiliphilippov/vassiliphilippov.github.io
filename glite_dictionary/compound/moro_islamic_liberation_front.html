Moro Islamic Liberation Front

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɔːroʊ ɪˈslæmɪk ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən frʌnt / (AmE), / ˈmɔːrəʊ ɪˈslæmɪk ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃ(ə)n frʌnt / (BrE)
a militant group formed in 1977 in the southern Philippines seeking to establish an independent Islamic state for the Moro people
militant group in the southern Philippines

Examples

The Moro Islamic Liberation Front has been involved in negotiations with the Philippine government for decades.
News reports detailed the latest clashes between the army and the Moro Islamic Liberation Front.
The peace accord was hailed as a new chapter for the Moro Islamic Liberation Front and the people of Mindanao.
