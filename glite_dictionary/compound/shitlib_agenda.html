shitlib agenda

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪt.lɪb əˈdʒɛndə / (AmE), / ˈʃɪt.lɪb əˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
A derogatory slang term for a progressive or liberal political agenda, viewed with contempt by the speaker.
slang insult for progressive agenda
slang offensive

Examples

Some commentators blame the shitlib agenda for rising polarization in society.
Online forums often use the phrase shitlib agenda when discussing progressive reforms.
He accused the media of pushing a shitlib agenda against traditional values.

Synset Examples

The shitlib agenda is criticized by many conservatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liberal agenda - progressive social or political policies

Etymology

Formed from 'shitlib' (slang insult for 'liberal') and 'agenda'.
