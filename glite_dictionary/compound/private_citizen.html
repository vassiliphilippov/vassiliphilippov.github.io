private citizen

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪvət ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
an ordinary person who does not hold any official or public position in government or authority
ordinary person not in public office

Examples

As a private citizen, she was not involved in any government decision-making.
After retiring from politics, he returned to life as a private citizen.
The mayor met with several private citizens to discuss the new park proposal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: public official - a person holding a position of authority in government or public office
