Aries the Ram

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛəriˌiz ðə ræm / (AmE), / ˈɛːriːz ðə ram / (BrE)
the first sign of the zodiac, represented by a ram; the sun enters this sign at the vernal equinox, approximately March 21 to April 19
first sign of the zodiac (ram)

Examples

People born under Aries the Ram are said to be energetic and adventurous.
March 21 marks the beginning of Aries the Ram in the zodiac calendar.
Astrologers believe Aries the Ram influences determination and leadership traits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aries - the first sign of the zodiac; symbolized by a ram
  • Degree Higher: Taurus the Bull - the second sign of the zodiac, following Aries
  • Degree Lower: Pisces the Fishes - the sign of the zodiac preceding Aries

Etymology

From Latin 'Aries' meaning 'ram', used in Roman astrology.
