roguish smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊɡɪʃ smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊɡɪʃ smaɪl / (BrE)
a smile that is attractive and shows playful or mischievous intentions
charming, mischievous smile

Examples

He gave her a roguish smile before revealing the surprise.
With a roguish smile, he admitted he had hidden the last slice of cake.
The actor's roguish smile made the character instantly endearing to the audience.

Synset Examples

His roguish smile won her over instantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grin - a broad smile
  • Synonym: smirk - a smile expressing smugness or self-satisfaction
