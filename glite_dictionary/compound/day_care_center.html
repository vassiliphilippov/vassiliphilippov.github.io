day care center

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌkɛr ˌsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌkeə ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
A place where young children are looked after during the day while their parents are at work.
facility for caring for children while parents work

Examples

After dropping his daughter off at the day care center, Mark headed straight to work.
The city is planning to open a new day care center near the shopping district.
Many parents rely on the day care center during school holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursery school - a school for young children, usually before they begin elementary school
  • Synonym: childcare - the care of children, especially while parents are at work
