every little thing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvri ˈlɪtəl θɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvri ˈlɪtl̩ θɪŋ / (BrE)
every detail or aspect of a person's life or a situation.
all aspects or details

Examples

Whenever we meet, he asks me about every little thing going on in my life.
She pays attention to every little thing when planning a party.
Please don't stress about every little thing; focus on what's really important.

Synset Examples

He greeted me warmly and said, 'How's every little thing with you?'
During our chat, she smiled and asked, 'How's every little thing in your life?'
When I saw her, I asked, 'So, how's every little thing going?'
