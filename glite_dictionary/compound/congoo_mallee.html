congoo mallee

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑŋɡu ˈmæli / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡu ˈmæli / (BrE)
A small, shrubby variety of mallee eucalyptus plant native to Australia.
small shrubby mallee eucalyptus

Examples

The congoo mallee thrives in the arid inland regions of Australia.
As part of the conservation effort, several congoo mallee specimens were replanted.
Botanists have studied the congoo mallee for its unique adaptations to dry climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallee - A type of Australian eucalyptus with multiple stems from ground level
