act of fornication

1. NOUN

/ ˈækt əv ˌfɔrnɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈækt əv ˌfɔːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of sexual intercourse between two people who are not married to each other
sex outside of marriage

Examples

The community elders harshly judged any act of fornication within their village.
He was dismissed from his position after his act of fornication became public knowledge.
In the past, engaging in an act of fornication was considered a serious social offense.

Synset Examples

They were condemned for their act of fornication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fornication - sexual intercourse between people not married to each other

Etymology

From Latin 'fornicatio' (illicit sexual intercourse), from 'fornicari' (to have illicit intercourse).
