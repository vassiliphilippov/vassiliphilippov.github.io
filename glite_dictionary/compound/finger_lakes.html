Finger Lakes

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɪŋɡər leɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə leɪks / (BrE)
a region in central New York State known for its series of long, narrow, glacial lakes aligned north-south
region of narrow lakes in New York State

Examples

The Finger Lakes region is famous for its stunning fall foliage and vineyards.
Many tourists travel to the Finger Lakes each year to enjoy boating and hiking.
She grew up near the Finger Lakes, surrounded by picturesque scenery.

Etymology

Named for the lakes' resemblance to fingers when viewed on a map.
