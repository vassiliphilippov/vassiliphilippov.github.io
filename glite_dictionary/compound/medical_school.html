medical school

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl skuːl / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl skuːl / (BrE)
a graduate school where students are trained to become doctors and obtain a medical degree
school for training doctors

Examples

After graduating from college, she decided to apply to medical school.
It takes several years to complete medical school and become a licensed physician.
He dreams of attending a top medical school in the United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: law school - a graduate school for legal education leading to a law degree
  • Synonym: medical college - an institution for training in medicine; sometimes synonymous
