temporal artery

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpərəl ˈɑrtəri / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
any of several arteries located on either side of the head, supplying blood to the temporal region of the brain and scalp
artery supplying temporal region
medical

Examples

The doctor palpated the temporal artery to check for signs of inflammation.
Headaches in the temple region can sometimes be linked to issues with the temporal artery.
The temporal artery biopsy confirmed the diagnosis of giant cell arteritis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artery - a blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart

Etymology

From Latin 'temporalis' (of the temples) + 'arteria' (artery).
