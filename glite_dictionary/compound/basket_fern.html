basket fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskət fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern cultivated for its finely divided greyish-green leaves, native to the West Indies and Central and South America
a tropical decorative fern species

Examples

The basket fern adds a touch of elegance to shaded patios and indoor spaces.
Gardeners often admire the delicate fronds of the basket fern in tropical collections.
She carefully misted her basket fern to keep its foliage lush and healthy.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪt fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt fɜːn / (BrE)
a large tropical fern that grows on trees or rocks, native to Asia, Polynesia, and Australia, known for its basket-shaped fronds
large tropical fern with basket-shaped fronds

Examples

The basket fern clung high on the tree, its leaves forming a green tangle.
Many gardeners value the basket fern for its unique appearance in shaded areas.
In Australia, you can find the basket fern thriving on both rocks and branches.
