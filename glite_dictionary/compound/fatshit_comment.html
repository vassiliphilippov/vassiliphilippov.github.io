fatshit comment

1. NOUN

/ ˈfætˌʃɪt ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈfætʃɪt ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
An absurd or ridiculously nonsensical remark.
ridiculously nonsensical remark
informal slang vulgar

I can't believe you said such a fatshit comment during the meeting.
