corn exchange

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / kɔːn ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a marketplace or building where grain, particularly corn, is bought and sold, especially for commercial purposes
marketplace for grain trading
british

Examples

The old corn exchange has been converted into a concert venue.
Merchants gathered at the corn exchange to negotiate deals for the coming season.
The city’s bustling corn exchange reflected the importance of agriculture to the local economy.
