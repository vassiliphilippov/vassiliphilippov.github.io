tube wrench

1. NOUN

/ tuːb rɛntʃ / (AmE), / tjuːb rɛntʃ / (BrE)
an adjustable wrench with two serrated jaws, used for gripping and turning pipes
adjustable wrench for pipes

Examples

He used a tube wrench to tighten the leaky pipe under the sink.
Make sure the tube wrench is adjusted properly so it grips the pipe securely.
The plumber reached for his tube wrench to remove the old section of piping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe wrench - an adjustable wrench for gripping and turning pipes
