maintenance staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪntənəns stæf / (AmE), / ˈmeɪntənəns stɑːf / (BrE)
the group of employees in a business or organization responsible for maintaining and repairing buildings, equipment, or facilities
group responsible for maintenance work

Examples

The maintenance staff quickly repaired the broken air conditioning system in the office.
Our hotel relies on a dedicated maintenance staff to keep the facilities in top condition.
Every morning, the maintenance staff checks all equipment to ensure everything is working properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: janitor - a person employed to clean and maintain a building
