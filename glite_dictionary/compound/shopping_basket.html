shopping basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ ˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a small basket with a handle, used for carrying goods while shopping, especially in a store or supermarket
basket for carrying purchased items in a store

Examples

She put several apples and a loaf of bread into her shopping basket.
Please leave your shopping basket at the entrance when you are finished shopping.
He realized he had forgotten his wallet only after filling his shopping basket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping cart - a wheeled cart for transporting goods in a supermarket
