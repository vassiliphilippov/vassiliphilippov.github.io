language lesson

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ ˌlɛsən / (BrE)
a period of instruction during which a language is taught or learned
session for learning a language

Examples

She attends a weekly language lesson to improve her Spanish skills.
The language lesson focused on basic conversational phrases.
Many students find their first language lesson both exciting and challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesson - a period of instruction in general
