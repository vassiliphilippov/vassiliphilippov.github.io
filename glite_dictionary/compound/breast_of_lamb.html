breast of lamb

1. NOUN

/ brɛst əv læm / (AmE), / brɛst əv læm / (BrE)
a cut of lamb containing the breastbone and surrounding muscles, often prepared as a roast or used in stews
cut of lamb including the breastbone

Examples

The chef recommended slow-cooking the breast of lamb for maximum tenderness.
She bought a breast of lamb to make a hearty stew for dinner.
A roasted breast of lamb pairs well with rosemary and garlic.
