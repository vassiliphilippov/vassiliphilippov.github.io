blended whiskey

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (BrE)
whiskey made by mixing two or more different whiskeys, or by combining whiskey with neutral spirits
whiskey made from a blend of whiskeys or neutral spirits

Examples

He ordered a glass of blended whiskey while waiting for his meal.
Some people prefer blended whiskey because of its smoother flavor.
This brand is known for producing high-quality blended whiskey.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single malt - whiskey made from malted barley in one distillery
