ball hawk

1. NOUN

/ bɔl hɑk / (AmE), / bɔːl hɔːk / (BrE)
A player, especially in team sports, who is particularly skilled at intercepting, stealing, or catching the ball.
athlete skilled at intercepting or stealing the ball

Examples

The team's best ball hawk intercepted two passes in the final quarter.
She became a legendary ball hawk for her quick reflexes on the basketball court.
Coaches value a good ball hawk because they can quickly turn defense into offense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interceptor - a player who frequently intercepts the ball
