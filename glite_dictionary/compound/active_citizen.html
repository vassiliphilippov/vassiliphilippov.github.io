active citizen

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪv ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / ˈæktɪv ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
a citizen who participates actively in community affairs, such as volunteering, community organizing, or initiatives like crime prevention and neighborhood watch
a citizen who takes an active community role

Examples

The government launched a programme to encourage every active citizen to participate in local projects.
As an active citizen, she volunteers at the food bank and organizes neighborhood clean-ups.
Community safety improved after several active citizens joined the neighborhood watch initiative.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: citizen - a legally recognized subject or national of a state
