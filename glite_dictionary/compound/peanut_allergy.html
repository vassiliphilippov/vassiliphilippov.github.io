peanut allergy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːˌnʌt ˈælərdʒi / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ˈælədʒi / (BrE)
A medical condition in which exposure to peanuts causes an allergic reaction.
allergic reaction to peanuts
medical

Examples

He carries an epinephrine injector because of his peanut allergy.
Schools often ban peanuts to protect students with a peanut allergy.
Her peanut allergy means she must check food labels very carefully.

Synset Examples

I have a peanut allergy that I must avoid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allergy - an abnormal reaction by the immune system to a substance
