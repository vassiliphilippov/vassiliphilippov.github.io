fruity drink

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːti drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈfruːti drɪŋk / (BrE)
a sweet beverage with a pronounced fruit flavor, often non-alcoholic and enjoyed especially by children or at parties
sweet beverage with fruit flavor

Examples

At the picnic, Anna enjoyed a cold fruity drink with her sandwich.
The café offers a signature fruity drink made from fresh berries and oranges.
Many children prefer a fruity drink over soda because of its sweet taste.

Synset Examples

They all loved the fruity dinosaur juice at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft drink - a non-alcoholic drink, usually carbonated
  • Degree Higher: cocktail - an alcoholic mixed drink, often sweet and fruity
