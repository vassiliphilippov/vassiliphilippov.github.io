grab and go

1. ADJ

/ ɡræb ənd ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡrab ənd ɡəʊ / (BrE)
designed to be purchased and taken away for immediate consumption, typically referring to food or drinks that are convenient for people in a hurry
food or drinks for quick consumption

Examples

The café's grab and go section makes breakfast during rush hour much easier.
Many commuters rely on grab and go lunches from local shops.
Our store now offers a wide selection of grab and go snacks for busy customers.

Synset Examples

The store offers a variety of grab and go meals.
I love the grab and go breakfast options at that café.
