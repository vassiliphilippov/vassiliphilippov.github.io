corn cake

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn keɪk / (AmE), / kɔːn keɪk / (BrE)
A flat cake made from cornmeal, typically baked in a pan or on a griddle; common in Southern and Midland American cuisine.
flat cake made from cornmeal
american

Examples

For breakfast, we served fluffy corn cakes with fresh honey and butter.
She fried each corn cake until it was golden brown on both sides.
The restaurant offers a special Southern-style corn cake topped with salsa and cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - a bread made from cornmeal, common in the southern United States
