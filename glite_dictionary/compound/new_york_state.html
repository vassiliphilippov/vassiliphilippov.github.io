New York State

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈjɔrk steɪt / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk steɪt / (BrE)
a state in the northeastern United States; one of the original 13 colonies, with Albany as its capital
U.S. state in the northeast; capital: Albany

Examples

She grew up in New York State before moving to California for college.
Winters in New York State can be long and snowy, especially in the northern regions.
The governor of New York State announced new policies to address climate change.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: New York - the city in southeastern New York State, the largest in the U.S.

Etymology

Named after the Duke of York (later King James II of England) when the English captured New Netherland in 1664.
