broad-leaved twayblade

1. NOUN

/ brɔd livd ˈtweɪˌbleɪd / (AmE), / brɔːd liːvd ˈtweɪbleɪd / (BrE)
a small orchid of western North America with two elliptic leaves and a slender cluster of small green flowers
a North American wild orchid with broad leaves

Examples

While hiking in the forest, we spotted a broad-leaved twayblade blooming by the stream.
The broad-leaved twayblade is known for its pair of wide, flat leaves and delicate green flowers.
Conservationists are monitoring the populations of broad-leaved twayblades in their natural habitat.
