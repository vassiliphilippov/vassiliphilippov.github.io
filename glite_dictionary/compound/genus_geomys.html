genus Geomys

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈdʒiːəˌmaɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈdʒiːəˌmɪs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Geomyidae, comprising various species of North American pocket gophers.
type genus of gophers (Geomys)

Examples

The genus Geomys includes several species of pocket gophers found in North America.
Researchers observed a population shift among members of the genus Geomys due to habitat loss.
Fossil evidence suggests that the genus Geomys has existed for millions of years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Geomys - any species within the genus Geomys, a type of pocket gopher

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Geomys', used in scientific classification.
