Day of Judgment

1. NOUN

/ deɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / deɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the day at the end of the world when God will judge every person according to their deeds.
final day when God judges all people

Examples

Many religious texts describe the Day of Judgment as a time of final reckoning for humanity.
He feared for his soul and often prayed about the coming Day of Judgment.
Stories of the Day of Judgment have shaped the beliefs of countless generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Judgment Day - Another term for the Day of Judgment, especially in Christian contexts
