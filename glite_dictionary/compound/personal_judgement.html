personal judgement

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənəl dʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl dʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
a legal decision ordering an individual or corporation to pay money damages
legal judgment for payment of money
legal

Examples

The court issued a personal judgement against the defendant for $10,000 in damages.
A personal judgement can affect an individual's credit rating and asset ownership.
After losing the lawsuit, the company faced a hefty personal judgement from the jury.
