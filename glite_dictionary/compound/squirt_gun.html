squirt gun

1. NOUN

/ skwɝːt ɡʌn / (AmE), / skwɜːt ɡʌn / (BrE)
a toy resembling a pistol that shoots a jet of water
toy pistol that squirts water

Examples

The children spent the afternoon chasing each other with squirt guns in the backyard.
He brought his favorite squirt gun to the pool party and challenged his friends to a water fight.
During the summer festival, vendors sold colorful squirt guns to help everyone keep cool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water pistol - a toy gun that shoots water
