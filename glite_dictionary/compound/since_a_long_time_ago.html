since a long time ago

1. ADV

/ sɪns ə lɔːŋ taɪm əˈgoʊ / (AmE), / sɪns ə lɒŋ taɪm əˈgəʊ / (BrE)
from or since a far distant time in the past until now.
from long ago until now

Examples

We have lived in this house since a long time ago.
She has collected rare coins since a long time ago.
This tradition has existed since a long time ago in our village.

Synset Examples

she knows him from way back

Related Terms

  • Synonym: long ago - in the distant past
  • Antonym: recently - not long ago, in the near past
