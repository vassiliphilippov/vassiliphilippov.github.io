West Indian jasmine

1. NOUN

/ wɛst ˈɪndiən ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / wɛst ˈɪndiən ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
A tall, sparsely branched, conical tree with large, fragrant yellow flowers featuring white centers, native to the West Indies.
tall tree with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the sweet scent of West Indian jasmine in full bloom.
Many landscapers choose West Indian jasmine for its striking yellow flowers and pleasant fragrance.
She planted a row of West Indian jasmine along the pathway to create a colorful border.
