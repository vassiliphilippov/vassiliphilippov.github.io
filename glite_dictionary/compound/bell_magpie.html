bell magpie

1. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈmæɡˌpaɪ / (AmE), / bɛl ˈmaɪɡˌpaɪ / (BrE)
A bluish-black fruit-eating bird, noted for its bell-like call.
a bluish-black bird with a bell-like call

Examples

The bell magpie is often heard in the early morning, its call echoing through the forest.
Several bell magpies perched in the tall trees, searching for ripe fruit.
Birdwatchers come from afar to catch a glimpse of the elusive bell magpie.
