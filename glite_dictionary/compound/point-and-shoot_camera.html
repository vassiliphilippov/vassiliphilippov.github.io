point-and-shoot camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪnt ən ˌʃut ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪnt ən ˌʃuːt ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
A small, lightweight camera with automatic settings, designed for simple, easy photography.
simple automatic camera

Examples

Many travelers prefer a point-and-shoot camera for its portability and simplicity.
I upgraded from a point-and-shoot camera to a DSLR for more manual control over my photos.
With a point-and-shoot camera, you can capture moments quickly without worrying about settings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact camera - small, portable camera designed for convenience

Etymology

From the instruction to 'point' the camera and 'shoot' (take the picture), reflecting its simplicity.
