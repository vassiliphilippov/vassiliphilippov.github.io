pay envelope

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ ˌɛn.vəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌɛn.vɪ.ləʊp / (BrE)
an envelope containing an employee's wages, traditionally given out at the end of a pay period
envelope containing wages

Examples

His weekly pay envelope contained cash and a payslip detailing his earnings.
She looked forward to opening her pay envelope every Friday afternoon.
With the rise of electronic banking, the traditional pay envelope has become rare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay packet - envelope containing wages or salary
