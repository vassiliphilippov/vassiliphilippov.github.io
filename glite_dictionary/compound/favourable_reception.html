favourable reception

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərəbəl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbl rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the act of accepting or welcoming something as satisfactory or pleasing
acceptance or positive welcome

Examples

The new policy received a favourable reception from both employees and management.
Her novel met with a favourable reception from the critics.
The proposal was given a favourable reception at the board meeting.

Synset Examples

he bought it on approval

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approval - the belief that something is good or acceptable; consent
