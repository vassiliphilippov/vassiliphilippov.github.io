to keep the peace

1. VERB

/ kip ðə pis / (AmE), / kiːp ðə piːs / (BrE)
to maintain calm and prevent conflict or disorder in a group or area
maintain calm and prevent conflict

Examples

The police were called in to keep the peace after the argument escalated.
She often acts as a mediator to keep the peace between her siblings.
It can be difficult to keep the peace in a tense work environment.

Synset Examples

It's important to keep the peace during family gatherings.
They hired a mediator to help keep the peace.
She always tries to keep the peace among her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restore order - to bring a situation back to a state of calm and organization
  • Antonym: cause a disturbance - to create disorder or conflict in a situation
