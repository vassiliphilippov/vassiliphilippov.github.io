top cat

1. NOUN

/ tɑp kæt / (AmE), / tɒp kæt / (BrE)
the most important or influential person in a group or organization
most important person; leader
informal

Examples

After the promotion, he quickly became the top cat in the department.
Everyone listened when the top cat spoke at the staff meeting.
Joan likes being the top cat and makes all the important decisions.

Synset Examples

He's the top cat among the sales team.
As the top cat, she leads all the meetings.
Being the top cat means more responsibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads a group or organization
  • Synonym: boss - a person in charge of a group of workers or an organization
