brood hen

1. NOUN

/ bruːd hɛn / (AmE), / bruːd hɛn / (BrE)
a domestic hen that is ready to brood and incubate eggs
hen ready to incubate eggs

Examples

The farmer placed the fertilized eggs under a brood hen to ensure proper incubation.
Once a brood hen settles into her nest, she rarely leaves until the chicks hatch.
They selected their best brood hen for a new clutch of eggs in the spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broody hen - a hen inclined to incubate eggs
  • Degree Lower: layer - a hen kept for producing eggs
