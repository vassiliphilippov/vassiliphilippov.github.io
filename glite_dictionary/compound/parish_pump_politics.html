parish pump politics

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp pəˈlɪtɪks / (AmE), / ˈparɪʃ pʌmp pəˈlɪtɪks / (BrE)
Political issues and concerns that are local in nature, often regarded as less significant than national or international matters.
local political issues or concerns
