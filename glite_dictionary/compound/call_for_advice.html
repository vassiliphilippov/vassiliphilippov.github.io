to call for advice

1. VERB

/ kɔl fər ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / kɔːl fə ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
to contact someone, typically by phone, in order to seek guidance or help about a particular issue
contact someone to seek guidance

Examples

Before making a final decision, Sarah decided to call for advice from her mentor.
If you're unsure about the new policy, just call for advice at the help desk.
He didn't hesitate to call for advice when the problem got complicated.

Synset Examples

I called Camille for advice on the project.
He called his mother for advice on the decision.
She called her friend Glincel for advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to consult - seek information or advice from someone
