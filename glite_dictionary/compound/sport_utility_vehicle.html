sport utility vehicle

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt juˈtɪlɪti ˈviəkl̩ / (AmE), / spɔːt juːˈtɪlɪti ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a large, powerful vehicle with four-wheel drive, built on a truck chassis, designed for both on- and off-road use
large four-wheel-drive vehicle

Examples

They bought a new sport utility vehicle to accommodate their growing family and outdoor adventures.
Many people prefer a sport utility vehicle for its elevated driving position and extra cargo space.
The rugged terrain was no match for their four-wheel-drive sport utility vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: SUV - abbreviation for sport utility vehicle
  • Degree Lower: crossover - a vehicle combining features of an SUV and a passenger car

2. NOUN

/ spɔrt juˈtɪləti ˈviːɪkl̩ / (AmE), / spɔːt juːˈtɪləti ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a high-performance, four-wheel-drive vehicle designed for both on-road and off-road use, typically built on a truck chassis
four-wheel-drive vehicle for on- and off-road use

Examples

He decided to trade in his sedan for a sport utility vehicle because of its versatility and space.
Many families choose a sport utility vehicle for its ability to handle rough terrain and bad weather.
The new sport utility vehicle model offers advanced safety features and improved fuel efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: SUV - abbreviation for sport utility vehicle

Etymology

From 'sport' and 'utility', combined in the 1970s–80s in American English to describe a versatile, rugged passenger vehicle.
