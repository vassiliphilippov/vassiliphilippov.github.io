art form

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt fɔrm / (AmE), / ɑːt fɔːm / (BrE)
a particular type or style of artistic expression, such as music, painting, literature, or dance
type of artistic expression

Examples

Many consider jazz to be a uniquely American art form.
Digital photography has emerged as a popular art form in recent years.
Poetry is an art form that allows writers to express deep emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genre - a category or style of art, music, or literature
