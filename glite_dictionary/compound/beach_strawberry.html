beach strawberry

1. NOUN

/ biːtʃ ˈstrɔːbəri / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈstrɒbəri / (BrE)
a wild strawberry plant (Fragaria chiloensis) native to the western United States and South America, used as a source for many cultivated strawberry varieties
wild strawberry plant, strawberry ancestor

Examples

The beach strawberry thrives along the coastal dunes of California.
Several cultivated varieties owe their origins to the beach strawberry.
Botanists study the beach strawberry for its resistance to certain plant diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry - a sweet soft red fruit with a seed-studded surface
