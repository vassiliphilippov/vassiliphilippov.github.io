electric toothbrush

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trɪk ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ / (BrE)
a toothbrush powered by an electric motor that moves the brush head automatically
motorized toothbrush

Examples

Dentists often recommend using an electric toothbrush for more effective cleaning.
I switched to an electric toothbrush last year and noticed an improvement in my dental health.
The battery in my electric toothbrush lasts for almost two weeks between charges.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manual toothbrush - a toothbrush operated by hand, without a motor
