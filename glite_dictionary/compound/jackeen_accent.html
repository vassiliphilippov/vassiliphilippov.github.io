jackeen accent

1. NOUN

/ dʒæˈkin ˈæksənt / (AmE), / dʒakˈiːn ˈæksənt / (BrE)
The distinctive accent of English spoken by natives of Dublin, Ireland.
distinctive Dublin, Ireland English accent

Examples

Her jackeen accent gave her away at the pub.
People often recognized his jackeen accent when he traveled outside of Dublin.
The podcast host spoke in a lively jackeen accent throughout the interview.

Synset Examples

Her jackeen accent gave her away at the pub.

Etymology

From 'jackeen', an Irish term for a native of Dublin, blended with 'accent'.
