botty bandit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑti ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒti ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
A humorous or playful term for someone considered silly, mischievous, or behaving as if they 'steal bottoms'; often used in jest, especially by or about children.
playful term for a mischievous or silly person
informal slang

Examples

He wore the mask of the botty bandit for Halloween.
The kids laughed and called their friend a botty bandit after he fell over the couch.
At the party, someone dressed up as a botty bandit and made everyone giggle.

Synset Examples
