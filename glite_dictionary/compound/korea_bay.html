Korea Bay

1. PROPN

/ kəˈriː.ə beɪ / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə beɪ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Yellow Sea located between the Liaodong Peninsula in China and western Korea.
bay between China and western Korea

Examples

Fishing boats are a common sight in Korea Bay during the summer months.
The strategic location of Korea Bay has made it important for both China and Korea.
Environmental scientists are studying water quality in Korea Bay due to industrial growth nearby.
