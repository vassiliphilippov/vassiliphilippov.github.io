cranberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrænˌbɛri lɪˈkɜr / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage made by infusing or flavoring alcohol with cranberries and sweeteners.
alcoholic drink flavored with cranberries

Examples

She served homemade cranberry liqueur as an aperitif at the holiday party.
The recipe calls for a splash of cranberry liqueur to add tartness and color.
Bottles of cranberry liqueur lined the shelves, ready to be gifted to friends.
