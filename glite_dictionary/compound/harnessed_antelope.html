harnessed antelope

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrnəst ˈæntɪˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɑːnɪst ˈæntɪləʊp / (BrE)
any of several antelopes of the genus Tragelaphus, having striped markings resembling a harness
antelope with harness-like stripes

Examples

The harnessed antelope is known for its distinctive white stripes across its back.
Wildlife photographers often seek out the elusive harnessed antelope in African forests.
The presence of a harnessed antelope indicates a healthy woodland ecosystem.
