local dish

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl dɪʃ / (BrE)
a traditional food item that is characteristic of a particular region or area
traditional food of a region

Examples

The city is famous for its unique local dish, which attracts many tourists every year.
Whenever I travel, I like to try at least one local dish in each place I visit.
That restaurant serves the best local dish I've tasted in the region.

Synset Examples

Cai png offers a variety of delicious local dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regional specialty - a food or product unique to a particular region
  • Degree Higher: national dish - a dish strongly associated with a particular nation
