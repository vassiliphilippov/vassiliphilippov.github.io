longhaired cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔŋˌhɛrd kæt / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌheəd kæt / (BrE)
a domestic cat with long, flowing fur
cat with long fur

Examples

The longhaired cat curled up in a sunny spot by the window.
A longhaired cat often needs more grooming than a short-haired one.
She adopted a beautiful longhaired cat from the animal shelter.

Synset Examples

The longhaired cat curled up in my lap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian cat - a breed of longhaired domestic cat with a round face and short muzzle
  • Antonym: shorthaired cat - a cat with short fur
