unemployed person

1. NOUN

/ ʌnɪmˈplɔɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ʌnɪmˈplɔɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who does not have a paid job but is available for and seeking work
someone without paid employment

Examples

After being laid off, he became an unemployed person searching for new opportunities.
The government offers financial assistance to every unemployed person in the country.
Next week's workshop is designed to help the unemployed person update their résumé and job skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jobless - being without a job
  • Antonym: employee - a person who is employed
