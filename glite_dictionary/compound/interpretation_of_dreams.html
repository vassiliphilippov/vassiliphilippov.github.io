interpretation of dreams

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌtɜrprəˈteɪʃən əv driːmz / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜːprɪˈteɪʃən əv driːmz / (BrE)
the act or process of analyzing and explaining the meaning or significance of dreams
analysis of the meaning of dreams

Examples

Sigmund Freud's work on the interpretation of dreams changed modern psychology.
She read several books on the interpretation of dreams hoping to understand her recurring nightmares.
The ancient Egyptians practiced the interpretation of dreams as a means to foresee the future.

Synset Examples

Many debates revolve around Sigmund's interpretations of dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dream analysis - the method of analyzing dreams' content for psychological meaning
