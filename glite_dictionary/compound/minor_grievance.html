minor grievance

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚ ˈɡriːvəns / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə ˈɡriːvəns / (BrE)
a small or relatively unimportant complaint or source of dissatisfaction
small or petty complaint

Examples

He filed a minor grievance with the manager about the noise in his room.
The committee meets weekly to address minor grievances from staff.
She brushed off their minor grievances and focused on the bigger issues.

Synset Examples

The petty-house was filled with minor grievances.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: grievance - a real or imagined wrong or complaint
  • Synonym: petty complaint - a trivial or insignificant complaint
