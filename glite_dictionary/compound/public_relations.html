public relations

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
the professional maintenance of a favorable public image by a company, organization, or individual
management of public image

Examples

The company hired a public relations firm to improve its image after the scandal.
Effective public relations can help a politician build trust with voters.
She works in public relations and handles media requests for the organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising - the activity of producing advertisements for commercial products or services
  • Synonym: spin - a particular emphasis or bias presented by public relations to influence perception
