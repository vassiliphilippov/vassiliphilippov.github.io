Admiralty Range

1. PROPN

/ ˈædmɚəlti reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈædmɪrəlti reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a mountain range in Antarctica located to the north of Victoria Land.
mountain range in Antarctica

Examples

The Admiralty Range is known for its rugged peaks and vast glaciers.
Scientists studied the geology of the Admiralty Range during their Antarctic expedition.
Mount Minto is the highest point in the Admiralty Range.

Etymology

Named after the British Admiralty in the 19th century.
