freeboard deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌbɔrd dɛk / (AmE), / ˈfriːbɔːd dɛk / (BrE)
the uppermost continuous watertight deck of a ship, used as the reference point for measuring a vessel's freeboard.
uppermost watertight deck of a ship

Examples

The inspector measured the height of the freeboard deck to ensure compliance with regulations.
It is vital that the freeboard deck remains watertight during voyages.
Cargo is loaded below the freeboard deck to maintain the ship's stability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck - a structure of planks or plates, horizontal on a ship, forming the principal floors
