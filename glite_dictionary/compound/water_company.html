water company

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company or public utility responsible for supplying water to homes and businesses in a particular area
company that supplies water

Examples

The water company announced a temporary shutdown for maintenance work in the area.
After a burst pipe, the water company responded quickly to restore supply.
She contacted the water company to inquire about her monthly bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility company - company providing essential services, such as electricity, gas, or water
