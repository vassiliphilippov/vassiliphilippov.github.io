Union of Serbia and Montenegro

1. PROPN

/ ˈjun.jən əv ˈsɚ.bi.ə ənd ˌmɑn.təˈni.ɡroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjuː.njən əv ˈsɜː.bi.ə ənd ˌmɒn.təˈniː.ɡrəʊ / (BrE)
the country formed by the republics of Serbia and Montenegro between 2003 and 2006, formerly known as the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia
country formed by Serbia and Montenegro (2003–2006)

Examples

The Union of Serbia and Montenegro was officially established in 2003.
After its dissolution in 2006, the Union of Serbia and Montenegro split into two independent countries.
International organizations recognized the Union of Serbia and Montenegro as the legal successor to the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia.
