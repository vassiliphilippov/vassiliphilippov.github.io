black diamond trail

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈdaɪəmənd treɪl / (AmE), / blak ˈdaɪəmənd treɪl / (BrE)
a ski slope designated as very difficult, typically marked with a black diamond symbol
very difficult ski slope

Examples

He lost his nerve halfway down the black diamond trail.
Only experienced skiers should attempt the steep black diamond trail on this mountain.
After mastering the easy runs, she challenged herself on a black diamond trail.

Synset Examples

They skied down the black diamond trail.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blue square trail - a moderately difficult ski slope
  • Degree Lower: green circle trail - an easy ski slope
  • Degree Higher: double black diamond trail - an extremely difficult ski slope
