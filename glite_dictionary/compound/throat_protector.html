throat protector

1. NOUN

/ θroʊt prəˈtɛktɚ / (AmE), / θrəʊt prəˈtɛktə / (BrE)
a piece of protective equipment worn around the neck by a hockey goalkeeper or baseball catcher to shield the throat from injury
neck protection for athletes

Examples

The hockey goalie always wears a throat protector to prevent serious injuries from pucks.
Some catchers prefer a lightweight throat protector that attaches to their mask.
The rules require all youth players to use a certified throat protector during games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protector - a device or person that protects
