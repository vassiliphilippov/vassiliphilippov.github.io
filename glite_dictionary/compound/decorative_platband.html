decorative platband

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛkəˈreɪtɪv ˈplætˌbænd / (AmE), / ˌdɛkəˈreɪtɪv ˈplætˌbænd / (BrE)
an ornamental flat band or molding used to decorate the surface of a wall, door, or window in architecture
ornamental architectural band or molding

Examples

The designer suggested adding a decorative platband above the entryway to enhance its appearance.
Restorers carefully cleaned the old decorative platbands around each window frame.
A carved decorative platband ran along the top edge of the façade, catching the afternoon light.

Synset Examples

The architect chose a decorative platband for the windows.

Etymology

From 'plat' (flat) + 'band'; 'platband' derived from French 'plate-bande' (flat edge or strip).
