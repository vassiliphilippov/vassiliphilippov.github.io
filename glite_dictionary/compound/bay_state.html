Bay State

1. PROPN

/ beɪ steɪt / (AmE), / beɪ steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Massachusetts, especially referencing its coastal location and historic significance as one of the original 13 colonies
nickname for the state of Massachusetts

Examples

Many colleges in the Bay State have rich histories going back to colonial times.
She took a summer trip to the Bay State to visit Boston and Cape Cod.
The Bay State is known for its pivotal role in American Revolutionary history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Massachusetts - a state in the northeastern United States, known as the 'Bay State'

Etymology

Refers to Massachusetts Bay; used as a nickname for Massachusetts since the 18th century.
