house organ

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / haʊs ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
A magazine, newsletter, or periodical published by an organization primarily for its employees, customers, or members.
business publication for employees or customers

Examples

The company’s house organ features employee achievements and upcoming events.
Subscribers to the house organ receive the latest updates on new products and company goals.
She wrote an article about workplace safety for her employer’s house organ.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsletter - A bulletin issued periodically to members of an organization
  • Synonym: in-house magazine - Magazine published internally by a company for its staff
