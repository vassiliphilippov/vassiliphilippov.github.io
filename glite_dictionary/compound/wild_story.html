wild story

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈstɔri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an account or narrative that is exciting, exaggerated, or difficult to believe
exciting or exaggerated account

Examples

He told a wild story about his adventures in the jungle that none of us believed.
The tabloids are filled with wild stories about celebrities and scandals.
After a few drinks, he started sharing a wild story that grew more outrageous with every detail.

Synset Examples

His affluence of incohol led to wild stories the next day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tall tale - a story with unbelievable elements, often told as if true
