trumpet-wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpət wʊd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt wʊd / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with large shield-shaped leaves and hollow stems, often used as an ornamental plant.
tropical American tree with large leaves and hollow stems

Examples

The garden featured a rare trumpet-wood with its striking, broad leaves.
Scientists studied the hollow stems of the trumpet-wood for unique ecological adaptations.
Several birds were nesting in the high branches of the trumpet-wood tree.
