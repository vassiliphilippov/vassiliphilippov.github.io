like a pro

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə proʊ / (AmE), / laɪk ə prəʊ / (BrE)
in a way that shows great skill or professionalism; as an expert would do
in a skilled, professional manner
informal

Examples

He fixed the leaking faucet like a pro, saving us a plumber's fee.
She navigated through the busy airport like a pro, never losing her way.
After a few lessons, he was driving like a pro.

Synset Examples

Look at you, handling everything like a pro!
She operates a sewing machine like a pro.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expertly - in a very skillful or expert way
  • Antonym: amateurishly - in an unskilled or amateur way
