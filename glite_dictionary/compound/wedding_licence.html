wedding licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌlaɪsns / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official document giving permission for two people to marry
official permission to marry

Examples

They needed a wedding licence before they could arrange the ceremony.
Without a wedding licence, the couple was not allowed to get married.
The clerk checked all their documents, including the wedding licence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage license - a document permitting a couple to marry
