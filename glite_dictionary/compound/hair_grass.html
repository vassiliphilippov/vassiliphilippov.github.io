hair grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a fine-leaved aquatic plant often used in aquariums, especially as an aerator
fine-leaved aquatic plant; aquarium aerator

Examples

Aquarium enthusiasts often choose hair grass for its fine texture and oxygenating properties.
The dense growth of hair grass provides shelter for small fish and invertebrates.
Maintaining healthy hair grass requires adequate lighting and regular trimming.
