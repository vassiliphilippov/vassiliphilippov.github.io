ginger pop

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɚ pɑp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə pɒp / (BrE)
A sweet, carbonated soft drink flavored with ginger; another term for ginger ale or ginger beer, chiefly in British English.
ginger-flavored fizzy drink
british

Examples

As a child, I loved drinking ginger pop at birthday parties.
She offered her guests a glass of ginger pop with ice and lemon.
In the UK, ginger pop is a nostalgic treat for many adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger ale - a sweet, ginger-flavored carbonated drink
  • Synonym: ginger beer - a spicier, ginger-flavored carbonated drink, sometimes alcoholic

Etymology

Pop (slang for carbonated drink) from 19th-century onomatopoeia, combined with ginger (the spice).
