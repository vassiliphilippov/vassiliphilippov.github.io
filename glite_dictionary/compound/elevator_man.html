elevator man

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtɚ mæn / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to operate an elevator in a building
male elevator operator

Examples

The elevator man greeted everyone with a friendly smile as they entered the lift.
In old hotels, an elevator man would control the doors and select your floor for you.
My grandfather worked as an elevator man in the city's tallest building in the 1950s.

Synset Examples

in England they call an elevator man a ‘liftman’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liftman - a man employed to operate a lift
