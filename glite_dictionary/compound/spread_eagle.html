spread eagle

1. NOUN

/ ˌsprɛd ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˌsprɛd ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a figure skating move where the skates are positioned heel to heel in a straight line, legs extended outward
figure skating move with heel-to-heel stance

Examples

The skater performed a beautiful spread eagle before starting his next jump.
Her routine included a perfect spread eagle that impressed the judges.
He practiced his spread eagle on the ice to improve his balance.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛd ˌiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɛd ˌiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
an emblem depicting an eagle with wings and legs spread, especially as shown on the obverse of the Great Seal of the United States
emblem of an eagle with wings and legs spread

Examples

The presidential seal features a bold spread eagle clutching arrows and an olive branch.
He studied the American spread eagle on the Great Seal for his art project.
The coin's reverse depicted a majestic spread eagle symbolizing freedom.
