omelette pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːmlət pæn / (AmE), / ˈɒmlət pan / (BrE)
a shallow frying pan with sloping sides, designed for cooking omelets
shallow frying pan for omelets

Examples

She used her favorite omelette pan to make breakfast every Sunday.
A nonstick omelette pan makes it easier to flip the eggs without breaking them.
The chef recommended investing in a good omelette pan for perfect results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frying pan - a flat pan used for frying foods
