church leadership

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ ˈliːdɚʃɪp / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈliːdəʃɪp / (BrE)
the group of individuals who guide, direct, and manage the affairs of a church; the act of providing such guidance and direction
guidance and management of a church

Examples

The bishop played a key role in shaping church leadership during the transition period.
Strong church leadership is essential for maintaining unity within the congregation.
She was recognized for her years of dedicated service in church leadership.

Synset Examples

A bishop's role is vital in church leadership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clergy - the body of people ordained for religious duties in the church
