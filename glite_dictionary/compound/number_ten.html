Number Ten

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚ tɛn / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə tɛn / (BrE)
a nickname for the UK Prime Minister, the Prime Minister's residence at 10 Downing Street, or the playmaking/key player in certain sports, especially football
UK Prime Minister, key role in sports
british

Examples

Many journalists gathered outside Number Ten to await the Prime Minister's statement.
The player wearing Number Ten orchestrated most of the team's attacks.
For years, fans have debated which Number Ten had the greatest influence on English football.
