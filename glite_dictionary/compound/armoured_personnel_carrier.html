armoured personnel carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmɚd ˌpɚˈsɑːnəl ˈkæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˌpɜːˈsɒnəl ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a military vehicle protected by armour, designed to transport soldiers safely in combat zones, often equipped with caterpillar tracks or wheels
military vehicle for transporting soldiers

Examples

The soldiers were safely transported to the front lines in an armoured personnel carrier.
An armoured personnel carrier rolled down the street, its tracks rumbling over the pavement.
During the military exercise, each unit was assigned an armoured personnel carrier for rapid movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored vehicle - a vehicle protected by armor, usually for military use
