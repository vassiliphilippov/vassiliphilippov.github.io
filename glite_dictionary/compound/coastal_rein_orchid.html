coastal rein orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊstəl reɪn ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstəɫ reɪn ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
a stout orchid native to central California through northern Washington, bearing racemes of white, fragrant, bilaterally symmetrical flowers
orchid native to US west coast

Examples

The coastal rein orchid thrives in damp meadows along the Pacific coast.
Botanists study the blooming patterns of the coastal rein orchid every spring.
We spotted several coastal rein orchids during our hike through the reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a plant of the family Orchidaceae, flowering with showy or bizarrely shaped blooms
