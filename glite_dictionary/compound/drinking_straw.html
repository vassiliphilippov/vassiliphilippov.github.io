drinking straw

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ strɔ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ strɔː / (BrE)
A thin tube, typically made of paper or plastic, used to suck liquids into the mouth.
tube for sucking drinks

Examples

She placed a drinking straw in her iced tea before sitting down.
Children often enjoy blowing bubbles with a drinking straw.
Plastic drinking straws are being replaced by paper ones in many restaurants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straw - a thin tube used to suck liquid into the mouth
