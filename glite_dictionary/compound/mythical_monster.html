mythical monster

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈmɑnstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a creature from folklore or legend, typically imagined as frightening, supernatural, and not existing in reality
creature from myth or legend

Examples

As a child, he was fascinated by stories of the mythical monster that haunted the nearby woods.
Many cultures have their own version of a mythical monster meant to frighten children into good behavior.
The film featured a terrifying mythical monster rising from the depths of the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legendary creature - a creature in legend or folklore
  • Antonym: real animal - a creature that exists in reality, as opposed to mythical ones
