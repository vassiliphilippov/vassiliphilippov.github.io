appointment calendar

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɔɪntmənt ˈkælənˌdɚ / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntmənt ˈkæl.ən.dər / (BrE)
a book or digital tool with a calendar used to record and organize appointments
calendar for recording appointments

Examples

My appointment calendar is full this week with meetings and deadlines.
She checked her appointment calendar before scheduling the new client.
Doctors often use a digital appointment calendar to manage patient visits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diary - a daily record of events or experiences
  • Synonym: planner - a book or pad for scheduling and organizing activities
