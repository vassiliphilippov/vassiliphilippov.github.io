tv room

1. NOUN

/ tiːˈviː ruːm / (AmE), / tiːˈviː ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building that is set aside for watching television
room for watching television

Examples

They converted the basement into a spacious tv room for the family.
We spent Saturday night relaxing in the tv room and watching movies.
The new house has a dedicated tv room separate from the living area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living room - a room in a house for general and informal everyday use
