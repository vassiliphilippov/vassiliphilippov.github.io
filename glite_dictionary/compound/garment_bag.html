garment bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrmənt bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt bæɡ / (BrE)
a flexible bag or suitcase designed to carry clothes without folding or wrinkling, often hung up when unfolded
a bag for carrying clothes wrinkle-free

Examples

She carefully packed her suit in a garment bag to avoid wrinkles during her trip.
Most airlines allow one carry-on and one garment bag per passenger.
He hung the garment bag in the closet as soon as he arrived at the hotel.
