redbird flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌbɝd ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbɜːd ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A low tropical American shrub known for its powerful emetic properties.
tropical shrub with emetic properties

Examples

The redbird flower is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its emetic effects.
Gardeners in tropical regions may cultivate the redbird flower for its bright blossoms.
Researchers are studying compounds from the redbird flower for their potential medicinal applications.
