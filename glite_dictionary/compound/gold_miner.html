gold miner

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊld ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / ɡəʊld ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to extract gold from the earth, especially by digging or panning in gold-bearing areas
person who mines gold

Examples

During the California Gold Rush, thousands of gold miners flocked to the West in search of fortune.
The gold miner spent hours every day panning for flakes in the cold river.
Modern gold miners often use advanced machinery to extract ore from deep underground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miner - a person who works in a mine extracting minerals
