feeling dead inside

1. X

/ ˈfilɪŋ dɛd ɪnˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ dɛd ɪnˌsaɪd / (BrE)
experiencing a state of emotional numbness or emptiness, often as a result of trauma, loss, or prolonged sadness
emotionally numb or empty
informal

Examples

After the breakup, she spent weeks feeling dead inside and uninterested in anything.
He described feeling dead inside after years of working at a job he hated.
Sometimes stress and disappointment leave people feeling dead inside despite outward appearances.

Synset Examples

He walked around feeling dead inside from the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numb - unable to feel, physically or emotionally
  • Synonym: empty - lacking emotion, purpose, or interest
