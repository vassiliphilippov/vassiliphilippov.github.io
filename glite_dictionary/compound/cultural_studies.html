cultural studies

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌl.tʃɚ.əl ˈstʌd.iz / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈstʌd.iz / (BrE)
an interdisciplinary academic field focusing on the analysis of cultural practices, power relations, and social constructs, often drawing from various theoretical perspectives
academic field analyzing culture and society

Examples

She decided to specialize in cultural studies to better understand media influences on society.
The university offers a master's program in cultural studies with a focus on globalization.
Many cultural studies scholars analyze popular music and television as sites of social meaning.

Synset Examples

In fact, in England as well as elsewhere, cultural studies has drawn upon, and embodied, an enormously wide range of theoretical positions, from humanism to poststructuralism, from Marx to Foucault, from pragmatism to psychoanalysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultural anthropology - academic field studying human societies and cultures
