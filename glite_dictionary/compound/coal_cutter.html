coal cutter

1. NOUN

/ koʊl ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a machine used in mining for cutting or undercutting a coal seam
mining machine for cutting coal

Examples

The old mine still had a coal cutter stored near the entrance.
Operating a coal cutter requires training due to its size and complexity.
They replaced the manual picks with a mechanical coal cutter to increase productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mining machine - machine used in mining operations
