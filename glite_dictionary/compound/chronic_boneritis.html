chronic boneritis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɑː.nɪk ˈboʊ.nɚ.aɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɒ.nɪk ˈbəʊ.nər.aɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A humorous, informal term for a persistent erection, playfully mimicking the style of a medical diagnosis.
persistent erection (humorous)
informal slang

Examples

After taking the medication, he joked that he was suffering from chronic boneritis.
During the comedy sketch, one character claimed to have chronic boneritis to explain his awkward behavior.
The term chronic boneritis is often used in jest when referring to persistent male arousal.

Synset Examples

He complained about his chronic boneritis at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: priapism - a medical condition involving a prolonged erection

Etymology

Humorous formation from 'chronic' and 'boner' plus the medical '-itis' suffix.
