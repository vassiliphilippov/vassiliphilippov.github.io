history lesson

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪstəri ˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈhɪstəri ˌlɛsən / (BrE)
a lesson in which historical facts or events are taught or discussed
a lesson about historical facts or events

Examples

Our teacher gave us a fascinating history lesson about ancient Egypt.
Every history lesson seems to spark my curiosity for different time periods.
The film turned out to be an unexpected history lesson on World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesson - an amount of teaching given at one time; a period of learning or teaching
