tunica albuginea testes

1. NOUN

/ ˈtunɪkə ælˌbjuːˈdʒiːniə ˈtɛstiːz / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnɪkə ælˌbjuːˈdʒiːniə ˈtɛstiːz / (BrE)
the dense, white fibrous covering of the testicle
fibrous covering of the testicle
medical

Examples

During the operation, the surgeon carefully opened the tunica albuginea testes to access the underlying tissue.

Etymology

From Latin: 'tunica' (covering, coat) + 'albuginea' (whitish) + 'testes' (testicle).
