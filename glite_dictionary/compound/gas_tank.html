gas tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæs tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡas taŋk / (BrE)
a container in a vehicle for storing the gasoline used to power the engine
container for vehicle gasoline

Examples

The car ran out of gas because the gas tank was nearly empty.
He noticed a leak coming from his motorcycle's gas tank.
Make sure the gas tank is full before starting your road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel tank - a tank for storing any type of fuel in a vehicle or machine
