football coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊt.bɔl koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A person who trains and directs a football team, developing strategies and overseeing players' skills and performance.
trainer/director of a football team

Examples

The football coach organized extra practices before the championship game.
As a football coach, she focuses on teamwork and discipline above all else.
The school's new football coach previously led a professional team to victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains athletes or teams
  • Synonym: manager - person responsible for administration or team strategy
