gainly posture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪnli ˈpɑstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪnli ˈpɒstʃə / (BrE)
a graceful or attractive way of standing or holding one's body
graceful or attractive stance

Examples

Her gainly posture impressed the judges during the competition.
He stood with a gainly posture that exuded quiet confidence.
Artists often sketch models with a gainly posture to capture elegance and poise.
