multicultural Marxism

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmɑrksɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmɑːksɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
a theoretical approach combining multicultural perspectives with Marxist analysis, often focusing on the intersections of class, culture, and identity
theory blending multiculturalism with Marxism

Examples

The academic conference featured several papers on multicultural Marxism and its relevance today.
Her research explores the ways multicultural Marxism addresses the intersection of race and class.
Critics argue that multicultural Marxism sometimes overlooks economic factors in favor of cultural issues.

The debate on multicultural Marxism continues in academia.
