yellow turnip

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈtɜrnəp / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈtɜːnɪp / (BrE)
the large yellow root of a rutabaga plant, used as food
root vegetable; rutabaga

Examples

The soup included diced yellow turnip along with carrots and potatoes.
At the market, she bought a large yellow turnip for tonight's stew.
Mashed yellow turnip is a popular side dish in many northern countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rutabaga - a large, yellow-fleshed root vegetable; Brassica napus napobrassica
  • Degree Lower: turnip - a round root vegetable with white flesh
