brass hat

1. NOUN

/ bræs hæt / (AmE), / brɑːs hæt / (BrE)
a high-ranking officer in the military, especially one with administrative or staff responsibilities
high-ranking military officer

Examples

The brass hat gave a stern lecture to the assembled troops.
Many decisions are made at headquarters by the brass hat rather than in the field.
Critics claimed that the brass hat was out of touch with ordinary soldiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top brass - senior military officers

Etymology

Alludes to the ornate gold or brass braid traditionally worn on the hats of senior military officers.
