Bahasa Malaysia

1. NOUN

/ bəˈhɑːsə məˈleɪziə / (AmE), / bəˈhɑːsə məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the standardized form of the Malay language used as the official language of Malaysia
Malay as spoken in Malaysia

Examples

At school, all lessons are taught in Bahasa Malaysia.
She is fluent in both English and Bahasa Malaysia.
The official documents were translated into Bahasa Malaysia for the local community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malay - the Austronesian language of the Malay people
  • Synonym: Bahasa Indonesia - the standardized form of Malay used as the official language of Indonesia

Etymology

From Malay 'bahasa' (language) and 'Malaysia', the country name.
