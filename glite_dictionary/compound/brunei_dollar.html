Brunei dollar

1. NOUN

/ bruːˈnaɪ ˈdɑːlər / (AmE), / bruːˈnaɪ ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Brunei, abbreviated as BND
currency unit of Brunei

Examples

The price of the imported car was listed as 40,000 Brunei dollars.
She exchanged her US dollars for Brunei dollars at the airport.
The hotel accepts both Singapore dollars and Brunei dollars for payment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Singapore dollar - the basic unit of currency in Singapore, also pegged to the Brunei dollar
