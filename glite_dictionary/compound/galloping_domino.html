galloping domino

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæl.ə.pɪŋ dəˈmiː.noʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ə.pɪŋ dəˈmiː.nəʊ / (BrE)
A rapidly escalating series of problems or consequences, each triggered by the previous one.
rapidly escalating chain of problems or consequences

Examples

The technical failure caused a galloping domino that affected every department in the company.
Poor communication led to a galloping domino of misunderstandings and project setbacks.
When the first order was delayed, a galloping domino quickly impacted the entire supply chain.

Synset Examples

One mistake created a galloping domino of further issues.
The project turned into a galloping domino effect of delays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domino effect - a chain reaction where one event sets off a sequence of similar events
  • Synonym: chain reaction - a sequence of events caused by the previous event
