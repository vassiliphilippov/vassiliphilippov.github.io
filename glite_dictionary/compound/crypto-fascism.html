crypto-fascism

1. NOUN

/ ˌkrɪptoʊˈfæʃɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəʊˈfæʃɪzəm / (BrE)
A political ideology that covertly incorporates or supports fascist principles while leveraging cryptocurrency technologies.
hidden fascism linked with cryptocurrency

Examples

Recent political manifestos have been accused of promoting crypto-fascism in subtle ways.
Academic circles have studied the relationship between crypto-fascism and online financial platforms.
He warned about the spread of crypto-fascism within decentralized tech communities.

Synset Examples

Debates about crypto-fascism have escalated recently.
The rise of crypto-fascism is concerning for democracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization

Etymology

From Greek kryptós (hidden) + fascism; modeled on 'crypto-' (hidden) and 'fascism'.
