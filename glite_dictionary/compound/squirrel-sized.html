squirrel-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwɝːrəl saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a squirrel
about the size of a squirrel

Examples

The archaeologists discovered a squirrel-sized dinosaur fossil in the newly excavated site.
A squirrel-sized hole in the fence allowed small animals to squeeze through.
She adopted an injured, squirrel-sized puppy that had been abandoned in the neighborhood.
