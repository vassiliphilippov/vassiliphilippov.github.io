sweet potato

1. NOUN

/ swit pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / swiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A tropical plant with large, edible, sweet-tasting, orange-fleshed roots, widely cultivated as a vegetable.
edible sweet orange-fleshed root vegetable

Examples

Roasted sweet potato is a delicious addition to any salad.
She made a pie using mashed sweet potato and spices.
Many cuisines around the world feature dishes with sweet potato as the main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yam - edible starchy tuber sometimes confused with sweet potato

2. NOUN

/ swit pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / swiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A large, sweet-tasting, starchy root vegetable with orange or white flesh, eaten as food and grown in warm climates.
large sweet edible tuber vegetable

Examples

We roasted a sweet potato with herbs for dinner last night.
The sweet potato is rich in vitamins and fiber.
She made a delicious sweet potato pie for the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yam - starchy tuber, often confused with sweet potato
