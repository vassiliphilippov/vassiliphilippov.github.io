Easter bunny

1. NOUN

/ ˈistɚ ˌbʌni / (AmE), / ˈiːstə ˌbʌni / (BrE)
an imaginary rabbit that brings Easter eggs and gifts to children at Easter
imaginary rabbit that brings Easter eggs

Examples

Every year, children look forward to a visit from the Easter bunny on Easter morning.
The tradition of the Easter bunny delivering chocolate eggs is popular in many countries.
My little brother left a carrot out for the Easter bunny last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Easter egg - a decorated egg given at Easter, often chocolate
  • Synonym: Santa Claus - a legendary figure who brings gifts to children at Christmas
