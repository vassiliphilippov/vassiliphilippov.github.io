family unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈfamɪli ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a social group consisting of parents and their children, regarded as a single household entity
parents and children as a social group

Examples

The family unit is often considered the foundation of society in many cultures.
In times of crisis, the strength of the family unit becomes especially important.
Many policies are designed to support the modern family unit in balancing work and home life.

Synset Examples

he wanted to have a good job before starting a family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear family - family group consisting only of parents and their children
  • Antonym: extended family - family including relatives beyond parents and children
