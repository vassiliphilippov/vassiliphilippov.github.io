graduation exercise

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃən ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃən ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a ceremony or academic event in which diplomas are officially conferred on graduating students
ceremony where diplomas are awarded

Examples

The university's graduation exercise will take place next Saturday in the main auditorium.
Parents and friends gathered to watch the graduation exercise and celebrate the achievements of the students.
Each senior looked forward to walking across the stage during the graduation exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commencement - official graduation ceremony, typically in American English
  • Synonym: convocation - formal academic assembly for degree conferral
