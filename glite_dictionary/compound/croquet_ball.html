croquet ball

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈkeɪ bɔl / (AmE), / krəʊˈkeɪ bɔːl / (BrE)
a wooden ball used in the game of croquet
ball used in croquet

Examples

Each player selects a croquet ball before the match begins.
He accidentally hit his croquet ball out of bounds, delaying the game.
The set includes four wooden mallets and a croquet ball for each color.

Related Terms
