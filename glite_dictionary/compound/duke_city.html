Duke City

1. PROPN

/ duːk ˈsɪti / (AmE), / djuːk ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A nickname for Albuquerque, New Mexico, often used informally in reference to the city.
nickname for Albuquerque, New Mexico

Examples

Every summer, thousands of tourists visit Duke City for its famous festivals.
After college, she moved to Duke City to start her new job.
The basketball team from Duke City won the state championship this year.

Synset Examples

Her family moved to Duke City last summer.
They spent the weekend exploring the vibrant Duke City.

Etymology

Refers to Albuquerque's namesake, the Duke of Alburquerque (Spanish nobleman).
