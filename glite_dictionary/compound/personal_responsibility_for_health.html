personal responsibility for health

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɝːsənl rɪˌspɑːnsəˈbɪləti fər hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪləti fə hɛlθ / (BrE)
the principle or belief that each individual is accountable for their own health-related choices and outcomes
accountability for one's own health choices

Examples

Public health campaigns often stress personal responsibility for health through lifestyle choices like diet and exercise.
He believes that personal responsibility for health is as important as access to medical care.
Debates around healthcare policy frequently mention personal responsibility for health when discussing the costs of treatment.

Synset Examples

Healthism promotes personal responsibility for health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: individual responsibility - the state of being personally accountable for actions or decisions
  • Antonym: collective responsibility - shared accountability among a group for actions or outcomes
