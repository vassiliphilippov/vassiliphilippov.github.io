genus Ginglymostoma

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌɡɪŋɡlɪˈmɒstəmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌɡɪŋ*ɡlɪˈmɒstəmə / (BrE)
A genus of sharks known as nurse sharks, commonly found in warm, shallow waters.
a genus of nurse sharks

Examples

The genus Ginglymostoma includes a variety of nurse sharks found in tropical waters.
Scientists studied the feeding habits of the genus Ginglymostoma in the Caribbean Sea.
Members of the genus Ginglymostoma are recognized by their broad heads and barbels near the mouth.

Etymology

From New Latin: 'ginglymo-' (hinge) + 'stoma' (mouth), referring to the jaw structure.
