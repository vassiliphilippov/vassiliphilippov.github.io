tub gurnard

1. NOUN

/ tʌb ˈɡɝːnɑrd / (AmE), / tʌb ˈɡɜːnɑːd / (BrE)
a species of gurnard fish (Chelidonichthys lucerna) found chiefly in European waters, recognized by its reddish color and distinctive pectoral fins
a species of European gurnard fish

Examples

The fisherman was excited to find a tub gurnard in his nets this morning.
We studied the vibrant pectoral fins of the tub gurnard in marine biology class.
The chef prepared a delicious dish using freshly caught tub gurnard.
