milieu control

1. NOUN

/ miːlˈjuː kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / miːlˈjɜː kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the management or regulation of an environment in order to influence the behavior of people within it
management of an environment to influence behavior

Examples

The new manager implemented milieu control to encourage collaboration among employees.
Strict milieu control was a key factor in the success of the rehabilitation program.
Teachers sometimes use milieu control to create a focused learning space.

Synset Examples

Effective milieu control can prevent negative influences.
They focused on milieu control to boost team morale.
Milieu control helped maintain a positive atmosphere at work.

Etymology

From French 'milieu' (middle, environment) and English 'control'.
