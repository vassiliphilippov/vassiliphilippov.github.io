mullein pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌl.ɪn pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈmʌl.ɪn pɪŋk / (BrE)
an ornamental garden plant with white woolly leaves and vibrant crimson flowers, originally from southeastern Europe
ornamental plant with crimson flowers

Examples

The mullein pink brightened the cottage garden with its vivid crimson blooms.
She decided to plant a row of mullein pink along the fence for their striking foliage.
Many gardeners cherish the mullein pink for its enduring summer flowers and silvery leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rose campion - an alternative name for mullein pink; same plant species
