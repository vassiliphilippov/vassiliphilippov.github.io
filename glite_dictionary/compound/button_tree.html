button tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt.ən tri / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ən triː / (BrE)
an evergreen tree or shrub with button-like fruit and heavy, hard, compact wood
evergreen tree or shrub with button-like fruit

Examples

The button tree is easily recognized by its rounded, button-like fruit.
Carpenters prize the wood of the button tree for its density and durability.
Several species are called button tree because of their distinctive fruit.
