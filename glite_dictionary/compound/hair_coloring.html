hair coloring

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈkʌlɚɪŋ / (AmE), / heə ˈkʌlərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of changing the color of hair, especially by applying dye or tint
the process of dyeing hair

Examples

She decided to try hair coloring for the first time to cover her gray strands.
Many salons offer professional hair coloring services for their clients.
Home hair coloring kits have become increasingly popular over the years.

  • Synonym: hair dye - substance used for coloring hair
