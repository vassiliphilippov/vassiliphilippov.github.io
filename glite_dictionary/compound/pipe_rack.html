pipe rack

1. NOUN

/ paɪp ræk / (AmE), / paɪp ræk / (BrE)
a stand or holder designed to store and display tobacco pipes for smokers
rack for holding tobacco pipes

Examples

He displayed his collection of pipes neatly on a wooden pipe rack in the study.
The antique pipe rack held a dozen finely crafted meerschaum pipes.
After cleaning his pipe, he placed it back on the pipe rack to dry.
