bimbo eruption

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪmboʊ ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɪmbəʊ ɪˈrʌpʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a sudden media scandal or controversy, typically involving allegations of inappropriate behavior with women, especially in a political context
media scandal involving a woman
informal slang american

Examples

The politician faced a bimbo eruption just days before the election.
Another bimbo eruption threatened to overshadow the administration's policy announcements.
The press conference quickly turned into a discussion about the latest bimbo eruption.

Synset Examples

His bimbo eruption was the talk of the town.
They laughed off the bimbo eruption as just gossip.
The scandal caused a bimbo eruption in the media.

Etymology

Formed in American political slang during the 1990s; 'bimbo' (slang for an attractive but unintelligent woman) + 'eruption'.
