golden thistle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈθɪsl̩ / (BrE)
any of several spiny Mediterranean herbs of the genus Scolymus with yellow flower heads
spiny Mediterranean herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The golden thistle is admired for its striking yellow blooms in Mediterranean gardens.
Farmers often remove golden thistle from their fields to protect their crops.
She pressed a dried golden thistle into her botanical journal as a keepsake.
