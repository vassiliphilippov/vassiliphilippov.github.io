glaringly apparent

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɛrɪŋli əˈpɛrənt / (AmE), / ˈɡleəriŋli əˈparənt / (BrE)
extremely obvious or easy to see; completely clear or evident
extremely obvious or evident

Examples

The contradiction in his statement was glaringly apparent to everyone in the room.
It became glaringly apparent that she had not prepared for the meeting.
The flaws in the design were glaringly apparent after only a few tests.

Synset Examples

His mistakes were glaringly apparent in the report.
The error was glaringly obvious to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
  • Synonym: apparent - clearly visible or understood; obvious
