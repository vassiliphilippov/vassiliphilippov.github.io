stomach ache

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌmək eɪk / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək eɪk / (BrE)
A pain or uncomfortable sensation in the stomach or abdominal area.
pain or discomfort in the stomach

Examples

I couldn't go to school because I had a terrible stomach ache.
She complained of a stomach ache after eating too much candy.
If you have a persistent stomach ache, you should see a doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abdominal pain - pain occurring in the abdomen
