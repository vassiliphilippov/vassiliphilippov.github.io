to cosseted by

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑs.ɪtɪd baɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒs.ɪtɪd baɪ / (BrE)
Given a lot of care, attention, or indulgence by someone, often in a way that makes one pampered or spoiled.
pampered or spoiled by someone

Examples

During her illness, she was cosseted by her friends who brought her food and company.
The actor was cosseted by fans wherever he went, making daily life challenging.
Some children, cosseted by wealth, struggle to become independent adults.

Synset Examples

He was cosseted by his parents throughout his childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pampered by - receiving excessive care or attention from someone
  • Antonym: neglected by - not receiving enough care or attention from someone

Etymology

From Middle English *cosset* (a pet lamb), of uncertain origin.
