sea boat

1. NOUN

/ siː boʊt / (AmE), / siː bəʊt / (BrE)
A boat designed to be seaworthy and capable of operating safely on the open sea.
boat suited for the open sea

Examples

The crew quickly evacuated into the sea boat when the ship started sinking.
Not every small vessel is considered a sea boat suitable for rough ocean voyages.
He was proud of his new sea boat, built to withstand the harshest storms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lifeboat - a small, sturdy boat used for rescue at sea
