wild meadow lily

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈmɛdoʊ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmɛdəʊ ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a lily native to eastern North America, with nodding yellow or reddish flowers spotted with brown
North American lily with yellow or reddish spotted flowers

Examples

We found a wild meadow lily growing by the edge of the creek.
The wild meadow lily blooms in early summer with delicate, spotted petals.
Many nature reserves protect the habitat of the wild meadow lily due to its regional significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - a plant with large prominent flowers, genus Lilium
