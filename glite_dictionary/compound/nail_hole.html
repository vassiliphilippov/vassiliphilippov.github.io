nail hole

1. NOUN

/ neɪl hoʊl / (AmE), / neɪl həʊl / (BrE)
a hole in a surface left after a nail has been removed
hole left after removing a nail

Examples

After removing the picture from the wall, a small nail hole was visible.
He used wood filler to cover the nail holes in the doorframe.
Before painting, she made sure to patch every nail hole in the plaster.
