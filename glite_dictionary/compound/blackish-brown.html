blackish-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈblækɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈblækɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with black; dark brown with a hint of black.
brown tinged with black

Examples

The cat had a beautiful blackish-brown coat that glistened in the sunlight.
She chose a blackish-brown paint for the living room to create a cozy atmosphere.
The feathers of the bird appeared mostly blackish-brown with a faint sheen.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color between red and yellow, typically dark or muted
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, resulting from the absence of light
