laboratory glassware

1. NOUN

/ ˌlæbrəˌtɔri ˈɡlæsˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˌlæbrəˈtɒri ˈɡlɑːswɛə / (BrE)
Equipment made of glass, such as beakers, flasks, and test tubes, used in scientific laboratories for experiments and other scientific work.
glass equipment used in laboratories

Examples

She carefully washed all the laboratory glassware after completing the experiment.
Accurate measurements depend on the cleanliness of the laboratory glassware.
Broken laboratory glassware must be disposed of according to safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beaker - a simple lipped cylindrical glass vessel used in laboratories
  • Synonym: test tube - a thin glass tube closed at one end, used for scientific experiments
