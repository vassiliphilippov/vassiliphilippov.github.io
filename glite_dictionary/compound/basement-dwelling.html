basement-dwelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈbeɪsmənt ˌdwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsmənt ˌdwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Living in a basement, often with the implication of being isolated or socially withdrawn.
living in a basement, often isolated

Examples

The basement-dwelling writer rarely went outside except for groceries.
Some gamers joke about being basement-dwelling enthusiasts with little social life.
Critics used the phrase basement-dwelling to stereotype reclusive internet users.

Synset Examples

The basement-dwelling artist found inspiration in darkness.
