bastardly gullion

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərdli ˈɡʌljən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstədli ˈɡʌljən / (BrE)
A deceitful, greedy, or gluttonous person, often malicious in their actions.
deceitful or greedy, malicious person
archaic

Examples

Nobody trusted the bastardly gullion in the merchant's guild.
She called him a bastardly gullion after he betrayed her confidence.
Tales warned children not to become a bastardly gullion lest they face social ruin.

Synset Examples

He was a bastardly gullion, always scheming against others.
Everyone knew he was a bastardly gullion at heart.
The bastardly gullion tried to undermine the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
  • Synonym: villain - a person who is deliberately cruel or evil

Etymology

From 'bastardly' (from Old French ‘bastard’) and 'gullion' (an archaic term for a base or vile person).
