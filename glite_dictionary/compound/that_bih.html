that bih

1. NOUN

/ ðæt bɪh / (AmE), / ðat bɪh / (BrE)
(slang) Used to refer admiringly to a stylish outfit, accessory, person, or thing, especially one that stands out in a positive or fashionable way.
slang for a standout outfit, accessory, person, or thing
slang

Examples

She walked into the room and everyone noticed that bih immediately.
Whenever I wear that bih, I get so many compliments on my style.
He just bought new sneakers, and let me tell you, that bih is fire.

Synset Examples

She always wears that bih to parties.
That bih is the cutest outfit I've seen.

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'that bitch', reappropriated in African-American Vernacular English (AAVE) and internet slang as a term of admiration.
