Vichy water

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːʃi ˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪʃi ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
sparkling mineral water from the springs at Vichy, France, or water resembling it in composition and taste.
sparkling mineral water from Vichy, France

Examples

She ordered a glass of Vichy water to accompany her meal.
The mineral taste of Vichy water is quite distinctive and refreshing.
Doctors sometimes recommended Vichy water for its supposed health benefits.

Etymology

From Vichy, a town in central France known for its mineral springs, plus English 'water'.
