effective deratization

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfɛktɪv ˌdɛrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktɪv ˌdɛrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the successful removal or extermination of rats or other pests from an area
successful removal of rats or pests

Examples

The city implemented effective deratization programs to curb the spread of disease.
Without effective deratization, the warehouse remained at risk for infestation.
Regular monitoring ensures that effective deratization is maintained over time.

Synset Examples

Effective deratization can prevent future infestations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat control - the regulation or elimination of rats
  • Synonym: pest eradication - the complete destruction or removal of pests

Etymology

From Latin 'de-' (down, away) + 'ratus' (rat), with 'effective' from Latin 'effectivus' (creative, effective).
