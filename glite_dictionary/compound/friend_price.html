friend price

1. NOUN

/ frɛnd praɪs / (AmE), / frɛnd praɪs / (BrE)
A discounted price offered to friends, usually lower than the standard price.
discounted price for friends
informal

Examples

He was happy to sell his bike to me at a friend price.
As a regular customer, I occasionally get the friend price on specialty coffees.
If you know the owner, you might be able to ask for the friend price.

Synset Examples

He offered her the friend price for the handmade jewelry.
The restaurant gave us a friend price for the group meal.
She managed to get the friend price on concert tickets.
