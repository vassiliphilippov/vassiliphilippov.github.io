Quebec City

1. PROPN

/ kəˈbɛk ˈsɪti / (AmE), / kəˈbɛk ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital city of the province of Quebec in Canada, known for its French-speaking population and historic architecture; located on the Saint Lawrence River.
capital city of Quebec, Canada

Examples

Many tourists visit Quebec City every year to experience its charming Old Town and vibrant culture.
Winter Carnival in Quebec City is one of the largest and most famous winter festivals in the world.
The historic district of Quebec City has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Etymology

Named after the Algonquin word "kebec," meaning 'where the river narrows.'
