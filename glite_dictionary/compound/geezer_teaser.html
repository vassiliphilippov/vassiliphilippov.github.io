geezer teaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡiːzər ˈtiːzər / (AmE), / ˈɡiːzə ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
something, especially a film or other entertainment, designed to appeal to older people, often by featuring familiar actors or nostalgic themes.
entertainment aimed at older people
informal

Examples

The new crime drama was marketed as a geezer teaser for fans of classic action stars.
Streaming platforms are releasing more geezer teasers to attract an older audience.
Critics described the film as a typical geezer teaser packed with aging actors.

Synset Examples

His jokes are always a bit of a geezer teaser, poking fun at old age.
The movie was a geezer teaser with plenty of nostalgic references.
The band's new album is a geezer teaser for older fans.

Etymology

Formed from 'geezer' (slang for an old man) and 'teaser' (something enticing or intended to attract attention).
