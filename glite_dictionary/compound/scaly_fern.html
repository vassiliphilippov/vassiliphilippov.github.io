scaly fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈskeɪli fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈskeɪli fɜːn / (BrE)
a small European fern with chaffy, leathery, and scaly fronds
a European fern with scaly fronds

Examples

The scaly fern thrives in damp, shaded woodlands across Europe.
Gardeners often appreciate the scaly fern for its distinctive textured foliage.
She identified the plant as a scaly fern by its leathery and chaffy fronds.
