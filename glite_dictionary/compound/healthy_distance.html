healthy distance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθi dɪˈstæns / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi dɪˈstɑːns / (BrE)
an appropriate or beneficial amount of space between people, either physically or emotionally, to maintain well-being and respect boundaries
appropriate space; beneficial separation

Examples

In order to avoid conflicts at work, they kept a healthy distance from office gossip.
Setting a healthy distance in relationships can promote mutual respect and understanding.
The therapist encouraged her to maintain a healthy distance from stressful family dynamics.

Synset Examples

They maintained a healthy distance from each other.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundary - a limit or border, especially one that protects personal space
  • Synonym: detachment - emotional distance to maintain objectivity
