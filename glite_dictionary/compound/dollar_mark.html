dollar mark

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːlər mɑːrk / (AmE), / ˈdɒlə mɑːk / (BrE)
the symbol ($) written before a number to show that the amount is in dollars
the symbol $ showing a dollar amount

Examples

Please write the dollar mark before the amount on the check.
She drew a large dollar mark on the whiteboard to illustrate her point.
The receipt showed the total with a dollar mark before the numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar sign - the symbol ($) representing dollars
