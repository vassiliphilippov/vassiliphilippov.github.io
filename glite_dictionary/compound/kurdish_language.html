Kurdish language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrdɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːdɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken by the Kurdish people in Turkey, Iran, Iraq, Syria, and surrounding regions
an Iranian language of the Kurds

Examples

Many people in northern Iraq speak Kurdish language as their first language.
She is studying the Kurdish language at university.
The festival celebrated traditional music and poetry in the Kurdish language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the main Iranian language spoken in Iran
  • Antonym: Turkish language - the language spoken primarily in Turkey

Etymology

From 'Kurd', referring to the Kurdish people, plus 'language'.
