thicket-forming

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɪkɪt ˌfɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɪkɪt ˌfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant or species) tending to grow together in dense groups or thickets.
growing in dense thickets

Examples

The bamboo is a thicket-forming species that can quickly dominate disturbed areas.
Many thicket-forming shrubs provide shelter for wildlife in forest edges and clearings.
Certain thicket-forming plants are intentionally used to create natural barriers along property lines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thicket - a dense group of bushes or small trees
