funeral director

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjʊnɚəl dəˌrɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊənərəl dɪˌrɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange and manage funerals, including preparing bodies and coordinating funeral services
person who manages funerals

Examples

The funeral director explained all the available options to the grieving family.
After her grandmother's passing, they contacted a funeral director to help plan the service.
The funeral director managed every detail to ensure the ceremony ran smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undertaker - a person whose business is preparing dead bodies for burial or cremation and making arrangements for funerals
