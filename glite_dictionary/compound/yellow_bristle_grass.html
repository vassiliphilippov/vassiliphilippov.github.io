yellow bristle grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈbrɪsəl ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈbrɪsl̩ ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a common weedy grass with bristly seed heads found in many temperate regions
a widespread weedy grass

Examples

The field was overrun with yellow bristle grass after months of neglect.
Farmers consider yellow bristle grass a troublesome weed in their pastures.
He pulled out several clumps of yellow bristle grass from the garden by hand.
