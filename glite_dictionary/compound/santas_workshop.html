Santa's workshop

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.təz ˈwɜrk.ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈsæn.təz ˈwɜːk.ʃɒp / (BrE)
a fictional place where Santa Claus and his helpers make toys for children to be delivered at Christmas
fictional place where Santa makes toys

Examples

Every December, the mall sets up a display that looks just like Santa's workshop to excite the children.
The movie showed elves bustling around inside Santa's workshop, building toys and wrapping gifts.
Her favorite holiday tradition is making crafts in her own pretend Santa's workshop.

Synset Examples

We decorated the house to look like Santa's workshop.
The kids imagined Santa's workshop filled with toys.
She dreams of visiting Santa's workshop one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Santa Claus - legendary figure who brings gifts to children at Christmas
