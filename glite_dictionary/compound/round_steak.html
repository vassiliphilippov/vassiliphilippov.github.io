round steak

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd steɪk / (AmE), / raʊnd steɪk / (BrE)
a lean cut of beef taken from the round, the area between the rump and the shank of a cow
a cut of beef from the round area

Examples

The recipe calls for one pound of round steak, sliced thinly.
You can tenderize round steak by marinating it overnight before cooking.
We grilled the round steak and served it with vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sirloin - a cut of beef from the upper part of the loin
  • Synonym: tenderloin - a tender cut of beef from the loin
