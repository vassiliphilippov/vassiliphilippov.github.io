monkey pod

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌpɒd / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree (Samanea saman), often grown for shade or ornament, with bipinnate leaves, globular clusters of crimson-stamened flowers, and seed pods sometimes eaten by cattle.
ornamental tropical tree with edible seed pods

Examples

The sprawling branches of the monkey pod provided shade to the village square.
Cattle grazed on the sweet seed pods dropped by the monkey pod tree.
The park is known for its ancient monkey pod trees that form a natural canopy.
