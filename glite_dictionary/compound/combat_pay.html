combat pay

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmˌbæt ˌpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˌpeɪ / (BrE)
extra money paid to military personnel who are serving in active combat zones
extra pay for military in combat

Examples

Soldiers deployed overseas received combat pay in addition to their regular salary.
The government recently increased combat pay for troops stationed in active war zones.
Serving in a designated combat area qualifies you for combat pay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazard pay - extra pay for dangerous work
