Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh

1. PROPN

/ rʌʃˈtriːjə swɑː.jəmˈseɪvək sʌŋ / (AmE), / rʌʃˈtriːjə swəˌjʌmˈsɛvək sʌŋ / (BrE)
a Hindu nationalist volunteer organization in India, founded in 1925, known for promoting Hindu values and social service among Hindus
Hindu nationalist volunteer organization in India

Examples

The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh has played a significant role in shaping India's political landscape.
Members of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh often participate in community service and educational activities.
The influence of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh can be seen in various aspects of Indian society.

Etymology
