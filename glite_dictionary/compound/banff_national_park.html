Banff National Park

1. PROPN

/ bænf næʃənl pɑrk / (AmE), / bænf ˈnæʃənəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a large national park in the Canadian Rockies, famous for its stunning mountain scenery, lakes, and diverse wildlife
national park in the Canadian Rockies

Examples

We spent our summer hiking and camping in Banff National Park.
Banff National Park attracts millions of tourists each year due to its breathtaking landscapes.
The turquoise waters of Lake Louise are one of the highlights of Banff National Park.

Etymology

Named after Banffshire in Scotland, the birthplace of two Canadian Pacific Railway directors.
