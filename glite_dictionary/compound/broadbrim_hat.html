broadbrim hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔˌbrɪm hæt / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌbrɪm hæt / (BrE)
a hat with a wide brim, worn to provide protection from the sun
hat with a wide brim for sun protection

Examples

She wore a broadbrim hat to shield her face at the beach.
The gardener's broadbrim hat kept the sun out of his eyes while he worked.
Tourists often buy a broadbrim hat before heading out on safari.

Synset Examples

She wore a stylish broadbrim hat to protect herself from the sun.
The cowboy's broadbrim hat shaded his eyes from the harsh light.
