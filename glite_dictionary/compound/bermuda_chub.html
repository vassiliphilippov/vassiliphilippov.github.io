Bermuda chub

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdə tʃʌb / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə tʃʌb / (BrE)
a species of food and game fish found near Bermuda and Florida, often seen following ships
a fish found near Bermuda and Florida

Examples

Fishermen often catch Bermuda chub near coral reefs in the Caribbean.
A school of Bermuda chub followed the boat as it sailed off the coast of Florida.
The grilled Bermuda chub was a highlight of our seafood meal.
