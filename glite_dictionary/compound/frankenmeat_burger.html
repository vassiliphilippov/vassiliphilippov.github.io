frankenmeat burger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfræŋkənˌmit ˈbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈfræŋkənˌmiːt ˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a burger made with genetically modified or artificial meat, rather than traditional animal meat
burger with artificial or genetically modified meat
informal

Examples

Some companies are developing frankenmeat burgers to offer a more sustainable alternative to traditional beef.
She was curious but hesitant to try the new frankenmeat burger on the menu.
Critics argue that the rise of the frankenmeat burger could change the food industry forever.

Synset Examples

The restaurant is known for its frankenmeat burgers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: veggie burger - a burger made from non-meat, plant-based ingredients
  • Degree Lower: burger - a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another food, that is cooked and eaten in a bread roll

Etymology

Blend of 'Frankenstein' and 'meat', referencing artificially created life, plus 'burger'.
