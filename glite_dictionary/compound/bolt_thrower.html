bolt thrower

1. NOUN

/ boʊlt ˈθroʊər / (AmE), / bəʊlt ˈθrəʊə / (BrE)
an ancient missile weapon designed to launch bolts or stones, used especially in warfare
ancient weapon for shooting bolts or stones

Examples

The soldiers operated a massive bolt thrower during the siege of the fortress.
Historians found remnants of a bolt thrower at the ancient battlefield site.
A Roman bolt thrower could propel deadly projectiles over considerable distances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catapult - an ancient weapon for hurling large stones or other projectiles
