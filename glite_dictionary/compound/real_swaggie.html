real swaggie

1. NOUN

/ ˈriəl ˈswæɡi / (AmE), / ˈriːəl ˈswæɡi / (BrE)
An informal term for a confident and stylish appearance or manner.
confident, stylish appearance (informal)
informal slang

Examples

She walked into the party with a real swaggie that turned everyone's head.
After his makeover, he showed up at school sporting a real swaggie.
The model's real swaggie stole the spotlight at the fashion show.

Synset Examples

Her new outfit gave her a real swaggie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swagger - a manner of walking with confidence and style; ostentatious style

Etymology

From 'real' (genuine) and slang 'swaggie' (from 'swagger,' style or confidence).
