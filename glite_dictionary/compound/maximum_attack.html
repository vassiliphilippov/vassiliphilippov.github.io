maximum attack

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæksɪməm əˈtæk / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪməm əˈtak / (BrE)
an intense or all-out assault, especially in military operations or sports, aiming to apply the greatest possible force or effort
intense or all-out assault or strategy

Examples

The coach urged his players to go for maximum attack in the second half.
Enemy forces launched a maximum attack at dawn to break the stalemate.
Playing with maximum attack can sometimes leave a team vulnerable to counterattacks.

Synset Examples

They launched a maximum attack to overwhelm the enemy.
The team went for maximum attack in the final minutes.
In sports, a maximum attack strategy can turn the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full-scale attack - a complete or total attack using all available resources
  • Synonym: all-out attack - an attack involving the utmost effort or resources
  • Antonym: defensive strategy - an approach focused on defense rather than offense
