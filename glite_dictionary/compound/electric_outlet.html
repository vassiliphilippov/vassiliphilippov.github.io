electric outlet

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaʊtlɛt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaʊtlət / (BrE)
A socket in a wall or other surface that provides access to electrical power for plugging in devices.
socket for connecting electrical devices

Examples

There is an electric outlet near the bed for charging your phone.
Make sure not to overload a single electric outlet with too many devices.
The hotel room only had one electric outlet, so we had to take turns charging our laptops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power outlet - a socket that provides electric power; another term for electric outlet
  • Antonym: plug - an object with metal pins that is put into an outlet to make an electrical connection
  • Synonym: socket - a device into which plugs are inserted to connect to electricity
