clothes hanger

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz ˈhæŋər / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈhæŋə / (BrE)
a frame shaped like human shoulders, used for hanging garments to keep them neat
a device for hanging clothes

Examples

She placed her dress on a clothes hanger and hung it in the closet.
Each suit comes with a sturdy clothes hanger for proper storage.
The laundry room was filled with wet shirts on every clothes hanger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hanger - a shaped device for hanging clothes
