drinking glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a container made of glass used for holding liquids to be drunk from
glass container for drinking liquids

Examples

She placed a clean drinking glass on the table before pouring the juice.
He accidentally knocked over his drinking glass and spilled water everywhere.
The restaurant serves wine in a separate drinking glass from the water glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tumbler - a flat-bottomed drinking glass without a stem or handle
  • Antonym: mug - a container for drinking, usually with a handle, and often made of ceramic
