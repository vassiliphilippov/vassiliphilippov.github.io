Tuvalu dollar

1. NOUN

/ tuːˈvɑːluː ˈdɑːlər / (AmE), / tuːˈvɑːluː ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official unit of currency in Tuvalu, used as legal tender alongside the Australian dollar
currency of Tuvalu

Examples

The government of Tuvalu issues commemorative Tuvalu dollar coins for collectors.
You can exchange your Australian dollars for Tuvalu dollar notes at the local bank.
Prices in local markets are listed in both the Tuvalu dollar and the Australian dollar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Australian dollar - the official currency of Australia, also used in Tuvalu
