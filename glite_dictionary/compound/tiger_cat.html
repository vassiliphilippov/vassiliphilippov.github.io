tiger cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡɚ kæt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə kæt / (BrE)
A domestic cat with a striped coat resembling that of a tiger.
domestic cat with a striped coat

Examples

The tiger cat lounged in the sun, her stripes glowing in the afternoon light.
We adopted a playful tiger cat from the animal shelter last week.
Children loved watching the tiger cat chase shadows across the living room floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tabby - a domestic cat with a distinctive striped or mottled coat

2. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡər kæt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə kæt / (BrE)
A medium-sized wildcat native to Central and South America, known for its dark-striped coat.
wildcat with dark-striped coat

Examples

The tiger cat is known for its agility and strikingly patterned coat.
Many conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the tiger cat in the Amazon rainforest.
Unlike domestic cats, the tiger cat prefers dense forests and rarely comes close to human settlements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocelot - a medium-sized spotted wildcat of Central and South America
