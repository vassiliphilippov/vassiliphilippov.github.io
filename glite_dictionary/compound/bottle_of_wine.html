bottle of wine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑt̬l əv waɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl əv waɪn / (BrE)
a glass container holding wine, typically for serving or storage
glass container of wine

Examples

Would you like to share a bottle of wine with us at dinner?
She brought a bottle of wine as a gift for the hosts.
After a long week, he enjoyed relaxing with a bottle of wine.

Synset Examples

He reached into the cabinet for a bottle of wine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wine bottle - a bottle specifically made to hold wine
