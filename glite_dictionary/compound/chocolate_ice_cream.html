chocolate ice cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːklət aɪs kriːm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət aɪs kriːm / (BrE)
Ice cream flavored with chocolate, typically made with cocoa or chocolate as one of the main ingredients.
ice cream with chocolate flavor

Examples

He ordered a bowl of chocolate ice cream for dessert.
The children happily fought over the last scoop of chocolate ice cream.
On a hot day, nothing beats a cone filled with chocolate ice cream.
