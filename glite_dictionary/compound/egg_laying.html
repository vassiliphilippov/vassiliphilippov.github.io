egg laying

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡ ˈleɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɛɡ ˈleɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of producing or depositing eggs, especially by birds or other animals
the act of producing eggs (esp. by birds)

Examples

The farmer studied the egg laying habits of his hens to maximize production.
Seasonal changes can have a major impact on the egg laying of wild birds.
After the egg laying period, the mother carefully guarded the nest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oviposition - the act of laying eggs (technical term)
