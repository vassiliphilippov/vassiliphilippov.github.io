pecan pie

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈkæn paɪ / (AmE), / pɪˈkɑːn paɪ / (BrE)
A sweet pie made with pecans, sugar, corn syrup, butter, and eggs, popular in American cuisine.
a sweet pie with pecans, sugar, and syrup

Examples

She baked a delicious pecan pie for Thanksgiving dinner.
Every year, my grandmother sends us a homemade pecan pie from Georgia.
The pecan pie at that bakery is famous throughout the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pumpkin pie - a sweet pie made from pumpkin puree and spices
