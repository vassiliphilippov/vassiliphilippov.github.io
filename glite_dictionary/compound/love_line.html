love line

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv laɪn / (AmE), / ˈlʌv laɪn / (BrE)
a crease on the palm of the hand, which palmists claim reflects aspects of a person's emotional or romantic nature
palm crease thought to show emotional nature

Examples

According to the fortune teller, my love line suggests a passionate relationship in my future.
She examined the love line on her palm, curious about what it might reveal about her emotions.
Some people believe that a deeply etched love line means a rich romantic life.
