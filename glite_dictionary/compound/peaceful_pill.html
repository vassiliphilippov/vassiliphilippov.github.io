peaceful pill

1. NOUN

/ ˈpisfəl pɪl / (AmE), / ˈpiːsfəl pɪl / (BrE)
something that brings calm or relaxation, especially as a personal remedy for stress or anxiety.
source of calm or relaxation

Examples

Taking long walks in the park has become my peaceful pill.
Yoga is her peaceful pill when life gets overwhelming.
Reading by the fire serves as a peaceful pill at the end of a busy day.

Synset Examples
