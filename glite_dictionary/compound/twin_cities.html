Twin Cities

1. NOUN

/ twɪn ˈsɪtiz / (AmE), / twɪn ˈsɪtiz / (BrE)
a nickname for the cities of Saint Paul and Minneapolis in Minnesota, US, which together form a major metropolitan area
nickname for Saint Paul and Minneapolis

Examples

The Twin Cities are known for their vibrant arts scene and numerous parks.
Many tech companies have opened offices in the Twin Cities in recent years.
Heavy snowfall is common in the Twin Cities during the winter months.
