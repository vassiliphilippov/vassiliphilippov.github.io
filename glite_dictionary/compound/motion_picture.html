motion picture

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊʃən ˌpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˌpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a story presented through a series of moving images, typically with sound, designed for entertainment or informative purposes; a movie or film
a movie; a film

Examples

The documentary motion picture drew attention to global climate change.
He has collected rare motion pictures from the early 20th century.
Attending a motion picture premiere was an exciting experience for the fans.

Synset Examples

they went to a movie every Saturday night
the film was shot on location

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie - a film; a motion picture
  • Synonym: film - a story or event recorded by a camera as a set of moving images; a motion picture

Etymology

From 'motion' (movement) + 'picture' (image), describing images that move.
