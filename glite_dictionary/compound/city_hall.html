city hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti hɔl / (AmE), / ˈsɪti hɔːl / (BrE)
A building that contains the main administrative offices of a municipal government, often where official city business is conducted.
municipal government building

Examples

The mayor will address the public at city hall this afternoon.
Many couples choose to have a simple wedding ceremony at city hall.
The protesters gathered outside city hall to demand changes in local policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: town hall - a building serving as the administrative center of a town
