unlisted security

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈlɪstɪd sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪstɪd sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
a security that is traded in the over-the-counter market and not listed on a formal stock exchange
security not listed on an exchange

Examples

Many small companies issue unlisted securities to raise capital outside major stock exchanges.
Investors should research thoroughly before buying unlisted securities due to lower regulatory oversight.
Trading unlisted security involves higher risks compared to publicly listed shares.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: listed security - a security that is listed on a formal stock exchange
