fire-breathing

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌbriðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌbriːðɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or creature) able to emit fire from the mouth, especially as performed in entertainment or as attributed to mythical dragons.
emitting fire from the mouth

Examples

The fire-breathing dragon defended its treasure from the knights.
A fire-breathing performer captivated the audience at the outdoor festival.
She designed a costume for a fire-breathing character in the school play.

Synset Examples

The circus featured a fire-breathing performer that amazed everyone.
The fire-breathing dragon was a highlight of the festival.
