ski tow

1. NOUN

/ ski toʊ / (AmE), / ski təʊ / (BrE)
a mechanical device for pulling skiers up a slope, usually consisting of a cable or rope powered by a motor
mechanical device for pulling skiers uphill

Examples

The children were excited to use the ski tow for their first trip up the beginner slope.
This small resort only has a single ski tow for guests to reach the top of the hill.
He lost his grip on the ski tow halfway up and had to walk the rest of the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski lift - a chair or gondola system for transporting skiers up a slope
  • Synonym: chairlift - a type of ski lift with chairs suspended from a cable
