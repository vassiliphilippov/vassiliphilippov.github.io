tidy tips

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪdi tɪps / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdi tɪps / (BrE)
a California annual wildflower (Layia platyglossa) with yellow petals tipped with white
California wildflower with yellow and white petals

Examples

The hills turned gold and white in spring as the tidy tips came into bloom.
Many hikers stopped to take pictures of the tidy tips growing along the trail.
Botanists appreciate how tidy tips brighten California’s grasslands with their cheerful flowers.
