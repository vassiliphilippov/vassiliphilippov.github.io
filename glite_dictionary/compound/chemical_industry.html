chemical industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of industry involved in the production and processing of chemicals and chemical products
sector producing chemicals and chemical products

Examples

The chemical industry plays a crucial role in the modern global economy.
Recent regulations have impacted how the chemical industry manages waste and emissions.
Many graduates find employment opportunities within the chemical industry after completing their studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmaceutical industry - the sector producing medicinal drugs
  • Degree Lower: petrochemical industry - the sector focusing on chemicals derived from petroleum and gas
