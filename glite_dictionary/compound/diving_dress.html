diving dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
a heavy, airtight suit supplied with air, worn by divers for underwater work, especially before modern scuba equipment
weighted airtight suit for divers

Examples

The museum displayed an old diving dress used by early underwater explorers.
He struggled to walk on deck in his heavy diving dress before beginning the descent.
Modern scuba gear has replaced the traditional diving dress in most underwater operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diving suit - protective clothing for underwater diving
