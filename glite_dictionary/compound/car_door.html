car door

1. NOUN

/ kɑr dɔr / (AmE), / kɑː dɔː / (BrE)
a door that is part of a car, used for entering or exiting the vehicle
door of a car

Examples

She opened the car door and stepped out onto the street.
Please make sure the car door is locked before leaving.
The child accidentally slammed his fingers in the car door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door - a hinged, sliding, or revolving barrier at the entrance to a building, room, or vehicle
