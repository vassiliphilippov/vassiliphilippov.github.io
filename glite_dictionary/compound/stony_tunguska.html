Stony Tunguska

1. PROPN

/ ˈstoʊni tʊnˈɡuskə / (AmE), / ˈstəʊni tʊnˈɡʊskə / (BrE)
a river in Siberia, Russia, flowing northwest and joining the Yenisei River as a tributary
river in Siberia, Russia

Examples

The Stony Tunguska River is known for its remote and pristine wilderness.
Scientists have conducted research along the Stony Tunguska because of its environmental importance.
The Stony Tunguska joins the Yenisei, one of Russia's largest river systems.
