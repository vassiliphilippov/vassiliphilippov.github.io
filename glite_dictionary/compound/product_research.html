product research

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.dʌkt rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈprɒd.ʌkt rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the process of investigating and analyzing consumer needs and preferences to determine desired features or characteristics of a product or service
investigation of product features desired by consumers

Examples

Our company conducted extensive product research before launching the new smartphone.
Effective product research helps businesses understand what their customers truly want.
She presented the findings from her product research at the marketing meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market research - general research into consumer preferences and markets
