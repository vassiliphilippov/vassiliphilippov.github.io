true jasmine

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / truː ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
A climbing deciduous shrub (Jasminum officinale) with fragrant white, yellow, or red flowers, used in making perfume and flavoring tea.
fragrant climbing shrub, Jasminum officinale

Examples

The garden was filled with the scent of true jasmine blooming along the old stone wall.
Perfume brands often highlight true jasmine as a luxurious ingredient in their fragrances.
She added dried true jasmine flowers to her tea for a delicate aroma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jasmine - a shrub with fragrant flowers, especially Jasminum species
