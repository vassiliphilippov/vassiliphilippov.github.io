chonker dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑŋkər dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒŋkə dɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a dog that is notably overweight or large
overweight or large dog (informal)
informal slang

Examples

My roommate's chonker dog takes up half the couch when he sprawls out for a nap.
The vet recommended a diet for their chonker dog to help it lose weight.
On social media, photos of his chonker dog went viral for its adorable size.

Synset Examples

He carries around his chonker dog everywhere he goes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fat dog - a dog that is overweight
  • Antonym: skinny dog - a dog that is thin or underweight

Etymology

From 'chonker', an internet slang variant of 'chunky', used humorously to describe animals with large or fat bodies.
