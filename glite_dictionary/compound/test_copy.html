test copy

1. NOUN

/ tɛst ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / tɛst ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
an impression of printed material made to check for errors before producing the final version
trial print to check for errors

Examples

The editor marked corrections on the test copy before approving the final print.
Please review the test copy for any typographical mistakes.
A test copy is always produced before mass printing to ensure quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proof - a trial print used for checking errors in typesetting or printing
