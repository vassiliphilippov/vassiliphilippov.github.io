hired man

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪərd mæn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd mæn / (BrE)
a man employed as a laborer, especially on a farm or ranch
male farm or ranch laborer

Examples

The hired man helped repair the fences after the storm swept through the valley.
They depended on the hired man to take care of the livestock during the planting season.
Our grandfather worked as a hired man on a dairy farm in his youth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmhand - a laborer employed on a farm
