autograph album

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtəˌɡræf ˈælbəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəɡrɑːf ˈælbəm / (BrE)
a book with blank pages for collecting autographs, typically from celebrities or notable individuals
book for collecting autographs

Examples

She asked the singer to sign her autograph album after the concert.
His autograph album contains signatures from many famous authors.
As a child, she started keeping an autograph album to remember the people she met.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: album - a book with blank pages for photographs, stamps, or collections
