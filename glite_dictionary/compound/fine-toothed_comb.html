fine-toothed comb

1. NOUN

/ faɪn tuːθt koʊm / (AmE), / faɪn tuːθt kəʊm / (BrE)
A comb with teeth set very close together, used for precise grooming or removal of small items such as lice.
comb with closely spaced teeth

Examples

The nurse used a fine-toothed comb to check the child’s hair for lice.
If you examine the contract with a fine-toothed comb, you won't miss any details.
My mother always kept a fine-toothed comb in her purse for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - A device with a row of teeth for untangling or arranging hair.
