feature of speech

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːt͡ʃɚ əv spiːt͡ʃ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
(Linguistics) A distinctive characteristic of a linguistic unit that distinguishes it from others of the same kind.
distinctive linguistic characteristic

Examples

Stress is an important feature of speech in differentiating words in English.
Nasalization can be a significant feature of speech in some languages.
Aspiration is a feature of speech that distinguishes certain consonants.
