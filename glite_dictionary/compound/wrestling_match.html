wrestling match

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛs.lɪŋ mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛslɪŋ matʃ / (BrE)
a sporting contest in which two people wrestle according to set rules
a contest between wrestlers

Examples

The crowd cheered loudly during the final wrestling match of the evening.
He trained for months to prepare for his first professional wrestling match.
Tickets for the championship wrestling match sold out within hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bout - a contest or trial of strength, often in wrestling or boxing
