melony shade

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛləni ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛləni ʃeɪd / (BrE)
a color that resembles the pinkish-red flesh of a watermelon
a color like watermelon flesh

Examples

The artist used a melony shade to add warmth to the summer sunset in her painting.
He chose curtains in a melony shade to brighten up the living room.
A melony shade of lipstick complemented her outfit perfectly.

Synset Examples

Her dress was a beautiful melony shade that caught everyone's attention.

Etymology

From 'melon' + 'shade', indicating a color similar to the flesh of a melon, especially watermelon.
