cherry-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɛri saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a cherry
about the size of a cherry

Examples

The chef placed a cherry-sized tomato on each plate for garnish.
She found a cherry-sized lump on her arm and decided to see a doctor.
The artist sculpted cherry-sized beads to decorate the necklace.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pea-sized - having the approximate size of a pea
  • Degree Higher: grape-sized - having the approximate size of a grape
