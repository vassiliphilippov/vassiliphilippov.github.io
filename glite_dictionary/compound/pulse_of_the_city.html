pulse of the city

1. NOUN

/ pʌls əv ðə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / pʌls əv ðə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the lively or energetic atmosphere and activity found in a city, especially as experienced or perceived by people within it
the city's lively or energetic atmosphere

Examples

Wandering through the busy streets, she felt the pulse of the city beating around her.
Late at night, the music and lights revealed the pulse of the city in every corner.
Writers and artists often draw inspiration from the pulse of the city as it changes through the seasons.

Synset Examples

The pulse of the city is vibrant and alive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartbeat of the city - the most vital or lively part of a city
