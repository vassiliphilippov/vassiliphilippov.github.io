outdoor woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who enjoys or regularly participates in outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, or sports
woman fond of outdoor activities

Examples

As an outdoor woman, she prefers weekends spent hiking in the mountains over time in the city.
The travel magazine featured an outdoor woman who had kayaked down the Amazon River.
Her friends admire her as an outdoor woman who inspires others to try new adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdoorsman - a man who enjoys or is skilled at outdoor activities
