liquid-fueled

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪkwɪd ˌfjuld / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˌfjʊəld / (BrE)
Powered or operated by liquid fuel.
powered by liquid fuel

Examples

The engineers tested a liquid-fueled rocket engine to achieve higher efficiency.
Many space launch vehicles are liquid-fueled for better control during flight.
The company switched to a liquid-fueled generator for its backup power system.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solid-fueled - powered by solid fuel
