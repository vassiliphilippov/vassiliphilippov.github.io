freezingly polite

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriːzɪŋli pəˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfriːzɪŋli pəˈlaɪt / (BrE)
Excessively formal and cold in one's politeness, creating an unfriendly or uncomfortable atmosphere.
excessively formal and coldly polite

Examples

She gave a freezingly polite nod and excused herself without a smile.
His freezingly polite reply made it clear he was not interested in continuing the conversation.
Despite her freezingly polite tone, the criticism was palpable.

Synset Examples

His freezingly polite manner made everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold - emotionless or lacking warmth in manner
  • Antonym: warm - friendly and welcoming in manner
