king devil

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
A European hawkweed (Hieracium florentinum) introduced in northeastern United States, often regarded as a troublesome weed.
a troublesome European hawkweed weed

Examples

The field was overrun with king devil, making it difficult for crops to grow.
Gardeners are advised to remove king devil as soon as it appears.
The bright yellow flowers of king devil are attractive but invasive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawkweed - any plant of the genus Hieracium
