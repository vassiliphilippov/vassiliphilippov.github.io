bottle green

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬l ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ɡriːn / (BrE)
a dark to moderate shade of green, often resembling the color of green glass bottles
dark green color

Examples

She wore a stunning bottle green dress to the party.
The painter used bottle green for the leaves in the mural.
Their new sofa is upholstered in a rich bottle green fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forest green - a dark green color
  • Degree Lower: olive green - a yellowish-green color, lighter than bottle green

Etymology

From the color of traditional green glass bottles.
