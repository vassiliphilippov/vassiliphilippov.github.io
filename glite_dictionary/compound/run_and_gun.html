to run and gun

1. VERB

/ rʌn ənd ɡʌn / (AmE), / rʌn ənd ɡʌn / (BrE)
to act quickly and aggressively, especially as a style in sports or games characterized by rapid movement and attacking play
act quickly and aggressively, esp. in sports/games

Examples

The team's offense relies on a run and gun approach to overwhelm opponents early in the game.
In professional basketball, some coaches prefer to run and gun instead of using a structured offense.
You need quick reflexes and sharp aim to master the run and gun style in this video game.

Synset Examples

In this game, you have to run and gun to survive.
He prefers the run and gun style of gameplay.
The team's strategy was to run and gun for quick points.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast-paced - involving rapid movement or action
  • Antonym: defensive - focused on resisting attack rather than acting aggressively
