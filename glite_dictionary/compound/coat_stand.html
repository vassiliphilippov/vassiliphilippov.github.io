coat stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊt ˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊt ˌstænd / (BrE)
A piece of furniture consisting of an upright pole with pegs or hooks for hanging coats and other clothing.
upright stand for hanging coats

Examples

She placed her wet umbrella and jacket on the coat stand by the door.
The antique coat stand in the hallway adds character to the entrance.
Guests can hang their coats on the coat stand when they arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat stand - an upright stand for hanging hats or coats
