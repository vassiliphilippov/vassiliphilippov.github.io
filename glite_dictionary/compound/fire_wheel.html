fire wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌwiːl / (BrE)
an annual plant of the central United States with showy, long-stalked yellow flower heads marked with scarlet or purple centers
flowering plant with scarlet-centered yellow blooms

Examples

The fire wheel brightened the prairie with its vibrant yellow and scarlet blooms.
Gardeners often plant fire wheels to add a splash of late-summer color to their beds.
A field of fire wheel in full bloom is a breathtaking sight in the Midwest.
