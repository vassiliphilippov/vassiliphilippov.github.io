rendering machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛndərɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈrɛndərɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to apply a layer of plaster or cement to walls, especially in construction work
machine for applying wall render

Examples

The construction crew used a rendering machine to quickly cover the exterior walls with cement.
Hiring a rendering machine saved them hours of manual plastering work on the project.
Advances in technology have made the rendering machine more efficient and user-friendly for builders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastering machine - machine for applying plaster to surfaces
