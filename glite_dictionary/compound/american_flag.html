American flag

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən flæɡ / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən flaɡ / (BrE)
the national flag of the United States of America, typically consisting of thirteen stripes and fifty stars
national flag of the United States

Examples

The American flag was flying proudly over the Capitol building.
She folded the American flag with careful precision after the ceremony.
Every classroom in the school displays an American flag near the chalkboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Stars and Stripes - another name for the flag of the United States
