sable brush

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪbəl brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈseɪbəl brʌʃ / (BrE)
An artist's brush made with hairs from the sable, valued for its fine point and flexibility.
artist's brush made from sable hairs

Examples

The artist used a sable brush to add fine details to the portrait.
A high-quality sable brush can make a significant difference in watercolor painting.
She carefully cleaned each sable brush after finishing her artwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paintbrush - a brush used for applying paint

Etymology

From 'sable' (the animal known for its fine fur) and 'brush.'
