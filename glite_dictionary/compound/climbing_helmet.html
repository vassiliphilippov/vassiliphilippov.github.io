climbing helmet

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛlmət / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a protective helmet designed to safeguard a climber's head from falling debris and impacts during climbs
protective helmet for climbing

Examples

Always wear a climbing helmet when tackling difficult outdoor routes to protect yourself from falling rocks.
She forgot her climbing helmet at home, so she had to borrow one from the gear shop.
Modern climbing helmets are lighter and offer better ventilation than older designs.

Synset Examples

Climbers may decide whether to wear a climbing helmet based on a number of factors such as the type of climb being attempted, concerns about weight, reductions in agility, added encumbrances, or simple vanity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - protective head covering, generally for safety
