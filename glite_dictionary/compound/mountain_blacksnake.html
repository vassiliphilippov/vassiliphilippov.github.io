mountain blacksnake

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈblækˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈblækˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a large, harmless, shiny black snake found in North America, especially in mountainous regions
a harmless North American black snake

Examples

We spotted a mountain blacksnake slithering quietly across the hiking trail.
The mountain blacksnake is often mistaken for a dangerous species, but it is completely harmless to humans.
Farmers appreciate the mountain blacksnake because it helps control rodent populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black rat snake - a large, nonvenomous black snake native to North America
