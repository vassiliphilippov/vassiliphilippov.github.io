widdle mess

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪd.əl mɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɪd.əl mɛs / (BrE)
an informal or childish term for a small or minor mess
a small or minor mess (informal, childish)
informal

Examples

The puppy made a widdle mess on the kitchen floor last night.
Sorry about the widdle mess; I'll clean it up right away.
She giggled when her toddler pointed at the spilled cereal and called it a widdle mess.

Synset Examples

The puppy made a widdle mess on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little mess - a small or minor mess

Etymology

Widdle is a baby-talk alteration of 'little'.
