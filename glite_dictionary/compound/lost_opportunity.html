lost opportunity

1. NOUN

/ lɔst ɑpərˈtunəti / (AmE), / lɒst ɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
A chance for success or progress that has been missed or is no longer available.
a missed chance for success

Examples

He regretted the lost opportunity to study abroad when he was younger.
The merger fell through, and many saw it as a lost opportunity for growth.
Investing early is important to avoid lost opportunities in the stock market.

Synset Examples

In her art, the acher represents lost opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missed opportunity - a missed chance or prospect
