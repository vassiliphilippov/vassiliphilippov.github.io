game-winning field goal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪm ˌwɪnɪŋ ˈfiːld ˌɡoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪm ˌwɪnɪŋ ˈfiːld ˌɡəʊl / (BrE)
a field goal that secures a team's victory, usually by putting them ahead in the final moments of a game
decisive field goal leading to victory

Examples

The team erupted in cheers after he kicked the game-winning field goal as time expired.
Her game-winning field goal will be remembered as a turning point in the championship.
Despite the pressure, he successfully made the game-winning field goal from forty yards out.

Synset Examples

The kicker made the game-winning field goal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field goal - a goal scored by kicking the ball through the uprights in football
