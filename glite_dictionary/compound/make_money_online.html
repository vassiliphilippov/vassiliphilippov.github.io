to make money online

1. VERB

/ meɪk ˈmʌni ɑnˈlaɪn / (AmE), / meɪk ˈmʌni ɒnˈlaɪn / (BrE)
to earn income by conducting business or providing services via the internet
earn income through the internet

Examples

Many people try to make money online by starting their own businesses or selling products.
She managed to make money online by offering freelance graphic design services.
There are dozens of legitimate ways to make money online, but some require more effort than others.
