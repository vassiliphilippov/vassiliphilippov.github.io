bronze medal

1. NOUN

/ brɑnz ˈmɛd.əl / (AmE), / brɒnz ˈmɛd.əl / (BrE)
a medal made of bronze, typically awarded for third place in a competition or event
third-place medal in competitions

Examples

She proudly displayed her bronze medal after finishing third in the race.
After a tough match, the home team secured the bronze medal in the tournament.
Winning a bronze medal at the Olympics is a significant achievement for any athlete.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gold medal - a medal awarded for first place in a competition
  • Degree Higher: silver medal - a medal awarded for second place in a competition
