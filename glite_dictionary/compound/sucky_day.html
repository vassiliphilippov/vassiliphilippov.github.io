sucky day

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌki deɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌki deɪ / (BrE)
An informal expression for a day that is unpleasant, frustrating, or disappointing.
an unpleasant or disappointing day
informal american

Examples

After getting stuck in traffic and losing her wallet, it turned into a really sucky day.
Whenever I have a sucky day at work, I treat myself to ice cream afterward.
He called to vent about his sucky day and how nothing went according to plan.

Synset Examples

I had a suckful day at work today.
She had a sucky day at work and just wanted to go home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad day - a day on which many things go wrong
  • Antonym: good day - a day on which many things go right

Etymology

Formed from the slang adjective 'sucky' (from 'suck', meaning to be bad or unpleasant) and 'day'.
