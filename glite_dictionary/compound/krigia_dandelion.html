Krigia dandelion

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːdʒə ˈdændəˌlaɪən / (AmE), / ˈkriːdʒiə ˈdændɪˌlaɪən / (BrE)
A small yellow-flowered herb of the genus Krigia, resembling dandelions, native to central and southeastern United States.
yellow-flowered herb resembling dandelion (US)

Examples

We spotted a cluster of Krigia dandelion growing near the woodland edge.
The Krigia dandelion is often mistaken for true dandelions due to its similar yellow blooms.
Botanists identified the rare Krigia dandelion during their field study in Georgia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandelion - a common wild plant with bright yellow flowers and notched leaves
