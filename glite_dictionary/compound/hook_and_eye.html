hook and eye

1. NOUN

/ hʊk ənd aɪ / (AmE), / hʊk ənd aɪ / (BrE)
A small fastening device for clothing, consisting of a metal hook that fits into an eye or loop.
clothing fastener with hook and loop

Examples

She replaced the missing hook and eye on her dress before the event.
The vintage coat was secured with a row of hook and eye fasteners.
Can you help me close the hook and eye at the back of my blouse?
