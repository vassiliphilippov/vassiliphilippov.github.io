tung oil

1. NOUN

/ tʌŋ ɔɪl / (AmE), / tʌŋ ɔɪl / (BrE)
A yellow oil obtained from the seeds of the tung tree, used chiefly as a drying oil in paints, varnishes, and wood finishes.
oil from the tung tree, used in finishes

Examples

The craftsman applied a coat of tung oil to the table to enhance its natural beauty.
Because tung oil dries quickly, it is ideal for use in wooden furniture and flooring.
Many artists prefer tung oil as a finish for protecting and enriching wood surfaces.

Etymology

From Chinese tōng yóu, referring to the oil from the tung tree (Vernicia fordii).
