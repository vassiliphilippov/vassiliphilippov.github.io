follicly challenged

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑː.lɪ.kli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈfɒ.lɪ.kli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
(humorous, informal) Lacking hair or having thinning hair; bald.
humorous term for bald or balding
informal

Examples

Many people become follicly challenged as they get older.
He made a joke about being follicly challenged at the annual office party.
Despite being follicly challenged, he wears his baldness with pride.

Synset Examples

Many people are follicly challenged as they age.
He felt confident despite being follicly challenged.
He always joked about being follicly challenged.
He embraced being follicly challenged with humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp

Etymology

Humorous adaptation combining 'follicle' (from Latin 'folliculus', small bag) and 'challenged' (in analogy with euphemisms like 'vertically challenged').
