potter wasp

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑtər wɑsp / (AmE), / ˈpɒtə wɒsp / (BrE)
A solitary wasp that builds vase-shaped mud nests for its eggs.
solitary wasp that makes mud nests

Examples

A potter wasp built its distinctive mud nest on the side of our shed.
The potter wasp carefully shaped the entrance to its vase-like nest.
Many gardeners admire the potter wasp for helping control pest insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mud dauber - a wasp that builds nests out of mud
