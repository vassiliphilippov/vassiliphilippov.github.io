Jersey City

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɜrzi ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːzi ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a city in northeastern New Jersey, United States, situated opposite Manhattan across the Hudson River.
city in northeastern New Jersey, USA

Examples

They decided to move to Jersey City because of its proximity to Manhattan.
The waterfront in Jersey City offers stunning views of the New York skyline.
Many new restaurants have opened in Jersey City over the past year.

Etymology

Named after the state of New Jersey and the word 'city', established in the early 19th century.
