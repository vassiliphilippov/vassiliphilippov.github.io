torchwood family

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔrtʃˌwʊd ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃˌwʊd ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of resinous or aromatic chiefly tropical shrubs or trees, scientifically known as Burseraceae
family of resinous tropical shrubs or trees

Examples
