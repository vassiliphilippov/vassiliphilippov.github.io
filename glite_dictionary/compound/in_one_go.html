in one go

1. X

/ ɪn wʌn ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɪn wʌn ɡəʊ / (BrE)
all at once; in a single attempt or action, without pausing or stopping
all at once; without stopping

Examples

She finished the giant jigsaw puzzle in one go without taking any breaks.
It’s impossible to eat that much ice cream in one go, no matter how hungry you are.
He watched all eight episodes of the series in one go on Saturday.

Synset Examples
