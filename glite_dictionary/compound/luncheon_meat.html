luncheon meat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌn.tʃən mit / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.tʃən miːt / (BrE)
a type of cooked, processed meat, usually sold in a can or loaf, that is sliced and served cold
cooked, sliced processed meat

Examples

She made a sandwich with luncheon meat and cheese for a quick lunch.
After the picnic, there were some slices of luncheon meat left over.
Many people serve luncheon meat on crackers as a simple snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold cut - a slice of cold cooked or processed meat
  • Synonym: spam - a brand of canned cooked meat; also used generically for similar products
