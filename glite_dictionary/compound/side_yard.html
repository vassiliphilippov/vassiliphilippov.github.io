side yard

1. NOUN

/ saɪd jɑrd / (AmE), / saɪd jɑːd / (BrE)
an area of land at either side of a house, often part of the property's yard
area at side of a house

Examples

The children played in the side yard while their parents sat on the porch.
She planted roses along the fence in the side yard.
The side yard offers enough space for a small garden or play area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backyard - an area at the back of a house
  • Synonym: front yard - an area at the front of a house
