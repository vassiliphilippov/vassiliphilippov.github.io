Saratoga spittlebug

1. NOUN

/ səˈræt̬əˌɡoʊ ˈspɪt̬əlˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / səˈrætəˌɡəʊ ˈspɪtlˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a species of spittlebug (Aphrophora saratogensis) that feeds on pine trees in the northern United States
a North American spittlebug species

Examples

The Saratoga spittlebug is notorious for damaging pine trees in northeastern forests.
Researchers study the Saratoga spittlebug to better understand its impact on pine ecosystems.
I found a bush covered in the foamy secretions typical of the Saratoga spittlebug.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spittlebug - an insect that produces frothy secretions as a nymph
