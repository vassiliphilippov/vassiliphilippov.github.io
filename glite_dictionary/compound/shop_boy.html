shop boy

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp bɔɪ / (AmE), / ʃɒp bɔɪ / (BrE)
A young male who works as a shop assistant.
young male shop assistant

Examples

The shop boy greeted customers with a friendly smile every morning.
As a shop boy in his uncle’s store, he learned the basics of retail.
They hired a new shop boy to help restock the shelves during the holiday rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shop assistant - a person who serves customers in a shop
