commercial message

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɜːr.ʃəl ˈmɛs.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl ˈmes.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an advertisement that is broadcast on radio or television, paid for by a sponsor
advertisement on radio or TV

Examples

The radio program was interrupted by a commercial message for car insurance.
During the television show, several commercial messages promoted new household products.
Please stay tuned after these commercial messages for more of your favorite music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertisement - a public notice promoting a product, service, or event
  • Antonym: public service announcement - a message broadcast for public interest, not for commercial gain
