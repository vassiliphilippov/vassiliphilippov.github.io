devil's food

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz fuːd / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz fuːd / (BrE)
A rich, very dark chocolate cake, typically moist and made with cocoa or melted chocolate.
rich, very dark chocolate cake

Examples

We served a delicious devil's food at the birthday party, and everyone loved how rich it was.
My grandmother bakes the best devil's food every holiday season as our family tradition.
He requested a devil's food cake for his graduation celebration.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: angel food cake - a light, airy sponge cake made with egg whites

Etymology

Probably referring to the 'sinfully' rich or dark nature of the cake, in contrast to 'angel food cake.'
