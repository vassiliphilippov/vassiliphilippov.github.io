unhealthy obsession

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhɛlθi əbˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθi əbˈsɛʃən / (BrE)
an excessive and harmful fixation or preoccupation with someone or something
harmful excessive fixation

Examples

Her unhealthy obsession with perfection was starting to affect her relationships.
He developed an unhealthy obsession with his weight, checking the scale multiple times a day.
The documentary explores society's unhealthy obsession with celebrity culture.

Synset Examples

She viewed his idolatry as unhealthy obsession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsession - an idea or thought that continually preoccupies a person's mind
