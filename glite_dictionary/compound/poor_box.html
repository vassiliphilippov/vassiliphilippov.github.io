poor box

1. NOUN

/ pʊr bɑks / (AmE), / pɔː bɒks / (BrE)
a box placed especially in a church for collecting money to be given to the poor
box in church for alms collection

Examples

At the entrance of the church, a small poor box invited donations from parishioners.
She dropped a few coins into the poor box to help support families in need.
The vicar regularly emptied the poor box to distribute its contents to the less fortunate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alms box - a box for receiving donations for the poor
