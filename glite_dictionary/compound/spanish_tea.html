Spanish tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈspænɪʃ ti / (AmE), / ˈspanɪʃ tiː / (BrE)
A rank-smelling tropical American plant of the amaranth family (pigweed), known for its pungent odor.
a rank-smelling tropical American pigweed

Examples

The strong odor of Spanish tea filled the garden on warm summer days.
Some local herbalists use Spanish tea as a traditional remedy, despite its pungent smell.
We found fields overrun with Spanish tea growing wild along the country roads.
