content creator

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tɛnt kriˈeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛnt kriˈeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who creates and publishes original material online, especially on social media or video platforms
person who produces content online

Examples

Many young people aspire to become a content creator on platforms like YouTube or TikTok.
The brand partnered with a popular content creator to promote its new product.
Being a full-time content creator requires creativity, consistency, and engagement with your audience.

Synset Examples

SlopTube is filled with content creators sharing their daily lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: influencer - a person who affects the opinions or behavior of others, especially on social media
