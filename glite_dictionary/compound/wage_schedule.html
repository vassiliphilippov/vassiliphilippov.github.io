wage schedule

1. NOUN

/ weɪdʒ ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / weɪdʒ ˈʃɛdjuːl / (BrE)
a list or table showing the wages paid for different jobs or positions within an organization
list showing wages for different jobs

Examples

The company updated its wage schedule to ensure fair pay across all departments.
Negotiations focused on adjusting the wage schedule for entry-level employees.
According to the wage schedule, skilled technicians earn higher hourly rates than trainees.
