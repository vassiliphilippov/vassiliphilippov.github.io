chicken farm

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where chickens are raised for their meat or eggs, usually for commercial sale.
farm where chickens are raised for sale

Examples

The family expanded their chicken farm to meet growing demand for local eggs.
Biosecurity is important on any chicken farm to prevent disease outbreaks.
She grew up on a chicken farm in rural Arkansas and knows a lot about raising poultry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poultry farm - farm where domesticated birds, especially chickens, are raised
