dramatic art

1. NOUN

/ drəˈmætɪk ɑrt / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk ɑːt / (BrE)
the art and technique of writing, producing, and performing plays for the theater
the art of writing and producing plays

Examples

She studied dramatic art at the famous conservatory in London.
Many universities offer degrees in dramatic art to train future actors and playwrights.
His deep appreciation for dramatic art led him to direct numerous stage productions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drama - the specific genre of literature intended for performance
  • Synonym: theater - the art or activity of performing plays
