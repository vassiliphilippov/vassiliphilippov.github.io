cross-country riding

1. NOUN

/ krɑs ˈkʌntri ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / krɒs ˈkʌntri ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of riding horses across natural terrain, often over obstacles, to demonstrate horsemanship
equestrian riding across natural terrain and obstacles

Examples

She spent the afternoon cross-country riding with her friends through the open fields.
The eventing competition includes a challenging cross-country riding segment.
Before the show, he practiced cross-country riding to improve his jumping skills over natural obstacles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show jumping - the sport of riding horses over a course of fences and other obstacles in an arena
