to pump water

1. VERB

/ pʌmp ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / pʌmp ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
to extract or move water using a pump, typically by mechanical means
extract or move water using a pump

Examples

Farmers often pump water from wells to irrigate their crops.
The city had to pump water out of the flooded subway tunnels.
They used a motor to pump water uphill for the reservoir.

Synset Examples

They plan to pump water from the lake for irrigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drain - to remove liquid from something, usually completely or partially
