used-car lot

1. NOUN

/ juːzd kɑɹ lɑt / (AmE), / juːzd kɑː lɒt / (BrE)
A premises, usually an open area, where used cars are displayed and sold by a dealer.
area where used cars are sold

Examples

She found a great deal on a sedan at the local used-car lot.
Many used-car lots offer financing options to attract more buyers.
He works as a salesman at the biggest used-car lot in town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car dealership - a business that sells new or used cars
