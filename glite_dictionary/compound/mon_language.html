Mon language

1. NOUN

/ mɑn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɒn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Mon-Khmer language spoken by the Mon people of Myanmar and Thailand.
language of the Mon people

Examples

The Mon language has a rich history dating back over a thousand years.
Many ancient inscriptions in Myanmar are written in Mon language.
She is studying the Mon language to learn more about her heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mon - an ethnic group of Myanmar and Thailand

Etymology

Named after the Mon people; 'Mon' from the native autonym, with 'language' from Old French 'langage'.
