homemade kimchi

1. NOUN

/ hoʊmˌmeɪd kɪmˈtʃi / (AmE), / həʊmˌmeɪd kɪmˈtʃi / (BrE)
Kimchi that is prepared at home, typically consisting of fermented vegetables and spices according to personal or traditional recipes.
kimchi made at home

Examples

Many families have their own recipes for homemade kimchi handed down through generations.
She served bowls of rice topped with her delicious homemade kimchi at the dinner party.
Making homemade kimchi can be a fun and rewarding kitchen project.

Synset Examples

Homemade kimchi can elevate any Korean dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kimchi - a spicy Korean dish of fermented vegetables
