government building

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvəmənt ˈbɪl.dɪŋ / (BrE)
a building that is used as the offices of a branch or agency of the government
building used for government offices

Examples

Security outside the government building was increased after the protests.
Many important documents are archived within the walls of this historic government building.
The city council will meet in the new government building downtown next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courthouse - a building where legal cases are heard
  • Synonym: capitol - a building where a legislative body meets
