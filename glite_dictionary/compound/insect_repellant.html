insect repellant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.sɛkt rɪˈpɛl.ənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sekt rɪˈpel.ənt / (BrE)
a chemical substance applied to skin, clothing, or other surfaces to discourage insects from landing or biting
chemical that repels insects

Examples

Please remember to apply insect repellant before you go hiking in the woods.
The shop sells a natural insect repellant that is safe for children.
She forgot her insect repellant and got bitten by mosquitoes all evening.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insecticide - a substance used to kill insects
