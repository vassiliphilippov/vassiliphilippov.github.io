teacher's pet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːtʃɚz ˌpɛt / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃəz ˌpɛt / (BrE)
A student who is particularly favored by a teacher, often receiving special attention or privileges.
a teacher's favorite student

Examples

Everyone could tell that Sarah was the teacher's pet because she always got extra help after class.
Being the teacher's pet made him unpopular with some of his classmates.
She didn't want to be called the teacher's pet, so she tried not to stand out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - a person or thing regarded with particular favor or preference
  • Antonym: outcast - someone excluded from a group or society
