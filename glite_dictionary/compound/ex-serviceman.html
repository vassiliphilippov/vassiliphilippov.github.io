ex-serviceman

1. NOUN

/ ɛkˈsɜrvɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ɛkˈsɜːvɪsˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who has previously served in the armed forces
man who served in the military

Examples

After retirement, the ex-serviceman became active in his local community.
The government offers special benefits for ex-servicemen and their families.
As an ex-serviceman, he often shared stories of his time in the navy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veteran - a person who has served in the armed forces

Etymology

From ex- (former) and serviceman (person serving in the armed forces).
