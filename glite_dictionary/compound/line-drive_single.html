line-drive single

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪn dɹaɪv ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn dɹaɪv ˈsɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
In baseball, a base hit achieved by striking the ball sharply and directly into the field on a line drive, allowing the batter to reach first base.
baseball: single from a line drive

Examples

He hit a line-drive single to left field in the third inning.
The rookie's line-drive single brought in the winning run.
With two outs, she managed a line-drive single past the shortstop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single - a base hit that allows the batter to reach first base
  • Synonym: line drive - a baseball hit where the ball is struck sharply and travels in a nearly straight line
