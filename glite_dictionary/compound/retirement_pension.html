retirement pension

1. NOUN

/ rəˈtaɪərmənt ˈpɛnʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt ˈpɛnʃən / (BrE)
a regular payment made to a person who has retired from work, typically provided by the government or an employer
regular payment after retirement

Examples

After working for forty years, Linda received a retirement pension from her employer.
Many people rely on their retirement pension as their main source of income in old age.
The government increased the minimum retirement pension to help support senior citizens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pension - a regular payment made during retirement
