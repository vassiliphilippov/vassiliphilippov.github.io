museumed out

1. ADJ

/ mjuːˌziːəmd aʊt / (AmE), / mjuːˌziːəmd aʊt / (BrE)
tired or exhausted after visiting too many museums, especially in a short period
exhausted from visiting many museums
informal

Examples

After two days in Paris, I was completely museumed out and needed a break from sightseeing.
If you're museumed out, we could spend the afternoon at a park instead.
She admitted she was museumed out by the end of the school trip.
