soapwort gentian

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊpwɚt ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpwɜːt ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Gentiana saponaria) with erect stems and leaves and flower buds resembling those of soapwort, commonly found in wet woodlands
North American woodland perennial plant

Examples

The soapwort gentian blooms late in the summer, adding a splash of blue to the woodland understory.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where soapwort gentian is found.
She spotted a cluster of soapwort gentian along the edge of the marsh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentian - any plant of the genus Gentiana, usually with showy blue flowers
