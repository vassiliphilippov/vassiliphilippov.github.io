parking ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ ˌtɪkət / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ ˌtɪkɪt / (BrE)
an official notice given for parking a vehicle in a restricted or prohibited area, usually requiring the payment of a fine
official notice for illegal parking

Examples

She found a parking ticket on her windshield when she returned to her car.
I've never received a parking ticket before, so I wasn't sure how to pay the fine.
He got a parking ticket for leaving his car in a no-parking zone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine - a sum of money to be paid as punishment for an offense
