beer can

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr kæn / (AmE), / ˈbɪə kæn / (BrE)
A metal container designed to hold and dispense beer.
a can holding beer

Examples

He opened a cold beer can while watching the football game.
Recycling beer cans helps reduce waste and benefits the environment.
She collected vintage beer cans from around the world.
