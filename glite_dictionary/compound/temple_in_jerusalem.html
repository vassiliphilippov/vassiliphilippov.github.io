Temple in Jerusalem

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɛmpəl ɪn dʒəˈruːsələm / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpəl ɪn dʒəˈruːsələm / (BrE)
Any of three ancient temples in Jerusalem that served as the main center for Jewish worship, the last of which was destroyed in AD 70.
ancient Jewish temples in Jerusalem

Examples

The destruction of the Temple in Jerusalem remains a pivotal event in Jewish history.
Pilgrims still visit the Western Wall, the last remnant of the Temple in Jerusalem.
Many ancient texts describe the rituals performed at the Temple in Jerusalem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wailing Wall - A remaining wall of the Second Temple, a site of Jewish prayer in Jerusalem
