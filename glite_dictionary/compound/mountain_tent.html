mountain tent

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn tɛnt / (BrE)
A lightweight, portable tent with a built-in floor and zippered flaps, designed for use in mountainous or harsh outdoor environments.
lightweight waterproof tent for mountains

Examples

The climbers set up a mountain tent to protect themselves from the harsh weather.
A good mountain tent should be durable, lightweight, and easy to assemble.
He packed his mountain tent before heading off on the hiking expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of fabric
