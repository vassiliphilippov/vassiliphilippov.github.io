known for

1. ADJ

/ noʊn fɔr / (AmE), / nəʊn fə / (BrE)
famous or recognized because of a particular quality, activity, or achievement.
famous or recognized for something

Examples

This city is known for its vibrant nightlife and delicious food.
She is known for her generosity and willingness to help others.
The brand is known for producing high-quality outdoor gear.

Synset Examples

The hospital is known for its good doctors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famous for - well-known because of a particular fact or quality
  • Antonym: unknown - not known or recognized
