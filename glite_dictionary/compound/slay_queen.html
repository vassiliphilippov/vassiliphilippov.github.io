slay queen

1. NOUN

/ sleɪ kwiːn / (AmE), / sleɪ kwiːn / (BrE)
An informal term for a woman who impresses others with her outstanding style, fashion, or confidence.
woman outstanding in style or confidence
informal slang

Examples

She walked into the club like a true slay queen, turning heads wherever she went.
Everyone knew her as the slay queen of Instagram because of her bold outfits and flawless makeup.
At her birthday party, all eyes were on the slay queen in her dazzling red dress.

Synset Examples

At the party, she was the slay queen in her stunning dress.
Her confidence made her the slay queen of the event.
Everyone admired her as the slay queen of the fashion show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion
  • Synonym: diva - a distinguished female singer or an admired women, often seen as confident

Etymology

From the slang verb 'slay' (to impress or perform extremely well) plus 'queen' as a title for a powerful or impressive woman.
