Cancer the Crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.sɚ ðə kræb / (AmE), / ˈkæn.sə ðə kræb / (BrE)
the fourth sign of the zodiac, represented by a crab; the sun is in this sign from about June 21 to July 22
fourth sign of the zodiac (Crab)

Examples

People born under Cancer the Crab are thought to be sensitive and intuitive.
The horoscope predicted major changes for those with Cancer the Crab as their zodiac sign.
She collects jewelry featuring the symbol of Cancer the Crab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cancer - the fourth sign of the zodiac; Cancer (astrology)
  • Degree Higher: Leo - the fifth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Lower: Gemini - the third sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Cancer' meaning 'crab', used as the name of a zodiac sign.
