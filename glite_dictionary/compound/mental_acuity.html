mental acuity

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl əˈkju.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl əˈkjuː.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability to think quickly and clearly; sharpness of mind and understanding
sharpness of mind and understanding

Examples

Her mental acuity made her a formidable chess opponent, able to anticipate every move.
Aging can sometimes affect mental acuity, but regular brain exercises may help maintain it.
Doctors measured his mental acuity after the head injury to ensure he had not suffered cognitive damage.

Synset Examples

His mental acuity impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental sharpness - quickness and clarity of mind
  • Antonym: mental dullness - slowness or lack of clarity of mind

Etymology

From Latin *acuere* (to sharpen); 'acuity' denotes keenness, and 'mental' relates to the mind.
