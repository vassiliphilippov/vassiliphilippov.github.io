raccoon dog

1. NOUN

/ rəˈkun dɔɡ / (AmE), / rəˈkuːn dɒɡ / (BrE)
a small wild canine of eastern Asia, with facial markings resembling those of a raccoon
wild Asian canine with raccoon-like face

Examples

The raccoon dog is native to forests and grasslands in eastern Asia.
Zookeepers explained that the raccoon dog is neither a raccoon nor a true dog.
Researchers studied the adaptability of raccoon dogs in new environments.
