diathermy machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪəˌθɜrmi məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəˌθɜːmi məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a medical device that uses high-frequency electric currents to produce heat in body tissues for therapeutic purposes
medical device for therapeutic tissue heating
medical

Examples

The diathermy machine was used during the operation to stop bleeding by coagulating blood vessels.
Modern physiotherapy clinics often have a diathermy machine for treating soft tissue injuries.
Before using the diathermy machine, the technician checked that no metal objects were present on the patient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diathermy - the use of high-frequency electric currents to generate heat within body tissues

Etymology

From 'diathermy' (Greek dia- 'through' + thermē 'heat') and 'machine'.
