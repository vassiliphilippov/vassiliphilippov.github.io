gamer neck

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmɚ nɛk / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmə nɛk / (BrE)
Neck pain or discomfort resulting from long periods of playing video games, often due to poor posture.
neck pain from extended gaming
informal

Examples

Many frequent players experience gamer neck after long gaming sessions.
Exercises and posture corrections can help prevent gamer neck.
He regularly takes breaks to avoid developing gamer neck.

Synset Examples

Stretching helps relieve my gamer neck discomfort.
He complained about his gamer neck after the marathon session.
After hours of playing, I finally felt the gamer neck pain.
