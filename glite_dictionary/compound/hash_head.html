hash head

1. NOUN

/ hæʃ hɛd / (AmE), / hæʃ hɛd / (BrE)
a person who habitually uses hashish
person who uses hashish
slang

Examples

He used to be a hash head before he quit all drugs.
Many hash heads prefer smoking in groups rather than alone.
The documentary followed the lives of several young hash heads in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pothead - a habitual user of marijuana

Etymology

Formed from 'hash' (short for hashish) and 'head' (slang for habitual user).
