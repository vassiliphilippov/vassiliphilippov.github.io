order Pandanales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər pænˈdeɪnəliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə pænˈdaneɪliːz / (BrE)
a botanical order that includes plant families such as Typhaceae, Sparganiaceae, and Pandanaceae
a botanical plant order
medical

Examples

The order Pandanales comprises several families of flowering plants, including the screw pines.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the order Pandanales in Southeast Asia.
The order Pandanales is known for its diverse range of aquatic and land plants.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Pandanaceae - the screw pine family of plants

Etymology

From Latin 'ordo' (order) and family name 'Pandanaceae', the principal family in the order.
