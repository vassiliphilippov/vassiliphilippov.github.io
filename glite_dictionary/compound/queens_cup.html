queen's cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwinz ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnz ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to western North America, with 1 or 2 white, starlike flowers on short, leafless stalks
a plant with starlike white flowers (North America)

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with queen's cup blooming in the shade.
Hikers admired the delicate queen's cup flowers along the mountain trail.
They photographed a cluster of queen's cup nestled among the ferns.
