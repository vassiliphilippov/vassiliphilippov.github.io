hand calculator

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈkælkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / hænd ˈkalkjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
a small, portable electronic calculator designed to be held in the hand or carried in a pocket
portable electronic calculator

Examples

He pulled a hand calculator from his pocket to check the figures quickly.
Teachers often allow students to use a hand calculator for complex math problems.
Before smartphones, a hand calculator was an essential tool for engineers and accountants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - an electronic device for making calculations
