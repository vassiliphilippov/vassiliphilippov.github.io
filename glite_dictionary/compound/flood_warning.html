flood warning

1. NOUN

/ flʌd ˈwɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / flʌd ˈwɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
an official notice given to warn people about the likelihood of flooding happening soon
official alert about possible flooding

Examples

The city issued a flood warning after heavy rain was forecast for the region.
When the flood warning was announced, residents quickly moved to higher ground.
Because of the flood warning, all schools in the area were closed for the day.

Synset Examples

We had to evacuate due to the flood warning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flood alert - notification about possible flood risk
