queer bashing

1. NOUN

/ kwɪr ˈbæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɪə ˈbæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
hostile acts or violence committed against LGBTQ+ people because of their sexual orientation or gender identity
violence against LGBTQ+ individuals

Examples

Activists spoke out against queer bashing at the city council meeting.
Media coverage of queer bashing has increased awareness of hate crimes.
He founded an organization that supports survivors of queer bashing.

Synset Examples

Queer bashing is a serious issue that needs addressing.
The community rallied against queer bashing after the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay bashing - violence or hostility against gay people

Etymology

From 'queer' (slang for LGBTQ+ people) + 'bashing' (violent physical attack, especially as punishment or expression of hatred).
