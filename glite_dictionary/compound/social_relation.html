social relation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the connection or association between living organisms, especially people, in social contexts
connection between people or organisms

Examples

Understanding social relation is essential for studying human societies and communities.
The experiment focused on how animals form social relations within groups.
Modern technology has dramatically changed the nature of social relation between individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relationship - the way in which two or more people or things are connected
  • Synonym: interaction - mutual or reciprocal action or influence
