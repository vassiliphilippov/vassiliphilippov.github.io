animal husbandry

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ˌhʌzbəndri / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˌhʌzbəndri / (BrE)
The science of breeding, caring for, and managing farm animals.
breeding and care of farm animals

Examples

She studied animal husbandry at university to learn about livestock breeding and care.
Modern animal husbandry practices focus on improving animal welfare and productivity.
Advances in animal husbandry have led to healthier livestock and increased food production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livestock farming - raising of domestic animals for food or other products

Etymology

From Latin animalis (living being) and Middle English husbandry (cultivation, management).
