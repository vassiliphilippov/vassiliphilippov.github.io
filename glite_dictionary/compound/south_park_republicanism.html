South Park Republicanism

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ pɑrk rɪˌpʌblɪˈkænɪzəm / (AmE), / saʊθ pɑːk rɪˌpʌblɪˈkænɪzəm / (BrE)
A political stance characterized by a mix of libertarianism, skepticism of political correctness, and humorous or satirical critique of both liberal and conservative views, inspired by themes from the television show South Park.
political stance inspired by South Park's satirical themes

Examples

The comedian is often associated with South Park Republicanism in his commentary and sketches.
Many young voters identified with South Park Republicanism during the early 2000s.
His views on freedom of speech reflect elements of South Park Republicanism.

Synset Examples

South Park Republicanism often satirizes traditional views.
Understanding South Park Republicanism requires a sense of humor.
The debate highlighted elements of South Park Republicanism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libertarianism - a political philosophy advocating minimal state intervention and maximum individual liberty
  • Degree Lower: conservatism - a political philosophy based on traditional values and resistance to change

Etymology

From the animated TV series 'South Park' (created 1997), combined with 'Republicanism', indicating a political philosophy associated with the Republican Party.
