stunt pilot

1. NOUN

/ stʌnt ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / stʌnt ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
a pilot who specializes in performing daring aerial maneuvers and stunts, often for public exhibitions or shows
pilot who performs aerial stunts

Examples

The stunt pilot amazed the crowd with a series of breathtaking loops and rolls.
During the airshow, a renowned stunt pilot performed a daring dive from high altitude.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a stunt pilot and traveling to perform at different airshows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerobatics - the practice of performing stunts and maneuvers in an aircraft
