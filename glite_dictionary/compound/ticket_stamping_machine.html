ticket stamping machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt ˈstæmpɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˈstæmpɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine that validates a ticket by stamping it with location and time information
machine that validates tickets by stamping

Examples

The passenger placed her ticket into the ticket stamping machine before boarding the train.
Make sure to use the ticket stamping machine at the entrance or you could be fined.
Many stations now have a ticket stamping machine on every platform for convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: validator - a device used to confirm the validity of tickets or documents
