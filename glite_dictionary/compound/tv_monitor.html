tv monitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiː ˈviː ˈmɑnɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˈviː ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
A display screen used in a television studio for viewing and monitoring the program being broadcast.
studio television display for monitoring broadcasts

Examples

The director watched the live feed closely on the tv monitor in the control room.
Please check the camera angle on the tv monitor before we go on air.
Several tv monitors display different channels so the staff can monitor all broadcasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - an electronic device for displaying images from a computer, camera, or video source
