will to win

1. NOUN

/ wɪl tə wɪn / (AmE), / wɪl tə wɪn / (BrE)
a strong determination or desire to succeed, especially in competition or difficult situations
strong determination to succeed

Examples

Her will to win was evident throughout the entire tournament.
With a remarkable will to win, he overcame many obstacles to reach the top.
The coach praised the team's will to win after their comeback victory.

Synset Examples

The team played with a will to win the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive - energy and determination to achieve things
  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve success
