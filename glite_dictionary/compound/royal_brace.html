royal brace

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl breɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl breɪs / (BrE)
A type of rope or brace used to secure or control the royal mast on a sailing ship.
brace for a ship's royal mast

Examples

The crew adjusted the royal brace before the storm hit.
A sudden gust tore the royal brace loose from the mast.
Maintenance of the royal brace is crucial during long voyages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brace - a rope used to control the angle of a ship's yard
