purple fringeless orchis

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈfrɪndʒləs ˈɔːrkɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈfrɪndʒləs ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
a species of orchid native to northeastern and alpine eastern North America, closely related to purple fringed orchids, with rosy-purple or violet flowers and finely toothed leaf divisions
a North American orchid species with violet flowers

Examples

The purple fringeless orchis blooms in early summer across wet meadows and marshes.
Botanists found several purple fringeless orchises growing along the forest stream.
She photographed a rare purple fringeless orchis while hiking in the Adirondack Mountains.
