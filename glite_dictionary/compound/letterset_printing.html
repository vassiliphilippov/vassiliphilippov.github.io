letterset printing

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚˌsɛt ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtəˌsɛt ˈprɪn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a printing process in which an image is transferred from a relief plate to a roller before being printed onto the substrate
printing process transferring image via a roller

Examples

The company switched to letterset printing to achieve higher print quality on plastic packaging.
Advancements in letterset printing made mass production of colorful labels more efficient.
He studied the mechanics of letterset printing for his thesis on packaging technologies.

Etymology

Formed from 'letterset' (patterned after lithoset, offset, etc.) and 'printing'.
