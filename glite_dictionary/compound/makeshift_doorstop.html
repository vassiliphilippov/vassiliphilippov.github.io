makeshift doorstop

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkˌʃɪft ˈdɔrˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkʃɪft ˈdɔːstɒp / (BrE)
an improvised object used temporarily to hold a door open or prevent it from closing
temporary improvised door holder

Examples

He grabbed a book to use as a makeshift doorstop during the meeting.
An empty paint can served well enough as a makeshift doorstop in the workshop.
Without a proper wedge, she used her bag as a makeshift doorstop to keep the door open.

Synset Examples

The old shoe was perfect as a makeshift doorstop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorstop - an object used to keep a door open or closed
