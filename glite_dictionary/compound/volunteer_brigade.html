volunteer brigade

1. NOUN

/ vəˈlʊnˌtir brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / ˌvɒl.ənˈtɪə brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a group of people who volunteer together for community service or civic duties
group of community service volunteers

Examples

The city organized a volunteer brigade to assist with disaster relief efforts.
After the earthquake, a volunteer brigade distributed food and supplies to affected families.
He led the volunteer brigade in cleaning up the local park every weekend.

Synset Examples

She joined the volunteer brigade to help the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volunteer group - a group of volunteers organized for an activity or project
