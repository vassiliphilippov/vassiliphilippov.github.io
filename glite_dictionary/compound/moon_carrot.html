moon carrot

1. NOUN

/ mun ˈkærət / (AmE), / muːn ˈkærət / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Seseli, characterized by dense clusters (umbels) of small white or pink flowers and finely divided leaves
plant of the genus Seseli

Examples

The moon carrot is easily recognized by its tall stems and delicate white flowers.
Wild moon carrot can often be found growing in rocky, sunlit fields.
Botanists studied the flowering patterns of the moon carrot throughout the summer.
