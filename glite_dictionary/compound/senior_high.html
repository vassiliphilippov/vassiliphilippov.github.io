senior high

1. NOUN

/ ˌsiːniɚ ˈhaɪ / (AmE), / ˌsiːniə ˈhaɪ / (BrE)
A secondary school, usually including grades 9 through 12, typically attended after junior high or middle school.
secondary school for grades 9–12

Examples

My older brother will graduate from senior high this spring.
She made many new friends after transferring to a different senior high.
Extracurricular activities are an important part of life at senior high.

Synset Examples

he goes to the neighborhood highschool

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high school - A school for students above primary school, typically grades 9–12 in the US
