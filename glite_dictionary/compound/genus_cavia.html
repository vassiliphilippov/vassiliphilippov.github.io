genus Cavia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkeɪ.vi.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkaː.vɪ.ə / (BrE)
The genus of rodents that includes guinea pigs, serving as the type genus of the family Caviidae.
genus of rodents including guinea pigs

Examples

The genus Cavia contains several species, the most familiar being the domestic guinea pig.
Scientists have studied members of the genus Cavia to understand their social behavior.
All animals in the genus Cavia share similar skeletal characteristics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guinea pig - domesticated rodent often kept as a pet, member of genus Cavia

Etymology

From Latin 'Cavia', the genus name, based on 'cavy' for guinea pigs.
