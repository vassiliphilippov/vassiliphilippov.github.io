summer flounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˈflaʊndɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
a flounder of the eastern coast of North America, valued as a food fish
a North American flounder species

Examples

Many anglers in the region try to catch summer flounder during the warm months.
The chef prepared a dish featuring freshly caught summer flounder.
Summer flounder are often found in shallow coastal waters in summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flounder - a type of flatfish
