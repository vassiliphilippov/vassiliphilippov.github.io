chaddi short

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌdi ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌdi ʃɔːt / (BrE)
a type of short trousers, typically worn for swimming or playing, especially by children in South Asia
shorts for swimming or play, South Asian
british

Examples

Many children prefer to wear chaddi shorts when going to the local pool.
He packed a pair of chaddi shorts for his beach holiday in Goa.
In summer, kids often run around in chaddi shorts playing cricket in the park.

Synset Examples

The kids played in chaddi shorts at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorts - short pants covering the upper part of the legs

Etymology

From Hindi 'chaddi' (underwear or shorts) and English 'short'.
