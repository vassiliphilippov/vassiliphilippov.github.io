developmentally challenged

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli ˈʧælɪndʒd / (AmE), / dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
a polite or euphemistic term referring to people who have significant intellectual or cognitive disabilities
having significant intellectual disabilities (euphemistic)

Examples

The new educational program focuses on helping the developmentally challenged achieve greater independence.
She volunteers at a center for the developmentally challenged in her community.
Special Olympics is an international event open to athletes who are developmentally challenged.

Synset Examples

he started a school for the retarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mentally retarded - having significantly below average intellectual functioning (now considered offensive)
