electric circuit

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsɜrkɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
A complete path through which electric current can flow, typically consisting of wires, components, and a power source.
a path for electric current

Examples

An electric circuit requires a closed loop for the current to flow properly.
If the electric circuit is broken, the light will go out immediately.
The teacher explained the basic components of an electric circuit to the students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circuit - a path for an electric current
