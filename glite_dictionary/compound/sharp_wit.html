sharp wit

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp wɪt / (AmE), / ʃɑːp wɪt / (BrE)
the ability to make quick, intelligent, and often humorous remarks
quick and intelligent humor

Examples

He was known for his sharp wit and clever comebacks at every dinner party.
The author's sharp wit shines through in her satirical essays.
Even in tough situations, his sharp wit would lighten the mood.

Synset Examples

Her sharp wit always keeps the conversation lively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quick wit - the ability to reply or think rapidly and cleverly
