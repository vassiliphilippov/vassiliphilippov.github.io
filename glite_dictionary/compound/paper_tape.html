paper tape

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ teɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə teɪp / (BrE)
a long, narrow strip of paper, often used for adding machines, calculators, or in telecommunication devices for recording information
narrow strip of paper

Examples

The technician loaded a fresh roll of paper tape into the calculator.
Old computers often used paper tape to store and transfer data.
He wrote his calculations on a strip of paper tape for record-keeping.
