confidence trick

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːnfɪdəns trɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒnfɪdəns trɪk / (BrE)
a dishonest scheme or fraud in which someone gains a victim's trust in order to deceive and cheat them, especially in gambling or selling worthless property
deceptive fraud relying on trust

Examples

He fell victim to a confidence trick and lost all his savings.
The police warned about a new confidence trick targeting elderly residents.
She recognized the signs of a confidence trick just in time to avoid being cheated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: con - an act of cheating or trickery, a swindle
  • Synonym: scam - a dishonest scheme; a fraud
