supporting structure

1. NOUN

/ səˈpɔrtɪŋ ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪŋ ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a structure that provides support to another object or part
structure that supports another

Examples

The columns serve as the main supporting structure of the old cathedral.
The engineer examined the supporting structure before approving further construction.
The insect’s exoskeleton acts as a supporting structure for its body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure of a building, vehicle, or object
  • Synonym: scaffolding - a temporary structure for support during building work
