to charge my phone

1. VERB

/ tʃɑrdʒ maɪ foʊn / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ maɪ fəʊn / (BrE)
to connect a mobile phone to a power source in order to restore its battery power
restore phone battery power

Examples

I forgot to charge my phone last night, so the battery died at work.
Can I use your charger to charge my phone quickly?
She always remembers to charge my phone before going out for the day.

Synset Examples

I need to charge my phone before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to recharge - restore electrical energy to something, e.g. a battery
