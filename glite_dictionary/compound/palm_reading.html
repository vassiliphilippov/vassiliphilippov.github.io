palm reading

1. NOUN

/ pɑm ˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / pɑːm ˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of telling someone's fortune by interpreting the lines and features of the palm of their hand
fortune-telling by reading palms

Examples

She visited a fair where palm reading was one of the attractions.
Many people turn to palm reading out of curiosity about their future.
The mystic offered palm reading services at her small studio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiromancy - the art of reading palms to tell fortunes
  • Synonym: fortune-telling - predicting a person's future, often as a profession
