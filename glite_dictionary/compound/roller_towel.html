roller towel

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlər ˌtaʊəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˌtaʊəl / (BrE)
a long towel with its ends sewn together to form a continuous loop, hung on a roller for repeated use, typically in public restrooms
towel looped on roller for repeated use

Examples

The restroom still had an old-fashioned roller towel hanging by the sink.
He wiped his hands on the roller towel after washing up.
They replaced the roller towel with individual paper towels for better hygiene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel - a piece of absorbent cloth used for drying
