good value

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈvælju / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈvaljuː / (BrE)
worth the money paid; something that is considered to be a good deal for its price
worth the price paid

Examples

This phone is good value for the features it offers.
Many students look for restaurants that provide good value on meals.
Although it's not the cheapest, the quality makes it good value.

Synset Examples

You get good value at that discount store.
The meal was delicious and a good value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain - something bought or offered at a price favorable to the buyer
  • Antonym: rip-off - something that is not worth what you pay for it
