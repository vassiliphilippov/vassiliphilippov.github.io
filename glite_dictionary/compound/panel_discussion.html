panel discussion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænəl dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈpænəl dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a formal discussion on a particular topic held by a group of people (the panel), typically in front of an audience
formal group discussion before an audience

Examples

The conference will open with a panel discussion on renewable energy.
She participated in an engaging panel discussion about the future of education.
A lively panel discussion followed the keynote speech, featuring experts from different fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debate - a formal discussion in which opposing arguments are put forward
