city girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or woman who lives in a city, typically one with sophisticated manners and fashionable clothing
female city dweller with sophistication

Examples

She was a city girl at heart and loved the hustle of urban life.
As a city girl, she always dressed in the latest fashions wherever she went.
Moving to the countryside was a big change for a lifelong city girl like Emily.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: country girl - a girl or woman from the countryside, often regarded as less sophisticated
