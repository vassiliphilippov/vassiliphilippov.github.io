Zombie Jesus

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑmbi ˈdʒizəs / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbi ˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
A satirical term referring to Jesus Christ depicted as a zombie, typically used humorously or irreverently in reference to the resurrection.
satirical depiction of Jesus as a zombie

Examples

The comic book features an irreverent take on Zombie Jesus battling the undead.
Every Easter, some memes circulate that jokingly refer to the holiday as the return of Zombie Jesus.
The artist faced controversy after unveiling a statue titled Zombie Jesus in the gallery.

Synset Examples

His artwork depicted a satirical version of Zombie Jesus.
