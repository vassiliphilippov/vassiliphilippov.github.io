rum cocktail

1. NOUN

/ rʌm ˈkɑkˌteɪl / (AmE), / rʌm ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink made with rum as the main ingredient, typically mixed with lime or lemon juice and other ingredients
mixed alcoholic drink with rum and citrus

Examples

He ordered a rum cocktail with extra lime at the bar.
The bartender recommended their signature rum cocktail on a hot summer evening.
Classic rum cocktails often feature a balance of sweetness and tartness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocktail - a mixed alcoholic beverage usually consisting of a spirit and various ingredients
