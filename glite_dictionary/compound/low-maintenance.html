low-maintenance

1. ADJ

/ ˌloʊ ˈmeɪn.tə.nəns / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈmeɪn.tə.nəns / (BrE)
Requiring little time, effort, or expense to maintain or care for.
requiring little upkeep

Examples

We wanted a low-maintenance pet that wouldn't need constant attention.
The backyard was designed to be low-maintenance, with drought-resistant plants and artificial turf.
She prefers low-maintenance friendships that don't require daily check-ins.

Synset Examples

He chose a low-maintenance car to save time and effort.
This plant is perfect for low-maintenance gardening.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-maintenance - requiring a lot of attention, effort, or care
