God's Will

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑdz wɪl / (AmE), / ɡɒdz wɪl / (BrE)
the power and intent of God regarded as directing or determining events in the world
the power and intent of God

Examples

Many believers accept that suffering is part of God's Will, even if they cannot understand it.
He prayed for guidance so he would not act against God's Will.
The community rebuilt after the disaster, trusting in God's Will to give them strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divine will - the will or purposes of a deity
