Ping River

1. PROPN

/ pɪŋ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pɪŋ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in western Thailand and a major tributary of the Chao Phraya River
river in western Thailand

Examples

The Ping River flows through Chiang Mai, providing water for agriculture and daily use.
During the rainy season, the Ping River can swell and flood nearby areas.
The city of Tak sits along the banks of the Ping River, benefiting from its fertile land.
