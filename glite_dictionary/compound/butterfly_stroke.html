butterfly stroke

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ stroʊk / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflʌɪ strəʊk / (BrE)
A swimming stroke in which both arms are swung forward together over the water while the legs perform a dolphin kick.
swimming stroke with both arms and a dolphin kick

Examples

She set a new record in the 100-meter butterfly stroke at the national championships.
Learning the butterfly stroke can be challenging for beginners because of the rhythm and coordination required.
He excels in both freestyle and butterfly stroke events at swim meets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freestyle - a swimming stroke in which the swimmer lies on their chest and moves the arms alternately in a forward motion
  • Synonym: breaststroke - a swimming stroke where the arms and legs move in a circular motion while facing downward
  • Synonym: backstroke - a swimming stroke performed on the back with alternating arm movements
