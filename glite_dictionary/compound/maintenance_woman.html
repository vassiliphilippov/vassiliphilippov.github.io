maintenance woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪntənəns ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪntənəns ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female skilled worker whose job is to repair and maintain equipment, buildings, or machinery.
female repair and maintenance worker

Examples

The maintenance woman quickly fixed the leaking pipe in the office kitchen.
As a maintenance woman, she is responsible for inspecting all the electrical systems regularly.
Everyone relies on the maintenance woman whenever something in the building breaks down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maintenance man - male skilled worker who repairs and maintains things
