horse breeding

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of mating horses to produce desirable qualities in offspring
the practice of breeding horses

Examples

He has dedicated his life to horse breeding on his family's ranch.
Modern horse breeding focuses on improving speed and stamina in racehorses.
The farm is renowned for its successful horse breeding program.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: animal breeding - the practice of mating animals to produce desired traits
