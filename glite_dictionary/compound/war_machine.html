war machine

1. NOUN

/ wɔr məˈʃin / (AmE), / wɔː məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
the organized armed forces and military resources of a nation, regarded collectively as a powerful entity used in warfare.
collective military forces of a nation

Examples

The war machine of the country was mobilized after the declaration of conflict.
Many feared the overwhelming power of the enemy's war machine.
Political leaders were hesitant to challenge the war machine directly.

Synset Examples

their military is the largest in the region
the military machine is the same one we faced in 1991 but now it is weaker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - armed forces; the military establishment of a country
