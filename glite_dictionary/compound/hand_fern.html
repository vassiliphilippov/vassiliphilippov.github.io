hand fern

1. NOUN

/ hænd fɝn / (AmE), / hænd fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern with fronds that are coarsely lobed or palm-shaped
tropical fern with lobed fronds

Examples

The hand fern is prized among plant enthusiasts for its unusual, hand-shaped fronds.
We spotted a rare hand fern growing in the damp understory of the rainforest.
Botanists study the habitat requirements of the hand fern to aid in its conservation.
