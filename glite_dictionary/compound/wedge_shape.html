wedge shape

1. NOUN

/ wɛdʒ ʃeɪp / (AmE), / wɛdʒ ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a shape that has a triangular cross-section, resembling a wedge
shape with a triangular cross-section

Examples

The doorstop had a wedge shape that kept the door open.
Cheese is often cut into slices with a wedge shape for serving.
Because of its wedge shape, the tool split the log easily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triangle - a plane figure with three straight sides and three angles
