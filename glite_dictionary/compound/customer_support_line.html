customer support line

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəmɚ səˈpɔrt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmə səˈpɔːt laɪn / (BrE)
a telephone line or service that customers can contact for help or assistance with products or services
service line for customer assistance

Examples

I called the customer support line when I had trouble with my order.
Their customer support line is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
After a long wait, someone finally answered the customer support line and resolved my issue.

Synset Examples

The customer support line was helpful today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpline - a telephone service offering help or information
  • Synonym: hotline - a direct and immediate phone line for assistance or information
