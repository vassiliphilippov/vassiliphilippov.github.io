Chinese holly

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈhɒli / (BrE)
a dense, rounded evergreen shrub native to China, with spiny leaves, widely grown as an ornamental plant
evergreen shrub with spiny leaves, native to China

Examples

The Chinese holly in her garden remains green throughout the winter.
Many landscapers plant Chinese holly for its dense foliage and attractive shape.
Because of its spiny leaves, Chinese holly provides excellent cover for small birds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - evergreen shrub or tree with prickly leaves and red berries
