stormtossed soul

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrm.tɔst soʊl / (AmE), / ˈstɔːm.tɒst səʊl / (BrE)
a person who is experiencing great emotional turmoil or distress
person in emotional turmoil

Examples

After months of uncertainty, she felt like a stormtossed soul searching for hope.
The novel's protagonist is a stormtossed soul grappling with his past mistakes.
He had the look of a stormtossed soul who had weathered many hardships.

Synset Examples

They felt like stormtossed souls lost at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troubled soul - a person who is emotionally troubled or distressed
  • Antonym: calm - free from agitation or turmoil; peaceful
