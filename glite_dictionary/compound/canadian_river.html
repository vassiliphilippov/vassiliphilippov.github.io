Canadian River

1. PROPN

/ kəˈneɪdiən ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river rising in northeastern New Mexico, flowing eastward across the Texas panhandle, and joining the Arkansas River in Oklahoma
river in the southern United States

Examples

The Canadian River forms an important part of the watershed in the southern Great Plains.
Many towns along the Canadian River rely on it for their water supply.
His family owns land near the banks of the Canadian River in Texas.
