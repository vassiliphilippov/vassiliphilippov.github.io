populated area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːpjəˌleɪtɪd ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪd ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a region or locality where people live, especially a city or town
region where people live

Examples

The government plans to build new roads connecting every populated area in the region.
After the disaster, many residents left the populated area for safer locations.
Wild animals are rarely seen in a heavily populated area like downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: settlement - a place where people establish a community
  • Antonym: uninhabited area - an area with no residents
