securities law

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊr.ɪ.tiz lɔ / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.tiz lɔː / (BrE)
the area of law regulating the issuance, sale, and trading of securities such as stocks and bonds
laws regulating the trade of securities
legal

Examples

Many companies consult legal experts to ensure compliance with securities law before issuing new shares.
Recent reforms have strengthened securities law to protect investors from fraud and market manipulation.
Violating securities law can result in heavy fines and criminal charges for those involved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial regulation - the supervision of financial institutions and markets by law
