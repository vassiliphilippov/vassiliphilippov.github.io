shaggymane mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæɡiˌmeɪn ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡiˌmeɪn ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
a common edible mushroom with an elongated shaggy white cap and black spores, scientifically known as Coprinus comatus.
a shaggy white edible mushroom

Examples

We found a large shaggymane mushroom in the field after the rain.
The shaggymane mushroom is best eaten when its cap is still white and uncurled.
Many foragers look for shaggymane mushrooms because they are easy to identify and delicious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mushroom - a fleshy, spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus
