piece of material

1. NOUN

/ pis əv məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / piːs əv məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a separate portion or section of fabric, often cut for a specific purpose
a separate section of fabric

Examples

She cut a piece of material to sew a patch onto the jacket.
Each child received a colorful piece of material for the craft project.
He chose a soft piece of material to line the inside of the box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fabric - cloth or textile material
  • Synonym: piece of cloth - a separate part consisting of cloth
