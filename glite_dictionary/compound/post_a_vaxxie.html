to post a vaxxie

1. VERB

/ poʊst ə ˈvæki / (AmE), / pəʊst ə ˈvæk.si / (BrE)
to upload or share a photograph of oneself after receiving a vaccination, typically on social media
share vaccination selfie online
informal

Examples

After receiving her booster shot, she decided to post a vaxxie on Instagram.
Many people post a vaxxie to encourage others to get vaccinated.
Last year, it was common to post a vaxxie to show support for the vaccination campaign.

Synset Examples

Posting a vaxxie on social media became a trend among friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selfie - a photograph that one has taken of oneself

Etymology

Blend of 'vaccine' and 'selfie'; 'vaxxie' emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic to denote a vaccine selfie.
