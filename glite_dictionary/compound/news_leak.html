news leak

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuz ˌlik / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz ˌliːk / (BrE)
an unauthorized, often deliberate, disclosure of confidential or sensitive information to the public or media
unauthorized disclosure of confidential information

Examples

The government was embarrassed by a news leak that revealed classified details of the negotiations.
After the news leak, the company launched an internal investigation to find the source.
Several politicians denied any involvement in the news leak to the press last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leak - an intentional or accidental release of secret information
