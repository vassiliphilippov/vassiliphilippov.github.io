footless chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtləs tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtləs tʃɛː / (BrE)
a chair that does not have legs or supporting feet
a chair without legs

Examples

The designer showcased a footless chair in the latest furniture exhibition.
Sitting on a footless chair gives the living room a futuristic touch.
He replaced all the traditional seats with footless chairs to create a low-profile look.

Synset Examples
