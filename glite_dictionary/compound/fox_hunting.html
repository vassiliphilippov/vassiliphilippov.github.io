fox hunting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑks ˌhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒks ˌhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of hunting a fox with trained hounds, typically involving hunters on horseback.
the hunting of foxes with hounds and horses

Examples

Many traditional country estates have hosted fox hunting events for generations.
The government passed a law banning fox hunting in several regions.
Protesters gathered outside the manor to voice their opposition to fox hunting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunt - to pursue and kill wild animals for sport or food
  • Synonym: coursing - the pursuit of game animals with dogs by sight rather than scent
