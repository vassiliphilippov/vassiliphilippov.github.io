fashion designer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən dɪˌzaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən dɪˌzaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is designing clothing and accessories, especially new styles or fashions
a person who designs clothing

Examples

She dreams of becoming a fashion designer and launching her own brand.
The fashion designer presented his new collection at Paris Fashion Week.
Many famous fashion designers started out as assistants in established studios.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: designer - a person who designs something
