sand berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænd ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsænd ˌbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen mat-forming shrub found in North America and northern Eurasia, with small white flowers, red berries, and leaves that turn red in autumn
mat-forming shrub with red berries

Examples

The forest floor was covered with sand berry plants, their red berries standing out against the green leaves.
In autumn, the leaves of the sand berry turn a vibrant red, adding color to the landscape.
Bears are known to eat the sand berry fruit when it is in season.
