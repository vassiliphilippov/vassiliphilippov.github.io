Alaska Standard Time

1. NOUN

/ əˈlæskə ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / əˈlæskə ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time used in most of Alaska, nine hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-9)
time zone used in most of Alaska

Examples

Anchorage observes Alaska Standard Time during the winter months.
Meetings are scheduled at 10 a.m. Alaska Standard Time.
The live broadcast aired at 7 p.m. Alaska Standard Time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hawaii-Aleutian Standard Time - another name for the time zone covering Hawaii and parts of Alaska
  • Degree Higher: Greenwich Mean Time - baseline international time from Greenwich, England
