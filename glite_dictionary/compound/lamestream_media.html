lamestream media

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪmstriːm ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˈleɪmstriːm ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
A disparaging term for mainstream media, suggesting it is unoriginal, biased, or lacks substance.
disparaging term for mainstream media
informal slang

Examples

Many people distrust the lamestream media and look for alternative news sources online.
The politician accused the lamestream media of ignoring important stories.
Critics argue that the lamestream media prioritizes sensationalism over real journalism.

Synset Examples

She criticized the lamestream culture for its lack of creativity.
The lamestream media often misrepresents the facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainstream media - traditional and established media outlets

Etymology

Blend of 'lame' (meaning weak or unoriginal) and 'mainstream media'.
