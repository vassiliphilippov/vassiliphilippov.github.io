shitload of

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌloʊd əv / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌləʊd əv / (BrE)
an extremely large amount of something (used informally and considered vulgar)
very large amount (vulgar, slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

There’s a shitload of food left over from the party.
I've got a shitload of work to do before Monday.
She made a shitload of money from her latest project.

Synset Examples

There’s a shitload of food left over from the party.
We received a shitload of complaints about the noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ton of - a large amount of something (informal)
  • Synonym: load of - a large quantity or number (informal)

Etymology

From 'shit' (vulgar slang) + 'load' (quantity).
