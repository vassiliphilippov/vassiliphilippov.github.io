pregame show

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriˌɡeɪm ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpriːɡeɪm ˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
a television program broadcast before a sports event, featuring analysis, highlights, and commentary
TV program before a sports event

Examples

Many fans tune in early to catch the pregame show before kickoff.
The pregame show featured interviews with both teams' coaches.
During the pregame show, analysts discussed the players to watch in tonight's game.

Synset Examples

She likes to watch highlights in the pregame show.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postgame show - television program broadcast after a sports event
