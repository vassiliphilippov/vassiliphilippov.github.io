rose-colored starling

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊz ˌkʌlərd ˈstɑrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌkʌləd ˈstɑːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a glossy black bird with a pink back and abdomen, found chiefly in Asia
Asian starling species with pink and black plumage

Examples

During their migration, large flocks of rose-colored starlings can be seen in southern Asia.
The rose-colored starling stood out among the local birds because of its unusual pink plumage.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a rose-colored starling in the countryside last spring.
