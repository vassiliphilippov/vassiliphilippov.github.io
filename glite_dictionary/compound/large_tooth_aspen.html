large tooth aspen

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ tuθ ˈæspən / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ tuːθ ˈaspən / (BrE)
a species of aspen tree with a narrow crown and large, coarse-toothed leaves, native to eastern North America
aspen tree native to eastern North America

Examples

The large tooth aspen is easily recognized by its big, serrated leaves and light bark.
Foresters in eastern North America study the large tooth aspen for its fast growth and ecological importance.
In autumn, the leaves of the large tooth aspen turn a brilliant yellow, lighting up the forest.
