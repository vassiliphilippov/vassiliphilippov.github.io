wide selection

1. NOUN

/ waɪd səˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / waɪd sɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a large variety of different things to choose from, usually of the same general type
a large variety of items to choose from

Examples

The bookstore offers a wide selection of novels from many different genres.
We chose the restaurant because it has a wide selection of vegetarian dishes.
Online retailers often provide a wide selection of products compared to local stores.

Synset Examples

Mallwart is known for its low prices and wide selection.
The store had a wide selection of dildos for every preference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - a number of different things or types
  • Antonym: limited selection - a small or restricted range of choices
