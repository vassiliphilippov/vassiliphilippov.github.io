lethal agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːθəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈliːθl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a substance or factor capable of causing death
something that can cause death

Examples

The laboratory tests revealed that the chemical was a lethal agent even at low concentrations.
Some nerve gases are classified as lethal agents due to their extreme toxicity.
Authorities warned that the package might contain a lethal agent, urging everyone to keep their distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadly - causing or able to cause death
  • Antonym: nonlethal - not capable of causing death
