air unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌjunɪt / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a military group or formation that is part of an air force, equipped for aerial operations
military group of the air force

Examples

The newly formed air unit was deployed to support the ground troops in the region.
Each air unit is responsible for maintaining its aircraft and readiness.
He served as a pilot in an elite air unit during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air force - the branch of a nation's military that conducts aerial warfare
