ballet position

1. NOUN

/ bəˈleɪ pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / bæˈleɪ pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a classical posture of the body, especially of the feet, used as a standard in ballet
standard body posture in ballet

Examples

The dancer practiced each ballet position until her movements were precise and graceful.
Learning the five basic ballet positions is essential for any beginner.
He demonstrated a perfect ballet position during the audition.
