trailer camp

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlɚ kæmp / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlə kæmp / (BrE)
A place where spaces for house trailers can be rented, often with utilities and other facilities provided.
place to rent spaces for trailers

Examples

They spent their summer vacation living in a trailer camp near the lake.
The hurricane forced dozens of families to seek temporary shelter in the local trailer camp.
The trailer camp offers full hookups for water, electricity, and internet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trailer park - area where trailers are parked and used as homes
