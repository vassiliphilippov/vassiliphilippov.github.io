Varied Lorikeet

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛrid ˈlɔrɪˌkit / (AmE), / ˈvɛərɪd ˈlɒrɪˌkiːt / (BrE)
a species of small, brightly colored parrot (Psitteuteles versicolor) native to northern Australia, known for its multi-colored plumage
a brightly colored Australian lorikeet

Examples

The Varied Lorikeet is often seen feeding on nectar in northern Australian forests.
A flock of Varied Lorikeets brightened the eucalyptus trees with their vivid colors.
Birdwatchers travel to Australia hoping to spot the elusive Varied Lorikeet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lorikeet - a small, brightly colored parrot from the Australasian region
