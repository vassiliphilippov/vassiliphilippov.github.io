whisky sour

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski saʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ˈsaʊə / (BrE)
A cocktail made with whiskey, lemon or lime juice, and sugar, often served over ice and garnished with a slice of fruit or a cherry.
cocktail with whiskey and citrus

Examples

She ordered a whisky sour at the bar before dinner.
His favorite drink is a classic whisky sour with extra lemon.
Many cocktail menus feature the whisky sour among their traditional options.
