roller skating

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlər ˌskeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˌskeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of moving on surfaces wearing shoes with small wheels attached to the bottom
skating on shoes with wheels

Examples

She has enjoyed roller skating since she was a child.
We spent the afternoon roller skating at the local park.
Roller skating is a great way to stay fit and have fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skating - the activity of skating on ice using ice skates
