night person

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / naɪt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person who prefers to be active at night and tends to stay up late rather than getting up early in the morning.
person who prefers to be active at night

Examples

My brother is a true night person and does his best work after midnight.
Being a night person, she finds it hard to wake up early for meetings.
Many artists and writers are night people who feel most inspired after dark.

Synset Examples

I'm not a morning person; I'm a night person.
As a night person, he thrives in the evening hours.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: morning person - someone who prefers to be active in the morning
