colour supplement

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌsʌpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
(British) a magazine printed in colour, included with a newspaper, particularly at weekends.
magazine included with a newspaper (British)
