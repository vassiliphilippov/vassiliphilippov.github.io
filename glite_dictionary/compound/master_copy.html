master copy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstər ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
the original version of a document, recording, or work from which copies are made
original version used to make copies

Examples

The master copy of the film is stored in a secure vault.
Please use the master copy to make the final prints for distribution.
They accidentally deleted the master copy, losing all the original edits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: original - the first or earliest version of something
