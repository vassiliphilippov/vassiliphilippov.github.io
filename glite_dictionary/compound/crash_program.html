crash program

1. NOUN

/ kræʃ ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / kræʃ ˈprəʊɡram / (BrE)
a rapid and intense course of training, research, or action, typically organized in response to an emergency or urgent need
intensive, urgent training or effort

Examples

The government launched a crash program to improve the country's energy infrastructure after the blackout.
She enrolled in a crash program to learn French before her transfer to Paris.
A crash program is now underway to develop vaccines in response to the outbreak.

Synset Examples

he took a crash course in Italian on his way to Italy
his first job was a crash course in survival and in learning how to get along with people
a crash programme is needed to create new jobs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash course - a short and intensive course of study
