Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations

1. PROPN

/ fuːd ənd ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃər ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən əv ðə ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / fuːd ənd ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən əv ðə ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz / (BrE)
the United Nations agency responsible for leading international efforts to defeat hunger and improve agriculture, food security, and nutrition worldwide
UN agency for food and agriculture

Examples

The Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations works to improve food security around the globe.
According to the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, world hunger remains a critical issue.
The report released by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations highlights progress in sustainable farming.
