classroom project

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsˌrum ˈprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsruːm ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a significant task or assignment carried out by students as part of their schoolwork, typically requiring research, collaboration, or creativity
major school task or assignment

Examples

For our science fair, each student had to complete a classroom project on renewable energy.
The teacher encouraged teamwork for the upcoming classroom project about local history.
Completing the classroom project taught me valuable skills in research and collaboration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignment - a piece of work given to someone as part of their job or studies
  • Synonym: project - a planned piece of work with a specific purpose, often requiring effort over a period of time
