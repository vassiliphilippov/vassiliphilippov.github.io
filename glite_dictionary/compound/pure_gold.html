pure gold

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ɡoʊld / (AmE), / pjʊə ɡəʊld / (BrE)
Gold that is completely free from other metals or impurities; gold of 100 percent purity.
gold of 100 percent purity

Examples

The wedding ring was made of pure gold, symbolizing eternal love.
Mining companies often seek out veins of pure gold deep within the mountains.
The medal was said to be pure gold, but in reality, it was only gold-plated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold - a valuable yellow metal used as a standard for money and in jewelry
