product introduction

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːdʌkt ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˈprɒdʌkt ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the action of launching a new product or product line to the market, often with advertising and promotion
launch of a new product

Examples

The company's latest product introduction has generated significant buzz in the technology industry.
Effective marketing strategies are critical for a successful product introduction.
Our firm specializes in consulting services for new product introductions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch - the act of starting or introducing something new, especially a product
