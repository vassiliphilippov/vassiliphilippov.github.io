surveillance tactic

1. NOUN

/ sɚˈveɪ.ləns ˈtæk.tɪk / (AmE), / səˈveɪ.ləns ˈtæk.tɪk / (BrE)
a method or strategy for monitoring or observing people, activities, or information, often for security or intelligence purposes
method for monitoring or observing

Examples

The agency developed a new surveillance tactic to track criminal activity online.
Covert cameras are a common surveillance tactic used in large retail stores.
Data analysis is increasingly used as a digital surveillance tactic by governments worldwide.

Synset Examples

The Stasi was infamous for its surveillance tactics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surveillance - close observation, especially of a suspect or group
  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a goal
