roguish look

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.ɡɪʃ lʊk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.ɡɪʃ lʊk / (BrE)
a facial expression that is playfully mischievous or slightly cheeky, often appearing charming
playfully mischievous facial expression

Examples

She gave him a roguish look before sneaking the last cookie from the plate.
With a roguish look, he suggested taking a shortcut through the forbidden garden.
The actor's roguish look charmed audiences in every scene of the movie.

Synset Examples

With a roguish look, he invited her to join the adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischievous look - a look showing playful mischief
  • Antonym: innocent look - a look showing innocence or naivety
