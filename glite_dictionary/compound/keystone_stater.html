Keystone Stater

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːstoʊn ˈsteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkiːstəʊn ˈsteɪtə / (BrE)
a native or resident of Pennsylvania, United States
person from Pennsylvania

Examples

As a proud Keystone Stater, she loves exploring the history of her home state.
Many Keystone Staters follow Penn State football with great enthusiasm.
He moved to Philadelphia and quickly felt welcomed by the Keystone Stater community.

Etymology

Named after Pennsylvania's nickname, the 'Keystone State'.
