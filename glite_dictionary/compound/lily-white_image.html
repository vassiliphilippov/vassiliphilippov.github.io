lily-white image

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪli waɪt ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɪli waɪt ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a reputation that is considered entirely pure, honest, or innocent
pure or innocent reputation

Examples

The company worked hard to maintain its lily-white image despite rumors of internal misconduct.
She feared that any scandal would destroy her carefully crafted lily-white image.
The politician's lily-white image was challenged after the controversial report emerged.

Synset Examples

They portrayed their lily-white image to attract more customers.
Her lily-white reputation made her a trusted leader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotless reputation - a reputation without any stain or blemish
  • Antonym: tarnished reputation - a reputation that has been damaged or stained

Etymology

Refers metaphorically to the whiteness of a lily, symbolizing purity; 'image' means public perception or reputation.
