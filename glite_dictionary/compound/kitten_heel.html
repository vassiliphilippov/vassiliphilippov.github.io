kitten heel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtn̩ hiːl / (AmE), / ˈkɪtn hiːl / (BrE)
A style of women's shoe with a thin, low heel, usually less than 2 inches high.
women's shoe with a low, thin heel

Examples

She wore elegant kitten heels to the party, matching her classic dress perfectly.
Many women choose kitten heels for work because they are both stylish and comfortable.
If you want a bit of height but don't like stilettos, try wearing kitten heels instead.

Synset Examples

Kitten heels are worth considering when you are too tall for stilettos, but don't feel good in flats.
