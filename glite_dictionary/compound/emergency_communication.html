emergency communication

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɜrdʒənsi kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
communication methods and systems used to relay information during a crisis or emergency situation, especially for safety or coordination purposes
communication during crises

Examples

Proper emergency communication can save lives during natural disasters.
The city upgraded its emergency communication system after the last earthquake.
First responders rely on clear emergency communication to coordinate rescue efforts.

Synset Examples

Shortwave radios are great for emergency communications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the act of conveying information
