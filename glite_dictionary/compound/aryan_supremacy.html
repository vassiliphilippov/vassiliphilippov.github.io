Aryan supremacy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛəriən səˈprɛməsi / (AmE), / ˈɛːrɪən səˈprɛməsi / (BrE)
the belief that Aryan people are superior to people of other races
belief in Aryan racial superiority
offensive

Examples

The group openly promoted Aryan supremacy in their propaganda materials.
Scholars have studied the historical roots of Aryan supremacy in Europe.
His rejection of Aryan supremacy led to conflicts with extremist organizations.

Synset Examples

The group's ideology was based on Aryan supremacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white supremacy - the belief that white people are superior to others
  • Antonym: racial equality - the principle that all races are equal

Etymology

Aryan from Sanskrit ā́rya (noble); supremacy from Latin supremus (highest, supreme).
