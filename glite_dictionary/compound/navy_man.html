navy man

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvi mæn / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi mæn / (BrE)
A male member of a navy; a man serving in a country's naval forces.
male member of a navy

Examples

His grandfather was a proud navy man who served in World War II.
At the ceremony, the young navy man was awarded a medal for his bravery.
She grew up listening to stories her father told as a navy man traveling the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works as a member of a ship's crew, especially in the navy
