mountain zebra

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈziːbrə / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈziːbrə / (BrE)
a species of zebra with narrow stripes, native to southern Africa and now endangered
an endangered zebra species from southern Africa

Examples

The population of the mountain zebra has declined drastically due to habitat loss.
We spotted a herd of mountain zebras grazing in a remote part of the national park.
Unlike the plains zebra, the mountain zebra has a distinctive grid-iron pattern on its rump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zebra - African wild animal with black-and-white stripes
