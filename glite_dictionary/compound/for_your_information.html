for your information

1. X

/ fɔr jʊr ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / fə jɔː ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
used to introduce information intended to inform or clarify, often politely or pointedly
used to introduce informative or clarifying details

Examples

For your information, the deadline has been extended by a week.
Just for your information, the wifi password is on the back of your keycard.
That policy changed last year, for your information.

Synset Examples

I wanted to tell you, for your information, that the deadline is tomorrow.
For your information, the meeting starts at noon.
Just for your information, the report was submitted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: FYI - abbreviation for 'for your information'
