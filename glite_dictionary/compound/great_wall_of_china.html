Great Wall of China

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt wɔl əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / ɡreɪt wɔːl əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
a massive ancient fortification built in northern China, stretching over 1,500 miles, originally constructed to protect against invasions
ancient Chinese defensive wall

Examples

Many tourists dream of visiting the Great Wall of China at least once in their lives.
Parts of the Great Wall of China have been restored to attract international visitors.
It is a common myth that the Great Wall of China is visible from space with the naked eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Great Wall - alternative term referring to the same structure
