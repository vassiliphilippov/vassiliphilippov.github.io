desert lynx

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt lɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈdɛzət lɪŋks / (BrE)
a wild cat of deserts in northern Africa and southern Asia, also known as the caracal, noted for its long tufted ears and sleek build
wild cat of African and Asian deserts

Examples

The desert lynx prowls the arid regions of northern Africa, hunting small mammals at dusk.
Wildlife photographers traveled to capture images of the elusive desert lynx in its natural habitat.
Unlike many other wild cats, the desert lynx thrives in harsh, dry environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caracal - another name for the desert lynx, a wild cat of Africa and Asia
  • Degree Lower: lynx - a medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears, found in Europe, Asia, and North America
