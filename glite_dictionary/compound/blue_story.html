blue story

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈstɔri / (AmE), / bluː ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an obscene or indecent joke or story, often containing sexual content or language considered improper
an indecent or obscene joke

Examples

He shocked the group with a blue story that made everyone laugh and blush.
Some people find a blue story entertaining, while others may take offense.
After telling a blue story, he quickly apologized to those who seemed uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty joke - an indecent or obscene joke
  • Antonym: clean joke - a joke without indecent or obscene content

Etymology

From the association of 'blue' with indecency or risqué content in the early 19th century.
