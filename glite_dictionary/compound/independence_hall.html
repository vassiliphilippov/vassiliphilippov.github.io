Independence Hall

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪndəˈpɛndəns hɔl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəns hɔːl / (BrE)
the historic building in Philadelphia where the Declaration of Independence and the United States Constitution were debated and adopted
historic building in Philadelphia, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit Independence Hall each year to learn about America's founding.
The signing of the Declaration took place inside Independence Hall.
Independence Hall is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
