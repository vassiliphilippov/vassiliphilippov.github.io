shell ginger

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl ˈdʒɪndʒər / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
A tropical plant (Alpinia zerumbet) grown for its glossy, oblong leaves and arching clusters of white, pink-tinted flowers with yellow, magenta-striped lips.
tropical plant with showy leaves and flowers

Examples

The shell ginger in her garden flourished under the tropical sun.
We planted shell ginger along the walkway for their attractive foliage.
A vase of fresh-cut shell ginger flowers added color to the dining room.
