spousal equivalent

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaʊzəl ɪˈkwɪvələnt / (AmE), / ˈspaʊzəl ɪˈkwɪvələnt / (BrE)
a person with whom one lives and shares a long-term intimate relationship similar to marriage, without being legally married
unmarried partner in a marriage-like relationship

Examples

After ten years together, she referred to Mark as her spousal equivalent on official forms.
Some companies offer health benefits to employees' spousal equivalents as well as to legal spouses.
He introduced his spousal equivalent at the company dinner, making it clear they shared a committed relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic partner - a person who lives with another in a long-term intimate relationship
  • Degree Lower: spouse - a person's married partner
