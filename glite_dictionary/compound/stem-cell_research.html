stem-cell research

1. NOUN

/ stɛm sɛl rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / stɛm sɛl rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
scientific investigation into stem cells and their medical applications, including their use in developing therapies
study of stem cells for medical use
medical

Examples

The university is renowned for its groundbreaking stem-cell research in regenerative medicine.
Ethical debates often arise in discussions about stem-cell research and its potential uses.
Advancements in stem-cell research could lead to cures for various diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stem cell - an undifferentiated cell with the potential to develop into specialized cells

Etymology

From 'stem cell' (cell with the ability to develop into different cell types) + 'research'.
