yoga guru

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊɡə ɡuˈru / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡə ˈɡʊəruː / (BrE)
an expert or spiritual teacher in the practice of yoga and meditation
expert teacher of yoga and meditation

Examples

After studying for years, he became a renowned yoga guru in his community.
Many people seek advice from a yoga guru to deepen their meditation practice.
The yoga guru led a peaceful retreat in the mountains.

Synset Examples

She consulted a yoga guru for guidance on meditation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yogi - a practitioner or teacher of yoga
  • Synonym: spiritual teacher - a person who provides spiritual guidance or instruction

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'yoga' (union, discipline) and 'guru' (teacher, master).
