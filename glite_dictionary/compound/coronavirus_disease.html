coronavirus disease

1. NOUN

/ kəˈroʊnəˌvaɪrəs dɪˈziz / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnəˌvaɪərəs dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
An infectious illness caused by a virus from the family Orthocoronavirinae, including illnesses such as COVID-19, typically affecting the respiratory system.
disease caused by a coronavirus
medical

Examples

She was hospitalized after testing positive for coronavirus disease.
Researchers are working to develop new treatments for coronavirus disease.
Millions worldwide have been affected by coronavirus disease since the outbreak began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: COVID-19 - A specific coronavirus disease caused by SARS-CoV-2
  • Synonym: SARS - Severe acute respiratory syndrome, caused by a coronavirus

Etymology

From 'coronavirus' (corona + virus) and 'disease', widely used since early 21st century.
