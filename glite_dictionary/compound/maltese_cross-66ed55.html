maltese cross

1. NOUN

/ mælˌtiz ˈkrɑs / (AmE), / mɔːlˌtiːz ˈkrɒs / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial plant (Lychnis chalcedonica) with clusters of scarlet flowers, cultivated in gardens.
scarlet-flowered Eurasian garden plant

Examples

Clusters of maltese cross brightened the far corner of her garden.
The maltese cross is prized for its vivid scarlet blooms.
He planted a row of maltese cross along the backyard fence.
