lion-hunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən ˌhʌn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən ˌhʌn.tə / (BrE)
A person who hunts lions, either for sport or professionally.
person who hunts lions

Examples

The legendary lion-hunter returned to the village after a successful expedition.
She read a biography of a famous lion-hunter who had explored East Africa.
As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a lion-hunter and traveling the savannah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunter - person who hunts wild animals
  • Degree Lower: lion - large wild cat native to Africa and parts of Asia

2. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən ˌhʌn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən ˌhʌn.tə / (BrE)
A person who seeks to associate with or attract well-known or fashionable people, especially as guests.
person seeking association with social celebrities

Examples

The party was full of lion-hunters eager to be seen with celebrities.
As a notorious lion-hunter, she never missed an opportunity to invite famous guests to her home.
He was more interested in being a lion-hunter than forming genuine friendships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and attends social events
