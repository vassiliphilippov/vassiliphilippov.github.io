round file

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd faɪl / (AmE), / raʊnd faɪl / (BrE)
a file with a cylindrical shape, typically used for smoothing or enlarging the inside of holes or curved surfaces
cylindrical file for holes or curves

Examples

He used a round file to smooth the edges of the drilled hole.
The mechanic reached for a round file to enlarge the opening slightly.
A round file is ideal for shaping the inside of curved surfaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat-tail file - a long, thin, round file used for similar purposes

2. NOUN

/ raʊnd faɪl / (AmE), / raʊnd faɪl / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic term for a trash can or wastebasket, used especially in office contexts to imply that something is discarded
euphemism for trash can or wastebasket

Examples

Whenever my boss rejects a report, it usually ends up in the round file.
He made a joke about adding my suggestion to the round file.
If your application isn't complete, it's likely to go straight to the round file.

Synset Examples

If it's not important, it goes straight to the round file.
He tossed the proposal into the round file.
She knew her idea would end up in the round file.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wastebasket - a small container for rubbish, typically found indoors
  • Synonym: trash can - a container for waste, especially in American English
