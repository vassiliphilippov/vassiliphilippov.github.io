television channel

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən ˈtʃænəl / (BrE)
a broadcast station on television and the programs it transmits, identified by a specific frequency or number
a TV broadcast station and its programs

Examples

She switched to her favorite television channel to watch the news.
The new television channel focuses exclusively on documentaries and educational programs.
Many children spend hours watching cartoons on the same television channel every afternoon.
