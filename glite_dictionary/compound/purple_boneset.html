purple boneset

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.pəl ˈboʊn.sɛt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pl ˈbəʊn.sɛt / (BrE)
A North American herb with whorled leaves and small clusters of flowers spotted with purple, belonging to the genus Eupatorium.
