pimento butter

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈmɛntoʊ ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / pɪˈmɛntəʊ ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter blended with mashed pimento, often used as a spread or flavoring.
butter mixed with mashed pimento

Examples

She spread a generous layer of pimento butter on her sandwich for added flavor.
The recipe calls for pimento butter to be served alongside the grilled vegetables.
We served crackers topped with pimento butter as an appetizer at the party.
