Commission on the Status of Women

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪʃən ɒn ðə ˈstætəs əv ˈwɪmən / (AmE), / kəˈmɪʃən ɒn ðə ˈstætəs əv ˈwɪmɪn / (BrE)
a United Nations commission under the Economic and Social Council that promotes gender equality and the advancement of women's rights globally
UN body promoting gender equality and women's rights

Examples

The Commission on the Status of Women hosts annual sessions to discuss progress on women's rights globally.
Many governments send delegates to the Commission on the Status of Women to share policy developments.
The Commission on the Status of Women was established to promote gender equality within the framework of the United Nations.
