ruthless goonda

1. NOUN

/ ˈruθləs ɡuːnˌdɑː / (AmE), / ˈruːθləs ˈɡuːndə / (BrE)
A particularly violent or aggressive thug, especially in South Asian contexts.
violent or aggressive thug

Examples

The neighborhood lived in fear of the ruthless goonda who controlled the streets.
Despite his charming appearance, he turned out to be a ruthless goonda involved in extortion.
Police arrested a ruthless goonda after a series of violent incidents.

Synset Examples

He had a reputation as a ruthless goonda in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal

Etymology

From Hindi-Urdu 'goṇḍā' (thug), commonly used in Indian English.
