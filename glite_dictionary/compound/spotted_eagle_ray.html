spotted eagle ray

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˈiːɡəl reɪ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈiːɡl̩ reɪ / (BrE)
a large ray found in warm seas, with a back marked by white or yellow spots
a species of ray with spotted back

Examples

While snorkeling, we were thrilled to see a spotted eagle ray gliding gracefully through the clear blue water.
The spotted eagle ray is easily recognized by its long, pointed fins and white spots.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitats of the spotted eagle ray around the world.
