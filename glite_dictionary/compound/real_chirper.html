real chirper

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈtʃɜːrpər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈtʃɜːpə / (BrE)
A person who is notably cheerful and talkative.
cheerful, talkative person

Examples

Everyone enjoyed having Emily at parties because she was a real chirper and kept the conversation lively.
Our new neighbor is a real chirper, always greeting everyone with a smile and a story.
If you need to brighten your day, just spend time with a real chirper like Kevin.

Synset Examples

She’s a real chirper, always in a good mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot
  • Antonym: introvert - a person who is shy or reserved
