health food

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ fuːd / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ fuːd / (BrE)
food that is considered to be especially good for one's health, often because it is natural or has not been processed
food believed to promote good health

Examples

Many people prefer to shop at stores that specialize in health food.
She started eating only health food after her illness.
The café offers a wide selection of health food options.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: junk food - food that is unhealthy or has little nutritional value
