pump-type pliers

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmp taɪp ˈplaɪərz / (AmE), / ˈpʌmp taɪp ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
A type of adjustable pliers with jaws set at an angle, used for gripping and turning nuts, bolts, and pipe fittings.
adjustable pliers for gripping/turning fittings

Examples

The mechanic reached for his pump-type pliers to loosen the stubborn pipe fitting.
She prefers using pump-type pliers when working on plumbing repairs at home.
You can adjust the opening of pump-type pliers to fit different sizes of nuts and bolts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjustable pliers - pliers with jaws that can be adjusted for size
