dadburn traffic

1. NOUN

/ ˈdædˌbɜrn ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / ˈdædˌbɜːn ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
Annoyingly heavy or frustrating traffic, expressed with the mild expletive 'dadburn' for emphasis.
frustrating or annoying traffic (US, informal)
informal american

Examples

I was late to work again thanks to that dadburn traffic.
She couldn't believe how much dadburn traffic there was on a Sunday morning.
If it weren't for the dadburn traffic, we'd have arrived on time.

Synset Examples

He missed the bus because of that dadburn traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic jam - a situation of vehicles stuck in heavy traffic

Etymology

"Dadburn" is a mild American euphemism for 'damned,' formed as a minced oath.
