true north

1. NOUN

/ tru nɔrθ / (AmE), / truː nɔːθ / (BrE)
A term used to refer to Canada, especially as a symbol of national values, guidance, or identity.
Canada as an ideal or national symbol

Examples

Many Canadians take pride in calling their country the true north.
Her speech invoked the resilience and unity of the true north.
The poem describes the landscapes and spirit of the true north with admiration.

Synset Examples

Canada is often referred to as the true north.
She embraced the values of the true north in her speech.

Etymology

Popularized in Canada’s national anthem, referencing its northern geography and symbolic steadfastness.
