mountain mint

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn mɪnt / (BrE)
any of a group of perennial herbs of the genus Pycnanthemum, native to eastern North America and California, known for their aromatic leaves and resemblance to true mint.
aromatic herb of genus Pycnanthemum

Examples

The garden was filled with the refreshing scent of mountain mint in the summer breeze.
Many pollinators are attracted to mountain mint because of its aromatic flowers.
She brewed a soothing tea using leaves from the mountain mint growing in her backyard.
