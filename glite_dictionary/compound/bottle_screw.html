bottle screw

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬l skruː / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.l̩ skruː / (BrE)
A device used for drawing corks from bottles; a corkscrew.
device for pulling corks from bottles

Examples

She reached for the bottle screw to open the vintage wine.
The old wooden-handled bottle screw belonged to his grandfather.
Make sure you have a bottle screw handy before the guests arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corkscrew - a tool for drawing corks from bottles
