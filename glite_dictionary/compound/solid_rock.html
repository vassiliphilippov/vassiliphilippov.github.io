solid rock

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːlɪd rɑːk / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd rɒk / (BrE)
used metaphorically to refer to someone or something that provides a strong and reliable foundation or support.
strong and reliable foundation (figurative)

Examples

Her unwavering support was the solid rock on which I built my confidence.
In challenging times, he proved to be the solid rock for his entire family.
Trust is the solid rock of every successful partnership.

Synset Examples

Their friendship has always been a solid rock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock - a person or thing that is a reliable support
