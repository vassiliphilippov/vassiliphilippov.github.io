marmalade bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrməˌleɪd bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməˌleɪd bʊʃ / (BrE)
an evergreen South American shrub with showy, trumpet-shaped orange flowers, often grown as an ornamental or houseplant
evergreen shrub with orange flowers

Examples

The marmalade bush brightened up the conservatory with its vivid orange blooms.
Gardeners recommend planting the marmalade bush in a sunny, sheltered spot for best results.
She admired the unique trumpet-shaped flowers of the marmalade bush on her patio.
