whisky neat

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski nit / (AmE), / ˈwɪski niːt / (BrE)
A serving of whisky with no ice, water, or mixer, typically served at room temperature.
whisky served plain, without ice or mixer

Examples

After a long day, he enjoyed a whisky neat at the local bar.
She prefers her whisky neat rather than with soda or ice.
The bartender asked if I wanted my whisky neat or on the rocks.

Synset Examples

he ordered a whiskey neat

Related Terms

  • Antonym: whisky on the rocks - whisky served over ice
