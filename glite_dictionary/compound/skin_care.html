skin care

1. NOUN

/ skɪn kɛr / (AmE), / skɪn keə / (BrE)
the practice of keeping the skin clean, healthy, and attractive, often by using products such as cleansers, moisturizers, and treatments
practices and products for maintaining healthy skin

Examples

Daily skin care routines can help prevent acne and dryness.
She invested in quality products for her skin care regimen.
The market for skin care is growing rapidly worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skincare - alternative spelling of 'skin care'
  • Synonym: beauty treatment - activities to improve the appearance of the body or face
