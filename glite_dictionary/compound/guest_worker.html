guest worker

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛst ˈwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛst ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who is allowed to work temporarily in a foreign country, usually under a legal arrangement without permanent residency rights
temporary foreign worker

Examples

The government created a program to attract guest workers for seasonal jobs in agriculture.
Many guest workers face challenges adapting to life in a new country.
After his contract expired, the guest worker had to return to his home country.

Synset Examples

a Moroccan guestworker in Canada was accused of aiding terrorists

Related Terms

  • Synonym: migrant worker - a person who moves from place to place for seasonal work, especially in agriculture
