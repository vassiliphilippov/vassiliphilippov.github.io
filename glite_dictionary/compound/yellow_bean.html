yellow bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A variety of snap bean with yellow pods, eaten as a vegetable.
a snap bean with yellow pods

Examples

She picked some fresh yellow beans from the garden for dinner.
The recipe called for both yellow beans and green beans to add color.
Farmers' markets often offer bundles of yellow beans during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - snap bean with green pods
