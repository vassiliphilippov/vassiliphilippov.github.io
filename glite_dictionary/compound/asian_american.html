Asian American

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃ(ə)n əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
A person living in the United States who is of Asian descent.
an American of Asian descent

Examples

Many Asian American students attend the university and participate in cultural organizations.
She is proud of her Asian American heritage and often shares her family's traditions during community events.
The film highlights the experiences of a young Asian American growing up in Los Angeles.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Asian - a person from Asia or of Asian descent
  • Degree Lower: American - a person from the United States
