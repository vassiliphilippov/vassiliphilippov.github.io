to wrangle a better deal

1. VERB

/ ˈræŋɡəl ə ˈbɛtər diːl / (AmE), / ˈræŋɡl ə ˈbɛtə diːl / (BrE)
to negotiate forcefully or skillfully in order to obtain more favorable terms in an agreement or transaction
negotiate to achieve better terms

Examples

After hours of discussion, she managed to wrangle a better deal for their new car purchase.
He was determined to wrangle a better deal before signing the contract.
Negotiating with several suppliers, they hoped to wrangle a better deal for bulk orders.

Synset Examples

He tried to wrangle a better deal for his client.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to negotiate - to try to reach an agreement through discussion
