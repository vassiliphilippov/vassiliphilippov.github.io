power meter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər ˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌmiːtə / (BrE)
A device that measures the amount of electrical power used by a circuit or appliance.
device measuring electric power usage

Examples

The power meter showed a significant increase in consumption after the new appliance was installed.
Technicians used a power meter to determine where most of the electricity was being used in the building.
You can monitor your home's energy usage by regularly checking the power meter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric meter - a device that measures the consumption of electricity
