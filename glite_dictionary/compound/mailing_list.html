mailing list

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of names and addresses used for sending information or advertising material to multiple people
list of people to send mail to

Examples

Our company maintains a mailing list to keep customers informed about new products.
If you want to receive the newsletter, sign up for the mailing list on our website.
They removed my address from the mailing list after I requested to unsubscribe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distribution list - a list of email addresses for sending group messages
