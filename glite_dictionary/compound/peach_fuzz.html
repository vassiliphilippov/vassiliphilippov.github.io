peach fuzz

1. NOUN

/ piːtʃ fʌz / (AmE), / piːtʃ fʌz / (BrE)
Very fine, soft hair, especially that found on a person's face, resembling the surface of a peach.
fine, soft facial hair

Examples

Many teenagers start to notice peach fuzz on their upper lips during puberty.
She brushed the peach fuzz on her baby's head gently with her hand.
The young boy's cheeks were covered in light peach fuzz, making him look even younger.

Synset Examples

She felt self-conscious about the peach fuzz on her cheeks.
The baby’s peach fuzz made him look even cuter.
His face was still covered in soft peach fuzz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - very fine, soft hair or feathers, especially on young animals or people

Etymology

Reference to the soft, downy surface of a peach.
