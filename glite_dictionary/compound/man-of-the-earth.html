man-of-the-earth

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn əv ði ˈɝːθ / (AmE), / ˌmæn əv ði ˈɜːθ / (BrE)
A tropical American trailing or climbing perennial herb with a very large starchy root, sometimes considered a source of sweet potato.

The man-of-the-earth is known for its massive, starchy root that can weigh several kilograms.

The roots of the man-of-the-earth can grow exceptionally long and thick underground.
