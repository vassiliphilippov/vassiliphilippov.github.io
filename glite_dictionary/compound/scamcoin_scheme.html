scamcoin scheme

1. NOUN

/ ˈskæm kɔɪn skiːm / (AmE), / ˈskam kɔɪn skiːm / (BrE)
a fraudulent enterprise involving the creation or promotion of worthless or deceptive cryptocurrencies
fraudulent cryptocurrency operation

Examples

The investors lost thousands of dollars in a scamcoin scheme that promised guaranteed returns.
New regulations aim to target those behind complex scamcoin schemes in the cryptocurrency market.
Before investing, always check to avoid falling victim to a scamcoin scheme.

Synset Examples

Many believe that scamcoin schemes will eventually collapse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptocurrency scam - fraudulent scheme involving digital currencies
  • Synonym: Ponzi scheme - an investment scam promising high returns with little risk to investors

Etymology

Blend of 'scam' (fraudulent scheme) and 'coin' (referring to cryptocurrency), plus 'scheme'.
