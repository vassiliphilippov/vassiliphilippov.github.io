turkey-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɝːki saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki saɪzd / (BrE)
being approximately as large as a turkey
about the size of a turkey

Examples

The newly discovered dinosaur was about turkey-sized, surprising many paleontologists.
We spotted a turkey-sized bird near the lake during our hike.
Some ancient mammals grew only to a turkey-sized stature before going extinct.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dog-sized - being approximately as large as a dog
  • Degree Higher: ostrich-sized - being approximately as large as an ostrich
