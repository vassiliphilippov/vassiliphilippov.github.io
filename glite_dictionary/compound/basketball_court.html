basketball court

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːt / (BrE)
an indoor or outdoor area marked with lines and prepared for playing basketball
a place where basketball is played

Examples

The school built a new basketball court behind the gymnasium.
She spent her afternoons practicing free throws on the basketball court.
Rain flooded the outdoor basketball court, making it unusable for the weekend tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tennis court - a rectangular area where tennis is played
