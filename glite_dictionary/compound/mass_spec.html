mass spec

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæs ˈspɛk / (AmE), / ˌmæs ˈspɛk / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation for mass spectrometry, an analytical technique used to measure the molecular composition of a sample.
abbreviation for mass spectrometry
informal
