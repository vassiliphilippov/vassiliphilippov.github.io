culinary delight

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɪˌnɛri dɪˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪnəri dɪˈlaɪt / (BrE)
a dish or food that is extremely enjoyable to eat
very enjoyable dish or food

Examples

The new French restaurant offers a culinary delight with every course.
Her famous chocolate cake is considered a culinary delight by everyone who tastes it.
We traveled across Italy in search of an authentic culinary delight.

Synset Examples

The meal was absolutely fantastico, a true culinary delight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicacy - a rare or luxurious food regarded as especially pleasing
