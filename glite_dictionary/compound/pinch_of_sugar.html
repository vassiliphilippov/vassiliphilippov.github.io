pinch of sugar

1. NOUN

/ pɪntʃ əv ˈʃʊɡər / (AmE), / pɪntʃ əv ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
a small amount of sugar, typically as much as can be held between the thumb and forefinger
a small amount of sugar

Examples

The chef sprinkled a pinch of sugar into the sauce to balance the flavors.
Add just a pinch of sugar to your tea for a subtle sweetness.
Sometimes a pinch of sugar can make tomatoes taste less acidic in salads.

Synset Examples

Add a pinch of sugar to the recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinch of salt - a small amount of salt, typically held between the fingers
