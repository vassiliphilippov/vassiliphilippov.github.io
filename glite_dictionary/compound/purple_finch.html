purple finch

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.pəl fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a North American finch with a raspberry-red head, breast, and rump in the male, and brown-streaked plumage in the female
a North American finch species

Examples

We spotted a purple finch hopping among the pine branches in the early morning light.
The male purple finch displays vibrant reddish plumage during the breeding season.
Several purple finches were feeding at the bird feeder outside our window.
