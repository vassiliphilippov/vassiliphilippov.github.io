game day

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪm deɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪm deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which an important sports match or competition takes place
day of a sports event

Examples

Many fans arrived at the stadium early on game day to get the best seats.
She always prepares a special meal for her family on game day.
The energy on campus was electric during game day celebrations.

Synset Examples

Everyone was excited for the game day festivities.
