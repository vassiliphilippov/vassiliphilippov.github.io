trouser press

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraʊzɚ prɛs / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə prɛs / (BrE)
a device for removing creases from trousers by pressing them between heated pads
device for pressing trousers

Examples

Every hotel room was equipped with a trouser press for the guests' convenience.
He used the trouser press to make sure his pants looked sharp for the meeting.
They bought a trouser press as a Christmas gift for their father.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press - a device for pressing clothes to remove wrinkles
