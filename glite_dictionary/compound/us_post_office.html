US Post Office

1. NOUN

/ ju ɛs poʊst ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / juː ɛs pəʊst ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
An independent agency of the US federal government responsible for mail delivery to individuals and businesses within the United States.
US mail delivery agency
american

Examples

I had to go to the US Post Office to mail my package this morning.
The US Post Office announced changes to its delivery schedule next month.
Many small towns rely on their US Post Office as a community hub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United States Postal Service - the official postal delivery agency of the United States
