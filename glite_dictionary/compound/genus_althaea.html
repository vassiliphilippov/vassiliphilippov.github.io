genus Althaea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ælˈθiːə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ælˈθiːə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, which includes hollyhocks and related species.
genus of flowering plants, includes hollyhocks

Examples

Many species in the genus Althaea are valued for their ornamental flowers.
The genus Althaea includes plants commonly known as hollyhocks and marshmallow.
Recent studies have debated whether some species should remain in the genus Althaea or be placed elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genus Alcea - A genus of flowering plants, sometimes considered synonymous with genus Althaea

Etymology

From New Latin 'Althaea', deriving from Greek 'althaía' (a mallow plant).
