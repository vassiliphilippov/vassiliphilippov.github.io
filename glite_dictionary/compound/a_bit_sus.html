a bit sus

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt sʌs / (AmE), / ə bɪt sʌs / (BrE)
Somewhat suspicious or questionable; slightly suspect.
somewhat suspicious; slightly suspect
informal slang

Examples

His explanation for being late sounded a bit sus to everyone in the room.
The way she hid her phone was a bit sus.
If a deal online looks a bit sus, it's probably best to avoid it.

Synset Examples

That behavior seems a bit sus to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspicious - having or showing a cautious distrust; suspecting something is wrong or dishonest
  • Antonym: legit - completely legal or genuine; not suspicious

Etymology

Clipping of 'suspicious'; 'sus' originates in British slang and was popularized online and through video games.
