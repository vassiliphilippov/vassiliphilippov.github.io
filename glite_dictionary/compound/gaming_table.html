gaming table

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table designed or used for gambling, often equipped with a gameboard, compartments, or slots for chips
table used for gambling

Examples

The casino floor was crowded, but he managed to find an open spot at the gaming table.
Each gaming table in the room was surrounded by eager players and flashing lights.
She lost track of time as she watched the action unfold at the blackjack gaming table.
