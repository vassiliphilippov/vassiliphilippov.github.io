turkey leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːki ˌlɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki ˌlɛɡ / (BrE)
the lower part of a turkey's leg, typically cooked and served as food
lower part of a turkey's leg (food)

Examples

He ordered a giant turkey leg at the fair and could barely finish it.
The chef seasoned each turkey leg before roasting them in the oven.
After Thanksgiving dinner, only a single turkey leg was left on the platter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drumstick - the lower part of a bird's leg, especially as food
