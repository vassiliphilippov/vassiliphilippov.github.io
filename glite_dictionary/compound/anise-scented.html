anise-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈænɪs ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈænɪs ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having the distinct smell of anise.
smelling of anise

Examples

The cake had an anise-scented glaze that reminded me of childhood holidays.
She wore an anise-scented perfume that lingered in the room long after she left.
A waft of anise-scented steam rose from the teapot, filling the kitchen with its aroma.
