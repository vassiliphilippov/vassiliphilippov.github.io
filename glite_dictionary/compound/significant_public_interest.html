significant public interest

1. NOUN

/ sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈpʌblɪk ˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈpʌblɪk ˈɪntrɛst / (BrE)
a large amount of attention or concern from the general public, often towards an event, issue, or subject
widespread public attention or concern

Examples

The proposed law has sparked significant public interest across the country.
The documentary attracted significant public interest upon its release.
Matters of significant public interest are often discussed in national media.

Synset Examples

Prop H8 has generated significant public interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public interest - the welfare or well-being of the general public
  • Synonym: public concern - widespread worry or attention among the general population
