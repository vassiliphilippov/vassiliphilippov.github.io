musical theme

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl θim / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl θiːm / (BrE)
the main melodic subject or idea in a piece of music, often repeated and developed throughout the composition
main melodic subject in music

Examples

The musical theme was introduced by the orchestra at the beginning of the symphony.
Each character in the film was given a distinct musical theme to represent them.
The composer skillfully transforms the original musical theme throughout the piece.

Synset Examples

the theme is announced in the first measures
the accompanist picked up the idea and elaborated it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theme - main subject in a piece of music or writing
