ill luck

1. NOUN

/ ɪl lʌk / (AmE), / ɪl lʌk / (BrE)
bad fortune; a condition in which things happen unfavorably or by mischance.
bad fortune; misfortune

Examples

Despite her ill luck, she managed to stay positive and hopeful for the future.
He blamed his ill luck for missing the train just seconds before it departed.
Sometimes ill luck follows you for a while before fortune changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad luck - misfortune; a state of experiencing unfortunate events
  • Antonym: good luck - fortune; a state of experiencing favorable outcomes
