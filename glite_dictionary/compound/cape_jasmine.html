cape jasmine

1. NOUN

/ keɪp ˈdʒæsmɪn / (AmE), / keɪp ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub widely cultivated for its large, fragrant, waxy white flowers and glossy leaves; also known as gardenia
evergreen shrub with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The cape jasmine filled the garden with its sweet scent every evening.
She picked a cape jasmine blossom and placed it in a vase on the table.
Many people choose cape jasmine as an ornamental plant because of its beautiful white flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardenia - an evergreen shrub with fragrant white flowers, synonym of cape jasmine

Etymology

The name refers to the Cape of Good Hope, from where the plant was erroneously thought to originate, and 'jasmine' for its scent.
