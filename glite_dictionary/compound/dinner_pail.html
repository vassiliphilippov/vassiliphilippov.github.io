dinner pail

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnɚ peɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə peɪl / (BrE)
a small container, typically a metal pail, used by a worker to carry lunch or dinner to work
container for carrying lunch or dinner

Examples

He took his dinner pail with him to the factory every morning.
The old dinner pail was dented from years of use.
Stories of the American worker often include mention of the humble dinner pail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunch pail - a small container used to carry lunch
