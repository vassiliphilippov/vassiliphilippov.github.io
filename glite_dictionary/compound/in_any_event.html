in any event

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈɛni ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɛni ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that supports, qualifies, or follows regardless of previous circumstances; anyway; in any case.
regardless; anyway; in any case

Examples

In any event, we will need to submit the report by Friday.
She might be late, but in any event, the meeting will start on time.
I don't know if the package arrived; in any event, I'll check with the mailroom.

Synset Examples

Anyhow, he is dead now
I think they're asleep; anyhow, they're quiet
I don't know what happened to it; anyway, it's gone
anyway, there is another factor to consider
I don't know how it started; in any case, there was a brief scuffle
in any event, the government faced a serious protest
but at any rate he got a knighthood for it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in any case - used to introduce a statement regardless of previous circumstances
  • Synonym: at any rate - used to suggest a statement holds true regardless of other factors
  • Synonym: anyhow - used to introduce a statement that supports or follows regardless of previous statements
