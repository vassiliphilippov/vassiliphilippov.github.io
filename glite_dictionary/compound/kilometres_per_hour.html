kilometres per hour

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈlɑːmɪtərz pər ˈaʊər / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtəz pə ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A unit of speed equal to the number of kilometres traveled in one hour.
unit of speed (km traveled per hour)

Examples

The car was travelling at 80 kilometres per hour when it passed us.
Cyclists can easily reach speeds of 30 kilometres per hour on flat roads.
The sign indicated a maximum speed limit of 50 kilometres per hour in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miles per hour - unit of speed (miles traveled per hour)
