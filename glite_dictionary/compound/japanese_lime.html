Japanese lime

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæn.ɪs laɪm / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæn.ɪs laɪm / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to Japan, often grown as an ornamental plant
ornamental tree native to Japan

Examples

The Japanese lime is prized for its attractive foliage and decorative appearance in gardens.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the Japanese lime under various climate conditions.
We planted a Japanese lime in the front yard to add shade and beauty to the landscape.
