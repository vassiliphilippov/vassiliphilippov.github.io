planting process

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæntɪŋ ˌprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈplɑːntɪŋ ˌprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
the series of steps or methods involved in planting seeds or young plants in soil
procedure for planting seeds or plants

Examples

The new irrigation system improved the planting process for local farmers.
Understanding the planting process is essential for a successful vegetable garden.
Automation has made the planting process faster and more efficient.

Synset Examples

The autogerminal device simplified the planting process immensely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sowing - the act of scattering seeds on or in the ground
  • Antonym: harvesting - the process of gathering mature crops from the fields
