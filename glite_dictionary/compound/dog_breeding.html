dog breeding

1. NOUN

/ dɔg ˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / dɒg ˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of mating selected dogs to produce offspring with specific qualities or characteristics.
mating dogs to produce desired traits

Examples

He has been involved in dog breeding for over twenty years.
Ethical dog breeding requires careful selection of healthy parents.
The seminar focused on advances in dog breeding techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeding - the mating and production of offspring by animals
