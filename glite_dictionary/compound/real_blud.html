real blud

1. NOUN

/ ɹiəl blʌd / (AmE), / ɹiːəl blʌd / (BrE)
(slang) A very close friend or mate; someone you trust and have a tight bond with.
close friend; trusted mate (slang)
slang british

Examples

He's my real blud; I trust him with everything.
You don't have to pretend around a real blud.
Whenever I'm in trouble, my real blud always has my back.

Synset Examples

He's a real blud; always borrowing money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mate - a friend or companion
  • Synonym: bro - informal term for brother or close male friend

Etymology

From 'real' plus 'blud', slang variant of 'blood', used for close friend or brotherhood, especially in UK urban slang.
