Kuwaiti dinar

1. NOUN

/ kuˈweɪti dɪˈnɑr / (AmE), / kuːˈweɪti dɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Kuwait, equal to 1,000 fils
basic monetary unit of Kuwait

Examples

The value of the Kuwaiti dinar is among the highest of all world currencies.
She exchanged her dollars for Kuwaiti dinars at the airport.
Many expatriates in Kuwait are paid their salaries in Kuwaiti dinar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinar - a monetary unit used in several countries

Etymology

From 'Kuwaiti,' relating to Kuwait, and 'dinar,' from Arabic 'dīnār', itself from Latin 'denarius' (a Roman coin).
