milking ability

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkɪŋ əˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkɪŋ əˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the capacity of an animal, especially livestock, to produce milk.
capacity to produce milk (animals)

Examples

Farmers often select cows for their exceptional milking ability.
Recent breeding has improved the milking ability of dairy goats.
The award was given to the cow with the highest milking ability in the herd.

Synset Examples

They are also extremely functional females which excel in fertility and milking ability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk production - the amount of milk produced by an animal
