skiing race

1. NOUN

/ ˈskiːɪŋ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈskiːɪŋ reɪs / (BrE)
a competitive event in which individuals or teams race on skis, usually down a slope or across snow-covered terrain
competitive race on skis

Examples

She trained all winter to compete in the annual skiing race at the resort.
The skiing race was postponed due to heavy snowfall and poor visibility.
Winning the skiing race required not just speed but also excellent technique on the steep slopes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski race - an alternative term for skiing race, often used interchangeably
