jade vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒeɪd vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪd vaɪn / (BrE)
a vigorous evergreen climbing plant from the Philippines with striking green, claw-shaped flowers often grown for ornamental purposes
Philippine climbing plant with green flowers

Examples

The jade vine attracts visitors to the botanical garden with its brilliant turquoise blossoms.
We saw a jade vine climbing up the trellis at the tropical plant exhibit.
Cultivating a jade vine in cooler climates requires a heated greenhouse.
