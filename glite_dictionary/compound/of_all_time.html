of all time

1. ADV

/ əv ɔl taɪm / (AmE), / əv ɔːl taɪm / (BrE)
Used to emphasize that something is the best, worst, most important, etc., that has ever existed or happened.
across all history; ever

Examples

He is considered the greatest chess player of all time.
That movie is one of the most successful of all time.
She broke the record for the fastest marathon of all time.

Synset Examples

did you ever smoke?
the best con man of all time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ever - at any time; used to mean 'at any point in history'
