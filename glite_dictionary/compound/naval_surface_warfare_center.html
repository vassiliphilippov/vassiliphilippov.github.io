Naval Surface Warfare Center

1. PROPN

/ ˈneɪvəl ˈsɜːrfɪs ˈwɔːrˌfɛr ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ˈsɜːfɪs ˈwɔːfɛə ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
A U.S. military agency responsible for providing scientific, engineering, and technical support related to surface warfare for the Navy.
U.S. Navy research and support agency for surface warfare

Examples

The Naval Surface Warfare Center employs thousands of engineers and scientists to support the U.S. Navy.
She visited the Naval Surface Warfare Center to learn about the latest advancements in naval technology.
A team from the Naval Surface Warfare Center collaborated with industry partners on the new defense project.
