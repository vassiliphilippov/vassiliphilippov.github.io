stannous fluoride

1. NOUN

/ ˈstænəs ˈflʊraɪd / (AmE), / ˈstanəs ˈflʊərʌɪd / (BrE)
a white, odorless powder used in toothpaste to prevent tooth decay by providing fluoride
compound used in toothpaste for dental care
medical

Examples

Many dentists recommend toothpaste with stannous fluoride to help fight cavities and gum disease.
The whitening toothpaste contains stannous fluoride as its active ingredient.
Research shows that stannous fluoride is effective in reducing dental sensitivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodium fluoride - another compound used to fluoridate toothpaste

Etymology

From 'stannous', meaning containing tin (from Latin 'stannum' for tin), and 'fluoride', a compound with fluorine.
