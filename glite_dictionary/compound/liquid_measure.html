liquid measure

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a unit of capacity for measuring the volume of liquids or liquid containers
unit for measuring liquid volume

Examples

The recipe calls for a liquid measure of two cups of milk.
Please use a proper liquid measure to ensure accuracy when pouring the solution.
Gallon and liter are common units of liquid measure in different parts of the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dry measure - unit of capacity for dry goods
  • Synonym: volume - the amount of space that a substance or object occupies
