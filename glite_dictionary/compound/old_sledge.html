old sledge

1. NOUN

/ oʊld slɛdʒ / (AmE), / əʊld slɛdʒ / (BrE)
A card game similar to all fours, in which a total of seven points is required to win.
card game similar to all fours

Examples

My grandfather taught me how to play old sledge when I was a child.
They spent hours at the cabin playing old sledge and telling stories.
At family gatherings, old sledge was always a popular card game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all fours - a trick-taking card game for two to six players
