butt hinge

1. NOUN

/ bʌt hɪndʒ / (AmE), / bʌt hɪndʒ / (BrE)
A hinge that is set flush into the edge of a door and its frame, commonly used for doors.
hinge set flush into door edge and frame

Examples

The carpenter installed a butt hinge to secure the new wooden door in place.
Most interior doors are fitted using a standard butt hinge for smooth opening and closing.
After years of use, the butt hinge had become rusty and needed replacement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hinge - a movable joint or mechanism by which a door, gate, or lid swings
