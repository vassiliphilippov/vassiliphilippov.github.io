Black Rock Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˌblæk ˈrɑk ˈdɛrzərt / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˈrɒk ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A desert region in northwestern Nevada, USA, known for its flat, dry lakebed and cultural events.
desert in northwestern Nevada, USA
