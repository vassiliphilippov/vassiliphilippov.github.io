packing plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a factory where animals are slaughtered, processed, and packed for sale as meat products
factory where livestock are processed for meat

Examples

The company built a new packing plant to increase meat production efficiency.
Hundreds of workers are employed at the packing plant outside town.
Safety regulations at the packing plant have recently become more stringent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slaughterhouse - a place where animals are killed for their meat
