checkout time

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛk.aʊt taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.aʊt taɪm / (BrE)
the latest time by which a hotel room must be vacated by a guest
latest time to vacate a hotel room

Examples

Our checkout time is 11 a.m., so please have your bags ready before then.
He asked the receptionist if it was possible to extend his checkout time.
Because of our early flight, we requested a special checkout time this morning.

Synset Examples

the checkout here is 12 noon

Related Terms

  • Antonym: check-in time - the earliest time you may occupy a hotel room
