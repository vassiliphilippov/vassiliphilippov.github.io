insurance agent

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊr.əns ˌeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərəns ˌeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
a person who is authorized to sell insurance policies and advise clients on insurance matters
person who sells insurance policies

Examples

The insurance agent explained the different policy options to the couple.
After the accident, she contacted her insurance agent for guidance on filing a claim.
He decided to become an insurance agent because he enjoys helping people protect their assets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurance broker - person who arranges insurance for clients, often independently
