steam chest

1. NOUN

/ stiːm tʃɛst / (AmE), / stiːm tʃɛst / (BrE)
the chamber in a steam engine from which steam is distributed to the cylinders
chamber distributing steam in an engine

Examples

The engineer inspected the steam chest for leaks before starting the locomotive.
If the steam chest is damaged, pressure cannot be maintained in the engine.
Proper maintenance of the steam chest is critical for efficient steam engine operation.
