naval installation

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvəl ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.vəl ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A facility used by a navy for supporting and servicing naval forces and operations.
facility for supporting navy forces

Examples

The new naval installation was built to enhance the country's maritime security.
Many personnel work at the naval installation to maintain the fleet's readiness.
During the tour, the admiral inspected every naval installation on the coast.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: military installation - a facility for military operations, broader than just the navy
  • Synonym: naval base - a main base or headquarters for naval forces
