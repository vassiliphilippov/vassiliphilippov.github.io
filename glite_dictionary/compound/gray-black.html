gray-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪˌblæk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌblæk / (BrE)
Having the color black with a tinge or mixture of grey.
black tinged with grey

Examples

The artist chose a gray-black shade to paint the stormy sky in her landscape.
His coat was a beautiful gray-black that shimmered under the light.
We replaced the carpet with a gray-black one to match the modern décor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charcoal - dark grayish-black color
  • Degree Higher: jet-black - pure, deep black
  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
