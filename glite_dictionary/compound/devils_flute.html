devil's flute

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz fluːt / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz fluːt / (BrE)
a metaphorical term for something powerfully enchanting or captivating, especially alluring music or melody
metaphorical term for captivating music

Examples

The sound of the devil's flute drifted through the night, captivating all who listened.
Legends spoke of musicians who could charm the world with their devil's flute.
The haunting melody was like a devil's flute, impossible to resist.

Synset Examples

He played the devil's flute, mesmerizing the crowd.
The music from the devil's flute enchanted everyone at the party.
The sound of the devil's flute could be heard from afar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: siren song - a seductive or deceptive appeal, especially through music
