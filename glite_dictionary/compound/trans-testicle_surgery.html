trans-testicle surgery

1. NOUN

/ trænz ˈtɛstɪkəl ˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / tranz ˈtɛstɪkəl ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a surgical procedure involving the removal, modification, or reconstruction of the testicles, typically as part of gender-affirming care for transgender individuals
gender-affirming testicular surgery
medical

Examples

Many patients seek trans-testicle surgery as part of their gender-affirming transition.
The doctor explained the risks and benefits of trans-testicle surgery during the consultation.
Advances in trans-testicle surgery have improved patient outcomes and satisfaction.

Synset Examples

Understanding trans-testicle surgery requires careful research.

Etymology

Formed from 'trans-', relating to transgender, and 'testicle', from Latin 'testiculus'.
