to brew booch

1. VERB

/ bruː buːtʃ / (AmE), / bruː buːtʃ / (BrE)
to make kombucha at home, especially using a fermentation process with live cultures
make homemade kombucha
informal slang

Examples

Every Sunday, she likes to brew booch with her friends in the kitchen.
I'm going to brew booch this weekend using a new pineapple recipe.
Many people started to brew booch at home during the pandemic.

Synset Examples

She started brewing her own booch at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kombucha - fermented, effervescent tea drink

Etymology

brew: Old English *breowan* (to brew); booch: shortening of kombucha, from Japanese *kombucha* (a tea made with kelp), but in English refers to the fermented tea drink.
