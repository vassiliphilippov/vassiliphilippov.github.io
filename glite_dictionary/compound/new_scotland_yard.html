New Scotland Yard

1. PROPN

/ nu ˈskɑtlənd jɑrd / (AmE), / nju ˈskɒtlənd jɑːd / (BrE)
the headquarters of the Metropolitan Police Service and its detective department in London
Metropolitan Police HQ (London)
british

Examples

Police officers at New Scotland Yard issued a statement regarding the investigation.
Security was tight outside New Scotland Yard following the press briefing.
Many major cases are handled by the detectives of New Scotland Yard each year.

Etymology

Named for its original location on Great Scotland Yard street in Westminster, London.
