Latin Quarter

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæt.ən ˈkwɔr.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈlæt.ɪn ˈkwɔː.tə / (BrE)
an area in Paris on the southern bank of the Seine, renowned for its artistic, intellectual, and student life
historic district in Paris known for student life

Examples

She always dreamed of living in the Latin Quarter while studying art in Paris.
The narrow streets of the Latin Quarter are filled with cafés and bookshops.
Many famous writers once gathered in the Latin Quarter to discuss their work.

Etymology

From Latin, referring to the language once commonly spoken by students and scholars in the area; 'Quarter' denotes a city district.
