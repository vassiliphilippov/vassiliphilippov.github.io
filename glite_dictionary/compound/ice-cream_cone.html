ice-cream cone

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌkrim koʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌkriːm kəʊn / (BrE)
A crisp, conical wafer used to hold a serving of ice cream.
conical wafer holding ice cream

Examples

On a hot day, a ice-cream cone is the perfect treat.
He bought her a chocolate ice-cream cone from the corner store.
Children waited in line to get their favorite flavor in an ice-cream cone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wafer - a thin, crisp biscuit; the edible cone part
