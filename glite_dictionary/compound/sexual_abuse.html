sexual abuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈbjus / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
The crime of forcing or coercing another person into unwanted sexual activity, often through force, threat, or manipulation.
crime of coerced or forced sexual activity

Examples

He was arrested and charged with sexual abuse of a minor.
Victims of sexual abuse often require long-term counseling and support.
The organization works to prevent sexual abuse through education and awareness programs.

Synset Examples

most states have replaced the common law definition of rape with statutes defining sexual assault

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual contact or behavior, often by force or threat
  • Degree Higher: rape - crime of forcing someone to have sexual intercourse without consent
