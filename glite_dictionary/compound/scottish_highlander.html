Scottish Highlander

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːtɪʃ ˈhaɪləndər / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈhaɪləndə / (BrE)
a person native to or living in the Highlands of Scotland, often associated with distinct cultural traditions
person from the Scottish Highlands

Examples

A proud Scottish Highlander wore his clan's tartan at the annual festival.
Many Scottish Highlanders maintained their traditions even after moving abroad.
The documentary followed the daily life of a modern Scottish Highlander in the remote glens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Highlander - a person from the Highlands region, especially of Scotland
  • Antonym: Lowlander - a person from the Lowlands of Scotland
