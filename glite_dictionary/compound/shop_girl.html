shop girl

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ʃɒp ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman who works as a sales assistant in a shop
young female shop assistant

Examples

The young shop girl greeted every customer with a friendly smile.
During the holidays, the shop girl worked extra hours to help with the busy crowds.
He asked the shop girl for advice on which tie would look best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales assistant - someone who sells goods in a shop
