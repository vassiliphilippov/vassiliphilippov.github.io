analog watch

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ə.lɔɡ wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈæn.ə.lɒɡ wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a watch that displays the time using rotating hands on a numbered dial rather than with digits
watch with hands on a dial

Examples

He prefers wearing an analog watch because he likes the traditional look of moving hands.
My grandfather gave me his old analog watch as a birthday present.
Learning to tell time on an analog watch can help children understand the concept of hours and minutes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: digital watch - a watch that shows the time with digits on a display

Etymology

From 'analog', meaning representing data by continuously variable quantities, and 'watch'.
