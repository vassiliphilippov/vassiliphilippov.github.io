proud ginge

1. NOUN

/ praʊd dʒɪndʒ / (AmE), / praʊd dʒɪndʒ / (BrE)
A person with red hair who embraces and takes pride in their identity as a redhead.
a red-haired person proud of their identity
informal british

Examples

After years of teasing, she now calls herself a proud ginge on social media.
He's a proud ginge who often celebrates National Redhead Day.
The campaign featured several proud ginges sharing their personal stories.

Synset Examples

She joked about being a proud ginge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginge - British informal term for a person with red hair
  • Synonym: redhead - a person with red hair

Etymology

From British slang 'ginge' (short for 'ginger,' meaning a person with red hair).
