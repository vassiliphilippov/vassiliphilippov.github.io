mountain range

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tn ˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
a line or group of connected mountains forming a continuous elevated area
a group of connected mountains

Examples

The Himalayas are the highest mountain range in the world.
A wide river ran parallel to the mountain range for miles.
We hiked along the edge of the mountain range, enjoying the breathtaking views.

Synset Examples

the valley was between two ranges of hills
the plains lay just beyond the mountain range

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain - a large natural elevation of the earth's surface
  • Degree Lower: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high as a mountain
