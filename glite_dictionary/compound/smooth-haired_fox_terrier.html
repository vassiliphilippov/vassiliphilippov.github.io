smooth-haired fox terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˌsmuð ˈhɛrd ˌfɑks təˈriər / (AmE), / ˌsmuːð ˈheəd ˌfɒks ˈtɛrɪə / (BrE)
a breed of fox terrier characterized by a short, smooth coat
dog breed with a smooth short coat

Examples

The smooth-haired fox terrier is known for its lively and affectionate personality.
We decided to adopt a smooth-haired fox terrier from the animal shelter.
Her family's smooth-haired fox terrier loves to chase squirrels in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wire-haired fox terrier - a breed of fox terrier with a dense, wiry coat
