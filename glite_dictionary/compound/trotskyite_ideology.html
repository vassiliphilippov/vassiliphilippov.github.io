Trotskyite ideology

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑtskiˌaɪt aɪˈdɪɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈtrɒtskiˌaɪt aɪˈdɪələdʒi / (BrE)
a political philosophy based on the ideas and theories of Leon Trotsky, particularly advocating permanent revolution and international socialism
political philosophy based on Leon Trotsky's ideas

Examples

The Trotskyite ideology played a significant role in shaping the strategies of left-wing movements in Europe.
She devoted her doctoral thesis to the analysis of Trotskyite ideology in Latin America.
Debates between supporters of Trotskyite ideology and Stalinism were fierce within the early communist parties.

Synset Examples

The Trotskyite ideology influenced many activists in the 20th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trotskyism - the political and economic theories of Leon Trotsky; synonym for Trotskyite ideology

Etymology

From Trotskyite (follower of Leon Trotsky) + ideology; Trotskyite from Leon Trotsky, Russian revolutionary.
