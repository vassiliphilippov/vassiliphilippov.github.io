auto accessory

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ əkˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ əkˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
an additional item designed to enhance the appearance, comfort, or functionality of an automobile
item added to an automobile

Examples

The shop sells every auto accessory imaginable, from seat covers to GPS devices.
He installed a new auto accessory to improve the car's sound system.
Many drivers consider floor mats to be an essential auto accessory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car accessory - an accessory for a car
