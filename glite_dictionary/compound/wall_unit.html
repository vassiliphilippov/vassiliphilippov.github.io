wall unit

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a large piece of furniture consisting of several sections or compartments, designed to be placed against one wall for storage or display
multi-section furniture for one wall

Examples

The living room's wall unit has shelves for books and a space for the television.
We bought a modern wall unit to organize our home office.
The oak wall unit in the dining room displays family photos and glassware.
