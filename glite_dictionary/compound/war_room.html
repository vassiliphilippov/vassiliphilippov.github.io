war room

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ruːm / (AmE), / wɔː ruːm / (BrE)
a room where strategic decisions are made, especially during military operations or political campaigns
room for strategic decision-making

Examples

The general called his advisers into the war room to discuss their next move.
During the election, the campaign’s war room was always bustling with activity.
In times of crisis, decisions are often made quickly inside the war room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: command center - a place where control and orders are issued, especially in emergencies or operations
