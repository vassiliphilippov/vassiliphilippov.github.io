latest news

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst nuz / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst njuːz / (BrE)
the most recent information or developments about a particular subject or event
most recent information or developments

Examples

Have you heard the latest news about the election results?
People were glued to their televisions, waiting for the latest news on the rescue operation.
I always check the website in the morning to get the latest news updates.

Synset Examples

Where got the latest news on the event?
Check the bulletin for the latest news updates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breaking news - news of very recent events reported immediately
  • Antonym: old news - information that is no longer new or relevant
