apartment house

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑɹtmənt haʊs / (AmE), / əˈpɑːtmənt haʊs / (BrE)
A building divided into separate apartments for people to live in.
building divided into apartments

Examples

They purchased an apartment house in the city for investment purposes.
The old apartment house was converted into spacious condominiums.
Many families live in the large apartment house on the corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apartment building - a building containing several apartments
