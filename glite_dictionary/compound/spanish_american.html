Spanish American

1. NOUN

/ ˌspænɪʃ əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌspænɪʃ əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
an American, especially in the United States, whose first language is Spanish or who is of Spanish-speaking origin
an American whose first language is Spanish

Examples

Many Spanish Americans maintain strong ties to their cultural heritage.
The school offers programs to support Spanish American students learning English.
A large population of Spanish Americans lives in the southwestern United States.
