palm nut

1. NOUN

/ pɑːm nʌt / (AmE), / pɑːm nʌt / (BrE)
the seed of any species of oil palm, especially those from which palm oil is extracted
seed of an oil palm

Examples

The palm nut is an important agricultural product in many tropical countries.
She used palm nuts to make a rich, flavorful soup.
Harvesting the palm nut is the first step in producing palm oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palm oil - an edible oil obtained from the fruit of oil palms
