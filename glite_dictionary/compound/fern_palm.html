fern palm

1. NOUN

/ fɜrn pɑm / (AmE), / fɜːn pɑːm / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the cycad family from southeastern India, with palmlike foliage resembling that of a fern.
a southeastern Indian cycad with palmlike leaves

Examples

The fern palm is prized in ornamental gardens for its striking, palmlike fronds.
Researchers discovered a rare species of fern palm growing in the rainforests of southeastern India.
The gardener explained that the fern palm requires well-drained soil and partial shade to thrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cycad - a type of ancient, palm-like plant
  • Degree Higher: palm - tropical tree with large, compound leaves
