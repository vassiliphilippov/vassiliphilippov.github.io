any longer

1. ADV

/ ˌɛni ˈlɔŋɚ / (AmE), / ˌeni ˈlɒŋə / (BrE)
(usually with negatives) at the present time or from now on; not anymore.
no more from now on (in negatives)

Examples

I don't work at that company any longer.
She doesn't live in this neighborhood any longer.
We can't ignore these problems any longer.

Synset Examples

Alice doesn't live here anymore
the children promised not to quarrel any more

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anymore - no longer; at the present time (in negatives)
