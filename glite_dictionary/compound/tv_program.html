TV program

1. NOUN

/ tiːˈviː ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / tiːˈviː ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a program that is broadcast on television, such as a show, series, or live event
program broadcast on television

Examples

We stayed up late last night to watch our favorite TV program.
The new detective TV program has received excellent reviews from critics.
Children often learn a lot from educational TV programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television show - a show produced for broadcast on television
  • Antonym: radio program - a program broadcast over the radio

Etymology

Abbreviation TV from 'television' + 'program' (originally from Greek programma, meaning 'a written public notice').
