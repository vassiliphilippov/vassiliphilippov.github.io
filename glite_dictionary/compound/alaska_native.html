Alaska Native

1. NOUN

/ əˈlæskə ˈneɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈlæskə ˈneɪtɪv / (BrE)
a member or descendant of any of the indigenous peoples native to Alaska
indigenous person of Alaska

Examples

The museum features artwork by several Alaska Native artists.
As an Alaska Native, she is passionate about preserving her cultural heritage.
Federal law provides specific protections for Alaska Native lands and resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Native American - a member of any of the indigenous peoples of North, Central, and South America
