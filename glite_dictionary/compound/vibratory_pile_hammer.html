vibratory pile hammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbrəˌtɔri paɪl ˈhæmər / (AmE), / ˈvɪbrətəri paɪl ˈhamə / (BrE)
a machine that drives or extracts piles into or from the ground using rapid vibrations instead of impact force
machine for driving piles using vibrations

Examples

The construction team used a vibratory pile hammer to drive steel beams into the riverbed.
A vibratory pile hammer is ideal for installing piles quickly in loose soil.
Regulations require operators of a vibratory pile hammer to wear proper hearing protection.

Synset Examples

Vibratory hammers can either drive in or extract a pile; extraction is commonly used to recover steel ‘H’ piles used in temporary foundation shoring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pile driver - machine or device for driving piles into the ground, typically by heavy impact
