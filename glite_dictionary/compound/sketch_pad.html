sketch pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɛtʃ pæd / (AmE), / ˈskɛtʃ pad / (BrE)
A book of blank sheets of paper for making sketches or drawings.
book of blank sheets for drawing

Examples

She carried a sketch pad wherever she went to capture new ideas.
The artist's sketch pad was filled with drawings of cityscapes.
He bought a new set of pencils and a sketch pad for his art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchbook - a book or pad of blank pages for drawing sketches
