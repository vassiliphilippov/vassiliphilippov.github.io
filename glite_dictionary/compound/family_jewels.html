family jewels

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈdʒuəlz / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈdʒuːəlz / (BrE)
(slang) A humorous or euphemistic term for the external male sex organs, especially the testicles.
slang term for male genitals
slang

He crossed his legs to protect his family jewels after hearing the bad news.
