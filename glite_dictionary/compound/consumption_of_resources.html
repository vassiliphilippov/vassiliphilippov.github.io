consumption of resources

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌmpʃən əv rɪˈsɔrsɪz / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmpʃən əv rɪˈsɔːsɪz / (BrE)
the use or expenditure of resources, especially natural or economic resources
use or expenditure of resources

Examples

Industrial growth often leads to increased consumption of resources and greater environmental impact.
Efficient technologies can help reduce the consumption of resources in manufacturing processes.
The report highlights the unsustainable consumption of resources by developed countries.

Synset Examples

The consumption of resources has increased drastically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resource consumption - the act of consuming resources
