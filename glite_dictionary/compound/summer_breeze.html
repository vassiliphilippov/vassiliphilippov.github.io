summer breeze

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ briːz / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə briːz / (BrE)
a gentle or mild wind that occurs during the summer season
gentle wind in summer

Examples

The curtains fluttered softly in the summer breeze drifting through the window.
We enjoyed our picnic as a light summer breeze kept us cool under the sun.
She closed her eyes and listened to the rustling leaves in the summer breeze.

Synset Examples

The banner waved proudly in the summer breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeze - a gentle wind
