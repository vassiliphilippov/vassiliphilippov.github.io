natural haircolor

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚl ˈhɛrˌkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ˈheəˌkʌlə / (BrE)
the original, undyed color of a person's hair as it grows naturally
original undyed hair color

Examples

She wanted to return to her natural haircolor after years of experimenting with blond and red shades.
Many people can't remember their natural haircolor after so many dye jobs.
The stylist matched the wig to the client's natural haircolor for a seamless look.

Synset Examples

His natural haircolor was hard to guess with all the dye.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dyed hair - hair that has been colored using artificial dye
