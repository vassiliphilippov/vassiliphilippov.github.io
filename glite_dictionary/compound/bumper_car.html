bumper car

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌmpɚ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə kɑː / (BrE)
a small, low-powered electrically driven vehicle ridden for fun on a special platform, typically at amusement parks, where riders intentionally collide with each other
amusement park vehicle for bumping into others

Examples

The children laughed as they raced their bumper car around the arena, trying to avoid being hit.
At the fair, we spent nearly an hour crashing bumper cars into our friends.
She skillfully steered her bumper car away from the oncoming vehicles.
