lapping machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæpɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used in the lapping process, where two surfaces are rubbed together with an abrasive material to achieve a high degree of smoothness or flatness
machine for lapping surfaces with abrasive

Examples

The engineer demonstrated how the lapping machine produced an exceptionally smooth finish on the metal disc.
Our factory recently invested in a modern lapping machine to improve surface quality.
Proper maintenance helps the lapping machine operate efficiently and extend its lifespan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grinding machine - a machine used to grind materials, often metal or stone

Etymology

From 'lapping' (machining process) + 'machine'. 'Lapping' comes from 'lap' (to polish).
