Republic of Macedonia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmæsəˈdoʊniə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmæsɪˈdəʊniə / (BrE)
A landlocked country on the Balkan Peninsula in southeastern Europe, which achieved independence from Yugoslavia in 1991; officially renamed North Macedonia in 2019.
country in the Balkans, now North Macedonia

Examples

The Republic of Macedonia declared its independence from Yugoslavia in 1991.
Many travelers explore the mountains and lakes of the Republic of Macedonia.
The dispute over the name Republic of Macedonia led to negotiations with Greece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Macedonia - current official name of the country previously known as Republic of Macedonia
