Our Lord's candle

1. NOUN

/ aʊɚ lɔrdz ˈkændl̩ / (AmE), / aʊə lɔːdz ˈkændl / (BrE)
a yucca plant native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, having a tall stem of creamy white flowers
a yucca plant with tall white flowers

Examples

The hills were dotted with blooming Our Lord's candle in early summer.
Botanists studied the remarkable structure of the Our Lord's candle flowers.
Many desert landscapes feature the tall, striking presence of Our Lord's candle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yucca - a genus of plants with sword-like leaves and tall clusters of white flowers
