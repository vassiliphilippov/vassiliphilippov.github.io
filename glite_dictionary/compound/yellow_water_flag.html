yellow water flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈwɔtər flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈwɔːtə flæɡ / (BrE)
a yellow-flowered iris native to Europe and North Africa, commonly found in wetlands and naturalized in parts of the United States
a yellow-flowered wetland iris

Examples

The yellow water flag brightened the edge of the pond with its showy blooms.
Gardeners often plant yellow water flag to bring color to wetland areas.
We spotted a field of yellow water flag while hiking through the marsh.

Related Terms
