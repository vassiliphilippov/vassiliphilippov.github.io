vase-shaped

1. ADJ

/ veɪs ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / vɑːz ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a vase
in the shape of a vase

Examples

The antique lamp had a vase-shaped base that gave it a unique look.
Archaeologists discovered a vase-shaped container made of clay at the site.
The designer chose a vase-shaped silhouette for the dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vase-like - resembling or similar to a vase
