Red Hand Defenders

1. PROPN

/ rɛd hænd dɪˈfɛndɚz / (AmE), / rɛd hænd dɪˈfɛndəz / (BrE)
a loyalist paramilitary group in Northern Ireland, composed mainly of Protestants, known for violent attacks against Catholic civilians and opposition to political settlement with the Irish Republic
Northern Ireland loyalist paramilitary group

Examples

The Red Hand Defenders claimed responsibility for a bombing in Belfast.
Authorities are investigating the connection between the Red Hand Defenders and other loyalist groups.
The activities of the Red Hand Defenders contributed to heightened tensions during the late 1990s.
