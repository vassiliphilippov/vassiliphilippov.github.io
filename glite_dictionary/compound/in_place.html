in place

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpleɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpleɪs / (BrE)
in the original or proper position or location; not moved from where it belongs
in the original position or location

Examples

Please keep the documents in place until the inspector arrives.
The tiles stayed in place during the entire storm.
After cleaning, make sure everything is in place before leaving the room.

Synset Examples

carcinoma in situ
the archeologists left the pottery in place

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of place - not in the proper or usual location

2. ADJ

/ ɪn pleɪs / (AmE), / ɪn pleɪs / (BrE)
arranged or positioned correctly; set up properly for use or function
properly arranged or set up

Examples

The chairs were already in place when the guests arrived.
All safety measures must be in place before the experiment can begin.
With the new procedures in place, productivity improved significantly.

Synset Examples
