diseased person

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzizd ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / dɪˈziːzd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is suffering from a disease
a person with a disease

Examples

The hospital has a separate ward for diseased persons who require isolation.
Public health officials are working to prevent contact between healthy and diseased persons.
Special care must be taken when handling food prepared by a diseased person.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care or treatment
  • Antonym: healthy person - a person not suffering from any disease
