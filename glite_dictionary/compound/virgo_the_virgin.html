Virgo the Virgin

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɝːɡoʊ ðə ˈvɝːdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːɡəʊ ði ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
the sixth sign of the zodiac, covering the period from about August 23 to September 22, symbolized by a maiden
the sixth zodiac sign (the Virgin)

Examples

People born under Virgo the Virgin are said to be analytical and practical.
The sun enters Virgo the Virgin at the end of August each year.
Astrologers believe that Virgo the Virgin is ruled by the planet Mercury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Virgo - sixth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Lower: Leo - fifth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: Libra - seventh sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'virgo' meaning 'maiden' or 'virgin'.
