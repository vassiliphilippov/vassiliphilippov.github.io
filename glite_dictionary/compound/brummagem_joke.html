Brummagem joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌmədʒəm dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌmɪdʒəm dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is considered cheap, tasteless, or of poor quality.
inferior or tasteless joke

Examples

His Brummagem joke fell flat at the dinner party and no one laughed.
She rolled her eyes at yet another Brummagem joke from her uncle.
Among all the witty remarks, his Brummagem joke stood out for its poor taste.

Synset Examples

His Brummagem jokes always made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad joke - a joke that is unfunny or of poor quality
  • Antonym: witticism - a clever or witty remark or joke

Etymology

Brummagem is a historical term for Birmingham, England, formerly used to describe cheap or counterfeit goods produced there.
