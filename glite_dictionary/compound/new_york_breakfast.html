New York breakfast

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈjɔrk ˈbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk ˈbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
A substantial or traditional breakfast style associated with New York, typically featuring bagels, lox, and similar items.
traditional New York-style breakfast, often with bagels and lox

Examples

At the hotel, I ordered a New York breakfast with freshly baked bagels and smoked salmon.
She loves to treat guests to a homemade New York breakfast on special occasions.
Business meetings often begin with a New York breakfast in Manhattan cafés.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed a delicious New York breakfast at the deli.
I always start my day with a hearty New York breakfast.
Nothing beats a classic New York breakfast of bagels and lox.
