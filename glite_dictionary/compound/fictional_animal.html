fictional animal

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪkʃənl ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈfɪkʃənəl ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal that exists only in fiction, such as stories or myths, and not in the real world
imaginary animal from fiction

Examples

The story features a fictional animal that can talk and fly.
Children often draw pictures of their favorite fictional animals from books and movies.
The unicorn is a well-known fictional animal found in many fairy tales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical creature - a being from myth or legend, often with supernatural powers
  • Antonym: real animal - an animal that actually exists in the real world
