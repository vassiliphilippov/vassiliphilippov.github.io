autumn crocus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔtəm ˈkroʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˈkrəʊkəs / (BrE)
a bulbous plant, native to western and central Europe, that produces white, purple, or lavender-and-white flowers in the autumn
flowering plant with autumn blooms

Examples

The autumn crocus adds a splash of color to the garden late in the year.
Be careful with autumn crocus, as all parts of the plant are poisonous.
She planted several autumn crocuses along the garden path.
