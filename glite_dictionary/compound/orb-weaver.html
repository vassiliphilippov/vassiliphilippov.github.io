orb-weaver

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrb ˌwivər / (AmE), / ˈɔːb ˌwiːvə / (BrE)
A spider of a large family (Araneidae) known for spinning wheel-shaped (orb) webs, found worldwide.
spider that spins orb-shaped webs

Examples

The orb-weaver spun an intricate circular web in the garden overnight.
Many orb-weavers are active during the evening, repairing their webs at dusk.
We found a colorful orb-weaver hanging quietly at the center of its web.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider - an eight-legged arthropod of the order Araneae
