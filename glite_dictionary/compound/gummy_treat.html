gummy treat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌmi ˌtrit / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi ˌtriːt / (BrE)
A chewy, often fruit-flavored candy made with gelatin or similar substances.
chewy, fruit-flavored candy

Examples

My niece loves to share her favorite gummy treat with her friends at school.
Each gummy treat in the bag had a different fruity flavor.
He reached for a gummy treat when he wanted a sweet snack while studying.

Synset Examples

I found some delicious garden gummy treats at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gummy bear - a small bear-shaped chewy candy
  • Synonym: jelly bean - small bean-shaped candy with a chewy interior
