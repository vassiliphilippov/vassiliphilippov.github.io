figure skating

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ ˌskeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə ˌskeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which individuals, pairs, or groups perform graceful movements, jumps, spins, and patterns on ice wearing skates
ice sport involving skating patterns and routines

Examples

She began figure skating when she was six years old and now competes at national events.
The Winter Olympics features several figure skating disciplines, including pairs and ice dancing.
Artistic expression is an important aspect of competitive figure skating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skating - skating on ice using bladed shoes
