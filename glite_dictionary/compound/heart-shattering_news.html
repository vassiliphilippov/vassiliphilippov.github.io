heart-shattering news

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈʃæt̬ɚɪŋ nuz / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈʃætərɪŋ njuːz / (BrE)
news that causes overwhelming emotional pain or distress
devastating or extremely distressing news

Examples

The heart-shattering news of the accident spread quickly through the town.
She struggled to speak after receiving such heart-shattering news about her childhood friend.
When he opened the letter, it contained the most heart-shattering news he could imagine.

Synset Examples

The heart-shattering news left the family devastated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastating news - news causing severe shock or grief
  • Antonym: good news - news that brings happiness or relief
