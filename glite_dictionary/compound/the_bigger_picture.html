the bigger picture

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈbɪɡər ˈpɪktʃər / (AmE), / ðə ˈbɪɡə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
the overall perspective, context, or most important facts about a situation, rather than specific details
overall perspective or context

Examples

When making decisions, it's important to consider the bigger picture rather than just immediate results.
She sometimes gets frustrated by small setbacks but tries to focus on the bigger picture.
Environmental policies must address the bigger picture instead of just short-term economic gains.

Synset Examples

She is too myopic to see the bigger picture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the whole picture - the entire situation or all the relevant facts
