broad-leaved goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ brɔd livd ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / brɔːd liːvd ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant (Solidago flexicaulis) with broad leaves and small clusters of yellow flowers.
species of goldenrod with broad leaves

Examples

The broad-leaved goldenrod blooms in late summer along shaded forest edges.
Gardeners often plant broad-leaved goldenrod for its vibrant yellow flowers and attractive foliage.
We identified the species as broad-leaved goldenrod by its wide leaves and arching stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a genus of plants with clusters of yellow flowers
