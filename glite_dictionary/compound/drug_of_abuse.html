drug of abuse

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ əv əˈbjus / (AmE), / drʌɡ əv əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
a drug taken for nonmedical purposes, often having mind-altering effects and the potential for addiction or harm
a nonmedical, addictive or harmful drug
medical

Examples

The seminar focused on the dangers associated with each type of drug of abuse.
Addiction to any drug of abuse can have severe health consequences.
Researchers are studying how quickly dependence can develop after using a drug of abuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcotic - a drug, especially one that is illegal, affects the mind or behavior
  • Synonym: recreational drug - a drug taken for nonmedical use, usually for enjoyment
