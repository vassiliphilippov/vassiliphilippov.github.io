regressive leftist agenda

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈɡrɛsɪv ˈlɛftɪst əˈdʒɛndə / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛsɪv ˈlɛftɪst əˈdʒendə / (BrE)
A set of political policies or beliefs promoted by certain left-leaning groups, regarded by critics as backward or harmful to social progress.
left-wing policies seen as backward

Examples

Critics accuse the council of pushing a regressive leftist agenda that stifles debate.
The columnist argued that the university's policies reflect a regressive leftist agenda.
Many believe that adopting a regressive leftist agenda could actually hinder societal progress.

Synset Examples

Many argue that regressive leftist agendas are harmful.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: progressive agenda - a set of forward-thinking or reform-oriented political policies
  • Synonym: leftist - a person with political views leaning to the left
