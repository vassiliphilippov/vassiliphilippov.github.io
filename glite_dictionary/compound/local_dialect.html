local dialect

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (BrE)
the particular form of a language characteristic of a specific region or community
regional form of a language

Examples

The villagers all speak in their local dialect rather than the national language.
Some old stories are best told in the local dialect to preserve their original charm.
She struggled to understand the local dialect when she first moved to the area.

Synset Examples

He learned to appreciate the local dialect while traveling.
In the local dialect, they often say nê when agreeing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dialect - a particular form of a language specific to a region or group
