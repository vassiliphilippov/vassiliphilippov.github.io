cauliflower soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪˌflaʊɚ suːp / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪˌflaʊə suːp / (BrE)
A soup made primarily from cauliflower, often blended with stock and other ingredients for a creamy texture.
soup made from cauliflower

Examples

I made a delicious cauliflower soup for dinner last night.
A steaming bowl of cauliflower soup is perfect on a cold winter day.
Many restaurants offer cauliflower soup as a vegetarian starter.

Synset Examples

This rich and creamy cauliflower soup is just the thing on a chilly day - and it's inexpensive too.
