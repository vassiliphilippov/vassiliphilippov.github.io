flashlight battery

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæʃˌlaɪt ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈflaʃlʌɪt ˈbatəri / (BrE)
A small dry battery that provides electrical power for a flashlight.
battery used to power a flashlight

Examples

She replaced the flashlight battery before going on the camping trip.
Make sure you bring an extra flashlight battery in case the current one dies.
The flashlight battery lasted all night during the power outage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battery - a device that stores and supplies electrical energy
