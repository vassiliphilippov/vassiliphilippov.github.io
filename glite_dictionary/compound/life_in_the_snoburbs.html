life in the snoburbs

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ɪn ðə snəˌbɜrbz / (AmE), / laɪf ɪn ðə snəˌbɜːbz / (BrE)
the experience of living in affluent and socially exclusive suburban areas, often associated with wealth and privilege
living in affluent, exclusive suburbs
informal

Examples

Many people imagine life in the snoburbs to be perfect, but it comes with its own challenges.
She grew bored with life in the snoburbs and longed for the city’s excitement.
The novel offers a witty take on life in the snoburbs and the pressures of keeping up appearances.

Synset Examples

Life in the snoburbs can feel isolating at times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suburb - an outlying district of a city, usually residential

Etymology

Blend of 'snob' and 'suburbs.'
