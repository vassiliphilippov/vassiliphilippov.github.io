quartz mill

1. NOUN

/ kwɔrts mɪl / (AmE), / kwɔːts mɪl / (BrE)
a machine for crushing or grinding quartz ore, especially to extract gold or other metals; a stamp mill used for this purpose
machine for crushing quartz ore

Examples

The old mining town still has a functioning quartz mill from the nineteenth century.
Many prospectors used a quartz mill to extract gold from the surrounding rock.
The noise of the quartz mill echoed across the valley as it crushed the ore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp mill - a machine for crushing ore by pounding
