dweeby glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈdwiːbi ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈdwiːbi ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Large or unfashionable glasses, especially those that give a person an awkward or nerdy appearance.
nerdy or awkward-looking glasses
informal slang

Examples

He swapped his old dweeby glasses for stylish frames and felt more confident.
Despite her dweeby glasses, she was the most popular person at the party.
People sometimes underestimate him because of his dweeby glasses and quiet demeanor.

Synset Examples

She wore those dweeby glasses, but they suited her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerd glasses - glasses often associated with a nerdy appearance

Etymology

From 'dweeby' (slang for awkwardly nerdy) + 'glasses'.
