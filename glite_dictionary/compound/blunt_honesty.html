blunt honesty

1. NOUN

/ blʌnt ˈɑnəsti / (AmE), / blʌnt ˈɒnəsti / (BrE)
the quality of being direct and straightforward in expressing the truth, sometimes in a way that may seem harsh or insensitive
direct, sometimes harsh, truthfulness

Examples

Her blunt honesty can sometimes offend people who prefer a more gentle approach.
He was admired for his blunt honesty, even when it was difficult to hear the truth.
I respect your blunt honesty, but a little diplomacy might help in sensitive situations.

Synset Examples

I appreciate your blunt honesty during the discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankness - the quality of being open and honest; frank speech or attitude
  • Antonym: tact - skill and sensitivity in dealing with others or with difficult issues
