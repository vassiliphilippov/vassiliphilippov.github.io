sense of craft

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv kræft / (AmE), / sɛns əv krɑːft / (BrE)
an intuitive ability or skill for creating things well, especially evident in artistic or handmade work
intuitive skill for making things well

Examples

Her sense of craft is apparent in every detail of her pottery.
Each garment displays the designer's meticulous sense of craft.
He brings a refined sense of craft to all his woodworking projects.

Synset Examples

Her sense of craft is evident in every piece of jewelry.
The artist’s sense of craft shines through in each painting.
He approaches woodworking with a true sense of craft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsmanship - skill in a particular craft or trade
