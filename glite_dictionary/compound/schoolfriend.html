schoolfriend

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a friend who attends or attended the same school as you
friend from the same school

Examples

I'll never forget my old schoolfriend who helped me during my first year.
She met her husband when they were both schoolfriends in high school.
Many people reconnect with schoolfriends at reunions years after graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classmate - a person who is in the same class at school or college

Etymology

Formed from 'school' + 'friend'.
