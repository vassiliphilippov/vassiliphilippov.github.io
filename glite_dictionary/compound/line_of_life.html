line of life

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪn əv laɪf / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn əv laɪf / (BrE)
a crease on the palm which palmists believe indicates the length of a person's life
palm crease linked with lifespan in palmistry

Examples

The palmist traced the line of life on her palm and smiled reassuringly.
Some claim that a short line of life signals an early death, but there is no scientific basis for this.
He studied his line of life, wondering what the future might hold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life line - crease on the palm, interpreted in palmistry
