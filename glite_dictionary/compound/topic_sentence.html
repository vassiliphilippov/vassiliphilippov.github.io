topic sentence

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːpɪk ˌsɛntəns / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪk ˌsɛntəns / (BrE)
A sentence that states the main idea or subject of its paragraph.
sentence stating a paragraph's main idea

Examples

Every paragraph in her essay began with a clear topic sentence.
A strong topic sentence helps the reader understand the focus of the paragraph.
The teacher asked each student to write a topic sentence for their chosen subject.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thesis statement - a statement that expresses the main idea of an essay or longer work
