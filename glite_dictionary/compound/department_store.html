department store

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtmənt stɔr / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt stɔː / (BrE)
a large retail store divided into various departments, each selling a different type of goods, such as clothing, furniture, or appliances
large shop with separate sections for different goods

Examples

She bought a new coat at the downtown department store during the winter sale.
Many department stores have struggled to compete with online retailers in recent years.
The department store has a wide range of products, from electronics to cosmetics.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: supermarket - a large self-service store selling food and household goods
