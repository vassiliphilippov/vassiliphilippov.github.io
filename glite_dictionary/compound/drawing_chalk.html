drawing chalk

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪŋ tʃɔk / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ tʃɔːk / (BrE)
a stick of colored chalk used by artists for drawing or sketching on paper or other surfaces
colored chalk for artistic drawing

Examples

She prefers to use drawing chalk for her landscape sketches.
The artist's hands were stained with drawing chalk by the end of the day.
Children enjoyed creating murals on the pavement with colored drawing chalk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastel - a stick of powdered pigment used for drawing
  • Synonym: chalk - a soft white or colored limestone used for writing or drawing
