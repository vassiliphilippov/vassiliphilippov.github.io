executive secretary

1. NOUN

/ ɪgˈzɛk.jə.tɪv ˈsɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɛk.jʊ.tɪv ˈsɛk.rə.təri / (BrE)
A secretary with administrative duties and responsibilities, often assisting high-level executives and handling confidential or complex tasks.
secretary with administrative responsibilities

Examples

As an executive secretary, Maria manages both scheduling and confidential correspondence for the company president.
The executive secretary prepared reports for the upcoming board meeting and coordinated travel arrangements for the CEO.
Qualified applicants must have several years of experience as an executive secretary in a corporate environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal assistant - an assistant who helps with both personal and work tasks
  • Degree Lower: secretary - a person employed to assist with correspondence, keep records, and do administrative work
