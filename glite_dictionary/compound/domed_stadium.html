domed stadium

1. NOUN

/ doʊmd ˈsteɪdiəm / (AmE), / dəʊmd ˈsteɪdiəm / (BrE)
a large sports arena with a roof, typically shaped like a dome, that covers the playing field and seating areas
sports arena with a domed roof

Examples

The Super Bowl was held in a domed stadium to protect fans and players from bad weather.
Many baseball teams prefer playing in a domed stadium for climate control and comfort.
The city's new domed stadium will host concerts and sporting events all year round.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: stadium - a large structure for sports events with rows of seats for spectators
