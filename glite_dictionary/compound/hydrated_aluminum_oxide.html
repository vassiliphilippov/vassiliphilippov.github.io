hydrated aluminum oxide

1. NOUN

/ haɪˌdreɪtɪd əˌlumɪnəm ˈɑksaɪd / (AmE), / haɪˌdreɪtɪd ˌaljʊˈmɪniəm ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound, Al(OH)₃, that occurs naturally as the mineral gibbsite
white crystalline mineral compound

Examples

Gibbsite is a naturally occurring form of hydrated aluminum oxide found in bauxite deposits.
The laboratory used pure hydrated aluminum oxide for their experiments.
Hydrated aluminum oxide has many industrial applications, especially in the production of aluminum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gibbsite - the mineral form of hydrated aluminum oxide
