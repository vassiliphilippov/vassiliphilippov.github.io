Atayalic language

1. NOUN

/ əˈtaɪ.ə.lɪk ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈtaɪ.ə.lɪk ˈlaŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a group of related Austronesian languages spoken by the Atayal people in northern Taiwan
Austronesian language group of Taiwan

Examples

Researchers have documented several dialects of the Atayalic language in remote Taiwanese villages.
The preservation of the Atayalic language is important for the cultural heritage of the Atayal people.
Linguists classify the Atayalic language as part of the Austronesian family spoken in Taiwan.

Etymology

From the name Atayal (an indigenous people of Taiwan) + -ic (adjective/adjectival suffix).
