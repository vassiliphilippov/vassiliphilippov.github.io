carpet beater

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pət ˈbiː.tər / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt ˈbiː.tə / (BrE)
a handheld tool used for hitting carpets to remove dust and dirt
tool for beating dust out of carpets

Examples

Before vacuum cleaners were common, people used a carpet beater to clean their rugs.
She found an old carpet beater in her grandmother’s attic.
The museum displayed a collection of antique carpet beaters and other cleaning tools.
