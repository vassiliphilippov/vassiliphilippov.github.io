tipu tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːpuː triː / (AmE), / ˈtiːpuː triː / (BrE)
A semi-evergreen tree native to South America, with odd-pinnate leaves and golden yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental.
South American ornamental tree with yellow flowers

Examples

The city planted tipu trees along the avenue for their beauty and shade.
A mature tipu tree produces a spectacular display of yellow blossoms in early summer.
Gardeners appreciate tipu trees for their rapid growth and ornamental value.
