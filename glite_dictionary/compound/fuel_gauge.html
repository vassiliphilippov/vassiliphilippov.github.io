fuel gauge

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːəl ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument that shows the amount of fuel left in a vehicle's tank
instrument showing remaining vehicle fuel

Examples

He glanced at the fuel gauge and realized he needed to stop for gas soon.
The fuel gauge was stuck on empty, even though the tank was full.
A faulty fuel gauge can make it hard to know when to refuel your car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas gauge - instrument showing the amount of gasoline in a tank
