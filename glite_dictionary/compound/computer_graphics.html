computer graphics

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈɡræfɪks / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈɡræfɪks / (BrE)
the technology or field concerned with creating, displaying, and manipulating visual images using computers
the field of creating images using computers

Examples

She studied computer graphics to learn how to design video game visuals.
Modern movies often rely heavily on computer graphics for their special effects.
The new software makes computer graphics more accessible to beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphics - visual images or designs on a surface
