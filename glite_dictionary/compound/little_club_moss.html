little club moss

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.əl klʌb mɑs / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl klʌb mɒs / (BrE)
any of numerous small, moss-like plants of the genus Selaginella, related to ferns and club mosses
small Selaginella genus plant

Examples

The forest floor was covered with a carpet of little club moss and other ferns.
Scientists discovered a new species of little club moss in the Amazon region.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate little club moss for its delicate, moss-like appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Selaginella - genus of small, spikemoss ferns
  • Degree Higher: club moss - any of several related spore-bearing plants of the family Lycopodiaceae

Etymology

From 'club moss,' referencing the similar appearance to true club mosses, and 'little' describing its small size.
