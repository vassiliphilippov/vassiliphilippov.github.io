red-legged partridge

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈlɛɡd ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈlɛɡd ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large western European partridge with red legs, grey-brown upper parts, and distinctive markings
a European partridge species with red legs

Examples

The red-legged partridge is often found in open farmland and grassland across western Europe.
Hunters in Spain prize the red-legged partridge for its challenging flight and excellent flavor.
A flock of red-legged partridges crossed the dirt path as we walked through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partridge - a medium-sized game bird with a plump body and short tail
