oxygen waster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑksɪdʒən ˈweɪstər / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒən ˈweɪstə / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person considered unproductive or useless, implying that they waste resources by merely existing
insult for a useless or unproductive person
informal slang

Examples

He called his lazy coworker an oxygen waster after yet another missed deadline.
Social media trolls are often dismissed as mere oxygen wasters by frustrated users.
She refused to date someone she considered an oxygen waster with no ambitions.

Synset Examples

She’s become an oxygen waster with her constant drama.
He’s just an oxygen waster, always complaining.
Don't be an oxygen waster; contribute positively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadbeat - a person who avoids work or responsibilities
  • Synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
