committal to writing

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪt̬.əl tə ˈraɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt.əl tə ˈraɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of putting something into written form
the act of writing something down

Examples

The contract is not valid until its committal to writing by both parties.
His ideas became clearer after the committal to writing process began.
Official records require the committal to writing of all statements.

Synset Examples

she did the thinking while he did the writing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writing - the activity or skill of marking coherent words on paper
