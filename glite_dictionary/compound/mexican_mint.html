Mexican mint

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛksɪkən mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən mɪnt / (BrE)
a herb native to Oaxaca, Mexico, known for its potent hallucinogenic effects; its active ingredient is salvinorin
a hallucinogenic herb from Mexico

Examples

The indigenous Mazatec people have traditionally used Mexican mint in spiritual rituals.
Researchers are studying Mexican mint for its unique psychoactive properties.
It is important to be cautious when consuming Mexican mint due to its strong effects.
