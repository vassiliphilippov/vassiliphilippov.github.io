quickie meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪki miːl / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki miːl / (BrE)
a meal that is prepared and eaten quickly, often with little effort or preparation
a fast, simple meal

Examples

After work, she put together a quickie meal before heading out again.
Some college students rely on a quickie meal when they're short on time.
We had a quickie meal at a small café before the show.

Synset Examples

Let's grab a quickie meal on the way home.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
  • Degree Higher: feast - a large and elaborate meal
