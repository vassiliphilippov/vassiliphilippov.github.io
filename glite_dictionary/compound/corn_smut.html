corn smut

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn smʌt / (AmE), / kɔːn smʌt / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by a fungus that infects maize (corn), producing large, swollen, greyish galls on the plant
fungal disease of corn

Examples

The farmer had to destroy several ears of corn because they were infected with corn smut.
Biologists study corn smut to understand fungal plant diseases and their effects on agriculture.
In Mexican cuisine, corn smut is known as huitlacoche and is considered a delicacy.
