folk metal

1. NOUN

/ foʊk ˈmɛtəl / (AmE), / fəʊk ˈmɛtəl / (BrE)
A genre of heavy metal music that incorporates elements of traditional folk music, originating in Europe in the 1990s.
heavy metal fused with folk music

Examples

Several bands emerged in the 1990s blending heavy metal with folk influences, helping to define folk metal as a distinct genre.
Fans of folk metal often appreciate the use of traditional instruments alongside electric guitars and drums.
The album was praised for its innovative approach to folk metal, mixing mythological themes with modern sounds.

Synset Examples

Even with the departure of Martin Walkyier in 2001, Skyclad remains an active folk metal group today after nearly two decades since their formation.
