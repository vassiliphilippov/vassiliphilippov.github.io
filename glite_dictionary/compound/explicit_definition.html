explicit definition

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsplɪsɪt dɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪsɪt dɛfɪˈnɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a definition that gives a precise and complete equivalent of the term being defined
precise, complete definition of a term

Examples

The mathematician provided an explicit definition of the function, making its properties immediately clear.
An explicit definition is often preferred in formal documents to avoid ambiguity.
This glossary lacks an explicit definition of several key technical terms.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: implicit definition - a definition that defines a term by specifying the properties it must satisfy, rather than an exact equivalent
