ballet dancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæleɪ ˌdænsər / (AmE), / ˈbæleɪ ˌdɑːnsə / (BrE)
A person trained in ballet and who performs as a member of a ballet company.
person who performs ballet

Examples

As a young ballet dancer, she practiced every morning before school.
The ballet dancer captivated the audience with his graceful movements.
Many children dream of becoming a professional ballet dancer when they grow up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancer - a person who dances
