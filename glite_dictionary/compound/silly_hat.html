silly hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪli hæt / (AmE), / ˈsɪli hæt / (BrE)
a hat designed to be humorous, whimsical, or amusing, often worn for entertainment or at parties
whimsical or humorous headpiece

Examples

He walked into the room wearing a bright red silly hat with feathers on top.
At the festival, many children enjoyed making their own silly hats from colorful paper.
Her collection includes every kind of silly hat you can imagine.

Synset Examples

She wore a silly hat to the costume party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny hat - a hat that looks humorous or causes amusement
