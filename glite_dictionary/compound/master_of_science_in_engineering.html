Master of Science in Engineering

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚ əv ˈsaɪəns ɪn ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə əv ˈsaɪəns ɪn ˌɛn(d)ʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
a postgraduate academic degree in engineering, typically focused on science and research-oriented studies within the field of engineering.
postgraduate engineering degree

Examples

She earned her Master of Science in Engineering at a prestigious university.
A Master of Science in Engineering is often required for advanced research roles.
He completed a Master of Science in Engineering before joining the aerospace industry.
