strawberry jam

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri dʒæm / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbəri dʒam / (BrE)
A sweet spread or preserve made from strawberries and sugar, often used on bread or pastries.
jam made from strawberries

Examples

She spread strawberry jam on her toast for breakfast.
Would you like some strawberry jam with your scone?
Homemade strawberry jam always tastes richer than the store-bought kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jam - a sweet spread made from fruit and sugar
