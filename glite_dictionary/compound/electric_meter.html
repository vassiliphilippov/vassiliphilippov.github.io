electric meter

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmitər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a device that measures and records the amount of electricity consumed by a residence, business, or machine
device measuring electricity usage

Examples

The technician checked the electric meter to determine how much power had been used this month.
Please read the numbers on your electric meter and send them to the utility company.
A new electric meter was installed when the house was renovated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electricity meter - device for measuring electric energy consumption
