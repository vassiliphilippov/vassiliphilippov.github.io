calling into question

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlɪŋ ˌɪntu kwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlɪŋ ˌɪntuː ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
an expression of doubt about the truth or validity of something; a challenge to what someone has said or done
expression of doubt or challenge

Examples

The new evidence is calling into question the reliability of the witness.
His motives were soon calling into question by the investigative committee.
The scientist's findings are calling into question several accepted theories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: challenge - an objection or call to justify something
  • Synonym: questioning - the action of expressing doubt or asking for justification
