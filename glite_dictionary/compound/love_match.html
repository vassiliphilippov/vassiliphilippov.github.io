love match

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈlʌv mætʃ / (BrE)
A marriage or romantic relationship based on mutual love, rather than arrangement or convenience.
marriage or relationship based on love

Examples

Their marriage was a true love match, not arranged by their families.
Some cultures still view a love match with suspicion compared to arranged marriages.
They met by chance, and soon everyone realized their relationship was a love match.
