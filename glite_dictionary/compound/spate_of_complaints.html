spate of complaints

1. NOUN

/ speɪt əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (AmE), / speɪt əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (BrE)
a sudden large number of complaints happening at the same time
a sudden large number of complaints

Examples

The company received a spate of complaints after releasing the faulty product.
There was a spate of complaints following the introduction of the new policy.
A spate of complaints flooded the city council's office last week.

Synset Examples

The company faced a spate of complaints from customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flood of complaints - an overwhelming number of complaints arriving at once

Etymology

From 'spate' (a sudden flood or outpouring), probably from Scottish, of unknown origin.
