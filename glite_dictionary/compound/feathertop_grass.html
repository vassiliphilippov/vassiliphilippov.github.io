feathertop grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðərˌtɑp ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfɛðətɒp ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A tropical African grass plant characterized by feathery, plume-like panicles.
African grass with feathery panicles

Examples

The garden was dotted with clusters of feathertop grass swaying gently in the breeze.
Farmers in the region often encounter feathertop grass growing wild along irrigation channels.
With its attractive plumes, feathertop grass is sometimes used in dried floral arrangements.
