block diagram

1. NOUN

/ blɑk ˈdaɪə.ɡræm / (AmE), / blɒk ˈdaɪə.ɡram / (BrE)
a simple diagram representing the main parts or functions of a system and how they are connected, especially in electronics or engineering
simplified schematic showing parts and connections

Examples

The engineer drew a block diagram to explain the circuit's layout to the team.
Understanding the block diagram helped us identify the source of the malfunction.
Most textbooks include a block diagram illustrating the main components of a computer system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schematic - a diagram, in particular of an electrical or mechanical system
