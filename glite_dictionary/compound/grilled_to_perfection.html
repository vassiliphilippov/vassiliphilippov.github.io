grilled to perfection

1. ADJ

/ ɡrɪld tə pərˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɡrɪld tə pəˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
cooked on a grill to an ideal degree, especially so that flavor and texture are optimal
cooked perfectly on a grill

Examples

The salmon was grilled to perfection and served with a fresh lemon wedge.
He prides himself on making steaks that are always grilled to perfection.
At the new restaurant, every piece of chicken was grilled to perfection and full of flavor.

Synset Examples

The snapper was grilled to perfection for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooked to perfection - prepared to the highest standard
