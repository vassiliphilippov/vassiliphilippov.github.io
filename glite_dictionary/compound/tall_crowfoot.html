tall crowfoot

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈkroʊˌfʊt / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈkrəʊˌfʊt / (BrE)
a perennial European buttercup with yellow spring flowers, widely naturalized especially in eastern North America
perennial European buttercup

Examples

The meadow was dotted with bright yellow tall crowfoot in early May.
Gardeners appreciate tall crowfoot for its hardy nature and cheerful flowers.
Invasive populations of tall crowfoot have been observed spreading in North American wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - a type of flowering plant with yellow flowers, genus Ranunculus
