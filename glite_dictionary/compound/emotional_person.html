emotional person

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənl ˈpɚsən / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
a person who experiences strong emotions easily and frequently
someone prone to strong emotions

Examples

As an emotional person, she often reacts strongly to both good and bad news.
He admits that being an emotional person can make tough conversations even harder.
It can be challenging for an emotional person to remain calm during stressful situations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stoic - a person who does not show or feel emotions easily
