giant cockroach

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈkɑkˌroʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈkɒk.rəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a large species of cockroach native to tropical regions of the Americas
large tropical American cockroach

Examples

The rainforest floor was teeming with giant cockroaches after the monsoon rains.
Entomologists study the behavior and habitat of the giant cockroach in South America.
She was startled to find a giant cockroach crawling across her campsite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockroach - a type of insect with a flattened body, long antennae, and leathery wings
