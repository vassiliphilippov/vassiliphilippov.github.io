Zen Buddhist

1. NOUN

/ zɛn ˈbuːdɪst / (AmE), / zɛn ˈbʊdɪst / (BrE)
A follower or adherent of the doctrines and practices of Zen Buddhism.
a follower of Zen Buddhism

Examples

The Zen Buddhist meditated quietly in the garden every morning.
As a Zen Buddhist, she values mindfulness and simplicity in her daily life.
The temple was founded by a group of Zen Buddhist monks in the 12th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Buddhist - a follower or adherent of Buddhism

Etymology

From Japanese 'Zen', a school of Buddhism, and English 'Buddhist'.
