sitar player

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈtɑr ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / sɪˈtɑː ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a musician who plays the sitar, a traditional Indian stringed instrument
musician who plays the sitar

Examples

The renowned sitar player performed a mesmerizing solo at the concert.
As a sitar player, she practices several hours every day to master her craft.
Many Western musicians collaborate with a sitar player to create unique fusion music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented
