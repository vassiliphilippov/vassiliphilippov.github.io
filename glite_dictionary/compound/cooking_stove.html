cooking stove

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ stoʊv / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ stəʊv / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance designed for cooking food, typically using gas, electricity, or another fuel.
appliance for cooking food

Examples

She turned the cooking stove on and began making breakfast for her family.
Many rural homes still use a wood-burning cooking stove to prepare meals.
The kitchen renovation included replacing the old cooking stove with a modern electric one.
