hard hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrd ˌhæt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd ˌhæt / (BrE)
A lightweight, protective helmet made of plastic or metal, worn to protect the head, especially by construction workers.
protective helmet worn by construction workers

Examples

All workers are required to wear a hard hat while on the construction site.
She forgot her hard hat in the break room, so she couldn't enter the work area.
His bright yellow hard hat made him easy to spot from a distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - a protective head covering
