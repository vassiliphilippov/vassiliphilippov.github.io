administrative official

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv əˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
a person who holds a position of authority or responsibility within a bureaucracy, managing or overseeing administrative functions
a person in a bureaucratic role

Examples

The administrative official signed the necessary documents to approve the project.
Every administrative official must adhere to the agency’s strict code of conduct.
The board appointed a new administrative official to streamline internal operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administrator - a person responsible for running a business, organization, etc.
  • Synonym: bureaucrat - an official in a government department, usually perceived as rigid or procedural
