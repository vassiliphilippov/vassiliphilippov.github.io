Enderby Land

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛndərˌbi lænd / (AmE), / ˈɛndəbi lænd / (BrE)
a region of Antarctica between Queen Maud Land and Wilkes Land, claimed by Australia
Antarctic region claimed by Australia

Examples

Scientists conducted climate research in Enderby Land during the summer expedition.
Australia asserts territorial claims over Enderby Land, which lies between Queen Maud Land and Wilkes Land.
Satellite images have revealed new glacial movements in Enderby Land.

Etymology

Named after the Enderby Brothers, a British whaling company whose ship discovered the region in 1831.
