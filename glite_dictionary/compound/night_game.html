night game

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪt ˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
A sports game or match played at night under artificial lights.
sports event played at night

Examples

The stadium was packed for the night game between the city rivals.
Players often say the atmosphere feels different during a night game.
Rain postponed the night game until the following week.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: day game - a game played during daylight hours
