scrubbing brush

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrʌbɪŋ brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈskrʌbɪŋ brʌʃ / (BrE)
a brush with short, stiff bristles used for cleaning surfaces thoroughly
brush for heavy cleaning

Examples

She used a scrubbing brush to clean the mud off her boots.
The tiles came up spotless after a vigorous scrub with a scrubbing brush and soapy water.
Every cupboard had a scrubbing brush stored underneath the sink for heavy cleaning jobs.

  • Synonym: scrub brush - another term for scrubbing brush
