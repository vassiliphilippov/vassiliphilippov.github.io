ice bear

1. NOUN

/ aɪs bɛr / (AmE), / aɪs beə / (BrE)
another name for the polar bear, a large white bear living in Arctic regions
polar bear; Arctic white bear

Examples

The documentary followed an ice bear as it hunted for seals on the frozen sea.
Children learned about the challenges faced by the ice bear due to climate change.
In the Arctic, the ice bear is a symbol of strength and resilience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polar bear - a large white-furred bear native to the Arctic region
