oval-fruited

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvəl ˈfruːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəl ˈfruːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) bearing fruit that is oval in shape.
bearing oval fruit

Examples

The botanist described the new species as oval-fruited due to the distinctive shape of its berries.
Several oval-fruited varieties of tomatoes are popular among gardeners for their unique appearance.
The oval-fruited tree produces small, sweet fruit each summer.
