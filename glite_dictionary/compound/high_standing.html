high standing

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a position of respect, esteem, or favorable reputation within a group or society
position of respect or favorable reputation

Examples

Her family enjoys high standing in the local community because of their charitable work.
The scientist earned high standing among her peers for her groundbreaking research.
Maintaining high standing in the industry requires continuous innovation and dedication.

Synset Examples

The team maintained a high standing in the league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prestige - the respect and admiration that someone or something has because of their achievements or quality
  • Synonym: status - a person's social or professional position or ranking
  • Antonym: low standing - a position of little respect or low reputation
