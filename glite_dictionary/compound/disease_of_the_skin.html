disease of the skin

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈziz əv ðə skɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈziːz əv ðə skɪn / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the skin is affected by an illness, disorder, or abnormal state
illness affecting the skin
medical

Examples

Eczema is a common disease of the skin that often causes itching and redness.
The doctor specializes in treating rare diseases of the skin.
Sun exposure can sometimes trigger a disease of the skin in susceptible individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin disease - disease affecting the skin
