discount chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪskaʊnt tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪskaʊnt tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a group of retail stores owned by the same company, selling goods at lower than usual prices to attract customers
group of stores offering discounted prices

Examples

The local discount chain is known for its wide selection of affordable household goods.
Many shoppers prefer to buy groceries at a discount chain to save money.
The company plans to expand its discount chain into new regions next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount store - a retail store that sells goods at lower prices
