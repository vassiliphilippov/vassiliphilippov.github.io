crown princess

1. NOUN

/ kraʊn ˈprɪnsɛs / (AmE), / kraʊn ˈprɪnsɪs / (BrE)
a female heir apparent to a throne; a princess who is first in the line of succession.
female heir apparent to a throne

Examples

The crown princess will officially represent her country at the international summit next month.
As crown princess, she is expected to eventually ascend to the throne after her father's reign.
Many people admire the crown princess for her dedication to charitable causes and public service.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: crown prince - male heir apparent to a throne

2. NOUN

/ kraʊn ˈprɪnsɪs / (AmE), / kraʊn ˈprɪnsɪs / (BrE)
the wife of a crown prince, or a female royal who is heir apparent to the throne in her own right
wife of crown prince or female heir apparent

Examples

The crown princess attended the opening ceremony with her husband, the crown prince.
After her marriage, she became the country's crown princess and took on royal duties.
Many admired the crown princess for her dedication to charitable causes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crown prince - the male royal who is heir apparent to the throne

Etymology

From 'crown' (referring to monarchy) + 'princess' (female royal title), patterned after 'crown prince.'
