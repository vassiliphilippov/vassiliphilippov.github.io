pop-up toaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑp ʌp ˈtoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈpɒp ʌp ˈtəʊstə / (BrE)
An electric toaster with slots into which slices of bread are placed vertically, automatically raising the toast when done.
electric toaster that raises toast automatically

Examples

The pop-up toaster in our kitchen can toast four slices at once.
After the bread was done, it shot out of the pop-up toaster and landed on the counter.
She bought a new pop-up toaster with adjustable browning controls for perfect toast every morning.

Synset Examples

My pop-up toaster is terrible, it always throws my toast on the ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toaster - an electric appliance for toasting bread
