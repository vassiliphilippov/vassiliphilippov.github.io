cheese market

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz ˈmɑrkət / (AmE), / tʃiːz ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
a market where cheese is sold, often referring to traditional or specialized markets for cheese
market selling cheese

Examples

The town’s weekly cheese market attracts tourists from around the world.
They visited a traditional Dutch cheese market to watch the famous cheese weighing ceremony.
Vendors at the cheese market offered samples of local and imported varieties.

Synset Examples

Dutchland is famous for its windmills and cheese markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market - a regular gathering for the sale of goods
