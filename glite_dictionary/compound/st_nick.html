St. Nick

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt nɪk / (AmE), / sənt nɪk / (BrE)
Santa Claus; the legendary figure said to bring presents to children at Christmas.
Santa Claus; Christmas gift-bringer

Examples

Every year, children write letters to St. Nick asking for their favorite toys.
On Christmas Eve, we put out cookies and milk for St. Nick.
Many families tell stories about St. Nick and his magical sleigh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Santa Claus - legendary figure who brings gifts at Christmas
  • Synonym: Father Christmas - British term for Santa Claus, Christmas gift-bringer

Etymology

Short for Saint Nicholas, from the Christian bishop known for his generosity.
