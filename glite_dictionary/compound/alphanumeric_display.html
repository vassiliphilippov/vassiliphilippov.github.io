alphanumeric display

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.fə.nuːˈmɛr.ɪk dɪsˈpleɪ / (AmE), / ˌal.fə.njuːˈmɛr.ɪk dɪsˈpleɪ / (BrE)
an electronic display that presents information using a combination of letters and numbers
display showing letters and numbers

Examples

The airport uses a large alphanumeric display to show flight information for travelers.
Each calculator features an alphanumeric display to make data entry easier.
Engineers updated the device with a brighter alphanumeric display for improved readability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digital display - a display that provides information in a digital form
