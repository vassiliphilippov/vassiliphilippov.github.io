dog bite

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ baɪt / (AmE), / dɒɡ baɪt / (BrE)
an injury caused by the teeth of a dog puncturing or tearing the skin
injury from a dog's bite

Examples

She had to get medical treatment after suffering a dog bite on her arm.
There was a public awareness campaign about preventing dog bites in children.
The wound from the dog bite took weeks to heal.
