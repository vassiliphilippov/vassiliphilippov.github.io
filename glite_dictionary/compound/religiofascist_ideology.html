religiofascist ideology

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌlɪdʒioʊˈfæʃɪst aɪˈdɪoʊlədʒi / (AmE), / rɪˌlɪdʒiəʊˈfæʃɪst aɪˈdɪələdʒi / (BrE)
A set of political beliefs combining extreme religious and fascist authoritarianism.
belief system combining religious and fascist authoritarianism

Examples

Scholars have debated the influence of religiofascist ideology in shaping certain governments' policies.
The party promoted a religiofascist ideology that blended dogmatic faith with political repression.
She wrote an article critiquing the resurgence of religiofascist ideology in modern discourse.

Synset Examples

Many activists oppose the rise of religiofascist ideologies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theocracy - a system of government in which priests rule in the name of God
  • Synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization

Etymology

Formed from 'religio-' (relating to religion) and 'fascist' (from Italian 'fascismo').
