post card

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊst ˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst ˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a card for sending messages by mail without an envelope, typically with a picture or design on one side
card for sending messages without envelope

Examples

I bought a beautiful post card of the Eiffel Tower during my trip to Paris.
Please send your grandmother a post card to let her know you arrived safely.
He collects vintage post cards from all over the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postcard - alternate spelling; card for sending messages without an envelope
