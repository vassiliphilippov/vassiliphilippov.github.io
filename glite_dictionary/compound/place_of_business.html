place of business

1. NOUN

/ ˌpleɪs əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˌpleɪs əv ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
a building or location where commercial activities such as sales, manufacturing, warehousing, or services are conducted
a location for conducting business

Examples

Please provide proof of your place of business when applying for a license.
The company recently moved its place of business to the city center.
All correspondence should be sent to our official place of business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business premises - the building or property used for business activities
  • Antonym: residence - a place where people live
