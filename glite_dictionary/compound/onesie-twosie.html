onesie-twosie

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn.zi ˈtuː.zi / (AmE), / ˈwʌn.zi ˈtuː.zi / (BrE)
A playful or childish term for a one-piece outfit or pajamas, especially worn by children.
playful term for one-piece pajamas
informal

Examples

The kids wore their onesie-twosie pajamas to the sleepover party.
She bought a bright pink onesie-twosie for her daughter to wear at bedtime.
At the costume event, everyone showed up in a colorful onesie-twosie.
