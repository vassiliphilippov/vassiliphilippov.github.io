weather outlook

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ ˈaʊtˌlʊk / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˈaʊtlʊk / (BrE)
a prediction or forecast about future weather conditions for a specific period or area
forecast of future weather conditions

Examples

The weather outlook suggests sunshine for most of the weekend.
According to the latest weather outlook, we can expect rain tomorrow afternoon.
She checked the weather outlook before deciding to go hiking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather forecast - a prediction of what the weather will be like
