meatbrain moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmit ˌbreɪn ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈmiːt ˌbreɪn ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief period of forgetfulness or confusion, often in a mildly self-deprecating manner
moment of forgetfulness or confusion
informal slang

Examples

I had a meatbrain moment when I forgot my own phone number.
That was a real meatbrain moment—I left my keys in the fridge again.
Everyone has a meatbrain moment now and then, so don't feel bad.

Synset Examples

Her meatbrain moment came during the quiz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain fart - a brief mental lapse or moment of forgetfulness
