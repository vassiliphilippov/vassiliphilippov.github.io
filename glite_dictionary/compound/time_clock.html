time clock

1. NOUN

/ taɪm klɑk / (AmE), / taɪm klɒk / (BrE)
a clock used to record the times that employees start and finish work
clock for recording employees' work hours

Examples

Employees must use the time clock when starting and ending their shifts.
The old time clock in the break room was recently replaced with a digital system.
If you forget to punch in at the time clock, notify your supervisor immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punch clock - a clock for recording arrival and departure by inserting a card
  • Synonym: time sheet - a record of the hours worked by an employee
