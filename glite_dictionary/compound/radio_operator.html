radio operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ ˌɑpəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˌɒpəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who operates a radio transmitter, especially as a job or duty
person who operates a radio transmitter

Examples

The radio operator quickly relayed the distress call to nearby ships.
During the expedition, our radio operator maintained constant communication with base camp.
She trained as a radio operator during her time in the military.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wireless operator - person who operates wireless transmission equipment
