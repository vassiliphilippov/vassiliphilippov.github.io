friend of choice

1. NOUN

/ frɛnd əv tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / frɛnd əv tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
a friend who is specially selected or preferred for a particular purpose or activity
preferred friend for a purpose

Examples

Whenever I want to go hiking, Sarah is my friend of choice.
He became my friend of choice when it came to solving complex puzzles.
For movie nights, Mark is always my friend of choice.

Synset Examples

She is my friend of choice for this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chosen friend - a friend deliberately selected
