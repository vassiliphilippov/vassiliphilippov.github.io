security guard

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti ɡɑrd / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a person employed to protect property, people, or premises and prevent unauthorized access or disturbances
person who protects property or people

Examples

The security guard checked everyone's bags before they entered the building.
A security guard patrols the mall at night to deter theft.
The museum hired a new security guard after the recent break-in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard - person who keeps watch or protects
