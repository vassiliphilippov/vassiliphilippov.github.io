tip-top table

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪp tɑp ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪp tɒp ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
a pedestal table with a hinged top that can be tilted to a vertical position for storage or display
pedestal table with tilting top

Examples

The antique dealer showed us a beautiful tip-top table from the eighteenth century.
When not in use, the tip-top table can be folded up to save space in the room.
He admired the craftsmanship of the tip-top table, especially the elegant hinge mechanism.

Etymology

Named for the table's top that can be 'tipped' up vertically.
