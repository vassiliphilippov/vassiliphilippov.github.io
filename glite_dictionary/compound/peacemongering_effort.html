peacemongering effort

1. NOUN

/ ˈpisˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ ˈefət / (BrE)
an action or set of actions carried out to promote peace, often with the aim of ending conflict or preventing hostility
action to promote peace

Examples

Her peacemongering effort at the international conference won widespread praise.
The committee's peacemongering effort played a crucial role in resolving the dispute.
Despite criticism, his persistent peacemongering effort eventually brought both sides to the table.

Synset Examples

His peacemongering efforts helped end the neighborhood feud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace effort - action to bring about peace
  • Synonym: peacemaking - the process of creating peace between parties

Etymology

Formed from 'peace' + 'mongering' (from 'monger', meaning peddler or promoter) + 'effort'.
