sensitive pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.sə.tɪv pi / (AmE), / ˈsɛns.ɪ.tɪv piː / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with leaflets that respond to touch; sometimes classified in the genus Cassia.
tropical plant with touch-responsive leaflets

Examples

Many gardeners are fascinated by the sensitive pea and its ability to close its leaflets when touched.
The sensitive pea thrives in warm, humid environments and is native to tropical America.
Researchers study the sensitive pea to understand plant movement and response to physical stimuli.
