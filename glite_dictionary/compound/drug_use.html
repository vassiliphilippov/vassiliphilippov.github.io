drug use

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ juːs / (AmE), / drʌɡ juːs / (BrE)
the act or practice of taking drugs, especially illegal or harmful substances
the consumption of drugs, often illegal or harmful

Examples

The school implemented new policies to reduce drug use among students.
He spoke openly about his past drug use during the interview.
Public health officials are concerned about rising rates of drug use in the community.

Synset Examples

The program aims to educate youths about drug use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substance abuse - the harmful or hazardous use of psychoactive substances, including alcohol and illicit drugs
  • Synonym: drug abuse - the habitual taking of addictive or illegal drugs
