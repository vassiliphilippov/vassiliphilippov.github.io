lake salmon

1. NOUN

/ leɪk ˈsæmən / (AmE), / leɪk ˈsæmən / (BrE)
an Atlantic salmon that lives in lakes, especially in New England and southeastern Canada
Atlantic salmon living in lakes

Examples

The lake salmon in this region have adapted to freshwater habitats for their entire lives.
Scientists are studying lake salmon populations to understand their migration patterns.
Anglers often travel to Canada to fish for the renowned lake salmon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantic salmon - a species of salmon native to the North Atlantic
