supporting player

1. NOUN

/ səˈpɔɹ.tɪŋ ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / səˈpɔː.tɪŋ ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
an actor in a play, film, or show who has a role that is not one of the main principal characters
actor with a non-leading role

Examples

The film’s supporting players were praised for their memorable performances.
Many supporting players go on to become stars in their own right.
The director cast talented supporting players to bring depth to the story.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lead - the main actor in a play or movie
  • Antonym: principal - a leading actor or performer in a show
  • Synonym: supporting actor - an actor in a supporting role
