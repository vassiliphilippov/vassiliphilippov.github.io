sailor girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlɚ ˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female sailor, especially one who serves in the navy
female sailor; woman in the navy

Examples

The documentary follows a sailor girl as she navigates life aboard an aircraft carrier.
During the parade, a young sailor girl proudly wore her uniform.
She dreamed of becoming a sailor girl and traveling the world by sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship or serves in the navy
