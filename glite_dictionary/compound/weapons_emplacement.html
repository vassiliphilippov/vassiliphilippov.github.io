weapons emplacement

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛpənz ɪmˌpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz ɪmˌpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
A prepared site or structure where a gun or artillery piece is positioned for firing.
site or structure for positioning a gun

Examples

The soldiers reinforced the weapons emplacement before nightfall to prepare for an attack.
A camouflaged weapons emplacement overlooked the valley, ready to defend the strategic position.
They mapped every weapons emplacement to plan the safest route through enemy territory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gun emplacement - a prepared position for a gun or artillery piece
