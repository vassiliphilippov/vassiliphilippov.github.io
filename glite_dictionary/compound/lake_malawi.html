Lake Malawi

1. PROPN

/ leɪk məˈlɑːwi / (AmE), / leɪk məˈlɑːwi / (BrE)
A large, long lake located in southeastern Africa, bordered by Tanzania, Mozambique, and Malawi.
large lake in southeastern Africa

Examples

Many fish species found in Lake Malawi are unique to its waters.
Tourists often visit Lake Malawi for its beautiful beaches and clear waters.
The shores of Lake Malawi are important for local communities and biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Nyasa - alternate name for Lake Malawi
