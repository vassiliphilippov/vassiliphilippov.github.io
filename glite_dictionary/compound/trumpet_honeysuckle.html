trumpet honeysuckle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (BrE)
An evergreen North American climbing vine, Lonicera sempervirens, with coral-red or orange tubular flowers.
evergreen North American vine with red or orange flowers

Examples

The trumpet honeysuckle in our garden attracts hummingbirds all summer long.
She planted a trumpet honeysuckle to cover the old fence in her backyard.
Clusters of bright flowers bloomed on the trumpet honeysuckle by the porch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeysuckle - a climbing shrub with sweetly scented flowers
