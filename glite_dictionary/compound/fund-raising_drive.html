fund-raising drive

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ draɪv / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ draɪv / (BrE)
an organized campaign to collect money for a particular cause or project
campaign to collect money for a cause

Examples

The school organized a fund-raising drive to support new playground equipment.
During the annual fund-raising drive, community members donated generously to the local food bank.
The hospital's fund-raising drive helped secure much-needed medical supplies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charity drive - an organized effort to collect money or items for a charitable purpose
  • Synonym: fund-raiser - an event or person that raises money for a cause
