ax handle

1. NOUN

/ ˈæks ˌhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈæks ˌhændl / (BrE)
the wooden or metal handle of an ax, used to grip and wield the tool
handle of an ax

Examples

He replaced the broken ax handle before heading back to his work in the woods.
The old ax handle was smooth and worn from years of chopping firewood.
She carved her initials into the ax handle as a gift for her grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - part of an object designed for holding or grasping
