federal agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official who works for a national government agency, especially one involved in law enforcement or investigations
official of a federal government agency

Examples

A federal agent led the raid on the suspected drug cartel headquarters.
As a federal agent, she often traveled across the country for investigations.
The federal agent showed his badge before entering the restricted area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another or an organization
