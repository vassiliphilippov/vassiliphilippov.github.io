love lyric

1. NOUN

/ lʌv ˈlɪrɪk / (AmE), / lʌv ˈlɪrɪk / (BrE)
the words or poetry expressing romantic feelings in a love song or poem
words or poetry in a love song/poem

Examples

The love lyric captured the intense emotions of first romance.
She spent hours analyzing the love lyric in her favorite ballad.
Classic poets often wrote the perfect love lyric to woo their beloved.
