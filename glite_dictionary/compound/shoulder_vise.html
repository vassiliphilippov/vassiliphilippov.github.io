shoulder vise

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldɚ vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə vaɪs / (BrE)
a woodworking vise with padded jaws designed to hold lumber securely without denting it
woodworking vise with padded jaws

Examples

The carpenter used a shoulder vise to hold the plank steady while he planed it.
A shoulder vise is ideal for clamping boards without leaving marks on the wood.
Older workbenches often feature a built-in shoulder vise for precise woodworking tasks.
