power structure

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌstrʌk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌstrʌk.tʃə / (BrE)
the way in which authority and influence are organized and distributed among people in a group, organization, or society
hierarchical organization of authority

Examples

The new president promised to reform the power structure within the government.
Understanding the power structure of a company is essential before initiating negotiations.
Social movements often seek to challenge the existing power structure in society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hierarchy - a system in which members are ranked according to status or authority
