genus Ulex

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈjuː.lɛks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈjuː.lɛks / (BrE)
a genus of spiny shrubs native to Eurasia, commonly known as gorse
a genus of spiny shrubs (gorse)

Examples

The genus Ulex includes several species of gorse native to Western Europe.
Botanists have classified all typical gorse plants under the genus Ulex.
Ulex europaeus is a well-known member of the genus Ulex.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorse - a spiny yellow-flowered shrub of the genus Ulex

Etymology

From Latin 'ulex' meaning 'gorse'.
