Popular Struggle Front

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɑːpjələr ˈstrʌɡəl frʌnt / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈstrʌɡl frʌnt / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization that split from al-Fatah in 1967 and has carried out attacks against Israel, later developing close ties with al-Fatah
Palestinian militant organization

Examples

The Popular Struggle Front was involved in several attacks across the Israeli-Lebanese border in the 1970s.
Some members of the Popular Struggle Front later reconciled with leaders of al-Fatah.
Reports suggest that the Popular Struggle Front maintains political ties with other Palestinian factions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: al-Fatah - major Palestinian political and military organization
