genus Eacles

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈiːkliz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈiːkliːz / (BrE)
A genus of large moths in the family Saturniidae, including the imperial moth and related species.
a genus of imperial moths

Examples

The genus Eacles includes the well-known imperial moth found in North America.
Several species within the genus Eacles display striking yellow and purple coloring.
Entomologists are studying the population decline of the genus Eacles in certain regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imperial moth - a large, yellow and purple moth in the family Saturniidae

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Eacles', the genus name created in modern taxonomy.
