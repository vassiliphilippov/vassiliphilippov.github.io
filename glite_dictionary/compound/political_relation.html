political relation

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
A social relationship involving the pursuit or exercise of authority, power, or influence, often through intrigue or maneuvering.
relationship involving political power or intrigue

Examples

Complex political relation dynamics within the organization led to frequent conflicts.
Recent events have strained the political relation between the two local parties.
Understanding the political relation among leaders can reveal much about an institution’s power structure.

Synset Examples

office politics is often counterproductive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political - relating to government or public affairs
  • Degree Lower: diplomatic relation - relationship between governments or nations
