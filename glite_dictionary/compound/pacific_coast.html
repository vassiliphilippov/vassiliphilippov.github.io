Pacific Coast

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk koʊst / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk kəʊst / (BrE)
the stretch of land along the Pacific Ocean, especially the western coastline of North, Central, or South America
land bordering the Pacific Ocean

Examples

Many surfers are attracted to the Pacific Coast because of its impressive waves.
The highway runs parallel to the Pacific Coast, offering stunning ocean views.
Seals and sea lions can often be seen basking along the Pacific Coast beaches.
