batty joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæti dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈbæti dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is silly or eccentric, often unconventional or odd.
silly or eccentric joke

Examples

His batty joke made everyone at the table laugh, even if it was a bit strange.
At family gatherings, Uncle Jim is famous for telling at least one batty joke.
She broke the tension with a batty joke about dancing penguins.

Synset Examples

He always made batty jokes at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly joke - a joke that is foolish or lacking seriousness
  • Antonym: serious joke - a joke with a serious tone or subject

Etymology

From 'batty', meaning eccentric or crazy, and 'joke'. 'Batty' derives from 'bat' (mad as a bat).
