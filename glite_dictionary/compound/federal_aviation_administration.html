Federal Aviation Administration

a U.S. government agency in the Department of Transportation responsible for regulating and overseeing the safety of civil aviation
U.S. civil aviation regulatory agency
american

Examples

The Federal Aviation Administration sets strict standards for airline safety in the United States.
Pilots must comply with Federal Aviation Administration regulations during every flight.
