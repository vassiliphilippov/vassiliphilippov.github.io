midnight snack

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌnaɪt snæk / (AmE), / ˈmɪdnaɪt snak / (BrE)
a small meal or light food eaten during the late hours of the night, especially around midnight
a light meal eaten late at night

Examples

She got up for a midnight snack after studying for hours.
Sometimes all you need is a midnight snack to help you fall back asleep.
He made some toast as a quick midnight snack before returning to bed.

Synset Examples

He planned to sneak out for a midnight snack.
