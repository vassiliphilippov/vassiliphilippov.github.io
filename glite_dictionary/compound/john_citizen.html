John Citizen

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑn ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
a generic name used to refer to an average or ordinary person, especially in Australia
generic name for an average person

Examples

The new tax policy will affect every John Citizen in the country.
As a typical John Citizen, he found the regulations confusing.
Politicians must remember the needs of the average John Citizen when making decisions.

Synset Examples

As a John Citizen, he felt his voice mattered.
The law should protect every John Citizen equally.
Every John Citizen deserves a fair chance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Doe - a name used for an anonymous or average man
  • Synonym: Joe Public - a generic name for an ordinary person in the public
  • Synonym: Jane Citizen - the female equivalent of 'John Citizen', used generically

Etymology

Patterned after the use of 'John' as a generic male forename; 'Citizen' indicating membership in society. Used particularly in Australia.
