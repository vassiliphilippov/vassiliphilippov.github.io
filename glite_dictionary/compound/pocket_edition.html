pocket edition

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ɪˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a version of a book published in a small, portable size, often as a paperback, for easy carrying
small, portable book edition

Examples

She always carries a pocket edition of her favorite novel when she travels.
The publisher released a pocket edition to make the classic more accessible to students.
I found a vintage pocket edition of Shakespeare in the used bookstore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paperback - a book bound in a flexible cover, often of paper
