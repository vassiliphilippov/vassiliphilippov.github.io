worship of man

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːʃɪp əv mæn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp əv mæn / (BrE)
the practice or belief of revering or worshipping human beings as divine or exceptionally important
reverence or worship of humans

Examples

Some ancient cultures practiced worship of man, elevating rulers to godlike status.
Modern critics argue that celebrity culture has developed into a subtle worship of man.
The documentary explores the consequences of worship of man in totalitarian societies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropolatry - the worship of human beings; excessive admiration for humanity
