Aare River

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑ:r ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɑː ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in north central Switzerland that flows northeast into the Rhine
river in central Switzerland

Examples

The Aare River winds through the city of Bern, offering beautiful views and recreational opportunities.
Tourists often enjoy swimming in the clean waters of the Aare River during summer.
The Aare River eventually merges with the Rhine near the town of Koblenz.

Etymology

Possibly from Old High German 'Aro', the ancient name for the river.
