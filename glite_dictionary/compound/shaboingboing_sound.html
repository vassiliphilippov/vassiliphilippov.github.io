shaboingboing sound

1. NOUN

/ ʃəˈbɔɪŋˌbɔɪŋ saʊnd / (AmE), / ʃəˈbɔɪŋˌbɔɪŋ saʊnd / (BrE)
a playful, comical bouncing noise, often used to describe sounds made by toys or in cartoons
playful, comical bouncing noise
informal

Examples

The ball made a loud shaboingboing sound as it bounced off the walls.
Every time the cartoon character jumped, there was a silly shaboingboing sound in the background.
The new toy is so much fun because it makes a funny shaboingboing sound every time you squeeze it.

Synset Examples

The new toy made a shaboingboing sound when it bounced.
