saber saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪbər sɔ / (AmE), / ˈseɪbə sɔː / (BrE)
a portable power saw with a reciprocating blade, used for cutting various materials and capable of using different blades for different cuts
portable power saw with a reciprocating blade

Examples

The carpenter used a saber saw to cut intricate shapes in the plywood.
Before installing the sink, she grabbed her saber saw to make the required opening in the countertop.
Different blades can be fitted to a saber saw depending on the material being cut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jigsaw - a portable power saw with a vertical reciprocating blade
