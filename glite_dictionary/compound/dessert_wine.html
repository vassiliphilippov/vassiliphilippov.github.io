dessert wine

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɜrt waɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːt waɪn / (BrE)
A sweet wine, typically still, served with dessert or after a meal.
sweet wine served with dessert

Examples

The sommelier suggested a dessert wine to accompany the chocolate cake.
She prefers a glass of dessert wine after dinner to finish her meal.
Many dessert wines are made from grapes harvested late in the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortified wine - wine to which alcohol has been added; often sweet and served after meals
  • Antonym: table wine - ordinary wine served with a meal, not sweet or fortified
