lighting circuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌsɝkɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
a circuit of wiring that supplies power to electric lights
circuit providing power to lights

Examples

The electrician installed a new lighting circuit in the living room ceiling.
If a lighting circuit trips, check the fuse box for any blown fuses.
Modern homes often have more than one lighting circuit to control various rooms independently.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: power circuit - a circuit supplying power to appliances generally
