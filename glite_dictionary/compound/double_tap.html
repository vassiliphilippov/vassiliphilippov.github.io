to double tap

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌb.əl tæp / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl tæp / (BrE)
to tap twice rapidly on a touchscreen or device screen, usually to activate a specific function
tap twice rapidly on a touchscreen

Examples

Just double tap the image to zoom in quickly.
When you double tap the screen, the video pauses.
She accidentally liked the post by double tapping it.

Synset Examples

A double tap on the photo saves it to your favorites.
You need to double tap the screen to unlock it.
She learned to double tap for quick access.
