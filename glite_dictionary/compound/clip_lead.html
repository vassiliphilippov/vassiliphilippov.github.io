clip lead

1. NOUN

/ klɪp lid / (AmE), / klɪp liːd / (BrE)
A short piece of wire with alligator clips at both ends, used to make temporary electrical connections.
wire with alligator clips at both ends

Examples

He connected the battery to the circuit using a clip lead for a quick test.
Always check that the clip lead is not frayed or damaged before using it.
The technician used a red and a black clip lead to measure the voltage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test lead - A flexible wire with connectors at each end for electrical testing
