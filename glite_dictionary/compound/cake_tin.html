cake tin

1. NOUN

/ keɪk tɪn / (AmE), / keɪk tɪn / (BrE)
a metal container used for baking cakes, available in various shapes and sizes
metal container for baking cakes

Examples

She greased the cake tin before pouring in the batter.
You can use a round cake tin for this recipe or try a square one for a different look.
Make sure the cake tin is deep enough to hold all the mixture.

Related Terms
