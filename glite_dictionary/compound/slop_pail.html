slop pail

1. NOUN

/ slɑp peɪl / (AmE), / slɒp peɪl / (BrE)
a large container used for collecting waste water from a washbasin or chamber pot
container for waste water

Examples

The old house still had a slop pail stored under the washstand.
She emptied the slop pail each morning before breakfast.
Back then, every bedroom was equipped with a slop pail for convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chamber pot - a portable container for urination or defecation, typically used in a bedroom
