to fill the pothole

1. VERB

/ fɪl ðə ˈpɑthoʊl / (AmE), / fɪl ðə ˈpɒthəʊl / (BrE)
to repair a depression or hole in a road surface by adding suitable material
to repair a hole in a road

Examples

After the heavy rains, the city workers were called to fill the pothole near the intersection.
The crew arrived early in the morning to fill the pothole before rush hour traffic.
Local residents asked the council to fill the pothole that was damaging their cars.

Synset Examples

They used slag to fill the potholes on the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patch the road - to repair damaged sections of a road
