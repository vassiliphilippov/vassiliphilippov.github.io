flame durrajong

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm ˈdʌrədʒɒŋ / (AmE), / fleɪm ˈdɜːrədʒɒŋ / (BrE)
A South Australian tree known for its panicles of brilliant scarlet flowers.
South Australian tree with scarlet flowers

Examples

The flame durrajong brightened the garden with bursts of red blooms in early spring.
Local Aboriginal communities consider the flame durrajong an important part of their natural heritage.
We admired the striking panicles of the flame durrajong during our visit to southern Australia.
