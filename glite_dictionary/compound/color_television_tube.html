color television tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tjuːb / (BrE)
a cathode-ray tube used in televisions to display images in full color
cathode-ray tube for color TV display

Examples

Many vintage televisions contain a color television tube that is now considered outdated technology.
Technicians struggled to repair the old color television tube in my grandmother's living room set.
The invention of the color television tube revolutionized home entertainment in the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cathode-ray tube - a vacuum tube displaying images, used in older televisions and monitors
  • Antonym: black-and-white television tube - a television tube that displays images using only shades of gray
