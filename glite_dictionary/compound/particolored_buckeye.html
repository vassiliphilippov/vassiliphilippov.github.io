particolored buckeye

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːr.tɪˌkʌl.ɚd ˈbʌk.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.tɪˌkʌl.əd ˈbʌk.aɪ / (BrE)
a buckeye that is marked or patterned with different colors or tints
buckeye with varied colors

Examples

A particolored buckeye flew past, its wings flashing shades of brown, orange, and white in the sunlight.
She collected a particolored buckeye specimen for her biology class because of its unique markings.
Gardeners enjoy watching the particolored buckeye as it moves among the flowers.
