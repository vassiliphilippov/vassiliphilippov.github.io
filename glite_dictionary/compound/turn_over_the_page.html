to turn over the page

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈoʊvɚ ðə peɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈəʊvə ðə peɪdʒ / (BrE)
to move to the next page of a book, magazine, or similar material by physically flipping it
to flip to the next page

Examples

She reached the end of the chapter and decided to turn over the page.
When instructed, please turn over the page and continue with the next questions.
After reading the first part, he paused before he turned over the page to see what happened next.

Synset Examples

Please turn over the page for more information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flip the page - to quickly turn a page over
