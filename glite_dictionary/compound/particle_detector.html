particle detector

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrtɪkəl dɪˈtɛktər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪkl dɪˈtɛktə / (BrE)
a device or chamber used to detect and make visible subatomic particles, typically in physics experiments
device for detecting subatomic particles

Examples

The particle detector revealed traces of a new subatomic particle during the experiment.
Scientists adjusted the particle detector to improve its sensitivity for measuring rare events.
A malfunction in the particle detector caused the researchers to repeat the test.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detector - device for detecting the presence of something
