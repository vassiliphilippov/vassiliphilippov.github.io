spreading pogonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛdɪŋ pəˈgoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdɪŋ pəˈgəʊniə / (BrE)
a North American orchid with magenta-pink flowers, having a funnel-shaped lip, found in northeastern United States; sometimes placed in the genus Pogonia
North American orchid with magenta-pink flowers

Examples

The wetland was full of blooming spreading pogonia during the summer.
We spotted a rare spreading pogonia on our hike in the marsh.
Botanists often study the unique flowers of the spreading pogonia.
