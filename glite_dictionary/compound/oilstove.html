oilstove

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪlˌstoʊv / (AmE), / ˈɔɪl.stəʊv / (BrE)
A stove that uses oil, such as kerosene, as a fuel for heating or cooking.
stove burning oil for heat or cooking

Examples

The old cabin was kept warm by an oilstove during the long winter nights.
She cooked a simple meal on the oilstove while the power was out.
Many remote cottages rely on an oilstove for both heating and cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kerosene heater - a portable heater fueled by kerosene
