scribbling block

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrɪblɪŋ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈskrɪblɪŋ blɒk / (BrE)
A pad of paper used for preliminary or quick writing, notes, or sketches.
pad for quick notes or sketches
british

Examples

She jotted down the address quickly on her scribbling block before she forgot it.
A scribbling block is always handy when inspiration strikes during a meeting.
He casually sketched a landscape on the corner of his scribbling block during the lecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notepad - a pad of blank paper for writing notes
