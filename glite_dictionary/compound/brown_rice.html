brown rice

1. NOUN

/ braʊn raɪs / (AmE), / braʊn raɪs / (BrE)
unpolished rice that retains its outer bran layer, giving it a brownish color and a nuttier flavor than white rice
unpolished whole-grain rice

Examples

For a healthier option, substitute brown rice for white rice in your meals.
She prefers the chewy texture of brown rice in her stir-fries.
Brown rice contains more fiber and nutrients compared to refined rice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: white rice - rice that has had the bran layer removed, making it white
