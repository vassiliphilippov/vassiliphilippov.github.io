bus fare

1. NOUN

/ bʌs ˌfer / (AmE), / bʌs ˌfeə / (BrE)
the amount of money paid for a ride on a bus or streetcar
money paid to ride a bus

Examples

The bus fare has increased to $2.50 for a single trip.
Please have your bus fare ready before boarding.
In some cities, the bus fare is cheaper if you use a travel card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fare - the price paid for a journey on public transport
  • Synonym: bus ticket - a ticket purchased for a bus journey
