biology lab

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈɑlədʒi læb / (AmE), / baɪˈɒlədʒi læb / (BrE)
A laboratory equipped for biological research or experiments.
laboratory for biological research

Examples

She spent hours in the biology lab studying microscopic organisms.
The school's new biology lab is equipped with state-of-the-art technology.
During the experiment, we grew bacteria cultures in the biology lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments, research, or teaching
