general store

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛn.ɚl stɔr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.ərəl stɔː / (BrE)
A shop, typically in a rural area or small town, that sells a wide range of goods needed by the local community.
rural shop selling various goods

Examples

The general store in town stocks everything from canned food to hardware supplies.
She grew up working in her family’s general store in the countryside.
After the only general store closed, residents had to drive miles for groceries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convenience store - small shop selling everyday items, often in urban areas
