Himalayan cedar

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɪməˈleɪən ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / ˌhɪmələˈjɑːn ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a tall evergreen coniferous tree native to the western Himalayas, known for its spreading branches with nodding tips and valued for its timber and ornamental appearance
a tall, ornamental Himalayan conifer

Examples

The Himalayan cedar is often planted in parks for its striking appearance.
The timber from the Himalayan cedar is highly prized for building and furniture.
Many gardens feature a mature Himalayan cedar to provide shade and beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cedar - a type of large evergreen coniferous tree, often valued for fragrant wood
