wooden demeanor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdən dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdən dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a manner or appearance that seems stiff, awkward, or lacking in emotion
emotionless or stiff manner

Examples

Despite his achievements, his wooden demeanor made public speaking difficult for him.
The interviewer's questions were met with a wooden demeanor and short answers.
Her wooden demeanor at the ceremony suggested she was deeply uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

His wooden demeanor made it hard to connect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wooden - lacking ease or emotion; stiff
