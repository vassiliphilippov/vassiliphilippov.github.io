mincing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnsɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil or machine used to cut or chop food, especially meat, into small pieces
kitchen device for chopping food

Examples

We used a mincing machine to prepare the beef for the burgers.
The old mincing machine in the pantry still works perfectly.
Many recipes call for a mincing machine to create a fine meat texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meat grinder - a machine for mincing meat; synonym for mincing machine
