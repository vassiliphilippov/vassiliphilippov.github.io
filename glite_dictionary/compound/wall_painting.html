wall painting

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
A painting executed directly onto a wall surface, often as decoration or artwork.
painting applied directly to a wall

Examples

The ancient villa was adorned with beautiful wall paintings depicting mythological scenes.
The restoration project aims to preserve the original wall painting in the old cathedral.
She admired the colorful wall painting that covered the entrance of the community center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mural - A large painting or artwork executed directly on a wall
