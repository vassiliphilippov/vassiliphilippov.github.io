serious illness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəs ˈɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs ˈɪlnəs / (BrE)
a grave or severe health condition that is likely to have significant or lasting effects
grave or severe health condition

Examples

The doctor said her symptoms could indicate a serious illness and ordered further tests.
Many families struggle emotionally and financially when a serious illness strikes a loved one.
Vaccinations protect people from the risk of developing a serious illness caused by infections.

Synset Examples

Exposure to the bacillus can cause serious illness.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mild illness - a minor or not severe health condition
