automatic pistol

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈpɪstəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmatɪk ˈpɪstl̩ / (BrE)
a handgun that continues to fire as long as the trigger is held and there is ammunition in the magazine
handgun that fires automatically while trigger is held

Examples

The police seized an automatic pistol during the raid on the suspect's house.
He showed me how to safely operate an automatic pistol at the firing range.
Carrying an automatic pistol without a license is illegal in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine pistol - a fully automatic pistol; another term for automatic pistol
  • Antonym: revolver - a handgun with a revolving cylinder, usually not automatic
