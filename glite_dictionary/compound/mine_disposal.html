mine disposal

1. NOUN

/ maɪn dɪˈspoʊzəl / (AmE), / maɪn dɪˈspəʊzəl / (BrE)
the process of removing, neutralizing, or rendering safe explosive mines, especially in military or civilian safety contexts
removal or neutralization of explosive mines

Examples

The army's specialized unit was called in for mine disposal after the conflict ended.
Advances in technology have made mine disposal safer and more efficient.
Training for mine disposal operations requires expertise and careful attention to detail.
