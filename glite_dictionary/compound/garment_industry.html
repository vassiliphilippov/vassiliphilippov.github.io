garment industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrmənt ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector concerned with the production and sale of clothing, especially fashionable apparel
business of making and selling clothing

Examples

International trade agreements have a major impact on the garment industry.
Many young designers dream of succeeding in the garment industry.
The rise of ethical fashion has changed the standards in the garment industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion industry - the industry related to the design, manufacture, and marketing of clothing and accessories
  • Degree Lower: textile industry - industry involved in the production of yarn, fabric and clothing materials
