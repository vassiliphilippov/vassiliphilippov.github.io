sea mouse

1. NOUN

/ si ˈmaʊs / (AmE), / siː ˈmaʊs / (BrE)
A large marine worm with a broad, flattened body covered in a mat of coarse hairs, found on the seabed.
marine worm with hairy back

Examples

Divers were fascinated by the bright colors of the sea mouse gliding across the ocean floor.
The biology lab kept a live sea mouse to show students its bristly appearance.
A sea mouse is not actually a mouse but a type of marine worm.
