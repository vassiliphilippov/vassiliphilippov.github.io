trash pile

1. NOUN

/ træʃ paɪl / (AmE), / træʃ paɪl / (BrE)
a collection or heap of refuse, garbage, or discarded materials
heap of discarded garbage or refuse

Examples

The children found an old bicycle in the trash pile behind the house.
After the cleanup, the trash pile blocked the alley for several hours.
Stacks of newspapers and bottles were tossed onto the growing trash pile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of waste material or refuse
  • Synonym: dump - a site for depositing garbage
