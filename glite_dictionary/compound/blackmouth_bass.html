blackmouth bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌmaʊθ bæs / (AmE), / ˈblakmaʊθ bɑːs / (BrE)
a small marine fish distinguished by a black mouth and gill cavity
a small marine fish with a black mouth

Examples

The blackmouth bass has a distinctive dark coloration inside its mouth and gill cavity.
Anglers often try to catch blackmouth bass during the spring season near rocky shorelines.
We spotted several blackmouth bass swimming together close to the surface of the water.
