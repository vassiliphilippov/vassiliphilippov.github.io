tobacconist's shop

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækənɪst ʃɑp / (AmE), / təˈbækənɪst ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where tobacco, cigarettes, cigars, and smoking accessories are sold.
shop selling tobacco products

Examples

I stopped by the tobacconist's shop to buy a box of fine cigars.
The tobacconist's shop on the corner carries a wide selection of pipes and accessories.
During his trip to London, he visited a historic tobacconist's shop near Piccadilly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tobacconist - person who sells tobacco and related products

Etymology

From 'tobacconist' (dealer in tobacco) + 'shop' (place of business).
