fat cat

1. NOUN

/ fæt kæt / (AmE), / fæt kæt / (BrE)
a wealthy and powerful person, especially one who is perceived as greedy or having undue influence
wealthy and influential person

Examples

After the merger, several fat cats walked away with huge bonuses.
The politician courted donations from fat cats in the business world.
Many people resent the power held by corporate fat cats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tycoon - a wealthy and powerful businessperson
  • Synonym: big shot - an important or influential person
  • Antonym: pauper - a very poor person

Etymology

Originally U.S. slang from the early 20th century, referring to wealthy political contributors.
