chest of drawers

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛst əv drɔrz / (AmE), / tʃɛst əv drɔːz / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with a set of drawers for storing clothes and personal items
furniture with drawers for clothes

Examples

We bought a beautiful oak chest of drawers for the bedroom.
He keeps his socks and T-shirts in the top chest of drawers.
The antique chest of drawers has ornate handles and four spacious drawers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dresser - a similar piece of furniture for storing clothes
