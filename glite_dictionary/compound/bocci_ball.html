bocci ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑtʃi ˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃi ˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A wooden or composite ball used in the Italian bowling game of bocce.
ball used in bocce

Examples

Players take turns rolling the bocci ball toward the pallino.
He bought a new set of bocci balls for the summer tournament.
The red bocci ball landed closest to the target.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bocce - Italian bowling game in which balls are rolled toward a smaller target ball

Etymology

From Italian 'bocce', plural of 'boccia' (ball), referring to the bowling game.
