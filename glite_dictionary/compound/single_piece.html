single piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl pis / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəl piːs / (BrE)
an object made or considered as one whole unit, not consisting of multiple parts
one whole unit; not multiple parts

Examples

The artist created the statue from a single piece of wood.
Please cut the cake into single pieces for each guest.
The dress was made from a single piece of fabric without any seams.

Synset Examples

The sculpture was carved from a single piece of marble.
