exhaust fumes

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɔst fjumz / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst fjuːmz / (BrE)
gases or smoke produced as waste by a vehicle engine and released into the air
waste gases from an engine

Examples

The city was filled with exhaust fumes after hours of heavy traffic.
Breathing in exhaust fumes can be harmful to your health.
New regulations aim to reduce exhaust fumes from diesel engines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emissions - gases released into the air, especially from vehicles or factories
  • Synonym: pollution - the presence of substances that contaminate the environment
