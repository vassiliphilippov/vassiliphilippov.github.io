plumbing issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈplʌmɪŋ ˌɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈplʌmɪŋ ˌɪsjuː / (BrE)
a problem or fault in the water supply, drainage, or other piping systems in a building
problem with water or pipe systems

Examples

The landlord fixed the plumbing issue in the kitchen last weekend.
We need to call a plumber because there seems to be a plumbing issue in the bathroom.
A minor plumbing issue can quickly turn into a costly repair if left untreated.

Synset Examples

He called a professional to inspect the plumbing issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leak - an unintended hole or crack in a pipe or container through which liquid or gas escapes
  • Synonym: blockage - an obstruction causing something to be blocked, especially in pipes
