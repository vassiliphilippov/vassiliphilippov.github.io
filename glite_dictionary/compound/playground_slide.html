playground slide

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɡraʊnd slaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɡraʊnd slaɪd / (BrE)
a piece of playground equipment with a smooth, sloping surface for children to slide down
sloping play equipment for sliding

Examples

The children raced each other to the top of the playground slide.
She waited her turn patiently before going down the tall playground slide.
Someone had polished the playground slide, making it extra fast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slide - a structure for sliding down in a playground
