almond-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːmənd ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the characteristic smell of almonds.
smelling of almond

Examples

The almond-scented candle filled the room with a sweet, nutty aroma.
She wore an almond-scented lotion that lingered all day.
The bakery's almond-scented pastries were especially popular during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almond-flavored - tasting of almond
