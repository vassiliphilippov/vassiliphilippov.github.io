service man

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs mæn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs mæn / (BrE)
a male skilled worker whose job is to repair machinery, equipment, or appliances
male repair worker

Examples

The service man arrived promptly to fix the leaking washing machine.
If your television breaks down, you should call a qualified service man.
The service man explained the problem and replaced the faulty part within minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairman - a person whose job is repairing machinery or appliances
  • Synonym: technician - a worker skilled in the technique of a particular art or craft
