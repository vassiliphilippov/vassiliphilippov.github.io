bed wetter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛd ˌwɛtər / (AmE), / ˈbɛd ˌwɛtə / (BrE)
a person, typically a child, who urinates involuntarily while asleep in bed
someone who urinates in bed while asleep

Examples

The doctor advised the bed wetter and his parents on possible treatments.
She was embarrassed about being a bed wetter as a child, but later outgrew it.
Support groups can help a bed wetter feel less alone with the problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enuretic - person who suffers from enuresis
