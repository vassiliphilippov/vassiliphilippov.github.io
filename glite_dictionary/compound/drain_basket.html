drain basket

1. NOUN

/ dreɪn ˈbæskət / (AmE), / dreɪn ˈbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A perforated container or strainer placed in a sink drain to catch debris while allowing water to pass through.
perforated strainer for sink drains

Examples

Before cleaning the dishes, she removed the drain basket to throw away food scraps.
It's important to empty the drain basket regularly to prevent clogs in the sink.
The plumber replaced the old drain basket with a new stainless steel one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strainer - a device with holes, used to separate solid matter from liquid
