mud bath

1. NOUN

/ mʌd bæθ / (AmE), / mʌd bɑːθ / (BrE)
a treatment in which a person bathes in warm mud, often for therapeutic or health benefits
bath in warm mud for health

Examples

My grandmother swears by a mud bath to soothe her arthritis pain.
Tourists often visit the resort to experience a rejuvenating mud bath.
After a relaxing mud bath, her skin felt remarkably soft and smooth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spa treatment - a therapeutic procedure at a health spa
