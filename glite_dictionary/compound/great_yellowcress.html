great yellowcress

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt ˈjɛloʊˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈjɛləʊˌkrɛs / (BrE)
a perennial herb found on streams and riversides throughout much of Europe, with yellow flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Nasturtium
a European riverside perennial herb

Examples

The riverbank was lined with tall great yellowcress in full bloom.
Botanists recorded several patches of great yellowcress during the survey.
You can often find great yellowcress near slow-moving streams in the countryside.
