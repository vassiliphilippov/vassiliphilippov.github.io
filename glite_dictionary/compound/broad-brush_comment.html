broad-brush comment

1. NOUN

/ ˌbrɔd ˈbrʌʃ ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˌbrɔːd ˈbrʌʃ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
A remark or statement that is general and lacks specific details or nuance.
general remark lacking detail

Examples

Her broad-brush comment about the team's efforts overlooked their individual achievements.
It is easy to make a broad-brush comment without knowing all the facts involved.
Critics accused the report of containing too many broad-brush comments and not enough specifics.

Synset Examples

His broad-brush comments ignored the nuances of the topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generalization - a general statement or concept obtained by inference from specific cases
