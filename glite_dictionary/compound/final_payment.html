final payment

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the last payment made to settle a debt or fulfill a financial obligation
last payment of a debt

Examples

After three years of monthly installments, he finally made his final payment on the car loan.
You will receive the title to your house once the bank gets the final payment.
She was relieved when she submitted the final payment and cleared her student debt.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: installment - a regular payment toward a debt
