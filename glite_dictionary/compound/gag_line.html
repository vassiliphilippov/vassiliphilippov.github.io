gag line

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæɡ ˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡæɡ ˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the final line or phrase of a joke or humorous story that delivers the punchline or main comedic point
joke's punchline or main comedic line

Examples

The comedian delivered the gag line with perfect timing, bringing the audience to laughter.
You could tell the whole story was leading up to that clever gag line.
He forgot the gag line and the joke fell flat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punchline - the final phrase or sentence of a joke that delivers the humor
