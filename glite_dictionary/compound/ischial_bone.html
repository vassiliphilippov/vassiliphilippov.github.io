ischial bone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪskiəl boʊn / (AmE), / ˈɪskɪəl bəʊn / (BrE)
The lower, posterior section of the hipbone, forming part of the pelvis.
lower part of the hipbone
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the fracture was located in the ischial bone of the pelvis.
Pressure sores often develop over the ischial bone area in patients who use wheelchairs.
During the procedure, the surgeon avoided damaging the ischial bone.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ilium - uppermost and largest part of the hipbone
  • Degree Higher: pubis - anterior section of the hipbone

Etymology

From Greek 'ischion' (hip joint).
