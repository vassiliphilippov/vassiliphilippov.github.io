agricultural practice

1. NOUN

/ ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃɚ.əl ˈpræk.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rɪˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈpræk.tɪs / (BrE)
a method or technique used in farming to cultivate crops or raise livestock
method or technique used in farming

Examples

Modern agricultural practice includes the use of advanced machinery and technology.
Sustainable agricultural practices are crucial for protecting the environment.
Many countries are adopting organic agricultural practices to meet consumer demand.

Synset Examples

They discussed agricultural practices at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farming method - a way or technique of carrying out farming

Etymology

From Latin 'agricultura' (farming) + 'practice' (from Greek 'praktikos', practical).
