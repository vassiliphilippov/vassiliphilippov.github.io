cubeb cigarette

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bɛb ˌsɪg.əˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɛb ˌsɪɡ.ərˈɛt / (BrE)
a cigarette containing cubeb, a spice sometimes used historically for medicinal purposes
cigarette containing cubeb spice

Examples

In the early 20th century, a cubeb cigarette was sometimes recommended for respiratory issues.
The doctor suggested trying a cubeb cigarette to relieve his sore throat, though the practice is now outdated.
She found an old advertisement for a cubeb cigarette in a vintage magazine collection.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cubeb - a spice made from the dried fruit of Piper cubeba

Etymology

From 'cubeb', a spice derived from the dried unripe fruit of Piper cubeba, and 'cigarette', from French 'cigarette'.
