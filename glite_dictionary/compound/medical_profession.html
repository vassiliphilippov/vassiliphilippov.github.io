medical profession

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl prəˈfɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl prəˈfɛʃən / (BrE)
the group of people who are qualified and licensed to practice medicine as a profession
the body of qualified medical practitioners

Examples

After years of training, she finally joined the medical profession as a licensed doctor.
The medical profession has made significant advances in the treatment of cancer.
Ethics and responsibility are essential values in the medical profession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physician - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • Synonym: medical practitioner - a professional who practices medicine
