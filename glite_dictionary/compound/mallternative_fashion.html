mallternative fashion

1. NOUN

/ mɔlˈtɜrnətɪv ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
A style of fashion characterized by alternative, often edgy clothing styles typically associated with youth culture and suburban malls.
alternative fashion style linked to malls
informal

Examples

She expresses herself through mallternative fashion, choosing band tees and chunky boots.
The rise of mallternative fashion in the 2000s gave teens a new sense of identity.
Many stores in suburban malls cater to the mallternative fashion crowd.

Synset Examples

The mallternative fashion scene attracts a unique crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alternative fashion - fashion style that is outside the mainstream, often with a rebellious edge

Etymology

Blend of 'mall' and 'alternative', referencing alternative fashion styles popularized in shopping malls.
