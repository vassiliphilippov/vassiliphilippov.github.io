tall white violet

1. NOUN

/ tɔl waɪt ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / tɔːl waɪt ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
a tall North American perennial plant with heart-shaped leaves and white flowers marked with purple streaks, belonging to the violet family
North American plant with white, purple-streaked flowers

Examples

The tall white violet blooms in early spring, brightening forest floors with its delicate flowers.
She spotted a tall white violet growing beside the hiking trail in the mountains.
Botanists studied the distribution of the tall white violet across North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - a small plant with heart-shaped leaves and purple, blue, or white flowers
