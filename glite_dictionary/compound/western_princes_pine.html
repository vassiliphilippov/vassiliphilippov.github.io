western prince's pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈprɪnsɪz paɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈprɪnsɪz paɪn / (BrE)
A Eurasian herbaceous plant bearing white or pinkish flowers arranged in a terminal cluster (corymb).
Eurasian herb with clustered white or pinkish flowers

Examples

The shady forest floor was covered in western prince's pine during our hike.
Researchers identified western prince's pine by its cluster of delicate pinkish flowers.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate western prince's pine for its ornamental blooms.
