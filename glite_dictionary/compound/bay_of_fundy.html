Bay of Fundy

1. PROPN

/ beɪ əv ˈfʌndi / (AmE), / beɪ əv ˈfʌndi / (BrE)
a bay of the North Atlantic situated between New Brunswick and Nova Scotia, Canada, famous for its exceptionally high tidal range
bay in Canada with extreme tides

Examples

The Bay of Fundy attracts thousands of tourists each year due to its dramatic tidal changes.
Whale watching is a popular activity in the Bay of Fundy.
Scientists study the Bay of Fundy to better understand tidal energy and marine ecosystems.
