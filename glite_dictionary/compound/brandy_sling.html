brandy sling

1. NOUN

/ ˈbræn.di slɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbræn.di slɪŋ / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink made by mixing brandy, sugar, and water, often served with ice and sometimes lemon peel.
cocktail of brandy, sugar, and water

Examples

He ordered a brandy sling at the bar to start the evening.
On cold nights, a warm brandy sling can be quite comforting.
The recipe for a classic brandy sling has changed little over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sling - a mixed drink made of a spirit, sugar, and water

Etymology

From 'brandy', a spirit distilled from wine, and 'sling', a term for a mixed drink, both of which entered English by the 18th century.
