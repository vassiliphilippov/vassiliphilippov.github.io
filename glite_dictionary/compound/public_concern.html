public concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk kənˈsɜːrn / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
an issue or matter that is important to, or affects, the wider community or society as a whole
issue important to the community

Examples

The rise in air pollution has become a major public concern in recent years.
Affordable housing remains a significant public concern in urban areas.
Water safety is always a public concern after heavy floods.

Synset Examples

Homeless dumping has become a serious public concern.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public interest - the welfare or well-being of the general public
