common spindle tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈspɪndəl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈspɪndl ˌtriː / (BrE)
a small, upright deciduous shrub (Euonymus europaeus) with tough white wood and bark and fruit used as a cathartic
a deciduous shrub, Euonymus europaeus

Examples

The common spindle tree is often found growing along the edges of woodlands across Europe.
Baskets and spindles were once crafted from the tough wood of the common spindle tree.
The bark and fruit of the common spindle tree have traditional medicinal uses, though they should be used with caution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spindle tree - any shrub of the genus Euonymus
