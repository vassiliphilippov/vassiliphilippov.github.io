silver jenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər ˈdʒɛni / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈdʒɛni / (BrE)
a silvery fish (mojarra) found along the sandy shores of the western Atlantic
a silvery mojarra fish

Examples

Fishermen often catch silver jenny along the warm beaches of Florida.
The silver jenny is prized for its delicate, silvery scales and mild flavor.
A school of silver jenny darted through the shallow waters near the shore.
