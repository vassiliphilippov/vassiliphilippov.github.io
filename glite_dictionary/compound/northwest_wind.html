northwest wind

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrθˌwɛst ˈwɪnd / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˌwɛst ˈwɪnd / (BrE)
A wind blowing from the northwest.
wind from the northwest

Examples

A strong northwest wind battered the coastline last night.
The temperature dropped quickly due to a cold northwest wind.
Sailors always watch for a shift to a northwest wind before heading out to sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: north wind - a wind from the north
  • Synonym: northerly wind - a wind coming from the north
