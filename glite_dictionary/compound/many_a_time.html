many a time

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛni ə ˈtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈmɛni ə ˈtaɪm / (BrE)
on many occasions; frequently; often
frequently; often

Examples

I've heard that old story many a time from my grandfather.
Many a time, she has stayed late to finish her work.
He has warned me many a time about the dangers of driving fast.

Synset Examples

She visited that café many a time and oft.
Many a time and oft, we enjoyed summer picnics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: often - frequently; many times
  • Antonym: seldom - not often; rarely
