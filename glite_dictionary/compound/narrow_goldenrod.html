narrow goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛroʊ ˈgoʊldəˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ ˈɡəʊldənrɒd / (BrE)
A species of goldenrod native to western North America, characterized by long, narrow clusters of small yellow flowers.
western North American goldenrod plant

Examples

The narrow goldenrod thrives in the dry regions of the American West.
She identified the plant by its long clusters of yellow flowers as narrow goldenrod.
Hikers marveled at the fields dotted with blooming narrow goldenrod in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago with clusters of yellow flowers
