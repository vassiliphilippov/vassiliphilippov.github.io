Siberian crab

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈbɪriən kræb / (AmE), / sɪˈbɪəriən kræb / (BrE)
an Asian wild crab apple tree (Malus baccata) cultivated for its small, acidic, usually red fruit used in preserves
Asian crab apple tree with small, tart fruit

Examples

The Siberian crab is often grown in gardens for its attractive flowers and colorful fruit.
Homemade jelly made from Siberian crab apples has a unique tart flavor.
Because of its hardiness, the Siberian crab thrives in cold climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a small, sour apple and the tree that bears it
