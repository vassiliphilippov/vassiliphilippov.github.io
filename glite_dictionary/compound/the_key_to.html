the key to

1. NOUN

/ ði ki tu / (AmE), / ðiː kiː tʊ / (BrE)
the essential element or means for achieving or understanding something
essential element for achieving something

Examples

Hard work is the key to passing your exams.
Trust is the key to a strong relationship.
Innovation is often the key to success in business.

Synset Examples

This document is the key to understanding the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the secret to - the crucial factor for achieving or understanding something
  • Synonym: the solution to - the answer or means of solving a problem
