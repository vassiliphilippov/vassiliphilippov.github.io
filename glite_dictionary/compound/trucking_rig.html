trucking rig

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌkɪŋ rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkɪŋ rɪɡ / (BrE)
a large truck made up of a tractor and trailer joined together, used for hauling freight over long distances
truck with tractor and trailer
american

Examples

The trucking rig thundered down the highway, hauling a load of fresh produce across state lines.
After years behind the wheel of his trucking rig, Joe knew every rest stop in the region.
The company invested in a new trucking rig to meet the increased demand for deliveries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semi-trailer truck - a truck pulling a semi-trailer
  • Synonym: lorry - British term for a large truck
