American green toad

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ɡriːn toʊd / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ɡriːn təʊd / (BrE)
a small green or yellow-green toad with small black bars and stripes, found in parts of North America
a species of small green toad

Examples

The American green toad is known for its distinctive coloring and markings.
Herpetologists often study the American green toad to understand its habitat preferences.
We spotted several American green toads near the edge of the pond.
