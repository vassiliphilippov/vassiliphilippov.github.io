ticket holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who possesses a ticket for admission or passage
person with an admission or travel ticket

Examples

Each ticket holder must present a valid ID at the entrance.
Only one ticket holder per invitation will be admitted to the event.
The train was delayed, causing frustration among the ticket holders.
