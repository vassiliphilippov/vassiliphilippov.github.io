word form

1. NOUN

/ wɝd fɔrm / (AmE), / wɜːd fɔːm / (BrE)
a particular written or spoken form of a word, as distinguished by its phonological or orthographic properties
specific written or spoken form of a word

Examples

When learning Spanish, you must memorize each word form for regular and irregular verbs.
The dictionary lists every word form derived from the root word.
She analyzed the word form of the noun to find its grammatical function.
