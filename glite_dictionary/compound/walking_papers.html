walking papers

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkɪŋ ˈpeɪpɚz / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ˈpeɪpəz / (BrE)
(informal) An official notice that one has been dismissed or discharged, especially from a job.
dismissal notice (job)
informal

Examples

After years at the company, Mark finally got his walking papers last Friday.
Rumors started flying when several employees were handed their walking papers after the merger.
She dreaded opening the envelope, afraid it might contain her walking papers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink slip - a notice of dismissal from employment
