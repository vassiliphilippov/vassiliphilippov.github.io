ultimate cockteaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltɪmət ˈkɑkˌtizər / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ˈkɒkˌtiːzə / (BrE)
A person who strongly provokes sexual interest or excitement in others but has no intention of reciprocating or engaging in sexual activity, especially in a teasing or provocative way.
person who intensifies sexual teasing without intent
informal vulgar

Examples

Everyone agreed that Mark was the ultimate cockteaser at the party, flirting with everyone but leaving every conversation unfinished.
She enjoyed the attention and had a reputation as the ultimate cockteaser among her friends.
His reputation as the ultimate cockteaser made people wary of taking his flirtations seriously.

Synset Examples
