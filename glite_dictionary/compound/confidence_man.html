confidence man

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɪ.dəns ˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dəns ˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who cheats people by gaining their trust and persuading them to believe something false
man who swindles by gaining trust

Examples

The notorious confidence man convinced investors to hand over large sums of money.
Mark Twain's story featured a clever confidence man who tricked entire towns.
After years on the run, the confidence man was finally arrested by the authorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: con man - a person who cheats others by means of a confidence trick
  • Synonym: swindler - a person who deceives others for personal gain

Etymology

From 'confidence' (trust) and 'man'; first recorded in mid-19th century America.
