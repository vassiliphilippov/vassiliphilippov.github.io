ear hook earring

1. NOUN

/ ɪr hʊk ˈɛr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪə hʊk ˈɛə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
an earring designed to be worn on the rim of the ear without requiring a piercing, often shaped as a hook or wrap that fits over the ear
non-pierced earring worn over the ear rim

Examples

She wore a sparkling ear hook earring that wrapped gracefully around her ear.
The ear hook earring is a great choice for people without pierced ears.
He admired the elaborate design of her silver ear hook earring at the art show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clip-on earring - an earring fastened to the earlobe by pressure rather than piercing
