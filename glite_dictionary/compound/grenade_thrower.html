grenade thrower

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈneɪd ˌθroʊɚ / (AmE), / ɡrəˈneɪd ˌθrəʊə / (BrE)
a soldier equipped to throw grenades, especially as part of an infantry unit
soldier who throws grenades

Examples

The grenade thrower led the assault on the enemy bunker.
Each squad included a designated grenade thrower for close combat situations.
During the exercise, the grenade thrower demonstrated proper technique for throwing fragmentation grenades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grenadier - historically, a soldier who specialized in throwing grenades
