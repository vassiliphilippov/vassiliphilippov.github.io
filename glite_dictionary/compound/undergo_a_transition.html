to undergo a transition

1. VERB

/ ˌʌndɚˈɡoʊ ə trænˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈɡəʊ ə trænˈzɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
to experience a significant or major change in one's condition, status, or circumstances
experience a major change

Examples

After twenty years as a teacher, Mark is undergoing a transition to a career in counseling.
The company is undergoing a transition as it shifts to sustainable energy sources.
Many students find themselves undergoing a transition from dependence to independence during college.

Synset Examples

She is undergoing a transition to become her true self.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transition - a process or period of changing from one state or condition to another
  • Synonym: transformation - a thorough or dramatic change in form or appearance
