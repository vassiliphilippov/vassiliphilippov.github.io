Democratic Party

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɛm.əˈkræt.ɪk ˈpɑɹ.ti / (AmE), / ˌdɛm.əˈkræt.ɪk ˈpɑː.ti / (BrE)
one of the two major political parties in the United States, traditionally associated with liberal and progressive policies
major U.S. political party

Examples

She is an active member of the Democratic Party and volunteers during election campaigns.
The Democratic Party held its national convention in Chicago this year.
Many young voters are attracted to the progressive policies promoted by the Democratic Party.
