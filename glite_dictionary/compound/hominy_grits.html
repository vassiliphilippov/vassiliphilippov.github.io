hominy grits

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːmɪni ɡrɪts / (AmE), / ˈhɒmɪni ɡrɪts / (BrE)
coarsely ground hulled corn that is boiled and served as a breakfast dish, especially in the southern United States
ground, boiled corn breakfast dish

Examples

In the South, people often eat hominy grits with butter and cheese for breakfast.
She ordered hominy grits as a side dish with her eggs.
My grandmother makes the creamiest hominy grits on Sunday mornings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grits - ground corn or maize boiled as food, especially in the southern U.S.

Etymology

"Hominy" from Powhatan (Virginia Algonquian) *rockahominy*, referring to parched corn; "grits" from Old English *grytt* meaning coarse meal.
