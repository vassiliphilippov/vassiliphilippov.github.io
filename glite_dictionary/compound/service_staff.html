service staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪs stæf / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs stɑːf / (BrE)
the employees in a business or organization who are responsible for maintaining facilities and providing support services, such as cleaning, maintenance, or other non-managerial roles
employees responsible for maintenance and support tasks

Examples

The service staff ensured that the hotel rooms were clean and well-maintained for every guest.
If you have any issues during your stay, please contact our service staff for assistance.
The restaurant hired additional service staff to handle the increased number of customers during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support staff - employees who provide administrative or technical assistance in an organization
