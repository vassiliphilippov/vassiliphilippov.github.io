globe pepper

1. NOUN

/ ɡloʊb ˈpɛpər / (AmE), / ɡləʊb ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
A round, sweet variety of pepper with thick flesh, similar to a bell pepper.
round sweet variety of pepper

Examples

She diced the globe pepper and added it to her salad for a burst of color and flavor.
The farmer grew several varieties, but the red globe pepper was his most popular at the market.
With its thick walls and mild taste, the globe pepper is ideal for stuffing and roasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bell pepper - a sweet pepper with a blocky shape
