Right Bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪt ˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌbaŋk / (BrE)
the part of Paris situated on the north bank of the River Seine, known for its cultural, commercial, and historical significance
north bank of the Seine in Paris

Examples

Many famous art galleries and shops are located on the Right Bank of Paris.
She preferred to stay at a hotel on the Right Bank during her visit to France.
The Right Bank has long been associated with business and luxury.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Left Bank - the region of Paris on the south bank of the Seine
