bowl of lau kui

1. NOUN

/ boʊl əv laʊ kuːi / (AmE), / bəʊl əv laʊ kuːi / (BrE)
A serving of the dish known as lau kui, typically presented in a bowl.
a serving of lau kui in a bowl

Examples

I ordered a bowl of lau kui at the new Asian restaurant downtown.
She offered her guests a bowl of lau kui as part of the meal.
After a long hike, nothing sounded better than a hot bowl of lau kui.

Synset Examples

He enjoyed a warm bowl of lau kui for breakfast.

Etymology

'Lau kui' is likely of East Asian origin; further etymology depends on the culinary culture of the dish.
