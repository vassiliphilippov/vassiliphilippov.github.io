water lettuce

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A tropical floating plant (Pistia stratiotes) with soft, wedge-shaped leaves that form a rosette, often found as a weed in rivers and lakes.
tropical floating aquatic plant (Pistia stratiotes)

Examples

A thick mat of water lettuce was choking the pond's surface.
The rapid spread of water lettuce poses a threat to native aquatic species.
Gardeners often remove water lettuce to prevent it from overtaking decorative ponds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pistia stratiotes - Botanical name for water lettuce
