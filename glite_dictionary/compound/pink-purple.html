pink-purple

1. ADJ

/ pɪŋk ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈpɜːpəl / (BrE)
A color that is predominantly purple with a tinged or noticeable shade of pink.
purple tinted with pink

Examples

The sunset was painted with streaks of pink-purple light across the sky.
She wore a lovely pink-purple dress to the wedding.
The artist mixed red and blue to create a vibrant pink-purple hue.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
