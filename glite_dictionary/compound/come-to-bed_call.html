come-to-bed call

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌm tə ˈbɛd kɔl / (AmE), / ˌkʌm tə ˈbɛd kɔːl / (BrE)
an invitation or signal indicating a wish for intimacy or for someone to join one in bed
invitation or signal for intimacy

Examples

She gave him a subtle come-to-bed call with just a glance across the room.
The movie ended with a humorous come-to-bed call from one character to another.
Ignoring his wife's come-to-bed call, he stayed up late finishing his work.

Synset Examples

His come-to-bed call echoed through the house.
