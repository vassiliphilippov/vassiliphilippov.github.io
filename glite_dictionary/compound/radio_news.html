radio news

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ nuz / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ njuːz / (BrE)
News programs or bulletins broadcast on the radio.
news broadcast on radio

Examples

She listens to the radio news every morning before going to work.
The radio news reported heavy traffic delays in the city center.
He prefers getting his updates from radio news rather than television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news - information about recent events
