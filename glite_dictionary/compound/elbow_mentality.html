elbow mentality

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlboʊ mɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊ mɛnˈtælɪti / (BrE)
a competitive attitude that puts one's own interests ahead of others, often disregarding collaboration or teamwork
competitive, self-serving mindset

Examples

The company's elbow mentality discouraged new employees from sharing ideas.
Many believe that an elbow mentality is necessary to succeed in highly competitive industries.
Her elbow mentality sometimes alienated her from her colleagues.

Synset Examples

The team's elbow mentality led to missed opportunities.
He always had an elbow mentality in the workplace.
Her elbow mentality prevented her from collaborating with others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutthroat - ruthlessly competitive or aggressive
  • Antonym: team spirit - willingness to cooperate and work as a team
