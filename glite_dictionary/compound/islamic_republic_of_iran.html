Islamic Republic of Iran

1. PROPN

/ ɪzˈlɑmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɪˈrɑn / (AmE), / ɪzˈlɑːmɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɪˈrɑːn / (BrE)
the official name for the country of Iran, a theocratic Islamic republic in western Asia, formerly known as Persia until 1935
official name of Iran (country)

Examples

The government of the Islamic Republic of Iran has issued a new travel advisory for foreign visitors.
The Islamic Republic of Iran is known for its rich cultural heritage and oil resources.
Many international negotiations involve officials from the Islamic Republic of Iran.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iran - the country commonly referred to as Iran
  • Synonym: Persia - former name for the country now known as Iran
