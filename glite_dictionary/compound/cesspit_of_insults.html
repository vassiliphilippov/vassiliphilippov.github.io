cesspit of insults

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛsˌpɪt əv ɪnˈsʌlts / (AmE), / ˈsɛs.pɪt əv ɪnˈsʌlts / (BrE)
a situation or place full of offensive or abusive remarks
situation filled with insults

Examples

The online forum turned into a cesspit of insults after the moderators left.
Political debates can devolve into a cesspit of insults if not kept civil.
Instead of constructive feedback, the meeting became a cesspit of insults.

Synset Examples

The debate quickly became a cesspit of insults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotbed of hostility - a place or situation that encourages hostile behavior
