food noise

1. NOUN

/ fuːd nɔɪz / (AmE), / fuːd nɔɪz / (BrE)
the sounds produced during the preparation or consumption of food, such as chopping, sizzling, or chewing
sounds from food prep or eating

Examples

The food noise from the busy kitchen could be heard throughout the house.
He found comfort in the familiar food noise of family dinners.
Some ASMR videos focus entirely on food noise for relaxation.

Synset Examples

I love the food noise of sizzling pans and clinking plates.
The restaurant was filled with food noise during lunch.
The food noise made me even hungrier at the market.
