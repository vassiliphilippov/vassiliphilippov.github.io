giant conch

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt kɑntʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɪənt kɒntʃ / (BrE)
a large species or variety of conch, a marine mollusc with a spiral shell
large type of conch shell

Examples

The giant conch is prized for both its meat and its beautiful shell.
Divers occasionally spot a giant conch resting among the coral reefs.
The shells of the giant conch can grow to impressive sizes, making them popular with collectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queen conch - a species of large conch, known for its pink shell
