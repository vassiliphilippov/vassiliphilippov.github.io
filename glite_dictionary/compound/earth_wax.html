earth wax

1. NOUN

/ ɜrθ wæks / (AmE), / ɜːθ wæks / (BrE)
a naturally occurring, waxy mineral mixture of hydrocarbons, found with petroleum and used in making ceresin and candles
a waxy mineral found with petroleum

Examples

The miners discovered a large amount of earth wax near the oil wells.
Some varieties of earth wax are ideal for making ceresin and candles.
The laboratory tested the chemical composition of the earth wax sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ozocerite - a naturally occurring mineral wax, synonym of earth wax
  • Degree Higher: ceresin - substance made from refined earth wax
