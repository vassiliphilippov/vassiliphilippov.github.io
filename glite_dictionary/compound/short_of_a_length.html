short of a length

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt əv ə lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ʃɔːt əv ə lɛŋθ / (BrE)
Not reaching the required or intended length or distance.
not meeting required length

Examples

The bridge construction was short of a length by just a few meters.
Her essay was short of a length and didn't meet the minimum word count.
The cable turned out to be short of a length for connecting both devices.

Synset Examples

The report was short of a length needed for submission.
He arrived short of a length for the race.
The fabric was short of a length for my project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insufficient - not enough; lacking in quantity or quality
  • Antonym: adequate - sufficient for a specific requirement
