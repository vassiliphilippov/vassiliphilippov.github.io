vervain family

1. NOUN

/ vɚˈveɪn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / vəˈveɪn ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical herbs, shrubs, and trees known as Verbenaceae, found mainly in the New World
the plant family Verbenaceae

Examples

Several species in the vervain family are prized for their medicinal properties.
The vervain family includes both shrubs and flowering herbs found throughout the Americas.
Gardeners often confuse members of the vervain family with those of the mint family due to similar appearances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Verbenaceae - the scientific name for the vervain family of plants
  • Degree Lower: mint family - a related plant family, the Lamiaceae

Etymology

From 'vervain', a common name for plants in the genus Verbena, plus 'family' in the biological sense.
