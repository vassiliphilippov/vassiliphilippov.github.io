elbow room

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlboʊ ˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊ ˌruːm / (BrE)
enough space to move or act freely, without feeling crowded or restricted
space for free movement

Examples

After rearranging the furniture, there was finally enough elbow room in the living room.
The hiking trail was so crowded that we barely had any elbow room to walk.
She prefers working in a large office where there's plenty of elbow room for everyone.

Synset Examples

room to pass
make way for
hardly enough elbow room to turn around

Related Terms

  • Synonym: space - available area, especially in a physical context
