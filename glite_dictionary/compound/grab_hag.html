grab hag

1. NOUN

/ ɡræb hæɡ / (AmE), / ɡræb hæɡ / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who aggressively seeks to take more than their fair share of attention or possessions, often in a greedy or pushy way
greedy or attention-seeking person (esp. woman)
informal

Examples

Every Halloween, she transforms into a classic grab hag.
The show poked fun at the office grab hag who always takes the last donut.
Some called her a grab hag because she dominated every conversation at the party.

Synset Examples

Every Halloween, she transforms into a classic grab hag.

The movie featured a comical grab hag character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greedy - having an intense or selfish desire for something
  • Antonym: generous - showing a readiness to give more than is necessary or expected
