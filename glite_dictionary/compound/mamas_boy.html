mama's boy

1. NOUN

/ məˈmɑz bɔɪ / (AmE), / məˈmɑːz bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or man who is excessively attached to his mother and typically perceived as lacking independence or masculine interests
boy overly attached to his mother

Examples

Everyone teased him for being a mama's boy because he called his mother several times a day.
Some people consider a mama's boy to be someone who always seeks his mother's approval before making decisions.
Although he was often labeled a mama's boy, he was proud of his close relationship with his mother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mummy's boy - boy excessively attached to his mother (British equivalent)
  • Antonym: daddy's girl - girl excessively attached to her father

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːməz bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməz bɔɪ / (BrE)
an adult man who is excessively attached to or influenced by his mother
man overly attached to his mother

Examples

Everyone knew he was a mama's boy, always running home for advice on everything.
She broke up with him because she didn't want to date a mama's boy.
His friends teased him for being a mama's boy after he called his mother three times a day.

Synset Examples

Her boyfriend is a total mama's boy, never leaves her side.
Being a mama's boy has its perks, he gets spoiled.
He's such a mama's boy, always seeking her advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother's boy - a man excessively attached to his mother
