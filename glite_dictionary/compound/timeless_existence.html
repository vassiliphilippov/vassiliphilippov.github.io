timeless existence

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmləs ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmləs ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
a state of eternal existence without beginning or end, especially as believed in some religions to characterize the afterlife
eternal existence; the afterlife (religious)

Examples

Many religions promise a timeless existence to those who lead a virtuous life.
The philosopher wrote about the possibility of timeless existence beyond our mortal world.
Some believe that achieving enlightenment results in timeless existence free from the cycle of birth and death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternity - infinite or unending time
  • Antonym: mortality - the state of being subject to death
