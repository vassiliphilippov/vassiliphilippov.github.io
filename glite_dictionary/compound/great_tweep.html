great tweep

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt twip / (AmE), / ɡreɪt twiːp / (BrE)
an especially good or friendly follower or user on Twitter
valued Twitter follower or user
informal

Examples

She's always supportive and positive online; she's truly a great tweep.
After I posted my story, a great tweep helped it go viral by sharing it widely.
It's nice to connect with a great tweep who understands your interests.

Synset Examples

I followed her on Twitter; she's a great tweep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tweep - a person who uses Twitter

Etymology

Blend of 'Twitter' and 'peep' (person), with 'great' as an intensifier.
