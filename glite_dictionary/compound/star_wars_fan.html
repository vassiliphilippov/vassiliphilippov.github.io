Star Wars fan

1. NOUN

/ stɑːr wɔrz fæn / (AmE), / stɑː wɔːz fan / (BrE)
a person who is very enthusiastic about the Star Wars films, books, or related media
enthusiast of the Star Wars franchise

Examples

He became a Star Wars fan after watching the original trilogy as a child.
Many Star Wars fans collect action figures and memorabilia from the movies.
The convention was filled with passionate Star Wars fans dressed as their favorite characters.

Synset Examples

Darth is a popular character among Star Wars fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who strongly admires a particular person, group, or thing
