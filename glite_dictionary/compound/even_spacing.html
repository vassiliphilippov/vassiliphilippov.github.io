even spacing

1. NOUN

/ ˈivən ˈspeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ˈspeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement of things so that the spaces between them are equal or regular
regularly equal arrangement of space

Examples

The architect recommended even spacing of the pillars for structural balance.
For the best results, maintain even spacing between the rows of plants in your garden.
Careful measurement ensures even spacing of each letter in the signage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform spacing - spacing arranged to be the same between items
