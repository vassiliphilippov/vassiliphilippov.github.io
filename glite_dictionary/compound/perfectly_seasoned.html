perfectly seasoned

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝː.fɪktli ˈsiː.zənd / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪktli ˈsiː.zənd / (BrE)
having an ideal balance of seasonings or spices, resulting in excellent flavor
with ideal flavor balance

Examples

The chef presented a perfectly seasoned steak with a side of roasted vegetables.
Her homemade soup was rich, hearty, and perfectly seasoned for the chilly evening.
Everyone praised the perfectly seasoned salad dressing at the dinner party.

Synset Examples

The dish was perfectly seasoned and incredibly savory.
