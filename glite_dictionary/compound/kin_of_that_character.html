kin of that character

1. NOUN

/ kɪn əv ðæt ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / kɪn əv ðæt ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
A person who feels a strong personal or emotional connection to a particular fictional character, often identifying with that character.
someone who strongly identifies with a fictional character
slang

Examples

She considers herself the kin of that character from her favorite anime series.
In fan communities, being the kin of that character often leads to close bonds with others.
He explained that he feels like the kin of that character, relating strongly to their story.

Synset Examples

He proudly identifies as a kinnie of that character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kinnie - someone who identifies closely with a fictional character

Etymology

From the word 'kin' (family, relation), used in fandom and online communities to describe a connection with a character.
