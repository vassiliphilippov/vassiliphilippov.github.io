west by north

1. NOUN

/ wɛst baɪ nɔrθ / (AmE), / wɛst baɪ nɔːθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point (11°15') north of due west; corresponding to 281°15' on a compass.
compass point between west and west-northwest

Examples

The ship altered its course to west by north to avoid the reef.
He set the heading at west by north as instructed by the navigator.
The weather vane pointed west by north during the afternoon storm.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: west - the direction toward the point of the horizon where the sun sets; 270° on a compass
  • Degree Higher: west-northwest - the compass point between west and northwest
