favorite subject

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvɚɪt ˈsʌbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈsʌbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
the academic course or area of study that a person likes most
most liked academic course

Examples

Mathematics was always my favorite subject in high school.
Whenever someone asks about her favorite subject, she immediately says chemistry.
After taking several courses, he decided that history was his favorite subject.

Synset Examples

Her favorite subject in school was geography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subject - an area of knowledge studied in school or college
