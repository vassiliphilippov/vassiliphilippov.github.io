federal deficit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl ˈdɛfəsət / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl ˈdɛfɪsɪt / (BrE)
The amount by which a government's annual spending exceeds its revenue.
government's annual budget shortfall

Examples

The federal deficit increased dramatically during the economic crisis.
Debates about how to reduce the federal deficit often dominate political campaigns.
Experts warn that a rising federal deficit may lead to higher interest rates.

Synset Examples

federal deficits can cause inflation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budget deficit - amount by which spending exceeds revenue in any budget
