strip-Jack-naked

1. NOUN

/ strɪp dʒæk ˈneɪkəd / (AmE), / strɪp dʒæk ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
A simple card game for two players in which the aim is to win all of the other player's cards.
two-player card game

Examples

The children played strip-Jack-naked for hours on a rainy afternoon.
He taught his younger sister how to play strip-Jack-naked with a deck of cards.
Strip-Jack-naked is an easy way to introduce kids to the rules of card games.
