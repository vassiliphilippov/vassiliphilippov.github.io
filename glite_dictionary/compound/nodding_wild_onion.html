nodding wild onion

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑdɪŋ waɪld ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈnɒdɪŋ waɪld ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
a North American wild onion (Allium cernuum) with nodding clusters of white to pink flowers
wild onion species with nodding flowers

Examples

The nodding wild onion blooms in midsummer on rocky hillsides.
Gardeners often plant nodding wild onion for its attractive drooping flowers.
Hikers spotted clusters of nodding wild onion along the woodland trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild onion - any wild species of the onion genus (Allium)
