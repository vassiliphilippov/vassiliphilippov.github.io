pig bed

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡ bɛd / (AmE), / pɪɡ bɛd / (BrE)
A bed of sand used as a mold in which pig iron is cast.
sand bed mold for pig iron casting

Examples

After the blast furnace run, the molten iron was poured into the pig bed to cool and solidify.
Traditional foundries often use a pig bed to shape and collect newly formed pig iron.
The shape of the pig bed channels the molten metal into manageable ingots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pig iron - crude iron as first obtained from a furnace
