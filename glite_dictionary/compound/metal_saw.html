metal saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬l sɔ / (AmE), / ˈmetl sɔː / (BrE)
a saw designed for cutting metal, typically operated with one hand
hand-held saw for cutting metal

Examples

He used a metal saw to cut through the rusty pipe.
The toolbox had a hammer, a wrench, and a metal saw for various repairs.
Always wear safety goggles when working with a metal saw to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hacksaw - a fine-toothed saw used especially for cutting metal
