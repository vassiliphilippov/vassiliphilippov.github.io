orange juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
the juice obtained from oranges, either freshly squeezed or bottled, typically consumed as a beverage
juice made from oranges

Examples

She prefers to drink a glass of orange juice with her breakfast every morning.
Could you buy some orange juice when you go to the store?
Freshly squeezed orange juice is much tastier than the bottled version.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juice - liquid extracted from fruits or vegetables
