Lake Tana

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ˈtɑːnə / (AmE), / leɪk ˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
the largest lake in Ethiopia, located in the north of the country and forming the main source of the Blue Nile
largest lake in Ethiopia; source of Blue Nile

Examples

Many tourists visit Lake Tana to explore its historic monasteries and enjoy its scenic beauty.
The Blue Nile begins its long journey to Sudan from Lake Tana in Ethiopia.
Environmentalists are working to protect the unique ecosystem around Lake Tana.
