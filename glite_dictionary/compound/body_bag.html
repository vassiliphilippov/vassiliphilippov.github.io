body bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi bæɡ / (BrE)
a large, sealed bag used for transporting a dead body, especially in military or emergency situations
bag for transporting a corpse

Examples

After the battle, each fallen soldier was placed in a body bag for transport to the morgue.
Emergency workers quickly filled a body bag after recovering the victim from the disaster site.
He recognized the body bag on the stretcher and knew it signaled another casualty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffin - a box in which a dead body is buried or cremated
