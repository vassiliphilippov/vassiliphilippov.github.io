track and field

1. NOUN

/ træk ənd fiːld / (AmE), / træk ənd fiːld / (BrE)
athletic sports that are performed on a running track or on the field, such as running, jumping, and throwing events
athletic sports on track or field

Examples

She excelled in track and field throughout her high school years.
Many universities offer scholarships for outstanding track and field athletes.
The Olympic Games feature a wide range of track and field events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletics - sports involving running, jumping, and throwing events
