for tea

1. ADP

/ fər ti / (AmE), / fə ˈtiː / (BrE)
to take part in tea as a light meal or social occasion, especially in the afternoon or early evening
to have tea as a meal or event
british

Examples

We're going to Aunt May's for tea after school.
Tom invited his friends over for tea on Saturday.
Do you want to join us for tea this evening?

Synset Examples

Every Sunday, I visit my granny for tea.
