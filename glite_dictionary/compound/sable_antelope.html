sable antelope

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪbəl ˈænˌtoʊp / (AmE), / ˈseɪbəl ˈantɪləʊp / (BrE)
a large African antelope (Hippotragus niger) with a characteristic dark coat and long, backward-curving horns
large African antelope with curved horns

Examples

The sable antelope is known for its striking black coat and impressive horns.
Wildlife photographers travel to Africa to capture images of the elusive sable antelope.
A group of sable antelopes grazed peacefully in the savanna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antelope - a swift-running deer-like animal native to Africa and Asia
