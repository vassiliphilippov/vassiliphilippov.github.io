overall look

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvɚˌɔl lʊk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɔːl lʊk / (BrE)
the general appearance or visual impression of something as a whole
general appearance or impression

Examples

The renovation completely changed the overall look of the house.
She wanted her wedding dress to match the overall look of the ceremony.
Using too many fonts can disrupt the overall look of your presentation.

Synset Examples

Keming can ruin the overall look of a design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the way that someone or something looks
  • Synonym: impression - the overall effect produced by something
