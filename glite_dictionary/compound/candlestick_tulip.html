candlestick tulip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.dəlˌstɪk ˈtu.lɪp / (AmE), / ˈkæn.dəl.stɪk ˈtjuː.lɪp / (BrE)
A Eurasian tulip species with small flowers that are marked with blotches at the base.
species of tulip with blotched flowers

Examples

The candlestick tulip is prized by gardeners for its unique flower patterns.
We planted several candlestick tulips along the front border of our garden.
Many Eurasian meadows are dotted with the colorful blossoms of the candlestick tulip.
