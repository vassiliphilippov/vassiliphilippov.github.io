fruiting body

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːtɪŋ ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈfruːtɪŋ ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
a specialized structure in fungi and some plants that produces and releases spores
spore-producing organ in fungi or plants

Examples

The mushroom is the fruiting body of a fungus, emerging above ground when it's ready to release spores.
Some lichens develop a visible fruiting body that helps them reproduce.
Scientists studied the fruiting body to understand how different fungi disperse their spores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sporocarp - structure in fungi or ferns that produces spores
