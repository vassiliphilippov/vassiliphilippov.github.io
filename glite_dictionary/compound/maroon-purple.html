maroon-purple

1. ADJ

/ məˈɹun ˈpɝpəl / (AmE), / məˈruːn ˈpɜːpl̩ / (BrE)
A shade of purple that is tinged with maroon or has a reddish-brown hue.
a purple color with maroon tint

Examples

She wore a beautiful dress in maroon-purple to the gala last night.
The artist mixed paints until he achieved exactly the maroon-purple shade he wanted for the sunset.
Their living room was decorated with cushions in maroon-purple and gold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
  • Synonym: maroon - a dark brownish-red color
