blue orchid

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / bluː ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a notable orchid found in northern India, known for its large pale to deep lilac-blue flowers
orchid with blue or lilac-blue flowers

Examples

The blue orchid is admired for its striking lilac-blue petals.
In spring, the blue orchid blooms abundantly across the northern Indian hills.
Botanists traveled to study the rare blue orchid in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: orchid - a plant of the family Orchidaceae, often with showy flowers
