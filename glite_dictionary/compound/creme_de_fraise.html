creme de fraise

1. NOUN

/ krɛm də ˈfrɛz / (AmE), / krɛm də ˈfrɛz / (BrE)
A sweet, strawberry-flavored liqueur used in cocktails and desserts.
strawberry-flavored liqueur

Examples

He added a splash of creme de fraise to the cocktail for a fruity twist.
The dessert was topped with creme de fraise for extra flavor.
Do you have any creme de fraise to mix in this strawberry martini?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creme de cassis - blackcurrant-flavored liqueur

Etymology

From French 'crème de fraise', meaning 'cream of strawberry'.
