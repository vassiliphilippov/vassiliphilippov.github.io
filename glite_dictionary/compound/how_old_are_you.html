how old are you

1. X

/ haʊ oʊld ɑr ju / (AmE), / haʊ əʊld ɑː juː / (BrE)
Used to ask someone about their age.
question to ask someone's age

Examples

At the doctor's office, the nurse asked, 'How old are you?' before taking my details.
Children are often curious and will readily ask, 'How old are you?'
When making a profile online, you may be prompted with the question 'How old are you?'.

Synset Examples

When meeting her for the first time, I asked, 'How old are you?'
She laughed and replied, 'How old are you to ask?'
