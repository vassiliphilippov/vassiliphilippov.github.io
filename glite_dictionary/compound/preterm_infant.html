preterm infant

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːˌtɝːm ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌtɜːm ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
an infant born before 37 weeks of gestation
infant born before 37 weeks
medical

Examples

The neonatal intensive care unit specializes in caring for preterm infants with complex medical needs.
Mother and doctors worried about the possible complications faced by her preterm infant.
Advances in medicine have increased the survival rates for preterm infants significantly over the last few decades.

Related Terms
