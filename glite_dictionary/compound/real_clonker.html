real clonker

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈklɑːŋkər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈklɒŋkə / (BrE)
an old or poorly performing vehicle, especially a car that is unreliable or in bad condition
old, unreliable car; poor vehicle
informal slang

Examples

After a few years, his pride and joy became a real clonker that hardly ever started.
We ended up buying a real clonker because it was all we could afford at the time.
That rusty van is such a real clonker that it broke down twice last week.

Synset Examples

That old car is a real clonker on the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clunker - an old or unreliable vehicle, especially a car

Etymology

Variant of 'clunker', likely from 'clunk' (imitative of a heavy, dull sound) + -er suffix.
