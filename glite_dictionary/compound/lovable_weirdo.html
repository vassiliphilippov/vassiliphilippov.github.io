lovable weirdo

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvəbəl ˈwɪrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvəbəl ˈwɪədəʊ / (BrE)
a person whose odd or eccentric behavior is considered charming or endearing
endearingly strange person
informal

Examples

Everyone in the office adores Max, the lovable weirdo who wears mismatched socks every day.
She's always been a lovable weirdo with her collection of vintage hats and quirky jokes.
Despite being a lovable weirdo, Tom is the heart of our group.

Synset Examples

He’s a lovable weirdo who always makes us laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
