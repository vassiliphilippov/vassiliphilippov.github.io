title page

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪt̬l̩ ˌpeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtl̩ ˌpeɪdʒ / (BrE)
the page at the beginning of a book that shows the title, author, and publisher
page in a book with title, author, publisher

Examples

The title page of the book was beautifully illustrated.
Please write your name on the title page before submitting the report.
On the title page, the publisher's logo is printed at the bottom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover page - the page that appears at the very front of a report or document, often similar in function to a title page
