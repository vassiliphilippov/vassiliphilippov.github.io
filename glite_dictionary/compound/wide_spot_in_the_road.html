wide spot in the road

1. NOUN

/ waɪd spɑt ɪn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / waɪd spɒt ɪn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
a place along a road where the road briefly widens, allowing vehicles to pull over or stop temporarily
a place beside a road to stop

Examples

We pulled over at a wide spot in the road to check the map.
A wide spot in the road provided a safe place to change the flat tire.
They chose a wide spot in the road to take photographs of the scenic view.

Synset Examples

We stopped at a wide spot in the road to stretch.
