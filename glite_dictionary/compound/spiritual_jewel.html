Spiritual Jewel

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl ˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtʃuːəl ˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
A member of the Taoist Trinity, identified with Lao-tse, regarded as a fundamental deity in Taoist cosmology.
a Taoist deity, member of Taoist Trinity

Examples

The temple was dedicated to the Spiritual Jewel, reflecting its central role in Taoist worship.
Scholars often debate the characteristics and symbolism attributed to the Spiritual Jewel within the Taoist Trinity.
Many Taoist scriptures mention the Spiritual Jewel as an embodiment of original energy and wisdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taoist Trinity - the triad of supreme deities in Taoism
