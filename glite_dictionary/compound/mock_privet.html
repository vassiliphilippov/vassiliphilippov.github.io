mock privet

1. NOUN

/ mɑk ˈprɪvət / (AmE), / mɒk ˈprɪvɪt / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub with white flowers and fruits resembling olives, often grown as an ornamental plant
evergreen shrub with white flowers and olive-like fruit

Examples

The hedge was planted with mock privet for its dense foliage and delicate white flowers.
Gardeners often choose mock privet as a hardy, decorative shrub for urban landscapes.
After the mock privet bloomed, small, olive-like fruits appeared on the branches.
