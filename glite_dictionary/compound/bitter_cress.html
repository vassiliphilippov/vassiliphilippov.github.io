bitter cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪt̬ɚ ˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə ˌkrɛs / (BrE)
any of several herbaceous plants of the genus Cardamine, typically with divided leaves and small white, pink or purple flowers, found worldwide except Antarctica.
herbaceous plant of the genus Cardamine

Examples

The garden was full of bitter cress blooming in early spring.
Weeds like bitter cress can quickly spread across a lawn if not controlled.
After the rain, patches of bitter cress appeared among the vegetables.
