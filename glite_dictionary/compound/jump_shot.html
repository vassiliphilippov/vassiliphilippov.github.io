jump shot

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌmp ʃɑt / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ʃɒt / (BrE)
A basketball shot in which the player jumps and releases the ball at the highest point of the jump.
basketball shot released at jump's peak

Examples

He scored the winning basket with a jump shot right before the buzzer.
Her jump shot is so smooth that defenders struggle to block it.
The coach spent extra time helping players perfect their jump shot technique.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: layup - a basketball shot made close to the basket, usually by banking the ball off the backboard
  • Degree Higher: three-pointer - a basketball shot made from beyond the three-point line
