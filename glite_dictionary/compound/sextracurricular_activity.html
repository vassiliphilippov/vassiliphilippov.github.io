sextracurricular activity

1. NOUN

/ sɛkstrəˌkɜrɪˈkjʊlər ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / sɛkstrəˌkjuːrɪˈkjʊlə ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
sexual or intimate activities pursued outside of schoolwork, including events or interests related to sexuality
sexual activity outside schoolwork
informal
