genus Thlaspi

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈθlæspi / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈθlaspi / (BrE)
a small genus of herbaceous plants of temperate regions, including pennycress, characterized by their rounded seedpods
plant genus including pennycress

Examples

Botanists have recently reclassified several species formerly included in genus Thlaspi.
The distinctive seedpods are a common feature of plants in genus Thlaspi.
Many farmers are familiar with genus Thlaspi because of the widespread pennycress in their fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pennycress - a common name for plants in the genus Thlaspi, especially Thlaspi arvense

Etymology

From New Latin Thlaspi, from Ancient Greek θλάσπι (thláspi), an old name for pennycress.
