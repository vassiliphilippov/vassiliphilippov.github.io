bog aster

1. NOUN

/ bɑɡ ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / bɒɡ ˈæstə / (BrE)
a North American plant of the aster family (Oclemena nemoralis), typically found in bogs and wet areas, with pale purple or white ray florets.
a wildflower found in bogs (Oclemena nemoralis)

Examples

We spotted a rare bog aster while hiking through the wetlands.
The bog aster blooms in late summer, dotting the marsh with soft purple flowers.
Conservationists are working to protect the natural habitats of the bog aster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the aster family with star-shaped flowers
