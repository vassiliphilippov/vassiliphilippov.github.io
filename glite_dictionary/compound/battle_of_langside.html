battle of Langside

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˈlæŋsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈlæŋsaɪd / (BrE)
a battle fought in 1568 near Glasgow in which Catholic forces supporting Mary Queen of Scots were defeated by Protestant forces
Scottish battle (1568), Mary Queen of Scots' defeat

Examples

The battle of Langside marked a turning point in Mary Queen of Scots' struggle for power.
Historians often debate the military strategies used at the battle of Langside.
After her defeat at the battle of Langside, Mary fled south into England.
