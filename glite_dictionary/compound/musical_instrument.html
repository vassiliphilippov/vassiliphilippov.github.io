musical instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
An object or device used for producing musical sounds, such as a piano, guitar, or violin.
device for producing musical sounds

Examples

She learned to play a new musical instrument every year while growing up.
The guitar is perhaps the most popular musical instrument among teenagers worldwide.
An orchestra brings together many different musical instruments to create a harmonious sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instrument - tool or device for a particular purpose, especially for producing music
