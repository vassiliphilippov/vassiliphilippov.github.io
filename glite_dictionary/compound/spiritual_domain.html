spiritual domain

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl doʊˈmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl dəʊˈmeɪn / (BrE)
a belief in a realm or sphere that is governed or influenced by a divine spirit or supernatural power
realm believed controlled by a divine spirit

Examples

Many religions teach that a spiritual domain exists beyond the material world.
The ceremony was believed to connect participants with the spiritual domain.
Some philosophers argue that the spiritual domain cannot be studied by science alone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiritual world - a nonphysical world or reality considered to exist beyond the physical world
