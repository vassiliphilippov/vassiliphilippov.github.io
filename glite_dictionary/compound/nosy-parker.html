nosy-parker

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊzi ˈpɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzi ˈpɑːkə / (BrE)
a person who is overly interested in other people's private affairs; a meddler.
meddlesome person
informal british

Examples

My neighbor is a real nosy-parker who always asks about my personal life.
Try not to be such a nosy-parker; let people have their privacy.
Nobody likes a nosy-parker poking around for gossip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meddler - someone who interferes in other people's business
  • Synonym: busybody - a person who is too interested in other people's private matters

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from the surname Parker, used as a generic name, combined with 'nosy' (overly inquisitive).
