rum baba

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌm ˌbɑbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌm ˌbɑːbə / (BrE)
A small, rich yeast cake soaked in rum-flavored syrup, often served with whipped cream or fruit.
yeast cake soaked in rum syrup

Examples

At the end of the meal, we shared a delicious rum baba topped with whipped cream.
The pâtissier specializes in French pastries, especially the rum baba.
My grandmother used to bake a rum baba every holiday for the whole family.

Etymology

From French 'baba au rhum,' with 'baba' meaning 'cake' and 'rhum' meaning 'rum'.
