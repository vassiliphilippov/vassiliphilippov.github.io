cruel mistress

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːəl ˈmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈkruːəl ˈmɪstrəs / (BrE)
used metaphorically to describe something, such as time or fate, that is harsh, unforgiving, or unrelenting in its effects
an unforgiving force or influence

Examples

For many artists, fame can be a cruel mistress that brings both adoration and suffering.
Ambition is often described as a cruel mistress that demands everything and gives little in return.
Time proved to be a cruel mistress, stealing his youth without mercy.

Synset Examples

Time is a cruel mistress that waits for no one.
Life, the cruel mistress, often dealt harsh lessons.
She called addiction a cruel mistress that trapped many.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harsh taskmaster - an authority figure or force that is very strict or demanding
