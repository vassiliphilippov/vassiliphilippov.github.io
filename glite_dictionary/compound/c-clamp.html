C-clamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌklæmp / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌklæmp / (BrE)
a metal clamp in the shape of the letter C, typically used for holding pieces of wood or metal together firmly while working on them
a clamp shaped like the letter C

Examples

He used a C-clamp to secure the pieces of wood while the glue dried.
Before welding the metal plates, she fastened them with a C-clamp.
Make sure to position the C-clamp carefully to avoid damaging the surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clamp - a device used to hold objects firmly together

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the letter 'C'.
