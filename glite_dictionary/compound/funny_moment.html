funny moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a time or situation that is amusing or causes laughter
an amusing or humorous incident

Examples

That movie had more than one funny moment that made the audience erupt in laughter.
We always reminisce about the funny moments from our family vacations.
His speech included a funny moment that lightened the mood.

Synset Examples

They shared sleep funny moments from their childhood.
We watched Shrek again for its hilarious moments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilarious moment - a very funny or extremely amusing incident
