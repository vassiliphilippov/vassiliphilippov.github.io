top dog

1. NOUN

/ tɑp dɔg / (AmE), / tɒp dɒɡ / (BrE)
a person who has the highest status, authority, or power in a group or organization
person in charge; leader

Examples

He quickly became the top dog at the company after several years of hard work.
The political campaign turned into a fierce battle to see who would emerge as the top dog.
In the world of tech startups, everyone wants to be the top dog.

Synset Examples

the head of the whole operation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boss - person who is in charge of a group, organization, or workplace
  • Antonym: underdog - a person expected to lose or with lower status in a competition
