closure by compartment

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊʒər baɪ kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊʒə baɪ kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a parliamentary procedure in which debate is limited or ended on specific sections of a bill rather than on the whole measure
parliamentary limit on debate of bill sections
legal

Examples

The speaker announced closure by compartment to expedite the passage of the bill.
Because of closure by compartment, only the finance sections of the bill were discussed in detail.
Opposition members objected to the use of closure by compartment during the legislative session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: closure (parliamentary) - ending or limiting debate in parliament
