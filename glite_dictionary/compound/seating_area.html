seating area

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːtɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈsiːtɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a part of a place where there are seats for several people to sit together
place with seats for people

Examples

The restaurant's seating area quickly filled up during lunchtime.
We reserved a table in the outdoor seating area by the fountain.
The airport has a spacious seating area near each gate.

Synset Examples

there is seating for 40 students in this classroom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seating - furniture or arrangements for people to sit
  • Synonym: waiting area - place where people sit while they wait for something
