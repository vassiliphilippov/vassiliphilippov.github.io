judicial decision

1. NOUN

/ dʒuˈdɪʃəl dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / dʒuˈdɪʃəl dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a ruling or determination made by a court of law on matters brought before it
a court's ruling or determination
legal

Examples

The Supreme Court issued a judicial decision that changed the interpretation of the law.
Every judicial decision sets a precedent for future cases in the legal system.
After years of litigation, the judicial decision finally brought closure to the dispute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgment - a decision pronounced by a court of law
