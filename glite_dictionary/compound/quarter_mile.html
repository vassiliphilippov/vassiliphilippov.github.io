quarter mile

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtɚ ˌmaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə ˌmaɪl / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to one-fourth of a mile, equivalent to 440 yards or approximately 402 meters
a unit of length, 1/4 mile

Examples

The race was a short sprint covering just a quarter mile.
He set a new record for the quarter mile in his electric car.
Their house is only a quarter mile from the nearest bus stop.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mile - a unit of linear measure equal to 1,760 yards (about 1,609 meters)
  • Degree Lower: yard - a unit of length equal to 3 feet (0.9144 meters)
