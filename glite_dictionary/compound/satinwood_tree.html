satinwood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæt.ɪn.wʊd triː / (AmE), / ˈsæt.ɪn.wʊd triː / (BrE)
An East Indian tree valued for its hard, lustrous yellowish wood often used in fine furniture and decorative work.
East Indian tree with valuable yellowish wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from rare satinwood tree, giving it a beautiful golden sheen.
Experts identified the species as a satinwood tree native to South Asia.
Harvesting satinwood tree is strictly regulated to protect endangered forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satinwood - the wood produced by the satinwood tree, noted for its yellowish sheen and used in fine woodworking
