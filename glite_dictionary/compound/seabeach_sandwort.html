seabeach sandwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌbiːtʃ ˈsændˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌbiːtʃ ˈsændˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a perennial succulent herb with small, solitary flowers that grows in coastal sandy areas
succulent coastal herb with small flowers

Examples

The fragile seabeach sandwort thrives in sandy coastal habitats along the Atlantic shoreline.
Conservationists are working to protect the seabeach sandwort from habitat erosion and human disturbance.
Botanists studied the adaptation of seabeach sandwort to salt spray and shifting sands.
