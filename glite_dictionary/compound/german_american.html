German American

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːmən əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
an American born in Germany or whose ancestors were German
an American of German descent

Examples

Many German Americans celebrate Oktoberfest with traditional foods and music.
The city has a vibrant German American community that preserves its heritage.
His grandparents were proud German Americans who immigrated in the early 1900s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: German - a native or inhabitant of Germany
