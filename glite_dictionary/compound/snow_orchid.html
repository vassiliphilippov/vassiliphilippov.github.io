snow orchid

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A waxy, white, nearly leafless plant with clustered stems and racemes of white flowers, native to the northwestern United States, northern California, and Idaho.
a waxy, white, nearly leafless plant of northwestern US

Examples

The snow orchid blooms early in the summer along mountain trails.
Botanists were excited to find a rare snow orchid in Idaho.
The delicate flowers of the snow orchid stood out against the forest floor.
