pocket flap

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt flæp / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt flæp / (BrE)
a piece of fabric that covers the opening of a pocket, usually to protect its contents or for decoration
piece of fabric covering a pocket opening

Examples

The jacket's pocket flap kept my keys from falling out.
She lifted the pocket flap to check for her train ticket.
Military uniforms often feature a buttoned pocket flap for extra security.
