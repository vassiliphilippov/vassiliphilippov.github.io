salad cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæl.əd ˌkrim / (AmE), / ˈsæl.əd ˌkriːm / (BrE)
a smooth, tangy, creamy salad dressing similar to mayonnaise, commonly used in the UK
creamy tangy salad dressing
british

Examples

He spread salad cream on his sandwich instead of mayonnaise.
Many British households keep a bottle of salad cream in the fridge for salads and sandwiches.
The tangy taste of salad cream sets it apart from other dressings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mayonnaise - a thick, creamy sauce made with egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice
