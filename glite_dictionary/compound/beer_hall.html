beer hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr hɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɪə hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room or establishment where beer is served and people often gather for entertainment or social events
establishment serving beer, often with entertainment

Examples

They spent the evening singing songs at the crowded beer hall.
The city is famous for its traditional beer halls that attract tourists and locals alike.
After work, the team gathered in a local beer hall to celebrate their success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pub - a bar or tavern serving alcoholic drinks, especially in Britain
