high tech

1. NOUN

/ haɪ tɛk / (AmE), / haɪ tɛk / (BrE)
Highly advanced technology, especially in electronics or computing.
advanced technological development, especially electronics

Examples

The new smartphone is the latest example of high tech innovation.
Many companies are investing in high tech to stay competitive in the market.
His laboratory is equipped with the most advanced high tech devices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting-edge - at the forefront of technological development; very advanced

Etymology

Shortened form of 'high technology', first used in English in the late 20th century.
