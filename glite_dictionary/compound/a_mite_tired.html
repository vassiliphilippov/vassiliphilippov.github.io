a mite tired

1. ADJ

/ ə maɪt ˈtaɪərd / (AmE), / ə maɪt ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Slightly tired; a little bit fatigued.
slightly tired
informal

Examples

After hiking all day, I'm a mite tired but still happy.
You look a mite tired; maybe you should take a break.
She admitted to feeling a mite tired after the long journey.

Synset Examples

I feel a mite tired after that long meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slightly tired - a little tired
  • Degree Higher: exhausted - extremely tired
  • Antonym: wide awake - fully alert, not tired

Etymology

From 'mite' (meaning a small amount) plus 'tired.'
