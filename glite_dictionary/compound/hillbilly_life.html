hillbilly life

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪlˌbɪli laɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɪlˌbɪli lʌɪf / (BrE)
The way of life, culture, and everyday experiences of people living in rural, traditionally Appalachian or Southern regions, often portrayed in a stereotypical manner.
lifestyle of rural Appalachian people

Examples

The documentary offered an honest look at hillbilly life in Appalachia.
Some writers romanticize hillbilly life, while others satirize it.
Tourists were fascinated by the traditions and music that define hillbilly life.

Synset Examples

The movie portrayed hillbilly life in a humorous way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hillbilly - an unsophisticated person from a rural, mountainous area, especially in the southern US

Etymology

From American English "hillbilly" (country person from a rural backwoods area), likely from hill + Billy (a generic given name).
