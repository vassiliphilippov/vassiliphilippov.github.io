financial support

1. NOUN

/ faɪˈnænʃəl səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
money or other financial resources given to help make something possible, such as a project, activity, or organization
money provided to help something happen

Examples

Our research was made possible with financial support from the government.
Without financial support, many students would not be able to attend university.
The charity relies on financial support from the public to continue its work.

Synset Examples

the foundation provided support for the experiment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funding - providing money for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: sponsorship - financial backing for an activity or organization
