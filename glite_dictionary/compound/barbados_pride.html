Barbados pride

1. NOUN

/ bɑːrˈbeɪdoʊz praɪd / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdɒs praɪd / (BrE)
an East Indian tree (Caesalpinia pulcherrima) with racemes of yellow-white flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant
ornamental tree with yellow-white flowers

Examples

The Barbados pride blooms beautifully in the garden each spring.
Gardeners prize Barbados pride for its clusters of vibrant flowers.
Several Barbados pride trees were planted along the main avenue for decoration.
