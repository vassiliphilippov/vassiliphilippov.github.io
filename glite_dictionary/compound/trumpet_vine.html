trumpet vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpət vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt vaɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen climbing plant native to North America, with showy coral-red or orange trumpet-shaped flowers
North American climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers

Examples

The trumpet vine climbed up the side of the house, covering it in bright orange blooms.
Gardeners often choose the trumpet vine to attract hummingbirds with its vibrant flowers.
Despite its beauty, the trumpet vine can become invasive if not properly controlled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeysuckle - a genus of shrubs and vines known for their fragrant flowers
