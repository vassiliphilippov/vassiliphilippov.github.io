humpy walk

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmpi wɔk / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpi wɔːk / (BrE)
an awkward or irregular manner of walking, usually characterized by uneven or jerky movements
awkward, irregular way of walking

Examples

His humpy walk made him stand out in the crowd.
After his injury, he developed a noticeable humpy walk.
People whispered about the reason for her humpy walk.

Synset Examples

His humpy walk made everyone curious about his health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limp - a tendency to walk with difficulty, typically from a damaged leg or foot
