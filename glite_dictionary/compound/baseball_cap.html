baseball cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl kæp / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl kap / (BrE)
a soft cap with a rounded crown and a stiff projecting bill at the front, typically worn as casual wear or for sports
casual cap with a bill

Examples

He wore his favorite baseball cap backward at the party.
A baseball cap is often part of a team's uniform.
She adjusted her baseball cap to block the sun from her eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cap - a kind of soft, flat hat without a brim
