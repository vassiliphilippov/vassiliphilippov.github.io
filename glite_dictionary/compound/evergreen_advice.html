evergreen advice

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvɚˌɡrin ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˌɡriːn ədˈvʌɪs / (BrE)
advice that remains relevant and useful regardless of changing circumstances or time.
timeless, always relevant advice

Examples

Her evergreen advice about saving money still applies no matter how the economy changes.
Teachers often share evergreen advice that guides their students throughout life.
One piece of evergreen advice is to treat others the way you wish to be treated.

Synset Examples

Her advice was evergreen and applicable at any time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timeless advice - advice that is always relevant and does not become outdated

Etymology

From 'evergreen', meaning always fresh or enduring, and 'advice'.
