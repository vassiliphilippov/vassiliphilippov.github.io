mamma's boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːməz bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmaməz bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or man who is excessively attached to his mother and considered to lack masculine independence or interests
a boy overly attached to his mother
informal

Examples

Everyone could tell that Jake was a mamma's boy because he called his mother several times a day.
The other children teased him for being a mamma's boy, but he didn't seem to mind.
As an adult, he struggled to make decisions without his mother and was still called a mamma's boy by his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: momma's boy - alternative spelling of 'mamma's boy'
  • Antonym: daddy's girl - a girl who is particularly close to her father
