Native Hawaiian

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.tɪv həˈwaɪ.i.ən / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv həˈwaɪ.i.ən / (BrE)
A member or descendant of the indigenous Polynesian people who originally inhabited the Hawaiian Islands.
indigenous person of Hawaii

Examples

The museum features traditional art created by Native Hawaiian artisans.
She is proud of her heritage as a Native Hawaiian and shares it with her community.
Many Native Hawaiians are active in preserving their language and customs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Polynesian - a person native to Polynesia, the broader cultural group to which Native Hawaiians belong

Etymology

From 'native' (born in a particular place) + 'Hawaiian' (from Hawaii).
