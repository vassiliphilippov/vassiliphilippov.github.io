fishpond

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ.pɑnd / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ.pɒnd / (BrE)
A pond, typically artificial, used for keeping or breeding fish.
artificial pond for keeping fish

Examples

We built a small fishpond in our backyard to raise koi carp.
The farm has several fishponds used for breeding tilapia.
Children love to feed the goldfish in the old fishpond at the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pond - a small body of still water, often artificial
