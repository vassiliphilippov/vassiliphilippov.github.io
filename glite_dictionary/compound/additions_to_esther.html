Additions to Esther

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɪʃənz tu ˈɛstər / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃənz tʊ ˈɛstə / (BrE)
An Apocryphal book containing supplementary passages added to the Book of Esther, found in some versions of the Old Testament.
supplementary texts added to the Book of Esther

Examples

The Additions to Esther are often included in the Septuagint but omitted from most Protestant Bibles.
Scholars study the Additions to Esther to understand differences between biblical traditions.
He quoted a passage from the Additions to Esther during his lecture on religious texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Book of Esther - a book in the Hebrew Bible telling the story of Esther
