to saw wood

1. VERB

/ sɔ wʊd / (AmE), / sɔː wʊd / (BrE)
to cut timber or wooden boards using a saw
cut timber using a saw

Examples

He learned to saw wood properly as part of his carpentry training.
Workers saw wood in the mill every morning before sunrise.
She spent the afternoon sawing wood for the fireplace.

Synset Examples

He saw wood all day at the lumberyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw - to cut something with a saw tool
