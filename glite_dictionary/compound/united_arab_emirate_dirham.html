United Arab Emirate dirham

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːnaɪtɪd ˌɛrəb ˈɛmɪrət ˈdɪrˌhæm / (AmE), / ˌjuːnaɪtɪd ˌærəb ˈemɪrət ˈdɪəˌræm / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in the United Arab Emirates, equal to 100 fils.
currency unit of United Arab Emirates

Examples

The price of the souvenir was 50 United Arab Emirate dirhams.
She exchanged her dollars for United Arab Emirate dirhams at the airport.
Most shops in Dubai accept payment in United Arab Emirate dirham.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirham - a unit of currency used in several Arab countries

Etymology

From Arabic 'dirham', ultimately from Greek 'drachmē' (a unit of weight and currency).
