death benefit

1. NOUN

/ dɛθ ˈbɛnəfɪt / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
an amount of money paid to the beneficiary of a deceased person under an insurance policy or pension plan
insurance payment to a beneficiary after death

Examples

The death benefit will be paid directly to the policyholder's spouse after their passing.
Many people purchase life insurance to provide a death benefit for their families.
The company increased the death benefit offered to employees as part of its new benefits package.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life insurance - insurance that pays out on the death of the insured person
