native orange

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪtɪv ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪv ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
a small Australian tree that bears edible dark purple fruit, native to certain regions of Australia
Australian tree with edible dark purple fruit

Examples

The native orange is prized by Australian foragers for its tasty berries.
We planted a native orange in our garden, hoping to attract more wildlife.
Aboriginal Australians have traditionally used the native orange as a food source in the wild.
