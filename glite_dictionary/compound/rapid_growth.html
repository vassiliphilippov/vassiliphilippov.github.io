rapid growth

1. NOUN

/ ˈræpɪd ˈɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˈrapɪd ˈɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
an increase in size, quantity, or importance that happens very quickly
very fast increase or expansion

Examples

The company experienced rapid growth after launching its new product line.
Urban areas often face challenges due to rapid growth in population.
The technology sector has seen rapid growth over the last decade.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slow growth - an increase that happens gradually or over a long period
  • Synonym: expansion - the process of becoming larger or more extensive
