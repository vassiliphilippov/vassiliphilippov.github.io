barrier strip

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæriɚ ˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈbærɪə ˌstrɪp / (BrE)
an electrical junction unit used to connect two or more wires securely without the need for plugs or special connectors
electrical junction unit for wire connections

Examples

The electrician installed a barrier strip to connect the wires inside the control panel.
Make sure each wire is securely fastened to the barrier strip to prevent loose connections.
A barrier strip simplifies changing or adding wires to the circuit.
