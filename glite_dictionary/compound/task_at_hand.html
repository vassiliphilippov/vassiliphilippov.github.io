task at hand

1. NOUN

/ tæsk æt hænd / (AmE), / tɑːsk ət hand / (BrE)
the particular job or assignment that is currently being dealt with or needs immediate attention
current task or immediate assignment

Examples

Let's concentrate on the task at hand and avoid distractions.
Despite the chaos, she remained focused on the task at hand.
He always gives his full attention to the task at hand, no matter how difficult it is.

Synset Examples

Stop navel-gazing and focus on the task at hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job - a task or piece of work, especially one that is paid
  • Synonym: assignment - a specific task or piece of work assigned to someone
