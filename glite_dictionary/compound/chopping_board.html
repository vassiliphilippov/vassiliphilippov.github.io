chopping board

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑpɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A flat board, typically made of wood or plastic, used for cutting meat, vegetables, or other food items.
board used to cut food

Examples

She diced the onions on the chopping board before adding them to the pan.
After slicing raw chicken, it's important to wash the chopping board thoroughly.
The kitchen has a large wooden chopping board next to the sink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting board - a board used for cutting food (American English term)
