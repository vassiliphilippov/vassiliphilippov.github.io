green-eyed beauty

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrin ˈaɪd ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈaɪd ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a woman considered beautiful and notable for her striking green eyes
beautiful woman with green eyes

Examples

She was known throughout the town as a green-eyed beauty who turned heads wherever she went.
Many poets have found inspiration in the mysterious gaze of a green-eyed beauty.
The novel's protagonist was a green-eyed beauty with an adventurous spirit.

Synset Examples

He described her as a captivating, green-eyed beauty.
