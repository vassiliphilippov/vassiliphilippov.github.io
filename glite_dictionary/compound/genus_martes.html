genus Martes

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɑːr.tiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɑː.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of carnivorous mammals in the family Mustelidae, including the martens.
genus of mustelids including martens

Examples

The genus Martes includes several species of agile, tree-dwelling carnivores.
Researchers discovered a new species within the genus Martes in the remote forests.
Members of the genus Martes are known for their luxurious fur and solitary habits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marten - any member of the genus Martes, a small weasel-like mammal

Etymology

From Latin 'martes', meaning 'marten'. Used in zoological classification.
