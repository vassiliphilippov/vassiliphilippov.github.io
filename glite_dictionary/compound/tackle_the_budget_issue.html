to tackle the budget issue

1. VERB

/ ˈtækəl ðə ˈbʌdʒɪt ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈtækəl ðə ˈbʌdʒɪt ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
to take action to address a financial problem or concern, especially in an organization or government.
address a financial problem

Examples

The council must tackle the budget issue before planning new projects.
We need to tackle the budget issue head-on if we want our business to survive.
Management is determined to tackle the budget issue in this quarter's review.

Synset Examples

Let's tackle the budget issues in our next meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: address the issue - to deal with or give attention to a problem or matter
  • Synonym: solve the budget issue - to find a solution to a financial problem
