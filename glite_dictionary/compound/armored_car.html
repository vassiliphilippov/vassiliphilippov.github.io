armored car

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmərd kɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd kɑː / (BrE)
a military vehicle on wheels equipped with light armor and typically armed with a machine gun, used for combat or reconnaissance
military vehicle with light armor on wheels

Examples

The convoy was led by an armored car equipped with a mounted machine gun.
During the mission, the soldiers advanced while protected by their armored car.
The enemy's armored car was spotted patrolling the outskirts of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armoured car - British spelling: military vehicle with light armor
  • Degree Higher: tank - heavily armored tracked combat vehicle

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmərd kɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd kɑː / (BrE)
a vehicle with reinforced armor plating, used to securely transport money or valuables
vehicle for secure transport of valuables

Examples

Two guards waited beside the armored car outside the bank.
The museum hired an armored car to deliver the priceless sculpture safely.
Every night, the casino's earnings are driven away in an armored car.

Synset Examples

the paintings were delivered to the museum in an air-conditioned armored car

Related Terms
