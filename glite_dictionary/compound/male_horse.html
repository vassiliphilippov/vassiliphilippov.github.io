male horse

1. NOUN

/ meɪl hɔrs / (AmE), / meɪl hɔːs / (BrE)
an adult male horse, especially one that has not been castrated
adult male horse

Examples

The male horse was separated from the mares to prevent unplanned breeding.
A stallion is a type of male horse that has not been castrated.
Many owners keep their male horses in individual stalls for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stallion - an uncastrated adult male horse
  • Antonym: mare - an adult female horse
  • Antonym: gelding - a castrated male horse
