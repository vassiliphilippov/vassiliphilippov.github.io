American lady crab

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈleɪdi kræb / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈleɪdi kræb / (BrE)
a small, brightly spotted crab found on sandy beaches along the Atlantic coast of the United States
a spotted Atlantic coast crab

Examples

We spotted an American lady crab scurrying across the sand at low tide.
The American lady crab is known for its colorful, spotted shell.
Children collected shells and watched a tiny American lady crab burrow into the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady crab - a crab known for its colorful, spotted shell
