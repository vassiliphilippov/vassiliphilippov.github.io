plot of ground

1. NOUN

/ plɑt əv ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / plɒt əv ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a small area of land, often marked out for the growth of specific plants or vegetation
small area of land for plants

Examples

Each family was given a plot of ground to grow vegetables.
The old gardener tended his plot of ground with great care.
They transformed a neglected plot of ground into a beautiful flowerbed.

Synset Examples

a bean plot
a cabbage patch
a briar patch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patch - a small area of land used for growing plants
