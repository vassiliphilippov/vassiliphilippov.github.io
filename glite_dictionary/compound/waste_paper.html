waste paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪst ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈweɪst ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper that has been discarded after use and is no longer needed.
discarded paper after use

Examples

Please put all your waste paper in the blue recycling bin at the end of the hall.
The office generates a large amount of waste paper every week.
She tore up the old documents and added them to the pile of waste paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recycling - the process of converting waste into reusable material
  • Synonym: scrap paper - paper left over or discarded, especially for use in rough work
