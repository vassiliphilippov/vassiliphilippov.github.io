to make an appearance

1. VERB

/ meɪk ən əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / meɪk ən əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
to attend or show up at an event, especially briefly or ceremoniously
attend or show up at an event

Examples

The mayor will make an appearance at the opening ceremony this afternoon.
It's rare for that actor to make an appearance at a local event.
She always makes sure to make an appearance at important family gatherings.

Synset Examples

He will make an appearance at the charity event.
The celebrity is expected to make an appearance tonight.
She rarely makes an appearance at family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to attend - to be present at an event
  • Synonym: to show up - to arrive or appear at a place, especially unexpectedly or after expected
