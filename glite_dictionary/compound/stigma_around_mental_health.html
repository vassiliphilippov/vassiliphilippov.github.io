stigma around mental health

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪɡmə əˈraʊnd ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈstɪɡmə əˈraʊnd ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ / (BrE)
the social disapproval and discrimination associated with mental health conditions or seeking psychological help
social disapproval of mental health issues

Examples

Many people avoid seeking therapy due to the stigma around mental health.
Campaigns are working hard to reduce the stigma around mental health in the workplace.
Breaking the stigma around mental health encourages more people to get the support they need.

Synset Examples

The stigma around mental health is slowly fading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental health stigma - disapproval or prejudice against people with mental health issues
