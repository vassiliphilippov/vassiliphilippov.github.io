teacher's certificate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtitʃɚz sərˌtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃəz səˌtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document stating that a person is qualified to teach in public schools
official teaching qualification document

Examples

After completing her degree, Sarah applied for a teacher's certificate so she could work in public schools.
Most states require a teacher's certificate for employment in their educational system.
He proudly displayed his teacher's certificate on the wall of his classroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaching certificate - a certificate authorizing someone to teach
