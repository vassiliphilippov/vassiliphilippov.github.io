leisure wear

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːʒər wɛr / (AmE), / ˈlɛʒə wɛə / (BrE)
informal and comfortable clothing designed to be worn while relaxing or during leisure activities
informal clothing for relaxing

Examples

After work, I like to change into leisure wear and unwind at home.
Many shops are expanding their leisure wear collections as demand grows.
She packed mostly leisure wear for her weekend getaway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activewear - clothing designed specifically for sports or physical exercise
  • Antonym: formal wear - clothing designed for formal occasions
