electric sander

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsændər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈsændə / (BrE)
a power tool in which sandpaper is moved at high speed by an electric motor, used for smoothing surfaces such as wood
power tool for sanding surfaces

Examples

Before painting the table, he used an electric sander to smooth the surface.
She bought an electric sander for her woodworking projects.
The electric sander makes finishing large pieces of furniture much easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sander - a tool or machine for smoothing surfaces using sandpaper
