ring-necked snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋ ˌnɛkt ˈsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ ˌnɛkt ˈsneɪk / (BrE)
A small, nonvenomous North American snake of the genus Diadophis, characterized by a yellow or orange band around its neck.
small North American snake with a neck ring

Examples

The ring-necked snake is easily identified by the yellow ring around its neck.
We spotted a ring-necked snake basking in the morning sun near the trail.
Although ring-necked snakes are common in our area, they are rarely seen due to their secretive nature.
