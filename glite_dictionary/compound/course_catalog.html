course catalog

1. NOUN

/ kɔrs ˈkætəlɔg / (AmE), / kɔːs ˈkætəlɒg / (BrE)
a publication listing the courses offered by a college or university, usually with details about requirements, schedules, and course descriptions
list of college or university courses

Examples

You can view the course catalog online to see all the classes available next semester.
The university updates its course catalog every year to reflect changes in the curriculum.
Before deciding on her major, she browsed through the entire course catalog to explore her options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prospectus - publication describing courses and requirements at an educational institution
