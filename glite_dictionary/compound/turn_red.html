to turn red

1. VERB

/ tɝn rɛd / (AmE), / tɜːn rɛd / (BrE)
to become visibly red in the face, especially as a result of embarrassment or shyness
become red in the face from embarrassment

Examples

Whenever someone compliments her, she can't help but turn red.
He turned red with embarrassment after forgetting his lines on stage.
The teacher's question made me turn red in front of the whole class.

Synset Examples

He turns red every time one farts near him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blush - to become red in the face, typically from embarrassment or shame
