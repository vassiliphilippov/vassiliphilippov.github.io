lemon geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən dʒəˈriːniəm / (BrE)
a variety of garden geranium with leaves that have a lemon scent, often grown for its fragrant foliage
geranium with lemon-scented leaves

Examples

The lemon geranium in her garden filled the air with a refreshing citrus scent.
She rubbed the leaves of the lemon geranium to release their distinctive fragrance.
Many gardeners plant lemon geranium for both its beauty and its aromatic foliage.
