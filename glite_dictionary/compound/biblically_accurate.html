biblically accurate

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪblɪkli ˈækjərət / (AmE), / ˈbɪblɪkli ˈækjʊrət / (BrE)
Consistent with the content, themes, or teachings of the Bible.
consistent with the Bible's texts or themes

She was praised for her biblically accurate interpretation of ancient prophecies.
