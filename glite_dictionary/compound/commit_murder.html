to commit murder

1. VERB

/ kəˈmɪt ˈmɜrdɚ / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
to unlawfully kill someone; to carry out the crime of murder.
to unlawfully kill someone

Examples

He was shocked to learn his neighbor had committed murder while abroad.
If you commit murder, you will face serious legal consequences.
The detective believed the suspect had committed murder for financial gain.

Synset Examples

He was accused of committing murder last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - the unlawful killing of a person; homicide
  • Synonym: commit a crime - to do something illegal; to break the law
