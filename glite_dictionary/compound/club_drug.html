club drug

1. NOUN

/ klʌb drʌɡ / (AmE), / klʌb drʌɡ / (BrE)
a controlled substance, such as ecstasy or ketamine, that is commonly used recreationally by young people in nightclubs, dance parties, or raves
recreational drug used at clubs and raves

Examples

Police are cracking down on the sale of club drugs in urban nightlife areas.
Ecstasy is one of the most well-known club drugs used at raves.
Doctors warned about the severe side effects of commonly abused club drugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party drug - a drug used for recreational purposes at parties
