red goatfish

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈɡoʊtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / rɛd ˈɡəʊtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a brightly colored fish with a scarlet body and two yellow to reddish stripes along its sides, found in tropical waters
a bright scarlet fish with yellowish stripes

Examples

We saw a red goatfish swimming near the coral reef during our dive.
The aquarium features several vibrant species, including the red goatfish.
Marine biologists study the behavior of the red goatfish in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatfish - a fish of the family Mullidae with barbels on its chin
