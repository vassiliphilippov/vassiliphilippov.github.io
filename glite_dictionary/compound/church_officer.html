church officer

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝːtʃ ˈɑfɪsər / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person who holds an official position of authority in a church
official in a church

Examples

The church officer was responsible for organizing the annual charity event.
She became a church officer after years of volunteering in the parish.
Every church officer must uphold the values and teachings of their denomination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: church official - a person holding an official post in a church
