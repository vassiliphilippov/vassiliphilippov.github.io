Great Salt Lake

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡreɪt sɔlt leɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt sɔːlt leɪk / (BrE)
a large, shallow saltwater lake located in northwestern Utah, USA, known for its high salinity
large saltwater lake in Utah, USA

Examples

The Great Salt Lake is a major landmark visible from space.
In summer, many visitors come to swim and sail on the Great Salt Lake.
Migratory birds rely on the wetlands surrounding the Great Salt Lake for food and shelter.
