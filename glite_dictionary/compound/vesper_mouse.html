vesper mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛspər maʊs / (AmE), / ˈvɛspə maʊs / (BrE)
An American woodland mouse with white feet and underparts.
a North American woodland mouse

Examples

The vesper mouse is most active during the night, searching the forest floor for seeds.
Researchers are studying the habitat preferences of the vesper mouse in eastern woodlands.
We spotted a vesper mouse near the edge of the field at dusk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white-footed mouse - a woodland mouse with white feet found in North America
