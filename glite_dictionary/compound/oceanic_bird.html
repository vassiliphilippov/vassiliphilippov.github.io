oceanic bird

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈʃænɪk bɝd / (AmE), / əʊˈʃænɪk bɜːd / (BrE)
a bird that lives primarily on the open ocean, far from land
bird that lives on the open sea

Examples

Albatrosses are among the most famous oceanic birds in the world.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of several oceanic birds across the Pacific.
Many oceanic birds spend months at sea without coming to land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seabird - a bird that lives near or on the sea
