open sesame

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpən ˈsesəˌmi / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˈsesəmi / (BrE)
a magical phrase or command that opens a locked door or barrier, especially as in the story of Ali Baba
magical command to unlock or open

Examples

He wished he knew the open sesame that would make his proposal successful.
For the explorers, the discovery of the lost map became their open sesame to hidden riches.
She approached the locked door and whispered open sesame, hoping for a little magic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: password - a secret word or phrase for access or entry

Etymology

From the Arabic folk tale 'Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves', where 'open sesame' is the magical phrase used to open a treasure cave.
