haafu community

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑː.fuː kəˌmjuː.nə.ti / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.fuː kəˌmjuː.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
A group of people in Japan who are of mixed Japanese and non-Japanese heritage.
people of mixed heritage in Japan

Examples

The haafu community in Tokyo often holds cultural exchange events.
Many in the haafu community are working to challenge traditional views of Japanese identity.
The school started a support group aimed at connecting students from the haafu community.

Synset Examples

The haafu community celebrates diversity and inclusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixed-race - Having parents of different races or ethnic backgrounds
  • Synonym: biracial - A person whose parents are of two different races

Etymology

From Japanese ハーフ (haafu, 'half'), used in Japan for people of mixed ethnic background; 'community' from English.
