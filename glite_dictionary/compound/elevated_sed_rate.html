elevated sed rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd sɛd reɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtɪd sɛd reɪt / (BrE)
an increased rate at which red blood cells settle at the bottom of a test tube, often indicating inflammation or disease
increased blood sedimentation rate
medical

Examples

The doctor was concerned when he saw my elevated sed rate in the lab results.
An elevated sed rate can be a sign of infection or chronic inflammation.
Your elevated sed rate might explain some of your ongoing symptoms.

Synset Examples

Her elevated sed rate indicated a possible infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sed rate - rate at which red blood cells settle in a period of one hour
  • Synonym: erythrocyte sedimentation rate - the full term for sed rate, a common blood test
