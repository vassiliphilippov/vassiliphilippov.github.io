bull fiddle

1. NOUN

/ bʊl ˈfɪd.əl / (AmE), / bʊl ˈfɪd.əl / (BrE)
an informal term for the double bass, the largest and lowest-pitched instrument of the violin family
informal term for double bass
informal

Examples

The jazz band featured a bassist expertly playing the bull fiddle in every number.
She learned to play the bull fiddle during college, joining the university orchestra.
The deep tones of his bull fiddle provided the rhythmic foundation for the ensemble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double bass - the largest and lowest-pitched bowed string instrument
