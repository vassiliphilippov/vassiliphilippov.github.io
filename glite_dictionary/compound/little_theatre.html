little theatre

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.əl ˈθi.ə.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈθɪə.tə / (BrE)
a small theater used for experimental drama or by collegiate or community performing groups.
small venue for experimental or community theater

Examples

The local actors performed a new play at the little theatre downtown.
She volunteers backstage at a little theatre specializing in avant-garde productions.
Many famous actors began their careers in little theatres around the country.
