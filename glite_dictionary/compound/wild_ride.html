wild ride

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ɹaɪd / (AmE), / waɪld ɹaɪd / (BrE)
an exciting or thrilling amusement park attraction or experience
exciting or thrilling amusement ride

Examples

The new rollercoaster is a wild ride from start to finish.
She wasn't expecting the haunted house to be such a wild ride.
Hold on tight—this old wooden coaster is a wild ride!

Synset Examples

The carnie told tales of the wild rides.

Related Terms
