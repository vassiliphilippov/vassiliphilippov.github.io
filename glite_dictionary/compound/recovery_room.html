recovery room

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkʌvəri ruːm / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌvəri ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a hospital where patients are closely monitored as they recover from the effects of anesthesia following surgery
hospital room for post-surgery care

Examples

After surgery, the nurse monitored him closely in the recovery room.
Visitors are not usually permitted in the recovery room until the patient is stable.
She woke up feeling groggy in the recovery room after her operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postanesthesia care unit - a hospital area for patients recovering from anesthesia, also called PACU
