rice burner

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪs ˌbɜrnər / (AmE), / ˈraɪs ˌbɜːnə / (BrE)
(slang, sometimes offensive) A small, efficient, or inexpensive car, especially one of East Asian make.
slang for small, efficient car (esp. Asian make)
slang offensive

Examples

Many car enthusiasts modify their rice burner with custom exhausts and spoilers.
He proudly showed off his new rice burner to his friends in the parking lot.
Despite its size, that old rice burner is surprisingly fast and reliable.

Synset Examples

She drives a rice burner that’s very fuel-efficient.
The rice burner zoomed past us on the highway.
His new car is a rice burner, great for speed.

Etymology

Slang, alluding to rice as a staple food in East Asia, used to refer to Asian-made cars.
