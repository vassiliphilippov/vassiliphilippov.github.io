raisin moth

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzən mɔθ / (AmE), / ˈreɪzɪn mɒθ / (BrE)
a small moth (Cadra figulilella) whose larvae infest and damage dried fruits and cereal products
moth pest of dried fruits

Examples

Dried figs in storage can be ruined by infestation with the raisin moth.
The raisin moth is a serious pest for raisin producers in California.
Farmers take measures to control raisin moth populations and protect their crops.
