ringneck snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋˌnɛk sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, nonvenomous North American snake, typically having a yellow or orange ring around its neck
small nonvenomous North American snake with neck ring

Examples

The ringneck snake is often found hiding under rocks or logs in wooded areas.
A ringneck snake will usually curl its tail and show its bright underside when threatened.
We spotted a tiny ringneck snake in the garden yesterday.
