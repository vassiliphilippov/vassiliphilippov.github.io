hair mousse

1. NOUN

/ hɛr mus / (AmE), / heə muːs / (BrE)
a light, airy foam that is sprayed onto the hair to aid in styling, providing hold and volume
a foamy hair styling product

Examples

She applied hair mousse to her curls before blow-drying them for extra volume.
Using hair mousse helps keep your hairstyle looking fresh all day.
My stylist recommended a lightweight hair mousse instead of gel for a softer finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairspray - a liquid sprayed onto hair to hold it in place
  • Synonym: hair gel - a thick styling product used for holding or shaping hair
