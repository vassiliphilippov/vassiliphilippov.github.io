laundry stiffener

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔndri ˌstɪfənɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndri ˌstɪfənə / (BrE)
a substance added to laundry to make fabrics stiffer after washing
substance for stiffening laundry

Examples

She added a laundry stiffener to the final rinse to keep her collars crisp.
The directions on the laundry stiffener recommend diluting before use.
Using a laundry stiffener can help your curtains hold their shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starch - a substance used to stiffen fabrics
