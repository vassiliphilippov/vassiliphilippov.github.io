sexual perversion

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl pərˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl pəˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
a sexual behavior or practice that deviates significantly from what is considered normal or acceptable by societal standards
aberrant sexual practice

Examples

The psychiatrist discussed various forms of sexual perversion during the lecture.
Many societies have different definitions of what constitutes sexual perversion.
In the early 20th century, certain behaviors were labeled as sexual perversion by medical professionals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual deviation - sexual behavior differing from the conventional norm

Etymology

From Latin 'pervertere' (to turn around, corrupt); 'perversion' from Old French and Medieval Latin.
