Third Reich

1. NOUN

/ θɝd ɹaɪk / (AmE), / θɜːd ʁaɪk / (BrE)
the period of Nazi rule in Germany under Adolf Hitler from 1933 to 1945
Nazi Germany under Hitler (1933–1945)

Examples

Many historical documentaries examine the rise and fall of the Third Reich.
The atrocities committed during the Third Reich are remembered as some of the darkest in history.
He wrote a book analyzing economic policies of the Third Reich.

Etymology

From German 'Drittes Reich' meaning 'Third Empire'; 'Reich' means 'empire', referring to the third in a succession after the Holy Roman Empire and the German Empire.
