scene of action

1. NOUN

/ sin əv ˈækʃən / (AmE), / siːn əv ˈækʃən / (BrE)
the place where an event or activity, especially a sporting event, takes place; a playing field or stadium.
venue for sports or events

Examples

The championship match drew thousands of fans to the scene of action.
Reporters arrived early at the scene of action to capture the pre-game excitement.
The football field became the scene of action as soon as the players ran on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arena - a large enclosed area used for sports or entertainment
  • Synonym: stadium - a large outdoor venue for sports events
  • Synonym: field - an open area of land used for sports
