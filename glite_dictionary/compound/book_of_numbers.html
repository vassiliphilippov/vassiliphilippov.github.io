Book of Numbers

1. PROPN

/ ˌbʊk əv ˈnʌmbərz / (AmE), / ˌbʊk əv ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
the fourth book of the Old Testament, detailing the census and experiences of the Israelites during their journey from Egypt to the Promised Land
the fourth book of the Old Testament

Examples

The Book of Numbers describes the journey of the Israelites through the desert.
Many biblical scholars analyze the census mentioned in the Book of Numbers.
In Sunday school, we studied stories from the Book of Numbers.

Etymology

From the Latin 'Numeri', translating the Hebrew 'Bemidbar' (in the wilderness), reflecting its narrative and census records.
