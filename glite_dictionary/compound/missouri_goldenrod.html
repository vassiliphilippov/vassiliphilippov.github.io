Missouri goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈzʊri ˈɡoʊlˌdənˌrɑd / (AmE), / mɪˈzʊəri ˈɡəʊl.dən.rɒd / (BrE)
a North American plant of the goldenrod family, similar to meadow goldenrod but generally smaller, native to central United States
a smaller North American goldenrod plant

Examples

We spotted a cluster of Missouri goldenrod growing along the trail.
Gardeners often choose Missouri goldenrod for its resilience and small stature.
The Missouri goldenrod blooms in late summer, attracting butterflies and bees.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: meadow goldenrod - a species of goldenrod that is similar but usually larger
