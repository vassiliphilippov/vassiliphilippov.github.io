Gulf of Naples

1. PROPN

/ ɡʌlf əv ˈneɪpəlz / (AmE), / ɡʌlf ɒv ˈneɪpl̩z / (BrE)
a large bay of the Tyrrhenian Sea, located along the southwestern coast of Italy near the city of Naples
bay near Naples, Italy

Examples

The Gulf of Naples is famous for its stunning views of Mount Vesuvius and the Amalfi Coast.
Many ancient Roman ruins are found around the Gulf of Naples.
Tourists enjoy sailing and swimming in the Gulf of Naples during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bay of Naples - another common name for the Gulf of Naples
