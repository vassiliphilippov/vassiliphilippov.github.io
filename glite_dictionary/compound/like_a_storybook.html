like a storybook

1. ADJ

/ laɪk ə ˈstɔːriˌbʊk / (AmE), / laɪk ə ˈstɔːriˌbʊk / (BrE)
(of an event or situation) appearing idealized, perfect, or as if taken from a fairy tale or fiction.
idealized or perfect, like in a fairy tale

Examples

The small village looked like a storybook with its charming cottages and blooming gardens.
Their wedding was so beautiful, it seemed like a storybook come to life.
The snow-covered city at night appeared like a storybook, enchanting and magical.

Synset Examples

Her childhood was like a storybook filled with adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storybook - a book containing stories, usually for children
