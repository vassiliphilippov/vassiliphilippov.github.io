cow manure

1. NOUN

/ kaʊ məˈnʊr / (AmE), / kaʊ məˈnjʊə / (BrE)
the dung or excreta of cows, especially when used as fertilizer for soil
cow dung used as fertilizer

Examples

Organic farmers often use cow manure to enrich the soil before planting their crops.
The pungent smell of cow manure filled the barnyard after the cows were moved.
She spread cow manure on her vegetable garden to help the plants grow better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compost - decayed organic material used as fertilizer
