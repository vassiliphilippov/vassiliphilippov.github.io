professional wrestling

1. NOUN

/ prəˌfəʃənəl ˈrɛslɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃ(ə)n(ə)l ˈrɛslɪŋ / (BrE)
a form of wrestling performed as an entertainment or sport, in which the participants are paid and the outcomes are often predetermined
entertaining, paid wrestling sport

Examples

He grew up watching professional wrestling with his older brothers every Saturday night.
Many athletes started their careers in professional wrestling before moving into acting or broadcasting.
Tickets for the professional wrestling event sold out within hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestling - the activity or sport of grappling as a contest
