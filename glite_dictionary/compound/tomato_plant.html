tomato plant

1. NOUN

/ təˈmeɪɾoʊ plænt / (AmE), / təˈmɑːtəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant native to South America, widely cultivated for its edible, typically red fruit; the source of tomatoes
plant that produces tomatoes

Examples

The tomato plant in our garden is producing ripe fruit earlier than expected.
She carefully watered each tomato plant every morning to ensure healthy growth.
Greenhouses allow farmers to grow the tomato plant all year round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tomato - the edible, typically red fruit of the tomato plant
