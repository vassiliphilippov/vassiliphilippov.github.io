vivid imagery

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪvɪd ˈɪmɪdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈvɪvɪd ˈɪmɪdʒəri / (BrE)
descriptive language that creates clear, strong, and striking visual impressions in the reader's mind
striking visual description in language

Examples

The novel is praised for its vivid imagery that brings every scene to life.
Her speech was full of vivid imagery, making the listeners feel as if they were there.
Artists often rely on vivid imagery to inspire their work and evoke emotions.

Synset Examples

The poem is rich in vivid imagery.
Use vivid imagery to draw in your audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagery - visually descriptive or figurative language
