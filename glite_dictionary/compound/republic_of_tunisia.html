Republic of Tunisia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv tuˈniʒə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv tjuːˈniːzɪə / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, on the Mediterranean coast, which gained independence from France in 1956; its capital is Tunis.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Tunisia is located between Algeria and Libya on the Mediterranean coast.
After gaining independence, the Republic of Tunisia developed its own national identity.
The capital city of the Republic of Tunisia is Tunis.

Synset Examples

southern Tunisia is mostly desert
