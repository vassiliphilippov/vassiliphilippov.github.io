long jump

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ dʒʌmp / (AmE), / lɒŋ dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors try to jump as far as possible from a running start
athletic event: jumping for distance

Examples

She set a new record in the long jump at the school track meet.
The long jump requires both speed and technique to achieve maximum distance.
He practiced his long jump landing to improve his performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triple jump - athletic event: hop, step, and jump for distance
