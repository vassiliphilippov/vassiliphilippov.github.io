greedy bankster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡridi ˈbæŋkstər / (AmE), / ˈɡriːdi ˈbæŋstə / (BrE)
A dishonest or corrupt banker or financial professional, especially one seen as greedy or exploitative.
dishonest or greedy banker
slang

Examples

After the crisis, many people blamed the greedy banksters for the financial collapse.
Her documentary exposes the role of greedy banksters in the housing market crash.
Politicians promised to impose stricter regulations on greedy banksters to prevent future scandals.

Synset Examples

The public protested against the greedy banksters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bankster - dishonest or corrupt banker or financier
  • Degree Lower: banker - person involved in banking business
  • Degree Higher: crook - dishonest person; criminal

Etymology

Blend of 'banker' and 'gangster', with 'greedy' added as modifier.
