darkest timeline

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑrkəst ˈtaɪmlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkɪst ˈtaɪmlaɪn / (BrE)
a hypothetical or exaggerated scenario in which everything has gone wrong or turned out negatively, often used humorously or to describe a difficult period
the worst possible scenario or period

Examples

Many people joked that 2020 felt like the darkest timeline after so many things went wrong.
After losing his job and apartment, he said he must be living in the darkest timeline.
Sometimes, when the news is especially grim, it feels like we've slipped into the darkest timeline.

Synset Examples

In the darkest timeline, everything went wrong.
He joked about living in the darkest timeline.
She felt trapped in the darkest timeline of her life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worst-case scenario - the most disastrous possible situation
  • Antonym: best-case scenario - the most favorable possible situation

Etymology

Popularized by the TV show 'Community' (2012), referring to an alternate reality where everything went bad.
