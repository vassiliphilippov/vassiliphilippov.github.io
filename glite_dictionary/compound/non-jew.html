non-Jew

1. NOUN

/ nɑn ˈdʒu / (AmE), / nɒn ˈdʒu / (BrE)
A person who is not Jewish, especially used to contrast with Jews in religious, cultural, or historical contexts.
person who is not Jewish

Examples

Most of the attendees at the ceremony were non-Jews interested in learning about Jewish traditions.
The book explores relationships between Jews and non-Jews in Eastern Europe.
He grew up as a non-Jew in a predominantly Jewish neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gentile - A person who is not Jewish
