shotgun wedding

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn ˈwɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtɡʌn ˈwɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
a hurried marriage, especially one arranged because the bride is pregnant
hurried marriage due to pregnancy

Examples

Rumors spread that their shotgun wedding was planned soon after they discovered the pregnancy.
Despite the circumstances of their shotgun wedding, the couple grew closer over time.
Her parents were shocked when she announced her shotgun wedding to everyone at dinner.

Synset Examples

They rushed into a shotgun wedding after the news.
Their shotgun wedding surprised all their friends.
The couple had a quick shotgun wedding last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forced marriage - a marriage entered into without the full, free consent of both parties

Etymology

From the notion of the bride's father threatening the groom with a shotgun to enforce marriage.
