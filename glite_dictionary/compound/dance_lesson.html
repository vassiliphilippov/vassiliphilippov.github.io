dance lesson

1. NOUN

/ dæns ˈlɛsən / (AmE), / dɑːns ˈlɛsən / (BrE)
an occasion or session in which someone is taught how to dance
an occasion of dance instruction

Examples

She signed up for a dance lesson to prepare for the wedding.
The community center offers a dance lesson every Saturday afternoon.
He felt nervous before his first dance lesson, but he soon enjoyed himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancing class - a class where people are taught to dance
