muskrat fur

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsˌkræt fɝ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsˌkræt fə / (BrE)
the brown fur obtained from a muskrat, often used in making garments and accessories
fur from a muskrat

Examples

The coat was lined with muskrat fur to keep it warm during winter.
Vintage hats made from muskrat fur are still found in some antique shops.
She admired the softness and sheen of the muskrat fur collar on her grandmother's jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muskrat - a large semi-aquatic North American rodent
  • Synonym: fur - the short, fine, soft hair of certain animals
