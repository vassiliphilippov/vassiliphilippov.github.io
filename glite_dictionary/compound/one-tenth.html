one-tenth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈtɛnθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈtɛnθ / (BrE)
One part out of ten equal parts; a tenth.
a tenth part; 1⁄10

Examples

One one-tenth of the cake was left after the party.
A one-tenth share of the company was given to each investor.
She paid one-tenth of the cost as a deposit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenth - each of ten equal parts; the ordinal form of ten
