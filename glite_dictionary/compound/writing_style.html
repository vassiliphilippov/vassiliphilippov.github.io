writing style

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌstaɪl / (BrE)
the distinctive manner in which a person expresses ideas in writing; a way of using language and structure in written work
distinctive manner of writing

Examples

The author's writing style is clear and concise, making the book easy to understand.
Her unique writing style sets her apart from other journalists in the industry.
Over the years, he experimented with different writing styles to find his true voice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: style - a particular way in which something is done, created, or performed
