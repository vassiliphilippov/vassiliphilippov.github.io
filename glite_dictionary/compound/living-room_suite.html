living-room suite

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm swiːt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm swiːt / (BrE)
a set of matching furniture designed for use in the living room, typically including sofas and chairs
set of living room furniture

Examples

We decided to buy a new living-room suite to replace our old sofa and chairs.
The elegant living-room suite perfectly matched the modern décor of their home.
She found a stylish living-room suite on sale at the furniture store.
