Unix weenie

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːnɪks ˈwiːni / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪks ˈwiːni / (BrE)
a person who is overly enthusiastic about or excessively devoted to Unix systems and their command-line interfaces, often to the point of excluding other operating systems
overly enthusiastic Unix systems fan
informal

Examples

Everyone in the IT department calls Sarah the resident Unix weenie because of her passion for shell scripts.
That new hire is a real Unix weenie—he even refuses to use Windows for email.
Back in the 1990s, being a Unix weenie was almost a badge of honor among hackers.

Synset Examples

He's such a Unix weenie, always showing off his terminal skills.
As a Unix weenie, she prefers command lines over graphical interfaces.
The conference was filled with Unix weenies discussing shell scripting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Unix guru - an expert or highly knowledgeable person about Unix systems
  • Degree Lower: Unix - a family of multitasking, multiuser computer operating systems

Etymology

From 'Unix', the name of a computer operating system (developed in the 1970s), and 'weenie', US informal slang for an overly enthusiastic or geeky person.
