golden oriole

1. NOUN

/ ˈgoʊl.dən ˈɔːr.i.oʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.dən ˈɔː.rɪ.əʊl / (BrE)
a brightly colored yellow songbird with black wings, native to Eurasia and North Africa
yellow and black songbird

Examples

The golden oriole stood out brilliantly among the green leaves.
Every spring, birdwatchers eagerly anticipate the arrival of the golden oriole in the woods.
A flash of yellow revealed the presence of a golden oriole near the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oriole - any of various Old World or New World songbirds with bright plumage
