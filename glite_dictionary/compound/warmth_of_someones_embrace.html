warmth of someone's embrace

1. NOUN

/ wɔrmθ ʌv sʌmwʌnz ɪmˈbreɪs / (AmE), / wɔːmθ ɒv sʌmwʌnz ɪmˈbreɪs / (BrE)
a feeling of comfort, affection, and safety experienced when someone is hugged
comfort and affection in a hug

Examples

He longed for the warmth of his mother's embrace after a difficult day.
The warmth of her embrace made him feel instantly at home.
Many people find comfort in the warmth of a loved one's embrace when they are sad.

Synset Examples

He felt safe in the warmth of her embrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hug - an act of holding someone tightly with one's arms to express affection
  • Synonym: embrace - the act of holding someone closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection
