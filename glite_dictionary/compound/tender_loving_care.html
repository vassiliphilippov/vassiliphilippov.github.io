tender loving care

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛndɚ ˈlʌvɪŋ ˈkɛɹ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə ˈlʌvɪŋ ˈkeə / (BrE)
care that is gentle, thoughtful, and attentive, especially toward someone in need of comfort or support.
gentle, thoughtful, and attentive care

Examples

This old house just needs some tender loving care to look beautiful again.
The shelter staff give every animal plenty of tender loving care.
