Carolina spring beauty

1. NOUN

/ kəˈroʊlɪnə sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊlɪnə sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a small perennial plant native to eastern North America, with slender leaves and typically pink flowers, similar to Claytonia virginica
North American wildflower; Claytonia caroliniana

Examples

We spotted a patch of Carolina spring beauty blooming along the woodland trail.
The delicate pink flowers of the Carolina spring beauty brighten up early spring forests.
Unlike its relative, the Carolina spring beauty typically has broader leaves and more vibrant petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spring beauty - any plant of the genus Claytonia, noted for delicate spring-blooming flowers
  • Degree Lower: Claytonia virginica - a related species of spring beauty with usually white or pink-striped flowers
