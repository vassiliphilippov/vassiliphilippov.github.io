weight of responsibility

1. NOUN

/ weɪt əv rɪˌspɑnsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / weɪt əv rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the heavy burden or pressure felt due to important duties or obligations
pressure from important obligations

Examples

After his promotion, the weight of responsibility sometimes kept him awake at night.
She struggled under the weight of responsibility as the team's leader.
For the first time, he understood the true weight of responsibility that comes with parenting.

Synset Examples

Under the captain's cloak, he felt the weight of responsibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burden - something that is carried; a duty or responsibility causing hardship
  • Synonym: onus - a duty or responsibility, especially one that is difficult
  • Synonym: load - something that is carried, figuratively a heavy responsibility
