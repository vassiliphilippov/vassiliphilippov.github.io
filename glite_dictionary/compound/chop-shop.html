chop-shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑp ˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒp ˌʃɒp / (BrE)
an illegal garage or workshop where stolen vehicles are dismantled for parts
illegal garage dismantling stolen vehicles

Examples

The police raided a chop-shop on the outskirts of the city, recovering several stolen cars.
Authorities have shut down a chop-shop operating under the guise of a repair business.
He was arrested after selling stolen parts traced back to a notorious chop-shop.

Synset Examples

The police raided a chop-shop in the industrial district.
The mechanic warned us about the nearby chop-shop.
He discovered the stolen car at a local chop-shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garage - a building for storing or repairing vehicles

Etymology

From 'chop' (to cut) and 'shop' (store or workshop), referring to cutting up vehicles.
