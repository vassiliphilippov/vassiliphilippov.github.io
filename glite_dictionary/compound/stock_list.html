stock list

1. NOUN

/ stɑk lɪst / (AmE), / stɒk lɪst / (BrE)
a detailed list of all the items currently held in stock, typically for inventory or sales purposes
list of items in stock

Examples

The store manager reviewed the stock list to decide which products needed to be reordered.
Please update the stock list so we have an accurate record of our inventory.
Every warehouse must maintain a current stock list for auditing purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inventory - a complete list of items such as goods in stock
  • Synonym: catalogue - a complete list of items, typically in systematic order
