paintball gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntˌbɔl ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntˌbɔːl ɡʌn / (BrE)
An air gun that shoots capsules filled with paint, used in the sport of paintball to mark opponents.
air gun used in paintball

Examples

Before joining the game, he made sure his paintball gun was fully loaded and working.
Many rental packages at the field include protective gear and a paintball gun.
She accidentally dropped her paintball gun during the final round, but quickly picked it up and continued playing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marker - another term for a paintball gun
