side-to-side

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaɪd tə ˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ˌsaɪd tə ˈsaɪd / (BrE)
moving or directed alternately left and right with respect to a central point or axis.
moving alternately left and right

Examples

The dancer impressed the audience with a side-to-side motion that was perfectly synchronized.
When I drive over bumps, there's often a side-to-side wobble in the steering wheel.
The boat rocked with a steady side-to-side movement in the gentle waves.

Synset Examples

the side-to-side motion of the boat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: back-and-forth - moving first in one direction and then in the opposite
