shovelnose catfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʌvəlˌnoʊz ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəlnəʊz ˈkatˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large catfish found in central United States, characterized by a flattened head and a projecting jaw
catfish with a flattened head of central U.S.

Examples

The shovelnose catfish is popular among anglers due to its distinctive appearance.
We spotted a large shovelnose catfish swimming near the riverbank.
The biology class studied the anatomy of the shovelnose catfish in detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catfish - a type of fish with whisker-like barbels around the mouth
