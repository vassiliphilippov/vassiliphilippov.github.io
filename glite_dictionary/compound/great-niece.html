great-niece

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡreɪt ˈnis / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈniːs / (BrE)
The daughter of one's niece or nephew.
niece's or nephew's daughter

Examples

My great-niece is coming to visit us during the holidays.
She often babysits her great-niece on weekends.
They were excited to meet their newborn great-niece for the first time.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: niece - the daughter of one's brother or sister
  • Degree Lower: nephew - the son of one's brother or sister
