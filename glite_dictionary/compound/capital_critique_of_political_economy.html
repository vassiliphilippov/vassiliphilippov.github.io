Capital: Critique of Political Economy

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæpɪtəl krɪˈtik ʌv pəˈlɪtɪkəl ɪˈkɑːnəmi / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl krɪˈtiːk əv pəˈlɪtɪkəl iːˈkɒnəmi / (BrE)
the major work by Karl Marx (published in 1867) presenting his theories of capitalism, economics, and political economy
foundational economic work by Karl Marx (1867)

Examples

Many scholars have studied Capital: Critique of Political Economy to understand the foundations of Marxist theory.
The first volume of Capital: Critique of Political Economy was published in 1867.
Modern economists still reference Capital: Critique of Political Economy in discussions about labor and value.
