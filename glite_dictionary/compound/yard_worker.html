yard worker

1. NOUN

/ jɑrd ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / jɑːd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works in a railway yard, responsible for tasks such as assembling or disassembling trains and supervising rail operations
railway yard employee

Examples

The yard worker coordinated the movement of freight cars throughout the busy railway yard.
During his shift, the yard worker ensured all trains were properly assembled and ready for departure.
After years as a yard worker, she was promoted to supervisor at the rail depot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switchman - person who operates railroad switches in a yard

2. NOUN

/ jɑrd ˈwɝkɚ / (AmE), / jɑːd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person employed to perform outdoor tasks such as mowing lawns, gardening, or general maintenance of a yard
person hired for outdoor yard tasks

Examples

The yard worker arrived early in the morning to trim the bushes and mow the grass.
They hired a yard worker for the summer to help with landscaping and garden chores.
As a student, he worked part-time as a yard worker to earn extra money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardener - a person employed to take care of a garden
  • Synonym: groundskeeper - a person who maintains the grounds of an estate, park, or facility
