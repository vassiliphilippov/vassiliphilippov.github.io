blue African lily

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈæfrɪkən ˈlɪli / (AmE), / bluː ˈæfrɪkən ˈlɪli / (BrE)
an African plant with bright evergreen leaves and clusters of deep violet-blue flowers, often grown as an ornamental
African ornamental plant with blue flowers

Examples

The blue African lily adds striking color to summer gardens.
She planted a row of blue African lily along the walkway for a dramatic effect.
Gardeners admire the blue African lily for its resilience and vibrant blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agapanthus - genus of plants commonly known as African lilies
