tidy appearance

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪdi əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdi əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
a neat and well-organized look or outward aspect, especially in relation to a person's clothing and grooming
neat, well-organized look

Examples

She always maintains a tidy appearance at work, no matter how busy she is.
A tidy appearance can create a positive first impression during job interviews.
Despite the rush, he managed to present a tidy appearance at the important event.

Synset Examples

He always maintains a tidy appearance for meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neat appearance - the quality of being clean and orderly in looks
  • Antonym: scruffy appearance - an untidy or shabby look
