the thrill of

1. NOUN

/ ðə θrɪl ʌv / (AmE), / ðə θrɪl ɒv / (BrE)
the intense excitement or exhilaration experienced from a particular activity or event
the excitement of an activity or event

Examples

She loved the thrill of performing live on stage before a huge audience.
Some people seek the thrill of extreme sports like bungee jumping or skydiving.
He missed the thrill of adventure after retiring from his job as a firefighter.

Synset Examples

She loved the thrill of the masquerade ball.
He loves the thrill of racing at the dragstrip.
He enjoyed the thrill of riding the swift boat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitement - a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness
