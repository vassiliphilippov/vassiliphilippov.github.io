plane seat

1. NOUN

/ pleɪn siːt / (AmE), / pleɪn siːt / (BrE)
a seat for a passenger on a commercial airplane
passenger seat on an airplane

Examples

She always tries to reserve a plane seat by the window.
The plane seats were surprisingly comfortable on the long flight.
I found my plane seat near the back of the aircraft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airline seat - a seat on a commercial airliner
  • Synonym: window seat - a seat next to the window of an airplane
  • Synonym: aisle seat - a seat next to the aisle of an airplane
