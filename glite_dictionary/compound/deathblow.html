deathblow

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθˌbloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ.bləʊ / (BrE)
A blow that kills, often delivered to end suffering or as a final, decisive act.
a blow that kills

Examples

The knight delivered the deathblow to the wounded dragon, ending its suffering.
The company's bankruptcy was the deathblow to its hopes of recovery.
He hesitated before striking the deathblow, knowing it would end the long battle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coup de grâce - a final blow or shot given to kill a wounded person or animal
