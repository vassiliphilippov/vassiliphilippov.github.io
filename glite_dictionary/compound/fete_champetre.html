fete champetre

1. NOUN

/ feɪt ʃæmˈpɛtər / (AmE), / feɪt ʃɑ̃ˈpɛtʁ / (BrE)
An outdoor party or gathering, typically elaborate and often associated with the French aristocratic tradition.
an elaborate outdoor party

Examples

The annual museum fundraiser was a lavish fete champetre held in the sculpture garden.
Guests strolled among the orchards during the fete champetre, enjoying music and refreshments.
Inspired by 18th-century tradition, the estate hosted a charming fete champetre last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden party - a social gathering held outdoors, typically in a garden

Etymology

From French 'fête champêtre' meaning 'rural festival'.
