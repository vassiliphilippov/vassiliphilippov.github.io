PIN number

1. NOUN

/ pɪn ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / pɪn ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a personal identification number used to gain access to secure accounts such as those for banking or mobile devices
personal code for account access

Examples

Please do not share your PIN number with anyone to ensure the security of your account.
He forgot his PIN number and couldn't withdraw cash from the ATM.
Many websites now require a PIN number for added security during login.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PIN - personal identification number used for account access

Etymology

A redundant form of 'PIN' (Personal Identification Number) plus 'number'; widely used in everyday language despite redundancy.
