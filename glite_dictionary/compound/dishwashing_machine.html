dishwashing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine designed for automatically washing dishes, cookware, and utensils
machine for washing dishes

Examples

We bought a new dishwashing machine to save time on cleaning up after meals.
The dishwashing machine has several settings for different types of dishes.
After the party, he loaded all the plates into the dishwashing machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dishwasher - a machine for washing dishes, commonly used synonym
