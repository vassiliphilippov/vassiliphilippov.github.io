rose window

1. NOUN

/ roʊz ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a large, circular window with decorative stone tracery, typically found in Gothic cathedrals
large circular window with tracery in churches

Examples

The cathedral's rose window filled the nave with colorful light.
The restoration project included careful cleaning of the rose window.
Tourists marveled at the intricate details in the rose window above the entrance.
