academic requirement

1. NOUN

/ əˌkædəmɪk rɪˈkwaɪrmənt / (AmE), / əˌkadəmɪk rɪˈkwʌɪəmənt / (BrE)
a course, qualification, or standard that must be fulfilled to gain admission to or complete an academic program
required qualification or standard in education

Examples

Most universities have at least one academic requirement for admission, such as standardized test scores.
She struggled to fulfill the academic requirements for her engineering degree.
This program allows for some flexibility in meeting academic requirements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prerequisite - something required as a prior condition
  • Synonym: qualification - a condition or standard that must be met
