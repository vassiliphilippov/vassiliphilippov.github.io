mid-January

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈdʒænjuˌɛri / (AmE), / mɪd ˈdʒanjuəri / (BrE)
the period around the middle part of January
the middle part of January

Examples

We are planning to launch the new product in mid-January.
By mid-January, most students have returned to campus for the new semester.
The weather tends to be particularly harsh in mid-January around here.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: end-January - the final part of January
  • Antonym: early January - the first part of January
