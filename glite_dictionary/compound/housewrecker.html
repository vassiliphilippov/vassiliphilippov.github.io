housewrecker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌrɛkə / (BrE)
A person or business whose job is to demolish or tear down houses.
person or company who demolishes houses

Examples

The company hired a housewrecker to tear down the old farmhouse.
As a professional housewrecker, she was used to clearing lots for new developments.
The housewrecker arrived early in the morning with heavy machinery.

Synset Examples

in England a housewrecker is called a ‘housebreaker’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demolisher - a person or machine that demolishes buildings
  • Synonym: bulldozer - a vehicle used for demolishing buildings

Etymology

Formed from 'house' + 'wrecker', using 'wrecker' in the sense of one who demolishes structures.
