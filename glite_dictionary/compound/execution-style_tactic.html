execution-style tactic

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən staɪl ˈtæk.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃən staɪl ˈtæk.tɪk / (BrE)
a method of violence resembling a formal execution, often involving killing at close range or in a controlled manner
violent method resembling formal execution

Examples

The police reported that the victim was killed in an execution-style tactic late last night.
Investigators discovered multiple victims, all murdered using execution-style tactics.
The film depicted the ruthless execution-style tactic employed by the organization to eliminate dissenters.

Synset Examples

The documentary revealed execution-style tactics used by gangs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: execution - the carrying out of a sentence of death on a condemned person
