North Pacific

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ pəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / nɔːθ pəˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
the part of the Pacific Ocean lying north of the equator
northern part of the Pacific Ocean

Examples

Researchers tracked ocean currents in the North Pacific to study climate change impacts.
Many migratory birds follow routes that span the vast North Pacific each year.
A powerful typhoon developed in the North Pacific before moving toward Japan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pacific Ocean - the largest and deepest of Earth's oceanic divisions
  • Antonym: South Pacific - the part of the Pacific Ocean lying south of the equator
