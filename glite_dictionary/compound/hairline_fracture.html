hairline fracture

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌlaɪn ˈfræktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌlaɪn ˈfræktʃə / (BrE)
a very thin crack in a bone that is not separated into pieces
very thin bone crack
medical

Examples

The X-ray revealed a hairline fracture in his wrist after the fall.
She was relieved to learn the injury was only a hairline fracture and not a full break.
After running the marathon, he developed a hairline fracture in his foot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fracture - the cracking or breaking of a hard object or material, especially a bone
