slinger ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪŋər rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋə rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular device fitted around an aircraft's propeller hub, designed to distribute antifreeze over the blades
aircraft propeller antifreeze device

Examples

The mechanic inspected the slinger ring to ensure it would evenly distribute the de-icing fluid.
Without a functioning slinger ring, the propeller could accumulate dangerous levels of ice.
Some older aircraft required manual filling of the slinger ring before flight in cold conditions.
