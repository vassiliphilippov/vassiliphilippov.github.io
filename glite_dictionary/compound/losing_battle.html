losing battle

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːzɪŋ ˈbæt̬əl / (AmE), / ˈluːzɪŋ ˈbætəl / (BrE)
an effort or struggle that is unlikely to succeed or is certain to fail
futile or hopeless struggle

Examples

She knew she was fighting a losing battle trying to keep her desk organized.
Despite their best efforts, the firefighters were fighting a losing battle against the spreading flames.
Trying to change his stubborn mind was like waging a losing battle.

Synset Examples

The team was fighting a losing battle against the clock.
He realized he was fighting a losing battle with his diet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uphill battle - a challenging or difficult struggle
  • Synonym: hopeless - having no chance of success; despairing
