clout goblin

1. NOUN

/ klaʊt ˈɡɑːblɪn / (AmE), / klaʊt ˈɡɒblɪn / (BrE)
A person, especially online, who obsessively seeks influence, popularity, or attention, often at the expense of sincerity or integrity.
person obsessed with gaining influence or attention
slang

Examples

His constant posting made him seem like a total clout goblin to his friends.
Some influencers are accused of being clout goblins who care only about followers.
Don't be a clout goblin; focus on genuine connections instead.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew he was just a clout goblin.
The clout goblin of the group stirred drama for views.
He acted like a clout goblin, always seeking attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention seeker - person who behaves in a way to attract attention

Etymology

From slang 'clout' (influence or social standing) and 'goblin' (mythical creature, used pejoratively).
