lithia water

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪθiə ˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈlɪθiə ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
mineral water that contains lithium salts, sometimes used for its supposed medicinal properties
mineral water with lithium salts

Examples

Some spas offer natural lithia water believed to have therapeutic effects.
He drank a bottle of lithia water every day for its purported health benefits.
The spring is famous for its pure lithia water rich in minerals.

Etymology

From 'lithia', referring to lithium salts, and 'water'.
