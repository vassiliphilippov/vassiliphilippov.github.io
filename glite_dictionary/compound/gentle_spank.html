gentle spank

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntɫ̩ spæŋk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntl spæŋk / (BrE)
a light slap, usually delivered to attract attention rather than to punish
a light slap for attention

Examples

The trainer gave the puppy a gentle spank to correct its behavior.
She gave her brother a gentle spank on the arm to get his attention.
Sometimes a gentle spank is all that's needed to stop mischief.

Synset Examples

He gave the dog a gentle spank to get its attention.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: spank - a slap with the open hand, especially on the buttocks
