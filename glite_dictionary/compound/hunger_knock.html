hunger knock

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌŋɡər nɑk / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡə nɒk / (BrE)
a strong sensation or signal in the body that indicates intense hunger, often experienced as a sound or feeling in the stomach
a strong feeling or sign of hunger

Examples

After skipping lunch, a sudden hunger knock made him realize how hungry he was.
She tried to ignore the persistent hunger knock during the afternoon meeting.
Long-distance runners often describe experiencing a hunger knock if they forget to eat before a race.

Synset Examples

I heard a hunger knock in my stomach after skipping breakfast.
He ignored the hunger knock and focused on his work.
The long meeting left everyone with a hunger knock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunger pang - a sharp physical feeling of hunger
