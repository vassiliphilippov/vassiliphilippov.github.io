Small Business Administration

1. PROPN

/ smɔl ˈbɪznəs ˌædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈbɪznɪs ˌædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United States government that supports and protects the interests of small businesses
US agency supporting small businesses
american

Examples

The Small Business Administration offers loans and grants to support new entrepreneurs across the country.
