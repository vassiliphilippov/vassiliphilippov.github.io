conference center

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns ˌsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fər.əns ˌsɛn.tə / (BrE)
a building or facility designed for hosting conferences, meetings, and similar gatherings
facility for hosting conferences and meetings

Examples

The annual medical symposium will be held at the new conference center downtown.
Many hotels have a dedicated conference center for business meetings and events.
The conference center offers several large rooms equipped with modern technology.
