bomber jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːmər ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɒmə ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A short jacket that is gathered at the waist and cuffs, often with a zipper front and sometimes with a fitted collar, originally based on military flight jackets.
short waist-length jacket, often zipped

Examples

She wore a sleek leather bomber jacket over her dress for a casual night out.
His vintage bomber jacket has patches from several different air force squads.
The bomber jacket became a popular fashion item in the late 20th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight jacket - a similar style jacket designed for military pilots
  • Degree Higher: parka - a warm, often fur-lined jacket covering the hips

Etymology

Named after flight jackets worn by bomber pilots during World War II.
