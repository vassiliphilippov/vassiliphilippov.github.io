bride-to-be

1. NOUN

/ braɪd tə ˈbi / (AmE), / braɪd tə ˈbiː / (BrE)
a woman who is engaged to be married
engaged woman, future bride

Examples

The bride-to-be was busy planning every detail of her wedding day.
Her friends threw a surprise party for the bride-to-be last weekend.
As a bride-to-be, she was excited to choose her wedding dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiancée - a woman engaged to be married
  • Antonym: groom-to-be - a man who is engaged to be married
