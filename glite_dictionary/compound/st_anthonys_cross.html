St. Anthony's cross

1. NOUN

/ ˌseɪnt ˈænθəniːz krɑs / (AmE), / ˌseɪnt ˈænθəniːz krɒs / (BrE)
a cross shaped like the Greek letter tau (T), traditionally associated with St. Anthony
cross shaped like Greek letter tau

Examples

The monk wore a pendant in the shape of a St. Anthony's cross around his neck.
She explained that the St. Anthony's cross symbolizes protection and faith.
The architect incorporated the design of a St. Anthony's cross into the church's stained glass window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tau cross - cross in the shape of the letter T

Etymology

Named after St. Anthony the Great, an early Christian monk, with whom this symbol is associated.
