grocery list

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsəri lɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri lɪst / (BrE)
a written or mental list of items to be bought at a grocery store
list of groceries to buy

Examples

I forgot to add eggs to my grocery list before going to the store.
She always makes a grocery list to stay organized and avoid impulse buys.
My phone has an app for creating a digital grocery list.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping list - a list of things you intend to buy
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06503268-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsəri lɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri lɪst / (BrE)
a written or mental list of items to be purchased from a grocery store, or, by extension, any list of diverse or miscellaneous items or demands
list of groceries or varied items

Examples

She quickly jotted down eggs, milk, and bread on her grocery list before leaving for the store.
The manager presented the board with a grocery list of necessary improvements.
He checked off each item as he found it on the grocery list in his hand.

Synset Examples

the union came to the table with a whole grocery list of demands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping list - a list of items to buy, especially groceries
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06503362-n
Synset POS: n
