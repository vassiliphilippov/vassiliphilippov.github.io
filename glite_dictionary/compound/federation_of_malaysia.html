Federation of Malaysia

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv məˈleɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the official name for the country of Malaysia, a constitutional monarchy in Southeast Asia on Borneo and the Malay Peninsula, which achieved independence from the United Kingdom in 1957
country in Southeast Asia; official name of Malaysia

Examples

The Federation of Malaysia was formed in 1963, uniting several territories under one government.
After gaining independence, the Federation of Malaysia became a founding member of ASEAN.
The constitution of the Federation of Malaysia establishes it as a constitutional monarchy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malaysia - country in Southeast Asia made up of Peninsular and East Malaysia
