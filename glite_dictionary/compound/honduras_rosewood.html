Honduras rosewood

1. NOUN

/ hɑnˈdurəs ˈroʊzˌwʊd / (AmE), / hɒnˈdjʊərəs ˈrəʊzwʊd / (BrE)
a Central American tree (Dalbergia stevensonii) valued for its hard, dark-streaked rosewood used in fine woodworking and musical instruments
Central American tree with valuable rosewood

Examples

The craftsman carefully selected a piece of Honduras rosewood for the guitar's back and sides.
Due to overharvesting, Honduras rosewood is now considered a vulnerable species.
Furniture made from Honduras rosewood is prized for its rich color and strength.
