retrosexual taste

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛtroʊˈsɛkʃuəl teɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈsɛkʃuəl teɪst / (BrE)
A preference or fondness for vintage or retro styles, fashions, or aesthetics.
preference for vintage or retro styles

Examples

Her retrosexual taste was clear from her collection of 1950s dresses and antique furniture.
Many interior designers have embraced a retrosexual taste to create nostalgic, stylish spaces.
His retrosexual taste extends to preferring vinyl records and classic cars over modern alternatives.

Synset Examples

Her retrosexual tastes are reflected in her vintage decor.

Etymology

Formed from 'retrosexual' (from 'retro-' + 'heterosexual') + 'taste'. 'Retro-' from Latin 'retro' meaning 'backwards'.
