all season long

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈsiːzən lɔŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈsiːzn lɒŋ / (BrE)
throughout the entire season; for the duration of the whole season
for the whole season

Examples

The team played consistently well all season long and finally won the championship.
Flowers bloomed in her garden all season long, bringing color to her yard.
He struggled with injuries all season long but never gave up.

Synset Examples

As a winter rat, I binge-watch shows all season long.
