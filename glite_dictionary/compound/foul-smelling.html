foul-smelling

1. ADJ

/ faʊlˈsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / faʊlˈsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having an extremely unpleasant, offensive odor.
having a very bad smell

Examples

The garbage bin was foul-smelling after sitting in the sun all day.
We had to leave the room because of the foul-smelling chemicals.
The foul-smelling sewer made it hard to breathe.

Synset Examples

a foul odor
the kitchen smelled really funky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant smell
