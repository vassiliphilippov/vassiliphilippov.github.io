meadow grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a type of grass that typically grows in moist meadows or pastures, commonly referring to species in the genus Poa
grass that grows in moist meadows

Examples

The field was filled with lush meadow grass swaying gently in the breeze.
Farmers often rely on meadow grass as an important food source for livestock.
After the rain, the meadow grass grew rapidly, covering the entire valley in green.
