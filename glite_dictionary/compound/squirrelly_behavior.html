squirrelly behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɝːli bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈskwɪəli bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
nervous, unpredictable, or erratic actions or conduct, especially in a way that makes others suspicious or uneasy
nervous or erratic conduct
informal

Examples

Her squirrelly behavior during the meeting made the team uneasy.
Whenever he feels stressed, he starts to show some really squirrelly behavior.
The manager addressed the intern's squirrelly behavior by having a calm conversation with her.

Synset Examples

His squirrelly behavior made everyone question his plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erratic - unpredictable or inconsistent in movement or behavior
  • Synonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed; anxious or apprehensive

Etymology

From 'squirrelly' (like a squirrel: nervously active or unpredictable) + 'behavior'.
