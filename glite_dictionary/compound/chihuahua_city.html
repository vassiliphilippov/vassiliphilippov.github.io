Chihuahua City

1. PROPN

/ tʃɪˈwɑːwə ˈsɪti / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwə ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a city in northern Mexico, the capital of the state of Chihuahua and a major commercial center
city in northern Mexico; capital of Chihuahua state

Examples

Many tourists visit Chihuahua City to explore its colonial architecture and vibrant culture.
The train to Copper Canyon departs from Chihuahua City every morning.
Chihuahua City plays an important role in Mexico's mining and manufacturing industries.

Etymology

Named after the state of Chihuahua, from Nahuatl *xicuahua* (dry, sandy place).
