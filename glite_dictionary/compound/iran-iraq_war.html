Iran-Iraq War

1. PROPN

/ ɪˌrɑːn aɪˈræk wɔr / (AmE), / ɪˌrɑːn aɪˈræk wɔː / (BrE)
A war fought between Iran and Iraq from 1980 to 1988, primarily over territorial disputes and control of the Shatt al-Arab waterway.
war between Iran and Iraq, 1980–1988

Examples

Many families were displaced during the Iran-Iraq War and struggled to rebuild their lives afterward.
The Iran-Iraq War had a profound impact on the politics and economy of the entire Middle East.
Historians continue to study the causes and consequences of the Iran-Iraq War.
