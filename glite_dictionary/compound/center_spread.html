center spread

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.tər ˌsprɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tə ˌsprɛd / (BrE)
the two facing pages at the center of a magazine, often used for a feature or large photo
central two pages in a magazine

Examples

The fashion magazine featured a stunning photo in its center spread this month.
She always checked the center spread first to see the special feature articles.
The advertising team purchased the center spread for their new product launch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double-page spread - an image or article spread across two facing pages
