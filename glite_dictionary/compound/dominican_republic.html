Dominican Republic

1. PROPN

/ dəˌmɪnɪkən rəˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / dəˌmɪnɪkən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
a country in the Caribbean occupying the eastern two-thirds of the island of Hispaniola, sharing the island with Haiti
country in the Caribbean on Hispaniola

Examples

She spent her summer vacation traveling across the Dominican Republic and exploring its beautiful beaches.
The capital city of the Dominican Republic is Santo Domingo, one of the oldest cities in the Caribbean.
Many people visit the Dominican Republic for its vibrant culture and tropical climate.
