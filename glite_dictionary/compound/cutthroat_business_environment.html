cutthroat business environment

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtˌθroʊt ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌθrəʊt ˈbɪznəs ɪnˌvaɪərənmənt / (BrE)
a business context characterized by intense, ruthless competition where individuals or companies will do almost anything to succeed
ruthlessly competitive business setting

Examples

Surviving in a cutthroat business environment requires resilience and strategic thinking.
Many startups fail due to the relentless pace of the cutthroat business environment.
She thrived in the cutthroat business environment of investment banking.

Synset Examples

Plain dealing is rare in today's cutthroat business environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruthless competition - competition without pity or scruples

Etymology

From 'cutthroat' (ruthless competitor) and 'business environment'.
