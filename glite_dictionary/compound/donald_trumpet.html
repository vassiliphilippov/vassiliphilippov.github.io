Donald Trumpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːnəld ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɒnəld ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
A humorous or critical nickname for Donald Trump, referencing his tendency to speak loudly or boastfully.
humorous/critical nickname for Donald Trump

Examples

Some critics referred to him as Donald Trumpet because of his brash remarks.
After his latest social media post, the nickname Donald Trumpet started trending again.
In her sketch, the comedian imitated Donald Trumpet exaggerating his achievements.

Synset Examples

In the debate, she sounded just like a Donald Trumpet.

Etymology

Blend of "Donald Trump" and "trumpet" to suggest loudness or boastfulness.
