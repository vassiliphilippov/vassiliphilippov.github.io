admission charge

1. NOUN

/ ədˈmɪʃ.ən tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ədˈmɪʃ.ən tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a fee that must be paid to enter a place, building, or event
fee for entering a place or event

Examples

There is a small admission charge for visitors to the museum.
Children under five do not have to pay the admission charge.
The concert's admission charge helps support local artists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - the fee paid for entering a place or event
