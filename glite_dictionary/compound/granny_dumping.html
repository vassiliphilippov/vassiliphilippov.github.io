granny dumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræn.i ˌdʌmp.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡran.i ˌdʌmp.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of abandoning elderly relatives, especially by leaving them at hospitals or care facilities without proper arrangements.
abandoning elderly relatives in care facilities

Examples

Reports of granny dumping have increased as families struggle with elder care responsibilities.
Many activists are calling for tougher laws to prevent granny dumping in local communities.
The hospital staff was shocked by the frequency of granny dumping cases during the holidays.

Synset Examples

Granny dumping is a serious issue in our community.
He faced criticism for his granny dumping in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abandonment - the act of leaving someone or something permanently and without intending to return
