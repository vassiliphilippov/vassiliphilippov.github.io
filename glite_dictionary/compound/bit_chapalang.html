bit chapalang

1. ADJ

/ bɪt tʃəˈpɑːlɑŋ / (AmE), / bɪt tʃəˈpalæŋ / (BrE)
Somewhat unusual or odd; a little strange, especially in an informal or idiosyncratic way (informal, chiefly Singapore English).
a little unusual or odd (informal)
informal

Examples

His sense of fashion is always bit chapalang, but it suits his personality.
The decorations at the party were bit chapalang, yet everyone found them charming.
My playlist is bit chapalang because I listen to songs from many different genres.

Synset Examples

Her cooking is delicious, but sometimes it's a bit chapalang.

Etymology

Colloquial Singapore English; 'chapalang' from Hokkien, literally meaning 'mixed up' or 'miscellaneous.'
