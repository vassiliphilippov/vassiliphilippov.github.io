ski pole

1. NOUN

/ ski poʊl / (AmE), / ski pəʊl / (BrE)
A long, slender pole with a pointed metal tip, used by skiers to assist with balance and propulsion on snow.
pole used for balance and propulsion in skiing

Examples

The instructor showed us how to grip the ski pole correctly while climbing the hill.
She planted her ski pole firmly in the snow before making the next turn.
I noticed that one of my ski poles was bent after the fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski - a long, narrow runner used for gliding over snow
