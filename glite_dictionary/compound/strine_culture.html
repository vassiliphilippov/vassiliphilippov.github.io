Strine culture

1. NOUN

/ straɪn ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / straɪn ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The distinctive features of Australian English and the culture that surrounds it.
Australian English and its associated culture
british

Examples

Many people find Strine culture fascinating because of its unique slang and accent.

Related Terms

Etymology

Strine: humorous rendering of 'Australian' in broad Australian accent; coined in 1960s.
