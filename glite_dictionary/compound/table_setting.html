table setting

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˌsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˌsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement of plates, cutlery, glasses, and other items on a dining table for a meal
arrangement of items on a dining table

Examples

The table setting featured fine china and sparkling crystal glasses.
She admired the elegant table setting before the guests arrived.
Each table setting included a personalized menu card.

Synset Examples

The table setting was elegant for the special occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: place setting - the set of tableware for one person at a meal
