statistical distribution

1. NOUN

/ stəˈtɪstɪkəl dɪˌstrɪbjuˈʃən / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl dɪsˌtrɪbjuˈʃən / (BrE)
an arrangement, often shown as a function or table, that displays the possible values of a statistical variable and the frequency with which each value occurs
arrangement showing frequency of values in statistics

Examples

The normal statistical distribution is commonly used to model measurement errors.
Understanding the statistical distribution of test scores helps teachers assess overall performance.
Each statistical distribution has unique characteristics, such as mean and variance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probability distribution - a function that describes the likelihood of different outcomes in a random experiment
