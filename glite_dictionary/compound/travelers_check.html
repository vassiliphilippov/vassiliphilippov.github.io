traveler's check

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævələrz ˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈtræv(ə)ləz ˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
a cheque issued by a bank or financial company, usable internationally and payable upon presentation to an authorized agent as proof of identity
cheque for international use, payable by agents

Examples

I carried a traveler's check in case I lost my wallet during the trip.
Most hotels and shops in Europe accept traveler's checks as a form of payment.
She exchanged her traveler's check at the bank for local currency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheque - written order directing a bank to pay money
  • Synonym: money order - written payment order similar to a check
