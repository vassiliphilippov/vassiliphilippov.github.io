water-milfoil family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːdər ˈmɪlˌfɔɪl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈmɪlˌfɔɪl ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous aquatic or semi-aquatic plants, scientifically known as Haloragaceae, in the order Myrtales
family of aquatic dicotyledonous plants

Examples

Members of the water-milfoil family are commonly found in freshwater habitats around the world.
