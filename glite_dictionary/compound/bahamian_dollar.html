Bahamian dollar

1. NOUN

/ bəˈheɪ.mi.ən ˈdɑː.lɚ / (AmE), / bəˈheɪ.mi.ən ˈdɒl.ə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in the Bahamas, equal to 100 cents
the currency of the Bahamas

Examples

Tourists can exchange US dollars for Bahamian dollars at most banks in Nassau.
The price of the meal was listed in both Bahamian dollars and US dollars.
He collected coins from different countries, including a rare Bahamian dollar from the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - a basic monetary unit in several countries, including the US and Canada
