ice-cream soda

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌkrim ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌkriːm ˈsəʊdə / (BrE)
a cold drink consisting of ice cream floating in a sweet carbonated beverage, usually flavored with syrup
drink of ice cream and soda

Examples

She ordered a chocolate ice-cream soda at the old-fashioned diner.
Nothing beats a cold ice-cream soda on a hot summer day.
The menu features classic drinks like the ice-cream soda and root beer float.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: float - a drink consisting of ice cream floating in soda
