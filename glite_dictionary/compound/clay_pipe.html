clay pipe

1. NOUN

/ kleɪ paɪp / (AmE), / kleɪ paɪp / (BrE)
a pipe, typically for smoking tobacco, made from clay
pipe for smoking, made of clay

Examples

The archaeologist found a clay pipe dating back to the 17th century.
He enjoyed smoking his favorite tobacco from a traditional clay pipe.
Many museums display beautifully decorated clay pipes from the Victorian era.
