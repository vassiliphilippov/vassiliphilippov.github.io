domestic help

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk hɛlp / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk hɛlp / (BrE)
a person employed to perform household chores, such as cleaning and cooking, for pay
a household servant for menial tasks

Examples

She hired a domestic help to assist with the cleaning and cooking.
The domestic help arrives every morning at seven o'clock.
Many families in the city rely on domestic help for daily chores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic worker - a person who works within an employer's household
  • Synonym: servant - a person employed to perform services, especially in a household
  • Antonym: employer - a person who employs someone for domestic help
