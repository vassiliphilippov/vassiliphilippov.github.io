lemon grove

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ɡroʊv / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ɡrəʊv / (BrE)
an area of land where lemon trees are grown, typically for commercial production of lemons
orchard of lemon trees

Examples

We walked through the lemon grove, enjoying the scent of fresh citrus in the air.
The old farmhouse overlooks a lemon grove that stretches to the horizon.
Many local businesses buy their fruit directly from the nearby lemon grove.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchard - an area where fruit trees are grown
