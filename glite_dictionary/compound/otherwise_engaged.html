otherwise engaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌðərˌwaɪz ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈʌðəwaɪz ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
busy or occupied with another commitment, making one unavailable for something else
busy with another commitment

Examples

I'm afraid I'm otherwise engaged and can't attend the meeting tonight.
She wanted to join the call, but was otherwise engaged at that time.
If you are otherwise engaged, feel free to reschedule our appointment.

Synset Examples

She would have come, but she’s otherwise engaged.
I can’t join you; I’m otherwise engaged this evening.
He promised to help, but he’s otherwise engaged right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busy - actively occupied or engaged; not free
  • Antonym: available - free to do something; not occupied
