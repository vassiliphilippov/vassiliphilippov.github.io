zinc spar

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪŋk spɑr / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋk spɑː / (BrE)
A mineral consisting of zinc carbonate, used as an ore of zinc; also known as smithsonite.
a mineral ore of zinc (smithsonite)

Examples

The museum displayed a rare specimen of zinc spar among other mineral samples.
Miners extracted zinc spar from the quarry to be processed for zinc.
Collectors prize zinc spar for its attractive crystal formations and range of colors.

Synset Examples

Smithsonite is a variably colored trigonal mineral which only rarely is found in well formed crystals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smithsonite - a mineral ore of zinc; zinc carbonate
