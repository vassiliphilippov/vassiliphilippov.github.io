shared knowledge

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛrd ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ʃeəd ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
knowledge or information possessed, developed, or understood collectively by a group of people
knowledge held collectively by a group

Examples

Building a strong team depends on fostering shared knowledge among all members.
Effective collaboration in organizations relies heavily on shared knowledge.
Online platforms have made the transfer of shared knowledge easier than ever before.

Synset Examples

The concept of a communiversity promotes shared knowledge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common knowledge - something that is generally known by most people
