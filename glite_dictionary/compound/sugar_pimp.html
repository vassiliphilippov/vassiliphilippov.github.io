sugar pimp

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ pɪmp / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə pɪmp / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who uses charm or money to attract and manage others, often for personal or financial gain
charming or wealthy manipulator for gain
slang

Examples

He acted like a sugar pimp, always showing off his expensive car to impress others.
Some people criticize the way a sugar pimp uses gifts and money to control relationships.
Rumors spread that she was dating a sugar pimp who managed multiple partners for profit.

Synset Examples

He thought he was a sugar pimp, flaunting his wealth.
Being a sugar pimp isn't as glamorous as it sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sugar daddy - an older man who gives money or gifts to a younger person in exchange for companionship or intimacy
