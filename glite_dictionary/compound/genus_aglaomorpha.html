genus Aglaomorpha

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌæɡliəˈmɔːrfə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌaɡliəˈmɔːfə / (BrE)
A genus of epiphytic ferns native to tropical Asia.
a fern genus of tropical Asia

Examples

Botanists have discovered several new species within the genus Aglaomorpha in Southeast Asia.
The genus Aglaomorpha is known for its adaptability to grow on tree trunks in humid forests.
Some members of the genus Aglaomorpha are popular ornamental plants due to their attractive fronds.
