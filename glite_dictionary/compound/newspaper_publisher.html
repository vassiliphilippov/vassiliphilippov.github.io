newspaper publisher

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈpʌblɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈpʌblɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or company that owns and is responsible for the production and distribution of a newspaper
owner or company producing newspapers

Examples

After years of hard work, she became the newspaper publisher for a major daily.
The newspaper publisher announced changes to the editorial policy to boost public trust.
Many aspiring journalists dream of one day becoming a newspaper publisher themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publisher - person or company that prepares and issues books, journals, or other works
