operating statement

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
a financial statement that shows a company's revenues, expenses, and operating results for a specific period
financial summary of operating results

Examples

The company's operating statement revealed a significant increase in quarterly profits.
Accountants use the operating statement to analyze expenses and revenues during the fiscal year.
Before making investments, many analysts carefully review the operating statement of a business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: income statement - a statement showing a company's revenue and expenses over a period; synonym
