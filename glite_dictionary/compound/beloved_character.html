beloved character

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlʌvd ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / bɪˈlʌvɪd ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
A character, especially in literature, film, or other media, who is greatly loved and admired by audiences or other characters.
a much-loved character in stories or media

Examples

The death of a beloved character can evoke a strong emotional response from readers.
Many fans voted Hermione as the most beloved character in the entire series.
His performance transformed a minor role into a truly beloved character on the show.

Synset Examples

Toriel's kindness made her a beloved character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: popular character - a character widely liked or admired by many people
  • Antonym: villain - a character who opposes the hero and is typically disliked
