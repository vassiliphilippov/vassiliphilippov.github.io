string orchestra

1. NOUN

/ strɪŋ ˈɔrkəstrə / (AmE), / strɪŋ ˈɔːkɪstrə / (BrE)
an orchestra consisting only of stringed instruments such as violins, violas, cellos, and double basses
orchestra of only stringed instruments

Examples

The school string orchestra performed beautifully at the winter concert.
She joined the city's string orchestra as a cellist last year.
A string orchestra typically includes violins, violas, cellos, and double basses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestra - a large group of musicians playing together
