turtle soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.t̬əl suːp / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.tl̩ suːp / (BrE)
A soup traditionally made with the flesh of green turtles, often considered a delicacy in some cuisines.
soup made from turtle meat

Examples

My grandmother used to make turtle soup for special occasions when she was young.
The restaurant's famous turtle soup is prepared from a traditional recipe passed down for generations.
Due to conservation concerns, authentic turtle soup is rarely found on modern menus.
