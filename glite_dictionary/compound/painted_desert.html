Painted Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a desert region on a high plateau in northeastern Arizona, known for its colorful, layered rock formations
desert region in northeastern Arizona, USA

Examples

The Painted Desert is famous for its striking bands of red, orange, and purple sediment.
On our road trip through Arizona, we made a detour to visit the Painted Desert.
Many artists have tried to capture the beauty of the Painted Desert on canvas.
