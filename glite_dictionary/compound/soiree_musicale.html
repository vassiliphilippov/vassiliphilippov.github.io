soiree musicale

1. NOUN

/ swɑːˈreɪ mjuːˈzɑːkəl / (AmE), / swɑːˈreɪ mjuːˈzɑːkæl / (BrE)
an evening party or gathering devoted to musical performances
musical evening party

Examples

She received an invitation to a soiree musicale hosted by the city's leading pianist.
The soiree musicale featured performances from several talented young composers.
Attending a soiree musicale was a highlight of their Paris visit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recital - a musical performance, especially by a soloist or small group

Etymology

From French 'soirée musicale' (musical evening), combining 'soirée' (evening) and 'musicale' (musical).
