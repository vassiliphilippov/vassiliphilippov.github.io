wine barrel

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈbɛrəl / (AmE), / waɪn ˈbærəl / (BrE)
a barrel specifically designed for storing and aging wine
barrel for storing wine

Examples

The winery stores its finest vintages in large wine barrels made from French oak.
The scent of aging wine filled the room where dozens of wine barrels lined the walls.
During the tour, we learned how a wine barrel can influence the flavor of the wine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel - a cylindrical container, usually made of wood, used for storing liquids or other substances
