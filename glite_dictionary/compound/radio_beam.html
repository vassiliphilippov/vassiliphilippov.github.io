radio beam

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ biːm / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ biːm / (BrE)
a narrow radio signal transmitted to guide pilots during darkness or poor weather conditions
a guiding narrow radio signal

Examples

Pilots rely on the radio beam to safely land when visibility is low.
The airport's radio beam was out of service due to severe weather.
He tuned the equipment to align with the radio beam before descending.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio signal - an electronic transmission sent as radio waves
