zinc ointment

1. NOUN

/ zɪŋk ˈɔɪnt.mənt / (AmE), / zɪŋk ˈɔɪnt.mənt / (BrE)
an ointment containing zinc, used to treat certain skin conditions such as rashes and minor irritations
ointment containing zinc for skin treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended applying zinc ointment to the affected area twice a day.
Parents often use zinc ointment to soothe their baby's diaper rash.
After getting a mild burn, she reached for the zinc ointment in the medicine cabinet.
