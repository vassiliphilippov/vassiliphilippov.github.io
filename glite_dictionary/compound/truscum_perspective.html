truscum perspective

1. NOUN

/ ˈtruːskʌm pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˈtruːskʌm pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
the belief that only people with gender dysphoria are genuinely transgender
view that only those with dysphoria are truly transgender
informal slang

Examples

Many activists criticize the truscum perspective for being exclusionary within the LGBTQ+ community.
Discussions about the truscum perspective often become heated on social media.
She rejected the truscum perspective in favor of a more inclusive view of transgender identity.

Synset Examples

The truscum perspective can spark intense discussions online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmedicalism - the belief that being transgender requires medical diagnosis or gender dysphoria
  • Antonym: tuccute perspective - the opposing view that being transgender does not require gender dysphoria

Etymology

Blend of 'true' and 'scum'; originated as an internet slang term referring to a particular subset of transgender discourse.
