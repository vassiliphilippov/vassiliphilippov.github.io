Usbek language

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌzˌbɛk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʌzˌbɛk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken primarily by the Uzbek people of Central Asia, especially in Uzbekistan.
Turkic language spoken in Uzbekistan

Examples

She became fluent in Usbek language during her travels in Central Asia.
The university offers a course in Usbek language and literature.
Many traditional stories are preserved in the Usbek language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uzbek language - the Turkic language spoken by the Uzbek

Etymology

From Uzbek (referring to the Uzbek people) + language.
