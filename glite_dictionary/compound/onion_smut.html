onion smut

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən ˌsmʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən ˌsmʌt / (BrE)
A fungal disease affecting onions, characterized by blackish blisters on their scales and leaves, particularly harmful to seedlings.
fungal disease of onions

Examples

The farmer lost half his crop to onion smut this spring.
Seedlings should be monitored for signs of onion smut in early growth stages.
Crop rotation is recommended to prevent the spread of onion smut in the fields.
