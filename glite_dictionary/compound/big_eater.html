big eater

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈitər / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈiːtə / (BrE)
a person who eats large quantities of food
person who eats a lot

Examples

My brother is such a big eater that he often has seconds and thirds at dinner.
At the buffet, being a big eater was definitely an advantage.
Even though she's a big eater, she always finds time to exercise.

Synset Examples

As a big eater, she never leaves food on her plate.
He’s always been a big eater at family dinners.
Being a big eater, he finished the whole pizza himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glutton - a person who eats and drinks excessively
