fullness of flavor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊlnəs əv ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʊlnəs əv ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
the quality of having a rich, complete, and satisfying taste
richness and completeness of taste

Examples

The homemade tomato sauce had a fullness of flavor that reminded her of her grandmother’s cooking.
This aged cheese develops a fullness of flavor not found in younger varieties.
Proper seasoning brings out the fullness of flavor in any dish.

Synset Examples

He appreciated the fullness of flavor in the dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: richness - the quality of being full of strong or pleasing flavor
