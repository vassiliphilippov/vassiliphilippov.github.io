wooden performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdn̩ pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈwʊdn pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an acting style that is stiff, unresponsive, and lacking in emotion or naturalness
stiff, lifeless acting

Examples

Many critics noted the actor’s wooden performance in the film.
Her otherwise engaging character was undermined by a wooden performance during the emotional scenes.
The director feared that a wooden performance would ruin the impact of the play.

Synset Examples

His wooden performance left the audience unimpressed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wooden - stiff, awkward, or lacking emotion in performance or speech
  • Antonym: animated performance - an acting style that is lively and full of energy
