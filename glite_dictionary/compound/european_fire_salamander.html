European fire salamander

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈfaɪr səˌlændər / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈfaɪə səˌlæməndə / (BrE)
a species of large, brightly colored salamander native to central and southern Europe, typically black with yellow markings
a European salamander species, black with yellow spots

Examples

The European fire salamander is known for its striking black and yellow coloring.
After the rain, we found a European fire salamander crawling near the forest edge.
Researchers study the European fire salamander to monitor the health of European woodland habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire salamander - a species of salamander famous for its vivid coloration
