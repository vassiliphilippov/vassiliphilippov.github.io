fly front

1. NOUN

/ flaɪ frʌnt / (AmE), / flaɪ frʌnt / (BrE)
An opening in a garment, especially trousers or a skirt, that is closed by a zipper or buttons hidden under a fold of fabric.
garment opening with hidden fasteners

Examples

The tailor recommended a fly front for a more polished appearance on the trousers.
These jeans feature a fly front with a hidden button closure for a sleek look.
Her skirt has a fly front that conceals the zipper, giving it a clean finish.
