face down

1. ADV

/ feɪs daʊn / (AmE), / feɪs daʊn / (BrE)
With the front surface or side facing downward.
with the front side downward

Examples

He dropped the letter face down on the desk so no one could read it.
During the yoga session, she lay face down on the mat and closed her eyes.
Make sure you place the photograph face down to avoid any scratches on the image.

Synset Examples

He fell face down on the bed, exhausted.
The book lay face down on the table.
She landed face down on the mat during practice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to face up - with the front surface or side facing upward
