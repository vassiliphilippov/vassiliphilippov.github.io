head-scratching

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛdˌskrætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌskrætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing confusion or puzzlement; difficult to understand or explain.
confusing or puzzling

Examples

The committee's decision was truly head-scratching and left everyone guessing at their reasons.
He found the instructions for the device to be unnecessarily head-scratching.
Some of her head-scratching comments made the meeting go off track.

Synset Examples

His head-scratching solution surprised everyone.
The results were so unexpected, it was head-scratching.
Her head-scratching behavior left us all confused.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baffling - impossible to understand; perplexing
  • Synonym: perplexing - difficult to understand; puzzling
  • Antonym: straightforward - easy to understand or simple

Etymology

From the literal act of scratching one's head in puzzlement, used figuratively since the 20th century.
