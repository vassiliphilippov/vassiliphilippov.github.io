lowest common multiple

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊɪst ˈkɑmən ˈmʌltəpəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊɪst ˈkɒmən ˈmʌltɪpl̩ / (BrE)
the smallest number that is exactly divisible by each member of a given set of numbers
smallest shared multiple (math)

Examples

To add fractions, first find the lowest common multiple of their denominators.
Twelve is the lowest common multiple of 3 and 4.
We solved the problem by calculating the lowest common multiple of the given numbers.

Synset Examples

the least common multiple of 12 and 18 is 36

Related Terms

  • Synonym: least common multiple - the smallest number that each of two or more numbers divides without remainder
