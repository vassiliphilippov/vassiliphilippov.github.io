International Development Association

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.tərˈnæʃ.ə.nəl dɪˌvɛl.əpˈmɛnt əˌsoʊ.siˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈnæʃ.ə.nəl dɪˌvɛl.əpˈmɛnt əˌsəʊ.siˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations, affiliated with the World Bank, that provides low-interest loans and grants to developing countries
agency of the UN and World Bank for development aid

Examples

The International Development Association supports projects to improve education and health in low-income countries.
Several African nations have received funding from the International Development Association for infrastructure development.
As part of the World Bank Group, the International Development Association focuses on the world's poorest countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: World Bank - an international financial institution that provides loans to countries for development projects
