look-alike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʊk əˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʊk əˌlaɪk / (BrE)
very similar in appearance to someone or something else; closely resembling another.
closely resembling another

Examples

The celebrity hired a look-alike to confuse the paparazzi.
Those two models are so similar they could be look-alikes.
The painting is a look-alike of the original masterpiece.

Synset Examples

they have look-alike cars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a person who looks very similar to another, especially an actor's stand-in
  • Antonym: original - the person or thing that is being imitated or resembles by a look-alike
