toll taker

1. NOUN

/ toʊl ˈteɪkɚ / (AmE), / təʊl ˈteɪkə / (BrE)
a person employed to collect tolls at a toll booth or toll plaza
person who collects tolls

Examples

The toll taker greeted each driver with a smile as they paid their fee.
After midnight, the toll taker closed the booth for the night shift to begin.
During rush hour, the toll taker worked quickly to keep the line moving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toll collector - a person who collects tolls; alternative term
