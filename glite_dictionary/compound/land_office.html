land office

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænd ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
a government office where the administration and sale of public land is managed
government office for public land affairs

Examples

The surveyor submitted the documents to the land office for processing.
Records at the land office date back to the 19th century.
After the territory opened for settlement, the land office saw a surge in activity.
