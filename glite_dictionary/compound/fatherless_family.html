fatherless family

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑðərləs ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðələs ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family or household in which the father is absent or not present.
family without a father

Examples

The charity aims to offer specialized support to fatherless families in urban areas.
Growing up in a fatherless family shaped his views on responsibility and independence.
Policy makers are concerned about the rising number of fatherless families in some communities.

Synset Examples

The charity focuses on helping fatherless families thrive.
He grew up in a fatherless household, facing many challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single-parent family - family headed by only one parent
  • Synonym: fatherless - lacking a father; without a father present
  • Antonym: two-parent family - family with both parents present
