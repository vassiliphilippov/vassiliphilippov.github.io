blow-by-blow

1. ADJ

/ bloʊ baɪ bloʊ / (AmE), / bləʊ baɪ bləʊ / (BrE)
Giving a detailed and sequential account of an event, describing each step or stage as it happens.
very detailed and sequential (account)

Examples

She gave a blow-by-blow description of the entire meeting to her colleague.
The documentary offers a blow-by-blow account of the historic race.
He wrote a blow-by-blow report of the football match for the local newspaper.

Synset Examples

a blow-by-blow account of the movie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detailed - including many facts or details; thorough
