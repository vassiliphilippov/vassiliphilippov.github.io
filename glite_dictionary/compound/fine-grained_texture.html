fine-grained texture

1. NOUN

/ faɪn ɡreɪnd ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / faɪn ɡreɪnd ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
a surface quality characterized by small, closely packed particles, giving it a smooth and even feel or appearance
smooth, even surface with small grains

Examples

The fine-grained texture of marble makes it ideal for intricate sculptures.
This flour's fine-grained texture helps create a light, fluffy cake.
Connoisseurs appreciate the fine-grained texture of premium dark chocolate.

Synset Examples

The fine-grained texture of the chocolate made it melt quickly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coarse texture - a rough surface quality with large, irregular particles or grains
  • Synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
