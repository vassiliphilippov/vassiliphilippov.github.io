wood-burning

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʊd ˌbɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊd ˌbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a device or appliance) fueled by wood, especially for heating or cooking.
fueled by wood

Examples

They installed a wood-burning stove to heat their cabin during the winter.
The cozy room featured a wood-burning fireplace that gave off a pleasant aroma of pine.
Many rural homes rely on wood-burning heaters for an affordable source of warmth.

Synset Examples

a wood-burning stove

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gas-fired - (of a device) fueled by gas
  • Synonym: coal-burning - (of a device) fueled by coal
