percentage sign

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsɛntɪdʒ saɪn / (AmE), / pəˈsɛntɪdʒ sʌɪn / (BrE)
the symbol '%' used to indicate that a number is expressed as a proportion out of 100
the symbol '%' for per cent

Examples

On most keyboards, the percentage sign is located above the number 5.
She scored ninety-five percentage sign on her final exam, which was her highest grade ever.
The percentage sign is used frequently in statistics and finance to denote proportions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - out of every hundred
