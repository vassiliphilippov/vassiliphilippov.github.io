saucer magnolia

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɚ mægˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsə mægˈnəʊlɪə / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub or small tree with large, open pink to purple flowers, native to Asia and widely grown as an ornamental plant
ornamental tree with large pink-purple flowers

Examples

A mature saucer magnolia can be the centerpiece of a spring garden, thanks to its showy blossoms.
The saucer magnolia in our yard dropped its petals after a sudden frost last week.
Botanists often recommend saucer magnolia for urban landscaping due to its striking flowers and moderate size.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnolia - any tree or shrub of the genus Magnolia
