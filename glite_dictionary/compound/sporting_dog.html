sporting dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔɹ.tɪŋ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈspɔː.tɪŋ dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog bred and trained to assist hunters in shooting game, especially by retrieving or pointing
dog trained to assist hunters

Examples

The sporting dog tracked the scent of the pheasant through the tall grass.
Many hunters rely on a sporting dog for retrieving game birds during the hunting season.
She adopted a sporting dog because of its intelligence and energetic personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gundog - a dog trained to hunt or retrieve game
