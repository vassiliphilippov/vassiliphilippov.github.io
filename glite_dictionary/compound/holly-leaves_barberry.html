holly-leaves barberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑli liz ˈbɑrbɛri / (AmE), / ˈhɒli liːvz ˈbɑːbəri / (BrE)
an ornamental evergreen shrub native to the Pacific coast of North America, with dark green pinnate leaves, yellow flowers, and blue-black berries
evergreen shrub with yellow flowers and blue-black berries

Examples

The holly-leaves barberry is often planted in gardens for its decorative foliage and bright berries.
I bought a holly-leaves barberry to add some color to my backyard.
Birds are attracted to the blue-black berries of the holly-leaves barberry in autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barberry - a shrub with thorny branches and edible red berries
