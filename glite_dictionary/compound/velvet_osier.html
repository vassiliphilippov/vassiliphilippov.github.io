velvet osier

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛlvət ˈoʊʒər / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt ˈəʊzɪə / (BrE)
a type of willow with long, flexible twigs, commonly used in basketry
willow used in basketry

Examples

The craftsman selected several velvet osier branches to weave a strong and beautiful basket.
Many rural communities cultivate velvet osier for use in traditional basket-making.
In spring, the supple shoots of velvet osier are harvested and soaked before weaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: osier - a willow tree or shrub with flexible twigs used in wickerwork
