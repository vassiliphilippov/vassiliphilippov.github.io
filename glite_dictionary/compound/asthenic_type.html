asthenic type

1. NOUN

/ æsˈθɛnɪk taɪp / (AmE), / æsˈθiːnɪk taɪp / (BrE)
A body type characterized by a slender, weak, and lightweight build, often used in medical or physiological classification.
slender, weak body type
medical

Examples

The patient was described as having an asthenic type physique during the medical examination.
Athletes with an asthenic type may excel in endurance sports due to their light frame.
Medical textbooks often refer to the asthenic type when discussing variations in human body structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ectomorph - a body type with a slim, linear appearance

Etymology

From Greek asthenēs (weak) + type.
