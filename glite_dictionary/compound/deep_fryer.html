deep fryer

1. NOUN

/ dip ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / diːp ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance used for cooking food by immersing it completely in hot oil for deep frying.
kitchen appliance for deep frying

Examples

She prepared crispy chicken wings in the deep fryer for the party.
Most restaurants use a commercial deep fryer to make French fries.
After using the deep fryer, remember to clean out the old oil.

Synset Examples

Deep fryers are used for cooking many fast foods, and making them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fryer - a container or appliance for frying food
  • Degree Lower: air fryer - a kitchen appliance that cooks food by circulating hot air, often as a healthier alternative to deep frying
