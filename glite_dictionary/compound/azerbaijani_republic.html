Azerbaijani Republic

1. PROPN

/ ˌæzərbaɪˈdʒɑni rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˌazəbʌɪˈdʒɑːni rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name for Azerbaijan, a landlocked country in southwestern Asia, formerly part of the Soviet Union
country in southwestern Asia, former Soviet republic

Examples

The Azerbaijani Republic declared its independence from the Soviet Union in 1991.
Oil exports are a major part of the economy in the Azerbaijani Republic.
Diplomatic relations between the Azerbaijani Republic and its neighbors have improved in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Azerbaijan - country in southwestern Asia, also known as Azerbaijani Republic

Etymology

From 'Azerbaijani', relating to Azerbaijan, and 'Republic', denoting its form of government.
