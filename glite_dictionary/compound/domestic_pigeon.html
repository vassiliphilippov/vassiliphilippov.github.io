domestic pigeon

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈpɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a pigeon (Columba livia domestica) that has been tamed and bred by humans, typically for food, sport, or as a pet
a tamed, bred pigeon kept by humans

Examples

The domestic pigeon has many color varieties thanks to centuries of selective breeding.
Some people race domestic pigeons as a popular hobby around the world.
The domestic pigeon can be trained to deliver messages over long distances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock dove - wild ancestor species of the domestic pigeon
