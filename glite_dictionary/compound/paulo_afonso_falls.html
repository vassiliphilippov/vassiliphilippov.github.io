Paulo Afonso Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˌpaʊloʊ ɑːˈfɔnsoʊ fɔlz / (AmE), / ˌpaʊləʊ ɑːˈfɒnsəʊ fɔːlz / (BrE)
a large and well-known waterfall on the São Francisco River in northeastern Brazil
waterfall in northeastern Brazil

Examples

The Paulo Afonso Falls are an important source of hydroelectric power for the region.
Many tourists visit Paulo Afonso Falls each year to witness its dramatic cascades.
The beauty of Paulo Afonso Falls is often compared to that of other great waterfalls around the world.

Etymology

Named after the nearby town of Paulo Afonso, Brazil; 'falls' refers to a waterfall.
