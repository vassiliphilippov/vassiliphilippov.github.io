genus Pilea

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːliə / (AmE), / ˈpiːliə / (BrE)
A genus of low-growing tropical perennial plants grown chiefly for their non-stinging foliage.
a genus of tropical perennial plants

Examples

The genus Pilea is popular among indoor plant enthusiasts for its attractive leaves.
Several species within the genus Pilea are valued for their ease of care and ornamental beauty.
Botanists recently discovered a new plant belonging to the genus Pilea in South America.

Etymology

From Latin 'Pilea', the genus name given by Linnaeus.
