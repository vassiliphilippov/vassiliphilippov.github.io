folk singer

1. NOUN

/ foʊk ˈsɪŋər / (AmE), / fəʊk ˈsɪŋə / (BrE)
a singer who performs folk songs, often drawing on traditional music from a particular culture or region
singer who performs folk songs

Examples

The folk singer captivated the audience with her haunting melodies and heartfelt lyrics.
He became a well-known folk singer during the 1960s revival of traditional music.
Local music festivals often feature at least one folk singer performing old ballads and stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk music - traditional music of a community or nation
