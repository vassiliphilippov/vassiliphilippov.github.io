deuced tired

1. ADJ

/ ˈdusɪd ˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈdjuːsɪd ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; utterly exhausted.
extremely tired

Examples

After working a double shift, he felt deuced tired and just wanted to sleep.
By midnight, everyone at the party looked deuced tired after so much dancing.
She admitted she was deuced tired and needed a break from studying.

Synset Examples

She was deuced tired after the long hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired; drained of energy

Etymology

From 'deuced' (an old euphemism for 'devilish' or 'damned'), used for emphasis.
