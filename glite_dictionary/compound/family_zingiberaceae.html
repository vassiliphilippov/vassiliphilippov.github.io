family Zingiberaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli zɪnˌdʒɪbəˈreɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli zɪnˌdʒɪbəˈreɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of tropical flowering plants, including ginger and related species, within the order Zingiberales.
plant family including ginger and relatives

Examples

The family Zingiberaceae includes important spice plants such as ginger and turmeric.
Many ornamental plants in the family Zingiberaceae are popular in tropical gardens.
Botanists study the family Zingiberaceae to learn about the diversity of monocot plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zingiber - the type genus of the ginger family

Etymology

From New Latin, from 'Zingiber', the type genus (from Greek zingiberis, meaning 'ginger').
