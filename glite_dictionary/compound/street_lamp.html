street lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrit læmp / (AmE), / ˈstriːt læmp / (BrE)
A lamp mounted on a post, used for lighting public streets.
lamp for lighting streets

Examples

The street lamp outside my house was broken for several days.
A moth fluttered around the street lamp late at night.
City workers replaced the old street lamps with energy-efficient LED models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetlight - a light illuminating a street, especially one mounted on a pole or post
  • Synonym: lamppost - a post supporting a street lamp
