easy to understand

1. ADJ

/ ˈizi tə ˌʌndɚˈstænd / (AmE), / ˈiːzi tə ˌʌndəˈstænd / (BrE)
simple and clear; not difficult to comprehend or interpret
simple and clear; not difficult to comprehend

Examples

The instructions were written in language that was easy to understand.
He prefers textbooks that are easy to understand over those filled with jargon.
Her summary made a complex topic easy to understand for everyone.

Synset Examples

Her explanation was simple and easy to understand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensible - able to be understood; intelligible
  • Antonym: incomprehensible - impossible or very difficult to understand
