race meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪs ˌmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪs ˌmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an event at which a series of horse races are held at the same venue on a particular date
event with series of horse races

Examples

We're planning to attend the race meeting at Ascot this summer.
The annual race meeting attracted visitors from all over the country.
Many people dress up for a race meeting, making it a major social occasion.

Synset Examples

the Epsom race meeting was an important social event

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horse race - a race between horses ridden by jockeys
