technical grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛknɪkəl ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkəl ɡreɪd / (BrE)
(of a chemical substance) having a level of purity sufficient for industrial or technical use but containing small amounts of impurities.
industrial-level purity; not highly pure

Examples

The laboratory ordered technical grade acetone for cleaning purposes.
This fertilizer contains technical grade chemicals, which are not pure enough for food production.
Technical grade solvents are often used in manufacturing but not in pharmaceuticals.

Synset Examples

technical-grade sulfuric acid

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: reagent grade - chemical grade of higher purity, suitable for laboratory and analytical use
