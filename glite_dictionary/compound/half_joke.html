half joke

1. NOUN

/ hæf dʒoʊk / (AmE), / hɑːf dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A statement that is partly humorous and partly serious.
partly humorous, partly serious statement

Examples

He made a half joke about quitting his job, but everyone knew he was serious.
Her comment started as a half joke, but it sparked a real discussion.
Sometimes a half joke reveals more truth than a straightforward statement.

Synset Examples

He made a half joke about working late.
Her comment was a half joke, but it got everyone thinking.
I said it as a half joke, but I meant it.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: joke - something said or done to provoke laughter
  • Synonym: quip - a witty or clever remark
