doorhandle

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌhændl / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌhændl / (BrE)
A device, often in the form of a knob or lever, used to release the catch and open a door.
device for opening a door
british

Examples

She turned the doorhandle and stepped quietly into the room.
The doorhandle was loose, so he tightened the screws.
In older houses, you might find a brass doorhandle on every door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorknob - a knob-shaped handle used to open a door
