college sweetheart

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪd͡ʒ ˈswitˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ ˈswiːthɑːt / (BrE)
a person with whom one has a romantic relationship during college, usually implying lasting affection or significant importance
romantic partner from college

Examples

After ten years apart, she reunited with her college sweetheart at a class reunion.
He proposed to his college sweetheart during their senior year.
Many people remember their college sweetheart fondly for years after graduation.

Synset Examples

She hopes to marry her college sweetheart soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high school sweetheart - romantic partner from high school
