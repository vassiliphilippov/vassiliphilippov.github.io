Egyptian pound

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈdʒɪpʃən paʊnd / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən paʊnd / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Egypt, equal to 100 piasters
the currency of Egypt

Examples

The price of the ticket was fifty Egyptian pounds.
Tourists usually exchange dollars for Egyptian pounds when they arrive in Cairo.
The value of the Egyptian pound has fluctuated significantly in recent years.
