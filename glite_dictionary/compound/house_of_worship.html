house of worship

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaʊs əv ˈwɜːrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌhaʊs əv ˈwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
A building where people gather for religious services or prayer.
building for religious worship

Examples

Each house of worship in the city opened its doors for the interfaith ceremony.
People traveled from distant towns to attend the festival at the local house of worship.
The new house of worship was designed to accommodate hundreds of congregants for prayer and celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: place of worship - a building dedicated to religious ceremonies and prayer
