hay conditioner

1. NOUN

/ heɪ kənˈdɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / heɪ kənˈdɪʃənə / (BrE)
a farm machine that crushes or crimps newly cut hay to speed up and make more uniform the drying process
farm machine for drying hay faster

Examples

The farmer used a hay conditioner to ensure the forage dried more quickly before baling.
Modern hay conditioners can improve both the quality and efficiency of the haymaking process.
He repaired the old hay conditioner before the start of the harvest season.
