well-padded shoes

1. NOUN

/ wɛl ˈpædɪd ʃuz / (AmE), / wɛl ˈpædɪd ʃuːz / (BrE)
shoes with thick or ample cushioning designed for extra comfort or support
shoes with extra cushioning

Examples

After buying well-padded shoes, Maria felt less tired after her shift.
Many athletes choose well-padded shoes to reduce the impact on their joints.
His doctor recommended well-padded shoes for better arch support.

Synset Examples
