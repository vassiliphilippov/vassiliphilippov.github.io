round table

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊnd ˌteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a meeting at which participants discuss a particular topic as equals, often to exchange views and ideas
discussion meeting among equals

Examples

The conference concluded with a round table on climate change and sustainable development.
She was invited to participate in a round table about women's leadership in business.
The round table allowed experts from different fields to share their perspectives openly.

Synset Examples

a roundtable on the future of computing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panel discussion - a formal discussion among a group of speakers in front of an audience

Etymology

Named for the legendary table of King Arthur, which symbolized equality among those seated at it.
