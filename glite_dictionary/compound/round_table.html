Round Table

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
(in Arthurian legend) the large circular table around which King Arthur and his knights assembled, symbolizing equality among those seated.
legendary table of King Arthur's knights

Examples

The knights gathered at the Round Table to discuss their next quest.
Many stories have been told about the legendary Round Table of Camelot.
Historians debate whether King Arthur's Round Table ever existed in reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: King Arthur - legendary British leader and central figure in Arthurian legend
  • Synonym: knight - a soldier in service to a king, especially in the Middle Ages
