black-stemmed spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk ˈstɛmd ˈspliːnˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˈstɛmd ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A fern native to tropical America, ranging from the southern United States to the West Indies and Mexico to Brazil.
tropical American fern species

Examples

The black-stemmed spleenwort thrives in humid, shaded forests across Central and South America.
We discovered a patch of black-stemmed spleenwort growing along the rocky riverbank.
Botanists often study the black-stemmed spleenwort for its unique adaptation to tropical climates.
