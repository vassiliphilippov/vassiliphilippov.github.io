general staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnərəl stæf / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl stɑːf / (BrE)
a group of military officers who assist a senior military leader in planning and directing overall military policy and operations
military planning officers

Examples

The general staff developed a new strategy to address the changing battlefield conditions.
After years of service, she was appointed to the general staff for her expertise in military logistics.
The army's general staff coordinated operations with allied forces during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staff - a group of officers assisting a commander
