fairy lantern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri ˌlæntɚn / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌlæntən / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Calochortus, characterized by their egg-shaped flowers
plant with egg-shaped flowers (Calochortus genus)

Examples

The fairy lantern blooms in the spring, adding delicate beauty to California meadows.
Botanists admired the rare fairy lantern they discovered along the woodland trail.
Gardeners are drawn to the fairy lantern for its unique, egg-shaped blossoms.
