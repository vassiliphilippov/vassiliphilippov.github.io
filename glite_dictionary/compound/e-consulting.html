e-consulting

1. NOUN

/ iː kənˈsʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / iː kənˈsʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
the business of providing professional advice and services such as web design, marketing, or IT solutions to clients remotely via the Internet
remote consulting services delivered online

Examples

The company chose to invest in e-consulting to streamline its online operations and reach a wider audience.
Many startups rely on e-consulting firms for advice about digital marketing and web development.
During the pandemic, e-consulting became essential for businesses transitioning to remote work.

Synset Examples

E-consulting for PCI program development offers a bridge to the experience and know-how that rural and community hospitals need in order to create and develop a sound program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consulting - the business of giving expert advice to other professionals

Etymology

Formed from 'e-' (electronic) and 'consulting' to denote consulting services delivered electronically.
