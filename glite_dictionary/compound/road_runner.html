road runner

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd ˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd ˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a person who participates in long-distance running races, especially marathons, on roads
long-distance race participant (on roads)

Examples

Many road runners gather at dawn to train for the upcoming marathon.
As a dedicated road runner, she spends most weekends participating in local races.
The community center holds a weekly club for beginner road runners to improve their endurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marathoner - a person who runs marathons
  • Antonym: sprinter - a person who runs short-distance races
