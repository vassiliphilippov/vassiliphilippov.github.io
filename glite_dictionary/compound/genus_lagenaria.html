genus Lagenaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈʤiːnəs læˈʤɛnəriə / (AmE), / ˈʤiːnəs læˈɡɛnəriə / (BrE)
A genus of climbing or trailing plants in the gourd family, whose species include the bottle gourd.
genus of plants including bottle gourds

Examples

The genus Lagenaria contains several species, most notably those producing bottle gourds.
Members of the genus Lagenaria are commonly cultivated for their distinctive hard-shelled fruits.
Botanists often study the genus Lagenaria due to its agricultural and cultural importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle gourd - a plant species in genus Lagenaria, grown for its edible fruit or as a container

Etymology

From Latin 'Lagenaria', from 'lagena' meaning 'bottle'.
