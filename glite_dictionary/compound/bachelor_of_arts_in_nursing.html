Bachelor of Arts in Nursing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætʃələr əv ɑrts ɪn ˈnɝsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbatʃələ əv ɑːts ɪn ˈnɜːsɪŋ / (BrE)
An undergraduate academic degree awarded for completing a course of study in nursing, typically combining arts and science curricula.
undergraduate degree in nursing

Examples

After earning her Bachelor of Arts in Nursing, Maria applied to several hospitals for her first job.
The university offers a Bachelor of Arts in Nursing with clinical placements in top medical centers.
He proudly displayed his Bachelor of Arts in Nursing diploma at his graduation party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bachelor of Science in Nursing - undergraduate degree in nursing, emphasizing science
