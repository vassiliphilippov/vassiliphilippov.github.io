pookie bear

1. NOUN

/ ˈpuːki bɛr / (AmE), / ˈpuːki beə / (BrE)
A term of endearment used to refer to someone you love or care for, often used between romantic partners or towards children.
term of endearment for a loved one
informal

Examples

Every time he came home, she greeted him with a hug and called him her pookie bear.
Don't be upset, pookie bear, everything will be okay.
He blushed when his girlfriend called him pookie bear in front of his friends.

Synset Examples

She called him her little pookie bear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honey - term of endearment for a loved one
  • Synonym: sweetie - term of endearment for someone you love
