expressive style

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsprɛsɪv staɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsɪv staɪl / (BrE)
a distinctive way of expressing ideas, emotions, or information, typically characteristic of a person, group, or period, especially in language, art, or music
distinctive manner of expression

Examples

Her expressive style of painting set her apart from other artists of her era.
The author's expressive style made the novel deeply moving and memorable.
You can recognize each composer's expressive style by the way they use rhythm and melody.

Synset Examples

all the reporters were expected to adopt the style of the newspaper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: style - a particular manner of doing or presenting things, especially in art or literature
