afterwash difference

1. NOUN

/ ˈæftəˌwɑʃ ˈdɪfɚəns / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌwɒʃ ˈdɪf.ər.əns / (BrE)
the change in appearance, especially shine, that is noticeable after washing something
change in shine after washing

Examples

The afterwash difference was remarkable when she polished the car after cleaning it.
A quality detergent can make a significant afterwash difference in your clothes' brightness.
He took pride in the afterwash difference he achieved on his old motorcycle.
