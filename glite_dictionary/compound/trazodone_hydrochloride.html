trazodone hydrochloride

1. NOUN

/ træzəˌdoʊn haɪˌdrɒklaɪraɪd / (AmE), / ˈtrazədəʊn hʌɪdrɒklʌɪraɪd / (BrE)
A drug used as an oral antidepressant and sedative, especially under the trade name Desyrel.
antidepressant and sedative drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed trazodone hydrochloride to help with her insomnia and depression.
Some patients experience drowsiness as a side effect of trazodone hydrochloride.
Trazodone hydrochloride is often used as a sedative for people who have trouble sleeping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desyrel - trade name for trazodone hydrochloride, an antidepressant drug
  • Synonym: antidepressant - drug used to alleviate symptoms of depression
