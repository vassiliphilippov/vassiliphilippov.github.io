moustache cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.tæʃ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.tɑːʃ ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a drinking cup with a bar inside the rim designed to keep a man's moustache out of the drink
cup with a moustache guard

Examples

My grandfather owns a rare Victorian moustache cup with a floral pattern.
The moustache cup was invented to help men enjoy hot beverages without getting their moustaches wet.
Antique collectors often search for a beautiful moustache cup to add to their collections.
