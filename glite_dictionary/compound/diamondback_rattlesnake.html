diamondback rattlesnake

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪəməndˌbæk ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəməndˌbæk ˈratəlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a large, venomous rattlesnake native to North America, notable for its diamond-shaped markings along its back
large venomous North American rattlesnake

Examples

A diamondback rattlesnake can deliver a potentially lethal bite if provoked.
Hikers spotted a diamondback rattlesnake basking in the sun on the trail.
The zoo houses a large diamondback rattlesnake in its reptile exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rattlesnake - any venomous snake with a rattle at the end of its tail, native to the Americas
