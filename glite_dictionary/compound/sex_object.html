sex object

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / seks ˈɒbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
a person regarded only as an object of sexual desire or gratification, rather than as an individual
person seen only as object of sexual desire

Examples

Many people criticize media for portraying women as mere sex objects.
He resented being treated like a sex object rather than a real person.
Some advertisements reduce men to sex objects, focusing only on their appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex symbol - person widely regarded as sexually attractive to the public
