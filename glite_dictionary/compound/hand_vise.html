hand vise

1. NOUN

/ hænd vaɪs / (AmE), / hænd vaɪs / (BrE)
a small tool with jaws operated by a screw, designed to be held in the hand for holding small objects securely during work.
small handheld clamping tool

Examples

The jeweler used a hand vise to hold the tiny ring steady while he worked.
You will need a hand vise to grip the wire tightly during repair.
She adjusted the hand vise before filing the metal piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vise - a clamping tool with two jaws used to hold an object firmly
