sense of community

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / sɛns əv kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
a feeling of belonging and connection to a group or society
feeling of belonging in a group

Examples

Volunteering at the local shelter gave her a strong sense of community.
A thriving neighborhood often fosters a sense of community among residents.
The annual festival helps build a sense of community within the town.

Synset Examples

Mosqueing provides a sense of community and belonging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belonging - feeling of being accepted or part of a group
