Eastern Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈiːstɚn ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a desert region in Egypt located between the Nile River and the Red Sea
desert in Egypt between Nile & Red Sea

Examples

The Eastern Desert is known for its rugged mountains and mineral resources.
Ancient trade routes once crossed the Eastern Desert to connect the Nile with the Red Sea.
Archaeologists have discovered ancient inscriptions in caves throughout the Eastern Desert.
