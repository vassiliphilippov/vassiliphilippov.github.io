yellow hornet

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ ˈhɔr.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˈhɔː.nɪt / (BrE)
A small, social wasp with yellow markings, commonly nesting in the ground.
a small yellow-marked social wasp

Examples

A yellow hornet built its nest near the back porch, which made everyone cautious.
If you spot a yellow hornet, it's best not to disturb its ground nest.
The sting of a yellow hornet can be quite painful if provoked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hornet - a large stinging wasp
