to stand on one leg

1. VERB

/ stænd ɑn wʌn lɛɡ / (AmE), / stænd ɒn wʌn lɛɡ / (BrE)
to remain upright while balanced on a single leg, with the other leg raised off the ground
balance upright on one leg

Examples

She tried to stand on one leg during yoga class but quickly lost her balance.
Flamingos are famous for their ability to stand on one leg for hours.
The doctor asked him to stand on one leg to test his coordination.

Synset Examples

She stood on one leg to balance better.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stand - to be in an upright position supported by one's feet
