green spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
a small fern with slender green fronds, commonly found in cool regions of the Northern Hemisphere
a small fern of cool regions

Examples

During our hike, we spotted several green spleenwort plants growing on the shaded rocks.
The green spleenwort is admired for its delicate, bright green fronds.
Botanists often study green spleenwort to better understand fern habitats in cold environments.
