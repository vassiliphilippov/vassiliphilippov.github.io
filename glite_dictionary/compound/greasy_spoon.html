greasy spoon

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡri.si ˌspun / (AmE), / ˈɡriː.si ˌspuːn / (BrE)
A small, inexpensive restaurant that typically serves fried or grilled foods and simple meals.
cheap restaurant serving fried food
informal

Examples

We stopped at a greasy spoon for breakfast on our road trip.
There's nothing like a classic greasy spoon to get a real English fry-up.
Most greasy spoons are known more for their charm than for their gourmet food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diner - an informal restaurant where people sit at long counters

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡrisi ˈspun / (AmE), / ˈɡriːzi ˈspuːn / (BrE)
a small, inexpensive restaurant or cafe, typically with a casual atmosphere and offering simple, often fried, food
inexpensive, informal restaurant or cafe

Examples

After a long drive, we stopped at a greasy spoon for burgers and coffee.
Many students gather at the local greasy spoon to grab a cheap breakfast before class.
The best fish and chips I’ve ever had were at a tiny greasy spoon by the harbor.

Synset Examples

We had breakfast at a charming greasy spoon.
I love visiting that old greasy spoon on weekends.
The greasy spoon diner serves the best pancakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diner - a small, informal restaurant serving inexpensive meals

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'greasy' (covered with or resembling grease) and 'spoon' (as an eating utensil), reflecting the cheap, often fried food and casual setting.
