beer mat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr mæt / (AmE), / ˈbɪə mæt / (BrE)
A small mat placed under a glass of beer to protect the surface from drips and spills.
mat for placing under a beer glass

Examples

He set his pint on a beer mat to avoid leaving rings on the table.
The bartender handed me a beer mat along with my drink.
A collection of vintage beer mats adorned the wall of the pub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coaster - a small mat for placing under cups or glasses
