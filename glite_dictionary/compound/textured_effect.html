textured effect

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛkstʃɚd ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃəd ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
a visual quality on a surface characterized by variations or patterns that simulate the feel or appearance of texture
visual quality simulating texture

Examples

The artist skillfully created a textured effect on the canvas using a palette knife.
The textured effect of the wallpaper made the room feel warmer and more inviting.
To achieve a rustic look, apply a glaze to the wall for a subtle textured effect.

Synset Examples

The artist used a dauber to create textured effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: texture - the feel, appearance, or consistency of a surface
