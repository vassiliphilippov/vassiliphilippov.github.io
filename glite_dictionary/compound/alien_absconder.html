alien absconder

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.li.ən æbˈskɑn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.ən æbˈskɒn.də / (BrE)
A foreign national who remains in the United States after being ordered deported by an immigration judge, thereby becoming a fugitive.
deported foreign national evading removal
legal american

Examples

The government launched a new program targeting alien absconders across several major cities.
Hundreds of alien absconders have been located and detained this year.
Legal advocates argue that the rights of alien absconders must still be protected during enforcement actions.

Synset Examples

the government has categorized more than 320,000 foreigners as alien absconders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fugitive - a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution
