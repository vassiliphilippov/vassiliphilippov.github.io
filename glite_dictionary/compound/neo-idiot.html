neo-idiot

1. NOUN

/ ˌniː.oʊˈɪdi.ət / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊˈɪdi.ət / (BrE)
A person regarded as foolish or ignorant in a modern context, especially in relation to contemporary trends or technology.
a modern fool or ignorant person
informal

Examples

The comedian mocked internet trolls as the new breed of neo-idiot.
Some people accuse reality TV stars of representing today's neo-idiot culture.
Her selfie obsession led critics to label her a neo-idiot.

Synset Examples

The article criticized the rise of the neo-idiot.
His comments revealed him to be a neo-idiot.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person

Etymology

From Greek 'neo-' (new) + 'idiot' (foolish person).
