purple mullein

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈmʌlɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈmʌlɪn / (BrE)
A Eurasian species of mullein with showy purple or pink flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant.
Eurasian mullein with purple or pink flowers

Examples

The purple mullein adds a splash of color to the garden with its tall flower spikes.
Gardeners often choose purple mullein for borders because of its vibrant blooms.
In some regions, purple mullein naturalizes easily and returns each year.
