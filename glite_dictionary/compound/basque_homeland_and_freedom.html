Basque Homeland and Freedom

1. NOUN

/ bæsk ˈhoʊmlænd ənd frɪˈdʌm / (AmE), / bask ˈhəʊmlənd ənd ˈfriːdəm / (BrE)
a Basque nationalist militant group founded in 1959, known for seeking independence for the Basque Country through acts of violence and terrorism.
