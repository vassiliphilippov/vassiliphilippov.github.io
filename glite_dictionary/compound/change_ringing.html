change ringing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ ˌrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ ˌrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or practice of ringing a set of tuned bells in a continuously varying sequence according to mathematical patterns
method of ringing bells in changing sequences

Examples

The church's tower was filled with the sound of change ringing on Sunday morning.
He joined a society dedicated to the ancient art of change ringing.
Learning change ringing requires both musical sense and mathematical skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bell-ringing - the act of ringing bells, especially in a church

Etymology

From the word 'change' (sequence) and 'ringing'; established in English bell-ringing tradition.
