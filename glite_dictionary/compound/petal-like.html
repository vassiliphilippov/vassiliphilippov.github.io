petal-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛt̬l̩ laɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛtl laɪk / (BrE)
having the appearance or qualities of a petal; resembling a petal in form or texture.
resembling a petal

Examples

The dancer wore a dress with petal-like layers that swirled gracefully as she moved.
Under the microscope, the fungus displayed petal-like structures around its center.
The designer created lamps with petal-like patterns that mimicked the softness of real flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petaline - of or relating to petals; resembling a petal
