pudendal slit

1. NOUN

/ pjuːˈdɛndl̩ slɪt / (AmE), / pjuːˈdɛndl̩ slɪt / (BrE)
the fissure or opening between the labia majora in the female external genitalia
anatomical opening between labia majora
medical

Examples

The doctor carefully examined the pudendal slit during the medical check-up.
Some species of mammals have differences in the structure of the pudendal slit.
An injury to the pudendal slit can require medical attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulva - the external female genital organs

Etymology

From Latin 'pudenda' (external genitals) and 'slit' (narrow opening).
