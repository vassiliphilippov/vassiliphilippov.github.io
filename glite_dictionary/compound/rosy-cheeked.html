rosy-cheeked

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊzi tʃikt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi tʃiːkt / (BrE)
(of a person) having cheeks that are pinkish or red, typically as a sign of health or freshness.
having pink or red, healthy-looking cheeks

Examples

The rosy-cheeked children played outside in the crisp winter air.
She was a rosy-cheeked woman, always smiling and full of life.
After spending a few minutes in the cold, his face became rosy-cheeked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flushed - having a pink or red color in the face, often from emotion or health
  • Antonym: pale - lacking color in the cheeks or face
