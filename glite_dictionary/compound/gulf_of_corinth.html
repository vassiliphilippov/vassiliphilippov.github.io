Gulf of Corinth

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʌlf əv kəˈrɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv kəˈrɪnθ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Ionian Sea located between central Greece and the Peloponnesus
inlet of the Ionian Sea in Greece

Examples

The Gulf of Corinth separates the Peloponnesus from mainland Greece.
Several towns along the Gulf of Corinth are popular destinations for tourists.
A bridge spans the narrowest point of the Gulf of Corinth, connecting the two regions.
