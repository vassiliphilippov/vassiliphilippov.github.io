to reach the summit

1. VERB

/ ritʃ ðə ˈsʌmɪt / (AmE), / riːtʃ ðə ˈsʌmɪt / (BrE)
to arrive at the top of a mountain or achieve the highest level of success
arrive at the top; achieve the highest point

Examples

After hours of climbing, they finally reached the summit and celebrated with a photograph.
She felt a great sense of accomplishment when she reached the summit of her academic career.
Many adventurers dream of reaching the summit of Mount Everest.

Synset Examples

The climber reached the summit just before sunset.
They reached the summit after a long hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
