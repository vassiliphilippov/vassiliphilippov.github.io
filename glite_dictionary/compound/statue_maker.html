statue maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstætʃuˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstætʃuːˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
an artist who creates statues or sculptures, especially by carving or modeling
an artist who makes statues

Examples

The city commissioned a famous statue maker to design a new public monument.
As a statue maker, she specialized in bronze figures of historical leaders.
The statue maker spent years perfecting the details of each sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures
