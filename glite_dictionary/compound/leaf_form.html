leaf form

1. NOUN

/ lif fɔrm / (AmE), / liːf fɔːm / (BrE)
any of the various shapes that leaves of plants can take, used to classify or identify plant species
shape of a plant leaf

Examples

The leaf form is an important characteristic used to identify tree species.
Different environmental conditions can influence the leaf form of certain plants.
Botanists often categorize plants based on their leaf form and arrangement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leaf shape - the form or outline of a leaf
