morale booster

1. NOUN

/ məˈræl ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
something that increases the confidence, enthusiasm, or spirit of a person or group
something that lifts morale

Examples

The team's surprising victory acted as a real morale booster for the entire community.
Freshly baked cookies every Friday served as a morale booster at the office.
Her encouraging words were exactly the morale booster he needed before the big exam.

Synset Examples

the sight of flowers every morning was my morale builder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiration - something that motivates or encourages
  • Antonym: discouragement - something that causes loss of confidence or hope
