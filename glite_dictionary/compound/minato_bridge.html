Minato Bridge

1. PROPN

/ miˈnɑːtoʊ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɪˈnɑːtəʊ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large cantilever bridge located in Osaka, Japan, spanning the port area
cantilever bridge in Osaka, Japan

Examples

The Minato Bridge is one of the main crossings over Osaka Bay.
Many tourists visit the Minato Bridge to admire its impressive engineering.
The Minato Bridge connects important areas in the city, easing traffic congestion.

Etymology

From Japanese 'Minato' (port) and English 'bridge'.
