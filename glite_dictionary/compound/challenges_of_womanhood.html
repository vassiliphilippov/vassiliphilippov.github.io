challenges of womanhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃælɪndʒɪz əv ˈwʊmənˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒɪz əv ˈwʊmənˌhʊd / (BrE)
the difficulties and obstacles commonly experienced by women due to their gender, especially in societal, cultural, and personal contexts
difficulties faced by women

Examples

The novel addresses the challenges of womanhood through the experiences of its main character.
Seminars on gender studies often highlight the challenges of womanhood in various cultures.
She spoke openly about the challenges of womanhood and how they shaped her career.

Synset Examples

The book explores the challenges of womanhood in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanhood - the state or condition of being a woman
