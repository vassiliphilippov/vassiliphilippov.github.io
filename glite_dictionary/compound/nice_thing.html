nice thing

1. NOUN

/ naɪs θɪŋ / (AmE), / naɪs θɪŋ / (BrE)
a valuable, attractive, or desirable possession; something that is considered pleasing to own or have
valuable or desirable possession

Examples

She likes to treat herself to a nice thing every now and then, like a new book or a scented candle.
After saving for months, he finally bought that nice thing he’d always wanted—a vintage watch.
It's disappointing when kids don't appreciate the effort it takes to get them a nice thing.

Synset Examples

The kids broke the vase again; this is why we can't have nice things.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury - something expensive and desirable but not essential
  • Antonym: necessity - something that is needed rather than merely desirable
