Yankee Thanksgiving

1. NOUN

/ ˈjæŋki ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjæŋki ˌθæŋksˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
Thanksgiving as celebrated in the northeastern United States, especially characterized by traditional New England customs and foods.
Thanksgiving in the northeastern US
american

Examples

Every Yankee Thanksgiving, families gather to share a feast and tell stories.
The old recipes she uses are a nod to classic Yankee Thanksgiving traditions.
Schools in New England often close for the entire week of Yankee Thanksgiving.

Synset Examples

Yankee Thanksgiving is filled with family and laughter.
They celebrate Yankee Thanksgiving with turkey and pie.
She loves the traditions of Yankee Thanksgiving each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thanksgiving - the annual US holiday for giving thanks, held on the fourth Thursday of November

Etymology

Yankee, a term for people from the northeastern US (especially New England), + Thanksgiving, the annual US holiday.
