telephone box

1. NOUN

/ ˈteləˌfoʊn bɑks / (AmE), / ˈtelɪfəʊn bɒks / (BrE)
A small enclosed structure with a public telephone, typically found in streets or public places.
enclosed public phone booth
british

Examples

The old red telephone box on the corner has become a local landmark.
During the rainstorm, she took shelter in a telephone box until her taxi arrived.
Many British villages still have at least one classic telephone box on display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone booth - enclosed public telephone compartment (American English)
