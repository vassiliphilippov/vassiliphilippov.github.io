brightness level

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪtnəs ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtnəs ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
the degree or measure of how much light is emitted or reflected by an object
degree of light emission or reflection

Examples

She adjusted the screen's brightness level to make it more comfortable for her eyes.
The brightness level of the lamp can be changed by using the dimmer switch.
Astronomers measure the brightness level of stars to determine their distance from Earth.

Synset Examples

its luminosity is measured relative to that of our sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luminosity - the intrinsic brightness of a celestial object
  • Synonym: intensity - the measurable amount of a property (like light, sound, etc.)
