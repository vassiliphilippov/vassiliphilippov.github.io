Lake Nyasa

1. PROPN

/ leɪk naɪˈæsə / (AmE), / leɪk naɪˈæsa / (BrE)
a large lake in southeastern Africa, located between Tanzania, Mozambique, and Malawi
a large lake in southeastern Africa

Examples

Many species of cichlid fish are found only in Lake Nyasa.
Researchers studied climate change by analyzing sediment from Lake Nyasa.
The border between Malawi and Tanzania runs through Lake Nyasa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Malawi - another name for Lake Nyasa, used especially in Malawi

Etymology

From the local Yao language, where 'nyasa' means 'lake'.
