douche boat

1. NOUN

/ duːʃ boʊt / (AmE), / duːʃ bəʊt / (BrE)
(slang, often humorous and sometimes offensive) A small boat used for parties, especially one associated with loud or boisterous behavior.
slang term for a small party boat
slang offensive

Examples

They rented a douche boat for their friend's birthday celebration.
That cove is always packed with douche boats blasting music on the weekends.
Some locals complain about the noise and antics coming from douche boats every summer.

Synset Examples

They rented a douche boat for the party on the lake.
The douche boat was filled with fun and laughter all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party boat - a boat hired or used for social gatherings and parties
