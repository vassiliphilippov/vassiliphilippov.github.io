guided missile cruiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsəl ˈkruːzər / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd ˈmɪsaɪl ˈkruːzə / (BrE)
a large warship equipped with guided missile weapons systems, designed for long-range attack and defense.
warship with guided missiles

Examples

The navy's new guided missile cruiser is equipped to defend against aerial and surface threats.
During the exercise, a guided missile cruiser successfully intercepted several incoming targets.
A guided missile cruiser typically forms the core of a naval battle group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruiser - a large fast warship
