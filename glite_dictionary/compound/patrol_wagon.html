patrol wagon

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊl ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊl ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
a van used by police to transport prisoners
police van for transporting prisoners

Examples

The police loaded the suspects into the patrol wagon after the arrest.
Several officers stood by as the patrol wagon arrived at the scene.
Protesters were taken away in a patrol wagon following the demonstration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paddy wagon - a police vehicle for transporting prisoners; synonym for patrol wagon
  • Synonym: police van - a van used by the police, especially for transporting prisoners
