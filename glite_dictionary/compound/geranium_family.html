geranium family

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈreɪniəm ˈfæməli / (AmE), / dʒəˈreɪnɪəm ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly herbaceous flowering plants, including the genus Geranium and related species (family Geraniaceae)
family of herbaceous plants (Geraniaceae)

Examples

The geranium family includes both perennial and annual flowering species.
Many members of the geranium family are popular in gardens for their colorful blooms.
Botanists study the geranium family for its diversity and adaptability to different climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Geraniaceae - the scientific name for the geranium family
  • Synonym: geranium - a plant of the genus Geranium, commonly called cranesbill
