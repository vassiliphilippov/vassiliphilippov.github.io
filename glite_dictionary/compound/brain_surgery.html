brain surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪn ˌsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which surgery is performed on the brain to treat diseases, injuries, or abnormalities
surgery performed on the brain
medical

Examples

He underwent brain surgery to remove a tumor.
Advancements in brain surgery have significantly improved patient survival rates.
Performing brain surgery requires years of specialized training and experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurosurgery - surgery of the nervous system, especially the brain and spinal cord
