roach coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊtʃ ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtʃ ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for a food truck or mobile food vendor, typically offering inexpensive, fast, or casual food
informal term for a food truck
informal american

Examples

Every morning, the roach coach parks by the construction site to serve breakfast burritos.
Many employees line up at the roach coach for tacos during their lunch break.
After the concert, fans gathered around a roach coach for late-night hot dogs.

Synset Examples

I grabbed tacos from the roach coach after work.
The roach coach parked outside the office during lunch.
The roach coach serves the best late-night snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food truck - a large vehicle equipped to cook and sell food

Etymology

American slang; 'roach' possibly referring humorously to doubts about cleanliness, and 'coach' meaning vehicle.
