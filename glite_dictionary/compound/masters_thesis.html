master's thesis

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚz ˈθiːsɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəz ˈθiːsɪs / (BrE)
A long piece of written research submitted as part of the requirements for a master's degree.
research essay for a master's degree

Examples

She spent two years researching and writing her master's thesis on environmental policy.
His master's thesis was published in a respected academic journal.
Before graduating, every candidate must defend a master's thesis before a faculty committee.

Synset Examples

The term graduate thesis is sometimes used to refer to both master's theses and doctoral dissertations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dissertation - a long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one written for a doctoral degree
