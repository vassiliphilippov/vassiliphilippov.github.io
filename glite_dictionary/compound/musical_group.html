musical group

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl ˌɡrup / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˌɡruːp / (BrE)
an organized group of musicians who perform together
group of musicians performing together

Examples

The musical group played at the festival to a crowd of thousands.
She joined a musical group in college and performed at several local venues.
Many famous artists started their careers in a musical group before going solo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians who play music together, especially pop or rock
  • Degree Higher: orchestra - a large musical ensemble of instrumentalists
