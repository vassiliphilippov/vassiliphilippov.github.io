true diplomat

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈdɪpləmæt / (AmE), / truː ˈdɪpləmæt / (BrE)
a person who is genuinely skilled in managing negotiations or sensitive situations effectively and tactfully
a genuinely skilled negotiator

Examples

She handled the conflicting opinions like a true diplomat, ensuring everyone's voice was heard.
During the tense meeting, his calm and thoughtful responses revealed that he was a true diplomat.
Whenever disputes arise in the office, everyone turns to her as a true diplomat to mediate.

Synset Examples

He acted like a true diplomat during the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomat - a person skilled in diplomacy or negotiations
