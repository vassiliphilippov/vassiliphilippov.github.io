mid-November

1. NOUN

/ mɪd nəˈvɛmbɚ / (AmE), / mɪd nəˈvɛmbə / (BrE)
the middle part of the month of November, typically around the 13th to 17th of November
middle part of November

Examples

We expect the new store to open in mid-November after all the renovations are complete.
The trees were still colorful in mid-November, with leaves covering the park trails.
By mid-November, the weather usually starts to get much colder in this region.
