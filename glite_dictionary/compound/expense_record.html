expense record

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈspɛns ˌrɛkərd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛns ˌrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a written account or document listing money that has been spent, typically for business or personal accounting purposes
written record of money spent

Examples

Please submit your expense record by the end of the month for reimbursement.
Keeping an up-to-date expense record helps with budgeting and tax preparation.
She reviewed every expense record to ensure all costs were accurately tracked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receipt - a written acknowledgment of having received money or goods
  • Synonym: invoice - a list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due
