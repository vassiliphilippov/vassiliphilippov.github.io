golden calla

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈkælə / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈkælə / (BrE)
Any of several plants of the genus Zantedeschia, especially those with yellow spathes, commonly cultivated as ornamentals.
a calla lily with a yellow spathe

Examples

The golden calla brightened the room with its vivid yellow spathes.
Gardeners often choose golden calla for their stunning appearance in floral arrangements.
She planted several golden callas along the edge of the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - a plant of the genus Zantedeschia, often with showy spathes
