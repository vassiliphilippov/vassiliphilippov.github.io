television monitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈmɑnɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
A screen used in a studio to view and monitor television programs as they are being broadcast.
monitor used to view broadcasts in a studio

Examples

The director watched the live feed carefully on the television monitor in the control room.
Technicians adjusted the color settings of the television monitor before the broadcast began.
During the news show, the anchors could see themselves on the television monitor just off camera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - electronic device for displaying images from a video source
