golf pro

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf proʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf prəʊ / (BrE)
a person who earns a living by playing or teaching golf professionally
professional golfer or golf instructor

Examples

Her dream is to become a golf pro on the PGA Tour.
The golf pro at the club offers lessons to both beginners and experienced players.
Many golf pros start their careers as instructors before competing in tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional golfer - a golfer who plays golf for money rather than as an amateur
  • Antonym: amateur golfer - a golfer who does not play for money
