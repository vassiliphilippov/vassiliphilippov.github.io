finding joy in movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪndɪŋ dʒɔɪ ɪn ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪndɪŋ dʒɔɪ ɪn ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
the experience of discovering pleasure or satisfaction through engaging in physical activity
discovering pleasure through physical activity

Examples

Many people discover that finding joy in movement helps them stay motivated to exercise regularly.
The coach encourages students to focus on finding joy in movement rather than just competing to win.
For some, finding joy in movement comes from dancing, while others prefer hiking or swimming.

Synset Examples

Fitness should be all about finding joy in movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoyment - the state or process of taking pleasure in something
