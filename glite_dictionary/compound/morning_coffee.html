morning coffee

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a cup of coffee typically consumed in the morning as part of a daily routine or to help wake up
coffee consumed in the morning

Examples

I can't function properly until I've had my morning coffee.
She always enjoys her morning coffee while reading the news.
The rich aroma of morning coffee filled the kitchen.

Synset Examples

I always start my day with a good morning coffee.
I like to add milk to my morning coffee.
I can't go without my morning coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakfast - the first meal of the day, typically eaten in the morning
  • Antonym: afternoon tea - a light meal with tea, typically served in the afternoon
