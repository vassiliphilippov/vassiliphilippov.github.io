perfect combo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrfɪkt ˈkɑmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪkt ˈkɒmbəʊ / (BrE)
a combination of two or more things that go extremely well together, especially in terms of taste, effect, or suitability
very well-matched combination
informal

Examples

The burger and fries make a perfect combo for lunch.
Fresh strawberries and cream are the perfect combo for summer desserts.
A cozy blanket and a good book are my idea of a perfect combo on a rainy day.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect match - an ideal pairing or fit
  • Antonym: odd couple - two people or things that are very different but together
