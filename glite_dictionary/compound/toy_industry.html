toy industry

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of business involved in the manufacturing and selling of toys for children
sector making and selling toys

Examples

The toy industry sees its highest sales during the holiday season each year.
New safety regulations were introduced to ensure the toy industry produces safer products for children.
Growing demand for educational toys is transforming the toy industry worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy business - commercial activity involving toys
  • Synonym: toy market - the market for buying and selling toys
