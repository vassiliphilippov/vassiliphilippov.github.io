license plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (BrE)
A metal or plastic plate on a motor vehicle, displaying its official registration number.
car's registration identification plate

Examples

He glanced at the license plate to write down the number.
Her car's license plate had been stolen overnight.
The police officer ran the license plate through the database.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: number plate - plate displaying a vehicle's registration number, chiefly British term
