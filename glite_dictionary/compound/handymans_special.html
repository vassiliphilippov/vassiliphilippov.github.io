handyman's special

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændiˌmænz ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˈhændimænz ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
a property, especially a house, offered for sale at a low price because it needs repair or renovation
property requiring repair or renovation

Examples

They saw the listing for a handyman's special and decided to take on the challenge.
After months of searching, she bought a handyman's special in the suburbs.
This handyman's special could become a beautiful home with some hard work and vision.

Synset Examples

They bought a handyman's special to renovate and flip.
This house is a handyman's special, full of potential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fixer-upper - a house or property in need of repair and renovation
