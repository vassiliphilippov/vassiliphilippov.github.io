elevator car

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtɚ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə kɑː / (BrE)
the compartment of an elevator in which passengers or goods are carried between floors in a building
compartment in an elevator

Examples

The elevator car stopped suddenly between floors, leaving everyone inside momentarily startled.
A technician inspected the elevator car to ensure it met all safety regulations before reopening it to the public.
There was not enough space in the elevator car for the delivery cart, so we waited for the next one.

Synset Examples

the car was on the top floor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lift car - the compartment of a British 'lift' where passengers ride
