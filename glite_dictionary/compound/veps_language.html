Veps language

1. NOUN

/ vɛps ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / vɛps ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Veps, a Finnic people indigenous to areas of northwestern Russia
Finnic language of the Veps people

Examples

The Veps language is considered endangered, with only a few thousand speakers remaining.
Linguists have studied the Veps language to understand its relationship to other Finnic tongues.
She is fluent in both Russian and the Veps language, using them at home and in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish language - the Uralic language of the Finns
  • Synonym: Estonian language - the Finnic language spoken in Estonia

Etymology

Veps, a self-designation of the ethnic group, from Russian Вепсы (Vepsy).
