saccharine movie

1. NOUN

/ ˈsækərɪn ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / ˈsakərɪn ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
a movie that is excessively sweet, sentimental, or emotionally manipulative
overly sentimental movie

Examples

She found the saccharine movie too cloying and predictable for her taste.
The critics panned the latest release as just another saccharine movie with no real depth.
Sometimes, a saccharine movie is exactly what you need on a rainy day.

Synset Examples

She avoided saccharine movies, preferring thrillers instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental film - a film characterized by excessive sentimentality

Etymology

From 'saccharine', meaning excessively sweet, from Latin 'saccharon' (sugar).
