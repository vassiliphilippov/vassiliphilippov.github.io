damn right proud

1. ADJ

/ dæm raɪt praʊd / (AmE), / dæm raɪt praʊd / (BrE)
Extremely proud; expressing strong and emphatic pride, often used informally or emphatically.
extremely proud (informal intensifier)
informal

Examples

He was damn right proud of the team’s hard work and success.
You should be damn right proud of everything you’ve accomplished this year.
She marched onto the stage damn right proud of her performance.

Synset Examples

I’m damn right proud of my achievements this year.
She felt darn right proud of her achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damn proud - extremely proud; informal intensifier
  • Degree Lower: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of achievements
