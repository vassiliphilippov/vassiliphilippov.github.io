bringing close together

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪŋɪŋ kloʊs təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪŋɪŋ kləʊs təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
the act of moving two parts, especially the cut edges of tissue, nearer or into contact with each other, typically in a medical or surgical context.
act of moving two things nearer, esp. tissues
medical

Examples

The surgeon ensured the bringing close together of the wound edges for a better healing process.
A successful bringing close together of the tissues reduces the risk of infection.
In this procedure, the bringing close together of blood vessels is performed with sutures.
