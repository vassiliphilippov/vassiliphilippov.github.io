recording studio

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˌstjuːdioʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˌstjuːdiəʊ / (BrE)
a room or building equipped for making sound or music recordings
place for making sound recordings

Examples

The band spent several weeks in the recording studio finishing their latest album.
She booked time at a professional recording studio to record her first song.
Many famous albums were produced in that legendary recording studio in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a room where an artist, photographer, or musician works
