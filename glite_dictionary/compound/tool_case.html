tool case

1. NOUN

/ tuːl keɪs / (AmE), / tuːl keɪs / (BrE)
a box, chest, or cabinet designed for holding and organizing hand tools
container for holding tools

Examples

He keeps all his screwdrivers neatly arranged in his tool case.
The mechanic reached into his tool case for a wrench during the repair.
After the project, she carefully put each item back into the tool case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolbox - a box for holding tools
  • Synonym: chest - a large, strong box, often used for storage
