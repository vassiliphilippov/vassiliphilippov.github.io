tone-deaf to others' feelings

1. ADJ

/ toʊn dɛf tu ˈʌðɚz ˈfilɪŋz / (AmE), / təʊn dɛf tʊ ˈʌðəz ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
unable or unwilling to recognize or understand other people's emotional cues and sensitivities
insensitive to others' emotions

Examples

He was completely tone-deaf to others' feelings and didn't notice she was upset.
Managers who are tone-deaf to others' feelings often create an unhappy workplace.
Her comments were so tone-deaf to others' feelings that they offended several people.

Synset Examples

Being tone-deaf to others' feelings can lead to conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insensitive - lacking awareness or concern for others' feelings
  • Antonym: empathetic - able to understand and share others' feelings

Etymology

Metaphorical extension of 'tone-deaf' (unable to distinguish musical pitch) to emotional awareness.
