wedding gown

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a formal dress worn by a bride during her wedding ceremony, typically white or ivory and often elaborate in design
bride's formal dress at a wedding

Examples

She spent months searching for the perfect wedding gown for her big day.
The wedding gown was adorned with delicate lace and sparkling beads.
After the ceremony, the bride carefully hung her wedding gown in a garment bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding dress - another term for wedding gown; bridal dress
