art dealer

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈdiːlər / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person who buys and sells works of art, often requiring expertise in esthetic evaluation
person who buys and sells works of art

Examples

The art dealer traveled to Paris to find rare paintings for her wealthy clients.
After years as an art dealer, he developed a keen eye for emerging talent.
The famous sculpture changed hands when an art dealer arranged its sale to a private collector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallery owner - a person who owns an art gallery, often also dealing in art
