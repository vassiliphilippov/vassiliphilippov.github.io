audio recording

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔdiˌoʊ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a recording of sound, especially as stored on a physical medium or in digital form
a recording of sound

Examples

The researcher stored the interview as an audio recording on his laptop for later analysis.
She listened to the old audio recording of her grandfather telling stories.
We created an audio recording of the concert to share with friends who couldn’t attend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sound recording - a recording of sounds, especially music or speech
  • Antonym: video recording - a recording of moving visual images, sometimes with sound

Etymology

From Latin 'audio', meaning 'I hear', combined with 'recording'.
