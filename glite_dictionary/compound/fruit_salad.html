fruit salad

1. NOUN

/ frut ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / fruːt ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
A dish consisting of a mixture of various types of fresh fruit, often served as a dessert.
dish of mixed fresh fruit

Examples

I made a large fruit salad for the summer picnic.
The restaurant serves a refreshing fruit salad with yogurt for dessert.
She prefers to eat fruit salad instead of cake after dinner.
