roofing material

1. NOUN

/ ˈrufɪŋ məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈruːfɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a building material used to cover and protect the surface of a roof
material for covering a roof

Examples

We chose a durable roofing material to make sure the new house would withstand harsh weather.
Metal is becoming a popular roofing material because of its longevity and modern appearance.
Homeowners should consider energy efficiency when selecting a roofing material for their property.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roofing - materials used for covering roofs
  • Antonym: insulation - material used to prevent passage of heat or sound
