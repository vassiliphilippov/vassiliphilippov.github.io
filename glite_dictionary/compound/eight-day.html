eight-day

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪt ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈeɪt ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
lasting for eight days or designed to operate for eight days between occasions of winding or maintenance.
lasting eight days

Examples

The eight-day journey took them across three countries and several mountain ranges.
We celebrated Hanukkah, the eight-day festival of lights, with family and friends.
He owns an antique eight-day clock that only needs to be wound once a week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seven-day - lasting for seven days
  • Degree Higher: nine-day - lasting for nine days
