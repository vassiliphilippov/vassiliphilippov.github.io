bowling alley

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ ˌæli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ ˌæli / (BrE)
a long, narrow lane in which a bowling ball is rolled toward pins in the game of bowling
the lane used in bowling

Examples

We spent Friday evening at the bowling alley with friends.
The city built a new bowling alley downtown to attract more visitors.
Rainy days are perfect for a visit to the bowling alley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling lane - a lane for rolling balls toward bowling pins

2. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ ˌæli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ ˌæli / (BrE)
a building or facility that contains several lanes for playing the game of bowling
building with lanes for bowling

Examples

My friends and I spent Saturday night at the bowling alley competing in a friendly tournament.
The city just opened a new bowling alley with state-of-the-art lanes and a snack bar.
When it started raining, the children went to the bowling alley instead of playing outside.

Related Terms
