cavalry horse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkævəlri hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈkævəlri hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse specially trained and used by soldiers in cavalry units for battle or military operations
horse trained for battle or cavalry

Examples

The general rode into battle atop his trusted cavalry horse, leading his troops fearlessly.
Each cavalry horse was rigorously trained to stay calm during the chaos of combat.
The museum displayed the ornate armor once worn by a cavalry horse in medieval times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warhorse - a horse used in battle; often synonymous with cavalry horse
