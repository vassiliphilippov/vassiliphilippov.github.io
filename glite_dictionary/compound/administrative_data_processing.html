administrative data processing

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtɪv ˈdeɪtə ˌprɑsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈdeɪtə ˌprɒsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of computers and related technology to manage, store, and process business or accounting information
business-related computerized data handling

Examples

Many companies rely on administrative data processing to streamline payroll and records management.
With improved administrative data processing, errors in financial reports have been significantly reduced.
The department invested in new software to enhance its administrative data processing capabilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data processing - the handling or manipulation of data by a computer
