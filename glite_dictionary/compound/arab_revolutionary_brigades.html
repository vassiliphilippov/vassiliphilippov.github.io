Arab Revolutionary Brigades

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrəb ˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri brɪˈɡeɪdz / (AmE), / ˈærəb ˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri brɪˈɡeɪdz / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization formed in 1974 as a splinter group from the PLO, known for conducting terrorist attacks in multiple countries
Palestinian militant group (1974–), PLO splinter

Examples

The Arab Revolutionary Brigades claimed responsibility for the bombing in Europe.
Many governments condemned the attacks orchestrated by the Arab Revolutionary Brigades in the late 1970s.
After splitting from the PLO, the Arab Revolutionary Brigades operated independently across several countries.
