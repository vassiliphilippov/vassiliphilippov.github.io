to enjoy life

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈdʒɔɪ laɪf / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ laɪf / (BrE)
to take pleasure in living; to find happiness and satisfaction in one's everyday existence
take pleasure in living

Examples

He decided to slow down and really enjoy life after his retirement.
Traveling allows her to relax and enjoy life away from work pressures.
Sometimes we forget to enjoy life while chasing after success.

Synset Examples

It's good to laugh and enjoy life with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live life to the fullest - to experience life as fully as possible
  • Synonym: make the most of - to use or enjoy something as much as possible
