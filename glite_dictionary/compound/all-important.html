all-important

1. ADJ

/ ɔl ɪmˈpɔrtənt / (AmE), / ɔːl ɪmˈpɔːtənt / (BrE)
Of the greatest importance; vitally significant.
of the highest importance

Examples

The company faced an all-important decision about whether to expand into new markets.
Getting enough sleep is an all-important factor for success in school.
She saved her last question for the all-important interview with the CEO.

Synset Examples

the all-important subject of disarmament
crucial information
in chess cool nerves are of the essence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crucial - extremely important or decisive
  • Synonym: essential - absolutely necessary; extremely important
  • Antonym: minor - of lesser importance or significance
