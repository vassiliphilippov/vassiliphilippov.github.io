full treatment

1. NOUN

/ fʊl ˈtritmənt / (AmE), / fʊl ˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the whole range of options or features available; everything that can be provided, usually as part of a service or product
everything available; the whole range offered

Examples

For her birthday, she booked a spa day and got the full treatment.
If you order the burger with the full treatment, it comes with all the toppings.
They offered the full treatment package, including a car wash, wax, and interior cleaning.
