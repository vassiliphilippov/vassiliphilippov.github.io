wild hairdo

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈhɛrdu / (AmE), / waɪld ˈheəduː / (BrE)
A hairstyle that is messy, unkempt, or extravagantly styled.
messy or extravagant hairstyle

Examples

He walked into the room sporting a wild hairdo that immediately drew attention.
The wind left her with a completely wild hairdo after the bike ride.
Her wild hairdo fit perfectly with her creative personality.

Synset Examples

She laughed at the burrhead's wild hairdo.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tame hairdo - A neat or controlled hairstyle
