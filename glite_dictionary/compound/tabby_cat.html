tabby cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæbi kæt / (AmE), / ˈtæbi kat / (BrE)
a domestic cat with a coat patterned with stripes, whorls, or spots, usually in a grey or brown color mottled with black
cat with striped or spotted coat

Examples

The children adopted a tabby cat from the animal shelter.
Her tabby cat loves to bask in the sunlight by the window.
You can recognize a tabby cat by its distinctive M-shaped mark on the forehead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tabby - a cat with a distinctive striped or mottled coat

Etymology

From 'tabby', originally from French 'tabis' (a rich, watered silk), referring to the striped pattern.
