Doctor of Optometry

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktɚ əv ɑpˈtɑmətri / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv ɒpˈtɒmətri / (BrE)
A professional doctoral degree awarded to graduates who are qualified to practice optometry.
professional degree in optometry
medical

Examples

After years of study, she finally earned her Doctor of Optometry degree.
The title Doctor of Optometry allows professionals to diagnose and treat vision problems.
He introduced himself as a Doctor of Optometry at the vision clinic.
