long-handled spade

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈhændəld speɪd / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈhændld speɪd / (BrE)
a spade with a long handle, designed for digging narrow ditches or reaching deeper into the ground
spade with a long handle for digging

Examples

He used a long-handled spade to reach the bottom of the deep trench.
A long-handled spade is essential for digging narrow ditches in the garden.
The gardener preferred a long-handled spade because it allowed for easier leverage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spade - a digging tool with a flat blade
