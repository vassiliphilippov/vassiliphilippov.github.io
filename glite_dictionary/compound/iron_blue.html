2. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.ərn bluː / (AmE), / ˈaɪən bluː / (BrE)
any of several blue pigments made from iron compounds, commonly used in paints, inks, and dyes.
a blue pigment made from iron compounds

Examples

The painter mixed iron blue with other pigments to achieve the perfect shade.
Historic textiles were often dyed with iron blue for a rich, vibrant color.
The factory specialized in producing iron blue for industrial applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Prussian blue - a deep blue pigment made from iron ferrocyanide, closely related to iron blue
