bread knife

1. NOUN

/ brɛd naɪf / (AmE), / brɛd naɪf / (BrE)
a long knife with a serrated blade used for cutting bread
knife with serrated blade for bread

Examples

She used a bread knife to slice the loaf neatly.
Make sure to use a bread knife so the bread doesn't get squished.
This bread knife has a comfortable handle and sharp serrations.
