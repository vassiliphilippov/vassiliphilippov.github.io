ewwy face

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛwi feɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛwi feɪs / (BrE)
a facial expression that shows disgust or distaste
face showing disgust
informal

Examples

She made an ewwy face when tasting the soup.
The kids pulled an ewwy face at the smell of the rotten eggs.
He couldn't help but make an ewwy face when he saw the dirty bathroom.

Synset Examples
