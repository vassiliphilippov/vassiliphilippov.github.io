mutton snapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtən ˌsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈmʌtən ˌsnæpə / (BrE)
a species of snapper fish (Lutjanus analis) found in the Atlantic, often marketed as red snapper
species of snapper fish (Lutjanus analis)

Examples

The chef recommended the grilled mutton snapper as the catch of the day.
Divers spotted a large mutton snapper swimming near the reef.
Fishing regulations limit the harvest of mutton snapper to protect the species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red snapper - a common name for several snapper fish species
