Usbeg language

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌz.bɛɡ ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʌz.bɛɡ ˌlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Uzbek people, primarily in Uzbekistan and neighboring countries
Turkic language of Uzbekistan

Examples

She is fluent in Usbeg language after living in Tashkent for many years.
The university offers classes in both Russian and Usbeg language.
Many historical texts in Central Asia are written in the Usbeg language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uzbek language - Standard spelling for the language spoken by Uzbeks

Etymology

From Usbeg (variant spelling of Uzbek), the name of the people, and language.
