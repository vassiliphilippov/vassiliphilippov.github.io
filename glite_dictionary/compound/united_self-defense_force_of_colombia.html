United Self-Defense Force of Colombia

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔrs əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ˈfɔːs ɒv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a right-wing paramilitary organization in Colombia, formed in 1997 as an alliance of regional groups, involved in armed conflict and drug trafficking
Colombian paramilitary group (1997–2006)

Examples

The United Self-Defense Force of Colombia was accused of numerous human rights violations during the conflict.
International reports linked the United Self-Defense Force of Colombia to large-scale drug trafficking operations.
The government began peace negotiations with the United Self-Defense Force of Colombia in the early 2000s.
