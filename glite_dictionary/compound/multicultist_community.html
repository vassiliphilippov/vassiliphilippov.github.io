multicultist community

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltɪst kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌltɪst kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group or collective that advocates for, supports, or promotes cultural diversity within society.
group supporting cultural diversity

Examples

The multicultist community in our city organizes events celebrating different heritages every year.
Critics accused the multicultist community of ignoring traditional values in favor of diversity.
She joined a multicultist community to support programs that include immigrants and minorities.

Synset Examples

The multicultist community promotes diversity and inclusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalism - the advocacy or support of multiple cultural traditions within a society
