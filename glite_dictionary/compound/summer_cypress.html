summer cypress

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˈkaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈkɪprɪs / (BrE)
a Eurasian plant (Kochia scoparia) with dense branching, whose green foliage turns purple-red in autumn
Eurasian plant with colorful autumn foliage

Examples

The summer cypress adds brilliant color to gardens in the fall.
Farmers planted rows of summer cypress along the field for erosion control.
In autumn, the summer cypress transforms from green to a vibrant purple-red.
