zipless bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪpləs bæg / (AmE), / ˈzɪpləs bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag designed without a zipper, typically for a smoother, more streamlined appearance.
bag without a zipper

Examples

She carried a stylish zipless bag to match her minimalist outfit.
For faster airport security checks, many travelers use a zipless bag.
The designer released a new line featuring every color of zipless bag imaginable.

Synset Examples

He prefers zipless bags for a sleek look.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: zipper bag - a bag equipped with a zipper
