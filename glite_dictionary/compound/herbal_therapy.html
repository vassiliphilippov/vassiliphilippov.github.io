herbal therapy

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜrbəl ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the use of plants or plant extracts for medicinal purposes, especially those not part of a normal diet
use of plants or plant extracts as medicine

Examples

Many people choose herbal therapy as an alternative to conventional medicine.
Before starting any herbal therapy, consult your doctor to avoid possible interactions.
The clinic offers various herbal therapy treatments for stress and anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phytotherapy - the treatment of disease by the use of plants
