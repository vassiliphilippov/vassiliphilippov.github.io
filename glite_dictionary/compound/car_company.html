car company

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / kɑː ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a business that manufactures and sells automobiles
company that makes and sells cars

Examples

She works for a well-known car company based in Germany.
Many car companies are investing heavily in electric vehicles.
Toyota is a multinational car company with factories around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automaker - a company that manufactures automobiles
  • Synonym: car manufacturer - a business that produces cars
