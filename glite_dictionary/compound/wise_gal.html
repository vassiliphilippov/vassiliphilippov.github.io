wise gal

1. NOUN

/ waɪz ɡæl / (AmE), / waɪz ɡæl / (BrE)
an informal term for a woman who is clever, knowledgeable, or quick-witted
clever or knowledgeable woman (informal)
informal

Examples

Everyone asks the wise gal in the office for advice about tricky problems.
She became known as the wise gal who always had an answer for everything.
In the group, it's easy to spot the wise gal by the way she solves challenges.

Synset Examples

That wise gal always has the best solutions.
Everyone turns to her because she's a wise gal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise guy - a person, especially a man, who is clever or smart in an annoying or know-it-all way
