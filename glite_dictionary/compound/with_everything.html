with everything

1. ADP

/ wɪð ˈɛvriˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪð ˈɛvriːθɪŋ / (BrE)
(when ordering food) including all available toppings or sides.
including all available toppings or sides (food)

Examples

I'll have my hot dog with everything, please.
She likes her pizza with everything, so don't leave out any toppings.
He ordered his sandwich with everything because he couldn't decide what to leave off.

Synset Examples

She always orders burgers with chips with everything.
