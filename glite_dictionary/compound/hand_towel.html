hand towel

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / hænd ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
A small towel used for drying the hands or face.
small towel for drying hands or face

Examples

She kept a clean hand towel next to the bathroom sink for guests.
After washing my hands, I dried them with a soft hand towel.
Please put the dirty hand towels in the laundry basket.
