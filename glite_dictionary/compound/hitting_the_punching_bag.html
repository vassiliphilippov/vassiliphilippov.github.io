hitting the punching bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪtɪŋ ðə ˈpʌntʃɪŋ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɪtɪŋ ðə ˈpʌn.tʃɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
the activity of striking a padded bag, typically for exercise, training, or stress relief
striking a padded bag for exercise

Examples

After a stressful day, he finds relief in hitting the punching bag at the gym.
Many athletes use hitting the punching bag as part of their training routine.
She visualized her problems while hitting the punching bag.

Synset Examples
