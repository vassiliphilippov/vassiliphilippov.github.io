kola nut tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlə nʌt triː / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt triː / (BrE)
a tropical tree that bears large brown nuts containing caffeine and is the natural source of cola extract
tree producing kola nuts (cola extract source)

Examples

The kola nut tree is cultivated extensively in West Africa for its valuable nuts.
Early formulations of cola beverages used extracts from the kola nut tree.
Farmers carefully tend each kola nut tree to maximize the quality of their harvest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kola nut - the nut produced by the kola nut tree, used as a stimulant and flavoring
  • Degree Higher: cola - a carbonated soft drink flavored in part with extract from the kola nut

Etymology

From West African languages (Yoruba 'kòlá'), adopted into English; 'nut' and 'tree' of Old English origin.
