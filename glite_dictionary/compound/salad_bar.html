salad bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd bɑr / (AmE), / ˈsæləd bɑː / (BrE)
a counter or buffet at which diners serve themselves a variety of salad ingredients and dressings
self-service buffet for salad ingredients

Examples

The new restaurant features a fresh salad bar with dozens of toppings.
After grabbing a plate, she piled her favorite vegetables onto it at the salad bar.
Hotels often offer a salad bar as part of their lunch buffet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffet - a meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes laid out on a table or counter
