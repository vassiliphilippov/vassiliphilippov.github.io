cheese spread

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz sprɛd / (AmE), / tʃiːz sprɛd / (BrE)
a soft, spreadable food made from cheese blended with butter, cream, or seasonings
soft, spreadable cheese mixture

Examples

She served crackers topped with cheese spread at the party.
I bought a garlic and herb cheese spread from the supermarket.
You can use cheese spread as a filling for sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spread - a soft food that can be spread on bread
