gun rest

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn rɛst / (AmE), / ɡʌn rɛst / (BrE)
the wale at the top of the side of a boat; the topmost planking of a wooden vessel
top plank on side of a vessel

Examples

The carpenter replaced the damaged gun rest to ensure the boat's structural integrity.
Years of exposure to saltwater had left the gun rest worn and splintered.
Historians examined the gun rest to determine the age of the old wooden schooner.
