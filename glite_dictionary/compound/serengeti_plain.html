Serengeti Plain

1. PROPN

/ sɛrənˈɡɛti pleɪn / (AmE), / sɛrənˈɡɛti pleɪn / (BrE)
a vast, open plain in northern Tanzania known for its rich wildlife and as the site of the annual migration of wildebeest and other animals
savanna region in northern Tanzania

Examples

The Serengeti Plain is famous for its annual wildebeest migration, attracting tourists from all over the world.
Lions, elephants, and zebras roam freely across the Serengeti Plain in Tanzania.
Many documentaries have showcased the impressive biodiversity of the Serengeti Plain.

Etymology

From Maasai *Serengit* (endless land).
