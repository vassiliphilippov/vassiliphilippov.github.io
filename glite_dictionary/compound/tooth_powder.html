tooth powder

1. NOUN

/ tuːθ ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / tuːθ ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
A substance in powdered form used to clean teeth, typically as an alternative to toothpaste.
powder used to clean teeth

Examples

My grandmother prefers tooth powder over toothpaste for brushing her teeth.
Some herbal tooth powder products are said to be effective against bad breath.
The chemist recommended a fluoride-based tooth powder for sensitive teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothpaste - a paste used to clean teeth, typically more common than tooth powder
