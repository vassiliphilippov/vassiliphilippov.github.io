practice range

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræk.tɪs reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪs reɪndʒ / (BrE)
an area equipped for practicing golf shots, usually with multiple driving tees and distance markers
area for practicing golf shots

Examples

He spent the afternoon at the practice range working on his swing.
Many golfers visit the practice range before starting their round.
The new practice range offers both covered and open-air hitting bays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving range - an area for practicing golf shots, similar in meaning
