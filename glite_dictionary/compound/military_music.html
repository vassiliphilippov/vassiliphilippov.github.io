military music

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
brisk and rhythmic music, often played by military bands, suitable for troops marching in a parade or ceremony
music for military marches and ceremonies

Examples

The band played military music as the soldiers marched through the city.
He has a large collection of recordings of famous military music from around the world.
Military music is often used to instill discipline and boost morale in the armed forces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: march - music with a strong regular rhythm suitable for marching
