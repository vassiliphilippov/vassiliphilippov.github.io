tiger cowrie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡɚ ˈkaʊri / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ˈkaʊri / (BrE)
a large tropical sea snail (Cypraea tigris) with a glossy, oval shell marked with dark spots, often used for ornamentation
tropical sea snail with spotted shell

Examples

The tiger cowrie is prized by collectors for its beautiful shell pattern.
She displayed a polished tiger cowrie among other shells on her shelf.
Many Pacific islanders have used the tiger cowrie shell in jewelry and decoration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowrie - a marine mollusc with a glossy, brightly patterned shell
