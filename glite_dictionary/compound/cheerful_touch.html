cheerful touch

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪr.fəl tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪə.fəl tʌtʃ / (BrE)
a small addition or detail that makes something seem brighter, happier, or more pleasant
a pleasant or uplifting addition

Examples

Adding flowers gave the living room a cheerful touch it had been missing.
Her colorful scarf provided a cheerful touch to her otherwise plain outfit.
The playful artwork brought a cheerful touch to the office walls.

Synset Examples

The rousie curtains added a cheerful touch to the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finishing touch - a final detail that completes or perfects something
  • Antonym: somber touch - an addition or detail that gives something a sad or restrained feeling
