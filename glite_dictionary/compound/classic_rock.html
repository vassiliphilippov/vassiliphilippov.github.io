classic rock

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk rɑk / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪk rɒk / (BrE)
a genre of rock music, typically from the late 1960s to the 1980s, characterized by enduring popularity and influence
rock music from the late 1960s to 1980s

Examples

Many radio stations dedicate entire segments to classic rock hits from the 1970s and 1980s.
He started playing guitar after being inspired by classic rock legends like Led Zeppelin and Pink Floyd.
The music festival will feature several renowned classic rock bands from the golden era of rock.

Synset Examples

She is a fan of classic rock music and attends concerts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock music - a genre of popular music with a strong rhythm and electric instruments
