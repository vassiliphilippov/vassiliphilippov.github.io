risky business

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪski ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪski ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a situation or activity that involves a high level of risk or danger
an activity involving significant risk

Examples

Starting a company from scratch is always risky business, especially in a competitive market.
Investing all your savings in one stock can be risky business.
Climbing without safety equipment is truly risky business.

Synset Examples

He singed his wings in the risky business deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: venture - a risky or daring journey or undertaking
