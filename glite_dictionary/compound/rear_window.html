rear window

1. NOUN

/ rɪr ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / rɪə ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
the window at the back of a car that allows the driver and passengers to see behind the vehicle
window at the back of a car

Examples

The rear window was fogged up, making it hard to see the traffic behind us.
Don't forget to clean the rear window before you start driving.
She looked out the rear window as the city lights faded in the distance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: windshield - the front window of a car
  • Synonym: side window - a window on the side of a vehicle
