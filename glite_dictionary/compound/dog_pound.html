dog pound

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ paʊnd / (AmE), / dɒɡ paʊnd / (BrE)
a public place where stray or unlicensed dogs are kept, usually temporarily
public enclosure for stray dogs

Examples

After being found wandering the streets, the lost puppy was taken to the dog pound.
Many people visit the dog pound to adopt a new pet and give it a home.
If your dog is missing, check the local dog pound to see if it has been picked up.

Synset Examples

unlicensed dogs will be taken to the pound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal shelter - a place where stray, abandoned, or surrendered animals are cared for
