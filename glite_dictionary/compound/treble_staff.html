treble staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛbəl stæf / (AmE), / ˈtrɛbəl stɑːf / (BrE)
the staff in musical notation on which the treble clef is placed, showing notes above middle C
music staff with treble clef

Examples

She learned to read notes on the treble staff before moving on to the bass staff.
Most melodies for the right hand in piano music are written on the treble staff.
The violin part is notated entirely on the treble staff in this piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treble clef - the clef that positions the G above middle C on the second line of the staff
