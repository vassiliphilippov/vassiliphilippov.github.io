iPad parent

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌpæd ˈpɛrənt / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌpæd ˈpærənt / (BrE)
a parent who regularly uses an iPad to educate or entertain their children
parent using iPad for kids' education or entertainment
informal

Examples

As an iPad parent, she carefully selects digital content for her son's learning.
Many iPad parents set screen-time limits to keep a healthy balance for their children.
He was labeled an iPad parent after recommending educational tablets to friends.

Synset Examples

As an iPad parent, she values educational apps for her kids.
The iPad parent always looks for the best apps for learning.
He’s become an iPad parent, balancing screen time and play.

Etymology

Formed from Apple's brand name 'iPad' and 'parent'.
