gun trigger

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn ˈtrɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈtrɪɡə / (BrE)
the small lever on a gun that is pulled to discharge the firearm
lever for firing a gun

Examples

He squeezed the gun trigger slowly, careful not to jerk it.
Accidentally pulling the gun trigger can cause a dangerous discharge.
The safety mechanism prevents the gun trigger from being engaged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trigger - the lever that is pressed to fire a gun
