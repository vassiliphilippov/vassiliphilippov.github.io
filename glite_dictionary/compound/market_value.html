market value

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkət ˈvælju / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˈvæljuː / (BrE)
the price that something can be sold for in an open and competitive market
price of something in an open market

Examples

The market value of the house increased after the renovations.
That painting’s market value is estimated to be several million dollars.
Stocks sometimes trade above or below their true market value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fair market value - price an asset would sell for on the open market
  • Antonym: book value - accounting value of an asset
