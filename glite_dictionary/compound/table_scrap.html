table scrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl skræp / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl skræp / (BrE)
a small amount of leftover food from a meal, often discarded or given to animals
leftover food from a meal

Examples

After dinner, she collected the table scraps to feed to the pigs.
Most table scraps end up in the trash unless they are composted.
The puppy begged at the table, hoping for some table scraps.

Synset Examples

He always saves his table scraps for the compost.
They fed the chickens with their table scraps.
The dog eagerly devoured the table scraps left behind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leftover - remaining food not eaten at a meal
