hair dye

1. NOUN

/ hɛr daɪ / (AmE), / heə daɪ / (BrE)
a substance used for coloring the hair
substance for coloring hair

Examples

She decided to buy a new hair dye to change her look for the summer.
The instructions on the hair dye box recommend wearing gloves during application.
Many salons offer professional hair dye services for clients seeking dramatic color changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair colorant - a product used to add color to hair
  • Antonym: bleach - a chemical for lightening hair color
