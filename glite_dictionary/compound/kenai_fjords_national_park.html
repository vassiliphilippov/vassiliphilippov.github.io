Kenai Fjords National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈkiːnaɪ ˈfjɔrdz ˈnæʃənəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkiːnaɪ ˈfjɔːdz ˈnæʃənəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in southern Alaska known for its rugged mountains, coastal fjords, wildlife viewing, and glaciers
national park in southern Alaska

Examples

Visitors travel from around the world to explore Kenai Fjords National Park and its stunning glaciers.
Humpback whales can often be seen in the waters near Kenai Fjords National Park.
The wildlife tours in Kenai Fjords National Park offer a chance to see puffins and sea otters up close.
