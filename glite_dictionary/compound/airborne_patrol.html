airborne patrol

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌbɔrn pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛəbɔːn pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
a patrol carried out or maintained by aircraft, typically for surveillance, reconnaissance, or security purposes
patrol conducted by aircraft

Examples

The border security team used an airborne patrol to monitor the remote area.
An airborne patrol spotted the missing hikers within hours of their disappearance.
Due to challenging terrain, the search relied heavily on airborne patrol units.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol - group or act of guarding or observing an area
