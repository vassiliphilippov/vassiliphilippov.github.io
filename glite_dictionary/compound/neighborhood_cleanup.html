neighborhood cleanup

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪbɚˌhʊd ˈkliːnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈneɪbəhʊd ˈkliːnʌp / (BrE)
an organized effort by members of a community to clean and improve their local area
community effort to clean a local area

Examples

The city organized a neighborhood cleanup to remove trash from local parks.
Each spring, residents participate in a neighborhood cleanup to beautify their streets.
Thanks to the neighborhood cleanup, the empty lot was transformed into a community garden.

Synset Examples

She volunteered at the cem to help with neighborhood cleanups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: community service - work intended to help people or improve a community
  • Synonym: litter pick - an event where volunteers collect trash in public spaces
