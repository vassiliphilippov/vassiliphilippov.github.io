tapioca pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˌtæpiˈoʊkə ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtæpiˈəʊkə ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a sweet, creamy dessert made with tapioca pearls, milk, and sugar, sometimes flavored with vanilla or spices
sweet pudding made with tapioca pearls

Examples

My grandmother always makes tapioca pudding for family gatherings.
The tapioca pudding at this restaurant is deliciously creamy and not too sweet.
After dinner, she served homemade tapioca pudding topped with a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rice pudding - a sweet pudding made with rice, milk, and sugar

Etymology

From Portuguese 'tapioca' (starch from cassava) and English 'pudding'.
