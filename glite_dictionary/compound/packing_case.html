packing case

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋ keɪs / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋ keɪs / (BrE)
a large strong box or crate used for packing goods for storage or shipment
large crate for storage or shipment

Examples

The antique vase was carefully placed in a packing case before shipping.
Several packing cases were stacked in the warehouse awaiting transport.
They opened a packing case to inspect the delicate equipment inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crate - a large container, typically made of wood, used to store or transport goods
