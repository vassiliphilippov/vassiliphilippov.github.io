Afro-wig

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfroʊ wɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈafrəʊ wɪɡ / (BrE)
a wig styled to resemble an Afro hairstyle, typically featuring thick, tightly curled hair
wig styled to look like an Afro

Examples

She wore an Afro-wig to the 1970s-themed costume party.
Many performers use an Afro-wig on stage to create a bold look.
The shop sells colorful Afro-wigs for celebrations and performances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Afro - a hairstyle with thick, bushy, and tightly curled hair

Etymology

Formed from 'Afro', relating to the Afro hairstyle, and 'wig'.
