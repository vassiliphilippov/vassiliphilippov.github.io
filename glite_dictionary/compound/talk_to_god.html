to talk to God

1. VERB

/ tɑk tə ɡɑd / (AmE), / tɔːk tə ɡɒd / (BrE)
to pray or engage in personal communication with God, often seeking guidance, comfort, or support
pray or seek divine guidance

Examples

Whenever she feels lost, she takes a moment to talk to God for comfort.
Many people find strength when they talk to God in difficult times.
He said he needed some time alone to talk to God before making his decision.

Synset Examples

When in doubt, I find it helps to talk to God.
Sometimes, I just need to talk to God about my troubles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to pray - to address a deity in order to express thanks or to ask for help
