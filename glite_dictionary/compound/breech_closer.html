breech closer

1. NOUN

/ bɹitʃ ˈkloʊzɚ / (AmE), / briːtʃ ˈkləʊzə / (BrE)
a metal block in breech-loading firearms that is withdrawn to insert a cartridge and replaced to close the breech before firing
metal block for closing firearm breech

Examples

The rifle's breech closer must be securely shut before firing.
After cleaning, he reinserted the breech closer into the barrel assembly.
The design of the breech closer improved the safety of breech-loading weapons.
