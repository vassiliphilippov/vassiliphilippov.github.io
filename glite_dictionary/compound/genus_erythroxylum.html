genus Erythroxylum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌɛr.ɪˈθrɒk.sɪ.ləm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌɛr.ɪˈθrɒk.sɪ.ləm / (BrE)
a large genus of South American shrubs and small trees in the family Erythroxylaceae, including the coca bush
genus of South American shrubs and trees, incl. coca bush

Examples

The coca bush, an important plant for indigenous cultures, belongs to the genus Erythroxylum.
Botanists identified several new species within the genus Erythroxylum in the Amazon region.
Research into the genus Erythroxylum has expanded our understanding of alkaloid production in plants.

Etymology

From Greek 'erythros' (red) + 'xylon' (wood), referring to the coloration of some species' wood.
