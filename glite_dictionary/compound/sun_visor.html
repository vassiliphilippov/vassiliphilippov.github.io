sun visor

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌvaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌvaɪzə / (BrE)
a hinged flap or shade attached above the windshield of a vehicle to block sunlight from the driver's or passenger's eyes
a hinged vehicle shade above the windshield

Examples

She adjusted the sun visor to stop the glare from hitting her eyes while she drove.
Most cars now come equipped with a sun visor on both the driver's and passenger's sides.
He pulled down the sun visor and checked his appearance in the small mirror attached to it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visor - a projecting front on a helmet or cap for shielding the eyes
