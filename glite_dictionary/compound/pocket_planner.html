pocket planner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˈplænər / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈplænə / (BrE)
A small calendar or organizer designed to fit into a pocket or wallet, typically used for scheduling and notes.
small portable calendar or organizer

Examples

She pulled her pocket planner out to check the date of the meeting.
Many students use a pocket planner to keep track of assignments and deadlines.
His old pocket planner was filled with scribbled notes and reminders.

Synset Examples

My grandfather still uses pocket calendars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diary - a book in which to note daily events and appointments
  • Synonym: notebook - a small book for writing notes in
