religious orientation

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˌɔːriɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a person's attitude or tendency toward religion or religious practices
attitude toward religion

Examples

Many universities welcome students of any religious orientation.
His religious orientation gradually changed as he learned more about different faiths.
Surveys often ask about participants' religious orientation for demographic analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faith - strong belief in religious doctrines
  • Antonym: skepticism - an attitude of doubt towards religion
