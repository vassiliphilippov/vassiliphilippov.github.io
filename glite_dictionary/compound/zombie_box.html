zombie box

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑmbi bɑks / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbi bɒks / (BrE)
an electronic device that is no longer functional or does not work as intended
non-functional electronic device
informal

Examples

The old computer was just a zombie box gathering dust under his desk.
After the power surge, their cable box turned into a zombie box overnight.
We kept the broken radio as a zombie box until we finally threw it away.

Synset Examples

He called it a zombie box because it never turned on.
The old TV became a zombie box in the corner.
We joked that the broken console was a zombie box.

Etymology

From "zombie" (meaning an animated corpse) and "box" (slang for electronic device), suggesting a device that appears intact but has no function.
