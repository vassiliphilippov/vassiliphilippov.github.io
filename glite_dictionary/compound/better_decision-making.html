better decision-making

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛt̬ɚ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making choices that are more informed, effective, and beneficial than others
the process of making more effective choices

Examples

Training in critical thinking skills helps promote better decision-making in the workplace.
Organizations that foster open communication tend to have better decision-making outcomes.
He credits his success to consistently striving for better decision-making in both his personal and professional life.

Synset Examples

Being perspicacious can lead to better decision-making.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision-making - the process of making decisions
