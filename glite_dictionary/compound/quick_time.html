quick time

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk taɪm / (AmE), / kwɪk taɪm / (BrE)
the normal marching pace of 120 steps per minute, used in military drill
normal military marching pace

Examples

The soldiers marched in quick time across the parade ground, keeping perfect rhythm.
After the command, the platoon shifted from quick time to double time without missing a beat.
During the ceremony, cadets proceeded in quick time, demonstrating their discipline and training.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: double time - a faster military marching pace, typically 180 steps per minute
