pink fritillary

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈfrɪtəˌlɛri / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈfrɪtɪləri / (BrE)
a California herbaceous plant with nodding, pinkish purple bell-shaped flowers, belonging to the lily family
California herb with pinkish purple flowers

Examples

The pink fritillary blooms in spring along the hillsides of California.
Researchers discovered a new patch of pink fritillary near the riverbank.
Gardeners admire the pink fritillary for its delicate pinkish-purple flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fritillary - any plant of the genus Fritillaria, with bell-shaped flowers
