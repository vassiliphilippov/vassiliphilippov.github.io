park commissioner

1. NOUN

/ pɑrk kəˌmɪʃəˈnɚ / (AmE), / pɑːk kəˌmɪʃəˈnə / (BrE)
An official responsible for the management and oversight of public parks.
official in charge of public parks

Examples

The city's park commissioner announced new plans for renovating the playgrounds.
As a park commissioner, she worked to increase green spaces throughout the community.
The park commissioner attended the opening ceremony of the new botanical garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commissioner - an official in charge of a department or organization
