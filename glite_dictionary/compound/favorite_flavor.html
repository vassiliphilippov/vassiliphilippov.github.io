favorite flavor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
the taste or variety that a person prefers most, especially of a food or drink
preferred taste or variety

Examples

Chocolate has always been my favorite flavor of ice cream.
What's your favorite flavor of potato chips?
She tried every gelato until she found her favorite flavor.

Synset Examples

Everyone brought their favorite flavors to the skittles party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - most liked or preferred; person or thing preferred above all others
