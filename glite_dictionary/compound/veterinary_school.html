veterinary school

1. NOUN

/ vəˈtɛrəˌnɛri skuːl / (AmE), / vəˈtɛrɪnəri skuːl / (BrE)
an institution of higher education specializing in the teaching and training of veterinary medicine and surgery.
institution teaching veterinary medicine

Examples

She was accepted into a prestigious veterinary school in California.
Many students apply to veterinary school after completing a degree in biology.
Attending veterinary school requires a strong interest in animal health and medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical school - an institution for training students in human medicine
