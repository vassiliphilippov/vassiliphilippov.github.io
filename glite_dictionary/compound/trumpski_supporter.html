Trumpski supporter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpski səˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpski səˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
A person who supports Donald Trump, often humorously or disparagingly implying Russian associations.
supporter of Donald Trump (humorous/disparaging)
informal

Examples

His uncle, a proud Trumpski supporter, wore a red hat to every family gathering.
She laughed off the accusation of being a Trumpski supporter and changed the subject quickly.
The protest drew both Trumpski supporters and opponents, sparking a lively debate.

Synset Examples

He joked about his friend being a Trumpski supporter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trump supporter - A person who supports Donald Trump

Etymology

Blend of 'Trump' and the mock-Russian suffix '-ski,' used to imply Russian influence, plus 'supporter.'
