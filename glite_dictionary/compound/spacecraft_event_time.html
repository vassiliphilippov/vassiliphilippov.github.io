spacecraft event time

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsˌkræft ɪˈvɛnt taɪm / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌkrɑːft ɪˈvɛnt taɪm / (BrE)
the coordinated universal time (UTC) as recorded or used on board a spacecraft, especially for timing events or operations
coordinated universal time on a spacecraft

Examples

The mission control team scheduled each procedure according to the spacecraft event time.
To ensure accuracy, engineers synchronized commands with the spacecraft event time.
Telemetry packets are stamped with spacecraft event time for precise tracking of onboard activities.
