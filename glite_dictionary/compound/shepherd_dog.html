shepherd dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛpərd dɔg / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpəd dɒg / (BrE)
A dog of any of various long-haired breeds, bred for herding and guarding sheep.
dog breed for herding sheep

Examples

The farmer relied on his faithful shepherd dog to keep the flock together.
A well-trained shepherd dog can guide sheep through difficult terrain.
They adopted a retired shepherd dog that had spent years working on a ranch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheepdog - a dog trained to herd or guard sheep
