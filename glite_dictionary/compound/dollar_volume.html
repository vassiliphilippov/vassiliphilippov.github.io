dollar volume

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlər ˈvɑljum / (AmE), / ˈdɒlə ˈvɒljuːm / (BrE)
the total value of transactions, sales, or assets measured in US dollars over a specific period
total value in dollars (business, finance)

Examples

The company's dollar volume increased significantly after the launch of the new product line.
Analysts are optimistic because the dollar volume of trades has reached record highs this quarter.
As our dollar volume climbs, we plan to reinvest more in expanding our store network.

Synset Examples

the store's dollar volume continues to rise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnover - the total value of sales made during a period
  • Synonym: sales volume - the number of units sold or total sales made
