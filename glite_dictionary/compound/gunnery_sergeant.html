gunnery sergeant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnəri ˌsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnəri ˌsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer in the US Marine Corps ranking above staff sergeant and below master sergeant or first sergeant
US Marine Corps noncommissioned officer rank
american

Examples

After twenty years of dedicated service, she was promoted to gunnery sergeant in the Marine Corps.
The gunnery sergeant led the platoon with discipline and experience during training exercises.
Every gunnery sergeant is responsible for the welfare and training of junior Marines.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: staff sergeant - a noncommissioned officer rank below gunnery sergeant
  • Degree Higher: master sergeant - a noncommissioned officer rank above gunnery sergeant
  • Degree Higher: first sergeant - a noncommissioned officer rank above gunnery sergeant
