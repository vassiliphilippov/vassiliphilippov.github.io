oil-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ɔɪlˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪlˌbeərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of rock, soil, or other material) containing oil, especially crude oil.
containing oil (especially of rocks or soil)

Examples

The geologists discovered an oil-bearing formation deep beneath the desert.
Many investments in the region were driven by its oil-bearing shale deposits.
The company drilled several wells in oil-bearing strata last year.
