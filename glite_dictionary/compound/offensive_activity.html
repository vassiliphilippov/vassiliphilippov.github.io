offensive activity

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɛnsɪv ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / əˈfɛnsɪv ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
behavior that shows a lack of politeness or respect for others, causing hurt or discomfort
lack of politeness or respect

Examples

Such offensive activity will not be tolerated in this workplace.
He apologized for his offensive activity at the dinner party.
The committee discussed how to address offensive activity in public spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offense - an act that violates social or moral rules and causes hurt or anger
  • Synonym: insult - a disrespectful or scornful remark or action
  • Antonym: courtesy - polite behavior showing respect for others
