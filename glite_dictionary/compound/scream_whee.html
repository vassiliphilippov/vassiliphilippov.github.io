to scream whee

1. VERB

/ skrim wi / (AmE), / skriːm wiː / (BrE)
to shout or exclaim 'whee' loudly as an expression of excitement or joy, especially in response to a sudden thrill or rush
shout of excitement or joy
informal

Examples

The children couldn't stop screaming whee as they ran down the hill.
We all screamed whee when the roller coaster took a sharp turn.
She loves screaming whee every time she jumps into the pool.

Synset Examples

We all screamed whee as the roller coaster dropped suddenly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shout - to say something very loudly
  • Synonym: to exclaim - to cry out suddenly in surprise, strong emotion, or pain
