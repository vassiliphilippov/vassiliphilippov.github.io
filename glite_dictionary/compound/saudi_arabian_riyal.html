Saudi Arabian riyal

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊdi əˈreɪbiən riˈɑl / (AmE), / ˌsaʊdi əˈreɪbiən riˈɑːl / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Saudi Arabia, subdivided into 100 halalas
currency unit of Saudi Arabia

Examples

He exchanged his dollars for Saudi Arabian riyals at the airport.
The cost of the meal was 50 Saudi Arabian riyals.
Foreign investors often monitor the value of the Saudi Arabian riyal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riyal - unit of currency used in several Arab countries
