bucktoothed smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌtuːθt ˈsmaɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌtuːθt ˈsmaɪl / (BrE)
a smile that exposes prominent front teeth, often creating a distinctive or endearing appearance
smile showing prominent front teeth

Examples

Her bucktoothed smile was so warm that everyone instantly felt at ease around her.
He flashed a bucktoothed smile when he heard the good news, unable to hide his excitement.
Children sometimes feel self-conscious about their bucktoothed smile, but it can be incredibly charming.

Synset Examples

Her bucktoothed smile was infectious and charming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gap-toothed - having a noticeable gap between the teeth
