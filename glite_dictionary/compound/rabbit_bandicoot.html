rabbit bandicoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪt ˈbændɪˌkut / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ˈbændɪˌkuːt / (BrE)
A bandicoot species with leathery ears resembling those of a rabbit.
bandicoot with rabbit-like ears

Examples

The rabbit bandicoot is known for its large, leathery ears and burrowing habits.
Researchers are studying the habitat needs of rabbit bandicoots in Australia.
Unlike other marsupials, the rabbit bandicoot primarily forages at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bilby - Another name for the rabbit bandicoot, a marsupial species
