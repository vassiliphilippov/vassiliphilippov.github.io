newspaper columnist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈkɑləmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
a journalist who regularly writes a column for a newspaper, often expressing opinions or commentary.
journalist who writes columns for newspapers

Examples

The newspaper columnist criticized the mayor's recent policy decisions in her latest piece.
As a newspaper columnist, he often receives letters from readers responding to his opinions.
The young journalist aspired to become a newspaper columnist known for thoughtful social commentary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columnist - a person who writes a regular column
