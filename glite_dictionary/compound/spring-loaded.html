spring-loaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈsprɪŋ ˌloʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋ ˌləʊdɪd / (BrE)
containing or operated by a compressed spring that presses one part against another or causes movement
containing a compressed spring mechanism

Examples

The toy uses a spring-loaded mechanism to launch the ball across the room.
He pressed the spring-loaded button and the compartment popped open instantly.
Many knives have spring-loaded blades for quick and easy access.
