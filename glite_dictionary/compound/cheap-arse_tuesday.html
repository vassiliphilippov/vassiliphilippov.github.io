cheap-arse Tuesday

1. NOUN

/ tʃip ɑrs ˈtuːz.deɪ / (AmE), / tʃiːp ɑːs ˈtjuːz.deɪ / (BrE)
An informal term for a day, usually Tuesday, when entertainment venues such as cinemas offer discounted prices.
discounted day (often cinema)
informal slang

Examples

The cinema is packed on cheap-arse Tuesday because everyone wants a deal.
We planned our outing specifically for cheap-arse Tuesday to save on tickets.
Some restaurants also offer half-price meals on cheap-arse Tuesday.

Synset Examples

It's cheap-arse Tuesday, so let's catch that flick!
On cheap-arse Tuesday, all tickets are just five bucks!
We always go to the movies on cheap-arse Tuesday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain day - day when goods or tickets are cheaper than usual

Etymology

Combines 'cheap-arse' (slang for stingy or frugal) with 'Tuesday', referring to discount tradition.
