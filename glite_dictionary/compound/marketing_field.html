marketing field

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkɪtɪŋ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ fiːld / (BrE)
the area of business that deals with promoting and selling products or services, including market research and advertising
area of business related to marketing

Examples

After graduating, he quickly found a job in the marketing field.
Many professionals in the marketing field attend international conferences to stay updated on industry trends.
She has over ten years of experience working in the marketing field.

Synset Examples

She is looking for work in the marketing field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing - the action or business of promoting and selling products or services
  • Synonym: advertising - the activity or profession of producing advertisements
