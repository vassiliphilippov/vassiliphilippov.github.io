Eurofizz cocktail

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊroʊˈfɪz ˈkɑːk.teɪl / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈfɪz ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
A sparkling cocktail popular in Europe, typically made with a mixture of spirits and carbonated beverages.
sparkling European cocktail

Examples

Everyone at the summer gathering enjoyed a refreshing Eurofizz cocktail by the pool.
The bartender recommended their signature Eurofizz cocktail for guests who like sparkling drinks.
She learned how to make a Eurofizz cocktail during her vacation in Paris.

Synset Examples

The party featured Eurofizz cocktails that everyone loved.

Etymology

Blend of 'Euro', referring to Europe, and 'fizz', referring to the drink's effervescence.
