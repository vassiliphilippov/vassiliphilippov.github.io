smooth-brained decision

1. NOUN

/ smuð breɪnd dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / smuːð breɪnd dɪˈsɪʒ(ə)n / (BrE)
a choice or action that is considered foolish or lacking in careful thought
a foolish or ill-considered decision
slang

Examples

Calling off the meeting at the last minute turned out to be a smooth-brained decision.
Investing all his savings in a risky venture was a smooth-brained decision.
Her smooth-brained decision to skip studying before the test resulted in a low grade.

Synset Examples

The smooth-brained decisions led to several mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolish decision - a decision made without good sense or careful thought
  • Synonym: rash decision - a decision made hastily, without consideration

Etymology

From internet slang 'smooth-brained', implying lack of mental 'wrinkles', i.e., shallow thinking.
