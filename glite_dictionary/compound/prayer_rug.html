prayer rug

1. NOUN

/ ˈpreɪər ˌrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌrʌɡ / (BrE)
a small carpet used by Muslims to kneel on during their prayers
small carpet for Muslim prayers

Examples

He carefully unrolled his prayer rug before beginning his morning prayers.
The design on her prayer rug was beautifully intricate and colorful.
During the trip, he always carried a small prayer rug in his backpack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prayer mat - another term for prayer rug, a mat used by Muslims during prayer
