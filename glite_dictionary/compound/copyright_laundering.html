copyright laundering

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːpɪˌraɪt ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiraɪt ˈlɔːndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal practice of concealing copyright infringement or disguising pirated content as original
concealing copyright infringement

Examples

The publishing firm engaged in copyright laundering to sell unlicensed translations of bestsellers.
Authorities are cracking down on copyright laundering in digital media platforms.
Emerging technologies have made copyright laundering easier but also easier to detect.

Synset Examples

The company was sued for copyright laundering through offshore accounts.
He was accused of copyright laundering to hide pirated content.
Copyright laundering poses a significant threat to artists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plagiarism - the practice of taking someone else's work or ideas and passing them off as one's own

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'money laundering,' combining 'copyright' (legal right to control copying of work) and 'laundering' (concealing illegal origin).
