pocket lighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˈlaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈlaɪtə / (BrE)
A small, portable device used to ignite cigars or cigarettes.
small portable lighter

Examples

He pulled a pocket lighter from his jacket and offered to light her cigarette.
Smoking is not allowed, but my friend still keeps a pocket lighter in his bag.
She bought a new pocket lighter as a souvenir from the gas station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighter - a device that produces a small flame for lighting cigarettes, etc.
