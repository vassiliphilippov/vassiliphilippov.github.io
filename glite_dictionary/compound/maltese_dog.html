Maltese dog

1. NOUN

/ mɔlˈtiz dɔg / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz dɒg / (BrE)
a small breed of toy dog with a long, straight, silky white coat
toy dog breed with silky white coat

Examples

Their Maltese dog loves to curl up on the sofa and be pampered.
A Maltese dog requires regular grooming to keep its white coat clean and shiny.
She adopted a Maltese dog from the animal shelter last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy dog - a small breed of dog kept chiefly as a pet

Etymology

Named after the island of Malta in the Mediterranean.
