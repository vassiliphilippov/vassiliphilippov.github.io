old-man-of-the-woods

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈmæn əv ðə wʊdz / (AmE), / əʊld ˈmæn əv ðə wʊdz / (BrE)
An edible, mild-tasting mushroom (Strobilomyces floccopus) found in coniferous woodlands of eastern North America, known for its dark, shaggy appearance.
edible mushroom of North America

Examples

We found a large old-man-of-the-woods growing near the pine trees during our hike.
The distinctive dark cap of the old-man-of-the-woods makes it easy to spot among forest mushrooms.
Many foragers enjoy the unique texture of old-man-of-the-woods in homemade soups and stews.
