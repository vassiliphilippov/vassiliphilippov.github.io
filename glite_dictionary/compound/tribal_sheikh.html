tribal sheikh

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪbəl ˈʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbəl ˈʃiːk / (BrE)
the leader of an Arab village, tribe, or extended family, often holding both social and political authority
leader of an Arab village or tribe

Examples

The tribal sheikh presided over the ceremony, offering blessings to each family member.
Negotiations between villages were led by the tribal sheikh to resolve the dispute.
As a respected tribal sheikh, he was responsible for maintaining order and justice in his community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chieftain - the leader of a tribe or clan
  • Synonym: leader - a person who leads a group or organization

Etymology

From Arabic 'shaykh' meaning 'elder' or 'chief'.
