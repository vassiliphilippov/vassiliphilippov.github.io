sexually attractive

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛkʃuəli əˈtræktɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəli əˈtræktɪv / (BrE)
capable of arousing sexual desire in others; appealing in a sexual way
arousing sexual desire

Examples

He was often described as sexually attractive by his classmates.
