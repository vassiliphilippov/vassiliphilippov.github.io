golden fairy lantern

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈfɛri ˈlæntɚn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈfeəri ˈlæntən / (BrE)
a globe lily of northern California with open clusters of clear yellow egg-shaped flowers
a yellow-flowered Californian globe lily

Examples

The golden fairy lantern blooms in spring throughout the hills of northern California.
Gardeners prize the golden fairy lantern for its delicate, egg-shaped yellow flowers.
While hiking, we spotted a cluster of golden fairy lanterns nestled among the grasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: globe lily - a lily with globe-shaped flowers
