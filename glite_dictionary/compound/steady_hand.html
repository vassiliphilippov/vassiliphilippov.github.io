steady hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛdi hænd / (AmE), / ˈstɛdi hand / (BrE)
calm and controlled grip or guidance, especially in challenging situations
calm, controlled guidance or grip

Examples

Her father guided her bicycle with a steady hand as she learned to ride.
The company survived the crisis thanks to the CEO's steady hand.
