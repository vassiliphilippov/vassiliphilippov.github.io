hot-water tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑt ˌwɔtər tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈhɒt ˌwɔːtə tæŋk / (BrE)
a container that both heats and stores water for supply in a building
container for heating and storing water

Examples

The plumber installed a new hot-water tank in the basement last week.
If the hot-water tank leaks, it can cause serious damage to your home.
Modern hot-water tanks are much more energy-efficient than older models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water heater - appliance for heating water
