high caliber

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈkæləbɚ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈkæləbə / (BrE)
of superior quality or ability; of a high standard or excellence
of superior quality or ability

Examples

She is known for her high caliber work in medical research.
The company hires only engineers of high caliber to maintain its reputation.
The event attracted speakers of high caliber from around the world.

Synset Examples

He is a player of high caliber in the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high quality - of superior standard or excellence
  • Antonym: low caliber - of inferior quality or ability

Etymology

From 'caliber', originally referring to the diameter of a gun barrel, figuratively extended to mean quality or worth.
