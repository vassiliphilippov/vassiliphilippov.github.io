Honest Abe

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːnəst eɪb / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst eɪb / (BrE)
a nickname for Abraham Lincoln, used to emphasize his reputation for honesty and integrity
nickname for Abraham Lincoln

Examples

Even critics respected Honest Abe for his unwavering principles.
Children enjoy stories about Honest Abe and his famous honesty.
During the debate, he was compared to Honest Abe for his straightforward answers.

Synset Examples

He was known as Honest Abe even among his critics.
They affectionately called him Honest Abe for his integrity.
The story of Honest Abe is famous in American folklore.
