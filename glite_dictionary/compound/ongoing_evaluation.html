ongoing evaluation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑnˌɡoʊɪŋ ˌiːˌvæljuˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌɡəʊɪŋ ˌiːˌvæljʊˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
An evaluation carried out continuously during the implementation phase of a project or program to monitor progress and inform decision-making.
continuous evaluation during project implementation

Examples

The team instituted an ongoing evaluation process to ensure issues could be addressed as the project progressed.
An ongoing evaluation helped the organization adjust its methods midway through the initiative.
Effective ongoing evaluation revealed unforeseen challenges during the early stages of the new curriculum rollout.

Synset Examples

Ongoing evaluation can influence both the scope of the programme and the selection of projects, as well as programme implementation arrangements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formative evaluation - assessment intended to monitor progress during a project or program
