financial loss

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl lɔs / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃl lɒs / (BrE)
A situation in which money is lost or the financial value of something decreases.
loss of money or financial value

Examples

After the market crash, the company reported a significant financial loss for the year.
Insurance can help protect individuals and businesses from unexpected financial loss.
His gambling problems eventually led to a devastating financial loss.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: profit - financial gain; opposite of financial loss
