red mulberry

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈmʌlbɛri / (AmE), / rɛd ˈmʌlbəri / (BrE)
A North American mulberry tree (Morus rubra) with dark purple, edible fruit.
North American mulberry tree with edible fruit

Examples

The red mulberry is native to eastern North America and produces sweet, dark fruit in summer.
Birds are attracted to the red mulberry tree because of its abundance of berries.
We picked red mulberries to make homemade jam from the ripest fruit we could find.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: white mulberry - A mulberry species (Morus alba) with white or pinkish fruit
  • Degree Higher: black mulberry - A mulberry species (Morus nigra) with black fruit
