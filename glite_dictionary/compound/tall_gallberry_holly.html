tall gallberry holly

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈɡælˌbɛri ˈhɑli / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈɡælˌbəri ˈhɒli / (BrE)
An evergreen shrub (Ilex coriacea) native to coastal areas of the United States from Virginia to Texas, also known as gallberry holly.
evergreen shrub of US coastal regions

Examples

The sandy soils of the region support large stands of tall gallberry holly.
Bees are attracted to the white flowers of the tall gallberry holly in the spring.
Many wildlife species feed on the berries produced by the tall gallberry holly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallberry - evergreen shrub Ilex species native to southeastern US
  • Degree Lower: holly - evergreen shrub or tree of the genus Ilex
