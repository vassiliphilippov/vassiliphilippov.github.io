rupestral plant

1. NOUN

/ ruˈpɛstrəl plænt / (AmE), / ruˈpɛstrəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that grows among rocks or rocky areas.
plant growing among rocks

Examples

Many alpine gardens feature rupestral plants that thrive in rocky terrain.
The botanist found several unique rupestral plants clinging to the cliffside.
Some rupestral plants have adapted to survive with very little soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxicolous plant - plant that grows on or among rocks

Etymology

From Latin 'rupestris,' meaning 'of rocks' or 'rocky.'
