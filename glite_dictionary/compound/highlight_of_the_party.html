highlight of the party

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌlaɪt əv ðə ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt əv ðə ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
the most exciting, impressive, or memorable part of a party or event
most memorable part of a party

Examples

The highlight of the party was definitely the live band that got everyone dancing.
Everyone agreed that the magician's performance was the highlight of the party.
For me, the highlight of the party was catching up with old friends.
