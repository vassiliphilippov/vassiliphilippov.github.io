writing implement

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ˈɪmpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˈɪmplɪmɛnt / (BrE)

object used for writing

Examples

He forgot his writing implement and had to borrow a pen from a classmate.
Different cultures have developed their own versions of the writing implement throughout history.
