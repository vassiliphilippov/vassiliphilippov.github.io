straw hat

1. NOUN

/ strɔ hæt / (AmE), / strɔː hæt / (BrE)
a stiff hat made from woven straw, typically with a flat crown and brim, worn as protection from the sun
stiff hat made of woven straw

Examples

He wore a straw hat to keep the sun off his face during the picnic.
A straw hat hung on the wall next to the old fishing rods.
Farmers often choose a straw hat for working in the fields in summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panama hat - a traditional brimmed straw hat from Ecuador
