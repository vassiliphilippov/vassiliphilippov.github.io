right-hand woman

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪt ˌhænd ˈwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˌraɪt ˌhænd ˈwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman who is the most trusted and helpful assistant to someone, especially in a position of authority
most trusted female assistant

Examples

She relied on her right-hand woman to manage the team's daily operations.
As her boss's right-hand woman, she handled all the important projects.
Without her trustworthy right-hand woman, the manager would struggle to keep up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: right-hand man - the most trusted and helpful male assistant
