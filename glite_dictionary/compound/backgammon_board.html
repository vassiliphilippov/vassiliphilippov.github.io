backgammon board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌɡæmən bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌɡæmən bɔːd / (BrE)
a rectangular board marked with 24 narrow triangles used for playing backgammon
board used for playing backgammon

Examples

The old wooden backgammon board belonged to my grandfather.
After dinner, they set up the backgammon board for a friendly game.
He packed his travel backgammon board before leaving for vacation.

Related Terms
