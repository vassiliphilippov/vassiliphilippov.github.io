work surface

1. NOUN

/ wɝk ˈsɝfɪs / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
A horizontal surface, such as a table or countertop, used for carrying out work, especially in kitchens, laboratories, or workshops.
flat surface for working on

Examples

Please clear the work surface before starting to cook.
The laboratory was equipped with a stainless steel work surface for experiments.
Be sure to disinfect the work surface after handling raw meat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countertop - a flat surface in a kitchen for preparing food
  • Synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat top for writing or working
  • Synonym: workbench - a strong table for working on, especially in a workshop
