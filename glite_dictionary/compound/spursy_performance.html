Spursy performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɜrzi pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈspɜːzi pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
A performance marked by inconsistency or failing to meet expectations, especially as associated with the Tottenham Hotspur football team.
characteristically inconsistent or disappointing showing (Tottenham Hotspur)
informal slang

Examples

The press described their latest loss as another Spursy performance in a crucial match.
Fans were frustrated by the team's Spursy performance after a promising first half.
That comeback win dispelled any fears of a typical Spursy performance this season.

Synset Examples

The team's Spursy performance made the fans proud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choke - to fail under pressure, especially in sports

Etymology

Formed from the nickname 'Spurs' (Tottenham Hotspur F.C.) plus '-y' and 'performance'. Popularized in 21st-century football slang.
