art class

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt klæs / (AmE), / ɑːt klɑːs / (BrE)
a class in which students learn to create visual art, such as drawing or painting
lesson in drawing or painting

Examples

I always look forward to art class on Fridays because I love painting.
The school offers an art class for beginners every Wednesday evening.
She learned how to use watercolors during her art class last semester.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art lesson - a lesson in which art techniques are taught

2. NOUN

/ ɑrt klæs / (AmE), / ɑːt klɑːs / (BrE)
a lesson or course in which students are taught drawing, painting, sculpture, or other visual arts
lesson in drawing, painting, etc.

Examples

She always looked forward to art class on Friday afternoons because she loved to paint.
In art class, we learned how to use watercolors and pastels to create landscapes.
The teacher displayed the students' paintings from art class in the school hallway.

Synset Examples

The children loved their bright pinny for art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art lesson - a lesson focused on teaching art skills
  • Degree Higher: art school - an institution specializing in teaching art
