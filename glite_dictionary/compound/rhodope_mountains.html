Rhodope Mountains

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈdoʊpi ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / rəˈdəʊpi ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in southeastern Europe, stretching along the border between Greece and Bulgaria
mountain range in Greece and Bulgaria

Examples

The Rhodope Mountains are known for their rich biodiversity and scenic landscapes.
Many ancient myths and legends are associated with the Rhodope Mountains.
Hikers often visit the Rhodope Mountains for their beautiful trails and unspoiled nature.

Etymology

From Greek Rhodópi, the traditional name of the region.
