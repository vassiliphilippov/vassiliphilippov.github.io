genus Mnium

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmniː.əm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmniː.əm / (BrE)
A genus of mosses that are similar to those of the genus Bryum, but typically larger in size.
a genus of larger mosses similar to Bryum

Examples

The genus Mnium can be found in damp, shady woodlands throughout the Northern Hemisphere.
Unlike some other mosses, members of the genus Mnium have leaves with a distinct midrib.
Scientists collected several samples from the genus Mnium to study their adaptation to moisture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genus Bryum - a genus of mosses related to Mnium, typically smaller

Etymology

From Neo-Latin *Mnium*, from Greek *mnion* (a kind of moss).
