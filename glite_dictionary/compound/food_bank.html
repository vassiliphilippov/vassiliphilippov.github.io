food bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuːd ˌbæŋk / (AmE), / ˈfuːd ˌbæŋk / (BrE)
an organization or place where food is collected and distributed to those in need
charity distributing food to those in need

Examples

After losing his job, David relied on the food bank to feed his family.
Local volunteers help sort donations at the community food bank every week.
The charity raised thousands of pounds to support the city's largest food bank.

Synset Examples

they set up a food bank for the flood victims

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soup kitchen - a place where free meals are served to those in need
