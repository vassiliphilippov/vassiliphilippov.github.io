Goodyear blimp

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʊdiˌjɪr blɪmp / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdɪə blɪmp / (BrE)
a well-known airship, operated by the Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company, often used for promotional purposes and event coverage
a famous advertising airship

Examples

The Goodyear blimp hovered above the festival, drawing everyone's attention.
Television networks often use shots from the Goodyear blimp during major sporting events.
Seeing the Goodyear blimp at night, lit up with advertisements, was an unforgettable sight.

Synset Examples

The Goodyear blimp floated majestically over the stadium.

Etymology

Named after the Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company, which operates the airship.
