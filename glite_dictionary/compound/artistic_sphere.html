artistic sphere

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪk sfɪr / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk sfɪə / (BrE)
the domain or field that relates to art and creativity, especially involving artistic professions, practices, or activities
the field related to art and creativity

Examples

She made significant contributions to the artistic sphere during her career.
Debates within the artistic sphere often influence broader cultural conversations.
His work was largely unknown outside the artistic sphere until recently.

Synset Examples

In the artistic sphere, he is well-respected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art world - the people and activities involved in producing and appreciating art
