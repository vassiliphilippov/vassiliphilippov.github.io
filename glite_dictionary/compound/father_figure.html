father figure

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑðɚ ˌfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə ˌfɪɡə / (BrE)
a man who provides guidance, support, and care similar to that of a father, often for someone who is not his biological child
man acting as a father to someone

Examples

After his father passed away, his coach became a father figure to him.
She always saw her grandfather as a father figure in her life.
The teacher acted like a father figure to the students who needed support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role model - a person looked to by others as an example to be imitated
