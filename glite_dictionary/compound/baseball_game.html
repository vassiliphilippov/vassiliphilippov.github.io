baseball game

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌbɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played between two teams of nine players, where players use a bat and ball to score runs by hitting the ball and running around bases.
a sport played with a bat and ball by two teams

Examples

We watched an exciting baseball game at the stadium last night.
Saturday's baseball game was postponed due to rain.
She played in her first baseball game with the local youth team.

Synset Examples

he played baseball in high school
there was a baseball game on every empty lot
there was a desire for National League ball in the area
play ball!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball - the sport in which a baseball game is played
  • Degree Lower: softball game - a similar bat-and-ball game, but with a larger ball and smaller field
