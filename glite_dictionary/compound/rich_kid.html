rich kid

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪtʃ kɪd / (AmE), / ɹɪtʃ kɪd / (BrE)
a child or young person who comes from a wealthy family
child from a wealthy family

Examples

She always got the newest gadgets because she was the rich kid in school.
The group was surprised when the rich kid volunteered at the shelter.
People unfairly assumed the rich kid never had any real problems.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew he was the rich kid of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: poor kid - a child from a poor family
