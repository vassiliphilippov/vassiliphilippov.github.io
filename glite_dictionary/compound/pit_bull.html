pit bull

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪt bʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪt bʊl / (BrE)
A dog of a breed known for its muscular build, strength, and loyalty, often associated with the American Pit Bull Terrier or related breeds.
a strong, muscular dog breed

Examples

Her pit bull is friendly and loves to play in the park.
The family rescued a pit bull from the local animal shelter.
Many people admire the pit bull for its loyalty and intelligence.

Synset Examples

She adopted a pit bull from the shelter last week.
His pit bull is known for being gentle with children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulldog - a stocky breed of dog with a wrinkled face

Etymology

From 'pit', referring to fighting pits, and 'bull', from bulldog ancestry.
