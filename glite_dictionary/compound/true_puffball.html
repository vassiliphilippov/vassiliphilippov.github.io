true puffball

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / truː ˈpʌf.bɔːl / (BrE)
a round fungus of the family Lycoperdaceae that emits a cloud of spores when mature
type of round spore-releasing fungus

Examples

When mature, a true puffball releases spores in a visible cloud if stepped on.
The children found a large true puffball while walking through the meadow.
Many nature enthusiasts enjoy observing the unique dispersal of spores by the true puffball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffball - a fungus with a round fruiting body that releases spores in a cloud
