mud monkey

1. NOUN

/ mʌd ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / mʌd ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
an informal, playful term for a child who is very messy, especially after playing outside
messy or playful child (informal)
informal

Examples

After a rainy afternoon, his daughter came inside looking like a true mud monkey.
Parents often joke that their kids turn into mud monkeys after a day at the park.
She scolded her little mud monkey for tracking dirt all over the house.

Synset Examples

He joked that his son was a little mud monkey after playing outside.
The mud monkey swung from tree to tree, covered in dirt.
