trusty sidekick

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌsti ˈsaɪdˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrʌsti ˈsaɪdkɪk / (BrE)
A reliable helper or companion who supports another, especially in adventure or challenging situations.
reliable helper or companion

Examples

Everyone wishes they had a trusty sidekick by their side during tough times.
The detective and his trusty sidekick solved the case together.
She acted as her brother’s trusty sidekick throughout the competition.

Synset Examples

Batman wouldn't be the same without his trusty sidekick, Robin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sidekick - A person's assistant or close associate, especially one who has less authority than that person.
