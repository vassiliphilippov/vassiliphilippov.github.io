wardrobe malfunction

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrdˌroʊb ˌmælfʌŋkˈʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːdrəʊb ˌmælfʌŋkˈʃən / (BrE)
an instance of accidental exposure or mishap with clothing, especially in public, causing embarrassment
accidental clothing mishap, usually embarrassing
informal

Examples

The actor tried to cover up his wardrobe malfunction as quickly as possible.
Her wardrobe malfunction at the awards show quickly went viral on social media.
Despite the unexpected wardrobe malfunction, she continued to walk the runway with confidence.

Synset Examples

His unexpected wardrobe malfunction was caught on camera.
The singer laughed off her wardrobe malfunction on stage.
She had a wardrobe malfunction during the fashion show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing mishap - a problem or accident involving clothing

Etymology

Popularized in early 2000s, especially after high-profile live TV incidents; formed from 'wardrobe' and 'malfunction'.
