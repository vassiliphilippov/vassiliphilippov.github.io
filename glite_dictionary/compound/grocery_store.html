grocery store

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsəri stɔɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or store where food and other everyday items are sold.
shop selling food and household items

Examples

She stopped by the grocery store to pick up some milk and bread after work.
Many small towns have only one grocery store serving the entire community.
I met an old friend while shopping at the local grocery store last week.

Synset Examples

the grocery store included a meat market

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supermarket - a large self-service grocery store selling foods and household goods
