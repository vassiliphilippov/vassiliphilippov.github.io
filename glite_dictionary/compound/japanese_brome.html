Japanese brome

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz broʊm / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz brəʊm / (BrE)
A species of grass native to the Mediterranean and temperate regions of Asia, often found in meadows, fields, and disturbed soils.
a grass species from Asia and the Mediterranean

Examples

The pasture was overrun with Japanese brome after years of neglect.
Researchers studied the spread of Japanese brome in temperate grasslands.
Efforts to control Japanese brome have become a major concern for conservationists.
