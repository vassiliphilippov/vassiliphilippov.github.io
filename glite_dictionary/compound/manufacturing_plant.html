manufacturing plant

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ.ɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃə.rɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a factory or facility where products are made on a large scale, usually using machinery.
factory for making products

Examples

The company invested heavily in a new manufacturing plant to increase production capacity.
Strict safety protocols are enforced at the manufacturing plant to protect workers.
Many jobs in the town depend on the local manufacturing plant remaining open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factory - a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured
