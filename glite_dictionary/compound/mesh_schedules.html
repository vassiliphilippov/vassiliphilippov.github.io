to mesh schedules

1. VERB

/ mɛʃ ˈskɛdʒuːlz / (AmE), / mɛʃ ˈʃɛdjuːlz / (BrE)
to coordinate or bring different people's timetables into alignment so that activities can be arranged together
coordinate different schedules

Examples

It took several emails to finally mesh schedules and confirm the lunch meeting.
We couldn't mesh schedules this week, so we'll try again next month.
She was relieved when everyone managed to mesh schedules for the project kickoff.

Synset Examples

The team had to mesh their schedules for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to synchronize - cause to occur at the same time; to coordinate
  • Antonym: conflict - to be in disagreement or not compatible, especially concerning times or schedules
