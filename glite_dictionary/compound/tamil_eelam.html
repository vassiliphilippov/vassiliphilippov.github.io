Tamil Eelam

1. PROPN

/ ˈtæmɪl iːˈlɑːm / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪl iːˈlɑːm / (BrE)
A proposed independent state in Sri Lanka, advocated by the Tamil Tigers for the Tamil population.
proposed Tamil state in Sri Lanka

Examples

Many Tamils dreamed of establishing Tamil Eelam as an independent nation.
The conflict between the government and supporters of Tamil Eelam lasted for decades.
International organizations debated the legitimacy of Tamil Eelam during peace negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sri Lanka - an island country in South Asia, formerly Ceylon
  • Synonym: Tamil Tigers - the militant separatist group which sought Tamil Eelam

Etymology

From Tamil: 'tamil' (Tamil people) + 'Eelam' (ancient Tamil name for Sri Lanka).
