Population Commission

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃən kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council responsible for issues related to population control and demographic policies
UN commission on population control

Examples

The Population Commission drafts reports to help guide international demographic policies.
Representatives discussed challenges such as urbanization during the Population Commission's annual meeting.
The Population Commission was established to provide advice on global population trends.
