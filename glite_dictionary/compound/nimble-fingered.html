nimble-fingered

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪmbəl ˌfɪŋɡɚd / (AmE), / ˈnɪmbəl ˌfɪŋɡəd / (BrE)
quick and skillful with the fingers, often especially adept at activities like stealing or picking pockets, either literally or figuratively
quick and skillful with the fingers

Examples

The nimble-fingered magician amazed the crowd with his sleight of hand tricks.
Pickpockets are often described as nimble-fingered criminals.
Her nimble-fingered movements made her an excellent pianist.

Synset Examples

a light-fingered burglar who can crack the combination of a bank vault

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light-fingered - having light, deft fingers, especially apt at stealing
