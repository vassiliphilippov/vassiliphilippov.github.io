Cape Fear River

1. PROPN

/ ˌkeɪp ˈfɪr ˌrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp ˈfɪə ˌrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in southeastern North Carolina, USA, flowing southeast into the Atlantic Ocean at Cape Fear
river in North Carolina, USA

Examples

The Cape Fear River is an important waterway for commerce and recreation in North Carolina.
