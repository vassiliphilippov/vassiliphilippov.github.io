draw poker

1. NOUN

/ drɔː ˈpoʊ.kɚ / (AmE), / drɔː ˈpəʊ.kə / (BrE)
A form of poker in which each player may discard some cards and receive substitutes from the dealer to try to improve their hand.
type of poker game with card exchange

Examples

He prefers playing draw poker at the casino because he likes the strategy of replacing cards.
At tonight's game, they decided to play draw poker instead of Texas hold 'em.
In draw poker, players can improve their hands by discarding and getting new cards.

Synset Examples

he played only draw and stud

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud poker - poker game where players receive a mix of face-up and face-down cards
