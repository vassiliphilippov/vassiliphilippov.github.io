boccie ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːtʃi bɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃi bɔːl / (BrE)
A wooden ball used in the Italian game of bocce, which players bowl toward a smaller target ball.
wooden ball used in bocce

Examples

She rolled the boccie ball carefully, trying to land it near the target.
Each player selects a boccie ball before the game begins.
The red boccie ball came to rest just inches from the pallino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bocce - Italian bowling game where balls are bowled toward a smaller target ball

Etymology

From Italian 'bocce' (balls), plural of 'boccia' (ball).
