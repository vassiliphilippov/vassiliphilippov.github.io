pearl-clutchy personality

1. NOUN

/ pɜrl ˈklʌtʃi pɚˈsæn.əˌlɑː.t̬i / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈklʌtʃi pəˈsæ.nə.lə.ti / (BrE)
A personality characterized by being easily shocked, offended, or disapproving, especially at things that are perceived as improper or scandalous.
person easily shocked or disapproving
informal

Examples

Her pearl-clutchy personality made her gasp at even the mildest jokes.
You might find his pearl-clutchy personality a bit much at parties.
Despite her pearl-clutchy personality, she tried to keep an open mind.

Synset Examples

She has a pearl-clutchy personality that often overreacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prude - a person who is easily shocked by matters relating to sex or nudity
  • Antonym: unshockable - not easily shocked or offended

Etymology

From the expression 'clutching pearls,' referencing a stereotypical gesture of shock or disapproval.
