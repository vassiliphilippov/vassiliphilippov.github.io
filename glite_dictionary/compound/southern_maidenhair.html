southern maidenhair

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈmeɪdənˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈmeɪdənheə / (BrE)
a delicate species of maidenhair fern with slender, shiny black stalks, found in many regions worldwide
a delicate species of maidenhair fern

Examples

The shady garden bed was lined with southern maidenhair, its feathery fronds rustling in the breeze.
Botanists value the southern maidenhair for its distinctive shiny black leaf stalks.
She carefully watered the southern maidenhair fern to keep its leaves lush and green.
