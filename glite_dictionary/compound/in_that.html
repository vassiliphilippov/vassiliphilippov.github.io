in that

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðæt / (AmE), / ɪn ðat / (BrE)
(formal) in or into that thing or place; within the thing just referred to.
within that thing or place (formal)
archaic

Examples

The contract contains a confidentiality clause, and all details must remain in that.
Any evidence found in that will be considered admissible in court.
She discovered a secret passage, and hidden in that was a box of letters.

Synset Examples

they can read therein what our plans are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therein - in that place, thing, or document (formal)
