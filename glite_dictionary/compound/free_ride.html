free ride

1. NOUN

/ friː raɪd / (AmE), / friː raɪd / (BrE)
an opportunity to benefit from something without effort, cost, or responsibility; or a journey provided without charge
benefit or transport without cost or effort

Examples

He accused his coworker of expecting a free ride without doing any of the work.
After the concert, she offered me a free ride home since we lived nearby.
Politicians warned that some companies were taking a free ride on taxpayer funding.

Synset Examples

He always expects a free ride without contributing.
She got a free ride home from her friend.
Don't take a free ride on someone else's efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handout - something given for free, especially money or goods
  • Synonym: free lunch - something acquired without effort or cost, often implying hidden costs
  • Antonym: pay one's way - to pay for one's own expenses instead of receiving for free
