Whitsun Tuesday

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪt.sən ˈtuːz.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɪt.sən ˈtjuːz.deɪ / (BrE)
The day after Whit Monday, the second day after Pentecost in the Christian calendar.
day after Whit Monday

Examples

In some rural communities, Whitsun Tuesday was marked by special church services and local festivals.
The tradition of processions on Whitsun Tuesday has faded in most regions.
She read about the customs that once took place on Whitsun Tuesday in Victorian England.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Whit Monday - the day after Pentecost in the Christian calendar

Etymology

From 'Whitsun' (Pentecost) and 'Tuesday'.
