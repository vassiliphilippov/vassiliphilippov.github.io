quirk bead

1. NOUN

/ kwɝk bid / (AmE), / kwɜːk biːd / (BrE)
A decorative molding or beading with a narrow groove separating it from the surface it adorns.
decorative molding with a narrow groove

Examples

The cabinet doors were finished with a quirk bead for added elegance.
The craftsman skillfully carved a quirk bead along the mantel's edge.
Restoring the window frame required replicating the original quirk bead molding.
