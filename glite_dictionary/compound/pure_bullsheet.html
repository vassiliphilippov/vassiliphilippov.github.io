pure bullsheet

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈbʊl.ʃit / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈbʊl.ʃiːt / (BrE)
Nonsense or false information; used as a euphemistic or mild substitute for 'bullshit'.
nonsense; mild form of 'bullshit'
informal

Examples

Everything he said about the project was pure bullsheet.
I could tell her excuse was pure bullsheet from the start.
Don't believe that story—it's pure bullsheet.

Synset Examples

That answer is pure bullsheet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - nonsense; empty or false talk (vulgar slang)

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'bullshit' (slang for nonsense), substituting 'sheet' for 'shit'.
