brain-teaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪnˌtizər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnˌtiːzə / (BrE)
A puzzle or problem that is difficult and requires careful thought to solve.
challenging puzzle or problem

Examples

Solving the brain-teaser took the whole afternoon, but it was worth it.
The teacher gave us a challenging brain-teaser to start the lesson.
Can you answer this brain-teaser without using a calculator?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - a game or problem that tests knowledge or ingenuity

Etymology

Formed from 'brain' (mind) and 'teaser' (something that teases or tests).
