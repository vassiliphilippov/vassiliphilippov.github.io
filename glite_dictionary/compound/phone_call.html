phone call

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌkɔl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌkɔːl / (BrE)
an act of contacting someone by telephone to speak with them
act of calling by telephone

Examples

I received an important phone call from my manager this morning.
She made a quick phone call to confirm the reservation before leaving.
After a long phone call with his mother, he felt much better.

Synset Examples

she reported several anonymous calls
he placed a phone call to London
he heard the phone ringing but didn't want to take the call

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call - an attempt to contact someone by phone
  • Synonym: telephone call - an act of communicating with someone by telephone
