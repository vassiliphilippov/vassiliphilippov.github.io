eggwhisk

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌwɪsk / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌwɪsk / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil with a handle and wire loops, used for beating eggs or whipping cream
kitchen tool for beating eggs

Examples

She used an eggwhisk to prepare the batter for the cake.
The eggwhisk made mixing the eggs and sugar much easier.
After washing the eggwhisk, he put it back in the drawer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisk - a utensil for whipping or mixing ingredients

Etymology

From 'egg' + 'whisk', meaning a whisk used for eggs.
