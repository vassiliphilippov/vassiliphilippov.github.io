worship and fellowship

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜːrʃɪp ənd ˈfɛloʊʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp ənd ˈfɛləʊʃɪp / (BrE)
communal religious activities, especially those involving collective worship and shared spiritual connection
communal religious activities

Examples

Many churches host weekly worship and fellowship events to strengthen their community.
The retreat provided a space for worship and fellowship among people from different backgrounds.
She valued the worship and fellowship she found in her congregation.

Synset Examples

The Jehovians gather regularly for worship and fellowship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worship - the act of showing reverence and adoration for a deity
  • Synonym: fellowship - friendly association, especially with people who share one's religious beliefs
