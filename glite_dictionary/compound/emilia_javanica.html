Emilia javanica

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɪliə dʒəˈvænɪkə / (AmE), / ɪˈmiːliə dʒəˈvænɪkə / (BrE)
A tropical African annual plant with scarlet, tassel-shaped flower heads, sometimes placed in the genus Cacalia.
tropical annual with scarlet tassel-shaped flowers

Examples

The bright red blooms of the Emilia javanica stood out in the tropical garden.
Gardeners appreciate Emilia javanica for its long-lasting, tassel-shaped flowers.
In some regions, Emilia javanica is grown as an ornamental plant for its vivid scarlet color.

Etymology

Genus name from Latinized form of Italian botanist Emilio; species epithet refers to Java, where it was also recorded.
