brain surgeon

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈsɝːdʒən / (AmE), / breɪn ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
a doctor who performs surgery on the brain and nervous system
doctor who operates on the brain

Examples

After years of study and training, she finally became a renowned brain surgeon.
The brain surgeon explained the risks of the operation in detail.
It takes steady hands and a sharp mind to be a successful brain surgeon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurosurgeon - a doctor specializing in surgery of the nervous system, especially the brain and spinal cord
