on what score

1. ADV

/ ɑn wʌt skɔr / (AmE), / ɒn wɒt skɔː / (BrE)
used to ask about the specific criteria, reason, or grounds for a decision, opinion, or action.
asking about criteria or reason

Examples

I’d like to know on what score you rejected my application.
Could you clarify on what score this plan was found inadequate?
She inquired on what score his proposal was preferred over others.

Synset Examples

I’d like to know on what score you rated the film.
Can you explain on what score you made that decision?
