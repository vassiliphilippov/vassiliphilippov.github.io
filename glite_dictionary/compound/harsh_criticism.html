harsh criticism

1. NOUN

/ hɑrʃ krɪˈtɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / hɑːʃ krɪˈtɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
severe and often unkind or unfair comments expressing strong disapproval or negative judgment
severe negative feedback

Examples

Her new book received harsh criticism from several well-known reviewers.
After making a mistake at work, he was subjected to harsh criticism by his manager.
Despite the harsh criticism, she remained confident in her abilities.

Synset Examples

As an antilifer, he often faced harsh criticism.
She was called a harpy for her sharp criticism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval or judgment of someone or something
  • Antonym: praise - expressing approval or admiration for someone or something
