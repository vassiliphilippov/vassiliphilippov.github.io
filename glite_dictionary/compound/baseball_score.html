baseball score

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl skɔr / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl skɔː / (BrE)
the number of runs earned by each team in a baseball game, showing the game's outcome or progress
number of runs in a baseball game

Examples

The final baseball score was 7-3 in favor of the home team.
He checked the latest baseball score on his phone during lunch.
The announcer updated the crowd on the baseball score after each inning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score - the number of points achieved in a game or contest
