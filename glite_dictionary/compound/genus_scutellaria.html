genus Scutellaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌskjuːtəˈlɛəriə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌskjuːtɪˈlɛəriə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the mint family, commonly known as skullcaps, which includes numerous species found worldwide.
genus of flowering plants (skullcaps)

Examples

The genus Scutellaria comprises over 300 species of flowering plants known as skullcaps.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of several members of the genus Scutellaria.
Many herbal supplements use extracts from the genus Scutellaria for their calming effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skullcap - a common name for several plants in the genus Scutellaria

Etymology

From Latin 'Scutellaria', from 'scutella' meaning 'small dish', referring to the shape of the calyx.
