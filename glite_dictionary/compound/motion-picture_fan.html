motion-picture fan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊʃən ˈpɪktʃər fæn / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˈpɪktʃə fæn / (BrE)
a person who enjoys watching motion pictures, especially by frequently going to see movies
person who enjoys watching movies

Examples

As a true motion-picture fan, she rarely misses a new release at the local cinema.
Many motion-picture fans eagerly await the annual film festival each spring.
Being a motion-picture fan means enjoying discussions about directors, genres, and movie history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moviegoer - person who goes to watch movies in theaters
  • Synonym: film buff - person with strong interest in films and cinema
