blue jeans

1. NOUN

/ bluː dʒinz / (AmE), / bluː dʒiːnz / (BrE)
Close-fitting trousers made of blue denim, typically worn for casual wear or manual work.
denim trousers; casual wear

Examples

She wore her favorite blue jeans to the party last night.
Classic blue jeans never go out of style, regardless of age.
After work, he changed into a t-shirt and blue jeans for comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeans - trousers made from denim

Etymology

Named for their blue indigo dye and the French word 'jean' for Genoa, Italy, where the fabric originated.
