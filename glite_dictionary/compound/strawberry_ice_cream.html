strawberry ice cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔˌbɛri aɪs ˈkrim / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːbəri aɪs ˈkriːm / (BrE)
Ice cream that is flavored with fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring, often containing pieces of strawberry.
ice cream with strawberry flavor

Examples

On a hot day, a scoop of strawberry ice cream is especially refreshing.
She decorated the cake with a layer of strawberry ice cream in the middle.
They sell homemade strawberry ice cream at the farmer's market every weekend.
