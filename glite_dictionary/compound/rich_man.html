rich man

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ mæn / (AmE), / rɪtʃ mæn / (BrE)
a man who possesses great wealth or abundance of money and assets
wealthy man

Examples

The rich man donated a large sum to the charity each year.
Everyone envied the rich man and his luxurious lifestyle.
The rich man invested in several businesses across the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wealthy man - man who has a lot of wealth
  • Antonym: poor man - man who has little or no wealth
