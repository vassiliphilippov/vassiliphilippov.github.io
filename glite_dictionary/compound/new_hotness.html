new hotness

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈhɑtnəs / (AmE), / njuː ˈhɒtnəs / (BrE)
something that is currently very popular or trendy; the latest new thing attracting excitement or interest
latest popular trend or thing
slang

Examples

Electric cars are definitely the new hotness in the auto industry right now.
Everyone wants to try the new hotness on social media apps.
What was last year's new hotness is already starting to look out of date.

Synset Examples

Everyone is talking about the new hotness in fashion.
This trend is the new hotness among influencers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trend - a general direction in which something is developing or changing
  • Synonym: fad - an intense but short-lived fashion or craze
  • Antonym: old hat - something that is not new or interesting anymore
