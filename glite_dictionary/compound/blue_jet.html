blue jet

1. NOUN

/ blu dʒɛt / (AmE), / bluː dʒɛt / (BrE)
a type of transient luminous event: a blue-colored electrical discharge that bursts upward from the tops of thunderclouds into the stratosphere
blue upward discharge from thunderclouds

Examples

Scientists observed a blue jet shooting upward from the thunderstorm during the experiment.
The pilot reported seeing a brilliant blue jet while flying above the active storm clouds.
Photographs of blue jets provide valuable data for understanding atmospheric electricity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red sprite - a large-scale, reddish electrical discharge occurring high above thunderstorm clouds
