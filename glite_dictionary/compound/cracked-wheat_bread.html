cracked-wheat bread

1. NOUN

/ krækt wiːt brɛd / (AmE), / krækt wiːt brɛd / (BrE)
Bread made with finely ground cracked wheat, typically coarser and more textured than regular wheat bread.
bread made with cracked wheat

Examples

I prefer cracked-wheat bread for its hearty texture and nutty flavor.
The bakery offers sandwiches made with fresh cracked-wheat bread every morning.
She bought a loaf of cracked-wheat bread to go with the homemade soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whole-wheat bread - bread made from whole wheat flour
  • Antonym: white bread - bread made from refined wheat flour
