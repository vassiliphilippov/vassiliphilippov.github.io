American wall fern

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən wɔl fɝn / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən wɔːl fɜːn / (BrE)
A chiefly lithophytic or epiphytic fern native to North America and East Asia, commonly found growing on rocks or walls.
a fern of North America and East Asia

Examples

The American wall fern is often seen growing in shaded crevices along old stone walls.
Gardeners appreciate the delicate foliage of the American wall fern for shady rock gardens.
On our hike through the forest, we identified several American wall ferns clinging to the boulders.
