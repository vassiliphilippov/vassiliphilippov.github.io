military training

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of preparing soldiers in military skills, tactics, and procedures
preparation of soldiers in military skills

Examples

After completing military training, the recruits were assigned to their units.
Physical fitness is an important part of military training.
The country requires all citizens to undergo a year of military training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basic training - initial training for new military recruits
  • Synonym: combat training - training in fighting techniques used in war
