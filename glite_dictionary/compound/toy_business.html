toy business

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
the industry involved in the manufacturing and sale of toys for children
industry making and selling toys

Examples

She runs a successful toy business that exports products worldwide.
Many innovations in the toy business are driven by changes in children's interests.
The toy business faces increased competition from digital entertainment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy industry - the sector dealing with the production and sale of toys
