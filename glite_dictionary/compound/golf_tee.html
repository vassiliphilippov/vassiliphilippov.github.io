golf tee

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ti / (AmE), / ɡɒlf tiː / (BrE)
A short peg, usually made of wood or plastic, placed into the ground to support a golf ball for the first stroke on each hole.
peg for holding a golf ball

Examples

He placed the ball carefully on the golf tee before taking his first shot.
Most golfers prefer wooden golf tees over plastic ones.
The golf tee broke when he hit the ball with his driver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tee - a small stand used to hold a ball in sports
