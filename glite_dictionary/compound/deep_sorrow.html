deep sorrow

1. NOUN

/ dip ˈsɑroʊ / (AmE), / diːp ˈsɒrəʊ / (BrE)
a feeling of intense sadness or grief
intense sadness or grief

Examples

After the loss of her pet, she was overwhelmed by deep sorrow for weeks.
The country expressed its deep sorrow over the tragedy that occurred.
His voice trembled with deep sorrow as he recounted the accident.

Synset Examples

The ache in his poem reflects deep sorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grief - intense sorrow, especially caused by someone's death
  • Degree Lower: sadness - the emotion of being sad
  • Degree Higher: despair - complete loss or absence of hope
