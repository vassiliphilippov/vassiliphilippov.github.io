white dipladenia

1. NOUN

/ waɪt dɪˌplædəˈniːə / (AmE), / waɪt dɪˌplædəˈniːə / (BrE)
A shrubby climbing plant with glossy leaves and white funnel-shaped flowers with yellow throats, often cultivated as an ornamental.
climbing plant with white funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

The white dipladenia brightened our garden all summer with its elegant flowers.
She chose a white dipladenia for her patio because of its vibrant blooms.
Many gardeners prefer the white dipladenia for its easy maintenance and dramatic appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dipladenia - a genus of tropical, climbing flowering plants in the dogbane family

Etymology

From 'dipladenia', the genus name, from Greek 'diplo-' (double) and 'aden' (gland), referring to the plant's paired glands; 'white' refers to the flower color.
