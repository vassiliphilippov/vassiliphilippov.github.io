Revolutionary People's Struggle

1. PROPN

/ rəˌvɔlʊˈʃəneri ˈpipəlz ˈstrʌɡəl / (AmE), / rɪˌvɒljʊˈʃənəri ˈpiːpəlz ˈstrʌɡl / (BrE)
a far-left Greek militant group formed in 1971 to oppose the military junta and promote revolutionary, anti-capitalist ideals
Greek far-left militant group (1971–2002)

Examples

The Greek authorities arrested several members of the Revolutionary People's Struggle after a series of bombings in Athens.
The Revolutionary People's Struggle was active during the 1970s and was known for its anti-capitalist stance.
Scholars have studied the impact of the Revolutionary People's Struggle on the political upheavals in modern Greece.
