charge of quarters

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrdʒ əv ˈkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ əv ˈkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
An enlisted person responsible for handling administrative matters for a military unit during after-hours duty.
after-hours military administrative duty

Examples

After dinner, Sergeant Miller served as charge of quarters until midnight.
The charge of quarters is responsible for monitoring visitors to the barracks.
Private Lee was assigned as the charge of quarters during the holiday weekend.
