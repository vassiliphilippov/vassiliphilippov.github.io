power system

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a network of electrical components used to generate, transmit, and distribute electricity over a region
system for regional electricity distribution

Examples

The city invested in a new power system to ensure reliable electricity for its residents.
Engineers monitor the power system to prevent blackouts during extreme weather conditions.
Upgrading the power system helped reduce energy loss and improve efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrical grid - an interconnected network for delivering electricity
