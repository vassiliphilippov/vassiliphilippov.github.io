goon squad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡun ˌskwɑd / (AmE), / ˈɡuːn ˌskwɒd / (BrE)
A group of people, especially hired thugs or enforcers, known for using force, intimidation, or rough tactics.
group of aggressive enforcers or thugs
informal

Examples

Rumors spread that the manager had sent his goon squad to intimidate the striking workers.
The politician denied any connection to the goon squad accused of threatening opponents.
After the altercation, security was accused of acting like a goon squad rather than professionals.

Synset Examples

The goon squad was notorious for their rough tactics.
The goon squad was brought in to handle the situation.
He laughed at the antics of the goon squad during the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enforcer - a person who imposes obedience or uses force
  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal

Etymology

From "goon" (slang for a thug or fool, early 20th century) + "squad."
