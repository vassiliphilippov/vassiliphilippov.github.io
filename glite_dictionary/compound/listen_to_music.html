to listen to music

1. VERB

/ ˈlɪsən tə ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪsən tə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
to pay attention to and enjoy music by hearing it
to hear and enjoy music

Examples

Every evening, I listen to music to relax after work.
He likes to listen to music when taking long walks through the park.
Some people find it easier to study if they listen to music in the background.

Synset Examples

She loves listening to music while cooking dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to listen - to pay attention to sounds with your ears
