het up

1. ADJ

/ hɛt ʌp / (AmE), / hɛt ʌp / (BrE)
(informal, dialectal) excited, agitated, or angry; heated up in emotion or temperature.
excited or agitated; heated up (dialectal)
informal

Examples

She always gets het up about politics during family dinners.
Try not to get too het up before your big presentation.
He was so het up after the argument that he couldn't sleep.

Synset Examples

a heated swimming pool
wiped his heated-up face with a large bandana
he was all het up and sweaty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heated up - excited or agitated; warmed up
  • Antonym: calm - not agitated or excited

Etymology

Dialectal variant of 'heated up', with 'het' from regional pronunciation of 'heated'.
