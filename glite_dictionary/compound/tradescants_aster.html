Tradescant's aster

1. NOUN

/ ˌtreɪdzkænts ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˌtreɪdzkænts ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A perennial flowering plant of the species Symphyotrichum tradescantii, known for its star-shaped purple or blue flowers.
species of flowering aster plant

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming Tradescant's aster in early autumn.
She planted Tradescant's aster to attract bees and butterflies to her yard.
Tradescant's aster thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - any of various plants of the genus Aster, having star-shaped flower heads

Etymology

Named after John Tradescant, a 17th-century English botanist.
