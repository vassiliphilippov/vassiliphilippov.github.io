in public

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
in a manner accessible to or observable by the general public; openly, not in private
openly; where others can see

Examples

He was embarrassed to speak in public for the first time.
Please do not use your phone in public places where it may disturb others.
Politicians often make promises in public that they do not keep in private.

Synset Examples

she admitted publicly to being a communist

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in private - in a manner not observable by the public; secretly or secretly
  • Synonym: publicly - in a manner accessible to or observable by the public; openly
