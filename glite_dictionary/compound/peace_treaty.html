peace treaty

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈtriti / (AmE), / piːs ˈtriːti / (BrE)
a formal agreement between two or more parties to end hostilities and establish peace
treaty to end conflict

Examples

The two countries signed a peace treaty after years of negotiation.
The peace treaty officially ended the war and restored diplomatic relations.
Many historians regard the peace treaty as a turning point in international relations.

Synset Examples

peace came on November 11th

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armistice - an agreement to stop fighting, often as a prelude to a peace treaty
