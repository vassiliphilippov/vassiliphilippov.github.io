computer storage

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈstɔɹɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈstɔːrɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a computer where data is stored, including devices such as hard drives, solid-state drives, and memory chips
part of a computer that stores data

Examples

The new laptop offers a terabyte of computer storage, making it ideal for storing large files.
Cloud services allow users to access their computer storage remotely from anywhere.
Upgrading your computer storage can significantly improve the speed and performance of your device.

Synset Examples

a memory and the CPU form the central part of a computer to which peripherals are attached

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memory - the part of a computer in which data is stored for immediate use
