legal community

1. NOUN

/ ˈliɡəl kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
All individuals who are qualified to practice law in a particular area or jurisdiction.
group of qualified lawyers in a region

Examples

The legal community has responded quickly to the proposed legislative changes.
She is highly respected within the international legal community.
Members of the legal community gathered for the annual awards ceremony.

Synset Examples

he was admitted to the bar in New Jersey

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar - the group of lawyers qualified to practice law
