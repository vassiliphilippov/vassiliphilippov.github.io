robust discussion

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈbʌst dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / rəˈbʌst dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a strong, lively, and vigorous conversation, often involving frank or forceful expression of opinions
vigorous or frank conversation

Examples

The board had a robust discussion about the future direction of the company.
Students engaged in a robust discussion during the seminar on ethics.
After a robust discussion, the team decided to change their approach.

Synset Examples

Their robust discussion led to a better solution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heated debate - an intense and emotional argument or discussion
