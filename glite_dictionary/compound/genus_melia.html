genus Melia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs məˈlaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs mɪˈliː.ə / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous trees in the family Meliaceae native to East India and Australia, with leaves resembling those of the ash.
genus of deciduous trees (Meliaceae)

Examples

The leaves of genus Melia closely resemble those of the common ash.
Several species within the genus Melia are valued for their ornamental wood.
Botanists classified the new tree species under the genus Melia due to its distinctive foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Melia - A genus of trees or shrubs in the family Meliaceae

Etymology

From Latin, taken from the Greek 'melia' meaning ash tree.
