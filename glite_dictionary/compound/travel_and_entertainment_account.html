travel and entertainment account

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ənd ɛn.tɚˈteɪnmənt əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ənd ɪn.təˈteɪnmənt əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account used by employees, especially salespersons or executives, for charging expenses related to business travel and entertaining clients
business expense account for travel and entertainment

Examples

The company provided a travel and entertainment account for its sales staff to cover client meetings and trips.
She was careful to submit all receipts for reimbursement from her travel and entertainment account.
Abuse of the travel and entertainment account can lead to disciplinary action within the firm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expense account - an account for reimbursable business expenses
