chain email

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪn ˈiːmeɪl / (AmE), / tʃeɪn ˈiːmeɪl / (BrE)
an email message sent to multiple people, often requiring recipients to forward it to others, typically as part of a chain letter scheme
an email version of a chain letter

Examples

My inbox is full of chain emails asking me to forward them to ten friends.
She warned her colleagues not to fall for chain email scams promising good luck.
Many chain emails threaten bad luck if you refuse to send them on.

Synset Examples

There are many forms of chain email that threaten death or the taking of one's soul by telling tales of others' deaths, such as the Katu Lata Kulu chain email, stating that if it is not forwarded, the receivers of the message will be killed by the spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chain letter - a letter asking recipients to send copies to others
