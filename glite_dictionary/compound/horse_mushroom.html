horse mushroom

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
a large edible mushroom with a hollow stem and a broad white cap, commonly found in grasslands
large edible white mushroom

Examples

We foraged several horse mushrooms in the meadow for our dinner.
The horse mushroom is prized for its rich, nutty flavor in many dishes.
While walking her dog, she spotted a cluster of horse mushrooms growing near the hedge.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mushroom - fungus with a cap, stem, and gills
