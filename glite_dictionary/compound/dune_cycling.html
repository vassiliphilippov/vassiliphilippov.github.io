dune cycling

1. NOUN

/ duːn ˈsaɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / djuːn ˈsaɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of bicycling or motorcycling on sand dunes, typically for recreation or sport.
cycling on sand dunes

Examples

The desert reserve offers exciting dune cycling excursions for adventure seekers.
He enjoys dune cycling because the sand dunes provide a unique challenge.
Safety gear is essential for anyone participating in dune cycling competitions.
