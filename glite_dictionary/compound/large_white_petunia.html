large white petunia

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ waɪt pəˈtuniə / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ waɪt pɪˈtjuːniə / (BrE)
an annual plant with large, nocturnally fragrant white flowers, cultivated for ornamental purposes
annual plant with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The large white petunia filled the garden with its delicate fragrance every evening.
She bought a pot of large white petunias to decorate her balcony.
Gardeners prize the large white petunia for its showy blooms and sweet scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petunia - a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family
