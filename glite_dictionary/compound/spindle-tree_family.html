spindle-tree family

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪndl̩ ˌtri ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈspɪndl ˌtriː ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of trees, shrubs, and woody vines, usually having bright-colored fruits, known botanically as Celastraceae
family of plants with bright-colored fruits (Celastraceae)

Examples

The spindle-tree family includes many attractive shrubs often used in landscaping.
Fruits of the spindle-tree family are popular with birds due to their bright colors.
Scientists are studying the medicinal properties of certain members of the spindle-tree family.
