sea starwort

1. NOUN

/ si stɑːrˌwɜrt / (AmE), / siː ˈstɑːwɜːt / (BrE)
a common European aster (Tripolium pannonicum) that grows in salt marshes
European salt marsh aster

Examples

The sea starwort thrives in coastal regions where the soil is frequently flooded with salt water.
While walking through the marsh, we spotted patches of blooming sea starwort.
Botanists often study sea starwort to understand plant adaptation to saline environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea aster - a European plant of salt marshes, also called sea starwort
  • Degree Higher: aster - any plant of the genus Aster, daisy-like perennials
