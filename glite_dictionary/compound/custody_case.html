custody case

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstədi keɪs / (AmE), / ˈkʌstədi keɪs / (BrE)
a legal proceeding to decide who will have custody of a child, typically following a divorce or separation
legal case deciding child custody
legal

Examples

After their separation, the couple was involved in a lengthy custody case over their two children.
The judge presiding over the custody case considered the best interests of the child.
She hired an attorney experienced in family law to represent her during the custody case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custody battle - a prolonged dispute over child custody
  • Synonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage
