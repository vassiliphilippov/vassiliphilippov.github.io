holy terror

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊli ˈtɛrər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈtɛrə / (BrE)
a very troublesome or difficult child, often behaving in an unruly or mischievous way
a very troublesome child

Examples

Their youngest son is a real holy terror and never sits still for a minute.
She was a holy terror as a toddler, always getting into trouble at home and school.
Don't invite Timmy unless you're ready for a holy terror to run wild at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brat - an annoying or badly behaved child
  • Antonym: angel - a well-behaved child (opposite: used idiomatically)
