pleasant-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈplɛzəntˌsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɛzəntˌsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
having an odor that is pleasing or agreeable
having a pleasing odor

Examples

The kitchen was filled with pleasant-smelling herbs like basil and thyme.
He bought a pleasant-smelling candle for the living room.
Her hair was always pleasant-smelling after she used her favorite shampoo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Antonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant smell
