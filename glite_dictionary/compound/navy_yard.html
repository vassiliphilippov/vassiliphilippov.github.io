navy yard

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvi jɑrd / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi jɑːd / (BrE)
a shipyard where naval vessels are built, maintained, and repaired by the military
military shipyard for naval vessels

Examples

The historic navy yard was once a hub of shipbuilding for the nation.
Many workers found employment at the navy yard during wartime.
The city developed around the navy yard, attracting businesses and families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipyard - a place where ships are built and repaired
