what are the odds

1. X

/ wʌt ɑr ði ɑdz / (AmE), / wɒt ɑː ði ɒdz / (BrE)
used to ask about the likelihood or probability that something will happen, often rhetorically to express surprise when something unexpected occurs
used to ask about likelihood or probability

Examples

What are the odds that we would both show up at the same café today?
She asked, 'what are the odds?' when she saw her childhood friend in a distant city.
After winning the lottery, he kept repeating, 'what are the odds!' in disbelief.

Synset Examples

I can't believe we ran into each other; what are the odds?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what's the chance - used to ask about the probability of something happening
  • Synonym: how likely - inquiring about probability or chance
