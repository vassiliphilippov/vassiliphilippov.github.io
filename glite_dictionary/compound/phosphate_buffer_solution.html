phosphate buffer solution

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊsfeɪt ˈbʌfər səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈfəʊsfeɪt ˈbʌfə səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
A solution that contains a phosphate buffer, used to maintain a stable pH in chemical and biological systems.
solution containing a phosphate buffer

Examples

Please prepare a phosphate buffer solution with a pH of 7.4 for the experiment.
A phosphate buffer solution is often used in biological research to maintain pH stability.
The technician added the sample to the phosphate buffer solution before analysis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffer solution - a solution that resists changes in pH when acid or alkali is added
