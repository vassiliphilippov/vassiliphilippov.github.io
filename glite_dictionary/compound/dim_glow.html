dim glow

1. NOUN

/ dɪm ɡloʊ / (AmE), / dɪm ɡləʊ / (BrE)
a soft, low light that is not very bright
soft, low-intensity light

Examples

The dim glow of the nightlight made the room feel safe and peaceful.
We could barely read the map by the dim glow of the dying campfire.
A dim glow filtered through the heavy curtains as dawn approached.

Synset Examples

After a long day, he loved to sit in the dim glow of the lamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faint light - a weak or barely perceptible light
  • Antonym: bright light - strong and intense light
