hoary golden bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔri ˈɡoʊldən bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːri ˈɡəʊldən bʊʃ / (BrE)
a western North American shrub with white, felt-like foliage and yellow flowers that turn red-purple
shrub with white felted leaves and yellow-to-red flowers

Examples

The hoary golden bush adds a splash of color to the desert landscape in spring.
Gardeners appreciate the hoary golden bush for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
Clusters of hoary golden bush lined the trail, their yellow flowers shining in the sun.
