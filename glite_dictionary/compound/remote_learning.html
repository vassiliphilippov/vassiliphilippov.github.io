remote learning

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈmoʊt ˈlɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of education in which instruction and learning take place online or at a distance, rather than in a traditional classroom setting
education conducted online or at a distance

Examples

Many schools implemented remote learning during the pandemic to keep students safe.
Some students find remote learning more flexible than attending classes in person.
Teachers had to adjust their lessons for remote learning environments quickly.

Synset Examples

During the pandemmy, we learned to adapt to remote learning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance learning - education in which teachers and students are separated by time or location
  • Synonym: online learning - education delivered primarily via the internet
