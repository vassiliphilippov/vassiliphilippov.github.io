milk shake

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlk ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪlk ʃeɪk / (BrE)
A sweet, frothy drink made of milk blended with flavoring and often ice cream or fruit.
a sweet, frothy milk drink

Examples

Every summer, I love to cool off with a vanilla milk shake from the ice cream shop.
He ordered a large chocolate milk shake to go with his burger and fries.
Strawberry milk shake is her favorite treat whenever she visits the diner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a creamy beverage made from fruit and other ingredients, usually without ice cream
  • Synonym: frappé - a drink made by blending ice, milk, and flavoring, often similar to a milk shake

Etymology

From 'milk' + 'shake', indicating a drink made by shaking milk with other ingredients.
