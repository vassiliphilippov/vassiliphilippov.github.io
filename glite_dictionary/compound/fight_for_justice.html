to fight for justice

1. VERB

/ faɪt fəɹ ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / faɪt fə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
to actively advocate or struggle to ensure fairness and the upholding of rights
actively advocate for fairness and rights

Examples

She dedicated her life to fight for justice in her community.
Lawyers often fight for justice on behalf of those who cannot speak for themselves.
Many activists are fighting for justice in the face of corruption and inequality.

Synset Examples

He said, 'You’re damn straight I’m going to fight for justice!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campaign - work in an organized and active way toward a goal, especially political or social change
  • Synonym: fight against injustice - oppose unfairness or violation of rights
