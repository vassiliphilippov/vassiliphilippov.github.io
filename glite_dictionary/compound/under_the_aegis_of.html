under the aegis of

1. ADP

/ ˈʌndɚ ði ˈiːdʒɪs ʌv / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ðiː ˈiːdʒɪs ɒv / (BrE)
with the protection, support, or sponsorship of someone or something, especially an organization or authority.
under the protection or support of

Examples

The new school was established under the aegis of an international charity organization.
All negotiations were carried out under the aegis of the United Nations.
The young scientists worked under the aegis of a respected research institution.

Synset Examples

The charity operates under the aegis of the local government.
Artists often work under the aegis of influential mentors.
The research was conducted under the aegis of a reputable university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under the auspices of - with the support or guidance of someone or an organization

Etymology

From Greek 'aigis' (goatskin shield), originally referring to Zeus or Athena's shield; 'aegis' came to mean 'protection'.
