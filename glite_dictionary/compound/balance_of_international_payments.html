balance of international payments

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæləns əv ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənl ˈpeɪmənts / (AmE), / ˈbæləns əv ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˈpeɪmənts / (BrE)
the systematic record of all economic transactions made between a particular country and the rest of the world over a year
system of recording a country's transactions with the world

Examples

The government closely monitors the balance of international payments to assess the country's economic health.
A persistent deficit in the balance of international payments may indicate underlying economic problems.
Improvements in exports can help a nation achieve a favorable balance of international payments.

Synset Examples

a favorable balance of payments exists when more payments are coming in than going out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance of payments - the difference in total value between payments into and out of a country
