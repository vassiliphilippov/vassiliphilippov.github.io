to monitor your spending

1. VERB

/ ˈmɑnɪtɚ jʊr ˈspɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪtə jə ˈspɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
to regularly check and keep track of the amount of money you spend
check and track money spent

Examples

It's important to monitor your spending each month to stay within your budget.
By using mobile apps, you can easily monitor your spending habits.
If you don't monitor your spending, you might run out of money before you realize it.
