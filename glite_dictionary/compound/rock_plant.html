rock plant

1. NOUN

/ rɑk plænt / (AmE), / rɒk plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that grows on or among rocks or is suitable for growing in a rock garden
plant suited to rocky areas or rock gardens

Examples

The rock plant thrived in the sandy soil between the stones in the garden.
Many gardeners choose a rock plant for its ability to grow in harsh, rocky landscapes.
She added another rock plant to her collection to brighten up the rockery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alpines - plants, especially small perennials, suited to alpine or rock gardens
