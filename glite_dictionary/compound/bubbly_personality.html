bubbly personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌbli pərˌsɒnˈæləti / (AmE), / ˈbʌbli pəˌsɒnˈælɪti / (BrE)
a cheerful, lively, and outgoing character
cheerful, lively character

Examples
