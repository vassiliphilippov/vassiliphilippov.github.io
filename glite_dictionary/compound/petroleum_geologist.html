petroleum geologist

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊliəm dʒiˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊliəm dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies the origin, occurrence, movement, accumulation, and exploration of oil and natural gas beneath the earth's surface.
specialist in oil and gas geology

Examples

The company hired a petroleum geologist to identify new oil reserves.
As a petroleum geologist, she spent months analyzing rock samples in the field.
A petroleum geologist uses geological data to guide drilling operations for oil and gas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geologist - a scientist who studies the earth's physical structure and substances
