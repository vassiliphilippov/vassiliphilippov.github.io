roof rack

1. NOUN

/ ruf ræk / (AmE), / ruːf ræk / (BrE)
a framework attached to the roof of a car or train for carrying luggage or other large items
framework on top of a vehicle for carrying luggage

Examples

We strapped our bicycles to the roof rack before setting off on the road trip.
The car's roof rack made it much easier to transport the kayak.
She loaded the camping gear onto the roof rack so there would be more space inside the vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luggage rack - a shelf or framework for holding luggage, especially on trains or buses
