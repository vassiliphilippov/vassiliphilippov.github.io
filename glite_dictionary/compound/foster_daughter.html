foster daughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːstər ˈdɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
A girl or woman who is raised by people who are not her biological parents, usually through a formal care arrangement, but is not legally adopted.
girl raised by non-biological parents

Examples

She loves her foster daughter as if she were her own child.
After years in the system, Maria finally found a caring home as a foster daughter.
The couple supports their foster daughter in all her academic pursuits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foster child - a child who is raised by people not their biological parents
  • Antonym: adopted daughter - a girl who has been legally adopted as a daughter
