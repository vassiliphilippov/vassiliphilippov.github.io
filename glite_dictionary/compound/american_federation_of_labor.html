American Federation of Labor

1. PROPN

/ əˌmɛrɪkən ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv ˈleɪbər / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪkən ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
A former federation of North American labor unions that merged with the Congress of Industrial Organizations in 1955 to form the AFL-CIO.
historic U.S. labor union federation
american

Examples

The American Federation of Labor played a key role in improving working conditions in the United States.
He studied the history of the American Federation of Labor and its influence on modern unions.
After the merger, the American Federation of Labor became part of the AFL-CIO.
