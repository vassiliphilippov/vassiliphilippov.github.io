Battle of Manila Bay

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv məˈnɪlə beɪ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv məˈnɪlə beɪ / (BrE)
a naval battle in 1898 during the Spanish-American War in which the United States fleet under Admiral Dewey defeated the Spanish fleet in Manila Bay, Philippines
naval battle in Spanish-American War, 1898

Examples

The Battle of Manila Bay marked the beginning of American naval dominance in the Pacific.
Admiral Dewey became a national hero after his victory at the Battle of Manila Bay.
Historians often cite the Battle of Manila Bay as a turning point in the Spanish-American War.
