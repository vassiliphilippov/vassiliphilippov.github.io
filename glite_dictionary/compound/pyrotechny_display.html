pyrotechny display

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈrɑːtɛkni dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / paɪˈrɒtɛkni dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
an event featuring a performance or show of fireworks or pyrotechnic effects
fireworks or pyrotechnic show

Examples

The city organized a stunning pyrotechny display for the festival finale.
Spectators gathered early to get the best view of the pyrotechny display on the riverbank.
The wedding celebration ended with an impressive pyrotechny display that lit up the night sky.

Synset Examples

The pyrotechny display was the highlight of the New Year's celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fireworks display - a public show featuring fireworks

Etymology

From 'pyrotechny' (art of making or using fireworks), from Greek 'pyr' (fire) + 'techne' (art), and 'display' (exhibition, show).
