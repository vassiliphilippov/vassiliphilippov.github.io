car park

1. NOUN

/ kɑr pɑrk / (AmE), / kɑː pɑːk / (BrE)
an area or building where people can leave their cars for a period of time
parking area for cars
british

Examples

She left her car in the busy car park behind the supermarket.
The new office building has a multi-storey car park for employees.
We searched for an empty space in the car park on Saturday morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking lot - an area where cars can be parked, especially in American English
