round bone

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd boʊn / (AmE), / raʊnd bəʊn / (BrE)
a bone that is approximately round in shape, as found in certain animals or anatomical contexts
bone roughly round in shape
medical

Examples

The butcher sold a round bone cut ideal for making broth.
Veterinary students studied the structure of a round bone during anatomy class.
The x-ray revealed a fracture in the dog's round bone.
