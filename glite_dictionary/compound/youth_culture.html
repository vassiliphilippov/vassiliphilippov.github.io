youth culture

1. NOUN

/ juθ ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / juːθ ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The way of life, values, interests, and behaviors shared by young people as a distinct cultural group.
culture and values of young people

Examples

Throughout the 1960s, youth culture had a profound influence on art and music.
Fashion brands often target youth culture to stay relevant and appeal to younger consumers.
Parents sometimes find it hard to keep up with the rapidly changing trends of youth culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subculture - a cultural group within a larger culture
