Gondi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊn.di ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒn.di ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Gond people in south central India
Dravidian language of central India

Examples

The Gondi language is spoken by millions of people across central India.
Researchers are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Gondi language.
Many traditional folktales are preserved in the Gondi language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Telugu - a Dravidian language spoken in southeastern India
  • Synonym: Kannada - a major Dravidian language spoken mainly in southwestern India

Etymology

From 'Gond', the name of the indigenous people, plus 'language'.
