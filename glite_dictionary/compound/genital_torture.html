genital torture

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnət̬l ˈtɔrtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪtl ˈtɔːtʃə / (BrE)
Deliberate infliction of pain or injury to the genitals, often as a form of abuse or violence.
deliberate infliction of pain to genitals
medical

Examples

The victim suffered severe injury due to genital torture inflicted by her attackers.
Human rights organizations condemned the use of genital torture as a method of extracting confessions.
Medical professionals are trained to recognize signs of genital torture in abuse cases.
