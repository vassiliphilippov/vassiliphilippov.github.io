piping guan

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpɪŋ ɡwɑn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpɪŋ ɡwæn / (BrE)
a large, arboreal bird of the family Cracidae found in Central and South America, known for its piping calls
a large bird of Central and South America

Examples

The rainforest was alive with the distinctive calls of the piping guan.
Researchers observed a group of piping guans feeding in the treetops.
The piping guan is threatened by habitat loss in its native regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guan - bird of the family Cracidae native to Central and South America
