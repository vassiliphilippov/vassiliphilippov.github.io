order Lechanorales

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊrdər lɛkəˈnɔːrəˌliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə lɛkəˈnɔːrəliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic group in some classification systems, consisting of all lichens that produce apothecia
lichen order producing apothecia

Examples

Many lichens in the order Lechanorales exhibit distinctive cup-shaped fruiting bodies.
Researchers discovered a new species within the order Lechanorales on the mountain slopes.
The order Lechanorales is distinguished by its members' method of spore production.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on the genus Lecanora + -ales (denoting taxonomic order).
