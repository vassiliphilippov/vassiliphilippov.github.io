early winter cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli ˈwɪntər krɛs / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈwɪntə krɛs / (BrE)
A plant of the species Barbarea verna, native to southwestern Europe and cultivated in Florida, known for its edible leaves.
plant species; edible leaves

Examples

The leaves of early winter cress are often added to salads for a peppery flavor.
In Florida, early winter cress is grown as a cool-season crop.
Botanists identified the field specimen as early winter cress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winter cress - plant of the genus Barbarea
