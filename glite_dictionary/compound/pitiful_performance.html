pitiful performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtɪfəl pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪfʊl pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
a performance that is extremely poor or disappointing
extremely poor performance

Examples

The actor apologized after his pitiful performance in the film was widely criticized.
Fans were disappointed by the team's pitiful performance in the championship match.
Despite months of preparation, their pitiful performance left the judges unimpressed.

Synset Examples

The team's performance was pitiful last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disastrous performance - a very unsuccessful or calamitous performance
  • Antonym: impressive performance - a performance that is very good or admirable
