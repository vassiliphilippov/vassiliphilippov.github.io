good for you

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd fər ju / (AmE), / ɡʊd fə juː / (BrE)
Beneficial to one's health; healthful.
beneficial for health

Examples

Eating fresh fruit and vegetables is good for you.
Doctors say that regular exercise is good for you.
Green tea is widely believed to be good for you.
