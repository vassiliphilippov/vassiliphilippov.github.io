ginger knob

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒɚ nɑb / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə nɒb / (BrE)
a small piece or section of ginger root, typically used in cooking
piece of ginger root

Examples

She added a ginger knob to the boiling water for extra flavor.
For the recipe, you'll need a freshly peeled ginger knob.
He always keeps a ginger knob in his kitchen for tea or stir-fries.

Synset Examples

He grated a ginger knob into the stir-fry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger root - the underground stem of the ginger plant used as a spice
