little-league team

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əl liːɡ tiːm / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl liːɡ tiːm / (BrE)
A team that plays in a Little League, typically composed of young children.
a youth baseball team

Examples

She coached her son's little-league team every summer.
The little-league team won their district championship last weekend.
Joining a little-league team helped him learn teamwork and discipline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Little League - a youth baseball organization for children

Etymology

From 'Little League', the name of a youth baseball organization founded in 1939 in Pennsylvania, USA.
