junk shop

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌŋk ʃɑp / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells cheap secondhand goods, often of little value
shop selling cheap secondhand goods

Examples

She loves searching for hidden treasures in the local junk shop every Saturday.
The old junk shop on the corner closed after being in business for fifty years.
We found a vintage lamp in a junk shop while traveling through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrift shop - a shop selling secondhand goods, usually for charity
  • Degree Higher: antique shop - a shop selling old and valuable items
