struggles of immigrants

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrʌɡəlz əv ˈɪmɪɡrənts / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡlz əv ˈɪmɪɡrənts / (BrE)
the difficulties and challenges experienced by people who move to another country to live, often due to language barriers, discrimination, or financial hardship
challenges experienced by immigrants

Examples

The documentary powerfully highlights the struggles of immigrants as they try to build new lives in a foreign land.
She wrote a novel exploring the struggles of immigrants adapting to life in a big city.
Many organizations offer support to help ease the struggles of immigrants in their communities.

Synset Examples

The film depicted the struggles of immigrants in Amerika.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immigrant - a person who comes to live permanently in a foreign country
  • Synonym: challenges - difficult situations or obstacles
