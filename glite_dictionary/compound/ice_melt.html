ice melt

1. NOUN

/ aɪs mɛlt / (AmE), / aɪs mɛlt / (BrE)
a substance, often in granular or liquid form, used to melt ice on surfaces like roads and sidewalks
substance used to melt ice

Examples

Before the snowstorm, we stocked up on ice melt to keep the walkways safe.
Please use ice melt on the front steps so no one slips.
Some types of ice melt are safer for pets and plants than others.

Synset Examples

Darling, the driveway is covered in ice again, I think we should buy an ice melter before someone slips and breaks a leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deicer - a substance that removes or prevents ice formation
