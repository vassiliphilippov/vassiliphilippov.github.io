divan bed

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvæn bɛd / (AmE), / dɪˈvæn bɛd / (BrE)
A low, armless couch that can be used as a seat during the day and as a bed at night.
armless couch serving as bed and seat

Examples

My grandmother keeps a divan bed in her living room for when guests stay overnight.
They bought a stylish divan bed to save space in their small apartment.
During the day, the divan bed is covered with cushions and used as a sofa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa bed - a sofa that can be converted into a bed

Etymology

From Turkish 'divan' (council, large cushion or sofa).
