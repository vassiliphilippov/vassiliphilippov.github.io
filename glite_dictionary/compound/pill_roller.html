pill roller

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪl ˌroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈpɪl ˌrəʊlə / (BrE)
a health professional, especially a pharmacist, trained in preparing and dispensing medicines
pharmacist or drug dispenser
informal

Examples

The pill roller filled my prescription with a friendly smile.
Some old-timers still refer to the local pharmacist as a pill roller.
Decades ago, a pill roller would hand-mix many medicines on the spot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacist - a health professional qualified to prepare and dispense medicinal drugs
