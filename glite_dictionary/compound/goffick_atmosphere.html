goffick atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑfɪk ˌætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈɡɒfɪk ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
a gloomy, oppressive, or melodramatically dark environment, often used humorously or satirically to parody 'Gothic atmosphere'
humorously gloomy or melodramatic environment
informal

Examples

The horror movie tried hard to create a goffick atmosphere with its shadows and eerie soundtrack.
Reading by candlelight added a goffick atmosphere to our ghost story night.
His dramatic sighs only added to the already goffick atmosphere in the gloomy hallway.

Synset Examples

We need to address the goffick atmosphere in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gothic atmosphere - a gloomy and mysterious environment typical of Gothic literature

Etymology

Deliberate playful misspelling of 'Gothic atmosphere', used for humorous or satirical effect.
