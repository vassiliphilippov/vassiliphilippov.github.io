Nyamwezi language

1. NOUN

/ njɑːmˈweɪzi ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / njæmˈwɛzi ˈlaŋgwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Nyamwezi people in central Tanzania
Bantu language of central Tanzania

Examples

The Nyamwezi language is spoken by nearly two million people in Tanzania.
Linguists have documented many unique features of the Nyamwezi language.
He has studied the Nyamwezi language to better understand Bantu linguistics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swahili - a Bantu language widely spoken in East Africa

Etymology

From Swahili 'Wanyamwezi' (people of the moon) + 'language'.
