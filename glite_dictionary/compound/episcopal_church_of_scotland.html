Episcopal Church of Scotland

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈpɪskəpəl tʃɝtʃ əv ˈskɑt.lənd / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪskəpəl tʃɜːtʃ əv ˈskɒt.lənd / (BrE)
The autonomous Anglican church in Scotland that is part of the worldwide Anglican Communion.
Scottish branch of the Anglican Communion

Examples

Members of the Episcopal Church of Scotland gathered in Edinburgh to celebrate the feast day.
The Episcopal Church of Scotland maintains its own liturgy and governance, separate from the Church of England.
She was baptized in the Episcopal Church of Scotland as a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Church of England - The English branch of the Anglican Communion
