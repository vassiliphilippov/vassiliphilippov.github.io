success story

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsɛs ˌstɔri / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
An account of a person, organization, or endeavor that achieves outstanding success, often serving as inspiration to others.
inspiring account of achievement

Examples

The launch of the new app quickly became a success story in the tech world.
Coming from humble beginnings, he is a real success story for his community.
The magazine features a weekly success story to motivate its readers.

Synset Examples

The startup's growth is a remarkable success story.
Her promotion is a true success story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumph - great victory or achievement
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success
