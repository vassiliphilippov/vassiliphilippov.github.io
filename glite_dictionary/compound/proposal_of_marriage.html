proposal of marriage

1. NOUN

/ prəˈpoʊzəl əv ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊzəl əv ˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instance of formally asking someone to marry you
an offer to marry someone

Examples

His proposal of marriage took her completely by surprise.
After a romantic dinner, she accepted his proposal of marriage.
A proposal of marriage is a significant milestone in many people's lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engagement - a formal agreement to get married
  • Antonym: rejection - the act of refusing an offer or proposal
