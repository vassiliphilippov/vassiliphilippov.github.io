sand leek

1. NOUN

/ sænd lik / (AmE), / sænd liːk / (BrE)
A European plant of the onion family, cultivated and used like leeks in cooking.
a European plant similar to the leek

Examples

The sand leek is often grown for its edible stems and leaves.
After harvesting, she used sand leek in a traditional stew recipe.
Unlike common leeks, sand leek has a slightly stronger flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leek - an edible plant of the onion family, cultivated for its leaf sheaths
  • Synonym: onion - a common vegetable plant with edible bulbs and leaves
