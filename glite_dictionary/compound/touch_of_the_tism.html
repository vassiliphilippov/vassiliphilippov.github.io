touch of the 'tism

1. NOUN

/ tʌt͡ʃ əv ðə ˈtɪzəm / (AmE), / tʌt͡ʃ ɒv ðə ˈtɪzəm / (BrE)
(slang, often offensive) A hint of characteristics associated with autism, used to describe eccentric or unusual behavior. The phrase is sometimes considered disparaging or insensitive.
hint of autism-like eccentricity (offensive, slang)
slang offensive

Examples

Some people felt his comments had a touch of the 'tism, making others uncomfortable.
The film's quirky protagonist is said to have a touch of the 'tism by some reviewers.
Joking that someone's fashion sense has a touch of the 'tism is considered insensitive and inappropriate.
