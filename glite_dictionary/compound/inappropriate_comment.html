inappropriate comment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈprəʊpriət ˈkɒmənt / (BrE)
a remark that is unsuitable, offensive, or not appropriate for the situation
unsuitable or offensive remark

Examples

His boss warned him after he made an inappropriate comment during the meeting.
Making an inappropriate comment on social media can damage your reputation.
The teacher quickly addressed the student's inappropriate comment to maintain order in class.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a creepo after making inappropriate comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offensive comment - remark that is likely to insult or upset people
  • Antonym: appropriate comment - remark that is suitable or fitting for the situation
