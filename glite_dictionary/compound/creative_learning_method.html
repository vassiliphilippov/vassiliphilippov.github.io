creative learning method

1. NOUN

/ kɹiˈeɪtɪv ˈlɝnɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / kɹiˈeɪtɪv ˈlɜːnɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)
a teaching approach that uses original, imaginative, or innovative techniques to facilitate learning
innovative educational approach

Examples

The school implemented a creative learning method to help students develop problem-solving skills.
A creative learning method often encourages students to think outside the box and explore new ideas.
Teachers are always searching for a creative learning method that makes complex topics easier to understand.

Synset Examples

Examinationism often overlooks creative learning methods.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: traditional learning method - a conventional or established approach to teaching and learning
  • Synonym: active learning - a teaching method that engages students directly in the learning process
