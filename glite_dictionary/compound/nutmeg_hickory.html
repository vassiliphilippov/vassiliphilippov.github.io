nutmeg hickory

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ˈhɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈnʌtmɛɡ ˈhɪkəri / (BrE)
A hickory tree native to the southern United States and Mexico, known for its hard, nutmeg-shaped nuts.
a species of hickory with nutmeg-shaped nuts

Examples

The nutmeg hickory produces distinctive hard nuts that resemble nutmeg in shape.
Foresters in Texas often encounter the nutmeg hickory while surveying woodland areas.
In autumn, the nuts of the nutmeg hickory are a valuable food source for wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hickory - a type of deciduous tree with strong wood and edible nuts
