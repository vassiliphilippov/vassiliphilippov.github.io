dark-coloured

1. ADJ

/ dɑrkˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / dɑːkˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having a dark color or shade; not light in color
having a dark color

Examples

The dark-coloured curtains made the room feel smaller.
She prefers dark-coloured clothes during the winter months.
A flock of dark-coloured birds flew over the field.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light-coloured - having a light color or shade
  • Synonym: dark - having very little light or being a deep color
