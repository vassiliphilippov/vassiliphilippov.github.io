funny joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni doʊk / (AmE), / ˈfʌni dəʊk / (BrE)
A remark or story that causes amusement or laughter.
humorous remark or story

Examples

He always has a funny joke ready to lighten the mood at work.
Everyone burst out laughing at her funny joke about the talking dog.
It's not easy to come up with a truly funny joke on the spot.

Synset Examples
