belted kingfisher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛltɪd ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛltɪd ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃə / (BrE)
a North American kingfisher with greyish-blue plumage and a distinctive chestnut band across its chest
North American kingfisher species

Examples

We spotted a belted kingfisher perched on a branch above the river.
The distinctive call of the belted kingfisher echoed across the lake.
A belted kingfisher swooped down to catch a fish with impressive speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingfisher - a brightly colored bird that catches fish by diving
