modern bidet

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɚn bɪˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɒdn bɪˈdeɪ / (BrE)
a bathroom fixture designed for personal hygiene, typically using a stream of water to clean the user's genital and anal areas, often equipped with advanced features such as heated seats or adjustable water jets
advanced bathroom hygiene fixture

Examples

Some luxury apartments are now installing modern bidets in every bathroom for enhanced comfort.
The modern bidet offers features like a dryer and seat warmer, making it popular among homeowners.
I was surprised to see a modern bidet in the hotel bathroom, complete with a remote control panel.

Synset Examples

The hotel room featured a modern bidet beside the toilet.
