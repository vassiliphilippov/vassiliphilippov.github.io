military man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri mæn / (BrE)
a man who serves in the armed forces; a male member of a military force
a male member of the armed forces

Examples

His grandfather was a decorated military man who participated in several campaigns.
As a military man, he was accustomed to discipline and order.
The ceremony honored every military man who served in the last war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army
  • Antonym: military woman - a woman who serves in the armed forces
