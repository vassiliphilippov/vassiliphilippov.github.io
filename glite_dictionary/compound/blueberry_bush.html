blueberry bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbluːbəri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A shrub of the genus Vaccinium that produces blueberries.
shrub that bears blueberries

Examples

We planted a blueberry bush in our backyard last spring.
Each blueberry bush was covered in ripe fruit by mid-July.
She cares for her blueberry bush carefully to ensure a good harvest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blueberry - a small, round, blue-purple berry from the genus Vaccinium
