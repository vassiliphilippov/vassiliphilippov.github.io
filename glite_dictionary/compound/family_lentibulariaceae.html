family Lentibulariaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛntəbjʊˌlɛriˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌlɛntɪbjʊˌlɛəriˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
A family of carnivorous aquatic or bog plants, including the genera Utricularia, Pinguicula, and Genlisea.
plant family of carnivorous aquatic or bog plants

Examples

Many species in the family Lentibulariaceae have evolved unique mechanisms for trapping small aquatic organisms.
Botanists often study the family Lentibulariaceae for insights into plant carnivory.
The family Lentibulariaceae includes fascinating plants like butterworts and bladderworts.

Etymology

From New Latin Lentibularia (type genus) + -aceae (family suffix).
