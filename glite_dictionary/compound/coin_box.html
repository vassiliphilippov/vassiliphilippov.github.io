coin box

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪn bɑks / (AmE), / kɔɪn bɒks / (BrE)
the part of a slot machine or other device that collects and holds coins inserted by users
receptacle for coins in machines

Examples

The technician unlocked the coin box to count the day's earnings from the slot machine.
After hours of play, the coin box was almost full and needed to be emptied.
Please insert the key to open the coin box and collect the deposited coins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cash box - a container for storing cash, especially in businesses
  • Synonym: money box - a container for storing coins, especially by children
