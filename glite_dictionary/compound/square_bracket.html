square bracket

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɛr ˈbrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈskwɛə ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
either of the two punctuation marks [ or ] used to enclose words, phrases, or figures in text
punctuation mark: [ or ]

Examples

Please put the quotation inside square brackets if you have made any changes.
She used square brackets to add her own comments to the original text.
Mathematical expressions are often grouped using square brackets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracket - punctuation mark (parenthesis, [ ] or { })
