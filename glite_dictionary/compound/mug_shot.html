mug shot

1. NOUN

/ mʌɡ ʃɑt / (AmE), / mʌɡ ʃɒt / (BrE)
A photograph of a person's face, typically taken by the police after a person is arrested and used for identification purposes.
police photograph of a person's face

Examples

The police released the mug shot of the suspect after his arrest.
Her mug shot appeared in several newspapers following the incident.
He was embarrassed when his old mug shot was found online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police photograph - a photograph taken by the police for records

Etymology

From slang 'mug' (face) plus 'shot' (photograph).
