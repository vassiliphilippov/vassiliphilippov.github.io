group discussion

1. NOUN

/ ɡruːp dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ɡruːp dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a structured conversation among several people about a specific topic, often to exchange ideas or reach a decision
structured conversation among several people

Examples

We had a lively group discussion about climate change during class.
Each applicant participated in a group discussion as part of the interview process.
The teacher encouraged a group discussion to explore different viewpoints on the issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discussion - an act of talking about something with another person or group
