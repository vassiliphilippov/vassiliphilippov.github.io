umpteenth time

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌmp.tiːnθ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈʌmp.tiːnθ taɪm / (BrE)
a very large but indefinite number of times, often suggesting annoyance or exasperation
very many times; often suggesting frustration

Examples

He forgot his keys for the umpteenth time this month.
She explained the procedure for the umpteenth time, trying not to sound impatient.
For the umpteenth time, please close the door behind you.

Synset Examples

This is the umpteenth time we’ve had this discussion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umpteenth - an indefinitely large number in a series

Etymology

Formed from 'umpteenth', an extended form of 'umpty' (meaning 'an indefinite number'), and 'time'.
