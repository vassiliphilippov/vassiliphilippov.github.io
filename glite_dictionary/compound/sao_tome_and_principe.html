Sao Tome and Principe

1. PROPN

/ saʊ təˈmeɪ ænd ˈprɪnsɪpeɪ / (AmE), / saʊ təˈmeɪ ænd ˈprɪnsɪpeɪ / (BrE)
an island nation in the South Atlantic Ocean off the west coast of Africa, consisting of two main islands; it gained independence from Portugal in 1975.
island country off west Africa

Examples

Sao Tome and Principe is known for its rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes.
Oil exploration has become increasingly important to the economy of Sao Tome and Principe.
Portuguese is the official language of Sao Tome and Principe, reflecting its colonial history.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'São Tomé e Príncipe,' meaning 'Saint Thomas and Prince.'
