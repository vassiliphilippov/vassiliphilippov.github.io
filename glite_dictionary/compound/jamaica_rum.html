Jamaica rum

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪ.kə rʌm / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪ.kə rʌm / (BrE)
a strong, flavorful rum distilled in Jamaica, known for its pungent taste and aroma
distinctive rum from Jamaica

Examples

That bar specializes in cocktails made with Jamaica rum to give them a unique flavor.
The rich aroma of Jamaica rum adds depth to traditional fruitcakes.
She brought back a bottle of Jamaica rum as a souvenir from her trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rum - an alcoholic drink distilled from sugarcane or molasses
