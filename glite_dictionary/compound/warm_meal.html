warm meal

1. NOUN

/ wɔrm mil / (AmE), / wɔːm miːl / (BrE)
a meal that is heated and typically comforting, as opposed to being served cold
a heated, comforting meal

Examples

After a long walk in the snow, he was grateful for a warm meal waiting at home.
The shelter provides a warm meal to anyone in need every evening.
A warm meal can lift your spirits on a chilly day.

Synset Examples

The beggar thanked her for the warm meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot meal - a meal that is served hot
  • Antonym: cold meal - a meal that is served cold
