straw wine

1. NOUN

/ strɔ waɪn / (AmE), / strɔː waɪn / (BrE)
a sweet wine made from grapes that have been partially dried on the vine or on straw mats before fermentation, resulting in concentrated sugars and flavors
sweet wine from partially dried grapes

Examples

She served a chilled glass of straw wine after dinner.
Many European straw wines are made using traditional sun-drying techniques.
The rich sweetness of straw wine pairs well with blue cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dessert wine - a sweet wine generally served with dessert
