beige flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪʒ flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪʒ flaɡ / (BrE)
a signal, typically a flag colored beige, used to indicate a pause or temporary halt, especially representing a truce
signal indicating a pause or truce

Examples

The racers slowed down as the beige flag was waved, signaling a temporary pause in the competition.
During negotiations, both sides agreed to raise a beige flag to halt their disputes for the day.
He saw the beige flag and knew it was time to stop and wait for further instructions.

Synset Examples

They raised the beige flag to signal a pause.
The beige flag was used to denote a truce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white flag - a white flag used as a symbol of surrender or truce
