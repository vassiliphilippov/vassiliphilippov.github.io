order Saprolegniales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌsæproʊˌlɛgˈnaɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌsæprəʊˌlɛgˈnaɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of chiefly aquatic fungi, including water molds and similar organisms
order of aquatic fungi
medical

Examples

Many species in the order Saprolegniales play a crucial role in the decomposition of organic matter in freshwater habitats.
The order Saprolegniales includes water molds that can become pathogenic to fish in aquaculture systems.
Research on the order Saprolegniales has provided insights into fungal adaptations to aquatic environments.

Etymology

From the genus name Saprolegnia and suffix -ales denoting taxonomic order (New Latin).
