liquid bleach

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd bliːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd bliːtʃ / (BrE)
a clear, water-based solution containing bleaching agents, especially sodium hypochlorite, used for disinfecting or whitening fabrics and surfaces
bleaching solution for laundry or cleaning

Examples

Please handle the liquid bleach carefully, as it can damage fabrics and irritate skin.
Adding a cup of liquid bleach to white laundry helps keep clothes bright.
The janitor used liquid bleach to disinfect the bathroom surfaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bleach - a chemical used to whiten or disinfect
