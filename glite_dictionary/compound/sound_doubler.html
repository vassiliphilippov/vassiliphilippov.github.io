sound doubler

1. NOUN

/ saʊnd ˈdʌblɚ / (AmE), / saʊnd ˈdʌblə / (BrE)
a device used to enhance audio quality by duplicating or enriching the original sound signal
audio device for enhancing sound quality

Examples

The new recording studio features a sound doubler to create a richer listening experience.
A sound doubler can make vocals and instruments stand out in a mix.
Engineers used a sound doubler to improve the clarity on the final track.

Synset Examples

The audio system includes a sound doubler for clarity.
