shaggy dog story

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæɡi dɔɡ ˌstɔri / (AmE), / ˈʃæɡi dɒɡ ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
A long, rambling joke or story which is deliberately pointless or absurd, with its humor arising from its lack of a satisfying ending.
deliberately pointless or absurdly long joke

Examples

He stood up at the party and told a hilarious shaggy dog story that had everyone groaning at the end.
The comedian's routine included a classic shaggy dog story that dragged on before a deliberately flat punchline.
I've heard that shaggy dog story before, but it still makes me laugh at how pointlessly complicated it is.

Etymology

From the title of an early example involving a shaggy dog; first used in the mid-20th century.
