real moaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːəl ˈmoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈməʊnə / (BrE)
a person who frequently complains, especially about trivial matters
person who often complains
informal

Examples

Ever since the new manager arrived, Tom has been a real moaner about every little change.
Don’t be such a real moaner—try to see the positives for once.
At work, she quickly gained a reputation as a real moaner who was never satisfied.

Synset Examples

She’s a real moaner when things don’t go her way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complainer - someone who often complains
  • Synonym: grumbler - person who grumbles or complains frequently
