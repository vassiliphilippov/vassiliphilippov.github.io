Kerry blue terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛri blu ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈkɛri blu ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a medium-sized Irish breed of terrier with a silky blue-grey coat
Irish breed of terrier with blue-grey coat

Examples

The Kerry blue terrier is known for its intelligence and loyalty.
At the dog show, Sarah proudly displayed her Kerry blue terrier.
Grooming a Kerry blue terrier requires regular brushing to maintain its distinctive coat.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: terrier - a breed of dog originally bred for hunting vermin

Etymology

Named after County Kerry in Ireland, where the breed originated.
