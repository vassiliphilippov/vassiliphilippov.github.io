Old Gentleman

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈdʒɛntlmən / (AmE), / əʊld ˈdʒɛntləmən / (BrE)
a respectful term for a wise or distinguished elderly man
respectful term for elderly man

Examples

Everyone in the neighborhood looks up to the Old Gentleman for advice.
The children gathered around while the Old Gentleman told them stories from his youth.
At the annual dinner, the Old Gentleman was the guest of honor.

Synset Examples

Everyone respects the Old Gentleman for his wisdom.
The Old Gentleman shared his life stories with the children.
The Old Gentleman always tips well at the café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old man - informal term for an elderly man, sometimes used in reference to a father or husband
