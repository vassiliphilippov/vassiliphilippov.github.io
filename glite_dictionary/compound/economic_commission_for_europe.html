Economic Commission for Europe

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈkɑnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fər ˈjʊrəp / (AmE), / iˈkɒnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fə ˈjʊərəp / (BrE)
the commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council responsible for promoting economic development and cooperation in Europe
UN agency for European economic development

Examples

The Economic Commission for Europe coordinates efforts among member states to improve economic policies.
As a delegate, she attended the annual Economic Commission for Europe conference in Geneva.
Several new trade guidelines were published by the Economic Commission for Europe last month.
