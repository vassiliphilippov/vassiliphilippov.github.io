political sympathies

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsɪmpəθiz / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsɪmpəθiːz / (BrE)
the opinions or feelings that someone has about political issues or parties, often indicating which side they support
views or feelings about political issues

Examples

Many assumed her political sympathies leaned to the left.
He kept his political sympathies private to avoid arguments at work.
The newspaper is known for its clear political sympathies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political views - opinions or beliefs about politics
  • Synonym: political allegiance - loyalty or support for a political party or ideology
