Chinese primrose

1. NOUN

/ ˈʧaɪ.niz ˈprɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz ˈprɪm.rəʊz / (BrE)
a species of primrose native to China and widely cultivated for its ornamental flowers, especially Primula sinensis
an ornamental primrose plant native to China

Examples

The Chinese primrose is popular for its colorful blossoms in winter gardens.
She planted a row of Chinese primrose along the garden path to brighten the area.
Botanists admire the delicate petals of the Chinese primrose for their unusual hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primrose - a flowering plant of the genus Primula
