cubic kilometer

1. NOUN

/ ˌkjuːbɪk kɪˈlɑːmɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˌkjuːbɪk kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a unit of volume equal to that of a cube with edges one kilometer long, equivalent to one billion cubic meters
unit of volume, one billion cubic meters

Examples

Scientists estimate the lake contains over 1,000 cubic kilometers of water.
If all that ice melted, it would release several cubic kilometers of water into the ocean.
The reservoir holds about two cubic kilometers of fresh water.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cubic meter - unit of volume, one meter each side
