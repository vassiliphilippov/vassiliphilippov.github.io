heart-leaved aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt ˌlivd ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌliːvd ˈastə / (BrE)
A perennial woodland aster of eastern North America, recognized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pale flowers.
perennial woodland aster of eastern North America

Examples

The heart-leaved aster thrives in shady woodland areas across eastern North America.
Gardeners often plant the heart-leaved aster for its attractive foliage and late-summer blooms.
While hiking, we spotted a cluster of heart-leaved asters growing near the forest edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the genus Aster, with star-shaped flower heads
