white avens

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈævənz / (AmE), / waɪt ˈævənz / (BrE)
A North American plant (Geum canadense) with white flowers, belonging to the rose family.
North American white-flowered plant (Geum canadense)

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with clusters of white avens in full bloom.
You can identify white avens by their small, five-petaled white flowers.
Many gardeners appreciate white avens for its ability to thrive in shady woodlands.

Examples

Botanists collected samples of white avens for their research on native wildflowers.
