arms deal

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz diːl / (AmE), / ɑːmz diːl / (BrE)
an agreement to buy or sell military weapons, often between countries or organizations
agreement to buy or sell military weapons

Examples

The government denied that any arms deal had taken place with the neighboring country.
Reports emerged about a secret arms deal involving advanced missile systems.
The international community imposed sanctions following the illegal arms deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weapons deal - a deal involving the sale or purchase of weapons
  • Antonym: peace treaty - a formal agreement to end conflict or war
