rock salt

1. NOUN

/ rɑk sɑlt / (AmE), / rɒk sɒlt / (BrE)
naturally occurring, coarse crystalline form of sodium chloride, often used for de-icing roads or in cooking
naturally occurring crystalline sodium chloride

Examples

In winter, workers spread rock salt on icy roads to prevent accidents.
The chef used coarse rock salt to season the roast beef.
Large deposits of rock salt are mined for industrial and culinary use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halite - the mineral form of sodium chloride; a synonym for rock salt
