little pressie

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈprɛsi / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈprɛsi / (BrE)
An informal term for a small gift.
small informal gift
informal

Examples

She gave her friend a little pressie to cheer her up after a tough week.
At the end of the party, each child went home with a little pressie.
He was delighted to find a little pressie waiting for him on his desk.

Synset Examples

I bought him a little pressie for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pressie - informal term for a present or gift
