wrist band

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪst ˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈrɪst ˌbænd / (BrE)
A strip of material, often elastic or decorative, worn around the wrist as an accessory or for practical purposes.
band or bracelet worn on the wrist

Examples

She wore a colorful wrist band as a symbol of support for the charity.
Athletes often use a wrist band to absorb sweat during games.
His hospital wrist band contained his identification and medical information.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracelet - an ornamental band or chain worn around the wrist
