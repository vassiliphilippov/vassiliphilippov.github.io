comparative anatomist

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpɛrətɪv əˈnætəmɪst / (AmE), / kəmˈpærətɪv əˈnætəmɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies and compares the anatomical structures of different animals
scientist who compares animal anatomy

Examples

As a comparative anatomist, she examined the skeletal similarities between birds and dinosaurs.
The work of a comparative anatomist often involves dissecting specimens from various species.
Many discoveries in evolutionary biology have been made by pioneering comparative anatomists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anatomist - a specialist in anatomy
