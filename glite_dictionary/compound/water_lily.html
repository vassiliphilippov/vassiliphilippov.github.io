water lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ər ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlɪli / (BrE)
An aquatic plant with large, round floating leaves and typically fragrant white, pink, or yellow flowers, belonging to the family Nymphaeaceae.
aquatic plant with floating leaves and showy flowers

Examples

A single water lily bloomed serenely on the surface of the pond.
The garden’s pond was filled with beautiful water lilies of various colors.
Claude Monet painted many canvases inspired by water lilies in his garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lotus - an aquatic plant with large flowers, sometimes confused with water lily
