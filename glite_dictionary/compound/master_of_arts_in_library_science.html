Master of Arts in Library Science

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ əv ˈɑrts ɪn laɪˈbrɛr.i ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər əv ˈɑːts ɪn laɪˈbrəri ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
A postgraduate degree awarded for advanced study in library science, usually focusing on the organization, management, and dissemination of information in libraries.
postgraduate degree in library science

Examples

She earned a Master of Arts in Library Science before becoming a university librarian.
Candidates with a Master of Arts in Library Science are preferred for this position.
After completing her Master of Arts in Library Science, she specialized in digital archival work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Master of Library Science - a master's degree specifically in library science
