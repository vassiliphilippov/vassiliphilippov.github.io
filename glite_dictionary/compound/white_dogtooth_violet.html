white dogtooth violet

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈdɔgˌtuθ ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / waɪt ˈdɒgˌtuːθ ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
A North American plant of the lily family, having solitary white flowers with yellow centers and blue or pink exteriors.
North American plant with white flowers

Examples

The white dogtooth violet blooms early in the spring in woodland areas.
Gardeners often plant white dogtooth violet for its delicate and attractive flowers.
We spotted a rare white dogtooth violet while hiking through the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogtooth violet - a plant of the lily family with mottled leaves and nodding flowers
