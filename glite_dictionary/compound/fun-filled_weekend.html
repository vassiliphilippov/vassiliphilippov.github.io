fun-filled weekend

1. NOUN

/ fʌn fɪld ˈwiːkˌɛnd / (AmE), / fʌn fɪld ˈwiːkˌɛnd / (BrE)
A weekend packed with enjoyable or entertaining activities.
weekend full of enjoyable activities

Examples

We're planning a fun-filled weekend at the amusement park with the kids.
After months of hard work, they finally enjoyed a fun-filled weekend by the seaside.
The festival organizers promise a fun-filled weekend for visitors of all ages.

Synset Examples

We visited Brisneyland for a fun-filled weekend.
