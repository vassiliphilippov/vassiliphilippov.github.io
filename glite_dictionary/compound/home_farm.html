home farm

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm fɑrm / (AmE), / həʊm fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm, usually located near a large house or estate, that supplies it with produce and provisions.
farm supplying a large estate

Examples

The produce from the home farm was used to supply the main house throughout the year.
She managed the home farm while her husband oversaw the estate's finances.
In Victorian times, many country houses relied on a home farm for fresh milk and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farm - an area of land used for growing crops or raising animals
