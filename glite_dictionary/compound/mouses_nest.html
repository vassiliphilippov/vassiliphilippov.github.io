mouse's nest

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊsɪz nɛst / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsɪz nɛst / (BrE)
a nest built by a mouse where it lives and raises its young
nest built by a mouse

Examples

We found a mouse's nest inside the old cardboard box in the attic.
The gardener disturbed a mouse's nest while turning over the compost heap.
A mouse's nest is often made of shredded paper, grass, and other soft materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure built by animals for laying eggs or giving birth
