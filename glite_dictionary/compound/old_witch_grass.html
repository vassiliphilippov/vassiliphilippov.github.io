old witch grass

1. NOUN

/ oʊld wɪtʃ ɡræs / (AmE), / əʊld wɪtʃ ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A North American grass with slender, brush-like panicles, often considered a weed on cultivated land.
a North American weed grass

Examples

The farmer spent hours pulling old witch grass from the edges of his cornfield.
In some regions, old witch grass is notorious for spreading quickly among crops.
The slender panicles of old witch grass wave gently in the summer breeze.
