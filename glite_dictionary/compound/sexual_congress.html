sexual congress

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈkɑŋɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈkɒŋɡrəs / (BrE)
the physical act of sexual intercourse, typically referring to the insertion of the penis into the vagina
sexual intercourse (formal)

Examples

The contract explicitly forbids sexual congress between employees.
He was accused of engaging in sexual congress with a minor, a serious criminal offense.
In ancient texts, sexual congress is often described in metaphorical language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual intercourse - the act of sexual union between individuals
  • Synonym: copulation - the act of mating or sexual union

Etymology

From Latin 'congressus' (a meeting), especially in the sense of union or coming together.
