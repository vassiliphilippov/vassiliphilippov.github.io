mixing faucet

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪksɪŋ ˌfɔsɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪksɪŋ ˌfɔːsɪt / (BrE)
a faucet that blends hot and cold water feeds to deliver a single regulated water temperature
faucet blending hot and cold water

Examples

The new mixing faucet allows you to easily adjust the water temperature in the sink.
Please replace the old taps with a more modern mixing faucet to improve convenience.
A mixing faucet provides both hot and cold water streams through a single spout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixer tap - British term for a faucet that blends hot and cold water
