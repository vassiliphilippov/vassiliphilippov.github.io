Teamsters Union

1. PROPN

/ ˈtiːmstɚz ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / ˈtiːmstəz ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a major American labor union representing truck drivers, warehouse workers, and others in transportation and related industries
major US labor union for truck drivers and more
american

Examples

The Teamsters Union called for a strike to protest wage cuts across the industry.
She has worked with the Teamsters Union for over a decade, representing warehouse employees.
Negotiations between the company and the Teamsters Union continued late into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: International Brotherhood of Teamsters - official name of the Teamsters Union

Etymology

From 'teamster' (a driver of a team of draft animals, later applied to truck drivers) + 'union'.
