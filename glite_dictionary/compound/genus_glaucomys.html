genus Glaucomys

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡlɔˈkoʊ.mɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡlaʊˈkəʊ.mɪs / (BrE)
A genus of New World flying squirrels, including the northern and southern flying squirrels.
genus of New World flying squirrels

Examples

The genus Glaucomys includes both the northern and southern flying squirrels.
Researchers have studied the habitat preferences of the genus Glaucomys in North America.
Conservation efforts focus on protecting members of the genus Glaucomys due to their declining populations.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind) and 'Glaucomys', from Greek 'glaukos' (gleaming, grey) and 'mys' (mouse).
