system of numeration

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstəm əv nuːˌmɛɹˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm əv njuːməˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a method or notation used for representing numbers, such as the decimal or binary system
a notation for representing numbers

Examples

The binary system of numeration is commonly used in computing.
Ancient civilizations developed their own system of numeration to record quantities and transactions.
The decimal system of numeration relies on ten digits, from zero to nine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numeral system - a system for expressing numbers with a set of symbols or rules
