deep-chested

1. ADJ

/ dipˈtʃɛstɪd / (AmE), / diːpˈtʃɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) having a large or prominent chest that extends downward, often indicating strength or stamina.
having a large or prominent chest

Examples

The deep-chested retriever is known for its stamina and lung capacity.
Greyhounds are typically deep-chested and built for speed.
The horse's deep-chested build makes it well-suited for long-distance riding.

Synset Examples

a deep-chested breed of dog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel-chested - having a large, rounded chest
