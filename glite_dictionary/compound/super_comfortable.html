super comfortable

1. ADJ

/ ˈsuːpər ˈkʌmfərtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsuːpə ˈkʌmfətəbl / (BrE)
Extremely comfortable; offering a very high degree of physical or emotional ease.
extremely comfortable
informal

Examples

The sofa in her living room is super comfortable and perfect for naps.
These new sneakers are super comfortable for walking long distances.
I found the bed at the hotel super comfortable after a long day of travel.
