first and last of its kind

1. NOUN

/ fɝst ənd læst əv ɪts kaɪnd / (AmE), / fɜːst ənd lɑːst əv ɪts kaɪnd / (BrE)
used to describe an event, occurrence, or thing that is unique and will not be repeated; an unprecedented and singular instance
unique and unrepeatable occurrence

Examples

The lunar mission was the first and last of its kind, making history for its originality.
Many believed the art exhibition would be the first and last of its kind because of its enormous cost.
The ceremony was the first and last of its kind, as it commemorated a once-in-a-lifetime achievement.

Synset Examples

This event is the first and last of its kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one of a kind - unique, unlike anything else
  • Synonym: unprecedented - never done or known before
