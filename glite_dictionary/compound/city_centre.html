city centre

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˌsentər / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌsentə / (BrE)
the central part of a city, often where the main commercial and cultural activities are concentrated
central part of a city

Examples

The city centre was crowded with shoppers on Saturday afternoon.
Many historic buildings are located in the city centre.
Roadworks in the city centre caused major traffic delays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downtown - the central area or main business district of a city (especially in North America)
