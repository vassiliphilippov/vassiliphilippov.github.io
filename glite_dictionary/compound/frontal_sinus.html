frontal sinus

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrʌn.t̬əl ˈsaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl ˈsaɪ.nəs / (BrE)
either of a pair of air-filled cavities located in the frontal bone of the skull, above the eyes
air cavity in the frontal bone
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor noticed that my frontal sinus was swollen.
Sinus infections can sometimes affect the frontal sinus, causing pain above the eyebrows.
A CT scan revealed a blockage in her left frontal sinus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sinus - an air-filled cavity within a bone or tissue

Etymology

From 'frontal', pertaining to the forehead, and 'sinus', from Latin 'sinus' (curve, cavity).
