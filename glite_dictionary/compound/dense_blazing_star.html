dense blazing star

1. NOUN

/ dɛns ˈbleɪzɪŋ stɑr / (AmE), / dɛns ˈbleɪzɪŋ stɑː / (BrE)
A perennial plant of southeastern and central United States with very dense spikes of purple flowers, often grown for cut flowers.
a perennial plant with dense purple flower spikes

Examples

The garden was brightened by the addition of several dense blazing star plants along the pathway.
Florists often use dense blazing star in arrangements because of its tall, vibrant spikes of purple flowers.
Many pollinators are attracted to the dense blazing star when it blooms during the summer months.
