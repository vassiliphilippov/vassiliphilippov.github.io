Nez Perce language

1. NOUN

/ nɛz ˈpɜrs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / nɛz ˈpɜːs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Shahaptian language spoken by the Nez Perce people of the northwestern United States
language of the Nez Perce people

Examples

She is studying the Nez Perce language as part of her research on indigenous cultures.
The Nez Perce language has several unique sounds not found in English.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Nez Perce language for future generations.

Etymology

From French 'nez percé' (pierced nose), the name given by French explorers to the Nez Perce people.
