climbing fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ fɜːn / (BrE)
Any of several ferns of the genus Lygodium that climb by twining their fronds around other plants or objects.
a twining fern of the genus Lygodium

Examples

The forest floor was covered with thick mats of climbing fern weaving up the trees.
Gardeners often struggle to control the rapid spread of climbing fern in warm, humid climates.
The climbing fern sends long fronds upward, using nearby plants for support.
