cant hook

1. NOUN

/ kænt hʊk / (AmE), / kænt hʊk / (BrE)
A tool similar to a peavey but with a hook instead of a spike, used for handling and turning logs.
tool for handling logs

Examples

The lumberjack grabbed the cant hook to turn the heavy log with ease.
Using a cant hook, she maneuvered the timber into the right position for cutting.
The old cant hook in the shed belonged to my grandfather, who worked in the logging industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peavey - a logging tool with a spike, used for handling logs
