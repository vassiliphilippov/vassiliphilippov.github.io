test equipment

1. NOUN

/ tɛst ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / tɛst ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
instruments or devices used to carry out tests or examinations, especially for measuring, analyzing, or verifying something
devices used for testing purposes

Examples

The laboratory was filled with test equipment for analyzing chemical samples.
Before the inspection, all test equipment was calibrated to ensure accurate results.
Technicians rely on specialized test equipment to diagnose electrical faults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testing device - a device used for testing
