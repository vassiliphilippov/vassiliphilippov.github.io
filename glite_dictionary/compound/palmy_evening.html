palmy evening

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːlmi ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlmi ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
An evening that is pleasant, peaceful, and relaxed, often with an atmosphere of prosperity or calm.
pleasant and relaxed evening

Examples

They spent a palmy evening on the terrace, enjoying the gentle breeze.
A palmy evening by the lake made their vacation truly memorable.
After a stressful week, the couple longed for a palmy evening of tranquility.

Synset Examples

They enjoyed a palmy evening under the stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idyllic evening - an evening that is extremely happy, peaceful, or picturesque

Etymology

From 'palmy', meaning prosperous or flourishing, ultimately from Latin 'palma' (palm tree), symbolizing victory or peace.
