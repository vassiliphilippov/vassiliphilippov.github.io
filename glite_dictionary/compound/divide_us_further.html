to divide us further

1. VERB

/ dɪˈvaɪd ʌs ˈfɝːðɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪd ʌs ˈfɜːðə / (BrE)
to increase separation or conflict among people; to make a group more divided or less united.
increase conflict or separation among people

Examples

The constant arguments about politics only divide us further as a society.
Instead of trying to solve problems, blaming others will only divide us further.
We must focus on what unites us, not on what could divide us further.

Synset Examples

Such slurs only serve to divide us further.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unite us - bring people together or make them more unified
