small arm

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ɑrm / (AmE), / smɔːl ɑːm / (BrE)
A portable firearm, such as a handgun, rifle, or shotgun, that can be carried and used by an individual.
portable firearm (e.g. rifle, handgun)

Examples

The army supplied the soldiers with modern small arms for better mobility.
Collectors often seek rare small arms from historic battles.
All personnel were required to complete training with a small arm before deployment.

Synset Examples

he wore his firearm in a shoulder holster

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firearm - a gun, especially a handgun or rifle, that can be carried
  • Degree Higher: long gun - a firearm designed to be fired from the shoulder, typically a rifle or shotgun
