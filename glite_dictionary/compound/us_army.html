U.S. Army

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuː ɛs ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs ˈɑːmi / (BrE)

the United States' land military force
american

Examples

After graduating from college, she decided to join the U.S. Army.
