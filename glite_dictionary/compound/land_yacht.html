land yacht

1. NOUN

/ lænd jɑt / (AmE), / lænd jɒt / (BrE)
A wheeled vehicle with a sail, designed to be driven by the wind across land, often used for sport or recreation.
wheeled sail-powered vehicle for land

Examples

We saw a land yacht racing across the dry lake bed, powered only by the wind.
After lessons on the beach, she confidently steered her land yacht along the shoreline.
Owning a land yacht is a great way to enjoy speed sports without water or snow.

Synset Examples

We spent the afternoon racing our land yacht.
They sailed the land yacht across the beautiful desert.
The land yacht glided smoothly over the dunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sand yacht - a similar wind-driven vehicle designed for use on sand
