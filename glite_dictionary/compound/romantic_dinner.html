romantic dinner

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈmæntɪk ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæntɪk ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
an intimate meal shared by a couple, intended to create or enhance a romantic atmosphere
intimate meal for couples

Examples

They celebrated their anniversary with a romantic dinner at a candlelit restaurant.
He surprised her with a romantic dinner at home, complete with flowers and music.
A romantic dinner can help couples reconnect after a busy week.

Synset Examples

We had a romantic dinner under the stars.
During their courtship, they often went on romantic dinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: date - an appointment to meet socially with a romantic interest
