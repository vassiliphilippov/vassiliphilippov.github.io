jenny ass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛni æs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛni as / (BrE)
A female donkey.
female donkey

Examples

The farmer introduced a new jenny ass to his herd for breeding purposes.
A jenny ass is usually more docile than a jackass.
They purchased a healthy young jenny ass at the local livestock market.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: jackass - a male donkey

Etymology

'Jenny' is a pet form of 'Jane' used for female animals; 'ass' from Latin 'asinus' (donkey).
