wind generator

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / wɪnd ˈdʒɛnəreɪtə / (BrE)
a machine that produces electrical power by converting the kinetic energy of wind into usable energy
machine producing electricity from wind

Examples

A modern wind generator can power hundreds of homes using only wind energy.
The company installed several wind generators along the coast to reduce their reliance on fossil fuels.
Regular maintenance ensures that each wind generator operates at peak efficiency throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wind turbine - a device that converts wind's kinetic energy into electrical power
