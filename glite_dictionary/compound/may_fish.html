may fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ fɪʃ / (BrE)
A black-barred fish found in bays and coastal marshes along the Atlantic and Gulf Coast of the United States.
a fish of Atlantic and Gulf Coast marshes

Examples

Local fishermen often catch may fish in the shallow waters near the marshes.
The distinctive black bars make the may fish easy to identify among other coastal species.
Researchers are studying the habitat of the may fish along the Gulf Coast.
