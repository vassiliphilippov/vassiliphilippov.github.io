mathematical product

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl ˈprɑdʌkt / (AmE), / ˌmæθəˈmætɪkəl ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
the result of multiplying two or more numbers or quantities together
result of multiplication in math

Examples

The mathematical product of 4 and 5 is 20.
To find the area of a rectangle, calculate the mathematical product of its length and width.
The mathematical product changes if any factor in the equation is altered.

Synset Examples

the product of 2 and 3 is 6

Related Terms

  • Synonym: product - the result of multiplying two numbers
