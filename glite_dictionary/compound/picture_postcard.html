picture postcard

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ ˈpoʊstkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˈpəʊstkɑːd / (BrE)
a postcard with a photograph or printed image on one side, typically depicting a place or scene
postcard with a picture on one side

Examples

She sent me a picture postcard from Paris showing the Eiffel Tower.
The store sells many colorful picture postcards of local landmarks.
He collected picture postcards from every country he visited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postcard - a card for sending a message by mail without an envelope

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ ˈpoʊstˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˈpəʊstkɑːd / (BrE)
A postcard featuring a photograph or attractive image, often used for sending messages; also used to describe something very visually attractive or picturesque.
postcard with a photo or picturesque view

Examples

He sent me a picture postcard from his vacation in Paris.
The snow-covered village looked like a picture postcard in the morning light.
Their garden is so beautiful—it could be on a picture postcard.

Synset Examples
