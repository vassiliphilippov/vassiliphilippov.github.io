dwarf dandelion

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf ˈdæn.dəˌlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈdæn.dɪˌlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a small yellow-flowered herb of central and southeastern United States, resembling a dandelion
a small yellow-flowered herb resembling a dandelion

Examples

The dwarf dandelion blooms in early spring along the forest edge.
Botanists often study the dwarf dandelion for its resemblance to common dandelions.
We spotted several dwarf dandelions growing on the hillside during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandelion - a common yellow-flowered plant of the genus Taraxacum
