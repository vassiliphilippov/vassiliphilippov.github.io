bag of chips

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡ əv tʃɪps / (AmE), / ˈbæɡ əv tʃɪps / (BrE)
a packet or sack containing potato chips or similar snack foods
container of potato chips or snacks

Examples

She opened a bag of chips to share with her friends at the party.
There was only one bag of chips left on the store shelf.
Watching movies is better with a big bag of chips and some soda.

Synset Examples

He grabbed a bag of chips from the pantry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packet of crisps - small bag containing potato chips (British English)
