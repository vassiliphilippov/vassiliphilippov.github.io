math teacher

1. NOUN

/ mæθ ˈtitʃər / (AmE), / mæθ ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a teacher who specializes in teaching mathematics
person who teaches mathematics

Examples

My favorite math teacher made algebra enjoyable for the whole class.
The math teacher assigned extra homework to help us prepare for the exam.
She decided to become a math teacher after discovering her passion for numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematics teacher - a teacher of mathematics
  • Synonym: science teacher - a teacher who specializes in science
  • Antonym: student - a person who learns from a teacher
