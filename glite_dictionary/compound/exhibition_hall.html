exhibition hall

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən hɔl / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room or building designed for hosting exhibitions, such as trade shows or art displays
large space for exhibitions or shows

Examples

The art fair was held in the main exhibition hall downtown.
Hundreds of people visited the exhibition hall to see the latest technology on display.
A new exhibition hall is being built to accommodate international trade shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallery - a room or building for showing works of art
