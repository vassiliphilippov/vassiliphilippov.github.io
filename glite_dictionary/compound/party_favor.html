party favor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrti ˌfeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌfeɪvə / (BrE)
A small gift or souvenir given to a guest at a party as a token of appreciation or as a memento.
small gift for party guests

Examples

Each child went home with a party favor from the birthday celebration.
At weddings, it is traditional to give guests a small party favor to thank them for coming.
The table was decorated with colorful party favors for everyone to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: souvenir - an object kept as a reminder of an event
  • Synonym: party gift - a gift given to guests at a party
