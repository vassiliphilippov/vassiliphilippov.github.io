tornado lantern

1. NOUN

/ tɔːrˈneɪdoʊ ˈlæntɚn / (AmE), / tɔːˈnɑːdəʊ ˈlæntən / (BrE)
an oil lamp with a glass chimney and a perforated metal lid to protect the flame from strong winds
wind-protected oil lamp with glass chimney

Examples

The old barn still had a tornado lantern hanging by the door.
During the storm, they lit a tornado lantern to keep the darkness away.
He polished the glass chimney of the tornado lantern until it shone brightly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hurricane lamp - oil lamp with glass chimney designed for windy conditions
