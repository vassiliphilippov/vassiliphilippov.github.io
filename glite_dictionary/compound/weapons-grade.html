weapons-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛpənz ˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛp.ənz ˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
Of a quality or purity sufficient for use in the manufacture of weapons, especially relating to nuclear, chemical, or biological materials.
suitable for making weapons

Examples

The facility was found to be producing weapons-grade uranium, raising international concerns.
Security measures are strict when handling weapons-grade materials in research labs.
Intelligence reports indicated the shipment contained weapons-grade nerve agents.

Synset Examples

weapons-grade plutonium
weapons-grade anthrax
