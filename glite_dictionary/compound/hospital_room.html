hospital room

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːspɪtl̩ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a hospital where patients receive care and treatment
patient care room in a hospital

Examples

The nurse checked on each patient in the hospital room every hour.
After surgery, he was transferred to a private hospital room for recovery.
Visitors are only allowed in the hospital room during specified hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ward - a large room in a hospital where a number of patients are accommodated
