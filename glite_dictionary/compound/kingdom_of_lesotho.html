Kingdom of Lesotho

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ləˈsuːtuː / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv lɛˈsuːtuː / (BrE)
a landlocked country in southern Africa, officially known as the Kingdom of Lesotho, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1966
country in southern Africa

Examples

The Kingdom of Lesotho is entirely surrounded by South Africa.
After gaining independence, the Kingdom of Lesotho established its own parliamentary system.
Tourists are drawn to the mountainous landscape of the Kingdom of Lesotho.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lesotho - the country of Lesotho, southern Africa

Etymology

From Sesotho 'Lesotho' (the land of the Sotho people).
