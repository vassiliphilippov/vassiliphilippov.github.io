telecom system

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌkɑm ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɒm ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system that enables telecommunications, allowing the transmission of information over long distances.
system for telecommunications

Examples

The government is investing heavily in its national telecom system to improve connectivity.
A robust telecom system is essential for modern businesses to communicate effectively with clients.
Upgrading the telecom system reduced dropped calls and improved data speeds for users.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telecommunications - long-distance transmission of information
  • Synonym: communication system - system for transfer of information

Etymology

Shortened from 'telecommunications system', from Greek 'tēle' (far) + Latin 'communicare' (to share).
