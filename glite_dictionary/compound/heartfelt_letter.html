heartfelt letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌfɛlt ˈlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtfɛlt ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
A sincere and emotional letter expressing genuine feelings or sentiments.
sincere and emotional letter

Examples

She found a heartfelt letter from her sister tucked under her pillow.
After the funeral, he received a heartfelt letter from each of his colleagues.
Writing a heartfelt letter helped him reconnect with his estranged father.

Synset Examples

She received a heartfelt epistle from her long-lost friend.
He wrote her a heartfelt love letter on Valentine's Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love letter - a letter expressing romantic feelings
  • Degree Lower: thank-you note - a letter expressing gratitude
