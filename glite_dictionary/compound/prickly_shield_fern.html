prickly shield fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪkli ʃild fɝn / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ʃiːld fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern with evergreen, leathery fronds that are covered in pale brown, chafflike scales.
evergreen North American fern with scaly fronds

Examples

The prickly shield fern thrives in shaded woodland areas across North America.
Gardeners often plant prickly shield fern for its year-round green fronds and textured appearance.
When hiking in the forest, you might notice the prickly shield fern by its leathery fronds covered with brown scales.
