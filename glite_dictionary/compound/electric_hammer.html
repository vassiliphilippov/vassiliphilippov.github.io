electric hammer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhæmɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈhamə / (BrE)
A hammer operated by an electric motor, used for driving nails or breaking surfaces.
power tool hammer with electric motor

Examples

The construction crew used an electric hammer to break through the concrete wall.
He replaced his old manual hammer with a new electric hammer for faster work.
Always wear safety goggles when operating an electric hammer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackhammer - a powerful percussive tool, often powered electrically or pneumatically, used to break up concrete or rock
