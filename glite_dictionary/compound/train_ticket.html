train ticket

1. NOUN

/ treɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / treɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that allows a person to travel on a train
ticket for travel on a train

Examples

She bought a train ticket to visit her family in the city.
You must present your train ticket before boarding the platform.
He lost his train ticket and had to purchase another one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ticket - a printed or digital slip showing payment for travel or entry
