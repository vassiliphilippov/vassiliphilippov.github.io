whipping girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪp.ɪŋ ɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈwɪp.ɪŋ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A woman who is punished or blamed for the faults or errors of others.
woman blamed for others' faults

Examples

She became the whipping girl at work, always blamed for mistakes she didn't make.
Far too often in history, a whipping girl has taken the fall for someone else's misdeeds.
Tired of being the whipping girl, she finally confronted her unfair boss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapegoat - a person blamed for the wrongdoing of others

Etymology

Modelled on 'whipping boy', by analogy; 'whipping boy' dates from the 17th century.
