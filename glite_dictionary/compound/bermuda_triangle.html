Bermuda Triangle

1. PROPN

/ bɚˈmjuː.də ˈtraɪ.æŋ.gəl / (AmE), / bəˈmjuː.də ˈtraɪ.æŋ.ɡl̩ / (BrE)
an area in the western Atlantic Ocean, roughly bounded by Miami, Bermuda, and Puerto Rico, reputedly the site of numerous unexplained disappearances of ships and aircraft
region in Atlantic reputed for mysterious disappearances

Examples

Many theories have been proposed to explain the mysterious events in the Bermuda Triangle.
Some people refuse to fly over the Bermuda Triangle because of its sinister reputation.
The Bermuda Triangle has inspired countless books, movies, and documentaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Devil's Triangle - another name for the Bermuda Triangle
