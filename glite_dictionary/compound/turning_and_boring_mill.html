turning and boring mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːnɪŋ ənd ˈbɔrɪŋ mɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ ənd ˈbɔːrɪŋ mɪl / (BrE)
a large machine tool with a vertical spindle and horizontal face plate, used for shaping and boring large workpieces by rotating them against a cutting tool.
machine tool for turning and boring large workpieces

Examples

The factory installed a new turning and boring mill to handle oversized metal components.
With the turning and boring mill, engineers can create precise cylindrical shapes from massive steel blocks.
Operators must be specially trained to use the turning and boring mill safely and efficiently.
