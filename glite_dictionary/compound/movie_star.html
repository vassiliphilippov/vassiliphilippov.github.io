movie star

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi stɑr / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi stɑː / (BrE)
A famous actor or actress who plays leading roles in films.
famous film actor or actress

Examples

She dreamed of becoming a movie star ever since she was a child.
The movie star arrived at the premiere surrounded by photographers.
Even though he is a major movie star, he remains humble and approachable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film star - a famous actor or actress in films
  • Synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment
