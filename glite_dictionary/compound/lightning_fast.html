lightning fast

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪtnɪŋ fæst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ fɑːst / (BrE)
Extremely fast; happening or moving with remarkable speed.
extremely fast; very rapid

Examples

Her reaction times are lightning fast when playing video games.
The chef made a lightning fast decision to save the dish.
With its lightning fast delivery, the new courier service has impressed many customers.

Synset Examples

His reflexes are lightning fast when playing sports.
The new processor runs lightning fast compared to the old one.
She responded to the email lightning fast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapid - happening or moving very quickly
  • Antonym: slow - not fast; moving or happening with little speed

Etymology

Formed from the noun 'lightning' + adjective 'fast', referring to the perceived speed of lightning in nature.
