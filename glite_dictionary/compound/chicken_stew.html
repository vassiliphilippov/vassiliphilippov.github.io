chicken stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn stu / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn stjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of chicken pieces cooked slowly with vegetables and seasonings in liquid.
stew made with chicken

Examples

She served a delicious chicken stew with carrots and potatoes for dinner.
During the winter, nothing warms me up like a bowl of chicken stew.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade chicken stew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stew - dish cooked by simmering ingredients in liquid
