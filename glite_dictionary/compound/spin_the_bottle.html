spin the bottle

1. NOUN

/ spɪn ðə ˈbɑːtəl / (AmE), / spɪn ðə ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
a party game in which players sit in a circle, take turns spinning a bottle, and the person to whom the bottle points when it stops must be kissed by the spinner or kiss the spinner
party kissing game with a spun bottle

Examples

At the party, the group decided to play spin the bottle to break the ice.
She had her first kiss during a game of spin the bottle at summer camp.
Whenever they were bored, the teenagers often suggested spin the bottle as an activity.
