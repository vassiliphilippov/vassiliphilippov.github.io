bear cub

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛr ˌkʌb / (AmE), / ˈbeə ˌkʌb / (BrE)
A young bear, especially one that is still dependent on its mother.
a young bear

Examples

The zoo recently welcomed a newborn bear cub that quickly became a visitor favorite.
A mother bear will fiercely protect her bear cub from any threat in the wild.
Photographers waited for hours to catch a glimpse of the playful bear cub in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cub - a young animal, especially a carnivorous mammal such as a bear, lion, or wolf
