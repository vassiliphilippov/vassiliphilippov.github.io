book binding

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the protective covering on the front, back, and spine of a book, typically made of materials such as leather, cloth, or paper
protective cover of a book

Examples

The antique novel's book binding was made of intricate gold-embossed leather.
The library paid to restore several rare volumes with damaged book binding.
She admired the old book binding for its craftsmanship and durability.

Synset Examples

the book had a leather binding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binding - the protective cover of a book
