bitter floom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪtər fluːm / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə fluːm / (BrE)
Any of several pink-flowered marsh plants of the eastern United States, resembling a true centaury.
pink-flowered US marsh plant

Examples

The wetlands were covered with bright bitter floom during spring.
Botanists often study the bitter floom for its similarities to true centaury.
She found a patch of bitter flooms growing by the marsh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a plant related in appearance to bitter floom
