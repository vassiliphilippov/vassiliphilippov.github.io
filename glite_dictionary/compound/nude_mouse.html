nude mouse

1. NOUN

/ nud maʊs / (AmE), / njuːd maʊs / (BrE)
a laboratory mouse with a genetic mutation causing hairlessness and a deficient immune system, commonly used in medical and scientific research
hairless laboratory mouse with immune deficiency

Examples

Researchers often use the nude mouse model to study cancer therapies in a controlled environment.
A nude mouse lacks a functional thymus, making it valuable for transplantation experiments.
The pharmaceutical company tested the new drug's efficacy on a group of nude mice.
