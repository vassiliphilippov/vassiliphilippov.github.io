beef loin

1. NOUN

/ bif lɔɪn / (AmE), / biːf lɔɪn / (BrE)
A cut of meat taken from the loin section of a cow, prized for its tenderness and flavor.
a cut of meat from a cow's loin

Examples

The chef prepared steaks using a perfectly aged beef loin.
You can roast a whole beef loin for a special occasion dinner.
Selecting a high-quality beef loin ensures your meal will be tender and flavorful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sirloin - a cut of beef from the upper part of the loin
  • Synonym: tenderloin - the most tender cut of beef from the loin
