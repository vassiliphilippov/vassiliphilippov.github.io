finishing coat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ koʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ kəʊt / (BrE)
the last layer of paint, plaster, varnish, or similar material applied to a surface to complete the finish
final layer applied to a surface

Examples

The walls look smooth after the finishing coat was applied yesterday.
It's important to let the finishing coat dry completely before moving the furniture back.
After several base layers, the painter carefully spread the finishing coat for a perfect result.

Synset Examples

we can't paint until they put on the finishing coat

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undercoat - a preliminary layer of paint or plaster applied before the finishing coat
