bird food

1. NOUN

/ bɝd fuːd / (AmE), / bɜːd fuːd / (BrE)
a mixture of seeds or other food specially prepared or given to birds
food mixture for birds

Examples

She filled the feeder with bird food every morning to attract sparrows and finches.
Buying quality bird food can help keep wild birds healthy during the winter.
The pet store has a wide selection of bird food suitable for different species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birdseed - seeds used as food for birds
