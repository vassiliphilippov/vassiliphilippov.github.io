putty knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌti ˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈpʌti ˌnaɪf / (BrE)
A flat-bladed tool used for mixing or applying putty or similar substances.
tool for applying or mixing putty

Examples

Use a putty knife to smooth the putty into the cracks before it dries.
He scraped the old paint off the window frame with a putty knife.
A putty knife is essential for any home repair toolkit.
