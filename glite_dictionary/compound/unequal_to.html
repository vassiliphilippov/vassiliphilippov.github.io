unequal to

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈikwəl tu / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːkwəl tʊ / (BrE)
not capable of dealing with or meeting the requirements of something; not able to handle a particular task or situation.
not able to meet requirements; not up to a task

Examples

He found himself unequal to the physical challenges of the expedition.
Despite her best efforts, Sarah was unequal to the task of managing the large team.
The committee feared they were unequal to the enormous responsibility entrusted to them.

Synset Examples

unequal to the demands put upon him

Related Terms

  • Antonym: equal to - able to cope with or meet requirements
