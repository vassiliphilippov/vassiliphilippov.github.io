Holy War Warriors

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊli wɔr ˈwɔriərz / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli wɔː ˈwɒriəz / (BrE)
A militant Islamic group in Indonesia subscribing to the Wahhabi creed, known for waging violent campaigns against Christians.
Indonesian Islamist militant group

Examples

The Indonesian government banned the Holy War Warriors for their involvement in violent attacks.
International reports identified the Holy War Warriors as an extremist group responsible for several bombings.
Many communities were displaced due to conflicts incited by the Holy War Warriors in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Laskar Jihad - Indonesian Islamist militant group, also known as Holy War Warriors
