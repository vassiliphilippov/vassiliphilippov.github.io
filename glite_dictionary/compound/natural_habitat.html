natural habitat

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl ˈhæbɪtæt / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈhabɪtat / (BrE)
the environment in which a particular species normally lives and grows
species' typical living environment

Examples

Many conservation efforts focus on protecting animals in their natural habitat.
Deforestation threatens the natural habitat of countless species around the world.
Photographers often wait for hours to capture animals in their natural habitat.

Synset Examples

Seeing a rare animal in its natural habitat is a thrilling experience.
Seeing a wild animal in its natural habitat was breathtaking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: habitat - the type of environment where an organism or group normally lives
