for example

1. ADV

/ fər ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / fə ˈɪɡ.zɑːm.pəl / (BrE)
used to introduce an example in order to illustrate a point or explain something more clearly.
introducing an example

Examples

There are many ways to improve your health; for example, you could start exercising regularly.
Certain fruits, for example, bananas and oranges, are high in potassium.
Many countries, for example, Canada and Sweden, have universal healthcare systems.

Synset Examples

take ribbon snakes, for example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for instance - used to introduce an example, similar meaning
