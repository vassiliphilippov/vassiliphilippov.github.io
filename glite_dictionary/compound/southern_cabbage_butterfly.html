southern cabbage butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈkæbɪdʒ ˈbʌtɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈkæbɪdʒ ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
a common white butterfly of North America, closely related to the cabbage butterfly, whose larvae feed on cabbage and similar plants
a North American cabbage butterfly

Examples

The southern cabbage butterfly is often seen fluttering around vegetable gardens in the spring.
Farmers take measures to protect their crops from the larvae of the southern cabbage butterfly.
I spotted a southern cabbage butterfly resting on a cabbage leaf during my morning walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabbage butterfly - a common white butterfly whose larvae are pests of cabbage and related crops
