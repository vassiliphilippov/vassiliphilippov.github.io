ticket taker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪk.ɪt ˌteɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.ɪt ˌteɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to check and collect tickets at the entrance to an event or venue
person who checks and collects tickets

Examples

The ticket taker checked each pass as people entered the theater.
When we arrived at the concert, the ticket taker greeted us with a smile.
At the stadium, the ticket taker asked to see my ticket before allowing me in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usher - person who shows people to their seats at events
