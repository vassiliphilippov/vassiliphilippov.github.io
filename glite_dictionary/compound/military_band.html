military band

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri bænd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri bænd / (BrE)
a group of musicians, typically associated with the armed forces, who play brass, woodwind, and percussion instruments
armed forces musical group

Examples

The military band performed a stirring march during the parade.
A military band often leads ceremonial events at state functions.
She joined the military band as a clarinet player after basic training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass band - a band mainly using brass instruments and percussion
