chrysanthemum dog

1. NOUN

/ krɪˈsænθəməm dɔɡ / (AmE), / krɪˈsanθəməm dɒɡ / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized terriers from Tibet, known for their resemblance to Old English sheepdogs and their fluffy, curled tails
Tibetan terrier breed with fluffy tail

Examples

She adopted a chrysanthemum dog from a rescue shelter last winter.
The chrysanthemum dog's distinctive fluffy tail makes it easy to recognize at dog shows.
Many people are charmed by the playful temperament of a chrysanthemum dog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibetan terrier - a dog breed from Tibet with long, flowing hair

Etymology

Named for the resemblance of the dog's facial hair to the petals of a chrysanthemum flower.
