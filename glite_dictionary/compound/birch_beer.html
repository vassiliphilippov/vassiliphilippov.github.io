birch beer

1. NOUN

/ bɝːtʃ bɪr / (AmE), / bɜːtʃ bɪə / (BrE)
A sweet, carbonated soft drink flavored with extract from the bark of birch trees.
carbonated drink flavored with birch bark

Examples

I tried birch beer for the first time at the county fair, and it had a unique, spicy flavor.
The restaurant served birch beer instead of root beer to give customers a taste of regional tradition.
He brought a case of birch beer to the picnic, and everyone enjoyed the refreshing drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: root beer - a sweet, carbonated drink flavored with herbal extracts
  • Synonym: sarsaparilla - a carbonated soft drink with sarsaparilla root flavoring
