sweet-scented geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˌswiːtˈsɛntɪd dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌswiːtˈsɛntɪd dʒəˈreɪnɪəm / (BrE)
a southern African plant of the geranium family, having fragrant lobed leaves and pink flowers, often cultivated for its scent
fragrant geranium plant from southern Africa

Examples

The sweet-scented geranium filled the room with a refreshing fragrance.
Gardeners often grow sweet-scented geraniums to add aroma to their gardens.
She gently rubbed the leaves of the sweet-scented geranium to release its pleasant smell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - any plant of the genus Geranium, a group of flowering plants
