cowhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or barn used for housing cows
a barn for cows

Examples

The old farm still has a stone cowhouse behind the main field.
Every morning, the farmer feeds the cattle in the cowhouse.
A fresh layer of straw was spread across the floor of the cowhouse for the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: byre - a cowshed or barn for cattle
  • Synonym: stable - a building where animals, especially horses or cattle, are kept

Etymology

From 'cow' + 'house', meaning a house or shelter for cows.
