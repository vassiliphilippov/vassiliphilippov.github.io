Kentucky wonder

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtʌki ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / kənˈtʌki ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
a variety of green bean plant with long, flat pods, commonly grown for its flavor and productivity
a flat-podded green bean variety

Examples

We planted Kentucky wonder beans in our garden this spring.
The Kentucky wonder is prized for its tender, flavorful pods.
He brought a basket of fresh Kentucky wonder beans from the farmer's market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - an edible podded variety of bean, often eaten as a vegetable
