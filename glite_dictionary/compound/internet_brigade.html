internet brigade

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.tɚ.nɛt brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.nɛt brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a group of people who act together online to promote a cause or viewpoint, often coordinating their actions as activists or supporters
coordinated group of online activists

Examples

After the announcement, an internet brigade flooded social media to show their support.
The celebrity relied on an internet brigade to boost the popularity of her latest campaign.
An internet brigade organized petitions and shared information to raise awareness about climate change.

Synset Examples

An internet brigade gathered online to debate the issue.
The internet brigade quickly mobilized to support the cause.
The internet brigade shared tips on how to stay safe online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troll army - organized group of people who disrupt or harass online
  • Synonym: cyber activists - people who use online platforms for activism
