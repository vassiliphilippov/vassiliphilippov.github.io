broccoloid plant

1. NOUN

/ brɑːˈkəloʊɪd plænt / (AmE), / brəˈkələʊɪd plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant belonging to the group of vegetables related to broccoli, such as cauliflower, cabbage, or Brussels sprouts.
