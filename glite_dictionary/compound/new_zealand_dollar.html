New Zealand dollar

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈziː.lənd ˈdɑ.lɚ / (AmE), / njuː ˈziː.lənd ˈdɒl.ə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of New Zealand
currency of New Zealand

Examples

The price of the imported car was listed as fifty thousand New Zealand dollars.
After traveling to Auckland, she exchanged her US dollars for New Zealand dollars.
The New Zealand dollar strengthened against the euro this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - the basic monetary unit of several countries, including the US and New Zealand
