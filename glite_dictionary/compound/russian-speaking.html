Russian-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌʃən ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to speak or communicate in the Russian language.
able to speak Russian

Examples

She joined a Russian-speaking community to improve her language skills.
Many Russian-speaking immigrants settled in the city during the 1990s.
The company is looking for a Russian-speaking translator to assist with international business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English-speaking - able to communicate in English
  • Degree Lower: Russian - the Russian language
