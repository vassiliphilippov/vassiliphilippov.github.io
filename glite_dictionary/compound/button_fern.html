button fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtən fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən fɜːn / (BrE)
A small fern native to New Zealand and Australia, characterized by trailing fronds with dark green, buttonlike leaflets.
fern with buttonlike leaflets

Examples

We placed a button fern in the window to brighten up the kitchen.
The button fern is prized for its ornamental, rounded leaves and cascading growth.
Gardeners recommend the button fern for terrariums because of its compact size and lush appearance.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt.ən fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ən fɜːn / (BrE)
A Jamaican fern with small, round, buttonlike leaflets, often grown as a houseplant.
Jamaican fern with buttonlike leaflets

Examples

The button fern is popular among houseplant enthusiasts for its attractive round fronds.
She placed the small button fern on her windowsill to brighten up the kitchen.
Because of its unique appearance, the button fern stands out in tropical gardens.
