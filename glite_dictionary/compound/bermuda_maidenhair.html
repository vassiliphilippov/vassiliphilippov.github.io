Bermuda maidenhair

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdə ˈmeɪdənˌhɛr / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə ˈmeɪdənheə / (BrE)
A delicate fern native to Bermuda, characterized by a creeping rootstock.
a delicate Bermudian fern

Examples

The Bermuda maidenhair thrives in shady, moist areas around the island.
Botanists were excited to discover a new patch of Bermuda maidenhair during their survey.
Some gardens in Bermuda feature Bermuda maidenhair to showcase native plants.
