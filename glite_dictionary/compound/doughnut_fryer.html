doughnut fryer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊnət ˈfraɪər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊnʌt ˈfraɪə / (BrE)
a machine used to fry large quantities of doughnuts at once, typically in bakeries or commercial kitchens
machine for frying doughnuts

Examples

The bakery invested in a new doughnut fryer to keep up with customer demand.
Cleaning the doughnut fryer is an important part of the daily maintenance routine.
Using a commercial doughnut fryer allows shops to produce dozens of doughnuts at once.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - an appliance used for deep frying food
