popty ping

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɒpti pɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒpti pɪŋ / (BrE)
An informal, humorous British term of Welsh origin meaning 'microwave oven.'
humorous term for a microwave oven
informal british

Examples

I love how the popty ping makes reheating so easy.
The popty ping can cook meals in just minutes.
We bought a popty ping for quick breakfasts.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microwave oven - an electric oven that heats food by microwave energy

Etymology

From Welsh 'popty' (oven) and the onomatopoeic 'ping' representing the sound a microwave makes when finished.
