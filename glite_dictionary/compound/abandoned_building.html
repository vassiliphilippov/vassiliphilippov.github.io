abandoned building

1. NOUN

/ əˈbændənd ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈbændənd ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
A building that is no longer used or occupied.
a building no longer used or occupied

Examples

Several artists have transformed an abandoned building into a vibrant community center.
Authorities sealed off the abandoned building after reports of unsafe conditions.

Synset Examples
