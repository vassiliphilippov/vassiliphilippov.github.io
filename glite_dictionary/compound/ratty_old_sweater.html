ratty old sweater

1. NOUN

/ ˈræti oʊld ˈswɛtər / (AmE), / ˈræti əʊld ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
A sweater that is worn-out and shabby in appearance, often due to age or heavy use.
worn-out or shabby sweater

Examples

He refused to throw away his ratty old sweater because it was so comfortable.
She lounged around the house all weekend in her ratty old sweater and jeans.
The ratty old sweater had holes in the elbows but kept him warm on chilly nights.

Synset Examples

He wore a ratty old sweater to the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shabby - in poor condition through long use or lack of care
  • Synonym: tattered - old and torn; in poor condition
