to watch the dawn break

1. VERB

/ wɑtʃ ðə dɔn breɪk / (AmE), / wɒtʃ ðə dɔːn breɪk / (BrE)
to look at and observe the moment when day begins and the sun starts to rise
observe sunrise

Examples

Every summer, we hike up the hill to watch the dawn break over the valley.
He set his alarm early so he could watch the dawn break from the beach.
They sat in silence to watch the dawn break, enjoying the peaceful start to their day.

Synset Examples

We woke up early to watch the dawn break.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunrise - the time when the sun appears above the horizon in the morning
  • Synonym: dawn - the beginning of the day when light first appears
