dwarf cape gooseberry

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf keɪp ˈɡusˌbɛri / (AmE), / dwɔːf keɪp ˈɡuːsbəri / (BrE)
a stout, hairy annual plant of eastern North America, producing sweet yellow fruits
hairy annual plant with sweet yellow fruits

Examples

The dwarf cape gooseberry thrived in my garden and produced a bountiful harvest of sweet yellow fruits.
Many gardeners appreciate the dwarf cape gooseberry for its ornamental leaves and edible fruit.
The jam made from dwarf cape gooseberry is both tangy and sweet, perfect for spreading on toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cape gooseberry - a plant with edible yellow-orange fruit, native to South America
