intercommunication system

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.tɚ.kəˌmjuː.nəˈkeɪ.ʃən ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a system that allows communication between different rooms or areas within a building, ship, or similar structure
internal building communication system

Examples

The hospital installed a new intercommunication system to improve staff coordination across wards.
Please use the intercommunication system if you need to contact reception from your room.
Modern ships are often equipped with an advanced intercommunication system for crew safety and convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercom - a system for communication within a building
