bladderwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈblædɚˌwɔrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈblæd.ə.wɔːt ˈfæm.əl.i / (BrE)
a family of carnivorous aquatic or bog plants, including genera such as Utricularia, Pinguicula, and Genlisea
family of carnivorous aquatic or bog plants

Examples

The bladderwort family includes several species known for their unique carnivorous habits.
Botanists study the bladderwort family to understand the evolution of plant carnivory.
