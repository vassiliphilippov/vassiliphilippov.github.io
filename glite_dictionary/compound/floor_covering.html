floor covering

1. NOUN

/ flɔr ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / flɔː ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a material or layer that is applied to cover a floor for decorative or protective purposes
material used to cover a floor

Examples

We need to choose a floor covering that is both durable and stylish for the living room.
The old floor covering was torn and stained, so they decided to replace it.
Tile is a popular floor covering in kitchens and bathrooms due to its water resistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet - thick woven fabric used as a floor covering
  • Synonym: rug - a small piece of thick material used as a floor covering
  • Synonym: linoleum - a type of smooth floor covering
