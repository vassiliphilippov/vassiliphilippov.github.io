bruh moment

1. NOUN

/ brʌ ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / brʌ ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A humorous or awkward situation that causes realization or surprise, often prompting a reaction of disbelief or amusement.
awkward, surprising, or funny moment
slang

Examples

It was a real bruh moment when I walked into the wrong classroom.
Everyone had a bruh moment when the power went out during the presentation.
Missing my bus by seconds was such a bruh moment for me.

Synset Examples

She had a bruh moment when she realized the exam was today.
When he dropped the cake, it was a bruh moment.
That fail was definitely a bruh moment for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkward moment - a socially uncomfortable or embarrassing situation

Etymology

From 'bruh', a slang variant of 'bro' (brother), popularized on the internet, plus 'moment'.
