list-processing language

1. NOUN

/ lɪst ˌproʊˈsɛsɪŋ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / lɪst ˌprəʊˈsɛsɪŋ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a programming language designed to handle and manipulate data organized in lists, often used in artificial intelligence and symbolic computation
programming language for manipulating lists

Examples

LISP was one of the earliest list-processing languages developed for artificial intelligence research.
The flexibility of a list-processing language makes it ideal for symbolic computation.
Many AI applications are implemented using a list-processing language due to its powerful handling of lists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lisp - a prominent list-processing language used in AI research
