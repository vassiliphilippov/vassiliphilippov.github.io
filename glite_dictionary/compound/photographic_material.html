photographic material

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊ.t̬əˈɡræf.ɪk məˈtɪr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təˈɡræf.ɪk məˈtɪə.ri.əl / (BrE)
paper or other substances sensitive to light, used for producing photographs or images.
light-sensitive paper for photos

Examples

The darkroom is stocked with photographic material for developing prints.
She purchased high-quality photographic material to ensure the best image results.
Careful handling of photographic material is essential to prevent accidental exposure to light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photographic paper - paper coated with a light-sensitive material for photographic prints
