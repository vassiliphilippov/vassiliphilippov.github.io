hurricane deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɝːrɪˌkeɪn dɛk / (AmE), / ˈhʌrɪkən dɛk / (BrE)
the uppermost deck on a passenger ship, usually exposed to the weather
top deck on a passenger ship

Examples

Passengers gathered on the hurricane deck to watch the sunset.
The strong wind made walking on the hurricane deck a challenge.
Crew members secured the chairs on the hurricane deck before the storm arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promenade deck - a deck on a passenger ship used for walking
  • Degree Lower: main deck - the principal deck of a ship

Etymology

From 'hurricane' (a violent storm) and 'deck', referring to its exposure to wind and weather.
