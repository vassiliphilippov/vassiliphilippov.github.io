fit jean

1. NOUN

/ fɪt dʒin / (AmE), / fɪt dʒiːn / (BrE)
A pair of jeans designed to fit closely to the body.
close-fitting jeans

Examples

She bought a pair of fit jeans for the concert this weekend.
The new fit jeans highlight his athletic build.
Many brands now offer fit jeans in different washes and styles.

Synset Examples

The blue fit jeans accentuate her figure perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skinny jean - jeans that are very tight-fitting throughout the leg
  • Antonym: straight jean - jeans with a straight cut from hip to hem
