Death Trail

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ treɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ treɪl / (BrE)
a hiking path that is especially dangerous or notorious for causing injuries or fatalities
a notoriously dangerous hiking path

Examples

The hikers prepared thoroughly before attempting the Death Trail in the mountains.
Many avoid the Death Trail because of its steep cliffs and unpredictable weather.
Rescue teams are often called to the Death Trail due to frequent accidents.

Synset Examples

They hiked the notorious Death Trail, known for its dangers.
