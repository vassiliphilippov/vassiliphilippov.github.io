purchase price

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːtʃəs praɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtʃəs praɪs / (BrE)
the amount of money paid to buy something; the price at which something is bought
the price paid to buy something

Examples

The purchase price of the house was lower than we expected.
Always check the final purchase price before agreeing to buy anything online.
She negotiated to reduce the purchase price of the car by $2,000.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: selling price - the price at which something is offered or sold to a buyer
