animal trainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪml ˈtreɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈænɪml ˈtreɪnə / (BrE)
a person who trains or exhibits animals, especially for performances or work
person who trains animals

Examples

The animal trainer taught the lions to perform tricks for the audience.
She became an animal trainer at the circus after years of working with horses.
The animal trainer used positive reinforcement to teach the dogs new behaviors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trainer - a person who trains people or animals
