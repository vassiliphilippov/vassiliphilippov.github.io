Dutch elm fungus

1. NOUN

/ dʌtʃ ɛlm ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ɛlm ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes Dutch elm disease, a serious disease affecting elm trees
fungus causing Dutch elm disease

Examples

The spread of Dutch elm fungus devastated many city parks in the 20th century.
Scientists are researching ways to prevent Dutch elm fungus from infecting healthy trees.
Because of Dutch elm fungus, entire streets of elms were cut down to stop the disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dutch elm disease - a serious disease of elm trees caused by a fungus
