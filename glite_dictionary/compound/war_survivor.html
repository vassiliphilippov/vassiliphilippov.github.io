war survivor

1. NOUN

/ wɔr səˈvaɪ.vər / (AmE), / wɔː səˈvaɪ.və / (BrE)
a person who has lived through and survived a war
person who survived a war

Examples

The charity provides counseling services for war survivors adjusting to civilian life.
As a war survivor, she shares her experiences with younger generations to promote understanding.
Many war survivors struggle with trauma long after the conflict has ended.

Synset Examples

As a war survivor, he advocates for peace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: survivor - a person who survives, especially after an ordeal
