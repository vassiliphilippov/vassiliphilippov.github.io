recreational medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl ˈmɛdɪsən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkrɪˈeɪʃ(ə)nəl ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the health, prevention, and treatment of injuries related to leisure or non-medical activities
medical field focused on leisure or non-medical pursuits
medical

Examples

After retiring, she developed an interest in recreational medicine, helping athletes recover from sports injuries.
The clinic specializes in recreational medicine, offering treatments for hiking-related problems and outdoor accidents.
Some doctors argue that recreational medicine can encourage healthier lifestyles through preventive care.

Synset Examples

He studies recreational medicine to promote wellness.
Recreational medicine is gaining popularity among young adults.
