fed up with

1. ADJ

/ fɛd ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / fɛd ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
annoyed or bored with something that has continued for too long
annoyed or bored by something persistent

Examples

I'm fed up with the amount of paperwork I have to do every week.
After hours of waiting, they were completely fed up with the delays.
Many workers are fed up with the lack of communication from management.

Synset Examples

I'm fed up with all the delays in this project.
She's fed up with the constant noise from construction.
He's fed up with the same excuses every time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bored with - tired and uninterested in something
  • Synonym: sick of - informal, extremely tired or annoyed by something
  • Antonym: interested in - finding something engaging or appealing
