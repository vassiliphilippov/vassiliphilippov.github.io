love story

1. NOUN

/ lʌv ˈstɔri / (AmE), / lʌv ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
A story, novel, or film that centers on a romantic relationship between its main characters.
a story about romance

Examples

Their new film is a classic love story set in Paris.
She enjoys reading a love story before bed every night.
The author became famous after publishing her first love story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romance - a story about romantic relationships
