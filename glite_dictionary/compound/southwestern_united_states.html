Southwestern United States

1. PROPN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərn ˌjuːnaɪtɪd ˈsteɪts / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstən ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts / (BrE)
the southwestern geographical region of the United States, typically including New Mexico, Arizona, Texas, California, and sometimes Utah and Colorado
region in the southwestern U.S.

Examples

The deserts of the Southwestern United States are home to unique plants and animals.
Many people move to the Southwestern United States for its warm climate and natural beauty.
Ancient indigenous cultures left remarkable ruins throughout the Southwestern United States.
