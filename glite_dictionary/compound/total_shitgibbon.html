total shitgibbon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtəl ˈʃɪtˌɡɪb.ən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈʃɪtˌɡɪb.ən / (BrE)
A slang insult for someone behaving in a foolish or obnoxious manner.
foolish or obnoxious person (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

When he cut me off in traffic, I thought he was a total shitgibbon.
Stop being a total shitgibbon and listen for a minute.
They called the rude commentator a total shitgibbon on social media.

Synset Examples

He acts like a total shitgibbon when he's angry.

Etymology

Blend of vulgar 'shit' and 'gibbon' (a kind of ape); popularized in online culture as a humorous insult.
