pet sitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛt ˌsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpet ˌsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of looking after someone's pet in their home while the owner is away
looking after pets while owner is away

Examples

She offered pet sitting services to neighbors during the holidays.
Many people rely on pet sitting when they travel for work.
Good pet sitting requires attention to each animal's unique needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babysitting - the activity of caring for children while their parents are away

Etymology

Formed from 'pet' + 'sitting', modeled after 'babysitting'.
