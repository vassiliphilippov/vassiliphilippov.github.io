exhaust pipe

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɔst paɪp / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst paɪp / (BrE)
A pipe that carries exhaust gases from the engine of a vehicle to the rear, usually connecting the exhaust manifold to the muffler.
pipe for vehicle exhaust gases

Examples

The mechanic discovered a leak in the exhaust pipe during the inspection.
After years of use, the car's exhaust pipe had become rusty and needed replacement.
Hot gases escaping from the exhaust pipe can be dangerous if the pipe is damaged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tailpipe - pipe at or near the end of the exhaust system
  • Synonym: muffler - device that reduces noise in a vehicle's exhaust system
