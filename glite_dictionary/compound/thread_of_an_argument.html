thread of an argument

1. NOUN

/ θɹɛd əv ən ˈɑːɹɡjəmənt / (AmE), / θɹɛd əv ən ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
the main line or central idea running through a person's reasoning or discussion
main idea in an argument

Examples

I lost the thread of his argument halfway through his presentation.
The debate moderator asked her to clarify the thread of her argument.
It's important to maintain the thread of your argument throughout your essay.

Synset Examples

Keep the thread of your argument clear and concise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: line of reasoning - a sequence of logical steps in an argument
