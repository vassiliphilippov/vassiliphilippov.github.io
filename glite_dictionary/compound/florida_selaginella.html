Florida selaginella

1. NOUN

/ flɔːrɪdə səˌlædʒɪˈnɛlə / (AmE), / flɒrɪdə sɪˌlajɪˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A species of spikemoss (Selaginella) found widely in Florida.
species of spikemoss native to Florida

Examples

The Florida selaginella thrives in moist, shaded areas throughout the state.
During our hike, we spotted patches of Florida selaginella along the forest floor.
Botanists are studying the distribution of Florida selaginella in local ecosystems.

Etymology

From 'Florida' (the U.S. state) and 'Selaginella' (genus name from Latin, a diminutive of 'selago', a kind of plant).
