buckthorn berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌθɔrn ˈbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌθɔːn ˈbɛri / (BrE)
The fruit of various buckthorn plants, sometimes used to obtain dyes or pigments.
fruit of the buckthorn plant

Examples

The bright yellow dye was extracted from the buckthorn berry using a traditional method.
Some artists use pigments made from buckthorn berries in their work.
The buckthorn berry is also sometimes used in herbal remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buckthorn - a type of shrub or small tree with thorny branches
