planing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪnɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine powered by electricity or another source, used to smooth or shape wood by shaving thin layers from its surface
powered tool for shaping or smoothing wood

Examples

The carpenter used a planing machine to create a smooth finish on the oak beams.
Safety goggles are essential when operating a planing machine in the workshop.
Modern planing machines can handle large quantities of lumber efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: planer - a machine tool for smoothing wood surfaces

Etymology

From 'plane' (a tool for shaping wood) + 'machine'.
