strawlike texture

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔˌlaɪk ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌlaɪk ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
a texture resembling that of straw, with a fibrous or coarse appearance
texture resembling straw

Examples

The basket had a strawlike texture that felt rough under my fingers.
Some breads develop a strawlike texture when they are overbaked or stale.
The wallpaper's strawlike texture added a rustic feel to the room.

Synset Examples

The artist used strawlike textures in her sculpture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fibrous - composed of or resembling fibers
  • Antonym: smooth texture - texture with an even, regular surface
