art historian

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt hɪˈstɔriən / (AmE), / ɑːt hɪˈstɔːrɪən / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in the history and development of art
expert in history of art

Examples

She became an influential art historian specializing in Renaissance paintings.
The museum invited a renowned art historian to give a lecture on Impressionism.
As an art historian, he spends much of his time analyzing ancient sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
