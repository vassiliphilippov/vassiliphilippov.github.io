Eupatorium aya-pana

1. NOUN

/ juːˌpeɪtɔːriəm ˌaɪəˈpɑːnə / (AmE), / juːˌpeɪtəˈrɪəm ˌaɪəˈpɑːnə / (BrE)
A low, spreading tropical American shrub with long slender leaves, used to make a mildly stimulating drink similar to tea; sometimes classified in the genus Eupatorium.
tropical shrub used to make herbal tea

Examples

The leaves of Eupatorium aya-pana are often brewed to make a traditional herbal drink.
Botanists have debated the correct classification of Eupatorium aya-pana within the genus.
Some herbalists recommend Eupatorium aya-pana as a mild natural stimulant.

Etymology

From the genus name Eupatorium, combined with local name aya-pana used in South America.
