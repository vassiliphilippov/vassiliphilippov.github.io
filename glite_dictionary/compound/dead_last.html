dead last

1. ADV

/ dɛd læst / (AmE), / dɛd lɑːst / (BrE)
in the very last position in a race, competition, or ranking; absolutely last
in the very last position
informal

Examples

Despite practicing for months, he finished dead last in the marathon.
The company ranked dead last in the customer satisfaction survey this year.
Even if you come in dead last, you should be proud you completed the challenge.

Synset Examples

He was proud to finish dead last but still tried.
Coming in dead last didn't discourage him from competing.
She finished dead last in the race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last - final position in a sequence or competition
