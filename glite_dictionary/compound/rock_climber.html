rock climber

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈklaɪmɚ / (AmE), / rɒk ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in the sport or activity of climbing natural rock formations or artificial rock walls
person who climbs rocks for sport

Examples

She is an experienced rock climber who has scaled mountains all over the world.
The rock climber carefully chose his next foothold on the steep cliff face.
Many rock climbers travel to Yosemite National Park for its famous granite walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climber - a person who climbs, especially as a sport
