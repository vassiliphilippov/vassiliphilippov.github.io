nylon stocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪ.lɑn ˈstɑː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.lɒn ˈstɒ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
a close-fitting, sheer covering for the foot and leg, made of nylon, typically worn by women
sheer legwear made of nylon

Examples

She wore a pair of elegant nylon stockings with her evening dress.
A run in her nylon stocking forced her to change before leaving for work.
During the 1940s, nylon stockings were considered a luxury item.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pantyhose - close-fitting legwear that covers the body from waist to toes, made of similar material
  • Synonym: tights - close-fitting leg covering, usually thicker than stockings, extending from the waist to the feet

Etymology

Nylon: from the trademarked name invented by DuPont in the 1930s; stocking: from Old English 'stoccing'.
