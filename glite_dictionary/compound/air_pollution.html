air pollution

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr pəˌluʃən / (AmE), / ˈeə pəˌluːʃən / (BrE)
the presence of harmful substances in the air, often caused by human activity, that may harm living beings or the environment
presence of harmful substances in the air

Examples

The city is taking steps to reduce air pollution caused by factories and cars.
Children are especially vulnerable to the health effects of air pollution.
High levels of air pollution can trigger asthma attacks in sensitive individuals.

Synset Examples

air pollution reduced the visibility

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pollution - the presence or introduction of harmful substances into the environment
  • Synonym: contamination - the action of making something impure by polluting or poisoning
