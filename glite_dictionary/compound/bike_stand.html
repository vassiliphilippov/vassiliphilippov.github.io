bike stand

1. NOUN

/ baɪk stænd / (AmE), / baɪk stænd / (BrE)
a rack or frame designed for parking and securing bicycles
rack for parking bicycles

Examples

You should lock your bicycle to the bike stand near the station entrance.
The new park was equipped with a bike stand for visitors.
All students are encouraged to use the bike stand to keep the campus tidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicycle rack - a rack used for holding parked bicycles
