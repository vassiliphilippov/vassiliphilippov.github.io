pipe dream

1. NOUN

/ paɪp driːm / (AmE), / paɪp driːm / (BrE)
an unrealistic or impractical hope or plan that is unlikely to be realized
unrealistic or impractical hope

Examples

He knew that owning a mansion by the sea was only a pipe dream for now.
Starting their own company seemed like a pipe dream until they finally made it happen.
For many, traveling to space remains a distant pipe dream.

Synset Examples

I have this pipe dream about being emperor of the universe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy - imaginative idea, not based in reality
  • Synonym: delusion - a false belief or impression
  • Antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist

Etymology

Refers to fantasies induced by smoking opium pipes in the late 19th century.
