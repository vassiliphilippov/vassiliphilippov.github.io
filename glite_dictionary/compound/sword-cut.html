sword-cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔrdˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːdˌkʌt / (BrE)
A scar left by a wound made with a sword.
scar from a sword wound

Examples

The old soldier bore a prominent sword-cut across his cheek.
Historians noted the sword-cut on the statue's face as a sign of battle.
He wore his sword-cut with pride, considering it a badge of honor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scar - a permanent mark left on the skin after a wound heals
