order Alismales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər æˈlɪsmeɪlz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌælɪsˈmeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of aquatic monocotyledonous herbaceous plants, including water plantain and related families
botanical order of aquatic herbs

Examples

The order Alismales includes a variety of freshwater aquatic plants.
Many species in the order Alismales are found in marshes and wetlands.
The taxonomy of the order Alismales has been revised with new molecular evidence.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Alismales', based on the genus 'Alisma' (water plantain).
