luminance unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːmɪnəns ˌjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈluːmɪnəns ˌjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit used for measuring luminance, such as the candela per square metre
unit of measurement for luminance

Examples

The brightness of the screen is often expressed in luminance units.
For accurate color calibration, you'll need to measure output using a standard luminance unit.
The technician recorded the increase in luminance units after replacing the lamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candela per square metre - the SI unit of luminance
