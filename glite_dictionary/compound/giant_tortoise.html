giant tortoise

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɔr.təs / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɔː.təs / (BrE)
a very large tortoise native to the Galapagos and Seychelles islands, known for its great size and longevity
a very large tortoise species

Examples

The giant tortoise can live for more than 100 years in the wild.
Scientists traveled to the Galapagos Islands to study the giant tortoise population.
A giant tortoise moves slowly across the sandy terrain, searching for food.
