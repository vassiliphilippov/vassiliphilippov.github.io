herbal tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɝːbəl ti / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl tiː / (BrE)
a hot drink made by infusing leaves, flowers, or other parts of herbs in boiling water, not containing true tea leaves
drink made from infused herbs, not true tea

Examples

She offered her guests a soothing cup of herbal tea after dinner.
Many people drink herbal tea to help them relax before bed.
Chamomile is a popular ingredient in herbal tea blends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infusion - a drink made by soaking herbs in liquid to extract flavors
