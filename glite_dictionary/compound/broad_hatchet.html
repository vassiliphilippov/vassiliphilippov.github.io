broad hatchet

1. NOUN

/ brɔd ˈhætʃɪt / (AmE), / brɔːd ˈhætʃɪt / (BrE)
a short-handled hatchet with a wide blade on one side and a hammerhead on the other, used especially for hewing
a hatchet with a wide blade and hammerhead

Examples

The carpenter skillfully used a broad hatchet to shape the wooden beam.
He purchased a new broad hatchet with a sturdy handle for his woodworking projects.
A broad hatchet is especially useful for splitting logs and rough shaping timber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatchet - a small axe with a short handle for use with one hand
