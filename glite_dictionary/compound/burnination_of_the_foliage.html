burnination of the foliage

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɝː.nɪˈneɪ.ʃən əv ðə ˈfoʊ.li.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌbɜː.nɪˈneɪ.ʃən əv ðə ˈfəʊ.li.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
(humorous) The act or process of burning or setting fire to vegetation, particularly with great enthusiasm or thoroughness.
humorous burning of vegetation
informal slang

Examples

The burnination of the foliage left the landscape bare.
After the fictitious dragon's rampage, the villagers mourned the burnination of the foliage.
The meme referenced the burnination of the foliage as a running joke in the community.

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from the playful alteration 'burninate' (originating as a fictional exaggeration of 'burn' in internet culture) and 'foliage'.
