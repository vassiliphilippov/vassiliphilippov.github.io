kidney begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdni bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni bɪˈɡəʊnɪə / (BrE)
A species of begonia with rounded, fleshy leaves that are reddish underneath, known for its kidney-shaped foliage and rhizomatous growth.
begonia with round, reddish-underleaf foliage

Examples

The kidney begonia is a beautiful houseplant prized for its unusual leaf shape.
She placed her kidney begonia on the windowsill to catch the morning sun.
Gardeners admire the reddish undersides of the kidney begonia's leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants with fleshy leaves
