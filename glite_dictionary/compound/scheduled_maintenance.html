scheduled maintenance

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɛdʒuːld meɪnˈteɪnəns / (AmE), / ˈʃedjuːld meɪnˈteɪnəns / (BrE)
maintenance that is carried out at a prearranged or regular time, rather than in response to a problem or breakdown
planned or regular maintenance activity

Examples

Our factory shuts down every quarter for scheduled maintenance to keep the machines running smoothly.
The airline canceled several flights because the airplanes required scheduled maintenance.
Following a strict scheduled maintenance routine helps prevent unexpected equipment failures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preventive maintenance - maintenance intended to prevent failures
  • Antonym: corrective maintenance - maintenance performed after a failure
