television news

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən nuːz / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən njuːz / (BrE)
news programs broadcast on television, typically providing reports and updates on current events.
news broadcast on television

Examples

Many people watch television news every evening to stay informed about world events.
She appeared on television news to discuss the recent developments in politics.
The television news covered the story in detail, providing live updates throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news bulletin - a short news broadcast
  • Antonym: radio news - news broadcast on the radio
