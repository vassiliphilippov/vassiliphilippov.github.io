earthmoving equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrθˌmuːvɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌmuːvɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
heavy machines used for digging, excavating, and moving soil or earth, especially in construction or mining
machines for digging and moving earth

Examples

The company purchased new earthmoving equipment to prepare the site for construction.
Modern earthmoving equipment can handle massive amounts of soil with great efficiency.
Safety standards must be followed when operating earthmoving equipment on any project.

Synset Examples

The construction project required extensive earthmoving equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excavator - a large machine for digging and moving earth
  • Synonym: bulldozer - a powerful machine for pushing earth and rocks
