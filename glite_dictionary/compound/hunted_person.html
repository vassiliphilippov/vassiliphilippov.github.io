hunted person

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is being pursued or chased, typically with the intention to capture or harm them
someone being chased or pursued

Examples

The hunted person managed to stay hidden in the forest for days.
In the story, the hunted person is always looking over their shoulder.
The authorities sympathized with the hunted person, who was wrongly accused of a crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fugitive - a person who has escaped from captivity or is in hiding
  • Antonym: pursuer - a person who chases or follows someone
