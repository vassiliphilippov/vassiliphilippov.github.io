soft roll

1. NOUN

/ sɔft roʊl / (AmE), / sɒft rəʊl / (BrE)
A small, round bread roll with a soft crust and interior, made with yeast.
bread roll with a soft crust

Examples

She ordered a soft roll with her soup at the café.
A fresh soft roll is perfect for making sandwiches.
The bakery specializes in soft rolls filled with various ingredients.
