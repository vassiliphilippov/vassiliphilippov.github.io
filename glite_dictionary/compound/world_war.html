world war

1. NOUN

/ wɝld wɔr / (AmE), / wɜːld wɔː / (BrE)
A war involving most of the world's major nations.
large-scale global war involving major nations

Examples

The devastation caused by the world war affected millions of people worldwide.
Historians continue to study the causes of each world war to understand global conflict.
There has not been another world war since 1945, but the fear of one still shapes international relations.
