internet site

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.t̬ɚˌnɛt saɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.nɛt saɪt / (BrE)
A location on the internet, typically consisting of a collection of related web pages, accessible via a single address.
location of related web pages on the internet

Examples

She launched a new internet site to showcase her photography portfolio.
Many consumers compare prices on an internet site before buying in stores.
The company's official internet site was redesigned to improve user experience.

Synset Examples

the Israeli web site was damaged by hostile hackers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: website - a collection of web pages under a single domain name
