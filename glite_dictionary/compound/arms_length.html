arm's length

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ɑːmz lɛŋ(k)θ / (BrE)
A distance that is far enough to prevent closeness, especially to avoid intimacy or undue influence.
a distance to avoid closeness

Examples

He kept everyone at an arm's length to maintain his privacy.
The company maintains an arm's length relationship with its suppliers.
She prefers to stay at arm's length from office politics.
