elegant brodiaea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlɪɡənt broʊˈdiːə / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪɡənt brəʊˈdiːə / (BrE)
a wildflower of western North America with an umbel of violet or blue-violet flowers on a leafless stalk, found from northern Oregon to southern California
a violet-flowered wildflower of western North America

Examples

The hillside was covered with blooming elegant brodiaea after the spring rains.
Botanists often seek out elegant brodiaea when surveying native plants in California.
She photographed a cluster of elegant brodiaea on her hike through the valley.

Etymology

From Brodiaea, named after Scottish botanist James Brodie, plus 'elegant' meaning graceful in form.
