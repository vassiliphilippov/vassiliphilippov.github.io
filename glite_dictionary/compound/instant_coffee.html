instant coffee

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstənt ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / ˈɪnstənt ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a soluble powder or granules made from dehydrated coffee, prepared by adding hot water
powdered coffee that dissolves in hot water

Examples

She always has a jar of instant coffee in her kitchen for busy mornings.
Many campers prefer instant coffee because it is light and easy to prepare.
The taste of instant coffee has improved a lot in recent years.

Synset Examples

the advantages of instant coffee are speed of preparation and long shelf life

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ground coffee - coffee beans that have been roasted and ground, not instant
