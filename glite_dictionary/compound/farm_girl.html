farm girl

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm ɡɜrl / (AmE), / fɑːm ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl who has grown up on a farm, often characterized by rural upbringing and familiarity with farming life
girl raised on a farm

Examples

She grew up as a farm girl and knew how to milk cows by the age of ten.
The story follows a farm girl who dreams of moving to the city.
Despite her fame, she still considers herself a farm girl at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farm boy - a boy who has grown up on a farm
