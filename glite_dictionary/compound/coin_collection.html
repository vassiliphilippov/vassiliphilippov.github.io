coin collection

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪn kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / kɔɪn kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the activity or hobby of collecting coins, often for study or investment
the hobby of collecting coins

Examples

Her coin collection includes rare coins from ancient Rome.
He started a coin collection as a child and still enjoys it today.
The museum features a fascinating coin collection from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numismatics - the study or collection of currency, including coins, tokens, and paper money

2. NOUN

/ kɔɪn kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / kɔɪn kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
A group of coins gathered and kept, often as a hobby or for investment.
a group of collected coins

Examples

He proudly displayed his coin collection to visitors in the living room.
My grandfather has a valuable coin collection dating back to the 1800s.
Starting a coin collection can be both educational and enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp collection - a collection of postage stamps
  • Synonym: numismatics - the study or collection of currency, including coins
