stopper knot

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑpɚ nɑt / (AmE), / ˈstɒpə nɒt / (BrE)
a knot tied at the end of a rope to prevent it from slipping through a hole or opening
knot that prevents rope from passing through a hole

Examples

She tied a stopper knot to keep the rope from slipping through the pulley.
Before finishing the project, make sure you use a stopper knot at the end of the cord.
A simple stopper knot can prevent accidents when sailing.
