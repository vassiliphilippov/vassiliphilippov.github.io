nursing school

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːsɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a school or college where students are trained to become nurses
school training students to become nurses

Examples

She was accepted into the nursing school at her local university.
After graduating from nursing school, he began working at the city hospital.
Many applicants compete for places at the nation's top nursing schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical school - a school where students are trained to become doctors
  • Synonym: law school - a school where students study law
