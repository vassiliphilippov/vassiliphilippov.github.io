order Platyctenea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌplætɪkˈtiːniː / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌplætɪkˈtiːniː / (BrE)
A taxonomic order within the class Tentaculata, comprising certain types of comb jellies distinguished by their flattened body form.
order of comb jellies (Tentaculata)

Examples

The order Platyctenea includes comb jellies with uniquely flattened bodies.
Recent research has expanded our understanding of the diversity within the order Platyctenea.
Marine biologists observed several new species belonging to the order Platyctenea during the expedition.

Etymology

From Greek 'platys' (flat) and 'ktene' (comb), referring to the flattened, comb-bearing form.
