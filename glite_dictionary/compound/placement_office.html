placement office

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪsmənt ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsmənt ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
an office or department that helps people find suitable jobs or employment opportunities, especially in educational institutions or organizations.
office helping find employment

Examples

The university's placement office helped hundreds of students secure internships and full-time positions after graduation.
After losing his job, Mark visited the placement office to explore new employment opportunities.
The placement office offers workshops on resume writing and interview skills for job seekers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employment agency - an agency that finds jobs for people seeking employment
  • Synonym: unemployment office - a government office helping unemployed people find work
