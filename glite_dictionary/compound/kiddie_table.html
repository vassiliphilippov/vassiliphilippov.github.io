kiddie table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdi ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪdi ˌteɪbl / (BrE)
a small table set aside for children to eat at during social gatherings or family meals
table for children at gatherings

Examples

We set up a kiddie table in the corner for the little ones.
At Thanksgiving, all the cousins gathered around the kiddie table.
She graduated from the kiddie table to join the adults for dinner.

Synset Examples

At family gatherings, the kids sit at the kiddie table.
She felt too old for the kiddie table this year.

Etymology

Formed from "kiddie" (child, diminutive of kid) + "table".
