quiet word

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪət wɝːd / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət wɜːd / (BrE)
a brief, private, and discreet conversation, often about a sensitive matter or to give advice
private or discreet conversation
british

Examples

After the meeting, she asked to have a quiet word with her supervisor.
He pulled me aside for a quiet word about my recent performance at work.
Could I have a quiet word before the others arrive?

Synset Examples

Let’s have a quiet word about this issue.
Can I have a quiet word with you in private?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: word - an act of speaking or a conversation
