veal parmigiana

1. NOUN

/ vil pɑːrməˈʒɑːnə / (AmE), / viːl pɑːˌmɪˈdʒɑːnə / (BrE)
A dish consisting of sautéed veal cutlet coated with breadcrumbs and cheese, topped with tomato sauce.
veal cutlet with breadcrumbs, cheese, and tomato sauce

Examples

For dinner, I ordered the veal parmigiana and a side of pasta.
The restaurant is famous for its classic veal parmigiana recipe.
He topped the veal parmigiana with extra mozzarella before serving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken parmigiana - breaded chicken cutlet with cheese and tomato sauce
  • Synonym: eggplant parmigiana - breaded eggplant dish with cheese and tomato sauce

Etymology

From Italian 'parmigiana', referring to the style of preparation using Parmesan cheese.
