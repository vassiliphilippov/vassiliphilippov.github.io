leopard cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpərd kæt / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəd kat / (BrE)
a small wildcat of southern Asia and Malaysia, characterized by a spotted coat resembling that of a leopard
small spotted wildcat of Asia

Examples

The leopard cat is common in the forests of Southeast Asia.
Some people mistakenly believe the leopard cat can be tamed as a pet.
Researchers are studying how habitat loss affects the leopard cat population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildcat - a small, undomesticated feline species
