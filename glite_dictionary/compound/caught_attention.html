to caught attention

1. VERB

/ kɔt əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / kɔːt əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
attracted interest or notice; caused people to look or pay attention
attracted notice or interest

Examples

The sudden movement in the corner caught attention during the meeting.
Her colorful dress instantly caught attention when she arrived at the event.
The headline was designed to catch attention and encourage readers to click.

Synset Examples
