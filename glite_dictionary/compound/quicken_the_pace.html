to quicken the pace

1. VERB

/ ˈkwɪkən ðə peɪs / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkən ðə peɪs / (BrE)
to move or progress more quickly; to increase the speed or urgency of an activity or process
increase speed or urgency

Examples

As the deadline approached, the team decided to quicken the pace of their work.
We need to quicken the pace if we want to finish before sunset.
The runners began to quicken the pace as they neared the finish line.

Synset Examples

The news will quicken the pace of the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hasten - to cause something to happen sooner or speed up
  • Antonym: to slow down - to move at a slower speed; to reduce pace or urgency
