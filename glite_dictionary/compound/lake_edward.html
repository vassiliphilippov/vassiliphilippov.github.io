Lake Edward

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ˈɛdwərd / (AmE), / leɪk ˈɛdwəd / (BrE)
a large lake in the Great Rift Valley, located between the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Uganda in East Africa.
lake in the Great Rift Valley, Africa

Examples

Lake Edward is known for its diverse wildlife, including numerous fish and bird species.
The border between Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo runs through Lake Edward.
Many communities rely on fishing in Lake Edward for their livelihood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake Albert - a neighboring lake in the Great Rift Valley between Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo
