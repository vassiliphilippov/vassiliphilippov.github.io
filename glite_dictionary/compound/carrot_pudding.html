carrot pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæɹət ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkærət ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet pudding made with grated carrots, often combined with milk, sugar, and spices, and served as a dessert.
sweet dessert made with grated carrots

Examples

My grandmother always prepares carrot pudding for family gatherings during the holidays.
In India, carrot pudding is commonly known as 'gajar ka halwa' and is enjoyed at festivals.
The restaurant’s signature dessert was a warm carrot pudding topped with toasted almonds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrot cake - a cake made with grated carrots and usually spices and nuts
