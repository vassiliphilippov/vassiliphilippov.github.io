prize fight

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪz ˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌfaɪt / (BrE)
A boxing match held between professional boxers for a cash prize.
boxing match for a cash prize

Examples

The championship prize fight attracted thousands of spectators to the arena.
He trained for months in preparation for his first prize fight.
Many young boxers dream of winning a prize fight and making a name for themselves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxing match - a contest in which two people fight with their fists
