half hatchet

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈhætʃət / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈhætʃɪt / (BrE)
A type of hatchet that features a half axe head, typically used for carpentry or outdoor work.
hatchet with a half axe head

Examples

He used a half hatchet to split small logs for the campfire.
A half hatchet is especially handy for both chopping and hammering tasks.
The carpenter’s toolbox included a well-worn half hatchet among other essential tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatchet - a small axe with a short handle
  • Degree Higher: axe - a tool with a heavy bladed head mounted across a handle
