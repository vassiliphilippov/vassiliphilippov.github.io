clever m*therfucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvɚ ˈmʌðɚˌfʌkɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə ˈmʌðəˌfʌkə / (BrE)
A person who is exceptionally clever or cunning, often used with both admiration and a vulgar tone.
exceptionally clever or cunning person (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Only a clever m*therfucker could have pulled off a trick like that without anyone noticing.
