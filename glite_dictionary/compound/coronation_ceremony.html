coronation ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔrəˈneɪʃən ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˌkɒrəˈneɪʃən ˈsɛrɪˌməʊni / (BrE)

the ceremony of crowning a monarch
