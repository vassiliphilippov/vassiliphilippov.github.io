blue-eyed grass

1. NOUN

/ bluː aɪd ɡræs / (AmE), / bluː aɪd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A small plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate blue flowers, belonging to the iris family.
plant with grass-like leaves and blue flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with blue-eyed grass blooming in the summer sun.
She planted blue-eyed grass along the edge of her garden for a splash of color.
Although it looks like grass, blue-eyed grass is actually related to irises.
