phantasy life

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tə.si laɪf / (AmE), / ˈfæn.təsi laɪf / (BrE)
an imaginary life created and experienced in one's mind, often as an escape from reality
an imagined life in a fantasy world

Examples

As a child, she spent hours absorbed in her phantasy life, pretending to be a mighty explorer.
Some therapists believe that escaping into a phantasy life can help people cope with trauma.
His phantasy life was often more vivid and satisfying than his real experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inner world - a person's internal mental experience
  • Synonym: daydream - a pleasant and spontaneous imagined experience
  • Synonym: fantasy life - an imaginary life (standard spelling)

Etymology

'Phantasy' from Greek 'phantasia' (imagination); 'life' from Old English 'līf'.
