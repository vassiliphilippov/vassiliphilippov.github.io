globe artichoke

1. NOUN

/ ɡloʊb ˈɑːrtɪtʃoʊk / (AmE), / ɡləʊb ˈɑːtɪtʃəʊk / (BrE)
a Mediterranean plant with a large thistlelike flower head that has edible fleshy leaves and a heart, commonly eaten as a vegetable
edible thistlelike plant (vegetable)

Examples

She served steamed globe artichoke with a tangy dipping sauce.
The heart of a globe artichoke is considered a delicacy.
Growing a globe artichoke in your garden requires rich, well-drained soil.
