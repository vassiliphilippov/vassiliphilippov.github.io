Midway Atoll

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪdˌweɪ əˈtoʊl / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.weɪ əˈtɒl / (BrE)
an atoll in the northwestern Hawaiian Islands, notable as the site of a significant U.S. naval base and the Battle of Midway in World War II
an atoll in the northwestern Hawaiian Islands

Examples

The Battle of Midway Atoll marked a turning point in World War II.
Many rare seabirds nest on Midway Atoll, making it an important wildlife refuge.
We visited Midway Atoll as part of our Pacific research expedition.

Etymology

Named for its location roughly midway between North America and Asia.
