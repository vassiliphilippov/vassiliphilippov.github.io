thought-image

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔt ˌɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt ˌɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a mental image created in the mind by the imagination
mental image produced by imagination

Examples

The poem evokes a thought-image of endless blue skies and wide open fields.
She tried to hold the thought-image of her childhood home in her mind.
During meditation, a peaceful thought-image drifted across his consciousness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental image - an image that occurs in the mind
