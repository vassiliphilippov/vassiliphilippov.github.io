radiotherapy equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdioʊˈθɛrəpi ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˈθerəpi ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the machines and apparatus used to treat diseases, especially cancer, with x-rays or radioactive substances
medical equipment for cancer treatment with radiation
medical

Examples

The hospital recently installed new radiotherapy equipment to improve cancer treatment for its patients.
Routine maintenance is crucial to ensure the radiotherapy equipment functions accurately and safely.
Advances in radiotherapy equipment have made cancer treatments more precise and effective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linear accelerator - device that produces high-energy x-rays for cancer treatment
  • Synonym: radiotherapy - treatment of disease using radiation
