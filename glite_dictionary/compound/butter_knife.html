butter knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtər naɪf / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə naɪf / (BrE)
A small knife with a dull, rounded blade, used for cutting or spreading butter.
small knife for spreading butter

Examples

She used a butter knife to spread jam on her toast.
Please pass me the butter knife so I can cut the bread.
The butter knife is kept in the top kitchen drawer.
