nautical signal flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔtɪkəl ˈsɪɡnəl flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪkəl ˈsɪɡnəl flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag used as part of an international code to send signals between ships at sea
flag used for ship-to-ship communication

Examples

The crew hoisted a nautical signal flag to indicate distress.
Each nautical signal flag has a specific meaning based on its color and design.
During the exercise, sailors practiced identifying every nautical signal flag from a distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal flag - a flag used to send signals or messages, especially on ships
