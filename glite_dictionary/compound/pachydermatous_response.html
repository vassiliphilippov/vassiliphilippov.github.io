pachydermatous response

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæk.ɪˈdɜːr.mə.təs rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌpæk.ɪˈdɜː.mə.təs rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
an emotionally insensitive or thick-skinned reaction, showing little or no concern for criticism or feelings
insensitive or thick-skinned reaction

Examples

His pachydermatous response to the team's concerns offended many colleagues.
Despite harsh criticism, she maintained a pachydermatous response throughout the meeting.
A pachydermatous response is not always helpful when dealing with sensitive topics.

Synset Examples

The pachydermatous response to feedback surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thick-skinned - not easily hurt by criticism or insults

Etymology

From Greek 'pachydermos' (thick-skinned) + 'response'.
