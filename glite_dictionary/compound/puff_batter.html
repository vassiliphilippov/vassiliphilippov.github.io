puff batter

1. NOUN

/ pʌf ˈbætər / (AmE), / pʌf ˈbatə / (BrE)
A batter made to produce light, hollow pastry cases, typically used to hold sweet or savory fillings.
light batter for hollow pastry cases

Examples

The chef expertly piped the puff batter onto the baking tray to make profiteroles.
To make perfect cream puffs, start by preparing a smooth puff batter.
Fill your puff batter shells with custard or whipped cream for a delicious dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choux pastry - a light pastry dough used for cream puffs and éclairs
