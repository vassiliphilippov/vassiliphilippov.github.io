parts department

1. NOUN

/ pɑrts dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / pɑːts dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the section of a business, especially in the automotive industry, responsible for selling replacement parts to customers or internal repair staff
business division selling replacement parts

Examples

The parts department was able to quickly supply the mechanic with the necessary replacement filter.
If you need a new headlight, just ask someone in the parts department at the dealership.
She started working in the parts department before being promoted to service manager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service department - division of a business providing repair or maintenance services
