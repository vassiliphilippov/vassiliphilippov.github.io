zaftig figure

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑf.tɪɡ ˈfɪɡ.jɚ / (AmE), / ˈzæf.tɪɡ ˈfɪɡ.jə / (BrE)
A woman's body shape that is attractively full, curvaceous, and voluptuous.
curvaceous or voluptuous body shape

Examples

The actress became famous for her zaftig figure and confident style.
Designers are increasingly celebrating women with a zaftig figure in their campaigns.
Many viewed her zaftig figure as a refreshing alternative to the ultra-slim ideal.

Synset Examples

The model was celebrated for her zaftig figure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hourglass figure - body shape with a defined waist and full hips and bust
  • Antonym: slender figure - thin or slim body shape

Etymology

From Yiddish 'zaftik' (juicy, succulent), itself from German 'saftig' (juicy).
