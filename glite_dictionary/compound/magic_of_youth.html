magic of youth

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪk əv juθ / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk əv juːθ / (BrE)
the enchanting or captivating qualities associated with being young
enchanting qualities of being young

Examples

Many writers try to capture the magic of youth in their stories.
The magic of youth often fades as responsibilities increase.
She longed to recapture the magic of youth she felt in her teenage years.

Synset Examples

She felt the magic of youth roll back the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: youth - the period when someone is young
