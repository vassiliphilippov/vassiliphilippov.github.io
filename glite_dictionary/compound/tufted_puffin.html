tufted puffin

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌftɪd ˈpʌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd ˈpʌfɪn / (BrE)
a northern Pacific puffin with distinctive large yellow tufts over each eye during the breeding season
a northern Pacific puffin with yellow eye tufts

Examples

We spotted a tufted puffin nesting on the rocky cliffs of the island.
The bright yellow plumes of the tufted puffin are especially noticeable during breeding season.
A group of tufted puffins gathered on the shore, drawing the attention of birdwatchers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffin - a seabird with a colorful beak and black-and-white plumage
