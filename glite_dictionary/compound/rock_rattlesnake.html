rock rattlesnake

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / rɒk ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a venomous rattlesnake (Crotalus lepidus) native to rocky mountains and hills in Mexico and the southwestern United States
a rattlesnake species of southwestern US and Mexico

Examples

We spotted a rock rattlesnake resting between the sun-warmed boulders.
The rock rattlesnake camouflages itself effectively among the rocks.
Hikers are warned to watch out for the rock rattlesnake in this region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rattlesnake - a type of venomous snake known for its rattling tail
