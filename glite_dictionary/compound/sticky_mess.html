sticky mess

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki mɛs / (AmE), / ˈstɪki mɛs / (BrE)
A difficult or unpleasant situation that is hard to resolve.
difficult or unpleasant situation

Examples

Getting caught in a lie can quickly turn into a sticky mess.
The company found itself in a sticky mess after the scandal broke.
If we don't find a solution soon, we'll be in a real sticky mess.

Synset Examples

I need to clean this sticky mess off the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky situation - an awkward or problematic situation
  • Synonym: predicament - a difficult or unpleasant situation
