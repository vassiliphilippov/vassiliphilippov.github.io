farm boy

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm bɔɪ / (AmE), / fɑːm bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who has grown up or lives on a farm, often implying rural upbringing or experience
boy raised on a farm

Examples

Growing up as a farm boy, he learned the value of hard work at an early age.
The novel tells the story of a farm boy who dreams of exploring the world beyond his village.
Despite living in the city now, he still calls himself a farm boy at heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farm girl - a girl who has grown up on a farm
