Carlsbad Caverns National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑrlzbæd ˈkævɚnz ˌnæʃəˈnæl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːlz.bad ˈkæv.ənz ˌnæʃ.ən.əl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in New Mexico, USA, famous for its extensive limestone caverns and impressive underground formations
national park in New Mexico with vast limestone caves

Examples

We spent an entire day exploring Carlsbad Caverns National Park and marveled at its gigantic stalactites.
Carlsbad Caverns National Park attracts thousands of tourists each year interested in its unique geology.
