bridge partner

1. NOUN

/ brɪdʒ ˈpɑːrt.nɚ / (AmE), / brɪdʒ ˈpɑːt.nə / (BrE)
a person who is your teammate on the same side in the card game bridge
teammate in bridge card game

Examples

Sarah has played with the same bridge partner for over ten years.
Your bridge partner can help you develop better strategies during the game.
Jack was disappointed when his usual bridge partner couldn't make it to the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person who takes part with another, especially in a game or business
