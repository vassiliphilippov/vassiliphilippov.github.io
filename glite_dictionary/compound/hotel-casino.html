hotel-casino

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl kəˈsiːnoʊ / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl kəˈsiːnəʊ / (BrE)

establishment with hotel and casino

Examples

The new hotel-casino in town attracts tourists with its luxury suites and gaming floor.
Many Las Vegas hotel-casinos offer world-class entertainment and fine dining options.
After checking in, guests can easily access the casino area without leaving the hotel-casino.
