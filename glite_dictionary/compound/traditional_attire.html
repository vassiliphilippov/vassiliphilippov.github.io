traditional attire

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənl əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
the clothing that is characteristic of and traditionally worn by a particular culture, community, or group, especially during ceremonies or festivals
clothing typical of a specific culture or tradition

Examples

Many dancers wore traditional attire during the cultural performance.
He admired the colorful patterns of her traditional attire at the wedding.
Tourists are often fascinated by the traditional attire unique to each region.

Synset Examples

She wore traditional attire to celebrate Boraga.
Using traditional attire as a costume is wrong; my culture is not your costume.
The Laplander wore traditional attire during the festival.
They admired the khokhlushka’s traditional attire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: costume - a set of clothes typical of a particular period, person, place, or activity
  • Antonym: modern dress - contemporary clothing worn in everyday life
