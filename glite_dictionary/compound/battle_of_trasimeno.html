battle of Trasimeno

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætəl əv trəˈsɪməˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv trəˈsɪmɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a battle in central Italy in 217 BC where Hannibal defeated the Romans under Flaminius during the Second Punic War
defeat of Romans by Hannibal, 217 BC

Examples

The battle of Trasimeno was one of the worst defeats suffered by Rome during the Second Punic War.
Historians often study the tactics used by Hannibal at the battle of Trasimeno.
The lakeside location of the battle of Trasimeno provided a strategic advantage for Hannibal's forces.
