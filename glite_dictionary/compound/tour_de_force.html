tour de force

1. NOUN

/ tʊr də ˈfɔrs / (AmE), / tʊə də ˈfɔːs / (BrE)
an outstanding or exceptional achievement, especially one demonstrating great skill or brilliance
masterly or brilliant achievement

Examples

Her performance in the play was a real tour de force that left the audience speechless.
The novel is considered a literary tour de force due to its originality and depth.
Directing the film was a technical tour de force that impressed critics around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - a work of outstanding artistry, skill, or workmanship
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success in achieving something

Etymology

From French, literally ‘feat of strength’.
