guest of honor

1. NOUN

/ ɡɛst əv ˈɑnɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛst əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
the person for whom a party, ceremony, or other gathering is held as a mark of respect or celebration
person honored at a gathering

Examples

The mayor was the guest of honor at the charity banquet last night.
We prepared a special seat for the guest of honor in the center of the table.
As the guest of honor, she was invited to give a speech at the event.
