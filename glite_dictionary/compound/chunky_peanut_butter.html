chunky peanut butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌŋki ˈpinʌt ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋki ˈpiːnʌt ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
peanut butter containing small pieces of peanuts for a coarse texture.
peanut butter with pieces of nuts

Examples

He always spreads chunky peanut butter on his morning toast.
Some people like the texture that chunky peanut butter adds to sandwiches.
The recipe calls for a cup of chunky peanut butter for extra crunch.

Synset Examples

He prefers chunky peanut butter over smooth.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth peanut butter - peanut butter with a uniform creamy texture
