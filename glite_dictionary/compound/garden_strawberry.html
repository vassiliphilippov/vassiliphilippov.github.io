garden strawberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˈstrɔːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈstrɒˌbəri / (BrE)
a widely cultivated plant with sweet red fruit, commonly eaten fresh or used in preserves and desserts
a sweet, red, cultivated fruit

Examples

The garden strawberry is a favorite fruit during the summer months.
Farmers grow garden strawberries on a large scale for commercial sale.
She added fresh garden strawberries to her morning cereal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry - the fruit of any strawberry plant
