in gear

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ɡɪr / (AmE), / ɪn ɡɪə / (BrE)
with the gears of a vehicle or machine engaged, so that movement is possible when power is applied
with gears engaged (vehicle/machine)

Examples

Make sure the handbrake is on before leaving the car in gear.
The engine stalled because the truck was not in gear.
She accidentally started the mower while it was still in gear.

Synset Examples

the car is in gear

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in neutral - with the gears disengaged, so the vehicle or machine cannot transmit power
