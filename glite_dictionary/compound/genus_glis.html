genus Glis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ɡlɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ɡlɪs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Gliridae, which includes dormice such as the edible dormouse.
type genus of Gliridae (dormice)

Examples

The genus Glis includes the well-known edible dormouse, found across Europe.
He studied the habitat preferences of species within the genus Glis.
Researchers discovered new genetic variations in the genus Glis during their fieldwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gliridae - the biological family of dormice

Etymology

From Latin 'Glis', meaning dormouse.
