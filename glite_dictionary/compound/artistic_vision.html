artistic vision

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪk ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the ability to imagine or create new and original ideas in art; creative imagination or perception in artistic work
creative imagination in art

Examples

Her artistic vision transformed the old building into an inspiring community space.
The director's unique artistic vision set the film apart from others in the genre.
We admire the painter for her artistic vision and originality.

Synset Examples

Her artistic vision brought fantasticness to every project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vision - the ability to imagine or plan the future with creativity
