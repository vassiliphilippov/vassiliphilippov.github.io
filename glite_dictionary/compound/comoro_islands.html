Comoro Islands

1. PROPN

/ kəˈmɔroʊ ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / kəˈmɔːrəʊ ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of three main islands and several islets in the Indian Ocean located between Mozambique and Madagascar
island group in the Indian Ocean

Examples

The Comoro Islands are known for their beautiful beaches and diverse marine life.
She spent a year conducting research on agriculture in the Comoro Islands.
Flights to the Comoro Islands are limited due to their remote location.

Etymology

From French 'Comores,' derived from Arabic 'Qamar' (moon).
