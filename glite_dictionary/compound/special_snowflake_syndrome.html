special snowflake syndrome

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəl ˈsnoʊˌfleɪk ˈsɪndroʊm / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl ˈsnəʊˌfleɪk ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
the tendency to believe one is uniquely special or entitled, often with a self-centered attitude
belief in being uniquely special and entitled
informal slang

Examples

Some managers struggle with employees who display special snowflake syndrome and expect constant praise.
Online forums often criticize users accused of special snowflake syndrome for demanding special treatment.
Her special snowflake syndrome made teamwork difficult because she felt her ideas were always superior.

Synset Examples

She thinks she has special snowflake syndrome at work.
They warned him about his special snowflake syndrome.
His attitude showed a clear case of special snowflake syndrome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcissism - excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's appearance

Etymology

Based on the idea that every snowflake is unique, popularized in 21st century internet culture.
