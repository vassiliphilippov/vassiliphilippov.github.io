town meeting

1. NOUN

/ taʊn ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / taʊn ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting of the inhabitants of a town, especially to discuss or decide community issues
meeting of town residents for community matters

Examples

The annual town meeting will be held at the community center next Saturday.
Many important local decisions are made during the town meeting.
At the town meeting, residents raised concerns about taxes and public safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assembly - a gathering of people for a common purpose
  • Synonym: city council - a legislative body for a city

2. NOUN

/ taʊn ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / taʊn ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting at which the qualified voters of a town assemble to conduct official business and make decisions about local government
a meeting of town voters for local government

Examples

At the town meeting, residents voted to approve the new budget proposal.
Every spring, the community gathers for the annual town meeting to discuss important local issues.
The town meeting system encourages direct participation in government decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assembly - a group of people gathered for a common purpose
  • Antonym: city council - an elected body governing a city
