orange bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ bæt / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ bæt / (BrE)
a species of bat native to northwestern Australia, characterized by orange or yellow fur
a bat of northwestern Australia with orange or yellow fur

Examples

The orange bat is easily recognized by its bright yellow-orange fur.
Scientists studied the habitat of the orange bat in northwestern Australia.
Local guides often point out the orange bat when leading nighttime wildlife tours.
