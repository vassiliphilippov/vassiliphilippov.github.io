Chickasaw National Recreation Area

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃɪk.əˌsɑː ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl ˌrɛk.riˈeɪ.ʃən ˈɛəri.ə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.əsɔː ˌnæʃ.ən.əl ˌrɛk.riˈeɪ.ʃən ˈeəri.ə / (BrE)
A national park in southern Oklahoma known for its mineral springs, streams, and natural beauty.
national park in Oklahoma, USA

Examples

We spent our vacation hiking and enjoying the springs at the Chickasaw National Recreation Area.
The Chickasaw National Recreation Area attracts nature lovers from across the country.
Birdwatchers often visit the Chickasaw National Recreation Area for its diverse wildlife.

Etymology

Named after the Chickasaw Nation; 'Chickasaw' from the indigenous people historically inhabiting the region.
