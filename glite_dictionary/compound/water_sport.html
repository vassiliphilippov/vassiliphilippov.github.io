water sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ spɔrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə spɔːt / (BrE)
any sport that is played in, on, or over water, such as swimming, surfing, or sailing
sport played in or on water

Examples

Kayaking is my favorite water sport because I love exploring lakes and rivers.
During the summer, many tourists try new water sports like windsurfing and jet skiing.
The resort offers various water sport activities, including snorkeling and paddleboarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watersports - plural form; refers to multiple water-based sports
