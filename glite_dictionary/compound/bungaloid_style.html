bungaloid style

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌŋɡəˌlɔɪd staɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋɡələʊɪd staɪl / (BrE)
A style of architecture or design that closely resembles that of a bungalow.
style resembling a bungalow

Examples

The couple decorated their home in a bungaloid style with wide eaves and a large porch.
Many new developments feature houses built in the bungaloid style to attract buyers seeking classic charm.
Their architect suggested a bungaloid style to create a cozy and approachable atmosphere.

Synset Examples

The bungaloid style suits their laid-back lifestyle perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bungalow - a low house with a broad front porch, typically one storey
  • Degree Higher: modernist style - architectural style emphasizing form and function over ornament

Etymology

Formed from 'bungalow' and '-oid' (meaning 'resembling'), patterned after the word 'bungalow'.
