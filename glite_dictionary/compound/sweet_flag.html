sweet flag

1. NOUN

/ swit flæɡ / (AmE), / swiːt flaɡ / (BrE)
A perennial marsh plant (Acorus calamus) with sword-shaped leaves and aromatic roots, often found in wetlands.
perennial marsh plant with aromatic roots

Examples

The sweet flag grows abundantly along the riverside.
Herbalists value the roots of sweet flag for their strong aroma.
He planted sweet flag in the wettest corner of his garden.
