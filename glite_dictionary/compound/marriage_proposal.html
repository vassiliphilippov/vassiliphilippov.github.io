marriage proposal

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ prəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ prəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
an act of formally asking someone to marry you
an offer of marriage

Examples

He made a marriage proposal over dinner at her favorite restaurant.
The couple announced their engagement after a surprise marriage proposal in Paris.
She was nervous as she prepared for her own marriage proposal to her longtime partner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proposal - an act of putting forward or suggesting something for consideration
