Spanish Armada

1. PROPN

/ ˌspænɪʃ ɑːrˈmɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌspænɪʃ ɑːˈmɑːdə / (BrE)
the large fleet sent by Spain in 1588 to invade England under King Philip II, famously defeated by the English navy
Spanish fleet sent against England in 1588

Examples

The defeat of the Spanish Armada marked a turning point in European history.
Many books have been written about the dramatic clash between England and the Spanish Armada.
The Spanish Armada was scattered and destroyed by storms and English fire ships.
