lookout man

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊk.aʊt mæn / (AmE), / ˈlʊk.aʊt mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to keep watch for a potential event, especially to warn others about danger or detection
a man who keeps watch for danger or events

Examples

The lookout man signaled when the guards were approaching.
During the robbery, a lookout man stood near the door, watching for police.
He was hired as a lookout man to alert the crew to any suspicious activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lookout - a person who watches for danger or events
