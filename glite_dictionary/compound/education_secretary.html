Education Secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the government minister in charge of the Department of Education, especially in the US or UK
government minister for education

Examples

The new Education Secretary announced major reforms to the school curriculum today.
As Education Secretary, she was responsible for setting national education policy.
The appointment of a new Education Secretary is often closely watched by teachers' unions.

Synset Examples

Carter appointed Shirley Hufstedler as the first Secretary of Education

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secretary of Education - official title for the head of the Department of Education

2. NOUN

/ ˌɛʤəˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the head of a government's Education Department, responsible for national education policy and administration
government minister for education

Examples

The appointment of the new Education Secretary was announced in Parliament yesterday.
As Education Secretary, she introduced several important reforms to the school curriculum.
The Education Secretary met with teachers' unions to discuss the upcoming changes.

Synset Examples

the post of Education Secretary was created in 1979

Related Terms

  • Synonym: education minister - the government official in charge of the education department in some countries
