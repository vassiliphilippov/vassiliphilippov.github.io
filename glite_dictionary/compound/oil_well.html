oil well

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl wɛl / (AmE), / ɔɪl wɛl / (BrE)
A deep hole drilled into the earth from which oil is extracted.
deep hole for oil extraction

Examples

The company drilled a new oil well in the desert to increase its production.
After decades of use, the oil well finally ran dry.
Each oil well requires constant maintenance to ensure safety and productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil rig - a structure with equipment for drilling oil wells
