moonshiny night

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnˌʃaɪni naɪt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌʃaɪni naɪt / (BrE)
A night brightly illuminated by the light of the moon.
night lit by moonlight

Examples

The forest appeared mysterious on a moonshiny night.
We went for a walk under the stars on a moonshiny night.
On a moonshiny night, the lake sparkled as if covered in silver.

Synset Examples

The moonshiny night made the forest look magical.
