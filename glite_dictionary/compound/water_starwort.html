water starwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈstɑrwɝt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈstɑːwɜːt / (BrE)
Any of several aquatic plants in the genus Callitriche, typically with star-shaped rosettes of floating leaves, found in America, Europe, and Asia.
star-shaped aquatic plant genus

Examples

The pond was filled with water starwort thriving among other aquatic plants.
Many conservationists are working to protect habitats where water starwort naturally grows.
She identified the delicate leaves as belonging to a species of water starwort.

Etymology

From 'water' and 'starwort', referring to the plant's aquatic habitat and the star-shaped pattern of its leaves.
