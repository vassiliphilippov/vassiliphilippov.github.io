religion of peace

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒən əv pis / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒən əv piːs / (BrE)
a religion, belief system, or faith tradition that places emphasis on peace, non-violence, and harmony among people
a religion emphasizing peace and non-violence

Examples

Many people refer to Buddhism as a religion of peace because of its teachings on compassion.
The leader emphasized that their faith was fundamentally a religion of peace and tolerance.
Some argue that every true religion of peace encourages dialogue and understanding among different communities.

Synset Examples

The movement promotes the idea of a religion of peace.
She described her faith as a religion of peace.
