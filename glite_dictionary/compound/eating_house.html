eating house

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːtɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or establishment where people can buy and eat meals; a restaurant or café
place to buy and eat meals

Examples

The new eating house on Main Street became popular with local workers.
Travelers often stopped at an eating house to rest and have lunch.
The old city was famous for its traditional eating houses, offering simple but hearty meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
  • Synonym: cafeteria - a restaurant where you serve yourself and pay before eating
