comfy mumu

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmfi ˈmuːmuː / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfi ˈmuːmuː / (BrE)
a loose, comfortable dress, typically worn casually at home
loose, comfortable dress for home wear
informal

Examples

After a long day at work, she slipped into her comfy mumu to relax.
He bought his grandmother a floral comfy mumu for her birthday.
Lounging on the couch, she felt perfectly at ease in her favorite comfy mumu.

Synset Examples

She wore a comfy mumu around the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muumuu - a woman's loose, brightly colored dress of Hawaiian origin

Etymology

'Comfy' is informal for 'comfortable'; 'mumu' from Hawaiian muʻumuʻu, describing a loose dress.
