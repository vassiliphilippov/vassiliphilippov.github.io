fare increase

1. NOUN

/ fɛr ɪnˈkriːs / (AmE), / feə ɪnˈkriːs / (BrE)
an increase in the amount charged for travel on public transportation
a rise in public transport charges

Examples

The city announced a fare increase for all subway lines starting next month.
Commuters are unhappy about the latest fare increase on buses and trains.
Last year’s fare increase affected thousands of daily travelers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fare rise - an increase in the sum charged for public transport; synonym for fare increase
