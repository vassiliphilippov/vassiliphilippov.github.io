colouring material

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚɪŋ məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.ər.ɪŋ məˈtɪə.ri.əl / (BrE)
any substance used primarily to give color to a material or surface.
substance used to give color

Examples

The artist selected a natural colouring material to create her earthy palette.
Certain insects can produce a red colouring material used in food and cosmetics.
This paint contains a new synthetic colouring material that resists fading in sunlight.

Synset Examples

she used a different color for the trim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigment - a substance that gives color to other materials
  • Synonym: dye - a substance used to change the color of something
