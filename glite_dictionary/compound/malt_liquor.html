malt liquor

1. NOUN

/ mɔlt ˈlɪkər / (AmE), / mɔːlt ˈlɪkə / (BrE)
a type of lager with a high alcohol content, typically exceeding the legal limit for standard beer or lager
strong alcoholic lager-type beverage

Examples

He bought a bottle of malt liquor from the corner store.
Some states have strict regulations on the sale of malt liquor.
She prefers regular beer to malt liquor because of the lower alcohol content.
