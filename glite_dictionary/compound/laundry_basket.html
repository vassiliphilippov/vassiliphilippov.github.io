laundry basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔndri ˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndri ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container for holding clothes that need to be washed or have just been washed
container for dirty or wet clothes

Examples

She dropped her dirty clothes into the laundry basket after coming home from the gym.
The laundry basket was overflowing, so it was time to do the wash.
He carried the laundry basket down the stairs to the laundry room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamper - a large basket for carrying or holding dirty laundry
