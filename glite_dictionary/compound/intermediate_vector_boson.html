intermediate vector boson

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈmidɪət ˈvɛktər ˈboʊsɑn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmiːdɪət ˈvɛktə ˈbəʊsɒn / (BrE)
a gauge boson that mediates weak interactions between elementary particles
particle mediating weak interactions

Examples

The discovery of the intermediate vector boson was a major milestone in particle physics.
Physicists detected the intermediate vector boson through experiments at CERN.
The weak force is explained by the exchange of an intermediate vector boson between particles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gauge boson - a boson that mediates fundamental forces in particle physics
