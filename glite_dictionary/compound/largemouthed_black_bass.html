largemouthed black bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌmaʊθt blæk bæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌmaʊθt blæk bɑːs / (BrE)
a large freshwater bass with a blackish-green back and a jaw that extends behind the eye, commonly found in North America; also known as the largemouth bass
a North American freshwater fish; largemouth bass

Examples

The largemouthed black bass is a favorite target among sport anglers due to its fighting spirit.
He caught a huge largemouthed black bass at the lake yesterday.
Biologists studied the feeding habits of the largemouthed black bass in different environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: largemouth bass - a North American freshwater game fish with a large mouth
