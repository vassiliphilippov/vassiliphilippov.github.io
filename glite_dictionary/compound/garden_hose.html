garden hose

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˌhoʊz / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌhəʊz / (BrE)
a flexible tube used to convey water for watering a lawn or garden
flexible tube for watering gardens

Examples

She watered the flowers with a garden hose every morning.
The garden hose was tangled up behind the shed.
After mowing the lawn, he rinsed off his boots using the garden hose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hose - a flexible tube conveying fluid
