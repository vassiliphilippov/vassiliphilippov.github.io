dental school

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdɛnt(ə)l skuːl / (BrE)
a college or school where students study to qualify as dentists
college for training dentists

Examples

She was accepted into a prestigious dental school after completing her undergraduate studies.
Many aspiring dentists take out loans to pay for dental school.
Competition for places at the dental school is extremely high.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical school - a college where students study to become doctors
