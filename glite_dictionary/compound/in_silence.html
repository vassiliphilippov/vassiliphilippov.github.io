in silence

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
without speaking or making any noise; quietly and without words or sound
without speaking or making noise

Examples

They listened in silence as the judge delivered the verdict.
The students waited in silence until the teacher entered the classroom.
She accepted the news in silence, showing no visible reaction.

Synset Examples

We stood in silence during the introit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silently - without making any sound; in silence
