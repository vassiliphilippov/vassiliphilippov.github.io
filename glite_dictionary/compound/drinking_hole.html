drinking hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ həʊl / (BrE)
A casual bar or pub where people gather to drink alcohol, especially in an informal or relaxed setting.
informal bar or pub
