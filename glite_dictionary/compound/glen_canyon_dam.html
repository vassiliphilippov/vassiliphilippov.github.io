Glen Canyon Dam

1. PROPN

/ ɡlɛn ˈkænjən dæm / (AmE), / ɡlɛn ˈkanyən dam / (BrE)
a large dam on the Colorado River in northern Arizona, constructed in 1964, forming Lake Powell.
dam on the Colorado River in Arizona

Examples

The Glen Canyon Dam generates hydroelectric power for much of the Southwest.
On our road trip, we stopped to view the Glen Canyon Dam and the vast Lake Powell behind it.
Engineers monitor water levels at the Glen Canyon Dam to manage river flow downstream.
