unique experience

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / juːˈniːk ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an event or situation that is unlike any other; a one-of-a-kind occurrence
a one-of-a-kind event or situation

Examples

Traveling alone in the Amazon rainforest proved to be a unique experience she would never forget.
Attending the traditional tea ceremony was a unique experience during his visit to Japan.
Winning the scholarship offered him a unique experience to study abroad for a year.

Synset Examples

Eating wild haggis is a unique experience for adventurous eaters.
