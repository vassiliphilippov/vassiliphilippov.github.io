irregardlessly of

1. ADP

/ ˌɪrɪˈɡɑrdlɪsli əv / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈɡɑːdlɪsli əv / (BrE)
(nonstandard) Without regard or consideration for; regardless of.
nonstandard, means 'regardless of'
informal

Examples

He continued to defend his opinions irregardlessly of what others thought.
Irregardlessly of the risks, she decided to travel alone.
The team proceeded with the plan irregardlessly of budget cuts.

Synset Examples

He pursued his goals irregardlessly of the obstacles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regardless of - without paying attention to; notwithstanding

Etymology

Blend of 'irregardless' (itself a nonstandard form) and 'regardless of'.
