deltoid eminence

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛl.tɔɪd ˈɛm.ə.nəns / (AmE), / ˈdɛl.tɔɪd ˈɛm.ɪ.nəns / (BrE)
a raised area on the outer surface of the humerus where the deltoid muscle attaches
raised area on humerus for deltoid attachment
medical

Examples

The surgeon identified the deltoid eminence as the point of muscle attachment on the humerus.
A fracture near the deltoid eminence can impact shoulder movement significantly.
The anatomy textbook labeled the deltoid eminence clearly for student reference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deltoid tuberosity - a more common term for the same anatomical feature on the humerus

Etymology

From Greek 'deltoeidēs' (shaped like the letter delta) and Latin 'eminentia' (a projecting part).
