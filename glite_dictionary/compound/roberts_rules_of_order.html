Robert's Rules of Order

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑbɚts ˈruːlz əv ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbəts ˈruːlz əv ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a manual of parliamentary procedure outlining rules for conducting meetings and making group decisions
manual of parliamentary procedure

Examples

The committee used Robert's Rules of Order to ensure an orderly discussion.
If you are chairing a meeting, familiarity with Robert's Rules of Order is essential.
According to Robert's Rules of Order, you need a second to proceed with the motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parliamentary procedure - rules and methods for conducting meetings
  • Synonym: standing orders - permanent rules governing meetings, especially in organizations

Etymology

Named after Henry Martyn Robert, its original author.
