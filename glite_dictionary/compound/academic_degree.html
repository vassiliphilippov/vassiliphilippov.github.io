academic degree

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk dɪˈɡri / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
A qualification awarded by a college or university to show that someone has completed a particular course of study.
qualification from college or university

Examples

She received her academic degree after years of dedicated study.
Obtaining an academic degree can open up many career opportunities.
He holds an academic degree in engineering from a prestigious university.

Synset Examples

he earned his degree at Princeton summa cum laude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: degree - qualification awarded by a university or college
