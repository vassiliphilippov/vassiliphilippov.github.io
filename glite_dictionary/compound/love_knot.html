love knot

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌnɒt / (BrE)
A decorative knot, often with interlacing loops, used as a symbol or token of love and affection.
decorative knot symbolizing love

Examples

She wore a delicate pendant in the shape of a love knot around her neck.
Medieval poets often referenced the love knot as a symbol of eternal affection.
The invitation was tied with a love knot, representing the couple’s bond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lover's knot - another term for a decorative knot symbolizing love
