painted turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈtɝːtəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈtɜːtəl / (BrE)
a common North American freshwater turtle with bright yellow and red markings on its shell and skin.
North American freshwater turtle with bright markings

Examples

We spotted a painted turtle basking in the sun by the pond.
The painted turtle is well-known for its colorful shell patterns.
Children enjoyed watching the painted turtles slide off the log and into the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtle - a reptile with a hard shell that lives in water or on land
