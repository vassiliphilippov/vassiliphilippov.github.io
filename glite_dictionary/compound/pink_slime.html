pink slime

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk slaɪm / (AmE), / pɪŋk slaɪm / (BrE)
a processed meat byproduct, typically made from beef trimmings, used as a filler in ground beef and other meat products, often regarded as unappetizing or controversial
controversial meat byproduct used in food
