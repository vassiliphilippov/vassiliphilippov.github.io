family Littorinidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛmli lɪtəˈrɪnɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfamɪli lɪtəˈrɪnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small to medium-sized marine snails, commonly known as periwinkles, found in coastal environments.
family of marine snails (periwinkles)

Examples

The family Littorinidae includes many species commonly found on rocky shorelines around the world.
Scientists have extensively studied members of the family Littorinidae for their adaptations to intertidal environments.
Shells from the family Littorinidae are often collected for research in marine biology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periwinkle - a small marine snail from the family Littorinidae

Etymology

From New Latin 'Littorinidae', based on genus 'Littorina', from Latin 'littoralis' meaning 'of the seashore'.
