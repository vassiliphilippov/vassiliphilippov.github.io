Commonwealth of the Bahamas

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːmənˌwɛlθ əv ðə bəˈhɑːməz / (AmE), / ˌkɒmənˌwɛlθ əv ðə bəˈhɑːməz / (BrE)
An island country in the Atlantic Ocean, east of Florida and Cuba, known for its beaches and as a popular winter resort destination.
island nation east of Florida and Cuba

Examples

Many tourists visit the Commonwealth of the Bahamas each year to enjoy its white sandy beaches.
The Commonwealth of the Bahamas gained independence from Britain in 1973.
Nassau is the capital city of the Commonwealth of the Bahamas.

Etymology

From English 'commonwealth' and 'Bahamas', with 'Bahamas' possibly derived from the Spanish 'bajamar' (shallow sea).
