office staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːfɪs stæf / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs stɑːf / (BrE)
The employees who work in an office, especially those doing professional, administrative, or clerical duties.
employees working in an office

Examples

The office staff organized a surprise party for their manager's birthday.
All members of the office staff must attend the weekly meeting on Monday morning.
The company hired two new people to join the office staff last month.

Synset Examples

the whole office was late the morning of the blizzard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staff - all the people employed by a particular organization
