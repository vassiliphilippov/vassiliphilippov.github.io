to diagnose with gastro

1. VERB

/ daɪəɡˈnoʊz wɪð ˈɡæstroʊ / (AmE), / daɪəɡˈnəʊz wɪð ˈɡæstrəʊ / (BrE)
to identify someone as having gastroenteritis, typically after a medical evaluation
be identified as having gastroenteritis
informal medical

Examples

After several days of vomiting, she was finally diagnosed with gastro by her family doctor.
Many children are diagnosed with gastro during the peak of flu season each year.
If you are diagnosed with gastro, it is important to stay hydrated and rest.

Synset Examples

He was diagnosed with gastro after the cruise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to diagnose - to identify a disease or problem by examining someone

Etymology

'Gastro' as an abbreviation for 'gastroenteritis', from Greek 'gaster' (stomach) and 'enteron' (intestine).
