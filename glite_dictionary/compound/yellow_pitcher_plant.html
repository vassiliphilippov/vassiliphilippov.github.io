yellow pitcher plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈpɪtʃər plænt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a species of pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, characterized by erect, yellow, trumpet-shaped pitchers with wide mouths and upright lids
a North American pitcher plant species with yellow pitchers

Examples

The yellow pitcher plant thrives in boggy, acidic soils in the southeastern United States.
Botanists visited the wetland to observe several species, including the yellow pitcher plant.
The striking yellow pitcher plant attracts insects with its bright color and nectar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - a carnivorous plant with a deep cavity filled with digestive liquid
