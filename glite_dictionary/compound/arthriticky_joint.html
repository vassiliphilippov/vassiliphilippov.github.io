arthriticky joint

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈθrɪtɪki dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ɑːˈθrɪtɪki dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
A joint that is affected by or exhibits symptoms of arthritis, often characterized by pain, stiffness, or swelling.
joint affected by arthritis
informal

Examples

The cold weather made her arthriticky joint ache more than usual.
An arthriticky joint often becomes swollen and stiff after heavy use.
He applied a heating pad to his arthriticky joint for relief.

Synset Examples

She complained of arthriticky joints after gardening all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arthritic joint - joint affected by arthritis

Etymology

From 'arthritis' (inflammation of a joint) and the suffix '-y' (forming adjectives).
