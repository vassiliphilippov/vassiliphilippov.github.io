chicken salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
A salad made primarily with chopped or shredded chicken, often mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients such as celery or grapes.
salad containing chopped chicken meat

Examples

He ordered a chicken salad sandwich for lunch at the café.
My mother always adds grapes to her famous chicken salad.
The picnic basket included a bowl of homemade chicken salad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egg salad - salad made with chopped hard-boiled eggs
