worldly belongings

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldli bəˈlɔŋɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli bɪˈlɒŋɪŋz / (BrE)
all the material possessions that a person owns; someone's entire physical property and assets
all material possessions owned by a person

Examples

After the fire, he realized all his worldly belongings were gone.
She packed her worldly belongings into a single suitcase and left town.
When moving to a new country, you cannot take all your worldly belongings with you.

Synset Examples

he left all his worldly possessions to his daughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessions - things that someone owns
  • Synonym: belongings - personal property; things you own
