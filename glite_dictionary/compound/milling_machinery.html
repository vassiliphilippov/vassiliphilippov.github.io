milling machinery

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪŋ məˈʃinəri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪŋ məˈʃɪənəri / (BrE)
machines used for grinding, crushing, or processing materials into smaller parts or powders.
machines for grinding or crushing materials

Examples

The factory invested in new milling machinery to increase production efficiency.
Modern milling machinery can process large quantities of grain in a short time.
Routine maintenance of milling machinery is crucial to ensure safe operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mill - a machine for grinding grain, pepper, etc.
