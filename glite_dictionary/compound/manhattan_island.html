Manhattan Island

1. PROPN

/ mænˈhæt.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / mænˈhæt.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
an island in southeastern New York City, forming the main part of the borough of Manhattan
main island of Manhattan, New York City

Examples

The skyscrapers of Manhattan Island are known around the world.
Many tourists visit Manhattan Island to see landmarks like Central Park and Times Square.
He grew up on Manhattan Island and knows every street and neighborhood.

Etymology

Named after the Lenape language term, possibly meaning 'island of many hills.'
