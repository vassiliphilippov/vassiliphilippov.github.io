Joe Citizen

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒoʊ ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / ˌdʒəʊ ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
an average or typical member of the public; an ordinary citizen
typical or average citizen

Examples

The new tax law is supposed to help Joe Citizen, not big corporations.
Many newspapers claim to speak for the interests of Joe Citizen.
When new regulations are proposed, it's important to consider how they affect Joe Citizen.

Synset Examples

Joe Citizen is often unaware of the political changes.
Policies should benefit Joe Citizen, not just the elite.
Joe Citizen wants transparency from the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Q. Public - a generic name for an average or typical citizen
  • Synonym: average Joe - an ordinary or typical man in society

Etymology

Based on the use of 'Joe' as a generic name for an ordinary man, combined with 'citizen.'
