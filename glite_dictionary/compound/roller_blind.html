roller blind

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlər blaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə blaɪnd / (BrE)
a window covering made of stiff fabric that is wound around a roller and can be raised or lowered by pulling a cord or other mechanism
window covering that rolls up

Examples

We decided to install a roller blind in the kitchen for extra privacy.
The roller blind jammed halfway when I tried to pull it down this morning.
She chose a blackout roller blind for her bedroom window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window shade - a piece of cloth or other material that covers a window to block sunlight
  • Synonym: curtain - a piece of material hung at a window to block or obscure light
