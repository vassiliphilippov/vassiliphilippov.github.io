microwave diathermy machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪkroʊˌweɪv daɪˈθɜrmi məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌweɪv daɪˈθɜːmi məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a device that uses microwave radiation to produce deep heating in body tissues for therapeutic purposes
device for therapeutic deep heating using microwaves
medical

Examples

The clinic recently purchased a new microwave diathermy machine for physiotherapy treatments.
A microwave diathermy machine can help reduce pain and muscle stiffness when used appropriately.
Proper training is essential before operating a microwave diathermy machine to ensure patient safety.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: shortwave diathermy machine - device for therapeutic heating using shortwave radiation

Etymology

From 'microwave' (short electromagnetic waves) + 'diathermy' (from Greek 'dia' meaning 'through' and 'therme' meaning 'heat').
