civil rights worker

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪvəl ˈraɪts ˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˌsɪvəl ˈraɪts ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person actively involved in advocating and working to secure equal rights and opportunities for members of minority groups, especially in the context of political or social movements
activist for equality and minority rights

Examples

As a civil rights worker, she participated in voter registration drives in the 1960s.
Many civil rights workers faced danger and harassment for standing up against racial segregation.
The museum exhibit highlights the sacrifices made by civil rights workers during the struggle for equality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social or political change
