fan palm

1. NOUN

/ fæn pɑm / (AmE), / fæn pɑːm / (BrE)
A palm tree with leaves that are shaped like a fan, divided into segments radiating from a single point.
palm with fan-shaped leaves

Examples

The fan palm thrives in warm, tropical regions across the world.
Several species of fan palm are cultivated as ornamental plants in gardens and parks.
The broad leaves of the fan palm provide excellent shade on sunny days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palm - a type of tree with long leaves and no branches
