fritter batter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɪtər ˈbætər / (AmE), / ˈfrɪtə ˈbætə / (BrE)
A mixture of flour, liquid, and sometimes eggs, used to coat or bind ingredients when making fritters.
batter used for making fritters

Examples

She prepared the fritter batter before dipping in slices of apple for dessert.
The secret to crisp vegetables is a light fritter batter seasoned with herbs.
Once the fritter batter was ready, he coated the fish pieces and fried them until golden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batter - a mixture of flour, liquid, and other ingredients for cooking
