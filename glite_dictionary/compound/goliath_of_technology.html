Goliath of technology

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈlaɪəθ əv tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɡəˈlaɪəθ əv tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A company or entity that is extremely large or dominant in the technology industry.
dominant tech industry player

Examples

Apple and Google are considered the Goliath of technology in today's digital world.
As a startup, it's hard to compete with a Goliath of technology like Microsoft.
Regulators are assessing whether certain Goliath of technology companies are stifling competition.

Synset Examples

In the industry, they’re the Goliath of technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant - a very large and powerful company or person
  • Antonym: David and Goliath - a metaphor for an underdog facing a much stronger opponent

Etymology

From the biblical Goliath, a giant defeated by David, symbolizing great power or dominance.
