tulipwood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlɪpˌwʊd triː / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪpˌwʊd triː / (BrE)
Any of several trees that produce wood known as tulipwood, which can be variously colored and is valued for woodworking.
tree producing tulipwood

Examples

The craftsman chose tulipwood tree for the unique color of its timber.
Many woodworking projects use wood harvested from the tulipwood tree.
Experts can sometimes identify the type of tulipwood tree based on the grain and hue of the wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tulip tree - a North American tree, Liriodendron tulipifera, also called tulipwood tree
