steel trap

1. NOUN

/ stil træp / (AmE), / stiːl træp / (BrE)
a trap made of steel with powerful springs and sharp, toothlike jaws used to catch and hold animals
spring-loaded animal trap with metal jaws

Examples

The hunter checked every steel trap he had set along the forest trail.
Environmentalists protested the use of steel traps because of the injuries they cause to wildlife.
A fox was caught in a steel trap near the edge of the property.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trap - a device for catching animals
