outdoor project

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr ˈprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a task or project that is carried out outside rather than indoors, often involving construction, gardening, or maintenance
a task or project done outdoors

Examples

The children spent the afternoon working on an outdoor project in the garden.
Before the rain started, I managed to finish my outdoor project.
Our school encourages students to participate in at least one outdoor project each semester.

Synset Examples

The Xbrick's durability makes it ideal for outdoor projects.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indoor project - a task or project carried out indoors
