shrew-sized

1. ADJ

/ ʃruː saɪzd / (AmE), / ʃruː saɪzd / (BrE)
Having approximately the same size as a shrew; very small.
of the size of a shrew; very small

Examples

The fossil was described as shrew-sized by the research team.
A shrew-sized animal could easily hide in the undergrowth.
He compared the robot to a shrew-sized creature in terms of scale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: small - of limited size or extent
