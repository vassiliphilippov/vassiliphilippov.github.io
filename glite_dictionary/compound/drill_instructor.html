drill instructor

1. NOUN

/ drɪl ɪnˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / drɪl ɪnˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a non-commissioned officer in the military who trains new recruits, especially in marching, discipline, and basic procedures
military trainer for recruits

Examples

The drill instructor barked orders as the recruits stood in perfect formation.
During basic training, the drill instructor taught us the importance of discipline and teamwork.
Every morning, our drill instructor led a rigorous exercise routine to keep us in top shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sergeant - a non-commissioned officer ranking above corporal and below staff sergeant
