law offender

1. NOUN

/ lɔ ˈəˌfɛndər / (AmE), / lɔː əˈfendə / (BrE)
a person who violates or disobeys the law
someone who breaks the law

Examples

The police are searching for the law offender who escaped custody last night.
A repeat law offender may face harsher penalties under the justice system.
The government has introduced new measures to rehabilitate law offenders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminal - a person who has committed a crime
