must-see

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌst ˌsi / (AmE), / ˈmʌst ˌsiː / (BrE)
A place, event, or work regarded as essential or highly recommended to see.
something essential or highly recommended to see

Examples

The Eiffel Tower is a must-see for anyone visiting Paris.
This exhibition is the season’s biggest must-see attraction.
The documentary was hailed as a must-see by several critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: must-read - a book or article considered essential to read

2. NOUN

/ ˈmʌst si / (AmE), / ˌmʌst ˈsiː / (BrE)
A place, event, show, or attraction that is highly recommended or regarded as essential to visit or experience.
highly recommended attraction or event

Examples

The city’s ancient cathedral is a must-see for history buffs.
That art exhibition was a real must-see this year.
If you visit Paris, the Eiffel Tower is an absolute must-see.

Synset Examples

The Roaring Meg is a must-see for tourists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: must-do - an activity that is considered essential or very desirable
