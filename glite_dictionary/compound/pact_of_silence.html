pact of silence

1. NOUN

/ pækt əv ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / pækt ɒv ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
an agreement between people to keep a secret or not to speak about something
agreement to keep silent about something

Examples

The friends entered into a pact of silence after the incident at the park.
Breaking their pact of silence would have meant betraying their trust in each other.
Journalists tried to uncover the truth, but a pact of silence kept the details hidden.

Synset Examples

They made a pact of silence about the surprise party.
Their pact of silence lasted through the whole school year.
The children formed a pact of silence after the secret was revealed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspiracy of silence - an agreement to avoid mentioning or acknowledging something
