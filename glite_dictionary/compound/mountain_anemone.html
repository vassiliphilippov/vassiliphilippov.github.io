mountain anemone

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntn̩ əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of the Rocky Mountains with silky foliage and bluish-white flowers
Rocky Mountain herb with blue-white flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with blooming mountain anemone in early summer.
Hikers admired the delicate petals of the mountain anemone along the Rocky Mountain trails.
A single, bright mountain anemone stood out among the alpine grasses.
