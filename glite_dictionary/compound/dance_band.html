dance band

1. NOUN

/ dæns bænd / (AmE), / dɑːns bænd / (BrE)
a group of musicians who play popular music, typically for people to dance to at social events
group playing music for dancing

Examples

The guests enjoyed dancing late into the night to the lively dance band.
A well-known dance band was hired to perform at the wedding.
He used to play saxophone in a dance band during his college years.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: orchestra - a group of musicians playing classical music
  • Degree Higher: band - a group of musicians playing music together
