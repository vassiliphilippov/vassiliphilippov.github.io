River Arno

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪvər ˈɑːrnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˈɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
A river in central Italy that rises in the Apennines and flows through Florence and Pisa to the Ligurian Sea.
river in central Italy

Examples

The city of Florence is divided by the River Arno.
He sailed down the River Arno during his trip to Tuscany.
Flooding from the River Arno has shaped the history of Pisa.

Etymology

From Italian 'Arno'.
