Andean condor

1. NOUN

/ ˈændiən ˈkɑːndɔr / (AmE), / ˈandiːən ˈkɒndɔː / (BrE)
a very large vulture native to the high Andes mountains, with black plumage and a white neck ruff
large vulture of the Andes mountains

Examples

The Andean condor is one of the largest flying birds in the world.
Conservationists are working to protect the Andean condor from extinction.
Spotting an Andean condor soaring above the Andes is an unforgettable experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: condor - a large New World vulture
