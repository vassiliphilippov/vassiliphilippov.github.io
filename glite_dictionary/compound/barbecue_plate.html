barbecue plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː pleɪt / (BrE)
a plate of food containing various barbecued or grilled items, often accompanied by side dishes such as beans, coleslaw, or bread
plate of assorted barbecued food

Examples

He ordered a massive barbecue plate piled high with ribs and brisket for dinner.
At the festival, every barbecue plate came with cornbread and coleslaw.
She offered me a rib from her barbecue plate.

Synset Examples

She offered me a rib from her barbecue plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbecue - food cooked on a grill or barbecue
