shopping spree

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ spri / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ spriː / (BrE)
A short period during which someone buys a large number of items or spends a lot of money shopping, often in an unrestrained or enthusiastic way.
period of excessive shopping

Examples

After receiving her bonus, Emily went on a shopping spree at the mall.
His shopping spree left him with bags of new clothes and shoes.
A shopping spree is her favorite way to celebrate a promotion.

Synset Examples

Every woman Jill loves a good shopping spree.
His pocketbook was lighter after a shopping spree.
Le shopping spree was a perfect way to unwind.
Her shopping spree will lighten her purse significantly.
His spendicitis flared up after the shopping spree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spree - a lively or wild outburst of activity
  • Synonym: binge - a period of excessive activity (often associated with eating, drinking, etc.)
