city editor

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˌɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌɛdɪtə / (BrE)
the editor at a newspaper responsible for overseeing and editing local news coverage
newspaper editor for local news

Examples

The city editor assigned reporters to cover the local election results.
After years as a reporter, she was promoted to city editor at the newspaper.
As city editor, he made sure every major city event received thorough coverage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news editor - the editor responsible for news at a newspaper, often with a broader remit than local news
