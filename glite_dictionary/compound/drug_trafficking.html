drug trafficking

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈtræfɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈtræfɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal trade or distribution of controlled substances, especially narcotic drugs
the illegal trade in drugs

Examples

The police launched a major operation to combat drug trafficking in the city.
He was arrested on charges of drug trafficking at the airport.
International cooperation is essential to effectively fight against drug trafficking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug dealing - the activity of selling illegal drugs
  • Synonym: drug smuggling - the secret or illegal transport of drugs across borders
