home economics

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks / (AmE), / həʊm ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks / (BrE)
the study or theory and practice of managing household and family affairs, including cooking, sewing, budgeting, and child care
household management study

Examples

She decided to major in home economics because she was interested in nutrition and family management.
In high school, everyone was required to take at least one home economics class.
The skills learned in home economics are useful for daily life, from cooking to budgeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic science - study of household management
