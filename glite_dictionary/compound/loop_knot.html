loop knot

1. NOUN

/ luːp nɑt / (AmE), / luːp nɒt / (BrE)
a knot made in a rope or cord to form a fixed loop that will not slip or change size
knot forming a fixed loop in a rope

Examples

To secure the tent, tie a loop knot at the end of the rope.
A loop knot is essential for making a fixed loop that won't slip under tension.
The sailor demonstrated how to make a simple loop knot in the cord.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowline - a specific type of loop knot forming a fixed loop at the end of a rope
