stress reliever

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛs rɪˌlivər / (AmE), / ˈstrɛs rɪˌliːvə / (BrE)
an object, activity, or method that helps reduce or alleviate feelings of stress
something that reduces stress

Examples

Meditation has always been a great stress reliever for me after a long day.
Many people use exercise as a stress reliever to cope with daily pressures.
Laughter can be an effective stress reliever during difficult times.

Synset Examples

The squishy toy was a stress reliever for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stress reducer - something that reduces stress
  • Antonym: stimulant - something that increases physiological or nervous activity
