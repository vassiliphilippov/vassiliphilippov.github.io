International Olympic Committee

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntɚˌnæʃənəl oʊˈlɪmpɪk kəˈmɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌnæʃənəl əʊˈlɪmpɪk kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
the organization responsible for organizing and overseeing the modern Olympic Games worldwide
governing body of the Olympic Games

Examples

The International Olympic Committee announced new rules for athlete participation in the upcoming Games.
Accusations of corruption were investigated by the International Olympic Committee.
The International Olympic Committee is based in Lausanne, Switzerland.
