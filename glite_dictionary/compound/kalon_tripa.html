Kalon Tripa

1. NOUN

/ kɑːˈloʊn ˈtriːpə / (AmE), / kəˈlɒn ˈtrɪpə / (BrE)
the chairman of the Kashag and the head of the Tibetan government-in-exile
head of Tibetan government-in-exile

Examples

The newly elected Kalon Tripa addressed the Tibetan community after taking office.
Lobsang Sangay served as the Kalon Tripa of the Tibetan government-in-exile from 2011 to 2012.
The Kalon Tripa plays a key role in representing Tibetans in the international arena.

Etymology

From Tibetan: 'kalon' (minister) + 'tripa' (chairperson).
