cartridge remover

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrtrɪdʒ rɪˈmuːvər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ rɪˈmuːvə / (BrE)
a mechanism in a firearm that extracts an empty cartridge case from the chamber and delivers it to the ejector
firearm mechanism for extracting spent cartridge cases

Examples

The cartridge remover ensured smooth extraction of spent shells during rapid firing.
After years of use, the cartridge remover in his rifle needed replacement.
Firearm maintenance manuals explain how to inspect and clean the cartridge remover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extractor - mechanism that removes spent cartridge cases from firearm chambers
