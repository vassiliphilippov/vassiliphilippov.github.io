difficult time

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪfɪkəlt taɪm / (AmE), / ˈdɪfɪkəlt tʌɪm / (BrE)
a period in which someone experiences hardship, stress, or challenges
period of hardship or challenge

Examples

After losing his job, Mark went through a difficult time but never gave up hope.
Families often come together to support each other during a difficult time.
She managed to stay positive even in the midst of a difficult time in her life.

Synset Examples

She felt the need to consolate him during difficult times.
He was there to support his friend during tough times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard time - period of hardship or adversity
  • Synonym: tough time - period marked by difficulty or challenge
