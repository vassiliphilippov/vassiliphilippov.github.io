volcanic eruption

1. NOUN

/ vɑlˈkænɪk ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪk ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
a sudden release of steam, ash, and lava from a volcano
sudden outburst from a volcano

Examples

The sudden volcanic eruption forced nearby villages to evacuate immediately.
Scientists monitor the mountain closely for any signs of a potential volcanic eruption.
The region's landscape was shaped thousands of years ago by a massive volcanic eruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eruption - a sudden outbreak, especially of something violent or noisy
