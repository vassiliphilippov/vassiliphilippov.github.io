gas worker

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person employed by a gas company, especially in the installation, maintenance, or repair of gas supply systems
person employed by a gas company

Examples

The gas worker arrived to check the safety of the boiler.
Several gas workers went on strike for better working conditions.
If you smell gas, call a gas worker immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gasman - a person who reads or repairs gas meters or lines
