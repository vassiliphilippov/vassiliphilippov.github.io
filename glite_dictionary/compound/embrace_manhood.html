to embrace manhood

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbreɪs ˈmænhʊd / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs ˈmanhʊd / (BrE)
to accept and take responsibility for one's role as an adult male
accept responsibility as an adult male

Examples

After years of self-doubt, he decided to embrace manhood and face his challenges directly.
Many cultures have rituals to help young men embrace manhood and their responsibilities.
He hesitated at first, but ultimately he embraced manhood with pride.

Synset Examples

He finally embraced his manhood after years of uncertainty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come of age - reach the age of maturity or responsibility
