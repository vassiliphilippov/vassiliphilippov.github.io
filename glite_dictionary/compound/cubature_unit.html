cubature unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bə.tʃɚ ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bə.tjʊər ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a unit used to measure volume or capacity
unit for measuring volume

Examples

The liter is a commonly used cubature unit in many countries.
When calculating the volume of a container, engineers select an appropriate cubature unit such as cubic meters.
Different industries may require different cubature units for their measurements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unit of volume - a unit used to measure the amount of space something occupies

Etymology

From Latin 'cubatura' (cubature) and 'unitas' (unity/unit).
