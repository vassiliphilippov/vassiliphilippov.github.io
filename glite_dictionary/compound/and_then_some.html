and then some

1. ADV

/ ænd ðɛn sʌm / (AmE), / ənd ðɛn sʌm / (BrE)
and much more; an additional amount beyond what was already mentioned or expected
an additional amount; more than expected

Examples

They promised to finish the work by Friday, and they delivered and then some.
He got his money back and then some after complaining about the service.
The movie met my expectations, and then some; it was truly outstanding.

Synset Examples

She scored a hundred on the test, and then some.

The project took longer than expected, and then some.
