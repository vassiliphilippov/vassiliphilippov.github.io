hickory nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪkəri nʌt / (AmE), / ˈhɪkəri nʌt / (BrE)
the edible, hard-shelled nut of North American hickory trees, especially of the shagbark hickory
nut of a North American hickory tree

Examples

We gathered hickory nuts in the woods each autumn for baking.
The squirrel cracked open a hickory nut with surprising strength.
Native Americans used hickory nuts as a staple food in their diet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pecan - edible nut of a species of hickory tree
