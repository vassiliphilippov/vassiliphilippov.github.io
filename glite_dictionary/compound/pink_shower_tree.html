pink shower tree

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈʃaʊ.ɚ tri / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈʃaʊ.ə triː / (BrE)
a tropical American semi-evergreen tree with upright clusters of pink or rose-colored flowers, cultivated as an ornamental
tropical tree with pink flower clusters

Examples

The pink shower tree bloomed beautifully in the botanical garden last spring.
Many gardeners choose the pink shower tree for its striking floral displays and minimal upkeep.
A mature pink shower tree can transform a plain yard into a vibrant, colorful landscape.
