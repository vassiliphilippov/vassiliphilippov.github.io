hard right

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd raɪt / (AmE), / hɑːd raɪt / (BrE)
The most extreme right-wing section of a political party or movement, characterized by very conservative or reactionary views.
extreme right-wing political stance

Examples

The party's hard right has gained influence in recent years.
Some policies were criticized as pandering to the hard right.
Debate broke out between the moderates and the hard right during the convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: far right - extreme right-wing political views or positions
  • Antonym: hard left - the extreme left wing in politics
