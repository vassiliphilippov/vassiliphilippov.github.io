cherry red

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri rɛd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri rɛd / (BrE)
a bright or deep red color resembling that of ripe cherries
a color like that of ripe cherries

Examples

The car was painted a striking cherry red that made it stand out on the road.
She wore a dress in cherry red for the party, which complemented her complexion perfectly.
The artist mixed several paints to achieve the perfect shade of cherry red for the still life.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: scarlet - a bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • Degree Higher: crimson - a deep red color with a hint of purple
