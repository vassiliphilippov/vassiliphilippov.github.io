Egyptian Islamic Jihad

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ɪsˈlɑmɪk dʒɪˈhɑd / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃ(ə)n ɪsˈlɑːmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd / (BrE)
a militant Islamic group, active since the late 1970s, that seeks to replace the Egyptian government with an Islamic state, operating in small underground cells
militant Islamic group in Egypt

Examples

The Egyptian Islamic Jihad played a significant role in several attacks during the 1980s and 1990s.
Authorities linked the Egyptian Islamic Jihad to a plot against government officials.
Several leaders of the Egyptian Islamic Jihad later merged with other extremist groups.
