prize money

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪz ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌmʌni / (BrE)
money awarded to the winner or winners of a competition or contest as a prize
money given as a prize

Examples

The tennis champion used her prize money to start a charity for children.
Contestants compete fiercely not just for the title but also for the prize money.
Most of the prize money was donated to local community projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award - something given as a result of merit, contest, or competition
  • Synonym: prize - something given to a winner in a competition
