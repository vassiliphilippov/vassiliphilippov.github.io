burn center

1. NOUN

/ bɝn ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / bɜːn ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a specialized hospital unit or facility equipped for the treatment of patients with severe burns
specialized medical facility for burn patients
medical

Examples

The city's only burn center provided expert care for victims of the fire.
After his accident, he was transferred to a burn center for specialized treatment.
Many doctors at the burn center are highly trained in reconstructive surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burn unit - a hospital unit that specializes in treating burns
