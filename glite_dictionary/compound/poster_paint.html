poster paint

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstər peɪnt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə peɪnt / (BrE)
a type of water-based paint, often brightly colored and inexpensive, used in art projects, especially by children
water-based paint for art projects

Examples

He used poster paint to create a colorful landscape in art class.
The children covered the cardboard with red poster paint for their project.
Make sure the poster paint dries before touching the artwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tempera - a painting medium using pigments mixed with water-soluble binders, such as egg yolk
