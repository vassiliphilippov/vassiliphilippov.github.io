suborder Blennioidea

1. NOUN

/ sʌbˌɔrdɚ blɛniˈɔɪdiə / (AmE), / sʌbˌɔːdə blɛniˈɔɪdiə / (BrE)
A taxonomic suborder of fishes that includes blennies, butterfishes, and gunnels.
taxonomic suborder of blennies and related fishes

Examples

The suborder Blennioidea includes many small, elongated marine fishes commonly found in coastal waters.
Researchers are studying the evolutionary relationships within the suborder Blennioidea.
Blennies, butterfishes, and gunnels all belong to the suborder Blennioidea.

Etymology

From New Latin Blennioidea, based on the type genus Blennius.
