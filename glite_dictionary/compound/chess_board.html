chess board

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛs bɔrd / (AmE), / tʃɛs bɔːd / (BrE)
A square board divided into 64 alternating light and dark squares, used for playing chess.
board for playing chess

Examples

He carefully arranged the pieces on the chess board before starting the game.
The tournament provided each player with a new chess board and set of pieces.
Dust had gathered on the old chess board left by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: checkerboard - a board marked with a pattern of checks, often used for games like checkers
