frontal eminence

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrʌntəl ˈɛmənəns / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntəl ˈɛmɪnəns / (BrE)
either of the two rounded prominences on the frontal bone just above each eye socket (orbit) in the human skull
rounded prominence on the forehead above the eye socket
medical

Examples

The frontal eminence is more prominent in children and tends to decrease with age.
During the examination, the doctor noted swelling near the frontal eminence.
Some people have a noticeable frontal eminence on one or both sides of the forehead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parietal eminence - rounded prominence on each side of the parietal bone of the skull

Etymology

From Latin 'frontalis' (of the forehead) and 'eminentia' (projection).
