ice rink

1. NOUN

/ aɪs rɪŋk / (AmE), / aɪs rɪŋk / (BrE)
an enclosed area with a floor of ice, used for ice skating, ice hockey, or similar activities
enclosed area for ice skating or hockey

Examples

Every winter, a temporary ice rink is set up in the city center.
The hockey team practiced at the local ice rink every morning.
She took her children to the ice rink for their first skating lesson.

Synset Examples

the crowd applauded when she skated out onto the ice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skating rink - a place where people go to skate (on ice or on roller skates)
