turd polishing

1. NOUN

/ tɝd ˈpɑlɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tɜːd ˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of attempting to make something fundamentally bad, worthless, or unattractive seem better, more appealing, or of higher quality.
making something bad seem better
slang vulgar

Examples

The redesign was just turd polishing; the product itself is still flawed.
Critics accused the company of turd polishing instead of fixing the real problems.
No amount of turd polishing will save a terrible script from being a bad movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lipstick on a pig - the act of making superficial changes to make something appear better than it is
