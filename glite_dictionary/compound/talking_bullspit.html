to talking bullspit

1. VERB

/ ˈtɔkɪŋ ˈbʊlspɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˈbʊlspɪt / (BrE)
speaking nonsense or irrelevant matters, often to evade the truth or avoid giving a direct answer
speaking nonsense; evasive talk
informal

Examples

He was clearly talking bullspit during the interview to impress the panel.
I wish politicians would stop talking bullspit and address real issues.
She accused him of talking bullspit instead of giving a straight answer.

Synset Examples

Stop talking bullspit and get to the point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - language that is deliberately misleading or nonsensical

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'bullshit', using 'spit' to avoid vulgarity.
