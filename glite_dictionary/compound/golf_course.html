golf course

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf kɔrs / (AmE), / ɡɒlf kɔːs / (BrE)
An area of land, usually landscaped and with a series of holes, designed for playing the game of golf.
area for playing golf

Examples

The championship will be held at the famous golf course next month.
He spends most weekends playing at the local golf course with friends.
A new golf course is being constructed on the outskirts of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairway - part of a golf course between the tee and the green
  • Synonym: driving range - practice area for hitting golf balls
