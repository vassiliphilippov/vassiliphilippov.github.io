stump spud

1. NOUN

/ stʌmp spʌd / (AmE), / stʌmp spʌd / (BrE)
a sharp hand shovel with a pointed blade, used for digging out roots and weeds
tool for digging out roots and weeds

Examples

The gardener used a stump spud to dig out the stubborn weed roots.
A stump spud is essential for clearing overgrown garden beds.
After hours of work with the stump spud, the roots were finally removed.
