Tongan language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑŋən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɒŋən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Polynesian language spoken by the people of Tonga.
language spoken in Tonga

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Tongan language.
The Tongan language uses a unique set of letters and pronunciation.
They offered translation services in the Tongan language for local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Samoan language - the Polynesian language spoken in Samoa
  • Synonym: Polynesian language - a language branch within the Austronesian family, including Tongan
