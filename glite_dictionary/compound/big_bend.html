Big Bend

1. PROPN

/ bɪɡ bɛnd / (AmE), / bɪɡ bɛnd / (BrE)
a region in southwestern Texas, United States, defined by a large bend in the course of the Rio Grande along the Mexican border
region in southwestern Texas shaped by the Rio Grande

Examples

Many people visit Big Bend each year to explore its rugged landscapes and diverse wildlife.
The Rio Grande makes a dramatic turn in the area known as Big Bend.
We planned a hiking trip in Big Bend National Park over the summer.
