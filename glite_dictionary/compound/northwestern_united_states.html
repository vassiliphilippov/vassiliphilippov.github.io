northwestern United States

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrθˈwɛstɚn ˌjuːnɪˈtɛd ˈsteɪts / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛstən ˌjuːnɪtɪd ˈsteɪts / (BrE)
the region of the United States located in the northwest, typically including states such as Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Montana, and sometimes Wyoming and Alaska
region in the northwest of the US

Examples

She moved to the northwestern United States to enjoy the beautiful scenery and mild climate.
The northwestern United States is known for its lush forests and abundant rainfall.
Wildfires have become more common in the northwestern United States during the summer months.
