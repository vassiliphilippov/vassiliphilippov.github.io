fore edge

1. NOUN

/ fɔr ɛdʒ / (AmE), / fɔːr ɛdʒ / (BrE)
the edge of a book’s pages opposite the spine, visible when the book is closed
edge of a book opposite the spine

Examples

When the book is closed, you can see the gilded fore edge shimmer in the light.
Some rare volumes have paintings hidden on the fore edge of the pages.
Please check the fore edge for any damage before shelving the book.
