physical break

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl breɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl breɪk / (BrE)
a short pause from work or activity taken for physical movement, exercise, or rest
pause for physical activity or rest

Examples

It's important to take a physical break every hour when working at a desk.
Many teachers now schedule a daily physical break to help students stay focused.
She stretched her legs during a quick physical break from studying.

Synset Examples

Take a physical break to boost your productivity.
After hours of sitting, a physical break is essential.
He always encourages a physical break during long meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break - a short period of time when you stop what you are doing and rest, eat, etc.
