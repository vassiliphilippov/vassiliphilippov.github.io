bad history

1. NOUN

/ bæd ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / bæd ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
a record of negative behavior, actions, or reputation in a person's or organization's past
a negative past or reputation

Examples

His company had a bad history with investors, so they were hesitant to trust him.
She was rejected from the club due to her bad history with other members.
The politician tried to hide his bad history during the election campaign.

Synset Examples

No one trusts a smearer with such a bad history.
