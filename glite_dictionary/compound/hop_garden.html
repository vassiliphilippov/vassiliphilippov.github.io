hop garden

1. NOUN

/ hɑp ˈɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / hɒp ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A field or garden where hop plants are cultivated, especially for use in brewing beer.
garden or field where hops are grown

Examples

The old brewery was surrounded by a lush hop garden that provided fresh ingredients for their ales.
During the summer, tourists can walk through the picturesque rows of the hop garden.
He took great pride in managing every aspect of his family's hop garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hopfield - a field where hops are grown
