big top

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ tɑp / (AmE), / bɪɡ tɒp / (BrE)
A large tent, especially one used for housing the main circus performance and its audience.
large circus tent

Examples

The crowd erupted in applause as the performers entered the big top.
After the show, the children explored behind the big top to meet the clowns.
They traveled from town to town, setting up the big top wherever they could.

Synset Examples

he was afraid of a fire in the circus tent
they had the big top up in less than an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circus tent - a large tent used at a circus for performances
