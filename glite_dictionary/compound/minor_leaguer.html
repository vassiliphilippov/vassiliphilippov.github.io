minor leaguer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚ ˌliːɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə ˌliːɡə / (BrE)
a player who is a member of a minor-league baseball team
baseball player in the minor leagues
american

Examples

After spending three years as a minor leaguer, he was finally called up to the majors.
Many minor leaguers dream of one day playing at Yankee Stadium.
The young minor leaguer impressed scouts with his powerful hitting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: major leaguer - a player on a major-league baseball team
