yellow spiny daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈspaɪni ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈspaɪni ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a slender perennial plant of western North America with weakly bristly leaves and yellow flower heads
North American perennial plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The yellow spiny daisy blooms throughout the summer in the high meadows.
Botanists identified several yellow spiny daisy plants in the national park.
She planted a yellow spiny daisy in her wildflower garden to attract bees.
