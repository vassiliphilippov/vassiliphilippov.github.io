prize-winning heifer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪz ˌwɪnɪŋ ˈhifər / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌwɪnɪŋ ˈhiːfə / (BrE)
a young female cow that has been awarded a prize for excellence in livestock competitions
young cow awarded a prize

Examples

She proudly led her prize-winning heifer into the arena for the final judging.
The prize-winning heifer brought attention to their family farm at the state fair.
After months of care, their prize-winning heifer earned the blue ribbon.

Synset Examples

She raised a prize-winning heifer at the county fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heifer - a young female cow that has not borne a calf
  • Synonym: prize-winning - having won a prize, especially in a competition
