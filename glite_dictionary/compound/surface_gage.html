surface gage

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfɪs ˌɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ˌɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A measuring instrument consisting of a scriber on an adjustable stand, used to test the accuracy of flat surfaces.
instrument for testing flatness of surfaces

Examples

The machinist used a surface gage to ensure the workpiece was perfectly level.
A surface gage is essential for marking precise lines on metal surfaces.
When checking the flatness of the table, he reached for his trusty surface gage.

  • Synonym: surface gauge - Alternate spelling for surface gage
