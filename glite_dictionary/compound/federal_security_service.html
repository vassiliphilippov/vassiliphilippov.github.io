Federal Security Service

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɛdərəl sɪˈkjʊrəti ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɛd(ə)rəl sɪˈkjʊərəti ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the principal internal security and counterintelligence agency of the Russian Federation, and successor to the Soviet KGB
Russian internal security and intelligence agency

Examples

The Federal Security Service increased surveillance operations in several Russian cities.
Reports indicate that the Federal Security Service has been involved in several major cyber investigations.
After the collapse of the Soviet Union, the Federal Security Service became the primary intelligence agency in Russia.
