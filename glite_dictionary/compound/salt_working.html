salt working

1. NOUN

/ sɔlt ˈwɝkɪŋ / (AmE), / sɒlt ˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The process or industry of manufacturing or extracting salt, especially from brine or mines.
salt manufacture or extraction

Examples

Traditional salt working techniques are still practiced in some coastal regions.
The museum features artifacts related to local salt working dating back centuries.
Environmental concerns have changed the methods used in modern salt working.

Synset Examples

From the north coast of Brittany to the Vendee region there is evidence of salt-working, salt being extracted from sea brine in ovens equipped with pans and then made into blocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salt production - the process of producing salt
  • Synonym: salt mine - a place where salt is extracted from the ground
