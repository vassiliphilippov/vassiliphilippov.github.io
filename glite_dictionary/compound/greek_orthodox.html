Greek Orthodox

1. ADJ

/ ɡrik ˈɔrθədɑks / (AmE), / ɡriːk ˈɔːθədɒks / (BrE)
Relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church, especially in its Greek tradition, practices, or members.
of the Eastern Orthodox Church, especially Greek tradition

Examples

She was married in a Greek Orthodox church in her hometown.
The Greek Orthodox celebration of Easter often falls on a different date than the Western Christian observance.
Many Greek Orthodox traditions have been passed down through generations in his family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eastern Orthodox - of or relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church as a whole
  • Antonym: Roman Catholic - of or relating to the Roman Catholic Church
