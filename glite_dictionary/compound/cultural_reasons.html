cultural reasons

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃɚəl ˈrizənz / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈriːzənz / (BrE)
motives or justifications based on shared beliefs, customs, or practices of a particular culture
motives based on culture or tradition

Examples

The company chose not to expand into that market for cultural reasons.
Wearing traditional clothing is often done for cultural reasons during holidays.
Some foods are avoided for cultural reasons rather than for health concerns.

Synset Examples

Eunuchs were often castrated for various cultural reasons.
He wants to learn Chinese for cultural reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religious reasons - motives or justifications based on religious beliefs or practices
  • Synonym: personal reasons - motives based on an individual's own needs or situations
