antifungal agent

1. NOUN

/ ˌæntiˈfʌŋɡəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈfʌŋɡəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
A substance that destroys or inhibits the growth of fungi.
substance that kills or inhibits fungi
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed an antifungal agent to treat the athlete's foot infection.
You should apply the antifungal agent directly to the affected area twice a day.
Laboratory tests confirmed that the antifungal agent was effective against multiple types of fungi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antifungal - a substance that destroys or inhibits the growth of fungi

Etymology

From 'anti-' meaning 'against' and Latin 'fungus' (fungus), combined with 'agent' meaning 'one who acts'.
