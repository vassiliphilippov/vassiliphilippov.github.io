stock market bourse

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑk ˌmɑrkət ˈbʊrs / (AmE), / ˈstɒk ˌmɑːkɪt ˈbʊəs / (BrE)
an organized market where stocks and other securities are bought and sold
stock exchange; securities market

Examples

The stock market bourse opened to mixed reactions following the economic report.
Foreign investors are closely watching developments at the main stock market bourse.
She works as an analyst at the country's largest stock market bourse.

Synset Examples

The stock market bourse opened to mixed reactions today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock exchange - an organized market for the trading of stocks and securities
  • Synonym: bourse - a European stock exchange

Etymology

‘Bourse’ is from French, originally meaning ‘purse’ and later referring to a stock exchange.
