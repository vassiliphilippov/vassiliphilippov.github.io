sanitary napkin

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænəˌtɛri ˈnæpkɪn / (AmE), / ˈsænɪtəri ˈnæpkɪn / (BrE)
A disposable absorbent pad worn by women during menstruation to absorb menstrual flow.
disposable absorbent pad for menstruation

Examples

She placed a sanitary napkin in her bag before leaving for school.
Some brands offer sanitary napkins with extra absorbency for overnight use.
Proper disposal of a sanitary napkin is important for hygiene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanitary pad - absorbent pad for menstruation
  • Synonym: tampon - cylindrical absorbent device inserted to absorb menstrual flow
