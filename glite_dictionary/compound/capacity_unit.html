capacity unit

1. NOUN

/ kəˈpæsɪti ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / kəˈpæsɪti ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit of measurement used to quantify volume or capacity
unit of measurement for volume

Examples

The liter is a common capacity unit used around the world.
In the United States, the gallon is a widely recognized capacity unit.
Scientists must convert between different capacity units when sharing data internationally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volume unit - a unit of measurement for the space an object or substance occupies
