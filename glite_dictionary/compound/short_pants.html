short pants

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt pænts / (AmE), / ʃɔːt pænts / (BrE)
trousers that end at or above the knee, typically worn in warm weather or for sports
trousers ending above the knee

Examples

He wore short pants to the picnic because the weather was hot.
Schoolchildren often wore short pants as part of their summer uniform.
In the early 20th century, boys commonly wore short pants until they reached a certain age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorts - trousers ending above the knee, shortened form
