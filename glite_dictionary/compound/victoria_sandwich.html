Victoria sandwich

1. NOUN

/ vɪkˈtɔriə ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a cake made with two layers of sponge cake and a jam filling between them, often topped with sugar or cream
sponge cake with jam filling
british

Examples

For her birthday, she requested a classic Victoria sandwich with strawberry jam in the center.
The baker displayed a beautifully decorated Victoria sandwich alongside other traditional British cakes.
Afternoon tea was served with a Victoria sandwich and delicate cups of Earl Grey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Victoria sponge - a sponge cake with jam and cream filling, similar to Victoria sandwich

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria, who was said to enjoy it with afternoon tea.
