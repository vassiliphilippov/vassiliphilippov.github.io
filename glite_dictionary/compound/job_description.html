job description

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb dɪˌskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / dʒɒb dɪˌskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
a written statement outlining the duties, responsibilities, and qualifications for a particular job position
statement of job duties and requirements

Examples

Before applying for the position, she carefully read the job description to understand the required skills and duties.
A clear job description helps both employers and employees know what is expected in the role.
The manager updated the job description to include new responsibilities after the recent restructuring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job specification - detailed statement of job qualifications and skills required
