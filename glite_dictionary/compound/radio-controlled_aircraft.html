radio-controlled aircraft

1. NOUN

/ ˌreɪdioʊ kənˈtroʊld ˈɛrkˌkræft / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊ kənˈtrəʊld ˈeəkrɑːft / (BrE)
an aircraft flown without a pilot on board, operated remotely by radio signals from a transmitter.
remotely operated aircraft using radio control

Examples

The competition featured a variety of radio-controlled aircraft performing aerobatic maneuvers.
He assembled the radio-controlled aircraft in his garage before testing it in the field.
Advances in technology have made radio-controlled aircraft more accessible to hobbyists of all ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drone - an unmanned aircraft controlled remotely or autonomously
  • Synonym: model aircraft - a small, remotely controlled or free-flight replica of an aircraft
