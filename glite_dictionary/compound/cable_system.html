cable system

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
A television system in which programs are transmitted to subscribers by signals sent through cables rather than broadcast over the air.
television system using cables

Examples

The cable system offers hundreds of channels and on-demand content.
Many rural areas installed a cable system to improve television reception.
He decided to upgrade to a digital cable system for better picture quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cable television - a system of delivering television programming via coaxial or fiber-optic cables
