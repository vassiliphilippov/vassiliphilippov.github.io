Jew bush

1. NOUN

/ dʒu bʊʃ / (AmE), / dʒuː bʊʃ / (BrE)
a low, tropical American shrub known for its powerful emetic properties
tropical American emetic shrub

Examples

The leaves of the Jew bush have been traditionally used as an emetic in folk medicine.
Botanists identified the specimen as a rare Jew bush native to the region.
Some gardeners warn that the Jew bush can be toxic if ingested.
