Port Jackson heath

1. NOUN

/ pɔrt ˈdʒæksən hiθ / (AmE), / pɔːt ˈdʒæksən hiːθ / (BrE)
a small shrub native to southern and western Australia, with pinkish to rosy purple tubular flowers
Australian shrub with pink-purple tubular flowers

Examples

The Port Jackson heath produces vibrant pink flowers in spring across coastal regions.
Gardeners prize the Port Jackson heath for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
We spotted several Port Jackson heath shrubs while hiking in southern Australia.
