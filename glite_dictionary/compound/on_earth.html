on Earth

1. ADV

/ ɑn ɝθ / (AmE), / ɒn ɜːθ / (BrE)
used as an intensifier to emphasize surprise, disbelief, or confusion, especially in questions.
used to express surprise or disbelief

Examples

Why on Earth would anyone want to climb that mountain during winter?
How on Earth did you manage to fix the car so quickly?
Where on Earth have you been all this time?

Synset Examples

I can't understand why on Earth he left so early.
Why on Earth didn't you tell me sooner?
Why on Earth would she do that?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the world - used for emphasis in questions expressing surprise or confusion
