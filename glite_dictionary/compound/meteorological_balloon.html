meteorological balloon

1. NOUN

/ ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl bəˈluːn / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a small unmanned balloon released into the atmosphere to collect data on weather and atmospheric conditions
instrument for measuring atmospheric conditions

Examples

The meteorological balloon was launched at dawn to gather data on upper air temperatures.
Scientists track the path of each meteorological balloon as it ascends through the atmosphere.
A meteorological balloon can reach altitudes of over 30 kilometers before bursting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather balloon - a balloon carrying instruments for measuring atmospheric conditions
