Go Fish

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊ fɪʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fɪʃ / (BrE)
A simple card game for two or more players in which participants ask each other for cards in an attempt to collect sets of four cards of the same rank.
children's card game involving asking for cards

Examples

The kids spent the afternoon playing Go Fish in the living room.
Do you remember how to play Go Fish or should I explain the rules again?
At the family reunion, everyone enjoyed a few rounds of Go Fish after dinner.
