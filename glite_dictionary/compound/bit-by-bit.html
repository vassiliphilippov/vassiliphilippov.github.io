bit-by-bit

1. ADV

/ ˌbɪt.baɪˈbɪt / (AmE), / ˌbɪt.baɪˈbɪt / (BrE)
gradually; by doing one thing at a time or little by little
gradually, one step at a time

Examples

They renovated the old house bit-by-bit until it was finally finished.
She learned to play the piano bit-by-bit, practicing a little each day.
The puzzle was solved bit-by-bit as more clues became available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradually - slowly over time, in small stages
  • Synonym: step-by-step - in a series of distinct steps
