double-breasted jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌb.əlˈbrɛs.tɪd ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˌdʌb.əlˈbrest.ɪd ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (BrE)
a jacket with overlapping front panels and two parallel rows of buttons
jacket with two rows of buttons

Examples

He wore a stylish double-breasted jacket to the formal dinner.
The tailor recommended a double-breasted jacket for a more classic look.
Her grandfather's old double-breasted jacket was made from heavy wool.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-breasted jacket - a jacket with a narrow overlap at the front and only one row of buttons
