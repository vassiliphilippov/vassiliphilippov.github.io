part of speech

1. NOUN

/ pɑrt əv spiːtʃ / (AmE), / pɑːt əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
one of the classes into which words are divided according to their grammatical functions, such as noun, verb, or adjective
category of word by grammar function

Examples

Every word in English belongs to a part of speech such as noun, verb, or adjective.
The dictionary entry lists the part of speech for each word.
Understanding the part of speech can help you use new vocabulary correctly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: word class - category of words with similar grammatical properties
