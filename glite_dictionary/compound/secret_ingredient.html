secret ingredient

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːkrət ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
A particular component, not revealed to others, that is believed to give a recipe, product, or process its distinctive or enhanced quality.
special component kept hidden; enhances recipe or result

Examples

No one could figure out what the secret ingredient in her famous cookies was.
He believes the secret ingredient in every successful team is trust.
The chef finally revealed that saffron was the secret ingredient in the soup.

Synset Examples

The secret ingredient in this dish is the smokum flavor.
You'll never guess the secret ingredient in this dish.
The secret ingredient makes this dish unique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingredient - a component part of any mixture or compound
