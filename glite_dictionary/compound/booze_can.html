booze can

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːz kæn / (AmE), / ˈbuːz kan / (BrE)
an informal term for a can containing alcoholic beverages, especially beer
a can of alcoholic drink (informal)
informal

Examples

He crushed the booze can after finishing his drink.
Please clean up those empty booze cans from the living room.
She carried a cold booze can to the picnic table.

Synset Examples

I found an empty booze can under the couch.
She tossed the empty booze can into the recycling bin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beer can - a can containing beer
