Pyrenees daisy

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈriniːz ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / pɪˈriːniːz ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A perennial plant of Portugal, similar in appearance to the oxeye daisy.
perennial Portuguese plant, like oxeye daisy

Examples

The Pyrenees daisy blooms in late spring and adds brightness to many Portuguese gardens.
She planted a Pyrenees daisy next to the roses for a splash of white and yellow.
Unlike the common oxeye, the Pyrenees daisy thrives best in the mild climate of southwestern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oxeye daisy - a similar daisy-like flowering plant (Leucanthemum vulgare)
