letter telegram

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtɚ ˈtɛləˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə ˈtɛlɪɡræm / (BrE)
A type of telegram sent abroad at a lower cost, delivered to the recipient the following day.
international overnight telegram service

Examples

She sent a letter telegram to her family overseas, knowing it would arrive the next day.
A letter telegram was the most affordable way to communicate across continents in the early 20th century.
Before the internet, many people relied on the letter telegram service for urgent international messages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph
