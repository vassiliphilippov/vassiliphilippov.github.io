low-altitude

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌæltɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌæltɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
occurring or taking place at a relatively low altitude, close to the ground or sea level
at a relatively low altitude

Examples

The helicopter performed a low-altitude maneuver over the river.
Many migratory birds travel at low-altitude to conserve energy.
The radar detected a low-altitude aircraft approaching from the west.

Synset Examples

a low-level strafing run

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-altitude - occurring at a relatively high altitude
