wanted poster

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑntɪd ˌpoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈwɒntɪd ˌpəʊstə / (BrE)
a public notice issued by law enforcement containing a picture and details of a person they seek to apprehend or question
public notice seeking a person (law enforcement)

Examples

The sheriff hung a wanted poster in the main street window to alert the townspeople.
After seeing his face on a wanted poster, the suspect decided to turn himself in.
Many old Western movies feature a wanted poster as a symbol of law and order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notice - a written or printed announcement or warning
