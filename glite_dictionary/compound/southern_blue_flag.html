southern blue flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌð.ɚn bluː flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsʌð.ən bluː flæɡ / (BrE)
a North American iris (Iris virginica), found chiefly in the eastern United States, with violet-blue flowers similar to the blue flag.
a North American iris with blue-violet flowers

Examples

The southern blue flag blooms in wetlands across the southeastern United States each spring.
Gardeners noted that the southern blue flag thrived best in well-drained, marshy soils.
During the hike, we spotted several clusters of southern blue flag along the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue flag - a species of iris with blue-violet flowers (Iris versicolor)
