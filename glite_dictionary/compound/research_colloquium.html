research colloquium

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɝːtʃ kəˈloʊkwiəm / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ kəˈləʊkwiəm / (BrE)
an academic meeting or seminar at which research findings are presented and discussed, usually among experts in a particular field
academic meeting for presenting research

Examples

The department hosts a research colloquium every Friday afternoon for graduate students and faculty.
She presented her latest findings at the annual research colloquium on cognitive science.
Attending the research colloquium allowed him to receive valuable feedback from peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seminar - a class or meeting for discussion or training
  • Synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion

Etymology

colloquium from Latin 'colloquium' (conversation, conference); research from Old French 'recercher' (to seek out).
