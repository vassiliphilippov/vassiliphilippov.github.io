bus ticket

1. NOUN

/ bʌs ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / bʌs ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that permits a person to travel on a bus
ticket for traveling on a bus

Examples

She bought a bus ticket to get to the city center.
You must show your bus ticket when boarding the vehicle.
He lost his bus ticket and had to buy another one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train ticket - a ticket for traveling on a train
  • Synonym: plane ticket - a ticket for traveling by airplane
