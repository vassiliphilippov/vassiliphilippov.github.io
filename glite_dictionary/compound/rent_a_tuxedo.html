to rent a tuxedo

1. VERB

/ rɛnt ə tʌkˈsidoʊ / (AmE), / rɛnt ə tʌkˈsiːdəʊ / (BrE)
to hire a tuxedo, a formal suit, for a temporary period
hire a formal suit temporarily

Examples

Many people choose to rent a tuxedo instead of buying one for special occasions.
He decided to rent a tuxedo for the high school prom.
If you rent a tuxedo, make sure to return it on time to avoid extra charges.

Synset Examples

They rented a tuxed for the wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hire a tuxedo - to hire a tuxedo for a temporary period (British equivalent)
