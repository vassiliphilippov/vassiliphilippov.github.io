Government Accounting Office

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvəmənt əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
an independent, nonpartisan federal agency in the United States that acts as the investigative arm of Congress, monitoring the use of public funds by the executive branch and ensuring governmental accountability
U.S. federal government audit agency
american

Examples

The Government Accounting Office released a report detailing the misuse of federal funds by several agencies last year.
Congress requested the Government Accounting Office to investigate the effectiveness of new healthcare policies.
Before making changes, lawmakers often consult the Government Accounting Office for their recommendations on best practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: General Accounting Office - former name of the Government Accountability Office in the U.S.
  • Synonym: Government Accountability Office - the current name for the U.S. congressional audit agency
