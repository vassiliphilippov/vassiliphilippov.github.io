metric shitload

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛtrɪk ˈʃɪtloʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪk ˈʃɪtləʊd / (BrE)
an extremely large quantity of something (vulgar and slang)
very large quantity (vulgar)
vulgar slang

Examples

After the merger, the company had a metric shitload of new clients to manage.
He inherited a metric shitload of money from his uncle.
There’s a metric shitload of paperwork to finish before the deadline.

Synset Examples

We received a metric shitload of applications for the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shitload - a very large amount
