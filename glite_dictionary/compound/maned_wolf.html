maned wolf

1. NOUN

/ meɪnd wʊlf / (AmE), / meɪnd wʊlf / (BrE)
a large, long-legged wild canid of South America, with reddish-brown fur and a distinctive mane along the neck and shoulders
South American wild canid with a mane

Examples

The maned wolf is known for its long legs and reddish coat.
Researchers have worked to protect the habitat of the maned wolf in Brazil.
A maned wolf at the zoo attracted attention with its tall, elegant appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wolf - large wild member of the dog family (Canis lupus)
