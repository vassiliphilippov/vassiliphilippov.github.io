PR woman

1. NOUN

/ pi ɑːr ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / piː ɑː ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman employed to arrange publicity or manage public relations for a company, organization, or public figure
female public relations professional

Examples

The company hired a PR woman to handle their press releases and media inquiries.
She worked as a PR woman for a major film studio in Hollywood.
Many events owe their success to the expertise of a talented PR woman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PR man - a man employed to arrange publicity or manage public relations
  • Synonym: publicist - a person who manages or generates publicity for a client or organization
