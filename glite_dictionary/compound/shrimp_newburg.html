shrimp Newburg

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɪmp ˈnuːˌbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ʃrɪmp ˈnjuːˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A dish consisting of shrimp cooked in a rich, creamy Newburg sauce, often served in a ring of rice.
shrimp dish in creamy Newburg sauce

Examples

The restaurant's signature shrimp Newburg was served in a delicate rice ring.
For a special dinner, she prepared homemade shrimp Newburg with a creamy sauce.
He ordered shrimp Newburg because he preferred shrimp to lobster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lobster Newburg - lobster in rich Newburg sauce

Etymology

Named after the dish 'Lobster Newburg,' created in the late 19th century and adapted for shrimp.
