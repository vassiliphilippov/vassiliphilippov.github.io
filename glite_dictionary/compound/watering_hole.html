watering hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚɪŋ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ həʊl / (BrE)
an informal place where people gather to drink or socialize, especially a bar or pub
informal bar or social drinking spot
informal

Examples

After work, the group usually heads to their favorite watering hole for a drink.
The city is full of lively watering holes where locals and tourists mingle.
We stumbled upon a cozy watering hole tucked away in a quiet alley.

Synset Examples

The local bar became our favorite watering hole after work.
Every weekend, they gather at the watering hole to catch up.
We met at the watering hole for drinks and laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar - place where alcoholic drinks are served
  • Synonym: pub - public house; a place for drinking and socializing

Etymology

From the original sense referring to a natural water source for animals, later figuratively applied to drinking establishments for people.
