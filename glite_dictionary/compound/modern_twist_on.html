modern twist on

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːdərn twɪst ɑn / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən twɪst ɒn / (BrE)
a contemporary reinterpretation or adaptation of something traditional or well-known
contemporary reinterpretation

Examples

The restaurant offers traditional dishes with a modern twist on classic flavors.
Her designs are a modern twist on vintage fashion trends.
The film gives a modern twist on an old fairy tale.

Synset Examples

The play was a modern twist on Sherlock Holmes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reinterpretation - an interpretation again in a new or different way
