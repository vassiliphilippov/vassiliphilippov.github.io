subway system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌb.weɪ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
an electric railway network operating mostly underground in a city, used for mass passenger transport
underground urban railway network

Examples

The city plans to expand its subway system to reach the outer suburbs.
Many commuters rely on the subway system for their daily travel in New York.
During rush hour, the subway system can become extremely crowded.

Synset Examples

in Paris the subway system is called the ‘metro’ and in London it is called the ‘tube’ or the ‘underground’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metro - urban underground railway system, especially outside North America
  • Synonym: subway - underground urban railway system
  • Synonym: underground - British term for urban railway system mostly below ground
  • Degree Lower: railway - train system, general, above or below ground
