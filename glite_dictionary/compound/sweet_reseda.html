sweet reseda

1. NOUN

/ swit rɪˈsiːdə / (AmE), / swiːt rɪˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a Mediterranean annual plant with dense, spikelike clusters of greenish or yellowish-white highly fragrant flowers, widely cultivated for its scent
fragrant Mediterranean annual plant

Examples

The garden was filled with the strong fragrance of sweet reseda in the summer evening.
Many gardeners grow sweet reseda for its attractive, aromatic blossoms.
The sweet reseda attracted bees and butterflies to the flower beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mignonette - another name for sweet reseda, a fragrant flowering plant
