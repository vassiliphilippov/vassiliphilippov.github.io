butter daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdər ˌdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ˌdeɪzi / (BrE)
a coarse annual herb with greyish-green leaves and yellow flowers, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.
yellow-flowered annual herb

Examples

The butter daisy thrives in dry, sunny gardens in the southwestern United States.
She planted a row of butter daisies along the fence to brighten the yard.
Wild butter daisy can add a splash of yellow to natural landscapes in Mexico.
