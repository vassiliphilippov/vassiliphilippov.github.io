bingo card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪŋgoʊ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋgəʊ kɑːd / (BrE)
a card printed with numbers in a grid, used by players in the game of bingo to mark off called numbers
card with numbers used in bingo

Examples

She cheered when she realized her bingo card was just one number away from a win.
Could you pass me a fresh bingo card before the next round begins?
The elderly woman carefully dabbed each square on her bingo card with a marker.

Synset Examples

I marked my bingo card as soon as I heard 'B12'.
Each player received a bingo card at the start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bingo - a game of chance where players mark numbers on a card as they're announced
