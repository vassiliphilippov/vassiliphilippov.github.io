Alpine celery pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn ˈsɛləri paɪn / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈsɛləri paɪn / (BrE)
A small shrubby tree of New Zealand belonging to the celery pine family, found in alpine regions.
New Zealand alpine shrubby tree

Examples

The Alpine celery pine thrives in the cool, mountainous regions of New Zealand.
Botanists are studying the resilience of the Alpine celery pine to extreme weather conditions.
We spotted several Alpine celery pines growing along the rocky slopes during our hike.
