dog flea

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ fli / (AmE), / dɒɡ fliː / (BrE)
a species of flea (Ctenocephalides canis) that infests dogs and cats, often causing itching and skin irritation
a flea species infesting dogs and cats

Examples

The veterinarian discovered that the dog's itching was caused by dog fleas.
Regular grooming helps protect pets from a dog flea infestation.
Treatment for dog flea bites should be started as soon as symptoms appear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat flea - a species of flea most commonly found on cats and dogs (Ctenocephalides felis)
