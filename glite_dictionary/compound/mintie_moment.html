Mintie moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnti ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɪnti ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a sweet, nostalgic, or particularly joyful experience, often remembered fondly
a sweet or nostalgic joyful experience
informal american
