distinctive feature

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɪŋktɪv ˈfiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋktɪv ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a characteristic that distinguishes someone or something from others; an attribute that makes something unique or notable
unique distinguishing characteristic

Examples

One distinctive feature of the building is its bright red door.
Her laugh is a distinctive feature that everyone recognizes instantly.
The animal’s stripes are its most distinctive feature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trait - a distinguishing quality or characteristic
  • Synonym: feature - a notable or prominent part or quality
