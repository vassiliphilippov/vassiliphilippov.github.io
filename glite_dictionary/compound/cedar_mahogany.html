cedar mahogany

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːdər məˈhɑːɡəni / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə məˈhɒɡəni / (BrE)
An African tree with lightweight, cedar-scented wood that varies in color from pink to reddish brown.
African tree with cedar-scented wood

Examples

The wood from cedar mahogany is prized by cabinet makers for its attractive color and light weight.
They imported several planks of cedar mahogany to use in the new furniture line.
The scent of cedar mahogany filled the carpentry workshop as the craftsman began cutting the boards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mahogany - Tropical hardwood tree and its wood, widely used in furniture making
