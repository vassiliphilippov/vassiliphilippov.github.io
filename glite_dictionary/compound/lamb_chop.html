lamb chop

1. NOUN

/ læm tʃɑp / (AmE), / læm tʃɒp / (BrE)
a small cut of lamb meat, usually including a rib or part of a rib, often cooked by grilling or frying
cut of lamb meat with a bone

Examples

He ordered a grilled lamb chop with mint sauce at the restaurant.
She bought several lamb chops to prepare for dinner.
The aroma of sizzling lamb chop filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pork chop - a chop cut from a pig
  • Synonym: mutton chop - a chop cut from the meat of a grown sheep

2. NOUN

/ læm tʃɑp / (AmE), / læm tʃɒp / (BrE)
a small piece of lamb, usually including a bone, that is cut from the rib or loin and cooked as food
a small cut of lamb with bone, for cooking

Examples

He ordered a lamb chop with mint sauce for dinner.
The chef grilled the lamb chops until they were perfectly tender.
Would you like your lamb chop cooked rare or medium?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pork chop - chop cut from a pig
