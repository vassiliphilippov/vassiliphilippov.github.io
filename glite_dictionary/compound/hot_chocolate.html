hot chocolate

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈtʃɑk(ə)lət / (AmE), / hɒt ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A warm, sweet beverage made by mixing cocoa powder or chocolate with milk and often sugar.
sweet warm drink with chocolate and milk

Examples

She made a cup of hot chocolate to warm herself after coming in from the snow.
Would you like some marshmallows with your hot chocolate?
On cold winter nights, I love to drink hot chocolate by the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocoa - a drink made by mixing cocoa powder with sugar and hot milk or water
  • Degree Lower: chocolate milk - a sweet drink made with milk and chocolate syrup or powder, served cold

sweet chocolate drink, served hot

Examples
