road builder

1. NOUN

/ roʊd ˈbɪldər / (AmE), / rəʊd ˈbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or company that constructs roads as a business or profession
person or company constructing roads

Examples

The road builder surveyed the area before starting construction on the new highway.
Several road builders were hired to finish the project ahead of schedule.
Becoming a road builder requires knowledge of both engineering and heavy machinery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - someone who constructs buildings or other structures
