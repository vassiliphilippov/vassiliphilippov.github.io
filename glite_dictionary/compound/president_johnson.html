President Johnson

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɛzɪdənt ˈdʒɑnsən / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt ˈdʒɒnsən / (BrE)
the 17th President of the United States (1808–1875), who succeeded Abraham Lincoln and was impeached but acquitted by one vote
17th U.S. President, succeeded Lincoln

Examples
