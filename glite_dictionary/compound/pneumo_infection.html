pneumo infection

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːmoʊ ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈnjuːməʊ ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
an infection involving the lungs, especially one related to or caused by pneumonia
infection involving the lungs or pneumonia
medical

Examples

Prompt treatment of a pneumo infection is essential to avoid serious complications.
The patient was diagnosed with a pneumo infection after developing a persistent cough and fever.
Doctors warned that untreated pneumo infections can lead to respiratory failure in severe cases.

Synset Examples

Pneumo infections can be very serious if untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pneumonia - infection that inflames air sacs in the lungs
  • Synonym: lung infection - an infection in the lungs

Etymology

From Greek pneuma (breath, air) and infection (from Latin infectio, act of polluting).
