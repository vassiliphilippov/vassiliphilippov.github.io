cracker bonbon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækər bɑnˌbɑn / (AmE), / ˈkrækə ˈbɒnbɒn / (BrE)
a party favor made from a decorated paper tube that pops when pulled apart, usually containing a small gift or candy inside
party favor that pops to reveal a gift or candy

Examples

The table was decorated with colorful cracker bonbons for each guest.
When they pulled the cracker bonbon, a tiny toy and confetti spilled out.
Children look forward to finding surprises inside every cracker bonbon at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas cracker - a party favor resembling a paper tube that pops and reveals a small gift, used especially at Christmas
