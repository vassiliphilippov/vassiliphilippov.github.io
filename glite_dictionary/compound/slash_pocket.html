slash pocket

1. NOUN

/ slæʃ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / slæʃ ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a pocket in a garment, usually below the waist, with a vertical or diagonal opening cut into the fabric
pocket with a slanted or vertical opening

Examples

He slipped his hands into the slash pocket of his new jacket, admiring the sleek design.
Many dress pants feature a slash pocket on each side for easy access.
The tailor suggested adding a slash pocket to the coat for a more modern look.
