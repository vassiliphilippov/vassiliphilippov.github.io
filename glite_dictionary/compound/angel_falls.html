Angel Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl fɔlz / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl fɔːlz / (BrE)
the world's highest waterfall, located in Venezuela, with a total height of 979 meters (3,212 ft); also known as Salto Ángel
world's highest waterfall in Venezuela

Examples

Many tourists dream of visiting Angel Falls to witness its breathtaking beauty.
The water at Angel Falls drops uninterrupted for nearly a kilometer.
During the dry season, the flow of Angel Falls is sometimes reduced to a thin mist.

Etymology

Named after Jimmie Angel, an American aviator who was the first to fly over the falls in 1933.
