grease gun

1. NOUN

/ ɡris ɡʌn / (AmE), / ɡriːs ɡʌn / (BrE)
A hand-operated tool that resembles a pistol and is used to force grease into machine parts.
tool for lubricating machinery

Examples

The mechanic used a grease gun to lubricate the joints of the tractor.
Always make sure your grease gun is filled before starting a maintenance job.
He bought a new grease gun for his workshop last week.
