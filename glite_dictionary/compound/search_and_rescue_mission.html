search and rescue mission

1. NOUN

/ sɜrtʃ ənd rəˈskjuː ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ənd rɪˈskjuː ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an operation organized to locate and assist people who are lost or in distress, especially after disasters or accidents
operation to find and rescue people in danger

Examples

The team launched a search and rescue mission after receiving reports of missing hikers.
Several helicopters were deployed during the search and rescue mission following the earthquake.
Coordinating a search and rescue mission requires trained personnel and specialized equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rescue mission - an operation to save people from danger
