mouthy attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊði ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈmaʊði ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A talkative or outspoken demeanor, often perceived as disrespectful or bold.
talkative or disrespectful demeanor
informal

Examples

Her mouthy attitude got her into trouble at school more than once.
He was known for his mouthy attitude, always challenging his teachers in class.
The coach warned the players that a mouthy attitude would not be tolerated on the team.

Synset Examples

Her mouthy attitude got her into trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouthy - tending to talk a lot, often in a disrespectful way
  • Synonym: cheeky - showing a lack of respect in a playful or disrespectful way
  • Antonym: respectful attitude - a demeanor showing respect to others
