plains lemon monarda

1. NOUN

/ pleɪnz ˈlɛmən moʊˈnɑrdə / (AmE), / pleɪnz ˈlɛmən məˈnɑːdə / (BrE)
An annual herb native to the southern United States, known for its lemon-scented leaves and colorful flowers.
herb native to southern United States

Examples

The plains lemon monarda thrives in hot, sunny fields across parts of Texas and Oklahoma.
Gardeners value plains lemon monarda for its fragrant foliage and attractive flowers.
Several pollinators, including bees and butterflies, are drawn to the blooms of plains lemon monarda.
