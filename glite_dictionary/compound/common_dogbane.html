common dogbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈdɒɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with pinkish flowers in loose clusters, traditionally used in folk medicine to treat joint pain or inflammation.
a North American perennial plant with pinkish flowers

Examples

Local herbalists sometimes use common dogbane to make remedies for aching joints.
The pinkish flowers of common dogbane attract many butterflies in the summer.
After hiking through the woods, we identified a patch of common dogbane near the stream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogbane - any plant of the genus Apocynum, known for its toxic properties
