wind harp

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd hɑrp / (AmE), / wɪnd hɑːp / (BrE)
a harp-like instrument with strings tuned in unison that produce sound when wind passes over them
musical instrument sounded by wind

Examples

The wind harp on the hillside created haunting melodies as the breeze blew through it.
Visitors marveled at the ethereal sounds produced by the wind harp in the sculpture garden.
She installed a wind harp in her backyard to enjoy its soothing music on windy days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aeolian harp - a stringed instrument played by the wind
