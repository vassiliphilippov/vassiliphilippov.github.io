a hit among the kids

1. NOUN

/ ə hɪt əˈmʌŋ ðə kɪdz / (AmE), / ə hɪt əˈmʌŋ ðə kɪdz / (BrE)
very popular or widely liked by children
very popular with children

Examples

The new animated movie was a hit among the kids at the party.
Her magic tricks turned out to be a hit among the kids during the school fair.
The colorful playground quickly became a hit among the kids in the neighborhood.

Synset Examples

The Skellington figure was a hit among the kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit - something or someone extremely popular
  • Antonym: flop - something that is unsuccessful or unpopular
