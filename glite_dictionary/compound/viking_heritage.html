Viking heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪkɪŋ ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪkɪŋ ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the cultural and historical background associated with the Vikings, including their traditions, ancestry, and influence
cultural background of the Vikings

Examples

She takes great pride in her Viking heritage and often studies her ancestors' traditions.
The museum's exhibition highlights the Viking heritage of the region.
Many festivals in Scandinavia celebrate their rich Viking heritage.

Synset Examples

The Norseman was proud of his Viking heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Viking - a member of the Norse people from Scandinavia during the Viking Age
  • Synonym: Norse heritage - cultural heritage relating to Norse people and their traditions
