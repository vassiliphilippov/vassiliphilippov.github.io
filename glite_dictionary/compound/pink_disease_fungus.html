pink disease fungus

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk dɪˈziz ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / pɪŋk dɪˈziːz ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes pink disease in citrus, coffee, rubber trees, and other plants
fungus causing pink disease in crops

Examples

The spread of pink disease fungus has affected citrus orchards across the region.
Proper sanitation can help prevent pink disease fungus from infecting rubber trees.
Researchers are developing new ways to combat pink disease fungus in coffee plantations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink disease - plant disease characterized by pink lesions on bark
