said that shit

1. X

/ sɛd ðæt ʃɪt / (AmE), / sɛd ðæt ʃɪt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Used to express surprise, disbelief, or shock at something someone has just said.
express disbelief at someone's words (vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

I stared at him, stunned that he actually said that shit out loud.
Whenever she gets mad, she doesn't care if she said that shit in front of everyone.
They couldn't believe he just said that shit during the meeting.

Synset Examples

I can't believe he said that shit!
