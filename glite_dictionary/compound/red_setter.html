red setter

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈsɛtər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a breed of dog from Ireland with a chestnut-brown or mahogany-red coat, also known as the Irish Setter
a breed of Irish dog with a red coat

Examples

The red setter bounded across the field with its characteristic graceful stride.
Many families choose a red setter for its friendly temperament and beautiful coat.
She entered her champion red setter in the national dog show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Irish Setter - a breed of gundog with a distinctive red coat
