to needs a filling

1. VERB

/ niːdz ə ˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / niːdz ə ˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that a tooth requires a dental restorative procedure to repair a cavity or damage
requires a dental restoration

Examples

My dentist told me that one of my molars needs a filling after examining the x-rays.
If your tooth needs a filling, it is best to get it done as soon as possible.
After biting into something hard, she discovered her tooth needs a filling.

Synset Examples

The dentist said my tooth needs a filling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filling - a dental restorative material used for repairing teeth
