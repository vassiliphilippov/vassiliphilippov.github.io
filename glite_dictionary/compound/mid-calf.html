mid-calf

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈkæf / (AmE), / mɪd ˈkɑːf / (BrE)
the middle part of the calf, between the knee and ankle
middle of the calf (leg)

Examples

The skirt reaches mid-calf, making it perfect for both casual and formal occasions.
She wore socks that came up to her mid-calf during the winter months.
A mid-calf injury forced him to stop running for several weeks.
