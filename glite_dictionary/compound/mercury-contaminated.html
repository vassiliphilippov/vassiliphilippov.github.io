mercury-contaminated

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɜrkjʊri kənˈtæməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɜːkjʊri kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
containing or affected by mercury, especially as a harmful pollutant
containing or tainted by mercury

Examples

The researchers found dangerously high levels of mercury in mercury-contaminated fish from the river.
Residents were warned not to drink water from the mercury-contaminated well until it was cleaned.
Eating mercury-contaminated seafood poses a serious health risk, especially to pregnant women and children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contaminated - made impure or harmful by exposure to pollution, especially chemicals or toxins
