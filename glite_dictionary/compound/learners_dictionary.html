learner's dictionary

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɝːnɚz ˌdɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnəz ˌdɪkʃəˌnəri / (BrE)
A dictionary designed specifically for people learning a foreign language, often providing simplified definitions and additional information to aid comprehension.
dictionary for language learners

Examples

Many students prefer to use a learner's dictionary because the definitions are easier to understand.
A good learner's dictionary includes example sentences to show how words are used in context.
She bought an English learner's dictionary to help with her studies abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book containing words and their meanings
