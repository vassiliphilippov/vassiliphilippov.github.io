red onion

1. NOUN

/ ɹɛd ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ɹɛd ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
A variety of onion with reddish-purple skin and mild, slightly sweet flavor, often used raw in salads and as a garnish.
mild onion with reddish-purple skin

Examples

She topped the salad with thinly sliced red onion for extra flavor.
A juicy hamburger with lettuce, tomato, and red onion is my favorite lunch.
He prefers red onion over yellow onion in his sandwiches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion - A round vegetable with a strong taste and smell, made of several layers of white, yellow, or red skin.
