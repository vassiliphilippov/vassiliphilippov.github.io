drawing pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪ.ɪŋ pɪn / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ pɪn / (BrE)
A short pin with a sharp point and a flat head, used for fastening papers to a wall, board, or similar surface.
short pin for fastening papers

Examples

She used a drawing pin to attach the notice to the cork board.
Be careful not to step on a drawing pin left on the floor.
He pressed a map onto the wall with a single drawing pin in each corner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thumbtack - A similar pin with a flat head used in American English

Etymology

From 'drawing' (referring to boards used for drawing) and 'pin'.
