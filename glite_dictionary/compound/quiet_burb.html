quiet burb

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪət bɜrb / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət bɜːb / (BrE)
an informal term for a peaceful suburban neighborhood or town
peaceful suburb (informal)
informal

Examples

She moved to a quiet burb after retiring from her busy city job.
They enjoyed raising their children in a quiet burb where everyone knew each other.
The house is located in a quiet burb just twenty minutes from downtown.

Synset Examples

I grew up in a quiet burb outside the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suburb - a residential area outside a city

Etymology

From 'quiet' (peaceful) and 'burb,' an informal shortening of 'suburb.'
