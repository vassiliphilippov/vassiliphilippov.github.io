Danish blue

1. NOUN

/ ˌdeɪnɪʃ ˈbluː / (AmE), / ˌdeɪnɪʃ ˈbluː / (BrE)
a type of blue cheese originating from Denmark, characterized by a sharp, tangy flavor and bluish veins
a Danish blue-veined cheese

Examples

Many restaurants feature Danish blue on their cheese platters for its strong flavor.
She crumbled some Danish blue over the salad to add a tangy kick.
The recipe calls for Danish blue but can be made with any blue cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue cheese - cheese containing blue veins of mold
