ultraviolet lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt læmp / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈvaɪələt læmp / (BrE)
a lamp that emits ultraviolet radiation, often used in scientific, medical, or industrial applications
lamp emitting ultraviolet radiation

Examples

The laboratory is equipped with an ultraviolet lamp for sterilizing instruments.
Artists sometimes use an ultraviolet lamp to make fluorescent paints glow in the dark.
Exposure to an ultraviolet lamp should be limited to avoid skin damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black light - lamp that emits ultraviolet light, making certain materials glow
