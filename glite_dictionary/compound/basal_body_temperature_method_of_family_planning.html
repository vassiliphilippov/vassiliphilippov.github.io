basal body temperature method of family planning

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.səl ˈbɑː.di ˈtɛmp.ɚ.tʃɚ ˈmɛθ.əd əv ˈfæm.ə.li ˈplæn.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.səl ˈbɒd.i ˈtɛmp.ər.tʃər ˈmeθ.əd əv ˈfæm.ɪ.li ˈplæn.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a natural method of contraception in which a woman monitors her basal body temperature to identify the fertile period of her menstrual cycle
contraceptive method using basal body temperature
medical

Examples

Many women rely on the basal body temperature method of family planning as a natural way to prevent pregnancy.
The basal body temperature method of family planning requires daily temperature tracking to identify fertile days.
Some couples combine the basal body temperature method of family planning with other natural contraception techniques for greater accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhythm method - a natural family planning method based on menstrual cycle timing
  • Synonym: contraception - the deliberate use of methods to prevent pregnancy
