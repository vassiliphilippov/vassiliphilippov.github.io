graph paper

1. NOUN

/ ɡræf ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ɡrɑːf ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper printed with a regular grid of fine lines, used for drawing graphs, charts, or diagrams.
paper with printed grid for drawing graphs

Examples

He drew the chart neatly on graph paper for his math class.
The engineer used graph paper to sketch the blueprint of the bridge.
If you need to plot data points precisely, use graph paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grid paper - paper printed with square grids of varying size
