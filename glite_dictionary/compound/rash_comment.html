rash comment

1. NOUN

/ ræʃ ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ræʃ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a remark made hastily or impulsively, often without careful thought
hasty or impulsive remark

Examples

He made a rash comment during the interview, which cost him the job.
Her rash comment upset everyone at the dinner table.
Realizing her rash comment was inappropriate, she quickly apologized.

Synset Examples

She regretted making a rash comment in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impulsive comment - remark made without forethought
  • Antonym: thoughtful comment - remark made after careful consideration
