order arms

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdɚ ˈɑrmz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˈɑːmz / (BrE)
a command and a position in military drill in which the rifle is held vertically at the right side with the butt resting on the ground
military rifle position and command

Examples

After the ceremony, the soldiers were ordered to order arms and stand at ease.
The drill instructor shouted 'order arms!', and each recruit snapped to the correct position.
During inspection, you must stand at order arms until told otherwise.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: present arms - a military rifle salute and command
