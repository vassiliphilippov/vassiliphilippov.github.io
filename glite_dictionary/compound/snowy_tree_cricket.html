snowy tree cricket

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊ.i tri ˈkrɪk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ.i triː ˈkrɪk.ɪt / (BrE)
a pale yellowish tree cricket native to and widely found across North America, known for its soft, high-pitched song
a pale North American tree cricket

Examples

The snowy tree cricket is sometimes called the 'thermometer cricket' because its chirps can be used to estimate temperature.
At night, the gentle chirping of a snowy tree cricket can be heard in many North American gardens.
Scientists studied the life cycle of the snowy tree cricket to better understand seasonal changes in insect behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tree cricket - a cricket of the subfamily Oecanthinae that lives in trees and shrubs
