taco sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːkoʊ sɔs / (AmE), / ˈtækəʊ sɔːs / (BrE)
a spicy tomato-based sauce, typically used as a condiment for tacos
spicy tomato-based taco condiment

Examples

She added some taco sauce to her burrito for extra flavor.
We ran out of taco sauce during the dinner party.
Store-bought taco sauce is convenient, but homemade versions can be even tastier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salsa - a spicy tomato-based sauce often served with Mexican food
  • Degree Higher: hot sauce - a liquid condiment made from chili peppers
