short list

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt lɪst / (AmE), / ʃɔːt lɪst / (BrE)
a list of selected candidates chosen from a longer list, from which a final choice will be made
list of final candidates

Examples

After several interviews, only five people made it onto the short list for the job.
The selection committee reviewed the applications and drew up a short list of finalists.
She was thrilled to discover her name on the short list for the literary award.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long list - a preliminary list of all possible candidates before narrowing down
