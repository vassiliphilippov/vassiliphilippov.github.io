jill of all trades

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪl əv ɔl treɪdz / (AmE), / dʒɪl əv ɔːl treɪdz / (BrE)
a woman who is skilled or competent in many different kinds of work or activities
woman skilled in many areas

Examples

She's truly a jill of all trades, always ready to offer help with any task.
The team relied on their jill of all trades to solve unexpected problems during the event.
As a jill of all trades, Emily balanced roles in both marketing and technical support.

Synset Examples

As a jill of all trades, she can fix almost anything.
She’s the jill of all trades in our group, always handy.
Being a jill of all trades helps her in her startup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack of all trades - a person skilled in many different jobs or activities

Etymology

Modeled on the phrase 'jack of all trades', with 'jack' replaced by 'jill', a traditional generic name for a girl or woman.
