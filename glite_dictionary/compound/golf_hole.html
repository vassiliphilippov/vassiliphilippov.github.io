golf hole

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf hoʊl / (AmE), / ɡɒlf həʊl / (BrE)
A single playing unit on a golf course, from tee to green, completed from the first stroke to when the ball is holed.
one playing unit in golf (tee to green)

Examples

He managed to finish the golf hole in just three strokes.
Each golf hole on the course offers its own unique challenges.
After playing the last golf hole, the group headed to the clubhouse.

Synset Examples

he played 18 holes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hole - a small opening in the ground on a golf course
