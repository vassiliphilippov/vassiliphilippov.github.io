Republic of Sierra Leone

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsiːərə liˈoʊn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv sɪə.rə lɪˈəʊn / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, known officially as the Republic of Sierra Leone, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1961
country in West Africa

Examples

She spent two years working in the Republic of Sierra Leone with a humanitarian organization.
The Republic of Sierra Leone is rich in minerals, especially diamonds.
After 1961, the Republic of Sierra Leone celebrated its independence annually.
