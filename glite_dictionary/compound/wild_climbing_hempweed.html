wild climbing hempweed

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmpˌwid / (AmE), / waɪld ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmpˌwiːd / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant of tropical America, noted for climbing up trees and bearing vanilla-scented flowers.
a tropical American climbing herb with scented flowers

Examples

The vine covering the old fence was identified as wild climbing hempweed.
The wild climbing hempweed filled the garden with a subtle vanilla scent each summer.
In tropical forests, wild climbing hempweed often grows up the trunks of tall trees.
