cactus mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæktəs maʊs / (AmE), / ˈkæktəs maʊs / (BrE)
a small, burrowing rodent of desert regions in the southwestern United States, known for inhabiting areas with cacti
desert rodent of southwestern US

Examples

The cactus mouse is adapted to survive with very little water in harsh desert climates.
Researchers caught several cactus mice during their nocturnal study of the Arizona desert.
The fur color of the cactus mouse helps it blend in with the sandy surroundings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouse - a small rodent with a pointed snout and long tail
