almond cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːmənd ˈkʊki / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A rich, sweet cookie made with ground almonds, often in a crescent shape.
cookie made with ground almonds

Examples

My grandmother bakes the best almond cookies every Christmas.
Would you like a cup of tea and an almond cookie?
The bakery's famous almond cookies are shaped like tiny crescents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macaroon - a small round cookie made with ground almonds or coconut
