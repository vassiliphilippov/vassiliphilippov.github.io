stuffed egg

1. NOUN

/ stʌft ɛɡ / (AmE), / stʌft ɛɡ / (BrE)
A hard-boiled egg cut in half, with the yolk mixed with mayonnaise and seasonings, then placed back into the white.
hard-boiled egg with seasoned yolk filling

Examples

She arranged the stuffed eggs neatly on a plate for the appetizer.
I love adding a bit of paprika to my stuffed eggs for extra flavor.
Every holiday, my grandmother makes her famous stuffed eggs for the whole family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deviled egg - hard-boiled egg with seasoned, often spicy, yolk filling
