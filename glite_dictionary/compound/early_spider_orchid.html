early spider orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːli ˈspaɪdɚ ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈspaɪdə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A spring-blooming orchid (Ophrys sphegodes) with yellow, green, or pink sepals and a broad brown velvety lip.
a species of spring-flowering orchid

Examples

The early spider orchid can be found in chalk grasslands across southern England.
Botanists are excited by the reappearance of the rare early spider orchid in the region.
The early spider orchid blooms in spring, displaying its distinctive velvety lip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider orchid - orchids of the genus Ophrys or similar with spider-like flowers
