Northern Marianas

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðərn məˌriːˈænəz / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən məˌrɪˈænəz / (BrE)
a self-governing territory in the western Pacific Ocean comprising all of the Mariana Islands except Guam
self-governing Pacific territory; Mariana Islands except Guam

Examples

The Northern Marianas attract tourists with their beautiful beaches and historical sites.
She moved to the Northern Marianas to teach English at a local school.
The economy of the Northern Marianas depends heavily on tourism and agriculture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Guam - a U.S. island territory in Micronesia, excluded from the Northern Marianas
