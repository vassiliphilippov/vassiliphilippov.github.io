Dutch case-knife bean

1. NOUN

/ dʌtʃ keɪs naɪf bin / (AmE), / dʌtʃ keɪs nʌɪf biːn / (BrE)
A tropical American perennial bean plant with red flowers and mottled black beans, similar to the common bean, often used as food in Britain.
a perennial tropical bean plant and its edible beans

Examples

The farmer proudly displayed his harvest of Dutch case-knife beans at the local market.
In some British recipes, Dutch case-knife bean is preferred for its rich flavor and texture.
The Dutch case-knife bean thrives in tropical climates and produces beautiful red flowers.
