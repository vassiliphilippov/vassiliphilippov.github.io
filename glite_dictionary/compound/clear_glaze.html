clear glaze

1. NOUN

/ klɪr ɡleɪz / (AmE), / klɪə ɡleɪz / (BrE)
a transparent coating applied to wood or other surfaces to enhance appearance and protect the material
transparent finish or coating for wood and surfaces

Examples

After sanding the tabletop, she applied a clear glaze for a glossy finish.
This cabinet has been protected with a durable clear glaze that won't yellow over time.
Many artists prefer a clear glaze to preserve the wood's natural color.

Synset Examples

He used a clear glaze to enhance the wood's beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: varnish - a liquid protective finish for wood or other materials
