stripe blight

1. NOUN

/ straɪp blaɪt / (AmE), / straɪp blaɪt / (BrE)
A disease of oats characterized by the appearance of striped lesions on the leaves, caused by fungal infection.
disease of oats with striped lesions

Examples

The farmer lost a large portion of his oat crop to stripe blight last year.
Researchers are working on resistant varieties to combat stripe blight in oats.
Symptoms of stripe blight include yellow or brown striped lesions on the leaves.
