ridge tile

1. NOUN

/ rɪdʒ taɪl / (AmE), / rɪdʒ taɪl / (BrE)
a specially shaped, often decorative tile used to cover and protect the ridge or apex of a roof
roof tile for covering roof ridge

Examples

The old cottage had hand-crafted ridge tiles along the peak of its roof.
After the storm, several ridge tiles needed to be replaced to prevent leaks.
Traditional ridge tile designs often include floral or animal motifs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tile - a flat or curved piece used for covering roofs, floors, or walls
