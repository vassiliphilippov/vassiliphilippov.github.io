Yakut language

1. NOUN

/ jɑːˈkuːt ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / jɑːˈkuːt ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Yakut people of northeastern Siberia
Turkic language of the Yakut people

Examples

She is studying the Yakut language as part of her research on Siberian cultures.
The Yakut language has preserved many ancient Turkic features.
There are efforts to revitalize the Yakut language among younger generations in Russia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sakha - Another name for the Yakut people or their language

Etymology

From Russian 'Якутский язык' (Yakutskiy yazyk), named after the Yakut (Sakha) people.
