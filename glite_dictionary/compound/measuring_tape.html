measuring tape

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ teɪp / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒ(ə)rɪŋ teɪp / (BrE)
A flexible ruler used for measuring length or distance, typically made of cloth, plastic, or metal and marked with units of measurement.
flexible ruler for measuring length

Examples

She used a measuring tape to check the dimensions of the table before ordering it online.
The tailor always keeps a measuring tape around his neck at work.
If you don't have a ruler, a measuring tape can work just as well for measuring longer items.

Synset Examples

You can find your height using a measuring tape on the wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape measure - a flexible ruler used to measure length or distance
  • Synonym: ruler - a straight-edged tool used to measure lengths
