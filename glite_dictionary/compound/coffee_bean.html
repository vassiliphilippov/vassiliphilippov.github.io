coffee bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi ˌbiːn / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌbiːn / (BrE)
the seed of the coffee tree, typically roasted and ground to make coffee
seed of coffee tree; source of coffee

Examples

The aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans filled the entire café.
She buys only organic coffee beans for her espresso machine.
After harvesting, the coffee beans are dried and sorted before export.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a drink made from roasted coffee beans
