bum steer

1. NOUN

/ bʌm stɪr / (AmE), / bʌm stɪə / (BrE)
advice or information that is wrong or misleading
misleading or incorrect advice
informal american

Examples

He blamed his failure on the bum steer he received from a coworker.
I realized too late that I'd been given a bum steer about the schedule.
She tried to help, but her bum steer only made things worse.

Synset Examples

He gave me a bum steer on which route to take.
Don't listen to him; he often gives bum steers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad advice - advice that is not helpful and may cause problems
  • Antonym: sound advice - advice that is reliable and helpful
