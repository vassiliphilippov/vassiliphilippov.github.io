Kaplan Group

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæplən ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈkap.lən ɡruːp / (BrE)
a Turkish Islamist militant organization with alleged ties to al-Qaeda, known for operating in Germany and seeking to overthrow the Turkish government and establish an Islamic state modeled on Iran
Turkish Islamist militant group

Examples

The German authorities arrested several leaders of the Kaplan Group in a coordinated raid.
Reports suggest that the Kaplan Group planned attacks against Turkish state institutions.
The international community considers the Kaplan Group a significant security threat.
