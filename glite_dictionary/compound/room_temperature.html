room temperature

1. NOUN

/ ɹum ˈtɛmpɚˌtʃɝ / (AmE), / ruːm ˈtɛmp(ə)rətʃə / (BrE)
the typical temperature of an indoor living space, usually considered to be around 20–22°C (68–72°F)
typical indoor temperature

Examples

Let the butter soften to room temperature before using it in the recipe.
The wine is best served at room temperature, not chilled.
She left the ice cream out until it melted to room temperature.

2. NOUN

/ ɹum ˈtɛmpɚətʃɚ / (AmE), / ruːm ˈtɛmpərətʃə / (BrE)
the typical ambient temperature of an indoor space, considered comfortable for humans, usually around 20–22°C (68–72°F)
typical indoor temperature

Examples

Allow the wine to reach room temperature before serving it to your guests.
The experiment should be conducted at room temperature for accurate results.
She left the butter out so it could soften to room temperature.

Synset Examples

We accepted the room temperature challenge at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambient temperature - the temperature of the surrounding environment
