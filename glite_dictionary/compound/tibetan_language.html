Tibetan language

1. NOUN

/ tɪˈbɛtən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɪˈbɛtən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Sino-Tibetan language spoken in Tibet and some surrounding regions
language spoken in Tibet

Examples

She is fluent in Tibetan language and often acts as a translator for tourists.
The Tibetan language uses a unique script derived from ancient Indian alphabets.
Many religious texts in the Himalayas are written in the Tibetan language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tibetan - of or relating to Tibet, its people, or their language

Etymology

From 'Tibet', the region, + 'language'.
