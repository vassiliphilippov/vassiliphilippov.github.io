canary yellow

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛri ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / kəˈneəri ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a moderate yellow colour with a slight greenish tinge, resembling the plumage of a canary bird
a moderate greenish yellow colour

Examples

She wore a stunning dress in canary yellow to the summer party.
The walls of the kitchen were painted a cheerful canary yellow.
His new bicycle came in a bright canary yellow color, making it easy to spot.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - the colour between green and orange in the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: lemon yellow - a bright, pure yellow color resembling that of a lemon

Etymology

Named after the canary bird, whose plumage is this colour.
