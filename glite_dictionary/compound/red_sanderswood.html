red sanderswood

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈsændərzˌwʊd / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsandəzˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tree native to India and the East Indies, valued for its hard, fragrant timber used in cabinetry, and dark red heartwood used as a dyewood
tree yielding valuable red dyewood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from rare red sanderswood, giving it a deep, rich hue.
Exporting red sanderswood is strictly regulated because of its endangered status.
Artisans value red sanderswood not only for its beauty, but also for the natural dye it produces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red sandalwood - tree with similar wood, common synonym in botany
