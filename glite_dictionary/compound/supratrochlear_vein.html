supratrochlear vein

1. NOUN

/ suː.prəˌtroʊˈklɪr veɪn / (AmE), / suː.prəˌtrɒˈklɪə veɪn / (BrE)
a small vein that drains the front of the scalp and joins with the supraorbital vein to form the angular vein
a vein in the forehead draining scalp blood
medical

Examples

The supratrochlear vein is located just above the medial part of the eyebrow.
In surgery, the supratrochlear vein must be carefully preserved to avoid excessive bleeding.
The supratrochlear vein unites with the supraorbital vein to form the angular vein.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: angular vein - vein formed by the union of the supratrochlear and supraorbital veins
  • Synonym: supraorbital vein - vein that drains the forehead and scalp above the orbit

Etymology

From Latin 'supra-' (above) + 'trochlea' (pulley), referring to its position above the trochlea of the eye.
