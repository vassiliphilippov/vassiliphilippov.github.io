piano stool

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ stuːl / (AmE), / piˈanəʊ stuːl / (BrE)
A stool designed for piano players, typically adjustable in height.
adjustable seat for piano players

Examples

He sat on the piano stool and began to play a beautiful melody.
The piano stool can be adjusted to fit players of different heights.
She bought a new piano stool to match her grand piano.
