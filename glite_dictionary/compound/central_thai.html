Central Thai

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛntrəl ˈtaɪ / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəl ˈtaɪ / (BrE)
The standard variety of the Thai language spoken in central Thailand, especially in and around Bangkok.
standard Thai spoken in central Thailand

Examples

Linguists often study Central Thai to understand the language's influence on regional dialects.
The majority of television broadcasts in Thailand use Central Thai for their programs.
She grew up in Bangkok and speaks Central Thai fluently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thai - the official language of Thailand
