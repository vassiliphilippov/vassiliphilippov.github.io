cover charge

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚ tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an additional fee that a restaurant, club, or other venue charges per person, not including the cost of food or drink
fee charged by a venue in addition to food or drink

Examples

There's a cover charge of ten dollars to enter the club.
Some restaurants include a cover charge on your bill for live music.
We left when we found out there was a cover charge just to get in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - a fee paid for entrance to a place or event
