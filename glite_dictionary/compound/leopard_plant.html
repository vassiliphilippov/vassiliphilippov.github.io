leopard plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpərd plænt / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəd plɑːnt / (BrE)
any of several ornamental plants of temperate Eurasia, especially Farfugium japonicum, grown for their yellow flowers and large spotted leaves
ornamental Eurasian plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The leopard plant thrived in the shady corner of the garden, adding color with its bright yellow blossoms.
Gardeners often choose the leopard plant for its striking, glossy leaves and ease of care.
I bought a leopard plant at the nursery to liven up my backyard patio.
