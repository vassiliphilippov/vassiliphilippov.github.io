intermaxillary suture

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈmæksɪˌlɛri ˈsuːtʃər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmæksɪləri ˈsjuːtʃə / (BrE)
the line of junction between the two maxillae of the upper jawbone.
joint between upper jaw bones
medical

Examples

The surgeon carefully aligned the intermaxillary suture during the reconstruction.
The development of the intermaxillary suture is essential for proper facial growth.
A fracture extending across the intermaxillary suture can complicate dental procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maxilla - the upper jawbone in vertebrates

Etymology

From Latin 'inter-' (between) and 'maxilla' (jaw).
