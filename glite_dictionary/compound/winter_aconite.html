winter aconite

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntər ˈækəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˈækənʌɪt / (BrE)
a small perennial plant native to Europe, grown for its bright yellow flowers that bloom early in spring, often appearing before the snow has melted
early-blooming yellow-flowered European plant

Examples

The delicate winter aconite flowers brightened the garden long before any other blooms appeared.
Even while snow still covered the ground, winter aconite pushed through with its cheerful yellow blossoms.
Gardeners often plant winter aconite for its ability to herald the arrival of spring.
