first team

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst ˌtiːm / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌtiːm / (BrE)
the main team representing a college or university in a sport, typically consisting of the best players
main college or university sports team

Examples

She was thrilled to be selected for the first team in her freshman year.
Making the first team is a great achievement for any college athlete.
The coach announced the new first team lineup before the championship game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: varsity - main team representing a school, college, or university in sports
