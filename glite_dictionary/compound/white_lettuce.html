white lettuce

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈlɛt̬ɪs / (AmE), / waɪt ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant of northeastern North America with drooping clusters of yellowish-white flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Prenanthes
herbaceous plant with yellowish-white flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with white lettuce in bloom during late summer.
Many gardeners admire white lettuce for its delicate drooping flowers.
I learned to identify white lettuce on my hike through the northeastern woods.
