New Zealand daisybush

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈziːlənd ˈdeɪzi bʊʃ / (AmE), / njuː ˈziːlənd ˈdeɪzi bʊʃ / (BrE)
a bushy shrub native to New Zealand, cultivated for its fragrant white flower heads
shrub with fragrant white flowers, native to New Zealand

Examples

The New Zealand daisybush brightened up the garden with its clusters of fragrant white flowers.
Gardeners often choose the New Zealand daisybush for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
We planted a New Zealand daisybush by the fence to add year-round greenery and springtime color.
