brief overview

1. NOUN

/ bɹif ˈoʊvɚˌvju / (AmE), / briːf ˈəʊvəvjuː / (BrE)
a short and concise summary of the main points or features of something
concise summary of main points

Examples

The introduction provided a brief overview of the novel's themes and characters.
At the meeting, she gave a brief overview of the upcoming changes.
This report offers a brief overview of the company's financial performance for the year.

Synset Examples

She gave a brief overview of the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
