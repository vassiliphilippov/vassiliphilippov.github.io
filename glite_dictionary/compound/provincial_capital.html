provincial capital

1. NOUN

/ prəˈvɪnʃəl ˈkæpɪtl̩ / (AmE), / prəˈvɪnʃəl ˈkæpɪtl̩ / (BrE)
the city that serves as the administrative center of a province
capital city of a province

Examples

Ottawa is not only the national capital of Canada but also the provincial capital of Ontario.
The population of the provincial capital increased rapidly after the new university was built.
She moved from her village to the provincial capital in search of better job opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capital - the most important city or town of a country or region
