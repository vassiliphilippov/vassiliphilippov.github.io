hand pump

1. NOUN

/ hænd pʌmp / (AmE), / hænd pʌmp / (BrE)
A device for pumping liquids or gases that is operated manually by hand.
manual pump operated by hand

Examples

The villagers drew water from the well using a hand pump.
A hand pump is often used to inflate bicycle tires when no electric pump is available.
Maintenance of the hand pump is essential to ensure a steady water supply.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pump - device for moving fluids or gases
