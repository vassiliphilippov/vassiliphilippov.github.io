boost in confidence

1. NOUN

/ bust ɪn kənˈfɪdəns / (AmE), / buːst ɪn kənˈfɪd(ə)ns / (BrE)
a sudden or noticeable increase in self-assurance or belief in oneself
increase in self-assurance

Examples

Getting praise from her manager gave her a real boost in confidence.
After his first win, he felt a noticeable boost in confidence that helped him compete better.
The training program provided participants with a valuable boost in confidence.

Synset Examples

He noticed a boost in confidence from higher testosterone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confidence boost - an increase in confidence; often used interchangeably
