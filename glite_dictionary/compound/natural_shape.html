natural shape

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the form or outline of something as created by nature, not altered or created by humans
shape formed by nature, not man-made

Examples

The stone was left in its natural shape to preserve its authenticity.
She admired the natural shape of the coastline, untouched by development.
Artists often draw inspiration from the natural shape of leaves and flowers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: artificial shape - a shape formed or changed by human activity
