Republic of Cameroon

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæməˈruːn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæməˈruːn / (BrE)
a country in central Africa on the Atlantic coast, formerly under French and British control until independence in 1960
country in central Africa

Examples

The Republic of Cameroon gained independence from colonial rule in 1960.
Trade between the Republic of Cameroon and neighboring countries is steadily increasing.
French and English are both official languages in the Republic of Cameroon.
