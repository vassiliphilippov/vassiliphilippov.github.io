stone drill

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn drɪl / (AmE), / stəʊn drɪl / (BrE)
a tool or machine designed for drilling holes in rock or stone
tool for drilling into rock

Examples

The workers used a stone drill to bore into the solid granite.
Archaeologists discovered an ancient stone drill at the excavation site.
This modern stone drill can penetrate tough rock layers with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - a tool for making holes in hard materials
