medical bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl bɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl bɪl / (BrE)
a statement listing the charges for medical services provided to a patient
statement of charges for healthcare services

Examples

After her surgery, she received a medical bill that detailed all the procedures and charges.
Many people are concerned about how to pay their medical bills after an unexpected illness.
The insurance company covered part of my medical bill, but I still had to pay the rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invoice - a list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due
