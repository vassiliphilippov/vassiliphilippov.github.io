spur wheel

1. NOUN

/ spɝ wɪl / (AmE), / spɜː wɪl / (BrE)
a gear wheel with teeth that project radially and mesh with another gear in the same plane
a gear wheel with radially projecting teeth

Examples

The spur wheel transfers power efficiently between parallel shafts in the machine.
A broken tooth on the spur wheel caused the whole system to malfunction.
Many mechanical clocks rely on a spur wheel for precise movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gear wheel - a wheel with teeth around its edge, used to transfer motion in machinery
  • Antonym: bevel gear - a gear where the axes of the two shafts intersect
