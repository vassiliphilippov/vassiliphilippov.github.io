automatic rifle

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈraɪfl / (BrE)
A self-loading rifle capable of firing rounds automatically with each pull of the trigger.
self-loading, fully automatic firearm

Examples

The soldier carried an automatic rifle during the mission.
Most countries restrict civilian ownership of automatic rifles.
He showed me a vintage automatic rifle from World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assault rifle - a rapid-fire, magazine-fed automatic rifle designed for infantry use
  • Degree Lower: semi-automatic rifle - a self-loading rifle that fires one shot per trigger pull
  • Degree Lower: submachine gun - a lightweight automatic gun designed to be fired from the shoulder or hip
