South West Africa

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ wɛst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / saʊθ wɛst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
the former name for Namibia, a country in southwestern Africa, used before it gained independence from South Africa in 1990
former name for Namibia

Examples

During the 1970s, South West Africa was administered by South Africa.
The people of South West Africa campaigned for independence for many years.
Historical maps often show Namibia labeled as South West Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Namibia - current name of the country formerly known as South West Africa
