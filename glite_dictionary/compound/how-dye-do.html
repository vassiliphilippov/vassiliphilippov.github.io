how-d'ye-do

1. NOUN

/ haʊ di du / (AmE), / haʊ dɪ ˈduː / (BrE)
An awkward, problematic, or troublesome situation or event; a commotion or fuss.
awkward or problematic situation

Examples

When he arrived late without his notes, it was quite a how-d'ye-do for the whole team.
Losing the keys just before the meeting created a real how-d'ye-do.
The sudden power outage caused a how-d'ye-do in the office.

Synset Examples

that's a fine how-d'ye-do

Etymology

Variant of 'how do you do,' used ironically to denote a troublesome situation.
