Republic of Uganda

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv juˈɡændə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv juˈɡændə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in East Africa, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1962.
country in East Africa

Examples

The Republic of Uganda became an independent nation in 1962.
Tourists are drawn to the natural beauty of the Republic of Uganda.
The Republic of Uganda is known for its diverse wildlife and landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Uganda - East African country; short form of Republic of Uganda
