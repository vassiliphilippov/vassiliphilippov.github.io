Gulf of Guinea

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv ˈɡɪni / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv ˈɡɪni / (BrE)
a large gulf off the southwest coast of Africa, forming part of the Atlantic Ocean
gulf off southwest Africa

Examples

Many cargo ships travel through the Gulf of Guinea on their way to ports in West Africa.
Piracy has been a growing concern in the waters of the Gulf of Guinea.
Oil exploration is a significant industry in the Gulf of Guinea region.
