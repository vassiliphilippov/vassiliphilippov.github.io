hair-splittingly precise

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛɚˌsplɪtɪŋli prəˈsaɪs / (AmE), / ˈheəˌsplɪtɪŋli prɪˈsaɪs / (BrE)
excessively detailed or meticulous to an unnecessary or extreme degree
extremely meticulous or detailed

Examples

The accountant was hair-splittingly precise in preparing the financial statements, checking every number twice.
While his hair-splittingly precise instructions, although thorough, made the process much slower.
Her attention to detail is impressive—she is hair-splittingly precise in her work.

Synset Examples

He was hair-splittingly precise in his measurements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exact - completely correct and without any mistakes
  • Synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail
  • Antonym: careless - not taking or showing enough care and attention

Etymology

From the phrase 'hair-splitting', meaning to make overly fine distinctions, and 'precise'.
