Cape Ann

1. PROPN

/ keɪp æn / (AmE), / keɪp æn / (BrE)
A peninsula in northeastern Massachusetts, north of Boston, extending into the Atlantic Ocean.
peninsula north of Boston, Massachusetts

Examples

The fishing communities on Cape Ann have a rich maritime heritage.
We spent our summer vacation exploring the rugged coastline of Cape Ann.
Artists are often drawn to the dramatic light and scenery found on Cape Ann.
