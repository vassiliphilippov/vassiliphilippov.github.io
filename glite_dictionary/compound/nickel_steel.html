nickel steel

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkəl stiːl / (AmE), / ˈnɪkəl stiːl / (BrE)
an alloy steel containing nickel, used for its strength and resistance to corrosion
alloy steel with nickel

Examples

Many industrial machines are made from nickel steel for added durability.
The bridge was reinforced with nickel steel beams to withstand heavy loads.
He studied the properties of nickel steel in his metallurgy class.

Related Terms
