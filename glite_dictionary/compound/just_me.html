just me

1. X

/ dʒʌst mi / (AmE), / dʒʌst miː / (BrE)
used to indicate that a statement expresses only one's own opinion or preference, not necessarily shared by others
expressing a personal preference or opinion

Examples

I don't really like spicy food, but that's just me.
You might think the movie was boring, but that's just me—I have unusual taste.
Maybe I'm being too picky; it's just me, I guess.

Synset Examples

I prefer to stay in on weekends; that's just me.
Some people love crowds, but that's just me.
