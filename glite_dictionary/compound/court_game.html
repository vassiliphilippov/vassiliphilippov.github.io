court game

1. NOUN

/ kɔrt ɡeɪm / (AmE), / kɔːt ɡeɪm / (BrE)
an athletic game played on a specially marked court, such as basketball, tennis, or volleyball
athletic game played on a court

Examples

Basketball is a popular court game played in schools across the country.
He excels at every court game, especially tennis and squash.
The gym offers facilities for several court games, including badminton and volleyball.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: field game - athletic game played on a field, such as soccer or football
