blue-eyed Mary

1. NOUN

/ ˌbluː.aɪd ˈmɛri / (AmE), / ˌbluː.aɪd ˈmɛri / (BrE)
An eastern North American wildflower with whorls of blue-and-white flowers, of the genus Collinsia.
eastern North American wildflower

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with clusters of blue-eyed Mary in full bloom.
We identified the delicate blue-eyed Mary by its bright blue and white blossoms.
In early spring, hikers often admire the charming blue-eyed Mary along shady woodland trails.
