magical experience

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪkəl ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪkəl ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an event or occasion that feels enchanting, wonderful, or unforgettable, often evoking a sense of wonder or delight
enchanting and memorable event

Examples

The concert was a magical experience that I will cherish forever.
Seeing the Northern Lights for the first time was truly a magical experience.
She described her wedding day as a magical experience filled with joy and laughter.

Synset Examples

Disney World is known for its magical experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable experience - an event that is easy to remember because it is special
  • Antonym: ordinary experience - an event that is routine or unremarkable
