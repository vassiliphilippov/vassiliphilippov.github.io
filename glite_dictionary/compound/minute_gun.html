minute gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnət ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt ɡʌn / (BrE)
A gun fired at intervals of one minute, especially during a military funeral or as a signal of mourning.
gun fired at one-minute intervals, often in funerals

Examples

A single minute gun was fired throughout the ceremony to honor the fallen soldiers.
During the procession, the solemn sound of the minute gun echoed across the parade ground.
As the flag was lowered, a minute gun marked each passing minute of silence.
