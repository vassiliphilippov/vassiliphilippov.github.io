film editing

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈɛd.ɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈed.ɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of selecting and arranging scenes to create a finished film
arranging and joining scenes in filmmaking

Examples

She took a class in film editing to learn how to put scenes together more effectively.
Advances in technology have changed the art of film editing dramatically over the years.
Good film editing can transform an average movie into a powerful and engaging story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editing - the process of preparing material for final presentation
