jewelry maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːəlri ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəlri ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes jewelry by designing, crafting, or assembling pieces such as rings, necklaces, or bracelets
person who makes jewelry

Examples

The jewelry maker spent hours crafting a unique pair of earrings for her client.
Many aspiring artists dream of becoming a jewelry maker and opening their own studio.
Each jewelry maker at the fair showcased different techniques and styles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeweler - a person or company that makes or sells jewelry
