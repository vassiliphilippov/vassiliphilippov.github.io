nothing fancy

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈfænsi / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈfænsi / (BrE)
Simple or plain; not elaborate, luxurious, or special.
simple or plain; not elaborate

Examples

I’d like a sandwich—nothing fancy, just ham and cheese.
He threw a party, but it was nothing fancy, just close friends and snacks.
The new place is nothing fancy, but it's comfortable and clean.

Synset Examples

It’s just my usual coffee order, nothing fancy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - simple, not decorated or elaborate
  • Antonym: elaborate - involving many careful details or complicated design
