avid book-reader

1. NOUN

/ ˈævɪd ˈbʊkˌridɚ / (AmE), / ˈævɪd ˈbʊkˌriːdə / (BrE)
a person who reads a large number of books with great enthusiasm
someone who reads books eagerly

Examples

My father is an avid book-reader who spends hours in the library every weekend.
Being an avid book-reader, Mia often recommends her favorite novels to her friends.
As an avid book-reader, he always keeps a novel in his backpack.

Synset Examples

As a avid book-reader, she visits the library weekly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookworm - a person devoted to reading
