plaster machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstər məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstə məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used for applying plaster or rendering to walls, especially in construction.
machine for applying wall plaster

Examples

The construction crew used a plaster machine to finish the new building quickly.
Operating a plaster machine requires proper training and safety equipment.
Modern plaster machines can cover large wall surfaces in a short amount of time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rendering machine - a machine that applies render (plaster) to walls
