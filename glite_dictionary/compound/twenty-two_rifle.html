twenty-two rifle

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈtu ˈraɪfl̩ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈraɪfl̩ / (BrE)
A rifle that uses .22 caliber ammunition, typically used for target shooting and small game hunting.
.22-caliber rifle

Examples

He learned to shoot cans off the fence with his trusty twenty-two rifle.
The twenty-two rifle is popular among beginners for its low recoil and affordability.
She carried her twenty-two rifle during the annual rabbit hunting season.
