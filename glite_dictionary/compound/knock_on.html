knock on

1. NOUN

/ nɑk ɑn / (AmE), / nɒk ɒn / (BrE)
In rugby, an instance of illegally knocking the ball forward with the hand or arm while attempting to catch it.
rugby: foul of knocking ball forward

Examples

The referee called a knock on, ending their promising attack.
He was frustrated after committing a knock on just meters from the try line.
A knock on resulted in a scrum to the opposing team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forward pass - illegal forward throw of the ball (in rugby or American football)
