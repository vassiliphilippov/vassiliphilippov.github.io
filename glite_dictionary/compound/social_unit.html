social unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a group or organization considered as a discrete part of a larger society or community
group or organization within society

Examples

The family is often considered the basic social unit in most cultures.
Each department acts as a distinct social unit within the university.
After the merger, the company restructured its social units to improve efficiency.

Synset Examples

the coach said the offensive unit did a good job
after the battle the soldier had trouble rejoining his unit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: group - a number of people or things that are together or in the same place
  • Synonym: organization - an organized group of people with a particular purpose
