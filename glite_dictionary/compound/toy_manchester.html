toy Manchester

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ ˈmæntʃɪstər / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈmantʃɪstə / (BrE)
a breed of small Manchester terrier, recognized for its miniature size and lively temperament
miniature breed of Manchester terrier

Examples

A toy Manchester makes an excellent companion for people living in apartments.
The toy Manchester requires regular exercise despite its small size.
At the dog show, several toy Manchesters competed in the terrier group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Manchester terrier - a breed of terrier dog originating in Manchester, England, known for its black and tan coat
