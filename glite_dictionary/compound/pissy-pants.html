pissy-pants

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪsi ˌpænts / (AmE), / ˈpɪsi ˌpɑːnts / (BrE)
an informal, derogatory term for a person who is overly sensitive, whiny, or complains excessively
overly sensitive or whiny person (slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

Stop being such a pissy-pants and try to have some fun.
He turned into a total pissy-pants when things didn't go his way.
Don't let her comments bother you; she's just a pissy-pants today.

Synset Examples

Don't be a pissy-pants; it's just a little rain.
He called her a pissy-pants for complaining about the movie.
Stop being such a pissy-pants and join us outside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crybaby - a person who cries or complains easily
  • Synonym: whiner - a person who complains a lot
