direct mailer

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkt ˈmeɪlɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈrekt ˈmeɪlə / (BrE)
a person or company that uses direct mail to advertise or sell merchandise to potential customers
person/company selling by direct mail

Examples

The direct mailer sent thousands of catalogs to potential customers last month.
Starting as a direct mailer, she quickly built a large and loyal client base.
A successful direct mailer needs to understand both marketing and postal regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mail-order company - company selling goods by mail order
  • Antonym: telemarketer - person selling by telephone marketing
