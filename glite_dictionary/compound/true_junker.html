true junker

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈdʒʌŋkər / (AmE), / truː ˈdʒʌŋkə / (BrE)
a person who regularly collects or restores items considered junk, especially as a hobby
person who collects or restores junk

Examples

My neighbor is a true junker, always coming home with old bicycles and furniture to refurbish.
Being a true junker, she can't resist stopping at every flea market she passes.
As a true junker, he sees value where others just see trash.

Synset Examples

He's a true junker, always finding treasures in old cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scavenger - a person who searches for and collects discarded items
