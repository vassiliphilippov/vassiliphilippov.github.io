sleeper goby

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpər ˌɡoʊbi / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə ˌɡəʊbi / (BrE)
A tropical fish similar in appearance to a goby, known for resting quietly on the bottom in shallow water.
a tropical fish resembling a goby

Examples

The aquarium has a beautiful sleeper goby resting on the sandy substrate.
In shallow tropical waters, the sleeper goby spends most of its time motionless near rocks.
We spotted several sleeper gobies hidden among the aquatic plants during our dive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goby - a small, usually bottom-dwelling fish of the family Gobiidae
