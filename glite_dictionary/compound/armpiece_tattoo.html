armpiece tattoo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmˌpis təˈtu / (AmE), / ˈɑːmˌpiːs təˈtɑːtuː / (BrE)
a tattoo that is located on the arm, usually covering a significant portion of the upper or lower arm
tattoo on the arm

Examples

She proudly showed off her new armpiece tattoo at the party.
His armpiece tattoo featured a detailed dragon winding around his biceps.
Many artists specialize in large armpiece tattoos that extend from the shoulder to the elbow.

Synset Examples

His armpiece tattoo was a symbol of courage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tattoo - a permanent design made on the skin with ink
