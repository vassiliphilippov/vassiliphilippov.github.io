phonograph album

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊnəˌɡræf ˈælbəm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnəɡrɑːf ˈælbəm / (BrE)
a book-like case or album designed for storing phonograph (vinyl) records
case for storing phonograph records

Examples

My grandfather showed me his old phonograph album filled with classic jazz records.
Collectors often hunt for rare phonograph albums in antique shops and flea markets.
The phonograph album was beautifully bound and had sleeves for each record.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record album - a collection of audio recordings issued as a single item on vinyl
