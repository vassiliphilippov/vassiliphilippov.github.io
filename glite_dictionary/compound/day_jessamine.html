day jessamine

1. NOUN

/ deɪ ˈdʒɛs.ə.mɪn / (AmE), / deɪ ˈdʒɛs.ə.mɪn / (BrE)
A West Indian evergreen shrub (Cestrum diurnum) with clusters of funnel-shaped white flowers that are fragrant during the day.
West Indian shrub with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the sweet scent of day jessamine each morning.
She planted day jessamine along the walkway for its fragrant blooms.
Unlike its nocturnal relative, day jessamine releases its fragrance in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: night jessamine - West Indian shrub with flowers fragrant at night
