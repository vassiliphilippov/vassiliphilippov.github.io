signal worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnəl ˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəl ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a railroad employee responsible for operating and maintaining signals and points in a railway yard
railroad employee handling signals and points

Examples

The signal worker ensured that the tracks were set correctly for the incoming train.
After a long day in the yard, the signal worker checked the last signal before going home.
Due to fog, the signal worker had to be extra vigilant to prevent accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signalman - a person in charge of railway signals
