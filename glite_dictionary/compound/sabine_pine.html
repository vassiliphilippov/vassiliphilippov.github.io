sabine pine

1. NOUN

/ səˈbiːn paɪn / (AmE), / səˈbiːn paɪn / (BrE)
A medium-sized pine tree native to southwestern California, with five needles per cluster and long cylindrical cones.
pine tree native to southwestern California

Examples

The forest reserve is home to the rare sabine pine, which is found only in southwestern California.
Botanists studied the cones of the sabine pine to learn more about its reproductive cycle.
We spotted a group of sabine pines growing along the mountain trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine - any tree of the genus Pinus, coniferous, with needle-like leaves
