biological warfare defense

1. NOUN

/ baɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈwɔrˌfɛr dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / baɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈwɔːˌfeə dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
measures and strategies designed to protect against the use of biological agents as weapons in warfare.
protection against biological weapons

Examples

The military invested heavily in biological warfare defense programs during the 20th century.
Training for biological warfare defense includes learning to identify and handle dangerous biological agents.
Modern hospitals have protocols for biological warfare defense in case of a potential bioterrorism attack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological warfare - use of biological agents as weapons in conflict
