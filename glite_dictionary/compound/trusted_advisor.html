trusted advisor

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌstəd ədˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstɪd ədˈvʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person who is relied upon to give sound advice and guidance, typically over a period of time
reliable counselor or guide

Examples

She has been my trusted advisor through every major decision in my career.
The president surrounded himself with a team of trusted advisors.
As a trusted advisor, he always puts his clients' best interests first.

Synset Examples

The story featured a eunuch as a trusted advisor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counselor - a person trained to give guidance on personal or professional problems
  • Synonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
  • Synonym: advisor - a person who gives advice, especially professionally
