chocolate root

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət ruːt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ruːt / (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant of the north temperate zone, with pinnate leaves and nodding flowers with a brown-purple calyx and orange-pink petals
a perennial plant with nodding, chocolate-colored flowers

Examples

The chocolate root is often admired in gardens for its striking nodding flowers.
Botanists studied the chocolate root for its unique combination of orange and pink petals.
She photographed a patch of chocolate root blooming in the woodland preserve.
