shoulder strap

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər ˌstræp / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə ˌstræp / (BrE)
a band, usually made of fabric or leather, that passes over the shoulder to support a garment, bag, or equipment
band over shoulder supporting a garment or bag

Examples

She adjusted the shoulder strap on her handbag before heading out the door.
The dress has thin shoulder straps that give it an elegant look.
He replaced the broken shoulder strap on his camera bag.
