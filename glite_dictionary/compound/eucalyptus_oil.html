eucalyptus oil

1. NOUN

/ juːˈkælɪptəs ɔɪl / (AmE), / juːˈkælɪptəs ɔɪl / (BrE)
A fragrant essential oil obtained from the leaves of eucalyptus trees, used as a remedy or in products for medicinal and aromatic purposes.
essential oil from eucalyptus leaves

Examples

She added a few drops of eucalyptus oil to the humidifier to ease her congestion.
Many natural cleaning products contain eucalyptus oil for its fresh scent and antibacterial properties.
Rubbing eucalyptus oil on the chest is a common home remedy for colds.

Etymology

From 'eucalyptus', the genus name of the tree, plus 'oil'.
