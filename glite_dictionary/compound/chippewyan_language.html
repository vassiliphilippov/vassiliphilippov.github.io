Chippewyan language

1. NOUN

/ t͡ʃɪˈpiːwjan ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / t͡ʃɪˈpiːwjan ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Chipewyan people of northern Canada, belonging to the Athabaskan language family.
language of the Chipewyan people

Examples

Several elders still speak Chippewyan language fluently in the community.
The dictionary documents many words found in the Chippewyan language.
He is learning the Chippewyan language to connect with his heritage.

Etymology

From Chipewyan, the name of the indigenous people who speak the language.
