halcyon atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈhælsiˌɑn ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈhælsiən ˈatməˌsfɪə / (BrE)
A peaceful and tranquil environment, free from disturbance or stress.
peaceful, tranquil environment

Examples

The halcyon atmosphere of the garden was perfect for meditation and reflection.
Everyone admired the halcyon atmosphere in the cozy mountain cabin during winter.
After years of conflict, the island finally enjoyed a halcyon atmosphere that attracted many visitors.

Synset Examples

The halcyon atmosphere at the beach calmed her nerves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serene atmosphere - an atmosphere that is calm and peaceful
  • Antonym: turmoil - a state of great disturbance or confusion

Etymology

From Greek 'alkyōn' (a mythical bird associated with calm seas), via Latin 'halcyon', often used poetically to describe calm and peaceful conditions.
