crap game

1. NOUN

/ kræp ɡeɪm / (AmE), / kræp ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A session or instance of playing the dice game craps, typically in informal settings.
session of playing craps (dice game)

Examples

He's been running a crap game in the back room all weekend.
They raided the basement just as the crap game was getting started.
After losing all his money at the crap game, he swore off gambling for good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craps - A gambling game played with two dice
