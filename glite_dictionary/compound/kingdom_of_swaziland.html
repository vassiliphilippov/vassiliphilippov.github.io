Kingdom of Swaziland

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv swɑːziˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv swɑːzɪˌlænd / (BrE)
the former official name of Eswatini, a landlocked monarchy in southeastern Africa that gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1968 and was a member of the Commonwealth
former name of Eswatini, African country

Examples

The Kingdom of Swaziland celebrated its 50th year of independence in 2018.
During her travels in Africa, she visited the Kingdom of Swaziland and learned about its culture.
The Kingdom of Swaziland officially changed its name to Eswatini in 2018.

Related Terms
