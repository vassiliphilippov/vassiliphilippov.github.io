smoke alarm

1. NOUN

/ smoʊk əˈlɑrm / (AmE), / sməʊk əˈlɑːm / (BrE)
a device that detects smoke and sounds an alarm to warn of a possible fire
device that warns of smoke/fire

Examples

The kitchen smoke alarm went off when the toast burned.
He checked the batteries in every smoke alarm in the house.
It's important to install a smoke alarm on every floor for safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke detector - a device that senses smoke, typically as an indicator of fire
