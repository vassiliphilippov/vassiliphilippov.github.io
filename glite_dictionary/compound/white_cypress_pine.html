white cypress pine

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈsaɪprəs paɪn / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsɪprəs paɪn / (BrE)
A small tree or shrub (Callitris columellaris) native to southern Australia, known for its aromatic wood and scale-like leaves.
Australian tree or shrub (Callitris columellaris)

Examples

The fence posts were made from durable white cypress pine timber.
Many Australian homes use white cypress pine flooring for its pleasant scent and strength.
A row of white cypress pine trees lines the edge of the property.
