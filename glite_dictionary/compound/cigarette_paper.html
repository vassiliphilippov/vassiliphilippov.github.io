cigarette paper

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a thin, specially made tissue paper designed to burn evenly and be sufficiently porous to control the burning of tobacco in a cigarette
thin paper for rolling cigarettes

Examples

He carefully rolled the tobacco in a cigarette paper before sealing it.
The store sells several brands of cigarette paper for those who roll their own cigarettes.
She prefers using a thinner cigarette paper because it burns more smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rolling paper - paper used for rolling tobacco into cigarettes
