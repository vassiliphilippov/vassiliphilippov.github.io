arrival of autumn

1. NOUN

/ əˈraɪvəl əv ˈɑtəm / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl əv ˈɔːtəm / (BrE)
the time when the autumn season begins
the beginning of the autumn season

Examples

Many people celebrate the arrival of autumn by going for hikes in the colorful woods.
The temperature dropped noticeably with the arrival of autumn last week.
Pumpkin-flavored treats suddenly appeared everywhere with the arrival of autumn.

Synset Examples

The tawn color of the leaves signaled the arrival of autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autumn - the season between summer and winter
  • Synonym: arrival of spring - the beginning of the spring season
  • Antonym: end of autumn - the time when the autumn season finishes
