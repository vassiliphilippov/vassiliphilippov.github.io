Harley Street

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑrli strit / (AmE), / ˈhɑːli striːt / (BrE)
A street in central London renowned for its concentration of private medical specialists' and consultants' offices.
London street known for private medical practices
british

Examples

Many of the country's top surgeons have offices on Harley Street.
After months of illness, she finally made an appointment with a specialist on Harley Street.
Harley Street is synonymous with high-quality private healthcare in the UK.
