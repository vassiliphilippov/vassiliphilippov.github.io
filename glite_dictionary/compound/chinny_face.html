chinny face

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪni feɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪni feɪs / (BrE)
A face characterized by a prominent chin.
face with a prominent chin

Examples

Her chinny face stood out in photographs from her childhood.
The actor was cast because of his distinctive chinny face and sharp features.
People sometimes feel self-conscious about having a chinny face, but it can be quite striking.

Synset Examples

His chinny face made him look younger than he was.
