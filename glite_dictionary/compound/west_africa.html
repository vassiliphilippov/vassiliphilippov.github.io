West Africa

1. PROPN

/ wɛst ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / west ˈaf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
a region in the western part of Africa, lying between the Sahara Desert and the Gulf of Guinea
region of western Africa

Examples

Many countries in West Africa gained independence during the 1960s.
Trade across West Africa dates back to ancient times.
She conducted research on the languages spoken in West Africa.
