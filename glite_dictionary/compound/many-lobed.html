many-lobed

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛni ˌloʊbd / (AmE), / ˈmeni ˌləʊbd / (BrE)
having many lobes; divided into or characterized by a number of rounded or projecting parts or sections.
having many lobes

Examples

The maple leaf is easily recognized by its many-lobed shape.
This fungus has a many-lobed cap that distinguishes it from other species.
The liver is a many-lobed organ essential for detoxification.
