Republic of Madagascar

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmædəˈɡæskər / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmædəˈɡæskə / (BrE)
an island country in the Indian Ocean off the southeastern coast of Africa, officially known as the Republic of Madagascar; it gained independence from France in 1960.
country on the island of Madagascar

Examples

The Republic of Madagascar is known for its unique biodiversity and lush rainforests.
After gaining independence in 1960, the Republic of Madagascar established its own government.
Many tourists travel to the Republic of Madagascar to explore its beautiful landscapes and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Madagascar - the island nation in the Indian Ocean
