peppery kick

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpəri kɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəri kɪk / (BrE)
a strong, spicy flavor or sensation, especially one that is noticeable and stimulating in food or drink
strong, spicy flavor or sensation

Examples

The salsa had a peppery kick that lingered pleasantly on my tongue.
She added extra radishes to give the salad a peppery kick.
This cheese offers a mild flavor at first, followed by a sudden peppery kick.

Synset Examples

The soup had a peppery kick that surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiciness - the quality of being spicy, having a strong, pungent flavor
