two-step process

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː ˌstɛp ˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈtuː ˌstɛp ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a procedure or method that involves two distinct stages or steps
procedure with two stages

Examples

The new software follows a two-step process for installation and activation.
Applying for membership requires a two-step process: first, fill out the form, then submit payment.
The solution is achieved through a two-step process involving measurement and analysis.

Synset Examples

The instructions are simple: it's just a two-step process.
