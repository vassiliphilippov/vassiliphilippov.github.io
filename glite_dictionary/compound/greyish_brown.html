greyish brown

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
A color that is a light shade of brown with a noticeable grey tint.
a light brown-grey color

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle greyish brown that gave the room a warm feeling.
Her scarf was a lovely shade of greyish brown that matched her boots perfectly.
They chose a greyish brown fabric for the new sofa to suit their neutral decor.

Synset Examples

she wore dun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dun - a dull greyish-brown color
  • Degree Higher: taupe - a dark grayish-brown color
