fancy restaurant

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.si rɛs.təˌrɑnt / (AmE), / ˈfæn.si ˈrɛs.tər.ɒnt / (BrE)
An upscale dining establishment known for elegant atmosphere, high-quality food, and superior service.
upscale dining establishment

Examples

They went to a fancy restaurant to celebrate their anniversary.
The dress code at a fancy restaurant is usually quite strict.
It's not every day you get to eat in a fancy restaurant with a famous chef.
