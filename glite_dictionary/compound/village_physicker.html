village physicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪlɪdʒ ˈfɪzɪkər / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪdʒ ˈfɪzɪkə / (BrE)
a healer or doctor serving a rural community, often relying on traditional remedies and practical medical knowledge
rural healer or doctor
archaic

Examples

The village physicker was well known for her herbal remedies and caring nature.
Many villagers would consult the village physicker before seeing a city doctor.
The village physicker learned his craft from his grandmother, who treated generations in the same countryside.

Synset Examples

He sought guidance from the village physicker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healer - person who cures or treats illnesses, especially by non-traditional means
  • Synonym: doctor - person qualified to treat people who are ill
  • Synonym: physician - a qualified medical practitioner

Etymology

Physicker: from Middle English 'physic' (medicine) + '-er' (one who practices); 'village' denotes locality.
