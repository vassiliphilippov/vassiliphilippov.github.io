stream of news

1. NOUN

/ striːm əv nuːz / (AmE), / striːm əv njuːz / (BrE)
a continuous and steady flow of news or information, especially as delivered through media or the internet
continuous flow of news

Examples

During the election, I followed the stream of news on several online platforms.
She turned off her phone to escape the never-ending stream of news about the pandemic.
A relentless stream of news can sometimes make it hard to focus on important issues.

Synset Examples

Many users were misled by the fake stream of news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news feed - a service that provides a constant update of news items
