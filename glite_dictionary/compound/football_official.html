football official

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl əˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
a person responsible for enforcing the rules during a football game
enforcer of rules in football game

Examples

The football official blew the whistle to signal the end of the match.
Fans cheered as the football official made a fair decision on the field.
If a player argues with a football official, they might receive a penalty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: referee - the main official who supervises a sports game
