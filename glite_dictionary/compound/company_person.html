company person

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌm.pə.ni ˈpɝː.sən / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pə.ni ˈpɜː.sən / (BrE)
an employee who is more loyal to their company than to their coworkers
employee loyal to employer over coworkers

Examples

As a true company person, she always puts the interests of the organization before anything else.
His colleagues viewed him as a company person because he never questioned management's decisions.
It's sometimes difficult for a company person to relate to coworkers who disagree with corporate policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company man - an employee who is extremely loyal to their company
