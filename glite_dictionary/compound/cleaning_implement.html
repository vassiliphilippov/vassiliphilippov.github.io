cleaning implement

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ ˈɪmpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ ˈɪmplɪmənt / (BrE)
a tool or device used for cleaning surfaces, objects, or spaces
tool or device for cleaning

Examples

Please store each cleaning implement in its designated holder after use.
The company provided every new employee with a basic cleaning implement such as a broom or a mop.
Make sure to disinfect your cleaning implements regularly to prevent the spread of germs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaning tool - object used to clean surfaces
  • Antonym: dirtying implement - tool or device that causes something to become dirty
