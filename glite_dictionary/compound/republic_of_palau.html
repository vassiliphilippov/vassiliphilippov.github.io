Republic of Palau

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv pəˈlaʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv paˈlaʊ / (BrE)
an island country in the western Pacific Ocean, consisting of several islands and associated with the United States
Pacific island country, associated with the US

Examples

The Republic of Palau is known for its pristine coral reefs and abundant marine life.
After gaining independence, the Republic of Palau established diplomatic relations with several countries.
Tourists from around the world visit the Republic of Palau for its world-class diving spots.
