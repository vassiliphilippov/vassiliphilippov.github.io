Himalayan rhubarb

1. NOUN

/ hɪˌmæl.jən ˈruː.bɑːrb / (AmE), / hɪˌmæliˈɑːn ˈruː.bɑːb / (BrE)
a large perennial herb native to the Himalayas, known for its thick rhizomes and use in traditional medicine and dye production
herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas

Examples

The roots of Himalayan rhubarb are used to produce a natural yellow dye.
Traditional healers often include Himalayan rhubarb in herbal remedies for digestive issues.
We saw several clusters of Himalayan rhubarb growing along the mountain trails.
