weapons plutonium

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛpənz pluːˈtoʊniəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz plʊˈtjuːniəm / (BrE)
Plutonium, especially plutonium-239, that has been recovered from dismantled nuclear weapons and is suitable for use in nuclear weapons.
plutonium from dismantled nuclear weapons

Examples

The treaty requires all weapons plutonium to be securely stored and monitored.
Scientists developed new methods to dispose of surplus weapons plutonium safely.
International agreements address the problem of weapons plutonium stockpiles left over from the Cold War.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plutonium - a radioactive metallic element, especially isotope 239
