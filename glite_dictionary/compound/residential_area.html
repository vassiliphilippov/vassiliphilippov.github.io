residential area

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl ˈɛəriə / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl ˈeəriə / (BrE)
A part of a city or town where people live, consisting mainly of private houses or apartments rather than businesses or factories.
district with houses or apartments

Examples

The city council plans to build a new park in the residential area to serve local families.
Heavy trucks are prohibited from driving through the residential area at night.
She moved from a busy commercial district to a quiet residential area closer to her children's school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suburb - an outlying residential district of a city
  • Antonym: industrial area - a district with factories or businesses, not primarily residences
