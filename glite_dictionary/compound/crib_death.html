crib death

1. NOUN

/ krɪb dɛθ / (AmE), / krɪb dɛθ / (BrE)
the sudden and unexpected death of a seemingly healthy infant, typically during sleep; also known as sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS)
sudden unexplained infant death during sleep
medical american

Examples

The couple was devastated by the crib death of their newborn son.
Doctors are still unsure what causes crib death, despite extensive research.
Health experts recommend safe sleeping practices to reduce the risk of crib death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sudden infant death syndrome - the medical term for unexplained infant death during sleep
