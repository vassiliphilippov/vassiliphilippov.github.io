brass knocker

1. NOUN

/ bræs ˈnɑkɚ / (AmE), / brɑːs ˈnɒkə / (BrE)
a door knocker made of brass, typically used as a decorative and functional fitting on the front door of a house
door knocker made of brass

Examples

She reached out and lifted the brass knocker, rapping it firmly against the heavy wooden door.
The ornate brass knocker gleamed in the afternoon sun, welcoming guests to the historic manor.
He replaced the old, rusted fixture with a new brass knocker that suited the house's style.

Synset Examples

The old house had a beautiful brass knocker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door knocker - a fixture on a door used for knocking
