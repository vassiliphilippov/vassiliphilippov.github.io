oyster bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər bɑr / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə bɑː / (BrE)
A bar or restaurant that specializes in serving oysters prepared in various ways.
restaurant or bar specializing in oysters

Examples

We decided to try the new oyster bar downtown for dinner last night.
She ordered a dozen fresh oysters at the oyster bar on the pier.
Many famous oyster bars in New York have been open for over a century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seafood restaurant - a restaurant that specializes in seafood dishes
