place of choice

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs əv tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / pleɪs əv tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
a location that is especially preferred or favored for a particular activity or purpose
preferred location for an activity

Examples

This café has become the place of choice for local artists to meet and share ideas.
The park is the place of choice for morning joggers in the city.
For business meetings, that hotel lobby remains the place of choice for professionals.

Synset Examples

This restaurant is the place of choice for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite spot - a location that is liked best for a particular reason
