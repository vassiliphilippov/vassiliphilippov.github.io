fruit punch

1. NOUN

/ fruːt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / fruːt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A sweet beverage made from mixed fruit juices and often served at parties, sometimes with added alcohol.
sweet mixed fruit beverage

Examples

She made a large bowl of fruit punch for the party guests.
On a hot day, nothing is more refreshing than a glass of fruit punch.
The recipe calls for pineapple juice, orange juice, and soda water to make the fruit punch.
