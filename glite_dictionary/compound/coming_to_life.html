to coming to life

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌmɪŋ tə laɪf / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ tə laɪf / (BrE)
becoming real, vivid, or active, especially after a period of inactivity or as the realization of something imagined or planned
becoming real or vivid

Examples

The old town square is finally coming to life with music and activity.
After hours of planning, their project is really coming to life now.
As the sun rose, the city gradually began coming to life.

Synset Examples

She envisioned her dreams coming to life on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to come alive - to become lively, exciting, or interesting
