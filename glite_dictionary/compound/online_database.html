online database

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑn.laɪn ˈdeɪ.təˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒn.laɪn ˈdeɪ.təˌbeɪs / (BrE)
A database that is accessible by computers over a network, especially the Internet.
database accessible over a network

Examples

Researchers used an online database to gather the latest scientific articles for their project.
You must create an account before you can access the online database for student records.
The company maintains an online database of customer orders that is updated in real time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: database - an organized collection of data stored electronically
