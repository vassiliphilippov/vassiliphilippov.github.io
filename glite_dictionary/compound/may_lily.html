May lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant with two large lance-shaped leaves, a cluster of small fragrant bell-shaped flowers, and scarlet berries, native to temperate regions of Eurasia.
low perennial plant with bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The woods were carpeted with blooming May lily as spring arrived.
She identified the plant as May lily by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers.
After flowering, the May lily produces bright scarlet berries in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily of the valley - similar perennial plant with fragrant white bell-shaped flowers
