as much as possible

1. ADV

/ æz mʌtʃ æz ˈpɑsəbl̩ / (AmE), / az mʌtʃ az ˈpɒsəbl̩ / (BrE)
to the greatest extent that is feasible or allowed; as far as circumstances permit.
to the greatest feasible extent

Examples

She tried to rest as much as possible before her big exam.
The hospital asks patients to walk as much as possible after surgery.
I avoid processed foods as much as possible to stay healthy.

Synset Examples

she helped him as much as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as far as possible - to the greatest extent possible
  • Antonym: as little as possible - to the smallest extent that is feasible
