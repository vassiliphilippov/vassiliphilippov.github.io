wedding march

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a musical piece played during a wedding procession, especially as the bride enters or the newlyweds exit
music played at a wedding procession

Examples

The organist played the wedding march as the bride walked down the aisle.
They chose the traditional wedding march for their ceremony.
As soon as the wedding march began, everyone stood and turned to watch the couple.
