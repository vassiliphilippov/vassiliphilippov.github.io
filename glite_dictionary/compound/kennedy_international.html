Kennedy International

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɛnədi ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛnədi ˌɪntəˈnæʃnəl / (BrE)
a major airport located on Long Island, east of New York City, officially known as John F. Kennedy International Airport
major airport in New York City area

Examples

Our flight to Paris departs from Kennedy International early tomorrow morning.
She works for a company based near Kennedy International Airport.
After landing at Kennedy International, we took a taxi into Manhattan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: JFK - abbreviation for John F. Kennedy International Airport

Etymology

Named after John F. Kennedy, the 35th President of the United States.
