green thumb

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin θʌm / (AmE), / ɡriːn θʌm / (BrE)
A natural ability or talent for growing plants successfully.
talent for gardening

Examples

My grandmother definitely has a green thumb; every plant she touches seems to thrive.
People say you need a green thumb to keep houseplants alive through winter.
If you don't have a green thumb, start with easy-to-grow succulents.

Synset Examples

With her green thumb, the plants flourished in her care.
His green thumb helped him cultivate a thriving vegetable garden.
She has a green thumb and grows beautiful flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
  • Antonym: brown thumb - a lack of ability to make plants grow
