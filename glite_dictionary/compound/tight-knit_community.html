tight-knit community

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪt nɪt kəˌmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈtaɪt nɪt kəˌmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group of people who are closely connected and supportive of one another.
closely connected, supportive group

Examples

The small town prided itself on being a tight-knit community where neighbors always helped each other.
After the crisis, the tight-knit community came together to support those affected.
Growing up in a tight-knit community taught her the value of friendship and cooperation.

Synset Examples

Being an Ishmaelite in a tight-knit community can be tough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close-knit community - A community with strong, supportive relationships among members
