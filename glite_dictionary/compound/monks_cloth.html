monk's cloth

1. NOUN

/ mʌŋks klɑθ / (AmE), / mʌŋks klɒθ / (BrE)
a heavy cotton or linen fabric with a coarse basket weave, often used for embroidery or drapery
heavy fabric with basket weave

Examples

The drapes were made from monk's cloth to add texture to the room.
She enjoys cross-stitching on monk's cloth because of its easy-to-count weave.
A piece of monk's cloth was used in the traditional upholstery project.
