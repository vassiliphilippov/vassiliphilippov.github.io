moony expression

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːni ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈmuːni ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
A dreamy, absent-minded, or whimsical look on someone's face, often showing deep thought or distraction.
dreamy or whimsical facial look

Examples

She gazed out the window with a moony expression, lost in her daydreams.
His moony expression during the meeting suggested he was thinking about something far away.
The child wore a moony expression after hearing the fairy tale.

Synset Examples

He had a moony expression while gazing at the stars.
The puppy had a moony look as it chased its tail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreamy look - a facial expression showing dreaminess or absent-mindedness
  • Synonym: vacant expression - a facial look that seems empty or absent

Etymology

From 'moony' (dreamy or distracted) + 'expression' (facial look).
