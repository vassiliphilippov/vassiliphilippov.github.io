Clapp's Favourite

1. NOUN

/ klæps ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / klæps ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
an early-season variety of pear, known for ripening in mid-August
early-season pear variety

Examples

We harvested the first Clapp's Favourite pears before the end of August.
The Clapp's Favourite tree produces fruit with a sweet and aromatic flavor.
Many gardeners choose Clapp's Favourite for its early harvest and reliable crops.

Synset Examples

Your Clapp's Favourite pear tree is in flowering group 4.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bartlett pear - a popular variety of pear, also known as Williams pear

Etymology

Named after Thomas Clapp, a 19th-century fruit grower from Massachusetts who first cultivated this variety.
