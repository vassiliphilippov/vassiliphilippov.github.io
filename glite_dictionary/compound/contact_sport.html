contact sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnˌtækt spɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɒntækt spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport in which physical contact between opposing players is an integral and unavoidable part of play.
sport involving physical player contact

Examples

Rugby is considered a contact sport because players often tackle each other directly.
Parents sometimes hesitate to let their children play a contact sport due to the risk of injury.
Hockey, a contact sport, requires athletes to wear protective gear.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: non-contact sport - a sport in which players avoid physical contact
  • Degree Higher: collision sport - a sport involving frequent forceful physical collisions
