light arm

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ɑrm / (AmE), / laɪt ɑːm / (BrE)
a firearm such as a rifle or pistol that is designed to be carried and operated by an individual, rather than by a crew.
individual firearm (rifle or pistol)

Examples

Soldiers were issued light arms for personal protection during the mission.
International treaties regulate the trade of light arms and ammunition across borders.
The police confiscated several illegal light arms from the suspects' vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firearm - a portable gun, such as a rifle or pistol
  • Synonym: small arm - a light weapon designed for individual use, such as a rifle or pistol

Etymology

Formed from 'light' (not heavy) and 'arm' (weapon).
