tobacco pipe

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ paɪp / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ paɪp / (BrE)
a tube with a small bowl at one end, used for smoking tobacco
smoking device with bowl for tobacco

Examples

He carefully packed his tobacco pipe and lit it by the fireside.
Antique collectors often prize an ornate tobacco pipe over more modern designs.
He enjoyed a tobacco pipe every evening after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe - a tube with a bowl, used for smoking tobacco
