thread blight

1. NOUN

/ θrɛd blaɪt / (AmE), / θrɛd blaɪt / (BrE)
A disease affecting tropical woody plants such as cacao, tea, or citrus, characterized by the growth of fungal threads on branches and foliage.
fungal disease of tropical woody plants

Examples

Thread blight has severely impacted cocoa plantations in the region this season.
Farmers are advised to check for signs of thread blight on their tea crops regularly.
The citrus trees were treated to prevent the spread of thread blight throughout the orchard.
