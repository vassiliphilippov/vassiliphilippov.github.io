final exam

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪ.nəl ɪɡˈzæm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.nəl ɪɡˈzæm / (BrE)
an examination given at the end of an academic term to assess a student's learning in a course
end-of-term academic examination

Examples

I'm nervous about my final exam tomorrow because it counts for half my grade.
She stayed up all night studying for her biology final exam.
After finishing his final exam, he felt relieved and ready for summer break.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - an examination to assess knowledge or skills
  • Degree Lower: midterm exam - an examination administered in the middle of an academic term
