orchidean beauty

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈkɪdiən ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ɔːˈkɪdiən ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A type of beauty that resembles or evokes the delicate, exotic qualities of orchids.
beauty resembling orchids

Examples

The painting captured the orchidean beauty of the rare tropical flower.
Her dress possessed an orchidean beauty that mesmerized everyone at the gala.
Writers often compare her grace and elegance to orchidean beauty.

Synset Examples

Her garden was filled with orchidean beauty and diversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchidaceous - very showy or exotic, like an orchid

Etymology

From 'orchidean' (derived from 'orchid,' a family of flowering plants) and 'beauty.'
