as usual

1. ADV

/ æz ˈjuʒuəl / (AmE), / az ˈjuːʒuəl / (BrE)
In the same way as is normal or expected; in the manner that is typical for a situation or person.
in the usual or expected manner

Examples

As usual, he forgot to lock the door when he left.
She made coffee as usual before heading to work.
The meeting started late as usual.

Synset Examples

as usual, she arrived late

Related Terms

  • Synonym: normally - in a normal manner; usually
  • Antonym: unusually - in a way that is not usual or expected
