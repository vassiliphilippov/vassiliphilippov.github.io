felt hat

1. NOUN

/ fɛlt hæt / (AmE), / fɛlt hæt / (BrE)
a hat made of felt, typically with a creased crown.
hat made of felt with a creased crown

Examples

He wore a classic felt hat to shield himself from the sun.
Many jazz musicians in the 1940s preferred a stylish felt hat as part of their look.
She found a vintage felt hat at the antique shop downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fedora - a soft felt hat with a curled brim and creased crown
  • Antonym: panama hat - a light-colored hat traditionally made from Panama straw
