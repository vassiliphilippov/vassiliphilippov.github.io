point of no return

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv noʊ rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv nəʊ rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
the stage in a process or situation after which it is impossible to go back or change your decision
stage after which reversal is impossible

Examples

The negotiation reached a point of no return, and both sides refused to compromise.
Once the rocket launches, it passes the point of no return and cannot come back.
After signing the contract, we had crossed the point of no return and were fully committed.
