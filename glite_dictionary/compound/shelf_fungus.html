shelf fungus

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛlf ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ʃɛlf ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A woody fungus that grows in the form of shelf-like structures on tree trunks or wooden surfaces.
woody fungus with shelf-like growth on trees

Examples

The fallen tree was covered in shelf fungus, giving it an almost sculptural look.
Some types of shelf fungus can live for many years on a host tree.
We found a striking shelf fungus growing out of an old stump in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracket fungus - A similar woody fungus growing on trees resembling a shelf or bracket; often synonymous with shelf fungus
