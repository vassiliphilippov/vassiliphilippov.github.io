golf ball

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf bɔl / (AmE), / ɡɒlf bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, hard, dimpled ball used in the game of golf
small dimpled ball for golf

Examples

He teed up his golf ball and prepared to take the first swing.
The golf ball landed just inches from the hole after the chip shot.
She bought a box of new golf balls before the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf - a game in which players use clubs to hit balls into holes on a course
