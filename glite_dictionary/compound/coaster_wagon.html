coaster wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊstɚ ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstə ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
a small four-wheeled cart for children, often used for riding downhill or carrying things
child's four-wheeled toy cart

Examples

The children raced down the hill in their bright red coaster wagon.
He loaded the picnic supplies into the coaster wagon before heading to the park.
Their old wooden coaster wagon was passed down from generation to generation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wagon - a vehicle with four wheels, often used for carrying loads
