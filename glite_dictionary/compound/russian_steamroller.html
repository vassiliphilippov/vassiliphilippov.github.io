Russian steamroller

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌʃən ˈstiːmˌroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˈstiːmˌrəʊlə / (BrE)
an overwhelming and unstoppable force, especially one that crushes all resistance
overwhelming and unstoppable force

Examples

The underdog team was no match for the Russian steamroller of the defending champions.
Many feared the company would become a Russian steamroller in the industry, crushing all competitors.
Facing a Russian steamroller of public opinion, the government was forced to reverse its decision.

Synset Examples

She described the competition as a Russian steamroller.
The team was hit by a Russian steamroller of deadlines.
The project felt like a Russian steamroller coming at us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juggernaut - a huge, powerful, and overwhelming force or institution

Etymology

Refers to the literal Russian steam-powered road rollers and their metaphorical use to describe overwhelming force, especially in military or political contexts.
