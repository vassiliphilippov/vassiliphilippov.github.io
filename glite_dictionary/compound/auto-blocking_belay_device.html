auto-blocking belay device

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtoʊ ˈblɑːkɪŋ bəˈleɪ dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊ ˈblɒkɪŋ bəˈleɪ dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a mechanical climbing device that automatically locks and stops the rope when pulled strongly by the climber, used for belaying
self-locking device for rope belaying in climbing

Examples

Make sure the auto-blocking belay device is properly threaded before you start the climb.
Many climbers prefer an auto-blocking belay device for added safety on multi-pitch routes.
The instructor demonstrated how to use the auto-blocking belay device with a rope and a carabiner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belay device - a device used to control a rope for climbing
