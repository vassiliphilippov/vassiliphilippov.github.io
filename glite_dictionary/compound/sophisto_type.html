sophisto type

1. NOUN

/ səˈfɪstoʊ taɪp / (AmE), / səˈfɪstəʊ taɪp / (BrE)
a person who has sophisticated or refined tastes, especially in art, culture, or lifestyle
person with sophisticated tastes
informal

Examples

The new restaurant caters to the sophisto type who appreciates fine wine and modern art.
She always felt out of place among the sophisto types at gallery openings.
At the cocktail party, every other guest seemed to be some kind of sophisto type talking about avant-garde film.

Synset Examples

The party was full of sophisto types discussing art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sophisticate - a person with worldly experience and knowledge of fashion and culture
  • Antonym: philistine - a person who is indifferent or hostile to culture and the arts

Etymology

Formed from "sophisto" (a playful shortening of "sophisticate") and "type" (meaning kind or sort of person).
