basic bitch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsɪk bɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪk bɪtʃ / (BrE)
a slang term for a person, especially a woman, perceived as unoriginal, mainstream, or conformist in tastes and lifestyle
person seen as unoriginal or mainstream
slang offensive

Examples

Some people label anyone who enjoys popular trends a basic bitch.
She joked that her love for reality TV made her a total basic bitch.
He was tired of being called a basic bitch just because he liked trendy things.

Synset Examples

He called his friend a basic bitch for loving mainstream music.
She always orders pumpkin spice lattes, such a basic bitch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poser - a person who pretends to be something they are not to impress others
  • Antonym: hipster - a person who follows the latest trends and fashions, especially outside the cultural mainstream

Etymology

From 'basic' (slang for unoriginal) + 'bitch' (slang term for a woman, often derogatory); popularized in the 2010s.
