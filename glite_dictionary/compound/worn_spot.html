worn spot

1. NOUN

/ wɔrn spɑt / (AmE), / wɔːn spɒt / (BrE)
A place on a surface where the material has been eroded or abraded due to repeated use or friction.
area eroded by use or friction

Examples

The old armchair had a worn spot where the fabric had thinned from years of use.
She noticed a worn spot on the carpet by the doorway.
After years of walking the same path, a worn spot appeared on the wooden steps.
