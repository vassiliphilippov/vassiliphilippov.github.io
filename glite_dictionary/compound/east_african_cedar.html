east African cedar

1. NOUN

/ ist ˈæfrɪkən ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / iːst ˈæfrɪkən ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a tropical African timber tree (Juniperus procera) known for its fragrant wood, commonly used in construction and carpentry
African timber tree with fragrant wood

Examples

The wood of the east African cedar is highly valued for its durability and aroma.
Carpenters in Kenya frequently use east African cedar for building furniture and homes.
The fragrance of east African cedar can linger in closets and chests for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cedar - a coniferous tree with fragrant wood, often used to describe several different tree species
