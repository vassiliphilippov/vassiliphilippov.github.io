wedding license

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official document granting legal permission for two people to marry
legal permission for marriage

Examples

They applied for a wedding license at city hall last week.
Without a wedding license, the ceremony would not be legally recognized.
The couple proudly displayed their signed wedding license after the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage license - official document permitting two people to marry
