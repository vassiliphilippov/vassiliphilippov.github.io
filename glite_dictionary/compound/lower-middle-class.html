lower-middle-class

1. ADJ

/ ˌloʊ.ɚ ˈmɪd.əl klæs / (AmE), / ˌləʊ.ə ˈmɪd.əl klɑːs / (BrE)
belonging to the lower part of the middle socioeconomic class; having moderate but not high income or status within a society
of the lower part of the middle class

Examples

She grew up in a lower-middle-class neighborhood on the outskirts of the city.
His lower-middle-class background influenced his values and career choices.
Many lower-middle-class families struggle to afford private health insurance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: middle-class - belonging to the social group between the upper and working classes
  • Degree Lower: working-class - belonging to the social group consisting mainly of people in manual or industrial work
  • Degree Higher: upper-middle-class - belonging to the upper part of the middle socioeconomic group
