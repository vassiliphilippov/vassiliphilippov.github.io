television camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
a camera used to capture images for television broadcasting, which converts visual images into electronic signals
camera for capturing images for television

Examples

The director signaled for the television camera to zoom in on the presenter.
A large television camera was positioned at the corner of the studio to capture wide-angle shots.
Engineers tested the new television camera before the live broadcast began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: video camera - camera that records moving images as electronic signals
