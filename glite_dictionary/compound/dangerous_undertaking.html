dangerous undertaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪndʒɚəs ˌʌndərˈteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒərəs ˌʌndəˈteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
An action, project, or task that involves significant risk or danger, often exciting or unlawful.
action or task involving significant risk

Examples

Climbing Mount Everest is considered a dangerous undertaking that only a few attempt.
He knew that smuggling rare animals was a dangerous undertaking, but the profit tempted him.
Attempting to escape from the prison turned out to be a dangerous undertaking with grave consequences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure - an unusual, exciting, and possibly hazardous undertaking
  • Synonym: risk - the possibility of loss or injury; a situation involving exposure to danger
