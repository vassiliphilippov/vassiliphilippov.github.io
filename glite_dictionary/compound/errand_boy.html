errand boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrənd bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛrənd bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man employed to carry messages or run errands, often for payment or as part of a menial job
boy employed to run errands

Examples

When he was fourteen, he worked as an errand boy for a local grocery store.
The company hired an errand boy to deliver packages across town.
In the early 20th century, it was common to see an errand boy running down the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - a person who carries messages
  • Synonym: errand girl - a girl who runs errands for payment
