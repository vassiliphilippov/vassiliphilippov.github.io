Yankee football

1. NOUN

/ ˈjæŋki ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / ˈjaŋki ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
American football, especially as played in the northeastern United States.
American football in the northeastern U.S.

Yankee football is known for its passionate fans.
