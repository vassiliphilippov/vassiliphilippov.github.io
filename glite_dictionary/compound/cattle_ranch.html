cattle ranch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætl ræntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkætəl rɑːn(t)ʃ / (BrE)
A large farm where cattle are raised, often including extensive land and necessary facilities.
large farm for raising cattle

Examples

Jake grew up working on his family's cattle ranch in Montana.
The cattle ranch stretched for miles across the open plains.
Many cattle ranches offer tours to visitors interested in ranching life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranch - a large farm for raising animals, especially cattle, or growing crops
