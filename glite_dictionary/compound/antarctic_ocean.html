Antarctic Ocean

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtɑːrktɪk ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ænˈtɑːktɪk ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the ocean surrounding Antarctica, also known as the Southern Ocean
ocean around Antarctica

Examples

Scientists study the Antarctic Ocean to better understand its role in regulating Earth's climate.
The Antarctic Ocean is home to a unique variety of marine life adapted to its cold waters.
Melting ice sheets are changing the salinity levels in the Antarctic Ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Southern Ocean - the ocean surrounding Antarctica
