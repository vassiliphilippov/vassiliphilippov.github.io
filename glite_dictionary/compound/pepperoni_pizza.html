pepperoni pizza

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛpəˈroʊni ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / ˌpɛpəˈrəʊni ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
A pizza topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and slices of pepperoni sausage.
pizza with pepperoni topping

Examples

We ordered a large pepperoni pizza for dinner last night.
The smell of freshly baked pepperoni pizza filled the kitchen.
She prefers extra cheese on her pepperoni pizza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pizza - an Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese, typically with added meat or vegetables

Etymology

Pepperoni from Italian 'peperone' (bell pepper); pizza from Italian 'pizza' (pie).
