Gidar language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡiːdɑr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡiːdɑː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, particularly in northern Cameroon, including areas such as the North Region and the Far North Region.
Chadic language of northern Cameroon

Examples

She is studying the Gidar language as part of her research on African linguistics.
The Gidar language is still spoken in several villages near Lake Chad.
Linguists have documented traditional stories in the Gidar language.
