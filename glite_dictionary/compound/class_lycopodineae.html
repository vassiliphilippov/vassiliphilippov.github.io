class Lycopodineae

1. NOUN

/ klæs ˌlaɪkəˌpɒdəˈniː.i / (AmE), / klɑːs ˌlɪkəpɒdɪˈniː.iː / (BrE)
an alternative scientific term for the class Lycopsida, a group of vascular plants including clubmosses and their relatives
alternative term for clubmoss plant class

Examples

The class Lycopodineae includes clubmosses and related plants found in many temperate forests.
Botanists sometimes use class Lycopodineae as an alternative to Lycopsida in scientific literature.
Fossils from the class Lycopodineae date back to the Devonian period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lycopsida - class of clubmosses and their relatives

Etymology

From New Latin 'Lycopodineae', based on genus 'Lycopodium' (clubmoss).
