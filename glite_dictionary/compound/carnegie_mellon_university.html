Carnegie Mellon University

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑrnəɡi məˈlən juːnɪˌvɝsəti / (AmE), / ˌkɑːnɪɡi məˈlɛn juːnɪˌvɜːsəti / (BrE)
A private research university in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, known for its science, engineering, and technology programs.
private research university in Pittsburgh, US

Examples

She graduated with honors from Carnegie Mellon University in computer science.
The robotics lab at Carnegie Mellon University is internationally renowned for its research.
Many leading tech companies recruit heavily from Carnegie Mellon University graduates.

Etymology

Named after founders Andrew Carnegie and the Mellon family.
