Right Arm of the Free World

1. NOUN

/ raɪt ɑrm əv ðə fri wɝld / (AmE), / raɪt ɑːm əv ðə friː wɜːld / (BrE)
A metaphorical term for a nation, organization, or entity regarded as the main supporter or defender of democratic values and freedom in the world, frequently used to refer to the United States.
chief supporter of democracy and freedom

Examples

Many consider the U.S. the Right Arm of the Free World in efforts to promote democracy.
The prime minister proudly referred to his country as the Right Arm of the Free World during the peace talks.
Activists often debate which nation truly acts as the Right Arm of the Free World today.

Synset Examples

Many consider the U.S. the Right Arm of the Free World.
Activists argued that democracy is the true Right Arm of the Free World.
He proudly called his country the Right Arm of the Free World.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader of the free world - a nation or person regarded as leading the group of nations supporting democracy
