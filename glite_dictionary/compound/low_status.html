low status

1. NOUN

/ loʊ ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
a position of inferior rank, social standing, or importance compared to others
inferior social or professional position

Examples

Children from families with low status often face additional challenges in education.
She felt that her low status at work made it hard to express her opinions.
Many people struggle to overcome the disadvantages of low status in society.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high status - a position of superior rank, social standing, or importance
  • Synonym: inferior - of lower rank, quality, or importance
  • Synonym: subordinate - lower in rank or position
