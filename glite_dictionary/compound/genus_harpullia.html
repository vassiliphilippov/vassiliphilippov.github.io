genus Harpullia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs hɑːrˈpuːliə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs hɑːˈpʊliə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees native to Asia and Africa, belonging to the family Sapindaceae.
genus of tropical Asian and African trees

Examples

The genus Harpullia includes several species valued for their ornamental foliage.
Botanists discovered a new species within the genus Harpullia in Southeast Asia.
Members of the genus Harpullia are known for their distinctive red and black seeds.

Etymology

From modern Latin genus name Harpullia, named after J. Harpull, a German botanist.
