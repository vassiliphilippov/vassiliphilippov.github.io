blade apple

1. NOUN

/ bleɪd ˈæpəl / (AmE), / bleɪd ˈap(ə)l / (BrE)
The small yellow to orange fruit of the Barbados gooseberry cactus, used in desserts, preserves, and jellies.
fruit of the Barbados gooseberry cactus

Examples

The chef used fresh blade apples to make a tart and flavorful jam.
During our trip to the Caribbean, we tasted a dessert made with blade apple.
The local market had stalls selling preserves made from blade apple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Barbados gooseberry - a cactus species bearing edible fruit, also called blade apple
