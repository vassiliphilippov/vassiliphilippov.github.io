spin the platter

1. NOUN

/ spɪn ðə ˈplætɚ / (AmE), / spɪn ðə ˈplætə / (BrE)
a children's game in which a round object, such as a plate, is spun on its edge and a player's name is called; the player must catch the spinning object before it falls or must accept a penalty
children's game with a spinning plate

Examples

The children decided to spin the platter on the kitchen floor during the party.
If you fail to catch it while playing spin the platter, you'll have to do a silly dance as forfeit.
We spent the afternoon playing spin the platter in the garden.
