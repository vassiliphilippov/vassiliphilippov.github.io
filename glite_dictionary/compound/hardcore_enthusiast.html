hardcore enthusiast

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdˌkɔr ɪnˈθuziˌæst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkɔː ɪnˈθjuːziæst / (BrE)
A person who is extremely dedicated and passionate about a particular activity, subject, or hobby.
extremely dedicated fan or devotee

Examples

The band attracted a group of hardcore enthusiasts who followed them to every show.
That level of detail will likely only appeal to hardcore enthusiasts of the genre.
Online forums are full of hardcore enthusiasts sharing their latest projects and discoveries.

Synset Examples

The documentary was aimed at hardcore enthusiasts.
