tossed salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑst ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˈtɒst ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A salad made by mixing various ingredients, such as vegetables, with a dressing before serving.
salad mixed with dressing

Examples

She ordered a tossed salad with her sandwich for lunch.
The restaurant served a fresh tossed salad as a starter.
He prefers his tossed salad with vinaigrette dressing.
