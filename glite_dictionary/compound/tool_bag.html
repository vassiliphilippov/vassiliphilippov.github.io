tool bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːl ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtuːl ˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a strong bag used for carrying tools, typically with compartments or pockets for organization
bag for carrying tools

Examples

The carpenter stored all his hammers and screwdrivers in his tool bag.
Before starting the repairs, she checked that her tool bag was fully stocked.
He slung his tool bag over his shoulder and walked to the construction site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toolbox - a box for keeping and carrying tools
