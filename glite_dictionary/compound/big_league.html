big league

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ liɡ / (AmE), / bɪɡ liːɡ / (BrE)
the highest level of competition in a sport, especially baseball; the major league
the highest level sports league

Examples

He finally made it to the big league after years in the minor leagues.
Competing in the big league requires intense dedication and skill.
Many athletes dream of playing in the big league someday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: major league - the principal league in professional sports
