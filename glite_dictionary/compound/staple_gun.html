staple gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪpəl ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsteɪpəl ɡʌn / (BrE)
A hand-held tool for driving heavy-duty staples into wood, plastic, or other materials.
hand tool for driving staples

Examples

I borrowed a staple gun to attach the fabric to the wooden frame.
The carpet installer used a staple gun for quick and secure fastening.
Always wear safety goggles when operating a staple gun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stapler - a device for fastening papers together with a metal staple
