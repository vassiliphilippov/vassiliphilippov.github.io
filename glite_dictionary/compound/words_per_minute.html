words per minute

1. NOUN

/ wɝdz pɚ ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / wɜːdz pə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
a unit for measuring the speed at which words are spoken, read, or typed, usually expressed as the number of words completed in one minute
measure of speed for speaking, reading, or typing

Examples

The test measured her typing speed at 75 words per minute.
To be a professional transcriptionist, you need to type at least 60 words per minute.
His reading improved, and now he can read more than 200 words per minute.
