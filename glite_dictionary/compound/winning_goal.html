winning goal

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnɪŋ ɡoʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪŋ ɡəʊl / (BrE)
the goal that secures victory for a team or player in a match or game
goal that ensures victory in a game

Examples

His winning goal sent fans into a frenzy at the stadium.
That winning goal will be remembered as the highlight of her career.
