muscle woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəl ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is hired or acts as a thug or bodyguard, often using physical strength or intimidation.
female thug or bodyguard

Examples

The gang leader was rarely seen without his trusted muscle woman by his side.
As a notorious muscle woman, she quickly gained a reputation for her fierce loyalty and strength.
Rumors spread that the new club owner had hired a muscle woman to keep unwanted guests out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscle man - a man who acts as a thug or bodyguard, noted for his strength
  • Synonym: bodyguard - a person hired to protect someone from harm or danger
