evening bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ bæg / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
a small handbag used by women for formal evening occasions, typically elegant in design
small formal handbag for evenings

Examples

She carried a sparkling evening bag to match her gown at the gala.
A velvet evening bag is the perfect accessory for a black-tie event.
She transferred her essentials from a tote to a evening bag before leaving for the theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clutch bag - a small, strapless handbag carried in the hand
