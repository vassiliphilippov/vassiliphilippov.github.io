ex-mayor

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksˈmeɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌɛksˈmeɪə / (BrE)
a former mayor
former mayor

Examples

After the election, the ex-mayor continued to be active in community projects.
The interview was conducted by the city's ex-mayor, who now runs a charity.
Many people still refer to her as ex-mayor Johnson, even though she left office years ago.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mayor - the elected head of a city, town, or municipality

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'ex-' (meaning 'former') to 'mayor'.
