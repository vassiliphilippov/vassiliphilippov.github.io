African violet

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈvaɪ.ə.lət / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈvaɪ.əl.ət / (BrE)
a small tropical African plant with soft, hairy leaves and clusters of violet, white, or pink flowers, commonly grown indoors as a houseplant
tropical houseplant with violet, pink, or white flowers

Examples

She keeps an African violet on her kitchen windowsill for a splash of color.
The African violet must be watered carefully to avoid leaf spots.
He gave his mother a blooming African violet as a gift.
