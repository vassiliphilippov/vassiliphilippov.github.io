Indigirka River

1. PROPN

/ ɪnˌdɪɡɪr.kə ˈrɪv.ər / (AmE), / ɪnˌdɪɡɪk.rə ˈrɪv.ər / (BrE)
A river in far eastern Siberia that flows northward to the Arctic Ocean.
river in eastern Siberia, Russia

Examples

The Indigirka River often freezes over in winter, creating dramatic icy landscapes.
Scientists conducted environmental research along the banks of the Indigirka River.
The Indigirka River is an important waterway for the indigenous communities in eastern Siberia.

Etymology

From Russian Индиги́рка (Indigírka), of local origin.
