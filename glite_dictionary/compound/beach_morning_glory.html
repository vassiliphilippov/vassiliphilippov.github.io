beach morning glory

1. NOUN

/ bitʃ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɒri / (BrE)
a prostrate perennial plant with trailing stems, found in coastal sand dunes from Florida to Texas, known for its large, showy flowers
coastal trailing plant with showy flowers

Examples

The beach morning glory spreads across the sand dunes, providing ground cover with its vines.
We spotted a beach morning glory in bloom during our walk along the Texas coast.
Conservationists are working to protect the beach morning glory and other coastal plants.
