cloud seeder

1. NOUN

/ klaʊd ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / klaʊd ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a person who carries out cloud seeding to induce or increase precipitation
person who seeds clouds

Examples

The cloud seeder prepared the silver iodide rockets for the upcoming experiment.
As a cloud seeder, she often monitored weather patterns to decide when to conduct operations.
Many farmers relied on the cloud seeder to help bring much-needed rain to their crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloud seeding - the process of dispersing substances into the air to encourage cloud precipitation
