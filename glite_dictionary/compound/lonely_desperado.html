lonely desperado

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊnli ˌdɛspəˈrɑdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊnli ˌdɛspəˈrɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a solitary person, often seeking adventure or love, and typically portrayed as isolated or emotionally distant
solitary adventurer or seeker of love

Examples

The novel's protagonist is a lonely desperado wandering the open plains in search of meaning.
In the café, he looked like a lonely desperado, nursing his drink and watching the door.
Critics called the film a classic tale of the lonely desperado who can never escape his past.

Synset Examples

The song tells the tale of a lonely desperado.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outlaw - a person who has broken the law and is running from authorities
  • Synonym: adventurer - a person who seeks exciting and risky experiences

Etymology

From 'lonely' (solitary) and Spanish 'desperado' (reckless person, literally 'desperate').
