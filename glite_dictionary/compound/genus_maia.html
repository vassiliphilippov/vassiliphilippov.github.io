genus Maia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪ.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Majidae, comprising a group of crabs found in nearly all parts of the world.
type genus of the Majidae crabs

Examples

The genus Maia includes several species of spider crabs distributed worldwide.
Marine biologists have studied the genus Maia to understand crab evolution.
Specimens from the genus Maia are often featured in zoological collections.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Majidae - family of crabs to which genus Maia belongs

Etymology

From Latin 'Maia', a Roman goddess; used as a genus name in scientific classification.
