culinary art

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɪˌnɛri ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪnəri ɑːt / (BrE)
the practice or skill of preparing, cooking, and presenting food, often as a professional discipline
the art of preparing and cooking food

Examples

She decided to study culinary art at a renowned cooking school in Paris.
Mastering culinary art requires patience, creativity, and a love of food.
The museum's new exhibit explores the history of culinary art across different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooking - the process of preparing food by heating it

Etymology

From Latin 'culinarius' (pertaining to the kitchen) and Old French 'art' (skill, craft).
