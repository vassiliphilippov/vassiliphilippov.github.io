open marriage

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.pən ˈmær.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən ˈmær.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a marriage in which both partners agree that each may have sexual relationships with other people outside the marriage, without guilt or jealousy
marriage allowing extramarital relationships

Examples

They decided to try an open marriage after discussing their feelings about monogamy.
Some couples find that an open marriage brings them closer by promoting honesty and trust.
An open marriage requires clear communication and mutual consent to work well.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monogamy - having a sexual relationship with only one partner at a time
