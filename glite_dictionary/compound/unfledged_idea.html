unfledged idea

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈflɛdʒd aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ʌnˈflɛdʒd aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea or concept that is immature or not fully developed
immature or undeveloped idea

Examples

His proposal was dismissed as just another unfledged idea lacking practical details.
The company's new project is still an unfledged idea and requires more research.
Many unfledged ideas are generated during brainstorming sessions before being refined.

Synset Examples

The unfledged ideas needed more development before pitching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half-baked idea - an idea that is not thoroughly thought out or finished

Etymology

From 'unfledged' (not having acquired feathers needed for flight) combined with 'idea'.
