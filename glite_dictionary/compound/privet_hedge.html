privet hedge

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪvət hɛdʒ / (AmE), / ˈprɪvɪt hɛdʒ / (BrE)
A hedge made from closely planted privet bushes, often used to form garden boundaries or screens.
hedge made from privet bushes

Examples

The old cottage was surrounded by a neatly trimmed privet hedge.
They decided to plant a privet hedge for extra privacy along the driveway.
Every spring, he carefully pruned the privet hedge to keep it looking tidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hedge - a row of shrubs or small trees planted close together
