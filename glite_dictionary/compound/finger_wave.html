finger wave

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ weɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə weɪv / (BrE)
a wave or gesture made by moving the fingers, often used as a greeting or farewell.
a wave using the fingers

Examples

The child gave a shy finger wave to her teacher before leaving the classroom.
As the train departed, he managed a quick finger wave through the window.
She greeted her neighbor with a friendly finger wave from across the street.
