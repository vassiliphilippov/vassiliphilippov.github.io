pillow lace

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊ leɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ leɪs / (BrE)
a handmade lace created by twisting and braiding threads wound on bobbins, with the pattern laid out on a pillow and marked by pins
handmade lace made on a pillow with bobbins

Examples

She inherited a beautiful piece of pillow lace from her grandmother.
Demonstrations on how to make pillow lace are given at the local museum.
The delicate pattern of the pillow lace caught everyone's attention at the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobbin lace - lace made by intertwining threads wound on bobbins
