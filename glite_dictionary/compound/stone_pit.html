stone pit

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn pɪt / (AmE), / stəʊn pɪt / (BrE)
a surface excavation where stone or slate is extracted from the ground; a quarry
surface excavation for stone extraction

Examples

The workers spent all day at the stone pit extracting blocks of granite.
Many old stone pits have since filled with water to become small lakes.
The village was built near a large stone pit that supplied materials for the church.

Synset Examples

a British term for ‘quarry’ is ‘stone pit’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarry - an open excavation for obtaining stone or slate
