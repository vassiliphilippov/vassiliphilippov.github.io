lesbionic art

1. NOUN

/ lɛzˈbaɪ.ɑnɪk ɑːrt / (AmE), / lɛzˈbaɪ.ɒnɪk ɑːt / (BrE)
art that reflects, represents, or is associated with lesbian identity and culture.
art linked with lesbian identity/culture

Examples

The museum opened a new exhibit focusing on lesbionic art from the 20th century.
She curated a show that highlighted the diversity of lesbionic art around the world.
Many artists say lesbionic art provides a space for authentic expression and community.

Synset Examples

The festival celebrated lesbionic art and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesbian art - art created by or focused on lesbians

Etymology

From 'lesbian' (relating to lesbians) and '-ic' (forming adjectives), with 'art' denoting creative works.
