highly infective

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪli ɪnˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
(of a microorganism) extremely capable of causing infection; very likely to transmit disease.
extremely likely to cause infection
medical

Examples

The laboratory was closed after a highly infective strain of bacteria was discovered.
Doctors took extra precautions when handling the highly infective virus.
A highly infective organism can spread quickly through a community if not contained.

Synset Examples

a highly infective organism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infectious - able to cause infection or disease
