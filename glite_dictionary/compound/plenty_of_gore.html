plenty of gore

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛnti əv ɡɔr / (AmE), / ˈplɛnti əv ɡɔː / (BrE)
a large amount of violent or bloody content, especially in films, books, or other media
a large amount of violent or bloody content

Examples

The new horror film promises plenty of gore for fans of the genre.
If you're squeamish, avoid this novel—it contains plenty of gore and disturbing scenes.
The video game was criticized for having plenty of gore but little substance.

Synset Examples

The book contained plenty of gore for horror fans.

Related Terms
