goat cheese

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊt tʃiz / (AmE), / ɡəʊt tʃiːz / (BrE)
A soft or semi-soft cheese made from the milk of goats, often noted for its tangy flavor and creamy texture.
cheese made from goat's milk

Examples

She served a salad topped with creamy goat cheese and walnuts.
The tangy flavor of goat cheese pairs nicely with roasted vegetables.
I bought some fresh goat cheese from the local farmer's market.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cheese - A food made from the pressed curds of milk
