flex harrow

1. NOUN

/ flɛks ˈhæroʊ / (AmE), / flɛks ˈharəʊ / (BrE)
an agricultural harrow with fine spring tines mounted on a flexible frame that follows the contour of the ground, used for weeding crops
flexible agricultural harrow for weeding crops

Examples

The farmer used a flex harrow to prepare the field before planting seeds.
Because of its contour-following design, a flex harrow is perfect for uneven terrain.
Weeding with a flex harrow helps reduce the need for herbicides in crop fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harrow - an agricultural implement with spikes or disks for breaking up and smoothing soil
