baby minder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪbi ˌmaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbi ˌmaɪndə / (BrE)
a person who looks after babies, usually in their own home, while the babies' parents are at work
person who cares for babies
british

Examples

We hired a baby minder to look after our son while we were at work.
A reliable baby minder is hard to find in the city.
The baby minder arrived every morning at eight o'clock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babysitter - a person who looks after children in the parents' absence, often for short periods
