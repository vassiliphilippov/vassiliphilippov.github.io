rating system

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
A method for classifying people, things, or performances according to their quality, merit, or amount, often with levels or grades.
system for classifying by quality or merit

Examples

The hotel chain adopted a new rating system to help guests choose accommodations.
Our school uses a rating system to evaluate teacher performance each year.
The movie industry's rating system determines which audiences are suitable for each film.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranking system - a system for arranging items by order of importance or achievement
