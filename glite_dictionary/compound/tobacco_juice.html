tobacco juice

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ dʒus / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ dʒuːs / (BrE)
saliva that has been stained brown by being mixed with chewing tobacco or snuff
saliva stained brown by tobacco

Examples

He spat a stream of tobacco juice into the old metal can by his feet.
Stains from tobacco juice lingered on the porch steps where he used to sit and chew.
The umpire warned players not to spit tobacco juice on the field.
