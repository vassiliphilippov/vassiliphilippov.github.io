love-philtre

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
A drink believed to have magical powers to cause the person who consumes it to fall in love with the giver.
magic potion causing love

Examples

The witch brewed a love-philtre for the prince, hoping to make him fall for the princess.
Legends often tell of a love-philtre given to unsuspecting victims by those desperate for affection.
She believed the mysterious love-philtre would finally win him over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love potion - a drink intended to cause love

Etymology

From Greek 'philtro', meaning a charm or spell, especially a love charm.
