genus Crotalaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs kroʊˌtæˈlɛər.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs krəʊˌtæˈlɑː.rɪ.ə / (BrE)
a large group of herbaceous plants with simple leaves and racemes of yellow flowers, primarily found in tropical Africa
a genus of tropical herbs with yellow flowers

Examples

The genus Crotalaria includes many species known for their attractive yellow flowers.
Botanists discovered a new member of the genus Crotalaria in tropical Africa.
Some species in the genus Crotalaria are cultivated for their nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Etymology

From Latin 'crotalum' (rattle), referring to the sound of the seeds in the pods.
