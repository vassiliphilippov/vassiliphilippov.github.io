third stomach

1. NOUN

/ θɜrd ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / θɜːd ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the third compartment of the stomach in a ruminant animal, situated between the reticulum and abomasum
third compartment of a ruminant's stomach

Examples

The third stomach of a cow, known as the omasum, absorbs water and nutrients from food.
Veterinarians examined the animal's third stomach for signs of disease.
The digestion process in ruminants relies on the third stomach to break down complex plant materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omasum - the third compartment of a ruminant's stomach, also known as the omasum
