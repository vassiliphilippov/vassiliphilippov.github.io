cockbrained mistake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌbreɪnd mɪˈsteɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌbreɪnd mɪˈsteɪk / (BrE)
A foolish or stupid error or blunder.
foolish or stupid error

Examples

The CEO resigned after making a cockbrained mistake that cost the company millions.
He realized too late that forgetting her birthday was a cockbrained mistake.
Their plan failed because of one cockbrained mistake in the calculations.

Synset Examples

She made a cockbrained mistake in her report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a stupid or careless mistake
  • Synonym: gaffe - an unintentional act causing embarrassment
  • Antonym: masterstroke - an outstandingly skillful action (opposite of a mistake)

Etymology

Formed from 'cockbrained' (foolish, eccentric) + 'mistake'.
