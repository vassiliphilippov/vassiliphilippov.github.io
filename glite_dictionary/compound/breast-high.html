breast-high

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɛstˌhaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌhaɪ / (BrE)
Up to the level of the breasts; reaching as high as the chest.
up to the level of the breasts

Examples

The corn in the field grew breast-high before harvest season began.
We waded breast-high through the rushing river to reach the other side.
The grass was breast-high, making it difficult to see the path ahead.

Synset Examples

we were standing breast-high in the water

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: waist-high - up to the waist; reaching as high as the waist
  • Degree Higher: shoulder-high - up to the shoulders; reaching as high as the shoulders
