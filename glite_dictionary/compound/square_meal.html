square meal

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr miːl / (AmE), / skweə miːl / (BrE)
a substantial, well-balanced, and nourishing meal
substantial and nourishing meal

Examples

Growing up during tough times, many families struggled to put a square meal on the table each day.
After a long hike, all we wanted was a hot shower and a square meal.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
