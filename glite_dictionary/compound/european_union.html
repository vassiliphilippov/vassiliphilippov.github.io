European Union

1. PROPN

/ jʊˌroʊpiən ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / jʊˌrəʊpiən ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
an economic and political union of European countries aimed at fostering economic cooperation and integration through shared policies and regulations
political and economic union of European countries

Examples

After Brexit, the United Kingdom is no longer part of the European Union.
The European Union works to ensure peace and prosperity among its members.
