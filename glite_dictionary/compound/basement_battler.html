basement battler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsmənt ˈbæt̬lɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsmənt ˈbætlə / (BrE)
an artist, performer, or competitor who develops their skills or competes in underground or informal settings, often literally or figuratively in basements
underground performer or competitor
slang

Examples

Many basement battlers go on to influence mainstream music after years in the underground scene.
The documentary followed several basement battlers as they prepared for an unofficial rap competition.
In the dance world, a basement battler is someone respected for their raw, grassroots talent.

Synset Examples

As a basement battler, he thrived in the underground scene.
The basement battler worked hard to improve his skills.
Every basement battler had their own unique fighting style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning
