pansy violet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænzi ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˈpænzi ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
A common violet native to the eastern United States, with large pale blue or purple flowers resembling pansies.
a North American violet with pansy-like flowers

Examples

In the shade of the old oak tree, pansy violet bloomed in abundance every spring.
Gardeners admire the pansy violet for its large, delicate flowers that brighten up any border.
She photographed a cluster of pansy violet after a morning rain shower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - a small plant with purple or white flowers, genus Viola
  • Synonym: pansy - a garden plant with large, colorful flowers, genus Viola
