potty mouth

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑti ˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈpɒti ˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a person who frequently uses vulgar or coarse language
person who uses vulgar language
informal

Examples

My grandmother scolded me for being a potty mouth after I swore at the table.
Even though he's a kind person, his potty mouth sometimes offends people.
Teachers warned students that a potty mouth would not be tolerated in class.

Synset Examples

Her potty mouth surprised everyone at the dinner party.
He got in trouble for having a potty mouth at school.
I wish he would stop his potty mouth around the kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foul-mouthed - using offensive or vulgar language
  • Antonym: clean-mouthed - avoiding vulgar or offensive language
