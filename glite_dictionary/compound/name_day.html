name day

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪm ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈneɪm ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A day dedicated to celebrating a person who has the same name as a saint, usually observed on the feast day of that saint.
celebration on a saint's feast day of the same name

Examples

In many European countries, people celebrate their name day instead of their birthday.
She received flowers and small gifts from her friends for her name day.
Finding your name day in the calendar is a cherished tradition for some families.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: birthday - the anniversary of the day on which a person was born
