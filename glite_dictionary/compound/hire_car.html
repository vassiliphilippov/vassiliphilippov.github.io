hire car

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪɚ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈhaɪə kɑː / (BrE)
a car that has been rented for temporary use, especially used in British English
car rented for short-term use
british

Examples

After her flight was delayed, she collected her hire car and drove straight to the conference center.
Tourists often reserve a hire car in advance to explore the countryside at their own pace.
He returned the hire car with a full tank of petrol as required by the company.

Synset Examples

she picked up a hire car at the airport and drove to her hotel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rental car - a car that has been rented for short-term use (American English)
