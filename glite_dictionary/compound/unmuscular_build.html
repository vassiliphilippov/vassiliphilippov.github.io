unmuscular build

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈmʌs.kjələr bɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə bɪld / (BrE)
A body type that lacks muscle mass; a physique not marked by developed muscles.
a body type lacking muscle mass

Examples

Despite his unmuscular build, he excelled in long-distance swimming.
People with an unmuscular build may find some sports more challenging.
She was proud of her achievements regardless of her unmuscular build.

Synset Examples

His unmuscular build didn't stop him from running marathons.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: muscular build - a body type with well-developed muscles
