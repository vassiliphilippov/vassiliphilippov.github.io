Hangzhou Bay

1. PROPN

/ hɑːŋˈdʒoʊ beɪ / (AmE), / hæŋˈdʒəʊ beɪ / (BrE)
a large bay formed by an inlet of the East China Sea, located near the city of Hangzhou in eastern China
bay in eastern China

Examples

The construction of a bridge across Hangzhou Bay has greatly shortened travel time between Shanghai and Ningbo.
Every year, thousands of tourists visit Hangzhou Bay to see the famous tidal bore.
Industrial development along Hangzhou Bay has brought both economic growth and environmental concerns.
