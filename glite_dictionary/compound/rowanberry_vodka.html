rowanberry vodka

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.ənˌbɛri ˈvoʊd.kə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.ənˌbɛri ˈvɒd.kə / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic drink made by infusing rowanberries with the addition of caramel, wine distillate, and fruit distillates
vodka infused with rowanberries and other additives

Examples

He offered her a glass of rowanberry vodka to accompany the meal.
Traditional recipes for rowanberry vodka have been passed down for generations in Eastern Europe.
The distinctive flavor of rowanberry vodka comes from both the berries and the addition of caramel.

Synset Examples

Rowan vodka goes well with game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic drink from Eastern Europe, usually made from grain or potatoes

Etymology

From 'rowanberry', the fruit of the rowan tree, and 'vodka', a distilled alcoholic beverage of Slavic origin.
