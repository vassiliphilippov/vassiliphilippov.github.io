endurance riding

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʊr.əns ˈraɪ.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjʊə.rəns ˈraɪ.dɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding horses over long distances, typically involving timed competitions
long-distance horseback riding sport

Examples

She has been training hard for the endurance riding competition next month.
Endurance riding requires both the horse and rider to have exceptional fitness levels.
Many people travel internationally to participate in endurance riding events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endurance race - a race testing stamina over a long distance
