giant perch

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt pɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt pɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a large species of perch, valued for its sporting and eating qualities, found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater habitats
a large edible species of perch

Examples

Anglers travel from around the world to catch the elusive giant perch in these rivers.
The chef prepared a delicious meal using freshly caught giant perch.
Conservation efforts are being made to protect the giant perch from overfishing.
