tax hike

1. NOUN

/ tæks haɪk / (AmE), / tæks haɪk / (BrE)
an increase in the rate or amount of taxes
an increase in taxes

Examples

The government announced a tax hike to fund new infrastructure projects.
Many citizens protested against the proposed tax hike last year.
After the tax hike, businesses faced increased financial pressure.

Synset Examples

a tax increase of 15 percent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax increase - an amount by which taxes are increased
  • Antonym: tax cut - a reduction in the rate or amount of taxes
