basket flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪt ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
An annual plant of the southwestern United States with purple flower heads and bracts resembling small baskets, cultivated as an ornamental.
ornamental annual plant with basket-like bracts

Examples

The garden was filled with the vivid purple blooms of the basket flower in early summer.
She chose to plant a basket flower for its unique basket-like bracts and decorative appeal.
Many gardeners appreciate the basket flower for attracting butterflies and adding color to borders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Centaurea americana - the scientific name for basket flower
  • Synonym: cornflower - a related plant in the same genus, with blue flower heads
