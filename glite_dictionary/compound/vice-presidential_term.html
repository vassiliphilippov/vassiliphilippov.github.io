vice-presidential term

1. NOUN

/ ˌvaɪs prɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl tɜrm / (AmE), / ˌvaɪs prɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl tɜːm / (BrE)
the period of time during which a vice president holds office
the tenure of a vice president

Examples

His vice-presidential term was marked by significant efforts in foreign policy.
The constitution limited the vice-presidential term to four years per election cycle.
She completed her vice-presidential term with high approval ratings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presidential term - the tenure of a president
