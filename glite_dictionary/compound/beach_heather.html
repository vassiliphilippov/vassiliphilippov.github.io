beach heather

1. NOUN

/ biːtʃ ˈhɛðər / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈhɛðə / (BrE)
a small heathlike plant with white down, typically found on beaches in northeastern North America
a heathlike beach plant of North America

Examples

Beach heather can often be found growing along the sandy shores of Cape Cod.
The delicate white down on beach heather helps protect it from harsh coastal winds.
Conservationists are monitoring populations of beach heather due to habitat loss.
