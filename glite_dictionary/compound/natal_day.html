natal day

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪtl deɪ / (AmE), / ˈneɪtl deɪ / (BrE)
the date on which a person was born; birthday
birthday; date of birth

Examples

We celebrated his natal day with a small family gathering.
In some cultures, the natal day is marked with special rituals and ceremonies.
She received many gifts and well wishes on her natal day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthday - the anniversary of the day on which a person was born

Etymology

From Latin natus (born) + English day.
