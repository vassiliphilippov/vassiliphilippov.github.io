rubber plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbər plænt / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree (Ficus elastica) often grown indoors as a houseplant, and also used as a source of natural rubber.
tropical tree, popular houseplant

Examples

The rubber plant in the corner of our living room has grown impressively tall.
She bought a small rubber plant to add greenery to her office desk.
Many people mistake the rubber plant for a purely decorative species, not realizing its industrial use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ficus - Genus of tropical trees and shrubs including the rubber plant
