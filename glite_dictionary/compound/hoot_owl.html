hoot owl

1. NOUN

/ huːt aʊl / (AmE), / huːt aʊl / (BrE)
An owl known for making hooting sounds, as distinguished from owls that screech.
an owl that hoots, not screeches

Examples

We heard a hoot owl calling from the woods at midnight.
The hoot owl can often be distinguished by its deep, resonant call.
Unlike the screech owl, the hoot owl has a more mellow, rhythmic song.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: screech owl - an owl that emits screeching calls
