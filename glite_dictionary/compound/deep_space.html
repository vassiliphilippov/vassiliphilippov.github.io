deep space

1. NOUN

/ dip speɪs / (AmE), / diːp speɪs / (BrE)
any region of space that lies outside the solar system; the vast distances between stars and galaxies.
outer regions beyond the solar system

Examples

Astronomers use powerful telescopes to study deep space and learn about distant galaxies.
The satellite was sent into deep space to gather data from beyond our solar system.
Many science fiction movies depict journeys through deep space to explore unknown worlds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outer space - the physical universe beyond the earth's atmosphere
