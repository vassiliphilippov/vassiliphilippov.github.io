funny meme

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni miːm / (AmE), / ˈfʌni miːm / (BrE)
a humorous image, video, or piece of text that is rapidly spread online, often with variations, and intended to make people laugh
humorous internet image or video
informal

Examples

That funny meme you sent me yesterday had me laughing all day long.
She loves collecting funny memes to share with her friends on social media.
Whenever I'm feeling down, looking at a funny meme always cheers me up.

Synset Examples

I can't stop laughing at this funny meme.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: meme - an image, video, or phrase spread online as a cultural idea

Etymology

From 'funny' (humorous) + 'meme', coined by Richard Dawkins in 1976 from Greek 'mīmēma' (that which is imitated).
