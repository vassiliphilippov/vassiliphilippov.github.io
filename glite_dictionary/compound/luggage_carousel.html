luggage carousel

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ˌkæɹəˈsɛl / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ˌkærəˈsɛl / (BrE)
a continuous moving belt at an airport from which arriving passengers collect their checked luggage
conveyor belt for collecting luggage at airports

Examples

Passengers gathered around the luggage carousel waiting for their bags to appear.
The luggage carousel was jammed with suitcases and travel gear.
We waited nervously at the luggage carousel, hoping our lost bag would turn up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baggage carousel - another term for luggage carousel
  • Synonym: conveyor belt - continuous moving band for transporting objects
