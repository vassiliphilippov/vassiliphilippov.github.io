big idea

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / bɪɡ aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
a significant or important concept, rationale, or reason, often underlying an action or plan
main concept or important reason

Examples

She explained the big idea behind the new marketing campaign with great enthusiasm.
Can you tell me what the big idea is before we start this project?
The big idea of the novel is about overcoming adversity.

Synset Examples

What's the big idea of changing the meeting time?
What's the big idea behind this new policy?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main idea - the chief point or central concept of something
  • Synonym: key point - the most important part or aspect of something
