advisory service

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvaɪzəri ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzəri ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a professional service that provides expert advice, recommendations, and guidance to clients, especially to support their decision making
professional advice service or consultancy

Examples

The company hired an advisory service to help improve its business strategy.
Our bank offers an advisory service for clients seeking investment guidance.
He contacted a financial advisory service before making his decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consulting service - a service that provides expert advice to clients
