grass poly

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs ˈpɑːli / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs ˈpɒli / (BrE)
an annual plant with small solitary pink flowers, native to Europe but widely naturalized in moist areas
a small annual plant with pink flowers

Examples

We found a rare grass poly blooming along the riverbank after the spring rains.
The grass poly is often overlooked due to its small and delicate flowers.
Conservationists are working to protect wetland habitats that support the grass poly.
