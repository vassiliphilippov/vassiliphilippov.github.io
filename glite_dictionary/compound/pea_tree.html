pea tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiˌtri / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌtriː / (BrE)
any shrub or small tree of the genus Caragana, having even-pinnate leaves, usually yellow flowers, and linear seed pods
shrub or small tree of genus Caragana

Examples

The pea tree is often planted as a windbreak in harsh climates.
Yellow blossoms covered the pea tree in the garden last spring.
Farmers use the hardy pea tree to prevent soil erosion on their land.
