defense purpose

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
a reason or objective related to protecting against attack or ensuring security, especially in military or official contexts
reason related to protection or security

Examples

The new regulations restrict the export of certain materials to defense purposes only.
The facility was originally constructed for defense purposes during the Cold War.
They argued that the funds should be allocated strictly for defense purposes, not for commercial projects.

Synset Examples

The glacis was well-designed for defense purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security purpose - reason or objective related to ensuring safety
