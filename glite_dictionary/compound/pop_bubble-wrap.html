to pop bubble-wrap

1. VERB

/ pɑp ˈbʌbəl ræp / (AmE), / pɒp ˈbʌbəl ræp / (BrE)
to burst the air-filled bubbles in a sheet of plastic protective wrap, often for fun or stress relief.
burst the bubbles on bubble-wrap

Examples

Whenever I feel anxious, I like to pop bubble-wrap to calm down.
Children often compete to see who can pop bubble-wrap the fastest.
She absentmindedly began to pop bubble-wrap while talking on the phone.

Synset Examples

I love popping bubble-wrap; it's so satisfying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burst - break open or apart suddenly and noisily
