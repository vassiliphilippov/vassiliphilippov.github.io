heterosexual person

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬əˈsɛk.tʃu.əl ˈpɝː.sən / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.əˈsɛk.tʃu.əl ˈpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a person who is sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex
person attracted to the opposite sex

Examples

As a heterosexual person, he has only dated women.
The study compared the experiences of heterosexual persons and those with other sexual orientations.
A heterosexual person may face different societal expectations than someone who is bisexual or homosexual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homosexual person - a person attracted to members of the same sex

Etymology

From Greek heteros (other, different) + Latin sexualis (relating to sex).
