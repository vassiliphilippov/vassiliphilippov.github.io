helpful community

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlp.fəl kəˈmjuː.nə.ti / (AmE), / ˈhɛlp.fəl kəˈmjuː.nə.ti / (BrE)
a group of people who support and assist each other, especially by sharing information or advice about a common interest or need
supportive group sharing advice or assistance

Examples

Our neighborhood is a helpful community where people look out for each other.
She joined a helpful community online that offered advice about starting a business.
Finding a helpful community at university made the first year much easier.

Synset Examples

Reddit is full of helpful communities for every interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: community - a group of people with a common characteristic or interest
  • Synonym: support group - group that provides emotional or practical support
