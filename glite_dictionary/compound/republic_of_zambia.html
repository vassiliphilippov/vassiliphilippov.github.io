Republic of Zambia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˈzæmbiə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈzæmbiə / (BrE)
The official name of Zambia, a landlocked country in southern-central Africa, formerly called Northern Rhodesia before independence in 1964.
country in southern-central Africa

Examples

The Republic of Zambia gained its independence from Britain in 1964.
Copper production is a major part of the economy in the Republic of Zambia.
Victoria Falls lies on the border between Zimbabwe and the Republic of Zambia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zambia - country in southern-central Africa
