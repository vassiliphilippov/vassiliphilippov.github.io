there and back

1. ADV

/ ðɛr ənd bæk / (AmE), / ðeə ənd bæk / (BrE)
to a destination and back again; covering both the outward and return journey
a journey to a place and back

Examples

We managed to walk there and back before sunset.
The train ticket price covers the journey there and back.
It only takes two hours to drive there and back.

Synset Examples

The hike took us there and back in the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: round trip - a journey to a place and back again
