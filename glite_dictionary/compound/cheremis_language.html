Cheremis language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛrəˌmis ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrəmɪs ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Finnic language spoken by the Cheremis (Mari) people, chiefly in the Mari El Republic of Russia
Finnic language of the Mari (Cheremis) people

Examples

The Cheremis language is also known as Mari and is spoken in the Volga region.
Researchers have published several grammars and dictionaries of the Cheremis language.
Many traditions and folk songs are preserved in the Cheremis language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mari language - the modern name for the Cheremis language, a Finnic language of Russia

Etymology

‘Cheremis’ from Russian ‘черемисы’ (Cheremisy), an older name for the Mari people.
