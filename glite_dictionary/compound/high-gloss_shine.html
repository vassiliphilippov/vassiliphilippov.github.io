high-gloss shine

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ɡlɑs ʃaɪn / (AmE), / haɪ ɡlɒs ʃaɪn / (BrE)
a very shiny, highly reflective surface or finish, typically produced by polishing
very shiny, reflective finish

Examples

The marble countertop had a high-gloss shine that made the kitchen look elegant.
For best results, buff the car until it achieves a high-gloss shine.
She admired her nails, which the manicurist had given a brilliant high-gloss shine.

Synset Examples

His shoes were polished to a high-gloss shine.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: matte finish - a non-shiny, dull surface finish
