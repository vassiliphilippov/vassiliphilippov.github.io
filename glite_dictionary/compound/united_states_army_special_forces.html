United States Army Special Forces

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːˈnaɪt̬ɪd steɪts ˈɑːrmi spəˈʃəl ˈfɔːrsɪz / (AmE), / ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts ˈɑːmi spəˈʃɛl ˈfɔːsɪz / (BrE)
a division of the United States Army trained for conducting unconventional warfare and guerilla operations
U.S. Army unit for unconventional warfare

Examples
