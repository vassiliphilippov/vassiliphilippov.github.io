leather strip

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚ strɪp / (AmE), / ˈlɛðə strɪp / (BrE)
a long, narrow piece of leather used for binding, fastening, or crafting purposes
a narrow piece of leather

Examples

He fastened the package with a leather strip to keep it secure.
A craftsman shaped the leather strip carefully for the belt.
The old book was bound using a strong leather strip along its spine.
