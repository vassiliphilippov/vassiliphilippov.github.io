Lake Urmia

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ɚˈmiːə / (AmE), / leɪk ɜːˈmiːə / (BrE)
a shallow, saline lake located in northwestern Iran, between the city of Tabriz and the eastern border of Turkey
saline lake in northwestern Iran

Examples

The shrinking of Lake Urmia has become a major environmental concern in Iran.
Many migratory birds depend on Lake Urmia as a vital stopover on their routes.
Researchers are studying ways to restore water levels in Lake Urmia.
