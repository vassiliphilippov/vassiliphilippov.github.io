racy article

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsi ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪsi ˈɑːtɪkl̩ / (BrE)
an article featuring content that is sexually suggestive or provocative
sexually suggestive or provocative article

Examples

The tabloid was notorious for publishing racy articles that pushed the boundaries of good taste.
Several parents complained about the inclusion of a racy article in the school magazine.
Despite criticism, the journalist defended her decision to write a racy article about dating scandals.

Synset Examples

The magazine featured several racy articles about celebrities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salacious article - an article that is lewd or appealing to sexual desire
  • Antonym: tasteful article - an article that is appropriate and not sexually provocative

Etymology

From 'racy', meaning lively or slightly improper, and 'article'; 'racy' may derive from late 17th-century English, originally used to describe wine with a distinctive flavor.
