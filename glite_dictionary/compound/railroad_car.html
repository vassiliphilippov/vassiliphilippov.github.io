railroad car

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɔd kɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɔːd kɑː / (BrE)
a vehicle with wheels designed to run on railway tracks, used for carrying passengers or goods
vehicle used on railways

Examples

The museum displayed a restored railroad car from the 19th century.
Passengers hurried to board the railroad car before the train departed.
A freight railroad car carried goods across the country.

Synset Examples

three cars had jumped the rails

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train car - a vehicle forming part of a train
  • Synonym: railcar - single railway vehicle for passengers or freight
  • Degree Lower: freight car - railroad car used for carrying goods
  • Degree Higher: passenger car - railroad car designed for carrying people
