lecture room

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛktʃɚ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈlɛktʃə ruːm / (BrE)
A room, especially in a college or university, where lectures are given to students.
room for giving lectures

Examples

The professor entered the lecture room just as the students were settling down.
All the seats in the lecture room were taken before the talk began.
She spent long hours preparing her slides for each lecture room presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classroom - a room in which a class of students is taught
