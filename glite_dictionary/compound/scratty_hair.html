scratty hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈskræti hɛr / (AmE), / ˈskrati heə / (BrE)
Hair that is disheveled, messy, or untidy in appearance.
messy or untidy hair
british

Examples

After the long hike, her scratty hair made her look wild and windswept.
He tried to smooth his scratty hair before the meeting but it wouldn’t stay down.
Children often come home with scratty hair after playing outside all afternoon.

Synset Examples

Her scratty hair was a result of the windy weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unkempt hair - hair that is not combed or tidied
