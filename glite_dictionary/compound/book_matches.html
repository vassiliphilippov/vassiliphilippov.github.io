book matches

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈmætʃɪz / (AmE), / bʊk ˈmatʃɪz / (BrE)
Small, flat paper matches attached together in a folding paper cover, designed to be struck on a special surface.
matches bound in a paper booklet

Examples

He took a book matches from the counter to light his cigarette.
Many restaurants used to give away free book matches with their logo printed on the cover.
The old desk drawer was filled with several forgotten book matches from hotels and bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchbook - a small paper container holding book matches
