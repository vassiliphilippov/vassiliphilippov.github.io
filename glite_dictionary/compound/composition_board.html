composition board

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən bɔrd / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən bɔːd / (BrE)
a stiff, moderately thick paperboard used for covers, mounting, and other applications
stiff, thick paperboard product

Examples

The artist mounted her drawing on a sheet of composition board for display.
We used composition board to make sturdy report covers in school.
Composition board is often chosen for its strength and affordability in crafting projects.
