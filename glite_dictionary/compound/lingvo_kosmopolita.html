Lingvo Kosmopolita

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪŋvoʊ ˌkɒsmoʊpəˈliːtə / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋvəʊ ˌkɒzməpɒˈliːtə / (BrE)
an artificial international auxiliary language invented by René de Saussure in the early 20th century
constructed language by René de Saussure

Examples

'Lingvo Kosmopolita' was designed to facilitate international communication.
Few people today are familiar with the grammar of Lingvo Kosmopolita.
The linguist published several papers about the principles of Lingvo Kosmopolita.

Etymology

From Esperanto 'Lingvo Kosmopolita' meaning 'Cosmopolitan Language'; created by linguist René de Saussure.
