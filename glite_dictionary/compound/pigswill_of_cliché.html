pigswill of cliché

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡˌswɪl əv kliˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌswɪl əv ˈkliːʃeɪ / (BrE)
a disorderly or worthless collection of overused and unoriginal expressions
mess of overused clichés

Examples

The critic dismissed the novel as a pigswill of cliché lacking any originality.
Her speech was little more than a pigswill of cliché and tired platitudes.
Viewers complained that the show devolved into a pigswill of cliché after the first season.

Synset Examples

He described the old movie as a pigswill of clichés.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliché - an overused phrase or opinion

Etymology

Pigswill: from 'pig' + 'swill' (food waste fed to pigs). Cliché: from French 'cliché' (stereotype, overused expression).
