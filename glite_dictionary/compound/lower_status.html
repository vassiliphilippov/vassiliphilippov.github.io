lower status

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊər ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊə ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the position or condition of being considered less important or less powerful than others; inferiority in rank or social standing
inferior rank or social position

Examples

People from rural areas sometimes feel they have a lower status in the city.
He resented being given lower status work despite his qualifications.
In traditional societies, women often occupied a lower status than men.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: higher status - a superior position or condition in social or organizational hierarchy
  • Synonym: inferiority - the state of being lower in status, quality, or rank
