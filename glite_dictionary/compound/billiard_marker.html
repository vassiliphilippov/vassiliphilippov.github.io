billiard marker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljərd ˈmɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈbɪljəd ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
a device used to keep the score during a game of billiards
device for keeping billiards score

Examples

The billiard marker was placed beside the table to record each player's score.
He glanced at the billiard marker to check how far ahead he was in the game.
They forgot to reset the billiard marker before starting a new match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoreboard - a large board for displaying scores in a game or sport
