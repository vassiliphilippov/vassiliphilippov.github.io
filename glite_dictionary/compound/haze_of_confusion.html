haze of confusion

1. NOUN

/ heɪz əv kənˈfjuːʒən / (AmE), / heɪz ɒv kənˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
a mental state in which a person feels unclear, uncertain, or unable to think clearly, as if clouded by a mist
a state of unclear or muddled thinking

Examples

After waking from surgery, he was in a haze of confusion and could not remember where he was.
The sudden news left the team in a haze of confusion, unsure what steps to take next.
She wandered through the day in a haze of confusion, struggling to focus on her tasks.

Synset Examples

She felt a haze of confusion after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusion - the state of being bewildered or unclear in one’s mind
