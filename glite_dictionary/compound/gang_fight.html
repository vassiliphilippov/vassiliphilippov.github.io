gang fight

1. NOUN

/ ɡæŋ faɪt / (AmE), / ɡæŋ faɪt / (BrE)
a violent physical confrontation between rival groups, especially of adolescents or young adults, affiliated as gangs
fight between rival gangs

Examples

The city has seen an increase in gang fights over the past year.
Several teenagers were injured in a gang fight near the subway station.
Police arrived too late to break up the gang fight that erupted last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brawl - a noisy, rough fight usually involving several people
  • Degree Higher: riot - a violent public disturbance involving many people
