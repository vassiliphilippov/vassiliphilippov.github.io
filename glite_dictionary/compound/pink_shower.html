pink shower

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈʃaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈʃaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a tropical American semi-evergreen tree with erect clusters of pink or rose-colored flowers, grown as an ornamental plant
ornamental tropical tree with pink flowers

Examples

The pink shower adds a splash of vibrant color to many tropical gardens.
In full bloom, the pink shower attracts butterflies with its beautiful flowers.
Gardeners often plant the pink shower as a decorative tree along avenues and parks.
