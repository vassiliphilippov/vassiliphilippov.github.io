pipe wrench

1. NOUN

/ paɪp rɛntʃ / (AmE), / paɪp rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a large adjustable tool with two serrated jaws used for gripping and turning pipes
tool for gripping and turning pipes

Examples

He tightened the leaking joint using a pipe wrench.
The plumber reached for his pipe wrench to loosen the stubborn pipe fitting.
Every toolbox should include a sturdy pipe wrench for plumbing repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monkey wrench - an adjustable wrench with smooth jaws, used for turning nuts and bolts
