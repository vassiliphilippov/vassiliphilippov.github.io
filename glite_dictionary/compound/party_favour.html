party favour

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti ˌfeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌfeɪvə / (BrE)
a small gift or souvenir given to guests at a party as a token of appreciation or as a memento of the event
small gift given to party guests

Examples

Each child at the birthday received a party favour as they left.
The bride and groom placed a party favour at every seat for their guests.
I still have the party favour from last year's celebration on my shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: souvenir - something kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event
