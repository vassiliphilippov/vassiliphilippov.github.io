flat earther

1. NOUN

/ flæt ˈɜrθər / (AmE), / flæt ˈɜːθə / (BrE)
a person who believes or claims that the Earth is flat, especially as part of a conspiracy theory
a person who believes the Earth is flat

Examples

Many people dismiss the arguments of a flat earther as pseudoscience.
The documentary featured interviews with a well-known flat earther from Ohio.
Debates between astronauts and flat earthers are common on online forums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspiracy theorist - a person who believes in conspiracy theories
