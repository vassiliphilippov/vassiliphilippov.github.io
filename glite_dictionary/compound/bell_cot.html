bell cot

1. NOUN

/ bɛl kɑt / (AmE), / bɛl kɒt / (BrE)
A small shelter for bells, often with a gable or shed roof, typically found on churches or chapels.
small shelter for bells

Examples

The old chapel's bell cot was beautifully restored last year.
A single bell hung in the modest bell cot above the village hall.
The architect included a decorative bell cot to preserve the building's historic charm.
