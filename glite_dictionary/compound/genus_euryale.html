genus Euryale

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs juːˈraɪəli / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs juːˈraɪəli / (BrE)
A genus of marine echinoderms known as basket stars, characterized by their branched, flexible arms.
genus of basket stars (echinoderms)

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species within the genus Euryale during their deep-sea expedition.
The intricate arms of creatures in the genus Euryale help them capture plankton efficiently.
Marine biologists often study the genus Euryale to understand echinoderm evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basket star - a marine echinoderm with highly branched arms

Etymology

From New Latin 'Euryale', from Greek Εὐρυάλη (Euryálē), a mythological figure, used in scientific naming.
