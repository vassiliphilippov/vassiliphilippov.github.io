coal shovel

1. NOUN

/ koʊl ˈʃʌvəl / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈʃʌvəl / (BrE)
a hand-held shovel used specifically for shoveling coal
shovel used for coal

Examples

He grabbed the coal shovel and began loading the furnace for the night.
A sturdy coal shovel makes cleaning up after a fire much easier.
The old coal shovel was hanging on a hook near the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shovel - a tool with a broad blade and typically upturned sides for digging or moving material
