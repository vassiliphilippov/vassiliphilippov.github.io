Jesus freak

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic or outspoken about their Christian faith, often to the point of being perceived as excessive or zealous.
extremely enthusiastic Christian
informal slang

Examples

Being a Jesus freak, she attends church every Sunday and tells everyone about her faith.
Some colleagues consider him a Jesus freak because he constantly talks about religion at work.
During the festival, a group of Jesus freaks handed out pamphlets on the street corner.

Synset Examples

Being a Jesus freak, she attends church every Sunday.
He's such a Jesus freak; always preaching at work.
The Jesus freak shared his testimony at the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive zeal, especially for a religious or political cause
  • Synonym: devotee - a person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about a particular activity or subject
  • Antonym: agnostic - a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God

Etymology

Blend of 'Jesus' and the slang 'freak' (enthusiast, eccentric person), popularized in the late 1960s and 70s in American counterculture.
