human dynamo

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˈdaɪnəˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈdaɪnəməʊ / (BrE)
a person who is extremely energetic and never seems to get tired
extremely energetic person

Examples

Everyone admires Jane because she is a human dynamo who never seems to slow down.
As a human dynamo, he juggled three jobs and still found time to volunteer.
Her colleagues call her a human dynamo due to her relentless energy at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live wire - a very lively and energetic person
  • Antonym: couch potato - a person who is habitually inactive or lazy

Etymology

From 'human' and 'dynamo', with 'dynamo' meaning a machine that generates energy, used metaphorically for a hardworking person.
