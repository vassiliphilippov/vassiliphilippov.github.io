rain gage

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the amount of rainfall over a given period
instrument for measuring rainfall

Examples

The meteorologist checked the rain gage to record last night's precipitation.
After the storm, the rain gage in the backyard was nearly full.
Farmers rely on a rain gage to monitor rainfall during the growing season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rain gauge - alternative spelling of rain gage, instrument for measuring rainfall
