ancient temple

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪnʃənt ˈtɛmpəl / (AmE), / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈtɛmpl̩ / (BrE)
A temple that is very old, often of historical or archaeological significance.
very old place of worship

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit the ancient temple every year to marvel at its architecture.
The ruins of the ancient temple stood atop the hill, overlooking the valley.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts beneath the ancient temple during the excavation.

Synset Examples

He visited the ancient temple during his travels.
He admired the intricate artwork on the outwall of the ancient temple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temple - a building dedicated to worship or religious ceremonies
