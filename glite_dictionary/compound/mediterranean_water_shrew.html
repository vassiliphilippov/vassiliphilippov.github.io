Mediterranean water shrew

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdəˌteɪrəˈniən ˈwɔtər ʃru / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪnɪən ˈwɔːtə ʃruː / (BrE)
a small semiaquatic mammal found in southern Europe and northwest Africa, belonging to the water shrew group
small aquatic mammal of southern Europe

Examples

The Mediterranean water shrew is known for its dense, water-repellent fur.
Conservation efforts are focused on protecting the habitat of the Mediterranean water shrew.
Researchers observed that the Mediterranean water shrew feeds mostly on aquatic insects and larvae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water shrew - a small, aquatic insectivorous mammal
