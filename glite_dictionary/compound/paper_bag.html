paper bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag made of paper, typically used by shops to carry purchased goods.
bag made of paper for carrying goods

Examples

She put the fresh bread in a paper bag at the bakery.
Many stores are switching from plastic to paper bags to reduce waste.
He carried his lunch to work every day in a paper bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastic bag - a bag made of thin plastic for carrying goods
