Battle of the Catalaunian Plains

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ði kəˌtæləˈluniən pleɪnz / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ði kəˌtælɔˈuːniən pleɪnz / (BrE)
a major battle in 451 in which Attila the Hun was defeated by Roman and Visigoth forces
historic battle in 451 defeating Attila the Hun

Examples

The Battle of the Catalaunian Plains marked a turning point in the struggle against the Huns.
Historians debate the exact location of the Battle of the Catalaunian Plains.
The Battle of the Catalaunian Plains is remembered as one of the last major victories of the Roman Empire.
