repair shed

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛr ʃɛd / (AmE), / rɪˈpeə ʃɛd / (BrE)
a large building, especially at an airport, where aircraft or other vehicles are stored and maintained
building for storing and maintaining vehicles, esp. aircraft

Examples

The damaged airplane was moved into the repair shed for extensive maintenance.
Security restrictions prevented unauthorized personnel from entering the repair shed at the airport.
The airline built a new repair shed to handle its expanding fleet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangar - a large building at an airport where aircraft are kept and maintained
