walnut liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔlnət lɪˈkɜr / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlnʌt lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic beverage made by infusing walnuts, typically with sugar and spices, in spirits.
alcoholic drink made from walnuts

Examples

We tasted a delicious walnut liqueur after dinner.
Her grandmother makes homemade walnut liqueur each autumn.
The recipe calls for a splash of walnut liqueur to enhance the dessert's flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a sweet, flavored spirit; general category for drinks like walnut liqueur
