spike rush

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪk rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪk rʌʃ / (BrE)
Any of various grass-like plants of the genus Eleocharis, typically found in wetland areas.
a grass-like wetland plant (genus Eleocharis)

Examples

The wetland was filled with tall spike rush swaying in the breeze.
Many species of waterfowl nest among the spike rush growing along the marsh edge.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where spike rush thrives.
