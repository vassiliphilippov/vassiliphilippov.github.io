surface area

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfɪs ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the total area of the outside surface of a three-dimensional object or a specific region enclosed within a boundary
total area of a surface

Examples

The architect calculated the surface area of the walls to estimate the amount of paint needed.
The surface area of the sphere determines how much heat it can absorb from the sun.
Cleaning a larger surface area will require more time and effort.

Synset Examples

the area of a rectangle
it was about 500 square feet in area

Related Terms

  • Synonym: area - the amount of space within the boundary of a flat object
