apple blight

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈapəl blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease that affects apple trees, typically causing damage to leaves, fruit, and sometimes killing the trees
disease of apple trees

Examples

The orchard suffered significant losses due to apple blight this season.
Farmers are seeking new ways to prevent apple blight from destroying their crops.
Proper pruning and fungicide can help reduce the risk of apple blight.
