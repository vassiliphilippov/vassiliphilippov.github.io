northern storm petrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn stɔrm ˈpɛtrəl / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən stɔːm ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
A small, sooty black seabird with white markings, found in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean regions.
a small, black seabird of the North Atlantic

Examples

We watched a northern storm petrel gliding over the waves during our boat trip.
The northern storm petrel is often seen feeding far from land.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of several northern storm petrels in the Atlantic Ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storm petrel - a small seabird of the family Hydrobatidae
