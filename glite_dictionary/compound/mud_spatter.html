mud spatter

1. NOUN

/ mʌd ˈspæt̬ɚ / (AmE), / mʌd ˈspætə / (BrE)
a splash or small amount of mud thrown onto a surface, often caused by moving vehicles
splash of mud thrown onto a surface

Examples

Be careful not to get mud spatter on your pants when walking down this road.
After the race, his car was covered in mud spatter from the track.
She wiped the mud spatter off her boots before entering the house.

Synset Examples

He tried to avoid the mud spatter from passing cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spatter - small splash of liquid or particles
