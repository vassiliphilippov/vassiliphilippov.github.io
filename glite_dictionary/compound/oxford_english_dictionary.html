1. PROPN

/ ˈɑks.fərd ˈɪŋ.glɪʃ ˌdɪk.ʃəˈnɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈɒks.fəd ˈɪŋ.glɪʃ ˌdɪk.ʃəˈnɛr.i / (BrE)
A comprehensive, historical dictionary of the English language published by Oxford University Press, widely regarded as the most authoritative record of English vocabulary and usage.
authoritative historical dictionary of English

Examples

Many linguists regard the Oxford English Dictionary as the most comprehensive dictionary of the English language.
She used the Oxford English Dictionary to research the historical development of English words.
The library has the complete print edition of the Oxford English Dictionary available for reference.
