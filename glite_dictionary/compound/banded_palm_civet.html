banded palm civet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændɪd pɑm ˈsɪvɪt / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd pɑːm ˈsɪvɪt / (BrE)
a small carnivorous mammal of Southeast Asia, having dark bands across its body and a long tail
a Southeast Asian civet with dark body bands

Examples

The banded palm civet is rarely seen due to its nocturnal habits.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the habitat of the banded palm civet.
Researchers observed a banded palm civet feeding on fruit in the rainforest canopy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civet - a small, mostly nocturnal mammal known for producing a musky secretion
