stink bell

1. NOUN

/ stɪŋk bɛl / (AmE), / stɪŋk bɛl / (BrE)
A foul-smelling herb found in California, with bell-shaped flowers, commonly growing as a weed in grainfields.
malodorous Californian weed with bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The field was overrun with stink bell after the heavy spring rains.
Farmers often try to remove stink bell from their grainfields due to its unpleasant odor.
We identified a patch of stink bell by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers and strong scent.
