game fish

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm fɪʃ / (AmE), / ɡeɪm fɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish prized for sport by anglers rather than for commercial value or food
fish valued for sport fishing

Examples

The river is famous for its abundant game fish, attracting anglers from all over the country.
Only catch-and-release fishing is allowed for game fish in this region.
Trout is one of the most popular game fish among fly fishermen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sport fish - fish caught primarily for sport
