loose woman

1. NOUN

/ lus ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / luːs ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is considered to have many sexual partners or behaves in a sexually promiscuous way, especially in a way regarded as immoral by others
sexually promiscuous woman (disparaging)

Examples

The townspeople often gossiped about her, calling her a loose woman behind her back.
He unfairly accused his wife of being a loose woman after seeing her talking to a neighbor.
The old novel described the protagonist as a loose woman, shocking readers of its time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulteress - a woman who commits adultery
  • Synonym: promiscuous - having many sexual relationships
