evening gown

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, formal dress worn by women for evening events such as parties, galas, or formal dinners
long formal women's dress for evening events

Examples

She looked stunning in her black evening gown at the charity gala.
The designer unveiled a new collection of evening gowns for the upcoming season.
It is customary to wear an evening gown to a formal wedding reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ball gown - formal dress worn for balls, typically with a full skirt
