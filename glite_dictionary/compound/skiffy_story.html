skiffy story

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪfi ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˈskɪfi ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
An informal or humorous term for a science fiction story.
informal term for science fiction story
informal slang

Examples

He enjoys reading skiffy stories when he wants something lighthearted and fun.
The magazine features several new skiffy stories by up-and-coming writers.
Some critics dismiss the novel as just another skiffy story, but fans appreciate its creativity.

Synset Examples

He loves skiffy stories about space adventures and aliens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: science fiction story - a story about imagined future science and technology
  • Degree Lower: fantasy story - a story featuring magical or supernatural elements

Etymology

'Skiffy' is a playful, humorous alteration of 'sci-fi,' sometimes used pejoratively or affectionately in science fiction fandom.
