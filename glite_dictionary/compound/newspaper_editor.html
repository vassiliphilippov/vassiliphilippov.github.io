newspaper editor

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈɛdɪtə / (BrE)
a person responsible for the editorial content and management of a newspaper
editor in charge of a newspaper

Examples

The newspaper editor approved the front-page story before it went to print.
After years as a newspaper editor, she decided to write a book about journalism.
He was promoted to newspaper editor after demonstrating strong leadership skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - a person who prepares and revises content for publication
