heart flutter

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈflʌtər / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈflʌtə / (BrE)
a feeling of excitement or nervousness, often caused by strong emotion
feeling of excitement or nervousness

Examples

Whenever she saw her crush, her heart flutter was impossible to hide.
The actor's charming smile made every fan's heart flutter with excitement.
Thinking about the exam gave him a sudden heart flutter.

Synset Examples

His romantic gestures made her heart flutter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterflies in one's stomach - a feeling of nervous anxiety in the stomach
