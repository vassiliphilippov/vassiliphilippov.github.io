City of the Angels

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪti əv ðə ˈeɪndʒəlz / (AmE), / ˈsɪti əv ðə ˈeɪndʒ(ə)lz / (BrE)
a nickname for Los Angeles, the most populous city in California and the motion picture capital of the United States
nickname for Los Angeles, California

Examples

Many people dream of making it big in the City of the Angels.
The City of the Angels is known for its film industry and beautiful beaches.
After moving to the City of the Angels, she quickly adjusted to the fast-paced lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Los Angeles - a major city in southern California, United States

Etymology

English translation of Spanish 'Los Angeles', meaning 'the angels'.
