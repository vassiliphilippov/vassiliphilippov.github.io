written text

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪtn tɛkst / (AmE), / ˈrɪtn tɛkst / (BrE)
something that is written, especially material that has been transcribed or copied, such as a typewritten version of dictation.
material in written form, especially transcribed

Examples

Please submit your written text by the end of the week for review.
The written text was created from an audio recording of the interview.
Teachers often ask students to analyze the written text to improve critical thinking skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transcript - a written or printed version of material originally presented in another medium
  • Synonym: text - the actual words written in a piece of literary or informational writing
