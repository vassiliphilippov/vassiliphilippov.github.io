1 Kings

1. PROPN

/ wʌn kɪŋz / (AmE), / wʌn kɪŋz / (BrE)
the first of two Old Testament books recounting the histories of the kings of Judah and Israel in the Bible
book of the Old Testament

Examples

Many stories about the prophet Elijah can be found in 1 Kings.
My professor assigned us chapters from 1 Kings for our comparative religion course.
The division of the kingdom after Solomon's reign is described in 1 Kings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: 2 Kings - the second book of Kings in the Old Testament

Etymology

Refers to the first 'Book of Kings' in the Old Testament, originally written in Hebrew.
