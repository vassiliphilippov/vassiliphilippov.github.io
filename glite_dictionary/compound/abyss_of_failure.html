abyss of failure

1. NOUN

/ əˈbɪs əv ˈfeɪljɚ / (AmE), / əˈbɪs əv ˈfeɪljə / (BrE)
a very deep or extreme state of failure or despair
extreme state of failure or despair

Examples

After the company's bankruptcy, many employees felt trapped in an abyss of failure.
The novel's protagonist struggles to escape his abyss of failure and find redemption.
Repeated setbacks pushed her dangerously close to an abyss of failure.

Synset Examples

The team's performance plunged them into an abyss of failure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: failure - lack of success in doing or achieving something
  • Degree Higher: despair - the complete loss of hope
