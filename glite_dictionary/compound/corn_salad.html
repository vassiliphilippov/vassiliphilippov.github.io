corn salad

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈsæləd / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈsæləd / (BrE)
a small annual plant (Valerianella locusta) with edible tender leaves, commonly used in salads
an edible leafy plant (Valerianella locusta)

Examples

She picked fresh corn salad from her garden for tonight's meal.
The supermarket stocks corn salad alongside other leafy greens.
Many chefs value corn salad for its mild flavor in spring salads.
