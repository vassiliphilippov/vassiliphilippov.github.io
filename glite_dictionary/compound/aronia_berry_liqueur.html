aronia berry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ əˈroʊniə ˈbɛri lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / əˈrəʊniə ˈbɛri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic liqueur made from aronia berries, known for its deep color and tart flavor.
liqueur made from aronia berries

Examples

He brought back a bottle of aronia berry liqueur from his trip to Eastern Europe.
The rich flavor of aronia berry liqueur makes it a popular ingredient in winter cocktails.
They served homemade aronia berry liqueur after dinner as a digestif.
