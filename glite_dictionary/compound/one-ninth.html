one-ninth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
Each of nine equal parts into which something is divided; 1/9.
one part of nine equal divisions

Examples

She divided the pizza and gave him one-ninth of the whole.
After the rainfall, only one-ninth of the city's water supply remained.
Each person at the table received exactly one-ninth of the cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ninth - the ordinal number corresponding to the number nine
  • Degree Higher: one-eighth - one part in eight equal parts
  • Degree Lower: one-tenth - one part in ten equal parts
