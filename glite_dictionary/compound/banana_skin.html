banana skin

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə skɪn / (AmE), / bəˈnænə skɪn / (BrE)
the outer covering of a banana, especially when it has been removed and discarded
peel of a banana (discarded)

Examples

He almost fell when he stepped on a banana skin left on the pavement.
The comedian used a banana skin in his slapstick routine.
Please throw the banana skin in the compost bin.

Synset Examples

he slipped on a banana skin and almost fell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana peel - the skin of a banana, especially when discarded
