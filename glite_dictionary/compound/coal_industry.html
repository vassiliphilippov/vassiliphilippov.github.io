coal industry

1. NOUN

/ koʊl ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy involved in the extraction, production, and distribution of coal
sector engaged in coal production

Examples

The coal industry has faced increasing pressure to reduce its environmental impact.
Thousands of jobs have been lost in the coal industry over the past decade.
Government regulations have significantly affected the profitability of the coal industry in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mining industry - sector involved in extraction of minerals
  • Synonym: energy sector - industries involved in production and supply of energy
