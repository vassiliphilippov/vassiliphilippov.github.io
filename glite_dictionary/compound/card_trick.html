card trick

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd trɪk / (AmE), / kɑːd trɪk / (BrE)
a trick or illusion performed using playing cards, often as a form of entertainment
magic trick with playing cards

Examples

The magician amazed the crowd with a clever card trick that nobody could figure out.
He practiced his favorite card trick for hours before showing it to his friends.
Learning a simple card trick can be a great way to entertain people at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic trick - an act intended to create an illusion of magic
