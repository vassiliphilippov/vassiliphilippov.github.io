intellectual masturbation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.t̬əˈlɛk.tʃu.əl ˌmæ.stɚˈbeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈlɛk.tʃu.əl ˌmæs.təˈbeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
lengthy or excessive theorizing or reasoning that serves no practical purpose and is often seen as self-indulgent or unproductive
excessive theorizing with no practical value
informal

Examples

Some academic conferences can seem like little more than intellectual masturbation to outside observers.
His lengthy treatise was dismissed as intellectual masturbation by critics who wanted actionable results.
Instead of solving real-world problems, the committee indulged in endless sessions of intellectual masturbation.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idle speculation - speculation or theorizing without practical purpose
  • Antonym: practical application - use of ideas in real, practical situations
