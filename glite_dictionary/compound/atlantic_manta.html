Atlantic manta

1. NOUN

/ ətˈlæntɪk ˈmæntə / (AmE), / ətˈlæntɪk ˈmantə / (BrE)
a large species of manta ray found in warm oceanic waters worldwide, especially common in the Gulf of Mexico and along the southern United States; noted for its vast wingspan, reaching up to 22 feet
large manta ray species found worldwide

Examples

The Atlantic manta is a majestic sight as it glides through the warm waters of the Gulf of Mexico.
Researchers tagged several Atlantic mantas to study their migratory patterns across the Atlantic.
A diver was thrilled to swim alongside a giant Atlantic manta off the coast of Florida.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manta ray - a large ray with triangular pectoral fins, typically found in warm seas
