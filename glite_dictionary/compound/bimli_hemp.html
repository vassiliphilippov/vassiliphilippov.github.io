bimli hemp

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪmli hɛmp / (AmE), / ˈbɪmli hɛmp / (BrE)
A valuable fiber plant of the East Indies, now widely cultivated for its strong fibers.
fiber plant of East Indies

Examples

The ropes were made from bimli hemp, prized for its durability.
Farmers in several Asian countries cultivate bimli hemp to supply the textile industry.
Textile manufacturers value bimli hemp for producing strong natural fibers.
