speech synthesizer

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ ˈsɪnθəˌsaɪzər / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ˈsɪnθɪsaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or software that produces artificial human speech, typically using electronic means
device producing artificial speech

Examples

The new phone comes equipped with a speech synthesizer for visually impaired users.
She programmed the robot's speech synthesizer to greet visitors in several languages.
A speech synthesizer can help people with voice impairments communicate more easily.

Synset Examples

The quality of a speech synthesizer is judged by its similarity to the human voice and by its ability to be understood clearly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: text-to-speech - technology that converts written text into spoken words
  • Synonym: voice synthesizer - a device or software that creates artificial speech sounds

Etymology

From 'speech' and 'synthesizer' (from Greek 'synthēsis', meaning 'putting together').
