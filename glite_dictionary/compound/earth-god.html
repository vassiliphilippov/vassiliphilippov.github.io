earth-god

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːθ ɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ ɡɒd / (BrE)
a deity associated with fertility, vegetation, and the earth
deity of fertility and the earth

Examples

The ancient tribe worshipped an earth-god to ensure a bountiful harvest.
Many cultures have myths about an earth-god who controls the cycles of nature.
Artifacts depicting the earth-god were discovered in the ruins of the old city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earth-goddess - a goddess of fertility and the earth
