thorny challenge

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔrni ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɔːni ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
a difficult or problematic situation, often requiring careful handling
difficult or problematic situation

Examples

Solving the city's traffic problems has proven to be a thorny challenge for urban planners.
Negotiating the contract terms turned into a thorny challenge for both parties involved.
Implementing new technology in an old system is often a thorny challenge for IT teams.
