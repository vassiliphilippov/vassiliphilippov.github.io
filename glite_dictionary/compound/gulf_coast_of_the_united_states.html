Gulf Coast of the United States

1. PROPN

/ ɡʌlf koʊst əv ði ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts / (AmE), / ɡʌlf kəʊst əv ði juːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the region of the United States comprising the states that border the Gulf of Mexico, specifically Alabama, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, and Texas
region along the US Gulf of Mexico coast

Examples

Hurricanes frequently impact the Gulf Coast of the United States during the summer and fall months.
Seafood is a significant part of the culture along the Gulf Coast of the United States.
Tourists flock to the beaches of the Gulf Coast of the United States for their warm climate and scenic views.
