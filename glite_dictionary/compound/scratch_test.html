scratch test

1. NOUN

/ skrætʃ tɛst / (AmE), / skrætʃ tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test for allergic sensitivity in which suspected allergens are applied to small scratches made in the skin
medical allergy sensitivity test
medical

Examples

My doctor performed a scratch test to find out which pollen I am allergic to.
The nurse explained how the scratch test could identify multiple allergens at once.
After the scratch test, several red spots appeared on his forearm, indicating allergies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin test - a general test for allergic reactions involving exposure to allergens on the skin
