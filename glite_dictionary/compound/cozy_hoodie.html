cozy hoodie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊzi ˈhʊdi / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi ˈhʊdi / (BrE)
a warm and comfortable sweatshirt with a hood, often worn for relaxation or casual wear
warm, comfortable hooded sweatshirt

Examples

She wrapped herself in her cozy hoodie and watched a movie on the couch.
A cozy hoodie is perfect for staying warm during outdoor evening events.
He gifted his sister a soft, cozy hoodie for her birthday.

Synset Examples

I love wearing my cozy hoodie on chilly days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoodie - a sweatshirt with a hood
