distribution list

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪstrəˈbjuʃən lɪst / (AmE), / ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people or addresses to whom a document, email, or other communication is sent
list of recipients for communications

Examples

Please add my email address to the distribution list for the monthly newsletter.
The meeting agenda was sent to the entire distribution list this morning.
She manages the distribution list to ensure all team members receive updates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailing list - a list of addresses to which the same information is sent
