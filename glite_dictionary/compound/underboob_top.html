underboob top

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚbuːb tɑp / (AmE), / ˈʌndəbuːb tɒp / (BrE)
A style of women's clothing, such as a crop top or bikini, designed to reveal the underside of the breasts.
top revealing underside of breasts

Examples

The new designer collection prominently features the underboob top in bold colors and patterns.
She felt confident and daring in her black underboob top at the summer festival.
Critics argue that the underboob top challenges traditional norms of modesty in fashion.

Synset Examples

The fashion trend of underboob tops is gaining popularity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crop top - A short top that exposes the stomach

Etymology

Blend of 'underboob' (exposed underside of the breast) and 'top' (upper garment).
