Kahane Chai

1. PROPN

/ kɑːˈhɑːnə kaɪ / (AmE), / kɑːˈhɑːnə kaɪ / (BrE)
a Jewish nationalist organization founded to combat antisemitism and advocate for the restoration of the biblical state of Israel, regarded as a terrorist organization by several governments
Jewish nationalist organization, considered terrorist by some

Examples

The Israeli government officially banned Kahane Chai as a terrorist group in the 1990s.
Several countries have listed Kahane Chai as a terrorist organization due to its violent activities.
Members of Kahane Chai advocated for the restoration of biblical Israel through controversial means.

Etymology

From Hebrew, literally 'Kahane Lives', referencing Rabbi Meir Kahane, its founder.
