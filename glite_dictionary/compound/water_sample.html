water sample

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌsæmpəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌsæmpəl / (BrE)
a quantity of water taken from a source for testing or analysis
sample of water for analysis

Examples

The scientist collected a water sample from the river to check for pollutants.
Each water sample was labeled and sent to the laboratory for microbial analysis.
If a water sample tests positive for bacteria, the local authorities are notified immediately.

Synset Examples

The bacillus was found in contaminated water samples.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sample - a small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like
