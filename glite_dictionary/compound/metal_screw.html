metal screw

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬l skru / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl skruː / (BrE)
A screw made of metal, typically used to fasten materials together in construction or mechanical work.
screw made of metal

Examples

The carpenter used a metal screw to secure the hinge to the door.
She purchased a pack of metal screws for her home improvement project.
To fix the handle, you'll need a short metal screw and a screwdriver.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wood screw - a screw designed to fasten wood, typically with a tapered body
