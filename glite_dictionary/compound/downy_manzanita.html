downy manzanita

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni mænzəˌniːtə / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni mænzəˌniːtə / (BrE)
an erect, openly branched shrub native to California, with young twigs that are covered in soft, woolly hairs
California shrub with woolly young twigs

Examples

The hillsides were dotted with downy manzanita shrubs in full bloom.
We identified the plant as downy manzanita due to its woolly young twigs.
Restoration projects often use downy manzanita for its ecological benefits.
