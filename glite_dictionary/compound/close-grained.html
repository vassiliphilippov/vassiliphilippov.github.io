close-grained

1. ADJ

/ kloʊs ˈɡreɪnd / (AmE), / kləʊs ˈɡreɪnd / (BrE)
having a dense or compact structure with small, closely packed grains or fibers, as in certain types of wood or stone.
having a dense or compact structure

Examples

This table is made from close-grained maple, which gives it a smooth finish.
The museum displayed a sculpture carved from close-grained marble.
Carpenters prefer close-grained wood for making instruments due to its strength and fine texture.

Synset Examples

close-grained birch
fine-grained rock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine-grained - having grains or particles that are small and closely packed
  • Antonym: coarse-grained - having a structure with large and loosely packed grains or fibers
