LinkedIn profile

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪŋkt.ˈɪn ˈproʊ.faɪl / (AmE), / ˌlɪŋkt.ˈɪn ˈprəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
a personal page on the LinkedIn platform that outlines an individual's professional experience, skills, and achievements
personal page on LinkedIn (professional network)

Examples

She updated her LinkedIn profile before applying for new jobs.
Recruiters often review your LinkedIn profile to assess your qualifications.
He added a professional photo to his LinkedIn profile to make it more appealing.

Synset Examples

She updated her LinkedIn profile with new skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional profile - a summary of one's professional experience and skills

Etymology

From the name of the website LinkedIn (launched 2003) + 'profile' (from Latin 'profilum').
