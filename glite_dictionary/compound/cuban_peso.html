Cuban peso

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bən ˈpeɪ.soʊ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bən ˈpeɪ.səʊ / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Cuba, equal to 100 centavos
the currency of Cuba

Examples

Tourists often exchange U.S. dollars for Cuban peso when visiting Havana.
The price of a cup of coffee is just a few Cuban pesos at local cafés.
The Cuban peso has seen several changes in value over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peso - basic monetary unit used in some Spanish-speaking countries
