chub mackerel

1. NOUN

/ tʃʌb ˈmækərəl / (AmE), / tʃʌb ˈmækərəl / (BrE)
a small species of mackerel (Scomber japonicus), found nearly worldwide, recognized for its commercial and culinary use
a small, widely distributed species of mackerel

Examples

Fishermen caught dozens of chub mackerel off the coast this morning.
The chub mackerel is often grilled or canned for consumption worldwide.
A school of chub mackerel moved quickly through the clear blue water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mackerel - a group of fast-swimming marine fish of the family Scombridae
