dance palace

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæns ˌpæləs / (AmE), / ˈdɑːns ˌpæləs / (BrE)
a large room or building used primarily for dancing, especially for social gatherings or events
large room or building for dancing

Examples

The old dance palace was restored and reopened to the public as a community center.
Hundreds of couples used to fill the dance palace every Saturday night.
The city is planning to build a new dance palace to host events and parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballroom - a large room for dancing, especially formal occasions
