Eastern silvery aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːstɚn ˈsɪlvəri ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈsɪlvəri ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant in the aster family, native to eastern North America, known for its narrow, silvery leaves and purple flowers (Symphyotrichum concolor).
species of North American flowering plant

Examples

The Eastern silvery aster is valued by gardeners for its showy purple blossoms in late summer.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the rare Eastern silvery aster.
Pollinators such as bees are attracted to the Eastern silvery aster when it blooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a genus of flowering plants with star-shaped flower heads
