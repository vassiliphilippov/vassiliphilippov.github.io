nut butter

1. NOUN

/ nʌt ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / nʌt ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
A spread made by grinding nuts into a creamy paste, sometimes including added oils or butter for texture.
spread made from ground nuts

Examples

She spread nut butter on her toast for a quick breakfast.
Many people prefer almond nut butter as a healthy alternative to peanut butter.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of your favorite nut butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peanut butter - spread made from ground peanuts
