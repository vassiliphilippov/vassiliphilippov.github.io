he-goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiː ˌɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈhiː ˌɡəʊt / (BrE)
A male goat.
male goat

Examples

The farmer separated the he-goat from the rest of the herd.
Legends often depict the he-goat as a wild and stubborn creature.
A mature he-goat typically grows impressive horns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billy-goat - male goat, especially when mature
  • Antonym: nanny-goat - female goat

Etymology

From 'he' (male) + 'goat'.
