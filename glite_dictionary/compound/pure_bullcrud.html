pure bullcrud

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈbʊlˌkrʌd / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈbʊlˌkrʌd / (BrE)
complete nonsense or foolishness; utter rubbish or falsehood, especially used to emphasize disbelief or contempt
complete nonsense; utter rubbish
informal

Examples

His excuse for being late was pure bullcrud and nobody believed him.
Don't give me that pure bullcrud—just tell me the truth.
Everyone saw through the politician's promises as pure bullcrud.

Synset Examples

She dismissed his nonsense as pure bullcrud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - vulgar slang for lies or nonsense
  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of 'bullshit,' using 'crud' as a milder substitute for 'shit'.
