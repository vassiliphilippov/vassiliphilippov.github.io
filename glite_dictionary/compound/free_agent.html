free agent

1. NOUN

/ ˌfriː ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌfriː ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
(sports) a professional athlete who is eligible to sign a contract with any team because they are not currently under contract to a specific team.
athlete available to sign with any team

Examples

After his contract ended, Tom became a free agent and signed with another team.
The basketball star will be a free agent next season, drawing interest from several teams.
Negotiations are ongoing as the free agent weighs offers from different clubs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contract player - an athlete under contract to a specific team

2. NOUN

/ friː ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / friː ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who acts independently, without being controlled by others, sometimes in an unpredictable or irresponsible manner
person acting independently or unpredictably

Examples

Ever since he left the company, John has been a free agent, following his own path.
In group work, it's hard to coordinate when one member is a free agent who won't stick to the plan.
She enjoyed being a free agent, making decisions without worrying about others' expectations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maverick - an independent-minded person
  • Synonym: loose cannon - a person who behaves unpredictably and can cause unintentional damage
