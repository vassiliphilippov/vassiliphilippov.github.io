Irish water spaniel

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪrɪʃ ˈwɔtər ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / ˌaɪrɪʃ ˈwɔːtə ˈspæniəl / (BrE)
A breed of large spaniel developed in Ireland, known for its curly liver-colored coat, distinctive topknot of long curls, and nearly hairless tail.
breed of large curly-coated Irish spaniel

Examples

The Irish water spaniel is known for its intelligence and playful personality.
She brought her Irish water spaniel to the dog park every Saturday for exercise.
Because of their waterproof coats, Irish water spaniels excel at retrieving in wet environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaniel - a breed of dog with long ears and a silky coat, originally bred for flushing game
