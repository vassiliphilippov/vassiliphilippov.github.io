one-armed bandit

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ɑrmd ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / wʌn ɑːmd ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
A slot machine, especially one in which pulling a lever activates the machine.
slot machine for gambling
slang american

Examples

Many tourists tried their luck at the one-armed bandit in the casino lobby.
He spent hours losing quarters to a stubborn old one-armed bandit.
The sound of dozens of one-armed bandits filled the air with jingles and bells.

Synset Examples

they spend hours and hours just playing the slots

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slot machine - a gaming machine operated by inserting coins and pulling a lever

Etymology

Originally American slang; so called from the single lever (the 'arm') operated by the player and the perception that the machine 'robs' players of their money.
