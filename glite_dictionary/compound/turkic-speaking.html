Turkic-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɜːrkɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in or using a Turkic language.
able to speak Turkic language(s)

Examples

Many of the Turkic-speaking peoples share similar cultural traditions.
The conference attracted scholars from several Turkic-speaking countries.
She grew up in a Turkic-speaking region of Central Asia.
