third hand

1. NOUN

/ θɝd hænd / (AmE), / θɜːd hand / (BrE)
information received from an indirect or unreliable source, not directly from those involved
indirect or unreliable information source

Examples

He dismissed the rumor because it came from a third hand.
The facts I have are only third hand, so please verify them yourself.
They doubted the story, knowing it was third hand information.

Synset Examples

He dismissed the rumor as it came from a third hand.
I heard about the event from a third hand.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: second hand - information received from someone who was not directly involved
