balsam capivi

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔl.səm kəˈpiː.vi / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.səm kəˈpiː.vi / (BrE)
an oleoresin obtained from the Copaifera tree, used in varnishes and ointments for its medicinal and preservative properties
an oleoresin used in varnishes and ointments

Examples

The artist mixed balsam capivi into his paints to improve their gloss and durability.
Some ointments use balsam capivi as a soothing ingredient for skin irritations.
When preparing traditional varnishes, craftsmen often add balsam capivi to enhance the finish.

Etymology

From Spanish 'bálsamo' and 'capivi', from Tupi 'kapiʔɨ' (the tree).
