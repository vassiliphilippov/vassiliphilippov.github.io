drool bucket

1. NOUN

/ ˈdruːl ˌbʌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈdruːl ˌbʌkɪt / (BrE)
Something extremely desirable or appetizing, or a person or animal that drools excessively.
something very desirable or someone who drools a lot
informal slang

Examples

Her chocolate cake was a real drool bucket at the party, with everyone eager for a slice.
That greasy pizza is such a drool bucket, I can't resist ordering it every weekend.
The bulldog is a notorious drool bucket after playing outside in the summer.

Synset Examples
