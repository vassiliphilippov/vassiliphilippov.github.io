American star grass

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A perennial grasslike plant native to North America, known for its star-shaped flowers or leaves.
a perennial American plant with star-shaped parts

Examples

The wetlands were filled with patches of American star grass blooming in the spring.
Gardeners often choose American star grass for its attractive, star-shaped flowers.
Scientists are studying how American star grass adapts to different soil conditions.
