patrol car

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊl kɑr / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊl kɑː / (BrE)
a police car used for patrolling streets, usually equipped with communication devices.
police car used for patrolling streets

Examples

The officer drove the patrol car slowly through the neighborhood, watching for any unusual activity.
Two patrol cars responded quickly to the scene of the robbery.
I saw a patrol car parked outside the grocery store last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police car - a car used by the police for transportation and patrol duties
