golden larch

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən lɑːrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən lɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a Chinese deciduous coniferous tree resembling a larch, with golden yellow leaves in autumn
Chinese conifer with golden leaves

Examples

The garden features a magnificent golden larch that turns bright yellow every autumn.
Many horticulturists admire the golden larch for its delicate, feathery foliage.
A row of golden larches lined the driveway, dazzling visitors with their vibrant color.
