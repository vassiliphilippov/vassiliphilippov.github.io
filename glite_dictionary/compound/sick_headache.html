sick headache

1. NOUN

/ sɪk ˈhɛdˌeɪk / (AmE), / sɪk ˈhed.eɪk / (BrE)
A severe, recurring headache often accompanied by nausea, now commonly known as a migraine.
severe recurring headache (migraine)
medical

Examples

She stayed home from work because her sick headache made it impossible to concentrate.
In the past, doctors often diagnosed migraines as sick headaches.
Recurring sick headaches affected her quality of life until she found effective medication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: migraine - a recurrent, throbbing headache often accompanied by nausea and visual disturbances
