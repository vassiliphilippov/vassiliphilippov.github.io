the good doctor

1. NOUN

/ ðə ɡʊd ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / ðə ɡʊd ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a respectful term used to refer to a physician, often implying admiration or affection
respectful term for a physician

Examples

Whenever anyone is unwell, they call the good doctor for advice.
Everyone in the town knows and respects the good doctor.
I have an appointment with the good doctor this afternoon.

Synset Examples

Everyone trusts the good doctor in our town.
He always has the right advice; he's the good doctor.
