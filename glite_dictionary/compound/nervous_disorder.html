nervous disorder

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrvəs dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition affecting the nervous system, resulting in abnormal functioning or symptoms.
disorder of the nervous system
medical

Examples

She was diagnosed with a nervous disorder after experiencing frequent muscle spasms and numbness.
Certain toxins can trigger a nervous disorder in both humans and animals.
Treatment for a nervous disorder often involves medication, therapy, and lifestyle changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurological disorder - disorder affecting the nervous system
