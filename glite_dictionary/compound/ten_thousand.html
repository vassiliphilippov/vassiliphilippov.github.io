ten thousand

1. NUM

/ tɛn ˈθaʊzənd / (AmE), / tɛn ˈθaʊzənd / (BrE)
The cardinal number equal to 10,000; the product of ten and one thousand.
the number 10,000

Examples

The stadium can hold up to ten thousand spectators for major events.
She donated ten thousand dollars to the local charity last year.
Over ten thousand people signed the petition in support of the new law.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thousand - the number 1,000
  • Degree Higher: hundred thousand - the number 100,000
