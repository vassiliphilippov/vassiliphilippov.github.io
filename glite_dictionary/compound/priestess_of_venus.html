priestess of Venus

1. NOUN

/ ˈpriːstɪs əv ˈviːnəs / (AmE), / ˈpriːstɪs əv ˈviːnəs / (BrE)
a female priest dedicated to the worship of Venus, the Roman goddess of love and beauty, especially as a symbolic or mythological figure
female priest of Venus in mythology

Examples

In art, she was often depicted as the priestess of Venus, surrounded by symbols of love.
The role of the priestess of Venus was central to the festival in ancient Rome.
Her costume as a priestess of Venus captivated the audience at the celebration.
