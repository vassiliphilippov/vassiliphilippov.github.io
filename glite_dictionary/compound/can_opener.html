can opener

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn ˌoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæn ˌəʊpənə / (BrE)
a device used for opening metal cans by cutting through the lid
device for opening metal cans

Examples

Please hand me the can opener so I can open this tin of beans.
We realized we had forgotten to pack a can opener on our camping trip.
This can opener is much easier to use than our old one.
