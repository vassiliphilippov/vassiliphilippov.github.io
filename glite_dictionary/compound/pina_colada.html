pina colada

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːnə kəˈlɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌpiːnə kəˈlɑːdə / (BrE)
A cocktail made with rum, pineapple juice, and coconut cream or coconut milk, usually served blended or shaken with ice.
rum, pineapple, and coconut cocktail

Examples

She ordered a pina colada at the beachside bar.
The bartender mixed a fresh pina colada with extra pineapple juice.
I have never tasted a pina colada before, but it smells delicious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daiquiri - a cocktail made with rum, citrus juice, and sugar
  • Synonym: margarita - a cocktail with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur

Etymology

From Spanish 'piña colada' meaning 'strained pineapple'.
