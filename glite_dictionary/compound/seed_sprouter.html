seed sprouter

1. NOUN

/ sid ˈspraʊtər / (AmE), / siːd ˈspraʊtə / (BrE)
A container or device designed to encourage the sprouting of seeds, typically for culinary or gardening purposes.
device for sprouting seeds

Examples

I keep my seed sprouter on the kitchen windowsill for easy access.
With a seed sprouter, you can enjoy fresh sprouts all year round.
After soaking the beans overnight, she placed them in the seed sprouter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprouter - a device or person that sprout seeds or other materials
