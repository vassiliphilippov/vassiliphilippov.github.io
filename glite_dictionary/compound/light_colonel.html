light colonel

1. NOUN

/ laɪt kəˈloʊnəl / (AmE), / laɪt kəˈləʊnəl / (BrE)
A commissioned officer in the United States Army, Air Force, or Marines ranking above major and below colonel; an informal term for lieutenant colonel.
informal term for lieutenant colonel
informal american

Examples

After serving twenty years, he finally achieved the rank of light colonel in the Air Force.
Many officers refer to a lieutenant colonel as a light colonel in informal conversation.
The promotion ceremony for the new light colonel was attended by his family and colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lieutenant colonel - officer rank above major and below colonel
  • Degree Higher: colonel - higher military officer rank, above lieutenant colonel
  • Degree Lower: major - officer rank below lieutenant colonel

Etymology

Colloquial shortening and alteration of 'lieutenant colonel'.
