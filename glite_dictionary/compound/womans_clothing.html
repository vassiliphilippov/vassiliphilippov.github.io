woman's clothing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmənz ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənz ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
clothing designed specifically for women to wear.
clothing for women

Examples

The store on Main Street specializes in woman's clothing and accessories.
Many designers release new woman's clothing collections every season.
She donated several bags of old woman's clothing to charity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: men's clothing - clothing designed for men to wear
