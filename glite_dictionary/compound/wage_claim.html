wage claim

1. NOUN

/ weɪdʒ kleɪm / (AmE), / weɪdʒ kleɪm / (BrE)
an official request or demand by workers or their union representatives for an increase in pay
demand for higher wages by workers or unions

Examples

The union filed a wage claim with management after negotiations stalled.
Teachers across the country have put forward a wage claim for better pay.
Management rejected the initial wage claim, proposing a lower increase instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay claim - a demand for wage increase made by employees or their unions
  • Antonym: wage offer - a pay increase proposed by management during negotiations
