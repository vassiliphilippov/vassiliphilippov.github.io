yeppity attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛpɪti ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈjɛpɪti ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude or demeanor that is enthusiastic, lively, and upbeat.
enthusiastic and lively demeanor
informal

Examples

Her yeppity attitude made the event more enjoyable.
Despite the early hour, Mark arrived at work with his usual yeppity attitude.
It was her yeppity attitude that inspired the whole team to stay positive.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy

Etymology

Formed from 'yep' (a variant of 'yes', expressing affirmation) plus '-ity' and 'attitude'.
