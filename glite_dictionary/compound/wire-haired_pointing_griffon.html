wire-haired pointing griffon

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪərˌhɛrd ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ɡrɪˈfɑn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌheəd ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ɡrɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized gun dog with a long head, a downy undercoat, and a harsh wiry outer coat, developed mainly in France
a breed of medium-sized, wiry-coated gun dog

Examples

The wire-haired pointing griffon is prized for its keen sense of smell during bird hunts.
We adopted a wire-haired pointing griffon because of its friendly temperament and intelligence.
Her wire-haired pointing griffon loves to swim and needs plenty of exercise every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: griffon - wire-coated breed of dog

Etymology

From French *griffon* (a type of wire-haired dog); 'pointing' refers to its use as a hunting dog that points game.
