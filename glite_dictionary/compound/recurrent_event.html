recurrent event

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɜːrənt ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌrənt ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an event that happens repeatedly at intervals over a period of time
an event that recurs over time

Examples

The study tracked each recurrent event to analyze overall health trends.
Power outages became a recurrent event in the city during the storm season.
In statistics, a recurrent event is often modeled using specific probability techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recurrence - the fact of occurring again
  • Antonym: one-off event - an event that occurs only once
