recording label

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˈleɪbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˈleɪbəl / (BrE)
a company that produces and markets musical recordings under a specific trade name
company producing and marketing music recordings

Examples

She signed her first contract with a major recording label at the age of twenty.
Many artists prefer to work with an independent recording label for greater creative control.
The recording label announced a new album release date for the popular band.

Synset Examples

the artists and repertoire department of a recording label is responsible for finding new talent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record company - a company that records and sells music
  • Degree Lower: independent label - a recording label not affiliated with major corporations
  • Degree Higher: major label - a large, dominant recording label in the music industry
