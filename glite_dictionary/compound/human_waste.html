human waste

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən weɪst / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən weɪst / (BrE)
the waste substances excreted from the human body, such as urine and feces
bodily excretions from humans

Examples

The lack of proper sanitation led to a build-up of human waste in the area.
Modern sewage systems are designed to safely dispose of human waste.
Exposure to untreated human waste can cause serious health problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewage - waste water and excrement conveyed in sewers
  • Synonym: excrement - solid waste matter discharged from the body
  • Degree Lower: animal waste - bodily waste excreted by animals
