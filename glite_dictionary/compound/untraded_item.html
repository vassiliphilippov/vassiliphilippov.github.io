untraded item

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈtreɪdɪd ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪdɪd ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
a good or article that has not been exchanged, bought, or sold
item not yet sold or traded

Examples

The manager noticed several untraded items taking up space in the warehouse.
Some untraded items were still listed in the end-of-year inventory report.
The auction included rare, previously untraded items from the collection.

Synset Examples

They found several untraded items in the inventory.
