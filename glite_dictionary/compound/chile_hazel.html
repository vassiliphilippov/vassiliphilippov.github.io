Chile hazel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪli ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
A Chilean shrub that produces coral-red fruit with an edible seed similar to a hazelnut.
Chilean shrub with hazelnut-like edible seeds

Examples

The edible seeds of the Chile hazel are prized for their unique flavor.
Botanists travel to South America to study the Chile hazel and its distinctive fruit.
The Chile hazel thrives in the temperate regions of central Chile.
