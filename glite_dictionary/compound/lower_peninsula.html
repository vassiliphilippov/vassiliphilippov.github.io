Lower Peninsula

1. PROPN

/ ˈloʊ.ɚ pəˈnɪn.sjʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə pəˈnɪn.sjʊ.lə / (BrE)
the southern of the two main peninsulas of the U.S. state of Michigan, located between Lake Michigan and Lake Huron
southern peninsula of Michigan, USA
american

Examples

Many people in Michigan live in the Lower Peninsula near Detroit.
The Mackinac Bridge connects the Lower Peninsula to the Upper Peninsula of Michigan.
Tourists often explore the lakes and forests of Michigan's Lower Peninsula.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Upper Peninsula - the northern of Michigan's two main peninsulas
