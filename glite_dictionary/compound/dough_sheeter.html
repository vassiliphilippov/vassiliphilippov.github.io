dough sheeter

1. NOUN

/ doʊ ˈʃitər / (AmE), / dəʊ ˈʃiːtə / (BrE)
a machine used to roll out dough into uniform sheets of a desired thickness, typically in commercial or industrial bakeries
machine that presses dough into sheets

Examples

The bakery invested in a new dough sheeter to improve production speed and consistency.
A dough sheeter is essential for making large batches of pastry with uniform thickness.
She carefully fed the pastry through the dough sheeter to prepare it for the next step.

Synset Examples

Most dough sheeters can handle a wide variety of dough depending on the machine manufacturer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dough roller - a device or machine used to flatten dough
  • Degree Lower: rolling pin - a kitchen tool used to manually roll out dough
