Battle of Omdurman

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˌɑm.dɚˈmæn / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ˌɒm.dəˈmæn / (BrE)
a battle fought in 1898 in which British and Egyptian forces under General Kitchener defeated the Sudanese army, marking the end of the Mahdist state in Sudan
1898 British-Egyptian victory over Sudanese forces

Examples

The Battle of Omdurman was a decisive moment in British colonial history.
Many historians analyze the tactics used at the Battle of Omdurman.
The outcome of the Battle of Omdurman solidified British control over Sudan.
