Republic of Paraguay

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌpærəˈgwaɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌparəˈɡwaɪ / (BrE)
the official name of Paraguay, a landlocked country in south central South America, which gained independence from Spain in 1811
official name of Paraguay (country in South America)

Examples

The Republic of Paraguay is bordered by Argentina, Brazil, and Bolivia.
Spanish is one of the official languages of the Republic of Paraguay.
The Republic of Paraguay declared its independence from Spain in 1811.
