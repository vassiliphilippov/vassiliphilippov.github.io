audio quality

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːdioʊ ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
the clarity, fidelity, and overall standard of sound produced or reproduced by an audio device or recording
clarity and fidelity of sound

Examples

The new headphones are known for their excellent audio quality even at high volumes.
Many music enthusiasts invest in high-end speakers to improve their audio quality.
Streaming services often compete by offering better audio quality for their subscribers.

Synset Examples

He preferred TDK for its reliability in audio quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sound quality - the standard of clarity and fidelity in sound
