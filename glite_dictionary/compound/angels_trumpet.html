angel's trumpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəlz ˌtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˌtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a South American plant, especially of the genus Brugmansia, cultivated for its large, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers
ornamental plant with trumpet-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the sweet scent of angel's trumpet blooming in the evening air.
She bought an angel's trumpet to add a dramatic touch to her backyard collection.
Be careful; all parts of the angel's trumpet plant are highly toxic if ingested.
