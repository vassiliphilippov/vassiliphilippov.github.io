twenty-five/eight

1. ADV

/ ˌtwɛnti faɪv ˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti faɪv ˈeɪt / (BrE)
(informal) Working extremely hard or nonstop; extremely busy or continuously occupied, beyond what is considered normal.
working extremely hard or nonstop
informal slang

Examples

Lately, she's been working twenty-five/eight to meet all her deadlines.
Running a new business means you're hustling twenty-five/eight just to keep up.
I'm so twenty-five/eight these days, I barely have time for myself.

Synset Examples

She’s working twenty-five/eight to achieve her goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-four/seven - all the time; constantly; 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

Etymology

A play on 'twenty-four/seven', meaning 24 hours a day, 7 days a week; 'twenty-five/eight' exaggerates the concept of nonstop activity.
