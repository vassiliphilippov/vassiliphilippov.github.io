Sparrow Unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈspæroʊ ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈspærəʊ ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a clandestine hit squad of the New People's Army in the Philippines, active especially during the 1980s, known for targeted assassinations
NPA hit squad in the Philippines

Examples

The Sparrow Unit became infamous for its swift and targeted attacks on government officials.
Many members of the Sparrow Unit operated undercover in urban areas during the conflict.
Reports indicated that a Sparrow Unit was responsible for the assassination in Manila.

Etymology

Named after the English word 'sparrow', referring to small birds known for their elusiveness, likening the members' stealth and agility.
