outside job

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtsaɪd dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job or work position taken in addition to one's main employment, typically to earn extra income
a job in addition to main employment

Examples

She manages an outside job while studying full-time.
He took an outside job to earn extra money.
Many teachers have an outside job to help cover their expenses.

Synset Examples
