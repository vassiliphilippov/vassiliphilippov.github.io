motion-picture photography

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊʃən ˌpɪktʃɚ fəˈtɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˌpɪktʃə fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the process or art of capturing images for a motion picture or film
film-making technique

Examples

Advances in motion-picture photography have transformed how stories are told on screen.
She studied motion-picture photography at the film academy to pursue a career behind the camera.
Motion-picture photography requires a deep understanding of lighting, composition, and movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinematography - the art of photographing and lighting motion pictures
  • Degree Lower: photography - the art or process of taking and processing photographs
