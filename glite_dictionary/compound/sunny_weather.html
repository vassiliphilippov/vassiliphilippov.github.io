sunny weather

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌni ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌni ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
Weather that is bright and full of sunshine, without clouds or rain.
bright, sun-filled weather

Examples

The kids were excited to play outside in the sunny weather.
Tourists flock to the city in summer to enjoy its sunny weather.
After a week of rain, the sunny weather was a welcome change.

Synset Examples

Cali is known for its beautiful beaches and sunny weather.
The forecast predicts sunny weather for the weekend.
We enjoyed the sunny weather while visiting Aridzona.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cloudy weather - weather with many clouds and little or no sunshine
  • Antonym: rainy weather - weather characterized by rain
  • Synonym: sunshine - direct sunlight or brightness
