coffee shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ʃɒp / (BrE)
A small restaurant that serves coffee and other drinks, as well as light snacks or meals.
small restaurant serving coffee and snacks

Examples

She met her friend at the local coffee shop after work.
The coffee shop was full of people reading and studying.
My favorite coffee shop serves homemade pastries and strong espresso.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - small restaurant serving coffee and light refreshments

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ʃɒp / (BrE)
A small, informal restaurant or café that primarily serves coffee, tea, and light refreshments.
place serving coffee and light food

Examples

We spent the afternoon chatting in the coffee shop downtown.
There is a new coffee shop opening next to the library this weekend.
She likes to study at her favorite coffee shop because it's quiet and cozy.
