packing needle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋ ˌnidl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋ ˌniːdl̩ / (BrE)
a large, strong needle used for sewing up canvas packages or heavy materials, especially in packing and shipping
large needle for sewing canvas packages

Examples

The sailor reached for a packing needle to repair the torn sail.
Canvas sacks were stitched closed using a sturdy packing needle.
He kept a spare packing needle in his toolbox for heavy-duty sewing tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle - a small slender tool with a sharp point for sewing
