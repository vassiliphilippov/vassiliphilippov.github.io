inter-service support

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntər ˈsɜrvɪs səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˌɪntə ˈsɜːvɪs səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
logistic or administrative support provided by one branch of the military to another branch
logistical or administrative aid between military branches

Examples

The navy relied on inter-service support to receive essential supplies from the air force.
Effective inter-service support is crucial during joint military operations.
The army and marines established clear channels for inter-service support to coordinate logistics.
