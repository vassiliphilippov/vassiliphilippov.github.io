booze cruising

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːz ˌkruːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbuːz ˌkruːzɪŋ / (BrE)
Engaging in recreational travel or social activity while drinking alcoholic beverages, especially in a casual, group setting.
drinking alcohol while traveling/socializing
informal slang

Examples

We plan to spend the afternoon booze cruising on the lake with our friends.
After a long week, they went booze cruising through the city in their minivan.
Some people think booze cruising is risky, but others see it as harmless fun.

Synset Examples

Booze cruising is a favorite summer activity for us.
We spent the day booze cruising along the river.
They enjoyed booze cruising at the beach all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering with entertainment and refreshments

Etymology

From 'booze' (slang for alcohol) + 'cruising' (traveling for pleasure).
