literate person

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əˌreɪt ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərət ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has the ability to read and write
person able to read and write

Examples

Becoming a literate person can open up many opportunities in life.
The organization works to ensure that every child becomes a literate person.
In some areas, only half the population is considered a literate person.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illiterate person - a person who cannot read or write
