soda niter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
a white crystalline compound (sodium nitrate, NaNO3) used especially as a fertilizer and in making explosives
sodium nitrate used in fertilizers and explosives

Examples

The company imports soda niter for use in agricultural fertilizers.
Some types of gunpowder contain soda niter as an oxidizing agent.
Because of its high solubility, soda niter dissolves quickly in water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sodium nitrate - a white crystalline compound used as a fertilizer and in explosives

Etymology

From 'soda' (referring to sodium) and 'niter' (from Greek nitron, meaning native sodium carbonate).
