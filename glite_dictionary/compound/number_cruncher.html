number cruncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚ ˌkrʌntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə ˌkrʌntʃə / (BrE)
a computer capable of performing a large number of mathematical calculations very quickly
high-speed calculating computer

Examples

The laboratory invested in a powerful number cruncher to handle its simulations.
In the 1980s, only government agencies could afford a number cruncher of that scale.
The new model is a true number cruncher, processing millions of calculations per second.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supercomputer - an extremely fast computer used for complex scientific calculations

2. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbɚ ˌkrʌntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə ˌkrʌntʃə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at or employed to perform complex and extensive mathematical or statistical calculations, especially using computers
person skilled at complex calculations

Examples

As a number cruncher, she quickly identified the error in the financial report.
The company hired a number cruncher to help analyze the massive data set.
He's more of a creative thinker than a number cruncher in his approach to business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statistician - an expert in or compiler of statistics
  • Synonym: mathematician - a person who is an expert in mathematics
  • Antonym: layperson - a person without specialized knowledge in a particular subject
