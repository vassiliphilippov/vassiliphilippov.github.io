tangerine tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌtændʒəˈrin tri / (AmE), / ˌtændʒəˈriːn triː / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree that produces tangerines; a type of mandarin orange tree cultivated for its sweet, bright orange fruit
tree that produces tangerine fruit

Examples

The tangerine tree in our backyard is heavy with ripe fruit this year.
Children love picking tangerines straight from the tangerine tree in the orchard.
A healthy tangerine tree needs plenty of sunlight and well-drained soil to thrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mandarin tree - tree that produces mandarin oranges
