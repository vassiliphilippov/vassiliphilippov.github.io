chi chi man

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiː ˌtʃiː mæn / (AmE), / ˈtʃiː ˌtʃiː man / (BrE)
(slang, often derogatory) A flamboyant or effeminate man, especially one perceived as affected; sometimes used as an insult.
flamboyant or effeminate man (often derogatory)
slang offensive

Examples

Some people use chi chi man as a derogatory term in Caribbean communities.
He was unfairly labeled a chi chi man because of his expressive fashion sense.
The song lyrics featured the controversial phrase chi chi man, sparking protests.

Synset Examples

He always acts like a chi chi man at parties.
Stop being a chi chi man and just enjoy yourself.

Etymology

Jamaican English, likely from 'chi chi' meaning 'effeminate', influenced by Caribbean slang.
