you know what

1. X

/ juː noʊ wʌt / (AmE), / juː nəʊ wɒt / (BrE)
Used to introduce a remark, draw attention to a new topic, or emphasize a point in conversation.
conversational phrase to introduce or emphasize a point
informal

Examples

You know what? I think it's time for a change.
You know what, let's just order pizza instead of cooking.
You know what? I actually enjoyed the movie more than I expected.

Synset Examples

You know what? I think we should try again.
