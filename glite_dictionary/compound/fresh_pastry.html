fresh pastry

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ ˈpeɪstri / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˈpeɪstri / (BrE)
A pastry that has been recently baked and is not stale.
recently baked pastry

Examples

Every morning, the bakery displays trays filled with fresh pastry to attract customers.
He enjoyed a cup of coffee with a warm, fresh pastry on the side.
Nothing beats the aroma of fresh pastry coming straight out of the oven.

Synset Examples

The coffee shop served an ool of fresh pastries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry - a dough of flour, water, and shortening that may be baked and used as a base or covering for pies, tarts, etc.
