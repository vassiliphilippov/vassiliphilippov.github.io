crime syndicate

1. NOUN

/ kraɪm ˈsɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / kraɪm ˈsɪndɪkət / (BrE)
an organized group of criminals who work together to carry out illegal activities
organized group of criminals

Examples

The authorities launched a major operation to dismantle the crime syndicate operating in the city.
He was arrested for his involvement with a notorious crime syndicate that was smuggling drugs across borders.
The documentary explores how the crime syndicate infiltrated legitimate businesses to launder money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mafia - an organized international body of criminals operating originally in Sicily
  • Synonym: cartel - a group of independent organizations formed to limit competition by controlling production and distribution
