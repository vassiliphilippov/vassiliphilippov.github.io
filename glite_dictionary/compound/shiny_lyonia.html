shiny lyonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaɪni laɪˈoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪni laɪˈəʊniə / (BrE)
A showy evergreen shrub of the southeastern United States with shiny leaves, angled branches, and clusters of pink to reddish, umbel-like flowers.
evergreen shrub with shiny leaves and pink flowers

Examples

The shiny lyonia adds year-round interest to gardens in the southeastern United States.
Clusters of pink flowers make the shiny lyonia a popular choice for ornamental planting.
Botanists value the shiny lyonia for its glossy foliage and unique branching.
