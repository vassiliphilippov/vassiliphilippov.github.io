emotional cripple

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənl ˈkrɪpəl / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl ˈkrɪpəl / (BrE)
A person who is severely limited in emotional development or expression, often due to psychological issues or past trauma.
person hindered by emotional issues
offensive

Examples

After his parents' divorce, he struggled for years as an emotional cripple.
She feared becoming an emotional cripple if she didn't deal with her childhood trauma.
The novel tells the story of an emotional cripple attempting to rebuild his life.

Synset Examples

After the breakup, he felt like an emotional cripple.
She didn't want to be an emotional cripple anymore.
Therapy helped him recover from being an emotional cripple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally stunted - lacking emotional maturity or growth
  • Antonym: well-adjusted - emotionally healthy and stable
