corner cabinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnər ˈkæbɪnət / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnə ˈkæbɪnət / (BrE)
A cabinet with a triangular or angled design made to fit into the corner of a room.
triangular cabinet for room corners

Examples

They placed a beautiful vase on the corner cabinet in the dining room.
The antique corner cabinet was perfect for displaying her collection of china.
We needed extra storage space, so we bought a corner cabinet for the kitchen.
