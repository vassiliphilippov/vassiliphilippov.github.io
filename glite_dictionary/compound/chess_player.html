chess player

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛs ˈpleɪ.ər / (AmE), / tʃɛs ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of chess, either recreationally or professionally
person who plays chess

Examples

She is a skilled chess player who has won several local tournaments.
Many professional chess players study famous matches to improve their strategies.
As a beginner chess player, he enjoys learning new tactics and openings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: player - person who participates in a game or sport
