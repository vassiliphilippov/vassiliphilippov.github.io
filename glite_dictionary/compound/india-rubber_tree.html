India-rubber tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiə ˈrʌbər triː / (AmE), / ˈɪndiə ˈrʌbə triː / (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree (Ficus elastica) often grown as a houseplant, and used as a source of natural rubber, especially Assam rubber.
tropical tree, source of natural rubber

Examples

The India-rubber tree is a popular houseplant known for its glossy leaves and resilience.
Latex extracted from the India-rubber tree was once an important source of natural rubber.
They kept their India-rubber tree near the window so it could get plenty of sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubber tree - a tree yielding latex from which rubber is made

Etymology

Named for 'India-rubber', an early term for natural rubber, due to the tree's latex.
