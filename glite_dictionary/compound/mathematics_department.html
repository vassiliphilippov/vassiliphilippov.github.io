mathematics department

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæθəˌmætɪks dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌmæθəˈmætɪks dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of a college or university responsible for teaching and research in mathematics
university division focusing on mathematics

Examples

She has been hired as a lecturer in the mathematics department at the university.
The mathematics department offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate courses.
Research from our mathematics department was recently published in an international journal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of a larger organization such as a university
