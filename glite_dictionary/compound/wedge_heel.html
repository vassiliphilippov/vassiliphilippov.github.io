wedge heel

1. NOUN

/ wɛdʒ hil / (AmE), / wɛdʒ hiːl / (BrE)
a heel that is an extension of the sole, forming a solid wedge-shaped base to the shoe
solid wedge-shaped shoe heel

Examples

She wore her favorite sandals with a wedge heel to the garden party.
The store offers boots with a wedge heel for extra comfort and style.
A wedge heel provides more stability than a traditional high heel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stiletto heel - a thin, high shoe heel, usually for women's shoes
