ping-pong ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋ pɑŋ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ pɒŋ bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, lightweight, hollow plastic ball used in the game of table tennis
light plastic ball for table tennis

Examples

She bought a new set of ping-pong balls for the tournament.
A ping-pong ball bounced off the table and rolled under the sofa.
It's amazing how high a ping-pong ball can bounce when dropped from a height.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table tennis ball - ball used in table tennis

Etymology

From 'ping-pong', a trademarked name for table tennis, with 'ball'.
