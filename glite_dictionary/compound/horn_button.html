horn button

1. NOUN

/ hɔrn ˈbʌtən / (AmE), / hɔːn ˈbʌtn̩ / (BrE)
a button in a vehicle that you press to sound the horn
button to sound a vehicle's horn

Examples

She pressed the horn button to alert the cyclist in front of her car.
The horn button is usually located in the center of the steering wheel.
He realized the horn button was broken when he tried to warn the other driver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn - device that makes a loud warning sound in a vehicle
