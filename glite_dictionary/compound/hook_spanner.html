hook spanner

1. NOUN

/ hʊk ˈspænər / (AmE), / hʊk ˈspænə / (BrE)
a type of wrench with a hooked end designed to engage a slot or notch in a nut or bolt head
wrench with a hooked end for nuts/bolts

Examples

The mechanic used a hook spanner to tighten the locking ring.
A hook spanner is essential for working with certain circular nuts.
Before starting the repair, make sure you have the correct size hook spanner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spanner - a tool for turning bolts and nuts
  • Synonym: wrench - a tool used for gripping and turning objects
