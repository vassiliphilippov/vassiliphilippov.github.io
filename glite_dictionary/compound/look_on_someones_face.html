look on someone's face

1. NOUN

/ lʊk ɑn ˈsʌmwʌnz feɪs / (AmE), / lʊk ɒn ˈsʌmwənz feɪs / (BrE)
an expression or appearance on a person's face that reveals their feelings or mood
facial expression showing feelings

Examples

There was a worried look on his face as he read the letter.
The delight was clear from the look on her face when she opened the gift.
You could tell he was upset by the look on his face.

Synset Examples

I knew she was unwell by the look on her face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - a look on someone's face showing feeling or thought
  • Antonym: poker face - an expression showing no emotion
