photoplate making

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtoʊˌpleɪt ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌpleɪt ˈmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of producing printing plates or cylinders using photographic methods.
making printing plates by photographic means

Examples

Advances in photoplate making greatly increased the speed and quality of mass printing.
The old method was replaced by photoplate making in the late nineteenth century.
Training in photoplate making is essential for workers in the traditional printing industry.

Synset Examples

photomechanics revolutionized the practice of printing

Etymology

From 'photo-' (relating to photography) + 'plate making'.
