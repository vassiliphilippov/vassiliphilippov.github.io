mountain lion

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən ˌlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn ˌlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a large wild cat (Puma concolor) of North and South America, with a plain tawny coat and a long tail, also known as a cougar or puma
large American wild cat

Examples

A mountain lion was spotted near the hiking trail early this morning.
The mountain lion is also known as a cougar or puma in different regions.
Park rangers warn visitors to avoid surprising a mountain lion while exploring the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cougar - another name for the mountain lion
  • Synonym: puma - another common name for the mountain lion
