trailhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlˌhɛd / (BrE)
the place where a trail begins, especially one marked as the starting point for hikers or travelers
the marked start of a trail

Examples

We met at the trailhead before starting our hike up the mountain.
The park map shows the location of every trailhead for easy access.
A sign at the trailhead gives information about the route and safety guidelines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trail - a path or track, especially for walking or hiking
