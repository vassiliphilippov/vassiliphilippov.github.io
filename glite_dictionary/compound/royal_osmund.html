royal osmund

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl ˈɑzmənd / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl ˈɒzmənd / (BrE)
a large fern with deeply rooted rhizomes and upright, bipinnate compound tufted fronds, found worldwide
large widely distributed fern

Examples

The royal osmund thrives in moist, shady woodland habitats across several continents.
Gardeners prize the royal osmund for its impressive, feather-like fronds.
We spotted a stand of royal osmund near the banks of the river while hiking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: royal fern - another common name for royal osmund (Osmunda regalis)
