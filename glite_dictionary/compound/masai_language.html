Masai language

1. NOUN

/ məˈsaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈsaɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Nilotic language spoken by the Masai people in Kenya and northern Tanzania
language of the Masai people

Examples

Many children in Kenya grow up speaking both Swahili and Masai language.
Linguists have studied the Masai language to understand its unique grammar.
He translated the health brochure into Masai language for the local villagers.

Etymology

Masai, from the name of the Maasai ethnic group, themselves calling their language 'Maa'.
