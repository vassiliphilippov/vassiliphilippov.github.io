Ouija board

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːdʒə bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwiːdʒə bɔːd / (BrE)
A board marked with letters of the alphabet and other symbols, used with a planchette to spell out messages, especially in attempts to communicate with spirits.
board used for spirit communication

Examples

She claimed the Ouija board spelled out messages from her late grandmother.
At the sleepover, the girls nervously gathered around the Ouija board by candlelight.
Many people are skeptical about whether a Ouija board can actually communicate with spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: planchette - small, movable indicator used on a Ouija board

Etymology

From French and German words for 'yes' (oui and ja); 'Ouija' was trademarked in the 1890s.
