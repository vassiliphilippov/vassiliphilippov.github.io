beyond doubt

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd daʊt / (AmE), / bɪˈjɒnd daʊt / (BrE)
without any possibility of being doubted; unquestionably certain.
certainly; without doubt

Examples

Her honesty is beyond doubt after years of loyal service.
The results of the experiment proved the theory beyond doubt.
It is beyond doubt that climate change is affecting global weather patterns.

Synset Examples

it was immediately and indubitably apparent that I had interrupted a scene of lovers
his guilt was established beyond a shadow of a doubt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: doubtful - uncertain; not definite

2. ADV

/ biˈɑnd ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / bɪˈɒnd ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
So certain or obvious that it cannot reasonably be questioned or disputed.
certain; undeniable

Examples

Her honesty was beyond doubt after the investigation cleared her name.
It is beyond doubt that climate change is affecting global weather patterns.
The authenticity of the painting was beyond doubt once experts verified its origins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undeniable - impossible to deny or dispute
  • Antonym: doubtful - not certain; full of doubt
