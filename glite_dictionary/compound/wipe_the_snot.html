to wipe the snot

1. VERB

/ waɪp ðə snɑt / (AmE), / waɪp ðə snɒt / (BrE)
to remove nasal mucus by wiping, usually from a person's or one's own nose
remove nasal mucus by wiping
informal

Examples

He quickly grabbed a tissue to wipe the snot from his son's nose.
It's important to wipe the snot away when you catch a cold.
She discreetly used her sleeve to wipe the snot during the meeting.

Synset Examples

She wiped the snot from her nose with a tissue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wipe - to clean by rubbing with a cloth or one's hand
