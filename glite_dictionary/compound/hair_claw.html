hair claw

1. NOUN

/ hɛr klɔ / (AmE), / heə klɔː / (BrE)
a hair clip with interlocking teeth connected by a spring, used to hold hair in place
hair clip with interlocking teeth

Examples

She used a hair claw to quickly pull her hair up before heading out.
The large hair claw held even her thick hair securely in place.
I bought a new tortoiseshell hair claw to match my outfit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair clip - a device for holding hair in place
