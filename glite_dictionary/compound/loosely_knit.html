loosely knit

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːsli nɪt / (AmE), / ˈluːsli nɪt / (BrE)
(of a group or organization) having only weak, distant, or informal connections among members or parts.
lacking close connections

Examples

The club was a loosely knit association of music lovers who met irregularly.
They formed a loosely knit coalition to advocate for environmental change.
A loosely knit network of volunteers helped organize the community event.

Synset Examples

a loosely knit group

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tightly knit - having strong, close connections within a group
  • Synonym: cohesive - closely united or integrated
