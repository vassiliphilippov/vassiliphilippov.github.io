social lion

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a person who is very much sought after in fashionable social circles; a prominent social celebrity
prominent, highly sought social celebrity

Examples

The party was filled with social lions eager to see and be seen.
After her latest film, she became the most sought-after social lion in the city.
He was once a social lion, but now he prefers solitude over the spotlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment or sport
  • Antonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence

Etymology

From 'social' (relating to society) + 'lion' (metaphor for someone notable or celebrated), popularized in 19th-century English.
