projection screen

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʒɛk.ʃən skrin / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛk.ʃən skriːn / (BrE)
a white or silvered surface onto which images or videos are projected for viewing, typically used in cinemas, classrooms, and presentations
surface for projecting images or videos

Examples

The presentation was displayed on a large projection screen at the front of the auditorium.
We set up a portable projection screen in the backyard for movie night.
The teacher pulled down the projection screen before starting the slideshow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screen - a flat surface onto which images are projected or displayed
