coco plum tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊkoʊ ˈplʌm tri / (AmE), / ˌkəʊkəʊ ˈplʌm triː / (BrE)
a small tropical American tree that produces edible plumlike fruit, commonly found near coastal areas
tropical tree with edible plumlike fruit

Examples

The coco plum tree thrives in sandy soils along Florida's coastlines.
Many birds are attracted to the coco plum tree because of its sweet fruit.
We planted a coco plum tree in our garden to enjoy its shade and fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocoplum - the fruit or the tree of the coco plum
