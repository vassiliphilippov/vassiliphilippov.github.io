proud lezzer

1. NOUN

/ praʊd ˈlɛzər / (AmE), / praʊd ˈlɛzə / (BrE)
A slang and often offensive term for a lesbian who is unapologetic and confident about her sexuality.
confident lesbian (slang/offensive)
slang offensive british

Examples

Despite the criticism, she remained a proud lezzer and supported other women to embrace their identities.
The documentary featured several proud lezzers sharing their experiences of coming out.
Calling herself a proud lezzer, she challenged stereotypes and stood up for LGBTQ+ rights.

Synset Examples

She's a proud lezzer and advocates for equality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesbian - A woman who is attracted to other women
  • Synonym: lezzer - Offensive slang term for a lesbian

Etymology

Lezzer: British slang alteration of 'lesbian,' often used disparagingly.
