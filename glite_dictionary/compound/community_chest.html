community chest

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti tʃɛst / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti tʃɛst / (BrE)
an organization or fund supported by contributions from community members, used to finance local social welfare services and charities
local charitable fund or organization

Examples

The local community chest raised thousands of dollars for families in need after the hurricane.
Each year, businesses donate generously to the community chest to support vital social programs.
Volunteers from the community chest organize events that benefit the elderly in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charity - organization that gives money or help to people in need

Etymology

First used in the early 20th century in the United States for collective charitable funds.
