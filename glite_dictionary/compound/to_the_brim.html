to the brim

1. ADV

/ tə ðə brɪm / (AmE), / tə ðə brɪm / (BrE)
completely full; filled to the very top edge or capacity.
completely full; filled to the top

Examples

She poured water to the brim of the glass, careful not to spill any.
The stadium was packed to the brim for the championship game.
The jar was filled to the brim with homemade cookies.

Synset Examples

He packed his suitcase to the brim for the trip.
The cup was filled to the brim with coffee.
He filled the basket to the brim with fresh fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full - containing as much as possible
  • Degree Higher: overflowing - flowing over the top because too full
