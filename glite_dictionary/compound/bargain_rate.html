bargain rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrɡən reɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡɪn reɪt / (BrE)
a price lower than the usual or standard rate, especially one offered as a special deal or discount
a price below the standard rate

Examples

We managed to book the hotel room at a bargain rate during the off-season.
The tour operator offered a limited number of tickets at a special bargain rate.
She always shops around to find a bargain rate for her flights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual price of something
