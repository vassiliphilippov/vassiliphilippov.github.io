dark red

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk rɛd / (AmE), / dɑːk rɛd / (BrE)
a deep shade of red with low brightness or lightness
a deep, low-brightness shade of red

Examples

He chose a dark red paint for the accent wall in the living room.
The wine had a rich, dark red color that looked almost purple.
She wore a dark red dress to the formal event.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: red - the color between orange and violet on the spectrum
