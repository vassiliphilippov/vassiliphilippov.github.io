bee house

1. NOUN

/ biː haʊs / (AmE), / biː haʊs / (BrE)
a shed or structure that holds a number of beehives
shed containing beehives

Examples

The bee house was buzzing with activity as the beekeeper inspected the hives.
They built a new bee house at the edge of the orchard to expand their honey production.
Each spring, the bee house fills with thousands of bees preparing for the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apiary - a place where beehives are kept
