lucky break

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌki breɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʌki breɪk / (BrE)
an unexpected event or opportunity that brings good fortune or success
unexpected good fortune or opportunity

Examples

After years of struggling as an actor, he finally got a lucky break and landed a major role.
Winning the scholarship was a lucky break that changed her life.
Sometimes a single lucky break can make all the difference in a career.

Synset Examples

That promotion was a lucky break for him.
That lucky break felt like having a horseshoe up my ass.
Winning the contest was his lucky break.
She got a lucky break when the audition went well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windfall - a sudden, unexpected piece of good fortune, especially involving money
  • Synonym: stroke of luck - an unexpected but fortunate occurrence
