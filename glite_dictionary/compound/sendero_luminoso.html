Sendero Luminoso

1. PROPN

/ senˈdɛɾo lʊˈminoʊsoʊ / (AmE), / sɛnˈdɛːɾəʊ lʊˈmiːnəsəʊ / (BrE)
A Maoist guerrilla organization in Peru, founded in the late 1960s, known for violent insurgency and attempts to overthrow the Peruvian government.
Maoist guerrilla group in Peru

Examples

The Peruvian government launched a major offensive against Sendero Luminoso in the 1990s.
Many rural areas were affected by violence from Sendero Luminoso during its peak.
Reports indicate that Sendero Luminoso financed its operations through involvement in the cocaine trade.

Synset Examples

Shining Path has been responsible for 30,000 deaths

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Shining Path - English equivalent name for Sendero Luminoso, Peruvian Maoist group

Etymology
