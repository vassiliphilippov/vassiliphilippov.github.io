deterrent example

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɝːənt ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛrənt ɪɡˈzɑːmpəl / (BrE)
an instance of punishment imposed to serve as a warning to others
punishment to warn others

Examples

The harsh sentence was intended as a deterrent example to others who might consider the same crime.
The company hopes the firing acts as a deterrent example against misconduct in the workplace.
Authorities used the high-profile case as a deterrent example to discourage similar offenses.

Synset Examples

they decided to make an example of him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: example - a person or thing regarded in terms of their fitness to be imitated
