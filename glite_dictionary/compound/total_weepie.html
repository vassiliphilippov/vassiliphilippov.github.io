total weepie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈwiː.pi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈwiː.pi / (BrE)
An informal term for a film that strongly evokes sadness or tears; a movie that makes viewers cry.
film that makes viewers cry
informal

Examples

Last night's drama was a total weepie, and nearly everyone in the cinema had tissues ready.
If you want something light, avoid that film—it's a total weepie from start to finish.
She recommended a total weepie to me because she knows I love emotional movies.

Synset Examples

That movie was a total weepie; I cried!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearjerker - a book, film, or song that evokes sadness or tears

Etymology

From informal use of 'weepie' (a film likely to make one weep) and 'total' for emphasis.
