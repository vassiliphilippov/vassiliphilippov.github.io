morning room

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house, often facing east, used for sitting or entertaining guests during the daylight hours
room for daytime sitting or entertaining

Examples

The sunlight streamed into the morning room, making it the perfect spot for tea and reading.
They reserved the morning room for family gatherings and quiet conversation during the day.
Fresh flowers always decorated the elegant morning room overlooking the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drawing room - a room in a house for entertaining guests
  • Synonym: parlor - a sitting room in a private house
