sticky toffee pud

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki ˈtɑfi pʊd / (AmE), / ˈstɪki ˈtɒfi pʊd / (BrE)
a shortened, informal term for sticky toffee pudding, a popular British dessert made with moist sponge cake, dates, and toffee sauce
informal term for sticky toffee pudding (British dessert)
informal british

Examples

I always order the sticky toffee pud when I visit that café.
Their sticky toffee pud won an award at the food festival last year.
Would you like to share a sticky toffee pud for dessert?

Synset Examples
