Australian blacksnake

1. NOUN

/ əˌstreɪliən ˈblækˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ɒˌstreɪliən ˈblækˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a large semiaquatic snake native to Australia, black on its upper side with a red belly
semiaquatic Australian snake with black back and red belly

Examples

The Australian blacksnake is well known for its distinctive red belly.
Researchers observed several Australian blacksnakes basking along the riverbank.
If you encounter an Australian blacksnake, it is best to leave it undisturbed.
