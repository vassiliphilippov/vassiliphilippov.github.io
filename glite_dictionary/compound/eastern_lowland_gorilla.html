eastern lowland gorilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈistərn ˈloʊlænd ɡəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈləʊlænd ɡəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a large subspecies of gorilla (Gorilla beringei graueri) native to the lowland forests of eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo
largest gorilla subspecies in eastern Congo

Examples

The eastern lowland gorilla is critically endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
Researchers have tracked eastern lowland gorillas in the dense forests of the Democratic Republic of the Congo.
A silverback eastern lowland gorilla can weigh up to 250 kilograms in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorilla - a large great ape native to Africa
