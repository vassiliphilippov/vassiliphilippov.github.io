Arabian jasmine

1. NOUN

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən ˈdʒæz.mɪn / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bɪ.ən ˈdʒæz.mɪn / (BrE)
An evergreen vine native to South and Southeast Asia, cultivated for its abundant, fragrant white flowers.
fragrant white-flowered evergreen vine

Examples

The Arabian jasmine in her garden blooms every summer, filling the air with a sweet fragrance.
He gave her a garland made from fresh Arabian jasmine flowers at the festival.
Arabian jasmine is often used to flavor tea in Southeast Asian countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jasmine - A shrub or vine with fragrant white or yellow flowers
