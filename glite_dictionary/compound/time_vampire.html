time vampire

1. NOUN

/ taɪm ˈvæmˌpaɪr / (AmE), / taɪm ˈvæm.paɪə / (BrE)
something that consumes a lot of time, often unnecessarily or without purpose
thing that wastes a lot of time
informal

Examples

Email can be a real time vampire if you check it constantly throughout the day.
That reality TV show became a time vampire for him during the holidays.
Unplanned meetings often prove to be the biggest time vampires in the office.

Synset Examples

Her constant questions turned the project into a time vampire.
Scrolling through social media is such a time vampire.
That meeting was a real time vampire for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time sink - an activity that requires a lot of time, often wastefully
  • Synonym: time waster - something or someone that wastes time

Etymology

Modelled on 'vampire' (from French, via German, from Hungarian 'vámpír'), used figuratively to mean something that drains resources.
