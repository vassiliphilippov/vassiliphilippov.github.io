to relieve stress

1. VERB

/ rɪˈliv strɛs / (AmE), / rɪˈliːv stres / (BrE)
to reduce or lessen mental tension, anxiety, or psychological pressure
reduce psychological pressure or tension

Examples

Regular exercise can help relieve stress and improve your mood.
Listening to music is one way she likes to relieve stress.
Deep breathing techniques are often used to relieve stress at work.
