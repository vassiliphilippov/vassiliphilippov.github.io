yellow-breasted bunting

1. NOUN

/ ˌjɛloʊˈbrɛstɪd ˈbʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊˈbrɛstɪd ˈbʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
a small songbird found in Russia and Siberia, recognized by its bright yellow underparts and distinctive plumage
a species of small yellow songbird

Examples

The yellow-breasted bunting has become increasingly rare due to habitat loss.
Birdwatchers traveled to Siberia to observe the elusive yellow-breasted bunting in its natural environment.
Conservationists are working to protect the yellow-breasted bunting from further decline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunting - a type of small songbird of the family Emberizidae
