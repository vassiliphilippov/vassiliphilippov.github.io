all night long

1. ADV

/ ɔl naɪt lɔŋ / (AmE), / ɔːl naɪt lɒŋ / (BrE)
for the entire duration of the night; throughout the whole night.
throughout the whole night

Examples

The party went on all night long until the sun came up.
She studied all night long for her final exams.
It rained all night long, making the streets slippery in the morning.

Synset Examples

Our neighbour's dog barked all night long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throughout the night - for the duration of the whole night
