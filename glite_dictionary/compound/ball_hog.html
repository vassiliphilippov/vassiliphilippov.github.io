ball hog

1. NOUN

/ bɔl hɑɡ / (AmE), / bɔːl hɒɡ / (BrE)
a player, especially in team sports, who keeps the ball to themselves and seldom passes to teammates
player who monopolizes the ball

Examples

The coach benched him because he was a ball hog who refused to pass.
No one likes playing with a ball hog on their team.
She was accused of being a ball hog after she tried to score every point herself.

Synset Examples

He played like a ball hog, never sharing the ball.
The coach warned him about being a ball hog.
Stop being a ball hog and pass it!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: team player - a player who works well and cooperates with teammates
  • Synonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned mainly with one's own advantage
