White River

1. PROPN

/ ˈwaɪt ˌrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌrɪvə / (BrE)
a tributary of the Mississippi River that flows southeast through southern Missouri and northern Arkansas in the United States
tributary of the Mississippi River in Missouri and Arkansas

Examples

The White River is known for its excellent trout fishing opportunities.
After heavy rains, the White River occasionally overflows its banks.
Several communities depend on the White River for their water supply.
