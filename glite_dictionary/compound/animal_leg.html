animal leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəml lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈænɪml lɛɡ / (BrE)
the limb of a non-human animal used for support and movement
limb of an animal for movement/support

Examples

The veterinarian examined the injured animal leg to determine the extent of the fracture.
The cheetah's powerful animal legs help it reach incredible speeds while hunting.
A thick layer of fur covers the animal leg of the Arctic fox, protecting it from the cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leg - a limb on which a person or animal walks and stands
