stanchless flow

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæntʃlɪs floʊ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːn(t)ʃlɪs fləʊ / (BrE)
a flow that is continuous and cannot be stopped or controlled
continuous, unstoppable flow

Examples

The stanchless flow of information in today's digital age can be overwhelming.
He marveled at the river's stanchless flow, which had endured for centuries.
Her creativity seemed like a stanchless flow, never running dry.

Synset Examples

The stanchless flow of ideas impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endless stream - an uninterrupted or perpetual stream

Etymology

Formed from 'stanch' (to stop the flow of something, from Old French estanchier) and 'less' (without).
