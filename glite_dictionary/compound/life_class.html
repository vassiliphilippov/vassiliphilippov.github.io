life class

1. NOUN

/ laɪf klæs / (AmE), / laɪf klɑːs / (BrE)
an art class in which students draw, paint, or sculpt from a live human model, typically nude
art class using a live model

Examples

She enrolled in a life class to improve her figure drawing skills.
Many art schools require students to attend at least one life class before graduating.
During the life class, the model held the same pose for twenty minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life drawing - the act or art of drawing a live human model
