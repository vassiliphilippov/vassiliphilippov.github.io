large-flowered calamint

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɑrdʒ ˈflaʊərd ˈkæləˌmɪnt / (AmE), / ˌlɑːdʒ ˈflaʊəd ˈkæləmɪnt / (BrE)
An aromatic herb with large pink flowers, native to southern and southeastern Europe, Anatolia, and northern Iran.
aromatic herb with large pink flowers

Examples

The large-flowered calamint is prized by gardeners for its showy pink blossoms.
In southern Europe, you can often find large-flowered calamint growing in the wild.
Her tea recipe called for fresh leaves from a large-flowered calamint plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calamint - an aromatic herb of the mint family
