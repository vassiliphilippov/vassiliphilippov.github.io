oil refinery

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl rɪˈfaɪnəri / (AmE), / ɔɪl rɪˈfaɪnəri / (BrE)
a factory where crude oil is processed and converted into useful products such as gasoline, diesel, and kerosene
factory for processing crude oil

Examples

The new oil refinery was built to meet the growing demand for fuel in the region.
Emissions from the oil refinery raised concerns among local residents about air quality.
After the spill, the oil refinery improved its safety and environmental procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refinery - a factory where substances are purified
