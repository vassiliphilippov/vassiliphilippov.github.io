word class

1. NOUN

/ wɝːd klæs / (AmE), / wɜːd klɑːs / (BrE)
a category into which words are grouped according to their grammatical function, such as noun, verb, or adjective
category of words by grammatical function

Examples

Every word class plays a vital role in the structure of a sentence.
Adjectives and adverbs belong to different word classes in English grammar.
It's important to identify the word class of each term when learning a new language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: part of speech - any of the categories into which words are grouped in grammar, such as noun, verb, adjective, etc.
