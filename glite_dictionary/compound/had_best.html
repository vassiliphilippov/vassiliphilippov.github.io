to had best

1. VERB

/ hæd bɛst / (AmE), / hæd bɛst / (BrE)
used to say that it is wise or advisable for someone to do something
strong advice; advisable action

Examples

You had best call her before she leaves the office.
If you want to avoid trouble, you had best tell the truth.
We had best take an umbrella in case it rains.

Synset Examples

You will do well to arrive on time tomorrow!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: should - used to give advice or suggest that something is a good idea
  • Synonym: ought to - used to indicate duty, advisability, or correctness
