filmish quality

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmɪʃ ˈkwɑlɪti / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmɪʃ ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
A style or appearance characteristic of a film, especially in terms of visual or narrative presentation.
resemblance to a film in style or look

Examples

The documentary had a filmish quality that captivated the audience.
Her wedding photos had a filmish quality, making them look like scenes from a classic movie.
The play was praised for its filmish quality in lighting and camera work.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinematic - having qualities characteristic of a cinema film
