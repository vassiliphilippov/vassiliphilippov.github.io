to boost community spirit

1. VERB

/ bust kəˌmjunəˈti spɪrɪt / (AmE), / buːst kəˌmjuːnɪti ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
to increase enthusiasm, unity, and participation among members of a community
increase enthusiasm and unity in a community

Examples

The town organized a festival to boost community spirit after the recent floods.
Volunteers helped repaint the playground to boost community spirit in the neighborhood.
A charity walk can boost community spirit and bring people together for a good cause.

Synset Examples

She organized an energetic event to boost community spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foster community spirit - encourage unity and support within a community
