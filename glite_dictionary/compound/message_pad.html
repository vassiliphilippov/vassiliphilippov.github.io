message pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsɪdʒ pæd / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪdʒ pad / (BrE)
a pad of paper designed for writing messages, especially for taking telephone or written notes to be delivered to someone
pad of paper for writing messages

Examples

She left a note for her colleague on the message pad next to the phone.
The receptionist handed me a message pad to jot down my details.
We keep a message pad by the front door for important reminders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notepad - a small pad of blank or lined paper for writing notes
