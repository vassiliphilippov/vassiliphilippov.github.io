European nut pine

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈrəʊpiən nʌt paɪn / (AmE), / jʊˈrəʊpiən nʌt paɪn / (BrE)
A medium-sized pine tree native to southern Europe, known for its spreading crown and edible sweet seeds resembling almonds.
pine tree with edible seeds, native to southern Europe

Examples

The European nut pine is cultivated for its tasty seeds used in Mediterranean cuisine.
Many gardens feature the European nut pine for its attractive shape and valuable pine nuts.
Harvesting pine nuts from the European nut pine requires patience and skill.
