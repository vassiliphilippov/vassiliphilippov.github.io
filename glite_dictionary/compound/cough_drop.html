cough drop

1. NOUN

/ kɑf drɑp / (AmE), / kɒf drɒp / (BrE)
A small medicated sweet intended to soothe a sore throat or suppress coughing.
medicated lozenge for sore throat

Examples

He sucked on a cough drop to soothe his scratchy throat during the meeting.
I always keep a pack of cough drops in my bag in winter.
The pharmacist recommended a menthol cough drop for her persistent cough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lozenge - a small medicinal tablet, often used for sore throats
