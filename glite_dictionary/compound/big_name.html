big name

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ neɪm / (AmE), / bɪɡ neɪm / (BrE)
a person or organization that is well-known and influential, especially within a particular field or industry
well-known or influential person or entity

Examples

She's become a big name in the fashion world over the last few years.
Many big names attended the charity gala.
The event attracted several big name sponsors.

Synset Examples

He's a big name in the tech industry.
That restaurant is a big name for fine dining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment or sport
  • Antonym: unknown - a person who is not known or recognized
