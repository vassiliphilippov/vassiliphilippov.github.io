oval-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvəl ˌbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəl ˌbɒdid / (BrE)
having a body that is shaped like an oval
with an oval-shaped body

Examples

The oval-bodied beetle is easily distinguished from other species by its shape.
Fossils show that many ancient fish were oval-bodied rather than streamlined.
She described the sculpture as oval-bodied with smooth curves and no sharp angles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oval - having the shape of an egg or ellipse
