split-brain technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplɪt ˌbreɪn tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈsplɪt ˌbreɪn tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the corpus callosum (and sometimes the optic chiasm) is severed to interrupt communication between the cerebral hemispheres
surgical procedure severing the corpus callosum
medical

Examples

The split-brain technique yielded invaluable insights into the lateralization of brain functions.
Researchers used the split-brain technique on animals to study how the hemispheres communicate.
After the split-brain technique was performed, the subject's behavior changed in specific ways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: split-brain - a condition resulting from severing the corpus callosum in the brain
