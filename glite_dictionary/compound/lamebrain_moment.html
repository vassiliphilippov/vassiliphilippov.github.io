lamebrain moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪmˌbreɪn ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈleɪmˌbreɪn ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an instance of foolishness or absent-mindedness; a time when someone does something silly or forgetful
instance of foolishness or absent-mindedness
informal slang

Examples

I had a lamebrain moment and left my keys in the car.
It was a total lamebrain moment when I sent the email to the wrong person.
Everyone has a lamebrain moment now and then, so don't worry about it.

Synset Examples

I had a lamebrain moment and lost my wallet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain fade - a temporary lapse of memory or concentration
  • Synonym: brain fart - a temporary mental lapse or failure to reason correctly

Etymology

From 'lamebrain' (slang for a foolish person), combining 'lame' and 'brain'.
