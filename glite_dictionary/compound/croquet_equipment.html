croquet equipment

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈkeɪ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / krəʊˈkeɪ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the mallets, balls, hoops (wickets), and other items required to play croquet
items used to play croquet

Examples

The club provided all the necessary croquet equipment for the tournament.
She carefully packed her croquet equipment after the game was finished.
Modern croquet equipment can be made from wood or durable plastic.
