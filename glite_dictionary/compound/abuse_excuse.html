abuse excuse

1. NOUN

/ əˈbjus ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / əˈbjuːs ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
an attempt to justify one's actions or behavior by citing past abuse or mistreatment suffered
justification by citing past abuse

Examples

The defendant's lawyer presented an abuse excuse in court, claiming past trauma shaped his actions.
Critics argue that using an abuse excuse undermines personal responsibility.
Her frequent abuse excuse for bad behavior was no longer accepted by her friends.

Synset Examples

He used the late hours as an abuse excuse for his mistakes.
They dismissed his complaints as an abuse excuse.
She realized using stress as an abuse excuse was not fair.
