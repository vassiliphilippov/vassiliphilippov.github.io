skin disease

1. NOUN

/ skɪn dɪˈziz / (AmE), / skɪn dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
a disease that affects the skin
disease of the skin

Examples

Eczema is a common skin disease that often causes itching and redness.
The doctor specializes in the treatment of rare skin diseases.
She consulted a dermatologist for her persistent skin disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dermatosis - any disease of the skin (medical term)
