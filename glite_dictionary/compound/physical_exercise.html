physical exercise

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈɛksɚˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a planned activity involving movement of the body and use of muscles to maintain or improve physical fitness
activity for fitness and health

Examples

Regular physical exercise is important for maintaining good health and reducing stress.
The school encourages students to do physical exercise every day.
He joined a gym to get more physical exercise after work.

Synset Examples

the doctor recommended regular exercise
he did some exercising
the physical exertion required by his work kept him fit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - activity that improves physical fitness
