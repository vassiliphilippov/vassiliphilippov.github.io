steam-powered

1. ADJ

/ stiːm ˈpaʊɚd / (AmE), / stiːm ˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
Powered by a steam engine or steam-generated motive force, especially referring to vehicles, machines, or devices.
powered by a steam engine

Examples

The museum features a restored steam-powered locomotive from the late nineteenth century.
Before the advent of diesel engines, steam-powered ships were common on the open seas.
He built a small steam-powered car as a demonstration project for the science fair.

Synset Examples

a steam-powered locomotive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam-driven - powered by a steam engine or steam pressure
