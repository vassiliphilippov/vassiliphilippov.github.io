kick pleat

1. NOUN

/ kɪk pliːt / (AmE), / kɪk pliːt / (BrE)
A pleat at the back or side of a straight skirt or coat to provide extra room for ease of movement while walking.
pleat in clothing for easier walking

Examples

The tailored dress featured a kick pleat at the back for added comfort when walking.
She chose a skirt with a kick pleat so she could move more freely at work.
Many pencil skirts have a kick pleat to prevent the fabric from restricting your steps.
