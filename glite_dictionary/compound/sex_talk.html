sex talk

1. NOUN

/ sɛks tɑk / (AmE), / sɛks tɔːk / (BrE)
a conversation or discussion about sexual matters, often in the context of education or guidance
discussion about sexual topics

Examples

Parents often struggle with initiating the sex talk with their teenagers.
The school organized a sex talk to educate students about safe practices.
He dreaded the inevitable sex talk with his daughter.

Synset Examples

They had an awkward sex talk over dinner.
She felt nervous giving her kids the sex talk.
