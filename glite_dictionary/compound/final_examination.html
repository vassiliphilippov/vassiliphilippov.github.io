final examination

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a test given at the end of an academic term to assess a student's knowledge of course material
test at end of academic term

Examples

All students must take the final examination to pass the course.
She spent two weeks preparing for her biology final examination.
The results of the final examination will be released next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exam - a formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency
  • Degree Lower: midterm examination - an examination administered in the middle of an academic term
