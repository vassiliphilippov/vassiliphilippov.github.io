storm cellar

1. NOUN

/ stɔrm ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / stɔːm ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
an underground shelter built to provide protection from severe storms, especially tornadoes
underground storm shelter

Examples

The family hurried into the storm cellar as the tornado siren blared across town.
They keep emergency supplies and water stocked in their storm cellar every spring.
During the violent storm, the neighbors took refuge in the old storm cellar behind the barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomb shelter - an underground shelter to protect against bombs
  • Synonym: tornado shelter - a reinforced room or underground structure for protection from tornadoes
