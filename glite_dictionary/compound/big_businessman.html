big businessman

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈbɪznɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈbɪznɪsmæn / (BrE)
a businessman who is very wealthy or influential
very wealthy or powerful businessman

Examples

He started as a humble clerk but became a big businessman with companies across the globe.
At the conference, several big businessmen discussed trends in international trade.
Rumors circulated that a big businessman was behind the major corporate takeover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tycoon - a wealthy, powerful person in business or industry
  • Synonym: magnate - a wealthy and influential businessperson
