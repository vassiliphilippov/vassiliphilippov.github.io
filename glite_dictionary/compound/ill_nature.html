ill nature

1. NOUN

/ ɪl ˈneɪt̓ʃɚ / (AmE), / ɪl ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
an irritable, disagreeable, or malevolent disposition
disagreeable or irritable temperament

Examples

His ill nature made it difficult for him to keep friends for long.
She answered the question with unnecessary ill nature, surprising everyone in the room.
Their constant arguments seemed to bring out the ill nature in both of them.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good nature - a kindly, pleasant, or amiable disposition
  • Synonym: bad temper - a tendency to become angry quickly or be easily annoyed
