prairie banana

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛr.i bəˈnæn.ə / (AmE), / ˈpreə.ri bəˈnæn.ə / (BrE)
a colloquial name for the North American pawpaw, a wild fruit-bearing tree found especially in prairies and woods
colloquial name for the North American pawpaw
informal

Examples

They found a ripe prairie banana on their morning walk through the meadows.
Local foragers prize the prairie banana for its custard-like fruit.
Pioneers often used prairie banana as a natural treat when crossing the plains.

Synset Examples

They spotted a prairie banana growing near the riverbank.
She found a prairie banana while hiking in the woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pawpaw - a North American fruit tree (Asimina triloba) with edible fruit
