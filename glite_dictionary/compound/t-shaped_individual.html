T-shaped individual

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiː ʃeɪpt ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈtiː ʃeɪpt ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəl / (BrE)
A person who possesses a wider range of general skills as well as deep expertise in a specific area.
person with broad skills and deep expertise

Examples

The company values T-shaped individuals who can contribute to multiple projects and specialize in one area.
As a T-shaped individual, she collaborates well across teams while leading in engineering.
Hiring more T-shaped individuals helped the team solve complex, interdisciplinary challenges.

Synset Examples

Our team needs more T-shaped individuals for better collaboration.
He's a T-shaped professional with skills in design and coding.

Etymology

From the visual metaphor of the letter 'T': the horizontal bar represents breadth of skills, and the vertical bar represents depth of knowledge.
