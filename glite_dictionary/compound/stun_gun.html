stun gun

1. NOUN

/ stʌn ɡʌn / (AmE), / stʌn ɡʌn / (BrE)
a weapon that temporarily incapacitates a person by delivering a nonlethal high-voltage electric shock
nonlethal electric weapon for incapacitating

Examples

The police officer used a stun gun to subdue the suspect without causing permanent harm.
Many people carry a stun gun for self-defense purposes.
After being hit with a stun gun, the intruder fell to the ground, unable to move.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taser - a brand of weapon that uses electric shocks to incapacitate, often used as a synonym for stun gun
