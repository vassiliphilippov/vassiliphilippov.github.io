plane ticket

1. NOUN

/ pleɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / pleɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that allows a person to travel on an airplane
ticket for air travel

Examples

I had to cancel my plane ticket because of an emergency.
She bought a plane ticket online for her trip to Paris.
Have you already booked your plane ticket for the conference?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airline ticket - a ticket issued by an airline for travel by air
