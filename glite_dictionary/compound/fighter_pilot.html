fighter pilot

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪtər ˌpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtə ˌpaɪlət / (BrE)
a military or naval aviator trained to fly and operate fighter aircraft, especially in combat situations
military aviator of fighter planes

Examples

The fighter pilot completed a daring mission over hostile territory.
Her grandfather served as a fighter pilot during World War II.
After years of training, he finally became a fighter pilot in the air force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - person who operates the controls of an aircraft
