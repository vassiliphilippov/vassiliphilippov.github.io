largemouth bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌmaʊθ bæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒmaʊθ bæs / (BrE)
a large North American freshwater fish, popular with anglers, with a wide mouth extending past the eye
a large North American freshwater fish

Examples

The lake is famous for its largemouth bass population, attracting anglers from around the region.
He proudly held up the largemouth bass he had caught early that morning.
Many specialists study the habitat requirements of the largemouth bass to improve conservation efforts.
