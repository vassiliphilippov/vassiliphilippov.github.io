submersible warship

1. NOUN

/ səbˈmɝsəbl ˈwɔrˌʃɪp / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːsəbl ˈwɔːˌʃɪp / (BrE)
A military vessel designed and equipped to operate underwater, typically for stealth or attack purposes.
military vessel for underwater operations

Examples

The navy launched a new submersible warship capable of operating undetected for weeks.
Enemy forces were unable to detect the submersible warship as it patrolled the area.
Advances in technology have improved the endurance and stealth of the modern submersible warship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submarine - a vessel designed to operate under water
