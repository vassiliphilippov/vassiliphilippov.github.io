about turn

1. NOUN

/ əˈbaʊt tɝn / (AmE), / əˌbaʊt ˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an act of turning completely around to face the opposite direction, especially as a military command or maneuver
a 180-degree turn, esp. in military context
british

Examples

The sergeant shouted "about turn!" and the soldiers spun to face the other way.
His announcement marked an about turn in government policy.
With an about turn, she marched out of the room without another word.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about-face - act of pivoting 180 degrees, especially in a military formation (US term)
