pure trash

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr træʃ / (AmE), / pjʊə træʃ / (BrE)
something that is considered extremely bad or of very low quality
extremely bad or low-quality thing

Examples

He thought the novel was pure trash and not worth reading.
Some critics called the show pure trash, but it still became popular.
Don't waste your money on that game, it's pure trash according to the reviews.

Synset Examples

He called that movie pure trash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish - things that are worthless or of poor quality
  • Synonym: junk - something useless or of low value
