off-the-shoulder

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔf ðə ˈʃoʊldər / (AmE), / ˌɒf ðə ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
(of clothing) not covering the shoulders, especially referring to a style where the neckline leaves the shoulders bare.
style of clothing exposing the shoulders

Examples

She wore a stunning off-the-shoulder gown to the gala.
This off-the-shoulder top is perfect for summer parties.
The bride chose an elegant off-the-shoulder wedding dress for her big day.
