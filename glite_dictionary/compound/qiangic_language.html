Qiangic language

1. NOUN

/ tʃiˈɑːŋɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tʃiˈaŋɪk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a group of Tibeto-Burman languages spoken mainly in Sichuan province, China
Tibeto-Burman language group of Sichuan, China

Examples

The Qiangic language family is known for its complex system of tones and sounds.
Many villagers in Sichuan still speak a Qiangic language as their first language.
Linguists are working to document the endangered Qiangic language varieties before they disappear.

Etymology

From Qiang, the name of an indigenous ethnic group of Sichuan, plus the English suffix -ic.
