garden variety

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑrdn vəˈraɪəti / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn vəˈraɪəti / (BrE)
Ordinary or typical; not special or unusual in any way.
ordinary or typical; not special

Examples

Her cold was just a garden variety illness, nothing serious.
The movie was a garden variety romantic comedy without any surprises.
Most of these problems are garden variety issues that can be fixed easily.

Synset Examples

His excuses were just garden variety complaints.
They used a garden variety of plants in the landscaping.
We picked up some garden variety tomatoes from the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commonplace - Ordinary; not unusual
  • Antonym: uncommon - Not ordinary or typical

Etymology

Originally, referring to plant varieties regularly grown in home gardens, contrasted with rare or exotic types.
