transition time

1. NOUN

/ trænˈzɪʃ.ən taɪm / (AmE), / trænˈzɪʃ.ən taɪm / (BrE)
a period during which someone or something adjusts to a change or adapts to new conditions
period of adjustment to change

Examples

The company allowed a three-month transition time to introduce the new software.
There is usually a transition time when employees adjust to a different management style.
After moving to a new city, she needed a transition time to settle in.

Synset Examples

There's a transition time before the new system goes live.
The project includes a transition time for employee training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adaptation period - time allocated for adapting to a new situation or change
  • Synonym: lag time - interval between an action and its effect, often requiring adjustment
