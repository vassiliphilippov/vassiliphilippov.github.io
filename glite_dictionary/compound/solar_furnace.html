solar furnace

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.lɚ ˈfɝː.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.lə ˈfɜː.nɪs / (BrE)
a device that uses a concave mirror to concentrate sunlight and produce very high temperatures, often for scientific or industrial purposes.
device concentrating sunlight to produce heat

Examples

The research team built a solar furnace to study materials at extremely high temperatures.
A solar furnace can reach temperatures over 3,000 degrees Celsius using only concentrated sunlight.
In some experiments, a solar furnace is used instead of conventional fuel-burning ovens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heliostat - a device with a mirror to reflect sunlight in a constant direction
