retrogreen style

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛtroʊ ˈɡriːn staɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊ ˈɡriːn staɪl / (BrE)
A fashion style that combines elements from past fashion trends with environmentally friendly materials or practices.
eco-friendly fashion with retro influences

Examples

She always chooses retrogreen style in her outfits, mixing vintage looks with organic fabrics.
The designer's latest collection features a retrogreen style that appeals to environmentally conscious consumers.
Many boutiques now showcase retrogreen style as a response to both nostalgia and sustainability trends.

Synset Examples

She embraced retrogreen styles in her fashion choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retro style - fashion inspired by trends from the past

Etymology

Blend of 'retro', from Latin 'retro' (backwards), and 'green' in the sense of being environmentally conscious.
