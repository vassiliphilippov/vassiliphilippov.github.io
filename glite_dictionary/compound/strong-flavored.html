strong-flavored

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɔŋ ˌfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋ ˌfleɪvəd / (BrE)
Having a pronounced or intense taste, often noticeable and easily distinguished.
having a pronounced taste

Examples

The cheese was so strong-flavored that only a small piece was enough for the sandwich.
She prefers strong-flavored coffee to the mild varieties served elsewhere.
Many Mediterranean dishes are known for their use of strong-flavored herbs and spices.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mild - having a gentle or not strong flavor
