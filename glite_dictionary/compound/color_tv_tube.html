color TV tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ ˌtiːˈviː tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtiːˈviː tjuːb / (BrE)
a vacuum tube used in color televisions to display images in full color
tube for displaying color TV images

Examples

The technician replaced the faulty color TV tube to restore the picture quality.
Many vintage televisions are prized for their original color TV tubes.
A color TV tube combines red, green, and blue signals to create a full-color display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cathode ray tube - a vacuum tube used to display images in older televisions and monitors
