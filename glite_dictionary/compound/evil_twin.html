evil twin

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvəl twɪn / (AmE), / ˈiːvəl twɪn / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles another but is considered to have opposite or bad traits, or a humorous reference to one's negative side or behavior
a person with similar appearance but bad traits

Examples

In the novel, the hero’s evil twin creates chaos and confusion.
Whenever I make a bad decision, I blame my evil twin.
The sitcom’s main character had an evil twin who showed up every season finale.

Synset Examples

My friend acts like my evil twin when she's angry.
In movies, the hero often has an evil twin.
He joked that his procrastination was his evil twin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doppelgänger - a double or lookalike of a person, often considered mysterious or sinister
  • Synonym: alter ego - a person's secondary or alternative personality
