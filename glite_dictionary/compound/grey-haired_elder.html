grey-haired elder

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡreɪ ˌhɛrd ˈɛldər / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪ ˌheəd ˈɛldə / (BrE)
an older person, especially one with grey hair, who is respected for their wisdom or experience
a wise, respected older person

Examples

The council sought guidance from the grey-haired elder before making their decision.
Villagers respected the grey-haired elder for her knowledge and fairness in disputes.
At the ceremony, he acted as the grey-haired elder, sharing stories from the past.

Synset Examples

She admired the wisdom of the grey-haired elder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elder - an older person, often respected for age or experience
  • Synonym: sage - a profoundly wise person
  • Antonym: youth - a young person
