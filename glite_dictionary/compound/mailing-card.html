mailing-card

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
A card designed for sending messages by mail without an envelope.
card for postal messages without envelope

Examples

She sent her grandmother a cheerful mailing-card from Paris.
The mailing-card arrived with a simple message and no return address.
I usually prefer to send a letter, but sometimes a mailing-card is more convenient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postcard - a card for sending messages by mail, usually with a picture on one side
