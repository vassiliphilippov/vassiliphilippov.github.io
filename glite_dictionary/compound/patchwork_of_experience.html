patchwork of experience

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætʃˌwɝk əv ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈpætʃwɜːk əv ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
a diverse or varied mixture of life experiences
varied mix of life experiences

Examples

Her patchwork of experience helped her understand people from many walks of life.
The author's patchwork of experience gives his writing a deeply personal touch.
Traveling to different countries contributed to a rich patchwork of experience.

Synset Examples

His patchwork of experiences made him a unique candidate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patchwork - piece of cloth made by sewing together different fabrics

Etymology

From 'patchwork', a term for a fabric made by sewing together pieces of different material, used metaphorically to describe a collection of varied elements.
