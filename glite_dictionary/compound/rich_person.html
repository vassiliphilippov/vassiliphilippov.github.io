rich person

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who possesses a great amount of material wealth
a wealthy individual

Examples

The charity event was attended by a rich person who donated a large sum of money.
It's not uncommon for a rich person to own multiple houses around the world.
Becoming a rich person is a dream for many, but it often requires hard work and luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millionaire - a person whose assets are worth at least a million dollars, pounds, etc.
  • Antonym: pauper - a very poor person
