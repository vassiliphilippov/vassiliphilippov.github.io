salt reed grass

1. NOUN

/ sɔlt rid ɡræs / (AmE), / sɒlt riːd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tall, reedlike grass common in salt meadows and marshlands
reedlike grass found in salt meadows

Examples

The salt reed grass growing along the shoreline provides shelter for many small animals.
Conservationists are restoring salt reed grass habitats to support coastal biodiversity.
She photographed the fields of salt reed grass waving gently in the sea breeze.
