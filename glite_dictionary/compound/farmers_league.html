farmers' league

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmərz liɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməz liːɡ / (BrE)
an organization or association formed to represent the interests of farmers
organization for farmers

Examples

He became president of the local farmers' league to advocate for agricultural reform.
The farmers' league organized a demonstration to protest new agricultural policies.
Membership in the farmers' league offers access to shared resources and expert advice.

Synset Examples

Joining the farmers' league helped him connect with others in agriculture.
The farmers' league meets monthly to discuss crop rotations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmers' union - an organization formed by or for farmers to advance common interests
