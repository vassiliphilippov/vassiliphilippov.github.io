Elvis Presley

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛlvɪs ˈprɛzli / (AmE), / ˈɛlvɪs ˈprɛzli / (BrE)
(1935–1977) An American rock and roll singer and actor known for his influential music, energetic performance style, and enduring impact on popular culture.
famous American rock singer and actor

Examples

Elvis Presley is often called the King of Rock and Roll for his major influence on the genre.
Many fans visit Graceland to honor Elvis Presley's legacy.
Elvis Presley's hip-shaking dance moves shocked and delighted audiences in the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock and roll - a genre of popular music that Elvis Presley is associated with
