carrying out

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæriɪŋ ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˈkæriɪŋ ˈaʊt / (BrE)
the act of performing or accomplishing something; the process of completing a task or action
act of performing or accomplishing

Examples

The carrying out of this plan will require careful coordination from all departments.
Strict procedures ensure the proper carrying out of safety measures in the laboratory.
The company was praised for its efficient carrying out of the construction project.

Synset Examples

they criticized his performance as mayor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performance - the act of carrying out a task or action
  • Synonym: execution - the act of carrying out or putting something into effect

Examples
