hunger for adventure

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌŋɡɚ fɚ ədˈvɛn.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡə fər ədˈvɛn.tʃə / (BrE)
a strong desire or longing to have exciting or unusual experiences
strong desire for excitement

Examples

His hunger for adventure led him to explore remote parts of the world.
Despite her comfortable life, she always had a hunger for adventure that couldn't be satisfied.
The book appeals to anyone with a hunger for adventure and curiosity about the unknown.

Synset Examples

She felt a sudden hunger for adventure and booked a trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yearning - a strong desire for something
  • Synonym: wanderlust - a strong desire to travel and explore the world
  • Degree Higher: adventure - an unusual, exciting, or daring experience
