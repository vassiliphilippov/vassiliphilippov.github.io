blackberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbɛri lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈblækbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic drink made by infusing or blending blackberries with spirits and sweetening agents.
alcoholic drink made from blackberries

Examples

She added a splash of blackberry liqueur to the cocktail for a fruity twist.
The dessert was served with a drizzle of blackberry liqueur over vanilla ice cream.
He received a bottle of blackberry liqueur as a gift from his friend in France.
