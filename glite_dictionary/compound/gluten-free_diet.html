gluten-free diet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡluːtən friː ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtən friː ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that excludes foods containing gluten, often prescribed for people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance
diet excluding foods with gluten

Examples

After being diagnosed with celiac disease, she switched to a gluten-free diet.
Many restaurants now offer gluten-free diet options on their menus.
A gluten-free diet requires careful planning to avoid hidden sources of gluten.
