sudden breakdown

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌdn ˈbreɪkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈsʌdn ˈbreɪkdaʊn / (BrE)
an unexpected and abrupt failure of machinery or equipment
unexpected machinery failure

Examples

The train was delayed due to a sudden breakdown in the engine.
She called for help after experiencing a sudden breakdown of her computer's hard drive.
A sudden breakdown of the elevator left everyone stranded between floors.

Synset Examples

His car had a sudden breakdown on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakdown - failure or collapse of machinery or systems
