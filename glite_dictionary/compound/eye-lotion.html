eye-lotion

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ ˌloʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌləʊʃən / (BrE)
A liquid preparation used to cleanse or soothe the eyes.
solution for cleansing the eyes

Examples

The doctor recommended using an eye-lotion twice a day to relieve irritation.
She bought a gentle eye-lotion to help cleanse her eyes after swimming.
Always check the ingredients before applying any eye-lotion to sensitive areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eyewash - a liquid used for washing the eyes
