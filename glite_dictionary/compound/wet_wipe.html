wet wipe

1. NOUN

/ wɛt waɪp / (AmE), / wɛt wʌɪp / (BrE)
a small disposable cloth moistened with liquid, used for cleaning surfaces or skin
moistened disposable cleaning cloth

Examples

She always carries a pack of wet wipes in her purse for emergencies.
We used a wet wipe to clean the baby's face after dinner.
Some restaurants provide a wet wipe at the end of your meal.

Synset Examples

She used a wet wipe to clean her hands after lunch.
Wet wipes are handy for traveling with kids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wipe - a disposable cloth or paper used for cleaning
