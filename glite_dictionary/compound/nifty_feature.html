nifty feature

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪfti ˈfitʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɪfti ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a clever, practical, or particularly useful characteristic of a product, system, or design
clever or useful characteristic

Examples

The smartphone's camera has a nifty feature that automatically enhances your photos.
One nifty feature of this coffee maker is the timer you can set overnight.
Her app offers a nifty feature that tracks spending and suggests saving tips.

Synset Examples

Her design included some nifty features for users.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feature - a distinctive attribute or aspect of something
