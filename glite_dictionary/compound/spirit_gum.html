spirit gum

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪt ˌɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌɡʌm / (BrE)
an adhesive made from gum and ether, used especially for attaching false hair or prosthetics to the skin
adhesive for attaching false hair to skin

Examples

The makeup artist applied spirit gum to secure the actor's fake mustache.
You should remove spirit gum with a special solvent to avoid irritating your skin.
Costume designers often use spirit gum for attaching beards and theatrical prosthetics.
