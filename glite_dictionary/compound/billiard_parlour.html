billiard parlour

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljərd ˌpɑrlər / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
a room or establishment where billiards or similar cue sports are played
room or place to play billiards

Examples

He spent most evenings at the local billiard parlour with his friends.
New laws require all billiard parlours to close by midnight in the city.
The billiard parlour was filled with the sound of clinking balls and quiet conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool hall - a place for playing pool; similar to a billiard parlour
