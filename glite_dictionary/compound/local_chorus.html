local chorus

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈkɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈkɒrəs / (BrE)
a community group of singers who perform together, typically within a town or local area
community singing group

Examples

She performed a solo as part of the local chorus during the spring concert.
The local chorus rehearses every Thursday evening at the community center.
Joining a local chorus is a great way to meet new people who love music.

Synset Examples

She joined the local chorus to improve her singing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choir - an organized group of singers, especially in a church or school
