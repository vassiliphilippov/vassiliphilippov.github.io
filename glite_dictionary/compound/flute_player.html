flute player

1. NOUN

/ fluːt ˈpleɪɚ / (AmE), / fluːt ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the flute, either professionally or as an amateur
person who plays the flute

Examples

The flute player performed a beautiful solo in the orchestra's spring concert.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a renowned flute player.
The jazz band was looking for an experienced flute player to join their group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flautist - a person who plays the flute
