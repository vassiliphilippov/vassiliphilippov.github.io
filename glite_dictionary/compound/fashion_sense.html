fashion sense

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃ.ən sɛns / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən sɛns / (BrE)
The ability to choose and combine clothing and accessories in a stylish or attractive way; a person's awareness of what looks good in fashion.
awareness and style in clothing choices

Examples

Her fashion sense always impresses her friends at every event.
He lacks fashion sense, often wearing mismatched socks and shirts.
The designer admired her natural fashion sense and confidence.

Synset Examples

Zelly is known for her unique fashion sense.
Harriet Lane was known for her stunning fashion sense.
Her fashion sense is a mix of giraffe sheep; very unique.
Her fashion sense makes her a true sophisticate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: style - distinctive appearance or manner of expression, especially in clothing
