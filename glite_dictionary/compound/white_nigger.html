white nigger

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈnɪɡɚ / (AmE), / waɪt ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and derogatory racial slur historically used in different contexts to insult or demean white individuals, sometimes by associating them with negative stereotypes attributed to Black people.
offensive racial slur for white people
offensive vulgar

Examples

The use of white nigger is considered extremely offensive and unacceptable in any context.
He faced backlash after referring to a colleague as a white nigger during a heated argument.
Discussions about the historical use of the phrase white nigger highlight its deeply racist connotations.
