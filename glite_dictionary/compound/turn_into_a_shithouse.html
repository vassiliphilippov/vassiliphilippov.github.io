to turn into a shithouse

1. VERB

/ tɝn ˈɪntu ə ˈʃɪthaʊs / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈɪntuː ə ˈʃɪthaʊs / (BrE)
(of a situation or place) to degenerate or descend into chaos, disorder, or squalor, often suddenly.
descend into chaos or disorder
vulgar slang

Examples

The meeting quickly turned into a shithouse when people started yelling over each other.
After the power went out, the whole event turned into a shithouse of confusion and mess.
What started as a calm discussion soon turned into a shithouse of accusations and chaos.

Synset Examples

The party turned into a shithouse by midnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to fall apart - to collapse or break down completely
  • Synonym: to go to hell - to deteriorate badly; to become chaotic or ruined

Etymology

From slang use of 'shithouse' (vulgar for filthy or chaotic place) + idiom 'turn into.'
