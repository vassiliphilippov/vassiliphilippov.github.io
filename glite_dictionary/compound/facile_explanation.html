facile explanation

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæsl ɛkˌspleɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfæs.əl ɪkˌspleɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation that is too simplistic or superficial to be accurate or convincing
an overly simple explanation

Examples

The journalist's facile explanation failed to address the deeper issues involved in the case.
Critics dismissed his theory as just another facile explanation lacking real evidence.
Students sometimes rely on a facile explanation when a topic is difficult to understand.

Synset Examples

His facile explanation didn't convince anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simplistic explanation - an explanation that is oversimplified and ignores complexities
