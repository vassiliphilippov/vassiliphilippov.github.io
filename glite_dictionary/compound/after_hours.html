after hours

1. ADV

/ ˈæftɚ ˈaʊɚz / (AmE), / ˈɑːftə ˈaʊəz / (BrE)
at a time that is outside normal business or working hours.
outside regular working hours

Examples

The library is sometimes open after hours for special events.
She enjoys going for a run after hours when the streets are quieter.
Many problems were solved by staff working after hours to meet the deadline.

Synset Examples

he often worked after hours

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overtime - time worked beyond regular hours
