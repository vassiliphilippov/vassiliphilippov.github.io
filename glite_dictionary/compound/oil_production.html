oil production

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl pɹəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɔɪl pɹəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process of extracting petroleum from the earth, typically by drilling wells
extraction of petroleum from the earth

Examples

The country increased its oil production to boost the national economy.
Environmental groups are concerned about the impact of oil production on local ecosystems.
Advances in technology have made oil production more efficient and less costly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum extraction - the process of removing crude oil from the ground
