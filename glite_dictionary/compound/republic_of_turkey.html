Republic of Turkey

1. PROPN

/ ɹɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtɝki / (AmE), / ɹɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
The official name of Turkey, a country in both southeastern Europe and western Asia, established as a republic in 1923 after the fall of the Ottoman Empire.
official name of Turkey

Examples

The Republic of Turkey was established in 1923 after the collapse of the Ottoman Empire.
Many historical sites in the Republic of Turkey attract millions of tourists each year.
The Republic of Turkey has a unique blend of European and Asian cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkey - A country located mainly on the Anatolian Peninsula in Western Asia, with a portion in the Balkans of Southeast Europe
