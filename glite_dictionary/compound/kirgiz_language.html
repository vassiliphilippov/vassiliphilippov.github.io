Kirgiz language

1. NOUN

/ kɪrˈɡiːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Kirghiz people, mainly in Kyrgyzstan and surrounding regions.
Turkic language spoken in Kyrgyzstan

Examples

Many people in Kyrgyzstan use the Kirgiz language in their daily lives.
He is fluent in both Russian and the Kirgiz language.
The Kirgiz language is taught in schools across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyz language - Turkic language spoken in Kyrgyzstan and by Kyrgyz people

Etymology

From Kirgiz, an alternative spelling of Kyrgyz, from Russian Киргиз (Kirgiz).
