baked egg

1. NOUN

/ beɪkt ɛɡ / (AmE), / beɪkt ɛɡ / (BrE)
an egg cooked individually with cream or butter, usually in a small ramekin
egg cooked in a ramekin with cream or butter

Examples

He ordered a baked egg with herbs and cream for brunch.
The recipe suggests serving a baked egg alongside toast soldiers.
Each guest was served a baked egg in a delicate ceramic ramekin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirred egg - egg baked in a flat-bottomed dish
