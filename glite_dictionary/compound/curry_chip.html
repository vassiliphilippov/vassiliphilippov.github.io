curry chip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜːri tʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈkʌri tʃɪp / (BrE)
A portion of fried potato chips seasoned with curry flavoring, commonly served as a snack or side dish.
fried chips with curry seasoning

Examples

The restaurant's curry chips are famous for their spicy and unique taste.
After a night out, we stopped for some curry chips from the takeaway.
He learned to make curry chips at home after trying them on holiday.

Synset Examples

We served curry chips as a unique appetizer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chips - thin slices of potato that have been fried until crisp
  • Synonym: french fries - long, thin pieces of potato that are fried and eaten hot
