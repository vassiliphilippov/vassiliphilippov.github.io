celebrity scandal

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛbrɪti ˈskændl̩ / (AmE), / səˈlɛbrəti ˈskændl̩ / (BrE)
A controversial or sensational event involving a famous person, often attracting widespread media attention.
controversial event involving a famous person

Examples

The magazine devoted an entire issue to the recent celebrity scandal that rocked Hollywood.
Social media quickly spread rumors about the celebrity scandal involving the famous singer.
After the celebrity scandal broke, the actor lost several endorsement deals.

Synset Examples

Everyone is talking about the latest celebrity scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scandal - an action or event causing public outrage or shock
