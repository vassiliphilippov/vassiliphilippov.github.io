wolf bean

1. NOUN

/ wʊlf ˌbin / (AmE), / wʊlf ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A white-flowered Eurasian herb widely cultivated for forage and to prevent soil erosion.
white-flowered herb used for forage and erosion control

Examples

The farmer planted wolf bean on the hillside to protect the soil from washing away.
Wolf bean is known for its ability to improve soil fertility in pastures.
The agricultural guide recommended wolf bean as a sustainable forage crop.
