business index

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˈɪndɛks / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
a statistical measure that provides information about economic or financial conditions, often used to track business activity over time
statistical measure of business conditions

Examples

The quarterly report included the latest business index to help investors track economic trends.
Analysts used a business index to compare growth rates between different countries.
The business index showed a sharp increase in manufacturing activity this year.

Synset Examples

this business index is computed relative to the base year of 2005

Related Terms

  • Synonym: index - an indicator, sign, or measure of something
