orange peel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrəndʒ piːl / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ piːl / (BrE)
the outer skin or rind of an orange, often used as flavoring or garnish
the rind of an orange

Examples

She added some finely grated orange peel to the cake batter for extra flavor.
Candied orange peel makes a delicious treat during the holidays.
The recipe calls for the zest of one orange peel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon peel - the rind of a lemon
  • Synonym: orange zest - finely grated outer skin of an orange, used as flavoring

2. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ pil / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ piːl / (BrE)
strips of orange rind that have been cooked in sugar and coated with sugar, often used as a sweet or flavoring
candied strips of orange rind

Examples

She decorated the cake with chopped orange peel for a burst of flavor.
He nibbled on a piece of sugary orange peel after dinner.
Homemade orange peel makes a delightful holiday treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candied peel - fruit rinds preserved with sugar
