dog's-tooth check

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡz tuθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡz tuːθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
a textile pattern consisting of small broken or jagged checks, also known as houndstooth
jagged textile check pattern

Examples

The blazer was tailored from a striking dog's-tooth check fabric.
She preferred classic styles, especially skirts in dog's-tooth check patterns.
His suit featured a bold black-and-white dog's-tooth check.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houndstooth - textile pattern of broken checks, similar in appearance

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the jagged shape of a dog's tooth.

2. NOUN

/ dɔɡz tuːθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / dɒɡz tuːθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
A textile pattern consisting of small, broken or jagged checks, resembling a series of elongated diamonds or uneven squares.
pattern of small jagged checks in textiles

Examples

Her coat was made from a classic dog's-tooth check fabric.
The designer chose dog's-tooth check for the autumn collection's tailored suits.
I love the sharp look dog's-tooth check gives to traditional jackets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houndstooth - a similar textile pattern of broken checks

Etymology

The name refers to the pattern's resemblance to a dog's tooth. Also known as 'houndstooth'.
