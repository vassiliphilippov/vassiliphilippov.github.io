senior vice president

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniər vaɪs ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə vaɪs ˌprɛzɪˈdənt / (BrE)
the highest-ranking vice president in an organization with more than one vice president, typically responsible for significant divisions or operations
ranking vice president in an organization

Examples

She was promoted to senior vice president after years of outstanding performance.
The senior vice president oversees the company's international operations.
During the meeting, the senior vice president presented the new strategic plan.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: vice president - an executive officer ranking below the president
