has-been-dom

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæzˌbiːn.dəm / (AmE), / ˈhæzˌbiːn.dəm / (BrE)
The state or condition of being a has-been; being no longer relevant or important.
state of being a has-been
informal

Examples

After her last album failed, she feared slipping into has-been-dom before turning thirty.
The inventor worked tirelessly, determined to avoid the trap of has-been-dom after his initial success.
Some stars embrace has-been-dom with humour, reinventing themselves as nostalgia acts.

Synset Examples

Living in has-been-dom isn't on her bucket list.
He wants to avoid has-been-dom at all costs.
The once-famous actor now resides in has-been-dom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolescence - the process of becoming obsolete or no longer used
  • Antonym: relevance - the quality of being closely connected or appropriate

Etymology

Formed from 'has-been' (someone formerly important) + suffix '-dom' (denoting state or condition).
