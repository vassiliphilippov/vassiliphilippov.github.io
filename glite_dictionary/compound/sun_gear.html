sun gear

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ɡɪɚ / (AmE), / sʌn ɡɪə / (BrE)
the central gear around which the planet gears revolve in an epicyclic (planetary) gear system
central gear in a planetary gear system

Examples

The sun gear transmits power to the surrounding planet gears in the gearbox.
During assembly, make sure the sun gear is properly aligned with the other components.
If the sun gear is damaged, the entire planetary gear system may fail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: planet gear - a gear that revolves around the sun gear in a planetary gear system
