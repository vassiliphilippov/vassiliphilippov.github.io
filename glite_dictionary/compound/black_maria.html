Black Maria

1. NOUN

/ blæk məˈriːə / (AmE), / blak məˈriːə / (BrE)
A card game, a form of whist in which players try to avoid winning tricks containing hearts or the queen of spades.
card game avoiding hearts and queen of spades

Examples

We spent the evening playing Black Maria with our friends.
In Black Maria, picking up the queen of spades can ruin your whole game.
She taught her grandchildren how to play Black Maria during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hearts - a card game where players try to avoid winning certain cards
