black Maria

1. NOUN

/ blæk məˈraɪ.ə / (AmE), / blæk məˈriː.ə / (BrE)
a police van used to transport prisoners
police van for transporting prisoners

Examples

The suspects were loaded into the black Maria after their arrest.
After the riot, several people were taken away in a black Maria.
The sight of the black Maria outside the courthouse drew a crowd.

Etymology

Origin unknown; attested in the U.S. from the 1830s, possibly named after a tough black boarding-house keeper in Boston or New York.
