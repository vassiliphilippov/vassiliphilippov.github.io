historical context

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl ˈkɑntɛkst / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl ˈkɒntɛkst / (BrE)
The background, conditions, or circumstances in which historical events occur, influencing how they are understood or interpreted.
background surrounding historical events

Examples

Understanding the historical context of a text can greatly enhance your interpretation of its themes.
The film accurately portrays the historical context of World War II.
Scholars often disagree about how much historical context should influence our reading of old laws.

Synset Examples

He studied the historical context of Aryan movements.
Nestorianism is often misunderstood in historical contexts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: context - the circumstances that form the setting for an event, statement, or idea
