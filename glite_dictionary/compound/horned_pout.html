horned pout

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd paʊt / (AmE), / hɔːnd paʊt / (BrE)
a small freshwater catfish, especially the brown bullhead (Ameiurus nebulosus), commonly found in eastern North America
a small catfish of eastern North America

Examples

The children were excited to catch a horned pout while fishing at the lake.
Many anglers in the region use worms to bait horned pout after sunset.
A horned pout can be identified by its whisker-like barbels and slightly flattened head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullhead - another name for the same species of catfish
