Black African

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈæfrɪkən / (AmE), / blæk ˈaf.rɪ.kən / (BrE)
a person from Africa who is of Black racial or ethnic background
person from Africa of Black descent

Examples

The census showed that the number of Black Africans in the city had increased.
She identifies as a Black African and is proud of her heritage.
Many Black Africans have made significant contributions to literature and science.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: African - a person from the continent of Africa
