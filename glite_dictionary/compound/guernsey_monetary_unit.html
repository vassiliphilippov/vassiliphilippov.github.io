Guernsey monetary unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɜrnzi ˈmʌnəˌtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːnzi ˈmʌnɪtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit used in Guernsey for its local currency
currency unit of Guernsey

Examples

The Guernsey monetary unit is separate from the British pound, though it is often accepted in local shops.
Exchange rates between the pound sterling and the Guernsey monetary unit are usually the same.
Tourists should be aware that the Guernsey monetary unit may not be accepted off the island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guernsey pound - the pound currency issued in Guernsey
  • Synonym: monetary unit - a standard unit of currency in a country
