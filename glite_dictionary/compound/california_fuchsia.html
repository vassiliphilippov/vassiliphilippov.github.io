California fuchsia

1. NOUN

/ kæləˌfɔrnjə ˈfjuːʃə / (AmE), / kælɪˌfɔːnɪə ˈfjuːʃə / (BrE)
a small shrub native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, with bright scarlet tubular flowers
shrublike plant with scarlet flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming California fuchsia, attracting dozens of hummingbirds each morning.
We planted some California fuchsia along the walkway for a splash of summer color.
Drought-tolerant and hardy, California fuchsia thrives in hot, sunny locations.
