Sami language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːmi ˌlæŋɡwɪd͡ʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language, or group of closely related languages, spoken by the Sami people in northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula.
language of the Sami people of northern Scandinavia

Examples

She is fluent in both Norwegian and Sami language.
The Sami language has several distinct dialects across the Arctic region.
Efforts are underway to preserve and revitalize the Sami language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnish language - the Uralic language spoken in Finland
  • Synonym: Norwegian language - the North Germanic language spoken in Norway

Etymology

Sami from the native ethnonym; "language" from Middle English, from Old French, from Latin "lingua".
