mock azalia

1. NOUN

/ mɑk əˈzeɪliə / (AmE), / mɒk əˈzeɪliə / (BrE)
A South African shrub with a swollen, succulent stem and showy pink and white flowers that bloom after the leaves fall, often grown as an ornamental plant in tropical regions.
succulent South African ornamental shrub

Examples

The mock azalia in her garden bloomed with beautiful pink and white flowers in the early summer.
Gardeners value the mock azalia for its decorative appearance and unusual succulent stem.
After the leaves dropped, the mock azalia put on a stunning floral display that drew the admiration of visitors.
