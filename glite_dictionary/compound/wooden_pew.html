wooden pew

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdn̩ pju / (AmE), / ˈwʊdn pjuː / (BrE)
a long bench made of wood, typically placed in rows in a church for people to sit on during services
long wooden bench, usually in churches

Examples

The sunlight streamed through the stained glass, illuminating the old wooden pew near the altar.
They spent most of the ceremony sitting quietly in a polished wooden pew.
The carpenter was hired to restore every wooden pew in the historic chapel.

Synset Examples

The old church had beautiful wooden pews.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pew - a long bench with a back, placed in rows in churches
