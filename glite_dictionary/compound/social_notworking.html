social notworking

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈnɒtˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈnɒtwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term referring to the lack of participation in social activities or ineffectiveness in social interactions.
lack of social engagement or interaction
informal

Examples

Extended periods of social notworking can make it difficult to form meaningful connections.
He attributed his loneliness to his habit of social notworking rather than reaching out to friends.
After months of social notworking, she decided to join a club to meet new people.

Synset Examples

Social notworking can lead to isolation and missed opportunities.
He joked about his social notworking during the pandemic.
The group was criticized for their social notworking habits.

Etymology

Blend of 'social networking' and 'not working', formed as a play on words.
