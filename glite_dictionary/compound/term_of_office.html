term of office

1. NOUN

/ tɝm əv ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / tɜːm əv ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
The fixed period of time during which a person holds a particular official position.
period holding a position or office

Examples

The president's term of office lasts four years according to the constitution.
She achieved a lot during her term of office as mayor.
After completing his term of office, he decided not to run for re-election.
