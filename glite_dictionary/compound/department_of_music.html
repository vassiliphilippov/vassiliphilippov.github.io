department of music

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
an academic division within a college or university responsible for teaching music and music appreciation
university academic division for music studies

Examples

She decided to enroll in the department of music to pursue her passion for piano.
The university's department of music hosts a concert series every spring.
Faculty in the department of music often collaborate with local orchestras and choirs.
