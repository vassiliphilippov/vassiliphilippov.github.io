biological process

1. NOUN

/ baɪ.əˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl ˈprɑ.ses / (AmE), / baɪ.əˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a process that takes place in a living organism, often involving chemical or physiological changes essential for life
process in a living organism

Examples

Photosynthesis is a biological process essential to plants and other organisms that convert sunlight into energy.
Aging is a natural biological process experienced by all living things.
Scientists study the biological process of cell division to better understand how organisms grow and heal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiological process - a process within an organism, especially related to bodily function
  • Synonym: chemical process - a process involving chemical changes, may occur in living or nonliving things
