solar magnetic field

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər məgˈnætɪk fiːld / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə məgˈnætɪk fiːld / (BrE)
the magnetic field produced by and surrounding the sun
the sun's magnetic field

Examples

Scientists study the solar magnetic field to better understand sunspots and solar flares.
The complex structure of the solar magnetic field influences space weather and conditions on Earth.
Changes in the solar magnetic field can affect satellite operations and communication systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic field - the region around a magnetic material or a moving electric charge within which the force of magnetism acts
