gunner's daughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnɚz ˈdɔtər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnəz ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
a tough woman or a child of a soldier, often implying early exposure to military life or firearms
tough woman or soldier's child

Examples

She was known as the gunner's daughter, tough and fearless.
Life as the gunner's daughter meant she learned to shoot early.
Every summer, the gunner's daughter would visit her father at the base.
