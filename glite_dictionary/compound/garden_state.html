Garden State

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑrdən steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of New Jersey, in the United States
nickname for New Jersey, US

Examples

New Jersey is often called the Garden State because of its lush farmland.
Many tourists visit the Garden State for its beautiful beaches and parks.
She grew up in the Garden State before moving to California.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Jersey - a state in the US, also known as the Garden State

Etymology

Refers to New Jersey's rich agricultural history and fertile land.
