potato chaser

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈtʃeɪsər / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
a person who is especially fond of potatoes or enjoys seeking out new potato dishes
enthusiast for potatoes and potato dishes

Examples

The local chef is a true potato chaser, always inventing new ways to cook potatoes.
As a proud potato chaser, she has tasted every kind of roasted potato in the city.
He called himself a potato chaser after trying mashed potatoes from every restaurant in the region.

Synset Examples

The food critic was known as a potato chaser in town.
As a potato chaser, she tried every variation of fries.
He considers himself a potato chaser, always looking for new recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foodie - a person with a particular interest in food
