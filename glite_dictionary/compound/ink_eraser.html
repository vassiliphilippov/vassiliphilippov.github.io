ink eraser

1. NOUN

/ ɪŋk ɪˈreɪsər / (AmE), / ɪŋk ɪˈreɪsə / (BrE)
An eraser designed specifically for removing ink marks from paper.
eraser for removing ink

Examples

The student used an ink eraser to remove the mistake from her notebook.
I always keep an ink eraser in my pencil case for accidental pen marks.
After he spilled ink on the page, he tried to clean it with an ink eraser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eraser - a rubber or device for removing pencil marks
