giving up

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the act of admitting defeat or stopping an attempt to achieve something
the act of admitting defeat

Examples

After years of struggling with the project, her giving up surprised everyone.
The team's giving up was seen as a sign that their morale had collapsed.
No one expected his sudden giving up after so much hard work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrender - the act of giving up and admitting defeat
  • Antonym: perseverance - continued effort in spite of difficulties or defeat

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvɪŋ ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ ˈʌp / (BrE)
the act of ceasing to make an effort; resignation or surrender, especially after struggle or persistence
the act of surrendering or quitting

Examples

After years of struggling with the project, his giving up surprised everyone.
The team's giving up in the final minutes led to their defeat.
Her giving up on her dreams was the saddest part of the story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quitting - the act of leaving or resigning from an effort or position
  • Antonym: perseverance - continued effort despite difficulties or delay in achieving success
