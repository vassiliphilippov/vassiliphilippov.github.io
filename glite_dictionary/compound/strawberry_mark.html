strawberry mark

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːbəri mɑːk / (BrE)
A soft red birthmark, usually raised, that resembles the surface of a strawberry; most commonly found in infants.
a soft red, raised birthmark
medical

Examples

The baby's strawberry mark was clearly visible on her cheek.
Some strawberry marks fade over time, while others may remain throughout life.
Doctors reassured the parents that the strawberry mark was harmless and would likely disappear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthmark - a colored mark on the skin present from birth
