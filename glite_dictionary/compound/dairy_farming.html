dairy farming

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛri ˌfɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeəri ˌfɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or practice of keeping cattle for the production of milk and milk products
business of producing milk and dairy

Examples

The region is well known for its dairy farming and high-quality cheeses.
Advances in technology have revolutionized dairy farming practices over the past few decades.
She grew up on a family-run dairy farming operation in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy - a place where milk is processed or sold, or milk products collectively
  • Degree Lower: cattle farming - the practice of raising cattle for milk or meat
  • Degree Higher: industrial dairy production - large-scale milk production using advanced techniques and technology
