smooth winterberry holly

1. NOUN

/ smuːð ˈwɪntərˌbɛri ˈhɑli / (AmE), / smuːð ˈwɪntəbəri ˈhɒli / (BrE)
A species of holly shrub (Ilex laevigata) native to eastern North America, known for its smooth leaves and bright red berries.
species of holly shrub (Ilex laevigata)

Examples

The smooth winterberry holly brightens winter gardens with its vibrant red fruit.
Gardeners often choose smooth winterberry holly for its tolerance of wet soils and decorative berries.
Birds are attracted to the berries of the smooth winterberry holly throughout the colder months.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: winterberry holly - a related holly shrub also known for its winter berries (Ilex verticillata)
