movie maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who produces or directs motion pictures; a filmmaker.
person who makes movies

Examples

As a movie maker, she is always looking for fresh stories to bring to the screen.
Many aspiring movie makers start with short films before moving to feature-length projects.
The renowned movie maker was honored at the film festival for his lifetime achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmmaker - a person who makes films, especially professionally
  • Synonym: director - a person in charge of making a film, play, or broadcast
  • Synonym: producer - a person responsible for the management of a film's production
