Republic of Ireland

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈaɪɚlənd / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈaɪələnd / (BrE)
the sovereign state occupying most of the island of Ireland, formerly part of the United Kingdom, independent since 1921
sovereign state in western Europe (Ireland)

Examples

She spent two years living in the Republic of Ireland after graduating from college.
The Republic of Ireland joined the European Economic Community in 1973.
Citizens of the Republic of Ireland can travel freely throughout the European Union.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ireland - island in the North Atlantic, including the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland
  • Antonym: Northern Ireland - region of the United Kingdom on the island of Ireland
