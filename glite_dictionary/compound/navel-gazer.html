navel-gazer

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvəl ˌɡeɪzər / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ˌɡeɪzə / (BrE)
a person who is excessively absorbed in their own thoughts, feelings, or introspections, often to the point of ignoring the outside world
self-absorbed or introspective person

Examples

He's such a navel-gazer, always analyzing his feelings instead of taking action.
Some critics dismiss poets as mere navel-gazers disconnected from real-world issues.
Don't be a navel-gazer—try focusing on others for a change.

Synset Examples

He's such a navel-gazer, always lost in thought.
Don’t be a navel-gazer; join the conversation!
As a navel-gazer, she often misses opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-absorbed - preoccupied with one's own feelings or interests
  • Synonym: introspective - characterized by or given to introspection
  • Antonym: outgoing - sociable; interested in others rather than oneself

Etymology

From the phrase 'navel-gazing,' originally referring to literal contemplation of one's navel as a form of meditation.
