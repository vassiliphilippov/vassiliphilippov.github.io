hand cream

1. NOUN

/ hænd kriːm / (AmE), / hænd kriːm / (BrE)
a cream designed to moisturize and protect the skin of the hands
moisturizing cream for the hands

Examples

She keeps a tube of hand cream in her purse for dry skin.
During winter, I apply hand cream several times a day.
The hand cream has a pleasant scent and absorbs quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moisturizer - a substance used for moisturizing the skin
