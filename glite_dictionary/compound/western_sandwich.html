western sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈsanwɪtʃ / (BrE)
A sandwich made with a western omelet, typically including ham, onions, and peppers, served between slices of bread.
sandwich with western omelet filling

Examples

I ordered a western sandwich for breakfast at the diner.
The western sandwich was packed with ham, onions, and green peppers.
He made a western sandwich using leftover omelet from yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: western omelet - an omelet with ham, onions, and peppers
