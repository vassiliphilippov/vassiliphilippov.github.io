crushed leather

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrʌʃt ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃt ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
Leather that has been treated to accentuate the grain pattern, often giving it a textured or wrinkled appearance.
leather with an accentuated grain texture

Examples

The designer handbag was made from crushed leather, giving it a unique, textured look.
Upholstery in crushed leather is both durable and stylish for modern interiors.
She preferred boots crafted from crushed leather for their rugged and distinctive appearance.
