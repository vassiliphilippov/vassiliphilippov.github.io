sewing room

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a home or building dedicated to sewing activities, usually equipped with sewing materials and machines
room dedicated for sewing

Examples

She turned the spare bedroom into a sewing room filled with fabric and patterns.
The old house has a dedicated sewing room at the back, next to the laundry.
Whenever I visit my grandmother, she is always working on a quilt in her sewing room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workroom - a room where work, especially manual work, is done
