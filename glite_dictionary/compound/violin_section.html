violin section

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈoʊlɪn ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / vaɪəˈlɪn ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the group of musicians within an orchestra who play the violin parts
group of violin players in an orchestra

Examples

The violin section led the melody during the symphony's second movement.
She joined the violin section after auditioning for the orchestra last spring.
The conductor praised the violin section for their precise timing and rich sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestra - a large group of musicians playing together on various instruments
