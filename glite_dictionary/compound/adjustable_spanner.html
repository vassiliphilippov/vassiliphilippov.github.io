adjustable spanner

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒʌstəbəl ˈspænɚ / (AmE), / əˈdʒʌstəbl ˈspænə / (BrE)
a spanner (wrench) with a movable jaw that allows it to fit nuts and bolts of different sizes
a wrench with an adjustable jaw

Examples

He used an adjustable spanner to loosen the stubborn bolt.
Every mechanic keeps an adjustable spanner in their toolbox for versatility.
My father taught me how to operate an adjustable spanner when I fixed my first bike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrench - a tool for gripping and turning objects
  • Synonym: spanner - a tool for gripping and turning nuts and bolts
