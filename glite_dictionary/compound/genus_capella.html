genus Capella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs kəˈpɛl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs kəˈpel.ə / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the family Scolopacidae, commonly known as snipes.
genus of birds (snipes)

Examples

The genus Capella comprises several species of snipes found in wetlands worldwide.
Birdwatchers often try to distinguish between different members of the genus Capella during migration season.
The unique tail feathers are a distinguishing feature of the genus Capella.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snipe - a wading bird with a long slender bill

Etymology

From Latin 'Capella', used in zoological taxonomy.
