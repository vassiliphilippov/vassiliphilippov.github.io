Old Bailey

1. PROPN

/ oʊld ˈbeɪli / (AmE), / əʊld ˈbeɪli / (BrE)
the Central Criminal Court in London, responsible for major criminal cases in England and Wales
central criminal court in London
british

Examples

He was tried at the Old Bailey for armed robbery.
Many famous cases have been heard at the Old Bailey.
The Old Bailey has played a key role in British legal history.

Etymology

Named after the street, Old Bailey, on which the court stands.
