Fiji time

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːdʒi taɪm / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi taɪm / (BrE)
an informal and relaxed approach to time and punctuality, characteristic of Fijian culture
relaxed attitude toward time

Examples

She quickly adjusted to the slower pace of Fiji time on the island.
