garden egg

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdən ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ɛɡ / (BrE)
a variety of eggplant with large, glossy edible fruit, especially cultivated in West Africa as a vegetable
a type of eggplant, cultivated vegetable

Examples

In her stew, she added chopped garden egg for extra flavor and texture.
The garden egg is a staple ingredient in many West African dishes.
She harvested a basket full of ripe garden eggs from her backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eggplant - a plant bearing large, egg-shaped purple fruit, commonly used as a vegetable
