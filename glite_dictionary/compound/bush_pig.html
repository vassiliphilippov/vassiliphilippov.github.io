bush pig

1. NOUN

/ bʊʃ pɪg / (AmE), / bʊʃ pɪg / (BrE)
a wild pig native to Africa, recognized for its coarse hair and adaptability to a variety of habitats
African wild pig species

Examples

We saw a bush pig foraging near the river during our safari.
The farmer was concerned that a bush pig had raided his crops again last night.
Unlike the warthog, the bush pig is usually active at night.

Synset Examples

The locals often joke about the size of a bush pig.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warthog - another species of wild pig found in Africa with distinctive facial warts
  • Synonym: wild boar - a wild ancestor of the domestic pig found in Europe and Asia
