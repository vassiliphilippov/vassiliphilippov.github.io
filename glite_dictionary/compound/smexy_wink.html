smexy wink

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɛksi wɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈsmɛksi wɪŋk / (BrE)
a flirtatious or attractively playful wink, often intended to be both smart and sexy
a flirtatiously attractive wink
slang informal

Examples

As he left the party, he threw a smexy wink over his shoulder at his crush.
Her smexy wink made everyone at the table burst out laughing.
The character gave the camera a smexy wink before the scene ended.

Synset Examples

He gave her a smexy wink across the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wink - a quick closing and opening of one eye, often as a signal or flirtation
  • Synonym: flirtatious - showing a playful sexual attraction; behaving in a flirtatious manner

Etymology

Blend of slang 'smexy' (from 'smart' + 'sexy') and 'wink'.
