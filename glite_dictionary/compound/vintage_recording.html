vintage recording

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ rɪˈkɔr.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ rɪˈkɔː.dɪŋ / (BrE)
a sound recording made in a previous era, often valued for its distinctive quality or historical significance
old sound recording of lasting value

Examples

Collectors often pay high prices for rare vintage recordings of jazz music.
Many music enthusiasts appreciate the warmth and character of vintage recordings from the 1960s.
She digitized her grandfather's vintage recordings to preserve them for future generations.

Synset Examples

I still use my old TDK tapes for some vintage recordings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antique recording - a very old or collectible recording, sometimes used similarly
  • Antonym: modern recording - a recent or current sound recording
