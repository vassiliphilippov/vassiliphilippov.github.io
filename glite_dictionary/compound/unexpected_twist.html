unexpected twist

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd twɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspɛktɪd twɪst / (BrE)
an occurrence in a story or situation that is surprising and changes the expected outcome or direction
surprising change in a story

Examples

The novel's ending included an unexpected twist that left readers stunned.
Just when she thought she had solved the puzzle, an unexpected twist changed everything.
That courtroom drama featured more than one unexpected twist in its plot.

Synset Examples

The movie was a melodrama with plenty of unexpected twists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot twist - a sudden and unexpected change in the course of a story
