great burdock

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt ˈbɜr.dɑk / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈbɜː.dɒk / (BrE)
a large burdock plant (Arctium lappa) with broad, heart-shaped leaves, native to Europe and Asia, sometimes cultivated for its medicinal and culinary uses
a large Eurasian burdock plant

Examples

The root of the great burdock is valued in traditional medicine across several cultures.
We spotted a towering great burdock in the corner of the old hedgerow.
Chefs sometimes use great burdock in Asian-inspired vegetable dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burdock - a plant with large leaves and prickly heads
