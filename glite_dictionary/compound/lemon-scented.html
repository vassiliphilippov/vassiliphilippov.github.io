lemon-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛmən ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having the smell or aroma of lemons.
smelling of lemons

Examples

The kitchen was filled with the fresh aroma of lemon-scented cleaner.
She bought a lemon-scented candle for her living room.
Many people prefer lemon-scented soap because it smells clean and refreshing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemony - having a taste or smell of lemons
  • Antonym: unscented - having no scent or fragrance
