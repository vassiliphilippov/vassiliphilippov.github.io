athletic game

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk ɡeɪm / (AmE), / əˈθlɛtɪk ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game that involves physical exercise or athletic competition.
game involving athletic activity

Examples

The school is hosting an athletic game between two local teams this weekend.
During the festival, several athletic games were organized for children and adults alike.
She has always enjoyed participating in any athletic game that challenges her stamina and skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sport - Activity involving physical exertion and skill in which individuals or teams compete against each other
