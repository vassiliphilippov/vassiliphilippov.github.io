wimpy stuff

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪmpi stʌf / (AmE), / ˈwɪmpi stʌf / (BrE)
something considered weak, ineffectual, or lacking in quality, especially compared to stronger alternatives
something weak or ineffective
informal

Examples

I asked for hot sauce, but all they had was that wimpy stuff that tastes like water.
He doesn't like wimpy stuff when it comes to exercise; he prefers real challenges.
This movie is too much wimpy stuff for horror fans who want some real scares.

Synset Examples

She prefers strong coffee, not that wimpy stuff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weak - lacking strength or power
  • Antonym: strong - having great strength or intensity

Etymology

From 'wimpy', an informal term meaning weak or feeble, from the cartoon character Wimpy (from Popeye), known for his lack of strength.
