orange fleabane

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrəndʒ ˈfliːˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈfliːˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a mat-forming herb native to Turkestan, having nearly double orange-yellow flowers; a plant of the genus Erigeron
herb with orange-yellow flowers from Turkestan

Examples

The botanical garden added orange fleabane to its collection of rare flowering plants.
The bright orange-yellow blossoms of orange fleabane bloom beautifully in early summer.
Gardeners recommend planting orange fleabane in well-drained soil to encourage healthy growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fleabane - any plant of the genus Erigeron, often with daisy-like flowers
