traditional recipe

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənəl ˈrɛsɪpi / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl ˈrɛsɪpi / (BrE)
A recipe that has been passed down through generations, often reflecting the culture or heritage of a family or community.
recipe handed down through generations

Examples

My grandmother taught me her traditional recipe for beef stew, and I cherish the memories every time I make it.
Many holiday feasts feature at least one traditional recipe that has been enjoyed by the family for decades.
Cooking a traditional recipe connects people to their history and cultural roots.

Synset Examples

Many Muslime families share traditional recipes during Ramadan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family recipe - a recipe passed within a family, often for several generations
