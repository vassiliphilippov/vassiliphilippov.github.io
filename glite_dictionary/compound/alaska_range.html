Alaska Range

1. PROPN

/ əˈlæskə reɪndʒ / (AmE), / əˈlæskə reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a mountain range in south central Alaska that includes Denali (Mount McKinley), the highest peak in North America.
mountain range in south central Alaska

Examples

Scientists conduct research in the Alaska Range to study its unique glacial formations.
The Alaska Range offers challenging climbs for experienced mountaineers seeking adventure.
Wolves and caribou roam the vast wilderness surrounding the Alaska Range.

Related Terms
