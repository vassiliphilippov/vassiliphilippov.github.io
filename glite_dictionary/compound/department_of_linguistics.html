department of linguistics

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtmənt əv lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks / (BrE)
An academic department within a university or college that is responsible for teaching and research in linguistics.
academic unit for linguistics studies

Examples

She was accepted into the department of linguistics at her university, where she plans to specialize in phonetics.
The department of linguistics will host a conference on endangered languages next spring.
Many students in the department of linguistics participate in fieldwork as part of their studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguistics - the scientific study of language
