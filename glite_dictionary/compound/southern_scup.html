southern scup

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn skʌp / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən skʌp / (BrE)
A species of porgy fish found in the southern Atlantic coastal waters of North America.
a porgy of southern Atlantic North America

Examples

Fishermen along the Atlantic coast often catch southern scup during the warm summer months.
The southern scup is prized for its mild flavor and flaky flesh.
Populations of southern scup have been monitored to ensure sustainable fishing practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porgy - a fish of the family Sparidae, also called scup
  • Synonym: scup - a fish of the Atlantic coast of North America, similar to a porgy
