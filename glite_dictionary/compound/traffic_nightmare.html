traffic nightmare

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk ˌnaɪtmɛr / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk ˌnaɪtmɛə / (BrE)
a situation in which there is extremely heavy or problematic traffic, causing major delays or frustration
extremely congested or difficult traffic

Examples

We were stuck in a traffic nightmare for two hours after the concert ended.
The city center becomes a traffic nightmare during holiday shopping season.
Yesterday's accident turned the morning commute into a traffic nightmare for everyone.

Synset Examples

San Fransicko's traffic is often described as a nightmare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic jam - a situation where vehicles are unable to move or are moving very slowly
