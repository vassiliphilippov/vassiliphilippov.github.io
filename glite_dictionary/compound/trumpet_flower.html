trumpet flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpət ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
an evergreen North American vine of the honeysuckle family with coral-red or orange trumpet-shaped flowers
evergreen vine with trumpet-shaped flowers

Examples

The trumpet flower attracts hummingbirds with its bright orange blossoms.
She planted a trumpet flower in her garden to add a splash of color to the fence.
The vine's trumpet flowers bloom from late spring through early autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trumpet vine - another name for the same plant species with trumpet-shaped flowers

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A Mexican evergreen climbing plant with large, solitary, funnel-shaped, fragrant yellow flowers marked by purple-brown ridges in the throat.
Mexican vine with large yellow flowers

Examples

The vibrant trumpet flower blooms attract hummingbirds and butterflies to the garden.
She planted a trumpet flower next to the trellis to add tropical beauty to her backyard.
The sweet fragrance of the trumpet flower filled the air on warm summer evenings.

3. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A woody vine native to the southern United States, with large, trumpet-shaped flowers and stems that show a cross in transverse section.
woody vine with trumpet-shaped flowers

Examples

The bright orange blooms of the trumpet flower attract hummingbirds to the garden every summer.
We planted a trumpet flower vine along the fence to add color and shade.
The unique cross in the stem makes the trumpet flower easy to identify among other vines.

  • Synonym: trumpet creeper - woody vine with trumpet-shaped flowers, synonym
