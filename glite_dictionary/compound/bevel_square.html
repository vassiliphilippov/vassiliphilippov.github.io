bevel square

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛvəl skwɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɛvəl skweə / (BrE)
a hand tool with two hinged arms used to measure or mark angles other than 90°
tool for measuring or marking angles

Examples

The carpenter used a bevel square to mark the angle on the board.
Before cutting, she adjusted the bevel square to 45 degrees.
A bevel square is essential for projects involving complex joinery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: try square - a tool for checking and marking right angles
