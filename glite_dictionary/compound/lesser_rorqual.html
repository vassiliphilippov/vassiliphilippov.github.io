lesser rorqual

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈrɔrkwəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈrɔːkwəl / (BrE)
a small baleen whale (Balaenoptera acutorostrata), also known as the minke whale, found in coastal waters of the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans
a species of small baleen whale

Examples

Marine biologists tracked a lesser rorqual as it migrated south for the winter.
We spotted a lesser rorqual surfacing near the coast during our whale-watching tour.
Unlike the larger blue whale, the lesser rorqual is more commonly seen near shorelines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minke whale - another common name for the same species of whale
  • Degree Higher: fin whale - a larger species of rorqual whale, closely related
