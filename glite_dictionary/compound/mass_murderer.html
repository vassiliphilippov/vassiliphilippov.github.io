mass murderer

1. NOUN

/ mæs ˈmɜrdərər / (AmE), / mæs ˈmɜːr.dər.ər / (BrE)
a person who kills many people in a single event or incident
person who commits multiple murders in one event

Examples

The police worked quickly to apprehend the mass murderer responsible for the tragedy.
Many documentaries explore the motives behind infamous mass murderers.
Communities often struggle to recover from the trauma caused by a mass murderer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serial killer - a person who murders several people over a period of time, usually following a characteristic pattern
  • Degree Lower: murderer - a person who kills another person unlawfully and with intent
