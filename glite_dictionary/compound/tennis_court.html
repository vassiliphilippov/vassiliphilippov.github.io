tennis court

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs kɔːt / (BrE)
an area marked with lines and equipped for playing the game of tennis
area for playing tennis

Examples

There is a newly built tennis court in the park downtown.
We booked the tennis court for two hours this afternoon.
Rain had left the tennis court too slippery to play on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - a marked area used for sports such as tennis, basketball, or squash
