wise woman

1. NOUN

/ waɪz ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / waɪz ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is valued for her wisdom and is trusted to give advice or guidance, often regarded historically as a healer or folk expert
a woman trusted for her wisdom and advice

Examples

Villagers often sought the help of the wise woman for remedies and guidance.
The wise woman was respected for her knowledge and understanding of herbs.
In the story, the hero consults a wise woman before his journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise man - a man valued for his wisdom and trusted for advice
