contour sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tʊr ʃit / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tjʊə ʃiːt / (BrE)
a bed sheet, usually with elastic edges, designed to fit snugly over a mattress
fitted bed sheet with elastic edges

Examples

She bought a new set of contour sheets for her queen-size bed.
The contour sheet stayed in place all night, unlike flat sheets that often bunch up.
He struggled to find a contour sheet that fit his unusually thick mattress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fitted sheet - a bed sheet with elastic corners to fit a mattress
