power point

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an electrical socket in a wall where electrical devices can be plugged in to receive power
electrical wall socket
british

Examples

The vacuum cleaner wouldn't work until I plugged it into the power point.
Don't overload the power point with too many appliances at once.
She checked that every power point in the office was switched off before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socket - a device in a wall for connecting electrical equipment to the power supply
  • Antonym: plug - a device for making an electrical connection by insertion into a socket
