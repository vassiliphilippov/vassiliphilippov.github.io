sense of style

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv staɪl / (AmE), / sɛns əv staɪl / (BrE)
a distinctive and attractive way of expressing oneself, especially in matters of fashion or appearance
distinctive personal style, especially in fashion

Examples

She always impresses me with her unique sense of style.
Even at a young age, he had an impeccable sense of style.
Having a strong sense of style can help you stand out in a crowd.

Synset Examples

She abounds in her own sense of style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion sense - an awareness or intuitive understanding of fashion and style
