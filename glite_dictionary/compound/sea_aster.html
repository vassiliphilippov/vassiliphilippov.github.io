sea aster

1. NOUN

/ si ˈæstər / (AmE), / si ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A European flowering plant of the daisy family that grows in salt marshes and coastal areas.
salt marsh European flowering plant

Examples

The sea aster is often found blossoming along coastal salt marshes in late summer.
Walking by the estuary, we spotted several sea asters with their pale lilac flowers.
Some conservation projects aim to protect wild habitats where sea aster thrives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the daisy family with star-shaped flower heads
