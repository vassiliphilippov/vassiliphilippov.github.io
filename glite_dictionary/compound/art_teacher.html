art teacher

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈtitʃɚ / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a teacher who instructs students in visual arts such as painting, drawing, or sculpture
teacher of visual arts

Examples

The art teacher introduced the class to watercolor painting techniques.
My sister is an art teacher at the local high school.
The art teacher organized a student exhibition at the community center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
