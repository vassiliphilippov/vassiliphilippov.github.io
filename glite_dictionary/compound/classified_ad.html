classified ad

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsəˌfaɪd æd / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪfaɪd æd / (BrE)
a short advertisement in a newspaper or magazine, grouped with similar small ads, often offering goods or services for sale
newspaper or magazine small advertisement

Examples

He placed a classified ad in the local newspaper to sell his bicycle.
Many people find jobs through classified ads in magazines and online platforms.
The classified ad section is at the back of the Sunday paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertisement - a public notice promoting products or services

Etymology

From the adjective 'classified' (arranged in categories) + 'ad', abbreviation of advertisement.
