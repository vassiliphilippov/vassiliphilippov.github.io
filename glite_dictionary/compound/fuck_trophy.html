fuck trophy

1. NOUN

/ fʌk ˈtroʊfi / (AmE), / fʌk ˈtrəʊfi / (BrE)
A meaningless or worthless prize, often used to dismiss the value of an accomplishment in a vulgar way.
meaningless or worthless prize (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

Winning that cheap medal was just a fuck trophy to him, and he didn't care about it.
The award felt like a fuck trophy after realizing how little effort was required to win.
She called the participation ribbon a fuck trophy—it meant nothing to her.
