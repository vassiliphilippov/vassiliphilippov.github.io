Qiang language

1. NOUN

/ tʃjɑŋ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tʃjɑːŋ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Tibeto-Burman language spoken in the Sichuan province of China
language of the Qiang people in Sichuan, China

Examples

There are only a few villages where the Qiang language is still spoken regularly.
She is conducting research on the grammar of the Qiang language.
Preserving the Qiang language is important for maintaining the community’s cultural heritage.

Etymology

From Qiang, the name of the ethnic group, plus 'language'.
