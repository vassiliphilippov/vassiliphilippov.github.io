spiky shoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪki ʃu / (AmE), / ˈspaɪki ʃuː / (BrE)
a shoe with pointed protrusions or spikes, often used for style or for grip in sports
footwear with pointed protrusions

Examples

He wore spiky shoes to stand out at the party.
Athletes often use spiky shoes for better traction on the field.
Her collection includes several pairs of fashionable spiky shoes.

Synset Examples

He wore spiky shoes for a unique look.
