common nutcracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈnʌtˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈnʌtˌkrækə / (BrE)
a large, brownish-black bird of the crow family, found in Eurasia, noted for feeding on pine seeds
Eurasian bird of the crow family

Examples

The common nutcracker is often seen cracking open pine cones to feed on the seeds.
We spotted a common nutcracker during our hike in the alpine forest.
Unlike other members of the crow family, the common nutcracker has a distinctive spotted plumage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutcracker - bird of the genus Nucifraga
