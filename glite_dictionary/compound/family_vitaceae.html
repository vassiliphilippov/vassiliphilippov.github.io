family Vitaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli vaɪˈteɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli vɪˈteɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, commonly known as the grape family, consisting mainly of woody vines
the grape plant family of vines
medical

Examples

Several important fruit crops, including grapes, belong to the family Vitaceae.
The family Vitaceae is widespread in tropical and temperate regions worldwide.
Ampelography is the study of identifying plants in the family Vitaceae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grapevine - a climbing plant of the genus Vitis, bearing grapes

Etymology

From Latin 'vitis' (vine) plus New Latin suffix '-aceae' denoting plant families.
