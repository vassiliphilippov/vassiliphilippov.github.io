lesser prairie chicken

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsɚ ˈprɛri ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈprɛəri ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
A species of prairie grouse, smaller than the greater prairie chicken, found in western Texas and nearby areas.
a smaller species of prairie grouse

Examples

The lesser prairie chicken inhabits grasslands in the southern plains of the United States.
Conservationists are working to protect the dwindling population of the lesser prairie chicken.
The lesser prairie chicken performs an elaborate mating dance during the spring.

Related Terms
