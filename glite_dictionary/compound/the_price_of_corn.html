the price of corn

1. NOUN

/ ðə praɪs əv kɔrn / (AmE), / ðə praɪs əv kɔːn / (BrE)
an expression used to question the relevance or connection of something being discussed
expression questioning relevance

Examples

He launched into a story that had nothing to do with the price of corn.
Why are you bringing up last week's weather? What does that have to do with the price of corn?
During the meeting, someone asked about the price of corn to point out how off-topic the discussion had become.

Synset Examples

Your excuse for being late, what does that have to do with the price of corn?
Discussing politics, what does that have to do with the price of corn?
Your opinion on fashion, what does that have to do with the price of corn?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the price of tea in China - an expression questioning the relevance of a statement or topic
