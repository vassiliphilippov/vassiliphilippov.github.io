two-man tent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtu mæn tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtuː mæn tɛnt / (BrE)
a tent designed to accommodate two people
tent for two people

Examples

We packed a two-man tent for our weekend hiking trip.
The couple slept comfortably in their two-man tent each night.
You can borrow my two-man tent if you don't have one for the camping festival.
