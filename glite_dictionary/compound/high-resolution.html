high-resolution

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˌrɛzəˈluʃən / (AmE), / haɪ ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
(of a device or image) producing images that are sharp and show a lot of detail.
producing sharp, detailed images

Examples

The new smartphone camera can take high-resolution photos even in low light.
We watched the documentary on a high-resolution television and the details were stunning.
That laboratory uses high-resolution microscopes for advanced biological research.

Synset Examples

high-resolution photography
a high-resolution lens
high-resolution television

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-resolution - producing images that are not sharp or finely detailed
  • Synonym: high-definition - offering a very clear and detailed picture, especially in television or video
