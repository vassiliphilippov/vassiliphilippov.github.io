contract killing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.trækt ˌkɪl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trækt ˌkɪl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of murdering someone in accordance with an agreement, usually for payment, by a hired killer
murder for hire

Examples

Police believe the murder was a contract killing ordered by a criminal gang.
He was arrested for allegedly arranging a contract killing of his business rival.
The film depicts the dark world of contract killing and organized crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assassination - the killing of someone important or famous, often for political reasons
  • Synonym: murder - the unlawful killing of a person with intent
