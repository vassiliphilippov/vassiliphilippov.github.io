before Christ

1. ADV

/ bɪˈfɔr kraɪst / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː kraɪst / (BrE)
used to indicate years before the supposed birth year of Jesus Christ, written after the year number (abbreviation: BC)
of years before the birth of Christ

Examples

The city was founded in 500 before Christ and became a major trading center.
Archaeologists dated the artifact to 1200 before Christ.
This civilization flourished many centuries before Christ.

Synset Examples

in 200 BC

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BC - abbreviation for 'before Christ', used with dates
  • Antonym: AD - used for years after the birth of Christ (Anno Domini)
  • Synonym: BCE - 'before common era', secular alternative to 'before Christ'
