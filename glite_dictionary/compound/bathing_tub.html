bathing tub

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ tʌb / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ tʌb / (BrE)
a large, open container designed to hold water for washing the body in
large container for bathing

Examples

She filled the bathing tub with warm water and lavender oil after a long day.
In older houses, you might find a clawfoot bathing tub in the bathroom.
The hotel suite features a luxurious marble bathing tub overlooking the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bathtub - a tub in which you can bathe
