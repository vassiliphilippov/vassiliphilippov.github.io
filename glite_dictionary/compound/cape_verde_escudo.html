Cape Verde escudo

1. NOUN

/ ˌkeɪp vɜrd ɛˈskuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp ˈvəːd ɛˈskuːdəʊ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Cape Verde, equal to 100 centavos
currency of Cape Verde

Examples

The Cape Verde escudo has a stable exchange rate with the euro.
Prices across the islands are listed in Cape Verde escudos.
Tourists can exchange euros for Cape Verde escudo at the airport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escudo - former currency unit in Portugal and other countries
  • Degree Lower: centavo - a monetary unit equal to one hundredth of the basic unit in many Spanish/Portuguese-speaking countries

Etymology

From Portuguese 'escudo', meaning 'shield', used as a currency name in several countries; Cape Verde was a former Portuguese colony.
