Jonah crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒoʊnə kræb / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊnə kræb / (BrE)
a large red deep-water crab found along the eastern coast of North America, valued as seafood
a large red crab of eastern North America

Examples

The fishermen hauled in several Jonah crabs along with their lobster traps.
Jonah crab meat is increasingly popular in North American seafood restaurants.
Marine biologists studied the migration patterns of the Jonah crab in winter.
