jockey cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑki kæp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒki kap / (BrE)
A close-fitting, brimmed cap, typically worn by jockeys while riding horses.
cap with a bill, worn by jockeys

Examples

He wore a bright red jockey cap during the race.
The jockey cap is designed to stay secure even at high speeds.
You can buy a replica jockey cap at the gift shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball cap - a soft cap with a rounded crown and a stiff bill

Etymology

From 'jockey' (a professional horse rider) and 'cap' (head covering).
