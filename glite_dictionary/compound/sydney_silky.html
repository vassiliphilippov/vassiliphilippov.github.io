Sydney silky

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪdni ˈsɪlki / (AmE), / ˈsɪdni ˈsɪlki / (BrE)
An Australian breed of toy dog known for its silky blue coat.
Australian toy dog breed with silky blue coat

Examples

The Sydney silky is a popular companion dog in Australia due to its lively temperament.
Her family adopted a young Sydney silky from the local shelter.
At the dog show, the Sydney silky drew attention for its beautiful coat and playful nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yorkshire terrier - Small dog breed with long silky coat, related ancestor of the Sydney silky

Etymology

Named after Sydney, Australia, where the breed originated, and 'silky' for the texture of its coat.
