pizza bone

1. NOUN

/ ˈpitsə boʊn / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə bəʊn / (BrE)
a dog chew shaped like a slice of pizza or pizza crust, given as a treat for dogs
chew treat for dogs shaped like pizza

Examples

After dinner, she rewarded her dog with a pizza bone for good behavior.
The pet store sells several flavors of pizza bone treats.
My puppy loves to gnaw on his pizza bone for hours.

Synset Examples

He chewed on the pizza bone while watching TV.
She gave her dog a pizza bone as a treat.
The dog buried his pizza bone in the yard.
