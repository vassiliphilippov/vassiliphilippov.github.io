beau monde

1. NOUN

/ boʊ ˈmɑːnd / (AmE), / bəʊ ˈmɒnd / (BrE)
the fashionable or stylish society; people who are prominent in fashionable circles
fashionable elite or high society

Examples

The city's beau monde gathered for the annual charity gala.
She was introduced into the beau monde after her marriage.
Rumors about the beau monde often filled the society pages of newspapers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high society - the social class of people with the highest social status
  • Synonym: jet set - wealthy and fashionable people who travel widely for pleasure

Etymology

From French 'beau monde' meaning 'beautiful world'.
