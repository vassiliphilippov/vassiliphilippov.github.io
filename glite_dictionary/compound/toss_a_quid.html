to toss a quid

1. VERB

/ tɑs ə kwɪd / (AmE), / tɒs ə kwɪd / (BrE)
to throw a £1 coin as a gesture for luck
throw a £1 coin for luck
british

Examples

Some tourists believe that if you toss a quid into the river, your wish will be granted.
