think piece

1. NOUN

/ θɪŋk piːs / (AmE), / θɪŋk piːs / (BrE)
an article in a newspaper, magazine, or journal presenting opinions or analysis rather than only factual reporting
analytical or opinion-based article

Examples

The magazine published a think piece exploring the implications of new genetic technology.
He was invited to write a think piece about the impact of social media on elections.
Many readers enjoy the Sunday section's think pieces on culture and society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editorial - an article expressing opinions or commentary, often by the editor
  • Synonym: feature article - a prominent article giving in-depth coverage of a topic

2. NOUN

/ θɪŋk pis / (AmE), / θɪŋk piːs / (BrE)
An article or essay that presents reflective or analytical commentary on a particular subject, often intended to provoke thought or discussion.
analytical or reflective article or essay

Examples

The magazine published a think piece exploring the ethics of genetic engineering.
She submitted a think piece to the newspaper about urban development.
After reading his think piece, I started rethinking my own assumptions about technology.

Synset Examples

I read a fascinating think piece about the future of AI.
The article was a compelling think piece on climate change.
He wrote a thought-provoking think piece on social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: op-ed - an article in a newspaper or magazine expressing the opinion of a named writer
