Desert Fox

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɛzɚt fɑks / (AmE), / ˈdɛzət fɒks / (BrE)
a nickname for German Field Marshal Erwin Rommel, noted for his military leadership in North Africa during World War II
nickname for Erwin Rommel, WWII general

Examples

During World War II, Desert Fox led the Afrika Korps with remarkable skill.
Many military historians study the strategies employed by the Desert Fox during the North African campaign.
The legacy of the Desert Fox remains controversial due to his role in Nazi Germany.

Etymology

Nickname derived from Rommel’s tactics in the North African desert campaign.
