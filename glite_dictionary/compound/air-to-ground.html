air-to-ground

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛr tə ˈɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˌeə tə ˈɡraʊnd / (BrE)
(of weapons or communications) operating from or designed to be fired from aircraft at targets on the ground.
from aircraft to ground targets

Examples

The fighter jet was equipped with air-to-ground missiles for the mission.
They developed a new air-to-ground radar system for detecting enemy vehicles.
The pilot received air-to-ground communication from the control center.

Synset Examples

air-to-surface missiles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air-to-surface - from aircraft to targets on the ground, especially of missiles; synonym of 'air-to-ground'
  • Antonym: surface-to-air - (of missiles or weapons) launched from the ground toward aircraft; antonym of 'air-to-ground'
