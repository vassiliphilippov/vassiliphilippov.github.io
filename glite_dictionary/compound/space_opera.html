space opera

1. NOUN

/ speɪs ˈɑːpɚə / (AmE), / speɪs ˈɒpərə / (BrE)
A subgenre of science fiction characterized by adventurous, dramatic stories set primarily in outer space, often involving conflict between powerful characters or empires.
adventurous science fiction set in space

Examples

The new movie is a classic space opera with epic battles and interstellar drama.
She wrote a bestselling space opera series that has a cult following.
Fans of space opera often enjoy detailed world-building and complex galactic politics.

Synset Examples

He loves the dramatic plots of space operas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: science fiction - fiction dealing with futuristic concepts, technology, or space travel
