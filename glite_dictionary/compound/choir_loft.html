choir loft

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪɚ lɔft / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪə lɒft / (BrE)
a raised gallery in a church where the choir is seated during services
gallery in a church for the choir

Examples

The organist played softly from the choir loft above the congregation.
Visitors admired the view of the nave from the old choir loft.
Members of the choir climbed the stairs to the choir loft before the service began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallery - raised platform or balcony in a church
