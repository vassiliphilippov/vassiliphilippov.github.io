bell deck

1. NOUN

/ bɛl dɛk / (AmE), / bɛl dɛk / (BrE)
The floor located beneath the bells in an open belfry, usually providing support and access within a bell tower.
floor under bells in belfry

Examples

The old church's bell deck was made of oak and showed signs of wear.
Visitors climbed to the bell deck to see the impressive array of church bells above.
Renovations included reinforcing the bell deck to support the newly cast bronze bells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belfry - a bell tower, or part of one, especially one that houses bells
