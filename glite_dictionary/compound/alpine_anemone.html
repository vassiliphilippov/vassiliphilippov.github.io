Alpine anemone

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.paɪn əˈnɛm.oʊni / (AmE), / ˈæl.paɪn əˈnɛm.əni / (BrE)
a perennial herb native to the Rocky Mountains, noted for its silky foliage and bluish-white flowers
a silky-foliaged herb with bluish-white flowers

Examples

We spotted an Alpine anemone blooming along the high mountain trail.
The delicate petals of the Alpine anemone trembled in the breeze.
Gardeners admire the Alpine anemone for its unusual foliage and graceful flowers.
