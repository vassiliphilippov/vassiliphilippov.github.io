travel tip

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl tɪp / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl tɪp / (BrE)
a piece of advice or useful information for people who are planning or undertaking a journey
advice or information for travelers

Examples

She gave me a great travel tip about avoiding airport crowds on weekends.
Before my trip to Italy, I researched online for helpful travel tips.
One useful travel tip is to pack light so it's easier to move around.

Synset Examples

Every globie I know loves sharing travel tips online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recommendation - a suggestion or piece of advice
  • Synonym: advice - guidance or suggestions for action
