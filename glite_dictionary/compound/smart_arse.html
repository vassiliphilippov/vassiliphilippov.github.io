smart arse

1. NOUN

/ smɑrt ɑrs / (AmE), / smɑːt ɑːs / (BrE)
a person who makes sarcastic, clever, or cheeky remarks, often in a way that is perceived as rude or irritating
sarcastic or cheeky person (British slang)
informal slang british

Examples

Don't be such a smart arse when your teacher asks a serious question.
Everyone laughed at the smart arse in the back row who always had a witty reply.
His constant smart arse attitude can get on people's nerves.

Synset Examples

Her smart arse remarks often lighten the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart ass - a person who makes clever or sarcastic remarks, especially in an irritating way (American usage)

Etymology

Formed from 'smart' (clever) and 'arse' (British English for 'buttocks'), with 'arse' used as a mild pejorative; British equivalent of 'smart ass.'
