signal light

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡnəl laɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəl laɪt / (BrE)
a fire or light set up as a signal, especially to communicate over a distance
fire or light used as a signal

Examples

The soldiers built a signal light on top of the hill to alert the villagers.
In times of emergency, a signal light could guide ships safely to shore.
A signal light blazed in the night, warning of an approaching enemy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beacon - a fire or light set up in a high or prominent position as a signal or warning
