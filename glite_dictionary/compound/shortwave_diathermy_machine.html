shortwave diathermy machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrtˌweɪv daɪˈθɜrmi məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtˌweɪv daɪˈθɜːmi məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a medical device that uses shortwave electromagnetic radiation to generate deep heat in body tissues for therapeutic purposes
medical device for deep tissue heating
medical

Examples

The shortwave diathermy machine was used to relieve the patient's deep muscle pain during therapy.
Physiotherapists often rely on a shortwave diathermy machine to promote healing in chronic injuries.
Before using the shortwave diathermy machine, the technician explained the procedure to the patient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diathermy - therapeutic production of heat in body tissue by electric currents
