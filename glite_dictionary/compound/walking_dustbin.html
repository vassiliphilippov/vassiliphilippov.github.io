walking dustbin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkɪŋ ˈdʌstˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ˈdʌstbɪn / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who eats excessively or messily, especially junk food
person who eats messily or excessively
informal

Examples

Ever since college started, my roommate has become a walking dustbin with all the takeout he orders.
Their teenage son is a walking dustbin, devouring whatever snacks he can find in the house.
At the party, everyone joked that Mike was the walking dustbin for leftover pizza and chips.

Synset Examples

That kid is a walking dustbin, always munching on chips.
She refers to her brother as a walking dustbin for snacks.
He eats so much junk food, he's a walking dustbin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glutton - person who habitually eats to excess
