genus Eucarya

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs juːˈkeɪ.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs juːˈkɑː.ri.ə / (BrE)
A genus of trees in the sandalwood family, commonly known as quandong trees, native to Australia.
genus of quandong trees

Examples

The genus Eucarya encompasses several species of Australian quandong trees.
Fruit from trees of the genus Eucarya is valued for its taste and nutritional content.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the genus Eucarya in Western Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quandong - an Australian tree or its edible fruit
