au fait

1. ADJ

/ oʊ ˈfeɪ / (AmE), / əʊ ˈfeɪ / (BrE)
fully knowledgeable about or familiar with a particular subject, situation, or recent developments.
fully informed; knowledgeable

Examples

She always makes sure she is au fait with the latest research in her field.
Before the meeting, I need to be au fait with the project details.
He is not au fait with current policies and often makes outdated statements.

Synset Examples

kept abreast of the latest developments
constant revision keeps the book au courant
always au fait on the latest events
up on the news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: up to date - informed about the latest developments
  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness

Etymology

From French "au fait" meaning "to the fact".
