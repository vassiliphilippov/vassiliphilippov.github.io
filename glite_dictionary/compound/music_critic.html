music critic

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk ˌkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who reviews and evaluates musical performances or recordings professionally
a professional reviewer of musical performances

Examples

The music critic praised the orchestra’s moving performance at the concert hall.
As a respected music critic, she writes insightful reviews for a leading newspaper.
Many aspiring musicians hope for a favorable review from a renowned music critic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critic - a person who judges or evaluates creative work
