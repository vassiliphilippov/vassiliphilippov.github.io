front hole

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt hoʊl / (AmE), / frʌnt həʊl / (BrE)
an opening located at the front of something, designed to provide convenient access
opening at the front for access

Examples

The new backpack design features a front hole for quick access to your keys.
Her jacket's front hole made it easier to fasten and unfasten.
Some modern appliances include a front hole for easier maintenance and repair.

Synset Examples

The sweater has a front hole for easy access.
