crabapple jelly

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræbˌæpəl ˈdʒɛli / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌæpəl ˈdʒɛli / (BrE)
a sweet and tart jelly made from the fruit of crabapple trees, typically used as a spread or condiment
jelly made from crabapples

Examples

She spread crabapple jelly on her toast every morning.
Homemade crabapple jelly makes a thoughtful gift during the holidays.
The tartness of crabapple jelly pairs well with roast meats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jelly - a sweet, clear spread made from fruit juice and sugar
