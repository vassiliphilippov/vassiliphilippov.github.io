exercise device

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksərˌsaɪz dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛksəsaɪz dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A piece of equipment designed to help a person perform physical exercise.
equipment for physical exercise

Examples

She set up a new exercise device in her home gym to improve her fitness routine.
Doctors often recommend using an exercise device to aid recovery after injuries.
The gym boasts a wide variety of exercise devices for different types of workouts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise equipment - machines or tools used for physical exercise
