unwoke attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈwoʊk ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈwəʊk ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an outlook or set of beliefs that shows little or no awareness of social injustices or inequalities
lack of awareness of social injustice
informal

Examples

His unwoke attitude led to heated debates during the meeting.
Many criticized the politician for expressing an unwoke attitude about equality issues.
The company’s outdated policies reflect an unwoke attitude toward diversity.

Synset Examples

The article highlighted how unwoke attitudes harm social progress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: woke - alert to social injustices and discrimination

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'woke' (aware of social injustice), plus 'attitude'.
