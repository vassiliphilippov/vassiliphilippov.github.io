senior high school

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniɚ haɪ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə haɪ skuːl / (BrE)
A public secondary school, typically including grades 9 through 12, attended after junior high or middle school.
public secondary school (grades 9–12)

Examples

Many students look forward to starting senior high school in the fall.
Her senior high school offers a wide variety of advanced placement courses.
After graduating from senior high school, he applied to several universities.

Synset Examples

he goes to the neighborhood highschool

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: junior high school - a school for students typically in grades 7–9, preceding senior high school
  • Synonym: high school - a general term for a secondary school, particularly in the U.S.
