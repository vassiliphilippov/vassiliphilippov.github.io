bed pillow

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈpɪloʊ / (AmE), / bɛd ˈpɪləʊ / (BrE)
a soft, rectangular cushion used for supporting the head while sleeping in bed
pillow used for sleeping

Examples

She fluffed her bed pillow before lying down for the night.
He bought a new bed pillow for added neck support.
The hotel room had two soft bed pillows on each bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pillow - a cushion for the head, usually used during sleep
