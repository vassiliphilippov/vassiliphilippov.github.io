complex function

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplɛks ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / kəmˈplɛks ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)

mathematical function with multiple variables or intricate behavior

Examples

The graph of the complex function revealed unexpected patterns in the data.
