table linen

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˌlɪnən / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˌlɪnɪn / (BrE)
cloth items such as napkins, tablecloths, or placemats used to cover a dining table and serve food.
cloth items for the dining table

Examples

She unfolded the table linen before setting the plates and silverware.
The restaurant prides itself on using crisp white table linen for every meal.
After dinner, she carefully washed and ironed all the table linen.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: linens - household articles made of cloth, such as sheets and towels
