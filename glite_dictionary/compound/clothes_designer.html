clothes designer

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / kləʊðz dɪˈzaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to create and design clothing items
person who creates clothing designs

Examples

She worked as a clothes designer in Paris for several years.
The clothes designer unveiled his latest collection at the fashion show.
Many aspiring artists dream of becoming a famous clothes designer one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion designer - a person who designs fashionable clothing and accessories
