daily routine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli ruˈtin / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
the usual sequence of activities that a person does every day
regular schedule of daily activities

Examples

Sticking to a daily routine helps me feel more organized and productive.
Her daily routine changed dramatically after she moved to a new city.
Children often benefit from having a consistent daily routine.

Synset Examples

My daily routine includes a morning workout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schedule - a plan of activities or events and when they will happen
