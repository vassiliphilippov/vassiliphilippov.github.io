heavy meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi miːl / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi miːl / (BrE)
a meal that is large and filling, often containing rich or substantial foods.
large, filling meal

Examples

After eating a heavy meal, he needed to lie down and rest for a while.
Doctors recommend avoiding a heavy meal before exercising.
The restaurant is famous for its heavy meals that keep you full all day.

Synset Examples

He felt a sharp pain in his bowel after the heavy meal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light meal - a small, easily digested meal
