inhalation general anaesthetic

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌheɪlˈeɪʃən ˌdʒɛnərəl ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ɪnˌheɪlˈeɪʃ(ə)n ˌdʒɛnərəl ˌanɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
a gas administered by inhalation to induce general anesthesia during medical procedures
gas used for general anesthesia by inhalation
medical

Examples

The patient was given an inhalation general anaesthetic before surgery.
Halothane is a commonly used inhalation general anaesthetic in many hospitals worldwide.
Care must be taken when choosing an inhalation general anaesthetic for patients with respiratory conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhalation anaesthetic - an anaesthetic administered by inhalation
  • Antonym: intravenous anaesthetic - an anaesthetic administered by injection into a vein
