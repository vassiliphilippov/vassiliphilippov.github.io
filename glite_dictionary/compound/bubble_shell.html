bubble shell

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ʃɛl / (BrE)
A small marine gastropod mollusc with a very thin, often delicate shell resembling a bubble.
small marine mollusc with thin shell

Examples

The bubble shell glided gracefully along the sandy sea floor.
Some species of bubble shell are prized by collectors for their delicate appearance.
During low tide, children found a tiny bubble shell washed up on the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastropod - mollusc of the class Gastropoda
