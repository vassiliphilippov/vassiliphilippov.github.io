spider fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdɚ fɚn / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə fɜːn / (BrE)
An Asiatic fern with slender, delicate fronds, introduced to North America as an ornamental plant.
Asiatic fern with delicate fronds

Examples

My grandmother grows a beautiful spider fern in her sunroom.
The leaves of the spider fern are fine and lacy, making it a popular decorative plant.
After moving to the United States, she brought along a cutting of her favorite spider fern.

Examples
