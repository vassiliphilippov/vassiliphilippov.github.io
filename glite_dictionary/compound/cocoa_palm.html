cocoa palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.koʊ pɑm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəʊ pɑːm / (BrE)
A tall tropical palm tree bearing coconuts as fruits; widely cultivated throughout tropical regions.
tall palm tree producing coconuts

Examples

The cocoa palm thrives in warm, coastal areas with plenty of sunlight.
Farmers harvest coconuts from the cocoa palm to produce oil and other products.
The beach was lined with tall cocoa palms swaying gently in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coconut palm - the palm tree that produces coconuts
