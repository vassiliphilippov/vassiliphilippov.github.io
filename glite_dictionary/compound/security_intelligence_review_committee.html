Security Intelligence Review Committee

1. PROPN

/ sɪˈkjʊrəti ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns rɪˈvjuː kəˈmɪti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns rɪˈvjuː kəˈmɪti / (BrE)
an independent Canadian government agency that monitors and reviews the activities of the Canadian Security Intelligence Service to ensure compliance with the law and protection of individual rights
Canadian government agency overseeing security intelligence activities

Examples

The Security Intelligence Review Committee issued a report on civil liberties last week.
As an independent watchdog, the Security Intelligence Review Committee ensures oversight of Canadian intelligence operations.
Public trust in the Security Intelligence Review Committee has grown due to its transparency.
