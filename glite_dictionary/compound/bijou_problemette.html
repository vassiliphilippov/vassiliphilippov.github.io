bijou problemette

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːʒuː ˌproʊbləˈmɛt / (AmE), / ˈbiːʒuː ˌprɒbləˈmɛt / (BrE)
A very small or minor problem, often used in a humorous or informal way.
a very minor or trivial problem
informal

Examples

We had a bijou problemette with the event schedule, but everything went smoothly after that.
It was only a bijou problemette, so there was no need to panic.
The designer faced a bijou problemette when the colors didn't match perfectly.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minor problem - a small or insignificant difficulty
  • Degree Higher: problem - a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful

Etymology

‘Bijou’ from French meaning ‘jewel, small and elegant’; ‘problemette’ is a playful diminutive of ‘problem’.
