high noon

1. NOUN

/ haɪ nun / (AmE), / haɪ nuːn / (BrE)
The exact middle of the day; noon, when the sun is at its highest point.
exact middle of the day

Examples

The sun was directly overhead at high noon, making the streets unbearably hot.
They agreed to meet in the square at high noon for the duel.
At high noon, the city was bustling with activity as workers took their lunch breaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midday - the middle of the day
  • Antonym: midnight - 12 o'clock at night; opposite of midday
