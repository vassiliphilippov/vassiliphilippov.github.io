family Tragulidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæmɪli træˈɡjuːlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli træˈɡjuːlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of small, primitive ruminant mammals known as chevrotains or mouse deer.
family of chevrotains (mouse deer)

Examples

The family Tragulidae includes several species commonly known as mouse deer.
Members of the family Tragulidae are found in parts of Asia and Africa.
Unlike larger ruminants, the family Tragulidae is characterized by its small size and primitive features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chevrotain - a small, deer-like animal native to Africa and Asia

Etymology

From New Latin Tragulidae, based on Tragul-, genus Tragus (from Greek tragos, 'goat'), with zoological family suffix -idae.
