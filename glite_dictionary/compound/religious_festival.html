religious festival

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
A festival or celebration observed for religious reasons.
a festival with religious significance

Examples

Eid al-Fitr is a major religious festival celebrated by Muslims worldwide.
Many communities gather for a religious festival at the end of harvest season.
Christmas is one of the most widely recognized religious festivals in the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: secular festival - a festival with no religious significance
  • Synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation
