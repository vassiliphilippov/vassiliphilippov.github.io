jet set life

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛt sɛt laɪf / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛt sɛt laɪf / (BrE)
A glamorous and luxurious lifestyle enjoyed by wealthy people who frequently travel to fashionable or exotic places.
wealthy, glamorous, frequent-travel lifestyle

Examples

He spent his twenties chasing the jet set life from party to party around the globe.
Magazines are full of stories about celebrities and their jet set life.
Many dream of living a jet set life, but few can afford its luxuries.

Synset Examples

Living the jet set life means constant travel.
She loves the jet set lifestyle, traveling the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet set - wealthy people who travel frequently to fashionable places

Etymology

From 'jet set', referring to wealthy globetrotters, popularized in the 1950s and 1960s from 'jet' (airplane) and 'set' (group).
