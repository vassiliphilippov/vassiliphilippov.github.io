KO punch

1. NOUN

/ ˌkeɪ ˈoʊ pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ ˈəʊ pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A hard punch that knocks an opponent out, making them unable to continue in a boxing match.
a punch that causes a knockout

Examples

With a single KO punch, the challenger won the national title in the first round.
The crowd erupted when he delivered a devastating KO punch to his opponent.
She trained for years to perfect her KO punch and finally achieved her goal in the championship match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout - the act of rendering an opponent unconscious in boxing
  • Degree Lower: jab - a quick, straight punch in boxing
  • Degree Higher: haymaker - a wild, powerful punch

Etymology

KO is an abbreviation of 'knockout'.
