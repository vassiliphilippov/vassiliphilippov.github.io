penny grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛni ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈpɛni ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A foul-smelling Eurasian weed with round, flat seed pods, now naturalized in North America; commonly known as field pennycress.
foul-smelling Eurasian weed with flat pods

Examples

The abandoned lot was overgrown with penny grass by early summer.
Farmers often consider penny grass a troublesome weed in their fields.
You can identify penny grass by its distinctive round seed pods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field pennycress - another common name for penny grass, a weed with round flat seed pods
