frog's lettuce

1. NOUN

/ frɔɡz ˈlɛt̬ɪs / (AmE), / frɒɡz ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
an aquatic plant of western Africa, Asia, and Europe, similar to Potamogeton, commonly found in still or slow-moving water
aquatic plant similar to Potamogeton

Examples

Frog's lettuce is often found in calm streams and ponds across Europe.
The aquarium was decorated with frog's lettuce to provide a natural habitat for the fish.
Researchers studied how frog's lettuce adapted to changes in water quality.
