phone company

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that provides telephone services to the public.
company providing telephone services

Examples

My phone company offers unlimited data plans at a reasonable price.
After switching to a new phone company, my monthly bill was much lower.
The phone company repaired the damaged lines after the storm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone company - company providing telephone services
