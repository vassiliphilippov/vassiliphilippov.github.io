white trash

1. NOUN

/ waɪt træʃ / (AmE), / waɪt trɑːʃ / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An insulting term for poor or working-class White people, especially in the rural southern United States.
offensive slang for poor white people
slang offensive

Examples

He was upset when someone called him white trash just because of where he grew up.
The term white trash is highly offensive and should not be used in polite conversation.
Some writers have explored the struggles faced by communities labeled as white trash.
