good example

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪɡˈzɑːmpəl / (BrE)
a person or thing regarded as worthy of imitation; a model to be followed
model to be imitated

Examples

She is a good example of what hard work and dedication can achieve.
Teachers often use his essay as a good example for the rest of the class.
The athlete set a good example for young people by volunteering in the community.

Synset Examples

an exemplar of success
a model of clarity
he is the very model of a modern major general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exemplar - someone or something serving as a model or ideal
  • Synonym: role model - a person looked to by others as an example to be imitated
  • Antonym: bad example - someone or something regarded as not to be imitated
