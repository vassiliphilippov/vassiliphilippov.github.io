car crash

1. NOUN

/ kɑr kræʃ / (AmE), / kɑː kræʃ / (BrE)
a collision involving one or more cars, often resulting in damage or injury
collision involving automobiles

Examples

The highway was blocked for hours after a serious car crash yesterday.
He suffered minor injuries in a car crash last year.
Witnesses described the car crash as very sudden and frightening.

Synset Examples

He witnessed a car crash while driving home from work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accident - an unexpected event, typically one that causes damage or injury
  • Synonym: collision - an instance of one moving object or person striking against another
