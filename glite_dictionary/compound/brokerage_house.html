brokerage house

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ haʊs / (BrE)
A business firm that buys and sells securities, commodities, or other assets on behalf of clients; a broker's place of business.
business firm acting as broker

Examples

She opened an account with a well-known brokerage house to start investing in stocks.
The brokerage house earned a commission for facilitating the transaction between buyer and seller.
Following the economic downturn, several brokerage houses merged to stay competitive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brokerage - the business or service of acting as a broker
  • Synonym: brokerage firm - another term for a brokerage house; business that acts as a broker
