erogenous zone

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɑdʒənəs zoʊn / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒdʒənəs zəʊn / (BrE)
an area of the body that is especially sensitive to sexual stimulation and can produce sexual arousal when touched
body area sensitive to sexual stimulation

Examples

The neck is considered an erogenous zone for many people.
Experimenting with different erogenous zones can enhance intimacy between partners.
Some scientists study how the brain processes touch from each erogenous zone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erotic zone - area of the body sensitive to sexual stimulation

Etymology

From Greek *erōgenēs* (producing love), from *eros* (love) + *-genēs* (born of).
