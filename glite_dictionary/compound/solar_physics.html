solar physics

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.lɚ ˈfɪz.ɪks / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.lə ˈfɪz.ɪks / (BrE)
the branch of astronomy that studies the physical properties and phenomena of the sun
branch of astronomy focused on the sun

Examples

She chose to specialize in solar physics for her postgraduate studies.
New discoveries in solar physics have improved our understanding of solar flares.
The conference focused on recent advancements in solar physics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heliophysics - the science of the physical study of the sun and its interaction with the solar system

Etymology

From Latin 'solaris' (of the sun) and Greek 'physis' (nature).
