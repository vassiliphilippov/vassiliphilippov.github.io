round-headed leek

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊnd ˌhɛdɪd lik / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌhɛdɪd liːk / (BrE)
an Old World species of leek with a spherical bulb, cultivated for its ornamental flowers and edible bulb.
species of leek with a round bulb

Examples

The gardener planted several round-headed leeks along the border for their striking purple blooms.
Chefs sometimes use the bulbs of the round-headed leek as a flavorful addition to dishes.
You can recognize a round-headed leek by its compact, spherical flower head and slender stem.
