happy return

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ˈhæpi rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an expression used to wish someone well, especially on their birthday, often as 'many happy returns (of the day)'
birthday or celebratory greeting

Examples

We wished Aunt May a happy return on her birthday.
Cards with the phrase 'many happy returns' lined the mantelpiece.
He smiled and said, 'Thank you for the happy return wishes.'

Synset Examples

Wishing you many happy returns on your birthday!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: many happy returns - traditional phrase to wish someone well on their birthday or special day

Etymology

From the idea of wishing someone many returns (repetitions) of the happy day.
