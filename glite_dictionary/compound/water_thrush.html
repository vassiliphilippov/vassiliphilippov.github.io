water thrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ θrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a brownish North American warbler that lives near streams and rivers
brownish North American warbler

Examples

The water thrush is often seen hopping along rocks by the riverbank.
We spotted a Northern water thrush in the wetlands yesterday afternoon.
Conservationists monitor the water thrush population to assess the health of streamside habitats.
