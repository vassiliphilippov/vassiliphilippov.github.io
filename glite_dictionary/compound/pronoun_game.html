pronoun game

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊˌnaʊn ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊnaʊn ɡeɪm / (BrE)
the practice of evading directness in conversation by using pronouns instead of specific names, often to avoid accountability or reveal information
evasive use of pronouns to avoid clarity

Examples

Tired of the pronoun game, Anna asked for direct answers from the team.
Journalists criticized the politician for resorting to the pronoun game during the press conference.
Managers often notice when employees play the pronoun game to deflect responsibility.

Synset Examples

They decided to stop playing the pronoun game and focus on solutions.
He always plays the pronoun game when asked about his mistakes.
In discussions, she tends to play the pronoun game to avoid accountability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evasion - the act of avoiding something or someone
