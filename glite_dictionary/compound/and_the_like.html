and the like

1. X

/ ənd ðə laɪk / (AmE), / ənd ðə laɪk / (BrE)
used to refer to similar things or people already mentioned
and similar things

Examples

They offer a variety of extracurricular activities such as chess, drama, and the like.
You should avoid caffeinated drinks, spicy foods, and the like before bedtime.
Students may bring pencils, erasers, and the like to the exam.

Synset Examples

They offer courses in cooking, baking, and the like.
She enjoys gardening, painting, and the like.
He collects stamps, coins, and the like.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: et cetera - and so forth; used at the end of a list to indicate additional similar items
