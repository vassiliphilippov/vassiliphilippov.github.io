family leave

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli liːv / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli liːv / (BrE)
time away from work granted to an employee to care for family members, especially for the birth or adoption of a child or to address family health needs.
employee time off for family care

Examples

Many companies now offer family leave to allow employees to care for newborn children.
She took several weeks of family leave when her father became seriously ill.
The government's new policy expands family leave benefits for adoptive parents.

Synset Examples

The terms ‘parental leave’ and ‘family leave’ include maternity, paternity, and adoption leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parental leave - time off work for parents after the birth or adoption of a child
