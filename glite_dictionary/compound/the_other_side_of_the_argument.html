the other side of the argument

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈʌðɚ saɪd əv ði ˈɑrgjəmənt / (AmE), / ði ˈʌðə saɪd əv ði ˈɑːgjumənt / (BrE)
the opposing viewpoint or position in a debate or discussion
opposing position in a discussion

Examples

Before making a decision, it's important to consider the other side of the argument.
She presented the other side of the argument during the meeting, challenging everyone's assumptions.
Listening to the other side of the argument can help you understand the issue more fully.

Synset Examples

He always sees the other side of the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterargument - an argument or reasoning put forward to oppose another argument
