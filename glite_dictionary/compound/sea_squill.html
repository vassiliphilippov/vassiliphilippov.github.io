sea squill

1. NOUN

/ si ˈskwɪl / (AmE), / si ˈskwɪl / (BrE)
a Mediterranean plant with spikes of small white flowers and a bulb used medicinally
Mediterranean plant with medicinal bulb

Examples

The sea squill blooms each year along the Mediterranean coastline.
Traditional remedies often use extracts from the bulb of the sea squill.
Many gardeners appreciate the dramatic appearance of sea squill in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squill - another name for the same or similar plant
