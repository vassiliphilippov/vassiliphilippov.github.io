hammer nose

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmɚ noʊz / (AmE), / ˈhamə nəʊz / (BrE)
A condition in which the nose is enlarged, with dilated follicles, redness, and prominent blood vessels, often linked to excessive alcohol consumption.
enlarged, reddened nose (medical)
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with hammer nose after noticing the redness and swelling.
Chronic alcohol abuse can result in the development of hammer nose.
People with hammer nose may also experience social discomfort due to its appearance.
