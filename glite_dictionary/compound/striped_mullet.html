striped mullet

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪpt ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a species of mullet (Mugil cephalus) with dark horizontal stripes, found in warm coastal waters and an important commercial fish in the eastern United States
a commercially important fish species

Examples

Fishermen along the Atlantic coast often catch striped mullet during the fall migration.
The diet of the striped mullet consists mainly of algae and detritus in shallow waters.
A record number of striped mullet were harvested in the Gulf this season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mullet - a family of marine fish known for having elongated bodies and forked tails
