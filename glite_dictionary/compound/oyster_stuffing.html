oyster stuffing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstɚ ˈstʌfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə ˈstʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
A bread-based stuffing made with oysters, typically served as a side dish or used to fill poultry.
stuffing made with oysters

Examples

My grandmother always prepares oyster stuffing for Thanksgiving dinner.
The recipe calls for fresh oysters to be added to the oyster stuffing mixture.
He was pleasantly surprised by how flavorful the oyster stuffing was.
