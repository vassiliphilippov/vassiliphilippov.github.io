skin disorder

1. NOUN

/ skɪn dɪsˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / skɪn dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition or disease that affects the skin.
disease affecting the skin
medical

Examples

Eczema is a common skin disorder that causes inflammation and itching.
The dermatologist specializes in treating various skin disorders.
Many people experience a skin disorder at some point in their lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skin disease - any disease affecting the skin
  • Synonym: dermatosis - any skin disorder or disease
