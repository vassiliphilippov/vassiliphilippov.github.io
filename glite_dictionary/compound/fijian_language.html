Fijian language

1. NOUN

/ fiˈdʒiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / fɪˈdʒiːən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Oceanic language spoken in Fiji by the Fijian people
language spoken in Fiji

Examples

He is fluent in both English and Fijian language.
The Fijian language uses a unique set of sounds not found in English.
She studied the Fijian language as part of her research on Pacific cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fiji - a country in the South Pacific Ocean

Etymology

From Fiji, the country name, plus 'an' and 'language'. 'Fiji' itself is an Anglicization of 'Viti', the island group's native name.
