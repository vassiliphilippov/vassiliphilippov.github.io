legendary battle

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛdʒənˌdɛri ˈbæt̬əl / (AmE), / ˌlɛdʒəndəri ˈbætəl / (BrE)
a battle that is famous or has become the subject of myth or legend, often celebrated in stories or history
famous or mythic conflict

Examples

The story of King Arthur is filled with references to legendary battles fought for the throne.
Many historians debate whether the legendary battle at Troy actually happened or is purely a myth.
The painting depicts a legendary battle between ancient warriors under a stormy sky.

Synset Examples

Orcban was known for its legendary battles and heroes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical battle - a battle that exists in myth or legend, not necessarily fact
