ice-wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌwaɡən / (BrE)
a horse-drawn wagon formerly used to deliver blocks of ice door to door
horse-drawn ice delivery wagon (historical)

Examples

In the early 1900s, the ice-wagon made stops at every house on the block.
Before electric refrigerators, families eagerly awaited the arrival of the ice-wagon on hot summer mornings.
The old photograph shows children chasing after the neighborhood ice-wagon as it rolled down the street.
