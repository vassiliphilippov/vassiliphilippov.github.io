ways and means

1. NOUN

/ weɪz ənd ˈmiːnz / (AmE), / weɪz ənd ˈmiːnz / (BrE)
methods and resources available for achieving a particular purpose, especially for raising money for government expenditures.
methods/resources to raise money, especially in government

Examples

The government is still searching for ways and means to fund the new healthcare initiative.
We must consider all possible ways and means to solve our financial difficulties.
Congress debated the ways and means of increasing tax revenue without hurting the economy.
