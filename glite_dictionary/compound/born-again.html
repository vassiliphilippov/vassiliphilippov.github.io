born-again

1. ADJ

/ bɔrn əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / bɔːn əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
Having experienced a spiritual rebirth, especially as a result of a personal conversion to faith in Christianity.
spiritually reborn or converted

Examples

After attending the revival meeting, she became a born-again Christian.
He describes himself as a born-again environmentalist following his life-changing experience in the Amazon.
Many born-again believers are deeply committed to their faith.

Synset Examples

a born-again Christian

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convert - a person who has adopted a new religious faith
