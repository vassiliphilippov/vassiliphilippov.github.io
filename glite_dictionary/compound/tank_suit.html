tank suit

1. NOUN

/ tæŋk suːt / (AmE), / tæŋk suːt / (BrE)
A woman's one-piece bathing suit with shoulder straps and no skirt or cutouts.
one-piece women's bathing suit

Examples

She wore a classic tank suit to the pool party.
The store offers tank suits in a variety of colors and patterns.
Many athletes prefer a tank suit for lap swimming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one-piece - a swimsuit made from a single piece of material

Etymology

From 'tank' (swimming pool) + 'suit,' named after early swimwear worn in swimming tanks.
