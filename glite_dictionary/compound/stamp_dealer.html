stamp dealer

1. NOUN

/ stæmp ˈdiːlər / (AmE), / stæmp ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells postage stamps, especially to collectors
person or business selling postage stamps

Examples

The stamp dealer showed me a rare collection from the 19th century.
She became a successful stamp dealer after turning her hobby into a business.
Many stamp dealers attend conventions to network and buy valuable items.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philatelist - a person who collects or studies stamps
