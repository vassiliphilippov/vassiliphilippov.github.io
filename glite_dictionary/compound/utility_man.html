utility man

1. NOUN

/ juˈtɪl.ə.ti mæn / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti man / (BrE)
a man employed to perform a variety of tasks, especially one who can fill in for different roles as needed
worker who fills in various roles

Examples

The coach relied on his utility man to cover any position during the game.
As a utility man, he was often called in to fix electrical or plumbing problems.
Companies appreciate having a skilled utility man who can handle unexpected jobs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handyman - a person skilled at a wide range of repairs, especially around the home

2. NOUN

/ juˈtɪl.ə.ti mæn / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti mæn / (BrE)
A male baseball player who is valued for the ability to play competently at several different positions.
baseball player able to play several positions

Examples

The team's utility man filled in at shortstop when the starter was injured.
Having a reliable utility man gives the manager more options during the season.
As a utility man, he played outfield, infield, and even caught in a pinch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility player - player able to play multiple positions in a sport, not gender-specific
