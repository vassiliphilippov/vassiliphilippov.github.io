reddish-lavender

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈlævəndər / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
of a lavender color with a tinge of red; somewhat red-lavender in hue
lavender color tinged with red

Examples

The designer chose a reddish-lavender fabric for the curtains to give the room a warm touch.
Her dress was a unique reddish-lavender that stood out among the usual colors.
Sunset cast a reddish-lavender glow over the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color or the plant of the same name
