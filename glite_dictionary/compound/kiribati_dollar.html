Kiribati dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɪrɪˈbɑːti ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ˌkɪrɪˈbɑːti ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Kiribati, used as legal tender alongside the Australian dollar
currency unit of Kiribati

Examples

The government announced a new design for the Kiribati dollar this year.
Most shops in Tarawa accept both the Kiribati dollar and the Australian dollar.
Travelers should be aware that the Kiribati dollar is not widely used outside the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Australian dollar - the official currency of Australia, also used in Kiribati as legal tender
