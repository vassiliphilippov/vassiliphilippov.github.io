kingdom Protoctista

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm ˌproʊˌtɑkˈtɪstə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm ˌprəʊˌtɒkˈtɪstə / (BrE)
A taxonomic kingdom in some biological classifications that includes all protists and their descendants, such as protozoa, algae, and related organisms, distinct from plants and animals.
taxonomic group of protists and similar organisms

Examples

Some biologists argue that kingdom Protoctista should include both unicellular and simple multicellular organisms.
The classification of algae has changed, with many now placed in the kingdom Protoctista.
Textbooks may list the euglenoids under kingdom Protoctista instead of plants or animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Protista - A former or alternative name for the kingdom that includes protists.

Etymology

From Greek 'prōtos' (first) and 'ktistos' (created).
