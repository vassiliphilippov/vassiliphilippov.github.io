hair oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈheə ɔɪl / (BrE)
a liquid cosmetic product applied to the hair to improve its texture, shine, or manageability
liquid cosmetic for hair care

Examples

She uses hair oil every morning to keep her hair smooth and shiny.
The store offers a wide selection of hair oils for different hair types.
Applying hair oil regularly can help prevent dryness and split ends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair tonic - a cosmetic liquid for the hair, used to style and condition
