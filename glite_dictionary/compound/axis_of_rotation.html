axis of rotation

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɪs əv roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈæksɪs əv rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the straight line about which an object rotates or spins
line an object rotates around

Examples

The Earth has an imaginary axis of rotation passing through its poles.
A spinning top remains balanced as long as its axis of rotation stays vertical.
Engineers must calculate the axis of rotation to design a stable turbine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotation - the action of rotating around an axis or center

Etymology

From Latin axis (axle, pivot) and rotation (from Latin rotatio, turning around).
