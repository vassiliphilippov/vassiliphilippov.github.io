black tea

1. NOUN

/ blæk ti / (AmE), / blæk tiː / (BrE)
A type of tea that is made from leaves that have been fully fermented, giving it a dark color and strong flavor.
tea made from fermented leaves

Examples

Would you like some black tea with your breakfast?
She prefers black tea over coffee in the mornings.
The restaurant serves both green and black tea after meals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: green tea - tea made from unfermented leaves
  • Degree Lower: oolong tea - partially fermented tea
