compass plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌm.pəs pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pəs pleɪn / (BrE)
a plane with a flexible sole, used for smoothing concave or convex surfaces in woodworking
woodworking plane for curved surfaces

Examples

The woodworker used a compass plane to smooth the inside of the curved chair seat.
A compass plane is ideal for shaping convex surfaces like the edge of a tabletop.
Because of its flexible sole, the compass plane can adapt to both concave and convex shapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spokeshave - a small woodworking tool for shaping and smoothing wood
