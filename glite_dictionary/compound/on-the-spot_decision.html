on-the-spot decision

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑn ði ˈspɑt dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌɒn ði ˈspɒt dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a decision made immediately, without prior preparation or consideration
a decision made instantly

Examples

Sarah made an on-the-spot decision to join her friends on the trip.
Sometimes, a manager has to make an on-the-spot decision based on limited information.
His on-the-spot decision saved them valuable time during the emergency.

Synset Examples

She made an on-the-spot decision to change the plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snap decision - a decision made quickly without careful thought
