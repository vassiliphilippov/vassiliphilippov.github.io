herp meme

1. NOUN

/ hɜrp miːm / (AmE), / hɜːp miːm / (BrE)
An internet meme featuring or related to reptiles and amphibians.
internet meme about reptiles and amphibians
informal slang

Examples

She created a herp meme comparing a gecko to a famous cartoon character.
The forum is full of hilarious herp memes that anyone interested in snakes would enjoy.
He sent me a herp meme about frogs that made me laugh out loud.

Synset Examples

He loves reptiles and often shares herp memes online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - an element of culture or a humorous image spread online

Etymology

Formed from 'herp', a colloquial term for reptiles and amphibians, and 'meme', an idea or element spread online.
