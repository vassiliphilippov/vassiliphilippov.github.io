widow's peak

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪdoʊz piːk / (AmE), / ˈwɪdəʊz piːk / (BrE)
a V-shaped point in the hairline in the center of the forehead
V-shaped point in the hairline

Examples

He always tried to cover his widow's peak with his bangs.
Her widow's peak gave her a distinctive and memorable look.
The barber trimmed around his widow's peak to keep it neat.

Etymology

From the superstition that a similar point in the headdress of a widow indicated impending widowhood.
