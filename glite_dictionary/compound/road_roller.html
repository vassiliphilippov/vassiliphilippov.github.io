road roller

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd ˌroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd ˌrəʊlə / (BrE)
a heavy vehicle with wide, smooth rollers, used for compacting surfaces such as roads or pavements during construction work
vehicle for compacting roads or pavements

Examples

The road roller leveled the asphalt, leaving a perfectly smooth surface behind.
Construction workers operated the road roller to compact the gravel before paving the new street.
A road roller passed by slowly, flattening the loose stones on the driveway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steamroller - a similar vehicle powered by steam, formerly used for road-making
