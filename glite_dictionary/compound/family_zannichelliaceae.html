family Zannichelliaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌzænɪˌkɛliˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌzanɪˌkɛlɪˈiːsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a plant family in some classification systems, comprising certain aquatic genera sometimes included in Potamogetonaceae; includes only one species.
plant family for certain aquatic species

Examples

The family Zannichelliaceae is sometimes considered a subset of Potamogetonaceae in some botanical classifications.
Research indicates that the family Zannichelliaceae consists of only one recognized species.
In older texts, you may find certain aquatic plants assigned to the family Zannichelliaceae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Potamogetonaceae - the pondweed family, sometimes including Zannichelliaceae genera

Etymology

From Latin Zannichellia (a plant genus) + New Latin -aceae (family suffix).
