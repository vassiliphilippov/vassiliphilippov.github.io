phone system

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system for transmitting sound, especially speech, over distances using telephone technology.
system for transmitting speech by telephone

Examples

The company upgraded its phone system to improve customer service.
Our office phone system allows for conference calls between multiple locations.
After the storm, the phone system was down for several hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone system - system for transmitting sound over a distance by means of electrical signals
