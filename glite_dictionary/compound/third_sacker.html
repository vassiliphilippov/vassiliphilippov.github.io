third sacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝːd ˈsækər / (AmE), / ˈθɜːd ˈsækə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a player who plays the position of third base.
baseball player at third base
informal

Examples

The team's new third sacker impressed everyone with his quick reflexes and strong arm.
After injuring his wrist, the third sacker was replaced for the rest of the season.
The coach praised the third sacker for making a crucial double play in the eighth inning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third baseman - player fielding third base in baseball
