oil rigger

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈrɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈrɪɡə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to work on an oil rig, typically involved in the extraction or processing of oil
person working on an oil rig

Examples

My brother works as an oil rigger in the North Sea.
The life of an oil rigger can be both challenging and rewarding.
After five years as an oil rigger, she decided to pursue a career onshore.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roughneck - oil field worker, especially on a drilling crew

Etymology

Formed from 'oil' and 'rig' with the agentive '-er' suffix.
