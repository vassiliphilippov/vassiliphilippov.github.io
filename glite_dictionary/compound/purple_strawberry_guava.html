purple strawberry guava

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝpəl ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ɡwɑːvə / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈstrɒbəri ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
A small tropical shrub or tree bearing deep red, oval fruit with edible flesh, native to South America.
tropical fruit-bearing shrub or tree

Examples

The purple strawberry guava thrives in subtropical regions and produces deep red fruit each season.
We picked several purple strawberry guava fruits from the tree to make a sweet jam.
The purple strawberry guava is prized for its fragrant, tangy pulp and ornamental value in gardens.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: strawberry guava - a species of guava with small, red, edible fruit
  • Degree Lower: guava - tropical tree or shrub, or its edible fruit
