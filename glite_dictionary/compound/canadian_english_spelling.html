Canadian English spelling

1. NOUN

/ kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the conventions of English spelling used in Canada, often combining British and American forms
English spelling conventions in Canada

Examples

Many textbooks in Canada follow Canadian English spelling rather than the American version.
He was confused by the mixture of British and American rules in Canadian English spelling.
Words like 'colour' and 'centre' are typical examples of Canadian English spelling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British English spelling - English orthography as used in the United Kingdom
  • Synonym: American English spelling - English orthography as used in the United States
