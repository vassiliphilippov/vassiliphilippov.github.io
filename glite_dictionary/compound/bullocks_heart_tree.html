bullock's heart tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊləkz hɑrt triː / (AmE), / ˈbʊləkz hɑːt triː / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Annona reticulata) that bears a bristly, heart-shaped, acidic fruit.
a tropical tree with heart-shaped fruit

Examples

The bullock's heart tree thrives in warm, humid climates and is prized for its unusual fruit.
Farmers often plant bullock's heart trees alongside other tropical fruit species to diversify their crops.
The fruit of the bullock's heart tree is sweet-tart and heart-shaped, making it popular at local markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard apple - a tropical fruit from various Annona species, closely related fruit

Etymology

Named for the heart-shaped appearance of its fruit resembling the heart of a bullock (young bull).
