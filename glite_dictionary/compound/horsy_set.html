horsy set

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔːrsi sɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsi sɛt / (BrE)
A group of people who are enthusiastically devoted to horses, especially horse riding and racing.
group enthusiastic about horses/riding

Examples

She grew up among the horsy set, attending riding competitions every weekend.
At the charity ball, the horsy set mingled and discussed the upcoming races.
Though he was new to the town, he quickly became friends with members of the horsy set.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: jet set - a wealthy and fashionable social group who travel widely and frequently
