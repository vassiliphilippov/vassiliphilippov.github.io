Dog Star

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡ stɑː / (BrE)
the brightest star in the night sky, also known as Sirius, located in the constellation Canis Major
the brightest star in the night sky (Sirius)

Examples

The ancient Egyptians admired the Dog Star and considered its rise an important event.
On clear nights, you can easily spot the Dog Star in the southern sky.
Many cultures have stories about Dog Star and its significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sirius - the brightest star in the night sky; also called the Dog Star
