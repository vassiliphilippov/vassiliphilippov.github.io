salad Niçoise

1. NOUN

/ səˌlɑd niˈswɑz / (AmE), / səˌlɑd niˈswɑːz / (BrE)
a French salad typically made with tomatoes, tuna, hard-boiled eggs, olives, anchovies, and capers, often served with a vinaigrette.
French salad with tuna, eggs, and vegetables

Examples

I ordered a salad Niçoise for lunch at the café.
Her homemade salad Niçoise was full of fresh vegetables and tuna.
The menu features a classic salad Niçoise with anchovies and olives.

Etymology

From French 'salade niçoise', meaning 'salad from Nice'.
