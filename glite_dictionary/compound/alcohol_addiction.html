alcohol addiction

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.kəhɔl əˌdɪk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈæl.kəhɒl əˌdɪk.ʃən / (BrE)
A condition characterized by a compulsive need to consume alcoholic drinks, leading to physical and psychological dependence and negative health or social effects.
dependence on alcoholic drinks
medical

Examples

After years of struggling with alcohol addiction, he finally sought help and entered a rehabilitation program.
The documentary explores the devastating impact of alcohol addiction on families and communities.
Doctors warned that without treatment, his alcohol addiction could lead to serious health complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcoholism - chronic or habitual intoxication; disease caused by alcohol dependence
