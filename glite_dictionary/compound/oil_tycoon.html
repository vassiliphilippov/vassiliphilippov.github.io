oil tycoon

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl taɪˈkun / (AmE), / ɔɪl taɪˈkuːn / (BrE)
A wealthy and powerful person who owns or controls significant businesses in the oil industry.
powerful oil industry businessperson

Examples

The oil tycoon invested billions of dollars in new drilling projects around the world.
As an oil tycoon, she wielded enormous influence over the country's energy policies.
Rumors spread quickly when the retired oil tycoon decided to write his memoirs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnate - a wealthy and influential person, especially in business
  • Synonym: industrialist - a person involved in the ownership and management of industry

Etymology

Tycoon from Japanese 'taikun' (great lord); 'oil' + 'tycoon'.
