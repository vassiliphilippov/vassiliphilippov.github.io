nonsmoking car

1. NOUN

/ nɑnˈsmoʊ.kɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / nɒnˈsməʊ.kɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car in which smoking is not permitted
rail car where smoking is banned

Examples

Please move to the nonsmoking car if you do not wish to be around tobacco smoke.
The train conductor announced that the next carriage was a nonsmoking car.
Many express trains now have only nonsmoking cars available for passengers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smoking car - a train car where smoking is permitted
