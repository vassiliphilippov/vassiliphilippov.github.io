juggling work and family

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌɡ.əlɪŋ wɝːk ənd ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡ.lɪŋ wɜːk ənd ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
managing both one's job and personal family responsibilities at the same time
balancing job and family duties

Examples

Many parents find juggling work and family to be a constant challenge.
After she started her new job, juggling work and family became even harder.
Support from employers can make juggling work and family much easier for employees.
