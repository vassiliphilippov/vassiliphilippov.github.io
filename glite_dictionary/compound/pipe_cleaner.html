pipe cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪp ˌkliːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp ˌkliːnə / (BrE)
a flexible tool made of tufted wire, used for cleaning the stem of a tobacco pipe or for crafts
flexible tool for cleaning pipes

Examples

He used a pipe cleaner to remove the residue from his old briar pipe.
Children often make crafts by twisting colored pipe cleaners into shapes.
The maintenance kit includes a brush and a pipe cleaner for proper cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaner - a device or substance for cleaning
