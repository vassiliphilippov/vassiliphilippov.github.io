wee smile

1. NOUN

/ wi smaɪl / (AmE), / wiː smaɪl / (BrE)
A small or slight smile.
a small or slight smile

Examples

The girl offered a wee smile when she received the compliment.
He managed just a wee smile after hearing the good news.
A wee smile appeared on her face as she remembered the joke.

Synset Examples

The child gave a wee smile when he saw the puppy.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: smile - to make a pleased or friendly expression by curving the lips upward

Etymology

From 'wee' (meaning small, especially in Scottish English) and 'smile'.
