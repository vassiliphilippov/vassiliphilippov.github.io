high sign

1. NOUN

/ haɪ saɪn / (AmE), / haɪ saɪn / (BrE)
a silent or discreet signal used to warn, greet, or recognize someone
discreet warning or recognition signal

Examples

He nodded and gave me the high sign to leave the room.
She waited for her friend to give her the high sign before approaching.
Before starting, he looked at his partner for the high sign.

Synset Examples

she started to speak but he gave her the high sign
