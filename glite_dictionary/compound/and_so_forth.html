and so forth

1. X

/ ænd soʊ fɔrθ / (AmE), / ənd səʊ fɔːθ / (BrE)
used to refer to other things of a similar kind already mentioned; and so on.
and other similar things

Examples

We spent the afternoon cleaning the house, washing the car, and so forth.
Bring your towel, sunscreen, hats, and so forth when you go to the beach.
The manual explains how to set up your account, change your password, and so forth.

Synset Examples

We discussed the budget, priorities, and so forth.
He likes hiking, biking, and so forth.
You can use it for work, leisure, and so forth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: and so on - used to indicate a continuation of items of a similar kind
  • Synonym: et cetera - used to indicate additional unspecified items; etc.
