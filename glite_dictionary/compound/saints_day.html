saint's day

1. NOUN

/ seɪnts deɪ / (AmE), / seɪnts deɪ / (BrE)
A day in the Christian calendar commemorating a particular saint, often celebrated with religious observances.
Christian day honoring a saint

Examples

All of the schools in town closed for the saint's day of the local patron.
Many Catholic countries hold parades to celebrate a saint's day.
On every saint's day, the church organizes a special service in honor of the saint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feast day - a day set aside for religious celebration, especially for a saint
