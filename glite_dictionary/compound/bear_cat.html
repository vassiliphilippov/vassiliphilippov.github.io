bear cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛr ˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈbeə ˌkæt / (BrE)
a large, reddish-brown, arboreal carnivorous mammal (the binturong) of Southeast Asia, resembling a raccoon and sometimes called the Asian bearcat
the binturong, an Asian mammal

Examples

The bear cat is native to the forests of Southeast Asia.
At the zoo, the bear cat captured everyone's attention with its bushy tail and playful antics.
Unlike most carnivores, the bear cat has a prehensile tail to help it move through trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binturong - the same animal; another name for the bear cat
