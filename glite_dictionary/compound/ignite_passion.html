to ignite passion

1. VERB

/ ɪɡˈnaɪt ˈpæʃ.ən / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnaɪt ˈpæʃ.ən / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel strong enthusiasm or excitement
inspire strong enthusiasm or excitement

Examples

Her innovative teaching style helped ignite passion for science among her students.
The documentary aimed to ignite passion about environmental protection in its viewers.
A good leader knows how to ignite passion in their team during challenging times.

Synset Examples

Her fiery speech ignited passion in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to inspire - to fill someone with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially creative or enthusiastic
  • Antonym: quell enthusiasm - to suppress or reduce feelings of enthusiasm or excitement
