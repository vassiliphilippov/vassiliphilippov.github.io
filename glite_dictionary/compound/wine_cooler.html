wine cooler

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈkuːlɚ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈkuːlə / (BrE)
a container, typically filled with ice, used to keep a bottle of wine chilled
ice-filled container for chilling wine

Examples

He placed the bottle of Chardonnay in the wine cooler to keep it properly chilled during dinner.
The waiter brought a wine cooler filled with ice to our table before serving the wine.
They forgot to bring a wine cooler, so the wine quickly warmed up on the picnic table.
