in full force

1. ADV

/ ɪn fʊl fɔrs / (AmE), / ɪn fʊl fɔːs / (BrE)
With the greatest possible strength, intensity, or participation; at maximum effect or presence.
with maximum strength or intensity

Examples

The fans turned out in full force to cheer on their team.
Wildflowers bloom in full force every spring across the valley.
After the holiday break, the office was back in full force on Monday.

Synset Examples

The volunteers showed up in full force to support the charity.
The marketing campaign launched in full force last week.
The police were present in full force during the protest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: en masse - all together, especially in a large group
  • Synonym: to the full - to the greatest possible degree or extent
