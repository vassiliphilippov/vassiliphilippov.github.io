New Zealand English spelling

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈziː.lənd ˈɪŋ.glɪʃ ˈspɛ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / njuː ˈziː.lənd ˈɪŋ.glɪʃ ˈspel.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the standard conventions of spelling English words as practiced in New Zealand, often following British patterns with some distinctive variations
spelling conventions of English in New Zealand

Examples

Many school textbooks in New Zealand use New Zealand English spelling rather than American variants.
Writers often consult style guides to ensure consistency in New Zealand English spelling.
You can set your word processor to check for New Zealand English spelling automatically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British English spelling - the spelling conventions of English as used in the UK
  • Antonym: American English spelling - the spelling conventions of English in the United States
