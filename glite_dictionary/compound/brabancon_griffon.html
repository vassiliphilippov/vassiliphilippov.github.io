Brabancon griffon

1. NOUN

/ brəˌbæŋkən ɡrɪˈfɒn / (AmE), / brəˌbæŋkən ɡrɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
A variety of Brussels Griffon dog with a short, smooth coat.
dog breed with a short smooth coat

Examples

The Brabancon griffon is known for its affectionate personality and smooth short coat.
At the dog show, her Brabancon griffon charmed the judges with its alert expression.
Unlike the rough-coated varieties, the Brabancon griffon has a sleek, glossy appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brussels griffon - small breed of toy dog, originally from Belgium

Etymology

From French Brabançon (from Brabant, a region in Belgium) + griffon (a type of dog).
