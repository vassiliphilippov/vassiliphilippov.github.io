own game

1. NOUN

/ oʊn ɡeɪm / (AmE), / əʊn ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a situation in which a person excels in, or is particularly skilled at, their area of expertise or activity
someone's area of expertise or skill

Examples

She was determined to outperform her rival at her own game.
Beating the champion at his own game was a remarkable achievement.
He's clever, but you're just as skilled at your own game.

Synset Examples

It's hard to beat someone at their own game when they know all the tricks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beat someone at their own game - to defeat someone by using their own methods or expertise
