drive line system

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪv laɪn ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv laɪn ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system in a motor vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the driving wheels
system transmitting engine power to wheels

Examples

The mechanic inspected the drive line system for damage after hearing a strange noise under the car.
A well-maintained drive line system ensures efficient power transfer and improved fuel economy.
Upgrading your vehicle's drive line system can enhance its off-road performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drivetrain - the components that transmit power from the engine to the wheels of a vehicle
