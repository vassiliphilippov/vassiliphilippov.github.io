male internal reproductive organ

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ɪnˈtɜrnəl ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / meɪl ɪnˈtɜːnəl ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
Any of the reproductive glands inside the male body, including the ampullary, vesicular, prostate, bulbourethral, and urethral glands.
