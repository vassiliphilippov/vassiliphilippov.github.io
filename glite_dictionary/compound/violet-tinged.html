violet-tinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt tɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight shade or touch of violet in color.
slightly tinted with violet

Examples

The horizon was violet-tinged as the sun began to set.
Her eyes had a unique, violet-tinged hue that caught everyone's attention.
A violet-tinged mist rolled over the mountains at dawn.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: violet - a bluish-purple color
  • Synonym: tinged - having a slight touch, flavor, or color of something
