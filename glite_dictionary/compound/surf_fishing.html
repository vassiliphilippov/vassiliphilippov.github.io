surf fishing

1. NOUN

/ sɝf ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / sɜːf ˈfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of fishing by casting bait or lures from the shore into the surf of the ocean
fishing from shore into the ocean surf

Examples

Many anglers enjoy surf fishing for striped bass during the summer months.
He bought a long rod specifically designed for surf fishing at the beach.
The tournament attracted dozens of enthusiasts from around the state for a weekend of surf fishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angling - the activity of fishing with a rod and line
