chick flick

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪk flɪk / (AmE), / tʃɪk flɪk / (BrE)
A movie, usually romantic or sentimental, that is intended to appeal primarily to women.
movie aimed at women, often romantic
informal

Examples

We decided to watch a chick flick for our girls' night in.
Although he usually prefers thrillers, he enjoyed the chick flick they chose.
The new release is being marketed as the ultimate summer chick flick.

Synset Examples

The chick flick made everyone laugh and cry.
She enjoys a good chick flick now and then.
We're watching a classic chick flick tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic comedy - a comedy film centered on romantic relationships
  • Antonym: action film - a movie genre oriented toward physical action

Etymology

From "chick" (informal: woman) + "flick" (slang: movie).
