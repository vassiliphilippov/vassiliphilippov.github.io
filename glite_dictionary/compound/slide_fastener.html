slide fastener

1. NOUN

/ slaɪd ˈfæsnɚ / (AmE), / slaɪd ˈfɑːsnə / (BrE)
a fastening device consisting of two toothed strips and a sliding piece that locks the strips together
fastening device with two toothed strips and slider

Examples

The slide fastener on my jacket got stuck, so I had to pull it gently to free it.
Most backpacks are secured with a slide fastener for easy opening and closing.
After years of use, the slide fastener on her suitcase needed to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zipper - fastener with two toothed strips and sliding tab
