printing concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ kənˈsɝːn / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a company engaged in the business of commercial printing
company specializing in commercial printing

Examples

The annual report was produced by a local printing concern specializing in high-quality brochures.
She started working at a printing concern that served major publishers across the region.
Many small businesses rely on a nearby printing concern for their marketing materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printing house - a company or establishment where printing is done, especially commercially
