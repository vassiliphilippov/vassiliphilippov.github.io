pine-barren sandwort

1. NOUN

/ paɪn ˈbærən ˈsændwɝt / (AmE), / paɪn ˈbarən ˈsændwɜːt / (BrE)
a deep-rooted perennial plant native to the pine barrens of the southeastern United States
plant native to southeastern U.S. pine barrens

Examples

The pine-barren sandwort is well adapted to the dry, sandy soils of Florida.
Botanists study the pine-barren sandwort for its resilience in harsh environments.
We saw clusters of pine-barren sandwort growing among the longleaf pines during our hike.
