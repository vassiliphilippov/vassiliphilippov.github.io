momo dumpling

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmoʊ ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊməʊ ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of stuffed dumpling, usually filled with meat or vegetables, popular in Nepalese, Tibetan, and other Asian cuisines.
Asian stuffed dumpling

Examples

Every Saturday, we order a plate of momo dumplings from the local Nepali restaurant.
She learned how to make spicy chicken momo dumplings from her grandmother.
Vegetarian momo dumplings are my favorite snack at festivals.

Synset Examples

The kids love their momo dumplings every Friday night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpling - a small mass of dough cooked by boiling or steaming, often filled

Etymology

From Nepali and Tibetan 'momo', referring to these steamed or fried dumplings.
