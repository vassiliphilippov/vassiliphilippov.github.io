agenda item

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒɛndə ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / əˈdʒɛndə ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an individual subject or topic listed for discussion or consideration, especially at a meeting
subject or topic in a meeting agenda

Examples

The first agenda item addressed the company's financial performance for the quarter.
We ran out of time before we could discuss the final agenda item on the list.
After a long debate, they moved on to the next agenda item.
