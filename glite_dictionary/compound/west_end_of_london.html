West End of London

1. PROPN

/ wɛst ɛnd əv ˈlʌn.dən / (AmE), / wɛst end əv ˈlʌn.dən / (BrE)
the district in west central London known for its main entertainment venues, theatres, and shopping areas
district in central London with theatres and shops

Examples

Many tourists visit the West End of London to see a musical or a play.
Shopping in the West End of London offers a wide range of high-end boutiques and department stores.
During the holiday season, the West End of London is bustling with visitors and bright decorations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: West End - entertainment and shopping district in central London
  • Antonym: City of London - the primary business and financial district of London
