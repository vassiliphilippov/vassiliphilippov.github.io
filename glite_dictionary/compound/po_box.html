PO Box

1. NOUN

/ pi oʊ bɑks / (AmE), / piː əʊ bɒks / (BrE)
a numbered compartment at a post office where mail is delivered and collected by the holder
numbered post office mail compartment

Examples

She asked the company to send her documents to her PO Box instead of her home address.
Many businesses use a PO Box to keep their mail secure.
His letters were always addressed to a PO Box in the city center.

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Post Office Box'.
