glandular disease

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæn.dʒə.lɚ dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˈɡlæn.djʊ.lə dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease or disorder affecting the glands of the body, often resulting in abnormal secretion or function.
disease affecting bodily glands
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a rare glandular disease that affected her metabolism.
Excessive sweating can sometimes be a symptom of an underlying glandular disease.
Early detection of any glandular disease can improve the effectiveness of treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endocrine disorder - a disorder of the endocrine glands affecting hormone production
