nougat bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːɡət bɑːr / (AmE), / ˈnuːɡət bɑː / (BrE)
a bar-shaped candy made of nougat, often coated in chocolate
bar-shaped nougat candy, often chocolate-coated

Examples

He unwrapped a nougat bar for a quick snack during the movie.
The store sells homemade nougat bars dipped in rich dark chocolate.
I prefer a nougat bar over other types of candy because of its chewy texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nougat - a chewy sweet made from sugar, honey, nuts, and egg whites
