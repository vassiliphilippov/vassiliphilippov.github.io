sales promotion

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz pɹəˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / seɪlz pɹəˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
an activity or set of activities designed to boost sales of a product or service, often supplementing regular advertising, such as discounts, contests, or special events
marketing activity to boost sales

Examples

The company launched a week-long sales promotion to increase interest in their new smartphone.
Supermarkets often use prize draws as part of their sales promotion strategies.
She works in sales promotion, planning campaigns to boost product visibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising - the activity of producing advertisements for commercial products or services
