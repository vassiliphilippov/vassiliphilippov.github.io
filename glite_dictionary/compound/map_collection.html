map collection

1. NOUN

/ mæp kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / mæp kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a set of maps gathered together, especially as a book or organized group
a set of maps in book or group form

Examples

The library has a vast map collection that includes rare eighteenth-century charts.
Our geography teacher brought out a map collection for the upcoming project.
Researchers visited the university’s map collection to study historical trade routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts
