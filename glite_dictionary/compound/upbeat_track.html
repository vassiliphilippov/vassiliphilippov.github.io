upbeat track

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpˌbit ˈtræk / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbiːt ˈtræk / (BrE)
a piece of music that is lively and cheerful, typically with an energetic tempo
lively, cheerful piece of music

Examples

Her favorite playlist always includes at least one upbeat track to lift her spirits.
The DJ chose an upbeat track to get the crowd dancing early in the evening.
Many commercials use an upbeat track to create a positive, energetic mood.

Synset Examples

She danced to Khia’s upbeat tracks last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively song - a song with a fast, energetic tempo
  • Synonym: cheerful tune - a tune that sounds happy and positive
