automatic gun

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ɡʌn / (BrE)
a firearm that reloads automatically and continues firing as long as the trigger is held down
a gun that fires automatically

Examples

The soldier fired his automatic gun at the target during the training exercise.
Due to strict laws, owning an automatic gun can be difficult in many countries.
An automatic gun can fire dozens of rounds with a single pull of the trigger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine gun - a fully automatic mounted or portable firearm
