vine snake

1. NOUN

/ vaɪn sneɪk / (AmE), / vaɪn sneɪk / (BrE)
A slender, tree-dwelling snake found from southern Arizona to Bolivia, known for its elongated body and resemblance to a vine.
slender tree-dwelling snake of the Americas

Examples

The vine snake blends in perfectly with the branches as it hunts for prey.
Researchers discovered a new subspecies of vine snake in the Amazon rainforest.
A vine snake can be difficult to spot due to its slender, green body.
