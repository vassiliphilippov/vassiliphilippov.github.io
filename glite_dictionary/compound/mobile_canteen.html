mobile canteen

1. NOUN

/ ˌmoʊ.bəl kænˈtin / (AmE), / ˌməʊ.baɪl kænˈtiːn / (BrE)
a vehicle or temporary facility that serves food and drinks, often to people at work sites or in the armed forces
vehicle or stall serving food/drinks

Examples

The hospital set up a mobile canteen to serve hot meals to emergency responders.
A mobile canteen parked near the construction site, offering coffee and snacks to workers.
During the event, a mobile canteen provided sandwiches and drinks to attendees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food truck - a large vehicle equipped to cook and sell food
