California four o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˌkælɪˈfɔrniə fɔr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə fɔːr əˈklɒk / (BrE)
A plant of the species Mirabilis californica, native to California, known for its purple-red flowers.
plant with purple-red flowers native to California

Examples

The garden is full of California four o'clocks blooming in the late afternoon.
She identified the plant as a California four o'clock because of its distinctive purple-red flowers.
Wild California four o'clock can be spotted along the coastal trails in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four o'clock - any plant of the genus Mirabilis

Etymology

The name combines 'California' (its native region) and 'four o'clock', a common name for the genus Mirabilis, referring to the time the flowers typically open.
