clock dial

1. NOUN

/ klɑk ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / klɒk ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
the face of a clock on which the time is shown by hands or numerals
face of a clock showing the time

Examples

The antique clock dial had Roman numerals painted in gold.
She cleaned the glass covering the clock dial to make it easier to read the time.
Some watches feature a luminous clock dial so you can see the time in the dark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dial - the face of an instrument with a scale
