little chief hare

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtl tʃiːf hɛr / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl tʃiːf heə / (BrE)
a small mammal of North America, also known as the North American pika, related to rabbits and hares and found in rocky mountain areas
North American pika; small mountain-dwelling mammal

Examples

We spotted a little chief hare darting among the rocks during our hike in the Rockies.
The little chief hare is well adapted to cold alpine environments and makes distinctive whistling calls.
Researchers study how climate change affects the habitat of the little chief hare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pika - small mountain-dwelling mammal related to rabbits
