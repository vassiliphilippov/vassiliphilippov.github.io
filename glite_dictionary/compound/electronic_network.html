electronic network

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)

system of interconnected electronic components
medical

Examples
