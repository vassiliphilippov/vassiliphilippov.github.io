marmalade plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrməˌleɪd plʌm / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməlˌeɪd plʌm / (BrE)
A type of brown oval fruit whose flesh is used to make excellent sherbet.
a brown oval fruit used in sherbet

Examples

The ripe marmalade plum has a sweet, tangy flavor that makes it ideal for desserts.
She bought a basket of marmalade plums at the market to make sherbet.
The chef recommended using marmalade plum in tropical fruit salads for extra flavor.
