French West Indies

1. PROPN

/ frɛntʃ wɛst ˈɪndiz / (AmE), / frɛntʃ wɛst ˈɪndiːz / (BrE)
the islands in the Caribbean's Lesser Antilles that are administered by France, including Guadeloupe, Martinique, Saint Martin, and Saint Barthélemy.
Caribbean islands under French administration

Examples

The French West Indies are popular tourist destinations known for their beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
She studied the colonial history of the French West Indies at university.
Agricultural exports are a major part of the economy in the French West Indies.
