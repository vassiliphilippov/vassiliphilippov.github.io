chocolate fudge

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːklət fʌdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət fʌdʒ / (BrE)
A soft, rich, sweet confection made with chocolate or cocoa, sugar, butter, and often milk.
soft sweet made with chocolate

Examples

We bought some delicious chocolate fudge from the bakery.
She made a batch of chocolate fudge for the school fundraiser.
The recipe calls for dark cocoa powder to give the chocolate fudge a rich flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fudge - a soft crumbly or chewy sweet made from sugar, butter, and milk
