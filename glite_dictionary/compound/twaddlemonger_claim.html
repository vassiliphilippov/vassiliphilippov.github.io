twaddlemonger claim

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɑdəlˌmʌŋɡɚ kleɪm / (AmE), / ˈtwɒdəlˌmʌŋɡə kleɪm / (BrE)
A statement or assertion that is nonsensical, foolish, or lacking in sense or evidence.
nonsensical or foolish claim

Examples

The article was dismissed as yet another twaddlemonger claim by critics.
He was notorious for making twaddlemonger claims that no one took seriously.
In the debate, she refuted her opponent's twaddlemonger claim with clear evidence.

Synset Examples

The article was full of twaddlemonger claims that lacked evidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - something that has no meaning or makes no sense
  • Synonym: twaddle - foolish or trivial talk or ideas

Etymology

From 'twaddle' (nonsense) + 'monger' (dealer, promoter) + 'claim'.
