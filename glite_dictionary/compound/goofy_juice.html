goofy juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːfi dʒus / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfi dʒuːs / (BrE)
(informal) Alcoholic drinks, especially as they cause silly or playful behavior.
alcoholic drink (informal, humorous)
