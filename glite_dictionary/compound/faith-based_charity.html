faith-based charity

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪθ beɪst ˈtʃær.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθ beɪst ˈtʃær.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
A charity organization whose activities and operations are founded on or influenced by religious beliefs.
charity based on religious beliefs

Examples

Many people donate to a faith-based charity because they support its religious mission.
The city partnered with a faith-based charity to provide shelter during the winter.
Volunteering at a faith-based charity helped her connect with her community through shared values.

Synset Examples

They run a faith-based charity to help the needy.
Her organization focuses on faith-based community service.
He joined a faith-based group to find support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charity - organization giving help to those in need
  • Synonym: religious charity - charitable organization explicitly linked with a particular religion
