bird cherry tree

1. NOUN

/ bɝd ˈtʃɛri tri / (AmE), / bɜːd ˈtʃɛri triː / (BrE)
A small cherry tree, often with small fruits, that is frequented or fed on by birds, especially Prunus padus.
a small cherry tree whose fruit birds eat

Examples

The bird cherry tree in our garden attracts dozens of finches every spring.
In the wild, the berries of the bird cherry tree are an important food source for many birds.
She planted a bird cherry tree to encourage more wildlife in her backyard.
