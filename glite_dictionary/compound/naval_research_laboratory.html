Naval Research Laboratory

1. PROPN

/ ˈneɪvəl rɪˈsɜrtʃ ˌlæbrəˈtɔri / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl rɪˈsɜːtʃ ˌlæb(ə)rəˈtəri / (BrE)
the principal scientific and technical research laboratory of the United States Navy, responsible for conducting basic and applied research across various scientific and engineering fields
U.S. Navy's main scientific research laboratory

Examples

The Naval Research Laboratory pioneered important developments in radar technology during World War II.
Many scientists at the Naval Research Laboratory work on advanced materials for naval applications.
She visited the Naval Research Laboratory to discuss potential collaborations on marine engineering research.
