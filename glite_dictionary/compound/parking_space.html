parking space

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (BrE)
an area marked or designated for parking a single vehicle, usually within a parking lot, garage, or along a street
a designated area to park a vehicle

Examples

We couldn't find a parking space near the restaurant, so we had to park several blocks away.
He reserved a parking space in the building's garage for his new car.
Drivers often compete for the last available parking space on busy streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking spot - an alternative term for a parking space
