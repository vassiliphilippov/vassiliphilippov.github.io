controversial comparison

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑn.trəˈvɝː.ʃəl kəmˈpɛɹ.ə.sən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trəˈvɜː.ʃəl kəmˈpæɹ.ɪ.sən / (BrE)
a comparison or analogy that provokes disagreement, dispute, or strong reactions.
comparison provoking disagreement

Examples

The journalist's controversial comparison sparked fierce debate among viewers.
Many found the professor's controversial comparison between the two political leaders to be inappropriate.
During the interview, her controversial comparison overshadowed the rest of her arguments.

Synset Examples

He made a controversial comparison to Xitler during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analogy - a comparison between two things for explanation or clarification
  • Synonym: controversy - public disagreement or heated discussion
