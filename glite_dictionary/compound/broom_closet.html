broom closet

1. NOUN

/ bruːm ˈklɑːzɪt / (AmE), / bruːm ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
a small room or cupboard used for storing brooms and other cleaning supplies
small room for cleaning supplies

Examples

She keeps the vacuum cleaner and dustpans in the broom closet by the kitchen.
The hotel staff unlocked the broom closet to get extra cleaning supplies.
The janitor opened the broom closet and took out a mop.
