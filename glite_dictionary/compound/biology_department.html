biology department

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈɑlədʒi dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / bʌɪˈɒlədʒi dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a college or university responsible for teaching and research in biology
academic division for biology

Examples

Maria works as a lab technician in the biology department at the university.
The biology department offers courses in genetics, ecology, and cell biology.
After the merger, the biology department gained several new faculty members specializing in marine science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - an administrative unit in an organization, especially a university
