waiter's assistant

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪdɚz əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈweɪtəz əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a person in a restaurant who helps waiters by setting tables, serving, and clearing away dishes
restaurant employee who assists waiters

Examples

The waiter's assistant quickly cleared the plates after each course was finished.
She started working as a waiter's assistant to gain experience in the hospitality industry.
The restaurant hired an additional waiter's assistant to help with the busy dinner shifts.
