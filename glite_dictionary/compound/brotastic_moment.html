brotastic moment

1. NOUN

/ broʊˈtæstɪk ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / brəʊˈtæstɪk ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an occasion that is exceptionally fun or enjoyable for a group of friends, especially men
very fun moment among friends
informal slang american

Examples

Their road trip was full of brotastic moments they’ll remember for years to come.
Watching the big game together turned into a truly brotastic moment for the whole group.
Every summer barbecue has at least one brotastic moment that gets talked about later.

Synset Examples

The game night was filled with brotastic moments and laughter.

Etymology

Blend of 'bro' (slang for 'brother' or male friend) and 'fantastic'.
