Kingdom of Bhutan

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv buːˈtɑːn / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv bjuːˈtɑːn / (BrE)
A landlocked country in the eastern Himalayas, northeast of India, officially known as the Kingdom of Bhutan.
landlocked nation in the eastern Himalayas

Examples

The Kingdom of Bhutan is renowned for its commitment to Gross National Happiness.
She spent a year teaching English in the Kingdom of Bhutan.
The Kingdom of Bhutan shares its borders with China and India.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bhutan - The country in the Himalayas, often used without the formal 'Kingdom of'
  • Degree Lower: Nepal - A landlocked country in the Himalayas, neighboring Bhutan
  • Degree Lower: India - A neighboring country to the southwest of Bhutan
