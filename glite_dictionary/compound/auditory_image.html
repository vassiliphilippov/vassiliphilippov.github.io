auditory image

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔdəˌtɔri ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtəri ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a mental representation of a sound as if heard in the mind, similar to actual auditory perception
mental representation of a sound

Examples

Musicians often use an auditory image to imagine how a piece will sound before they play it.
The vivid auditory image of waves crashing helped her relax during meditation.
He could recall the melody perfectly, holding an auditory image of each note in his mind.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: visual image - a mental representation of something seen
