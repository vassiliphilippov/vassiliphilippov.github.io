softboy aesthetic

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔftˌbɔɪ əsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbɔɪ ɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
a style and attitude, especially among young men, characterized by sensitivity, emotional openness, and a gentle appearance
style showing sensitivity and vulnerability
slang

Examples

The artist’s style embodies the softboy aesthetic.
He prefers the softboy aesthetic over the more traditional masculine look.
Magazines have highlighted the rise of the softboy aesthetic in youth culture.

Synset Examples

The artist’s style embodies the softboy aesthetic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softboy - a young man presenting as sensitive or emotionally open, often with an artistic style
  • Degree Higher: softness - the quality of being gentle or sensitive

Etymology

Formed from 'softboy' (modern slang term for a sensitive, emotionally expressive man) and 'aesthetic.'
