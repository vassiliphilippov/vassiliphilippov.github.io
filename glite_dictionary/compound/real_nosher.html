real nosher

1. NOUN

/ ˈriəl ˈnɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈnɒʃə / (BrE)
A person who especially enjoys eating snacks or frequently eats between meals.
person who loves eating snacks

Examples

Her dad is a real nosher who keeps the pantry stocked with chips and crackers.
Ever since college, I’ve been a real nosher, always having popcorn or fruit on hand.
If you’re a real nosher, you’ll love these healthy snack recipes.

Synset Examples

He’s a real nosher, always munching on snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snacker - a person who eats snacks

Etymology

From 'real' (emphatic) + 'nosher', from Yiddish 'nashn' (to nibble, snack).
