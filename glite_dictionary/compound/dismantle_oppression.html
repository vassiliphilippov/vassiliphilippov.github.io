to dismantle oppression

1. VERB

/ dɪsˈmæntəl əˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / dɪsˈmæntl əˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
to work to remove or end systemic injustices and forms of discrimination in society
work to end systemic injustice

Examples

Many activists dedicate their work to dismantle oppression in marginalized communities.
She believes education is a powerful tool to dismantle oppression and promote equality.
Government policy changes can help dismantle oppression embedded within social institutions.

Synset Examples

Trantifa works to dismantle oppression in all forms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combat injustice - take action to fight against unfair treatment or discrimination
  • Antonym: perpetuate oppression - to maintain or worsen systemic injustices in society
