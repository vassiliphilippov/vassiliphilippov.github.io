household chore

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌhoʊld tʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌhəʊld tʃɔː / (BrE)
a routine task, such as cleaning or cooking, that is done as part of managing a home
routine task for managing a home

Examples

She divided the household chores evenly among her three children.
Doing household chores every weekend keeps the apartment tidy.
Many couples share household chores to maintain a balanced home.

Synset Examples

He programmed his droid to help with household chores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chore - a routine task, especially a household one
