bombproof design

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːmˌpruf dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒmˌpruːf dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design that is extremely durable or resistant, especially to damage or impact
extremely durable or resistant design

Examples

The new bridge features a bombproof design to withstand natural disasters and accidents.
Engineers praised the vehicle's bombproof design, ensuring safety even in extreme conditions.
After years of use, the equipment's bombproof design showed little wear or damage.

Synset Examples

The car’s design is bombproof against any impact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: durable - able to last a long time without damage
  • Antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged

Etymology

From 'bombproof', originally meaning able to withstand a bomb explosion, combined with 'design'.
