slender spike rush

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɛndər spaɪk rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə spaɪk rʌʃ / (BrE)
A fine-leaved aquatic plant of the spike rush family, often used as an aerator in aquariums.
aquatic spike rush plant

Examples

The aquarium was filled with slender spike rush to help oxygenate the water.
Many aquarists prefer slender spike rush because of its delicate appearance and easy maintenance.
Dense clusters of slender spike rush provided excellent hiding spots for the small fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spike rush - a type of grass-like aquatic or marshland plant of the genus Eleocharis
