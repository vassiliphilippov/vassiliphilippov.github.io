Master in Business Administration

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ ɪn ˈbɪz.nəs ˌæd.mɪ.nɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər ɪn ˈbɪz.nɪs ˌæd.mɪ.nɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A postgraduate academic degree focusing on business management and administration.
postgraduate business degree

Examples

She decided to pursue a Master in Business Administration after working for several years.
Earning a Master in Business Administration can open doors to leadership positions.
His Master in Business Administration helped him launch a successful startup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MBA - abbreviation for Master in Business Administration

Etymology

Formed from English master (a postgraduate degree) + business administration.
