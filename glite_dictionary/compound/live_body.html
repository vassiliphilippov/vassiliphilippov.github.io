live body

1. NOUN

/ laɪv ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / laɪv ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
the body of a living animal or person, as distinguished from a dead body
body of a living being

Examples

The rescue team was relieved to find a live body in the wreckage.
Search dogs are trained to detect the scent of a live body as opposed to a cadaver.
She was grateful when they pulled her from the rubble, thankful to be a live body among the survivors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: corpse - the dead body of a person or animal
