Persian violet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrʒən ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
a perennial plant grown especially as a houseplant for its fragrant bluish to dark lavender flowers
ornamental houseplant with fragrant lavender flowers

Examples

She placed a Persian violet on the windowsill to brighten up the kitchen.
The Persian violet needs indirect sunlight and regular watering to thrive indoors.
Its delicate fragrance makes the Persian violet a popular choice among houseplant enthusiasts.
