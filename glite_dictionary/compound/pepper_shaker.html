pepper shaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər ˌʃeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ˌʃeɪkə / (BrE)
A container with a perforated lid used for sprinkling ground pepper.
container for sprinkling ground pepper

Examples

Please pass the pepper shaker so I can season my soup.
The restaurant tables were set with a pepper shaker next to each salt shaker.
She accidentally knocked over the pepper shaker, spilling pepper all over the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salt shaker - a shaker with a perforated top for sprinkling salt
