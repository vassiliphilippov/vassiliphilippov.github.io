almond-leaves willow

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːlmənd liːvz ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːlmənd liːvz ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
A willow tree native to the western United States, with leaves resembling those of peach or almond trees.
willow with almond-like leaves

Examples

The almond-leaves willow flourishes along the banks of western rivers.
Botanists admire the unique foliage of the almond-leaves willow.
We planted an almond-leaves willow in our backyard for its beautiful, almond-shaped leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - a tree or shrub of the genus Salix, typically with narrow leaves
