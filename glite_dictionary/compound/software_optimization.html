software optimization

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔft.wɛr ˌɑp.tə.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɒft.wɛː ˌɒp.tɪ.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of altering a software system to improve its performance, efficiency, or resource usage.
improving software performance or efficiency

Examples

The development team focused on software optimization to reduce the application's memory usage.
Significant improvements in response time were achieved through software optimization.
Effective software optimization can lead to faster, more reliable programs with lower operational costs.

Synset Examples

Over time, the stakes in software optimization and compliance have risen exponentially as license types proliferate and grow more complex, especially as licensing and delivery models continue to change and grow more complex with virtualization and cloud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimization - the process of making something as effective or functional as possible
