velvet flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛlvət flaʊər / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt flaʊə / (BrE)
a tropical plant whose young leaves are used as leafy vegetables and whose seeds can be used as cereal
tropical plant used for food

Examples

The young leaves of the velvet flower are a popular ingredient in various African dishes.
Farmers in the region cultivate velvet flower both for its nutritious leaves and its edible seeds.
Traditionally, the seeds of the velvet flower are ground into flour and used as a cereal.
