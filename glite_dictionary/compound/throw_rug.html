throw rug

1. NOUN

/ ˈθroʊ ˌrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊ ˌrʌɡ / (BrE)
A small, light rug that can be easily moved and used to cover part of a floor for decorative or protective purposes.
small movable rug

Examples

The new throw rug in the living room adds a splash of color to the space.
She bought several throw rugs to protect the hardwood floors from scratches.
A patterned throw rug can completely change the look of a small room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: area rug - a rug that covers only part of a floor
