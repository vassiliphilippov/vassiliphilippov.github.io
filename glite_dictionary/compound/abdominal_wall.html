abdominal wall

1. NOUN

/ əbˈdɑm.ə.nəl wɔl / (AmE), / æbˈdɒm.ɪ.nəl wɔːl / (BrE)
the layer of muscle and tissue forming the outer structure of the abdomen
outer muscle layer of abdomen
medical

Examples

The abdominal wall protects the internal organs from injury.
A hernia occurs when part of an organ pushes through the abdominal wall.
Strengthening your abdominal wall can help improve your posture and core stability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abdomen - the part of the body between the chest and pelvis
