face mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs ˌmæsk / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌmɑːsk / (BrE)
A protective covering worn over the face, especially in sports such as baseball, football, or hockey, to prevent injury.
protective covering for the face (sports)

Examples

The football player put on his face mask before heading onto the field.
Ice hockey goalies rely on a sturdy face mask to protect themselves from flying pucks.
The umpire wore a face mask to guard against stray balls during the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - a hard or padded protective hat
  • Synonym: visor - a projecting part of a helmet covering the face or eyes
