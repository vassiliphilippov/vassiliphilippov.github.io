time of departure

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv dɪˈpɑrtʃər / (AmE), / taɪm əv dɪˈpɑːtʃə / (BrE)
the scheduled time at which a vehicle, such as a bus, train, or airplane, is set to leave its starting point
scheduled leaving time (transport)

Examples

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the time of departure.
The time of departure for flight 432 has been delayed due to weather conditions.
You can check the time of departure on the train schedule posted in the station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: departure time - the scheduled time something leaves
