family business

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
A company or organization owned and controlled by members of a single family.
company owned by one family

Examples

After graduation, she joined the family business and helped expand it internationally.
The family business has been passed down for three generations and remains successful.
Many local shops in this area are actually family businesses handed down through the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family firm - company owned and managed by a family
