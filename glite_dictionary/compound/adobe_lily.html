adobe lily

1. NOUN

/ əˈdoʊbi ˈlɪli / (AmE), / əˈdəʊbi ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A California wildflower with pinkish-purple flowers, found especially in adobe soil areas.
California herb with pinkish-purple flowers

Examples

The adobe lily blooms in early spring, adding color to the California hillsides.
We spotted a rare adobe lily while hiking through the grassy valley.
Gardeners prize the adobe lily for its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - any plant of the genus Lilium with prominent flowers
