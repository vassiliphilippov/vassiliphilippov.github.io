real wobbler

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈwɑb.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈwɒb.lə / (BrE)
something that is highly uncertain or unpredictable in outcome.
uncertain or unpredictable thing

Examples

The weather this spring has been a real wobbler, changing from sun to snow in a single day.
Investors are hesitant because the economy looks like a real wobbler this quarter.
Her ability to finish the race was a real wobbler after her recent injury.

Synset Examples

That new policy is a real wobbler; it's hard to predict its effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild card - an unpredictable or unknown factor
