line of heart

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv hɑrt / (AmE), / laɪn əv hɑːt / (BrE)
a crease on the palm, interpreted in palmistry as indicating a person's emotional nature
crease on the palm in palmistry

Examples

The line of heart is commonly associated with emotional stability and relationships in palmistry.
During the reading, the palmist examined the line of heart to gain insight into her client's romantic life.
According to legend, a deep line of heart signifies a person who loves deeply and sincerely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heart line - the same crease on the palm in palmistry
