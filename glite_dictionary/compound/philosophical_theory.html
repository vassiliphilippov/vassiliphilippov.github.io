philosophical theory

1. NOUN

/ fɪˌlɑsəˈfɪkəl ˈθɪəri / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒsəˈfɪkəl ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
a doctrine or system of ideas accepted by followers of a particular philosophy
doctrine or system in philosophy

Examples

The concept of utilitarianism is a famous philosophical theory discussed by many thinkers.
His dissertation explores a philosophical theory about the nature of reality.
Students debated whether the philosophical theory was compatible with modern scientific findings.
