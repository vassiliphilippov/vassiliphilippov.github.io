mountain pride

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˌpraɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˌpraɪd / (BrE)
a mat-forming plant with deep pink flowers on short erect leafy stems, found in rocky places at high elevations from Oregon to California
a high-elevation plant with pink flowers

Examples

The vibrant flowers of mountain pride brighten the rocky slopes each summer.
Hikers often spot mountain pride growing among the boulders at higher elevations.
Botanists study mountain pride for its ability to thrive in harsh mountain conditions.
