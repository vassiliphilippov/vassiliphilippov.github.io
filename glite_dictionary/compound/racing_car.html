racing car

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.sɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.sɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
A car designed and built to compete in motor racing events, typically capable of very high speeds and specialized handling.
car built for racing

Examples

He has always dreamed of driving a racing car on a professional circuit.
The new racing car features advanced aerodynamics and a more powerful engine.
During the competition, her racing car reached speeds of over 200 miles per hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports car - a car designed for performance at high speeds, not limited to racing
