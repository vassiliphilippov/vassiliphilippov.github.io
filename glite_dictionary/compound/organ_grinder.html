organ grinder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɡən ˌɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən ˌɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a street performer who plays a barrel organ, often with a monkey; also used metaphorically to refer to the person who is in charge or the main agent in a situation, as opposed to a subordinate.
street performer with a barrel organ; person in charge (metaphorical)

Examples

When he lost his job, he felt like an organ grinder, performing endlessly for a few coins.
The organ grinder attracted a large crowd in the city square with his lively music.
If you want results, talk to the organ grinder, not the monkey.

Synset Examples

She advised him to deal with the organ grinder, not the monkey.
Remember, it's the organ grinder, not the monkey, that makes the decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel organ - a mechanical musical instrument played by turning a handle

Etymology

From 'organ' (a musical instrument) and 'grinder' (one who operates or turns something); refers to those who played barrel organs on the streets in the 19th and early 20th centuries.
