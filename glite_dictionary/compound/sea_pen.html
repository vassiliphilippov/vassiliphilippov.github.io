sea pen

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi pɛn / (AmE), / ˈsi pɛn / (BrE)
a colonial marine animal with a soft, featherlike body, belonging to the order Pennatulacea, found especially in warm waters
featherlike marine colonial animal

Examples

Divers spotted several large sea pens swaying gently on the sandy sea floor.
A sea pen resembles a feather quill anchored in the mud or sand of the ocean bottom.
The biologist explained that a sea pen is actually a colony of tiny polyps working together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft coral - a colonial marine organism with a soft, flexible body; related group within Cnidaria
