Kera language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɛərə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Mayo-Kebbi Est Region of Chad, particularly in the Mont d'Illi department near Lake Tikem
a Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists study the Kera language to understand the diversity of Chadic languages in Africa.
The Kera language is spoken by communities living near Lake Tikem in Chad.
Efforts are underway to preserve traditional stories told in the Kera language.
