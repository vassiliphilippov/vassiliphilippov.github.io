graphic art

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræfɪk ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪk ɑːt / (BrE)
The visual arts of drawing, painting, or printmaking, as opposed to the performing arts or sculpture.
visual arts like drawing or printmaking

Examples

She studied graphic art in college, focusing on both printmaking and illustration.
Many museums have dedicated galleries for graphic art, displaying works on paper.
The techniques used in graphic art have evolved dramatically with digital tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine art - art concerned with beauty or aesthetic value

Etymology

From Greek 'graphikos' (pertaining to drawing or writing) + 'art'.
