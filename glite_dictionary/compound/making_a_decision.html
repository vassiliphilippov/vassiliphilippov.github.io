making a decision

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkɪŋ ə dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkɪŋ ə dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
the process of choosing or determining a course of action from several options
the act of choosing between options

Examples

Before making a decision, gather as much information as possible.
Effective leaders excel at making a decision even under pressure.
She hesitated for hours before finally making a decision about the job offer.

Synset Examples

Before making a decision, count to three.
He wanted to know the cost before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decide - to choose or judge after consideration
  • Synonym: decision-making - the process of making decisions
