California tree poppy

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪfərniə tri ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / kəˈlɪfəniə tri ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
a tall branching subshrub native to California and Mexico, cultivated for its silvery-blue leaves and large, fragrant white flowers
tall California shrub with large white flowers

Examples

The California tree poppy adds a striking presence to drought-tolerant gardens.
Gardeners prize the California tree poppy for its silvery foliage and showy white blooms.
We saw a beautiful California tree poppy in full flower during our trip to southern California.
