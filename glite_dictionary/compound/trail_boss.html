trail boss

1. NOUN

/ treɪl bɑs / (AmE), / treɪl bɒs / (BrE)
the person in charge of driving and overseeing a herd of cattle, especially on a cattle drive
person in charge of a cattle drive

Examples

The trail boss led the cattle drive across the open plains.
As trail boss, he made sure everyone knew their duties for the day.
After years as a trail boss, she earned a reputation for toughness and fairness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreman - a person in charge of a group of workers
  • Degree Lower: cowhand - a person who tends cattle; a cowboy or cowgirl
  • Degree Higher: rancher - an owner or manager of a ranch, sometimes overseeing trail bosses
