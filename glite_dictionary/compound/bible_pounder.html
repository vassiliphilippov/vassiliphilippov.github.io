Bible pounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪbəl ˌpaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl ˌpaʊndə / (BrE)
a person who aggressively promotes religious beliefs or frequently quotes the Bible, often in a forceful or zealous manner
zealous promoter of religious beliefs

Examples

Some politicians are criticized as Bible pounders for mixing religion with public policy.
He was known as the office Bible pounder, often quoting scripture at every meeting.
Although she is a devout Christian, she dislikes being labeled a Bible pounder.

Synset Examples

Being a Bible pounder doesn’t always win hearts.
She’s a Bible pounder who believes in literal interpretations.
He’s known as a Bible pounder, always quoting scripture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bible thumper - a person who aggressively promotes religious beliefs, especially Christianity
