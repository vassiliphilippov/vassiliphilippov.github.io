discount business

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈdɪskaʊnt ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a business that sells goods or services at reduced prices, usually below the typical retail value
business selling goods at reduced prices

Examples

After years of running a small boutique, Maria decided to open a discount business to attract budget-conscious shoppers.
The rise of online shopping has significantly impacted traditional discount businesses in many cities.
Many consumers rely on their local discount business for affordable household essentials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount store - a retail store that sells goods at lower prices than typical retail stores
