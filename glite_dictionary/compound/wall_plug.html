wall plug

1. NOUN

/ wɔl plʌɡ / (AmE), / wɔːl plʌɡ / (BrE)
a socket in a wall into which an electrical device can be plugged to receive power
electrical socket in a wall

Examples

She couldn't find an available wall plug for her laptop in the crowded café.
Be sure your charger is securely inserted into the wall plug.
Many new homes include a USB port next to the standard wall plug.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power outlet - a device providing electric power in a building
