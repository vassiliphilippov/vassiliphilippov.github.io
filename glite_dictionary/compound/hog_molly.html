hog molly

1. NOUN

/ hɑɡ ˈmɑli / (AmE), / hɒɡ ˈmɒli / (BrE)
a freshwater fish (typically referring to the river chub, Nocomis micropogon) found in warm, clear, shallow streams in North America
a type of freshwater fish

Examples

We spotted a hog molly darting beneath the rocks in the creek.
The hog molly is often used as bait by local anglers.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the hog molly population in this region.
