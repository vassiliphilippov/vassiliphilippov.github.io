body lotion

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
A moisturizing cream or liquid applied to the skin of the body, typically after bathing, to keep it soft and hydrated.
moisturizing cream or liquid for the body

Examples

She applies body lotion every morning to keep her skin soft and smooth.
After a hot shower, using a good body lotion helps prevent dryness.
The hotel provided complimentary body lotion and shampoo in the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moisturizer - a substance used for moisturizing the skin
