domestic cat

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk kæt / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk kat / (BrE)
A small, usually furry, domesticated mammal (Felis catus) kept as a pet or for catching mice.
small domesticated feline pet

Examples

The domestic cat is one of the most popular pets in the world.
My grandmother has a domestic cat that loves to nap in the sun.
Scientists studied the behavior of the domestic cat compared to its wild relatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat - a small, domesticated carnivorous mammal known for catching mice
