done deal

1. NOUN

/ dʌn dil / (AmE), / dʌn diːl / (BrE)
an agreement or arrangement that has been completed and cannot be changed
finalized agreement or arrangement

Examples

Once the funds arrive, it will be a done deal.
After the negotiations ended, the merger was a done deal.
He can't back out now; it's already a done deal.

Synset Examples

Once the funds arrive, it will be a done deal.
The contract is signed; it’s a done deal now.
Once we agree on the terms, it’s a done deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fait accompli - thing that has already happened or been decided before those affected hear about it
