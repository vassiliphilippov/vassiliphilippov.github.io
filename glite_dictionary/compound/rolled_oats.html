rolled oats

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊld oʊts / (AmE), / ˌrəʊld ˈəʊts / (BrE)
oats that have been steamed and flattened into flakes, used especially in baking and for making oatmeal or porridge.
oats flattened into flakes for cooking

Examples

She bought a bag of rolled oats to make homemade granola.
Many healthy breakfast recipes start with rolled oats as the main ingredient.
You can substitute rolled oats for breadcrumbs in some baking recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oatmeal - meal made from ground or rolled oats; also, porridge made from oats
