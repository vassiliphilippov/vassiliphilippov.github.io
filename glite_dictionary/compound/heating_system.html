heating system

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system or apparatus that provides heat to a building or part of a building.
system for warming buildings

Examples

The heating system broke down last night, leaving the house cold all morning.
We decided to upgrade the old heating system to a more energy-efficient model.
Their apartment building uses a central heating system that keeps every unit warm in winter.

Synset Examples

the heating system wasn't working
they have radiant heating

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central heating - system where heat is provided from a single source, usually for the whole building

2. NOUN

/ ˈhidɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
An apparatus or arrangement for supplying heat to a building or part of a building.
apparatus for heating a building

Examples

The old heating system broke down in the middle of winter, leaving the house freezing.
We decided to upgrade our heating system to a more energy-efficient model.
A central heating system provides warmth to all the rooms in the building.

Synset Examples

The gasman came to check the heating system today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furnace - an enclosed apparatus for producing heat
  • Synonym: boiler - a device for generating steam or hot water for heating
  • Antonym: air conditioning - a system for controlling temperature and humidity, usually cooling
