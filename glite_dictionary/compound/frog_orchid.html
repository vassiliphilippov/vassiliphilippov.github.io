frog orchid

1. NOUN

/ frɑɡ ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / frɒɡ ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
A small orchid (Coeloglossum viride) with hooded, green to yellow-green flowers often tinged with purple, found in temperate regions.
a small green-flowered orchid

Examples

We spotted a rare frog orchid growing in the meadow near the woods.
The frog orchid is easily missed because of its greenish flowers blending with the grass.
Several frog orchids were observed by botanists conducting a survey in the national park.

2. NOUN

/ frɔɡ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / frɒɡ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a small green orchid of the genus Habenaria, found in meadows and grasslands, with greenish flowers that resemble frogs
a small green meadow orchid

Examples

The frog orchid is often overlooked because of its greenish flowers that blend with the grass.
She found several frog orchids growing along the edge of the meadow.
Because it is rare in some regions, the frog orchid is protected in certain countries.
