30th birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝːtiɪθ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiəθ ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
the anniversary marking thirty years since a person's birth
anniversary of turning thirty

Examples

She invited everyone to her 30th birthday dinner at a fancy restaurant.
His parents gave him a new watch for his 30th birthday.
Many people see their 30th birthday as an important milestone in life.

Synset Examples

She celebrated her 30th birthday with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthday - the anniversary of the day on which a person was born
