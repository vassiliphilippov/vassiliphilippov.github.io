computer scientist

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of computers, including their design, theory, and practical applications
expert in computer science

Examples

As a computer scientist, she researches artificial intelligence and machine learning.
Many computer scientists contributed to the development of the Internet.
My brother is training to become a computer scientist at university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer engineer - a person who designs and develops computer hardware and software
