rehabilitation program

1. NOUN

/ riˌhæbəˈlɪteɪʃən ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / riˌhæbɪlɪˈteɪʃən ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a formal course of treatment or therapy designed to restore a person's health or abilities after illness, injury, or substance abuse
course of treatment to restore health or ability

Examples

After his accident, he enrolled in a rehabilitation program to regain his mobility.
Many addiction centers offer a rehabilitation program tailored to individual needs.
The hospital developed a new rehabilitation program for stroke survivors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rehabilitation - process of restoring health or normal life
