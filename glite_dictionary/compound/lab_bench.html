lab bench

1. NOUN

/ læb bɛntʃ / (AmE), / læb bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A long, sturdy table in a laboratory, used for scientific work and experiments.
table used for scientific work

Examples

The scientist set up her equipment carefully on the lab bench.
At school, students are expected to keep their lab benches clean after each experiment.
Each lab bench was supplied with a sink and a gas tap for chemistry lessons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a sturdy table at which manual work is done
