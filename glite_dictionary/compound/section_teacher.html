section teacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃən ˈtiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈsekʃən ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who is responsible for teaching a section of a large college or university course, often leading smaller groups separate from main lectures
teacher for a course section in college

Examples

Each section teacher is responsible for grading and discussing assignments with their group of students.
My section teacher was very approachable and helped clarify difficult topics from the lectures.
The university hires graduate students to serve as section teachers for large introductory classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaching assistant - someone who assists with teaching in a college course, often leading smaller groups or sections
