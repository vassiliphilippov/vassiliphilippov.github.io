red-flowered silky oak

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈflaʊɚd ˈsɪlki oʊk / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈflaʊəd ˈsɪlki əʊk / (BrE)
a tall shrub of eastern Australia, with cylindrical clusters of red flowers and pinnatifid leaves that are silky and grey beneath
Australian shrub with red flowers and silky leaves

Examples

The red-flowered silky oak adds a vibrant splash of color to gardens in eastern Australia.
Botanists studied the unique leaves of the red-flowered silky oak for their silky, grey underside.
Many birds are attracted to the nectar-rich blossoms of the red-flowered silky oak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silky oak - Australian tree with silky leaves and yellow or red flowers
