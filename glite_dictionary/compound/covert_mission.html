covert mission

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊvɚt ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkəʊvət ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a secret or concealed operation, especially one carried out by military, intelligence, or law enforcement agencies
secret or hidden operation

Examples

The agency sent a team on a covert mission to gather intelligence behind enemy lines.
During the Cold War, many covert missions remained classified for decades.
He trained for months before being assigned his first covert mission.

Synset Examples

The spy was involved in several covert missions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret mission - a mission that is kept hidden from public knowledge
  • Antonym: overt mission - an openly conducted mission
