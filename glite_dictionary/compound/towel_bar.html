towel bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊəl bɑr / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəl bɑː / (BrE)
a horizontal bar, usually attached to a wall, used for hanging towels
bar for hanging towels

Examples

She hung her damp towel neatly on the towel bar in the bathroom.
The new apartment came with a chrome towel bar above the sink.
Please make sure the towel bar is firmly attached to the wall before using it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel rack - a frame or bar for holding towels, especially several
  • Degree Lower: hand towel - a small towel used for drying hands
