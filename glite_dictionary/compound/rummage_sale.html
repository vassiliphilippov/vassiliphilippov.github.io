rummage sale

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌmɪdʒ seɪl / (AmE), / ˈrʌmɪdʒ seɪl / (BrE)
a sale of used or donated items, typically organized to raise money for a charity or cause
sale of used/donated items, usually for charity

Examples

Our neighborhood church is hosting a rummage sale to raise funds for the food pantry.
You can often find unique treasures at a rummage sale if you're willing to search.
The community center held a rummage sale with all proceeds going to local charities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garage sale - sale of used items, usually at someone's home
  • Synonym: jumble sale - British term for a sale of donated items, often for charity

Etymology

From 'rummage' (to search through), referring to sorting through items for sale.
