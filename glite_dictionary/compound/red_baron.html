Red Baron

1. PROPN

/ rɛd ˈbɑːrn / (AmE), / rɛd ˈbɑːrən / (BrE)
the nickname of Manfred von Richthofen, a famous German fighter pilot in World War I known for his striking red aircraft and credited with numerous aerial victories.
famous German fighter pilot of World War I

Examples

The legendary Red Baron became a symbol of aerial combat prowess during World War I.
Many aviation enthusiasts still admire the exploits of the Red Baron today.
During history class, we learned about the famous German ace known as the Red Baron.

Synset Examples

They named their café after the legendary Red Baron.
He dressed up as the Red Baron for Halloween.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Manfred von Richthofen - German World War I flying ace nicknamed the Red Baron

Etymology

Nickname of Manfred von Richthofen; 'Red' from his aircraft's color, 'Baron' from his title as a German nobleman.
