murder simulator

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdɚ ˌsɪmjəˌleɪɾɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə ˌsɪmjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
a game or program that allows users to simulate murder scenarios, often for entertainment or training purposes
game or program simulating murder scenarios

Examples

The controversial video game was labeled a murder simulator by critics.
He spent hours on the murder simulator to prepare for crime scene analysis competitions.
Parents expressed concern that exposure to a murder simulator could desensitize teenagers.

Synset Examples
