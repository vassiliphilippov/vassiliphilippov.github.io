waste of time

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv taɪm / (AmE), / weɪst əv taɪm / (BrE)
an activity or action that is not worthwhile or productive and results in lost time
something not worth spending time on

Examples

Replying to spam emails is a complete waste of time.
Some people feel that watching reality TV is just a waste of time.
If you keep arguing, this meeting will become a total waste of time.

Synset Examples

the waste of time could prove fatal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time waster - a person or thing that wastes time
