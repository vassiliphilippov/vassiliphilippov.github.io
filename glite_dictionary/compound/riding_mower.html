riding mower

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌmoʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ ˌməʊə / (BrE)
a lawnmower powered by an engine, designed to be ridden rather than pushed
motorized lawnmower you can ride on

Examples

He used his riding mower to cut the large lawn in less than an hour.
After buying a riding mower, yard work became much easier for the family.
The farmhand repaired the old riding mower before starting work on the fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lawn mower - a machine for cutting grass
