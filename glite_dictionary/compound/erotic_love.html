erotic love

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɑːtɪk lʌv / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒtɪk lʌv / (BrE)
a deep, passionate feeling of sexual desire and attraction towards another person
sexual desire and attraction

Examples

Many poets have written openly about erotic love in their verses.
Some people distinguish erotic love from deeper emotional connections like friendship or companionship.
The novel explores themes of erotic love as well as longing and loss.

Synset Examples

their love left them indifferent to their surroundings
she was his first love

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: romantic love - love characterized by romance and emotional intimacy
  • Degree Lower: lust - strong sexual desire without emotional attachment

Etymology

From Greek *erōtikos* (relating to sexual love) and English 'love'.
