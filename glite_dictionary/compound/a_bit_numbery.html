a bit numbery

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈnʌmbəri / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈnʌmbəri / (BrE)
Slightly too technical, mathematical, or abstract in nature.
somewhat technical or abstract
informal

Examples

The professor's explanation was a bit numbery and hard to follow for most students.
I like the design, but this version looks a bit numbery compared to the others.
Her presentation was a bit numbery, focusing too much on raw data and statistics.

Synset Examples

The decorations are a bit numbery for my taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical - having or requiring specialized knowledge
  • Synonym: abstract - existing in thought or as an idea but not having a physical existence
