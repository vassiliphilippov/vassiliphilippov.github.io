slip of paper

1. NOUN

/ slɪp əv ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / slɪp əv ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a small, thin sheet of paper, often used for notes, receipts, or forms
small sheet of paper

Examples

She wrote her phone number on a slip of paper and handed it to me.
Please sign your name at the bottom of the slip of paper.
A tiny slip of paper fell out when I opened the old book.

Synset Examples

a receipt slip
a withdrawal slip
