under glass

1. ADV

/ ˈʌndɚ ˈɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈɡlɑːs / (BrE)
displayed or preserved in a protective case, typically made of glass, for viewing or safeguarding purposes
displayed or kept in a glass case

Examples

The priceless manuscript is kept under glass to prevent damage from handling.
Visitors admired the butterfly collection, each specimen carefully arranged under glass.
The museum displayed ancient jewelry under glass to protect it from dust and theft.

Synset Examples

The rare artifact is kept under glass for protection.
