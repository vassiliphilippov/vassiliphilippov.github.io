rising revenue

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈrɛvəˌnu / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈrɛvənjuː / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of money earned by a business, organization, or government over a period of time
increasing income or earnings

Examples

The company's rising revenue enabled it to expand operations internationally last year.
Analysts are optimistic due to rising revenue in the technology sector.
Despite higher costs, their rising revenue led to greater overall profits.

Synset Examples

Higher ticket prices contributed to their rising revenue.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: falling revenue - a decrease in income or earnings
