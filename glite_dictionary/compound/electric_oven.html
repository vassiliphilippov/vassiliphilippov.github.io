electric oven

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈʌvən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛk.trɪk ˈʌv.ən / (BrE)
An oven powered by electricity, used for baking, roasting, or heating food.
oven powered by electricity

Examples

We bought a new electric oven to replace our old gas model.
The electric oven heats up quickly and bakes evenly.
She prefers using an electric oven for baking cakes.
