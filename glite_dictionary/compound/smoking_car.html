smoking car

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmoʊkɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car or compartment where smoking is permitted
railway car for smokers

Examples

The train offered a smoking car for passengers who wished to light up during their journey.
In the past, every long-distance train had a designated smoking car.
She avoided the smoking car because she was sensitive to the smell of tobacco.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoking compartment - compartment on a train where smoking is allowed
  • Antonym: non-smoking car - train car where smoking is prohibited
