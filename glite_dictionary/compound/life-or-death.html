life-or-death

1. ADJ

/ ˌlaɪf ər ˈdɛθ / (AmE), / ˌlaɪf ɔː ˈdɛθ / (BrE)
Of the utmost importance, often involving a choice or situation where living or dying is at stake.
vitally important, often involving survival

Examples

The surgeons made a life-or-death decision during the operation.
In a life-or-death situation, every second counts.
The rescue team responded to a life-or-death emergency on the mountain.

Synset Examples

a life-and-death struggle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vital - absolutely necessary or essential
  • Antonym: trivial - of little value or importance; not serious
