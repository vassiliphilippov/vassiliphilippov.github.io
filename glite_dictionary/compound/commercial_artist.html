commercial artist

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɝːʃəl ˈɑːrtɪst / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
an artist who creates drawings, paintings, or designs used in advertising, publishing, and related commercial fields
artist producing work for advertising and commerce

Examples

She hired a commercial artist to design the new company logo.
Many commercial artists work as freelancers for advertising agencies.
As a commercial artist, he creates packaging designs for major brands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illustrator - an artist who draws or creates images for books or advertisements
