gym shoe

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm ʃu / (AmE), / dʒɪm ʃuː / (BrE)
A light-weight canvas shoe with a flexible rubber sole, typically worn for sports or physical exercise.
light canvas athletic shoe

Examples

He put on his gym shoes before heading to basketball practice.
Remember to bring your gym shoes for the physical education class tomorrow.
Her old gym shoes were covered in paint from art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneaker - a soft shoe worn for sports or casual occasions
  • Synonym: plimsoll - British term for lightweight rubber-soled canvas shoe
  • Antonym: dress shoe - formal shoe typically made of leather
