clustered lady's slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌstɚd ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəd ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
A wild orchid of western North America with several short stems, each bearing two broad leaves and two to four drooping brownish to greenish flowers with purple-mottled pouches.
species of wild orchid in western North America

Examples

The clustered lady's slipper thrives in the moist woodlands of British Columbia.
Researchers discovered a patch of clustered lady's slipper orchids along the hiking trail.
He photographed the delicate flowers of the clustered lady's slipper during his fieldwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - any of several orchids with slipper-shaped pouches

Etymology

Named for the clustered growth and the slipper-shaped flowers, resembling a lady's slipper.
