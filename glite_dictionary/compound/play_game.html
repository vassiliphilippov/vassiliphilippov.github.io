to play game

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / pleɪ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
to engage in activities, often for enjoyment or recreation, that involve rules and competition or amusement
take part in recreational activity

Examples

The children love to play games in the park after school.
We sometimes play games together as a family on Friday nights.
She was too tired to play games and decided to read instead.

Synset Examples

They often play games to unwind on weekends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play - to take part in games or recreational activities
