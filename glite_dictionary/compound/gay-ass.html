gay-ass

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪ ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ ˌas / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) Extremely flamboyant or bold in style, especially in reference to clothing or appearance, sometimes used as an insult.
very flamboyant or bold (slang, offensive)
slang offensive

Examples

He showed up at the party in a gay-ass outfit that made everyone stare.
That's the most gay-ass shirt I've ever seen—I love it!
Some people criticized the celebrity for wearing a gay-ass suit on live television.

Synset Examples

She wore a gay-ass outfit that turned heads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flamboyant - (of a person or their behavior) tending to attract attention because of exuberance, confidence, and stylishness

Etymology

Formed in slang by combining 'gay' (meaning flamboyant or associated with LGBTQ+ stereotypes) with the intensifying suffix '-ass' in informal speech.
