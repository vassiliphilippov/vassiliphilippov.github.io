sick call

1. NOUN

/ sɪk kɔl / (AmE), / sɪk kɔːl / (BrE)
the designated time when members of the military report to a medical officer as being ill or needing medical attention
military time for reporting illness

Examples

Each morning, soldiers must assemble at sick call if they require treatment for any illness or injury.
During sick call, the medic assessed several cases of minor illness.
You should report to sick call if you're feeling unwell.
