skimo lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈskiːmoʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈskiːməʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of life centered around ski mountaineering, often emphasizing adventure, physical fitness, and outdoor pursuits in snowy mountain environments.
lifestyle focused on ski mountaineering

Examples

Many aspire to the skimo lifestyle for its blend of adventure and physical challenge.
Social media influencers frequently share aspects of their skimo lifestyle during winter months.
Living the skimo lifestyle often involves early mornings, long ascents, and a strong connection to nature.

Synset Examples

The 'skimo lifestyle fascinates many outdoor enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski mountaineering - the sport of climbing mountains on skis or carrying them and descending on skis

Etymology

Blend of 'ski mountaineering' (abbreviated as 'skimo') and 'lifestyle'.
