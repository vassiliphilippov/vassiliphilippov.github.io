symbol-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəl ˌwʌrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəl ˌwʌʃɪp / (BrE)
the practice of worshipping symbols rather than what they represent
worship of symbols

Examples

The sect's focus on symbol-worship was criticized by reformers seeking deeper meaning.
Some philosophers argue that symbol-worship can distract believers from true spiritual experience.
The history class discussed how symbol-worship shaped the culture of ancient civilizations.
