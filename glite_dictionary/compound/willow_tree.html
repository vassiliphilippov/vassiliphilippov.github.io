willow tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪloʊ triː / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊ triː / (BrE)
A deciduous tree or shrub of the genus Salix, typically with slender branches and narrow leaves, often found near water.
deciduous tree of the genus Salix

Examples

The old willow tree by the river provided welcome shade during the summer.
A pair of ducks nested beneath the drooping branches of the willow tree.
She loved to paint the delicate leaves of the willow tree in her garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - any tree or shrub of the genus Salix
