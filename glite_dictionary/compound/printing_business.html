printing business

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ ˌbɪznɪs / (BrE)
A company or firm that provides commercial printing services, such as printing books, magazines, brochures, or marketing material.
company providing commercial printing services

Examples

After years working in advertising, she decided to open her own printing business.
The printing business faced challenges as more clients moved to digital media.
Our company has relied on the same printing business for all our brochures and flyers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: print shop - a shop or company that offers printing services
