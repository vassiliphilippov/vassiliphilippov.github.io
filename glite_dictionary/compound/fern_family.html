fern family

1. NOUN

/ fɜrn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / fɜːn ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a group of plants consisting of ferns and closely related species, classified as a family in botanical taxonomy
group of related ferns in botany

Examples

The fern family is known for its distinctive fronds and reproduction by spores.
Botanists have discovered new species within the fern family in tropical rainforests.
Most members of the fern family thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fern - a non-flowering plant with feathery or leafy fronds
  • Synonym: plant family - a group of plants classified together in taxonomy
