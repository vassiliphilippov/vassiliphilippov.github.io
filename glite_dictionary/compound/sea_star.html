sea star

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi ˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌstɑː / (BrE)
A marine echinoderm with a star-shaped body, typically having five arms extending from a central disk.
star-shaped marine animal

Examples

The colorful sea star moved slowly along the ocean floor in search of food.
Scientists are studying how a sea star can regenerate its lost arm.
We spotted a bright orange sea star in a tide pool at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starfish - another common name for sea star
