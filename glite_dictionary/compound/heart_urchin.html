heart urchin

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt ˌɜrtʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌɜːtʃɪn / (BrE)
a sea urchin with a heart-shaped body enclosed in a rigid, spiny shell
sea urchin with a heart-shaped shell

Examples

We found a heart urchin washed up on the shore during our morning walk.
The shell of the heart urchin is prized by collectors for its unique shape.
Marine biologists study the burrowing habits of the heart urchin in sandy seabeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea urchin - marine animal with a round, spiny shell
