in due course

1. ADV

/ ɪn du ˈkɔrs / (AmE), / ɪn djuː ˈkɔːs / (BrE)
at the appropriate time; when the time is right or expected
at the appropriate time

Examples

Your application will be reviewed in due course, and you will be contacted if you are shortlisted.
All the necessary documents will be provided in due course.
She assured us that the results would be announced in due course.

Synset Examples

we'll get to this question in due course

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eventually - after a period of time; in the end
  • Antonym: immediately - at once; without delay
