first love

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst lʌv / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst lʌv / (BrE)
the first person one has a romantic relationship or strong affection for, often remembered as significant or formative
one's first romantic relationship

Examples

Everyone remembers their first love and how it changed them.
She met her first love while studying abroad in Italy.
His first love taught him valuable lessons about trust and heartbreak.

Synset Examples

She always remembers her first love fondly.
Their first love often shapes their future relationships.
He never forgot his first love from high school.
