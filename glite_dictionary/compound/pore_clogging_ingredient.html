pore clogging ingredient

1. NOUN

/ pɔɹ ˈklɑɡɪŋ ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / pɔː ˈklɒɡɪŋ ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
an ingredient in a cosmetic or skincare product that tends to block the pores of the skin, potentially leading to breakouts or acne
ingredient that blocks skin pores

Examples

Some people avoid products with pore clogging ingredients to prevent acne.
Always check labels for pore clogging ingredients if you have sensitive skin.
Dermatologists often recommend non-comedogenic products without pore clogging ingredients.

Synset Examples

The efficiency of comedogenic ingredients is determined by one's skin type.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comedogenic ingredient - ingredient likely to cause comedones by blocking pores
