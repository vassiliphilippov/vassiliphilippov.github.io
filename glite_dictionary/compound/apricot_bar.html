apricot bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt bɑr / (AmE), / ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt bɑː / (BrE)
A sweet baked bar or dessert made with apricot jam or filling, often layered with pastry or crumb topping.
sweet baked bar with apricot filling

Examples

She brought a tray of homemade apricot bars to the bake sale.
I always enjoy having an apricot bar with my afternoon tea.
These apricot bars are sweet, tangy, and perfect for dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit bar - a sweet bar made with fruit filling or flavoring
