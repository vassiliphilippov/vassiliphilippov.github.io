dinner jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A semiformal jacket for men, typically black or dark, worn as part of evening dress.
semiformal men's evening jacket

Examples

He looked very elegant in his dinner jacket at the reception.
A black dinner jacket is required for the event.
He bought a new dinner jacket for the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuxedo - a formal evening suit for men, especially in American English
