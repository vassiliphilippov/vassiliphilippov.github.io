Gila Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈhiːlə ˈdezərt / (AmE), / ˈhiːlə ˈdezət / (BrE)
a desert region located in southern Arizona, known for its arid climate and unique flora and fauna
desert area in southern Arizona

Examples

The unique cacti of the Gila Desert attract botanists from around the world.
Many animals in the Gila Desert have adapted to survive with very little water.
The Gila Desert is a popular destination for hiking and wildlife observation.

Etymology

Named after the Gila River; 'Gila' from Spanish, meaning 'musical' or possibly from a Native American word.
