four-lined plant bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr laɪnd ˈplænt bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɔː laɪnd ˈplɑːnt bʌɡ / (BrE)
a yellow or orange leaf bug with four black stripes down its back, native to central and eastern North America
striped North American leaf bug

Examples

The four-lined plant bug causes unsightly spots on many garden plants.
Gardeners often look for signs of the four-lined plant bug during early summer.
Damage from the four-lined plant bug is often mistaken for fungal infections.
