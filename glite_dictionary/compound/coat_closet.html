coat closet

1. NOUN

/ koʊt ˈklɑsɪt / (AmE), / kəʊt ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
A closet used for storing coats and other outerwear.
closet for storing coats

Examples

Please hang your jacket in the coat closet by the front door.
The coat closet was too small to fit all our winter clothes.
They searched the coat closet for an extra umbrella before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wardrobe - a tall, freestanding cupboard used for storing clothes
