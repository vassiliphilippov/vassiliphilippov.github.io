live jazz

1. NOUN

/ laɪv dʒæz / (AmE), / laɪv dʒæz / (BrE)
Jazz music performed in real time by musicians, typically at a venue, rather than a recording.
jazz performed live by musicians

Examples

The club is famous for its live jazz performances every Friday night.
We enjoyed dinner with an amazing live jazz band playing in the background.
Tickets for tonight's live jazz concert sold out within hours.

Synset Examples

The restaurant features live jazz every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live music - music performed in person rather than recorded
  • Antonym: recorded jazz - jazz music recorded and played back, not performed live
