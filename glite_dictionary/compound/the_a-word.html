the a-word

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈeɪ wɝd / (AmE), / ði ˈeɪ wɜːd / (BrE)
a euphemistic way of referring to an offensive or taboo word beginning with 'a', most commonly 'ass' or 'asshole'
euphemism for an offensive word starting with 'a'

Examples

She was shocked when her coworker let slip the a-word during the meeting.
Parents often tell their children not to say the a-word at school.
When he stubbed his toe, he accidentally muttered the a-word.

Synset Examples

She dropped the a-word during our conversation.
Don't use that a-word around the kids.
He promised not to say the a-word again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the f-word - euphemism for an offensive word beginning with 'f'
  • Synonym: the n-word - euphemism for an offensive racial slur beginning with 'n'
