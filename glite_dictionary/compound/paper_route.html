paper route

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər ruːt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ruːt / (BrE)
an assigned area or path along which a person delivers newspapers regularly, usually daily or weekly
route for delivering newspapers

Examples

When I was a teenager, I had a paper route that required getting up before sunrise.
He saved enough money from his paper route to buy a new bike.
Delivering newspapers on a paper route taught her responsibility at a young age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery route - a route along which items are delivered

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ruːt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ruːt / (BrE)
a job or route assigned to a person, especially a young person, for regularly delivering newspapers to customers
job or route for delivering newspapers
american

Examples

When I was thirteen, I had a paper route every morning before school.
He saved enough money from his paper route to buy a bicycle.
Many kids used to have a paper route as their first job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newspaper round - route for delivering newspapers (British English)
