Hebei province

1. PROPN

/ heɪˈbeɪ ˈprɑvɪns / (AmE), / hɛˈbeɪ ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a populous province in northeastern China, surrounding Beijing and Tianjin
province in northeastern China

Examples

The industrial output of Hebei province has grown rapidly in recent years.
Many bird species can be found in the wetlands of Hebei province.
She traveled to Hebei province for a cultural exchange program.

Etymology

From Chinese 河北 (Héběi), meaning 'north of the river', referring to its location north of the Yellow River.
