fly-fishing

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪ ˌfɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ ˌfɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or technique of fishing with a rod and line using artificial flies as bait.
fishing using artificial flies as lures

Examples

He spent the afternoon fly-fishing in the mountain stream.
Many people find fly-fishing to be a relaxing and meditative hobby.
She learned fly-fishing from her grandfather during summer holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angling - fishing with rod and line
