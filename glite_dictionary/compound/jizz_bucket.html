jizz bucket

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪz ˈbʌkɪt / (AmE), / dʒɪz ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
A highly vulgar and derogatory term for a disrespectful or obnoxious person.
vulgar insult; disrespectful person
vulgar slang offensive
