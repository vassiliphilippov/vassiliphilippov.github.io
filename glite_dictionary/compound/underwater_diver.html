underwater diver

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndɚˌwɔtɚ ˈdaɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌwɔːtə ˈdaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who works or spends time beneath the surface of water, especially using special breathing equipment
a person who works below water

Examples

The underwater diver explored the sunken shipwreck off the coast.
As an underwater diver, she often works in challenging and dangerous environments.
They hired an experienced underwater diver to inspect the bridge’s submerged supports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scuba diver - a person who swims underwater using a scuba set
  • Synonym: diver - a person who dives as a sport, occupation, or hobby
