Norwegian krone

1. NOUN

/ nɔːˈwiːdʒən ˈkroʊnə / (AmE), / nɔːˈwiːdʒən ˈkrəʊnə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of Norway, subdivided into 100 øre
currency of Norway

Examples

The exchange rate for the Norwegian krone fluctuates against the euro daily.
She exchanged her euros for Norwegian krone before visiting Oslo.
The price of the ticket was 150 Norwegian krone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: krone - the currency unit used in Norway and Denmark

Etymology

From Norwegian 'krone' meaning 'crown'.
