silk grass

1. NOUN

/ sɪlk ɡræs / (AmE), / sɪlk ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a valuable forage grass found in dry upland areas and plains of western North America to northern Mexico
forage grass of North America

Examples

Ranchers often rely on silk grass as a primary food source for cattle in dry regions.
The resilient nature of silk grass makes it well-suited to the arid plains of northern Mexico.
During droughts, silk grass remains one of the few grasses that continue to thrive.
