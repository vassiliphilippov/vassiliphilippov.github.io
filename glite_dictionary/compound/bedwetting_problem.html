bedwetting problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌwɛtɪŋ ˈprɑːbləm / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌwɛtɪŋ ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
a difficulty or issue involving involuntary urination during sleep
involuntary urination during sleep issue

Examples

Many children eventually outgrow their bedwetting problem without medical intervention.
If a bedwetting problem persists after age seven, parents often seek professional advice.
The school nurse spoke sensitively about how to address a bedwetting problem in students.

Synset Examples
