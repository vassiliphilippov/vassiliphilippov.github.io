God Almighty

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑd ɔlˈmaɪti / (AmE), / ɡɒd ɔːlˈmaɪti / (BrE)
a name for the Judeo-Christian God, emphasizing His supreme power and authority
the Judeo-Christian God; supreme deity

Examples

Many believers pray to God Almighty for guidance and strength.
He thanked God Almighty for the blessings in his life.
People often invoke God Almighty in moments of crisis or awe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the Lord - another term for the Judeo-Christian God
  • Synonym: Allah - the Islamic term for God
