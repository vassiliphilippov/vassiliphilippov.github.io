dropping zone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑpɪŋ zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpɪŋ zəʊn / (BrE)
an area designated for military supplies, equipment, or personnel to be dropped by aircraft to ground forces
designated area for airborne supply drops

Examples

The helicopter dropped the crates accurately in the dropping zone marked by smoke grenades.
Troops rushed to collect medical supplies from the dropping zone as soon as the signal was given.
In hostile territory, locating a secure dropping zone is essential for resupplying soldiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop zone - designated area for parachute landings or supply drops
