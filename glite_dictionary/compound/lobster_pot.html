lobster pot

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbstɚ pɑt / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə pɒt / (BrE)
A container or trap, usually made of wicker, wire, or plastic, used for catching lobsters in the sea.
trap for catching lobsters

Examples

Fishermen checked each lobster pot early in the morning to see if anything had been caught.
The storm washed several lobster pots ashore during the night.
Each lobster pot is marked by a brightly colored buoy floating on the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab pot - trap for catching crabs
