plastic container

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk kənˈteɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk kənˈteɪnə / (BrE)
A container made from plastic, typically used for storing or carrying things.
container made from plastic

Examples

We keep leftover food in plastic containers to prevent it from spoiling.
The shelves were lined with plastic containers filled with colorful beads.
Please recycle any plastic container after using its contents.

Synset Examples

He prefers plastic containers for their durability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: container - an object used for holding something
