Black Forest

1. PROPN

/ blæk ˈfɔːrɪst / (AmE), / blæk ˈfɒrɪst / (BrE)
a mountainous and densely wooded region in southwestern Germany, famous for its scenic beauty
mountainous wooded region of southwestern Germany

Examples

They spent their honeymoon hiking in the Black Forest and enjoying the breathtaking views.
The Black Forest is known for its traditional cuckoo clocks and delicious cakes.
Several fairy tales by the Brothers Grimm are set in the Black Forest.

Etymology

From German Schwarzwald, literally 'black forest', from the dense growth of dark fir trees.
