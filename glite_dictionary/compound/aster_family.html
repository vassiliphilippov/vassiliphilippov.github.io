aster family

1. NOUN

/ ˈæstər ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈɑːstə ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants with heads composed of many florets, including asters, daisies, sunflowers, and dandelions
family of flowering plants (asters, daisies, sunflowers)

Examples

The aster family includes a wide variety of popular garden flowers and weeds.
Sunflowers, marigolds, and dandelions all belong to the aster family.
Many members of the aster family are known for their daisy-like appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composite family - another term for aster family; family of plants with composite flower heads
  • Synonym: sunflower family - alternative name for aster family; includes sunflowers and related plants
