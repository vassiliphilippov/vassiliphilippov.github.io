ham and eggs

1. NOUN

/ hæm ənd ɛɡz / (AmE), / ham ənd ɛɡz / (BrE)
a dish consisting of eggs, usually scrambled or fried, served with slices of ham, typically eaten for breakfast
dish of eggs and ham

Examples

She ordered ham and eggs for breakfast at the diner.
On Sunday mornings, he liked to cook ham and eggs for his family.
The menu featured classic dishes such as ham and eggs and pancakes.
