Isthmus of Tehuantepec

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪsθməs əv tɛˌwɑːn.təˈpɛk / (AmE), / ˈɪsθməs əv tɛˌwæntɪˈpɛk / (BrE)
the narrowest part of southern Mexico, an isthmus between the Bay of Campeche in the north and the Gulf of Tehuantepec in the south
narrowest part of southern Mexico (geographical isthmus)

Examples

The Isthmus of Tehuantepec is considered a vital corridor for trade and transportation within Mexico.
Several important oil pipelines cross the Isthmus of Tehuantepec to link the Gulf of Mexico with the Pacific Ocean.
The climate along the Isthmus of Tehuantepec is influenced by its proximity to both the Gulf and the Pacific.

Etymology

From Nahuatl 'tecuani-tepec' meaning 'jaguar hill'.
