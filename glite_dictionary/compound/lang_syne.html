lang syne

1. NOUN

/ læŋ saɪn / (AmE), / læŋ saɪn / (BrE)
A time long past; the distant past, especially as remembered with nostalgia.
the distant past (nostalgic use)
archaic

Examples

She often reminisced about the joys of lang syne with her childhood friends.
He longed for the traditions of lang syne during the holidays.
Many stories were told of love and loss in the days of lang syne.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olden days - a period of time in the distant past

Etymology

From Scots 'lang syne' meaning 'long since'.
