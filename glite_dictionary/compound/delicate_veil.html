delicate veil

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛlɪkət veɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkət veɪl / (BrE)
A fine, light piece of fabric worn over the face, often for decoration, modesty, or ceremonial purposes.
fine, light fabric worn over the face

Examples

The bride walked down the aisle with a delicate veil covering her face.
She lifted her delicate veil to greet the guests after the ceremony.
A delicate veil of lace added an air of mystery to her appearance.

Synset Examples

She wore a delicate veil during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veil - a piece of fine material worn by women to protect or conceal the face
