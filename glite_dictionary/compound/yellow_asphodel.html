yellow asphodel

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ əˈsfɑːdɛl / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ əˈsfɒdɛl / (BrE)
a perennial plant with a leafy stem and fragrant yellow flowers, native to Europe
a European plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with blooming yellow asphodel in early summer.
Botanists identified the rare yellow asphodel growing near the lake.
She pressed a delicate yellow asphodel between the pages of her book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asphodel - a plant of the lily family with clusters of flowers
