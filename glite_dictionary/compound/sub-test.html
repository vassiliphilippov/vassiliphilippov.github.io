sub-test

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌb tɛst / (AmE), / ˈsʌb tɛst / (BrE)
one of several smaller tests that together form part of a larger testing procedure or battery
a smaller component of a larger test

Examples

The psychologist reviewed each sub-test before interpreting the overall results of the assessment.
Reading comprehension is assessed as a separate sub-test within the language evaluation battery.
She scored highest on the vocabulary sub-test of the standardized exam.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: test - an examination or assessment to measure knowledge, ability, or performance
