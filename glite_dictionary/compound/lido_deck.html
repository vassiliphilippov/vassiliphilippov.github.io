lido deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːdoʊ dɛk / (AmE), / ˈliːdəʊ dɛk / (BrE)
an open deck on a passenger ship or cruise liner, usually including a swimming pool, used for sunbathing and relaxation
open deck with a swimming pool on a ship

Examples

The children spent most of the afternoon swimming on the lido deck of the cruise ship.
Passengers relaxed on sun loungers around the pool on the lido deck.
Pools and bars on the lido deck are popular features of modern cruise ships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sun deck - an open deck on a ship designed for sunbathing

Etymology

From Italian 'lido' (shore, beach), influenced by the Lido di Venezia; 'deck' from maritime vocabulary.
