Nesselrode pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛsəlˌroʊd ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛsəlˌrəʊd ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a rich frozen dessert made with chestnuts, maraschino cherries, candied fruits, and flavored with liqueur or rum
rich frozen dessert with chestnuts and fruits

Examples

The chef prepared a classic Nesselrode pudding for dessert at the holiday banquet.
A traditional Nesselrode pudding contains candied fruits and is often flavored with rum.
Her grandmother's recipe for Nesselrode pudding has been passed down through generations.

Etymology

Named after Count Karl Nesselrode (1780–1862), a Russian diplomat; the dessert was created for him by his chef.
