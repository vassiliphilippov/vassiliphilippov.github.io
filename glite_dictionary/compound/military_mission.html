military mission

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an operation or task assigned to a military unit or personnel by a higher authority, typically with a defined objective
operation assigned to military personnel

Examples

The team prepared for a challenging military mission deep in enemy territory.
Every military mission requires detailed planning and precise execution to succeed.
She returned from her military mission abroad with valuable experience and new skills.

Synset Examples

the planes were on a bombing mission

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mission - a specific task or duty assigned to a person or group
  • Synonym: operation - a military action or movement with a particular objective
