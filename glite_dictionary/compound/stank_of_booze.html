to stank of booze

1. VERB

/ stæŋk əv buːz / (AmE), / stæŋk əv buːz / (BrE)
had a strong, unpleasant smell of alcohol
smelled strongly of alcohol
informal

Examples

After returning from the bar, he stank of booze and could barely walk straight.
Her clothes stank of booze after the wild party last night.
When he entered the office, everyone noticed he stank of booze from the night before.

Synset Examples

He stank of booze and was a total cuntbreath.
