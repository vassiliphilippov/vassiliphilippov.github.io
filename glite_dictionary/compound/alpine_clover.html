alpine clover

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn ˈkloʊvər / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
A European clover plant found in mountainous regions, typically with fragrant pink flowers.
mountain clover with pink flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with delicate alpine clover blooming in the early summer.
Botanists studied the fragrance of the alpine clover found at high elevations.
Many hikers admire the bright pink flowers of the alpine clover along mountain trails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clover - a small plant with trifoliate leaves and dense flower heads, often white, pink, or red
