tennis player

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays tennis, especially as a sport or profession
person who plays tennis

Examples

Serena Williams is a famous tennis player known for her powerful serves.
Many young tennis players dream of winning Wimbledon one day.
He started training as a tennis player when he was just six years old.

Related Terms
