speccy glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛksi ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈspɛksi ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Spectacles that are stylish or fashionable, often worn as a fashion statement.
fashionable spectacles
informal british

Examples

He wore his speccy glasses to the party and got several compliments.
Her new speccy glasses gave her outfit a quirky edge.
Many teenagers now choose speccy glasses even if they don't need prescription lenses.

Synset Examples

He wore his speccy glasses with pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glasses - a pair of lenses for correcting or assisting defective eyesight

Etymology

From informal 'speccy' (diminutive of 'spectacles') + 'glasses'.
