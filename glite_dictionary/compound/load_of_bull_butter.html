load of bull butter

1. NOUN

/ loʊd əv bʊl ˌbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ləʊd əv bʊl ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Something that is nonsense or untrue; lies.
nonsense; lies (informal/slang)
informal slang

Examples

Everything he said about winning the lottery was just a load of bull butter.
She rolled her eyes and called his story a load of bull butter.
Don't listen to him—his explanation is a load of bull butter.

Synset Examples

His excuse was just a load of bull butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - nonsense; lies; very informal or rude variant
