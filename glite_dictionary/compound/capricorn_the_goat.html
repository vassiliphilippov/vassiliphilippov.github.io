Capricorn the Goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæprɪkɔrn ðə ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈkæprɪkɔːn ðə ɡəʊt / (BrE)
the tenth sign of the zodiac, represented by a goat, which the sun enters approximately from December 22 to January 19
the tenth zodiac sign (the Goat)

Examples

Born on January 5, she is a proud Capricorn the Goat and believes in horoscopes.
Many people consult their Capricorn the Goat sign for guidance at the start of the year.
The symbol of Capricorn the Goat appears frequently in astrology books and charts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Capricorn - the tenth sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Capricornus' (goat horn), denoting the constellation and zodiac sign.
