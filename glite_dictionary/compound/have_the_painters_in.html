to have the painters in

1. VERB

/ hæv ðə ˈpeɪntərz ɪn / (AmE), / hæv ðə ˈpeɪntəz ɪn / (BrE)
to employ or invite professional painters to work in one's home or premises
employ professional painters for home/work

Examples

We're planning to have the painters in next week to redecorate the living room.
Whenever they have the painters in, the whole house smells of fresh paint for days.
It's best to have the painters in before you move your furniture back in.

Synset Examples

It's time to have the painters in and brighten the rooms.
