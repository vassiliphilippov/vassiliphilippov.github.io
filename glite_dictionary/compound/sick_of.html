sick of

1. ADJ

/ sɪk əv / (AmE), / sɪk ɒv / (BrE)
Extremely annoyed or bored by something because you have experienced too much of it.
fed up or annoyed due to excess

Examples

She was sick of hearing the same excuses every day at work.
After weeks of rain, everyone grew sick of the gloomy weather.
I am sick of arguing about the same old issues over and over.

Synset Examples

grew more and more disgusted
fed up with their complaints
sick of it all
sick to death of flattery
gossip that makes one sick
tired of the noise and smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fed up - bored, annoyed, or dissatisfied, especially after a long period
  • Synonym: tired of - bored or annoyed by repeated experience
