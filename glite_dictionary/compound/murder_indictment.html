murder indictment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdɚ ɪnˈdaɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə ɪnˈdaɪtmənt / (BrE)
a formal legal charge accusing someone of the crime of murder
formal accusation of murder (legal)
legal

Examples

The prosecutor announced that a murder indictment had been filed against the suspect.
After months of investigation, the grand jury handed down a murder indictment.
He pleaded not guilty to the murder indictment during the court hearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indictment - a formal charge or accusation of a serious crime
