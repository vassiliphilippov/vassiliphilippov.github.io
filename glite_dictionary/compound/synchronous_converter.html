synchronous converter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs kənˈvɝ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs kənˈvɜː.tə / (BrE)
an electrical machine that converts alternating current to direct current, or vice versa, using synchronous operation
electrical machine converting AC to DC or DC to AC

Examples

The railway station used a synchronous converter to supply power to its electric trains.
Engineers replaced the old synchronous converter with a modern solid-state rectifier.
A single synchronous converter can efficiently deliver direct current for industrial processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotary converter - an electrical machine that converts AC to DC or DC to AC
