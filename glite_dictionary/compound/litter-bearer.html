litter-bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬ɚ ˌbɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə ˌbeərə / (BrE)
a person who helps carry a stretcher, especially in a military or emergency situation
person who carries a stretcher

Examples

During the battle, the litter-bearer rushed to evacuate the wounded soldier from the front line.
A well-trained litter-bearer is crucial in disaster response teams for safely moving injured people.
As a litter-bearer, he had to carry stretchers long distances over rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stretcher-bearer - person who carries a stretcher, especially in emergencies or war

Etymology

From 'litter' meaning a stretcher or portable bed, and 'bearer' meaning one who carries.
