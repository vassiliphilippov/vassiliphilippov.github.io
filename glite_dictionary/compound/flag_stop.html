flag stop

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ stɑp / (AmE), / flæɡ stɒp / (BrE)
A railway station where trains stop only if signaled by a passenger or station agent, or a small station between main stops.
railway station where trains stop only on signal

Examples

The train will only halt at the flag stop if someone signals the conductor.
We waited on the platform of the small flag stop for the afternoon train.
Some rural communities are served solely by a flag stop station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: request stop - a stop where a bus or train halts only if requested
