Red Delicious

1. NOUN

/ rɛd dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / rɛd dɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
A variety of apple with bright red skin, known for its sweet flavor and widespread cultivation.
sweet, bright red apple variety

Examples

She brought a shiny Red Delicious from the market for her lunch.
Many people prefer the crisp texture of a Red Delicious over other apple varieties.
The orchard specializes in growing Red Delicious apples for supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Golden Delicious - another widely grown apple variety, yellow-skinned and sweet
