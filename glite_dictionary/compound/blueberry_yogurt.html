blueberry yogurt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌbɛri ˈjoʊɡɚt / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri ˈjɒɡət / (BrE)
A type of yogurt that contains sweetened blueberries or blueberry jam.
yogurt with blueberries or blueberry jam

Examples

She had a bowl of blueberry yogurt for breakfast this morning.
The store was out of blueberry yogurt, so I picked up peach instead.
Children often enjoy the sweet flavor of blueberry yogurt as a healthy snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry yogurt - yogurt with strawberries or strawberry jam
