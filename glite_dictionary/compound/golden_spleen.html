golden spleen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈspliːn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈspliːn / (BrE)
Any of various low-growing aquatic herbs belonging to the genus Chrysosplenium, typically found in moist habitats.
low aquatic herb, genus Chrysosplenium

Examples

The stream banks were dotted with golden spleen thriving among the moss.
Researchers studied the water filtration effect of golden spleen in wetland areas.
She identified the plant as a species of golden spleen, native to this region.
