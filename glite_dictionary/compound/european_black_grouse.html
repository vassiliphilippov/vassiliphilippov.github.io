European black grouse

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən blæk ɡraʊs / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən blæk ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a large northern European grouse species, black in color, with a distinctive lyre-shaped tail
a northern European grouse with a lyre-shaped tail

Examples

The European black grouse is known for its dramatic mating display during spring.
Wildlife researchers observed several European black grouse in the Scottish Highlands.
The distinctive tail feathers help identify the male European black grouse in the field.
