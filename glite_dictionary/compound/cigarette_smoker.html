cigarette smoker

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt ˈsmoʊkər / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡəˈrɛt ˈsməʊkə / (BrE)
a person who habitually smokes cigarettes
person who smokes cigarettes

Examples

My grandfather was a cigarette smoker for over forty years before he quit.
The designated area outside the building is reserved for cigarette smokers.
Health warnings are designed to deter young people from becoming cigarette smokers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoker - a person who smokes tobacco or other substances
