pastry cook

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪstri kʊk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstri kʊk / (BrE)
A chef who specializes in making pastries and desserts.
chef specializing in pastries and desserts

Examples

The hotel hired a new pastry cook to improve their dessert menu.
As a skilled pastry cook, she can create stunning wedding cakes from scratch.
The pastry cook arrived early to prepare croissants for the breakfast rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry chef - chef specializing in pastry and desserts
