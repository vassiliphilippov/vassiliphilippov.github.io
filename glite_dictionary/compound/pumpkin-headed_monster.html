pumpkin-headed monster

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmpkɪn ˌhɛdɪd ˈmɑnstər / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpkɪn ˌhɛdɪd ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a fictional creature, especially in stories or folklore, depicted as a monster with a pumpkin-shaped head
fictional monster with a pumpkin-like head

Examples

The children dressed up as a pumpkin-headed monster for Halloween.
In the story, the pumpkin-headed monster guarded the haunted house.
Many decorations feature a smiling pumpkin-headed monster during autumn festivals.

Synset Examples

The kids thought the pumpkin-headed monster was funny, not scary.
