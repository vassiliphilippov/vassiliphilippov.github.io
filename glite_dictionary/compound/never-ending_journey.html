never-ending journey

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɛvɚˈɛndɪŋ ˈdʒɝːni / (AmE), / ˌnɛvəˈɛndɪŋ ˈdʒɜːni / (BrE)
an experience or process that appears to continue without conclusion or end
an experience with no apparent end

Examples

Their search for answers became a never-ending journey as new questions kept arising.
Writing a novel can sometimes feel like a never-ending journey filled with challenges and revisions.
After hours on the highway, the road trip seemed like a never-ending journey to the children.

Synset Examples

Driving through the flatland felt like a never-ending journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endless journey - a journey that goes on forever
