total fuckmuppet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈfʌkˌmʌp.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈfʌkˌmʌp.ɪt / (BrE)
A vulgar and highly derogatory slang term for a person regarded as extremely foolish or incompetent.
extremely foolish or incompetent person (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

I can't believe you let that happen, you total fuckmuppet!
After breaking the printer for the third time, he looked like a total fuckmuppet.
Don't be such a total fuckmuppet when you're presenting to clients.

Synset Examples

He's acting like a total fuckmuppet in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: moron - a very foolish or stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability
