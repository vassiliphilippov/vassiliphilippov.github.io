tablet-armed chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæblət ɑrmd tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈtæblət ɑːmd tʃeə / (BrE)
a chair with one arm extended and flattened to serve as a writing surface, commonly used in classrooms or auditoriums
chair with writing arm

Examples

The classroom was filled with tablet-armed chairs arranged in neat rows.
She placed her laptop on the tablet-armed chair and started taking notes.
We replaced the old desks with modern tablet-armed chairs for better flexibility.
