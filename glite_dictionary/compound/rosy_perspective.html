rosy perspective

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzi pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
an outlook or attitude that is overly optimistic or positive, often disregarding potential difficulties
overly optimistic outlook

Examples

Even in difficult times, he always seemed to have a rosy perspective on life.
Don't let your rosy perspective blind you to the real challenges ahead.
Her rosy perspective can sometimes make her underestimate potential risks.

Synset Examples

With a rosy perspective, she faced her challenges head-on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimistic - hopeful and confident about the future
  • Antonym: pessimistic - expecting the worst or seeing the negative side

Etymology

From 'rosy', meaning optimistic or hopeful, combined with 'perspective'.
