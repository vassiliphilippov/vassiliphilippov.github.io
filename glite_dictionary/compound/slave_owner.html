slave owner

1. NOUN

/ sleɪv ˈoʊnər / (AmE), / sleɪv ˈəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who owns and holds another person as a slave
person who owns slaves

Examples

The plantation's slave owner imposed harsh rules and punishments on those he enslaved.
Historians have documented the lives of many slave owners in the southern United States.
She inherited the estate from her father, who was a notorious slave owner in the 1800s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slaveholder - a person who holds slaves
