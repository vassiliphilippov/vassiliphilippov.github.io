sweet taffy

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈtæfi / (AmE), / swiːt ˈtæfi / (BrE)
a type of chewy candy made from sugar, flavorings, and sometimes butter, often pulled and stretched during its preparation
a chewy, pulled sugar candy

Examples

The beachside shop sold brightly colored sweet taffy in dozens of flavors.
She handed out pieces of sweet taffy to the children after the parade.
I always get sweet taffy when I visit the amusement park.

Synset Examples

He chewed on a piece of sweet taffy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taffy - a chewy candy made from boiled sugar and flavorings
