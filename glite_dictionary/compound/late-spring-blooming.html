late-spring-blooming

1. ADJ

/ leɪt sprɪŋ ˈbluː.mɪŋ / (AmE), / leɪt sprɪŋ ˈbluː.mɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming in the latter part of the spring season.
flowering in late spring

Examples

These late-spring-blooming roses add vibrant color to the garden when earlier flowers have faded.
Gardeners often look forward to the arrival of late-spring-blooming perennials for a fresh burst of color.
The tulips in that section are late-spring-blooming, appearing several weeks after the crocuses.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: early-spring-blooming - (of a plant) blooming in the early part of spring
