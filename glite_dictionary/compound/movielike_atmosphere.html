movielike atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuviˌlaɪk ˈætməˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌlaɪk ˈatməˌsfɪə / (BrE)
an atmosphere that is reminiscent of or similar to that found in movies, often evoking a sense of drama, glamour, or unreality
an atmosphere resembling that of a movie

Examples

The rooftop dinner had a movielike atmosphere that left guests enchanted.
The lighting and music created a movielike atmosphere in the small café.
Her surprise arrival gave the event a sudden movielike atmosphere everyone noticed.

Synset Examples

The party had a movielike atmosphere with its decorations.
