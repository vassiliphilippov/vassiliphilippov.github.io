hunter's chicken

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɚz ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntəz ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
A chicken casserole cooked with tomatoes, mushrooms, and herbs in the Italian style, similar to chicken cacciatore.
chicken dish with tomatoes and mushrooms

Examples

The restaurant's special tonight is hunter's chicken served with crusty bread.
She learned to make hunter's chicken after traveling through Italy last summer.
A hearty hunter's chicken is perfect for a cozy family dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken cacciatore - Italian chicken stew with tomatoes and herbs

Etymology

From the Italian dish ‘pollo alla cacciatora’, meaning ‘hunter-style chicken’.
