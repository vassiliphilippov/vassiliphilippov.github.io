urgent attention

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrdʒənt əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈɜːdʒənt əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
immediate focus, action, or care due to the seriousness or importance of a situation
immediate focus or care due to urgency

Examples

The matter requires urgent attention to prevent further damage.
We need to give urgent attention to the safety issues raised in the report.
Broken equipment often goes unrepaired unless it receives urgent attention.

Synset Examples

The plumbing in this building needs urgent attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediate attention - attention or action given at once, without delay
  • Antonym: routine attention - regular care or maintenance without urgency
