alkaline-loving

1. ADJ

/ ˈæl.kəˌlaɪn ˌlʌv.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæl.kəˌlaɪn ˌlʌv.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Thriving or flourishing in alkaline environments, especially used of plants that require a soil pH above 7.
thriving in alkaline environments

Examples

Many alkaline-loving plants thrive in regions with limestone-based soils.
Gardeners should check the pH level before planting alkaline-loving species.
The nursery specializes in alkaline-loving shrubs and perennials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calcicole - a plant that thrives in alkaline soils
  • Antonym: acid-loving - thriving in acidic environments (pH below 7)
