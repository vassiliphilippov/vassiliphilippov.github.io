push button

1. NOUN

/ pʊʃ ˈbʌtən / (AmE), / pʊʃ ˈbʌtən / (BrE)
A small switch operated by pressing it with a finger, used to control a device electrically.
electrical switch operated by pressing

Examples

Please press the push button to start the machine.
The new coffee maker has a push button for quick brewing.
Every floor in the building can be selected using a push button in the elevator.

Synset Examples

the elevator was operated by push buttons
the push beside the bed operated a buzzer at the desk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
