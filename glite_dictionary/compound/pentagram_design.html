pentagram design

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn.təˌɡræm dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.təˌɡræm dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a decorative arrangement or depiction of a five-pointed star, often symbolizing mystical or cultural significance
decorative five-pointed star motif

Examples

The ancient amulet was engraved with an intricate pentagram design.
She chose a silver ring with a pentagram design for its symbolic meaning.
The tattoo artist specializes in mystical symbols, including the pentagram design.
