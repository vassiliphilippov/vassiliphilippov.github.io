good-for-naught

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd fə nɔt / (AmE), / ɡʊd fə nɔːt / (BrE)
Having little or no value; worthless or useless.
worthless; of no use

Examples

Many considered the abandoned house a good-for-naught structure that should be torn down.
She refused to waste any more time on his good-for-naught promises.
The old lawnmower proved completely good-for-naught after another unsuccessful attempt to start it.

Synset Examples

a sorry horse
a sorry excuse
a lazy no-count, good-for-nothing goldbrick
the car was a no-good piece of junk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-for-nothing - worthless or useless person or thing
