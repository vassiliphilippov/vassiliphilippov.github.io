glass cutter

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæs ˈkʌt̬ər / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut or engrave designs on glass surfaces
person who cuts or engraves glass

Examples

The skilled glass cutter created intricate floral patterns on the vase.
She apprenticed for years before becoming a master glass cutter.
The museum is exhibiting works by a renowned glass cutter from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraver - person who engraves designs on materials

/ ɡlæs ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs ˈkʌtə / (BrE)

tool for cutting glass

Examples

The professional used a glass cutter to neatly split the pane in two.
Always wear gloves when operating a glass cutter to avoid injuries.
She bought a new glass cutter for her stained glass hobby.
