to seem fishy

1. VERB

/ siːm ˈfɪʃi / (AmE), / siːm ˈfɪʃi / (BrE)
to appear suspicious or not completely honest
appear suspicious or dishonest
informal

Examples

His explanation seemed fishy to the police, so they continued to question him.
If something seems fishy, it's best to double-check the details before agreeing.
At first, the job offer seemed fishy, but I decided to investigate further.

Synset Examples

The deal seemed fishy, so I declined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seem suspicious - to give the impression of being dishonest or questionable
