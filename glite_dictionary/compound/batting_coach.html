batting coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætɪŋ ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who trains and advises players on how to improve their batting skills, especially in baseball or cricket
person who teaches batting skills

Examples

The team hired a new batting coach to help improve their offense.
After working with the batting coach, her hitting average increased significantly.
The batting coach offered valuable advice on stance and timing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains athletes or teams
  • Synonym: batting instructor - person who provides specialized batting training
