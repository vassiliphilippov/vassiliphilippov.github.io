rail line

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈreɪl laɪn / (BrE)
a set of railroad tracks and their supporting roadbed forming a route for trains
route consisting of railroad tracks

Examples

The new rail line will connect the city center with the airport.
Maintenance crews repaired a damaged section of the rail line after the storm.
This region's economy grew rapidly after the construction of the first rail line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway - route or system of railroad tracks and trains
  • Synonym: track - the pair of rails on which trains run
