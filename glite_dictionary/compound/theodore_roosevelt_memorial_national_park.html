Theodore Roosevelt Memorial National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈθiːədɔr ˈroʊzəˌvɛlt məˈmɔriəl næʃəˌnæl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈθiːədɔː ˈrəʊzəvɛlt mɪˈmɔːriəl ˈnæʃənəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in North Dakota, United States, preserving the landscape around former President Theodore Roosevelt's ranch and dedicated to his memory
national park in North Dakota, USA

Examples

Thousands of visitors each year are drawn to Theodore Roosevelt Memorial National Park for its stunning badlands scenery.
Buffalo roam freely across the prairies of Theodore Roosevelt Memorial National Park.
During their road trip, they stopped to hike in Theodore Roosevelt Memorial National Park.

Etymology

Named after Theodore Roosevelt (1858–1919), 26th President of the United States.
