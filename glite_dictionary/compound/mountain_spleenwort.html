mountain spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈspliːnwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
a small fern found on mountains in eastern North America, belonging to the spleenwort group
a small mountain fern of eastern North America

Examples

We spotted a beautiful mountain spleenwort growing in the rocky crevice.
The mountain spleenwort is well-adapted to cool, shaded mountain slopes.
Botanists study the distribution of mountain spleenwort in eastern forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleenwort - any fern of the genus Asplenium
