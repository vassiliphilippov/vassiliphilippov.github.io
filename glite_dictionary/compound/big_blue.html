Big Blue

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ bluː / (AmE), / bɪɡ bluː / (BrE)
a large and powerful 15,000-pound fragmentation bomb, known for its reliability and destructive capacity; used by the US military as one of the largest conventional bombs, particularly in Afghanistan.
large US conventional bomb used in Afghanistan

Examples

The US military deployed the Big Blue bomb during operations in Afghanistan.
Few conventional weapons match the destructive power of the Big Blue.
News reports described the impact of the Big Blue on enemy positions.
