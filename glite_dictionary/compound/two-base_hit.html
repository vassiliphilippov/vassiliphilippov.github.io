two-base hit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuˌbeɪs hɪt / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌbeɪs hɪt / (BrE)
A base hit in baseball on which the batter safely reaches second base.
base hit reaching second base (baseball)

Examples

He celebrated after hitting a two-base hit in the fourth inning.
The team needed a two-base hit to tie the score in the last inning.
She watched as her brother smacked a two-base hit into left field.

Synset Examples

he hit a double to deep centerfield

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a hit allowing the batter to reach second base (baseball)
