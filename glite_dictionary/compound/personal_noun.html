personal noun

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl naʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl naʊn / (BrE)
A noun that refers to a person, such as an occupation or role.
noun denoting a person

Examples

In the phrase 'English teacher', 'teacher' is the personal noun referring to a person.
'Artist' is a personal noun because it denotes someone who creates art.
When forming job titles in English, a personal noun is often used, such as 'manager' or 'consultant'.

Synset Examples

The fact that ‘baroque flautist’ is the personal noun form can be deduced from the fact that ‘flautist’ is the personal noun form of the head noun ‘flute’.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proper noun - A noun that names a specific individual, place, or organization
  • Antonym: common noun - A noun that is not the name of a specific individual or place
