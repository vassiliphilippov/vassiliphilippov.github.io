Department of Homeland Security

1. PROPN

the U.S. federal government department responsible for protecting the country from threats and overseeing homeland security measures
U.S. federal agency for homeland security
american

Examples

The Department of Homeland Security coordinates responses to natural disasters and terrorist threats.
After the attacks of September 11, 2001, the Department of Homeland Security was established to streamline national protection efforts.
Every airline passenger in the United States is subject to security regulations enforced by the Department of Homeland Security.
