impregnable defense

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbl̩ dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
A defense that cannot be overcome, breached, or defeated.
defense impossible to breach or defeat

Examples

The fortress was protected by an impregnable defense that withstood every assault.
He devised an impregnable defense against cyber attacks to safeguard company data.
The team's strategy relied on building an impregnable defense to frustrate their opponents.

Synset Examples

He built an impregnable defense for his online accounts.
The castle's walls were impregnable against any attack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invulnerable defense - a defense that cannot be harmed or damaged
  • Antonym: vulnerable defense - a defense that is easily breached or defeated
