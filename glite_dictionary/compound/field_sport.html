field sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld spɔrt / (AmE), / ˈfiːld spɔːt / (BrE)
a sport that is played outdoors, especially on a field, such as football, rugby, or cricket
sport played outdoors

Examples

Hockey is a popular field sport in many countries.
He prefers field sports like soccer and rugby to indoor games.
The park is often used for local field sport tournaments.
