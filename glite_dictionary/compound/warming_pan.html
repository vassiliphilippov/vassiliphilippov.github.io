warming pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrmɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmɪŋ pæn / (BrE)
a long-handled metal pan with a cover, filled with hot coals and used for warming a bed before use
bed-warming pan with hot coals

Examples

Before electric blankets were invented, people often used a warming pan to heat their beds in winter.
The antique warming pan displayed above the fireplace once belonged to my great-grandparents.
After filling the warming pan with hot coals, she slid it beneath the sheets to warm them before bedtime.

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrmɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmɪŋ pæn / (BrE)
a pan designed to keep food warm before serving
pan for keeping food warm

Examples

The soup stayed hot for hours in the warming pan.
He placed the bread in a warming pan to keep it fresh and warm during the meal.
Our restaurant uses a warming pan to ensure all dishes are served at the right temperature.

Synset Examples

He served the dish in a warming pan to keep it hot.
