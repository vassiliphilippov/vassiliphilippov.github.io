party wall

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti wɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti wɔːl / (BrE)
a wall built along the boundary between two properties and shared by the owners of both
shared wall between properties
legal

Examples

The two houses are separated by a party wall that is maintained jointly by the owners.
If you want to make changes to a party wall, you may need your neighbor's permission.
Disputes over the repair of the party wall are resolved according to local building regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundary wall - a wall marking the edge of a property
