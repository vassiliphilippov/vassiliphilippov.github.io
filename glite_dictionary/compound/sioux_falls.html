Sioux Falls

1. PROPN

/ suː ˈfɔlz / (AmE), / suː ˈfɔːlz / (BrE)
the largest city in South Dakota, located in the southeastern part of the state
city in southeastern South Dakota

Examples

Many people visit Sioux Falls each year to see the beautiful parks and the famous waterfalls.
She moved to Sioux Falls for a new job opportunity at the hospital.
The weather in Sioux Falls can be quite harsh during the winter months.

Etymology

Named after the Sioux people and the nearby falls of the Big Sioux River.
