lamestream source

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪmˌstriːm ˈsɔrs / (AmE), / ˈleɪmˌstriːm ˈsɔːs / (BrE)
A mainstream news outlet or media source regarded as dull, unoriginal, or lacking credibility, often used disparagingly.
mainstream media viewed as unoriginal or dull
informal slang

Examples

Many people distrust the lamestream source because they feel it repeats the same stories as everyone else.
He accuses every lamestream source of ignoring real issues.
As journalism changes, fewer young people rely on any lamestream source for their news.

Synset Examples

He prefers alternative news over lamestream sources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainstream media - traditional, widely accepted media outlets
  • Antonym: alternative media - media sources outside mainstream organizations, often with different viewpoints

Etymology

Blend of 'lame' and 'mainstream', plus 'source'.
