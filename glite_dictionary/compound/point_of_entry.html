point of entry

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv ˈɛn.tɹi / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv ˈɛn.tri / (BrE)
an official place, such as an airport or seaport, where people and goods are allowed to enter a country and where they are subject to customs and immigration checks
official location for entry into a country

Examples

The customs officer checked our passports at the point of entry.
Every point of entry into the country has strict health regulations.
Shipping containers are inspected at each point of entry to prevent contraband.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: port of entry - an official entry point (e.g., at a border) where customs and immigration are conducted
