circus acrobat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːkəs ˈækrəˌbæt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəs ˈækrəbæt / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs acrobatic feats as part of a circus show
acrobat performing in a circus

Examples

The circus acrobat amazed the crowd by flipping through the air with ease.
As a child, Emma dreamed of becoming a circus acrobat and performing under the big top.
During the parade, the circus acrobat balanced on a moving horse to entertain the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrobat - a person skilled in performing gymnastic feats
