baseball manager

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ˈmænɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
the person responsible for directing and overseeing the players, strategy, and overall management of a baseball team
coach and leader of a baseball team

Examples

The baseball manager made a crucial decision in the final inning.
After years as a player, he became the team's baseball manager.
The baseball manager called for a pitching change during the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a person who trains and directs athletes or sports competitors
