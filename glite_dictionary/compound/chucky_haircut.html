chucky haircut

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌki ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌki ˈheəkʌt / (BrE)
A haircut that appears uneven, messy, or roughly cut, often lacking professional finish.
uneven or messy haircut
informal

Examples

He was embarrassed by his chucky haircut after trying to trim it himself.
Everyone at school noticed her chucky haircut and asked if she needed help fixing it.
After the dog chewed on his hair, he ended up with a rather chucky haircut.

Synset Examples

His haircut looks a bit chucky, don’t you think?
