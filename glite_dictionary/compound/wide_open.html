wide open

1. ADJ

/ waɪd ˈoʊ.pən / (AmE), / waɪd ˈəʊ.pən / (BrE)
completely open, available, or unrestricted; not limited or obstructed in any way.
completely open or unrestricted

Examples

After the merger, the market was wide open for new competitors to enter.
She left the window wide open, letting in the fresh morning air.
The competition is still wide open with no clear favorite to win.

Synset Examples

The field was wide open for new opportunities.
The door was wide open, inviting the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: closed - not open or accessible
