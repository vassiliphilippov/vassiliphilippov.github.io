en clair

1. ADV

/ ɑ̃ klɛər / (AmE), / ɑ̃ klɛː / (BrE)
in plain language; expressed without any code or ambiguity.
in plain (unencoded) language

Examples

The message was transmitted en clair for everyone to read.
Please explain your findings en clair so that the public can understand.
He summarized the technical report en clair for the committee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plainly - in clear, straightforward language

Etymology

From French 'en clair' meaning 'in clear [text]'.
