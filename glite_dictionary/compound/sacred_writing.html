sacred writing

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪkrɪd ˈraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪkrɪd ˈraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a text or scripture regarded as holy or venerated in religious worship
text considered holy

Examples

The Bible is an example of a sacred writing revered by millions of Christians.
Many religions have their own sacred writing containing spiritual teachings and laws.
He spent years studying the sacred writing of ancient civilizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scripture - a sacred religious text
