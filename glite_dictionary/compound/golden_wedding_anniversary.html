golden wedding anniversary

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌænəˈvɜrsɛri / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌanɪˈvɜːsəri / (BrE)
the 50th anniversary of a wedding, typically celebrated as a major milestone in a marriage
50th wedding anniversary

Examples

We are planning a big party for our grandparents' golden wedding anniversary next month.
The couple received a letter from the Queen on their golden wedding anniversary.
Celebrating a golden wedding anniversary is a rare and joyful occasion.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: silver wedding anniversary - the 25th wedding anniversary
  • Degree Higher: diamond wedding anniversary - the 60th wedding anniversary

Etymology

From the tradition of associating precious metals with significant wedding anniversaries; 'golden' representing 50 years.
