roast pork

1. NOUN

/ roʊst pɔrk / (AmE), / rəʊst pɔːk / (BrE)
Pork prepared and cooked by roasting, often served as a main dish.
pork cooked by roasting

Examples

She served roast pork with applesauce for Sunday dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its crispy roast pork and rich gravy.
After hours in the oven, the roast pork was tender and flavorful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roast beef - beef prepared and cooked by roasting
  • Synonym: roast chicken - chicken prepared and cooked by roasting
