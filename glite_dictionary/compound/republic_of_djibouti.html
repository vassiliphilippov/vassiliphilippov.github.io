Republic of Djibouti

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv dʒɪˈbuːti / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv dʒɪˈbuːti / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Africa located on the Somali Peninsula, formerly under French control and independent since 1977.
country in northeastern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Djibouti is strategically located near the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden.
She traveled to the Republic of Djibouti for a conference on regional security.
Since its independence, the Republic of Djibouti has played a vital role in East African affairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Djibouti - the short name for the Republic of Djibouti, a country in northeastern Africa
