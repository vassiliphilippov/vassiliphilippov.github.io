cactus euphorbia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæktəs juːˈfɔːrbiə / (AmE), / ˈkæktəs juːˈfɔːbɪə / (BrE)
a small tree native to southern Africa, with upright, angled branches resembling a candelabrum
southern African tree with upright, angled branches

Examples

The cactus euphorbia is often seen dotting the arid landscapes of southern Africa.
Some gardeners cultivate cactus euphorbia for its striking, sculptural appearance.
Unlike true cacti, the cactus euphorbia is native to the Old World.
