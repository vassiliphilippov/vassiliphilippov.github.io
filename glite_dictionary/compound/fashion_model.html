fashion model

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ(ə)n ˈmɒd(ə)l / (BrE)
A person, typically a woman, employed to wear and display clothing and accessories for designers, photographers, or fashion shows.
person who displays clothing for fashion industry

Examples

She started her career as a fashion model in Milan when she was just eighteen.
The designer hired a fashion model to showcase the new spring collection.
Many people think being a fashion model is glamorous, but it also requires a lot of hard work.

Synset Examples

she was too fat to be a mannequin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - person employed to display clothes or pose for artists and photographers
