bottling plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬lɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a factory where beverages are transferred into bottles and sealed for distribution
factory where beverages are bottled

Examples

The new bottling plant increased production by 50 percent last year.
Tour groups often visit the bottling plant to learn about the beverage packaging process.
After the drink was formulated, it was shipped to the bottling plant for packaging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brewery - factory where beer is brewed and bottled
  • Synonym: bottling line - the machinery or assembly used for filling and capping bottles
