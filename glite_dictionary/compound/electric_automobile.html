electric automobile

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˌɔtəmoʊˈbil / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˌɔːtəməˈbiːl / (BrE)
A car that uses electricity stored in batteries as its main source of power instead of gasoline or diesel.
car powered by electricity

Examples

She decided to buy an electric automobile to reduce her carbon footprint.
Charging an electric automobile at home is convenient for many city dwellers.
The government offers incentives to encourage consumers to purchase an electric automobile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric car - a car powered by electricity
