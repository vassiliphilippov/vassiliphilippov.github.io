leather soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚ ˌsoʊp / (AmE), / ˈlɛðə ˌsəʊp / (BrE)
a mild soap specially formulated for cleansing and conditioning leather articles such as shoes, saddles, or upholstery
soap for cleaning and conditioning leather

Examples

Apply leather soap to your boots to keep the leather soft and clean.
After a muddy ride, I used leather soap on my saddle to remove dirt and condition the material.
Regular treatment with leather soap can help prevent cracking and aging of leather furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saddle soap - a cleaning and conditioning soap for leather, especially saddles
