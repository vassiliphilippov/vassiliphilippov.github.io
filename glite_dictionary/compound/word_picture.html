word picture

1. NOUN

/ wɝd ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a vivid or graphic verbal description that creates a clear image in the reader's or listener's mind
vivid verbal description

Examples

The novelist painted a word picture of the bustling city streets at dawn.
Through her speech, she created a word picture of hope and resilience.
His description of the countryside was such a vivid word picture that I felt I was there.

Synset Examples

too often the narrative was interrupted by long word pictures
the author gives a depressing picture of life in Poland
the pamphlet contained brief characterizations of famous Vermonters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: word portrait - a detailed written or spoken description of a person or scene
