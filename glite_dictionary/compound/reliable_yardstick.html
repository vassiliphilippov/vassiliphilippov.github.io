reliable yardstick

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl ˈjɑrd.stɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ.ə.bəl ˈjɑːd.stɪk / (BrE)
a dependable standard or measure used to assess progress, quality, or performance
dependable standard for assessment

Examples

Customer satisfaction is often seen as a reliable yardstick for business success.
Test scores are not always the most reliable yardstick of a child's potential.
The committee lacked a reliable yardstick to judge the effectiveness of the new program.

Synset Examples

We need a reliable yardstick to evaluate our progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benchmark - a standard or point of reference for comparison
  • Synonym: yardstick - a standard for making a critical judgment; a criterion
