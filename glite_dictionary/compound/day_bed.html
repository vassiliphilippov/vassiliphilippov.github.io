day bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌbɛd / (BrE)
A sofa or couch that can be used by day and converted into a bed for sleeping, often serving as both seating and a bed.
sofa convertible into a bed

Examples

She bought a day bed for the guest room so it could serve as both a sitting area and a place to sleep.
The day bed in the sunroom is perfect for napping during the afternoon.
We converted the old couch into a day bed to save space in the apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa bed - a sofa that can be converted into a bed
  • Synonym: futon - a mattress or couch that can be used as a bed or sofa, originally Japanese
