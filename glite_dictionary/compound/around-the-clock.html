around-the-clock

1. ADJ

/ əˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɑk / (AmE), / əˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɒk / (BrE)
happening continuously throughout the entire day and night; without stopping
continuously, all day and night

Examples

The hospital provides around-the-clock emergency services to patients.
Due to the storm, cleanup crews were working around-the-clock to restore power.
She appreciated the around-the-clock support offered by the nursing staff.
