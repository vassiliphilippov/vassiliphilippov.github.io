lily pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪli ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈlɪli ˌpad / (BrE)
a flat, round, floating leaf of a water lily plant, typically found on the surface of freshwater ponds and lakes
floating leaf of a water lily

Examples

A frog sat peacefully on a lily pad in the middle of the pond.
Their boat glided past clusters of green lily pads floating on the water's surface.
We watched dragonflies hovering above the lily pads at the edge of the lake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lily - an aquatic plant with large floating leaves and showy flowers
