homophobic attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊməˈfoʊbɪk ˈætɪtud / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈfəʊbɪk ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a negative feeling, belief, or disposition toward LGBTQ+ people, stemming from prejudice or fear
prejudiced feeling or belief toward LGBTQ+ people

Examples

The principal condemned any homophobic attitude expressed at school assemblies.
A homophobic attitude can lead to discrimination in the workplace.
Media coverage has helped reduce the prevalence of homophobic attitude in recent years.

Synset Examples

The film addresses the consequences of homophobic attitudes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homophobia - prejudice or dislike against LGBTQ+ people
  • Antonym: tolerant attitude - accepting and open-minded disposition toward others

Etymology

From 'homophobic' (from 'homophobia', coined in 1960s) + 'attitude' (from Latin 'aptitudo', fitness).
