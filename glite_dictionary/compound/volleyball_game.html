volleyball game

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑliˌbɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈvɒlibɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played between two teams who use their hands to hit an inflated ball over a high net, aiming to ground it on the opponent's side.
game played with a ball and net between two teams

Examples

Our school hosted an exciting volleyball game last Friday evening.
She sprained her ankle during a friendly volleyball game at the beach.
Several students signed up to participate in the charity volleyball game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volleyball - the sport itself, without specifying an instance of play
