rowan vodka

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.ən ˈvoʊd.kə / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.ən ˈvɒd.kə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic spirit made by infusing rowan berries with natural caramel, wine distillate, and fruit distillates.
vodka infused with rowan berries

Examples

The bartender recommended rowan vodka as a unique aperitif with a fruity note.
She brought a bottle of rowan vodka back from her trip to Russia.
Many traditional recipes pair rowan vodka with hearty, game-based dishes.

Synset Examples

Rowan vodka goes well with game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - A clear distilled alcoholic beverage
