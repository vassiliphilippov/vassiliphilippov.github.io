meat hook

1. NOUN

/ mit hʊk / (AmE), / miːt hʊk / (BrE)
a strong, pointed hook used for hanging carcasses of animals, typically in a butcher's shop or slaughterhouse
hook for hanging animal carcasses

Examples

The butcher carefully hung the beef on a meat hook in the cold storage room.
Stainless steel meat hooks are commonly used in industrial slaughterhouses.
He cautioned new workers to be careful around the sharp meat hook to avoid injuries.
