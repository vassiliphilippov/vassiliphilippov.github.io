constant update

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnstənt ʌpˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt ʌpˌdeɪt / (BrE)
a situation in which software or a system is frequently modified or improved with new changes or versions
frequent software or system changes

Examples

The app became difficult to use because of the constant updates disrupting its layout.
Users often complain about the constant updates required to keep their devices secure.
Thanks to constant updates, the program now has many new features.

Synset Examples

Using Microshaft can be frustrating with constant updates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frequent update - an update that happens regularly or often
