statistical method

1. NOUN

/ stəˈtɪstɪkəl ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)
a procedure or technique used to analyze, interpret, or represent statistical data
procedure for analyzing statistical data

Examples

Researchers chose an appropriate statistical method to analyze the experimental results.
Understanding the limitations of each statistical method is crucial for valid data interpretation.
The professor explained which statistical method would best suit our data set.
