BB gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈbi ˈbi ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˈbiː ɡʌn / (BrE)
A type of air gun that fires small metal balls (BBs) using compressed air or gas.
air gun firing BB pellets

Examples

He received a BB gun for his birthday when he turned twelve.
The store only sells BB guns and other air rifles, not real firearms.
Always wear protective glasses when using a BB gun to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air gun - gun using compressed air to fire projectiles

Etymology

From 'BB', the size of the ammunition (ball bearing), plus 'gun'.
