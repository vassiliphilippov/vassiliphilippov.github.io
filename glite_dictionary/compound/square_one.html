square one

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr wʌn / (AmE), / skwɛə wʌn / (BrE)
the starting point of an activity, especially after a setback or failure that forces one to begin again
the starting point after a setback

Examples

After months of investigation, the team was forced to return to square one.
When her plan failed, she found herself back at square one.
Each time a new obstacle appeared, it felt like going back to square one.

Synset Examples

the police are now back at square one after having arrested and released 27 men
she has tried to diet but always ends up back at square one

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starting point - the beginning stage or position of an activity
  • Synonym: ground zero - the beginning or most basic level of something
