rabid enthusiasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪd ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ˈræbɪd ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
extreme or fanatical enthusiasm, often to an excessive or irrational degree
extreme or fanatical enthusiasm

Examples

The fans displayed rabid enthusiasm throughout the concert, cheering and singing along to every song.
His rabid enthusiasm for the new project made the whole team more motivated to succeed.
Social media users often show rabid enthusiasm for their favorite celebrities, defending them passionately online.

Synset Examples

She had a rabid enthusiasm for collecting rare coins.
