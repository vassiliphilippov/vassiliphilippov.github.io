age bracket

1. NOUN

/ eɪdʒ ˈbrækət / (AmE), / eɪdʒ ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
a range of ages considered as a group, typically for statistical or demographic purposes
group of people in a specific age range

Examples

Children in the 5-10 age bracket are eligible for free vaccinations.
Most of the participants fell within the 30-40 age bracket.
The survey results were analyzed by age bracket to identify trends among different generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: age group - group of people categorized by age
