tricky situation

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪki sɪˈtʃueɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtrɪki sɪtjʊˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a circumstance that is difficult, complicated, or awkward to deal with
complicated or difficult circumstance

Examples

He found himself in a tricky situation when his two friends argued and asked him to take sides.
Negotiating the contract turned into a tricky situation once both companies demanded more concessions.
Whenever there's a tricky situation, she manages to keep calm and solve it efficiently.

Synset Examples

It’s a tricky situation, as the actress said to the bishop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: difficult situation - a situation that is hard to handle
  • Synonym: predicament - a difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation
