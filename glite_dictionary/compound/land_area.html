land area

1. NOUN

/ lænd ˈɛəriə / (AmE), / lænd ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a portion of land used for a particular purpose, such as building, farming, or development
portion of land used for a purpose

Examples

The developer purchased a land area near the city center for a new housing project.
Local farmers are protesting the reduction of land area available for agriculture.
The company measured the total land area needed for its new warehouse complex.

Synset Examples

he wanted some acreage to build on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot - a small piece of land for a specific purpose, especially building or gardening
  • Synonym: acreage - an area of land measured in acres, often used for farming or building
