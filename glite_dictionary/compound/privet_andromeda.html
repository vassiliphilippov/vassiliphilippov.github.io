privet andromeda

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪvət ænˈdrɑːmɪdə / (AmE), / ˈprɪvɪt ænˈdrɒmɪdə / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub with many branches and dense, soft clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers.
deciduous shrub with white bell flowers

Examples

The privet andromeda blooms in early spring, brightening gardens with its delicate flowers.
We planted a privet andromeda near the fence for added privacy and beauty.
Gardeners appreciate privet andromeda for its hardy nature and attractive white blossoms.
