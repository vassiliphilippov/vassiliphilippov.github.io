drugs bust

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡz bʌst / (AmE), / drʌɡz bʌst / (BrE)
an incident in which the police seize illegal drugs, often as part of a raid or investigation
police seizure of illegal drugs

Examples

The police carried out a major drugs bust at the abandoned warehouse last night.
Several people were arrested during the drugs bust in the city center.
The newspaper reported on the largest drugs bust in the region's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug raid - a police operation to search for and seize illegal drugs
