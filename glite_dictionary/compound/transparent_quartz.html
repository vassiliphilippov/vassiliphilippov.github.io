transparent quartz

1. NOUN

/ trænˈspɛr.ənt kwɔrts / (AmE), / trænˈspær.ənt kwɔːts / (BrE)
a clear variety of quartz used in the manufacture of electronic and optical equipment
clear quartz for electronics and optics

Examples

The laboratory ordered several rods of transparent quartz for their new laser system.
Lenses made from transparent quartz offer high resistance to thermal shock.
They inspected a flawless piece of transparent quartz before it was cut for an optical instrument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rock crystal - clear, colorless variety of quartz
