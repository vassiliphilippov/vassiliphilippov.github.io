Gothmas celebration

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑθ.məs ˌsɛl.əˈbreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθ.məs ˌsɛl.ɪˈbreɪ.ʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A holiday celebration or party with a Gothic theme, often featuring dark aesthetics and traditions associated with the Gothic subculture.
Gothic-themed holiday party or gathering

Examples

Our friends host a Gothmas celebration every December, complete with eerie decorations and midnight feasts.
The annual Gothmas celebration brings together fans of Gothic music and fashion.
At this year's Gothmas celebration, attendees exchanged handmade gifts inspired by classic horror stories.

Synset Examples

They exchanged spooky gifts during their Gothmas celebration.
She wore a black dress to the Gothmas party.

Etymology

Blend of 'Goth' (relating to the Gothic subculture) and 'Christmas', modeling alternative holiday names.
