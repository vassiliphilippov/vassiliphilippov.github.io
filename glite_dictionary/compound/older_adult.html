older adult

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊldər əˈdʌlt / (AmE), / ˈəʊldə əˈdʌlt / (BrE)
a person who is advanced in age, typically considered to be in the later years of life; an elderly person
an elderly person

Examples

Many older adults prefer to live independently for as long as possible.
The program offers free health screenings specifically for older adults.
Social isolation can have a significant impact on the well-being of older adults.

Synset Examples

She volunteers to help older adults in her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elderly - a person who is old or advanced in age
