combat injury

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmˌbæt ˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
an injury sustained by military personnel as a direct result of combat or active military operations
injury caused by military combat

Examples

The soldier was evacuated after suffering a severe combat injury to his leg.
Advances in medical treatment have improved survival rates for those with combat injuries.
He was awarded a medal for bravery following the combat injury he sustained during the mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battle wound - an injury received during battle
  • Degree Lower: injury - physical harm or damage to someone's body
