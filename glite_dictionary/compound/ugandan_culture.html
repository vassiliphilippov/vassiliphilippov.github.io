Ugandan culture

1. NOUN

/ juːˈɡændən ˈkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / juːˈɡændən ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the customs, beliefs, social practices, and traditions characteristic of the people of Uganda
customs and traditions of Uganda

Examples

Tourists are often fascinated by the music and dance found in Ugandan culture.
Many aspects of Ugandan culture reflect a blend of indigenous and colonial influences.
She studied Ugandan culture to better understand the country's social structure.

Synset Examples

The Ugandan culture is vibrant and full of traditions.
