change of mind

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv maɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv maɪnd / (BrE)
a decision to reverse a previous opinion or choice
reversal of an earlier decision

Examples

After much thought, she had a change of mind and decided to accept the offer.
It's not uncommon for people to experience a change of mind after hearing new evidence.
The company's sudden change of mind surprised many of its employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconsideration - the process of considering something again, possibly changing a decision
  • Synonym: U-turn - an abrupt or complete reversal of policy or opinion
