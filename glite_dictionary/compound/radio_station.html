radio station

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪəʊ ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where programs are produced and transmitted for broadcast over AM or FM radio frequencies.
station producing radio broadcasts

Examples

She tunes in to her favorite radio station every morning during her commute.
The radio station hosted a live concert for local bands on Friday night.
Many communities rely on their local radio station for news and emergency updates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcasting station - facility transmitting radio or television programs
  • Antonym: television station - station for producing and transmitting television broadcasts
