savings and loan association

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd loʊn əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋz ənd ləʊn əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a financial institution that accepts savings deposits and is primarily engaged in making mortgage loans, especially for residential property
type of financial institution specializing in home mortgages

Examples

The local savings and loan association helped many families buy their first homes.
Regulations require a savings and loan association to maintain a high percentage of home mortgage loans.
She opened an account at the savings and loan association downtown to take advantage of their competitive interest rates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: building society - a similar type of institution in the UK, mainly for savings and mortgages
