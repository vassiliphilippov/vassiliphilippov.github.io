balanced diet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbælənst ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈbælənst ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet that includes the right proportions of different foods and all the nutrients necessary for good health
diet with all necessary nutrients

Examples

Eating a balanced diet is essential for maintaining overall health and preventing disease.
Children need a balanced diet to support their growth and development.
Nutritionists recommend a balanced diet that includes fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthy diet - a diet promoting health and well-being
