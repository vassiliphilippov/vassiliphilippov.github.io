director of first impressions

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛktɚ əv fɝst ɪmˈprɛʃənz / (AmE), / dəˈrɛktə əv fɜːst ɪmˈprɛʃənz / (BrE)
a person, usually a receptionist or front desk staff member, responsible for greeting visitors and representing an organization favorably upon first contact
person responsible for greeting and representing an organization

Examples

Every client is greeted by our director of first impressions at the front desk.
As the company's new director of first impressions, Matt ensures every visitor feels welcome.
The director of first impressions sets the tone for the entire workplace.

Synset Examples
