high dex

1. ADJ

/ haɪ dɛks / (AmE), / haɪ dɛks / (BrE)
Having a high dexterity attribute, especially in video games or role-playing games, which often improves agility, accuracy, or speed.
high dexterity attribute (gaming)
informal
