sunflower oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ə ɔɪl / (BrE)
Oil obtained from the seeds of the sunflower, commonly used in cooking and food products.
oil from sunflower seeds

Examples

She prefers cooking with sunflower oil because of its light flavor.
A bottle of sunflower oil stood on the kitchen shelf.
Many salad dressings contain sunflower oil as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable oil - edible oil derived from plants, used for cooking
