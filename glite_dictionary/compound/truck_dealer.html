truck dealer

1. NOUN

/ trʌk ˈdiːlər / (AmE), / trʌk ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a business or person that buys and sells trucks, especially as their primary trade
business or person selling and buying trucks

Examples

The company became the largest truck dealer in the region within five years.
He visited a truck dealer to compare prices on used vehicles.
Most truck dealers offer financing options for their commercial customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car dealer - a business or person that buys and sells cars
