crackerass comment

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækərˌæs ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkrækərˌas ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
A silly, ridiculous, or contemptible comment, often with offensive or derogatory connotations relating to race.
ridiculous or derogatory comment (offensive, slang)
offensive slang

Examples

The comedian's crackerass comment offended half the audience immediately.
Her social media posts are full of crackerass comments that make people roll their eyes.
He regretted making that crackerass comment after seeing everyone's reaction.

Synset Examples

The crackerass comments from her pals were hilarious.

Etymology

From 'cracker', a derogatory term for a white person, and 'ass', vulgar intensifier; combined to form a compound insult.
