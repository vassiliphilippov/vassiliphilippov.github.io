bluish-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Grey in color with a slight tinge of blue.
a shade of grey with blue tint

Examples

The sky was a bluish-grey just before sunrise, hinting at the coming storm.
Her dress matched the bluish-grey color of her eyes perfectly.
The artist used a bluish-grey shade to capture the misty morning atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue-grey - A color intermediate between blue and grey
  • Degree Lower: grey - Color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: blue - Color between green and violet in the visible spectrum
