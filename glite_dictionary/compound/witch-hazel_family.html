witch-hazel family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzəl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzəl ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Hamamelidaceae) that includes genera such as Hamamelis, Corylopsis, Fothergilla, Liquidambar, and Parrotia
family of flowering plants (Hamamelidaceae)

Examples

The witch-hazel family consists of trees and shrubs known for their ornamental value.
Several members of the witch-hazel family produce fragrant flowers in early spring.
Liquidambar, commonly called sweetgum, is a genus in the witch-hazel family.
