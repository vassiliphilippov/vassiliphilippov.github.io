fudgy brownie

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌdʒi ˈbraʊni / (AmE), / ˈfʌdʒi ˈbraʊni / (BrE)
a brownie that is dense, soft, and rich, with a moist, almost fudge-like texture
very moist, dense brownie

Examples

These fudgy brownies are so rich and chocolaty that one piece is enough to satisfy my sweet tooth.
She baked a batch of fudgy brownies for the bake sale, and they sold out in minutes.
If you like a gooey dessert, you'll love a fudgy brownie fresh from the oven.

Synset Examples

I prefer fudgy brownies over cake any day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brownie - a small, square piece of rich chocolate cake
  • Antonym: cake-like brownie - a brownie with a lighter, more cake-like texture

Etymology

From 'fudge' (a rich, soft confection) and 'brownie' (a chocolate cake-like dessert).
