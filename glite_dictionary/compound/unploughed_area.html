unploughed area

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈplaʊd ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ʌnˈplaʊd ˈeəriə / (BrE)
an area of land that has not been ploughed or cultivated
land not cultivated or tilled

Examples

Rare wildflowers grew across the unploughed area at the edge of the farm.
The birds nested in an unploughed area away from the fields.
We mapped all remaining unploughed areas for conservation purposes.

Synset Examples

We discovered wildlife thriving in the unploughed areas.
She admired the beauty of the unploughed landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fallow land - land left unplanted for a period to restore its fertility
  • Antonym: ploughed field - a field that has been turned over for cultivation
