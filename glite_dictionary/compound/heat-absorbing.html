heat-absorbing

1. ADJ

/ ˈhiːt əbˌzɔrbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːt əbˌzɔːbɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a chemical reaction or compound) occurring or formed with the absorption of heat.
absorbing heat, usually in reactions

Examples

The mixture underwent a heat-absorbing reaction when the chemicals were combined.
This material has heat-absorbing properties, making it useful for thermal insulation.
Certain heat-absorbing compounds are essential in industrial cooling processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endothermic - (of a reaction or process) accompanied by or requiring heat absorption
