to hear voices

1. VERB

/ hɪr ˈvɔɪsɪz / (AmE), / hɪə ˈvɔɪsɪz / (BrE)
to experience the sensation of hearing sounds or voices that are not actually present, often as a symptom of certain mental health conditions
perceive imaginary voices; hallucinate voices

Examples

The patient began to hear voices shortly after the incident.
People with certain conditions may hear voices even when no one else is present.
She was frightened when she realized she could hear voices nobody else could hear.

Synset Examples

She claimed to hear voices that guided her decisions.
Sometimes I feel like I can hear voices calling me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hallucinate - to perceive things that are not present
