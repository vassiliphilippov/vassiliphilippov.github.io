meteor impact

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdiər ˈɪmpækt / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪə ˈɪmpakt / (BrE)
the event of a meteor colliding with the surface of the Earth or another planet
collision of a meteor with Earth or a planet

Examples

The massive meteor impact that created the crater can be seen from space.
Scientists believe a meteor impact caused the extinction of the dinosaurs.
A recent study examined the environmental effects of a meteor impact in Siberia.

Synset Examples

The museum had a fascinating exhibit on meteor impacts.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: meteorite - a meteor that has landed on the surface of Earth
  • Degree Higher: asteroid impact - collision of an asteroid with Earth or a planet
