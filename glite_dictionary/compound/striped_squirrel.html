striped squirrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪpt ˈskwɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
a small eastern American squirrel with a striped back and cheek pouches, often semiterrestrial in habit
small American squirrel with stripes

Examples

The striped squirrel darted across the garden, its tiny paws carrying a nut to its burrow.
Researchers observed the behavior of the striped squirrel as it filled its cheek pouches with seeds.
A striped squirrel can often be seen scurrying along fallen logs in eastern forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chipmunk - small striped ground squirrel native to North America
