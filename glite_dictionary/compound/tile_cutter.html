tile cutter

1. NOUN

/ taɪl ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / taɪl ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a tool designed for cutting floor or wall tiles to desired sizes and shapes
tool for cutting tiles

Examples

The tile cutter made it easy to get clean, precise edges for the bathroom floor.
Before installing new tiles, make sure you have a sharp tile cutter on hand.
He borrowed a tile cutter from his neighbor to finish the kitchen renovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutter - a tool, machine, or person that cuts something
