skillet corn bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪlɪt kɔrn ˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt kɔːn ˌbrɛd / (BrE)
A type of cornbread, often with ham or bacon bits, cooked in a skillet, giving it a crisp crust and rich flavor.
cornbread with ham or bacon, cooked in a skillet

Examples

She made her famous skillet corn bread for the family reunion picnic.
The skillet corn bread was filled with bacon bits and had a perfectly golden crust.
Nothing goes better with chili than a slice of hot skillet corn bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - A bread made from cornmeal, typically baked or fried
