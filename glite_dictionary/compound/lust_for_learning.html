lust for learning

1. NOUN

/ lʌst fər ˈlɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / lʌst fə ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a strong and intense desire or passion for acquiring knowledge or education
strong desire for learning

Examples

His lust for learning led him to read dozens of books every month.
Children with a natural lust for learning often excel in diverse subjects.
She attributes her success to an unquenchable lust for learning and self-improvement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiosity - a strong desire to know or learn something
