reality challenged

1. ADJ

/ riˈæləti ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / riˈalɪti ˈtʃalɪndʒd / (BrE)
having little awareness of what is practical or realistic; out of touch with reality, often in a humorous or informal sense.
out of touch with reality (informal)
informal

Examples

Some politicians seem completely reality challenged when discussing economic issues.
If you're reality challenged, you might believe any story you hear on the internet.
Her plan for the business was ambitious, but a bit reality challenged.

Synset Examples

He seems a bit reality challenged about his job.
Some of her ideas are a bit reality challenged.
That proposal is definitely reality challenged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delusional - having false or unrealistic beliefs or opinions
  • Antonym: practical - concerned with what is effective or sensible rather than theoretical
