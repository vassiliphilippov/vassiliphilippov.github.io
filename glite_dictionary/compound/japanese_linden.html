Japanese linden

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæn ɪs ˈlɪndən / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæn ɪs ˈlɪndən / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to Japan, valued for its ornamental qualities
medium-sized tree from Japan used ornamentally

Examples

The Japanese linden is often planted along city streets for its pleasing appearance.
Botanists admire the broad leaves of the Japanese linden in the wild.
We chose a Japanese linden for our garden because of its graceful shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linden - a type of tree known for its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers
