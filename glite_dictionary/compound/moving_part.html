moving part

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ pɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ pɑːt / (BrE)
a component in a machine, system, or process that moves or changes position during operation
component of a machine or system that moves

Examples

You should always check each moving part for signs of wear before starting the engine.
Lubrication is essential for every moving part of the clockwork mechanism to function smoothly.
When troubleshooting, focus on the moving part that is making an unusual noise.

Synset Examples

The engine has a moving part that needs lubrication.
The project has one critical moving part to monitor.
Each moving part contributes to the machine's efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanism - a system of parts working together in a machine
  • Antonym: stationary part - a component in a mechanism that does not move
