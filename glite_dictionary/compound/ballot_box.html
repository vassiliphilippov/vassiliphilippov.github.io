ballot box

1. NOUN

/ ˈbælət ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbælət ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a sealed container in which voters place their completed ballots during an election
container for voting ballots

Examples

The seal on the ballot box was broken only after all voting had ended.
Volunteers carried the ballot box to the counting center under police escort.
Each ballot box was clearly labeled with the precinct number to avoid confusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballot - a paper or electronic form used for voting
  • Synonym: polling station - location where people go to vote in an election
