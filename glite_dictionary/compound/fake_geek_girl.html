fake geek girl

1. NOUN

/ feɪk ɡik ɡɜrl / (AmE), / feɪk ɡiːk ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a woman who is accused of pretending to be knowledgeable or genuinely interested in geek culture, especially in contexts such as gaming, comics, or fandom events.
derogatory term for a woman accused of pretending interest in geek culture
offensive informal

Many women find the label fake geek girl both insulting and unfair.
