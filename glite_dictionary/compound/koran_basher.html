Koran basher

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑːn ˌbæʃər / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːn ˌbæʃə / (BrE)
a person who harshly criticizes or attacks the Koran, often in a hostile or disrespectful manner
a person who attacks the Koran

Examples

The politician insisted he was not a Koran basher, but was seeking open dialogue.
Some articles accused the author of being a notorious Koran basher.
It's important to distinguish between thoughtful criticism and the attitude of a Koran basher.

Synset Examples

It's not fair to label him a Koran basher.
He was accused of being a Koran basher during the debate.
The group condemned any Koran basher rhetoric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible basher - a person who aggressively promotes or criticizes the Bible

Etymology

Formed from 'Koran' (the Islamic holy book) and 'basher' (one who attacks or criticizes). Modeled after compounds such as 'Bible basher'.
