spirit of freedom

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪt əv ˈfriːdəm / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt əv ˈfriːdəm / (BrE)
the essential quality, feeling, or atmosphere associated with being free or unconstrained
essence or feeling of being free

Examples

The painting radiates a spirit of freedom that inspires everyone who sees it.
During the celebrations, a spirit of freedom swept through the crowd.
Her poetry captures the spirit of freedom that defined a generation.

Synset Examples

The libertoon artist captures the spirit of freedom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freedom - the state of being free, not constrained or restricted
