office boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɪs bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ɪs bɔɪ / (BrE)
a young male employee in an office who does routine tasks such as running errands and delivering messages
young male office assistant

Examples

The office boy was asked to deliver documents to the accounts department.
They hired an office boy to handle daily errands and help with filing paperwork.
During his summer vacation, Tom worked as an office boy at his father's company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - a person who delivers messages or packages
  • Antonym: office girl - a young female employed to do odd jobs in a business office
