throw pillow

1. NOUN

/ θroʊ ˈpɪloʊ / (AmE), / θrəʊ ˈpɪləʊ / (BrE)
A small decorative cushion, often placed on sofas, chairs, or beds for comfort or ornament.
decorative cushion for furniture

Examples

She bought a new throw pillow to add a splash of color to the sofa.
The throw pillow complemented the bedroom’s décor perfectly.
He arranged the throw pillow and blanket neatly on the armchair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cushion - a soft bag of material used for sitting, kneeling, or leaning on
