cleanup crew

1. NOUN

/ ˈklinˌʌp kru / (AmE), / ˈkliːnʌp kruː / (BrE)
a group of people assigned to clean up a place, especially after an event or accident
group responsible for cleaning up

Examples

The cleanup crew arrived early to prepare the venue for the next event.
After the storm, the city sent a cleanup crew to clear debris from the streets.
The festival organizers hired a special cleanup crew to manage waste and recycling.

Synset Examples

The cleanup crew removed the foreign muck from the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaning crew - group of people whose job is to clean spaces
  • Synonym: janitor - person employed to clean and maintain a building
