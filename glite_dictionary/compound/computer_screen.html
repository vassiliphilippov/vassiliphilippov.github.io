computer screen

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːt̬ɚ skriːn / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə skriːn / (BrE)
A screen used for displaying the output from a computer.
display screen for a computer

Examples

She cleaned her computer screen regularly to remove dust and fingerprints.
The new computer screen is larger and displays colors more vividly.
He adjusted the brightness on his computer screen to reduce eye strain during work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - an apparatus that displays images and text from a computer
