superior epigastric vein

1. NOUN

/ suːˈpɪriər ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk veɪn / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that accompanies the superior epigastric artery, draining blood from the upper part of the anterior abdominal wall
vein accompanying the superior epigastric artery
medical

Examples

The superior epigastric vein drains blood from the upper abdominal wall.
During surgery, the superior epigastric vein must be carefully preserved to maintain blood flow.
The anatomy textbook includes a diagram of the superior epigastric vein and its accompanying artery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inferior epigastric vein - a vein that accompanies the inferior epigastric artery

Etymology

From Latin: 'epigastricus superior' meaning 'upper upon the stomach'.
