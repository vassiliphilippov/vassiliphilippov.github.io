silver sheet facade

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ʃit fəˈsɑd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ʃiːt fəˈsɑːd / (BrE)
the exterior surface of a building made from reflective silver-colored material, often metal sheets, for aesthetic or functional purposes
building exterior made of reflective metal sheets

Examples

The new museum's silver sheet facade gleamed in the afternoon sun, attracting attention from passersby.
Several modern office towers in the city feature a silver sheet facade for a futuristic look.
A silver sheet facade can help reflect sunlight, reducing heat absorption in warm climates.

Synset Examples

The architect designed the building with a silver sheet facade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facade - the principal front of a building
