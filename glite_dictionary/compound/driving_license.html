driving license

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˌlaɪs(ə)ns / (BrE)
an official document permitting a person to drive a motor vehicle on public roads
official permit to drive a vehicle

Examples

You must carry your driving license with you at all times when operating a vehicle.
She finally passed her test and received her driving license last week.
If you lose your driving license, you need to report it to the authorities immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driver's license - official permit to drive a vehicle (American English)
