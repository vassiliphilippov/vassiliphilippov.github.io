transportation company

1. NOUN

/ trænspərˈteɪʃən ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / trænspɔːˈteɪʃən ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that provides transport services for people or goods.
company providing transport services

Examples

She got a job working for a major transportation company in the city.
The transportation company expanded its services to include international shipping.
Many transportation companies are investing in environmentally friendly vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrier - a company that transports goods or passengers
  • Synonym: haulier - a company specializing in transporting goods by road (British English)
