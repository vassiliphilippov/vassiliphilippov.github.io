in the field

1. ADP

/ ɪn ðə fiːld / (AmE), / ɪn ðə fiːld / (BrE)
engaged in practical work or research in a specific profession, area of study, or location outside a central office or laboratory
doing practical work or research in a specific area

Examples

After years of studying, she was excited to finally gain experience in the field.
Engineers often spend days in the field collecting data for their projects.
The biologist observed animal behavior firsthand while working in the field.

Synset Examples

The abortion doctor has years of experience in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on site - at the place where a business or activity happens
