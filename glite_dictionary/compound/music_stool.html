music stool

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk stuːl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk stuːl / (BrE)
a backless, often adjustable seat for musicians, especially used by piano players
adjustable seat for musicians

Examples

He sat on the music stool and adjusted it to the right height before playing.
The old music stool in the corner belonged to my grandmother, who was a pianist.
She bought a new adjustable music stool for her piano lessons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano stool - a stool specifically used when playing the piano
