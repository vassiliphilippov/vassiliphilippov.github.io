heavy machinery

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi məˈʃinəri / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi məˈʃiːnəri / (BrE)
large powerful machines, especially those used in construction, mining, or similar heavy-duty work
large machines used in construction or industry

Examples

The construction crew relied on heavy machinery to excavate the site quickly.
Authorities warned residents to stay clear of the heavy machinery operating near the new bridge.
Transporting heavy machinery requires special permits and careful planning.

Synset Examples

Heavy machinery operates continuously at the building site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipment - tools or machinery needed for a specific purpose
  • Antonym: light machinery - smaller and less powerful machines for lighter tasks
