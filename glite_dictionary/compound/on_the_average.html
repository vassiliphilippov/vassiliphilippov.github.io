on the average

1. ADV

/ ɑn ði ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
typically; as is usually the case when all instances are considered together
typically; usually, considering all cases

Examples

On the average, students finish the exam in about two hours.
Households on the average spend less on transportation than on housing.
On the average, the region receives less than 20 inches of rain per year.

Synset Examples

on average he watches three movies a week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on average - usually, taking the mean value
