pharmaceutical company

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪkəl ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˌfɑːmətˈsuːtɪkəl ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company engaged in the research, development, production, and sale of medicinal drugs
company producing medicinal drugs

Examples

The pharmaceutical company invested millions in developing a new cancer treatment.
Many jobs in the city are tied to the presence of a major pharmaceutical company.
The pharmaceutical company announced a recall of one of its best-selling medicines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug company - a company that manufactures and sells drugs

Etymology

pharmaceutical from Greek pharmakeutikos, and company from Old French compagnie.
