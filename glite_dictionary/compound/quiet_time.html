quiet time

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪət taɪm / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪət taɪm / (BrE)
a period of silence or calm when there is little or no noise or activity, often used for rest, reflection, or relaxation
a period of silence or calm

Examples

After a stressful day, I like to have some quiet time to relax and unwind.
Parents often schedule quiet time in the afternoon so young children can rest.
She closed her office door to enjoy a little quiet time before her next meeting.

Synset Examples

Please go away; I need some quiet time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peace and quiet - a state of calm and lack of disturbance
