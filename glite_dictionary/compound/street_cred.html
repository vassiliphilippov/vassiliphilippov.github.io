street cred

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɹit krɛd / (AmE), / ˈstɹiːt krɛd / (BrE)
Respect or credibility among young, fashionable urban people, especially for authenticity and style.
urban credibility or respect
informal

Examples

Wearing vintage sneakers gave him serious street cred at the party.
The artist's gritty lyrics earned her street cred with fans across the city.
He lost some street cred when he started driving a luxury car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credibility - the quality of being trusted and believed in

Etymology

Shortened from 'street credibility'.
