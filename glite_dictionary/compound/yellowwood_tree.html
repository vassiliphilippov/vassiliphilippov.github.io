yellowwood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌwʊd ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌwʊd ˌtriː / (BrE)
any of various trees that have yellowish wood or produce a yellow dye or extract, especially those of the genus Cladrastis or related genera.
tree with yellowish wood or yellow extract

Examples

The yellowwood tree is prized for the distinctive color of its timber.
Gardeners admire the yellowwood tree for its attractive spring blossoms and smooth bark.
Some species of yellowwood tree are used to produce a natural yellow dye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cladrastis - genus of trees often called yellowwood
  • Synonym: yellowwood - yellow wood from such a tree
