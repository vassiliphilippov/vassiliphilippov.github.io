unsuccessful person

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnsəkˈsɛsfl ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəkˈsɛsfl ˈpɜːsn / (BrE)
a person who repeatedly fails or does not achieve their goals; someone who is not successful
a person who fails repeatedly

Examples

Despite working hard, he felt like an unsuccessful person after so many setbacks in his career.
The movie tells the story of an unsuccessful person trying to turn their life around.
She refused to see herself as an unsuccessful person despite her challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: failure - a person who fails to succeed
  • Synonym: loser - a person who is habitually unsuccessful or unlucky
