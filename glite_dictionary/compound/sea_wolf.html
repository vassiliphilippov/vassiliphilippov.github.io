sea wolf

1. NOUN

/ si ˈwʊlf / (AmE), / siː ˈwʊlf / (BrE)
A predatory black-and-white toothed whale with a large dorsal fin, commonly known as the killer whale or orca, found in cold seas.
another name for the killer whale (orca)

Examples

The sea wolf is known for its intelligence and complex social structure.
Early sailors often feared the sea wolf due to its size and strength.
Researchers tracked a pod of sea wolves migrating through the icy waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer whale - large predatory marine mammal; also known as orca
  • Synonym: orca - scientific and common name for the killer whale
