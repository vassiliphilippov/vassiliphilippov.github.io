skinny jeans

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪni ˌdʒinz / (AmE), / ˈskɪni ˌdʒiːnz / (BrE)
tight-fitting denim trousers that are narrow at the legs and ankles
tight-fitting denim trousers

Examples

She bought three pairs of skinny jeans during the summer sale.
Skinny jeans have become a staple in modern fashion for both men and women.
He paired his skinny jeans with a loose T-shirt for a casual look.

Synset Examples

He prefers skinny jeans for a trendy look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeans - denim trousers, especially blue denim
