styrax family

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪræks ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈstaɪræks ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of shrubs and trees, Styraxaceae, widely distributed and belonging to the order Ebenales
family of shrubs and trees (Styraxaceae)

Examples

The styrax family includes many species of ornamental shrubs.
Various members of the styrax family are valued for their fragrant resin.
The botanist specialized in studying plants of the styrax family.

Etymology

From the genus name Styrax, used as the type genus for the family, and the English 'family'.
