silvery-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˈgriːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˈgriːn / (BrE)
having a light, shiny green color, resembling the sheen of silver
light, shiny green color

Examples

The olive trees shimmered with their silvery-green leaves in the afternoon sun.
She wore a beautiful dress in a delicate silvery-green shade.
A silvery-green moss carpeted the damp stones by the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver-green - having a greenish color with a silvery sheen
