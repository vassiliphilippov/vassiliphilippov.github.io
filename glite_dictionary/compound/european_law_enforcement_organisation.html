European Law Enforcement Organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊrəˈpiːən lɔː ɪnˈfɔːrsmənt ˌɔːrgənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈpiːən lɔː ɪnˈfɔːsmənt ˌɔːgənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the agency of the European Union responsible for enhancing police cooperation and effectiveness among EU member states; also known as Europol
EU police cooperation agency
legal

Examples

The European Law Enforcement Organisation coordinates operations to combat organized crime across the EU.
Officials from the European Law Enforcement Organisation attended the summit on cybercrime.
The European Law Enforcement Organisation is often referred to by its abbreviation, Europol.
