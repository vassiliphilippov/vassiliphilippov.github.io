cool and stylish

1. ADJ

/ kul ənd ˈstaɪlɪʃ / (AmE), / kuːl ənd ˈstaɪlɪʃ / (BrE)
fashionably attractive or impressive in appearance or style
fashionably attractive, impressive

Examples

She walked into the party looking cool and stylish in her new dress.
His apartment is cool and stylish, with modern furniture and unique art pieces.
People admired her ability to appear both cool and stylish no matter the occasion.

That jacket is really cool and stylish.
