cow pen

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊ ˌpɛn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ ˌpɛn / (BrE)
an enclosure or fenced area for keeping cattle
enclosure for cattle

Examples

The farmer led the cattle back into the cow pen after grazing.
The old cow pen behind the barn needed repair before winter arrived.
Children watched as the calves played together in the cow pen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corral - an enclosure for confining livestock, especially in North America
