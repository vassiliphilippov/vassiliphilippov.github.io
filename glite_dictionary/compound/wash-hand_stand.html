wash-hand stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃˌhænd ˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌhænd ˌstænd / (BrE)
a table or stand designed to hold a basin and pitcher for washing, commonly used before indoor plumbing; chiefly British
stand for basin and pitcher for washing
british

Examples

A beautiful antique wash-hand stand stood in the corner of the bedroom.
In old country houses, the wash-hand stand was an essential piece of furniture.
She placed a porcelain pitcher on the wash-hand stand beside a delicate basin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washstand - a piece of furniture for holding a wash basin and jug; North American and general
