black diamond

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈdaɪəmənd / (AmE), / blæk ˈdaɪəmənd / (BrE)
an inferior, dark-colored diamond used in industry for drilling and polishing rather than as a gemstone
dark industrial-use diamond

Examples

Only black diamonds were hard enough to cut through the tough rock.
The company imports black diamonds for use in their drilling equipment.
Although not suitable for jewelry, black diamond fragments are highly valued in industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrial diamond - diamond used for industrial purposes rather than jewelry

2. NOUN

/ blæk ˈdaɪmənd / (AmE), / blak ˈdʌɪəmənd / (BrE)
a rare type of diamond that is opaque and black in color
rare opaque black diamond

Examples

She wore a stunning black diamond ring to the gala.
A black diamond is prized by collectors for its unique appearance.
The museum showcased an exhibit featuring several rare black diamonds.

Synset Examples
