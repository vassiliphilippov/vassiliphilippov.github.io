personnel pouch

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsɑːnəl paʊt͡ʃ / (AmE), / pəˈsɒnəl paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a bag used to hold the body of a dead soldier, typically for transportation
bag for dead soldier's body
medical

Examples

The medics carefully placed the remains in a personnel pouch before transportation.
Every personnel pouch was tagged with the soldier's identification details for processing.
After the battle, the recovery team collected the fallen using a personnel pouch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body bag - a bag for storing or transporting dead bodies
