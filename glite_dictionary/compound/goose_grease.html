goose grease

1. NOUN

/ ɡus ɡris / (AmE), / ɡuːs ɡriːs / (BrE)
Fat obtained from geese, often used in cooking or traditional remedies.
fat obtained from geese

Examples

Many traditional recipes call for goose grease as a cooking fat.
She remembered her grandmother rubbing goose grease on her chest to treat a cough.
In gourmet kitchens, goose grease is prized for roasting potatoes.

Related Terms
