kingfisher daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃər ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃə ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a South African flowering plant with softly hairy leaves and bright blue daisy-like flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental.
South African plant with blue daisy-like flowers

Examples

We planted a row of kingfisher daisies along the garden border for their bright blue blooms.
The kingfisher daisy thrives in sunny locations and well-drained soil.
Gardeners often choose the kingfisher daisy for its long flowering season and vivid coloration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Felicia amelloides - the scientific name of the kingfisher daisy
