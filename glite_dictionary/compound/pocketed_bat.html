pocketed bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪtɪd bæt / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtɪd bat / (BrE)
a small brown species of bat found in California and northern Mexico
small brown bat of California and northern Mexico

Examples

The pocketed bat is commonly seen near streams in northern Mexico.
Researchers discovered a new roosting site for the pocketed bat in California.
Unlike some species, the pocketed bat prefers dryer habitats.
