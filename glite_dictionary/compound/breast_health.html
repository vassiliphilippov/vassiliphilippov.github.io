breast health

1. NOUN

/ brɛst hɛlθ / (AmE), / brɛst hɛlθ / (BrE)
the condition of being healthy and free from disease in the breasts, often with reference to prevention and early detection of problems
health and well-being of the breasts

Examples

Regular self-examinations are an important part of maintaining breast health.
Many organizations promote awareness about breast health during October.
Eating a balanced diet can support overall breast health in women.

Synset Examples

The doctor explained the importance of breast health.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: breast cancer - a malignant tumor that develops from breast cells
