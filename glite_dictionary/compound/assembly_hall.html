assembly hall

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɛmbli hɔl / (AmE), / əˈsɛmbli hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room or building where people gather for meetings, events, or other group activities
large room for group gatherings

Examples

The school's assembly hall was filled with students for the graduation ceremony.
Town meetings are usually held in the assembly hall at the community center.
Concerts and performances often take place in the university's assembly hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auditorium - a large room or building for public gatherings
