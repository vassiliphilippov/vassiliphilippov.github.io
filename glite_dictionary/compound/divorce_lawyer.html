divorce lawyer

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɔrs ˈlɔjər / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːs ˈlɔːjə / (BrE)
a lawyer who specializes in handling cases of divorce or annulment
lawyer specializing in divorce cases

Examples

She hired a divorce lawyer to ensure her rights were protected during the proceedings.
A skilled divorce lawyer can help clients reach an amicable settlement.
Both parties consult their own divorce lawyer before signing the final agreement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family lawyer - a lawyer specializing in family-related legal matters
  • Synonym: barrister - a type of lawyer in the UK who represents clients in court
