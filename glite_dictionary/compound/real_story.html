real story

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiəl ˈstɔɹi / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈstɔːɹi / (BrE)
the true or authentic account of what happened, especially as opposed to rumors, speculation, or false versions
the true account of events

Examples

She promised to tell me the real story behind their sudden decision to leave.
You won't believe the real story until you hear it from someone who was there.
After all the rumors, a journalist uncovered the real story of what happened that night.

Synset Examples

No one knows the real story; who shot John?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: true story - a factual or authentic account of events
  • Antonym: lie - an intentionally false statement
