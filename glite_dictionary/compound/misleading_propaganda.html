misleading propaganda

1. NOUN

/ mɪsˈlidɪŋ prɑːpəˈɡændə / (AmE), / mɪsˈliːdɪŋ prɒpəˈɡændə / (BrE)
information that is spread with the intention of deceiving people or giving a false impression
deceptive information or propaganda

Examples

The government was accused of spreading misleading propaganda to manipulate public opinion.
Many people were swayed by misleading propaganda during the election season.
Social media platforms struggled to contain the spread of misleading propaganda online.

Synset Examples

The campaign was filled with misleading propaganda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disinformation - false information spread deliberately to deceive
  • Degree Lower: propaganda - information, especially biased or misleading, used to promote a cause or point of view
