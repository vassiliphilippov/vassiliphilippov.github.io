little spotted skunk

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtl ˈspɑtɪd skʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈspɒtɪd skʌŋk / (BrE)
a small species of skunk with a marbled black and white coat, native to the United States and Mexico
a small black-and-white skunk species

Examples

The little spotted skunk is known for its acrobatic handstands when threatened.
Researchers discovered a new population of little spotted skunk in northern Mexico.
Unlike other skunks, the little spotted skunk has a distinct marbled pattern on its fur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted skunk - a member of the genus Spilogale, a type of skunk with spotted coat patterns
