battery acid

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætəri ˌæsɪd / (AmE), / ˈbætəri ˌæsɪd / (BrE)
a solution of dilute sulfuric acid used as the electrolyte in storage batteries, especially car batteries
dilute sulfuric acid for storage batteries

Examples

Be careful when handling battery acid; it can cause severe burns.
The mechanic cleaned up the battery acid that had leaked onto the car's engine bay.
If your skin comes into contact with battery acid, rinse immediately with plenty of water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sulfuric acid - a strong, corrosive mineral acid with the formula H₂SO₄
