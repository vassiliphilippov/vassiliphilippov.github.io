crying shame

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraɪɪŋ ʃeɪm / (AmE), / ˈkraɪɪŋ ʃeɪm / (BrE)
a situation that causes strong regret or is considered especially unfortunate or distressing
regrettable or unfortunate situation

Examples

It's a crying shame that so many historic buildings have been demolished.
Many believe it's a crying shame that talented artists go unrecognized.
It's a crying shame to waste such an opportunity.

Synset Examples

It's a crying shame that the event was canceled.
Seeing her waste her skills is a crying shame.
The loss of such talent is a crying shame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tragedy - an event causing great suffering or distress
  • Antonym: blessing - something that brings happiness or benefit
