lollipop woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːliˌpɑp ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɒlɪpɒp ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to help children cross the road safely near a school, especially by holding up a stop sign
woman who helps children cross roads safely
british

Examples

The lollipop woman stopped traffic so the children could cross the street safely.
Every morning, the friendly lollipop woman greets the students by the school gate.
The new lollipop woman wears a bright yellow jacket and carries a large stop sign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crossing guard - a person who helps people cross the road safely, especially near schools
  • Synonym: lollipop man - a man hired to help children cross a road safely near a school

Etymology

From the resemblance of the crossing guard's stop sign to a lollipop; 'lollipop man' and 'lollipop lady' are similar terms in British English.
