interior decoration

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɪriər ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the art or business of decorating the inside of a building or room, especially with regard to color, furnishings, and materials
decoration of building interiors

Examples

She has a passion for interior decoration and spends hours picking out fabrics and colors.
The hotel hired a professional to oversee the interior decoration of its new lobby.
Courses in interior decoration are becoming increasingly popular at design schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior design - the art or practice of designing the interior decoration of a room or building

2. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɪriər ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə ˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the art or process of designing and arranging the layout and furnishings of the inside of a building to achieve a pleasing or functional environment
arranging and designing interior spaces

Examples

She hired a professional to handle the interior decoration of her new apartment.
Modern interior decoration often blends functionality with aesthetic appeal.
His passion for interior decoration inspired him to start his own design business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior design - the art or practice of planning and supervising the design and execution of architectural interiors and their furnishings
  • Antonym: exterior decoration - the decoration or ornamentation of the outside of a building
