cherry apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˈapəl / (BrE)
An Asian species of wild crab apple cultivated in many varieties for its small, acidic, usually red fruit used in preserves.
Asian crab apple with small, red, acidic fruit

Examples

The orchard features several varieties of cherry apple known for their tart flavor.
You can use cherry apple fruit to make a delicious homemade jelly.
Each autumn, the bright red cherry apple trees add color to the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a wild apple tree with small, sour fruit
