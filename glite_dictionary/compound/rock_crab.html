rock crab

1. NOUN

/ rɑk kræb / (AmE), / rɒk kræb / (BrE)
a crab found along the eastern coast of North America, known for its hard shell and preference for rocky habitats
a crab from eastern North America

Examples

We found a rock crab hiding among the tidal pools on the beach.
The fisherman caught several rock crabs along the rocky shoreline.
A rock crab has a distinct reddish shell and strong claws.
