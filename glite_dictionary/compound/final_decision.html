final decision

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a judgment or ruling that concludes a legal case, leaving only the enforcement of that judgment
conclusive ruling in a legal case
legal

Examples

The court issued its final decision after months of deliberation.
Once a final decision is made, the parties must comply with the ruling.
The appeal was denied, making the original verdict the final decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgment - a decision by a court of law
