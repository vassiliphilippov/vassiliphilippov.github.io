ringlet butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋlət ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlət ˈbʌtəflʌɪ / (BrE)
any of several butterflies of the family Satyridae, typically brown or gray-brown with eyespots on the wings
brown butterfly with eyespots

Examples

The ringlet butterfly is recognized by the distinctive eyespots on its wings.
During our walk, we spotted a few ringlet butterfly specimens resting in the grass.
Many gardens attract the ringlet butterfly by providing plenty of wild grasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satyr butterfly - any butterfly of the subfamily Satyrinae
