cultural belief

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃɚəl bɪˈlif / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
an idea or principle widely shared within a particular culture, often influencing behavior, customs, and attitudes
shared idea or principle of a culture

Examples

Many cultural beliefs shape the way people approach health and medicine.
The festival is rooted in ancient cultural beliefs about the changing seasons.
Teachers should be mindful of their students’ diverse cultural beliefs in the classroom.

Synset Examples

Cultural beliefs about the hymen vary across societies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tradition - a custom or belief passed down within a group or society
  • Synonym: superstition - a belief or practice resulting from ignorance or fear of the unknown
