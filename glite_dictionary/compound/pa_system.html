P.A. system

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌpiːˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of electronic equipment used to amplify and distribute sound so that it can be heard in public spaces.
public address sound amplification system

Examples

The announcement was made over the P.A. system so everyone in the building could hear it.
During the concert, her voice was projected through a powerful P.A. system.
The school upgraded its P.A. system for better sound quality in assemblies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public address system - an electronic system for amplifying sound in public spaces

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address system'.
