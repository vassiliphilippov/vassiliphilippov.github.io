PA system

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiː ˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌpiː ˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
An electronic amplification system used to make announcements or transmit sound in public areas.
public sound amplification system

Examples

The airport PA system announced a gate change for the flight.
The school uses a PA system to inform students about important events.
He tested the PA system before the concert began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public address system - system for amplifying sound for an audience

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address system'.
