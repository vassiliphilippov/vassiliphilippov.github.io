mahogany furniture

1. NOUN

/ məˈhɑːɡəni ˈfɝːnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / məˈhɒɡəni ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
furniture that is made from mahogany wood, known for its reddish-brown color and durability
furniture made of mahogany wood

Examples

The antique dealer specialized in restoring mahogany furniture to its original luster.
Her living room is elegantly decorated with mahogany furniture and soft, neutral colors.
The rich tone of mahogany furniture added warmth to the old library.
