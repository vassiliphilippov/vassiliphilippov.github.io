emblematic of

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛmbˈlɑːmætɪk ʌv / (AmE), / ˌɛmbləˈmatɪk ɒv / (BrE)
serving as a symbol or representative of a particular quality, idea, or concept.
serving as a symbol of something

Examples

The art deco architecture downtown is emblematic of the city's innovative spirit.
Her generosity is emblematic of the values her family holds dear.
The flag's colors are emblematic of the nation's history and unity.

Synset Examples

The statue is emblematic of the city's rich history.
The dove is emblematic of peace and harmony.
His struggles are emblematic of the larger societal issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: symbolic of - representative or serving as a symbol of something
  • Synonym: representative of - serving as a typical example or representation of something
