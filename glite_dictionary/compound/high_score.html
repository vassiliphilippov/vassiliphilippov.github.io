high score

1. NOUN

/ haɪ skɔɚ / (AmE), / haɪ skɔː / (BrE)
the highest number of points achieved in a game or competition, especially in video or arcade games
the highest score in a game

Examples

After weeks of practice, he finally achieved a new high score on the arcade machine.
The player proudly entered her initials next to the high score display.
They competed to see who could set the high score in the trivia challenge.

Synset Examples

He spent hours playing Squiggol, trying to beat his high score.
She beat her high score on Donkey Kong last night.
