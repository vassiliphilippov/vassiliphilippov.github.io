white pelican

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈpɛlɪkən / (AmE), / waɪt ˈpelɪkən / (BrE)
a large species of pelican found in North America, with white plumage and black wing feathers
large white American pelican species

Examples

The white pelican is often seen gliding gracefully over lakes and marshes.
During migration, flocks of white pelicans can cover great distances together.
A white pelican can have a wingspan of nearly ten feet.
