genus Anguilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ænˈɡwɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs æŋˈɡwɪl.ə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Anguillidae, comprising the true eels found in both freshwater and saltwater environments.
type genus of eel family Anguillidae

Examples

The genus Anguilla includes both European and American freshwater eels.
Scientists are studying migration patterns in the genus Anguilla.
Species of the genus Anguilla are known for their remarkable long-distance journeys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eel - a long, snake-like fish of various families, including Anguillidae

Etymology

From Latin 'anguilla' meaning eel.
