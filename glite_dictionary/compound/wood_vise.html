wood vise

1. NOUN

/ wʊd vaɪs / (AmE), / wʊd vaɪs / (BrE)
A device with padded jaws used to hold wood or lumber securely without damaging its surface while working on it.
padded woodworking vise

Examples

The wood vise kept the board steady while he planed the surface.
Before sanding, she clamped the plank in a wood vise to prevent movement.
A wood vise is essential in any carpentry workshop for holding pieces securely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vise - a mechanical device used to hold an object firmly in place
