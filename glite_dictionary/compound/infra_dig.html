infra dig

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnfrə ˈdɪɡ / (AmE), / ˌɪnfrə ˈdɪɡ / (BrE)
Regarded as beneath one's dignity; considered inappropriate for someone of a certain status.
beneath one's dignity

Examples

He considered manual labor to be infra dig and refused to participate.
Some believe it is infra dig for a CEO to answer customer service emails.
She thought wearing casual clothes to the gala would be infra dig.

Synset Examples

considered helping with the dishes to be infra dig

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demeaning - making someone feel inferior or of less value
  • Synonym: humiliating - causing embarrassment or shame
  • Antonym: honorable - deserving respect or high regard

Etymology

From Latin infra dignitatem ('beneath dignity').
