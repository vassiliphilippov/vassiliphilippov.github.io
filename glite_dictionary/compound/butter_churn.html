butter churn

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚ tʃɝːn / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə tʃɜːn / (BrE)
a container in which cream is agitated to make butter by separating butterfat from buttermilk
vessel for making butter from cream

Examples

The old farmhouse still had a wooden butter churn in the kitchen corner.
She used a butter churn to make fresh butter every week.
The museum displays an antique butter churn from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: churn - a container or machine in which cream or milk is agitated to make butter
