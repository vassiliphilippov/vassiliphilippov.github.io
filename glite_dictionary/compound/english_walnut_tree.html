English walnut tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈwɔlnʌt tri / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈwɔːlnʌt triː / (BrE)
A Eurasian tree (Juglans regia) cultivated for its large edible nuts and hard, richly grained wood.
tree producing edible English walnuts

Examples

The English walnut tree is widely grown in orchards throughout California for its valuable nuts.
We planted an English walnut tree in our backyard last spring, and it has already started to grow.
The furniture was crafted from the wood of an old English walnut tree that once stood on the estate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walnut tree - a tree that produces walnuts
  • Degree Lower: black walnut - a different species of walnut tree with darker wood and smaller nuts
