fangsome beast

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæŋsəm bist / (AmE), / ˈfæŋsəm biːst / (BrE)
A frightening or fearsome creature, typically imagined as having prominent fangs.
fearsome creature with fangs

Examples

The villagers hid in terror when the fangsome beast emerged from the forest.
Legend spoke of a fangsome beast lurking in the caves near the river.
Stories about the fangsome beast kept children from wandering into the woods at night.

Synset Examples

The fangsome beast terrified everyone in the village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monster - a large, frightening creature
  • Synonym: beast - an animal, especially a large or dangerous one

Etymology

Formed from 'fang' (tooth) and the adjective-forming suffix '-some', meaning 'having the quality of', plus 'beast'.
