spelling bee

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌbi / (AmE), / ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌbiː / (BrE)
A competition in which participants are eliminated if they fail to spell words correctly.
word spelling contest

Examples

Jessica won her school's spelling bee after spelling every word correctly.
The annual spelling bee attracted hundreds of eager students from around the city.
He practiced for months to prepare for the regional spelling bee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spelling contest - competition to correctly spell words

Etymology

Possibly from the American English sense of 'bee' meaning a gathering for communal work, of uncertain origin.
