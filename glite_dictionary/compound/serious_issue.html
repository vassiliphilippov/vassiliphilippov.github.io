serious issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəs ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs ˈɪʃu / (BrE)
A problem or matter of great importance that demands careful attention or action.
a significant or important problem

Examples

Climate change has become a serious issue that governments need to address urgently.
Cyberbullying is considered a serious issue in many schools today.
The report highlights poverty as a serious issue affecting millions worldwide.

Synset Examples

Starvation is a serious issue in many developing countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: problem - a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful
