nerve-shredder

1. NOUN

/ nɜːrv ˈʃrɛdər / (AmE), / nɜːv ˈʃrɛdə / (BrE)
an event, situation, or thing that causes intense anxiety or stress
thing causing intense anxiety or stress
informal

Examples

The championship match turned out to be a real nerve-shredder for fans watching in the stadium.
Waiting for the test results was a complete nerve-shredder for her.
Final exams can be a nerve-shredder for even the most prepared students.

Synset Examples

His reckless driving turned our road trip into a nerve-shredder.
Public speaking is a nerve-shredder for many people.
The constant deadlines made my job a real nerve-shredder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail-biter - an event causing suspense or anxiety
  • Antonym: stress-reliever - something that reduces stress
