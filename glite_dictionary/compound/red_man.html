red man

1. NOUN

/ ɹɛd mæn / (AmE), / ɹɛd man / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) An offensive and outdated term for a male Native American.
offensive slang for Native American man
slang offensive

Examples

The old books sometimes use the term red man to refer to a Native American, but it is now considered deeply offensive.
History classes today acknowledge that words like red man are inappropriate and disrespectful.
He was criticized for using the outdated term red man in his speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redskin - offensive slang term for Native American
  • Antonym: Native American - a member of any of the indigenous peoples of the Americas
