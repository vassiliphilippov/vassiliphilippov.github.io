viewing platform

1. NOUN

/ ˈvjuɪŋ ˌplætˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈvjuː.ɪŋ ˌplæt.fɔːm / (BrE)
a raised structure or designated area built for people to observe events, scenery, or attractions
raised structure for observation

Examples

We climbed the stairs to the viewing platform for a better look at the festival.
The viewing platform offered spectacular views of the city skyline.
Crowds gathered on the viewing platform to watch the parade pass below.

Synset Examples

They watched the rocket take off from the viewing platform.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observation deck - an elevated platform for observing scenery or events, especially in tall buildings
