plastering machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstərɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstərɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to apply plaster or render to walls and ceilings, typically in construction or building work
machine for applying plaster to walls

Examples

The workers used a plastering machine to finish the entire wall in just a few hours.
Investing in a plastering machine can greatly reduce manual labor on construction sites.
Before painting, a plastering machine was used to create an even surface on all the interior walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rendering machine - machine used for applying render to walls
