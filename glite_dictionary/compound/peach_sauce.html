peach sauce

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ sɔs / (AmE), / piːtʃ sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet sauce made from peaches, commonly used as a condiment or accompaniment, especially in Chinese cuisine.
sweet peach-based sauce for food

Examples

She poured peach sauce over the slices of roast duck.
The restaurant served spring rolls with a tangy peach sauce on the side.
You can make your own peach sauce using fresh peaches and a little vinegar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum sauce - a sweet-sour sauce commonly served with Chinese dishes, made from plums
