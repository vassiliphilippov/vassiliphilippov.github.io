Isthmus of Panama

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪsθməs əv pəˈnɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈɪsθməs əv pəˈnæmə / (BrE)
a narrow strip of land that connects Central America and South America, separating the Caribbean Sea from the Pacific Ocean; formerly known as the Isthmus of Darien
narrow land connecting Central and South America

Examples

Many species crossed the Isthmus of Panama during the Great American Biotic Interchange.
The construction of the Panama Canal transformed trade through the Isthmus of Panama.
Balboa was the first European to cross the Isthmus of Panama in 1513.
