Oriental garlic

1. NOUN

/ əˈriːən.tl ˈɡɑr.lɪk / (AmE), / ɒˈriː.ən.tl ˈɡɑː.lɪk / (BrE)
a perennial plant of eastern Asia, similar to chives but larger, cultivated for its edible leaves and bulbs
Asian plant similar to chives

Examples

The chef added freshly chopped Oriental garlic to the salad for extra flavor.
Farmers in eastern Asia often cultivate Oriental garlic for both its leaves and bulbs.
You can substitute Oriental garlic for chives in many stir-fry dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garlic - a plant with a strong flavor used in cooking
