exotic blend

1. NOUN

/ ɪgˈzɑtɪk blɛnd / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɒtɪk blend / (BrE)
A mixture, especially of tea or coffee, that contains unusual or foreign ingredients or flavors.
unusual or foreign-flavored mixture, typically tea or coffee

Examples

The menu featured an exotic blend of oolong and tropical fruit teas.
She enjoys starting her day with an exotic blend rather than ordinary black tea.
This exotic blend combines spices and herbs rarely found in Western teas.

Synset Examples

The teahouse offers a variety of exotic blends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blend - a mixture of different substances or elements
