summer festival

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
An organized public event held annually during the summer to celebrate the season, often featuring music, food, and activities.
festival held in summer

Examples

The city organizes a summer festival every year, attracting thousands of visitors from across the region.
Families look forward to the live music, food stalls, and fireworks at the annual summer festival.
Local artists and performers showcased their talents at the summer festival this weekend.

Synset Examples

They established an encampment for the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - An organized series of events such as music, dance, and food, usually for celebration
