war criminal

1. NOUN

/ wɔr ˈkrɪmɪnl̩ / (AmE), / wɔː ˈkrɪmɪnl̩ / (BrE)
a person who commits serious violations of international law during times of war, such as crimes against humanity or war crimes
person who commits serious crimes during war

Examples

The court convicted the former general as a war criminal for his actions during the conflict.
Many believe that those responsible should be prosecuted as war criminals by an international tribunal.
She worked tirelessly to bring evidence against suspected war criminals to light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war crime - a serious violation of international law committed during war
