world-beater

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is exceptionally successful or far superior to others in its field
an outstandingly successful person or thing

Examples

Her performance on the tennis court this year has made her a true world-beater.
The new smartphone is a world-beater with its innovative design and speed.
The company’s latest car engine turns them into a potential world-beater in automotive technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champion - a person who has defeated all opponents in a competition
  • Antonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning
