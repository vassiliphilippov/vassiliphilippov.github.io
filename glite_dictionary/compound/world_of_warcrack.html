World of Warcrack

1. NOUN

/ wɝld əv ˈwɔr.kræk / (AmE), / wɜːld əv ˈwɔː.kræk / (BrE)
A slang term for the popular online fantasy game 'World of Warcraft,' used humorously to imply addiction or excessive playing.
slang for 'World of Warcraft,' implying addiction
slang

Examples

Many gamers jokingly refer to the game as World of Warcrack because of its addictive nature.
He confessed that he lost an entire weekend to World of Warcrack.
Whenever a new expansion is released, players flock back to World of Warcrack in droves.

Synset Examples

The addiction to the World of Warcrack is hard to break.
Friends joked that he was lost in the World of Warcrack.
He spent hours in the World of Warcrack instead of studying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: World of Warcraft - Popular online multiplayer fantasy game released in 2004

Etymology

Formed by blending 'World of Warcraft' with 'crack' (a slang term for an addictive drug), highlighting the game's reportedly addictive nature.
