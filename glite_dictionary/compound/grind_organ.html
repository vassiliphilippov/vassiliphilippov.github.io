grind organ

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraɪnd ˌɔːr.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪnd ˌɔː.ɡən / (BrE)
a mechanical musical instrument that produces music by rotating a cylinder fitted with pegs, often operated by turning a handle
mechanical musical instrument with a rotating cylinder

Examples

The street musician played a grind organ that drew a small crowd of children.
Antique collectors often seek rare grind organs for their historical value.
With each turn of the handle, the grind organ filled the market square with cheerful tunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel organ - mechanical musical instrument with a rotating barrel

Etymology

From the verb 'grind' (to turn a handle) and 'organ' (musical instrument).
