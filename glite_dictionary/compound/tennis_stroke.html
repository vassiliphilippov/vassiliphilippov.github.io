tennis stroke

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs stroʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs strəʊk / (BrE)
the act of hitting a tennis ball with a racket, especially as part of play in tennis
act of hitting a tennis ball

Examples

She practiced her tennis stroke every afternoon to improve her game.
His powerful tennis stroke impressed the coach during the tryouts.
A good tennis stroke requires proper grip and timing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tennis shot - a shot made in tennis; hitting the ball during play
