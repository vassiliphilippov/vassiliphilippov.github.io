good night

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
A polite or conventional expression used to bid someone farewell in the evening or before going to sleep.
evening or bedtime farewell

Examples

She kissed her children and softly said, good night before turning off the light.
He waved to his friends and wished everyone a good night as he left the party.
After finishing their phone call, they both said good night and hung up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - a conventional expression used when parting
  • Antonym: good morning - a conventional greeting said in the morning

2. INTJ

/ ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
Used to express farewell or to wish someone a pleasant night, especially before parting or going to bed.
expression used to wish a pleasant night

Examples

She smiled and said, "good night!" as she closed the door.
After the movie ended, everyone hugged and wished each other good night.
He sent a quick text to his friend saying good night before falling asleep.

Synset Examples

She whispered good night before turning off the lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - expression used to say farewell
  • Antonym: good morning - expression used to wish a pleasant morning
