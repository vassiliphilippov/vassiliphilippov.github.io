St. John River

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt dʒɑn ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / seɪnt dʒɒn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river that originates in Maine, USA, and flows northeast through New Brunswick, Canada, emptying into the Bay of Fundy.
river in Maine and New Brunswick

Examples

The St. John River is known for its stunning scenery and diverse wildlife.
Flooding along the St. John River often impacts nearby communities in New Brunswick.
We took a boat tour along the St. John River during our summer vacation.
