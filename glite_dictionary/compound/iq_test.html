IQ test

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈkju tɛst / (AmE), / aɪ ˈkjuː tɛst / (BrE)
a standardized test designed to measure a person's intelligence quotient (IQ)
test to measure intelligence quotient

Examples

She took an IQ test as part of her job application process.
Children often take an IQ test to assess their learning needs in school.
After studying psychology, he became interested in how IQ tests are constructed and interpreted.

Synset Examples

they used to think that intelligence is what an intelligence test tests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intelligence test - a test designed to measure intelligence

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'intelligence quotient' combined with 'test'.
