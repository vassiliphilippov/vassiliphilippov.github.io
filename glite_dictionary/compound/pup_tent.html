pup tent

1. NOUN

/ pʌp tɛnt / (AmE), / pʌp tɛnt / (BrE)
A small, wedge-shaped tent, usually without a floor or windows, typically used for camping or by the military.
small wedge-shaped tent

Examples

We set up our pup tent by the lake before sunset.
The soldiers spent the night in a pup tent during the training exercise.
Because the pup tent is lightweight, it's perfect for backpacking trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth supported by poles

Etymology

From 'pup' (a young animal, suggesting small size) and 'tent', first used in U.S. military slang in the 19th century.
