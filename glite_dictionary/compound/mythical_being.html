mythical being

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
An imaginary creature or entity found in myths, legends, or folklore.
creature from myth or legend

Examples

The unicorn is a famous example of a mythical being often depicted in stories.
Many cultures have legends about at least one mythical being that protects them from harm.
As a child, she was fascinated by every kind of mythical being she read about in fairy tales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythological creature - a creature from mythology or folklore
  • Antonym: real being - a being that truly exists, as opposed to imaginary
