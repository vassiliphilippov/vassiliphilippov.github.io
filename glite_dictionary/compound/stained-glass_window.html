stained-glass window

1. NOUN

/ ˌsteɪnd ˈɡlæs ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsteɪnd ˈɡlɑːs ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window made from pieces of colored glass, often arranged in decorative patterns, typically found in churches or other significant buildings
decorative window made of colored glass

Examples

The church is famous for its beautiful stained-glass window depicting scenes from the Bible.
A single stained-glass window cast colorful patterns across the wooden floor.
The artist spent months designing the intricate details of the new stained-glass window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stained glass - colored glass used for decorative windows
