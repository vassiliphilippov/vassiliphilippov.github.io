mud pie

1. NOUN

/ mʌd paɪ / (AmE), / mʌd paɪ / (BrE)
a mass of mud shaped into a pie, typically made by children while playing outdoors
pie-shaped lump of mud made as child's play

Examples

When she was little, she loved making mud pies in the backyard after it rained.
The children proudly displayed their mud pies on the porch steps for everyone to see.
My shoes got ruined after I accidentally stepped on a mud pie during recess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mud - soft, wet earth
