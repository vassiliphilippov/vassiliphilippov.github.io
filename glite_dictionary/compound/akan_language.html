Akan language

1. NOUN

/ əˈkɑːn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈkæn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Kwa language spoken primarily in Ghana and Ivory Coast
language spoken in Ghana and Ivory Coast

Examples

Linguists have documented many proverbs in the Akan language.
She translated the book from English into the Akan language.
Many students in Ghana grow up speaking the Akan language at home.

Etymology

From Akan, the ethnic group name, of uncertain origin.
