vas deferens

1. NOUN

/ væs ˈdɛf.əˌrɛnz / (AmE), / væs ˈdɛf.ərəns / (BrE)
the duct in the male reproductive system that carries sperm from the epididymis to the ejaculatory duct
male reproductive duct carrying sperm
medical

Examples

During a vasectomy, the surgeon cuts the vas deferens to prevent sperm from reaching the ejaculate.
The vas deferens transports sperm from the testicle to the urethra.
Damage to the vas deferens can affect a man's fertility.

Etymology

From Latin 'vas' (vessel) and 'deferens' (carrying down).
