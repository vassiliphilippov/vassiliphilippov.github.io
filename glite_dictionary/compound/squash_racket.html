squash racket

1. NOUN

/ skwɑʃ ˈrækət / (AmE), / skwɒʃ ˈrækɪt / (BrE)
a light, long-handled racket used for playing squash, typically with a small, oval head
racket used in playing squash

Examples

He bought a new squash racket before joining the club tournament.
Please remember to bring your squash racket to practice tomorrow.
Her old squash racket was too heavy, so she replaced it with a lighter one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tennis racket - racket used for playing tennis
