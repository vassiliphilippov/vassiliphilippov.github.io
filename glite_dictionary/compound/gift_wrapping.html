gift wrapping

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft ˈræpɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪft ˈræpɪŋ / (BrE)
Decorative paper, material, or packaging used to wrap gifts for presentation.
decorative material for wrapping gifts

Examples

She chose bright red gift wrapping for her friend's birthday present.
The store offers complimentary gift wrapping during the holidays.
After opening her present, she carefully folded the gift wrapping to reuse it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrapping paper - paper used for wrapping gifts
