holiday tradition

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈhɒlɪˌdeɪ trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a customary practice or activity regularly carried out during a holiday period
custom practiced during a holiday

Examples

Opening gifts on Christmas morning is a beloved holiday tradition in many families.
Baking pumpkin pie together is our favorite holiday tradition.
Every year, the town parade has become an important holiday tradition for locals.

Synset Examples

Dressing up as Santy is a holiday tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tradition - a long-established custom or belief
  • Synonym: custom - a habitual practice, especially of a community
