non-flowering plant

1. NOUN

/ nɑn ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / nɒn ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that does not produce flowers, such as mosses, ferns, or conifers
plant that does not produce flowers

Examples

Ferns are a common type of non-flowering plant found in forests around the world.
Unlike roses, which bloom in spring, mosses are non-flowering plants and reproduce by spores.
Some gardens include non-flowering plants like evergreens for year-round greenery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flowering plant - a plant that produces flowers and seeds
