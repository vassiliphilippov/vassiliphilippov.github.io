typewriter carriage

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪtər ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtə ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a typewriter that holds and moves the paper as you type
moving part of a typewriter holding paper

Examples

She replaced the ribbon and cleaned the typewriter carriage before starting her novel.
After typing each line, you must push the typewriter carriage back to the left.
The old typewriter carriage moved smoothly, making a distinct bell sound at the end of each row.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carriage return - the mechanism or act of returning the typewriter carriage to the starting position
