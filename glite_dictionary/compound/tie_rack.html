tie rack

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ræk / (AmE), / taɪ ræk / (BrE)
a small rack designed for storing and organizing neckties
rack for storing neckties

Examples

He installed a tie rack inside the closet to keep his neckties organized.
The tie rack in the store displayed dozens of colorful designs.
She bought a rotating tie rack as a gift for her husband.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes rack - a frame or stand for hanging clothes
