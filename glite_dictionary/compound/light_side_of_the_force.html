light side of the Force

1. NOUN

/ laɪt saɪd əv ðə fɔrs / (AmE), / laɪt saɪd əv ðə fɔːs / (BrE)
in the Star Wars universe, the aspect of the Force associated with goodness, virtue, and morality, followed by the Jedi
good aspect of the Force in Star Wars

Examples

The Jedi strive to follow the light side of the Force in all their actions.
Belief in the light side of the Force helped her resist the temptation of evil.
In the Star Wars saga, many characters struggle between the dark and the light side of the Force.

Synset Examples

The Jedi focus on the light side of the Force.
He always believed in the light side of the Force.
She chose to embrace the light side of the Force.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dark side of the Force - evil aspect of the Force in Star Wars

Etymology

Popularized by the Star Wars franchise (1977–), where 'light side' refers to virtue and 'the Force' is a central mystical concept.
