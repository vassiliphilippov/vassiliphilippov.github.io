half mask

1. NOUN

/ hæf mæsk / (AmE), / hɑːf mɑːsk / (BrE)
a mask that covers only the upper part of the face, usually with holes for the eyes
mask covering the upper face

Examples

She wore a glittering half mask to the masquerade ball.
The actor donned a white half mask for his role in the play.
A half mask allows the mouth to remain visible while concealing the eyes and forehead.
