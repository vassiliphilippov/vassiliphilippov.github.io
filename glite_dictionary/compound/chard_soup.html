chard soup

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrd suːp / (AmE), / tʃɑːd suːp / (BrE)
A soup prepared with chard as a main ingredient, often combined with other vegetables or seasonings.
soup made with chard

Examples

The restaurant's special today is a creamy chard soup served with fresh bread.
My grandmother always makes chard soup during the winter to warm us up.
If you have leftover chard, you can make a simple and healthy chard soup.

Related Terms
