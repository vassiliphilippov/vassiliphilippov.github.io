wedding day

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which a wedding ceremony is held
the day a wedding takes place

Examples

She remembered every detail of her wedding day even years later.
They chose a beautiful venue for their wedding day.
Rain on your wedding day is said to be good luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony, especially considered as including the associated celebrations
