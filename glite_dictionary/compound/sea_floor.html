sea floor

1. NOUN

/ si ˈflɔr / (AmE), / siː ˈflɔː / (BrE)
the bottom surface of a sea or ocean
bottom surface of a sea or ocean

Examples

The deep sea floor is home to many unique and mysterious creatures.
Scientists use submersibles to explore the sea floor for geological research.
Thick layers of sediment have accumulated on the sea floor over millions of years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seabed - the bottom of a sea or ocean
  • Antonym: sea surface - the top layer of a sea or ocean
