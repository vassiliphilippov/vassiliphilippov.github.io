almond oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːlmənd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow fatty oil obtained from sweet or bitter almonds, used in cooking, cosmetics, and medicine
oil extracted from almonds

Examples

She massages her face every night with almond oil to keep her skin soft.
The recipe called for a tablespoon of almond oil instead of olive oil.
Many hair products contain almond oil to promote shine and strength.
