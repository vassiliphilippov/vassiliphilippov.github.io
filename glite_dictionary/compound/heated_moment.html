heated moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtɪd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief period marked by intense emotion, passion, or excitement, often resulting in impulsive words or actions
intensely emotional or passionate period

Examples

In a heated moment, she blurted out how she really felt about the project.
Tempers flared during the heated moment at the meeting, but everyone calmed down soon after.
He apologized later, admitting that it was said in a heated moment and he didn't mean it.

Synset Examples

That was a heated gaming moment during the final match.
We had a heated gaming moment over who made the best play.
In a heated moment, he shouted, 'What a cocksucker!'

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calm moment - a time marked by calmness or tranquility
