blinkie advertisement

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋki ˌædvərˌtaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋki ədˈvɜːtɪsmənt / (BrE)
an advertisement, especially online, featuring flashing lights to attract attention
advertisement with flashing lights
