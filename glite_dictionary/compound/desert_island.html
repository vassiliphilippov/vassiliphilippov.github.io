desert island

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈdez.ət ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
An uninhabited island, typically in a remote location, often imagined as the setting for being stranded or isolated.
uninhabited or remote island

Examples

Many people dream of escaping to a desert island to find peace and solitude.
The survivors washed up on a desert island with no supplies.
In classic literature, being cast away on a desert island is a popular theme.

Synset Examples

His fantasy involves being stranded on a desert island.
In survival stories, a desert island is often featured.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uninhabited island - island with no permanent human residents
  • Antonym: mainland - the principal landmass of a region, as opposed to islands
