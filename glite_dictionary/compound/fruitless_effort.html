fruitless effort

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːtləs ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / ˈfruːtləs ˈɛfət / (BrE)
an attempt or series of actions that does not achieve the desired result; an unsuccessful effort
an unsuccessful attempt

Examples

After months of searching, their fruitless effort ended with no answers or progress.
He realized that arguing with her was a fruitless effort and decided to give up.
All the team’s fruitless efforts left them exhausted but unresolved.

Synset Examples

Our efforts to find a solution were fruitless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futile effort - an effort with no chance of success
  • Antonym: successful effort - an attempt that achieves the desired result
