leopard lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛpərd ˌlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈlɛpəd ˌlɪzəd / (BrE)
a large lizard found in western United States and northern Mexico, marked with many dark spots
a large spotted lizard of the US and Mexico

Examples

The leopard lizard is well-camouflaged among desert rocks and sand.
During our hike, we spotted a leopard lizard basking in the sun.
Leopard lizards are known for their speed and agility when hunting.
