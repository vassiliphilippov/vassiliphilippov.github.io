silver hake

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər heɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə heɪk / (BrE)
A species of fish found off the Atlantic coast of North America, valued as a food fish.
a North American Atlantic food fish

Examples

The silver hake is an important catch for fisheries along the New England coast.
Fresh silver hake was served at the restaurant last night.
Scientists are studying the migration patterns of silver hake in the Atlantic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hake - a type of marine fish in the cod family
