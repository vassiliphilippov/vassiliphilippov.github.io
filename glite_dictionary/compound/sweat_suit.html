sweat suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛt suːt / (AmE), / ˈswɛt suːt / (BrE)
A matching set of loose, comfortable clothing consisting of sweatpants and a sweatshirt, typically worn for exercise or casual wear.
matching set of sweatpants and sweatshirt

Examples

After a long day at work, she changed into her favorite sweat suit to relax at home.
The team wore matching sweat suits during their warm-up before the game.
He bought a new sweat suit for his morning runs in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - a jacket and trousers worn for sports or exercise
