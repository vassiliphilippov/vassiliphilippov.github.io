power cable

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər ˈkeɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˈkeɪbəl / (BrE)
A cable used to carry electrical power from one place to another.
cable that carries electrical power

Examples

The technician replaced the broken power cable on the computer.
Always make sure the power cable is unplugged before attempting any repairs.
They tripped over a loose power cable in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power cord - a flexible electrical cable with a plug that connects an appliance to a power source
