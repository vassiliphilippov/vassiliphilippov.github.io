Harvard alumni

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvərd ælˈumni / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvəd ælˈjuːmaɪ / (BrE)
People who have graduated from Harvard University.
graduates of Harvard University

Examples

Many Harvard alumni hold influential positions in government and business.
The annual reunion brought together hundreds of Harvard alumni from around the world.
Several Harvard alumni contributed to the development of the new campus building.

Synset Examples

Harvard alumni often return to mentor students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alumnus - a graduate or former student of a particular school, college, or university
  • Synonym: graduate - a person who has completed a course of study at a school, college, or university
