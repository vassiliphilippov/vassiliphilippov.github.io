spiritual world

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl wɜrld / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjʊəl wɜːld / (BrE)
the realm believed to be inhabited or governed by divine spirits or supernatural beings, distinct from the physical world
realm of divine or supernatural spirits

Examples

Many ancient cultures believed in the existence of a spiritual world beyond our own.
She sought guidance from the spiritual world through meditation and prayer.
The novel explores the conflict between the material world and the spiritual world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: material world - the physical realm, as opposed to the spiritual world
