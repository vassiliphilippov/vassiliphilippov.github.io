shattered dream

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæt̬ɚd driːm / (AmE), / ˈʃætəd driːm / (BrE)
a hope or aspiration that has been completely destroyed or lost
lost hope or aspiration

Examples

After losing the championship, his shattered dreams left him feeling empty.
She struggled to recover from the shattered dream of becoming a professional dancer.
Many refugees arrive in new countries carrying shattered dreams but hopeful hearts.

Synset Examples

He returned home to a broken heart and shattered dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken dream - a hope or aspiration that has been destroyed
