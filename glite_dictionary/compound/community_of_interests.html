community of interests

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti əv ˈɪntrəsts / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ɒv ˈɪntrɪsts / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more people or groups share common goals, concerns, or objectives
shared goals or concerns

Examples

The two companies formed a community of interests to pursue their mutual business goals.
Despite their differences, the parties established a community of interests for environmental advocacy.
The treaty was possible because the nations recognized a community of interests in regional stability.

Synset Examples

the preachers and the bootleggers found they had a community of interests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common ground - shared opinions or interests between parties
