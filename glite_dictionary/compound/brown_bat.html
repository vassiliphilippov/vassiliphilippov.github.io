brown bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊn bæt / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn bat / (BrE)
A small or medium-sized insect-eating bat, typically brown in color, found in caves, trees, and buildings worldwide.
a small/medium insect-eating bat

Examples

The brown bat is commonly seen at dusk, hunting insects near water.
Many farmers appreciate the brown bat for its role in controlling crop pests.
A colony of brown bats was discovered roosting in the abandoned barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bat - a nocturnal flying mammal
