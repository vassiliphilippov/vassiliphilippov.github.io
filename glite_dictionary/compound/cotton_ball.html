cotton ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːtən bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒtən bɔːl / (BrE)
A fungal disease that affects cranberries, characterized by the appearance of cottony masses on the berries.
fungus disease of cranberries

Examples

The cranberry crop was ruined by cotton ball this year.
Farmers are learning new ways to prevent cotton ball in their bogs.
Early detection of cotton ball can help protect cranberry yields.
