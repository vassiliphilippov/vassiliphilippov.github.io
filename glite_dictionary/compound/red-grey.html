red-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color that is tinged or mixed with red.
grey color mixed with red

Examples

The sky had a red-grey hue at sunset, blending warmth with cool tones.
He painted the wall in a red-grey shade to add subtle color to the room.
Her scarf was a soft red-grey, matching the autumn leaves perfectly.
