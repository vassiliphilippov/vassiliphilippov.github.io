petrol gage

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtroʊl ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəl ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument in a vehicle that shows the amount of petrol (gasoline) left in the fuel tank
instrument showing petrol remaining in a vehicle
british

Examples

The petrol gage showed that the tank was nearly empty, so we stopped at the next service station.
Always check your petrol gage before starting a long journey.
My car's petrol gage isn't working, so I fill up the tank regularly just in case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - instrument showing the level of fuel in a vehicle's tank

Etymology

From 'petrol' (British English for gasoline) + 'gage', variant spelling of 'gauge'.
