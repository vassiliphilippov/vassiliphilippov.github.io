musculus sphincter ani

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.kjə.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər ˈæ.naɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər ˈæ.nɪ / (BrE)
the sphincter muscle surrounding and controlling the anus
muscle controlling the anus
medical

Examples

The musculus sphincter ani plays a critical role in controlling bowel movements.
Damage to the musculus sphincter ani can result in incontinence.
During the procedure, the surgeon examined the patient's musculus sphincter ani for abnormalities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal sphincter - muscle that closes the anal opening

Etymology

From Latin musculus (little mouse, muscle), sphincter (binder), and ani (of the anus).
