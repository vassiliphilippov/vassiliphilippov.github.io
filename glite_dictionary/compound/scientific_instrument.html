scientific instrument

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈəntɪfɪk ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / saɪˈɛntɪfɪk ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a device or tool used for scientific research, measurement, or experimentation
tool for scientific research

Examples

Modern laboratories use a variety of scientific instruments to analyze biological samples.
The microscope is a scientific instrument that revolutionized our understanding of the microscopic world.
She specializes in designing new scientific instruments for medical research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apparatus - equipment or machinery needed for a particular purpose
