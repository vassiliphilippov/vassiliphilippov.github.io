California laurel

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfərniə ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəniə ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
A tree native to the Pacific coast of North America, with aromatic leaves, small umbellate flowers, and hard, tough wood.
Pacific coast aromatic tree

Examples

The wood of the California laurel is prized by craftsmen for its durability and beauty.
Aromatic leaves from the California laurel are sometimes used to flavor food.
We saw a mature California laurel growing beside the river during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bay laurel - a related aromatic tree (Laurus nobilis)
