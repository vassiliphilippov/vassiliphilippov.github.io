Gulf of Sidra

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv ˈsɪdrə / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv ˈsɪdrə / (BrE)
a wide inlet of the Mediterranean Sea on the north coast of Libya
inlet of the Mediterranean Sea in Libya

Examples

The Gulf of Sidra has been a strategic location in maritime history.
Oil tankers frequently pass through the Gulf of Sidra on their way to Europe.
Libya's northern coastline stretches along the Gulf of Sidra for hundreds of kilometers.
