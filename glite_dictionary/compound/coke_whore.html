coke whore

1. NOUN

/ koʊk wɔːr / (AmE), / kəʊk wɔː / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person, often perceived negatively, who is addicted to cocaine or is excessively involved in drug-fueled partying.
a person addicted to cocaine or partying (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

She accused him of being a coke whore after another wild party.
He hated the way people at the club whispered and called her a coke whore.
The movie explored the destructive life of a self-proclaimed coke whore.
