steak knife

1. NOUN

/ steɪk naɪf / (AmE), / steɪk naɪf / (BrE)
A sharp table knife with a serrated or straight edge, designed for cutting steak and other meats at the table.
sharp knife for cutting steak

Examples

She used a steak knife to cut through the thick piece of meat.
Every place setting included a serrated steak knife and a fork.
After washing the dishes, he noticed a steak knife was missing from the set.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: table knife - a knife used for eating at the table
