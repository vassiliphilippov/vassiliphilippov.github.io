booze cruiser

1. NOUN

/ buz kruːzər / (AmE), / buːz kruːzə / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who frequently drinks alcohol socially, or a vehicle used for transporting or consuming alcohol during social events.
person or vehicle associated with drinking alcohol socially
informal slang

Examples

The booze cruiser always brings the best drinks to every party.
They rented a booze cruiser for the lake trip, stocked with snacks and beer.
Known as a booze cruiser, she was the life of every social gathering.

Synset Examples

The booze cruiser always brings the best drinks.
He's known as the local booze cruiser at parties.
Every gathering is lively with the booze cruiser around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcohol
  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol

Etymology

From 'booze' (slang for alcohol or to drink alcohol) + 'cruiser' (vehicle or one who cruises).
