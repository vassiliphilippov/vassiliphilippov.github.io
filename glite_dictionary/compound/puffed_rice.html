puffed rice

1. NOUN

/ pʌft raɪs / (AmE), / pʌft raɪs / (BrE)
rice grains that have been expanded and lightened by heating, resulting in a crisp and airy texture
rice grains expanded by heat

Examples

She made a snack with puffed rice, peanuts, and spices.
The cereal is made from puffed rice and dried fruit.
I bought a bag of puffed rice from the grocery store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rice - grains of the grass species Oryza sativa, a staple food
