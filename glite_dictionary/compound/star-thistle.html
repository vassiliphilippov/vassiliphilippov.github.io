star-thistle

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / stɑː ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
A Mediterranean annual or biennial herb with pinkish to purple flowers surrounded by spine-tipped scales, naturalized in America.
Mediterranean herb with spiny flower heads

Examples

The field was covered in star-thistle, its spiny flowers glowing in the sunlight.
Gardeners often struggle to remove invasive star-thistle from their property.
Hikers must be careful, as the sharp spines of the star-thistle can easily prick exposed skin.
