smooth woodsia

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈwʊdzi.ə / (AmE), / smuːð ˈwʊdzi.ə / (BrE)
a rock-inhabiting fern found in Arctic and subarctic regions of Europe to eastern Asia, known for its smooth fronds
rock-dwelling fern of Arctic/subarctic Eurasia

Examples

The smooth woodsia thrives in rocky crevices across Arctic and subarctic Europe.
During our hike, we spotted a rare smooth woodsia growing beside the stream.
Scientists are studying how the smooth woodsia adapts to extremely cold environments.
