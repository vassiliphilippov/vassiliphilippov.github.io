university student

1. NOUN

/ juˈnɪvərsəti ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəːsəti ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a person who is enrolled at a university to study for a degree or other qualification
student enrolled at a university

Examples

Many university students choose to study abroad for one semester.
The library was crowded with university students preparing for their final exams.
As a university student, she joined several academic clubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: college student - a student enrolled at a college
  • Degree Higher: graduate student - a student who has completed a first degree and is studying for a further qualification
  • Degree Lower: school pupil - a child attending school, usually before higher education
