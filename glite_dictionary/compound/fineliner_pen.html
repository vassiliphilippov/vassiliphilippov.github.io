fineliner pen

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnˌlaɪnər pɛn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnˌlaɪnə pɛn / (BrE)
A felt-tip pen with a very thin tip, used for precise drawing or writing fine lines.
felt-tip pen with a thin tip for fine lines

Examples

I use a fineliner pen to add detail to my sketches.
Her handwriting looks neat and crisp with a fineliner pen.
Architects often prefer a fineliner pen for technical drawings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: felt-tip pen - a pen with a porous tip of felt for drawing or writing

Etymology

Formed from 'fine' + 'liner,' indicating a pen for making fine lines.
