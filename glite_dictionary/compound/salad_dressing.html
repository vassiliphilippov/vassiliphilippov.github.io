salad dressing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsæləd ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
a cold sauce, often creamy or oily, served with salads to add flavor
sauce for salads

Examples

She poured a creamy salad dressing over the fresh vegetables.
Do you prefer vinaigrette or ranch salad dressing on your salad?
The restaurant makes all their salad dressing from scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinaigrette - a thin, oily salad dressing of oil and vinegar
  • Synonym: mayonnaise - a thick, creamy dressing often used for salads
