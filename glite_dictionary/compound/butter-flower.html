butter-flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ər ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Ranunculus, commonly known as buttercups, recognized for their bright yellow flowers
a plant of the Ranunculus genus (buttercup)

Examples

The field was covered with bright butter-flowers swaying gently in the breeze.
Children often pick butter-flowers to weave into garlands.
Some people believe that butter-flowers can predict sunny weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - a plant of the genus Ranunculus, with bright yellow flowers
