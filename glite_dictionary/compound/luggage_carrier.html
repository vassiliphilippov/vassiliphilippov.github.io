luggage carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ˌkæriər / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ˌkæriə / (BrE)
a rack or device attached to a vehicle, especially a bicycle, for carrying luggage or other items
rack for carrying luggage on a vehicle

Examples

She strapped her backpack onto the luggage carrier before cycling to the station.
The new model of the bicycle comes with a sturdy luggage carrier at the back.
I installed a luggage carrier on my car for our road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bike rack - a rack for carrying things on a bicycle
  • Synonym: roof rack - a rack on top of a car for carrying luggage
