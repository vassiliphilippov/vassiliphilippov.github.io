yupster scene

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʌpstər ˌsiːn / (AmE), / ˈjʌpstə ˌsiːn / (BrE)
A trendy urban lifestyle or social environment associated with young professionals who blend the attitudes of yuppies with hipster tastes.
trendy young professional urban culture
informal slang

Examples

She felt out of place in the yupster scene that dominated her new neighborhood.
Many new cafes cater to the tastes of the yupster scene in the downtown area.
As rents rose, the yupster scene began to displace long-time residents.

Synset Examples

They laughed at how the yupster scene was taking over the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hipster scene - social scene characterized by hipster culture

Etymology

Blend of 'yuppie' (young urban professional) and 'hipster.'
