bus plunge

1. NOUN

/ bʌs plʌndʒ / (AmE), / bʌs plʌndʒ / (BrE)
a serious accident in which a bus suddenly and dangerously falls from a height, such as off a bridge or cliff
serious accident where a bus falls from a height

Examples

The news reported a fatal bus plunge in the mountains this morning.
Emergency crews arrived quickly at the scene of the bus plunge.
The survivors of the bus plunge recounted their harrowing experience to journalists.

Synset Examples

Authorities investigated the bus plunge that happened last night.
Survivors described the terrifying moments of the bus plunge.
