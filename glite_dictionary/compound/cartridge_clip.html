cartridge clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ klɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ klɪp / (BrE)
a metal frame or container that holds cartridges together for loading into a firearm, especially an automatic gun
holder for cartridges in a firearm

Examples

The soldier quickly loaded a new cartridge clip into his rifle during the drill.
Make sure the cartridge clip is securely inserted before firing the weapon.
He found an old cartridge clip while cleaning out the attic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magazine - a chamber for holding cartridges to be fed into the gun's chamber
