towel ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊəl rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəl rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular hoop attached to a wall for hanging a towel
a circular hoop for holding a towel

Examples

She hung the hand towel neatly on the towel ring by the sink.
We installed a new towel ring in the guest bathroom for convenience.
The chrome towel ring matches the rest of the bathroom fixtures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel rail - a bar attached to a wall for hanging towels
