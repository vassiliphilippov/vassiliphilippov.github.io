animal virus

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a virus that infects animals, as opposed to plants or bacteria
virus that infects animals
medical

Examples

The animal virus quickly spread among the livestock, causing concern for local farmers.
Researchers are studying how an animal virus can sometimes cross over to infect humans.
Vaccines are often developed to control outbreaks of dangerous animal viruses in agricultural settings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bacteriophage - a virus that infects bacteria
  • Degree Lower: plant virus - a virus that infects plants
  • Synonym: virus - an infectious agent that replicates inside living cells
