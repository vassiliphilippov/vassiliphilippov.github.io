motor inn

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ɪn / (BrE)
A hotel designed for motorists, typically providing direct access from rooms to a parking area.
a hotel for motorists with parking access

Examples

We decided to stay at a motor inn just off the highway for convenience.
The motor inn had free parking right outside every room.
Many travelers prefer a motor inn for easy access to their vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motel - a hotel for motorists, usually with parking outside rooms
