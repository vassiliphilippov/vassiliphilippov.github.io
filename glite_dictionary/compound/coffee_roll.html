coffee roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi roʊl / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi rəʊl / (BrE)
A sweet yeast-raised roll, often flavored with spices, raisins, or nuts, and sometimes finished with a glaze, traditionally served with coffee.
sweet, yeasted pastry roll often served with coffee

Examples

I enjoyed a freshly baked coffee roll with my morning tea.
The bakery sells coffee rolls filled with raisins and topped with icing.
He always orders a coffee roll to go with his latte at the café.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinnamon roll - a sweet roll with cinnamon filling and icing
