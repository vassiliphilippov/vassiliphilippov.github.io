word division

1. NOUN

/ wɝːd dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / wɜːd dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the separation of a word into parts, especially when breaking a word at the end of a line in writing or printing
breaking a word at line end

Examples

Proper word division is important for readability in books and newspapers.
He checked the dictionary to ensure the word division at the line break was correct.
The software automatically determines where word division should occur in your document.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyphenation - the use of hyphens to divide a word at the end of a line
