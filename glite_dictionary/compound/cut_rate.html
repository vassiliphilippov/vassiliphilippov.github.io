cut rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌt ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkʌt ˌreɪt / (BrE)
A price that is lower than the usual or standard price.
a lower-than-standard price

Examples

The store offered televisions at a cut rate during the holiday sale.
You can often find last season's clothing at a cut rate.
He bought his new car at a cut rate because it was a year old.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount - a reduction from the usual cost of something
  • Antonym: premium - a price that is higher than the standard
