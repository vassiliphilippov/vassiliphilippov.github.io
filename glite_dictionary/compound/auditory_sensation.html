auditory sensation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔdəˌtɔri sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪt(ə)ri sɛnˈseɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the subjective experience of hearing a sound or sounds
the experience of hearing

Examples

The sudden auditory sensation startled her as she walked through the dark corridor.
Some people experience an auditory sensation even in complete silence, a phenomenon known as tinnitus.
The test measures a child's response to an auditory sensation using various tones and frequencies.

Synset Examples

he strained to hear the faint sounds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hearing - the faculty or sense by which sound is perceived
