percussion section

1. NOUN

/ pərˈkʌʃən ˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / pəˈkʌʃən ˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the part of a band or orchestra consisting of musicians who play percussion instruments, such as drums, cymbals, and xylophones
part of a band/orchestra with percussion instruments

Examples

The percussion section added excitement to the concert with their energetic rhythms.
Our school's orchestra has an impressive percussion section featuring a wide range of instruments.
During rehearsal, the conductor focused on timing with the percussion section to synchronize the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: string section - part of an orchestra consisting of string instruments
  • Synonym: brass section - part of an orchestra or band consisting of brass instruments
  • Synonym: woodwind section - part of an orchestra consisting of woodwind instruments
