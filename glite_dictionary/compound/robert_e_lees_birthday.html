Robert E Lee's Birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːbərt i ˈliːz ˈbɜːrθ.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbət iː ˈliːz ˈbɜːθ.deɪ / (BrE)
a holiday observed on January 19 in some southern U.S. states to commemorate the birth of Confederate General Robert E. Lee
U.S. holiday marking Robert E. Lee's birth
american

Examples

Robert E Lee's Birthday is observed in several southern states as a public holiday each January.
Some schools and government offices close to honor Robert E Lee's Birthday.
Controversy sometimes arises around the celebration of Robert E Lee's Birthday due to its historical associations.
