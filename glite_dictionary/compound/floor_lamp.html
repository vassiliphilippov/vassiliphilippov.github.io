floor lamp

1. NOUN

/ flɔr læmp / (AmE), / flɔː læmp / (BrE)
a tall lamp designed to stand on the floor and provide lighting to a room
a tall lamp standing on the floor

Examples

She placed a floor lamp next to the sofa to brighten the living room.
The new floor lamp has a dimmer switch for adjustable lighting.
We bought a modern floor lamp to match our minimalist decor.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: table lamp - a lamp designed to stand on a table
