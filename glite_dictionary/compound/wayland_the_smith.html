Wayland the Smith

1. PROPN

/ ˈweɪ.lənd ðə smɪθ / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.lənd ðə smɪθ / (BrE)
a legendary blacksmith and king of the elves in European mythology, known for his supernatural skills and identified with Norse Volund
legendary smith in European mythology

Examples

The legend of Wayland the Smith is celebrated in many medieval tales and ballads.
Local folklore claims that Wayland the Smith once lived near the ancient burial mounds.
In Norse mythology, Wayland the Smith was renowned for crafting magical weapons and treasures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Volund - mythological Norse smith identified with Wayland

Etymology

Old English 'Wēland', related to Old Norse Völundr, a mythological smith.
