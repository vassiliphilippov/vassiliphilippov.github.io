cutting off

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtɪŋ ˈɔf / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ ˈɒf / (BrE)
the act of removing something by cutting; severance by means of cutting
the act of removing by cutting

Examples

The cutting off of the branch was necessary to save the tree.
After the cutting off of electricity, the whole neighborhood was left in darkness.
He regretted the cutting off of communication with his old friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: severance - the act of cutting off or separating
