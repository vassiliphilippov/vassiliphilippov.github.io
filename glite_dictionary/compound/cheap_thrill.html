cheap thrill

1. NOUN

/ tʃip θrɪl / (AmE), / tʃiːp θrɪl / (BrE)
an experience or activity that provides excitement or pleasure at little cost or effort
inexpensive source of excitement

Examples

He gets a cheap thrill from playing practical jokes on his friends.
Some people look for a cheap thrill in every new experience they try.
Riding the bus just to listen to strangers' conversations can be a cheap thrill.

Synset Examples

Riding the roller coaster was a cheap thrill for the kids.
Watching horror movies alone at night is my cheap thrill.
Jumping off the diving board gave me a cheap thrill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrill - a sudden feeling of excitement and pleasure
