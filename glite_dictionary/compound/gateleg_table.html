gateleg table

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪtˌlɛɡ ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtlɛɡ ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
A table with one or more hinged drop leaves, supported by legs that swing out like gates.
table with drop leaves and hinged legs

Examples

The antique gateleg table in the dining room can be extended to seat eight people.
She folded down the leaves of the gateleg table to save space in the kitchen.
A gateleg table is perfect for small apartments due to its versatile design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop-leaf table - table with hinged leaves that can be lowered

Etymology

Named for the gate-like action of the swinging legs.
