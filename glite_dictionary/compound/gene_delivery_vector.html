gene delivery vector

1. NOUN

/ dʒin dɪˈlɪvəri ˈvɛktɚ / (AmE), / dʒiːn dɪˈlɪvəri ˈvɛktə / (BrE)
a vehicle or mechanism used to transfer genetic material into cells, typically in research or therapy
vehicle for transferring genes into cells
medical

Examples

The scientist used a gene delivery vector to introduce the therapeutic gene into the patient's cells.
Selecting the most efficient gene delivery vector is crucial for successful gene therapy.
Viral gene delivery vectors are often used because of their ability to enter host cells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vector - an organism or vehicle that transmits genetic material or disease
