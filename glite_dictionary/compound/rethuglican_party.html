Rethuglican party

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈθʌɡlɪkən ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / rɪˈθʌɡlɪkən ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A pejorative slang term for the Republican Party in the United States, implying thuggish behavior or policies.
pejorative term for the U.S. Republican Party
slang offensive american

Examples

Some commentators argue that the Rethuglican party has lost touch with its original values.
She refuses to vote for any candidate associated with the Rethuglican party.
The term Rethuglican party is often used by critics in online debates.

Synset Examples

Many voters are frustrated with the Rethuglican party's policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Republican Party - one of the two main political parties in the United States

Etymology

Blend of 'Republican' and 'thug'.
