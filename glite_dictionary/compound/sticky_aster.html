sticky aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈstɪki ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a wild aster with leafy stems and flower heads with narrow, bright reddish-lavender or purple rays, native to western Colorado to Arizona
a wild aster of the western US

Examples

The sticky aster blooms in late summer across the hills of Arizona.
Hikers admired the brilliant purple rays of the sticky aster along the trail.
Botanists are studying how the sticky aster adapts to dry, rocky soils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the daisy family with star-shaped flower heads
