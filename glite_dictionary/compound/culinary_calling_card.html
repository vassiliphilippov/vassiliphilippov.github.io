culinary calling card

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɪˌneri ˈkɔlɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪnəri ˈkɔːlɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a dish that serves as a distinctive representation of a chef's personal style or approach to cooking
signature dish representing a chef

Examples

The chef's culinary calling card is his inventive take on classic French cuisine.
Her homemade pasta quickly became her culinary calling card at the new restaurant.
That spicy seafood stew is widely considered the chef's culinary calling card by local food critics.

Synset Examples

The chef's signature dish was his culinary calling card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signature dish - a dish uniquely associated with a chef or restaurant
