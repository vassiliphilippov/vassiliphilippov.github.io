foreign correspondent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrən ˌkɔrəˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ˌkɒrəˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
a journalist who reports news and provides commentary from a foreign country for publication or broadcast
journalist reporting from abroad

Examples

The foreign correspondent covered the elections in South America for the newspaper.
After several years as a foreign correspondent in the Middle East, she returned home.
He always dreamed of working as a foreign correspondent reporting on global affairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war correspondent - a journalist who covers news in war zones
