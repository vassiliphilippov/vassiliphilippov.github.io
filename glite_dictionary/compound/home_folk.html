home folk

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm foʊk / (AmE), / həʊm fəʊk / (BrE)
people from one's own hometown or region; those who share the same place of origin as oneself.
people from one's own hometown

Examples

Her home folk welcomed her back after many years abroad.
He missed his home folk whenever he traveled for work.
The village festival was a chance for home folk to gather and celebrate.
