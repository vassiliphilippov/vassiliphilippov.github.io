retirement program

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈtaɪərmənt ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
A plan or system that allows people to save money to be used after they retire from work.
plan for saving money for retirement

Examples

She contributed to her company's retirement program for over thirty years.
Many people worry about whether their retirement program will provide enough income.
The new law encourages small businesses to offer a retirement program to their employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pension plan - plan that pays a pension after retirement
  • Degree Higher: retirement - the period of life after one stops working
