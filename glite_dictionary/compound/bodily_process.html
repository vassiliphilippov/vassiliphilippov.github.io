bodily process

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdɪli ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a natural function or activity that takes place within a living body, such as breathing, digestion, or circulation
organic function occurring in the body

Examples

Breathing is a vital bodily process necessary for survival.
Digestion is a complex bodily process that breaks down food into nutrients.
Stress can affect many bodily processes, including sleep and immune response.

Synset Examples

respiratory activity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiological process - a process or function characteristic of living organisms or their parts
