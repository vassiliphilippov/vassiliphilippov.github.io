to provide aid

1. VERB

/ prəˈvaɪd eɪd / (AmE), / prəˈvaɪd eɪd / (BrE)
to offer assistance or support, especially in the form of help to people or countries in need
give assistance or support

Examples

Several organizations provide aid to communities affected by natural disasters.
Doctors Without Borders continues to provide aid in many conflict zones worldwide.
The charity was founded specifically to provide aid to orphans.

Synset Examples

They provide aid to various developing countries.
The government is providing aid to the refugee camps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assist - to give help or support
  • Synonym: aid - help, typically financial or practical support
