golden crownbeard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈkraʊnˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈkraʊnˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A coarse, greyish-green annual herb with yellow flowers, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.
yellow-flowered southwestern US annual herb

Examples

The golden crownbeard blooms in dry, sandy soils throughout the desert regions.
We spotted several golden crownbeards along the trail during our hike in Arizona.
Gardeners sometimes plant golden crownbeard to attract native pollinators.
