passing shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsɪŋ ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ ʃɒt / (BrE)
(in tennis) a shot that is hit past an opponent at the net, out of their reach.
tennis shot hit past a net player

Examples

She executed a brilliant passing shot down the line to win the point.
His opponent rushed the net, but he managed a perfect passing shot.
The crowd cheered as the passing shot sailed beyond the reach of the volleyer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lob - a high shot in tennis, often used as an alternative to a passing shot
