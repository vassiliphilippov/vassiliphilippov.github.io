big fat bonus

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ fæt ˈboʊ.nəs / (AmE), / bɪɡ fæt ˈbəʊ.nəs / (BrE)
a large or substantial financial reward, especially one given in addition to regular pay
large financial reward or incentive
informal

Examples

After closing the deal, she received a big fat bonus from her company.
He was hoping for a big fat bonus at the end of the year, but it didn't happen.
Sales staff work hard all quarter to earn that big fat bonus.

Synset Examples

He received a big fat bonus this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bonus - an extra payment given as a reward
