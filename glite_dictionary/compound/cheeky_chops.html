cheeky chops

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiki tʃɑps / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːki tʃɒps / (BrE)
a playful term for a person, especially a child, who behaves in a boldly impudent or cheeky way
playful term for a boldly cheeky person
informal british

Examples

Her little brother is such a cheeky chops, always making everyone laugh with his bold comments.
Don't you get smart with me, cheeky chops!
She called her son cheeky chops after he tried to trick her for a cookie.

Synset Examples

That cheeky chops thinks he can get away with anything.
Look at him, the cheeky chops with that grin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheeky - boldly rude or impudent; showing lack of respect in a playful way
