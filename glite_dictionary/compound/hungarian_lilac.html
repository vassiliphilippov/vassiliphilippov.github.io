Hungarian lilac

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋˈɡɛriən ˈlaɪlæk / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛːrɪən ˈlɪlæk / (BrE)
A central European shrub with elliptic leaves and upright clusters of lilac or deep violet flowers, cultivated for its ornamental value.
central European ornamental shrub

Examples

The Hungarian lilac blooms earlier than many other lilac varieties in the garden.
She planted a row of Hungarian lilacs along the fence for their beautiful deep violet flowers.
Botanists admire the resilience of the Hungarian lilac in colder climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lilac - a shrub with fragrant purple or white flowers
