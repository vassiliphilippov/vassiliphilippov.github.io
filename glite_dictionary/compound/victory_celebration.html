victory celebration

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪk.tɚ.i ˌsɛl.əˈbreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈvɪk.təri ˌsɛl.ɪˈbreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an event or gathering held to mark and rejoice in a win, especially after a battle or sports competition
an event marking a win

Examples

After their championship win, the team held a massive victory celebration in the city square.
The general addressed the troops during the victory celebration that followed the end of the conflict.
Fans poured into the streets for a spontaneous victory celebration that lasted late into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebration - an event or activity in which people rejoice or commemorate something
