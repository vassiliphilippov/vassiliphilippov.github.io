Irish person

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Ireland.
person from Ireland

Examples

The museum features artifacts contributed by an Irish person who lived in Dublin in the 1800s.
As an Irish person, she celebrates St. Patrick's Day with great enthusiasm every year.
Every Irish person I met on my trip was incredibly friendly and welcoming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Irishman - a man from Ireland
  • Synonym: Irishwoman - a woman from Ireland
