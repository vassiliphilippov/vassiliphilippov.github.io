Kamba language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmbə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkæmbə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Kamba people in Kenya
language of the Kamba people in Kenya

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Kamba language.
The Kamba language is taught in some schools in eastern Kenya.
Researchers studied the structure of the Kamba language as part of their linguistic fieldwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bantu language - a group of African languages including Kamba
