diving petrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvɪŋ ˈpɛtrəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
A small seabird of the Southern Hemisphere, known for its diving ability and resembling a miniature auk.
small Southern Hemisphere diving seabird

Examples

The diving petrel plunged into the sea in search of food.
We spotted a flock of diving petrels skimming across the waves near the Antarctic coast.
Unlike most seabirds, the diving petrel uses its wings to propel itself underwater.
