pencil box

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnsəl ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsl ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a container, usually rectangular, for holding pencils and other small stationery items
box for holding pencils

Examples

She put a new set of colored pencils in her pencil box before starting the art class.
My pencil box is filled with erasers, crayons, and a small ruler.
Children are often excited to choose a pencil box with their favorite cartoon characters on it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pencil case - a case for holding pencils and other writing instruments
