long fly

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ flaɪ / (AmE), / lɒŋ flaɪ / (BrE)
a gymnastic exercise in which the gymnast makes a long leap from a vaulting horse
gymnastics leap from vaulting horse

Examples

The athlete performed a flawless long fly during her gymnastic routine.
With practice, his long fly improved in both distance and technique.
Learning how to land safely after a long fly is crucial for beginners.
