minor detail

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚ dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə dɪˈteɪl / (BrE)
an aspect or point that is considered insignificant or not important
an insignificant or trivial aspect

Examples

She spent hours worrying about every minor detail of the event, even things no one would notice.
Don't get stuck focusing on a minor detail when the main idea is more important.
The architect revised the plan to adjust a few minor details before final approval.

Synset Examples

Honestly, who cares about the minor details?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trivial detail - an unimportant or minor aspect
  • Antonym: major detail - an important or significant aspect
