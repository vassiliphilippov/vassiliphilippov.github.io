International Labour Organization

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.tɚˈnæʃ.ə.nəl ˈleɪ.bɚ ˌɔr.ɡən.əˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈleɪ.bə ˌɔː.ɡənaɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations dedicated to promoting workers' rights, improving working conditions, and advancing social justice worldwide
UN agency for labor rights and standards

Examples

The International Labour Organization sets global standards for fair working conditions.
He attended a conference organized by the International Labour Organization on child labor.
Membership in the International Labour Organization is considered essential for countries concerned with labor rights.
