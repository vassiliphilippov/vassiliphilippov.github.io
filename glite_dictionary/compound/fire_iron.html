fire iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a metal implement used at a fireplace or hearth, such as a poker, tongs, or shovel, for tending a fire
metal implement used at a fireplace

Examples

The old house still had a set of fire irons next to the hearth.
He reached for the fire iron to poke the dying embers back to life.
After cleaning the ashes, she arranged the fire irons neatly on their stand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poker - a metal rod for stirring a fire
  • Synonym: tongs - a tool for handling logs or coals in a fireplace
  • Synonym: shovel - a tool for moving ashes in a fireplace
