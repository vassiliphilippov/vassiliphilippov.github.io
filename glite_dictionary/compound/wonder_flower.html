wonder flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚ ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A South African perennial plant with long-lasting spikes of white blossoms, often used as winter cut flowers in Europe and America.
South African flowering plant used as winter cut flower

Examples

The florist recommended the wonder flower for its striking white blossoms that last for weeks in a vase.
Many winter bouquets in Europe feature the elegant wonder flower imported from South Africa.
She was amazed by the long, spiky blooms of the wonder flower displayed at the exhibition.
