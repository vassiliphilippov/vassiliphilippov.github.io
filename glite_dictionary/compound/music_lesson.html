music lesson

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk ˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌlɛsən / (BrE)
an occasion when someone is taught to play a musical instrument or to sing
instructional session in music

Examples

She has a music lesson every Tuesday afternoon after school.
He started taking music lessons at the age of six to learn the piano.
During my music lesson, I practiced scales and a new song.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesson - a period of learning or teaching
