Atlantic sea bream

1. NOUN

/ əˈtlæn.tɪk si ˌbɹim / (AmE), / ətˈlæn.tɪk si ˌbriːm / (BrE)
a species of sea bream found in warm Atlantic waters, valued as a food fish
a sea bream species from Atlantic waters

Examples

Fishermen off the coast caught a large Atlantic sea bream early this morning.
The restaurant specializes in grilled Atlantic sea bream served with seasonal vegetables.
Conservation efforts focus on maintaining healthy populations of Atlantic sea bream in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea bream - a general term for several species of marine fish in the family Sparidae
