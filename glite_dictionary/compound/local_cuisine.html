local cuisine

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
the traditional dishes and cooking styles characteristic of a particular region or area
traditional food of a region

Examples

Every time I travel, I make sure to sample the local cuisine.
The restaurant specializes in local cuisine using fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
Many festivals celebrate the unique local cuisine of their communities.

Synset Examples

Every tourist should try the local cuisine.
Nagoya is famous for its delicious local cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regional cuisine - cuisine characteristic of a particular region
  • Degree Higher: national cuisine - cuisine traditional to an entire country
