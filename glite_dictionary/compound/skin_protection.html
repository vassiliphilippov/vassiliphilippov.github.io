skin protection

1. NOUN

/ skɪn prəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / skɪn prəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of preventing damage or harm to the skin, especially from sun, chemicals, or injury
prevention of skin damage

Examples

Dermatologists recommend skin protection to reduce the risk of premature aging and skin diseases.
Children should be taught the importance of skin protection before going outdoors.
Many cosmetics now include ingredients aimed at providing extra skin protection.

Synset Examples

Wearing sunscreen is essential for skin protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunscreen - a lotion or cream that protects the skin from the sun's ultraviolet rays
  • Antonym: sunburn - redness and inflammation of the skin caused by overexposure to the sun
