dwarf golden chinkapin

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf ˈgoʊldən ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈɡəʊldən ˈtʃɪŋkəpɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub similar to the golden chinkapin, native to the mountainous regions of California.
a Californian evergreen shrub, like the golden chinkapin

Examples

The dwarf golden chinkapin thrives on rocky slopes in the Sierra Nevada.
Botanists are studying how dwarf golden chinkapin adapts to drought conditions.
A cluster of dwarf golden chinkapin was found near the trailhead by the park rangers.
