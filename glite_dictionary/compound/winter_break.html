winter break

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚ breɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə breɪk / (BrE)
A holiday period for schools and universities that occurs during the winter season, with timing and length varying by country.
school holiday in winter

Examples

Many students travel abroad during winter break to visit their families or go on vacation.
The university campus will be closed for two weeks during winter break.
She used her winter break to catch up on reading and relax at home.

Synset Examples

The Chinese New Year is also a school holiday called winter holiday or winter break, which usually last 1 month.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spring break - a school holiday in the spring
