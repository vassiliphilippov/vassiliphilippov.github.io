aerial ping pong

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriəl pɪŋ pɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl pɪŋ pɒŋ / (BrE)
a game similar to ping pong, played with flying discs, balloons, or other objects in the air, often in an informal or recreational setting
an airborne version of ping pong

Examples

The children spent hours playing aerial ping pong with beach balls at the park.
During family gatherings, we often invent new rules for aerial ping pong.
You can play aerial ping pong indoors with balloons to avoid breaking anything.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed a game of aerial ping pong during the summer picnic.
The kids played aerial ping pong in the backyard with balloons.
