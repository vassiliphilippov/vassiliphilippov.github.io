Native American

1. NOUN

/ ˌneɪ.tɪv əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˌneɪ.tɪv əˈmer.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
a member of any of the Indigenous peoples originally living in North or South America before European colonization
Indigenous person from the Americas

Examples

She is a Native American who grew up on a reservation in New Mexico.
Many Native American cultures have rich traditions of storytelling and art.
The museum exhibit highlighted the history of Native American peoples before European contact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indigenous - originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native

Etymology

Formed from 'native' (from Latin 'nativus', innate) and 'American', referring to the continents of the Americas.
