garden spider

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdən ˌspaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn̩ ˌspaɪdə / (BrE)
a common type of spider found in European gardens, known for spinning orb-shaped webs
common European orb-weaving spider

Examples

A garden spider built its web between the sunflowers in our backyard.
Many people are fascinated by the intricate webs of the garden spider.
Despite its large size, the garden spider is harmless to humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orb-weaver - a spider that spins a spiraled, wheel-shaped web
