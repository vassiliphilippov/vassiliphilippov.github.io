loud pedal

1. NOUN

/ laʊd ˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / laʊd ˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
the right pedal on a piano that lifts the dampers from the strings, allowing them to continue vibrating and thus sustaining the sound
right piano pedal for sustaining sound

Examples

She pressed the loud pedal to let the final chord ring out.
The piece requires careful use of the loud pedal for proper expression.
He accidentally held the loud pedal down, causing the notes to blur together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustain pedal - a pedal on a piano that sustains sound by lifting the dampers
