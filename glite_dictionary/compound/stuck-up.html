stuck-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌstʌkˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌstʌkˈʌp / (BrE)
Behaving as though you are better than other people; conceited and arrogant.
conceited or arrogant (informal)
informal

Examples

I can't stand how stuck-up she acts around everyone at work.
He came across as really stuck-up at the party, barely talking to anyone.
The new student seemed stuck-up, but she turned out to be very kind once we got to know her.

Synset Examples

a snotty little scion of a degenerate family
they're snobs — stuck-up and uppity and persnickety

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snobbish - having or showing the attitude of people who think they are better than others
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
