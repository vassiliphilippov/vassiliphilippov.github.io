picker-upper

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkərˌʌpər / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəˌʌpə / (BrE)
something that lifts one's mood or spirits; something that makes someone feel better or more energetic.
something that boosts mood or spirits
informal

Examples

A cup of coffee can be a real picker-upper when you're feeling tired.
After the disappointing news, her friends planned a picker-upper to cheer her up.
His unexpected visit was just the picker-upper I needed that day.

Synset Examples

He always tells jokes to be a picker-upper in the group.
A warm smile is a great picker-upper on a tough day.
Her good news was a real picker-upper for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick-me-up - something (such as a drink or event) that makes you feel better or more energetic
