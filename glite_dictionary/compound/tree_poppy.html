tree poppy

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / tri ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub of the southwestern United States and Mexico, often grown for its fragrant golden-yellow flowers
evergreen shrub with golden-yellow flowers

Examples

The tree poppy adds a splash of color to the dry landscape with its bright flowers.
Many gardeners admire the fragrant blooms of the tree poppy.
The tree poppy thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
