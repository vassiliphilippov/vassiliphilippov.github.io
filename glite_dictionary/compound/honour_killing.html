honour killing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːnər ˌkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒnə ˌkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the killing of a family member, usually a woman or girl, who is perceived to have brought dishonor or shame upon the family
killing for perceived family honor

Examples

The court sentenced the father for committing an honour killing in his community.
Activists work tirelessly to raise awareness about the issue of honour killing worldwide.
The documentary examined the tragic consequences of honour killing in certain societies.

Synset Examples

The case of honour killing shocked the community.
Awareness campaigns aim to prevent honour killing.
They condemned the honour killing as barbaric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - the unlawful killing of a person with intent
