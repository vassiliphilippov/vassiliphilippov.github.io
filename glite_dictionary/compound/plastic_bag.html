plastic bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag made from thin plastic material, used especially for carrying shopping or storing items
bag made of thin plastic

Examples

She reused the plastic bag to pack her lunch for work.
Many supermarkets now charge customers for each plastic bag they use.
He collected the groceries and placed them in a plastic bag for easy carrying.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: paper bag - a bag made of paper, used for carrying items
