ice cool

1. ADJ

/ aɪs kuːl / (AmE), / aɪs kuːl / (BrE)
extremely calm and unflustered, especially in stressful situations.
very calm under pressure

Examples

Even during the crisis, the CEO remained ice cool and made logical decisions.
The goalkeeper was ice cool as he saved the final penalty shot.
She handled the difficult questions with an ice cool composure.

Synset Examples

He remained ice cool under pressure during the exam.
They stayed ice cool despite the intense competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unflappable - not easily upset or disturbed
  • Antonym: nervous - easily agitated or anxious
