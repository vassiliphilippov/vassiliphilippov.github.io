screw bean

1. NOUN

/ skru ˈbin / (AmE), / skruː ˈbiːn / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico, known for its spirally twisted seed pods
a North American shrub with twisted seed pods

Examples

The screw bean thrives in arid environments along riverbanks in the southwestern deserts.
Its distinctive spirally twisted pods make the screw bean easy to recognize.
Many animals feed on the screw bean's nutritious seeds during the dry months.
