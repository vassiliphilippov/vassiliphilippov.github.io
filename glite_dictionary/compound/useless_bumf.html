useless bumf

1. NOUN

/ ˈjusləs bʌmf / (AmE), / ˈjuːsləs bʌmf / (BrE)
Unimportant or trivial paperwork or documentation, often considered unnecessary or excessive.
trivial or unnecessary paperwork
british informal slang

Examples

His file was filled with useless bumf that no one read.
She spent hours sorting through useless bumf sent by the company.
Let’s clear this desk of all the useless bumf before the meeting.

Synset Examples

The desk was cluttered with useless bumf from the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red tape - excessive bureaucracy or adherence to official rules and formalities

Etymology

'Bumf' is British slang from 'bum-fodder,' originally referring to toilet paper and by extension to worthless paperwork.
