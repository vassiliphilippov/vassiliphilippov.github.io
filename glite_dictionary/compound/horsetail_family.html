horsetail family

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrˌsteɪl ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌsteɪl ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of vascular plants (Equisetaceae), the only surviving family of the order Equisetales, commonly known as horsetails, considered fern allies.
family of plants including horsetails

Examples

The horsetail family is known for its jointed stems and rough texture.
Fossils show that the horsetail family was abundant millions of years ago.
Modern members of the horsetail family are often found in wetlands and riverbanks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Equisetaceae - the botanical family of horsetails
  • Synonym: fern - type of vascular plant related to horsetails
