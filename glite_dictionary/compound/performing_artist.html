performing artist

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɔrmɪŋ ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmɪŋ ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who entertains an audience by presenting a dramatic or musical work, such as an actor, musician, or dancer
entertainer who presents live works

Examples

The performing artist captivated the audience with her emotional piano recital.
Several performing artists gathered to rehearse for the upcoming theater festival.
As a performing artist, he travels the world to bring live music to new audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertainer - a person whose job is to entertain others
  • Antonym: visual artist - an artist who creates works in visual media such as painting or sculpture
