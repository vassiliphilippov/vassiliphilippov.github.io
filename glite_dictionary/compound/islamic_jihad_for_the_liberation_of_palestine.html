Islamic Jihad for the Liberation of Palestine

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈslɑmɪk dʒɪˈhɑd fɔr ðə ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ʌv ˈpælɪstaɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd fə ðə ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ɒv ˈpælɪstiːn / (BrE)
a Shiite militant organization, strongly linked to Iran, seeking the creation of an Iranian-style Islamic state in Lebanon and known for using car bombs as a primary weapon
Shiite militant group linked to Iran

Examples

The Islamic Jihad for the Liberation of Palestine claimed responsibility for the bombing in Beirut.
Intelligence reports have identified the Islamic Jihad for the Liberation of Palestine as having strong links to Iran.
The group known as the Islamic Jihad for the Liberation of Palestine was active during the Lebanese Civil War.
