fish meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ ˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌmiːl / (BrE)
a ground, dried product made from fish, used as fertilizer and as feed for domestic animals
ground dried fish used as fertilizer or feed

Examples

The farmer used fish meal as an organic fertilizer for his crops.
Poultry diets are often supplemented with fish meal to increase their protein content.
The production of fish meal helps reduce waste by utilizing fish byproducts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish food - food for fish, especially in aquariums
