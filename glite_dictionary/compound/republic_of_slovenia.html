Republic of Slovenia

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv sloʊˈviːniə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv sləʊˈviːniə / (BrE)
a country in central Europe, bordered by Italy, Austria, Hungary, and Croatia, which became independent from Yugoslavia in 1991
country in central Europe, independent since 1991

Examples

She traveled through the Republic of Slovenia during her summer holiday.
After gaining independence in 1991, the Republic of Slovenia rapidly developed its economy.
The Republic of Slovenia is known for its alpine scenery and vibrant cultural life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovenia - the country in central Europe, also commonly known as the Republic of Slovenia
  • Degree Higher: Yugoslavia - the former federation which the Republic of Slovenia was part of before 1991
