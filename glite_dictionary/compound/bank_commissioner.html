bank commissioner

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk kəˈmɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / bæŋk kəˈmɪʃənə / (BrE)
an official appointed to supervise banks, especially as a state superintendent of banks
official overseeing banks

Examples

The bank commissioner announced new regulations for lending practices this year.
As bank commissioner, she was responsible for investigating several fraudulent activities.
The appointment of a new bank commissioner brought greater oversight to state banks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank examiner - person who inspects banks for compliance and soundness
