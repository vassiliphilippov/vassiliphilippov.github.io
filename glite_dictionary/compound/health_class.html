health class

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ klæs / (AmE), / hɛlθ klɑːs / (BrE)
a class in a school where students learn about health, including topics such as nutrition, personal hygiene, and human sexuality
school class teaching health education

Examples

Every student is required to take health class in their first year of high school.
We learned about the dangers of smoking in our health class last week.
The teacher invited a nurse to speak to the health class about first aid.

Synset Examples

She learned about the vagina in her health class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex education - teaching about human sexuality and reproduction
