vigil candle

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʤəl ˈkændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdʒɪl ˈkændl / (BrE)
A candle that is lit by a worshiper in a church, often as an act of devotion or prayer.
candle lit for devotional purposes in church

Examples

The worshiper quietly lit a vigil candle and knelt down to pray.
Rows of vigil candles flickered gently in the dimly lit church.
She placed a vigil candle near the altar in memory of her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: votive candle - a small candle intended to be burned as a votive offering
