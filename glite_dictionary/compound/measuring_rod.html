measuring rod

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ rɑd / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ rɒd / (BrE)
a device marked with regular intervals used as a standard reference to measure length or distance
instrument for measuring length

Examples

The surveyor used a measuring rod to determine the boundaries of the property.
A measuring rod marked in centimeters is essential for accurate experiments.
Before tape measures, carpenters relied on a wooden measuring rod for their work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yardstick - measuring instrument for length, standard of comparison
  • Synonym: ruler - straight-edged strip for drawing lines and measuring length
