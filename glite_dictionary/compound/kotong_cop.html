kotong cop

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.tɔŋ kɑp / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tɒŋ kɒp / (BrE)
A police officer who is involved in corruption, especially one who solicits or accepts bribes.
corrupt police officer soliciting bribes
slang

Examples

The city has launched a campaign to crack down on every kotong cop operating at checkpoints.
Rumors spread quickly about the new officer being a kotong cop who demanded weekly payments from drivers.
She refused to pay the kotong cop and reported the incident to the authorities.

Synset Examples

She was shocked to learn her neighbor was a kotong cop.
He was known as the kotong cop, always asking for bribes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corrupt cop - A police officer engaged in illegal activities or misconduct
  • Antonym: honest cop - A police officer who is not involved in corruption or wrongdoing

Etymology

From Filipino 'kotong' (extortion or bribe) + English 'cop' (police officer).
