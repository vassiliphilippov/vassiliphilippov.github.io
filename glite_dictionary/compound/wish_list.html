wish list

1. NOUN

/ wɪʃ lɪst / (AmE), / wɪʃ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of things, events, or achievements that someone wants or hopes to have or experience
list of things one desires

Examples

She keeps a wish list of places she hopes to visit someday.
For her birthday, he asked for a wish list so he could buy her something she wanted.
The company reviewed employees’ wish list of changes for the new office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bucket list - a list of things a person wants to do before they die
