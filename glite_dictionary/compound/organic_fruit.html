organic fruit

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈɡænɪk frut / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡænɪk fruːt / (BrE)
Fruit grown without synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or other artificial chemicals.
fruit grown without synthetic chemicals

Examples

Many people prefer to buy organic fruit at the farmers' market.
Organic fruit often costs more than conventionally grown produce.
She made a salad with fresh organic fruit for breakfast.

Synset Examples

I love visiting the buer for organic fruits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organic vegetable - vegetable grown without synthetic additives
  • Antonym: conventional fruit - fruit grown with synthetic additives
