vehicular traffic

1. NOUN

/ vəˈhɪkjələr ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / vɪˈhɪkjʊlə ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the movement or presence of vehicles, such as cars and trucks, traveling on roads in a particular area
movement of vehicles on roads

Examples

During rush hour, vehicular traffic on Main Street often comes to a standstill.
The city implemented new rules to control vehicular traffic in residential neighborhoods.
Heavy vehicular traffic contributes to increased air pollution in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - the movement of vehicles, ships, people, etc., in an area
  • Antonym: pedestrian traffic - the movement of people walking in an area
