Pepsi Cola

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɛp.si koʊˈlə / (AmE), / ˈpɛp.si ˈkəʊ.lə / (BrE)
A trademarked carbonated soft drink known for its sweet, cola flavor, produced by the PepsiCo company.
trademarked cola soft drink

Examples

Would you like a Pepsi Cola or something else to drink?
The restaurant serves both Pepsi Cola and other soft drinks.
He compared the taste of Pepsi Cola to its main competitor in a blind test.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Coca-Cola - a rival major brand of cola soft drink

Etymology

From 'pepsin' (an enzyme) and 'cola' (the kola nut), first marketed in 1898 in the United States.
