Crazy Glue

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪzi ɡluː / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi ɡluː / (BrE)
a brand of fast-acting, strong adhesive used to bond materials together
a well-known brand of strong adhesive

Examples

I used Crazy Glue to repair the broken vase in the living room.
Be careful: Crazy Glue can bond your fingers together instantly if you're not cautious.
The craftsman swears by Crazy Glue for tough repair jobs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: super glue - a fast-acting strong adhesive, often used generically
