rocket base

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkɪt beɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt beɪs / (BrE)
A military installation where rocket missiles are stored, launched, or maintained.
military site for rocket missiles

Examples

The government announced the construction of a new rocket base in the northern region.
During the crisis, access to the rocket base was restricted to authorized personnel only.
Intelligence reports indicated suspicious activity near the suspected rocket base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missile base - a military base for storing and launching missiles
