little pekkie

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtəl ˈpɛki / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl ˈpɛki / (BrE)
A term of endearment used to refer to someone with affection, often implying cuteness or fondness.
affectionate term implying cuteness

Examples

Ever since she was born, Anna has been our little pekkie and brings us so much joy.
He smiled and called his granddaughter his little pekkie after she gave him a hug.
Whenever I was feeling sad, my mother would gently call me her little pekkie to cheer me up.

Synset Examples

My grandmother always called me her little pekkie.
