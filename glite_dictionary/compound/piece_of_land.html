piece of land

1. NOUN

/ pis əv lænd / (AmE), / piːs əv lænd / (BrE)
an area or portion of land, usually considered as a distinct entity
a specific area or portion of land

Examples

The family purchased a piece of land on which they plan to build a house.
After years of saving, he finally bought a small piece of land near the river.
Farmers in the region each own a piece of land for growing crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot - a small piece of ground, especially used for a specific purpose
  • Synonym: parcel of land - a defined portion of land, especially one considered as property
