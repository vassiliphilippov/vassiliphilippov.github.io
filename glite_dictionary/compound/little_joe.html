Little Joe

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɪtəl ˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˌlɪtəl ˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
A slang term for the number four, especially in dice games such as craps.
slang for the number four (dice games)
slang

Examples

He rolled a Little Joe and everyone cheered at the table.
Superstitious players believe a Little Joe can change the game's luck.
The dealer called out 'Little Joe!' as the dice landed on two and two.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four - the cardinal number after three
