golden mole

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən moʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən məʊl / (BrE)
A small, burrowing mammal of southern Africa with iridescent fur, belonging to the family Chrysochloridae.
burrowing mammal with iridescent fur

Examples

The golden mole spends most of its life underground, rarely coming to the surface.
Unlike true moles, the golden mole has distinctive, iridescent fur that shimmers in the light.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect several endangered golden mole species in South Africa.
