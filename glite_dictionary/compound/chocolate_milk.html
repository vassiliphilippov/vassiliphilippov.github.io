chocolate milk

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət mɪlk / (BrE)
A sweet beverage made of milk flavored with chocolate syrup or powder.
milk flavored with chocolate

Examples

She poured herself a large glass of chocolate milk after dinner.
Children often prefer chocolate milk over plain milk as a treat.
I made homemade chocolate milk by mixing cocoa powder with cold milk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot chocolate - a hot drink made with milk or water and chocolate or cocoa powder
