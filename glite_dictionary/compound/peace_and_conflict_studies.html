peace and conflict studies

1. NOUN

/ pis ənd kənˈflɪkt ˈstʌdiz / (AmE), / piːs ənd kənˈflɪkt ˈstʌdiz / (BrE)
an academic field that examines violent and nonviolent behavior, the causes and mechanisms of conflict, and processes that promote a more peaceful society
academic field of conflict and peace analysis

Examples

She decided to pursue a master's degree in peace and conflict studies to better understand global disputes.
Many universities now offer courses in peace and conflict studies as part of their social science programs.
Peace and conflict studies explores both the reasons for conflicts and the strategies to foster peace.

Synset Examples

Peace and Conflict Studies are assuming increasing importance both internationally and within nations as governments and non-government organisations struggle to find ways of resolving conflicts without recourse to violence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conflict resolution - the process of resolving a dispute or conflict
