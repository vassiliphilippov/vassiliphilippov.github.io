meat market for talent

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈmɑrkət fɚ ˈtælənt / (AmE), / miːt ˈmɑːkɪt fə ˈtælənt / (BrE)
a competitive environment in which job seekers are compared and judged, often in an impersonal or transactional manner
competitive, impersonal job search environment

Examples

The job fair felt like a meat market for talent, with recruiters quickly sizing up each resume.
More and more, graduate recruitment events have become a meat market for talent rather than a place for thoughtful discussion.
He was discouraged by the meat market for talent that greeted him at the networking session.

Synset Examples

He compared the job fair to a meat market for talent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meat market - place or situation characterized by impersonal selection or judgment

Etymology

Based on the expression 'meat market' (an impersonal market or selection process), applied metaphorically to the job market.
