red woman

1. NOUN

/ ɹɛd ˈwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ɹɛd ˈwʊm.ən / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) An insulting term for a female Native American.
offensive term for a female Native American
offensive slang

Examples

The book contained outdated language, including the term red woman, which is now considered highly offensive.
Some historical documents refer to a Native American woman as a red woman, reflecting prejudiced attitudes of the past.
Using a term like red woman is disrespectful and should be avoided in modern conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red man - offensive term for a Native American man

Etymology

Formed from 'red' used as a historical, offensive racial term for Native Americans plus 'woman'.
