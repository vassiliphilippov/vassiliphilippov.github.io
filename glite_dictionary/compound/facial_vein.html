facial vein

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl veɪn / (BrE)
any of several veins that drain blood from the face.
vein draining blood from the face
medical

Examples

The facial vein plays an important role in draining blood from the superficial areas of the face.
An infection can sometimes spread through the facial vein to deeper structures.
During surgery, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the facial vein.
