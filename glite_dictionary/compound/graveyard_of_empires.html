Graveyard of Empires

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪvˌjɑrd əv ˈɛmˌpaɪrz / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvjɑːd əv ˈɛmpaɪəz / (BrE)
a region, especially Afghanistan, noted for causing the downfall of several powerful empires through history
region where many empires have failed

Examples

Historians often refer to Afghanistan as the Graveyard of Empires because of its resistance to conquest.
British and Soviet forces both met their match in the Graveyard of Empires.
The documentary traced centuries of conflict in the region called the Graveyard of Empires.

Synset Examples
