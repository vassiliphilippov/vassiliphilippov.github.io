cornflower aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌflaʊ.ər ˈæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌflaʊ.ə ˈæs.tə / (BrE)
an erect perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, with large heads of usually blue flowers
perennial plant with blue flowers, southeastern US

Examples

The cornflower aster brightened the garden with its vibrant blue blossoms in late summer.
Many pollinators are attracted to the large heads of the cornflower aster.
Botanists have noted that the cornflower aster is native to the southeastern United States.
