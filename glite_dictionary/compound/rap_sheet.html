rap sheet

1. NOUN

/ ræp ʃit / (AmE), / ræp ʃiːt / (BrE)
an official record listing a person's criminal arrests and convictions, kept by the police
a police record of criminal arrests and convictions
informal american

Examples

The suspect had a long rap sheet including burglary and assault charges.
Before hiring employees, some companies check if applicants have a rap sheet.
His rap sheet showed several minor offenses committed when he was a teenager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: criminal record - official documentation of a person's criminal history

Etymology

Originally American slang, 'rap' meaning criminal charge plus 'sheet' meaning record; first used in the early 20th century.
