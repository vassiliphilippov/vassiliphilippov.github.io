scarlet haw

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːrlət hɔ / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt hɔː / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree of the eastern United States with few thorns, white flower clusters, and bright orange-red berries
shrub or tree with red berries in eastern US

Examples

The scarlet haw is often planted for its attractive orange-red berries in autumn.
We spotted several birds feeding on the fruit of the scarlet haw during our hike.
Gardeners appreciate the scarlet haw for its showy spring flowers and resilience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawthorn - a type of shrub or tree with thorny branches and berries
