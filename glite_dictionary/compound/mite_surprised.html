mite surprised

1. ADJ

/ maɪt səˈpraɪzd / (AmE), / maɪt səˈpraɪzd / (BrE)
Feeling slightly or a little surprised.
slightly surprised
informal

Examples

She was a mite surprised by the unexpected gift.
I was a mite surprised to see him at the meeting after all.
Tom looked mite surprised when he heard the news.

Synset Examples

She was a mite surprised by the unexpected gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slightly surprised - feeling only a small amount of surprise
  • Degree Higher: very surprised - feeling a strong or intense amount of surprise

Etymology

From 'mite' meaning 'a small amount' + 'surprised.'
