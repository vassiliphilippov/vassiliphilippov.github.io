throughout history

1. ADV

/ θruˈaʊt ˈhɪst(ə)ri / (AmE), / θruˈaʊt ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
At all times in the past; during the entire course of human history.
during the entire course of history

Examples

Slavery has existed in some form throughout history.
Artists have used symbolism throughout history to express complex ideas.
Women have played vital roles throughout history, even if records sometimes overlooked their contributions.

Synset Examples

The term Aryan has been misused throughout history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: since ancient times - from earliest periods to the present
