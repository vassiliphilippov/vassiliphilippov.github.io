gross weight

1. NOUN

/ ɡroʊs weɪt / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs weɪt / (BrE)
the total weight of goods including their packaging, container, or wrapping
total weight including packaging

Examples

The shipping company calculates charges based on the gross weight of the container, not just the items inside.
Always check the gross weight listed on the label before purchasing bulk products.
Customs regulations require declaration of the gross weight for all imported goods.

Synset Examples

Now that the bottles are filled, we need to weigh them one more time to establish their gross weight.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: net weight - the weight of goods without any packaging or container
