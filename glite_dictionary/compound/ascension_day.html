Ascension Day

1. PROPN

/ əˈsɛnʃən deɪ / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃən deɪ / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the festival commemorating the Ascension of Christ into heaven, observed on the 40th day after Easter.
Christian festival marking Christ's Ascension

Examples

Many Christians attend special church services on Ascension Day.
Schools in some countries close for Ascension Day celebrations.
Ascension Day falls exactly forty days after Easter Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Easter - Christian festival of Christ's resurrection, 40 days before Ascension Day

Etymology

From Latin 'ascensio' (a rising), referring to Christ's ascent to heaven.
