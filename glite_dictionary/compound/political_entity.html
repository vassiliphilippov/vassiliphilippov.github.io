political entity

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈɛntɪti / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈɛntɪti / (BrE)
a unit, such as a country or state, that has its own government and political responsibilities
a government unit such as a country or state

Examples

The United Nations recognizes more than one hundred political entities worldwide.
Each political entity is responsible for enacting its own laws and regulations.
Hong Kong is considered a unique political entity due to its special administrative status.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nation - a large group of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular state or territory
  • Synonym: state - an organized political community under one government
