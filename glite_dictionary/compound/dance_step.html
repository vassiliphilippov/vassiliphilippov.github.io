dance step

1. NOUN

/ dæns stɛp / (AmE), / dɑːns stɛp / (BrE)
a sequence of movements made with the feet that forms part of a particular dance
sequence of foot movements in dance

Examples

She practiced the dance step until she could perform it smoothly.
Learning every dance step in the routine took several weeks.
He demonstrated a simple dance step for the beginners.

Synset Examples

he taught them the waltz step

Related Terms

  • Synonym: step - a movement made by lifting the foot and putting it down in another place
  • Synonym: dance move - a movement or pattern used in dancing
