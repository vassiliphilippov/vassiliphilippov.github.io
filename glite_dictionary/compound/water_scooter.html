water scooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈskuːtər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈskuːtə / (BrE)
a small, motorized watercraft ridden like a scooter, designed for recreational use on water
small motorized watercraft for riding on water

Examples

Tourists enjoyed racing across the lake on a water scooter during their summer vacation.
He rented a water scooter at the beach to explore the coastline.
Riding a water scooter can be exhilarating but safety precautions are important.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet ski - trademarked name for a popular type of water scooter
