mid-eighties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈeɪtiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈeɪtiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 80 and 90
ages 80–90 in life

Examples

My grandmother is in her mid-eighties and still enjoys daily walks.
He published his memoirs when he was in his mid-eighties.
Many people remain active well into their mid-eighties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eighties - the period between ages 80 and 89
