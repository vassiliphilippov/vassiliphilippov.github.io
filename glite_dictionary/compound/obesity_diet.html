obesity diet

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈbisɪti ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / əʊˈbiːsɪti ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a dietary plan specifically intended to help reduce body fat and manage obesity
diet for weight loss in obesity
medical

Examples

Her doctor recommended an obesity diet to help her achieve a healthier weight.
The clinic offers personalized obesity diet plans for patients struggling with excessive weight.
Following an obesity diet can significantly lower the risk of related health problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weight-loss diet - a diet intended to reduce body weight
