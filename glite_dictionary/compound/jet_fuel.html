jet fuel

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛt ˈfjuːl / (AmE), / dʒet ˈfjʊəl / (BrE)
a type of fuel designed for use in jet aircraft engines
fuel used in jet aircraft engines

Examples

The airport has strict regulations for storing jet fuel safely.
Rising prices of jet fuel have increased airfares worldwide.
Technicians performed quality checks on the jet fuel before the flight.

Synset Examples

The plane's engines roared to life, fueled by jet fuel.
They filled the tank with jet fuel before takeoff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation fuel - fuel used to power aircraft
