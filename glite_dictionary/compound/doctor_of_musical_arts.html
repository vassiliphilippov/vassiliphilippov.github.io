Doctor of Musical Arts

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktɚ əv ˈmjuzɪkəl ɑrts / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv ˈmjuːzɪkəl ɑːts / (BrE)
A doctoral degree awarded in the field of musical arts, typically emphasizing advanced performance, composition, or conducting.
doctoral degree in musical arts

Examples

After years of rigorous study, Emily received her Doctor of Musical Arts degree.
Applicants for the Doctor of Musical Arts program must already hold a master's in music.
The university renowned for its Doctor of Musical Arts graduates held a special commencement ceremony.
