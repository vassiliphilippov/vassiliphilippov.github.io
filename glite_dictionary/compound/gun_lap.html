gun lap

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn læp / (AmE), / ɡʌn læp / (BrE)
the final lap of a race, marked by a gunshot to signal its start
final lap of a race signaled by a gunshot

Examples

The runners gave everything they had during the gun lap.
After the starting pistol signaled the gun lap, the crowd cheered loudly.
Setting a new record, she overtook her rival in the gun lap.

Synset Examples

The race ended with a thrilling gun lap.
He sprinted ahead during the gun lap to secure his victory.
The final gun lap was intense as competitors pushed hard.
