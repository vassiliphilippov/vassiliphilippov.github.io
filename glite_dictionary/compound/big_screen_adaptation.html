big screen adaptation

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ skrin ˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / bɪɡ skriːn ˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a film version of a book, play, or other existing work, especially one originally in a different medium
film version of a book or play

Examples

The new movie is a big screen adaptation of a classic children’s novel.
Many fans eagerly anticipated the big screen adaptation of the bestselling fantasy series.
She was cast as the lead in the big screen adaptation of the popular stage play.

Synset Examples

The movie is a big screen adaptation of a novel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film adaptation - a version of a source work made into a film
