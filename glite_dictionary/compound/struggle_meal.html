struggle meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrʌɡəl miːl / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡəl miːl / (BrE)
an inexpensive and simple meal prepared with limited resources, often eaten when money is tight
cheap meal made with limited resources
informal

Examples

After losing his job, he relied on struggle meals like canned soup and toast.
College students often share their favorite struggle meal recipes online.
My go-to struggle meal is a peanut butter sandwich when I can't afford to eat out.

Synset Examples

Her favorite struggle meal is instant noodles with veggies.
They had a struggle meal of rice and beans.
During tough times, we often resorted to struggle meals.
