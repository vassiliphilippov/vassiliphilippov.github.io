typewriter ribbon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪtər ˈrɪbən / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtə ˈrɪbən / (BrE)
a long, narrow strip of inked fabric or material used in a typewriter to transfer characters onto paper
inked strip for typewriters

Examples

She replaced the typewriter ribbon before starting her next letter.
The ink on the old typewriter ribbon had dried out, making the text faint.
You need a new typewriter ribbon if your typewriter starts producing pale letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ribbon - a narrow strip of fabric, used here as the inked part of a typewriter
