airing cupboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrɪŋ ˌkʌbərd / (AmE), / ˈeərɪŋ ˌkʌbəd / (BrE)
a warm cupboard, usually containing a water heater, used for drying clothes and storing linens
warm cupboard for drying clothes
british

Examples

She kept the fresh towels in the airing cupboard to keep them warm and dry.
After washing the sheets, he placed them in the airing cupboard overnight.
Our old house has a large airing cupboard next to the bathroom.
