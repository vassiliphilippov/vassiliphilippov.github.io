banana split

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə splɪt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə splɪt / (BrE)
a dessert consisting of a banana sliced lengthwise and topped with scoops of ice cream, sauces, nuts, and whipped cream
ice cream dessert with banana and toppings

Examples

She ordered a banana split for dessert at the ice cream parlor.
The children shared a giant banana split on a hot summer day.
A classic banana split usually comes with chocolate, strawberry, and vanilla ice cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sundae - an ice cream dessert with syrup, fruit, nuts, etc.
