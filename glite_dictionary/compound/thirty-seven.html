thirty-seven

1. NUM

/ ˌθɝːti ˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˌθɜːti ˈsɛvən / (BrE)
The number seven more than thirty; equal to 37.
the number 37

Examples

We have thirty-seven students enrolled in the course this semester.
The museum displays thirty-seven paintings from the artist's early period.
At exactly thirty-seven minutes past the hour, the train arrived at the station.
