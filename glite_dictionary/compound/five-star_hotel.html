five-star hotel

1. NOUN

/ faɪv stɑr hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / faɪv stɑː həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A luxury hotel that has been given the highest rating of five stars, indicating superior service and amenities.
luxury hotel with top five-star rating

Examples

They booked a room at a five-star hotel for their honeymoon.
Many celebrities prefer to stay at a five-star hotel when visiting the city.
The conference was held in a prestigious five-star hotel downtown.

Synset Examples

They stayed at a five-star hotel for their anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury hotel - a hotel offering very high standards of comfort and service
