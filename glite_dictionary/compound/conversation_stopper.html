conversation stopper

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃən ˌstɑːpər / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən ˌstɒpə / (BrE)
a remark or statement that leaves others with no polite or easy way to respond
remark ending conversation abruptly

Examples

His comment about layoffs was a real conversation stopper at the dinner table.
Mentioning politics at family gatherings is often a conversation stopper.
When she revealed the surprise ending, it was such a conversation stopper that no one knew what to say next.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversation killer - a comment that ends a conversation suddenly or awkwardly
