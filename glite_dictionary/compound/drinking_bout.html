drinking bout

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ baʊt / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ baʊt / (BrE)
a period of excessive or prolonged drinking of alcoholic beverages, often without interruption
extended period of heavy drinking

Examples

He disappeared for three days during a wild drinking bout with old friends.
After his drinking bout, he vowed never to touch alcohol again.
The police were called when their drinking bout turned into a loud argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binge - a period of excessive indulgence, especially in eating or drinking
