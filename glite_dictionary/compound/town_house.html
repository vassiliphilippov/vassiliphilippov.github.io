town house

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊn ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtaʊn ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A house that is one of a row of identical or similar houses situated side by side and sharing common walls.
row house sharing side walls

Examples

They recently bought a town house in the city center to be closer to work.
Each town house has its own small garden at the back.
The architect designed a row of modern town houses along the waterfront.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: row house - a house in a row of similar houses sharing side walls
