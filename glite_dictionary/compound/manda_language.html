Manda language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːndə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmandə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in south central India, especially in Odisha's Kalahandi district
Dravidian language of Odisha, India

Examples

The Manda language is an important part of the cultural heritage in Kalahandi district.
Linguists study the Manda language to preserve its unique grammatical structure.
Many children in the Thuamur Rampur region learn the Manda language at home before going to school.
