drug baron

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈbɑːrən / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈbærən / (BrE)
a person who controls an organization involved in the illegal drug trade, typically on a large scale
leader of large illegal drug operation

Examples

The police spent years gathering evidence against the notorious drug baron who controlled the region.
After his arrest, the drug baron was charged with multiple counts of trafficking and money laundering.
Rumours circulated that the mansion belonged to a powerful drug baron operating out of South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug lord - a person with similar authority in the illegal drug trade
