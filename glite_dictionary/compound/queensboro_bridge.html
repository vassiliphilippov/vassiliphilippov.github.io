Queensboro Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈkwiːnzbəˌɹoʊ ˈbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnzbərəʊ ˈbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a cantilever bridge spanning the East River in New York City, connecting Manhattan and Queens
major bridge in New York City

Examples

We drove across the Queensboro Bridge to get from Manhattan to Queens.
The Queensboro Bridge is famous for its unique cantilever structure and views of the city.
During the marathon, runners cross the Queensboro Bridge on their way into Manhattan.
