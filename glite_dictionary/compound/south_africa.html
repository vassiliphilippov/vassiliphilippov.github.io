South Africa

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
a country at the southern tip of Africa, officially known as the Republic of South Africa
country in southern Africa

Examples

She traveled to South Africa to volunteer with a wildlife conservation group.
South Africa is known for its diverse cultures and eleven official languages.
The 2010 FIFA World Cup was held in South Africa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Republic of South Africa - the official name of South Africa
  • Synonym: Boer - a descendant of the Dutch settlers in South Africa
