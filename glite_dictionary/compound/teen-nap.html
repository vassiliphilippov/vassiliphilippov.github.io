teen-nap

1. NOUN

/ tin-næp / (AmE), / tiːn-nap / (BrE)
a short sleep taken by a teenager, usually to recover energy during the day
short nap taken by a teenager

Examples

After a long morning at school, she took a teen-nap before starting her assignments.
Many high school students find that a quick teen-nap boosts their afternoon productivity.
He always feels better after a teen-nap following soccer practice.

Synset Examples

A quick teen-nap helped her focus on homework later.
He took a teen-nap after school to recharge.
Teen-naps are essential for surviving long study sessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nap - a short sleep, especially during the day
