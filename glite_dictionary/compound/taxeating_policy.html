taxeating policy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksˌiːtɪŋ ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / ˈtæksˌiːtɪŋ ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
a government policy regarded as excessively exploitative or burdensome in taxation
exploitative tax policy

Examples

Many citizens challenged the taxeating policy during the election debates.
Critics accused the administration of implementing another taxeating policy that harmed small businesses.
The mayor promised to repeal any taxeating policy and promote fairer taxation.

Synset Examples

The taxeating policies led to public outrage and protests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax policy - government rules concerning taxation

Etymology

From 'tax' + 'eating', suggesting consumption of resources by taxation.
