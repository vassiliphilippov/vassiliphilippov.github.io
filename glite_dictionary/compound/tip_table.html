tip table

1. NOUN

/ tɪp ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / tɪp ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
a small table with a top that can be tilted to a vertical position, usually for storage or saving space
table with a tilting, vertical top

Examples

The antique tip table in the hallway is easy to store because its top folds upright.
She inherited a delicate mahogany tip table from her grandmother.
After dinner, he set the tip table against the wall to open up more space in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tilt-top table - table with a hinged top that tilts vertically
