point of honor

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv ˈɑnɚ / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
a matter or issue that is considered to directly affect one's honor or reputation and is regarded as requiring serious attention or action
matter affecting personal honor

Examples

For him, apologizing became a point of honor after the misunderstanding.
She fought back, considering it a point of honor to defend her reputation.
Settling the dispute was a serious point of honor among the members of the club.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: principle - a fundamental truth or proposition that serves as the foundation for a system of belief or behavior
