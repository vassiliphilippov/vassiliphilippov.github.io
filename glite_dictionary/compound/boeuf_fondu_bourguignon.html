boeuf fondu bourguignon

1. NOUN

/ bəʊ f fɒnˈduː bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (AmE), / bəʊ f fɒnˈduː bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
A dish consisting of cubes of beef cooked in hot oil, typically at the table, and then dipped in a variety of flavorful sauces.
beef cubes cooked in oil, dipped in sauces

Examples

We enjoyed a traditional boeuf fondu bourguignon at the restaurant last night.
For his birthday, she prepared boeuf fondu bourguignon with several homemade dipping sauces.
The chef recommends pairing boeuf fondu bourguignon with a rich red wine.

Etymology

French, literally 'Burgundian melted beef'.
