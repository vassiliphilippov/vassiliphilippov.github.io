soldier-fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊldʒɚ ˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊldʒə ˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A type of large squirrelfish, typically found in tropical marine waters, known for their bright colors and spiny fins.
a large squirrelfish species

Examples

The coral reef was teeming with vibrant soldier-fish swimming among the rocks.
Divers were excited to spot a large school of soldier-fish near the shipwreck.
At the aquarium, the bright red soldier-fish quickly became a favorite among visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirrelfish - a common name for fish of the family Holocentridae
