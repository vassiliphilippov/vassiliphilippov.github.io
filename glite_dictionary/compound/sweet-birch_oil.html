sweet-birch oil

1. NOUN

/ swit bɝtʃ ɔɪl / (AmE), / swiːt bɜːtʃ ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic oil with a strong wintergreen scent, used externally for minor muscle and joint pain relief
aromatic oil for muscle pain relief

Examples

Many athletes use sweet-birch oil as a natural remedy for sore muscles after exercising.
The scent of sweet-birch oil filled the room when the massage therapist began her work.
Applying sweet-birch oil to minor aches can provide a soothing, warming sensation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil of wintergreen - an oil with a similar scent and use, derived from wintergreen
