Nob Hill

1. PROPN

/ nɑb hɪl / (AmE), / nɒb hɪl / (BrE)
A fashionable and wealthy neighborhood in San Francisco known for its historic mansions and prominence.
fashionable San Francisco neighborhood

Examples

Many tourists visit Nob Hill to see its grand hotels and historic architecture.
She grew up in Nob Hill, surrounded by beautiful views of the bay.
The city's iconic cable car line runs straight through Nob Hill.

Etymology

Likely from 'nabob' (wealthy person), shortened in local usage.
