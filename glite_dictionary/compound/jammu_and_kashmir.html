Jammu and Kashmir

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɑmu ənd kəʃˈmɪr / (AmE), / ˈdʒæmuː ənd kæʃˈmɪə / (BrE)
a region in southwestern Asia, primarily administered by India and Pakistan, whose sovereignty is disputed between the two countries
disputed territory in southwestern Asia

Examples

The political situation in Jammu and Kashmir has been a subject of international attention for decades.
Many travelers are drawn to the beautiful landscapes of Jammu and Kashmir.
The sovereignty of Jammu and Kashmir remains a contentious issue between India and Pakistan.

Etymology

From Jammu (a city in the region) and Kashmir (from Sanskrit 'Kaśmīra', the historical name for the valley)
