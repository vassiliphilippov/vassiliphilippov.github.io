tassel hyacinth

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæsəl haɪˌæsɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈtæsəl hʌɪˌasɪnθ / (BrE)
a Mediterranean plant with a cluster of sterile bluish-violet flowers forming a tuft above the fertile flowers
Mediterranean plant with tufted violet flowers

Examples

The tassel hyacinth adds a unique splash of color to Mediterranean gardens each spring.
Botanists are studying the tassel hyacinth for its unusual flower structure.
We saw fields of tassel hyacinth blooming along the rocky hillsides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyacinth - a type of bulbous flowering plant of the genus Hyacinthus
