yellow spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ spɒt / (BrE)
any of various plant diseases caused by fungi or viruses and characterized by yellow spots on the leaves
plant disease causing yellow leaf spots

Examples

The farmer noticed yellow spot affecting several rows of his tomato plants.
Early treatment is important if you want to control yellow spot on your crops.
Gardeners are advised to remove leaves showing signs of yellow spot to prevent the spread of the disease.

2. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ spɒt / (BrE)
a small yellowish central area of the retina, rich in cones, responsible for clear, detailed vision
central retinal area (macula) for sharp vision
medical

Examples

The yellow spot is essential for tasks such as reading and recognizing faces.
Damage to the yellow spot can lead to central vision loss.
Ophthalmologists often examine the yellow spot when diagnosing retinal diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macula - an area of the retina responsible for sharp central vision
