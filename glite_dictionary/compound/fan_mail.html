fan mail

1. NOUN

/ fæn ˌmeɪl / (AmE), / fan ˌmeɪl / (BrE)
letters or messages sent by admirers to a celebrity or public figure
letters from admirers to celebrities

Examples

The actress receives a huge amount of fan mail every week.
He spent hours reading fan mail from his loyal supporters.
Some fan mail includes requests for autographs or personal advice.

Synset Examples

he hired someone to answer his fan mail

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hate mail - letters or messages expressing hostility or abuse, often sent to public figures
