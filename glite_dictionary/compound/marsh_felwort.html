marsh felwort

1. NOUN

/ mɑrʃ ˈfɛl.wɝt / (AmE), / mɑːʃ ˈfel.wɜːt / (BrE)
A perennial plant found in damp mountain areas of Eurasia and North America, with dull blue or violet flowers.
a perennial plant of damp mountains with blue or violet flowers

Examples

The marsh felwort blooms in late summer across alpine wetlands.
We spotted several marsh felworts growing beside the mountain stream.
The marsh felwort is prized by botanists for its delicate violet flowers.
