vernier micrometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrnɪr maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈvɜːnɪə maɪˈkrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A micrometer equipped with a vernier scale, allowing for extremely accurate and fine measurements of dimensions.
precision instrument with vernier scale for fine measurements

Examples

The engineer used a vernier micrometer to measure the thickness of the metal sheet with high precision.
You need a vernier micrometer for tasks requiring accuracy beyond that of standard calipers.
To check the diameter of the wire, she carefully operated the vernier micrometer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: micrometer - An instrument used for precise measurement of small distances
  • Synonym: vernier caliper - A caliper fitted with a vernier scale for precise measurements

Etymology

‘Vernier’ from French, after Pierre Vernier, inventor of the vernier scale; ‘micrometer’ from Greek mikros (‘small’) and metron (‘measure’).
