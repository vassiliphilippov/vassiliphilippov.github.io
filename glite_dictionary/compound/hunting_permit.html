hunting permit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ pərˌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ pəˌmɪt / (BrE)
an official document that allows a person to hunt and kill a particular species of animal during a specified period
authorization to hunt specific animals

Examples

You must carry your hunting permit with you at all times during deer season.
She applied online for a hunting permit before traveling to Alaska.
The park ranger asked to see their hunting permit before letting them continue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting license - an official permit allowing the holder to hunt
