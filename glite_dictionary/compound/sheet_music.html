sheet music

1. NOUN

/ ʃit ˈmju.zɪk / (AmE), / ʃiːt ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
printed or written music notation, especially as separate pages or books, used for playing or studying a musical composition
printed or written music notation

Examples

He placed the sheet music on the piano before beginning to play.
The choir relies on sheet music during rehearsals to stay in tune.
I bought some vintage sheet music from a store specializing in old musical scores.

Synset Examples

she turned the pages of the music as he played

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score - the written form of a musical composition for orchestral or ensemble use
