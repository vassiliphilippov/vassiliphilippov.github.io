whiskey jug

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski dʒʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɪski dʒʌɡ / (BrE)
A jug used for storing or serving whiskey, especially of a type traditionally made of ceramic or glass.
jug for storing or serving whiskey

Examples

The old man poured himself a drink from the whiskey jug on the table.
She collected antique whiskey jugs as a hobby.
A large whiskey jug sat in the corner, half-empty.
