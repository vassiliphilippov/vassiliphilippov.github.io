toper's nose

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊpərz noʊz / (AmE), / ˈtəʊpəz nəʊz / (BrE)
An enlarged, reddish nose with prominent blood vessels, often associated with excessive alcohol consumption.
enlarged, red nose from heavy drinking

Examples

His toper's nose was unmistakable in the dim light of the pub.
The character in the novel was described as having a pronounced toper's nose from years of drinking.
Victorian caricatures often depicted politicians with a toper's nose to suggest their fondness for alcohol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhinophyma - a medical term for bulbous, red nose often from rosacea

Etymology

From 'toper' (a habitual drinker) + 'nose', used descriptively since the 19th century.
