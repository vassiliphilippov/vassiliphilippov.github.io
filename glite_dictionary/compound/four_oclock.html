four o'clock

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɔr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˌfɔːr əˈklɒk / (BrE)
any of several ornamental plants of the genus Mirabilis, especially Mirabilis jalapa, whose flowers typically open in the late afternoon.
ornamental plant with late afternoon-blooming flowers

Examples

The four o'clock in my garden has vibrant pink and yellow flowers.
After a hot day, the four o'clock begins to bloom as the sun goes down.
Many gardeners plant four o'clocks for their fragrant, colorful flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mirabilis - the genus of flowering plants known as four o'clocks

Etymology

From the flowers' habit of opening around four o'clock in the afternoon.
