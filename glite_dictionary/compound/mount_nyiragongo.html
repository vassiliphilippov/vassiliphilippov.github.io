Mount Nyiragongo

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt nɪrəˈɡɑŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / maʊnt nɪərəˈɡɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
an active volcano in eastern Congo, famed for its large and persistent lava lake
active volcano in eastern Congo

Examples

Scientists closely monitor Mount Nyiragongo due to its frequent eruptions and dangers to nearby communities.
The lava lake inside Mount Nyiragongo is one of the most remarkable geological phenomena in the world.
Villagers were forced to evacuate when Mount Nyiragongo erupted unexpectedly last year.
