bumper sticker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌmpɚ ˌstɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌmpə ˌstɪkə / (BrE)
a sticker attached to a vehicle's bumper, often bearing a slogan, message, or image, especially of a humorous or political nature
sticker with message on a car bumper

Examples

Her new car has a bumper sticker that says 'Save the Earth'.
He collected dozens of bumper stickers from different states during his road trip.
The bumper sticker on that van made me laugh out loud.

Synset Examples

She put a funny bumper sticker on her car.
His car had a funny taco bumper sticker.
The bumper sticker on his car was hilarious.
His car is covered in quirky bumper stickers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sticker - an adhesive label or decal
