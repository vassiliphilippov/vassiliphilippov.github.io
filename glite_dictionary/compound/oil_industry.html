oil industry

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of business involved in the exploration, extraction, production, refining, and distribution of oil and its products
the business sector dealing with oil production and products

Examples

The oil industry has a significant impact on the global economy.
After years of working in the oil industry, she became an expert in energy markets.
Environmental regulations are changing the way the oil industry operates worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum industry - industry concerned with crude oil and related products
