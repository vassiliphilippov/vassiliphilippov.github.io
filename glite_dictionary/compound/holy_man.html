holy man

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊli mæn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli mæn / (BrE)
a man regarded as especially holy or spiritually significant, often revered for his religious devotion or practices
man regarded as spiritually holy

Examples

Pilgrims traveled for miles to seek the blessing of the holy man who lived in the mountains.
Many considered Mahatma Gandhi a holy man because of his spiritual teachings and way of life.
The villagers gathered around the holy man for guidance and prayers during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saint - a person acknowledged as holy or virtuous and typically regarded as being in heaven after death
