funny paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a section of a newspaper that contains comic strips or humorous cartoons
newspaper comics section
informal american

Examples

On Sunday mornings, the kids would rush to read the funny paper before breakfast.
My grandfather collected funny papers from different decades as a hobby.
She clipped her favorite comic from the funny paper and put it on the fridge.

Synset Examples

He joked, 'I'll see you in the funny papers if you keep that up!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comics - cartoon strips published in newspapers or magazines
  • Synonym: comic strip - a sequence of drawings in newspapers telling a story or joke
