launch area

1. NOUN

/ lɔntʃ ˈɛriə / (AmE), / lɔːntʃ ˈeəriə / (BrE)
A designated platform or site from which rockets or spacecraft are launched.
site for launching rockets or spacecraft

Examples

The engineers inspected the launch area before giving clearance for takeoff.
Security protocols were tightened around the launch area during the space mission.
The new satellite was assembled and transported to the launch area last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launch pad - a platform from which rockets are launched
