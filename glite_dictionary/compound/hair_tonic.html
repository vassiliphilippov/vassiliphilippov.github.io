hair tonic

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / heə ˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
a liquid hair product applied to improve hair appearance, manageability, or scalp health
liquid product for hair care

Examples

Rub a small amount of hair tonic into your scalp after washing.
He always used hair tonic to keep his hair slicked back.
The classic barbershop scent reminded me of my grandfather’s hair tonic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair oil - oil-based product applied to hair for shine and conditioning
  • Antonym: shampoo - liquid soap used for washing hair
