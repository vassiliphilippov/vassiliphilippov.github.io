creative thinker

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪtɪv ˈθɪŋkər / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv ˈθɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who is able to generate original ideas or solve problems in inventive ways
person skilled at innovative thinking

Examples

As a creative thinker, she was able to find solutions no one else considered.
Tech companies are always looking to hire a creative thinker for their innovation teams.
The workshop was designed to help participants become more of a creative thinker in their daily work.

Synset Examples

the great minds of the 17th century

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovator - person who introduces new ideas or methods
  • Synonym: visionary - person with original ideas about what the future could be like
