plan of action

1. NOUN

/ plæn əv ˈækʃən / (AmE), / plæn ɒv ˈækʃən / (BrE)
a detailed scheme or strategy for achieving a specific goal or dealing with a situation
strategy or scheme to achieve a goal

Examples

Before we start the project, we need a clear plan of action to follow.
After the meeting, she outlined a detailed plan of action for the team.
Without a plan of action, it's easy to lose focus and direction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strategy - a plan designed to achieve a particular goal
