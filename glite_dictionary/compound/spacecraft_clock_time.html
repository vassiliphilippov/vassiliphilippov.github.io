spacecraft clock time

1. NOUN

/ ˈspeɪsˌkræft klɑk taɪm / (AmE), / ˈspeɪs.krɑːft klɒk taɪm / (BrE)
the time indicated by a clock located on board a spacecraft, typically used for mission coordination and data synchronization.
time shown by a spacecraft's onboard clock

Examples

The data transmission was synchronized to the spacecraft clock time for accuracy.
Engineers referenced the spacecraft clock time to determine when the anomaly occurred.
All onboard experiments are scheduled according to spacecraft clock time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mission elapsed time - the time elapsed since the start of a space mission
