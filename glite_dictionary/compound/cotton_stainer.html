cotton stainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.tən ˌsteɪ.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒt.ən ˌsteɪ.nə / (BrE)
a type of true bug that feeds on developing cotton, damaging and staining the lint
insect that stains and damages cotton

Examples

The farmer lost part of his crop due to an infestation of cotton stainers.
Cotton stainers not only reduce yield but also decrease the quality of the cotton fiber.
Researchers are developing new methods to control cotton stainer populations in plantations.
