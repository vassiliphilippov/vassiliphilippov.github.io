slag tag

1. NOUN

/ slæɡ tæɡ / (AmE), / slæɡ tæɡ / (BrE)
A children's game similar to tag, played with its own specific rules.
children's game variant of tag

Examples

The children invented a new rule for slag tag to make it more challenging.
At summer camp, we played slag tag almost every afternoon before dinner.
She quickly became the champion of slag tag at her school.

Synset Examples

We used to play slag tag until the sun set.
The kids played a game of slag tag at recess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tag - a game in which one child chases others in an attempt to touch one of them
