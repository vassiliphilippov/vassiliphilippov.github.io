ground cloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊnd klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd klɒθ / (BrE)
a waterproof piece of cloth laid on the ground, especially under a tent, to protect against moisture
waterproof protective sheet for the ground

Examples

We spread the ground cloth beneath our tent before setting it up for the night.
Make sure your ground cloth covers the entire floor area to keep out moisture.
After the hike, we shook out the ground cloth to remove any dirt and debris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groundsheet - a waterproof sheet placed on the ground, especially under a tent
