manic pixie dream girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪk ˈpɪksi drim ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈmænɪk ˈpɪksi driːm ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female character in film or fiction depicted as quirky, whimsical, and free-spirited, whose main purpose is to inspire a male protagonist to embrace life’s adventures
quirky, inspiring female character trope
informal

Examples

She always felt typecast as the manic pixie dream girl in romantic comedies.
The movie's love interest is a classic manic pixie dream girl who shakes up the hero's routine life.
Critics argue that the manic pixie dream girl trope limits female characters to supporting roles.

Synset Examples

He fell for her quirky charm, typical of a manic pixie dream girl.
She transformed his life like a manic pixie dream girl.
Her unpredictable spirit fit the manic pixie dream girl trope perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trope - a recurring theme or device in literature, film, or art

Etymology

Coined by film critic Nathan Rabin in 2007 describing a character type in movies.
