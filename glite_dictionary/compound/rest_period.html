rest period

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛst ˌpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈrɛst ˌpɪəriəd / (BrE)
a period of time set aside for relaxation or recovery from activity
a pause for relaxation or recovery

Examples

Workers are encouraged to take a rest period every two hours to avoid fatigue.
After the intense workout, the coach announced a short rest period for the team.
During the exam, there was a scheduled rest period to help students recharge.

Synset Examples

people actually accomplish more when they take time for short rests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break - a short interval for rest or relaxation during work or activity
  • Synonym: interval - a pause or intervening period of time
