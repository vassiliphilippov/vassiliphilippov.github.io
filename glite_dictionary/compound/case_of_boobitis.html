case of boobitis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪs əv buːˈbaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪs əv buːˈbaɪtɪs / (BrE)
a humorous term for an episode or state of laziness
humorous term for laziness
informal slang

Examples

He blamed his missed deadline on a sudden case of boobitis.
After lunch, I felt a case of boobitis coming on and didn't want to move from the sofa.
Don't let a case of boobitis ruin your productivity this week!

Synset Examples

He joked that his laziness was a case of boobitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laziness - state of being unwilling to work or use energy
  • Synonym: sloth - habitual disinclination to exertion; laziness

Etymology

Formed jokily from 'boob' (slang for a foolish person or chest) and '-itis' (signifying inflammation or disease), mimicking medical terminology.
