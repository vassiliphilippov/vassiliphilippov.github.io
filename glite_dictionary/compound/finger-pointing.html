finger-pointing

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡər ˌpɔɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌpɔɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of blaming others for problems instead of seeking solutions
blaming others instead of solving problems

Examples

There was a lot of finger-pointing after the deadline was missed.
She grew tired of all the finger-pointing and wanted to focus on solutions instead.
Government officials engaged in finger-pointing rather than addressing the underlying issues.

Synset Examples

Instead of finger-pointing, we should collaborate to fix the issue.
During the meeting, there was too much finger-pointing and not enough solutions.
The finger-pointing began as soon as the project failed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blame - the act of assigning responsibility for a fault or wrong
  • Antonym: accountability - the fact or condition of being responsible; opposite of blaming
