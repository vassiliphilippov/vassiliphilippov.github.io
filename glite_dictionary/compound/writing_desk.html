writing desk

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪ.t̬ɪŋ ˌdɛsk / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.tɪŋ ˌdɛsk / (BrE)
a desk with a flat or sloping surface designed for writing, often featuring drawers or compartments for stationery
desk for writing

Examples

She placed her letters neatly on the writing desk by the window.
The antique writing desk was carefully restored for the study.
He prefers using a writing desk with a sloped top for drafting letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat surface for working at, especially writing or reading

2. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (BrE)
A portable case equipped with writing materials and a surface for writing.
portable case for writing

Examples

She opened her antique writing desk to reveal old letters and ink pots.
He carried a small writing desk while traveling to compose his correspondence.
The museum displayed a Victorian-era writing desk used by famous authors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writing table - a table designed for writing, typically with drawers for stationery
