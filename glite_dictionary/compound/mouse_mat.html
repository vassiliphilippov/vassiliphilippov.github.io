mouse mat

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊs ˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊs ˌmat / (BrE)
A small pad placed under a computer mouse to improve its movement and accuracy.
pad used under a computer mouse

Examples

He bought a new mouse mat to make his cursor move more smoothly.
The company printed their logo on every mouse mat they gave away at the conference.
My desk feels incomplete without a mouse mat under my mouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouse pad - A pad for a computer mouse; chiefly used in American English
