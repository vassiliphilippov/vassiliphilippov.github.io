brompheniramine maleate

1. NOUN

/ broʊmˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn məˈleɪət / (AmE), / brəʊmˌfɛnɪˈræmɪn məˈleɪət / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to relieve symptoms of allergic reactions such as hay fever and rhinitis
antihistamine drug for allergies
medical

Examples

Your doctor may prescribe brompheniramine maleate to treat your seasonal allergies.
Brompheniramine maleate is often found in over-the-counter cold medications.
Some people experience drowsiness as a side effect of brompheniramine maleate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antihistamine - drug that counteracts the effects of histamine
