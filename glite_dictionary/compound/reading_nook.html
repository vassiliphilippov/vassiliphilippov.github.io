reading nook

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ nʊk / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ nʊk / (BrE)
A small, comfortable area designed or used for reading.
small, comfy area for reading

Examples

She added pillows and a lamp to create a cozy reading nook by the window.
The children disappeared into their reading nook every afternoon after school.
He dreamed of building a reading nook in his home library.

Synset Examples

He converted the attic into a cozy reading nook.
The small room was perfect for her reading nook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reading corner - a small corner set aside for reading
  • Degree Higher: nook - a small space or corner
