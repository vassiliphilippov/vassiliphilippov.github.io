Fukien language

1. NOUN

/ fuˈkiːən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / fjʊˈkiːən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the forms of Chinese spoken in Fujian (Fukien) province, especially Min Chinese dialects such as Hokkien and Min Nan.
Chinese dialects of Fujian province

Examples

Many overseas Chinese communities speak the Fukien language at home.
The Fukien language has several mutually unintelligible dialects within Fujian province.
She grew up speaking Fukien language before learning Mandarin in school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Min Chinese - branch of Chinese languages including dialects spoken in Fujian province

Etymology

Fukien is an older English spelling of Fujian, a province in southeastern China.
