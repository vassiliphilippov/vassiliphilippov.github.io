principal investigator

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪnsəpəl ɪnˈvɛstəˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪpəl ɪnˌvɛstɪˈɡeɪtə / (BrE)
the lead scientist responsible for the planning and execution of a research project or experiment
lead scientist of a research project

Examples

The principal investigator is responsible for securing funding and overseeing all aspects of the study.
Dr. Kim was named principal investigator on the new cancer research grant.
Each principal investigator must submit an annual progress report to the university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: researcher - a person who carries out academic or scientific research
  • Degree Lower: co-investigator - a scientist who assists the principal investigator in a research project
