fake-ass smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪk ˌæs smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪk ˌɑːs smaɪl / (BrE)
An insincere or disingenuous smile that is obviously not genuine.
insincere smile; not genuine
informal slang

Examples

She gave me a fake-ass smile when she saw me, but I could tell she was annoyed.
He wore a fake-ass smile during the whole meeting to hide his disappointment.
Whenever her boss walks by, she puts on that annoying fake-ass smile.

Synset Examples

Don't give me that fake-ass smile; just be real.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forced smile - a smile made when not genuinely felt
  • Antonym: genuine smile - a smile showing real emotion or feeling
