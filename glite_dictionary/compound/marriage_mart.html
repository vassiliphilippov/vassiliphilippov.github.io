marriage mart

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ mɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ mɑːt / (BrE)
a business or agency that arranges or facilitates marriage contracts, often by matching potential spouses
business arranging marriages

Examples

A new marriage mart opened downtown, promising to match hopeful singles with compatible partners.
During the 19th century, some social events acted as an informal marriage mart for the upper class.
She found her husband through a reputable marriage mart that catered to professionals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchmaking agency - an organization that arranges meetings between people seeking marriage
