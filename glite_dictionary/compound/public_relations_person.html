public relations person

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to promote and maintain a favorable public image for an organization, individual, or company
person who manages public image

Examples

Our company hired a public relations person to handle media inquiries and press releases.
As a public relations person, she often organizes events to boost the company’s reputation.
The politician’s public relations person worked hard to repair his public image after the scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicist - a person responsible for publicizing a person, organization, or event
  • Synonym: press agent - a person employed to generate publicity for a client
