manual of arms

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn.ju.əl əv ɑrmz / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ju.əl əv ɑːmz / (BrE)
a prescribed drill or set of procedures for handling and presenting a rifle, especially in a military context
military procedure for handling a rifle

Examples

The recruits practiced the manual of arms until every movement was precise and synchronized.
Mastering the manual of arms is an essential part of basic military training.
During the parade, soldiers executed the manual of arms with impressive discipline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - a training exercise in military or other procedures
