baby blue-eyes

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi bluː.aɪz / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi bluː.aɪz / (BrE)
a delicate annual plant native to California, having blue flowers marked with dark spots
California annual plant with blue flowers

Examples

The baby blue-eyes bloomed in abundance across the Californian hillside in the spring.
