Caloosahatchee Canal

1. NOUN

/ kəˌluːsə.həˈtʃi ˈkæn.əl / (AmE), / kəˌluːsə.həˈtʃi ˈkan.əl / (BrE)
A canal in southern Florida connecting Lake Okeechobee with the Caloosahatchee River, forming part of the Cross-Florida Waterway.
canal in southern Florida

Examples

The Caloosahatchee Canal plays a crucial role in managing water flow in southern Florida.
Boaters can navigate from Lake Okeechobee to the Gulf of Mexico via the Caloosahatchee Canal.
Environmental concerns have arisen about the water quality in the Caloosahatchee Canal.
