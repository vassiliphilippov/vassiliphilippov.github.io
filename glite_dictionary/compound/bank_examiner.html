bank examiner

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ɪɡˈzæmɪnɚ / (AmE), / bæŋk ɪɡˈzæmɪnə / (BrE)
A person officially appointed to audit and inspect the accounts and records of banks within a certain jurisdiction.
official bank auditor/inspector

Examples

The bank examiner spent several days reviewing the institution's financial records.
After discrepancies were found, a bank examiner was called in to investigate further.
It's the duty of a bank examiner to ensure banks comply with all regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auditor - a person who officially examines financial accounts
