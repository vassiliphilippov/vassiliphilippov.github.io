accounting system

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of methods and procedures for recording and tracking the financial transactions of a business or organization.
set of methods for recording financial transactions

Examples

The company upgraded its accounting system to improve financial reporting efficiency.
A good accounting system helps businesses keep accurate records of income and expenses.
Auditors reviewed the accounting system for compliance with industry regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ledger - a book or collection of accounts in which account transactions are recorded
