wooden smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdən smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈwʊdn smaɪl / (BrE)
A fake or emotionless smile, lacking genuine warmth or feeling.
fake or emotionless smile

Examples

She greeted her new colleagues with a wooden smile, unsure of how to fit in.
He posed for the photo, but his expression was nothing more than a wooden smile.
During the tense reunion, Anna managed only a wooden smile instead of a heartfelt greeting.

Synset Examples

She gave a wooden smile during the awkward moment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forced smile - a smile that is not genuine, done to conceal true feelings
