research staff

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ stæf / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ stɑːf / (BrE)
the group of employees engaged in research activities, especially in a university, library, or laboratory
group of research employees

Examples

The research staff at the university published several groundbreaking studies this year.
All members of the research staff are required to adhere to strict ethical guidelines.
Funding cuts may result in a reduction of the research staff at the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research team - a group collaborating on research; another term for a collective of research workers
