wild raspberry

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈræzˌbəri / (BrE)
A raspberry plant that grows naturally without cultivation, especially the common European species; its fruit is typically red or orange.
raspberry plant or fruit growing wild

Examples

We picked wild raspberries along the forest trail this morning.
Wild raspberry bushes can often be found growing at the edges of fields.
Her favorite jam is made from wild raspberries she gathers each summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - an edible soft fruit related to the blackberry
