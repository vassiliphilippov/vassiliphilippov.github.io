garbage woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to collect and dispose of refuse
female refuse collector

Examples

The garbage woman waved to the children as she drove by in the early morning.
After years as a garbage woman, she was promoted to supervisor of the sanitation team.
Every Wednesday, the garbage woman picks up the trash on our street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage man - a man employed to collect and dispose of refuse
