Same language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːmi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the nomadic Sámi people in northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula.
language of the Sámi people of northern Scandinavia

Examples

The Same language has several distinct dialects spoken across Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula.
Linguists have studied the unique features of the Same language to understand Uralic language development.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Same language as it faces challenges from declining numbers of speakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sámi - an indigenous people of northern Europe
  • Synonym: Lappish - another term for the Sámi language

Etymology

From the ethnonym Sámi, referring to the indigenous people of the region.
