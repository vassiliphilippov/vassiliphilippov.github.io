thin-skinned

1. ADJ

/ θɪn skɪnd / (AmE), / θɪn skɪnd / (BrE)
easily offended or sensitive to criticism or insults
easily offended; overly sensitive

Examples

She can be very thin-skinned about even mild feedback.
Politicians often appear thin-skinned when confronted by critics.
Because he was so thin-skinned, he took every joke personally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive - easily affected by criticism or remarks
  • Antonym: thick-skinned - not easily offended or affected by criticism
