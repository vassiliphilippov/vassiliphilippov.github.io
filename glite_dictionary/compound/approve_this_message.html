to approve this message

1. VERB

/ əˈpruv ðɪs ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈpruːv ðɪs ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
to formally give consent to or endorse the content of a statement or message, often used in official or public communications
formally endorse a message

Examples

The senator ended the advertisement with, 'I approve this message.'
Company executives must approve this message before it is sent to clients.
After careful consideration, she announced, 'I approve this message.'

Synset Examples

After reviewing the proposal, I approve this message.
The board unanimously agreed, so I approve this message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to endorse - to declare one's public approval or support of
  • Synonym: to approve - to officially agree to or accept as satisfactory
