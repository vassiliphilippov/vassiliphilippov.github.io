Kyrgyz Republic

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪrɡɪz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɪəɡɪz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name of Kyrgyzstan, a landlocked country in Central Asia, bordering northwestern China, that became independent from the Soviet Union in 1991.
official name of Kyrgyzstan, Central Asian country

Examples

The government of the Kyrgyz Republic announced new measures to boost the economy.
After gaining its independence, the Kyrgyz Republic established diplomatic relations with many countries.
She traveled through the mountains of the Kyrgyz Republic during her research expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kyrgyzstan - common short form for Kyrgyz Republic, Central Asian country

Etymology

From the name of the Kyrgyz people plus Republic, from Latin 'res publica' (public matter).
