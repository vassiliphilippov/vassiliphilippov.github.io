family gathering

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when members of a family meet, usually for a social event or celebration
social event for family members

Examples

We're hosting a family gathering next weekend to celebrate my grandmother's birthday.
The annual family gathering brings relatives from across the country together.
Food is always the highlight of any family gathering in our house.

Synset Examples

We made a traditional costard for the family gathering.

Uncle Shmuel always brings the best stories to family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reunion - a meeting of people who have not seen each other for some time, especially family or classmates
