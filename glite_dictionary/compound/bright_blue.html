bright blue

1. ADJ

/ braɪt bluː / (AmE), / braɪt bluː / (BrE)
of a vivid, deep blue color with a hint of purple, resembling a clear October sky
vivid, purplish blue color

Examples

She wore a bright blue dress that stood out at the party.
The artist chose bright blue paint to capture the sky in his landscape.
I love waking up to bright blue autumn mornings.

Synset Examples

October's bright blue weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: azure - bright blue in color, like a cloudless sky
