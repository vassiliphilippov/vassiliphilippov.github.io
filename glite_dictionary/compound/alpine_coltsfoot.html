alpine coltsfoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn ˈkoʊltsˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈkɒltsˌfʊt / (BrE)
A rhizomatous perennial herb with purple-red flowers, often used as groundcover; sometimes classified in the genus Tussilago.
purple-flowered groundcover herb

Examples

The rocky slopes were covered in alpine coltsfoot, adding color to the landscape.
Gardeners often use alpine coltsfoot as a groundcover in cool, damp areas.
In some regions, alpine coltsfoot blooms early in spring before the snow has fully melted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coltsfoot - a perennial herb with yellow flowers, often used in traditional medicine
