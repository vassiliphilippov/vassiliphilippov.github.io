to wave one's hand dismissively

1. VERB

/ weɪv wʌnz hænd dɪsˈmɪsɪvli / (AmE), / weɪv wʌnz hænd dɪsˈmɪsɪvli / (BrE)
to make a gesture with the hand that shows disregard or rejection
gesture showing disregard or dismissal

Examples

She waved her hand dismissively and turned away from the conversation.
When the idea was brought up, he waved his hand dismissively and laughed.
The manager waved her hand dismissively, clearly uninterested in further questions.

Synset Examples

He waved his hand dismissively, saying, 'Pish, who cares?'
He waved his hand dismissively and muttered, 'Bah!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to wave off - to dismiss or reject something with a wave of the hand
