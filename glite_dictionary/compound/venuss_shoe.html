Venus's shoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnəs ɪz ʃuː / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs ɪz ʃuː / (BrE)
any of several orchids of the genus Paphiopedilum that have slender stems and waxy flowers with pouchlike lips, resembling a slipper.
orchid of the Paphiopedilum genus with pouchlike flowers

Examples

The Venus's shoe orchid is prized by collectors for its unique pouch-shaped blossom.
Botanists discovered a new species of Venus's shoe in the rainforests of Southeast Asia.
Her garden features several rare Venus's shoe orchids alongside other exotic plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - any orchid with a pouchlike lip, especially those of the genera Cypripedium and Paphiopedilum
  • Synonym: Venus's slipper - common name for orchids with slipper-shaped flowers, chiefly Cypripedium and Paphiopedilum

Etymology

Refers to the Roman goddess Venus, alluding to the slipper-shaped flower; see also 'Venus's slipper'.
