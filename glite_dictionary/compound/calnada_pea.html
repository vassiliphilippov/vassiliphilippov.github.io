Calnada pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkælnədə pi / (AmE), / ˈkælnədə piː / (BrE)
a common perennial climbing plant found in temperate regions of Eurasia and North America, bearing dense elongate clusters of flowers
perennial climbing plant with clustered flowers

Examples

The Calnada pea thrives in both wild meadows and cultivated gardens across Europe and North America.
Researchers studied the flower structure of the Calnada pea to understand its pollination strategy.
Dense clusters of pale flowers are a distinguishing feature of the Calnada pea in the spring.
