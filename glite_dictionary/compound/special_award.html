special award

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəl əˈwɔrd / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl əˈwɔːd / (BrE)
an official recognition given for notable achievements or contributions
recognition for notable achievements

Examples

The committee gave her a special award for outstanding community service.
He proudly displayed his special award on the mantelpiece.
A special award was created to honor her lifelong dedication to education.

Synset Examples

He received a special award for his service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award - a prize or other mark of recognition given for achievement
