sack of shit

1. NOUN

/ sæk əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / sæk əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
(vulgar, offensive, slang) An object, event, or explanation regarded as worthless, contemptible, or poorly done.
worthless or contemptible thing (vulgar, slang)
vulgar offensive slang

Examples

Many critics said the new film was just a sack of shit and not worth watching.
I can't believe you turned in that sack of shit of a report to your boss.
Please don't give me another sack of shit excuse for being late.

Synset Examples

The presentation was a sack of shit from start to finish.
His excuses were just a sack of shit.
Don't give me that sack of shit, I know the truth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piece of crap - something worthless or of poor quality (slang, less vulgar)
  • Synonym: garbage - something worthless or of poor quality
