quick snog

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk snɑg / (AmE), / kwɪk snɒg / (BrE)
A brief and usually passionate kiss, especially in a casual or spontaneous setting.
brief, passionate kiss (informal, British)
informal british

Examples

They shared a quick snog before leaving for the airport.
She gave him a quick snog goodbye at the party.
Catching a quick snog behind the gym became their little secret.

Synset Examples
