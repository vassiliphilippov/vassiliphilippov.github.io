Ron language

1. NOUN

/ rɑn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɒn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, particularly in Plateau State, Bokkos, Barakin-Ladi, and Mangu areas
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

Linguists have documented the Ron language to help preserve its unique features and traditions.
She grew up speaking the Ron language at home and learned English at school.
The vocabulary of the Ron language reflects the region's history and culture.
