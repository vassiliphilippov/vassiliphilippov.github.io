sussy baka

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌsi ˈbɑːkə / (AmE), / ˈsʌsi ˈbɑːkə / (BrE)
(slang) A playful or teasing expression for someone acting suspicious or silly; means 'suspicious fool'.
playful slang for suspicious or silly person
slang

Examples

Stop acting like a sussy baka and just tell me the truth.
After losing the game in a silly way, he called himself a sussy baka.
Whenever someone acts suspiciously in our group chat, we jokingly call them a sussy baka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sus - slang for 'suspicious' or 'suspect'
  • Synonym: baka - Japanese word for 'fool' or 'idiot'

Etymology

Blend of 'sussy' (from 'suspicious', internet slang) and Japanese 'baka' (fool or idiot).
