electronic mail

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk meɪl / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk meɪl / (BrE)
a system for sending messages and documents electronically from one computer to another, typically over a network such as the Internet
messages sent electronically between computers

Examples

I received the document via electronic mail within seconds of its being sent.
Many businesses now rely on electronic mail to communicate with clients and suppliers.
She checks her electronic mail every morning before starting work.

Synset Examples

you cannot send packages by electronic mail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - messages sent and received electronically between computers
