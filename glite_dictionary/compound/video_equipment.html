video equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdioʊ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
Electronic devices used to record, display, transmit, or receive images and sound in the form of video.
devices for recording or displaying video

Examples

The school purchased new video equipment for their media studies program.
She set up her video equipment before interviewing the artist.
Advancements in video equipment have made filmmaking more accessible to amateurs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: audio equipment - devices for recording, transmitting, or reproducing sound
