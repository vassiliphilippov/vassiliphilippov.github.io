party girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
an attractive young woman who attends parties, often professionally, to entertain or socialize with men
young woman hired to entertain at parties

Examples

The club owner hired a party girl to make the event more lively.
Rumors spread about his involvement with a party girl at the exclusive gathering.
She worked as a party girl in several upscale nightclubs throughout the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escort - a person, often a woman, hired to accompany someone socially
  • Synonym: party boy - a man who frequently attends parties for entertainment or professional reasons

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who frequently seeks out social events and nightlife for enjoyment; a woman known for lively, outgoing behavior at parties and gatherings
woman who loves parties and nightlife

Examples

She had a reputation as the party girl who could never miss a night out.
Although she worked hard during the week, on weekends she transformed into a true party girl.
Despite being labeled a party girl, she also maintained excellent grades in college.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew her as the party girl of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialite - a person who is well known in fashionable society and attends many social events
  • Antonym: homebody - a person who prefers staying at home rather than going out to social events
