ancient shrine

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪnʃənt ʃraɪn / (AmE), / ˈeɪnʃənt ʃraɪn / (BrE)
a historical place of worship that dates back to ancient times, often of cultural, religious, or archaeological significance
historical place of worship

Examples

The pilgrims gathered at the ancient shrine to perform their rituals.
Archaeologists have studied the ancient shrine for clues about early human civilization.
Many locals believe the ancient shrine has healing powers.

Synset Examples

The ancient shrine attracted many tourists each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy due to its associations with divinity or a sacred person or relic
