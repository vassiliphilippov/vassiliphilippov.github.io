building complex

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldɪŋ ˈkɑmplɛks / (AmE), / ˈbɪldɪŋ ˈkɒmpleks / (BrE)
a group of interconnected or related buildings forming a single structural unit or serving a common purpose
group of related buildings

Examples

The new hospital building complex includes a surgery wing, emergency room, and outpatient clinic.
Several companies share office space within the same building complex downtown.
A security guard monitors the entrance to the building complex at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campus - the grounds and buildings of a university or college
  • Synonym: complex - a group of similar buildings or facilities located near each other
