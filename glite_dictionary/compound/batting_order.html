batting order

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾɪŋ ˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ ˌɔːdə / (BrE)
(in baseball) the sequence in which a team's players are scheduled to bat during a game.
the order of batters in baseball

Examples

The coach changed the batting order to give the new player a chance at the top.
She memorized the batting order before the game started.
Their best hitter was moved up in the batting order for today's match.

Synset Examples

the managers presented their cards to the umpire at home plate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lineup - the arrangement or list of players in a team
