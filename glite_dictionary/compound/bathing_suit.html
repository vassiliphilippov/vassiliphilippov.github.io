bathing suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ suːt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ sjuːt / (BrE)
a piece of clothing worn for swimming, typically made of tight-fitting material
swimming garment

Examples

She packed her favorite bathing suit for the beach vacation.
The hotel pool requires all guests to wear a bathing suit.
After hours in the sun, his bathing suit was almost dry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming
  • Degree Lower: bikini - a two-piece swimsuit for women
  • Degree Lower: trunks - shorts worn for swimming, typically by men
