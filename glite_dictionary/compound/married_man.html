married man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærid mæn / (AmE), / ˈmærid mæn / (BrE)
a man who is legally married, especially in relation to his wife
man who is married

Examples

The married man wore a gold wedding ring on his left hand.
She discovered that she had fallen in love with a married man.
As a married man, he prioritized his family's needs over his own.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: husband - a married man, especially in relation to his wife
  • Antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man
