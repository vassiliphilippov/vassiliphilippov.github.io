cardioid mike

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrdiˌɔɪd ˈmaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪɔɪd ˈmaɪk / (BrE)
A microphone that is most sensitive to sound coming from the front and sides, with a heart-shaped (cardioid) pickup pattern.
microphone with heart-shaped pickup pattern

Examples

The recording engineer recommended using a cardioid mike to reduce background noise during the interview.
She set up a cardioid mike to capture the singer’s voice with minimal room echo.
Most podcasts rely on a cardioid mike for its ability to focus on the speaker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardioid microphone - a microphone with a heart-shaped directional sensitivity pattern
  • Antonym: omnidirectional microphone - a microphone that picks up sound equally from all directions

Etymology

From 'cardioid', meaning heart-shaped, and 'mike', a shortening of 'microphone'.
