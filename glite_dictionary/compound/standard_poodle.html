standard poodle

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændərd ˈpuːdəl / (AmE), / ˈstændəd ˈpuːdl̩ / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized poodle, known for its intelligence, elegance, and curly coat, often used as a companion dog or in dog shows
breed of medium-sized poodle

Examples

The standard poodle excelled in obedience training and always impressed the judges.
She adopted a standard poodle because of the breed's friendly temperament and low shedding.
At the dog park, three standard poodles played together with remarkable energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poodle - a type of dog breed known for its curly coat and intelligence
