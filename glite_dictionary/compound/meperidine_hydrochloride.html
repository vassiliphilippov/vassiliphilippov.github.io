meperidine hydrochloride

1. NOUN

/ məˈpɛrəˌdin ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / mɛˈpɛrɪdiːn ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a synthetic narcotic drug used to treat moderate to severe pain, known by the trade name Demerol
synthetic narcotic pain medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed meperidine hydrochloride for the patient's post-surgical pain.
Because of her allergy to morphine, she was given meperidine hydrochloride as an alternative.
Proper disposal of meperidine hydrochloride is essential due to its controlled substance status.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Demerol - trade name for meperidine hydrochloride
  • Synonym: morphine - a natural narcotic painkiller
  • Antonym: naloxone - a drug used to counteract narcotic overdose

Etymology

From 'meperidine' (a synthetic opioid) and 'hydrochloride' (its salt form).
