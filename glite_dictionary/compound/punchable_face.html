punchable face

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌntʃəbəl feɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃəbəl feɪs / (BrE)
a face that arouses strong irritation or provokes a desire to hit the person
face provoking irritation or hostility
informal

Examples

He has such a punchable face that it's hard to ignore his smug expression.
Her constant smirk and punchable face made her unpopular in the office.
Some comedians joke about politicians with particularly punchable faces in their routines.

Synset Examples

She has a punchable face that annoys everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoying - causing irritation or annoyance
  • Synonym: unpleasant - not pleasing or agreeable
