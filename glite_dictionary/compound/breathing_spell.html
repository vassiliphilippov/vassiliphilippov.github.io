breathing spell

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriːðɪŋ spɛl / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ spel / (BrE)
a short period of rest or relief from work or difficulty
a brief period of rest or relief

Examples

After hours of negotiations, the committee took a breathing spell before resuming their discussion.
He used the breathing spell between projects to catch up on sleep.
The team welcomed the breathing spell granted by the unexpected delay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respite - a short period of relief from something difficult or unpleasant
  • Synonym: break - a short rest period during work or activity
