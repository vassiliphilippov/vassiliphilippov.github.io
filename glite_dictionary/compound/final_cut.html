final cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl kʌt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl kʌt / (BrE)
the final edited version of a film or movie as approved officially by the director and producer
the approved final version of a film

Examples

The director fought hard to ensure he retained the final cut of his film.
After months of editing, the final cut was finally ready for the premiere.
Studio executives sometimes demand changes even after seeing the final cut.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: rough cut - an early edited version of a film before final changes

2. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl kʌt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl kʌt / (BrE)
the finished and approved version of a film or video, ready for distribution or release
completed version of a film or video

Examples

After months of editing, the movie reached its final cut and was ready for theaters.
She was disappointed when her scene was left out of the final cut.
Only the director has the authority to approve the final cut of the documentary.

Synset Examples

The director approved the final cut of the film.
She was thrilled to see her work in the final cut.

Related Terms
