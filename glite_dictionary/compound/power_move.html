power move

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər muːv / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə muːv / (BrE)
a bold or strategic action taken to gain advantage or demonstrate strength, influence, or control
bold action to gain advantage or control

Examples

Quitting without notice was considered a power move by her colleagues.
Negotiating for a higher position during the meeting was his signature power move.
Releasing the information early proved to be a power move in the competition.

Synset Examples

Quitting without notice was a bold power move.
He made a power move by negotiating a better salary.
Launching the campaign early was a strategic power move.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assertive action - an action done confidently to assert oneself
  • Antonym: weakness - lack of strength or power, opposite quality
