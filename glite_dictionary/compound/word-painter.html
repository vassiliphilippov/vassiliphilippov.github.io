word-painter

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːd ˌpeɪntɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːd ˌpeɪntə / (BrE)
A writer who is skilled at creating vivid or picturesque descriptions in writing.
writer with vivid descriptive skill

Examples

The novel's author is a true word-painter, bringing every scene vibrantly to life.
Critics praised her as a word-painter capable of making settings feel almost real.
With the imagination of a word-painter, he turned simple moments into unforgettable images.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wordsmith - a skilled user of words
