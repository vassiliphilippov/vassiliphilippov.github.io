at play

1. ADJ

/ æt pleɪ / (AmE), / æt pleɪ / (BrE)
having an influence or being involved in a situation or process
involved or influential

Examples

There are several complex factors at play in this negotiation.
We don't yet understand all the dynamics at play in climate change.
During the crisis, multiple interests were clearly at play.

Synset Examples

There are many factors at play in this situation.
The kids are at play in the backyard.
Creativity was at play during the brainstorming session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: involved - participating in or connected with a situation
  • Synonym: at work - having an effect or influence; exerting force
