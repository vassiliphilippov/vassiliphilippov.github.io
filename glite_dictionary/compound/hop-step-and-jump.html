hop-step-and-jump

1. NOUN

/ hɑp stɛp ənd dʒʌmp / (AmE), / hɒp stɛp ənd dʒʌmp / (BrE)
an athletic event in which a competitor performs a hop, a step, and a jump in succession without stopping, also known as the triple jump
athletic event: hop, step, and jump in sequence

Examples

The athlete trained for months to improve his hop-step-and-jump distance.
She broke the school record in the hop-step-and-jump at the championship meet.
Judges carefully measured each competitor's hop-step-and-jump to ensure accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triple jump - athletic event: hop, step, and jump; common modern name

Etymology

Derived from the actions performed in the event: hop, step, and jump.
