classical ballet

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪkəl bəˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪkəl ˈbæl.eɪ / (BrE)
A traditional style of ballet characterized by precise, formalized movements and techniques, often set to classical music.
traditional ballet style with formal technique

Examples

She has been training in classical ballet since she was six years old.
The performance showcased the elegance and discipline of classical ballet.
Many modern dancers study classical ballet as a foundation for their technique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballet - a highly stylized dance form with formal steps and gestures
