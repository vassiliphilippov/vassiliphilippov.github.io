rival club

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪ.vəl klʌb / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.vəl klʌb / (BrE)
a club, team, or organization that competes with another, especially in sports or similar activities
a competing club or team

Examples

The match between the rival clubs drew thousands of enthusiastic fans.
Tensions often run high when rival clubs face each other in the finals.
After years of competition, the two rival clubs finally agreed to host a friendly event together.

Synset Examples

There was a skirmish between the rival clubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opponent - a person or group that opposes another in a contest
  • Antonym: ally - a person or group joined with another for a common purpose
