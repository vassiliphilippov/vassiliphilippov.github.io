cunning linguist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌnɪŋ ˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋ ˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (BrE)
A person who is highly skilled and clever in the use or study of language, often noted for wit or linguistic ingenuity.
clever and skillful language expert
informal

Examples

As a cunning linguist, Maria could decipher even the most obscure texts.
The conference invited a cunning linguist to discuss the evolution of language.
Everyone admired how the cunning linguist could craft puns in several languages.

Synset Examples

Being a cunning linguist helped her with translations.
The cunning linguist quickly picked up new phrases.
He's known as a cunning linguist among his peers.
Her interest in cunning linguistics led to a successful career.
Cunning linguistics often reveal hidden meanings in texts.
He studied cunning linguistics to understand wordplay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguist - A person skilled in languages or linguistics

Etymology

From 'cunning' (clever, skillful) + 'linguist' (language expert); used as a playful collocation.
