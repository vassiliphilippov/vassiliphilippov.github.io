Bosnian-Herzegovinian monetary unit

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑːzniən hɛrtsəˈgoʊvɪniən məˈnɛtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ˌbɒzniən hɛˌtsɪgəʊˈviːniən məˈnɛtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Bosnia and Herzegovina, also known as the convertible mark
currency unit of Bosnia and Herzegovina

Examples

The Bosnian-Herzegovinian monetary unit is pegged to the euro to ensure financial stability.
Tourists often exchange euros for the Bosnian-Herzegovinian monetary unit when visiting Sarajevo.
He converted his savings into the Bosnian-Herzegovinian monetary unit before moving to Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convertible mark - official currency of Bosnia and Herzegovina
