flat-coated retriever

1. NOUN

/ ˌflæt ˌkoʊtɪd rɪˈtrivər / (AmE), / ˌflæt ˌkəʊtɪd rɪˈtriːvə / (BrE)
a breed of dog originally from England with a long, shiny black or liver-colored coat, known for retrieving game on land and in water
a breed of retriever dog with a shiny, flat coat

Examples

The flat-coated retriever is known for its friendly personality and glossy coat.
Many hunters choose a flat-coated retriever because of its reliable retrieving skills.
A well-trained flat-coated retriever can work in both water and on land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retriever - a dog bred and trained to retrieve game for hunters
