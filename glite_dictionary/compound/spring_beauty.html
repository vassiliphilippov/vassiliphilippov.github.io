spring beauty

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A small North American plant with a single pair of succulent leaves midway up the stem, a loose cluster of white, pink, or rose bowl-shaped flowers, and an edible corm.
North American flowering plant with edible corm

Examples

Hikers often spot the delicate spring beauty blooming on forest floors in early April.
She carefully dug up a spring beauty to taste its edible corm, as described in the guidebook.
In the meadows, patches of spring beauties signal the arrival of warmer weather.
