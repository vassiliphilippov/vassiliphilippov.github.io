curly grass fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɝːli ɡræs fɝːn / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli ɡrɑːs fɜːn / (BrE)
a rare small fern of northeastern North America, characterized by slender spiraling fronds and forming dense tufts
small North American fern with spiraling fronds

Examples

The curly grass fern grows in dense tufts along the forest floor.
Botanists searched the wetlands for the elusive curly grass fern.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where the curly grass fern thrives.
