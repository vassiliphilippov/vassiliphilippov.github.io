gun enclosure

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn ɪnˈkloʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɡʌn ɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
A self-contained platform designed to house guns, often capable of rotating to aim at targets.
rotatable housing for guns

Examples

The gun enclosure was heavily armored to protect the crew from enemy fire.
Maintenance on the gun enclosure must be performed regularly to ensure its rotation mechanism works smoothly.
During the assault, the gun enclosure provided critical supporting fire for advancing troops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gun turret - A rotating armored structure for guns on ships, tanks, or fortifications
