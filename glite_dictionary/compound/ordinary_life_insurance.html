ordinary life insurance

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːr.dəˌner.i laɪf ɪnˈʃʊr.əns / (AmE), / ˌɔː.dɪn.əri laɪf ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns / (BrE)
a type of life insurance policy with a fixed coverage amount and level annual premiums, paid throughout the insured's lifetime
life insurance with fixed coverage and level annual premiums

Examples

He decided to purchase ordinary life insurance to guarantee financial security for his family.
Many financial advisors recommend ordinary life insurance due to its consistent premiums over a lifetime.
Unlike term policies, ordinary life insurance remains in effect as long as the premiums are paid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whole life insurance - life insurance for the entire lifetime of the insured, commonly synonymous with ordinary life insurance
