barely legible

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrli ˈlɛdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbeəli ˈlɛdʒɪbəl / (BrE)
so difficult to read that only a small amount can be understood; almost unreadable
almost unreadable; very hard to decipher

Examples

The writing on the faded receipt was barely legible after years in his wallet.
She scrawled a quick note in pencil, but it was barely legible.
The old manuscript's text is barely legible due to water damage and age.

Synset Examples

Her notes were microscopic, barely legible.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: illegible - impossible to read; not readable
  • Degree Lower: legible - clear enough to read; readable
