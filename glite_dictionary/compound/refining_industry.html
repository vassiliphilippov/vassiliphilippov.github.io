refining industry

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfaɪnɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of industry engaged in processing crude oil into usable petroleum products such as gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel
sector processing crude oil into petroleum products

Examples

Many countries depend heavily on the refining industry for economic growth and energy supply.
Stricter environmental regulations have impacted the operations of the refining industry worldwide.
Advancements in technology are helping the refining industry reduce emissions and improve efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum industry - the sector involved in extraction, refining, and distribution of petroleum
  • Synonym: oil industry - industry involved in exploration, extraction, refining, transporting and marketing of oil
