Turcoman language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrkəmæn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkəmən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken primarily by the Turkmen people in Turkmenistan and surrounding regions
Turkic language of the Turkmen people

Examples

She is fluent in Turcoman language thanks to her childhood in Turkmenistan.
The Turcoman language is closely related to Turkish and Azerbaijani.
A rich tradition of poetry exists in the Turcoman language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkmen - the modern name of the Turkic language spoken in Turkmenistan

Etymology

From Turcoman/Turkmen (referring to the ethnic group) + language.
