drinking water

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is safe and suitable for people to drink
water safe for drinking

Examples

During the hike, they ran out of drinking water and had to purify stream water.
Access to clean drinking water is a major global concern.
The tap in this room does not provide drinking water; please use the kitchen instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potable water - water that is safe to drink
