pet keet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛt kiːt / (AmE), / ˈpɛt kiːt / (BrE)
A small pet bird, specifically a parakeet kept as a companion animal.
a pet parakeet
informal

Examples

When I was a child, I had a bright green pet keet that would whistle every morning.
Her pet keet escaped from its cage and perched on the curtain rod.
They bought a new cage for their pet keet to give it more space to play.

Synset Examples

He used to have a pet keet that loved to sing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - a small, long-tailed, seed-eating parrot
