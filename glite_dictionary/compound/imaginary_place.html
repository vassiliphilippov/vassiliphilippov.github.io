imaginary place

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmædʒɪˌnɛri pleɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈmadʒɪnəri pleɪs / (BrE)
A place that exists only in the imagination, especially as described in fiction or religious writings.
place existing only in imagination or fiction

Examples

Many myths describe an imaginary place where heroes embark on quests.
He longed to escape to an imaginary place far from the stresses of real life.
The author created an elaborate imaginary place inhabited by magical creatures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utopia - an imagined perfect place or state
