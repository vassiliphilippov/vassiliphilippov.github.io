extra cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkstrə tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə tʃiːz / (BrE)
an additional amount of cheese added to a dish, especially upon request in restaurants or when ordering food
additional cheese on a dish

Examples

I always ask for extra cheese on my pizza because I love the gooey texture.
Would you like to add extra cheese to your sandwich for fifty cents more?
The pasta came loaded with extra cheese, making it even more delicious.

Synset Examples

I ordered a juicy burger with extra cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese - a solid food made from the pressed curds of milk
