raging tire fire

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdʒɪŋ ˈtaɪɚ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈreɪdʒɪŋ ˈtaɪə ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
an intense and uncontrollable fire involving large quantities of burning tires, often producing thick smoke and environmental hazards
intense, uncontrolled fire involving burning tires

Examples

The chemical plant was threatened by a raging tire fire that sent black smoke into the air.
Local firefighters struggled for days to contain the raging tire fire on the outskirts of the city.
Environmentalists warned about toxic pollution from the raging tire fire near the river.

Synset Examples

They had to evacuate the area due to the raging tire fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tire fire - a fire involving burning tires
