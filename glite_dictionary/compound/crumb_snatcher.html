crumb snatcher

1. NOUN

/ krʌm ˈsnætʃɚ / (AmE), / krʌm ˈsnætʃə / (BrE)
an informal term for someone, often a child or animal, who tries to take leftover food or snacks in a sneaky way
someone who sneakily takes leftover food
informal

Examples

My little brother is such a crumb snatcher, always waiting to grab the last cookie.
At the picnic, the dog acted like a crumb snatcher, hoping someone would drop something.
As soon as the cake came out, the crumb snatcher kids lined up for leftovers.

Synset Examples

Watch out for that crumb snatcher; he’ll steal your snack!
The seagull was a notorious crumb snatcher at the beach.
The child was a crumb snatcher, always eyeing my cookies.
