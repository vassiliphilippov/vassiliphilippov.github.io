in agreement

1. ADJ

/ ɪn əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ɪn əˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
having the same opinion as others; sharing a view or feeling with someone else
sharing the same opinion

Examples

The board was in agreement about the new policy proposals.
Both scientists were in agreement regarding the results of the experiment.
Despite a long debate, they finally found themselves in agreement.

Synset Examples

agreed in their distrust of authority

Related Terms

  • Synonym: united - being joined or in agreement
  • Antonym: at odds - in disagreement; not agreeing
