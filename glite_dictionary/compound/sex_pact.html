sex pact

1. NOUN

/ sɛks pækt / (AmE), / sɛks pakt / (BrE)
an agreement made between two or more people about engaging in sexual activity or behavior
an agreement about sexual activity

Examples

The group of friends formed a sex pact before graduation as a joke.
Rumors spread quickly about the couple's alleged sex pact.
They created a sex pact to lose their virginity by the end of high school.

Synset Examples

Their friends teased them about the secret sex pact.
They made a silly sex pact during their college years.
