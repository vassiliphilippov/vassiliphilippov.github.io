circus tent

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.kəs tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kəs tent / (BrE)
a large, portable tent made of canvas, used to house the audience and performers during a circus performance
large tent for a circus performance

Examples

The children were amazed by the size of the circus tent when they arrived at the show.
Workers spent hours setting up the enormous circus tent before the first performance of the season.
During the thunderstorm, everyone quickly took shelter inside the circus tent.

Synset Examples

he was afraid of a fire in the circus tent
they had the big top up in less than an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big top - another term for a circus tent

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɝkəs tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəs tɛnt / (BrE)
A large tent used to house circus performances and audiences.
large tent for circus performances

Examples

The children eagerly hurried into the circus tent to find their seats before the show.
Colorful lights decorated the top of the circus tent, making it visible from miles away.
After the performance, the crew began dismantling the circus tent for their next destination.

Synset Examples

The circus tent was filled with excited children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big top - the main tent in a circus, especially the largest one where performances occur
