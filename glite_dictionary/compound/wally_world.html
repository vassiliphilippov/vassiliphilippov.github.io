Wally World

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɑli wɝld / (AmE), / ˈwɒli wɜːld / (BrE)
a fictional amusement park or theme park, especially as referenced in popular culture
fictional amusement park

Examples

The kids couldn't stop talking about their trip to Wally World after watching the movie.
Every summer, our family plans an imaginary adventure to Wally World.
Some people use Wally World as a nickname for any big amusement park.

Synset Examples

Wally World was packed with families on holiday.
We spent the whole day at Wally World enjoying rides.
The kids were excited to visit Wally World this summer.

Etymology

Popularized by the 1983 film 'National Lampoon's Vacation' as the name of a theme park.
