life's worth

1. NOUN

/ laɪfs wɝθ / (AmE), / laɪfs wɜːθ / (BrE)
the total value or significance of a person's life
the value or significance of one's life

Examples

He spent years searching for the true life's worth beyond material success.
Many people measure their life's worth by their relationships and contributions to others.
After the accident, she reevaluated her life's worth and priorities.

Synset Examples

He invested all his life's worth into that business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life's meaning - the sense of purpose or significance in someone's life
  • Synonym: life's work - the chief occupation or achievement of a person's life
