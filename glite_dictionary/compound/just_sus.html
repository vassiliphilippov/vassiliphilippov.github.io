just sus

1. ADJ

/ dʒʌst sʌs / (AmE), / dʒʌst sʌs / (BrE)
Used to describe something or someone as simply suspicious or questionable; only suspicious.
simply suspicious or questionable
informal slang

Examples

His story about being late was just sus and nobody believed him.
Those last-minute changes to the contract seemed just sus to the lawyer.
The way he avoided eye contact was just sus to everyone in the group.

Synset Examples

She thought his excuses were just sus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sus - slang for suspicious or questionable
  • Synonym: suspicious - having or showing distrust; questionable

Etymology

"Sus" is a slang shortening of "suspicious".
