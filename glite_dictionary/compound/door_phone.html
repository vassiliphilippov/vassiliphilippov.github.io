door phone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔr ˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdɔː ˌfəʊn / (BrE)
an electronic device installed at a building entrance that allows two-way voice communication between someone outside and residents inside, often used for security before granting access
intercom system at a building entrance

Examples

We installed a new door phone so we can speak to visitors before letting them in.
The apartment building's door phone system allows residents to answer calls from the front entrance.
If the door phone rings, please check who it is before unlocking the door.

Synset Examples

The door phone is connected to a secure communication system used to control the opening of the door giving access to any kind of buildings, offices, or apartment blocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intercom - an electrical device allowing two-way communication in a building
