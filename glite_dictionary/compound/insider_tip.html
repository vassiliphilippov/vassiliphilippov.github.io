insider tip

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.saɪ.dɚ tɪp / (AmE), / ˈɪn.saɪ.də tɪp / (BrE)
a piece of confidential or privileged information, often giving someone an advantage
confidential information giving an advantage

Examples

She received an insider tip about the company’s upcoming merger before it was announced.
Thanks to an insider tip, they bought their tickets just before prices doubled.
He claimed his investment was based on an insider tip he’d overheard at the office.

Synset Examples

Moscow Mike always had the best insider tips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tip - a small piece of advice or useful information
