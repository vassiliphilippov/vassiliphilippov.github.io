Lindy move

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪndi muːv / (AmE), / ˈlɪndi muːv / (BrE)
a dance step or pattern associated with the Lindy Hop style
dance step in Lindy Hop

Examples

She performed a complicated Lindy move at the swing dance night.
Each Lindy move requires sharp timing and energetic footwork.
Learning a new Lindy move can be both challenging and rewarding.

Synset Examples

Her Lindy moves were the highlight of the dance competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance step - a sequence of movements in dancing

Etymology

From 'Lindy Hop', a dance style developed in Harlem in the late 1920s; 'move' meaning dance step.
