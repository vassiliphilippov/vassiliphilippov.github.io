cargo helicopter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ ˈhɛlɪˌkɑptər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ ˈhɛlɪˌkɒptə / (BrE)
a helicopter designed or used for transporting cargo
helicopter for carrying cargo

Examples

The cargo helicopter delivered essential supplies to the remote village after the flood.
Engineers are developing a new cargo helicopter that can transport heavier loads.
A large cargo helicopter hovered over the construction site to deliver building materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helicopter - a type of aircraft with rotating blades that allow for vertical takeoff and landing
