fertilized egg

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝː.t̬ɪ.laɪzd ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.tɪ.laɪzd ɛɡ / (BrE)
An egg that has been joined with a sperm cell and has begun the early stages of development into an embryo.
egg joined with sperm, beginning development

Examples

The doctor explained that the fertilized egg would soon begin dividing into multiple cells.
After fertilization, the fertilized egg implants itself in the wall of the uterus.
Scientists observed the development of a fertilized egg under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zygote - a cell formed by the union of two gametes; the first stage of development after fertilization
