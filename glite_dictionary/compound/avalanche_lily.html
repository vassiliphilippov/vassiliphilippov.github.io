avalanche lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈæv.əˌlænʃ ˌlɪl.i / (AmE), / ˈæv.əl.ɑːnʃ ˌlɪl.i / (BrE)
a perennial herb with large white flowers, often marked with orange, found near the snow line in the northwestern United States
perennial herb with white flowers, US northwest

Examples

In early spring, patches of avalanche lily cover the meadows as the snow melts.
The delicate blooms of the avalanche lily are a common sight in the Cascade Mountains.
Hikers stopped to admire a cluster of avalanche lilies near the trail.
