three-on-the-tree

1. NOUN

/ θɹi ɑn ðə tɹi / (AmE), / θɹi ɒn ðə tɹi / (BrE)
a three-speed manual transmission in a vehicle with the gear shift mounted on the steering column
three-speed manual transmission on the steering column

Examples

My grandfather's old truck had a three-on-the-tree that was tricky to shift at first.
It's rare to find a car with a three-on-the-tree these days.
He fondly recalled learning to drive on a three-on-the-tree when he was a teenager.

Synset Examples

He drove the classic car with a three-on-the-tree transmission.
The vintage truck had a nostalgic three-on-the-tree setup.
Learning to drive a three-on-the-tree was a fun challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-on-the-floor - a four-speed manual transmission with the gear shift on the floor

Etymology

Named for the combination of three gears and the 'tree', a slang term for the steering column in vehicles.
