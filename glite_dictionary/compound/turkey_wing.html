turkey wing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːki wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki wɪŋ / (BrE)
the wing of a turkey, often used as food
wing of a turkey used as food

Examples

She ordered a turkey wing with her meal at the restaurant.
The recipe calls for one large turkey wing to add flavor to the soup.
Roasting a turkey wing takes less time than cooking the whole bird.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turkey leg - the leg of a turkey, often used as food
