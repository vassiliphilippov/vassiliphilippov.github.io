corporation law

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən lɔ / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃən lɔː / (BrE)
The area of law that deals with the formation, operation, regulation, and dissolution of corporations.
law concerning corporations
legal

Examples

She decided to specialize in corporation law after graduating from law school.
Recent changes in corporation law have affected how companies are managed.
His research focuses on the relationship between ethics and corporation law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company law - the law relating to companies (especially in the UK); synonym of corporation law
