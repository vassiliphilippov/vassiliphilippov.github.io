latest tech

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst tɛk / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst tɛk / (BrE)
the most advanced or modern technology available at the present time
the newest technology

Examples

This new smartphone uses latest tech to deliver a truly unique experience.
Our factory has invested heavily in latest tech for automation and efficiency.
Students are eager to learn about the latest tech in artificial intelligence.

Synset Examples

Equipped with the latest tech, the sea rover gathered data efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting-edge technology - technology at the forefront of development
  • Synonym: state-of-the-art - the most advanced stage of development in a device or technique
  • Antonym: obsolete technology - technology that is outdated and no longer used
