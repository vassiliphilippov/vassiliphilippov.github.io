pari-mutuel machine

1. NOUN

/ ˌpæri mjuˈtjuːəl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˌpæri mjuːˈtjuːəl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A computer that records bets and distributes the total amount wagered among winners.
machine for recording and settling bets

Examples

All bets must be placed through the pari-mutuel machine before the race begins.
The pari-mutuel machine instantly calculates the payouts based on total bets.
Advances in technology have made the pari-mutuel machine faster and more reliable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: totalizator - a device for registering and displaying bets and dividends at a racetrack

Etymology

From French 'pari mutuel' (mutual betting), describing the system used.
