for a bargain price

1. ADV

/ fɔɹ ə ˈbɑːɹɡən praɪs / (AmE), / fə ə ˈbɑːɡɪn praɪs / (BrE)
at a cost that is much lower than expected or usual; at a very low price
at a very low price

Examples

We managed to buy the car for a bargain price at the auction.
She found designer shoes for a bargain price during the sale.
The tickets went on sale for a bargain price and sold out quickly.

Synset Examples

we bought the house for a song

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheap - low in price; costing little
  • Synonym: for a song - for very little money
