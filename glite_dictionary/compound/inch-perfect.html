inch-perfect

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪntʃˌpɜrfɪkt / (AmE), / ˈɪntʃˌpɜːfɪkt / (BrE)
done or achieved with the highest degree of precision; extremely accurate or exact
extremely precise or accurate

Examples

Her inch-perfect timing impressed the entire panel of judges.
The footballer delivered an inch-perfect pass to his teammate, leading to a winning goal.
Engineers needed to make inch-perfect adjustments to ensure the machinery ran smoothly.

Synset Examples

His performance was inch-perfect, earning him a standing ovation.
The architect designed an inch-perfect model of the building.
It was an inch-perfect throw that won the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precise - exact and accurate in detail
  • Synonym: accurate - free from error; correct in all details

Etymology

Formed from 'inch' (a small unit of measurement) and 'perfect', implying perfection down to the smallest measure.
