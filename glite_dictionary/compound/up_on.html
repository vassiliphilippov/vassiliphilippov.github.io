up on

1. ADJ

/ ʌp ɑn / (AmE), / ʌp ɒn / (BrE)
having knowledge of or being well informed about something; familiar with the latest details or developments in a subject
well informed about something

Examples

She is really up on the latest trends in technology.
I'm not up on classical music, so I couldn't name the composer.
Make sure you stay up on all the new regulations before starting the project.

Synset Examples

kept abreast of the latest developments
constant revision keeps the book au courant
always au fait on the latest events
up on the news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abreast - informed about the latest news or information
  • Synonym: au fait - familiar; knowledgeable; well informed
