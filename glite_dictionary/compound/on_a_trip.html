on a trip

1. ADP

/ ɑn ə trɪp / (AmE), / ɒn ə trɪp / (BrE)
traveling away from home, usually for leisure, business, or another purpose, to visit a destination
traveling to a destination

Examples

She is currently on a trip to Japan with her sister.
While on a trip, he discovered a new favorite hobby.
Many students go on a trip abroad during their summer break.

Synset Examples

He was on a trip to Europe last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trip - a journey or excursion, especially for pleasure
