Victory in Europe Day

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪktəri ɪn jʊˈroʊp deɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɪktəri ɪn ˈjʊərəp deɪ / (BrE)
the day marking the Allied victory over Nazi Germany in Europe during World War II, celebrated on May 8, 1945
Allied WWII victory day in Europe

Examples

Every year, veterans gather to commemorate Victory in Europe Day and remember those who fought in World War II.
Parades and celebrations took place across Britain on Victory in Europe Day in 1945.
Schools often teach students about the significance of Victory in Europe Day in modern history classes.
