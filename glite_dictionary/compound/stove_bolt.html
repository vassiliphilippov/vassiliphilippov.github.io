stove bolt

1. NOUN

/ stoʊv boʊlt / (AmE), / stəʊv bəʊlt / (BrE)
A small machine bolt with a round or flat head, typically used in assembling metal parts or sheet metal.
small machine bolt for metal assembly

Examples

He tightened the panel with a stove bolt to keep it secure.
The mechanic replaced the rusty stove bolt with a new one.
Several stove bolts are needed to assemble the metal frame.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: machine bolt - a bolt with a hexagonal or square head, used with a nut
  • Degree Higher: screw - a metal fastener with a helical ridge
