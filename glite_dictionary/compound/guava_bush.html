guava bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡwɑːvə bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡwɑːvə bʊʃ / (BrE)
a small tropical American shrub or tree, widely cultivated in warm regions for its sweet, yellow, globular fruit
small tropical shrub with sweet fruit

Examples

The guava bush in her garden produces fruit every summer.
They planted a row of guava bushes along the fence for their sweet harvest.
The leaves of the guava bush can also be used to make tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guava - the fruit of the guava bush or tree

Etymology

From Spanish 'guayaba', ultimately from the Taíno word 'guayabo' (guava tree).
