beautiful scenery

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːtəfəl səˈnɛɹi / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtəfʊl sɪˈnɪəri / (BrE)
attractive and visually pleasing views of natural landscapes, such as mountains, forests, or coastlines
attractive natural landscapes

Examples

We stopped several times to admire the beautiful scenery along the mountain road.
The hotel room offered beautiful scenery overlooking the lake and surrounding hills.
Travelers come here to experience the beautiful scenery and peaceful atmosphere.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed the beautiful scenery during our trip to Kiwiland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenery - the natural features of a landscape considered in terms of their appearance
