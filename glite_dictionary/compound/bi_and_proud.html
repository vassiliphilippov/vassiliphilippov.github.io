bi and proud

1. ADJ

/ baɪ ənd praʊd / (AmE), / baɪ ənd praʊd / (BrE)
Openly identifying as bisexual and embracing this identity with pride.
openly bisexual and proud of it
informal

Examples

It's important for everyone to feel comfortable being bi and proud in their community.
