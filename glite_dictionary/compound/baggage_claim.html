baggage claim

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡɪdʒ kleɪm / (AmE), / ˈbæɡɪdʒ kleɪm / (BrE)
the area in an airport where arriving passengers collect their checked luggage.
airport area for collecting checked luggage

Examples

After landing, passengers made their way to baggage claim to collect their suitcases.
The airport's baggage claim area was crowded with travelers waiting for their luggage.
She waited anxiously at baggage claim, hoping her bags had not been lost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luggage carousel - conveyor belt where luggage appears in baggage claim area
