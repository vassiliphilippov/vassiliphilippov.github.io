crab cocktail

1. NOUN

/ kræb ˈkɑkˌteɪl / (AmE), / kræb ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
A dish consisting of cold cooked crabmeat served with a tangy or spicy sauce, typically as an appetizer.
appetizer of crabmeat with sauce

Examples

She ordered a crab cocktail as her starter at the seafood restaurant.
The crab cocktail was garnished with fresh parsley and a wedge of lemon.
At the party, they served a delicious crab cocktail in individual martini glasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrimp cocktail - appetizer of cold shrimp and sauce
