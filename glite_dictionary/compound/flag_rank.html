flag rank

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ ræŋk / (AmE), / flæɡ raŋk / (BrE)
the rank held by a flag officer, such as an admiral or general, within a navy or armed forces
military rank of a flag officer

Examples

She was promoted to flag rank after years of distinguished service.
Only officers of flag rank were allowed to attend the meeting.
The nomination to flag rank is considered a significant achievement in the navy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flag officer - a senior military officer entitled to fly a flag to mark the command
