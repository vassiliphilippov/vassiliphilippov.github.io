weenie roast

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːni roʊst / (AmE), / ˈwiːni rəʊst / (BrE)
an informal outdoor gathering where frankfurters are cooked over an open fire and served as the main dish
outdoor event for roasting frankfurters
informal american

Examples

Every summer, our family hosts a weenie roast at the lake with friends and neighbors.
The scout troop ended their hike with a classic weenie roast around the campfire.
We brought marshmallows and buns for the weenie roast in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbecue - a meal or gathering where meat or other food is cooked out of doors on a rack over an open fire or grill

Etymology

From 'weenie', a diminutive of 'wiener', itself from German 'Wiener' (Viennese), referring to sausages, and 'roast', meaning to cook by direct heat.
