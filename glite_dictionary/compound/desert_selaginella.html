desert selaginella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzərt səˌlædʒəˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˈdɛzət sɛˌlædʒɪˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A prostrate spikemoss plant native to desert regions, especially in California, belonging to the genus Selaginella.
a spikemoss plant of California deserts

Examples

The desert selaginella thrives in arid regions where few other plants can survive.
Scientists observed that desert selaginella forms resilient mats on sandy soil.
After the spring rains, large patches of desert selaginella became vividly green in the California desert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spikemoss - common name for plants in the genus Selaginella

Etymology

From 'desert' and the genus name 'Selaginella', which is from Latin 'selago', a kind of plant.
