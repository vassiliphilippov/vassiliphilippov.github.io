Colorado Springs

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːləˌrædoʊ ˈsprɪŋz / (AmE), / ˌkɒlərəʊ ˈsprɪŋz / (BrE)
a city in east central Colorado, situated at the base of the Rocky Mountains, known for its tourist attractions and as the site of the United States Air Force Academy.
city in Colorado, USA

Examples

Many visitors come to Colorado Springs each year to see the Garden of the Gods.
After graduating, she was stationed at the Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs.
The mountains near Colorado Springs offer excellent hiking and breathtaking views.

Etymology

Named after the Colorado River and the area's natural springs.
