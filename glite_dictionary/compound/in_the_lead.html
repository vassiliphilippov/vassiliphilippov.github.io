in the lead

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ðə lid / (AmE), / ɪn ðə liːd / (BrE)
holding the leading position or having a higher score than others in a competition or contest
having the leading position in a contest

Examples

After a thrilling final lap, she was in the lead as she crossed the finish line.
The company remained in the lead throughout the quarter thanks to their innovative products.
With one game left, our team is currently in the lead by two points.

Synset Examples

he is ahead by a pawn
the leading team in the pennant race

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead - in advance; in front; having an advantage
  • Antonym: behind - at the back; having a lower position or score
