mid-August

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈɔɡəst / (AmE), / mɪd ˈɔːɡəst / (BrE)
the middle part of the month of August
middle of August

Examples

We usually go on vacation in mid-August when the weather is warmest.
The project deadline is set for mid-August this year.
By mid-August, the crops are ready to be harvested.
