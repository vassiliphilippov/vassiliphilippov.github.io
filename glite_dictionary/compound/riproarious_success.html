riproarious success

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɪpˈrɔriəs səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˌrɪpˈrɔːriəs səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
A highly lively and enjoyable success, often marked by loud celebration or great enthusiasm.
very lively and enjoyable success

Examples

The annual festival was a riproarious success that people talked about for months afterward.
Her new play turned out to be a riproarious success, drawing praise from critics and audiences alike.
The charity concert was such a riproarious success that they decided to make it an annual event.

Synset Examples

The party was a riproarious success with everyone dancing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumph - a great victory or achievement
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success

Etymology

Formed from 'riproarious', from 'rip-roaring' (extremely lively or noisy), and 'success'.
