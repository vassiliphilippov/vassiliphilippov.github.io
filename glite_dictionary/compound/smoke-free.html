smoke-free

1. ADJ

/ smoʊk friː / (AmE), / sməʊk friː / (BrE)
(of a place or environment) having no smoke, especially because smoking is not allowed there.
where smoking is not allowed; free from smoke

Examples

The hotel prides itself on being completely smoke-free for the comfort of its guests.
Smoking is prohibited in all smoke-free restaurants and offices in the city.
Many airlines offer smoke-free flights to promote a healthier environment.

Synset Examples

smoke-free offices and restaurants

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smoky - filled with or smelling of smoke
