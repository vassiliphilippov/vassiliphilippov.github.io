construction worker

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to build or repair buildings, roads, or other structures, typically working on construction sites
a person who works in building construction

Examples

The city hired dozens of construction workers to build the new sports stadium.
As a construction worker, he often starts his day before sunrise.
Many construction workers wear helmets and reflective vests for safety on the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - a person who constructs buildings
