pitching change

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃɪŋ tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃɪŋ tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the act of replacing one pitcher with another during a baseball game
replacement of a pitcher in baseball

Examples

The coach made a pitching change in the seventh inning to counter the opposing team's strongest hitter.
After a rough start, the team's first pitching change came earlier than expected.
The home crowd cheered as the relief pitcher took the mound following a strategic pitching change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substitution - the act of replacing one player with another in a game
