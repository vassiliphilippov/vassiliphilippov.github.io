nut bar

1. NOUN

/ nʌt bɑr / (AmE), / nʌt bɑː / (BrE)
A sweet snack made of a mixture of nuts and sugar set on a pastry base and cut into bars.
sweet snack with nuts on pastry base

Examples

He offered me a nut bar with my coffee, and it was delicious.
The bakery sells a variety of nut bars, including ones with almonds and pecans.
Making a homemade nut bar is easy if you have some mixed nuts and honey.
