software optimisation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔftˌwɛɹ ˌɑptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɒftweə ˌɒptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of improving a software system to make it more efficient or reduce its resource usage.
improving efficiency of software

Examples

The team focused on software optimisation to reduce the application's memory usage and speed up performance.
After the latest software optimisation, the system's startup time was cut in half.
Modern companies invest heavily in software optimisation to ensure their products remain competitive.

Synset Examples

Over time, the stakes in software optimization and compliance have risen exponentially as license types proliferate and grow more complex, especially as licensing and delivery models continue to change and grow more complex with virtualization and cloud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimization - The act of making something as effective as possible
