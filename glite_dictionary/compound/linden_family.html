linden family

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪndən ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈlɪndən ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of chiefly trees and shrubs, including the genus Tilia and several others, found mainly in tropical and temperate regions, especially southeastern Asia and Brazil.
family of trees and shrubs (Tilia etc.)

Examples

The linden family includes well-known trees such as the linden and some tropical species.
Many plants in the linden family have commercial uses, particularly for their fibers and flowers.
Researchers are studying the distribution of the linden family across various continents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tiliaceae - the scientific family name for the linden family
