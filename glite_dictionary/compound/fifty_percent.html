fifty percent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪf.ti pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪf.ti pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
One half or 50 out of every 100 parts, expressed as a percentage.
one half as a percentage

Examples

Voting turnout reached fifty percent in the last election, which was considered low.
If you score fifty percent or more, you will pass the test.
Fifty percent of the water in the lake evaporated during the drought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half - one of two equal parts of a whole
