Federal Security Bureau

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɛdərəl səˈkjʊrəti bjʊˈroʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl sɪˈkjʊərəti bjʊəˈrəʊ / (BrE)
the principal domestic security and counterintelligence agency of the Russian Federation, which succeeded the Soviet KGB
Russian security and counterintelligence agency

Examples

The Federal Security Bureau has played a key role in Russian domestic intelligence gathering since the 1990s.
Many analysts attribute the agency's rise in influence to the time when Vladimir Putin led the Federal Security Bureau.
Reports claim that the Federal Security Bureau is heavily involved in combating cyber threats within Russia.
