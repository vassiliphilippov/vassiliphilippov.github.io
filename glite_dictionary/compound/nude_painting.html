nude painting

1. NOUN

/ nud ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / njuːd ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a painting that depicts a naked human figure, typically as an artistic subject
painting of a naked human figure

Examples

The gallery showcased a stunning nude painting by a renowned artist.
He spent weeks working on a nude painting for his final art project.
Some critics praised the boldness of the nude painting displayed in the main hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nude - a painting, sculpture, or other artistic representation of a naked human figure
