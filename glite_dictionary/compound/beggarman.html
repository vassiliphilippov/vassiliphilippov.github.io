beggarman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛɡərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡəmæn / (BrE)
a man who begs for money or food in the streets; a male beggar
a male beggar

Examples

The old beggarman sat on the corner, hoping for a few coins from passersby.
In the story, a beggarman surprises everyone by revealing he was a prince in disguise.
As a child, he was always kind to the poor beggarman who walked through the village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beggar - a person who lives by asking for money or food
