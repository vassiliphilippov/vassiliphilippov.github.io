order Xyridales

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔrdər zaɪˈrɪdeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌɔːdə zaɪˈrɪdeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of monocotyledonous flowering plants, mainly tropical herbs, including the family Xyridaceae
botanical order of monocot herbs
medical

Examples

Recent molecular studies have clarified the classification of order Xyridales within flowering plants.
The family Xyridaceae is the largest family found in the order Xyridales.
Tropical wetlands often support several herbaceous species from the order Xyridales.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Xyridales', based on the genus 'Xyris' and standard suffix '-ales' for plant orders.
