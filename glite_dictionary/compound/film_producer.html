film producer

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm pɹəˈduːsɚ / (AmE), / fɪlm prəˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person responsible for overseeing and managing the production of a film, including financing, coordination, and organization
person managing film production

Examples

The film producer secured funding from several major studios for the new project.
As a successful film producer, she has worked with some of the biggest directors in Hollywood.
Every film producer faces challenges in keeping production on schedule and within budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: producer - a person responsible for the business side of making a film, play, or recording
