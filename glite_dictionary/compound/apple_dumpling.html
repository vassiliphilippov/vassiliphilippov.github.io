apple dumpling

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of an apple wrapped in pastry dough and baked, often served with a sweet sauce.
baked dessert of apple in pastry

Examples

She baked delicious apple dumplings for the family gathering.
The warm apple dumpling was served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
Have you ever tried making apple dumplings from scratch?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpling - small ball of dough cooked and served in various ways
