St. Johns River

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt dʒɑn(z) ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / seɪnt dʒɒnz ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in northeastern Florida, United States, flowing northward to Jacksonville and then eastward to empty into the Atlantic Ocean.
river in northeastern Florida, USA

Examples

Many recreational activities such as boating and fishing take place on the St. Johns River each year.
The St. Johns River is one of the few major rivers in the United States that flows north.
Jacksonville is located along the banks of the St. Johns River, providing scenic views of the city.
