naked eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.kɪd aɪ / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.kɪd aɪ / (BrE)
the eye when used without the aid of any optical instruments such as telescopes or microscopes
vision without optical aids

Examples

It is not safe to look directly at the sun with the naked eye.
The comet was visible to the naked eye last night.
Some bacteria are too small to be seen with the naked eye.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unaided eye - the eye without help from instruments
