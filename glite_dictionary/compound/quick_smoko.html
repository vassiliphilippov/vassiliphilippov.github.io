quick smoko

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ˈsmoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / kwɪk ˈsməʊkəʊ / (BrE)
A short break from work for smoking, especially as observed in Australian and New Zealand workplaces.
brief smoking break
informal

Examples

The workers took a quick smoko before returning to the construction site.
Let's have a quick smoko and then finish the paperwork.
He suggested a quick smoko outside before the meeting resumed.

Synset Examples

Let's take a quick smoko before we continue working.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoko - a short break from work, especially to smoke or have tea (Austral., N.Z.)

Etymology

From 'quick' + Australian English 'smoko' (a short break from work, originally to smoke a cigarette).
