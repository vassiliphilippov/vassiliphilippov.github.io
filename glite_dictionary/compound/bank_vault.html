bank vault

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk vɔlt / (AmE), / baŋk vɔːlt / (BrE)
a strong, secure room or compartment in a bank used for storing money, valuables, and important documents
secure room for valuables in a bank

Examples

The stolen jewels were kept in the bank vault for maximum security.
Access to the bank vault is restricted to authorized personnel only.
During the renovation, workers discovered an old bank vault hidden behind a wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - a secure lockable box for valuables
