garden project

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˈprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a planned activity or undertaking related to gardening, such as creating, maintaining, or improving a garden
planned gardening activity or undertaking

Examples

The school organized a garden project to teach students about growing vegetables and flowers.
He spent his weekend planning a new garden project for the backyard.
Funding was granted for a community garden project that would benefit local families.

Synset Examples

She grabbed a shovel to help with the garden project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardening - the activity of working in a garden, growing and taking care of plants
