scrotal vein

1. NOUN

/ ˈskroʊ.tl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈskrəʊ.tl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that carries blood from the scrotum to the pudendal veins
vein in the scrotum
medical

Examples

A blockage in a scrotal vein can cause swelling and discomfort.
During surgery, the doctor carefully examined the patient's scrotal vein.
The scrotal vein plays an essential role in draining blood from the scrotal area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pudendal vein - a vein in the pelvis that receives blood from the genital area
