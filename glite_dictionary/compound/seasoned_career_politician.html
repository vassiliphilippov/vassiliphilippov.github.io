seasoned career politician

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːzənd kəˈrɪr pɑːˈlɪtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənd kəˈrɪə pɒˈlɪtɪʃən / (BrE)
a politician who has extensive experience and has had a long and established career in politics
experienced, long-serving politician

Examples

The mayor is a seasoned career politician who has served in office for over three decades.
As a seasoned career politician, she navigated the complex negotiations with ease.
During the campaign, the seasoned career politician drew on years of public service experience.

Synset Examples

The debate featured a seasoned career politician.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politician - a person who is professionally involved in politics
