airplane landing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpleɪn ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of bringing an aircraft down to the ground safely at the end of its flight
act of bringing an aircraft down to the ground

Examples

The airplane landing was smooth despite the heavy rain.
Passengers applauded after a difficult airplane landing in strong winds.
The pilot practiced his airplane landing skills during flight training.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: takeoff - the act of an aircraft leaving the ground
