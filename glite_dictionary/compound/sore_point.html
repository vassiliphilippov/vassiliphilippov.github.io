sore point

1. NOUN

/ sɔr pɔɪnt / (AmE), / sɔː pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an issue or topic that causes hurt feelings or discomfort when mentioned
a sensitive or uncomfortable topic

Examples

The company's decision to lay off workers remains a sore point for many employees.
It's always a sore point when someone mentions her previous job.
Parking has been a sore point in our neighborhood for years.

Synset Examples

The budget cuts have become a sore point in discussions.
His late arrival is a sore point among the team.
Bringing up his ex is a sore point for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touchy subject - topic that easily causes offense or discomfort
  • Synonym: hot button - topic that triggers strong emotional reactions
