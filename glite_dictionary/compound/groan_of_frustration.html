groan of frustration

1. NOUN

/ ɡroʊn əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɡrəʊn əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a deep, audible sound made to express annoyance or disappointment
sound expressing annoyance or disappointment

Examples

When the computer crashed again, he let out a groan of frustration.
A groan of frustration echoed through the room when the meeting was extended.
She stifled a groan of frustration as she realized she had left her keys at home.

Synset Examples

She let out a groan of frustration after losing the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sigh of relief - an audible breath expressing relief
