business people

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˌpiːpəl / (BrE)
People engaged in commerce or business activities, especially executives or professionals in the business world.
people involved in business, especially executives

Examples

Many business people travel frequently for meetings and conferences.
The seminar was attended by business people from around the world.
Successful business people often share their strategies through books and lectures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executive - a person with senior managerial responsibility in a business
  • Synonym: entrepreneur - a person who organizes and operates a business
