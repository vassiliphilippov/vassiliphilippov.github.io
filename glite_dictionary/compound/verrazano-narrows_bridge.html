Verrazano-Narrows Bridge

1. PROPN

/ vəˌræzəˈnoʊ ˈnæroʊz brɪdʒ / (AmE), / vəˌrɑːzəˈnəʊ ˈnærəʊz brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large suspension bridge spanning the Verrazano Narrows between Brooklyn and Staten Island in New York City
suspension bridge in New York City

Examples

The Verrazano-Narrows Bridge is an essential link between Brooklyn and Staten Island.
Many runners cross the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge at the start of the New York City Marathon.
The sweeping views from the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge make it a unique driving experience.

Etymology

Named after Giovanni da Verrazzano, an Italian explorer, and the Verrazano Narrows strait.
