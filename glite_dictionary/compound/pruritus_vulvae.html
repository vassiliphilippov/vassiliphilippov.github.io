pruritus vulvae

1. NOUN

/ pruˈraɪtəs vʊlˈvaɪ / (AmE), / pruːˈraɪtəs vʊlˈviː / (BrE)
persistent itching of the external female genitalia
itching of female genital area
medical

Examples

The patient reported severe pruritus vulvae lasting for several weeks.
Treatment for pruritus vulvae often includes topical creams and improved hygiene.
Chronic pruritus vulvae can be a symptom of underlying infections or dermatological conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itching - an uncomfortable sensation on the skin causing a desire to scratch
  • Synonym: pruritus - general medical term for itching

Etymology

From Latin 'pruritus' meaning itch and 'vulvae', genitive of 'vulva' meaning womb or female external genitalia.
