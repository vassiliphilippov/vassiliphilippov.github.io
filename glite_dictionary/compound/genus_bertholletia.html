genus Bertholletia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs bɛrˌθɑːˈliː.ti.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bɛˌθɒlˈliː.tɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical South American trees in the family Lecythidaceae, including the Brazil nut tree (Bertholletia excelsa).
genus of trees including the Brazil nut

Examples

The genus Bertholletia contains only one species, the well-known Brazil nut tree.
Botanists study the unique reproductive system of the genus Bertholletia.
Commercial harvesting of nuts from the genus Bertholletia provides income for many communities in the Amazon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bertholletia excelsa - the Brazil nut tree, species within this genus

Etymology

From French naturalist Claude Louis Berthollet, after whom the genus was named.
