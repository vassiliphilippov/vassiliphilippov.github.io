hub for

1. NOUN

/ hʌb fɔr / (AmE), / hʌb fə / (BrE)
a central place where a particular activity, interest, or group is focused or develops
central place for activity or group

Examples

The downtown area has become a hub for new restaurants and nightlife.
This university is a hub for innovation and scientific research.
The city is recognized as a hub for the fashion industry.

Synset Examples

This cafe is a Hub for artists in the area.
Apelanta has become a hub for tech startups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center for - a place serving as the main place for a particular activity
  • Synonym: focal point for - the main or most important place for a particular activity or interest
