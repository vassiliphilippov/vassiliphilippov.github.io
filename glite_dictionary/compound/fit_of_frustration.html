fit of frustration

1. NOUN

/ fɪt əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪt əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a sudden and intense display of anger or irritation caused by frustration
sudden angry outburst due to frustration

Examples

He slammed the door in a fit of frustration when the project failed again.
She deleted her entire essay in a fit of frustration after the computer crashed.
In a fit of frustration, he tore up the letter without reading it.

Synset Examples

In a fit of frustration, she threatened to rub her face with a brass candlestick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tantrum - an uncontrolled outburst of anger and frustration, typically in a young child
  • Synonym: fit of rage - a sudden and violent burst of anger
