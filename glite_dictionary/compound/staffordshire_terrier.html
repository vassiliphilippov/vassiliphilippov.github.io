Staffordshire terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæfərdˌʃɪr ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈstæfədʃɪə ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
A muscular American breed of terrier with a short, smooth, stiff coat.
American breed of muscular terrier dog

Examples

The Staffordshire terrier is known for its loyalty and protective nature.
Our neighbors adopted a young Staffordshire terrier from the animal shelter last month.
She entered her prize-winning Staffordshire terrier in the national dog show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pit bull terrier - A dog breed similar to the Staffordshire terrier, often confused with it
  • Synonym: bull terrier - Another breed of terrier developed from bull and terrier crosses

Etymology

Named after Staffordshire, a county in England where the breed's ancestors originated.
