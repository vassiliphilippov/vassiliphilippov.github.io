animal oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəməl ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ɔɪl / (BrE)
oil obtained from animal sources, such as fats rendered from fish or mammals
oil derived from animals

Examples

Whale blubber was once a major source of animal oil for lamps and machinery.
Some traditional soaps are made using animal oil instead of vegetable oil.
The factory specializes in refining various types of animal oil for industrial use.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vegetable oil - oil derived from plants and vegetables
