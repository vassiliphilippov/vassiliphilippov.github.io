architectonic elegance

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑrkɪˈtɑnɪk ˈɛləɡəns / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɒnɪk ˈɛlɪɡəns / (BrE)
the quality of being elegantly structured or harmonious in architectural design or form
elegant quality in architectural design

Examples

Many critics praised the building's architectonic elegance and innovative use of space.
The museum's architectonic elegance became a hallmark of the city's skyline.
He sought to achieve architectonic elegance in every structure he designed.

Synset Examples

The architect's vision had an architectonic elegance to it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
