quirky humor

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɝki ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːki ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
humor that is unconventional, unique, or noticeably eccentric in character
unconventional or eccentric humor

Examples

The movie is full of quirky humor that sets it apart from ordinary comedies.
Her quirky humor always brings a smile to our faces at the office.
Viewers loved the show's blend of wit and quirky humor.

Synset Examples

In the game, the character wog is known for its quirky humor.
Everyone knows that coot has a quirky sense of humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadpan humor - humor delivered with an impassive, expressionless manner
  • Degree Higher: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Degree Lower: conventional humor - humor that follows traditional or commonly accepted forms
