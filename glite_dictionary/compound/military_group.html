military group

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ɡruːp / (BrE)
a unit that is part of an armed force, typically organized for a particular military purpose or function.
a unit within an armed force

Examples

The military group was deployed overseas to support peacekeeping efforts.
Each military group specializes in different areas of combat and logistics.
A new commander was assigned to lead the military group during the joint exercises.

Synset Examples

he sent Caesar a force of six thousand men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military unit - a subdivision of an armed force, similar to a military group
