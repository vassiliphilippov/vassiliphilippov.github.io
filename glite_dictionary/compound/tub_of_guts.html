tub of guts

1. NOUN

/ tʌb əv ɡʌts / (AmE), / tʌb əv ɡʌts / (BrE)
a container filled with intestines or similar animal entrails, often used in cooking or butchery
container filled with intestines

Examples

The butcher carried a tub of guts to the back room for processing.
After the hunt, they cleaned the ducks over a tub of guts.
In the kitchen, someone gagged at the sight of the tub of guts on the table.

Synset Examples

He joked about finding a tub of guts in the horror movie.
She couldn't look at the tub of guts without feeling queasy.
