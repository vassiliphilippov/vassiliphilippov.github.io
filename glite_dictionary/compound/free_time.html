free time

1. NOUN

/ fri taɪm / (AmE), / friː taɪm / (BrE)
time when one is not working or occupied with other duties or responsibilities
time without duties or work

Examples

I like to read novels in my free time.
He spends most of his free time playing basketball with friends.
Teachers often use their free time to catch up on personal tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisure - free time for relaxation or recreation
  • Antonym: work - activity involving mental or physical effort for duties or responsibilities

2. NOUN

/ friː taɪm / (AmE), / friː taɪm / (BrE)
Time when you are not working or busy and can do what you enjoy.
time available for leisure activities

Examples

I like to read novels in my free time.
She spent her free time learning to play the guitar.
How do you usually spend your free time?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisure - free time when you are not working or studying

3. NOUN

/ fri taɪm / (AmE), / friː taɪm / (BrE)

Examples

She spends most of her free time volunteering at the animal shelter.
During their free time, the children played games in the garden.
