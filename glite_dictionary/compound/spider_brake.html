spider brake

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdər breɪk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə breɪk / (BrE)
An Asiatic fern (Pteris multifida) that has been introduced to America, known for its delicate, divided fronds.
Asiatic fern species (Pteris multifida)

Examples

The greenhouse has a variety of ferns, including the beautiful spider brake.
Gardeners often choose spider brake for its elegant, feathery foliage.
The spider brake is easy to grow in shaded areas with moist soil.
