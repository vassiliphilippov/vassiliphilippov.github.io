in a mess

1. ADP

/ ɪn ə mɛs / (AmE), / ɪn ə mɛs / (BrE)
in a state of disorder or untidiness; disorganized or messy
in a disordered or untidy state

Examples

After the party, the whole house was in a mess and needed to be cleaned.
Whenever he's feeling stressed, his desk ends up in a mess.
She found her notes in a mess after accidentally dropping them on the floor.

Synset Examples

Her room is always in a mess.
The kitchen was left in a mess after cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untidy - not tidy; messy or disordered
  • Antonym: orderly - arranged neatly or in order
