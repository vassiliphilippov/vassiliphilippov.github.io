with flair and panache

1. ADV

/ wɪð flɛr ənd pəˈnæʃ / (AmE), / wɪð fleə ənd pəˈnaʃ / (BrE)
with an impressive combination of style, originality, and confidence
in a stylish and original way

Examples

He delivered his presentation with flair and panache, captivating the entire audience.
The artist decorated the studio with flair and panache, making it truly unique.
Every dish was plated with flair and panache, impressing even the toughest critics.

Synset Examples

The restaurant serves dishes with flair and panache.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylishly - in a stylish way

Etymology

'Panache' is from French meaning 'plume, style.'
