pig lead

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡ lɛd / (AmE), / pɪɡ lɛd / (BrE)
Lead metal that has been cast into ingots known as pigs for storage or transport.
lead cast into ingots called pigs

Examples

The factory stores the raw pig lead in large warehouses before processing it further.
After the smelting process, pig lead is transported to various manufacturers.
She explained that pig lead refers to blocks of lead shaped for easy handling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingot - metal cast into a block or bar, especially for storage or transport

Etymology

From 'pig', meaning a mass of metal cast into blocks, and 'lead', the element.
