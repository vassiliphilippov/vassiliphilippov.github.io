order Marattiales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər mərˌætiˈeɪliˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə məˌrætiˈeɪlɪiːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of lower ferns, corresponding to the family Marattiaceae.
an order of lower ferns

Examples

The order Marattiales includes several ancient lineages of tropical ferns.
Scientists have studied spore formation within the order Marattiales to understand fern evolution.
The order Marattiales is considered one of the most primitive groups among living ferns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marattiaceae - the family equivalent to order Marattiales

Etymology

From neo-Latin; named after the genus Marattia, with order suffix '-ales' used in taxonomy.
