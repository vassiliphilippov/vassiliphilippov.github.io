American foxhound

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfɑːkˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfɒkshaʊnd / (BrE)
A breed of foxhound developed in the United States, known for hunting foxes both alone and in packs.
breed of American hunting dog

Examples

The American foxhound is renowned for its stamina and gentle nature.
At the dog show, several American foxhounds competed in the hunting category.
She adopted an American foxhound after learning about their friendly temperament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foxhound - a breed of dog used in fox hunting
