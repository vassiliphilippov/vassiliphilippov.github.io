spud head

1. NOUN

/ spʌd hɛd / (AmE), / spʌd hɛd / (BrE)
An informal, mildly derogatory term for a foolish or lazy person.
foolish or lazy person (informal)
informal

Examples

Stop being such a spud head and help me with the groceries!
He laughed and called his brother a spud head after he forgot his keys again.
You won't get anything done if you act like a spud head all day.

Synset Examples

Stop being such a spud head and help me!
He called his friend a spud head for being lazy.
Don't be a spud head; let's get moving!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockhead - a stupid person
  • Synonym: lazybones - a lazy person

Etymology

From 'spud' (slang for potato) + 'head', likening a person's head to a potato to imply dullness or stupidity.
