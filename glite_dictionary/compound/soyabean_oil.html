soyabean oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪbəˌn ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪəbiːn ɔɪl / (BrE)
an edible oil extracted from soybeans, widely used in cooking and food production
oil derived from soybeans

Examples

Most commercial salad dressings contain soyabean oil as the main ingredient.
She prefers cooking with soyabean oil because of its mild flavor.
The label indicates that the product is made from 100% pure soyabean oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soybean oil - alternative spelling: oil extracted from soybeans
