bidet tap

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈdeɪ tæp / (AmE), / bɪˈdeɪ tap / (BrE)
A tap or faucet installed on a bidet to supply water for washing.
tap on a bidet for water

Examples

The new bathroom comes with a modern bidet tap that includes a temperature control knob.
Please make sure the bidet tap is tightly closed after use to prevent dripping.
He replaced the old faucet with a sleek chrome bidet tap for better hygiene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bidet - a low bathroom fixture for washing the lower body
  • Synonym: faucet - a device that controls the flow of liquid, especially water
