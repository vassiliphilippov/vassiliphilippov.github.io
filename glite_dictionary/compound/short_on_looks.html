short on looks

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrt ɑn lʊks / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ɒn lʊks / (BrE)
not attractive in appearance; lacking visual appeal or physical beauty
lacking in physical attractiveness

Examples

Despite being short on looks, the apartment was cozy and affordable.
He joked that he was a bit short on looks but made up for it with personality.
This smartphone is short on looks, but it's packed with features.

Synset Examples

She felt short on looks compared to her friends.
The car is a bit short on looks but great on performance.
The restaurant is short on looks but serves fantastic food.
