Mahratti language

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɑːti ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈrɑːti ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Indic language spoken mainly in Maharashtra, India, and written in the Devanagari script
Indic language of Maharashtra, India

Examples

My grandmother speaks Mahratti language fluently, having grown up in central India.
Some historical documents in the museum are written in the Mahratti language.
Learning the Mahratti language helped her connect with the local community in Maharashtra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marathi - the modern standard name for the Mahratti language

Etymology

From Marathi (Marāṭhī), the language's own name for itself.
