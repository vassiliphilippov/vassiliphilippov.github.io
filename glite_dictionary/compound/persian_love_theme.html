Persian love theme

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrʒən lʌv θiːm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən lʌv θiːm / (BrE)
A romantic theme, especially in literature, art, or celebrations, that is inspired by or reflects elements of Persian culture.
romantic theme inspired by Persian culture

Examples

The event was decorated with a Persian love theme, featuring Persian poetry and floral motifs.
Authors often draw on the Persian love theme when writing about passionate romance.
The couple chose a Persian love theme for their wedding, complete with traditional music and colors.

Synset Examples
