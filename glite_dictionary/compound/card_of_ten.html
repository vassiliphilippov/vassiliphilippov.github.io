card of ten

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd əv tɛn / (AmE), / kɑːd əv tɛn / (BrE)
a prepaid card or booklet that allows the holder to make ten purchases or entries, typically offered as a special deal or for convenience in dining, entertainment, or public transport
prepaid card for ten purchases

Examples

She bought a card of ten bus rides to save money on her commute.
The theater's card of ten tickets came at a discounted rate for frequent visitors.
Many cafes offer a card of ten coffees so regular customers get a free drink after ten purchases.

Synset Examples

Using a card of ten makes dining out more affordable.
The café offered a card of ten for regular customers.
He bought a card of ten movie tickets for the summer.
