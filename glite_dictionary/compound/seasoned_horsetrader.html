seasoned horsetrader

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːzənd ˈhɔrsˌtreɪdər / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənd ˈhɔːsˌtreɪdə / (BrE)
a person who is highly experienced or skilled at bargaining or negotiation, especially in a shrewd or astute manner
skilled or shrewd negotiator

Examples

She approached the contract discussion like a seasoned horsetrader, never missing an opportunity to get a better deal.
His reputation as a seasoned horsetrader made him invaluable during business mergers.
After years in politics, she became a seasoned horsetrader in legislative negotiations.

Synset Examples

He acted like a seasoned horsetrader at the auction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negotiator - person who negotiates agreements
  • Synonym: bargainer - someone skilled at striking bargains

Etymology

From 'seasoned' (experienced) and 'horsetrader', originally someone who trades horses, metaphorically extended to able negotiators.
