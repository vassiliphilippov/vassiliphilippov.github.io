compact fluorescent light

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpækt flɔːˈrɛsənt laɪt / (AmE), / kəmˈpækt flɒˈrɛsənt laɪt / (BrE)
a small, energy-efficient fluorescent light bulb designed to replace traditional incandescent bulbs
energy-saving fluorescent light bulb

Examples

Many households have replaced old bulbs with compact fluorescent light bulbs to save energy.
She bought a compact fluorescent light for her reading lamp because it lasts longer.
The compact fluorescent light in the hallway emits a cool, white glow.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CFL - abbreviation for compact fluorescent lamp or light
  • Antonym: incandescent bulb - traditional light bulb using a filament heated to produce light
  • Degree Higher: LED bulb - energy-efficient light bulb using light-emitting diodes
