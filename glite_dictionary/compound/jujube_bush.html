jujube bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːdʒuː bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːdʒuː bʊʃ / (BrE)
A spiny shrub or small tree, native to Asia, bearing dark red, edible fruits known as jujubes.
spiny tree with edible red fruits

Examples

We planted a jujube bush in the backyard for its attractive fruit.
The jujube bush thrives in dry climates and poor soils.
Birds love to eat the sweet fruit produced by the jujube bush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jujube - the fruit of the jujube bush or tree

Etymology

From Persian 'zayzafun' via French 'jujube', referring to the jujube fruit; 'bush' from Old English 'bysc'.
