passenger vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsəndʒɚ ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪndʒə ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A vehicle designed to carry multiple passengers, typically used for public transportation.
vehicle for transporting passengers

Examples

The city invested in new passenger vehicles to improve its public transport system.
Many commuters rely on passenger vehicles such as buses and trains every day.
A passenger vehicle must meet strict safety regulations to carry people in urban areas.

Synset Examples

he always rode the bus to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus - large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road
  • Synonym: coach - large motor vehicle for long-distance passenger travel
  • Synonym: taxi - car licensed to transport passengers in return for payment
