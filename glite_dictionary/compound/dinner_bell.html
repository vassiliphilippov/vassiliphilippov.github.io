dinner bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər bɛl / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə bɛl / (BrE)
a bell rung to signal that dinner is ready or being served
bell rung to announce dinner

Examples

As soon as the dinner bell rang, the children hurried inside.
On the farm, the sound of the dinner bell could be heard across the fields.
She polished the old dinner bell that had been in the family for generations.
