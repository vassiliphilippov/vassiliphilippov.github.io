caramel apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærəˌmɛl ˈæpəl, ˈkɑːrml ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkærəməl ˈæpəl / (BrE)
An apple coated in a layer of caramelized sugar, typically served on a stick as a sweet treat.
apple covered in caramelized sugar

Examples

She bought a caramel apple at the county fair and ate it as she walked around.
Each caramel apple was topped with chopped nuts for extra crunch.
Making a homemade caramel apple is a fun autumn tradition for many families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candied apple - an apple coated in a hard sugar candy shell
  • Synonym: toffee apple - British: an apple on a stick dipped in toffee
