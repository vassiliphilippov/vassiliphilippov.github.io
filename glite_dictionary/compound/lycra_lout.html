Lycra lout

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪkrə laʊt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkrə laʊt / (BrE)
a cyclist who wears tight-fitting Lycra clothing and is regarded as inconsiderate or showy, often in a negative or critical way
flashy or inconsiderate cyclist in tight Lycra
informal british

Examples

The park was crowded with one Lycra lout after another racing along the paths.
She complained about the Lycra louts who ignored pedestrian crossings.
After buying a new bike, he worried about turning into a Lycra lout.

Synset Examples

That Lycra lout on the bike needs to watch the road.
The Lycra lout sped past us on the trail.
He dressed like a Lycra lout for the cycling event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive person
  • Synonym: cyclist - someone who rides a bicycle

Etymology

Lycra is a trademark for a type of stretch fabric; 'lout' from Middle English, meaning an uncouth person.
