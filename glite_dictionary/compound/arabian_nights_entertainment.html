Arabian Nights' Entertainment

1. NOUN

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən naɪts ɪnˌtɝː.teɪn.mənt / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən naɪts ɪnˌtɜː.teɪn.mənt / (BrE)
a famous collection of Middle Eastern folktales, originally in Arabic, dating from the 10th century, also known as The Thousand and One Nights
collection of Middle Eastern folktales

Examples

As a child, I was fascinated by the magical tales in the Arabian Nights' Entertainment.
He collected rare editions of the Arabian Nights' Entertainment from antique bookstores.
Many movies have drawn inspiration from the stories found in the Arabian Nights' Entertainment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: One Thousand and One Nights - another name for Arabian Nights' Entertainment

Etymology

From Arabic 'Alf Layla wa-Layla' (One Thousand and One Nights), first translated into French in the early 18th century as 'Les Mille et Une Nuits'.
