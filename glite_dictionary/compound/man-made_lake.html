man-made lake

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænˌmeɪd leɪk / (AmE), / ˈmænˌmeɪd leɪk / (BrE)
A lake that has been artificially created by humans, often to store water for a community or for resource management.
artificially created lake

Examples

The city gets its water supply from a man-made lake on the outskirts.
Many recreational activities are held around the man-made lake every summer.
After the dam was built, a man-made lake formed behind it, changing the landscape considerably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reservoir - an artificial or natural lake for storing water
