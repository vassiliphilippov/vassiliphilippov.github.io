communication channel

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ˈtʃænəl / (BrE)
a method or system used to convey information or messages between people, groups, or devices
method for conveying information

Examples

We established a new communication channel to facilitate discussions between departments.
All employee feedback should be sent through the official communication channel provided by the company.
Social media has become an important communication channel for businesses to reach customers.

Synset Examples

it must go through official channels
lines of communication were set up between the two firms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: channel - a means of communication or passage
