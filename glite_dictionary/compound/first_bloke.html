First Bloke

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst bloʊk / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst bləʊk / (BrE)
the male partner of the Australian Prime Minister or a head of state, especially when formally recognized by this title
male partner of Australia's Prime Minister or head of state
british

Examples

Australia's First Bloke is known for his community service.
The First Bloke attended the charity event yesterday.
The First Bloke made a surprise appearance at the rally.

Etymology

Australian English; from 'first' (principal) + 'bloke' (man, fellow; British and Australian slang for man).
