macaroni and cheese

1. NOUN

/ məˌkæroʊni ən ˈtʃiz / (AmE), / məˌkærəni ənd ˈtʃiːz / (BrE)
A dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with a cheese sauce, usually baked or cooked together.
pasta dish with cheese sauce

Examples

My favorite comfort food is macaroni and cheese fresh out of the oven.
She brought a big bowl of macaroni and cheese to the family picnic.
For dinner, we had homemade macaroni and cheese with a crispy breadcrumb topping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mac and cheese - informal shortened form of macaroni and cheese
