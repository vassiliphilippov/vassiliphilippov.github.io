barred pickerel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrd ˈpɪkəɹəl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːd ˈpɪkərəl / (BrE)
A small but lively species of pickerel fish found in the Atlantic coastal states of North America.
species of small pickerel fish

Examples

Anglers often seek the barred pickerel for its spirited fight despite its small size.
The barred pickerel can be distinguished by its dark vertical stripes and slender body.
While fishing along the Atlantic coast, she caught a colorful barred pickerel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pickerel - a type of freshwater fish in the pike family
