box family

1. NOUN

/ bɑks ˈfæməli / (AmE), / bɒks ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
A family (Buxaceae) of widely distributed evergreen shrubs and trees, including the boxwood.
family of evergreen shrubs and trees (Buxaceae)

Examples

Several species in the box family are popular in formal garden designs.
The box family includes both shrubs and small trees found worldwide.
Many plants in the box family have small, glossy leaves and dense growth patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxwood - an evergreen shrub or small tree; member of the box family
