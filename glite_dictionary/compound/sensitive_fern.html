sensitive fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnsətɪv fɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsətɪv fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern native to eastern North America and eastern Asia, with spreading fronds that may fold slightly when touched.
a fern with folding fronds, native to eastern North America and Asia

Examples

The sensitive fern thrives in moist woods and along stream banks.
Gardeners admire the delicate fronds of the sensitive fern.
When brushed lightly, the sensitive fern sometimes appears to fold its leaves.
