ship route

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪp raʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪp ruːt / (BrE)
a regularly used path at sea followed by ships or vessels
regularly used sea path for ships

Examples

The cargo vessel followed the established ship route to avoid potential hazards.
Many international ship routes are monitored for safety and environmental protection.
Changes in weather conditions can force ships to deviate from their usual ship route.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea lane - a regularly used route in the sea for ships
