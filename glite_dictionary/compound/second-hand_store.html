second-hand store

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkəndˌhænd stɔr / (AmE), / ˈsɛkəndˌhænd stɔː / (BrE)
a shop that sells used goods at reduced prices
shop selling used goods

Examples

We found a vintage lamp at the second-hand store downtown.
She volunteers every Saturday at the local second-hand store.
Many students buy their furniture from a second-hand store to save money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrift shop - a shop selling secondhand goods, often for charity
  • Degree Higher: antique shop - a shop selling collectibles and antiques
  • Antonym: boutique - a small shop, typically selling fashionable clothes or accessories; usually new items
