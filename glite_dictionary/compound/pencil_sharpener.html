pencil sharpener

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnsəl ˌʃɑːrpnər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəl ˌʃɑːpənə / (BrE)
A device used to make the tip of a pencil sharp by shaving its wood and graphite.
device for sharpening pencils

Examples

She reached into her bag for a pencil sharpener before the test began.
The classroom has an electric pencil sharpener mounted on the wall.
I always carry a small pencil sharpener in my pencil case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharpener - a device for making something sharp
