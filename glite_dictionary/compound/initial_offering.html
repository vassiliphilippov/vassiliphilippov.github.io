initial offering

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈnɪʃəl ˈɔfərɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃəl ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
a company's first offer to sell its stock to the public, usually through a stock exchange
first public sale of a company's stock

Examples

The tech company's initial offering attracted major investors from around the world.
After the initial offering, shares were quickly traded on the open market.
They raised millions of dollars through their initial offering last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: initial public offering - the first sale of stock by a company to the public (IPO)
