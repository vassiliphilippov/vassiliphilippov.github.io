mountain starwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈstɑrˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈstɑːwɜːt / (BrE)
A small boreal or alpine flowering plant of the sandwort family, typically found in mountainous or northern regions.
small alpine or boreal sandwort plant

Examples

The mountain starwort thrives in the harsh conditions of high-altitude meadows.
Botanists discovered several species of mountain starwort during their research trip to the tundra.
Tiny white flowers make the mountain starwort easy to spot among the rocky alpine terrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwort - any plant of the genus Arenaria, small flowering plants usually found in sandy soils
