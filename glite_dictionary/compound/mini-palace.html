mini-palace

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪni ˌpæləs / (AmE), / ˈmɪni ˌpælɪs / (BrE)
A small but luxuriously decorated or furnished home or space.
small, luxurious home or space

Examples

They renovated their apartment into a mini-palace with gold accents and velvet drapes.
Even a modest cabin can feel like a mini-palace when it's tastefully decorated and well-lit.
She refers to her walk-in closet as her own private mini-palace.

Synset Examples

Her backyard is now a minipalace with fairy lights and decorations.

Etymology

Formed from 'mini-' (meaning small) and 'palace' (a large, splendid house).
