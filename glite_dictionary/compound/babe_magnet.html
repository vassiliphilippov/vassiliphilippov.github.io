babe magnet

1. NOUN

/ beɪb ˈmæɡ.nət / (AmE), / beɪb ˈmæɡ.nət / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is highly attractive to potential romantic partners
person attractive to potential romantic partners
informal

Examples

His new sports car has turned him into a real babe magnet.
Some people think a puppy is the ultimate babe magnet.
Jake's confidence and wit make him something of a babe magnet at parties.

Synset Examples

That smile makes him a real babe magnet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartthrob - an attractive and charismatic person, especially one who is the object of romantic attention
  • Synonym: chick magnet - a person, often a man, who is very attractive to women
