flight maneuver

1. NOUN

/ flaɪt məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / flaɪt məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)
an action or movement performed by an aircraft to change its flight path or attitude
movement or action performed by an aircraft

Examples

The pilot performed a flight maneuver to avoid turbulence during the descent.
Learning different flight maneuvers is essential for any aspiring fighter pilot.
The instructor demonstrated a complex flight maneuver during the training session.
