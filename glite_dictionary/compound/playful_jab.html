playful jab

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl dʒæb / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl̩ dʒab / (BrE)
a remark or comment intended to tease or amuse without causing offense
teasing or light-hearted remark

Examples

He couldn't resist making a playful jab about my new haircut.
Sara gave Tom a playful jab during the meeting, and everyone laughed.
Their conversation was full of playful jabs, but it was all in good fun.

Synset Examples

The term wog is often used as a playful jab among friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - to make fun of or provoke in a playful way
  • Synonym: quip - a witty or sarcastic remark
