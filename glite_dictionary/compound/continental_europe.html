Continental Europe

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːn.tɪˈnen.t̬əl ˈjʊr.əp / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.təl ˈjʊə.rəp / (BrE)
the mainland of Europe, excluding its islands such as the British Isles
mainland of Europe (excluding islands)

Examples

She spent her summer backpacking across Continental Europe, visiting several countries on the mainland.
Goods imported from Continental Europe often arrive by ferry or train through the Channel Tunnel.
The climate in Continental Europe can be quite different from that of the British Isles.

Synset Examples

Englishmen like to visit the Continent but they wouldn't like to live there
