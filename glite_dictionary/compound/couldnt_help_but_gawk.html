to couldn't help but gawk

1. VERB

/ ˌkʊdənt ˈhɛlp bət ˈɡɔk / (AmE), / ˌkʊdnt ˈhɛlp bət ˈɡɔːk / (BrE)
Was unable to prevent oneself from staring in a rude or amazed way.
unable to resist staring
informal

Examples

I couldn't help but gawk at the breathtaking view from the mountain top.
Many tourists couldn't help but gawk at the celebrity as she walked by.
He couldn't help but gawk when he saw the size of the luxurious mansion.

Synset Examples

She couldn't help but gawk at the stunning dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gawk - to stare openly and stupidly
