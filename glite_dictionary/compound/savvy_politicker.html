savvy politicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsævi pəˈlɪtɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsævi pəˈlɪtɪkə / (BrE)
A person who is shrewd and astute in political operations or maneuvering.
shrewd political operator
informal

Examples

The mayor is a savvy politicker who always gets support for his proposals.
Everyone underestimated her, but she turned out to be a remarkably savvy politicker.
You need to be more than just passionate—you must be a savvy politicker to survive in this game.

Synset Examples

He’s known as a savvy politicker in local politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politician - someone who is active in party politics
  • Synonym: operator - a person who operates in a shrewd or manipulative way, especially in politics

Etymology

From 'savvy', informal for shrewd or knowledgeable (from Spanish 'sabe', from Latin 'sapere', to know), and 'politicker', derived from 'politics'.
