creative thinking

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪtɪv ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / kriːˈeɪtɪv ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the ability to use imagination and new ideas to create something or solve problems
the ability to think creatively

Examples

Her creative thinking led the team to a unique solution to the problem.
The company encourages creative thinking to drive innovation and progress.
Teachers often design activities to foster creative thinking in their students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovation - the introduction of new ideas or methods
  • Antonym: critical thinking - objective analysis and evaluation of issues to form a judgment
