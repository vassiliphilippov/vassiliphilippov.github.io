silver spoon upbringing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ spuːn ˌʌpˈbrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə spuːn ˌʌpˈbrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a childhood or background characterized by wealth and privilege
privileged or wealthy background

Examples

His silver spoon upbringing gave him access to elite schools and exclusive networks.
Many people believe a silver spoon upbringing can shelter you from real-world problems.
Despite her silver spoon upbringing, she was known for her generosity and down-to-earth attitude.

Synset Examples

His silver spoon upbringing shaped his worldview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: born with a silver spoon in one's mouth - to be born into wealth and privilege

Etymology

From the phrase 'born with a silver spoon in one's mouth,' symbolizing inherited wealth or privilege.
