air freight service

1. NOUN

/ ɛr freɪt ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / eə freɪt ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a commercial service that transports goods by aircraft from one place to another
service for transporting goods by air

Examples

The company launched a new air freight service to meet growing international demand.
We chose an air freight service to ensure the products arrived quickly and safely.
Many businesses rely on air freight services to transport perishable goods around the world.

Synset Examples

The company specializes in air freight services.
