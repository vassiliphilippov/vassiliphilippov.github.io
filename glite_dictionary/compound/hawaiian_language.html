Hawaiian language

1. NOUN

/ həˈwaɪ.i.ən ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i.ən ˈlaŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Polynesian language spoken by the native people of Hawaii
Polynesian language of Hawaii

Examples

She is studying the Hawaiian language at university to connect with her heritage.
Many traditional chants are still performed in the Hawaiian language.
Revival of the Hawaiian language has been a significant cultural movement in recent decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hawaiian - the native language of Hawaii
  • Synonym: Maori language - Polynesian language spoken in New Zealand

Etymology

From Hawaii, the name of the islands where the language originates, plus 'language.'
