perspective on

1. NOUN

/ pərˈspɛktɪv ɑn / (AmE), / pəˈspɛktɪv ɒn / (BrE)
a particular way of thinking about or viewing a specific subject or issue
a particular viewpoint on something

Examples

She offered a fresh perspective on the debate that shifted everyone's thinking.
His perspective on success is shaped by years of failure and persistence.
This report provides an international perspective on climate change challenges.

Synset Examples

Her perspective on the issue was refreshing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viewpoint - a particular attitude or way of considering a matter
  • Synonym: standpoint - an attitude to or outlook on issues, typically arising from one's circumstances or beliefs
