to lock pick

1. VERB

/ lɑk pɪk / (AmE), / lɒk pɪk / (BrE)
To open a lock without using its key, typically by manipulating the mechanism with special tools.
open a lock without a key

Examples

The burglar tried to lock pick the door before escaping.
She learned how to lock pick as part of her locksmith training.
He was caught attempting to lock pick the office safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick - to open a lock with a special instrument
