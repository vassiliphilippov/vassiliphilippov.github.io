highway engineer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌweɪ ɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.weɪ ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a civil engineer who specializes in the design, construction, and maintenance of roads and highways
engineer specializing in road and highway design

Examples

After graduating, she became a highway engineer working on major infrastructure projects across the state.
A highway engineer must carefully consider traffic flow and safety when designing new roads.
The city consulted a highway engineer to improve the intersection and reduce congestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil engineer - an engineer who designs and maintains public works
