District of Criminals

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪstrɪkt əv ˈkrɪmɪnəlz / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrɪkt əv ˈkrɪmɪnəlz / (BrE)
a humorous or fictional name for a place reputed to have high levels of crime
humorous/fictitious crime-infamous place
informal

Examples

The mayor promised to change the reputation of the District of Criminals by increasing police presence.
Locals joke that after dark, the District of Criminals is off-limits to everyone.
The media sensationalized the old neighborhood by calling it the District of Criminals.
