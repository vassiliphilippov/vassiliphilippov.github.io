power breakfast

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚ ˌbrɛkfəst / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌbrɛkfəst / (BrE)
a meeting where influential people discuss business matters over breakfast
business breakfast meeting

Examples

The executives scheduled a power breakfast to finalize the deal.
She attended a power breakfast with industry leaders at the downtown hotel.
Many major decisions are made during a power breakfast rather than in a boardroom.
