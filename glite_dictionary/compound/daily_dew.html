daily dew

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli duː / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli djuː / (BrE)
any of several insect-eating bog plants of the genus Drosera, characterized by leaves covered with sticky hairs that trap and digest insects
insect-eating plant of the genus Drosera

Examples

The leaves of the daily dew glistened with sticky droplets in the morning sunlight.
Insect lovers are fascinated by the way daily dew traps its prey on its mucilaginous hairs.
During our nature walk, we found a rare species of daily dew seducing flies on the bog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sundew - another common name for plants of the genus Drosera
