to tell a tale

1. VERB

/ tɛl ə teɪl / (AmE), / tɛl ə teɪl / (BrE)
to narrate a story or give an account of events; sometimes used figuratively when something reveals evidence of what has happened
narrate a story or reveal evidence

Examples

The ancient walls tell a tale of centuries of history and conflict.
After the ordeal, he was grateful to still be alive to tell the tale.
Her worn hands tell a tale of years spent working in the fields.

Synset Examples

The ruins tell their own tale of a past civilization.
She faced many challenges but lived to tell the tale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to narrate - to tell a story or describe a sequence of events
  • Synonym: to relate - to give an account of; to tell
  • Antonym: to conceal - to hide or prevent from being known
