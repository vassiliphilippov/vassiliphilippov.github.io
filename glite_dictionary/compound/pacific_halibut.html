Pacific halibut

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈhælɪbət / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈhælɪbət / (BrE)
A large flatfish of the flounder family, found in the northern Pacific Ocean and valued as a food fish.
species of flatfish in the northern Pacific

Examples

The Pacific halibut is a popular catch among commercial and recreational fishermen.
Fresh Pacific halibut fillets are prized for their delicate flavor and firm texture.
The annual migration patterns of the Pacific halibut have been studied extensively by marine biologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halibut - large flatfish of the flounder family
