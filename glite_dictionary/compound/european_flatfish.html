European flatfish

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈflætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈflætˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a common flatfish found in European waters, valued as a food fish
flatfish species found in Europe

Examples

The European flatfish is highly valued in many coastal cuisines.
Fishermen in the North Sea often catch European flatfish for local markets.
A traditional recipe calls for baking European flatfish with lemon and herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaice - a common European species of flatfish
