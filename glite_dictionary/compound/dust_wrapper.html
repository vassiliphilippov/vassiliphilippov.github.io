dust wrapper

1. NOUN

/ dʌst ˈræpər / (AmE), / dʌst ˈræpə / (BrE)
a removable paper cover that protects a book and often contains promotional information or artwork
paper jacket for a book

Examples

She carefully removed the dust wrapper before reading the newly purchased novel.
The dust wrapper on this edition features a striking illustration by a famous artist.
Collectors often look for rare books with the original dust wrapper in good condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust jacket - a removable paper cover to protect a book
