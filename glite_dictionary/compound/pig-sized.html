pig-sized

1. ADJ

/ pɪɡ saɪzd / (AmE), / pɪɡ saɪzd / (BrE)
having approximately the same size as a pig
about the size of a pig

Examples

The mysterious creature spotted in the woods was described as pig-sized by several witnesses.
A pig-sized boulder blocked the narrow mountain trail, making it hard to pass.
He built a model spaceship that was nearly pig-sized, much larger than anyone expected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog-sized - having approximately the same size as a dog
  • Degree Lower: cat-sized - having approximately the same size as a cat
  • Degree Higher: cow-sized - having approximately the same size as a cow
