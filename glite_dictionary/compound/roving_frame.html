roving frame

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊvɪŋ freɪm / (AmE), / ˈrəʊvɪŋ freɪm / (BrE)
A textile machine that draws and slightly twists sliver to produce roving, preparing it for spinning.
machine transforming sliver into roving

Examples

The factory upgraded its equipment by installing a modern roving frame for better efficiency.
Workers carefully monitored the roving frame to ensure the yarn was consistent and strong.
A malfunction in the roving frame can disrupt the entire spinning process in the textile mill.

Synset Examples

Simplex machines give the attenuated sliver a small amount of twist to ensure the required strength of the roving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simplex machine - alternative term for a roving frame in textiles
