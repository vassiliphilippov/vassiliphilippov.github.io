thrill killer

1. NOUN

/ θrɪl ˈkɪlɚ / (AmE), / θrɪl ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that spoils or diminishes excitement or enjoyment
something that spoils excitement
informal

Examples

Heavy regulations can be a real thrill killer at amusement parks.
His lack of enthusiasm was the ultimate thrill killer for our plans.
Unexpected delays acted as a thrill killer for the event's atmosphere.

Synset Examples

Bureaucracy can often be a thrill killer in projects.
His constant negativity was a thrill killer for the team.
The rain was a thrill killer for our outdoor plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils other people's enjoyment
