nice to see someone again

1. X

/ naɪs tə si ˈsʌmwʌn əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / naɪs tə si ˈsʌmwɒn əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
A polite expression used to show pleasure at meeting or seeing someone a second or subsequent time.
expression of pleasure at a reunion

Examples

It's always nice to see someone again after such a long time.
She smiled and said it was nice to see someone again at the reunion.
He greeted me warmly, saying how nice to see someone again it was.

Synset Examples

It was nice to see you again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good to see someone again - polite expression of pleasure at meeting someone again
  • Degree Lower: nice to meet someone - polite greeting on meeting someone for the first time
