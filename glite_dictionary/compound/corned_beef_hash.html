corned beef hash

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn(d) bif hæʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnd biːf hæʃ / (BrE)
A dish made of chopped corned beef, potatoes, and onions, mixed together and usually fried.
dish of corned beef, potatoes, and onions

Examples

My favorite breakfast is corned beef hash with a fried egg on top.
She ordered corned beef hash at the diner when she couldn't decide what to eat.
Leftover potatoes and meat are perfect for making homemade corned beef hash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hash - dish of chopped meat and potatoes
