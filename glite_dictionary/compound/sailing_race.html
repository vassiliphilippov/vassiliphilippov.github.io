sailing race

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlɪŋ reɪs / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪŋ reɪs / (BrE)
a competitive event in which crews race yachts against each other over a set course
a race between crews of yachts

Examples

The annual sailing race attracts competitors from around the world.
Weather conditions play a crucial role in the outcome of any sailing race.
She trained all summer to prepare for the prestigious sailing race next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regatta - a series of boat races, especially those involving sailing boats
