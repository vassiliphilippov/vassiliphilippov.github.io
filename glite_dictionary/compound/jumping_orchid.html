jumping orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A species of orchid with both male and female flowers on the same raceme, notable for ejecting pollen when a sensitive part is touched.
an orchid species that ejects pollen on touch

Examples

The jumping orchid is fascinating because it ejects pollen when touched.
Botanists studied the jumping orchid to understand its unique pollination process.
Many tropical gardens feature the jumping orchid due to its unusual floral mechanism.
