friend with benefit

1. NOUN

/ frɛnd wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (AmE), / frɛnd wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
A friend with whom one has a sexual relationship without commitment to a romantic partnership.
friend who is also a casual sexual partner
informal

Examples

He admitted that she was just his friend with benefit rather than a girlfriend.
Some people prefer having a friend with benefit over being in a committed relationship.
She made it clear that she wanted to remain simply a friend with benefit, nothing more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friends with benefits - friends who regularly have sex without a romantic relationship
