volleyball player

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑliˌbɔl ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈvɒliˌbɔːl ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of volleyball, either professionally or recreationally
person who plays volleyball

Examples

The volleyball player spiked the ball with impressive strength during the match.
As a dedicated volleyball player, she trains six days a week.
Many volleyball players participate in both indoor and beach tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise
