airplane mechanics

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpleɪn məˈkænɪks / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn mɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
the skill or practice of building and repairing airplanes
the craft of building and repairing airplanes

Examples

He studied airplane mechanics before working for the airline.
The course in airplane mechanics covers both construction and repair of aircraft.
Advances in airplane mechanics have made flying much safer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aeronautical engineering - the study and design of aircraft
