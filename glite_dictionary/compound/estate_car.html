estate car

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsteɪt kɑr / (AmE), / ɪˈsteɪt kɑː / (BrE)
a car with an extended rear section and a rear door, providing extra space for luggage or cargo behind the rear seats
car with extra cargo space at the back
british

Examples

They decided to rent an estate car for their family holiday to have enough space for luggage.
The new model of estate car offers improved fuel efficiency and ample cargo capacity.
He prefers driving an estate car because it suits his lifestyle with two dogs and outdoor gear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: station wagon - a car with extended rear cargo area and rear door (chiefly North American)

Etymology

British English term, originally referring to cars used for transporting guests and luggage on country estates.
