railroad ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɔd ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪlrəʊd ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that entitles the holder to travel on a railroad train
ticket for train travel

Examples

He handed the conductor his railroad ticket before boarding the train.
Without a valid railroad ticket, passengers are not allowed onto the platform.
She kept her old railroad ticket as a souvenir from the memorable journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train ticket - a ticket for travel by train; especially in British English
  • Degree Lower: ticket - a piece of paper or card that gives the holder a certain right, especially to enter a place or travel
