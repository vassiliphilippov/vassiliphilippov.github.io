stickup man

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪkˌʌp mæn / (AmE), / ˈstɪkʌp man / (BrE)
a man who commits armed robbery, especially by threatening people to steal from them
male armed robber

Examples

The stickup man threatened the cashier with a gun and demanded all the money in the register.
Police arrested the stickup man shortly after the armed robbery at the convenience store.
The neighborhood grew anxious after a stickup man was reported in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: robber - a person who steals, especially by force or threat
  • Synonym: thief - a person who steals another person's property
