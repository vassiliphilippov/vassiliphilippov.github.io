suburban area

1. NOUN

/ səˈbɝbən ˈɛə.ri.ə / (AmE), / səˈbɜːbən ˈeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
A residential district situated on the outskirts of a city, typically characterized by family homes and lower population density than urban centers.
residential district near a city

Examples

Many families move to a suburban area to enjoy quieter neighborhoods and better schools.
The city's rapid growth led to the expansion of the suburban area in all directions.
Commuters from the suburban area often face heavy traffic during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suburb - an outlying district of a city, especially a residential one
  • Antonym: urban area - a region surrounding a city, typically with high population density
