parade ground

1. NOUN

/ pəˈreɪd ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / pəˈreɪd ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a large, level area used for holding parades, especially in military settings
area for holding parades

Examples

The soldiers assembled on the parade ground for the morning inspection.
After the ceremony, the cadets marched proudly across the parade ground.
A statue honoring veterans stands at the edge of the parade ground.
