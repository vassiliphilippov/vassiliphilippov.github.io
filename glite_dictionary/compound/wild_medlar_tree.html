wild medlar tree

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈmɛdlɚ tri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmɛdlə triː / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree of southern Africa that bears edible fruit.
southern African fruit tree

Examples

The wild medlar tree is known for its sweet, edible fruit enjoyed by local communities.
Many animals in the region feed on the fruit of the wild medlar tree.
Researchers are studying the wild medlar tree for its potential nutritional benefits.
