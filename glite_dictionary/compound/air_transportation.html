air transportation

1. NOUN

/ ɛr ˌtrænspərˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛə ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the movement of people or goods by aircraft over distances through the air
transport of people or goods by aircraft

Examples

Recent advances in air transportation have significantly reduced travel time between continents.
Cargo companies rely on air transportation for the rapid delivery of urgent goods.
The growth of global tourism is closely tied to the expansion of air transportation networks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviation - the operation and production of aircraft
  • Antonym: rail transportation - the movement of goods or people by train
  • Antonym: sea transportation - the movement of goods or people by ships or boats
