true gent

1. NOUN

/ tru dʒɛnt / (AmE), / truː dʒɛnt / (BrE)
a man who behaves in a very courteous, gentlemanly manner
courteous, gentlemanly man
informal british

Examples

Everyone agreed that Jonathan was a true gent at the wedding reception.
He proved himself a true gent by helping her with her heavy bags.
It's rare to meet a true gent these days.

Synset Examples

He was a true gent, always holding the door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentleman - a polite or courteous man
