immaculately clean

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈmækjələtli klin / (AmE), / ɪˈmækjʊlətli kliːn / (BrE)
extremely clean and tidy; spotless.
extremely clean; spotless

Examples

The kitchen was immaculately clean after hours of preparation for the guests.
She keeps her home immaculately clean despite having three small children.
I was impressed by how immaculately clean the office was when I arrived.

Synset Examples

The lavatory was immaculately clean after the renovation.
