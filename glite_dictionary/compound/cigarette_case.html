cigarette case

1. NOUN

/ sɪɡəˌrɛt keɪs / (AmE), / sɪɡərɛt keɪs / (BrE)
a small, flat container used for holding cigarettes, often carried in a purse or pocket
small container for holding cigarettes

Examples

He pulled a silver cigarette case from his pocket and offered me a smoke.
Her antique cigarette case was decorated with delicate engravings.
The cigarette case made a clicking sound when she snapped it shut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: case - a container used for holding or protecting something
