TV star

1. NOUN

/ tiː viː stɑr / (AmE), / tiː viː stɑː / (BrE)
A person who is famous for appearing in television programmes, especially as a main performer or actor.
famous person on television

Examples

After winning the popular drama series, she became a TV star overnight.
Many children dream of becoming a TV star when they grow up.
He used his fame as a TV star to start a successful business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television celebrity - a person who is well known for their appearances on television
