backhand shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌhænd ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbakhænd ʃɒt / (BrE)
a stroke in tennis or other sports played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke
stroke with back of hand facing direction

Examples

She won the point with a powerful backhand shot down the line.
His backhand shot was surprisingly accurate despite the awkward position.
Practicing your backhand shot can greatly improve your tennis game.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forehand shot - stroke played with the palm of the hand facing the direction of the stroke
