Khoikhoi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔɪkɔɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪkɔɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the group of languages belonging to the Khoisan family, spoken by the Khoikhoi people of Namibia and South Africa
Khoisan languages of the Khoikhoi people

Examples

Many linguists have studied the Khoikhoi language to understand its unique click consonants.
She is fluent in several Khoikhoi languages, thanks to her work in Namibia.
The Khoikhoi language is an important part of southern Africa's cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Khoisan language - language family including San and Khoikhoi languages

Etymology

From Khoikhoi, meaning 'men of men' in the Khoikhoi language.
