favorite drinking hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərət ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ həʊl / (BrE)
a bar, pub, or similar establishment that is regularly frequented and popular with a certain group of people
popular bar or pub regularly visited

Examples

Every Friday, we meet at our favorite drinking hole after work.
The old tavern became their favorite drinking hole during college.
She suggested we find a new favorite drinking hole with better music.

Synset Examples

The local pub is a favorite drinking hole for college students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watering hole - a place, especially a bar, where people gather to drink alcohol
