second chance

1. NOUN

/ ˈsekənd tʃæns / (AmE), / ˈsekənd tʃɑːns / (BrE)
an opportunity to try again after failing or being unsuccessful the first time
opportunity to try again

Examples

Everyone deserves a second chance to prove themselves after making a mistake.
The coach decided to give the player a second chance despite his earlier errors.
After being released from prison, she was grateful for a second chance at a better life.

Synset Examples

She joked that every sinner deserves a second chance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opportunity - a time or set of circumstances that makes it possible to do something
