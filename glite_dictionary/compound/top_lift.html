top lift

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp lɪft / (AmE), / ˈtɒp lɪft / (BrE)
the bottom layer of a shoe heel, usually made of a durable material to resist wear
bottom layer of a shoe heel

Examples

The cobbler replaced the worn top lift on my boots to extend their life.
You can tell the quality of a shoe by the material used for its top lift.
Over time, the top lift may need to be replaced to maintain proper support.
