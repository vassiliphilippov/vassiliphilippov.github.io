dressed ore

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛst ɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛst ɔː / (BrE)
ore from which impurities have been removed, leaving the desired mineral
purified ore with impurities removed

Examples

After processing, only the dressed ore was transported to the refinery for further extraction.
The mining facility specializes in separating impurities to produce high-quality dressed ore.
Export contracts usually specify the grade of dressed ore rather than the raw extracted material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concentrate - the product of ore after enrichment; similar meaning in mining
