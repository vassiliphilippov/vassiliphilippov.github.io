well-endowed with resources

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl ɪnˈdaʊd wɪð rɪˈsɔrsɪz / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ɪnˈdaʊd wɪð rɪˈsɔːsɪz / (BrE)
Richly supplied or provided with resources, especially referring to organizations, institutions, or places that possess ample or abundant resources.
richly supplied with resources

Examples

The university is well-endowed with resources, which allows it to support a wide range of student programs.
Several museums in the city are well-endowed with resources thanks to generous donations.
A company well-endowed with resources can invest heavily in research and development.

Synset Examples

The school is well-endowed with resources for arts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-resourced - having sufficient or plentiful resources
