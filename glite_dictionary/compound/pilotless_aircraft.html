pilotless aircraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪlətləs ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlətləs ˈeəˌkrɑːft / (BrE)
An aircraft operated by remote control without an onboard pilot.
remotely operated aircraft without a pilot

Examples

The military tested a new pilotless aircraft capable of staying airborne for over twenty-four hours.
Agricultural companies are investing in pilotless aircraft to monitor crops more efficiently.
As technology advances, we are likely to see more pilotless aircraft in both commercial and private sectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drone - an unmanned aircraft controlled remotely or autonomously
  • Synonym: unmanned aerial vehicle - an aircraft operated without a human pilot onboard
