Christmas green

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs ɡriːn / (BrE)
any of various club mosses with long creeping stems and upright branches, often used for decoration at Christmas.
a club moss used for Christmas decoration

Examples

Many people use Christmas green to create festive wreaths during the holidays.
The forest floor was covered in thick patches of Christmas green beneath the trees.
Florists often sell bundles of Christmas green for seasonal arrangements.
