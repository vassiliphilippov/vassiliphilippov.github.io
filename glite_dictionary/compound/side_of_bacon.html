side of bacon

1. NOUN

/ saɪd əv ˈbeɪkən / (AmE), / saɪd əv ˈbeɪkən / (BrE)
A large piece of bacon consisting of the cured and salted whole side of a pig, usually including the abdominal wall; often sliced before sale or use.
whole cured side of pork (bacon)

Examples

The butcher delivered a fresh side of bacon to the restaurant early this morning.
She hung a side of bacon in the cellar to cure for the winter.
They served thick slices cut from a smoked side of bacon at breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacon - salted and cured meat from the back or sides of a pig
