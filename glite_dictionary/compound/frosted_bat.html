frosted bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɑstəd bæt / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstɪd bat / (BrE)
a Eurasian bat species with a fur coat marked by distinctive white-tipped hairs
Eurasian bat with white-tipped fur

Examples

The frosted bat is easily recognized by the white tips on its fur.
Researchers studied the habitats of the frosted bat in Eastern Europe.
A frosted bat was spotted roosting in the old barn.
