head restraint

1. NOUN

/ hɛd rɪˈstreɪnt / (AmE), / hɛd rɪˈstreɪnt / (BrE)
A padded support attached to the upper part of a car seat, designed to limit head movement and reduce the risk of whiplash in a collision.
car seat safety support for the head

Examples

The car's head restraint is adjustable to fit drivers of different heights.
Properly positioning your head restraint can help prevent neck injuries in an accident.
Many modern vehicles come equipped with active head restraints for increased safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headrest - a cushion on the back of a seat supporting the head
