social status

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈstætəs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈstætəs / (BrE)
A person's position or rank in a social hierarchy, often determined by factors such as wealth, occupation, education, and family background.
a person's rank in society

Examples

In many cultures, clothing and manners are used to express social status.
She achieved a high social status through her successful career and philanthropic work.
Changes in the economy can affect the social status of entire communities.

Synset Examples

the British are more aware of social status than Americans are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: status - position or rank in relation to others, especially in a social context
