ader wax

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪdər wæks / (AmE), / ˈɑːdə wæks / (BrE)
a natural waxy mineral, composed of hydrocarbons, found with petroleum and used in making ceresin and candles
a waxy mineral found with petroleum

Examples

Pure ader wax was extracted from the mineral deposit for laboratory testing.
The factory uses ader wax as an ingredient in the production of high-quality candles.
Some ceresin blends are made by refining natural ader wax.
