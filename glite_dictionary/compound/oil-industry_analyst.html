oil-industry analyst

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˌɪndəstri ˈænəlɪst / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˌɪndəstri ˈænəlɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to analyze and report on trends, developments, and financial aspects of the oil industry
an expert who analyzes the oil industry

Examples

The oil-industry analyst predicted that crude prices would rise due to political instability.
As an oil-industry analyst, she often appears on news programs to interpret market shifts.
Many investors rely on reports from an oil-industry analyst when making decisions about energy stocks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industry analyst - expert who analyzes a particular industry
