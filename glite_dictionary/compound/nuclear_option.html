nuclear option

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈɑp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈɒp.ʃən / (BrE)
An extreme or drastic course of action used as a last resort, especially when all other alternatives have failed.
drastic last resort action

Examples

The union leaders warned that striking was their nuclear option if talks broke down.
Some politicians argued that invoking the nuclear option would damage the institution's traditions.
Facing mounting losses, the company considered closing its main plant as a nuclear option.

Synset Examples

We may have to consider the nuclear option if negotiations fail.
The CEO mentioned the nuclear option during the crisis meeting.
Using the nuclear option would be a last resort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last resort - a final course of action when all else fails
  • Synonym: drastic measure - an extreme action taken in response to a situation

Etymology

Based on the term 'nuclear' in reference to the power and consequences of nuclear weapons, applied figuratively to emphasize extremity.
