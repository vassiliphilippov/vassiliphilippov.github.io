farmer's calendar

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmərz ˈkælənˌdɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməz ˈkælɪndə / (BrE)
An annual publication containing weather forecasts, planting schedules, and other practical information arranged by the calendar year, primarily for agricultural use.
annual publication with weather forecasts and agricultural info

Examples

Many rural families rely on the farmer's calendar to plan their planting and harvesting seasons.
The farmer's calendar includes phases of the moon, weather predictions, and gardening tips.
My grandfather never missed buying the latest farmer's calendar each spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almanac - annual calendar with weather forecasts and miscellany
