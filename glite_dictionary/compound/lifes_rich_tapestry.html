life's rich tapestry

1. NOUN

/ laɪfs rɪtʃ ˈtæpəstri / (AmE), / laɪfs rɪtʃ ˈtæpɪstri / (BrE)
the complex and varied nature of people's experiences throughout life, both joyful and challenging
the variety and complexity of life experiences

Examples

Every person you meet weaves a new thread into life's rich tapestry.
The ups and downs we face are what make life's rich tapestry so fascinating.
She believes that even failures contribute to life's rich tapestry.

Synset Examples

Every experience, good or bad, is part of life's rich tapestry.
Traveling the world has helped me be part of life's rich tapestry.
Family traditions truly be part of life's rich tapestry.
