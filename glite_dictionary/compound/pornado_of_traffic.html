pornado of traffic

1. NOUN

/ pɔrˈneɪdoʊ əv ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / pɔːˈneɪdəʊ əv ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
A sudden, overwhelming surge of website visitors, often triggered by viral content or a major online event.
a sudden surge of website visitors
slang informal

Examples

After the video went viral, the website was hit by a pornado of traffic unlike anything they'd seen before.
The server crashed due to the sudden pornado of traffic following the celebrity's leaked photos.
The marketing team was unprepared for the pornado of traffic generated by their controversial campaign.

Synset Examples

After the leak, the site experienced a pornado of traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic spike - a sharp increase in website visitors over a short period
  • Synonym: traffic surge - a sudden increase in the volume of web traffic

Etymology

Blend of 'porn' and 'tornado,' used metaphorically for intensity and speed.
