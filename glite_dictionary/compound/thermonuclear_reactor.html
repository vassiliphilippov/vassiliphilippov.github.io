thermonuclear reactor

1. NOUN

/ ˌθɝː.moʊˈnuː.kli.ɚ riˈæktər / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məʊˈnjuː.kli.ə riˈæktə / (BrE)
A nuclear reactor in which energy is produced by controlled nuclear fusion reactions.
reactor generating energy via nuclear fusion
medical

Examples

Scientists are working to design a thermonuclear reactor capable of providing limitless clean energy.
The development of a functional thermonuclear reactor could revolutionize the global energy industry.
Funding for the international thermonuclear reactor project has reached several billion dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuclear reactor - a device that produces energy from controlled nuclear reactions

Etymology

From thermo- (relating to heat) + nuclear (pertaining to the nucleus of an atom) + reactor.
