jail cell

1. NOUN

/ dʒeɪl sɛl / (AmE), / dʒeɪl sɛl / (BrE)
a small room in a jail or prison where a prisoner is held
room where a prisoner is kept

Examples

The guard walked past each jail cell to ensure all the prisoners were accounted for.
She spent the night in a jail cell after being arrested for protest.
He paced nervously inside his jail cell, waiting for his court hearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cell - a small room in a prison
  • Synonym: prison cell - a small room in a prison where a prisoner is held
