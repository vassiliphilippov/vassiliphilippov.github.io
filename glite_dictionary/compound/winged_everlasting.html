winged everlasting

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋd ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋd ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
An Australian plant (Ammobium alatum) widely grown for its silvery-white flowers with bright yellow centers and long winged stems.
Australian flowering plant with silvery blooms

Examples

The winged everlasting is prized by gardeners for its striking contrast of silver and gold.
Dried winged everlasting flowers are often used in long-lasting floral arrangements.
We planted several winged everlasting in the rock garden to add year-round interest.
