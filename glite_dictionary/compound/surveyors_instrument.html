surveyor's instrument

1. NOUN

/ sɝˈveɪ.ɚz ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (AmE), / səˈveɪ.əz ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
a tool or device used by surveyors for measuring and mapping land or structures
tool for measuring and mapping land

Examples

The surveyor's instrument helped determine the boundaries for the new construction site.
He kept each surveyor's instrument carefully calibrated to ensure accurate measurements.
Before beginning the mapping, she set up her surveyor's instrument on a stable tripod.
