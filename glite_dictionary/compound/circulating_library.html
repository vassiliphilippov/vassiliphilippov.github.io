circulating library

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrkjəˌleɪtɪŋ ˈlaɪbrɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊleɪtɪŋ ˈlaɪbrəri / (BrE)
A library that lends books to its members or subscribers for use outside the premises.
library lending books for external use

Examples

The town's circulating library was a popular gathering place for readers of all ages.
She became a member of the local circulating library to borrow new novels every month.
In the nineteenth century, the circulating library played a vital role in making books accessible to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lending library - a library that loans books for external use
