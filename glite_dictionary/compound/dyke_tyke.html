dyke tyke

1. NOUN

/ daɪk taɪk / (AmE), / daɪk taɪk / (BrE)
an assertive and confident young woman, especially one who is openly defiant or unconventional
assertive, unconventional young woman
informal

Examples

The dyke tyke never hesitated to challenge authority at school.
People admired the dyke tyke for her confidence and refusal to conform.
As a self-proclaimed dyke tyke, she led the club with energy and bold ideas.
