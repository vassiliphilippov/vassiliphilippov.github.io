hen party

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛn ˌpɑr.ti / (AmE), / ˈhen ˌpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a party for women only, especially one held for a woman who is about to get married
women-only party, often before a wedding

Examples

She is planning to have her hen party in London with all her closest friends.
The hen party was filled with laughter, games, and a lot of dancing.
At her sister's hen party, they surprised her with a karaoke night.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stag party - a party for men only, especially before a wedding
