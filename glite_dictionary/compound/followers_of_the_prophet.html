Followers of the Prophet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑloʊɚz əv ðə ˈprɑfɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊəz əv ðə ˈprɒfɪt / (BrE)
A Nigerian-based Islamic fundamentalist group known for extremist activities and suspected of planning terrorist attacks.
Islamic extremist group in Nigeria

Examples

The Nigerian military is actively combating the influence of the Followers of the Prophet in the northern region.
International news agencies reported that the Followers of the Prophet were behind several recent attacks.
Many citizens have been displaced due to violence attributed to the Followers of the Prophet.
