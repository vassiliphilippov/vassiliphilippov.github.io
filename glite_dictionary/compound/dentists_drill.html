dentist's drill

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.tɪsts drɪl / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tɪsts drɪl / (BrE)
a high-speed rotary tool used by dentists for cutting into teeth and preparing cavities
tool dentists use to cut teeth

Examples

The noise of the dentist's drill made her feel anxious before her appointment.
Modern dentist's drills are designed to reduce discomfort for patients.
He flinched as the high-pitched whine of the dentist's drill started.
