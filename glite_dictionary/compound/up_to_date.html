up to date

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌp tə ˈdeɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌp tə ˈdeɪt / (BrE)
having or reflecting the latest fashion, style, or information; modern and fashionable
fashionable; modern in style or ideas

Examples

The hotel has an up to date fitness center with the newest equipment.
Their website needs to be more up to date to attract younger customers.
