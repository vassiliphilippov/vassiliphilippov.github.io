Indian wrestle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈrɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈrɛsəl / (BrE)
A playful contest in which two participants lie on the ground and try to force each other's raised leg to the ground, or a traditional wrestling style originating from India.
playful leg-wrestling contest; wrestling style from India

Examples

The kids decided to have an Indian wrestle to see who was the strongest.
At summer camp, an Indian wrestle quickly became everyone's favorite game.
He challenged his friend to an Indian wrestle on the grass after school.

Synset Examples

They engaged in an Indian wrestle, playfully pushing each other around.
The children enjoyed an Indian wrestle during playtime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestling - the sport or act of grappling with an opponent by holding and trying to throw or force them down

Etymology

Combination of 'Indian' (referring here to native peoples or the country of India) and 'wrestle'.
