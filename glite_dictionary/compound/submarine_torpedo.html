submarine torpedo

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbməˌrin tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbməˈriːn tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a torpedo specifically designed to be launched from a submarine
torpedo for launching from a submarine

Examples

The navy equipped its newest vessels with an advanced submarine torpedo system.
A submarine torpedo can travel for long distances to reach its target underwater.
During the exercise, they successfully launched a submarine torpedo from beneath the surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: torpedo - a self-propelled underwater missile
