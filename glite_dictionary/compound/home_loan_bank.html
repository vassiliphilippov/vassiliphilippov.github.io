Home Loan Bank

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm loʊn bæŋk / (AmE), / həʊm ləʊn bæŋk / (BrE)
one of a network of regional banks in the U.S. that provide short-term credit advances to thrift institutions within their region.
U.S. regional bank providing credit to thrifts
american

Examples

The local savings association received funding from the Home Loan Bank to support its lending activities.
Financial regulators oversee the operations of each Home Loan Bank to ensure economic stability.
A network of Home Loan Banks was created to foster a healthy mortgage market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Federal Home Loan Bank - primary institution within the Federal Home Loan Bank System
