European cranberrybush

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈkrænˌbɛriˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈkrænˌbɛriˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub native to Europe and Asia, with clusters of white flowers and small bright red berries
Old World shrub with red berries

Examples

The European cranberrybush is prized for its attractive red berries and ornamental value in gardens.
Wildlife often feeds on the berries of the European cranberrybush during autumn.
Many people plant European cranberrybush as a decorative hedge in their yards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranberry - edible red berry from various shrubs in the genus Vaccinium
