herb doctor

1. NOUN

/ hɝb ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
A practitioner who treats illnesses through the use of medicinal herbs.
person who heals with herbs

Examples

The herb doctor prepared a special blend of roots to treat my cold.
People in rural areas often visited an herb doctor instead of a conventional physician.
She apprenticed with an experienced herb doctor to learn about traditional remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbalist - a person who grows, studies, or uses herbs, especially for medicinal purposes
