starvation wage

1. NOUN

/ stɑrˈveɪʃən weɪdʒ / (AmE), / stɑːˈveɪʃən weɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extremely low wage that is not enough to cover basic living expenses
extremely low, insufficient wage

Examples

Many people in the industry are paid a starvation wage and struggle to afford food and housing.
The government is criticized for allowing businesses to pay workers a starvation wage.
Despite working full time, he still receives only a starvation wage.

Synset Examples

Many workers struggle to survive on a starvation wage.
They protested against the company's starvation wage policies.
Her job pays a starvation wage, barely covering rent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poverty wage - a wage so low that it keeps workers in poverty
