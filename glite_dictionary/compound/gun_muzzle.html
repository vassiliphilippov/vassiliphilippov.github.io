gun muzzle

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn ˈmʌzəl / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈmʌzl̩ / (BrE)
the open circular end of a gun barrel from which the bullet or projectile exits
open end of a gun barrel

Examples

Never point the gun muzzle at anything you do not intend to shoot.
The hunter cleaned the gun muzzle carefully after each use.
A flash was visible from the gun muzzle when the rifle was fired.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muzzle - the open end of any firearm barrel
