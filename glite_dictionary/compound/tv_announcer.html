TV announcer

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈvi əˈnaʊnsər / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː əˈnaʊnsə / (BrE)
A person who presents information, introduces programs, or provides commentary on television.
person presenting or commenting on television

Examples

The TV announcer introduced the evening news with a warm smile.
As a TV announcer, she must remain calm and professional during live broadcasts.
Many viewers recognize the voice of their favorite TV announcer instantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presenter - person who introduces television or radio programs
  • Synonym: newsreader - person who reads the news on television or radio
