oyster fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
An edible mushroom with a soft greyish cap that grows in shelving clusters on decaying wood.
edible mushroom with greyish cap

Examples

Many chefs prefer using oyster fungus in stir-fries for its delicate flavor and texture.
We found a large cluster of oyster fungus growing on the decaying log in the forest.
The cultivation of oyster fungus has become increasingly popular among home gardeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oyster mushroom - another name for oyster fungus, edible mushroom species Pleurotus ostreatus
