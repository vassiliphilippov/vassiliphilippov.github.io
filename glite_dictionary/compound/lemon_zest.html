lemon zest

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən zɛst / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən zɛst / (BrE)
the thin, brightly colored outer layer of a lemon's peel, grated or peeled off and used to add flavor to food and drinks
grated outer peel of a lemon used in cooking

Examples

Add a teaspoon of lemon zest to the batter for a fresh, citrusy flavor.
The recipe calls for both juice and lemon zest to enhance the cake's aroma.
She garnished the salad with a sprinkle of lemon zest for extra brightness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zest - the outer, colored skin of a citrus fruit, grated for flavor
