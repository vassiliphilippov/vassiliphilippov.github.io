topic of discussion

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːpɪk əv dɪˈskʌʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪk əv dɪˈskʌʃ.ən / (BrE)
a subject or issue that people are talking about or considering in conversation or debate
subject being discussed

Examples

The impact of climate change has become a topic of discussion in many classrooms.
Her sudden resignation was the main topic of discussion at the meeting.
Whether remote work is here to stay remains a popular topic of discussion among professionals.

Synset Examples

Womanspreading has become a topic of discussion in public transport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subject - matter being discussed or considered
