emergency procedure

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɜrdʒənsi prəˈsidʒər / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a set of actions or steps to be followed in response to an urgent and unexpected situation, especially to handle a medical emergency
urgent action plan, often medical
medical

Examples

All staff must know the emergency procedure for a fire in the building.
The nurses immediately followed the emergency procedure when the patient went into cardiac arrest.
Doctors are trained to stay calm and efficient during any emergency procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protocol - official procedure or system of rules
  • Antonym: routine - a regular procedure, often contrasted with emergency ones
