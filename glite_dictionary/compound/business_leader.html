business leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˌliːdə / (BrE)
a person who is very wealthy or influential in the business world
influential or wealthy businessperson

Examples

He became a business leader after transforming his small startup into a global corporation.
Many aspiring entrepreneurs look up to successful business leaders for inspiration and advice.
The conference attracted business leaders from various industries to discuss global economic trends.

Synset Examples

an oil baron

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tycoon - a wealthy and powerful businessperson
  • Synonym: magnate - a powerful or influential person in business or industry
  • Synonym: baron - a powerful business leader, especially in a particular industry
