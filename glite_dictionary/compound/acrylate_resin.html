acrylate resin

1. NOUN

/ əˈkrɪleɪt ˈrɛzɪn / (AmE), / əˈkrɪleɪt ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a synthetic resin made from acrylate polymers, used for casting, molding, coatings, and adhesives due to its glassy thermoplastic properties
type of synthetic resin used in coatings and adhesives

Examples

The company developed a new acrylate resin for use in automotive paints.
Due to its durability, acrylate resin is widely used in outdoor coatings.
Scientists are researching how acrylate resin can improve the adhesion of medical bandages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrylic resin - a similar synthetic resin made from acrylic acid or its derivatives

Etymology

From 'acrylate', based on 'acrylic acid', + 'resin'.
