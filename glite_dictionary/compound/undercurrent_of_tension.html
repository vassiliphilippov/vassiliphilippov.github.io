undercurrent of tension

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌkɝːrənt əv ˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌrənt əv ˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
a hidden or underlying feeling of stress or unease present in a situation, not openly acknowledged
a hidden sense of stress in a situation

Examples

Throughout the negotiations, there was an undercurrent of tension that made everyone uneasy.
Despite the friendly conversation, an undercurrent of tension was obvious between the two rivals.
The family's holiday dinner was marred by an undercurrent of tension after the recent argument.

Synset Examples

There was an undercurrent of tension during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tension - mental or emotional strain; anxiety
  • Synonym: undercurrent - a hidden feeling or tendency that is different from the one shown openly
