disapproving frown

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪs.əˈpruː.vɪŋ ˈfraʊn / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈpruː.vɪŋ ˈfraʊn / (BrE)
a facial expression in which one's eyebrows are drawn together and lips pressed to show disapproval
facial expression showing disapproval

Examples

She received a disapproving frown from her boss when she arrived late to the meeting.
His mother gave him a disapproving frown after seeing him eat dessert before dinner.
With a disapproving frown, the judge silenced the noisy courtroom.

Synset Examples

The teacher gave a disapproving frown after the student's excuse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scowl - an angry or bad-tempered expression
