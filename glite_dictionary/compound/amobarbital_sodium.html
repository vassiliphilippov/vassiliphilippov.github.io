amobarbital sodium

1. NOUN

/ ˌæmoʊˈbɑːrbɪˌtɑːl ˈsoʊdiəm / (AmE), / ˌaməʊˈbɑːbɪˌtɑːl ˈsəʊdiəm / (BrE)
the sodium salt form of amobarbital, used as a sedative and hypnotic in medicine
sedative-hypnotic drug; sodium salt of amobarbital
medical

Examples

The doctor administered amobarbital sodium to the patient as a sedative before the procedure.
Because of its potential for dependence, amobarbital sodium is only prescribed in specific cases.
Researchers studied the effects of amobarbital sodium on sleep patterns in clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amobarbital - barbiturate drug used as a sedative and hypnotic

Etymology

From 'amobarbital' and 'sodium' (the salt form). 'Amobarbital' from 'amyl' and 'barbital'.
