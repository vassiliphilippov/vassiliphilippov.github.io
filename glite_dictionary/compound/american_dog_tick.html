American dog tick

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən dɔɡ tɪk / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən dɒɡ tɪk / (BrE)
A common species of tick (Dermacentor variabilis) found in North America, known to transmit Rocky Mountain spotted fever and tularemia.
species of tick found in North America

Examples

The American dog tick is a common vector of disease in the eastern United States.
After hiking in the woods, he checked his clothes for American dog ticks.
Veterinarians warn pet owners about the dangers of the American dog tick.
