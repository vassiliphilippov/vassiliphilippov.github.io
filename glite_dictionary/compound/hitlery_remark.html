Hitlery remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪtlɚi rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhɪtləri rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
A remark that is considered extremely offensive or authoritarian, likened to those associated with Adolf Hitler.
extremely offensive or authoritarian comment
offensive

Examples

The journalist condemned the speaker's Hitlery remark as dangerous and inappropriate.
Many people were offended by her Hitlery remark during the debate.
His Hitlery remarks have no place in a respectful discussion.

Synset Examples

The politician's Hitlery remarks sparked outrage among the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authoritarian remark - a comment expressing strict obedience to authority

Etymology

Formed from 'Hitler' with the adjectival suffix '-y', used pejoratively to liken speech to Adolf Hitler.
