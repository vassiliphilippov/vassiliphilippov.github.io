Forrest Trump

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.ɪst trʌmp / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪst trʌmp / (BrE)
A nickname for a controversial or polarizing political figure, suggesting a mix of perceived naivety or simplicity (after Forrest Gump) and controversy or outspokenness (after Donald Trump).
controversial political nickname
informal

Examples

Some pundits have labeled the inexperienced candidate a modern-day Forrest Trump.
The columnist described her as a Forrest Trump in her approach to public speaking.
His reputation as a Forrest Trump grew with every controversial statement he made.

Synset Examples

The media dubbed him the Forrest Trump of politics.
In his debates, he often resembled a Forrest Trump character.
His antics made him a real Forrest Trump figure.

Etymology

Blend of 'Forrest Gump' (the fictional character) and 'Donald Trump' (the American politician/businessman).
