San Joaquin Valley

1. PROPN

/ sæn wɔːˈkiːn ˈvæli / (AmE), / sæn wɔːˈkiːn ˈvæli / (BrE)
a large fertile valley in central California, noted for its agricultural production
valley in central California, US

Examples

Fresno is a major city located in the San Joaquin Valley of California.
Many crops, such as almonds and grapes, are grown in the fertile San Joaquin Valley.
The San Joaquin Valley is known for its hot summers and agricultural output.

Etymology

Named after the San Joaquin River; 'San Joaquin' means 'Saint Joachim' in Spanish.
