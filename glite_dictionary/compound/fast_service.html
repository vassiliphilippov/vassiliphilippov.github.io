fast service

1. NOUN

/ fæst ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
service that is provided quickly and efficiently, with minimal waiting time
quick and efficient service

Examples

The restaurant is known for its fast service and friendly staff.
Customers appreciate fast service during their lunch breaks.
Thanks to the fast service, we were able to finish our meal and get back to work on time.

Synset Examples

He joked about the McChurch's fast services and casual atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slow service - service that is provided slowly or with delays
