artificial kidney

1. NOUN

/ ɑːr.t̬əˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.ni / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl ˈkɪd.ni / (BrE)
a machine that uses dialysis to filter impurities and waste products from the blood, functioning as a replacement for the kidneys
dialysis machine; kidney replacement device
medical

Examples

The patient was connected to an artificial kidney during his regular dialysis session.
Doctors explained how the artificial kidney would clean her blood when her own kidneys failed.
Portable versions of the artificial kidney are being developed for home use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dialysis machine - machine used to perform dialysis for patients with kidney failure
  • Degree Lower: kidney - organ in the body that removes waste from the blood
