hitching bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪtʃɪŋ bɑr / (AmE), / ˈhɪtʃɪŋ bɑː / (BrE)
a fixed horizontal bar to which horses are tied to prevent them from wandering away
bar for tying horses

Examples

The old saloon still has a hitching bar out front for visiting riders.
He tied his horse to the hitching bar before going inside the store.
Rust had eaten through most of the metal hitching bar, making it unsafe for use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitching post - a post for tying horses to prevent them from straying
