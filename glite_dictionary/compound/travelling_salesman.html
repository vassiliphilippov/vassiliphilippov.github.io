travelling salesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræv(ə)lɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈtræv(ə)lɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
a salesperson who travels from place to place to visit and sell to customers, rather than working from a fixed location
a salesperson who travels to visit customers

Examples

As a travelling salesman, he spent most weeks on the road, visiting small towns across the region.
The life of a travelling salesman can be lonely, with long days away from family.
My grandfather worked as a travelling salesman, selling farm equipment throughout the Midwest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesman - a person whose job is to sell things
