Nan River

1. PROPN

/ næn ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / næn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in western Thailand that flows southward to join the Ping River and forms the Chao Phraya River
river in western Thailand

Examples

The Nan River is an important waterway for communities in western Thailand.
The city of Phitsanulok lies along the Nan River.
During the rainy season, the Nan River often floods nearby areas.
