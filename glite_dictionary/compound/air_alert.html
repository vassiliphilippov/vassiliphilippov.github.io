air alert

1. NOUN

/ ɛr əˈlɝt / (AmE), / eə əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a warning signal indicating the start of preparations for an imminent enemy air attack
warning of imminent air attack

Examples

As soon as the air alert sounded, everyone rushed to the shelters.
The city practiced monthly air alert drills during the war.
Residents waited anxiously for the all-clear after the air alert was issued.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air raid warning - a warning of an impending air raid

2. NOUN

/ ɛr əˈlɝt / (AmE), / eə əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
A period during which military and civilian agencies are on heightened readiness for a possible enemy air attack.
period of readiness for possible air attack

Examples

During the air alert, everyone sought shelter in the designated safe areas.
The air alert lasted for two hours before the all-clear signal was given.
Military personnel remained vigilant throughout the air alert period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air raid alert - a warning signal or period for an imminent air raid

3. NOUN

/ ɛr əˈlɜrt / (AmE), / eə əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a military condition in which combat aircraft are airborne and ready to respond to an operation or threat
state of airborne combat readiness

Examples

All fighter jets were placed on air alert in response to the incoming threat.
After the warning, the squadron remained at air alert throughout the night.
The base commander ordered an air alert as tensions in the region escalated.
