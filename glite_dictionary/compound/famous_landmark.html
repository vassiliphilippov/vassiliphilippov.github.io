famous landmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪməs ˈlændˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməs ˈlɑːndˌmɑːk / (BrE)
A site or monument that is well known and easily recognized, often of historical, cultural, or architectural importance.
well-known site or monument

Examples

The Statue of Liberty is a famous landmark that attracts millions of visitors every year.
She took a guided tour to see every famous landmark in the capital.
Photographers often try to capture the perfect angle of each famous landmark they visit.

Synset Examples

The Eiffel Tower is a famous landmark in Paris.
Many famous landmarks are located in Springfield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landmark - an object or feature of a landscape that is easily seen and recognized from a distance, especially one that enables someone to establish their location
