ballroom music

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌrum ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌruːm ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
a genre of popular music composed especially to accompany ballroom dancing
music composed for ballroom dancing

Examples

The orchestra played ballroom music all night, filling the floor with dancers.
Her parents met at a club famous for its live ballroom music.
Modern ballroom music often mixes traditional sounds with contemporary rhythms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance music - music composed to accompany dancing in general
