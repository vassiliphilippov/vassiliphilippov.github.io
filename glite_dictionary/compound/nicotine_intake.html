nicotine intake

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkətiːn ˈɪnteɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɪkətiːn ˈɪnteɪk / (BrE)
the act or process of consuming nicotine, especially through activities like smoking or vaping
consumption of nicotine

Examples

Doctors advise patients to reduce their nicotine intake to lower health risks.
Monitoring your daily nicotine intake can help if you want to quit smoking.
Studies show that excessive nicotine intake increases the risk of heart disease.

Synset Examples

He decided to quit smoking to reduce his nicotine intake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoking - the action or habit of inhaling smoke from burning tobacco
