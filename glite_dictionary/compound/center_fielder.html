center fielder

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.tɚ ˌfiːl.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈsen.tə ˌfiːl.də / (BrE)
the player who occupies the position in center field in baseball or softball, responsible for fielding balls hit into the area between left and right field
player in center field (baseball/softball)
american

Examples

The center fielder made an incredible catch at the warning track.
As a center fielder, she needs great speed and awareness to cover the outfield effectively.
After injuring his arm, he switched from pitcher to center fielder.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: outfielder - a player who plays in the outfield in baseball or softball
  • Synonym: left fielder - the player who plays in left field in baseball or softball
  • Synonym: right fielder - the player who plays in right field in baseball or softball
