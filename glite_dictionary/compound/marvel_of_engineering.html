marvel of engineering

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrvəl əv ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.vəl əv ˌen.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
an impressive or remarkable achievement in the field of engineering, often referring to a structure, invention, or design that demonstrates exceptional technical skill or ingenuity
impressive engineering achievement

Examples

The Golden Gate Bridge is considered a marvel of engineering by many civil engineers.
That ancient Roman aqueduct is still a marvel of engineering two thousand years after it was built.
The spacecraft's compact and powerful engine was hailed as a marvel of engineering in the news.

Synset Examples

The vintage airship was a marvel of engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical marvel - an impressive or remarkable technical achievement
