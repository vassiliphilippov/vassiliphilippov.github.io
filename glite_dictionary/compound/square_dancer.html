square dancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɛr ˌdænsər / (AmE), / ˈskwɛə ˌdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in square dancing, a type of folk dance performed by four couples arranged in a square
a person who does square dancing

Examples

My grandmother has been a dedicated square dancer for over twenty years.
A group of square dancers entertained the crowd at the county fair.
He became a passionate square dancer after attending his first barn dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square dance - a type of folk dance in which four couples form a square
