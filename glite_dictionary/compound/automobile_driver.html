automobile driver

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑː.t̬ə.moʊˌbiːl ˈdraɪ.vər / (AmE), / ˌɔː.tə.məˌbiːl ˈdraɪ.və / (BrE)
a person who drives an automobile, especially a racing car at high speeds
person who drives a car, especially in races

Examples

The professional automobile driver took the lead on the final lap of the race.
Becoming an automobile driver in Formula 1 requires years of training and talent.
Spectators cheered as the skilled automobile driver navigated the tight corners at record speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driver - a person who drives a vehicle
