poor person

1. NOUN

/ pʊr ˈpɜːr.sən / (AmE), / pɔː ˈpɜː.sən / (BrE)
a person who has little or no money, possessions, or resources
person with little or no money

Examples

In the small village, the poor person struggled to find enough food each day.
Charities often focus their work on helping the poor person improve their living conditions.
Growing up as a poor person taught her the value of hard work and perseverance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pauper - a very poor person
  • Antonym: rich person - a person with substantial wealth or possessions
