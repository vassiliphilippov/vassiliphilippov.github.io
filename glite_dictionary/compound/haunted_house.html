haunted house

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑntɪd haʊs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntɪd haʊs / (BrE)
a house believed to be inhabited by ghosts or supernatural beings
house believed to be inhabited by ghosts

Examples

Many people refuse to walk past the haunted house at night.
They say the old mansion on the hill is a haunted house.
We visited a haunted house for Halloween and it was terrifying.

Synset Examples

That haunted house is really scary at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghost house - a house said to be haunted by ghosts
