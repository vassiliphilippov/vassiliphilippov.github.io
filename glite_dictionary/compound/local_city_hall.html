local city hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˌsɪ.ti hɔl / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˌsɪ.ti hɔːl / (BrE)
the municipal building in a particular area where civic functions and civil ceremonies are held
municipal building for civil functions

Examples

The mayor announced the new policy at the local city hall during a public meeting.
Many couples choose to get married at their local city hall for a simple ceremony.
Residents gathered at the local city hall to vote in the municipal election.

Synset Examples

The wedding was held at the local city hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city hall - municipal building used for city administration
  • Synonym: town hall - building used for local government administration, especially in smaller towns
