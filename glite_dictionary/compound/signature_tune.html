signature tune

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃɚ tuːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nə.tʃə tjuːn / (BrE)
a distinctive piece of music used to identify a performer, dance band, or radio/TV program, especially at the start or end of a broadcast
melody identifying a person or program

Examples

The show's signature tune is instantly recognizable to millions of listeners every morning.
Every famous radio program in the 1950s had its own signature tune to open and close the broadcast.
The orchestra played the band's signature tune as they walked on stage to thunderous applause.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theme song - a song or melody strongly associated with a program, film, or person
