SGA infant

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs dʒi ˈeɪ ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / ˌɛs dʒiː ˈeɪ ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
a newborn whose size and weight are significantly below the average for babies of the same gestational age
newborn much smaller than average for age
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the baby was an SGA infant and would need special monitoring.
Premature and SGA infants often face greater challenges in early development.
Research shows that nutrition is especially important for SGA infants during their first year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: preterm infant - an infant born before 37 weeks of gestation
  • Synonym: low birth weight infant - an infant with lower than average birth weight

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'small for gestational age', from medical terminology.
