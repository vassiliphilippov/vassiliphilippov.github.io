Hungarian pointer

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋˈɡɛriən ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛəriən ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
A breed of Hungarian hunting dog with a deep reddish coat, similar in appearance to the Weimaraner; also known as the Vizsla.
Hungarian breed of hunting dog (Vizsla)

Examples

The Hungarian pointer is known for its athletic build and intelligence.
She proudly showed her new Hungarian pointer at the dog show last weekend.
Many hunters favor the Hungarian pointer for its keen sense of smell and loyalty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vizsla - a Hungarian breed of hunting dog with a golden rust coat
  • Degree Higher: Weimaraner - a German breed of large hunting dog with a short, gray coat
