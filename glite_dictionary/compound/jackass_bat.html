jackass bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒækˌæs bæt / (AmE), / ˈdʒakˌas bæt / (BrE)
a large bat of the southwestern United States, noted for its spotted appearance and exceptionally large ears
a large southwestern US bat with big ears

Examples

The jackass bat is easy to recognize by its enormous ears when spotted at dusk.
Several jackass bats roosted in the cave near Tucson, Arizona.
Researchers are studying the behavior of the jackass bat in arid regions.
