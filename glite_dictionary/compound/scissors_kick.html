scissors kick

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪzɚz kɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəz kɪk / (BrE)
a swimming kick performed by moving the legs in a scissor-like motion, commonly used in the sidestroke
swimming kick with a scissor motion

Examples

The swimming instructor demonstrated the scissors kick to help students master the sidestroke.
Her scissors kick was smooth and efficient, allowing her to glide gracefully through the water.
To improve your sidestroke, practice the scissors kick with slow, controlled motions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sidestroke - a swimming stroke performed on the side using the scissors kick
