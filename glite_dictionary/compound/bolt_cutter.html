bolt cutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlt ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlt ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a heavy-duty tool with long handles and strong jaws used for cutting through metal bolts, chains, or padlocks
tool for cutting bolts or metal chains

Examples

He grabbed the bolt cutter from the toolbox to break the lock on the gate.
Security personnel used a bolt cutter to remove the old, rusted chain.
A pair of bolt cutters is essential for emergency rescue operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wire cutter - a hand tool for cutting wire
