Loya Jirga

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɔɪə ˈdʒɪrɡə / (AmE), / ˌlɔɪə ˈdʒɪəɡə / (BrE)
A grand council or assembly in Afghanistan, often convened to resolve national political conflicts or major issues.
Afghan grand council or assembly

Examples

The president called a Loya Jirga to discuss the future of the government's constitution.
A historic Loya Jirga was convened to address national reconciliation efforts.
Delegates from across the country participated in the Loya Jirga to give their input on the peace process.

Synset Examples

he convened a Loya Jirga that persuaded tribal leaders to acquiesce

Etymology

From Pashto 'loya' (grand) + 'jirga' (assembly or council).
