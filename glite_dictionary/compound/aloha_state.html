Aloha State

1. PROPN

/ əˈloʊ.hə steɪt / (AmE), / əˈləʊ.hə steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Hawaii in the United States
nickname for Hawaii (U.S. state)

Examples

Many tourists dream of visiting the Aloha State for its stunning beaches and welcoming culture.
Pineapples and hula dancing are iconic symbols of the Aloha State.
She moved to the Aloha State after falling in love with its vibrant atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hawaii - the 50th state of the United States, located in the central Pacific

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'aloha' (greeting, farewell, love) and 'state' as Hawaii's official state nickname.
