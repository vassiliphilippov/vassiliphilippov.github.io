dropped egg

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑpt ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpt ɛɡ / (BrE)
an egg cooked by gently breaking it into simmering water, without its shell; a poached egg
egg cooked in simmering water (poached)

Examples

For breakfast, she made a dropped egg on toast with some fresh herbs.
A perfectly cooked dropped egg should have a soft yolk and just-set white.
The recipe calls for a dropped egg, but you can also use a boiled egg if you prefer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poached egg - egg cooked without the shell in simmering water

Etymology

From the verb 'drop' (to let fall) and 'egg'; refers to the action of dropping the egg into water.
