to spring out of bed

1. VERB

/ sprɪŋ aʊt əv bɛd / (AmE), / sprɪŋ aʊt əv bɛd / (BrE)
to get out of bed quickly and energetically
get out of bed quickly

Examples

She managed to spring out of bed as soon as the sun rose.
When the phone rang, he sprang out of bed and ran to answer it.
Every morning, the children spring out of bed excited for the day ahead.

Synset Examples

He sprang out of bed when he heard the alarm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jump out of bed - to get out of bed quickly and suddenly
