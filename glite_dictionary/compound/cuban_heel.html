Cuban heel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bən hiːl / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bən hiːl / (BrE)
a broad, medium-height heel, slightly tapered at the back, found on women's shoes and some men's boots
broad, medium shoe heel

Examples

She bought a pair of boots with a stylish Cuban heel for the winter.
The dancer's shoes had a distinctive Cuban heel that gave her extra height.
Many vintage shoes feature a Cuban heel for both comfort and elegance.

Etymology

Named for its association with Latin American, particularly Cuban, dance shoes and style.
