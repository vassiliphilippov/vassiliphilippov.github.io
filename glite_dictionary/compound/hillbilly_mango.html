hillbilly mango

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪlˌbɪli ˈmæŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɪlˌbɪli ˈmaŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A variety of mango noted for its distinctive flavor, often associated with rural or country areas.
type of mango, rural variety

Examples

She brought a ripe hillbilly mango to share at the family barbecue.
The market sells fresh hillbilly mangoes every summer, straight from local farms.
Many people say that the flavor of a hillbilly mango is richer than most other varieties.

Synset Examples

He brought hillbilly mango to the picnic for everyone to try.
She made a delicious salsa with hillbilly mango and jalapeños.
