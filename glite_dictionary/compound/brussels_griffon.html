Brussels griffon

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌsəlz ɡrɪˈfɑn / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz ɡrɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
a breed of very small, sturdy dogs of Belgian origin, noted for their wiry coat and short, bearded muzzle
breed of small Belgian dog

Examples

The Brussels griffon is known for its expressive face and lively personality.
Many owners describe their Brussels griffon as affectionate and loyal companions.
A Brussels griffon won several awards at the international dog show this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: griffon - a breed of dog with a wiry coat

Etymology

Named after Brussels, Belgium, and French 'griffon' (wire-haired dog).
