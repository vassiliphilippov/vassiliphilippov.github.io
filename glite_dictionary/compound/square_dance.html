square dance

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr dæns / (AmE), / skwɛː dans / (BrE)
a traditional American country dance for four couples arranged in a square, following a caller's instructions
a type of American group folk dance

Examples

At the local festival, everyone joined in for a lively square dance in the community hall.
She learned the steps for a square dance during her time in Texas.
The music picked up as the caller led the group through a traditional square dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barn dance - an informal social dance, often with folk dances, held in a barn
