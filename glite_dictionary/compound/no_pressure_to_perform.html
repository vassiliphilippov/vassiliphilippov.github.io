no pressure to perform

1. NOUN

/ noʊ ˈprɛʃɚ tu pɚˈfɔrm / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈprɛʃə tuː pəˈfɔːm / (BrE)
freedom from expectations or stress regarding achievement or results in a particular situation
absence of stress or expectations to achieve

Examples

The coach told the team there was no pressure to perform in the friendly match.
With no pressure to perform, she felt more relaxed and confident during the audition.
He appreciated that his new job came with no pressure to perform right away.

Synset Examples

During the interview, they emphasized there was no pressure to perform.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no pressure - absence of stress or expectations
