Donald Duck

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɑnəld dʌk / (AmE), / ˈdɒnəld dʌk / (BrE)
a famous Disney cartoon character, a short-tempered anthropomorphic duck typically dressed in a sailor suit
Disney cartoon character; anthropomorphic duck

Examples

Donald Duck is one of the most recognizable cartoon characters in the world.
My little brother loves watching Donald Duck cartoons on Saturday mornings.
Walt Disney first introduced Donald Duck to audiences in the 1930s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mickey Mouse - another Disney cartoon character, Donald Duck's friend
  • Synonym: Goofy - Disney cartoon character, a friend of Donald Duck
