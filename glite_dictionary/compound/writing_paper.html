writing paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
paper made into thin sheets, especially suitable for writing letters, correspondence, or manuscripts by hand
paper for writing letters or manuscripts

Examples

She chose her favorite writing paper to send a letter to her grandmother.
The quality of the writing paper makes a big difference when using a fountain pen.
He bought a set of elegant writing paper for formal invitations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationery - paper and other materials used for writing
