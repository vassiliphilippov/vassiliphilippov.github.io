sick parade

1. NOUN

/ sɪk pəˈreɪd / (AmE), / sɪk pəˈreɪd / (BrE)
the daily military assembly where personnel report themselves as sick to the medical officer
military assembly for reporting illness

Examples

After the training exercise, several soldiers attended sick parade complaining of minor injuries.
Private Jenkins was excused from duties because he reported to sick parade with a fever.
The medic checked everyone at sick parade before making any recommendations for leave.
