restaurant chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛst(ə)ˌrɑnt tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɛst(ə)ˌrɒnt tʃeɪn / (BrE)
A group of restaurants, usually of the same brand, with standardized menus and operations, owned or franchised by the same company.
group of related restaurants under one brand

Examples

The new restaurant chain opened ten locations across the city last year.
Many families enjoy eating at a familiar restaurant chain when traveling.
He works as a manager for a popular restaurant chain known for its burgers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franchise - a business that is authorized to sell a company's goods or services in a particular area
  • Antonym: independent restaurant - a restaurant that is not part of a chain or franchise
