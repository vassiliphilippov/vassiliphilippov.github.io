honor killing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːnər ˌkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒnə ˌkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the killing of a family member, especially a woman or girl, by relatives who believe the victim has brought dishonor upon the family
murder to restore perceived family honor

Examples

The court case brought international attention to the problem of honor killing in the region.
Activists are working to end the practice of honor killings around the world.
The tragedy of honor killing highlights the clash between tradition and human rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honor crime - a crime committed to restore family honor
