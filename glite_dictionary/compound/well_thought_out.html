well thought out

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl ˌθɔt ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˌθɔːt ˈaʊt / (BrE)
carefully planned or considered; showing the result of thorough and deliberate thought
carefully planned or considered

Examples

Her argument for the new policy was well thought out and persuasive.
The team presented a well thought out plan during the meeting.
Every detail of the event was well thought out to ensure its success.

Synset Examples

the paper was well thought out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefully considered - given thoughtful consideration
  • Antonym: thoughtless - lacking careful thought or consideration
