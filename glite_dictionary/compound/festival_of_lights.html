Festival of Lights

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɛstəvəl əv laɪts / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪvəl əv laɪts / (BrE)
Another name for Hanukkah, the eight-day Jewish holiday commemorating the rededication of the Temple in Jerusalem in 165 BC.
another name for Hanukkah, a Jewish holiday

Examples

Every December, our family gathers to celebrate the Festival of Lights with traditional foods and songs.
The city hosted a public menorah lighting for the Festival of Lights, drawing a large crowd.
Learning about the history behind the Festival of Lights helped students appreciate its significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hanukkah - the Jewish Festival of Lights
