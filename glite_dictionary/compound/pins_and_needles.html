pins and needles

1. NOUN

/ pɪnz ənd ˈniːdəlz / (AmE), / pɪnz ənd ˈniːdlz / (BrE)
a tingling or prickling sensation in the skin, often caused by pressure on nerves or reduced blood flow
tingling sensation in the skin

Examples

After sitting with her legs crossed for too long, she felt pins and needles in her foot.
A rush of pins and needles ran through his hand as he shook it out.
Standing up quickly, she was startled by the pins and needles sensation in her legs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tingling - a slight stinging or prickling sensation
  • Antonym: numbness - lack of sensation or feeling
