sleep talking

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːp ˌtɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsliːp ˌtɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or phenomenon of speaking aloud while asleep, usually without awareness
speaking while asleep

Examples

My brother's sleep talking can sometimes be heard from the next room.
Some people have entire conversations while sleep talking and never remember a thing.
The doctor assured her that sleep talking is usually harmless and quite common.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somniloquy - the technical term for talking in one's sleep
