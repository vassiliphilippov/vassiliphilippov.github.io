farmer's market

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrmərz ˌmɑːrkət / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməz ˌmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
an open-air market where farmers sell fresh produce and other goods directly to consumers
market for fresh farm products

Examples

Every Saturday morning, I visit the farmer's market to buy fresh vegetables and fruit.
Local artisans sell homemade bread and cheese at the farmer's market.
The city sponsors a farmer's market downtown during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market - an open space or building where goods are bought and sold
