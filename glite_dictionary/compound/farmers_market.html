farmers market

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmərz ˌmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməz ˌmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
a market where farmers sell their produce, such as fruits and vegetables, directly to consumers
market for direct sale of farm produce

Examples

Every Saturday morning, local vendors set up stalls at the farmers market downtown.
I love buying fresh vegetables and homemade bread from the farmers market near my house.
The community supports local agriculture by shopping at the weekly farmers market.

Synset Examples

They sell various flavors of booch at the farmer's market.
The farmers market is busiest at noontime.
The farmers' market felt like a lively meat market.
