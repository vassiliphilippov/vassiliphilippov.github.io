hot cereal

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / hɒt ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
A cereal dish made from grains and served hot, such as oatmeal or porridge.
cereal dish served hot

Examples

Many people eat hot cereal for breakfast on cold winter mornings.
My favorite hot cereal is oatmeal topped with fresh fruit.
If you prefer something warm, you can try a bowl of hot cereal instead of toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porridge - soft food made by boiling grains in milk or water
  • Antonym: cold cereal - cereal served cold, usually with milk
