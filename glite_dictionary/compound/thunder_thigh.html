thunder thigh

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌndɚ ˌθaɪ / (AmE), / ˈθʌndə ˌθaɪ / (BrE)
a thigh that is large and muscular, often regarded as a sign of strength or fitness
large, muscular thigh (often fit/strong)
informal

Examples

She credits her thunder thighs to years of cycling and weightlifting.
He was proud of his thunder thighs and their role in his athletic performance.
On social media, many fitness influencers celebrate their thunder thighs as a symbol of health and strength.
