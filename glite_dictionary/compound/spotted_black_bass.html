spotted black bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd blæk bæs / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd blæk bæs / (BrE)
a species of freshwater fish (Micropterus punctulatus) in the sunfish family, native to North America and known for its distinctive spotted appearance
a species of freshwater fish in North America

Examples

The spotted black bass is popular among anglers for its fighting spirit.
Unlike largemouth bass, the spotted black bass prefers clearer, faster-moving water.
She caught a large spotted black bass while fishing in the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black bass - a group of freshwater fish species in North America
