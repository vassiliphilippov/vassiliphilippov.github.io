Freeper community

1. NOUN

/ ˈfripər kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈfriːpə kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
A group of conservative users who participate on the Free Republic website, commonly known as 'Freepers'.
conservative users of Free Republic

Examples

The Freeper community organized an online campaign to support their chosen candidate.
Discussions within the Freeper community often focus on conservative politics and current events.
She joined the Freeper community to connect with like-minded individuals online.

Synset Examples

The Freeper community gathered to strategize for the elections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Freeper - A member of the Free Republic website

Etymology

From 'Freeper', nickname for members of the Free Republic website, combined with 'community'.
