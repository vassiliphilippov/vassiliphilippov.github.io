equal-opportunity employer

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːkwəl əˈpɑːrtʃuːnəti ɛmˈplɔɪər / (AmE), / ˌiːkwəl ɒpəˈtjuːnəti ɪmˈplɔɪə / (BrE)
a company or organization that hires people regardless of race, religion, gender, age, or other protected characteristics; an employer committed to non-discriminatory hiring practices
employer with non-discriminatory hiring practices

Examples

The company advertises itself as an equal-opportunity employer, welcoming applicants from all backgrounds.
Being an equal-opportunity employer is important for attracting a diverse workforce.
Our organization is proud to maintain its status as an equal-opportunity employer.

Synset Examples

The company prides itself on being an equal-opportunity employer.
