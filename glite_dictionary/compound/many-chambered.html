many-chambered

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛniˈtʃeɪmɚd / (AmE), / ˌmɛniˈtʃeɪməd / (BrE)
Having many chambers or compartments inside.
having multiple chambers

Examples

The many-chambered heart of a mammal efficiently separates oxygenated and deoxygenated blood.
Ancient tombs often featured many-chambered designs to accommodate multiple burials.
The many-chambered structure of the nautilus shell provides it with buoyancy and stability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multi-chambered - having more than one chamber
