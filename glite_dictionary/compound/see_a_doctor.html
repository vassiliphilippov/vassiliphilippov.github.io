to see a doctor

1. VERB

/ si ə ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / siː ə ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
to visit or consult a medical professional for advice or treatment
visit or consult a medical professional

Examples

You should see a doctor if your symptoms get worse.
I think it's time for me to see a doctor about my back pain.
After several days of fever, Mark decided to see a doctor for a proper diagnosis.

Synset Examples

He finally decided to see a doctor about his bunion.
I need to see the doctor for my cough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consult a doctor - to seek medical advice from a doctor
  • Degree Lower: see a dentist - to visit or consult a dental professional
