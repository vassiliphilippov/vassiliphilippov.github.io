linear meter

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪniɚ ˈmitɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪniə ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument whose pointer moves in direct proportion to the measured quantity
measuring instrument with proportional display

Examples

The technician used a linear meter to ensure the voltage reading was accurate.
A linear meter gives a straightforward indication of the measured value.
Calibration is crucial for a linear meter to maintain its proportional response.

Synset Examples

We need 15 linear meters of this chain-link fence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analog meter - measuring instrument with a pointer display
