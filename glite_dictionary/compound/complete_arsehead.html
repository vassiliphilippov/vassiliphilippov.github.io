complete arsehead

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplit ˈɑːrsˌhɛd / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ˈɑːshɛd / (BrE)
an extremely foolish or obnoxious person (used as an insult, chiefly British slang)
very foolish or obnoxious person (slang)
slang british

Examples

He's being a complete arsehead about the whole situation, refusing to listen to anyone else.
She called him a complete arsehead after his rude comments during dinner.
You don't have to act like a complete arsehead every time you lose at games.

Synset Examples
