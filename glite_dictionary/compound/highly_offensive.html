highly offensive

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪli əfˈɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli əˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
Extremely insulting, hurtful, or likely to cause strong anger or resentment.
extremely insulting or hurtful

Examples

His comments during the meeting were highly offensive to many of his colleagues.
Posting highly offensive material online can lead to disciplinary action.
The comedian apologized for making a highly offensive joke about the audience.
