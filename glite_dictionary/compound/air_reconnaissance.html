air reconnaissance

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr rɪˌkɑnəˈsɑns / (AmE), / ˈeə rɪˌkɒnɪˈsɒns / (BrE)
the observation or collection of information about enemy forces or positions from the air, either visually or using airborne sensors
observation of enemy forces from the air

Examples

The army deployed air reconnaissance to locate enemy positions before launching the attack.
Advances in technology have made air reconnaissance more effective and precise than ever before.
Due to poor weather, air reconnaissance missions were temporarily suspended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconnaissance - military observation to locate an enemy or ascertain strategic features
