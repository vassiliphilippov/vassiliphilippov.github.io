dress shop

1. NOUN

/ drɛs ʃɑp / (AmE), / drɛs ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells women's clothing and related accessories, especially dresses and jewelry
shop selling women's dresses and accessories

Examples

She bought her wedding gown at a lovely little dress shop downtown.
After work, they browsed the latest styles in a trendy dress shop.
The dress shop had a beautiful collection of summer dresses and statement jewelry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boutique - a small shop selling fashionable clothes or accessories
