marriage counseling

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ ˌkaʊnsəˌlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ ˌkaʊns(ə)lɪŋ / (BrE)
professional guidance provided to couples to help resolve marital problems and improve their relationship
guidance for couples with marital issues

Examples

They decided to seek marriage counseling after months of arguing about finances.
Many couples find that marriage counseling helps them communicate more effectively.
The Smiths began marriage counseling to rebuild trust in their relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: couples therapy - therapy for couples to improve relationships or resolve conflicts
