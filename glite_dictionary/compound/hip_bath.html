hip bath

1. NOUN

/ hɪp bæθ / (AmE), / hɪp bɑːθ / (BrE)
a bathtub or bath in which only the hips and buttocks are immersed while the user sits
bath immersing hips and buttocks

Examples

The doctor recommended a daily hip bath to relieve her discomfort.
After his surgery, he found a warm hip bath soothing and therapeutic.
She prepared the hip bath by filling it with lukewarm water and a handful of salts.

Related Terms
