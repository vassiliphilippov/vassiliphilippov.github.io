snakes and ladders

1. NOUN

/ sneɪks ənd ˈlædɚz / (AmE), / sneɪks ənd ˈlædəz / (BrE)
a board game in which players move counters according to dice rolls, climbing ladders and sliding down snakes depending on where they land
children's board game with snakes and ladders

Examples

We played snakes and ladders on a rainy afternoon to pass the time.
The rules of snakes and ladders are simple enough for young children to enjoy.
He gave his niece a new snakes and ladders set for her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chutes and ladders - American name for the same board game, replacing snakes with chutes
