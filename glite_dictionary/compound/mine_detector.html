mine detector

1. NOUN

/ maɪn dɪˈtɛktər / (AmE), / maɪn dɪˈtɛktə / (BrE)
an electronic device used to locate explosive mines, typically underground or underwater
device for locating explosive mines

Examples

The soldiers used a mine detector to safely navigate through the old battlefield.
Carrying a mine detector, she carefully swept the area before the construction crew began work.
Modern mine detectors have improved sensitivity compared to earlier models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metal detector - a device for detecting metal objects
