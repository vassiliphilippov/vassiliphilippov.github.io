sad trombone

1. NOUN

/ sæd trɑmˈboʊn / (AmE), / sæd trɒmˈbəʊn / (BrE)
A sound effect, typically a descending slide on a trombone, used to humorously indicate disappointment, failure, or embarrassment.
sound effect indicating disappointment

Examples

Whenever the answer was wrong, the game show played a sad trombone.
He sent a meme of a sad trombone to express his disappointment.
The class burst out laughing when the teacher imitated a sad trombone after the failed experiment.

Synset Examples

Her response was met with a sad trombone sound.

When he missed the deadline, it was a sad trombone moment.
