movielike quality

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːviˌlaɪk ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌlaɪk ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
a characteristic or atmosphere resembling that of a movie, often suggesting vividness, drama, or cinematic style
a quality reminiscent of a movie

Examples

The novel's movielike quality made it easy for readers to imagine every scene vividly.
His vacation photos had a movielike quality that impressed his friends.
There was a movielike quality to the thunderstorm as lightning flashed dramatically over the city.

Synset Examples

Her story had a movielike quality that captivated everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinematic - having qualities typical of a motion picture
