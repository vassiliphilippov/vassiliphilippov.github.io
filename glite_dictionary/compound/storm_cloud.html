storm cloud

1. NOUN

/ stɔrm klaʊd / (AmE), / stɔːm klaʊd / (BrE)
A heavy, dark cloud that signals impending rain or a storm.
dark cloud indicating a coming storm

Examples

The horizon was filled with storm clouds that warned of approaching rain.
Dark storm clouds gathered overhead, making the afternoon seem like night.
Farmers hurried to finish their work as storm clouds rolled in quickly from the west.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thundercloud - a dark cloud that produces thunder and lightning
  • Synonym: rain cloud - a cloud from which rain falls or looks likely to fall
