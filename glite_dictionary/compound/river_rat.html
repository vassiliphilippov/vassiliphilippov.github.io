river rat

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvɚ ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌræt / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who spends a lot of time near rivers or enjoys activities on or around water
person fond of rivers and water activities
informal

Examples

He spent his childhood as a river rat, always exploring the banks and swimming in the currents.
The local river rats know every bend and rapid along this stretch of the river.
She proudly calls herself a river rat after years of canoeing and fishing adventures.

Synset Examples

Being a river rat means you love outdoor adventures.
He grew up as a river rat, always near the water.
The kids are river rats, playing by the stream.
