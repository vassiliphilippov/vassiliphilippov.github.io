straight hang

1. NOUN

/ streɪt hæŋ / (AmE), / streɪt hæŋ / (BrE)
a hang performed on the rings or parallel bars in gymnastics, with the body upright and arms at the sides
gymnastics position with body erect and arms at sides

Examples

The gymnast performed a perfect straight hang before beginning her routine on the rings.
Improve your shoulder strength by holding a straight hang for as long as you can.
His coach emphasized the importance of a controlled straight hang on the parallel bars to build endurance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead hang - a hanging position with the arms extended above the head, often used in climbing or exercise
