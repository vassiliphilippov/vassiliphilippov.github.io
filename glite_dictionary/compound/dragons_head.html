dragon's head

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræɡənz hɛd / (AmE), / ˈdræɡənz hɛd / (BrE)
A North American herb with sharply serrated lance-shaped leaves and spikes of blue to violet flowers, also known as Dracocephalum.
North American herb with violet flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece was a stunning patch of dragon's head in full bloom.
Gardeners recommend dragon's head for its hardy nature and attractive violet flowers.
She identified the plant by its distinct leaves and realized it was dragon's head.
