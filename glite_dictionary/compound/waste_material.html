waste material

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪst məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈweɪst məˌtɪəriəl / (BrE)
material that is no longer useful or required and is discarded as worthless or unwanted.
discarded unwanted material

Examples

The factory produces a large amount of waste material that must be disposed of safely.
Much of the waste material from construction sites can be recycled.
New regulations require companies to reduce the waste material they generate.

Synset Examples

they collect the waste once a week
much of the waste material is carried off in the sewers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste - unwanted or unusable material
