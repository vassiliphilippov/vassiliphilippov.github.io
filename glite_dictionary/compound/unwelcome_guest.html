unwelcome guest

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈwɛlkəm ɡɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlkəm ɡɛst / (BrE)
a person who attends an event without an invitation or permission, often without paying or being wanted
person present without invitation or permission

Examples

The party was going well until an unwelcome guest showed up and caused trouble.
We had to ask the unwelcome guest to leave the wedding reception.
No one wanted to confront the unwelcome guest who had slipped in unnoticed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gatecrasher - a person who attends an event without an invitation
