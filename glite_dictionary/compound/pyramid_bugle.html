pyramid bugle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪrəmɪd ˈbjuːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪrəmɪd ˈbjuːɡl̩ / (BrE)
A European evergreen perennial plant (Ajuga pyramidalis) with purple or blue flower spikes and a low-growing, carpeting habit.
European evergreen perennial plant

Examples

The pyramid bugle is valued by gardeners for its vibrant blue flowers and dense ground cover.
You can often find pyramid bugle growing wild in alpine meadows across Europe.
To create a lush border, plant pyramid bugle in partial shade and keep the soil moist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ajuga - genus of flowering plants including bugles
  • Degree Higher: bugle - any plant of the genus Ajuga
