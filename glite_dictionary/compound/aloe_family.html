aloe family

1. NOUN

/ ˈæloʊ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈaləʊ ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a group of flowering plants including the genus Aloe, sometimes considered a family or subfamily separate from the Liliaceae in some classification systems
a plant family including aloe species

Examples

Many succulents in the aloe family are prized for their medicinal properties.
The aloe family is sometimes grouped within the Liliaceae in older texts.
Gardeners often select plants from the aloe family for their drought tolerance.
