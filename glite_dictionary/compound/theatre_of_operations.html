theatre of operations

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̬ɚ əv ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə əv ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
A region or area where active military operations are taking place.
area of active military operations

Examples

The general inspected the theatre of operations before launching the next phase of the campaign.
Many soldiers spent years in the European theatre of operations during World War II.
Medical teams were deployed close to the theatre of operations to treat wounded personnel rapidly.

Synset Examples

the army was in the field awaiting action
he served in the Vietnam theater for three years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battlefield - the place where a battle is fought
