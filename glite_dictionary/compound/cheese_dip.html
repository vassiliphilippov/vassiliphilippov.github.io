cheese dip

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz dɪp / (AmE), / tʃiːz dɪp / (BrE)
A dish made by melting cheese and often other ingredients into a savory dip, usually served with chips, bread, or vegetables.
savory dip made from cheese

Examples

I brought a homemade cheese dip to the party, and everyone loved it.
She served the hot cheese dip with tortilla chips for the guests.
They enjoyed snacking on crackers and cheese dip during the movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queso - Mexican-style melted cheese dip
