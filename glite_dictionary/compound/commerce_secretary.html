Commerce Secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmɚs səˈkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɜːs səˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
the government official who is the head of the Department of Commerce in the United States
head of the U.S. Department of Commerce
american

Examples

President Biden appointed Gina Raimondo as the new Commerce Secretary in 2021.
The Commerce Secretary plays a crucial role in shaping U.S. trade policies.
Past Commerce Secretaries have had significant influence on economic development.

Synset Examples

the position of Commerce Secretary was created in 1913

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secretary of Commerce - official head of the U.S. Department of Commerce
