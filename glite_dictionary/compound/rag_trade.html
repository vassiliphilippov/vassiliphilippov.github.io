rag trade

1. NOUN

/ ræɡ treɪd / (AmE), / ræɡ treɪd / (BrE)
the clothing and fashion industry, especially making and selling fashionable clothes
clothing and fashion industry
british

Examples

After college, she quickly found a job in the rag trade and began designing clothes.
Many young people aspire to work in the rag trade because of its glamour and creativity.
The rag trade has faced major changes with the rise of online shopping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion industry - the business sector concerned with the design, manufacture, and marketing of clothes

Etymology

Probably from 'rag' (slang for clothing) plus 'trade'. Commonly used in British English.
