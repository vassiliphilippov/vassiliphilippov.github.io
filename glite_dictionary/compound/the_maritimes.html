the Maritimes

1. PROPN

/ ðə ˈmɛrɪˌtaɪmz / (AmE), / ðə ˈmærɪˌtaɪmz / (BrE)
the Canadian provinces of New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island, considered together as a region.
eastern Canadian region: NB, NS, PEI

Examples

Many people enjoy visiting the Maritimes for their beautiful coastal scenery and friendly communities.
Tourism is a major industry in the Maritimes, especially in the summer months.
The culture of the Maritimes is influenced by both Acadian and Celtic traditions.

Etymology

From 'maritime,' meaning 'related to the sea,' reflecting the provinces' coastal geography.
