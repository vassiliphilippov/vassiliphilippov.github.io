lens of experience

1. NOUN

/ lɛnz əv ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / lɛnz əv ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
a way of looking at or interpreting things that is shaped by one's past experiences
perspective shaped by experience

Examples

She views every challenge through the lens of experience she gained from her career.
Judges often need to avoid the lens of experience to remain impartial.
Traveling abroad changed his lens of experience and taught him to be more open-minded.

Synset Examples

The lens of experience helped her make better decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perspective - a particular way of seeing or thinking about something
