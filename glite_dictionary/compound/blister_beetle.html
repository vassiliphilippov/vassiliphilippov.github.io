blister beetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɪstɚ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈblɪstə ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
A type of beetle that produces a secretion capable of causing blisters on the skin.
beetle causing skin blisters

Examples

A blister beetle crawled onto his hand, causing painful irritation.
Farmers often worry about blister beetles contaminating their hay crops.
Some medical balms were once made from the secretions of the blister beetle.
