to protect nature

1. VERB

/ prəˈtɛkt ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / prəˈtɛkt ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
to take action to prevent harm to the natural environment and conserve natural resources
take action to conserve the environment

Examples

We should all do our part to protect nature for future generations.
Many organizations work tirelessly to protect nature from pollution and destruction.
Teaching children how to protect nature is important for a sustainable future.

Synset Examples

It's important to protect nature and not defile it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preserve the environment - maintain the environment in its original or existing state
  • Antonym: harm the environment - cause damage to the natural environment
