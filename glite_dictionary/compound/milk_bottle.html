milk bottle

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk ˈbɑːɾl / (AmE), / mɪlk ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
A bottle designed to hold or serve milk, especially one used for feeding infants.
bottle for holding or feeding milk

Examples

She warmed the milk bottle before giving it to her infant.
Please rinse out the milk bottle after you use it.
Old-fashioned glass milk bottles are now considered collectible items.

Synset Examples

He recycled the empty milk bottle after use.
The baby cried for his milk bottle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baby bottle - a bottle with a nipple used for feeding babies
