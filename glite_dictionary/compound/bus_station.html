bus station

1. NOUN

/ bʌs ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / bʌs ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where buses regularly stop and passengers can get on or off, usually with facilities for waiting.
place where buses stop for passengers

Examples

The city has built a new bus station to help commuters transfer between routes.
She waited patiently at the bus station, watching the arrivals board for her bus to appear.
There is a taxi rank right outside the main entrance of the bus station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus terminal - a station where bus journeys begin or end
