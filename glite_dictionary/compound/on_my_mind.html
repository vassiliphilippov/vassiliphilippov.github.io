on my mind

1. X

/ ɑn maɪ maɪnd / (AmE), / ɒn maɪ maɪnd / (BrE)
continuously occupying one's thoughts; being frequently thought about
frequently in one's thoughts

Examples

You've been on my mind all day, and I can't stop thinking about you.
The exam results are on my mind this week—I keep worrying about them.
There's something important on my mind that I’d like to discuss with you.

Synset Examples

He’s always on my mind when I travel.
You’ve been on my mind lately.
The new project is constantly on my mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in one's thoughts - being thought about; occupying someone's attention
