active agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪv ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈæktɪv ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
A substance in a mixture or formulation that has a direct chemical or biological effect.
chemically or biologically effective substance

Examples

The disinfectant's active agent destroys bacteria and viruses on contact.
Researchers isolated the active agent responsible for the drug's therapeutic effects.
The cream contains an active agent that reduces skin inflammation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: active ingredient - the part of a product that produces the intended effect
