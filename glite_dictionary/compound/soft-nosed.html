soft-nosed

1. ADJ

/ sɔft noʊzd / (AmE), / sɒft nəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a bullet) having a soft tip that expands upon impact to increase damage.
bullet with a soft expanding tip

Examples

Hunters often use soft-nosed bullets for greater stopping power.
The police recovered a soft-nosed round from the crime scene.
Because soft-nosed ammunition expands on impact, it is considered more humane for certain types of hunting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hollow-point - a bullet with a hollowed-out tip to expand on impact
