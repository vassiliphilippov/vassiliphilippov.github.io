Higher National Diploma

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪər ˈnæʃənl dɪˈploʊmə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪə ˈnæʃənl dɪˈpləʊmə / (BrE)
a vocational qualification awarded in the UK and some other countries, typically after two years of post-secondary study, preparing students for a career or further study
UK vocational qualification after two years of study
british

Examples

After completing his Higher National Diploma, he started working as an engineering technician.
Many students choose a Higher National Diploma to gain practical skills before entering the workforce.
She progressed from a Higher National Diploma to a degree course at the university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diploma - an official document certifying the successful completion of a course of study
