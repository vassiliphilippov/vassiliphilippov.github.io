football field

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl ˌfild / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ˌfiːld / (BrE)
A rectangular area marked with lines and equipped with goals, on which the game of football is played.
area where football is played

Examples

The team gathered on the football field for their early morning practice.
He scored the winning goal from the far end of the football field.
After the game, fans rushed onto the football field to celebrate with the players.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitch - the area or surface on which a game is played, especially in British English
