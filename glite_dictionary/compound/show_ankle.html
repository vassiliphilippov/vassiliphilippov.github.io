to show ankle

1. VERB

/ ʃoʊ ˈæŋkəl / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈaŋkəl / (BrE)
to reveal one's ankle, especially in a way considered provocative or suggestive, often referring to social conventions of modesty in former times
reveal one's ankle suggestively

Examples

In Victorian times, to show ankle was considered scandalous for a woman.
When she hiked up her dress as she ran, she inadvertently began to show ankle.
He joked that the party was so formal, no one dared to show ankle.

Synset Examples

He was surprised to see her show ankle in the warm weather.
In that dress, she would definitely show ankle.
She wore a skirt that would show ankle when she danced.
