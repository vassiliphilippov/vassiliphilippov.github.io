Canadian Security Intelligence Service

1. PROPN

/ kəˈneɪdiən sɪˈkjʊrəti ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən sɪˈkjʊərəti ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the Canadian government agency responsible for collecting, analyzing, and reporting security intelligence to protect national security
Canada's main security intelligence agency

Examples

The Canadian Security Intelligence Service investigates threats to national security in Canada and abroad.
News reports say the Canadian Security Intelligence Service warned officials about potential cyberattacks.
He applied for a position as an analyst at the Canadian Security Intelligence Service.
