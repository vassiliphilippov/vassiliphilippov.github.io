trade unionist

1. NOUN

/ treɪd ˈjuːnjənɪst / (AmE), / treɪd ˈjuːnjənɪst / (BrE)
a member of a trade union, typically one who is active in promoting workers' interests
member of a trade union

Examples

As a proud trade unionist, she participated in every rally organized by the workers.
The government met with trade unionists to discuss improvements in labor laws.
He has been a trade unionist throughout his career, fighting for better pay and safer conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: union member - a person who belongs to a labor union

Etymology

From 'trade union' (an organized association of workers formed to protect their rights) + '-ist' (a person who practices or is concerned with something).
