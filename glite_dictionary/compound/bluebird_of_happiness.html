bluebird of happiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌbɝd əv ˈhæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈbluːbɜːd əv ˈhapɪnəs / (BrE)
a symbol of supreme happiness or perfect joy, often used as a metaphor for ultimate contentment in life
symbol of perfect happiness

Examples

For years she searched for the bluebird of happiness, hoping to find true contentment.
Some say that pursuing the bluebird of happiness is more important than actually finding it.
The painting of a bluebird of happiness brought a sense of optimism to the entire room.

Synset Examples

A sunny day always brings the bluebird of happiness.
She felt like she had found the bluebird of happiness.
He believed love was the bluebird of happiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happiness - the state of being happy

Etymology

Popularized by Maurice Maeterlinck’s 1908 play *The Blue Bird*; bluebirds have long been seen as symbols of happiness in folklore.
