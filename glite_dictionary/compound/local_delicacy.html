local delicacy

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈdɛlɪkəsi / (BrE)
a food that is considered a special or traditional treat in a particular region or area
regional specialty food

Examples

The guide recommended we try a local delicacy made from fresh seafood.
She always brings home a local delicacy from her travels abroad.
In Thailand, fried insects are considered a local delicacy.

Synset Examples

He introduced me to Palawala, a local delicacy worth trying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specialty - a type of food for which a region or restaurant is particularly known
