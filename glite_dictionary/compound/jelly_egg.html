jelly egg

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛli ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli ɛɡ / (BrE)
A small, egg-shaped piece of jellied candy with a sugar-glazed coating, often sold as a confectionery treat, especially during Easter.
egg-shaped jellied candy

Examples

My grandmother fills a basket with jelly eggs every Easter for the grandchildren.
The colorful jelly eggs were the first to disappear from the candy dish.
She selected a red jelly egg and popped it into her mouth, smiling at the fruity flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jelly bean - small, bean-shaped jellied candy with sugar coating
