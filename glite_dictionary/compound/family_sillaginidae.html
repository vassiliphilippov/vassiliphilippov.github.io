family Sillaginidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlədʒɪˌnaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈsɪlədʒɪˌniːdiː / (BrE)
A small family of small food fishes found in the shallow waters of the Pacific Ocean around Indonesia, known for their use as food.
family of small Pacific food fishes

Examples

Researchers discovered a new species in the family Sillaginidae along Indonesia's coast.
The family Sillaginidae plays an important role in local fisheries in the Pacific region.
Several members of the family Sillaginidae are popular ingredients in Southeast Asian cuisine.

Etymology

From New Latin Sillaginidae, based on the type genus Sillago, from Latin.
