fashion plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfaʃ.ən pleɪt / (BrE)
an illustration showing the latest styles in clothing, especially one found in magazines or books
illustration of latest clothing styles

Examples

The 19th-century magazine featured a colorful fashion plate on its cover every month.
She collected vintage fashion plates as a way to study historical trends in women's clothing.
The exhibit displayed several fashion plates from the Victorian era, revealing the changing shapes of dresses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion illustration - artistic depiction of clothing or accessories for display

2. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃ.ən pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən pleɪt / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who is extremely concerned with his dress and appearance, often following the latest fashions
person concerned with appearance and dress

Examples

Ever since college, Michael has been a true fashion plate with every outfit meticulously chosen.
At every party, she arrived as the fashion plate of the evening, drawing everyone's attention.
The magazine featured a famous fashion plate known for setting new style trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance

Etymology

Originally referred to an illustrated plate showing current fashions in magazines (19th century); later applied figuratively to people who exemplify fashionable dress.
