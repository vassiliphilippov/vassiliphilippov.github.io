spring vetchling

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a bushy European perennial plant with nodding racemose violet-blue flowers, belonging to the pea family
European perennial with blue flowers

Examples

The spring vetchling brightened the woodland with its violet-blue blossoms.
Gardeners often plant spring vetchling for early-spring color and pollinator attraction.
I spotted a cluster of spring vetchling along the riverbank during my walk.
