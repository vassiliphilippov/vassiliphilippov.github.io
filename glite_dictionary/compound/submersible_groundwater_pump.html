submersible groundwater pump

1. NOUN

/ səbˈmɜrsəbl ˈɡraʊndˌwɔtɚ pʌmp / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːsəbəl ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtə pʌmp / (BrE)
A pump designed to be submerged in water and used to sample or extract water from underground sources.
pump for extracting underground water

Examples

The team used a submersible groundwater pump to collect water samples from the deep well.
Regular maintenance of the submersible groundwater pump ensures a steady supply of clean water.
Installing a submersible groundwater pump improved irrigation efficiency on the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submersible pump - a pump that operates while completely submerged in liquid
