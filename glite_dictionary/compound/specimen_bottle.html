specimen bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛsəmɪn ˌbɑːtəl / (AmE), / ˈspɛsɪmɪn ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a bottle used for collecting and storing biological samples, such as urine, for medical examination
bottle for biological samples

Examples

The nurse handed me a specimen bottle to collect a urine sample for testing.
Always label the specimen bottle clearly before sending it to the lab.
Patients should ensure the specimen bottle is tightly sealed to avoid contamination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: container - an object used for holding or storing things
