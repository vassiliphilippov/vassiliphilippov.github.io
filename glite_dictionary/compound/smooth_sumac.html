smooth sumac

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈsʌmæk / (AmE), / smuːð ˈsʌmæk / (BrE)
a common shrub native to eastern North America, with smooth, waxy compound leaves, green flowers in clusters, and red berries; not poisonous
nonpoisonous North American shrub with red berries

Examples

The smooth sumac is easily identified by its bright red autumn foliage and clusters of red berries.
Wildlife often feed on the fruit of the smooth sumac during winter months.
Landscapers value smooth sumac for its ability to control soil erosion on slopes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sumac - any shrub or small tree of the genus Rhus
