baby grand

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˌɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌɡrænd / (BrE)
a small grand piano, typically shorter in length and suitable for smaller spaces
a small grand piano

Examples

Their living room was just big enough for a baby grand in the corner.
She always dreamed of playing a baby grand at her recitals.
The polished wood of the baby grand gleamed under the spotlight.
