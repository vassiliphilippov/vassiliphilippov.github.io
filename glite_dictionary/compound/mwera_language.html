Mwera language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmwɛrə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmwɛərə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in the southern coastal region of Tanzania
Bantu language of coastal Tanzania

Examples

The Mwera language is primarily used among communities in southeastern Tanzania.
Linguists have studied the Mwera language to understand its unique phonetic structure.
She wrote her thesis on the oral traditions preserved in the Mwera language.
