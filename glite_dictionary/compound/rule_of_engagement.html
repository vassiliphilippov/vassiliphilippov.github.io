rule of engagement

1. NOUN

/ ruːl əv ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ruːl əv ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
a directive or set of guidelines specifying the circumstances and limitations under which military or other personnel may initiate or engage in combat or other interactions
military engagement guidelines

Examples

The general briefed the soldiers on the new rule of engagement before the mission.
Each operation is governed by a specific rule of engagement to minimize civilian casualties.
All personnel must strictly follow the rule of engagement during peacekeeping deployments.

Synset Examples

The military issued a new rule of engagement for the operation.

Understanding the rule of engagement is crucial for the mission.
