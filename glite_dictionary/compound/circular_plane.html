circular plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrkjələr pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə pleɪn / (BrE)
a woodworking plane with a curved, flexible sole designed for shaping concave or convex surfaces
woodworking plane with flexible sole

Examples

The carpenter used a circular plane to create the rounded edge of the tabletop.
For curved moldings, a circular plane is much more effective than a standard flat plane.
Restoring antique furniture often requires a circular plane to match the original contours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a tool used to smooth or shape wood surfaces
