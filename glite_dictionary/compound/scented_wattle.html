scented wattle

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntɪd ˈwɒt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɪd ˈwɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A tropical American thorny shrub or small tree with fragrant yellow flowers, used in perfumery.
tropical shrub or tree with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The scented wattle blooms in late spring, filling the air with its sweet fragrance.
Many perfumers value scented wattle for its aromatic yellow flowers.
Gardeners in tropical regions often plant scented wattle to attract pollinators and add color.
