print shop

1. NOUN

/ prɪnt ʃɑp / (AmE), / prɪnt ʃɒp / (BrE)
a place where printing is carried out, typically as a business
shop where printing is done

Examples

He took the posters to the print shop to have them professionally printed.
Local businesses often rely on the print shop for flyers and brochures.
After college, she worked in a print shop learning the basics of graphic design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printing house - a business or building where printing is done, often at a larger scale
  • Synonym: copy shop - a shop that offers photocopying and related services
