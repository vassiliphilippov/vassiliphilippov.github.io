2. NOUN

/ ɡræs pɪŋk / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs pɪŋk / (BrE)
A North American orchid (Calopogon tuberosus), with pink flowers commonly found in wet meadows and bogs.
a North American pink-flowered orchid

Examples

The wet meadow was bright with blooming grass pinks in early summer.
The grass pink is admired for its delicate beauty and vibrant color.
