horned violet

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
A European species of viola with a long, slender corolla spur, commonly grown for its colorful flowers.
European viola species with long corolla spur

Examples

The horned violet is prized by gardeners for its striking purple and yellow blooms.
You can find horned violet growing wild across parts of Europe in spring.
Many hybrid varieties have been developed from the original horned violet species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viola - any plant of the genus Viola, including violets and pansies
