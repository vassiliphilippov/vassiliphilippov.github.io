Bluegrass Country

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːɡræs ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈbluːɡrɑːs ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
an area in central Kentucky known for its fertile bluegrass pastures and thoroughbred horse breeding.
region of central Kentucky noted for bluegrass and horses

Examples

The rolling hills of Bluegrass Country are famous for their scenic beauty and historic horse farms.
She traveled to Bluegrass Country to attend the annual horse racing festival.
Many visitors to Kentucky make sure to explore Bluegrass Country for its unique charm and heritage.

Etymology

Named for the abundance of bluegrass (Poa pratensis) found in the region.
