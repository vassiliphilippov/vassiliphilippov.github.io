brown Betty

1. NOUN

/ ˌbraʊn ˈbɛti / (AmE), / ˌbraʊn ˈbɛti / (BrE)
a baked pudding made with apples and breadcrumbs, often sweetened and spiced
baked apple and breadcrumb pudding

Examples

For dessert, she made a delicious brown Betty served warm with vanilla ice cream.
The old cookbook included a recipe for brown Betty among classic American desserts.
Have you ever tasted a brown Betty at the county fair?
