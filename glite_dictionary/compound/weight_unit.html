weight unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪt ˌjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈweɪt ˌjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a standard unit used for measuring weight or mass, such as a gram, pound, or kilogram
standard unit of weight measurement

Examples

The recipe calls for sugar to be measured in weight units like grams or ounces.
Scientists around the world use the kilogram as the standard weight unit.
When shipping goods, choosing the correct weight unit is crucial for accurate pricing.

Synset Examples

he placed two weights in the scale pan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unit of measurement - a fixed quantity used to express a physical quantity
  • Synonym: mass unit - a unit used to measure mass
