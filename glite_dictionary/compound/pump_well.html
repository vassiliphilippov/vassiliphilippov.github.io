pump well

1. NOUN

/ pʌmp wɛl / (AmE), / pʌmp wɛl / (BrE)
an enclosure in the middle of a ship's hold that protects the ship's pumps
enclosure protecting ship's pumps

Examples

The engineer inspected the pump well to ensure there was no blockage affecting the bilge system.
Regular cleaning of the pump well is crucial for maintaining a ship's drainage efficiency.
Access to the pump well was necessary during the maintenance operation in the hold.
