flue stop

1. NOUN

/ fluː stɑp / (AmE), / fluː stɒp / (BrE)
A stop on an organ that produces sound with the tone quality of a flue pipe.
organ stop with flue pipe tone

Examples

The cathedral organ features a variety of flue stops that create rich, mellow sounds.
He adjusted the flue stop to achieve a softer tone for the hymn.
Many organists prefer using a flue stop for accompanying choral pieces.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reed stop - organ stop imitating a reed instrument
