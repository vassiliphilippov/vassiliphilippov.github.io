water caltrop

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈkæltrɑp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈkæltrɒp / (BrE)
an aquatic plant of the genus Trapa, grown for its edible horned nut
aquatic plant with edible nut

Examples

The water caltrop is a popular snack during some Asian festivals.
Farmers harvest water caltrops from ponds in late autumn.
The unique horn-like shape of the water caltrop makes it easy to recognize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water chestnut - aquatic plant whose edible crisp corm is used in cooking
