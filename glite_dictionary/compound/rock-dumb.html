rock-dumb

1. ADJ

/ rɑk dʌm / (AmE), / rɒk dʌm / (BrE)
Extremely foolish or completely stunned, as if rendered speechless or senseless.
Extremely foolish or stunned
informal

Examples

After forgetting his password for the tenth time, he felt rock-dumb.
When I won the award, I stood there rock-dumb with surprise.
Please, don't act rock-dumb during the meeting—just ask for help if you need it.

Synset Examples

He felt rock-dumb after forgetting his lines.
She was rock-dumb when he surprised her with a gift.
Don't be rock-dumb; just follow the instructions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumb - lacking the power of speech or, informally, stupid
