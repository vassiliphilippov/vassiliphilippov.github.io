mentally retarded

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.təli ɹɪˈtɑɹ.dɪd / (AmE), / ˈmen.təl.i rɪˈtɑː.dɪd / (BrE)
(offensive, dated) People collectively who have significant intellectual or cognitive impairment; now usually referred to as people with intellectual disabilities.
people with intellectual disabilities (offensive, dated)
offensive
