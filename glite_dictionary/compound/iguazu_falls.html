Iguazu Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˌiɡwəˈzu fɔlz / (AmE), / ˌiːɡwɑːˈzuː fɔːlz / (BrE)
a large and spectacular waterfall on the border of Argentina and Brazil, renowned for its size and beauty
famous South American waterfall

Examples

Tourists from around the world visit Iguazu Falls to witness its breathtaking scenery.
The thundering roar of Iguazu Falls can be heard from miles away.
On their trip to South America, they took a boat ride near Iguazu Falls for a closer view.

Etymology

From Guarani *y* (water) + *guasu* (big), referring to 'big water.'
