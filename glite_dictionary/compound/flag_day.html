Flag Day

1. PROPN

/ flæɡ deɪ / (AmE), / flæɡ deɪ / (BrE)
A day in the United States commemorating the adoption of the national flag, observed on June 14.
U.S. national observance of the flag
american

Examples

Every year on Flag Day, schools across the country hold special ceremonies.
The local parade to celebrate Flag Day attracts hundreds of visitors.
Some people display the American flag outside their homes on Flag Day.
