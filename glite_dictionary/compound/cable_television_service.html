cable television service

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌtɛl.əˌvɪʒ.ən ˈsɜːr.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌtel.ɪˈvɪʒ.ən ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a system of delivering television programs to viewers by transmitting signals through coaxial or fiber-optic cables rather than by broadcast airwaves.
system for delivering TV via cables

Examples

We decided to subscribe to a cable television service to access more channels.
The cable television service in our area offers high-definition sports packages.
Many people are switching from cable television service to streaming platforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satellite television - a system delivering TV programming via satellite signals
  • Antonym: broadcast television - television transmitted over the air via radio waves
