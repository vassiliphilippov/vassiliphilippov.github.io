permabitch attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.məˌbɪtʃ ˈæt̬.ə.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.məˌbɪtʃ ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
an unfriendly, hostile, or difficult demeanor that is consistently present
persistent unfriendly demeanor (slang)
slang informal

Coworkers avoided him due to his constant permabitch attitude in meetings.
Despite her permabitch attitude, she was very loyal to those close to her.
