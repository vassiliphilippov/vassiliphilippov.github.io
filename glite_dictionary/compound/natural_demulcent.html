natural demulcent

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚəl dɪˈmʌlsnt / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl dɪˈmʌls(ə)nt / (BrE)
A substance derived from natural sources that soothes irritation, especially of mucous membranes.
soothing natural substance for irritation
medical

Examples

Many herbal teas contain natural demulcents that help relieve sore throats.
Aloe vera gel is often praised as a natural demulcent for irritated skin.
Slippery elm is a traditional natural demulcent used to soothe digestive discomfort.

Synset Examples

Honey is a natural demulcent for irritated mucous membranes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demulcent - a substance that soothes irritation, especially of mucous membranes

Etymology

From Latin 'demulcere' (to soothe), with 'natural' from Latin 'natura'.
