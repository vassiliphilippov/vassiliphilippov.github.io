pint-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaɪnt saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnt saɪzd / (BrE)
Very small; much smaller than usual or expected.
very small in size

Examples

Their pint-sized puppy barked furiously at the mail carrier.
He bought a pint-sized laptop that's perfect for travel.
The pint-sized cupcakes were gone in minutes at the party.

Synset Examples

The pint-sized dog stole my sandwich.
His pint-sized car fits perfectly in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miniature - very small, much smaller than usual
  • Synonym: tiny - extremely small in size

Etymology

From 'pint,' a small volume measurement, with 'sized' to indicate the scale; figurative use since mid-20th century.
