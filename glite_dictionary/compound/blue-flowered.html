blue-flowered

1. ADJ

/ bluː ˈflaʊɚd / (AmE), / bluː ˈflaʊəd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having flowers that are blue in color.
having blue flowers

Examples

The blue-flowered plant was the standout feature in the spring garden.
Several blue-flowered species thrive in cool, shaded areas of the forest.
She chose a blue-flowered dress that matched the color of her eyes.
