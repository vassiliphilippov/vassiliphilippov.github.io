theatre ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiːətər ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket granting entry to a theater, typically for a performance or show
ticket for entry to a theater

Examples

She forgot to bring her theatre ticket to the play and had to buy a new one.
Online booking makes it easy to purchase a theatre ticket for your favorite show.
I found an old theatre ticket from a musical I saw years ago in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ticket - a piece of paper or card that gives the holder a certain right, especially to enter a place or travel on transport
