department of chemistry

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
the academic division within a university or college responsible for teaching and research in chemistry.
academic division for chemistry

Examples

She was appointed head of the department of chemistry at the university last year.
The department of chemistry offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate courses.
Our research was supported by grants from the department of chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemistry department - the department responsible for chemistry
  • Degree Lower: department of physics - academic department for teaching and research in physics
