broadcasting station

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔdˌkæstɪŋ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A facility equipped and authorized to transmit radio or television programs to the public.
facility for transmitting radio/TV programs

Examples

The local broadcasting station aired the emergency alert during the storm.
She got a job as a producer at the city’s main broadcasting station.
Many new technologies are changing the way a broadcasting station serves its audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio station - a station for broadcasting radio programs
  • Synonym: television station - a station for broadcasting television programs
