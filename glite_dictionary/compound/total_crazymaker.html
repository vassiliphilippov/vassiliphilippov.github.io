total crazymaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊɾl ˈkreɪziˌmeɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈkreɪziˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who persistently causes chaos, confusion, or drama in social situations
person who causes chaos or drama
informal

Examples

Have you met Lisa's new roommate? She's a total crazymaker and always stirs up trouble.
I try to avoid any total crazymaker at work to keep the day peaceful.
He used to be calm, but lately, he's been acting like a total crazymaker about everything.

Synset Examples

Avoid that guy; he's a total crazymaker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person who habitually causes difficulty or problems
  • Antonym: peacemaker - a person who helps to prevent or stop conflict
