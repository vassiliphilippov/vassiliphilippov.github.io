tech company

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / tɛk ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a company whose primary business involves developing or selling technology-related products or services
company focusing on technology products or services

Examples

She hopes to start her career at a leading tech company after graduation.
Many tech companies are headquartered in Silicon Valley.
The tech company announced a breakthrough in artificial intelligence technology this year.

Synset Examples

She moved to Redmond for a job at a tech company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology company - a company engaged in the technology sector

Etymology

Shortened from 'technology company'.
