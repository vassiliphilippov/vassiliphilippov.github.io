male reproductive gland

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪl ɹiˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl ɹiˌprəˈdʌktɪv ɡlænd / (BrE)
a gland in the male body that produces sperm or male sex hormones, such as the testes or prostate
gland producing sperm or male hormones
medical

Examples

The male reproductive gland plays a crucial role in the production of sperm.
Diseases affecting the male reproductive gland can lead to fertility issues.
Some cancers originate in the male reproductive gland, such as the prostate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testis - the primary male reproductive gland, producing sperm and hormones
  • Synonym: prostate - a gland in males that contributes to seminal fluid
