rank order

1. NOUN

/ ræŋk ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ræŋk ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
an arrangement of people or things according to their relative rank, position, or importance
arrangement by rank or order

Examples

The students were listed in rank order based on their exam scores.
Promotion in the army depends on your rank order within your unit.
Applicants are admitted according to a rank order established by the selection committee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hierarchy - a system in which people or things are arranged by rank
