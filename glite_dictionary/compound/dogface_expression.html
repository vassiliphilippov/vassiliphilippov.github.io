dogface expression

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔgˌfeɪs ɛkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɒgˌfeɪs ɪksˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
an affectionate or endearing facial expression, typically resembling the look of a pleading or loving dog
affectionate, dog-like facial expression
informal

Examples

She gave him a dogface expression when asking for a favor.
His dogface expression was impossible to resist, just like a puppy's.
With a practiced dogface expression, she persuaded her parents to let her stay out late.

Synset Examples

Her dogface expression melted everyone's hearts.
