Pridnestrovian Moldavian Republic

1. PROPN

/ prɪdˌnɛstroʊviən mɒlˈdeɪviən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / prɪdˌnɛstrəʊviən mɒlˈdeɪviən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
a de facto unrecognized state in Eastern Europe, which has declared independence from Moldova, also known as Transnistria
unrecognized state in Eastern Europe (Transnistria)

Examples

Few countries recognize the Pridnestrovian Moldavian Republic as an independent state.
The Pridnestrovian Moldavian Republic declared independence from Moldova in 1990.
International organizations often refer to the Pridnestrovian Moldavian Republic as Transnistria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Transnistria - another name for the Pridnestrovian Moldavian Republic
