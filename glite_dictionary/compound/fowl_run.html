fowl run

1. NOUN

/ faʊl rʌn / (AmE), / faʊl rʌn / (BrE)
an enclosed area or yard where poultry are kept
enclosure for keeping poultry

Examples

The chickens wandered around the fowl run pecking at the ground.
After building a new fowl run, the farmer was able to keep more ducks and hens.
The children helped clean out the fowl run every week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - a structure for housing chickens
  • Degree Higher: aviary - a large enclosure for keeping birds
