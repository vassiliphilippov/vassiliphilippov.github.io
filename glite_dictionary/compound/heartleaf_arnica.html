heartleaf arnica

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌlif ɑrˈnɪkə / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌliːf ɑːˈnɪkə / (BrE)
a wildflower with heart-shaped leaves and broad yellow flower heads, native to alpine areas west of the Rockies from Alaska to southern California
wildflower with heart-shaped leaves

Examples

We stopped to admire the blooming heartleaf arnica along the hiking trail.
Botanists study how heartleaf arnica adapts to high-altitude environments.
The heartleaf arnica is easily recognized by its bright yellow flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arnica - a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, often used medicinally
