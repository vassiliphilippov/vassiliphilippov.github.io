tax income

1. NOUN

/ tæks ˈɪn.kəm / (AmE), / tæks ˈɪn.kəm / (BrE)
income received by a government from taxes imposed on individuals and businesses.
government income from taxes

Examples

The government's tax income increased significantly after the new legislation was passed.
Many public projects are funded using tax income collected from citizens and corporations.
Lower tax income may result in cuts to essential public services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax revenue - government income generated from taxation
