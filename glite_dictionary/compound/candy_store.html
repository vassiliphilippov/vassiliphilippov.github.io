candy store

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændi stɔr / (AmE), / ˈkændi stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells candy and other sweets.
shop selling sweets and candy
american

Examples

The children spent their allowance at the candy store around the corner.
Walking into the candy store was like entering a world of color and sugar.
She dreamed of owning her own candy store someday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet shop - a shop selling sweets and confectionery (British English equivalent)
