Pacific Standard Time

1. NOUN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time zone of the Pacific coast of the United States and Canada, 8 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-8)
time zone for US/Canada Pacific coast

Examples

The conference call is scheduled for 3 p.m. Pacific Standard Time.
Tickets go on sale at midnight Pacific Standard Time tonight.
Please note that business hours are from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Pacific Standard Time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pacific Time - time observed in the Pacific Time Zone, including both standard and daylight time
  • Antonym: Eastern Standard Time - time zone used in the eastern states of the US, GMT-5
  • Antonym: Greenwich Mean Time - the mean solar time at the Royal Observatory, Greenwich, London
