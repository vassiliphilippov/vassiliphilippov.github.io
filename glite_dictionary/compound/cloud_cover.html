cloud cover

1. NOUN

/ klaʊd ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / klaʊd ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
the extent to which the sky is covered by clouds, typically expressed as a percentage or in descriptive terms
extent of sky covered by clouds

Examples

The cloud cover increased throughout the afternoon, making it difficult to see the sun.
Weather forecasters predicted heavy cloud cover for most of the weekend.
Clear skies are expected tomorrow after today's thick cloud cover.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: overcast - sky completely covered by clouds
  • Degree Lower: clear sky - sky with no clouds
