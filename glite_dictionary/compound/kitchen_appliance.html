kitchen appliance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən əˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃən əˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
A device or machine for use in the kitchen, especially for preparing food.
device for preparing food in the kitchen

Examples

Modern homes often have several kitchen appliances like microwaves and blenders.
Cleaning a kitchen appliance regularly can help it last longer.
She installed a new dishwasher, making it her favorite kitchen appliance.
