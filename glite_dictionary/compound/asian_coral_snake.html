Asian coral snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən ˈkɔɹəl sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈeɪʒ(ə)n ˈkɒrəl sneɪk / (BrE)
A venomous coral snake native to Asia, particularly found in India, known for its bright coloration and potent neurotoxic venom.
a venomous coral snake native to Asia, especially India

Examples

The Asian coral snake is renowned for its vivid red, yellow, and black bands.
Scientists have studied the venom of the Asian coral snake for potential medical applications.
Though beautiful, the Asian coral snake should be avoided due to its potent bite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coral snake - venomous snake known for its bright bands of color
