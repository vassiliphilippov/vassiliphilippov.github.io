hair style

1. NOUN

/ hɛr staɪl / (AmE), / heə staɪl / (BrE)
a particular way in which hair is cut, shaped, or arranged, especially for aesthetic effect
arrangement or style of hair

Examples

She changed her hair style for the wedding, opting for an elegant updo.
The celebrity's new hair style set a trend among her fans.
Choosing a flattering hair style can boost your confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairstyle - alternative spelling; arrangement of hair
