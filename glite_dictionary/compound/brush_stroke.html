brush stroke

1. NOUN

/ brʌʃ stroʊk / (AmE), / brʌʃ strəʊk / (BrE)
a mark or line made by a brush, especially as part of a painting
mark made by a brush in painting

Examples

You can see each brush stroke in the texture of the painting.
The artist used bold brush strokes to convey emotion in her work.
Each brush stroke adds depth and character to the canvas.

Synset Examples

The artist's brush stroke was bold and expressive.
The artist's widdly brush strokes created a beautiful effect.
