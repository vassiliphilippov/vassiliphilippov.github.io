mutual fund company

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuː.tʃu.əl fʌnd ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.tʃu.əl fʌnd ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a financial institution that manages a pooled fund by regularly selling and redeeming its shares to investors
company managing mutual funds

Examples

She invested her savings in a mutual fund company to diversify her portfolio.
The mutual fund company issues and redeems shares to meet investor demand.
Many people trust a mutual fund company to manage their retirement investments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutual fund - an investment vehicle pooling money from many investors
