Afghan hound

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfˌɡæn haʊnd / (AmE), / ˈaf.ɡæn haʊnd / (BrE)
a tall, elegant breed of dog with a long, silky coat, originally from Afghanistan, known for its grace and speed
breed of tall, graceful dog with long silky coat

Examples

Her Afghan hound won best in show at the national dog competition last weekend.
The Afghan hound is admired for its flowing coat and dignified appearance.
He brushed his Afghan hound every day to keep its fur smooth and tangle-free.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saluki - another ancient breed of sighthound from the Middle East

Etymology

Named after Afghanistan, the breed's country of origin.
