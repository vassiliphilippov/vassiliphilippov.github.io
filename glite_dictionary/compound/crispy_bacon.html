crispy bacon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪspi ˈbeɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspi ˈbeɪkən / (BrE)
bacon that is cooked until it becomes firm and crunchy
bacon cooked until crunchy

Examples

The smell of crispy bacon filled the kitchen every Sunday morning.
She topped her salad with bits of crispy bacon for extra flavor.
Many people love crispy bacon in their breakfast sandwiches.

Synset Examples

I can't resist a breakfast with crispy bacon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacon - salted and cured meat from the back or sides of a pig
