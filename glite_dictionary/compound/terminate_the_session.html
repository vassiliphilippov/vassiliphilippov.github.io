to terminate the session

1. VERB

/ ˈtɝː.məˌneɪt ðə səˈʃɛn / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.mɪˌneɪt ðə səˈʃen / (BrE)
to bring an ongoing operation or process to an end, especially in computing or formal contexts
end an ongoing operation or process

Examples

For security reasons, please terminate the session when you are finished.
The administrator decided to terminate the session after discovering suspicious activity.
Before leaving the workstation, always remember to terminate the session properly.

Synset Examples

You must terminate the session to save changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: end the session - to stop or finish the ongoing session
  • Antonym: initiate the session - to start an operation or session
