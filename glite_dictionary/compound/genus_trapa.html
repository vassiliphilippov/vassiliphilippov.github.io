genus Trapa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtreɪpə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtreɪpə / (BrE)
a small genus of aquatic perennial herbs native to Eurasia, including the water chestnut
a plant genus containing the water chestnut

Examples

Botanists have identified the genus Trapa as the source of the edible water chestnut.
The genus Trapa is commonly found in slow-moving rivers and lakes throughout Eurasia.
Research on invasive species has focused on several members of the genus Trapa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water chestnut - edible aquatic plant of the genus Trapa

Etymology

From Latin Trapa, the classical name for water chestnut.
