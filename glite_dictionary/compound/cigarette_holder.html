cigarette holder

1. NOUN

/ sɪgəˌrɛt ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / sɪgəˌrɛt ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a slender tube into which a cigarette is inserted for smoking, held in the hand or mouth to keep the cigarette at a distance
tube for holding a cigarette while smoking

Examples

She elegantly placed her cigarette holder between her fingers before lighting the cigarette.
The actor used a long cigarette holder as a prop in the vintage film.
Vintage shops often sell ornate cigarette holders from the early twentieth century.
