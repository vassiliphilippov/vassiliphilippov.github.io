to receive a compliment

1. VERB

/ rɪˈsiv ə kɑm.plə.mənt / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːv ə ˈkɒm.plɪ.mənt / (BrE)
to be given an expression of praise or admiration by someone
be given praise or admiration

Examples

He was surprised to receive a compliment on his presentation from the company director.
It's always nice to receive a compliment about your cooking when hosting a dinner party.
She blushed when she received a compliment on her new haircut.

Synset Examples

She received a compliment for a great job at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay a compliment - to express praise or admiration to someone
  • Synonym: give a compliment - to say something nice or admiring to someone
  • Antonym: insult - an expression or remark that is rude or offensive
