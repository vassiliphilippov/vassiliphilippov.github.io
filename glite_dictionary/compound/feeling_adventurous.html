feeling adventurous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfilɪŋ ædˈvɛntʃərəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ ədˈvɛntʃərəs / (BrE)
experiencing a desire to take risks or try new and exciting things
willing to take risks or try new things

Examples

I'm feeling adventurous tonight, so let's try that new spicy restaurant downtown.
When she's feeling adventurous, she likes to go hiking in unfamiliar places.
He's not usually spontaneous, but today he's feeling adventurous and wants to learn how to surf.

Synset Examples

You know what? I'm feeling adventurous today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try novel experiences
  • Antonym: cautious - careful to avoid potential problems or dangers
