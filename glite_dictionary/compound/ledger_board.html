ledger board

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛdʒər bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒə bɔːd / (BrE)
a horizontal support, especially the top rail of a fence or balustrade
top rail of a fence or balustrade

Examples

The carpenter measured the length of the ledger board before installing it on the fence.
To secure the railing properly, ensure the ledger board is level and firmly attached.
Rotting in the ledger board can weaken the entire structure of the balustrade.
