water ice

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ aɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə aɪs / (BrE)
A frozen dessert containing no milk, typically made from fruit juice and sugar, with a soft, mushy consistency.
frozen dessert made from fruit juice, no milk

Examples

During the summer, I love eating water ice in all different flavors.
The kids enjoyed a lemon water ice after playing in the park.
Philadelphia is famous for its delicious water ice stands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorbet - a sweet iced dessert made from fruit juice and sugar, similar to water ice
  • Degree Higher: gelato - an Italian-style ice cream made with milk and sugar
  • Degree Higher: ice cream - a frozen dessert containing cream or milk and sugar
