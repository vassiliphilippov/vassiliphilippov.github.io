penile implant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːnaɪl ˈɪmplænt / (AmE), / ˈpiːnaɪl ˈɪmplɑːnt / (BrE)
a medical device surgically placed in the penis to create an artificial erection, typically used to treat erectile dysfunction
device implanted in penis for erection
medical

Examples

After years of unsuccessful treatments, Mark decided to get a penile implant to address his condition.
The doctor explained the risks and benefits of a penile implant before scheduling the surgery.
Most men with severe erectile dysfunction who opt for a penile implant report high satisfaction rates.

Etymology

From Latin 'penis' (tail, penis) and Latin 'implantare' (to plant in).
