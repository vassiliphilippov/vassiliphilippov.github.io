so dutty

1. ADJ

/ soʊ ˈdʌti / (AmE), / səʊ ˈdʌti / (BrE)
Extremely dirty or messy; in a very unclean state.
extremely dirty or messy
informal

Examples

The kitchen was so dutty that no one wanted to cook in it.
After the party, the living room looked so dutty that cleaning took hours.
The playground had become so dutty after the storm that it needed a thorough wash.

Synset Examples

Her room was so dutty that she decided to clean it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filthy - very dirty; covered with filth
  • Synonym: dirty - not clean; covered or marked with an unclean substance

Etymology

'Dutty' is a nonstandard spelling and pronunciation of 'dirty', used in Caribbean English and some dialects.
