hidden gold mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən ɡoʊld maɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɪd.ən ɡəʊld maɪn / (BrE)
a valuable source or opportunity that is not immediately obvious or accessible
an unsuspected valuable resource

Examples

The old files turned out to be a hidden gold mine of market research data.
Her travel blog is a hidden gold mine for anyone planning a trip to Europe.
This small library is a hidden gold mine for rare historical books.

Synset Examples

The abandoned site was a hidden gold mine for treasure hunters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold mine - a source of great wealth or abundance
