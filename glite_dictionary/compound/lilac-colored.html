lilac-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪlæk ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪlək ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having a pale purple color, like that of the lilac flower.
pale purple in color

Examples

She wore a lilac-colored dress to the spring wedding, which matched the flowers in her bouquet.
The living room walls are painted a soft lilac-colored hue that gives the space a calming atmosphere.
A lilac-colored scarf draped around her neck added a gentle touch to her outfit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavender - a pale purple color, similar to that of lavender flowers
  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
  • Synonym: violet - bluish-purple color, named after the violet flower

Etymology

Formed from "lilac" (the flower, from Arabic and Persian origins) + "colored".
