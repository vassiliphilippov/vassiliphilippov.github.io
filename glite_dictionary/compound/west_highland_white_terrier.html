West Highland white terrier

1. NOUN

/ wɛst ˈhaɪlənd waɪt ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / wɛst ˈhaɪlənd waɪt ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a small white terrier breed with a long coat, developed in Scotland.
small Scottish white terrier breed

Examples

The West Highland white terrier is known for its friendly and energetic personality.
She adopted a West Highland white terrier puppy from the local animal shelter.
At the dog show, his West Highland white terrier won first place in its group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrier - a type of small, lively dog originally bred for hunting

Etymology

Named after the West Highlands region of Scotland, where the breed was developed.
