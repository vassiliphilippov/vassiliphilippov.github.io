cool hunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːl ˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈkuːl ˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person whose job or role is to identify and predict new and emerging trends, especially in fashion or popular culture
trend-spotter, forecaster of new trends

Examples

The magazine hired a cool hunter to discover what styles would be popular next year.
As a professional cool hunter, she attends many fashion shows and art events.
Tech companies rely on a cool hunter to help them appeal to younger customers.

Synset Examples

As a cool hunter, he always knows the latest trends.
The fashion magazine featured her as a cool hunter this month.
Being a cool hunter means spotting trends before they explode.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendspotter - a person who identifies new trends
  • Synonym: forecaster - a person who predicts future trends or developments

Etymology

Formed from 'cool' (fashionable, trendy) and 'hunter' (one who hunts or seeks), popularized in late 20th century marketing.
