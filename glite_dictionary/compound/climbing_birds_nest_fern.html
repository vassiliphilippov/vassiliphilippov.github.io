climbing bird's nest fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ bɜrdz nɛst fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ bɜːdz nɛst fɜːn / (BrE)
a type of fern native to tropical Africa, Australasia, and Polynesia, known for its climbing habit and nest-like fronds
a tropical climbing species of bird's nest fern

Examples

The climbing bird's nest fern thrives in the humid forests of Polynesia.
I spotted a climbing bird's nest fern growing up the trunk of a large tree.
Many gardeners prize the climbing bird's nest fern for its unusual appearance and foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bird's nest fern - a fern with rosette-forming fronds resembling a bird's nest
