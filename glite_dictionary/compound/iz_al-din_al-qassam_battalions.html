Iz Al-Din Al-Qassam Battalions

1. PROPN

/ ɪz æl ˈdiːn æl kɑːˈsæm bəˈtæljənz / (AmE), / ɪz æl ˈdiːn æl kəˈsæm bəˈtæljənz / (BrE)
the armed wing of Hamas, responsible for suicide bombings and various attacks on Israel
military wing of Hamas

Examples

The Iz Al-Din Al-Qassam Battalions are frequently mentioned in reports about the conflict in Gaza.
Authorities have identified the Iz Al-Din Al-Qassam Battalions as the main military wing of Hamas.
The operations of the Iz Al-Din Al-Qassam Battalions drew international attention after a series of attacks.

Etymology

Named after Izz ad-Din al-Qassam, a Syrian Muslim preacher and resistance leader; Arabic origin.
