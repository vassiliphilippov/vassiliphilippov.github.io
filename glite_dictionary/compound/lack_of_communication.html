lack of communication

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæk əv kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlæk əv kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
an absence or insufficient exchange of information or ideas between people or groups
insufficient exchange of information

Examples

The main reason for the project's failure was a lack of communication among team members.
Their relationship suffered due to a persistent lack of communication.
A lack of communication between management and staff led to confusion and mistakes.

Synset Examples

They expressed their flustration about the lack of communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscommunication - failure to communicate information clearly
  • Antonym: communication - the exchange of information or ideas
