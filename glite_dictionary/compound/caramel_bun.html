caramel bun

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærəˌmɛl bʌn / (AmE), / ˈkærəmɛl bʌn / (BrE)
A sweet pastry made by rolling dough with sugar and nuts, slicing it, and baking it in muffin tins with honey or sugar and butter at the bottom.
sweet pastry with caramelized bottom

Examples

She brought a tray of freshly baked caramel buns to the family breakfast.
The caramel bun was gooey and sweet, perfect with a cup of coffee.
He followed his grandmother's recipe to make delicious caramel buns for the bake sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky bun - sweet roll with a sticky syrupy topping, often including nuts
