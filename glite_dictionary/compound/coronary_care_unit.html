coronary care unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəˌnɛri kær ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ə.nəri keə ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a hospital department specially staffed and equipped to provide intensive care for patients with serious heart problems
hospital unit for intensive cardiac care
medical

Examples

He spent three days in the coronary care unit after his heart attack.
The hospital recently upgraded its coronary care unit with the latest monitoring equipment.
Relatives were relieved to hear she was stable in the coronary care unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensive care unit - hospital unit for patients requiring close monitoring and intensive treatment
