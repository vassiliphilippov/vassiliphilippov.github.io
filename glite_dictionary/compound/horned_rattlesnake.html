horned rattlesnake

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd ˈræt̬lˌsneɪk / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈræt(ə)lˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a small, pale-colored rattlesnake found in the deserts of the southwestern United States, known for moving in an S-shaped curve and having horn-like scales above its eyes
desert rattlesnake with horn-like scales

Examples

The horned rattlesnake is well-camouflaged against the pale sands of the desert.
While hiking in Arizona, I spotted a horned rattlesnake slithering between the rocks.
The distinctive horns above the eyes help the horned rattlesnake blend into its sandy environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rattlesnake - a venomous American snake with a series of interlocking segments at the end of its tail, which it vibrates to produce a rattling sound
