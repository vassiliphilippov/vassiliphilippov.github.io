pearl barley

1. NOUN

/ pɜrl ˈbɑrli / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈbɑːli / (BrE)
barley that has been polished to remove its outer husk and bran layers, resulting in small, round, white grains used in cooking
hulled and polished barley grain

Examples

Add pearl barley to the soup to give it a hearty texture.
She bought a bag of pearl barley for making a traditional stew.
After rinsing the pearl barley, cook it until the grains are tender.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barley - a type of cereal grain used for food and brewing
