Swan River everlasting

1. NOUN

/ swɒn ˈrɪvər ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / swɒn ˈrɪvə ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
an Australian annual plant with light pink, nodding flower heads, sometimes classified in the genus Helipterum
an Australian annual with pink nodding flowers

Examples

The Swan River everlasting adds a delicate pink hue to spring wildflower displays in Western Australia.
Gardeners favor the Swan River everlasting for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
I photographed a field full of blooming Swan River everlasting near Perth last September.
