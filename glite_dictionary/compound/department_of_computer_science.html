department of computer science

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv kəmˈpjuːtə ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
an academic department at a college or university that focuses on teaching and research in computer science
academic unit for computer science

Examples

She applied to the department of computer science to pursue her graduate studies.
The department of computer science organizes seminars on the latest technology trends every semester.
After joining the department of computer science, he began working on artificial intelligence research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faculty of computer science - another term for an academic unit specializing in computer science
