kneading board

1. NOUN

/ ˈnidɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈniːdɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a large, flat wooden board used for kneading dough
large wooden board for kneading dough

Examples

She placed the dough on the kneading board and began to work it with her hands.
After baking, he quickly cleaned the kneading board before storing it away.
A sturdy kneading board helps keep the kitchen counter clean while preparing bread.

Synset Examples

When it comes to making pizza dough, a good wooden kneading board makes a big difference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breadboard - a board on which dough is kneaded or bread is sliced
