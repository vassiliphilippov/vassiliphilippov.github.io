card sharper

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ˈʃɑrpər / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈʃɑːpə / (BrE)
a person who cheats professionally at card games to win money or valuables
professional card game cheater

Examples

The notorious card sharper was finally caught after years of cheating gamblers in underground clubs.
It's hard to spot a skilled card sharper because they are experts at sleight of hand.
He lost his fortune to a card sharper who dealt secretly from the bottom of the deck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: card shark - a person who is highly skilled at card games, sometimes implying cheating

Etymology

From 'card', referring to playing cards, and 'sharper', meaning a swindler or cheat (from 'sharp' in the sense of someone who is cunning or shrewd).
