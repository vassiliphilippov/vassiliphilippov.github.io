yellow flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ flaɡ / (BrE)
a common species of iris (Iris pseudacorus) with yellow flowers, native to Europe and North Africa and often naturalized in North America
a species of yellow-flowered iris

Examples

The pond was bordered by tall yellow flag irises in full bloom.
Gardeners often plant yellow flag to add color to wetlands and water gardens.
The yellow flag is known for thriving in marshy areas across Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iris - a genus of flowering plants with showy flowers, including yellow flag
