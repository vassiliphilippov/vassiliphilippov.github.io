brandy snifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbræn.di ˌsnɪf.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈbræn.di ˌsnɪf.tə / (BrE)
a short-stemmed, wide-bowled glass with a narrow top, used for serving brandy or similar spirits
glass for serving brandy

Examples

He gently swirled the cognac in his brandy snifter, savoring the aroma.
A crystal brandy snifter makes an elegant gift for any spirits enthusiast.
The bartender polished each brandy snifter before placing them on the shelf.
