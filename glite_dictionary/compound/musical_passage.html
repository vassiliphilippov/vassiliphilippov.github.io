musical passage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl ˈpæs.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl ˈpæs.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a short section or segment of a musical composition, especially one with distinctive character or function
short section of music

Examples

The soloist performed the difficult musical passage with remarkable precision.
That musical passage always reminds me of my childhood.
He struggled to memorize the intricate musical passage before the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passage - a section of a musical composition
