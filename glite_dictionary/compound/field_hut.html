field hut

1. NOUN

/ fiːld hʌt / (AmE), / fiːld hʌt / (BrE)
a small, temporary shelter used by military personnel in the field
temporary military shelter

Examples

The soldiers spent the night in a field hut to stay out of the rain.
Each field hut was stocked with basic supplies and bedding for the troops.
During the training exercise, they constructed a makeshift field hut from canvas and wooden poles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bivouac - a temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers
