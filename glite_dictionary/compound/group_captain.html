group captain

1. NOUN

/ ɡruːp ˈkæp.tɪn / (AmE), / ɡruːp ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
a senior commissioned officer in the Royal Air Force, equivalent in rank to a colonel in the army
RAF officer rank; colonel equivalent
british

Examples

She was promoted to group captain after twenty years of distinguished service.
The group captain addressed the pilots before the mission began.
As a group captain, he oversaw several squadrons on the base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colonel - an army officer rank equivalent to group captain
