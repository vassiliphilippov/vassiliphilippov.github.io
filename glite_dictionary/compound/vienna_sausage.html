Vienna sausage

1. NOUN

/ viˈɛnə ˈsɔsɪdʒ / (AmE), / viˈenə ˈsɒsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a short, slender sausage made from finely ground meat, usually sold precooked in cans or jars
small, slender precooked sausage

Examples

She opened a can of Vienna sausage for a quick snack.
My grandmother used to pack Vienna sausage sandwiches for our picnics in the park.
At the international market, you can find imported Vienna sausage in the canned foods aisle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frankfurter - a type of smoked sausage similar to a hot dog
  • Synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a long bun

Etymology

Named after Vienna, Austria, where sausages of this style originated.
