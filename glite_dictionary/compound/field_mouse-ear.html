field mouse-ear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˈmaʊsˌɪr / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˈmaʊsɪə / (BrE)
A densely tufted perennial chickweed found in the north temperate zone.
a tufted perennial chickweed plant

Examples

The meadows were covered with field mouse-ear, adding a soft texture to the grassland.
She identified the delicate white blooms of field mouse-ear during her botanical survey.
Conservationists noted a decline in field mouse-ear populations due to habitat changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouse-ear chickweed - another name for the plant Cerastium, closely related species
