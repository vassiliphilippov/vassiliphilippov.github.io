irregardful attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪr.ɪˈɡɑrd.fəl ˈæt̬.ə.tud / (AmE), / ˌɪr.ɪˈɡɑːd.fəl ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a lack of consideration or attention; an attitude of ignoring the needs or feelings of others
lack of consideration or attention

Examples

His irregardful attitude caused problems among his colleagues, who felt ignored and undervalued.
The manager's irregardful attitude toward feedback led to decreased team morale.
People are often frustrated by leaders who show an irregardful attitude when making important decisions.

Synset Examples

His irregardful attitude led to many mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disregard - lack of consideration or attention
