compact mirror

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpækt ˈmɪrər / (AmE), / kəmˈpakt ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
a small, portable mirror, typically in a round, folding case, used for personal grooming and often carried in a handbag or purse
small folding mirror for personal grooming

Examples

She pulled out her compact mirror to check her lipstick before the interview.
Most compact mirrors fit easily inside a purse or small bag.
His gift included a custom-engraved compact mirror for her birthday.

Synset Examples

I bought a new compact mirror today, it has an additional magnifying mirror and battery-powered LED lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand mirror - a mirror designed to be held in the hand rather than attached to a wall or stand
