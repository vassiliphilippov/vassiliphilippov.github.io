defending team

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛndɪŋ tim / (AmE), / dɪˈfendɪŋ tiːm / (BrE)
in sports, the team that is attempting to prevent the opposing team from scoring points or goals
team preventing opponent from scoring (sports)

Examples

The defending team quickly reorganized to stop the fast break.
Coach emphasized discipline and communication for the defending team during practice.
While the defending team was focused on the striker, the midfielder slipped by to score.

Synset Examples

his teams are always good on defense

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attacking team - the team attempting to score in sports
