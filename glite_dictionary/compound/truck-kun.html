Truck-kun

1. PROPN

/ trʌk kʊn / (AmE), / trʌk kʊn / (BrE)
(in anime and internet memes) a fictional or personified truck responsible for sending characters to another world, especially by running them over; often used humorously.
anime meme: truck sending characters to another world
informal

Examples

Fans joke about Truck-kun appearing whenever a new isekai anime is released.
The protagonist was sent to another world after being hit by Truck-kun.
Memes about Truck-kun continue to circulate on social media among anime fans.

Synset Examples

He joked that he might get hit by Truck-kun someday.
In the anime world, Truck-kun is notorious for isekai.
Truck-kun is often a character in many anime stories.

Etymology

From English 'truck' and Japanese honorific '-kun', a familiar suffix for addressing males, used here humorously.
