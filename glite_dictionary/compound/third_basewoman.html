third basewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝd ˈbeɪsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈθɜːd ˈbeɪsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
(in baseball) a female player who plays the position of third base.
female third-base player in baseball

Examples

The team's third basewoman made an incredible throw to first for the out.
As a young athlete, she dreamed of becoming a professional third basewoman.
Their third basewoman is known for her quick reflexes and strong arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third baseman - a player who plays third base in baseball (generically or male)

Etymology

Formed from 'third base' + 'woman', on the pattern of 'third baseman'.
