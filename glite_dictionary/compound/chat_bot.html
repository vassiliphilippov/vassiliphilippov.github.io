chat bot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃæt bɑt / (AmE), / ˈtʃæt bɒt / (BrE)
A computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the internet.
software simulating conversation

Examples

The chat bot answered my questions quickly and accurately.
Many websites now feature a chat bot to help customers with common issues.
She interacted with a chat bot that could recommend movies based on her preferences.

Synset Examples

The chat bot answered my questions quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virtual assistant - a software agent that can perform tasks or services for an individual
  • Antonym: human - a person, as distinct from a computer or robot

Etymology

From 'chat' (online conversation) + 'bot' (short for robot).
