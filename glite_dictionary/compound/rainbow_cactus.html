rainbow cactus

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌboʊ ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ ˈkaktəs / (BrE)
a stout, cylindrical cactus native to the southwestern United States and nearby Mexico, known for its colorful spines
a colorful cylindrical cactus of the American Southwest

Examples

We spotted a rainbow cactus blooming beside the desert trail.
The rainbow cactus gets its name from the vivid bands of color on its spines.
Collectors prize the rainbow cactus for its resilience and ornamental beauty.
