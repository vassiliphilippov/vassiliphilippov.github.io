work-shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːk ʃɝːt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a sturdy shirt made of durable material, intended for manual or physical work
a durable shirt for manual work

Examples

He changed into a clean work-shirt before heading out to the construction site.
Her faded work-shirt was stained with paint after hours of decorating the house.
Many ranchers prefer a thick cotton work-shirt for comfort and protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workwear - clothing designed to be worn for work, especially manual or physical labor
