sponge mop

1. NOUN

/ spʌndʒ mɑp / (AmE), / spʌndʒ mɒp / (BrE)
A cleaning tool consisting of a handle attached to an absorbent sponge, used for wet mopping floors.
cleaning tool with an absorbent sponge head

Examples

He cleaned the kitchen floor thoroughly using a sponge mop and soapy water.
The sponge mop is ideal for absorbing spills quickly on tile floors.
After rinsing, squeeze out the sponge mop before continuing to clean.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mop - a tool for cleaning floors, typically with absorbent material on a handle
