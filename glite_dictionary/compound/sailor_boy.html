sailor boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a male member of the navy, especially a young or junior one
young male naval serviceman

Examples

The old postcard showed a sailor boy waving proudly from the deck of the ship.
As a child, he dreamed of growing up to be a brave sailor boy and seeing the world.
The young sailor boy wrote letters home describing his adventures at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship, especially in the navy
  • Degree Higher: naval officer - a commissioned officer in the navy
