Kingdom of Northumbria

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv nɔrˈθʌmbriə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm ɒv nɔːˈθʌmbriə / (BrE)
a former Anglo-Saxon kingdom in northern England that existed until 876 AD
historical Anglo-Saxon kingdom in northern England

Examples

The Kingdom of Northumbria played a crucial role in early medieval British history.
Archaeologists have uncovered many artifacts from the Kingdom of Northumbria era.
During the 7th century, the Kingdom of Northumbria was known for its centers of learning.

Etymology

From Old English 'Norðhymbrar' (people north of the Humber) and 'kingdom'.
