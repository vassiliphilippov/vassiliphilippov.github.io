speech therapy

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the treatment of speech and communication disorders, usually by a trained therapist, to improve clarity and fluency of spoken language
treatment for speech disorders
medical

Examples

After suffering a stroke, he began speech therapy to recover his ability to communicate.
Children with language delays often benefit from speech therapy provided at school.
Her progress in speech therapy was impressive, and she soon spoke clearly again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speech-language pathology - study and treatment of speech and language disorders
  • Degree Higher: occupational therapy - therapy to help people develop or recover daily living skills
