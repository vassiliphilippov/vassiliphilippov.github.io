draining board

1. NOUN

/ ˈdreɪnɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a sloping flat area beside a kitchen sink, used for draining washed dishes and utensils into the sink
sloped surface beside a sink for draining dishes

Examples

She left the clean plates to dry on the draining board.
Water collected on the draining board after washing the pans.
The new kitchen has a stainless steel sink with a built-in draining board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dish rack - rack for holding dishes as they dry
