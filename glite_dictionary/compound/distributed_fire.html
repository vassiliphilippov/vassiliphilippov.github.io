distributed fire

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɪb.ju.tɪd faɪɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪb.juː.tɪd faɪə / (BrE)
a military firing tactic in which fire is spread over an area to effectively engage a target
military tactic of spreading fire across an area

Examples

The platoon used distributed fire to cover the entire advancing enemy line.
Instructors emphasized the effectiveness of distributed fire against wide area targets.
Modern artillery units often employ distributed fire in their operations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concentrated fire - military tactic where fire is focused on a specific target or location
