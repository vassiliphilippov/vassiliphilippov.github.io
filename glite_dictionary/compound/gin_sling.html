gin sling

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪn slɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒɪn slɪŋ / (BrE)
A cocktail made with gin, sweetened and typically served with lemon and sometimes soda water.
gin-based cocktail

Examples

He ordered a gin sling to cool off on the hot summer afternoon.
The bartender taught me how to mix a classic gin sling with fresh lemon and soda.
She sipped her gin sling slowly, enjoying its sweet and refreshing taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gin - a clear alcoholic spirit distilled from grain or malt and flavored with juniper berries
  • Synonym: sling - an alcoholic drink made with spirit, water, and a sweetener

Etymology

Sling, from English (meaning a drink with spirit, water, and sugar); gin from Dutch 'jenever'.
