violet-tinted

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌtɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˌtɪntɪd / (BrE)
Having a slight coloration or shade of violet.
having a hint of violet color

Examples

The sky turned a violet-tinted hue as the sun set.
She wore a violet-tinted dress that shimmered under the lights.
Through the violet-tinted glass, the room was bathed in a soft, dreamy glow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple-tinted - having a slight tint of purple
  • Synonym: violet - a bluish-purple color
