Arabian Gulf

1. PROPN

/ əˈreɪ.bi.ən ɡʌlf / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bɪ.ən ɡʌlf / (BrE)
another name for the Persian Gulf, a shallow arm of the Arabian Sea between Iran and the Arabian Peninsula, noted for its oil fields.
alternative name for the Persian Gulf

Examples

The discovery of oil in the Arabian Gulf transformed the region's economy.
Maritime boundaries in the Arabian Gulf have been a source of dispute among neighboring countries.
Many international shipping routes pass through the Arabian Gulf each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian Gulf - a shallow arm of the Arabian Sea between Iran and the Arabian Peninsula
