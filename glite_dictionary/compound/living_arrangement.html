living arrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ əˌreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ əˌreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the way in which people organize and manage their residence together, especially with regard to who lives with whom
the organization of people's shared living situation

Examples

Their living arrangement changed when his brother moved in with them.
Many college students find creative living arrangements to save on rent.
After the divorce, they had to discuss a new living arrangement for the children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cohabitation - the state of living together and having a sexual relationship without being married
