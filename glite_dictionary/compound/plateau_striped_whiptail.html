plateau striped whiptail

1. NOUN

/ pləˈtoʊ ˈstraɪpt ˈwɪptaɪl / (AmE), / plæˈtəʊ ˈstraɪpt ˈwɪptaɪl / (BrE)
a small, slender lizard with distinct longitudinal stripes, native to the Colorado Plateau region from Arizona to western Colorado
a striped lizard of the Colorado Plateau

Examples

The plateau striped whiptail is often found darting across sandy areas in Arizona and western Colorado.
Researchers tracked the movements of several plateau striped whiptails to study their behavioral patterns.
The plateau striped whiptail is known for its agility and striking longitudinal stripes.
