main man

1. NOUN

/ meɪn mæn / (AmE), / meɪn man / (BrE)
a person who is the most important or trusted in a particular situation or group
most important or trusted person
informal

Examples

He is my main man when it comes to business advice.
Everyone knows he's the main man in this project.
Whenever I need help, I call my main man for support.
