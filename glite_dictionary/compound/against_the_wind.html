against the wind

1. ADV

/ əˈɡɛnst ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
in a direction opposite to that in which the wind is blowing
moving opposite to the wind's direction

Examples

The runners struggled as they raced against the wind during the last lap.
It's harder to sail against the wind than with it.
She wrapped her coat tightly, walking against the wind across the open field.

Synset Examples

they flew upwind

Related Terms

  • Antonym: with the wind - in a direction the same as that in which the wind is blowing
