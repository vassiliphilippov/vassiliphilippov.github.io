Emerald Isle

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛmɚəld aɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛmərəld aɪl / (BrE)
a poetic name for the island of Ireland, including both the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland
poetic name for Ireland

Examples

Many tourists dream of visiting the Emerald Isle to experience its breathtaking countryside and welcoming culture.
She often reminisced about her family's roots on the Emerald Isle.
The legends and myths of the Emerald Isle have inspired countless writers and poets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ireland - the island country west of Great Britain

Etymology

Refers to Ireland's lush green landscape; 'emerald' symbolizing the green color and 'Isle' meaning island.
