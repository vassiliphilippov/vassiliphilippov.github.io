written document

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪt.ən ˈdɑk.jə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.ən ˈdɒk.jʊ.mənt / (BrE)
an official or formal piece of writing that provides information or records facts
official or informational writing

Examples

The contract is a legally binding written document that both parties must sign.
Please submit a written document summarizing your findings before the end of the week.
A passport is an official written document issued by a government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: document - a piece of written, printed, or electronic matter that provides information or evidence
