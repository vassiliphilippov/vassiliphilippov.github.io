liquid soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd soʊp / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd səʊp / (BrE)
a type of soap in liquid form, used for washing hands or body
soap in liquid form for washing

Examples

Many people prefer using liquid soap because it is more hygienic than bar soap.
The bathroom sink is always stocked with liquid soap for guests to use.
She bought a lavender-scented liquid soap for the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bar soap - soap in solid bar form
