mechanical piano

1. NOUN

/ məˈkænɪkəl piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪkəl piˈanəʊ / (BrE)
a piano that plays automatically using a roll of perforated paper to operate the keys mechanically
self-playing piano operated by paper roll

Examples

The old saloon had a mechanical piano playing ragtime tunes in the corner.
She bought a restored mechanical piano to add charm to her vintage-themed home.
A mechanical piano can fill a room with music even when no one is around to play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: player piano - a piano that plays automatically using a paper roll or similar mechanism
