college student

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪdʒ ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a person who is enrolled as a student at a college or university, especially an undergraduate
student enrolled at a college or university

Examples

After moving to Boston, she became a full-time college student at a local university.
Many college students struggle to balance their studies with part-time jobs.
The movie follows the life of a college student adjusting to campus life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undergraduate - a university or college student who has not yet received a first degree
