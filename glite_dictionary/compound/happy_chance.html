happy chance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi tʃæns / (AmE), / ˈhæpi tʃɑːns / (BrE)
an unexpected or fortuitous piece of good luck; a lucky accident
a stroke of unexpected good luck

Examples

It was a happy chance that led her to discover the hidden café.
By happy chance, the weather cleared just in time for the wedding ceremony.
A happy chance brought the two old friends together after many years apart.

Synset Examples

he finally got his big break

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good luck - chance events that are fortunate
  • Antonym: misfortune - an unfortunate condition or event
