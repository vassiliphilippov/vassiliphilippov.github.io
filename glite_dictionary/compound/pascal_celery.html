pascal celery

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæskəl ˈsɛləri / (AmE), / ˈpæskəl ˈsɛləri / (BrE)
a variety of celery with green stalks, commonly grown and sold commercially
a common green-stalked celery variety

Examples

She prefers the crisp taste of pascal celery in her salads.
Most supermarket celery in the United States is actually pascal celery.
The recipe called for chopped pascal celery to add flavor and crunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celery - a crunchy green vegetable with long stalks
