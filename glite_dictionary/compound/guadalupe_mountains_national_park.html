Guadalupe Mountains National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ ˈmaʊntənz ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑːrk / (AmE), / ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ ˈmaʊntɪnz ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in western Texas known for its rugged mountain scenery, including the highest point in Texas, and areas of desert wilderness
national park in Texas with highest peak

Examples

We spent a week hiking in Guadalupe Mountains National Park, exploring its scenic trails and vast wilderness.
The highest point in Texas can be found within Guadalupe Mountains National Park.
Many visitors are drawn to Guadalupe Mountains National Park for its unique desert flora and historic sites.
