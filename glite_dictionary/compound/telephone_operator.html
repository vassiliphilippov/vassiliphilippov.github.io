telephone operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˈɑpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtelɪfəʊn ˈɒpəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to assist telephone callers in connecting to the desired person or service
person who connects telephone calls

Examples

The telephone operator helped me reach my grandmother in another city.
In the early days of telecommunication, every call passed through a telephone operator.
She worked as a telephone operator at the hotel, connecting guests with external lines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operator - a person who operates equipment or controls a system
