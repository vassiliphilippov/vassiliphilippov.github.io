news item

1. NOUN

/ nuːz ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / njuːz ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
a single piece of news, especially as reported in a newspaper, magazine, or broadcast
individual report or story in the news

Examples

The newspaper featured a prominent news item about the recent election results on its front page.
She read every news item related to the local community to stay informed.
The editor decided to publish the news item despite its controversial nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news story - a report of recent events or information in the news
