anise cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪs ˌkʊki / (AmE), / ˈænɪs ˌkʊki / (BrE)
a cookie flavored with anise seed and usually made without butter
cookie flavored with anise seed

Examples

My grandmother baked a batch of anise cookies for the holidays every year.
The anise cookie has a delicate licorice flavor that sets it apart from other treats.
He served anise cookies with tea, delighting his guests with their unique taste.

Related Terms
