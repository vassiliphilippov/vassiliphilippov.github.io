traddy outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrædi ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrædi ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
a style of clothing characterized by traditional or vintage designs, often inspired by mid-20th-century fashion
traditional or vintage style of clothing
informal

Examples

Her traddy outfit stood out at the party.
He prefers a traddy outfit with tweed jackets and loafers when attending formal events.
Many people admire her unique style, especially when she wears a traddy outfit to vintage fairs.

Synset Examples

Her traddy outfit stood out at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preppy - classic, neat, collegiate style of clothing
  • Synonym: vintage - clothing from a previous era, especially earlier than 20 years old

Etymology

From 'traddy', a shortening of 'traditional', plus 'outfit'.
