jet-propelled plane

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛt prəˈpɛld pleɪn / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛt prəˈpɛld pleɪn / (BrE)
An airplane powered by one or more jet engines.
airplane powered by jet engines

Examples

The military showcased a new jet-propelled plane capable of reaching supersonic speeds.
Early in aviation history, the jet-propelled plane revolutionized long-distance travel.
She watched as the sleek jet-propelled plane soared into the cloudless sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet plane - an airplane powered by a jet engine
  • Antonym: propeller plane - an airplane powered by one or more propellers
