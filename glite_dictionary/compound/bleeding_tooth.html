bleeding tooth

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːdɪŋ tuːθ / (AmE), / ˈbliːdɪŋ tuːθ / (BrE)
a type of Caribbean gastropod with reddish, tooth-like projections around the shell opening
Caribbean gastropod with red projections

Examples

The bleeding tooth is often found hiding among coral reefs in the Caribbean.
The shell of the bleeding tooth is easily recognized by its distinctive red markings.
Divers were excited to spot a rare bleeding tooth during their underwater survey.
