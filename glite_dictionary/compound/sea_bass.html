sea bass

1. NOUN

/ siː ˈbæs / (AmE), / siː ˈbæs / (BrE)
any of various edible and sport fish of the Atlantic coast of the United States, having an elongated body and a long spiny dorsal fin.
type of Atlantic marine fish

Examples

The chef grilled a fresh sea bass with lemon and herbs for dinner.
Anglers come from miles around to catch sea bass during the peak season.
The Atlantic sea bass population has been recovering due to new fishing regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bass - a type of fish, especially those in the family Serranidae

2. NOUN

/ siː bæs / (AmE), / siː bæs / (BrE)
the firm, white flesh of various saltwater fish of the family Serranidae, used as food
flesh of certain saltwater fish used as food

Examples

The chef recommended grilled sea bass with lemon and herbs for dinner.
She ordered the sea bass fillet, which was served with a fresh vegetable medley.
Fresh sea bass is a highlight on the restaurant's seafood menu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bass - the flesh of various marine or freshwater fish called 'bass'
