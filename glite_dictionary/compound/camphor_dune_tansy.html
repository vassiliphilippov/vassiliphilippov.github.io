camphor dune tansy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm.fər duːn ˈtæn.zi / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fɔː duːn ˈtæn.zi / (BrE)
A densely hairy plant with rayless flowers, native to the San Francisco Bay area.
hairy plant from San Francisco Bay area

The camphor dune tansy thrives in the sandy soils of the San Francisco Bay area.
