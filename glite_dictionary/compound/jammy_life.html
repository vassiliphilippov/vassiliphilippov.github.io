jammy life

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæmi laɪf / (AmE), / ˈdʒæmi laɪf / (BrE)
a life characterized by comfort, ease, and good fortune, often with little effort
a comfortable or fortunate life
informal british

Examples

After winning the lottery, he settled into a jammy life of luxury and travel.
Many people dream of having a jammy life without stress or worry.
Her jammy life makes it seem like she never faces any real problems.

Synset Examples

She's living a jammy life with all her travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy life - a life without hardship or trouble

Etymology

British slang 'jammy' meaning lucky or fortunate, from 'jam', meaning something desirable.
