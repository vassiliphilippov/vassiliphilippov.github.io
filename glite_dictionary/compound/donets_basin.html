Donets Basin

1. PROPN

/ doʊˌnɛts ˈbeɪsɪn / (AmE), / dəʊˌnɛts ˈbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
an industrial region and coal-mining area located in eastern Ukraine and southwestern Russia, known for its major economic and historical significance
industrial region in eastern Ukraine and Russia

Examples

The Donets Basin has been a major center for coal mining since the late 19th century.
Recent conflicts have greatly affected the industrial output of the Donets Basin.
Many experts study the Donets Basin to understand the economic development of Eastern Europe.

Etymology

Named after the Donets River; 'basin' refers to the geological coal basin found in the area.
