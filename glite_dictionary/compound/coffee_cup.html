coffee cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌkʌp / (BrE)
A small cup, typically with a handle, used for drinking coffee.
a cup used for drinking coffee

Examples

She placed her coffee cup on the saucer and smiled.
After washing the dishes, I accidentally broke my favorite coffee cup.
The waiter brought a steaming coffee cup to my table early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacup - a cup used for drinking tea
  • Degree Higher: mug - a large cup, typically used for hot drinks
