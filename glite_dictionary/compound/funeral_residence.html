funeral residence

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjunərəl ˈrɛzɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl ˈrɛzɪdəns / (BrE)
A mortuary where people who knew the deceased can come to pay their last respects.
mortuary for last respects

Examples

The family gathered at the funeral residence to share memories of their loved one.
A viewing will be held at the local funeral residence tomorrow evening.
Many friends came to the funeral residence to pay their respects and offer condolences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funeral home - an establishment where the dead are prepared for burial or cremation and where a funeral may be held
