loud snorker

1. NOUN

/ laʊd ˈsnɔrkər / (AmE), / laʊd ˈsnɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who snores very loudly, especially in a way that disturbs others
person who snores loudly
informal

Examples

My husband is such a loud snorker that I can hear him from the next room.
Anyone who has shared a tent with a loud snorker knows how hard it is to sleep.
She's a loud snorker, so we always offer her the guest room furthest away.

Synset Examples

He was a loud snorker during the movie, annoying everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snorer - a person who snores

Etymology

From 'snore' (to breathe noisily during sleep), with 'snorker' formed as a humorous agent noun.
