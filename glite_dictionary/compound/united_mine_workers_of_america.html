United Mine Workers of America

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnaɪtɪd maɪn ˈwɜrkərz əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd maɪn ˈwɜːkəz əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (BrE)
a labor union representing mine workers in North America, particularly in the United States
labor union of North American mine workers

Examples

The United Mine Workers of America played a crucial role in improving miners' working conditions in the early 20th century.
Many mining communities relied on the support provided by the United Mine Workers of America.
John L. Lewis was a prominent leader of the United Mine Workers of America for several decades.
