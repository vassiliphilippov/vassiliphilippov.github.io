plow horse

1. NOUN

/ plaʊ hɔrs / (AmE), / plaʊ hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse used for pulling a plow, especially in farming
horse used for pulling a plow

Examples

The old plow horse calmly pulled the heavy plow across the field.
After many years of loyal service, the plow horse was finally retired by the farmer.
They watched as the plow horses worked side by side in the muddy soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draft horse - a large horse bred for heavy work such as pulling loads
