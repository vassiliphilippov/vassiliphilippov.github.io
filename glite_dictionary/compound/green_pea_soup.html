green pea soup

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡrin pi ˈsup / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn piː ˈsuːp / (BrE)
A soup made from fresh green peas, stock, and often shredded lettuce, onion, and celery.
soup made from green peas and vegetables

Examples

On a cool spring evening, she prepared a pot of green pea soup for her family dinner.
The restaurant's special today is homemade green pea soup served with crusty bread.
Fresh herbs give the green pea soup a vibrant flavor and color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea soup - a soup made using peas as the main ingredient
