muskus grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌskəs ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈmʌskəs ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A low annual European herb of the geranium family, naturalized in America and similar to alfilaria.
a European herb similar to alfilaria

Examples

The field was overgrown with muskus grass, making it difficult to walk through.
Gardeners in California sometimes encounter muskus grass among native plants.
Scientists studied the rapid spread of muskus grass in the western United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alfilaria - a Mediterranean herb naturalized in America, related plant
