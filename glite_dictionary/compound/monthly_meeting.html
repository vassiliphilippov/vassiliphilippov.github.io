monthly meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌnθli ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθli ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting that takes place once every month
meeting held every month

Examples

The company holds a monthly meeting to discuss workplace progress and new initiatives.
Please prepare the report before our monthly meeting next week.
During the monthly meeting, team leaders share their updates with the rest of the staff.

Synset Examples

We have our monthly meeting next Wednesday.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: annual meeting - a meeting held once a year
