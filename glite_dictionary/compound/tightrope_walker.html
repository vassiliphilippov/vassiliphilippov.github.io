tightrope walker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪtˌroʊp ˈwɔːkər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪt.rəʊp ˈwɔː.kə / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs feats or routines while balancing on a tightrope or slack rope, usually high above the ground
person performing acrobatics on a tightrope

Examples

The tightrope walker captivated the audience with her daring stunts above the circus ring.
During the parade, a tightrope walker balanced carefully between two tall buildings.
Becoming a skilled tightrope walker takes years of practice and intense concentration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acrobat - a performer skilled in feats of agility and balance
  • Degree Lower: tightrope - a tightly stretched rope or wire on which acrobats perform
