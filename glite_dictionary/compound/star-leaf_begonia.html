star-leaf begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑr ˌlif bɪˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈstɑː ˌliːf bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
A type of begonia with rhizomes, leaves shaped with pointed, star-like lobes, and pink flowers.
type of begonia with star-shaped leaves

Examples

The star-leaf begonia is popular among plant enthusiasts for its striking foliage.
She displayed her collection of star-leaf begonias by the window to catch the morning sun.
Botanists admire the unique patterns on the leaves of the star-leaf begonia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants with brightly colored flowers and ornamental leaves
