heart monitor

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈmɑnɪtɚ / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈmɒnɪtə / (BrE)
a piece of electronic equipment used for continuous observation and measurement of a person's heart activity, especially in medical settings
device for monitoring heart activity
medical

Examples

The nurse checked the patient's heart monitor for any irregularities in their heartbeat.
After surgery, she was connected to a heart monitor to ensure there were no complications.
Portable heart monitors allow patients to record their heart activity while at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ECG monitor - device that measures the electrical activity of the heart
  • Degree Higher: defibrillator - device used to restore normal heartbeat by sending electric pulses to the heart
