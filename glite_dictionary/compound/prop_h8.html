Prop H8

1. NOUN

/ prɑp eɪtʃ eɪt / (AmE), / prɒp eɪtʃ eɪt / (BrE)
a derogatory nickname for a California ballot proposition (Proposition 8, 2008) that opposed same-sex marriage
nickname for anti-gay marriage California ballot measure
informal american

Examples

Many activists gathered to protest Prop H8 outside the court building.
The slogan 'No on Prop H8' appeared on signs across the country.
Supporters of marriage equality frequently spoke out against Prop H8 in public forums.

Synset Examples

We rallied for Prop H8 at the city hall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Proposition 8 - California ballot measure banning same-sex marriage (2008)
  • Antonym: marriage equality - the state of being able to marry regardless of gender or sexuality

Etymology

A play on 'Proposition 8' and 'hate,' using '8' as 'ate/hate'.
