November 5

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈvɛmbɚ fɪfθ / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛmbə fɪfθ / (BrE)
The anniversary of the discovery of the Gunpowder Plot in 1605, marked in Britain with bonfires and fireworks, also known as Guy Fawkes Night.
anniversary of Gunpowder Plot discovery (Britain)
british

Examples

Every year on November 5, people in Britain celebrate with fireworks and bonfires.
Schools often teach children about the history behind November 5 and the Gunpowder Plot.
My family enjoys watching the fireworks display on November 5 every autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Guy Fawkes Night - British celebration marking the defeat of the Gunpowder Plot
  • Synonym: Bonfire Night - Another name for Guy Fawkes Night in Britain

Etymology

November from Latin 'novem' (nine), as the ninth month of the Roman calendar; '5' for the date. The event commemorates the thwarted Gunpowder Plot of 1605.
