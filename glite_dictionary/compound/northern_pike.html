northern pike

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn paɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən paɪk / (BrE)
a large, predatory freshwater fish of northern regions, known for its elongated body and voracious appetite, scientifically named Esox lucius
a large predatory freshwater fish

Examples

The northern pike is prized by anglers for its size and fighting spirit.
We saw a massive northern pike lurking in the shallow reeds of the lake.
A mature northern pike can eat fish nearly half its own length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pike - any large predatory freshwater fish of the genus Esox
