perennial salt marsh aster

1. NOUN

/ pəˈrɛniəl sɔlt mɑrʃ ˈæstər / (AmE), / pəˈrɛniəl sɒlt mɑːʃ ˈæstə / (BrE)
a perennial species of flowering plant (Symphyotrichum tenuifolium) found in salt marshes, belonging to the aster family.
a perennial salt marsh plant of the aster family

Examples

The perennial salt marsh aster thrives in brackish coastal wetlands through the summer.
Researchers studied the perennial salt marsh aster to examine its tolerance to saline environments.
She photographed a cluster of perennial salt marsh asters blooming along the tidal creek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the genus Aster, with star-shaped flowers
