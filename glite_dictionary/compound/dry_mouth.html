dry mouth

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
Abnormal dryness of the mouth caused by reduced secretion of saliva.
abnormally dry condition of the mouth
medical

Examples

Many medications can cause dry mouth as a side effect.
He complained of a persistent dry mouth during the examination.
Drinking water throughout the day can help relieve dry mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xerostomia - medical term for abnormal dryness of the mouth
