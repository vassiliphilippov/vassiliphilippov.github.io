blob of paint

1. NOUN

/ blɑb əv peɪnt / (AmE), / blɒb ɒv peɪnt / (BrE)
a small, rounded mass or lump of paint
small lump of paint

Examples

A blob of paint landed on her shirt while she was working.
Kids love to add a blob of paint to their art projects.
He accidentally dripped a blob of paint onto the floor.

Synset Examples

The blob of paint spilled across the canvas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop of paint - a small, round amount of paint
  • Synonym: smear of paint - a spread or streak of paint
