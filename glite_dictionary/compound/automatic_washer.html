automatic washer

1. NOUN

/ əˌtɑː.mə.tɪk ˈwɑː.ʃɚ / (AmE), / ɔːˌtɒ.mə.tɪk ˈwɒʃ.ər / (BrE)
a machine used in the home for washing clothes and linens automatically, without manual effort
home appliance for washing laundry automatically

Examples

We decided to replace our old dryer and automatic washer with more efficient models.
The automatic washer finished its cycle, and she transferred the clothes to the dryer.
Many apartments are equipped with an automatic washer and dryer for residents' convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washing machine - a machine for washing clothes automatically
