put differently

1. ADV

/ pʊt ˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt.li / (AmE), / pʊt ˈdɪf.ər.ənt.li / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that expresses the same idea in a different way
expressed in another way

Examples

Put differently, the new plan will cost less but take longer to implement.
Many people lost their jobs during the crisis; put differently, unemployment soared.
Put differently, what you are suggesting is not feasible within our current budget.

Synset Examples

in other words, we are broke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in other words - used to introduce a different way of expressing something
  • Synonym: to put it another way - used for saying something in a different way
