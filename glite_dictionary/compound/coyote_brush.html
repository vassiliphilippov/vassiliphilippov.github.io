coyote brush

1. NOUN

/ kaɪˈoʊti brʌʃ / (AmE), / kaɪˈəʊti brʌʃ / (BrE)
A widely spreading evergreen shrub of the southwestern United States, with small flower heads arranged in a leafy panicle.
evergreen shrub of southwestern US

Examples

The hillsides were covered in coyote brush after the recent rains.
Wildlife often take shelter under the dense foliage of coyote brush.
Many gardeners plant coyote brush for erosion control and native landscaping.
