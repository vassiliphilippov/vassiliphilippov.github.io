Mozambique Channel

1. PROPN

/ moʊˌzæmbiːk ˈtʃæn.əl / (AmE), / məʊˌzæmˈbiːk ˈtʃæn.əl / (BrE)
a channel of the Indian Ocean between Madagascar and the southeastern coast of Africa
channel between Madagascar and Africa

Examples

Cargo ships frequently navigate the Mozambique Channel on their way to ports in East Africa.
The Mozambique Channel is known for its rich marine biodiversity and warm ocean currents.
During the cyclone season, storms often pass through the Mozambique Channel between Madagascar and the mainland.
