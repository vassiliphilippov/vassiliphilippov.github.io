noonday prayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊn.deɪ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈnəʊn.deɪ ˈpreə / (BrE)
a prayer traditionally recited at noon, often as part of a daily religious practice
prayer recited at noon

Examples

Every day at work, she pauses for noonday prayer in her office.
The group gathered in the chapel for noonday prayer during the retreat.
The tradition of noonday prayer offers a peaceful moment to reconnect with one's faith.

Synset Examples

Noonday prayers are a tradition in her culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midday prayer - a prayer recited at midday as part of religious observance
