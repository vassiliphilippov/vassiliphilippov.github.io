short-spurred fragrant orchid

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt spɝd ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ʃɔːt spɜːd ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A species of wild orchid similar to the fragrant orchid (Gymnadenia conopsea), but with smaller flowers, shorter stems, and much shorter spurs.
a species of wild orchid with short spurs

Examples

Many botany enthusiasts search fields in June hoping to spot the short-spurred fragrant orchid.
The short-spurred fragrant orchid is less common than other similar species in the region.
She photographed a rare cluster of short-spurred fragrant orchids near the edge of the meadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant orchid - an orchid species known for its sweet scent (Gymnadenia conopsea)
