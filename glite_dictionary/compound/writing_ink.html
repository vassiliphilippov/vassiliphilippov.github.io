writing ink

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ɪŋk / (BrE)
Ink specially formulated for use in writing pens, typically permanent or washable.
ink used in writing pens

Examples

The calligrapher uses only the finest writing ink for her creations.
Many students prefer washable writing ink in case of mistakes.
He accidentally spilled blue writing ink on his shirt while taking notes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ink - colored liquid used for writing, printing, or drawing
