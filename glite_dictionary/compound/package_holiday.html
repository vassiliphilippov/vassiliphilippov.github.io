package holiday

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ ˈhɑlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ ˈhɒlɪdeɪ / (BrE)
a holiday organized by a travel company that includes the cost of travel, accommodation, and sometimes meals and activities, all for an inclusive price
holiday with inclusive travel and accommodation

Examples

This year, they booked a package holiday to Spain with flights and hotel included.
A package holiday can save travelers time and money compared to planning every detail independently.
Many families choose a package holiday for the simplicity and convenience it offers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all-inclusive - including everything in the price, especially meals and facilities
