red underwing

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈʌndərˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / rɛd ˈʌndəˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
a moth with dull forewings and striking red-marked hind wings, especially of the genus Catocala.
a moth with red-marked hind wings

Examples

The red underwing is often spotted resting on tree trunks, blending in until disturbed.
She photographed a beautiful red underwing in her garden last summer.
Naturalists admire the vivid colors displayed when a red underwing opens its wings.
