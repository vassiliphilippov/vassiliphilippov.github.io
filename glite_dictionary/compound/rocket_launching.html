rocket launching

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkɪt ˌlɔntʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌlɔːntʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of sending a rocket or missile into the air under its own power
the act of sending a rocket into the air

Examples

The rocket launching was visible for miles as the spacecraft soared into the sky.
Engineers monitored every detail leading up to the rocket launching last night.
Crowds gathered at the coast to witness the rocket launching firsthand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rocket launch - the act or instance of launching a rocket
