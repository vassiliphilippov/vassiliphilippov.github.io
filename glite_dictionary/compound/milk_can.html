milk can

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk kæn / (AmE), / mɪlk kan / (BrE)
a large metal container with a lid, used for storing and transporting milk
large metal container for transporting milk

Examples

The old farmer still used a milk can to collect fresh milk every morning.
Several rusty milk cans were displayed outside the dairy museum.
He lifted the heavy milk can onto the back of the pickup truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk churn - a similar large container for transporting milk, especially in British English
