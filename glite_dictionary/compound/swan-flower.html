swan-flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑn ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈswɒn ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
Any of several orchids of the genus Cycnoches with slender, arching columns of flowers resembling the neck of a swan.
orchid of the genus Cycnoches

Examples

The swan-flower is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant, swan-like blooms.
He added a rare swan-flower to his botanical collection.
Botanists studied the unique pollination methods of the swan-flower.

Etymology

From 'swan' (the bird) and 'flower', referring to the flower's resemblance to a swan's neck.
