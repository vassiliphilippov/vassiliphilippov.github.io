dragon's mouth

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræɡənz maʊθ / (AmE), / ˈdræɡənz maʊθ / (BrE)
a bog orchid (Arethusa bulbosa) native to North America, usually with a single fragrant magenta-pink flower and a wide gaping corolla
bog orchid with showy magenta-pink flower

Examples

A single dragon's mouth stood out amid the green moss of the wetland.
The vibrant blossom of the dragon's mouth orchid is easy to spot in the boggy meadow.
Botanists visited Canada to observe the rare dragon's mouth in its natural habitat.
