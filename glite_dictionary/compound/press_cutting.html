press cutting

1. NOUN

/ prɛs ˈkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / prɛs ˈkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
an article or piece cut out from a newspaper or magazine, typically kept for reference or as a record
article cut from newspaper or magazine
british

Examples

She kept a press cutting of her first published interview in her scrapbook.
The journalist brought a press cutting to support his claims during the meeting.
He found a press cutting about the historic event among his grandfather's belongings.

Synset Examples

he searched through piles of letters and clippings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clipping - an article cut from a newspaper or magazine
