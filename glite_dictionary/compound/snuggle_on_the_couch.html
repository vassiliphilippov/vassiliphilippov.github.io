to snuggle on the couch

1. VERB

/ ˈsnʌɡəl ɑn ðə kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡl ɒn ðə kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
to cuddle or lie close together for warmth or affection while sitting or lying on a couch
cuddle or lie close together on a couch

Examples

On cold winter nights, we love to snuggle on the couch and watch movies together.
The children would often snuggle on the couch while reading their favorite storybooks.
After a long day, she likes to snuggle on the couch with her dog and relax.

Synset Examples

Her pug loves to snuggle on the couch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuddle - to hold someone or something close in your arms, especially for comfort or affection
