pizza parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtsə ˌpɑːrlər / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
A restaurant or shop where pizzas are made and sold.
shop or restaurant selling pizza

Examples

We decided to meet at the new pizza parlor downtown after work.
The pizza parlor offers a wide variety of toppings and fresh dough every day.
Many families enjoy celebrating birthdays at their favorite pizza parlor.
