northern harrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈhɛriər / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈhæriə / (BrE)
A medium-sized bird of prey found in North America and Europe, known for hunting over open land and marshes.
a bird of prey found in North America and Europe

Examples

We spotted a northern harrier gliding low over the marsh in search of prey.
The northern harrier builds its nest on the ground in open fields and wetlands.
During migration season, several northern harriers can be seen soaring above the grasslands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hen harrier - a similar harrier species mainly found in Europe and Asia
