iron mold

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn ˌmoʊld / (AmE), / ˈaɪən məʊld / (BrE)
A stain or spot caused by rust or sometimes ink, especially on fabric or paper.
stain caused by rust or ink

Examples

The old tablecloth was ruined by an unsightly iron mold near the corner.
She tried several remedies to remove the iron mold from the pages of the antique book.
Iron mold spots can be very stubborn and often require professional cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stain - a mark or discoloration left on a material
