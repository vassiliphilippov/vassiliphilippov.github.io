nancy attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈnænsi ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈnænsi ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An overly effeminate or weak manner or demeanor, often used disparagingly.
overly effeminate or weak manner
informal slang

Examples

Some critics accused the performer of having a nancy attitude on stage.
His father disapproved of what he called a nancy attitude in his son.
The coach warned the players not to show any sign of a nancy attitude during the game.

Synset Examples

He found the nancy attitude of the group off-putting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • Antonym: masculine demeanor - having or showing qualities traditionally associated with men

Etymology

From 'nancy' (slang for an effeminate man) + 'attitude'. 'Nancy' is a diminutive of the feminine name 'Ann' or 'Anne'.
