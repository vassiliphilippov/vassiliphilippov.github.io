one-fourth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
One of four equal parts; a quarter.
a quarter; one of four equal parts

Examples

She ate one-fourth of the apple and saved the rest for later.
One-fourth of the students failed to submit their assignments on time.
The recipe calls for one-fourth cup of sugar.
