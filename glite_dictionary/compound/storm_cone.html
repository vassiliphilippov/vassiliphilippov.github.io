storm cone

1. NOUN

/ stɔrm koʊn / (AmE), / stɔːm kəʊn / (BrE)
a canvas cone hoisted as a warning signal to indicate approaching high winds, especially at sea or in coastal areas
canvas warning signal for high winds

Examples

The harbor master ordered the storm cone to be raised before the gale arrived.
Sailors quickly recognized the meaning of the storm cone fluttering atop the signal mast.
A red storm cone was visible from the docks, warning all vessels of dangerous winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storm warning - an official signal warning of an approaching storm
