appetite suppressant

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəˌtaɪt səˈprɛsənt / (AmE), / ˈæpɪtaɪt səˈprɛsənt / (BrE)
a drug or substance that reduces or suppresses the desire to eat
drug that reduces appetite

Examples

Doctors may prescribe an appetite suppressant to help patients trying to lose weight.
Some over-the-counter appetite suppressants claim to reduce hunger and boost energy.
She started taking an appetite suppressant as part of her weight loss program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anorectic - a drug that causes loss of appetite
