class Myxomycetes

1. NOUN

/ klæs ˌmɪksoʊˈmaɪəsiːts / (AmE), / klɑːs ˌmɪksəʊˈmɪəsɪːts / (BrE)
a class of organisms known as true slime molds, belonging to the division Myxomycota
a class of true slime molds

Examples

The class Myxomycetes is studied for its unique lifecycle and movement.
Scientists have discovered new species within the class Myxomycetes in tropical forests.
Slime molds in the class Myxomycetes can form large, visible colonies during certain stages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slime mold - a simple organism with a slime-like stage in its life cycle
  • Synonym: division Myxomycota - the broader division to which true slime molds belong

Etymology

From Greek myxa (slime, mucus) + mycetes (fungus).
