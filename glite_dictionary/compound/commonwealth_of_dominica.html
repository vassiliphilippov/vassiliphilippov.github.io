Commonwealth of Dominica

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑː.mənˌwɛlθ əv dəˈmɪn.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˌkɒ.mənˌwɛlθ əv dəˈmɪn.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
The official name of Dominica, a country and island nation in the eastern Caribbean Sea.
country in the eastern Caribbean Sea

Examples

The Commonwealth of Dominica gained its independence from Britain in 1978.
Tourism is a major industry in the Commonwealth of Dominica due to its natural beauty and rainforests.
The prime minister of the Commonwealth of Dominica addressed the United Nations assembly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dominica - the island country in the eastern Caribbean Sea

Etymology

Commonwealth: from common + wealth, originally meaning a political community; Dominica: from Latin 'dies Dominica' (Sunday), the day of its discovery.
