men's store

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛnz stɔr / (AmE), / ˈmenz stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or department that specializes in selling men's clothing and accessories.
shop for men's clothing

Examples

I found a great suit at the new men's store downtown.
He always shops at the same men's store for his business attire.
Many men's stores offer free tailoring with your purchase.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: women's store - a shop specializing in women's clothing and accessories
  • Synonym: clothing store - a shop selling clothes for men, women, or children
