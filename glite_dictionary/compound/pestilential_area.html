pestilential area

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl ˈeəriə / (BrE)
An area that is dangerous to health because of the presence of disease or harmful conditions.
place harmful to health

Examples

Local authorities declared the market a pestilential area after an outbreak of disease was traced there.
Residents were warned to stay away from the pestilential area near the stagnant water.
Medical teams worked to sanitize the pestilential area before allowing people to return.

Synset Examples

They avoided the pestilential area due to health concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contaminated area - an area infected or polluted with harmful substances or organisms

Etymology

From Latin pestilentialis, meaning 'pertaining to plague or pestilence'; combined with 'area'.
