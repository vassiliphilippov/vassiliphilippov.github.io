great bowerbird

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡreɪt ˈbaʊ.ər.bɜrd / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈbaʊ.ə.bɜːd / (BrE)
a large species of bowerbird native to northern Australia, known for building elaborate structures to attract mates
large Australian bowerbird species

Examples

The great bowerbird constructs intricate bowers decorated with shells and stones to impress potential mates.
We spotted a great bowerbird near the river during our trip to northern Australia.
Unlike other species, the great bowerbird often uses shiny objects to decorate its bower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowerbird - bird known for building elaborate bowers to attract mates
