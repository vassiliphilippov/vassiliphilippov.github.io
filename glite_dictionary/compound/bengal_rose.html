Bengal rose

1. NOUN

/ bɛnˈɡɔl roʊz / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡɔːl rəʊz / (BrE)
a shrubby species of rose native to China, Rosa chinensis, which is an ancestor of many cultivated garden roses
a species of Chinese rose plant

Examples

The Bengal rose is prized for its continuous blooming throughout the year.
Many modern garden roses have been bred from the Bengal rose.
Botanists study the Bengal rose to understand the genetics of cultivated roses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: China rose - alternative name for the Bengal rose plant, Rosa chinensis
  • Degree Lower: rose - a prickly bush or shrub that typically bears red, pink, yellow, or white fragrant flowers
