Atlantic bottlenose dolphin

1. NOUN

/ əˈtlæn.tɪk ˌbɑ.tl̩ˌnoʊs ˈdɑl.fɪn / (AmE), / ətˈlæn.tɪk ˌbɒt.l̩ˌnəʊs ˈdɒl.fɪn / (BrE)
A common species of dolphin found in the northern Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean, frequently kept and trained in captivity.
a common dolphin species of the Atlantic and Mediterranean

Examples

Many aquariums feature Atlantic bottlenose dolphins in their shows and exhibits.
The Atlantic bottlenose dolphin is known for its playful nature and high intelligence.
Researchers are studying the social behaviors of wild Atlantic bottlenose dolphins off the coast of Florida.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottlenose dolphin - a dolphin species known for its short beak and curved mouth
