Republic of Armenia

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɑrˈmiːniə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ɑːˈmiːniə / (BrE)
a country in the South Caucasus region of Eurasia, landlocked and situated between Europe and Asia; commonly known as Armenia
country in the South Caucasus (Eurasia)

Examples

The Republic of Armenia declared its independence from the Soviet Union in 1991.
Tourists are drawn to the Republic of Armenia for its ancient monasteries and scenic landscapes.
The capital of the Republic of Armenia is Yerevan, one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Armenian - relating to Armenia, its people, or language
