field hospital

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌhɑːspɪtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌhɒspɪtl̩ / (BrE)
a temporary hospital set up near a battlefield or disaster area, used to provide emergency medical care
temporary hospital near battlefield

Examples

The army erected a field hospital to treat wounded soldiers near the front lines.
After the earthquake, a field hospital was established to help the injured.
Doctors worked tirelessly inside the field hospital during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mobile hospital - a hospital that can be moved easily and set up quickly, similar to a field hospital
