water travel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌtrævəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌtrævəl / (BrE)
the act or process of traveling by water, especially by boat, ship, or other watercraft.
traveling by water

Examples

Many explorers relied on water travel to reach new continents in the past.
The brochure highlights the benefits of water travel along the scenic river routes.
Advancements in technology have made water travel safer and more comfortable than ever before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea travel - traveling by sea
  • Antonym: air travel - traveling by aircraft
  • Antonym: land travel - traveling by land
