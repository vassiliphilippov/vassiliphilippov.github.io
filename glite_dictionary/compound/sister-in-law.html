sister-in-law

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstər ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / ˈsɪstə ɪn lɔː / (BrE)

sister of spouse

Examples

My sister-in-law lives just a few blocks away from us.
During the holidays, my sister-in-law always brings her famous chocolate cake.
He introduced me to his sister-in-law at the family reunion.
