silver vixen

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪl.vɚ ˈvɪk.sən / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.və ˈvɪk.sən / (BrE)
An attractive older woman, especially one with grey or silver hair and confident style.
attractive older woman, often with grey hair

Examples

Her elegance and wit marked her as the silver vixen at every social event.
The town's most famous silver vixen was known for her glamorous style.
Many admired the silver vixen for embracing her natural hair and aging gracefully.

Synset Examples

Her style and confidence made her the silver vixen of the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cougar - an older woman seeking a relationship with a younger man

Etymology

Formed from 'silver', referring to hair color, and 'vixen', meaning a spirited or attractive woman.
