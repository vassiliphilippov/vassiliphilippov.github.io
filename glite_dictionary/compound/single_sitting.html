single sitting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a single uninterrupted period spent doing something, especially reading, watching, or eating
one uninterrupted session

Examples

He finished the novel in a single sitting because he was so engrossed.
It's rare for me to watch an entire movie in a single sitting these days.
They ate the whole cake in a single sitting, which surprised everyone.

Synset Examples

She could devour a whole book in a single sitting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stretch - an uninterrupted period of time spent doing something
