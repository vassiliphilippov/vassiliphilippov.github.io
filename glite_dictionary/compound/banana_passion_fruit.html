banana passion fruit

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˈpæʃən ˌfrut / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˈpæʃən ˌfruːt / (BrE)
a tropical fruit with an elongated shape and soft, sweet pulp, produced by several species of Passiflora, cultivated for food and juice.
an elongated tropical fruit of several Passiflora species

Examples

I picked a ripe banana passion fruit from the vine in the greenhouse.
The dessert was topped with slices of banana passion fruit and fresh cream.
Many people enjoy banana passion fruit juice because of its unique flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion fruit - the round or oval fruit of the passionflower, with a sweet aromatic pulp
