Republic of Zimbabwe

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv zɪmˈbɑːbweɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv zɪmˈbɑːbweɪ / (BrE)
The official name for Zimbabwe, a landlocked country in south central Africa that became independent from the United Kingdom in 1980.
country in south central Africa

Examples

The Republic of Zimbabwe became independent in 1980 after a long struggle for liberation.
Tourists are attracted to the natural beauty found in the Republic of Zimbabwe.
The president addressed the citizens of the Republic of Zimbabwe in a televised speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Zimbabwe - country in southern Africa

Etymology

From the Shona 'Zimbabwe,' meaning 'house of stone'; 'Republic' from Latin 'res publica' (public affair).
