Beijing bikini

1. NOUN

/ ˌbeɪˈʒɪŋ bəˈkini / (AmE), / ˌbeɪˈdʒɪŋ bɪˈkiːni / (BrE)
(slang) The practice, especially in China, of men rolling up their shirts to expose their stomachs in hot weather.
slang for shirt rolled to expose stomach
slang

Examples

During the hot summer months, many men adopt the Beijing bikini to cool off.
Some cities have tried to ban the Beijing bikini on public transport.
The sight of the Beijing bikini is common in parks on humid afternoons.

Synset Examples

I bought a colorful Beijing bikini for my vacation.
She wore a Beijing bikini while lounging by the pool.
The fashion show featured models in stunning Beijing bikinis.

Etymology

From 'Beijing' (the capital of China) and 'bikini', used humorously to describe the exposed midriff.
