Prince of Wales heath

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɪns əv ˈweɪlz hiːθ / (AmE), / ˌprɪns əv ˈweɪlz hiːθ / (BrE)
a South African shrub (scientific name Erica canaliculata) cultivated for its abundant white flowers
a South African shrub with abundant white flowers

Examples

The Prince of Wales heath is prized by gardeners for its delicate white blossoms.
She planted a Prince of Wales heath near the garden path to add color in the spring.
In full bloom, the Prince of Wales heath attracts a variety of bees and butterflies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heath - a shrubland habitat or the plants that grow there, especially species of Erica
