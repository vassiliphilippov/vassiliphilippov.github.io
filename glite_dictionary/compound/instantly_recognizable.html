instantly recognizable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪnstəntli ˌrɛkəgˈnaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəntli ˌrɛkəɡˈnaɪzəbl / (BrE)
immediately and easily able to be identified or known without confusion.
immediately easy to identify

Examples

The brand's logo is instantly recognizable around the world.
His instantly recognizable voice made the radio broadcast unforgettable.
The city skyline is instantly recognizable in photographs.

Synset Examples

Her outfit was iconic, instantly recognizable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iconic - widely recognized and well-established
  • Antonym: indistinguishable - not able to be identified as different or distinct
