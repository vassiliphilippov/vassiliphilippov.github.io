screen time

1. NOUN

/ skrin taɪm / (AmE), / skriːn taɪm / (BrE)
The amount of time a person spends using devices with screens, such as televisions, computers, or smartphones.
time spent using electronic screens

Examples

Parents often limit their children's screen time to encourage outdoor play.
Many workers experience eye strain due to excessive screen time at their jobs.
Reducing my screen time before bed helps me sleep better.

Synset Examples

Too much screen time resulted in a headache.
