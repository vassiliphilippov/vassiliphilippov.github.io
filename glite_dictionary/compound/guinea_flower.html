guinea flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪni ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
any of several Australasian evergreen vines cultivated for their large, bright yellow single flowers
Australasian flowering vine with yellow flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece was a beautiful guinea flower with its vibrant yellow blooms.
Many landscapers choose guinea flower because it thrives in warm, sunny climates.
She planted several guinea flowers along the fence to add color and texture to the yard.
