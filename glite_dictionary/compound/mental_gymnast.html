mental gymnast

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl ˈdʒɪm.næst / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl ˈdʒɪm.næst / (BrE)
A person who is highly skilled at thinking quickly and solving complex mental challenges.
person adept at mental challenges

Examples

As a mental gymnast, she thrives on challenging puzzles and riddles.
The debate team needed a mental gymnast to think on their feet during competitions.
His reputation as a mental gymnast made him a favorite contestant on quiz shows.

Synset Examples

She’s a mental gymnast, solving puzzles in minutes.
Being a mental gymnast helps her in competitive games.
His ability to think quickly makes him a mental gymnast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: problem solver - a person who finds solutions to problems
  • Synonym: brainiac - an exceptionally intelligent person; someone good at tasks requiring intelligence

Etymology

By analogy with 'gymnast' (from Greek 'gumnastēs', an athlete), referring to mental agility rather than physical.
