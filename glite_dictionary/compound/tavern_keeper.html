tavern keeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtævərn ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈtævən ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
A person who owns or manages a tavern or public house.
owner or manager of a tavern

Examples

The tavern keeper welcomed travelers with a warm meal and a mug of ale.
Stories of the old tavern keeper were still told in the village long after he retired.
As a tavern keeper, she knew most of the town's secrets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innkeeper - a person who owns or manages an inn
  • Degree Lower: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
