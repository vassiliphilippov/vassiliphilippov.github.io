antisocial personality disorder

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈsoʊ.ʃəl pɚˌsɑː.nəˈlæ.t̬i dɪˌsɔːr.dɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsəʊ.ʃəl pɜːˌsɒn.ˈæl.ɪ.ti dɪˌsɔː.də / (BrE)
A mental disorder characterized by disregard for the rights of others, lack of empathy or guilt, and persistent patterns of antisocial or impulsive behavior.
mental disorder marked by disregard for others
medical

Examples

The doctor determined that the patient showed clear signs of antisocial personality disorder.
Many people with antisocial personality disorder may have a history of criminal behavior.
He struggled to maintain relationships due to his antisocial personality disorder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychopathy - chronic mental disorder with abnormal or violent social behavior
  • Synonym: sociopathy - mental disorder with antisocial attitudes and behavior
