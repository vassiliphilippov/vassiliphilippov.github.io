animal group

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ɡruːp / (BrE)
a number of animals considered or classified together as a unit, especially as a biological or behavioral grouping
collection or unit of animals

Examples

The researchers observed the behavior of the animal group in the wild.
A new animal group was introduced to the reserve to promote diversity.
Each animal group responded differently to the changes in their environment.
