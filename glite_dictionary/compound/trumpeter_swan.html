trumpeter swan

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpətər swɑn / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtə swɒn / (BrE)
a large, pure white wild swan native to western North America, known for its loud, resonant call
large North American wild swan

Examples

The trumpeter swan is admired for its grace and its distinctive, trumpet-like call.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of the trumpeter swan in recent years.
We spotted a flock of trumpeter swans gliding across the lake at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swan - large waterbird with a long neck
