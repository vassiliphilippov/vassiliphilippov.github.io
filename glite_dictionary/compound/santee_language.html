Santee language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.ti ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsæn.ti ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Siouan language traditionally spoken by the Santee people, a subgroup of the Dakota Sioux Native Americans.
language of the Santee Dakota Sioux

Examples

The Santee language has been passed down through generations among the Dakota Sioux people.
Scholars are working to document the Santee language before it becomes endangered.
He is fluent in both English and the Santee language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dakota language - the Siouan language of the broader Dakota Sioux peoples

Etymology

From Santee, the name of a subgroup of the Dakota Sioux, and 'language'.
