shitnugget move

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌnʌɡɪt muːv / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtnʌɡɪt muːv / (BrE)
A foolish, reckless, or extremely unwise action.
foolish or reckless action (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

Pulling out in front of a speeding car was a total shitnugget move.
Forgetting his passport before the flight was the biggest shitnugget move he ever made.
"Yeah, that was a shitnugget move," she admitted after ruining the surprise party.

Synset Examples

He made a shitnugget move by ignoring everyone's advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a stupid or careless mistake
  • Antonym: masterstroke - an extremely skillful move or action
