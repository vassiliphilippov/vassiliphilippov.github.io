supply closet

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪ ˈklɑːzət / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ˈklɒzɪt / (BrE)
a closet or small room used for storing supplies, such as office materials, cleaning products, or other necessities
closet for storing supplies

Examples

The janitor unlocked the supply closet to restock the paper towels.
Employees must return items to the supply closet after using them.
The office manager keeps the supply closet organized and well-stocked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storage closet - a closet used for storing various items
