suboccipital area

1. NOUN

/ sʌbˌɑksɪˈpɪtl ˈɛriə / (AmE), / sʌbˌɒksɪˈpɪtl ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the region located beneath the occipital bone at the base of the skull
area beneath the occipital bone
medical

Examples

The patient reported stiffness in his suboccipital area after the accident.
A massage therapist applied gentle pressure to the client's suboccipital area to relieve tension.
Swelling in the suboccipital area can sometimes indicate an underlying neurological issue.

Synset Examples

He described pain in his suboccipital area to the physician.
The doctor examined the suboccipital region for tension.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occipital region - the region of the head near the occipital bone

Etymology

From Latin 'sub' (under, below) + 'occipital' (from 'occiput,' back of the head) + 'area' (region).
