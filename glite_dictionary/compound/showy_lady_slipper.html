showy lady slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈleɪ.di ˌslɪp.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈleɪ.di ˌslɪp.ə / (BrE)
a pale pink wild orchid of northeastern North America, distinguished by an inflated, pouch-like lip
pink wild orchid of northeastern North America

Examples

The showy lady slipper thrives in wet meadows and boggy forests across northeastern America.
Photographers travel every spring to capture the showy lady slipper in full bloom.
Conservationists are working to protect the wetlands where the showy lady slipper grows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady slipper - any orchid of the genus Cypripedium, with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch
