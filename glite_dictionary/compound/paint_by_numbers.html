paint by numbers

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbɚz / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbəz / (BrE)
A method of making a picture by filling in numbered areas with specific colors according to a key, often as a kit for beginners or as a simple, guided art activity.
art method using numbered color sections

Examples

The children spent hours enjoying their paint by numbers kits during the holiday.
She found the paint by numbers approach relaxing and satisfying after a long day at work.
Critics described the movie as a paint by numbers thriller, following a predictable formula.
