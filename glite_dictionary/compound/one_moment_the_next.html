one moment... the next

1. X

/ wʌn ˈmoʊmənt ðə nɛkst / (AmE), / wʌn ˈməʊmənt ðə nɛkst / (BrE)
used to describe two contrasting situations, actions, or feelings that happen in very close succession, suggesting rapid change
used for rapid, contrasting changes

Examples

One moment... the next, the weather changed from sunny to a heavy downpour.
He can be laughing one moment... the next in tears, his moods change so quickly.
One moment... the next, she was confident, then suddenly full of doubt.

Synset Examples

He is so fickle; one moment he loves pizza, the next he hates it.
