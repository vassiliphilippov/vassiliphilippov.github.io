for good

1. ADV

/ fər ɡʊd / (AmE), / fə ɡʊd / (BrE)
permanently; for all time without change
permanently; forever

Examples

After years of traveling, she decided to settle down for good.
The shop has closed its doors for good and will not reopen.
He gave up smoking for good after his health scare.

Synset Examples

he is permanently disabled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: permanently - in a way that lasts forever
  • Antonym: temporarily - for a short time only
