Chinese evergreen

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃaɪ.niz ˈɛv.ɚˌgriːn / (AmE), / ˌtʃaɪ.niːz ˈɛv.ə.ɡriːn / (BrE)
an ornamental plant with large green or variegated leaves, often grown indoors for its attractive foliage
an indoor ornamental plant with variegated leaves

Examples

She decorated her office with a Chinese evergreen to bring in some greenery.
The Chinese evergreen is known for thriving in low-light conditions, making it ideal for homes.
Because of its tolerance to shade, a Chinese evergreen is a popular choice for beginners in plant care.
