glittery sticker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɪt̬ɚi ˈstɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪtəri ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
A decorative sticker with a shiny, sparkling surface, often used for arts, crafts, or embellishments.
shiny, sparkling decorative sticker

Examples

She put a glittery sticker on the front cover of her notebook.
Each invitation was sealed with a gold glittery sticker to make it special.
The teacher rewarded excellent work with a smiley-face glittery sticker.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the glittery sticker on their homework.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sticker - an adhesive label or decoration
