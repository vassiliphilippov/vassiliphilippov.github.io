at hand

1. ADJ

/ ət hænd / (AmE), / ət hænd / (BrE)
close in space; within easy reach or nearby
nearby; within reach

Examples

The library is conveniently at hand for all the students in the area.
All the necessary tools were at hand during the emergency repair.
Whenever she travels, she keeps her passport at hand just in case.

Synset Examples

the town is close at hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearby - close in distance; not far away
  • Antonym: far away - distant; at a great distance
