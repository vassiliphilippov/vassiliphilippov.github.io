keyboard cowboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːbɔrd ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a person who behaves boldly or aggressively online, especially in ways they would not in person
bold or aggressive online persona
informal slang

Examples

After getting called out for his comments, the keyboard cowboy quickly deleted his posts.
It's easy to be a keyboard cowboy when you never have to face someone in real life.
She ignored the threats made by the keyboard cowboy, knowing he would never confront her offline.

Synset Examples

He's a keyboard cowboy, always bragging online.
The keyboard cowboy never leaves his computer.
She’s a keyboard cowboy, fighting battles from home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet troll - a person who deliberately provokes others online
  • Synonym: cyberbully - someone who harasses or intimidates others online

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'cowboy' (a reckless, daring person) and 'keyboard' (referring to internet/computer use).
