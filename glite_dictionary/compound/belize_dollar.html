Belize dollar

1. NOUN

/ bəˈliːz ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / bəˈliːz ˈdɒlər / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Belize, subdivided into 100 cents
currency of Belize

Examples

She exchanged her US dollars for Belize dollars at the border.
The price of the tour was listed as 150 Belize dollars.
You can use both US dollars and Belize dollars in many shops in Belize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - the name of various basic monetary units in several countries
