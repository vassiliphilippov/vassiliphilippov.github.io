Muslim attire

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʊzləm əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʊzlɪm əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
clothing traditionally worn by Muslims that reflects cultural or religious identity, such as hijabs, abayas, or kufis
traditional clothing worn by Muslims

Examples

Many women choose to wear Muslim attire as an expression of their faith.
The school's dress code is respectful of Muslim attire such as hijabs and long dresses.
During the festival, most participants were dressed in traditional Muslim attire.

Synset Examples

Her Muslimistic attire reflected her deep faith.
