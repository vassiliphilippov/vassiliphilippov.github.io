TV audience

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː ˈɔːdiəns / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː ˈɔːdiəns / (BrE)
the group of people who watch a particular television program or broadcast
group watching a TV program

Examples

The final episode attracted a massive TV audience across the country.
Advertisers are eager to reach a younger TV audience with their latest commercials.
The awards show failed to draw a large TV audience this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: viewers - people who watch television programs
