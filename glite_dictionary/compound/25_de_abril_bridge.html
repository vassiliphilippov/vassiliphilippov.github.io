25 de Abril Bridge

1. PROPN

/ twɛnti faɪv də əˈbriːl brɪdʒ / (AmE), / twɛnti faɪv də əˈbriːl brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A suspension bridge spanning the Tagus River in Lisbon, Portugal, noted for its resemblance to San Francisco's Golden Gate Bridge.
suspension bridge in Lisbon, Portugal

Examples

The 25 de Abril Bridge is one of Lisbon's most iconic landmarks, connecting the city to Almada.
Driving across the 25 de Abril Bridge offers spectacular views of the Tagus River and the Lisbon skyline.
Many tourists mistake the 25 de Abril Bridge for San Francisco's Golden Gate Bridge due to their striking similarities.

Etymology

Named after the Carnation Revolution of 25 April 1974, which restored democracy in Portugal.
