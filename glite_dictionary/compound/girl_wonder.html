girl wonder

1. NOUN

/ ɡɜrl ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / ɡɜːl ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
a young girl who is exceptionally talented or successful at an early age
exceptionally talented young girl

Examples

At only twelve, the girl wonder was already composing symphonies that amazed professional musicians.
The tech world celebrated the girl wonder who developed a popular app before her teens.
Every news outlet wanted to interview the girl wonder after she broke the national record.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boy wonder - an extremely talented young male person
