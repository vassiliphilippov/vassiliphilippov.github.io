Italian greyhound

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən ˈɡreɪˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ɪˈtæl.i.ən ˈɡreɪ.haʊnd / (BrE)
a small, slender breed of dog originally developed from the greyhound, known for its elegance and affectionate nature
a small slender dog breed

Examples

The Italian greyhound is a graceful dog that makes a loyal companion.
Because of its slim build, the Italian greyhound is best suited for indoor living.
She adopted two Italian greyhounds from a local rescue organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greyhound - a tall, slender, fast breed of dog; ancestor of the Italian greyhound

Etymology

Named for its country of origin, Italy, and its descent from the greyhound breed.
