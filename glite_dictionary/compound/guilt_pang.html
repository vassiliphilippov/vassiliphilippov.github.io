guilt pang

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪlt pæŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪlt pæŋ / (BrE)
a sudden sharp feeling of guilt or remorse
sudden sharp feeling of guilt

Examples

He felt a sudden guilt pang after lying to his friend.
Whenever she passed the old house, a guilt pang struck her for what she had done.
A guilt pang overwhelmed him as he remembered his harsh words.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
  • Synonym: pangs of conscience - feelings of moral regret or remorse
