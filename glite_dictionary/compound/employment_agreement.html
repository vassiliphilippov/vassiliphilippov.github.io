employment agreement

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪmənt əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt əˈɡriːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
a formal contract between an employer and an employee outlining the terms and conditions of employment
contract covering terms of employment

Examples

Before starting her new job, she carefully read the entire employment agreement.
The employment agreement specifies the employee's salary, benefits, and responsibilities.
Both parties signed the employment agreement during the onboarding process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employment contract - a contract specifying the terms of employment; synonym for employment agreement
