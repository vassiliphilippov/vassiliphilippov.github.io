television equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˌtelɪˈvɪʒən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
electronic devices used for broadcasting or receiving images and sound by means of television signals
devices for broadcasting or receiving television

Examples

The studio had the latest television equipment installed for high-definition broadcasting.
Modern television equipment is much more compact than the bulky sets from decades ago.
Engineers serviced the television equipment to ensure flawless live transmission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television set - an electronic device that receives television signals and displays images and sound
