California bluebell

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪfərniə ˈbluːˌbɛl / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈniːə ˈbluːˌbɛl / (BrE)
an annual plant of southern California with intricately branched stems and clusters of aromatic deep blue bell-shaped flowers
annual plant with blue bell-shaped flowers

Examples

Hikers in the spring may spot the vivid California bluebell blooming along the trails.
Gardeners often choose the California bluebell for its colorful and aromatic flowers.
The California bluebell thrives in sandy soils and full sunlight.

Examples

Gardeners in arid regions often plant California bluebells for their vibrant color and drought tolerance.
We spotted a rare California bluebell while hiking in southern California.
