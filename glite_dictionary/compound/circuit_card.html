circuit card

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜr.kɪt kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kɪt kɑːd / (BrE)
a printed circuit board that can be inserted into an expansion slot in a computer to enhance its capabilities
computer board for expanding functions

Examples

The technician installed a new circuit card to give the computer additional USB ports.
If your graphics performance is slow, upgrading the circuit card might help.
Many computers allow users to add a circuit card for wireless connectivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expansion card - a printed circuit board inserted into a computer expansion slot to add functions
