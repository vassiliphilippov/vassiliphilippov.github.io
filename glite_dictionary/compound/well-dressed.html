well-dressed

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈdrɛst / (BrE)
Wearing stylish and neat clothing; dressed in a tasteful and tidy manner.
wearing stylish and neat clothing

Examples

She always appears well-dressed at important meetings.
Despite traveling all day, he managed to look perfectly well-dressed.
The guests at the gala were exceptionally well-dressed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shabby - wearing old or untidy clothes; poorly dressed
  • Synonym: smart - neatly and stylishly dressed

2. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈdrɛst / (BrE)
Wearing smart, stylish, or elegant clothes.
wearing elegant or stylish clothing

Examples

She always arrives at work well-dressed and confident.
The well-dressed gentleman greeted us at the door.
It was a party for the city's most well-dressed residents.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smartly dressed - wearing smart or stylish clothing
