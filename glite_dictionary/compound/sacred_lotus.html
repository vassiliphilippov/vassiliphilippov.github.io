sacred lotus

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪkrɪd ˈloʊtəs / (AmE), / ˈseɪkrɪd ˈləʊtəs / (BrE)
A water plant native to eastern Asia, widely cultivated for its large pink or white flowers and often regarded as sacred in several cultures.
Asian water plant with large flowers

Examples

The sacred lotus is admired for its beauty and its significance in many Asian religions.
Botanists have studied the sacred lotus for its remarkable ability to repel water and remain clean.
A pond filled with blooming sacred lotus creates a stunning and tranquil scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lotus - an aquatic plant with large, showy flowers
