bearded iris

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪrdɪd ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɪədɪd ˈaɪərɪs / (BrE)
a type of iris, either wild or cultivated, distinguished by hairlike structures on the falls (the drooping sepals) of its flowers
type of iris with hairy sepals

Examples

The bearded iris blooms in a dazzling array of colors in late spring.
She planted several varieties of bearded iris along the garden path.
A close look at the bearded iris reveals the soft hairs on its sepals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iris - a plant with showy flowers, of the genus Iris
