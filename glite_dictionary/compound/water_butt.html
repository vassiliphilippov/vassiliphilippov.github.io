water butt

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər bʌt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə bʌt / (BrE)
a large container placed outside to collect and store rainwater, typically from a roof.
container for storing rainwater outdoors

Examples

The gardener filled his watering can from the water butt at the corner of the shed.
Installing a water butt helps conserve water during dry seasons.
Heavy rain last night topped up our water butt to the brim.
