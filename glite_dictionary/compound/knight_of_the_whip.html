knight of the whip

1. NOUN

/ naɪt əv ðə wɪp / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə wɪp / (BrE)
a highly skilled rodeo performer or a strict or authoritative figure known for discipline or enforcement
skilled rodeo performer or strict disciplinarian

Examples

His reputation as a knight of the whip earned him both fear and admiration among his peers.
During the show, the crowd watched as the knight of the whip performed daring stunts.
The principal was a real knight of the whip when it came to discipline at the school.

Synset Examples

He was known as the knight of the whip in the rodeo.
