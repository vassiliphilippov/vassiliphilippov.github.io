soup plate

1. NOUN

/ suːp pleɪt / (AmE), / suːp pleɪt / (BrE)
A deep plate with a wide rim, used for serving soup.
deep plate for serving soup

Examples

She placed the hot minestrone in a soup plate and handed it to her guest.
The restaurant served the chowder in a shallow soup plate with a slice of bread on the side.
I need to buy matching soup plates for my dinner set.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowl - a round, deep dish used for holding food or liquid
