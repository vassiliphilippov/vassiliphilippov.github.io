logistic support

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈdʒɪstɪk səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ləʊˈdʒɪstɪk səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
the provision of supplies, equipment, transportation, and services to support military or organizational operations
assistance with supplies and services, especially for military

Examples

The army relied heavily on logistic support to ensure the timely delivery of food and ammunition.
Efficient logistic support was critical during the disaster relief mission.
Without proper logistic support, the operation would have been impossible to sustain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logistics - the detailed organization and implementation of complex operations

Etymology

From 'logistic', relating to logistics (from French 'logistique', from 'loger', to lodge), and 'support'.
