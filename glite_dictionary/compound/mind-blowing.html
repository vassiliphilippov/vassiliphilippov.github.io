mind-blowing

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪnd ˌbloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnd ˌbləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive, surprising, or shocking, especially in a way that powerfully affects the mind or senses.
astonishing or intensely impressive
informal

Examples

The special effects in that movie were absolutely mind-blowing.
Her performance in the concert was so mind-blowing that it left the audience speechless.
The view from the top of the mountain was mind-blowing and unforgettable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - very surprising; causing amazement
  • Antonym: unimpressive - not making an impression; not striking
