spice-scented

1. ADJ

/ spaɪsˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / spaɪsˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having a smell of spices; fragrant with the aroma of spices.
smelling of spices

Examples

The kitchen was spice-scented after she finished baking her famous gingerbread cookies.
A spice-scented breeze drifted from the market, tempting passersby.
We relaxed in a spice-scented bath infused with cinnamon and cloves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
