soccer field

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːkɚ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈsɒkə fiːld / (BrE)
an area of grass or artificial turf marked out for playing soccer, with specific dimensions and markings
area for playing soccer

Examples

The players ran onto the soccer field to begin their match.
Each soccer field must have appropriate markings and goalposts.
Rain turned the soccer field into a muddy mess after the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: football pitch - British term for a soccer field
