freshman class

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃmən klæs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃmən klɑːs / (BrE)
the group of students in their first year at a college or high school
group of first-year students
american

Examples

The freshman class attended an orientation session on the first day of school.
This year's freshman class is larger than ever before at the university.
She was elected president of the freshman class in her first semester.
