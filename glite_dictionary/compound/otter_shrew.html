otter shrew

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑtər ˌʃru / (AmE), / ˈɒtə ˌʃruː / (BrE)
An amphibious African mammal resembling an otter, belonging to the family Potamogalidae, and feeding mainly on insects.
African amphibious insectivorous mammal

Examples

The rare otter shrew spends much of its life swimming in African rivers.
Researchers observed an otter shrew catching insects along the riverbank.
Unlike true otters, the otter shrew belongs to a completely different mammalian family.
