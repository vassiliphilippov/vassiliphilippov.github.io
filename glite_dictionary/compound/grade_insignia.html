grade insignia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪd ɪnˈsɪɡniə / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪd ɪnˈsɪɡniə / (BrE)
A V-shaped badge, usually worn on the sleeve, indicating military rank and length of service.
V-shaped badge showing military rank

Examples

Soldiers proudly displayed their grade insignia on their sleeves after the ceremony.
The sergeant's grade insignia indicated many years of loyal service.
He carefully sewed the new grade insignia onto his uniform after his promotion.

Synset Examples

they earned their stripes in Kuwait

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chevron - a badge or insignia in the shape of a V worn on a uniform to indicate rank, service, or achievement
