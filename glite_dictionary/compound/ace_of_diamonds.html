ace of diamonds

1. NOUN

/ eɪs əv ˈdaɪəməndz / (AmE), / eɪs əv ˈdaɪəməndz / (BrE)
the playing card that is the ace in the suit of diamonds
ace playing card in diamonds suit

Examples

She smiled as she revealed the ace of diamonds during the game.
Pulling the ace of diamonds from the deck, he knew he had a chance to win.
The magician made the ace of diamonds disappear right before our eyes.
