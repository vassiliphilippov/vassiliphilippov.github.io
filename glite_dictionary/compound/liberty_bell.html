Liberty Bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbɚti bɛl / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəti bɛl / (BrE)
A large, historic bell located in Philadelphia, famously rung on July 8, 1776, to mark the public reading of the Declaration of Independence in the United States.
historic American bell in Philadelphia

Examples

Many tourists visit Philadelphia to see the Liberty Bell each year.
The crack in the Liberty Bell is one of its most recognizable features.
The Liberty Bell symbolizes freedom and independence in American culture.
