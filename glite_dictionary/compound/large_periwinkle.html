large periwinkle

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈpɛɹiˌwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈpɛɹɪˌwɪŋkl̩ / (BrE)
an ornamental plant with variegated foliage, often used in window boxes and gardens, also known as Vinca major
a variegated ornamental plant (Vinca major)

Examples

She planted large periwinkle along the edge of her garden for its attractive variegated leaves.
The window boxes were filled with blooming large periwinkle and other colorful flowers.
Gardeners value large periwinkle for its hardy nature and decorative foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periwinkle - a trailing evergreen plant of the genus Vinca
