blanching machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæntʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈblɑːntʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used to blanch, boil, or sterilize fruits and vegetables, typically as part of food processing.
machine for blanching fruits or vegetables

Examples

The factory installed a new blanching machine to improve its vegetable processing line.
Operators must ensure the blanching machine is properly maintained for food safety compliance.
Using a blanching machine reduces the preparation time before freezing green beans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to blanch - briefly boil food to loosen skins or soften
