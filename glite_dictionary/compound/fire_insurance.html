fire insurance

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ɪnˌʃʊrəns / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ɪnˌʃʊərəns / (BrE)
insurance that provides coverage against financial loss caused by fire damage to property
insurance covering fire-related losses

Examples

After the accident, their fire insurance helped cover the cost of rebuilding their house.
Most homeowners in the area are required to carry fire insurance due to the risk of wildfires.
He filed a claim with his fire insurance company after a small kitchen fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insurance - coverage by contract to provide compensation for loss, damage, or injury
