total nutcase

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈnʌt.keɪs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈnʌt.keɪs / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is considered crazy or extremely eccentric
crazy or eccentric person (informal)
informal

Examples

Everyone at the office thinks he's a total nutcase for building a treehouse in his living room.
She laughed and called her best friend a total nutcase for skydiving on her birthday.
People thought the inventor was a total nutcase until his ideas changed the world.

Synset Examples

He’s a total nutcase for running marathons every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutcase - a crazy or eccentric person
