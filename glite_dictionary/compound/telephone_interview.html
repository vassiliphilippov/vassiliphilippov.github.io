telephone interview

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˈɪntərˌvju / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
an interview that is conducted over the telephone rather than in person
interview conducted over the telephone

Examples

He had a telephone interview for the job because the company was based overseas.
Many companies use a telephone interview as the first step in their hiring process.
During the telephone interview, she was asked about her previous work experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interview - a formal meeting in which one or more persons question, consult, or evaluate another person
