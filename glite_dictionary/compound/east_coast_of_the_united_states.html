East Coast of the United States

1. PROPN

/ ist koʊst əv ði ˌjuː.naɪ.tɪd steɪts / (AmE), / iːst kəʊst əv ði ˌjuːˈnaɪ.tɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the eastern seaboard region of the United States, especially the area between Boston and Washington, D.C.
eastern seaboard region of the U.S.

Examples

Many major cities, including New York and Philadelphia, are located on the East Coast of the United States.
She moved from California to the East Coast of the United States for a new job opportunity.
The East Coast of the United States often experiences hurricanes during late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: East Coast - the eastern coastal region of a country, especially the U.S.
  • Antonym: West Coast of the United States - the western coastal region of the United States
