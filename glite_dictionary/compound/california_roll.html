California roll

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪfərniə roʊl / (AmE), / kəˈlɪfəniə rəʊl / (BrE)
a type of sushi roll typically made with avocado, crab (or imitation crab), and cucumber, often with rice on the outside
sushi roll with avocado and crab

Examples

Many people who are new to sushi start with a California roll because of its familiar flavors.
He learned how to make a California roll by watching online videos.
The restaurant's California rolls are topped with sesame seeds for extra flavor.

Synset Examples

I ordered a California roll for lunch today.
She made a delicious California roll at the party.
The California roll is my favorite sushi dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sushi - a Japanese dish of vinegared rice with seafood or vegetables

Etymology

Named after the U.S. state of California, where it was popularized in the 1960s.
