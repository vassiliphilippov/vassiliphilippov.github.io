Oreo cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrioʊ ˈkʊki / (AmE), / ˈɔːriəʊ ˈkʊki / (BrE)
a chocolate sandwich cookie with a sweet white cream filling, typically sold under the trademark Oreo
chocolate sandwich cookie with cream filling

Examples

She dunked her Oreo cookie into a glass of cold milk.
The dessert was topped with crumbled Oreo cookies for extra crunch.
Have you ever tried an Oreo cookie milkshake at that restaurant?

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cookie - a small, sweet baked treat, often with a crispy or chewy texture

Etymology

Originally a trademark of Nabisco, introduced in 1912.
