fetid horehound

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛtɪd ˈhɔrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd ˈhɔːhaʊnd / (BrE)
an ill-smelling European herb with wrinkled leaves and whorls of dark purple flowers, known for its unpleasant odor
ill-smelling European herb

Examples

The aroma of fetid horehound filled the air as we walked through the meadow.
Some gardeners avoid planting fetid horehound due to its unpleasant smell.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of fetid horehound despite its odor.
