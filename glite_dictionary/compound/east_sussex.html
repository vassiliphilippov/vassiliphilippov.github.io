East Sussex

1. PROPN

/ ist ˈsʌsɪks / (AmE), / iːst ˈsʌsɪks / (BrE)
a county in southern England, bordering Kent, Surrey, West Sussex, and the English Channel
county in southern England

Examples

Many tourists visit East Sussex to explore its historic coastal towns and beautiful countryside.
The University of Sussex is located near Brighton in East Sussex.
She grew up in a small village in East Sussex before moving to London.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: West Sussex - county in southern England, bordering East Sussex

Etymology

Named for its location as the eastern part of the historic county of Sussex (from Old English 'Sūþsēaxe' meaning 'South Saxons').
