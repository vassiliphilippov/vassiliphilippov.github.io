wood drake

1. NOUN

/ wʊd dreɪk / (AmE), / wʊd dreɪk / (BrE)
A male wood duck, a brightly coloured North American duck species (Aix sponsa).
male wood duck

Examples

The brilliant feathers of the wood drake shimmered in the sunlight as he swam across the pond.
Birdwatchers spotted a wood drake displaying his colors during mating season.
Compared to the plain brown female, the wood drake is exceptionally vivid and eye-catching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood duck - a species of duck (Aix sponsa) known for its colorful plumage
  • Antonym: hen - female duck
