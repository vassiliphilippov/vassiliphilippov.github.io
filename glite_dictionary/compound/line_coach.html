line coach

1. NOUN

/ laɪn koʊtʃ / (AmE), / laɪn kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an assistant coach in American football responsible for coaching the linemen
assistant football coach for linemen
american

Examples

After five years as an offensive line coach, he was promoted to coordinator.
The team's line coach worked closely with the defensive linemen during practice.
She became the first female line coach in the league's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistant coach - coach who assists the head coach
