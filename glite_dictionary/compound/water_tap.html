water tap

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər tæp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə tæp / (BrE)
A device for controlling the flow of water from a pipe or container.
device for controlling water flow

Examples

She turned on the water tap to wash her hands.
Please make sure the water tap is closed tightly after use.
A steady drip came from the old water tap in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap - a device for controlling the flow of liquid from a pipe or container
  • Synonym: faucet - a device that controls the flow of liquid, especially water
