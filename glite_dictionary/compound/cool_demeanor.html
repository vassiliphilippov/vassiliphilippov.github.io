cool demeanor

1. NOUN

/ kul dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / kuːl dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a calm, composed, and unflappable manner, especially in stressful situations
calm and composed manner

Examples

Despite the chaos, she maintained a cool demeanor throughout the crisis.
His cool demeanor often reassured his anxious team members before big presentations.
Reporters admired the prime minister’s cool demeanor during the heated debate.

Synset Examples

He's got a cool demeanor even under pressure.
Her ice cool demeanor impressed everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: composure - the state of being calm and in control of oneself
  • Antonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed; lacking calmness
