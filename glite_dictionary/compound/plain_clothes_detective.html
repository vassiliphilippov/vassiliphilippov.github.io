plain clothes detective

1. NOUN

/ pleɪn kloʊðz dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / pleɪn kləʊðz dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a police detective who wears ordinary civilian clothing while on duty, rather than a uniform, to avoid drawing attention
detective in civilian clothes

Examples

The plain clothes detective observed the suspect from across the street.
Nobody realized that the woman in the café was actually a plain clothes detective.
Several plain clothes detectives were assigned to monitor the parade for any suspicious activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detective - a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate crimes
