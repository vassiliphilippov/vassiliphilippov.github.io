conic Verpa

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnɪk ˈvɜr.pə / (AmE), / ˈkɒnɪk ˈvɜː.pə / (BrE)
A species of morel mushroom with a smooth, cone-shaped cap and a fragile stalk.
species of morel mushroom with smooth, conic cap

Examples

The conic Verpa is often mistaken for true morels due to its similar appearance.
Harvesters advised caution when collecting conic Verpa because its fragile stalk breaks easily.
Researchers examined the conic Verpa to study its unique cap structure.

Etymology

From Latin 'conicus' (conical) and 'Verpa' (genus name of certain fungi).
