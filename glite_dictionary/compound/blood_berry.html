blood berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌd ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈblʌd ˌbɛri / (BrE)
A bushy tropical American houseplant with white to pale pink flowers and clusters of scarlet berries.
tropical houseplant with scarlet berries

Examples

The gardener placed a blood berry in the sunniest corner of the greenhouse.
Clusters of scarlet blood berries contrasted beautifully with the plant's glossy leaves.
Many plant enthusiasts grow the blood berry for its decorative fruit.
