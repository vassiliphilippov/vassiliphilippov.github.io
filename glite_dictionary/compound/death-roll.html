death-roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ ˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌrəʊl / (BrE)
a list of people who have been killed, especially in a war or disaster
list of the dead (in war or disaster)

Examples

The official death-roll released by authorities shocked the nation.
Journalists rushed to update the death-roll after the devastating earthquake.
The army published the death-roll along with the names of survivors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casualty list - a list of people killed, wounded, or missing in an event
