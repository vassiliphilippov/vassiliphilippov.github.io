BB shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiː ˈbiː ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˈbiː ʃɒt / (BrE)
a small round pellet, typically made of metal, fired from an air rifle or BB gun
small pellet for BB gun or air rifle

Examples

He found a BB shot lodged in the wooden fence after target practice.
The BB shot bounced off the tin can without leaving a mark.
Always wear eye protection when handling a BB shot and air rifle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pellet - a small, rounded, compressed mass of material, often fired from an airgun
  • Synonym: BB gun - an air gun designed to fire BB shots

Etymology

From 'BB', denoting a standard size of small shot, and 'shot', meaning a pellet fired from a gun.
