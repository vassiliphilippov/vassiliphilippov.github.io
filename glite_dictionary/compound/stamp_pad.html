stamp pad

1. NOUN

/ stæmp pæd / (AmE), / stæmp pæd / (BrE)
a small block of absorbent material soaked with ink, used to apply ink evenly to a rubber stamp
ink-soaked pad for rubber stamps

Examples

She pressed the stamp firmly onto the stamp pad before making her mark on the paper.
Please replace the ink in the stamp pad if it starts to dry out.
The office manager ordered a new stamp pad for the front desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ink pad - a pad soaked with ink, used for inking stamps
