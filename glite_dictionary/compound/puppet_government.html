puppet government

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌp.ɪt ˈɡʌv.ɚn.mənt / (AmE), / ˈpʌp.ɪt ˈɡʌv.ən.mənt / (BrE)
A government that is controlled and directed by an outside authority, often lacking real power or independence.
government controlled by outside authority

Examples

After the invasion, a puppet government was installed to serve the interests of the occupying country.
Historians often debate whether that regime was truly independent or merely a puppet government.
Citizens protested the puppet government for failing to represent their true interests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protectorate - a state that is controlled and protected by another
  • Antonym: independent government - a government free from external control

Etymology

From 'puppet' (a figure moved by strings) and 'government'.
