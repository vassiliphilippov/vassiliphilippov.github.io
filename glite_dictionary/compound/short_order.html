short order

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
An order for food that can be prepared and served very quickly, especially in a restaurant or diner.
quickly prepared food order

Examples

The diner is famous for its short order breakfasts served in minutes.
I'll have a short order of eggs and toast, please.
Working in a short order kitchen requires speed and attention to detail.
