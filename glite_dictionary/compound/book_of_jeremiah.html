Book of Jeremiah

1. PROPN

/ ˌbʊk əv ˌdʒɛrɪˈmaɪə / (AmE), / ˌbʊk əv ˌdʒɛrɪˈmaɪə / (BrE)
A book in the Old Testament containing the prophecies and oracles of the prophet Jeremiah.
book of the Old Testament, prophecies of Jeremiah

Examples

Many scholars study the Book of Jeremiah to analyze its historical context and literary style.
The Book of Jeremiah contains messages of warning as well as hope for the people of Israel.
During the lecture, the professor quoted several passages from the Book of Jeremiah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jeremiah - Hebrew prophet after whom the Book of Jeremiah is named

Etymology

From the Hebrew Bible; named after the prophet Jeremiah.
