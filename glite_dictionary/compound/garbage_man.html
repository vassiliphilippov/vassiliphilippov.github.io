garbage man

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ mæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to collect and dispose of garbage or refuse
man who collects garbage

Examples

The garbage man comes early every Tuesday to collect the household trash.
My son waved at the garbage man as the truck drove by our house.
The garbage man works hard to keep the neighborhood clean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage collector - a person whose job is to collect garbage, regardless of gender
  • Synonym: dustman - a man employed to collect domestic refuse (British)
