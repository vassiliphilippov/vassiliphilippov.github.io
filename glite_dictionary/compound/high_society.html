high society

1. NOUN

/ haɪ səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / haɪ səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
the group of people in a community or country who are rich, fashionable, and influential
wealthy and fashionable social elite

Examples

The wedding attracted members of high society from all over the country.
She quickly rose through the ranks of high society after marrying a wealthy businessman.
Stories about high society often appear in the society pages of major newspapers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upper class - the social group that is considered the highest in status or wealth
  • Antonym: low society - the lower or working classes regarded as less privileged
