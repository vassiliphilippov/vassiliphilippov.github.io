artichoke heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrtɪˌtʃoʊk hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪˌtʃəʊk hɑːt / (BrE)
the tender, edible central part of an artichoke flower bud, often eaten as a delicacy or used in cooking
central edible part of an artichoke

Examples

She added chopped artichoke hearts to the salad for extra flavor.
Marinated artichoke hearts are a popular appetizer in Mediterranean cuisine.
The recipe calls for one can of artichoke hearts, drained and sliced.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: artichoke - a large, round, green vegetable with thick, pointed leaves and a heart at its center
