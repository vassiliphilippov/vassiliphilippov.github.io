biology laboratory

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈɑlədʒi ˌlæbrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / baɪˈɒlədʒi ˌlæbrəˈtəri / (BrE)
A laboratory equipped and used for research or experiments in the field of biology.
laboratory for biological research

Examples

All the experiments were conducted in the biology laboratory at the university.
The new school building includes a state-of-the-art biology laboratory for students.
Proper safety procedures must be followed in any biology laboratory to prevent accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building equipped for scientific experiments
