latest sneaker trend

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst ˈsnikər trɛnd / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst ˈsniːkə trɛnd / (BrE)
the most recent and popular style of sneakers to attract widespread attention or use
most recent popular sneaker style

Examples

She always tries to have the latest sneaker trend before anyone else at school.
The latest sneaker trend features bold colors and chunky soles this season.
Social media influencers often set the latest sneaker trend with their unique shoe choices.

Synset Examples

Everydude was wearing the latest sneaker trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trend - a general direction in which something is developing or changing
  • Synonym: latest fashion - the most current or up-to-date style
  • Antonym: classic sneaker - a sneaker style that remains popular over time, not limited to latest trends
