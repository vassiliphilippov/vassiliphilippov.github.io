to raise awareness

1. VERB

/ reɪz əˈwɛr.nəs / (AmE), / reɪz əˈweə.nəs / (BrE)
to make more people conscious of or informed about a particular issue, problem, or cause
make more people conscious of an issue

Examples

The charity launched a campaign to raise awareness about plastic pollution in the oceans.
We held a seminar to raise awareness of mental health issues among teenagers.
Social media is often used to raise awareness for various humanitarian causes.
