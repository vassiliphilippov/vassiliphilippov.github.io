dying gasp of hope

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪɪŋ gæsp əv hoʊp / (AmE), / ˈdaɪɪŋ ɡɑːsp əv həʊp / (BrE)
a final, desperate expression of hope before losing all optimism
final expression of hope before giving up

Examples

His last attempt at reconciliation was a dying gasp of hope before resigning to loneliness.
In the face of overwhelming odds, her speech was a dying gasp of hope for her struggling team.
The community's petition felt like a dying gasp of hope as the deadline approached.

Synset Examples

She wrote her final letter in a dying gasp of hope.
