Maltese cat

1. NOUN

/ mɔlˈtiz ˌkæt / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz ˌkæt / (BrE)
a short-haired cat with a bluish-grey coat, especially as referred to in the United States
short-haired bluish-grey cat

Examples

The Maltese cat is admired for its striking bluish-grey fur and gentle temperament.
Many American households have adopted a Maltese cat as an affectionate companion.
He spotted a handsome Maltese cat basking in the afternoon sunlight on the windowsill.

Etymology

From 'Maltese', relating to Malta, where similar cats were believed to originate.
