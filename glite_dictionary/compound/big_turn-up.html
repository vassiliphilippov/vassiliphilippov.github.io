big turn-up

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈtɝn ˌʌp / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈtɜːn ˌʌp / (BrE)
A situation in which a large number of people attend an event or gathering.
large attendance at an event

Examples

The music festival saw a big turn-up despite the rain.
There's likely to be a big turn-up for the grand opening tonight.
Organizers were pleased with the big turn-up at the community meeting.

Synset Examples

We expect a big turn-up at the charity event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnout - the number of people who attend or participate in an event
