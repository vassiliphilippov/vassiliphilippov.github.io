Gulf of Campeche

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv kəmˈpeɪtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv kəmˈpeɪtʃeɪ / (BrE)
a large arm of the Gulf of Mexico located to the west of the Yucatan Peninsula in southeastern Mexico.
bay in the Gulf of Mexico, west of Yucatan

Examples

Many oil fields have been discovered in the Gulf of Campeche, making it a crucial energy resource for Mexico.
Hurricanes forming in the Gulf of Campeche can pose serious threats to the surrounding coastal areas.
Fishermen rely on the rich waters of the Gulf of Campeche for their daily catch.
