military adviser

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪl.ɪˌtɛri ædˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.ɪ.tər.i ədˈvaɪ.zə / (BrE)
a military officer who serves as an adviser to the armed forces of an allied or friendly nation
military officer advising allied forces

Examples

The country requested several military advisers to help train their army in modern tactics.
As a military adviser, she was instrumental in developing cooperation between the two allied forces.
After years of service as a military adviser overseas, he returned home with a wealth of experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adviser - a person who gives advice in a particular field
