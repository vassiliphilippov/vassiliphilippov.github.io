nativity scene

1. NOUN

/ nəˈtɪv.ə.ti siːn / (AmE), / nəˈtɪv.ɪ.ti siːn / (BrE)
A model or tableau representing the birth of Jesus Christ, typically displayed at Christmas.
model of Christ's birth

Examples

Every year, the children help set up the nativity scene in our living room.
The town square features a large nativity scene during the holiday season.
She collects miniature figures to add to her family's nativity scene collection.

Synset Examples

The nativity scene was beautifully arranged in the church.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crèche - a model or tableau depicting the nativity; synonym for nativity scene

Etymology

From 'nativity', from Latin natīvitas (birth), and 'scene'.
