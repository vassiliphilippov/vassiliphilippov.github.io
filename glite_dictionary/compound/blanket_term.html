blanket term

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæŋkɪt tɝm / (AmE), / ˈblæŋkɪt tɜːm / (BrE)
a general word or phrase used to include a variety of different things, groups, or ideas
general word covering many specifics

Examples

The term 'media' is often used as a blanket term for television, radio, newspapers, and the internet.
'Vegetarian' is a blanket term that describes people with many different dietary habits.
Many people use 'addiction' as a blanket term to cover a range of dependencies.

Synset Examples

Education is a blanket term covering various fields.
The phrase 'healthy food' is a blanket term for many diets.
Sustainability is often used as a blanket term.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umbrella term - A synonym referring to a term that encompasses a broad range of concepts.
