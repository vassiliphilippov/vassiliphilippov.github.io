love-token

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌtoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌtəʊkən / (BrE)
A keepsake given as a symbol or reminder of love.
keepsake symbolizing love

Examples

He always carried the love-token she had given him before departing for the war.
The silver locket became a cherished love-token that she wore every day.
Many couples exchange rings as a love-token to commemorate their relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keepsake - an object kept as a reminder or souvenir
  • Synonym: souvenir - an object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event
