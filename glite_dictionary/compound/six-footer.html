six-footer

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪksˈfʊtər / (AmE), / ˌsɪksˈfʊtə / (BrE)
a person who is at least six feet tall
person at least six feet tall

Examples

Despite his age, the six-footer still played basketball with impressive skill.
My brother is a six-footer and never has trouble reaching high shelves.
Several six-footers were chosen for the volleyball team this year.
