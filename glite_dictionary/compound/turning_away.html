turning away

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝnɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
the act of deliberately avoiding someone or something, or preventing something from happening or continuing
deliberate avoidance or prevention

Examples

His turning away from responsibility disappointed his colleagues.
The turning away of refugees at the border sparked international criticism.
Her turning away from unhealthy habits improved her quality of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avoidance - the act of keeping away from or not doing something
  • Synonym: rejection - the dismissing or refusing of a proposal or idea
