catcher's mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃɚz mæsk / (AmE), / ˈkætʃəz mɑːsk / (BrE)
A protective mask worn by the catcher in baseball to shield the face from injury.
protective mask for baseball catcher

Examples

The umpire checked the catcher's mask to ensure it was secure before the game started.
After taking a foul ball to the catcher's mask, the player paused to recover.
He polished his catcher's mask before every game for good luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face
