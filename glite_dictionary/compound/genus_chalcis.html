genus Chalcis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæl.sɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæl.sɪs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Chalcididae, comprising certain chalcid wasps.
type genus of Chalcididae (chalcid wasps)

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species within the genus Chalcis in the tropical rainforest.
The genus Chalcis is known for its metallic-colored chalcid wasps.
Entomologists study the genus Chalcis to better understand the diversity of parasitic wasps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chalcid wasp - A small wasp of the family Chalcididae

Etymology

From Latin 'Chalcis', the genus name.
