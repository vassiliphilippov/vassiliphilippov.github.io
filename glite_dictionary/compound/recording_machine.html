recording machine

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a device or piece of equipment used to make audio or video recordings
device for making audio or video recordings

Examples

The old recording machine was used to capture the famous speech from the 1940s.
She bought a new digital recording machine for her music studio.
Engineers tested the recording machine to ensure it produced high-quality audio files.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recorder - a device for recording sound or images
