strong calling

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ˈkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈkɒlɪŋ / (BrE)
a powerful inner urge or conviction to pursue a particular profession, career, or path in life
powerful sense of vocation or purpose

Examples

After volunteering at the hospital, she realized she had a strong calling to become a nurse.
He felt a strong calling to help people who were less fortunate than himself.
Despite criticism from others, Jane could not ignore her strong calling to teach.

Synset Examples

As a priestling, he felt a strong calling to serve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vocation - a strong feeling of suitability for a particular career or occupation
