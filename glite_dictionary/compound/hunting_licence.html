hunting licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official permit that allows someone to hunt and kill specific types of animals during a certain period
permit to hunt animals

Examples

Before heading to the woods, make sure you have a valid hunting licence.
He was fined for hunting deer without a hunting licence.
Obtaining a hunting licence often requires passing a safety course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing licence - permit allowing one to catch fish legally
