purple cress

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl krɛs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl̩ krɛs / (BrE)
a small perennial herb native to cooler regions of North America, bearing racemose purple flowers
a North American herb with purple flowers

Examples

We spotted a cluster of purple cress blooming near the forest trail.
The delicate flowers of purple cress attract many early spring pollinators.
Gardeners appreciate purple cress for its vibrant color in shaded areas.
