dental care

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.tl̩ kɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɛnt.əl keə / (BrE)
the maintenance of healthy teeth through regular cleaning, examination, and treatment by dental professionals
maintenance and treatment of teeth

Examples

Regular dental care can prevent cavities and gum disease.
The clinic offers affordable dental care for families without insurance.
Good dental care includes brushing, flossing, and routine dentist visits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oral hygiene - the practice of keeping one's mouth clean to prevent dental problems
