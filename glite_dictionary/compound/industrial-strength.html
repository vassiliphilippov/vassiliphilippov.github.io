industrial-strength

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˌdʌstriəl ˈstrɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ɪnˌdʌstriəl ˈstrɛŋθ / (BrE)
extremely strong, powerful, or durable; much more concentrated or effective than what is typical for household use
extremely strong or powerful

Examples

She used an industrial-strength cleaner to remove the stubborn stains from the floor.
This sunscreen is industrial-strength, so you know you’ll be protected all day at the beach.
The comic’s jokes are so sharp, you’ll need industrial-strength skin to handle them.

Synset Examples

industrial-strength detergent
weapons-grade salsa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerful - having great power or strength

Etymology

Formed from 'industrial' (relating to industry) and 'strength', originally indicating products suitable for industrial use, later extended metaphorically.
