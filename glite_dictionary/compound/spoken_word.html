spoken word

1. NOUN

/ ˈspoʊkən wɝd / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkən wɜːd / (BrE)
a word that is pronounced or uttered aloud, as opposed to being written or thought
a word that is spoken aloud

Examples

Hearing a spoken word can trigger different emotions compared to reading it on paper.
You must correctly repeat every spoken word during the language test.
He recorded each spoken word for the project on pronunciation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: written word - a word that is written down, not spoken
