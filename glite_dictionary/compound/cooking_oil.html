cooking oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
oil derived from plants or seeds, used in preparing and cooking food.
vegetable-based oil used in cooking

Examples

She poured some cooking oil into the pan before adding the onions.
For deep-frying, it's important to use enough cooking oil to fully submerge the food.
Many people choose olive cooking oil for its flavor and health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable oil - oil extracted from plants, commonly used in cooking
