rock hind

1. NOUN

/ rɑk haɪnd / (AmE), / rɒk haɪnd / (BrE)
a species of grouper fish (Epinephelus adscensionis) found around rocky coasts or reefs in the Atlantic Ocean
a type of grouper fish

Examples

The rock hind is often spotted hiding among boulders on the ocean floor.
Divers admire the colorful spots on the rock hind when exploring coral reefs.
Fishing regulations protect the rock hind to help maintain healthy marine populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grouper - a large fish in the sea bass family
