Yakubian influence

1. NOUN

/ jəˈkuːbiən ˈɪnfluəns / (AmE), / jəˈkjuːbiən ˈɪnflʊəns / (BrE)
the distinctive artistic style or cultural impact associated with Yakubian culture, especially as seen in later works or movements
artistic or cultural influence from Yakubian culture

Examples

The painter's latest collection shows a strong Yakubian influence in both technique and subject matter.
Art historians often debate the extent of Yakubian influence on early modern European artists.
The sculptor incorporated Yakubian influence into her work, blending it with contemporary forms.

Synset Examples

In the gallery, I admired the Yakubian influences on modern art.
