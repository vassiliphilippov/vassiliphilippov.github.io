knee slapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈni ˌslæpər / (AmE), / ˈniː ˌslæpə / (BrE)
A joke or remark that is extremely funny and causes people to laugh loudly.
a very funny joke or remark

Examples

That comedian always has at least one knee slapper in his routine.
Her story about the wedding was a real knee slapper.
The joke he told last night was such a knee slapper that I couldn't stop laughing.

Synset Examples

She told a knee slapper that had everyone laughing.
That joke was a real knee slapper!
His comedy routine was full of knee slappers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: howler - a joke or story that causes loud laughter

Etymology

From the custom of slapping one's knee to indicate strong amusement.
