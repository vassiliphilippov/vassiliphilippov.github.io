High Sierra

1. PROPN

/ haɪ siˈɛɹə / (AmE), / haɪ sɪˈeə.rə / (BrE)
a mountain range in eastern California, USA, forming the highest part of the Sierra Nevada and including Mount Whitney, the tallest peak in the contiguous United States.
mountain range in eastern California, USA

Examples

Hiking in the High Sierra offers breathtaking views and challenging trails.
The High Sierra is home to a diverse range of plant and animal species.
Many adventurers climb Mount Whitney, the highest peak in the High Sierra.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sierra Nevada - a major mountain range in the western United States, of which the High Sierra is a part
