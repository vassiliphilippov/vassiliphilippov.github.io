local economy

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ɪˈkɑː.nə.mi / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ɪˈkɒn.ə.mi / (BrE)
the economic activities and financial condition of a specific area or community, especially as influenced by businesses, employment, and local resources
economic activity of a particular area

Examples

The growth of small businesses has strengthened the local economy over the past decade.
A new factory is expected to boost the local economy by creating hundreds of jobs.
When the mine closed, the entire local economy suffered a significant downturn.

Synset Examples

Tourism has revitalized the local economy in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: national economy - the economic system and activity of an entire nation
  • Degree Higher: regional economy - the economic activities of a larger area than a local community, such as a region
