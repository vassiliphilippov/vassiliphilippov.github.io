talent agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtælənt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈtælənt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to represent performers, such as actors or musicians, and find them work
person representing entertainers

Examples

After years of struggling, she finally found a talent agent to represent her in Los Angeles.
A good talent agent can open doors to bigger auditions and better roles.
The actor thanked his talent agent for helping him land the part.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agent - a person who acts on behalf of another
