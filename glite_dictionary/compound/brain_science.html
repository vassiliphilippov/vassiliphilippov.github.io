brain science

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / breɪn ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the scientific study of the brain, especially as a branch of neuroscience
study of the brain in neuroscience

Examples

Advances in brain science are helping us understand how memory works.
She majored in brain science at university and now works in a research lab.
New discoveries in brain science could lead to treatments for neurological diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neuroscience - the scientific study of the nervous system
