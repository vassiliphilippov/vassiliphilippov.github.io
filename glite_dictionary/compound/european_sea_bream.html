European sea bream

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən siː briːm / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən siː briːm / (BrE)
a species of edible fish found in European coastal waters, commonly used in cooking
an edible fish of European coastal waters

Examples

Grilled European sea bream is a popular dish in Mediterranean cuisine.
The restaurant's menu features fresh European sea bream caught off the coast of Spain.
Fishermen bring in baskets of European sea bream every morning for the local markets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gilthead - another name for the European sea bream
