golden club

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən klʌb / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən klʌb / (BrE)
an aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States, with blue-green leaves and a club-shaped spadix covered in small yellow flowers
aquatic plant with yellow club-shaped flower

Examples

The marsh was filled with golden club blooming beside the water's edge.
Botanists documented the rare appearance of golden club in the restored wetlands.
I spotted a patch of golden club while kayaking through the bayou.
