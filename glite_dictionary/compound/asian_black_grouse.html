Asian black grouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən blæk ɡraʊs / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃ(ə)n blæk ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a species of black grouse native to western Asia, known for its dark plumage and displaying behavior
a black grouse species found in western Asia

Examples

The Asian black grouse inhabits dense forests and is well-adapted to the mountain regions of western Asia.
Birdwatchers travel to remote mountain areas to observe the unique mating dance of the Asian black grouse.
Conservationists are studying the habitat requirements of the Asian black grouse to help protect the species from decline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black grouse - a species of grouse with dark plumage found in Eurasia
