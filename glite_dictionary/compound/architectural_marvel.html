architectural marvel

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəl ˈmɑːrvəl / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃərəl ˈmɑːvəl / (BrE)
A building or structure admired for its outstanding design or construction.
remarkable building or structure

Examples

The Golden Gate Bridge is considered an architectural marvel by engineers and tourists alike.
She traveled across the world to see the architectural marvel of the ancient temple.
Many believe the new museum is a modern architectural marvel that redefines the city's skyline.

Synset Examples

The cathedral's dome is an architectural marvel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - a work of outstanding artistry or skill
