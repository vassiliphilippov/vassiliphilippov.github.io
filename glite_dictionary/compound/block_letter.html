block letter

1. NOUN

/ blɑk ˈlɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / blɒk ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a letter of the alphabet written in simple, separate, and typically capitalized form, not joined or embellished
a letter written in simple, non-cursive form

Examples

Please print your name in block letters on the form.
The address on the envelope was written in neat block letters.
Teachers often ask young children to use block letters when learning to write.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cursive - writing in which the letters are joined together
