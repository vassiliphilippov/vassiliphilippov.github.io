stretch pants

1. NOUN

/ strɛtʃ pænts / (AmE), / strɛtʃ pænts / (BrE)
Trousers made from a stretchy, elastic fabric that fit closely to the body.
trousers made from stretchy fabric

Examples

She bought a pair of stretch pants for her yoga classes.
Many dancers prefer wearing stretch pants because they allow for greater movement.
His stretch pants were comfortable enough for both lounging and working out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leggings - tight-fitting stretch trousers covering the legs
  • Synonym: trousers - an outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg
