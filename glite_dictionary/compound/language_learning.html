language learning

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ ˈlɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of acquiring the ability to understand, speak, read, or write a language, typically one that is not one's native tongue
process of acquiring a language

Examples

Many adults struggle with language learning because it requires regular practice and motivation.
Immersion is considered one of the most effective methods for language learning.
New technologies have made language learning more accessible to people around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: language acquisition - the process by which people learn a language; especially the first language as children
