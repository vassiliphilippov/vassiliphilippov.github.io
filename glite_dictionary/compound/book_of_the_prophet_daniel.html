Book of the Prophet Daniel

1. PROPN

/ ˌbʊk əv ðə ˈprɑːfɪt ˈdænjəl / (AmE), / ˌbʊk əv ðə ˈprɒfɪt ˈdænjəl / (BrE)
an Old Testament book recounting the apocalyptic visions and experiences of Daniel in the court of Nebuchadnezzar.
Old Testament book about Daniel's visions and experiences

Examples

The Book of the Prophet Daniel contains some of the most famous visions in biblical literature.
Many scholars have studied the apocalyptic themes in the Book of the Prophet Daniel.
The lion's den story appears in the Book of the Prophet Daniel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Book of Daniel - Old Testament book about the prophet Daniel

Etymology

Daniel from Hebrew *Dānīʾēl*, meaning 'God is my judge'.
