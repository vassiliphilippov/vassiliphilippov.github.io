spotted dick

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑtɪd dɪk / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd dɪk / (BrE)
A traditional British steamed suet pudding containing dried currants or raisins.
British steamed suet pudding with currants
british

Examples

We enjoyed a delicious helping of spotted dick with custard after dinner.
Many traditional English pubs serve spotted dick as a dessert option.
Grandma always baked a homemade spotted dick for family gatherings.

Etymology

Likely from 'spotted' (with spots, referring to the fruit) and 'dick,' an old British dialect term for pudding.
