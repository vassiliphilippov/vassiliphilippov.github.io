makeshift vase

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkˌʃɪft veɪs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkʃɪft vɑːz / (BrE)
a temporary or improvised container used to hold flowers when a proper vase is unavailable
improvised container for flowers

Examples

With no vase on hand, she placed the roses into a makeshift vase made from a glass jar.
He grabbed an empty water bottle to serve as a makeshift vase for the wildflowers.
At the picnic, a tin cup acted as their makeshift vase for the centerpiece.

Synset Examples

He used a tomato can as a makeshift vase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vase - a decorative container for holding flowers
