covered stadium

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚd ˈsteɪdiəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd ˈsteɪdiəm / (BrE)
a stadium that is equipped with a roof to provide shelter from the elements
stadium with a roof

Examples

The match was held in a covered stadium to protect the players from the heavy rain.
Spectators appreciated the comfort of the covered stadium during the snowstorm.
Building a covered stadium made events possible throughout the year, regardless of the weather.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open-air stadium - a stadium without a roof
