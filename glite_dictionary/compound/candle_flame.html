candle flame

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændl fleɪm / (AmE), / ˈkændl fleɪm / (BrE)
the visible light and flame produced by a burning candle
the flame of a burning candle

Examples

The candle flame flickered gently in the breeze.
She stared at the candle flame, lost in thought.
A single candle flame illuminated the dark room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to flame - the hot glowing body of ignited gas
