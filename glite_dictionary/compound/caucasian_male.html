Caucasian male

1. NOUN

/ kɔːˈkeɪʒən meɪl / (AmE), / kɔːˈkeɪʒ(ɪ)ən meɪl / (BrE)
A man or boy who is classified as belonging to the white race, especially in demographic or social contexts.
a white male person

Examples

The form required each applicant to state whether they were a Caucasian male or belonged to another group.
As a Caucasian male, he was part of the largest demographic in the study.
The casting call specified that the role was for a Caucasian male aged 30 to 40.

Synset Examples

He identified as a Caucasian male in the survey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white male - a man or boy of white race or European descent

Etymology

'Caucasian' from the Caucasus region, used since the 18th century in racial classifications; 'male' from Latin 'mas', 'malis', meaning male or man.
