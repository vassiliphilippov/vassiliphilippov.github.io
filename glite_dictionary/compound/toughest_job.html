toughest job

1. NOUN

/ ˈtəfəst dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈtʌfəst dʒɒb / (BrE)
The most difficult or demanding role or position someone can have.
the most challenging role or position

Examples

She always said that being a parent was the toughest job in the world.
Leading the rescue mission turned out to be his toughest job yet.
Managing a team through a crisis is often the toughest job a leader can face.

Synset Examples

Being a mother is the toughest job I've ever had.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tough job - a job or position that is very difficult or demanding
  • Antonym: easiest job - a job or position that is the least difficult or demanding
