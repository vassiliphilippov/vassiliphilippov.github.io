croupier's rake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːpiərz reɪk / (AmE), / ˈkruːpɪəz reɪk / (BrE)
a small rake used by a croupier to move chips around on a gambling table
small rake for moving chips in casinos

Examples

The croupier's rake is used to collect chips from losing bets after each round.
He watched the croupier expertly maneuver the croupier's rake across the green felt.
Without a croupier's rake, it would be difficult to move all the chips efficiently on a busy table.
