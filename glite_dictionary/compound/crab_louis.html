crab Louis

1. NOUN

/ kræb ˈluːɪs / (AmE), / kræb ˈluːɪs / (BrE)
A salad made with crabmeat and lettuce, typically dressed with a creamy Louis sauce.
crab salad with Louis dressing

Examples

For lunch, I ordered the crab Louis, which was fresh and delicious.
The restaurant is famous for its classic crab Louis served with homemade dressing.
She prepared a crab Louis for the summer picnic, complete with crisp lettuce and ripe tomatoes.

Etymology

Named after Louis Davenport, an early 20th-century restaurateur in Spokane, Washington, USA.
