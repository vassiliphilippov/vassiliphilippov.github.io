decision making

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsɪʒən ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒ(ə)n ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of reaching a decision, especially after considering different options or information.
the process of making decisions

Examples

Effective decision making is crucial for business leaders at all levels.
Teamwork often improves the quality of decision making in organizations.
She attended a workshop on ethical decision making for healthcare professionals.

Synset Examples

a good executive must be good at decision making

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision - a choice or conclusion made after consideration
