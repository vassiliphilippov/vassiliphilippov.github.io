lame response

1. NOUN

/ leɪm rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / leɪm rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
an answer or reply that is inadequate, unconvincing, or lacks substance
unconvincing or weak answer

Examples

Her lame response did nothing to satisfy the interviewer's curiosity.
The politician's lame response to the scandal angered the public.
Don't give me a lame response—I want an honest explanation.

Synset Examples

She gave a lame response to the tough question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weak answer - an answer that lacks strength or conviction
  • Antonym: good answer - an answer that is appropriate and well-expressed
