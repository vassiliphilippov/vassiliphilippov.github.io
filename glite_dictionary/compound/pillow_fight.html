pillow fight

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊ faɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ faɪt / (BrE)
a playful fight in which people hit each other with pillows, often as a children's game or at sleepovers
a playful fight with pillows

Examples

The children started a spontaneous pillow fight before bedtime.
At the sleepover, everyone joined in a noisy pillow fight in the living room.
Feathers flew everywhere during their epic pillow fight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playfight - a playful fight, usually not intended to harm
