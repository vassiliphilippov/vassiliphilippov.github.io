undecylenic acid

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.dɪˌsɪˈlɛn.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˌsɪˈliː.nɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (BrE)
a fatty acid (C11H20O2) found in perspiration and used in the treatment of fungal infections of the skin
a fatty acid found in perspiration
medical

Examples

The antifungal cream contains undecylenic acid to treat athlete’s foot.
Researchers detected elevated levels of undecylenic acid in the perspiration samples.
Doctors sometimes recommend undecylenic acid for mild fungal infections on the skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatty acid - a carboxylic acid with a long hydrocarbon chain, found in fats and oils

Etymology

From undecylene (the related hydrocarbon) and -ic acid.
