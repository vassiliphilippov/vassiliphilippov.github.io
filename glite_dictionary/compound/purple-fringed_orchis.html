purple-fringed orchis

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈfrɪndʒd ˈɔrtʃɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈfrɪnʤd ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
A North American orchid with large, pale, purple flowers and fringed petals, similar to Habenaria psycodes.
a North American orchid with fringed purple flowers

Examples

The purple-fringed orchis blooms in late spring across damp meadows and woodlands.
Many hikers stop to admire the delicate flowers of the purple-fringed orchis along the trail.
Botanists documented the spread of the purple-fringed orchis in several North American regions.
