intelligence information

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌtɛlədʒəns ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɛlɪdʒəns ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
secret or confidential information about an enemy or potential threat, especially collected for military or governmental purposes
secret information about an enemy

Examples

The agency intercepted intelligence information that revealed the location of enemy forces.
Classified intelligence information was shared with the allied commanders before the operation began.
Spies were trained to collect intelligence information without attracting attention.

Synset Examples

we sent out planes to gather intelligence on their radar coverage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intelligence - information gathering about an enemy or rival
