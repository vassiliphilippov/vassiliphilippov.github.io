sparrow-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈsper.oʊ.saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈspær.əʊ.saɪzd / (BrE)
having the approximate size of a sparrow; small and birdlike in scale
about the size of a sparrow

Examples

The twins spotted a sparrow-sized bird hopping across the balcony rail.
She found a sparrow-sized statue hidden among the garden plants.
The drone, built for surveillance, was barely sparrow-sized.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tiny - very small in size or amount
  • Degree Lower: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
