golf bag

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf bæɡ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf bæɡ / (BrE)
a large bag, typically with a shoulder strap or handles, used for carrying golf clubs, balls, and other golf equipment
bag for carrying golf clubs and balls

Examples

He slung his golf bag over his shoulder and headed to the first tee.
The golf bag has special compartments for each type of club.
She bought a lightweight golf bag to make walking the course easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf club - a club used to hit the ball in golf
