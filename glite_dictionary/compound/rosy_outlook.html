rosy outlook

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzi ˈaʊtlʊk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi ˈaʊtlʊk / (BrE)
an optimistic or hopeful view of a situation or of life in general
optimistic view of life or situation

Examples

Despite the challenges, Rebecca maintained a rosy outlook and encouraged her team to stay positive.
Investors with a rosy outlook expect the market to recover quickly.
Even after failing the exam, he refused to lose his rosy outlook on the future.

Synset Examples

She has a rosy outlook on life and always smiles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future or the success of something
  • Antonym: pessimism - the tendency to see the worst aspect of things or believe the worst will happen
