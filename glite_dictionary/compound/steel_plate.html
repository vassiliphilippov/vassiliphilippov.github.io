steel plate

1. NOUN

/ stiːl pleɪt / (AmE), / stiːl pleɪt / (BrE)
a flat, typically rectangular piece of steel, often used in construction, manufacturing, or engineering
flat piece of steel

Examples

The workers installed a steel plate to reinforce the damaged section of the bridge.
He ordered a custom-sized steel plate for the machine part.
The welder expertly fused two steel plates together to create a sturdy frame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheet metal - metal formed into thin, flat pieces
  • Synonym: iron plate - a flat plate made of iron
