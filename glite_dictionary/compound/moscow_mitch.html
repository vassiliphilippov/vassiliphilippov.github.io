Moscow Mitch

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑːskoʊ mɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɒskəʊ mɪtʃ / (BrE)
A derogatory nickname for Mitch McConnell, especially used by critics to suggest untrustworthiness or alleged sympathy toward Russia.
derogatory nickname for Mitch McConnell
informal offensive american

Examples

Many protesters referred to the senator as Moscow Mitch during the rally.
Some political commentators criticized the use of the term Moscow Mitch as unfairly inflammatory.
The nickname Moscow Mitch gained traction on social media after a contentious vote.

Synset Examples

Moscow Mitch has been criticized for his policies.
Some voters are calling out Moscow Mitch on social media.

Etymology

Formed by combining 'Moscow' (capital of Russia) with 'Mitch' (the forename of Mitch McConnell), to suggest alleged ties or sympathies with Russia.
