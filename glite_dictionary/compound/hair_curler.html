hair curler

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈkɝːlɚ / (AmE), / heə ˈkɜːlə / (BrE)
a small cylindrical device that is wound into hair to produce curls or waves
device for curling hair

Examples

She wrapped her wet hair around a hair curler before going to bed.
My grandmother used to wear hair curlers all morning on Sundays.
He bought a set of heated hair curlers to style his hair quickly.

Synset Examples

a woman with her head full of curlers is not a pretty sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roller - a cylindrical tube used for curling hair
