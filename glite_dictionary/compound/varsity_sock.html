varsity sock

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑrsəti sɑk / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsəti sɒk / (BrE)
A knee-length sock traditionally worn for athletic events, especially as part of a school or college sports uniform.
athletic sock for sports teams

Examples

He pulled up his varsity sock before stepping onto the football field.
The school colors were displayed proudly on every player's varsity sock.
After practice, she tossed her sweaty varsity socks into the laundry bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletic sock - sock designed specifically for sports or physical activity

Etymology

From 'varsity,' a colloquial shortening of 'university,' used in the context of school athletics.
