student residence

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːdnt ˌrɛzɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt ˌrɛzɪdəns / (BrE)
A building on a college or university campus that provides living accommodation for students.
college or university student housing

Examples

The university offers a range of student residences to suit different budgets and preferences.
After being accepted, she applied for a room in a student residence close to campus.
Many international students make friends quickly in a student residence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormitory - a large bedroom for a number of people in a school or institution
  • Synonym: hall of residence - a large building, especially in a college or university, where students live
