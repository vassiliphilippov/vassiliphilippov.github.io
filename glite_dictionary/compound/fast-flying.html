fast-flying

1. ADJ

/ fæst ˈflaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈflaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
moving swiftly through the air; able to travel or move very quickly while flying.
moving swiftly while flying

Examples

The fast-flying jet soared above the clouds in a matter of seconds.
We watched a flock of fast-flying birds cross the sky at sunrise.
His reflexes resembled those of a fast-flying insect, quick and precise.

Synset Examples

fast-flying planes
played the difficult passage with flying fingers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swift - moving very quickly
  • Antonym: slow-flying - moving slowly through the air
