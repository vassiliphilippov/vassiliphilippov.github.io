common good

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ɡʊd / (BrE)
the benefit or interests of all members within a community or society collectively
the benefit of the community as a whole

Examples

The new policy was designed to serve the common good rather than individual interests.
Leaders must balance personal ambition with a responsibility toward the common good.
Protecting the environment is essential for the common good of future generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public interest - the welfare or well-being of the general public
  • Antonym: self-interest - one's personal interest or advantage
