wedding night

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛd.ɪŋ naɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛd.ɪŋ naɪt / (BrE)
the night after a wedding, traditionally the first night the newly married couple spend together as spouses
night after a wedding for the newlyweds

Examples

They checked into a luxurious hotel suite for their wedding night.
Many people have special expectations for their wedding night.
Stories about the wedding night are common in movies and literature.
