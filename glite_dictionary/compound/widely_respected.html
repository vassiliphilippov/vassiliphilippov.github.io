widely respected

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪdli rɪˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdli rɪˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
regarded with great admiration or esteem by a large number of people
esteemed or admired by many people

Examples

The professor is widely respected in his field for his groundbreaking work.
Her honest approach to leadership made her widely respected among colleagues.
The company is widely respected for its commitment to ethical practices.

Synset Examples

She is widely respected for her research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highly regarded - regarded with high esteem or approval
  • Antonym: disreputable - not respected; having a bad reputation
