fish ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
a small, round ball or patty made of flaked fish and mashed potatoes, typically fried or boiled and served as a snack or in soup
fried or boiled ball of fish and potatoes

Examples

He ordered a bowl of soup with three fish balls floating in it.
The chef prepared crispy fish balls using fresh cod and herbs.
Fish balls are a popular street food in many Southeast Asian countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishcake - a patty of flaked fish mixed with potato and seasonings

2. NOUN

/ fɪʃ bɔl / (AmE), / fɪʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
A small, round ball made from ground fish, often mixed with other ingredients and seasonings, and usually cooked by boiling or frying.
ball of ground fish, often seasoned

Examples

He ordered a bowl of soup containing several fish balls for lunch.
Fish balls are a popular street food in many Asian countries.
The chef explained how to prepare fish balls using fresh cod and herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishcake - a small cake or patty made from fish and other ingredients
