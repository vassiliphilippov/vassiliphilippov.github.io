pride of California

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪd əv ˌkælɪˈfɔrnjə / (AmE), / ˈpraɪd əv ˌkælɪˈfɔːnɪə / (BrE)
a shrubby perennial plant native to California, with large pink or violet flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental.
ornamental California perennial plant

Examples

The pride of California adds vibrant color to the garden each spring.
Many horticulturists recommend planting pride of California for its striking appearance and resilience.
She admired the blossoming pride of California lining the path through the botanical garden.
