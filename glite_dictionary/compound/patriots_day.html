Patriot's Day

1. PROPN

/ ˈpeɪtriəts deɪ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtriəts deɪ / (BrE)
a public holiday observed on the third Monday in April in Massachusetts and Maine, commemorating the battles of Lexington and Concord in 1775
public holiday in MA and ME commemorating 1775 battles
american

Examples

Many schools in Massachusetts close for Patriot's Day each April.
The Boston Marathon is traditionally held on Patriot's Day.
She traveled to Maine to witness the Patriot's Day reenactments.
