breeze block

1. NOUN

/ briːz blɑk / (AmE), / briːz blɒk / (BrE)
a lightweight concrete block made with cinder or similar aggregate, used in building construction
lightweight concrete building block
british

Examples

They built the garden wall out of breeze blocks for a quick, sturdy solution.
Stacks of breeze blocks were delivered to the construction site this morning.
He painted the breeze block shed to make it look more attractive.

Synset Examples

cinder blocks are called ‘breeze blocks’ in Britain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinder block - a lightweight building block made from concrete and cinders
