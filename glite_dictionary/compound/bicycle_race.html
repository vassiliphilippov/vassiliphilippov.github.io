bicycle race

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkəl reɪs / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ reɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which people ride bicycles to see who finishes first
competition involving bicycle riding

Examples

He trained for months to win the annual bicycle race in his hometown.
The bicycle race attracted participants from all over the world this year.
During the bicycle race, several riders broke away from the main group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition of speed
