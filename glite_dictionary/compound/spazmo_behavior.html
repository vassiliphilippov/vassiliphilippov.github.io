spazmo behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈspæzmoʊ bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈspæzməʊ bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Clumsy or overly energetic behavior, often in a way that draws attention or causes amusement. Usually used informally or humorously.
clumsy or hyperactive behavior
informal slang

Her spazmo behavior made everyone laugh at the party.

Etymology

Formed from 'spaz', an informal/slang term for a clumsy or hyperactive person, plus '-mo' as a playful suffix.
