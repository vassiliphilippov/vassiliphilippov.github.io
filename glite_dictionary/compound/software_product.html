software product

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔft.wɛr ˈprɑd.əkt / (AmE), / ˈsɒft.weə ˈprɒd.ʌkt / (BrE)
a computer program or suite of programs that is developed and sold as a commercial product
commercially sold computer program

Examples

The company launched a new software product aimed at improving project management for small businesses.
You must purchase a license before you can legally use the software product.
After months of testing, the development team finally released their software product to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: application - a program designed to perform a particular function
  • Synonym: software package - a collection of related programs sold as a unit
