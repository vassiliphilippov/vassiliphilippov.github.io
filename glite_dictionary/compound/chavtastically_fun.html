chavtastically fun

1. ADJ

/ tʃævˈtæstɪkli fʌn / (AmE), / tʃævˈtæstɪkli fʌn / (BrE)
Extremely enjoyable in a way that is characteristic of chav style or culture.
extremely enjoyable in chav style
informal slang british

Examples

The party was chavtastically fun, with flashy outfits and loud music all night.
Everyone agreed the theme park trip was chavtastically fun from start to finish.
Her birthday celebration was chavtastically fun, complete with glitter and pop music.

Synset Examples

The event was chavtastically fun, filled with laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chavtastic - excellent or impressive in a chav way
  • Synonym: fun - amusing, entertaining, or enjoyable

Etymology

Blend of 'chav' (British slang for a stereotype of lower-class youth) and 'fantastically fun'.
