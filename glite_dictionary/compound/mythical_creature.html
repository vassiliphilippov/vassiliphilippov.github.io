mythical creature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈkriːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪkəl ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
an imaginary being or monster from folklore, legend, or mythology, often with supernatural powers
imaginary being from myth or folklore

Examples

The unicorn is a well-known mythical creature that has fascinated people for centuries.
Many cultures have tales featuring at least one kind of mythical creature guarding treasure.
He dressed as a mythical creature for the fantasy-themed party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legendary creature - a creature from legend or traditional stories
