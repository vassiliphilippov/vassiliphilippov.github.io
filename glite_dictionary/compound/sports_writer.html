sports writer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a journalist who specializes in writing about sports events and topics
journalist specializing in sports

Examples

The sports writer covered the championship game with detailed analysis and insightful interviews.
As a sports writer, she traveled to different cities to report on major tournaments.
He became a renowned sports writer after publishing his book on baseball history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
