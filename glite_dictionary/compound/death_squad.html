death squad

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ ˌskwɑd / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌskwɒd / (BrE)
a clandestine group, often officially sanctioned, that carries out the killing of political opponents or suspected criminals, typically without legal proceedings
secret group that kills political targets

Examples

The notorious death squad operated at night, targeting government critics with impunity.
Several human rights organizations have documented death squad activities in the region.
In the 1980s, journalists risked their lives to expose death squad atrocities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit squad - a group assigned to kill a specific person
