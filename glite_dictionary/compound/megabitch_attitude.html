megabitch attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛɡəˌbɪtʃ ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəˌbɪtʃ ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An extremely negative, hostile, or aggressively unpleasant demeanor or manner.
extremely negative or hostile demeanor
informal offensive

Examples

No one wanted to work with her because of her megabitch attitude.
His critique was fair, but her megabitch attitude made it hard to accept.
The team's progress stalled when a megabitch attitude began to dominate meetings.

Synset Examples

That megabitch attitude isn't helping her career.

Related Terms
