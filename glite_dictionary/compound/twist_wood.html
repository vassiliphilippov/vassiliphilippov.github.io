twist wood

1. NOUN

/ twɪst wʊd / (AmE), / twɪst wʊd / (BrE)
A vigorous deciduous European shrub commonly found along roadsides, bearing red berries that turn black as they ripen.
European deciduous shrub with berries

Examples

We found a large twist wood growing near the old stone wall.
The berries of the twist wood change from red to black as they mature.
Gardeners often plant twist wood for its attractive foliage and berries.
