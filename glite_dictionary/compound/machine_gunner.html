machine gunner

1. NOUN

/ məˈʃin ˌɡʌnər / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn ˌɡʌnə / (BrE)
a member of the armed forces trained to operate a machine gun
soldier who operates a machine gun

Examples

The machine gunner took cover behind the sandbags during the attack.
A skilled machine gunner can provide vital support to advancing infantry.
During training, each machine gunner learned to assemble and disassemble the weapon quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunner - a member of the military who operates a gun or artillery piece
