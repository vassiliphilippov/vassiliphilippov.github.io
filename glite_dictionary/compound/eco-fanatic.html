eco-fanatic

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːkoʊ fəˈnætɪk / (AmE), / ˈiːkəʊ fəˈnætɪk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic about environmental causes and committed to environmentally friendly living, sometimes with excessive zeal.
zealous supporter of environmentalism

Examples

Some people see him as an eco-fanatic because he refuses to drive a car or fly in airplanes.
The company hired an eco-fanatic to lead its sustainability initiatives.
Critics claimed she was an eco-fanatic, but her passion inspired many to make greener choices.

Synset Examples

Being an ecofanatic, he composts everything possible.
Her ecofanatic lifestyle inspires others to go green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmentalist - a person who is concerned with or advocates for environmental protection
  • Synonym: eco-warrior - someone who actively works to protect the environment
  • Antonym: eco-skeptic - someone who is doubtful or critical of environmental issues or environmentalism

Etymology

Formed from 'eco-', relating to the environment, and 'fanatic', from Latin ‘fanaticus’ (inspired by a deity, frenzied).
