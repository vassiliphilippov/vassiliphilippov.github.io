up-and-coming artist

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person in the arts who is becoming prominent or likely to achieve success soon
emerging talent in the arts

Examples

The gallery features work by several up-and-coming artists from the region.
As an up-and-coming artist, she is often invited to participate in local exhibitions.
Many critics consider him the most promising up-and-coming artist in contemporary photography.

Synset Examples

She is an up-and-coming artist in the local scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rising star - a person who is quickly gaining fame or prominence
