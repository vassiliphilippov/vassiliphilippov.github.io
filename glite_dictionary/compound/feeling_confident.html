feeling confident

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈkɑːnfɪdənt / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ ˈkɒnfɪdənt / (BrE)
having a strong belief in one's abilities or worth; self-assured
having self-assurance; self-confident

Examples

She walked into the interview feeling confident about her qualifications.
After weeks of practice, he was finally feeling confident on stage.
I am feeling confident that this project will succeed.
