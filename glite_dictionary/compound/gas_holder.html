gas holder

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a large, airtight container, typically spherical or cylindrical, used for storing gas until it is needed as fuel
container for storing fuel gas

Examples

The old gas holder near the railway station is now a city landmark.
Engineers inspected the gas holder to ensure it was safe for continued use.
The skyline was once dominated by a huge gas holder serving the local industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gasometer - a container for measuring or storing gas
