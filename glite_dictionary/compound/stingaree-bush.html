stingaree-bush

1. NOUN

/ ˌstɪŋəˈriː bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˌstɪŋəˈriː bʊʃ / (BrE)
a spiny evergreen drought-resistant shrub with showy rose and purple flowers, forming dense thickets on dry, rocky slopes, especially in California
spiny evergreen California shrub

Examples

The stingaree-bush thrives on the rocky slopes of southern California, adding color to the arid landscape.
Hikers are advised to avoid dense thickets of stingaree-bush because of its spiny branches.
Each spring, the stingaree-bush is covered with vibrant rose and purple blooms.
