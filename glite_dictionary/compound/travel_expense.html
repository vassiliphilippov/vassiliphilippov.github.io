travel expense

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ɪkˌspɛns / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ɪkˌspɛns / (BrE)
an amount of money spent on travel for work purposes, typically paid by the employer
costs related to work travel

Examples

Employees must submit all travel expenses within one week of returning from their trip.
My company covers travel expenses for conferences and client meetings.
She kept detailed receipts of her travel expenses for reimbursement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business expense - money spent in the course of business that is deductible or reimbursable
