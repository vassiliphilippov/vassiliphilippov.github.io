Christmas cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs keɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs keɪk / (BrE)
a rich fruitcake, typically covered with icing and marzipan, traditionally eaten at Christmas
rich fruitcake eaten at Christmas

Examples

Her grandmother bakes a delicious Christmas cake every year for the whole family.
Traditionally, a Christmas cake is decorated with white icing and festive figurines.
We enjoyed a slice of Christmas cake with our afternoon tea on Christmas Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruitcake - a cake made with dried fruits and nuts, sometimes soaked in spirits
