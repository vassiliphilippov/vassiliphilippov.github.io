camera flash

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæm(ə)rə flæʃ / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə flæʃ / (BrE)
a device or burst of light on a camera used to illuminate a scene when taking a photograph
device or light for camera photos

Examples

The camera flash startled everyone at the party.
Please turn off your camera flash during the performance.
The photographer used a powerful camera flash to brighten the dark room.

Synset Examples
