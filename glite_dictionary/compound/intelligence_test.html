intelligence test

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns tɛst / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns tɛst / (BrE)
a standardized test designed to measure a person's intelligence or intellectual ability
test of intelligence or mental ability

Examples

The school administered an intelligence test as part of the student assessment process.
He scored surprisingly well on the intelligence test given by the psychologist.
Some people believe that an intelligence test cannot capture all aspects of human intellect.

Synset Examples

they used to think that intelligence is what an intelligence test tests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: IQ test - a test designed to measure a person's intelligence quotient (IQ)
