hunting guide

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ɡaɪd / (BrE)
a person whose job is to guide and assist hunters, especially in unfamiliar areas
person who guides hunters

Examples

The experienced hunting guide led us deep into the forest before dawn.
If you’re new to the area, hiring a hunting guide is strongly recommended.
Our hunting guide knew exactly where to find deer during the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guide - a person who leads or shows the way to others
