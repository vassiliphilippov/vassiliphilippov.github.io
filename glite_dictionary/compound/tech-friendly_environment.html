tech-friendly environment

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈfrɛndli ɪnˌvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / tɛk ˈfrɛndli ɪnˌvaɪrənmənt / (BrE)
An environment that is supportive of technology and encourages innovation.
supportive place for technology and innovation

Examples

The university has created a tech-friendly environment to attract talented students and faculty.
Government policies can help cultivate a tech-friendly environment for emerging industries.
Employees are more motivated and productive in a tech-friendly environment.

Synset Examples

Many startups thrive in E-stonia's tech-friendly environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology-friendly - welcoming or receptive to technology
  • Synonym: innovative - featuring new methods; advanced and original
