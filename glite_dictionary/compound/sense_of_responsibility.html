sense of responsibility

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv rɪˌspɑnsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / sɛns əv rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the feeling or awareness that you are obliged to do something or take care of someone or something
awareness of obligations or duties

Examples

She has always shown a strong sense of responsibility toward her younger siblings.
His sense of responsibility made him the right choice for team leader.
A good manager tries to instill a sense of responsibility in all employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsibility - the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something or someone
