Barbiefication of women

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɑr.bi.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən ʌv ˈwɪm.ɪn / (AmE), / ˌbɑː.bi.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən ɒv ˈwɪm.ɪn / (BrE)
The portrayal or treatment of women according to unrealistic, idealized physical standards based on Barbie dolls.
idealized representation of women based on Barbie

Examples

Many critics argue that the Barbiefication of women contributes to unrealistic beauty standards in society.
The fashion industry has been accused of promoting the Barbiefication of women through advertising.
Her documentary explores the lasting effects of the Barbiefication of women on young girls' self-esteem.

Synset Examples

She criticized the Barbiefication of women in popular culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: objectification of women - treating women as objects for sexual or aesthetic pleasure rather than as people
  • Synonym: sexualization of women - the act of making something or someone sexual in character or quality, especially women
  • Antonym: empowerment of women - the process of increasing the strength and confidence of women

Etymology

From 'Barbie' (the fashion doll brand launched by Mattel in 1959) + '-fication' (from Latin 'facere', to make).
