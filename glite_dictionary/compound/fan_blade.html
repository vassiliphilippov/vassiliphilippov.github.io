fan blade

1. NOUN

/ fæn bleɪd / (AmE), / fæn bleɪd / (BrE)
a flat, narrow part of a rotating fan that moves air when the fan operates
blade of a rotating fan

Examples

The fan blade broke off and needed to be replaced.
After years of use, dust had collected on each fan blade.
Make sure the fan blade is securely attached before turning on the fan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blade - a flat, cutting or thrusting part of an implement or weapon
