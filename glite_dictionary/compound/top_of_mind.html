top of mind

1. ADJ

/ tɑp əv maɪnd / (AmE), / tɒp əv maɪnd / (BrE)
(of a subject or issue) considered to be the first or most important in someone's thoughts or priorities.
main priority or concern

Examples

Sustainability is always top of mind for our company when designing new products.
With the election approaching, economic issues are top of mind for many voters.
Keeping customer trust top of mind is essential for long-term success.

Synset Examples

Her health remains a top of mind concern for her family.
Keep safety at the top of mind during the project.
Customer satisfaction is always top of mind for us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foremost - most prominent or important
  • Synonym: priority - something regarded as more important than another
  • Antonym: afterthought - an idea occurring later or considered less important
