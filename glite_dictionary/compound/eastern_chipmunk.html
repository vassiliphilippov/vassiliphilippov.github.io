eastern chipmunk

1. NOUN

/ ˈistɚn ˈtʃɪpˌmʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈtʃɪpmʌŋk / (BrE)
a small North American rodent with distinctive dark and light stripes along its back and large cheek pouches, found in the eastern United States and Canada
striped rodent native to eastern North America

Examples

We spotted an eastern chipmunk darting across the forest floor gathering seeds.
The eastern chipmunk stores food in its cheek pouches for the winter.
Children enjoyed watching a curious eastern chipmunk near the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chipmunk - a small squirrel-like rodent with cheek pouches and stripes
