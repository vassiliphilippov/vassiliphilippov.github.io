king penguin

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (BrE)
a large species of penguin (Aptenodytes patagonicus) found on islands near the Antarctic Circle, known for its striking orange markings and upright posture
large penguin species near Antarctica

Examples

The king penguin is known for its bright orange head patches.
During their breeding season, thousands of king penguins gather on remote islands.
We observed a group of king penguins waddling along the shore.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: emperor penguin - the largest species of penguin, native to Antarctica
