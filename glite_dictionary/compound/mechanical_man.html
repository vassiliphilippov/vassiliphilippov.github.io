mechanical man

1. NOUN

/ məˈkænɪkəl mæn / (AmE), / mɪˈkænɪkəl man / (BrE)
an automaton or robot designed to resemble a human being in appearance or function
a humanoid robot or automaton

Examples

The museum displayed a mechanical man built in the nineteenth century to amuse visitors.
Early science fiction often featured a mechanical man as a servant or protector.
She described her invention as a mechanical man capable of playing chess autonomously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • Synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex tasks automatically
