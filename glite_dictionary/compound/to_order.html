to order

1. ADV

/ tə ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / tə ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
made or done according to the specific requirements or instructions of a customer, rather than in advance or in standard sizes.
made to specific requirements

Examples

Many wedding dresses are designed and sewn to order.
The carpenter built the cabinets to order for our unique kitchen layout.
Those suits are made to order, not sold off the rack.

Synset Examples

he had the shoes made to order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custom-made - made according to the specifications of an individual buyer
  • Antonym: off the shelf - produced in standard sizes or forms, not custom made
