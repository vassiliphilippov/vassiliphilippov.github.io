hunting season

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌsiːzən / (BrE)
A legally designated period during which it is permitted to hunt and kill a particular species of animal.
legally permitted hunting period

Examples

The hunting season for deer begins in early November.
Local authorities announced that the hunting season would be shortened this year.
During hunting season, the forests are filled with hunters from around the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: open season - time when hunting certain animals is legally allowed
