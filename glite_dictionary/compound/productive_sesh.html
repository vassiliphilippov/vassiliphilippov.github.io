productive sesh

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʌktɪv sɛʃ / (AmE), / prəˈdʌktɪv sɛʃ / (BrE)
an informal term for a session that is highly effective or yields worthwhile results
informal: effective or worthwhile session
informal

Examples

After three hours of brainstorming, we finally wrapped up a productive sesh.
That was the most productive sesh we've had all month, with everyone contributing ideas.
Let's hope tomorrow's meeting is as much of a productive sesh as today.

Synset Examples

It was a productive sesh with lots of insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: productive session - a session that yields beneficial results
  • Antonym: unproductive sesh - an informal term for a session that was not effective or worthwhile

Etymology

Productive from Latin 'productivus'; 'sesh' is a slang clipping of 'session'.
