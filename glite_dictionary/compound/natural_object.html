natural object

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚl ˈɑbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈɒbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
an object that exists in nature and has not been made or significantly altered by humans
object found in nature, not man-made

Examples

The geologist collected several natural objects like rocks and shells for study.
Unlike art sculptures, natural objects have not been shaped by human hands.
Some museums have entire exhibits dedicated to unique natural objects such as minerals or fossils.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: artifact - an object made or modified by humans
