iron tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərn ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˌtriː / (BrE)
a small, slow-growing deciduous tree of northern Iran, known for its low domed shape and very hard wood
deciduous tree of northern Iran

Examples

The leaves of the iron tree turn a brilliant red in autumn.
Due to its dense wood, the iron tree is often used for making durable tools.
Botanists study the iron tree for its unique adaptation to arid climates.
