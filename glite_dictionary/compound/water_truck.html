water truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
A truck equipped with a tank for transporting and dispensing water, commonly used for dust control, irrigation, or firefighting.
truck for transporting and dispensing water

Examples

The city sent a water truck to spray the dusty roads during the summer.
Workers filled the swimming pool using a water truck after the drought restrictions were lifted.
A water truck was deployed to help control the wildfire in the remote area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank truck - a truck designed to carry liquids or gases in bulk
