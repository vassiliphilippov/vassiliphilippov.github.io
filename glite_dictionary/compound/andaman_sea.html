Andaman Sea

1. NOUN

/ ˈændəmæn si / (AmE), / ˈandəmæn siː / (BrE)
a part of the northeastern Indian Ocean, lying to the west of the Malay Peninsula and east of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, forming the southeastern part of the Bay of Bengal.
a sea west of the Malay Peninsula, part of the Bay of Bengal

Examples

The Andaman Sea is known for its clear turquoise waters and beautiful coral reefs.
Several islands in the Andaman Sea are popular tourist destinations in Thailand and Myanmar.
During the monsoon season, storms frequently sweep across the Andaman Sea.
