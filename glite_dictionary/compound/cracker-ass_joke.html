cracker-ass joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækər æs dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈkrækə ˌɑːs ˈdʒəʊk / (BrE)
An extremely poor, unfunny, or awkward joke, often used derisively or dismissively.
very poor or unfunny joke
slang offensive american

Examples

Nobody laughed at his cracker-ass joke during the meeting.
Samantha rolled her eyes when Tom tried to break the ice with a cracker-ass joke.
I've heard enough of your cracker-ass jokes for one night.

Synset Examples

That cracker-ass joke fell flat at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad joke - a joke that is not funny or successful
  • Degree Lower: pun - a play on words used as a joke (sometimes considered corny or bad)

Etymology

From 'cracker-ass', a derogatory slang term for a foolish or contemptible person (originally an insult against poor white Southerners), combined with 'joke'.
