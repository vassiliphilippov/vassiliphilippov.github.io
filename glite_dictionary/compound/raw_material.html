raw material

1. NOUN

/ rɔː məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / rɔː məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
A basic substance in its natural state, used to produce goods, finished products, or energy through manufacturing or processing.
basic substance used in manufacturing

Examples

Cotton is an important raw material in the textile industry.
The company imports raw materials to manufacture its goods locally.
Steel production relies heavily on the availability of quality raw materials.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: finished product - an item that has been manufactured from raw materials
