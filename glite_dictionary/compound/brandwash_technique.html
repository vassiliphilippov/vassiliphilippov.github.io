brandwash technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrændˌwɑʃ tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈbrændˌwɒʃ tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
A method used to manipulate or influence public perception of a brand, often through marketing or advertising strategies.
method to manipulate brand perception

Examples

The company unveiled a new brandwash technique to reshape their public image after the scandal.
Many firms invest heavily in brandwash techniques to maintain consumer loyalty.
Critics argue that this brandwash technique creates unrealistic expectations among customers.

Synset Examples

They used brandwash techniques to boost their image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to brainwash - to manipulate or alter someone's beliefs or perceptions
  • Synonym: advertising campaign - a series of advertisement activities for promoting a product or brand

Etymology

Blend of 'brand' and 'brainwash', plus 'technique' from French 'technique' (art, skill).
