genus Xiphophorus

1. NOUN

/ ˌzaɪfəˈfɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˌzɪfəˈfɔːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of small freshwater fish native to Central America, including popular aquarium species known as swordtails and platies.
genus of swordtail and platy fish

Examples

The genus Xiphophorus includes several species prized by aquarium hobbyists.
Many biologists study the genetics of fish in the genus Xiphophorus.
Swordtail fish, belonging to the genus Xiphophorus, are known for their distinctive tail shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swordtail - a small freshwater fish of the genus Xiphophorus, popular in aquariums

Etymology

From Greek xiphos (sword) and phoros (bearing).
