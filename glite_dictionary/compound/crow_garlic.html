crow garlic

1. NOUN

/ kɹoʊ ˈɡɑɹ.lɪk / (AmE), / krəʊ ˈɡɑː.lɪk / (BrE)
a wild plant of the onion family with a strong, pungent smell, native to Europe and western Asia (Allium vineale)
wild onion-like plant

Examples

The field was dotted with crow garlic swaying in the breeze.
Farmers sometimes consider crow garlic a troublesome weed in pastures.
You can smell the strong aroma of crow garlic after the rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild garlic - another wild plant in the onion family (Allium ursinum)
