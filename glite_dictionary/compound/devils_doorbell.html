devil's doorbell

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɔrbɛl / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɔːbɛl / (BrE)
a metaphor for a sound or signal that suggests the coming of trouble or danger
ominous sound or signal

Examples

The haunting sound of the devil's doorbell echoed in the night, sending shivers down my spine.
Superstitious villagers avoided the church at midnight, fearing they'd hear the devil's doorbell.
Each unexpected creak in the old house felt like the devil's doorbell ringing out a warning.
