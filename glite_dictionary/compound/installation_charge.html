installation charge

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a fee that is charged for installing equipment, machinery, or services
fee for installing something

Examples

There is an installation charge for setting up the new cable service.
The appliance came with a free installation charge as part of the promotion.
Customers often complain about the high installation charge for home security systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: installation fee - a fee charged for installation
  • Degree Higher: service charge - a fee charged for services rendered
