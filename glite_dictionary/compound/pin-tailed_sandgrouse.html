pin-tailed sandgrouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪn teɪld ˈsænd ɡraʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɪn teɪld ˈsænd ɡraʊs / (BrE)
A species of sandgrouse found in Europe and Africa, known for its elongated central tail feathers.
a sandgrouse species with long central tail feathers

Examples

The pin-tailed sandgrouse is known for its distinctive long tail feathers.
During spring, large flocks of pin-tailed sandgrouse gather at watering holes across the savannah.
Researchers spotted a pin-tailed sandgrouse nesting in the Spanish countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandgrouse - any bird of the family Pteroclidae, native to arid regions
