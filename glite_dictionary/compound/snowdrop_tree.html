snowdrop tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌdrɑp tri / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌdrɒp triː / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to the southeastern United States, known for its showy white flowers resembling those of the snowdrop.
tree with snowdrop-like white flowers

Examples

The snowdrop tree is often planted for its attractive white blossoms in spring.
Gardeners in the South appreciate the snowdrop tree for its ornamental value.
The snowdrop tree thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
