Ivory Coast

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪvəri koʊst / (AmE), / ˈaɪvəri kəʊst / (BrE)
A country in western Africa, officially known as Côte d'Ivoire, located on the Gulf of Guinea.
country in western Africa

Examples

The president of Ivory Coast addressed the nation in a televised speech.
Tourism in Ivory Coast has grown steadily over the last decade.
She spent her childhood in Ivory Coast before moving to Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Côte d'Ivoire - official French name for Ivory Coast

Etymology

Named for its historic involvement in the ivory trade; also known as Côte d'Ivoire (French for 'Ivory Coast').
