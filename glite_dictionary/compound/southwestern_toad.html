southwestern toad

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛstərn ˈtoʊd / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛstən ˈtəʊd / (BrE)
a stocky, uniformly warty toad found near washes and streams in the semiarid southwestern United States
warty toad of the southwestern US

Examples

The southwestern toad can often be seen basking on rocks near desert streams.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the southwestern toad in New Mexico.
Unlike other species, the southwestern toad thrives in semiarid environments.
