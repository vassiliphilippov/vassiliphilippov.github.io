supple willow

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌpəl ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsʌpəl ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a willow tree or its branches known for being flexible and easily bent without breaking, often used in basket-making and weaving
flexible, pliable willow tree or branches

Examples

The artisan chose supple willow to weave an intricate basket.
Long lengths of supple willow are ideal for constructing curved garden arches.
The supple willow bent gracefully in the wind but did not snap.

Synset Examples

He crafted a basket from the supple willow branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - a type of tree with slender, flexible branches
