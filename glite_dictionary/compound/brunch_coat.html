brunch coat

1. NOUN

/ brʌntʃ koʊt / (AmE), / brʌntʃ kəʊt / (BrE)
a woman's short, informal housecoat or wrapper, typically worn at home over other clothes
a woman's short housecoat

Examples

After breakfast, she slipped into her brunch coat to tidy up the kitchen.
The elegant brunch coat was a popular choice for women in the 1950s.
She answered the door in her floral brunch coat, surprised to see visitors so early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housecoat - a woman's robe for wear around the house
