stinking clover

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈkloʊvər / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a plant of western North America with trifoliate leaves and white or pink spider-shaped flowers, sometimes used as an ornamental
a plant with trifoliate leaves and spider-shaped flowers

Examples

The stinking clover brightened the roadside with its delicate pink blooms.
Gardeners sometimes plant stinking clover as an ornamental in mixed borders.
I recognized the stinking clover by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and unusual scent.
