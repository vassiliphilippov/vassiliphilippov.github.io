whiskey on the rocks

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski ɑn ðə rɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ɒn ðə rɒks / (BrE)
whiskey served with ice cubes but without any other mixers.
whiskey served over ice

Examples

He ordered a whiskey on the rocks to unwind after a long day at work.
Would you like your whiskey on the rocks or neat?
She sipped her whiskey on the rocks while chatting with friends at the bar.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: whiskey neat - whiskey served plain without ice or mixers
