to find you well

1. VERB

/ faɪnd ju wɛl / (AmE), / faɪnd juː wɛl / (BrE)
used, especially in written communication, to express the hope that the person being addressed is in good health
express hope for someone's good health (in correspondence)

Examples

I hope this message finds you well after your recent move.
She began the email with 'I trust this letter finds you well.'
Whenever I receive a letter that says 'finds you well,' it makes me smile.

Synset Examples

Dear Joy, I hope this letter finds you well.
