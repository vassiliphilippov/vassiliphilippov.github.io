air conditioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr kənˌdɪʃənər / (AmE), / ˈeə kəndɪʃənə / (BrE)
A machine or system that cools and dries the air in a room or building.
device that cools and dries air

Examples

The hotel room was pleasantly cool thanks to the air conditioner.
She bought a new air conditioner for her apartment during the summer heatwave.
Without an air conditioner, the office became unbearable in July.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air conditioning - the process or system of cooling and drying the air

2. NOUN

/ ˈɛr kənˌdɪʃənər / (AmE), / ˈeə kənˌdɪʃənə / (BrE)
A machine or system that cools and often dehumidifies the air in a room or building.
machine that cools indoor air

Examples

The air conditioner keeps the office comfortable during the hot summer months.
I bought a new air conditioner to help with the humidity in my apartment.
After the old air conditioner broke, the house became unbearably stuffy.

Synset Examples

we rarely turn on the air-conditioner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air conditioning - the process or system of controlling air temperature and humidity
