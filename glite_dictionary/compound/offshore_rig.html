offshore rig

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔfˌʃɔr rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈɒfʃɔː rɪɡ / (BrE)
a large structure situated in the sea, either floating or fixed to the seabed, equipped for drilling wells to extract oil or gas beneath the ocean floor
structure for drilling oil or gas at sea

Examples

The workers spent three weeks on the offshore rig before returning home.
New safety regulations have been introduced for every offshore rig in the region.
A powerful storm forced the evacuation of an offshore rig last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil platform - a large structure used to extract and process oil and gas from beneath the sea
  • Synonym: drilling rig - a machine or structure that creates holes in the earth's surface for oil or gas
