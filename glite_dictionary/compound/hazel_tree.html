hazel tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪzəl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl ˌtriː / (BrE)
An Australian tree cultivated for its ornamental value, fine-grained wood, and edible nuts.
Australian tree with edible nuts and fine wood

Examples

The hazel tree in their garden produces an abundance of delicious nuts every year.
Furniture makers value the wood of the hazel tree for its fine texture.
They planted a hazel tree in their yard for both its beauty and its edible nuts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazel - a type of small deciduous tree with edible nuts
