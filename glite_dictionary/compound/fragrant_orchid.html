fragrant orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A European orchid with dense spikes of fragrant pink, lilac, or red flowers, notable for its conspicuous spurs.
a European orchid with fragrant, colorful flowers

Examples

The fragrant orchid is often admired for its lovely scent and beautiful colors in wild meadows.
We spotted several fragrant orchids blooming along the hiking trail in June.
Gardeners appreciate the fragrant orchid for its vibrant flowers and sweet aroma.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: orchid - a plant of the Orchidaceae family with complex flowers
