information technology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.fɚˈmeɪ.ʃən tɛkˈnɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən tɛkˈnɒ.lə.dʒi / (BrE)
the study or use of systems (especially computers and telecommunications) for storing, retrieving, and sending information
computing and telecommunications systems

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in information technology after graduating from university.
The company's information technology department upgraded all the servers last weekend.
Modern businesses rely heavily on information technology to manage their data and communications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer science - the study of computers and computational systems
