chest pain

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛst peɪn / (AmE), / tʃɛst peɪn / (BrE)
Discomfort or pain felt in the chest, often indicating an underlying medical condition.
pain or discomfort in the chest
medical

Examples

He went to the emergency room because he was experiencing severe chest pain.
The doctor asked if she had any chest pain after exercising.
Sudden chest pain should never be ignored, as it can signal a serious problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angina - chest pain caused by reduced blood flow to the heart
  • Degree Higher: heart attack - sudden and severe chest pain caused by interruption of blood supply to the heart
