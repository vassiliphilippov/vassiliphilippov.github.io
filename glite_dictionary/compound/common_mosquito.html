common mosquito

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑm.ən məˈskitoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən məˈskiːtəʊ / (BrE)
A species of mosquito, especially the Culex pipiens, that is commonly found in houses and is known for biting humans.
species of house mosquito, Culex pipiens

Examples

The common mosquito is often found breeding in standing water near houses.
Most bites from the common mosquito are annoying but not dangerous.
Scientists study the habits of the common mosquito to control the spread of diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mosquito - small flying insect that feeds on blood
