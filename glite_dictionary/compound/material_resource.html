material resource

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɪriəl rɪˈsɔrs / (AmE), / məˈtɪəriəl rɪˈzɔːs / (BrE)
a tangible asset or possession, such as equipment or raw materials, used to achieve a purpose or meet a need
tangible asset or possession

Examples

The company relied on its material resources to expand production and meet growing demand.
During the disaster, the government distributed material resources such as food and medical supplies.
Many organizations must manage their material resources carefully to remain financially stable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resource - something that can be used for support or help
