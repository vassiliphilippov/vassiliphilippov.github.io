baffle board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæfəl bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbæfəl bɔːd / (BrE)
a flat plate used to control or direct the flow of fluid or energy, especially in engineering or acoustic applications
plate directing flow of fluid or energy

Examples

The loudspeaker's baffle board was designed to minimize sound distortion.
Engineers installed a baffle board to direct airflow in the ventilation system.
Water flows evenly around the baffle board inside the filter tank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baffle - a device that regulates the flow of a fluid or energy

Etymology

From 'baffle' (to restrain or regulate) + 'board' (flat object); technical usage emerged in the 20th century.
