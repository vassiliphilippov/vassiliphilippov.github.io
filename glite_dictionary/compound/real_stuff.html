real stuff

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiəl stʌf / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl stʌf / (BrE)
something that is genuine or authentic, especially as opposed to an imitation or substitute
something authentic or genuine
informal

Examples

This wine is the real stuff, aged for twenty years in French oak barrels.
After years of cheap imitations, she finally got her hands on the real stuff.
If you want to impress them, bring the real stuff to the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine article - something that is truly what it is said to be
  • Antonym: fake - something made to look real in order to trick people
