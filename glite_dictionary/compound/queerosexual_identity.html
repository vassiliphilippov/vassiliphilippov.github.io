queerosexual identity

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɪəroʊˈsɛkʃuəl aɪˈdɛntəti / (AmE), / ˌkwɪərəʊˈsɛkʃuəl aɪˈdɛntɪti / (BrE)
a sexual identity that encompasses and embraces a range of diverse sexual orientations beyond traditional categories
embracing diverse sexual orientations

Examples

She feels empowered by her queerosexual identity and the sense of community it provides.
His art explores the complexities inherent in a queerosexual identity.
Discussions about queerosexual identity are becoming more visible in academic circles.

Synset Examples

Her queerosexual identity allows her to love freely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queer - denoting or relating to a non-normative sexual or gender identity
  • Synonym: sexual identity - a person's identification with a particular sexual orientation

Etymology

From 'queer' (slang for non-normative sexual/gender identities) + 'sexual' + 'identity'.
