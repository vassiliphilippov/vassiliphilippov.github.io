aquatic bird

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwɑːtɪk bɝːd / (AmE), / əˈkwætɪk bɜːd / (BrE)
A bird that lives on or around water and is adapted for swimming, wading, or diving.
bird adapted for life in water

Examples

Many species of aquatic bird migrate thousands of miles each year to find suitable breeding grounds.
The wetland sanctuary provides a habitat for a wide variety of aquatic birds.
Swans are a familiar type of aquatic bird known for their graceful movements on water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterfowl - a bird that lives on or around water
  • Antonym: land bird - a bird that primarily lives on land
