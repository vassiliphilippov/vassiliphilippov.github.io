accaroid resin

1. NOUN

/ ˈækəˌrɔɪd ˈrɛzɪn / (AmE), / ˈækərɔɪd ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
an alcohol-soluble resin obtained from certain Australian trees, used in varnishes and in making paper
resin from Australian trees used in varnishes and paper

Examples

The factory imported accaroid resin from Australia to use in high-quality varnishes.
Paper manufacturers sometimes add accaroid resin to improve the strength of their products.
The distinctive scent of accaroid resin is noticeable during the varnish-making process.
