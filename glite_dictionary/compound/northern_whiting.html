northern whiting

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðɚn ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a species of whiting found along the east coast of the United States, closely resembling the king whiting
a fish found on the US east coast

Examples

Fishermen often catch northern whiting in the shallow waters off New Jersey.
The northern whiting is prized for its delicate flavor and flaky texture.
Marine biologists study the population trends of northern whiting along the Atlantic coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: king whiting - a similar species of whiting fish
