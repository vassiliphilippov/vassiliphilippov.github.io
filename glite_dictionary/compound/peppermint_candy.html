peppermint candy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpərmɪnt ˈkændi / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəmɪnt ˈkændi / (BrE)
A sweet confection flavored with peppermint oil, often enjoyed as a hard candy or mint.
sweet flavored with peppermint oil

Examples

She offered me a peppermint candy after dinner to freshen my breath.
The jar on the counter was filled with red and white peppermint candies.
He enjoys sucking on a peppermint candy when he needs a little pick-me-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mint - any confection flavored with mint
