veterinary surgeon

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛtəˌnɛri ˈsɝdʒən / (AmE), / ˌvɛtərɪnəri ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A qualified doctor who diagnoses and treats diseases, injuries, and disorders in animals; a veterinarian.
animal doctor; veterinarian
british

Examples

After earning her degree, she started working as a veterinary surgeon at a local animal hospital.
The horse's injury required a skilled veterinary surgeon to perform the operation.
Many pet owners trust their veterinary surgeon for advice on their animals' health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veterinarian - a person qualified to treat diseased or injured animals
  • Synonym: vet - informal term for veterinary surgeon or veterinarian

Etymology

From Latin 'veterinarius' (of cattle) and 'surgeon,' from Old French 'cirurgien'.
