African monitor

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈmɑː.nɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈmɒ.nɪ.tə / (BrE)
a large, carnivorous lizard of the genus Varanus found in Africa, known for its robust build and predatory habits
large lizard native to Africa

Examples

The African monitor is renowned for its agility and strength in the wild.
Researchers observed a African monitor raiding a crocodile’s nest for eggs.
Young African monitors grow quickly, reaching impressive sizes within a few years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor lizard - any large lizard of the genus Varanus
