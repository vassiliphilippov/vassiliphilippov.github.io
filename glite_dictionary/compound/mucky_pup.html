mucky pup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌki pʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌki pʌp / (BrE)
an affectionate term for a child or dog who is dirty or messy
dirty or messy child or dog (informal British)
informal british

Examples

Sophie returned home after football practice looking like a real mucky pup.
Don't let the mucky pup jump on the sofa while he's still wet!
Grandma shook her head and called me a mucky pup when she saw my muddy boots.

Synset Examples

He laughed, calling his son a mucky pup after the soccer match.
After playing in the rain, my dog became a mucky pup.
The kids returned from the park looking like little mucky pups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messy - untidy or dirty in appearance
  • Antonym: clean - free from dirt or mess, the opposite of dirty
