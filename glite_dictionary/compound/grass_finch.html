grass finch

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a common North American finch known for its evening song
North American finch, evening singer

Examples

We watched a grass finch perch on the fence as dusk settled over the field.
The cheerful song of the grass finch filled the garden every evening in summer.
Birdwatchers delight in spotting the colorful grass finch during their spring migrations.

/ ɡræs fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs fɪntʃ / (BrE)

Examples

Many bird enthusiasts admire the vibrant colors of the grass finch in their aviaries.
The grass finch thrives in grassland habitats across Australia.
She bought a pair of grass finches to add to her collection of exotic birds.
