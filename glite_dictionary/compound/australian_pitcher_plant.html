Australian pitcher plant

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪljən ˈpɪtʃər plænt / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a carnivorous perennial herb native to western Australia, characterized by green, pitcher-shaped leaves with a hinged lid, both edged in red
carnivorous plant of western Australia

Examples

The Australian pitcher plant traps insects with its unique tubular leaves.
Botanists discovered a new colony of Australian pitcher plant in western Australia.
The Australian pitcher plant is admired for its elegant green pitchers with red-edged lids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - a plant with leaves adapted as pitfall traps for insects
