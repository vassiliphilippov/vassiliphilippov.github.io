handheld vacuum cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌhɛld ˈvækˌjum ˈkliːnər / (AmE), / ˈhændˌhɛld ˈvæk.juːm ˈkliː.nə / (BrE)
A small, portable vacuum cleaner, typically powered by batteries or mains electricity, designed for cleaning small areas or spills.
portable small vacuum cleaner

Examples

She used a handheld vacuum cleaner to remove crumbs from the car seats.
A handheld vacuum cleaner is perfect for quick cleanups in the kitchen.
Many people keep a handheld vacuum cleaner handy for pet hair on furniture.

Synset Examples

Some battery-powered handheld vacuums are wet/dry rated; the appliance must be partially disassembled and cleaned after picking up wet materials, to avoid developing unpleasant odors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electric device for cleaning floors by suction
