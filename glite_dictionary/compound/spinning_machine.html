spinning machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪnɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used in the textile industry for spinning fibers into yarn or thread
textile machine for spinning yarn or thread

Examples

The invention of the spinning machine revolutionized the textile industry during the Industrial Revolution.
Modern factories often use a spinning machine to efficiently produce large quantities of yarn.
The museum displayed an original 19th-century spinning machine used by early textile workers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinning jenny - an early multi-spindle spinning machine
  • Synonym: loom - a machine for weaving yarn into fabric
