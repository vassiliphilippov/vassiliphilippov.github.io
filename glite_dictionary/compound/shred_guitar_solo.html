shred guitar solo

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɛd ɡɪˈtɑɹ ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ʃrɛd ɡɪˈtɑː ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a fast and technically impressive guitar solo, typically featuring rapid picking, intricate runs, and advanced techniques, especially in rock or metal music
fast, skillful guitar solo
informal

Examples

The crowd cheered loudly after his shred guitar solo during the concert.
Learning to play a shred guitar solo takes years of practice.
Her shred guitar solos have inspired many young musicians to pick up the instrument.

Synset Examples

He likes to shred guitar solos during band practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guitar solo - an instrumental passage featuring the guitar, usually in rock music

Etymology

From 'shred' (slang for play intensely or with great skill) and 'guitar solo'.
