ice water

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈwɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / aɪs ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
water served extremely cold, usually with ice cubes added
water served very cold with ice

Examples

She asked the waiter for a glass of ice water with her meal.
Nothing is as refreshing as ice water on a hot summer day.
He spilled some ice water on the table by accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iced water - water chilled or served with ice; synonym for 'ice water'
