old broad

1. NOUN

/ oʊld brɔd / (AmE), / əʊld brɔːd / (BrE)
an informal term for an older woman, often implying she is tough or strong-willed
informal term for an older woman
informal

Examples

Everyone in the neighborhood knows the old broad who runs the bakery.
Despite her age, that old broad still rides her motorcycle every weekend.
You have to respect an old broad who speaks her mind and doesn't back down.

Synset Examples

She's an old broad, but full of energy.
