needle bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdəl bɑr / (AmE), / ˈniːdəl bɑː / (BrE)
the reciprocating rod or bar in a sewing machine to which the needle is attached and which moves the needle up and down during sewing.
part of a sewing machine holding the needle

Examples

The needle bar must be properly aligned to prevent the machine from skipping stitches.
She replaced the broken needle bar in her vintage sewing machine.
If the needle bar is bent, it can cause serious sewing problems.
