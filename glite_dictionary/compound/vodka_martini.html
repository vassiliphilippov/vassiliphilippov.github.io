vodka martini

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑːdkə mɑrˈtini / (AmE), / ˈvɒdkə mɑːˈtiːni / (BrE)
a martini cocktail prepared with vodka instead of gin
martini made with vodka

Examples

She ordered a vodka martini with a twist at the bar.
Many people prefer a vodka martini over the traditional gin version.
The recipe for a classic vodka martini includes vodka, dry vermouth, and an olive garnish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: martini - a cocktail made with gin and vermouth, garnished with an olive or lemon twist
