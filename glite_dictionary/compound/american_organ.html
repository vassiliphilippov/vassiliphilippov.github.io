American organ

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈɔːr.ɡən / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈɔː.ɡən / (BrE)
a type of free-reed keyboard instrument in which air is drawn through reeds by suction bellows, producing a harmonium-like sound
free-reed keyboard instrument with suction bellows

Examples

She inherited an old American organ from her great-grandmother.
The musician demonstrated the difference between the harmonium and the American organ.
The church still uses a vintage American organ for Sunday services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonium - a similar free-reed keyboard instrument using pressure bellows
