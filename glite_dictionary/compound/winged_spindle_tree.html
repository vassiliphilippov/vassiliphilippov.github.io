winged spindle tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪŋd ˈspɪn.dl̩ triː / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋd ˈspɪn.dl triː / (BrE)
A bushy deciduous shrub native to northeastern Asia and central China, with branches featuring thin, wide corky wings and vibrant red foliage in autumn.
shrub with corky-winged branches, red in autumn

Examples

The leaves of the winged spindle tree turn a brilliant red in the fall, making it a popular choice for gardens.
They planted a winged spindle tree near the walkway for its unusual winged branches.
In its native habitat, the winged spindle tree thrives in well-drained, sunny locations.
