southern bog lemming

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn bɑɡ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən bɒɡ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
a small rodent (Synaptomys cooperi) found in low bogs and meadows in northeastern and central United States and southern Canada
a small North American rodent

Examples

The southern bog lemming is rarely seen because it hides in dense grasses and moss.
Several conservation efforts aim to protect the habitat of the southern bog lemming.
Researchers observed a southern bog lemming in the marsh for the first time in years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemming - a small, short-tailed, furry rodent found in northern regions
