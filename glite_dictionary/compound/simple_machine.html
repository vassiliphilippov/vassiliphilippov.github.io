simple machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmpəl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a basic mechanical device, such as a lever or pulley, that changes the direction or magnitude of a force to help perform work
basic mechanical device that changes force

Examples

A lever is a common type of simple machine that makes lifting easier.
Pulley systems are used as simple machines to move heavy loads.
The inclined plane is one of the oldest simple machines in history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine - an apparatus using mechanical power to perform work
