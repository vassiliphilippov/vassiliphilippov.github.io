jelly doughnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛli ˈdoʊnʌt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli ˈdəʊnʌt / (BrE)
A doughnut with a soft, raised texture, filled with jelly or jam.
doughnut filled with jelly or jam

Examples

Sam bought a jelly doughnut from the bakery for breakfast.
She bit into the jelly doughnut and got jam on her fingers.
The café is famous for its freshly made jelly doughnuts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - a small fried cake of sweetened dough, typically in the shape of a ring or ball
