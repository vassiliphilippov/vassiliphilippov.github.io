corn poppy

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
an annual European poppy with bright red flowers, commonly found in grain fields and sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant
a red-flowered European poppy

Examples

The bright red corn poppy is a familiar sight in the fields of Europe.
She planted corn poppies along the edge of her vegetable garden for a splash of color.
During spring, the fields were covered in corn poppies swaying in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy - any plant of the genus Papaver, typically with showy flowers
