weapons carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛpənz ˌkæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz ˌkæriə / (BrE)
a light military truck designed to transport mortars, machine guns, and their crews
light military vehicle for carrying weapons and crews

Examples

The platoon loaded their supplies onto the weapons carrier before dawn.
During the exercise, the weapons carrier moved quickly across the field.
Each weapons carrier was equipped to support both mortars and machine guns.
