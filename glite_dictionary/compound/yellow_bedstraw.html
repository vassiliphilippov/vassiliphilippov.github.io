yellow bedstraw

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
a common perennial plant with small yellow flowers, found in North America, Europe, and Asia; also known as Galium verum.
yellow-flowered perennial plant (Galium verum)

Examples

The field was covered with bright clusters of yellow bedstraw.
She identified the wildflower as yellow bedstraw because of its tiny yellow blossoms.
In herbal medicine, yellow bedstraw has been used for centuries for its reputed properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedstraw - herbaceous plant of the genus Galium
