antibacterial drug

1. NOUN

/ ˌænti bəˈktɪriəl drʌg / (AmE), / ˌænti bækˈtɪəriəl drʌg / (BrE)
a drug that destroys bacteria or inhibits their growth
a drug that kills or stops bacteria
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed an antibacterial drug to treat her infection.
Improper use of an antibacterial drug can lead to resistance.
Researchers are developing a new antibacterial drug to combat resistant bacteria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antibiotic - a substance used to kill or inhibit bacteria and other microorganisms
