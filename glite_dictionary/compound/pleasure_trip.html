pleasure trip

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛʒər trɪp / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə trɪp / (BrE)
a journey taken for enjoyment rather than for business or other practical reasons
trip for enjoyment

Examples

We're planning a pleasure trip to the coast this weekend to relax and unwind.
Their last pleasure trip abroad was filled with sightseeing and gourmet food.
She saved up all year for a pleasure trip to Italy with her friends.

Synset Examples

many summer excursions to the shore
it was merely a pleasure trip
after cautious sashays into the field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excursion - a short journey usually made for pleasure
  • Antonym: business trip - a journey undertaken for work or business purposes
