chemical mechanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈmɛkəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈmɛkənɪzəm / (BrE)
the sequence of atomic or molecular steps by which a chemical reaction occurs
sequence of steps in a chemical reaction

Examples

The researchers proposed a new chemical mechanism for the transformation of the compound.
Understanding the chemical mechanism helps scientists predict the outcome of reactions.
The article explains the chemical mechanism behind the drug's effectiveness in treating the disease.

Synset Examples

he determined unique mechanisms for the photochemical reactions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reaction mechanism - the detailed step-by-step process of a chemical reaction
