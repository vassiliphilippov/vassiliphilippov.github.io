personal growth

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɜrsənl ˈɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl ˈɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
the process of improving character, self-knowledge, or abilities over time through conscious effort and self-reflection
improvement of oneself over time

Examples

Reading widely can significantly contribute to your personal growth.
Attending workshops was a turning point in his journey of personal growth.
Many people seek personal growth through meditation and self-reflection.

Synset Examples

Negativity thinking can hinder personal growth.
Her book is all about personal growth and self-discovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-improvement - the process of making oneself better through one's own efforts
