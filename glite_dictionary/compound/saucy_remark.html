saucy remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsi rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsi rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment that is boldly disrespectful or cheekily amusing
cheeky or bold comment

Examples

She couldn't help but laugh at his saucy remark during the meeting.
Whenever he made a saucy remark, his friends would roll their eyes and chuckle.
Her saucy remark caught the teacher off guard and amused the entire class.

Synset Examples

His saucy remarks often made people laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sassy remark - a remark that is disrespectfully bold or cheeky
