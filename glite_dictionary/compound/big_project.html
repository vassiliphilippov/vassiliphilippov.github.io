big project

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a significant, important, or ambitious task or assignment, often requiring considerable time and effort
significant or ambitious task or assignment

Examples

She dedicated months of her life to completing the big project at work.
The company announced a big project that will create thousands of new jobs.
Finishing his big project gave him a huge sense of accomplishment.

Synset Examples

He felt like a god that failed after his big project flopped.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: major undertaking - a large or highly important task or project
