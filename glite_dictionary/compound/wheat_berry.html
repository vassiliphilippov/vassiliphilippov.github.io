wheat berry

1. NOUN

/ wiːt ˈbɛri / (AmE), / wiːt ˈbɛri / (BrE)
the whole, unprocessed kernel of wheat, including the bran, germ, and endosperm, often used as a food grain or ground into flour
whole unprocessed wheat grain

Examples

She added wheat berries to the salad for extra texture and nutrition.
For breakfast, try cooking wheat berries as a hot cereal with milk and honey.
The baker prefers to grind his own flour from wheat berries for a fresher taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheat - a cereal grain used for food
