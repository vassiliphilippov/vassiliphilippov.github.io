clever hack

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvɚ hæk / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə hæk / (BrE)
an ingenious trick or solution, especially for solving a problem quickly or efficiently
ingenious trick or solution
informal

Examples

He impressed his colleagues with a clever hack that automated the tedious report generation process.
This guide is full of clever hacks for saving time in the kitchen.
Jane found a clever hack to organize her overflowing inbox in just minutes.

Synset Examples

He used a clever hack to speed up his computer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaround - a method for overcoming a problem or limitation in a system
  • Synonym: trick - a clever action or scheme to solve a problem
  • Antonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake

Etymology

"Hack" in this sense derives from computer slang meaning a quick, clever fix or solution; "clever" intensifies the ingenuity.
