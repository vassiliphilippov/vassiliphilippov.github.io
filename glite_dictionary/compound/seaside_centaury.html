seaside centaury

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌsaɪd ˈsɛntʃəri / (AmE), / ˈsiːsaɪd ˈsɛntʃəri / (BrE)
a plant of the centaury family that grows in coastal regions, typically with pinkish flowers
a coastal variety of centaury plant

Examples

The rare seaside centaury blooms along the dunes during late summer.
Botanists discovered a patch of seaside centaury thriving on the windswept cliffs.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where seaside centaury is found.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a plant of the gentian family, typically with pink flowers
