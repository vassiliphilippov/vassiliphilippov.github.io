3rd October Organization

1. NOUN

/ θɜrd ɑkˈtoʊbɚ ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / θɜːd ɒkˈtəʊbə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a militant Marxist-Leninist group formed in 1975 aiming to force Turkey to acknowledge the Armenian genocide, pay reparations, and cede territory to Armenia
militant group formed in 1975 targeting Turkey over the Armenian genocide

Examples

The 3rd October Organization claimed responsibility for several attacks during the 1980s.
Authorities increased security in response to threats from the 3rd October Organization.
The activities of the 3rd October Organization brought international attention to the Armenian cause.
