real dasher

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈdæʃər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈdaʃə / (BrE)
A person, especially in soccer, who is very fast and skillful.
fast and skillful soccer player

Examples

The team relied on their real dasher to break through the defense and score goals.
Everyone on the field noticed that she was a real dasher, outpacing her opponents with ease.
As a real dasher, he quickly became the star player on his youth soccer team.

Synset Examples

He was a real dasher on the soccer field.
