State of Kuwait

1. PROPN

/ steɪt əv kuˈweɪt / (AmE), / steɪt əv kjuˈeɪt / (BrE)
the official name of Kuwait, an Arab country in Asia located on the northwestern coast of the Persian Gulf, known for its significant petroleum resources
official name of the country Kuwait in Asia

Examples

The State of Kuwait is a leading producer of petroleum in the Middle East.
Many expatriates work and live in the State of Kuwait for its economic opportunities.
The national day of the State of Kuwait is celebrated each year in February.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kuwait - an Arab country in the Middle East, bordering the Persian Gulf

Etymology

From Arabic دَوْلَة الْكُوَيْت (Dawlat al-Kuwayt), meaning 'State of Kuwait'.
