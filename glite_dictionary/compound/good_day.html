good day

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd deɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊd deɪ / (BrE)
used as a polite or formal way of saying goodbye when parting; a farewell greeting
formal or polite farewell greeting

Examples

As he left the office, he turned and said, 'good day' to everyone.
After the meeting, she gave him a polite 'good day' before leaving.
In Victorian novels, characters often part ways with a formal 'good day'.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - used to express farewell when parting
  • Antonym: hello - used as a greeting when meeting someone
