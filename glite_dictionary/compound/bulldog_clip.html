bulldog clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌdɔɡ klɪp / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.dɒɡ klɪp / (BrE)
a strong, spring-loaded metal clip with flat jaws, used for holding sheets of paper together
spring-loaded metal paper clip

Examples

She used a bulldog clip to keep all the documents neatly together on her desk.
A large stack of papers was held by a single bulldog clip at the corner.
He opened the bulldog clip to add more forms to the bundle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binder clip - a similar spring-loaded paper holding clip

Etymology

Named for its grip resembling a bulldog's strong jaws.
