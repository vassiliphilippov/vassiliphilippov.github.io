full general

1. NOUN

/ fʊl ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / fʊl ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
a general officer of the highest rank in the army, above lieutenant general.
the highest rank of general officer

Examples

He was promoted to full general after decades of distinguished service.
As a full general, her authority extended over all regional commanders.
The ceremony marked his appointment as a full general in the armed forces.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: general - an officer of high rank in the army
  • Degree Higher: field marshal - the highest rank in some armies, above full general
