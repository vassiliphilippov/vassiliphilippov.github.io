perfect rimshot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.fɪkt ˈrɪmˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt ˈrɪm.ʃɒt / (BrE)
a precisely timed sharp drum sound effect used to emphasize the punchline of a joke or comedic moment
accurate joke-punctuating drum sound

Examples

The comedian delivered the punchline, and the drummer played a perfect rimshot to heighten the laughter.
Every time she told a joke, she hoped for a perfect rimshot from the band.
That sketch would have fallen flat without the perfect rimshot at the end.

Synset Examples

He punctuated his joke with a perfect rimshot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rimshot - a drum sound produced by hitting the rim and head simultaneously
