field of study

1. NOUN

/ fild ʌv ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / fiːld əv ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
a particular branch of knowledge, typically studied or researched in an academic setting
a branch of academic knowledge

Examples

She changed her field of study from biology to psychology after her first year.
Advancements in technology are influencing every field of study.
It is important to choose a field of study that matches your interests and strengths.

Synset Examples

in what discipline is his doctorate?
teachers should be well trained in their subject
anthropology is the study of human beings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discipline - a branch of learning or scholarly instruction
  • Synonym: subject - an area of knowledge studied or taught in school, college, or university
