reaper binder

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːpər ˈbaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈriːpə ˈbaɪndə / (BrE)
a machine used for cutting grain and binding it into sheaves
machine that cuts and binds grain

Examples

The old reaper binder in the museum shows how farming was done a century ago.
He repaired the reaper binder after it broke down during the wheat harvest.
Farmers relied on the reaper binder before modern combine harvesters became common.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: combine harvester - a machine that reaps, threshes, and cleans grain crops
