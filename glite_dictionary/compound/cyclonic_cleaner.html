cyclonic cleaner

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈklɑːnɪk ˈkliːnər / (AmE), / saɪˈklɒnɪk ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a type of vacuum cleaner that uses a spinning airflow to separate dust and debris into a detachable cylindrical bin, rather than using filtration bags
vacuum cleaner with spinning dust separation

Examples

Many modern households use a cyclonic cleaner for more efficient dust removal.
The new cyclonic cleaner requires less frequent emptying than a traditional vacuum.
Thanks to its design, a cyclonic cleaner can maintain suction even as the bin fills up.

Synset Examples

Cyclonic cleaners do not use filtration bags.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical appliance that cleans surfaces by suction

Etymology

Formed from 'cyclonic', relating to cyclones, referring to the swirling airflow, and 'cleaner'.
