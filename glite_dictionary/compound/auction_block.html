auction block

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔk.ʃən ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈɔːk.ʃən ˌblɒk / (BrE)
a platform from which an auctioneer conducts sales
platform for auction sales

Examples

The valuable painting was displayed on the auction block before the bidding began.
After much anticipation, the antique vase finally went up on the auction block.
Many rare items have been sold from the auction block at that historic house.

Synset Examples

they put their paintings on the block

Related Terms

  • Synonym: block - a solid piece of hard material such as wood, typically with flat surfaces
