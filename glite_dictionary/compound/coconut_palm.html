coconut palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌkʌt pɑm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌkʌt pɑːm / (BrE)
a tall tropical palm tree that produces coconuts and is widely cultivated throughout tropical regions
tall tropical tree bearing coconuts

Examples

The coconut palm is an iconic sight along many tropical beaches.
Farmers rely on the coconut palm for both food and raw materials.
A strong wind toppled a coconut palm near the shoreline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palm tree - a tree of the family Arecaceae with a single, unbranched trunk
