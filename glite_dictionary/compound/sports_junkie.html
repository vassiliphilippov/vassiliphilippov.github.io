sports junkie

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic about sports, often to the point of obsession
person obsessed with sports
informal slang

Examples

My brother is a total sports junkie who watches every major game and tournament.
As a sports junkie, she travels across the country to see her favorite teams play.
He became a sports junkie after discovering the excitement of live football matches.

Synset Examples

He's a self-proclaimed sports junkie who never misses a game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports fanatic - a person with an intense and overwhelming passion for sports
  • Degree Lower: sports enthusiast - a person who is very interested in sports

Etymology

Modelled on "junkie" (slang for addict) + "sports"; "junkie" from "junk" (narcotic drug).
