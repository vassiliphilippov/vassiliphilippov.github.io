sign of affection

1. NOUN

/ saɪn əv əˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / saɪn əv əˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
a gesture or action that shows love, fondness, or warmth toward someone
gesture expressing love or fondness

Examples

Giving someone a warm hug is often seen as a sign of affection.
He left flowers on her desk as a small sign of affection.
Cats purring while sitting on your lap can be considered a sign of affection.

Synset Examples

She always called her grandmother Senga, a sign of affection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: token of affection - something, such as an object or gesture, that expresses love or fondness
  • Antonym: sign of hostility - a gesture or action showing anger or unfriendliness
