preemployment training program

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a training program designed to prepare individuals for entry into employment, typically focusing on job skills and workplace readiness.
training program preparing for employment

Examples

The company offers a preemployment training program to help new hires adapt to the workplace.
After completing the preemployment training program, participants felt more confident about their job prospects.
Many youth benefit from a preemployment training program before starting their first job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job training - instruction to develop skills for a job
