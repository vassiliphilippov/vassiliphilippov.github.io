crisphead lettuce

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪspˌhɛd ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspˌhɛd ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A type of lettuce with crisp, tightly packed light-green leaves forming a firm head, commonly known as iceberg lettuce.
lettuce with tightly packed crisp leaves

Examples

The salad was made with fresh crisphead lettuce and ripe tomatoes.
She prefers crisphead lettuce because of its crunchy texture and mild flavor.
A head of crisphead lettuce keeps well in the refrigerator for several days.

Synset Examples

iceberg is still the most popular lettuce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iceberg lettuce - a common variety of crisphead lettuce with firm, pale leaves
  • Antonym: butterhead lettuce - a type of lettuce with soft, loosely folded leaves
