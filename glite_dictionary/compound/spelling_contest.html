spelling contest

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌkɑntɛst / (AmE), / ˈspɛlɪŋ ˌkɒntɛst / (BrE)
a competition in which participants are eliminated if they spell a word incorrectly
competition to spell words correctly

Examples

She practiced every evening for the upcoming spelling contest at her school.
The winner of the spelling contest correctly spelled every challenging word.
Many students signed up to compete in the annual spelling contest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spelling bee - a spelling competition, especially for children
