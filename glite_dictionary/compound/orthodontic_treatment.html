orthodontic treatment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔr.θoʊˈdɑn.tɪk ˈtriːt.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɔː.θəˈdɒn.tɪk ˈtriːt.mənt / (BrE)
dental treatment that corrects irregularities of the teeth or their alignment, typically using braces or other mechanical devices
dental treatment to straighten teeth
medical

Examples

After months of orthodontic treatment, her teeth were finally straight.
His dentist recommended orthodontic treatment to correct his bite.
Many teenagers receive orthodontic treatment with braces during high school.

Synset Examples

orthodontic treatment of facial abnormalities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: braces - devices used in orthodontic treatment to align and straighten teeth

Etymology

From Greek 'orthos' (straight) + 'odous' (tooth), with 'treatment' from Latin 'tractare' (to handle, treat).
