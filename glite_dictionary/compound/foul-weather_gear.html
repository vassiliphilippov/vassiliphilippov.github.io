foul-weather gear

1. NOUN

/ faʊlˈwɛðɚ ɡɪr / (AmE), / faʊlˈwɛðə ɡɪə / (BrE)
protective clothing worn to keep the wearer dry and warm in bad weather, especially rain or storms
protective clothing for bad weather

Examples

Sailors always put on their foul-weather gear before heading out in a storm.
She packed her foul-weather gear in preparation for the hiking trip.
Make sure your foul-weather gear is waterproof and fits well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raincoat - a waterproof coat worn to protect against rain
  • Degree Lower: windbreaker - a jacket designed to protect against wind and light rain
