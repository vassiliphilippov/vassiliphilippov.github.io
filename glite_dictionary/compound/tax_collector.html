tax collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæks kəˌlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈtæks kəˌlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect taxes on behalf of the government
person who collects government taxes

Examples

The tax collector arrived at each house to ensure everyone paid their dues.
People in the town often complained about the strictness of the tax collector.
In history, the role of tax collector was sometimes unpopular among citizens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxman - informal term for a tax collector
