gum anime

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌm ˈænɪmi / (AmE), / ɡʌm ˈænɪmi / (BrE)
Any of various resins or oleoresins, especially those obtained from certain tropical trees and used in varnishes and incense.
a type of resin from tropical trees

Examples

Artisans often use gum anime in the production of high-quality varnishes.
The incense was made with a blend of frankincense and gum anime.
Collectors value rare samples of gum anime for their historical uses in perfumery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gum - a viscous secretion of plants, often used as a base for resins
  • Synonym: resin - a sticky substance from plants used in varnishes and adhesives

Etymology

From Latin 'anime', from French 'anime', referring to a type of resin.
