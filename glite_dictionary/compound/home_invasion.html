home invasion

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ɪnˈveɪʒən / (AmE), / həʊm ɪnˈveɪʒən / (BrE)
The crime of illegally entering a dwelling while the residents are present, typically to commit robbery, assault, or another violent act.
burglary of a home while residents are present

Examples

The police responded quickly to reports of a home invasion in the middle of the night.
After the terrifying home invasion, the family installed a security system.
The news warned residents to be aware of a recent increase in home invasions in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burglary - illegal entry to commit a crime, especially theft
