half-slip

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf slɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf slɪp / (BrE)
A woman's undergarment that hangs from the waist and is worn under a skirt to help the skirt hang smoothly and prevent it from being see-through.
women's underskirt garment

Examples

She wore a half-slip beneath her dress for extra comfort.
The delicate fabric of her skirt required a half-slip underneath.
She bought a new half-slip to wear to the formal event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slip - a woman's loose-fitting undergarment, usually full-length or half-length
