Land of Enchantment

1. PROPN

/ ˌlænd əv ɪnˈtʃæntmənt / (AmE), / ˌlænd əv ɪnˈtʃɑːntmənt / (BrE)
the official nickname for the U.S. state of New Mexico, referring to its scenic beauty and cultural heritage
nickname for New Mexico, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit the Land of Enchantment to experience its unique blend of cultures and landscapes.
The Land of Enchantment boasts some of the most stunning deserts in North America.
She moved to the Land of Enchantment after falling in love with Santa Fe on a road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Mexico - a state in the southwestern United States

Etymology

Originated as an official slogan and nickname for New Mexico in the early 20th century.
