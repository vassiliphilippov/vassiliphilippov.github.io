Russian Orthodox

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌʃən ˈɔrθədɑks / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃən ˈɔːθədɒks / (BrE)
of, relating to, or characteristic of the branch of the Eastern Orthodox Church based in Russia.
relating to the Russian branch of the Eastern Orthodox Church

Examples

She attended a Russian Orthodox Easter service with her family.
The cathedral is known for its traditional Russian Orthodox architecture and beautiful icons.
He converted to the Russian Orthodox faith after moving to Moscow.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Eastern Orthodox - relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church as a whole
