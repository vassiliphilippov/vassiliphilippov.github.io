greyhound racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪˌhaʊnd ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪhaʊnd ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which greyhounds race around a track, typically chasing a mechanical lure.
sport of racing greyhounds

Examples

He enjoys going to greyhound racing events every weekend with his friends.
Local charities benefit from the proceeds of the annual greyhound racing fundraiser.
Concerns about animal welfare have led to stricter regulations in greyhound racing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog racing - the sport of racing dogs, especially sighthounds
