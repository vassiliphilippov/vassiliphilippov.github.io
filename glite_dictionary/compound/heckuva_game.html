heckuva game

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛkəvə ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɛkəvə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
an impressive or exciting game, often used to express admiration or enthusiasm, especially in informal contexts
impressive or exciting game (informal)
informal

Examples

Last night's basketball match was a heckuva game and kept everyone on the edge of their seats.
Fans were still talking about the heckuva game they witnessed over the weekend.
That was a heckuva game, with both teams playing their absolute best.

Synset Examples

That was a heckuva game we watched last night.
That was a helluva game last night!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helluva game - an impressive or exciting game

Etymology

Colloquial blend of 'heck of a' (as an euphemism for 'hell of a') and 'game.'
