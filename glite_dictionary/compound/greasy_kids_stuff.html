greasy kid's stuff

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːsi kɪdz stʌf / (AmE), / ˈɡriːzi kɪdz stʌf / (BrE)
Cheap or unsophisticated entertainment intended for children, often considered lacking in depth or quality.
unsophisticated children's entertainment
informal

Examples

I usually skip greasy kid's stuff and look for shows with more substance.
The movie critic dismissed the sequel as more greasy kid's stuff.
He outgrew greasy kid's stuff and now prefers classic films.

Synset Examples

He only likes greasy kid's stuff, like cartoons.
I avoid greasy kid's stuff in my films.
That movie was just greasy kid's stuff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kiddie fare - entertainment aimed at young children
  • Antonym: highbrow - entertainment considered intellectual or sophisticated (opposite in connotation)

Etymology

Originally used to describe inexpensive hair products marketed to children; later applied figuratively to childish entertainment.
