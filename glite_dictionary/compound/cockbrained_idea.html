cockbrained idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌbreɪnd aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌbreɪnd aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is foolish, senseless, or absurd
foolish or nonsensical idea

Examples

That cockbrained idea is not going to work.
She dismissed his cockbrained idea without a second thought.
Launching such a risky campaign was a truly cockbrained idea.

Synset Examples

That cockbrained idea is not going to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harebrained idea - an idea that is extremely foolish or ill-considered

Etymology

Formed by combining 'cockbrained' (from 'cock', used in similar compounds to denote foolishness, and 'brained', meaning 'having a mind') and 'idea'.
