energy efficiency

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi ɪˈfɪʃənsi / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ɪˈfɪʃənsi / (BrE)
the use of less energy to perform the same function or achieve the same result
using less energy for the same output

Examples

Upgrading old appliances can greatly improve energy efficiency in your home.
The building was designed with energy efficiency as a top priority.
Governments around the world are setting new standards for energy efficiency in the automotive industry.

Synset Examples

They plan to renovate the outwall to improve energy efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energy saving - reducing energy consumption
