tea urn

1. NOUN

/ ti ɝːn / (AmE), / tiː ɜːn / (BrE)
a large metal container in which tea is brewed and from which it is served, typically used in gatherings or public places
large container for brewing and serving tea

Examples

At the church event, volunteers filled cups from the steaming tea urn throughout the afternoon.
Please remember to clean the tea urn after the meeting, as it is used by everyone.
A traditional British afternoon tea often features a large silver tea urn at the center of the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: samovar - a metal urn, often used in Russia, for heating water for tea
