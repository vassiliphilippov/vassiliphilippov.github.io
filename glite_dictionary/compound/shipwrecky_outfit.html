shipwrecky outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌrɛki ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪprɛki ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
An outfit that looks disheveled, messy, or as if it has been through rough conditions.
disheveled or messy outfit

Examples

She showed up to the brunch in a shipwrecky outfit, turning heads with her unconventional look.
After traveling for two days, his shipwrecky outfit seemed appropriate for the adventure.
Despite her shipwrecky outfit, she radiated confidence wherever she went.

Synset Examples

His shipwrecky outfit made him stand out at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shabby - in poor condition through long use or lack of care
  • Synonym: scruffy - untidy or dirty in appearance
  • Antonym: smart outfit - neat and stylish clothing
