goose plum

1. NOUN

/ ɡus plʌm / (AmE), / ɡuːs plʌm / (BrE)
A wild plum tree native to eastern and central North America, bearing red-orange fruit with yellow flesh.
North American wild plum tree

Examples

The woods behind her house were filled with goose plum trees in early summer.
Birds often feast on the ripe fruit of the goose plum in August.
He made a delicious jam from a basket of freshly picked goose plums.
