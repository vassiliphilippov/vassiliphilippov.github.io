rock star

1. NOUN

/ rɑk stɑr / (AmE), / rɒk stɑː / (BrE)
A famous and successful singer or musician who performs rock music; someone who is regarded as a star in the genre of rock.
famous rock musician

Examples

After the concert, the rock star was surrounded by fans asking for autographs.
She dreamed of becoming a rock star since she first picked up a guitar.
In the 1970s, being a rock star meant living a life of fame and excess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop star - a famous singer or musician in pop music
  • Synonym: rock musician - someone who performs or composes rock music
