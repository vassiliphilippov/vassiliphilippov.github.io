calming color

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlmɪŋ ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmɪŋ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a color, such as blue or green, thought to create a relaxed and peaceful feeling
a color that promotes relaxation

Examples

Soft blue is often considered a calming color in bedrooms and offices.
Many spas decorate their walls with calming colors to help clients relax.
People may choose a calming color palette to reduce stress in their home environment.

Synset Examples

She chose to wallpaper her bedroom in calming colors.
