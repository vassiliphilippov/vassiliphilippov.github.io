red cabbage

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkabɪdʒ / (BrE)
a type of cabbage with compact heads of purplish-red leaves, often used in salads and cooking
cabbage with purplish-red leaves

Examples

She added some finely sliced red cabbage to the salad for a splash of color.
Pickled red cabbage is a popular side dish in many European countries.
The vibrant hue of red cabbage makes it a decorative garnish for various dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabbage - a leafy green vegetable, typically with a dense head of leaves
