ski mask

1. NOUN

/ ski mæsk / (AmE), / skiː mɑːsk / (BrE)
A close-fitting woolen or synthetic mask that covers most of the face, worn to protect against cold, especially while skiing.
face mask worn for warmth while skiing

Examples

The cold wind bit through my jacket, but my ski mask kept my face warm.
He put on a ski mask before heading out onto the slopes.
During the blizzard, she relied on her ski mask for protection against the elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balaclava - close-fitting head covering covering most of the face
