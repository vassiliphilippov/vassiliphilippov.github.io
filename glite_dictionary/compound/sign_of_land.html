sign of land

1. NOUN

/ saɪn əv lænd / (AmE), / saɪn ɒv lænd / (BrE)
an indication or evidence that land is nearby, especially as observed by people at sea
evidence that nearby land is present

Examples

The sailors rejoiced when they finally spotted a sign of land beyond the fog.
After weeks at sea, the flock of birds was a welcome sign of land.
Clouds over the horizon can sometimes be mistaken for a sign of land by anxious travelers.

Synset Examples

They watched the horizon for signs of land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landfall - the sighting or reaching of land after a voyage
