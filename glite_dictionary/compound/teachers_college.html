teachers college

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiː.tʃɚz ˈkɑː.lɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtiː.tʃəz ˈkɒ.lɪdʒ / (BrE)
a college or school that provides training and education for future teachers, especially at the elementary level
school for training teachers

Examples

After graduating from teachers college, she began teaching first grade at a local school.
Many rural areas established their own teachers colleges in the early twentieth century.
He applied to the teachers college to pursue a career in elementary education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: normal school - a school for training teachers, especially at the elementary level
