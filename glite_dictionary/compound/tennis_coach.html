tennis coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a person who trains and instructs tennis players to improve their skills and performance
person who trains tennis players

Examples

After hiring a new tennis coach, her game improved dramatically within a few months.
The school's tennis coach organized extra practice sessions before the big tournament.
He hopes to become a professional tennis coach after he retires as a player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a person who trains athletes or teams
