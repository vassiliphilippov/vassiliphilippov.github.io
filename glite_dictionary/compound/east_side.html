east side

1. NOUN

/ ist saɪd / (AmE), / iːst saɪd / (BrE)
the side of an area, building, or object that is oriented toward or located on the east.
side facing or on the east

Examples

The entrance is located on the east side of the building.
They planted a garden along the east side of the house to catch the morning sun.
The city's east side has seen rapid development over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: west side - the side that is on the west
  • Antonym: north side - the side that is on the north
  • Antonym: south side - the side that is on the south
