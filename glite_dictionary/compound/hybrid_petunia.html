hybrid petunia

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ.brɪd pəˈtuː.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪ.brɪd pɛˈtjuː.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
a type of petunia plant that is a hybrid, typically resulting from a cross between Petunia axillaris and Petunia integrifolia, known for its single or double flowers in a variety of colors from white to purple
garden plant; hybrid petunia variety

Examples

The gardener planted several hybrid petunias along the garden path for a splash of vibrant color.
Modern hybrid petunia varieties tend to be more resistant to disease and bloom longer than older types.
She chose a hybrid petunia with double purple flowers to brighten her balcony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petunia - a type of flowering plant often grown as an ornamental
