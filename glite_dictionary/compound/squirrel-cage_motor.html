squirrel-cage motor

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɝːrəl keɪdʒ ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl keɪdʒ ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
An electric motor that uses a rotor shaped like a squirrel cage, commonly found in induction motors.
type of electric motor with cage-like rotor

Examples

The factory installed a new squirrel-cage motor to improve energy efficiency.
A squirrel-cage motor is commonly used in industrial applications for its durability and reliability.
The engineer explained how the squirrel-cage motor operates without electrical connections to the rotor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: induction motor - an electric motor in which power is supplied to the rotor by electromagnetic induction

Etymology

Named for the rotor's resemblance to an exercise wheel for squirrels (squirrel cage).
