blind stitch machine

1. NOUN

/ blaɪnd stɪtʃ məˈʃin / (AmE), / blaɪnd stɪtʃ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A sewing machine designed to join two pieces of fabric with stitches that are invisible or nearly invisible on the finished surface.
machine for making near-invisible stitches

Examples

The tailor used a blind stitch machine to hem the dress without showing any stitching.
Many professional sewing workshops invest in a blind stitch machine for high-quality finishes.
A blind stitch machine is ideal for attaching cuffs and hems where visible stitches are undesirable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blind stitch - a stitch that is barely visible on the fabric's surface
