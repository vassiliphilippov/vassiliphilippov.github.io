birthday cake

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ keɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ keɪk / (BrE)

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ keɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ keɪk / (BrE)

cake for a birthday celebration

Examples

She brought a beautiful birthday cake to the party, decorated with pink icing and candles.
The children sang happily as their friend blew out the candles on the birthday cake.
Every year, his mother bakes him a chocolate birthday cake with his name written in frosting.
