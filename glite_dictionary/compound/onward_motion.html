onward motion

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑn.wɚd ˈmoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɒn.wəd ˈməʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of moving forward, especially toward a goal or destination
the act of moving forward

Examples

The hikers resumed their onward motion after a brief rest at the summit.
Technological innovation often drives the onward motion of society.
Despite the obstacles, her determination fueled the onward motion toward her dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: progress - forward or onward movement toward a destination or goal
  • Synonym: advance - the process of moving forward or making progress
