arm guard

1. NOUN

/ ɑrm ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɑːm ɡɑːd / (BrE)
A padded protective covering for the arm, worn by athletes such as football players and hockey goalkeepers to prevent injury.
padded protective covering for the arm (sports)

Examples

The football player strapped on his arm guard before heading onto the field.
Hockey goalkeepers rely on a sturdy arm guard to protect themselves from fast-moving pucks.
She forgot her arm guard and ended up with a painful bruise after the match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shin guard - padded protective covering for the shin, worn in sports
