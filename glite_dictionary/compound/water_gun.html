water gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɡʌn / (BrE)
a toy gun designed to squirt water, typically used by children for play
toy gun for squirting water

Examples

The children ran around the garden, laughing and spraying each other with water guns.
He bought a colorful water gun for his nephew's birthday.
During the summer picnic, everyone joined in the water gun fight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirt gun - a toy that squirts water; synonym for water gun
