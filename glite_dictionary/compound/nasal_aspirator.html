nasal aspirator

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪzl ˈæspəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈneɪzl ˈaspɪˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a small medical device used to suction mucus or congestion from the nasal passages, typically in infants or young children
device for suctioning mucus from the nose
medical

Examples

The pediatrician recommended using a nasal aspirator to clear the baby's congested nose.
She gently used a nasal aspirator to help her infant breathe more easily.
Modern nasal aspirators are designed for safe and easy use at home.
