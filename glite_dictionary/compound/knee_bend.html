knee bend

1. NOUN

/ niː bɛnd / (AmE), / niː bend / (BrE)
an exercise in which one repeatedly bends the knees to lower and raise the body, typically to strengthen the leg muscles
squat exercise for leg muscles

Examples

The trainer demonstrated a proper knee bend to help us strengthen our leg muscles.
He does thirty knee bends every morning as part of his workout routine.
After several sets of knee bends, her thighs felt much stronger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squat - an exercise where one crouches down by bending the knees, then returns to standing
