sales representative

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / seɪlz ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person whose job is to represent a company and sell its products or services to customers
person who sells products for a company

Examples

The sales representative visited several clients during her business trip.
If you have any questions about the product, please speak with our sales representative.
As a sales representative, he is responsible for meeting monthly sales targets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is to sell things, especially in a store
