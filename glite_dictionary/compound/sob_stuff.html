sob stuff

1. NOUN

/ sɑb stʌf / (AmE), / sɒb stʌf / (BrE)
Stories, language, or content intended to provoke strong sympathy or sentimentality, especially by focusing on personal distress.
overly sentimental content
informal

Examples

The film was full of sob stuff, clearly aiming to make the audience cry.
She dismissed his explanation as just more sob stuff to gain pity.
Editors cut out most of the sob stuff to keep the article factual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sob story - an exaggerated or manipulative account of hardship intended to evoke sympathy
