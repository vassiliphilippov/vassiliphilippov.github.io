Santa Paws

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.tə pɔz / (AmE), / ˈsan.tə pɔːz / (BrE)
A playful name for Santa Claus, especially used in relation to pets during the Christmas season.
playful pet-themed Santa Claus name

Examples

Every year, the animal shelter hosts a Santa Paws event for families and their pets.
Children left treats for Santa Paws along with their pets' Christmas stockings.
Our dog's photo with Santa Paws became our holiday card.

Synset Examples

Kids eagerly await Santa Paws every holiday season.
We celebrate Christmas with our beloved Santa Paws.
Santa Paws delivered gifts to all the good pets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Santa Claus - mythical figure who brings gifts to children at Christmas

Etymology

Modeled after 'Santa Claus' with 'Paws' replacing 'Claus' as a pun, referencing animal paws.
