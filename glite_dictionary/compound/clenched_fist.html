clenched fist

1. NOUN

/ klɛntʃt fɪst / (AmE), / klɛn(t)ʃt fɪst / (BrE)
a hand with the fingers tightly closed into the palm, often as an expression of anger or determination or in preparation for striking
hand with fingers closed into the palm

Examples

She raised her clenched fist in a gesture of defiance.
In anger, he pounded the table with his clenched fist.
The protestors marched forward, each with a clenched fist held high.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fist - a hand with the fingers closed tightly against the palm
