emery cloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛməri klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈɛməri klɒθ / (BrE)
A strong cloth coated with powdered emery, used for smoothing or polishing metal and other materials.
cloth coated with abrasive emery powder

Examples

He used emery cloth to remove rust from the old pipe.
Before painting, you should smooth the surface with emery cloth for best results.
The mechanic reached for a strip of emery cloth to polish the metal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandpaper - paper coated with abrasive material, used similarly for smoothing surfaces

Etymology

From emery, a granular rock used as an abrasive, and cloth.
