automobile insurance

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑː.t̬ə.moʊˌbiːl ɪnˈʃʊr.əns / (AmE), / ˌɔː.tə.məˌbiːl ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns / (BrE)
Insurance that provides coverage for loss or damage resulting from theft, traffic accidents, or other risks involving vehicles.
insurance for vehicles against accidents or theft

Examples

Many drivers find that automobile insurance premiums can be quite expensive in large cities.
He renewed his automobile insurance policy before the previous one expired.
You are legally required to have automobile insurance to drive a car in most countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car insurance - insurance for cars against accidents, theft, etc.
