turn of expression

1. NOUN

/ tɝn əv ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / tɜːn əv ɪkˈspreʃən / (BrE)
a distinctive or characteristic way of phrasing something in speech or writing
distinctive way of expressing an idea

Examples

Her witty turn of expression made the story far more entertaining than the events themselves.
He is admired for his unique turn of expression in political speeches.
That turn of expression captures exactly what I was trying to say.

Synset Examples

John's succinct turn of phrase persuaded her that it would not be a good idea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn of phrase - a distinctive way of expressing something
