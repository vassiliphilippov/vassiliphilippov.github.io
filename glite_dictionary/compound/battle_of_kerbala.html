Battle of Kerbala

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv kərˈbɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv kɜːˈbɑːlə / (BrE)
the battle fought in 680 CE near Karbala, Iraq, in which Husayn ibn Ali, the grandson of Muhammad, and his followers were killed by the forces of the Umayyad caliph Yazid I
battle of 680 CE in Karbala, Iraq

Examples

The Battle of Kerbala is commemorated each year during the Islamic month of Muharram.
Many historians consider the Battle of Kerbala a turning point in Islamic history.
The tragedy of the Battle of Kerbala inspired countless works of literature and art.

Synset Examples

the Battle of Kerbala is the basis for the Ta'ziyehs that are performed annually
