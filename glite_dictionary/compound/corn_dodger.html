corn dodger

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈdɑdʒər / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈdɒdʒə / (BrE)
A small, oval cake made from corn bread, usually baked or fried, chiefly in the southern United States.
small oval corn bread cake (Southern US)
american

Examples

She served hot corn dodgers alongside the stew for dinner.
My grandmother taught me how to fry perfect corn dodgers.
At the festival, the smell of freshly baked corn dodgers filled the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornbread - bread made from cornmeal, popular in the Southern United States
