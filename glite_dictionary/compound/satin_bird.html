satin bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈsætən bɝd / (AmE), / ˈsætɪn bɜːd / (BrE)
A species of bird native to southeast Australia, the male of which is glossy violet-blue and the female light grey-green.
Australian bird with striking male plumage

Examples

The male satin bird displays vibrant plumage during mating season.
We spotted a pair of satin birds building a nest in the eucalyptus tree.
Naturalists admire the iridescent feathers of the satin bird in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowerbird - bird from the family Ptilonorhynchidae noted for elaborate courtship structures
