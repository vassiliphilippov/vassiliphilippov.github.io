National Labor Relations Board

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæʃənəl ˈleɪbər rəˈleɪʃənz bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəl ˈleɪbə rɪˈleɪʃənz bɔːd / (BrE)
an independent agency of the United States government that mediates disputes between employers and labor unions
US agency mediating labor disputes
american

Examples

The National Labor Relations Board issued a decision in favor of the striking workers.
Whenever collective bargaining reaches an impasse, the National Labor Relations Board may step in to help resolve the conflict.
Employers must comply with all regulations enforced by the National Labor Relations Board.
