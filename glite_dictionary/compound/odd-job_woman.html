odd-job woman

1. NOUN

/ ɑd dʒɑb ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ɒd dʒɒb ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is skilled at performing various small tasks or odd jobs, often involving minor repairs or maintenance
a woman who does various small or odd jobs

Examples

The neighbors hired an odd-job woman to fix their creaky door and repaint the fence.
As an odd-job woman, she helped with everything from gardening to small household repairs.
The local community center employs an odd-job woman for weekly maintenance tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odd-job man - a man who does various odd jobs or small tasks
