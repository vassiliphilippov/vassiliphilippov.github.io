spine-tingling

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪn ˌtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪn ˌtɪŋɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a strong feeling of excitement or fear; thrilling or chilling to the senses
causing excitement or fear

Examples

The film's spine-tingling soundtrack heightened the suspense in every scene.
There was a spine-tingling silence just before the storm hit.
Her spine-tingling scream echoed through the empty house.

Synset Examples

The movie had a spine-tingling plot twist at the end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chilling - causing fright or a cold feeling; making you feel very afraid or nervous
