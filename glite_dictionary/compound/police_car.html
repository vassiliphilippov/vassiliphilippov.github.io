police car

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis kɑr / (AmE), / pəˈliːs kɑː / (BrE)
A car used by the police, typically equipped with communication systems and used to patrol streets and respond to incidents.
car used by the police for patrols

Examples

The police car sped down the street with its siren blaring.
Two officers got out of the police car and approached the building.
After the call came in, a police car arrived at the scene within minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol car - a police car used chiefly for patrolling
  • Synonym: squad car - another word for a police car, chiefly American
