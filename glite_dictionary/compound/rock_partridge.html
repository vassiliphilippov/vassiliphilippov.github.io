rock partridge

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɒk ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ / (BrE)
a medium-sized game bird (Alectoris graeca) native to mountainous regions of southern Europe, known for its greyish plumage and red legs
a bird species of southern Europe's mountains

Examples

The rock partridge nests on steep, rocky slopes far above the tree line.
Hikers in the Alps might spot a rock partridge among the stones.
Unlike the red-legged partridge, the rock partridge is often found at higher elevations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partridge - a medium-sized game bird in the pheasant family
