nocturnal emission

1. NOUN

/ nɑkˈtɝnəl ɪˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / nɒkˈtɜːnəl ɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an involuntary ejaculation of semen during sleep, often occurring during a dream
involuntary ejaculation during sleep
medical

Examples

Many adolescent boys experience their first nocturnal emission during puberty.
Educational materials often explain that a nocturnal emission is a normal part of development.
He was embarrassed after having a nocturnal emission but soon learned it was common.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wet dream - involuntary ejaculation during sleep, usually a sexual dream

Etymology

From Latin 'nocturnus' (of the night) and 'emissio' (a sending out).
