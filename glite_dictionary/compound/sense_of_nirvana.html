sense of nirvana

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv nɪrˈvɑːnə / (AmE), / sɛns əv nɪəˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
a feeling or experience of perfect happiness or peace
feeling of perfect happiness or peace

Examples

After hours of hiking in the peaceful forest, she was overcome by a sense of nirvana.
The music brought about a sense of nirvana that lasted long after the concert ended.
He entered a deep meditation and achieved a sense of nirvana he had never felt before.

Synset Examples

He felt a sense of nirvana while meditating in silence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bliss - perfect happiness; a state of great joy
  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: misery - a state or feeling of great physical or mental distress or discomfort

Etymology

‘Nirvana’ from Sanskrit *nirvāṇa*, meaning ‘blown out’ or ‘extinguished’, referring to liberation from suffering.
