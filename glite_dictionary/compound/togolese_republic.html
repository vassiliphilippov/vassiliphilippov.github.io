Togolese Republic

1. PROPN

/ toʊɡəˌliz rəˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / təʊɡəˌliːz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name of Togo, a country on the western coast of Africa along the Gulf of Guinea
official name of Togo (West African country)

Examples

The capital of the Togolese Republic is Lomé.
After gaining independence, the Togolese Republic joined the United Nations.
The Togolese Republic is bordered by Ghana, Benin, and Burkina Faso.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Togo - a country in West Africa; short form of the Togolese Republic
