chuck short ribs

1. NOUN

/ tʃʌk ʃɔrt rɪbz / (AmE), / tʃʌk ʃɔːt rɪbz / (BrE)
A cut of beef taken from the area between the chuck and the brisket, consisting of short sections of rib bone with surrounding meat.
beef cut from between chuck and brisket

Examples

The chef braised chuck short ribs until they were tender and flavorful.
At the butcher's shop, I asked for two pounds of chuck short ribs for tonight's dinner.
Chuck short ribs are perfect for slow-cooked dishes like stews and barbecues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: short ribs - beef ribs containing a short piece of bone and surrounding meat
