Arctic mouse-ear

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrktɪk ˈmaʊs ɪr / (AmE), / ˈɑːktɪk ˈmaʊs ɪə / (BrE)
a small perennial plant (Cerastium arcticum) found in Arctic regions and mountainous areas of Europe, with small white flowers and hairy leaves
plant of Arctic and European mountains

Examples

The Arctic mouse-ear blooms in late spring, adding delicate white flowers to the tundra landscape.
Researchers found Arctic mouse-ear thriving on the rocky slopes of the Swiss Alps.
Despite the harsh climate, Arctic mouse-ear manages to grow in areas with little soil.
