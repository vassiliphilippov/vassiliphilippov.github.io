starlight tour

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrˌlaɪt tʊr / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌlaɪt tʊə / (BrE)
an organized nighttime excursion to observe stars and constellations, typically in places with minimal artificial light
nighttime trip to observe stars

Examples

The starlight tour revealed dozens of constellations we had never seen before.
They booked a starlight tour through the national park during their holiday.
A clear sky made our starlight tour an unforgettable experience.

Synset Examples

The starlight tour revealed constellations hidden from city lights.
