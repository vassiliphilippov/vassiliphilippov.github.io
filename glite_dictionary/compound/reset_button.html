reset button

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌsɛt ˌbʌtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌsɛt ˌbʌtən / (BrE)
a button on a device that restores it to its original or default state when pressed
button to restore device to default state

Examples

If your computer freezes, try pressing the reset button to restart it.
The technician reached for the reset button to fix the malfunctioning router.
Many electronic gadgets include a tiny reset button you can press with a pin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power button - button to turn a device on or off
