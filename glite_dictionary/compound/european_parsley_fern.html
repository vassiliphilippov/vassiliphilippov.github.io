European parsley fern

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈrəʊpiən ˈpɑːrsli fɜrn / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈpɑːsli fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern of Europe and Asia Minor with a short slender rhizome and densely tufted bright green fronds that resemble parsley
European fern with parsley-like fronds

Examples

The European parsley fern thrives in rocky, well-drained soils across mountainous regions.
Botanists noted the delicate fronds of the European parsley fern during their field survey.
Gardeners value the European parsley fern for its ornamental, parsley-like leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parsley fern - fern with fronds resembling parsley
