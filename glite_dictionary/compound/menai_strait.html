Menai Strait

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛ.naɪ streɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛ.naɪ streɪt / (BrE)
a narrow stretch of water in northern Wales separating Anglesey Island from the mainland.
a strait in north Wales between Anglesey and the mainland

Examples

The Menai Strait is famous for its strong tidal currents and diverse marine life.
They crossed the Menai Strait via the iconic suspension bridge.
A boat tour along the Menai Strait offers stunning views of both Anglesey and the Welsh mainland.
