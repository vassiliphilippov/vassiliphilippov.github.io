money changer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌni ˌtʃeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌtʃeɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person or business that exchanges the currency of one country for that of another
person or business exchanging currencies

Examples

I went to the money changer at the airport to get some euros.
Street money changers often offer competitive rates to tourists.
You should check the exchange rate before visiting a money changer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currency exchange - the business of exchanging one currency for another
