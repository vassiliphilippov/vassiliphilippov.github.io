drum major

1. NOUN

/ drʌm ˈmeɪdʒər / (AmE), / drʌm ˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
the leader of a marching band or drum corps who directs performances and often leads the group during processions
leader of a marching band or drum corps

Examples

The drum major led the band onto the field with a dramatic salute.
After years of practice, she was named drum major of her high school marching band.
The impressive uniform of the drum major included a tall, feathered hat.
