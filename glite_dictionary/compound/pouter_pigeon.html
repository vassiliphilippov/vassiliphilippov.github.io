pouter pigeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊtər ˈpɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtə ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a breed of domestic pigeon known for inflating its crop, making its breast appear greatly enlarged
breed of pigeon with a puffed-out breast

Examples

The pouter pigeon is prized by breeders for its spectacularly puffed-out breast.
She keeps several rare pouter pigeons in her dovecote.
At the bird show, the pouter pigeon attracted much attention for its unusual appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigeon - a bird with a compact body and short legs, commonly found in cities and bred in many varieties
