purple poppy mallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.pəl ˈpɑː.pi ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈpɒp.i ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
A hairy perennial plant native to central United States, with round, deeply lobed leaves and loose clusters of large crimson-purple or cherry-red flowers.
hairy perennial plant with crimson-purple flowers

Examples

The purple poppy mallow brightened the garden with its striking blossoms.
Gardeners prize purple poppy mallow for its drought tolerance and vivid flowers.
During summer, fields in Kansas are dotted with purple poppy mallow in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallow - plants of the family Malvaceae with soft, hairy leaves and flowers
