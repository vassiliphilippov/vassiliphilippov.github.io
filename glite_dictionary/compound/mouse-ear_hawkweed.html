mouse-ear hawkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaʊs ɪr ˈhɑkˌwid / (AmE), / ˌmaʊs ɪə ˈhɔːkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A European hawkweed plant with soft, hairy leaves, sometimes classified in the genus Hieracium.
European hawkweed with hairy leaves

Examples

The field was dotted with the bright yellow flowers of mouse-ear hawkweed.
She identified the fuzzy leaves as belonging to mouse-ear hawkweed during the nature walk.
Gardeners sometimes mistake mouse-ear hawkweed for a common dandelion due to its similar flower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawkweed - a plant of the daisy family relating to the genus Hieracium
