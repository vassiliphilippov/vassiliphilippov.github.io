house cooling party

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / haʊs ˈkuːlɪŋ ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a party held to celebrate moving out of a home, often as a farewell gathering
farewell party before moving out

Examples

We decided to invite all our friends to the house cooling party before the big move.
Her house cooling party was filled with laughter, memories, and a few tears.
They threw a memorable house cooling party to say goodbye to their old neighbors.

Synset Examples

Let's throw a house cooling party next week.
His house cooling party was a big success.
We celebrated with a house cooling party after the move.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: housewarming party - a party to celebrate moving into a new home

Etymology

Formed in analogy to 'housewarming party'; from 'house' + 'cooling', indicating leaving, and 'party'.
