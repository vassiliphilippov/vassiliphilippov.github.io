professional pugilist

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈpjuːdʒɪlɪst / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈpjuːdʒɪlɪst / (BrE)
A person who is trained and paid to fight in boxing matches; a professional boxer.
a trained professional boxer

Examples

He trained for years to become a professional pugilist and compete at the national level.
Many admire the discipline required to be a successful professional pugilist.
After retiring as a professional pugilist, she became a respected coach.

Synset Examples

He aspired to become a professional pugilist one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxer - a person who fights professionally in boxing matches

Etymology

From Latin 'pugil' (boxer, fighter), with 'professional' indicating paid occupation.
