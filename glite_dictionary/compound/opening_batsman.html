opening batsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpənɪŋ ˈbætsmən / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ ˈbætsmən / (BrE)
the player who is among the first to bat for a team in a cricket match, usually setting the tone for the team's innings
first batsman in cricket

Examples

The opening batsman faced the first delivery of the match with great composure.
As an opening batsman, she had to deal with fast and aggressive bowling.
The team's opening batsman scored a century, giving them a strong start.

Synset Examples

The coach praised the opening batsman for his aggressive play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opener - player who opens the batting in cricket
