computer architecture

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtər ˌɑːrkəˌtɛktʃər / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˌɑːkɪˌtɛktʃə / (BrE)
the structure and organization of a computer's hardware and system software, determining how the components interact and function together
structure of a computer's hardware and software
medical

Examples

Advances in computer architecture have greatly improved processing speeds in recent years.
A solid understanding of computer architecture is essential for designing efficient software.
She wrote her dissertation on the evolution of computer architecture over the past five decades.

Synset Examples

the architecture of a computer's system software

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer system - a complete, functional computer including all hardware and software

Etymology

Formed from 'computer' and 'architecture', applying the term from building design to computer systems.

2. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)
The design and organization of the fundamental components of a computer system, including the relationships and structure among its hardware or software parts.
design and structure of computer systems

Examples

Many advances in computer architecture have led to dramatic increases in processing speed over the past decades.
He specializes in computer architecture and has designed several high-performance processors.
Understanding computer architecture is essential for software developers working close to the hardware.
