falcon-gentle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæl.kən ˈdʒɛn.t̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl.kən ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
A female falcon, especially a female peregrine falcon, used in falconry.
female falcon, especially peregrine

Examples

The falcon-gentle is prized in falconry for her speed and agility.
Only the most skilled handlers are entrusted with a falcon-gentle.
Legend held that a falcon-gentle brought luck to the hunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peregrine falcon - a species of falcon known for speed and hunting skills

Etymology

Middle English; from Old French "faucon gent", meaning 'noble falcon'.
