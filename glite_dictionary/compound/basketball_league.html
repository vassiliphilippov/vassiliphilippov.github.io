basketball league

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətbɔl liɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskətbɔːl liːɡ / (BrE)
an organization consisting of basketball teams that compete against each other in a series of games
organization of basketball teams

Examples

He joined a local basketball league to improve his skills and meet new friends.
The national basketball league attracts thousands of fans every season.
Several schools formed a basketball league to encourage sportsmanship among students.
