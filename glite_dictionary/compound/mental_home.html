mental home

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.təl hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl həʊm / (BrE)
a hospital for people with mental illnesses, especially those unable to care for themselves; a psychiatric institution
hospital for psychiatric patients

Examples

After his breakdown, he was admitted to a mental home for treatment.
Many mental homes were built on the outskirts of cities in the 19th century.
She worked as a nurse in a mental home for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental hospital - hospital for the treatment of mental illness
  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - hospital specializing in psychiatric care
