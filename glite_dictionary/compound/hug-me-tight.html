hug-me-tight

1. NOUN

/ ˌhʌɡ.miˈtaɪt / (AmE), / ˌhʌɡ.miˈtaɪt / (BrE)
a close-fitting short jacket for women, fashionable in the 19th century
a woman's fitted jacket (historic)
archaic

Examples

She wore a velvet hug-me-tight over her dress to keep warm in the chilly evening air.
The museum's fashion exhibit displayed a crimson hug-me-tight from the Victorian era.
In portraits of the period, ladies are often shown wearing a decorative hug-me-tight fastened with elaborate buttons.

Etymology

Named for its snug, 'hugging' fit on the body, resembling an embrace.
