closeup lens

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊs.ʌp lɛnz / (AmE), / ˈkləʊs.ʌp lɛnz / (BrE)
a photographic lens designed with a short focal length to enable sharp focus on subjects at a close distance
lens for close-range photography

Examples

She attached a closeup lens to her camera to photograph the tiny flowers.
A closeup lens allows photographers to capture fine details in small objects.
He bought a new closeup lens for his nature photography assignment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macro lens - a lens designed for extreme close-ups and capturing small subjects in detail
