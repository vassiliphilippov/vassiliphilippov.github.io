two-year-old

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː jɪr ˌoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtuː jə ˌəʊld / (BrE)
A child, animal, or thing that is two years old.
a person, animal, or thing aged two years

Examples

Their two-year-old is learning to talk very quickly.
The two-year-old car still runs like new.
We adopted a two-year-old dog from the shelter.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtuː jɪr oʊld / (AmE), / ˈtuː jə əʊld / (BrE)
A racehorse that is two years old, especially one participating in races restricted to horses of that age.
a two-year-old racehorse

Examples

The trainer was proud of his promising two-year-old after it won its first race.
Many two-year-olds compete in prestigious summer events for young horses.
This two-year-old is showing a lot of potential on the track.
