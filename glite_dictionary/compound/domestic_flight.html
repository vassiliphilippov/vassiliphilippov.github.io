domestic flight

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk flaɪt / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk flaɪt / (BrE)
a flight that both departs from and arrives within the same country
flight within the same country

Examples

She booked a domestic flight from New York to Los Angeles for her business trip.
Many domestic flights were delayed due to heavy snowfall across the country.
Passengers must show a valid ID when boarding a domestic flight in the United States.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: international flight - a flight between different countries

2. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk flaɪt / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk flaɪt / (BrE)
a flight that takes place within the same country, not crossing international borders
flight within the same country

Examples

She booked a domestic flight from New York to Los Angeles.
Due to bad weather, several domestic flights were delayed.
Passengers taking a domestic flight usually have fewer customs procedures to complete.

Synset Examples

The airline offers domestic flights across the country.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: international flight - a flight between different countries
