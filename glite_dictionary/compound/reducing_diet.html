reducing diet

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈdusɪŋ ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːsɪŋ ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet intended to promote weight loss, especially by reducing fat
diet for weight loss

Examples

Her doctor recommended a reducing diet to help lower her cholesterol and lose weight.
Many people find it difficult to stick to a reducing diet during the holidays.
A reducing diet often limits the intake of sugars and fats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weight-loss diet - a diet designed to help lose body weight
  • Antonym: maintenance diet - a diet designed to maintain current body weight
