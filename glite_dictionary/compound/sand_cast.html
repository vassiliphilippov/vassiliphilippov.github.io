to sand cast

1. VERB

/ sænd kæst / (AmE), / sænd kɑːst / (BrE)
to form (an object) by pouring molten metal into a mold made of sand
form by pouring metal into sand mold

Examples

Large metal sculptures are often sand cast to achieve intricate details.
The engineer decided to sand cast the wheel for superior strength.
Many antique tools were originally sand cast in small foundries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cast - to shape (metal or other material) by pouring into a mold
