Adam and Eve

1. PROPN

/ ˌædəm ənd ˈiv / (AmE), / ˌædəm ənd ˈiːv / (BrE)
the first man and woman in the Bible, considered the ancestors of humanity, whose story is told in the Book of Genesis
the first humans in the Bible

Examples

Many artists have depicted Adam and Eve in paintings and sculptures throughout history.
The story of Adam and Eve explains the concept of original sin in Christian theology.
Some scholars interpret the tale of Adam and Eve as symbolic rather than literal.
