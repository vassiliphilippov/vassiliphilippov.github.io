Bahrain Island

1. PROPN

/ bəˈreɪn ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / bəˈreɪn ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the largest island of Bahrain, located in the Persian Gulf, forming the main part of the country Bahrain
the main island of Bahrain in the Persian Gulf

Examples

Many tourists visit Bahrain Island to experience its rich history and modern architecture.
The majority of the country's population lives on Bahrain Island.
Bridges connect Bahrain Island with nearby smaller islands and Saudi Arabia.

Etymology

From Arabic البحرين (al-Baḥrayn) meaning 'the two seas'.
