cock tease

1. NOUN

/ kɑk tiz / (AmE), / kɒk tiːz / (BrE)
A person who gives sexual attention or encouragement without the intention of engaging in sexual activity, especially so as to arouse frustration; considered vulgar and offensive.
person who sexually teases with no intent (vulgar, slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

She was labeled a cock tease for flirting without any intention of following through.
He accused her of being a cock tease, though she insisted she was just friendly.
Some people use the term cock tease in a derogatory way to shame women for their behavior.
