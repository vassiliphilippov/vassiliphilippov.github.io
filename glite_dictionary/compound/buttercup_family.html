buttercup family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəkʌp ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, Ranunculaceae, that includes buttercups and related species
family of flowering plants (Ranunculaceae)

Examples

Many common wildflowers in the meadow belong to the buttercup family.
The buttercup family contains more than 2,000 species of plants worldwide.
Columbines and clematis are less obvious members of the buttercup family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ranunculaceae - scientific family name for buttercup family
