conjugal visitation right

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑndʒəɡəl vɪˌzɪteɪʃən raɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dʒʊ.ɡəl vɪ.zɪˈteɪ.ʃən raɪt / (BrE)
the legal right granted to a prisoner allowing private visits with their spouse, typically for sexual intercourse
prisoner's right to private spousal visits
legal

Examples

The inmate was granted a conjugal visitation right after several years of good behavior.
Debate continues over whether prisoners should be allowed conjugal visitation rights in the correctional system.
Her campaign advocated for the restoration of conjugal visitation rights for married inmates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conjugal visit - a private visit in prison, typically for sexual relations between a prisoner and their spouse

Etymology

From Latin conjugalis (of marriage) and English visitation, with legal usage arising in the 20th century.
