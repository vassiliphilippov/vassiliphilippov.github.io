expanded glass

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈspændɪd ɡlæs / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændɪd ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A lightweight, opaque cellular form of glass made by heating crushed glass with powdered carbon, used mainly for insulation and construction.
lightweight, cellular glass used for insulation

Examples

The building was insulated with expanded glass to improve energy efficiency.
Because of its low weight and insulating properties, expanded glass is popular in modern construction.
Fire-resistant and moisture-proof features make expanded glass especially suitable for harsh environments.

Synset Examples

Cellular glass is used for insulation.
Depending on the density, cellular glass can be either black or white.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cellular glass - A type of glass with a cellular structure used for insulation
  • Synonym: foam glass - Glass material with a foam-like structure, synonym of expanded glass
