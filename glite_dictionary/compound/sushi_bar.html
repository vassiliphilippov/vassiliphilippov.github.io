sushi bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuʃi bɑr / (AmE), / ˈsuːʃi bɑː / (BrE)
A restaurant or counter where sushi is prepared and served, often in a casual setting.
restaurant or counter serving sushi

Examples

We decided to try the new sushi bar in the neighborhood for dinner.
She sat at the sushi bar and watched the chef prepare her meal.
Many sushi bars offer a wide selection of sake and fresh fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sushi - Japanese dish of vinegared rice with seafood or vegetables

Etymology

From Japanese 'sushi' (vinegared rice dish) + English 'bar' (counter for serving food or drink).
