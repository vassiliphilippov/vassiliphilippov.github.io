centre bit

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.tɚ bɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə bɪt / (BrE)
a drill bit with a sharp central point for guidance and two side cutters, used for boring holes in wood
drill bit with central point and side cutters

Examples

The carpenter selected a centre bit to drill a clean, round hole in the wooden plank.
You need a centre bit if you want precise holes with minimal deviation.
She replaced the standard drill bit with a centre bit for better accuracy in her project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auger bit - a drill bit for boring holes in wood, typically with a screw point
