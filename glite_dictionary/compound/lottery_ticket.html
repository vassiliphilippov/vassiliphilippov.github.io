lottery ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːtəɹi ˌtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɒtəri ˌtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that gives its holder a chance to win a prize in a lottery
ticket for a chance-based drawing

Examples

She always dreams about winning big with a lottery ticket.
He found an old lottery ticket in his coat pocket and checked the numbers.
Brenda bought a lottery ticket at the gas station on her way home.

Synset Examples

She buys a lottery ticket every week for fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lottery - a game of chance in which tickets are sold and prizes are drawn at random
