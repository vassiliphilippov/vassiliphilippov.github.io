jenny wren

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛni ˌrɛn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛni ˌrɛn / (BrE)
a small, lively brown wren of the Northern Hemisphere, especially the Eurasian wren, noted for its short, upright tail and insectivorous diet
a small brown wren bird

Examples

A jenny wren flitted among the branches searching for insects.
He identified the tiny bird as a jenny wren by its short, cocked tail.
In her garden, a pair of jenny wrens built their nest in a flowerpot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wren - a small, active brown songbird with a short upright tail

Etymology

From the female given name Jenny and 'wren', used affectionately as a common name for the bird.
