traditional rosbif

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənəl rɒzˈbif / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl rɒzˈbiːf / (BrE)
A classic French dish of roast beef, typically prepared and served in a traditional style.
classic French roast beef dish

Examples

Many Parisian bistros still serve traditional rosbif on Sundays.
The chef takes pride in perfecting his traditional rosbif recipe.
Our culinary tour included a delicious dinner of traditional rosbif.

Synset Examples

The tourists were delighted to try traditional rosbif.

Etymology

From French 'rosbif,' itself from English 'roast beef.'
