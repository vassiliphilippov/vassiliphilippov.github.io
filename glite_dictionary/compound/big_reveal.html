big reveal

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ rɪˈvil / (AmE), / bɪɡ rɪˈviːl / (BrE)
an event or moment when something important or dramatic is made known, often after suspense or anticipation
dramatic disclosure or revelation

Examples

The final episode featured the big reveal that shocked all the viewers.
After months of speculation, the company orchestrated a big reveal for their latest product.
She wore a mask all night, saving her identity for the big reveal at midnight.

Synset Examples

Now for the big reveal of our surprise!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revelation - the act of revealing something, especially something surprising or previously secret
  • Synonym: disclosure - the act of making new or secret information known
