British people

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪt̬.ɪʃ ˈpiː.pəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.ɪʃ ˈpiː.pəl / (BrE)
the people who are natives or inhabitants of Great Britain, or who are of British descent.
people of Great Britain

Examples

Many British people enjoy traditional afternoon tea with scones and sandwiches.
The history of the British people is filled with cultural achievements and innovations.
After moving abroad, she often missed the humor of British people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Briton - a native or inhabitant of Great Britain
