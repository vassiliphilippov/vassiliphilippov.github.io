umbrageous spot

1. NOUN

/ ʌmˈbreɪdʒəs spɑt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbreɪdʒɪəs spɒt / (BrE)
A place that is shaded or sheltered, especially from sunlight.
shaded or sheltered area

Examples

They rested in an umbrageous spot by the edge of the meadow, enjoying the cool air.
She chose an umbrageous spot beneath the old oak tree for her afternoon reading.
Picnickers gathered at the umbrageous spot to escape the intensity of the summer sun.

Synset Examples

They found an umbrageous spot to sit and relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shade - an area where direct sunlight is blocked

Etymology

Umbrageous from Latin 'umbra' (shade), commonly found in literary contexts.
