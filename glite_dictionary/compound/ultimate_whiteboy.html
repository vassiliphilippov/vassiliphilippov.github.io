ultimate whiteboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltəmət ˈwaɪtbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ˈwaɪtbɔɪ / (BrE)
A highly stereotypical young white male, especially in behavior or appearance, often used humorously or disparagingly.
stereotypical young white male
informal slang

His friends teased him, calling him the ultimate whiteboy.
