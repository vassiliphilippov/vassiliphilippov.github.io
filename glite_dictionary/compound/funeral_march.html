funeral march

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuː.nɚl mɑːrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.nərəl mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a slow, solemn piece of music composed or played for a funeral procession
slow music for a funeral procession

Examples

The orchestra played a funeral march as the casket was carried down the aisle.
Chopin’s funeral march is often performed at solemn ceremonies around the world.
They walked in silence behind the hearse, accompanied by the haunting sounds of a funeral march.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirge - a funeral song or tune expressing mourning
