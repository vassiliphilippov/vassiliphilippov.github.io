humorous take

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmərəs teɪk / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəs teɪk / (BrE)
an interpretation or perspective of something that is meant to be funny or amusing
funny interpretation or viewpoint

Examples

His humorous take on office politics had everyone in stitches during the presentation.
The comedian offered a humorous take on current events that made the news more enjoyable to watch.
She wrote a humorous take on parenting that resonated with many readers.

Synset Examples

Everyone loved the spanisher's humorous take on the situation.
The Cunstitution was a humorous take on serious laws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satirical take - an interpretation or viewpoint that uses satire
  • Antonym: serious take - an interpretation or viewpoint that is grave or earnest
