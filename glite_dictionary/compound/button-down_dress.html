button-down dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt.ən daʊn drɛs / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ən daʊn drɛs / (BrE)
A dress with a collar and buttons running down the front.
dress with front buttons and a collar

Examples

She wore a button-down dress to the job interview, making a smart first impression.
The new collection features a button-down dress in several floral prints.
A classic button-down dress can be dressed up or down for different occasions.

Synset Examples

Her button-down dress was perfect for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirt dress - a dress with a shirt-like collar, typically buttoned down the front
