quick thrust

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk θrʌst / (AmE), / kwɪk θrʌst / (BrE)
a rapid forward movement or jab, especially with a weapon or hand
a rapid forward movement or jab

Examples

With a quick thrust, the fencer scored a point against her opponent.
He opened the box with a quick thrust of his knife.
The cat made a quick thrust at the dangling string.

Synset Examples

He made a quick thrust with the sword during practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jab - a quick, sharp blow or poke
  • Synonym: lunge - a sudden forward movement, often with a weapon
