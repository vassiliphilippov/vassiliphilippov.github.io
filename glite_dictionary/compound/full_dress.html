full dress

1. NOUN

/ fʊl drɛs / (AmE), / fʊl drɛs / (BrE)
formal evening attire for men, typically including a tailcoat, waistcoat, and trousers, worn at very formal events
formal men's evening attire

Examples

The ambassador attended the state banquet in full dress, complete with a white bow tie and decorations.
Wearing full dress is required at royal ceremonies and certain high-society events.
He carefully prepared his full dress suit for the charity gala, making sure every detail was correct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white tie - the most formal evening dress code; a synonym for full dress
  • Degree Lower: black tie - a less formal evening dress code, typically involving a dinner jacket or tuxedo
