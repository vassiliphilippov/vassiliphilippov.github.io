wild parsley

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈpɑr.sli / (AmE), / waɪld ˈpɑːs.li / (BrE)
any of various uncultivated plants in the family Apiaceae, with foliage resembling that of carrots or parsley
uncultivated plant resembling parsley

Examples

The meadow was filled with wild parsley and other native plants.
Foragers should be cautious, as some types of wild parsley can be toxic.
She mistook the leafy plant for wild parsley and added it to the salad.
