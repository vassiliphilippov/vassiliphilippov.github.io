Ugandan shilling

1. NOUN

/ juːˈɡændən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / juːˈɡændən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Uganda, equal to 100 cents
currency unit of Uganda

Examples

The price of a cup of coffee in Kampala is about 2,500 Ugandan shillings.
She exchanged her dollars for Ugandan shillings at the airport.
The Ugandan shilling has remained stable against the dollar this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shilling - a former British coin and monetary unit
