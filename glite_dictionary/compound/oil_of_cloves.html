oil of cloves

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl əv kloʊvz / (AmE), / ɔɪl əv kləʊvz / (BrE)
an essential oil extracted from cloves, commonly used to flavor medicines and for its aromatic and medicinal properties
essential oil from cloves, used medicinally

Examples

Dentists sometimes use oil of cloves to numb tooth pain.
The distinctive aroma in the remedy comes from oil of cloves.
She added a drop of oil of cloves to the mixture for its soothing effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clove oil - essential oil from cloves, alternate term for oil of cloves
