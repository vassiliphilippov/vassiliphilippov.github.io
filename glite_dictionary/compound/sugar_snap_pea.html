sugar snap pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ snæp pi / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə snæp piː / (BrE)
A variety of pea plant that produces crisp, rounded edible pods which can be eaten whole, both raw and cooked.
variety of pea with edible, crisp pods

Examples

I like to add sugar snap peas to my salads for extra crunch.
The stir-fry included chicken, carrots, and fresh sugar snap peas.
Children enjoy eating sugar snap peas straight from the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow pea - a type of pea with flat, edible pods
  • Degree Higher: pea - the small, round seed or seed-pod of the pod fruit Pisum sativum

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ snæp pi / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə snæp piː / (BrE)
a type of pea with a sweet flavor, featuring a crisp, edible pod that is rounded rather than flat
crisp, sweet edible-podded pea

Examples

She added fresh sugar snap peas to the salad for extra crunch.
You can eat sugar snap peas raw or lightly steamed as a healthy snack.
Farmers markets often sell sugar snap peas in the spring when they're at their sweetest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snow pea - a type of edible-podded pea with flat pods
