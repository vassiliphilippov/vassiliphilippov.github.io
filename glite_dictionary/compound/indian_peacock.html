Indian peacock

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈpiːˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈpiːˌkɒk / (BrE)
a large, colorful pheasant native to India, known for the male's vibrant, iridescent tail feathers
colorful Indian bird with ornate male tail

Examples

The Indian peacock is celebrated for its dazzling blue and green plumage.
Many visitors come to the park to photograph the Indian peacock displaying its magnificent tail.
The national bird of India is the Indian peacock due to its beauty and grace.

Synset Examples

The vibrant colors of the Indian peacock mesmerized the guests.
The Indian peacock danced beautifully during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peafowl - generic term for birds of the Pavo genus
  • Antonym: peahen - female of the peafowl species
