ball club

1. NOUN

/ bɔl klʌb / (AmE), / bɔːl klʌb / (BrE)
A team of professional baseball players who play and travel together as a unit.
professional baseball team

Examples

The Chicago Cubs are a famous ball club with a loyal fan base.
He spent ten years playing for the same ball club before retiring.
Each ball club must submit its season roster by the first of April.

Synset Examples

each club played six home games with teams in its own division

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball team - A team that plays the sport of baseball
