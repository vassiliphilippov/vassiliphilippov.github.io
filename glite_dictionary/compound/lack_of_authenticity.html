lack of authenticity

1. NOUN

/ læk əv ɔːˌθɛntɪˈsɪti / (AmE), / læk əv ɔːˌθɛntɪˈsɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being genuine, true, or real
absence of genuineness or truth

Examples

The artwork was beautiful, but its lack of authenticity disappointed collectors.
Critics pointed out the lack of authenticity in the restaurant’s supposedly traditional recipes.
Consumers are often wary of products with a lack of authenticity in their branding.

Synset Examples

They called the event fake and gay due to its lack of authenticity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: authenticity - the quality of being genuine or real
