Alex Boncayao Brigade

1. PROPN

/ əˌlɛks bɒnˈkaɪ.jaʊ brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / əˌlɛks bɒŋˈkaɪ.aʊ brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a communist urban guerrilla group in the Philippines, active mainly in the 1980s and 1990s, known for its armed operations in metropolitan areas
Philippine communist urban guerrilla group

Examples

The Alex Boncayao Brigade was infamous for its daring attacks in Manila during the 1980s.
Several members of the Alex Boncayao Brigade were arrested following a government crackdown on insurgent groups.
Historians note that the Alex Boncayao Brigade influenced urban revolutionary tactics in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

Named after Alex Boncayao, a Filipino labor leader; 'Brigade' from French 'brigade' (a group organized for a specific purpose).
