Bay of Bengal

1. PROPN

/ ˌbeɪ əv bɛnˈɡɔl / (AmE), / ˌbeɪ əv bɛnˈɡɔːl / (BrE)
a large bay that forms part of the northeastern Indian Ocean, lying to the east of India and west of Myanmar and Bangladesh
a large bay in the northeastern Indian Ocean

Examples

The Bay of Bengal is often struck by powerful cyclones during the monsoon season.
Fishing in the Bay of Bengal supports the livelihoods of many communities in eastern India and Bangladesh.
Several major rivers, including the Ganges and Brahmaputra, flow into the Bay of Bengal.
