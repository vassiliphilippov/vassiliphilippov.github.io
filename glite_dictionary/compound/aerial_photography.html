aerial photography

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriəl fəˈtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
The process of taking photographs of the ground or objects from the air, especially using aircraft, drones, or satellites.
photography from the air

Examples

He specializes in aerial photography for real estate companies to showcase properties from above.
Advances in drone technology have made aerial photography more accessible than ever before.
Many environmental studies rely on aerial photography to monitor changes in landscapes over time.

Synset Examples

He uses a drone to capture stunning aerial photography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photography - the art or practice of taking and processing photographs
