reading clinic

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ ˌklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ ˌklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a clinic that provides specialized help for people with reading disabilities
clinic for reading disabilities

Examples

The school opened a new reading clinic to support students struggling with literacy.
Children with dyslexia may benefit from attending a reading clinic for individualized instruction.
After several sessions at the reading clinic, his reading skills improved noticeably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literacy clinic - a clinic that offers services to improve literacy, including reading and writing skills
