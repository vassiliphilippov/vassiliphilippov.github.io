Flame Tokay

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm toʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / fleɪm təʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a variety of table grape with purplish-red skin, known for its large, juicy berries and sweet flavor
variety of purplish-red table grape

Examples

The Flame Tokay grape is prized for its vibrant color and juicy texture.
She bought a bag of Flame Tokay grapes from the farmer's market.
Many vineyards in California specialize in growing Flame Tokay grapes.
