car rental

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈrɛntəl / (AmE), / kɑː ˈrɛntəl / (BrE)
a car that has been rented for temporary use, typically from a rental agency
a car rented for temporary use

Examples

We decided to book a car rental for our trip around the island.
The car rental was waiting for us at the airport when we landed.
After her accident, the insurance company covered the cost of a car rental.

Synset Examples

she picked up a hire car at the airport and drove to her hotel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rental car - a car that has been rented
  • Degree Lower: lease car - a car leased for use rather than bought
