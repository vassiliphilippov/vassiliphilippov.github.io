sales event

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlz ɪˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈseɪlz ɪˌvɛnt / (BrE)
an occasion when goods are sold at specially reduced prices, often for a limited time
temporary sale with reduced prices

Examples

The store announced a three-day sales event with discounts on all electronics.
Many shoppers lined up early to take advantage of the Black Friday sales event.
During the holiday sales event, customers can find significant savings on clothing and gifts.

Synset Examples

they held a sale to reduce their inventory
I got some great bargains at their annual sale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sale - an occasion when goods are sold at reduced prices
