cardboard box

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌbɔrd bɑks / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdbɔːd bɒks / (BrE)
a container made from cardboard, typically used for packing or storing items
a box made of cardboard

Examples

We used a cardboard box to store our old books in the attic.
After moving, the kitchen was still full of empty cardboard boxes.
The delivery arrived in a large cardboard box sealed with tape.

Synset Examples

He packed his belongings in a sturdy cardboard box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carton - a large container made from cardboard or plastic, often for beverages or packaging
