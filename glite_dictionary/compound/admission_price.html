admission price

1. NOUN

/ ədˈmɪʃ.ən ˌpraɪs / (AmE), / ədˈmɪʃ.ən ˌpraɪs / (BrE)
the amount of money required to enter a place, event, or activity
fee charged for entry

Examples

The admission price for the museum is ten dollars per person.
We debated whether the admission price was worth it before buying our tickets.
During the summer, the admission price to the park is reduced for children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admission fee - the charge for entering a place or event
