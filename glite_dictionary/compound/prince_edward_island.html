Prince Edward Island

1. PROPN

/ ˌprɪns ˈɛdwərd ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˌprɪns ˈɛdwəd ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
an island province of Canada located in the Gulf of Saint Lawrence, known as the smallest Canadian province by area and population
island and smallest province of Canada

Examples

Many tourists visit Prince Edward Island each summer to enjoy its scenic beaches and delicious seafood.
Anne of Green Gables is famously set on Prince Edward Island.
The potatoes grown on Prince Edward Island are known throughout Canada for their quality.

Etymology

Named after Prince Edward, Duke of Kent and Strathearn (1767–1820).
