water conservation

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌkɑnsərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the practice of using water efficiently to reduce unnecessary usage and preserve water resources
efficient use and preservation of water

Examples

Schools are teaching children about water conservation to promote sustainable habits.
Drought conditions have increased the importance of water conservation across the state.
Simple steps like fixing leaks and turning off the tap can greatly aid water conservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservation - the prevention of wasteful use of a resource
