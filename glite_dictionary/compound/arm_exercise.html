arm exercise

1. NOUN

/ ɑrm ˈɛksərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ɑːm ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
a physical activity or movement designed to strengthen and improve the muscles of the arm
exercise for strengthening arm muscles

Examples

He follows a strict routine of arm exercise to build strength.
Using resistance bands is a popular form of arm exercise.
After weeks of consistent arm exercise, she noticed her muscles were more defined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - activity requiring physical effort to improve health or fitness
