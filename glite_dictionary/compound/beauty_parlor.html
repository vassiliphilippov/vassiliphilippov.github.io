beauty parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti ˌpɑːrlər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
a shop where hairdressers and beauticians provide hair, skin, and cosmetic treatments
shop offering hair and beauty treatments

Examples

She spent the afternoon at the beauty parlor getting her hair styled.
After work, they went to the beauty parlor for a manicure and facial.
The beauty parlor on Main Street just opened a new spa section.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty salon - a shop where hairdressers and beauticians work
  • Synonym: hairdresser - a person whose job is to cut, style, and care for hair
