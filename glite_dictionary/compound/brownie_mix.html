brownie mix

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊni mɪks / (AmE), / ˈbraʊni mɪks / (BrE)
a commercially prepared mixture containing the ingredients needed to make brownies, typically requiring the addition of a few liquid ingredients before baking
packaged mix for making brownies

Examples

I bought a box of brownie mix to make dessert for the party.
With just eggs and oil, you can turn brownie mix into delicious treats in no time.
She prefers making brownies from scratch rather than using brownie mix.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brownie - a small, rich, chocolate cake
