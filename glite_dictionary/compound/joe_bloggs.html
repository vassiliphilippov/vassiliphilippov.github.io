Joe Bloggs

1. NOUN

/ dʒoʊ blɑɡz / (AmE), / dəʊ ˈblɒɡz / (BrE)
A name used in British English to refer to an average, typical man; an ordinary person.
average or typical man (UK)
british

Examples

The instructions were written so that even Joe Bloggs could understand them.
In the competition, Joe Bloggs stood no chance against the professionals.
They wanted to know what the average Joe Bloggs thought about the new policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Doe - hypothetical average man, often used in legal contexts (US)
  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical human being

Etymology

Bloggs is used as a placeholder surname in British English; often paired with common first names like Joe or Fred.
