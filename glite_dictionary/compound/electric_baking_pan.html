electric baking pan

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbeɪkɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbeɪkɪŋ pan / (BrE)
An electrically powered pan used for baking, grilling, frying, and other cooking tasks.
electrically powered multi-function baking pan

Examples

We decided to cook the fish in the electric baking pan to get an even crust.
An electric baking pan can be used for making tortillas as well as grilling meat.
After the power outage, we couldn't use the electric baking pan to prepare breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electric skillet - an electrically heated frying pan
