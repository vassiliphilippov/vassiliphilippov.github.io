stammery response

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæməri rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈstaməri rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
A hesitant or faltering reply, often marked by stammering or nervousness.
hesitant or faltering reply

Examples

Her stammery response made it clear she was unprepared for the interview.
He tried to hide his anxiety, but his stammery response gave him away.
When asked about his whereabouts, his stammery response only raised more suspicion.

Synset Examples

Feeling nervous, he gave a stammery response to the question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halting reply - an uncertain or interrupted response
  • Antonym: confident answer - a sure and self-assured reply
