Saddam's Martyrs

1. PROPN

/ səˈdɑːmz ˈmɑr.tɚz / (AmE), / səˈdæmz ˈmɑː.təz / (BrE)
a feared paramilitary force established in 1995 by young soldiers loyal to Saddam Hussein, tasked with suppressing his domestic opponents
Iraqi paramilitary group loyal to Saddam Hussein (est. 1995)

Examples

The regime deployed Saddam's Martyrs to crush the uprising in the south.
Fear of Saddam's Martyrs kept many critics silent during the 1990s.
Many former members of Saddam's Martyrs went into hiding after the invasion.
