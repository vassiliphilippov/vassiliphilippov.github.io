bundle of energy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌndəl əv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ˈbʌndəl əv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
A person or animal who is extremely lively, enthusiastic, and energetic.
extremely energetic and lively person or animal

Examples

Ever since she woke up, Mia has been a bundle of energy, bouncing around the house.
The new puppy is a real bundle of energy and never seems to get tired.
Despite having very little sleep, John was still a bundle of energy at the morning meeting.

Synset Examples

She’s a bundle of energy, lighting up every room.
That puppy is a bundle of energy, always running around.
After coffee, I felt like a bundle of energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: live wire - a person who is energetic and full of enthusiasm
