unsmotherable passion

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈsmʌðərəbəl ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsmʌðərəbl ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
A passion that cannot be suppressed, stifled, or extinguished.
passion that cannot be suppressed

Examples

He had an unsmotherable passion for adventure that led him to travel the world.
Her unsmotherable passion for painting inspired everyone who visited her studio.
Despite the many obstacles, his unsmotherable passion for music never faded.

Synset Examples

He had an unsmotherable passion for adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrepressible passion - a passion that cannot be controlled or restrained
  • Antonym: suppressed passion - a passion that has been subdued or kept under control
