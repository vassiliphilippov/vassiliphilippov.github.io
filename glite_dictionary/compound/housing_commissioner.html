housing commissioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪŋ kəˌmɪʃənər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ kəˌmɪʃənə / (BrE)
an official responsible for overseeing and managing public housing programs, typically within a government agency
official in charge of public housing

Examples

The housing commissioner announced new policies to improve affordable housing across the city.
After years of service, the housing commissioner retired with a legacy of urban renewal projects.
The mayor appointed a new housing commissioner to tackle the city's growing homelessness crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commissioner - an official in charge of a department or agency
