cock gobbler

1. NOUN

/ kɑk ˈɡɑb.lɚ / (AmE), / kɒk ˈɡɒb.lə / (BrE)
A vulgar slang term for a person considered to be foolish or boastful, typically used in a derogatory or insulting manner.
vulgar insult for a foolish or boastful person
slang vulgar
