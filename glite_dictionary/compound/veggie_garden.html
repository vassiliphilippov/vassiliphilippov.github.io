veggie garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒi ˌɡɑrdən / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒi ˌɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
An area of ground where vegetables are grown, especially for personal use.
garden for growing vegetables
informal

Examples

We spent the weekend preparing the soil for our new veggie garden.
Fresh tomatoes from her veggie garden made the salad extra delicious.
Many people started a veggie garden during the lockdown to grow their own food.

Synset Examples

He finally planted a veggie garden in his backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable garden - a garden for growing vegetables
