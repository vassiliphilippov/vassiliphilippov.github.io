to sort by date

1. VERB

/ sɔrt baɪ deɪt / (AmE), / sɔːt baɪ deɪt / (BrE)
to arrange items or records in order according to their dates, usually from earliest to latest or vice versa
arrange items by date order

Examples

Please sort by date to see the most recent emails at the top.
The documents were sorted by date before being filed in the cabinet.
You can sort by date or name to organize the photos in this folder.

Synset Examples

Can you sort these files by date?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sort - to arrange systematically or classify
