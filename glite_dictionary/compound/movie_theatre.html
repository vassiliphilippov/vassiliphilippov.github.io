movie theatre

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌθiətɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
A building or room where films are shown to an audience.
cinema; place showing films

Examples

We decided to meet at the movie theatre to watch the new superhero film.
The movie theatre was packed on opening night for the popular comedy.
She works at a local movie theatre selling popcorn and tickets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema - A place where films are shown for public entertainment
