local milliner

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl mɪˈlɪnər / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl mɪˈlɪnə / (BrE)
a shop in a local area that specializes in making and selling women's hats
shop specializing in women's hats

Examples

She commissioned a beautiful hat from her local milliner for the wedding.
The local milliner displayed her new spring collection in the shop window.
Many customers visited the local milliner to prepare for Derby Day.

Synset Examples

She visited the local milliner for a custom piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milliner - a person who makes or sells women's hats
