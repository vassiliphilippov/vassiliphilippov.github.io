ahead of schedule

1. ADV

/ əˈhɛd əv ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / əˈhed əv ˈʃedjuːl / (BrE)
finished or completed before the planned or expected time
completed earlier than planned

Examples

The construction of the new bridge was completed ahead of schedule, impressing local officials.
If you finish your assignments ahead of schedule, you can take some time off.
The team managed to deliver the final report ahead of schedule this year.
