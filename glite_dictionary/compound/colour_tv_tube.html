colour TV tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌtiːˈviː tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtiːˈviː tjuːb / (BrE)
an electronic device in a television set that produces images in full color, typically using cathode ray tube (CRT) technology
tube producing color images in TVs
british

Examples

Many vintage televisions use a colour TV tube instead of modern display technologies.
The repairman replaced the broken colour TV tube in the old set.
A colour TV tube was considered a marvel of technology in its time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color TV tube - a television tube that displays images in full color (American spelling)
