hired gun

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪərd ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ɡʌn / (BrE)
a professional killer who is employed to kill someone, usually for money
professional killer for hire

Examples

The mafia boss sent a hired gun to eliminate his rival.
Police believe the murder was carried out by a hired gun rather than a personal enemy.
She discovered her business partner had secretly arranged for a hired gun to scare her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitman - a person hired to kill someone
  • Synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone important for political or religious reasons

2. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪɚd ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
a person who is employed to perform a specific task, especially one that is difficult or requires ruthlessness
person hired for a tough task

Examples

The company relied on a hired gun for the tough negotiations.
She made a reputation as a hired gun who could turn failing businesses around.
He became the firm's top hired gun for solving complex legal disputes.
