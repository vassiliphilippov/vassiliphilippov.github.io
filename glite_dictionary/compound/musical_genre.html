musical genre

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl ˈʒɑnrə / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈʒɒ̃rə / (BrE)
A category that identifies pieces of music as belonging to a shared tradition or set of conventions based on style, form, or content.
style or category of music

Examples

Jazz is a musical genre that originated in the African-American communities of New Orleans.
Her favorite musical genre is classical because of its complexity and elegance.
The awards ceremony recognized artists from every musical genre, from rock to hip-hop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music genre - A category or style of music distinguished by form, content, or tradition
  • Synonym: musical style - A style or manner of musical composition or performance

Etymology

Genre from French, meaning 'kind' or 'type'.
