garden truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
fresh fruits and vegetables grown for sale in the market
fresh produce for market

Examples

The local farmers brought their garden truck to the Saturday market in baskets and crates.
She set up a roadside stand to sell garden truck from her family's farm.
In the past, small towns relied on fresh garden truck delivered daily by local growers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: produce - fresh fruits and vegetables, especially as sold in markets

Etymology

‘Truck’ in this sense comes from Middle English ‘trukken’ (to exchange), meaning ‘trade’ or ‘barter’—not related to the vehicle.
