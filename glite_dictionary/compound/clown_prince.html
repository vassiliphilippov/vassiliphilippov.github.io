clown prince

1. NOUN

/ klaʊn prɪns / (AmE), / klaʊn prɪns / (BrE)
A person who is widely recognized as a leading figure in comedy, especially for their humorous or entertaining persona.
leading figure in comedy or humor

Examples

The comedian was often called the clown prince of the late-night show.
As the clown prince of the team, he always made everyone laugh.
His role as the clown prince helped lighten the mood during tense moments.

Synset Examples

He embraced his title as the clown prince with pride.
The clown prince always had the best jokes.
He was known as the clown prince of the comedy scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joker - a person who jokes or acts in a humorous way
