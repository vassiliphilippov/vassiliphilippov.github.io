strong coffee

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ˈkɑːfi / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee that has a strong, robust flavor or high concentration of coffee.
coffee with intense flavor or strength

Examples

I can't start my day without a cup of strong coffee to wake me up.
The waiter recommended their strong coffee to those who needed an energy boost.
After a long night, she craved nothing more than some strong coffee and toast.

Synset Examples

Shebrew is known for its strong coffee and cozy atmosphere.
I prefer strong coffee; weak tea just doesn't energize me.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weak coffee - coffee that is mild or diluted in flavor or strength
