three-legged

1. ADJ

/ θriːˈlɛɡɪd / (AmE), / θriːˈlɛɡɪd / (BrE)
Having three legs, or designed as if with three legs.
having three legs

Examples

The artist built a three-legged table out of recycled wood.
A three-legged race is a common event at school sports days.
Our old dog became three-legged after surgery but still runs happily.
