equestrian sport

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɛstriən spɔrt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɛstriən spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport or competitive activity involving horsemanship and skill in riding horses.
competitive horse riding sport

Examples

Jumping and dressage are both popular equestrian sports in international competitions.
She has trained for years to excel in equestrian sport, focusing on show jumping events.
The Olympics feature several equestrian sports, including eventing and dressage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseback riding - the act or sport of riding horses

Etymology

From Latin 'equestrianus' (of horsemen), from 'equus' (horse), and 'sport'.
