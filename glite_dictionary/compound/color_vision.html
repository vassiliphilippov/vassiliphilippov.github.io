color vision

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
the ability to perceive and distinguish different colors with the eyes
ability to see colors

Examples

Many animals have limited color vision compared to humans.
The test showed her color vision was perfectly normal.
People with impaired color vision may have difficulty distinguishing red from green.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: color blindness - inability to see colors normally
