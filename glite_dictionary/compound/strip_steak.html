strip steak

1. NOUN

/ strɪp steɪk / (AmE), / strɪp steɪk / (BrE)
a cut of beef steak from the upper part of the short loin, known for its tenderness and flavor
a tender beef steak cut from the short loin

Examples

He ordered a perfectly cooked strip steak for dinner at the restaurant.
The chef seasoned the strip steak with salt and pepper before grilling it.
At the butcher shop, she asked for two thick strip steaks for the weekend barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sirloin steak - a steak cut from the rear back portion of the animal, near the short loin
  • Degree Higher: tenderloin - the most tender cut of beef, found near the short loin
