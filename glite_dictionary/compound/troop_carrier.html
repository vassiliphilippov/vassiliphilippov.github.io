troop carrier

1. NOUN

/ truːp ˈkæriər / (AmE), / truːp ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a land, sea, or air vehicle designed to transport soldiers or military personnel
vehicle for transporting troops

Examples

The army used a troop carrier to move soldiers to the front lines.
During the mission, the troop carrier landed under heavy enemy fire.
Many paratroopers exited the troop carrier as it flew over the target area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transport - general term for vehicles or means of carrying things or people
