horse-godmother

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈɡɑdˌmʌðɚ / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈɡɒdˌmʌðə / (BrE)
an experienced person who mentors or guides others in horse riding or equestrian matters
equestrian mentor or guide

Examples

She is my horse-godmother, always encouraging me at the stables and giving advice when I need it most.
After struggling with my riding technique, I turned to my horse-godmother for help and reassurance.
Every beginner hopes to find a horse-godmother at their first riding club.

Synset Examples

She acts like my horse-godmother, always guiding me.
Every equestrian needs a horse-godmother for advice.
My horse-godmother helped me choose the right saddle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mentor - an experienced and trusted adviser
  • Synonym: fairy godmother - a person who unexpectedly helps or supports someone in need, especially in a mentor-like way

Etymology

Blend of 'horse' and 'godmother', modeled after 'fairy godmother'.
