field of operations

1. NOUN

/ fiːld əv ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / fiːld əv ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
an area where active military actions are taking place during a conflict or war
area of active military action

Examples

The general inspected the field of operations to assess the situation firsthand.
Many supplies must be delivered daily to support troops in the field of operations.
The division was redeployed from one field of operations to another during the campaign.

Synset Examples

the army was in the field awaiting action
he served in the Vietnam theater for three years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theater of operations - a region or area where military events occur
