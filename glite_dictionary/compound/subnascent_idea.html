subnascent idea

1. NOUN

/ sʌbˈnæsənt aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / sʌbˈnæsənt aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is in the earliest stages of development; an emerging or developing concept
an emerging or developing idea
