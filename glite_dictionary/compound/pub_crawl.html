pub crawl

1. NOUN

/ pʌb krɔl / (AmE), / pʌb krɔːl / (BrE)
an activity in which a group visits several pubs or bars in succession, typically having a drink at each one
tour of several pubs or bars

Examples

We organized a pub crawl for Lisa’s birthday, visiting five different bars in one night.
During the annual charity pub crawl, participants raise money while enjoying drinks at each stop.
A pub crawl is a fun way to discover new places in the city with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to bar hop - to move from one bar to another for drinks

2. NOUN

/ pʌb krɔl / (AmE), / pʌb krɔːl / (BrE)
an event or activity in which a group of people visits several pubs or bars in succession, typically drinking at each one
visiting several pubs in a single outing

Examples

We decided to organize a pub crawl through all the new bars in town.
Last year's pub crawl ended with everyone singing in the street.
She met her partner during a university pub crawl.
