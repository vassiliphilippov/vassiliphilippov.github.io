voice for radio

1. NOUN

/ vɔɪs fər ˈreɪdiˌoʊ / (AmE), / vɔɪs fə ˈreɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
a voice that is particularly well-suited for radio broadcasting, often meaning it is pleasant, clear, and engaging
a voice well-suited for radio

Examples

Her voice for radio made her popular as a host on morning shows.
Many people think he has a voice for radio but not a face for television.
The announcer's voice for radio is deep, rich, and instantly recognizable.

Synset Examples

He's got a face for television and a voice for radio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio voice - a pleasant or effective voice for speaking on the radio
