finger scanning

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡər ˌskænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌskænɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of electronically scanning a person's fingerprints for biometric identification
electronic fingerprint biometric identification

Examples

Airport security uses finger scanning to verify the identity of travelers.
Some smartphones now unlock with finger scanning instead of passwords.
The company installed finger scanning devices to control access to restricted areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fingerprint recognition - the identification of a person using fingerprints
