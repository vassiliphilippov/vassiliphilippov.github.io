Republic of Cote d'Ivoire

1. PROPN

/ ɹɪˈpʌblɪk əv koʊt diˈvwɑɹ / (AmE), / ɹɪˈpʌblɪk ɒv kəʊt diˈvwɑː / (BrE)
a country in western Africa on the Gulf of Guinea, known for its relative prosperity and political stability
country in West Africa

Examples

The Republic of Cote d'Ivoire is renowned for its cocoa production.
Officials from the Republic of Cote d'Ivoire attended the African Union summit.
Tourism in the Republic of Cote d'Ivoire has been increasing steadily in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ivory Coast - alternative name for Republic of Cote d'Ivoire

Etymology

French Cote d'Ivoire means 'Ivory Coast'.
