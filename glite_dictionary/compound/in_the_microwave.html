in the microwave

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə ˈmaɪkroʊˌweɪv / (AmE), / ɪn ðə ˈmaɪkrəʊˌweɪv / (BrE)
using a microwave oven to heat or cook food or drink.
using a microwave oven

Examples

She usually reheats leftovers in the microwave when she's in a hurry.
Please defrost the chicken in the microwave before cooking it.
My oatmeal is ready after just two minutes in the microwave.
