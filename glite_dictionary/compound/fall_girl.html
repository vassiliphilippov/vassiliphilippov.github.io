fall girl

1. NOUN

/ fɔl ɡɜrl / (AmE), / fɔːl ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A woman who is easily deceived or taken advantage of; a gullible female, sometimes used for someone who is made to take the blame for others' misdeeds.
gullible woman; scapegoat

Examples

She was always the fall girl in every scheme, ending up blamed for mistakes she hadn't made.
In the con, they needed a fall girl to distract suspicion from the real culprit.
Don't let yourself be the fall girl when trouble starts brewing at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall guy - a person who is easily deceived or blamed for something

Etymology

Modelled after 'fall guy', with 'fall' referring to being duped or set up for blame.
