antiattrition measure

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.ti.əˈtrɪʃ.ən ˈmɛʒ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.əˈtrɪʃ.ən ˈmɛʒ.ə / (BrE)
a strategy or method designed to reduce employee turnover and retain staff
employee retention strategy

Examples

The company adopted several antiattrition measures to improve employee satisfaction.
Effective antiattrition measures can help reduce costs associated with hiring and training new staff.
HR managers regularly review their antiattrition measures to ensure a stable workforce.

Synset Examples

We're focusing on antiattrition measures this quarter.
His antiattrition methods reduced turnover significantly.
The company implemented antiattrition strategies to keep employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retention strategy - a plan to keep employees in an organization
  • Antonym: employee turnover - the rate at which employees leave an organization

Etymology

From 'anti-' meaning 'against' and 'attrition' (gradual reduction), referring to methods against staff reduction.
