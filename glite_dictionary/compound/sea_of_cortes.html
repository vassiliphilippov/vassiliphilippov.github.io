Sea of Cortes

1. PROPN

/ ˌsi əv kɔrˈteɪz / (AmE), / ˌsiː əv kɔːˈteɪz / (BrE)
A gulf located to the west of the mainland of Mexico, also known as the Gulf of California.
gulf west of Mexico's mainland

Examples

Many marine biologists conduct research in the Sea of Cortes due to its rich biodiversity.
The yacht sailed along the coast of the Sea of Cortes during the summer expedition.
Tourists are drawn to the Sea of Cortes for its stunning views and unique wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gulf of California - another name for the Sea of Cortes

Etymology

Named after Hernán Cortés, Spanish conquistador.
