California olive

1. NOUN

/ kæləˌfɔrniə ˈɑlɪv / (AmE), / kælɪˌfɔːniə ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
a tree of the Pacific coast (genus Osmanthus) with aromatic foliage, small clusters of flowers, olivelike fruit, and hard durable wood
Pacific coast tree with olivelike fruit

Examples

The California olive is prized for its tough wood and drought resistance.
In spring, the California olive produces clusters of fragrant, creamy flowers.
You can find California olive trees growing wild along the coastal hills of California.
