backup man

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌʌp mæn / (AmE), / ˈbækʌp man / (BrE)
a man who replaces or supports another, especially in dangerous or difficult situations
substitute or supporting man

Examples

When the team leader was injured, the backup man stepped in to finish the task.
In risky police operations, a backup man is always assigned as extra support.
They designated a backup man in case anything went wrong during the climb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stand-in - a person who takes the place of another, especially temporarily
  • Synonym: substitute - a person who replaces another
