block plane

1. NOUN

/ blɑk pleɪn / (AmE), / blɒk pleɪn / (BrE)
a small hand plane used chiefly for smoothing or trimming the end grains of wood
small woodworking plane for end grains

Examples

He used a block plane to smooth the rough edges of the shelf.
A block plane is ideal for trimming the end grain of a piece of wood.
The carpenter reached for his block plane to finish the woodworking project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a tool for smoothing or shaping wood
