park bench

1. NOUN

/ pɑrk bɛntʃ / (AmE), / pɑːk bɛntʃ / (BrE)
a long seat, typically with a back, found in public parks for people to sit on
long seat in a public park

Examples

She sat on a park bench and read her book in the afternoon sun.
The old park bench was painted green by local volunteers.
They met every Saturday on the same park bench to feed the pigeons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bench - a long seat for several people
