stamp of approval

1. NOUN

/ stæmp əv əˈpruːvəl / (AmE), / stæmp əv əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
an official sign of endorsement or acceptance of something
official endorsement or acceptance

Examples

The project won't proceed until it receives the stamp of approval from the board.
Her proposal quickly got the stamp of approval from the committee.
Getting the manager’s stamp of approval made everyone more confident about the plan.

Synset Examples

He received a stamp of approval from his boss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endorsement - the act of giving one's public approval or support
  • Synonym: seal of approval - an official mark or statement showing something is accepted
