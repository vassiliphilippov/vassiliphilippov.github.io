costume designer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːstuːm dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈkɒstjuːm dɪˈzaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design or supply costumes, especially for plays, films, or other performances
person who designs costumes for productions

Examples

The costume designer created stunning outfits for the actors in the period drama.
As a costume designer, she works closely with the director to achieve the right look.
Every successful production relies on a talented costume designer to bring its characters to life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wardrobe - the department or collection of costumes used in a play or movie
  • Degree Lower: fashion designer - a person who designs clothing and accessories
