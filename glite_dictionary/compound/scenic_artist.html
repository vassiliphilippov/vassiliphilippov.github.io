scenic artist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːnɪk ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈsiːnɪk ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
an artist who specializes in painting or designing scenery, especially for stage productions, film, or television.
artist specializing in scenery for stage or film

Examples

She worked as the scenic artist on several major Broadway shows.
The scenic artist skillfully recreated historical backdrops for the film.
A talented scenic artist can transform a bare stage into a vivid landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenic designer - a person who designs sets and scenery for productions
