Old North State

1. PROPN

/ oʊld nɔrθ steɪt / (AmE), / əʊld nɔːθ steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of North Carolina, one of the original thirteen colonies of the United States
nickname for North Carolina, USA

Examples

Many people know North Carolina as the Old North State.
The Old North State is famous for its beautiful coastline and mountain ranges.
He moved to the Old North State for its warm weather and rich history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Carolina - a state in the southeastern United States

Etymology

Refers to North Carolina's historic position as the northernmost of the two Carolinas; attested since the 18th century.
