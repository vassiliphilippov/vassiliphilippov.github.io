indelible mark

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɛləbəl mɑrk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdeləbl mɑːk / (BrE)
a lasting effect, impression, or influence that cannot be forgotten or erased
lasting impression or effect

Examples

The tragedy left an indelible mark on the community.
Her words made an indelible mark on everyone who heard them.
Winning the championship created an indelible mark in the team's history.

Synset Examples

The memories of that trip left an indelible mark on me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lasting impression - an impression or effect that endures for a long time
