stamp battery

1. NOUN

/ stæmp ˈbæt̬.ɚ.i / (AmE), / stæmp ˈbæt.ər.i / (BrE)
A set of heavy stamps operated together in one mortar for crushing ores, especially in gold mining.
machine for crushing ores

Examples

The old gold mine was equipped with a stamp battery to crush the quartz rock.
Engineers restored the historic stamp battery as part of the mining museum exhibit.
The noise from the stamp battery could be heard from miles away during the rush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamp mill - a mill in which ore is crushed with stamps
