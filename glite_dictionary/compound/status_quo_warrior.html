status quo warrior

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtəs ˌkwoʊ ˈwɔriər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtəs ˌkwəʊ ˈwɒriə / (BrE)
a person who vigorously defends or supports the existing state of affairs, opposing change or reform
person who opposes change; defends existing situation

Examples

As a status quo warrior, she resisted any changes at the office.
Every organization seems to have at least one status quo warrior blocking innovation.
The debate became heated between the reformers and the status quo warrior in the group.

Synset Examples

As a status quo warrior, she resisted any changes at the office.
He's known as the status quo warrior in our team meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservative - a person who is averse to change and holds traditional values
  • Antonym: progressive - a person favoring or advocating progress, change, or reform
