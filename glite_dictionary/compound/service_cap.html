service cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrvɪs kæp / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs kæp / (BrE)
a flat, circular-topped cap with a visor, worn as part of a military or official uniform
military or official uniform cap

Examples

The officer placed his service cap neatly on the table before the meeting began.
All soldiers are required to wear their service cap during the parade.
He adjusted his service cap before stepping onto the stage for the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peaked cap - a similar military or uniform cap with a visor, also called a peaked cap
  • Degree Lower: beret - a round, flat, soft cap, often used in the military but lacks a visor
