silicone rubber

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlɪkoʊn ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪkəʊn ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
a synthetic rubber made from silicone elastomers, known for staying flexible, resilient, and strong over a wide range of temperatures
a flexible synthetic rubber made from silicone

Examples

The baking mat was made of silicone rubber, so it could withstand high oven temperatures.
Engine gaskets often use silicone rubber due to its excellent thermal stability.
He purchased a silicone rubber seal to replace the worn one in the bathroom sink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastomer - a polymer with elastic properties
