hourglass-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊərˌɡlæs ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈaʊəɡlɑːs ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a shape that resembles an hourglass, with a narrow middle and wider upper and lower sections.
having the shape of an hourglass

Examples

She wore a dress that accentuated her hourglass-shaped figure.
The designer created an hourglass-shaped vase as part of the new collection.
That building’s striking, hourglass-shaped silhouette stands out against the city skyline.
