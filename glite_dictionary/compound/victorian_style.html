Victorian style

1. NOUN

/ vɪkˈtɔriən staɪl / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriən staɪl / (BrE)
a decorative style associated with the reign of Queen Victoria, characterized by ornate details, rich colors, and eclectic revival of earlier historical styles
ornate style from Queen Victoria's era

Examples

Their living room features antique furniture and wallpaper in the Victorian style.
The mansion was restored to its original Victorian style, complete with intricate woodwork.
She chose a Victorian style wedding dress with lace and elaborate details.

Synset Examples

Her home is decorated in a charming Victorian style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Victorian - relating to the period of Queen Victoria's reign

Etymology

Named after Queen Victoria (reigned 1837–1901), whose era influenced the style.
