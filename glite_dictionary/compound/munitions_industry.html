munitions industry

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɪʃənz ˌɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / mjʊˈnɪʃənz ˌɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
the sector of industry concerned with the manufacture of weapons and military equipment
industry producing weapons and military equipment

Examples

The munitions industry experienced rapid growth during the war years.
Government contracts provided a significant boost to the munitions industry.
Some activists protested against the influence of the munitions industry on foreign policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arms industry - the industry responsible for manufacturing and selling weapons and military technology

Etymology

From 'munition' (from Latin 'munitio', meaning fortification or defense) and 'industry'.
