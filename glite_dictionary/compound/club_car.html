club car

1. NOUN

/ klʌb kɑr / (AmE), / klʌb kɑː / (BrE)
a railroad car fitted with a bar, tables, and lounge chairs for passengers' socializing and relaxation
railroad car with bar and lounge area

Examples

The train's club car was crowded with passengers enjoying drinks after dinner.
We spent the evening playing cards in the comfortable club car.
She preferred to relax in the club car rather than in her regular seat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining car - railroad car where meals are served to passengers
