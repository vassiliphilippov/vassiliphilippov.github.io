gas washing bottle

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈwɑʃɪŋ ˌbɑtəl / (AmE), / ɡas ˈwɒʃɪŋ ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a laboratory bottle used to saturate a liquid with a gas by bubbling the gas through it
laboratory bottle for gas-liquid saturation

Examples

The chemist used a gas washing bottle to purify the air before passing it into the reaction vessel.
Hydrogen chloride gas is often introduced into solution through a gas washing bottle.
Remember to check that the gas washing bottle contains enough liquid before starting the experiment.
