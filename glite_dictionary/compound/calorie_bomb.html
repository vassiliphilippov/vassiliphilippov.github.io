calorie bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.ə.ri bɑm / (AmE), / ˈkæl.əri bɒm / (BrE)
A food item that is extremely high in calories, often considered unhealthy due to its high energy content.
very high-calorie food item
informal

Examples

The triple cheeseburger was a total calorie bomb, but it tasted amazing.
She avoided the calorie bomb desserts at the party and chose fresh fruit instead.
Ordering the extra-large fries turned my lunch into a real calorie bomb.

Synset Examples

Eating that pizza was a calorie bomb I regret.
That burger is a calorie bomb, I can't resist it.
This dessert is a total calorie bomb!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk food - food that is unhealthy and high in calories, fat, or sugar
