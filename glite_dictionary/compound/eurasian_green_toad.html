Eurasian green toad

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈreɪʒən ɡrin toʊd / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒən ɡriːn təʊd / (BrE)
a species of toad native to Eurasia, characterized by its variable but mainly green coloration
species of toad with green coloring native to Eurasia

Examples

The Eurasian green toad is commonly found in gardens and open fields across southern Europe.
Scientists are studying the breeding habits of the Eurasian green toad to better understand amphibian adaptation.
During spring, the distinctive calls of the Eurasian green toad can be heard near ponds and streams.
