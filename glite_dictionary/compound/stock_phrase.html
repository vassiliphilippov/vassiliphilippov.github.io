stock phrase

1. NOUN

/ stɑk freɪz / (AmE), / stɒk freɪz / (BrE)
a commonly used expression or saying, often regarded as overused or clichéd
a commonly used or clichéd expression

Examples

The politician ended his speech with the same stock phrase he'd used countless times before.
Whenever he's uncertain what to say, he repeats a familiar stock phrase.
In job interviews, try to avoid using too many stock phrases in your answers.

Synset Examples

He relied on a stock phrase to sound more professional.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliché - an overused or unoriginal phrase
