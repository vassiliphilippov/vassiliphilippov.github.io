bubble and squeak

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl ənd skwik / (AmE), / ˌbʌb.əl ənd ˈskwiːk / (BrE)
A British dish made by frying leftover cooked cabbage and potatoes, sometimes with meat.
British dish of fried cabbage and potatoes
british

Examples

We had bubble and squeak for breakfast, made from last night's leftovers.
Traditional bubble and squeak is often served with eggs or bacon.
She learned how to make bubble and squeak from her grandmother.

Etymology

The name is from the sounds made while cooking the dish.
