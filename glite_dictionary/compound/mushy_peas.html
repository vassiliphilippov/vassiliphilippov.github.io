mushy peas

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌʃi piːz / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃi piːz / (BrE)
A dish of marrowfat peas that have been soaked overnight, boiled until soft, and often served with fish and chips.
soft-boiled peas dish (UK)
british

Examples

Every Friday night, I order fish, chips, and mushy peas at the local pub.
The classic British side dish, mushy peas, pairs perfectly with flaky fried fish.
She learned to make homemade mushy peas from her grandmother's recipe.
