Cheviot Hills

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃɛviət hɪlz / (AmE), / ˈʃɛvɪət hɪlz / (BrE)
a range of hills forming part of the border between northern England and southern Scotland
hills on the England-Scotland border

Examples

The Cheviot Hills are popular with hikers seeking scenic views along the border.
Many ancient battles were fought in the shadow of the Cheviot Hills.
She spent her childhood in a village nestled among the Cheviot Hills.
