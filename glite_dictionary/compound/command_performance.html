command performance

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmænd pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːnd pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an outstanding or exceptional performance, often given to impress important people or audiences
outstanding, impressive performance

Examples

Her command performance at the awards ceremony drew praise from critics and fans alike.
The pianist delivered a command performance that brought the audience to its feet.
Everyone agreed it was a true command performance that would be remembered for years.

Synset Examples

He was praised for his command performance during the show.
The actor delivered a command performance that wowed everyone.
Her command performance earned her a standing ovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outstanding performance - a performance that is notably excellent or impressive

Etymology

From 'command' (order or request), originally referring to performances ordered by a monarch or other important figure.
