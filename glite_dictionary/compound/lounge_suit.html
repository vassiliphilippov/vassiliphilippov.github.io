lounge suit

1. NOUN

/ laʊndʒ suːt / (AmE), / laʊndʒ suːt / (BrE)
a suit consisting of a matching jacket and trousers or skirt, worn for business or informal occasions
type of matching business suit

Examples

He wore a smart lounge suit to the interview.
The invitation specified that guests should dress in a lounge suit or cocktail dress.
Her navy lounge suit looked both professional and stylish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business suit - a formal suit worn for business

Etymology

From 'lounge' (a room for sitting) + 'suit'; originally referring to informal suits for urban wear.
