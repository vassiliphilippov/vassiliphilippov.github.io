mental abnormality

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬əl æb.nɔrˈmæl.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl æb.nɔːˈmæl.ə.ti / (BrE)
a condition in which a person's mental functions deviate from what is considered normal or healthy
deviation from normal mental function

Examples

The defendant's mental abnormality was considered during the trial.
Early diagnosis of a mental abnormality can improve treatment outcomes.
Researchers are studying the genetic basis of certain mental abnormalities in children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental disorder - a disorder that affects a person's mood, thinking, or behavior
  • Antonym: mental health - the state of well-being in which a person realizes their abilities
