tax assessment

1. NOUN

/ tæks əˈsɛsmənt / (AmE), / tæks əˈsɛsmənt / (BrE)
an official valuation of property or income used to determine the amount of tax owed
official valuation for tax purposes

Examples

The county conducted a tax assessment to determine the new property tax rates.
After the tax assessment, homeowners received notices of their updated property values.
She disputed the tax assessment because she believed her house was overvalued.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appraisal - an act of assessing or estimating the value of something, especially property
