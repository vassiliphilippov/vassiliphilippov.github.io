time sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪm ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm ʃiːt / (BrE)
a document used for recording the amount of time an employee has worked during a specific period
record of hours worked by employees

Examples

Every week, employees must submit their time sheet for approval.
I forgot to fill out my time sheet before leaving the office.
The company uses a digital time sheet system to track attendance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timesheet - alternative spelling for 'time sheet', same meaning
