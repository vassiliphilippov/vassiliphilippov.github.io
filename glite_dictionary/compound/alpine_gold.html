alpine gold

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn goʊld / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ɡəʊld / (BrE)
a low, tufted plant of the northwestern United States with hairy stems, each topped by a yellow flower head with short, narrow petals.
tufted yellow-flowered plant of NW US

Examples

The bright yellow blooms of alpine gold can be spotted along mountain trails in spring.
Gardeners admire alpine gold for its resilience in rocky, challenging soils.
She collected samples of alpine gold for her wildflower research project.
