interactive science experiment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈæk.tɪv ˈsaɪəns ɪkˈspɛr.ə.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈræk.tɪv ˈsaɪəns ɪkˈspɛr.ɪ.mənt / (BrE)
a science experiment designed to actively involve participants in hands-on learning and engagement
hands-on, engaging science activity

Examples

The class participated in an interactive science experiment that demonstrated the principles of magnetism.
Teachers often use an interactive science experiment to make lessons more memorable for students.
She presented her project as an interactive science experiment at the school fair.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the interactive science experiment in class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: science experiment - a test conducted to discover or demonstrate a scientific principle
