surveyor's level

1. NOUN

/ sɚˈveɪ.ɚz ˈlɛv.əl / (AmE), / sɜːˈveɪ.əz ˈlɛv.əl / (BrE)
a surveying instrument consisting of a small telescope with an attached spirit level, mounted to rotate around a vertical axis, used to measure relative heights of land
surveying instrument for measuring heights

Examples

The construction team used a surveyor's level to ensure the foundation was perfectly horizontal.
Precision in land measurement requires a surveyor's level and a steady hand.
He adjusted the surveyor's level before recording the elevation data.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: level - instrument for establishing a horizontal plane
