crowberry family

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊˌbɛri ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊˌbɛri ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of heathlike shrubs, scientifically known as Empetraceae, found in cool temperate regions
a family of heathlike shrubs (Empetraceae)

Examples

The crowberry family is commonly found in arctic and subarctic regions.
Botanists studied several species within the crowberry family for their ecological impact.
Some members of the crowberry family produce edible, small black berries.
