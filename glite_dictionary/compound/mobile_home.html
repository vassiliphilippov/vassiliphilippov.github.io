mobile home

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.bəl hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.bəl həʊm / (BrE)
a large prefabricated structure, typically equipped with utilities, that can be transported and used as a permanent residence
prefabricated transportable home

Examples

They decided to buy a mobile home and move to the countryside.
Many retirees choose to live in a mobile home community for its affordability.
Their mobile home was parked near the lake, offering a beautiful view every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trailer - a vehicle equipped for living in, designed to be towed
