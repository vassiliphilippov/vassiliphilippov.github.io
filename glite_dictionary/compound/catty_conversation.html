catty conversation

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæti ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkæti ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a conversation that is spiteful or malicious, often involving unkind remarks about others
spiteful or malicious talk

Examples

Their catty conversation quickly escalated into a heated exchange of insults.
I prefer to stay out of catty conversations that involve talking behind others' backs.
Some reality TV shows thrive on the drama of catty conversation between contestants.

Synset Examples

I try to avoid catty conversations at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malicious gossip - idle talk with intent to harm
  • Antonym: friendly conversation - pleasant or amicable exchange

Etymology

From 'catty' (spiteful, malicious) + 'conversation'. 'Catty' is from comparing sharp, mean comments to those stereotypically attributed to a cat.
