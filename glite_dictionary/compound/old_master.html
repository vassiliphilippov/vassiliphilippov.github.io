old master

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈmæstər / (AmE), / əʊld ˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
a great European painter, especially one from before the 19th century, or a painting by such an artist
pre-19th-century European painter

Examples

The museum is famous for its rich collection of old masters from the Italian Renaissance.
Many art collectors seek out works by old masters such as Rembrandt and Vermeer.
She specializes in restoring paintings by the old masters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Renaissance painter - an artist from the European Renaissance period
  • Antonym: modern artist - an artist working after the early 19th century
