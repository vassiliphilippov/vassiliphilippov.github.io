nopey attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊpi ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈnəʊpi ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a dismissive or negative attitude, often characterized by a tendency to refuse, disagree, or be uncooperative
dismissive or negative attitude
informal

Examples

Her nopey attitude during meetings made the team less productive.
Whenever asked to help, he responded with a nopey attitude that discouraged others.
That nopey attitude isn’t going to get us anywhere.

Synset Examples

Her nopey attitude made it hard to collaborate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negative attitude - a pessimistic or unconstructive mindset
  • Antonym: cooperative attitude - a willing and helpful mindset
