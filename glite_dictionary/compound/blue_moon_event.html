blue moon event

1. NOUN

/ bluː muːn ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / bluː muːn ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
An event or occurrence that is very rare or infrequent.
a very rare event

Examples

Winning the lottery is a blue moon event for most people.
It was a blue moon event when the small town hosted an international music festival.
Discovering a painting by a famous artist at a garage sale is truly a blue moon event.

Synset Examples

Finding a job like that is a blue moon event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: once in a blue moon - very rarely; almost never

Etymology

From the phrase 'once in a blue moon', where 'blue moon' refers to a rare lunar event.
