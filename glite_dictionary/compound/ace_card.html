ace card

1. NOUN

/ eɪs kɑrd / (AmE), / eɪs kɑːd / (BrE)
A key advantage, resource, or strategy used to achieve success, especially one kept in reserve for a decisive moment.
decisive advantage or strategy

Examples

Sarah kept her ace card until the final negotiation round.
The team's ace card was their experienced striker who scored in the last minute.
He revealed his ace card only when all other options had failed.

Synset Examples

He played the ace card during the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trump card - a winning card or decisive resource
  • Synonym: secret weapon - a hidden advantage or asset used to ensure success
