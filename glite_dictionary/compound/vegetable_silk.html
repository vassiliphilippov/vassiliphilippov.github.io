vegetable silk

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ sɪlk / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl sɪlk / (BrE)
a soft, silky plant fiber obtained from the kapok tree, used for stuffing pillows and insulation
plant fiber from kapok tree

Examples

Pillows filled with vegetable silk are light and comfortable.
The insulation material was made from vegetable silk harvested in Southeast Asia.
Because vegetable silk is naturally buoyant, it was once used in life jackets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kapok - the silky fibers from the seed pods of the kapok tree
