mail car

1. NOUN

/ meɪl kɑr / (AmE), / meɪl kɑː / (BrE)
a railway car designed for transporting and sorting mail while the train is in transit
railway car for transporting mail

Examples

The old mail car on display at the museum shows how postal workers sorted letters as the train traveled.
During the journey, the mail car was bustling with activity as clerks sorted thousands of parcels.
Security measures in the mail car were strict to prevent theft of valuable packages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway car - a vehicle on a train for carrying passengers or goods
  • Synonym: postal van - a vehicle used for transporting mail
