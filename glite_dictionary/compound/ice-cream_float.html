ice-cream float

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪs ˌkrim floʊt / (AmE), / ˈaɪs ˌkriːm fləʊt / (BrE)
A cold drink consisting of ice cream floating in a soft drink or soda.
drink with ice cream and soda

Examples

After a hot day in the sun, he ordered an ice-cream float to cool off.
The diner is famous for its classic ice-cream float served with cola and vanilla ice cream.
She made an ice-cream float at home using orange soda and a scoop of vanilla.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: root beer float - a type of ice-cream float made with root beer
