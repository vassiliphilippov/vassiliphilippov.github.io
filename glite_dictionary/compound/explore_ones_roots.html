to explore one's roots

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈsplɔɚ wʌnz ruːts / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɔː wʌnz ruːts / (BrE)
to investigate or learn about one's family origins, ancestry, or cultural heritage
investigate one's ancestry or heritage

Examples

Many people travel abroad to explore their roots and gain a deeper understanding of their family history.
She spent years researching family trees to explore her roots and connect with distant relatives.
Moving to Ireland gave him the opportunity to explore his roots and learn more about his ancestors.
