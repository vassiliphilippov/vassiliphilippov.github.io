completely and utterly

1. ADV

/ kəmˈplitli ənd ˈʌtɚli / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːtli ənd ˈʌtəli / (BrE)
used for emphasis to mean totally and absolutely; to the greatest possible extent
totally and absolutely; for emphasis

Examples

I was completely and utterly lost after the first turn in the city.
The garden has been completely and utterly transformed since last year.
He found himself completely and utterly alone in the empty house.

Synset Examples

I am completely and utterly satisfied with the service.
She was completely and utterly shocked by the surprise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - totally; completely
  • Synonym: completely - entirely; fully
  • Antonym: partially - only in part; not completely
