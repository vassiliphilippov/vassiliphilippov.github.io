sparkplug wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑrkˌplʌg ˌrɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɑːkˌplʌɡ ˌrɛntʃ / (BrE)
a tool designed to remove or tighten spark plugs in the cylinder head of an internal combustion engine
tool for removing or tightening spark plugs

Examples

Make sure you have a sparkplug wrench before attempting to replace your car's spark plugs.
He used a sparkplug wrench to loosen the stubborn plug from the engine.
Without a proper sparkplug wrench, the job would have taken much longer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrench - a tool used for gripping and turning objects such as pipes and fasteners
