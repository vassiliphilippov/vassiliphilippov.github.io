gas oven

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈʌvən / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈʌvən / (BrE)
A domestic oven that is powered by gas, used for cooking and baking.
oven powered by gas

Examples

We decided to replace our old stove with a new gas oven for better temperature control.
The recipe suggests using a gas oven, but it can work in an electric one too.
Every evening, delicious smells filled the kitchen as the bread baked in the gas oven.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: electric oven - an oven powered by electricity

2. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈəvən / (AmE), / ɡas ˈʌvən / (BrE)
A cremation chamber that is fueled by gas for incinerating bodies.
cremation chamber using gas

Examples

The facility uses a modern gas oven for efficient cremation services.
During the inspection, authorities examined the gas oven for compliance with safety regulations.
The gas oven can reach extremely high temperatures necessary for cremation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crematorium - a building containing a furnace for cremating bodies
