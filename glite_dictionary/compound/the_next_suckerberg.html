the next Suckerberg

1. NOUN

/ ðə nɛkst ˈsʌkərˌbɝːɡ / (AmE), / ðə nɛkst ˈsʌkəbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a person aspiring to become extremely successful in social media or as a tech entrepreneur, especially by emulating high-profile figures like Mark Zuckerberg
aspiring top tech entrepreneur
informal

Examples

After selling his app, people began calling him the next Suckerberg in tech magazines.
She dreams of launching a startup and becoming the next Suckerberg in the industry.
Journalists are always searching for the next Suckerberg to interview about the future of social media.

Synset Examples

Everyone wants to be the next Suckerberg in tech.
He joked about being the next Suckerberg of social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the next big thing - a person or thing likely to become very important or successful

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'the next Zuckerberg', using a play on Mark Zuckerberg's name.
