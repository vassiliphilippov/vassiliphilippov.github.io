meat grinder

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈɡraɪndər / (AmE), / miːt ˈɡraɪndə / (BrE)
a kitchen device or machine used for cutting or finely mincing meat by forcing it through small holes
device for finely mincing meat

Examples

My grandmother uses a meat grinder to make her own sausage at home.
The butcher put the steak through the meat grinder twice for the perfect texture.
Cleaning the meat grinder after use is important to prevent bacteria buildup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mincer - a kitchen appliance for cutting or mincing food, especially meat
