athletic type

1. NOUN

/ əθˈlɛtɪk taɪp / (AmE), / æθˈlɛtɪk taɪp / (BrE)
a person who is muscular and has a large, robust bone structure
muscular, large-boned person

Examples

She has always been considered an athletic type, with broad shoulders and strong legs.
Many sports teams look for the athletic type when recruiting new players.
His doctor described him as an athletic type because of his muscular build.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ectomorph - a person with a lean and delicate body build
  • Antonym: endomorph - a person with a soft and rounder body type
