target market

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡət ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
the particular group of consumers at which a product, service, or marketing campaign is aimed
specific group aimed for marketing

Examples

The new smartphone was designed to appeal strongly to its target market of young professionals.
Understanding your target market can help you develop more effective advertising strategies.
The advertisement failed to reach its target market and resulted in poor sales.

Synset Examples

The company created a detailed profile of their target market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audience - the people reached by a book, radio or television programme, etc.
  • Synonym: niche market - a small, specialized market for a particular product or service
