lamb's lettuce

1. NOUN

/ læmz ˈlɛtʃɪs / (AmE), / læmz ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a small leafy green plant (Valerianella locusta) often grown for use in salads; also known as corn salad
a leafy salad plant (Valerianella locusta)

Examples

She sprinkled lamb's lettuce on top of the salad for a fresh, nutty flavor.
Lamb's lettuce grows well in cool weather and is easy to harvest for home gardens.
At the farmers' market, you can often find organic lamb's lettuce alongside other leafy greens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn salad - another term for lamb's lettuce, same plant
