jazz standard

1. NOUN

/ dʒæz ˈstændɚd / (AmE), / dʒæz ˈstændəd / (BrE)
A well-known musical composition that is widely recognized and performed within the jazz genre.
classic, widely played jazz piece

Examples

Miles Davis recorded many jazz standards that have become essential listening for all jazz musicians.
The band performed several jazz standards at the club, delighting the audience with familiar melodies.
Learning to play jazz standards is an important step for anyone wanting to master improvisation.

Synset Examples

That solo was good enough for jazz standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard - a musical composition regarded as a permanent part of a repertoire, especially in jazz
