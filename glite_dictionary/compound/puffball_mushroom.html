puffball mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌfˌbɔl ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈpʌfbɔːl ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
A type of round, ball-shaped fungus that releases spores in a cloud when mature and disturbed, commonly found in fields and forests.
round, spore-releasing fungus

Examples

We saw a large puffball mushroom growing near the old tree stump.
The children were fascinated when the puffball mushroom released a cloud of spores.
It is best to identify a puffball mushroom carefully before considering it edible.

Synset Examples

He found a puffball mushroom in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffball - A type of fungus producing a ball-shaped fruiting body

Etymology

From 'puffball' (referring to the spore-puffing appearance) + 'mushroom'.
