color television

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
a television that displays images in color rather than black and white
television that shows images in color
american

Examples

My grandparents bought their first color television in the 1970s.
The documentary aired in stunning detail on my new color television.
She remembers when color television was a luxury in most households.
