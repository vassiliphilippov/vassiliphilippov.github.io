morning smoothie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˈsmuði / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
a blended drink made with fruits, vegetables, or other ingredients, typically consumed at breakfast time
blended breakfast drink

Examples

I like to pack my morning smoothie with spinach and berries for extra nutrition.
She always drinks a morning smoothie before heading to work.
You can prepare your morning smoothie the night before to save time.

Synset Examples

He added a banana to his morning smoothie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a thick, cold beverage made from blended fruit, vegetables, and liquids
