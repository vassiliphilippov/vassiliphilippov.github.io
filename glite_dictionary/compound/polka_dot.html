polka dot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊlkə dɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɒlkə dɒt / (BrE)
a pattern consisting of regularly spaced circular spots, typically used on fabric or clothing
regularly spaced circular spot pattern

Examples

She wore a lovely dress covered in polka dots to the summer party.
The curtains in the nursery have a playful polka dot pattern in bright colors.
Retro swimsuits with bold polka dot designs are making a fashion comeback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dot - a small round mark or spot

Etymology

From the dance name 'polka' (originating in Czech 'polka'), combined with 'dot'; 19th-century fashion promotion drew metaphorical connection to the lively dance.
