living stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
a small African succulent plant of the genus Lithops, with thick leaves resembling stones and solitary yellow or white flowers
succulent plant with stone-like leaves

Examples

Many collectors prize the unusual appearance of living stone plants in their succulent gardens.
The living stone survives harsh climates by mimicking surrounding pebbles to avoid being eaten.
When the living stone blooms, it produces a delicate yellow or white flower.

Etymology

Named for its leaf shape and appearance, which resemble stones; 'living' + 'stone.'
