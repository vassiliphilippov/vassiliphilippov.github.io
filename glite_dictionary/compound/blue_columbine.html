blue columbine

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈkɑːləmbaɪn / (AmE), / blu ˈkɒləmbaɪn / (BrE)
a columbine plant native to the Rocky Mountains, recognized for its long-spurred blue flowers
Rocky Mountain plant with blue flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with vibrant blue columbine blooming in the spring.
She spotted a rare blue columbine while hiking in the Rockies.
Many wildflower enthusiasts admire the unique shape of the blue columbine blossom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columbine - a plant of the genus Aquilegia with distinctive spur-shaped flowers
