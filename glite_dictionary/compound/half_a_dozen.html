half a dozen

1. NOUN

/ ˌhæf ə ˈdʌzən / (AmE), / ˌhɑːf ə ˈdʌz(ə)n / (BrE)
a group or set of six people or things
six; group of six

Examples

I bought half a dozen eggs on my way home from work.
There were only half a dozen people in the audience.
He received half a dozen replies to his invitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: six - the number 6
