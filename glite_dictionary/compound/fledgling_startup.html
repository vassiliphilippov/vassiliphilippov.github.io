fledgling startup

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɛdʒlɪŋ ˈstɑːrtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈflɛdʒlɪŋ ˈstɑːtʌp / (BrE)
A newly established business, especially one that is in its early stages and often innovative or technology-focused.
newly established business

Examples

The fledgling startup secured its first round of investment from venture capitalists.
Last year, she left her corporate job to join a fledgling startup in the tech sector.
Despite limited resources, the fledgling startup quickly attracted the attention of larger competitors.

Synset Examples

The fledgling startup is gaining popularity fast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: startup - a newly created business
