green banana

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin bəˈnænə / (AmE), / ɡriːn bəˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
A banana that has not yet ripened and is still green in color.
an unripe banana

Examples

His lunchbox usually includes a green banana because he likes them firmer.
Don’t eat that green banana; it will be too starchy and hard.
I bought a bunch of green bananas to ripen at home.

Synset Examples

My grandmother always told me, don't buy green bananas.
I say don't buy green bananas; they won't ripen.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ripe banana - a banana that is fully ripe, usually yellow or spotted
