banker's check

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋkərz tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkəz tʃɛk / (BrE)
a cheque issued by a bank, payable on demand, and considered more secure than a personal cheque
certified bank-issued cheque

Examples

She paid the deposit with a banker's check to ensure the funds were immediately available.
Most real estate transactions require payment by banker's check for added security.
The embassy instructed me to submit a banker's check for the visa processing fee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cashier's check - a check drawn by a bank on its own funds and signed by the cashier
