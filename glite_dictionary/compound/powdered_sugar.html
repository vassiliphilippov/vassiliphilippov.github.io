powdered sugar

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.dɚd ˈʃʌɡ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.dəd ˈʃʌɡ.ə / (BrE)
Sugar crushed or ground into a fine powder, often used in baking and for sprinkling on desserts.
sugar ground into a fine powder

Examples

To finish the cake, she dusted it with a generous amount of powdered sugar.
Many frosting recipes call for powdered sugar because of its smooth texture.
He sprinkled some powdered sugar over the pancakes before serving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confectioners' sugar - another name for powdered sugar, especially in the US
  • Synonym: icing sugar - British term for powdered sugar
