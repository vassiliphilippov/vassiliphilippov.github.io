spinning rod

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪnɪŋ rɑd / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ rɒd / (BrE)
a fishing rod specifically designed for casting spinning lures, typically equipped with a spinning reel
fishing rod for spinning lures

Examples

He caught three trout using his new spinning rod and a small silver lure.
A spinning rod is ideal for beginners because it is easy to cast and control.
She chose a lightweight spinning rod for fishing in the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing rod - a long, flexible rod used for catching fish
  • Degree Lower: baitcasting rod - a fishing rod designed for baitcasting reels
