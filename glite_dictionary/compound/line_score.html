line score

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪn ˌskɔr / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn ˌskɔː / (BrE)
a summary showing the runs or points scored by each team in each period of a sporting event, usually presented in tabular form
summary of scoring in a game
american

Examples

The newspaper published the line score for last night's baseball game.
On the scoreboard, the line score showed the home team leading after seven innings.
After the match, fans checked the line score to see how each inning played out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: box score - a detailed summary of a sports game including statistics for individual players
