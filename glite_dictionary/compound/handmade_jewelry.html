handmade jewelry

1. NOUN

/ ˌhændˈmeɪd ˈdʒuːəlri / (AmE), / ˌhændˈmeɪd ˈdʒuːəlri / (BrE)
Jewelry crafted by hand rather than by machine or mass production.
jewelry made by hand

Examples

She makes handmade jewelry using colorful beads and silver wire.
Many tourists buy handmade jewelry as souvenirs from local markets.
His shop specializes in unique handmade jewelry crafted by skilled artisans.

Synset Examples

She met a peddler who sold beautiful handmade jewelry.
