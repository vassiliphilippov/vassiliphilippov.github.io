fingerprint specialist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪnt ˌspɛʃəˈlɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt ˌspɛʃəˈlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained in identifying and analyzing fingerprints, often for forensic or legal purposes
expert in analyzing fingerprints

Examples

The police consulted a fingerprint specialist to help solve the burglary case.
As a fingerprint specialist, she examines evidence with great attention to detail.
Every major crime investigation team includes at least one fingerprint specialist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forensic scientist - an expert applying scientific principles to analyze evidence for crime investigation
