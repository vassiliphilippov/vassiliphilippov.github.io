keen eye

1. NOUN

/ kin aɪ / (AmE), / kiːn aɪ / (BrE)
an ability to notice or recognize small details, often used for people who are observant or perceptive
sharp ability to notice details

Examples

The detective's keen eye helped him spot the hidden clue at the crime scene.
She has a keen eye for art and can immediately tell which paintings are valuable.
A keen eye for detail is essential for quality control in manufacturing.

Synset Examples

The grafter had a keen eye for healthy plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp eye - an ability to notice details or be observant
  • Synonym: keen ear - an ability to notice subtle sounds or details in sound
