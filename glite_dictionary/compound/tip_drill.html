tip drill

1. NOUN

/ tɪp drɪl / (AmE), / tɪp drɪl / (BrE)
A practice exercise, especially in team sports, where players repeatedly tip or deflect the ball to improve passing, reaction time, or coordination.
sports exercise involving repeated ball tipping

Examples

The coach emphasized the importance of the tip drill during practice.
Our team does the tip drill before every game to warm up.
She improved her coordination by practicing the tip drill regularly.

Synset Examples
