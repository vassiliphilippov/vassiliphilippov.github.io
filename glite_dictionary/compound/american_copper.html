American copper

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkɑːpər / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkʌpə / (BrE)
a small copper-colored butterfly (Lycaena phlaeas) commonly found in central and eastern North America
a North American copper-colored butterfly

Examples

The American copper is often seen fluttering over open fields during the summer months.
Gardeners enjoy watching American copper butterflies visiting their wildflowers.
I photographed an American copper perched on a clover blossom.
