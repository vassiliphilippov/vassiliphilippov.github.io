corn gluten feed

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈɡluːtən fid / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈɡluːtən fiːd / (BrE)
a coarse feed for livestock, consisting mainly of corn gluten, a by-product of corn processing
livestock feed made from corn gluten

Examples

The farmer supplemented the cattle’s diet with corn gluten feed to improve their weight gain.
Many feed mills produce corn gluten feed as a by-product of corn processing.
Bulk shipments of corn gluten feed are commonly used in large dairy operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn gluten meal - a finer meal made from corn gluten, often used as animal feed or herbicide
