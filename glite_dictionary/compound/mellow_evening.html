mellow evening

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛloʊ ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛləʊ ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
an evening characterized by a relaxed, calm, and pleasant atmosphere
relaxed and calm evening

Examples

After a long week at work, she looked forward to a mellow evening at home.
They spent a mellow evening listening to soft music and chatting quietly.
A glass of wine and good company made for a truly mellow evening.

Synset Examples

He enjoys a mellow evening with a good book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxed evening - an evening spent in a relaxed manner
