porcupine ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔːrk.jəˌpaɪn bɔl / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.kjʊˌpaɪn bɔːl / (BrE)
a dish consisting of meat patties rolled in rice and simmered in a tomato sauce
meatballs with rice in tomato sauce

Examples

My grandmother used to make porcupine balls for Sunday dinner every week.
The rice in the porcupine balls sticks out, making them look like little spiky creatures.
You can freeze leftover porcupine balls and reheat them for a quick meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meatball - a small ball of ground meat often mixed with other ingredients
