commuter train

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjuːtɚ treɪn / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːtə treɪn / (BrE)
a passenger train that is mainly used by people traveling regularly between home and work or school, especially in a metropolitan area
regular passenger train for commuters

Examples

I take the commuter train into the city every morning for work.
Many suburbs have expanded since the commuter train makes it easy to reach downtown.
The commuter train was delayed due to track maintenance this evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suburban train - a train serving suburbs, often used interchangeably with 'commuter train'
