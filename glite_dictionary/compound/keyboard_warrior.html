keyboard warrior

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːbɔrd ˈwɔriər / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd ˈwɒriə / (BrE)
a person who posts aggressive, confrontational, or critical comments online, typically without engaging in real-world confrontation
aggressive online critic
informal

Examples

On social media, he acts like a total keyboard warrior, but in person he's much quieter.
Don't pay attention to the keyboard warriors attacking you online.
Many keyboard warriors never back up their words with real actions.

Synset Examples

The keyboard warrior posted aggressive comments online.
He’s just a keyboard warrior, not ready to face reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troll - someone who posts inflammatory messages online to provoke others
