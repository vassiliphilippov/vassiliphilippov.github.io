hornet's nest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrnəts ˌnɛst / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnɪts ˌnɛst / (BrE)
A nest built by hornets or wasps, typically made of a papery material and serving as their habitation.
nest built by hornets or wasps

Examples

The children discovered a hornet's nest hanging from the tree branch in their backyard.
Approaching a hornet's nest without proper protection can be very dangerous.
After the old shed collapsed, we found a massive hornet's nest inside the walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasp's nest - nest built by wasps
