to tape record

1. VERB

/ teɪp rɪˈkɔrd / (AmE), / teɪp rɪˈkɔːd / (BrE)
to record sounds or voices using a tape recorder
record with a tape recorder

Examples

She used an old machine to tape record the interview.
In the 1980s, it was common to tape record your favorite radio shows.
He secretly tape recorded their conversation for reference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - to capture sound or performance onto a storage medium
