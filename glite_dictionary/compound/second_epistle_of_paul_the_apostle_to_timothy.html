Second Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɛkənd ɪˈpɪsəl ʌv pɔl ði əˈpɑsəl tu ˈtɪməθi / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ɪˈpɪsəl əv pɔːl ði əˈpɒsl tuː ˈtɪməθi / (BrE)
the eighteenth book of the New Testament, consisting of Paul's second letter to Timothy, offering pastoral advice and encouragement
New Testament book; Paul's second letter to Timothy

Examples

Many pastors turn to the Second Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy for guidance on leadership.
In Bible study, we read passages from the Second Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy.
Paul's advice to Timothy in the Second Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy remains relevant today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: First Epistle of Paul the Apostle to Timothy - the first letter of Paul to Timothy in the New Testament

Etymology

Epistle from Greek 'epistolē' (letter); Timothy from Greek 'Timotheos'.
