fashion no-no

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˈnoʊ noʊ / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən ˈnəʊ nəʊ / (BrE)
a fashion choice that is considered socially unacceptable or frowned upon
unacceptable fashion choice
informal

Examples

Wearing socks with sandals is considered a fashion no-no by many style experts.
Mixing too many patterns in one outfit can quickly become a fashion no-no.
Some believe that wearing black and navy together is a fashion no-no, but others disagree.

Synset Examples

Wearing white after Labor Day is a fashion no-no.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faux pas - a social blunder or mistake
