foxface fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑksˌfeɪs fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌfeɪs fɪʃ / (BrE)
a tropical marine fish of the rabbitfish family (Siganidae), characterized by its elongated snout and bold black-and-yellow markings, native to the Indo-Pacific region
tropical reef fish with black and yellow markings

Examples

Many aquarists keep a foxface fish in their saltwater tanks for its striking coloration.
The foxface fish uses its sharp spines for protection against predators.
A foxface fish can help control algae growth in a marine aquarium.

Synset Examples

The foxface fish is known for its unique appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbitfish - tropical marine fish of the family Siganidae
