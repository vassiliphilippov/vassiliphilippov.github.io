painted cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˌkʌp / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Castilleja, especially those with dense spikes of bright, hooded flowers and colorful bracts, commonly called Indian paintbrush.
plant of the genus Castilleja with bright bracts

Examples

Wild painted cup flowers added a splash of red to the meadow in early spring.
The painted cup is known for its distinctive, brightly colored bracts that often outshine its actual flowers.
Many hikers enjoy spotting the delicate painted cup along mountain trails during the blooming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indian paintbrush - common name for Castilleja species with showy bracts
