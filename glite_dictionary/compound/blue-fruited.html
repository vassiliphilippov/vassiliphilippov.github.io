blue-fruited

1. ADJ

/ bluː ˈfruːtɪd / (AmE), / bluː ˈfruːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) bearing blue-colored fruit.
bearing blue fruit

Examples

The blue-fruited shrubs in the garden attract many species of birds.
Several blue-fruited varieties thrive in temperate climates.
They planted a blue-fruited bush next to the red-berried hedge.
