poor man's orchid

1. NOUN

/ pʊr mænz ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / pɔː mænz ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Schizanthus, known for finely divided leaves and showy, variegated flowers resembling orchids
plant of the genus Schizanthus

Examples

The poor man's orchid adds vibrant color to gardens thanks to its striking flowers.
Many gardeners choose the poor man's orchid as an affordable alternative to true orchids.
In spring, the poor man's orchid blooms with clusters of delicate, multi-colored petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Schizanthus - a genus of South American flowering plants with orchid-like flowers
