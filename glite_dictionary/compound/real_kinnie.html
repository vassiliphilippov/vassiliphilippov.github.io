real kinnie

1. NOUN

/ ɹiəl ˈkɪni / (AmE), / ɹɪəl ˈkɪni / (BrE)
A person who strongly identifies with or is a passionate fan of a particular character, series, or fandom, often to an intense or enthusiastic degree.
ardent fan or passionate enthusiast
slang

Examples

She’s a real kinnie for that anime character and has posters all over her room.
If you’re a real kinnie, you might cosplay your favorite character at conventions.
His dedication to the franchise proves he’s a real kinnie among his friends.

Synset Examples

She's a real kinnie when it comes to her favorite shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - an enthusiastic admirer or supporter

Etymology

From slang "kinnie," derived from "kinning" (identifying with a fictional character), combined with "real" as an intensifier.
