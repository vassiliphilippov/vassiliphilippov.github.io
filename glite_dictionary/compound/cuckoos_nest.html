cuckoo's nest

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkuːz nɛst / (AmE), / ˈkʊkuːz nɛst / (BrE)
A disparaging term for a psychiatric hospital or mental institution.
pejorative term for a mental hospital

Examples

After his erratic behavior, his friends joked he'd be sent to the cuckoo's nest.
The novel's setting is a notorious cuckoo's nest where the patients struggle for dignity.
Using the phrase cuckoo's nest to describe a hospital is now considered insensitive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses

Etymology

Cuckoo (a bird associated with madness in folklore) + nest; extended into slang for mental institutions.
