staying power

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪ.ɪŋ ˌpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.ɪŋ ˌpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
the ability to continue doing something difficult, endure, or remain effective over a long period of time
ability to endure or persist

Examples

Success in this industry requires considerable staying power and determination.
Although she struggled at first, her staying power impressed everyone by the end of the competition.
The film proved its staying power by remaining in theaters for several months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endurance - the ability to continue despite fatigue or hardship
  • Synonym: perseverance - continued effort to achieve something despite difficulties
  • Synonym: stamina - the physical or mental strength to do something for a long time
