order Lyginopteridales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdɚ laɪˌdʒɪnɒptəˈraɪdiːlz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə laɪˌdʒɪnɒptəˈraɪdiːlz / (BrE)
an extinct order of fossil gymnospermous trees or climbing plants from the Devonian period, known as seed ferns
extinct order of Devonian seed ferns

Examples

Paleobotanists have found fossils of order Lyginopteridales in several Devonian rock layers.
The order Lyginopteridales includes some of the earliest known seed ferns.
Many species of order Lyginopteridales resembled both ferns and seed plants.
