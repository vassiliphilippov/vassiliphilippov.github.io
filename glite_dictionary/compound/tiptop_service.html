tiptop service

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪpˌtɑp ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪptɒp ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
service of excellent or very high quality
excellent quality service

Examples

We received tiptop service at the hotel throughout our stay.
She always demands tiptop service when dining out.
The company is known for providing tiptop service to all its clients.

Synset Examples

The restaurant's service was tiptop this evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-class service - service of the highest or very good quality
  • Antonym: poor service - service of low or unsatisfactory quality

Etymology

From 'tiptop', meaning 'the highest point or degree', originally slang from the 18th century.
