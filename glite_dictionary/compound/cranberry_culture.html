cranberry culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrænˌbɛri ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the cultivation or growing of cranberries as a crop, especially for commercial purposes
cultivation of cranberries

Examples

The region is famous for its successful cranberry culture and annual harvest festivals.
Advances in cranberry culture have improved both yield and berry quality over the last decade.
She wrote her thesis on sustainable methods in cranberry culture practices in North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranberry farming - the practice of growing cranberries as an agricultural product
