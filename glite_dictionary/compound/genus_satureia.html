genus Satureia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs sæˈtʃuː.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs səˈtjuː.ri.ə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic herbs in the mint family, including savory, often used in cooking and herbal medicine.
genus of savory herbs in the mint family

Examples

Members of the genus Satureia are commonly known as savory herbs in cooking.
Researchers studied the essential oils extracted from several genus Satureia species.
Gardeners often cultivate plants in the genus Satureia for their fragrant leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savory - any aromatic herb of the genus Satureia, used in cooking

Etymology

From Latin *Satureia*, the classical name for savory.
