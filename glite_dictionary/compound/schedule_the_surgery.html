to schedule the surgery

1. VERB

/ ˈskɛdʒuːl ðə ˈsɜrdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈʃɛdjuːl ðə ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
to arrange a specific time for a medical operation to take place
arrange time for a medical operation

Examples

The doctor will schedule the surgery as soon as the test results come in.
We need to schedule the surgery for a day when a specialist is available.
After discussing the options, they decided to schedule the surgery for early next month.

Synset Examples

They scheduled the surgery for next Thursday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: book the surgery - to reserve a time for a medical operation
  • Antonym: cancel the surgery - to call off a planned medical operation
