tennis club

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs klʌb / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs klʌb / (BrE)
an organization or place where people meet to play tennis together
organization for playing tennis

Examples

She has been a member of the tennis club for over ten years.
The tennis club hosts tournaments every summer for players of all levels.
He joined a local tennis club to improve his skills and meet new people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports club - an organization for people interested in a particular sport
