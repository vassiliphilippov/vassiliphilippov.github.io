total shitbucket

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l̩ ˈʃɪtbʌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈʃɪtbʌkɪt / (BrE)
(slang) A situation, project, or thing that is a complete failure or disaster.
complete failure or disaster (slang)
slang vulgar

If this trip becomes a total shitbucket, at least we’ll have a funny story to tell.
