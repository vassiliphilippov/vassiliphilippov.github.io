wide spot

1. NOUN

/ waɪd spɑt / (AmE), / waɪd spɒt / (BrE)
a broad area at the side of a road where vehicles can stop or park temporarily
area by a road for stopping or parking

Examples

The driver pulled into a wide spot to check the map and rest.
We had lunch at a wide spot overlooking the lake.
The police advised him to wait at the next wide spot for assistance.

Synset Examples

There's a wide spot in the road where we can park.
The truck pulled over to a wide spot in the road.
