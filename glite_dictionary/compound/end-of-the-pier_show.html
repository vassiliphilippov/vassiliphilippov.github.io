end-of-the-pier show

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛnd əv ðə pɪr ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛnd əv ðiː pɪə ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a variety entertainment show typically performed at a British seaside pier, known for its light, popular, and often comedic content
variety show at a seaside pier
british

We watched an end-of-the-pier show in Brighton during our summer holiday.
The old theatre now hosts a weekly end-of-the-pier show featuring comedy and song.
Traditional end-of-the-pier shows are famous for their slapstick humor and family-friendly acts.
