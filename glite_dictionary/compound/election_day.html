election day

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛkʃən deɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛkʃən deɪ / (BrE)
the day on which an election is held, especially the fixed day for national or local voting in a country
day when an election is held

Examples

Voters lined up at the school gymnasium on election day to cast their ballots.
Many people choose to volunteer at polling stations on election day.
The results of election day often shape the future of a country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polling day - the day when people vote in an election (chiefly British)
