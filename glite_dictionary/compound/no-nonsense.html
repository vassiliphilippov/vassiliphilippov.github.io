no-nonsense

1. ADJ

/ ˌnoʊˈnɑnsəns / (AmE), / ˌnəʊˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
not tolerating anything frivolous, irrelevant, or unnecessary; practical and straightforward in manner or approach
practical, not tolerating trivialities

Examples

Her no-nonsense attitude earned her respect from her colleagues.
The coach has a no-nonsense approach to training that gets results.
We need a no-nonsense solution to this ongoing problem.

Synset Examples

the no-nonsense tones of a stern parent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice
  • Synonym: serious - grave in manner or disposition
  • Antonym: frivolous - not serious or sensible, silly or trivial
