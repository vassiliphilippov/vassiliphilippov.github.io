tawny eagle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔni ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːni ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large brownish eagle found in Africa and parts of Asia, known for its pale to reddish-brown plumage
a brownish eagle of Africa and Asia

Examples

The tawny eagle is often seen soaring above African savannas in search of prey.
We watched a tawny eagle build its nest high in an acacia tree.
During the safari, I spotted several tawny eagles hunting together near a waterhole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eagle - a large bird of prey with a massive hooked bill and long broad wings
