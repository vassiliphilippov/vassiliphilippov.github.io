corporate executive

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːr.pɚ.ɪt ɪɡˈzɛk.jə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pər.ət ɪɡˈzɛk.jʊ.tɪv / (BrE)
A senior manager or leader responsible for the strategic direction of a business corporation.
senior manager in a corporation

Examples

As a corporate executive, Sarah was responsible for overseeing global marketing operations.
The annual conference drew hundreds of corporate executives from around the world.
He worked his way up from sales to become a top corporate executive at the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executive - a person with senior managerial responsibility in a business
