alpine sunflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈælpaɪn ˈsʌnˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈsʌnˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a whitish, hairy plant with featherlike leaves and sturdy stems, each bearing a large, solitary yellow flower, found in mountainous regions of the north-central United States
mountainous yellow-flowered plant of the US

Examples

The alpine sunflower blooms in late spring on the rocky slopes of the Rockies.
Hikers were delighted to spot a rare alpine sunflower along the trail.
Each alpine sunflower had a bright yellow head that stood out against the snow patches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunflower - a tall plant with a large yellow flower head and edible seeds
