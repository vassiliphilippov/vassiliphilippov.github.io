cliff diving

1. NOUN

/ klɪf ˈdaɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / klɪf ˈdaɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of diving into water from a high, steep cliff
diving into water from a cliff

Examples

She traveled to Acapulco to watch the famous cliff diving competitions.
Many thrill-seekers attempt cliff diving during the summer months.
Proper training is essential before engaging in dangerous activities like cliff diving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high diving - diving into water from a springboard or platform at a significant height
