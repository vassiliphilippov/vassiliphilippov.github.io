friends forever

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndz fəˈrɛvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndz fəˈrɛvə / (BrE)
used to express a commitment to lifelong friendship
lifelong friendship commitment

Examples

After graduation, they hugged and promised to be friends forever.
She gave me a bracelet that says friends forever as a symbol of our bond.
No matter where life takes us, we'll always be friends forever.

Synset Examples
