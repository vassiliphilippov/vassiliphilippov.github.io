to join a library

1. VERB

/ dʒɔɪn ə ˈlaɪbrɛri / (AmE), / dʒɔɪn ə ˈlaɪbrəri / (BrE)
to become a member of a library in order to borrow books or use its services
become a member of a library

Examples

After moving to the new city, I decided to join a library to access more books.
You need proof of address if you want to join a library in this town.
She encouraged her children to join a library to develop their love of reading.

Synset Examples

I borrowed this novel; why buy a book when you can join a library?
I prefer borrowing; why buy a book when you can join a library?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to enroll - to officially join an organization or course
