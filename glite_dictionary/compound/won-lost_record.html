won-lost record

1. NOUN

/ wʌn lɔst ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / wɒn lɒst ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
(in sports) a summary or record that shows the number of games won and lost by a team or individual during a season or period.
record of wins and losses in sports
american

Examples

After ten games, the team has an impressive won-lost record of 8-2.
Her won-lost record this season shows significant improvement over last year.
The coach emphasized the importance of maintaining a positive won-lost record throughout the season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - an official list of achievements, such as wins or losses
  • Synonym: win-loss record - another way to refer to the won-lost record
