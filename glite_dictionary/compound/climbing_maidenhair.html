climbing maidenhair

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈmeɪdənˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈmeɪdənheə / (BrE)
a tropical fern with delicate, climbing fronds, native to the Old World and naturalized in places such as Jamaica and Florida
a tropical climbing fern

Examples

The climbing maidenhair added a lush, green touch to the garden wall.
Botanists have documented climbing maidenhair growing wild in southern Florida.
Many gardeners appreciate the delicate appearance of climbing maidenhair in shaded areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maidenhair fern - any fern of the genus Adiantum, known for its delicate fronds
