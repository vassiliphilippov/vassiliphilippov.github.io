total windbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈwɪndˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈwɪndˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a person who talks excessively, especially about trivial matters, and often bores others
person who talks too much, boringly

Examples

He turned out to be a total windbag during the meeting, dominating every discussion.
Nobody likes sitting next to a total windbag on long flights.
She's smart, but some people unfairly label her as a total windbag because of her enthusiasm.

Synset Examples

I find him to be a total windbag at parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about unimportant things
  • Synonym: blowhard - a person who brags or boasts a lot
  • Antonym: taciturn - someone who is reserved or says little

Etymology

From 'windbag' (a talkative or boastful person) with modifier 'total' for emphasis.
