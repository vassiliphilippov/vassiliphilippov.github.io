god-king

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑd kɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡɒd kɪŋ / (BrE)
A ruler who is considered to possess divine status or authority.
divine or deified ruler

Examples

The ancient pharaoh was worshipped as a god-king by his people.
Many myths tell of a god-king descending from the heavens to lead the nation.
The title of god-king was reserved for only the most powerful rulers.

Synset Examples

The emperor was revered as a god-king by his followers.
In ancient times, a god-king ruled with absolute power.
The legend spoke of a god-king who brought peace to the land.

Etymology

Formed from 'god' and 'king', indicating a ruler regarded as a deity.
