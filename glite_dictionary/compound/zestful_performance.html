zestful performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɛstfəl pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈzɛstfʊl pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
a display or presentation characterized by great energy and enthusiasm
energetic and enthusiastic display

Examples

The actor's zestful performance earned him a standing ovation from the crowd.
Critics praised the orchestra's zestful performance for its vitality and excitement.
Her zestful performance brought new life to an old classic.

Synset Examples

He gave a zestful performance that captivated the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirited performance - a lively and energetic display or act
  • Antonym: lackluster performance - a dull or uninspired display or act
