Jamaican dollar

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪkən ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪkən ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official unit of currency in Jamaica, subdivided into 100 cents
currency of Jamaica

Examples

You can exchange U.S. dollars for Jamaican dollars at the airport.
The price tag listed items in both Jamaican dollars and U.S. dollars.
Tourists often wonder about the value of the Jamaican dollar before traveling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - unit of currency used in several countries, including the United States, Canada, and Australia
