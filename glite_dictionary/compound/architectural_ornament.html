architectural ornament

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəl ˈɔːrnəmənt / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃərəl ˈɔːnəmənt / (BrE)
a decorative feature or object added to a building to enhance its appearance
decorative feature on a building

Examples

The museum's entrance was adorned with an intricate architectural ornament modeled after classical Greek motifs.
During restoration, craftsmen carefully repaired every architectural ornament on the historic façade.
Art students studied each architectural ornament to understand the building's elaborate design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decoration - something that decorates or adorns

Etymology

From Latin 'architectura' (architecture) and 'ornamentum' (decoration).
