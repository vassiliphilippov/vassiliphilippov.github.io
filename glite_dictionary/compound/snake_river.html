Snake River

1. PROPN

/ sneɪk ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / sneɪk ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in the northwestern United States, flowing from Wyoming westward to join the Columbia River
major river in northwestern US

Examples

The Snake River winds through several states before joining the Columbia River.
Early explorers mapped the course of the Snake River during their journey west.
The scenic beauty along the Snake River attracts many tourists and nature lovers.
