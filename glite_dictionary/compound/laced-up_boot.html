laced-up boot

1. NOUN

/ ˌleɪst ˈʌp buːt / (AmE), / ˌleɪst ˈʌp buːt / (BrE)
a boot fastened by laces running through eyelets or hooks
boot fastened with laces

Examples

He put on his laced-up boots before heading out into the snow.
Her laced-up boots provided good ankle support during the climb.
Fashion magazines often feature models wearing stylish laced-up boots in the fall.

Synset Examples

He wore his laced-up boots for the hike.
She admired the laced-up design of the new sneakers.
The laced-up dress she wore was stunning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lace-up - a shoe or boot fastened with laces
