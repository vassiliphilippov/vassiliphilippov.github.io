Peter Pan collar

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtɚ pæn ˈkɑːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə pæn ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A flat collar with rounded ends that meet at the front, often used in women's and children's clothing.
a collar with rounded ends meeting in front

Examples

The dress was finished with a delicate Peter Pan collar that gave it a vintage charm.
Many school uniforms feature a white Peter Pan collar as part of their distinctive design.
She loved the blouse's Peter Pan collar because it looked neat without needing a tie.

Etymology

Named after the character Peter Pan, as worn by the actress Maude Adams in the 1905 stage production.
