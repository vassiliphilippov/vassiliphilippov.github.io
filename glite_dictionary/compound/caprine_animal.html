caprine animal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpraɪn ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈkæprɪn ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal belonging to the group that includes goats and their relatives, typically characterized by agility, a beard, and straight horns
goat or goat-like animal

Examples

The mountain is home to several rare caprine animals adapted to rocky terrain.
Biologists observed the behavior of a caprine animal among the herd.
Some caprine animals possess remarkable climbing abilities that help them escape predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goat - a domesticated animal known for its agility and beard
  • Antonym: sheep - a ruminant animal closely related to goats, typically without a beard or straight horns

Etymology

From Latin 'caprinus' (of or pertaining to a goat).
