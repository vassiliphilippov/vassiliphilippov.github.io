Battle of Rocroi

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬əl əv roʊˈkrwɑː / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv rɒˈkrwɑː / (BrE)
a decisive battle fought in 1643 during the Thirty Years' War, in which French forces defeated the Spanish army
1643 battle; French defeated Spanish

Examples

The Battle of Rocroi marked a turning point in European military history.
Many historians regard the Battle of Rocroi as the decline of Spanish dominance.
He studied the tactics employed at the Battle of Rocroi in his military strategy class.
