snow blower

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊ ˌbloʊər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ ˌbləʊə / (BrE)
a machine for removing snow by collecting it and forcefully throwing it aside through a chute
machine for removing snow

Examples

We use a snow blower to clear the driveway after heavy storms.
The old snow blower finally broke down in the middle of winter.
Operating a snow blower can be much faster than shoveling by hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowplow - a vehicle or device for clearing snow; sometimes pushes rather than throws snow
