fine-feathered friend

1. NOUN

/ ˌfaɪn ˈfɛðɚd ˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˌfaɪn ˈfɛðəd ˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a pet bird, especially a parrot, regarded with affection
beloved pet bird, often a parrot

Examples

When his fine-feathered friend sang in the morning, he always felt cheerful.
Her parrot is more than a pet—he's truly her fine-feathered friend.
On Sundays, they take their fine-feathered friend out in the garden for some sunshine.

Synset Examples

My parakeet is a fine-feathered friend who keeps me company.
The parrot became her fine-feathered friend and learned to talk.
She treats her dog like a fine-feathered friend, always spoiling him.
