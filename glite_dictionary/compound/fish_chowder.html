fish chowder

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ ˈtʃaʊdər / (AmE), / fɪʃ ˈtʃaʊdə / (BrE)
A thick soup made with fish, usually including vegetables, potatoes, and milk or cream.
a thick soup made with fish

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its creamy fish chowder served with fresh bread.
She decided to make fish chowder for dinner using cod and clams.
On cold evenings, a hot bowl of fish chowder is especially comforting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chowder - a thick soup, often containing seafood and potatoes
