pond lily

1. NOUN

/ pɑnd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / pɒnd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A type of water lily with large floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers, found in temperate and tropical regions.
aquatic plant with large floating flowers

Examples

A magnificent pond lily bloomed at the edge of the quiet lake.
The garden's centerpiece is a cluster of white pond lilies floating on the water.
Dragonflies hovered above the pond lily, drawn by its brilliant color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lily - any aquatic plant of the family Nymphaeaceae with floating leaves and flowers
