vocal about

1. ADJ

/ ˈvoʊkəl əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəl əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
expressing opinions or feelings openly and strongly, especially on a particular subject
openly expressing opinions

Examples

She has always been vocal about the need for environmental reform.
Many students are increasingly vocal about their concerns over tuition fees.
The coach was vocal about his dissatisfaction with the referee's decisions.

Synset Examples

Novax supporters are vocal about their stance on vaccination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outspoken - expressing opinions directly and honestly, even if they offend people
  • Antonym: reticent - not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily
