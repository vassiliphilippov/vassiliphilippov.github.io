high school sweetheart

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ skuːl ˈswit.hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ skuːl ˈswiːt.hɑːt / (BrE)
a person with whom one had a romantic relationship during high school, often implying a lasting or nostalgic connection
romantic partner from high school years

Examples

After twenty years apart, he finally reunited with his high school sweetheart.
Many people marry their high school sweetheart, but others move on to new relationships.
She kept in touch with her high school sweetheart throughout college.

Synset Examples

She was excited to wed her high school sweetheart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweetheart - a person with whom one is in a romantic relationship
