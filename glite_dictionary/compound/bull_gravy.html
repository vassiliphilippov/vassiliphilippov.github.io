bull gravy

1. NOUN

/ bʊl ˈɡreɪvi / (AmE), / bʊl ˈɡreɪvi / (BrE)
Nonsense or meaningless talk; statements that are insincere or lack truth.
nonsense; meaningless talk
informal

Examples

I don't believe a word of that bull gravy he told us yesterday.
Her story sounded like pure bull gravy from start to finish.
Next time, spare me the bull gravy and just tell the truth.
