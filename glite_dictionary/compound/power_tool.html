power tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər tuːl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə tʊːl / (BrE)
A tool that is powered by an electric motor or engine, rather than operated by hand.
electrically or motor-driven tool

Examples

Always wear safety glasses when using a power tool to protect your eyes from debris.
The carpenter purchased a new power tool to speed up his work in the workshop.
Many power tools require regular maintenance to function safely and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hand tool - a tool operated by hand rather than by motor or electricity
