basketball shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ʃɒt / (BrE)

attempt to score by throwing a basketball

Practicing your basketball shot daily can significantly improve your accuracy.
