Indian coral tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈkɔɹəl triː / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈkɒrəl triː / (BrE)
a small to medium-sized thorny tree native to tropical Asia and northern Australia, known for its dense clusters of scarlet or crimson flowers and black seeds
tropical Asian/Australian tree with scarlet flowers

Examples

The Indian coral tree blooms with brilliant red flowers every spring in the garden.
Gardeners admire the Indian coral tree for its striking blossoms and unique thorny branches.
In northern Australia, the Indian coral tree is often planted as an ornamental shade tree.
