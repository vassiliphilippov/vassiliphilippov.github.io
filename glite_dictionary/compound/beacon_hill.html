Beacon Hill

1. PROPN

/ ˈbiːkən hɪl / (AmE), / ˈbiːkən hɪl / (BrE)
a historic and fashionable district of Boston, Massachusetts, known for its Federal-style rowhouses and the Massachusetts State House
historic Boston district; Massachusetts State House location

Examples

They rented an apartment on Beacon Hill to be closer to downtown Boston.
The Massachusetts State House stands at the top of Beacon Hill.
Beacon Hill is famous for its brick sidewalks and gas-lit streetlamps.
