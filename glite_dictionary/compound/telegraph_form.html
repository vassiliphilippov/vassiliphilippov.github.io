telegraph form

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌɡræf fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪɡrɑːf fɔːm / (BrE)
a printed or written form used to compose and send a telegram
form for sending a telegram

Examples

Please fill out the telegraph form before handing it to the clerk.
The museum displays an old telegraph form used in the early 20th century.
He wrote his message carefully on the telegraph form to avoid mistakes.
