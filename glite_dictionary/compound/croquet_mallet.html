croquet mallet

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈkeɪ ˈmælɪt / (AmE), / krəʊˈkeɪ ˈmælɪt / (BrE)
a long-handled, flat-ended mallet used to strike the ball in the game of croquet
mallet used in croquet

Examples

She gripped the croquet mallet tightly as it was her turn to play.
The set included four colored balls and a croquet mallet.
After the handle broke, he had to buy a new croquet mallet for the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallet - a hammer with a large, usually wooden head
