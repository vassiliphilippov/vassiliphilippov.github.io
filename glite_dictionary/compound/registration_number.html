registration number

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
the unique number assigned to a motor vehicle and displayed on its license plate for identification
number on a vehicle's license plate

Examples

You are required to display your car’s registration number clearly at all times.
Police recorded the registration number of every vehicle entering the parking lot.
She reported her stolen car’s registration number to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: license plate - metal plate on a vehicle showing the registration number
