musical note

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl noʊt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk(ə)l nəʊt / (BrE)
a symbol in musical notation indicating the pitch and duration of a musical sound
symbol for pitch and length in music

Examples

She played each musical note with perfect clarity during the piano recital.
The composer wrote a single musical note on the sheet as a starting point for the melody.
He struggled to read the tiny musical notes in the old manuscript.

Synset Examples

the singer held the note too long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: note - a symbol denoting musical pitch and duration
