offset press

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːfˌsɛt prɛs / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsɛt prɛs / (BrE)
a printing press used for offset printing, where the inked image is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket and then to the printing surface
printing press for offset printing

Examples

The publishing house installed a new offset press to speed up production and improve print quality.
Many newspapers rely on a offset press for their daily printing operations.
Training operators to use an offset press requires a thorough understanding of its mechanical systems.

Synset Examples

Many modern offset presses use computer-to-plate systems as opposed to the older computer-to-film work flows, which further increases their quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printing press - a machine for printing text or pictures from type or plates
  • Synonym: offset printing - a printing technique in which an inked image is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket, then to the printing surface

Etymology

From 'offset', describing the printing method, and 'press'; 'offset' from the way the image is transferred.
