cut of lamb

1. NOUN

/ kʌt əv læm / (AmE), / kʌt əv læm / (BrE)
a portion of meat that has been sectioned from a lamb, typically prepared for cooking or serving
portion of meat from a lamb

Examples

The chef recommended roasting this cut of lamb with rosemary and garlic for the best flavor.
Each cut of lamb offers a distinct texture and taste, from the rack to the shank.
She purchased a tender cut of lamb for their holiday dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamb - a young sheep, especially one that is less than one year old, or its meat
  • Synonym: cut of beef - portion of meat sectioned from a cow
