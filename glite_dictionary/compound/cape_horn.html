Cape Horn

1. PROPN

/ keɪp hɔrn / (AmE), / keɪp hɔːn / (BrE)
A rocky headland belonging to Chile, located at the southernmost tip of South America, south of Tierra del Fuego.
southernmost headland of South America

Examples

Many sailors fear rounding Cape Horn because of its treacherous waters.
The voyage around Cape Horn marked a turning point in the expedition's history.
Fierce storms are common near Cape Horn, making navigation difficult.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Cabo de Hornos', named after the Dutch city of Hoorn.
