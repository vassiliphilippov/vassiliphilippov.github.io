to express feelings

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈsprɛs ˈfiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
to communicate one's emotions or thoughts, especially by speaking, writing, or showing them through actions or expressions
communicate one's emotions

Examples

It can be difficult for some people to express feelings openly in public.
Writing poetry helps her to express feelings she can't say out loud.
Children often express feelings through drawings and play.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: repress feelings - to hold back or suppress one's emotions
  • Synonym: express oneself - to communicate one's thoughts or feelings
