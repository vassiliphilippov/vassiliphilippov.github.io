wall rocket

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a yellow-flowered European plant (Diplotaxis tenuifolia) that grows on old walls and in waste places; considered a weed in North America
a yellow-flowered plant that often grows on walls

Examples

Wall rocket often thrives in cracks between stones of old buildings throughout Europe.
Gardeners sometimes remove wall rocket to control its spread among other plants.
In North America, wall rocket is considered an invasive weed in some regions.
