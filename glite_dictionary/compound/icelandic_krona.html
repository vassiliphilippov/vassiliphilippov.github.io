Icelandic krona

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈslændɪk ˈkroʊnə / (AmE), / aɪˈslændɪk ˈkrəʊnə / (BrE)
the official currency unit of Iceland, abbreviated as ISK.
currency unit of Iceland

Examples

The prices in Reykjavik are listed in Icelandic krona, which can be confusing for visitors.
I exchanged my euros for Icelandic krona at the airport.
The value of the Icelandic krona has fluctuated significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: krona - the monetary unit of Sweden and formerly of other Nordic countries
  • Synonym: Icelandic currency - the money in circulation in Iceland

Etymology

From Icelandic 'króna', meaning 'crown', cognate with Swedish 'krona' and Danish 'krone'.
