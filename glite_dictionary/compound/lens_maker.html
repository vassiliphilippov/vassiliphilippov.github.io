lens maker

1. NOUN

/ lɛnz ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / lɛnz ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is making lenses, especially those used in eyeglasses to correct vision defects
person who makes corrective lenses

Examples

The skilled lens maker carefully crafted a new pair of glasses for the patient.
Advancements in technology have changed how a lens maker designs corrective lenses.
Historically, being a lens maker required both scientific knowledge and fine manual skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optician - a person qualified to make and supply eyeglasses and contact lenses
