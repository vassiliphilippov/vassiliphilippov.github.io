dance master

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæns ˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdɑːns ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a professional who teaches dancing, typically in a formal or classical setting
professional dance teacher

Examples

The dance master instructed the students in traditional ballet techniques.
She hoped to learn the waltz from a renowned dance master at the academy.
In the 18th century, a dance master was an important figure at royal courts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance teacher - a person who teaches dance
