Republic of San Marino

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv sæn məˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv san məˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
an independent country in southern Europe, completely surrounded by Italy and known as the world's oldest republic
independent country surrounded by Italy

Examples

The Republic of San Marino is famous for its medieval architecture and stunning mountain views.
Despite its small size, the Republic of San Marino issues its own postage stamps and coins.
Many tourists visit the Republic of San Marino every year to learn about its long history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: San Marino - the country of San Marino; a microstate in Europe

Etymology

From Italian 'Repubblica di San Marino', named after Saint Marinus, its founder.
