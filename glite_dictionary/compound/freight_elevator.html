freight elevator

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪt ˌɛləˌveɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfreɪt ˌɛlɪˌveɪtə / (BrE)
An elevator designed primarily for transporting goods rather than people, usually larger and stronger than passenger elevators.
elevator for transporting goods

Examples

The boxes were loaded onto the freight elevator to be taken up to the warehouse on the sixth floor.
Only staff are allowed to operate the freight elevator during business hours.
The building's freight elevator is large enough to transport pallets of goods.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: passenger elevator - an elevator designed to carry people
