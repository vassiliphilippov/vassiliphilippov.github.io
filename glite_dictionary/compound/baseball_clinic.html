baseball clinic

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a meeting or session where baseball players receive specialized instruction, training, and evaluation from coaches or experts
baseball instructional meeting or session

Examples

She attended a baseball clinic over the weekend to improve her pitching skills.
The local team organized a baseball clinic for young athletes in the community.
Professional pitchers often coach at the annual baseball clinic held each summer.

Synset Examples

a summer baseball clinic for promising young players

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinic - a meeting for specialized instruction or training
