fuckity day

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌk.ɪ.ti deɪ / (AmE), / ˈfʌk.ɪ.ti deɪ / (BrE)
A day that is frustrating, unpleasant, or generally bad.
frustrating or bad day (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar informal

Examples

After spilling coffee on my shirt and missing the bus, it was shaping up to be a real fuckity day.
Some days are just so overwhelming you can't help but call it a fuckity day.
By noon, everything had gone wrong and I knew it was going to be another fuckity day.

Synset Examples

It's just another fuckity day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad day - a day characterized by misfortune or unhappiness
