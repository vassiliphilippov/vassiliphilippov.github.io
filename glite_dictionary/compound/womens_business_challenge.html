women's business challenge

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪmɪnz ˈbɪznɪs ˈʧælɪnʤ / (AmE), / ˈwɪmɪnz ˈbɪznɪs ˈʧælɪnʤ / (BrE)
an issue or difficulty specifically faced by women in business environments, such as discrimination, lack of representation, or work-life balance challenges
issue faced by women in business

Examples

Her article highlighted the women's business challenges that persist in the tech industry.
Mentoring programs can help address many women's business challenges by providing advice and support.
The conference focused on women's business challenges such as equal pay and promotion opportunities.

Synset Examples

She started a podcast about women's business challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gender discrimination - unfair treatment based on gender, especially in workplaces
  • Synonym: glass ceiling - an invisible barrier preventing women from advancing to top positions
