doodie head

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːdi hɛd / (AmE), / ˈduːdi hɛd / (BrE)
A childish or playful insult for someone who is being foolish or silly.
childish insult for a silly person
informal

Examples

He called his friend a doodie head after losing the game.
Don't act like a doodie head when we're trying to solve a problem.
She laughed and shouted, 'You're such a doodie head!'

Synset Examples

He always calls his brother a doodie head during arguments.
Stop being a doodie head and help me out!
Don't call me a doodie head just because I disagreed!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dummy - a foolish or stupid person (informal insult)
  • Synonym: blockhead - a stupid person (informal insult)
