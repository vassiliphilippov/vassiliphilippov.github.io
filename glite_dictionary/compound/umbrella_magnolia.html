umbrella magnolia

1. NOUN

/ ʌmˈbrɛl.ə mægˈnoʊ.li.ə / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə mæɡˈnəʊ.li.ə / (BrE)
a small deciduous tree native to eastern North America, with large, umbrella-like leaves at the ends of its branches and creamy white flowers
deciduous North American tree with umbrella-like leaves

Examples

The umbrella magnolia is prized for its striking foliage and fragrant blooms.
We planted an umbrella magnolia near the patio to create some summer shade.
In spring, the umbrella magnolia produces clusters of large, creamy white flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnolia - any tree or shrub of the genus Magnolia, known for its large flowers
