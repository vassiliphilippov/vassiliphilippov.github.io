needle drop

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdəl drɑp / (AmE), / ˈniːdl drɒp / (BrE)
the moment when a song begins playing, especially in a film, show, or event, often creating a notable effect or atmosphere
moment when a song starts playing

Examples

The sudden needle drop of the classic track brought the crowd to its feet.
Every time there's a perfect needle drop in a film, it makes the scene unforgettable.
Critics praised the show's use of a well-timed needle drop to introduce the final act.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cue - a signal for something to begin, especially a piece of music
