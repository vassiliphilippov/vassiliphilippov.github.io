court of domestic relations

1. NOUN

/ kɔrt əv dəˈmɛstɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / kɔːt əv dəˈmɛstɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
A court in some U.S. states that deals with family law cases, especially those involving children, such as custody, divorce, and child support.
U.S. court handling family law cases
legal american

Examples

The court of domestic relations will review the custody agreement next Tuesday.
She filed for divorce in the court of domestic relations in her county.
Matters involving child support are often settled in a court of domestic relations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family court - a court that deals with cases involving family law, such as divorce and child custody
