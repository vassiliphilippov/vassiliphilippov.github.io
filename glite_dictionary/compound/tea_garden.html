tea garden

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈɡɑrdən / (AmE), / ti ˈɡɑːdən / (BrE)
a public garden or outdoor area where tea and light refreshments are served to visitors
public garden where tea is served

Examples

We enjoyed afternoon tea in the beautiful tea garden by the river.
The hotel has a lovely tea garden that opens in the summer months.
Many tourists visit the old tea garden for its traditional English scones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant selling light meals and drinks
