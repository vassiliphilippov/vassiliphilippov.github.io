hori shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔːri ʃɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈhɒri ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese garment, usually a loose-fitting shirt worn for festivals or casual occasions
traditional Japanese loose-fitting shirt

Examples

He wore a hori shirt to the summer festival.
Many tourists buy a hori shirt as a souvenir when visiting Japan.
Her blue hori shirt featured a floral design popular in traditional Japanese patterns.

Synset Examples

He wore a hori shirt to the summer festival.

Etymology

From Japanese, with 'hori' referring to a traditional style; borrowed as a cultural term in English.
