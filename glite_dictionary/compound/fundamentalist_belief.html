fundamentalist belief

1. NOUN

/ ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst bəˈliːf / (AmE), / ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
a belief marked by strict and literal adherence to specific religious doctrines, typically resisting modern interpretation or flexibility
strict religious doctrine adherence

Examples

Some communities are shaped by their fundamentalist beliefs and emphasize traditional values.
Her fundamentalist belief led her to reject new scientific theories.
The debate was influenced by participants who held strong fundamentalist beliefs.

Synset Examples

Their fundamentalist beliefs limit open discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fundamentalism - strict maintenance of ancient or fundamental doctrines, usually in religion
