nom de clavier

1. NOUN

/ nɔm də klæˈvjeɪ / (AmE), / nɒm də klæˈvjeɪ / (BrE)
a pseudonym used on the internet or by a gamer, especially as a username or online identity
online pseudonym or username

Examples

Many gamers create a unique nom de clavier to keep their real identity private.
Her fans only know her by her nom de clavier on social media.
He experimented with several noms de clavier before settling on his favourite.

Synset Examples

He chose a fun nom de clavier for his gaming account.
Her nom de clavier is 'TechQueen' in online forums.
Under her nom de clavier, she shares tech tips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudonym - a false or invented name used by someone, especially an author
  • Synonym: username - a name used to identify oneself on a computer or online service

Etymology

From French, literally 'name of the keyboard', modelled after 'nom de plume' ('pen name').
