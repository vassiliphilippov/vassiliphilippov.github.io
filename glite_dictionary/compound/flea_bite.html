flea bite

1. NOUN

/ ˈfliː ˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfliː ˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A very minor inconvenience or annoyance, especially compared to something much more serious.
minor inconvenience or annoyance

Examples

Compared to the challenges she had faced last year, this setback was just a flea bite.
The money lost in the deal was a flea bite to the wealthy investor.
Missing one meeting is a flea bite compared to the problems we've had before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trifle - something of little value or importance
  • Synonym: nuisance - something that causes slight annoyance

2. NOUN

/ fliː baɪt / (AmE), / fliː baɪt / (BrE)
A small swelling or irritation of the skin caused by the bite of a flea.
a bite on the skin from a flea

Examples

She noticed a flea bite on her ankle after walking the dog.
The child kept scratching the flea bite on his arm.
It's important to clean a flea bite to prevent infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mosquito bite - a swelling or irritation caused by the bite of a mosquito
  • Degree Higher: insect bite - skin irritation from any insect bite
