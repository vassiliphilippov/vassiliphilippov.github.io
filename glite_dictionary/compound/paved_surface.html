paved surface

1. NOUN

/ peɪvd ˈsɝːfɪs / (AmE), / peɪvd ˈsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
a flat area or pathway that is covered with paving material such as concrete, asphalt, or stones
flat area covered with paving

Examples

The parking lot was redesigned with a new paved surface to improve drainage.
Bicyclists prefer a paved surface for a smoother and safer ride.
Heavy trucks can damage a poorly constructed paved surface over time.
