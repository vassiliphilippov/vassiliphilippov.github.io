angst bunny

1. NOUN

/ æŋst ˈbʌni / (AmE), / æŋst ˈbʌni / (BrE)
a person who is excessively anxious or worried, often about small or imagined problems
person who worries excessively
informal slang

Examples

My roommate is a total angst bunny when it comes to meeting deadlines.
Don't turn into an angst bunny over something you can't control.
She admits she's a bit of an angst bunny, especially before big events.

Synset Examples

She tends to act like an angst bunny during exams.
Stop being such an angst bunny and enjoy the moment.
He calls himself an angst bunny for worrying too much.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worrier - a person who worries a lot
  • Antonym: calm - free from anxiety or worry

Etymology

Formed from the German word 'angst' (anxiety) and 'bunny' (a term of endearment or someone timid); used to describe anxious individuals humorously.
