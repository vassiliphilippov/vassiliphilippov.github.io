quick lip

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk lɪp / (AmE), / kwɪk lɪp / (BrE)
a brief, disrespectful response or retort spoken sharply or with attitude
brief, disrespectful retort

Examples

He was sent to the principal's office for giving the teacher a quick lip.
Whenever she gets frustrated, her quick lip tends to get her into trouble.
His quick lip made it hard for him to get along with his boss.

Synset Examples

She gave him a quick lip when he asked about her plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backtalk - disrespectful or cheeky replies to authority
