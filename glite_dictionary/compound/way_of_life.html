way of life

1. NOUN

/ weɪ əv laɪf / (AmE), / weɪ əv laɪf / (BrE)
a manner or style of living that reflects a person's or group's habits, values, and norms
manner or style of living

Examples

Living in a rural village has given her a very different way of life from someone in the city.
Respect for elders is an important part of their community's way of life.
Recycling and sustainability have become a way of life for many people in recent years.
