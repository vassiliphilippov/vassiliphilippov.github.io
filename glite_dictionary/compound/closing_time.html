closing time

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊzɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
the time of day at which a business or public establishment stops serving customers and shuts its doors to the public
time a business closes to the public

Examples

Please be aware that closing time is at 10 PM sharp.
Just before closing time, the staff began cleaning up the cafe.
He rushed to the library, hoping to arrive before closing time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opening time - the time when a business or establishment opens to the public

2. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊzɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
The time at which a business, especially a store, bar, or restaurant, stops operating for the day.
time when business closes for day

Examples

The store manager announced that it was closing time and asked everyone to leave.
Many people rush to finish their shopping right before closing time.
We planned to meet just before closing time so we could have one last drink.

Synset Examples

At closing time, the streets filled with people leaving.
The shopkeeper announced it was closing time for the day.
We rushed to the bar before closing time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opening time - the time at which a business begins operating each day
