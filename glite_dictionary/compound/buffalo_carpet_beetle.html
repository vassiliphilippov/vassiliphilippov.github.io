buffalo carpet beetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌfəloʊ ˈkɑrpɪt ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ ˈkɑːpɪt ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small beetle with black, red, and white markings, known for feeding on carpets and other fabrics
a small carpet-damaging beetle

Examples

The buffalo carpet beetle can cause serious damage to wool rugs and clothing.
We found a buffalo carpet beetle infestation in the attic last spring.
Pest control recommended treating the house to get rid of buffalo carpet beetles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet beetle - a beetle whose larvae feed on natural fibers in carpets and fabrics
