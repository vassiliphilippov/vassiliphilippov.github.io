by the sea

1. ADP

/ baɪ ðə siː / (AmE), / baɪ ðə siː / (BrE)
situated close to or beside the sea
situated near the sea

Examples

We rented a small cottage by the sea for our summer vacation.
She enjoys taking long walks by the sea whenever she visits Brighton.
Their restaurant is located by the sea, offering beautiful ocean views.

Synset Examples

We spent the day relaxing by the sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seaside - the area near the sea, especially where people go for recreation
