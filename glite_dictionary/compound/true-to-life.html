true-to-life

1. ADJ

/ truː tə laɪf / (AmE), / truː tə laɪf / (BrE)
closely representing real life in a way that is accurate and realistic; lifelike.
realistic; accurately representing real life

Examples

The artist's true-to-life portraits captured every nuance of his subjects.
The film's depiction of family dynamics felt incredibly true-to-life.
These true-to-life stories help readers connect with the characters.

Synset Examples

the novel about ghetto life was true to life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lifelike - very similar to the real thing or resembling life closely
