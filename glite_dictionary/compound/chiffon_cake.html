chiffon cake

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪˈfɑn keɪk / (AmE), / ʃɪˈfɒn keɪk / (BrE)
A very light cake made with vegetable oil, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder, and flavorings, characterized by its airy texture.
very light, airy cake

Examples

She served a slice of chiffon cake with fresh strawberries and whipped cream.
For my birthday, I requested a homemade lemon chiffon cake.
The light texture of chiffon cake makes it perfect for summer desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponge cake - a light, soft cake made with eggs, sugar, and flour
  • Antonym: pound cake - a dense cake made with equal parts butter, sugar, eggs, and flour

Etymology

From French 'chiffon' meaning 'rag' or 'cloth', referencing the cake's light, airy texture.
