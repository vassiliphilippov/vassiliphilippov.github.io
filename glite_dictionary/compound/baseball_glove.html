baseball glove

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ɡlʌv / (BrE)
a large leather glove worn by baseball fielders to catch and field the ball
leather glove used in baseball fielding

Examples

He bought a new baseball glove before the start of the season.
She forgot her baseball glove at home and had to borrow one for practice.
A high-quality baseball glove can last for many years with proper care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mitt - a type of glove used by catchers and first basemen in baseball
