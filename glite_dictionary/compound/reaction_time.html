reaction time

1. NOUN

/ riˈækʃən taɪm / (AmE), / riˈækʃən taɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time that passes between a stimulus and a person's or thing's response to it
interval between stimulus and response

Examples

The goalkeeper's reaction time was impressive as he dived to save the penalty.
Older adults may experience a slower reaction time while driving at night.
Video games can help improve your reaction time and hand-eye coordination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: response time - the time taken to respond to a request or event
