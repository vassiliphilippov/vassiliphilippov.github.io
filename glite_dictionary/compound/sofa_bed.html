sofa bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊfə ˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈsəʊfə ˌbɛd / (BrE)
A large, padded seat for two or more people that can be converted into a bed.
convertible sofa and bed

Examples

We decided to buy a sofa bed for the guest room to save space.
The sofa bed was surprisingly comfortable to sleep on for a few nights.
He pulled out the sofa bed when his friends stayed over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa - A long, upholstered seat with a back and arms, for several people.
  • Synonym: couch - A long, comfortable seat for reclining or seating several people.
