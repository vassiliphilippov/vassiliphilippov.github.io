school of music

1. NOUN

/ skuːl əv ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / skuːl əv ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
an educational institution specializing in the study and teaching of music
institution for music study

Examples

She hopes to attend a prestigious school of music after high school.
The city opened a new school of music to support young musicians.
Graduates of the school of music often find jobs in orchestras and bands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservatory - academy for advanced study of music or the arts
