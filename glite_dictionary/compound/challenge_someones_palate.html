to challenge someone's palate

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃælɪndʒ ˈsʌmwʌnz ˈpælət / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒ ˈsʌmwənz ˈpælət / (BrE)
to introduce food or drink that tests or stimulates a person's sense of taste, especially in complexity or unfamiliarity
to test someone's sense of taste

Examples

This spicy curry will definitely challenge your palate if you're not used to hot foods.
The chef aims to challenge diners' palates with creative combinations of ingredients.
He prefers wines that challenge his palate rather than those that are simply easy to drink.

Synset Examples

He enjoys wines that challenge his palate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to stimulate - encourage or arouse increased activity or interest
