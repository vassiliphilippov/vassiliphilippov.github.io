Free State

1. PROPN

/ friː steɪt / (AmE), / friː steɪt / (BrE)
a province in central South Africa, formerly known as the Orange Free State, named Free State since 1997
province in central South Africa

Examples

She grew up in the Free State and often visits her family there.
The Free State is known for its agriculture and open plains.
The government of the Free State announced new economic initiatives last week.
