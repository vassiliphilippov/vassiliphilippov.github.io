snapping shrimp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnæpɪŋ ʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈsnæpɪŋ ʃrɪmp / (BrE)
A small marine shrimp known for producing a snapping sound with one of its enlarged claws.
marine shrimp that snaps its claw

Examples

The snapping shrimp creates loud noises underwater to communicate and hunt prey.
Scientists recorded the distinct sounds produced by snapping shrimp colonies on coral reefs.
A single snapping shrimp can generate a noise loud enough to stun small fish.
