red gram

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ɡræm / (AmE), / red ɡram / (BrE)
a tropical woody legume (Cajanus cajan), widely cultivated for its edible seeds, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions
a tropical legume, also known as pigeon pea

Examples

Farmers in India often grow red gram for both food and soil enrichment.
The dish was made with red gram, tomatoes, and a blend of spices.
After harvesting, the red gram pods are dried before removing the seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigeon pea - another name for red gram, the edible seeds of Cajanus cajan
