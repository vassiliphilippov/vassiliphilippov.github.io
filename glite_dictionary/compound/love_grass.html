love grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several grasses of the genus Eragrostis, often grown for forage or to prevent soil erosion
grass of the genus Eragrostis

Examples

Farmers planted love grass to restore the eroded hillside quickly.
Love grass provides excellent forage for livestock in dry climates.
Conservationists recommend love grass for stabilizing sandy soils.
