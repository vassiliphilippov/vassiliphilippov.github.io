service abroad

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪs əˈbrɑd / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs əˈbrɔːd / (BrE)
naval service performed on a ship stationed at sea or outside the home country.
naval service at sea or overseas

Examples

During his service abroad, he visited several foreign ports as part of his duties.
She received recognition for her service abroad with the navy.
Many sailors found service abroad to be both challenging and rewarding.
