in other news

1. X

/ ɪn ˈʌðɚ nuːz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈʌðə njuːz / (BrE)
used to introduce a new subject or topic, especially after discussing something else; typically used in news reporting or conversation.
phrase introducing a new topic

Examples

In other news, the city council approved the new budget last night.
In other news, scientists have discovered a new species of butterfly.
In other news, the weather is expected to improve over the weekend.

Synset Examples

In other news, the mayor announced new policies today.
In other news, the local team won the championship.
In other news, the concert has been postponed.
