slimeball stunt

1. NOUN

/ ˈslaɪmˌbɔl stʌnt / (AmE), / ˈslaɪmbɔːl stʌnt / (BrE)
an act that is particularly deceitful, dishonest, or underhanded, typically in a way that is considered slimy or unethical
deceitful or underhanded act
informal slang

Examples

Everyone was shocked when he tried another slimeball stunt to get ahead at work.
Pulling a slimeball stunt like that won’t win you any friends here.
Her boss’s slimeball stunt was the last straw, so she decided to quit.

Synset Examples

I can't believe he pulled that slimeball stunt again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty trick - a dishonest or sneaky act to gain advantage
  • Synonym: slimeball - a contemptible or repulsive person

Etymology

Formed from 'slimeball' (slang for a despicable or repulsive person) and 'stunt' (an act or trick, often implying dishonesty in this context).
