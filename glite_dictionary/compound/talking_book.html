talking book

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔkɪŋ ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˌbʊk / (BrE)
a recording of a book being read aloud, typically intended for people who are visually impaired or prefer listening to books
audio recording of a book being read aloud

Examples

The library offers a wide selection of talking books for visually impaired readers.
She enjoyed listening to a talking book during her long commute to work.
Many classic novels are available as talking books these days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audiobook - a recording of a text being read aloud
