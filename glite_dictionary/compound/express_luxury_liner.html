express luxury liner

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsprɛs ˈlʌkʃəri ˈlaɪnər / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs ˈlʌkʃəri ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
A large passenger ship designed for fast travel and equipped with luxurious accommodations and amenities.
fast, luxurious passenger ship

Examples

The express luxury liner promised its passengers an unparalleled experience of comfort and speed.
Passengers admired the grand staircase and elegant ballrooms onboard the express luxury liner.
Competition between shipping companies led to the launch of a new express luxury liner every few years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocean liner - a large passenger ship sailing on a regular schedule across the seas
