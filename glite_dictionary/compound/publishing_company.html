publishing company

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a business that publishes books, magazines, newspapers, or other works
company engaged in publishing

Examples

She sent her manuscript to several publishing companies but received no response.
The publishing company decided to launch a new series of children's books next year.
After years of working as an editor, he started his own publishing company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publisher - a person or company that publishes books, magazines, etc.
