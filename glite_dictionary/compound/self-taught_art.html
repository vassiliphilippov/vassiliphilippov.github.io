self-taught art

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ˈtɔt ɑrt / (AmE), / ˌself ˈtɔːt ɑːt / (BrE)
A genre of visual art created by individuals with no formal training, often by people who do not identify as professional artists.
art made by untrained, non-professional artists

Examples

She specializes in self-taught art, collecting unique pieces from local communities.
The exhibition showcases self-taught art created by people with no formal education in the arts.
Many museums now dedicate space to self-taught art alongside more traditional works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outsider art - art by creators outside the mainstream art world, often self-taught
  • Synonym: folk art - traditional art produced by people who are often self-taught
