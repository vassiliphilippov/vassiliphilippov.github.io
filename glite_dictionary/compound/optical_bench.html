optical bench

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑptɪkəl bɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl bɛntʃ / (BrE)
An instrument consisting of a rigid support on which optical components can be mounted, used for observing and measuring optical phenomena.
instrument for optical experiments

Examples

The optical bench allowed the students to align the mirrors precisely for their experiment.
Scientists used an optical bench to measure the focal length of the lens.
Each laboratory was equipped with an optical bench for advanced physics demonstrations.
