craft fair

1. NOUN

/ kræft fɛr / (AmE), / krɑːft feə / (BrE)
An event where handmade items made by artisans are displayed and sold to the public.
event selling handmade crafts

Examples

Every summer, the local community hosts a craft fair featuring jewelry and pottery.
She bought a handwoven scarf at the craft fair downtown last weekend.
Stalls at the craft fair were filled with unique wooden toys and homemade candles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market - an open space or event where goods are sold to the public
