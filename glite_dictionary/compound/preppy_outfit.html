preppy outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛpi ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɛpi ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
A style of clothing that is characteristic of American preparatory schools, typically featuring clean-cut, collegiate garments such as polo shirts, blazers, and khakis.
collegiate-style clothing typical of prep schools

Examples

She chose a preppy outfit for the interview, hoping to make a good impression.
His closet is full of preppy outfits like sweaters, chinos, and loafers.
At the party, everyone admired her preppy outfit with its classic navy blazer and striped tie.

Synset Examples

He wore a preppy outfit to the country club.
Her preppy style includes polo shirts and khakis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preppy - relating to a style or person associated with prep schools

Etymology

From 'preppy', derived from 'prep school' (short for preparatory school), plus 'outfit'.
