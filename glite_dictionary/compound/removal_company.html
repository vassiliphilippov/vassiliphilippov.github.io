removal company

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈmuː.vəl ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / rɪˈmjuː.vəl ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a company that transports the possessions of people or businesses from one location to another, typically during a move
company moving possessions to new location

Examples

We hired a removal company to help with our move across town.
The removal company packed all our furniture and loaded it into their van.
Many removal companies offer storage options for your belongings during the transition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moving company - a company specializing in helping people and businesses move their goods to a new place (chiefly US)
