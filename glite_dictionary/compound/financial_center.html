financial center

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
The area of a city where banks and other financial institutions are concentrated.
area where financial institutions are concentrated

Examples

The city's financial center is bustling with activity during trading hours.
London is known as a leading global financial center.
New skyscrapers are being constructed in the financial center to accommodate growing businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business district - the part of a city where commercial activity is concentrated
