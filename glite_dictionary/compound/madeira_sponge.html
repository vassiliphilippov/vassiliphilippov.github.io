Madeira sponge

1. NOUN

/ məˈdɛrə spʌndʒ / (AmE), / məˈdɪərə spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a rich, close-textured sponge cake, traditionally served with a glass of Madeira wine
a rich sponge cake served with Madeira wine

Examples

For afternoon tea, she baked a classic Madeira sponge to serve with coffee.
The Madeira sponge had a moist, buttery crumb that melted in my mouth.
He enjoyed a slice of Madeira sponge alongside a glass of dessert wine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponge cake - a light cake made with eggs, flour, and sugar

Etymology

Named after Madeira wine, with which it is traditionally served.
