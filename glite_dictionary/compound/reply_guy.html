reply guy

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈplaɪ ɡaɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈplaɪ ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who habitually responds to others on social media or online platforms, often in a predictable or attention-seeking manner
frequent, predictable online responder
informal slang

Examples

On Twitter, he's known as a reply guy because he comments on nearly every post.
She muted the reply guy who kept responding with unsolicited advice.
No one likes a reply guy who never brings anything new to the conversation.

Synset Examples

Being a reply guy often annoys others in online discussions.
Don't be a reply guy; add something original to the conversation.
He always plays the reply guy, commenting on every post.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mansplainer - a man who explains something, typically to a woman, in a condescending or patronizing way
