Greek Church

1. PROPN

/ ɡrik tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ɡriːk tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
The national church of Greece, an autonomous branch of the Eastern Orthodox Church.
state church of Greece (Eastern Orthodox)

Examples

The Greek Church has a rich tradition of Byzantine liturgical music.
Many holidays in Greece are based on celebrations of the Greek Church.
The Greek Church played a significant role during the country's struggle for independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eastern Orthodox Church - the group of Christian churches including the Greek and Russian Orthodox Churches
