bell seat

1. NOUN

/ bɛl siːt / (AmE), / bɛl siːt / (BrE)
A seat on certain 18th-century chairs that is shaped like a bell.
seat shaped like a bell (18th-century chairs)

Examples

The antique chair featured a beautifully carved bell seat that added elegance to the design.
Furniture historians often refer to the bell seat as characteristic of certain 18th-century craftsmanship.
Restorers carefully repaired the damaged bell seat to preserve the chair’s original style.
