flowering maple

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ˈmeɪ.pəl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub or small tree of the genus Abutilon, grown for its maple-shaped leaves and showy flowers
ornamental plant with maple-like leaves

Examples

The flowering maple brightened up her garden with its colorful blossoms.
He bought a flowering maple to add some texture beside the walkway.
Many gardeners choose flowering maple for its attractive foliage and long blooming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abutilon - a genus of plants including the flowering maple
