blood circulation

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˌsɜrkjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / blʌd ˌsɜːkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the continuous movement of blood throughout the body, driven by the heart, delivering oxygen and nutrients and removing waste products
flow of blood through the body

Examples

Exercise helps improve blood circulation and keeps your heart healthy.
Poor blood circulation can lead to health problems such as cold hands and feet.
Massage therapy is often used to stimulate blood circulation throughout the body.

Synset Examples

The doctor prescribed trental to improve blood circulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circulation - movement of a fluid within a closed system
