poppy family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑpi ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpɒpi ˌfæməli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, including herbs and shrubs, characterized by milky and often colored sap and capsular fruits; scientifically known as Papaveraceae
family of flowering plants; Papaveraceae

Examples

Many species in the poppy family are valued for their ornamental flowers.
The poppy family includes both annual and perennial plants.
Opium is derived from the milky sap of a plant in the poppy family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Papaveraceae - the scientific name for the poppy family of plants
  • Synonym: poppy - a flowering plant of the genus Papaver, a member of the poppy family
