redevelopment authority

1. NOUN

/ ˌriː.dɪˈvɛl.əp.mənt ɔːˈθɔːr.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈvɛl.əp.mənt ɔːˈθɒr.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a public agency responsible for planning and overseeing the renovation or renewal of deteriorated urban areas
public agency renovating urban areas

Examples

The city council partnered with the redevelopment authority to revitalize the downtown district.
A local redevelopment authority was established to manage the restoration of abandoned factories.
Funding decisions for the housing project were made by the redevelopment authority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urban renewal - the process of redeveloping and revitalizing deteriorated city areas
