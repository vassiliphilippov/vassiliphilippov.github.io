pepper steak

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər steɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə steɪk / (BrE)
a steak covered with crushed peppercorns, pan-broiled, and served with a brandy-and-butter sauce
steak with crushed pepper sauce

Examples

I ordered the pepper steak with a side of mashed potatoes.
The restaurant is famous for its delicious pepper steak.
He learned to cook a classic pepper steak for special occasions.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpɚ steɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə steɪk / (BrE)
a dish consisting of strips of steak sautéed with green peppers and onions, often served with a savory sauce
dish of steak with peppers and onions

Examples

He ordered pepper steak with a side of rice for dinner last night.
The pepper steak at that restaurant is cooked perfectly every time.
I like to make pepper steak when I need a quick and tasty meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beef stir-fry - a dish of beef sautéed quickly with vegetables, often Asian-style
