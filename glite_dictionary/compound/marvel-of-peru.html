marvel-of-Peru

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑrvəl əv pəˈru / (AmE), / ˌmɑːvəl əv pəˈruː / (BrE)
A common ornamental plant (Mirabilis jalapa) with fragrant flowers, often red, purple, yellow, or white, that typically open in late afternoon.
ornamental plant with fragrant, late-blooming flowers

Examples

The marvel-of-Peru adds bursts of color to gardens with its multi-colored blossoms.
Each evening, the marvel-of-Peru unfurls its fragrant flowers around sundown.
I planted a marvel-of-Peru by the fence, and now its yellow flowers attract butterflies every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: four-o'clock - Another name for Mirabilis jalapa, a plant with late-opening flowers

Etymology

Named for its reputed place of origin, Peru.
