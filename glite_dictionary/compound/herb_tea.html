herb tea

1. NOUN

/ hɝb tiː / (AmE), / hɜːb tiː / (BrE)
A hot drink made by infusing leaves, flowers, or other parts of herbs in boiling water, usually without containing traditional tea leaves.
drink made by infusing herbs in water

Examples

She offered me a cup of herb tea to help me relax before bed.
Many people drink herb tea for its calming and healthful properties.
The café serves a variety of herb teas, including chamomile and peppermint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbal tea - infusion made from herbs, also called herb tea
