trinogamous relationship

1. NOUN

/ traɪˈnɑːɡəməs rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / traɪˈnɒɡəməs rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a romantic relationship involving exactly three partners, where all individuals are connected in a single partnership
three-person romantic partnership

Examples

Their trinogamous relationship works because all three communicate openly and honestly.
She explained that a trinogamous relationship requires just as much commitment as any other.
Friends were curious about the dynamics of their trinogamous relationship.

Synset Examples

They embraced their trinogamous lifestyle with open hearts.
Their trinogamous relationship has surprised everyone.

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'tri-' (three) and '-nogamous' (patterned on 'monogamous'), meaning 'involving three in a committed relationship.'
