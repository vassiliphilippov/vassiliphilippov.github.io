flowering cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a shrub or tree of the genus Prunus grown for its showy white or pink blossoms, often ornamental in gardens and parks
ornamental tree with showy blossoms

Examples

The flowering cherry in her garden bursts into pink every spring.
Many parks are famous for their avenues of flowering cherry trees.
A flowering cherry provides stunning color and attracts visitors during its brief bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry blossom - the flower of any cherry tree, especially in Japan
