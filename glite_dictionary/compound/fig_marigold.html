fig marigold

1. NOUN

/ fɪɡ ˈmɛriˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / fɪɡ ˈmærɪˌɡəʊld / (BrE)
any of several South African plants of the genus Mesembryanthemum, cultivated for their showy pink or white flowers
South African flowering plant (Mesembryanthemum)

Examples

The vibrant fig marigold brightened up the rock garden with its colorful blossoms.
She planted several fig marigolds along the garden path for a splash of color.
The florist recommended fig marigold as a drought-tolerant choice for sunny borders.
