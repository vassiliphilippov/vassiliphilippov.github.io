pediculosis capitis

1. NOUN

/ pɪˌdɪkjəˈloʊsɪs kəˈpaɪtɪs / (AmE), / pɪˌdɪkjʊˈləʊsɪs kəˈpætɪs / (BrE)
Infestation of the scalp with head lice.
scalp infestation with head lice
medical

Examples

The school nurse confirmed that the child had pediculosis capitis after a careful examination.
Proper hygiene and regular hair checks can help prevent pediculosis capitis among children.
Several students were treated for pediculosis capitis during the recent outbreak at the primary school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head lice - infestation of the scalp with lice, especially in children

Etymology

From Latin *pediculus* (louse) and *capitis* (of the head).
