half volley

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈvɑli / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈvɒli / (BrE)
In tennis, a stroke played by hitting the ball immediately after it bounces off the ground.
tennis stroke hit just after the bounce

Examples

He executed a perfect half volley to win the point.
It takes great skill to control the ball on a half volley.
She was caught off guard and had to play a difficult half volley.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: volley - a shot in tennis or similar sports where the ball is struck before it touches the ground
  • Degree Lower: groundstroke - a shot in tennis after the ball has bounced
