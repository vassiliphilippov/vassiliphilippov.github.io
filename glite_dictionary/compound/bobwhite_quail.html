bobwhite quail

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑbˌwaɪt kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌwaɪt kweɪl / (BrE)
A North American game bird with a distinctive call, named for the whistling sound 'bob-white'.
North American game bird

Examples

We spotted a bobwhite quail hiding in the tall grass during our morning walk.
The distinctive call of the bobwhite quail can be heard throughout the countryside in early summer.
Conservation efforts are helping to protect the habitat of the bobwhite quail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quail - a small, short-tailed game bird

Etymology

Named for the bird’s call, which sounds like 'bob-white'.
