calm air

1. NOUN

/ kɑm ɛr / (AmE), / kɑːm eə / (BrE)
A condition in which the wind is moving at less than 1 knot, corresponding to 0 on the Beaufort scale.
wind moving at less than 1 knot

Examples

The weather report predicted calm air throughout the evening, ideal for outdoor dining.
Hot balloons operate best in calm air, ensuring a smooth and safe flight.
Fishermen took advantage of the calm air at dawn to launch their boats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: still - absence of wind or movement in the air
