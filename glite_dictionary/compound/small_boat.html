small boat

1. NOUN

/ smɔl boʊt / (AmE), / smɔːl bəʊt / (BrE)
a boat that is small in size, often used for short trips or inshore activities
a boat of small size

Examples

The fisherman set out early in his small boat to catch the morning tide.
Only small boats are allowed on the lake to protect the environment.
They rowed their small boat across the calm harbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinghy - a small open boat, often carried on a ship
