well-being

1. NOUN

/ wɛlˈbiɪŋ / (AmE), / wɛlˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy
state of health and happiness

Examples

Regular exercise can greatly improve your physical and mental well-being.
The company cares deeply about the well-being of its employees.
Fresh air and sunlight contribute to a child's overall well-being.

Synset Examples

the town was finally on the upbeat after our recent troubles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - general health, happiness, and prosperity
  • Antonym: ill-being - a state of being unwell or unhappy

Etymology

From Middle English 'wel being', a compound of 'well' (in good health) + 'being'.
