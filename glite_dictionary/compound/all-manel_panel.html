all-manel panel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔl ˌmænəl ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːl ˌmænəl ˈpænəl / (BrE)
A discussion panel composed entirely of men, often used to highlight a lack of gender diversity.
panel with only male members

Examples

The tech summit was called out for featuring an all-manel panel on diversity.
Organizers apologized after the keynote session turned out to be another all-manel panel.
She refused to participate in an all-manel panel, advocating for more female representation.

Synset Examples

The conference was criticized for its all-manel panel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all-male - consisting entirely of men

Etymology

Blend of 'all-male' and 'panel', with playful reference to 'manel' as a portmanteau of 'man' and 'panel'.
