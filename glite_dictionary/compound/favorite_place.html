favorite place

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərət pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt pleɪs / (BrE)
a location that a person prefers above all others
most preferred location

Examples

The café on the corner is my favorite place to relax after work.
Every summer, we visit our favorite place by the lake.
Her bedroom is her favorite place to read and unwind.

Synset Examples

This is my favorite place in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite spot - a location someone loves most
  • Antonym: least favorite place - a location someone dislikes most
