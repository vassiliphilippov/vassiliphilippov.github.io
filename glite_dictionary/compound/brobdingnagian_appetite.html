Brobdingnagian appetite

1. NOUN

/ ˌbrɑb.dɪɡˈnæ.ɡi.ən ˈæp.əˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˌbrɒb.dɪŋˈnæ.ɡi.ən ˈap.ɪ.taɪt / (BrE)
An extremely large or excessive appetite; a voracious desire for food.
an enormous or excessive appetite

Examples

He sat down to dinner with a Brobdingnagian appetite, devouring everything in sight.
Despite her slender build, she was famous for her Brobdingnagian appetite at family gatherings.
The chef was amazed by his guest’s Brobdingnagian appetite and kept bringing out more food.

Synset Examples

Her Brobdingnagian appetite surprised everyone at the dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ravenous - extremely hungry
  • Synonym: insatiable appetite - an appetite that cannot be satisfied

Etymology

From 'Brobdingnag,' a land of giants in Jonathan Swift's 'Gulliver's Travels' (1726), plus 'appetite.'
