lawn party

1. NOUN

/ lɔn ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / lɔːn ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering held outdoors, typically on a lawn, for refreshments and entertainment
outdoor social gathering on a lawn

Examples

We were invited to a magnificent lawn party at the governor's residence.
The annual lawn party raised money for local charities.
Guests enjoyed afternoon tea and games at the lawn party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden party - an outdoor party held in a garden
