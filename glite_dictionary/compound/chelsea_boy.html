Chelsea boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛlsi bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛlsi bɔɪ / (BrE)
A fashionable young man, typically from Chelsea in London, known for stylish dress and a particular attitude or lifestyle.
fashionable young man from Chelsea, London
british

Examples

He was recognized as a true Chelsea boy by his impeccable fashion sense and confidence.
The club was crowded with Chelsea boys showing off the latest trends.
After moving to London, he quickly adopted the Chelsea boy look.

Synset Examples

She dated a charming Chelsea boy from London.
He always dresses like a typical Chelsea boy on weekends.
The Chelsea boy was known for his stylish outfits.

Etymology

Named after the Chelsea district in London, noted for its trendsetting youth culture from the 1960s onward.
