creepoid behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈkriːpˌɔɪd bəˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈkriːpˌɔɪd bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
strange, disturbing, or unsettling actions that make others uncomfortable, often reminiscent of or resembling a 'creep'
unsettling or creepy actions
informal slang

Examples

His creepoid behavior made everyone at the party feel on edge.
Coworkers complained about his creepoid behavior during late-night shifts.
She was quick to leave when she noticed his creepoid behavior toward strangers.

Synset Examples

His creepoid behavior made everyone at the event uncomfortable.
She avoided him because of his creepoid tendencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creepy - causing a feeling of fear or unease
  • Antonym: normal behavior - actions considered typical or acceptable

Etymology

From 'creepoid', a blend of 'creep' and the suffix '-oid' (resembling), and 'behavior'.
