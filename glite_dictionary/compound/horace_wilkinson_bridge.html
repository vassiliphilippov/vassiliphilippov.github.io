Horace Wilkinson Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˌhɔːrɪs ˈwɪlkɪnsən brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪs ˈwɪlkɪnsən brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a cantilever bridge spanning the Mississippi River at Baton Rouge, Louisiana.
bridge across the Mississippi at Baton Rouge

Examples

Traffic was delayed on the Horace Wilkinson Bridge due to maintenance work.
The Horace Wilkinson Bridge is a vital link for commuters in Baton Rouge.
At sunset, the Horace Wilkinson Bridge offers a stunning view of the Mississippi River.

Etymology

Named after Horace Wilkinson, a local Louisiana legislator.
