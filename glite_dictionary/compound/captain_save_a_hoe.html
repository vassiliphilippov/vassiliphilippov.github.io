captain save a hoe

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæp.tɪn ˈseɪv ə hoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkæp.tɪn ˈseɪv ə həʊ / (BrE)
(slang) A man who habitually tries to rescue or help women, especially in a way considered excessive, naïve, or self-important.
man who overzealously helps women (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

Everyone teased Marcus for being a captain save a hoe when he intervened in yet another argument.
She warned him that his captain save a hoe attitude might get him into trouble someday.
Online forums often mock users who act like captain save a hoe toward people they've never met.

Synset Examples

Being captain save a hoe isn't sustainable; he needs to focus on himself.
She joked that he was captain save a hoe, always helping women in distress.
He acted like captain save a hoe, always rescuing his friends from trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white knight - a man who acts chivalrously to gain approval, often online; similar slang term

Etymology

From the comic-like merging of 'captain' (heroic leader) and 'save a hoe' (slang for saving a promiscuous woman), popularized in 1990s hip-hop culture.
