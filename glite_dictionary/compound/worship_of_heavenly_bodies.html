worship of heavenly bodies

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrʃɪp əv ˈhɛvənli ˈbɑdiz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp ɒv ˈhevənli ˈbɒdiz / (BrE)
the practice of worshipping planets or stars as deities or spiritual entities
religious worship of planets or stars

Examples

Ancient civilizations often engaged in worship of heavenly bodies as part of their religious practices.
The worship of heavenly bodies was common in many early cultures, reflecting awe of the night sky.
Scholars study how the worship of heavenly bodies influenced art and architecture in history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astrolatry - the worship of stars or celestial bodies
