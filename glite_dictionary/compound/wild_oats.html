wild oats

1. NOUN

/ waɪld oʊts / (AmE), / waɪld əʊts / (BrE)
any of various perennial plants of the genus Uvularia, having yellowish, drooping, bell-shaped flowers, found in North America.
plant with yellow, bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The forest floor was covered with wild oats blooming in the early spring.
Gardeners admire the delicate yellow flowers produced by wild oats.
While hiking, we spotted a patch of wild oats growing along the shaded path.
