tape cartridge

1. NOUN

/ teɪp ˈkɑːrt̬rɪdʒ / (AmE), / teɪp ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A cartridge containing magnetic tape, used with audio or video recorders, or in computer systems for data storage.
magnetic tape container for recorders or computers

Examples

Many classic albums were released on tape cartridge before cassette tapes became popular.
The old computer uses a tape cartridge for backing up data.
He found a tape cartridge full of vintage radio recordings in his attic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic tape - A tape coated with magnetic material used for recording sound or data
