crew neckline

1. NOUN

/ kruː ˈnaɪk.laɪn / (AmE), / kruː ˈnaɪk.laɪn / (BrE)
A rounded, close-fitting neckline that sits at the base of the neck, extending from shoulder to shoulder, commonly found on sweaters and T-shirts.
rounded, close-fitting sweater neckline

Examples

I prefer sweaters with a crew neckline because they keep my neck warm.
The shirt's crew neckline gives it a classic and casual look.
This dress features a delicate lace overlay and a simple crew neckline.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: V-neck - a neckline in the shape of a V at the front of a garment

Etymology

Named after crewneck sweaters originally worn by rowing crews.
