passion tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæʃ.ən tri / (AmE), / ˈpæʃ.ən triː / (BrE)
A flowering plant, typically the passionflower or passionfruit plant, known for its attractive flowers and edible fruit.
a flowering plant with showy flowers and fruit

Examples

A passion tree can add a tropical touch to any garden.
The fruit harvested from our passion tree makes delicious juice.
Many gardeners choose the passion tree for its colorful blossoms and sweet scent.

Synset Examples

They planted a passion tree for its fragrant flowers.
The passion tree in my garden is blooming beautifully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionflower - A vine or shrub with distinctive flowers, of the genus Passiflora.
  • Synonym: passionfruit - The edible fruit of certain passionflower plants.

Etymology

From 'passion', referring to the passionflower, and 'tree', referring to its growth habit.
