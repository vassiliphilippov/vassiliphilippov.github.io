simultaneous operation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəs ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪməlˈteɪnɪəs ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of carrying out two or more operations at the same time
two or more operations carried out at once

Examples

The new software allows for simultaneous operation of multiple applications on the same device.
Effective communication is essential when coordinating the simultaneous operation of several machines.
In medical procedures, the simultaneous operation of equipment ensures greater efficiency and safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parallel processing - processing of data by several processors at the same time
