rate of flow

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪt əv floʊ / (AmE), / ˈreɪt əv fləʊ / (BrE)
the quantity of a fluid passing through a given point or area per unit of time
quantity of fluid passing per unit time

Examples

The engineer measured the rate of flow to ensure the pump was working efficiently.
An increased rate of flow can cause erosion in riverbanks over time.
Doctors monitor the rate of flow of intravenous fluids during surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flow rate - rate at which fluid flows; alternative wording
