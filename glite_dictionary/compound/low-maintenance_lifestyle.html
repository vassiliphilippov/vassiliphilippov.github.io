low-maintenance lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊˈmeɪntənəns ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈmeɪntənəns ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living that is simple and easy, requiring little effort, time, or resources to maintain.
simple, easy lifestyle needing little effort

Examples

She enjoys a low-maintenance lifestyle that allows her more free time and less stress.
Many retirees seek a low-maintenance lifestyle when they move to smaller homes.
Adopting a low-maintenance lifestyle often means spending less money and having fewer commitments.

Synset Examples

She loves her low-maintenance lifestyle without much fuss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minimalist lifestyle - a lifestyle characterized by minimal possessions and simplicity
