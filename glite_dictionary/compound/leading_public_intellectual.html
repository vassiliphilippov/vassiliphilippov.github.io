leading public intellectual

1. NOUN

/ ˈlidɪŋ ˈpʌblɪk ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈliːdɪŋ ˈpʌblɪk ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl / (BrE)
a prominent thinker or scholar who is widely recognized for contributing to public debates and shaping societal ideas
prominent public thinker or scholar

Examples

As a leading public intellectual, she often writes on important social and ethical issues.
The conference invited several leading public intellectuals to discuss the future of democracy.
His reputation as a leading public intellectual grew after publishing his groundbreaking book.

Synset Examples

He is regarded as a leading public intellectual today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public intellectual - an intellectual who addresses societal issues for a broad audience
