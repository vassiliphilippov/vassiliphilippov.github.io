ENT man

1. NOUN

/ iː ɛn tiː mæn / (AmE), / iː ɛn tiː mæn / (BrE)
A male doctor who specializes in conditions affecting the ear, nose, or throat.
male ear, nose, and throat specialist
medical informal

Examples

After weeks of sinus pain, I finally made an appointment with the ENT man at the clinic.
My father worked as an ENT man for over twenty years, helping thousands of patients.
Whenever children in the family have ear infections, we consult our trusted ENT man.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ENT specialist - medical doctor specializing in the ear, nose, and throat
  • Synonym: ENT woman - female specialist in the disorders of the ear, nose, or throat

Etymology

ENT is an abbreviation of 'ear, nose, and throat', referring to the medical specialty otorhinolaryngology.
