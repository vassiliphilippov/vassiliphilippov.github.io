Roman deity

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.mən ˈdi.aɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən ˈdiː.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the ancient Romans
god or goddess of ancient Rome

Examples

Jupiter, a powerful Roman deity, was considered the king of the gods.
Many temples in Rome were dedicated to a specific Roman deity.
The myth of Venus, a famous Roman deity, fascinated artists for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greek deity - a god or goddess worshipped by the ancient Greeks
  • Synonym: Roman god - male deity worshipped by the ancient Romans
  • Synonym: Roman goddess - female deity worshipped by the ancient Romans
