compact disc recordable

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmˌpækt dɪsk rɪˈkɔrdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpækt dɪsk rɪˈkɔːdəbəl / (BrE)
A type of compact disc that can be written to once, after which data becomes permanent and the disc can only be read from.
writable-once compact disc

Examples

I used a compact disc recordable to back up my important files.
Many music albums in the early 2000s were distributed on compact disc recordable formats.
The software allows you to burn data onto a compact disc recordable for permanent storage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact disc - an optical disc used to store digital data
  • Degree Higher: compact disc rewritable - a compact disc that can be written, erased, and rewritten several times
