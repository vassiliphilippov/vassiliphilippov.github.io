Gulf Coast

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌlf koʊst / (AmE), / ɡʌlf kəʊst / (BrE)
the coastal region along the Gulf of Mexico, especially in the southern United States
coastal region along the Gulf of Mexico

Examples

Hurricane-force winds battered the Gulf Coast last night, causing widespread flooding.
Tourists flock to the Gulf Coast each year for its beaches and seafood.
Many residents of the Gulf Coast rely on fishing and shipping industries for their livelihood.
