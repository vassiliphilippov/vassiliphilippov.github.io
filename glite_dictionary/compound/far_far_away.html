far far away

1. ADV

/ fɑr fɑr əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fɑː fɑː əˈweɪ / (BrE)
at a very great distance; in a place that is extremely remote, often used for emphasis or in a literary context
very distant or remote place

Examples

As a child, she loved listening to tales set far far away.
The spaceship traveled to galaxies far far away from Earth.
He always wanted to escape to somewhere far far away and start anew.

Synset Examples

He dreams of adventures in places far far away.
The story takes us to a kingdom far far away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distant - far away in space or time
  • Synonym: remote - situated far from the main centers; distant
