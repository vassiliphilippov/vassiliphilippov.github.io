Black person

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / blæk ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person with dark skin, especially someone of African ancestry
a person of African descent or with dark skin

Examples

Many historians have studied how the experiences of a Black person in the 20th century differed from others.
Being the only Black person in her class made her feel isolated at times.
The museum features exhibits celebrating the achievements of every Black person in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: African American - a Black person of American nationality or descent
  • Antonym: white person - a person of European descent or with light skin
