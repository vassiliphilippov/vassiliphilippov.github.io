blowing up

1. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊɪŋ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbləʊɪŋ ˌʌp / (BrE)
an angry or severe scolding or rebuke
severe scolding or rebuke
informal

Examples

After arriving late for the third time, Mark got a real blowing up from his boss.
The coach gave the team a stern blowing up after their poor performance.
She dreaded going home because she expected a blowing up from her parents about her grades.

Synset Examples

he deserved the berating that the coach gave him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: berating - a strong verbal scolding or criticism
  • Synonym: reprimand - an official rebuke or criticism
  • Antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
