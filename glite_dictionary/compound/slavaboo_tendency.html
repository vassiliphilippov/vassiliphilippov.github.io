Slavaboo tendency

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɑːvəˌbuː tɛnˈdɛnsi / (AmE), / ˈslɑːvəˌbuː tɛnˈdɛnsi / (BrE)
an excessive or fetishizing fascination with Slavic culture, customs, or people, often by non-Slavic individuals
fetishizing fascination with Slavic culture
informal

Examples

Her Slavaboo tendency was obvious from her bookshelf filled with Russian literature and folk tales.
In online discussions, he was accused of having a Slavaboo tendency due to his romanticized view of Eastern Europe.
Their Slavaboo tendencies became clear when they insisted on using only Slavic names for their pets.

Synset Examples

Her Slavaboo tendencies show in her love for traditional dances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weeaboo - a non-Japanese person obsessed with Japanese culture

Etymology

Formed from 'Slav' (referring to Slavic peoples) and '-aboo', a playful suffix from 'weeaboo' (originally used about Japanese culture enthusiasts).
