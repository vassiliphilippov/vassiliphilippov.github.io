the last word

1. NOUN

/ ðə læst wɝd / (AmE), / ðə lɑːst wɜːd / (BrE)
the final decision, authority, or opinion in a discussion or matter
final decision or authority

Examples

Ultimately, the CEO has the last word on all hiring decisions.
In their household, his grandmother always had the last word about the menu.
No changes will be made to the plan unless the director gives the last word.

Synset Examples

He gave the last word on the project deadline.
The last word was left to the manager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final say - the ultimate authority or decision, especially after other opinions have been expressed
