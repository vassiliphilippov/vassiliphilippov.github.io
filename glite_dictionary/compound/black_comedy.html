black comedy

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈkɑːmədi / (AmE), / blæk ˈkɒmədi / (BrE)
Comedy that treats serious, disturbing, or taboo subjects in a humorous or satirical manner.
humor dealing with dark or taboo topics

Examples

The movie is a black comedy that finds humor in life’s darkest moments.
Many critics praised the play’s bold use of black comedy to address taboo issues.
He has a talent for writing black comedy that challenges audiences to think differently about tragedy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black humor - humor making light of serious or taboo subjects
  • Synonym: gallows humor - grim or ironic humor in desperate situations
