scene painter

1. NOUN

/ siːn ˈpeɪntɚ / (AmE), / siːn ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
an artist who paints scenery, especially for theatrical productions or pictures featuring landscapes and other scenic views
artist painting scenery or landscapes

Examples

The theater hired a skilled scene painter to create the elaborate backdrops for the new play.
As a scene painter, she specialized in crafting realistic landscapes on large canvases.
The job of a scene painter requires both artistic talent and technical skill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scenic artist - a person who paints scenery for stage productions

2. NOUN

/ siːn ˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / siːn ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
a person who paints theatrical scenery for stage productions
person who paints theatre scenery

Examples

The scene painter worked late to create beautiful backdrops for the play.
As a skilled scene painter, she was in high demand during the busy theatre season.
Every major production relies on a talented scene painter to bring the stage to life.

Related Terms
