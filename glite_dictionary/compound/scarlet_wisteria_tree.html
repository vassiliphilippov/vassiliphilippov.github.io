scarlet wisteria tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːrlət wɪˈstɪriə triː / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt wɪˈstɪəriə triː / (BrE)
a softwood tree with loose clusters of usually red or pink flowers, native to tropical Australia and Asia, and naturalized in southern Florida and the West Indies
tropical tree with red or pink flower clusters

Examples

The scarlet wisteria tree is prized for its vibrant blossoms in tropical gardens.
Gardeners in southern Florida sometimes plant the scarlet wisteria tree for its ornamental value.
The scarlet wisteria tree has naturalized in parts of the West Indies, where it adds color to the landscape.
