Turco-Albanian history

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɝːkoʊ ælˈbeɪniən ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˌtʊə.kəʊ ælˈbeɪ.ni.ən ˈhɪs.təri / (BrE)
the history of interactions and relations between Turkish and Albanian peoples and cultures, especially during the Ottoman period
history of Turkish-Albanian relations

Examples

The seminar explored important events in Turco-Albanian history during the Ottoman Empire.
She wrote her thesis on migration patterns in Turco-Albanian history.
Debates on cultural influence often reference Turco-Albanian history as a case study.

Synset Examples

They discussed Turco-Albanian history at the cultural festival.

Etymology

‘Turco-’ from Italian ‘turco’ (Turk) and ‘Albanian’, relating to Albania; used to designate shared history or cultural aspects.
