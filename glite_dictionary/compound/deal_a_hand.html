to deal a hand

1. VERB

/ diːl ə hænd / (AmE), / diːl ə hænd / (BrE)
to distribute a set of playing cards to each player in a game
distribute cards to players

Examples

Before we begin, someone needs to deal a hand to each player.
Could you deal a hand so we can get started?
He was assigned to deal a hand at the start of every round.

Synset Examples
