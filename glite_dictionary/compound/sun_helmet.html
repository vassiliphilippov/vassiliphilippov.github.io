sun helmet

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈhɛlmət / (AmE), / sʌn ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
A lightweight hat with a brim, worn in tropical countries to protect the head and neck from the sun.
tropical sun-protective hat

Examples

Explorers in the desert often wore a sun helmet to shield themselves from intense heat.
He packed his sun helmet and sunscreen before heading on safari in Kenya.
The old photograph showed soldiers in uniform, each wearing a distinctive sun helmet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pith helmet - a type of lightweight sun helmet made from pith
