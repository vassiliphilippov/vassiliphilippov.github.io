patent system

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætnt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈpætnt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a legal system for granting and protecting the rights of inventors to exclusively exploit their inventions for a certain period
legal system protecting inventors' rights
legal

Examples

Many countries have implemented a patent system to encourage technological innovation.
Critics argue that the current patent system can sometimes hinder rather than promote progress.
The reform of the patent system is a major topic among policymakers and inventors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patent law - the area of law dealing with patents and inventors' rights
