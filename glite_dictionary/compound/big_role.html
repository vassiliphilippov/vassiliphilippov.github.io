big role

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ roʊl / (AmE), / bɪɡ rəʊl / (BrE)
an important or significant part or function in a process, event, or situation
important part or function

Examples

Technological innovation has played a big role in shaping modern society.
She had a big role in organizing the conference.
Climate change will have a big role in determining our future.

Synset Examples

Neology plays a big role in contemporary linguistic research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: major role - an important or leading part or function
  • Antonym: small role - an unimportant or minor part or function
