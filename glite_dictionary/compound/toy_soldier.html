toy soldier

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ ˈsoʊlʤɚ / (AmE), / tɔɪ ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
A small figurine, typically made of plastic or metal, designed to resemble a soldier and used as a child's toy.
child's figurine resembling a soldier

Examples

He lined up each toy soldier on the windowsill, ready for battle.
As a child, she collected antique toy soldiers from different countries.
The old toy soldier was missing its helmet but still stood proudly among the toys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: action figure - a posable doll representing a character, often a hero or soldier
