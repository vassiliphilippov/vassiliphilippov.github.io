electromagnetic transducer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊmæɡˌnɛtɪk trænzˈdjuːsər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊmæɡˌnɛtɪk trænzˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
A device that converts electromagnetic waves or magnetic fields to and from electrical signals.
device converting electromagnetic and electrical signals

Examples

The radar system uses an electromagnetic transducer to detect incoming signals.
Recent advances have improved the efficiency of the electromagnetic transducer in portable devices.
Each speaker contains an electromagnetic transducer that converts electrical signals into sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transducer - a device that converts one form of energy to another
