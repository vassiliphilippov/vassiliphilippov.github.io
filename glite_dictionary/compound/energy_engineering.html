energy engineering

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the branch of engineering concerned with improving energy efficiency, managing energy use, and developing alternative energy technologies
branch of engineering focused on energy efficiency and management

Examples

The company hired an energy engineering firm to improve their building's efficiency.
Many important advances in sustainability have come from the field of energy engineering.
