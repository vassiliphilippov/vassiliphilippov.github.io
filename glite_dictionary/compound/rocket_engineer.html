rocket engineer

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑː.kɪt ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˈrɒk.ɪt ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
an engineer who designs, builds, and tests rockets or rocket propulsion systems
engineer specializing in rockets

Examples

After earning his degree, Tom became a rocket engineer at a leading aerospace company.
The rocket engineer carefully calculated the fuel requirements for the mission.
As a rocket engineer, she contributed to the development of new propulsion systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerospace engineer - an engineer who works on the design and development of aircraft and spacecraft
