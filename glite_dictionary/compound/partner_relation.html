partner relation

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrtnər rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtnə rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the legal or ethical responsibility of business partners to act in the best interests of one another within a partnership
duty of partners to each other in a partnership
legal

Examples

Every partner relation requires each member to be honest and transparent in all matters.
The firm's partner relation includes sharing both profits and liabilities.
A breach of partner relation can lead to serious legal consequences for those involved.
