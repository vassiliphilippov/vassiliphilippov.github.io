Boobus Americanus

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊbəs əˌmɛrɪˈkænəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊbəs əˌmɛrɪˈkænəs / (BrE)
A disparaging term for the average American, viewed as ignorant or lacking critical awareness.
derogatory term for ignorant American
offensive

Examples

Many commentators complain that Boobus Americanus is easily misled by sensational news coverage.
The book harshly criticizes political campaigns for pandering to Boobus Americanus.
He dismissed his critics as typical examples of Boobus Americanus, uninterested in nuance or fact.

Synset Examples

Critics argue that Boobus Americanus ignores important issues.

Etymology

Pseudo-Latin formation; 'boobus' as a mockery of 'booby', meaning a fool, and 'Americanus', imitating Latin species names.
