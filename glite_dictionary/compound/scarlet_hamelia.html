scarlet hamelia

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑrlɪt həˈmiːliə / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt həˈmiːlɪə / (BrE)
a tropical shrub with bright orange to scarlet or crimson flowers, native to Florida, the West Indies, Mexico, and Brazil
tropical shrub with scarlet flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece was a scarlet hamelia in full bloom, attracting dozens of hummingbirds.
Many landscapers in Florida use scarlet hamelia for its vibrant flowers and wildlife appeal.
The scarlet hamelia thrives in warm climates and requires little maintenance.
