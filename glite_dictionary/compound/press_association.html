press association

1. NOUN

/ prɛs əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / prɛs əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization that gathers and provides news reports to newspapers and other media outlets, typically distributing them electronically or by wire
news-gathering and distributing agency

Examples

The press association issued a statement about the unfolding political crisis.
Many newspapers rely on a press association for international news coverage.
She works for the national press association as a senior correspondent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news agency - an organization that supplies news reports to media outlets
