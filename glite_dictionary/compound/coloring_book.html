coloring book

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚɪŋ bʊk / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.ər.ɪŋ bʊk / (BrE)
A book containing line drawings for children to color, typically using crayons or colored pencils.
book of drawings for coloring

Examples

My niece received a new coloring book for her birthday and spent hours coloring in the pictures.
Children enjoyed the coloring book during the rainy afternoon at camp.
The store sells a variety of coloring books featuring animals and cartoon characters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activity book - book with puzzles and activities for children
