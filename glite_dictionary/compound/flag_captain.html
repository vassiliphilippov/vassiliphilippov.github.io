flag captain

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ ˈkæp.tɪn / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
the captain in command of a flagship, particularly when it serves as the command ship for an admiral
captain of a flagship

Examples

The flag captain reported directly to the admiral during naval operations.
As flag captain, she was responsible for both the flagship and the admiral’s orders.
The experienced flag captain coordinated the fleet’s maneuvers from the bridge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captain - officer in command of a ship or vessel
