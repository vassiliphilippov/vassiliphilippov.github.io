buffalo wing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌfəloʊ wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ wɪŋ / (BrE)
a deep-fried chicken wing coated in a spicy sauce, typically served as an appetizer
spicy chicken wing appetizer

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its spicy buffalo wings and homemade sauces.
She ordered a plate of buffalo wings as a starter for the table.
We watched the football game while eating buffalo wings and celery sticks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken wing - a cut of chicken consisting of the wing

Etymology

Named after Buffalo, New York, where the dish originated.
