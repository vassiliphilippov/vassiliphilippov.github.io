long drive

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ draɪv / (AmE), / lɒŋ draɪv / (BrE)
a journey by car that takes a long time or covers a large distance
a lengthy car journey

Examples

After a long drive, we finally reached the cabin just before nightfall.
The family prepared snacks and games for the long drive ahead.
He felt exhausted after the long drive through the mountains.

Synset Examples

The car's hood was hot after the long drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road trip - a journey made by car, typically for leisure
