total douchewad

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈduːʃ.wæd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈduːʃ.wæd / (BrE)
An extremely rude, obnoxious, or contemptible person (insulting, vulgar slang).
an extremely obnoxious or contemptible person (slang insult)
slang vulgar offensive

Examples

I can't believe you called her that in front of everyone—you were being a total douchewad.
Sometimes my little brother acts like a total douchewad when he doesn't get his way.
Nobody likes working with a total douchewad who constantly insults the team.

Synset Examples

He acted like a total douchewad during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an irritating or contemptible person
  • Synonym: asshole - a very rude or unpleasant person (offensive slang)

Etymology

Formed from the slang insult 'douche', derived from French 'douche' (shower), plus 'wad' (slang, contemptible person).
