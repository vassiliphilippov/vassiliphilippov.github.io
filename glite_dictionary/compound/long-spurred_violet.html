long-spurred violet

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ spɝd ˈvaɪəlɪt / (AmE), / lɒŋ spɜːd ˈvaɪəlɪt / (BrE)
A violet of eastern North America with lilac-purple flowers and a long slender spur.
a North American violet with long slender spurred flowers

Examples

The long-spurred violet blooms in shady woodlands throughout the spring.
Gardeners appreciate the delicate appearance of the long-spurred violet in naturalized settings.
We spotted a cluster of long-spurred violets growing near the forest trail.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: violet - a plant with purple or blue flowers in the genus Viola
