chemistry lab

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪstri læb / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪstri læb / (BrE)
A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching in chemistry.
room for chemistry experiments and research

Examples

The students spent their afternoon working in the chemistry lab on a new experiment.
She forgot her safety goggles on the table in the chemistry lab.
Renovations to the chemistry lab will improve both safety and equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - room or building for scientific experiments or research
