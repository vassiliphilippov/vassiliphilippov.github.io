fishsicle treat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌsɪkəl tɹit / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌsɪkəl tɹiːt / (BrE)
A frozen dessert or snack made in the shape of a fish, often colorful and intended for children.
frozen treat shaped like a fish

Examples

After playing outside, the children cooled off with a fishsicle treat from the freezer.
The new ice cream truck offers a popular fishsicle treat among its novelty items.
She posted a photo of her bright blue fishsicle treat on social media.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the colorful fishsicle treats.

Etymology

Blend of 'fish' and 'popsicle,' referencing its shape and frozen nature.
