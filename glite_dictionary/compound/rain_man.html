rain man

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪn mæn / (AmE), / ˈreɪn man / (BrE)
a person who displays extraordinary mental ability, especially exceptional memory or calculation skills, often in a manner reminiscent of an autistic savant
person with exceptional mental abilities
informal

Examples

Ever since he was a child, people have called him a rain man because of his exceptional recall for numbers and facts.
In meetings, Sarah is the office rain man, never forgetting a single detail from presentations.
The quiz show contestant was nicknamed rain man by fans after breaking the long-standing record for correct answers.

Synset Examples

He’s like a rain man when it comes to remembering dates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savant - a person with exceptional mental abilities, especially in a specific area
  • Synonym: genius - a person of exceptionally high intelligence

Etymology

From the 1988 film 'Rain Man', in which the character Raymond Babbitt, an autistic savant, displays remarkable memory and calculation abilities.
