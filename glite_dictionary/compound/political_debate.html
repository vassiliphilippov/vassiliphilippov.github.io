political debate

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
a formal discussion or argument about political issues, often involving different viewpoints
discussion or argument about political issues

Examples

The candidates participated in a heated political debate on national television.
Immigration policy was the main topic of last night's political debate.
Many students attended the political debate organized by the university's discussion society.

Synset Examples

The term 'russist' is often used in political debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argument - a discussion in which people express different opinions, often angrily
