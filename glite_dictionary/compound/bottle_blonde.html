bottle blonde

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːtəl blɑnd / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəl blɒnd / (BrE)
A person whose hair has been bleached or dyed blonde, rather than being naturally blonde.
person with dyed blonde hair

Examples

She reinvented her look as a bottle blonde and suddenly became the talk of the town.
Many celebrities are actually bottle blondes rather than natural ones.
He admitted he was a bottle blonde after viewers noticed his roots on television.

Synset Examples

She is known for her striking bottle blonde hair.
His girlfriend is a bottle blonde who loves to experiment with colors.
Many bottle blonde women frequent the salon for touch-ups.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural blonde - person with naturally blonde hair

Etymology

From the practice of using bottled hair bleach or dye to achieve blonde hair.
