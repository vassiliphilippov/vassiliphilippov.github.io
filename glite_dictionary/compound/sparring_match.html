sparring match

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑrɪŋ mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɑːrɪŋ mætʃ / (BrE)
a practice or exhibition bout in boxing or martial arts, not intended as a formal contest
practice/exhibition bout (boxing/martial arts)

Examples

The two fighters scheduled a sparring match to prepare for the upcoming tournament.
During the sparring match, both boxers focused on improving their defensive techniques.
His coach closely observed every sparring match to identify areas for improvement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spar - to practice boxing with an opponent in a non-competitive way
