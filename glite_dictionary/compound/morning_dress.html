morning dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌdrɛs / (BrE)
formal men's daytime attire consisting of a tailcoat, waistcoat, and striped trousers, typically worn at weddings and formal events
men's formal daytime suit

Examples

He wore a traditional morning dress to the royal wedding.
The invitation specified that all male guests should appear in morning dress.
During the ceremony, the groom looked distinguished in his morning dress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: evening dress - formal evening attire for men

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
a woman's informal dress worn for housework, especially in the earlier part of the day
informal house dress for women

Examples

She changed into a morning dress before beginning her household chores.
Floral patterns were very popular for a morning dress in the early 20th century.
Her simple cotton morning dress was both comfortable and practical for daily tasks.
