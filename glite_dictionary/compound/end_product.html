end product

1. NOUN

/ ɛnd ˈprɑdʌkt / (AmE), / ɛnd ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
the final result or outcome of a process, especially in manufacturing or development.
final result or outcome of a process

Examples

The end product of their research was a revolutionary new drug.
After months of hard work, the end product exceeded everyone’s expectations.
Every stage of the process affects the quality of the end product.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: byproduct - a secondary result, not the primary outcome
  • Degree Lower: product - something that is produced as a result of a process
