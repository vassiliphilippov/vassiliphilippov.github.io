St Patty's Day

1. NOUN

/ seɪnt ˈpætiːz deɪ / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpætiːz deɪ / (BrE)
an informal name for St. Patrick's Day, a holiday celebrated on March 17th that honors Irish culture and heritage
informal term for St. Patrick's Day
informal

Examples

We're planning to go out and watch the parade on St Patty's Day.
Many people wear green and attend parties on St Patty's Day.
Every year, the city hosts a big festival for St Patty's Day.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: St. Patrick's Day - the traditional name for the holiday celebrating Irish culture and Saint Patrick

Etymology

Informal shortening and alteration of 'St. Patrick's Day', referring to Saint Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland.
