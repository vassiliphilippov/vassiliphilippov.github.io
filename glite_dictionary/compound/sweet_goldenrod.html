sweet goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈɡoʊl.dənˌrɑd / (AmE), / swiːt ˈɡəʊl.dənˌrɒd / (BrE)
A North American goldenrod (Solidago odora) with aromatic leaves used to make a medicinal tea.
North American aromatic goldenrod plant

Examples

The leaves of sweet goldenrod are often brewed into herbal tea for their soothing properties.
During the summer, sweet goldenrod brightens meadows with its clusters of yellow flowers.
Many herbalists recognize sweet goldenrod as a useful plant for traditional remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - any of various chiefly North American plants with clusters of small yellow flowers
