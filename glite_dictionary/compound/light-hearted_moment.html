light-hearted moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪt ˌhɑrtɪd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌhɑːtɪd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief period characterized by cheerfulness, humor, or carefree attitudes
cheerful or carefree occasion

Examples

She cracked a joke, creating a light-hearted moment during the tense meeting.
The reunion was filled with stories and light-hearted moments that brought smiles to everyone.
Even in stressful times, a light-hearted moment can lift everyone’s spirits.

Synset Examples

In a light-hearted moment, he shouted, 'let's go, Brandon!'
In a light-hearted moment, he referred to himself as a Jewbag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light-hearted - cheerful and carefree in disposition
  • Antonym: serious moment - a brief period characterized by seriousness or gravity
