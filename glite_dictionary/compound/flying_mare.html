flying mare

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ mɛr / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ meə / (BrE)
a wrestling move in which an opponent is thrown over the shoulder by a sudden twisting motion
wrestling maneuver involving a shoulder throw

Examples

The wrestler won the match with a perfect flying mare that took his opponent by surprise.
She practiced the flying mare repeatedly to improve her technique before the competition.
A swift flying mare can change the momentum of a wrestling bout in seconds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hip throw - a wrestling or judo throw involving the hip
  • Synonym: body slam - a wrestling maneuver in which an opponent is lifted and thrown to the mat
