family doctor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈdɑktər / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a general practitioner who provides medical care for people of all ages within a family, often over many years
doctor providing primary care for a family

Examples

Our family doctor has been taking care of us for over a decade.
Whenever my children feel unwell, we always schedule an appointment with our family doctor.
The family doctor recommended we see a specialist for further tests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: general practitioner - a doctor providing general medical treatment, not specialized
  • Antonym: specialist - a doctor specializing in a particular branch of medicine
