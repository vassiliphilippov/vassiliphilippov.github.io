baby-killer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.biˌkɪl.ər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.biˌkɪl.ə / (BrE)
An offensive term for a person who kills or is accused of killing children or infants, often used as an insult.
derogatory term for one who kills children or infants
offensive

Examples

The media outrage labeled the defendant a baby-killer after the tragic incident.
Protesters shouted 'murderer' and baby-killer as they marched outside the facility.
He was deeply hurt when someone called him a baby-killer on social media.

Synset Examples

She was furious at the baby-killer comments about her choices.
The politician was labeled a baby-killer for his controversial policies.
His reckless driving made him feel like a baby-killer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child murderer - a person who kills a child; more formal/neutral term
