overstuffed chair

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚˈstʌft tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstʌft tʃɛː / (BrE)
a large and comfortable upholstered armchair, heavily padded and typically with broad arms and back
heavily padded, comfortable armchair

Examples

She curled up in the overstuffed chair with a cup of tea and a book.
The living room features a brown leather sofa and a matching overstuffed chair.
He fell asleep in the overstuffed chair by the fireplace after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms

Etymology

From 'overstuffed' (excessively filled with padding) + 'chair'.
