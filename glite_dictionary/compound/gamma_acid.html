gamma acid

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæmə ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡæmə ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a crystalline organic compound used in the manufacture of certain azo dyes
chemical used in making azo dyes

Examples

The production of red azo dyes often involves the use of gamma acid as an intermediate.
Scientists studied the properties of gamma acid to optimize the dye synthesis process.
Proper handling of gamma acid is important due to its chemical reactivity in the laboratory.
