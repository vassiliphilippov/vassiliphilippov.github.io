filing system

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪlɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪlɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a method of organizing documents or information into files, often in alphabetical or other systematic order, for easy retrieval
system for organizing files

Examples

The new filing system made it much easier to locate important documents quickly.
Each department uses a different filing system to manage their records.
Switching to a digital filing system helped reduce paper clutter in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archive - a collection of historical documents or records
  • Degree Higher: database - an organized collection of data
