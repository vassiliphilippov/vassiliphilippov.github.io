oil nut

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl nʌt / (AmE), / ɔɪl nʌt / (BrE)
an oily, fleshy fruit (drupe) of the rabbitwood tree, valued for its oil content
oily fruit of the rabbitwood

Examples

The oil nut is harvested for its rich oil used in cooking and cosmetics.
She examined the oil nut from the rabbitwood tree before pressing it for oil.
Local people often collect oil nuts during the harvest season.
