dead white European male

1. NOUN

/ dɛd waɪt jʊˈrəʊpiən meɪl / (AmE), / dɛd waɪt jʊəˈrəʊpiən meɪl / (BrE)
A term used to stereotype historical figures, especially in academia or the arts, as representing traditional, privileged perspectives, typically Western, male, and of European descent.
stereotype for traditional privileged Western figures

Examples

The university's curriculum was criticized for focusing mainly on dead white European males.
Discussions about art history often center on the contributions of dead white European males.
Some students demand a broader syllabus that is not dominated by dead white European males.
