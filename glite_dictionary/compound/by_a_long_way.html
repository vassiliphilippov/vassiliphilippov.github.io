by a long way

1. ADV

/ baɪ ə lɔŋ weɪ / (AmE), / baɪ ə lɒŋ weɪ / (BrE)
By a large amount or to a significant extent; by far.
by a significant margin

Examples

She is the top student in her class by a long way.
That was by a long way the most difficult decision I've ever made.
The company outperformed its rivals by a long way this year.

Synset Examples

She's the best player on the team by a long way.
This is the most challenging project by a long way.
It was the hardest exam I faced by a long way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: by far - by a considerable margin
