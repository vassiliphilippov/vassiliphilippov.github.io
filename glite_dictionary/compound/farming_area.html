farming area

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
A rural region where agriculture is the main activity or occupation.
region where agriculture is practiced

Examples

Many families in the farming area depend on crops for their livelihoods.
The farming area was hit hard by drought this year.
Development is threatening wildlife habitats in the local farming area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmland - land that is used for farming
  • Antonym: urban area - a region characterized by a high population density and developed infrastructure
