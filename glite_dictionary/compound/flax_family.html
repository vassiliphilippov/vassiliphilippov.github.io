flax family

1. NOUN

/ flæks ˈfæməli / (AmE), / flæks ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Linaceae), including flax and related species, widely distributed in temperate and subtropical regions
family of flowering plants, Linaceae

Examples

The flax family comprises several genera, including both herbaceous and woody plants.
Linseed oil is derived from the seeds of plants in the flax family.
Members of the flax family are found in many parts of the world.
