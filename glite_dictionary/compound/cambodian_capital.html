Cambodian capital

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈboʊdiən ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / kæmˈbəʊdiən ˈkæpɪtəl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cambodia, also known as Phnom Penh
capital city of Cambodia
