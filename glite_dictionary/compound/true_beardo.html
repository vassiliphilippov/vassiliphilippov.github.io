true beardo

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈbɪrdoʊ / (AmE), / truː ˈbɪədəʊ / (BrE)
a man who takes great pride in grooming and maintaining his beard, often seen as embodying the bearded lifestyle
man with a well-groomed, cared-for beard
informal

Examples

Tom takes so much care of his beard that everyone calls him a true beardo.
At the festival, there were plenty of true beardos proudly showing off their facial hair.
Becoming a true beardo requires patience and attention to grooming.

Synset Examples

His friends joked that he was a true beardo, always grooming his facial hair meticulously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beardo - a man with a beard

Etymology

Beardo, from 'beard' + informal suffix '-o', meaning a bearded man; 'true' added for emphasis.
