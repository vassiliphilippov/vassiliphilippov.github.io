on account of

1. ADP

/ ɑn əˈkaʊnt ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn əˈkaʊnt ɒv / (BrE)
because of; as a result of
because of; due to

Examples

The concert was canceled on account of heavy rain.
He was absent from work on account of illness.
Several roads were closed on account of flooding.
