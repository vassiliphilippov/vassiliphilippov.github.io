to give a little smile

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə ˈlɪt̬l̩ smaɪl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ˈlɪtl smaɪl / (BrE)
to smile briefly or gently, often to convey a modest or reserved reaction
to smile briefly or gently

Examples

She gave a little smile when she heard the good news.
He couldn't help but give a little smile at the child's excitement.
During the meeting, Anna gave a little smile to show her appreciation.

Synset Examples

She gave a little smile before leaving the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smile - to make a facial expression in which the corners of the mouth turn up, showing happiness or amusement
