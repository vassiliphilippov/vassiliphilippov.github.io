coordinated universal time

1. NOUN

/ koʊˌɔrdəˌneɪtɪd juːˈnɪvərsəl taɪm / (AmE), / kəʊˌɔːdɪneɪtɪd juːˈnɪvəsəl taɪm / (BrE)
The standard time system based on Greenwich Mean Time, but updated with leap seconds to stay in sync with Earth's rotation; used as the basis for civil time worldwide.
global standard time system

Examples

All official schedules are published in coordinated universal time to avoid confusion across time zones.
The launch was timed precisely at 14:00 coordinated universal time.
Flight plans reference coordinated universal time to ensure clarity for international operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greenwich Mean Time - the mean solar time at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich, London
