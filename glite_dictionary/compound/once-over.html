once-over

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌns ˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈwʌns ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a rapid and cursory examination or inspection of someone or something
quick examination or inspection
informal

Examples

She gave the dress a once-over before deciding to buy it.
The mechanic did a quick once-over on the engine and found the problem.
Give your essay a once-over to check for any mistakes before you submit it.

Synset Examples

I gave him the once-over

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glance - a quick look at something
