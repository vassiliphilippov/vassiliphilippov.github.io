Suriname River

1. PROPN

/ ˌsʊrɪˌnɑm ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌsjʊərɪˌnɑːm ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in Suriname that flows northward to the Atlantic Ocean
major river in Suriname, South America

Examples

The Suriname River is an important waterway for transportation in the region.
Many small villages are situated along the banks of the Suriname River.
During the rainy season, the Suriname River often rises and floods the surrounding areas.

Etymology

Named after Suriname, the country through which it flows.
