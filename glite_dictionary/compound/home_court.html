home court

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm kɔrt / (AmE), / həʊm kɔːt / (BrE)
in basketball and other sports, the court where a team plays its home games.
court where home team plays (sports)

Examples

The crowd cheered loudly as their team defended their home court.
They have a strong record on their home court this season.
Winning on the opponent's home court can be especially challenging.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: away court - the court where the opposing team plays its home games
