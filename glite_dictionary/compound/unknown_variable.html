unknown variable

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈnoʊn ˈvɛriəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊn ˈveəriəbl / (BrE)
a symbol in an equation representing a value that is not yet known or has yet to be determined
symbol for an undetermined value in equations

Examples

In the equation 3x + 5 = 20, x is the unknown variable.
Scientists often use an unknown variable to represent an outcome they are trying to predict.
Solving for the unknown variable requires rearranging the equation.

Synset Examples

The x in the equation represents the unknown variable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variable - a symbol representing a quantity that may assume any value
