financial goal

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl ɡoʊl / (AmE), / faɪˈnanʃəl ɡəʊl / (BrE)
a specific objective or target relating to the management, saving, or spending of money
target in money management

Examples

Setting a financial goal can help you stay focused on saving for a new car.
Reaching your financial goals requires planning and discipline.
Her financial goal for the year is to save enough for a down payment on a house.

Synset Examples

His focus on CO$ helped him reach his financial goals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goal - an aim or desired result
