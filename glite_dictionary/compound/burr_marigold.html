burr marigold

1. NOUN

/ bɝː ˈmærɪˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / bɜː ˈmærɪˌɡəʊld / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Bidens with yellow flowers and spiny seed heads that attach to fur or clothing
plant of genus Bidens with sticky seeds

Examples

The fields were dotted with burr marigold blooming after the late summer rains.
After hiking through the meadow, her socks were covered in seeds from the burr marigold.
Gardeners often remove burr marigold to prevent its prickly seeds from spreading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beggarticks - another common name for plants of the genus Bidens
