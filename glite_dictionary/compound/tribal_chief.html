tribal chief

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪbəl tʃiːf / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.bəl tʃiːf / (BrE)
the leader or head of a tribe or clan, especially in traditional societies
chief or leader of a tribe

Examples

The tribal chief addressed the gathering with a powerful speech about unity.
During the ceremony, the tribal chief wore a headdress made of feathers and beads.
After years of service, he was chosen to succeed his father as tribal chief.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chieftain - the leader of a tribe or clan
