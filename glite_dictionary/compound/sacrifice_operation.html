sacrifice operation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsækrəˌfaɪs ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsæk.rɪ.faɪs ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an act in which a person deliberately kills or injures others while causing their own death, usually by means of an explosive device
deliberate self-killing attack on others

Examples

The terrorist group claimed responsibility for the sacrifice operation that targeted the embassy.
Intelligence agencies warned of possible sacrifice operations in the city during the festival.
He was radicalized online before carrying out the sacrifice operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suicide attack - an attack in which the perpetrator intends to kill themselves as part of the act
