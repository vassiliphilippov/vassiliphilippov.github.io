gas fitting

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡas ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a pipe, valve, meter, or similar device used to convey gas from the main supply to fixtures within a building
pipe or device for conveying gas in buildings

Examples

A licensed technician inspected every gas fitting in the old house for safety.
The plumber replaced a worn gas fitting to prevent leaks in the kitchen.
Building codes require all gas fittings to meet strict safety standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe - a tube for conveying gas, water, etc.
  • Synonym: valve - a device for controlling the flow of gas or liquid
