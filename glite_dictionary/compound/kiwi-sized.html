kiwi-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkiːwi saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkiːwi saɪzd / (BrE)
approximately the same size as a kiwi fruit
about the size of a kiwi fruit

Examples

The doctor found a kiwi-sized lump on his shoulder.
She handed me a kiwi-sized stone she found at the beach.
The new speaker is small, about kiwi-sized, but has powerful sound.
