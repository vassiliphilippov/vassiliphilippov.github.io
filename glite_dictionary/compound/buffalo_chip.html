buffalo chip

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌfəloʊ tʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ tʃɪp / (BrE)
a piece of dried dung from a buffalo, often used as fuel.
dried buffalo dung used as fuel

Examples

Early settlers collected buffalo chips to use as cooking fuel on the plains.
A pile of buffalo chips lay near the campfire, ready to be burned.
The children found a large buffalo chip during their hike through the prairie.
