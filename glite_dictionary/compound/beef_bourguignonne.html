beef Bourguignonne

1. NOUN

/ bif bʊr-gi-ˈnjɔn / (AmE), / biːf bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
A French dish of beef, mushrooms, and onions stewed in red wine and seasonings.
beef stewed in red wine with mushrooms and onions

Examples

The restaurant's signature dish was beef Bourguignonne, rich with red wine and tender onions.
She decided to prepare beef Bourguignonne for the special family dinner on Sunday.
At the cooking class, they learned how to make traditional beef Bourguignonne from scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beef Stroganoff - a dish of beef sautéed and served in a sauce with sour cream

Etymology

From French 'Boeuf Bourguignon', meaning 'Burgundian beef'; named after the Burgundy region of France.
