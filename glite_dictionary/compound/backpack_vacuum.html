backpack vacuum

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌpæk ˈvæk.ju.əm / (AmE), / ˈbæk.pæk ˈvæk.juː.əm / (BrE)
a vacuum cleaner consisting of a small canister strapped onto the user's back, typically used for commercial cleaning
vacuum cleaner worn on the back

Examples

The janitor used a backpack vacuum to clean the carpeted hallways quickly and efficiently.
Many commercial cleaners prefer the backpack vacuum for its portability and ease of use in large spaces.
Before starting her shift, she checked the filters on her backpack vacuum as part of her routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canister vacuum - a vacuum cleaner with a separate canister for collecting dirt and debris
