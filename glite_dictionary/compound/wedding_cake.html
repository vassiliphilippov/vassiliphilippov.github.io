wedding cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ keɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ keɪk / (BrE)
a rich, often multi-tiered cake decorated with icing and served at a wedding reception
ornately decorated cake for weddings

Examples

They spent hours choosing the design for their wedding cake.
The wedding cake was elegantly decorated with white roses and silver ribbons.
Guests eagerly awaited the moment when the couple would cut the wedding cake.
