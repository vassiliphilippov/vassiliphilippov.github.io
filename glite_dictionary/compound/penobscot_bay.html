Penobscot Bay

1. PROPN

/ pəˈnɑbˌskɑt beɪ / (AmE), / pəˈnɒbˌskɒt beɪ / (BrE)
a large inlet of the Atlantic Ocean located on the coast of eastern Maine, United States
bay in eastern Maine, USA

Examples

The schooners sailed across Penobscot Bay at sunrise, their sails glowing in the early light.
Our family vacation included kayaking along the beautiful coastline of Penobscot Bay.
Many artists have painted scenes inspired by the rugged shores of Penobscot Bay.

Etymology

Named after the Penobscot Native American tribe inhabiting the region.
