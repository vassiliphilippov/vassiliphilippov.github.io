do-it-yourself

1. ADJ

/ ˌduː ɪt jɚˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˌduː ɪt jəˈsɛlf / (BrE)
made, built, or done by oneself rather than by a professional; relating to the practice of building, repairing, or decorating things oneself.
done or made by oneself, not a professional

Examples

They bought a do-it-yourself kit to build their own bookshelves.
The rise of do-it-yourself home improvement has created a huge new market for tools.
He prefers do-it-yourself repairs rather than hiring a plumber.

Synset Examples

their house was a do-it-yourself project
he opened a do-it-yourself store

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DIY - abbreviation for do-it-yourself; the activity of doing DIY

Etymology

Originated in the mid-20th century from the phrase 'do it yourself,' reflecting the postwar DIY movement.
