funny money

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˌmʌni / (BrE)
currency or payment that is counterfeit, not genuine, or not accepted as real money
counterfeit or worthless currency
informal

Examples

After realizing the bills were funny money, the cashier immediately called the police.
Some tourists tried to pay with funny money that the shop refused to accept.
He got scammed when someone paid him in funny money for his old laptop.

Synset Examples

They offered him funny money that wasn't worth anything.
His check was just funny money, totally worthless.
She wouldn't accept that funny money from the street vendor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterfeit money - money made in imitation to deceive; fake currency
  • Antonym: legal tender - money that must be accepted if offered in payment of a debt
