yellow berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ ˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
the fruit of certain buckthorn plants that is used to produce dyes or pigments
buckthorn fruit used as a dye

Examples

Some traditional textiles are colored using dye extracted from the yellow berry.
The yellow berry of the buckthorn produces a distinctive yellow pigment.
Botanists studied the properties of the yellow berry to improve natural dyeing techniques.
