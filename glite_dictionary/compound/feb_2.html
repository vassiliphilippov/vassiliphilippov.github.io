Feb 2

1. NOUN

/ fɛb səˈkənd / (AmE), / fɛb səˈkənd / (BrE)
February 2; the Christian feast day commemorating the presentation of Christ in the temple, also a quarter day in Scotland.
February 2; Christian feast and Scottish quarter day

Examples

In some churches, Feb 2 is celebrated as Candlemas, marking the presentation of Christ.
Rent payments were traditionally due on Feb 2 in parts of Scotland.
Every year, the congregation gathers on Feb 2 to observe this important feast day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Candlemas - Christian feast observed on February 2
