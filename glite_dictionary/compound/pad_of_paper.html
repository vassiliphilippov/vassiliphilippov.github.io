pad of paper

1. NOUN

/ pæd əv ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / pæd əv ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a set of sheets of paper joined together along one edge, used for writing or drawing
sheets of paper bound together

Examples

She tore a sheet from her pad of paper to jot down the message.
Every student was asked to bring a pad of paper to class.
He bought a new pad of paper for his sketches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notepad - small book of blank paper for writing notes
