Ebro River

1. PROPN

/ ˈeɪ.broʊ ˈrɪv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈiː.brəʊ ˈrɪv.ə / (BrE)
a major river in northeastern Spain that flows into the Mediterranean Sea
river in northeastern Spain

Examples

The Ebro River is one of the longest rivers in Spain, known for its rich history and scenic beauty.
The city of Zaragoza is located on the banks of the Ebro River.
Many species of birds can be seen near the delta of the Ebro River.
