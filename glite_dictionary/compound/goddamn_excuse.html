goddamn excuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdˌdæm ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌdæm ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
An excuse regarded as especially frustrating, worthless, or unacceptable, often used to express strong irritation or anger.
worthless or infuriating excuse
vulgar

Examples

I’m tired of hearing the same old goddamn excuse every time something goes wrong.
She stormed out, calling his apology just another goddamn excuse.
Don’t give me a goddamn excuse—tell me what really happened.

Synset Examples

He’s always making goddamned excuses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excuse - a reason or explanation put forward to defend or justify a fault or offense
