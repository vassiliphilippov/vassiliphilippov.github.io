tired of

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪɚd ʌv / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəd ɒv / (BrE)
bored or annoyed by something, especially because it has been happening for too long or too often; having had enough of something.
bored or annoyed by too much of something

Examples

She was tired of answering the same questions every single day.
After years in the same job, he became tired of the daily routine.
I'm tired of watching the same movie over and over again.

Synset Examples

grew more and more disgusted
fed up with their complaints
sick of it all
sick to death of flattery
gossip that makes one sick
tired of the noise and smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fed up with - annoyed or bored with something that has happened too much
  • Synonym: sick of - having a strong distaste from surfeit; thoroughly bored or annoyed
