change of magnitude

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪndʒ əv ˈmæɡnɪˌtjud / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ əv ˈmæɡnɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a change in the amount or size of something; the process or result of becoming larger, smaller, or otherwise different in scale
change in amount or size

Examples

The sudden change of magnitude in the river's flow surprised local engineers.
Climate scientists study the change of magnitude in global temperatures over the past century.
Any change of magnitude in the company's profits will be reported to shareholders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: change - the act or process of becoming different
