benefit album

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛnɪfɪt ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪfɪt ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
An album released to raise funds or awareness for a charitable cause, often featuring various artists.
album made for a charitable cause

Examples

The band released a benefit album to raise money for disaster relief efforts.
Several famous musicians collaborated on a benefit album supporting cancer research.
Purchasing the benefit album helps support local charities in your community.
