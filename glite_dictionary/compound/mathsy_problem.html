mathsy problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæθsi ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / ˈmæθsi ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
an informal term for a problem involving or resembling the type of thinking required in mathematics
problem requiring mathematical thinking
informal british

Examples

Whenever I see a mathsy problem, I can't resist trying to solve it.
The teacher gave us a particularly tricky mathsy problem for homework this week.
Some students enjoy the challenge of a good mathsy problem more than standard exercises.

Synset Examples

I find mathsy problems challenging yet rewarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: math problem - a problem that can be solved using mathematics

Etymology

From 'mathsy', an informal derivative of 'maths' (British English for 'mathematics'), + 'problem'.
