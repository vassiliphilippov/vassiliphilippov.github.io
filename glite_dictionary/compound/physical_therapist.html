physical therapist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
a healthcare professional who treats injury or physical dysfunction through exercises, manual therapy, and other physical methods
health professional treating injuries by physical methods
medical

Examples

After her surgery, she saw a physical therapist twice a week to regain strength in her leg.
The physical therapist developed a custom exercise plan to help his patient recover.
You may need a referral from your doctor to visit a physical therapist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiotherapist - a healthcare professional who treats injury or dysfunction with physical methods

Etymology

From 'physical' (relating to the body) and 'therapist' (person trained to treat illness or disability).
