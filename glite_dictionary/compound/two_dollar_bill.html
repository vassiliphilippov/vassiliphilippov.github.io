two dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ tuː ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / tuː ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A United States paper currency note with a value of two dollars.
US bill worth 2 dollars
american

Examples

I was surprised to receive a two dollar bill as change at the store.
Some collectors find the two dollar bill to be a rare and interesting item.
She tucked a crisp two dollar bill into the birthday card for her nephew.
