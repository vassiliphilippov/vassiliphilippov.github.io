tea ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ti ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal cultural ritual involving the preparation, serving, and drinking of tea, especially in Japan or China
formal ritual of preparing and serving tea

Examples

He studied the tea ceremony to better understand Japanese culture.
The tea ceremony requires precision and mindfulness from the host.
Tourists were fascinated by the traditional tea ceremony demonstration.
