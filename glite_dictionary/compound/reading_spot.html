reading spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ spɑt / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ spɒt / (BrE)
a place that is specially chosen or arranged for reading
place set aside for reading

Examples

She curled up in her favorite reading spot by the window every afternoon.
The library created a special reading spot for children with colorful cushions and soft lighting.
It took weeks to find the perfect reading spot in the new city park.

Synset Examples

The smallest room in the house became a cozy reading spot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reading nook - a small, comfortable corner or space designed for reading
