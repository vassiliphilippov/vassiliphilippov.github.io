paytriot movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪtriət muːv.mənt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtriət ˈmuːv.mənt / (BrE)
A movement or campaign advocating for Americans to support the country by buying American-made products.
campaign promoting buying American-made goods

Examples

The paytriot movement has gained traction among people seeking to support local businesses.
Advocates of the paytriot movement argue that buying American-made products strengthens the economy.
Some criticize the paytriot movement as being more symbolic than effective.

Synset Examples

The paytriot movement encourages buying American-made products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buy American - encouragement or policy of purchasing American-made goods

Etymology

Blend of 'pay' and 'patriot', formed in imitation of 'patriot'.
