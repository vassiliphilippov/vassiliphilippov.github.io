racial tension

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪʃəl ˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl ˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
A state of hostility, conflict, or strained relations between people or groups of different races.
hostility or conflict between races

Examples

The city experienced increased racial tension after the controversial court verdict.
Efforts to reduce racial tension include community dialogues and cultural exchange programs.
During that time period, racial tension was at its peak in many urban areas.

Synset Examples

Using the phrase white nigger highlights racial tensions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racial conflict - conflict between groups based on race
  • Antonym: harmony - peaceful relations without conflict
