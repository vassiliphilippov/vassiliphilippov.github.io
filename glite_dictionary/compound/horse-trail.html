horse-trail

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs treɪl / (AmE), / hɔːs treɪl / (BrE)
A trail, path, or track designated for the use of people riding horses.
trail for people riding horses

Examples

The horse-trail winds through the forest and offers beautiful views of the mountains.
Riders are asked to remain on the marked horse-trail to protect the natural habitat.
It's important to check if a horse-trail is open before planning your ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bridle path - a path or trail for horses
