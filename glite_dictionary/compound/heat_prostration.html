heat prostration

1. NOUN

/ hiːt prɑːˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / hiːt prɒˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by dizziness, nausea, and weakness due to loss of body fluids and electrolytes from exposure to heat; also known as heat exhaustion.
illness caused by fluid loss from heat
medical

Examples

After working outside all day in the sun, the worker collapsed from heat prostration.
Symptoms of heat prostration include dizziness, nausea, and excessive sweating.
To prevent heat prostration, drink plenty of water and avoid strenuous activity in high temperatures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heat exhaustion - A condition with similar symptoms caused by exposure to high temperatures; synonym for heat prostration
