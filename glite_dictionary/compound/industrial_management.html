industrial management

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈmanɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the branch of engineering concerned with planning, organizing, and overseeing the integration of people, materials, and energy in productive processes
engineering branch for managing industry systems

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in industrial management after graduating from university.
The conference focused on new trends in industrial management and automation.
Effective industrial management can greatly improve productivity and efficiency in manufacturing plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrial engineering - the field applying engineering principles to industrial systems and processes
