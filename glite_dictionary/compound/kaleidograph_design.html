kaleidograph design

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlaɪdəˌɡræf dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / kəˈlaɪdəˌɡrɑːf dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
an artistic pattern or composition created using visual effects resembling those of a kaleidoscope
artistic pattern with kaleidoscope effect

Examples

The gallery featured several kaleidograph designs inspired by traditional mandalas.
She used digital tools to create a striking kaleidograph design for the album cover.
Children enjoyed making their own kaleidograph designs during the workshop.

Synset Examples

Her kaleidograph designs mesmerized everyone at the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kaleidoscopic - having complex patterns or colors like those seen in a kaleidoscope

Etymology

From 'kaleidoscope', from Greek kalos (beautiful) + eidos (form), and 'graph', from Greek graphein (to write or draw).
