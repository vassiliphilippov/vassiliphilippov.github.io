pork sausage

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk ˈsɔsɪdʒ / (AmE), / pɔːk ˈsɔːsɪdʒ / (BrE)
A sausage made primarily from pork, often seasoned and encased in a skin.
sausage made from pork

Examples

He enjoyed a hearty breakfast of eggs and pork sausage every morning.
The butcher makes his own pork sausage using traditional recipes.
They served grilled pork sausage with mustard and sauerkraut at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sausage - A food made from ground meat in a casing
