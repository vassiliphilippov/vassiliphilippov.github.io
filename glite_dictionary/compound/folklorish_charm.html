folklorish charm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊk.lɔːr.ɪʃ tʃɑrm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊk.lər.ɪʃ tʃɑːm / (BrE)
a quaint or traditional appeal reminiscent of folk culture or traditions
quaint, traditional appeal

Examples

The village inn retained a folklorish charm that made every guest feel at home.
Her handmade decorations added a folklorish charm to the holiday celebration.
The town square had a folklorish charm, with its wooden stalls and traditional music.

Synset Examples

The festival had a folklorish charm to it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk charm - an appeal or quality characteristic of folk culture
  • Synonym: old-world charm - appeal stemming from traditional or old-fashioned qualities
