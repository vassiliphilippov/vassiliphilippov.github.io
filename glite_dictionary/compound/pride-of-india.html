pride-of-India

1. NOUN

/ ˌpraɪd əv ˈɪndiə / (AmE), / ˌpraɪd əv ˈɪndɪə / (BrE)

2. NOUN

/ ˌpraɪd əv ˈɪndiə / (AmE), / ˌpraɪd əv ˈɪndiə / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to northern India and China, known for its purple blossoms and small inedible yellow fruits; also cultivated in the southern United States as a shade tree.
tree with purple blossoms, native to India/China

Examples

The pride-of-India is admired for its vibrant purple blossoms each spring.
Gardeners in the southern US often plant pride-of-India trees for their dense shade and ornamental value.
The yellow fruits of the pride-of-India tree are small and not suitable for eating.
