band player

1. NOUN

/ bænd ˈpleɪ.ər / (AmE), / bænd ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who plays a musical instrument in a band, especially in a military band.
member of a band; especially in a military band

Examples

Ever since he was a child, he dreamed of becoming a band player in the army.
The band player tuned her trumpet before the big parade.
Every band player must attend rehearsals twice a week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument, especially professionally
  • Degree Higher: bandmaster - the leader or conductor of a band
