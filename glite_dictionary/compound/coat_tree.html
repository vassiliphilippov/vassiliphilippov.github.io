coat tree

1. NOUN

/ koʊt triː / (AmE), / kəʊt triː / (BrE)
a tall upright stand with hooks or pegs for hanging coats, hats, or other clothing
upright stand for hanging coats

Examples

She hung her jacket on the coat tree by the door.
The hallway was cluttered with umbrellas and a tall coat tree.
He bought a wooden coat tree for his new office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat stand - a stand for hanging hats or coats
