installment debt

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstɑlmənt dɛt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɔːlmənt dɛt / (BrE)
A debt that is repaid in regular, fixed payments over a set period of time.
debt repaid in fixed installments

Examples

Many people have installment debt in the form of car loans or personal loans.
Paying off installment debt consistently can help improve your credit score.
She reduced her installment debt by making extra payments each month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: installment loan - a loan repaid in regular, fixed payments
