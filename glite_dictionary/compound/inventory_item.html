inventory item

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnvənˌtɔri ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ˈɪnvəntrɪ ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an individual object or product that is recorded as part of an inventory
an item listed in an inventory

Examples

Each inventory item is checked and logged in the system every week.
Before the audit, make sure every inventory item has a barcode label attached.
The company had to write off several obsolete inventory items last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock - goods or merchandise kept on the premises of a business or warehouse
