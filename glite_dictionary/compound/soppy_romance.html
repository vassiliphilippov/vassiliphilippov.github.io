soppy romance

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑpi roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈsɒpi rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
A love story that is excessively sentimental or emotional, often to the point of being considered cheesy or lacking in depth.
overly sentimental love story

Examples

She couldn't get through another soppy romance without rolling her eyes once or twice.
The movie turned out to be just a soppy romance, not the gripping drama I had hoped for.
He usually avoids soppy romance novels and prefers thrillers instead.

Synset Examples

He couldn't handle the soppy romance in the book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental - having or arousing excessive, emotional feelings
  • Synonym: cheesy - lacking subtlety, cliché, or taste; overly sentimental
