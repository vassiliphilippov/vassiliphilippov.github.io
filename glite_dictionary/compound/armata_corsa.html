Armata Corsa

1. PROPN

/ ɑrˈmɑːtə ˈkɔrsə / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɑːtə ˈkɔːsə / (BrE)
a Corsican terrorist organization founded in 1999 that opposed links between Corsican nationalists and organized crime
Corsican terrorist group founded in 1999
