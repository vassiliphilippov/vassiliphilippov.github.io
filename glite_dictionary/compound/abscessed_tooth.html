abscessed tooth

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbˌsɛst tuθ / (AmE), / ˈabsɛst tuːθ / (BrE)
A tooth surrounded by tissue in which an abscess has formed, usually causing pain and swelling.
tooth with an abscess
medical

Examples

She went to the dentist because her abscessed tooth was causing severe pain.
Antibiotics may be prescribed to treat an abscessed tooth before further dental procedures.
An abscessed tooth can lead to swelling in the face if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothache - pain in or around a tooth
  • Degree Lower: cavity - a decayed area of a tooth
