sea king

1. NOUN

/ si kɪŋ / (AmE), / siː kɪŋ / (BrE)
a male Viking pirate chief, especially as referred to in history or legend
Viking pirate chief

Examples

The legendary sea king led countless raids along the European coast.
Many Icelandic sagas feature a sea king as the hero of the story.
Historians believe the title of sea king was both respected and feared among Vikings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pirate - a person who attacks and robs ships at sea
