pork and beans

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk ənd binz / (AmE), / pɔːk ənd biːnz / (BrE)
a dish consisting of dried beans cooked with pork and tomato sauce, often sold in cans
beans cooked with pork and tomato sauce

Examples

My grandmother used to serve pork and beans with homemade bread for dinner.
We brought several cans of pork and beans on our camping trip.
He opened a can of pork and beans for a quick and easy lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baked beans - beans cooked slowly in sauce, commonly with molasses or pork
