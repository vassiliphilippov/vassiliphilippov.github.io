one-quarter

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈkwɔrtɚ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
One of four equal parts of something; a quarter.
one of four equal parts

Examples

I ate one-quarter of the pizza for lunch.
The recipe calls for one-quarter cup of sugar.
One-quarter of the students were absent today.
