piece of ground

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / piːs əv ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a portion or area of land, especially one with defined boundaries or considered for a specific use
an area or portion of land

Examples

The developer purchased a piece of ground outside the city for the new housing project.
She inherited a small piece of ground from her grandfather, which she turned into a garden.
We walked across a vacant piece of ground that will soon become a park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot - a small area of land marked out for a specific purpose
  • Synonym: parcel - a defined piece of land, especially for sale or development
