lemon heaven

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
a delicious lemon-flavored dessert, or an extremely enjoyable experience for those who love lemons
delightful lemon-flavored dessert or experience

Examples

The lemon tart was pure lemon heaven from the very first bite.
If you love citrus, this pie is absolute lemon heaven.
The bakery's new pastry offers a slice of lemon heaven to every customer.

Synset Examples
