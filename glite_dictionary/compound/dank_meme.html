dank meme

1. NOUN

/ dæŋk miːm / (AmE), / daŋk miːm / (BrE)
an internet meme that is considered highly amusing, absurd, or of especially good quality within online communities
highly amusing or absurd internet meme
slang

Examples

That video is a classic dank meme everyone shares.
He spends hours online searching for the funniest dank meme to share with his friends.
Social media feeds are often filled with the latest dank memes after a big event.

Synset Examples

That video is a classic dank meme everyone shares.
His post was filled with dank memes from the internet.
She loves sending me the latest dank memes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - a humorous image, video, or text that is copied and spread rapidly by Internet users

Etymology

From 'dank' (slang meaning excellent or high quality) and 'meme' (from Greek 'mimema', meaning 'imitated').
