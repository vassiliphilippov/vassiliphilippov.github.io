carpet loom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pət luːm / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt luːm / (BrE)
a loom that is specifically designed for weaving carpeting.
loom for weaving carpeting

Examples

The factory installed a new carpet loom to increase its production capacity.
Traditional artisans used a hand-operated carpet loom to create intricate rugs.
Learning how to operate a carpet loom requires both skill and patience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loom - a machine or device for weaving threads or yarns to produce textiles
