chef's specialty

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛfs ˈspɛʃəlti / (AmE), / ʃɛfs ˈspɛʃəlti / (BrE)
a dish that a chef is particularly skilled at making or is especially known for
signature dish of a chef

Examples

The restaurant's most popular dish is the chef's specialty, a spicy seafood paella.
If you're unsure what to order, try the chef's specialty for a true taste of the kitchen's best.
The menu highlights the chef's specialty, which changes with the seasons.

Synset Examples

The chef's specialty was a creamy pock-pudding with berries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signature dish - a dish strongly associated with a particular chef or restaurant
