mechanized cavalry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛk.əˌnaɪzd ˈkæv.əl.ri / (AmE), / ˈmɛk.ə.naɪzd ˈkæv.əl.ri / (BrE)
an armored military unit equipped with motor vehicles instead of horses
armored military unit with motor vehicles

Examples

The division's mechanized cavalry was deployed at the front to scout enemy positions.
Many armies replaced horse-mounted units with mechanized cavalry during the twentieth century.
On the battlefield, mechanized cavalry provided both mobility and protection against attacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored cavalry - military units using armored fighting vehicles

Etymology

From 'mechanized', relating to machines, and 'cavalry', from French 'cavalerie' (mounted troops).
