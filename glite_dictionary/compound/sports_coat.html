sports coat

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌkəʊt / (BrE)
a lightweight, single-breasted jacket, often worn for informal occasions, sometimes featuring stripes in club or school colors
lightweight, informal men's jacket

Examples

He wore a navy sports coat and grey slacks to the meeting.
The school's official sports coat is striped with blue and gold.
A sports coat is perfect for smart-casual occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazer - single-breasted formal jacket, often with insignia, similar to a sports coat
