music teacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk ˌtitʃər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is teaching music to students
person who teaches music

Examples

Ms. Parker is the new music teacher at our school this year.
The music teacher organized a concert for the students and their families.
As a music teacher, he helps children discover their passion for playing instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
