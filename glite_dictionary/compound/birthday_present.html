birthday present

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ ˈprɛz.ənt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ ˈprɛz.ənt / (BrE)
a gift given to someone in celebration of their birthday
gift given for a birthday

Examples

She received a beautiful birthday present from her parents.
I wrapped the birthday present with colorful paper and ribbons.
He was thrilled when he opened his birthday present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift - something given willingly to someone without payment; a present
