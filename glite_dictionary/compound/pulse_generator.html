pulse generator

1. NOUN

/ pʌls ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtɚ / (AmE), / pʌls ˈdʒɛnəreɪtə / (BrE)
a device that produces electrical pulses, typically used in testing, measurement, or signal processing applications, with adjustable pulse rate and duration
device producing electrical pulses

Examples

The pulse generator allows engineers to simulate digital signals for circuit testing.
A malfunction in the pulse generator caused erratic readings on the oscilloscope.
Modern pulse generators feature precise controls for pulse width and frequency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal generator - device that generates electronic signals for testing or measurement
