month by month

1. ADV

/ mʌnθ baɪ mʌnθ / (AmE), / mʌnθ baɪ mʌnθ / (BrE)
in a way that occurs or changes each month; from one month to the next
each month; monthly progression

Examples

The project advanced month by month, reaching new milestones each time.
His health gradually improved month by month after the operation.
Profits increased month by month throughout the fiscal year.

Synset Examples

month by month, the betrayal gnawed at his heart
