to acting shitnuts

1. VERB

/ ˈæktɪŋ ˈʃɪtnʌts / (AmE), / ˈæktɪŋ ˈʃɪtnʌts / (BrE)
behaving in an extremely crazy or irrational manner
behaving wildly or irrationally
informal slang vulgar

Examples

After drinking too much coffee, he started acting shitnuts at the office.
Everyone thought she was acting shitnuts when she danced on the table.
Stop acting shitnuts and try to focus for a minute.

Synset Examples

She was acting shitnuts after missing the bus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acting crazy - behaving in a crazy or unpredictable way
  • Antonym: acting normal - behaving in a typical or sane manner
