fatal accident

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪtəl ˈæksɪdənt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəl ˈæksɪdənt / (BrE)
an accident that results in death
accident causing death

Examples

The highway was closed for hours after a fatal accident involving two trucks.
Investigators are working to determine the cause of the fatal accident at the construction site.
Wearing a seatbelt can often prevent a fatal accident in the event of a collision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accident - an unexpected event, often causing harm or damage
