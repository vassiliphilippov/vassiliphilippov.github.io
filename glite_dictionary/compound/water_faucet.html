water faucet

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈfɑsɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈfɔːsɪt / (BrE)
A device for controlling the flow of water from a pipe or cask.
faucet for drawing water

Examples

He turned the water faucet on to fill his glass with cold water.
A leaking water faucet can waste a surprising amount of water over time.
Please make sure to shut off the water faucet before leaving the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap - a device by which a flow of liquid or gas from a pipe or container can be controlled
