pastry board

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪstri bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstri bɔːd / (BrE)
a large flat board, usually made of wood, used as a surface for kneading dough or rolling out pastry
board for kneading dough or pastry

Examples

She dusted the pastry board with flour before rolling out the dough.
A wooden pastry board helps keep the dough cool and dry while baking.
After baking, he wiped the pastry board clean and put it away.

Synset Examples

When it comes to making pizza dough, a good wooden kneading board makes a big difference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kneading board - a board used for kneading dough
