quick comeback

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk ˈkʌmbæk / (AmE), / kwɪk ˈkʌmbæk / (BrE)
a rapid, often witty reply, especially given in response to a remark or insult
rapid or witty reply

Examples

She always has a quick comeback when someone tries to tease her.
His quick comeback left the entire room laughing.
During the debate, her quick comeback completely disarmed her opponent.

Synset Examples

The buster was known for his quick comebacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - a sharp, witty, or cutting reply
  • Synonym: repartee - conversation or speech characterized by quick, witty comments or replies
