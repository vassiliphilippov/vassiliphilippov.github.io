clear sign

1. NOUN

/ klɪr saɪn / (AmE), / klɪə saɪn / (BrE)
an obvious or unmistakable indication of something
obvious indication

Examples

The dark clouds were a clear sign that a storm was coming.
His improved grades were a clear sign of his hard work.
Leaving the lights on was a clear sign that someone was still in the office.

Synset Examples

The police cruiser parked outside the building was a clear sign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evidence - something that shows or proves that something else exists or is true
  • Degree Lower: hint - a slight or indirect indication or suggestion
