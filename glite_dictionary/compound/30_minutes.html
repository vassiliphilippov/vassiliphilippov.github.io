30 minutes

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜːrti ˈmɪnɪts / (AmE), / ˈθɜːti ˈmɪnɪts / (BrE)
A period of time equal to half an hour.
half an hour

Examples

The meeting will last 30 minutes and then we'll take a break.
Please wait 30 minutes before swimming after eating.
She finished the exam in just 30 minutes.
