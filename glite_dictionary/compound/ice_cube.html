ice cube

1. NOUN

/ aɪs kjuːb / (AmE), / aɪs kjuːb / (BrE)
A small, typically square or rectangular piece of ice made artificially and used to cool drinks.
small block of ice for cooling drinks

Examples

She dropped an ice cube into her glass of lemonade to keep it cold.
The cocktail was served with a single large ice cube that melted slowly.
Please refill the tray so we have enough ice cubes for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice - frozen water, especially as used to cool things
