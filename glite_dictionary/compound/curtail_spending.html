to curtail spending

1. VERB

/ kɚˈteɪl ˈspɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / kɜːˈteɪl ˈspɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
to reduce the amount of money that is being spent, especially in order to save or avoid debt
reduce expenses; spend less

Examples

The government announced new measures to curtail spending during the economic downturn.
Businesses are looking for ways to curtail spending without sacrificing quality.
Families often have to curtail spending when facing unexpected expenses.

Synset Examples

We need to curtail spending to save money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut back on spending - to reduce the amount of money spent
  • Antonym: increase spending - to spend more money; increase expenditures
