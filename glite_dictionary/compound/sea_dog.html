sea dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi dɔg / (AmE), / ˈsi dɒg / (BrE)
an experienced sailor, especially one who has spent many years at sea
experienced sailor

Examples

The old sea dog shared tales of his adventures in distant ports.
Only a true sea dog would know how to handle a storm like that.
The young crew admired the wisdom of the veteran sea dog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - a person whose job is to work as a member of the crew of a ship or boat
