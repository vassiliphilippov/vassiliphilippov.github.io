biting point

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a critical or intense moment, especially when emotions or stakes are at their highest
critical or intense moment

Examples

The negotiations reached a biting point when both sides refused to compromise.
During the debate, emotions hit a biting point as tempers flared.
The drama in the film builds steadily toward its biting point.

Synset Examples

At the biting point, emotions ran high.
He reached a biting point in the negotiation.
The biting point of the argument came unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crisis point - the most intense or crucial point of something
  • Synonym: turning point - a decisive change in a situation
