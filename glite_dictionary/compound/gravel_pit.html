gravel pit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrævəl pɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡrævəl pɪt / (BrE)
an open excavation or quarry from which gravel is obtained
an excavation for producing gravel

Examples

The contractor sourced much of the material from a nearby gravel pit.
Abandoned gravel pits can eventually fill with water and become small lakes.
The town opposed the creation of a new gravel pit near the residential area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarry - an open excavation for obtaining stone, slate, or minerals
