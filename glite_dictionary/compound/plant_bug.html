plant bug

1. NOUN

/ plænt bʌɡ / (AmE), / plɑːnt bʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored insect that feeds on plant juices, belonging to the family Miridae
small insect that feeds on plant juices

Examples

The tomato crop was damaged by a swarm of plant bugs feeding on the stems.
Gardeners often struggle to control plant bugs on their rose bushes.
Some species of plant bug can transmit diseases to fruit plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mirid - a bug of the family Miridae, to which plant bugs belong
