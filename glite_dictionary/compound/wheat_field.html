wheat field

1. NOUN

/ wiːt fiːld / (AmE), / wiːt fiːld / (BrE)
A field in which wheat is cultivated.
field where wheat is grown

Examples

The vast wheat field shimmered in the afternoon sun.
During harvest, dozens of workers gathered in the wheat field to collect the ripe grain.
A narrow dirt path cut through the middle of the wheat field behind the farmhouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornfield - a field in which corn is grown
