road burn

1. NOUN

/ roʊd bɝːn / (AmE), / rəʊd bɜːn / (BrE)
an abrasion of the skin caused by friction with a road surface, typically from falling or sliding on pavement
skin abrasion from contact with pavement

Examples

The cyclist had a nasty road burn on her leg after the accident.
It's important to clean a road burn thoroughly to prevent infection.
He wore knee pads to protect himself from road burn while skateboarding.

Synset Examples

She got road burn after falling off her bike.
Road burn is a common injury for skateboarders.
He treated his road burn with antiseptic ointment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abrasion - an area damaged by scraping or wearing away
  • Synonym: graze - a minor scrape on the skin
