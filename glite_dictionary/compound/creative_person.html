creative person

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪt̬ɪv ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose work demonstrates imagination, originality, and sensitivity
a person known for creativity and imagination

Examples

As a creative person, she always finds new ways to solve problems.
The company is seeking a creative person to lead its marketing team.
Being a creative person can mean seeing opportunities where others see obstacles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially as a profession
