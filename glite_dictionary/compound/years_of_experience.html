years of experience

1. NOUN

/ jɪrz əv ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / jɪəz əv ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
the length of time someone has spent gaining knowledge or skills in a particular field or activity
length of time spent gaining knowledge or skills

Examples

Her years of experience in teaching made her a valuable mentor to new staff.
He drew on his years of experience when tackling the complex project.
They hired her because of her years of experience in the finance sector.

Synset Examples

The hoary old professor shared wisdom from his years of experience.
