Denali National Park

1. PROPN

/ dəˈnɑːli ˌnæʃəˈnæl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / dəˈnɑːli ˌnæʃəˈnal ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a large national park in Alaska renowned for its dramatic peaks, including Mount Denali (formerly Mount McKinley), and extensive wilderness areas
national park in Alaska, home to Denali

Examples

Hiking in Denali National Park offers breathtaking views of rugged mountains and vast tundra.
Wildlife such as grizzly bears and moose can often be spotted in Denali National Park.
Every summer, thousands of tourists travel to Denali National Park to experience its natural beauty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yellowstone National Park - a national park in Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho, USA
  • Synonym: Yosemite National Park - a national park in California, USA

Etymology

"Denali" is from the Koyukon language, meaning 'the high one'; "National Park" from English.
