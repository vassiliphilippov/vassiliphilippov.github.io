property master

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpərti ˈmæstər / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəti ˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
a member of the stage or film crew responsible for acquiring, organizing, and maintaining props used in a production
person in charge of props in theater or film

Examples

After rehearsals, the property master carefully checked each prop to ensure nothing was missing.
The property master worked closely with the director to create an authentic period setting.
A good property master must be organized and creative to handle the demands of live theater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prop master - member of the crew responsible for props
