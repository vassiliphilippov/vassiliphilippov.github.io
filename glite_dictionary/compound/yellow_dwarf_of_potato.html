yellow dwarf of potato

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ dɔrf əv pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ dɒːf əv pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A viral disease of potato plants characterized by stunted growth and yellowing of the leaves.
potato plant viral disease

Examples

The yellow dwarf of potato can severely reduce crop yields if not managed properly.
Farmers are advised to use disease-free seed potatoes to prevent yellow dwarf of potato from spreading.
Symptoms of yellow dwarf of potato include yellowing leaves and stunted plant growth.
