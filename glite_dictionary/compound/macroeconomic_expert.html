macroeconomic expert

1. NOUN

/ ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪk ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
an economist who specializes in macroeconomics, focusing on large-scale economic factors and trends
specialist in macroeconomics

Examples

The government consulted a macroeconomic expert before launching the new fiscal policy.
As a macroeconomic expert, she provides valuable insight into national economic trends.
Many conferences invite at least one macroeconomic expert to discuss inflation and employment rates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: macroeconomist - a person specializing in macroeconomics
