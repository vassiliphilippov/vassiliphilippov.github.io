raspberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈræzˌbɛri lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈrɑːzbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a sweet alcoholic beverage flavored with raspberries or raspberry juice
a sweet liqueur flavored with raspberries

Examples

She added a splash of raspberry liqueur to her cocktail for a fruity twist.
The dessert was drizzled with raspberry liqueur to enhance its flavor.
He brought back a bottle of French raspberry liqueur as a souvenir from his trip.

Synset Examples

Homemade raspberry liqueur is a good remedy for common cold.
