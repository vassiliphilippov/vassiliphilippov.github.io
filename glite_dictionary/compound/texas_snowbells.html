Texas snowbells

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksəs ˈsnoʊˌbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˈsnəʊˌbɛlz / (BrE)
A rare species of styrax native to the southwestern United States, recognized as threatened.
rare threatened styrax plant

Examples

The Texas snowbells are in bloom along the riverbanks every spring.
Conservationists are making efforts to protect the endangered Texas snowbells.
We spotted several Texas snowbells during our hike in southwest Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Styrax - a genus of flowering plants including the snowbells
