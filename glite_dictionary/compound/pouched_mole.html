pouched mole

1. NOUN

/ paʊtʃt moʊl / (AmE), / paʊtʃt məʊl / (BrE)
a small burrowing marsupial of Australia that resembles a mole in appearance and habits
small burrowing Australian marsupial

Examples

The pouched mole spends most of its life underground in the sandy deserts of Australia.
Although it looks like a true mole, the pouched mole is actually a marsupial with a pouch for its young.
Researchers observed a pouched mole emerging briefly at dusk before digging a new tunnel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marsupial mole - an alternative name for the pouched mole, a marsupial resembling a mole
