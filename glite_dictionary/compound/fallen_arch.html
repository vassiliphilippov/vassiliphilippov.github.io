fallen arch

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑlən ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɒlən ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
A condition in which the arch of the foot is abnormally flattened, causing the entire sole to touch the ground when standing.
flattened arch of the foot
medical

Examples

She visited a specialist because her fallen arch was causing persistent pain in her foot.
After years of running on hard surfaces, he developed a fallen arch in his left foot.
People with a fallen arch may need special insoles to provide adequate support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flat foot - a foot with a flattened arch
