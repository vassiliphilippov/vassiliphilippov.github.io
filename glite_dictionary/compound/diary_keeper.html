diary keeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪəri ˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəri ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who regularly writes in a diary or journal, recording personal experiences or thoughts
person who keeps a diary or journal

Examples

As a dedicated diary keeper, she wrote about her daily life every night before bed.
Many historians value the insights provided by the writings of a diary keeper from the past.
He became a diary keeper to help process his feelings during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarist - a person who writes a diary
