online propaganda campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔnˌlaɪn ˌprɑpəˈɡændə kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn ˌprɒpəˈɡændə kamˈpeɪn / (BrE)
a coordinated effort to influence public opinion or behavior by spreading information, ideas, or rumors through the internet
strategy to influence opinion via the internet

Examples

The government allegedly orchestrated an online propaganda campaign to sway the outcome of the election.
Researchers exposed an elaborate online propaganda campaign targeting social media users with disinformation.
Many people are unaware when they are being influenced by a coordinated online propaganda campaign.

Synset Examples

Kremlebots are often used in online propaganda campaigns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propaganda - information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote a cause
