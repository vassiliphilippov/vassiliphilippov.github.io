day off

1. NOUN

/ deɪ ɔf / (AmE), / deɪ ɒf / (BrE)
A day on which one is not required to work.
a day not spent working

Examples

She requested a day off next week to attend her sister's wedding.
It's important to take a day off occasionally to recharge and relax.
My day off always falls on a Thursday, so I usually run errands then.

Synset Examples

Thursday is his day off

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holiday - a day of festivity or recreation when no work is done
  • Antonym: workday - a day on which work is usually done
