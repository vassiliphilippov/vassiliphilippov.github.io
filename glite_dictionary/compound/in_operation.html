in operation

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪn ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
functioning or actively working; currently in effect or use
functioning or in effect

Examples

The factory has been in operation for more than a hundred years.
Several new policies are now in operation at the university.
Once the machines are in operation, the production line will be much faster.

Synset Examples

de facto apartheid is still operational even in the ‘new’ African nations
bus service is in operation during the emergency
the company had several operating divisions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operational - working or ready for use
  • Antonym: out of operation - not functioning or active
