Twin Falls

1. PROPN

/ twɪn fɔlz / (AmE), / twɪn fɔːlz / (BrE)
A waterfall in the Snake River in southern Idaho, United States, consisting of two major drops.
waterfall in southern Idaho

Examples

The spectacular Twin Falls attract thousands of visitors each year to southern Idaho.
We took a boat tour on the Snake River to get a closer look at Twin Falls.
The power station near Twin Falls supplies electricity to the surrounding region.
