man of affairs

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn əv əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / ˈmæn əv əˈfeəz / (BrE)
a man who is actively involved in commercial or industrial business
man engaged in business

Examples

After years abroad, he returned home as a respected man of affairs.
The mayor was known as a man of affairs, with interests spanning several industries.
As a young man of affairs, he quickly made a name for himself in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: businessman - a man involved in business
