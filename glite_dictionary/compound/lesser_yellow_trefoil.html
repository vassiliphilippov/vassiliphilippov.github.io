lesser yellow trefoil

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈjɛloʊ ˈtriːfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈlesə ˈjɛləʊ ˈtriːfɔɪl / (BrE)
A clover native to Ireland with yellowish flowers; often regarded as the true or original shamrock.
Irish clover with yellow flowers

Examples

Some botanists believe the lesser yellow trefoil is the true Irish shamrock.
The lesser yellow trefoil blooms with clusters of yellow flowers each spring.
You can spot lesser yellow trefoil in meadows throughout Ireland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamrock - a clover-like plant, symbol of Ireland
