snap brim

1. NOUN

/ snæp brɪm / (AmE), / snæp brɪm / (BrE)
a hat brim that can be turned up on one side and down on the other, typically found on fedoras and similar hats
a hat brim that can be turned up or down

Examples

His fedora featured a stylish snap brim that he angled just above his eyes.
The snap brim adds versatility, allowing you to adjust the hat's look for different occasions.
Many classic movie stars favored hats with a snap brim for their distinctive profile.
