answering machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.sɚ.ɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an electronic device that answers telephone calls and records messages from callers when no one is available to answer
device that records phone messages

Examples

Please leave a message after the beep on the answering machine.
The office answering machine was full by Monday morning.
I bought a new answering machine to make sure I never miss important calls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voicemail - a system for electronically storing telephone messages
