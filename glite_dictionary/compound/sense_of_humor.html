sense of humor

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
the ability to appreciate or express what is funny or amusing
ability to appreciate or express humor

Examples

Karen's sense of humor helped lighten the mood during tense meetings.
You need a good sense of humor to enjoy this quirky television show.
Even in difficult times, he never lost his sense of humor.

Synset Examples

she didn't appreciate my humor
you can't survive in the army without a sense of humor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wit - the ability to use words in a quick and inventive way to create humor
  • Antonym: humorless - lacking a sense of humor
