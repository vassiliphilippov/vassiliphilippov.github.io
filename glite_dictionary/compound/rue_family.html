rue family

1. NOUN

/ ruː ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ruː ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Rutaceae) that have strongly scented leaves and flowers divided into four or five parts, including many citrus plants
family of strongly scented flowering plants, Rutaceae

Examples

The rue family includes orange, lemon, and other citrus plants.
Many members of the rue family have aromatic leaves used in cooking or perfumery.
The botanist identified the shrub as belonging to the rue family.
