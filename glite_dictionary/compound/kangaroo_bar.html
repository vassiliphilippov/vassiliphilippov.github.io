kangaroo bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæŋɡəˌru bɑr / (AmE), / ˈkæŋɡəˌru bɑː / (BrE)
An informal bar, often featuring a lively and energetic atmosphere.
informal, lively bar
informal

We decided to meet at the kangaroo bar because it always has a fun crowd.
The kangaroo bar was packed with people dancing and laughing all night long.
After work, they went to a kangaroo bar for drinks and live music.
