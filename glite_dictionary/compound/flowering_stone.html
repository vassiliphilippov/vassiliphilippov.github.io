flowering stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊərɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
A succulent plant of the genus Lithops, native to Africa, characterized by thick leaves that resemble stones and produce solitary yellow or white flowers.
African succulent plant resembling a stone

Examples

The flowering stone blended perfectly with the pebbles, making it difficult to spot in the garden.
Botanists admire the flowering stone for its unique ability to mimic its rocky surroundings.
Each spring, the flowering stone produces a single bright bloom that stands out among the gray leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lithops - genus of stone-like succulent plants native to Africa
