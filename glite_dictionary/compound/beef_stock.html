beef stock

1. NOUN

/ bif stɑk / (AmE), / biːf stɒk / (BrE)
A flavorful liquid made by simmering beef, bones, and vegetables, used as a base for soups and sauces.
flavorful liquid from simmered beef and bones

Examples

The chef added beef stock to the stew to enhance its flavor.
Homemade beef stock gives soups a richer taste than store-bought versions.
She simmered bones and vegetables for hours to make her own beef stock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken stock - a stock made with chicken
  • Synonym: broth - a savory liquid in which meat, fish, or vegetables have been cooked
