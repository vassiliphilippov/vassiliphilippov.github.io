white mallow

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / waɪt ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
A European perennial plant with triangular ovate leaves and lilac-pink flowers, often found naturalized in the United States.
European perennial plant with lilac-pink flowers

Examples

The white mallow blooms beautifully in the late spring, adding color to the garden.
Gardeners appreciate the resilience of white mallow in a variety of soils and climates.
We spotted a patch of white mallow growing wild along the country road.
