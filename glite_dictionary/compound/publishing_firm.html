publishing firm

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ fɜrm / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ fɜːm / (BrE)
A company that is engaged in the publishing of books, magazines, newspapers, or other materials.
company in the publishing business

Examples

She started her career at a well-known publishing firm in New York.
The publishing firm released three bestsellers last year.
After months of negotiations, he finally signed a contract with a major publishing firm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publisher - a person or company that prepares and issues books, journals, or music for sale
