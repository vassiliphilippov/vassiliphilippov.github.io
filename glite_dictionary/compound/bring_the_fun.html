to bring the fun

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ ðə fʌn / (AmE), / brɪŋ ðə fʌn / (BrE)
to make a situation lively, enjoyable, or entertaining; to create a fun atmosphere
make a situation enjoyable
informal

Examples

Whenever Sarah arrives, she really brings the fun to our parties.
The organizers promised to bring the fun to this year's festival with games and music.
If you want your event to stand out, you need someone who can bring the fun.

Synset Examples

The Hennies always bring the fun to any event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to liven up - to make something more lively or exciting
