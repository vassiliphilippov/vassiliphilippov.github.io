automotive vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəˈmoʊtɪv ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈməʊtɪv ˈviːɪkl / (BrE)
a self-propelled wheeled vehicle, such as a car, truck, or bus, that does not run on rails
self-propelled road vehicle

Examples

The new law requires every automotive vehicle to have updated emission standards.
Electric cars are a type of automotive vehicle that is becoming more popular every year.
Buses, trucks, and passenger cars all fall under the category of automotive vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motor vehicle - a road vehicle powered by an engine, especially an internal combustion engine; synonym for automotive vehicle

Etymology

From 'automotive' (meaning self-propelling, from French and Latin roots) and 'vehicle' (from Latin vehiculum, means of transport).
