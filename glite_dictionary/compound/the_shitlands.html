the Shitlands

1. NOUN

/ ðə ˈʃɪtlændz / (AmE), / ðə ˈʃɪtləndz / (BrE)
(derogatory, slang) A disparaging term for undesirable, neglected, or run-down outlying areas of a city or region.
derogatory term for neglected outlying area
slang offensive

Examples

Some locals avoid the Shitlands because of their poor reputation and lack of facilities.
After moving to the Shitlands, she found it difficult to access reliable public transport.
The city council promised to invest more in the Shitlands to improve living conditions.
