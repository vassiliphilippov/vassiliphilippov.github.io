to drop in for coffee

1. VERB

/ drɑp ɪn fər ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / drɒp ɪn fə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to visit someone informally, usually for a short time, with the specific purpose of having coffee together
visit someone informally for coffee

Examples

We often drop in for coffee at Jenny's place on Sundays.
Feel free to drop in for coffee whenever you're in the neighborhood.
After their meeting, they decided to drop in for coffee to catch up.

Synset Examples

You can drop in for coffee after work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to drop in - to visit someone without prior arrangement
