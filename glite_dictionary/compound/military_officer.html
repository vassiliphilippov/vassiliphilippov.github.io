military officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person in the armed forces who holds a position of authority or command, usually having a commissioned rank
armed forces authority figure

Examples

The military officer gave orders for the troops to advance at dawn.
After years of dedicated service, she became the youngest military officer in her unit.
A skilled military officer must make quick and decisive judgments in the field.

Synset Examples

an officer is responsible for the lives of his men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: officer - a person holding a position of authority or command in the armed forces
  • Antonym: enlisted - a member of the armed forces who does not hold commissioned rank
