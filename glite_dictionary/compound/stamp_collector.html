stamp collector

1. NOUN

/ stæmp kəˈlɛktɚ / (AmE), / stæmp kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who collects postage stamps as a hobby or for study
person who collects postage stamps

Examples

My grandfather has been a passionate stamp collector since he was a child.
As a stamp collector, she enjoys finding rare stamps from around the world.
The exhibition featured displays created by local stamp collectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philatelist - a person who collects or studies stamps
