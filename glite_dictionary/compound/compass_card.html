compass card

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmpəs kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəs kɑːd / (BrE)
a circular card mounted on a compass that rotates so that its north mark points to the magnetic north, indicating bearings
rotating card in a compass showing bearings

Examples

The captain checked the compass card to ensure the ship stayed on course.
A properly balanced compass card is essential for accurate navigation at sea.
During the storm, the spinning of the compass card made it difficult to determine the correct heading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compass - an instrument for determining direction, usually with a magnetized needle
