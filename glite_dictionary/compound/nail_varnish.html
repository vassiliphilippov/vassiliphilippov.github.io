nail varnish

1. NOUN

/ neɪl ˈvɑrnɪʃ / (AmE), / neɪl ˈvɑːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a colored or clear cosmetic liquid applied to fingernails or toenails to provide a protective or decorative coating
cosmetic liquid for coloring nails

Examples

She painted her toenails with a bright red nail varnish before the party.
The clear nail varnish gave her nails a beautiful, glossy finish.
He accidentally spilled nail varnish on the carpet, leaving a stubborn stain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail polish - a similar cosmetic liquid applied to nails
