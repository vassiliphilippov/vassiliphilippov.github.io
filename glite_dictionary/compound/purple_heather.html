purple heather

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈhɛðɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈhɛðə / (BrE)
a semi-prostrate evergreen herb native to the western United States, known for its purple flowers
a purple-flowered evergreen herb of the western US

Examples

The hills were covered in purple heather during the late spring.
Botanists are studying the resilience of purple heather in changing climates.
Gardeners in the region often cultivate purple heather for its ornamental value.
