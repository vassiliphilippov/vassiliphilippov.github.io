high spirits

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / haɪ ˈspɪərɪts / (BrE)
a feeling of great happiness, excitement, or optimism; a lively and cheerful mood
a lively, cheerful mood

Examples

After receiving the good news, she was in high spirits for the rest of the day.
Despite the rainy weather, the children remained in high spirits during the field trip.
Their team celebrated the victory in high spirits long into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elation - a state of great joy or pride
  • Antonym: depression - a state of low mood and aversion to activity
