nothing but whoreshit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈhɔrˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈhɔːˌʃɪt / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar way of saying that something is complete garbage or utter nonsense.
(offensive, vulgar) utter nonsense; rubbish
offensive vulgar

Examples

He dismissed the rumors as nothing but whoreshit and refused to comment further.
Their explanation was nothing but whoreshit from start to finish.
I can't believe you believe that; it's nothing but whoreshit.

Synset Examples

This project is nothing but whoreshit; we need changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • Synonym: bullshit - vulgar slang for lies or nonsense
