telegraph operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌɡræf ˈɑpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪɡrɑːf ˈɒpəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to send and receive messages using a telegraph
person who sends and receives telegraph messages

Examples

The telegraph operator quickly transmitted the urgent message across the country.
In the old western town, the telegraph operator was a key figure in communication.
During wartime, every telegraph operator had to work long and stressful hours.
