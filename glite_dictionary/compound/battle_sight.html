battle sight

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬.əl saɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætl saɪt / (BrE)
an arrangement of firearm sights that enables quick aiming at short ranges
quick-aim sight arrangement for firearms

Examples

The rifle came equipped with a battle sight for rapid engagement of close-range targets.
Soldiers rely on the battle sight to quickly align their weapons under pressure.
He adjusted his battle sight before entering the close-quarters training area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight - a device on a firearm used for aiming
