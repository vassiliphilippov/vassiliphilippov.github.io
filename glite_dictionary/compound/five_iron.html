five iron

1. NOUN

/ faɪv ˈaɪɚn / (AmE), / faɪv ˈaɪən / (BrE)
a golf club with an iron head that has a moderate loft, typically used for mid-range shots on the fairway
golf club for mid-range shots

Examples

He used a five iron to reach the green from the fairway.
My five iron is my favorite club for mid-distance approach shots.
She selected her five iron for the 170-yard par three.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: iron - a golf club with a flat, angled metal head
  • Degree Higher: six iron - a golf club with slightly more loft than a five iron
  • Degree Lower: four iron - a golf club with slightly less loft than a five iron
