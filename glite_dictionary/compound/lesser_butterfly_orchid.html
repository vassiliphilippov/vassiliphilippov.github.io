lesser butterfly orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈbʌtərflaɪ ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈbʌtəflaɪ ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a south European orchid with fragrant, greenish-white flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Habenaria
a south European orchid species

Examples

The lesser butterfly orchid can be found blooming in meadows during late spring and early summer.
Many nature reserves in southern Europe strive to protect the delicate lesser butterfly orchid.
Unlike its close relative, the lesser butterfly orchid has smaller, more subtly scented flowers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: greater butterfly orchid - a related orchid species with larger flowers
