chop steak

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑp steɪk / (AmE), / tʃɒp steɪk / (BrE)
a patty made from ground cooked beef, similar to a hamburger but typically served without a bun
patty of ground cooked beef

Examples

He ordered a chop steak with mashed potatoes for dinner.
The restaurant serves a classic chop steak covered in gravy.
For a hearty meal, try grilling a chop steak and serving it with vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger steak - a patty of ground beef, usually broiled or fried
