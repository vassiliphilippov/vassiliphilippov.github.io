ditto mark

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪtoʊ mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈdɪtəʊ mɑːk / (BrE)
a symbol (usually ″) used in writing or printing to show that the word or figure above it should be repeated
symbol indicating repetition

Examples

Please use a ditto mark instead of writing the same entry twice.
The table included several ditto marks to indicate repeated information.
He mistakenly entered the value instead of using a ditto mark in the list.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ditto - the word or symbol indicating something is the same as previously mentioned

Etymology

From Italian 'detto' (said), used in bookkeeping to indicate repetition.
