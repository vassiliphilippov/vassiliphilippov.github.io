mallcore look

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔlˌkɔr lʊk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːlˌkɔː lʊk / (BrE)
A style of fashion associated with mall culture, typically featuring flashy, colorful, or branded clothing and accessories.
fashion style linked to mall culture
informal

Examples

She decided to try the mallcore look for her first day at the new school.
His bright sneakers and graphic tee completed his mallcore look.
Many teens are drawn to the mallcore look for its colorful and bold style.

Etymology

Formed from 'mallcore' (fashion/style associated with shopping malls) and 'look' (appearance).
