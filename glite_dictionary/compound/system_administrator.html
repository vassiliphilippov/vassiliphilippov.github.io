system administrator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪstəm ədˌmɪnɪˌstreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm ədˌmɪnɪstreɪtə / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing and maintaining a computer system or network, especially in a business or institution
person managing computer systems or networks

Examples

Our system administrator is responsible for updating the company's security protocols.
If you experience any issues with your email, contact the system administrator.
The system administrator conducted routine maintenance to prevent downtime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sysadmin - informal abbreviation for system administrator
