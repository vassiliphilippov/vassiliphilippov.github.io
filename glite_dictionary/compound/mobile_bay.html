Mobile Bay

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈbiːl beɪ / (AmE), / məʊˈbiːl beɪ / (BrE)
a bay off the Gulf of Mexico in southwestern Alabama, United States, into which the Mobile River flows
bay in Alabama, USA, fed by the Mobile River

Examples

The storm caused severe flooding along the shores of Mobile Bay.
Many migratory birds stop at Mobile Bay during their journey south.
The city of Mobile lies just north of Mobile Bay.

Etymology

Named for the Mobile River and the city of Mobile; 'Mobile' derives from the Maubila, a Native American people.
