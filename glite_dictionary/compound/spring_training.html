spring training

1. NOUN

/ sprɪŋ ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
preseason practice and training that takes place in the spring, especially for professional baseball teams before the regular season begins
preseason sports training in spring
american

Examples

Every year, fans travel to Arizona to watch their team's spring training games.
After spring training, the roster is finalized for the first regular season game.
The rookie impressed the coaches during spring training and earned a spot on the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preseason - the period before the regular sports season
