United States Government Accountability Office

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnaɪt̬ɪd steɪts ˌɡʌvərnmənt əˌkaʊntəˈbɪləti ˈɔːfɪs / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪtɪd steɪts ˌɡʌvnmənt əˌkaʊntəˈbɪlɪti ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
an independent nonpartisan federal agency, serving as the investigative arm of the U.S. Congress, responsible for overseeing government spending and accountability
U.S. government oversight agency

Examples

The United States Government Accountability Office released a report highlighting inefficiencies in federal spending.
Congress asked the United States Government Accountability Office to audit the new healthcare program.
The United States Government Accountability Office plays a crucial role in ensuring transparency within the federal government.
