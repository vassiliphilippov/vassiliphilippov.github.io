rocket salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑː.kɪt ˌsæl.əd / (AmE), / ˈrɒk.ɪt ˌsæl.əd / (BrE)
A leafy green plant with a peppery flavor, often eaten raw in salads; also known as arugula.
leafy salad plant (arugula)

Examples

She sprinkled fresh rocket salad over the pizza before serving it.
The rocket salad gives the dish a spicy, peppery kick that I love.
During summer, we grow rocket salad in our backyard for our daily meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arugula - North American name for rocket salad, a leafy salad green

Etymology

From Italian 'ruccola' or 'rucola', adopted through British usage as 'rocket'; 'salad' from Old French 'salade'.
