American marten

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈmɑɹtən / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
A North American marten (Martes americana) with brownish fur, valued for its pelt.
a North American marten valued for its fur

Examples

The American marten is often found in northern forests and is known for its luxurious fur.
Trappers once heavily hunted the American marten for the fur trade in North America.
Climate change threatens the habitat of the American marten in parts of Canada.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marten - a small wild mammal with soft fur, related to weasels
