Nepalese rupee

1. NOUN

/ nəˈpɑːliz ruˈpiː / (AmE), / nɛˈpɑːliːz ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency of Nepal, equal to 100 paisa
the currency of Nepal

Examples

The price was listed in Nepalese rupee, so I had to convert it to dollars.
Tourists often exchange their money for Nepalese rupee upon arrival at the airport.
The value of the Nepalese rupee fluctuated significantly over the last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - the basic monetary unit of several South Asian countries

Etymology

From 'Nepal' and Hindi/Urdu 'rupiya' (silver coin, rupee).
