automated moderator

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəˌmeɪtɪd ˈmɑːdɚˌreɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˌmeɪtɪd ˈmɒd.əˌreɪ.tə / (BrE)
a computer program designed to monitor and manage online discussions automatically, enforcing rules and filtering content without human intervention
computer program managing online discussions automatically

Examples

Many forums now rely on an automated moderator to enforce community guidelines.
The automated moderator quickly removed spam posts before users saw them.
An automated moderator helps reduce the workload for human moderators in large communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moderator - a person or program that monitors and manages discussions
