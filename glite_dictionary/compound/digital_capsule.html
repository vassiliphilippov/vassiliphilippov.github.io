digital capsule

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈkæpsəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈkæpsjuːl / (BrE)
a compact and self-contained digital storage unit used to store and preserve digital information
compact digital storage unit

Examples

The photographs from the event were archived in a digital capsule for future generations.
Scientists are developing a digital capsule to preserve critical data against technological obsolescence.
He stored the information in a digital capsule to ensure its long-term security.
