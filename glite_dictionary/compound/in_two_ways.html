in two ways

1. ADV

/ ɪn tu weɪz / (AmE), / ɪn tuː weɪz / (BrE)
in a manner involving two different methods, reasons, or aspects; in twofold fashion
in a twofold manner

Examples

You can interpret the painting in two ways, depending on your perspective.
Her actions helped the company in two ways: financially and by improving morale.
The study can be approached in two ways, either qualitatively or quantitatively.

Synset Examples

he was doubly wrong

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doubly - in a double manner or to twice the extent
