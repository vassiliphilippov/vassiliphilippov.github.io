government agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official who represents or acts on behalf of a government or its department
official representative of a government

Examples

The government agent arrived to inspect the facility and ensure compliance with regulations.
As a government agent, she often traveled to remote communities to oversee local projects.
He was questioned by a government agent about his recent trip abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: official - a person holding public office or having official duties
