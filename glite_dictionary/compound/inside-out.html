inside-out

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.saɪdˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.saɪdˈaʊt / (BrE)
with the inner surface facing outward or exposed
with the inner side exposed

Examples

She accidentally put her T-shirt on inside-out before leaving the house.
If your jacket is inside-out, the label will be visible.
He wore his socks inside-out without noticing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right-side-out - with the correct (outer) surface on the outside
