bouncing putty

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈpʌti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈpʌti / (BrE)
a soft, elastic silicone-based material that becomes more elastic when force is applied, used in products such as golf ball centers and shock-absorbing padding
elastic silicone compound, used as padding or in golf balls

Examples

The golf ball's core contains bouncing putty to help it rebound efficiently.
Engineers use bouncing putty as a shock absorber in delicate electronic devices.
The children enjoyed stretching and shaping bouncing putty during the science experiment.
