hurricane's eye

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɝː.ɪ.keɪnz aɪ / (AmE), / ˈhʌr.ɪ.keɪnz aɪ / (BrE)
the calm, clear center of a hurricane, surrounded by the most intense part of the storm
calm center of a hurricane

Examples

The hurricane's eye passed directly over the island, offering a brief moment of calm.
Meteorologists use radar to track the movement of the hurricane's eye.
As the hurricane's eye approached, the winds suddenly died down and the sky cleared.

Synset Examples

The hurricane's eye is the storm center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye (of a storm) - the calm, center area of any strong storm system
