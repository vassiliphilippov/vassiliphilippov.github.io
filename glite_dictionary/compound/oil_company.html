oil company

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company involved in the production, refining, or distribution of oil, especially petroleum.
company involved in the oil industry

Examples

The world's largest oil company announced record profits this year.
Many people blame the oil company for the recent environmental disaster.
She got a job as an engineer at an international oil company after graduating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum company - company engaged in the production and distribution of petroleum
  • Degree Higher: energy company - company that produces or supplies energy, including oil, gas, or electricity
