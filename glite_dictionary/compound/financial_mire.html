financial mire

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl maɪr / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl maɪə / (BrE)
a situation in which a person or organization is experiencing serious financial trouble or difficulty
difficult financial situation

Examples

After losing his job, Mark quickly found himself trapped in a financial mire.
The company struggled for years to climb out of its financial mire.
Their costly mistakes left them sinking deeper into a financial mire.

Synset Examples

They had to find a way out of the financial mire.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mire - a difficult or unpleasant situation from which it is hard to escape
