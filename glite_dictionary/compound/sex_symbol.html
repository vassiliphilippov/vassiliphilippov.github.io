sex symbol

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / seks ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
A person, especially a celebrity, who is widely recognized as exceptionally sexually attractive.
celebrity known for sexual attractiveness

Examples

The actress became a sex symbol in the 1950s thanks to her roles in popular films.
Many young stars are labeled as sex symbols by the media, sometimes against their wishes.
The singer dismissed the idea of being seen as a sex symbol and focused on her music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icon - a famous person admired for an important quality or talent
