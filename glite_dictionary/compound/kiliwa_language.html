Kiliwa language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːliːwə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkiːliːwə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Yuman language spoken by the Kiliwa people of Baja California, Mexico
Yuman language of the Kiliwa people

Examples

Linguists have documented the Kiliwa language to help preserve it for future generations.
She is one of the few people who still speak the Kiliwa language fluently.
A class on the Kiliwa language was offered at the university to promote indigenous culture.

Etymology

From the name of the Kiliwa people, indigenous to Baja California.
