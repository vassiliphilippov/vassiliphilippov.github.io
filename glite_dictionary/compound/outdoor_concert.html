outdoor concert

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr ˈkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː ˈkɒnsət / (BrE)
A concert that takes place in an open-air or outside location, rather than indoors.
concert held outdoors

Examples

We enjoyed a picnic during the outdoor concert in the park.
Rain delayed the start of the outdoor concert by an hour.
Every summer, the city hosts an outdoor concert series on the main square.

Synset Examples

The musician brought an accordion to the outdoor concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concert - a musical performance, especially by an orchestra or band
