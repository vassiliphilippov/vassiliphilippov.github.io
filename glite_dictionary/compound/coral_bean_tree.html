coral bean tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəl ˈbin ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl ˈbiːn ˌtriː / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub or small tree with racemes of deep red flowers and black-spotted red seeds, native to the southeastern United States and Central America
flowering shrub with red seeds and flowers

Examples

The striking red flowers of the coral bean tree attract hummingbirds throughout the spring.
Gardeners appreciate the coral bean tree for its ornamental seeds and drought tolerance.
We planted a coral bean tree near the fence to add color to the garden.
