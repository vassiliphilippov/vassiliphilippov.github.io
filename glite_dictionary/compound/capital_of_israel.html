capital of Israel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpɪɾəl əv ˈɪzriɛl / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtəl əv ɪzˈriːəl / (BrE)
Jerusalem: the city designated as the capital and largest city of Israel, with major religious, historical, and political significance, though its status as capital is disputed.
Jerusalem, Israel's capital city (status disputed)

Examples

The United States recognizes Jerusalem as the capital of Israel.
Tourists flock to the capital of Israel to visit its historic and religious sites.
The disputed status of the capital of Israel remains a topic of international debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jerusalem - the city known as the capital of Israel
