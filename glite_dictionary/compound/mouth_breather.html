mouth breather

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊθ ˌbriːðɚ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθ ˌbriːðə / (BrE)
(informal, slang, derogatory) A person regarded as unintelligent or lacking social skills, often used as a mild insult.
unintelligent or socially awkward person (informal insult)
informal slang

Examples

Despite his lack of experience, no one expected him to act like a total mouth breather during the interview.
Online trolls are often dismissed as anonymous mouth breathers with nothing better to do.
If you keep ignoring social cues, people might start calling you a mouth breather.

Synset Examples

He was such a mouth breather during the presentation.
Stop being a mouth breather and pay attention!
Her comment made him feel like a total mouth breather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence

Etymology

Originally referring literally to someone who breathes through the mouth, extended figuratively in slang since the late 20th century.
