living room

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
A room in a house for general social, leisure, and relaxing activities, typically furnished for sitting and often used for entertaining guests.
main sitting and relaxing room in a house

Examples

We spent the evening watching television together in the living room.
Guests gathered in the living room before dinner was served.
She decorated her living room with bright colors and comfortable chairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lounge - a room for sitting, talking, and relaxing in a house

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ rʊm / (BrE)
A room in a house for everyday social activities, where people can sit, relax, and spend time together.
main sitting room in a house

Examples

We gathered in the living room to watch a movie together.
She redecorated her living room with new furniture and bright colors.
The children played quietly in the living room while their parents cooked dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sitting room - a room in a house for sitting and relaxing
  • Synonym: drawing room - a more formal sitting room in a house
  • Synonym: parlor - an old-fashioned term for a sitting room
