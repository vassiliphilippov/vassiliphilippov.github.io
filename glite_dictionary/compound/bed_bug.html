bed bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛd ˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɛd ˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, wingless insect that lives in beds and feeds on the blood of humans and animals, often causing itchy bites
parasitic insect that infests beds

Examples

We discovered a bed bug infestation in our hotel room after noticing several bites on our arms.
It's important to thoroughly clean your mattress if you suspect bed bugs are present.
The exterminator found evidence of bed bugs hiding in the cracks of the headboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flea - small wingless jumping insect that feeds on blood
