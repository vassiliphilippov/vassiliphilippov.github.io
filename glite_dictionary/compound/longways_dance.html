longways dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔŋ.weɪz dæns / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ.weɪz dɑːns / (BrE)
A type of country dance performed with couples arranged in two long lines facing each other.
country dance with couples in two lines

Examples

The longways dance is a staple of English country dancing events.
At the festival, everyone joined in a traditional longways dance to celebrate the harvest.
Learning the steps of a longways dance can be challenging for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contra dance - A similar type of country dance with couples in lines facing each other
