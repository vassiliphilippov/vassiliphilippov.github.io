Norway spruce

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔr.weɪ sprus / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.weɪ spruːs / (BrE)
a tall, pyramidal evergreen tree native to northern Europe, with dark green needles and long, drooping cones
a European evergreen tree with hanging cones

Examples

The Norway spruce is often used as a Christmas tree because of its classic shape and sturdy branches.
Gardeners plant Norway spruce to create windbreaks and provide shade in large landscapes.
The forest was dominated by tall Norway spruce with dark green needles and hanging cones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spruce - a type of coniferous evergreen tree
