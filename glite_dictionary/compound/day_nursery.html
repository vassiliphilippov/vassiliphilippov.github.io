day nursery

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌnɜr.sə.ri / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌnɜː.sər.i / (BrE)
A place where young children are cared for during the day while their parents are at work.
place where young children are cared for during the day

Examples

Children at the day nursery spend their time playing, learning, and napping.
Many working parents rely on a day nursery to care for their children during office hours.
The city council is planning to open a new day nursery near the downtown area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursery school - a school for young children, usually between the ages of three and five
  • Synonym: childcare center - a facility that provides care for young children while parents are at work
