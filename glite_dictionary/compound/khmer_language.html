Khmer language

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈmɛː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Cambodia, belonging to the Mon-Khmer branch of the Austroasiatic language family.
official language of Cambodia

Examples

Many people in Cambodia speak Khmer language as their first language.
She is learning Khmer language to communicate better with her colleagues in Phnom Penh.
The ancient inscriptions were written in Khmer language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodian - the language spoken in Cambodia

Etymology

From Khmer 'Khmer' (name of the ethnic group and their language).
