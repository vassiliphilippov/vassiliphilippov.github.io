watering pot

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːt̬ɚɪŋ pɑt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ pɒt / (BrE)
a container with a handle and a spout, often with a perforated nozzle, used to sprinkle water over plants
container for watering plants

Examples

She filled the watering pot and gently nourished her flowers every morning.
I borrowed your watering pot because mine had sprung a leak.
The old watering pot in the shed still works perfectly for small indoor plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watering can - container for sprinkling water on plants
