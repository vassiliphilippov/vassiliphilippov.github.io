roast veal

1. NOUN

/ roʊst viːl / (AmE), / rəʊst viːl / (BrE)
A cut of veal prepared or suitable for roasting.
veal cut suitable for roasting

Examples

The chef recommended the roast veal with seasonal vegetables for tonight’s special.
She ordered a generous portion of roast veal at the Italian restaurant.
For Sunday dinner, we served roast veal with potatoes and gravy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veal - meat from a young calf
