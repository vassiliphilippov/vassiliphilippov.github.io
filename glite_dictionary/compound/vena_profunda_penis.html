vena profunda penis

1. NOUN

/ ˌveɪnə prəˈfʌndə ˈpiːnɪs / (AmE), / ˌviːnə prəˈfʌndə ˈpiːnɪs / (BrE)
a deep vein of the penis that drains into the prostatic plexus
deep vein of the penis
medical

Examples

The vena profunda penis plays an important role in venous drainage during penile erection.
Damage to the vena profunda penis can affect normal blood flow.
The surgeon carefully identified the vena profunda penis before proceeding with the procedure.

Etymology

From Latin: vena (vein), profunda (deep), penis (penis).
