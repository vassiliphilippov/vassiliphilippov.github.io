kilometers per hour

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈlɑːmɪtərz pər ˈaʊər / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtəz pər ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A unit for measuring speed, expressing the number of kilometers traveled in one hour.
unit of speed (km traveled per hour)

Examples

The speed limit on that highway is 100 kilometers per hour.
Her new car can reach 200 kilometers per hour with ease.
We were driving at seventy kilometers per hour when it started to rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miles per hour - unit of speed: miles traveled in one hour
