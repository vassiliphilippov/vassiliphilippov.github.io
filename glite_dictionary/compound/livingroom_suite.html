livingroom suite

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋˌruːm swiːt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋˌruːm swiːt / (BrE)
a set of matching or coordinated furniture designed for use in the living room, typically including a sofa and armchairs
set of furniture for living room

Examples

We decided to upgrade our old livingroom suite with a modern design.
The matching colors of the livingroom suite created a cozy atmosphere in the house.
She carefully arranged the livingroom suite around the fireplace to make it the focal point.
