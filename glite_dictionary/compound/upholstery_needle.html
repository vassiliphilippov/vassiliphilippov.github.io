upholstery needle

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˈhoʊlstəri ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / ʌpˈhəʊlstəri ˈniːd(ə)l / (BrE)
a strong, thick sewing needle, often curved, used for sewing upholstery fabrics and materials
needle for sewing upholstery

Examples

The upholsterer selected a sturdy upholstery needle to repair the torn cushion.
Curved upholstery needles are ideal for sewing around corners and difficult angles.
She learned how to thread an upholstery needle when restoring antique chairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle - a slender, pointed instrument used for sewing
