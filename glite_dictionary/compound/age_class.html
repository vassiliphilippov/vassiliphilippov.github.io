age class

1. NOUN

/ eɪdʒ klæs / (AmE), / eɪdʒ klɑːs / (BrE)
a group of people or organisms classified together according to their age range
group classified by age range

Examples

In biology, scientists often study how an age class responds to environmental changes.
The seniors formed their own age class at the community center.
Marketing strategies may differ for each age class targeted by the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: age group - people with similar ages considered as a group
