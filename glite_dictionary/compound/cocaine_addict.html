cocaine addict

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈkeɪn ˈædɪkt / (AmE), / kəʊˈkeɪn ˈadɪkt / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to cocaine
person addicted to cocaine

Examples

The clinic offers counseling and support to every cocaine addict who walks through its doors.
As a former cocaine addict, she now helps others recover from addiction.
The documentary follows the daily struggles of a young cocaine addict living on the streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to a substance, especially drugs
