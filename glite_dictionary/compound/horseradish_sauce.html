horseradish sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌrædɪʃ sɔs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsrædɪʃ sɔːs / (BrE)
A creamy white sauce made with grated horseradish root and often mustard, typically served as a condiment with meat or fish.
creamy sauce with horseradish and mustard

Examples

She served roast beef with a generous helping of horseradish sauce on the side.
The sharpness of horseradish sauce adds a kick to mild white fish dishes.
He prefers his sandwiches with some horseradish sauce for extra flavor.
