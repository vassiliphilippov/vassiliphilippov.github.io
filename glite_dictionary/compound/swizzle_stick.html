swizzle stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪzəl stɪk / (AmE), / ˈswɪzəl stɪk / (BrE)
a small stick for stirring cocktails or mixed drinks
small stick for stirring drinks

Examples

He used a swizzle stick to mix the ingredients of his cocktail.
There was a plastic swizzle stick in every drink at the party.
She collected colorful swizzle sticks from bars around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stirrer - a tool for stirring drinks or substances

Etymology

From 'swizzle', a type of mixed drink, possibly of West Indian origin, and 'stick'.
