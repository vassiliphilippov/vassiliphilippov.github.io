off-kilter approach

1. NOUN

/ ɔf ˈkɪltɚ əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ɒf ˈkɪltə əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an unconventional or unusual method or way of doing something
unconventional method or approach

Examples

The team's off-kilter approach led to creative solutions that nobody had predicted.
By taking an off-kilter approach, the artist challenged viewers' expectations.
Some executives are uncomfortable with an off-kilter approach to problem-solving, preferring traditional methods.

Synset Examples

Her off-kilter approach to work surprised her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unorthodox approach - approach that does not conform to traditional methods
  • Antonym: conventional approach - typical or commonly accepted method

Etymology

From the idiomatic expression 'off-kilter', meaning not in the usual or expected position, combined with 'approach'.
