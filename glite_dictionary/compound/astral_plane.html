astral plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈæstrəl pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈæstrəl pleɪn / (BrE)
a spiritual or non-physical realm of existence, often believed to be accessible through meditation, dreams, or after death
spiritual/non-physical realm

Examples

Some people believe that the soul travels on the astral plane during dreams.
He described his meditation as a journey through the astral plane.
Writers of occult fiction often explore adventures on the astral plane.

Synset Examples

Meditation helps me connect with the astral plane.
They discussed experiences on the astral plane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirit world - realm or domain inhabited by spirits
  • Antonym: physical plane - the physical level of existence

Etymology

From astral (relating to the stars or spirit world) + plane (level of existence), popularized in 19th-century esoteric literature.
