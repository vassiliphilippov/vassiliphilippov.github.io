World War II

1. PROPN

/ wɜrld wɑr tu / (AmE), / wɜːld wɔː tuː / (BrE)
the war fought between the Allies and the Axis powers from 1939 to 1945, involving most of the world's nations and resulting in significant global change
the global war from 1939 to 1945

Examples

Many families were separated during World War II due to the conflict and upheaval.
The effects of World War II can still be seen in global politics today.
Her grandfather served in the navy throughout World War II.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: World War I - the global war fought from 1914 to 1918, also known as the First World War
