beautiful glade

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːtəfəl ɡleɪd / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtəfəl ɡleɪd / (BrE)
an open, grassy area in a forest that is particularly attractive or pleasing to look at
attractive open area in a forest

Examples

They spread their blanket in the beautiful glade and enjoyed the peaceful afternoon.
Sunlight filtered through the trees, illuminating the beautiful glade ahead.
The hikers rested in a beautiful glade surrounded by wildflowers.

Synset Examples

We found a beautiful glade perfect for a picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glade - an open, grassy area in a forest
