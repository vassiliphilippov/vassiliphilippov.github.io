crape jasmine

1. NOUN

/ kreɪp ˈdʒæsmɪn / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈdʒæsmɪn / (BrE)
A tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant, wavy or crimped white flowers, native to South and Southeast Asia.
tropical shrub with white, crimped flowers

Examples

The garden's centerpiece is a thriving crape jasmine with clusters of fragrant blooms.
After sunset, the crape jasmine releases a strong, sweet scent that fills the air.
Horticulturists recommend planting crape jasmine in partial shade for best results.
