prison wife

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzən waɪf / (AmE), / ˈprɪzn waɪf / (BrE)
a woman whose spouse is serving a prison sentence
woman married to an incarcerated person

Examples

Becoming a prison wife changed her life in unexpected ways.
Many prison wives face emotional and financial challenges while their partners are incarcerated.
She joined a support group for prison wives to help her cope.

Synset Examples

Being a prison wife is often a lonely journey.
She became a prison wife after marrying him in jail.
