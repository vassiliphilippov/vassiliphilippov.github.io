drooping juniper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdruːpɪŋ ˈdʒuːnɪpər / (AmE), / ˈdruːpɪŋ ˈdʒuːnɪpə / (BrE)
a small coniferous tree native to western Texas and the mountains of Mexico, characterized by spreading branches with drooping branchlets
a small tree with drooping branches, native to Texas and Mexico

Examples

The drooping juniper can be found along rocky slopes in western Texas.
Many botanists study the unique branch structure of the drooping juniper.
We saw several drooping junipers during our trip in the Mexican mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juniper - evergreen shrub or tree of the genus Juniperus
