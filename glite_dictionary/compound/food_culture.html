food culture

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈkʌlt͡ʃɚ / (AmE), / fuːd ˈkʌlt͡ʃə / (BrE)
the customs, practices, and traditions related to the preparation, sharing, and consumption of food within a society or region
customs and traditions around food

Examples

She was fascinated by the food culture she encountered while traveling in Japan.
Local markets are a great way to experience a city's unique food culture.
Changes in food culture can reflect broader social and economic shifts.

Synset Examples

She loves the food culture in Radelaide.
