Lucy in the sky with diamonds

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːsi ɪn ðə skaɪ wɪð ˈdaɪəməndz / (AmE), / ˈluːsi ɪn ðə skaɪ wɪð ˈdaɪəməndz / (BrE)
A slang term for the hallucinogenic drug LSD (lysergic acid diethylamide).
slang for LSD (lysergic acid diethylamide)
slang

Examples

He claimed he was on Lucy in the sky with diamonds the night of the party.
There are songs that reference Lucy in the sky with diamonds as a metaphor for psychedelic experiences.
Rumors spread that the phrase Lucy in the sky with diamonds was a code for LSD.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LSD - The drug lysergic acid diethylamide; a powerful hallucinogen

Etymology

Popularized by the 1967 Beatles song, possibly as a coded reference to LSD via the initials.
