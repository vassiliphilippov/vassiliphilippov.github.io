web celeb

1. NOUN

/ wɛb səˈlɛb / (AmE), / wɛb səˈlɛb / (BrE)
A person who is famous primarily because of their presence or activity on the internet.
internet-based celebrity
informal

Examples

Many teenagers aspire to become a web celeb by posting videos on social media platforms.
The company hired a web celeb to promote its new product to a younger audience.
Despite being a web celeb, she prefers to keep her private life out of the spotlight.

Synset Examples

The fashion brand collaborated with a popular web celeb.
As a web celeb, he constantly interacts with fans online.
She became a web celeb overnight after her viral video.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet celebrity - a person famous for activity on the internet
  • Degree Higher: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment or sport

Etymology

Blend of 'web' (short for 'World Wide Web') and 'celeb' (informal for 'celebrity').
