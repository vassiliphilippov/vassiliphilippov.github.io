juice reamer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒus ˌriːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːs ˌriːmə / (BrE)
A kitchen tool with a conical ridged center, used for extracting juice from citrus fruits by pressing and twisting.
tool for extracting juice from citrus fruits

Examples

She used a juice reamer to squeeze fresh lemon juice for the recipe.
A wooden juice reamer makes it easy to extract every last drop from oranges.
The juice reamer is stored with the other kitchen utensils in the top drawer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citrus juicer - device for extracting juice from citrus fruits
