knotted marjoram

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑtɪd ˈmɑrdʒərəm / (AmE), / ˈnɒtɪd ˈmɑːdʒərəm / (BrE)
An aromatic herb of the Mediterranean region and Turkey, with small clustered leaves, used in cooking and as a garnish.
aromatic Mediterranean herb

Examples

Fresh knotted marjoram adds a distinct flavor to Mediterranean dishes.
They grow knotted marjoram in their garden for culinary use.
The bouquet garni recipe called for a sprig of knotted marjoram among other herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oregano - herb used for flavoring, closely related species
  • Degree Lower: sweet marjoram - another species of marjoram, similar in use
