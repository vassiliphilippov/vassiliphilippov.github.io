cembra nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛmbrə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈsɛmbrə nʌt / (BrE)
the edible seed of the Swiss pine (Pinus cembra), valued for its mild flavor and nutritional content
seed of the Swiss pine

Examples

The delicious cembra nut is often eaten raw or roasted in Alpine regions.
She collected a handful of cembra nuts beneath the branches of a Swiss pine.
Oil extracted from the cembra nut is sometimes used in traditional recipes.

Etymology

From the scientific name of the Swiss pine, Pinus cembra.
