American centaury

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈsɛn.tɔːr.i / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈsɛn.tʃəri / (BrE)
A pink-flowered marsh plant native to the eastern United States, resembling the true centaury.
pink-flowered marsh plant of eastern US

Examples

The marsh was populated with clusters of American centaury blooming among the grasses.
Botanists surveyed the area and found several specimens of American centaury near the shoreline.
Gardeners have started cultivating American centaury as an attractive wildflower in native plant gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a small plant of the gentian family with pink or white flowers
