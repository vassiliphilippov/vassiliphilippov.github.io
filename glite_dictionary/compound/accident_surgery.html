accident surgery

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɪdənt ˌsɜrdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈæksɪdənt ˌsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the surgical treatment of injuries and wounds caused by accidents
surgery for treating accident injuries
medical

Examples

After the car crash, she was immediately taken to accident surgery for emergency treatment.
Doctors specializing in accident surgery often work long hours in trauma centers.
The hospital recently expanded its accident surgery department to handle more complex cases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trauma surgery - surgical specialty dealing with treating traumatic injuries
