Western silvery aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstɚn ˈsɪlvəri ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈsɪlvəri ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant (Aster sericeus) with silvery leaves and lavender to violet flowers, found on prairies and open woods.
North American plant with silvery leaves and violet flowers

Examples

The Western silvery aster blooms each summer across the open Midwestern prairies.
Conservationists are working to protect habitats where the Western silvery aster still thrives.
We spotted several Western silvery asters during our hike through the grasslands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the large genus Aster, with daisy-like flowers
