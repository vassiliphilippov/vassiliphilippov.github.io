financial assistance

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
money or material support given to help a person, group, or cause in need
monetary or material support

Examples

The university offers financial assistance to students from low-income families.
Many charities rely on financial assistance from donors to continue their work.
After the disaster, the government provided financial assistance to those who lost their homes.

Synset Examples

economic assistance to depressed areas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aid - help or support, especially in the form of money or resources
  • Synonym: grant - money given by an organization for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: financial aid - money provided to help pay for education or other purposes
