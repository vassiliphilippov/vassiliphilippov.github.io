cinnamon bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnəmən brɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən brɛd / (BrE)
Bread that is flavored with cinnamon, often containing raisins, and usually served sweet.
sweet bread flavored with cinnamon

Examples

She baked a fresh loaf of cinnamon bread for breakfast.
The aroma of cinnamon bread filled the entire kitchen.
Would you like a slice of cinnamon bread with your tea?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raisin bread - bread containing raisins, often flavored with cinnamon
