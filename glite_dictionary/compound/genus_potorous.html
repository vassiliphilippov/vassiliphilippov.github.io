genus Potorous

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs poʊˈtɔːr.oʊs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs pəˈtɔː.rəʊs / (BrE)
A genus of small marsupials known as potoroos, native to Australia.
genus of Australian marsupials (potoroos)

Examples

The genus Potorous includes several species of small marsupials found in Australia.
Scientists have studied the habitat preferences of animals within the genus Potorous.
One member of the genus Potorous, the long-footed potoroo, is listed as endangered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potoroo - A small marsupial of the genus Potorous

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind) and 'Potorous,' the genus name for potoroos, from an indigenous Australian word.
