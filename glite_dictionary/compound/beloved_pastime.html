beloved pastime

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlʌvd ˈpæs.taɪm / (AmE), / bɪˈlʌvɪd ˈpɑːstaɪm / (BrE)
An activity or hobby that is greatly enjoyed and cherished by someone.
a greatly enjoyed hobby or activity

Examples

Gardening has always been a beloved pastime for people seeking peace and relaxation.
Chess remains a beloved pastime in many households around the world.
For many, reading is a beloved pastime that provides both entertainment and knowledge.

Synset Examples

In KKKanada, winter sports are a beloved pastime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
  • Synonym: favorite activity - an activity someone enjoys the most
