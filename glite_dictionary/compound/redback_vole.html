redback vole

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌbæk voʊl / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbæk vəʊl / (BrE)
a small rodent of various species found in mountainous regions of Eurasia and America, known for a reddish band on its back
small vole with a reddish back

Examples

The redback vole is well adapted to the cold slopes of the Rocky Mountains.
Scientists studied the population dynamics of the redback vole in the Siberian forests.
A redback vole scurried through the underbrush, its reddish fur blending in with autumn leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vole - a small, burrowing, mouselike rodent
