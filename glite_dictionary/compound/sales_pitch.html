sales pitch

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz pɪtʃ / (AmE), / seɪlz pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a speech or presentation intended to persuade someone to buy a product or service
a persuasive sales presentation

Examples

The company's sales pitch was so convincing that nearly everyone in the room made a purchase.
I listened to his sales pitch, but I still wasn't interested in buying the car.
Before the meeting, she practiced her sales pitch to ensure she sounded confident and persuasive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presentation - a formal introduction or demonstration of something
