metal band

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬l ˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəl ˌbænd / (BrE)
a group of musicians who perform heavy metal music, characterized by loud, aggressive sounds and amplified instruments
group playing heavy metal music

Examples

After graduating from school, he joined a metal band and started touring across Europe.
Many influential metal bands emerged in the 1980s, shaping the sound of modern rock.
She has been the lead singer of a metal band for over a decade.

Synset Examples

He plays guitar with a metal band.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heavy metal - a genre of loud, aggressive rock music

Etymology

Formed from 'metal' (referring to heavy metal music) and 'band' (musical group).
