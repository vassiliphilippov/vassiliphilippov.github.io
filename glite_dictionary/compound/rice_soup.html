rice soup

1. NOUN

/ raɪs suːp / (AmE), / raɪs suːp / (BrE)
A soup made with rice and vegetables, often served as a light meal or appetizer.
soup with rice and vegetables

Examples

She made a delicious rice soup for lunch using leftover vegetables.
Whenever I feel under the weather, I crave a warm bowl of rice soup.
The restaurant offers rice soup as a vegetarian option on their menu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken soup - a soup typically made with chicken and vegetables
