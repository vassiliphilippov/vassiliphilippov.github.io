kero lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛroʊ læmp / (AmE), / ˈkɛərəʊ læmp / (BrE)
A lamp that uses kerosene as fuel for producing light.
kerosene-fueled lamp

Examples

We used a kero lamp during the power outage last night.
The old cabin was lit by a single kero lamp hanging from the ceiling.
Many campers still rely on a kero lamp for reliable outdoor lighting.

Synset Examples

He prefers kero lamps for their bright light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kerosene lamp - lamp powered by kerosene

Etymology

Shortening of 'kerosene lamp'. 'Kerosene' from Greek 'keros' (wax).
