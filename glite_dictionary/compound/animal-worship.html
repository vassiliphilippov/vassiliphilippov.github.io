animal-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ɪ.məl ˌwɝː.ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɪ.məl ˌwɜː.ʃɪp / (BrE)
The practice of worshipping or revering animals as sacred beings, often as part of religious or cultural beliefs.
worship or reverence of animals

Examples

Some ancient civilizations practiced animal-worship by considering certain animals to be divine.
The pharaoh's reverence for cats was a form of animal-worship in ancient Egypt.
Topics like animal-worship are commonly discussed in courses on comparative religion.

Related Terms
