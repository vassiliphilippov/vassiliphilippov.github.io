three-year-old

1. ADJ

/ ˌθriː jɪr ˈoʊld / (AmE), / ˌθriː jə ˈəʊld / (BrE)
A person, animal, or thing that is three years old.
a person or thing three years of age

Examples

The three-year-old girl eagerly ran towards the playground.
A three-year-old bottle of wine may taste quite different from a newer one.
They adopted a three-year-old dog from the shelter last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toddler - a young child who is just beginning to walk
