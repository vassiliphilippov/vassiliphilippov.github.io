brownish-purple

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈpɜrpl̩ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
of a dark purple color tinged with brown
dark purple tinged with brown

Examples

The sky turned a brownish-purple just before the storm rolled in.
She wore a brownish-purple scarf that complemented her outfit perfectly.
The artist mixed red, blue, and a touch of yellow to achieve a unique brownish-purple shade.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mauve - a pale purple color
  • Degree Higher: aubergine - a dark purple color resembling eggplant skin
