real Gumby

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ˈɡʌmbi / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈɡʌmbi / (BrE)
A person who is very flexible or adaptable.
a highly flexible or adaptable person
informal

Examples

Everyone admires how Mark handles change; he's a real Gumby in the office.
Our coach is a real Gumby, always adapting our tactics mid-match.
If you want to succeed in this field, you need to be a real Gumby.

Synset Examples

His flexible work style makes him a real Gumby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flexible - able to bend easily or adapt to changes
  • Synonym: adaptable - able to adjust to new conditions easily

Etymology

From 'Gumby', a pliable clay animation character created by Art Clokey in the 1950s.
