to grab a sandwich

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ə ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɡræb ə ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
to quickly get and eat a sandwich, usually as a simple or fast meal
quickly get and eat a sandwich
informal

Examples

I'm going to grab a sandwich before the meeting starts.
She decided to grab a sandwich at the café during her lunch break.
After his workout, he likes to grab a sandwich on the way home.

Synset Examples

He quickly grabbed a sandwich before heading out.
I love grabbing a sandwich from Wawa on my way home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to have a bite - to eat a small or quick meal
  • Antonym: sit-down meal - a meal eaten at leisure while sitting at a table
