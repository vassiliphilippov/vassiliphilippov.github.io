gas fitter

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈfɪtər / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈfɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to install and repair gas pipes, fixtures, and appliances
person who installs and repairs gas installations

Examples

The gas fitter replaced the old stove with a new, safer model.
If you suspect a leak, it's best to call a professional gas fitter immediately.
The landlord hired a gas fitter to install heating in all the apartments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumber - person who installs and repairs water pipes and fixtures
