database management

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪs ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəbeɪs ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the process of creating, organizing, and maintaining a database, including the control and handling of its data
creation and maintenance of a database

Examples

Good database management is essential for ensuring data integrity in large organizations.
She specializes in database management and frequently implements new security measures.
Learning about database management is a core part of most computer science curricula.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: database administration - the activity of managing and maintaining a database
  • Degree Lower: database - an organized set of related data stored electronically
  • Degree Higher: database management system - software used to manage and manipulate databases
