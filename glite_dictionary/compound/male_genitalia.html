male genitalia

1. NOUN

/ meɪl dʒəˈnɪtəliə / (AmE), / meɪl dʒɪˈnɪtɪlɪə / (BrE)
the external reproductive organs of a male
external male sex organs
medical

Examples

The doctor examined his male genitalia for any signs of infection.
In some cultures, discussions about male genitalia remain taboo.
Certain diseases specifically affect the male genitalia and require prompt medical attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitals - the external reproductive organs in general

Etymology

From Latin 'genitalia' (organs of reproduction), from 'genitalis' (pertaining to birth).
