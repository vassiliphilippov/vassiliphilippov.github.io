cinnamon-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the color of cinnamon; a light brown tinged with red or yellow.
of a light brown, cinnamon-like color

Examples

She wore a cinnamon-coloured dress to the autumn festival.
The bird's cinnamon-coloured feathers helped it blend into the forest.
He painted the walls a warm, cinnamon-coloured hue.
