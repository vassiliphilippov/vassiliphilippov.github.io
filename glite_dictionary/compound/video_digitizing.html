video digitizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdioʊ ˈdɪdʒɪtˌaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊ ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of capturing, converting, and storing video images in a digital format for use by a computer
converting video to a digital format

Examples

Video digitizing allows analog footage to be edited on a computer and shared online.
The quality of the final project depends on the settings used during video digitizing.
Many older home videos require video digitizing before they can be stored safely on a hard drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digitization - the process of converting information into a digital format
  • Degree Higher: video editing - modifying and arranging video segments using software
