career-limiting move

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪr ˌlɪmɪtɪŋ muv / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə ˌlɪmɪtɪŋ muːv / (BrE)
an action or decision that is likely to harm or hinder one's advancement in their career
action harming career prospects

Examples

Forgetting to back up the financial records was a career-limiting move that cost her credibility.
He realized too late that insulting his boss was a career-limiting move.
Declining the overseas assignment turned out to be a career-limiting move in the long run.

Synset Examples

Skipping the training was a clear career-limiting move.
Quitting without notice was a career-limiting move.
Taking that job could be a career-limiting move.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless mistake, often with bad consequences
