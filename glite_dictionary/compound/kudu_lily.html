kudu lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːdu ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈkuːdu ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A South African shrub with a swollen, succulent stem and striking pink and white flowers that bloom after the leaves fall, widely grown as an ornamental in tropical regions.
South African ornamental shrub with pink and white flowers

Examples

The kudu lily blooms impressively with pink and white flowers after its leaves have fallen.
Many gardeners in tropical climates appreciate the resilience and beauty of the kudu lily.
The thick, succulent stem of the kudu lily helps it survive periods of drought.
