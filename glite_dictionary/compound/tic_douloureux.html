tic douloureux

1. NOUN

/ tik duː.luːˈruː / (AmE), / tik duː.luːˈrɜː / (BrE)
A severe, sudden pain disorder affecting one or more branches of the trigeminal nerve, also known as trigeminal neuralgia.
severe facial pain disorder (trigeminal neuralgia)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with tic douloureux after describing severe, stabbing facial pain.
Treatment for tic douloureux often involves medication to relieve nerve pain.
Doctors sometimes refer to tic douloureux as the most painful condition known to medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trigeminal neuralgia - a condition causing severe facial pain along the trigeminal nerve

Etymology

From French, literally 'painful tic', referring to the painful spasms caused by the condition.
