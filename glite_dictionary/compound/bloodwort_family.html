bloodwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌdˌwɔrt ˈfæmɪli / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, some genera of which were formerly classified in Liliaceae, including species such as bloodwort
a plant family including the bloodwort

Examples

The bloodwort family contains several species with medicinal properties.
Botanists debate the classification of plants within the bloodwort family and the related Liliaceae.
Flowers in the bloodwort family are often grown in gardens for their striking appearance.
