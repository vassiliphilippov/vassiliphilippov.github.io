National Aeronautics and Space Administration

1. PROPN

/ ˌnæʃənəl ɛrˌɑːnɒˈtɪks ənd speɪs ˌædmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl ˌeəˌrɒˈnɔːtɪks ənd speɪs ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
The independent U.S. government agency responsible for the nation's civilian space program and for aeronautics and aerospace research.
U.S. government space agency (NASA)
american

Examples

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration launched its latest mission to Mars last year.
After graduating, she applied for a job at the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is known worldwide for its achievements in space exploration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NASA - acronym for National Aeronautics and Space Administration

Etymology

Formed in 1958; derived from the full formal title of the U.S. agency, commonly abbreviated as NASA.
