shared out

1. ADJ

/ ʃɛrd aʊt / (AmE), / ʃɛəd aʊt / (BrE)
distributed in portions, often equally, according to a plan or purpose
divided and distributed in portions

Examples

After the harvest, the grain was shared out among the villagers.
The money from the fundraiser was shared out equally between the teams.
Responsibilities for the group project were shared out so that no one was overburdened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divided - separated into portions or parts
  • Synonym: allocated - assigned or distributed for a particular purpose
