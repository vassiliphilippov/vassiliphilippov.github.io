common rose mallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən roʊz ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən rəʊz ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
a showy shrub native to salt marshes of the eastern United States, with large rose-colored flowers
a marsh shrub with large rose flowers

Examples

The common rose mallow can be found blooming in the wetlands throughout summer.
Gardeners appreciate the common rose mallow for its striking and colorful blossoms.
We spotted a common rose mallow near the edge of the salt marsh on our walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hibiscus - a genus of flowering plants including rose mallows
