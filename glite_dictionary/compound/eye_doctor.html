eye doctor

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / aɪ ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the eye.
specialist in medical care of the eye

Examples

I have an appointment with the eye doctor next week to check my vision.
Her eye doctor prescribed new glasses for her astigmatism.
When was the last time you visited an eye doctor?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ophthalmologist - a medical doctor specializing in eye and vision care
