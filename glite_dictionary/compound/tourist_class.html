tourist class

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪst klæs / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst klɑːs / (BrE)
the least expensive accommodations on a ship, train, or airplane, typically offering basic comfort and few amenities
least expensive travel class

Examples

We booked our tickets in tourist class to save money on the trip.
The amenities in tourist class are basic, but the journey was still comfortable.
On the cruise, most families traveled in tourist class while some opted for first class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economy class - the cheapest travel class on passenger transport, especially airplanes
