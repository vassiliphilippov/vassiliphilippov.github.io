toilet book

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət bʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt bʊk / (BrE)
a book intended for light, casual reading, often kept in a bathroom for use during brief visits
book for casual bathroom reading
informal

Examples

She always kept a toilet book on the bathroom shelf for guests.
That crossword puzzle collection is really more of a toilet book than serious literature.
Many people read a toilet book to pass the time during short bathroom breaks.

Synset Examples
