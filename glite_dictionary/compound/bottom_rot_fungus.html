bottom rot fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːtəm rɑt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm rɒt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes bottom rot, a disease affecting the lower parts of lettuce and some other plants
fungus causing bottom rot in lettuce

Examples

The farmer identified the presence of bottom rot fungus in his lettuce crop early in the season.
Proper drainage helps prevent bottom rot fungus from infecting young plants.
When conditions are humid, bottom rot fungus can spread rapidly through a greenhouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottom rot - plant disease affecting the lower parts of plants, especially lettuce
