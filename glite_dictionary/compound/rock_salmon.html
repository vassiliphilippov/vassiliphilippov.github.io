rock salmon

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈsæm.ən / (AmE), / rɒk ˈsæm.ən / (BrE)
any of several coarse fish species, such as dogfish or wolffish, when used as food, especially in British fish and chip shops.
coarse fish used as food (dogfish, wolffish)

Examples

The local chippy offers rock salmon and chips as a traditional dish.
Many people are surprised to learn that rock salmon is actually made from dogfish.
He ordered rock salmon instead of cod to try something different.
