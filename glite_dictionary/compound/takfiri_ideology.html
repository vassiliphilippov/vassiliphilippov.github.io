takfiri ideology

1. NOUN

/ tækˈfiːri aɪˌdɪɑːˈlɑdʒi / (AmE), / takˈfɪəri aɪˌdɪəˈlɒdʒi / (BrE)
A radical Islamic doctrine that sanctions violence against those deemed apostate or non-believers.
radical Islamic doctrine sanctioning violence

Examples

The government's report detailed the spread of takfiri ideology among certain extremist groups.
Several community leaders condemned takfiri ideology as fundamentally opposed to traditional teachings.
He argued that takfiri ideology undermines efforts for peace and tolerance in the region.

Synset Examples

Many view the takfiri ideology as a dangerous radicalization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jihadism - Islamic militancy or doctrine advocating armed struggle
  • Synonym: Salafism - Ultra-conservative reform movement within Sunni Islam

Etymology

From Arabic takfīr (excommunication for apostasy); ideology from Greek idea (form, pattern) + -logia (study of).
