lucky duckling

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌki ˈdʌklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌki ˈdʌklɪŋ / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is considered very fortunate or favored.
a fortunate or favored person (informal)
informal

Examples

Everyone called her a lucky duckling after she won the lottery twice.
You’re such a lucky duckling to get front row tickets to the concert!
He’s the lucky duckling who always manages to find free parking downtown.

Synset Examples

She’s the lucky duckling who found a perfect job.
That lucky duckling just got a brand new car.
He always seems to find money; he’s a lucky duckling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lucky duck - an informal term for a fortunate person
