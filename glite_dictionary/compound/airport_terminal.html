airport terminal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpɔrt ˈtɜrmɪnl̩ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpɔːt ˈtɜːmɪnl̩ / (BrE)
a building at an airport where passengers embark and disembark and where air freight is handled
building at an airport for passengers and freight

Examples

The new airport terminal features expanded check-in counters and security checkpoints.
After collecting his luggage, he walked out of the airport terminal to find a taxi.
Renovations to the old airport terminal will improve passenger comfort and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terminal - a building where passengers transfer between vehicles or journeys
