bally good

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæli ɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈbæli ɡʊd / (BrE)
Very good; excellent (used as a mild intensifier in British English, often expressing enthusiasm or approval).
very good (British informal)
informal british

Examples

He did a bally good job fixing the car, if you ask me.
That was a bally good suggestion, old chap!
Everyone agreed it was a bally good party last night.

Synset Examples

That was a bally good performance last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bloody good - very good; excellent (British informal intensifier)
  • Synonym: excellent - extremely good; outstanding

Etymology

From 'bally', a euphemistic alteration of 'bloody' (used as an intensifier), combined with 'good'.
