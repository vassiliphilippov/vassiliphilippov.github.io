horse parsley

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈpɑr.sli / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈpɑː.sli / (BrE)
A European herb, somewhat resembling celery, widely naturalized in Britain and often cultivated as a potherb.
a European herb resembling celery

Examples

She added freshly chopped horse parsley to the soup for extra flavor.
The coastline is dotted with wild horse parsley growing among the grasses.
Gardeners appreciate horse parsley for its culinary uses and decorative foliage.
