showy goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈɡoʊl.dənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈɡəʊl.dənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a North American species of goldenrod (Solidago speciosa) having tall, bright yellow flower clusters and smooth, erect stems
North American goldenrod species (Solidago speciosa)

Examples

The showy goldenrod blooms in late summer, brightening meadows with its yellow blossoms.
Gardeners often plant showy goldenrod to attract butterflies and bees.
We identified the showy goldenrod by its dense, upright flower spikes and smooth stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - any plant of the genus Solidago, with clusters of small yellow flowers
