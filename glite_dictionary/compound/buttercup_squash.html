buttercup squash

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəkʌp skwɒʃ / (BrE)
a variety of winter squash with a drum-shaped fruit, dark green rind with greyish markings, and sweet orange flesh
a variety of winter squash

Examples

The recipe calls for roasted buttercup squash to add a rich, sweet flavor to the soup.
She found a beautiful buttercup squash at the farmers' market and decided to bake it for dinner.
Unlike other varieties, buttercup squash has dense, creamy flesh that's perfect for purees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squash - edible gourds of the genus Cucurbita

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərˌkʌp skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəkʌp skwɒʃ / (BrE)
A drum-shaped winter squash with a dark green rind marked in silver or grey, and sweet orange flesh.
type of drum-shaped winter squash

Examples

She roasted the buttercup squash with olive oil and herbs for a delicious side dish.
The farmer displayed several varieties, including buttercup squash, at the market.
With its sweet flavor and vibrant color, buttercup squash is perfect for soups and stews.
