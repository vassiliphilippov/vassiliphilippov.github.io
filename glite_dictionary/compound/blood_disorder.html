blood disorder

1. NOUN

/ blʌd dɪˈsɔrdɚ / (AmE), / blʌd dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
a disease that affects the blood, such as anemia, hemophilia, or leukemia
disease affecting the blood

Examples

She was diagnosed with a blood disorder that required regular treatment.
Some blood disorders are inherited, while others develop later in life.
Research into new therapies for blood disorder patients is ongoing.
