Mount Rushmore State

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ˈrʌʃmɔːr steɪt / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈrʌʃmɔː steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of South Dakota, in the north-central United States
nickname for South Dakota, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit the Mount Rushmore State each year to see its famous national monument.
She moved to the Mount Rushmore State for work and quickly fell in love with its landscapes.
The governor of the Mount Rushmore State announced a new economic development plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South Dakota - a state in north-central United States

Etymology

Named after the Mount Rushmore National Memorial, a famed landmark in South Dakota.
