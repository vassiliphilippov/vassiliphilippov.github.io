theater company

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiːət̬ɚ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
an organization or group of people that produces and performs plays
group producing and performing plays

Examples

She joined a local theater company after graduating from college.
The theater company is putting on a new production this spring.
He has worked with the same theater company for over a decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatre troupe - a group of actors who perform together
  • Antonym: orchestra - a large group of musicians who play together
