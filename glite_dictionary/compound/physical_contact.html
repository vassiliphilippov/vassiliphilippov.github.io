physical contact

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈkɑntækt / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈkɒntækt / (BrE)
the state or act of touching or being touched by another person or object; direct bodily interaction.
the act or state of touching physically

Examples

The trainer emphasized the importance of reducing physical contact during the flu outbreak.
Some sports, like wrestling, require a great deal of physical contact between players.
Children may need guidance to understand appropriate and inappropriate physical contact with others.

Synset Examples

her fingers came in contact with the light switch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touch - to make physical contact with something
  • Synonym: contact - the act or state of touching
