building site

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪl.dɪŋ ˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.dɪŋ ˌsaɪt / (BrE)
an area of land where construction work is taking place or is planned to take place
area prepared for construction

Examples

The new library will be built on a vacant building site near the center of town.
Hard hats are required for all workers entering the building site.
The developer purchased several building sites for upcoming projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: construction site - area where construction work is ongoing

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldɪŋ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪldɪŋ saɪt / (BrE)
an area of land where construction work is being carried out
place where construction is happening

Examples

The children were warned not to play near the building site.
A new shopping mall is being developed on the old building site.
Safety regulations are strictly enforced on the building site.

Synset Examples

The construction crew arrived early at the building site.
The noise from the construction site was unbearable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: construction site - an area where construction work is taking place
