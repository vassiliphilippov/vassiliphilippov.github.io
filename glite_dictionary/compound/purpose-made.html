purpose-made

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəs meɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpəs meɪd / (BrE)
specially designed and made for a particular use or purpose.
specifically designed for a use

Examples

The furniture was purpose-made to fit the unique dimensions of the room.
They installed a purpose-made ramp to improve wheelchair access.
Each tool in the workshop was purpose-made for a specific task.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custom-made - made to a particular customer's specifications
  • Antonym: off-the-shelf - available from stock and not custom made
