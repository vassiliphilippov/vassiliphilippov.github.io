racy scene

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsi siːn / (AmE), / ˈreɪsi siːn / (BrE)
a part of a book, film, or other narrative that is sexually suggestive or provocative
sexually suggestive part of a narrative

Examples

The movie was criticized for including a particularly racy scene that pushed the limits of its rating.
Some readers found the racy scenes in the novel unnecessary for the story.
Despite the racy scene, the film managed to maintain a tasteful tone overall.

Synset Examples

The book contained some racy scenes that surprised readers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: explicit scene - scene in a narrative with clear sexual content
