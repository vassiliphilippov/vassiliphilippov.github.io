car pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑr ˌpuːl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː ˌpuːl / (BrE)
a group of people who arrange to share car journeys, taking turns to drive while others are passengers, usually to save money and reduce traffic
group sharing car journeys

Examples

The employees formed a car pool to commute to work together and save on fuel costs.
Joining a car pool is an effective way to reduce traffic congestion in the city.
Each member of the car pool takes a turn driving one week out of every month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpool - agreement between people to share riding in one car
