lone gunman

1. NOUN

/ loʊn ˈɡʌnmən / (AmE), / ləʊn ˈɡʌnmən / (BrE)
a single person who commits a shooting, acting without accomplices
single shooter acting alone

Examples

The police have confirmed that the suspect was a lone gunman acting independently.
Reports indicate a lone gunman was responsible for the attack.
After hours of searching, authorities apprehended the lone gunman near the city park.

Synset Examples

The police are searching for the lone gunman in the area.
Witnesses described the incident involving a lone gunman.
The movie featured a thrilling chase after a lone gunman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunman - a person with a gun, especially a criminal or hired killer
