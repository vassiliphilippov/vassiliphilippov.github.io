high-pitched bark

1. NOUN

/ haɪ pɪtʃt bɑrk / (AmE), / haɪ pɪtʃt bɑːk / (BrE)
a sharp, high-frequency bark made by a dog, usually sounding shrill or piercing
a sharp, high-frequency dog bark

Examples

The puppy's high-pitched bark startled everyone in the room.
Whenever the mail carrier approached, the dog would respond with a high-pitched bark.
Despite his small size, his high-pitched bark was surprisingly loud.

Synset Examples

The dog let out a high-pitched bark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bark - the sharp sound made by a dog
