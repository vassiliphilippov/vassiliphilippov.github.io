alpha dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈælfə dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈælfə dɒɡ / (BrE)
the dominant individual or leader in a group, especially one who asserts authority over others
dominant leader in a group

Examples

Despite being new to the team, he quickly became the alpha dog everyone turned to for direction.
In high school social circles, there's often an alpha dog who sets the trends for others to follow.
Jessica doesn't like office politics, but she's become the alpha dog in her department due to her confidence.

Synset Examples

He always acts like the alpha dog in group discussions.
They need an alpha dog to lead the project effectively.
In the office, she's definitely the alpha dog of the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leader - person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
  • Antonym: follower - person who is led or commanded by another

Etymology

From 'alpha', denoting the highest-ranking individual in pack animals, especially wolves, and 'dog'.
