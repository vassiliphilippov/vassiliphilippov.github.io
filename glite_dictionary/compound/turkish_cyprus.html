Turkish Cyprus

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɝːkɪʃ ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪʃ ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
The self-declared state in the northern part of the island of Cyprus, officially known as the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, recognized only by Turkey.
self-declared state in northern Cyprus

Examples

The government of Turkish Cyprus is recognized only by Turkey.
Peace talks between Cyprus and Turkish Cyprus have continued for decades.
Many international flights to Turkish Cyprus must pass through Turkey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Northern Cyprus - self-declared state in northern Cyprus
