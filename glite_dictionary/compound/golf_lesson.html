golf lesson

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ˈlɛs.ən / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ˈlɛs.ən / (BrE)
an instructional session in which someone is taught how to play golf or improve their golfing skills
instruction session for learning golf

Examples

After just one golf lesson, her swing improved noticeably.
He scheduled a golf lesson with the club professional on Saturday morning.
Many beginners benefit from taking a golf lesson before playing their first round.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesson - a period of instruction; a class or tutorial
