ear doctor

1. NOUN

/ ɪr ˈdɑktər / (AmE), / ɪə ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear
doctor specializing in ear diseases

Examples

My daughter had trouble hearing, so we took her to see an ear doctor.
The ear doctor recommended special drops to treat the infection.
Have you made an appointment with the ear doctor yet?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otologist - a medical specialist in diseases of the ear
  • Synonym: ear, nose, and throat doctor - doctor specializing in the ear, nose, and throat
