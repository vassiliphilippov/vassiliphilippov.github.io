mop handle

1. NOUN

/ mɑp ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / mɒp ˈhændl / (BrE)
the long rod or stick attached to a mop, used for holding and moving it while cleaning
long stick of a mop

Examples

The mop handle broke while I was cleaning the kitchen floor.
She leaned the mop handle against the wall when she finished.
This mop handle is adjustable, making it easier to use for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - the part of an object designed to be held
