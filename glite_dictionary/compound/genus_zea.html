genus Zea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈziː.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈziː.ə / (BrE)
A genus of grasses in the family Poaceae, including maize (corn) and related species.
botanical genus of maize/corn

Examples

Botanists classify maize under the genus Zea due to its unique characteristics.
The genus Zea contains several cultivated and wild species of grasses.
Research on the genus Zea has been instrumental in understanding crop domestication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn - common cereal plant (maize)
  • Synonym: maize - another term for corn, especially outside North America

Etymology

From Latin 'zea' (a kind of grain).
