film fern

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm fɜrn / (AmE), / fɪlm fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern of the genus Hymenophyllum, native to tropical humid regions, with thin, translucent leaves.
a fern with thin, translucent leaves (genus Hymenophyllum)

Examples

The delicate leaves of the film fern almost appear transparent in the morning light.
Many collectors prize the film fern for its unique appearance and tropical origin.
Researchers found several species of film fern thriving in the humid rainforest understory.
