comma butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmə ˌbʌtɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmə ˌbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
A species of anglewing butterfly (Polygonia c-album) found in Eurasia, identified by a distinctive comma-shaped white mark on the underside of each hind wing.
anglewing butterfly with comma-shaped mark

Examples

The comma butterfly is easily recognized by its jagged wing edges and unique white mark.
We spotted a comma butterfly resting on a leaf during our walk through the woods.
Gardeners often plant nettles to attract the comma butterfly to their gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anglewing butterfly - butterflies from the genus Polygonia, known for their jagged wings
