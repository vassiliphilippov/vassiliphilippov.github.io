railroad line

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɑd laɪn / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɒd laɪn / (BrE)
a commercial organization that operates a system of trains to carry passengers or freight
organization operating a train system

Examples

The railroad line announced new schedules for its passenger trains.
Several small railroad lines merged to create a regional network.
The historic railroad line was known for its luxury sleeper cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway company - a company operating railway trains
  • Synonym: bus line - organization operating a system of buses
