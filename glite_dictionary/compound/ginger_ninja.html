ginger ninja

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪndʒər ˈnɪndʒə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə ˈnɪndʒə / (BrE)
an agile and skilled person with red hair, especially in a playful or admiring sense
agile, skilled red-haired person
informal british

Examples

On the football field, he's known as the ginger ninja for his speed and clever moves.
After dodging everyone at the party, she was called a ginger ninja by her friends.
With his trademark red hair and quick reflexes, people nicknamed him the ginger ninja.

Synset Examples

She danced like a ginger ninja on stage.
He's a ginger ninja when it comes to martial arts.

Etymology

Colloquial blend of 'ginger' (red-haired) and 'ninja' (Japanese martial artist).
