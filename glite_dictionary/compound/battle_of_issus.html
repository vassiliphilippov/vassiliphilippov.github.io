Battle of Issus

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾl əv ˈɪsəs / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈɪsəs / (BrE)
a battle fought in 333 BC in which Alexander the Great defeated the Persians led by Darius III
battle in 333 BC between Alexander and Darius III

Examples

The Battle of Issus marked a turning point in Alexander the Great's campaign against Persia.
Historians often analyze the tactics used at the Battle of Issus to explain Alexander's military genius.
Many ancient sources provide detailed accounts of the Battle of Issus and its significance.
