florists' chrysanthemum

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrɪsts kraɪˈsænθəməm / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪsts krɪˈsænθəməm / (BrE)
a cultivated species of chrysanthemum (Chrysanthemum morifolium), native to China and widely used as an ornamental flower by florists
a cultivated chrysanthemum species used in floristry

Examples

Many people give florists' chrysanthemum as gifts to celebrate special occasions.
The greenhouse was filled with vibrant florists' chrysanthemum in every color.
She arranged a bouquet using only pink florists' chrysanthemum for the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chrysanthemum - a popular perennial flowering plant of the genus Chrysanthemum

Etymology

From florists' (relating to florists) + chrysanthemum, from Greek 'chrysos' (gold) + 'anthemon' (flower).
