malted milkshake

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔltɪd ˈmɪlkˌʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪd ˈmɪlkˌʃeɪk / (BrE)
A milkshake prepared with malted milk powder, giving it a distinctive sweet, toasty flavor.
milkshake with malted milk powder
american

Examples

She ordered a chocolate malted milkshake with her burger at the diner.
Nothing beats a cold malted milkshake on a hot summer day.
Do you want a regular milkshake or a malted milkshake with extra malt powder?

Synset Examples

I prefer malted milkshakes over regular ones.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: milkshake - a sweet drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings

Etymology

From 'malted milk', a powdered gruel of malted barley, wheat flour, and evaporated milk, added to 'milkshake'.
