from nowhere

1. ADV

/ frəm ˈnoʊˌwɛr / (AmE), / frəm ˈnəʊˌweə / (BrE)
suddenly and unexpectedly; without any warning or apparent cause.
suddenly and unexpectedly

Examples

The car appeared from nowhere and startled the pedestrians.
An idea popped from nowhere just as I was about to give up.
The storm sprang up from nowhere, catching everyone off guard.

Synset Examples

your cousin arrived out of thin air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of the blue - without warning or unexpectedly
