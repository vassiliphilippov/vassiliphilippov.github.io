line personnel

1. NOUN

/ laɪn pɚˈsɑnəl / (AmE), / laɪn pəˈsɜːnl / (BrE)
employees who are directly involved in the core activities or operations of an organization, having direct responsibility for achieving its main objectives
employees involved in core organizational activities

Examples

As line personnel, she oversees the factory's daily production schedules.
The company distinguishes between line personnel and staff roles during organizational audits.
Effective communication between managers and line personnel is crucial for achieving production targets.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: staff personnel - employees who provide support functions rather than direct involvement in core operations
