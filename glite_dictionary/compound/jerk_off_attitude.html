jerk off attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝk ɑf ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːk ɒf ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
A dismissive or careless demeanor, often showing a lack of respect or concern.
dismissive or careless demeanor
informal vulgar american

Examples

His jerk off attitude made everyone at the meeting feel unappreciated and ignored.
The coach was frustrated with her star player's jerk off attitude during practice.
You need to drop that jerk off attitude if you want people to take you seriously.

Synset Examples

She rolled her eyes, annoyed by his jerk off attitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indifferent attitude - a demeanor lacking interest or concern
  • Antonym: respectful attitude - a demeanor showing respect or consideration
