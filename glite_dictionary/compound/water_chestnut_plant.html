water chestnut plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌtʃɛsˌnʌt plænt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌtʃɛsˌnʌt plɑːnt / (BrE)
an aquatic plant of the genus Trapa, known for its spiny four-pronged edible nutlike fruits
aquatic plant with edible nutlike fruits

Examples

The water chestnut plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater lakes and ponds.
Chefs use the spiny fruit of the water chestnut plant in a variety of Asian dishes.
The invasive spread of the water chestnut plant can disrupt local aquatic ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water chestnut - the edible nutlike fruit of the water chestnut plant
