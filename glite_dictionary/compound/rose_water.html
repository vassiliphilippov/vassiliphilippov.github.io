rose water

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊz ˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
a fragrant liquid made by steeping rose petals in water, used in perfumes, cosmetics, and cooking
water scented with roses

Examples

She sprinkled a few drops of rose water onto the dessert for added fragrance.
In traditional skincare routines, rose water is used as a natural toner.
The delicate scent of rose water lingered in the air after the ceremony.
