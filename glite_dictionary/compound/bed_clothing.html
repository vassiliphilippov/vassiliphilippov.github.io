bed clothing

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / bɛd ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
the sheets, blankets, pillowcases, and other coverings used on a bed
bed covers and linens

Examples

The hotel staff changed the bed clothing every morning to ensure cleanliness.
She bought new bed clothing to match the color scheme of her bedroom.
During winter, heavier bed clothing is needed to stay warm at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedding - bedclothes; materials used to cover a bed
  • Synonym: bedclothes - sheets and coverings for a bed
