sage advice

1. NOUN

/ seɪdʒ ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / seɪdʒ ədˈvʌɪs / (BrE)
counsel or guidance that is considered wise, thoughtful, and prudent
wise and thoughtful counsel

Examples

Her grandmother always offered sage advice during difficult times.
He sought sage advice from his mentor before making the decision.
Whenever I feel lost, I remember the sage advice my teacher gave me.

Synset Examples

The ould one gave me some sage advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise counsel - prudent and knowledgeable guidance
