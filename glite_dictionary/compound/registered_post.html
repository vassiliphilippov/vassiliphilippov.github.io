registered post

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd poʊst / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd pəʊst / (BrE)
a postal service in which mail is officially recorded and insured to ensure safe delivery to the recipient
postal service with official tracking and proof of delivery

Examples

He sent the documents via registered post to ensure they would arrive safely.
The parcel must be sent by registered post so it can be tracked.
I received the contract by registered post and had to sign for it on delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registered mail - mail that is recorded and tracked for added security
