jungle bunny

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋɡəl ˌbʌni / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl ˌbʌni / (BrE)
A highly offensive and derogatory term for a Black person or person of color.
offensive racist slur
offensive

Examples

The use of the term jungle bunny is deeply hurtful and entirely unacceptable in any context.
He was condemned for referring to someone as a jungle bunny during the meeting.
Many find the phrase jungle bunny to be profoundly racist and dehumanizing.

Synset Examples

Using 'jungle bunny' in conversation is completely unacceptable.
