drip mat

1. NOUN

/ drɪp mæt / (AmE), / drɪp mæt / (BrE)
a small mat placed under a drinking glass or bottle to protect a surface from moisture or spills
small mat for protecting surfaces from drink condensation

Examples

Please make sure to put your glass on a drip mat to avoid leaving rings on the table.
The bartender set a fresh drip mat under each pint before serving.
We ordered new branded drip mats for our pub's anniversary celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coaster - a small mat for placing under a glass to protect a surface
