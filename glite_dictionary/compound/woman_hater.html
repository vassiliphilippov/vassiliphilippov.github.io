woman hater

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən ˌheɪtər / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən ˌheɪtə / (BrE)
a person who strongly dislikes or is hostile towards women
someone who hates women

Examples

He was unfairly labeled a woman hater after his critical remarks about female colleagues.
The story's villain is portrayed as a bitter woman hater who cannot stand being around women.
Despite his reputation as a woman hater, he has several close female friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misogynist - a person who dislikes, despises, or is strongly prejudiced against women
  • Degree Higher: misanthrope - a person who dislikes or distrusts humankind in general
