square block

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr blɑk / (AmE), / skweə blɒk / (BrE)
a block that has the approximate shape of a cube, with equal or nearly equal length, width, and height
block shaped like a cube

Examples

The children built a tower using each square block from the toy set.
He placed a heavy square block on top of the stack to keep it stable.
Architects often use a square block of foam to visualize a building's form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cube - a three-dimensional shape with six equal square faces
