bluish green

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green tinged with blue; green with a bluish tone
green with a bluish tint

Examples

The walls of her bedroom were painted a soft bluish green that felt calming.
He wore a tie in a striking bluish green that stood out at the party.
The artist mixed colors until she created the perfect bluish green for the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a medium to dark bluish-green color

2. NOUN

/ ˈbluːɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
A color that is a mixture of blue and green, having characteristics of both.
a color between blue and green

Examples

The artist chose a bluish green for the background of the painting.
Her living room walls are painted a soft bluish green.
That sweater comes in a lovely bluish green shade.
