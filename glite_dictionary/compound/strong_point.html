strong point

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / strɒŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an area in which a person excels or has a special advantage
area of special skill or advantage

Examples

Public speaking has always been her strong point.
Problem-solving is definitely one of his strong points at work.
Cooking is not my strong point, but I make a decent salad.

Synset Examples

cooking is his forte

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forte - a person's strong suit or most highly developed characteristic, talent, or skill
  • Antonym: weak point - an area in which a person is lacking or performs poorly
