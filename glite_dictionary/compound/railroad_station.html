railroad station

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɑd ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɔːd ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where trains stop to pick up or drop off passengers or goods.
place where trains stop for passengers or goods

Examples

We met at the railroad station before catching our train to the city.
The old railroad station has been converted into a museum.
Freight is unloaded at the railroad station every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train station - a place where trains stop for passengers or goods
  • Synonym: bus station - a place where buses pick up or drop off passengers
