fruit crush

1. NOUN

/ fruːt krʌʃ / (AmE), / fruːt krʌʃ / (BrE)
a drink made by squeezing or crushing fruit, often sweetened and sometimes diluted with water
sweet drink made by crushing fruit

Examples

She served a chilled fruit crush at the summer party.
The menu offers a variety of fruit crushes, including mango and berry.
A homemade fruit crush is both refreshing and healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit juice - liquid naturally contained in fruit or vegetable tissue
