course of lectures

1. NOUN

/ kɔrs əv ˈlɛk.tʃɚz / (AmE), / kɔːs əv ˈlɛk.tʃəz / (BrE)
a series of lectures on a particular subject, typically forming part of an academic curriculum
series of lectures on a subject

Examples

She enrolled in a course of lectures on European history this semester.
The university offers a course of lectures in quantum mechanics for advanced students.
His course of lectures was praised for being both informative and engaging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecture series - a series of lectures, often by different speakers, on various topics or one theme
  • Degree Lower: seminar - a class at a university or college where a small group discusses a topic
