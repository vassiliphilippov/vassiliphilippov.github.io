World Trade Center site

1. NOUN

/ wɜrld treɪd ˈsɛntɚ saɪt / (AmE), / wɜːld treɪd ˈsentə saɪt / (BrE)
the area in Lower Manhattan where the World Trade Center stood before its destruction in 2001, now the location of the rebuilt complex and memorials
site where World Trade Center stood in NYC

Examples

Tourists from around the world come to visit the World Trade Center site every year.
After the attacks, the World Trade Center site became a place of remembrance and rebuilding.
Construction crews worked for years to clear debris from the World Trade Center site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ground Zero - the site in New York City where the World Trade Center buildings were destroyed on September 11, 2001
