custom bumper sticker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəm ˈbʌmpɚ ˈstɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm ˈbʌmpə ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
a bumper sticker made to individual order, often displaying a personalized message or design
personalized vehicle bumper sticker

Examples

She ordered a custom bumper sticker with her favorite quote for her new car.
Many businesses promote their brands by giving away custom bumper stickers at events.
At the shop, you can design your own custom bumper sticker online before purchase.

Synset Examples

They sell custom bumper stickers at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bumper sticker - a sticker attached to a vehicle's bumper, usually bearing a message
