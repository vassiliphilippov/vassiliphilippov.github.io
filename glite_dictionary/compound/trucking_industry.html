trucking industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌkɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in transporting goods and freight by trucks
goods transportation by trucks

Examples

The trucking industry plays a vital role in delivering goods across the country.
Rising fuel costs have a significant impact on the trucking industry each year.
Many people find employment opportunities in the trucking industry due to its constant demand for drivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freight industry - the industry involved in transportation of goods by any means
  • Synonym: shipping industry - business of transporting goods by sea or air
