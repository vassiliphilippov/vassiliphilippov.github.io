Mister Wrong

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstɚ ˈrɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə ˈrɒŋ / (BrE)
A man who is unsuitable or disappointing as a romantic partner.
unsuitable romantic partner (man)

Examples

After a string of failed relationships, Jane learned to recognize Mister Wrong quickly.
She thought she had finally met the one, but he turned out to be Mister Wrong.
Sometimes it's easy to mistake Mister Wrong for someone you'll want in your life.

Synset Examples

He turned out to be Mister Wrong, just as I suspected.
I realized too late that he was Mister Wrong for me.
She always seemed to date Mister Wrong, never Mister Right.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Mister Right - the ideal or most suitable man as a romantic partner

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'Mister Right,' meaning the ideal male romantic partner; 'wrong' meaning unsuitable or bad.
