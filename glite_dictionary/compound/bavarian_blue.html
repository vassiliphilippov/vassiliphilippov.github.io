Bavarian blue

1. NOUN

/ bəˈvɛriən blu / (AmE), / bəˈvɛːriən bluː / (BrE)
a type of blue cheese made in Bavaria, known for its creamy texture and distinct blue veins
Bavarian blue-veined cheese

Examples

The cheese board featured a piece of Bavarian blue alongside brie and cheddar.
She enjoys crumbling Bavarian blue over her salads for a tangy flavor.
At the specialty deli, I found an imported Bavarian blue that was perfectly creamy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue cheese - cheese containing streaks of blue or green mold
