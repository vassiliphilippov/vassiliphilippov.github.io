Academy of Television Arts and Sciences

1. PROPN

/ əˈkædəmi əv ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ɑrts ənd ˈsaɪənsɪz / (AmE), / əˈkadəmi əv ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən ɑːts ənd ˈsaɪənsɪz / (BrE)
an American organization that presents the annual Emmy Awards for outstanding achievements in television
US television awards academy

Examples

The Academy of Television Arts and Sciences announced the nominees for this year’s Emmy Awards.
She received recognition from the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences for her work in television production.
The Academy of Television Arts and Sciences has a long history of celebrating excellence in the television industry.
