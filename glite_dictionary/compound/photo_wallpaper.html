photo wallpaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtoʊ ˈwɔlˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊ ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A decorative wall covering made of wallpaper printed with photographs or photographic images, typically used on a single wall as a feature.
decorative wallpaper with photographic images

Examples

We decided to put photo wallpaper on the living room wall to create a striking focal point.
The photo wallpaper of a forest scene makes the bedroom look peaceful and inviting.
You can choose a custom photo wallpaper to match your personal style and home decor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpaper - decorative paper for covering walls
