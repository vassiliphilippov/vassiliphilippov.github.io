spotted gum

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ɡʌm / (BrE)
a large Australian gum tree with characteristic mottled bark, especially Corymbia maculata and closely related species
Australian tree with mottled bark

Examples

The furniture was crafted from spotted gum for durability and beauty.
Many Australian forests are home to tall spotted gum trees.
The smooth, dappled bark of the spotted gum makes it easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gum tree - any tree that exudes gum, especially eucalypts
