heartsink moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌsɪŋk ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtsɪŋk ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A moment in which one feels a sudden and deep sense of disappointment, discouragement, or dread.
moment of deep disappointment or discouragement
british

Examples

I had a heartsink moment when I realized I had lost my passport at the airport.
There was a heartsink moment for the team when the final score was announced.
Opening the letter from the university brought a heartsink moment as I read the rejection.

Synset Examples

There was a heartsink moment when the project was canceled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letdown - a feeling of disappointment
  • Antonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation following release from anxiety
