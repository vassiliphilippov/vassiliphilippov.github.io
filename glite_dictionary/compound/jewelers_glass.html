jeweler's glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːələrz ˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələz ˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a small magnifying lens used by jewelers to closely examine gemstones and other fine details
magnifying lens for jewelers

Examples

The diamond was inspected under a jeweler's glass to check for any flaws.
She always kept her jeweler's glass handy for examining intricate pieces.
A jeweler's glass helps reveal the tiny details in precious stones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loupe - a small magnifying glass used by jewelers and watchmakers
