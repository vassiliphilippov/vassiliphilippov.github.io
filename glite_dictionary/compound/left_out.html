left out

1. ADJ

/ lɛft aʊt / (AmE), / lɛft aʊt / (BrE)
Feeling excluded from a group or activity; not included or involved.
excluded from a group or activity

Examples

Jenny felt left out when her friends went to the movie without her.
No one wants to be left out of the conversation at dinner parties.
He sometimes worries about feeling left out at family gatherings.

Synset Examples

She felt left out, being in the dark about the office gossip.
He felt left out as the parade passed him by.
Food left out too long can easily contaminate.
She felt left out, as everyone else got to go, bo jio!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: included - being part of a group or activity
