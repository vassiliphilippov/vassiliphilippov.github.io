water pistol

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌpɪs.təl / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.tə ˌpɪs.təl / (BrE)
a toy gun that shoots out a stream of water, used for play
toy gun that squirts water

Examples

The children chased each other around the yard with their water pistols.
He filled his water pistol and waited for his friends to come outside.
During the summer picnic, the team had a water pistol fight to cool off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirt gun - a toy gun that squirts water
