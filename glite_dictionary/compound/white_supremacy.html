white supremacy

1. NOUN

/ waɪt səˈprɛməsi / (AmE), / waɪt səˈprɛməsi / (BrE)
The belief or ideology that white people are inherently superior to people of other races and should dominate society.
racist belief in white racial superiority

Examples

The rise of white supremacy in the 20th century had devastating consequences for many societies.
Laws and policies were sometimes influenced by white supremacy and its supporters.
Many activists have dedicated their lives to fighting white supremacy in all its forms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racism - prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against people based on their race
  • Synonym: white supremacist - a person who believes in white supremacy
  • Antonym: racial equality - the state in which access to rights and opportunities is unaffected by race
