pack of wet wipes

1. NOUN

/ pæk əv wɛt waɪps / (AmE), / pak əv wɛt waɪps / (BrE)
a small bundle or container of moist disposable cloths used for cleaning skin or surfaces
container of moist cleaning cloths

Examples

She gave her child a pack of wet wipes to clean his hands after eating.
The travel-sized pack of wet wipes fits easily in my handbag.
After a picnic, we used a pack of wet wipes to tidy up.

Synset Examples

I always carry a pack of wet wipes for quick cleanups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wet wipe - a small moist disposable cloth for cleaning
