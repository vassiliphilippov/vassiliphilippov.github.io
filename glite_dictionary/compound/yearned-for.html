yearned-for

1. ADJ

/ jɝnd fɔr / (AmE), / jɜːnd fɔː / (BrE)
Greatly desired or longed for.
greatly desired or longed for

Examples

For years she searched for her yearned-for homeland.
The medal was his most yearned-for possession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desired - wanted or wished for
  • Synonym: coveted - greatly desired and envied

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'yearn' + past participle '-ed' and preposition 'for'.
