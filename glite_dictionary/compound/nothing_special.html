nothing special

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
not particularly remarkable, noteworthy, or impressive; ordinary or average
unremarkable; ordinary

Examples

The show was enjoyable but nothing special compared to others I've seen.
Her cooking is fine, but honestly it's nothing special.
The hotel room was clean and comfortable, yet nothing special.

Synset Examples

The movie was entertaining, but ultimately nothing special.
His gift was thoughtful, but it was nothing special.
The restaurant was nice, but the food was nothing special.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • Antonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
