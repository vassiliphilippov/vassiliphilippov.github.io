tree clubmoss

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈklʌbˌmɑs / (AmE), / triː ˈklʌb.mɒs / (BrE)
A perennial evergreen plant of North America, Lycopodium obscurum, with creeping stems and upright leafy shoots, commonly called ground pine or princess pine.
forest plant also known as princess pine

Examples

We spotted a cluster of tree clubmoss along the shaded forest floor during our hike.
The tree clubmoss is often used in holiday decorations due to its evergreen foliage.
Botanists are working to conserve habitats that contain rare plants like tree clubmoss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubmoss - any low-growing evergreen plant resembling moss, in the family Lycopodiaceae
  • Synonym: ground pine - another common name for Lycopodium obscurum or tree clubmoss
