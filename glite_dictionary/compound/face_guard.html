face guard

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a strong wire mesh attached to the front of a football helmet to protect the player's face
protective mesh on football helmets

Examples

The new helmet features a reinforced face guard for added protection.
He damaged his face guard during the last play and had to replace it.
All players are required to wear a face guard while on the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face mask - a mask worn on the face for protection (general term)
