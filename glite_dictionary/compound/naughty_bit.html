naughty bit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔti bɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɔːti bɪt / (BrE)
an informal term for a part of something, especially a book, film, or joke, that is considered risqué or mildly indecent
informal risqué or indecent part
informal

Examples

Everyone giggled when the comedian hinted at the naughty bit in his story.
The editor suggested removing the naughty bit to make the article suitable for all audiences.
She covered her eyes during the naughty bit of the film.

Synset Examples

He laughed at the naughty bit in the book.
The movie had a naughty bit that sparked controversy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risqué part - a part that is daringly close to being indecent or improper
  • Degree Higher: indecent - not conforming with generally accepted standards of behavior or propriety
  • Degree Lower: innocent bit - a part that is harmless or inoffensive
