supply ship

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪ ʃɪp / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship designed to transport and deliver supplies to other ships at sea or in remote locations
ship that delivers supplies to other ships

Examples

The navy deployed a supply ship to provide food and fuel to vessels stationed offshore.
After several weeks at sea, the crew eagerly awaited the arrival of the supply ship.
A supply ship delivered medical equipment to the research station in the Arctic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replenishment ship - a ship that provides supplies to naval vessels, often at sea
