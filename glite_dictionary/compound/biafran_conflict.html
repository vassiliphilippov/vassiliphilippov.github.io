Biafran conflict

1. PROPN

/ biˈæfrən ˈkɑnflɪkt / (AmE), / baɪˈæfrən ˈkɒnflɪkt / (BrE)
the civil war fought in Nigeria from 1967 to 1970, when the region of Biafra attempted to secede and declare independence
Nigeria's 1967–1970 civil war

Examples

The Biafran conflict resulted in widespread famine and suffering among civilians.
Her grandfather told stories about living through the Biafran conflict in southeastern Nigeria.
The Biafran conflict continues to influence Nigerian politics and social dynamics decades later.
