modest proposal

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɪst prəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪst prəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
a suggestion or plan that is simple, moderate, and not overly ambitious
simple, unambitious suggestion

Examples

She put forward a modest proposal to reduce company expenses.
Their modest proposal for community improvement was well received by the committee.
The startup began with a modest proposal for a new mobile app.

Synset Examples

The article presented a modest proposal for urban renewal.
She suggested a modest proposal to improve efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proposal - a plan or suggestion put forward for consideration
