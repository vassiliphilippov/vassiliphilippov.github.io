sweet oil

1. NOUN

/ swit ɔɪl / (AmE), / swiːt ɔɪl / (BrE)
Mild vegetable oil used as food, especially olive oil or oil from edible rapeseed.
mild edible vegetable oil, esp. olive oil

Examples

The recipe called for a tablespoon of sweet oil to be added to the dressing.
She gently massaged a few drops of sweet oil into her dry hair.
Old home remedies often recommend sweet oil for soothing earaches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive oil - oil obtained from olives, used as food and in cooking
