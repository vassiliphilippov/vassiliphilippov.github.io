great loot

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt lut / (AmE), / ɡreɪt luːt / (BrE)
a collection of highly valuable items or rewards, especially obtained as a result of a quest or adventure
highly valuable items or rewards
informal

Examples

After defeating the dragon, the heroes discovered great loot hidden in the cave.
Players compete in the game to collect great loot and upgrade their equipment.
She bragged to her friends about the great loot she had found at the flea market.

Synset Examples

The treasure map promised great loot at the end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loot - stolen or valuable goods, especially taken in war or discovered as treasure
