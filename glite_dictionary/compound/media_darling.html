media darling

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdiə ˈdɑrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə ˈdɑːlɪŋ / (BrE)
A person who receives especially favorable attention from the media, often due to charisma, popularity, or public appeal.
person favored by the media

Examples

After her best-selling novel was published, she became a media darling almost overnight.
The young athlete is the latest media darling to capture the public’s attention.
Once a media darling, the CEO is now facing harsh criticism from reporters.

Synset Examples

The young actress quickly became a media darling after her breakout role.
The politician was a media darling until the scandal broke.
He was labeled a media darling due to his philanthropic efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: media star - a person who is prominent or highly featured in the media
