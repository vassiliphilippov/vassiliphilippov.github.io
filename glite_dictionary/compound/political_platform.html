political platform

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈplæt.fɔrm / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈplætfɔːm / (BrE)
a formal document outlining the aims and principles of a political party or candidate
statement of party aims and principles

Examples

The candidate endorsed every proposal in the party's political platform.
Many voters were attracted by the environmental policies outlined in her political platform.
The committee spent months drafting their official political platform before the convention.

Synset Examples

their candidate simply ignored the party platform
they won the election even though they offered no positive program

Related Terms

  • Synonym: platform - statement of the aims and policies of a political party
