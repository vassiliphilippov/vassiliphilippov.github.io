slopseller's shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɑpˌsɛlərz ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈslɒpˌsɛləz ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells cheap, ready-made clothing, especially in historical contexts
shop selling cheap ready-made clothes
archaic

Examples

The sailors visited the slopseller's shop to buy affordable clothes before departing.
In old London, every street seemed to have its own slopseller's shop.
She bought a sturdy coat from a slopseller's shop near the docks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outfitter - a shop selling men's clothing
  • Antonym: tailor's shop - a shop where custom clothing is made and sold

Etymology

From 'slop' (cheap ready-made clothing) + 'seller', referring to merchants in the 17th–19th centuries who sold inexpensive garments.
