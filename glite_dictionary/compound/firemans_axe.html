fireman's axe

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚmənz æks / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəmənz æks / (BrE)
a type of axe with a long handle and a head featuring one cutting edge and a pointed end, used by firefighters for breaking through obstacles
axe used by firefighters, with blade and pick

Examples

The firefighter swung his fireman's axe to break down the locked door.
A fireman's axe is essential equipment for any emergency rescue team.
With its sharp blade and pointed tip, a fireman's axe can cut through wood and pry open barriers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axe - a tool with a heavy metal blade used for chopping
