tough customer

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ˈkʌstəmɚ / (AmE), / tʌf ˈkʌstəmə / (BrE)
a person who is difficult to deal with or persuade, especially in business or negotiations
person who is hard to deal with

Examples

As a negotiator, she's known to be a tough customer who never backs down.
He proved to be a tough customer, refusing every offer the sales team made.
If you're looking for an easy deal, he's not the tough customer you want to approach.

Synset Examples

His sales resistance makes him a tough customer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard case - a person who is tough or hard to deal with
  • Antonym: easy mark - a person who is easily deceived or persuaded
