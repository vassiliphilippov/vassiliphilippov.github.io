parlour trick

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrlɚ trɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə tɹɪk / (BrE)
a simple or informal magic trick or performance, usually given for entertainment in a casual or social setting
simple magic trick for entertainment

Examples

He always has a new parlour trick ready to amuse his friends at parties.
The magician taught the children a simple parlour trick involving coins.
Her favorite parlour trick was making small objects disappear.

Synset Examples

He showed off his parlour trick to impress the kids.
His parlour trick amazed everyone at the gathering.
She performed a classic parlour trick with cards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic trick - an act or performance using tricks or illusions

Etymology

From 'parlour', meaning a sitting room for entertaining guests, and 'trick' meaning a cunning act or feat.
