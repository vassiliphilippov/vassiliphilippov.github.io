Republic of the Marshall Islands

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈmɑrʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈmɑːʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
an island country in the Pacific Ocean, comprising the Marshall Islands and operating as a republic in free association with the United States
island country in the Pacific Ocean

Examples

The Republic of the Marshall Islands became independent in 1986 but continues to have close ties with the United States.
Rising sea levels are a significant concern for the people of the Republic of the Marshall Islands.
The official currency of the Republic of the Marshall Islands is the US dollar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Marshall Islands - a group of islands in the Pacific Ocean; also used for the country
