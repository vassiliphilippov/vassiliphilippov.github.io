board rule

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd ruːl / (AmE), / bɔːd ruːl / (BrE)
a measuring device or standard used to calculate the board feet in lumber.
measuring tool for board feet in lumber

Examples

The carpenter used a board rule to estimate how much lumber he needed for the project.
Every lumberyard worker is trained to read a board rule accurately.
A board rule simplifies the process of measuring irregularly shaped boards.
