royal flush

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ / (BrE)
A poker hand consisting of the ace, king, queen, jack, and ten all in the same suit, regarded as the highest possible hand.
highest poker hand (A, K, Q, J, 10 same suit)

Examples

He won the championship with a royal flush in the final hand.
A royal flush is so rare that some players never see one in their lifetime.
When she revealed her royal flush, the whole table was stunned.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: straight flush - a poker hand with five consecutive cards of the same suit

Etymology

From 'royal', referring to the highest ranking, and 'flush', an established poker hand of cards in the same suit.
