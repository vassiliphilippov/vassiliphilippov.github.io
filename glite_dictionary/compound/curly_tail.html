curly tail

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrli teɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli teɪl / (BrE)
a tail that has a natural curve or forms a spiral shape
tail that curls or spirals

Examples

The pug's curly tail is one of its most recognizable features.
Many pigs are bred to have a distinctive curly tail for both health and identification purposes.
She admired the puppy's little curly tail as it wagged happily.

Synset Examples

The dog has a curly tail that wags joyfully.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: straight tail - a tail that does not curve or spiral
