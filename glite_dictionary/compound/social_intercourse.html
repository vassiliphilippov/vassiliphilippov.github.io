social intercourse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈɪntərkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈɪntəkɔːs / (BrE)
the exchange of ideas and feelings through conversation and interaction between people
interaction or communication between people

Examples

She valued social intercourse and enjoyed lively discussions at dinner parties.
A lack of social intercourse can lead to feelings of isolation and loneliness.
Clubs and societies provide opportunities for social intercourse among members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the act of sharing or exchanging information, thoughts, or feelings
