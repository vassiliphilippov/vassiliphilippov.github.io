heart surgery

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈsɝrdʒəri / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
a surgical procedure performed on the heart to correct or treat a disease or condition
surgical operation on the heart
medical

Examples

After his diagnosis, he was scheduled for heart surgery next month.
Advances in heart surgery have greatly improved patient outcomes over recent decades.
The recovery period following heart surgery can be physically and emotionally demanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiac surgery - surgery performed on the heart or great vessels
