rug merchant

1. NOUN

/ rʌɡ ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / rʌɡ ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person or business that sells rugs, especially as a principal line of trade
seller of rugs

Examples

The local rug merchant offered a wide selection of Persian carpets.
She negotiated with a rug merchant in the bustling market during her trip to Istanbul.
After inheriting the business, he became the third-generation rug merchant in his family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet dealer - a person or business that sells carpets
