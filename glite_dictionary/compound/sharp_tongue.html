sharp tongue

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp tʌŋ / (AmE), / ʃɑːp tʌŋ / (BrE)
a tendency to speak in a critical or harsh manner
critical or harsh way of speaking

Examples

She is known for her sharp tongue and quick wit at meetings.
Though his sharp tongue sometimes offends people, his advice is usually right.
Having a sharp tongue can make it hard to make friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caustic - very critical or sarcastic in speech
  • Antonym: tactful - showing sensitivity and carefulness in speech
