Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades

1. NOUN

/ iz ædˈdiːn al kɑːˈsɑːm brɪˈɡeɪdz / (AmE), / iz ædˈdiːn al kɑːˈsæm brɪˈɡeɪdz / (BrE)
the military wing of Hamas, responsible for carrying out armed attacks and suicide bombings, particularly against Israeli targets
military wing of Hamas

Examples

The Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades are considered a significant military force within the Palestinian territories.
Israeli authorities attributed the rocket attack to the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades.
Many governments have designated the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades as a terrorist organization.

Etymology

Named after Izz ad-Din al-Qassam, a Syrian Muslim preacher and militant leader who resisted British and French rule in the Levant.
