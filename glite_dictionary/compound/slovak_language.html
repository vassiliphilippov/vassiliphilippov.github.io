Slovak language

1. NOUN

/ ˈsloʊ.væk ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsləʊ.væk ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Slavic language spoken chiefly in Slovakia
language of Slovakia

Examples

She decided to learn Slovak language before traveling to Bratislava.
Many signs in Slovakia are written in both English and Slovak language.
As a linguist, he compared the Slovak language to other Slavic tongues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovak - a native or inhabitant of Slovakia or their language

Etymology

From Slovakia, the country where the language is spoken, and Slovak, the name of its people.
