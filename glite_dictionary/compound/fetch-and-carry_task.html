fetch-and-carry task

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɛtʃ ən ˈkæri tæsk / (AmE), / ˌfɛtʃ ən ˈkæri tɑːsk / (BrE)
a menial and repetitive job, often involving simple errands or manual chores
menial, repetitive job or errand

Examples

She was tired of being assigned every fetch-and-carry task in the office.
The intern felt his first week was nothing but fetch-and-carry tasks.
For months, he performed only fetch-and-carry tasks before getting real responsibility.

Synset Examples

His job felt like a fetch-and-carry task every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drudgery - hard, menial, or dull work
