motherloving nuisance

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðɚˌlʌvɪŋ ˈnuːsəns / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌlʌvɪŋ ˈnjuːsəns / (BrE)
An annoying or troublesome person, often used humorously or as a mild euphemism.
annoying person (humorous, euphemistic)
informal

Examples

My little brother can be a real motherloving nuisance when I'm trying to work.
You're such a motherloving nuisance sometimes, but I can't help but laugh.
Don't be a motherloving nuisance to your classmates during the trip.

Synset Examples

Stop being such a motherloving nuisance during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuisance - an annoying or troublesome person or thing
  • Synonym: pest - someone who annoys or bothers others

Etymology

Created as a euphemistic substitution for 'motherfucking', using 'motherloving' to soften or humorously reference the original term.
