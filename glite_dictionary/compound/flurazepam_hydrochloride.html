flurazepam hydrochloride

1. NOUN

/ flʊˌræzɪˌæm haɪˌdrɑːkləˌraɪd / (AmE), / fljʊəˌræziˌam haɪˌdrɒkləˌraɪd / (BrE)
A medication used as a tranquilizer, especially for treating insomnia; known by the trade name Dalmane.
a tranquilizer for treating insomnia
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed flurazepam hydrochloride to help the patient manage chronic insomnia.
Because of its sedative effects, flurazepam hydrochloride should be used with caution in elderly patients.
Some people experience drowsiness the next day after taking flurazepam hydrochloride.
