mason's trowel

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪsənz ˈtraʊəl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪsənz ˈtraʊəl / (BrE)
a hand tool with a flat, pointed blade used by bricklayers or masons for spreading and shaping mortar or cement
tool for spreading mortar in masonry

Examples

The bricklayer carefully applied mortar to the bricks with his mason's trowel.
Every apprentice must learn how to hold a mason's trowel properly.
He bought a new mason's trowel before starting the restoration project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trowel - a small hand tool with a flat blade, used for spreading mortar or plaster
