intersection point

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃən pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛkʃən pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a point at which two or more lines, curves, or surfaces meet or cross each other
point where lines or curves meet

Examples

The intersection point of the two roads was marked by a large sign.
In geometry, the intersection point of two lines can be found by solving their equations.
The engineer calculated the exact intersection point of the beams for the construction plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vertex - a point where two or more lines or edges meet
