groceries order

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsəriːz ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəriːz ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
an order placed for the purchase and delivery or pickup of food and household items from a grocery store
order for food and household items

Examples

She forgot to add milk to her groceries order, so she had to shop again.
