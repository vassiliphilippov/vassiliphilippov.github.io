olio of essays

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊliˌoʊ əv ˈɛseɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊliəʊ əv ˈɛseɪz / (BrE)
a diverse collection of essays, often on a variety of subjects
diverse collection of essays

Examples

The anthology is an olio of essays by both established and emerging writers.
His published works include an olio of essays that cover philosophy, art, and personal experiences.
She compiled an olio of essays reflecting her journeys across three continents.

Synset Examples

The book is an olio of essays on various subjects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscellany - a mixture or collection of various items, especially writings

Etymology

From Italian 'olio' (oil, mixture), used in English to mean medley or miscellany; 'essays' meaning written works.
