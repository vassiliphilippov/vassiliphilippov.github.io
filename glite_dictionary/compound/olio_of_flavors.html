olio of flavors

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊlioʊ əv ˈfleɪvərz / (AmE), / ˈəʊliəʊ əv ˈfleɪvəz / (BrE)
A mixture of various flavors from different sources or traditions.
mixture of various flavors

Examples

The chef's new dish was an olio of flavors that delighted everyone's palate.
Dinner at their house is always an olio of flavors, representing multiple cultures.
Her cooking style brings together an unexpected olio of flavors in every meal.

Synset Examples

Her recipe was an olio of flavors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things
  • Synonym: blend - a mixture of different things or qualities

Etymology

Olio from Italian *olla* (stew pot), meaning a medley or miscellaneous mixture.
