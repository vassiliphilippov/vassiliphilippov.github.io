microscope slide

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪkrəˌskoʊp slaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəˌskəʊp slaɪd / (BrE)
a small, flat, rectangular piece of glass used to hold specimens for examination under a microscope
glass plate for microscopic samples

Examples

The scientist placed a thin tissue sample on the microscope slide for observation.
Before viewing the bacteria, you must properly clean and prepare the microscope slide.
She carefully labeled each microscope slide so she could identify the samples later.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glass slide - a piece of glass used to hold specimens under a microscope
