conversational partner

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːrtnər / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person who is engaged in a conversation with another
person in a conversation

Examples

During the interview, the conversational partner asked several insightful questions.
It is important to listen carefully to your conversational partner in order to communicate effectively.
She found her new conversational partner very engaging and thoughtful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interlocutor - a person who takes part in a conversation
