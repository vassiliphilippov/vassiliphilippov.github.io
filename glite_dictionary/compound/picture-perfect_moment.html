picture-perfect moment

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪktʃər ˈpɜrfɪkt ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃə ˈpɜːfɪkt ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an ideal or flawless moment, resembling a scene worthy of a photograph
an ideal or flawless moment

Examples

They managed to catch a picture-perfect moment with the newlyweds just as the sun set.
The birth of their child was a picture-perfect moment they would never forget.
As the fireworks lit up the sky, everyone paused for a picture-perfect moment together.

Synset Examples

They captured a picture-perfect moment during the sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect moment - an ideal or flawless moment

Etymology

Formed from 'picture-perfect', modeled on the idea of a moment so flawless it could be captured in a photograph.
