pepper grinder

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər ˌɡraɪndər / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ˌɡraɪndə / (BrE)
A device used to crush or mill whole peppercorns into ground pepper for seasoning food.
device for grinding peppercorns

Examples

She twisted the pepper grinder to add a dash of spice to her salad.
The waiter offered freshly ground pepper from a large pepper grinder at the table.
I bought a new pepper grinder because my old one stopped working.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper mill - a device for grinding peppercorns
