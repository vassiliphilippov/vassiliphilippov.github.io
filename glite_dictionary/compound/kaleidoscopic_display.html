kaleidoscopic display

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlaɪdəˈskɑpɪk dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / kəˌlaɪdəˈskɒpɪk dɪsˈpleɪ / (BrE)
A presentation or arrangement that features a shifting variety of bright colors and patterns, resembling what is seen through a kaleidoscope.
colorful, ever-changing visual presentation

Examples

The parade was a kaleidoscopic display with costumes sparkling in the sunlight.
We were mesmerized by the kaleidoscopic display of fireworks over the harbor.
The artist's paintings create a kaleidoscopic display of shapes and vibrant hues.

Synset Examples

The festival was a kaleidoscopic display of lights and colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacle - an impressive or visually striking display
  • Antonym: monochrome - a visual presentation in shades of a single color

Etymology

Formed from 'kaleidoscopic' (from kaleidoscope, from Greek 'kalos' beautiful + 'eidos' form + 'skopein' to look) and 'display'.
