tanker truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋkər ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈtaŋkə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle with a tank for transporting liquids, gases, or dry bulk cargo by road
road vehicle for transporting liquids or gases

Examples

A tanker truck delivered fuel to the gas station early this morning.
After the accident, a tanker truck began leaking chemicals onto the highway.
The farm relies on a tanker truck to bring fresh water during droughts.

Synset Examples

Some less visible distinctions amongst tank trucks have to do with their intended use: compliance with human food regulations, refrigeration capability, acid resistance, pressurization capability, and more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank truck - a truck for transporting liquids or gases; another common term for tanker truck

Etymology

From 'tanker' (a vehicle or vessel for carrying liquids) + 'truck'.
