rule of grammar

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːl əv ˈɡræmər / (AmE), / ˈruːl əv ˈɡræmə / (BrE)
a principle or guideline that determines the correct structure and usage of words in a language
guideline for correct language structure

Examples

Understanding each rule of grammar is essential for writing correctly in English.
Some people find it challenging to remember every rule of grammar when learning a new language.
Teachers often review a rule of grammar before assigning a writing exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grammar - the system and structure of a language
