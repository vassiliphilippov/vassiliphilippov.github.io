parcel of land

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrsəl əv lænd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsəl əv lænd / (BrE)
an area of land, especially one that is considered as a unit for sale, ownership, or management
an area of land considered as a unit

Examples

They purchased a parcel of land to build their new home on.
The city plans to develop the vacant parcel of land near the river.
Each farmer owns a separate parcel of land in the valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot - a small piece of land marked out for a purpose, especially building
  • Synonym: tract - a large area of land
