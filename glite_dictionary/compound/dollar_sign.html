dollar sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlər saɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒlə saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol ($) used before a number to show that the amount is in dollars
the symbol $ indicating dollars

Examples

Please write the dollar sign before the amount on the check.
He drew a big dollar sign on the whiteboard to illustrate his point.
Many people associate the dollar sign with wealth and success.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cent sign - the symbol (¢) representing cents
