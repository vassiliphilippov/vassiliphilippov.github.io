dumb luck

1. NOUN

/ dʌm lʌk / (AmE), / dʌm lʌk / (BrE)
good fortune or success that happens by chance rather than through skill or effort
accidental good fortune

Examples

He passed the final exam by dumb luck rather than by studying.
Sometimes all you need is a little dumb luck to get ahead in life.
Finding the last parking spot was just dumb luck on her part.

Synset Examples

He made the team by dumb luck, not skill.
She found a $100 bill on the ground, just dumb luck.
Winning the lottery was pure dumb luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luck - success or failure apparently brought by chance rather than one's own actions
  • Synonym: fortune - chance or luck as an external, arbitrary force affecting human affairs
