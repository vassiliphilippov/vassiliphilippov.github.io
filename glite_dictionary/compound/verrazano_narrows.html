Verrazano Narrows

1. NOUN

/ vɛrəˌzɑnoʊ ˈnæroʊz / (AmE), / vɛrəˌzɑːnəʊ ˈnærəʊz / (BrE)
the narrow tidal channel separating Staten Island from Brooklyn in New York City
tidal channel between Staten Island and Brooklyn

Examples

The Verrazano Narrows serves as the entrance to the upper New York Bay.
Many ships pass through the Verrazano Narrows on their way to New York Harbor.
The famous Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge spans the Verrazano Narrows, connecting Brooklyn and Staten Island.

Etymology

Named after Giovanni da Verrazzano, Italian explorer, and 'narrows' meaning a narrow channel of water.
