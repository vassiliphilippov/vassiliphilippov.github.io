total creepo

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈkripoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈkriːpəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is extremely creepy or makes others feel uneasy
very creepy or unsettling person
informal slang

Examples

After only five minutes of conversation, she could tell he was a total creepo.
Don't go near that house—everyone says the guy next door is a total creepo.
He got banned from the club because people complained he was acting like a total creepo.

Synset Examples

She felt uneasy around him; he seemed like a total creepo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creep - a person who behaves in an unpleasantly strange or suspicious way
  • Synonym: weirdo - a person who is very strange or eccentric
