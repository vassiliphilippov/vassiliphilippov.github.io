ultimate antagonist

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltəmət ænˈtæɡənɪst / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ænˈtæɡənɪst / (BrE)
the main and final opposing character in a story, game, or other narrative work
main final opposing character

Examples

The ultimate antagonist was revealed only in the final chapter of the novel.
As the ultimate antagonist, the villain posed a nearly insurmountable challenge to the heroes.
Players must defeat the ultimate antagonist to complete the last level of the game.

Synset Examples

In the game, Killary is the ultimate antagonist.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: antagonist - a character who actively opposes the protagonist
  • Antonym: protagonist - the main character or leading figure in a story
