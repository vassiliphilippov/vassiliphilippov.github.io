whore's-bird

1. NOUN

/ hɔɹz bɝːd / (AmE), / hɔːz bɜːd / (BrE)
A person, especially a man, who is promiscuous or frequently engages in casual sexual relationships.
promiscuous person
archaic slang

Examples

Everyone gossiped about the new employee, calling him a whore's-bird behind his back.
Despite his charming ways, she refused to date a known whore's-bird.
In old novels, the term whore's-bird was used for men with many lovers.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew he was a whore's-bird, never settling down.
She acted like a whore's-bird, flitting from guy to guy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
  • Synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently enters into casual sexual relationships

Etymology

From 'whore' (sexually promiscuous woman) + 'bird' (slang for person).
