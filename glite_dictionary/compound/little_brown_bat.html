little brown bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈbraʊn bæt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈbraʊn bat / (BrE)
a small, widespread species of North American bat, known for its brown fur and common presence
a common small North American bat species

Examples

The little brown bat often roosts in attics during the summer months.
Researchers studied the diet of the little brown bat in several forested areas.
Populations of the little brown bat have declined due to white-nose syndrome.
