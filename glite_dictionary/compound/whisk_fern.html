whisk fern

1. NOUN

/ wɪsk fɝn / (AmE), / wɪsk fɜːn / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the genus Psilotum, characterized by its brush-like appearance, lack of roots, and simple branching stems.
tropical plant resembling a whisk broom

Examples

The whisk fern thrives in humid tropical forests where soil nutrients are scarce.
Scientists find the whisk fern fascinating because it lacks true roots.
The whisk fern's unique appearance makes it popular among plant collectors.
