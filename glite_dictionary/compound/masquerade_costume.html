masquerade costume

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæskəˈreɪd ˈkɑːstum / (AmE), / ˌmæskəˈreɪd ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
a costume worn as a disguise at a masquerade party, often including a mask and elaborate clothing
disguise worn at a masquerade party

Examples

She wore a striking masquerade costume complete with a feathered mask to the ball.
It took him weeks to create the perfect masquerade costume for the annual party.
Everyone admired the intricate details on her masquerade costume as she entered the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: costume - clothing worn to represent a character or type
