double chin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl tʃɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl tʃɪn / (BrE)
A layer of fatty tissue under the chin that creates the appearance of a second chin.
a fold of fat under the chin

Examples

She tries to hide her double chin in photographs by tilting her head.
After losing weight, his double chin became much less noticeable.
Some people are self-conscious about having a double chin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chin - the lower part of the face below the mouth
