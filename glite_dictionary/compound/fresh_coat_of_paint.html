fresh coat of paint

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ ˈkoʊt əv peɪnt / (AmE), / frɛʃ kəʊt əv peɪnt / (BrE)
a newly applied layer of paint, typically used to improve or refresh the appearance of a surface
newly applied layer of paint

Examples

The kitchen looks much brighter after getting a fresh coat of paint on the walls.
Sometimes all a room needs is a fresh coat of paint to feel brand new.
He gave his old bicycle a fresh coat of paint to restore its original look.
