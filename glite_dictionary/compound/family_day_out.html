family day out

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli deɪ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli deɪ aʊt / (BrE)
a day spent together by a family engaging in leisure activities outside the home
day spent on leisure with family

Examples

We planned a family day out at the zoo to celebrate Emma's birthday.
A family day out to the seaside is always fun for everyone.
They enjoy having a family day out every month to reconnect and relax.

Synset Examples

Every Sunday is a family day out for us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outing - a short trip taken for leisure or pleasure
