stream orchid

1. NOUN

/ striːm ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / striːm ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
an orchid native to western North America, typically growing along streams or ponds, with leafy stems and greenish-brown and pinkish flowers.
a North American orchid found near streams

Examples

The stream orchid thrives in wet, shaded areas along creeks and ponds.
We found a rare stream orchid blooming beside the mountain stream.
Botanists documented the habitat of the stream orchid in western North America.
