pricing system

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪsɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈpraɪsɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a method or arrangement used to determine the prices of goods or services in a market or organization
method for setting prices

Examples

The company introduced a new pricing system to remain competitive in the market.
A transparent pricing system helps customers understand what they are paying for.
Governments often regulate the pricing system for essential utilities like water and electricity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pricing policy - a set of guidelines adopted by a business to establish prices
