under scrutiny

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndɚ ˈskruːtəni / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈskruːtəni / (BrE)
being carefully examined or observed, especially in order to judge or assess something
closely examined or observed

Examples

The company’s financial records are under scrutiny after reports of misconduct.
Every aspect of the new policy is under scrutiny by the media.
After the accident, the safety procedures came under scrutiny from regulatory agencies.

Synset Examples

The circumstances put her under immense scrutiny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under investigation - being formally investigated or analyzed
  • Synonym: under observation - being watched or monitored closely
