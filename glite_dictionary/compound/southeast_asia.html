Southeast Asia

1. PROPN

/ ˌsaʊθˌist ˈeɪʒə / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˌiːst ˈeɪʒə / (BrE)
a region of Asia comprising the countries south of China, east of India, including Indochina, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Singapore
region of Asia including Indochina, Indonesia, Philippines, and Singapore

Examples

She traveled across Southeast Asia for six months, exploring Thailand, Vietnam, and Cambodia.
The climate in Southeast Asia is typically hot and humid throughout the year.
Many spices commonly used in cooking originate from Southeast Asia.
