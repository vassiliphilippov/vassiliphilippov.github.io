honey-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈhʌni sɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the smell or fragrance of honey.
smelling of honey

Examples

The garden was filled with honey-scented blossoms that attracted bees and butterflies alike.
She chose a honey-scented candle to create a warm and inviting atmosphere in her living room.
After a rain shower, the air was honey-scented from the blooming wildflowers nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Synonym: honeyed - having the taste or smell of honey
