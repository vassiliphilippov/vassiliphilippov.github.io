Pessi attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛsi əˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈpɛsi əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude characterized by pessimism or a consistently negative outlook on situations or outcomes.
pessimistic or negative outlook
informal

Examples

Her Pessi attitude made it hard for the team to stay motivated.
Whenever something goes wrong, he blames it on his Pessi attitude.
You need to drop that Pessi attitude if you want to enjoy the trip.

Synset Examples

I can't stand his Pessi attitude during meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pessimism - tendency to see the worst or expect the worst

Etymology

Blend of 'pessi-' (shortened from 'pessimistic') and 'attitude'.
