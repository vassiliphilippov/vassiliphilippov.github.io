pitbull attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtbʊl ˈætəˌtud / (AmE), / ˈpɪtbʊl ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an extremely aggressive or determined manner of dealing with situations or people
very aggressive or determined approach

Examples

The team's pitbull attitude helped them overcome multiple setbacks during the project.
You need a pitbull attitude if you want to survive in the competitive world of sales.
His pitbull attitude made him both respected and feared by his colleagues.

Synset Examples

She has a pitbull attitude when it comes to negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenacity - the quality of being very determined; determination
  • Synonym: aggression - forceful or hostile behavior or attitude
  • Antonym: passivity - lack of active response or resistance

Etymology

From 'pitbull', a breed of dog known for its tenacity, used metaphorically to describe human behavior.
