slabbing mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæbɪŋ mɪl / (AmE), / ˈslæbɪŋ mɪl / (BrE)
a steel rolling mill used to produce slabs from ingots or blooms
machine for making steel slabs

Examples

The slabbing mill processes raw ingots into thick steel slabs for further rolling.
Workers monitored the slabbing mill to ensure the slabs met quality standards.
Upgrades to the slabbing mill have increased efficiency and reduced production costs.
