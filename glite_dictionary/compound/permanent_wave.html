permanent wave

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.mə.nənt weɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.mə.nənt weɪv / (BrE)
a method of setting hair in waves or curls that last for several months by treating it with heat and chemicals
hair styled with lasting curls or waves

Examples

She decided to get a permanent wave before her high school reunion.
The salon specializes in giving clients a natural-looking permanent wave.
My grandmother wore her hair in a tight permanent wave for years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perm - a hairstyle produced by setting the hair in waves or curls using chemicals
