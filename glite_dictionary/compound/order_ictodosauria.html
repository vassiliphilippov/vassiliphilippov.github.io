order Ictodosauria

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ɪktəˌdoʊˈsɔriə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ɪktəˌdəʊˈsɔːrɪə / (BrE)
an extinct order of reptiles that lived during the late Triassic period
extinct reptiles of the late Triassic

Examples

Fossils of the order Ictodosauria have been discovered in several parts of Europe and North America.
Scientists believe that members of the order Ictodosauria played a role in the ecosystem of the late Triassic seas.
The order Ictodosauria is of great interest to paleontologists studying the evolution of marine reptiles.
