home to

1. ADP

/ hoʊm tu / (AmE), / həʊm tʊ / (BrE)
serving as the natural place where someone or something lives or exists
serving as a habitat or dwelling place

Examples

The rainforest is home to a diverse array of plants and animals.
This antique house is home to a family of six.
The city is home to several world-famous museums.

Synset Examples

The tree trunk was hollow and home to many birds.
The garden was home to a large serpent.
The garden was home to a large toad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: habitat - the natural home of an animal, plant, or other organism
