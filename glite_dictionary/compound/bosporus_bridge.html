Bosporus Bridge

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːspərəs brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɒspərəs brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a suspension bridge in Istanbul, Turkey, spanning the Bosporus Strait and connecting the European and Asian parts of the city
major bridge over the Bosporus in Istanbul

Examples

The Bosporus Bridge is one of the most iconic landmarks in Istanbul.
Traffic on the Bosporus Bridge is often heavy during rush hour.
The marathon route crosses the Bosporus Bridge, linking two continents.

Etymology

From the name of the Bosporus Strait, from Greek Bosporos (meaning 'ox-ford'), combined with bridge.
