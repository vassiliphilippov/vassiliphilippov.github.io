rubber cement

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbər səˈmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə sɪˈmɛnt / (BrE)
a liquid adhesive made by dissolving unvulcanized rubber in a solvent, used for bonding paper and other materials
liquid adhesive made with rubber

Examples

The artist used rubber cement to mount her photographs onto the display board.
Please avoid getting rubber cement on your hands, as the solvent can be irritating.
He bought a bottle of rubber cement for his craft project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glue - a general adhesive substance used for sticking things together
  • Synonym: adhesive - a substance used for sticking objects or materials together
