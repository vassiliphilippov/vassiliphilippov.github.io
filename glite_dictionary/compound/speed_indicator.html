speed indicator

1. NOUN

/ spiːd ɪn.dəˌkeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / spiːd ɪn.dɪˌkeɪ.tə / (BrE)
a device fitted to a vehicle that measures and displays its speed
device showing a vehicle's speed

Examples

The speed indicator on the dashboard was slightly above the speed limit.
Older cars often used a dial-type speed indicator instead of a digital one.
He checked the speed indicator before accelerating on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedometer - an instrument in a vehicle that shows how fast it is going
