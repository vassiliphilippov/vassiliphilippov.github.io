date plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪt ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt ˌplʌm / (BrE)
An Asiatic persimmon tree (Diospyros lotus) cultivated for its small yellow or purplish-black edible fruit, valued particularly in Afghanistan and nearby regions.
edible fruit of an Asiatic persimmon tree

Examples

The small, sweet fruits of the date plum are enjoyed as a delicacy in some Central Asian countries.
Botanists often study the date plum for its resilience in dry climates.
She bought a basket of date plums at the market to make a traditional dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persimmon - a fruit from trees of the genus Diospyros
