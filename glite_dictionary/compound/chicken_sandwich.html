chicken sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˌsænwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˌsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of bread with a filling of sliced or cooked chicken.
sandwich made with chicken filling

Examples

I ordered a chicken sandwich and a cup of coffee for lunch.
The restaurant's grilled chicken sandwich is one of their most popular items.
She packed a chicken sandwich for her picnic at the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandwich - Two slices of bread with a filling between them
