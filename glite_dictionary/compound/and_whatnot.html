and whatnot

1. X

/ ænd ˈwʌt.nɑt / (AmE), / ənd ˈwɒt.nɒt / (BrE)
used to refer to additional things of a similar kind that have not been specified; and other such things.
and other similar things
informal

Examples

The garage was filled with old bikes, boxes, and whatnot that we hadn't used in years.
She likes hiking, painting, and whatnot during her free time.
Bring your notebook, pens, and whatnot to the meeting tomorrow.

Synset Examples

The store sells clothes, accessories, and whatnot.
They discussed politics, economics, and whatnot over dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: and so on - and other things of a similar type
  • Synonym: et cetera - and the rest; and so forth
