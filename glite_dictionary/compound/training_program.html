training program

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ˌproʊ.ɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ˌprəʊ.ɡræm / (BrE)
a planned series of activities or courses designed to teach or develop particular skills or knowledge
plan or course to teach specific skills

Examples

She enrolled in a training program to become a certified nurse.
The company launched a new training program for its employees last month.
Our training program includes both theoretical lessons and practical workshops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: course - a series of lessons or lectures on a particular subject
  • Synonym: training - the process of learning the skills needed for a particular job or activity
