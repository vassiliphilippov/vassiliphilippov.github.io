Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka

1. PROPN

/ dɛməˈkrætɪk ˈsoʊʃəlɪst rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ʃriː ˈlæŋkə / (AmE), / dɛməˈkratɪk ˈsɒʃəlɪst rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ʃriː ˈlæŋkə / (BrE)
The official name of Sri Lanka, a republic on the island of Ceylon in South Asia, which became independent from the United Kingdom in 1948.
