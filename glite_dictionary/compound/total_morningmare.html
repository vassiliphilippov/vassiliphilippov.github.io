total morningmare

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬əl ˈmɔrnɪŋˌmɛr / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈmɔːnɪŋˌmeə / (BrE)
a morning that goes disastrously or frustratingly wrong
disastrous or frustrating morning
informal

Examples

After spilling coffee on my shirt and missing the bus, my day started as a total morningmare.
The kids were late for school, the car wouldn't start—just another total morningmare.
She called it a total morningmare when her alarm failed and everything went wrong before work.

Synset Examples

Waking up late was a total morningmare for me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightmare - a frightening or unpleasant dream or situation

Etymology

Blend of 'morning' and 'nightmare', modeled after the informal use of 'nightmare'.
