Australian pine

1. NOUN

/ əˌstreɪliən paɪn / (AmE), / ɒˌstreɪliən paɪn / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Australia, often grown as an ornamental, with heavy, hard, red wood.
tropical Australian ornamental tree

Examples

The Australian pine is often planted along coastal areas to provide shade and windbreaks.
The furniture was crafted from the dense wood of the Australian pine.
Many parks feature Australian pine trees because of their fast growth and attractive appearance.
