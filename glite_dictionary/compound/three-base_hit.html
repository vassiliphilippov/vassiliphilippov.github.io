three-base hit

1. NOUN

/ ˌθriː beɪs hɪt / (AmE), / ˌθriː beɪs hɪt / (BrE)
(in baseball) a hit that allows the batter to reach third base safely.
baseball hit reaching third base

Examples

He thrilled the crowd with a three-base hit in the bottom of the ninth inning.
Her three-base hit helped the team take the lead.
The batter sprinted around the bases for a three-base hit during the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triple - (baseball) a three-base hit
