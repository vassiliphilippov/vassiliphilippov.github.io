armed forces

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmd ˌfɔrsɪz / (AmE), / ˈɑːmd ˌfɔːsɪz / (BrE)
the combined military organizations of a nation, including its army, navy, air force, and sometimes other branches
a nation's military organizations

Examples

The president ordered the armed forces to assist in disaster relief after the hurricane.
All citizens are required to serve in the armed forces for two years.
The country's armed forces held a parade to celebrate Independence Day.

Synset Examples

their military is the largest in the region
the military machine is the same one we faced in 1991 but now it is weaker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - the military forces of a country
