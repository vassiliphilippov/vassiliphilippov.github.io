genus Litchi

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈliː.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈlɪtʃ.i / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees in the family Sapindaceae, native to China and Southeast Asia, best known for producing the edible lychee fruit.
genus of tropical fruit trees

Examples

The genus Litchi is best known for the popular lychee fruit.
Botanists have classified several species within the genus Litchi, although Litchi chinensis is most commonly cultivated.
Research on the genus Litchi focuses on improving disease resistance and fruit yield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lychee - the edible fruit produced by trees of the genus Litchi

Etymology

From Latinized form of Cantonese 荔枝 (lèi jī).
