robbery suspect

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːbəri səˈspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈrɒbəri səˈspɛkt / (BrE)
a person who is believed to have committed or been involved in a robbery
someone suspected of a robbery

Examples

The police arrested a robbery suspect after reviewing surveillance footage.
Witnesses identified the robbery suspect from a lineup at the station.
During the investigation, officers questioned the robbery suspect about his whereabouts on the night of the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspect - a person thought to be guilty of a crime or offense
  • Synonym: robber - a person who commits robbery
