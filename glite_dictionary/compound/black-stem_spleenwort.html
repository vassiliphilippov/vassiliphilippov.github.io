black-stem spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk stɛm ˈspliːnwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˌblæk stɛm ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
a small fern of tropical America, recognized by its dark, slender stem, found from the southern United States to the West Indies and Brazil
fern with a dark stem, native to tropical America

Examples

The black-stem spleenwort thrives in shaded, rocky habitats across the southeastern United States.
Botanists catalogued several black-stem spleenworts growing along the riverbank in Jamaica.
She admired a cluster of black-stem spleenwort ferns during her hike in Brazil.
