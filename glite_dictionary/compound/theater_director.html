theater director

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiː.ət̬ɚ dɪˈrɛk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈθɪə.tə dɪˈrɛk.tə / (BrE)
A person responsible for supervising the actors and guiding the artistic and dramatic aspects of a stage production.
supervisor of stage production and actors

Examples

The theater director worked closely with the cast to bring the script to life.
Aspiring actors often seek advice from a well-respected theater director.
The production owes much of its success to the vision of its theater director.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: director - a person who supervises the creative aspects of a production
  • Antonym: producer - a person responsible for the business and managerial aspects of a production
