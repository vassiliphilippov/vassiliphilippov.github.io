false wintergreen

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈwɪntərˌɡrin / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈwɪntəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
An evergreen plant native to eastern North America, with leathery leaves and many white flowers.
North American evergreen plant with white flowers

Examples

The woods were full of false wintergreen growing beneath the taller trees.
Botanists often study the unique white flowers of the false wintergreen.
Hikers in eastern North America may recognize false wintergreen by its leathery green leaves.
