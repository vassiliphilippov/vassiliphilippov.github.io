physical education

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a school subject involving training and activities to develop physical fitness and care for the body, often including sports and exercises
school subject involving sports and exercise

Examples

My favorite subject at school has always been physical education because I love playing team sports.
The government is investing more in physical education to encourage healthier lifestyles among students.
All students are required to take physical education classes in their first two years of high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PE - abbreviation for physical education
