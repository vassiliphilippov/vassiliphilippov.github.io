angle bracket

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡəl ˈbrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡl ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
a metal support shaped like an L, used to join two parts at an angle, especially at a right angle
L-shaped metal support bracket

Examples

He secured the shelves to the wall using a sturdy angle bracket.
The table is reinforced underneath with two steel angle brackets.
You need an angle bracket to properly mount the cabinet corner.
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡəl ˌbrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡl ˌbrækɪt / (BrE)
either of the punctuation marks < or >, used in computer programming and sometimes to enclose text
punctuation mark: < or >

Examples

In HTML, tags are enclosed in angle brackets to define elements.
The programmer forgot to close one of the angle brackets in the code.
Some markup languages require you to use angle brackets around special commands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracket - a pair of marks [ ] or ( ) used to enclose words
