silvertop palmetto

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvərˌtɑp pælˈmɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvətɒp pælˈmɛtəʊ / (BrE)
A small, stocky fan palm native to southern Florida and Cuba, known for its silvery foliage.
fan palm of southern Florida and Cuba

Examples

The silvertop palmetto is often planted for its attractive, fan-shaped leaves.
Wildlife in southern Florida depend on the silvertop palmetto for food and shelter.
He recognized the silvertop palmetto by its distinctive silvery fronds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palm - tree or shrub of the family Arecaceae
