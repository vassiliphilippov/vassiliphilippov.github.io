clitoral vein

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪtəɹəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈklɪtərəl veɪn / (BrE)
Any of the veins that drain blood from the clitoris.
vein serving the clitoris
medical

Examples

The surgeon was careful to avoid damaging the clitoral vein during the procedure.
Because the clitoral vein is small and delicate, it can be difficult to identify in medical imaging.
An injury to the clitoral vein can lead to localized swelling or discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clitoris - female genital organ, sensitive erectile tissue
  • Synonym: vein - blood vessel carrying blood to the heart
