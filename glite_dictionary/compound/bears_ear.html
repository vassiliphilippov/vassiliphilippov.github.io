bear's ear

1. NOUN

/ bɛrz ɪr / (AmE), / bɛəz ɪə / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered primrose native to the Alps, often grown as an ornamental plant.
yellow-flowered alpine primrose

Examples

The gardener planted several bear's ear in the rock garden for their colorful blooms.
I admired the bright yellow flowers of the bear's ear during my hike in the Alps.
Many alpine enthusiasts cultivate bear's ear for its beauty and resilience.
