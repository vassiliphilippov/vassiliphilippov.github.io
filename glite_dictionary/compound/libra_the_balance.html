Libra the Balance

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːbrə ðə ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈliː.brə ðə ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
the seventh sign of the zodiac, represented by the scales, with the sun in this sign from about September 23 to October 22.
seventh sign of the zodiac (the Scales)

Examples

People born under Libra the Balance are said to value fairness and harmony.
The sun enters Libra the Balance around the autumn equinox each year.
She enjoys reading about the traits associated with Libra the Balance in astrology books.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Libra - the seventh sign of the zodiac, also called 'the Balance'

Etymology

From Latin 'Libra' (scales, balance), the astrological sign symbolized by a balance scale.
