dwarf nipplewort

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf ˈnɪpəlˌwɜrt / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈnɪpəlwɜːt / (BrE)
a small European herb (Minuartia hybrida) with small yellow flowers, found in grasslands and open habitats
a small European herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The dwarf nipplewort thrives in sandy soils throughout parts of central Europe.
Botanists recently discovered a rare population of dwarf nipplewort in a protected meadow.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate dwarf nipplewort for its delicate appearance and small yellow flowers.
