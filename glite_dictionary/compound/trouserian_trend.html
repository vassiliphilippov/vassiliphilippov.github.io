trouserian trend

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraʊzəriən trɛnd / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzəriən trɛnd / (BrE)
a fashion trend characterized by a distinctive style of trousers or trouser-focused outfits
a fashion style focused on trousers

The trouserian trend has inspired retailers to expand their pants selections.
