Barberton daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrbərtən ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbətən ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A widely cultivated South African perennial plant (Gerbera jamesonii) with large, brightly colored flower heads, typically orange or red.
bright South African daisy (Gerbera jamesonii)

Examples

The Barberton daisy adds vibrant color to gardens throughout the summer.
She received a bouquet featuring a bright orange Barberton daisy as the centerpiece.
Many florists use the Barberton daisy in arrangements for its bold appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerbera - a genus of plants including the Barberton daisy
  • Degree Higher: daisy - any of several plants of the family Asteraceae with showy flowers

Etymology

Named after Barberton, a town in South Africa, where the species is native.
