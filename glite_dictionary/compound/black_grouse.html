black grouse

1. NOUN

/ blæk ɡraʊs / (AmE), / blak ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a large Eurasian grouse, the male of which is bluish-black with a lyre-shaped tail
a large Eurasian grouse species

Examples

The male black grouse performs an elaborate display to attract a mate in spring.
We spotted a black grouse while hiking in the Scottish Highlands.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered black grouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capercaillie - a large woodland grouse, related species
