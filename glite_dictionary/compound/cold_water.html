cold water

1. NOUN

/ koʊld ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
discouragement or criticism intended to dampen enthusiasm for a plan, hope, or expectation
discouragement; disapproval of a plan

Examples

The committee threw cold water on the proposal for a new park.
Her negative comments really poured cold water on my excitement.
Whenever we suggest new ideas, he always throws cold water on them.

Synset Examples

she poured cold water on the whole idea of going to Africa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wet blanket - a person who discourages others or dampens enthusiasm
