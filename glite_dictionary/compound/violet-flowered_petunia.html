violet-flowered petunia

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌflaʊɚd pəˈtuniə / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˌflaʊəd pəˈtjuːniə / (BrE)
a petunia plant or small shrub with solitary violet to rose-red flowers
petunia with violet or rose flowers

Examples

The violet-flowered petunia brightened the garden with its vibrant blooms throughout spring.
She planted a row of violet-flowered petunias along the walkway to add a splash of color.
Horticulturists recommend the violet-flowered petunia for its hardiness and attractive appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petunia - a genus of flowering plants with colorful trumpet-shaped flowers
