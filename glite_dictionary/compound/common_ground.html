common ground

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən ˈɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈɡraʊnd / (BrE)
Shared beliefs, interests, or opinions that enable people or groups to reach an agreement or mutual understanding.
shared basis for agreement or understanding

Examples

The two parties found common ground on the issue of environmental protection.
Despite their differences, they managed to identify some common ground and work together.
Finding common ground is essential in building strong team relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agreement - harmony or shared opinion among people
  • Antonym: disagreement - lack of harmony or shared opinion
