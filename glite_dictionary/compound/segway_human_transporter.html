Segway (Human Transporter)

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˈhjuː.mən trænsˌpɔr.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˈhjuː.mən trænsˌpɔː.tə / (BrE)
A trademarked, self-balancing personal transportation device with two wheels, designed for use on level pedestrian surfaces.
two-wheeled self-balancing personal transporter

Examples

She rode her Segway (Human Transporter) across the city park to work.
Tours of the city are often offered using a Segway (Human Transporter).
After falling from his Segway (Human Transporter), he decided to wear a helmet next time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal transporter - a small motorized vehicle for individual transport

Etymology

Trademark from Segway Inc.; possibly a play on 'segue' (to move smoothly).
