panty girdle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.ti ˈɡɝː.dəl / (AmE), / ˈpæn.ti ˈɡɜː.dəl / (BrE)
a woman's undergarment that combines the support of a girdle with the coverage of panties
woman's shaping undergarment

Examples

She wore a panty girdle under her dress for extra support.
The store offers several styles of panty girdle to suit different needs.
In the 1960s, many women considered the panty girdle a wardrobe essential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girdle - an elastic corset for women
  • Synonym: panties - women's underwear covering the lower trunk and hips

Etymology

From 'panty' (shortened from 'panties') + 'girdle', meaning a supportive undergarment.
