American Revolutionary War

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri wɔr / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri wɔː / (BrE)
the war (1775–1783) in which the American colonies fought for independence from Great Britain
war for American independence (1775–1783)

Examples

Many historians have studied the causes and effects of the American Revolutionary War.
The colonies declared independence during the American Revolutionary War.
Key battles of the American Revolutionary War include Saratoga and Yorktown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: War of Independence - another term for the American Revolutionary War
  • Synonym: American Revolution - political and social movement that led to American independence
