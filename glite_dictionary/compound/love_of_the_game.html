love of the game

1. NOUN

/ lʌv əv ðə ɡeɪm / (AmE), / lʌv əv ðə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a strong passion or enthusiasm for a sport or activity, regardless of external rewards
passion for a sport or activity

Examples

She kept playing simply for the love of the game, not for fame or money.
Many fans admire athletes who compete because of their love of the game.
His dedication to coaching comes from his genuine love of the game.

Synset Examples

They do it for the love of the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - strong emotion or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: enthusiasm - intense and eager enjoyment
