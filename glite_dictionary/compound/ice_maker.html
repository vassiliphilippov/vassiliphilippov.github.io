ice maker

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈmeɪkɚ / (AmE), / aɪs ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
a device in a refrigerator or a separate appliance that automatically produces ice cubes
appliance for making ice cubes

Examples

The new refrigerator has an ice maker that produces fresh cubes every hour.
After dinner, she filled everyone's glasses with ice from the ice maker.
If your ice maker stops working, check the water supply line first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice machine - a larger, often commercial machine for making ice
