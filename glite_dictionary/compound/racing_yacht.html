racing yacht

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪŋ jɑt / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ jɒt / (BrE)
A fast, often expensive yacht designed or equipped specifically for racing, either under sail or powered by engine.
yacht designed for racing

Examples

He spent years restoring his racing yacht to compete in prestigious regattas.
The racing yacht sliced through the waves with impressive speed.
She joined the crew of a famous racing yacht for the summer season.
