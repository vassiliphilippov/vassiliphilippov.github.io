sleeping bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌbæɡ / (BrE)
A large padded and insulated bag for sleeping in, typically used outdoors or when camping.
padded bag for sleeping outdoors

Examples

I forgot to pack my sleeping bag for the camping trip.
She crawled into her sleeping bag as the temperature dropped at night.
Their tent was filled with colorful sleeping bags and backpacks.
