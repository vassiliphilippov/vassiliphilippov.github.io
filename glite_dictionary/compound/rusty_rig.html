rusty rig

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌsti rɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti rɪɡ / (BrE)
An Australian tree resembling the banyan, often planted for ornamental purposes; also introduced into South Africa for use as brushwood.
Australian tree resembling the banyan
british

Examples

A row of rusty rigs was planted along the driveway for shade.
The rusty rig is valued for its resilience in dry climates.
Gardeners recommend the rusty rig as an ornamental alternative to the banyan.
