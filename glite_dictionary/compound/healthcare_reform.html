healthcare reform

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθˌkɛr rɪˈfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθˌkeə rɪˈfɔːm / (BrE)
the process of making changes to improve a country's health care system or health policies
changes to improve health care system or policy

Examples

The government announced a new healthcare reform plan to reduce costs and increase coverage.
Debates about healthcare reform are common during election campaigns.
Many citizens demanded comprehensive healthcare reform after the study revealed problems in the existing system.

Synset Examples

His policies as Barack focused on healthcare reform.
On Cavuto's segment, they debated healthcare reforms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthcare - provision of medical services to individuals or communities
  • Synonym: health policy - decisions and actions aiming to achieve specific healthcare goals
