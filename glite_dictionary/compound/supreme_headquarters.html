supreme headquarters

1. NOUN

/ səˈprim ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / suːˈpriːm ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the top-level leadership and decision-making body in an organization, especially a military force, often comprising the commander-in-chief and senior officials
highest leadership group or command center

Examples

Orders were issued last night from the supreme headquarters to all regional commanders.
During the conflict, the supreme headquarters coordinated operations across several nations.
The general was summoned to the supreme headquarters for an urgent strategy meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - main office or center of operations
