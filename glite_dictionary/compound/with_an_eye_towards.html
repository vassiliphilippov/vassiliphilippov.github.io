with an eye towards

1. ADP

/ wɪð ən aɪ təˈwɔrdz / (AmE), / wɪð ən aɪ təˈwɔːdz / (BrE)
considering or planning for something, especially with a particular goal or outcome in mind
planning or considering with a future goal

Examples

She designed the room with an eye towards comfort and functionality.
They are developing the software with an eye towards future scalability.
He chose his classes with an eye towards a career in medicine.

Synset Examples

She approached the renovation with an eye towards sustainability.
