junior status

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːniər ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the state of holding a lower rank or position, especially as compared to others in an organization or hierarchy
state of being junior in rank or position

Examples

She was initially given junior status in the company, but quickly advanced to a managerial role.
After transferring schools, he had to accept junior status despite his previous experience.
New employees typically start with junior status and work their way up the corporate ladder.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: senior status - the state of holding a higher rank or longer service
