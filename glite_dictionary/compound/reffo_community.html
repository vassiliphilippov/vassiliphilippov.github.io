reffo community

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛfoʊ kəˈmjunɪti / (AmE), / ˈrɛfəʊ kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group or network of people in Australia who support refugees, often composed of former refugees and their descendants.
group supporting refugees in Australia
informal american

Examples

The reffo community organizes events to help newcomers adjust to Australian life.
Many members of the reffo community share stories of their migration experiences.
The reffo community in Melbourne is known for its cultural festivals.

Synset Examples

The reffo community supports new arrivals in Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refugee community - A community formed by or supporting refugees

Etymology

‘Reffo’ is an Australian slang form originating from ‘refugee’.
