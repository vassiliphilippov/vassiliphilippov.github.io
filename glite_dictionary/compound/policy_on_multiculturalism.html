policy on multiculturalism

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑləsi ɑn mʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɒləsi ɒn mʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəm / (BrE)
an official government strategy or set of rules concerning the support and management of cultural diversity within a society
government strategy regarding cultural diversity

Examples

The new government's policy on multiculturalism encourages the celebration of all cultural backgrounds.
She studied how France's policy on multiculturalism developed over the past decades.
Debates about immigration are often linked to a country's policy on multiculturalism.

Synset Examples

The country’s policy on multiculturalism has sparked debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalism - the presence of, or support for, several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society
