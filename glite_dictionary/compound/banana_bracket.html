banana bracket

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˈbrækɪt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
A humorous term for an unpredictable or unconventional tournament structure, especially one that defies typical expectations or logical prediction.
unpredictable tournament structure (humorous)
informal

Examples

The office pool used a banana bracket, making it nearly impossible to predict a winner.
Every year, fans joke about how their banana bracket gets busted in the first round.
Analysts laughed at the coach's suggestion to follow a banana bracket instead of traditional seeding.

Synset Examples

The tournament's banana bracket is full of surprises.
His predictions for the banana bracket were completely wrong.
She filled out her banana bracket with great enthusiasm.
