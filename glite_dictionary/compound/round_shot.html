round shot

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd ʃɑt / (AmE), / raʊnd ʃɒt / (BrE)
A solid spherical projectile formerly fired from a cannon.
solid spherical cannon projectile

Examples

The fortress walls were battered by round shot throughout the siege.
During the battle, the ship was struck by a massive round shot.
Archaeologists found several round shots buried near the old battlefield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cannonball - A heavy, round metal ball shot from a cannon
