house agent

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / haʊs ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange for the sale or letting of houses and other properties
agent who arranges property sales or lettings
british

Examples

The house agent showed us several flats in the city center.
After speaking with the house agent, they decided to make an offer on the cottage.
Many house agents advertise available rental properties in local newspapers.

Synset Examples

in England they call a real estate agent a ‘land agent’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real estate agent - a person whose business is selling houses, land, etc. for clients
  • Synonym: land agent - in England, an agent who manages or sells land and property
