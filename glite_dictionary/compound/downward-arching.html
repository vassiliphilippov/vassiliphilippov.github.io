downward-arching

1. ADJ

/ ˈdaʊnwɚd ˈɑrtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnwəd ˈɑːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Curving or bending in a downward direction.
curving downward

Examples

The elegant bridge has a downward-arching structure that spans the river.
She admired the downward-arching branches of the willow tree.
The roof’s downward-arching design helps channel rainwater efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upward-arching - curving upwards
