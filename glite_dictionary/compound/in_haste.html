in haste

1. ADV

/ ɪn heɪst / (AmE), / ɪn heɪst / (BrE)
in a hurried or hasty manner; quickly, often without careful thought
hurriedly, without delay

Examples

She left the office in haste, forgetting her umbrella behind.
The contract was signed in haste and later caused many problems.
He packed his bags in haste when he heard the news.

Synset Examples

the way they buried him so hurriedly was disgraceful
hastily, he scanned the headlines
sold in haste and at a sacrifice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hastily - in a hurried or hasty manner
  • Antonym: slowly - in a slow or unhurried manner
