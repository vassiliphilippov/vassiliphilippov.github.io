monkey pinscher

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ˈpɪn(t)ʃər / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˈpɪn(t)ʃə / (BrE)
A small European breed of dog, resembling a terrier, with rough dark hair and a tufted muzzle; also known as the Affenpinscher.
small, wiry-haired European dog breed

Examples

The monkey pinscher is known for its curious, lively nature and unique appearance.
She adopted a monkey pinscher from the animal shelter last spring.
Grooming a monkey pinscher can be a challenge due to its wiry coat and bushy face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Affenpinscher - Toy dog breed with a monkey-like expression

Etymology

From German 'Affenpinscher' meaning 'monkey-like terrier', due to its facial appearance; 'Affe' means 'monkey' and 'Pinscher' means 'terrier' or 'biter'.
