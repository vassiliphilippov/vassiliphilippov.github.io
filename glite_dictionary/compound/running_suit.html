running suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ suːt / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ sjuːt / (BrE)
A set of matching jacket and pants, typically made of absorbent fabric, worn by joggers while running or exercising.
matching athletic jacket and pants for jogging

Examples

He wore his favorite running suit during the chilly morning jog.
She bought a new running suit made of lightweight, breathable material.
Most athletes prefer a running suit that absorbs sweat quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - matching jacket and trousers for athletic use
