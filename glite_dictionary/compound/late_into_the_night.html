late into the night

1. ADV

/ leɪt ˈɪntu ðə naɪt / (AmE), / leɪt ˈɪntə ðə naɪt / (BrE)
until a very late hour at night; for much of the night.
until a very late hour at night

Examples

She stayed up late into the night finishing her essay.
The children played late into the night during the summer holiday.
He worked late into the night trying to solve the problem.

Synset Examples

The smell of the lamp filled the room as he read late into the night.
