oyster dressing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of stuffing made with oysters, typically used as a side dish or filling for poultry or seafood.
stuffing made with oysters

Examples

She served oyster dressing alongside the roasted turkey at Thanksgiving dinner.
Some Southern recipes for oyster dressing include celery, onions, and day-old bread.
The restaurant’s signature dish is chicken stuffed with oyster dressing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffing - a mixture used to fill meat or vegetables before cooking
  • Synonym: oyster stuffing - stuffing that contains oysters, often used interchangeably with oyster dressing
