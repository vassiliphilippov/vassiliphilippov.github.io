bullshit job

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌʃɪt dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈbʊlʃɪt dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job that is considered pointless, unfulfilling, or lacking any real value or meaning
pointless or meaningless job
vulgar

Examples

Many people remain trapped in a bullshit job just to pay the bills.
He finally left his bullshit job to pursue his passion in writing.
After years of doing a bullshit job, she decided to start her own business.

Synset Examples

Many consider that role a bullshit job.
She felt stuck in a bullshit job with no future.
He quit his bullshit job for something meaningful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead-end job - a job with no prospects for advancement or improvement

Etymology

Popularized by anthropologist David Graeber in his 2018 book 'Bullshit Jobs'.
