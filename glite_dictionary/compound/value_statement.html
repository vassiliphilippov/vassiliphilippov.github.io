value statement

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæl.ju ˌsteɪt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈvæljuː ˌsteɪt.mənt / (BrE)
a statement that expresses the desirability or worth of something, often reflecting beliefs or principles
statement expressing desirability or principles

Examples

The company's value statement emphasizes integrity and respect for customers.
A clear value statement can help guide employees' behavior in difficult situations.
Education is often promoted through value statements about its importance to society.
