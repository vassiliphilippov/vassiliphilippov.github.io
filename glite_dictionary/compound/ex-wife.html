ex-wife

1. NOUN

/ ɛksˈwaɪf / (AmE), / ɛksˈwaɪf / (BrE)
a woman who was formerly married to a particular man
former wife of a man

Examples

His ex-wife still lives in the same neighborhood as him.
After the divorce, he remained friends with his ex-wife.
The party became awkward when both his current wife and ex-wife showed up.

Synset Examples

all his exes live in Texas

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ex-husband - man who was formerly a particular woman's husband
  • Degree Lower: wife - married woman

Etymology

Formed from 'ex-' (former) and 'wife'.
