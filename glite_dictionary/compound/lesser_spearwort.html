lesser spearwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈspɪrˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈspɪəwɔːt / (BrE)
A semiaquatic Eurasian perennial plant of the buttercup family, with spear-shaped leaves, naturalized in New Zealand.
semiaquatic Eurasian plant with spear-shaped leaves

Examples

The pond was fringed with lesser spearwort and rushes.
Botanists easily identified the lesser spearwort by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
In New Zealand, lesser spearwort has become naturalized along wet riverbanks.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: greater spearwort - similar aquatic plant but larger in size

Etymology

From the English 'lesser' (smaller) + 'spearwort' (referring to spear-shaped leaves and 'wort' meaning plant).
