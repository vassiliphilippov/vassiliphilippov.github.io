musical performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
the act of presenting music before an audience, typically as a live event or recital
live presentation of music

Examples

The orchestra gave a stunning musical performance last night at the concert hall.
She attended a live musical performance featuring her favorite jazz band.
Many students participated in the annual musical performance organized by the school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concert - a public musical performance
  • Synonym: recital - a performance of music or dance, often by a soloist
