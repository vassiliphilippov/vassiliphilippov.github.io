powerful priestess

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚfəl priˈstɛs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊəfəl priːˈstɛs / (BrE)
a priestess believed to possess great magical or spiritual powers
a priestess with great magical powers

Examples

Legends tell of a powerful priestess who protected the village from harm.
The powerful priestess used her abilities to heal the sick and bless the crops.
In the story, a powerful priestess challenges the king's authority with her wisdom.

Synset Examples

The old legend spoke of a powerful priestess who could heal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: priestess - a female priest, especially in ancient religions
