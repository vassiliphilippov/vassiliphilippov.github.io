animal order

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a rank in the classification of animals, below class and above family, grouping together related families
taxonomic rank in animal classification

Examples

The domestic cat belongs to the animal order Carnivora.
Primates is an animal order that includes monkeys, apes, and humans.
Scientists debated where to classify a newly discovered species within its animal order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: order - a taxonomic rank used in biological classification
