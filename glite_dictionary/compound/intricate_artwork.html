intricate artwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.trɪ.kət ˈɑrt.wɝk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trɪ.kət ˈɑːt.wɜːk / (BrE)
artistic work characterized by complex and detailed patterns or design.
detailed and complex artistic work

Examples

The museum displayed several intricate artwork pieces from the Baroque period.
She spent months creating an intricate artwork inspired by nature.
Tourists marveled at the intricate artwork carved into the palace doors.

Synset Examples

He admired the intricate artwork on the outwall of the ancient temple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornate - elaborately decorated, especially in art or architecture
  • Antonym: simple artwork - artistic work having few details or complexity
