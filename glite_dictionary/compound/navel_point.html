navel point

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvəl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the central point or midpoint of something; the figurative center.
central or middle point

Examples

The town square was often referred to as the navel point of the community.
Many philosophers debated whether the temple was truly the navel point of the world.
Some cultures mark a special spot as the navel point from which all distances are measured.

Synset Examples

the Incas believed that Cuzco was the navel of the universe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center - the middle point of something
  • Antonym: periphery - the outer edge or boundary

Etymology

Formed from 'navel' (the belly button, often used figuratively for the center) and 'point'.
