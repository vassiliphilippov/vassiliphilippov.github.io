crested myna

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛstɪd ˈmaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a dark brown bird of southeastern Asia, recognized by its prominent crest and often found in urban areas
species of crested bird native to southeastern Asia

Examples

The crested myna is easily recognized by its distinctive tuft of feathers on its head.
In some cities, the crested myna has adapted well to urban environments.
We spotted a pair of crested mynas near the temple during our trip to southern China.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myna - an Asian and Australasian starling

Etymology

From 'crested' (having a crest) and 'myna', from Hindi mainā, a starling-like bird.
