skin color

1. NOUN

/ skɪn ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / skɪn ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
the natural color of a person's skin, especially as a visible feature of the face
the natural color of a person's skin

Examples

Her skin color changed slightly after she spent the summer outdoors.
The doctor checked for any unusual changes in his skin color.
People come in many different skin colors and shades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complexion - the natural color, texture, and appearance of the skin, especially of the face
