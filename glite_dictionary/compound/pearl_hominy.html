pearl hominy

1. NOUN

/ pɜrl ˈhɑmɪni / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈhɒmɪni / (BrE)
hominy that has been milled into medium-sized pellets, typically used in cooking
hominy milled into medium-sized pellets

Examples

The recipe calls for a cup of pearl hominy to be added to the stew.
She found pearl hominy at the specialty grocery store downtown.
Traditional Southern dishes often feature pearl hominy as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hominy - hulled corn kernels, with hull and germ removed, used as food
