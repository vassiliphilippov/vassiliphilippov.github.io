Lefebvrist community

1. NOUN

/ ləˈfɛv.rɪst kəˈmjun.ə.ti / (AmE), / ləˈfɛv.rɪst kəˈmjuː.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
a group of people who follow the traditionalist Catholic teachings of Archbishop Lefebvre
followers of Archbishop Lefebvre's teachings

Examples

The Lefebvrist community gathered for a special mass to celebrate an important feast day.
Debates between the Lefebvrist community and other Catholics often center on liturgical practices.
After Vatican II, the Lefebvrist community sought to preserve older forms of worship.

Synset Examples

The Lefebvrist community gathered for a special mass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traditionalist - a person who supports tradition and resists change
  • Antonym: mainstream Catholic - members of the Catholic Church who adhere to Vatican II reforms

Etymology

From the name of Archbishop Marcel Lefebvre (1905–1991), a French Roman Catholic archbishop known for traditionalist views, combined with 'community'.
