total nubcake

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊtəl ˈnʌbˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈnʌbˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A complete fool or an extremely incompetent person, especially in informal or online contexts.
a complete fool or incompetent person
slang informal

Examples

Don't be such a total nubcake when trying out this new game.
After missing the bus twice, I felt like a total nubcake.
Everyone at the table laughed when she called herself a total nubcake for forgetting her keys.

Synset Examples

After missing the meeting, he felt like a total nubcake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noob - an inexperienced or new person, especially online
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking sense or judgment

Etymology

Formed from 'nub' (slang for a novice) and 'cake' (as in 'noobcake', a playful insult in online gaming).
