Statistical Commission

1. PROPN

/ stəˈtɪstɪkəl kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the main body of the United Nations responsible for coordinating international statistical activities and standards among member states.
UN body for international statistics

Examples

The Statistical Commission meets annually to set statistical standards and priorities for member countries.
Changes in global economic reporting were discussed by the Statistical Commission earlier this year.
Delegates from each country presented their data collection methods to the Statistical Commission.
