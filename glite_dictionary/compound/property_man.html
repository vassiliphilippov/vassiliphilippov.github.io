property man

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpərti mæn / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəti mæn / (BrE)
a male member of the stage crew responsible for managing props in a theatrical production
male stage crew member in charge of props

Examples

The property man ensured that all the necessary items were placed on stage before each scene.
As a property man, he took great pride in his careful organization of the props room.
During rehearsal, the director called for the property man to bring out the antique suitcase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: property master - person responsible for props, regardless of gender
