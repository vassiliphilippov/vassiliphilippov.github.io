Crater Lake National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈkreɪtər leɪk ˌnæʃəˈnæl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkreɪtə leɪk ˌnæʃəˈnɑːl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Oregon, USA, known for containing the deepest lake in the United States, formed in the caldera of an extinct volcano
national park in Oregon with deepest lake in the US

Examples

Many tourists travel to Oregon each year to visit Crater Lake National Park and see its deep blue waters.
The stunning scenery at Crater Lake National Park attracts photographers and nature lovers from around the world.
Scientists study the unique ecosystem within Crater Lake National Park to learn about volcanic lakes.
