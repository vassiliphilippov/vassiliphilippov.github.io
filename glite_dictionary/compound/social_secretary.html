social secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
a person employed to manage someone's social correspondence and appointments
person managing social correspondence and events

Examples

She hired a social secretary to help organize her frequent charity events and dinner parties.
As the ambassador's social secretary, he coordinated all of her official invitations and receptions.
The busy celebrity relies on her social secretary to keep her schedule running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: secretary - a person employed to handle correspondence and routine office tasks
