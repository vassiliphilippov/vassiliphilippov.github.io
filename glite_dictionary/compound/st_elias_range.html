St. Elias Range

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ɪˈlaɪ.əs reɪndʒ / (AmE), / seɪnt ɪˈlaɪ.əs reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a range of mountains along the border between Alaska and the Yukon Territory, known for high peaks including Mount St. Elias.
mountain range on Alaska–Yukon border

Examples

The St. Elias Range features some of the highest peaks in North America.
Glaciers in the St. Elias Range are studied for their impact on sea levels.
Mountaineers are drawn to the St. Elias Range for its challenging climbs and remote beauty.

Etymology

Named after Mount St. Elias, which was named by explorers after the biblical prophet Elias (Elijah).
