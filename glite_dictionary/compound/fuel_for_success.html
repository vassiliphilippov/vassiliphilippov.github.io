fuel for success

1. NOUN

/ fjuːəl fər səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / fjʊəl fə səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
something that motivates or inspires someone to achieve success or make progress
motivating force behind achievement

Examples

Her passion for music became the fuel for success in her career.
Hard work and determination are often the fuel for success in business.
Support from friends acted as the fuel for success during his challenging journey.

Synset Examples

Her passion for art became the fuel for her success.
Coffee is my fuel for the long workday ahead.
The team's enthusiasm served as fuel for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motivation - the reason or reasons for acting or behaving in a particular way
  • Synonym: inspiration - a sudden brilliant or timely idea or stimulus for activity
