employee-owned business

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˌplɔɪ.i ˈoʊnd ˈbɪz.nəs / (AmE), / ɪmˌplɔɪ.i ˈəʊnd ˈbɪz.nɪs / (BrE)
A business in which the employees hold significant or complete ownership of the company.
business owned by its employees

Examples

The company transitioned to an employee-owned business after the founder retired.
Working at an employee-owned business often leads to increased staff engagement and loyalty.
Several prominent supermarkets in the country operate as employee-owned businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooperative - a business owned and run by its members
