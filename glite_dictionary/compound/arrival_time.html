arrival time

1. NOUN

/ əˈraɪvəl taɪm / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl taɪm / (BrE)
the scheduled or expected time at which a vehicle or person arrives at a particular place
scheduled or expected time of arrival

Examples

The train's arrival time is listed as 3:45 PM on the schedule.
Please check the arrival time before you head to the airport.
Due to the storm, the arrival time of our flight was delayed by two hours.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: departure time - the scheduled or expected time of leaving a place
