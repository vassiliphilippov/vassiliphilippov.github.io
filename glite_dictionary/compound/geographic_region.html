geographic region

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk ˈriː.dʒən / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk ˈriː.dʒən / (BrE)
An area of the Earth's surface that is defined by natural, physical, or human-made boundaries.
area defined by physical or human features

Examples

The Amazon rainforest is the largest geographic region of tropical forest in the world.
This project focuses on economic development in a specific geographic region of Africa.
Certain diseases are more common in one geographic region than in others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: region - an area, especially part of a country or the world
