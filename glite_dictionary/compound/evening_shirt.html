evening shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ ˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ ˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A man's formal white shirt with a stiff, starched front, typically worn with evening dress such as a tuxedo.
formal men's shirt for evening wear

Examples

He paired his black tuxedo with a crisp evening shirt and a bow tie.
Traditional evening shirts often have pleated fronts for added elegance.
Remember to wear an evening shirt to the formal dinner tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dress shirt - a formal shirt, typically with a collar and cuffs, worn with suits or formal attire
