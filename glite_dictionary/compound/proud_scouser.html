proud Scouser

1. NOUN

/ praʊd ˈskaʊsər / (AmE), / praʊd ˈskaʊsə / (BrE)
A person from Liverpool, UK, who feels a strong sense of pride in their city or heritage.
a person from Liverpool proud of their roots
informal british

Examples

He's a proud Scouser who cheers for Liverpool FC at every match.
As a proud Scouser, she wears her accent like a badge of honor.
Many proud Scousers celebrate their city's music and cultural influence.

Synset Examples

As a proud Scouser, he loved sharing his city's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scouser - a person from Liverpool, UK

Etymology

Scouser: from 'scouse', a type of stew associated with Liverpool, plus -er for a person from the city.
