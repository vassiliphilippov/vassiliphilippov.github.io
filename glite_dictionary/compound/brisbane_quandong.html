Brisbane quandong

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪzˌbeɪn ˈkwɒn.dɒŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪz.bən ˈkwɒn.dɒŋ / (BrE)
an Australian tree with hard white timber, glossy green leaves, white flowers, and one-seeded glossy blue fruit
Australian tree with blue fruit and white timber
british

Examples

The Brisbane quandong produces a distinctive blue fruit that is attractive to birds.
Furniture made from Brisbane quandong wood is highly valued for its durability.
We spotted a tall Brisbane quandong during our bushwalk near Brisbane.

Etymology

From 'Brisbane', the Australian city, and Wiradjuri 'quandong', a name used for several native fruit-bearing trees.
