kitchen garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən ˈɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃᵊn ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
a garden, typically near a house, where vegetables, herbs, and sometimes fruit are grown for household use
small garden for growing vegetables and herbs

Examples

She enjoys picking fresh tomatoes from her kitchen garden every morning.
The kitchen garden behind the cottage is filled with herbs and lettuces.
He started a small kitchen garden to provide his family with organic vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable garden - a garden where vegetables are grown
