device characteristic

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvaɪs ˌkɛrəktəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
a measurable property of a device, typically determined under specific conditions
measurable property of a device

Examples

The voltage-current device characteristic is an important parameter for engineers to consider.
Before selecting a component, it is essential to analyze its device characteristics under different operating conditions.
Manufacturers publish device characteristic curves to help users understand product performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specification - an explicit set of requirements to be satisfied by a material, design, product, or service
