police van

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis væn / (AmE), / pəˈliːs væn / (BrE)
a van used by the police to transport prisoners or detainees
van for transporting prisoners

Examples

The suspect was loaded into the police van after his arrest.
Several police vans were parked outside the courthouse.
Protesters were escorted to a police van for questioning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paddy wagon - police vehicle for transporting prisoners
  • Degree Higher: van - a vehicle larger than a car, used for transporting goods or people
