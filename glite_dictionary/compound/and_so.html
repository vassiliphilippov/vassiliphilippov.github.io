and so

1. ADV

/ ænd soʊ / (AmE), / ənd səʊ / (BrE)
used to introduce what happens next, or to indicate a result or consequence.
introduces a consequence or what happens next

Examples

She missed the bus, and so she arrived late to the meeting.
It started to rain, and so we moved the picnic indoors.
He forgot his umbrella, and so he got wet walking home.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: then - used to introduce what happens next or as a consequence
