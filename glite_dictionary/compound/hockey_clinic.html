hockey clinic

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a session or event where hockey players receive specialized coaching and evaluation to improve their skills
specialized hockey training session

Examples

She attended a hockey clinic over the weekend to improve her defensive skills.
The coach organized a hockey clinic for local youth players.
After the hockey clinic, the players felt more confident on the ice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training camp - a camp where athletes train intensively in a sport
