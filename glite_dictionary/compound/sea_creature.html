sea creature

1. NOUN

/ si ˈkriːtʃɚ / (AmE), / siː ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
an animal that lives in the sea, such as a fish, mollusc, or marine mammal
animal living in the sea

Examples

The divers were amazed by the colorful sea creature swimming near the coral reef.
Children are often fascinated by stories about mysterious sea creatures lurking in the deep ocean.
The aquarium has a special exhibit dedicated to rare sea creatures from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marine animal - an animal that lives in the sea
