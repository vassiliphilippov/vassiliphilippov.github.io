bed cover

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛd ˌkʌv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛd ˌkʌv.ə / (BrE)
A decorative or protective covering placed over a bed, often for ornament or cleanliness.
covering placed over a bed

Examples

She chose a new bed cover with a floral pattern for her bedroom.
The hotel room's bed cover was neatly folded at the foot of the bed.
Cleaning the bed cover regularly helps keep dust and allergens away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedspread - a decorative cloth used to cover a bed
  • Synonym: blanket - a large piece of fabric used for warmth on a bed
