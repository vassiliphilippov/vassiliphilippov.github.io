bulwark for human rights

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌwɑrk fər ˈhjuːmən raɪts / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.wək fə ˈhjuːmən raɪts / (BrE)
an organization, action, or person that provides strong support or protection for human rights
strong support for human rights

Examples

The international court is often seen as a bulwark for human rights in global politics.
Journalists sometimes act as a bulwark for human rights by exposing injustice.
Many believe that free speech is a crucial bulwark for human rights in any democracy.

Synset Examples

The organization serves as a bulwark for human rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulwark - a person, institution, or principle that acts as a defense
