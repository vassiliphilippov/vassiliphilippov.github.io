Big Sur

1. PROPN

/ bɪɡ sɝ / (AmE), / bɪɡ sɜː / (BrE)
a scenic coastal region in central California, renowned for its dramatic cliffs, rugged coastline, and natural beauty
scenic coastal region in California

Examples

We spent the weekend exploring the breathtaking views of Big Sur.
Artists and writers have long been inspired by the rugged landscapes of Big Sur.
The drive along Highway 1 through Big Sur is considered one of the most beautiful in the world.
