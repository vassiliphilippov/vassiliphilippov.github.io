stupid-head

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːpɪdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪdˌhɛd / (BrE)
(informal, childish) A silly or foolish person, often used as a playful insult among children.
playful insult for a silly or foolish person
informal

Examples

Stop calling your sister a stupid-head, it's not nice.
He felt like a real stupid-head after messing up the simple instructions.
Sometimes we tease each other by saying, 'Don't be such a stupid-head!'

Synset Examples

He called me a stupid-head for forgetting his birthday.
Don't be a stupid-head; it's just a game.
Stop being a stupid-head and listen to me!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dummy - a person who is not very intelligent or is easily tricked
  • Synonym: fool - a person who behaves in a silly way
