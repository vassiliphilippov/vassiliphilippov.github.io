slow-burn thriller

1. NOUN

/ ˌsloʊ ˈbɝːn ˈθrɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˌsləʊ ˈbɜːn ˈθrɪlə / (BrE)
A thriller, especially a film or book, in which suspense and tension are developed gradually rather than through fast-paced action.
thriller with gradually building suspense

Examples

The director's latest film is a slow-burn thriller that rewards patient viewers with a powerful climax.
Critics praised the novel as a slow-burn thriller that keeps readers on edge without relying on constant action.
If you enjoy stories that unfold gradually, you'll love this slow-burn thriller set in rural France.

Synset Examples

The movie was a slow-burn thriller that kept me guessing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriller - a novel, play, or movie with an exciting plot, typically involving crime or espionage
