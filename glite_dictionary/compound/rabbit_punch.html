rabbit punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈræb.ɪt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈræb.ɪt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A short, chopping blow delivered to the back of the neck, especially one considered illegal in boxing or fighting sports.
illegal blow to back of the neck

Examples

Delivering a rabbit punch in boxing is both dangerous and illegal.
The referee warned him after he threw a rabbit punch at his opponent's neck.
He collapsed after receiving a sudden rabbit punch during the fight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punch - a blow with the fist

Etymology

Named for the method used by hunters to kill rabbits quickly by striking the back of the neck.
