blue-headed vireo

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈhɛdɪd ˈvɪrioʊ / (AmE), / bluː ˈhɛdɪd ˈvɪriəʊ / (BrE)
a small songbird of northeastern North America, having a bluish-gray head and olive-green back, belonging to the vireo family
songbird with bluish-gray head

Examples

The blue-headed vireo is often spotted in mixed forests during migration season.
We observed a blue-headed vireo building its nest high in a spruce tree.
Birdwatchers were excited to hear the distinct song of the blue-headed vireo early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vireo - any of several small to medium-sized songbirds of the Americas
