Bolognese pasta sauce

1. NOUN

/ bəˌloʊnˈjeɪz ˈpɑstə sɔs / (AmE), / bəˌlɒnˈjeɪz ˈpɑːstə sɔːs / (BrE)
a rich Italian sauce for pasta, made with ground beef, tomatoes, vegetables, and sometimes mushrooms and ham
Italian meat-based pasta sauce

Examples

He made a delicious lasagna using his grandmother's secret Bolognese pasta sauce recipe.
I prefer spaghetti with Bolognese pasta sauce over other pasta dishes.
The restaurant's Bolognese pasta sauce is slow-cooked for hours for a richer flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ragù - Italian meat-based sauce for pasta

Etymology

From Italian 'alla bolognese', meaning 'in the style of Bologna'.
