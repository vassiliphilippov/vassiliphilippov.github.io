to cast your vote

1. VERB

/ kæst jʊr voʊt / (AmE), / kɑːst jɔː vəʊt / (BrE)
to formally submit your vote in an election or decision-making process
formally submit a vote

Examples

Remember to cast your vote at the polling station before it closes.
She was excited to finally cast her vote in the presidential election.
Citizens are encouraged to cast their vote on important issues affecting the community.

Synset Examples

Make sure to cast your vote before the deadline.
It's important to cast your vote in every election.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vote - a formal expression of choice in an election or meeting
