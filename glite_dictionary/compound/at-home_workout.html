at-home workout

1. NOUN

/ æt hoʊm ˈwɝːk.aʊt / (AmE), / at həʊm ˈwɜːk.aʊt / (BrE)
an exercise routine or session performed at home rather than at a gym or fitness facility
exercise routine done at home

Examples

After a long day at work, she prefers an at-home workout rather than going to the gym.
During the pandemic, many people switched to an at-home workout to stay active.
He found an effective at-home workout that requires no equipment at all.

Synset Examples

They have an at-home workout plan for beginners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workout - a session of physical exercise
