in virtue of

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈvɝːtʃu əv / (AmE), / ɪn ˈvɜːtjuː əv / (BrE)
because of; by reason of
because of; due to

Examples

He received the award in virtue of his dedication and years of service.
The contract was considered valid in virtue of the parties' agreement.
She was permitted entry in virtue of her official position.

Synset Examples

He received the award in virtue of his dedication.
She was promoted in virtue of her hard work.
In virtue of their friendship, he trusted her completely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: because of - on account of; due to
  • Synonym: by reason of - as a result of; because of
