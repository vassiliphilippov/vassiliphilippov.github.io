play-box

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ.bɑks / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.bɒks / (BrE)
A box used by a child, especially at a boarding school, to store toys and personal belongings.
box for child's toys and things

Examples

At boarding school, each student kept their treasures locked safely in a play-box.
She opened her old play-box to find childhood memories tucked away inside.
His play-box was filled with comics, small toys, and handwritten letters from home.
