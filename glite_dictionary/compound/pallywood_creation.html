Pallywood creation

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæliˌwʊd kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpæliˌwʊd kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a piece of media content, especially video or photographs, alleged to have been staged or manipulated by Palestinians for propaganda purposes
alleged staged Palestinian media content

Examples

The spokesperson dismissed the video as merely a Pallywood creation intended to sway international opinion.
Some analysts argue that labeling every controversial clip a Pallywood creation undermines legitimate reporting.
The journalist was criticized for sharing what many believed to be a Pallywood creation without verifying its authenticity.

Synset Examples

He dismissed the viral video as just another Pallywood creation.
The filmmaker accused them of using Pallywood tactics in the documentary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propaganda - information especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote a political cause
  • Synonym: disinformation - false information spread deliberately to deceive

Etymology

Blend of 'Palestinian' and 'Hollywood', referring to the perceived staging of events similar to film productions, and 'creation' meaning something made.
