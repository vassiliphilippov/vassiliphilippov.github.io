red diaper baby

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈdaɪpɚ ˌbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈdaɪpə ˌbeɪbi / (BrE)
A person who was raised in a politically leftist or radical environment, especially as the child of Communist or socialist parents.
child raised in a leftist household
informal american

Examples

Growing up as a red diaper baby, she attended protests with her parents from a young age.
Many red diaper babies find their upbringing influences their political views as adults.
He proudly embraced his identity as a red diaper baby during college debates.

Synset Examples

As a red diaper baby, he grew up surrounded by activism.
Being a red diaper baby influenced her views on social justice.
He was a red diaper baby, raised in a politically active family.
The red-diaper kid became an activist later in life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leftist - A person with left-wing political views
  • Synonym: communist - A person who supports or believes in the principles of communism
  • Antonym: conservative - A person with right-wing political views; opposite political background

Etymology

From 'red' (a common term for Communists and leftists) plus 'diaper' (referring to infancy), popularized in the mid-20th century in the US.
