purple-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl waɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl waɪt / (BrE)
White in color with a tinge or shade of purple.
white tinged with purple

Examples

The petals turned a delicate purple-white as the flower matured.
She chose a purple-white fabric for the wedding dress, giving it a subtle, unique glow.
Morning mist gave the garden a purple-white hue that lasted until the sun rose.
