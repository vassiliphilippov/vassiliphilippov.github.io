anise tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪs tri / (AmE), / ˈænɪs triː / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen shrubs and small trees of the genus Illicium, known for their aromatic leaves and seeds.
evergreen shrub or tree of genus Illicium

Examples

The anise tree is often grown for its fragrant leaves and attractive flowers.
Some cultures use the seeds of the anise tree as a spice in traditional dishes.
The garden features an ancient anise tree that blooms every spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anise - an aromatic plant whose seeds are used as a spice
