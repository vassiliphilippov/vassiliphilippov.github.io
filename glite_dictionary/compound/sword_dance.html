sword dance

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd dæns / (AmE), / sɔːd dɑːns / (BrE)
a traditional dance performed by men involving stepping over swords or brandishing them, often as part of a ritual or celebration
a dance involving swords

Examples

The Highland sword dance is a famous part of Scottish culture.
During the festival, performers captivated the crowd with a lively sword dance.
He trained for months to master the intricate steps of the sword dance.
