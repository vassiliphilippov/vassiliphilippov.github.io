dagger fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæɡər fɝn / (AmE), / ˈdæɡə fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American evergreen fern with pinnate leaves and dense clusters of lance-shaped fronds.
North American evergreen fern

Examples

We spotted a lush dagger fern growing along the shady forest trail.
The dagger fern is known for its striking, lance-shaped fronds.
Gardeners often choose the dagger fern for its evergreen foliage and attractive appearance.
