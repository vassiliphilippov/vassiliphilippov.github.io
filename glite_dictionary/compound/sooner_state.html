Sooner State

1. PROPN

/ ˈsuːnɚ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuːnə steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the state of Oklahoma in the south central United States.
nickname for Oklahoma, US state

Examples

Many country musicians are originally from the Sooner State.
The Sooner State is famous for its wide prairies and tornadoes.
She grew up in the Sooner State before moving to Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oklahoma - a state in the south central region of the United States

Etymology

From 'Sooner', a term for settlers who entered Oklahoma territory before it was officially opened to settlement, plus 'state'.
