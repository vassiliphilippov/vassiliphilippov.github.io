a hundred times

1. ADV

/ ə ˈhʌndrəd taɪmz / (AmE), / ə ˈhʌndrəd taɪmz / (BrE)
By a multiple or factor of one hundred; one hundred times as much or as many.
by a factor of one hundred

Examples

If production increases a hundred times, costs will need to be re-evaluated.
This new drug is a hundred times more effective than the previous treatment.
The telescope is so powerful it can magnify objects a hundred times.

Synset Examples

their money increased a hundredfold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hundredfold - by a factor of one hundred
