guidance system

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdəns ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdəns ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of equipment that automatically directs the course or trajectory of a vehicle, especially missiles or spacecraft.
automatic vehicle directing system

Examples

The missile uses a sophisticated guidance system to ensure it stays on its intended path.
Modern aircraft rely heavily on their guidance system for autopilot and navigation functions.
Engineers tested the new guidance system during the rocket's maiden flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navigation system - a system for determining and controlling position and movement
