boat bug

1. NOUN

/ boʊt bʌɡ / (AmE), / bəʊt bʌɡ / (BrE)
A carnivorous aquatic insect with paddle-like hind legs for swimming.
aquatic insect with paddle-like legs

Examples

The pond was full of boat bugs skimming across the water's surface.
Biologists studied the movement of the boat bug and its unique paddle-like legs.
Children watched as a boat bug caught a small insect near the reeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water boatman - aquatic bug with oar-like legs for swimming, often used synonymously; another common type
