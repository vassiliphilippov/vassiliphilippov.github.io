healthcare service

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθˌkɛr ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθˌkeə ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a service provided to individuals or communities to promote, maintain, monitor, or restore health
service related to medical care

Examples

The government spends billions each year on healthcare services for its citizens.
Access to quality healthcare services can greatly improve community well-being.
Many rural areas struggle due to a shortage of available healthcare services.

Synset Examples

Healthcare services abound in the Pill Hill district.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical service - service concerned with the treatment of illness or injury
