cotton mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːtən mɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒtən mɪl / (BrE)
A factory where cotton is processed and woven into textiles.
factory for making cotton textiles

Examples

The old cotton mill was converted into luxury apartments.
In the nineteenth century, many towns grew around the local cotton mill.
Her grandfather worked at a cotton mill during the industrial revolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: textile mill - a factory for making textiles
