booby hatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuː.bi ˌhætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbuː.bi ˌhætʃ / (BrE)
A slang and dated term for a mental institution or insane asylum, often used pejoratively or humorously.
dated slang for insane asylum
slang offensive

Examples

After his wild outburst, some joked he might end up in the booby hatch.
Stories about the old booby hatch still circulate around town today.
He laughed off their worries, saying he wasn’t ready for the booby hatch just yet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in the treatment of serious mental disorders

Etymology

Formed from 'booby' (fool, simpleton) and 'hatch' (door or opening); 'booby' dates to the 16th century from Spanish 'bobo' (foolish).
