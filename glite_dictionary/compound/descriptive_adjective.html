descriptive adjective

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈskrɪptɪv ˈædʒɪktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪptɪv ˈadʒɪktɪv / (BrE)
An adjective that describes a quality, attribute, or characteristic of a noun, such as 'nervous' in 'a nervous person'.
adjective describing a noun's quality

Examples

The word 'blue' in 'blue sky' is a descriptive adjective that tells us the color of the sky.
Teachers often ask students to use more descriptive adjectives in their creative writing.
In the phrase 'tall building', 'tall' functions as a descriptive adjective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: qualitative adjective - an adjective denoting a quality or characteristic of a noun
