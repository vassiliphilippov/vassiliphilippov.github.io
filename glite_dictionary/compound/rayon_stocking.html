rayon stocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.ɑn ˈstɑk.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.ɒn ˈstɒk.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A women's stocking made from rayon, a semi-synthetic fiber, valued for its sheerness and smooth texture.
women's stocking made from rayon

Examples

She wore a pair of elegant rayon stockings with her evening dress.
During the 1940s, rayon stockings were popular due to shortages of silk and nylon.
The vintage shop had several packs of unopened rayon stockings from the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nylon stocking - women's stocking made from nylon
  • Synonym: silk stocking - women's stocking made from silk

Etymology

From 'rayon', coined in early 20th century from French 'rayon' (ray, beam of light), referring to its lustrous appearance, and 'stocking' (a close-fitting covering for the foot and leg).
