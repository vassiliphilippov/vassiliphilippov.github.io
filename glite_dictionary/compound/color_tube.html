color tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚ tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə tjuːb / (BrE)
a picture tube in a television that displays images in full color, using combinations of red, green, and blue phosphors
television tube displaying color images

Examples

The old TV in the basement still has its original color tube and works perfectly.
Technicians replaced the faulty color tube to restore the television's picture quality.
In the 1970s, having a color tube was a sign of modern home entertainment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picture tube - the cathode-ray tube in a television set that displays the image
