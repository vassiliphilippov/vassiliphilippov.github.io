Indian salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a perennial herb with white flowers, sometimes used for its edible leaves as greens in the southeastern United States
herb with edible leaves in the US

Examples

The leaves of Indian salad can be added to fresh salads for a peppery flavor.
In the southeastern United States, Indian salad grows wild in moist woodlands.
She picked some Indian salad from her garden to serve with dinner.
