eye color

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / aɪ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
The color of the iris of a person's or animal's eye, determined by the pigmentation present.
iris pigmentation (color of the eye)

Examples

Her striking blue eye color made her stand out in the crowd.
Scientists have discovered that genes control eye color in surprisingly complex ways.
He wears contact lenses to change his eye color for different roles.

Synset Examples

The genetics of eye color, like hair color, are still being worked out, and it's clear that several genes can modify the eventual outcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair color - the color or shade of a person's or animal's hair
