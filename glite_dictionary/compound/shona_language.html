Shona language

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊnə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊnə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken primarily in Zimbabwe and parts of Mozambique and Zambia.
Bantu language of Zimbabwe

Examples

Several million people in Zimbabwe speak Shona language as their mother tongue.
She is studying the Shona language to better understand Zimbabwean culture.
Many traditional stories have been passed down orally in the Shona language.

Etymology

From Shona, the name of the ethnic group that speaks the language.
