sketch block

1. NOUN

/ skɛtʃ blɑk / (AmE), / skɛtʃ blɒk / (BrE)
a book of blank sheets of paper used for making sketches or drawings
book of blank paper for sketches

Examples

She always kept a sketch block in her backpack to capture interesting scenes she encountered.
The artist filled every page of his sketch block with imaginative drawings.
I bought a new sketch block for my art class this semester.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchbook - a book with blank pages for drawing sketches
