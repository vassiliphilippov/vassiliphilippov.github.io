coffee junkie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːfi ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to or obsessed with drinking coffee
someone addicted to coffee
informal

Examples

My brother is such a coffee junkie that he can't function without his morning brew.
As a self-proclaimed coffee junkie, she owns three different coffee makers.
The office is full of coffee junkies who live for their next cup.

Synset Examples

He's a coffee junkie, needing his fix every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caffeine addict - someone addicted to caffeine

Etymology

From slang use of "junkie" (an addict) + "coffee".
