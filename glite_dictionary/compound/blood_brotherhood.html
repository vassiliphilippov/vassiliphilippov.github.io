blood brotherhood

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˈbrʌðɚˌhʊd / (AmE), / blʌd ˈbrʌðəhʊd / (BrE)
A close, sworn friendship or bond between individuals, often sealed by a symbolic act such as mixing blood.
close, sworn friendship bond

Examples

They formed a blood brotherhood after surviving the ordeal together.
The tradition of blood brotherhood has deep roots in many cultures around the world.
Their blood brotherhood meant that they trusted each other completely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blood brother - a person who has sworn loyalty or friendship in a symbolic blood-sharing ritual
