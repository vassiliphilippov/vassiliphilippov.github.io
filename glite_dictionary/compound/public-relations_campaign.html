public-relations campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk rɪˈleɪʃənz kamˈpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized series of activities designed to improve or influence the public image of an organization or individual
campaign to improve public image

Examples

The company launched a public-relations campaign to address concerns about their environmental impact.
After the scandal, a major public-relations campaign helped restore her reputation.
Their new public-relations campaign focused on community involvement and transparency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - a series of advertisements to promote a product or idea
