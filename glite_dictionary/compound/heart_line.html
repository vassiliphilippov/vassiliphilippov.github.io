heart line

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt laɪn / (AmE), / hɑːt laɪn / (BrE)
a crease on the palm of the hand, thought in palmistry to reveal aspects of a person's emotional nature
line on palm associated with emotions in palmistry

Examples

The palm reader examined my heart line to give insights about my romantic life.
Palmists believe that a long heart line suggests a caring and loving personality.
Her heart line curves gently upward toward her fingers, according to the chart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life line - another crease on the palm, said to indicate vitality in palmistry
  • Synonym: head line - crease on the palm, thought to represent intellect in palmistry
