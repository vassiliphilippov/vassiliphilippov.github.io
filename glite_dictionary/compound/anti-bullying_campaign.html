anti-bullying campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.ti ˈbʊl.i.ɪŋ kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti ˈbʊl.i.ɪŋ kamˈpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized effort or initiative aimed at preventing and addressing bullying, especially in schools or workplaces
organized effort to prevent bullying

Examples

The school launched an anti-bullying campaign to create a safer environment for all students.
His dedication to the anti-bullying campaign inspired many parents and teachers to get involved.
Posters for the anti-bullying campaign were displayed throughout the community center.

Synset Examples

His anti-bullying campaign gained a lot of support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campaign - an organized course of action to achieve a goal
