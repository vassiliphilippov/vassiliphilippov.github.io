Lanai Island

1. PROPN

/ ləˈnaɪ ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ləˈnaɪ ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
an island in central Hawaii, known for its pineapple plantations
Hawaiian island, pineapple-growing area

Examples

Many tourists visit Lanai Island for its secluded beaches and luxury resorts.
Lanai Island was once famous for supplying nearly all of the world's pineapples.
During their trip to Hawaii, they took a ferry to Lanai Island to explore its natural beauty.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Lanaʻi, the name of the island.
