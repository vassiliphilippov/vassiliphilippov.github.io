love affair

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv əˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈlʌv əˌfeə / (BrE)
a romantic or sexual relationship between two people, especially when it is secret or illicit
romantic or sexual relationship

Examples

Their secret love affair lasted for more than a year before anyone found out.
The novel tells the story of a tragic love affair set in Paris.
Rumors about the politician's love affair quickly spread through the office.

Related Terms
