pile-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈpaɪl ʌp / (BrE)
a crash involving several vehicles, often occurring on a road or highway
multi-vehicle traffic accident

Examples

A thick fog caused a massive pile-up on the motorway this morning.
Emergency services worked quickly to clear the pile-up and help the injured.
Several lanes were closed for hours due to the pile-up involving ten vehicles.

Synset Examples

He witnessed a pile-up of cars during the storm.
There was a massive pile-up on the highway this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash - collision between vehicles or with an object
  • Synonym: accident - an unexpected event, often resulting in damage or injury
