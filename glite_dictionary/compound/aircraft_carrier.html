aircraft carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.kræft ˌkæɹ.i.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈeə.krɑːft ˌkæɹ.i.ə / (BrE)
A large warship equipped with a long, flat deck for the takeoff and landing of military aircraft.
warship for launching and landing planes

Examples

The aircraft carrier deployed a squadron of jets during the training exercise.
A new aircraft carrier joined the naval fleet last year.
Engineers are improving the design of modern aircraft carriers for greater efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warship - armed naval ship for warfare

Etymology

Formed from 'aircraft' and 'carrier', indicating a ship that carries aircraft.
