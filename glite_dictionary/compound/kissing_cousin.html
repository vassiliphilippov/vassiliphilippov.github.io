kissing cousin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪsɪŋ ˌkʌzən / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ ˌkʌzən / (BrE)
A relative who is not closely related but familiar enough to be greeted with a kiss; a distant relative with whom one has a close relationship.
distant but familiar relative

Examples

She invited several kissing cousins to the family reunion this summer.
Although we're only kissing cousins, we grew up together and are very close.
At the wedding, every kissing cousin greeted the bride with a warm embrace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relative - person connected by blood or marriage
