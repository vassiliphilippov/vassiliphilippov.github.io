rooftop garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːf.tɑp ˈɡɑr.dn̩ / (AmE), / ˈruːf.tɒp ˈɡɑː.dn̩ / (BrE)
a garden situated on the roof of a building, often created for recreational, aesthetic, or environmental purposes
garden on a building's roof

Examples

It was relaxing to have breakfast in their rooftop garden overlooking the city skyline.
The company created a rooftop garden to provide green space for employees.
They grew fresh vegetables and herbs in the rooftop garden above their apartment.

Synset Examples

The architect designed a stunning rooftop garden with a rooftree.

  • Synonym: roof garden - a garden located on a building's roof
