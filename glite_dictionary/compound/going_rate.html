going rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊɪŋ reɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ reɪt / (BrE)
the price or wage that is generally accepted as standard at a particular time for something
standard or current price or wage

Examples

The going rate for babysitting in this city is $15 an hour.
Before selling your car, research the going rate so you get a fair price.
Many workers expect their salaries to increase in line with the going rate for their job.

Synset Examples

The going rate for rent in this area is quite high.
The going rate for that service has increased significantly.
You should check the going rate before hiring anyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market rate - the price a product or service would fetch in an open market
