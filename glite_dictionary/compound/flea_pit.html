flea pit

1. NOUN

/ fliː pɪt / (AmE), / fliː pɪt / (BrE)
a cinema, theater, or other venue that is dirty, uncomfortable, or in poor condition.
dirty or uncomfortable venue
informal british

Examples

Last night we ended up at a flea pit with sticky floors and broken chairs.
The old movie theater was such a flea pit that I didn't want to sit down.
He refused to stay in a flea pit when there were nicer hotels nearby.

Synset Examples

The concert venue felt like a flea pit with no ventilation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dive - a disreputable or run-down bar or club

Etymology

Probably from the association of old, unkempt venues with fleas and other pests.
