manimony payment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪˌmoʊni ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪˌməʊni ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
A financial settlement awarded to a man following a divorce, especially as court-ordered support from an ex-wife.
financial settlement for divorced men

Examples

After the divorce, Mark received a manimony payment from his former spouse.
The court ruled in favor of a monthly manimony payment to support his living expenses.
Debates around manimony payment have grown as more women become primary earners.

Synset Examples

Their agreement included a significant manimony payment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alimony - court-ordered financial support paid after divorce

Etymology

Blend of 'man' and 'alimony'.
