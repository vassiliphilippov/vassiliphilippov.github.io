parts catalogue

1. NOUN

/ pɑrts ˈkæt̬əlˌɔɡ / (AmE), / pɑːts ˈkætəlɒɡ / (BrE)
a book or online listing that advertises and provides details and prices for parts used in machines or vehicles
list of machine or vehicle parts for sale

Examples

The mechanic checked the parts catalogue to find the correct replacement for my car.
Many manufacturers provide a digital parts catalogue with up-to-date pricing.
Browsing the parts catalogue helped me decide which components I needed to order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalogue - a complete list of items, typically one in alphabetical or other systematic order
