pinko nonsense

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋkoʊ ˈnɑnsəns / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkəʊ ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
Ideas or statements characterized as leftist or overly liberal, often used with a dismissive or derogatory tone.
leftist or overly liberal ideas
informal slang american

Examples

He called the proposal pinko nonsense and refused to listen further.
Some commentators dismissed the senator's suggestions as mere pinko nonsense.
You can't just brush off every criticism as pinko nonsense.

Synset Examples

They dismissed her ideas as pinko nonsense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leftist - person or ideas with left-wing political views
  • Synonym: nonsense - something that makes no sense or has no meaning

Etymology

From 'pinko' (slang for a left-wing person) + 'nonsense'. 'Pinko' arose in 20th-century American English.
