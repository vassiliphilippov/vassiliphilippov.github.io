rock-climbing equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑk ˌklaɪmɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌklaɪmɪŋ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the specialized gear and devices used for the sport of rock climbing or other types of climbing, such as ropes, harnesses, carabiners, and helmets
gear and devices for rock climbing

Examples

He made sure all his rock-climbing equipment was in perfect condition before the trip.
The rental shop offers a wide selection of rock-climbing equipment for beginners and advanced climbers alike.
Proper rock-climbing equipment is essential for safety during challenging ascents.

Synset Examples

A haul bag refers to a large, tough, and often unwieldy bag into which supplies and climbing equipment may be thrown.
