Seven Wonders of the World

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvən ˈwʌndərz əv ðə wɝld / (AmE), / ˈsɛvən ˈwʌndəz əv ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
the seven most famous and impressive structures of the ancient world, regarded as remarkable achievements of architecture and engineering
the ancient world's most famous monuments

Examples

The Seven Wonders of the World include the Great Pyramid of Giza and the Hanging Gardens of Babylon.
Many historians believe that some of the Seven Wonders of the World may never have existed.
She was fascinated by ancient history, especially the Seven Wonders of the World.
