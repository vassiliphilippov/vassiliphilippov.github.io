exam paper

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzæm ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzæm ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a set of written questions, or the physical document on which an examination is taken, especially in a school or university
written test; examination document

Examples

She looked relieved as she handed in her exam paper at the end of the test.
The teacher spent all weekend marking the students' exam papers.
Make sure your name is clearly written at the top of the exam paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - a procedure intended to establish the quality, performance, or reliability of something, especially before widespread use
  • Synonym: examination - a formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency in a subject or skill
