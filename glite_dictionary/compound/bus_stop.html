bus stop

1. NOUN

/ bʌs stɑp / (AmE), / bʌs stɒp / (BrE)
A place along a bus route where buses regularly stop for passengers to get on and off.
place where buses pick up/drop off passengers

Examples

We waited at the bus stop for over fifteen minutes this morning.
There’s a bus stop just around the corner from my apartment.
Children were standing at the bus stop in the rain, hoping the bus would arrive soon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bus station - a large facility where buses start or end routes and passengers embark/disembark
