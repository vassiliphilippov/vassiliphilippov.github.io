water fountain

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌfaʊntən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌfaʊntɪn / (BrE)
a public device that provides a stream of drinking water for people to drink directly from
public device for drinking water

Examples

There's a water fountain near the entrance of the park if you need a drink.
Students lined up to use the water fountain between classes.
The old water fountain was replaced with a modern touch-activated model.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinking fountain - a public device supplying water for drinking directly
