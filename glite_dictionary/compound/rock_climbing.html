rock climbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑk ˌklaɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌklaɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of climbing natural rock formations, typically using ropes and special equipment for safety.
sport of climbing rocks

Examples

She has been interested in rock climbing ever since she visited Colorado last year.
The club offers weekend trips for those who want to try rock climbing for the first time.
Good balance and strong grip are important in rock climbing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountaineering - the sport of climbing mountains, often including hiking and climbing on rock, snow, and ice
  • Degree Lower: bouldering - a form of rock climbing performed on small rock formations or artificial walls without ropes
