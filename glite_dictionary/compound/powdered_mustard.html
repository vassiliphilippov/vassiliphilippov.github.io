powdered mustard

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚd ˈmʌstɚd / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdəd ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
Mustard seeds that have been ground into a fine, dry powder, often used as a spice or condiment.
ground mustard seeds in powder form

Examples

She added a teaspoon of powdered mustard to the sauce for extra flavor.
Some people use powdered mustard as a natural remedy, but it should be handled with care.
The recipe calls for both vinegar and powdered mustard to achieve a tangy taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mustard powder - another term for powdered mustard
