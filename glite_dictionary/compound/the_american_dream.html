the American Dream

1. NOUN

/ ði əˈmɛɹɪkən dɹim / (AmE), / ði əˈmɛɹɪkən dɹiːm / (BrE)
the ideal that every US citizen has the opportunity to achieve success and prosperity through hard work, determination, and initiative
the ideal of success and opportunity in the US

Examples

Many immigrants come to the US in search of the American Dream.
For some, owning a home is the ultimate symbol of the American Dream.
Critics argue that the American Dream is no longer attainable for everyone.

Synset Examples

The American Dream seems out of reach for some.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opportunity - a situation that makes it possible to do something
  • Synonym: upward mobility - the ability to move to a higher social or economic position
