big game

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
large wild animals hunted for sport, such as elephants, lions, or buffalo
large animals hunted for sport

Examples

The safari was organized for photographers hoping to see big game in its natural habitat.
Conservation efforts aim to protect big game from overhunting and illegal poaching.
