Pabir language

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːbɪr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpæbɪə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken primarily in Borno State, Nigeria, and neighboring regions south of Lake Chad
a Chadic language of Nigeria

Examples

The Pabir language is spoken by people in parts of northeastern Nigeria.
Linguists have studied the grammar and vocabulary of the Pabir language extensively.
She is fluent in both English and the Pabir language.
