third deck

1. NOUN

/ θɝd dɛk / (AmE), / θɜːd dɛk / (BrE)
the deck located below the main deck on a ship.
deck below the main deck (ship)

Examples

The crew stored the cargo on the third deck for the transatlantic voyage.
Many sailors slept in cramped hammocks hanging from the beams of the third deck.
During a storm, water sometimes leaked onto the third deck, causing concern among the crew.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: main deck - the principal deck of a ship
