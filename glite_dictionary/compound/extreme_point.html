extreme point

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstrim pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːm pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the point that is farthest from the center or middle of something, often used in geometry and geography
furthest point from the center

Examples

The extreme point of the peninsula is marked by a lighthouse.
Mathematicians often study the extreme point of a set when analyzing its boundaries.
After hours of hiking, we finally reached the extreme point of the mountain ridge.
