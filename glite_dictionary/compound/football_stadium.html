football stadium

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl ˈsteɪdiəm / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ˈsteɪdiəm / (BrE)
A stadium designed and used for playing and watching football games.
stadium for football games

Examples

The fans filled the football stadium long before the match started.
This city is planning to build a new football stadium downtown.
Loud cheers echoed throughout the football stadium after the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stadium - a large building for sporting events, concerts, etc.
