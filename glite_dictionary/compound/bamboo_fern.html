bamboo fern

1. NOUN

/ bæmˈbuː fɝːn / (AmE), / bamˈbuː fɜːn / (BrE)
a fast-growing, sturdy Japanese fern cultivated for its attractive broad, dark-green pinnate fronds
a Japanese fern with broad pinnate fronds

Examples

The bamboo fern thrives best in shady, moist environments.
She added a bamboo fern to her indoor plant collection for its striking fronds.
Gardeners often choose bamboo ferns for their resilience and ornamental value.
