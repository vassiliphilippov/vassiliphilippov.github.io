Republic of Seychelles

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv seɪˈʃɛlz / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv seɪˈʃɛlz / (BrE)
An island country in the Indian Ocean, off East Africa, consisting of numerous islands; independent since 1976.
island country in the Indian Ocean

Examples

The Republic of Seychelles is known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant coral reefs.
After gaining independence in 1976, the Republic of Seychelles established its own government.
Tourists often visit the Republic of Seychelles to experience its unique wildlife and tropical climate.
