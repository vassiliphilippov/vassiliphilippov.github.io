mocha coffee

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.kə ˈkɔ.fi / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.kə ˈkɒf.i / (BrE)
A superior dark coffee made from beans originally from Arabia, especially from the port of Mocha in Yemen.
superior dark coffee from Arabia

Examples

He always starts his morning with a cup of mocha coffee brewed from freshly ground beans.
The rich aroma of mocha coffee filled the entire café, attracting coffee lovers from all around.
She brought back some authentic mocha coffee after her trip to Yemen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arabica coffee - coffee made from Coffea arabica beans, generally milder and considered superior
  • Antonym: robusta coffee - coffee made from Coffea canephora, generally stronger and more bitter

Etymology

From Mocha, a port in Yemen, once a major coffee-exporting center.
