to walk hand in hand

1. VERB

/ wɔk hænd ɪn hænd / (AmE), / wɔːk hænd ɪn hænd / (BrE)
(of two people) to walk closely together while holding each other's hands, especially as a sign of love or affection.
walk together holding hands

Examples

They strolled through the city, walking hand in hand as the sun set.
Every morning, the elderly couple would walk hand in hand along the beach.
She felt happy and safe walking hand in hand with her partner.

Synset Examples

I saw the couple walking hand in hand at the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hold hands - to clasp another person's hand with one's own, often as a sign of affection
