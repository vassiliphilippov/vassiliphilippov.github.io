guinea gold vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪni ɡoʊld vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni ɡəʊld vaɪn / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen vines native to Australasia, widely grown in warm regions for their large, bright yellow single flowers.
Australasian evergreen vine with yellow flowers

Examples

The guinea gold vine thrives best in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.
Gardeners love the guinea gold vine for its vibrant yellow blossoms that attract bees and butterflies.
We planted a guinea gold vine along the fence, and it quickly covered the trellis with flowers.
