cuntservative view

1. NOUN

/ kʌntˈsɜːrvətɪv vjuː / (AmE), / kʌntˈsɜːvətɪv vjuː / (BrE)
An extremely conservative or traditional opinion, expressed in a way intended to be highly insulting or vulgar.
highly insulting term for ultra-conservative views
slang offensive vulgar

Examples

His cuntservative view of women's roles sparked outrage among the audience.
You can't expect progress if a cuntservative view always blocks new ideas.
The editorial criticized the cuntservative view as outdated and harmful to social progress.

Synset Examples

His cuntservative views clash with modern ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservative - holding traditional values and views
  • Antonym: progressive - favoring social reform or new ideas

Etymology

Blend of the vulgar expletive 'cunt' and 'conservative'.
