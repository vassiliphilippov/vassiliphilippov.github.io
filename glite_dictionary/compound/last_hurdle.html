last hurdle

1. NOUN

/ læst ˈhɜr.dl̩ / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈhɜː.dl̩ / (BrE)
the final obstacle or difficulty that must be overcome to achieve something
final obstacle to overcome

Examples

Securing the funding was the last hurdle in completing the project.
Passing the final exam proved to be his last hurdle before graduating.
The team faced their last hurdle just before the product went to market.

Synset Examples

Getting the approval was the last hurdle before launching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final obstacle - the last challenge to be faced
  • Antonym: first hurdle - the initial obstacle to be overcome
