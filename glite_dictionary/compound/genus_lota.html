genus Lota

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈloʊ.tə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈləʊ.tə / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fish in the family Gadidae, containing only the burbot (Lota lota).
genus of freshwater fish (burbot)

Examples

The genus Lota is comprised of a single species, commonly known as the burbot.
Ichthyologists studied the unique adaptations found in fish belonging to the genus Lota.
In northern lakes, only the genus Lota represents the cod family in freshwater environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burbot - a freshwater fish (Lota lota) of the cod family

Etymology

From Latin 'lota,' the name for the burbot.
