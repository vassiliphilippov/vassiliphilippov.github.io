Doctor of Humanities

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktɚ əv hjuːˈmænɪtiz / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv hjuːˈmænɪtiz / (BrE)
An honorary doctoral degree awarded in recognition of achievements in the humanities.
honorary degree in the humanities

Examples

She was awarded a Doctor of Humanities degree by the university for her lifelong contribution to literature.
Several renowned scholars have received the title of Doctor of Humanities in recognition of their work.
After the ceremony, he proudly mentioned his new Doctor of Humanities honor to his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honorary doctorate - a degree awarded as an honor without the usual requirements
