second trimester

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd traɪˈmɛstər / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd traɪˈmɛstə / (BrE)
the period from the 13th to the 27th week of pregnancy
middle stage of pregnancy
medical

Examples

During her second trimester, she experienced more energy and fewer symptoms of morning sickness.
It is common for doctors to schedule an anatomy scan during the second trimester of pregnancy.
Many women feel the baby’s first movements in their second trimester.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first trimester - the period from conception to the 12th week of pregnancy
  • Degree Higher: third trimester - the period from the 28th week to birth in pregnancy

Etymology

From Latin 'trimestris' (of three months); 'second' indicating the sequence of three trimesters.
