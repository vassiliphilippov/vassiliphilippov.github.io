to gather data

1. VERB

/ ˈɡæðɚ ˈdeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈɡæðə ˈdeɪtə / (BrE)
to collect information, especially for analysis or study
collect information

Examples

The company uses surveys to gather data on customer satisfaction.
Scientists gather data from field studies to support their research.
Before making any decisions, the analyst spent weeks gathering data.

Synset Examples

An orbiter was launched to gather data about Venus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collect data - to assemble or accumulate information
