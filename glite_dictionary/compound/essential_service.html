essential service

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəl ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a service considered necessary for the health, safety, or well-being of individuals, especially those requiring assistance
necessary service for health and safety

Examples

During emergencies, the government ensures that essential services such as healthcare and water supply remain operational.
The charity provides essential services to elderly residents in rural areas.
Strikes in essential services like public transportation can significantly disrupt daily life.

Synset Examples

WAIF provides essential services for those in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vital service - a service essential for functioning or survival
