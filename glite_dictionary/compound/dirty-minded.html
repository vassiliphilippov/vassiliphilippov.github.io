dirty-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɝːti ˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Tending to see or think about sexual meanings or innuendoes, especially in an inappropriate or lewd way.
having a tendency for lewd or sexual thoughts

Examples

It was only a harmless joke, but his dirty-minded comments made everyone uncomfortable.
Some people are so dirty-minded that they twist innocent remarks into something obscene.
She rolled her eyes at his dirty-minded interpretation of her story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lewd - crude and offensive in a sexual way
  • Antonym: innocent - free from sexual intent or lewd thoughts
