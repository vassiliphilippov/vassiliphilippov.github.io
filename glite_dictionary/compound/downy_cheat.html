downy cheat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni tʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni tʃiːt / (BrE)
An annual or winter annual grass of the Mediterranean, with softly hairy leaves; also known as soft brome.
Mediterranean grass species with hairy leaves

Examples

The fields were covered with downy cheat after a wet spring.
Researchers studied how downy cheat competes with native grasses for resources.
The softly hairy leaves help downy cheat retain moisture in its Mediterranean habitat.
