Republic of Austria

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɔstriə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɒstriə / (BrE)
the official name of Austria, a landlocked and mountainous country in central Europe known for its history and cultural heritage
the official name of Austria, central European country

Examples

The Republic of Austria is famous for its alpine scenery and winter sports.
She studied music history in the Republic of Austria for a semester abroad.
After World War I, the Republic of Austria was established as a federal state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Austria - a country in central Europe
  • Synonym: Vienna - capital city of Austria

Etymology

From Latin 'res publica' (public affair) and the German 'Österreich' (eastern realm).
