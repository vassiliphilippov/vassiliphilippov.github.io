meme magic

1. NOUN

/ miːm ˈmædʒɪk / (AmE), / miːm ˈmadʒɪk / (BrE)
the supposed power of internet memes to influence culture, opinions, or events, often in unexpected ways
influence or power of internet memes
informal slang

Examples

Some internet communities truly believe in the power of meme magic to shape real-world events.
After the joke went viral, it seemed like meme magic had turned it into a national conversation.
Many attribute the sudden popularity of the slogan to meme magic at work.

Synset Examples

He believes in meme magic to rally support online.
Some say meme magic can change public opinion.
The team's victory felt like meme magic in action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - an idea, behavior, or style that spreads online

Etymology

From 'meme' (a cultural element spread online) and 'magic' (supernatural power or influence).
