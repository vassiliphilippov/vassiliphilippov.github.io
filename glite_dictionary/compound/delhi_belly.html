Delhi belly

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛli ˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈdɛli ˌbɛli / (BrE)
an upset stomach or digestive illness contracted while traveling, especially in India, often due to contaminated food or water
traveler's stomach upset, especially in India
informal

Examples

After eating raw vegetables from a street vendor, he came down with Delhi belly.
Many tourists carry medication to prepare for the possibility of Delhi belly.
You should drink only bottled water to avoid getting Delhi belly.

Synset Examples

She learned to avoid street food to prevent Delhi belly.
I was stuck at home with Delhi belly for days.
Traveling in India, he caught a case of Delhi belly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler's diarrhea - diarrhea experienced by travelers, typically caused by infection

Etymology

Formed from the city name Delhi and the rhyming word 'belly', referring to stomach problems experienced by travelers.
