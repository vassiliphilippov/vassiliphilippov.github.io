luciferous smile

1. NOUN

/ luˈsɪfərəs smaɪl / (AmE), / luˈsɪfərəs smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile that brings light or joy; a radiant or uplifting smile.
radiant, uplifting smile

Examples

Her luciferous smile brightened the entire room.
He greeted everyone with a luciferous smile that put them instantly at ease.
The photograph captured her luciferous smile in a moment of pure happiness.

Synset Examples

Etymology

From Latin 'lucifer' (light-bringer) and English 'smile'.
