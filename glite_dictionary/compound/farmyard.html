farmyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmˌjɑːd / (BrE)
an open area next to farm buildings, often used for storage, animals, or equipment
area next to farm buildings

Examples

The children played in the farmyard while their parents cleaned the barn.
Chickens wandered freely around the farmyard, pecking for grain.
A tractor was parked in the middle of the farmyard after a long day of work.
