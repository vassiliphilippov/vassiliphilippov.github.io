night letter

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ˈlɛtɚ / (AmE), / naɪt ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a telegram sent at a reduced rate for delivery the following day, typically sent at night
a delayed, cheaper telegram service

Examples

In the early 20th century, sending a night letter was a popular way to save money on telegrams.
Because it was less urgent, the night letter was delivered the next day rather than immediately.
He recalled receiving a night letter from his family, filled with news from home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph and delivered in written or printed form
