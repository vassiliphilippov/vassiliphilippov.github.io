highest common factor

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ.əst ˈkɑː.mən ˈfæk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.ɪst ˈkɒm.ən ˈfæk.tə / (BrE)
the largest positive integer that exactly divides each of a given set of integers without leaving a remainder
largest integer dividing a set of numbers exactly

Examples

To solve this problem, first find the highest common factor of 18 and 24.
Mathematicians often refer to the highest common factor as the greatest common divisor.
We calculated the highest common factor to simplify the fraction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greatest common divisor - the largest integer that divides two or more numbers without a remainder
