slam against

1. NOUN

/ slæm əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / slæm əˈɡenst / (BrE)
a strong criticism or verbal attack directed at someone or something
strong criticism or attack

Examples

The report was a direct slam against the company’s management practices.
Her review turned into a slam against the authors rather than the book itself.
Many saw his remarks as a slam against the new policy.

Synset Examples

His argument was a slam against the opposition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attack - an aggressive criticism or assault
  • Antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration, opposite of criticism
