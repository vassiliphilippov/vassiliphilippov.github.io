Indian Ocean

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the third largest ocean in the world, situated between Africa, Asia, Australia, and the Southern Ocean
major ocean between Africa, Asia, and Australia

Examples

The Indian Ocean is known for its warm waters and complex monsoon systems.
Several important trade routes pass through the Indian Ocean.
Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atlantic Ocean - major ocean between the Americas, Europe, and Africa
  • Synonym: Pacific Ocean - largest ocean, between Asia/Australia and the Americas

Etymology

Named after India, as early European geographers associated the ocean's northern parts with the Indian subcontinent.
