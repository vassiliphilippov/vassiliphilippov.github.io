house decorator

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈdɛk.əˌreɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / haʊs ˈdɛk.əˌreɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to plan and arrange the appearance and furnishings of the interior of houses
interior design specialist for houses

Examples

They hired a house decorator to give their living room a modern look.
The house decorator suggested soft colors to make the space feel larger.
After seeing the beautiful results, she recommended her house decorator to all her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior decorator - a person who decorates the interior of buildings for aesthetic effect
