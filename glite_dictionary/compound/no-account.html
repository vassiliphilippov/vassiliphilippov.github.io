no-account

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
An idle or worthless person; someone regarded as insignificant or of little value.
worthless or insignificant person
informal

Examples

He called his brother a no-account who never kept a steady job.
The sheriff warned the town to watch out for any no-account drifters.
After years of being labeled a no-account, he decided to prove everyone wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-for-nothing - a person who is lazy or worthless
  • Synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or employment

Etymology

Formed from 'no' + 'account', indicating 'of no value or worth'.
