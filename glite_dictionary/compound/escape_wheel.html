escape wheel

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈskeɪp wil / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp wiːl / (BrE)
a toothed gear in a mechanical clock or watch that engages with the escapement mechanism, regulating its movement
toothed gear in clocks or watches

Examples

The escape wheel is a critical component in the regulation of a mechanical watch.
Dust or dirt can cause the escape wheel to malfunction, affecting the timekeeping accuracy.
The watchmaker carefully placed the escape wheel back into position during the repair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escapement - mechanism in clocks and watches that controls movement of the gear train
