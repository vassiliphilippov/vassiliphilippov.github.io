English professor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ prəˈfɛsər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ prəˈfɛsə / (BrE)
a university or college teacher who specializes in teaching English language or literature
university teacher of English

Examples

My sister is an English professor at the local university.
The English professor assigned us a challenging essay on Shakespeare.
After many years of study, he finally became an English professor at a prestigious college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecturer - a person who gives lectures, especially in a university
  • Synonym: professor - a university academic of the highest rank
