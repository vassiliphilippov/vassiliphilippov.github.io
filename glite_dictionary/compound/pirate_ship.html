pirate ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪrət ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpaɪərət ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship operated by pirates, typically used for attacking and robbing other vessels at sea
ship used by pirates

Examples

Legend tells of a hidden treasure aboard a pirate ship that sank off the coast.
In the story, the children are captured by a fearsome pirate ship.
The museum features a life-sized replica of an 18th-century pirate ship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pirate - a person who attacks and robs ships at sea
