marital bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛrɪtəl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈmarɪtəl bɛd / (BrE)
the relationship or shared intimacy between a married couple, especially as symbolized by their shared bed
the relationship between a married couple

Examples

Many secrets and confessions are whispered in the marital bed.
After years of marriage, the marital bed became a place of comfort and routine.
The lawyer argued that the infidelity had disrupted the sanctity of the marital bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage - the legally or formally recognized union of two people as partners in a personal relationship
  • Synonym: wedlock - the state of being married; marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'maritalis' (relating to marriage) and English 'bed.'
