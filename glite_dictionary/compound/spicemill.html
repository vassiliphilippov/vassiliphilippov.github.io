spicemill

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsˌmɪl / (BrE)
A small mill used for grinding whole spices into powder or smaller fragments for cooking or seasoning.
mill for grinding spices

Examples

She added freshly ground cinnamon from the spicemill to the cake batter.
A high-quality spicemill can make a big difference in your cooking.
They keep several types of spicemills on the kitchen counter for convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper mill - a mill for grinding peppercorns
  • Synonym: grinder - a device for grinding substances such as pepper or spices

Etymology

From 'spice' and 'mill', referring to a device for grinding spices.
