safety isle

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti aɪl / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti aɪl / (BrE)
a curbed area in a roadway, excluded from vehicular traffic, providing a safe space for pedestrians
curbed pedestrian safety zone in roadway

Examples

The city installed a safety isle in the middle of the busy intersection to protect pedestrians.
Children waited on the safety isle before crossing to the other side of the road.
After the renovations, each crosswalk now leads directly to a safety isle for added security.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic island - raised area in a road for pedestrian safety
