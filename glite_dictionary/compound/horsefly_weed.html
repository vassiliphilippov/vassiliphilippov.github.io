horsefly weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌflaɪ wid / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌflaɪ wiːd / (BrE)
a much-branched, erect herb with bright yellow flowers, native to eastern North America, especially from Massachusetts to Florida
a bright yellow-flowered herb of eastern North America

Examples

We found a patch of horsefly weed growing along the edge of the forest trail.
The bright yellow flowers of horsefly weed make it easy to spot in the summer.
Gardeners sometimes plant horsefly weed to attract pollinators to their gardens.
