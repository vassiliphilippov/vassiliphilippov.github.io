golden warbler

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈwɔːrblər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈwɔːblə / (BrE)
A small American wood warbler with a yellow throat, found in regions of the Americas.
yellow-throated American wood warbler

Examples

The golden warbler is often spotted along riverbanks in Central America.
I heard the distinctive call of a golden warbler in the early morning.
Birdwatchers come here hoping to catch a glimpse of the rare golden warbler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow warbler - a similar species of wood warbler with yellow plumage
