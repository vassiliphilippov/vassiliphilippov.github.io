Esselen language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsəˌlɛn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɛsɛlən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extinct Hokan language once spoken by the Esselen people of central California
extinct language of the Esselen people

Examples

The Esselen language has not been spoken fluently since the early 20th century.
Linguists have studied the Esselen language to learn about the region's history.
Efforts to document the Esselen language help preserve the heritage of the Esselen people.

Etymology

Esselen, the name of the indigenous people of central California; language classification from Hokan.
