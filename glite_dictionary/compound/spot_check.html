spot check

1. NOUN

/ spɑt tʃɛk / (AmE), / spɒt tʃɛk / (BrE)
an examination or inspection of work, performance, or product quality carried out unexpectedly and at random times
random unannounced inspection

Examples

The supervisor conducted a spot check of the assembly line to ensure safety protocols were being followed.
Random spot checks help maintain high standards in the laboratory.
She was caught off guard by a spot check of her work emails.

Synset Examples

spot checks ensure a high level of performance by employees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - the act of examining something closely

2. NOUN

/ spɑt tʃɛk / (AmE), / spɒt tʃɛk / (BrE)
an inspection or review carried out without prior notice, usually to assess compliance or quality
an unannounced inspection or review

Examples

The supervisor decided to conduct a spot check on the cleanliness of the cafeteria.
Random spot checks are used to discourage cheating during exams.
A spot check of the accounts revealed several discrepancies.

Synset Examples

The teacher conducted a spot check of homework assignments.
The manager did a spot check on the inventory.
They performed a spot check on the workers' safety gear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - a careful examination or scrutiny
  • Synonym: audit - an official examination of records or performance
