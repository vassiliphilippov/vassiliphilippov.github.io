star earthball

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈɝθˌbɔl / (AmE), / stɑː ˈɜːθˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A type of earthball fungus whose smooth upper surface begins buried in sand and whose fruiting body opens into star-like segments.
a star-shaped earthball fungus

Examples

The star earthball is often found in sandy soils where its body opens in star-like segments.
Scientists studied the growth patterns of the star earthball to understand its unique fruiting structure.
On our nature walk, we spotted a rare star earthball with its rays spread wide open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthball - a type of fungus with a round fruiting body
