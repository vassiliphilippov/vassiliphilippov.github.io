God's Wisdom

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑdz ˈwɪzdəm / (AmE), / ɡɒdz ˈwɪzdəm / (BrE)
the infinite and perfect knowledge possessed by God or a divine being
divine omniscience or perfect knowledge

Examples

Some theologians have written extensively about God's Wisdom and how it guides the universe.
She found comfort in believing that God's Wisdom surpasses all human understanding.
In many religions, believers pray for a glimpse of God's Wisdom to help them make difficult decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omniscience - the state of knowing everything
