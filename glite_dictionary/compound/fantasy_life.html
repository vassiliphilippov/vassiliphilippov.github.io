fantasy life

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tə.si laɪf / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si laɪf / (BrE)
an imaginary or unreal life that a person envisions, often as an escape from reality
an imaginary or unreal life in one's mind

Examples

She often escapes into her fantasy life when reality becomes too stressful.
The novel explores the protagonist's rich fantasy life as a refuge from his lonely existence.
Psychologists say that having a fantasy life can be healthy, as long as it doesn’t interfere with daily activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distracts one's attention from the present
