deadline fighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛdlaɪn ˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdlaɪn ˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person who works effectively under pressure to meet tight deadlines
person effective under deadline pressure

Examples

Her reputation as a deadline fighter made her the go-to person for urgent projects.
The team relied on their deadline fighter to push through the final hours before launch.
As a deadline fighter, he performed best when time was running out.

Synset Examples

Being a deadline fighter helped him finish the project early.
As a deadline fighter, he thrives under pressure.
She’s known as a deadline fighter in our office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workaholic - a person who compulsively works hard and long hours
  • Antonym: procrastinator - someone who delays or postpones tasks until the last minute
