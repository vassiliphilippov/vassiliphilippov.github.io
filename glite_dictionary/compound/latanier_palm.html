latanier palm

1. NOUN

/ ləˈtæniɚ pɑm / (AmE), / ləˈtæniə pɑːm / (BrE)
A type of fan palm native to the southern United States and the Caribbean region.
fan palm of the southern U.S. & Caribbean

Examples

The latanier palm can often be found growing along the coastal areas of Florida.
Gardeners in the Caribbean prize the latanier palm for its broad, elegant leaves.
I collected several fronds from a latanier palm to use for weaving baskets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan palm - Palm tree with fan-shaped leaves

Etymology

From French 'latanier', referring to fan palms.
