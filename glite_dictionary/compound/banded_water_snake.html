banded water snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæn.dɪd ˈwɔ.tɚ sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd ˈwɔː.tə sneɪk / (BrE)
a nonvenomous snake (Nerodia fasciata) found in the western United States, known for its dark bands and preference for habitats near water
a North American water-dwelling snake with dark bands

Examples

We spotted a banded water snake sunning itself on a log near the riverbank.
The distinctive markings of the banded water snake help it blend into its watery habitat.
Despite its fearsome appearance, the banded water snake is harmless to humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water snake - a snake that lives in or near water
