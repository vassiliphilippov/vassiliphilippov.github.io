major screwup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdʒər ˈskruːˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈskruːˌʌp / (BrE)
a significant or serious mistake; a major blunder
a serious mistake or blunder
informal

Examples

The IT team's decisions led to a major screwup that affected the entire company.
Forgetting to save the document before closing was a major screwup on his part.
Admitting to the major screwup helped rebuild trust with the client.

Synset Examples

That was a major screwup on my part.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • Degree Higher: fiasco - a complete and ignominious failure
