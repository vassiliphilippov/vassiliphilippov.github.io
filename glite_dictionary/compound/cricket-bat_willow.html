cricket-bat willow

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪkɪt bæt ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt bæt ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a Eurasian willow tree (Salix alba caerulea) with greyish leaves and upright branches, valued for the wood used in making cricket bats
willow tree used for cricket bats

Examples

The famous English batsman preferred a bat made from cricket-bat willow for its superior quality.
Most of the world's finest cricket bats are crafted from cricket-bat willow grown in England.
The farm specializes in cultivating cricket-bat willow for export to sporting goods manufacturers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - a tree or shrub of the genus Salix, typically found near water
