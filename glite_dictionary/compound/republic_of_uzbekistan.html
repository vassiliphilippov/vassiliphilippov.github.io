Republic of Uzbekistan

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌuzbɛkˈɪstæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌuzbɛkˈɪstɑːn / (BrE)
a landlocked country in Central Asia, formerly a part of the Soviet Union, officially known as the Republic of Uzbekistan.
country in Central Asia

Examples

The Republic of Uzbekistan declared independence from the Soviet Union in 1991.
Tourists are increasingly attracted to the stunning architecture in the Republic of Uzbekistan.
The Silk Road once passed through regions that are now part of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
