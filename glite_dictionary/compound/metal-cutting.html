metal-cutting

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛt̬l ˌkʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmetl ˌkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a tool or machine) hard and sharp enough to cut metal; designed for or used in cutting metal.
suitable for cutting metal

Examples

The factory invested in new metal-cutting equipment for the production line.
He specialized in repairing metal-cutting machines used in automotive manufacturing.
Only metal-cutting tools should be used on steel and other hard materials.
