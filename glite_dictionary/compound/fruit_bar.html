fruit bar

1. NOUN

/ fruːt bɑr / (AmE), / fruːt bɑː / (BrE)
A type of sweet baked snack made with chopped fruit, either mixed into the dough or layered, and cut into bars.
sweet bar-shaped snack with fruit

Examples

She packed a fruit bar in her lunch for a healthy snack.
The bakery sells delicious fruit bars filled with apricots and nuts.
He enjoyed a homemade fruit bar with his afternoon tea.
