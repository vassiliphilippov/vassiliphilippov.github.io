red mange

1. NOUN

/ rɛd mændʒ / (AmE), / rɛd mɒndʒ / (BrE)
a skin disease of dogs and other animals caused by mites, usually resulting in hair loss and redness of the skin
skin disease in animals caused by mites

Examples

The veterinarian diagnosed the puppy with red mange after noticing the characteristic hair loss.
Left untreated, red mange can worsen and lead to severe skin infections in animals.
Many pet owners worry when they hear their dog might have red mange.

Synset Examples

The dog suffered from red mange after the exposure.
He treated his pet for red mange promptly.
Red mange can be distressing for both pets and owners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demodectic mange - mange in animals caused by Demodex mites; another term for red mange
  • Antonym: sarcoptic mange - a different type of mange caused by Sarcoptes mites
