pecuniary resource

1. NOUN

/ pɪˈkjuːniːˌɛri rɪˈsɔrs / (AmE), / pɪˈkjuːnɪəri rɪˈsɔːs / (BrE)
assets, funds, or other resources in the form of money
money-based assets or resources

Examples

The organization struggled after losing its main pecuniary resource last year.
A solid pecuniary resource is essential for sustaining long-term business operations.
His lack of any pecuniary resource made investing in the project impossible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial resource - resources or assets in the form of money or property

Etymology

From Latin pecuniarius (relating to money), from pecunia (money).
