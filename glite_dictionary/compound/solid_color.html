solid color

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑlɪd ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a color that is uniform and does not include patterns, gradients, or variations in shade
uniform color without pattern or variation

Examples

The artist chose a solid color background to emphasize the subject of the painting.
For this design, please select a solid color fabric without any prints or textures.
A solid color tie is often considered appropriate for formal business attire.

Synset Examples

Using an opacifier is essential for achieving that solid color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monochrome - a picture or design in shades of a single color
