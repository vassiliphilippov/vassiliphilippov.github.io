microscope stage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪ.kroʊˌskoʊp steɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krəˌskəʊp steɪdʒ / (BrE)
the flat platform on a microscope where a specimen is placed for observation
platform on microscope for specimen

Examples

The glass slide was carefully positioned on the microscope stage for examination.
Adjusting the microscope stage allows you to bring different parts of the specimen into view.
The microscope stage has clips to hold the slide securely during observation.
