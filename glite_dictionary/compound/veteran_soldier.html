veteran soldier

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛt̬.ɚn ˈsoʊl.dʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛt.ər.ən ˈsəʊl.dʒə / (BrE)
a member of the armed forces who has served for a long time or has seen a lot of active service in combat
experienced military serviceman

Examples

The platoon looked up to the veteran soldier for his leadership during tough missions.
After years on the front lines, the veteran soldier retired with honors.
New recruits listened intently as the veteran soldier shared stories of past battles.

Synset Examples

the veterans laughed at the new recruits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veteran - a person who has long experience in a particular field, especially military service
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation, especially in the military
