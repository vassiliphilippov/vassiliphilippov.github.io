magic underwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪk ˈʌndərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk ˈʌndəwɛə / (BrE)
Underwear believed to provide good luck or spiritual protection, sometimes referenced humorously or in relation to religious beliefs.
underwear believed to bring luck or protection

Examples

She swears her magic underwear will help her ace the interview.
Some people wear magic underwear for protection at major life events.
He laughed when she mentioned her lucky magic underwear.

Synset Examples

She joked about her magic underwear helping her win.
They believed their magic underwear would help them succeed.
He insists his magic underwear brings him good luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lucky charm - an object believed to bring good luck
  • Synonym: amulet - an ornament or piece of jewelry thought to give protection against evil, danger, or disease
