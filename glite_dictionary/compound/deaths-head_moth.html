death's-head moth

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθz hɛd moʊθ / (AmE), / ˈdɛθs hɛd məʊθ / (BrE)
A large European hawkmoth, notable for the skull-like marking on its back.
large moth with skull-like marking

Examples

The death's-head moth is famous for the skull-shaped pattern on its thorax.
She was fascinated by the unique markings of the death's-head moth she saw in the museum.
A death's-head moth flew into the garden at dusk, startling the children with its eerie design.
