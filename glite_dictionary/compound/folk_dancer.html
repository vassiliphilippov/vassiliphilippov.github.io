folk dancer

1. NOUN

/ foʊk ˈdænsər / (AmE), / fəʊk ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
A person who performs traditional folk dances, often as part of a group or in cultural events.
person who performs folk dances

Examples

She is a talented folk dancer who travels around the world performing.
The festival will feature a troupe of folk dancers from Hungary.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a professional folk dancer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dancer - a person who dances, especially professionally
  • Synonym: folk dance - a traditional dance that is characteristic of a particular people or region
