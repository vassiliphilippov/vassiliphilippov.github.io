Iraqi Kurdistan

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈrɑːki kɜːrˈdɪstɑːn / (AmE), / ɪˈrɑːki kɜːdˈɪstɑːn / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northwestern Iraq, predominantly inhabited by Kurds
autonomous Kurdish region in Iraq

Examples

She traveled to Iraqi Kurdistan to learn more about the local culture and politics.
Oil production plays a major role in the economy of Iraqi Kurdistan.
After years of conflict, Iraqi Kurdistan established a regional government with significant autonomy.

Etymology

Formed from 'Iraqi' (of Iraq) and 'Kurdistan' (land of the Kurds).
