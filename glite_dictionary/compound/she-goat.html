she-goat

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiˌɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈʃiːˌɡəʊt / (BrE)
A female goat.
female goat

Examples

The farmer kept a she-goat for milk production on her small homestead.
A single she-goat can provide enough milk for a family every day.
The she-goat grazed peacefully beside her young kids in the meadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nanny goat - female goat
  • Antonym: he-goat - male goat

Etymology

Formed by combining 'she-' (denoting female) with 'goat'.
