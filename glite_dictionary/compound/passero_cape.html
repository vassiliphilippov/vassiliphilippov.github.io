Passero Cape

1. PROPN

/ pɑˈsɛroʊ keɪp / (AmE), / pɑˈsɛərəʊ keɪp / (BrE)
A cape forming the southeastern tip of the island of Sicily, Italy.
cape at southeastern tip of Sicily

Examples

Many migratory birds can be seen near Passero Cape during the spring and autumn seasons.
The lighthouse at Passero Cape is a popular landmark for visitors exploring Sicily's coast.
Fishermen often gather at Passero Cape because of its rich marine life.
