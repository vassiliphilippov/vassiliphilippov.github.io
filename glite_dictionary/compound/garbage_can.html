garbage can

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ kæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ kan / (BrE)
A container, typically with a lid, used for holding household waste until it is collected.
container for household waste

Examples

We put the recycling in the blue garbage can outside.
The raccoons knocked over the garbage can last night, scattering trash everywhere.
Please remember to take out the garbage can before the truck arrives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash can - a bin for holding rubbish (primarily American English)
  • Synonym: dustbin - a British English term for a rubbish container
