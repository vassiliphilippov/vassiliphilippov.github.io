domestic goat

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ɡoʊt / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ɡəʊt / (BrE)
a domesticated species of goat kept for its milk, meat, or wool
domesticated species of goat

Examples

The domestic goat provides milk, cheese, and meat for people around the world.
A herd of domestic goats grazed quietly in the pasture.
Many farmers raise the domestic goat for its soft and valuable wool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goat - a hardy domesticated ruminant animal with backward curving horns
