Poyang Lake

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔɪ.jæŋ leɪk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.jæŋ leɪk / (BrE)
the largest freshwater lake in China, located in Jiangxi province and connected to the Yangtze River by a canal
lake in central China

Examples

Scientists have been monitoring water levels in Poyang Lake due to concerns about drought.
Migratory birds flock to Poyang Lake each winter, making it a key wildlife habitat.
The local fishermen depend on Poyang Lake for their livelihoods.
