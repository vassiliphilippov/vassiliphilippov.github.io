teaching reading

1. NOUN

/ ˈtitʃɪŋ ˈridiŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ ˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or profession of instructing beginners in how to read
instruction of beginners in reading

Examples

She specialized in teaching reading to young children in elementary schools.
Effective teaching reading strategies are essential for early childhood education.
Many teachers attend workshops on teaching reading methods to improve their classroom practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literacy instruction - teaching practices focused on developing reading and writing skills
