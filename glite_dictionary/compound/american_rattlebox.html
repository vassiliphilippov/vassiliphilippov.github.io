American rattlebox

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈrætlˌbɑks / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈræt(ə)lbɒks / (BrE)
A tropical American annual herb with inflated pods containing ripe seeds that rattle when shaken.
tropical plant with rattling seed pods

Examples

The seeds of the American rattlebox make a distinctive sound when the plant is shaken by the wind.
Many gardeners grow American rattlebox for its attractive flowers and interesting pods.
The American rattlebox is often found in warm climates across Central and South America.
