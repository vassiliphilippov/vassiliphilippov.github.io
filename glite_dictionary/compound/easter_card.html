Easter card

1. NOUN

/ ˈistɚ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈiːstə kɑːd / (BrE)
a greeting card sent to convey good wishes at Easter
greeting card for Easter

Examples

My grandmother always sends me a beautiful Easter card every spring.
We wrote a heartfelt message inside the Easter card for our neighbors.
The children made their own Easter cards at school to take home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a decorative card sent to convey good wishes
