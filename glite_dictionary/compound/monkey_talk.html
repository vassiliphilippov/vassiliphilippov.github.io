monkey talk

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki tɔk / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki tɔːk / (BrE)
Nonsense or trivial conversation; silly or inconsequential talk.
nonsense or trivial conversation
informal

Examples

During the meeting, his monkey talk distracted everyone from the agenda.
After a few minutes of monkey talk, the manager called the team to order.
She was tired of all the monkey talk and wanted to discuss serious matters.

Synset Examples

They indulged in monkey talk during their coffee break.
Let's avoid monkey talk and focus on the project.
His monkey talk made the meeting less serious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - language or ideas that are silly or have no meaning
  • Synonym: gibberish - spoken or written language that cannot be understood because it has no meaning or is nonsense
