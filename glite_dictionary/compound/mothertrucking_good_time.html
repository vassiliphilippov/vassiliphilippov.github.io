mothertrucking good time

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðərˌtrʌkɪŋ ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌtrʌkɪŋ ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
An extremely enjoyable experience; a very fun occasion, using a euphemistic intensifier for emphasis.
an extremely enjoyable experience
informal slang

Examples

We had a mothertrucking good time at the festival last night.
That party was a mothertrucking good time from start to finish!
Honestly, anyone looking for fun will have a mothertrucking good time at this place.

Synset Examples

She had a mothertrucking good time at the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good time - an enjoyable or pleasant experience

Etymology

Euphemistic alteration of the obscenity 'motherfucking', combined with 'good time'.
