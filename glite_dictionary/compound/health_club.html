health club

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ˌklʌb / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ˌklʌb / (BrE)
a place where people go to exercise, typically providing gym equipment, fitness classes, and other facilities for improving physical fitness
a fitness center or gym

Examples

She joined a health club to improve her overall fitness and meet new people.
Many health clubs offer group classes such as yoga, spinning, and Pilates.
The new health club downtown has a swimming pool and personal trainers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gym - a room or building equipped for physical exercise
