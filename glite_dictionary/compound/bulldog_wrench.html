bulldog wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌdɑɡ rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.dɒɡ rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a type of wrench designed to provide a firm, non-slip grip, often used for turning pipes or rounded fasteners
firm-grip wrench for pipes or rounded fasteners

Examples

The mechanic reached for a bulldog wrench to loosen the stubborn pipe fitting.
A bulldog wrench provides the extra grip needed on rusted bolts.
She bought a new set that included a bulldog wrench for plumbing tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe wrench - an adjustable wrench for gripping and turning pipes
