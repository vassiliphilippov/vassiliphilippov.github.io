guylike approach

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪˌlaɪk əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪˌlaɪk əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A method or style that is typical of, or stereotypically associated with, men.
stereotypically male method or style

Examples

His guylike approach to negotiations surprised his colleagues.
The manager's guylike approach sometimes clashes with the company's collaborative culture.
She found his guylike approach both refreshing and occasionally frustrating.

Synset Examples

He has a guylike approach to problem-solving.
