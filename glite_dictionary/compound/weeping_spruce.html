weeping spruce

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːpɪŋ sprus / (AmE), / ˈwiːpɪŋ spruːs / (BrE)
a medium-sized spruce tree of California and Oregon known for its pendulous, drooping branches
a spruce tree with drooping branches

Examples

The weeping spruce in their backyard is admired for its graceful, cascading branches.
Gardeners often plant weeping spruce to add visual interest to landscapes.
The nursery specializes in rare conifers, including the striking weeping spruce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spruce - an evergreen tree with needle-like leaves and cones
