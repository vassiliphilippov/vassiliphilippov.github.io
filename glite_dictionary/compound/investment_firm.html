investment firm

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvɛstmənt fɜrm / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstmənt fɜːm / (BrE)
a company that invests money on behalf of clients by selling shares and buying securities issued by other companies
company investing client funds in securities

Examples

She started her career at an investment firm specializing in technology stocks.
Many retirees trust an investment firm to manage their savings for the future.
The investment firm announced a new fund focused on sustainable energy projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investment company - a company whose main business is holding and managing securities for investment purposes
  • Synonym: brokerage - a firm that buys and sells securities for clients
