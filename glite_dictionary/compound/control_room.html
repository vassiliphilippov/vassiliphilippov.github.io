control room

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtroʊl ruːm / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl ruːm / (BrE)
a room equipped with instruments and controls for monitoring and managing operations, such as in a recording studio, power plant, or broadcast station
room for monitoring and control operations

Examples

The band watched the sound engineer at work in the control room through a glass window.
Security staff monitor all the cameras from the control room 24 hours a day.
The main power switch is located in the control room at the center of the facility.
