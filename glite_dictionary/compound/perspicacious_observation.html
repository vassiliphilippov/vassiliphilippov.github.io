perspicacious observation

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɜr.spɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs ˌɑːb.zɚˈveɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.spɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs ˌɒb.zəˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an observation or remark that demonstrates keen insight and sharp understanding
keen and insightful comment

Examples

Her perspicacious observation during the meeting helped clarify the main issue for everyone.
The reviewer’s perspicacious observation revealed subtle details most readers missed.
We appreciated the professor’s perspicacious observation about the causes behind the economic shift.

Synset Examples

Her perspicacious observations impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insightful remark - a comment showing clear understanding

Etymology

From Latin 'perspicax' (sharp-sighted) + English 'observation'.
