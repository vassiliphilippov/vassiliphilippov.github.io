fuckton of

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌtʌn ʌv / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌtʌn ɒv / (BrE)
a very large amount of something (used informally and considered vulgar)
large amount (vulgar, informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

She bought a fuckton of snacks for the party, more than anyone could possibly eat.
There's a fuckton of paperwork to get through before we can leave for the weekend.
He has a fuckton of experience in this industry despite his young age.

Synset Examples

I have a fuckton of laundry to do this weekend.
There was a fuckton of traffic on the way home.
He spent a fuckton of money on his new car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ton of - a large amount of something (informal)
  • Synonym: shitload of - a very large amount of something (vulgar)
  • Synonym: fuckload of - a very large amount of something (vulgar)

Etymology

Formed from the vulgar intensifier 'fuck' and 'ton' (meaning a large amount).
