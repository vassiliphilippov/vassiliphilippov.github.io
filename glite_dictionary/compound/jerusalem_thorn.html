Jerusalem thorn

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈruːsələm θɔrn / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm θɔːn / (BrE)
a thorny shrub native to Eurasia, known for its dry, woody, winged fruit; often cultivated or found in the wild in warm regions
thorny Eurasian shrub with winged fruit

Examples

The Jerusalem thorn produces beautiful yellow flowers in the spring.
We planted a Jerusalem thorn in the garden to provide some shade.
The distinctive fruits of the Jerusalem thorn are easily recognized by their winged appearance.
