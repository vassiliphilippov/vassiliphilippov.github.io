day care

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌkeə / (BrE)
care of children during the day while their parents are at work or otherwise occupied, typically provided at a facility or by a caregiver.
care of children during the day

Examples

She drops her son off at day care every morning before work.
Many parents rely on day care when school is out for the summer.
The new day care center opened in our neighborhood last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childcare - the care of children, especially by someone other than the child's parents
