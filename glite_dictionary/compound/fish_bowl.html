fish bowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ bəʊl / (BrE)
A situation in which a person or group has very little or no privacy, being observed by many people.
situation with no privacy

Examples

Being a celebrity means living in a fish bowl where everyone knows your business.
After becoming the principal, she found it hard to adjust to life in a fish bowl.
Reporters made the politician feel like he was trapped in a fish bowl.

Synset Examples

the president lives in a goldfish bowl

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldfish bowl - situation with little or no privacy

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ ˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌbəʊl / (BrE)
a clear, usually round container used to keep small fish as pets
glass bowl for keeping pet fish

Examples

She kept her goldfish in a large fish bowl on the kitchen table.
The children gazed curiously at the colorful fish swimming inside the fish bowl.
After cleaning the fish bowl, he returned it to its stand by the window.

Related Terms
