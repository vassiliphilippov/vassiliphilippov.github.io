terminal point

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜːrmɪnl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːmɪnəl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the final or end point in a process, path, or series, especially in mathematics or transportation contexts.
final or end point

Examples

The terminal point of the journey was marked by a large monument.
In mathematics, the terminal point of a vector is where it ends.
Each subway line has its own terminal point at the edge of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endpoint - a point marking the end of a line segment or process

Etymology

From Latin 'terminus' meaning end or boundary, and 'point', referring to a specific location.
