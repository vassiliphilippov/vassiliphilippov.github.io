sesame family

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛsəmi ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəmi ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, scientifically known as Pedaliaceae, which includes sesame and related species
plant family including sesame

Examples

The sesame family includes several species cultivated for their edible seeds and oil.
Researchers are studying plants in the sesame family for their drought resistance.
The sesame family comprises herbs and shrubs found mostly in tropical regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pedaliaceae - the botanical name for the sesame family
