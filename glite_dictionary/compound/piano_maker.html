piano maker

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / piˈænəʊ ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes pianos
person/company that makes pianos

Examples

The famous piano maker unveiled a new grand piano at the music convention.
Many apprentices studied under the skilled piano maker in Vienna.
She is the third generation in her family to become a piano maker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano tuner - a person who tunes pianos
