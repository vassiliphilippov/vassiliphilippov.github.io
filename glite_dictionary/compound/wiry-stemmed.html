wiry-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪri stɛmd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəri stɛmd / (BrE)
Having a thin, tough, and flexible stem resembling wire, especially used to describe certain plants.
having a tough, wire-like stem

Examples

The wiry-stemmed plant survived harsh winds on the coastline.
Many grasses are wiry-stemmed, allowing them to flex without breaking.
Gardeners often recommend wiry-stemmed varieties for rocky or exposed locations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: woody-stemmed - having a stem made of wood
