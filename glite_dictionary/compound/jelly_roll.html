jelly roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛli roʊl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli rəʊl / (BrE)
A thin sponge cake spread with jam or sweet filling and rolled up into a spiral.
rolled cake with sweet filling

Examples

My grandmother taught me how to bake a jelly roll for the holidays.
They enjoyed slices of jelly roll with their afternoon tea.
The bakery always sells out of jelly rolls during the festival.

Synset Examples

The kids loved the colorful jelly roll at the party.
I baked a delicious jelly roll for dessert.
She sliced the jelly roll and served it with whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Swiss roll - a rolled sponge cake filled with jam or cream, similar to a jelly roll
