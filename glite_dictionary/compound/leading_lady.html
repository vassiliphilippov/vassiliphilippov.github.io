leading lady

1. NOUN

/ ˈlidɪŋ ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈliːdɪŋ ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
an actress who plays the principal female role in a play, film, or other production
principal female role in play or film

Examples

Olivia Colman was cast as the leading lady in the new historical drama.
The director praised his leading lady for her emotional performance.
After years of supporting roles, she finally became the leading lady on Broadway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leading man - actor playing the principal male role in a production
