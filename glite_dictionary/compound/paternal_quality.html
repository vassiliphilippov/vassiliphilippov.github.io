paternal quality

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtɝːnəl ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəl ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
the kindness, protectiveness, and other traits typical of or suitable for a father
qualities typical of a father

Examples

Her mentor displayed a paternal quality that made her feel safe and supported as she started her new job.
The coach's paternal quality was evident in the way he cared for his young team.
In times of crisis, people often seek out those with a paternal quality for guidance and reassurance.

Synset Examples

the gentleness and fatherliness of the strange old man eased her fears

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatherliness - the state of having the qualities of a father

Etymology

From Latin 'paternus' (of a father) + 'quality' (from Latin 'qualitas').
