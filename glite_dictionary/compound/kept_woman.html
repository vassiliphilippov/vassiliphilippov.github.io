kept woman

1. NOUN

/ kɛpt ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / kɛpt ˈwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a woman who is financially supported by a lover, often in an extramarital relationship
woman financially supported by a lover

Examples

The wealthy businessman supported his kept woman in a luxurious apartment across town.
Rumors spread quickly in the village about who might be a kept woman.
She was tired of being a kept woman and wanted independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mistress - a woman who has a continuing extramarital sexual relationship with a man
