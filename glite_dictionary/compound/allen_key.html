Allen key

1. NOUN

/ ˈælən ki / (AmE), / ˈælən kiː / (BrE)
A tool with a hexagonal cross section, used to drive bolts and screws with a hexagonal socket.
hexagonal tool for socket screws

Examples

You need an Allen key to assemble most flat-pack furniture.
He searched the toolbox but couldn't find the right size Allen key.
The bicycle's seat can be adjusted easily with an Allen key.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hex key - tool for driving hexagonal socket screws
  • Degree Higher: wrench - tool for gripping and turning objects

Etymology

Named after the Allen Manufacturing Company, an early manufacturer.
