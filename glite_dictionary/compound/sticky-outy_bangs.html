sticky-outy bangs

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki ˈaʊti bæŋz / (AmE), / ˈstɪki ˈaʊti bæŋz / (BrE)
Bangs that are styled to stick out away from the forehead, rather than lying flat.
bangs that stick out from the forehead
informal

Examples

Her sticky-outy bangs gave her hairstyle a playful, unique look.
Every morning, he spends extra time shaping his sticky-outy bangs before leaving for work.
The stylist suggested sticky-outy bangs to add more volume and flair to my hair.

Synset Examples

She loves her sticky-outy bangs that frame her face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bangs - front part of the hair cut and styled to hang over the forehead
