football team

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl tim / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl tiːm / (BrE)
A group of players who form a team to play football (soccer or American football).
group of players forming a football team

Examples

Our town's football team won the championship last night.
She has supported the same football team since she was a child.
The coach praised his football team for their hard work and dedication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: football club - a club that has a football team
  • Synonym: football - the sport played by a football team
