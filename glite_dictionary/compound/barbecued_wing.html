barbecued wing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjuːd wɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuːd wɪŋ / (BrE)
a chicken wing cooked in barbecue sauce, usually grilled or baked
chicken wing cooked in barbecue sauce

Examples

I ordered a plate of barbecued wings for the table to share.
The smoky flavor of the barbecued wing made it my favorite appetizer.
During the game, we snacked on barbecued wings and nachos.

Related Terms
