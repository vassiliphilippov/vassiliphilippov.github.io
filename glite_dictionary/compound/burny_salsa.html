burny salsa

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːni ˈsɑːlsə / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni ˈsælsə / (BrE)
A spicy salsa that creates a burning sensation in the mouth.
spicy salsa causing burning sensation
informal

Examples

The burny salsa was so hot that even the bravest guests hesitated to try it.
She loves making burny salsa with extra jalapeños for her friends.
After just one bite of the burny salsa, his eyes began to water.

Synset Examples

She avoided the burny salsa at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spicy salsa - salsa with a strong, hot flavor
