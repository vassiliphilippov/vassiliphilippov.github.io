Western Australia coral pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ɔːˈstreɪliə ˈkɔːrəl piː / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ɔːˈstreɪlɪə ˈkɒrəl piː / (BrE)
a vigorous climbing plant native to the forests of Western Australia, known for its dense clusters of bright rose-purple flowers
West Australian climbing plant with rose-purple flowers

Examples

The Western Australia coral pea adds a splash of color to gardens with its vibrant flowers.
Many gardeners choose the Western Australia coral pea for its rapid growth and ornamental beauty.
In its native habitat, the Western Australia coral pea can be seen climbing over rocks and tree trunks.
