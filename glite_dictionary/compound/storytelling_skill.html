storytelling skill

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔriˌtɛlɪŋ skɪl / (AmE), / ˈstɔːritɛlɪŋ skɪl / (BrE)
the ability to tell stories in an engaging and effective way
ability to tell stories engagingly

Examples

Her storytelling skill captivated the audience from the very first sentence.
Good storytelling skill can make even the simplest events seem fascinating.
He has developed his storytelling skill over years of practice and experience.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows Zee for his amazing storytelling skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storytelling - the activity of telling stories
  • Synonym: narrative skill - ability to construct and present a narrative
