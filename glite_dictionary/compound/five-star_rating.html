five-star rating

1. NOUN

/ faɪv stɑr ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / faɪv stɑː ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a mark or classification indicating the highest standard or quality, typically on a five-point scale
the highest quality rating

Examples

The new hotel quickly earned a five-star rating for its exceptional service and amenities.
Many customers rely on a five-star rating to choose the best restaurants in town.
The film received a five-star rating from several prominent critics.

Synset Examples

The restaurant received a five-star rating from critics.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: four-star rating - a mark indicating one level below the highest quality

Etymology

From the use of 'star' symbols in rating systems, with five stars typically representing the maximum score.
