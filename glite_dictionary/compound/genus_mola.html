genus Mola

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmoʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈməʊ.lə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Molidae, comprising large ocean sunfishes.
genus of ocean sunfishes

Examples

The genus Mola includes some of the heaviest bony fish in the world.
Scientists classified the ocean sunfish under the genus Mola.
Members of the genus Mola are best known for their distinctive, flattened bodies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mola mola - the ocean sunfish, the most common species in the genus Mola

Etymology

From Latin 'mola', meaning millstone, referring to the shape of the fish.
