shaving cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪvɪŋ kriːm / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ kriːm / (BrE)
A substance applied to the face to produce a lather that softens the beard and aids in shaving.
lathering cream for shaving

Examples

He applied shaving cream to his face before picking up the razor.
This brand of shaving cream leaves your skin feeling smooth and refreshed.
You forgot to buy shaving cream at the store yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaving foam - a similar product, often dispensed as a foam
  • Antonym: aftershave - a lotion applied after shaving
