carpenter's saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz sɔː / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəntəz sɔː / (BrE)
a hand-operated saw used by carpenters for cutting wood
a hand saw used for cutting wood

Examples

He used a carpenter's saw to cut the wooden plank in half.
The old toolbox contained a hammer, nails, and a carpenter's saw.
With his carpenter's saw, he trimmed the door to fit the frame.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand saw - a saw with a handle operated by one hand
