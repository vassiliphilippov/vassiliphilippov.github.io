lock ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑk ˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒk ˌrɪŋ / (BrE)
A washer or circular part used to prevent a nut from loosening.
washer that keeps a nut from loosening

Examples

He installed a new lock ring to keep the wheel in place.
The mechanic checked that the lock ring was secure before finishing the repair.
If the lock ring is missing, the entire assembly could come loose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lock washer - a washer designed to prevent a nut or bolt from loosening
