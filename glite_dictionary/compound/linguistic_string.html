linguistic string

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk strɪŋ / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk strɪŋ / (BrE)
A linear sequence of words, as they appear in speech or writing, regarded as a unit in linguistics.
sequence of words in language

Examples

The parser analyzes each linguistic string to determine its grammatical structure.
Researchers often study linguistic strings to understand patterns in natural language.
In computational linguistics, a linguistic string can be processed by algorithms for translation.
