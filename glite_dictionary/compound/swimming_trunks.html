swimming trunks

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ trʌŋks / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ trʌŋks / (BrE)
Shorts designed to be worn by men or boys when swimming.
men's swim shorts

Examples

He packed his swimming trunks before heading to the beach.
Most pools require men to wear proper swimming trunks for hygiene reasons.
After the race, he wrung out his wet swimming trunks on the deck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming
  • Synonym: bathing suit - clothing worn for swimming or sunbathing
