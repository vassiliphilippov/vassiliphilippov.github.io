of unsound mind

1. ADJ

/ ʌv ʌnˈsaʊnd ˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / ɒv ʌnˈsaʊnd ˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
not having full mental capacity or understanding; legally not considered competent, especially for standing trial or making certain decisions
lacking mental capacity; legally incompetent
legal

Examples

The judge declared the defendant of unsound mind and unfit to stand trial.
Anyone found of unsound mind may not be allowed to sign legal documents.
The will was contested on the grounds that the testator was of unsound mind when it was made.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: of sound mind - having full mental capacity; legally competent
