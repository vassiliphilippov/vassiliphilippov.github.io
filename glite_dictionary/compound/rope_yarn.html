rope yarn

1. NOUN

/ roʊp jɑrn / (AmE), / rəʊp jɑːn / (BrE)
A strand or bundle of fibers that is twisted together with others to make a rope.
strand used to make rope

Examples

The old sailor showed us how to twist rope yarn into a sturdy cord.
Each length of rope yarn was carefully selected for strength before making the final rope.
Loose rope yarn can be used for minor repairs on a ship.
