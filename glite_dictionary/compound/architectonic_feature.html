architectonic feature

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑrkɪˈtɑnɪk ˈfiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɒnɪk ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
an element or component that forms part of the architectural design of a structure
element of architectural design

Examples

The museum is renowned for its unique architectonic features that blend modern and classical styles.
During the restoration, several original architectonic features were carefully preserved.
Large windows are an architectonic feature common in contemporary homes.

Synset Examples

The building's architectonic features impressed all visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: architectural feature - a distinguishing part of a building's structure

Etymology

From architectonic, relating to architecture or structured design (from Greek 'arkhitektonikos'), and feature, meaning a distinguishing part.
