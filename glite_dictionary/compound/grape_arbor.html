grape arbor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪp ˌɑːrbər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪp ˌɑːbə / (BrE)
A framework covered with climbing grapevines, typically used to support their growth and provide shade.
structure for growing grapevines

Examples

The old grape arbor in my grandmother's garden provided a cool, shady retreat in the summer.
Clusters of ripe fruit hung from the grape arbor near the back porch.
He built a wooden grape arbor to create a pleasant space for outdoor dinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pergola - A structure consisting of parallel colonnades supporting an open roof of girders and cross rafters
