toothed sword fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːt̬ɪd sɔrd fɝn / (AmE), / ˈtuːtɪd sɔːd fɜːn / (BrE)
A tropical American fern cultivated for its finely divided, greyish-green foliage, native to the West Indies and southern Mexico to Brazil.
tropical American fern with divided grey-green foliage

Examples

The toothed sword fern is a popular choice for adding lush texture to tropical gardens.
Gardeners in humid regions often use the toothed sword fern for ground cover in shaded areas.
The finely divided leaves of the toothed sword fern create an elegant look in botanical collections.
