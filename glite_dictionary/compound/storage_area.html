storage area

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a space in a ship or aircraft used for storing cargo
place on a ship or aircraft for cargo

Examples

The crew checked the storage area to ensure all the cargo was securely fastened.
Additional crates were placed in the aft storage area of the aircraft.
Access to the ship's storage area is restricted during the flight.
