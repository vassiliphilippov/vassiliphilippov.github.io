family Holocentridae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌhɑləˌsɛnˈtrɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˌhɒləsɛnˈtrɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine fish, known as squirrelfishes and soldierfishes, found mostly in tropical reefs.
family of squirrelfishes and soldierfishes

Examples

The family Holocentridae includes many brightly colored reef fish found in tropical waters.
Members of the family Holocentridae are often called squirrelfishes due to their appearance and nocturnal habits.
Biologists study the family Holocentridae to understand reef ecosystem dynamics.

Etymology

From scientific Latin 'Holocentridae', the family name formed from genus 'Holocentrus' + '-idae' (family suffix).
