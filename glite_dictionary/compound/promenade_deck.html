promenade deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑməˌneɪd dɛk / (AmE), / ˈprɒməˌnɑːd dɛk / (BrE)
a deck on a passenger ship where passengers can walk for pleasure, usually located on an upper level
deck for walking on a passenger ship

Examples

We spent the afternoon strolling along the promenade deck enjoying the sea breeze.
Several passengers gathered on the promenade deck to watch the sunset.
The cruise ship's promenade deck offers spectacular ocean views and plenty of seating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck - a floor on a ship

Etymology

From French 'promenade' (a walk or stroll), combined with 'deck', originally nautical.
