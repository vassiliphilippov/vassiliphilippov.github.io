grim-gram

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪm-ɡræm / (AmE), / ɡrɪm-ɡræm / (BrE)
A social media post that conveys negative or unpleasant themes.
negative social media post

Examples

After a tough week, he shared a grim-gram about his frustrations with work.
Sometimes her timeline feels overwhelming because there are too many grim-grams.
People unfollow accounts that post too many grim-grams to maintain a positive feed.

Synset Examples

The hashtag was trending due to multiple grim-grams.
He avoided posting grim-grams to stay positive.
Her social media was filled with grim-grams about the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doomscrolling - habit of scrolling through negative news online

Etymology

Blend of 'grim' (unpleasant, depressing) and 'Instagram' (social media platform).
