sun worshiper

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / sʌn ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who worships or reveres the sun, especially as part of a religion or ritual
person who worships the sun

Examples

Historically, the ancient Egyptians were well known as sun worshipers who revered Ra as their chief deity.
On summer holidays, she becomes a true sun worshiper, spending hours relaxing on the beach.
The festival attracted sun worshipers from around the world, all celebrating the return of spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sun worship - an act or practice of worshipping the sun
