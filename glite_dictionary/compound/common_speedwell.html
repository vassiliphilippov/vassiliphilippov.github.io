common speedwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈspidˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈspiːdwɛl / (BrE)
a hairy perennial plant native to Europe, with pale blue or lilac flowers in axillary racemes, belonging to the genus Veronica
