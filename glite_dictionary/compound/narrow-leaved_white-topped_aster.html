narrow-leaved white-topped aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæroʊ liːvd ˌwaɪt tɑpt ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːvd ˌwaɪt tɒpt ˈæstə / (BrE)
A variety of white-topped aster characterized by its narrow leaves.
a variety of white-topped aster with narrow leaves

Examples

The garden was brightened by the presence of several narrow-leaved white-topped asters in full bloom.
Experts recommend planting narrow-leaved white-topped aster for late-season color and pollinator support.
Identifying a narrow-leaved white-topped aster is possible by observing its slender leaves and distinctive white flowers.
