missile defense system

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsəl dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmɪsaɪl dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system designed to detect, track, and intercept incoming missiles to protect against attacks.
system for defending against missiles

Examples

The new missile defense system is capable of intercepting threats at long range.
Countries are investing heavily in missile defense systems to enhance national security.
The navy successfully tested its missile defense system during the joint military exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-missile system - a system specifically designed to intercept and destroy missiles
