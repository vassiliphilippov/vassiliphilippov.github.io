state of the art

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ði ɑrt / (AmE), / steɪt əv ði ɑːt / (BrE)
the most advanced level of development of a technique, technology, or field at a given time
the most advanced level of development

Examples

The laboratory is equipped with state of the art technology for genetic research.
Our hotel features a state of the art fitness center available to all guests.
This phone represents the state of the art in mobile communication.

Synset Examples

the state of the art in space travel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting edge - the most advanced stage in the development of something
  • Antonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
