God's work

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑdz wɝk / (AmE), / ɡɒdz wɜːk / (BrE)
selfless actions or service that benefit others, often regarded as serving a higher or divine purpose
selfless or divinely inspired service

Examples

Many believe that caring for the sick is God's work in action.
He devoted his entire life to doing God's work among the poor.
Some see teaching in underprivileged areas as a form of God's work.

Synset Examples

Volunteering at the shelter feels like doing God's work.
Helping others is truly God's work in our community.
She believes education is God's work for a better future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good deed - an act intended to help others
