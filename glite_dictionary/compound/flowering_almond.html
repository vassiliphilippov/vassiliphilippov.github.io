flowering almond

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚɪŋ ˈælmənd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈɑːlmənd / (BrE)
a woody shrub native to Asia, cultivated for its showy white or pale pink blossoms and smooth red fruit
ornamental Asian shrub with pink or white flowers

Examples

The flowering almond added a splash of color to the spring garden.
Gardeners often plant flowering almond for its delicate pink blossoms.
A row of flowering almond bushes lined the walkway, attracting bees and butterflies.

2. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈɑːlmənd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈɑːlmənd / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, cultivated for its pink or white flowers and often trilobed leaves
ornamental Chinese shrub with pink or white flowers

Examples

The flowering almond blooms early in the spring, brightening gardens with its delicate flowers.
They planted a row of flowering almonds along the fence to add color to the landscape.
After pruning, the flowering almond responded with an even more impressive display the following season.
