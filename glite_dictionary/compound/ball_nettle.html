ball nettle

1. NOUN

/ bɔl ˈnɛtəl / (AmE), / bɔːl ˈnɛtəl / (BrE)
a coarse, prickly weed with pale yellow flowers and yellow, berrylike fruit, commonly found in the southern and eastern United States
a prickly weed with yellow flowers and fruit

Examples

The ball nettle thrives in sandy soils throughout the southeastern states.
Gardeners often remove ball nettle because its prickly stems can be painful to touch.
The yellow fruit of the ball nettle resembles small berries, but they are not edible.
