Swiss Army knife

1. NOUN

/ swɪs ˈɑrmi naɪf / (AmE), / swɪs ˈɑːmi naɪf / (BrE)
A compact, multi-functional tool with several attachments, such as blades and screwdrivers, often used as a metaphor for someone or something with many skills or functions.
versatile multi-tool or multi-skilled person

Examples

He never goes hiking without his trusty Swiss Army knife in his backpack.
With her broad range of abilities, she is the Swiss Army knife of the team.
The Swiss Army knife includes scissors, a file, and a corkscrew among its many tools.

Synset Examples

The Swiss Army knife is perfect for camping trips.
He always carries a Swiss Army knife for emergencies.
Her multitasking skills are like a Swiss Army knife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multi-tool - a tool with multiple built-in functions

Etymology

Originally manufactured for the Swiss Army by Victorinox and Wenger since the late 19th century.
