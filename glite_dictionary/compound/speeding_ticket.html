speeding ticket

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːdɪŋ ˌtɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈspiːdɪŋ ˌtɪkɪt / (BrE)
an official notice issued by police to a driver who is caught driving faster than the allowed speed limit
official notice for driving above speed limit

Examples

She received a speeding ticket for driving 20 miles over the limit.
Paying off his speeding ticket cost him more than he expected.
If you get a speeding ticket, your insurance rates might increase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking ticket - an official notice issued for parking illegally
