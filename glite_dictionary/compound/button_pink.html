button pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtən pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ən pɪŋk / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the pink family (Dianthus species), having many branches and clusters of small, rounded pink flowers; closely related to sweet William.
a pink-flowered perennial plant

Examples

The button pink is often found in cottage gardens due to its attractive blossoms.
She planted button pinks alongside sweet Williams to create a vibrant border.
Botanists admire the button pink for its hardiness and lovely cluster-shaped flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet William - A species of Dianthus with clusters of bright-colored flowers
