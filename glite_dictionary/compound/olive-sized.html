olive-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːlɪv saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv saɪzd / (BrE)
about the same size as an olive; as large as an olive
about the size of an olive

Examples

The doctor found an olive-sized lump in her neck during the examination.
She handed me an olive-sized stone she had picked up on the beach.
The rare insect was only olive-sized, making it difficult to spot in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea-sized - about the size of a pea
  • Synonym: grape-sized - about the size of a grape
  • Degree Higher: egg-sized - about the size of an egg
