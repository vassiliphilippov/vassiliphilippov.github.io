amusement arcade

1. NOUN

/ əˈmjuzmənt ɑrˈkeɪd / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzmənt ɑːˈkeɪd / (BrE)
a place or venue containing coin-operated game machines for entertainment, such as video games and pinball machines
venue with coin-operated game machines

Examples

The children spent all afternoon at the amusement arcade playing racing games and winning tickets.
In the seaside town, the amusement arcade was crowded with tourists enjoying the old pinball machines.
He saved his pocket money so he could visit the amusement arcade every Saturday with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arcade - a covered passage with arches along one or both sides; also, a venue with coin-operated machines
