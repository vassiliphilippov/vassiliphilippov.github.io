true Connecticunt

1. NOUN

/ tru kəˈnɛtɪkʌnt / (AmE), / tru kəˈnɛtɪkʌnt / (BrE)
A highly derogatory and offensive slang term for an unpleasant or disliked person from Connecticut.
derogatory term for an unpleasant person from Connecticut
slang offensive vulgar american

Examples

His rude comments about the state made him a true Connecticunt.
People called her a true Connecticunt after she insulted everyone at the gathering.
Referring to someone as a true Connecticunt is likely to cause offense and tension.
