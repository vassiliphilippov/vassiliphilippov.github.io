cultural scene

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌl.tʃɚl siːn / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl siːn / (BrE)
the artistic and cultural life, events, and activities in a particular place or community
artistic and cultural activities of a place

Examples

The city's cultural scene attracts artists and performers from around the world.
Berlin's cultural scene is vibrant, with numerous galleries and music venues.
Tourists are often impressed by the rich cultural scene found in New Orleans.

Synset Examples

Joburg is known for its rich cultural scene and nightlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art scene - the community and activities related to the arts in a specific area
  • Synonym: nightlife - social activities or entertainment available at night in a town or city
