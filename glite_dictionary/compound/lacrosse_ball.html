lacrosse ball

1. NOUN

/ ləˈkrɔs bɔl / (AmE), / ləˈkrɒs bɔːl / (BrE)
a small rubber ball used in the sport of lacrosse
small rubber ball for lacrosse

Examples

The referee inspected the lacrosse ball before the start of the game.
He forgot to bring an extra lacrosse ball to practice.
The kids played catch using a worn-out lacrosse ball in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lacrosse - a team sport played with a stick and ball
