biggest buttmuncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɡəst ˈbʌtˌmʌntʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡɪst ˈbʌtmʌntʃə / (BrE)
An extremely annoying or bothersome person (slang, insult, humorous or rude).
an extremely irritating person (slang, humorous insult)
slang informal vulgar

Examples

Everyone thinks Brad is the biggest buttmuncher in the office when he acts like that.
Why does she always have to be the biggest buttmuncher at family events?
He was voted "biggest buttmuncher" as a joke in the yearbook.

Synset Examples

She’s the biggest buttmuncher in our group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttmunch - an annoying or contemptible person (slang)

Etymology

From slang "buttmunch" (an annoying or contemptible person), with 'biggest' as an intensifier.
