yellow horned poppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ hɔrnd ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ hɔːnd ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
a yellow-flowered Eurasian plant (Glaucium flavum) with glaucous leaves, naturalized along sandy shores in eastern North America
a yellow-flowered coastal plant

Examples

The yellow horned poppy brightens the dunes with its striking yellow flowers.
Botanists discovered a cluster of yellow horned poppy growing near the shoreline.
The leaves of the yellow horned poppy are thick and bluish-green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy - a flowering plant of the family Papaveraceae
