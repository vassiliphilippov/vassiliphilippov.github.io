spirit rapping

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪt ˌræpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌræpɪŋ / (BrE)
a supposed form of communication with spirits involving rapping sounds, believed to be responses from spirits of the dead
alleged communication with spirits by rapping sounds

Examples

The group gathered in the dark room, convinced that spirit rapping would allow them to contact their lost relatives.
Nineteenth-century séances often featured spirit rapping as a dramatic sign of ghostly presence.
Skeptics argued that spirit rapping was nothing more than clever trickery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiritism - the belief or doctrine that spirits of the dead can communicate with the living
