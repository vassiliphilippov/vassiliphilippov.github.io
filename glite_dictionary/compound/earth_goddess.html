earth goddess

1. NOUN

/ ɜɹθ ˈɡɑdəs / (AmE), / ɜːθ ˈɡɒdɪs / (BrE)
a goddess associated with the earth, fertility, and plant life in various mythologies and religions
goddess linked to earth and fertility

Examples

Many ancient cultures worshipped an earth goddess as the source of fertility and abundance.
The statue depicts an earth goddess surrounded by flowers and harvest symbols.
The earth goddess was believed to protect the land and ensure successful crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother earth - the personification of the earth as a nurturing mother; similar mythological figure
  • Antonym: earth god - a male deity associated with the earth
