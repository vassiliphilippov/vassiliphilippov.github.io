gin rickey

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪn ˈrɪki / (AmE), / dʒɪn ˈrɪki / (BrE)
A cocktail made with gin, lime juice, and carbonated water, usually served over ice.
gin-based cocktail with lime and soda

Examples

She ordered a gin rickey to enjoy on the patio.
A refreshing gin rickey is perfect for a hot summer afternoon.
He learned how to mix a classic gin rickey at bartending school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rickey - a cocktail of liquor, lime juice, and carbonated water

Etymology

Rickey: from Colonel Joe Rickey, 19th-century American lobbyist, who popularized the drink.
