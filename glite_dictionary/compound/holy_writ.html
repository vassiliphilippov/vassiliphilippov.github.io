Holy Writ

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊli ˈrɪt / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈrɪt / (BrE)
the sacred writings of Christianity; the Bible.
the Christian scriptures; the Bible

Examples

Many believers turn to Holy Writ for guidance during challenging times.
He devoted his life to studying Holy Writ and sharing its teachings.
The church service began with a reading from Holy Writ.

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible - the Christian scriptures; the sacred book of Christianity
  • Synonym: Scripture - the sacred writings of Christianity contained in the Bible

Etymology

From Old English 'hālig' (holy) + 'writ' (writing, scripture).
