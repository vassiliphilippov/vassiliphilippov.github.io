North Sea

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ si / (AmE), / nɔːθ siː / (BrE)
the sea located between Great Britain, Scandinavia, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, and France, forming part of the Atlantic Ocean
sea between Britain and Scandinavia

Examples

Oil rigs dot the surface of the North Sea, extracting valuable resources from beneath its waves.
Fishing has long been a major industry in the North Sea region.
Many ferries cross the North Sea between Britain and mainland Europe.
