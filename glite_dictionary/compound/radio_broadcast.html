radio broadcast

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ ˈbrɔːdkæst / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˈbrɔːdkɑːst / (BrE)
a program or transmission sent out by radio; a broadcast transmitted over the radio waves
broadcast transmitted via radio

Examples

The live radio broadcast brought breaking news directly into people’s homes.
She hosted a popular radio broadcast every Monday evening.
Many listeners tuned in to the radio broadcast of the concert last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcast - a program sent out by radio or television
  • Antonym: television broadcast - a broadcast transmitted by television
