hawk owl

1. NOUN

/ hɑk ˈaʊl / (AmE), / hɔːk ˈaʊl / (BrE)
A grey-and-white diurnal, hawk-like owl found in the northern parts of the Northern Hemisphere.
diurnal hawk-like owl of northern regions

Examples

The hawk owl is well adapted to hunting during the northern daylight hours.
Birdwatchers in Scandinavia frequently hope to spot a rare hawk owl during winter.
Unlike most owls, the hawk owl is active during the day, searching for small mammals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Northern hawk owl - a particular species of hawk owl (Surnia ulula)
