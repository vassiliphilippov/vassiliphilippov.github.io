sulphur mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌlfər maɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə maɪn / (BrE)
a mine where sulphur is extracted from the ground
a mine for extracting sulphur

Examples

The company invested heavily in a new sulphur mine in Indonesia.
Many workers found employment at the local sulphur mine throughout the twentieth century.
Environmental regulations require strict monitoring at any sulphur mine to prevent pollution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mine - an excavation in the earth for extracting minerals
