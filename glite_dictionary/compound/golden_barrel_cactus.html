golden barrel cactus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈbærəl ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈbærəl ˈkaktəs / (BrE)
a large, round cactus native to central Mexico, with golden-yellow spines and pale yellow flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant
a round cactus with golden spines

Examples

The golden barrel cactus is a popular choice for rock gardens because of its striking appearance.
Several golden barrel cacti were planted along the path to add color and texture.
A mature golden barrel cactus can grow over a foot in diameter and live for many decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Echinocactus grusonii - scientific name for the golden barrel cactus
