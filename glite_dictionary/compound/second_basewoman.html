second basewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈbeɪsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈbeɪsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
(baseball) a female player who plays the position of second base on a team.
female baseball player at second base

Examples

She became the first second basewoman to win the league's defensive award.

Several talented second basewomen competed for a spot on the national team.
