visiting firewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ ˈfaɪərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ ˈfaɪəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
an important or distinguished female visitor, often from another organization or location
important or distinguished female visitor

Examples

The conference hosted a visiting firewoman from a leading research institute in London.
As a visiting firewoman, she was given a tour of the entire facility and met with senior staff.
The school's faculty were eager to hear insights from the visiting firewoman during the special seminar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visiting fireman - an important or distinguished visitor, usually male

Etymology

Modelled on 'visiting fireman', itself from U.S. slang referring to a traveling inspector or dignitary.
