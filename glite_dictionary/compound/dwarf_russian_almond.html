dwarf Russian almond

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf ˈrʌʃən ˈɑlmənd / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈrʌʃən ˈɑːlmənd / (BrE)
A small Asiatic shrub (Prunus tenella) cultivated for its rosy red flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.
ornamental Asiatic shrub with rosy red flowers

Examples

The dwarf Russian almond is prized for its vibrant display in spring gardens.
Gardeners often choose the dwarf Russian almond to create colorful borders.
Despite its name, the dwarf Russian almond is native to parts of Asia rather than Russia.
