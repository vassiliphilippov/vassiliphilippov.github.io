portable circular saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrtəbəl ˈsɝkjələr sɔ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl ˈsɜːkjʊlə sɔː / (BrE)
A circular saw designed to be easily carried and operated by hand, typically featuring a handle and used for cutting wood, metal, or other materials.
handheld circular power saw

Examples

He used a portable circular saw to quickly cut the plywood sheets on the construction site.
Always wear protective goggles when operating a portable circular saw for safety reasons.
The carpenter replaced his old saw with a lightweight portable circular saw for greater convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular saw - a power-saw with a toothed circular blade
