great toe

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt toʊ / (AmE), / ɡreɪt təʊ / (BrE)
the first and largest innermost toe of the foot; the big toe
the big toe; first, largest toe
medical

Examples

The great toe provides support and balance when walking or running.
He fractured his great toe while playing football last weekend.
Swelling of the great toe is a common symptom of gout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big toe - the largest toe of the foot
