visual communication

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The transmission of information and ideas using visual elements such as signs, symbols, images, and design.
communication using visual elements

Examples

Modern advertising relies heavily on visual communication to convey messages quickly and effectively.
Artists use visual communication to express emotions that words sometimes cannot capture.
Effective visual communication is crucial for designing user-friendly websites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonverbal communication - communication without the use of words, including visual, gestures, and facial expressions
