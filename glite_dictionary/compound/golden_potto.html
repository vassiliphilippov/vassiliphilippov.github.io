golden potto

1. NOUN

/ ˈgoʊldən ˈpɑtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈpɒtəʊ / (BrE)
a small, yellowish primate native to West Africa, belonging to the species Arctocebus calabarensis
a yellowish West African primate

Examples

The golden potto is found in the tropical forests of West Africa.
Researchers observed the golden potto feeding on insects and fruit at night.
Because of habitat loss, the golden potto faces growing threats in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potto - a slow-moving African primate related to the loris
