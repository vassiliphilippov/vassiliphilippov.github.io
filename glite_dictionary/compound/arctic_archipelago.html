Arctic Archipelago

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːrktɪk ˌɑːrtʃɪˈpɛləˌgoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːktɪk ˌɑːtʃɪˈpɛləɡəʊ / (BrE)
a group of islands located north of mainland Canada and within or near the Arctic Circle
islands north of mainland Canada in the Arctic

Examples

The Arctic Archipelago is home to unique wildlife adapted to extreme cold.
Explorers have long navigated the icy waters of the Arctic Archipelago.
Much of the Arctic Archipelago remains uninhabited due to its harsh climate.
