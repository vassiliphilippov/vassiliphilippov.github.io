chesticle problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛstɪkəl ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛstɪkəl ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term referring to issues or concerns related to a man's chest, such as the development of male breasts.
humorous term for male breast issues
informal slang

Examples

His doctor told him not to worry about his chesticle problem and recommended simple exercises.
He joked about his chesticle problem during the comedy show, making the audience laugh.
After years of ignoring it, he finally sought advice for his chesticle problem.

Synset Examples

He was embarrassed to admit he had a chesticle problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gynecomastia - medical term for the enlargement of male breast tissue

Etymology

Blend of 'chest' and 'testicle,' used humorously to refer to male chest issues.
