musical harmony

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈhɑːrməni / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈhɑːməni / (BrE)
the aspect of music concerned with the composition and progression of chords to create a pleasing sound
structure of music relating to chords

Examples

Understanding musical harmony is essential for composing complex pieces of music.
The choir director explained the basics of musical harmony to the students before rehearsal.
Jazz musicians often experiment with different forms of musical harmony during improvisation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmony - the combination of simultaneously sounded musical notes to produce chords and chord progressions
