ball cock

1. NOUN

/ bɔl kɑk / (AmE), / bɔːl kɒk / (BrE)
A valve operated by a floating ball, used to regulate the level of liquid in a tank, especially in toilets and cisterns.
valve with floating ball controlling water level

Examples

The plumber replaced the faulty ball cock to stop the tank from overflowing.
Water in the toilet cistern is regulated by a ball cock mechanism.
If the float on the ball cock gets stuck, the tank may not refill properly.

Etymology

From 'ball' (referring to the float) and 'cock' (an old term for a valve or tap).
