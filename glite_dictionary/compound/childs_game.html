child's game

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪldz ɡeɪm / (AmE), / tʃaɪldz ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game that is played and enjoyed by children
game played by children

Examples

Hopscotch is a classic child's game that requires only chalk and a small stone.
They taught the foreign visitors a traditional child's game from their village.
Some adults still remember the excitement of playing their favorite child's game years ago.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game - an activity that one engages in for amusement or fun
