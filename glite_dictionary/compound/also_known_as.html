also known as

1. ADV

/ ˌɔlsoʊ ˈnoʊn æz / (AmE), / ˌɔːlsəʊ ˈnəʊn æz / (BrE)
used to indicate another name or identity by which someone or something is also called
another name or identity

Examples

The author J.K. Rowling is also known as Robert Galbraith.
The street is also known as Main Avenue in old maps.
Leonardo da Vinci was also known as the Renaissance man for his broad interests.

Synset Examples

Mr. Smith, alias Mr. Lafayette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alias - another name or assumed identity
