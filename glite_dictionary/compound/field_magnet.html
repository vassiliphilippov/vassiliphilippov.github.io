field magnet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌmæɡ.nət / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌmæɡ.nət / (BrE)
a magnet that creates the magnetic field in a dynamo or electric motor
magnet producing magnetic field in a dynamo or motor

Examples

The field magnet is essential for generating the necessary magnetic flux in electric motors.
Replacing the field magnet restored the generator's original performance.
In some dynamos, the field magnet is made from soft iron instead of permanent magnets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electromagnet - a magnet created by electric current
