online shopping

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑnˌlaɪn ˈʃɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn ˈʃɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of buying goods or services over the internet
buying goods or services on the internet

Examples

Many people find online shopping more convenient than going to physical stores.
The pandemic led to a sharp increase in online shopping worldwide.
She prefers online shopping because of the wide variety and easy price comparisons.

Synset Examples

The survey revealed 41% of people prefer online shopping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-commerce - commercial transactions conducted electronically on the internet
