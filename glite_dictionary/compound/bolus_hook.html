bolus hook

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊləs hʊk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləs hʊk / (BrE)
a device in a Jacquard loom used to raise or lower the harness, controlling the movement of warp threads to create patterns.
device in Jacquard looms for harness control

Examples

Each bolus hook in the Jacquard loom must be carefully adjusted to ensure precise patterning.
A damaged bolus hook can disrupt the entire weaving process by misguiding the warp threads.
Technicians inspected the bolus hook for wear before starting the loom.
