drop-leaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑp ˌlif / (AmE), / ˈdrɒp ˌliːf / (BrE)
a hinged section of a table that can be raised to extend the surface and supported by a bracket or similar mechanism
hinged extendable leaf on a table

Examples

The antique table had a drop-leaf that could be lifted for extra guests.
We folded down the drop-leaf to save space in the kitchen.
This desk features a convenient drop-leaf for added workspace when you need it.
