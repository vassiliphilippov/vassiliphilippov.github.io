pity porn

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪti pɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɪti pɔːn / (BrE)
Content, especially on social media or in fundraising, that is designed to elicit strong feelings of sympathy or pity in a way that may be manipulative or exploitative.
content designed to provoke excessive sympathy
informal slang

Examples

Some reality shows exploit contestants' hardships for pity porn rather than genuine storytelling.
His charity campaign was criticized as pity porn that objectified its subjects.
Social media can be full of pity porn, playing on viewers' emotions for engagement.

Synset Examples

Her posts often veer into pity porn territory.
Scrolling through social media felt like watching pity porn.
He realized his story was just pity porn for likes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poverty porn - depiction of poor people to evoke sympathy, often in an exploitative way

Etymology

Formed by combining "pity" and "porn," extending "-porn" as a suffix to refer to something exploitative or sensational.
