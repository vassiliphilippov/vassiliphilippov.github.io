officer-involved incident

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːfəsər ɪnˈvɑːlvd ˈɪnsɪdənt / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪsə ɪnˈvɒlvd ˈɪnsɪdənt / (BrE)
an event or situation involving the participation or actions of one or more police officers, especially when there are significant legal or public interest consequences
event involving police officers

Examples

The police chief addressed the media about the officer-involved incident that occurred downtown last night.
The city has strict protocols for handling any officer-involved incident to ensure transparency and accountability.
Community leaders demanded answers after the recent officer-involved incident sparked widespread protests.

Synset Examples

An investigation is underway for the officer-involved incident.
