toasty sweater

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊsti ˈswɛtər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊsti ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
A sweater that is particularly warm and cozy, providing comfort in cold weather.
warm, cozy sweater

Examples

She wrapped herself in her toasty sweater before heading outside on the chilly morning.
After coming in from the snow, he changed into a toasty sweater and thick wool socks.
My grandma knitted me a toasty sweater that keeps me warm all winter.

Synset Examples

I love wearing my toasty sweater in winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweater - a knitted garment worn on the upper body for warmth
  • Synonym: jumper - (British) a warm knitted garment worn on the upper body
