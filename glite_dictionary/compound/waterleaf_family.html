waterleaf family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːtərˌliːf ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəliːf ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family (Hydrophyllaceae) of perennial herbaceous plants found mainly in woodlands, including the waterleaf and related species.
family of woodland herbs (Hydrophyllaceae)

Examples

The waterleaf family includes several species with attractive lavender flowers.
Many wildflowers in the waterleaf family thrive in shaded forest environments.
Botanists consider the waterleaf family closely related to the borage family.
