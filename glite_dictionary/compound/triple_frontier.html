Triple Frontier

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrɪpəl frʌnˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl frɒnˈtɪə / (BrE)
the border region where Argentina, Brazil, and Paraguay meet, known as a center for contraband, drug trafficking, and money laundering
border region of Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay

Examples

The Triple Frontier is notorious for its lawlessness and cross-border smuggling.
Authorities increased security operations in the Triple Frontier after reports of illegal activities.
Many journalists have investigated criminal networks operating in the Triple Frontier area.
