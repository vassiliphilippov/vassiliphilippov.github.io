people's choice award

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːpəlz tʃɔɪs əˈwɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəlz tʃɔɪs əˈwɔːd / (BrE)
an award given to the winner chosen by public vote, rather than by a panel of judges
award decided by public voting

Examples

She won the people's choice award for her outstanding performance in the film.
The museum received a people's choice award for its innovative exhibit.
Voting for the people's choice award closes at midnight tonight.

Synset Examples

The people's choice awards celebrate popular favorites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award - something given as a prize or recognition
