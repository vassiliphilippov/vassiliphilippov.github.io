round-leaved rein orchid

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd liːvd reɪn ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / raʊnd liːvd reɪn ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A species of orchid with large greenish-white flowers, growing on a single stalk between two round or elliptic basal leaves, native to North America.
a species of North American orchid

Examples

The round-leaved rein orchid thrives in damp meadows across North America.
Botanists studied the leaves of the round-leaved rein orchid to help identify it.
We found several round-leaved rein orchids growing along the edge of the forest.
