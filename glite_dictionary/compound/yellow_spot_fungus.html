yellow spot fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ spɑt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ spɒt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes yellow spot, a disease affecting sugarcane, particularly in Australia
fungus causing yellow spot disease in sugarcane

Examples

Researchers found that the yellow spot fungus was responsible for the decline in sugarcane yields in Queensland.
The spread of yellow spot fungus can be controlled by using resistant varieties of sugarcane.
Farmers are advised to monitor fields for signs of yellow spot fungus during the growing season.
