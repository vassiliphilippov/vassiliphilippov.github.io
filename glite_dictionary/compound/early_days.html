early days

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli deɪz / (AmE), / ˈɜːli deɪz / (BrE)
the initial period of development or activity in a process, project, or event
initial period or beginning stage

Examples

We're still in the early days of the research, so results are preliminary.
The company faced many challenges in its early days.
It's only the early days of the project, but the team is optimistic.

Synset Examples

  • Antonym: end - the final part or conclusion of something
