masc side

1. NOUN

/ mæsk saɪd / (AmE), / mæsk saɪd / (BrE)
the aspect of a person's character or behavior that expresses traditionally masculine traits
aspect expressing masculine traits or behavior
informal

Examples

In her new job, she feels comfortable showing her masc side around her colleagues.
Many people grapple with how to balance their fem and masc side in different situations.
During the debate, he defended his actions as a way of expressing his masc side.

Synset Examples

He expressed his masc side during the competition.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fem side - the aspect expressing traditionally feminine traits or behavior

Etymology

Shortened form of "masculine side." "Masc" is a clipping of "masculine."
