steam fitting

1. NOUN

/ stiːm ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / stiːm ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the installation, maintenance, and repair of equipment and pipes for steam, heating, ventilation, or refrigeration systems
installation/maintenance of steam or heating equipment

Examples

He learned steam fitting as an apprentice and went on to install heating systems in office buildings.
The hospital's maintenance department is responsible for all steam fitting and ventilation work.
Advancements in steam fitting have made building heating much more efficient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumbing - the installation and repair of water supply and drainage systems

Etymology

From 'steam' (vaporized water) + 'fitting' (a part used to join pipes).
