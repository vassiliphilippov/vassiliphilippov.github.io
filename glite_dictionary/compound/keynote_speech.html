keynote speech

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiˌnoʊt spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkiːnəʊt spiːtʃ / (BrE)
A speech, usually at a meeting or conference, that sets out the central theme or most important message.
main speech highlighting a central theme

Examples

The conference began with a keynote speech delivered by a leading scientist.
She was invited to give the keynote speech at the annual charity gala.
His inspiring keynote speech set the tone for the rest of the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: address - a formal speech delivered to an audience
  • Synonym: lecture - an educational speech given to an audience
