iced-tea spoon

1. NOUN

/ aɪst ti spun / (AmE), / aɪst tiː spuːn / (BrE)
A long-handled teaspoon used for stirring iced tea or other tall beverages.
long-handled teaspoon for tall drinks

Examples

He stirred his lemonade with an iced-tea spoon to dissolve the sugar.
The iced-tea spoon reached all the way to the bottom of the tall glass.
She bought a set of iced-tea spoons for serving summer drinks.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring tea or coffee and eating some foods
