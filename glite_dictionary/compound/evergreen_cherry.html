evergreen cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡrin ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A small, spiny-leaved evergreen shrub or tree native to California, bearing white flowers and commonly known as the California wild plum.
California evergreen shrub or tree; wild plum

Examples

The evergreen cherry is often planted in California gardens for its attractive foliage.
Several birds nest in the branches of the evergreen cherry each spring.
Botanists studied the ecology of the evergreen cherry in the wild coastal regions.
