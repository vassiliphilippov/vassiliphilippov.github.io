melodic theme

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɑː.dɪk θiːm / (AmE), / məˈlɒ.dɪk θiːm / (BrE)
the principal melodic idea or subject in a musical composition, forming the basis for development and variation
main melodic subject in music

Examples

The composer's melodic theme recurs throughout the symphony, unifying the movements.
The pianist introduced a gentle melodic theme that set the mood for the entire piece.
Each variation is based on the original melodic theme established at the beginning.

Synset Examples

the theme is announced in the first measures
the accompanist picked up the idea and elaborated it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theme - the subject or recurring idea in a piece of music
