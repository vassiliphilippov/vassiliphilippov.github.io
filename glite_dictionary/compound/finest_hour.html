finest hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəst ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnɪst ˈaʊə / (BrE)
the moment of greatest success or achievement in a person's life or career
moment of greatest achievement

Examples

Winning the Nobel Prize was undoubtedly her finest hour.
The team's finest hour came when they won the championship against all odds.
He will always be remembered for his finest hour during the rescue.

Synset Examples

She considers her speech to be her finest hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowning moment - the most triumphant or significant moment
  • Antonym: lowest point - the worst or most difficult moment
