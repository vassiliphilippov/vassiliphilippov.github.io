writing table

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪ.tɪŋ ˌteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.tɪŋ ˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a desk or table with a flat surface, used for writing, often equipped with drawers or compartments
table or desk for writing

Examples

The antique writing table in her study was made of mahogany.
He placed his books neatly on the writing table before starting his letter.
A lamp and notebook sat at the corner of the writing table by the window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat top for writing or working
