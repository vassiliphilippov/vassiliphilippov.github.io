waiting area

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪtɪŋ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a room or designated space with seating where people can wait, often found in places like airports, hospitals, or hotels
room or space for people to wait

Examples

We sat quietly in the waiting area until the doctor called our name.
The airport's waiting area was crowded with travelers and their luggage.
Please proceed to the waiting area and someone will assist you shortly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lounge - a public room for waiting or relaxing, often in public buildings
