onion dome

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.jən doʊm / (AmE), / ˈʌn.jən dəʊm / (BrE)
A dome shaped like a bulb, typically found in Russian and Byzantine church architecture.
bulb-shaped dome, often in Russian churches

Examples

The famous cathedral is recognized for its colorful onion dome towers.
Many tourists photograph the impressive onion dome on top of the Orthodox church.
Architects studied traditional Russian designs before recreating the onion dome in their project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dome - a rounded roof or vault, forming part of a building
