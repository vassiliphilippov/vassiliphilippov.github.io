bracketed blenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈbræk.ɪ.tɪd ˈblɛni / (AmE), / ˈbræk.ɪ.tɪd ˈblɛni / (BrE)
a small, eel-like fish commonly found in shallow waters of the northern Atlantic
small Atlantic eel-like fish

Examples

The bracketed blenny is often seen darting between rocks in tidal pools along the coast.
Marine biologists studied the feeding habits of the bracketed blenny in their natural habitat.
A bracketed blenny can camouflage itself easily among seaweed and stones.
