soyboyish style

1. NOUN

/ sɔɪˌbɔɪɪʃ ˈstaɪl / (AmE), / sɔɪˌbɔɪɪʃ ˈstaɪl / (BrE)
a style or manner of dress and behavior associated with men perceived as lacking traditional masculinity, often characterized by a soft or non-aggressive appearance
style linked to non-masculine men
slang informal

Examples

He embraced his soyboyish style, not caring what others thought.
Many criticized his soyboyish style, but it reflected his genuine personality.
The magazine featured a guide to achieving a soyboyish style for modern men.

Synset Examples

He embraced his soyboyish style, not caring what others thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soyboy - a man regarded as lacking traditional masculine qualities
  • Degree Lower: metrosexual - a man especially meticulous about grooming and appearance
  • Synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly

Etymology

Formed from 'soyboy' (slang term implying lack of masculinity, from 'soy' plus 'boy') and '-ish' (having the qualities of), plus 'style'.
