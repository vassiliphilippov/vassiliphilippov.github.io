cocktail dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌteɪl drɛs / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl drɛs / (BrE)
a woman's dress suitable for semi-formal or formal evening occasions, typically shorter and more elegant than a day dress
elegant dress for semi-formal events

Examples

She wore a stunning cocktail dress to the awards ceremony.
The invitation called for a cocktail dress or smart suit.
I bought a new black cocktail dress for the company party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening gown - a long formal dress for evening wear
  • Antonym: day dress - a dress worn for ordinary daytime activities

Etymology

From 'cocktail' (referring to a semi-formal social gathering) + 'dress'.
