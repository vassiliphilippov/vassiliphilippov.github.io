Economic Commission for Latin America

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈkɑnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fər ˌlæt̬ɪn əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / ɪˈkɒnəmɪk kəˈmɪʃən fə ˌlætɪn əˈmɛrɪkə / (BrE)
the commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council that focuses on economic development in Latin America
UN body for Latin American economic development

Examples

The Economic Commission for Latin America was established to promote regional cooperation and development.
Policymakers attended the annual meeting of the Economic Commission for Latin America in Santiago, Chile.
Statistics published by the Economic Commission for Latin America are influential in shaping economic policy throughout the continent.
