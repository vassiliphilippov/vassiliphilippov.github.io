shrouded in mist

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃraʊdəd ɪn mɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃraʊdɪd ɪn mɪst / (BrE)
covered or hidden by mist, making something difficult to see clearly
covered or hidden by mist

Examples

The old castle stood shrouded in mist, giving it an air of mystery.
Early in the morning, the lake is often shrouded in mist, hiding the far shore from view.
The valley remained shrouded in mist until the sun came up and burned it away.

Synset Examples

The mountains looked beautiful at a distance, shrouded in mist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enveloped in fog - completely surrounded by fog
