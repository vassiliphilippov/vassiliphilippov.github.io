to push through adversity

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ θruː ædˈvɜːrsəti / (AmE), / pʊʃ θruː ədˈvɜːsɪti / (BrE)
to continue making effort and succeed despite facing difficulties or challenges
overcome difficulties or challenges

Examples

Despite many setbacks, she kept pushing through adversity and finally achieved her dream.
The athlete’s ability to push through adversity inspired his teammates to do the same.
Many successful entrepreneurs stress the importance of pushing through adversity when faced with failures.

Synset Examples

She’s a fighter, always pushing through adversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to persevere - to persist in a course of action despite difficulty
  • Antonym: to give up - to stop making an effort; to quit
