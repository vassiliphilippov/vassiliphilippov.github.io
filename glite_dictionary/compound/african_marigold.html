African marigold

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈmær.əˌgoʊld / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈmær.ɪˌgəʊld / (BrE)
a tall, branching annual plant with large yellow or orange flower heads, native to Mexico and Central America, widely cultivated as an ornamental.
ornamental plant with large yellow/orange flowers

Examples

African marigold seeds should be sown in early spring for summer blooms.
The bright petals of the African marigold attract butterflies to the garden.
She planted an African marigold border along the walkway for a burst of color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marigold - a plant with bright yellow or orange flowers, widely cultivated for ornament
