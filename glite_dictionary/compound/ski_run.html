ski run

1. NOUN

/ ski rʌn / (AmE), / ski rʌn / (BrE)
a trail or slope that is specially prepared and maintained for skiing
prepared trail or slope for skiing

Examples

The mountain resort features a new ski run that offers breathtaking views.
After last night's snowfall, the ski run was freshly groomed and ready for skiers.
She completed the difficult ski run without falling once.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski slope - an area of a mountain prepared for skiing
