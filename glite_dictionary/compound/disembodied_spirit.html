disembodied spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsɛmˈbɑdiːd ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɛmˈbɒdɪd ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a supernatural being believed to exist without a physical body, sometimes appearing to humans as a ghost or presence
supernatural being lacking a physical body

Examples

Some believe that a disembodied spirit can linger in places where they once lived.
The old mansion was rumored to be haunted by a disembodied spirit.
During the séance, the medium claimed to communicate with a disembodied spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, often believed to appear to the living
  • Synonym: apparition - a supernatural appearance of a person or thing, especially a ghost
