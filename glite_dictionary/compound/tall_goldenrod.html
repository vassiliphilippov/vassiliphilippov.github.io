tall goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a North American plant (Solidago altissima) of the daisy family, noted for its tall stems and clusters of small yellow flowers
a tall North American goldenrod plant

Examples

The tall goldenrod blooms in late summer, adding bright yellow to the meadows.
Botanists studied the height of tall goldenrod found in different habitats across the state.
She planted tall goldenrod in her garden to attract butterflies and bees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago, with clusters of small yellow flowers
