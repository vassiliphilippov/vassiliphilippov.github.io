Lena River

1. PROPN

/ ˈleɪnə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈliːnə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in Siberia, Russia, flowing north into the Laptev Sea
large Siberian river in Russia

Examples

The Lena River is one of the longest rivers in the world, stretching over 4,000 kilometers.
During the spring thaw, the Lena River can cause extensive flooding in the surrounding Siberian plains.
Many explorers have studied the ecology along the Lena River and its vast delta region.
