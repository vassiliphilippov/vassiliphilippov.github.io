volcanic landscape

1. NOUN

/ vɑlˈkænɪk ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / vɒlˈkanɪk ˈlan(d)skɛɪp / (BrE)
Land that has been shaped or formed by volcanic activity, such as lava flows, craters, and ash deposits.
terrain shaped by volcanic activity

Examples

The hikers marveled at the volcanic landscape dotted with black lava rocks and deep craters.
Iceland is famous for its dramatic volcanic landscape, which attracts photographers from around the world.
The film was set in a rugged volcanic landscape that added to the story's sense of danger.

Synset Examples

They explored the volcanic landscape on their hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lava field - area covered by solidified lava from a volcanic eruption
  • Synonym: crater - bowl-shaped depression caused by volcanic activity
