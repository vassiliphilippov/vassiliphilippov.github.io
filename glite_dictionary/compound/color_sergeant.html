color sergeant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
a sergeant in a military color guard who carries one of the regiment's flags or colors during ceremonies
military sergeant carrying a flag in a color guard

Examples

The color sergeant marched at the front of the parade holding the regimental flag.
Each color sergeant is carefully selected for their exemplary service and discipline.
During the ceremony, the color sergeant stood proudly beside the other members of the color guard.
