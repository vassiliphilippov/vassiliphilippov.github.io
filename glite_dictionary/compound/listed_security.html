listed security

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪstɪd sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈlɪstɪd sɪˈkjʊərəti / (BrE)
A security, such as a stock or bond, that is officially admitted to trading on a recognized securities exchange.
security traded on an exchange

Examples

The company's shares became a listed security after approval from the stock exchange.
Investors often prefer a listed security for its liquidity and regulatory oversight.
Not every bond is a listed security; some are traded privately between institutions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlisted security - security not traded on an official exchange
