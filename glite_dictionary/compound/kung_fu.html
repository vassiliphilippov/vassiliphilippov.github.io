kung fu

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌŋ ˈfu / (AmE), / ˌkʌŋ ˈfuː / (BrE)
A Chinese martial art involving physical exercises, fighting techniques, and self-defense.
Chinese martial art

Examples

She has studied kung fu for over five years and now teaches children.
Many movies showcase dramatic kung fu fight scenes.
He demonstrated his kung fu moves at the talent show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martial art - any of several Asian systems of combat and self-defense

Etymology

From Chinese gōngfu (功夫), meaning 'skill' or 'achievement'.
