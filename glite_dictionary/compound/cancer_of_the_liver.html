cancer of the liver

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsɚ əv ðə ˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkænsə əv ðə ˈlɪvə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor affecting the liver, often occurring as a spread from cancer elsewhere in the body.
malignant tumor of the liver
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with cancer of the liver after experiencing severe abdominal pain and jaundice.
Treatment options for cancer of the liver may include surgery, chemotherapy, or targeted therapy.
Early detection of cancer of the liver can improve outcomes, but the disease is often found at an advanced stage.

  • Synonym: liver cancer - malignant tumor of the liver
