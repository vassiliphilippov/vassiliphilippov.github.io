heated argument

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtɪd ˈɑːrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪd ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
a very intense or passionate disagreement, often involving strong emotions or raised voices
intense or passionate disagreement

Examples

We had a heated argument about the company's new policies during the meeting.
A heated argument broke out between the neighbors over the property line.
Their heated argument ended with both of them storming out of the room.

Synset Examples

He called her a r0dent during the heated argument.
We had a heated argument about politics last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
  • Synonym: dispute - a disagreement, especially an official one
  • Antonym: conversation - an informal talk involving two people or a small group
