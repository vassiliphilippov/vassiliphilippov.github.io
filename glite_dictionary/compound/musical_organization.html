musical organization

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group or institution of musicians who perform together as an ensemble or collective
group or institution of performing musicians

Examples

The city allocated funds to support the local musical organization and its youth programs.
She joined a musical organization that specializes in Baroque music.
Many schools encourage students to participate in a musical organization to foster teamwork and creativity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians who play together, often popular or jazz music
  • Synonym: orchestra - a large instrumental ensemble typical of classical music
  • Synonym: choir - an organized group of singers
