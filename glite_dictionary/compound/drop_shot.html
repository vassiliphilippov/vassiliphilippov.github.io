drop shot

1. NOUN

/ drɑp ʃɑt / (AmE), / drɒp ʃɒt / (BrE)
a softly hit shot in tennis or similar sports that lands just over the net and is intended to fall quickly, making it difficult for the opponent to reach
soft shot landing just over the net (tennis)

Examples

She surprised her opponent with a perfectly executed drop shot that barely cleared the net.
His drop shot landed just inside the line, winning the point.
During the match, he used the drop shot to break his rival's rhythm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lob - a shot in tennis that is hit high and deep into the opponent's court
