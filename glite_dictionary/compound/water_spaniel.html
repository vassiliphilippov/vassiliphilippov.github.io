water spaniel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ spæniəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌspæniəl / (BrE)
a breed of large dog with a curly coat, developed for retrieving game from water, especially in hunting waterfowl
large, curly-coated hunting dog

Examples

The Irish water spaniel is known for its distinctive curly coat and intelligence.
She trained her water spaniel to retrieve ducks from the pond.
Many hunters value the loyalty and stamina of a water spaniel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaniel - a breed of dog with long ears, used in hunting
