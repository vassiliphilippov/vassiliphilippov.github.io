sharp-cornered

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑrp ˌkɔrnərd / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːp ˌkɔːnəd / (BrE)
having one or more distinctly pointed or acute angles, as opposed to being rounded or smooth
having pointed or acute angles

Examples

The new table had sharp-cornered edges that made it dangerous for young children.
They replaced the old sharp-cornered shelves with safer, round ones.
He drew a sharp-cornered shape on the board to illustrate his point.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rounded - having smooth, curved edges, not pointed
