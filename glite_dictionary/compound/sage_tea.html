sage tea

1. NOUN

/ seɪdʒ ti / (AmE), / seɪdʒ tiː / (BrE)
A beverage made by infusing sage leaves in hot water.
herbal infusion of sage leaves

Examples

Many people drink sage tea to soothe a sore throat.
I enjoy a cup of sage tea in the evening for its relaxing properties.
The herbal shop offers several blends, including chamomile and sage tea.

Synset Examples

Sage tea has a calming effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbal tea - drink made from infusion of herbs, not true tea leaves
