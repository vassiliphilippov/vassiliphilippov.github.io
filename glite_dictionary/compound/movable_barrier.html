movable barrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvəbəl bəˈrɪr / (AmE), / ˈmjuːvəbəl ˈbæɹɪə / (BrE)
a barrier that can be moved to allow or restrict passage
barrier that can be moved

Examples

The workers placed a movable barrier across the road to block traffic during repairs.
Festival organizers used a movable barrier to control the flow of the crowd.
Please remove the movable barrier so delivery trucks can enter the loading area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrier - a structure that blocks or restricts movement
