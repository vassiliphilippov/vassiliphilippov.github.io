mine run

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪn ˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪn ˌrʌn / (BrE)
Ordinary or average; not special or distinguished in any way.
ordinary; not special

Examples

The show was just a mine run production, nothing particularly memorable.
She received a mine run education at an average public school.
Most of the candidates were mine run and failed to stand out during the interview process.

Synset Examples

run-of-the-mill boxing
your run-of-the-mine college graduate
as unexceptional an incident as can be found in a lawyer's career

Related Terms

  • Synonym: run-of-the-mill - ordinary, not exceptional; lacking distinction
  • Antonym: exceptional - unusually good; outstanding

Etymology

From mining, where 'run-of-the-mine' refers to ore as it comes from the mine, unselected for quality.
