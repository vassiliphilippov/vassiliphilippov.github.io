post-office box number

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊst ˌɑːfɪs bɑːks ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst ˌɒfɪs bɒks ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
the number assigned to a post-office box at a post office, used for collecting mail
number for a post-office box

Examples

Please send your application to post-office box number 245 in London.
His business correspondence was always directed to his post-office box number rather than his home address.
To receive important documents securely, she provided her post-office box number on the form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: post-office box - a locked box at a post office where a person's mail is kept until collected
