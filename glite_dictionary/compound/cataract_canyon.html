Cataract Canyon

1. PROPN

/ ˈkætərækt ˈkænjən / (AmE), / ˈkætərækt ˈkænjən / (BrE)
a deep, scenic canyon carved by the Colorado River in southeastern Utah, known for its whitewater rapids and dramatic landscapes
canyon on the Colorado River in Utah

Examples

Many adventurers seek out Cataract Canyon for its challenging whitewater rafting experience.
Stunning rock formations rise above the Colorado River in Cataract Canyon.
The expedition included a week-long journey through Cataract Canyon and its surrounding wilderness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Grand Canyon - famous canyon on the Colorado River in Arizona, USA
