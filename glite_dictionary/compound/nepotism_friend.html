nepotism friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛp.əˌtɪz.əm frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈnɛp.əˌtɪz.əm frɛnd / (BrE)
a person who benefits from favoritism based on family ties or personal connections, especially in employment or other opportunities
person favored due to connections

He was accused of being a nepotism friend when he joined the company's board of directors.
The team resented having a nepotism friend among them because of his special treatment.
