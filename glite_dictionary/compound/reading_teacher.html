reading teacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdɪŋ ˌtiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ ˌtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a teacher who specializes in instructing students how to read
teacher of reading

Examples

The school hired a new reading teacher to support struggling learners.
As a reading teacher, she developed creative activities to engage her students.
My sister became a reading teacher after earning her degree in education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literacy coach - an educator who helps students improve reading and writing skills
  • Synonym: teacher - person whose job is to teach
