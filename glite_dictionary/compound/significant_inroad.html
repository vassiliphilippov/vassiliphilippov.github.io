significant inroad

1. NOUN

/ sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈɪnˌroʊd / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt ˈɪnˌrəʊd / (BrE)
a major advance or substantial progress in a particular area or activity
major progress or advance

Examples

The new technology has made significant inroads into the renewable energy sector.
She hopes her research will achieve significant inroads in the fight against cancer.
In recent years, online retailers have made significant inroads into traditional markets.

Synset Examples

The new policy made significant inroads in education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance - progress or improvement in something
  • Synonym: breakthrough - a significant or sudden advance, development, or achievement
