short-range

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
Operating or effective only over a small or limited distance.
effective over a short distance

Examples

The army deployed several short-range missiles near the border.
This short-range walkie-talkie is perfect for use on construction sites.
Their short-range goals include improving local infrastructure.

Synset Examples

short-range planes
a short-range shot

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-range - operating or effective over a long distance
