yeast dough

1. NOUN

/ jist doʊ / (AmE), / jiːst dəʊ / (BrE)
Dough that is made with yeast as a leavening agent, causing it to rise during baking.
dough leavened with yeast

Examples

She made a sweet yeast dough for the holiday bread.
Many traditional pastries begin with a basic yeast dough recipe.
Let the yeast dough rise in a warm place until it doubles in size.

Synset Examples

I know this recipe for yeast dough says I can use dried yeast, but I prefer using fresh yeast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bread dough - dough used to make bread, often yeast-leavened
