black flag

1. NOUN

/ blæk flæɡ / (AmE), / blæk flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag, typically featuring a white skull and crossbones on a black background, used to symbolize a pirate ship
pirate ship flag with skull and crossbones

Examples

The sight of the black flag on the horizon struck fear into the hearts of sailors.
Legend says pirates raised the black flag before attacking merchant vessels.
A tattered black flag flew atop the replica pirate ship in the harbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jolly Roger - the traditional flag of pirates, showing a skull and crossbones
