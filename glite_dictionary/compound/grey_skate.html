grey skate

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ skeɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ skeɪt / (BrE)
a common European skate, a type of fish often used as food
European skate fish used as food

Examples

The restaurant serves fresh grey skate caught from the North Sea.
Local fishermen reported a decline in grey skate populations.
She ordered grilled grey skate for her main course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skate - a type of ray fish with a flattened body
