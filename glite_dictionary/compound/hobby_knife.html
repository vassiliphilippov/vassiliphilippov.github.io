hobby knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːbi ˌnaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɒbi ˌnaɪf / (BrE)
A small, sharp knife with a replaceable blade, used in arts and crafts for precise cutting.
small craft knife for precise cutting

Examples

He carefully traced the outline of the model airplane with a hobby knife.
Always use a cutting mat when working with a hobby knife to protect your desk.
She used a hobby knife to create intricate details on her papercraft project.

Synset Examples

I can't finish this sculpture without my favourite hobby knife; I know it's just a scalpel, but it brings me luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scalpel - a small, sharp knife used in surgery or crafts
