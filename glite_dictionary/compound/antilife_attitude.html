antilife attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ti.laɪf ˈæt̬.ə.tud / (AmE), / ˈæn.ti.laɪf ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a belief or philosophy that opposes or rejects life-affirming values and principles
belief opposed to life-affirming values

Examples

Many critics accused the novel of promoting an antilife attitude through its destructive themes.
The philosopher cautioned against adopting any antilife attitude that devalues human existence.
Her speech challenged the corporate culture’s subtle antilife attitude towards creativity and growth.

Synset Examples

She rejected the antilife attitudes prevalent in society.
His actions seemed to promote an antilife philosophy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: life-affirming - supporting positive, life-enhancing values
