understanding of history

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndɚˈstændɪŋ əv ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ əv ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
knowledge or comprehension of historical events, developments, and the contexts in which they occurred
knowledge of historical events and contexts

Examples

Her understanding of history allows her to draw insightful parallels between past and present events.
A deep understanding of history is essential for interpreting the causes of global conflicts.
The professor praised his student's understanding of history during the class discussion.

Synset Examples

His understanding of history was broad and shallow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knowledge of history - awareness or familiarity with historical facts
