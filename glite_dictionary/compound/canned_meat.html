canned meat

1. NOUN

/ kænd miːt / (AmE), / kænd miːt / (BrE)
Meat that has been preserved and sealed in a can or tin to extend its shelf life.
meat preserved in a can or tin

Examples

After the hurricane, we relied on canned meat and vegetables for our meals.
She added some canned meat to the stew for extra protein.
Many campers bring canned meat because it does not require refrigeration.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: canned food - food preserved and sealed in a can or tin
  • Antonym: fresh meat - meat that is not preserved or processed
