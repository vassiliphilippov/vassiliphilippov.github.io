family Moraceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli mɔːˈreɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mɒˈreɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of trees or shrubs with milky sap, including mulberries and figs; sometimes includes the genus Cannabis in certain classifications.
a family of trees and shrubs with milky sap

Examples

The family Moraceae includes well-known plants such as the fig and the mulberry.
Botanists debate whether the genus Cannabis should be classified within the family Moraceae.
Latex from species in the family Moraceae can sometimes cause allergic reactions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Moraceae - the scientific name for the mulberry family of plants
  • Synonym: mulberry - a type of tree or its fruit, belonging to this family

Etymology

From modern Latin Moraceae, based on the genus Morus (mulberry).
