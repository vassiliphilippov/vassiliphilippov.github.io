to net fish

1. VERB

/ nɛt fɪʃ / (AmE), / nɛt fɪʃ / (BrE)
to catch fish using a net
catch fish with a net

Examples

Fishermen often net fish in the early morning when the waters are calm.
Researchers use special equipment to net fish for scientific studies.
Local regulations restrict the times when you can net fish in the river.
