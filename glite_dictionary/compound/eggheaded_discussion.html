eggheaded discussion

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌhɛdɪd dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌhɛdɪd dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
A conversation or debate that is extremely intellectual, academic, or overly focused on theoretical ideas, often to the point of being inaccessible to most people.
overly intellectual or academic debate
informal

Examples

The eggheaded discussion about quantum mechanics went over most people’s heads.
We tried to steer the meeting away from another eggheaded discussion and focus on practical solutions.
Her eyes glazed over during the eggheaded discussion of 19th-century philosophers.

Synset Examples

The eggheaded discussion left everyone confused.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intellectual debate - a debate focused on intellectual or academic subjects
  • Antonym: practical discussion - a discussion focused on real-world applications, opposite of theoretical

Etymology

From 'egghead', a slang term for an intellectual or academic, plus 'discussion'.
