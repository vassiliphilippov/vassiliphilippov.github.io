ragged orchis

1. NOUN

/ ˈræɡɪd ˈɔːrkɪs / (AmE), / ˈræɡɪd ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
A fringed orchid native to the eastern United States, characterized by greenish flowers with deeply cut lips.
North American orchid with fringed flowers

Examples

The ragged orchis blooms in late spring in damp meadows across the eastern United States.
Botanists admire the delicate, lacerated lip of the ragged orchis flower.
We spotted a rare ragged orchis during our hike through the wetlands.
