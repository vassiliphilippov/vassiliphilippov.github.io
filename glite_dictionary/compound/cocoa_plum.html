cocoa plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.koʊ plʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəʊ plʌm / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub (Chrysobalanus icaco) or its plum-shaped edible fruit, ranging from whitish to black, often used in preserves.
tropical American fruit used in preserves

Examples

The jam was made from cocoa plum picked fresh that morning.
In some Caribbean countries, cocoa plum is a popular ingredient in desserts.
We found wild cocoa plum while exploring the coastal forest.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ plʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ plʌm / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Chrysobalanus icaco) bearing edible, plumlike fruit, often found in coastal areas.
tropical tree with edible plumlike fruit

Examples

The cocoa plum is often used in making jams and jellies in the Caribbean.
We found several ripe cocoa plum fruits along the sandy beach.
The botanist studied the leaves and fruit of the cocoa plum for her research.
