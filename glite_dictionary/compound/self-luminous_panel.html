self-luminous panel

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈluːmɪnəs ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈluːmɪnəs ˈpanəl / (BrE)
a panel that emits its own light, often used for illumination or display purposes without requiring external lighting
panel that emits its own light

Examples

The laboratory installed a self-luminous panel to ensure constant illumination during power outages.
Emergency exit signs often feature a self-luminous panel for visibility in the dark.
The artist incorporated a self-luminous panel into the sculpture to create a glowing effect at night.

Synset Examples

They designed a self-luminous panel for better visibility.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: backlit panel - a panel illuminated from behind by an external light source
