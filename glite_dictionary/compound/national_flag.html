national flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃənəl flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈnaʃənəl flaɡ / (BrE)
an official flag that represents and symbolizes a particular nation or country
official flag representing a nation

Examples

Every country has its own national flag proudly displayed on public buildings.
During the Olympics, athletes march under their national flag at the opening ceremony.
The students learned how to fold the national flag properly during the assembly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flag - a piece of cloth with a special design used as a symbol, especially of a country
