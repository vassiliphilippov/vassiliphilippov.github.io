home renovation

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / həʊm ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making improvements or repairs to a home to update its appearance, functionality, or value
updating or improving a home

Examples

After their home renovation, the kitchen felt much brighter and more modern.
They saved for years to afford a major home renovation project last summer.
A well-planned home renovation can increase the value of your property.

Synset Examples

The home renovation was completed by Adam Tiler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renovation - the act of improving by renewing and restoring
