vertically challenged

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝːtɪkli tʃəˈlændʒd / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkli tʃəˈlændʒd / (BrE)
used humorously or euphemistically to describe someone who is short in height
humorous term for short stature
informal

Examples

Many vertically challenged people find clever ways to reach high shelves.
He often makes light of being vertically challenged when meeting new people.
Although she is vertically challenged, she makes up for it with her big personality.

Synset Examples

She joked about being vertically challenged at the concert.
The vertically challenged man climbed the ladder with ease.
He embraced being vertically challenged with confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: short - having little height; not tall
