malignant nature

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɪɡnənt ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / məˈlɪɡnənt ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a deeply harmful, malevolent, or evil disposition or character trait
malevolent or harmful character

Examples

Many feared the CEO's malignant nature after witnessing how he treated his employees.
Her malignant nature slowly drove away all her friends over the years.
The author described the villain's malignant nature through chilling dialogue and sinister actions.

Synset Examples

His malignant nature made him difficult to work with.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malevolent - having or showing a wish to do evil to others
  • Antonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
