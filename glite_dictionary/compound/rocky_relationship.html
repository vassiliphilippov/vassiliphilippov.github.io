rocky relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑki rɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈrɒki rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship marked by frequent difficulties, disagreements, or instability
relationship with frequent difficulties

Examples

They have had a rocky relationship ever since they moved in together.
Despite their rocky relationship, they managed to stay friends after the breakup.
Years of misunderstandings led to a rocky relationship between the two business partners.

Synset Examples

Their relationship has been rocky since the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strained relationship - relationship characterized by tension or unease
