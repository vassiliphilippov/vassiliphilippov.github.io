bozoid moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌzɔɪd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌzɔɪd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
an instance of silly or foolish behavior
instance of silly or foolish behavior
informal slang

Examples

He realized he'd just had a bozoid moment when he tried to start his car without the keys.
Her presentation was nearly perfect except for one bozoid moment when she called the CEO by the wrong name.
We laughed at Tom's bozoid moment when he wore mismatched shoes to the meeting.

Synset Examples

Despite his bozoid moments, he remained endearing to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake
  • Synonym: gaffe - an unintentional act causing embarrassment

Etymology

From 'bozo' (slang for a foolish person) + '-oid' (resembling), combined with 'moment' (brief period).
