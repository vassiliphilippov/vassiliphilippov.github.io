military plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft that is specially designed and used by the armed forces, especially for combat or defense purposes
aircraft used by the military

Examples

The military plane flew over the city as part of the airshow.
A new military plane was developed to enhance national security.
Several nations participated in joint exercises with their latest military planes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter plane - a military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat
  • Antonym: civilian plane - an aircraft used for non-military purposes
