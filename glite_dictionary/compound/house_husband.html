house husband

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈhʌzbənd / (AmE), / haʊs ˈhʌzbənd / (BrE)
A man who manages household duties and cares for the home while his wife earns the family income.
man who manages home while wife works

Examples

After losing his job, Tom became a house husband while his wife started her new career.
Being a house husband allowed Mark to spend more time with his children.
The couple decided that he would become a house husband so she could pursue her dream job.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: housewife - a married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home
