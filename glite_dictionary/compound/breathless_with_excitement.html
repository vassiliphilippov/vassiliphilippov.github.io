breathless with excitement

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɛθləs wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθləs wɪð ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
so excited or thrilled that one feels unable to breathe normally or is overwhelmed by anticipation
overwhelmed with thrill or anticipation

Examples

The announcement of the winner left the audience breathless with excitement.
She opened the envelope, breathless with excitement as she revealed the results.
The children waited breathless with excitement for the magician to perform his next trick.

Synset Examples

The knife thrower's act left everyone breathless with excitement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathless - unable to breathe easily; showing intense emotion or excitement
