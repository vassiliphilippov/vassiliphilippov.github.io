injured party

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.dʒɚd ˈpɑːr.ti / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dʒəd ˈpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a person who has been physically harmed or killed, especially as the result of an accident or wrongdoing
person physically harmed or killed

Examples

The injured party was taken to the hospital immediately after the incident.
The insurance company will compensate the injured party for medical expenses.
Paramedics arrived quickly to assist the injured party at the scene of the crash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victim - a person harmed, injured, or killed as a result of a crime, accident, or other event
