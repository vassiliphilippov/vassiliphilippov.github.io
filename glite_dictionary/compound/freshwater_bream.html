freshwater bream

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃˌwɔtər ˈbriːm / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃˌwɔːtə ˈbriːm / (BrE)
any of various usually edible freshwater fishes with compressed bodies and shiny scales, especially those in the genus Lepomis.
edible freshwater fish with compressed body

Examples

We caught several freshwater bream near the lake shore this morning.
Many anglers prefer to grill freshwater bream for its mild flavor.
The region is known for its abundance of freshwater bream species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bream - edible freshwater or marine fish with a deep, laterally compressed body
