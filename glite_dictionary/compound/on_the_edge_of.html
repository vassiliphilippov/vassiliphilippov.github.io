on the edge of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ði ɛdʒ ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn ði ɛdʒ əv / (BrE)
very close to achieving or experiencing something significant or important
close to achieving or experiencing something

Examples

After years of research, the scientists were on the edge of a major discovery.
The company is on the edge of launching a revolutionary product this year.
She knew she was on the edge of achieving her lifelong dream.

Synset Examples

He felt he was on the edge of a breakthrough.
