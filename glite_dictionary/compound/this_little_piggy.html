This Little Piggy

1. NOUN

/ ðɪs ˈlɪt̬l ˈpɪɡi / (AmE), / ðɪs ˈlɪtl ˈpɪɡi / (BrE)
a traditional English nursery rhyme that counts toes, beginning with the words 'This little piggy went to market'
traditional children's nursery rhyme

Examples

Every night, Grandma would recite This Little Piggy to the children before bedtime.
The toddler giggled as his father played This Little Piggy with his toes.
She memorized This Little Piggy and proudly performed it at school.

Synset Examples

The children sang 'This Little Piggy' with enthusiasm, ending with a cheerful 'chinny chin chin!'
