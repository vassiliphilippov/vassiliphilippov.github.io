skincare treatment

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪnˌkɛr ˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / ˈskɪnkeə ˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
a procedure or regimen applied to improve the appearance and health of the skin
procedure to improve skin appearance or health

Examples

She booked a skincare treatment to refresh her skin before the wedding.
Many spas offer a variety of skincare treatments tailored to different skin types.
After the skincare treatment, his face felt smoother and looked healthier.

Synset Examples

Her complexion glowed after the skincare treatment.
