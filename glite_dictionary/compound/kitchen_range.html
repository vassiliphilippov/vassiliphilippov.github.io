kitchen range

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn reɪndʒ / (BrE)
A large kitchen appliance, typically combining an oven and a cooktop, used for cooking food.
appliance for cooking food

Examples

She bought a new kitchen range for her renovated home.
The kitchen range has both gas burners and an electric oven.
Cleaning the kitchen range is part of my weekly chores.

Synset Examples

dinner was already on the stove

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - a kitchen appliance for cooking or heating
  • Degree Lower: oven - enclosed compartment for cooking food
