eccentric person

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsɛntrɪk ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsentrɪk ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who has unconventional or odd habits, opinions, or behavior
person with unconventional habits or behavior

Examples

The town's most eccentric person painted his house in bright polka dots.
Known as the eccentric person in her neighborhood, she kept a pet chicken on a leash.
Despite being an eccentric person, he was well-liked for his unique perspective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odd person - a person whose behavior or personality seems strange or unusual
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who behaves in a conventional way

Etymology

From Latin eccentricus (unconventional, odd), literally 'out of the center'.
