Isthmus of Suez

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪsθməs əv suˈɛz, ˈsuˌzɛz / (AmE), / ˈɪsθməs əv ˈsjuːɛz, ˈsuːɛz / (BrE)
the narrow land bridge in northeastern Egypt that connects the continents of Africa and Asia
land bridge connecting Africa and Asia

Examples

The Isthmus of Suez plays a crucial role in international trade due to its strategic location.
Many ancient trade routes crossed the Isthmus of Suez connecting Africa with Asia.
The construction of the Suez Canal transformed the Isthmus of Suez into a major global shipping route.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Suez Canal - a man-made waterway crossing the Isthmus of Suez

Etymology

Isthmus from Greek *isthmos* (neck, narrow passage); Suez from the port city in Egypt.
