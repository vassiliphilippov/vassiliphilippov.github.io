hot pants

1. NOUN

/ hɑt pænts / (AmE), / hɒt pants / (BrE)
Very short, tight-fitting shorts worn especially by women as an outer garment.
very short, tight-fitting women's shorts

Examples

She wore her favorite hot pants to the summer festival.
In the 1970s, hot pants became a popular fashion trend among young women.
The dancers performed in sequined hot pants and colorful tops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shorts - short trousers that reach only to the knees or thighs
