banyan day

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæn.jən deɪ / (AmE), / ˈbæn.jən deɪ / (BrE)
A pleasant day suitable for outdoor activities, especially when celebrated to appreciate banyan trees.
pleasant outdoor day, often for banyan tree appreciation

Examples

The community organized a banyan day to promote environmental awareness and spend time outdoors.
Many families enjoy having a picnic on a banyan day beneath the sprawling trees.
Last year’s banyan day included nature walks and storytelling sessions for children.

Synset Examples

It was a lovely banyan day, ideal for family gatherings.
They celebrated a banyan day with outdoor yoga and meditation.
Today is a banyan day, perfect for a picnic under the trees.
