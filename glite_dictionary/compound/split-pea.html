split-pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplɪt pi / (AmE), / ˈsplɪt piː / (BrE)
a dried, hulled pea that has been split; commonly used in soups and stews
dried, split pea used in cooking

Examples

I made a hearty split-pea soup for dinner last night.
The recipe calls for one cup of dried split-peas and some vegetables.
Split-pea dishes are popular in many cultures around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - a small, round, green seed eaten as a vegetable
