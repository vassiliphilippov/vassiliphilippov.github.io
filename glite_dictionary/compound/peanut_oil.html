peanut oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːnʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
A pale yellow oil extracted from peanuts, used chiefly in cooking and sometimes in making soap.
edible oil made from peanuts

Examples

Heat the peanut oil in a large wok before adding the vegetables.
Some people avoid peanut oil due to allergies.
Traditional soap makers sometimes use peanut oil in their recipes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable oil - any oil obtained from plants, used in cooking
  • Degree Higher: olive oil - oil pressed from olives, used in cooking
