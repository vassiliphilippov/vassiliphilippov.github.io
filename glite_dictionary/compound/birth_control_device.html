birth control device

1. NOUN

/ bɝθ kənˈtroʊl dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / bɜːθ kənˈtrəʊl dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A device used to prevent conception and pregnancy.
device for preventing conception
medical

Examples

She discussed different birth control devices with her doctor before making a decision.
The use of birth control devices has become more common over the past few decades.
Many schools include information about birth control devices in their health education programs.

Related Terms
