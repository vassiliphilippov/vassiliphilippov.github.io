solace in solitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑlɪs ɪn səˈluːt̬ud / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪs ɪn səˈluːtjuːd / (BrE)
A state of comfort or peace experienced when one is alone.
comfort found in being alone

Examples

After a stressful week, she sought solace in solitude by spending time in the mountains.
Many writers claim they find their greatest inspiration and solace in solitude.
He prefers to find solace in solitude rather than in the company of others.

Synset Examples

Being schizoid, he often found solace in solitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
  • Antonym: loneliness - a feeling of sadness from being alone
