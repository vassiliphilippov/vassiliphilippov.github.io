punch-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌntʃ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃ ˌʌp / (BrE)
a physical fight between people that involves punching, especially with fists
a physical fistfight
british informal

Examples

The football match was interrupted by a punch-up between rival fans.
There was a minor punch-up outside the pub last night.
Tempers flared and they ended up having a punch-up in the street.

Synset Examples

the quarrel ended in a punch-up

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fistfight - a fight with fists between two people
  • Degree Higher: brawl - a noisy and rough fight involving several people
