medical evacuation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌɛvəˈkjuːˌeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˌiːvækjʊˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the transportation of people who are injured or ill, usually by aircraft, to a place where they can receive medical treatment
transport of patients to medical care
medical

Examples

After the accident, a medical evacuation was arranged to fly the injured workers to the nearest hospital.
The military organized a rapid medical evacuation for soldiers wounded in battle.
Helicopters are often used in medical evacuation operations in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medevac - abbreviation for medical evacuation
