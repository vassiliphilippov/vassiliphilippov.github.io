Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia

1. PROPN

/ ˌrɛvəˌluʃəˈnɛri ɑːrmd ˈfɔrˌsiz əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / ˌrɛvəˌluːʃənˈɛri ɑːmd ˈfɔːsɪz əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
a Marxist-Leninist guerrilla organization in Colombia, founded in 1964, known for its involvement in armed conflict, kidnapping, and drug trafficking
Colombian guerrilla organization

Examples

The Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia have been engaged in armed conflict with the government for decades.
Many kidnapping incidents in rural Colombia were attributed to the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia.
The peace agreement with the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia marked a turning point in the nation's history.
