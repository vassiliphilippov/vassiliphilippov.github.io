oak chestnut

1. NOUN

/ oʊk ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / əʊk ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Castanopsis, native to parts of Asia, valued for its wood and nuts
tree of the genus Castanopsis

Examples

The oak chestnut is prized in Southeast Asia for its strong, durable timber.
Many oak chestnuts produce edible nuts enjoyed by wildlife and people alike.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the oak chestnut in different climates.
