pure bliss

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr blɪs / (AmE), / pjʊə blɪs / (BrE)
a state of complete happiness or joy; utter contentment
complete happiness; utter joy

Examples

Spending a quiet afternoon reading by the fireplace was pure bliss for her.
The first bite of chocolate cake after a long day felt like pure bliss.
Lying on the beach with the sun warming my face is pure bliss.

Synset Examples

An angel date with him was pure bliss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great unhappiness or suffering
