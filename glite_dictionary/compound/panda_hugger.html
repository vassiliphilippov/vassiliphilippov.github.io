panda hugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.də ˌhʌɡ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpæn.də ˌhʌɡ.ə / (BrE)
a person who advocates for and cares about pandas, especially their protection and conservation
advocate for panda conservation

Examples

At the conference, several panda huggers spoke about preserving wildlife habitats.
The children dressed up as panda huggers for the school’s conservation day.
She became known as a dedicated panda hugger after volunteering at the sanctuary.
