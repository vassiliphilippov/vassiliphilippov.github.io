winged pigweed

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋd ˈpɪɡˌwid / (AmE), / wɪŋd ˈpɪɡˌwiːd / (BrE)
a bushy annual weed native to central North America, with greenish flowers and seeds that have wing-like structures
a bushy North American weed with winged seeds

Examples

The field was overgrown with winged pigweed by late summer.
Farmers often struggle to control winged pigweed in their crops due to its rapid spread.
The seeds of winged pigweed are easily dispersed by wind, thanks to their wing-like structures.
