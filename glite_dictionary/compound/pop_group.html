pop group

1. NOUN

/ pɑp ɡrup / (AmE), / pɒp ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of musicians who perform popular (pop) music, typically as a band or vocal group
a band performing pop music

Examples

The new pop group released their first album to critical acclaim.
She joined a pop group when she was just sixteen years old.
Many pop groups from the 1980s are making a comeback tour this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians, especially playing rock or pop music
  • Degree Lower: rock band - a group that plays rock music
  • Degree Lower: boy band - a pop group consisting of young male singers

Etymology

From 'pop', short for 'popular (music)' + 'group'.
