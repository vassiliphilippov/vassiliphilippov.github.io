detached house

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtætʃt ˈhaʊs / (AmE), / dɪˈtʃætʃt ˈhaʊs / (BrE)
a house that stands alone and is not physically connected to any other dwelling
a free-standing house

Examples

They recently bought a detached house in the suburbs to have more space and privacy.
A typical detached house has its own garden and driveway.
Many families prefer a detached house to avoid noise from neighbors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: semi-detached house - a house joined to another house on one side only
