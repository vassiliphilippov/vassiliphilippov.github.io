2. NOUN

/ steɪt dɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / steɪt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)

US state government department
american

Examples

Each State Department handles its own regulations regarding vehicle registration.
The budget was approved by the State Department of Education last week.
Officials from various State Departments attended the annual conference.
