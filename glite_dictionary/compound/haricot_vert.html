haricot vert

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɑːriːkoʊ ˈvɛr / (AmE), / ˌæ.rɪ.kəʊ ˈvɛə / (BrE)
a very small and slender green bean, especially one used in French cuisine
small, slender green bean

Examples

The chef garnished the dish with fresh haricot verts and almonds.
We served steamed haricot verts alongside the roast chicken.
She prefers haricot vert because of their delicate texture and flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - an unripe, young pod of a bean plant, eaten as a vegetable

Etymology

From French 'haricot vert' meaning 'green bean'.
