power module

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊɚ ˌmɑːdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə ˌmɒdjuːl / (BrE)
an electronic component or unit that supplies power to operate other devices or modules within a system
electronic unit supplying power to other modules

Examples

The power module failed, causing the entire system to shut down unexpectedly.
You must ensure the power module is compatible with all the components in your circuit.
Technicians replaced the faulty power module during routine maintenance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power supply - a device that provides electrical power to a system or device
