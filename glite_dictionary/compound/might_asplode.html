to might asplode

1. VERB

/ maɪt æsˈploʊd / (AmE), / maɪt æsˈpləʊd / (BrE)
(humorous, informal) Used to suggest that someone might suddenly burst with strong emotions.
joking way to say 'might explode' emotionally
informal slang

Examples

If you keep teasing her, she might asplode from embarrassment.
Sometimes I get so stressed, I feel like I might asplode.
He was so excited about the news, I thought he might asplode.

Synset Examples

Her emotions might asplode if she doesn’t calm down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to explode - to burst or cause to burst violently and noisily

Etymology

Blend of 'explode' and comic misspelling 'asplode,' popularized in internet culture.
