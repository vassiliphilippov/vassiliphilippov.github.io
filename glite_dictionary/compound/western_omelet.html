western omelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈɑːmlət / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈɒmlət / (BrE)
an omelet made with diced ham, peppers, and onions, typically served as a firm, folded dish
omelet with ham, peppers, and onions

Examples

She ordered a western omelet with extra cheese for breakfast.
The western omelet at that diner always comes with a side of hash browns.
Have you ever tried making a western omelet at home?

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: omelet - a dish of beaten eggs cooked quickly with butter or oil, sometimes folded around fillings

Etymology

Named after the western United States, where the dish gained popularity.
