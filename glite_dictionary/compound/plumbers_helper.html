plumber's helper

1. NOUN

/ ˈplʌmɚz ˈhɛlpɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌməz ˈhɛlpə / (BrE)
a tool with a rubber suction cup attached to a stick, used to clear blockages from drains and pipes
tool for clearing clogged drains (plunger)

Examples

The plumber's helper did the trick and the water started draining again.
Every household should have a plumber's helper for emergency clogs.
He reached for the plumber's helper when the sink backed up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plunger - tool with suction cup for clearing drains

2. NOUN

/ ˈplʌmɚz ˈhɛlpɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌməz ˈhɛlpə / (BrE)
a person who assists a plumber by performing basic tasks and manual work
assistant to a plumber

Examples

He worked as a plumber's helper before apprenticing to get his license.
The plumber's helper carried the tools and handed them over when needed.
Many high school students have taken summer jobs as a plumber's helper to gain experience.
