storage device

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔɹɪd͡ʒ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːɹɪdʒ dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A piece of equipment that is used to store digital information, such as a hard drive, USB stick, or memory card.
device for storing digital information

Examples

Before buying a computer, consider how much data the storage device can hold.
She transferred her photos to an external storage device for safekeeping.
Many businesses use cloud-based storage devices to back up important files.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data storage - the process or capacity of saving information digitally
  • Synonym: memory - the part of a computer where data is stored
