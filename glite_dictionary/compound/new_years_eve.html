New Year's Eve

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈjɪrz ˈiv / (AmE), / nju ˈjɪəz ˈiːv / (BrE)
The last day of the year, especially the evening preceding New Year's Day, often marked with celebrations and festivities.
the last day of the year

Examples

We always host a big party on New Year's Eve to ring in the new year.
Fireworks lit up the sky at midnight on New Year's Eve.
Many people make resolutions just before midnight on New Year's Eve.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: New Year's Day - the first day of the year
