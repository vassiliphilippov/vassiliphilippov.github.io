chocolate-covered doughnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɔklət ˌkʌvɚd ˈdoʊnʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˌkʌvəd ˈdəʊnʌt / (BrE)
A doughnut that is coated with a layer of chocolate, usually on the top.
doughnut coated with chocolate

Examples

She picked out a chocolate-covered doughnut from the pastry display at the café.
His favorite treat after school is always a chocolate-covered doughnut with sprinkles.
The bakery sells both plain and chocolate-covered doughnuts every morning.

Synset Examples

I treated myself to a chocolate-covered doughnut this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - a small, usually ring-shaped fried cake
  • Degree Higher: chocolate éclair - a pastry filled with cream and topped with chocolate
