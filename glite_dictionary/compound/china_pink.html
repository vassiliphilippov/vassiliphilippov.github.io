china pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.nə pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.nə pɪŋk / (BrE)
an ornamental plant (Dianthus chinensis) with deeply toothed rose-lilac flowers, often grown as an annual
ornamental plant with rose-lilac flowers

Examples

Every summer, the border was filled with blooming china pink alongside marigolds and zinnias.
She planted china pink in her window boxes for a splash of color.
The delicate, toothed petals of the china pink were admired by garden visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dianthus - the genus of flowering plants including the pinks and carnations
