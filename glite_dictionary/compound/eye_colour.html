eye colour

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / aɪ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
The colour of the iris in a person's or animal's eye, determined by pigmentation.
colour of the eye's iris

Examples

Her eye colour changed subtly in the sunlight, appearing almost green.
Genetics play a major role in determining a child's eye colour.
He admired the striking eye colour that set her apart from her siblings.

Synset Examples

The genetics of eye color, like hair color, are still being worked out, and it's clear that several genes can modify the eventual outcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye color - color of the iris (American spelling)
