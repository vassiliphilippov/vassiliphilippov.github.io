diplomatic negotiations

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk nəˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk nəˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃənz / (BrE)
talks or discussions between representatives of different countries aimed at resolving issues or reaching agreements
negotiations between countries

Examples

After months of intense diplomatic negotiations, the two countries finally signed a trade agreement.
The president praised the success of diplomatic negotiations in easing regional tensions.
Ongoing diplomatic negotiations have prevented the dispute from escalating into open conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomacy - the activity of managing international relations
  • Synonym: peace talks - formal discussions to end conflict or war

Etymology

From 'diplomatic', relating to diplomacy (Greek diplōma, meaning 'folded paper'), and 'negotiations' (Latin negotiatio, meaning 'business' or 'bargaining').
