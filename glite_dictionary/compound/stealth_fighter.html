stealth fighter

1. NOUN

/ stɛlθ ˈfaɪtər / (AmE), / stɛlθ ˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
a fighter aircraft designed to be difficult to detect by radar, often used for precise targeting and equipped with advanced weapon systems
radar-evading fighter aircraft

Examples

The military introduced a new stealth fighter capable of evading the most advanced radar systems.
Pilots trained extensively to operate the stealth fighter in complex combat scenarios.
A stealth fighter was seen leading the airstrike during the operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stealth bomber - a bomber aircraft designed to avoid detection by radar
  • Synonym: fighter jet - a military jet aircraft designed for air-to-air combat
