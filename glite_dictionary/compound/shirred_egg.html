shirred egg

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪrd ɛɡ / (AmE), / ʃɪəd ɛɡ / (BrE)
An egg baked in a shallow dish, usually with cream or butter, until the white is set and the yolk is still soft.
egg baked in a dish with cream or butter

Examples

He ordered a shirred egg with cream and herbs for breakfast.
The chef demonstrated how to make a perfect shirred egg in a ramekin.
A shirred egg pairs well with toast and a side of fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baked egg - an egg cooked in its shell or a dish by baking

Etymology

From 'shir' (to bake in a shallow dish), of uncertain origin, plus 'egg'.
