flow diagram

1. NOUN

/ floʊ ˈdaɪ.əˌɡræm / (AmE), / fləʊ ˈdaɪəˌɡræm / (BrE)
a diagram representing the sequence of operations in a computer program, process, or system
diagram showing process steps

Examples

The engineer used a flow diagram to illustrate how the software processes data.
We reviewed the flow diagram to better understand the steps involved in payroll processing.
A clear flow diagram can help identify potential bottlenecks in a system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowchart - diagram showing steps in a process using symbols and arrows
