sun marigold

1. NOUN

/ sʌn ˈmærɪˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / sʌn ˈmærɪˌɡəʊld / (BrE)
any of several South African plants grown for their abundant yellow daisylike flowers and aromatic foliage, often cultivated as ornamentals
South African plant with yellow daisylike flowers

Examples

The sun marigold brightened the garden with its vibrant yellow blossoms.
Many gardeners choose sun marigolds for their ability to thrive in hot, sunny spots.
She picked a bouquet of sun marigolds to add color to her kitchen table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marigold - a plant with bright yellow or orange flowers, often grown as an ornamental
