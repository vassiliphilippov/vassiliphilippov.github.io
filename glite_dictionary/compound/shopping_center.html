shopping center

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːpɪŋ ˌsɛntɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
A group of shops and sometimes restaurants, typically with parking facilities, built together as a single development.
group of shops built together

Examples

The new shopping center has over fifty stores and several restaurants.
She parked her car and walked into the shopping center to buy some clothes.
They decided to meet at the shopping center for lunch and a bit of shopping.

Synset Examples

a good plaza should have a movie house
they spent their weekends at the local malls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mall - a large building or group of connected buildings containing shops, restaurants, etc.
