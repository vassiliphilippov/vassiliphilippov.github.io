rock brake

1. NOUN

/ rɑk breɪk / (AmE), / rɒk breɪk / (BrE)
any of several small ferns that grow on rocks, primarily in tropical and warm temperate regions
a type of fern growing on rocks

Examples

We found several species of rock brake clinging to the cliff’s surface.
