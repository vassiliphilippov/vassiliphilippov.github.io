symbolic representation

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈbɑlɪk ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlɪk ˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
something visible or tangible that stands for or represents something else which is not directly perceivable, often by association or convention.
visible sign standing for something else

Examples

The national flag serves as a symbolic representation of the country's values and history.
In literature, the raven is often used as a symbolic representation of death or mystery.
Mathematical equations can be seen as a symbolic representation of real-world relationships.

Synset Examples

the eagle is a symbol of the United States

Related Terms

  • Synonym: symbol - a mark, sign, or object that is regarded as representing something else
