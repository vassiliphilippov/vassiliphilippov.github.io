milkwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌwɔrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkwət ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Polygalaceae), including trees, shrubs, and herbs widely distributed in both hemispheres
family of flowering plants (Polygalaceae)

Examples

The milkwort family contains over a thousand species of plants worldwide.
Many members of the milkwort family have colorful, unique flowers.
Botanists carefully catalogued the species belonging to the milkwort family.
