Drug Enforcement Agency

1. PROPN

/ drʌɡ ɪnˈfɔrsmənt ˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / drʌɡ ɪnˈfɔːsmənt ˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
The United States federal agency responsible for enforcing laws and regulations concerning narcotics and controlled substances.
US federal agency enforcing drug laws

Examples

The Drug Enforcement Agency conducted a major operation to dismantle the cartel.
Several arrests were made after the Drug Enforcement Agency raided the warehouse last night.
She testified as an expert witness on behalf of the Drug Enforcement Agency in court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DEA - Abbreviation of Drug Enforcement Agency
