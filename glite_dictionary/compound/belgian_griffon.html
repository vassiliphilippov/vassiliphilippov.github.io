Belgian griffon

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈɡrɪf.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈɡrɪf.ɒn / (BrE)
a breed of very small, compact dogs from Belgium with a wiry coat and short, bearded muzzle
small Belgian dog breed with wiry coat

Examples

The Belgian griffon is known for its lively personality and curious nature.
She entered her Belgian griffon in the annual dog show and won first prize.
Many people are drawn to the Belgian griffon because of its expressive face and affectionate temperament.

Etymology

French griffon (wire-haired dog), referencing the breed's Belgian origin.
