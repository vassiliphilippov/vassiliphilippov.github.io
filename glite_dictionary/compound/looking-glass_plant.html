looking-glass plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkɪŋ ˌɡlæs plænt / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪŋ ˌɡlɑːs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a small tree native to coastal regions of the Old World tropics, noted for leaves with silvery undersides
tree with silvery-underleaf foliage

Examples

The looking-glass plant thrives in the salty air of tropical coastlines.
Gardeners value the looking-glass plant for its attractive, silver-backed leaves.
The leaves of the looking-glass plant shimmer in the sunlight, giving the tree its name.
