drop scone

1. NOUN

/ drɑp skoʊn / (AmE), / drɒp skɒn / (BrE)
A small, thick pancake made by dropping a spoonful of batter onto a griddle or frying pan.
small pancake cooked on a griddle

Examples

She served freshly made drop scones with butter and jam for breakfast.
To make drop scones, simply spoon the batter onto a hot griddle and cook until golden brown.
The children enjoyed helping their grandmother prepare drop scones on Sunday mornings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on a hot surface
