unwed couple

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈwɛd ˈkʌpəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛd ˈkʌpəl / (BrE)
a man and woman, or two people, in a romantic relationship who are not legally married to each other
a couple not legally married

Examples

Many landlords would not rent apartments to an unwed couple in the past.
The unwed couple decided to celebrate their anniversary with a trip abroad.
Being an unwed couple can sometimes bring up unexpected legal and social questions.

Synset Examples

They faced challenges as an unwed couple.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married couple - a pair of people who are legally married to each other
