wash-hand basin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃ hænd ˌbeɪ.sən / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃ hænd ˌbeɪ.sən / (BrE)
a bowl fixed to a wall or supported on a pedestal, used for washing one's hands and face, especially in a bathroom
bathroom basin for washing hands
british

Examples

There was a wash-hand basin installed in every room for guests' convenience.
He rinsed his hands in the wash-hand basin before dinner.
The plumber came to fix a leak under the wash-hand basin this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sink - a large bowl in a kitchen or bathroom used for washing, especially in American English
  • Synonym: washbasin - a basin for washing, especially the hands and face
  • Synonym: washbowl - a bowl for washing, especially used in the bedroom or bathroom
