physical phenomenon

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl fəˈnɑmɪnɑn / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
A natural event or occurrence that involves the physical properties of matter and energy.
natural event involving matter and energy

Examples

A rainbow is a physical phenomenon caused by the refraction and reflection of sunlight in water droplets.
Gravity is a fundamental physical phenomenon that affects all objects with mass.
Scientists study every physical phenomenon to better understand the laws of nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural phenomenon - an event occurring in nature, not human-made
