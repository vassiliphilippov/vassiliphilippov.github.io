operational cell

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənəl sɛl / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəl sɛl / (BrE)
A small group within a terrorist or clandestine organization that carries out secret operations or attacks.
terrorist group that carries out operations

Examples

Authorities discovered an operational cell planning an attack in the city center.
Intelligence agencies are constantly monitoring for signs of a new operational cell.
The dismantling of the operational cell prevented a major security incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeper cell - a terrorist group that remains dormant until activated
