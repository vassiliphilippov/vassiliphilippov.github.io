commercial enterprise

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɝːʃəl ˈɛn.tɚ.praɪz / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl ˈɛn.tə.praɪz / (BrE)
a business organization or activity involved in the buying and selling of goods or services for profit
a company or business involved in commerce

Examples

After years of planning, the family launched a commercial enterprise selling fresh produce.
Expanding internationally is a major milestone for any commercial enterprise.
His new commercial enterprise attracted investors from all over the country.

Synset Examples

computers are now widely used in business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business - the activity of making, buying, or selling goods or providing services for money
  • Antonym: nonprofit - an organization not conducted primarily to make a profit

Examples
