class feeling

1. NOUN

/ klæs ˈfilɪŋ / (AmE), / klɑːs ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
feelings of envy, resentment, or solidarity held by members of one social or economic class toward another
feelings toward another social class

Examples

There was a strong sense of class feeling among the factory workers during the strike.
Political leaders sometimes exploit class feeling to gain support from disadvantaged groups.
Literature often explores how class feeling shapes relationships between characters from different backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: class consciousness - awareness of one's own social or economic class and its interests
