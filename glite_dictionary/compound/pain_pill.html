pain pill

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪn pɪl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪn pɪl / (BrE)
a pill taken to relieve physical pain; an analgesic in tablet form
a pill for relieving pain

Examples

The doctor prescribed a pain pill to help with her back injury.
He took a pain pill after his dental surgery to ease the discomfort.
Please do not drive after taking a strong pain pill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analgesic - a drug used to relieve pain
  • Synonym: painkiller - a drug or medicine that relieves pain
