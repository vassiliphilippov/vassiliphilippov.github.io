best guy friend

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ɡaɪ frɛnd / (AmE), / bɛst ɡaɪ frɛnd / (BrE)
a male friend to whom someone feels closest, without any romantic attraction
closest male friend (non-romantic)

Examples

She calls James her best guy friend because they've known each other since childhood.
Even though they spend so much time together, he's just her best guy friend.
My best guy friend always gives the best advice when I need it.

Synset Examples

She considers him her best guy friend, no romantic interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best friend - the closest, most trusted friend
