golden trumpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a vigorous evergreen climbing plant native to South America, with glossy leaves and large golden-yellow flowers, grown as an ornamental
South American climbing plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The golden trumpet blooms throughout the summer, adding a bright splash of color to the garden.
Gardeners often train the golden trumpet to climb trellises and fences for decorative purposes.
Because the golden trumpet thrives in warm climates, it is popular in tropical landscaping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Allamanda - genus of tropical American flowering plants known as golden trumpet
