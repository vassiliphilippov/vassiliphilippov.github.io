apple sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl sɔs / (AmE), / ˈap.əl sɔːs / (BrE)
a sweetened and often spiced puree made from cooked apples
sweetened puree of cooked apples

Examples

She served roast pork with a side of apple sauce.
The children enjoyed their apple sauce as a healthy snack.
Homemade apple sauce can be stored in the refrigerator for several days.
