internet culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.t̬ɚ.nɛt ˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.nɛt ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the customs, practices, memes, and norms that develop from people interacting online, especially in social media and virtual communities
online customs and social norms

Examples

Much of modern internet culture is shaped by viral memes and inside jokes.
The rise of social media has accelerated the spread of internet culture across the globe.
Understanding internet culture is essential for brands hoping to connect with younger audiences.

Synset Examples

The term coomer reflects a specific internet culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - an idea, behavior, or style that spreads online, often humorous
