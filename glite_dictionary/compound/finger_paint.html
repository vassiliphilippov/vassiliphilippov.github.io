finger paint

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌpeɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌpeɪnt / (BrE)
A type of paint with a jelly-like consistency, designed for applying with the fingers rather than brushes, especially by children.
jelly-like paint for finger painting

Examples

The children created colorful pictures using finger paint in art class.
Finger paint is easy to wash off hands and clothing, making it ideal for young kids.
She bought several jars of finger paint for her daughter's birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finger painting - the activity of painting with the fingers using finger paint
