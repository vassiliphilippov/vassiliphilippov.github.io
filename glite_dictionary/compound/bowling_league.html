bowling league

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ liːɡ / (BrE)
an organized group of bowling teams that compete against each other over a season
an organized group of competing bowling teams

Examples

He joined a bowling league to meet new people and improve his game.
The bowling league holds matches every Wednesday night at the local alley.
Many companies sponsor teams in the city’s bowling league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: league - an association of sports teams that compete against each other
  • Synonym: bowling team - a team who compete together in the sport of bowling
