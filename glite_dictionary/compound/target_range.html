target range

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡət reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a place where people practice shooting at stationary targets, often for training or sport.
place for shooting practice

Examples

The army recruits were taken to the target range for their weekly training session.
She improved her accuracy considerably after spending several afternoons at the target range.
Due to safety regulations, all visitors must wear protective gear on the target range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting range - place for shooting practice
  • Synonym: firing range - facility for firearms training or practice
