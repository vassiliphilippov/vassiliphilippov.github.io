bar graph

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑr ˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈbɑː ˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A chart that uses rectangular bars with lengths proportional to the values they represent, used to compare quantities.
chart with bars representing data

Examples

The report includes a bar graph comparing monthly sales across different regions.
Our teacher explained how to create a bar graph in math class today.
The bar graph clearly shows the increase in website visitors over the last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar chart - A chart with bars whose lengths represent data values
