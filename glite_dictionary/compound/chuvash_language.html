Chuvash language

1. NOUN

/ tʃuːˈvɑːʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tʃuːˈvæʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Chuvash people, primarily in the Chuvash Republic of Russia
Turkic language of the Chuvash people

Examples

He is fluent in both Russian and Chuvash language.
The Chuvash language has unique features setting it apart from other Turkic languages.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Chuvash language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkic - relating to the family of languages that includes Turkish, Uzbek, and Chuvash

Etymology

From Russian 'чувашский язык' (Chuvashskiy yazyk).
