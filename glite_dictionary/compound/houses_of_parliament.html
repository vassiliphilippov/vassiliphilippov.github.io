Houses of Parliament

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪz əv ˈpɑːrləmənt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪz əv ˈpɑːləmənt / (BrE)
the building in London where the House of Commons and the House of Lords meet to conduct parliamentary business
UK parliament building in London

Examples

The tourists admired the illuminated Houses of Parliament from across the River Thames.
A major debate is scheduled to take place in the Houses of Parliament next week.
The Houses of Parliament are known for their iconic clock tower, often referred to as Big Ben.
