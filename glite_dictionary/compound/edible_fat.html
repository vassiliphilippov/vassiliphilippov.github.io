edible fat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbl̩ ˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl ˌfæt / (BrE)
fatty substances from animal or plant sources that are suitable and safe for human consumption, such as butter, lard, or vegetable oils.
fat suitable for eating

Examples

The chef only uses high-quality edible fat in his pastries to ensure a rich flavor.
Some plant-based edible fat, like olive oil, is considered healthier than animal fat.
Consumers are advised to limit their intake of saturated edible fat for better heart health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lard - rendered pork fat used in cooking
  • Synonym: butter - solid dairy product made from churned cream
