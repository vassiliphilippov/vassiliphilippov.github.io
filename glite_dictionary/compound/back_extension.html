back extension

1. NOUN

/ bæk ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / bæk ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
an exercise in which one bends backward at the waist, typically to strengthen the lower back muscles and improve spinal health
exercise to strengthen lower back

Examples

My trainer added back extension exercises to my weekly routine to build core strength.
After doing a back extension, she noticed her posture improved over time.
Many people at the gym use the Roman chair for back extension exercises.
