population scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃən ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies the growth, density, and vital statistics of populations.
scientist studying population dynamics

Examples

The population scientist was invited to discuss demographic changes in urban areas.
As a population scientist, Dr. Smith analyzes birth and mortality rates.
Many population scientists use complex models to project population growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demographer - an expert in the statistical study of populations
