rock bottom

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / rɒk ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
the lowest possible level or point; the absolute minimum
the lowest possible point

Examples

After losing his job and his home, John felt he had hit rock bottom.
The company's profits reached rock bottom last year before starting to recover.
The negotiations fell to rock bottom when neither side would compromise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nadir - the lowest point; the very bottom
  • Antonym: peak - the highest point; maximum
  • Synonym: bottom - the lowest part or point of something
