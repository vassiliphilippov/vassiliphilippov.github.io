North Channel

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˈtʃænəl / (BrE)
a strait between Northern Ireland and Scotland, connecting the Atlantic Ocean with the Irish Sea
strait between Northern Ireland and Scotland

Examples

Many ferries cross the North Channel between Scotland and Northern Ireland each day.
The strong currents in the North Channel can make navigation challenging for ships.
In history, the North Channel has been an important route for trade and migration.
