corporate welfare bum

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrpərət ˈwɛlfɛr bʌm / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərət ˈwɛlfə bʌm / (BrE)
a person, especially a business executive or corporation leader, who is regarded as undeserving of government financial aid or corporate subsidies
person seen as undeserving corporate aid

Examples

The bailout sparked outrage, with many calling the CEO a corporate welfare bum.
Some politicians campaign against so-called corporate welfare bums who profit from taxpayer-funded subsidies.
Critics argue that subsidies turn big companies into corporate welfare bums living off government support.

Synset Examples

Some see the subsidy recipient as a corporate welfare bum.
He referred to the CEO as a corporate welfare bum after the bailout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare bum - pejorative term for a person considered to be abusing social welfare

Etymology

Modeled after 'welfare bum'; 'welfare' + slang sense of 'bum' (undeserving recipient), combined with 'corporate' to reflect aid to businesses.
