basket star

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskət stɑr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal related to brittle stars, with long, slender, complexly branched arms radiating from a central disc
marine animal with branched arms

Examples

The basket star unfurled its delicate arms across the reef, searching for plankton in the current.
Marine biologists studied a basket star collected during the deep-sea expedition.
At the aquarium, visitors were fascinated by the intricate movements of the basket star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brittle star - a related marine animal with slender arms radiating from a central disc
