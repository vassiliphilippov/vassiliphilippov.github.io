Republic of Malta

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˈmɔltə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈmɔːltə / (BrE)
An island country in the Mediterranean Sea, officially known as the Republic of Malta, which gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1964.
island country in the Mediterranean

Examples

The Republic of Malta became a member of the European Union in 2004.
Tourists are drawn to the Republic of Malta for its beautiful coastlines and historical sites.
After gaining independence in 1964, the Republic of Malta established its own government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malta - island and country in the Mediterranean Sea
