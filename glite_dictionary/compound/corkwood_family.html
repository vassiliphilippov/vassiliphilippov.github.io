corkwood family

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrkˌwʊd ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːkwʊd ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of flowering plants consisting solely of the genus Leitneria, often classified in its own order.
family of plants (Leitneria genus)

Examples

The corkwood family is notable for containing only one extant species in North America.
Botanists often place the corkwood family in its own order due to its unique characteristics.
Members of the corkwood family are rarely encountered outside specialized wetland habitats.
