write-in candidate

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪt ɪn ˈkændɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ɪn ˈkændɪdeɪt / (BrE)
a candidate in an election whose name does not appear on the ballot and who is voted for by having their name written in by voters
candidate not listed on the ballot

Examples

Despite not being on the ballot, she received enough votes as a write-in candidate to win the election.
Many voters chose to support a write-in candidate after being dissatisfied with the official nominees.
He encouraged his supporters to vote for him as a write-in candidate in the upcoming mayoral race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candidate - a person who is nominated for election
