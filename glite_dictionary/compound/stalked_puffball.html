stalked puffball

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔkt ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈstɔːkt ˈpʌfˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A mushroom of the genus Tulostoma, characterized by having a stalk and resembling a puffball.
type of stalked mushroom (Tulostoma)

Examples

The stalked puffball can often be found growing in dry, sandy areas.
Unlike the typical round puffball, the stalked puffball has a noticeable stem elevating its spore case.
Mycology students studied the stalked puffball to learn about the diversity of fungi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puffball - A round, spore-producing fungus that emits clouds of spores when mature

2. NOUN

/ stɔkt ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / stɔːkt ˈpʌfˌbɔːl / (BrE)

puffball mushroom with a visible stalk

Examples

The stalked puffball can often be found in dry, sandy soils after heavy rains.
Scientists studied the unique spores of the stalked puffball species found in the desert.
Unlike its close relatives, the stalked puffball has a distinct stem supporting its spherical cap.
