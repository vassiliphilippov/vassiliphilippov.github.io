outta time

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊɾə taɪm / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə taɪm / (BrE)
(informal) Having no time left; with all available time used up.
no time left; time is up
informal

Examples

We realized we were outta time before we could solve the problem.
Sorry, but I'm outta time and have to leave now.
She rushed through her speech because she was almost outta time.

Synset Examples

We’re outta time; let’s finish this tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of time - no more time available; time limit reached
  • Antonym: in time - having time left; before a deadline
