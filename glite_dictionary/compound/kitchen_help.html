kitchen help

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən hɛlp / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃən hɛlp / (BrE)
a person or group of people employed to assist with routine tasks in a kitchen, especially in restaurants or institutions
person hired to assist in a kitchen

Examples

The restaurant was so busy last night that the manager hired extra kitchen help.
Many students work as kitchen help over the summer to earn some extra money.
We’re looking for reliable kitchen help to assist with food preparation and cleaning duties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitchen worker - person who works in a kitchen
  • Degree Lower: dishwasher - person employed to wash dishes in a kitchen
  • Degree Higher: cook - person who prepares food as their job
