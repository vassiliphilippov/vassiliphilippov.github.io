celebrity signature

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛbrəti ˈsɪɡnətʃɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛbrəti ˈsɪɡnətʃə / (BrE)
the autograph or handwritten name of a famous person, often collected as a souvenir or keepsake
autograph from a famous person

Examples

He proudly showed off the celebrity signature he got at the concert.
Collectors often pay a premium for an authentic celebrity signature.
She displayed her collection of celebrity signatures in a special album.

Synset Examples

He collected various celebrity signatures at the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autograph - a signature, especially that of a famous person, written as a keepsake
