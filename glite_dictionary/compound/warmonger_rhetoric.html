warmonger rhetoric

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrˌmʌŋɡɚ rɛˈθɒrɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌmʌŋɡə ˈrɛθ.ər.ɪk / (BrE)
language or statements intended to encourage or justify war or military aggression
language promoting war or aggression

Examples

The senator's warmonger rhetoric was widely criticized by peace activists.
Media analysts discussed how warmonger rhetoric could escalate international tensions.
Many citizens grew tired of the constant warmonger rhetoric in political debates.

Synset Examples

Peace groups often oppose warmonger rhetoric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war-mongering - encouraging or promoting war
