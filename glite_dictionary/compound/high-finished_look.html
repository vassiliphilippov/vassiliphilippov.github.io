high-finished look

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈfɪnɪʃt lʊk / (AmE), / haɪ ˈfɪnɪʃt lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance that is very polished, refined, and finished to a high standard
polished and refined appearance

Examples

The new office decor gives the space a high-finished look that impresses clients.
She chose tiles with a high-finished look for the bathroom renovation.
The car's interior had a high-finished look thanks to the polished wood accents.

Synset Examples

The furniture has a high-finished look that's very appealing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polished - having a smooth, refined, and finished surface or appearance
