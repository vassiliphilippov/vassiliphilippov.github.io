hug drug

1. NOUN

/ hʌɡ drʌɡ / (AmE), / hʌɡ drʌɡ / (BrE)
A slang term for the synthetic drug MDMA (ecstasy), known for its effects of increased feelings of empathy and affection.
slang for MDMA/ecstasy
slang

Examples

At the club, some people were openly discussing the effects of the hug drug they had taken.
Authorities warned of a new batch of hug drug pills circulating in the city.
The hug drug is popular at dance parties for its reputation of increasing sociability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - street name for MDMA
  • Synonym: MDMA - the chemical drug methylenedioxymethamphetamine
  • Degree Lower: love drug - slang for drugs that promote feelings of closeness
