corn whisky

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈwɪski / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈwɪski / (BrE)
a type of whiskey distilled from a mash containing at least 80 percent corn
whiskey made mostly from corn

Examples

He ordered a glass of corn whisky at the bar to go with his dinner.
Traditional corn whisky is known for its sweet and robust flavor profile.
Many distilleries in Kentucky are famous for producing high-quality corn whisky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bourbon - American whiskey made primarily from corn but with a different production process
