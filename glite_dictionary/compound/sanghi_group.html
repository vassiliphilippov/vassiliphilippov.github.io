sanghi group

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑŋɡi ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈsʌŋɡi ɡruːp / (BrE)
an organization promoting or advocating Hindu nationalist ideology, especially in India
Hindu nationalist organization in India

Examples

The sanghi group held a rally to promote their cultural agenda last weekend.
Critics often accuse the sanghi group of spreading divisive ideologies.
Several sanghi groups operate youth programs in various Indian cities.

Synset Examples

Many sanghi groups gather to discuss cultural issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: RSS - Hindu nationalist organization in India (Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh)

Etymology

From Hindi 'Sanghi' (member of the RSS, Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh) + English 'group'.
