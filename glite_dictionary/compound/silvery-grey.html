silvery-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvəri ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəri ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey colour that resembles or suggests silver; silvery in appearance.
grey resembling silver

Examples

Her hair turned a beautiful silvery-grey as she grew older.
The artist chose a silvery-grey paint to mimic the light on the river's surface.
He wore a silvery-grey suit that sparkled slightly under the lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver - a shiny greyish-white precious metal or its color
  • Synonym: grey - a color between black and white; having a neutral tone
