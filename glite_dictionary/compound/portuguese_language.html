Portuguese language

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔrtʃəˈɡiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtʃʊˈɡiːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken mainly in Portugal and Brazil, as well as other countries and regions
Romance language of Portugal and Brazil

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Portuguese language.
Books in the Portuguese language are widely available in Brazil.
Learning the Portuguese language can help you travel in Portugal and Mozambique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Spanish language - the Romance language spoken in Spain and Latin America
  • Synonym: Portuguese - of or relating to Portugal or its people or language

Etymology

From Portuguese 'português', relating to Portugal.
