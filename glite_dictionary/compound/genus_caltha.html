genus Caltha

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæl.θə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈkæl.θə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the buttercup family, Ranunculaceae, commonly known for marsh marigolds.
plant genus (buttercup family: marsh marigolds)

Examples

The genus Caltha includes several species of marsh marigold found in wet habitats.
Botanists have studied the genus Caltha to understand its unique adaptation to marshy environments.
One well-known member of the genus Caltha is the bright yellow marsh marigold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caltha palustris - a species of marsh marigold within the genus Caltha

Etymology

From Latin 'caltha' meaning 'marigold'.
