order Testudinata

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər tɛˌstuːdɪˈneɪtə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə tɛˌstjuːdɪˈneɪtə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of reptiles that includes all tortoises and turtles
taxonomic order of turtles and tortoises

Examples

Many species within the order Testudinata are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
The order Testudinata is characterized by a bony or cartilaginous shell that develops from their ribs.
Modern research has revealed much about the evolutionary history of the order Testudinata.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtle - any reptile of the order Testudinata
  • Synonym: tortoise - land-dwelling reptile of the order Testudinata

Etymology

From New Latin Testudinata, from Latin testudo (tortoise).
