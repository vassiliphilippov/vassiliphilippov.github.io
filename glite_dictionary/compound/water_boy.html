water boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a male assistant, especially in sports, whose job is to provide drinking water to players or participants.
male assistant who supplies water

Examples

The water boy quickly refilled the bottles during the football game.
Despite his young age, he was proud to serve as the team's water boy.
She thanked the water boy for keeping everyone hydrated during the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water carrier - person who carries and supplies water, often used in sports
