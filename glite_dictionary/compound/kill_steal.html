kill steal

1. NOUN

/ kɪl stil / (AmE), / kɪl stiːl / (BrE)
in online gaming, the act of taking credit for a kill that another player has mostly achieved, often by delivering the final blow
taking credit for another's kill in gaming
slang

Examples

Players often get angry when someone performs a kill steal in a heated battle.
After his teammate's kill steal, David felt frustrated and left the game early.
Some games try to limit kill steal behavior by sharing rewards among team members.

Synset Examples

In gaming, a kill steal can upset teammates.
He was accused of a kill steal during the match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: KS - abbreviation for 'kill steal' in gaming
