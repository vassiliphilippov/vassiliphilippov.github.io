crooked dealing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrʊkɪd ˈdiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkɪd ˈdiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
dishonest or unethical behavior, especially in business or financial matters
dishonest or unethical business conduct

Examples

The investigation uncovered years of crooked dealing within the company.
Voters were outraged by the politician's crooked dealing with contractors.
After repeated warnings, the agency took action against his crooked dealing.

Synset Examples

He was known for his crooked dealings in business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial gain
  • Antonym: honest dealing - conducting business fairly and honestly
