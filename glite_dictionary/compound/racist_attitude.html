racist attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪst æˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪst əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a set of beliefs, feelings, or behaviors characterized by prejudice or discrimination against people based on their race
prejudice or discrimination based on race

Examples

Anyone expressing a racist attitude in the workplace may face disciplinary action.
The politician's racist attitude was exposed during the heated debate.
Confronting a racist attitude requires both courage and understanding.

Synset Examples

His comments revealed his racist attitudes towards others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racial prejudice - prejudice based on race
  • Antonym: tolerance - willingness to accept differences
