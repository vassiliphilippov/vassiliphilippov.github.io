retrosexual style

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛt.roʊˈsɛk.ʃu.əl staɪl / (AmE), / ˌrɛt.rəʊˈsɛk.ʃu.əl staɪl / (BrE)
A style of dress or appearance that emphasizes classic, vintage, or traditionally masculine aesthetics.
classic/vintage-inspired style

Examples

His retrosexual style is evident in his vintage leather jackets and classic haircuts.
Many modern designers are bringing back the retrosexual style in their latest collections.
She appreciates a partner with a retrosexual style, preferring timeless looks over trendy fashions.

Synset Examples

He prefers a retrosexual style, favoring classic fashion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: metrosexual - a man interested in fashion and personal care, urban, and modern

Etymology

Formed from 'retro-' (from Latin 'retro', meaning backward) + 'sexual', modeled on 'metrosexual'.
