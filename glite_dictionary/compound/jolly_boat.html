jolly boat

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑli ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒli ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a small boat carried by a sailing ship, used by the crew for general tasks and transport
small ship's boat for general use

Examples

The crew lowered the jolly boat to ferry supplies ashore.
After the storm, the stranded sailors survived by taking refuge in the jolly boat.
Each morning, the captain inspected the jolly boat for damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yawl - a two-masted sailing boat
