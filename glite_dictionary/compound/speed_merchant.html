speed merchant

1. NOUN

/ spiːd ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / spiːd ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person who drives especially fast or is passionate about speed, particularly in vehicles
person who loves or drives at high speed

Examples

Everyone knows Tom is a real speed merchant on the highway.
The police are cracking down on speed merchants this month.
His reputation as a speed merchant made him a natural fit for the racing team.

Synset Examples

He's such a speed merchant; he drives way too fast.
As a speed merchant, he enjoys racing cars on weekends.
The speed merchant got pulled over for reckless driving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedster - a person who drives at high speeds
