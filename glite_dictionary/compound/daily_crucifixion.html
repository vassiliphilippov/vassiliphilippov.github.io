daily crucifixion

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli kruːsəˈfɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˌkruːsɪˈfɪkʃən / (BrE)
a state of constant or ongoing suffering or hardship, often felt as if it must be endured every day
ongoing suffering; constant hardship

Examples

For some, battling anxiety feels like a daily crucifixion that never truly ends.
Facing criticism at work became a kind of daily crucifixion for him.
Her memoir describes the daily crucifixion of living with chronic pain.

Synset Examples

He referred to his struggles as a daily crucifixion.
