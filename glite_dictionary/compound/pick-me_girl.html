pick-me girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk mi ˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpɪk mi ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or woman who seeks attention or approval, especially from men, by putting herself down or disparaging other women
girl seeking validation by putting herself down
slang

Examples

She was acting like a pick-me girl, laughing at jokes that made fun of herself.
People often criticize a pick-me girl for seeking male approval by putting down other women.
Despite being talented, she played the pick-me girl and dismissed her own achievements.

Synset Examples

Every time she speaks, it’s clear she’s a pick-me girl.
She’s such a pick-me girl, always trying to be the center of attention.
That pick-me girl always downplays her accomplishments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick-me boy - a boy who seeks validation by putting himself down or seeking attention

Etymology

From the phrase 'pick me,' implying a desire to be chosen, especially in a romantic or social context; emerged in internet slang in the late 2010s.
