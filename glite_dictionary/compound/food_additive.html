food additive

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈæd.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / fuːd ˈæd.ɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
a substance added to food to improve its flavor, appearance, or shelf-life
substance added to food for improvement

Examples

Many processed foods contain at least one food additive to enhance their taste or color.
The label lists every food additive used in the manufacturing process.
Strict regulations limit which food additive chemicals can be used in baby food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: additive - a substance added to something to alter or improve it
