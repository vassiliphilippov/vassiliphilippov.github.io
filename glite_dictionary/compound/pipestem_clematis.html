pipestem clematis

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪp.stɛm ˈklɛmə.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp.stɛm ˈklɛm.ə.tɪs / (BrE)
A clematis plant native to California, known for its woody stems resembling pipestems.
a clematis species native to California

Examples

The pipestem clematis is well adapted to the dry hillsides of California.
Gardeners appreciate the unique flowers that appear on the pipestem clematis each spring.
Wildlife often seek shelter among the tangled vines of the pipestem clematis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clematis - a genus of climbing plants with showy flowers
