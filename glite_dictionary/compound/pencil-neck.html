pencil-neck

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnslˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəlˌnɛk / (BrE)
a derogatory slang term for a person who is thin, weak, or perceived as overly intellectual
derogatory slang for a thin, weak, or nerdy person
informal offensive slang

Examples

The coach called him a pencil-neck after he missed another tackle on the field.
Some bullies used to tease the new student, calling him a pencil-neck.
He's no pencil-neck—he actually trains at the gym every day.

Synset Examples

Her pencil-neck friend knows everything about computers.
The pencil-neck engineer fixed the issue quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weakling - a person who is physically weak
