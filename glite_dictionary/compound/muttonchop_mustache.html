muttonchop mustache

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtənˌtʃɑp məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌtənˌtʃɒp məˈstæʃ / (BrE)
a style of mustache where the facial hair extends down the sides of the face along the jawline, resembling mutton chops
a mustache style with wide sideburns

Examples

In the 19th century, the muttonchop mustache was a popular symbol of masculinity.
He decided to grow a muttonchop mustache for his role in the historical play.
The barber gave him tips on maintaining his thick muttonchop mustache.

Synset Examples

The character wore a muttonchop mustache in the film.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sideburns - facial hair grown on the sides of the face in front of the ears

Etymology

From 'muttonchop' for its resemblance to a chop of mutton, and 'mustache' referring to facial hair above the upper lip.
