polling place

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊlɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊlɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place where people go to cast their votes in an election
location for casting votes in an election

Examples

After checking the address, we found our polling place and voted early in the morning.
The city opened an additional polling place to accommodate more voters this year.
Don’t forget to bring your ID to your designated polling place on Election Day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polling station - a place where people go to vote in an election (British English)
