coat rack

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊt ˌræk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊt ˌræk / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with hooks for hanging coats and hats
rack with hooks for coats and hats

Examples

Please hang your jacket on the coat rack by the door.
The hallway looked neater once we added a coat rack for guests.
He placed his umbrella and hat on the old wooden coat rack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat stand - a stand for holding hats, often similar in form to a coat rack
