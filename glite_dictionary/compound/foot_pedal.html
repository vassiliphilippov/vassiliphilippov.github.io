foot pedal

1. NOUN

/ fʊt ˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / fʊt ˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
A lever operated by the foot, used to control a mechanism or device.
lever operated by the foot

Examples

The sewing machine is controlled by a foot pedal located at the base.
She pressed the foot pedal to speed up the mixing in the kitchen appliance.
Many cars use a foot pedal for the brake system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pedal - a lever pressed by the foot to control something
