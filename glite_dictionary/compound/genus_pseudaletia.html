genus Pseudaletia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌsjuː.dəˈliː.ʃə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌsjuː.dəˈliː.tɪə / (BrE)
a genus of moths whose larvae are known as armyworms
genus of moths (armyworm larvae)

Examples

The larvae of the genus Pseudaletia are considered major agricultural pests.
Recent studies have focused on the migration patterns of moths in the genus Pseudaletia.
Experts are working to control the spread of genus Pseudaletia moths in cornfields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armyworm - larva stage of certain moths, known as crop pest

Etymology

From New Latin, combining 'pseudo-' (false) and Greek 'Aletia' (a related genus name).
