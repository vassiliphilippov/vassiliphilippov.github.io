clean water

1. NOUN

/ klin ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / kliːn ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is free from impurities and safe to drink or use
water free from impurities

Examples

Access to clean water is essential for good health.
After the storm, many residents struggled to find clean water for drinking and cooking.
The organization helps provide clean water to remote villages.

Synset Examples

They installed the filter to clean water downstream.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contaminated water - water containing impurities or harmful substances
