whizbang idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪzˌbæŋ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈwɪzˌbaŋ aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
An impressive or remarkably clever idea.
impressive or remarkable idea
informal american

Examples

She came up with a whizbang idea for marketing the new product.
That whizbang idea could change the way we think about transportation forever.
Everyone was impressed by his whizbang idea during the brainstorming session.

Synset Examples

His whizbang idea won him the innovation award.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brainwave - a sudden clever idea
  • Synonym: stroke of genius - an outstandingly clever idea
  • Antonym: bad idea - an idea that is not good or effective

Etymology

From American slang 'whiz-bang' (something striking or flashy), originally from early 20th-century US military slang for a type of shell.
