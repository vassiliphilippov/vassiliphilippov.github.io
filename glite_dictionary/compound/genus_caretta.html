genus Caretta

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs kəˈrɛ.tə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs kəˈrɛ.tə / (BrE)
A genus of large sea turtles, including the loggerhead turtle (Caretta caretta).
sea turtle genus including loggerhead

Examples

The genus Caretta includes the well-known loggerhead turtle found in oceans worldwide.
Research on the genus Caretta has contributed to marine conservation efforts.
Egg-laying patterns of turtles in the genus Caretta have been carefully studied by zoologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loggerhead turtle - a large, broad-headed sea turtle (Caretta caretta)

Etymology

From Latin 'caretta', used in scientific classification.
