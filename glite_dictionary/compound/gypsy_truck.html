gypsy truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪpsi trʌk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsi trʌk / (BrE)
A mobile vehicle, often brightly decorated, used for selling crafts or goods, particularly in a traveling or itinerant fashion.
decorated vehicle for selling crafts

Examples

The artists traveled from town to town in a gypsy truck, displaying their wares at local festivals.
Visitors were drawn to the gypsy truck by its bright colors and lively music.
She transformed an old van into a gypsy truck to sell her jewelry at markets across the country.

Synset Examples

They drove around in a colorful gypsy truck selling handmade crafts.

Etymology

From 'gypsy', historically referring to Romani people, and 'truck'; often associated with brightly painted vehicles used in traveling fairs or markets.
