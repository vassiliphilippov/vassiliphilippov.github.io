Great Mendenhall Glacier

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt ˈmɛndənˌhɔl ˈɡleɪʃər / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈmɛndənhɔːl ˈɡleɪsiə / (BrE)
a large Piedmont glacier located near Juneau in southeastern Alaska, known for its accessibility and scenic views
large glacier near Juneau, Alaska

Examples

The Great Mendenhall Glacier attracts thousands of visitors each year with its breathtaking blue ice.
