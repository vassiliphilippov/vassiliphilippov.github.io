American Virgin Islands

1. PROPN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈvɜr.dʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈvɜː.dʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
A group of more than 130 islands in the southeastern Virgin Islands, which are an unincorporated territory of the United States.
US territory in the Virgin Islands

Examples

The American Virgin Islands attract thousands of tourists each year for their beautiful beaches.
Many cruise ships dock at the American Virgin Islands during their Caribbean routes.
