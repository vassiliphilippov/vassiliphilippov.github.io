ammonium alum

1. NOUN

/ əˈmoʊniəm ˈæləm / (AmE), / əˈməʊniəm ˈaləm / (BrE)
a white crystalline double sulfate of aluminum and ammonium, used in water purification, dyeing, and other industrial processes.
double sulfate of aluminum and ammonium

Examples

The laboratory ordered more ammonium alum for the next set of experiments.
Industrial textile processes often use ammonium alum as a mordant.
You should wear gloves when handling ammonium alum to avoid skin irritation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alum - a group of double sulfates of aluminum and another metal
