abstract artist

1. NOUN

/ ˈæbˌstrækt ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈæb.strækt ˈɑː.tɪst / (BrE)
an artist who creates works of art that do not attempt to represent external reality, focusing on shapes, colors, and forms instead
artist creating abstract art

Examples

The gallery is hosting an exhibition of works by a renowned abstract artist this month.
As an abstract artist, she prefers to express emotions through colors and shapes rather than realistic images.
Many critics have praised the innovative style of the young abstract artist from New York.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstract painter - a painter who produces abstract art
  • Antonym: realist artist - an artist who depicts subjects as they appear in everyday life
