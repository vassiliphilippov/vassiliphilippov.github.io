single mom

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl mɑm / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl mɒm / (BrE)
A mother who raises her child or children without the presence or support of a partner.
mother raising children alone

Examples

After her divorce, she became a single mom working two jobs to support her children.
Being a single mom can be both challenging and rewarding at the same time.
Many single moms find strength and community through local support groups.

Synset Examples

She struggled as a single mom with a deadbeat dad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single mother - a mother raising children alone
  • Antonym: single dad - a father raising children alone
