spoke misalignment

1. NOUN

/ spoʊk ˌmɪsaɪˈlaɪnmənt / (AmE), / spəʊk ˌmɪsaɪˈlaɪnmənt / (BrE)
The condition in which the spokes of a bicycle wheel are not properly adjusted, causing the wheel to be out of true.
improper adjustment of bicycle spokes

Examples

Persistent spoke misalignment can shorten the lifespan of a bicycle wheel.
If you notice unusual wobbling, spoke misalignment may be the cause.
A skilled mechanic can quickly fix spoke misalignment during a routine service.

Synset Examples

Spoke misalignment causes the wheel to wobble side to side often touching the brake pads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheel out of true - a condition where a wheel does not rotate straight due to spoke or rim issues
