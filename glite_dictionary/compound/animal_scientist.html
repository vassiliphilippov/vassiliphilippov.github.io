animal scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪml ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈænɪml ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a specialist in the scientific study of animals, especially their biology, behavior, and management
specialist in animal science

Examples

The animal scientist conducted research on livestock nutrition and welfare.
As an animal scientist, she studied the behavior of endangered species.
Many animal scientists collaborate with farmers to improve animal husbandry practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - an expert in the scientific study of animals (zoology)
