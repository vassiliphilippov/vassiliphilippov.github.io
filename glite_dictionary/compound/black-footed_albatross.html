black-footed albatross

1. NOUN

/ ˌblæk ˈfʊtɪd ˈæl.bəˌtrɔs / (AmE), / ˌblak ˈfʊtɪd ˈal.bəˌtrɒs / (BrE)
a large seabird of the North Pacific, noted for its dark plumage and distinctive black feet
North Pacific seabird with black feet

Examples

The black-footed albatross is commonly found soaring over the northern Pacific Ocean.
Researchers attach tracking devices to some black-footed albatrosses to study their migration patterns.
A black-footed albatross glides effortlessly just above the waves, searching for fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - large seabird of the Diomedeidae family
