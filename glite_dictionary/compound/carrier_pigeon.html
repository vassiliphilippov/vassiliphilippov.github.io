carrier pigeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæriɚ ˌpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈkæriə ˌpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a homing pigeon trained to carry messages from one place to another
homing pigeon used to carry messages

Examples

During the war, a carrier pigeon delivered crucial information to the front lines.
The museum displayed a model of a carrier pigeon with a small message capsule on its leg.
Before modern technology, armies relied on the carrier pigeon for communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homing pigeon - pigeon trained to return home over long distances

Etymology

From 'carrier' (one who carries) + 'pigeon' (a bird species); refers to the bird's role in carrying messages.
