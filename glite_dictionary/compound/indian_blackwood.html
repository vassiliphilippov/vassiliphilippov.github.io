Indian blackwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈblækwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈblækwʊd / (BrE)
a tree native to India (Dalbergia latifolia) valued for its dark purple hardwood, often used in fine woodworking and musical instruments
tree with dark purple hardwood, native to India

Examples

The furniture was crafted from Indian blackwood, giving it a rich, elegant finish.
Many clarinets are made from Indian blackwood because of its excellent acoustic properties.
The conservation of Indian blackwood is crucial due to its high demand in various industries.
