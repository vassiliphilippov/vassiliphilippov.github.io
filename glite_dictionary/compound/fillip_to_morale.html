fillip to morale

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪl.ɪp tə məˈræl / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.ɪp tə məˈrɑːl / (BrE)
an action or event that provides a boost or improvement in morale
an action that boosts morale

Examples

Winning the local championship provided a much-needed fillip to morale among the team members.
A surprise bonus acted as a fillip to morale in the company.
Her encouraging speech was a real fillip to morale during tough times.

Synset Examples

A little recognition can provide a fillip to morale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morale booster - something that increases morale

Etymology

From 'fillip', meaning a stimulus or boost, ultimately from Middle English, originally meaning a snap of the fingers.
