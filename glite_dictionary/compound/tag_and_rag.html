tag and rag

1. NOUN

/ tæɡ ənd ræɡ / (AmE), / tæɡ ənd ræɡ / (BrE)
a practice or workflow system in which participants are tagged or checked off (tagged), and records are collected or checked (ragged), as a way to monitor efficiency or safety
a workflow or safety checking system

Examples

The coach introduced a tag and rag method to keep track of players during drills.
Our company adopted a tag and rag approach to strengthen safety inspections on the factory floor.
The tag and rag procedure helped the team identify potential bottlenecks in the workflow.
