magnetic variation

1. NOUN

/ mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˌvɛriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˌvɛəriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the angle at a given location between magnetic north and true north, used in navigation and cartography
angle between magnetic and true north

Examples

The pilot checked the magnetic variation before plotting the course.
Maps must be updated regularly to account for changes in magnetic variation.
Global positioning today reduces the issues once created by inaccurate magnetic variation figures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic declination - the angle between magnetic north and true north, synonym for magnetic variation
