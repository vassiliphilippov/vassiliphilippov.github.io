racially challenged

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪʃəli tʃəˈlændʒd / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəli tʃəˈlændʒd / (BrE)
Used, often humorously or euphemistically, to refer to someone or something that is racially insensitive, or awkward regarding issues of race.
euphemism for racially insensitive
informal

Examples

He was careful not to sound racially challenged during the meeting.
Some jokes can come across as racially challenged, even if unintended.
The company issued an apology for its racially challenged advertising campaign.

Synset Examples

He jokingly referred to himself as racially challenged in discussions.
The racially challenged situation requires sensitive handling.
Her comments were seen as racially challenged and sparked debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racially insensitive - showing lack of awareness or respect for racial issues
