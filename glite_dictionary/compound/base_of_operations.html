base of operations

1. NOUN

/ beɪs əv ˌɑpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / beɪs əv ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
a place or facility from which military or other organized activities are launched or coordinated
main location for launching operations

Examples

The company established a base of operations in Singapore to expand into Asia.
The hikers used the lodge as their base of operations for exploring the mountains.
During the campaign, the army set up a temporary base of operations near the border.

Synset Examples

the attack wiped out our forward bases

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - the main office or center of operations for an organization
