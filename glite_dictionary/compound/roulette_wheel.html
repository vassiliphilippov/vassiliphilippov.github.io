roulette wheel

1. NOUN

/ ruˈlɛt wiːl / (AmE), / ruːˈlɛt wiːl / (BrE)
A horizontal wheel with numbered slots, used in the game of roulette, on which bets are placed as to the slot in which a small ball will come to rest.
wheel used in the game of roulette

Examples

The croupier spun the roulette wheel and everyone at the table watched in anticipation.
She placed her chips on red, hoping the roulette wheel would land in her favor.
After several rounds, he noticed a pattern in the way the roulette wheel seemed to spin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roulette - a gambling game in which players bet on which section a ball will land on a spinning wheel

Etymology

From French 'roulette', meaning 'little wheel'.
