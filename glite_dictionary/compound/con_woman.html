con woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkɒn ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who tricks people into giving her money or valuables by gaining their trust
female swindler, confidence trickster

Examples

The con woman posed as a wealthy investor to swindle dozens out of their savings.
Authorities finally captured the con woman who had been running elaborate scams for years.
People were shocked to learn their trusted advisor was actually a cunning con woman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: con man - a man who tricks people by gaining their trust
  • Synonym: con artist - a person who deceives others by gaining their trust

Etymology

From 'con' (short for 'confidence', in 'confidence trick') + 'woman'.
