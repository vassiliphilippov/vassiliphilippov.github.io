basketball team

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl tim / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtˌbɔːl tiːm / (BrE)
A group of players who play basketball together as a single unit, especially in organized competitions.
group of basketball players

Examples

The school's basketball team won the regional championship last year.
She was selected to join the national basketball team after a series of impressive games.
Every Saturday, the local basketball team practices at the community gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: team - a group of people working together, especially in sport or work
