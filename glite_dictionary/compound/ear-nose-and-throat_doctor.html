ear-nose-and-throat doctor

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪr ˌnoʊz ənd ˈθroʊt ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / ˌɪə ˌnəʊz ənd ˈθrəʊt ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose, and throat
doctor for ear, nose, and throat disorders
medical

Examples

My ear-nose-and-throat doctor recommended surgery to improve my breathing.
After weeks of sore throats, she made an appointment with an ear-nose-and-throat doctor.
The ear-nose-and-throat doctor diagnosed him with a sinus infection.
