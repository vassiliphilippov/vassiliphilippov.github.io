physics laboratory

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪks ləˌbɔrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪks ləˈbɒrətəri / (BrE)
A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching in the field of physics.
laboratory for physics experiments or research

Examples

The university has a state-of-the-art physics laboratory for its science students.
She spent long hours conducting experiments in the physics laboratory.
Safety goggles are required at all times when working in the physics laboratory.

Related Terms
