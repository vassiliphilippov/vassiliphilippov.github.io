mental soundness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntl saʊndnɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛntl saʊndnəs / (BrE)
a healthy psychological state characterized by good judgment and rational thinking
healthy psychological state, good judgment

Examples

The doctor evaluated her mental soundness before making any recommendations.
Proving his mental soundness was necessary for him to return to work.
Stress and lack of sleep can gradually erode one's mental soundness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanity - the condition of having a healthy mind
  • Antonym: insanity - severe mental illness; loss of sanity
