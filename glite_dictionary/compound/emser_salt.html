Emser salt

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmzər sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈɛmsə sɒlt / (BrE)
a medicinal salt obtained from the mineral waters of Bad Ems, used especially for treating conditions of the upper respiratory tract
medicinal salt from Bad Ems mineral water
medical

Examples

Gargling with Emser salt can help relieve a sore throat.
Many patients with sinus problems use Emser salt for nasal irrigation.
The pharmacy stocks Emser salt for customers seeking natural respiratory remedies.

Synset Examples

Gargling with Emser salt water is a quick way to heal an infected throat.

Etymology

Named after Bad Ems, a spa town in Germany, where the salt is extracted.
