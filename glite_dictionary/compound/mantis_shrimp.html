mantis shrimp

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæntɪs ʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈmæntɪs ʃrɪmp / (BrE)
A tropical marine crustacean with large, spiny, grasping appendages, known for its vibrant colors and extremely powerful claws.
tropical marine crustacean with powerful claws

Examples

The mantis shrimp is famous for its striking, vibrant colors and extraordinary eyesight.
Some species of mantis shrimp can shatter aquarium glass with their powerful claws.
Researchers study the mantis shrimp to learn more about its unique vision and hunting technique.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the praying mantis and its shrimplike body.
