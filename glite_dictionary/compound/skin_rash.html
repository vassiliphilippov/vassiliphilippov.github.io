skin rash

1. NOUN

/ skɪn ræʃ / (AmE), / skɪn ræʃ / (BrE)
a noticeable area of reddened, irritated, or inflamed skin, often marked by spots or bumps, due to illness or irritation
area of reddened, irritated skin

Examples

The child developed a skin rash after eating strawberries.
Wearing certain fabrics can cause a skin rash in sensitive individuals.
If you notice a skin rash that doesn't go away, consult a doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rash - an area of reddened or irritated skin
