musical arrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
a piece of music that has been adapted for performance by a particular set of voices or instruments
adapted piece of music for specific performers

Examples

The choir performed a beautiful musical arrangement of a classic folk song.
That musical arrangement highlights the strings and gives the piece a fresh sound.
She specializes in creating musical arrangements for jazz ensembles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrangement - a version of a musical composition adapted for performance
