neckbeardy shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌbɪrdi ʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌbɪədi ʃɜːt / (BrE)
An informal term for a shirt associated with nerd culture or fandom, often with graphic designs referencing games, comics, or internet memes.
shirt linked to nerd or fandom culture
informal

Examples

He wore a neckbeardy shirt featuring a classic video game character to the convention.
You can always spot a true fan by their collection of neckbeardy shirts.
Her wardrobe is full of neckbeardy shirts with references to science fiction movies.

Synset Examples

He wore a neckbeardy shirt to the comic book store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graphic tee - a T-shirt with a printed design or logo, often used for self-expression
  • Synonym: fandom - the state of being a fan of something, especially in nerd/geek culture

Etymology

From 'neckbeard' (an internet term for certain nerd stereotypes) plus 'shirt'.
