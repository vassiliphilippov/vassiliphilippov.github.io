coumarone-indene resin

1. NOUN

/ ˌkuːməˌroʊn ɪnˈdiːn ˈrɛz.ɪn / (AmE), / ˌkuːməˌrəʊn ɪnˈdiːn ˈrɛz.ɪn / (BrE)
a thermoplastic resin produced by polymerizing indene and coumarone, used in coatings, paints, and asphalt tiles
thermoplastic resin used in coatings and paints

Examples

The manufacturer recommends using coumarone-indene resin for a more durable surface coating.
Paints containing coumarone-indene resin resist weathering better than conventional formulas.
The addition of coumarone-indene resin improved the flexibility of the asphalt tiles.

Etymology

From 'coumarone' and 'indene', the chemical compounds used in its production.
