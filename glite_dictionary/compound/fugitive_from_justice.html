fugitive from justice

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːdʒətɪv frəm ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / ˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv frəm ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
a person who is sought by law enforcement authorities and attempts to avoid arrest or prosecution
person evading law enforcement

Examples

The police spent weeks searching for the fugitive from justice after his escape.
He was declared a fugitive from justice following the indictment.
Several fugitives from justice were apprehended near the border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fugitive - a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest
