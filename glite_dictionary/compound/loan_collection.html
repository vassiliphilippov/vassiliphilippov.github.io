loan collection

1. NOUN

/ loʊn kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ləʊn kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a set of artworks or items temporarily loaned by their owners for public exhibition
set of loaned items for exhibition

Examples

The museum's new exhibit features a loan collection of Impressionist paintings.
Several rare manuscripts from private owners are part of the loan collection on display this month.
The gallery organized a loan collection to celebrate the city's art history.
