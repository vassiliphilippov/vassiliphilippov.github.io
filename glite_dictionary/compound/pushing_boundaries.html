pushing boundaries

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊʃɪŋ ˈbaʊndəriz / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃɪŋ ˈbaʊnd(ə)riz / (BrE)
challenging established limits or norms, often in a creative, innovative, or rebellious way
challenging limits or norms

Examples

The artist is known for pushing boundaries in every exhibit she creates.
Innovative companies thrive by pushing boundaries and disrupting traditional industries.
He got in trouble at school for constantly pushing boundaries and questioning the rules.
