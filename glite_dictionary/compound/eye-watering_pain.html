eye-watering pain

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ ˌwɔtərɪŋ peɪn / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌwɔːtərɪŋ peɪn / (BrE)
pain that is extremely intense or severe, causing a physical reaction such as tears
extremely intense or severe pain

Examples

He experienced eye-watering pain in his ankle after the accident.
The dentist warned her there might be some eye-watering pain after the procedure.
When he broke his arm, the eye-watering pain made it hard for him to think straight.

Synset Examples

She felt an eye-watering pain after the surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excruciating pain - intensely severe pain
