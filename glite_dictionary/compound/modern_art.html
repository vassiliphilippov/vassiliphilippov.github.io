modern art

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdərn ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən ɑːt / (BrE)
art produced from the late 19th century onwards, often characterized by a break with traditional styles and a focus on innovation and new techniques
art from late 19th century onwards

Examples

The exhibition features rare pieces of modern art from renowned painters.
She wrote her thesis on the impact of technology in modern art.
Many people debate whether abstract paintings should be considered modern art or something else.

Synset Examples
