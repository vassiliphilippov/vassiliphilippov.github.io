Teflon-coated

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛflɑn ˈkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛflɒn ˈkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having a surface coated with Teflon, giving it non-stick properties, especially in cookware.
covered with non-stick Teflon

Examples

The Teflon-coated pan allowed her to fry eggs without using any oil.
This baking sheet is Teflon-coated, so cookies won't stick to it.
He bought a set of Teflon-coated pots for his new kitchen.

Synset Examples

His teflon-coated frying pan makes cooking a breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: non-stick - Preventing food from sticking to the surface

Etymology

Teflon is a trademark for polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), first registered in 1945 by DuPont.
