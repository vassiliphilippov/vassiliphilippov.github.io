worldly concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldli kənˈsɝːn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a matter or interest related to the practical affairs and material aspects of daily life, as opposed to spiritual or religious matters
matter related to material life, not spiritual

Examples

His mind was often occupied by worldly concerns rather than spiritual matters.
The monks renounce worldly concerns to focus on their faith.
She tried to put aside all worldly concerns during her meditation retreat.

Synset Examples

they consider the church to be independent of the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: material concern - a concern with physical or material matters
  • Antonym: spiritual concern - a concern with spiritual or religious matters
