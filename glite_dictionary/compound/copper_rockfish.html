copper rockfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːpər ˈrɑkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˈrɒkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of rockfish with a coppery coloration found in the Pacific coastal waters of North America
a Pacific North American species of rockfish

Examples

The diver spotted a copper rockfish hiding among the kelp near the shore.
Scientists are studying how the copper rockfish adapts to different water temperatures.
A copper rockfish can live for several decades in its natural habitat.
