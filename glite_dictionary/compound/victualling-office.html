victualling-office

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪtʃu.əlɪŋ ˌɑː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪt.ju.əl.ɪŋ ˌɒf.ɪs / (BrE)
a government office responsible for supplying food, especially to a navy or other military forces; now historical
office supplying food, esp. for navy (historical)
archaic

Examples

The victualling-office was responsible for provisioning the entire fleet during the long voyage.
After decades of service, the victualling-office was finally closed in the late nineteenth century.
He requested funding from the victualling-office to improve food storage for sailors.

Synset Examples

The victualling-office is crucial for naval operations.
The victualling-office ensured the fleet was well supplied.
He worked at the victualling-office, managing food supplies for sailors.

Etymology

From 'victual' (from Latin *victus*, food) + 'office'.
