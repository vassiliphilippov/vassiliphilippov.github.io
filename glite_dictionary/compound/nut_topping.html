nut topping

1. NOUN

/ nʌt ˈtɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / nʌt ˈtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer or covering made from chopped or ground nuts, used to add flavor and texture to food
chopped nuts used as a topping

Examples

The cheesecake was finished with a generous nut topping for extra crunch.
Ice cream sundaes often include a nut topping as a flavorful garnish.
She sprinkled a nut topping over the salad to add texture and taste.

Synset Examples

The recipe calls for a nut topping for crunch.
