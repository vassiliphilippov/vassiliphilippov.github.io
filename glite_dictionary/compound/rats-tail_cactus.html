rat's-tail cactus

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætsˌteɪl ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈrætsˌteɪl ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
a tropical American cactus with slender, trailing stems and large, showy crimson flowers that bloom for several days
tropical cactus with trailing stems and crimson flowers

Examples

The rat's-tail cactus is prized for its striking crimson flowers and trailing stems.
She placed a rat's-tail cactus on her balcony for its unique appearance.
Botanists often recommend the rat's-tail cactus to those who want an easy-to-care-for plant.
