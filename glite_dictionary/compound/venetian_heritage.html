Venetian heritage

1. NOUN

/ vəˈniʃən ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / vɪˈniːʃən ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the cultural traditions, history, and influences originating from Venice or its people
cultural background from Venice

Examples

The city is proud of its Venetian heritage, visible in its festivals and traditions.
Many families in the region trace their Venetian heritage back for centuries.
The museum's collection highlights the Venetian heritage in art and craftsmanship.

Synset Examples

The architecture in the city reflects its Venetian heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Venetian - a native or inhabitant of Venice; relating to Venice
