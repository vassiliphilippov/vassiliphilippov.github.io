mill agent

1. NOUN

/ mɪl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / mɪl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
the person responsible for managing a mill on behalf of an absentee owner.
manager of a mill for an absentee owner

Examples

The mill agent oversaw daily operations and reported directly to the absentee owner.
As a trusted mill agent, she was responsible for handling all financial matters of the factory.
The success of the textile plant was attributed to a skilled mill agent who managed both workers and resources.
