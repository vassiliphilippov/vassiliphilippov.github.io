mess jacket

1. NOUN

/ mɛs ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / mɛs ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A waist-length formal jacket, usually with a pointed back, worn by military officers for formal dinners or occasions in the mess.
waist-length formal military jacket

Examples

The officer wore a distinctive mess jacket to the formal dinner.
His mess jacket was adorned with medals and gold trim for the event.
Tailors must ensure each mess jacket fits precisely to maintain a sharp appearance.

Etymology

From 'mess' (military dining room) and 'jacket'; refers to jackets worn in the officers' mess.
