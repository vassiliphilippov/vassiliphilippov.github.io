outlook on life

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌlʊk ɑn laɪf / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlʊk ɒn laɪf / (BrE)
a person's general attitude or perspective towards life and its experiences
attitude or perspective towards life

Examples

After traveling the world, his outlook on life changed dramatically.
She credits a positive outlook on life for her ability to overcome difficulties.
Different cultures can influence a person's outlook on life in various ways.

Synset Examples

Her outlook on life is always positive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attitude - the way you think and feel about something
  • Synonym: perspective - a particular attitude towards or way of regarding something
