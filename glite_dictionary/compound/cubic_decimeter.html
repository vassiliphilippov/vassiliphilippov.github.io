cubic decimeter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdiː.sɪˌmiː.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdiː.sɪˌmiː.tə / (BrE)
A metric unit of volume equal to one liter, or 1,000 cubic centimeters.
metric unit of volume (1,000 cm³)

Examples

The tank has a capacity of 75 cubic decimeters, which is equivalent to 75 liters.
Scientists measured the volume of the container and found it to be one cubic decimeter.
He filled the aquarium with exactly ten cubic decimeters of water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liter - A metric unit of volume, equal to one cubic decimeter

Etymology

From Latin "decimus" (tenth) and Greek "metron" (measure), combined to form the metric term "decimeter."
