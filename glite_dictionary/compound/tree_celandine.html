tree celandine

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈsɛlənˌdin / (AmE), / tri ˈsɛləndiːn / (BrE)
a small Central American tree that bears racemes of purple-tinted green flowers
small Central American tree with purple-tinted green flowers

Examples

The tree celandine is prized in gardens for its unique purple-tinted green flowers.
Several botanists studied the tree celandine in its native habitat in Central America.
We planted a tree celandine near the south fence for its attractive blossoms.
