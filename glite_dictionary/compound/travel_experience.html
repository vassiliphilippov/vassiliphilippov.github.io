travel experience

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an event, activity, or feeling that a person has while traveling, especially one that is memorable or significant
an experience had while traveling

Examples

Her first travel experience abroad taught her a lot about independence.
Each travel experience offers new insights into different cultures.
He recounted his favorite travel experiences during the dinner party.

Synset Examples

She started a blog to share her travel experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journey - an act of traveling from one place to another
