shopping needs

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːpɪŋ niːdz / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ niːdz / (BrE)
the requirements or items a person wants or needs to buy when shopping
requirements or desires related to shopping

Examples

She made a list of her shopping needs before going to the supermarket.
Online stores cater to a wide variety of shopping needs these days.
You can find almost everything for your shopping needs at the new mall.

Synset Examples

Everyone heads to Mallwart for their shopping needs.
