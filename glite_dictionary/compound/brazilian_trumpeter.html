Brazilian trumpeter

1. NOUN

/ brəˈzɪliən ˈtrʌmpətər / (AmE), / brəˈzɪliən ˈtrʌmpɪtə / (BrE)
a South American bird, native to Brazil and Guiana, often kept to protect poultry; known for its loud, trumpet-like call
South American bird with a trumpet-like call

Examples

The Brazilian trumpeter is valued by farmers for guarding their chickens against predators.
I heard the distinctive call of a Brazilian trumpeter while visiting the rainforest.
Some people keep a Brazilian trumpeter as a pet because of its unusual vocalizations.
