fruit drink

1. NOUN

/ fruːt drɪŋk / (AmE), / fruːt drɪŋk / (BrE)
a sweetened beverage made from diluted fruit juice, often containing added sugar or flavoring
sweetened beverage with diluted fruit juice

Examples

He offered the children a cold fruit drink on a hot summer day.
Many fruit drinks contain more sugar than real fruit juice.
She prefers a natural fruit drink instead of soda.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: fruit juice - the juice extracted from fruit
