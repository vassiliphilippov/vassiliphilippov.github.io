city line

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti laɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪti laɪn / (BrE)
the boundary that marks the limits of a city
city boundary; city limit

Examples

The new shopping mall is located just outside the city line.
Property taxes tend to change significantly once you cross the city line.
The signs at the city line welcome visitors to the town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city limit - the furthest boundary of a city
