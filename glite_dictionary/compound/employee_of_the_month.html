employee of the month

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪi əv ðə mʌnθ / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪi əv ðə mʌnθ / (BrE)
a recognition given to an employee for outstanding performance during a particular month
award for best worker of the month

Examples

After all her hard work, Julia was finally named employee of the month at her company.
The employee of the month receives a special parking spot and a certificate.
Each department selects an employee of the month to recognize dedication and excellence.

Synset Examples

David won the employee of the month award.
