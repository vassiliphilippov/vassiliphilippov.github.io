sun deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌdɛk / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌdɛk / (BrE)
An open, unroofed deck or platform, typically built on a ship or building, intended for sunbathing or relaxation in the sun.
open, unroofed deck for sunbathing

Examples

We spent the afternoon lounging on the sun deck by the pool.
The cruise ship's sun deck offers panoramic views of the ocean.
Guests gathered on the hotel's rooftop sun deck to enjoy the sunset.

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' and 'deck', where 'deck' refers to a flat surface or platform.
