critical appraisal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪdɪkəl əˈpreɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl əˈpreɪzəl / (BrE)
a careful and systematic evaluation of something to judge its value, quality, or significance
systematic evaluative analysis

Examples

The professor conducted a critical appraisal of the newly published research article.
Before adopting the new policy, the committee performed a critical appraisal of all available evidence.
Every medical intervention should be subject to a thorough critical appraisal for safety and effectiveness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evaluation - the making of a judgment about value or quality
