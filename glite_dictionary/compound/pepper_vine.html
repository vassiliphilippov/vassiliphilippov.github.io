pepper vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə vaɪn / (BrE)
any of several shrubby or climbing plants of the genus Piper, including varieties used for culinary spices or ornamental purposes.
a shrubby or climbing plant of genus Piper

Examples

The pepper vine can be found growing in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.
Gardeners often cultivate pepper vine for its aromatic leaves and decorative appearance.
The black pepper we use in cooking is produced from the dried fruit of a pepper vine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Piper - the genus of pepper plants
