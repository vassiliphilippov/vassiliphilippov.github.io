to extend a hand

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈstɛnd ə hænd / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnd ə hænd / (BrE)
to offer help, assistance, or friendship, often shown by reaching out one's hand toward someone
offer help or friendship

Examples

During the crisis, neighbors were quick to extend a hand to those in need.
She decided to extend a hand to her new colleague to make him feel welcome.
When he saw the newcomer struggling, he didn't hesitate to extend a hand.

Synset Examples

She was quick to extend her hand to help.
He decided to extend his hand in friendship.
It's polite to extend your hand when greeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to lend a hand - offer help or assistance
