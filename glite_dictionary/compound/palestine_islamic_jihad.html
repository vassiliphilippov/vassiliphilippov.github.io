Palestine Islamic Jihad

1. PROPN

/ ˌpæl.əˌstiːn ɪˈslɑː.mɪk dʒɪˈhæd / (AmE), / ˌpæl.ɪˌstiːn ɪˈslæm.ɪk dʒɪˈhæd / (BrE)
a militant Palestinian organization established in 1979 that advocates for the creation of an Islamic state in Palestine and the destruction of Israel; it is smaller and more militant than Hamas
militant Palestinian Islamist group formed in 1979

Examples

The Palestine Islamic Jihad has claimed responsibility for several attacks in Israel.
Security officials are concerned about cooperation between Hamas and Palestine Islamic Jihad.
Palestine Islamic Jihad was founded in 1979 in Gaza by Fathi Shaqaqi and Abd al-Aziz Awda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hamas - a larger Palestinian Islamic militant organization and political party
