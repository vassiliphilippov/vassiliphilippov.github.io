gas gauge

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡæs ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument in a vehicle that shows the amount of gasoline remaining in the fuel tank
instrument showing fuel level in a vehicle

Examples

The driver checked the gas gauge to see if there was enough fuel for the trip.
When the gas gauge reads empty, it's time to refuel the car.
The mechanic said the gas gauge was not working properly and needed to be fixed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - instrument showing amount of fuel in a vehicle
