bond rating

1. NOUN

/ bɑnd ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / bɒnd ˈreɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
an evaluation of the credit quality of a bond issue, assessing the likelihood that the issuer will default on payments
credit quality assessment of a bond

Examples

The company's new bond rating reflects its improved financial stability.
After the downgrade, the bond rating for the city’s debt fell to junk status.
Investors pay close attention to a bond rating before deciding whether to buy a particular security.

Synset Examples

the bonds of highest quality are said to have bond ratings of AAA

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credit rating - an assessment of a borrower's creditworthiness in general
