old-fashioned look

1. NOUN

/ oʊldˈfæʃənd lʊk / (AmE), / əʊldˈfæʃənd lʊk / (BrE)
a style or appearance typical of a past era; reminiscent of earlier fashions or trends
style typical of a past era

Examples

The living room was decorated with an old-fashioned look that evoked memories of the 1920s.
He enjoys giving his photographs an old-fashioned look using special filters.
The bakery's interior has an old-fashioned look that customers find inviting.

Synset Examples

Her dress had an old-fashioned look that was charming.
He appreciates the old-fashioned look of vintage cars.
She prefers an old-fashioned look when decorating her home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vintage - relating to something from or characteristic of a previous era, especially in style or appearance
  • Antonym: modern look - a style or appearance characteristic of the current era
