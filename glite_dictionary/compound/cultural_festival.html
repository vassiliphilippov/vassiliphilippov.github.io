cultural festival

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
an event organized to celebrate the traditions, arts, and customs of a particular community or culture
event celebrating culture or traditions

Examples

Every summer, the city hosts a cultural festival featuring music, dance, and traditional crafts.
The cultural festival attracted artists and performers from various countries.
Schools often organize a cultural festival to teach students about different customs and heritages.

Synset Examples

Many tourists flock to Klanada for its cultural festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festival - an organized series of performances or events
