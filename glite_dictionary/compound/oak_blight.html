oak blight

1. NOUN

/ oʊk blaɪt / (AmE), / əʊk blaɪt / (BrE)
a black plant louse that infests oak and dogwood trees
plant louse on oaks and dogwoods

Examples

The leaves of the old oak tree were covered with oak blight after a warm, wet spring.
Several dogwoods in the park died last year due to an outbreak of oak blight.
Gardeners sprayed the trees to protect them from oak blight infestations.
