Samoyedic-speaking

1. ADJ

/ sæˌmɔɪˈɛdɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / sæˌmɔɪˈɛdɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in or using a Samoyedic language.
communicating in Samoyedic language(s)

Examples

The Samoyedic-speaking peoples inhabit regions of northern Siberia.
Linguists documented several endangered Samoyedic-speaking communities during their field research.
He works with Samoyedic-speaking elders to preserve their oral traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian-speaking - able to communicate in Russian
  • Synonym: Finnic-speaking - able to communicate in a Finnic language

Etymology

Samoyedic refers to a group of Uralic languages spoken in Siberia; formed by combining 'Samoyedic' with 'speaking'.
