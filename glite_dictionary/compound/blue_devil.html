blue devil

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
A coarse, prickly European weed with spikes of blue flowers, now naturalized in the United States.
a weed with blue flowers

Examples

The blue devil has spread to many fields across the Midwest.
Gardeners struggle to remove blue devil due to its tough, prickly stems.
Clusters of blue devil appear every summer, adding color to the landscape.

2. NOUN

/ bluː ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
a common name for the sodium salt form of amobarbital, used medically as a barbiturate sedative and hypnotic
barbiturate sedative (amobarbital sodium)
medical

Examples

The patient was administered a blue devil to help induce sleep before the procedure.
Some people abused blue devils for their sedative effects in the mid-20th century.
Doctors seldom prescribe blue devils today due to the risk of addiction.
