edible fruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl frut / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl̩ fruːt / (BrE)
A fruit that is suitable and safe for eating, typically one with sweet flesh.
fruit suitable for eating

Examples

A banana is a common edible fruit enjoyed around the world.
Many wild plants bear edible fruit that is harvested for food.
The market offers a wide variety of edible fruit, including apples, berries, and melons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit - the usually sweet and edible reproductive body of a seed plant
