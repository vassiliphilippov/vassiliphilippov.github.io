hyssop loosestrife

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪsəp ˈluːsˌstraɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɪsəp ˈluːsˌtraɪf / (BrE)
An annual plant with small solitary pink flowers, native to Europe and now widespread in moist areas.
