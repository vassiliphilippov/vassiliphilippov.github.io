artillery unit

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪl.ɚ.i ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪl.ə.ri ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a military group equipped and trained to operate large, heavy guns
military group using heavy guns

Examples

The commanding officer inspected the artillery unit before their deployment to the front lines.
An artillery unit provided crucial support during the battle by shelling enemy positions.
Modern technology has greatly improved the efficiency of every artillery unit in the army.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: infantry unit - an army unit composed of foot soldiers
  • Degree Lower: artillery battery - a subdivision of an artillery unit with several guns
  • Degree Higher: artillery regiment - a larger formation comprising multiple artillery units
