order Mycelia Sterilia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər mɪˈsiːliə stɪˈrɪliə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə mɪˈsiːlɪə stɪˈrɪlɪə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of imperfect fungi characterized by the absence of a known spore stage
order of imperfect fungi without a known spore stage

Examples

Many species classified under order Mycelia Sterilia are crucial for studying fungal biodiversity.
Because order Mycelia Sterilia lacks a known spore stage, identification relies heavily on mycelial characteristics.
Research into order Mycelia Sterilia has advanced our understanding of asexual fungal reproduction.

Etymology

From New Latin, combining 'Mycelia' (plural of mycelium, referring to fungus filaments) and 'Sterilia' (sterile, without spores).
