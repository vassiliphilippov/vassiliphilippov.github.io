order Juglandales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər dʒʌɡˌlænˈdeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə dʒʌɡˌlanˈdeɪliːz / (BrE)
A former order of flowering plants comprising the family Juglandaceae, including walnuts and hickories.
a plant order including walnuts/hickories

Examples

The order Juglandales contains nut-bearing trees such as walnuts and hickories.
Many botanists now classify species from the order Juglandales within other taxonomic groups.
The classification of the order Juglandales has changed with advances in genetic research.

Etymology

From Latin *Juglans* (walnut) and -ales (denoting plant orders).
