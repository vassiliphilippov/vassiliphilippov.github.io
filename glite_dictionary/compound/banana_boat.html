banana boat

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə boʊt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə bəʊt / (BrE)
a ship specially designed or used for transporting bananas from tropical regions
ship for transporting bananas

Examples

The banana boat arrived at the port carrying its load of fresh fruit.
Old banana boats were a common sight in Caribbean harbors during harvest season.
He worked as an engineer on a banana boat plying the route between Jamaica and the United States.

2. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˌboʊt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə bəʊt / (BrE)
a long, inflatable recreational boat shaped like a banana, usually pulled by a speedboat and ridden by several people for fun
inflatable recreational boat towed by a speedboat

Examples

The children squealed with delight as the banana boat bounced over the waves.
We booked a banana boat ride during our trip to the seaside resort.
Wearing life jackets, the group climbed onto the banana boat for an exciting adventure.
