cookie sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊki ʃit / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ʃiːt / (BrE)
a flat, rectangular metal tray used for baking cookies or biscuits in an oven
flat tray for baking cookies or biscuits

Examples

After greasing the cookie sheet, he arranged the dough in neat rows for baking.
Place the cookies on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper for best results.
She pulled the warm, golden cookies off the cookie sheet with a spatula.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baking tray - a flat, rectangular or square tray for baking foods in an oven (British English)
  • Synonym: baking sheet - an alternative term for 'cookie sheet'
