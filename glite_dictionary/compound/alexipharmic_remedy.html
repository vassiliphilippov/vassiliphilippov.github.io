alexipharmic remedy

1. NOUN

/ əˌlɛksɪˈfɑrmɪk ˈrɛmədi / (AmE), / əˌlɛksɪˈfɑːmɪk ˈrɛmɪdi / (BrE)
a remedy or treatment that counteracts poison or relieves anxiety
remedy that counteracts poison or anxiety
medical

Examples

The physician administered an alexipharmic remedy to neutralize the effects of the toxin.
She relied on an herbal alexipharmic remedy to alleviate her persistent anxiety.
Throughout history, people have sought an alexipharmic remedy for venoms and harmful substances.

Synset Examples

She always kept an alexipharmic remedy for her anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antidote - a medicine taken to counteract a particular poison

Etymology

From Greek alexipharmakon, from alexein (to ward off) + pharmakon (poison, drug).
