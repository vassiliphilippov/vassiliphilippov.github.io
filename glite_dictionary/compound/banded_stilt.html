banded stilt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændɪd stɪlt / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd stɪlt / (BrE)
a species of web-footed Australian wading bird with reddish-brown markings across the chest
Australian wading bird with chest band

Examples

Large flocks of banded stilt can sometimes be seen at salt lakes during the wet season.
The distinctive reddish chest band makes the banded stilt easy to identify among other waders.
Conservationists monitor banded stilt populations as an indicator of the health of inland wetlands.
