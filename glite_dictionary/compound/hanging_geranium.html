hanging geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋɪŋ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈhæŋɪŋ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a commonly cultivated South American plant with trailing stems, rounded leaves, and rosy flowers, often grown in hanging baskets
trailing plant with rosy flowers for hanging baskets

Examples

The hanging geranium added a splash of color to the porch throughout the summer.
She chose a vibrant hanging geranium for the garden show display.
Bright pink hanging geraniums cascaded down from the window boxes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pelargonium - genus of flowering plants including geraniums
