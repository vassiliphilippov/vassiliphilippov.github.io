Guiana Shield

1. PROPN

/ giˈænə ʃiːld / (AmE), / giˈɑːnə ʃiːld / (BrE)
a large, ancient geological plateau in northern South America, extending from Venezuela through Guyana to northern Brazil
mountainous plateau in northern South America

Examples

The Guiana Shield is home to some of the world's oldest rock formations.
Scientists study biodiversity in the Guiana Shield to better understand rainforest ecosystems.
The Guiana Shield stretches across several countries, including Venezuela and Brazil.
