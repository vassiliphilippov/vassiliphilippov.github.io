job application

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒɒb ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A formal request, usually written, made to an employer for employment.
formal request for employment

Examples

He filled out a job application for the open position at the company.
Your job application will be reviewed by the hiring manager next week.
Many graduates send out dozens of job applications before they get an interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: application - a formal request to be considered for a position or to do something
