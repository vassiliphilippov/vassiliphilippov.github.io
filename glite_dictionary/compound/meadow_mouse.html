meadow mouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ maʊs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ maʊs / (BrE)
A small rodent commonly found in grasslands of northern United States and Canada; also called a vole.
a small North American grassland rodent

Examples

We spotted a meadow mouse darting through the tall grass near the riverbank.
The field biologist studied the habits of the meadow mouse for her research on local wildlife.
Farmers often find meadow mice living in the edges of their hay fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vole - a small rodent related to the meadow mouse
