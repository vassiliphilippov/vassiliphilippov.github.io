air jacket

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
an inflatable life jacket designed to keep a person afloat in water
inflatable life jacket

Examples

The sailor quickly put on his air jacket before jumping into the sea.
Modern air jackets automatically inflate if they come into contact with water.
Wearing an air jacket can greatly increase your chances of survival in an accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life jacket - a jacket that keeps a person afloat in water
