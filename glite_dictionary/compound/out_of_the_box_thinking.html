out of the box thinking

1. NOUN

/ aʊt əv ðə bɑks ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / aʊt əv ðə bɒks ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice or ability of approaching problems in creative and unconventional ways
creative, unconventional problem-solving

Examples

The company encouraged out of the box thinking to develop innovative products.
Her out of the box thinking led to a breakthrough in the design process.
Many successful entrepreneurs are known for their out of the box thinking.

Synset Examples

We need out of the box thinking to solve this issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lateral thinking - solving problems through an indirect and creative approach
