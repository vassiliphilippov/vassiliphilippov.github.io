updog joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌp.dɔg dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈʌp.dɒg dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a humorous prank where someone tries to get another person to say 'What's updog?' so they can reply with a pun, as in 'Not much, what's up with you?'
a wordplay prank involving the phrase 'updog'
slang

Examples

He always tries to catch new employees with the classic updog joke during their first week.
My sister rolled her eyes when her friend used the updog joke at the dinner table.
After falling for the updog joke once, I never ask 'What's updog?' anymore.

Synset Examples

He tried to trick me with that updog joke again.
I can’t believe you fell for the updog prank!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deez nuts joke - a prank involving a set-up question leading to a punchline using the phrase 'deez nuts'
  • Synonym: practical joke - a trick played on someone to make them look foolish or amuse others

Etymology

From the phrase 'What's up, dog?' transformed into a pun involving 'updog'.
