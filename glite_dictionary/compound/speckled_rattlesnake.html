speckled rattlesnake

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛkəld ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈspɛkld ˈrætəlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
a species of rattlesnake (Crotalus mitchellii) native to southwestern Arizona and Baja California, typically patterned with markings that blend into the environment
species of rattlesnake with blended markings

Examples

The speckled rattlesnake is well camouflaged against the desert rocks.
Visitors to southwestern Arizona should be cautious of the speckled rattlesnake.
Researchers tagged several speckled rattlesnakes to study their movement patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rattlesnake - a venomous American snake with a jointed tail rattle
