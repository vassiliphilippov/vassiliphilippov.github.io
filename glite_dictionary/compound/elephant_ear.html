elephant ear

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlɪfənt ɪr / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪfənt ɪə / (BrE)
a tropical plant of the genus Alocasia, known for its large, showy, heart-shaped leaves and boat-shaped spathe with reddish berries
tropical plant with large heart-shaped leaves

Examples

The elephant ear in my garden grew enormous leaves over the summer.
Many tropical gardens feature elephant ear as a striking ornamental plant.
After planting the elephant ear bulbs, she watered them thoroughly.

Etymology

Named for its large, ear-shaped leaves resembling those of an elephant.
