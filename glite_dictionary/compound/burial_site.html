burial site

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛriəl saɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːriəl saɪt / (BrE)
an area of land where dead bodies are buried; a place used for burials.
place where bodies are buried

Examples

Archaeologists discovered an ancient burial site deep within the forest.
The city protected the historic burial site from new construction projects.
Many families visit the burial site of their loved ones on special anniversaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cemetery - a large burial ground, especially one not associated with a church
  • Synonym: graveyard - a burial ground, often one that is beside a church
