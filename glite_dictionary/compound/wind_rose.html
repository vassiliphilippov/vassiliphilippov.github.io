wind rose

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd roʊz / (AmE), / wɪnd rəʊz / (BrE)
A diagram that shows the frequency and strength of winds blowing from different directions over a specific period at a particular location.
diagram showing wind directions and strengths

Examples

The meteorologist presented a wind rose to illustrate prevailing wind patterns at the airport.
A wind rose is often used in environmental studies to analyze air quality impacts.
Each wind rose shows not only the directions but also the strength and frequency of winds.
