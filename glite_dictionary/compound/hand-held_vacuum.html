hand-held vacuum

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌhɛld ˈvæk.juːm / (AmE), / ˈhænd.hɛld ˈvæk.juːm / (BrE)
a small, portable vacuum cleaner powered by mains electricity or rechargeable batteries, designed for cleaning minor messes
small portable vacuum cleaner

Examples

She quickly grabbed the hand-held vacuum to clean up the crumbs on the sofa.
A compact hand-held vacuum is perfect for cleaning car interiors and small spaces.
This new hand-held vacuum runs for thirty minutes on a single charge.

Synset Examples

Some battery-powered handheld vacuums are wet/dry rated; the appliance must be partially disassembled and cleaned after picking up wet materials, to avoid developing unpleasant odors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical appliance for cleaning floors and other surfaces by sucking up dust and dirt
