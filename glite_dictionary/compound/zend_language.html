Zend language

1. NOUN

/ zɛnd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / zɛnd ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an ancient Iranian language, historically used in Zoroastrian scripture and religious texts
ancient Iranian language of Zoroastrian texts

Examples

The Zend language is preserved mainly in the sacred texts of Zoroastrianism.
Scholars often study the Zend language to better understand ancient Persian culture and religion.
Many religious ceremonies were conducted in the Zend language centuries ago.

Etymology

From Persian ‘zand’, referring to commentary on Zoroastrian scriptures.
