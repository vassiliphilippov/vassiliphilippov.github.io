money printer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌni ˌprɪn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌprɪn.tər / (BrE)
an activity or source that generates easy or seemingly unlimited profit with little effort
a source of easy or unlimited profit
informal

Examples

During the cryptocurrency boom, mining rigs were seen as a money printer by many investors.
Some people think owning rental properties is like having a money printer in the basement.
His new app turned out to be a money printer, earning thousands every week.

Synset Examples

Investing in the stock market can feel like a money printer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cash cow - a business or investment that consistently generates substantial profits
