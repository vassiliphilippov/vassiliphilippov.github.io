bit by bit

1. ADV

/ bɪt baɪ bɪt / (AmE), / bɪt baɪ bɪt / (BrE)
gradually; a little at a time.
gradually, little by little

Examples

Over the years, she saved enough money bit by bit to buy her own house.
The old building was restored bit by bit until it looked new again.
He learned to play the guitar bit by bit, practicing every evening.

Synset Examples

the research structure has developed piecemeal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little by little - at a slow pace; gradually; by small degrees
  • Synonym: piecemeal - one piece at a time; gradually; not all at once
