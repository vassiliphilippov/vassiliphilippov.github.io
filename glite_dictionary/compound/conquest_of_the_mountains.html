conquest of the mountains

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːŋkwɛst əv ðə ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋkwɛst əv ðə ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
the act of successfully climbing or exploring mountains
successfully climbing or exploring mountains

Examples

Her conquest of the mountains inspired climbers around the world to follow in her footsteps.
The book details the team's dangerous conquest of the mountains in the Himalayas.
Celebrating his conquest of the mountains, he raised a flag at the summit.

Synset Examples

Her conquest of the mountains was a thrilling adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountaineering - the sport or activity of climbing mountains
