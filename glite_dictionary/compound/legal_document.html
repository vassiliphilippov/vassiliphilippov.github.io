legal document

1. NOUN

/ ˈliɡəl ˈdɑːkjumənt / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈdɒkjumənt / (BrE)
an official paper that records a legal agreement, grant of rights, or contractual relationship
paper recording legal agreement or rights
legal

Examples

You should keep a copy of every legal document related to your house.
The lawyer explained the terms written in the legal document before I signed it.
A will is an important legal document that outlines what happens to your assets after death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contract - a written or spoken agreement that is legally binding
  • Synonym: deed - a legal document regarding the ownership of property
