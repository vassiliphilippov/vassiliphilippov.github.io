celery blight

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛl.ə.ɹi blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.əri blaɪt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting celery, characterized by spots or lesions on the leaves
fungal leaf disease of celery

Examples

Celery blight can devastate crops if not treated promptly with fungicides.
Farmers check their fields regularly for early signs of celery blight.
The lab identified the lesions as symptoms of celery blight.
