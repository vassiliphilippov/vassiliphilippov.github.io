silver lime

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər laɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə laɪm / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to eastern Europe and Asia Minor, noted for its leaves with a white, downy underside; widely planted as an ornamental tree.
a large ornamental tree with white-backed leaves

Examples

The silver lime provides dense shade and is favored in many public parks.
We admired the silvery underside of the silver lime's leaves on our walk.
Silver lime trees are often chosen for city planting because of their ornamental foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lime tree - a type of deciduous tree of the genus Tilia
