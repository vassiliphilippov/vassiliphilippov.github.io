sweet white violet

1. NOUN

/ swit waɪt ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / swiːt waɪt ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
a low-growing plant of eastern North America with fragrant white flowers marked by purple veins
North American violet with fragrant white flowers

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with delicate sweet white violet blossoms in early spring.
She collected a bouquet of sweet white violet for the botanical class.
We spotted several sweet white violet plants growing along the shady creek bank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - any of various small plants of the genus Viola, with purple, blue, or white flowers
