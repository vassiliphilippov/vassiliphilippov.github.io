Columbia University

1. PROPN

/ kəˈlʌmbiə ˌjunəˈvɜrsəti / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbiə ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)
A prestigious private university located in New York City, known for its academic excellence and research.
university in New York City

Examples

She was accepted to Columbia University to study political science.
Many Nobel Prize winners have graduated from Columbia University.
The campus of Columbia University is located in Manhattan.

Etymology

Named after Christopher Columbus, with 'university' from Latin 'universitas'.
