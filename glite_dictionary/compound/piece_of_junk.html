piece of junk

1. NOUN

/ pis əv dʒʌŋk / (AmE), / piːs əv dʒʌŋk / (BrE)
an object that is considered worthless or of very poor quality
something worthless or very poor quality

Examples

I can't believe you paid so much for that piece of junk—it barely works.
After a few months, the phone started acting up and turned into a piece of junk.
She was disappointed that the so-called antique was really just a piece of junk.

Synset Examples

That old car is just a piece of junk.
I finally threw away that piece of junk from the garage.
He called the broken printer a piece of junk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - something of little or no value; rubbish
