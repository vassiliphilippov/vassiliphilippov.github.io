diplomatic building

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
a government building where diplomats live or work, such as an embassy or consulate
government building for diplomats

Examples

The foreign minister held a meeting at the diplomatic building downtown.
Extra security was provided around every diplomatic building during the summit.
They renovated the old mansion to serve as a new diplomatic building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embassy - official residence or offices of an ambassador
  • Synonym: consulate - official building or offices where a consul works
