fusion of flavors

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːʒən əv ˈfleɪvərz / (AmE), / ˈfjʊʒən əv ˈfleɪvəz / (BrE)
a combination of distinct culinary tastes or traditions blended together in food or drink
blend of different culinary tastes

Examples

The chef impressed the guests with his signature fusion of flavors in every dish.
This restaurant is known for its unique fusion of flavors from Asia and Latin America.
The fusion of flavors in this dessert makes it truly unforgettable.

Synset Examples

The food in Gringolandia is a delightful fusion of flavors.
The restaurant offers a fusion of Indish flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fusion cuisine - style of cooking combining elements from different culinary traditions
