to dress ship

1. VERB

/ drɛs ʃɪp / (AmE), / drɛs ʃɪp / (BrE)
to decorate a ship with flags for a ceremonial or festive occasion
decorate a ship with flags

Examples

The crew worked all morning to dress ship for the admiral's arrival.
Every year during the festival, the harbor is filled with vessels that dress ship in colorful flags.
Before the parade, the captain ordered his men to dress ship as a mark of respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dress - to decorate or adorn; to put clothes on
