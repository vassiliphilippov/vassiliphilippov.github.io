window dresser

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ˌdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌdrɛsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange and decorate shop window displays to attract customers
person who designs shop window displays

Examples

The window dresser spent hours creating a stylish seasonal display for the department store.
Our boutique hired a talented window dresser to attract more foot traffic with creative designs.
As a window dresser, she loves experimenting with colors and themes for each new collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display designer - a person who designs visual displays for shops or exhibitions
