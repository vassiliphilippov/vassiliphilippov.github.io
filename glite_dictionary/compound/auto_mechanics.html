auto mechanics

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːtoʊ məˈkænɪks / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ mɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
the craft, skill, or profession of building and repairing automobiles
craft of repairing automobiles

Examples

She decided to study auto mechanics at the local vocational college.
The school offers courses in auto mechanics for aspiring technicians.
His interest in auto mechanics started when he helped his father fix their old truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile repair - the process or business of fixing cars
