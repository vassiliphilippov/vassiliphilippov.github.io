stone-dead

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn dɛd / (AmE), / stəʊn dɛd / (BrE)
Completely dead; having no life at all.
completely dead

Examples

The bird lay stone-dead after flying into the window.
By the time the doctor arrived, the patient was stone-dead.
He checked the battery, but it was stone-dead and wouldn't power anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead - no longer alive

Etymology

From the combination of 'stone' and 'dead', likening the absence of life to the inertness of a stone.
