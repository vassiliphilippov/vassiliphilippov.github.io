backroom girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌruːm ˌɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈbækˌruːm ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who serves as an expert adviser, working behind the scenes and involved in making important decisions without holding an official or public role.
female behind-the-scenes expert adviser

Examples

Despite her low profile, the backroom girl was responsible for many of the company's strategic moves.
The political party relied heavily on the talents of a skilled backroom girl for policy advice.
Many backroom girls in technology companies contribute crucial expertise without seeking public attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backroom boy - a man who serves as an expert adviser working behind the scenes
