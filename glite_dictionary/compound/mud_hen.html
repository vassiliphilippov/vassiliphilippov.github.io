mud hen

1. NOUN

/ mʌd hɛn / (AmE), / mʌd hɛn / (BrE)
A common name for the American coot, a medium-sized water bird found in North America, known for its dark plumage and lobed feet.
North American water bird (American coot)

Examples

We spotted a mud hen swimming across the pond early this morning.
The mud hen built its nest hidden among the reeds near the shore.
Birdwatchers often visit the lake to observe mud hens during migration season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coot - a medium-sized water bird with rounded body and lobed toes
