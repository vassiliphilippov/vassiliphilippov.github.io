United States Postal Inspection Service

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd steɪts ˈpoʊ.stəl ɪnˌspɛk.ʃən ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd steɪts ˈpəʊ.stəl ɪnˌspɛk.ʃən ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
the federal law enforcement agency responsible for protecting the U.S. Postal Service, its employees, and customers from crime and misuse of the mail
federal law enforcement agency for postal crimes
american

Examples

The United States Postal Inspection Service investigates mail fraud and other postal crimes across the country.
After receiving suspicious packages, the United States Postal Inspection Service launched an inquiry.
Members of the United States Postal Inspection Service work closely with other law enforcement agencies.
