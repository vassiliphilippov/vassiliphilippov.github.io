Chinese fried rice

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz fraɪd raɪs / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz fraɪd raɪs / (BrE)
a dish of boiled rice quickly stir-fried with scallions, eggs, and often minced pork or shrimp
stir-fried rice dish with eggs and meat

Examples

I ordered Chinese fried rice and sweet and sour chicken at the restaurant.
The chef taught us how to make authentic Chinese fried rice with shrimp.
He prefers Chinese fried rice with extra vegetables and no pork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fried rice - rice dish cooked by stir-frying with vegetables, eggs, and often meat
