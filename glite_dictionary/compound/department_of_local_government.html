department of local government

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrt.mənt əv ˈloʊ.kəl ˈɡʌv.ərn.mənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːt.mənt əv ˈləʊ.kəl ˈɡʌv.ən.mənt / (BrE)
a permanent administrative division established to carry out the functions and services of a local government
local government administrative department

Examples

The city council announced that the department of local government would oversee the new urban development project.
Funding for education is managed by a specialized department of local government in this municipality.
Each department of local government is responsible for providing services such as waste collection and road maintenance.
