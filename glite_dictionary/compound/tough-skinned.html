tough-skinned

1. ADJ

/ tʌf skɪnd / (AmE), / tʌf skɪnd / (BrE)
having a hard or tough outer covering; not easily penetrated or damaged
having a tough outer covering

Examples

Many reptiles are tough-skinned, making them less vulnerable to predators.
The tough-skinned fruit can withstand rough handling during transport.
She compared herself to a tough-skinned animal, able to endure harsh criticism without flinching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thick-skinned - not easily hurt physically or emotionally; having a thick skin
