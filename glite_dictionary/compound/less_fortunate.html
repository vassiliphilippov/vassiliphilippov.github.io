less fortunate

1. NOUN

/ lɛs ˈfɔɹ.tʃən.ət / (AmE), / lɛs ˈfɔː.tjʊ.nət / (BrE)
people who do not have as much money, advantages, or opportunities as others; the disadvantaged or poor.
people who are disadvantaged or poor

Examples

Many charities focus their efforts on helping the less fortunate in the community.
During the holidays, she volunteers to provide meals for the less fortunate.
Donations were collected to support the less fortunate after the disaster.

Synset Examples

They hope to find it in their hearts to help the less fortunate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: privileged - having more advantages, wealth, or opportunities
  • Synonym: underprivileged - not having the rights or advantages that most people have
  • Synonym: poor - lacking sufficient money to live comfortably
