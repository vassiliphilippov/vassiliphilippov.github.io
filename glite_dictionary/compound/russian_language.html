Russian language

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌʃən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Slavic language that is the official language of Russia and widely spoken in several other countries
Slavic language, official in Russia

Examples

She is fluent in Russian language after years of studying abroad.
The Russian language uses the Cyrillic alphabet instead of the Latin alphabet.
Many scientific papers have been published in the Russian language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russian - a native or inhabitant of Russia; also the language
