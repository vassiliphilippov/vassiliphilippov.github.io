tobacco pouch

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ paʊtʃ / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a small bag used for carrying pipe tobacco
small bag for carrying tobacco

Examples

He reached into his pocket for his tobacco pouch and began to fill his pipe.
The old leather tobacco pouch was well-worn from years of use.
Collectors sometimes seek out antique tobacco pouches for their unique designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pouch - a small bag or sack
