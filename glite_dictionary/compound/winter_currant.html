winter currant

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntər ˈkɜrənt / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a North American flowering shrub (Ribes sanguineum) with clusters of red or pink flowers, also called flowering currant or red-flowering currant
a flowering shrub (Ribes sanguineum)

Examples

The winter currant produces bright pink flowers early each spring.
Gardeners often plant winter currant to attract hummingbirds and bees.
In mild climates, the winter currant can provide color when few other shrubs are blooming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowering currant - another common name for Ribes sanguineum
  • Synonym: red-flowering currant - alternative common name for Ribes sanguineum
