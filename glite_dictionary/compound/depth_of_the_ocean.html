depth of the ocean

1. NOUN

/ dɛpθ əv ði ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / dɛpθ əv ði ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the deepest parts or regions of the ocean, far below the surface
deepest parts of the ocean

Examples

Scientists use special equipment to explore the depth of the ocean.
Many mysterious creatures inhabit the depth of the ocean where sunlight cannot reach.
The pressure increases dramatically as you descend into the depth of the ocean.

Synset Examples

The depths of the ocean remain unfathomable to scientists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abyss - a bottomless or extremely deep chasm, especially in the ocean
