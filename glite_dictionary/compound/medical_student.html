medical student

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
A student who is enrolled in a medical school, training to become a doctor.
student in medical school

Examples

The medical student spent long hours studying anatomy for her upcoming exam.
During their third year, every medical student completes clinical rotations in various hospital departments.
He became interested in global health issues while he was a medical student.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: intern - A recent medical graduate gaining supervised practical experience
