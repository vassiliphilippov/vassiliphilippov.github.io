cavalry sword

1. NOUN

/ ˈkævəlri sɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkævəlri sɔːd / (BrE)
A stout sword with a curved blade and thick back, used by cavalry soldiers.
sword used by cavalry, curved blade

Examples

The officer drew his cavalry sword as the charge began.
Many museums display ornate cavalry swords from the 19th century.
He practiced his fencing technique with an antique cavalry sword.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saber - a sword with a curved, single-edged blade
