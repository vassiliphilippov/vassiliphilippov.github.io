ugly ass

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌɡli æs / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli as / (BrE)
Extremely ugly; very unattractive, used as a vulgar or slang intensifier.
vulgar slang for 'very ugly'
slang vulgar

Examples

He wore an ugly ass sweater to the party and everyone laughed.
That is one ugly ass painting on your wall.
She called his haircut ugly ass, but he didn't care.
