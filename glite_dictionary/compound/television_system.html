television system

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A telecommunication system used for transmitting images and sound of stationary or moving objects between distant points, enabling visual broadcasting over distance.
system for transmitting visual images and sound over distance

Examples

The new television system provides high-definition channels for a better viewing experience.
Engineers upgraded the television system to transmit live sporting events from remote locations.
Every household in the city was connected to the national television system by the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcasting - the transmission of programs or information by radio or television
