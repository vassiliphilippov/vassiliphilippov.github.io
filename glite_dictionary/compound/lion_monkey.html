lion monkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən ˌmʌŋ.ki / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən ˌmʌŋ.ki / (BrE)
a small South American marmoset with silky fur and a long nonprehensile tail, known for its mane-like appearance
South American marmoset with silky fur and long tail

Examples

The lion monkey is easily recognized by its golden mane and expressive face.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered lion monkey.
A group of lion monkeys was spotted swinging through the rainforest canopy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamarin - small South American monkeys related to marmosets
