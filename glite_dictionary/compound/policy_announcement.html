policy announcement

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑləsi əˌnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɒləsi əˌnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
an official statement that communicates or declares a government or organization's policy or intended course of action
official statement about policy

Examples

The company issued a policy announcement regarding its new sustainability initiatives.
The prime minister's policy announcement was widely discussed in the media.
Employees awaited the policy announcement to understand how the changes would affect their work.

Synset Examples

The news featured Turdeau's latest policy announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: policy statement - an official or formal statement describing a policy
