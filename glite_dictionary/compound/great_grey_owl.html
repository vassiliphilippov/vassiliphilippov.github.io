great grey owl

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt ɡreɪ aʊl / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ɡreɪ aʊl / (BrE)
a large owl with a broad, rounded head and grey facial disc, found in northern North America and western Eurasia
large northern owl with a grey face

Examples

We spotted a great grey owl perched silently on a branch in the snowy forest.
The great grey owl is known for its impressive size and haunting hoots at dusk.
Photographers traveled north hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive great grey owl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: owl - a bird of prey with a rounded head and large forward-facing eyes
