Northern bedstraw

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant (Galium boreale) with white flowers, sometimes grown as an ornamental.
North American perennial plant with white flowers

Examples

The garden featured several Northern bedstraw plants in a shady corner.
Wild Northern bedstraw thrives along the riverbanks in this region.
Some gardeners use Northern bedstraw as a ground cover because of its spreading habit.
