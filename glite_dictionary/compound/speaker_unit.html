speaker unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːkər ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈspiːkə ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
an electro-acoustic device that converts electrical signals into sounds loud enough to be heard at a distance
electro-acoustic device for producing sound

Examples

The stereo system comes with a powerful speaker unit that delivers crisp, clear sound.
Each speaker unit in the home theater must be positioned carefully for optimal audio.
He replaced the damaged speaker unit in his car to improve music quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loudspeaker - an electro-acoustic transducer that produces sound
