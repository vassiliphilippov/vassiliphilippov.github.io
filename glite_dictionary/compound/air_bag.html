air bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a safety device in a vehicle that rapidly inflates with air during a collision to cushion and protect passengers from injury
vehicle safety device that inflates on impact

Examples

The air bag deployed instantly when the car crashed into the barrier.
Modern cars usually come equipped with both front and side air bags for added protection.
He credited the air bag with saving his life during the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seat belt - a belt used to secure a person in a vehicle seat for safety
