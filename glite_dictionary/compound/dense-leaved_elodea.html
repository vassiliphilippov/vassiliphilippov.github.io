dense-leaved elodea

1. NOUN

/ dɛns livd ɪˈloʊdiə / (AmE), / dɛns liːvd ɪˈləʊdiə / (BrE)
an aquatic plant with deep green, dense foliage, often used to oxygenate aquariums; sometimes classified in the genus Egeria
aquatic plant with dense green leaves

Examples

Many aquarium enthusiasts recommend adding dense-leaved elodea to improve water oxygen levels.
The deep green foliage of dense-leaved elodea makes it a popular choice among aquatic plant keepers.
He decorated his fish tank with strands of dense-leaved elodea for both beauty and function.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elodea - genus of aquatic plants known as waterweeds
  • Synonym: egeria - genus sometimes containing dense-leaved elodea
