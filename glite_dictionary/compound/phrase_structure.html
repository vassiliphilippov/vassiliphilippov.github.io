phrase structure

1. NOUN

/ freɪz ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / freɪz ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
the grammatical arrangement of words and groups of words within a sentence, forming larger structural units
sentence's grammatical arrangement of words

Examples

Understanding phrase structure is crucial for analyzing complex sentences in linguistics.
The phrase structure of this sentence follows typical English grammatical rules.
Children gradually learn the phrase structure of their native language as they grow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: syntax - the arrangement of words and phrases to form sentences
