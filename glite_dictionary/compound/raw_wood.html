raw wood

1. NOUN

/ rɔ wʊd / (AmE), / rɔː wʊd / (BrE)
wood that has not been processed, finished, or painted
unfinished, unpainted wood

Examples

The builder ordered a shipment of raw wood for the new project.
It is important to sand raw wood before applying any paint or varnish.
The shelf was made from raw wood and still had a rough surface.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: finished wood - wood that has been sanded, stained, or painted
