low and slow

1. ADV

/ loʊ ənd sloʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ ənd sləʊ / (BrE)
(of cooking) done at a low temperature over a long period to enhance tenderness or flavor.
cooking at low temperature for a long time

Examples

For the perfect pulled pork, you need to cook it low and slow for several hours.
Many stews turn out best when left to simmer low and slow all afternoon.
She recommends roasting the brisket low and slow to ensure the meat stays tender.

Synset Examples

He prefers to cook his meats low and slow for the best flavor.
They grilled the vegetables low and slow for added sweetness.
The ribs cooked low and slow for perfect tenderness.
