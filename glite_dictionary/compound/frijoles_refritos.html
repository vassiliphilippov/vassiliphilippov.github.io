frijoles refritos

1. NOUN

/ friˈxoʊleɪz rɛˈfritoʊz / (AmE), / friˈxəʊleɪz rɛˈfriːtəʊz / (BrE)
A Mexican dish of cooked, mashed beans that are fried, usually with lard and seasonings.
Mexican fried mashed beans dish

Examples

I always order frijoles refritos as a side when I eat at my favorite Mexican restaurant.
The recipe calls for pinto beans to make authentic frijoles refritos.
He spread a layer of frijoles refritos on the tortilla before adding cheese.

Etymology

From Spanish 'frijoles' (beans) + 'refritos' (well-fried, refried).
