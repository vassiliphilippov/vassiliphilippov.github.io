dark-eyed junco

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑrkˌaɪd ˈdʒʌŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkˌaɪd ˈdʒʌŋkəʊ / (BrE)
a common North American songbird of the sparrow family, noted for its greyish plumage and dark eyes, scientifically known as Junco hyemalis.
North American songbird with grey plumage and dark eyes

Examples

A flock of dark-eyed juncos visited our backyard during the winter months.
Birdwatchers often spot the dark-eyed junco foraging on the forest floor.
The distinctive markings of the dark-eyed junco make it easy to identify among other sparrows.
