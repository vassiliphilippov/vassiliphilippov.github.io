omental bursa

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈmɛntəl ˈbɜrsə / (AmE), / əʊˈmɛntəl ˈbɜːsə / (BrE)
An isolated compartment of the peritoneal cavity located behind the stomach.
cavity behind the stomach (anatomy)
medical

Examples

During surgery, the omental bursa provides access to the pancreas and posterior stomach wall.
Fluid accumulation in the omental bursa can be detected with abdominal imaging techniques.
The doctor explained that the infection had spread to the omental bursa after the initial diagnosis.

Etymology

From Latin 'omentum' (a fold of peritoneum) and 'bursa' (pouch, sac).
