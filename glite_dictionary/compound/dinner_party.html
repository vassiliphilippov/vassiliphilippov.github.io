dinner party

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering at which guests are invited to have dinner together
social event involving dinner with guests

Examples

We're hosting a dinner party for eight friends this Saturday evening.
At the dinner party, everyone complimented the chef on her delicious food.
They met for the first time at a mutual friend's dinner party.

Synset Examples

guests should never be late to a dinner party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
A social gathering at which a meal, usually an elaborate one, is served to guests during the evening.
social event with an evening meal

Examples

We're hosting a dinner party for twelve guests next Saturday.
He met his future business partner at a formal dinner party.
The conversation at the dinner party ranged from politics to art.

Synset Examples

She presented a delicate niglette at the dinner party.
