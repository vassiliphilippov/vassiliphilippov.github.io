first name

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst ˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌneɪm / (BrE)
the name given to someone at birth or baptism and used before a family name; a given name
given name; name before surname

Examples

He introduced himself by his first name only.
Please write your first name in the box.
Her first name is Emily, but everyone calls her Em.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surname - family name; name shared with other family members
  • Synonym: given name - forename; name given at birth
  • Synonym: forename - name before the surname
