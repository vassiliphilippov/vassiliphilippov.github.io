stone-sober

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn ˈsoʊbər / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
completely sober; not affected by alcohol at all.
completely sober

Examples

Despite the wild party, he stayed stone-sober the entire night.
She was stone-sober when she delivered her testimony.
After months of drinking, he decided to remain stone-sober for the sake of his health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sober - not affected by alcohol

Etymology

Formed by combining 'stone-' (as intensifier) with 'sober'.
