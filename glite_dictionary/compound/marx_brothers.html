Marx Brothers

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrks ˌbrʌðərz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːks ˌbrʌðəz / (BrE)
a family of American comedians, known for their anarchic and influential humor in vaudeville, film, and stage from the early to mid-20th century
iconic American comedy family group

Examples

Many consider the Marx Brothers to be pioneers of American slapstick comedy.
The films of the Marx Brothers are still popular with audiences today.
