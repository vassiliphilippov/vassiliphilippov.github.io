school newspaper

1. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / skuːl ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a newspaper produced, written, and published by the students of a school, often about school news and events
student-produced school publication

Examples

She writes articles for the school newspaper every week.
The school newspaper covered the recent football championship in detail.
Our school newspaper won a prize for best student publication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student newspaper - a newspaper written and published by students, often at universities or colleges

2. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈnuːzˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / skuːl ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a newspaper or periodical published by students of a school, usually reporting on school events, activities, and issues relevant to the student body
student-produced publication for a school

Examples

She was the editor of the school newspaper during her senior year.
The article about the science fair appeared in the latest edition of the school newspaper.
Several students submitted poems to be published in the school newspaper.

Synset Examples

He wrote a brilliant piece for the school newspaper.

Related Terms
