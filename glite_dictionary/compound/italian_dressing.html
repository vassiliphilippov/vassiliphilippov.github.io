Italian dressing

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliən ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A vinaigrette-style salad dressing flavored with garlic and herbs such as oregano, basil, and dill.
herbed vinaigrette salad dressing

Examples

She prefers her salads topped with Italian dressing over creamy options.
The restaurant offers house-made Italian dressing with fresh herbs.
I poured some Italian dressing over the vegetables before roasting them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinaigrette - a mixture of oil, vinegar, and seasonings used as a salad dressing; synonym
