peace and understanding

1. NOUN

/ pis ənd ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / piːs ənd ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a state or desire in which people live in harmony and have mutual comprehension or clarity of intention
harmony and mutual comprehension

Examples

After years of conflict, the two communities now strive for peace and understanding.
Her speech emphasized the importance of building peace and understanding among different cultures.
Many international organizations aim to promote peace and understanding throughout the world.

Synset Examples

They yearn for peace and understanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmony - agreement or peaceful coexistence
  • Antonym: discord - disagreement or lack of harmony
