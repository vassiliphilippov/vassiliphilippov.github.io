omnidirectional radio range

1. NOUN

/ ɑm.nɪ.daɪˈrɛk.ʃə.nəl ˈreɪ.di.oʊ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɒm.nɪ.dɪˈrɛk.ʃə.nəl ˈreɪ.di.əʊ reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a system of radio beacons used in aviation, providing aircraft with precise information about their position and bearing
aviation system for aircraft navigation

Examples

Pilots rely on the omnidirectional radio range system for accurate en route navigation.
The airport's omnidirectional radio range was upgraded to enhance flight safety.
Her research focused on the history and development of omnidirectional radio range technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: VOR - abbreviation for very high frequency omnidirectional radio range, the most common implementation of the system
  • Degree Lower: non-directional beacon - a type of radio navigation beacon providing limited navigational information
