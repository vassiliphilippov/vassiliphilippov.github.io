million instructions per second

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪljən ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz pər ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈmɪljən ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz pə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
a unit for measuring the processing speed of a computer's CPU, equal to one million instructions executed per second
unit of computer CPU processing speed

Examples

The old computer could only process 2 million instructions per second at peak performance.
Modern CPUs often achieve speeds of several hundred million instructions per second.
A benchmark showed that the processor could handle 50 million instructions per second.

Synset Examples

4 MIPS is 4,000,000 instructions per second

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MIPS - abbreviation for million instructions per second, a unit of CPU speed
