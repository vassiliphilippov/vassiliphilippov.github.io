devil-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
the practice of worshipping or showing reverence to devils or evil spirits, often through rituals or ceremonies
ritual worship of devils or evil spirits

Examples

Many believed that the remote village was a center for devil-worship and other occult practices.
The novel explores the dark consequences of devil-worship among a group of misguided youths.
Reports of devil-worship in the abandoned church caused widespread fear in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satanism - the worship of Satan; similar practice to devil-worship
