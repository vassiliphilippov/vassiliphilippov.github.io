ice show

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ʃoʊ / (AmE), / aɪs ʃəʊ / (BrE)
an entertainment performance in which ice skaters put on routines, often choreographed to music and featuring costumes or storytelling
ice skating entertainment performance

Examples

The family enjoyed the spectacular ice show at the arena last weekend.
He bought tickets for the new Disney ice show featuring famous cartoon characters.
Professional skaters train for months before participating in an international ice show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skating - the sport or activity of moving on ice using skates
  • Synonym: figure skating - the sport of performing spins, jumps, and dance on ice skates, usually as a competition
