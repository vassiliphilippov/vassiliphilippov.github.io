electrical device

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A device that produces, uses, or is powered by electricity.
device using or powered by electricity

Examples

Please unplug any electrical device before cleaning it with water.
The museum exhibits several early electrical devices, including the first light bulb.
It's important to use a surge protector when connecting a new electrical device to the power outlet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electronic device - device that operates using electronic circuits
