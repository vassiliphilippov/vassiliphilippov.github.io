brick facade

1. NOUN

/ brɪk fəˈseɪd / (AmE), / brɪk fəˈsɑːd / (BrE)
the exterior front of a building that is covered or constructed with bricks
front of a building made of bricks

Examples

The new restaurant stands out thanks to its bright brick facade.
Years of weathering had given the old brick facade a charming, rustic look.
The museum preserved the historic brick facade while updating the building's interior.

Synset Examples

The architect designed the house with a classic brick facade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facade - the front of a building
