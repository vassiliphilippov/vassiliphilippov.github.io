liquid ecstasy

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd ˈɛkstəsi / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈɛkstəsi / (BrE)
A street name for gamma hydroxybutyrate (GHB), a drug often used recreationally for its euphoric and sedative effects.
street name for the drug GHB
informal

Examples

He was arrested for possession of liquid ecstasy at the nightclub.
Some people mistakenly believe that liquid ecstasy is safer than other recreational drugs.
Authorities warn about the risks associated with the use of liquid ecstasy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: GHB - gamma hydroxybutyrate, a central nervous system depressant drug
