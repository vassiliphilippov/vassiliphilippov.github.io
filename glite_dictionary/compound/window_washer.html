window washer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ˌwɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌwɒʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean the glass surfaces of windows, especially on buildings
person who cleans windows as a job

Examples

The window washer climbed up the tall building to clean the glass panels.
Every morning, the window washer arrives to keep the office windows spotless.
A professional window washer uses special tools for safety and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaner - a person whose job is to clean, especially in buildings
