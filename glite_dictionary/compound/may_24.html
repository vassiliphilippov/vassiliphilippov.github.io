May 24

1. NOUN

/ meɪ twɛnti fɔr / (AmE), / meɪ ˈtwɛnti ˈfɔː / (BrE)
(in Britain, historical) the anniversary of Queen Victoria's birth, celebrated on the 24th of May.
anniversary of Queen Victoria's birth (Britain)
british

Examples

Celebrations on May 24 were an important part of Victorian Britain.
In Canada, May 24 is still observed as Victoria Day, marking the queen's birthday.
People used to hold parades and parties on May 24 to honor Queen Victoria.
