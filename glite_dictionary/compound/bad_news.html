bad news

1. NOUN

/ bæd nuːz / (AmE), / bæd njuːz / (BrE)
unpleasant or unwelcome information, typically news that brings disappointment or distress
unpleasant or unwelcome information

Examples

She dreaded telling her family the bad news about the accident.
We received some bad news from the doctor this morning.
No one likes to be the bearer of bad news.

Synset Examples

She was the messenger who brought bad news.

  • Antonym: good news - pleasant or welcome information
