Christian holy day

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstʃən ˈhoʊli deɪ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən ˈhəʊli deɪ / (BrE)
a religious holiday observed by Christians, such as Christmas or Easter
religious holiday for Christians

Examples

Easter is one of the most important Christian holy days celebrated around the world.
Many schools close for major Christian holy days such as Christmas.
She attends church regularly, especially on significant Christian holy days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christian holiday - a festival or day of religious celebration for Christians
  • Degree Lower: holiday - a day set aside for celebration or commemoration
