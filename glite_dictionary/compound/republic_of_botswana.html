Republic of Botswana

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv bɑtˈswɑnə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv bɒtˈswɑːnə / (BrE)
a landlocked country in south-central Africa, which gained independence from Britain in 1966; official name of Botswana
country in south-central Africa

Examples

The Republic of Botswana is known for its stable democracy and thriving wildlife tourism.
After gaining independence in 1966, the Republic of Botswana rapidly developed its mining industry.
Gaborone is the capital city of the Republic of Botswana.
