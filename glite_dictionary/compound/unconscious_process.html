unconscious process

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈkɑn.ʃəs ˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒn.ʃəs ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a mental process that takes place without one's direct awareness or conscious control
mental process outside conscious awareness

Examples

Much of human behavior is guided by unconscious processes that we do not recognize.
Therapists often try to bring unconscious processes to the surface during psychoanalysis.
Dreams can be influenced by unconscious processes at work in the mind.

Synset Examples

the process of denial

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conscious process - a mental process you are directly aware of
