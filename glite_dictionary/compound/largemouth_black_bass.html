largemouth black bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌmaʊθ blæk bæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌmaʊθ blæk bæs / (BrE)
a large species of black bass (Micropterus salmoides) native to North America, known for its wide mouth and popular among anglers
a large North American black bass species

Examples

The largemouth black bass is highly sought after by sport fishermen in North America.
With its wide jaws, the largemouth black bass can swallow prey nearly half its own size.
The pond was stocked with largemouth black bass to encourage recreational fishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: largemouth bass - a species of black bass with a large mouth
  • Antonym: smallmouth bass - a species of black bass with a smaller mouth
