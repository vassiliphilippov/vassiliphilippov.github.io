sound reproduction

1. NOUN

/ saʊnd ˌriˌprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / saʊnd ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process or quality of reproducing or replicating sound, especially by electronic or mechanical means
reproducing sound electronically or mechanically

Examples

The quality of sound reproduction greatly influences the listening experience on a stereo system.
Modern smartphones offer impressive sound reproduction for their compact size.
High-fidelity sound reproduction is essential for professional music studios.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audio reproduction - replication or playback of audio signals
