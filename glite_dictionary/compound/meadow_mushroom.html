meadow mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
a common edible mushroom (Agaricus campestris) found in grassy areas; also cultivated commercially for food
an edible mushroom found in fields and lawns

Examples

She picked a meadow mushroom from the grass for her salad.
The meadow mushroom is prized by chefs for its delicate flavor.
After the rain, clusters of meadow mushrooms appeared in the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field mushroom - another term for meadow mushroom
  • Degree Lower: button mushroom - the common cultivated variety of edible mushroom, related species
