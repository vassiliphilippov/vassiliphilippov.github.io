professional person

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənl ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is engaged in a profession that typically requires specialized education or training, such as law, medicine, or engineering
person in a learned profession

Examples

As a professional person, she must adhere to strict ethical standards in her work.
The clinic only accepts applications from qualified professional persons.
It can be challenging for a professional person to balance work with personal life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional - a person who is a member of a profession
