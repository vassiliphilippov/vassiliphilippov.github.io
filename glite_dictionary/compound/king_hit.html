king hit

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ hɪt / (AmE), / kɪŋ hɪt / (BrE)
A sudden, powerful blow, often delivered unexpectedly and capable of knocking someone out; also, a significant or decisive event.
sudden, powerful blow; decisive event
british

Examples

The boxer knocked out his opponent with a king hit in the third round.
The movie's plot delivered a king hit of emotion in the final scene.
He was suspended for delivering a dangerous king hit during the game.

Synset Examples

The movie's climax was a king hit of action.
That punch was a king hit, knocking him out.
He delivered a king hit that ended the match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punch - a blow with the fist
  • Synonym: blow - a powerful strike or hit

Etymology

Originally Australian English, 'king hit' refers to a 'king' sized (very powerful) strike.
