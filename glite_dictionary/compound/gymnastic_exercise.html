gymnastic exercise

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪmˈnæstɪk ˈɛksaɪz / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈnastɪk ˈɛksəsaɪz / (BrE)
A physical activity or movement, often performed on special apparatus, designed to develop or display strength, agility, and balance.
exercise for strength and agility in gymnastics

Examples

The coach demonstrated a challenging gymnastic exercise on the parallel bars.
Proper warm-up is essential before attempting any strenuous gymnastic exercise.
She mastered a new gymnastic exercise for the upcoming competition.
