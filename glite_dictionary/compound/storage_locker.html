storage locker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔrɪdʒ ˌlɑkər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˌlɒkə / (BrE)
a small compartment with a lock, used for storing personal belongings or valuables, typically in public places
compartment for storing belongings

Examples

He kept his gym bag in the storage locker while he exercised.
The train station offers a storage locker service for travelers with heavy luggage.
She opened her storage locker to retrieve her books before class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: locker - a small cupboard or compartment with a lock
