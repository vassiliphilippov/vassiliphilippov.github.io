Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria

1. PROPN

/ dəˌmɑkrətɪk ənd pəˈpjuːlər rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ælˈdʒɪəriə / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrətɪk ənd pəˈpjuːlə rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ælˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
the official name of Algeria, a country in northwestern Africa bordering the Mediterranean Sea, with a mainly Sunni Muslim population; achieved independence from France in the early 1960s
official name of Algeria

Examples

The official name of Algeria is the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria.
After gaining independence, the nation adopted the title Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria.
