meadow bright

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ braɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ braɪt / (BrE)
A swamp plant of Europe and North America with bright yellow flowers that resemble buttercups.
swamp plant with yellow flowers

Examples

A cluster of meadow bright bloomed by the edge of the stream, lighting up the wet ground.
The wetland restoration project reintroduced meadow bright to the local ecosystem.
Children picked meadow bright to put in their spring wildflower bouquets.
