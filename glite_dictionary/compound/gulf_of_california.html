Gulf of California

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡʌlf əv ˌkæləˈfɔrniə / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əv ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə / (BrE)
a long narrow gulf of the eastern Pacific Ocean separating the Baja California Peninsula from the mainland of northwestern Mexico
gulf between Baja California and Mexico

Examples

The Gulf of California is home to a rich diversity of marine life.
Many tourists visit the beaches along the Gulf of California for fishing and water sports.
Environmental groups are working to protect the ecosystem of the Gulf of California.
