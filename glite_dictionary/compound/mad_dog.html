mad dog

1. NOUN

/ mæd dɔg / (AmE), / mad dɒg / (BrE)
a person who acts in a wildly aggressive or irrational manner
person acting aggressively or irrationally

Examples

After the referee's decision, he charged onto the field like a mad dog.
Her temper flared, and she yelled at everyone as if she were a mad dog.
Critics described the dictator as ruling his country like a mad dog.

Synset Examples

He acted like a mad dog when provoked.
She was mad dog about her favorite sports team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - person who behaves in a wildly foolish or irrational manner
  • Synonym: hothead - person who quickly becomes angry or violent
