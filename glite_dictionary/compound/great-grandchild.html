great-grandchild

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡreɪt ˈɡræn(d)tʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈɡræn(d)tʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child of your grandson or granddaughter.
a child of one's grandchild

Examples

She was thrilled to meet her first great-grandchild at the family reunion.
He is a proud great-grandfather to five great-grandchildren.
Her great-grandchild brought her flowers on her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grandchild - a child of one's son or daughter
