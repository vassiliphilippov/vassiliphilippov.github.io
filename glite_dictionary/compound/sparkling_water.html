sparkling water

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑrklɪŋ ˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋ ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is carbonated, usually by the addition of carbon dioxide, producing a bubbly, effervescent beverage.
carbonated water; fizzy drink

Examples

Would you like sparkling water or still water with your meal?
He ordered a glass of sparkling water with lemon at the café.
Many people enjoy sparkling water as a refreshing alternative to sugary sodas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soda water - carbonated water, typically plain without flavoring
  • Antonym: still water - water without carbonation
