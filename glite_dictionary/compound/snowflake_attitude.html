snowflake attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊfleɪk ˈætəˌtud / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊfleɪk ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude or mindset of being overly sensitive to criticism or convinced of one's own uniqueness, often viewed negatively.
being overly sensitive or self-unique
informal slang

Examples

Her snowflake attitude made it difficult to have honest discussions with her.
Some critics accuse younger generations of having a snowflake attitude toward differing opinions.
He was quick to take offense, which many saw as a classic snowflake attitude.

Synset Examples

His snowflake attitude made teamwork challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowflake - a person who is overly sensitive or feels entitled to special treatment

Etymology

From 'snowflake' (slang for someone deemed overly sensitive or unique) and 'attitude'.
