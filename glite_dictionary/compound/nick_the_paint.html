to nick the paint

1. VERB

/ nɪk ðə peɪnt / (AmE), / nɪk ðə peɪnt / (BrE)
to scratch or make a small chip in a painted surface
scratch or chip painted surface

Examples

Try not to nick the paint while installing the new shelves.
She accidentally nicked the paint on the car door with her keys.
Even a small rock can nick the paint on your bicycle frame.

Synset Examples

Be careful not to nick the paint when moving the furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scratch - to scrape or damage the surface of something lightly
