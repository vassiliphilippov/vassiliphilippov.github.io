cattle thief

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæt̬l̩ θiːf / (AmE), / ˈkætl̩ θiːf / (BrE)
a person who steals cattle or other livestock
person who steals livestock

Examples

The ranch was plagued for years by a notorious cattle thief who was never caught.
In the Old West, a cattle thief could be sentenced to severe punishment if apprehended.
Modern technology has made it easier to catch a cattle thief before large numbers of livestock disappear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rustler - a person who steals livestock, especially cattle; synonym for 'cattle thief'
