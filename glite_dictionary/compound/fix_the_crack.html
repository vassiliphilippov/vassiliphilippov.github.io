to fix the crack

1. VERB

/ fɪks ðə kræk / (AmE), / fɪks ðə kræk / (BrE)
to repair a break or hole in a surface, making it whole or smooth again
repair a break or hole in a surface

Examples

After noticing water leaking, she decided to fix the crack in the pipe immediately.
The mechanic said it would only take a few minutes to fix the crack in the windshield.
A special filler can be used to fix the crack in the concrete sidewalk.

Synset Examples

He used plaster to fix the crack in the wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repair - to restore something that is broken or damaged to a good condition
