divergent thinker

1. NOUN

/ dəˈvɝːdʒənt ˈθɪŋkər / (AmE), / dɪˈvɜːdʒənt ˈθɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who approaches problems in an unconventional way, often generating innovative and original ideas or solutions
person who thinks creatively and innovatively

Examples

She is known as a divergent thinker who often suggests radically new approaches to old problems.
The company values employees who are divergent thinkers and bring fresh perspectives to projects.
As a divergent thinker, he excels at brainstorming many possible solutions before choosing the best one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creative thinker - a person who thinks in creative ways
  • Antonym: convergent thinker - a person who focuses on finding a single, correct solution to a problem
