backbone of

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌboʊn əv / (AmE), / ˈbækbəʊn ɒv / (BrE)
the most important support, foundation, or central element of something
main support or foundation

Examples

Education is the backbone of our nation's progress.
Respect is the backbone of a healthy team environment.
Scientific research forms the backbone of technological advancements.

Synset Examples

Trust is the backbone of any successful relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundation - the basis or groundwork of anything
  • Synonym: core - the central or most important part of something
  • Synonym: pillar - a fundamental principle or support
