comfort of home

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmfərt əv hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfət əv həʊm / (BrE)
the feeling of ease, convenience, and relaxation that one experiences while at home
ease and relaxation at home

Examples

She prefers working from the comfort of home rather than commuting to the office every day.
Online shopping allows you to buy groceries from the comfort of home.
Nothing compares to watching a movie with your family in the comfort of home.

Synset Examples

She attended a virtual conference from the comfort of home.
