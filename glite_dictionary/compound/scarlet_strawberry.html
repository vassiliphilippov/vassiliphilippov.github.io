scarlet strawberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑr.lət ˈstrɔːˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt ˈstrɒˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
A North American wild strawberry species with sweet scarlet fruit, considered the source of many cultivated strawberries.
North American wild strawberry species

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with scarlet strawberries ripe for picking.
Many gardeners appreciate the hardiness of the scarlet strawberry plant.
Researchers traced the genetic origins of modern strawberries to the scarlet strawberry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild strawberry - a species of small, sweet, wild-growing strawberry
  • Degree Higher: cultivated strawberry - the commercially grown large-fruited strawberry
