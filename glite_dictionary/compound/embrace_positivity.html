to embrace positivity

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbreɪs pɑːzəˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs pɒzɪˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
to accept, support, and actively encourage a positive attitude and way of thinking
accept and encourage positive thinking

Examples

After a difficult year, I decided to embrace positivity and focus on my personal growth.
Many leaders encourage their teams to embrace positivity in the workplace to boost morale.
She found that when she began to embrace positivity, her relationships and outlook improved dramatically.

Synset Examples

It's time to s/ negativity and embrace positivity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: embrace negativity - to accept or focus on negative thoughts or outcomes
