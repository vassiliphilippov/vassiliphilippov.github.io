July 1

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈlaɪ fɜrst / (AmE), / dʒʊˈlaɪ fɜːst / (BrE)
The legal holiday in Canada commemorating the anniversary of the formation of the Dominion of Canada in 1867, now known as Canada Day.
Canadian national holiday; Canada Day

Examples

Every year on July 1, Canadians celebrate their nation’s history and heritage.
The parade in Ottawa attracted thousands of visitors for the July 1 festivities.
Fireworks are a traditional way to mark July 1 across Canada.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Canada Day - National holiday of Canada on July 1
