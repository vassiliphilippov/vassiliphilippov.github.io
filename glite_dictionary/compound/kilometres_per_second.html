kilometres per second

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈlɑːmɪtərz pər səˈkɑːnd / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtəz pə səˈkənd / (BrE)
a unit of speed equal to the number of kilometres traveled in one second
unit of speed: kilometres travelled per second

Examples

The spacecraft traveled at an astonishing speed of 12 kilometres per second.
Light moves through a vacuum at nearly 300,000 kilometres per second.
Scientists measured the meteor's velocity to be around 70 kilometres per second.
