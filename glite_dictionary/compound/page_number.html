page number

1. NOUN

/ peɪdʒ ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / peɪdʒ ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
the number that identifies a particular page in a book, document, or other written material
number identifying a specific page

Examples

Please write the page number at the top of your assignment.
The index refers to each topic's page number for easy reference.
She lost her place in the novel and couldn't recall the exact page number.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pagination - the process or system of assigning numbers to pages
