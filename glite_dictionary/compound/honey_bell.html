honey bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni bɛl / (AmE), / ˈhʌni bɛl / (BrE)
an African shrub with trailing stems and slender, yellow, honey-scented flowers, borne singly or in pairs
African shrub with yellow, honey-scented flowers

Examples

The garden's collection featured a rare honey bell in full bloom.
Botanists admire the honey bell for its fragrant yellow flowers that attract pollinators.
We planted a honey bell near the patio to add a touch of sweet scent to the evenings.
