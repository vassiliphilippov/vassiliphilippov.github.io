direction sign

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkʃən saɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃən saɪn / (BrE)
a road sign that provides information about locations or possible destinations for drivers
road sign indicating directions or locations

Examples

The direction sign pointed the drivers toward the nearest hospital.
She missed her exit because the direction sign was obscured by tree branches.
Many tourists rely on direction signs to navigate unfamiliar roads.

Synset Examples

Direction signs are far more varied internationally than other classes of sign, as the Vienna Convention does not specify sizes, colours, symbols or positions of such signs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signpost - a post with signs for directions or information
  • Synonym: road sign - a sign beside or above a road providing information to drivers
