high roller

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / haɪ ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who gambles or spends large sums of money, especially in a casino.
person who gambles large amounts

Examples

The casino treats every high roller to luxury suites and personal service.
Only a true high roller would bet a thousand dollars on a single hand.
He gained a reputation as a high roller after several nights of extravagant gambling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big spender - a person who spends a lot of money freely
  • Antonym: penny-pincher - someone who is very reluctant to spend money

2. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / haɪ ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who spends large amounts of money freely and ostentatiously, especially in casinos or on entertainment
person who spends or gambles large sums

Examples

The casino caters to high rollers who expect VIP treatment and luxury suites.
As a high roller, she was given complimentary tickets to exclusive shows and events.
Many high rollers travel to Las Vegas for the thrill of betting large sums on the tables.
