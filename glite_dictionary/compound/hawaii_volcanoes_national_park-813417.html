Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park

1. PROPN

/ həˈwaɪ.i voʊˈkeɪnoʊz ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i vɒlˈkeɪnəʊz ˌnæʃ.ən.əl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park on the island of Hawaii, known for containing active volcanoes such as Kīlauea and Mauna Loa
national park in Hawaii with active volcanoes

Examples

We visited Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park to see the lava flows from Kīlauea.
Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park offers unique opportunities to hike among active volcanoes and lava tubes.
Researchers at Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park study the area's rare flora and dramatic geological changes.
