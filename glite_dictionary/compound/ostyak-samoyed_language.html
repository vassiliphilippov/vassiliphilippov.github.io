Ostyak-Samoyed language

1. NOUN

/ ˌɒstiæk səˈmɔɪɛd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɒstiak səˈmɔɪɛd ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Ostyak-Samoyed people, also known as the Selkup language
Uralic language of the Ostyak-Samoyed (Selkup) people

Examples

The Ostyak-Samoyed language is classified within the Uralic language family.
Few young people today are fluent in the Ostyak-Samoyed language due to increasing use of Russian.
Several linguists are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Ostyak-Samoyed language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Selkup language - another name for Ostyak-Samoyed language

Etymology

From the names 'Ostyak' and 'Samoyed,' ethnonyms for indigenous peoples of Siberia; now more commonly called Selkup.
