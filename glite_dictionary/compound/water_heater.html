water heater

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌhitɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that heats and stores water for domestic or commercial use.
device for heating and storing water

Examples

The plumber installed a new water heater in the basement.
If your water heater isn't working, you'll have cold showers until it's fixed.
Some people use a tankless water heater to save energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boiler - an apparatus for heating water or other fluids
