latest caper

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtəst ˈkeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɪst ˈkeɪpə / (BrE)
a person's most recent adventure or mischievous escapade, often implying something bold or unconventional
most recent adventure or escapade

Examples

Journalists rushed to report on the actor's latest caper in Paris.
Her latest caper involved organizing a midnight picnic on the rooftop.
Everyone was still talking about his latest caper at the office party.

Synset Examples

His latest caper involved a daring escape from the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caper - a playful or mischievous adventure
