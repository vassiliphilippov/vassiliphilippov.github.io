freshwater fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃˌwɔtɚ fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃˌwɔːtə fɪʃ / (BrE)
the flesh of fish that live in fresh water, eaten as food
fish from fresh water used as food

Examples

Many restaurants serve freshwater fish like trout and catfish on their menus.
The chef recommended pairing the white wine with a mild freshwater fish dish.
She prefers the delicate flavor of freshwater fish over stronger saltwater varieties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saltwater fish - fish from salt water used as food
