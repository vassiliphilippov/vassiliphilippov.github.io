Sequoia National Park

1. PROPN

/ sɪˈkwɔɪ.ə ˈnæʃ.nəl pɑːrk / (AmE), / sɪˈkwɔɪ.ə ˈnæʃ.nəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in California, USA, known for its giant sequoia trees and including Mount Whitney, the highest peak in the contiguous United States.
national park in California, USA

Examples

Every summer, thousands of tourists visit Sequoia National Park to see the massive trees.
We hiked to the summit of Mount Whitney in Sequoia National Park last year.
Sequoia National Park is famous for its breathtaking landscapes and ancient forests.

Etymology

Named after the giant sequoia tree, itself named for the Cherokee leader Sequoyah.
