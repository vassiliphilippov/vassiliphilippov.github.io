irregularity in heartbeat

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærəti ɪn ˈhɑrtˌbit / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarəti ɪn ˈhɑːtbɪt / (BrE)
an abnormal rhythm or pattern of heartbeats, differing from the normal regular beating of the heart
abnormal heart rhythm or pattern
medical

Examples

The doctor detected an irregularity in heartbeat during the routine check-up.
She was hospitalized after an irregularity in heartbeat showed up on her ECG.
A sudden irregularity in heartbeat can be a sign of a serious cardiac problem.

Synset Examples

The irregularity in his heartbeat was concerning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrhythmia - medical term for an abnormal heart rhythm
