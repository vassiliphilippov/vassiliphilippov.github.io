safety deposit box

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti dɪˈpɑzɪt bɑks / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti dɪˈpɒzɪt bɒks / (BrE)
A secure, fireproof metal container, typically located in a bank, used for storing valuable items.
secure box in a bank for valuables

Examples

She keeps her jewelry and important documents in a safety deposit box at the bank.
After his grandfather passed away, he discovered a safety deposit box full of old coins.
It's wise to store your property deed in a safety deposit box for extra protection.
