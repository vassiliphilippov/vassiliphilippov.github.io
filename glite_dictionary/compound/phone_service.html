phone service

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a service provided by a public utility that enables people to make and receive telephone calls
telephone utility service

Examples

Many rural areas still have limited access to reliable phone service.
She called the phone service provider to report an outage in her neighborhood.
With the rise of mobile technology, traditional landline phone service has become less common.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone service - service provided for making telephone calls
