rug rider

1. NOUN

/ rʌɡ ˈraɪdər / (AmE), / rʌɡ ˈraɪdə / (BrE)
A carefree or adventurous person, especially one known for surfing or seeking excitement.
carefree adventurer, often in surfing

Examples

She likes to think of herself as a true rug rider who chases the perfect wave.
After quitting his job, he became a rug rider and traveled along the coast.
The documentary follows several rug riders as they seek adventure on remote beaches.

Synset Examples

She calls herself a rug rider in the surfing community.
He felt like a rug rider during the bumpy ride.
