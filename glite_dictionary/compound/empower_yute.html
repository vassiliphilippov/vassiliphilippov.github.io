to empower yute

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈpaʊ.ər juːt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpaʊə juːt / (BrE)
To enable young people to gain confidence or authority, often by providing opportunities or resources.
enable young people to gain power or confidence
informal slang

Examples

Local organizations work hard to empower yute by offering educational workshops and internships.
The charity’s mission is to empower yute through music and art programs.
Community leaders launched a new initiative to empower yute facing social and economic challenges.

Synset Examples

The program aims to empower yute in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empower youth - enable young people to gain confidence or authority

Etymology

Empower from English prefix 'em-' (to put into) + 'power', and 'yute', a slang form of 'youth' from Jamaican Creole influence.
