blood cup

1. NOUN

/ blʌd kʌp / (AmE), / blʌd kʌp / (BrE)
A scarlet European fungus with a cup-shaped fruiting body (ascocarp).
scarlet cup-shaped European fungus

Examples

The blood cup is easily recognized by its bright red, cup-shaped fruiting body in woodland areas.
Fungi enthusiasts often search for the elusive blood cup after heavy spring rains.
The blood cup adds a splash of color to the forest floor during early spring.
