friendship plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndʃɪp ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a low-growing plant native to Central and South America, with velvety brownish-green toothed leaves and clusters of small green flowers, often grown as a houseplant
plant with velvety leaves, grown as a houseplant

Examples

The friendship plant makes an attractive addition to any indoor garden with its soft, textured leaves.
She bought a friendship plant as a gift for her neighbor's new apartment.
Because the friendship plant is easy to grow, it's popular among beginner houseplant enthusiasts.
