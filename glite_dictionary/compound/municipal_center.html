municipal center

1. NOUN

/ mjuˈnɪsəpəl ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
the central area of a city where civic or administrative buildings are located
main civic area of a city

Examples

The new library was built in the municipal center to serve the entire community.
Many city festivals are held in the municipal center during the summer months.
The municipal center houses the city council and administrative offices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city center - the central part of a city, especially used in British English
  • Synonym: downtown - the main business or commercial area of a city, especially in North America
