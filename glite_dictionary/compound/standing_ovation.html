standing ovation

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɪŋ oʊˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ əʊˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
an enthusiastic show of appreciation or approval in which the audience rises to its feet and applauds loudly
enthusiastic applause with the audience standing

Examples

The singer received a standing ovation at the end of her performance.
After the emotional speech, the entire audience gave him a standing ovation.
It was clear the play was a success when the crowd erupted into a standing ovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ovation - an enthusiastic show of public approval or applause
  • Synonym: applause - clapping by an audience as a sign of approval
