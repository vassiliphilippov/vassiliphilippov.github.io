car racing

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / kɑː ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of racing automobiles, usually on a track or course, for competition or entertainment
sport of racing cars

Examples

He has been fascinated by car racing since he was a child.
The city is hosting its annual car racing championship this weekend.
Many people find car racing thrilling to watch on television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auto racing - the sport of racing automobiles, especially in organized events
