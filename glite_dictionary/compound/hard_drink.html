hard drink

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd drɪŋk / (AmE), / hɑːd drɪŋk / (BrE)
An alcoholic beverage that is distilled rather than fermented, such as whiskey, vodka, or rum; spirits.
distilled alcoholic beverage; spirit

Examples

He prefers a hard drink like whiskey over beer.
The bar offers a wide selection of hard drinks for those who enjoy spirits.
Doctors recommend limiting your intake of hard drinks for better health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirit - strong alcoholic drink made by distillation
