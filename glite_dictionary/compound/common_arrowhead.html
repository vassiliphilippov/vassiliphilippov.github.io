common arrowhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈæroʊˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈærəʊˌhɛd / (BrE)
a North American aquatic plant (Sagittaria latifolia) with arrow-shaped leaves and edible tubers, often found in wetlands and shallow waters
North American aquatic plant (Sagittaria latifolia)

Examples

We spotted a patch of common arrowhead growing along the edge of the lake.
The leaves of the common arrowhead are easily recognized by their distinctive shape.
Many wetland birds feed on the seeds of the common arrowhead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sagittaria latifolia - scientific name of the common arrowhead plant
