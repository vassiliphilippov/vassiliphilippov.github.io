bio lab

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.oʊ læb / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.əʊ læb / (BrE)
A laboratory where biological research or experiments are conducted.
laboratory for biological research

Examples

Our school has a new bio lab equipped with the latest microscopes and computers.
She spent most of her summer internship working in the university's bio lab.
Students are required to wear safety goggles when conducting experiments in the bio lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biology laboratory - laboratory specializing in biological sciences
  • Antonym: chem lab - laboratory for chemistry research

Etymology

Shortened form of 'biology laboratory'; 'bio-' from 'biology' + 'lab', informal for 'laboratory'.
