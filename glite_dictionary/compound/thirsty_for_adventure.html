thirsty for adventure

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrsti fər ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈθɜːsti fə ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
eager or strongly desirous of experiencing new and exciting things
eager for new experiences

Examples

After years of routine, she suddenly felt thirsty for adventure and booked a solo trip to South America.
His stories always made us thirsty for adventure and eager to explore new places.
Many young people are thirsty for adventure after graduating and want to see the world.

Synset Examples

He was thirsty for adventure and ready to travel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventurous - willing to take risks or try out new methods, ideas, or experiences
  • Antonym: bored - feeling weary because one is unoccupied or lacks interest in one's current activity
