sexual morality

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl məˈrælɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl məˈralɪti / (BrE)
Principles or standards of right and wrong relating to sexual behavior or relationships.
ethics regarding sexual behavior

Examples

Debates about sexual morality often arise in schools and communities around the world.
Her parents held traditional views on sexual morality and expected her to follow strict rules.
Laws regarding sexual morality vary greatly from one culture to another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morality - principles concerning the distinction between right and wrong or good and bad behavior
