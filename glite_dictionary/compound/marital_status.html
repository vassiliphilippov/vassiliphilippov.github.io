marital status

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛrɪtəl ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːrɪtəl ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the condition of being married, single, divorced, or widowed; a person's legal relationship status regarding marriage
a person's legal relationship status (married, single, etc.)

Examples

On the application form, you must indicate your marital status as single, married, or divorced.
Employers are not allowed to discriminate based on a person's marital status.
Her marital status changed when she got married last year.

Etymology

From Latin 'maritalis' (of marriage) and Latin 'status' (state, condition).
