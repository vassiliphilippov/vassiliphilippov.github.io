ballet master

1. NOUN

/ bəˈleɪ ˌmæstər / (AmE), / bæˈleɪ ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a man who directs, teaches, and rehearses dancers for a ballet company
male director and instructor of ballet dancers

Examples

The ballet master guided the dancers through a rigorous rehearsal before the opening night.
As a respected ballet master, he devoted his life to perfecting the technique of his company.
The young dancers admired their ballet master for both his strict discipline and inspiring passion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ballet mistress - a woman who directs and teaches and rehearses dancers for a ballet company
