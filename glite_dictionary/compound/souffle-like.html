souffle-like

1. ADJ

/ suːˈfleɪ laɪk / (AmE), / suːˈfleɪ laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the light, airy texture of a souffle.
resembling a souffle

Examples

The cake had a souffle-like texture that melted in my mouth.
Her voice carried a souffle-like softness, light and delicate.
We enjoyed a souffle-like omelet for breakfast, fluffy and perfectly risen.
