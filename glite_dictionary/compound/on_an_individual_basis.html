on an individual basis

1. ADV

/ ɑn ən ɪnˈdɪvɪdʒuəl ˈbeɪsɪs / (AmE), / ɒn ən ɪnˈdɪvɪdʒuəl ˈbeɪsɪs / (BrE)
separately; with each case or person considered one at a time, not collectively
separately; one at a time

Examples

Students are assessed on an individual basis to ensure fair evaluation.
Requests for flexible work schedules are considered on an individual basis rather than with a standard policy.
Medical treatments are often tailored on an individual basis depending on the patient's needs.

Synset Examples

taken individually, the rooms were, in fact, square
the fine points are treated singly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: individually - considering each one separately
  • Antonym: collectively - as a group or whole, not separately
