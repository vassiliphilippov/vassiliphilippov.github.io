escape cock

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈskeɪp kɑk / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp kɒk / (BrE)
a valve in a pressurized container (such as a steam boiler) that opens automatically to release pressure when it becomes dangerously high
valve releasing dangerous pressure

Examples

The escape cock must be checked regularly to ensure it will operate under excess pressure.
When the steam pressure rises too high, the escape cock automatically opens to prevent an explosion.
The engineer explained the importance of the escape cock in maintaining safe operating conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety valve - a device that automatically releases a substance from a boiler, pressure vessel, or other system when the pressure exceeds preset limits

Etymology

From 'escape', meaning to get free, and 'cock', an old term for a valve or tap.
