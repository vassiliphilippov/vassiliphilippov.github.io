gaming persona

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmɪŋ pərˈsoʊnə / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmɪŋ pəˈsəʊnə / (BrE)
an identity or character adopted by a person when playing video games, often distinct from their real-life identity
an adopted identity in video games

Examples

Her gaming persona is a courageous knight, quite different from her shy real-life personality.
Many streamers carefully craft their gaming persona to entertain their audience.
He created a new gaming persona for the online tournament, complete with a backstory and costume.

Synset Examples

He refers to his gaming persona as a troon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avatar - a graphical representation of a user in a video game or online community
