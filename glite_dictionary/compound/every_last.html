every last

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛvri læst / (AmE), / ˈɛvri lɑːst / (BrE)
Used for emphasis to mean every single one, without exception.
emphatic form of 'every'

Examples

She collected every last piece of evidence to ensure nothing was missed.
The children ate every last cookie on the plate.
He searched for every last detail in the report, determined not to overlook anything.

Synset Examples

every last one of you

Related Terms

  • Synonym: every - all of the individual people or things in a group
