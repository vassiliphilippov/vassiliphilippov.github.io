pebble plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛb.əl plænt / (AmE), / ˈpɛb.əl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a small South African plant of the genus Mesembryanthemum, grown for its attractive pink or white flowers and pebble-like appearance
succulent plant with pebble-like appearance

Examples

The pebble plant thrives in dry, sunny conditions and requires little water.
She admired the tiny pink blossoms on her collection of pebble plants.
Because the pebble plant blends with stones, it is often overlooked in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living stone - a succulent plant that resembles a stone, including species of Lithops and related genera
