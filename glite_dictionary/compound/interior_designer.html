interior designer

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɪriɚ dɪˈzaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə dɪˈzaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to plan and decorate the inside spaces of buildings
person who designs building interiors

Examples

The company hired an interior designer to create a modern, comfortable workspace.
As an interior designer, she specializes in luxury hotel makeovers.
Our new house looks amazing thanks to the creativity of our interior designer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorator - person who decorates interiors of buildings
