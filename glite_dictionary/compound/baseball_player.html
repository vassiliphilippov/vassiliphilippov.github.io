baseball player

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
A person who plays the sport of baseball, especially as a member of a team or as a profession.
person who plays baseball

Examples

The team's new baseball player hit a home run in his first game.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a professional baseball player.
She cheered enthusiastically for her favorite baseball player all season long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise
