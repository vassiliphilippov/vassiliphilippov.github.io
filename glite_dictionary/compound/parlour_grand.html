parlour grand

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrlɚ ˌɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlə ˌɡrænd / (BrE)
a small type of grand piano, typically suitable for use in homes or small rooms
a small grand piano

Examples

They bought a parlour grand to fit perfectly in their living room.
The music teacher recommended purchasing a parlour grand for the recital space.
Unlike a concert grand, a parlour grand is better suited for smaller interiors.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: grand piano - a large, horizontal piano with a wing-shaped body
  • Degree Lower: baby grand - an even smaller grand piano
