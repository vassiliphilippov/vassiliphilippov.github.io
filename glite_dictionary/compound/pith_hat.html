pith hat

1. NOUN

/ pɪθ hæt / (AmE), / pɪθ hæt / (BrE)
a lightweight helmet, typically made from pith or similar material, worn in tropical regions for protection from the sun
light tropical sun helmet

Examples

The explorer wore a pith hat to shield himself from the intense midday sun.
Early photographs of colonial officials often show them in white suits and pith hats.
She placed her old pith hat on the shelf as a souvenir of her time abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pith helmet - a lightweight sun helmet made from pith

Etymology

From 'pith', the spongy tissue in plant stems, used as material, and 'hat'.
