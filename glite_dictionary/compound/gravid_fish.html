gravid fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrævɪd fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡrævɪd fɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish that is carrying eggs inside its body
fish carrying eggs

Examples

The gravid fish was carefully transferred to a separate tank to protect her eggs.
Researchers observed several gravid fish preparing nests in the shallow waters.
A gravid fish often shows a noticeably swollen belly before spawning.

Synset Examples

The gravid fish swam close to the surface before spawning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gravid - pregnant or carrying eggs, especially in animals

Etymology

From Latin gravidus (pregnant, heavy), used in scientific contexts to mean 'carrying eggs or young.'
