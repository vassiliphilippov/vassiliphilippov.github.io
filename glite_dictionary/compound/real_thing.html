real thing

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl θɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl θɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Something that is genuine, authentic, or exactly what it is claimed or expected to be.
something genuine or authentic
informal

Examples

He finally found a cup of Italian espresso that tasted like the real thing.
You can't beat homemade bread; it’s the real thing compared to store-bought loaves.
After many cheap imitations, this phone is the real thing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genuine article - something that is truly authentic or original
  • Antonym: fake - something that is not genuine or is an imitation
