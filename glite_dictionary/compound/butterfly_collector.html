butterfly collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtɚˌflaɪ kəˈlɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person, especially an entomologist, who collects and studies butterflies and moths
person who collects butterflies and moths

Examples

As a child, Jane dreamed of becoming a butterfly collector and traveling the world.
The museum invited a renowned butterfly collector to exhibit his rare specimens.
Many butterfly collectors keep detailed records of their sightings in field journals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entomologist - a scientist who studies insects
