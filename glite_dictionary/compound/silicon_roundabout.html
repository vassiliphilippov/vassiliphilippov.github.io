Silicon Roundabout

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪlɪkən ˈraʊndəˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪkən ˈraʊndəbaʊt / (BrE)
an area in East London, around Old Street, known for its concentration of technology companies and startups
tech hub area in East London

Examples

Many international investors are now interested in the opportunities offered by Silicon Roundabout.
She works for a startup that recently moved its office to Silicon Roundabout.
Tech events often take place in Silicon Roundabout, attracting talent from across Europe.

Synset Examples

Many tech companies are based in Silicon Roundabout.
Investment in Silicon Roundabout is increasing rapidly.
Silicon Roundabout is thriving with startups.

Etymology

Modeled on 'Silicon Valley', combining 'Silicon' (from Silicon Valley) and 'Roundabout' (referring to the Old Street traffic circle).
