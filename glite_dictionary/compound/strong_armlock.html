strong armlock

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ˈɑrmˌlɑk / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈɑːmlɒk / (BrE)
a firm and forceful grip or hold on someone's arm, typically to restrain movement or as a wrestling technique
firm hold on someone's arm

Examples

The judo champion used a strong armlock to pin her opponent to the mat.
The police officer maintained a strong armlock while escorting the suspect.
He surprised everyone with his strong armlock during the match.

Synset Examples

She had a strong armlock that surprised her rivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armlock - a wrestling hold that locks an opponent's arm
