yam family

1. NOUN

/ jæm ˈfæməli / (AmE), / jæm ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, Dioscoreaceae, that includes yams and related species
the plant family Dioscoreaceae, including yams

Examples

The yam family includes several plants with starchy tubers used as food in many cultures.
Botanists classify yams under the yam family, which is scientifically known as Dioscoreaceae.
Many wild species in the yam family have medicinal properties and are studied for their uses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dioscoreaceae - the botanical family that comprises yams and related plants
  • Synonym: yam - a starchy tuber of various climbing plants in the Dioscoreaceae family
