wall tent

1. NOUN

/ wɔl tɛnt / (AmE), / wɔːl tɛnt / (BrE)
a large canvas tent with four vertical walls, providing more interior space than traditional tents
large tent with vertical walls

Examples

The hunters set up a wall tent near the edge of the forest for their week-long expedition.
We stayed warm and comfortable inside the wall tent despite the snow outside.
A spacious wall tent was chosen for the family camping trip to accommodate everyone.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth, supported by poles and ropes
