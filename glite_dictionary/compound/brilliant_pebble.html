brilliant pebble

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪliənt ˈpɛbəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljənt ˈpɛbəl / (BrE)
a code name for a small computerized heat-seeking missile designed to intercept and destroy enemy missiles, developed as part of the Strategic Defense Initiative.
code name for an anti-missile weapon

Examples

The defense department invested millions in the brilliant pebble project during the late 20th century.
Critics doubted the effectiveness of brilliant pebble technology in countering advanced enemy missiles.
Research into the brilliant pebble system influenced modern missile defense strategies.
