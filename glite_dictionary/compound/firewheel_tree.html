firewheel tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərˌwiːl ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌwiːl ˌtriː / (BrE)
An eastern Australian tree (Stenocarpus sinuatus), widely grown for its glossy leaves and circular clusters of bright red to orange-scarlet flowers.
Australian tree with bright red circular flower clusters

Examples

The firewheel tree is admired for its brilliant scarlet blooms in many Australian gardens.
A mature firewheel tree can provide ample shade in a backyard setting.
The circular flower clusters of the firewheel tree attract many birds and pollinators.
