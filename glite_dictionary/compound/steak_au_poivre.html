steak au poivre

1. NOUN

/ steɪk oʊ ˈpwɑːv / (AmE), / steɪk əʊ ˈpwɑːv / (BrE)
A dish consisting of steak coated with crushed peppercorns, pan-broiled, and typically served with a brandy and butter sauce.
steak with peppercorns and sauce

Examples

He ordered the steak au poivre for dinner at the French restaurant.
The chef recommended pairing the steak au poivre with a robust red wine.
I decided to try making steak au poivre at home after watching a cooking show.

Etymology

From French 'steak au poivre', meaning 'steak with pepper'.
