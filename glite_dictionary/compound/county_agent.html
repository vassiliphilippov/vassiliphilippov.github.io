county agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnti ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnti ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official employed by the government to provide advice and assistance in agriculture and home economics to people in rural areas
government adviser for rural agriculture and home economics
american

Examples

The county agent visited the farm to give advice on crop rotation.
Many rural families rely on their county agent for up-to-date information about pest control.
The county agent organized a workshop on new agricultural techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extension agent - government advisor for agriculture and rural development
