sun spurge

1. NOUN

/ sʌn spɝːdʒ / (AmE), / sʌn spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a European weed (Euphorbia helioscopia) with small yellow-green flowers that turn toward the sun
a European weed with sun-turning flowers

Examples

The sun spurge often invades gardens, thriving in sunny, open soil.
Botanists studied the way sun spurge flowers tilt towards the light during the day.
You can identify sun spurge by its small leaves and the unique cluster of yellow-green flowers.
