heartfelt poetry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌfɛlt ˈpoʊɾri / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtfɛlt ˈpəʊɪtri / (BrE)
Poetry that expresses sincere and profound emotions.
poetry expressing deep emotions

Examples

Her heartfelt poetry resonated with readers who had experienced similar emotions.
The competition encouraged young writers to submit their best pieces of heartfelt poetry.
Many people turn to heartfelt poetry as a way of coping with life's challenges.

Synset Examples

He expressed his misery through heartfelt poetry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lyric poetry - poetry expressing the poet's personal feelings or emotions
