banded purple

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæn.dɪd ˈpɜr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈbæn.dɪd ˈpɜː.pəl / (BrE)
A North American butterfly with blue-black wings intersected by a broad white band, known scientifically as Limenitis arthemis arthemis.
a North American butterfly species

Examples

We spotted a banded purple resting on a maple leaf in the garden.
The banded purple is admired for its striking white band across dark wings.
Many hikers enjoy watching banded purples fluttering along woodland trails in the summer.
