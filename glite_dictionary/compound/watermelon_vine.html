watermelon vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtərˌmɛlən vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌmɛlən vaɪn / (BrE)
the climbing or trailing plant (Citrullus lanatus) native to Africa, which produces the watermelon fruit
the plant that produces watermelons

Examples

The watermelon vine sprawled across the garden, producing several ripe fruits by midsummer.
Farmers carefully monitor the soil moisture to help the watermelon vine thrive.
She was amazed at how quickly the watermelon vine grew after the first rains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watermelon - large, round fruit with green rind and sweet, juicy red flesh
