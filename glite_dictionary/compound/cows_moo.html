cow's moo

1. NOUN

/ kaʊz mu / (AmE), / kaʊz muː / (BrE)
the characteristic sound that a cow makes; a low, drawn-out call
the sound made by a cow

Examples

The gentle cow's moo could be heard across the quiet meadow.
She woke up to the familiar cow's moo outside her window every morning.
The children giggled when they tried to imitate a cow's moo at the petting zoo.

Synset Examples

Every morning, I hear a cow's moo from the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moo - the sound that a cow makes
