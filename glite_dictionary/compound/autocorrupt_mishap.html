autocorrupt mishap

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtoʊkəˈrʌpt ˈmɪshæp / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊkəˈrʌpt ˈmɪshap / (BrE)
an error in text caused by automatic correction software altering words incorrectly
an error from automatic text correction
informal

Examples

She laughed out loud when an autocorrupt mishap changed her message to something embarrassing.
We've all experienced an autocorrupt mishap that completely changed the meaning of a text.
Thanks to an autocorrupt mishap, his dinner invitation became a confusing joke.

Synset Examples

My text messages often suffer from autocorrupt mishaps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typo - a mistake in printed or typed text
