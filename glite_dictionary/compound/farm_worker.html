farm worker

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / fɑːm ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who is employed to work on a farm, especially performing physical labor
person employed to do farm work

Examples

During the harvest season, the farm worker starts work at sunrise every day.
Many farm workers travel from region to region looking for employment.
The farm worker operated heavy machinery as part of his daily duties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
  • Antonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
