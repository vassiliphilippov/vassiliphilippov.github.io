ballet company

1. NOUN

/ bəˈleɪ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / bæˈleɪ ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
an organization or group of dancers that produces and performs ballets
group producing and performing ballets

Examples

She was thrilled to join the prestigious ballet company after years of rigorous training.
The ballet company will perform Swan Lake at the new theater downtown next month.
Many young dancers dream of touring internationally with a renowned ballet company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance company - a company that produces and performs various types of dance
  • Synonym: theater company - a company that produces and performs plays or dramas
