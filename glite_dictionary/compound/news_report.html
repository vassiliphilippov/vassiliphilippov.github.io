news report

1. NOUN

/ nuz rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / njuːz rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a short or detailed written or spoken account of recent events, especially as presented by the media
short account of current events in media

Examples

The evening news report summarized the major events of the day.
She watched the news report to stay informed about the election results.
A breaking news report interrupted the regular television program.

Synset Examples

the report of his speech
the story was on the 11 o'clock news
the account of his speech that was given on the evening news made the governor furious

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news story - an article or report describing a news event
