in the news

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə nuz / (AmE), / ɪn ðə njuːz / (BrE)
being reported in newspapers, on television, or by other media at the present time
being reported by the media

Examples

The local farmer was in the news after rescuing a child from the river.
That company has been in the news for its latest environmental initiative.
Every time a celebrity is in the news, rumors start to spread.

Synset Examples

She heard something awful in the news today.
