old-field toadflax

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊld ˌfiːld ˈtoʊdflæks / (AmE), / ˈəʊld ˌfiːld ˈtəʊdflæks / (BrE)
A North American plant with racemes of blue-violet flowers, belonging to the toadflax family.
North American plant with blue-violet flowers

Examples

The meadows were dotted with patches of old-field toadflax in full bloom.
Botanists studied the spread of old-field toadflax across abandoned farmland.
She planted old-field toadflax to attract bees and butterflies to her garden.
