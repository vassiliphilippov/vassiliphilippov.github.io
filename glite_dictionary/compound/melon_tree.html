melon tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛlən ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈmɛlən ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with large, deeply divided leaves and yellow, oblong fruit, commonly known as papaya.
tropical tree bearing papaya fruit

Examples

The melon tree in our backyard produces fruit all year round.
After moving to the tropics, she planted a melon tree to enjoy fresh papayas.
The huge leaves of the melon tree provide excellent shade in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papaya - a tropical fruit and the tree bearing it
