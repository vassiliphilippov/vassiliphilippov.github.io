frozen dessert

1. NOUN

/ ˈfroʊzən dɪˈzɝt / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən dɪˈzɜːt / (BrE)
a sweet dish that is made by freezing ingredients, such as ice cream, sorbet, or frozen yogurt
sweet dish prepared by freezing

Examples

Many people enjoy a frozen dessert like ice cream on a hot summer day.
The restaurant offered a selection of frozen desserts including sorbet and gelato.
She made her own frozen dessert using fresh berries and yogurt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice cream - a soft, sweet frozen food made with milk and cream
  • Synonym: sorbet - a sweet, icy frozen dessert made with fruit juice or puree
