California dandelion

1. NOUN

/ ˌkælɪˈfɔrniə ˈdændəˌlaɪən / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə ˈdændɪˌlaɪən / (BrE)
A plant species, originally from Europe, now widespread in North America; it has yellow flower heads and leaves similar to a cat's ears.
plant with yellow flowers and cat's ear-shaped leaves

Examples

Many wildflower enthusiasts search for the California dandelion in meadows every spring.
The leaves of the California dandelion resemble those of a cat's ears, making identification easier.
Unlike the common dandelion, the California dandelion is less widespread but equally beautiful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandelion - a common plant with yellow flowers and notched leaves
