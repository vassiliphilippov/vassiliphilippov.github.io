outboard motorboat

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtbɔrd ˈmoʊtərˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɔːd ˈməʊtəbəʊt / (BrE)
A motorboat powered by an outboard motor mounted externally at the stern.
motorboat with outboard motor

Examples

They used an outboard motorboat to reach the island quickly.
The fisherman started the outboard motorboat early in the morning.
Renting an outboard motorboat is popular at the marina.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inboard motorboat - motorboat powered by an engine installed inside the hull
