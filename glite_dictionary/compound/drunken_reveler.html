drunken reveler

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌŋkən ˈrɛvələr / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋkən ˈrɛvələ / (BrE)
a person who participates in wild celebrations, typically while under the influence of alcohol
person who parties and drinks heavily

Examples

The police had to disperse a crowd after a drunken reveler started a fight.
As midnight approached, every drunken reveler on the street seemed determined to keep the party going.
One drunken reveler stumbled into the fountain, drawing laughter from the rest of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reveler - person who enjoys lively festivities
  • Synonym: party animal - informal term for someone who enjoys partying a lot
