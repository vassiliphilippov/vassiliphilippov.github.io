ore processing

1. NOUN

/ ɔr ˈprɑːˌsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔː ˈprəʊsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the industrial process of crushing and separating ore to extract valuable substances or remove waste using various techniques
the treatment of ore to extract valuables

Examples

The new plant specializes in ore processing using state-of-the-art equipment.
Advances in ore processing have made mining more efficient and environmentally friendly.
A thorough understanding of ore processing is vital for metallurgical engineers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extraction - the act of removing valuable substances from ore or other materials
