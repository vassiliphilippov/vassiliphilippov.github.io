yellow clintonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ klɪnˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ klɪnˈtəʊnɪə / (BrE)
a woodland plant of temperate North America, with yellow nodding flowers and small round blue fruits
North American woodland plant with yellow flowers

Examples

During our hike, we spotted several yellow clintonia plants growing by the shaded forest path.
The distinctive blue berries of yellow clintonia appear in late summer after the flowers have faded.
She photographed the delicate, bell-shaped flowers of the yellow clintonia for her wildflower journal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluebead lily - another common name for Clintonia species, especially Clintonia borealis

Etymology

Clintonia is named after DeWitt Clinton, a 19th-century American politician and naturalist.
