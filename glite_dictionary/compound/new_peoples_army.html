New People's Army

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈpipəlz ˈɑrmi / (AmE), / njuː ˈpiːplz ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
the armed wing of the Communist Party of the Philippines, a Maoist guerrilla organization formed in 1969 to overthrow the Philippine government
Maoist guerrilla group in the Philippines

Examples

The government accused the New People's Army of carrying out attacks in the southern provinces.
After decades of conflict, peace talks with the New People's Army were resumed in Manila.
The New People's Army is known for its hit-and-run tactics against military forces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Communist Party of the Philippines - the political party associated with the New People's Army
