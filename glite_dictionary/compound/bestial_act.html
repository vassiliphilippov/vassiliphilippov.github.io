bestial act

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛstʃəl ækt / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪəl akt / (BrE)
an action characterized by extreme cruelty or inhumaneness
cruel or inhumane action

Examples

The report detailed the bestial acts committed during the conflict.
Witnesses described the bestial act as something they would never forget.
The court condemned the perpetrator's bestial acts as inexcusable.

Synset Examples

They were horrified by the bestial acts in the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atrocity - an extremely wicked or cruel act
  • Synonym: barbarity - extreme cruelty or brutality

Etymology

From Latin 'bestia' (beast), referencing animal-like cruelty.
