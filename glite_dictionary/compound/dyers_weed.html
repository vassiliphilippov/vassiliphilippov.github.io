dyer's weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪərz wiːd / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəz wiːd / (BrE)
an eastern North American herb, also known as dyer's rocket, whose yellow flowers are used for producing dyes
plant with yellow flowers used in dyeing

Examples

The flowers of dyer's weed were prized for their vivid yellow dye.
Historically, textile artisans would gather dyer's weed during the blooming season.
Some natural dye enthusiasts still cultivate dyer's weed for its traditional coloring properties.
