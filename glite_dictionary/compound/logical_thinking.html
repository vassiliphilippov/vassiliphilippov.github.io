logical thinking

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of reasoning in a clear, consistent, and rational manner
reasoning in a logical, coherent way

Examples

Her logical thinking helped her solve complex problems quickly and efficiently.
Employers often look for candidates with strong logical thinking skills.
The course encourages logical thinking and analytical reasoning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reasoning - the action of thinking about something in a sensible way
