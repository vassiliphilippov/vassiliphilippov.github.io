Key West

1. PROPN

/ ki ˈwɛst / (AmE), / kiː ˈwɛst / (BrE)
a town on the westernmost of the Florida Keys, located in the Gulf of Mexico, famous for its unique history and subtropical climate
city: Florida island town in the Gulf of Mexico

Examples
