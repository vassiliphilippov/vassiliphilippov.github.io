military personnel

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪlɪˌtɛri pɚˈsɑːnəl / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛəri pəˈsɒnəl / (BrE)
Persons who serve in the armed forces; members of a military force.
members of the armed forces

Examples

The country increased its military personnel to strengthen national defense.
All military personnel must wear their uniforms during official ceremonies.
Many military personnel are deployed overseas for peacekeeping missions.

Synset Examples

two men stood sentry duty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army

2. NOUN

/ ˌmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈpɜrsəˌnɛl / (AmE), / ˌmɪlɪˌtɛːri pəˈsɒnəl / (BrE)
members of the armed forces, including soldiers, sailors, airmen, and others serving in a military organization collectively.
members of the armed forces

Examples

The government increased benefits for all military personnel stationed overseas.
Families of military personnel often endure long periods of separation during deployments.
The ceremony honored the bravery and sacrifice of fallen military personnel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soldiers - members of an army who fight on land
  • Antonym: civilians - people who are not members of the armed forces
