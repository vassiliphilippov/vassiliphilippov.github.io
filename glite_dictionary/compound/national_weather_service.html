National Weather Service

1. PROPN

/ ˌnæʃənl ˈwɛðɚ ˌsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˌnæʃnəl ˈwɛðə ˌsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the federal agency of the United States within the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration responsible for weather forecasting and the preparation of weather maps
US agency for weather forecasting
american

Examples

The National Weather Service has issued a tornado warning for the area.
Meteorologists at the National Weather Service monitor severe storms around the clock.
