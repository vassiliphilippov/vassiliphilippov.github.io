tin pyrites

1. NOUN

/ tɪn paɪˈraɪtiːz / (AmE), / tɪn paɪˈraɪtiːz / (BrE)
a dark grey metallic mineral that is a source of tin, also known as stannite
a mineral ore of tin

Examples

The mine produced large quantities of tin pyrites during the 19th century.
Geologists identified the dark, metallic crystals as tin pyrites in the rock sample.
Tin pyrites is an important ore for extracting industrial tin.
