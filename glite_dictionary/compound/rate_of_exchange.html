rate of exchange

1. NOUN

/ reɪt əv ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪt əv ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the value at which the currency of one country can be exchanged for that of another at a particular time; the exchange rate
value for exchanging one currency for another

Examples

The rate of exchange fluctuates daily depending on the global market.
Before traveling abroad, she checked the rate of exchange between the dollar and the euro.
Banks usually set the rate of exchange slightly higher than the market rate for profit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exchange rate - the price of one currency expressed in terms of another; synonym for 'rate of exchange'
