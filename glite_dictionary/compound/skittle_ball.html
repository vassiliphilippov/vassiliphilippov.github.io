skittle ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪt̬l bɔl / (AmE), / ˈskɪtl bɔːl / (BrE)
a ball used in the game of skittles to knock down ninepins
ball used in skittles game

Examples

He rolled the skittle ball with precision and knocked down all ninepins.
Each player gets three turns with the skittle ball during the round.
The old wooden skittle ball showed signs of years of use at the pub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skittle - a game in which players knock down pins with a ball
  • Synonym: bowling ball - a ball used in bowling to knock down pins

Etymology

From 'skittle', denoting the game or the pin, and 'ball'. 'Skittle' traces to Middle English 'skytel', related to Scandinavian roots.
