African walnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfrɪkən ˈwɑlnʌt / (AmE), / ˈaf.rɪ.kən ˈwɒl.nʌt / (BrE)
A tropical African tree valued for its timber, which resembles mahogany in appearance and quality.
tropical African timber tree

Examples

The furniture was made from African walnut, giving the room a warm, rich look.
Many luxury car interiors feature veneers of African walnut for a polished finish.
Sustainable harvesting of African walnut is important to maintain the species and its habitats.
