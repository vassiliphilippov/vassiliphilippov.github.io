blueberry pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌbɛri paɪ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri paɪ / (BrE)
A pie filled with blueberries and sugar, often served as a dessert.
pie filled with blueberries

Examples

Every summer, my grandmother bakes a delicious blueberry pie using fresh berries from her garden.
The aroma of blueberry pie filled the whole house during the holiday.
She served a slice of warm blueberry pie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apple pie - pie made with apples and sugar
  • Synonym: cherry pie - pie made with cherries and sugar
