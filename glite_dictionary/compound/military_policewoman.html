military policewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri pəˈlisˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pəˈliːswʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of the military police responsible for maintaining discipline among soldiers and guarding prisoners.
female member of military police

Examples

The military policewoman inspected the soldiers’ identification cards at the entrance gate.
After completing her training, she became the first military policewoman in her unit.
A military policewoman escorted the prisoner to the holding cell following the investigation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military policeman - male member of the military police
  • Synonym: military police - the military organization responsible for policing soldiers
