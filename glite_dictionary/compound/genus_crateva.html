genus Crateva

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs krəˈtiː.və / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs krəˈtiː.və / (BrE)
a genus of tropical small trees or shrubs in the family Capparaceae, known for their showy flowers
tropical genus of small trees or shrubs

Examples

Botanists identified the tree as belonging to the genus Crateva, common in tropical regions.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of shrubs in the genus Crateva.
The showy flowers of many trees in the genus Crateva attract numerous pollinators.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus Crateva, named after Cratevas, a Greek botanist.
