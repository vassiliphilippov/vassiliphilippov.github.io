two-time loser

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː taɪm ˈluːzər / (AmE), / ˈtuː taɪm ˈluːzə / (BrE)
a person who has failed two times, especially in relationships or important pursuits
person who has failed twice

Examples

After losing his job and his girlfriend, he considered himself a two-time loser.
The film tells the story of a two-time loser trying to turn his life around.
She laughed off her failed romances, calling herself a two-time loser in love.

Synset Examples

After failing twice, he felt like a two-time loser.
She joked about being a two-time loser in love.
Being labeled a two-time loser motivated him to try harder.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: loser - a person who fails to succeed
