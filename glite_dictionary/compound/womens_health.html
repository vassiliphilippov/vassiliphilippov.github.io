women's health

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪmɪnz hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈwɪmɪnz hɛlθ / (BrE)
the branch of medicine and healthcare concerned with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting the female body
medical care specific to females

Examples

Annual checkups are an important part of women's health and early detection of diseases.
The clinic specializes in women's health, offering services like family planning and prenatal care.
Many medical journals are dedicated to topics in women's health.

Synset Examples

Understanding the vagina is essential for women's health.
The gynotician discussed women's health issues at the seminar.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: men's health - medical care specific to males
  • Synonym: gynecology - the branch of medicine dealing with the female reproductive system
