Theodore Roosevelt National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈθiːəˌdɔːɹ ˈroʊzəˌvɛlt ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑɹk / (AmE), / ˈθiːədɔː ˈrəʊzəvɛlt ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
A national park in North Dakota, USA, named after President Theodore Roosevelt, encompassing the area of his former ranch and preserving prairie and badlands landscape.
national park in North Dakota, USA

Examples

We visited Theodore Roosevelt National Park last summer and hiked through the stunning badlands.
Bison and wild horses can be seen roaming freely in Theodore Roosevelt National Park.
Many tourists travel to North Dakota specifically to experience the scenery of Theodore Roosevelt National Park.

Etymology

Named after Theodore Roosevelt (1858–1919), 26th President of the United States, in honor of his conservation efforts.
