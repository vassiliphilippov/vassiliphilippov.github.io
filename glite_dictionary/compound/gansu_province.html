Gansu province

1. PROPN

/ ɡɑnˈsu ˈprɑːvɪns / (AmE), / ɡænˈsuː ˈprɒvɪns / (BrE)
a province in north-central China, formerly part of the Silk Road trade routes to Central and South Asia
province in north-central China

Examples

Many ancient relics have been discovered in Gansu province due to its historical importance on the Silk Road.
The arid landscape of Gansu province creates unique challenges for agriculture.
Tourists travel to Gansu province to see the famous Mogao Caves and desert scenery.
