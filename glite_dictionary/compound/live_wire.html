live wire

1. NOUN

/ laɪv waɪɚ / (AmE), / laɪv waɪə / (BrE)
A person who is very lively, energetic, and alert.
energetic or lively person

Examples

Everyone loves working with Jenna because she's such a live wire in the office.
The party really started when that live wire from marketing showed up.
He might be small, but on the field he's a real live wire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dynamo - someone who is very energetic and dynamic
  • Synonym: go-getter - an ambitious, energetic person who is determined to succeed
  • Antonym: sluggard - a lazy, slow-moving person

Etymology

From the image of an electric wire carrying a live current, figuratively applied to a person full of energy.
