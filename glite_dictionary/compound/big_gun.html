big gun

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɡʌn / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɡʌn / (BrE)
an important and influential person; someone considered powerful or significant in a particular area
important or influential person
informal

She quickly established herself as one of the big guns in academic research.
