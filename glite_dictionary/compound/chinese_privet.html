Chinese privet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.niz ˈprɪv.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz ˈprɪv.ɪt / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree native to China, Korea, and Japan, with pointed leaves and white flowers in long upright clusters; often grown as an ornamental plant
an evergreen shrub native to East Asia

Examples

The hedges of Chinese privet bordered the entire property, providing both privacy and beauty.
Invasive Chinese privet has spread rapidly throughout the southeastern United States.
Gardeners often prune Chinese privet to maintain its shape as an ornamental shrub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese privet - an evergreen shrub similar to Chinese privet, native to Japan
