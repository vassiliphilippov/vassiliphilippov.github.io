folding saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊldɪŋ sɔ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ sɔː / (BrE)
A portable saw with a toothed blade that folds into its handle for safety and convenience.
portable saw with folding blade

Examples

For camping trips, a folding saw is much easier to carry than a regular saw.
He used his folding saw to cut firewood for the campfire.
The folding saw safely tucks the blade into the handle when not in use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocketknife - small folding knife that fits in a pocket
