just another gringo

1. NOUN

/ dʒəst əˈnʌðɚ ˈɡrɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəst əˈnʌðə ˈɡrɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
an informal, sometimes dismissive term referring to a foreign tourist, especially an American, perceived as typical or undistinguished
stereotypical foreign tourist, esp. American
informal

Examples

Locals often roll their eyes when they see just another gringo taking selfies at the plaza.
Tired of being treated like just another gringo, he tried to learn some Spanish and fit in.
She didn't want to seem like just another gringo, so she made an effort to respect local customs.

Synset Examples

They joked that every tourist was just another gringo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gringo - a foreigner, especially an American, in Latin America

Etymology

gringo from Spanish, meaning foreigner, especially an English-speaking one.
