academic program

1. NOUN

/ əˌkædəmɪk ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / əˌkadəmɪk ˈprəʊɡram / (BrE)
a structured course of study in the liberal arts and sciences, usually in preparation for higher education
structured educational course for higher studies

Examples

The university offers a wide academic program in the liberal arts and sciences.
Enrolling in an academic program helped her prepare for medical school.
High school students can choose from several academic programs before applying to college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curriculum - the subjects comprising a course of study in a school or college
