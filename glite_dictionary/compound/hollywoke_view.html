Hollywoke view

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːliˌwoʊk vjuː / (AmE), / ˈhɒliˌwəʊk vjuː / (BrE)
A satirical or critical term for progressive social and political beliefs, especially those associated with celebrities or Hollywood.
satirical term for progressive stances in Hollywood
informal slang

Examples

Her Hollywoke view on the issue drew both praise and criticism.
Some critics argue that the film was made to reflect a Hollywoke view rather than artistic vision.
He dismissed their concerns as just another Hollywoke view from Hollywood elites.

Synset Examples

Her Hollywoke views sparked a lively debate online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woke - aware of social injustices and progressive causes

Etymology

Blend of 'Hollywood' and 'woke' (slang for socially aware), with 'view' meaning opinion.
