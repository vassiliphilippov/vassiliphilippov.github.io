big girl

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɡɝl / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl who has reached a stage of maturity, or an adult woman, often used to suggest responsibility or growing up
mature girl or adult woman
informal

Examples

Now that you're a big girl, you can tie your own shoes.
She handled the situation like a big girl and apologized for her mistake.
Her parents treated her as a big girl after her first day at college.

Synset Examples

Being a big girl means taking responsibility for your actions.
She’s grown into a big girl, ready for school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woman - an adult female human
  • Antonym: little girl - a young female child, not yet mature
