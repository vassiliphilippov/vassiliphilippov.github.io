baba au rhum

1. NOUN

/ bɑːˌbɑ oʊ ˈrʌm / (AmE), / baˌbaː əʊ ˈrʌm / (BrE)
A small yeast cake soaked in rum syrup, often served with whipped cream or fruit.
small cake soaked in rum syrup

Examples

For dessert, I ordered the baba au rhum, which was delightfully moist and fragrant.
The chef's signature baba au rhum is served with a dollop of fresh whipped cream.
She baked a traditional baba au rhum to impress her dinner guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rum baba - small yeast cake soaked in rum syrup (alternative English name)

Etymology

From French 'baba au rhum', meaning 'baba with rum', where 'baba' is a type of small cake.
