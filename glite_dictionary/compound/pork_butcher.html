pork butcher

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk ˈbʊtʃər / (AmE), / pɔːk ˈbʊtʃə / (BrE)
a butcher who sells pork and products made from pork
vendor of pork and pork products

Examples

The local pork butcher is famous for his homemade sausages and bacon.
After moving to the city, he found work as a pork butcher in a busy market.
Many traditional European towns still have a dedicated pork butcher shop in the center.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: butcher - a person whose trade is cutting up and selling meat
