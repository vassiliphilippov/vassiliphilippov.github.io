to track expenses

1. VERB

/ træk ɪkˈspɛnsɪz / (AmE), / træk ɪkˈspɛnsɪz / (BrE)
to monitor or record the amount of money that is spent, typically for budgeting and financial control purposes
monitor money spent

Examples

It's important to track expenses during a business trip to stay within budget.
Many people use apps to track expenses and manage their finances.
She started to track expenses more carefully after realizing how much she was spending each month.

Synset Examples

They decided to track their expenses this month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keep track of - to monitor or keep a record of something
