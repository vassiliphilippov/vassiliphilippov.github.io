brain disorder

1. NOUN

/ breɪn dɪˈsɔrdər / (AmE), / breɪn dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
any disorder or disease that affects the brain, impairing its normal functioning.
disorder or disease of the brain
medical

Examples

Alzheimer's disease is a common brain disorder among elderly people.
A healthy lifestyle may help reduce the risk of developing a brain disorder.
Medical research has led to new treatments for various brain disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurological disorder - a disorder of the nervous system, including the brain

2. NOUN

/ breɪn dɪˈsɔɹdɚ / (AmE), / breɪn dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition that impairs the normal function of the brain.
a medical condition affecting the brain
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a brain disorder after showing signs of memory loss and confusion.
New treatments for brain disorder are being developed in neuroscience laboratories around the world.
Head injuries can sometimes lead to a serious brain disorder requiring long-term care.

Synset Examples

His research focuses on neurology and brain disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurological disorder - disorder affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves
