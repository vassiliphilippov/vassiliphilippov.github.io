chichi gathering

1. NOUN

/ ʃiːˈʃiː ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃiːˈʃiː ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
An elegant or pretentious social event, often characterized by stylishness or ostentation.
stylish or pretentious social event
informal

Examples

They threw a lavish chichi gathering to impress their new neighbors.
At the chichi gathering, everyone wore designer clothes and sipped champagne.
He felt out of place at the chichi gathering with its exclusive guest list and fancy decor.

Synset Examples

She prefers more casual events over chichi gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soiree - an evening party or gathering, especially in a private house
  • Degree Lower: party - a social gathering of invited guests

Etymology

From French 'chichi' (affectedly elegant, pretentious).
