to find refuge

1. VERB

/ faɪnd ˈrɛfjudʒ / (AmE), / faɪnd ˈrɛfjuːdʒ / (BrE)
to seek or obtain a safe place or shelter from danger or hardship
seek safety or shelter

Examples

Many refugees find refuge in neighboring countries during times of conflict.
During the thunderstorm, hikers rushed to find refuge in a nearby cave.
After losing his home, he struggled to find refuge in the crowded city.

Synset Examples

We found refuge in the ark during the storm.
The soldiers took refuge in the citadel during the siege.
He took refuge in the bunker during the emergency drill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take refuge - to go to a place for safety or shelter
