suicide pact

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɪˌsaɪd pækt / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪˌsaɪd pakt / (BrE)
an agreement between two or more people to kill themselves together at a specific time and place
agreement to commit suicide together

Examples

The two lovers entered into a suicide pact after facing opposition from their families.
Police discovered the note outlining the details of their suicide pact.
The tragedy of the suicide pact left the community searching for answers.

Synset Examples

the two lovers killed themselves in a suicide pact

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pact - a formal agreement between two or more people or groups
