playful spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A lively and fun-loving personality that enjoys amusement and light-hearted activities.
lively, fun-loving personality

Examples

Her playful spirit always brings laughter to any gathering.
Even at work, his playful spirit lightens the mood of his colleagues.
The child's playful spirit was evident in her joyful games and constant laughter.

Synset Examples

His playful spirit reminded me of Tinkerbell from the movies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischievous - enjoying playful tricks and causing harmless trouble
  • Antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or not playful
