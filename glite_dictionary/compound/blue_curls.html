blue curls

1. NOUN

/ bluː kɝlz / (AmE), / bluː kɜːlz / (BrE)
any of several North American plants of the genus Trichostema, having whorls of small blue flowers
plant with small blue flowers (Trichostema genus)

Examples

Native bees are often attracted to the blue curls growing along the trail.
The delicate whorls of the blue curls added a splash of color to the garden.
She identified the wildflowers as blue curls based on their unique scent and blue blooms.
