butter dish

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə dɪʃ / (BrE)
A small dish, often with a cover, used to hold and serve butter at the dining table.
container for serving butter

Examples

She placed the butter dish in the center of the table for breakfast.
Our new ceramic butter dish keeps the butter fresh longer.
Please pass me the butter dish so I can add some to my toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butter - a pale yellow edible fatty substance made from cream and used as a spread or in cooking
