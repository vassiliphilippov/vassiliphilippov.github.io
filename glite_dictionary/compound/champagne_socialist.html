champagne socialist

1. NOUN

/ ʃæmˈpeɪn ˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ʃamˈpeɪn ˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who professes left-wing, socialist, or egalitarian views while enjoying a lifestyle perceived as luxurious or privileged.
leftist with a lavish lifestyle

Examples

Many accused the politician of being a champagne socialist because of his expensive tastes.
Despite calling herself a champagne socialist, she spent weekends at her country estate.
Critics labeled him a champagne socialist after he campaigned for workers' rights while flying first class.

Synset Examples

He enjoys luxury but claims to be a champagne socialist.
They criticized him for being a champagne socialist at the fundraiser.
Her lifestyle contradicts her views as a champagne socialist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limousine liberal - A wealthy liberal or progressive who is perceived as being hypocritical or disconnected from the experiences of ordinary people

Etymology

From 'champagne', a symbol of luxury, and 'socialist'. Used since the late 20th century in British and American English.
