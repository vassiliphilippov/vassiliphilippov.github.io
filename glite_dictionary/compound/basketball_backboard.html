basketball backboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ˈbækbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ˈbækbɔːd / (BrE)
a raised, usually rectangular board with a basket attached, used as a surface off which basketball players may bounce the ball to score points
vertical board with basket in basketball

Examples

The ball bounced off the basketball backboard and went into the net.
He practiced layups by aiming at different spots on the basketball backboard.
The gym installed a new glass basketball backboard for the tournament.

Synset Examples

he banked the shot off the backboard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backboard - the board behind the basket in basketball
