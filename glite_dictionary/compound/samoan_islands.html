Samoan Islands

1. PROPN

/ səˈmoʊ.ən ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / səˈməʊ.ən ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
a group of volcanic islands in the South Pacific, known for their tropical climate, scenic landscapes, and Polynesian culture
volcanic islands in the South Pacific

Examples

The Samoan Islands are famous for their stunning beaches and vibrant Polynesian traditions.
Many travelers visit the Samoan Islands to experience their unique mix of culture and natural beauty.
Ecologists study the biodiversity of the Samoan Islands due to their volcanic origin and diverse habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Samoa - the western part of the Samoan Islands, an independent nation
  • Synonym: American Samoa - an unincorporated US territory in the eastern Samoan Islands
