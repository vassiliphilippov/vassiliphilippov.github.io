turd burglar

1. NOUN

/ tɝd ˈbɝːɡlər / (AmE), / tɜːd ˈbɜːɡlə / (BrE)
(slang) An insult for someone who is sneaky or dishonest, usually in a humorous or playful way.
slang insult for a sneaky or dishonest person
slang informal vulgar

Examples

My roommate hid my keys and called himself a turd burglar when I couldn't find them.
Stop being such a turd burglar and give me back my phone charger!
They all laughed when Jen was crowned the office turd burglar for stealing snacks.

Synset Examples

She joked that he was acting like a turd burglar by taking her dessert.
The prankster was labeled the turd burglar of the group.
He laughed, calling his friend a turd burglar for stealing his fries.

Related Terms
