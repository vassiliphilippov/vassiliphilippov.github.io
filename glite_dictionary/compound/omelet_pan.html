omelet pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑm.lət ˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈɒm.lɪt ˌpæn / (BrE)
A shallow pan with a long handle, used especially for cooking omelets.
shallow pan for cooking omelets

Examples

He bought a new omelet pan to try making breakfast for his family.
You should use an omelet pan for best results when cooking a fluffy omelet.
The chef reached for his favorite omelet pan before starting the next order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frying pan - flat-bottomed pan used for frying food
