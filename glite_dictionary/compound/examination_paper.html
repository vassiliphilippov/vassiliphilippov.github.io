examination paper

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃən ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃən ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A set of written questions used in a formal test to assess knowledge or skill.
test document with written questions

Examples

The students turned in their examination paper at the end of the two-hour test.
Each examination paper must be signed by the invigilator before being submitted.
She studied hard to make sure she could answer every question on the examination paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exam - A formal test of a person's knowledge or proficiency
