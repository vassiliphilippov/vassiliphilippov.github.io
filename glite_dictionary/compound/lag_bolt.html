lag bolt

1. NOUN

/ læɡ boʊlt / (AmE), / læɡ bəʊlt / (BrE)
a heavy screw with a square or hexagonal head, used for fastening heavy timbers, and driven in with a wrench
heavy screw with square or hex head, driven by wrench

Examples

The contractor tightened the lag bolt to secure the beam in place.
Before hanging the heavy shelf, make sure to use a lag bolt for extra support.
She replaced the rusted lag bolt with a new one during the repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lag screw - heavy screw similar to a lag bolt, used for fastening wood
