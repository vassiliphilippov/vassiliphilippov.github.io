helldesk staff

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlˌdɛsk stæf / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌdɛsk stɑːf / (BrE)
customer support or technical staff, often humorously or cynically so-called, tasked with handling user inquiries and troubleshooting problems
customer support or technical staff (humorous/slang)
informal slang

Examples

The helldesk staff struggled to keep up with calls during the outage.
If you need your password reset, just contact the helldesk staff.
Working as helldesk staff can be both stressful and rewarding.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpdesk staff - customer support or technical staff

Etymology

Blend of 'helldesk' (a humorous or sardonic alteration of 'helpdesk') + 'staff'.
