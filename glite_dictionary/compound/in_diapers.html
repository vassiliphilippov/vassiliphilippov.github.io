in diapers

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈdaɪpɚz / (AmE), / ɪn ˈdaɪpəz / (BrE)
wearing diapers, especially referring to infants or young children who have not yet been toilet trained.
wearing diapers (for infants)

Examples

Their youngest child is still in diapers, so they're not planning any long trips.
When I met her, she was just a toddler in diapers.
Some daycare centers specialize in caring for children who are in diapers.

Synset Examples

The baby was crawling around the living room in diapers.
