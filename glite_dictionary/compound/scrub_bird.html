scrub bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrʌb bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈskrʌb bɜːd / (BrE)
a small, fast-running Australian bird resembling a wren, found in brush or scrubby habitats
small Australian bird found in scrub

Examples

The rare scrub bird was spotted darting among the dense underbrush.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered scrub bird.
We heard the distinctive call of a scrub bird while hiking through the Australian bush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wren - small, active songbird with a short upright tail
