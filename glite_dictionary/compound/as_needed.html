as needed

1. ADV

/ æz ˈniːdɪd / (AmE), / æz ˈniːdɪd / (BrE)
as required; when and only when necessary or called for
as required; when necessary

Examples

Adjust the seasoning as needed before serving the soup.
You can take painkillers as needed for headaches.
Apply more glue as needed until the parts are secure.
