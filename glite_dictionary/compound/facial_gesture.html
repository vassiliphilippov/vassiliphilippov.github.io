facial gesture

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl ˈdʒɛstʃər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈdʒɛstʃə / (BrE)
A movement or expression made using the muscles of the face, often to convey emotion or communicate nonverbally.
expression or movement using facial muscles

Examples

She made a facial gesture of surprise when she heard the news.
His disapproving facial gesture was obvious even though he said nothing.
Actors often practice different facial gestures to convey a range of emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facial expression - a form of nonverbal communication using the face
  • Degree Higher: gesture - a movement of part of the body to express an idea or feeling
