ulcer diet

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌlsɚ ˌdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈʌlsə ˌdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting of foods that are gentle on the digestive system and do not irritate ulcers
diet for ulcer sufferers
medical

Examples

After being diagnosed with a stomach ulcer, his doctor recommended an ulcer diet to reduce discomfort.
She carefully followed an ulcer diet, avoiding spicy and acidic foods altogether.
Hospitals often provide patients recovering from surgery with an ulcer diet to prevent digestive irritation.

Synset Examples

he ate a bland diet because of his colitis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bland diet - a diet with foods that avoid irritation to the digestive tract
