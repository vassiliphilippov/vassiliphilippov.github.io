volleyball net

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑliˌbɔl nɛt / (AmE), / ˈvɒlibɔːl nɛt / (BrE)
A high net used in volleyball to separate the two teams, over which the ball must be hit during play.
net used in volleyball to divide teams

Examples

The referee checked that the volleyball net was set at the correct height before the match started.
She accidentally touched the volleyball net during the serve, resulting in a fault.
They repaired the torn volleyball net before continuing their game on the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: net - an open-meshed fabric, cord, or rope, often used in sports or as a barrier
