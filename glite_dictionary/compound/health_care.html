health care

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ˌkeə / (BrE)
the organized provision of medical services to individuals or a community for maintaining or improving health
provision of medical services

Examples

Access to affordable health care remains a major issue in many countries.
The new policy provides improved health care coverage for all residents.
She works in the health care industry as a nurse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine - the science or practice of treating illness and injuries
  • Synonym: public health - the science of protecting the health of populations

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ˌkeə / (BrE)
the organized provision of medical services to maintain or improve a person's physical and mental well-being
organized medical services for health

Examples

Access to quality health care is essential for a thriving community.
The rising cost of health care is a major concern in many countries.
She works in health care as a pediatric nurse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical care - the professional treatment for illness or injury
