hermit thrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜːrmɪt θrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːmɪt θrʌʃ / (BrE)
A North American songbird, noted for its complex and melodious song, with a brown back and spotted breast.
North American songbird with a beautiful song

Examples

We heard the hermit thrush singing beautifully in the forest at dawn.
The hermit thrush is often admired for its haunting song, especially during spring mornings.
Many birdwatchers travel to North America just to spot the elusive hermit thrush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrush - a songbird of the family Turdidae, typically with a spotted breast
