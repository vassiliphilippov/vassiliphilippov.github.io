cotton pony

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːtən ˈpoʊni / (AmE), / ˈkɒtən ˈpəʊni / (BrE)
a small, gentle pony typically used for children to ride, especially at fairs or events
gentle pony for children

Examples

All the kids at the birthday party wanted to ride the cotton pony in the petting zoo.
The fair featured a friendly cotton pony for children and beginners to try.
She remembers her first time on a cotton pony at the county festival.

Synset Examples

She was thrilled to ride the cotton pony for her birthday.
At the festival, kids can ride the cotton pony all day long.
