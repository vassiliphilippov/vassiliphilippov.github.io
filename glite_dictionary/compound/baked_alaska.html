baked Alaska

1. NOUN

/ beɪkt əˈlæskə / (AmE), / beɪkt əˈlæskə / (BrE)
a dessert consisting of cake and ice cream covered with meringue, which is browned quickly in an oven
ice cream cake dessert with browned meringue

Examples

The restaurant's signature dessert is baked Alaska, flambéed at your table.
She prepared a baked Alaska for her dinner guests, impressing everyone with the dramatic presentation.
I've never tried making baked Alaska before because it seems quite complicated.

Etymology

Named after the U.S. state of Alaska; originated in the 19th century to highlight the contrast of hot and cold.
