wandering albatross

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑndərɪŋ ˈælbəˌtrɔs / (AmE), / ˈwɒndərɪŋ ˈæl.bə.trɒs / (BrE)
a very large seabird of the albatross family, with mainly white plumage and long, wide black wings, known for its extensive range and ability to fly great distances
very large white seabird with black wings

Examples

The wandering albatross has the largest wingspan of any living bird.
We spotted a wandering albatross soaring above the Southern Ocean.
The wandering albatross can glide for hours without flapping its wings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - a large seabird with long narrow wings
