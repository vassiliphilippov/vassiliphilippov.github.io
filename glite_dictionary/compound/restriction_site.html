restriction site

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈstrɪkʃən saɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪkʃən sʌɪt / (BrE)
a specific sequence on DNA where a restriction enzyme can cut the molecule
site on DNA cut by restriction enzyme
medical

Examples

The EcoRI enzyme cuts DNA only at its specific restriction site.
Mutation of a single base pair can eliminate a restriction site from a DNA molecule.
Scientists use restriction sites to clone genes into plasmids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restriction enzyme - an enzyme that cuts DNA at or near specific recognition sites
