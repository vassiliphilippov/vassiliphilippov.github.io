armored catfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmɚd ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A South American catfish with the body covered in bony plates, often found in freshwater habitats.
South American catfish with bony plates

Examples

The aquarium has several armored catfish living peacefully with other tropical species.
Some armored catfish can adapt well to life in home fish tanks.
The river's bottom is home to different kinds of armored catfish, easily identified by their hard bony plates.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: catfish - a freshwater or marine fish with whisker-like barbels around the mouth
