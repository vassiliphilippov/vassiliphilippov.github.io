liquor store

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkər stɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɪkə stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells alcoholic beverages, such as wine, beer, or spirits, for consumption off the premises.
shop selling alcoholic drinks for take-away

Examples

We stopped by the liquor store to pick up a bottle of wine for dinner.
There's a new liquor store opening downtown next week.
He works part-time at a liquor store near his apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-licence - a British term for a shop selling alcoholic drinks for consumption elsewhere
