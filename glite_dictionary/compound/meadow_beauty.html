meadow beauty

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdoʊ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a North American wildflower of the genus Rhexia, usually with pink-purple to magenta flowers, commonly found in moist meadows and fields
North American wildflower (Rhexia genus)

Examples

We spotted a cluster of meadow beauty blooming along the edge of the pond.
The vibrant flowers of the meadow beauty attract numerous pollinators in the summer months.
Botanists are working to conserve habitats where the meadow beauty naturally thrives.
