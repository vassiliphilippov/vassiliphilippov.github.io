fifth part

1. NOUN

/ fɪfθ pɑrt / (AmE), / fɪfθ pɑːt / (BrE)
One of five equal portions into which something is divided; 1/5 of a whole.
one of five equal parts; 1/5

Examples

Each student was given a fifth part of the cake to make the division fair.
A fifth part of the voters chose the third candidate in the election.
The land was split into five sections, and each heir received a fifth part.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one fifth - one part in five equal parts; 1/5
  • Degree Higher: quarter - one part in four equal parts; 1/4
