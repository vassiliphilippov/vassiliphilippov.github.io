stapling machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪplɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈsteɪplɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A device used to fasten sheets of paper together by inserting and clinching a metal staple.
device for fastening paper with staples

Examples

She used the stapling machine to attach all the reports together before the meeting.
The office recently purchased an electric stapling machine to speed up paperwork processing.
Please fix the stapling machine; it's jamming every time I try to use it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stapler - A small, hand-operated device for putting staples into paper
