pubic region

1. NOUN

/ ˈpjuːbɪk ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbɪk ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
the lower part of the abdomen just above the external genital organs
lower abdomen above genitals

Examples

Medical professionals often examine the pubic region for signs of injury or infection.
Hair growth typically begins in the pubic region during puberty.
He experienced pain in his pubic region after a minor accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groin - the area between the abdomen and the thigh

Etymology

From Latin 'pubis' (the pubic bone) and 'region' (area).
