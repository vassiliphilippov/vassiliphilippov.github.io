Republic of El Salvador

1. PROPN

/ ɪl sæl.vəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ɛl sæl.vəˈdɔː / (BrE)
A country in Central America, located on the Pacific coast, officially known as the Republic of El Salvador.
country in Central America

Examples

The Republic of El Salvador experienced significant political changes in the late 20th century.
Coffee is one of the main exports of the Republic of El Salvador.
Many tourists are drawn to the Pacific beaches of the Republic of El Salvador.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: El Salvador - country in Central America

Etymology

From Spanish 'El Salvador' meaning 'The Savior'.
