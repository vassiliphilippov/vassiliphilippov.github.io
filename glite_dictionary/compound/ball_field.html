ball field

1. NOUN

/ bɔl fiːld / (AmE), / bɔːl fiːld / (BrE)
A field or ground used for playing baseball.
a field for playing baseball

Examples

The little league team met for practice every afternoon at the ball field.
Freshly mowed grass covered the ball field after the rainstorm.
Spectators filled the stands surrounding the ball field on opening day.
