defense system

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of weapons and related technology designed to protect a region from attack.
equipment and technologies for regional defense

Examples

The country invested heavily in a new defense system to protect its borders from missile attacks.
Engineers are constantly upgrading the defense system to keep up with emerging threats.
Every military base has its own defense system tailored to the surrounding environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weapons system - a coordinated set of weapons and equipment operated together
  • Antonym: offensive system - a system designed for attacking rather than defending
