UNIX guru

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːnɪks ˈɡuːruː / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪks ˈɡʊəruː / (BrE)
an expert or authority on the UNIX operating system
expert on UNIX operating system

Examples

Whenever we hit a server issue, we call our UNIX guru for help.
She built a reputation as the office UNIX guru after solving several difficult problems.
The company's UNIX guru taught the new engineers how to manage the system efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expert - someone with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation

Etymology

UNIX, name of the operating system, plus 'guru' from Sanskrit (teacher, expert).
