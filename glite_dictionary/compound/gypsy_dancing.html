gypsy dancing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪpsi ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsi ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dance typical of Andalusian Gypsies, marked by vigorous, rhythmic movements and energetic clapping and foot stamping.
traditional Andalusian Gypsy dance style

Examples

The performance of gypsy dancing captivated the entire audience with its fiery energy.
She learned gypsy dancing during her travels across southern Spain.
Gypsy dancing often features dramatic clapping and fast footwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flamenco - a traditional Spanish art form involving song and dance, closely related to gypsy dancing
