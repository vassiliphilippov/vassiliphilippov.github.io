Pimlico Race Course

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪm.lɪˌkoʊ reɪs ˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈpɪm.lɪˌkəʊ reɪs ˌkɔːs / (BrE)
A famous racetrack in Baltimore, Maryland, known for hosting the Preakness Stakes in thoroughbred horse racing.
historic horse racing track in Baltimore

Examples

Thousands of fans gathered at the Pimlico Race Course to watch the annual Preakness Stakes.
The historic Pimlico Race Course has been a staple of the Baltimore sporting scene for over a century.
She always dreamed of seeing a race at the legendary Pimlico Race Course one day.
