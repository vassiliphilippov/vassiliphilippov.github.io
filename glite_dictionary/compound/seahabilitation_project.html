seahabilitation project

1. NOUN

/ siː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən ˈprɑː.dʒekt / (AmE), / siː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən ˈprɒ.dʒɛkt / (BrE)
a project focused on restoring and rehabilitating marine ecosystems, such as coral reefs or seagrass beds
marine ecosystem restoration project

Examples

The government increased funding for a seahabilitation project along the coastline.
Scientists collaborated with local communities on a seahabilitation project to restore seagrass beds.
A successful seahabilitation project can boost marine biodiversity and support sustainable fisheries.

Synset Examples

They launched a seahabilitation project to restore coral reefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marine conservation - protection and preservation of ecosystems in oceans and seas
  • Synonym: restoration project - a project aimed at returning an ecosystem to its original state

Etymology

Blend of 'sea' and 'rehabilitation,' referring to ecological restoration in marine environments.
