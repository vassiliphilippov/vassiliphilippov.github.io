Republic of Lithuania

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌlɪθuˈeɪniə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌlɪθjuˈeɪniə / (BrE)
the official name of Lithuania, a country in northeastern Europe on the Baltic Sea, with its capital at Vilnius
official name of the country Lithuania in Europe

Examples

The Republic of Lithuania declared its independence from the Soviet Union in 1990.
Vilnius is the capital and largest city of the Republic of Lithuania.
Tourists often visit the beautiful Baltic coastline of the Republic of Lithuania.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lithuania - a country in northeastern Europe on the Baltic Sea
  • Synonym: Vilnius - the capital city of Lithuania

Etymology

From Latin 'Lituania', the name of the region, and 'res publica', meaning 'public affair' or 'republic'.
