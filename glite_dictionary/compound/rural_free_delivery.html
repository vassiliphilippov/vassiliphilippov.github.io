rural free delivery

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʊr.əl fri dɪˈlɪv.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈrʊə.rəl fri dɪˈlɪv.ər.i / (BrE)
a government service providing free delivery of mail to residents in rural areas, especially in the United States
free mail delivery service in rural areas

Examples

The introduction of rural free delivery transformed communications for farmers across the United States.
Many small towns benefited economically when rural free delivery routes were established in their areas.
Prior to rural free delivery, rural residents had to travel long distances to collect their mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parcel post - postal service for sending parcels and packages
