contagious enthusiasm

1. NOUN

/ kənˈteɪdʒəs ɪnˈθuziæzəm / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
enthusiasm that is easily transmitted to and inspires others
enthusiasm that spreads to others

Examples

Her contagious enthusiasm motivated the entire team to work harder.
It's his contagious enthusiasm that makes him such a great teacher.
The crowd responded to her contagious enthusiasm with cheers and applause.

Synset Examples

He had a contagious enthusiasm that inspired others.
Nigel's enthusiasm was contagious during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infectious enthusiasm - enthusiasm that is easily spread to others
