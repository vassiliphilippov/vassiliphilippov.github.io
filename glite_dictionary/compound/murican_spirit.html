Murican spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɚɪkən ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɜːrɪkən ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A humorous or informal reference to American patriotism, values, and national character, often exaggerating or parodying stereotypical aspects of American culture.
humorous term for American patriotism or culture
informal slang american

Examples

The Murican spirit was evident during the Independence Day celebrations.
With a display of fireworks and barbecue, their Murican spirit could not be questioned.
Online memes often poke fun at exaggerated Murican spirit with images of bald eagles and flags.

Etymology

Mock-phonetic spelling of 'American,' used humorously to mimic exaggerated or stereotypical American speech.
