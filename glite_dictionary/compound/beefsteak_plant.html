beefsteak plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːfˌsteɪk plænt / (AmE), / ˈbiːfˌsteɪk plɑːnt / (BrE)
an Asian annual herb (Perilla frutescens) grown for its ornamental red or purple foliage
ornamental plant with red or purple leaves

Examples

We planted a beefsteak plant in the garden for its striking purple foliage.
The beefsteak plant thrives best in partial shade and moist soil.
Her salad was garnished with leaves from the beefsteak plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perilla - another name for the beefsteak plant or related species

Etymology

Named for the red color of its leaves, which resemble raw beefsteak.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbifˌsteɪk plænt / (AmE), / ˈbiːfsteɪk plɑːnt / (BrE)
A South American ornamental herb with green to purple or red branches, colorful foliage, and spikes of insignificant woolly flowers.
South American ornamental plant

Examples

The beefsteak plant is often grown for its striking purple foliage in garden borders.
Gardeners prize the beefsteak plant for its unique color and decorative value.
She planted a beefsteak plant beside the walkway to add a tropical touch.
