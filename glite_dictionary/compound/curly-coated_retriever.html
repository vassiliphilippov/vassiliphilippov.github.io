curly-coated retriever

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɝːli ˌkoʊtɪd rɪˈtrivər / (AmE), / ˌkɜːli ˌkəʊtɪd rɪˈtriːvə / (BrE)
a breed of large, strong dog from England with a tightly curled black or liver-colored coat, known for retrieving game from land or water
English breed of retriever with curly coat

Examples

The curly-coated retriever is well known for its intelligence and stamina on hunting trips.
She brought her curly-coated retriever to the dog show and won first prize.
A curly-coated retriever requires regular grooming to keep its distinct coat in good condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retriever - dog bred to retrieve game
