grammatical category

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈmætɪkəl ˈkætəˌɡɔri / (AmE), / ɡrəˈmatɪk(ə)l ˈkatəɡəri / (BrE)
(Linguistics) A class of words or forms distinguished by shared grammatical properties, such as tense, gender, or number.
class of words/forms by grammatical properties

Examples

Tense is an important grammatical category in English verbs.
Gender and number are common grammatical categories in many languages.
The teacher explained how each grammatical category affects sentence structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grammatical class - a group of words with common grammatical properties
  • Synonym: part of speech - category such as noun, verb, etc.
