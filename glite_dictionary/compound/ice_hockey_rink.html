ice hockey rink

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈhɑki rɪŋk / (AmE), / aɪs ˈhɒki rɪŋk / (BrE)
A specially prepared ice surface with marked lines and goals, used for playing ice hockey.
rink for playing ice hockey

Examples

The teams met at the local ice hockey rink for their regular Saturday night game.
After school, she practiced her skating at the ice hockey rink.
Building an ice hockey rink requires careful attention to temperature and surface quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rink - a smooth, flat area of ice for skating
