coin toss

1. NOUN

/ kɔɪn tɑs / (AmE), / kɔɪn tɒs / (BrE)
an act of throwing a coin in the air and making a decision based on which side lands facing up
decision by flipping a coin

Examples

We settled the dispute with a coin toss to keep things fair.
The referee used a coin toss to decide which team would kick off.
It all came down to a coin toss in the final round.

Synset Examples

A coin toss determined who would go first in the game.
They settled the argument with a simple coin toss.
The winner of the match was decided by a coin toss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coin flip - an act of tossing a coin to make a decision
