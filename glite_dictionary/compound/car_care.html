car care

1. NOUN

/ kɑr kɛr / (AmE), / kɑː kɛə / (BrE)
the practice of maintaining a car in good condition and working order through regular cleaning, servicing, and repairs
maintenance of a car in good condition

Examples

Proper car care extends the life of your vehicle and prevents costly repairs.
She takes pride in her car care, making sure everything runs smoothly.
Following a regular car care routine can improve your car's performance and safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maintenance - the process of keeping something in good condition
