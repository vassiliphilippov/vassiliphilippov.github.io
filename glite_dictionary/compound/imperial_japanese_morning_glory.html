imperial Japanese morning glory

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɪriəl dʒəˈpæniz ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌɡlɔri / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl dʒəˈpæniːz ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌɡlɔːri / (BrE)
a cultivated variety of morning glory (Ipomoea nil) popular in Japan, known for its showy, multicolored flowers and ornamental foliage
a decorative morning glory variety (Ipomoea nil)

Examples

He grows several imperial Japanese morning glories on his balcony for their brilliant blooms.
The imperial Japanese morning glory is prized for its intricate patterns and vibrant pinks, blues, and purples.
Gardeners showcase their rare imperial Japanese morning glory cultivars at the annual flower festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morning glory - a climbing plant with showy trumpet-shaped flowers
