physical ability

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl əˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl əˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the capacity to perform physical tasks or actions, as distinct from mental ability.
capacity to perform physical tasks

Examples

Her physical ability allowed her to complete the race ahead of most competitors.
Due to his injury, his physical ability was temporarily reduced, but he soon recovered.
Tests were conducted to measure both physical ability and mental performance in the participants.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mental ability - the capacity to think, understand, and process information
