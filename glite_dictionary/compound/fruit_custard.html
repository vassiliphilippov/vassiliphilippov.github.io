fruit custard

1. NOUN

/ fruːt ˈkʌstɚd / (AmE), / fruːt ˈkʌstəd / (BrE)
a dessert made by combining a sweet custard with pieces of fruit
dessert of custard with fruit

Examples

We served fruit custard for dessert at the dinner party.
My grandmother taught me how to make fruit custard using fresh mangoes and bananas.
Chilled fruit custard is especially popular during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard - a sweet or savory dish made with milk, eggs, and sugar, usually thickened by heat
