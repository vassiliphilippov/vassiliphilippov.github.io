less-traveled

1. ADJ

/ lɛsˈtrævəld / (AmE), / lɛsˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a place) not visited by many travelers; little frequented or explored.
not visited by many travelers

Examples

She dreamed of exploring less-traveled paths far from the crowds.
The guidebook focuses on destinations that are less-traveled and more authentic.
Many adventurers prefer the thrill of discovering less-traveled regions.

Synset Examples

the tourist's desire to visit less-traveled countries

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-traveled - visited by many travelers
