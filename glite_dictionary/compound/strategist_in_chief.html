strategist in chief

1. NOUN

/ ˈstræt̬ədʒɪst ɪn tʃiːf / (AmE), / ˈstratɪdʒɪst ɪn tʃiːf / (BrE)
The person who is the principal or main strategist, responsible for leading planning and strategy in an organization or project.
chief strategist or main planner

Examples

As the strategist in chief, she devised the company's long-term growth plan.
He was appointed strategist in chief after years of successful campaigns.
The team's strategist in chief coordinated every step of their moves.

Synset Examples

He is the strategist in chief for our project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief strategist - principal strategist in an organization
