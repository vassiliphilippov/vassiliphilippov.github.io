soda jerker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌdʒɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌdʒɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who operates a soda fountain and serves drinks, especially in a drugstore or ice cream parlor
person operating a soda fountain
archaic

Examples

In the 1950s, my grandfather worked as a soda jerker at the local drugstore.
The soda jerker crafted a perfect root beer float for every customer.
Many teenagers once found summer jobs as a soda jerker in American diners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soda fountain - a counter where soft drinks and ice cream are served

Etymology

From 'soda' (a carbonated drink) and 'jerker,' referring to the pulling or 'jerking' of the soda fountain handle.
