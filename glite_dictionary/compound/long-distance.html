long-distance

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋ ˌdɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ ˌdɪstəns / (BrE)
covering or involving a large distance; operating over a long distance, especially in reference to travel, transport, or communication.
covering or operating over a large distance

Examples

He is training to become a long-distance runner specializing in marathons.
The long-distance train takes passengers across several states overnight.
They managed to maintain a long-distance relationship for over two years.

Synset Examples

a long-distance runner
a long-distance freight train
she ran off with a long-distance truck driver

Related Terms

  • Antonym: short-distance - covering or operating over a short distance
