bite plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪt pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪt pleɪt / (BrE)
a removable dental appliance placed on the palate, used for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes
removable dental appliance for diagnosis or therapy
medical

Examples

The dentist recommended a bite plate to help with her jaw alignment issues.
Wearing a bite plate at night can reduce teeth grinding and associated discomfort.
He was fitted with a bite plate to aid in his orthodontic treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retainer - removable dental appliance that helps keep teeth in position
