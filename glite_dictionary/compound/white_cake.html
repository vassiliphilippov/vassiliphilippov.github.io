white cake

1. NOUN

/ waɪt keɪk / (AmE), / waɪt keɪk / (BrE)
A light, pale-colored cake made without egg yolks, typically using egg whites, flour, sugar, and butter.
pale cake made without egg yolks

Examples

She baked a delicious white cake for the birthday party.
The recipe calls for egg whites only to achieve the perfect white cake texture.
I prefer white cake over chocolate cake for weddings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: yellow cake - cake made with whole eggs, resulting in a yellow color
  • Synonym: sponge cake - light, airy cake made with eggs but often whole eggs
