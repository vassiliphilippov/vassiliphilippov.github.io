two times

1. ADV

/ tu taɪmz / (AmE), / tuː taɪmz / (BrE)
by a factor of two; twice as much or as many
by a factor of two

Examples

Their profits grew two times over the last quarter.
You need to mix two times the normal amount of water for this recipe.
The new computer is two times faster than the previous model.

Synset Examples

the price increased twofold last year

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twice - two times, on two occasions or by a factor of two
  • Synonym: twofold - by two times as much or as many
