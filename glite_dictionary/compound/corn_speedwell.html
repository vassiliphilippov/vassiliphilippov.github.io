corn speedwell

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (BrE)
an annual plant with small blue flowers, typically found in waste places in Europe and America
blue-flowered annual plant

Examples

A patch of corn speedwell was growing by the side of the road.
The delicate blue flowers of corn speedwell are easy to spot in early spring.
Gardeners often remove corn speedwell as it can spread quickly in disturbed soil.
