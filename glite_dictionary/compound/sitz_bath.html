sitz bath

1. NOUN

/ sɪts bæθ / (AmE), / sɪts bɑːθ / (BrE)
a shallow bath in which only the hips and buttocks are immersed in water, typically used for therapeutic purposes
shallow bath for hips and buttocks
medical

Examples

After her surgery, the doctor recommended a sitz bath to help reduce discomfort and promote healing.
Nurses often advise patients to use a sitz bath for relief from hemorrhoids.
You can prepare a warm sitz bath at home using a special basin that fits over the toilet.

Etymology

From German 'Sitzbad', meaning 'sitting bath' ('sitzen': to sit, 'Bad': bath).
