barrel maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɹəl ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbærəl ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs wooden barrels or tubs
maker of wooden barrels or tubs

Examples

The village once relied on its skilled barrel maker for storing wine and grain.
As a young apprentice, he learned the secrets of a barrel maker.
The old barrel maker still uses traditional tools in his workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooper - person who makes or repairs barrels and casks
