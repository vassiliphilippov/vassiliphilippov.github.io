light breeze

1. NOUN

/ laɪt briːz / (AmE), / laɪt briːz / (BrE)
A wind blowing at a speed of 4-7 knots (2 on the Beaufort scale), felt on the face and rustling leaves.
wind at 4-7 knots (Beaufort 2)

Examples

A light breeze stirred the curtains in the early morning sun.
It was perfect weather for sailing, with just a light breeze to fill the sails.
The leaves trembled in the light breeze that swept through the park.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gentle breeze - A breeze of 8-12 knots (Beaufort scale 3)
  • Degree Lower: calm - No noticeable wind
