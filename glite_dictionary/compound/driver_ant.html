driver ant

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvər ænt / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvə ænt / (BrE)
a large, nomadic tropical ant of central and eastern Africa that travels in columns and preys mainly on other insects, known for aggressive swarming behavior
tropical nomadic predatory ant

Examples

The jungle floor erupted with driver ants moving in a relentless line.
Researchers observed a colony of driver ants hunting for insects across the forest.
Villagers quickly moved aside when they saw driver ant swarms approaching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: army ant - a collective term for several nomadic ant species that hunt in coordinated groups
