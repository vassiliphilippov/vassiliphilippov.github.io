individual creativity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.dəˈvɪd͡ʒ.u.əl ˌkriː.eɪˈtɪv.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈvɪd.ju.əl ˌkriː.eɪˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the unique creative ability or expression shown by a single person, especially as distinct from group or collective creativity
a person's unique creative ability

Examples

Many artists believe that individual creativity is essential for innovation in any field.
Some workplaces encourage individual creativity by allowing flexible work hours and open-ended projects.
Education systems that nurture individual creativity often produce more independent and critical thinkers.

Synset Examples

Critics argue that corporatism stifles individual creativity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creativity - the ability to produce original and unusual ideas
