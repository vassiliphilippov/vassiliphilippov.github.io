narrow-leaved everlasting pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæroʊ liːvd ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ piː / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːvd ˌɛvəlˈɑːstɪŋ piː / (BrE)
A European perennial plant (Lathyrus sylvestris) with purple and pink mottled flowers, sometimes grown for fodder or as green manure.
European perennial plant with purple-pink mottled flowers

Examples

The narrow-leaved everlasting pea is often used as green manure to enrich the soil.
Gardeners appreciate the vibrant colors of the narrow-leaved everlasting pea when it blooms in late summer.
Farmers sometimes cultivate the narrow-leaved everlasting pea to provide fodder for livestock.
