plant life

1. NOUN

/ plænt laɪf / (AmE), / plɑːnt laɪf / (BrE)
the collective plants of a particular region, environment, or period; vegetation
the plants in a region; vegetation

Examples

The plant life in the rainforest is astonishingly diverse and colorful.
Pollution can seriously damage the plant life in lakes and rivers.
After the fire, the plant life slowly began to recover over the following years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetation - plants collectively, especially in a particular area
  • Antonym: animal life - animals collectively in a particular region or environment
