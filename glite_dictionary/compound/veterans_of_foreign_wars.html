Veterans of Foreign Wars

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɛt̬.ɚ.ənz əv ˈfɔr.ən ˈwɔrz / (AmE), / ˈvɛt.ər.ənz əv ˈfɒr.ən ˈwɔːz / (BrE)
an organization of United States military veterans who have served in foreign wars, dedicated to supporting veterans and promoting patriotism
US veterans' organization for those who served overseas
american

Examples

My grandfather has been a member of the Veterans of Foreign Wars for over forty years.
The local Veterans of Foreign Wars post hosts a charity dinner every Memorial Day.
