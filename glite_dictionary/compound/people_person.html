people person

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəl ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who enjoys being with and interacting with other people
outgoing, sociable person

Examples

As a people person, Jane was always the first to welcome new colleagues to the office.
His skills as a people person made him perfect for customer service roles.
You can tell she's a people person by how easily she connects with strangers.

Synset Examples

She's definitely a people person, always making new friends.
Being a people person helps her in her sales job.
As a people person, he thrives in social gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: introvert - a person preferring solitude or small groups to large social gatherings
  • Synonym: extrovert - a person who is outgoing and socially confident
