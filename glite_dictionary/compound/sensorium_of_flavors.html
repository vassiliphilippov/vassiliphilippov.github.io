sensorium of flavors

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈsɔriəm əv ˈfleɪvərz / (AmE), / sɛnˈsɔːrɪəm əv ˈfleɪvəz / (BrE)
a vivid and intense collection of flavor sensations experienced simultaneously
vivid collection of flavor sensations

Examples

The chef's tasting menu was a true sensorium of flavors, each dish more exciting than the last.
As the spices blended on her tongue, she felt immersed in a sensorium of flavors.
Exploring the new fusion restaurant was like stepping into a sensorium of flavors.

Synset Examples

Walking through the market was an overwhelming sensorium of flavors.

Etymology

From Latin 'sensorium' (seat of sensation) and English 'flavor'.
