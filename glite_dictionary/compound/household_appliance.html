household appliance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌhoʊld əˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈhaʊshəʊld əˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
A device or machine, especially one powered by electricity, used to perform a specific task in the home, such as cleaning, cooking, or food preservation.
electric device used for home tasks

Examples

The store offers a wide range of household appliances, from refrigerators to washing machines.
She replaced all the old household appliances in her kitchen with modern, energy-efficient models.
Proper maintenance can extend the life of your household appliance significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appliance - A device or piece of equipment designed to perform a specific task
