award-winning

1. ADJ

/ əˈwɔrd ˌwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈwɔːd ˌwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
Having received one or more awards, especially for excellence in a particular field.
having received one or more awards

Examples

The award-winning author signed copies of her latest novel at the bookstore.
They attended an award-winning theater production last night.
This award-winning documentary sheds light on an important social issue.

Synset Examples

this award-winning bridge spans a distance of five miles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prize-winning - having won a prize
