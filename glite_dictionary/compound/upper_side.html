upper side

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpɚ saɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpə saɪd / (BrE)
the highest or uppermost surface of an object or structure
the top surface of something

Examples

He painted only the upper side of the shelf for a smooth finish.
Place the sticker on the upper side of the box where it will be visible.
The sun warmed the upper side of the rock throughout the afternoon.

Synset Examples

put your books on top of the desk
only the top side of the box was painted

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underside - the lower surface of an object
  • Synonym: top - the uppermost surface or part of something
