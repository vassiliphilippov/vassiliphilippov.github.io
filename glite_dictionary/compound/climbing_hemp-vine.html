climbing hemp-vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmp vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmp vaɪn / (BrE)
a tropical American climbing herb with vanilla-scented flowers, often found growing up trees
herbaceous vine with vanilla-scented flowers

Examples

The climbing hemp-vine covered the lower branches of the old oak, adding a sweet fragrance to the air.
Gardeners often appreciate the climbing hemp-vine for its ability to quickly scale trellises.
In its native habitat, climbing hemp-vine winds its way up tall trees, displaying clusters of pale flowers.
