peacock flower fence

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiˌkɑk ˈflaʊɚ ˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɒk ˈflaʊə ˈfɛns / (BrE)
an East Indian ornamental tree with racemes of yellow-white flowers, cultivated for its decorative value
East Indian ornamental tree

Examples

The peacock flower fence is often grown in gardens for its striking yellow-white blooms.
Many botanical gardens feature the peacock flower fence as a decorative plant along pathways.
They planted a peacock flower fence at the entrance to create a welcoming display of flowers.
