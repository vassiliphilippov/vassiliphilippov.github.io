steel blue

1. NOUN

/ stil blu / (AmE), / stiːl bluː / (BrE)
a shade of blue tinged with grey, resembling the color of steel
greyish-blue color

Examples

The dress was a striking shade of steel blue, giving it a modern edge.
We painted the kitchen walls steel blue to create a calm atmosphere.
A pair of steel blue eyes stared out from the cover of the magazine.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blue - the color between green and violet in the spectrum; the sky on a clear day
  • Degree Higher: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue

Etymology

Named after the color of steel, which often appears bluish-grey.
