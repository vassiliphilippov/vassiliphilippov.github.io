automobile battery

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəmoʊˌbil ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl ˈbætəri / (BrE)
A rechargeable lead-acid battery that supplies electrical energy to a motor vehicle, typically a 12-volt battery essential for starting and running electrical systems.
rechargeable battery in a motor vehicle

Examples

When her automobile battery died, she had to call for roadside assistance.
It is important to check the water level in your automobile battery regularly.
A mechanic replaced the old automobile battery with a new one to fix the starting issue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car battery - battery that supplies electricity to a car, synonym of automobile battery
