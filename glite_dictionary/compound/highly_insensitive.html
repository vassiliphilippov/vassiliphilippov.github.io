highly insensitive

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪli ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
Extremely lacking in sensitivity or consideration for others' feelings or circumstances.
extremely lacking in sensitivity

Examples

His comment about her appearance was highly insensitive and upset everyone in the room.
It would be highly insensitive to joke about such a serious topic.
Posting that meme was highly insensitive given the current situation.

Synset Examples

The term 'rape face' is highly insensitive and should be avoided.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: insensitive - lacking sensitivity; not considerate of others' feelings
  • Degree Higher: offensive - causing someone to feel deeply hurt, upset, or angry
