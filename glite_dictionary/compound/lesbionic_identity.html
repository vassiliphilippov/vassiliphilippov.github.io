lesbionic identity

1. NOUN

/ lɛzˈbaɪ.ɑn.ɪk aɪˈdɛn.tɪ.ti / (AmE), / lɛzˈbaɪ.ɒn.ɪk aɪˈdɛnt.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The condition or experience of identifying as a lesbian, especially in relation to one's social or cultural self-understanding.
state of identifying as a lesbian

Examples

After years of confusion, she began to accept her lesbionic identity.
The novel explores the challenges associated with embracing a lesbionic identity.
Support groups can help people feel comfortable with their lesbionic identity.

Synset Examples

She embraced her lesbionic identity with pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesbian - a woman who is romantically or sexually attracted to other women
