citrus mealybug

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtrəs ˈmiːlˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrəs ˈmiːlˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a species of mealybug (Planococcus citri) that feeds on many cultivated plants and is especially damaging to citrus crops
insect pest of citrus plants

Examples

Farmers have struggled to control the citrus mealybug population in their orchards.
The citrus mealybug can cause significant economic losses in the citrus industry.
Applying neem oil is a common method for managing citrus mealybug infestations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mealybug - a small, soft-bodied insect that secretes a powdery wax
