to travel abroad

1. VERB

/ ˈtrævəl əˈbrɔd / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl əˈbrɔːd / (BrE)
to go to or visit countries outside one's own country
go to foreign countries

Examples

Many students choose to travel abroad during their gap year to learn about different cultures.
Her job requires her to travel abroad several times a year.
He always dreamed of the opportunity to travel abroad and experience life outside his homeland.

Synset Examples

She spent her summer holiday traveling abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel overseas - to go to countries across seas; synonym for 'travel abroad'
