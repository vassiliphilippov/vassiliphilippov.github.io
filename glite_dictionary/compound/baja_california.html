Baja California

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːhɑː ˌkælɪˈfɔrniə / (AmE), / ˈbɑːhə ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə / (BrE)
a mountainous peninsula in northwest Mexico, extending south from the US border and bordered by the Pacific Ocean and the Gulf of California
peninsula in northwest Mexico

Examples

Many tourists visit Baja California for its beautiful beaches and desert landscapes.
The waters around Baja California are famous for whale watching.
Baja California separates the Gulf of California from the Pacific Ocean.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Lower California'.
