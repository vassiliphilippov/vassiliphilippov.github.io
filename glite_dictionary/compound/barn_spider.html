barn spider

1. NOUN

/ bɑrn ˈspaɪdɚ / (AmE), / bɑːn ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
a large orange and tan orb-weaving spider with darkly banded legs, common in barns and known for spinning a new web each day
orb-weaving spider found in barns

Examples

The barn spider spun a perfectly round web in the corner of the rafters.
Children often recognize the barn spider as the character Charlotte from the famous story.
On autumn mornings, the dew highlights the barn spider's intricate web outside the old stable.

Synset Examples

the barn spider was made famous in E. B. White's book ‘Charlotte's Web’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orb-weaver - a spider that spins a wheel-shaped web
