groundnut oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊnd.nʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd.nʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
A pale yellow edible oil extracted from peanuts, used for cooking and in food products.
edible oil from peanuts

Examples

Many chefs prefer groundnut oil for frying because of its high smoke point.
She bought a bottle of groundnut oil to use in stir-fry recipes.
Traditional soaps in some regions are made using groundnut oil as a main ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peanut oil - oil extracted from peanuts, used especially in cooking
