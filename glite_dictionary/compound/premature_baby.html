premature baby

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛm.əˈtʃʊr ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˌpriː.məˈtjʊə ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
A baby born before 37 weeks of pregnancy have been completed.
baby born before full term

Examples

The hospital's neonatal unit specializes in caring for premature babies.
She was born a premature baby and had to stay in the incubator for several weeks.
Research shows that premature babies may face higher health risks in early childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preterm infant - An infant born before the usual 37 weeks of gestation
