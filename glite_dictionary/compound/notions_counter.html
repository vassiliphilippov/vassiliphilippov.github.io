notions counter

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊʃənz ˌkaʊntər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊʃənz ˌkaʊntə / (BrE)
A counter or section in a store where small articles, especially sewing supplies such as buttons, thread, pins, etc., are sold.
counter selling small sewing articles

Examples

She stopped by the notions counter to pick up some buttons for her new dress.
The notions counter was neatly stocked with colorful spools of thread and pins.
Many customers visit the notions counter for last-minute sewing supplies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notions department - section of a store for sewing sundries
