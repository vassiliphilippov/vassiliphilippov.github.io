ram's-head lady's slipper

1. NOUN

/ ræmz hɛd ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpɚ / (AmE), / ræmz hɛd ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
a rare orchid of northern North America with a brownish-green flower and a distinctive red-and-white lip resembling a ram's head
species of North American orchid

Examples

The ram's-head lady's slipper blooms in late spring in cool, shaded forests.
Botanists were thrilled to discover a colony of the rare ram's-head lady's slippers in the park.
The distinctive lip of the ram's-head lady's slipper makes it easy to identify among wild orchids.

Related Terms
