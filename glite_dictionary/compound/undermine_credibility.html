to undermine credibility

1. VERB

/ ˌʌndɚˈmaɪn krəˌdɪbɪˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈmaɪn krɪˌdɛbɪˈlɪti / (BrE)
to weaken or damage the trustworthiness or reliability of someone or something
weaken someone's trustworthiness or reliability

Examples

Negative media coverage can undermine credibility even when the facts are unproven.
If her lies are exposed, it will undermine credibility within the team.
Continual mistakes by leadership can quickly undermine credibility with employees.

Synset Examples

The scandal could undermine the politician's credibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damage credibility - harm the trustworthiness or reliability
  • Antonym: bolster credibility - strengthen the trustworthiness or reliability
