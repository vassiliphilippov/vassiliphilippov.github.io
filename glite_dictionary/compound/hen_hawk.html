hen hawk

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛn hɑk / (AmE), / ˈhɛn hɔːk / (BrE)
A nontechnical term for any hawk said to prey on poultry.
hawk that preys on poultry

Examples

Farmers often worry that a hen hawk will attack their chickens.
The presence of a hen hawk nearby made the hens restless all day.
Some rural communities once placed bounties on hen hawks to protect their poultry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawk - bird of prey in the family Accipitridae
