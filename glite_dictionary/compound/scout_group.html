scout group

1. NOUN

/ skaʊt ɡruːp / (AmE), / skaʊt ɡruːp / (BrE)
a local unit or organization of Girl or Boy Scouts, typically consisting of members grouped by age or section
unit of Girl or Boy Scouts

Examples

Emily joined her local scout group to learn camping and leadership skills.
The scout group meets every Thursday evening at the community center.
Our scout group is planning a hiking trip in the mountains next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troop - a group of scouts; an alternative term for a unit of scouts
