amount of money

1. NOUN

/ əˈmaʊnt əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / əˈmaʊnt əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
a quantity or sum of money; the total money used, needed, or available for a particular purpose
sum or quantity of money

Examples

It's amazing how much an unexpected repair can cost; even a small amount of money can make a difference.
They raised a large amount of money for charity through the event.
