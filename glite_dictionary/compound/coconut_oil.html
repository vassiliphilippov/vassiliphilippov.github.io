coconut oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊˌkəˌnʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkənʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an edible oil extracted from the meat or kernel of coconuts, used in cooking and skincare
oil extracted from coconuts

Examples

She uses coconut oil for baking and frying because of its subtle flavor.
Many people apply coconut oil to their hair for added shine and moisture.
The recipe calls for a tablespoon of coconut oil instead of butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive oil - oil obtained from olives, used in cooking and dressings
