crazy bone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪzi ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈkreɪzi ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the area at the elbow where striking the ulnar nerve produces a sharp tingling sensation
spot at the elbow causing tingling if hit

Examples

When I hit my crazy bone on the table, my whole arm tingled painfully.
She laughed as the tingling from her crazy bone slowly faded away.
Avoid leaning on your crazy bone during meetings or you might get a nasty shock.

Synset Examples

the funny bone is not humerus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funny bone - the spot at the elbow involving the ulnar nerve causing a tingling sensation if knocked
