1-hitter

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈhɪtər / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhɪtə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a game in which a pitcher allows the opposing team only one hit.
baseball: game allowing only one hit

Examples

The rookie pitcher threw a brilliant 1-hitter against the league's top team.
Last night's 1-hitter was his best performance of the season.
A 1-hitter is a rare feat that few pitchers achieve in their careers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: no-hitter - a game in which a pitcher allows no hits
