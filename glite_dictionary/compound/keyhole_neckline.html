keyhole neckline

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːˌhoʊl ˈnɛkˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌhəʊl ˈnɛklaɪn / (BrE)
A neckline on a garment, usually a dress or blouse, featuring a small, rounded or oval opening just below the collarbones.
neckline with a small opening below the collarbones

Examples

The dress had a delicate keyhole neckline that added a subtle, elegant touch.
She preferred blouses with a keyhole neckline for a slightly more fashionable look.
A keyhole neckline can make an otherwise simple dress stand out at an event.
