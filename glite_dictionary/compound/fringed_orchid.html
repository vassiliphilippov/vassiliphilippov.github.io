fringed orchid

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒd ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
any of several American orchid species with a characteristic fringed or deeply cut lip, flowering in summer
American orchid with a fringed lip

Examples

The rare fringed orchid was spotted blooming along the wet meadow's edge.
Many wildlife photographers seek out fringed orchids for their unusual and beautiful flowers.
Botanists studied the morphology of the fringed orchid to better understand its pollination strategy.
