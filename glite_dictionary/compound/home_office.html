home office

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / həʊm ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the main office or administrative center of an organization, especially its headquarters
main administrative center of an organization

Examples

The company's home office is located in Chicago, where most strategic decisions are made.
She transferred to the home office after several years working in the regional branch.
Reports from all subsidiaries are sent to the home office for review each quarter.

Synset Examples

many companies have their headquarters in New York

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - the main office or center of operations of an organization
