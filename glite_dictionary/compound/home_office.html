Home Office

1. PROPN

/ hoʊm ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / həʊm ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the department of the British government responsible for domestic affairs such as immigration, security, and law and order
UK government department for domestic affairs
british

Examples

The Home Office announced new policies on immigration this morning.
Many police matters fall under the jurisdiction of the Home Office.
The report was sent to the Home Office for review before publication.
