handing over

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændɪŋ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈhændɪŋ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
the act of giving something or transferring control or responsibility to another person
the act of transferring to another

Examples

The handing over of keys marked the official transfer of the property.
After the election, the handing over of power went smoothly.
His sudden handing over of responsibilities surprised the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrender - the act of giving something up, typically under compulsion
  • Antonym: retention - the act of keeping or holding onto something
