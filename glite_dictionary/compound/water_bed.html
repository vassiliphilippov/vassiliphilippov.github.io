water bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər bɛd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə bɛd / (BrE)
A bed with a mattress made of strong plastic that is filled with water and used for sleeping or resting.
bed with a water-filled mattress

Examples

She bought a new water bed because she found it more comfortable than a regular mattress.
The old water bed in the guest room finally started leaking after several years of use.
Some people find sleeping on a water bed soothing, while others prefer a firmer surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mattress - a rectangular pad for sleeping on, often filled with foam, springs, etc.
