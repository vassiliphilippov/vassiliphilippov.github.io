three-petaled

1. ADJ

/ θriː ˈpɛtɫ̩d / (AmE), / θriː ˈpɛtəld / (BrE)
(of flowers) having three petals.
having three petals (flowers)

Examples

The trillium is a three-petaled flower commonly found in the forest.
She admired the delicate three-petaled blooms in the botanical garden.
Many irises have a distinct three-petaled shape that makes them easy to identify.
