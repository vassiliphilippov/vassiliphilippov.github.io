crystal ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs.təl bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.təl bɔːl / (BrE)
A glass or crystal sphere used by fortune-tellers or clairvoyants to predict the future or gain hidden knowledge.
glass sphere used by fortune-tellers

Examples

The fortune-teller gazed into her crystal ball and predicted a bright future for me.
Many movies show witches using a crystal ball to reveal secrets from afar.
Skeptics argue that a crystal ball has no real power to foresee events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oracle - a person or device used to predict the future
  • Synonym: prophecy - a prediction of future events
