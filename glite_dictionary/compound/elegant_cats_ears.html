elegant cat's ears

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlɪɡənt kæts ɪrz / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪɡənt kæts ɪəz / (BrE)
a small plant with slender, bent stems and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers with petals resembling cat's ears, native to parts of the northwestern United States
