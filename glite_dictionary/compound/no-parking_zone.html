no-parking zone

1. NOUN

/ noʊ ˈpɑrkɪŋ zoʊn / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈpɑːkɪŋ zəʊn / (BrE)
an area where vehicles are not permitted to park
area where parking is not allowed

Examples

You will get a ticket if you leave your car in a no-parking zone.
She didn’t see the sign and accidentally parked in a no-parking zone.
Delivery trucks are often forced to stop briefly in no-parking zones during busy hours.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: parking zone - area designated for vehicle parking

2. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ ˈpɑrkɪŋ zoʊn / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈpɑːkɪŋ zəʊn / (BrE)
An area in which parking vehicles is not allowed.
area where parking is prohibited

Examples

The officer issued him a ticket for leaving his car in a no-parking zone.
Make sure you don't stop in a no-parking zone during rush hour.
They towed her vehicle because it was parked in a no-parking zone.

Synset Examples

You can't park there, mate; it's a no-parking zone.
