working girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkɪŋ ˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A young woman who is employed, especially one who works for her living.
employed young woman

Examples

When her family struggled financially, Maria became a working girl to help make ends meet.
The documentary highlights the daily life of a working girl in the city.
Many working girls commute long distances for their jobs every day.
