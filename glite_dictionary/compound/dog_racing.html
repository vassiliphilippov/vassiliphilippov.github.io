dog racing

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A race between dogs, typically held as a sporting event where people bet on the outcome.
competition where dogs race, often for betting

Examples

Dog racing is a popular sport in some countries, attracting large crowds and significant bets.
After hearing about concerns for animal welfare, some cities have moved to ban dog racing.
The excitement at the dog racing track was palpable as the race began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greyhound racing - racing with greyhounds, a typical form of dog racing
