green peach aphid

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin piːtʃ ˈæfɪd / (AmE), / ɡriːn piːtʃ ˈæfɪd / (BrE)
a yellowish green aphid (Myzus persicae) that feeds on and is particularly destructive to peach trees and other cultivated plants
destructive yellowish green aphid, pest of peach trees

Examples

The orchard suffered heavy losses due to infestation by the green peach aphid.
Farmers applied insecticides to control the spread of the green peach aphid on their crops.
The green peach aphid transmits numerous plant viruses, making it a significant agricultural pest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aphid - a small sap-sucking insect; general term for aphids
