opium den

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpiəm dɛn / (AmE), / ˈəʊpiəm dɛn / (BrE)
a place where people buy and smoke opium, especially one associated with illicit or historical use
place for opium smoking

Examples

The authorities raided the opium den hidden in a back alley last night.
In the nineteenth century, many travelers wrote about the mysterious atmosphere of the Shanghai opium den.
Artists and writers sometimes frequented a local opium den seeking inspiration or escape.
