cervix uteri

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrvɪks juːˈtɪraɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪks juːˈtɪəraɪ / (BrE)
the lower, necklike opening of the uterus connecting it to the vagina
lower opening of the uterus
medical

Examples

During a routine examination, the doctor checked the cervix uteri for any abnormalities.
Cervical cancer develops in the cervix uteri, which connects the uterus to the vagina.
A Pap smear is performed to detect changes in the cells of the cervix uteri.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cervix - lower, narrow part of the uterus

Etymology

From Latin 'cervix' (neck) and 'uteri' (of the uterus).
