north of

1. ADP

/ nɔrθ əv / (AmE), / nɔːθ ɒv / (BrE)
in a position towards the north, relative to a specified point or place
towards the north from a point

Examples

The new development is just north of the old industrial district.
Several small villages lie north of the river.
The hotel is conveniently situated north of the main highway.

Synset Examples

The cabin is located north of the lake.
We drove north of the city to explore the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: south of - in a position towards the south, relative to a specified point or place
  • Synonym: north - the direction towards the top of the map; one of the cardinal points
