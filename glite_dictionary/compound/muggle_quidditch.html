muggle quidditch

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌɡəl ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡl̩ ˈkwɪdɪtʃ / (BrE)
A team sport inspired by the fictional game of Quidditch in the Harry Potter series, played on the ground by non-magical people, with rules adapted for real-world play.
real-world team sport based on Harry Potter's Quidditch

Examples

She started playing muggle quidditch with her university team last semester.
The rules of muggle quidditch are adapted to make the game playable without magic.
International muggle quidditch tournaments attract players from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quidditch - fictional magical sport from the Harry Potter series
  • Synonym: muggle - non-magical person in the Harry Potter universe

Etymology

From 'muggle' (non-magical person in Harry Potter, created by J.K. Rowling) and 'Quidditch' (fictional sport in Harry Potter).
