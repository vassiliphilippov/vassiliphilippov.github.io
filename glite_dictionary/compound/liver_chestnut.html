liver chestnut

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɚ ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
A horse coat color that is a solid, dark brown shade of chestnut.
horse coat color: solid dark brown

Examples

Her new horse is a beautiful liver chestnut with a glossy coat.
The stable specializes in breeding liver chestnut Thoroughbreds for competition.
Among all the horses, the liver chestnut stood out for its rich, dark color.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: chestnut - a horse coat color of a reddish-brown shade

Etymology

From 'liver' (the organ, referencing the color) + 'chestnut' (a reddish-brown horse color).
