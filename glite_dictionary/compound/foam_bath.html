foam bath

1. NOUN

/ foʊm bæθ / (AmE), / fəʊm bɑːθ / (BrE)
A substance in the form of crystals, powder, or liquid that produces foam, adds fragrance, and softens water when added to a bath.
substance that foams and scents bathwater

Examples

She poured a generous amount of foam bath into the tub to create fragrant bubbles.
A foam bath can make the bathing experience more relaxing and luxurious.
The children enjoyed playing with the thick bubbles from the foam bath their mother used.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bubble bath - product creating bubbles or foam in bathwater
