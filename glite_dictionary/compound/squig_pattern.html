squig pattern

1. NOUN

/ skwɪɡ ˈpætɚn / (AmE), / skwɪɡ ˈpætn̩ / (BrE)
a design or shape consisting of irregular, curvy lines
irregular, curvy design or shape

Examples

The notebook was covered in squig patterns drawn with colored pens.
Her dress featured bold squig patterns that caught everyone's attention.
They painted the mural with overlapping squig patterns for a playful effect.

Synset Examples

The artist's squig patterns were truly unique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squiggle - a short, irregular curve or twist in writing or drawing
  • Degree Lower: zigzag - a pattern made of sharp turns in alternating directions
