roach holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊtʃ ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtʃ ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a small device, often metal tweezers, used by smokers to hold the remains of a marijuana cigarette ('roach') for easier consumption
device for holding a marijuana cigarette butt
informal slang

Examples

He used a roach holder to finish smoking the joint without burning his fingers.
She always keeps a roach holder in her bag for when the cigarette gets too short.
The roach holder made it much easier to enjoy every last bit of the joint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roach (marijuana) - the butt end of a marijuana cigarette
