theater light

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiədɚ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə laɪt / (BrE)
A light fixture or lamp used for illumination on a theater stage during performances.
light fixture used in theaters

Examples

The technician adjusted the theater light before the curtain rose.
Every theater light was carefully positioned to create dramatic shadows on stage.
A broken theater light delayed the start of the evening performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage light - light used to illuminate a stage during performances
