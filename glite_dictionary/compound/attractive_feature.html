attractive feature

1. NOUN

/ əˈtræktɪv ˈfitʃər / (AmE), / əˈtræktɪv ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a characteristic of something or someone that is pleasing or likely to attract attention or interest
pleasing or attention-attracting quality

Examples

The spacious garden is the most attractive feature of the house.
One attractive feature of this phone is its long battery life.
The city boasts many attractive features, including parks and historic buildings.

Synset Examples

flowers are an attractor for bees

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drawcard - a feature that attracts people
  • Antonym: disadvantage - an undesirable or unattractive feature
