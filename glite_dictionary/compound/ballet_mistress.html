ballet mistress

1. NOUN

/ bæˈleɪ ˌmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈbæleɪ ˌmɪstrəs / (BrE)
a woman responsible for directing, teaching, and rehearsing dancers in a ballet company
female director and teacher in a ballet company

Examples

The ballet mistress worked tirelessly with the company to perfect the choreography.
As a young dancer, she was inspired by the discipline of her ballet mistress.
The ballet mistress called everyone to the barre for warm-up exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballet master - a man who directs and teaches and rehearses dancers for a ballet company

Etymology

From French 'ballet' and English 'mistress' (female leader or teacher).
