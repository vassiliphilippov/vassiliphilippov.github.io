infringement of copyright

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt əv ˈkɑːpiraɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt əv ˈkɒpiraɪt / (BrE)
the act of violating the exclusive rights granted to a copyright holder, such as reproducing or distributing copyrighted material without permission
violation of copyright law
legal

Examples

The company was sued for infringement of copyright after distributing the artist's music without permission.
Universities must ensure they avoid infringement of copyright when sharing digital materials with students.
He received a cease and desist letter accusing him of infringement of copyright over the use of protected logos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copyright infringement - the unauthorized use of material protected by copyright
  • Degree Lower: copyright - the exclusive legal right to reproduce, publish, or sell original work
