tot lot

1. NOUN

/ tɑt lɑt / (AmE), / tɒt lɒt / (BrE)
a small outdoor playground or play area designed for young children, especially toddlers
small playground for young children
american

Examples

Every afternoon, parents gather at the tot lot to watch their children play.
The new apartment complex features a safe and colorful tot lot for residents' families.
After lunch, the daycare class went to the tot lot for some fresh air and fun.

Synset Examples

She took her toddler to the tot lot every day.
The children played happily in the tot lot.
We visited the tot lot for some outdoor fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playground - an outdoor area for children to play in, usually with equipment

Etymology

Formed from 'tot' (a young child) and 'lot' (an area of land).
