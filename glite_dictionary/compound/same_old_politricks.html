same old politricks

1. NOUN

/ seɪm oʊld pəˈlɪtrɪks / (AmE), / seɪm əʊld pəˈlɪtrɪks / (BrE)
The familiar and expected forms of deceptive or manipulative behavior in politics, often repeated over time.
predictable deceptive political practices
informal slang

Examples

People are growing disillusioned with the same old politricks every time there's a campaign.
She saw through the same old politricks that the candidate was using to win support.
After decades of the same old politricks, voters demanded real change.

Synset Examples

He was tired of the same old politricks every election.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political trickery - Deceitful or dishonest actions in politics

Etymology

Blend of 'same old' (meaning usual or unchanged) and 'politricks,' a punning alteration of 'politics' using 'trick.'
