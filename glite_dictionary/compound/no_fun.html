no fun

1. ADJ

/ noʊ fʌn / (AmE), / nəʊ fʌn / (BrE)
not enjoyable or entertaining; not pleasant to be around or experience
not enjoyable; not entertaining

Examples

After staying home sick all week, being alone was no fun at all.
Working late hours every night is no fun if you never get a break.
The board game wasn't no fun because everyone argued about the rules.

Synset Examples

He’s always so serious; he’s no fun at parties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fun - enjoyment, amusement, or light-hearted pleasure
