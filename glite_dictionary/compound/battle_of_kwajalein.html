Battle of Kwajalein

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv kwɑː.dʒəˌleɪn / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv ˌkwɑːdʒəˈleɪn / (BrE)
A major amphibious assault during World War II in January 1944, where American forces captured a Japanese-held atoll in the Pacific.
World War II Pacific battle, 1944

Examples

The Battle of Kwajalein marked a significant turning point in the Pacific campaign of World War II.
During the Battle of Kwajalein, Allied forces encountered fierce resistance from Japanese defenders.
Many historians study the strategies used in the Battle of Kwajalein to understand amphibious warfare.
