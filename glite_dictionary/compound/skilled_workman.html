skilled workman

1. NOUN

/ skɪld ˈwɜrkmən / (AmE), / skɪld ˈwɜːkmən / (BrE)
a male worker who has acquired special skills or training for a particular job
male worker with specialized skills

Examples

The company hired a skilled workman to repair the machinery.
As a skilled workman, he takes great pride in the quality of his craft.
During the restoration, a skilled workman was brought in to handle the delicate materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skilled worker - a worker who has acquired special skills, regardless of gender
  • Antonym: unskilled worker - a worker without special skills or training
