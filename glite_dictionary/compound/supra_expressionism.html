supra expressionism

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.prə ɛkˌsprɛʃ.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈsuː.prə ɪkˌsprɛʃ.ənɪz.əm / (BrE)
An artistic movement that aimed to transcend the limits of expressionism, seeking new forms of expression beyond traditional emotional and subjective representation.
art movement beyond expressionism

Examples

The exhibition showcased paintings from the supra expressionism movement, challenging viewers with their radical new forms.
Many critics believe that supra expressionism took the emotional core of expressionism and pushed it to conceptual extremes.
Interest in supra expressionism surged in the 20th century as artists sought to break traditional expressive boundaries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: expressionism - an art movement focused on representing emotions and subjective experience

Etymology

From Latin 'supra' (above, beyond) + 'expressionism'.
