news show

1. NOUN

/ nuz ʃoʊ / (AmE), / njuːz ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A television or radio program that reports on current events, often featuring interviews and commentary.
television or radio program about current events

Examples

She appears as a guest on the morning news show every Friday.
The local news show covered the city council meeting in detail.
Many people rely on a nightly news show to stay informed about world events.

Synset Examples

we watch the 7 o'clock news every night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newscast - a broadcast report of news
  • Synonym: news program - a program focusing on news and current events
