regular recurrence

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛɡjələr rɪˈkɝrəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə rɪˈkɜːrəns / (BrE)
the act or fact of something happening again at consistent, fixed intervals.
repetition at fixed intervals

Examples

The town holds a festival with regular recurrence every spring.
Migraines can have a regular recurrence, making them predictable for some patients.
Eclipses follow a pattern of regular recurrence due to celestial mechanics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recurrence - the process of occurring again
