on the lines of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə laɪnz ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə laɪnz ɒv / (BrE)
similar to or in the manner of; inspired by or following the example of something or someone
similar to; inspired by

Examples

The new building was constructed on the lines of a traditional Japanese temple.
Her novel was written on the lines of classic detective stories.
The marketing campaign was planned on the lines of last year's award-winning strategy.

Synset Examples

His speech was on the lines of promoting community values.
Her dress was designed on the lines of a vintage style.
The proposal was created on the lines of last year's successful project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the style of - done in the manner or style of someone or something else
  • Synonym: after the fashion of - following the model or example of someone or something
