pierid butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪr.i.ɪd ˈbʌt̬.ɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪər.i.ɪd ˈbʌt.əˌflaɪ / (BrE)
A pale-colored butterfly of the family Pieridae, having three pairs of well-developed legs, including common species such as the cabbage white.
a pale butterfly of the Pieridae family

Examples

The cabbage white is a well-known species of pierid butterfly found in gardens worldwide.
Scientists study the migratory patterns of several pierid butterflies in Europe and Asia.
A pierid butterfly landed briefly on the blooming daisies in the meadow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white butterfly - common name for many pierid butterflies, especially the cabbage white
  • Synonym: yellow butterfly - common name for yellow species of the Pieridae family

Etymology

From family name Pieridae, derived from type genus Pieris; ultimately from Latin 'Pieris', a Muse.
