Tamil language

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæmɪl ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪl ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Dravidian language spoken by the Tamil people in southern India and Sri Lanka.
Dravidian language of southern India and Sri Lanka

Examples

She speaks Tamil language fluently with her relatives in Chennai.
Learning the Tamil language can be challenging for non-native speakers.
The Tamil language has a rich literary tradition dating back thousands of years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Telugu language - Dravidian language spoken in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana
  • Synonym: Malayalam language - Dravidian language spoken in Kerala

Etymology

From Tamil தமிழ் (Tamiḻ), the name of the language.
