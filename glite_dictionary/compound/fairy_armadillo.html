fairy armadillo

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri ˌɑːrməˌdɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌɑːməˈdɪləʊ / (BrE)
a very small armadillo native to Argentina, with pale silky hair and pink plates on its head and neck
small Argentine armadillo with pink plates

Examples

The fairy armadillo is rarely seen in the wild due to its shy and nocturnal behavior.
After years of research, scientists finally photographed a living fairy armadillo in its natural habitat.
Children were fascinated by the picture of the tiny fairy armadillo in their animal encyclopedia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armadillo - a burrowing mammal with a protective bony shell
