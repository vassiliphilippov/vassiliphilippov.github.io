foxglove family

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑksˌɡlʌv ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌɡlʌv ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants that includes foxgloves, snapdragons, toadflaxes, figworts, and related species, often classified in the family Scrophulariaceae.
family of flowering plants including foxglove

Examples

The foxglove family contains a wide variety of colorful garden plants.
Researchers have discovered new medicinal compounds in members of the foxglove family.
Snapdragons, a popular garden flower, belong to the foxglove family.
