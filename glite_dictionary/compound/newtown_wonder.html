Newtown Wonder

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːtaʊn ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtaʊn ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
a variety of apple, primarily used in cooking, with bright scarlet skin and crimson red stripes
variety of cooking apple

Examples

The Newtown Wonder is an excellent choice for making apple pies due to its tart flavor.
He planted a Newtown Wonder tree in his orchard last spring.
Cooks appreciate the firm texture of the Newtown Wonder when preparing preserves.
