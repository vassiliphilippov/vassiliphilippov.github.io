electronics industry

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪks ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪks ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy consisting of companies that design, manufacture, and sell electronic products
sector for manufacturing electronic products

Examples

The electronics industry has seen rapid growth due to advances in technology.
Many jobs in Asia are supported by the electronics industry.
Environmental concerns are rising within the electronics industry regarding e-waste management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrical industry - the sector that manufactures electrical equipment more generally
