family reunion

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli riˈjuːniən / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli riˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a social gathering or event for members of the same family, often to celebrate or reconnect
gathering of family members

Examples

Every summer, my relatives travel from all over the country for our annual family reunion.
Photos from yesterday’s family reunion filled her social media feed.
They planned a special family reunion to celebrate their grandparents’ fiftieth wedding anniversary.

Synset Examples

They gathered for a family reunion to honor their -ther.
We had a family reunion with all the first cousins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reunion - a social gathering of people who have been separated
