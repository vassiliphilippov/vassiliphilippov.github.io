job interview

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb ˈɪntɚˌvju / (AmE), / dʒɒb ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
a formal meeting in which an applicant is questioned to determine their suitability for a job
formal meeting for employment selection

Examples

He prepared carefully for the job interview by researching the company.
During her job interview, she was asked about her previous work experience.
Many people feel nervous before attending a job interview.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interview - a formal discussion to assess suitability for something
