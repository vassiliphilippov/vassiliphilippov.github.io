effective persuader

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfɛktɪv pərˈsweɪdər / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktɪv pəˈsweɪdə / (BrE)
a person who is able to influence others successfully
someone who influences others successfully

Examples

Sarah has become a highly effective persuader since she started her sales job.
Being an effective persuader is essential for leaders and managers.
The lawyer's reputation as an effective persuader helped win difficult cases.

Synset Examples

Her charm makes her an effective persuader in negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convincer - someone who convinces others
  • Synonym: influencer - a person who influences others, especially on social media
  • Antonym: manipulator - someone who controls or influences others in a clever or unscrupulous way
