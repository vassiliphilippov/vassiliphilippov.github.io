flip chart

1. NOUN

/ flɪp tʃɑrt / (AmE), / flɪp tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a large pad of paper with sheets fixed at the top, used for presenting information that can be displayed by flipping each sheet over
large pad of hinged sheets for presentations

Examples

During the meeting, the manager used a flip chart to illustrate the company's quarterly results.
She wrote each project milestone on a new sheet of the flip chart for the team to see.
A flip chart is ideal for brainstorming sessions where new ideas are added in real time.
