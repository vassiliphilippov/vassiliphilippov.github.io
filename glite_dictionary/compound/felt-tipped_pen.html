felt-tipped pen

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɛlt ˈtɪpt pɛn / (AmE), / ˌfɛlt ˈtɪpt pɛn / (BrE)
A pen with a writing tip made of felt or porous material, used for drawing or writing.
pen with a felt tip for writing or drawing

Examples

She labeled each moving box with a felt-tipped pen to keep things organized.
Using a red felt-tipped pen, the teacher marked corrections on our essays.
Children love to use a felt-tipped pen when making colorful drawings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marker - a pen with a broad, often felt tip, for marking or drawing
