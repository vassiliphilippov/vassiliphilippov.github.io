to reignite passion

1. VERB

/ ˌriːɪɡˈnaɪt ˈpæʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪɡˈnaɪt ˈpæʃən / (BrE)
to revive strong interest, enthusiasm, or affection that had diminished or faded
restore lost enthusiasm or affection

Examples

After years of routine, they took a trip to reignite passion in their relationship.
Reading her old journals helped her reignite passion for writing.
The coach organized new activities to reignite passion among the players.

Synset Examples

They hope to reignite their passion for the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to rekindle - to relight or revive (something, especially a feeling)
  • Synonym: to restore - to bring back (a feeling, situation, etc.)
