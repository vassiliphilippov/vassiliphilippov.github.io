pendulum clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn.dʒə.ləm klɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.djʊ.ləm klɒk / (BrE)
a clock that is regulated and kept accurate by a swinging pendulum
clock regulated by a swinging pendulum

Examples

The antique pendulum clock in the hallway chimes every hour with a deep, resonant sound.
My grandfather carefully wound his old pendulum clock every Sunday evening.
Restoring the damaged pendulum clock required replacing the broken suspension spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather clock - a tall, freestanding pendulum clock
