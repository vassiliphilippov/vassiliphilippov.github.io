social service

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
An organized program or activity designed to help improve the well-being of disadvantaged or vulnerable people in society.
organized welfare activities for disadvantaged people

Examples

The government increased funding for social service programs to support low-income families.
She has dedicated her career to working in social service agencies.
Effective social service delivery can significantly improve the quality of life for many people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - government aid for those in need
