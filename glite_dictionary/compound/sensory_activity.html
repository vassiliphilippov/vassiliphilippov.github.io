sensory activity

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛn.sə.ri ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.səri ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
an activity designed to stimulate or engage the senses, often used in educational or therapeutic contexts
activity to stimulate the senses

Examples

The teacher set up a sensory activity with sand and water to help children explore different textures.
Therapists often use sensory activities to support children with sensory processing difficulties.
Painting with scented markers can be a fun sensory activity for young children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensory experience - an event or occurrence perceived through the senses
