ace of hearts

1. NOUN

/ eɪs əv hɑrts / (AmE), / eɪs əv hɑːts / (BrE)
the playing card that has a single heart symbol and ranks highest in the heart suit
the ace card in the heart suit

Examples

She turned over the ace of hearts and won the hand in an instant.
The magician pulled an ace of hearts out of his sleeve to amaze the audience.
In the final round, drawing the ace of hearts gave him the victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace of spades - the ace card in the spade suit
  • Synonym: ace of clubs - the ace card in the club suit
  • Synonym: ace of diamonds - the ace card in the diamond suit
