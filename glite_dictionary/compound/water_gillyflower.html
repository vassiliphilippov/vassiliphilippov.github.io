water gillyflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔːtər ˈɡɪliˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈɡɪliˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a plant of the eastern United States (featherfoil), with submerged, spongy, inflated flower stalks and white flowers
aquatic plant with spongy stems and white flowers

Examples

The pond was filled with water gillyflower blooming in early spring.
Researchers studied the water gillyflower to understand its unique spongy stems.
We spotted several water gillyflowers growing along the marshy bank.
