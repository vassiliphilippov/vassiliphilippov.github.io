banshee-like wail

1. NOUN

/ ˈbænʃi laɪk weɪl / (AmE), / ˈbanʃi laɪk weɪl / (BrE)
a loud, mournful, and piercing cry resembling the traditional wail attributed to a banshee
mournful, piercing cry

Examples

A banshee-like wail pierced the night when the news was announced.
The sudden banshee-like wail from the baby startled everyone in the room.
As thunder rolled, her banshee-like wail sent shivers down my spine.

Synset Examples

His banshee-like wail echoed through the empty halls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wail - a prolonged high-pitched cry of pain, grief, or anger

Etymology

From 'banshee', a female spirit in Irish mythology whose wail is an omen of death, and 'wail', meaning a prolonged high-pitched cry of pain or grief.
