para-legal work

1. NOUN

/ ˌpærəˈliːgəl wɜːrk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈliːgəl wɜːk / (BrE)
legal tasks or activities carried out by someone who is not a qualified lawyer
legal tasks by a non-lawyer

Examples

After her training, she began para-legal work at a law firm in the city.
Much of the document preparation and research is considered para-legal work.
The office hired an assistant to handle routine para-legal work and support the attorneys.

Synset Examples

He specializes in para-legal work for the firm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paralegal - a person trained to assist a lawyer but not qualified as a lawyer

Etymology

From 'para-' (beside, related to) and 'legal', indicating support of legal work without formal qualification as a lawyer.
