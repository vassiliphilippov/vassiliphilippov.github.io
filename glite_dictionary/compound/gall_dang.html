gall dang

1. X

/ ɡɔl dæŋ / (AmE), / ɡɔːl dæŋ / (BrE)
(informal) A mild euphemistic exclamation used to express annoyance or emphasis, substituting for 'goddamn' or similar stronger terms.
mild exclamation of annoyance or emphasis
informal

Examples

It's just too gall dang early to be awake on a Saturday!
He couldn't open the gall dang jar no matter how hard he tried.
That gall dang computer keeps freezing every time I need it.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goddamn - used to express anger or emphasis; strong expletive

Etymology

Altered form of 'goddamn', avoiding blasphemy or strong language.
