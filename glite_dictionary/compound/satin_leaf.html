satin leaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈsætən liːf / (AmE), / ˈsætɪn liːf / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with dark, hard, heavy wood and small plum-like purple fruit, known for its glossy foliage.
tropical American tree with glossy leaves

Examples

The satin leaf is admired for its striking bronze-green foliage.
Carpenters value the strong wood of the satin leaf for specialized projects.
We saw a satin leaf tree bearing small purple fruit during our trip to the rainforest.
