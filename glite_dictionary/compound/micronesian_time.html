Micronesian time

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈniː.ʒən taɪm / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəˈniː.zi.ən taɪm / (BrE)
a humorous term for a relaxed or flexible attitude toward punctuality and scheduling, typically associated with Micronesian culture
relaxed attitude toward punctuality

Examples

On Micronesian time, being half an hour late is perfectly acceptable.
We agreed to start the meeting at eight, but with Micronesian time it began closer to nine.
He joked that he must be running on Micronesian time after showing up late.

Synset Examples

In Micronesian time, punctuality is more of a suggestion.
Her flight was delayed because of Micronesian time.
Let's meet at noon, but remember, it's Micronesian time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: island time - a relaxed approach to timekeeping, common in island cultures
