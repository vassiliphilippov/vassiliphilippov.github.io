whiff of perfume

1. NOUN

/ wɪf əv pərˈfjuːm / (AmE), / wɪf əv pəˈfjuːm / (BrE)
a brief, light smell of perfume caught in the air
brief smell of perfume

Examples

A whiff of perfume drifted through the open window and lingered in the room.
He noticed a whiff of perfume every time she passed by.
The garden party was filled with laughter and the occasional whiff of perfume.

Synset Examples

There was a whiff of perfume as she walked by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scent - a distinctive smell, especially one that is pleasant
  • Antonym: odor - a particular smell, especially an unpleasant one
