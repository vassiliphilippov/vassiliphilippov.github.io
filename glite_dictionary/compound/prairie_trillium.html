prairie trillium

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛri ˈtrɪliəm / (AmE), / ˈpreəri ˈtrɪliəm / (BrE)
A North American plant of the genus Trillium, native to central United States, featuring dark purple, stalkless flowers.
North American plant with dark purple flowers

Examples

We saw a cluster of prairie trillium blooming along the woodland edge.
The distinctive dark purple flowers make the prairie trillium easy to identify in the spring.
Gardeners appreciate prairie trillium for its unique foliage and showy blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trillium - A genus of perennial flowering plants with three-petaled flowers
