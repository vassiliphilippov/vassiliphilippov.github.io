hog snapper

1. NOUN

/ hɑɡ ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / hɒɡ ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
a large wrasse found in the western Atlantic Ocean, with the mature male's head resembling a pig's snout
large Atlantic wrasse fish

Examples

The diver spotted a large hog snapper near the coral reef.
Grilled hog snapper is considered a delicacy in some coastal regions.
He identified the fish by its distinct snout as a male hog snapper.
