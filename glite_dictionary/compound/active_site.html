active site

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.tɪv saɪt / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪv saɪt / (BrE)
the region of an enzyme or antibody where a chemical reaction or binding occurs
part of an enzyme where reaction happens
medical

Examples

The substrate fits precisely into the enzyme's active site to initiate the reaction.
Mutations can alter the active site and reduce enzyme efficiency.
Researchers are studying how drugs interact with the active site of viral proteins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binding site - location on a protein that binds to specific molecules
