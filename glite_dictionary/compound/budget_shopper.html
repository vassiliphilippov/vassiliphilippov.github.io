budget shopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdʒət ˌʃɑpɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt ˌʃɒpə / (BrE)
a person who prefers to buy goods and services at the lowest possible prices
someone seeking low-cost options

Examples

As a budget shopper, Maria always waits for seasonal sales before making large purchases.
The new supermarket caters to the budget shopper with its wide range of affordable products.
Many budget shoppers use coupons and compare prices before choosing where to shop.

Synset Examples

Tesco Value has great deals for budget shoppers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain hunter - someone looking for the best deals or low prices when shopping
