pet shop

1. NOUN

/ pɛt ʃɑp / (AmE), / pɛt ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where people can buy pet animals and related supplies.
shop selling pet animals and supplies

Examples

We visited the pet shop to buy a hamster and some food.
The pet shop on Main Street has a wide variety of tropical fish.
Working at a pet shop taught her a lot about caring for animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pet store - A store where pets and pet supplies are sold (mainly US English)
