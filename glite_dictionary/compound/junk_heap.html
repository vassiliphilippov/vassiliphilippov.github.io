junk heap

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌŋk hiːp / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk hiːp / (BrE)
an untidy accumulation of discarded or worthless objects, especially in a pile
pile of discarded or worthless items

Examples

After years of neglect, the back yard became a junk heap filled with broken furniture and old appliances.
He bought the car for a low price, but it turned out to be a total junk heap.
The abandoned building site was nothing more than a junk heap by the end of the year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of rubbish or waste
  • Synonym: dump - a place where waste or unwanted items are left
  • Antonym: treasure - something of great value; the opposite of junk
