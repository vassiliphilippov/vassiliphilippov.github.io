Persian language

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜːrʒən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of Persia (Iran), including its ancient and modern forms, known as Farsi.
language of Iran (Farsi)

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Persian language.
The poetry of Rumi was originally written in the Persian language.
He decided to study the Persian language as part of his linguistic research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Farsi - Modern Persian, the official language of Iran

Etymology

From Latin 'Persia' + English 'language'; 'Persian' from Old Persian 'Pārsa'.
