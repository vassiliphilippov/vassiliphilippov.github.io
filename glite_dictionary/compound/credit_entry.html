credit entry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˌɛntri / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌɛntri / (BrE)
An accounting record showing an amount added to income, capital, or liabilities in an account.
an accounting record of income or capital added

Examples

The accountant recorded a credit entry in the company's cash account for the received payment.
At the end of the month, all credit entries must be balanced against the corresponding debits.
A credit entry increases the equity in the business according to double-entry bookkeeping.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: debit entry - an accounting record showing an amount subtracted or owed
