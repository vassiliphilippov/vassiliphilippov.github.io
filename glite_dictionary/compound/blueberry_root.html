blueberry root

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌbɛri ruːt / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri ruːt / (BrE)
A tall plant native to eastern North America and Asia, producing blue, berrylike fruit and possessing a thick, knotty rootstock formerly used in medicine.
plant with blue fruit and medicinal rootstock

Examples

The thick root of blueberry root was once commonly used to make traditional medicines.
Wild blueberry root thrives in damp forests and is valued for its blue fruit.
Botanists studied the properties of blueberry root for its possible health benefits.
