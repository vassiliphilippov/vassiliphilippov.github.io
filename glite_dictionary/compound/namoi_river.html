Namoi River

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæmɔɪ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈnæmɔɪ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in southeastern Australia that flows northwest to join the Darling River
river in southeastern Australia

Examples

The Namoi River is an important water source for agriculture in New South Wales.
Flooding along the Namoi River has affected several nearby communities this year.
Many native fish species inhabit the waters of the Namoi River.

Etymology

From a Kamilaroi (Gamilaraay) Aboriginal word, possibly meaning 'forked'.
