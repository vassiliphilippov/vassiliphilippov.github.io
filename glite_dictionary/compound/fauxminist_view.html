fauxminist view

1. NOUN

/ foʊˈmɪnɪst vjuː / (AmE), / fəʊˈmɪnɪst vjuː / (BrE)
An attitude or stance that appears to support feminism but does not reflect genuine commitment to feminist values or actions.
superficial support of feminism

Examples

Many criticized the politician’s fauxminist view as performative rather than impactful.
Corporate leaders sometimes adopt a fauxminist view to appeal to progressive audiences without enacting real change.
She called out the ad campaign for promoting a fauxminist view without supporting women behind the scenes.

Synset Examples

Her fauxminist views were evident in her social media posts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feminism - advocacy of women's rights and gender equality
  • Synonym: hypocrisy - claiming moral standards to which one's own behavior does not conform

Etymology

Blend of 'faux' (French, meaning false) and 'feminist'.
