promotional expense

1. NOUN

/ prəˈmoʊʃənəl ɪkˈspɛns / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃənəl ɪkˈspɛns / (BrE)
The money spent on advertising or publicizing a product, service, or company.
cost of advertising or promoting a product

Examples

The company's promotional expense increased significantly during the holiday campaign.
Our monthly budget includes a separate line for promotional expense.
Reducing promotional expense can sometimes hurt overall sales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising expense - costs spent specifically on advertising activities
