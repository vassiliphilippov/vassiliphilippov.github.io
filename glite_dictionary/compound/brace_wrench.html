brace wrench

1. NOUN

/ breɪs rɛntʃ / (AmE), / breɪs rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a wrench with a handle shaped like a crank and a socket head, used to turn bolts or nuts
wrench with crank-shaped handle and socket head

Examples

The mechanic used a brace wrench to loosen the rusted bolt.
A brace wrench is particularly useful when extra leverage is needed.
He reached into his toolbox and pulled out a sturdy brace wrench.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socket wrench - a wrench with a socketed head used to turn a fastener
