teeny-weeny

1. ADJ

/ ˌtiːni ˈwiːni / (AmE), / ˌtiːni ˈwiːni / (BrE)
Very small; extremely tiny.
very small; tiny
informal

The puppy was so teeny-weeny that it could fit in the palm of my hand.
