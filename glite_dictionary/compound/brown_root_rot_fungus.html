brown root rot fungus

1. NOUN

/ braʊn ruːt rɑt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / braʊn ruːt rɒt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that infects and causes brown root rot disease in plants such as pea, potato, and cucumber
fungus causing brown root rot disease

Examples

The brown root rot fungus can devastate entire potato fields if left untreated.
Researchers are developing new treatments to combat the brown root rot fungus in cucumber crops.
Early detection of brown root rot fungus is crucial for managing agricultural losses.
