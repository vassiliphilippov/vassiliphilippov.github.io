buckwheat pancake

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌwiːt ˈpæn.keɪk / (AmE), / ˈbʌkwiːt ˈpæn.keɪk / (BrE)
a thin, flat cake made from buckwheat flour, often served for breakfast
pancake made from buckwheat flour

Examples

She ordered a stack of buckwheat pancakes with fresh berries and maple syrup for breakfast.
Many people enjoy buckwheat pancake topped with honey as a nutritious morning meal.
These gluten-free buckwheat pancakes are a popular choice for those with wheat allergies.

Synset Examples

Buckwheat pancakes are a healthy breakfast choice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin flat cake of batter, typically fried on both sides in a pan
