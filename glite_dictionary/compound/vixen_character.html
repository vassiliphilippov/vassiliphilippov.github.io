vixen character

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪksən ˈkærəktər / (AmE), / ˈvɪksən ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
a female character in literature, film, or other media who is portrayed as seductive or alluring, often using her attractiveness to influence others
seductive female character (media trope)

Examples

The film's main antagonist is a classic vixen character who uses her charm to manipulate others.
Many comic books feature a vixen character to add intrigue and appeal to the story.
She was typecast as the vixen character in several popular television dramas.

Synset Examples

The marketing campaign featured a vixen character to attract attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: femme fatale - an attractive and mysterious woman who leads men into dangerous situations

Etymology

Based on 'vixen' (from Old English *fyxen*, female fox) combined with 'character'.
