real chugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiəl ˈtʃʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈtʃʌɡə / (BrE)
A person who works hard and persistently, often continuing steadily despite difficulties.
hardworking, persistent person
informal

Examples

She managed to finish every project on time because she's a real chugger at work.
Even after hours at the gym, Mark is still a real chugger when everyone else is exhausted.
You have to be a real chugger to make it through med school.

Synset Examples

I admire him; he’s a real chugger when it comes to workouts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grafter - a hard-working person
  • Antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work
