crew member

1. NOUN

/ kru ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / kruː ˈmembə / (BrE)
a person who is part of a group working together, especially on a ship, aircraft, or in a film or television production
member of a working group or team

Examples

Every crew member must attend the safety briefing before the flight.
The director thanked each crew member for their hard work on the set.
As a crew member on the ship, she traveled to ports all over the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: team member - a person who belongs to a team

2. NOUN

/ kruː ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / kruː ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who is part of a group working together on a plane, ship, or other organized operation, especially as part of a flight crew
member of a flight crew or ship's crew

Examples

Every crew member on the plane went through emergency training.
The captain thanked each crew member personally after the successful voyage.
As a crew member, she is responsible for passenger safety during flights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crewman - a member of a crew, especially on a ship or aircraft
