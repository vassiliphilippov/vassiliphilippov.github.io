kokoi venom

1. NOUN

/ koʊˌkɔɪ ˈvɛnəm / (AmE), / kəʊˌkɔɪ ˈvɛnəm / (BrE)
a potent neurotoxin present in the skin of the kokoi frog, capable of causing severe or fatal effects in predators or humans
neurotoxin from kokoi frog

Examples

The poison dart contains kokoi venom, making it extremely dangerous to touch.
Researchers are studying the effects of kokoi venom on the human nervous system.
Predators avoid the frog because its skin secretes kokoi venom.
