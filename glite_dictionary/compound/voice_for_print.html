voice for print

1. NOUN

/ vɔɪs fər prɪnt / (AmE), / vɔɪs fə prɪnt / (BrE)
a style of writing that is appropriate or suitable for publication, especially in print media
writing style suitable for publication

Examples

The author spent years honing his voice for print before submitting his work to publishers.
Developing a voice for print can be challenging for those used to conversational writing.
Editors often help new writers refine their voice for print to meet publication standards.

Synset Examples

He struggled to develop a voice for print that felt authentic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writing style - the manner or technique an author uses to write
