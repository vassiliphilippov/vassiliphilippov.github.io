sugar spoon

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːɡɚ spuːn / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə spuːn / (BrE)
a small spoon, often shaped like a seashell, used for serving sugar
small spoon for serving sugar

Examples

She placed the sugar spoon next to the bowl during the tea party.
The antique sugar spoon had a delicate seashell design on its bowl.
Please use the sugar spoon instead of your own to sweeten your coffee.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: teaspoon - a small spoon used typically for stirring tea or coffee
  • Degree Higher: tablespoon - a large spoon used for serving food
