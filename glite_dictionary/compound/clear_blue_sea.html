clear blue sea

1. NOUN

/ klɪr blu si / (AmE), / klɪə bluː siː / (BrE)
a calm body of seawater that is both clear and blue, often considered especially beautiful
calm, beautiful blue seawater

Examples

She gazed out over the clear blue sea and felt instantly relaxed.
The island's beaches are famous for their clear blue sea and fine white sand.
We spent the afternoon swimming in the clear blue sea under a cloudless sky.

Synset Examples

She longed to dive into the clear blue sea.
The clear blue water looked inviting on a hot day.
