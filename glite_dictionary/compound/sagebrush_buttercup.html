sagebrush buttercup

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈseɪdʒˌbrʌʃ ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
a small, early-flowering plant of western North America, with shiny yellow flowers, typically found in sagebrush habitats
a yellow-flowered plant of western North America

Examples

The fields were dotted with blooming sagebrush buttercup after the early spring rains.
We identified the sagebrush buttercup by its shiny yellow petals and small size.
Hiking in western Montana, she was delighted to spot a rare sagebrush buttercup near the trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - a common name for plants of the genus Ranunculus, known for yellow flowers
