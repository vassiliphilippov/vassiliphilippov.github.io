collar blight

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlər blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒlə blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease affecting the trunks of pear and apple trees, characterized by lesions and decay near the ground level of the trunk
plant disease of pear and apple tree trunks

Examples

The orchard suffered heavy losses due to collar blight attacking the apple trees.
Proper drainage can reduce the risk of collar blight in pear orchards.
Symptoms of collar blight include bark decay and cankers near the base of the tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire blight - a serious plant disease affecting apples, pears, and related plants
