eleventeen years old

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌlɛvənˈtiːn jɪrz oʊld / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛvənˈtiːn jɪəz əʊld / (BrE)
humorously used to describe a fictional age between eleven and twelve
fictional playful age between eleven and twelve
informal

Examples

When asked his age, he grinned and said he was eleventeen years old.
She insists she's eleventeen years old whenever she wants to act older than eleven.
He joked about being eleventeen years old to get away with staying up late.

Synset Examples

She said she's eleventeen years old today.

Etymology

Blend of 'eleven' and 'seventeen' by analogy with existing numbers, used humorously.
