e-mail message

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːˌmeɪl ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈiːˌmeɪl ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
A message sent electronically over the internet using the email system.
a message sent via email

Examples

I received an e-mail message from my colleague about the meeting.
Please check your e-mail message for the document I just sent you.
He forgot to attach the file to his e-mail message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - a system for sending messages electronically
  • Degree Lower: text message - a written message sent by mobile phone

Etymology

‘E-mail’ is a shortening of ‘electronic mail;’ ‘message’ from Latin ‘missus’ (a sending).
