postage meter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstɪdʒ ˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪdʒ ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a machine used for imprinting prepaid postage on mail and recording the total charge, especially for bulk mailings
machine for imprinting postage on mail

Examples

The company uses a postage meter to streamline its monthly mailings.
After calibrating the postage meter, she processed hundreds of outgoing letters efficiently.
Many offices invest in a postage meter to save time and money on bulk mail.
