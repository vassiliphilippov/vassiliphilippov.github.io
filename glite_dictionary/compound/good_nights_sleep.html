good night's sleep

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd naɪts sliːp / (AmE), / ɡʊd naɪts sliːp / (BrE)
A period of sleep during the night that is sufficiently restful to restore energy and improve well-being.
restful, restorative night of sleep

Examples

After a good night's sleep, I felt ready to tackle the day ahead.
Doctors recommend a good night's sleep to help your body recover from illness.
She finally enjoyed a good night's sleep after days of stress and worry.
