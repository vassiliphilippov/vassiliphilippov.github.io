auto-mechanic

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːtoʊ məˈkænɪk / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ mɪˈkænɪk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to repair and maintain automobiles
car repair and maintenance worker

Examples

The auto-mechanic diagnosed the problem with my engine within a few minutes.
After her car broke down, she called an auto-mechanic for assistance.
He works as an auto-mechanic at a local repair shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanic - a person who repairs and maintains machinery

Etymology

From 'auto-' (short for automobile) and 'mechanic' (from Greek mēkhanikos, relating to machines).
