nuclear terrorism

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈter.əˌrɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈter.ər.ɪzm / (BrE)
The use or threat of use of nuclear devices or radioactive materials by terrorist organizations to cause widespread destruction and fear.
terrorism involving nuclear materials

Examples

Governments worldwide are preparing for the possibility of nuclear terrorism by strengthening security around nuclear facilities.
The threat of nuclear terrorism remains a major concern for international security agencies.
Experts met to discuss how to prevent nuclear terrorism at a recent global summit.

Synset Examples

assaults on nuclear power plants is one form of nuclear terrorism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrorism - the unlawful use of violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in pursuit of political aims
