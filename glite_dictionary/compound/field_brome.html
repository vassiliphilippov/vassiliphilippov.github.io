field brome

1. NOUN

/ fiːld broʊm / (AmE), / fiːld brəʊm / (BrE)
an annual grass native to Europe and temperate Asia, often found in cultivated fields and waste places
an annual grass of Europe and Asia

Examples

The farmer noticed that field brome was spreading quickly across his land.
Many botanists study field brome due to its adaptability to different soils.
We found patches of field brome growing along the rural pathways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brome - any grass of the genus Bromus
