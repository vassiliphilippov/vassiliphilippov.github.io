jump rope

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌmp roʊp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp rəʊp / (BrE)
A length of rope, often with handles at each end, used for skipping over as it is swung under the feet and over the head.
rope for skipping and jumping over

Examples

She bought a new jump rope to practice her skipping skills at the gym.
Children were laughing and playing with a jump rope at recess.
Using a jump rope is a great way to improve cardiovascular fitness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skipping rope - A rope used for jumping over; common British English term for jump rope

2. NOUN

/ dʒʌmp roʊp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp rəʊp / (BrE)
A game or exercise in which a rope is swung over and under the body while one or more people jump over it as it passes under their feet.
game or exercise with jumping over rope

Examples

The children played jump rope at recess until the bell rang.
Many athletes use jump rope as part of their regular workout routine.
She learned new tricks with her jump rope and performed them at the talent show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skipping rope - a British term for the rope or the game of jumping over it
