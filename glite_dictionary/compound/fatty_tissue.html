fatty tissue

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæti ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈfæti ˈtɪsjuː / (BrE)
Body tissue made up of cells that store fat, serving as a source of energy and helping to cushion and insulate the body.
body tissue storing fat
medical

Examples

Polar bears have a thick layer of fatty tissue to keep them warm in freezing temperatures.
During weight loss, the body burns fatty tissue for energy.
Doctors found an abnormal accumulation of fatty tissue around his liver.

Synset Examples

fatty tissue protected them from the severe cold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adipose tissue - body tissue containing stored fat, similar to fatty tissue
