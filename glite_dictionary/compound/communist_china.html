Communist China

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːmjənɪst ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnɪst ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
The People's Republic of China, especially as governed by the Communist Party since 1949.
China under Communist rule

Examples

Many Western governments did not officially recognize Communist China until the 1970s.
After 1949, Communist China implemented a series of sweeping land reforms.
Trade relations with Communist China have steadily increased since the 1980s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: China - A country in East Asia
  • Synonym: People's Republic of China - Official name for Communist-ruled China since 1949
  • Antonym: Republic of China - The government of China before 1949
