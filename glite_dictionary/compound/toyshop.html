toyshop

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells children's toys
shop selling toys for children

Examples

We stopped at the toyshop to buy a birthday present for Ethan.
The new toyshop in town has a wide selection of board games and dolls.
As a child, she dreamed of owning every item in the local toyshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy store - a shop where toys are sold (American English)
