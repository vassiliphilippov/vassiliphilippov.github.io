stewing pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuːɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ˈstjuːɪŋ pæn / (BrE)
a deep saucepan used for stewing food
deep pan for stewing food

Examples

She placed the vegetables and beef into the stewing pan to simmer for several hours.
Make sure your stewing pan has a tight-fitting lid to retain moisture.
After washing the stewing pan, she put it back on the kitchen shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saucepan - a deep cooking pan with a handle, used for cooking on a stove
