long-distance runner

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔŋ ˌdɪstəns ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ ˌdɪstəns ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in races that cover long distances, especially marathons and similar events
athlete who runs long-distance races

Examples

As a long-distance runner, she trains for hours every day to improve her endurance.
Mo Farah is a famous long-distance runner who has won multiple Olympic medals.
It takes both physical and mental strength to be a successful long-distance runner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marathoner - an athlete who runs marathons
