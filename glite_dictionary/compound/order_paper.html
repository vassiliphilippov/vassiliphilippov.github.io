order paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a printed document listing the subjects to be discussed or the business to be conducted in a legislative assembly on a particular day
legislative agenda document

Examples

The clerk distributed the order paper to all members before the session began.
According to today's order paper, the budget debate will take place in the afternoon.
Any amendments must be submitted prior to the publication of the order paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agenda - a list of items to be discussed at a meeting
