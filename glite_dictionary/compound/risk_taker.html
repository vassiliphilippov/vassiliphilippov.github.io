risk taker

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪsk ˌteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrɪsk ˌteɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is willing to take risks, often seeking gain or excitement by doing so
person willing to take risks

Examples

Sarah is a natural risk taker, always eager to try new and challenging activities.
Entrepreneurs are often described as risk takers who are not afraid of failure.
Being a risk taker can lead to both great rewards and significant setbacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daredevil - person who takes daring or reckless risks
  • Synonym: adventurer - person who enjoys exciting and often dangerous activities
  • Antonym: play-it-safe - a person who avoids taking risks
