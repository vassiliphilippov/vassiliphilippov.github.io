palm kernel

1. NOUN

/ pɑm ˈkɝnəl / (AmE), / pɑːm ˈkɜːnəl / (BrE)
the seed of an oil palm, from which palm kernel oil is extracted
seed of an oil palm

Examples

Many foods in West Africa are cooked using palm kernel oil.
The palm kernel is separated from the palm fruit during processing.
Farmers collect palm kernels to produce both animal feed and cooking oil.
