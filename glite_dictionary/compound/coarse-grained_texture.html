coarse-grained texture

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrs ɡreɪnd ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːs ɡreɪnd ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
A texture characterized by a rough or uneven surface, with large, easily distinguishable particles or features.
rough or uneven surface texture

Examples

The pot had a coarse-grained texture that made it pleasant to grip.
The rocks in this region show a coarse-grained texture due to slow cooling.
A coarse-grained texture can add a rustic quality to finished wood surfaces.

Synset Examples

The artist preferred a coarse-grained texture for her sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fine-grained texture - A texture with a smooth or even surface and very small, hard-to-distinguish features
  • Synonym: coarse - Rough in texture or structure
