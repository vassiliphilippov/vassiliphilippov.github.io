sex organ

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / sɛks ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an organ involved in sexual reproduction, such as the penis, vagina, testes, or ovaries
organ involved in sexual reproduction

Examples

The doctor explained how each sex organ plays a critical role in reproduction.
Research focuses on the development of sex organs during puberty.
Different animals have evolved a variety of sex organs suited to their environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitalia - the external sex organs
  • Synonym: reproductive organ - any organ involved in reproduction
