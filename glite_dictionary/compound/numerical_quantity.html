numerical quantity

1. NOUN

/ nuˈmɛrɪkəl ˈkwɑn.t̬ə.t̬i / (AmE), / njuːˈmɛrɪkəl ˈkwɒn.tɪ.ti / (BrE)
an amount or value expressed as a number, rather than described qualitatively
amount expressed as a number

Examples

The scientist measured the chemical reaction using a numerical quantity rather than descriptive terms.
Temperature is a numerical quantity that can be compared across different samples.
Please enter the numerical quantity of items you wish to purchase in the box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: number - a mathematical value used to count, measure, or label
  • Synonym: quantity - an amount or number of something
