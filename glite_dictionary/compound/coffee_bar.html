coffee bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi bɑr / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi bɑː / (BrE)
A small restaurant or café where coffee, drinks, and light snacks are served.
small café serving coffee and snacks

Examples

I met Sarah at the new coffee bar across the street for a quick chat.
The city is full of independent coffee bars offering unique brews and pastries.
After work, he likes to unwind at a coffee bar with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - small restaurant selling light meals and drinks
  • Synonym: coffeehouse - establishment where coffee and other refreshments are served
