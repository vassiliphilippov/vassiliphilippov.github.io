guilt feelings

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪlt ˈfilɪŋz / (AmE), / ɡɪlt ˈfiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
a sense of remorse or responsibility for an offense or wrongdoing
remorse for an offense or wrongdoing

Examples

She was overwhelmed by guilt feelings after lying to her friend.
Therapists often help patients process their guilt feelings to promote emotional healing.
After the accident, his guilt feelings made it difficult for him to sleep at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
  • Degree Higher: shame - a painful feeling of humiliation or distress caused by consciousness of wrong
