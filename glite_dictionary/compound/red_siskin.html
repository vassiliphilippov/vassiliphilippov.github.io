red siskin

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈsɪskɪn / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsɪskɪn / (BrE)
A South American finch with bright red plumage and black head, wings, and tail.
a South American scarlet finch

Examples

The red siskin is now considered an endangered species due to habitat loss and illegal trapping.
Birdwatchers traveled to Venezuela to catch a glimpse of the vibrant red siskin in the wild.
The red siskin's striking coloration makes it a favorite among aviculture enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finch - a small to medium-sized passerine bird of the family Fringillidae
