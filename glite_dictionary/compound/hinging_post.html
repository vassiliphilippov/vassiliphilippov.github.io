hinging post

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪndʒɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ˈhɪndʒɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
the gatepost upon which a gate is mounted and swings open or closed
gatepost supporting a hinged gate

Examples

The new metal gate was attached to the hinging post with heavy-duty hinges.
Over time, the wooden hinging post began to rot and needed to be replaced.
Before installing the gate, make sure the hinging post is firmly set in the ground.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: latching post - the gatepost on which a gate's latch is fixed
  • Synonym: gatepost - the vertical post at either side of a gate
