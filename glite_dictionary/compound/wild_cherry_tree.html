wild cherry tree

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈtʃɛri tri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈtʃɛri triː / (BrE)
A cherry tree growing in the wild, especially as opposed to a cultivated variety.
an uncultivated cherry tree

Examples

The old orchard was overgrown with wild cherry trees that blossomed each spring.
Birds love to nest in the branches of the wild cherry tree near the stream.
We found ripe fruit on a wild cherry tree during our hike in the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry tree - tree that bears cherries
