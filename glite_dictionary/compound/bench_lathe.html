bench lathe

1. NOUN

/ bɛntʃ ˈleɪð / (AmE), / bɛntʃ ˈleɪð / (BrE)
A small lathe designed to be mounted on a workbench, typically used for precision work on metal or wood.
small lathe mounted on a workbench

Examples

He used the bench lathe to carefully shape the small metal rod.
The bench lathe is ideal for making precise parts in a home workshop.
Many jewelers rely on a bench lathe for detailed finishing work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lathe - a machine for shaping wood, metal, or other material
