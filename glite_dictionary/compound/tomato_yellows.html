tomato yellows

1. NOUN

/ təˈmeɪtoʊ ˈjɛloʊz / (AmE), / təˈmɑːtəʊ ˈjɛləʊz / (BrE)
A viral disease of tomato plants characterized by yellowing of the leaves and stunted growth.
plant disease causing yellowing in tomatoes

Examples

If untreated, tomato yellows can cause severe crop losses for farmers.
The yellowing and curling of leaves indicated that their plants had tomato yellows.
Gardeners are advised to check for signs of tomato yellows during the growing season.
