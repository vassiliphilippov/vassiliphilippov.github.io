energy sector

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi ˌsɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˌsɛktə / (BrE)
the part of a country's economy concerned with the production and distribution of energy, such as oil, gas, electricity, and renewables
economic segment for energy production and distribution

Examples

The government's investment in the energy sector aims to promote clean technologies.
Many jobs have been created as the energy sector shifts toward renewables.
Policy changes can have a major impact on the energy sector worldwide.

Synset Examples

The nucleocrat held significant power in the energy sector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energy industry - industry engaged in production and supply of energy
