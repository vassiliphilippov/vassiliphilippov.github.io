dribbling drunk

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪblɪŋ drʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈdrɪblɪŋ drʌŋk / (BrE)
a person who is extremely intoxicated, often behaving in a helpless or foolish manner due to excessive alcohol consumption
a person who is very drunk
informal

Examples

The bartender refused to serve the dribbling drunk staggering at the counter.
One dribbling drunk tried to dance and knocked over a chair.
I don't want to end up like a dribbling drunk at every party.

Synset Examples

The party ended with a dribbling drunk making a scene.
Everyone laughed at the dribbling drunk trying to sing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drunk - a person who is intoxicated by alcohol
  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
