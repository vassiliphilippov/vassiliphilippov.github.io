sumo wrestler

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːmoʊ ˈrɛslər / (AmE), / ˈsuːməʊ ˈrɛslə / (BrE)
A professional athlete who competes in sumo, a traditional Japanese form of wrestling.
professional competitor in sumo wrestling

Examples

After years of intense training, he finally became a respected sumo wrestler in Tokyo.
The sumo wrestler used his size and skill to overpower his opponent during the match.
Children watched in awe as the sumo wrestler performed the ceremonial stomp before the competition.

Synset Examples

sumo wrestlers are large heavy men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestler - a person who takes part in wrestling

Etymology

From Japanese 'sumō' (sumo) + English 'wrestler'.
