vintage clothing

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪntɪdʒ ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪntɪdʒ ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
fashionable or collectible clothing from a previous era, especially considered valuable or stylish today
fashionable old clothing

Examples

She loves shopping for vintage clothing at local thrift stores.
Many designers draw inspiration from vintage clothing for their new collections.
His outfit combined vintage clothing with modern accessories for a unique style.

Synset Examples

The emporium was filled with vintage clothes and antiques.
She has a wardrobe full of vintage clothing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retro - clothing or style imitating a recent past era, especially from mid-20th century onward
  • Degree Higher: antique - object made in an earlier period, typically at least 100 years old
