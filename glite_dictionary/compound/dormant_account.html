dormant account

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrmənt əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈdɔːm(ə)nt əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
A bank account, typically a savings account, that has shown no customer-initiated activity for a set period, except for the posting of interest.
inactive bank account over a period

Examples

The bank closes any dormant account that remains unused for more than two years.
After discovering a dormant account, she needed to provide identification to reactivate it.
Interest continued to accumulate in his dormant account despite the lack of transactions.

Synset Examples

the dormant account reverted to the state under escheat laws

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inactive account - an account with no recent activity
