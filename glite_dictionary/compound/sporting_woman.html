sporting woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrdɪŋ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who enjoys or takes part in outdoor activities or sports
woman keen on outdoor activities

Examples

She has always been a sporting woman, spending her weekends hiking and cycling in the countryside.
At the awards dinner, the mayor praised her as a true sporting woman for her dedication to community sports.
Known among her friends as a sporting woman, she never misses an opportunity to try something adventurous outdoors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sportswoman - a woman who is actively involved in sports
  • Antonym: sportsman - a man who is actively involved in sports
