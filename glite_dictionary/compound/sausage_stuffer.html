sausage stuffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɪdʒ ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
a device used to force ground meat into a casing to make sausages, typically operated by hand
device for filling sausage casings

Examples

The butcher filled the casings with pork using a sausage stuffer.
I bought a new sausage stuffer for my homemade bratwursts.
A sausage stuffer makes the process much faster and less messy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sausage maker - a person or device that makes sausages
