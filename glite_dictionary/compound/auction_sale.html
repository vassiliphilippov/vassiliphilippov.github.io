auction sale

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔkʃən seɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːkʃən seɪl / (BrE)
a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder
a sale to the highest bidder

Examples

The house was sold at an auction sale after the owner moved abroad.
Several rare paintings will be offered at tomorrow's auction sale.
The company recovered its losses by organizing an auction sale of surplus equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auction - a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder
