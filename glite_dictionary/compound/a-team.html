A-team

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ ˌtiːm / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˌtiːm / (BrE)
a group of the best members, especially in military or organizational settings, chosen for their skills or leadership abilities
elite or main group of people

Examples

Only the company's A-team was allowed to handle such a complex project.
During the rescue mission, they called in the A-team for their expertise.
He was proud to be selected as part of the A-team for the new assignment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: B-team - a secondary group, usually less skilled or experienced
  • Synonym: elite - the best or most skilled members of a group

Etymology

From the letter 'A' indicating top ranking or primary, combined with 'team'.
