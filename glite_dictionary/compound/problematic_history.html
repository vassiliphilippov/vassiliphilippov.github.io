problematic history

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɑːbləˈmætɪk hɪsˈtɔri / (AmE), / ˌprɒbləˈmætɪk hɪsˈtəri / (BrE)
A background or past event associated with controversy, sensitivity, or issues regarded as objectionable or troublesome.
controversial or sensitive background/past

Examples

The report explores the problematic history of colonialism in the region.
That monument has a problematic history many people would rather not confront.
Educators should not ignore the problematic history behind certain national holidays.

Synset Examples

It's important to acknowledge the problematic history of pickaninny Christmas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversial history - a history marked by controversy or public disagreement
  • Degree Lower: history - the whole series of past events connected with a person or thing
