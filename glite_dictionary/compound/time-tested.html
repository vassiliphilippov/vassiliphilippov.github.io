time-tested

1. ADJ

/ taɪm ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / taɪm ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
having been tried and proven reliable or effective over a long period of time
proven reliable by experience

Examples

This is a time-tested method for preserving food.
The company relies on time-tested principles to ensure quality and consistency.
His advice comes from time-tested experience in the industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proven - demonstrated by evidence or trial to be true or reliable
  • Antonym: unproven - not shown to be true or reliable by experience or evidence
