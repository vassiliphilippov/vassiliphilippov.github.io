lovely ginge

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvli dʒɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvli dʒɪndʒ / (BrE)
A pleasant or attractive shade of red hair.
pleasant shade of red hair
informal british

Examples

She dyed her hair a lovely ginge to brighten her look.
Many celebrities are famous for their natural lovely ginge locks.
He always admired the soft, lovely ginge of her hair.

Synset Examples

He has a lovely ginge in his hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger - a person with red hair or the color itself

Etymology

'Ginge' is a British informal shortening of 'ginger' (as in ginger-haired, meaning red-haired).
