marmalade box

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrməˌleɪd bɑks / (AmE), / ˈmɑːməleɪd bɒks / (BrE)
a tree native to the West Indies and northern South America that bears succulent, edible, orange-sized fruit
tree with edible orange-sized fruit

Examples

The marmalade box produces delicious fruit that is prized in local markets.
We planted a marmalade box in the garden for its shade and edible fruit.
Many birds are attracted to the ripe fruit of the marmalade box.
