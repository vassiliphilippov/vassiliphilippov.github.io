Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈrɑːwi ˌɛrəb ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / səˈrɑːwiː ˌærəb ˌdɛm.əˈkræt.ɪk rɪˈpʌb.lɪk / (BrE)
A partially recognized state in the western Maghreb region of North Africa, claiming sovereignty over Western Sahara.
partially recognized state in North Africa

Examples

The Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic proclaimed its independence in 1976.
Many countries do not officially recognize the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic as a sovereign state.
Delegates from the Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic attended the summit on African affairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Western Sahara - a disputed territory in North Africa
