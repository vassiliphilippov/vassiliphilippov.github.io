off fleek

1. ADJ

/ ɑf flik / (AmE), / ɒf fliːk / (BrE)
(slang) Not looking attractive or well-styled; lacking a good appearance.
not looking attractive
slang

Examples

After the rain ruined my hair, I felt totally off fleek all day.
His outfit was a bit off fleek compared to the rest of the group at the party.
If your makeup is off fleek, just touch it up before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on fleek - looking perfect or extremely well-groomed

Etymology

Formed by analogy with the slang phrase 'on fleek', where 'fleek' is of uncertain origin.
