work flow

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːk floʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk fləʊ / (BrE)
the sequence or rate of progress of tasks or work being completed, especially in a business or industrial setting
sequence or rate of progress in work

Examples

The new software improved our work flow and saved us several hours each week.
Interruptions can disrupt the work flow in any office environment.
Efficient work flow is essential for meeting project deadlines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workflow - work flow spelled as one word; alternative form of the same concept
