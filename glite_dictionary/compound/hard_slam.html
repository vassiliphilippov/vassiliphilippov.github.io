hard slam

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd slæm / (AmE), / hɑːd slæm / (BrE)
a very forceful and loud closing of a door or similar object
forceful closure of a door

Examples

She gave the door a hard slam before leaving the room.
The window closed with a hard slam during the storm last night.
A hard slam of the gate startled everyone in the yard.

Synset Examples

She gave the door a hard slam before leaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slam - the act of shutting something forcefully and noisily
