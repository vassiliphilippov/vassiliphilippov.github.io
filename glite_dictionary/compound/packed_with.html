packed with

1. ADJ

/ pækt wɪð / (AmE), / pækt wɪð / (BrE)
containing a very large number or amount of something; filled to capacity with people, things, or qualities.
filled to capacity; containing a large amount

Examples

The stadium was packed with excited fans for the championship game.
Her bag was packed with everything she could possibly need for the trip.
The market is packed with fresh fruits and vegetables every weekend.

Synset Examples
