video iPod

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪdioʊ ˈaɪpɒd / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊ ˈaɪpɒd / (BrE)
a model of Apple's iPod portable media player capable of playing video files in addition to audio
iPod capable of playing video files

Examples

The new video iPod lets users watch TV shows and movies on the go.
She loaded her favorite music videos onto her video iPod before her trip.
When it was released, the video iPod quickly became a must-have gadget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iPod - Apple's portable digital music player

Etymology

iPod is an Apple Inc. trademark; 'video' from Latin 'videre' (to see).
