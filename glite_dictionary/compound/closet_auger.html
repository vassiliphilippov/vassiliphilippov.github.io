closet auger

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɑːsət ˈɔːɡər / (AmE), / ˈklɒzɪt ˈɔːɡə / (BrE)
a plumbing tool with a flexible shaft and crank handle, used to clear blockages from toilets
plumbing tool for unclogging toilets

Examples

The plumber used a closet auger to remove the blockage from the toilet.
It is a good idea to have a closet auger handy for stubborn clogs.
After several attempts with a plunger, I finally cleared the toilet using a closet auger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumber's snake - a flexible tool used to clear drain clogs

Etymology

From 'closet' (archaic term for toilet) and 'auger' (boring tool), reflecting its use in toilets.
