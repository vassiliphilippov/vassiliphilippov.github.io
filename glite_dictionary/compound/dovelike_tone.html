dovelike tone

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊvˌlaɪk toʊn / (AmE), / ˈdəʊvˌlaɪk təʊn / (BrE)
a gentle, soothing way of speaking, resembling the softness or peace commonly associated with a dove
gentle and soothing manner of speaking

Examples

She used a dovelike tone to calm the worried patients in the waiting room.
With a dovelike tone, the teacher reassured the class after the loud thunderstorm.
His dovelike tone made everyone feel safe and welcome during the meeting.

Synset Examples

He spoke in a dovelike tone that soothed the children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soothing - having a calming effect
  • Antonym: harsh tone - an unpleasant, aggressive, or rough manner of speaking
