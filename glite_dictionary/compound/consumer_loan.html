consumer loan

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsuːmɚ loʊn / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmə ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan granted to an individual for personal, family, or household purposes, typically unsecured and based on the borrower's ability to repay
loan for personal use

Examples

She applied for a consumer loan to finance a new car.
A consumer loan often requires proof of steady income and good credit history.
Banks are offering lower interest rates on consumer loans this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal loan - a loan for personal, non-business purposes
