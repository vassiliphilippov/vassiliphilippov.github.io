badminton court

1. NOUN

/ ˈbædˌmɪntən kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈbadˌmɪntən kɔːt / (BrE)
an area marked out for playing badminton, typically with a net dividing the two sides
area for playing badminton

Examples

We reserved the badminton court for an hour-long match this evening.
The new sports center has an indoor badminton court with official markings.
After sweeping the badminton court, the players started warming up for their game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tennis court - area marked out for playing tennis
