commodity brokerage

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɑdədi ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdəti ˈbrəʊkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a firm or company that buys and sells commodities on behalf of clients for a commission or fee
firm dealing in commodity trading

Examples

She started her career at a well-known commodity brokerage in Chicago.
Many investors rely on a commodity brokerage to access futures markets.
The commodity brokerage reported higher profits after an increase in oil trading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brokerage - a firm that acts as an intermediary, buying and selling assets for clients
