devil worshiper

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəl ˌwɜrʃəɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl ˌwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who worships the devil or evil supernatural beings
person who worships the devil

Examples

Rumors spread that a devil worshiper lived in the old, abandoned house.
The villagers became suspicious of anyone they believed to be a devil worshiper.
She wrote an article about the history of devil worshipers in modern culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satanist - a person who worships Satan
