free weight

1. NOUN

/ fri weɪt / (AmE), / friː weɪt / (BrE)
a piece of exercise equipment, such as a dumbbell or barbell, that is not attached to a machine and is lifted and moved freely by the user
unattached exercise equipment for lifting

Examples

She prefers using free weights at the gym instead of weight machines.
A personal trainer showed him how to lift free weights safely and effectively.
Many athletes incorporate free weights into their strength training routines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumbbell - a short bar with a weight at each end, used for exercise
  • Synonym: barbell - a long bar with weights attached at each end, used in weightlifting
