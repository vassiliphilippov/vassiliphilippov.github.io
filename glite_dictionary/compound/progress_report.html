progress report

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːɡrəs rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡres rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a report detailing the work accomplished or progress made over a specified period of time
report on work accomplished

Examples

The manager asked everyone to submit a progress report by the end of the week.
Her teacher gave her parents a detailed progress report during the meeting.
We discussed the latest progress report at the monthly update session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: status report - a report on the current status of work or a project
