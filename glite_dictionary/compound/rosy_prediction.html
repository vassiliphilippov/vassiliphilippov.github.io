rosy prediction

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊzi prɪˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi prɪˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
an optimistic forecast or expectation, often suggesting undue hopefulness
optimistic forecast or expectation

Examples

Analysts offered a rosy prediction for the company's growth over the next year.
Despite the challenges, she made a rosy prediction about the project's outcome.
Not everyone agreed with his rosy prediction for the future of the industry.

Synset Examples

His rosy predictions about the stocks proved accurate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimistic forecast - a forecast that expects the best possible outcome
