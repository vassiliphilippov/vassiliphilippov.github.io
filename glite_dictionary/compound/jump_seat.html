jump seat

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌmp siːt / (AmE), / dʒʌmp siːt / (BrE)
a small, folding seat in an automobile or other vehicle, intended for occasional use
folding seat in a vehicle

Examples

The taxi had an extra jump seat that folded away when not in use.
She sat on the jump seat during the crowded ride downtown.
He unfolded the jump seat to make room for the additional passenger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folding seat - a seat that can be folded up when not in use

2. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌmp siːt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmp siːt / (BrE)
a seat in a vehicle, often smaller or temporary, that can be folded up or down when not in use
folding seat in a vehicle

Examples

The flight attendant demonstrated how to secure the jump seat before takeoff.
We added an extra jump seat in the back of the van for our trip.
He unfolded the jump seat so another passenger could have a place to sit.

Synset Examples

He sat in the jump seat next to the driver.
She prefers the jump seat because it feels more spacious.

Etymology

Originally American English, possibly from the seat 'jumping' up when released.
