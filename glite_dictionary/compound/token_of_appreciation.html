token of appreciation

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊkən əv əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkən əv əˌpriːʃɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a small gift or gesture given to express gratitude to someone
small gift or gesture of gratitude

Examples

After the event, each volunteer was given a token of appreciation for their hard work.
He sent his teacher a token of appreciation at the end of the semester.
The company presented long-serving staff with a token of appreciation during the ceremony.

Synset Examples

She received a token of appreciation from her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thank-you - an expression of thanks or gratitude, often in the form of a note or gift
