crash course

1. NOUN

/ kræʃ kɔrs / (AmE), / kræʃ kɔːs / (BrE)
A short and intensive course of study or training designed to teach essential information or skills in a limited amount of time, often in response to urgent need.
short, intensive training course

Examples

She enrolled in a crash course in French before her business trip to Paris.
The new employees were given a crash course on company procedures during orientation.
After his computer broke, Mark took a crash course in basic IT repair.

Synset Examples

he took a crash course in Italian on his way to Italy
his first job was a crash course in survival and in learning how to get along with people
a crash programme is needed to create new jobs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensive course - a course that covers material in a concentrated, fast-paced format
  • Synonym: refresher course - a course aimed at updating or reviewing skills or knowledge
