house of ease

1. NOUN

/ haʊs əv iz / (AmE), / haʊs əv iːz / (BrE)
a place that provides comfort, relaxation, and respite from stress or difficulty
a place of comfort and relaxation

Examples

He considered his garden a house of ease after a long day at work.
They designed their cabin to be a true house of ease for the whole family.
After her stressful week, she sought solace in her own house of ease.

Synset Examples

He found the house of ease a perfect getaway from stress.
Their home is a house of ease, filled with comfort.
The spa felt like a house of ease for weary travelers.
