San Diego Bay

1. PROPN

/ sæn diˈeɪɡoʊ beɪ / (AmE), / san dɪˈeɪɡəʊ beɪ / (BrE)
a large natural bay of the Pacific Ocean in southern California, bordering the city of San Diego
bay in southern California, USA

Examples

Tourists enjoy sailing in San Diego Bay for its beautiful scenery and calm waters.
The naval base is strategically located on the shores of San Diego Bay.
Wildlife enthusiasts often observe migratory birds around San Diego Bay during the winter months.

Etymology

Named after the city of San Diego, itself from Spanish 'San Diego' (Saint Didacus).
