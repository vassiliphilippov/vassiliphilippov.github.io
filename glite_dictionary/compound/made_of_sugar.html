made of sugar

1. ADJ

/ meɪd əv ˈʃʊɡɚ / (AmE), / meɪd əv ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
not overly sensitive, easily hurt, or fragile; able to withstand criticism or difficulties without complaint.
not overly sensitive or fragile

Examples

Even after hearing harsh feedback, he proved he wasn't made of sugar.
Kids today need to realize they're not made of sugar and can cope with a bit of stress.
She walked through the rain, saying she wasn't made of sugar.

Synset Examples

Don’t be so sensitive; you’re not made of sugar.
She handles criticism well; she's not made of sugar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tough - able to endure hardship or criticism; not easily affected
  • Antonym: fragile - easily damaged or emotionally sensitive
