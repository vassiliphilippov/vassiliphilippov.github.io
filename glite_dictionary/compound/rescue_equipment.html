rescue equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛskju ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛskjuː ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
equipment, such as tools and devices, used for rescuing people from dangerous or emergency situations.
tools and devices for rescue in emergencies

Examples

The firefighters checked their rescue equipment before heading into the burning building.
All passenger trains are required to carry rescue equipment such as stretchers and first aid kits.
Proper maintenance of rescue equipment can make a critical difference during emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life-saving equipment - devices or tools used specifically to save lives in emergencies
