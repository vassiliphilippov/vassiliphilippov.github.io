hugemongous shipment

1. NOUN

/ hjuːdʒ.mɑŋ.ɡəs ˈʃɪp.mənt / (AmE), / hjuːdʒˈmɒŋ.ɡəs ˈʃɪp.mənt / (BrE)
an extremely large delivery of goods or supplies
very large delivery of goods
informal slang

Examples

The warehouse received a hugemongous shipment of electronics from overseas yesterday.
We weren't expecting such a hugemongous shipment and had to clear extra space for it.
Our store's inventory doubled after this hugemongous shipment of merchandise arrived.

Synset Examples

They delivered a hugemongous shipment of supplies to the shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humongous - very large in size
  • Synonym: enormous shipment - very large delivery of goods

Etymology

Blend of 'huge' and the mock-suffix '-mongous' (from 'humongous') plus 'shipment'.
