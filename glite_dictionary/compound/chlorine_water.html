chlorine water

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɔːrin ˈwɑːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɔːriːn ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
an aqueous solution of chlorine used as a bleaching agent or disinfectant
aqueous chlorine solution

Examples

The laboratory prepared chlorine water for use in the disinfection experiment.
She used chlorine water as a bleaching agent to remove stains from the fabric.
Always handle chlorine water with care due to its strong oxidizing properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bleach - a chemical used to whiten or disinfect
