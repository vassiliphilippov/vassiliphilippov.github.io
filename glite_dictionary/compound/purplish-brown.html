purplish-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝplɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged or shaded with purple.
brown tinged with purple

Examples

The bird's feathers were a distinct purplish-brown shade that caught the sunlight beautifully.
The artist mixed colors until she achieved the perfect purplish-brown for the autumn leaves in her painting.
He chose a purplish-brown suit that stood out among the more traditional colors at the event.
