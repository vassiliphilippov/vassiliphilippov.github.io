free hand

1. NOUN

/ fri hænd / (AmE), / friː hænd / (BrE)
the freedom to act or make decisions without being restricted by others
freedom to act without restriction

Examples

The manager was given a free hand to modernize the department as he saw fit.
With a free hand, the architect was able to design a truly unique building.
If you give her a free hand, she can solve the problem very quickly.

Synset Examples

many have doubts about giving him a free hand to attack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autonomy - independence or freedom to act
  • Antonym: control - the power to direct or restrain
