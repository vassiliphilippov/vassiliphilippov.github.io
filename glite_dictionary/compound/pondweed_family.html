pondweed family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑndˌwid ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpɒndˌwiːd ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of aquatic plants that grow in ponds and slow-moving streams, sometimes including the family Zosteraceae.
family of aquatic plants

Examples

Many species within the pondweed family play a vital role in aquatic ecosystems.
Researchers have identified new genera in the pondweed family from freshwater habitats.
The pondweed family includes both submerged and floating-leaved aquatic plants.
