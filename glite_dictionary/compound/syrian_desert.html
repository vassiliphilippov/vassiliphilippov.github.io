Syrian Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪriən ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən ˈdezət / (BrE)
a vast desert region in northern Arabia, spanning western Iraq, southern Syria, eastern Jordan, and northern Saudi Arabia
desert region in northern Arabia

Examples

The Syrian Desert is known for its harsh climate and arid landscape.
Ancient trade routes often crossed the Syrian Desert on the way to Mesopotamia.
Archaeologists have found several prehistoric sites scattered throughout the Syrian Desert.
