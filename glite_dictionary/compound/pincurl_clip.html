pincurl clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌkɝl klɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌkɜːl klɪp / (BrE)
a small metal clip used to hold pin curls in place while hairstyling
clip for holding pin curls

Examples

She secured each curl with a pincurl clip before heading to bed.
The stylist used a pincurl clip to set the waves in my hair.
After removing each pincurl clip, her hair had perfect curls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair clip - a clip used to hold hair in place
