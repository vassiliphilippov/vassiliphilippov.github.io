store detective

1. NOUN

/ stɔːr dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / stɔː dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a person employed by a store to detect and prevent shoplifting or theft by customers or staff, usually working undercover
undercover employee preventing theft in stores

Examples

The store detective watched the shopper carefully as she browsed the aisles.
After catching several shoplifters, the store detective received praise from the store manager.
A store detective must be observant and able to remain unnoticed while performing their duties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security guard - a person employed to protect a building or people
  • Antonym: shoplifter - a person who steals goods from a shop
