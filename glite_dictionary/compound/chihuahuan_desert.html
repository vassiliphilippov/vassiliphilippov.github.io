Chihuahuan Desert

1. PROPN

/ tʃɪˈwɑwən ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / tʃɪˈwɑːwən ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A large desert region in northern Mexico and the southwestern United States, covering parts of Texas, New Mexico, and Arizona.
desert in northern Mexico and U.S. Southwest

Examples

Scientists study the unique plant life of the Chihuahuan Desert.
Many rare animal species inhabit the vast Chihuahuan Desert region.
Rainfall is scarce in the Chihuahuan Desert, making its ecosystem remarkably adapted.

Etymology

Named after the Mexican state of Chihuahua, where much of the desert lies.
