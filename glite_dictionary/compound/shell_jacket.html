shell jacket

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A waist-length jacket, often tapering to a point at the back, traditionally worn by military officers at formal dinners.
short formal jacket worn by officers

Examples

The officer wore his shell jacket to the regimental dinner.
A well-tailored shell jacket completed her historical reenactment outfit.
Many antique shell jackets are collected by military enthusiasts.
