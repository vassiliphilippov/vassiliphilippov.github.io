house-builder

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or company that builds houses as a business
person or company that builds houses

Examples

The house-builder promised to complete the new homes by next spring.
After years of experience, she started her own business as a house-builder.
Several house-builders are offering eco-friendly options for modern homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - a person or company engaged in the construction of buildings
