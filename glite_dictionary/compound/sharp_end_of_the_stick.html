sharp end of the stick

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp ɛnd əv ðə stɪk / (AmE), / ʃɑːp end əv ðə stɪk / (BrE)
the pointed part of a stick or similar object, capable of piercing or cutting
the pointed part of a stick or object

Examples

Be careful with the sharp end of the stick when cleaning up the branches.
He accidentally poked himself with the sharp end of the stick while walking through the woods.
The sharp end of the stick made it easy to draw lines in the dirt.

Synset Examples

The sharp end of the stick pierced the cardboard.
