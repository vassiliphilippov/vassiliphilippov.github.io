pond-scum parasite

1. NOUN

/ pɑnd skʌm ˈpærəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / pɒnd skʌm ˈpærəsaɪt / (BrE)
an aquatic fungus of the genus Synchytriaceae that lives parasitically on pond scum
aquatic fungus parasitizing pond scum

Examples

A pond-scum parasite can devastate algae populations in freshwater lakes.
Researchers discovered a new species of pond-scum parasite affecting local ponds.
The presence of a pond-scum parasite is a sign of ecological imbalance.
