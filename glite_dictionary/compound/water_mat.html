water mat

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌmat / (BrE)
An aquatic herb with yellowish flowers found in central and western regions of the United States.
aquatic herb with yellowish flowers (U.S.)

Examples

During our hike, we spotted a water mat blooming along the edge of the pond.
The water mat provides essential habitat for aquatic insects and birds.
Researchers are studying the distribution of water mats in the western United States.
