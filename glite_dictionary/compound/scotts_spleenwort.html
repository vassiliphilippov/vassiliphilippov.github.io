Scott's spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ skɑts ˈsplinwɜrt / (AmE), / skɒts ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A common North American fern with shiny black stems, belonging to the genus Asplenium.
North American fern with black stems

Examples

I spotted a rare Scott's spleenwort growing on the shaded rocky hillside.
Botanists are studying how Scott's spleenwort adapts to different environments in North America.
The polished black stems make Scott's spleenwort easy to identify among other ferns.
