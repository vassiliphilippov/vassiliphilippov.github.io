Dinka language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪŋkə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋkə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Nilotic language spoken by the Dinka people of South Sudan
Nilotic language of South Sudan

Examples

Linguists have extensively studied the Dinka language to document its unique features.
The Dinka language has several dialects spoken across different regions of South Sudan.
Her grandmother taught her songs and stories in the Dinka language.

Etymology

Dinka, name of the ethnic group in South Sudan, plus 'language'.
