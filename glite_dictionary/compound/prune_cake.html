prune cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈprun keɪk / (AmE), / ˈpruːn keɪk / (BrE)
a moist cake made with prunes that have been pureed
cake made with pureed prunes

Examples

Grandma baked a delicious prune cake for the holiday dinner.
This prune cake recipe is moist and full of flavor thanks to the fruit puree.
If you like fruit desserts, you should try making a traditional prune cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruitcake - a cake containing mixed dried fruit and nuts
