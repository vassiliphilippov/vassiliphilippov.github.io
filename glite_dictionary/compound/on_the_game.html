on the game

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
alert, attentive, and actively performing at one's best; fully engaged in a task or competition
alert and performing at one's best
informal

Examples

The coach praised her for being on the game throughout the tournament.
It's important to stay on the game when facing tough competition.
He drinks coffee in the morning to make sure he's on the game at work.

Synset Examples

Staying focused helps me to remain on the game.
She proved she was on the game by winning the competition.
He's always on the game, looking for new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the ball - alert and attentive; quick to understand and react
