Bambi lover

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæmbi ˌlʌvər / (AmE), / ˈbæmbi ˌlʌvə / (BrE)
a person who has a strong affection for cute animals or for the character Bambi, often showing a love for wildlife
person fond of cute animals or Bambi

Examples

Her friends call her a Bambi lover because she always rescues stray kittens and puppies.
As a dedicated Bambi lover, he volunteers every weekend at the animal shelter.
The film inspired a generation of Bambi lovers to care more about wildlife.

Synset Examples

He’s a Bambi lover and can’t resist cute animals.
As a Bambi lover, she collects all the memorabilia.
Being a Bambi lover, she volunteers at the wildlife shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal lover - a person who is fond of animals

Etymology

From 'Bambi', the 1942 Disney animated film about a young deer, and 'lover', a person who loves something.
