plastic laminate

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk ˈlæməˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk ˈlæmɪneɪt / (BrE)
a material consisting of several layers of plastic bonded together, often used as a surface covering for furniture, countertops, and walls.
multi-layer bonded plastic material

Examples

The kitchen countertops were finished with plastic laminate to make them durable and easy to clean.
Many office desks are covered in plastic laminate for a modern look and resistance to stains.
The contractor recommended plastic laminate panels for the new cabinets because of their affordability.
