showy sunflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A tall, rough-leaved perennial plant with large flower heads, native to central United States.
Tall North American perennial sunflower

Examples

The showy sunflower brightened the entire meadow with its golden blooms.
We planted several showy sunflowers along the fence to attract birds and butterflies.
The showy sunflower is a favorite among gardeners in the central United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunflower - a tall plant with large yellow flowers and edible seeds
