soap dispenser

1. NOUN

/ soʊp dɪˈspɛnsər / (AmE), / səʊp dɪˈspensə / (BrE)
A device or container that dispenses liquid soap when pressed or operated.
device for dispensing liquid soap

Examples

She filled the soap dispenser in the kitchen with a fresh bottle of liquid soap.
Most public restrooms now have an automatic soap dispenser to reduce the spread of germs.
Our bathroom soap dispenser matches the sink and towel rack perfectly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispenser - a device that releases a substance in controlled amounts
