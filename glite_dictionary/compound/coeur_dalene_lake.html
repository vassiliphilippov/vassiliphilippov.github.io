Coeur d'Alene Lake

1. PROPN

/ koʊˌɜːr dəˈleɪn leɪk / (AmE), / kɜːˌdəˈleɪn leɪk / (BrE)
A large lake in northern Idaho, United States, known for its scenic beauty and recreational activities.
lake in northern Idaho, US

Examples

Many tourists visit Coeur d'Alene Lake each summer for boating and fishing.
The sunrise over Coeur d'Alene Lake is truly breathtaking.
Scientists have been studying the water quality of Coeur d'Alene Lake for several years.

Etymology

Named after the Coeur d'Alene people; 'Coeur d'Alene' is French for 'heart of an awl'.
