hack driver

1. NOUN

/ hæk ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / hæk ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
A person who drives a taxi for a living.
taxi driver

Examples

The hack driver waited patiently outside the station for new passengers.
Years ago, she worked as a hack driver in the busy streets of New York.
The hack driver knew every shortcut in town to avoid the traffic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi driver - a person who drives a taxi for a living

Etymology

From 'hack' (short for 'hackney carriage,' a horse-drawn taxi) + 'driver.'
