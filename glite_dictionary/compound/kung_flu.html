kung flu

1. NOUN

/ kʌŋ flu / (AmE), / kʌŋ fluː / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for COVID-19, used to mock or blame Chinese people for the pandemic.
offensive slang for COVID-19
slang offensive

Examples

Using the term kung flu in a public speech sparked widespread outrage.
He was criticized for making a joke about kung flu during the meeting.
The media condemned references to kung flu as racially insensitive and inflammatory.

Synset Examples

The term 'kung flu' was widely criticized for being offensive.
Using 'kung flu' in discussions about the pandemic is inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: COVID-19 - The infectious disease caused by the novel coronavirus that emerged in 2019

Etymology

Blend of 'kung fu' (Chinese martial art) and 'flu' (influenza), playing on the phonetic similarity.
