stanchless enthusiasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæntʃləs ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ˈstɑːntʃləs ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
Unrestrained or limitless enthusiasm; enthusiasm that cannot be stopped or controlled.
unrestrained or boundless enthusiasm
archaic

Examples

Her stanchless enthusiasm inspired everyone at the charity event.
Despite many setbacks, his stanchless enthusiasm never wavered.
The team was driven forward by their leader's stanchless enthusiasm for the project.

Synset Examples

Her stanchless enthusiasm made the event unforgettable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbounded enthusiasm - enthusiasm with no limits or restrictions
  • Antonym: restrained enthusiasm - enthusiasm that is kept under control

Etymology

From 'stanchless', an archaic form meaning 'incapable of being stopped', from Old French 'estanchier' (to stanch), ultimately from Latin 'stagnare' (to pool, to stagnate).
