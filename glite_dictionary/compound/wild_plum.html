wild plum

1. NOUN

/ waɪld plʌm / (AmE), / waɪld plʌm / (BrE)
A plum tree or shrub that grows naturally without being cultivated.
uncultivated plum tree or shrub

Examples

We found a wild plum tree growing near the riverbank.
The forest was full of wild plum bushes covered in tiny, tart fruits.
She made delicious jam from the wild plum harvest in late August.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - a sweet, juicy fruit or the tree on which it grows

2. NOUN

/ waɪld plʌm / (AmE), / waɪld plʌm / (BrE)
the fruit of a wild plum tree, especially those native to the southern United States, typically smaller and more tart than cultivated plums
fruit of a wild-growing plum tree

Examples

She gathered a basket of wild plums from the woods near her house.
The jelly was made using freshly picked wild plums for a tart flavor.
Birds flocked to the tree when the wild plums were ripe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - sweet, fleshy fruit of the plum tree
