cow pie

1. NOUN

/ kaʊ paɪ / (AmE), / kaʊ paɪ / (BrE)
A piece of cow feces, typically found in fields or pastures.
piece of cow feces

Examples

Watch your step in the field, or you might step in a cow pie.
The children laughed as they tried to avoid the cow pies while running through the pasture.
She used a shovel to clear away the cow pies from the barnyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dung - manure or animal excrement, especially from livestock
