wellspring of new ideas

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌsprɪŋ əv nu aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌsprɪŋ əv njuː aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
a source from which many new and creative ideas originate or are continuously produced
source of abundant creativity or inspiration

Examples

The city has become a wellspring of new ideas for technology companies in recent years.
Her artistic collaborations are always a wellspring of new ideas and innovation.
Education should be a wellspring of new ideas that encourages critical thinking in students.

Synset Examples

Her creativity is a constant wellspring of new ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fount of inspiration - a source from which inspiration flows
  • Synonym: source - the place or person from which something originates

Etymology

Formed from 'wellspring', from Old English 'wiellspring' (a spring of water), figuratively used for a source, and 'idea', from Greek 'idéa' (form, pattern).
