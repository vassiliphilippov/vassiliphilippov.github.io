high-performance

1. ADJ

/ haɪ pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / haɪ pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
(of machines, vehicles, or systems) designed or modified to operate at a level much higher than usual in terms of speed, power, or efficiency.
giving superior performance

Examples

The company unveiled a new high-performance laptop aimed at professional gamers.
We need high-performance materials to build the next generation of airplanes.
She drives a high-performance sports car that accelerates quickly.

Synset Examples

a high-performance car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-powered - having a lot of power or energy; similar adjective for devices or people
