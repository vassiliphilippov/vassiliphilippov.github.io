banana smoothie

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˈsmuːði / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
A drink made by blending bananas, often with milk, yogurt, or other ingredients, usually served cold.
blended drink made with bananas

Examples

She prepared a refreshing banana smoothie after her morning workout.
Would you like a banana smoothie with your pancakes?
The café offered a vegan banana smoothie made with almond milk.

Synset Examples

I made a banana smoothie for breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a thick, cold beverage made by blending fruit and other ingredients
