genus Beroe

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈbɛr.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈbɛr.əʊ / (BrE)
A genus of marine animals in the phylum Ctenophora, coextensive with the class Nuda.
genus of comb jellies (Ctenophora)

Examples

The genus Beroe includes several species of comb jellies found in marine environments.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of animals in the genus Beroe.
All members of the genus Beroe lack tentacles, distinguishing them from other ctenophores.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Beroe', used in biological taxonomy.
