coming-out party

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌmɪŋ ˈaʊt ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˌkʌmɪŋ ˈaʊt ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a celebration held to mark the occasion of someone publicly revealing their sexual orientation or gender identity
celebration of revealing one's sexual orientation

Examples

Jamie invited close friends to their coming-out party last Saturday night.
The coming-out party helped Alex feel supported and celebrated by the community.
They decorated the house for a joyful coming-out party full of acceptance and love.

Synset Examples

They hosted a coming-out party to celebrate their new identity.
She planned a grand coming-out party for her friends.
The coming-out party was filled with love and acceptance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coming out - publicly declaring one's LGBTQ+ identity

Etymology

Extension of 'coming out' (publicly acknowledging one's LGBTQ+ identity), influenced by the earlier sense of a debutante's social introduction.
