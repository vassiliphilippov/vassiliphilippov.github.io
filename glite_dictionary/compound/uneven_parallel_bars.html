uneven parallel bars

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈivən ˈpæɹəˌlɛl bɑɹz / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːvən ˈpærəlɛl bɑːz / (BrE)
a pair of horizontal bars set at different heights, used as apparatus in women's gymnastics for exercises and routines
gymnastics apparatus with two bars at different heights

Examples

She performed a flawless routine on the uneven parallel bars at the competition.
The uneven parallel bars require great upper body strength and flexibility.
After hours of practice, she finally nailed her dismount from the uneven parallel bars.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: parallel bars - apparatus of two bars at the same height used in gymnastics, primarily by men
