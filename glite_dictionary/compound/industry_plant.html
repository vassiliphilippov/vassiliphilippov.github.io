industry plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.də.stri plænt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.də.stri plɑːnt / (BrE)
an artist or performer whose career is promoted by an industry entity while being presented as if they have achieved independent success, often to appear more authentic or relatable
artist secretly promoted by industry as independent
slang

Examples

Many fans suspected the new rapper was an industry plant because of his quick rise to fame.
Documentary filmmakers explored the topic of the industry plant in modern pop music.
The accusation of being an industry plant can damage an artist’s credibility with their audience.

Synset Examples

He was accused of being an industry plant by fans.
The new artist seems like an industry plant to many skeptics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manufactured artist - an artist whose persona or career was created and promoted by industry executives
