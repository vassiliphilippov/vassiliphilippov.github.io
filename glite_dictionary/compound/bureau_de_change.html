bureau de change

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjʊəroʊ də ˈʃɑːnʒ / (AmE), / ˌbjʊərəʊ də ˈʃɑːnʒ / (BrE)
an office or establishment where foreign currencies can be exchanged
place for exchanging foreign currency

Examples

We stopped at the airport bureau de change to get some euros before heading into the city.
Many hotels have a bureau de change in the lobby for guests' convenience.
The rates at this bureau de change are better than at most banks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currency exchange - the process or place of exchanging one country's currency for another

Etymology

From French, meaning 'office of exchange'.
