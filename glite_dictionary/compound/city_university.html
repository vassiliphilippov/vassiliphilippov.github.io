city university

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti juˌnɪvərˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɪti juːnɪˌvɜːsɪti / (BrE)
A university located in a large city, typically serving the urban population and often focusing on practical and professional courses.
university located in a large city

Examples

Many students choose to attend a city university for its proximity to business and cultural centers.
Living near a city university gave her access to a wide range of internships and job opportunities.
Unlike rural colleges, a city university often has strong partnerships with local industries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urban university - a university located in a city or urban area
