house detective

1. NOUN

/ haʊs dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / haʊs dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a private detective employed by a hotel or retail store to prevent theft or monitor activity
private detective working for a hotel or store

Examples

The house detective noticed suspicious activity near the elevators.
She worked for several years as a house detective in a downtown hotel.
If you cause trouble in the lobby, the house detective will quickly intervene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: store detective - a private detective employed by a store to prevent theft
