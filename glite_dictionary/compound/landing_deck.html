landing deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændɪŋ ˌdɛk / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ ˌdɛk / (BrE)
the upper deck of an aircraft carrier that serves as a runway for aircraft to take off and land
runway deck on an aircraft carrier

Examples

The fighter jet touched down smoothly on the landing deck despite strong winds.
During operations, every crew member on the landing deck must wear protective gear.
The landing deck was cleared to prepare for the next aircraft arrival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight deck - the upper deck of an aircraft carrier used for aircraft takeoff and landing
