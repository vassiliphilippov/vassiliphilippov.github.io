edible bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl ˈbiːn / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbəl ˈbiːn / (BrE)
a seed of a plant in the legume family that can be eaten as food
an eatable leguminous seed

Examples

She added some edible beans to the soup for extra protein.
Many cultures rely on edible beans as a staple food.
You should soak edible beans overnight before cooking them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bean - an edible seed, typically kidney-shaped, of various plants in the legume family
