clip farmer

1. NOUN

/ klɪp ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / klɪp ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
A person who regularly edits and uploads video clips, especially as an online content creator.
person who creates and edits video clips
informal

Examples

The clip farmer uploaded new content to his channel every week to keep his audience engaged.
As a successful clip farmer, she earned a living by editing and posting trending video snippets.
Many aspiring clip farmers use social media to reach a wider audience and build their online following.
