cork gun

1. NOUN

/ kɔrk ɡʌn / (AmE), / kɔːk ɡʌn / (BrE)
A toy gun, usually a pistol or rifle, that shoots cork bullets.
toy gun that shoots corks

Examples

The boy aimed his cork gun at the target and pulled the trigger.
At the birthday party, children had fun playing with a cork gun.
He bought a vintage cork gun at the antique store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy gun - A gun-shaped toy that fires projectiles or simulates gunfire
