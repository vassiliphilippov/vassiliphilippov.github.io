small loan company

1. NOUN

/ smɔl loʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / smɔːl ləʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a finance company that offers loans to individuals who may not qualify for traditional bank loans, often for relatively small amounts
company offering small loans to high-risk borrowers

Examples

She turned to a small loan company after her bank denied her application for a personal loan.
Many small loan companies charge higher interest rates due to increased risk.
The small loan company requested detailed information about his employment history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance company - a company that lends money to individuals or businesses
