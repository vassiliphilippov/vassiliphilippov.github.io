savings account

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪvɪŋz əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋz əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
a bank account that pays interest on money deposited and is intended for saving rather than daily transactions
interest-earning bank deposit account

Examples

He opened a savings account to put aside money for his future.
The savings account earns a higher interest rate than the checking account.
Transferring funds from your savings account to your checking account is easy online.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: checking account - bank account for day-to-day transactions
