amplitude level

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmplɪˌtud ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈamplɪˌtjuːd ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
The magnitude or strength of a signal, measured on a scale of amplitude.
magnitude of a signal on an amplitude scale

Examples

The engineer adjusted the amplitude level to reduce background noise.
When recording audio, maintaining a consistent amplitude level is essential for clear sound quality.
An excessively high amplitude level can lead to signal distortion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: signal level - the strength or magnitude of a signal
