chucky vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌki vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌki vaɪb / (BrE)
an unsettling or creepy feeling reminiscent of the fictional horror character Chucky, often suggesting something is eerily lifelike or sinister
a creepy feeling like the Chucky character
informal

Examples

The abandoned house definitely has a chucky vibe at night.
That old doll in the attic gives off a real chucky vibe.
I don't like this shop; the mannequins have such a chucky vibe.

Synset Examples

That doll gives me a chucky vibe, very creepy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creepy - causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease
  • Synonym: sinister - giving the impression that something harmful or evil is happening or will happen

Etymology

From the name 'Chucky,' the murderous doll character in the Child's Play horror film series, combined with 'vibe' (atmosphere or feeling).
