Northern snow bedstraw

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn snoʊ ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən snəʊ ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with stolons and small white flowers, sometimes cultivated as an ornamental.
North American perennial plant with white flowers

Examples

Northern snow bedstraw is often chosen for its delicate white flowers in garden landscapes.
The creeping stems of Northern snow bedstraw help it spread quickly across the ground.
Wildlife sometimes shelters beneath the foliage of Northern snow bedstraw in its native habitat.
