high status

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / haɪ ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
a position of prestige or superiority in a social or professional context
prestigious or superior position

Examples

She achieved high status in her company after years of hard work.
In many cultures, doctors enjoy high status and social respect.
The family’s high status was evident from their influential connections.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low status - a position of little prestige or influence
