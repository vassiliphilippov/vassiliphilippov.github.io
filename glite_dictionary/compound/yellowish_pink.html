yellowish pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˈpɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.ɪʃ ˈpɪŋk / (BrE)
a shade of pink that is tinged with or has a hint of yellow
a pink color with a yellow tint

Examples

The dress was a delicate shade of yellowish pink that complemented her complexion.
He painted the sky in a soft yellowish pink at dawn, capturing the early morning light.
We chose a yellowish pink for the nursery walls to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
  • Degree Higher: yellow - a color like that of a lemon or gold
