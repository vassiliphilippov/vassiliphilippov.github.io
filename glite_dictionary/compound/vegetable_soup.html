vegetable soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbəl suːp / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbl suːp / (BrE)
A soup made with a variety of vegetables, often in a seasoned broth.
soup made with assorted vegetables

Examples

She served a steaming bowl of vegetable soup for lunch on the chilly afternoon.
Many people enjoy vegetable soup as a healthy and comforting meal option.
My grandmother’s homemade vegetable soup is famous for its delicious blend of flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minestrone - an Italian soup made with vegetables and often pasta or rice
  • Antonym: chicken soup - soup made with chicken and often vegetables or noodles
