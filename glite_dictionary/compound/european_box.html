European box

1. NOUN

/ juːˈroʊpiən bɑks / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən bɒks / (BrE)
a large, slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree, commonly used for hedges, borders, and topiary figures
an evergreen shrub used for hedges and topiary

Examples

The formal garden was lined with neatly trimmed European box to create elegant borders.
She planted European box along the pathway for year-round greenery.
The landscaper recommended European box because it responds well to shaping and clipping.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxwood - an evergreen shrub or small tree with dense, hard wood, often used for hedges or ornamental gardening
