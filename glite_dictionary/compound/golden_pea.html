golden pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən pi / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən piː / (BrE)
A bushy herb native to the western United States, known for its yellow, pea-like flowers.
bushy western US herb with yellow flowers

Examples

The garden was full of blooming golden pea plants, adding a splash of yellow.
Many gardeners in the western US choose the golden pea for its drought tolerance and bright flowers.
We spotted several golden pea shrubs while hiking in the foothills.
