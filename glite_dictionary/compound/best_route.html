best route

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ruːt / (AmE), / bɛst ruːt / (BrE)
the most efficient or effective path or course for travelling from one place to another
most effective travel path

Examples

Before leaving, I checked the map to find the best route to the airport.
Local guides can help visitors discover the best route to the summit.
Using GPS, she always tries to drive the best route during rush hour.

Synset Examples

Every trucker knows the best routes to avoid traffic.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: shortcut - a quicker way of getting somewhere than the usual route
  • Degree Lower: detour - an alternative route that is not necessarily optimal
  • Degree Lower: scenic route - a route chosen for its pleasant scenery, not necessarily its efficiency
  • Antonym: worst route - a route that is least effective or least efficient
