Mexican valium

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛksɪkən ˈvæliəm / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈvæliəm / (BrE)
A street name for the drug flunitrazepam, commonly known as Rohypnol, sometimes used illicitly as a sedative or recreational drug.
