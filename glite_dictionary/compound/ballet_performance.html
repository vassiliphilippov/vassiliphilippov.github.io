ballet performance

1. NOUN

/ bəˈleɪ pɚˌfɔrməns / (AmE), / bəˈleɪ pəˌfɔːməns / (BrE)
A performance consisting of or featuring ballet dancing, typically presented on stage by trained dancers.
a stage performance of ballet

Examples

The ballet performance last night was breathtaking.
She purchased tickets to the annual ballet performance at the city theater.
Critics praised the lead dancer's talent in the new ballet performance.
