butterfly weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ wiːd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌflaɪ wiːd / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to eastern and southern United States, noted for its bright orange flowers, often attracting butterflies
a plant with bright orange flowers that attracts butterflies

Examples

The bright orange flowers of butterfly weed attract monarchs and other butterflies to the garden.
We planted butterfly weed along the fence to support local pollinators.
Butterfly weed is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sunlight.
