whorled caraway

1. NOUN

/ wɜrld kəˈraɪ / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈkærəweɪ / (BrE)
a species of caraway with leaves arranged in whorls around the stem
caraway with whorled leaves

Examples

The whorled caraway can be found in mountain meadows across Europe.
Scientists studied the medicinal properties of the whorled caraway for its unique essential oils.
Gardeners appreciate the decorative foliage of the whorled caraway in perennial borders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caraway - an aromatic plant whose seeds are used in cooking
