Canadian tuxedo

1. NOUN

/ kəˈneɪdiən tʌkˈsiːdoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən tʌkˈsiːdəʊ / (BrE)
A casual outfit consisting of a denim jacket worn with denim jeans, usually in matching or similar shades.
denim jacket and jeans outfit

Examples

She decided to wear a Canadian tuxedo to the music festival for a trendy, casual look.
He loves his Canadian tuxedo, claiming denim on denim never goes out of style.
The group showed up in matching Canadian tuxedos for the themed party.

Synset Examples

She looked stylish in her Canadian tuxedo at the concert.
The Canadian tuxedo is a beloved fashion statement here.
He wore a Canadian tuxedo to the casual party.

Etymology

Humorous term referencing a denim-on-denim outfit, with 'Canadian' alluding to stereotypes about Canadian fashion.
