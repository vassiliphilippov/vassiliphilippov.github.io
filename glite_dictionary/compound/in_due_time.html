in due time

1. ADV

/ ɪn du taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn djuː taɪm / (BrE)
at the appropriate or expected time; when the right moment arrives
at the appropriate time

Examples

You will receive your results in due time once all the papers have been graded.
All your efforts will pay off in due time, so be patient.
The garden will bloom beautifully in due time if you continue to care for it.

Synset Examples

we'll get to this question in due course

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in due course - at the appropriate or natural time; eventually
