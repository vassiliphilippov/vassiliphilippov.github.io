able-bodied seaman

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪbəl ˈbɑːdiːd ˈsiːmən / (AmE), / ˌeɪbəl ˈbɒdɪd ˈsiːmən / (BrE)
a sailor in the merchant marine who is trained and skilled, ranking above ordinary seaman
skilled merchant sailor

Examples

After years of training, he was promoted to able-bodied seaman on the cargo ship.
The duties of an able-bodied seaman include operating lifeboats and assisting with navigation.
Each able-bodied seaman must demonstrate proficiency in emergency procedures.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ordinary seaman - unskilled or lower-ranking sailor in the merchant marine
