business establishment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ɛˌstæblɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ɪˌstæblɪʃmənt / (BrE)
a place where commercial activities or services are carried out, including factories, stores, or offices
a place where business is conducted

Examples

There is a new business establishment opening downtown that sells handmade furniture.
Local taxes vary depending on the location of your business establishment.
Every business establishment must comply with health and safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company - a commercial business
  • Antonym: residence - a place where people live
