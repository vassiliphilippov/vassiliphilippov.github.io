sense tagger

1. NOUN

/ sɛns ˈtæɡər / (AmE), / sɛns ˈtaɡə / (BrE)
a computer program that assigns labels indicating the meanings of words or expressions in text
program that labels word meanings

Examples

The linguistics team used a sense tagger to automatically annotate the text with word meanings.
Training a sense tagger requires a large, accurately labeled corpus.
Modern AI systems often integrate a sense tagger to improve language understanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: part-of-speech tagger - a program that labels words in text with their grammatical category
