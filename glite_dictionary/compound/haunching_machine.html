haunching machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔntʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used for making shaped cuts in the end or along the edge of a board, typically in woodworking or carpentry.
woodworking machine for shaped cuts

Examples

The haunching machine can produce precise joints for window frames and doors.
After aligning the board, she fed it into the haunching machine to cut the desired shape.
Many carpentry shops invest in a haunching machine for efficient mass production.
