orchid tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔr.kɪd ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈɔː.kɪd ˌtriː / (BrE)
A small tree native to East India, known for its orchid-like flowers and hard, dark wood.
tree with orchid-like flowers

Examples

The orchid tree in her garden bloomed with spectacular purple flowers every spring.
Gardeners admire the orchid tree for both its beautiful blossoms and unusual foliage.
A craftsman prized the dark, hard wood of the orchid tree for making furniture.
