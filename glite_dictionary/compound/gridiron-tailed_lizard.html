gridiron-tailed lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪdˌaɪrn teɪld ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪdˌaɪən teɪld ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
A swift lizard with a long, black-banded tail and long legs, found in the deserts of the United States and Mexico.
a lizard with a banded tail, found in US and Mexican deserts

Examples

The gridiron-tailed lizard is known for sprinting quickly across the hot desert sands.
Researchers studied the gridiron-tailed lizard to understand how it survives extreme temperatures.
We spotted a gridiron-tailed lizard basking on a rock during our trip to Arizona.
