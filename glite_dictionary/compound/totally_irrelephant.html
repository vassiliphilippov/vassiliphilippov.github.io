totally irrelephant

1. ADJ

/ ˈtoʊtəli ɪˈrɛlɪfənt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəli ɪˈrɛlɪfənt / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Completely irrelevant; having no bearing on the subject, used as a pun on 'totally irrelevant'.
completely irrelevant (humorous pun)
informal slang

Examples

Her last comment about the weather was totally irrelephant to our project discussion.
I love your puns, but that joke was totally irrelephant right now.
Bringing up old rumors is totally irrelephant to our current debate.

Synset Examples

Focusing on that is totally irrelephant right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrelevant - not connected with or relevant to something

Etymology

Blend of 'totally irrelevant' and 'elephant', used humorously online.
