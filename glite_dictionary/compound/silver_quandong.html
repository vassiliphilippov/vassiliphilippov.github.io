silver quandong

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˈkwɑnˌdɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈkwɒndɒŋ / (BrE)
a pale, easily worked timber obtained from the quandong tree, often used in woodworking and furniture making
pale timber from the quandong tree

Examples

This elegant chair was crafted from silver quandong, known for its light color and smooth texture.
Carpenters appreciate silver quandong because it is easy to shape and finish.
The museum's display includes artifacts made from silver quandong wood native to Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quandong - an Australian tree bearing edible fruit and useful timber
