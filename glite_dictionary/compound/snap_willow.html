snap willow

1. NOUN

/ snæp ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / snæp ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
a large willow tree with stiff branches that break easily
large willow tree with brittle branches

Examples

The branches of a snap willow can be broken off with little effort.
Several snap willows grew along the riverbank, providing shade and shelter for animals.
During the storm, the snap willow lost many of its brittle branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willow - a tree or shrub with narrow leaves and flexible branches
