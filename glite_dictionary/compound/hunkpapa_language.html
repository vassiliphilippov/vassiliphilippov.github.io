Hunkpapa language

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋˈpɑːpə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / hʌŋˈpɑːpə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Siouan language spoken by the Hunkpapa people, a group of the Lakota Sioux
language of the Hunkpapa people

Examples

Few fluent speakers of the Hunkpapa language remain today, making preservation efforts even more important.
She studied the Hunkpapa language to better understand Lakota oral traditions and stories.
A dictionary of the Hunkpapa language was published to help younger generations reconnect with their heritage.

Etymology

Hunkpapa is the name of one of the seven bands of the Lakota Sioux; from the Lakota term for 'those who camp by themselves.'
