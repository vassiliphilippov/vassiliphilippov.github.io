footsteps-of-spring

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌstɛps əv ˈsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtstɛps əv ˈsprɪŋ / (BrE)
A sanicle plant native to the northwestern United States and British Columbia, noted for its yellow flowers.
a yellow-flowered sanicle plant

In the meadows, clusters of footsteps-of-spring bloom brightly each April.
