sulphur-crested cockatoo

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌlfɚ ˌkrɛstɪd kɑːkəˌtu / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˌkrɛstɪd ˌkɒkəˈtuː / (BrE)
a large white cockatoo native to Australia and nearby islands, notable for its yellow erectile crest and loud calls
large white cockatoo with yellow crest

Examples

A sulphur-crested cockatoo perched on the fence, showing off its bright yellow crest.
Many sulphur-crested cockatoos gather noisily in city parks in Australia.
The loud screech of a sulphur-crested cockatoo can be heard from a great distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockatoo - a type of parrot with a large, showy crest
