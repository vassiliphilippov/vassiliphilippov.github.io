old witchgrass

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈwɪtʃˌɡræs / (AmE), / əʊld ˈwɪtʃˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A North American grass with slender, brushy flower panicles, often considered a weed on cultivated land.
a weedy North American grass

Examples

The invasion of old witchgrass in the cornfields reduced crop yields significantly.
Farmers often struggle to control the spread of old witchgrass on their land.
Weeds like old witchgrass can be difficult to eradicate from cultivated soil.
