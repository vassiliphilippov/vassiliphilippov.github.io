the Hill

1. NOUN

/ ði hɪl / (AmE), / ði hɪl / (BrE)
a metonym for the U.S. Congress or the area in Washington, D.C., where the Capitol Building stands and Congress meets
U.S. Congress/location in Washington, D.C.
american

Examples

After the announcement, reporters rushed to the Hill for comment from lawmakers.
Lobbyists spent weeks discussing the new bill with staffers on the Hill.
The senator has worked on the Hill for over two decades.

Synset Examples

they are debating the budget today on Capitol Hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Capitol Hill - the area in Washington, D.C., where the U.S. Capitol is located

Etymology

Refers to Capitol Hill in Washington, D.C., the seat of the U.S. Congress.
