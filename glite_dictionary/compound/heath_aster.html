heath aster

1. NOUN

/ hiːθ ˈæstər / (AmE), / hiːθ ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a common North American perennial plant with heathlike leaves and small white flower heads
common North American perennial plant

Examples

The heath aster thrives in dry, open fields across much of North America.
Gardeners appreciate the heath aster for its delicate, daisy-like white flowers.
Wildlife often visits patches of heath aster when they are in full bloom in late summer.

Examples
