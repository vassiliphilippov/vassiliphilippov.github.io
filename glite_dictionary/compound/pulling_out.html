pulling out

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊlɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈpʊlɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
the practice of withdrawing the penis from the vagina before ejaculation as a method of birth control
method of birth control by withdrawal

Examples

Many couples rely on pulling out as their primary form of contraception.
He explained to his doctor that they were only using pulling out to avoid pregnancy.
It's important to know that pulling out is less effective than other birth control methods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: withdrawal method - method of birth control by withdrawing the penis before ejaculation
