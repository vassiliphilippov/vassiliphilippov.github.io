cafeteria Christian

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæfəˈtɪriə ˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˌkæfəˈtɪəriə ˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
a Christian who selects only certain beliefs or teachings from Christianity rather than following all official doctrines
Christian who chooses specific beliefs

Examples

Some accuse him of being a cafeteria Christian because he disagrees with church doctrine on several issues.
As a cafeteria Christian, she supports some biblical teachings but rejects others.
The debate centered around whether it's acceptable to be a cafeteria Christian in modern society.

Synset Examples

He's a cafeteria Christian, embracing only certain teachings.
Her beliefs as a cafeteria Christian are quite unique.
Many cafeteria Christians struggle with traditional doctrines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafeteria Catholic - a Catholic who selects particular Church doctrines to follow

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'cafeteria Catholic', from 'cafeteria', suggesting selective choice as in a cafeteria.
