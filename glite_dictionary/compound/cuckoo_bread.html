cuckoo bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkuː brɛd / (AmE), / ˈkʊkuː brɛd / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with heart-shaped three-part leaves and white flowers with purple veins, commonly found in moist woodlands.
Eurasian woodland plant with trifoliate leaves and purple-veined white flowers

Examples

The shady forest floor was dotted with cuckoo bread blooming in early spring.
Botanists identified several clusters of cuckoo bread alongside the stream.
Children used to collect cuckoo bread leaves to press in their notebooks.
