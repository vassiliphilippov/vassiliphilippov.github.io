web television

1. NOUN

/ wɛb ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / wɛb ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
original television content produced and distributed for viewing over the internet, rather than through traditional broadcast or cable networks
TV content broadcast online

Examples

The award-winning series was one of the first web television hits to gain a massive online following.
Many young audiences now prefer web television over traditional cable shows.
The production quality of web television has dramatically improved in the past decade.

Synset Examples

The Guild is an award winning web television series about the lives of an online guild called The Knights of Good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streaming television - television content distributed over the internet by streaming, not traditional broadcast
