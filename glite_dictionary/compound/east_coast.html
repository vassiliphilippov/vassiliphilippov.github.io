East Coast

1. PROPN

/ ist koʊst / (AmE), / iːst kəʊst / (BrE)
the eastern seaboard of the United States, especially the region stretching from Boston to Washington, D.C.
eastern seaboard of the United States

Examples

Many major cities, like New York and Philadelphia, are located along the East Coast.
She moved from California to the East Coast to attend college.
The East Coast often experiences hurricanes during the summer months.
