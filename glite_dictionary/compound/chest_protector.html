chest protector

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛst prəˈtɛktɚ / (AmE), / tʃɛst prəˈtɛktə / (BrE)
a padded protective garment worn over the chest by athletes such as baseball catchers or football players to prevent injury
padded sports protective gear for the chest

Examples

The catcher put on his chest protector before heading onto the field.
Football players wear a chest protector to reduce the risk of injury during tackles.
She adjusted her chest protector to ensure a comfortable fit before practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catcher's gear - protective equipment used by the catcher in baseball
  • Synonym: shin guard - protective covering for the shin in sports
