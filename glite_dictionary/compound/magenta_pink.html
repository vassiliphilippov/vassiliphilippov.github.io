magenta pink

1. ADJ

/ məˈdʒɛntə pɪŋk / (AmE), / məˈdʒɛntə pɪŋk / (BrE)
A shade of pink tinged with magenta, appearing as a vivid purplish-pink color.
a vivid pink with magenta tint

Examples

The decorator suggested using magenta pink accents to brighten up the living room.
Her dress was a bold shade of magenta pink that caught everyone's attention.
The artist blended red and blue to achieve the perfect magenta pink for the sunset sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magenta - a purplish-red color
  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color

Etymology

From 'magenta', the name of a reddish-purple dye discovered in 1859, and 'pink', the color.
