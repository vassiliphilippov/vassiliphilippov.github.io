heavy equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
Large machines and vehicles used for construction, excavation, or industrial purposes, such as bulldozers, cranes, and excavators.
large machinery for construction or industry

Examples

Construction workers operated several pieces of heavy equipment at the new building site.
Special licenses are required to drive heavy equipment like cranes and bulldozers.
The company specializes in renting out heavy equipment for industrial projects.

Synset Examples
