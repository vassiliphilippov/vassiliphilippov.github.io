Hamburg parsley

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæm.bɜːrɡ ˌpɑːr.sli / (AmE), / ˈhæm.bɜːɡ ˌpɑː.sli / (BrE)
A type of parsley with smooth leaves and a large edible root, resembling a parsnip and often used as a vegetable.
parsley with edible root like parsnip

Examples

We grew Hamburg parsley in our garden for its delicious roots.
The chef substituted Hamburg parsley for parsnip in the recipe.
Roasted Hamburg parsley makes a unique and tasty side dish.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: parsnip - an edible root vegetable with a sweet flavor
  • Synonym: parsley - a common culinary herb with flat or curly leaves
