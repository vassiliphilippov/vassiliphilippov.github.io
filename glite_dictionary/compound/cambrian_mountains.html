Cambrian Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæmbriən ˈmaʊn.tənz / (AmE), / ˈkæmbriən ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a range of rugged hills and plateaus running north to south through central Wales
mountain range in central Wales

Examples

The Cambrian Mountains offer breathtaking views across central Wales.
Many rivers in Wales originate in the Cambrian Mountains.
The rugged terrain of the Cambrian Mountains is popular with hikers and cyclists.

Etymology

Named after Cambria, the Latin name for Wales.
