Washington D.C.

1. PROPN

/ ˌwɑʃɪŋˈtən ˌdiːˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌwɒʃɪŋˈtən ˌdiːˈsiː / (BrE)
The capital city of the United States, located in the District of Columbia, functioning as the seat of the federal government.
the capital of the United States

Examples

Many tourists visit Washington D.C. to see the monuments and museums.
She moved to Washington D.C. for a job with a government agency.
The president delivered a speech in Washington D.C. last week.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Washington - the U.S. state or another use of the name

Etymology

Named after George Washington and the District of Columbia.
