stunning view

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ vju / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ vjuː / (BrE)
An extremely beautiful or impressive sight, especially one seen from a particular place.
extremely beautiful sight

Examples

The hotel room offered a stunning view of the ocean at sunrise.
We stopped at the mountain lookout to admire the stunning view below us.
From the balcony, she enjoyed a stunning view of the city skyline at night.

Synset Examples

We hiked to Necker's Knob for a stunning sunset view.
The Belvedere restaurant is famous for its stunning views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathtaking view - view so impressive as to take one's breath away
  • Synonym: spectacular view - very impressive or dramatic sight
