book of maps

1. NOUN

/ bʊk əv mæps / (AmE), / bʊk əv mæps / (BrE)
A book containing a collection of maps, typically on various geographical regions or themes.
book containing a collection of maps

Examples

He always keeps a book of maps in his car for road trips.
The library has an old book of maps from the eighteenth century.
We used a book of maps to find our way through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atlas - a book of maps or charts
