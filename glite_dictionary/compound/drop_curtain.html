drop curtain

1. NOUN

/ drɑp ˈkɝː.tən / (AmE), / drɒp ˈkɜː.tən / (BrE)
a curtain in a theater that is lowered or raised onto the stage from above, often used as background scenery
theater curtain lowered or raised from above

Examples

The stage crew quickly lowered the drop curtain before the next scene began.
A beautifully painted drop curtain added depth to the set design.
During intermission, the drop curtain concealed the actors as they changed the scenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backdrop - a painted cloth used as part of the scenery in a theater
