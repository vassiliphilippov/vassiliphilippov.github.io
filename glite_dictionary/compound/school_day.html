school day

1. NOUN

/ skuːl deɪ / (AmE), / skuːl deɪ / (BrE)
A day on which a school is open and students are expected to attend classes.
day when school classes are held

Examples

She always wakes up early on a school day to get ready.
After a long school day, the students felt tired but accomplished.
They were disappointed to learn that the field trip was scheduled on a school day.

Synset Examples

go to bed early because tomorrow is a school day

Related Terms

  • Antonym: holiday - a day when school is not in session

2. NOUN

/ skuːl deɪ / (AmE), / skuːl deɪ / (BrE)
The period during which classes are held at a school; the time when students and teachers are present for instruction.
period when school is in session

Examples

The science fair was scheduled for the last school day before summer vacation.
After a long school day, Maria liked to spend time reading at home.
Parents are encouraged to volunteer during the school day to support classroom activities.

Synset Examples

stay after school
he didn't miss a single day of school
when the school day was done we would walk home together
