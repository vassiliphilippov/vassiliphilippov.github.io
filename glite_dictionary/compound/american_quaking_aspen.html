American quaking aspen

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkweɪkɪŋ ˈæspən / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈkweɪkɪŋ ˈæspən / (BrE)
A slender deciduous tree native to North America, with leaves that tremble in the lightest breeze.
slender North American aspen tree

Examples

We saw a grove of American quaking aspens shimmering in the autumn sunlight.
The leaves of the American quaking aspen flutter even in the gentlest wind.
Wildlife often finds shelter among the American quaking aspen trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quaking aspen - an aspen tree whose leaves tremble in the breeze
