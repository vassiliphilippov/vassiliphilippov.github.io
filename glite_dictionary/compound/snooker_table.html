snooker table

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnukɚ ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈsnuːkə ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A large, rectangular table covered with cloth, used for playing snooker, typically featuring six pockets and special markings.
table used for playing snooker

Examples

The club recently bought a new snooker table for the members to enjoy.
He spent hours polishing the surface of the snooker table before an important match.
The old snooker table in the pub was the centerpiece of many friendly competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool table - table used for playing pool or billiards
