jazz festival

1. NOUN

/ dʒæz ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / dʒaz ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
a festival featuring live performances by jazz musicians or bands, often held outdoors and over several days
a festival with live jazz music performances

Examples

Every summer, thousands of people attend the jazz festival in Montreal.
The city hosted its first jazz festival in 1985, attracting famous musicians from around the world.
Tickets for the annual jazz festival usually sell out months in advance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music festival - a festival featuring multiple music performances or acts
