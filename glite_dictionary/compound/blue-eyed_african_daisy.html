blue-eyed African daisy

1. NOUN

/ bluːˌaɪd ˈæfrɪkən ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / bluːˌaɪd ˈæfrɪkən ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A bushy perennial plant native to South Africa, bearing white or violet flowers with blue centers that can cover large areas in its native habitat.
South African perennial plant with blue-centered flowers

Examples

In the spring, the hills were covered with blooming blue-eyed African daisies.
The blue-eyed African daisy is prized for its vibrant, colorful display in gardens.
Gardeners often choose the blue-eyed African daisy for its resilience and striking appearance.
