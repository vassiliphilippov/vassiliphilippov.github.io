crucible furnace

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːsɪbəl ˈfɝːnəs / (AmE), / ˈkruːsɪbəl ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
a furnace used to heat and melt metals inside refractory crucibles.
furnace for melting metal in crucibles

Examples

The factory installed a new crucible furnace to improve their metal casting process.
During the experiment, the alloy was melted in a crucible furnace at high temperatures.
Operators must wear protective gear when working near the crucible furnace due to intense heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furnace - an enclosed structure for heating materials
