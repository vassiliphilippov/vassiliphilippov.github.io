big toe

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ toʊ / (AmE), / bɪɡ təʊ / (BrE)
the largest and innermost toe on the human foot
the largest toe on the foot

Examples

She stubbed her big toe on the edge of the door.
Athletes often injure their big toe while running.
The doctor examined his swollen big toe after the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallux - the anatomical term for the big toe
