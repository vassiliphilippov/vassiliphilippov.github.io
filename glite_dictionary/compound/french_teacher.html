French teacher

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈtiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / frentʃ ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who teaches the French language
teacher of the French language

Examples

My French teacher gave us an interesting assignment on Parisian culture.
The French teacher organized a class trip to France.
She became a French teacher after studying abroad in Lyon for two years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: language teacher - a person who teaches a language
