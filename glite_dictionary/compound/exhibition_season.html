exhibition season

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛɡ.zɪˈbɪʃ.ən ˈsiː.zən / (AmE), / ˌɛks.ɪˈbɪʃ.ən ˈsiː.zən / (BrE)
the period before the start of the official football or baseball season, during which teams play practice games
pre-season period for sports practice games

Examples

Football teams often test new strategies during the exhibition season.
The players suffered an injury in the exhibition season and missed several regular season games.
Tickets are usually cheaper for games held in the exhibition season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preseason - the period before the official sports season focusing on practice and preparation
