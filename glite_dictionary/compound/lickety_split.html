lickety split

1. ADV

/ ˈlɪkəti ˌsplɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪkəti ˌsplɪt / (BrE)
very quickly; at great speed
very quickly; at high speed
informal american

Examples

He ran out the door lickety split when he heard the ice cream truck.
As soon as the bell rang, the students packed up lickety split.
She finished the whole project lickety split before anyone else.

Synset Examples

she tackled the job lickety-split

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow pace; not quickly

Etymology

Originated in the United States in the mid-19th century, possibly as a fanciful construction with rhyming reduplication.
