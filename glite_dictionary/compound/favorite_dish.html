favorite dish

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt dɪʃ / (BrE)
a particular meal or food that a person enjoys more than any other
most liked meal or food

Examples

Spaghetti carbonara is my favorite dish when I eat at Italian restaurants.
After a long trip, she asked her mother to cook her favorite dish.
He always requests his favorite dish on his birthday.

Synset Examples

He decided to repeat his favorite dish for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite food - food someone likes best
