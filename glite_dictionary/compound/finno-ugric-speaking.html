Finno-Ugric-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪnoʊˈjuːɡrɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfɪnəʊˈjuːɡrɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to communicate in a Finno-Ugric language.
speaking a Finno-Ugric language

Examples

Many Finno-Ugric-speaking communities maintain unique cultural traditions across northern Europe.
The research focused on Finno-Ugric-speaking minorities in Russia and their linguistic heritage.
Her grandparents are Finno-Ugric-speaking, having grown up in Estonia and Hungary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finno-Ugric - relating to the family of languages including Finnish and Hungarian

Etymology

From Finno-Ugric, referring to a family of languages including Finnish, Hungarian, and Estonian, and speaking.
