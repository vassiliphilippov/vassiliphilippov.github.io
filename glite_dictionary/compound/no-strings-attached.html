no-strings-attached

1. ADJ

/ ˌnoʊ ˌstrɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˌstrɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (BrE)
(of an agreement or relationship) having no special conditions or restrictions; not requiring any commitment or obligation.
without conditions or obligations

Examples

She made it clear she wanted a no-strings-attached arrangement with no expectations or commitments.
They offered a no-strings-attached donation to the charity, asking for nothing in return.
After his last relationship, he was only interested in no-strings-attached dating.

Synset Examples

She prefers no-strings-attached relationships.
He offered a no-strings-attached deal to his friend.
They agreed on a no-strings-attached arrangement.
