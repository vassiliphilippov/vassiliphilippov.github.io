gray goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a North American plant (Solidago nemoralis) with yellow flowers, sometimes used in dyeing
a wildflower used in dyeing

Examples

The fields were dotted with blooming gray goldenrod in late summer.
Dyers once used gray goldenrod to create a yellow tint for fabrics.
I spotted several clusters of gray goldenrod along the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago with yellow flowers
