lively atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪvli ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvli ˈætmosˌfɪə / (BrE)
an environment that is full of energy, excitement, or activity
energetic, vibrant environment

Examples

The café had a lively atmosphere with music playing and people chatting cheerfully.
Her birthday party was a success thanks to the lively atmosphere created by the decorations and guests.
The festival is famous for its lively atmosphere that keeps visitors entertained all day.

Synset Examples

At the shelter, dogs bark, cats meow, creating a lively atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festive atmosphere - an environment that is cheerful and celebratory
  • Antonym: dull atmosphere - an environment that is lacking in excitement or energy
