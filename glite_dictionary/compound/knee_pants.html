knee pants

1. NOUN

/ niː pænts / (AmE), / niː pænts / (BrE)
short trousers that extend to or just above the knee, traditionally worn by boys in the late 19th and early 20th centuries
short trousers ending above the knee

Examples

Boys in the early 1900s typically wore knee pants with long socks.
The old photograph shows children dressed in knee pants and jackets.
He read a story set in an era when knee pants were in fashion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knickerbockers - loose-fitting breeches gathered at or just below the knee
