Battle of Magenta

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætəl əv məˈdʒɛntə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv məˈdʒɛntə / (BrE)
a battle fought in 1859 in which French and Sardinian forces led by Napoleon III defeated the Austrians under Francis Joseph I
1859 battle; French/Sardinian victory over Austria

Examples

The Battle of Magenta was a turning point in the Second Italian War of Independence.
Napoleon III's leadership was crucial during the Battle of Magenta in 1859.
Many historians study the Battle of Magenta to understand Franco-Austrian relations in the 19th century.
