navy blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvi blu / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi bluː / (BrE)
a very dark shade of blue, resembling the color of a naval officer's uniform
a dark shade of blue

Examples

She wore a stunning navy blue dress to the gala last night.
The walls were painted in navy blue to create a calm and elegant atmosphere.
His favorite suit is navy blue, perfect for both business and formal occasions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: blue - a color intermediate between green and violet
  • Degree Higher: midnight blue - an even darker shade of blue than navy blue

Etymology

Named for the dark blue uniforms worn by the British Royal Navy since the 18th century.
