chimney corner

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪmni ˌkɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni ˌkɔːnə / (BrE)
a corner of a room near or beside a fireplace, traditionally considered a cozy or favored spot
corner by a fireplace

Examples

She sat quietly in the chimney corner, reading her book by the firelight.
The old cottage had a cozy chimney corner where guests would gather in winter.
Stories were often told in the chimney corner on long, cold evenings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hearth - the floor of a fireplace, often used to mean the area around the fireplace
