crotch dumpling

1. NOUN

/ krɑtʃ ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (AmE), / krɒtʃ ˈdʌmplɪŋ / (BrE)
A humorous or affectionate term for a person who is awkward, silly, or for a small child sitting or cuddling in someone's lap.
humorous or cute term for a silly person or cuddly child
informal slang

Examples

After a long day, the toddler curled up on his father’s lap like a little crotch dumpling.
He laughed and called himself a crotch dumpling when he tripped over his own feet.
Her cat became a warm crotch dumpling whenever she sat down to read.

Synset Examples

He joked about being a crotch dumpling during the long car ride.
When he sat down, the cat became a little crotch dumpling.
The toddler, a real crotch dumpling, snuggled into his mother's lap.
