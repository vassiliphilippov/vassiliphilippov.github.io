knit stitch

1. NOUN

/ nɪt stɪtʃ / (AmE), / nɪt stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a basic stitch in knitting made by inserting the needle through the front of the loop from the left-hand side
basic knitting stitch technique

Examples

She began the scarf with a simple knit stitch pattern.
After learning the knit stitch, he practiced for hours to perfect it.
The instructions say to alternate between knit stitch and purl stitch for the ribbed effect.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: purl stitch - a basic knitting stitch made by drawing the yarn through from the back instead of the front
