office-bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔfɪs ˌbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs ˌbeərə / (BrE)
a person who holds an official position, especially in a society, club, or organization
person holding an official position

Examples

The newly elected office-bearer outlined her vision for the organization at the annual meeting.
Every office-bearer is expected to uphold the association's ethical standards.
A new set of office-bearers will be chosen at the end of the financial year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: official - a person holding public office or having official duties

Etymology

From 'office' (official position) + 'bearer' (one who carries or holds something).
