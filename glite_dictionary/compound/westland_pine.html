Westland pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛst.lənd paɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɛst.lənd paɪn / (BrE)
A timber tree native to New Zealand, valued for its shiny white wood.
New Zealand timber tree with shiny white wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from Westland pine, prized for its lustrous appearance.
They imported Westland pine for the restoration of the historic building.
Many New Zealand forests are home to the native Westland pine.
