humorous spin

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmərəs spɪn / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəs spɪn / (BrE)
an interpretation or presentation of something intended to be funny or light-hearted
funny interpretation or presentation

Examples

The comedian put a humorous spin on current events during his stand-up routine.
She tried to give her disappointing report a humorous spin to lighten the mood.
The article offered a humorous spin on traditional holiday recipes.

Synset Examples

Pantifa is a humorous spin on political activism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comic twist - an unexpected, funny element added to a story or event
