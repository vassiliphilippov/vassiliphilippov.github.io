extra large

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkstrə ˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrə ˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a size of clothing for people who are larger than average; usually abbreviated as XL
clothing size larger than large (XL)

Examples

He always buys his T-shirts in extra large for a comfortable fit.
Do you have this jacket in extra large?
The label says the sweater is an extra large, but it feels smaller.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: large - a clothing size larger than medium but smaller than extra large
  • Degree Higher: extra extra large - a clothing size larger than extra large (often written as XXL)
