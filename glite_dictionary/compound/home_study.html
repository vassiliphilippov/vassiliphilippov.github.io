home study

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / həʊm ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
a course or program of study that is conducted at home instead of in a classroom setting
course of study done at home

Examples

She completed a home study program while working full-time.
Due to his illness, he had to switch to home study for the semester.
Many adults pursue home study to balance education with their busy schedules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance learning - education that takes place remotely, often online
  • Synonym: correspondence course - a course of study in which lessons are sent by mail to the student
