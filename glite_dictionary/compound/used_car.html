used car

1. NOUN

/ juzd kɑr / (AmE), / juːzd kɑː / (BrE)
a car that has been previously owned by someone else; not new
car that is not new; previously owned

Examples

He bought a used car to save money on his first vehicle.
The dealership specializes in selling used cars with low mileage.
After trading in her old vehicle, she decided to purchase a used car instead of a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secondhand car - a car that is not new, previously owned
  • Antonym: new car - a car that has not been previously owned
