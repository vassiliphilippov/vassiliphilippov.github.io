family Characeae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌkærəˈsiːiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌkærəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
A family of green algae, commonly known as stoneworts, that superficially resemble horsetail ferns.
a family of green algae (stoneworts)

Examples

Scientists found that family Characeae members play a crucial role in freshwater ecosystems.
The unique structure of family Characeae can sometimes be mistaken for horsetail ferns by casual observers.
Research into family Characeae has increased our understanding of green algae evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stonewort - a common name for plants in the family Characeae

Etymology

From New Latin Characeae, based on the type genus Chara.
