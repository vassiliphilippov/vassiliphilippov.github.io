electric razor

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈreɪzər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈreɪzə / (BrE)
A razor that is powered by an electric motor and used for shaving hair.
razor powered by an electric motor

Examples

He replaced his old blade with a new electric razor for a more comfortable shave.
Many travelers prefer to pack a compact electric razor for convenience.
My electric razor needs to be charged before I can use it again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: razor - a tool with a sharp blade for shaving hair
