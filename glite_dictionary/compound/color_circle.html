color circle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌsɜrkl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌsɜːkl̩ / (BrE)
A diagram in which colors are arranged around a circle to show relationships between primary, secondary, and complementary colors.
circular chart showing color relationships

Examples

Artists frequently consult the color circle when choosing harmonious palettes for their work.
The concept of complementary colors is easily illustrated with a color circle.
In her design class, she created a vibrant color circle to demonstrate color relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color wheel - A circular diagram that shows the colors of the spectrum and their relationships.
