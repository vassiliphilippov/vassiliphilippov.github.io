salad plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæləd pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæləd pleɪt / (BrE)
A small plate or shallow bowl used for serving individual portions of salad.
plate for individual servings of salad

Examples

The restaurant set a salad plate at each place before the main course arrived.
She bought a new set of salad plates for her dinner parties.
Please place your salad plate on the upper left side of your dinner setting.
