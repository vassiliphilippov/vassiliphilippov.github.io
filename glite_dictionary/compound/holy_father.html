Holy Father

1. PROPN

/ ˈhoʊli ˈfɑðər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
a title for the Pope, the head of the Roman Catholic Church
the Pope; head of Roman Catholic Church

Examples

Crowds gathered in St. Peter's Square to see the Holy Father deliver his blessing.
The Holy Father addressed issues of peace and justice in his annual message.
Many Catholics write letters to the Holy Father seeking guidance or support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pope - the Bishop of Rome and leader of the Roman Catholic Church
