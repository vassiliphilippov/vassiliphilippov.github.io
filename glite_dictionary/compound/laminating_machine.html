laminating machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæməˌneɪtɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈlæmɪneɪtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to cover materials such as paper or cards with a thin protective layer of plastic or other material
machine that laminates materials

Examples

The school secretary used the laminating machine to preserve all of the students' certificates.
Our office recently purchased a new laminating machine for ID badge production.
Please make sure the laminating machine is turned off after you finish using it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laminator - a device or machine that laminates; another term for laminating machine
