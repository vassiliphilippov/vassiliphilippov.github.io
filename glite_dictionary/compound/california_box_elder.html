California box elder

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfɔrniə bɑks ˈɛldər / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈnɪə bɒks ˈɛldə / (BrE)
a species of maple tree native to the Pacific coast of the United States, distinguished by its white mature fruits
a maple tree native to the Pacific coast US

Examples

The California box elder thrives along streams on the Pacific coast.
We saw a mature California box elder with clusters of white fruits in the park.
Landscapers often choose the California box elder for its hardiness and unique foliage.
