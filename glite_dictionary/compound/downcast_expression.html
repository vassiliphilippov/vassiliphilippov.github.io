downcast expression

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊnˌkæst ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnkɑːst ɪkˈspreʃən / (BrE)
a facial expression showing sadness or dejection
sad or dejected facial look

Examples

She entered the room with a downcast expression, barely glancing at anyone.
His downcast expression made it clear that he had received bad news.
Despite her downcast expression, she assured everyone she was feeling fine.

Synset Examples

His downcast expression revealed his disappointment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sad expression - a facial look that shows sadness
  • Antonym: cheerful expression - a facial look that shows happiness or cheerfulness
