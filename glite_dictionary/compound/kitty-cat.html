kitty-cat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtiˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈkɪtiˌkat / (BrE)
An informal term for a domestic cat, especially used by or when speaking to children.
informal term for a domestic cat
informal

Examples

My daughter loves playing with her kitty-cat after school.
A little kitty-cat was curled up on the windowsill, purring softly in the sun.
He adopted a stray kitty-cat and gave it a cozy home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat - a small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur
  • Synonym: kitty - informal term for a cat
