economic inequality

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌkɑːnəmɪk ˌɪnɪˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / iːˌkɒnəmɪk ˌɪnɪˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
The unequal distribution of wealth, income, or resources among individuals or groups within a society.
disparity in wealth or income

Examples

Many believe that economic inequality leads to social unrest within communities.
Governments around the world are trying to address economic inequality through policy reforms.
The report highlights how globalization can both reduce and increase economic inequality.

Synset Examples

Critics argue that neoliberal reforms widen economic inequality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: income inequality - disparity in levels of income among individuals or groups
  • Synonym: wealth inequality - disparity in the distribution of financial and material assets
