cine projector

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪni prəˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / ˈsɪni prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device that projects successive frames of film onto a screen to create moving images
device for projecting motion pictures

Examples

The school bought a new cine projector for its weekly film screenings.
Many old movies were shown in the hall using a cine projector.
The museum displayed a restored cine projector from the 1950s as part of its exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film projector - device that projects motion picture film onto a screen

Etymology

Cine from 'cinema', derived from French cinéma, and 'projector' from Latin proiectus (thrown forth) via English.
