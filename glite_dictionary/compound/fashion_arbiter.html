fashion arbiter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˈɑːrbɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən ˈɑːbɪtə / (BrE)
a person who establishes or decides what is fashionable; an influential authority in fashion trends
decider of fashion trends

Examples

As a fashion arbiter, she influenced designers and magazine editors worldwide.
The magazine named him this year's most influential fashion arbiter.
In Paris, a well-known fashion arbiter attended all the runway shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas

Etymology

From 'fashion' (style or trend) + 'arbiter' (Latin arbiter, judge or decision-maker).
