spiny puffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪni ˈpʌfər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˈpʌfə / (BrE)
A type of pufferfish characterized by having rigid or erectile spines on its body.
a pufferfish with spines

Examples

The spiny puffer inflates itself when threatened, making its spines stand out.
Many divers are intrigued by the unique defense mechanisms of the spiny puffer.
The aquarium recently added a spiny puffer to its marine exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pufferfish - any of various fishes of the family Tetraodontidae that inflate themselves as a defense mechanism
