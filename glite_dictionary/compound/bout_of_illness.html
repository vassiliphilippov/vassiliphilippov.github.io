bout of illness

1. NOUN

/ baʊt əv ˈɪlnəs / (AmE), / baʊt əv ˈɪlnɪs / (BrE)
a short period marked by sickness or disease
short period of sickness

Examples

After a long bout of illness, he finally returned to his usual routine.
She missed several meetings due to a sudden bout of illness.
Children can often recover quickly, even after a severe bout of illness.

Synset Examples

After a brief bout of illness, she's back to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illness - a state of being physically or mentally unwell
