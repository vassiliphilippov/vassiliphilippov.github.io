rise of socialism

1. NOUN

/ raɪz əv ˈsoʊʃəlɪzəm / (AmE), / raɪz əv ˈsəʊʃəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the period or process during which socialist ideas and movements become increasingly popular or influential in society
growth in popularity of socialism

Examples

The rise of socialism in the early 20th century led to major changes in government policies.
Many historians have studied the rise of socialism in response to economic inequality.
Debates about the causes of the rise of socialism continue among scholars.

Synset Examples

The rise of socialism influenced the recent elections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialism - a political and economic theory advocating collective or governmental ownership and administration of production and distribution
