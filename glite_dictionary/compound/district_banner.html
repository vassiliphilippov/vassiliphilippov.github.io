district banner

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪstrɪkt ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrɪkt ˈbanə / (BrE)
A banner used as a symbol by the knights of a particular district, especially in historical or heraldic contexts.
banner symbolizing a district in heraldry

Examples

The knights gathered beneath their district banner during the ceremony.
Each district banner bore unique colors and emblems to represent its region.
Historians studied the symbols sewn onto the ancient district banner displayed in the museum.
