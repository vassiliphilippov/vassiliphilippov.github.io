gas gage

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡas ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a device that shows how much gasoline remains in a vehicle's fuel tank
device showing amount of gasoline in vehicle
american

Examples

The gas gage on my dashboard showed that the tank was almost empty.
She glanced at the gas gage before deciding to stop for fuel.
If your gas gage is broken, refuel more frequently to avoid running out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - a device that shows the amount of fuel in any vehicle's tank
