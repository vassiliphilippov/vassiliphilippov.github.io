macho-man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːtʃoʊ mæn / (AmE), / ˈmatʃəʊ man / (BrE)
a man who displays strong masculinity, often emphasizing physical strength, virility, and sexual prowess
overtly masculine or virile man
informal

Examples

The actor was known as a macho-man because of his tough roles and muscular physique.
Some people feel pressured to act like a macho-man to fit in with their peers.
His macho-man image often masked his genuine sensitivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladies' man - a man who is popular with women and seeks many romantic relationships
  • Antonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of women; unmanly

Etymology

From Spanish 'macho' (male, masculine) and English 'man'.
