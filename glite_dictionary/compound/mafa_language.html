Mafa language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːfə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːfə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken primarily south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon
a Chadic language spoken near Lake Chad

Examples

Linguists have studied the Mafa language to better understand Chadic language families.
Many people in northern Cameroon speak the Mafa language as their mother tongue.
The Mafa language has unique sounds and grammatical structures compared to neighboring languages.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Chadic language - a group of related languages spoken in central and western Africa
