law student

1. NOUN

/ lɔː ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / lɔː ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a student who is studying law, especially at a law school or university
student studying law

Examples

She is a hardworking law student preparing for her final exams.
As a law student, he spends long hours reading case law in the library.
The university holds an annual moot court competition for law students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
