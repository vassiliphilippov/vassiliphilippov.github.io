northeast by north

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɔrtˈist baɪ nɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːst baɪ nɔːθ / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point north of northeast, or at an angle of 33°45′ north of east
compass point between northeast and north-northeast

Examples

The ship was heading northeast by north to avoid the storm.
On the map, the trail runs in a northeast by north direction from the cabin.
The wind shifted from northeast to northeast by north late in the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: northeast - the compass point halfway between north and east
  • Degree Higher: north-northeast - the compass point between north and northeast
