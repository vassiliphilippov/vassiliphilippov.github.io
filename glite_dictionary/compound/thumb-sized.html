thumb-sized

1. ADJ

/ θʌm saɪzd / (AmE), / θʌm saɪzd / (BrE)
approximately the same size as a human thumb
about the size of a thumb

Examples

She picked up a thumb-sized piece of chocolate and popped it into her mouth.
The museum displayed a thumb-sized fossil that was millions of years old.
They found a thumb-sized rock in the garden while digging for new plants.

Synset Examples

The recipe called for a thumb-sized ginger knob.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fingernail-sized - about the size of a fingernail
  • Degree Higher: hand-sized - about the size of a hand
