walrus mustache

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔl.rəs məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːl.rəs məˈstɑːʃ / (BrE)
A thick, bushy mustache that droops over the mouth, resembling the whiskers of a walrus.
thick, drooping mustache style

Examples

He sported a massive walrus mustache that drooped well below his lip.
The detective’s walrus mustache made him instantly recognizable in the crowded room.
After decades, his trademark walrus mustache was as grand as ever.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: handlebar mustache - a mustache with long, curved ends resembling handlebars

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the whiskers of a walrus (the marine mammal).
