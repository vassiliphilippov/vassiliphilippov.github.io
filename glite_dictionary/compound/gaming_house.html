gaming house

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪ.mɪŋ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.mɪŋ haʊs / (BrE)
a public establishment where various games of chance are played for money, typically as a business; a gambling house or casino
building where gambling games are played

Examples

The police raided the gaming house suspected of illegal activities last night.
Local regulations strictly control where a gaming house can be established.
She lost a fortune after frequenting a notorious gaming house in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - a public place for gambling and entertainment
  • Synonym: gambling den - an establishment where people participate in illegal gambling
