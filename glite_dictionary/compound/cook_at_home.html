to cook at home

1. VERB

/ kʊk æt hoʊm / (AmE), / kʊk ət həʊm / (BrE)
to prepare and make meals in one's own home instead of eating out or ordering food
prepare meals in one's own home

Examples

Many people prefer to cook at home to save money and eat healthier meals.
After a long day at work, she rarely has the energy to cook at home.
They invited friends over and decided to cook at home instead of going to a restaurant.

Synset Examples

We can order pizza, or- cook at home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to dine out - to eat a meal in a restaurant
  • Antonym: order in - to have food delivered to one's home from a restaurant
