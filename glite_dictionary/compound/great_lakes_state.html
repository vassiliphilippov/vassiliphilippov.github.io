Great Lakes State

1. PROPN

/ ˌɡreɪt ˈleɪks steɪt / (AmE), / ˌɡreɪt ˈleɪks steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Michigan, located in the Great Lakes region, with Lansing as its capital.
nickname for the U.S. state of Michigan

Examples

She grew up in the Great Lakes State before moving to Chicago for college.
The Great Lakes State is famous for its scenic shorelines and automotive industry.
Tourism has always been an important part of the economy in the Great Lakes State.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Michigan - a U.S. state in the Great Lakes region; capital: Lansing

Etymology

Refers to Michigan's location among the Great Lakes of North America.
