pancake batter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænˌkeɪk ˈbætər / (AmE), / ˈpænkeɪk ˈbætə / (BrE)
a liquid mixture of flour, eggs, milk, and other ingredients, used as the base for making pancakes
mixture used for making pancakes

Examples

She poured the pancake batter onto the hot griddle, watching it bubble and rise.
The secret to fluffy pancakes is letting the pancake batter rest before cooking.
We ran out of pancake batter halfway through breakfast on Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batter - a liquid mixture of flour and other ingredients, used in baking and cooking
