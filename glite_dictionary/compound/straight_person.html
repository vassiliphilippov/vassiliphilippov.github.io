straight person

1. NOUN

/ streɪt ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / streɪt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person whose sexual orientation is toward people of the opposite sex; a heterosexual individual
heterosexual individual

Examples

Many straight people support equal rights for the LGBTQ+ community.
He came out as gay, but his friend is a straight person.
As a straight person, she wanted to learn more about Pride events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heterosexual - a person attracted to members of the opposite sex
  • Antonym: gay person - a person attracted to members of the same sex
