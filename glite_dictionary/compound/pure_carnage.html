pure carnage

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈkɑrnɪdʒ / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈkɑːnɪdʒ / (BrE)
a situation involving intense violence and destruction, often with widespread damage or death
scene of extreme violence or destruction

Examples

The riot quickly escalated into pure carnage as cars were overturned and fires broke out.
After the crash, the scene was pure carnage with debris scattered everywhere.
The battlefield was left in a state of pure carnage after the fierce fighting ended.

Synset Examples

The movie's final scene was pure carnage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slaughter - the killing of many people or animals in a violent way
  • Synonym: massacre - an indiscriminate and brutal slaughter of many people
