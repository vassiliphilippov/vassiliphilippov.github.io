marsh rosemary

1. NOUN

/ mɑɹʃ ˈroʊzˌmɛɹi / (AmE), / mɑːʃ ˈrəʊzˌməri / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Limonium, found in temperate salt marshes, with spikes of white or mauve flowers.
plant of the genus Limonium, salt marsh habitats

Examples

The marsh rosemary thrives in coastal wetlands with high salt content.
Wild marsh rosemary adds a splash of color to the salt marsh in summer.
Botanists studied a rare species of marsh rosemary during their field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea lavender - common name for Limonium plants
