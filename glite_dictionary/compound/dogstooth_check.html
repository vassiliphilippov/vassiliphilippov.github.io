dogstooth check

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔɡzˌtuθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡzˌtuːθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
a woven fabric pattern featuring small, broken or jagged checks, resembling a dog's tooth, used in clothing and upholstery
pattern of small broken checks in fabric

Examples

The suit's dogstooth check pattern gave it a classic, sophisticated look.
She bought a scarf with a bold dogstooth check design for the winter.
Interior designers often use dogstooth check fabrics to add visual interest to chairs and sofas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houndstooth - a fabric pattern of jagged checks, similar to dogstooth check but typically larger

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the jagged shape of a dog's tooth.
