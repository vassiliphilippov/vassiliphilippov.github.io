deposit box

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑzɪt bɑks / (AmE), / dɪˈpɒzɪt bɒks / (BrE)
a secure, fireproof metal box, typically kept in a bank, used for storing valuables, documents, or other important items
secure box in a bank for valuables

Examples

She stored her jewelry in a deposit box at the local bank for safekeeping.
After his grandfather passed away, Tom accessed the family's deposit box to retrieve important documents.
Renting a deposit box gives people peace of mind when storing valuable possessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe deposit box - a locked box in a bank used to store valuables
