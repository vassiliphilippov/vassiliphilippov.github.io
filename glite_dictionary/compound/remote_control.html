remote control

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈmoʊt kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
A device used to operate a machine or apparatus, such as a television, from a distance without direct physical contact.
device for operating machines from a distance

Examples

I can't find the remote control; it must have slipped behind the sofa.
She turned up the volume with the remote control while watching her favorite show.
Most modern air conditioners come with a remote control for convenience.

Synset Examples

he lost the remote for his TV

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clicker - informal term for a remote control device
