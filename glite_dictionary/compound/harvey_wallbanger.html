Harvey Wallbanger

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːrvi ˈwɔlˌbæŋər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvi ˈwɔːlˌbæŋə / (BrE)
A cocktail made with vodka, orange juice, and Galliano liqueur.
vodka, orange juice, and Galliano cocktail

Examples

He ordered a Harvey Wallbanger at the bar to start the evening.
The signature drink at the party was the Harvey Wallbanger, served in tall glasses.
She learned how to mix a Harvey Wallbanger for her cocktail-making class.

Etymology

Named in the 1970s, possibly after a fictional surfer character created as part of a marketing campaign for Galliano liqueur.
