dog biscuit

1. NOUN

/ dɔg ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / dɒg ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A hard, dry biscuit prepared as food for dogs.
hard biscuit for dogs

Examples

She gave her puppy a dog biscuit as a reward for good behavior.
The store sells a variety of dog biscuits with different flavors and shapes.
He keeps a box of dog biscuits by the door for neighborhood dogs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog treat - a snack or reward food given to a dog, often including biscuits
