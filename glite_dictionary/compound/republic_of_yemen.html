Republic of Yemen

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈjɛmən / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈjen.ən / (BrE)
A country on the southwestern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, formed by the unification of North Yemen and South Yemen in 1990.
Country in southwestern Arabian Peninsula

Examples

Republic of Yemen has experienced significant political changes since its unification in 1990.
Travelers often visit the Republic of Yemen to explore its rich cultural heritage and historical sites.
The government of the Republic of Yemen faces ongoing challenges related to security and development.
