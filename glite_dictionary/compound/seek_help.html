to seek help

1. VERB

/ siːk hɛlp / (AmE), / siːk hɛlp / (BrE)
to ask for or attempt to obtain assistance or support, especially in dealing with a problem or difficult situation
ask for assistance or support

Examples

If you are struggling with stress, it's important to seek help from a professional.
She finally decided to seek help when her anxiety became overwhelming.
Many people hesitate to seek help because they fear being judged.

Synset Examples

Doctors advised him to seek help for priapism immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ask for help - to request help or assistance
  • Antonym: offer help - to provide or give assistance
