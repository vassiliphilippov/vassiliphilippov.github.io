softball game

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔftˌbɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌbɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game similar to baseball, played on a smaller field with a larger, softer ball.
sport resembling baseball with a larger ball

Examples

We watched a softball game at the local park on Saturday afternoon.
She joined the company’s softball game to meet new colleagues and stay active.
Rain postponed the championship softball game until next weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softball - a ball used in the game of softball or the sport itself
  • Synonym: baseball - a bat-and-ball game played between two teams
