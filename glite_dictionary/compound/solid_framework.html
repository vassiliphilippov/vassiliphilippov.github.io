solid framework

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑlɪd ˈfreɪmˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd ˈfreɪmwɜːk / (BrE)
a strong and dependable structure or basis for organizing or planning something
strong, dependable planning structure

Examples

The team developed a solid framework to guide the new policy's implementation.
Without a solid framework, the organization struggled to coordinate its efforts.
Successful businesses often rely on a solid framework of procedures and principles.

Synset Examples

The project is based on a solid framework for success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundation - the base or underlying support for something
  • Synonym: scaffolding - temporary structure for support in construction or planning
