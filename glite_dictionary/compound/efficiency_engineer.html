efficiency engineer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfɪʃənsi ˌɛnʤəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ɪˈfɪʃənsi ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
an expert who specializes in improving the efficient use of machines and personnel within an organization
expert in improving efficiency at work

Examples

The company hired an efficiency engineer to streamline their manufacturing process.
As an efficiency engineer, she recommended new procedures to reduce wasted time.
An efficiency engineer can identify areas where resources are being underutilized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrial engineer - an engineer who designs processes and systems to improve quality and productivity
