ace of clubs

1. NOUN

/ eɪs əv klʌbz / (AmE), / eɪs ɒv klʌbz / (BrE)
the playing card that is the ace in the suit of clubs
the ace card in the club suit

Examples

She drew the ace of clubs and won the hand instantly.
In bridge, the ace of clubs can be a powerful opening card.
He needed the ace of clubs to complete his royal flush.
