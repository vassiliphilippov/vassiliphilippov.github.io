electronic organ

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a keyboard musical instrument that produces sound electronically to simulate a pipe organ
an electronic keyboard simulating a pipe organ

Examples

He played the wedding march on the electronic organ, filling the hall with music.
The church replaced its old pipe organ with a new electronic organ.
Learning to play the electronic organ can be easier for beginners than mastering a traditional pipe organ.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pipe organ - a large musical instrument using pipes sounded by air
