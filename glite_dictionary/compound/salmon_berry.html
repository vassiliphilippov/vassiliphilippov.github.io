salmon berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsælmən ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsælmən ˌbɛri / (BrE)
a white-flowered raspberry native to western North America and northern Mexico, bearing thimble-shaped orange berries
orange-berried raspberry of North America

Examples

The salmon berry ripens in late spring along the coastal trails of Alaska.
Birds and bears are attracted to the vivid orange fruits of the salmon berry.
She foraged a handful of salmon berries during her hike through the damp forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a soft red edible fruit and the bush that bears it
