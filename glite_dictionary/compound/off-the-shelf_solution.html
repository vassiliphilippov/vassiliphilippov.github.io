off-the-shelf solution

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔf ðə ˈʃɛlf səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒf ðə ˈʃɛlf səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a product or service that is ready-made and available for immediate use without customization
ready-made product or service

Examples

The company implemented an off-the-shelf solution to streamline its finances.
Many small businesses prefer an off-the-shelf solution because it is cost-effective and quick to deploy.
After comparing options, we decided an off-the-shelf solution would best suit our needs.

Synset Examples

We chose an off-the-shelf solution for quick deployment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: custom solution - a product or service specifically designed for a client's needs

Etymology

From the phrase 'off the shelf', meaning taken directly from available stock rather than made to order.
