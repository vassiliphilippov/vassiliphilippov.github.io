combat casualty

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmˌbæt ˈkæʒuəlti / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.bæt ˈkæʒ.ju.əl.ti / (BrE)
the loss of military equipment during battle or as a result of combat action
loss of equipment in battle

Examples

The unit suffered a significant combat casualty when their main tank was destroyed in the attack.
Reports from the field included several combat casualty entries for damaged vehicles and lost equipment.
During the skirmish, the loss of communication devices was classified as a combat casualty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casualty - a person killed or injured in a war or accident
