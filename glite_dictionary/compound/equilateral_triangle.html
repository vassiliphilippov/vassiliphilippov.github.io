equilateral triangle

1. NOUN

/ ˌikwəˈlætərəl ˈtraɪæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl ˈtraɪæŋɡəl / (BrE)
A triangle with all three sides of equal length and all angles equal.
triangle with equal sides and angles

Examples

Each side of an equilateral triangle has the same length.
The teacher drew an equilateral triangle on the board to demonstrate symmetry.
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of an equilateral triangle.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: isosceles triangle - triangle with two equal sides
  • Antonym: scalene triangle - triangle with all sides of different lengths

Etymology

From Latin 'aequilateralis', meaning 'having equal sides'.
