powder flask

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdər flæsk / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə flɑːsk / (BrE)
a container, often made from a hollow animal horn, used for carrying gunpowder, especially in the past
container for carrying gunpowder

Examples

The soldier carefully filled his powder flask before setting out for the day.
Early hunters often carried a powder flask made from a steer horn.
He displayed his antique powder flask alongside old rifles in the museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powder horn - container made from an animal horn for carrying gunpowder
