sugar-plum fairy

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ plʌm ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə plʌm ˈfeəri / (BrE)
A person who is especially whimsical, fanciful, or light-hearted, often in a playful or magical way.
fanciful or whimsical person

Examples

He's such a sugar-plum fairy, always dreaming up new adventures and spreading joy.
During the festival, she danced through the crowd like a sugar-plum fairy, enchanting everyone she met.
My niece is a little sugar-plum fairy, full of whimsy and boundless imagination.

Synset Examples

The holiday season makes me feel like a sugar-plum fairy.

Etymology

From the title of the character 'Sugar Plum Fairy' in Tchaikovsky's ballet 'The Nutcracker' (1892).
