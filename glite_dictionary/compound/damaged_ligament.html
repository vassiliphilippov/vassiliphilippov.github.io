damaged ligament

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæmɪdʒd ˈlɪɡəmənt / (AmE), / ˈdamɪdʒd ˈlɪɡəmənt / (BrE)
a ligament that has been torn, stretched, or otherwise injured, especially in a joint such as the knee or ankle
injured ligament in a joint
medical

Examples

After the fall, he was diagnosed with a damaged ligament in his ankle.
Surgery was required to repair the damaged ligament in her knee.
Physical therapy helped him recover from a damaged ligament sustained during the game.

Synset Examples

His knee injury involved a damaged ligament that needed surgery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprain - an injury to a ligament, typically caused by wrenching a joint
  • Synonym: torn ligament - a ligament that has been completely or partially torn
