white fringed orchis

1. NOUN

/ waɪt frɪndʒd ˈɔːrkɪs / (AmE), / waɪt frɪndʒd ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
a bog orchid of eastern North America with a spike of pure white fringed flowers
North American orchid with white fringed flowers

Examples

We spotted a rare white fringed orchis while hiking in the bog.
The white fringed orchis blooms in late summer across wet meadows and marshes.
Botanists have been working to protect habitats for the white fringed orchis.
