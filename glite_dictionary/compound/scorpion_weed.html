scorpion weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔrpiən wiːd / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpiən wiːd / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Phacelia, an herbaceous plant with clusters of purple or blue flowers, commonly found in North America.
plant of the genus Phacelia

Examples

The hillside was covered with blooming scorpion weed in early spring.
Gardeners sometimes plant scorpion weed to attract pollinators to their gardens.
Hikers should avoid touching scorpion weed, as it can sometimes cause skin irritation.
