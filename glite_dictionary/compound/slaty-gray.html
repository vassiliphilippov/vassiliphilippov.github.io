slaty-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈsleɪti ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsleɪti ɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a dark gray color resembling that of slate or granite.
dark gray color like slate

Examples

The distant mountains appeared a deep slaty-gray in the evening light.
She chose slaty-gray tiles for the kitchen floor to match the countertops.
Clouds gathered overhead, turning the sky a threatening slaty-gray.

Synset Examples

the slaty sky of dawn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - the color between black and white; a neutral tone

Etymology

From 'slate' (a type of fine-grained gray rock) + 'gray'.
