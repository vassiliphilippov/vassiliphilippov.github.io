cover version

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚ ˌvɝːʒən / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə ˌvɜːʃən / (BrE)
A recording of a song that has previously been recorded or made popular by another artist.
new recording of a previously released song

Examples

Their band released a cover version of a classic 1980s hit.
The cover version was even more popular than the original recording.
Her soulful cover version brought new life to an old folk song.

Synset Examples

they made a cover of a Beatles' song

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remake - a new recording or performance of an existing song or work, often with changes
