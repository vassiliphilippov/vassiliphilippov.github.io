Pomo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊmoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊməʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Kulanapan language spoken by the Pomo people of Northern California.
language of the Pomo people

Examples

There are ongoing efforts to preserve and revitalize the Pomo language among tribal communities.
The Pomo language is an important part of the cultural heritage of Northern California's indigenous peoples.
She attended a workshop on the Pomo language to learn more about her ancestry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pomo - a member of a Native American people from Northern California

Etymology

Pomo: from the name of the indigenous Pomo people of Northern California.
