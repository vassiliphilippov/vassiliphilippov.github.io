purchasing department

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːtʃəsɪŋ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtʃəsɪŋ dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of a company or organization responsible for buying goods and services needed for its operations
division responsible for company purchases

Examples

The purchasing department handled all negotiations with suppliers to secure better prices.
Our purchasing department works closely with logistics to ensure timely delivery of materials.
The manager of the purchasing department approved the order for new laptops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procurement department - division in a company responsible for acquiring goods and services
