water tank truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑtɚ tæŋk trʌk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə tæŋk trʌk / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle equipped with a tank for transporting and distributing water
motor vehicle for carrying and dispersing water

Examples

The municipality sent a water tank truck to provide clean water after the main supply was disrupted.
During the drought, several water tank trucks delivered water to rural villages.
A water tank truck sprayed the streets to settle the dust in the construction zone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tanker truck - a truck designed to carry liquids or gases in bulk
