straight man

1. NOUN

/ streɪt mæn / (AmE), / streɪt mæn / (BrE)
a male performer in a comedy act who plays a serious role and sets up jokes for the comedian
serious partner in a comedy act

Examples

The straight man plays a crucial role in making the comedian’s jokes successful.
In classic duos, the straight man rarely gets the laughs but is essential to the act.
She loves watching comedy teams where the straight man subtly controls the scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stooge - a performer who gives a comedian opportunities to make jokes, often by acting as a foil

2. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪt ˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈstreɪt ˌman / (BrE)
a performer in a comedy duo who plays a serious role in order to highlight the humor of their partner
comedy performer playing the serious role

Examples

He plays the straight man in their routines, allowing his partner to deliver the punchlines.
In the film, her role as the straight man made the comedic scenes more effective.
The comedian needed a reliable straight man to balance the act.

Synset Examples

He's the perfect straight man for the comedy duo.
In the sketch, she plays the straight man to his antics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: comic - performer who delivers jokes in a comedy duo
