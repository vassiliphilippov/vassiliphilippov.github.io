fairy-slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri ˌslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
A rare orchid found in northern temperate bogs, bearing a single white to pink flower with purple markings, on a reddish stalk above one basal leaf.
a rare northern bog orchid

Examples

We found a breathtaking fairy-slipper growing in the shade of the mossy forest.
The delicate blooms of the fairy-slipper are easily missed unless you look closely.
Botanists were excited to spot several fairy-slippers during their survey of the wetland.
