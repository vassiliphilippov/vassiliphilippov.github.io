fairy light

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri laɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeəri laɪt / (BrE)
A small, colored decorative light, often used in strings for festive or ornamental purposes, especially at Christmas.
small decorative light for festivities

Examples

She strung fairy lights around the balcony for a warm, festive glow.
The fairy lights gave the garden party a magical atmosphere.
Each year, we hang fairy lights on the Christmas tree as part of our tradition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas light - a light used to decorate during Christmas
