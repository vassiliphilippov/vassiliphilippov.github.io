digital brownshirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl ˈbraʊnˌʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtl ˈbraʊnʃɜːt / (BrE)
an aggressively zealous online activist who seeks to suppress or silence opposing viewpoints, often using intimidation or harassment
aggressive online activist who suppresses dissent

Examples

Several journalists faced coordinated attacks from digital brownshirts after publishing critical articles.
Some forums have policies to quickly ban known digital brownshirts to keep discussions civil.
The politician denounced campaigns run by digital brownshirts attempting to intimidate her supporters.

Synset Examples

He called out the digital brownshirts for their aggressive tactics.
The online trolls acted like digital brownshirts, silencing dissent.
We must resist the digital brownshirts who spread misinformation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyberbully - a person who uses digital platforms to harass others
  • Synonym: troll - someone who posts inflammatory messages online

Etymology

From 'brownshirt', referring to paramilitary supporters of the Nazi Party, combined with 'digital' to indicate online behavior.
