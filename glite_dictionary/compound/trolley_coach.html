trolley coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑli ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A passenger bus powered by electricity drawn from overhead wires, typically used for public transport.
electric bus using overhead wires

Examples

The city replaced its old trolley coach fleet with newer electric buses.
During the parade, a vintage trolley coach carried visitors through the historic district.
Many people prefer the quiet ride of a trolley coach compared to a diesel bus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trolleybus - bus powered by electricity from overhead wires
  • Degree Lower: bus - road vehicle for carrying passengers
