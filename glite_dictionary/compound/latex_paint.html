latex paint

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪtɛks peɪnt / (AmE), / ˈleɪtɛks peɪnt / (BrE)
a water-based paint that uses a latex binder, commonly used for interior and exterior surfaces
water-based paint with latex binder

Examples

I decided to use latex paint for the living room walls because it dries quickly.
Most modern homes are painted with latex paint due to its easy cleanup and low odor.
After a few coats of latex paint, the old desk looked brand new.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oil paint - paint made with an oil-based binder

Etymology

From 'latex' (the binding material) and 'paint'.
