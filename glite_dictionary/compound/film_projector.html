film projector

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm prəˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / fɪlm prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
A device that projects successive frames from a reel of film onto a screen to display moving pictures.
device for projecting film onto a screen

Examples

The old cinema still uses a film projector to show classic movies every weekend.
She learned how to operate the film projector for her school's movie night.
With the advent of digital technology, the film projector became less common in modern theaters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: projector - device that projects images onto a surface
