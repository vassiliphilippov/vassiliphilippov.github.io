Spanish lime tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈspænɪʃ laɪm triː / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ laɪm triː / (BrE)
a tropical American tree that produces a small edible fruit with green leathery skin and sweet, juicy, translucent pulp
tropical tree with edible fruit

Examples

The Spanish lime tree thrives in the warm climates of the Caribbean and Central America.
Each summer, the market sells fresh fruit from the Spanish lime tree.
Children eagerly gathered the ripe fruit that had fallen from the old Spanish lime tree in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mamoncillo - another name for the Spanish lime tree
  • Synonym: genip - another name for the Spanish lime tree or its fruit
