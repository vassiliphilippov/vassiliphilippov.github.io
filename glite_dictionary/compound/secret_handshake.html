secret handshake

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːkrət ˈhændʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt ˈhændʃeɪk / (BrE)
a private gesture or handshake used as a sign of membership, friendship, or shared knowledge, often kept hidden from outsiders
private gesture of greeting

Examples

The two friends revealed their secret handshake during the school's talent show.
She taught her younger brother their secret handshake so he could join their club.
Mastering the secret handshake was a requirement for all new team members.

Synset Examples
