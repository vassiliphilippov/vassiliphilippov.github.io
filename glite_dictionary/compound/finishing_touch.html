finishing touch

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ tʌtʃ / (BrE)
a final detail or addition that completes or perfects something
final detail that completes something

Examples

The fresh flowers on the table were the finishing touch to the beautiful dinner setting.
She added a dash of pepper as the finishing touch to her homemade soup.
The artist signed his name, giving the portrait its finishing touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final touch - last improvement or addition
  • Synonym: crowning achievement - culminating accomplishment or final triumph
  • Antonym: beginning - the point in time or space at which something starts
