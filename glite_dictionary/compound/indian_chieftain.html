Indian chieftain

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈtʃiːftən / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃiːftən / (BrE)
the leader of a group or tribe of Native Americans
leader of a Native American tribe

Examples

The council met under the guidance of the respected Indian chieftain.
An Indian chieftain negotiated peace with the settlers.
Stories were told about the bravery of the Indian chieftain who led his people through hardship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief - leader of a tribe or clan
