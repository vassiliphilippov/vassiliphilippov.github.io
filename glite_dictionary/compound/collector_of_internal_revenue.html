collector of internal revenue

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛktər əv ˌɪntərnəl rɪˈvɛnju / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktə əv ˌɪntənəl rɪˈvɛnjuː / (BrE)
an official responsible for collecting taxes on income and other revenues for the government
government tax collection official

Examples

The collector of internal revenue visited the company to review its tax records.
As a collector of internal revenue, she worked to ensure compliance with federal tax laws.
He was appointed collector of internal revenue for the district last year.
