buried beneath

1. X

/ ˈbɛrid bəˈniθ / (AmE), / ˈbɛrid bɪˈniːθ / (BrE)
hidden under something so as to be covered or concealed by it, often referring to objects, material, or secrets
hidden under something

Examples

The documents were buried beneath a pile of old magazines.
Centuries-old artifacts remain buried beneath layers of volcanic ash.
The truth was buried beneath years of secrecy and misinformation.

Synset Examples

They found treasure buried beneath the old oak tree.
The treasure lies buried down below the old oak tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buried under - hidden or covered by something above it
  • Antonym: exposed - not hidden or covered; visible
