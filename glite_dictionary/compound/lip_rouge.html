lip rouge

1. NOUN

/ lɪp ruːʒ / (AmE), / lɪp ruːʒ / (BrE)
a cosmetic preparation used to color the lips; lipstick
cosmetic for coloring lips

Examples

She applied a deep red lip rouge before leaving for the party.
Classic film stars were known for wearing bold lip rouge on screen.
A small container of lip rouge was found in her vintage makeup collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lipstick - a cosmetic in stick form used for coloring lips

Etymology

From French 'rouge' meaning 'red', referring to cosmetic coloring.
