shortness of breath

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrt.nəs əv brɛθ / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.nəs əv brɛθ / (BrE)
difficulty in breathing or a feeling of not getting enough air; also called dyspnea.
difficulty breathing; dyspnea
medical

Examples

He went to the doctor because he was experiencing shortness of breath after mild exercise.
Allergies can sometimes cause shortness of breath and wheezing in affected individuals.
The nurse asked if I had any symptoms like fever or shortness of breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyspnea - medical term for shortness of breath
