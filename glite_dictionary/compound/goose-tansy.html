goose-tansy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːs ˌtænzi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs ˌtænzi / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant with leaves that are silvery underneath, found in northern parts of the United States, Europe, and Asia.
a perennial herbaceous plant with silvery leaves

Examples

The leaves of the goose-tansy gleam silver in the early morning sunlight.
Goose-tansy grows wild across much of Scandinavia and northern Canada.
Gardeners sometimes plant goose-tansy as ground cover in their flower beds.
