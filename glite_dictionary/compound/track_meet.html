track meet

1. NOUN

/ træk mit / (AmE), / træk miːt / (BrE)
a sporting competition in which athletes compete in various track and field events, typically between different teams or schools
athletics competition with track and field events

Examples

Our school is hosting a track meet this Saturday, and several local teams will participate.
She won three gold medals at the regional track meet last year.
The coach encouraged everyone to do their best at the upcoming track meet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletics meeting - British term for a competitive athletics event
