oozlum bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈuːzləm bɜrd / (AmE), / ˈuːzləm bɜːd / (BrE)
a mythical bird, especially in British humor, said to fly in ever-decreasing circles until it disappears, often used to describe aimless or confused behavior
mythical bird signifying aimless confusion
british

Examples

After hours of confusing instructions, the team wandered like an oozlum bird with no direction.
She described the project as chasing an oozlum bird, never getting anywhere.
His explanation went in circles, much like an oozlum bird disappearing from sight.

Synset Examples

He joked that he felt like an oozlum bird, going around in circles.
They say the oozlum bird flies in circles until it disappears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wild goose chase - a futile or hopeless pursuit, especially one that is aimless

Etymology

Origin uncertain; recorded in British military slang from the mid-20th century, possibly from playful coinage.
