picnic area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪknɪk ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpɪknɪk ˌeəriə / (BrE)
an outdoor place equipped or set aside for people to have picnics, often with tables and facilities
outdoor space for picnicking

Examples

We found a lovely picnic area near the lake for our family lunch.
The park's picnic area includes several tables and barbecue grills.
Children played while their parents relaxed at the shaded picnic area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picnic spot - an informal location suitable for a picnic
