council chamber

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.səl ˌtʃeɪm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl ˌtʃeɪm.bə / (BrE)
a room where a council or committee meets to conduct official business
room for council or committee meetings

Examples

The mayor addressed the city council in the council chamber during the weekly meeting.
Photographs are not permitted inside the council chamber unless authorized.
The historic council chamber was renovated to improve its acoustics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boardroom - room where a board of directors meets
