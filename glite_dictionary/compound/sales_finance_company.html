sales finance company

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz fəˈnæns ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / seɪlz fɪˈnɑːns ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that purchases installment sales contracts from retail merchants, usually at a discount, and collects payments from customers
company buying retail sales contracts

Examples

The retailer sold its installment contracts to a sales finance company to receive immediate cash.
A sales finance company usually collects monthly payments directly from the customer.
Many automobile dealers work closely with a sales finance company to manage their financing operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance company - a company that makes loans to individuals or businesses
