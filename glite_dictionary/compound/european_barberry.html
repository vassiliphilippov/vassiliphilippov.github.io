European barberry

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈrəʊpiən ˈbɑːrˌbɛri / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈbɑːbəri / (BrE)
an upright deciduous shrub native to Europe, widely naturalized in the United States, with clusters of juicy red berries
a European shrub with clusters of red berries

Examples

The European barberry is popular for its vibrant red berries and prickly stems.
Gardeners often plant European barberry as an ornamental shrub due to its attractive foliage.
Wild birds feed on the berries of the European barberry during the fall and winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barberry - any shrub of the genus Berberis, especially those with edible red berries
