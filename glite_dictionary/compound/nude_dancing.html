nude dancing

1. NOUN

/ njud ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / njuːd ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
Erotic dancing performed with little or no clothing, typically intended to arouse or entertain an audience.
erotic dancing with minimal clothing

Examples

The club offered nude dancing as its main attraction.
She became famous for her nude dancing performances across Europe.
Some people consider nude dancing to be a form of artistic expression.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striptease - erotic dance in which the performer removes clothing

Etymology

From 'nude', meaning naked or unclothed, and 'dancing', the act of moving rhythmically to music.
