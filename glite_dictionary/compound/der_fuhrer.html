Der Fuhrer

1. PROPN

/ dɛr ˈfyʊrər / (AmE), / də ˈfjʊərə / (BrE)
the title used for Adolf Hitler, the leader of Nazi Germany from 1934 to 1945
title of Adolf Hitler, Nazi Germany leader

Examples

Few figures in history are as infamous as Der Fuhrer for the crimes of the Nazi regime.
Propaganda posters often depicted Der Fuhrer as the sole savior of Germany.
He referred to himself as Der Fuhrer to emphasize his absolute authority over the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Adolf Hitler - German Nazi dictator during World War II (1889–1945)

Etymology

German, meaning 'the leader'; from 'der' (the) + 'Führer' (leader).
