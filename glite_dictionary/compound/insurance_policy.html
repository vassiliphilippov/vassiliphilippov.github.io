insurance policy

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊr.əns ˈpɑːl.ə.si / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns ˈpɒl.ɪ.si / (BrE)
A written contract or certificate providing details of insurance coverage between the insurer and policyholder.
written contract of insurance

Examples

I renewed my insurance policy before it expired to ensure continuous coverage.
Carefully reading the terms of your insurance policy can help avoid surprises during a claim.
The company offers various types of insurance policy to suit different customer needs.

Synset Examples

you should have read the small print on your policy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: policy - a document stating the terms of an insurance contract
