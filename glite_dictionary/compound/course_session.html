course session

1. NOUN

/ kɔrs ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / kɔːs ˈsɛʃən / (BrE)
a regularly scheduled class or meeting that is part of a larger course of study
scheduled class or meeting of a course

Examples

Each course session will include a discussion and a short quiz.
Attendance at every course session is required to pass the class.
The professor announced that the next course session would be held in a different room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: class session - a meeting of a class
  • Synonym: lecture - an educational talk or presentation, often as part of university teaching
