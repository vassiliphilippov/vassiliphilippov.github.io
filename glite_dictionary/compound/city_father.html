city father

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˈfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
an important and respected official in the government of a city, such as a member of the city council
important municipal official

Examples

The city father attended the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the new town library.
For decades, she served as a city father, shaping the future of the municipality.
Many residents look to the city fathers for leadership during challenging times.
