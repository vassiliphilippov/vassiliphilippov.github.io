anal glaucoma

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.nəl ɡlɔˈkoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.nəl ɡlɔˈkəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A humorous slang term for excessive stubbornness or preoccupation with trivial details, resulting in an inability to see the broader situation.
humorous term for excessive stubbornness
slang informal

Examples

Her anal glaucoma made every team meeting drag on with endless minor corrections.
Because of his anal glaucoma, he couldn't accept any changes to the original plan.
If you let anal glaucoma take over, you'll miss the main purpose of the project.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nitpicking - focusing excessively on minor details
  • Synonym: pedantic - overly concerned with minor details or rules

Etymology

Coined as a play on 'anal' (colloquial for obsessively detail-focused) and 'glaucoma' (an eye disease causing blindness), punningly suggesting someone unable to 'see' beyond their rearward focus.
