quite a

1. ADV

/ ˈkwaɪt ə / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪt ə / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone or something is unusually noticeable, remarkable, or impressive (not used with negatives)
remarkably impressive or exceptional

Examples

That was quite a story you told—it amazed everyone in the room.
We had quite a storm last night, with thunder shaking the windows.
He's quite a musician, able to play several instruments flawlessly.

Synset Examples

her victory was quite something
she's quite a girl

quite a walk
we've had quite an afternoon
