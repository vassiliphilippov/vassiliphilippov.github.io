anti-sway bar

1. NOUN

/ ˌæntiˈsweɪ bɑr / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsweɪ bɑː / (BrE)
A rigid metal bar connected to the suspension of a vehicle to reduce body roll and stabilize the chassis during turns.
bar stabilizing a vehicle's suspension

Examples

Many modern vehicles include an anti-sway bar for improved cornering stability.
After installing a new anti-sway bar, the car handled sharp turns much better.
The mechanic checked the anti-sway bar for wear during the inspection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stabilizer bar - a bar connecting opposite wheels to reduce body roll
  • Synonym: sway bar - an alternative term for anti-sway bar in vehicles
