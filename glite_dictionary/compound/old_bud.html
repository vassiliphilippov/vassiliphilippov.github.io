old bud

1. NOUN

/ oʊld bʌd / (AmE), / əʊld bʌd / (BrE)
an informal term for a long-time or childhood friend
long-time friend (informal)
informal

Examples

I ran into an old bud at the coffee shop and we reminisced about high school.
After ten years apart, my old bud and I picked up right where we left off.
Whenever I feel down, I know I can always call my old bud for support.

Synset Examples

I just met my old bud from college yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old friend - a friend from the past or someone with whom one has had a long relationship
