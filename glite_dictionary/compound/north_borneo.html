North Borneo

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ bɔrˈnioʊ / (AmE), / nɔːθ ˈbɔːniəʊ / (BrE)
the former name for the northern part of Borneo, now called Sabah, a state of Malaysia
former name of Sabah, Malaysia

Examples

During the colonial era, North Borneo was administered by the British North Borneo Company.
Oil palm plantations have transformed the landscape of North Borneo over the past fifty years.
Tourists visiting North Borneo today will find it referred to as Sabah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sabah - a state in Malaysia on the island of Borneo, formerly known as North Borneo

Etymology

Named for its location in the northern part of Borneo; 'Borneo' from Malay 'Borneo'.
