lazy fat-ass

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪzi ˈfæt ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈleɪzi ˈfæt ˌɑːs / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar slang insult for someone perceived as both lazy and overweight.
offensive insult for a lazy, overweight person
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

He's such a lazy fat-ass, he won't even get up to answer the door.
They called their roommate a lazy fat-ass after he slept all day and left a mess.
Please stop calling yourself a lazy fat-ass; it's not true and it's not healthy.

Synset Examples

She laughed at her friend for being a lazy fat-ass.
His friends teased him for being a lazy fatfuck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slob - a lazy and messy person
