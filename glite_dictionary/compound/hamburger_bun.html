hamburger bun

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmˌbɝɡɚ bʌn / (AmE), / ˈhæmbəːɡə bʌn / (BrE)
a round, soft bread roll sliced horizontally and typically used to hold a hamburger patty and its accompaniments
bread roll for hamburgers

Examples

She toasted the hamburger bun before adding the beef patty and toppings.
The recipe calls for sesame seeds sprinkled on top of each hamburger bun.
I bought a pack of whole wheat hamburger buns for the barbecue this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - a small round bread roll
