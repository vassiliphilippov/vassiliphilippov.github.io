surplus to requirements

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɜr.pləs tu rɪˈkwaɪr.mənts / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.pləs tə rɪˈkwaɪə.mənts / (BrE)
more than what is needed or wanted; no longer necessary or useful
no longer needed or useful

Examples

After the restructuring, several employees were considered surplus to requirements and let go.
We decided to sell the furniture that was surplus to requirements.
Once the new system was installed, the old equipment became surplus to requirements.

Synset Examples

The outdated software was deemed surplus to requirements.
She cleared out surplus to requirements items from her closet.
His old laptop was surplus to requirements after the upgrade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redundant - not or no longer needed or useful; superfluous
  • Antonym: necessary - needed to be done or achieved; essential
