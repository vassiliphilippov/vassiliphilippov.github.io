news magazine

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuz məɡəˌzin / (AmE), / ˈnjuːz məɡəˌziːn / (BrE)
a magazine that reports and analyzes current events, usually published weekly or at regular intervals
magazine reporting on current events

Examples

She subscribes to a weekly news magazine to stay updated on current affairs.
The news magazine published an in-depth analysis of the election results.
Several international news magazines covered the story extensively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newspaper - a publication, usually issued daily or weekly, containing news, articles, and advertisements
