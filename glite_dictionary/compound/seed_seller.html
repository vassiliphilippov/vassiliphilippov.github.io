seed seller

1. NOUN

/ sid ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / siːd ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person or company that sells seeds for planting
person or company selling seeds

Examples

The seed seller offered a wide variety of vegetable and flower seeds at the market.
Many farmers rely on their local seed seller for high-quality crops each season.
After retiring, she opened a small shop as a seed seller in her hometown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seedsman - someone who sells seeds for planting
