bath mat

1. NOUN

/ bæθ mæt / (AmE), / bɑːθ mæt / (BrE)
a small rug or towel placed on the bathroom floor to stand on after bathing or showering, used to absorb water and prevent slipping
mat to stand on after a bath or shower

Examples

She placed a bath mat on the floor to keep her feet dry.
After his shower, he stepped onto the soft bath mat.
The blue bath mat matches the color of the bathroom tiles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mat - a piece of fabric material placed on a surface
