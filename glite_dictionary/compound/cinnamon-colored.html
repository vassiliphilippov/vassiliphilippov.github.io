cinnamon-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the warm, light to reddish-brown color of cinnamon spice.
having the color of cinnamon

Examples

The artist chose a cinnamon-colored paint to evoke a warm, inviting atmosphere in the living room.
She wore a beautiful cinnamon-colored dress that complemented her complexion perfectly.
The dog's cinnamon-colored fur shone in the afternoon sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chestnut - a medium reddish-brown color
