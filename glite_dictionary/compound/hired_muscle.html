hired muscle

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪɚd ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ˈmʌsəl / (BrE)
people who are paid to provide physical protection or intimidation, often acting as enforcers or bodyguards
paid enforcers or bodyguards

Examples

The boss brought in some hired muscle to intimidate the competitors.
After receiving threats, they decided to employ hired muscle for protection.
Rumors spread that the company uses hired muscle to collect debts.

Synset Examples

He was just hired muscle, nothing more than security.
They hired muscle to ensure the shipment arrived safely.
The club used hired muscle to manage rowdy patrons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodyguard - person employed to protect someone from harm
  • Synonym: enforcer - person who enforces the will of others with force or threats
