yellow hue

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ hju / (AmE), / ˈjeləʊ hjuː / (BrE)
a particular shade or tint of the color yellow
a shade or tint of yellow

Examples

She chose a yellow hue for the kitchen to create a warm, inviting space.
The artist mixed several paints to achieve the perfect yellow hue for the sunflower petals.
With age, the pages of the old book took on a faint yellow hue.
