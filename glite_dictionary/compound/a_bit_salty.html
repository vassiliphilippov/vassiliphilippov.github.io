a bit salty

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈsɔlti / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈsɒlti / (BrE)
Somewhat rude or sarcastic in tone or manner.
slightly rude or sarcastic
informal

Examples

She was a bit salty when she commented on my new haircut.
His jokes can get a bit salty if he's had a long day.
The review was a bit salty, but it still made me laugh.

Synset Examples

His comments were a bit salty today.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: salty - rude or sarcastic in speech or attitude

Etymology

From the slang use of 'salty' for irritable or sharp-tongued, originally nautical slang for experienced and tough.
