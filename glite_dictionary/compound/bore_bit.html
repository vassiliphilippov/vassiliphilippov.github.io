bore bit

1. NOUN

/ bɔr bɪt / (AmE), / bɔː bɪt / (BrE)
a tool or attachment for a drill, designed for penetrating rock or other hard materials
drill tool used for penetrating rock

Examples

The engineer selected a specialized bore bit to drill through the granite.
They replaced the worn-out bore bit before resuming operations at the quarry.
Oil exploration companies often use a diamond-tipped bore bit for deep drilling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill bit - a cutting tool attached to a drill for making holes
