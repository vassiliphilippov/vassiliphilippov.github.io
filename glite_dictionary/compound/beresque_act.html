beresque act

1. NOUN

/ bɚˈɛsk æk̩t / (AmE), / bəˈrɛsk akt / (BrE)
An artistic performance that combines elements of burlesque and dance.
burlesque-dance performance

Examples

The theater featured a beresque act that enthralled the audience with its humor and choreography.
She trained for months to perfect her role in the new beresque act.
Tickets sold out within hours for the city's most anticipated beresque act of the year.

Synset Examples

Her dance performance was a stunning beresque act.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burlesque show - a theatrical entertainment featuring parody and striptease

Etymology

Blend of 'burlesque' and 'dance', formed by combining part of each word with 'act'.
