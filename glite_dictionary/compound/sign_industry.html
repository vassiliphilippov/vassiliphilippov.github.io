sign industry

1. NOUN

/ saɪn ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / saɪn ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector involved in the production of signs, including design, manufacturing, and installation.
industry producing signs

Examples

He started working in the sign industry after learning graphic design.
The sign industry has evolved with the advent of digital printing technology.
Many companies in the sign industry specialize in custom outdoor signage for businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign-making - the process of producing signs
