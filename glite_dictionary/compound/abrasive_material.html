abrasive material

1. NOUN

/ əˈbreɪsɪv məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / əˈbreɪsɪv məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a substance, such as sandpaper or powder, used for grinding, polishing, or cleaning surfaces by wearing them down
substance used for grinding or polishing

Examples

Diamond is often used as an abrasive material in industrial applications.
Always wear protective gloves when handling any type of abrasive material.
The surface was smoothed using a fine abrasive material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abrasive - a substance used for grinding or polishing
