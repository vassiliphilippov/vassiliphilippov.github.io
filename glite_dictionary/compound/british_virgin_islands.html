British Virgin Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˌbrɪtɪʃ ˈvɜrdʒɪn ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ ˈvɜːdʒɪn ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the Caribbean Sea, a British Overseas Territory, consisting of more than 40 islands, of which about 15 are inhabited
British Overseas Territory in the Caribbean

Examples

Many tourists visit the British Virgin Islands each year for their pristine beaches and sailing opportunities.
The hurricane caused significant damage to the British Virgin Islands in 2017.
