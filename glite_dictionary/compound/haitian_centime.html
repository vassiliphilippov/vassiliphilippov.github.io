Haitian centime

1. NOUN

/ heɪˈʃən sɛnˈtiːm / (AmE), / heɪˈʃən sɒ̃ˈtim / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Haiti, equal to one hundredth of a gourde
monetary unit of Haiti

Examples

The coin is worth only one Haitian centime, making it nearly obsolete in daily transactions.
Prices are sometimes listed in Haitian centimes as well as in gourdes.
He found an old Haitian centime while sorting through his coin collection.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gourde - the basic monetary unit of Haiti

Etymology

Centime from French 'centime' meaning 'hundredth', used in various Francophone monetary systems.
