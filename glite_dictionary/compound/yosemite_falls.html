Yosemite Falls

1. PROPN

/ joʊˈsɛmɪti fɔlz / (AmE), / jəʊˈsɛmɪti fɔːlz / (BrE)
a series of dramatic waterfalls located in Yosemite National Park, California, and known as one of the tallest waterfalls in North America
series of waterfalls in Yosemite, California

Examples

We hiked all day to see the breathtaking Yosemite Falls from the valley floor.
Each spring, melting snow swells Yosemite Falls to its peak flow, impressing countless visitors.
During the dry season, Yosemite Falls can diminish to a mere trickle.

Etymology

"Yosemite" likely from a Native American (Miwok) word meaning 'killer' or 'those who kill'.
