mint-scented

1. ADJ

/ mɪnt ˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having the smell or fragrance of mint.
having a mint fragrance

Examples

The fresh mint-scented air reminded her of summer afternoons in the garden.
He bought a mint-scented candle to freshen up his apartment.
She applied a mint-scented lotion to her hands after washing them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minty - having a flavor or smell of mint
