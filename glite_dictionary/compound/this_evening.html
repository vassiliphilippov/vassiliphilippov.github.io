this evening

1. ADV

/ ðɪs ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ðɪs ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
During the evening of the current day; at some time between late afternoon and night today.
during the evening of today

Examples

Are you free this evening to discuss the project?
The weather forecast says it might rain this evening.
I'm planning to make a special dinner this evening for my family.
