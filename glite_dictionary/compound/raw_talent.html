raw talent

1. NOUN

/ rɔː ˈtælənt / (AmE), / rɔː ˈtalənt / (BrE)
natural ability or aptitude, especially when it is impressive and not yet refined by training or experience.
natural, undeveloped impressive ability

Examples

The coach was amazed by her raw talent on the basketball court.
Although he lacks experience, his raw talent is undeniable.
With some proper training, her raw talent could make her a champion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural ability - inborn skill or aptitude
  • Antonym: developed skill - ability refined by training and experience
