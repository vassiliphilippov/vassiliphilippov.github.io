Lake Trasimenus

1. PROPN

/ leɪk træzɪˈmiːnəs / (AmE), / leɪk træzɪˈmiːnəs / (BrE)
the site of a major battle in 217 BC during the Second Punic War, where Hannibal ambushed and defeated a Roman army led by Flaminius
site of a 217 BC battle between Hannibal and Rome

Examples

The Battle of Lake Trasimenus was one of the most successful ambushes in military history.
Hannibal's tactics at Lake Trasimenus are still studied by military strategists today.
Many soldiers lost their lives at Lake Trasimenus during the Second Punic War.
