tool-and-die work

1. NOUN

/ ˌtuːl ən ˈdaɪ ˌwɝːk / (AmE), / ˌtuːl ən ˈdaɪ ˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the craft or occupation of designing, making, and repairing specialized tools and dies used in manufacturing processes
manufacturing craft of making tools and dies

Examples

He found steady employment in tool-and-die work at the local manufacturing plant.
Advancements in tool-and-die work have improved the efficiency of the automotive industry.
Her expertise in tool-and-die work made her an invaluable member of the engineering team.
