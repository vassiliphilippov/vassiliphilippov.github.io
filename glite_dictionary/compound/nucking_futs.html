nucking futs

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌkɪŋ fʌts / (AmE), / ˈnʌkɪŋ fʌts / (BrE)
Completely crazy or foolish; used informally, often humorously, as a spoonerism of a vulgar phrase.
completely crazy or foolish (slang)
informal slang

Examples

I thought the plan was nucking futs, but somehow it worked.
You must be nucking futs to go skydiving in this weather!
Her nucking futs energy always makes our meetings a bit wild.

Synset Examples

That idea is completely nucking futs; it won't work.
After that last meeting, I felt nucking futs!
He's been acting nucking futs since he lost his job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or wildly foolish
  • Synonym: nuts - slang for crazy or insane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; reasonable; not crazy

Etymology

Formed as a spoonerism of the phrase 'fucking nuts', substituting initial sounds for humorous or euphemistic effect.
