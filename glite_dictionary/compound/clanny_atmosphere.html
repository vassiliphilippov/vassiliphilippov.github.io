clanny atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæni ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈklæni ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
An environment that feels warm, cozy, and welcoming, often like that of a close-knit family or group.
warm and welcoming environment

Examples

The clanny atmosphere at the café made everyone feel right at home.
She enjoyed the clanny atmosphere of the small-town festival.
Despite being strangers, the group quickly developed a clanny atmosphere during their retreat.

Synset Examples

The clanny atmosphere at the party was inviting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homely atmosphere - a cozy or comfortable environment
  • Antonym: hostile atmosphere - an unfriendly or unwelcoming environment

Etymology

From 'clanny', relating to a clan or close-knit group, and 'atmosphere', from Greek 'atmos' (vapor) + 'sphaira' (sphere).
