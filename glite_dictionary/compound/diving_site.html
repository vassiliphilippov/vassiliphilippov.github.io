diving site

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvɪŋ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ saɪt / (BrE)
a location that is suitable or popular for scuba diving
place for scuba diving

Examples

The coral reef near the island is a renowned diving site for marine enthusiasts.
We booked a boat tour to reach the remote diving site recommended by our instructor.
Many tourists visit this national park because of its excellent diving sites.

Synset Examples

The old shipwreck was a popular diving site.
