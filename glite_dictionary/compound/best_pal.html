best pal

1. NOUN

/ bɛst pæl / (AmE), / bɛst pæl / (BrE)
a person's closest and dearest friend; a best friend
closest friend; best friend
informal

Examples

He has always been my best pal, even since childhood.
Whenever I need advice, I turn to my best pal for help.
After moving to a new city, she soon found a best pal at work.

Synset Examples

He’s my best pal; we do everything together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best friend - a person's closest friend
