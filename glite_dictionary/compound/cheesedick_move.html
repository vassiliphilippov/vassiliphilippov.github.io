cheesedick move

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiːzˌdɪk muːv / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌdɪk məʊv / (BrE)
an action that is foolish, cowardly, or seen as lacking integrity, often causing annoyance or loss
foolish or cowardly action
informal slang

Examples

Calling your teammate out in public was a cheesedick move and didn’t help the situation.
That referee made a total cheesedick move by ignoring the obvious foul.
Everyone agreed it was a cheesedick move to quit without notice.

Synset Examples

That cheesedick move cost us the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowardly act - an act lacking courage
  • Antonym: brave move - an act showing courage or boldness
