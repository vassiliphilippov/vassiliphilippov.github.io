perfect shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.fɪkt ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt ʃɒt / (BrE)
a photograph that is exceptionally well-composed, timed, or executed
an exceptionally good photograph

Examples

It took Sarah several tries before she got the perfect shot of the bird in flight.
The photographer waited patiently for the perfect shot as the light changed at sunset.
He was thrilled when his perfect shot was featured in a national magazine.

Synset Examples

She captured a perfect shot of the sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapshot - an informal photograph taken quickly
  • Synonym: photo - a picture produced using a camera
