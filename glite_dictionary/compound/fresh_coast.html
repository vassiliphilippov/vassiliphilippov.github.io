Fresh Coast

1. PROPN

/ frɛʃ koʊst / (AmE), / frɛʃ kəʊst / (BrE)
a nickname for a region, especially along the Great Lakes, celebrated for its beaches and natural scenery
region known for beaches and scenery

Examples

Every summer, thousands of tourists visit the Fresh Coast to enjoy its pristine water and sandy beaches.
The Fresh Coast has become a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and families alike.
She dreams of buying a cottage on the Fresh Coast to wake up to lake views every morning.

Synset Examples

We spent our vacation relaxing on the Fresh Coast beaches.
The Fresh Coast is famous for its stunning sunsets.
