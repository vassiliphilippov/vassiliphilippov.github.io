rape suspect

1. NOUN

/ reɪp ˈsʌs.ˌpɛkt / (AmE), / reɪp ˈsʌs.pɛkt / (BrE)
A person who is suspected of having committed rape.
person suspected of rape

Examples

The police interrogated the rape suspect at the station earlier this morning.
After a week-long investigation, authorities arrested a rape suspect in connection with the case.
The identity of the rape suspect has not yet been released to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspect - person believed to be guilty of a crime
