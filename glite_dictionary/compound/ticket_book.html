ticket book

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkət bʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt bʊk / (BrE)
a book containing tickets that can be torn out and used separately
book of removable tickets

Examples

She bought a ticket book for the city bus, which lasted her two weeks.
The inspector asked to see my ticket book before letting me board the train.
You can get a discount if you purchase a ticket book instead of single fare tickets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: book of tickets - a book containing tickets to be used individually
