St John's wort family

1. NOUN

/ sɛnt ˈdʒɑnz wɜrt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / sənt ˈdʒɒnz wɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of mostly tropical trees, shrubs, and vines (Hypericaceae) known for producing oils, resins, and some usable timber.
family of tropical plants (Hypericaceae)

Examples

The St John's wort family includes plants that are valued both for their medicinal and ornamental uses.
Many members of the St John's wort family are known for producing resins and essential oils.
Botanists have identified more than 400 species in the St John's wort family worldwide.
