ah pui

1. NOUN

/ ɑ ˈpwi / (AmE), / ɑː ˈpwi / (BrE)
a term used in some cultures, especially in Southeast Asia, to refer to a young woman, often affectionately or playfully, or to a younger sibling or friend
term for a young woman or younger sibling/friend

Examples

The ah pui at the party kept making everyone laugh with her jokes.
Her older brother always looks out for his little ah pui.
Among the group of friends, she's definitely the youngest ah pui.

Synset Examples

The ah pui at the party kept making funny faces.
She’s an ah pui who loves to take selfies.

Etymology

From Hokkien Chinese, where 'ah' is a term of address and 'pui' means 'girl' or 'younger woman'.
