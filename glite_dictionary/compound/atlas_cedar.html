Atlas cedar

1. NOUN

/ ˈætləs ˈsidɚ / (AmE), / ˈætləs ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a tall evergreen tree native to the Atlas Mountains of North Africa, with blue-green leaves, often planted as an ornamental
tall evergreen tree of the Atlas Mountains

Examples

The Atlas cedar is prized for its striking blue-green foliage in many landscape gardens.
Many parks in Europe feature mature Atlas cedar specimens lining their walkways.
Snow dusted the branches of the old Atlas cedar behind the villa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cedar - a type of large evergreen tree with fragrant wood
