temporary state

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpəˌrɛri steɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəri steɪt / (BrE)
a condition or situation that lasts for only a limited period of time
a condition lasting for a short time

Examples

Her happiness was just a temporary state caused by the good news.
The city was in a temporary state of emergency after the storm.
Fatigue is often a temporary state that passes with rest.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permanent state - a condition that lasts indefinitely or without significant change
