pinball machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnˌbɔl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈpɪnbɔːl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a large, coin-operated game apparatus in which a ball is shot onto a slanted playfield and kept in motion by flippers, bumpers, and various targets to score points
coin-operated ball game apparatus

Examples

He spent hours playing pinball machine games at the arcade.
The old diner still has a vintage pinball machine in the corner.
They restored a classic pinball machine as part of their game collection.

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a pinball machine a ‘pin table’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pinball - a game in which a ball scores points by hitting targets inside a machine
