gem of a restaurant

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛm əv ə ˈrɛstəˌrɑnt / (AmE), / dʒɛm əv ə ˈrɛstəˌrɒnt / (BrE)
a restaurant that is outstanding or delightful, often unexpectedly so or not widely known
outstanding or hidden restaurant

Examples

We stumbled upon a gem of a restaurant while exploring the old part of town.
That tiny cafe turned out to be a real gem of a restaurant with delicious homemade food.
Locals consider the bistro a gem of a restaurant that's often overlooked by tourists.

Synset Examples

She found a gem of a restaurant hidden in the alley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hidden gem - something excellent but not widely known or recognized

Etymology

Figurative use of 'gem' (precious stone) to refer to something excellent or highly valued.
