China fleece vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪnə fliːs vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnə fliːs vaɪn / (BrE)
a perennial climbing vine, native to western China and Russia, with twining stems and racemes of fragrant greenish flowers
a twining perennial vine from China and Russia

Examples

The China fleece vine quickly covered their garden fence with green foliage.
Gardeners prize the China fleece vine for its vigorous growth and fragrant blossoms.
A row of China fleece vine plants lined the walkway, providing shade in the summer.
