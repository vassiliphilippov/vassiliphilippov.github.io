quill pen

1. NOUN

/ kwɪl pɛn / (AmE), / kwɪl pɛn / (BrE)
A pen made from a bird's feather, traditionally used for writing before the invention of metal pens.
pen made from a feather

Examples

The scribe dipped his quill pen into the ink before writing the letter.
Artists sometimes use a quill pen for drawing fine lines in their work.
Before metal nibs became common, most people wrote with a quill pen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quill - a feather used as a writing tool
