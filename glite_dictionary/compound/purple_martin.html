purple martin

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.pəl ˈmɑr.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈmɑː.tɪn / (BrE)
a large North American swallow (Progne subis), of which the male is blue-black and the female is duller in color
large North American swallow species

Examples

A flock of purple martins swooped gracefully over the lake at dusk.
The male purple martin has distinct glossy blue-black plumage.
Gardeners build houses to attract purple martins because they help control insect populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swallow - a small migratory songbird with long pointed wings and a forked tail
