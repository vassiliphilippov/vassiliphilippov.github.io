body odor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌəʊdə / (BrE)
An unpleasant smell from the human body, often caused by perspiration and a lack of personal hygiene.
unpleasant smell from the body

Examples

She was self-conscious about her body odor after exercising at the gym.
Regular bathing and deodorant can help prevent body odor.
During the hot summer months, body odor can become more noticeable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodor - an unpleasant or offensive smell
  • Antonym: fragrance - a pleasant or sweet smell
