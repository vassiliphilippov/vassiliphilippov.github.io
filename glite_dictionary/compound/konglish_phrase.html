Konglish phrase

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːŋlɪʃ freɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋlɪʃ freɪz / (BrE)
a phrase composed of elements of both Korean and English, often used in informal contexts by Korean speakers
phrase mixing Korean and English

Examples

Many teenagers use Konglish phrases when chatting with friends online.
Her essay contained several Konglish phrases, making it difficult for non-Koreans to understand.
Teachers encourage students to avoid using Konglish phrases in formal English writing.

Synset Examples

They often mix Konglish phrases in casual conversations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Konglish - a hybrid form of language mixing Korean and English words

Etymology

Blend of 'Korean' and 'English'; 'Konglish' is based on the names of the two languages.
