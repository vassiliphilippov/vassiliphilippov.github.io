venae dorsales penis superficiales

1. NOUN

/ ˌviːnaɪ dɔːrˈseɪliːz ˈpiːnɪs sʊˌpɜːrˈfɪʃiəliːz / (AmE), / ˌviːniː dɔːˈseɪliːz ˈpiːnɪs sjuːˌpɜːˈfɪʃiəliːz / (BrE)
The superficial dorsal veins of the penis; paired veins on each side that drain into the external pudendal veins.
superficial dorsal veins of the penis
medical

Examples

During the surgery, the venae dorsales penis superficiales were carefully preserved to avoid excessive bleeding.
The anatomy class studied diagrams of the venae dorsales penis superficiales to understand venous drainage of the male genitalia.
In certain conditions, the venae dorsales penis superficiales may appear more prominent under the skin.

Etymology

From Latin: 'venae' (veins), 'dorsales' (dorsal), 'penis' (of the penis), 'superficiales' (superficial).
