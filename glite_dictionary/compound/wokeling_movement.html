wokeling movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈwoʊk.lɪŋ ˈmuːv.mənt / (AmE), / ˈwəʊk.lɪŋ ˈmuːv.mənt / (BrE)
A social movement, often used pejoratively, referring to people or groups advocating for progressive social justice causes, especially perceived as overly idealistic or performative.
(pejorative) progressive social justice movement
informal

Examples

Their organization joined the wokeling movement to campaign for climate justice.
Critics dismiss the reforms as just another victory for the wokeling movement.
Some politicians accused the wokeling movement of stifling free speech.

Synset Examples

The wokeling movement focuses on social justice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woke - aware of social and political injustice, especially racism

Etymology

Blend of 'woke' (aware of social injustice) and the diminutive suffix '-ling', suggesting a follower; movement from English.
