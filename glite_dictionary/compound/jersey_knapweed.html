Jersey knapweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜrzi ˈnæpˌwid / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːzi ˈnapwiːd / (BrE)
a variety of knapweed (Centaurea) found especially in Jersey, often grown as an ornamental plant for its purple flowers
a variety of knapweed plant

Examples

The Jersey knapweed adds a splash of vibrant purple to many island meadows.
Gardeners often choose Jersey knapweed for its ability to attract pollinators.
We spotted a cluster of Jersey knapweed growing wild along the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knapweed - a plant in the Centaurea genus
