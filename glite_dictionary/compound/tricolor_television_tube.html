tricolor television tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪˌkʌlər ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən tuːb / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌkʌlə ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tjuːb / (BrE)
a color television tube that uses three primary color elements to produce a full range of colors
television tube using three primary colors

Examples

The old living room set had a tricolor television tube that created vibrant images.
Engineers developed the tricolor television tube to improve color accuracy in early TVs.
Replacing a faulty tricolor television tube was once a common TV repair job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: color television tube - a television tube capable of displaying colors
