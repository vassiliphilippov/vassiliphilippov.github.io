rotating mechanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.teɪ.tɪŋ ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.teɪ.tɪŋ ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
a mechanical device or system designed to rotate or cause rotation
device designed to rotate

Examples

The engineer inspected the rotating mechanism to ensure it was functioning properly.
If the rotating mechanism jams, the machine could stop working entirely.
Modern wind turbines rely on a sophisticated rotating mechanism to convert wind into electricity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotation - the action of rotating around an axis
