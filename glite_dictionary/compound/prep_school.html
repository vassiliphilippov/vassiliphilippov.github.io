prep school

1. NOUN

/ prɛp skuːl / (AmE), / prɛp skuːl / (BrE)
a private school that prepares students for college or, in Britain, for public school
private preparatory school

Examples

She attended a prestigious prep school before enrolling at university.
Some parents choose to send their children to a prep school for a better education.
The prep school offers extracurricular activities like debate and rowing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preparatory school - a school that prepares students for further education

Etymology

Short for 'preparatory school'; 'prep' from 'preparatory'.
