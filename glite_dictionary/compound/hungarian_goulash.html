Hungarian goulash

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋˈɡɛriən ɡuˈlɑʃ / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛriən ˈɡuːlæʃ / (BrE)
a rich, hearty stew of meat and vegetables, highly seasoned with paprika, originating from Hungary
Hungarian paprika-seasoned meat stew

Examples

She prepared a traditional Hungarian goulash for the family dinner on Sunday.
The restaurant’s signature dish is a savory Hungarian goulash served with fresh bread.
On cold winter nights, nothing warms me up like a bowl of Hungarian goulash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goulash - a stew of meat and vegetables, seasoned with paprika

Etymology

Hungarian, from Hungarian 'gulyás' (herdsman or goulash).
