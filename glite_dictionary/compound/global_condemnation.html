global condemnation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊbl ˌkɑn.dəmˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.bəl ˌkɒn.dəmˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
widespread criticism or disapproval expressed by people or nations around the world.
worldwide criticism or disapproval

Examples

The company's actions drew global condemnation from governments, activists, and the media.
There was global condemnation after the news of the environmental disaster broke.
The invasion triggered immediate global condemnation and calls for sanctions.

Synset Examples

Apartheid South Africa faced global condemnation for its policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: universal condemnation - criticism or disapproval from everyone or everywhere
  • Antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
