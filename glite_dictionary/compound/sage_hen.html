sage hen

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪdʒ hɛn / (AmE), / ˈseɪdʒ hɛn / (BrE)
a large grouse found in the sagebrush regions of North America, also known as the greater sage-grouse
large grouse of western North America

Examples

We spotted a sage hen among the sagebrush during our hike.
The sage hen is known for its elaborate courtship dance every spring.
Populations of the sage hen have declined due to habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sage grouse - another name for sage hen (the bird)
