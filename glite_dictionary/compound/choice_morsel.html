choice morsel

1. NOUN

/ tʃɔɪs ˈmɔr.səl / (AmE), / tʃɔɪs ˈmɔː.səl / (BrE)
a small, particularly tasty piece of food, often considered desirable or rare
small, tasty piece of food

Examples

At the banquet, every guest was offered a choice morsel from the chef's special platter.
The puppy eagerly waited for a choice morsel to fall from the dinner table.
He savored the choice morsel, letting its rich flavor linger on his tongue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidbit - a small piece of tasty food; a delicacy
  • Synonym: delicacy - something pleasing to eat that is considered rare or luxurious
