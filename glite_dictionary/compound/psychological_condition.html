psychological condition

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪ.kəˈlɑ.dʒɪ.kəl kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.kəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a state of mental health or functioning that is relatively stable or characteristic, often referring to disorders, patterns, or features of the mind
state or disorder of mental health

Examples

Her therapist diagnosed her with a psychological condition that required ongoing treatment.
Living with a psychological condition can pose challenges, but support systems are available.
Researchers study the effects of stress on the development of psychological conditions in children.

Synset Examples

Stress isn't the only emotional or mental state that can benefit from regular meditation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental disorder - a disorder that affects mental functioning or behavior
