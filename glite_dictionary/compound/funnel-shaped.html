funnel-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌnəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈfʌnəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a funnel, wide at the top and narrowing towards the bottom.
shaped like a funnel

Examples

The jellyfish had a funnel-shaped body that helped it navigate through the water.
Rain poured down from the funnel-shaped clouds during the storm.
The scientist examined a funnel-shaped beaker for use in her experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conical - shaped like a cone
