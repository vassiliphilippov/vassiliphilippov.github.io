traffic circle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræfɪk ˌsɜːrkəl / (AmE), / ˈtræfɪk ˌsɜːkəl / (BrE)
A circular road junction where traffic moves around a central island, with vehicles entering and leaving at various points.
circular road junction

Examples

The town installed a new traffic circle to improve the flow of vehicles at the intersection.
Drivers must yield to vehicles already in the traffic circle before entering.
There was confusion among some motorists about how to navigate the traffic circle safely.

Synset Examples

the accident blocked all traffic at the rotary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundabout - circular road junction where traffic moves around a central island
