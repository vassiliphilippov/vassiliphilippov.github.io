cricket match

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪkɪt mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt matʃ / (BrE)
a game of cricket played between two teams
game between two cricket teams

Examples

The local club is organizing a cricket match this weekend.

Thousands of fans attended the international cricket match at Lord's.
