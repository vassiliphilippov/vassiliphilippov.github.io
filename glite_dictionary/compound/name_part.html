name part

1. NOUN

/ neɪm pɑrt / (AmE), / neɪm pɑːt / (BrE)
the role in a play of the character whose name appears in the title of the work
role named in the play's title

Examples

After years of playing supporting roles, she finally got the name part in the new production.
The audition for the name part attracted many aspiring actors.
Playing the name part of Hamlet is a great honor for any performer.
