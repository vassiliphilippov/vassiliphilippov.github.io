to do better than that

1. VERB

/ du ˈbɛtɚ ðæn ðæt / (AmE), / duː ˈbɛtə ðæn ðat / (BrE)
to perform at a higher standard or to surpass a previously demonstrated level of performance or quality
perform at a higher standard

Examples

If you want to win the competition, you’ll have to do better than that.
Her last essay was good, but I know she can do better than that.
The coach told the team they must do better than that in the next match.

Synset Examples

Come on, you can do better than that!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to outperform - to do better than someone else or another thing; to surpass
  • Antonym: to fall short - to fail to reach a required standard
