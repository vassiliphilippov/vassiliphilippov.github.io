too bad

1. ADJ

/ tu bæd / (AmE), / tuː bæd / (BrE)
used to express that something is unfortunate or deserving of regret.
expressing regret or misfortune
informal

Examples

It's too bad that you couldn't join us for the party.
The weather was too bad for us to go hiking yesterday.
It’s too bad she had to leave early and missed the concert.

Synset Examples

regrettable remarks
it's regrettable that she didn't go to college
it's too bad he had no feeling himself for church

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regrettable - deserving regret or causing disappointment
  • Synonym: unfortunate - causing or marked by bad luck; regrettable
