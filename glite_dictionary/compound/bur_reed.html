bur reed

1. NOUN

/ bɝ ˈrid / (AmE), / bɜː ˈriːd / (BrE)
a marsh plant with long, narrow leaves and round prickly fruit, especially of the genus Sparganium
marsh plant with prickly fruit

Examples

We spotted a bur reed growing along the edge of the pond.
The spiky fruit of the bur reed can sometimes cling to animal fur.
Restoring wetlands often involves replanting native species like bur reed.
