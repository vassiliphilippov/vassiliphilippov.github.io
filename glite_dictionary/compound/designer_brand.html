designer brand

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnər brænd / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnə brænd / (BrE)
a brand known for high-fashion clothing and accessories created by prominent designers
brand known for high-fashion clothing

Examples

Designer brands often launch limited-edition collections that sell out quickly.
Many celebrities endorse designer brands at red carpet events.
