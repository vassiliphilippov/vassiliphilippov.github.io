smorgasbord of

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɔrɡəsˌbɔrd əv / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːɡəsˌbɔːd ɒv / (BrE)
a wide and diverse assortment or selection of things
wide assortment or selection

Examples

The festival provided a smorgasbord of entertainment options for both children and adults.
Her library shelves held a smorgasbord of novels, from mysteries to science fiction.
The symposium featured a smorgasbord of perspectives from international experts.

Synset Examples

The buffet offered a smorgasbord of international dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - the quality or state of being different or diverse; an assortment
  • Synonym: selection - a range of things from which a choice can be made

Etymology

From Swedish 'smörgåsbord' (sandwich table), adopted into English to describe a buffet and figuratively extended to wide variety.
