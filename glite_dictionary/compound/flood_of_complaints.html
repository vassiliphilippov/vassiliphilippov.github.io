flood of complaints

1. NOUN

/ flʌd əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (AmE), / flʌd əv kəmˈpleɪnts / (BrE)
a very large number of complaints occurring at the same time; an overwhelming volume of expressions of dissatisfaction
very large number of complaints

Examples

After the announcement, the company received a flood of complaints from disappointed customers.
The faulty product release triggered a flood of complaints to the support team.
The mayor promised action after facing a flood of complaints about the new traffic rules.

Synset Examples

The new policy could send the flood of complaints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: torrent of complaints - a sudden, violent outpouring of complaints
