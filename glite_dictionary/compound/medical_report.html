medical report

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
an official document detailing the results of a medical examination of a patient
written account of a medical examination

Examples

The doctor handed me the medical report after the examination was complete.
Her lawyer requested a copy of the medical report to support her case.
The medical report showed no signs of abnormality in his blood tests.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: medical record - a complete record of a patient's medical history and care
