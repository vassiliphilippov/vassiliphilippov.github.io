far point

1. NOUN

/ fɑr pɔɪnt / (AmE), / fɑː pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the most distant point or final destination reached during a journey or activity
most distant point in a journey

Examples

We stopped for lunch at the far point of our cycling route.
The view from the far point of the peninsula made the long walk worthwhile.
Reaching the far point felt like a real achievement after hours of hiking.

Synset Examples

We reached the far point just before sunset.
The far point in our journey was breathtaking.
The far point of the hike offers stunning views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destination - the place to which someone is going or sent
