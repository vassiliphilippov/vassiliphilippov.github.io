sieva bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːvə biːn / (AmE), / ˈsiːvə biːn / (BrE)
A small, flat green bean similar to the lima bean, often grown for its edible seeds.
a small flat green bean, like a lima bean

Examples

She added some sieva beans to the vegetable stew for extra flavor.
The farmers grew both lima beans and sieva beans in their fields.
You can find sieva beans in specialty grocery stores during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - a type of edible bean with flat, pale green seeds

2. NOUN

/ ˈsiːvə ˌbiːn / (AmE), / ˈsiːvə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A variety of bush bean plant cultivated especially in the southern United States, known for its small, flat, edible seeds.
a type of bush bean with flat edible seeds

Examples

The farmer planted a field of sieva beans to sell at the local market.
I love adding cooked sieva beans to my summer salads for extra protein.
Sieva beans are especially popular in southern United States cuisine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - an edible flat bean, the seed of a tropical American plant
