tea table

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiː ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a small table used for serving tea, especially during afternoon tea
small table for serving tea

Examples

The antique tea table in the corner was beautifully decorated with a lace cloth and fine china.
She set out scones and sandwiches on the tea table for her guests to enjoy.
They gathered around the tea table in the sunroom for a relaxing afternoon chat.
