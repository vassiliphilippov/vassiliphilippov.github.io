moving staircase

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈster.keɪs / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈsteə.keɪs / (BrE)
A stairway with steps that move continuously on a circulating belt, used for transporting people between floors.
mechanized stairway with moving steps

Examples

The shopping mall features a moving staircase to help customers travel between levels.
He preferred using the moving staircase instead of climbing the stairs after a long day.
A moving staircase can transport large groups of people efficiently in busy places.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escalator - a moving staircase for carrying people between floors
