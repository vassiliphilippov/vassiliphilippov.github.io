royal coronation

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪ.əl ˌkɔː.rəˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl ˌkɒ.rəˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the formal ceremony in which a monarch is officially crowned and invested with regalia
ceremony of crowning a monarch

Examples

The royal coronation attracted thousands of spectators from all over the world.
Plans for the royal coronation have been underway for months, with elaborate decorations throughout the city.
The cathedral was chosen as the venue for the royal coronation, continuing a centuries-old tradition.

Synset Examples

She wore a beautiful gown for the royal coronation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coronation - the act or ceremony of crowning a sovereign
