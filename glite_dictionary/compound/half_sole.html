half sole

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf soʊl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf səʊl / (BrE)
a replacement sole for a shoe that covers only the front part, from the shank to the toe
front part of a shoe sole

Examples

The cobbler glued a new half sole onto my worn leather shoes.
Many people choose a half sole repair to extend the life of their favorite footwear.
After years of wear, the half sole had become thin and needed replacement.
