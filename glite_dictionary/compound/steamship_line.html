steamship line

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːmʃɪp laɪn / (AmE), / ˈstiːmʃɪp laɪn / (BrE)
A company or organization that operates a fleet of steamships for commercial transportation of passengers or cargo.
company operating steamships

Examples

The steamship line announced a new route between New York and London.
He worked for a major steamship line in the early twentieth century.
Several steamship lines competed fiercely for transatlantic business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping line - a company that operates ships for transporting goods or passengers
