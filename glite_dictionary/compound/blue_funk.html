blue funk

1. NOUN

/ bluː fʌŋk / (AmE), / bluː fʌŋk / (BrE)
a state of nervous depression or deep sadness, often accompanied by anxiety or fear
state of nervous depression or sadness

Examples

After losing his job, Tom fell into a blue funk and barely left the house for weeks.
The rainy weather left me in a blue funk all weekend long.
She was in such a blue funk that even her favorite movie couldn't cheer her up.

Synset Examples

he was in a funk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depression - a mental state of persistent sadness and low mood
  • Synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
