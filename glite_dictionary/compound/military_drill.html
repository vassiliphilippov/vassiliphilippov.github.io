military drill

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri drɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri drɪl / (BrE)
systematic training in marching, maneuvering, and the use of weapons, especially as part of preparation for military service.
training in marching and use of weapons

Examples

The recruits spent hours every day practicing military drill under the hot sun.
Discipline and attention to detail are enforced through strict military drill.
She watched the regiment perform a complex military drill during the parade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - training exercise, especially military
