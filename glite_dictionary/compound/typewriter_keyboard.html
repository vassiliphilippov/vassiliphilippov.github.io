typewriter keyboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪtər ˈkiːˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtə ˈkiːˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a keyboard on a typewriter for manually entering characters to print them on paper
keyboard of a typewriter

Examples

She admired the antique typewriter keyboard with its round, raised keys.
The layout of a typewriter keyboard influenced the design of early computer keyboards.
Dust had gathered between the keys of the old typewriter keyboard in the attic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard - a set of keys on a device for inputting data
  • Synonym: typewriter - a machine with keys for typing characters onto paper
