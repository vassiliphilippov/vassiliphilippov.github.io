phone bill

1. NOUN

/ foʊn bɪl / (AmE), / fəʊn bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the charges for telephone service over a period of time
statement of telephone charges

Examples

My phone bill was much higher than expected this month.
She checked her phone bill to see if there were any international charges.
Please pay the phone bill before the due date to avoid service interruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility bill - a bill for public services such as electricity, water, or gas
