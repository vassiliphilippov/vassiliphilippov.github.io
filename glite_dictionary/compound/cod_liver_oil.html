cod liver oil

1. NOUN

/ kɑd ˈlɪvɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / kɒd ˈlɪvə ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil extracted from the livers of cod and similar fish, used as a dietary supplement for its vitamins A and D
oil from cod fish liver, dietary supplement

Examples

Many parents gave their children cod liver oil daily to help boost their immune systems.
Some people dislike the taste of cod liver oil, but take it for its health benefits.
Modern supplements often use cod liver oil as a natural source of vitamin D.
