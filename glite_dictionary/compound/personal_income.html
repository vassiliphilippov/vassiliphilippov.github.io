personal income

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrsənl ˈɪnkʌm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈɪnkʌm / (BrE)
the total income received by an individual, including wages, investments, and other sources, before taxes are deducted
an individual's total income before taxes

Examples

Your personal income determines how much you must pay in taxes each year.
After losing his job, his personal income dropped significantly.
They used their personal income to invest in real estate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: household income - total income received by all members of a household
