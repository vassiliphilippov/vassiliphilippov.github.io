love ya

1. X

/ lʌv jə / (AmE), / lʌv jə / (BrE)
An informal way of expressing affection or fondness toward someone, often in casual speech or text messages.
informal expression of affection
informal

Examples

She ended the call with a cheerful 'love ya,' making him smile.
At the end of every conversation, they say 'love ya' to each other.
He sent a quick message: 'love ya, see you soon!'

Synset Examples

Their quick text ended with a simple, 'Love ya!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love you - expressing love to someone
