teacup and saucer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌkʌp ənd ˈsɔsɚ / (AmE), / ˈtiːkʌp ənd ˈsɔːsə / (BrE)
a matching set consisting of a small cup for tea and the dish (saucer) that goes with it
cup and dish set for tea

Examples

The antique shop displayed a beautiful teacup and saucer set in the window.
She carefully carried the steaming teacup and saucer to her guest.
After breaking the teacup and saucer, John apologized and cleaned up the mess.

Synset Examples

She served tea in a delicate teacup and saucer.
