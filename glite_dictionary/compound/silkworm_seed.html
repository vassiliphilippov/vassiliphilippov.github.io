silkworm seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlkˌwɜrm siːd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkwɜːm siːd / (BrE)
the eggs of a silkworm, especially as used in silk production
eggs of a silkworm

Examples

Farmers store silkworm seed carefully to ensure a healthy silk harvest.
The quality of the silkworm seed greatly affects the production of silk.
She ordered fresh silkworm seed from a reliable supplier for the new season.
