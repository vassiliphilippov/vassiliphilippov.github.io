tropic bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑpɪk bɝd / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk bɜːd / (BrE)
a mostly white, web-footed tropical seabird, typically found far from land, known for its long tail feathers
tropical seabird with long tail feathers

Examples

We spotted a tropic bird soaring above the open ocean during our boat trip.
The distinctive tail streamers of the tropic bird make it easy to identify.
Many photographers travel to remote islands just to capture images of the elusive tropic bird.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seabird - a bird that lives mainly at sea
