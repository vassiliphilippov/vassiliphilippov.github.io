piece of the action

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ðə ˈækʃən / (AmE), / piːs əv ði ˈækʃən / (BrE)
a share in an activity, especially one that may lead to profit or excitement
share in an opportunity or activity
informal

Examples

Once the new business started making money, everyone wanted a piece of the action.
She heard about the investment opportunity and asked how she could get a piece of the action.
If you're planning something exciting, count me in—I want a piece of the action!

Synset Examples

He made sure to get a piece of the action.
Everyone wants a piece of the action now.
I want a piece of the action in this project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: share - a part of something that has been divided between several people
  • Synonym: cut - a share or portion of something, especially money
