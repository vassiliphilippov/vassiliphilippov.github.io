Arafura Sea

1. PROPN

/ ˌærəˈfjʊrə si / (AmE), / ˌarafˈjʊərə siː / (BrE)
a marginal sea of the western Pacific Ocean, located between northern Australia and southern New Guinea
sea between Australia and New Guinea

Examples

Fishing is an important industry in the Arafura Sea due to its abundant marine life.
The Arafura Sea connects to the Timor Sea and the Coral Sea through various straits.
The storm forced the ship to change course across the Arafura Sea.
