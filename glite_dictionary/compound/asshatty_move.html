asshatty move

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsˌhæti muːv / (AmE), / ˈasˌhæti muːv / (BrE)
an action that is foolish, inconsiderate, or irritating, typically provoking annoyance or disapproval
foolish or inconsiderate action (slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

That was an asshatty move on his part.
Pulling out in front of traffic like that was a really asshatty move.
She apologized for her asshatty move at the meeting.

Synset Examples

That was such an asshatty move on his part.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asshat - a foolish or irritating person (slang)
  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or stupid mistake

Etymology

From 'asshat' (slang for a foolish person) + '-y' (adjectival suffix) + 'move'.
