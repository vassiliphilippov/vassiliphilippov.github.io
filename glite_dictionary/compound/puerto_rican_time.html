Puerto Rican time

1. NOUN

/ ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkən taɪm / (AmE), / ˌpwɜːtəʊ ˈriːkən taɪm / (BrE)
the tendency for events or people to run later than scheduled, reflecting a relaxed attitude to punctuality, especially in Puerto Rican culture
relaxed approach to punctuality (Puerto Rican culture)

Examples

Don't worry if they're not here yet—it's just Puerto Rican time.
The meeting started much later than scheduled, in true Puerto Rican time.
She isn't actually late; she's just operating on Puerto Rican time.

Synset Examples
