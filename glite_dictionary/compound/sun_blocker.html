sun blocker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌblɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌblɒkə / (BrE)
a cream or lotion applied to the skin to filter out ultraviolet light and protect against sunburn
cream or lotion for sun protection

Examples

Before heading to the beach, she applied sun blocker to her arms and face.
Make sure you use a sun blocker with a high SPF for maximum protection.
Many parents choose a gentle sun blocker for their children during outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunscreen - cream or lotion protecting skin from the sun
  • Synonym: sunblock - substance protecting skin from UV rays
