halter-sack style

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔltər sæk staɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltə sæk staɪl / (BrE)
A casual clothing style characterized by a halter neckline and a loose, sack-like fit, often used for dresses or tops.
loose halter-neck casual clothing style

Examples

The halter-sack style is popular at beach parties for its comfort and ease of movement.
She bought a new dress in the halter-sack style for her summer vacation.
Designers often recommend the halter-sack style for its flattering fit on many body types.

Synset Examples

The halter-sack style is popular at beach parties.

Etymology

From 'halter', referring to a strap around the neck, and 'sack', meaning a loose-fitting garment.
