rocket launcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːkɪt ˌlɔːntʃər / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˌlɔːntʃə / (BrE)
a device or weapon designed to launch rockets, typically used in military operations.
device or weapon for launching rockets

Examples

The soldiers prepared the rocket launcher before the start of the operation.
A new portable rocket launcher has been developed for use by infantry units.
He demonstrated how to operate the rocket launcher safely during the training session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missile launcher - a device designed to launch missiles
