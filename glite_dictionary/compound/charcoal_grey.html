charcoal grey

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːrˌkoʊl ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a very dark shade of grey, resembling the color of charcoal
very dark grey color

Examples

She chose a charcoal grey suit for the interview to convey professionalism.
The walls of the living room were painted in a sophisticated charcoal grey.
His car is a sleek charcoal grey that never seems to go out of style.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - a color intermediate between black and white
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, completely without light
