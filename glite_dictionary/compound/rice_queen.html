rice queen

1. NOUN

/ raɪs kwiːn / (AmE), / raɪs kwiːn / (BrE)
A woman who is honored as a leading figure in rice farming, often at agricultural fairs or community events.
title for a prominent female in rice farming

Examples

She was crowned the rice queen at the annual agricultural festival.
The rice queen gave a heartfelt speech about supporting local farmers.
Winning the title of rice queen was a great honor for her and her family.
