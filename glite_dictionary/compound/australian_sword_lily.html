Australian sword lily

1. NOUN

/ əˌstreɪljən ˈsɔrd ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪlɪən ˈsɔːd ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A sedgelike, spring-flowering herb native to Australia, characterized by clustered flowers covered with woolly hairs.
Australian flowering herb with woolly flowers

Examples

The Australian sword lily blooms in early spring, adding a splash of color to native gardens.
Botanists are studying the unique woolly flowers of the Australian sword lily.
The Australian sword lily thrives in sandy soils and open woodlands across Australia.
