face pack

1. NOUN

/ feɪs pæk / (AmE), / feɪs pæk / (BrE)
A cosmetic cream or mask applied to the face to cleanse, tone, or beautify the skin.
cosmetic mask for cleansing the face

Examples

She applied a face pack to refresh her skin after a long day.
This natural face pack helps to reduce oiliness and prevent acne.
Many spas include a herbal face pack as part of their beauty treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facial mask - a mask or cream applied to the face for cosmetic purposes
