family Meliaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli mɛliˈeɪsiˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mɛlɪˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
A family of tropical trees and shrubs, many of which are valued for their timber and ornamental uses.
tropical trees and shrubs family

Examples

Several valuable timber species belong to the family Meliaceae.
Mahogany is one of the most well-known members of the family Meliaceae.
Botanists are studying disease resistance in the family Meliaceae.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Meliaceae', based on the genus 'Melia' and the family suffix '-aceae'.
