quiz program

1. NOUN

/ kwɪz ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / kwɪz ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a television or radio show in which contestants compete by answering questions, often for prizes
broadcast game show involving questions and answers

Examples

She appeared as a contestant on a popular quiz program last night.
Many quiz programs attract viewers with large cash prizes for the winners.
The quiz program tested both general knowledge and quick thinking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game show - a television or radio show in which people play games or answer questions to win prizes
