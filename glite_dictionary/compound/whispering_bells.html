whispering bells

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪspərɪŋ bɛlz / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərɪŋ bɛlz / (BrE)
a sticky herb native to arid or desert areas of the southwestern United States, with drooping yellow flowers
desert plant with drooping yellow flowers

Examples

Whispering bells are often found blooming after winter rains in the deserts of California.
The delicate yellow flowers of whispering bells sway gently in the breeze.
