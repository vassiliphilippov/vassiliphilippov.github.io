golf stroke

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf stroʊk / (AmE), / ɡɒlf strəʊk / (BrE)
the act of swinging a golf club with the intention of hitting a golf ball
swing of a golf club to hit the ball

Examples

She improved her golf stroke after taking a few lessons from a professional coach.
Analyzing the mechanics of your golf stroke can help lower your score.
He practiced his golf stroke for hours on the driving range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroke - a single movement of a tool, weapon, or part of the body
