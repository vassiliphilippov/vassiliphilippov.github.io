long history

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
a significant or notable past or tradition, often extending over a considerable period of time
a significant or notable past

Examples

The company has a long history of innovation in the technology sector.
There is a long history of rivalry between the two schools.
Our town has a long history of celebrating cultural diversity.

Synset Examples

Her family has a long history of serving in the fauj.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heritage - something inherited from the past; tradition
  • Synonym: tradition - customs or beliefs passed down through generations
