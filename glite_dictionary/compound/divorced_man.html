divorced man

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɔrst mæn / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːst mæn / (BrE)
a man who is no longer married due to legal divorce from his wife
man no longer married due to divorce

Examples

After years of marriage, he became a divorced man and started a new chapter in his life.
The support group was specifically for divorced men looking to rebuild their confidence.
As a divorced man, he enjoyed spending time with his children on weekends.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: divorced woman - a woman who is no longer married due to divorce
