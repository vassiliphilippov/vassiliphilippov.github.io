compact-disk burner

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑmˌpækt ˈdɪsk ˈbɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ˌkɒmˌpækt ˈdɪsk ˈbɜːnə / (BrE)
A device used for recording data or music onto compact disks (CDs).
device for recording CDs

Examples

He bought a new compact-disk burner to make backup copies of his music collection.
This computer comes with a built-in compact-disk burner for creating your own audio CDs.
Many offices no longer use a compact-disk burner since most data is transferred digitally now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CD writer - device for recording data onto compact disks
  • Degree Lower: compact disk - a small optical disk on which data is digitally recorded
