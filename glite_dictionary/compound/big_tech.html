Big Tech

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ tɛk / (AmE), / bɪɡ tɛk / (BrE)
The largest and most influential technology companies, especially those dominating the internet, digital services, and social media sectors.
the largest technology companies

Examples

Big Tech faces increased government scrutiny over its data collection practices.
Many worry about the influence Big Tech has on public debate and democracy.
Several startups are challenging the dominance of Big Tech in the market.

Synset Examples

Big Tech companies are shaping our future.
Concerns about privacy surround Big Tech firms.
Regulations are tightening around Big Tech industries.

Etymology

Formed from 'big' and 'tech', shorthand for 'technology', referring collectively to dominant technology firms.
