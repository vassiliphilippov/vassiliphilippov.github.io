small hours

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ˈaʊɚz / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈaʊəz / (BrE)
The early hours of the morning, soon after midnight and before dawn.
hours just after midnight

Examples

She often worked into the small hours to meet her deadlines.
The party lasted well into the small hours of the morning.
He finally got to bed in the small hours after finishing his assignment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wee hours - very early hours of the morning, just after midnight
