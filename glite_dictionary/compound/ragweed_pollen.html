ragweed pollen

1. NOUN

/ ˈræɡˌwid ˈpoʊlən / (AmE), / ˈræɡwiːd ˈpɒlən / (BrE)
pollen produced by the ragweed plant, known for causing allergic reactions in many people
pollen from ragweed plant; common allergen
medical

Examples

Many people experience sneezing and itchy eyes during ragweed pollen season.
Tests showed that she was highly allergic to ragweed pollen.
Levels of ragweed pollen are highest in late summer and early autumn.
