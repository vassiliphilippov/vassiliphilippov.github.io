Yellowstone National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌstoʊn ˈnæʃənəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊstəʊn ˈnæʃənəl pɑːk / (BrE)
a large national park in the western United States, famous for its geysers, wildlife, and scenic landscapes; the first national park established in the U.S.
U.S. national park known for geysers and wildlife

Examples

We planned our vacation to include a visit to Yellowstone National Park this summer.
The geysers and hot springs of Yellowstone National Park attract millions of tourists each year.
Bison and grizzly bears roam freely across the vast landscapes of Yellowstone National Park.
