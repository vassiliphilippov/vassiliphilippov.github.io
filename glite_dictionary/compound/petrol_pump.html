petrol pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtɹəl pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈpɛt.rəl pʌmp / (BrE)
a device at a service station for pumping gasoline or petrol from underground tanks into vehicles
fuel dispenser at a service station
british

Examples

The attendant filled the car's tank at the petrol pump.
During the fuel shortage, several petrol pumps were closed across the city.
He accidentally drove away with the nozzle still attached to the petrol pump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas pump - a device for pumping gasoline; chiefly American term
