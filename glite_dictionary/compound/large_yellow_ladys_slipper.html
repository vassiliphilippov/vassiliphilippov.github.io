large yellow lady's slipper

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈjɛloʊ ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈjɛləʊ ˈleɪdɪz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
A plant native to eastern and central North America, with slightly fragrant greenish-yellow flowers marked with purple.
North American orchid with yellow, purple-marked flowers

Examples

The large yellow lady's slipper is found blooming in woodlands during late spring.
Gardeners prize the large yellow lady's slipper for its striking, colorful blossoms.
