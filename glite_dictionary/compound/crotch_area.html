crotch area

1. NOUN

/ krɑtʃ ˈɛriə / (AmE), / krɒtʃ ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the region around the groin, especially where the legs join the torso
region around the groin

Examples

The jeans were uncomfortable because the crotch area was too tight.
She applied powder to prevent chafing in the crotch area during her hike.
The doctor examined the crotch area for signs of irritation.

Synset Examples

The pants were too tight in the crotch area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groin - the area between the abdomen and thigh
  • Synonym: crotch - the area where the legs join the body
