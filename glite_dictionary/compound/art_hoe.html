art hoe

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt hoʊ / (AmE), / ɑːt həʊ / (BrE)
(slang) A person, typically young, who passionately engages in and embraces art and creativity, often associated with a distinct, artistic social media aesthetic.
person with strong passion for art and creativity
slang

Examples

She proudly calls herself an art hoe and spends weekends in galleries or painting in the park.
Many art hoes share their creative projects and aesthetic outfits on Instagram and Tumblr.
He decorated his room with vintage posters and sketches, embracing the art hoe lifestyle.
