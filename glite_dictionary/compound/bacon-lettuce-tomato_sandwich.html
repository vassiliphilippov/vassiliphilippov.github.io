bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.kən ˈlɛ.tɪs təˈmeɪ.toʊ ˈsæn.wɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.kən ˈlɛ.tɪs təˈmɑː.təʊ ˈsæn.wɪdʒ / (BrE)
A sandwich made with slices of bacon, fresh lettuce, and tomato, usually served between slices of toasted bread and often with mayonnaise.
sandwich with bacon, lettuce, and tomato

Examples

I ordered a bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich with extra mayonnaise for lunch.
The café is famous for their delicious bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich on sourdough bread.
She made a quick bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich before heading to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: BLT - abbreviation for bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich
