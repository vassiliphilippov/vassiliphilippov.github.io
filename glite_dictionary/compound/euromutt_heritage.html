Euromutt heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʊəroʊˌmʌt ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌmʌt ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
The condition or fact of being of mixed heritage from various European dog breeds.
mixed European dog breed heritage
informal

Examples

Thanks to his Euromutt heritage, the puppy has a unique look and temperament.
The shelter specializes in dogs with Euromutt heritage that need loving homes.
Her Euromutt heritage is obvious in her diverse coat colors and playful personality.

Synset Examples

His Euromutt heritage makes family gatherings quite interesting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutt - a dog of mixed breed
  • Antonym: purebred - a dog descended from ancestors of the same breed; not mixed

Etymology

Blend of 'Euro-' (referring to Europe) and 'mutt' (slang for a mixed-breed dog), plus 'heritage.'
