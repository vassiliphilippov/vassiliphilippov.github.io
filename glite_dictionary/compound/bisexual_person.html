bisexual person

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈsɛkʃuəl ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / baɪˈsɛkʃuəl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is sexually attracted to both men and women
person attracted to both sexes

Examples

As a bisexual person, Alex feels comfortable in both LGBTQ and heterosexual communities.
The film featured a bisexual person as the main character, which was praised for its representation.
Many celebrities have openly identified as a bisexual person in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homosexual person - a person who is sexually attracted to people of their own sex
  • Antonym: heterosexual person - a person who is attracted to people of the opposite sex

Etymology

From 'bisexual' (from Latin 'bi-' meaning 'two' and 'sexual') + 'person'.
