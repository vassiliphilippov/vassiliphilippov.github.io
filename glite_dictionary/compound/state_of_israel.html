State of Israel

1. PROPN

/ steɪt əv ˈɪzrəɪl / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˈɪzreɪl / (BrE)
The official name for the country of Israel, a Jewish republic in southwestern Asia at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea, established in 1948.
official name for the country of Israel

Examples

The State of Israel was established as an independent nation in 1948.
Many countries maintain diplomatic relations with the State of Israel.
Hundreds of thousands of tourists visit the State of Israel each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Israel - country in the Middle East on the Mediterranean Sea
  • Antonym: Palestine - region in the Middle East including Israel and its surrounding areas
