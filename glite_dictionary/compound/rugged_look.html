rugged look

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡɪd lʊk / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡɪd lʊk / (BrE)
a rough, strong, and attractive appearance, especially in a man's face or style
rough yet attractive appearance

Examples

With his stubble and weathered features, he has a rugged look that many find appealing.
The actor's rugged look set him apart from the other leading men in Hollywood.
Despite his rugged look, he was known for his gentle personality.

Synset Examples

He has a strong jaw that gives him a rugged look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handsome - good-looking, especially in a masculine way
