picture rail

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃər reɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə reɪl / (BrE)
a narrow horizontal rail fixed to a wall, from which pictures can be hung
horizontal rail for hanging pictures on a wall

Examples

The paintings hung from the picture rail, giving the room a classic look.
He used wire to suspend the frame from the picture rail in the hallway.
Victorian homes often feature a picture rail just below the ceiling.
