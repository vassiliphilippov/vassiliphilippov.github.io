cancer weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsɚ wiːd / (AmE), / ˈkænsə wiːd / (BrE)
A common name for a sage native to the eastern United States, known for its hairy leaves and small purple flowers.
North American sage plant

Examples

The field was covered in cancer weed, giving it a soft purple hue.
Some gardeners find cancer weed difficult to remove from lawns.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of cancer weed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sage - an aromatic herb used in cooking and medicine
