deceased loved one

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsist lʌvd wʌn / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːst lʌvd wʌn / (BrE)
a person, such as a relative or friend, who has died
a person who has died (fondly remembered)

Examples

The entire family attended the memorial service for their deceased loved one.
She placed flowers on the grave of her deceased loved one every year.
Counseling can help people cope with the loss of a deceased loved one.

Synset Examples

The family gathered to honor their deceased loved one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deceased - a person who has died
