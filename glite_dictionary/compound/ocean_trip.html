ocean trip

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃən trɪp / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən trɪp / (BrE)
a journey made by traveling across the ocean, typically by ship or boat
journey across the ocean

Examples

She dreamed of taking an ocean trip to distant lands.
The ocean trip lasted nearly a month before they reached their destination.
After saving for years, they finally booked an ocean trip together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voyage - a long journey involving travel by sea or in space
  • Synonym: cruise - a journey on a ship for pleasure
