yew family

1. NOUN

/ ju ˈfæməli / (AmE), / juː ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of coniferous trees and shrubs, Taxaceae, which includes the yew and related plants
family of coniferous plants, Taxaceae

Examples

The yew family contains trees and shrubs well known for their toxic seeds.
Plants belonging to the yew family are often used in ornamental gardens.
Taxol, a cancer-fighting drug, is derived from members of the yew family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taxaceae - the botanical name for the yew family
