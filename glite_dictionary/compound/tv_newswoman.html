TV newswoman

1. NOUN

/ tiː viː ˈnuːzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / tiː viː ˈnjuːzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who presents or reports news stories on television.
female television news reporter or anchor

Examples

After years of hard work, she became the leading TV newswoman in her city.
Many young girls looked up to the well-respected TV newswoman for inspiration.
The TV newswoman delivered the breaking story with clarity and poise.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: TV newsman - a man who reports news stories via television
  • Synonym: newsreader - person who reads the news on television or radio
