to weigh the costs against the benefits

1. VERB

/ weɪ ðə kɑsts əˈgɛnst ðə ˈbɛnəfɪts / (AmE), / weɪ ðə kɒsts əˈɡɛnst ðə ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (BrE)
to carefully compare the disadvantages and advantages of something before making a decision
evaluate disadvantages and advantages

Examples

Before choosing a new health insurance plan, you should weigh the costs against the benefits to find the best option.
The committee spent hours trying to weigh the costs against the benefits of the proposed merger.
It is important to weigh the costs against the benefits when making large purchases.

Synset Examples

Weigh the costs against the benefits of the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to weigh up - to consider and compare carefully
  • Synonym: to consider - to think about carefully before making a decision
