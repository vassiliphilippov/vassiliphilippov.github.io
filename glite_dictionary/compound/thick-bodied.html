thick-bodied

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɪkˌbɑdid / (AmE), / ˈθɪkˌbɒdid / (BrE)
Having a thick or stout body or form, especially in reference to animals or people.
having a thick or stout body

Examples

The thick-bodied snake moved slowly across the sunny path.
She sketched a thick-bodied fish with exaggerated features for her art project.
Many beetles are thick-bodied compared to other types of insects.
