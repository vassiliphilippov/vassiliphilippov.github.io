to the rhythm of

1. ADP

/ tə ðə ˈɹɪðəm ʌv / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈɹɪðəm ɒv / (BrE)
in accordance with the beat, tempo, or pattern of something, especially music
in accordance with the beat or tempo

Examples

She danced to the rhythm of the jazz music all night.
The children marched to the rhythm of the drumbeats during the parade.
Leaves swayed gently to the rhythm of the wind.

Synset Examples

She danced to the rhythm of Gary Glitter's songs.
