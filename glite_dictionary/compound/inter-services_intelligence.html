Inter-Services Intelligence

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪntər ˈsɜːrvɪsɪz ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / ˌɪntə ˈsɜːvɪsɪz ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns / (BrE)
the principal intelligence agency of Pakistan, influential in political and military affairs, and known for its activities both domestically and internationally.
Pakistan's main intelligence agency

Examples

The Inter-Services Intelligence has been the subject of controversy in international relations.
Experts believe that the Inter-Services Intelligence plays a key role in shaping Pakistan's foreign policy.
The acronym ISI is often used to refer to the Inter-Services Intelligence agency in the media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CIA - the Central Intelligence Agency of the United States
  • Synonym: MI6 - the Secret Intelligence Service of the United Kingdom

Etymology

Formed from 'inter-' (between), 'services' (as in military branches), and 'intelligence' (information gathering).
