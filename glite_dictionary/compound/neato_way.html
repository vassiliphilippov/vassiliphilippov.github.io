neato way

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːtoʊ weɪ / (AmE), / ˈniːtəʊ weɪ / (BrE)
an informal, enthusiastic term for a cool or clever method or approach to doing something
informal way to say a cool or clever method
informal american

Examples

She showed us a neato way to organize all our cables.
That’s a neato way of remembering everyone’s birthday!
I learned a neato way to fold shirts from a video online.

Synset Examples

What a neato way to solve that problem!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cool way - an impressive or admirable method

Etymology

From 'neato', an American informal variant of 'neat' meaning 'excellent' or 'cool', combined with 'way'.
