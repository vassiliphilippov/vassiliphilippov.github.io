Aster shortii

1. NOUN

/ ˈæstər ˈʃɔrti / (AmE), / ˈæstə ˈʃɔːti / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, typically bearing blue flowers.
a perennial plant with blue flowers, native to southeastern US

Examples

The garden was enhanced by the addition of several Aster shortii plants in bloom.
Wild populations of Aster shortii can still be found in protected forest areas.
Botanists study Aster shortii for its ecological role in native habitats.

Etymology

Named after Dr. Charles Wilkins Short, an American botanist (1794–1863).
