on the face of

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə feɪs ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə feɪs ɒv / (BrE)
judging only by what is immediately obvious or apparent, without considering deeper factors
as it appears at first glance

Examples

On the face of it, the proposal seems reasonable, but there are hidden problems.
Many claims look true on the face of them but fall apart under scrutiny.
On the face of his record, he's a strong candidate for promotion.

Synset Examples

On the face of the report, everything looks alright.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at first glance - on initial consideration or appearance
