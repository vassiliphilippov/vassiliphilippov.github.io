body stocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌstɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌstɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
a one-piece, tight-fitting undergarment for women that covers the torso and sometimes the arms and legs
tight-fitting one-piece undergarment

Examples

She wore a sheer body stocking under her dress for extra comfort.
The dancer chose a flesh-colored body stocking for the performance.
Many costumes use a body stocking as their base layer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leotard - a close-fitting one-piece garment covering the torso but not the legs or arms
