ring-necked parakeet

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋˌnɛkt ˈpæɹəˌkit / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛkt ˈparəkiːt / (BrE)
a large green parakeet native to Africa and South Asia, with a distinctive ring around its neck
species of green parakeet with a neck ring

Examples

A flock of ring-necked parakeets was spotted in the city park early this morning.
The ring-necked parakeet is known for its bright green feathers and distinctive neck ring.
Many people enjoy keeping the ring-necked parakeet as a pet because of its playful nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - a small to medium-sized parrot, usually with a long tail
