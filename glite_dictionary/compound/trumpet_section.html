trumpet section

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the group of musicians in a band or orchestra who play trumpets or cornets
group of trumpet or cornet players in an ensemble

Examples

The trumpet section played a powerful fanfare at the start of the concert.
She has been the leader of the trumpet section for over five years.
The conductor asked the trumpet section to play more softly during the second movement.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: brass section - the group of brass instrument players in an orchestra or band
