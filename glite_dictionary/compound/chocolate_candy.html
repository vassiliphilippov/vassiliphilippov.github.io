chocolate candy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑk.lət ˈkændi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒk.lət ˈkændi / (BrE)
a candy made with chocolate as a main ingredient, often sweet and eaten as a treat or snack
candy made with chocolate

Examples

She handed out chocolate candy to the children at the birthday party.
I have a weakness for chocolate candy after dinner.
The store sells a wide variety of gourmet chocolate candy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate - a food prepared from roasted cacao beans
  • Synonym: candy - a sweet food made mainly of sugar
