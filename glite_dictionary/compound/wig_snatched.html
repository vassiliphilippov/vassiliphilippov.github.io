wig snatched

1. ADJ

/ wɪɡ snætʃt / (AmE), / wɪɡ snætʃt / (BrE)
(slang) To be suddenly shocked, amazed, or impressed, often in a dramatic or humorous way.
suddenly shocked or amazed (slang)
slang

Examples

When the singer hit that high note, my wig snatched—I’ve never heard anything like it.
Her plot twist had everyone’s wig snatched and left us gasping for air.
Every time this show drops a new season, my wig snatched all over again.

Synset Examples

He had his wig snatched in that heated debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mind blown - extremely amazed or surprised
  • Antonym: unfazed - not surprised or affected

Etymology

Originally from drag and LGBTQ+ culture, referencing the image of a wig being snatched off in surprise.
