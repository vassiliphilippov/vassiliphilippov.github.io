Scorpio the Scorpion

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔːrpioʊ ðə skɔːrˈpiən / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.əʊ ðə skɔːˈpɪ.ən / (BrE)
the eighth sign of the zodiac, which the sun enters about October 23 and leaves about November 21; represented by a scorpion
eighth zodiac sign; October-November

Examples

People born under Scorpio the Scorpion are said to be passionate and determined.
The sun enters Scorpio the Scorpion around October 23rd each year.
Astrologers believe that Scorpio the Scorpion brings intensity and transformation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scorpio - the eighth sign of the zodiac; October-November
  • Degree Lower: Taurus the Bull - second sign of the zodiac; April-May
  • Degree Higher: Sagittarius the Archer - ninth sign of the zodiac; November-December

Etymology

From Latin 'Scorpio' (scorpion), a zodiac constellation associated with the mythological scorpion.
