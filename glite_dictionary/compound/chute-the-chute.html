chute-the-chute

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃuːt ðə ˈtʃuːt / (AmE), / ˌtʃuːt ðə ˈtʃuːt / (BrE)
an early amusement park ride featuring an elevated railway with steep inclines and sharp curves, typically ending with a splash into water
a historic amusement park ride with drops and curves

Examples

Crowds lined up to ride the chute-the-chute at the old amusement park.
The chute-the-chute thrilled visitors with its steep railway and dramatic splashdown.
Her grandfather remembers his first ride on a chute-the-chute as an exciting adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roller coaster - an amusement ride with steep drops and curves on rails

Etymology

From 'chute' (French for slide) and the phrase 'shoot the chute', referring to descending a slide into water.
