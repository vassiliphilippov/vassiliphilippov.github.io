bar star

1. NOUN

/ bɑɹ stɑɹ / (AmE), / bɑː stɑː / (BrE)
a popular or highly skilled bartender, recognized for their expertise and presence behind the bar
skilled or popular bartender
informal

Examples

Everyone in town knows the bar star for his inventive cocktails and friendly service.
After winning several mixology competitions, she became the new bar star at the nightclub.
The bar star always draws a crowd with his impressive flair bartending tricks.

Synset Examples

The bar star was serving drinks all night long.
He became the bar star after winning the cocktail competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - a person who serves drinks at a bar
