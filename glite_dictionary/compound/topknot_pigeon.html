topknot pigeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑpˌnɑt ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌnɒt ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a large Australasian pigeon noted for its prominent, bushy crest of feathers on its head
Australian pigeon with a crest

Examples

We spotted a topknot pigeon perched high in the eucalyptus tree.
The topknot pigeon is recognized by its long, wispy crest of feathers.
Many birdwatchers travel to Australia hoping to see the rare topknot pigeon.

Synset Examples

The topknot pigeon is known for its unique appearance.
