15 minutes

1. NOUN

/ fɪfˈtiːn ˈmɪnɪts / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːn ˈmɪnɪts / (BrE)
A period of time equal to one quarter of an hour.
a quarter of an hour

Examples

The meeting will start in 15 minutes, so please be on time.
She finished her homework in just 15 minutes.
You should let the dough rest for at least 15 minutes before baking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarter hour - a period of 15 minutes
