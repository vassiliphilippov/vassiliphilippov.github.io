state of mind

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv maɪnd / (AmE), / steɪt əv maɪnd / (BrE)
a person's temporary mental or emotional condition
temporary mental or emotional condition

Examples

Her calm state of mind helped her handle the emergency.
Meditation can improve your state of mind and reduce stress.
He was not in the right state of mind to make important decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mood - a temporary state of mind or feeling
  • Synonym: mindset - a person's usual attitude or mental state
