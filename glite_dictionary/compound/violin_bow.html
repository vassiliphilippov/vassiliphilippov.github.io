violin bow

1. NOUN

/ vaɪˈoʊlɪn boʊ / (AmE), / vaɪˈɒlɪn bəʊ / (BrE)
A thin, typically curved stick strung with horsehair, used to play the violin by drawing it across the strings.
bow used to play a violin

Examples

She tightened the hair on her violin bow before starting to play.
The violin bow requires careful maintenance to produce the best sound.
He forgot his violin bow at home and had to borrow one for the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bow - a stick strung with horsehair, used to play string instruments
