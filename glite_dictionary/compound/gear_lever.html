gear lever

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪr ˈliːvər / (AmE), / ɡɪə ˈliːvə / (BrE)
a device in a vehicle used to change gears by engaging and disengaging the transmission
device for changing gears in vehicles
british

Examples

She reached for the gear lever to shift into third gear.
In British cars, the gear lever is usually located between the front seats.
He accidentally knocked the gear lever out of position while adjusting his seat.

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a gearshift a ‘gear lever’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gearshift - device to change gears in a vehicle (American term)
