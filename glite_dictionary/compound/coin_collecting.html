coin collecting

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔɪn kəˌlɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪn kəˌlɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
the hobby or practice of collecting and studying coins and other forms of money
the hobby of collecting coins

Examples

Her passion for coin collecting began when she found an old penny in her grandfather’s attic.
He joined a club to meet others who share his interest in coin collecting.
The museum hosted an exhibit showcasing rare finds from decades of coin collecting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: numismatics - the study or collection of currency, including coins
