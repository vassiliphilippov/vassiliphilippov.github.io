very important person

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛri ɪmˈpɔrtənt ˈpɜːrsən / (AmE), / ˌvɛri ɪmˈpɔːtənt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person of great influence, status, or importance, often treated with special privileges or attention
person of influence or high status

Examples

The hotel reserved the top floor for every very important person attending the conference.
After decades of hard work, he finally felt like a very important person in his field.
Security escorted the very important person through a private entrance away from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: VIP - Abbreviation for 'very important person', used for influential people
