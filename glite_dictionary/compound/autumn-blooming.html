autumn-blooming

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔːtəm ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) flowering or coming into bloom during the autumn season.
flowering in autumn

Examples

Many gardens feature autumn-blooming chrysanthemums for late color.
The autumn-blooming sedum provides nectar for bees in September.
Some autumn-blooming plants thrive even after temperatures begin to drop.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spring-flowering - flowering in spring
  • Antonym: spring-blooming - blooming in spring
  • Antonym: summer-blooming - blooming in summer
  • Antonym: winter-blooming - blooming in winter
