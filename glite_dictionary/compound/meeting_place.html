meeting place

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːtɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a public or designated location where people gather, especially for open discussion or group activities
a place for people to gather or meet

Examples

The library serves as a popular meeting place for local community groups.
We agreed to use the café as our meeting place before the event started.
The town hall is often used as a meeting place for open discussions and public forums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: venue - a place where events or meetings are held
  • Synonym: assembly hall - a large room or building used for public gatherings

2. NOUN

/ ˈmitɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place designated for people to gather, especially for discussions or organized activities
designated location for gatherings

Examples

We decided that the local park would be the best meeting place for the event.
The church hall acts as a meeting place for many community groups.
He wrote down the address of the meeting place so he wouldn't forget it.

Synset Examples

The café is our regular meeting place for discussions.
Let's choose a convenient meeting place for everyone.
The library serves as a great meeting place for study groups.
