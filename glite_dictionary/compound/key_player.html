key player

1. NOUN

/ kiː ˈpleɪɚ / (AmE), / kiː ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
an important or influential person or member in a group, situation, or organization; someone who plays a crucial role in events or outcomes
important or influential person/member

Examples

She quickly became a key player in the negotiations thanks to her expertise.
The company is considered a key player in the global tech industry.
Without their captain, the team lacked a key player and struggled to win matches.

Synset Examples

Her position in the team made her a key player.
His testimony could implicate several key players.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mainstay - chief support or main figure in a group
  • Antonym: minor player - a person or member with little influence or importance
