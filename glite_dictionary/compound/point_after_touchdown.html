point after touchdown

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt ˈæftɚ tʌʃˌdaʊn / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ˈɑːftə tʌʃˌdaʊn / (BrE)
In American football, a point scored by successfully kicking the ball through the uprights immediately after a touchdown.
extra point in American football
american

Examples

The kicker made the point after touchdown, putting his team ahead by one.
Missing the point after touchdown cost the team the victory.
He lined up for the point after touchdown following the impressive drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extra point - an additional point scored after a touchdown by kicking the ball through the goalposts
