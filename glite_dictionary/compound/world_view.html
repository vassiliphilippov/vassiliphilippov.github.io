world view

1. NOUN

/ wɜrld vju / (AmE), / wɜːld vjuː / (BrE)
A particular philosophy or conception of the world and of human life.
philosophy or outlook on life and the world

Examples

Her world view was shaped by growing up in a multicultural environment.
The book explores how religion and science influence a person's world view.
Traveling extensively broadened his world view and challenged his assumptions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outlook - a person's point of view or general attitude to life
  • Synonym: perspective - a particular attitude toward or way of regarding something; a point of view
