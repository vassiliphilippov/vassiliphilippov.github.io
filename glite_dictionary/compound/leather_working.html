leather working

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðɚ ˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛðə ˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The craft or practice of making items from leather, often requiring skill and precision.
the craft of making items from leather

Examples

She took up leather working as a hobby and now makes custom wallets and belts.
The museum offered a demonstration of traditional leather working techniques.
His passion for leather working began after attending a workshop on artisanal crafts.
