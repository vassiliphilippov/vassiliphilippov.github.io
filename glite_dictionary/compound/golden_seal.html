golden seal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən siːl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən siːl / (BrE)
a perennial herb native to northeastern North America, having a thick yellow rootstock and large, rounded leaves, often used in herbal medicine
a North American medicinal herb

Examples

Many herbalists recommend golden seal to support immune health.
The root of golden seal is often used to make tinctures and extracts.
Wild populations of golden seal are at risk due to overharvesting.
