Sagittarius the Archer

1. PROPN

/ sæˌdʒɪˈtɛɹiəs ði ˈɑɹtʃɚ / (AmE), / sæˌdʒɪˈteəriəs ði ˈɑːtʃə / (BrE)
the ninth sign of the zodiac, represented as an archer; the sun is in this sign approximately from November 22 to December 21.
ninth zodiac sign, the Archer

Examples

People born under Sagittarius the Archer are often said to be adventurous and optimistic.
The symbol for Sagittarius the Archer is commonly depicted as a centaur drawing a bow.
My birthday falls within the dates of Sagittarius the Archer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sagittarius - the ninth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: zodiac - an imaginary belt in the sky with twelve signs

Etymology

From Latin Sagittarius ('archer').
