nerve-wracking experience

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrv ˌrækɪŋ ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ˈnɜːv ˌrækɪŋ ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an experience that causes intense stress, worry, or anxiety
anxious, stressful situation

Examples

Giving my first public speech was a truly nerve-wracking experience.
Waiting for the test results was a nerve-wracking experience for everyone involved.
Flying during a severe storm turned into a nerve-wracking experience for the passengers.

Synset Examples

Driving through the plait fog was a nerve-wracking experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stressful experience - an experience that causes stress
  • Antonym: calming experience - an experience that brings calm or relaxation
