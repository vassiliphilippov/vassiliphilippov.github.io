European tortoise

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈtɔr.təs / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈtɔː.tɔɪs / (BrE)
a small species of land tortoise native to southern Europe
small land tortoise of southern Europe

Examples

The European tortoise is commonly found in Mediterranean regions.
She kept a European tortoise as a pet in her garden.
Wild European tortoises are protected in many parts of southern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortoise - a land-dwelling reptile with a domed shell
