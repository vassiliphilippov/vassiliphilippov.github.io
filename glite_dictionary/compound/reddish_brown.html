reddish brown

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
A color that is primarily brown with a noticeable tinge of red.
brown with a tinge of red

Examples

The leaves turned a beautiful reddish brown in the autumn sunlight.
He painted the old barn door a deep reddish brown to match the roof.
Her hair had a natural reddish brown hue that glimmered in the light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auburn - a reddish-brown color, especially used for hair
