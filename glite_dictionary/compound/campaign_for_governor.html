campaign for governor

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈpeɪn fɚ ɡəˈvɝnɚ / (AmE), / kæmˈpeɪn fə ˈɡʌvənə / (BrE)
an organized effort to be elected as the governor of a state, involving events, speeches, and other activities to win voters' support.
effort to be elected state governor

Examples

She launched her campaign for governor with a rally downtown.
The campaign for governor was filled with intense debates and advertisements.
During his campaign for governor, he promised to improve public education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: election campaign - an organized effort to win an election for public office
  • Synonym: gubernatorial campaign - a campaign for the office of state governor
