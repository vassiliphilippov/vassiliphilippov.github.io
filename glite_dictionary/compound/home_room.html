home room

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ruːm / (AmE), / həʊm ruːm / (BrE)
a classroom where students in a particular grade or section meet regularly for attendance and administrative tasks, supervised by a designated teacher
classroom for administrative and attendance purposes in schools
american

Examples

Every morning, students gather in their home room before going to their first class.
The teacher took attendance in the home room and handed out the weekly schedule.
Her home room was located in the east wing of the school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: form room - classroom in British schools where attendance is taken and general administration is done
