club member

1. NOUN

/ klʌb ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / klʌb ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a club or organization
person belonging to a club

Examples

He became a club member to meet new people and participate in activities.
As a club member, she receives special discounts and invitations to exclusive events.
Every club member is expected to attend the annual general meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: member - a person belonging to a group or organization
