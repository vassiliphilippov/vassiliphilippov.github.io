service club

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs klʌb / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs klʌb / (BrE)
An organization of professional or business people formed to promote community service and charitable activities.
organization for public and charitable service

Examples

Many members of the service club volunteered at the local food drive this weekend.
He joined a service club to meet new people and help improve the community.
The service club organized scholarships for outstanding high school students every year.
