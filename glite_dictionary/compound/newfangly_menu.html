newfangly menu

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːˌfæŋɡli ˈmɛnju / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌfæŋɡli ˈmɛnjuː / (BrE)
A menu that features trendy, innovative, or fashionable dishes or presentations, often updated frequently.
trendy or innovative menu

Examples

The restaurant has a newfangly menu that changes every week.
Some customers prefer traditional dishes over the newfangly menu offered here.
Our chef enjoys creating a newfangly menu with unexpected flavor combinations.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newfangled - newly invented or introduced, often in a showy way

Etymology

From 'newfangly' (derivative of 'newfangled', meaning newly invented or introduced) + 'menu' (from French).
