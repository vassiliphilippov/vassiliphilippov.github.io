Tanzanian shilling

1. NOUN

/ tænˈzeɪniən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / tanˈzeɪniən ˈʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Tanzania, equal to 100 cents
currency of Tanzania

Examples

The Tanzanian shilling has remained relatively stable against the US dollar this year.
She exchanged her dollars for Tanzanian shillings before traveling to Dar es Salaam.
Official prices are always listed in Tanzanian shilling on government websites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shilling - a former British coin and various currencies, including in East Africa
