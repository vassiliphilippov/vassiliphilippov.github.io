proud munter

1. NOUN

/ praʊd ˈmʌntər / (AmE), / praʊd ˈmʌntə / (BrE)
A person who openly embraces or is unbothered by being considered physically unattractive.
someone who owns being unattractive (slang, informal)
informal slang british

Examples

Even though some people judge her looks, she refers to herself as a proud munter and wears whatever she likes.
He jokes about being a proud munter to encourage others to feel comfortable in their own skin.
On social media, the hashtag proud munter has become a symbol of self-acceptance for many.

Synset Examples

She laughed, saying she's a proud munter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: munter - a physically unattractive person (British slang)

Etymology

British slang 'munter' (an unattractive person) plus 'proud'.
