cunty thing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌnti ˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌnti ˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
An extremely offensive or rude remark or action, considered highly inappropriate and vulgar.
offensive or rude remark or act
offensive vulgar

Examples

Please don't say another cunty thing at the meeting, or you'll offend everyone.
He apologized for the cunty thing he said last night.
Many people were upset by her cunty thing during the discussion.

Synset Examples

That was a cunty thing to say, honestly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - a rude or offensive remark or act
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
