wanker's cramp

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæŋkərz kræmp / (AmE), / ˈwæŋkəz kræmp / (BrE)
An informal term for a painful condition in the hand caused by excessive or repetitive use, such as typing or gaming, often used humorously.
pain in the hand from overuse (humorous, informal)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

After spending all afternoon playing video games, he joked about his wanker's cramp.
She claimed her wanker's cramp was acting up after a long day at the keyboard.
Typing reports nonstop gave him a case of wanker's cramp by evening.
