binge-vomit syndrome

1. NOUN

/ bɪndʒ ˈvɑmɪt ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / bɪndʒ ˈvɒmɪt ˈsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
A disorder of eating marked by cycles of excessive food consumption (binges) followed by fasting or self-induced vomiting, typically involving alternating cravings and aversion to food.
eating disorder with bingeing and vomiting cycles
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with binge-vomit syndrome after experiencing repeated cycles of overeating and subsequent purging.
Support groups can be helpful for those struggling with binge-vomit syndrome and related eating disorders.
After months of therapy, her symptoms of binge-vomit syndrome began to subside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulimia nervosa - an eating disorder involving binge eating followed by purging
