United States Trade Representative

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːˈnaɪt̬ɪd steɪts ˈtreɪd ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts ˈtreɪd ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
the executive agency of the United States government responsible for developing and promoting the President's policies on international trade
U.S. executive agency for trade policy
american

Examples

The United States Trade Representative announced new tariffs on imported steel goods.

Negotiations were led by officials from the United States Trade Representative's office.
