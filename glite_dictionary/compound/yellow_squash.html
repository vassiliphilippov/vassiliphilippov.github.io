yellow squash

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ skwɒʃ / (BrE)
a type of summer squash with smooth, yellow skin and usually an elongated neck, grown for use as a vegetable
a yellow-skinned summer squash

Examples

We grilled yellow squash with garlic and olive oil for a healthy side dish.
She prefers yellow squash over zucchini in her summer salads.
The farmer’s market had baskets full of fresh yellow squash last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer squash - a type of squash harvested when immature; includes varieties like yellow squash and zucchini

2. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈjeləʊ skwɒʃ / (BrE)
A type of summer squash with yellow skin and yellowish flesh, typically featuring an elongated neck.
type of yellow-skinned summer squash

Examples

She added sliced yellow squash to the vegetable stir-fry for a splash of color.
At the farmer's market, I bought some fresh yellow squash for tonight's dinner.
Roasted yellow squash is a popular summer side dish in many American households.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer squash - a general type of squash harvested while immature, including yellow squash and zucchini
