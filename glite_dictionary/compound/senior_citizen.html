senior citizen

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːn.jɚ ˈsɪt.ɪ.zən / (AmE), / ˈsiː.nɪə ˈsɪt.ɪ.zən / (BrE)
an older person, typically someone who is retired or over a certain age, such as 60 or 65, depending on context
an elderly person, typically retired

Examples

Many businesses offer discounts to senior citizens on certain days of the week.
The city council is planning new recreational programs for senior citizens.
As a senior citizen, she enjoys participating in community events and social clubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elderly - an older person, especially one who is old or frail
  • Synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension, especially from the state on retirement
