electronic text

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk tɛkst / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk tɛkst / (BrE)
Text that is stored, displayed, or transmitted in digital form for use by a computer.
text in digital computer-readable form

Examples

The library offers many classic novels as electronic text for online reading.
You can download the electronic text version of the research paper from the website.
Converting documents to electronic text makes them easier to search and share.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-text - electronic text; digital text format
