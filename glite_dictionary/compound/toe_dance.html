toe dance

1. NOUN

/ toʊ dæns / (AmE), / təʊ dɑːns / (BrE)
a dance performed on the tips of the toes, typically in ballet.
dance on the tips of the toes

Examples

She trained for years to master the toe dance for her performance as the lead swan.
The audience was amazed by the dancer's graceful toe dance routine.
Performing a toe dance requires both strength and precision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointe - the technique of dancing on the tips of the toes in ballet
  • Synonym: ballet - a highly stylized form of dance
