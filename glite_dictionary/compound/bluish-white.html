bluish-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
White in color with a slight tint of blue.
white with a blue tint

Examples

The pearls had a bluish-white sheen that shimmered in the sunlight.
She chose a paint with a bluish-white hue for the living room walls.
The mountain was covered in a bluish-white mist early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bluish - somewhat blue in color
  • Degree Higher: blue - of the color blue
