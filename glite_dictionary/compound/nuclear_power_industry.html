nuclear power industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˈɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈpaʊ.ə ˈɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
the group of industries involved in generating electricity through nuclear power technology
industries generating electricity from nuclear power

Examples

The nuclear power industry faces ongoing debate over safety and disposal of radioactive waste.
Investment in the nuclear power industry surged after new government incentives were introduced.
Several countries rely heavily on their nuclear power industry to meet national electricity demand.

Synset Examples

Since about 2001 the term nuclear renaissance has been used to refer to a possible nuclear power industry revival, driven by rising fossil fuel prices and new concerns about meeting greenhouse gas emission limits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energy industry - businesses and sectors producing and supplying energy
