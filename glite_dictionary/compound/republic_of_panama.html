Republic of Panama

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌpænəˈmɑ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌpænəˈmɑː / (BrE)
A country in Central America, located on the Isthmus of Panama, which gained independence from Colombia in 1903.
Central American country on the Isthmus of Panama

Examples

The Republic of Panama is famous for its canal linking the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.
After gaining independence, the Republic of Panama established its own constitution.
The biodiversity of the Republic of Panama attracts researchers and tourists from across the globe.

  • Synonym: Panama - country in Central America
