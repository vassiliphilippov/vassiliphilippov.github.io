dishwashing liquid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɑʃɪŋ ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃɪŋ ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
a low-sudsing liquid detergent designed for washing dishes, especially by hand
detergent for washing dishes by hand

Examples

She poured some dishwashing liquid onto the sponge before cleaning the plates.
The store was out of dishwashing liquid, so I had to use soap instead.
Always dilute dishwashing liquid with water to avoid leaving residue on your dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dish soap - liquid soap for washing dishes
