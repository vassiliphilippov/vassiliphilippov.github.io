chocolate bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət bɑr / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət bɑː / (BrE)
A solid, bar-shaped piece of chocolate, usually intended as a sweet snack or treat.
a bar-shaped piece of chocolate candy

Examples

She always keeps a chocolate bar in her desk drawer for an afternoon treat.
After dinner, the children each received a chocolate bar as a dessert.
He unwrapped the chocolate bar and broke off a piece to share with his friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candy bar - A bar-shaped piece of sweet confectionery, often including chocolate and other ingredients
