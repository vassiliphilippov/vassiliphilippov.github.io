zombie dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑmbi dæns / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbi dɑːns / (BrE)
A style of dance that imitates the movements of zombies, typically featuring stiff, jerky motions and dramatic poses.
dance style imitating zombies

Examples

Every Halloween party seems to feature at least one group performing the zombie dance to thriller music.
The students choreographed an elaborate zombie dance for the school's talent show.
He taught his friends the basic steps of the zombie dance for their next performance.

Synset Examples

She perfected her moves for the zombie dance competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonwalk - a dance move in which the dancer glides backward while appearing to walk forward
  • Synonym: robot dance - a dance style that mimics the movements of a robot
