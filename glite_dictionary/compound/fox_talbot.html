Fox Talbot

1. PROPN

/ fɑks ˈtæl.bət / (AmE), / fɒks ˈtæl.bət / (BrE)
(1800–1877) English inventor and pioneer in photography who published the first book illustrated with photographs.
English photography pioneer (1800–1877)

Examples

Fox Talbot is celebrated for his groundbreaking contributions to the development of photography.
The museum's new exhibit explores the achievements of Fox Talbot and early photographic processes.
Without Fox Talbot, the history of photography would look very different.
