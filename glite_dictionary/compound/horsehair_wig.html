horsehair wig

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌhɛr wɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːshɛə wɪɡ / (BrE)
a wig made from horsehair, traditionally worn as part of formal dress by judges and barristers in some courts, especially in the United Kingdom
wig made from horsehair, worn in courts

Examples

The judge donned his horsehair wig before entering the courtroom.
Traditional horsehair wigs remain a symbol of the English legal system.
She visited a London shop specializing in horsehair wigs for barristers.

Synset Examples

the English judiciary wear their traditional horsehair wigs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wig - an artificial covering of hair for the head
