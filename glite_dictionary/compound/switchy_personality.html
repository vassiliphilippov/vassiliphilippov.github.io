switchy personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃi pɚˌsænəˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃi pəˌsænlˈɪti / (BrE)
A personality that changes unpredictably or frequently, especially in mood or attitude.
unpredictably changing personality
informal

Examples

She is difficult to work with because of her switchy personality.
His switchy personality makes him unpredictable in team decisions.
People with a switchy personality often surprise their friends with sudden mood changes.

Synset Examples

He has a switchy personality that keeps everyone guessing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mercurial - quick and changeable in temperament
  • Antonym: consistent - acting or done in the same way over time
