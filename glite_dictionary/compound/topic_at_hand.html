topic at hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːpɪk ət hænd / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪk ət hand / (BrE)
the subject that is currently being discussed or considered
current subject of discussion

Examples

Let's focus on the topic at hand instead of getting distracted by unrelated issues.
She tried to steer the meeting back to the topic at hand whenever the conversation wandered.
Before we finish, is there anything else to add regarding the topic at hand?

Synset Examples

But seriously folks, let’s get back to the topic at hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subject at hand - the current subject of discussion or consideration
  • Synonym: matter at hand - the issue or matter currently being discussed
