graduating class

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrædʒuˌeɪtɪŋ klæs / (AmE), / ˈɡrædjueɪtɪŋ klɑːs / (BrE)
the group of students who complete their studies and graduate from a school or college in the same year
group of students graduating together

Examples

The graduating class posed for a group photo after the ceremony.
Our graduating class raised money to plant a tree on campus.
This year's graduating class includes students from over 20 countries.
