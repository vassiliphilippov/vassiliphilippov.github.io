hairball formula

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrˌbɔl ˈfɔrmjələ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbɔːl ˈfɔːmjʊlə / (BrE)
a type of cat food formulated to help prevent the formation of hairballs in a cat's stomach
cat food to prevent hairballs

Examples

After switching to a hairball formula, my cat stopped having digestive issues.
The vet recommended a special hairball formula for our long-haired kitten.
Many pet stores stock several brands of hairball formula to suit different cats' needs.

Synset Examples

Princess Fluffypaw is throwing up again, maybe we should get her on hairball formula?

Related Terms
