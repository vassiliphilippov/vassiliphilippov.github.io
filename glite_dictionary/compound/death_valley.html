Death Valley

1. PROPN

/ dɛθ ˈvæli / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈvæli / (BrE)
a desert valley in eastern California and southwestern Nevada, known as one of the hottest places on Earth and containing the lowest point in North America
desert area in California and Nevada, USA

Examples

Temperatures in Death Valley can reach record highs during the summer months.
Many tourists visit Death Valley National Park to experience its unique landscape.
The lowest point in North America is found in Death Valley, at Badwater Basin.
