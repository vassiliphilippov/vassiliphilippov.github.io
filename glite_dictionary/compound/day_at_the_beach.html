day at the beach

1. NOUN

/ deɪ æt ðə bitʃ / (AmE), / deɪ æt ðə biːtʃ / (BrE)
a day spent at a beach, typically for relaxation or recreation
a day spent enjoying the beach

Examples

We spent a memorable day at the beach building sandcastles and swimming.
A day at the beach is the perfect way to relax after a busy week.
She fondly recalls every day at the beach from her childhood summers.

Synset Examples

Every summer, we plan a day at the beach.
That day at the beach was unforgettable.
