shower in a can

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊɚ ɪn ə kæn / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə ɪn ə kæn / (BrE)
a portable spray or product used as a substitute for a traditional shower, typically to quickly clean the body without water
portable spray for quick cleaning

Examples

After finishing his hike, Tom used a shower in a can to freshen up instantly.
Many busy travelers carry a shower in a can for times when water isn’t available.
For hospital patients who can't bathe, nurses sometimes provide a shower in a can.
