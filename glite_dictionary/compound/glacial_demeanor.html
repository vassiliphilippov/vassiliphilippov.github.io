glacial demeanor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡleɪʃəl dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪəl dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
a manner or attitude that is extremely cold, unapproachable, or unfriendly
extremely cold or unfriendly attitude

Examples

She spoke to the newcomer with a glacial demeanor, barely acknowledging his presence.
Despite her glacial demeanor, colleagues respected her professionalism.
His glacial demeanor during meetings discouraged others from sharing their ideas.

Synset Examples

His glacial demeanor made it hard to approach him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icy demeanor - a cold or unfriendly manner
  • Antonym: warm demeanor - a friendly and approachable manner

Etymology

From 'glacial', meaning icy or extremely cold, and 'demeanor', meaning outward manner or behavior.
