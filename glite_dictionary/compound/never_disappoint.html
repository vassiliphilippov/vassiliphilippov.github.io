to never disappoint

1. VERB

/ ˈnɛvɚ ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt / (BrE)
to always meet or exceed expectations; to never fail to satisfy or impress
to always meet expectations

Examples

No matter what dish you order from this restaurant, it never disappoints.
Her holiday recommendations never disappoint when we're planning a trip.
That author's books never disappoint, so I preorder every new release.

Synset Examples

Ah Tiong's recommendations never disappoint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: never fail - to always succeed or meet expectations
  • Antonym: to disappoint - fail to meet the expectations or hopes of
