Sunshine State

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌnʃaɪn steɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnʃaɪn steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Florida in the southeastern United States, known for its warm climate and sunshine
nickname for the state of Florida (USA)

Examples

Many retirees move to the Sunshine State for its pleasant weather and beaches.
Tourism is a major industry in the Sunshine State, especially in cities like Orlando and Miami.
The hurricane season can be intense in the Sunshine State, but its residents are well prepared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Florida - a state in the southeastern United States
