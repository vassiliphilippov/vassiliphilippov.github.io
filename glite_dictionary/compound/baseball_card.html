baseball card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl kɑːd / (BrE)
a small collectible card featuring a photograph and statistics of a baseball player, often traded or collected as a hobby
collectible card featuring a baseball player

Examples

He has been collecting baseball cards since he was a child.
A rare baseball card can be worth thousands of dollars to collectors.
She traded her duplicate baseball cards with her friends after school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trading card - a collectible card, often part of a set, depicting sports figures or other subjects
