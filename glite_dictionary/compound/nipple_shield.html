nipple shield

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪpəl ʃiːld / (AmE), / ˈnɪpəl ʃiːld / (BrE)
a thin protective cover, usually made of rubber or plastic, placed over a woman's nipple during breastfeeding to protect or assist with feeding
protective cover for breastfeeding nipples
medical

Examples

The lactation consultant recommended a nipple shield to help with sore nipples.
She found nursing much less painful after using a nipple shield.
Hospitals sometimes provide a nipple shield for mothers having trouble with breastfeeding.
