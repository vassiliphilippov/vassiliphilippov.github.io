full of bogosity

1. ADJ

/ fʊl əv boʊˈɡɒsɪti / (AmE), / fʊl əv bəʊˈɡɒsɪti / (BrE)
Containing a large amount of nonsense or falsehood; completely lacking in truth or credibility.
filled with nonsense or falsehood
informal slang

Examples

That conspiracy theory is just full of bogosity from start to finish.
The website's exaggerated reviews were full of bogosity.
Sometimes political speeches are so full of bogosity it's hard to take them seriously.

Synset Examples

His claims about the product were full of bogosity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bogus - not genuine or true; fake
  • Synonym: nonsense - ideas or statements that are not true or sensible

Etymology

From 'bogosity', a playful slang term derived from 'bogus' (fraudulent or fake), with the suffix '-ity'.
