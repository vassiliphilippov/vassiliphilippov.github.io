knockdown-dragout

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑkˌdaʊn ˈdræɡˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈnɒkˌdaʊn ˈdræɡˌaʊt / (BrE)
(of an argument, fight, etc.) extremely violent and intense, often involving physical force or heated confrontation.
extremely violent and intense

Examples

The two politicians had a knockdown-dragout debate on live television.
They ended their partnership after a knockdown-dragout argument about finances.
It was a knockdown-dragout fight that left both competitors exhausted.

Synset Examples

a knock-down-and-drag-out fight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violent - involving physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill

Etymology

From the literal words 'knock down' and 'drag out', originally referring to a physical fight.
