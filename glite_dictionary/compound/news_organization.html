news organization

1. NOUN

/ nuːz ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / njuːz ˌɔːɡənʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
An organization that gathers, produces, and distributes news reports to the public or to other media outlets.
organization that collects and distributes news

Examples

The news organization broke the story before any other outlet could report it.
Several international news organizations covered the event from multiple perspectives.
She works as an editor for a major news organization in New York.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news agency - an organization that supplies news reports to subscribing news organizations

company that gathers and distributes news

Examples
