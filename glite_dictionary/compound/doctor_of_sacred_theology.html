Doctor of Sacred Theology

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktər əv ˈseɪkrɪd θiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv ˈseɪkrɪd θɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the highest academic degree in theology awarded in the pontifical university system of the Catholic Church
highest Catholic Church theology degree

Examples

He received his Doctor of Sacred Theology at the Pontifical Gregorian University in Rome.
The title Doctor of Sacred Theology signifies the highest level of theological scholarship in the Catholic Church.
After years of study, she was finally awarded the Doctor of Sacred Theology degree.

Synset Examples

STD is from the Latin Sanctae Theologiae Doctor

Etymology

From Latin Sanctae Theologiae Doctor, meaning 'Doctor of Sacred Theology.'
