jewelry dealer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːəlri ˌdiːlər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəlri ˌdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells jewelry as a primary activity
person or firm trading in jewelry

Examples

The jewelry dealer displayed a collection of rare gemstones in his shop.
After inheriting the necklace, she consulted a jewelry dealer to have it appraised.
Many jewelry dealers attend international trade fairs to find unique pieces for their clients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeweler - a person who makes, sells, or repairs jewelry
