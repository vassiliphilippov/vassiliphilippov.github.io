banking industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy consisting of banks and other financial institutions involved in accepting deposits, lending money, and providing related financial services collectively
banks and related financial institutions collectively

Examples

The banking industry has undergone significant changes due to advances in digital technology.
Regulations are in place to ensure the stability of the banking industry.
Many people lost their jobs when the banking industry faced a major crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banking sector - division of the economy related to banks and banking activities
