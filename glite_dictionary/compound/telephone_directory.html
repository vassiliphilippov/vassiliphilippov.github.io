telephone directory

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn dəˈrɛktəri / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn dɪˈrɛktəri / (BrE)
a book or online resource containing an alphabetical list of telephone subscribers and their telephone numbers
book listing phone numbers and subscribers

Examples

She searched the telephone directory for her friend's new number.
Many households used to keep a telephone directory next to the phone.
These days, most people use online services instead of a printed telephone directory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone book - a book that lists telephone numbers and names
