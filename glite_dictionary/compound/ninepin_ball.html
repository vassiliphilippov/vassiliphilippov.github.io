ninepin ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪnpɪn bɔl / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnpɪn bɔːl / (BrE)
A ball used to knock down pins in the game of ninepins.
ball used in ninepins

Examples

He gripped the ninepin ball tightly before taking his shot at the pins.
Unlike a bowling ball, a ninepin ball is often smaller and without finger holes.
The old alley displayed several antique ninepin balls from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling ball - ball used in modern bowling games
