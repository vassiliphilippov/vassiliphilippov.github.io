flight of steps

1. NOUN

/ flaɪt əv stɛps / (AmE), / flaɪt əv stɛps / (BrE)
a set of stairs between two floors or landings
set of stairs between landings

Examples

A narrow flight of steps led down to the cellar.
He paused at the top of the flight of steps to catch his breath.
The museum's main entrance is reached by a long flight of steps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staircase - a structure containing a series of steps
  • Degree Lower: step - an individual flat surface in a staircase
