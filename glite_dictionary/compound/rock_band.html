rock band

1. NOUN

/ rɑk bænd / (AmE), / rɒk band / (BrE)
A group of musicians who perform rock and roll or rock music, typically using electric guitars, bass, drums, and vocals.
group that performs rock music

Examples

The rock band released their debut album to critical acclaim.
She always dreamed of playing guitar in a famous rock band.
The rock band performed at the festival in front of thousands of fans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians who play together
