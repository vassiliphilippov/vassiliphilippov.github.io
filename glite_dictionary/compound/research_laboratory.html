research laboratory

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ ˈlæbrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ ləˈbɒrətəri / (BrE)
a place equipped for conducting scientific research or experiments
workplace for scientific research

Examples

She spent several years working in a research laboratory studying infectious diseases.
The university recently opened a new research laboratory for physics students.
Scientists in the research laboratory developed a groundbreaking vaccine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building equipped for scientific experiments
