ticket collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkət kəˌlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt kəˌlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to check and collect tickets from people entering an event, venue, or public transport
person who checks and collects tickets

Examples

The ticket collector checked everyone's tickets before allowing them to enter the concert hall.
Please have your tickets ready for the ticket collector at the station entrance.
A friendly ticket collector walked down the aisle, checking each passenger’s ticket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conductor - person who collects fares or tickets on public transportation
