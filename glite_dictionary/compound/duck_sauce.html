duck sauce

1. NOUN

/ dʌk sɔs / (AmE), / dʌk sɔːs / (BrE)
a thick, sweet and tangy sauce served as a condiment with Chinese dishes, especially in Chinese-American cuisine
sweet and tangy Chinese condiment

Examples

She dipped her egg roll into the duck sauce before taking a bite.
You can find packets of duck sauce with most Chinese takeout orders.
The sweet and sour flavor of duck sauce complements fried wontons perfectly.
