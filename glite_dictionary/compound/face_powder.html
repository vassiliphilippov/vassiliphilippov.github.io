face powder

1. NOUN

/ feɪs ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / feɪs ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a fine cosmetic powder applied to the face to improve or set one's complexion
cosmetic powder for the face

Examples

She dusted her cheeks with face powder before leaving for the party.
This brand's face powder gives a natural, matte finish throughout the day.
She keeps a compact of face powder in her purse for quick touch-ups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powder - a substance composed of fine particles
