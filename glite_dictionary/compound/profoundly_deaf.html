profoundly deaf

1. ADJ

/ prəˈfaʊndli dɛf / (AmE), / prəˈfaʊndli dɛf / (BrE)
Completely unable to hear; having total hearing loss.
totally unable to hear

Examples

The school has resources to support children who are profoundly deaf.
After the illness, she became profoundly deaf and learned to communicate through sign language.
The charity focuses on helping profoundly deaf adults access job opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: deaf - unable to hear
