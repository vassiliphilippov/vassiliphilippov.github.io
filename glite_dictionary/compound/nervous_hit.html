nervous hit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrvəs hɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs hɪt / (BrE)
a sudden surge of anxiety or a sharp loss of confidence or value, especially in response to stress or uncertainty
sudden bout of anxiety or loss of confidence/value

Examples

After hearing the bad news, she took a nervous hit and struggled to focus.
Markets suffered a nervous hit following the unexpected announcement.
He experiences a nervous hit whenever he speaks in front of a large audience.

Synset Examples

He always takes a nervous hit before his big presentations.
The stock market felt a nervous hit after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panic attack - a sudden episode of intense fear or anxiety
  • Synonym: shock - a sudden and significant disturbance or surprise
