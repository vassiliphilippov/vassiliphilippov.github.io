sustainable logging

1. NOUN

/ səˈsteɪnəbl ˈlɑgɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnəbl ˈlɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of harvesting timber in ways that preserve the health of forests and protect the environment for future generations
environmentally responsible timber harvesting

Examples

The new forest management plan requires all operations to follow sustainable logging methods.
Many companies are shifting to sustainable logging to reduce environmental impact.
Sustainable logging helps maintain biodiversity and prevents soil erosion in forested areas.

Synset Examples

The forester taught us about sustainable logging.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear-cutting - removal of all trees from an area of forest
