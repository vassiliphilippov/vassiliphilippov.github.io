military recruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri rɪˈkrut / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri rɪˈkruːt / (BrE)
a person who has recently joined the armed forces and is undergoing initial training
recently enlisted soldier

Examples

After basic training, each military recruit is assigned to a specific unit.
The sergeant addressed the group of military recruits early in the morning.
As a military recruit, she had to adapt quickly to a strict routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recruit - a person newly enlisted in an organization, especially the armed forces
