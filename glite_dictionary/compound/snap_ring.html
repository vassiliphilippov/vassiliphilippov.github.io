snap ring

1. NOUN

/ snæp rɪŋ / (AmE), / snæp rɪŋ / (BrE)
A metal ring with a spring-loaded clip that can quickly attach ropes or cables to other objects, commonly used in mountaineering and mechanical assemblies.
metal ring with spring clip for fastening

Examples

She used a snap ring to quickly secure the rope to the anchor point.
Check that each snap ring is properly closed before starting the climb.
The kit comes with several snap rings for attaching tools to your harness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carabiner - a metal loop with a spring-loaded gate used for quick connection
