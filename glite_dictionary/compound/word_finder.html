word finder

1. NOUN

/ wɝd ˈfaɪndɚ / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈfaɪndə / (BrE)
a reference book or tool designed to help users find a word they cannot recall, often organized by meanings or concepts
reference for finding words by meaning

Examples

The word finder helped me recall the right term for my essay.
She uses a word finder when writing poetry to choose precise words.
I keep a word finder on my desk for times when I'm stuck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thesaurus - reference book listing synonyms and related words
