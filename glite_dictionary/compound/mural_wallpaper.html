mural wallpaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjʊrəl ˈwɔlˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈmjʊərəl ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A decorative wallpaper featuring a large printed design or scene, usually applied to a single wall as a focal point in a room.
decorative wall-covering with a printed scene

Examples

They chose a mural wallpaper depicting a forest scene for their living room accent wall.
The designer recommended a custom mural wallpaper to create a dramatic focal point in the dining area.
Installing a mural wallpaper can transform an ordinary space into something remarkable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallpaper - paper used to cover and decorate the interior walls of rooms
