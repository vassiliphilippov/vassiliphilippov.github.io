slow to respond

1. ADJ

/ sloʊ tə rɪˈspɑnd / (AmE), / sləʊ tə rɪˈspɒnd / (BrE)
taking a long time to reply or react to something
taking a long time to reply

Examples

He tends to be slow to respond in group chats, often missing important updates.
Some customers are slow to respond to follow-up emails from the support team.
When under stress, she becomes slow to respond to questions and requests.

Synset Examples

He's always slow to respond to text messages.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively
  • Antonym: quick to respond - replying or reacting without delay
