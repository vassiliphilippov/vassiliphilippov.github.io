fiesta flower

1. NOUN

/ fiˈɛstə ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / fiˈɛstə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A straggling annual herb native to California, with deep purple or violet flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Nemophila.
Californian herb with purple/violet flowers

Examples

The fiesta flower adds a splash of violet color to California's spring wildflower displays.
Botanists sometimes classify the fiesta flower within the genus Nemophila.
Many gardeners admire the delicate blossoms of the fiesta flower in their native habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nemophila - Genus of annual herbs including the fiesta flower
