funeral parlour

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːnɚəl ˈpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
a place where the bodies of deceased people are prepared for burial or cremation and where visitors may pay their respects
place for preparing the dead and paying respects

Examples

The family gathered at the funeral parlour to say their final goodbyes.
After the ceremony, friends visited the funeral parlour to pay their respects.
The town has only one funeral parlour, run by the same family for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortuary - a place where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation
