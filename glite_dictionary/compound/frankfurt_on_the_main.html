Frankfurt on the Main

1. PROPN

/ ˈfræŋkfɝt ɑn ðə meɪn / (AmE), / ˈfraŋkfət ɒn ðə maɪn / (BrE)
A major city in central Germany, known as an industrial, commercial, and financial center; also called Frankfurt am Main.
German industrial and financial city

Examples

The European Central Bank has its headquarters in Frankfurt on the Main.
Many international conferences are held in Frankfurt on the Main each year.
After flying into Frankfurt on the Main, we took a train to Berlin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Frankfurt - A city in Germany, also known as Frankfurt am Main

Etymology

German 'Frankfurt am Main' meaning 'Frankfurt on the Main (River)'.
