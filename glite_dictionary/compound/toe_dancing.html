toe dancing

1. NOUN

/ toʊ ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / təʊ ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of dance, especially ballet, performed on the tips of the toes, typically with special shoes
dance performed on tiptoe

Examples

The ballerina amazed the audience with her toe dancing during the performance.
He began training in toe dancing at the age of ten.
Toe dancing requires strength, balance, and precise technique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointe - the act of dancing on the tips of the toes, especially in ballet
  • Synonym: ballet - a highly stylized dance form often involving toe dancing
