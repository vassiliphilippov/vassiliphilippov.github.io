common watercress

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən ˈwɔtərˌkrɛs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈwɔːtəkres / (BrE)
a perennial aquatic plant (Nasturtium officinale) with pungent, fleshy leaves, commonly used in salads, as a garnish, or as a potherb
aquatic plant used in salads

Examples

Fresh common watercress adds a peppery flavor to salads and sandwiches.
We spotted common watercress growing along the edge of the stream.
The chef used common watercress as a garnish for the fish dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watercress - an aquatic plant with peppery leaves, used in salads
