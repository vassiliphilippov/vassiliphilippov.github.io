no-account loser

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ əˈkaʊnt ˈluːzər / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ əˈkaʊnt ˈluːzə / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person regarded as worthless or lacking in success
person considered worthless or a failure
informal offensive

Examples

Some people called him a no-account loser, but he never let the insults get to him.
She refused to waste her time with a no-account loser who didn't care about his future.
The novel's protagonist is a no-account loser struggling to find purpose in an unforgiving town.

Synset Examples

He’s a no-account loser who never tries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loser - a person who fails to succeed or is generally unsuccessful
