clutch bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌtʃ bæg / (AmE), / ˈklʌtʃ bæɡ / (BrE)
a small, strapless handbag, typically carried in the hand, often used by women for formal occasions
small strapless handbag for women

Examples

She completed her evening look with a sparkling clutch bag and matching heels.
At the wedding, all the bridesmaids carried elegant silver clutch bags.
I keep my phone and lipstick in a small clutch bag when I go out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbag - a woman's bag for carrying personal items
  • Synonym: purse - a small bag for carrying money and personal items
