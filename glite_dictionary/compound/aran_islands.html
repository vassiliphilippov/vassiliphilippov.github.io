Aran Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈærən ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈærən ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of three small islands off the west coast of Ireland, at the entrance to Galway Bay, known for their ancient monuments and traditional Irish culture
islands off Ireland's west coast, entrance to Galway Bay

Examples

Many tourists visit the Aran Islands to experience traditional Irish culture and breathtaking landscapes.
The fishermen of the Aran Islands have preserved their customs for generations.
She bought a knitted sweater that was handmade on the Aran Islands.
