data link

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə lɪŋk, ˈdætə lɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə lɪŋk, ˈdætə lɪŋk / (BrE)
a connection or circuit between two or more locations used for transmitting and receiving data electronically
circuit for transmitting data

Examples

The data link between the two offices ensures that files can be shared instantly.
Engineers monitored the data link for any sign of disruption during the experiment.
A secure data link is critical for transferring sensitive information between headquarters and remote locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - an act of linking or joining two things together
