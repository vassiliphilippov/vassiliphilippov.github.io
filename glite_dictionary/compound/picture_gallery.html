picture gallery

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ ˌɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌɡæləri / (BrE)
a room or building where paintings and other works of art are exhibited to the public
room or building for displaying art

Examples

The new picture gallery features works by several renowned artists.
On our visit to London, we spent an afternoon enjoying the picture gallery at the museum.
Many schools organize field trips to the local picture gallery to introduce students to art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art gallery - a gallery that displays works of art, especially paintings
