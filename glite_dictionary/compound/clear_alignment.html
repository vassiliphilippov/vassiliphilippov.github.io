clear alignment

1. NOUN

/ klɪr əˈlaɪnmənt / (AmE), / klɪə əˈlaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a state of obvious agreement or conformity with a particular idea, policy, or group
obvious agreement or conformity

Examples

There was a clear alignment between the company's values and its sustainability initiatives.
Her decision showed a clear alignment with her political party's core beliefs.
The research findings indicated a clear alignment among all team members on the project's goals.

Synset Examples

His policies reflected a clear alignment with capitalist roadism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alignment - the state of being in agreement or proper position
  • Synonym: agreement - a situation in which people have the same opinion
  • Antonym: disagreement - lack of consensus or conformity
