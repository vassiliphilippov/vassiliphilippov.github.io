thin-leaved bilberry

1. NOUN

/ θɪn livd ˈbɪlˌbɛri / (AmE), / θɪn liːvd ˈbɪlˌbəri / (BrE)
an erect blueberry plant native to the western United States, with solitary flowers and somewhat sour berries
a western US blueberry species with sour berries

Examples

The thin-leaved bilberry thrives in mountainous regions of the western United States.
Researchers studied the nutritional properties of the thin-leaved bilberry fruit.
Hikers enjoy spotting thin-leaved bilberry plants along the trail in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blueberry - a small sweet blue-black edible berry or the plant on which it grows
  • Synonym: bilberry - a European wild blueberry plant or its fruit
