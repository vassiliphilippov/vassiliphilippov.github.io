Australian language

1. NOUN

/ əˈstreɪliən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of the indigenous languages spoken by Australian Aboriginal peoples, distinct from English and other languages later brought to Australia.
an indigenous language of Australian Aboriginal peoples

Examples

Many linguists study the unique features of Australian language families.
She grew up speaking an Australian language in her remote community.
Efforts to preserve Australian language traditions are increasing across the country.
