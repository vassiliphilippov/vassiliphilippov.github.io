fagless environment

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæɡləs ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɡləs ɪnˈvʌɪrənm(ə)nt / (BrE)
A place or situation where smoking is not allowed or where there is no cigarette smoke.
place where smoking is not allowed
informal british

Examples

She appreciated the fagless environment in her new office building.
After the smoking ban, many cafes became a fagless environment where families felt welcome.
The hotel proudly advertised its fagless environment to attract non-smokers.

Synset Examples

She enjoyed the fagless environment of the new team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke-free - not allowing or free from cigarette smoke

Etymology

From British slang 'fag' (cigarette) + '-less' (without) + 'environment'.
