roundup of cattle

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊnd.ʌp əv ˈkæt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd.ʌp əv ˈkæt.əl / (BrE)
the act of gathering cattle together, especially for counting, branding, or moving them
gathering of cattle

Examples

The annual roundup of cattle takes place every spring on the ranch.
Cowboys spent days preparing for the roundup of cattle before moving the herd north.
After a successful roundup of cattle, the ranchers celebrated with a big meal.

Synset Examples

The last roundup of cattle was a success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roundup - the act of driving or bringing cattle together
