potential cure

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtɛnʃəl kjʊr / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃəl kjʊə / (BrE)
a treatment, medication, or procedure that might be able to cure a disease or medical condition in the future
possible cure or remedy for a disease

Examples

Many scientists are working to develop a potential cure for Alzheimer's disease.
Researchers announced they have discovered a potential cure for a rare genetic disorder.
The drug shows promise as a potential cure, but more testing is needed.

Synset Examples

The new treatment could be a potential cure for cancer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cure - a treatment that eliminates a disease or condition
  • Synonym: treatment - medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury
