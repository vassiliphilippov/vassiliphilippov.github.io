Kingdom of Belgium

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈbɛl.dʒəm / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.dəm əv ˈbɛl.dʒəm / (BrE)
the official name for the country Belgium, a constitutional monarchy in northwestern Europe, known as the headquarters of the European Union and NATO
official name of Belgium, European country

Examples

The headquarters of the European Union is located in the Kingdom of Belgium.
Many tourists visit the Kingdom of Belgium each year for its medieval cities and rich heritage.
The Kingdom of Belgium is known for its chocolate, waffles, and diverse culture.
