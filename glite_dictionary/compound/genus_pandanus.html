genus Pandanus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs pænˈdeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs panˈdanəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Pandanaceae, comprising tropical plants known as screw pines.
type genus of screw pines

Examples

The genus Pandanus includes hundreds of species found throughout tropical regions.
Botanists studied the genus Pandanus to understand its role in coastal ecosystems.
Several economically important plants belong to the genus Pandanus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw pine - any plant of the genus Pandanus, tropical, palm-like plant

Etymology

From Latin 'Pandanus', the genus name, ultimately from Malay 'pandan', referring to the screw pine.
