shopworn furniture

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑp.wɔrn ˈfɝnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒp.wɔːn ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
furniture that is worn or slightly damaged from being on display in a shop, or considered outdated
worn or outdated furniture from display

Examples

She decided to buy the shopworn furniture because it fit her budget.
The manager discounted the shopworn furniture after months of display.
Although the shopworn furniture had a few scratches, it was still sturdy and attractive.

Synset Examples

The shopworn furniture still had its charm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secondhand furniture - furniture that has been owned and used by someone before

Etymology

From 'shopworn' (damaged by display in a shop) + 'furniture'.
