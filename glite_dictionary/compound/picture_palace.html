picture palace

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ ˌpælɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌpælɪs / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a cinema, a theatre where films are shown to the public
old term for a cinema or movie theater
archaic british

Examples

The old picture palace on Main Street has been closed for decades.
In the 1920s, everyone dressed up to visit the local picture palace.
She fondly remembered her childhood visits to the grand picture palace in her hometown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema - a theatre where films are shown
  • Synonym: movie theater - a place for viewing movies; mainly American English
