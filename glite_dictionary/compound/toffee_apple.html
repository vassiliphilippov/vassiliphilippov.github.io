toffee apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑfi ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈtɒfi ˈæpəl / (BrE)
An apple coated with a hard, sweet layer of caramelized sugar, often served on a stick as a treat.
apple coated in hard caramelized sugar
british

Examples

We bought a toffee apple at the fair and ate it on the way home.
Children love to eat a toffee apple as a special treat in autumn.
The toffee apple was sticky and sweet, with a bright red coating.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candy apple - an apple coated with a hard sugar candy, mainly US term
