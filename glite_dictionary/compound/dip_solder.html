to dip solder

1. VERB

/ dɪp ˈsɑldɚ / (AmE), / dɪp ˈsɒldə / (BrE)
to solder by immersing the object in a bath of molten solder
to solder by immersion in molten solder

Examples

To make the circuit board connections, they dip solder the components in a special bath.
He carefully dip soldered each wire to ensure a strong electrical bond.
Some factories dip solder hundreds of parts in a single operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solder - join with melted metal alloy
