cream-of-tartar tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌkriːm əv ˈtɑrtər triː / (AmE), / ˌkriːm əv ˈtɑːtə triː / (BrE)
an Australian tree (Owenia acidula) having an acid-tasting fruit that resembles a gourd
Australian tree with acid fruit

Examples

The cream-of-tartar tree is native to northern Australia and valued for its tart fruit.
Many Indigenous communities use the fruit of the cream-of-tartar tree in traditional recipes.
We found a cream-of-tartar tree growing near the riverbank during our bushwalk.
