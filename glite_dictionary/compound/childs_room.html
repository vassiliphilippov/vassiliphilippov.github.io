child's room

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪldz ruːm / (AmE), / tʃaɪldz ruːm / (BrE)
a bedroom intended for use by a child
a bedroom for a child

Examples

The twins decorated their child's room with bright colors and playful animal posters.
A small bookshelf in the child's room was filled with fairy tales and picture books.
Every child's room should have a safe night light to make the space feel comforting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursery - a room in a house for a young child or baby
