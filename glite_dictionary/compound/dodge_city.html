Dodge City

1. PROPN

/ dɑdʒ ˈsɪti / (AmE), / dɒdʒ ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Kansas, United States, historically famous as a rowdy frontier cow town.
historical city in Kansas, United States

Examples

Many stories from the Old West are set in Dodge City, emphasizing its reputation as a lawless town.
Tourists flock to Dodge City to experience its Wild West heritage and visit historic sites.
After moving to Dodge City, she became fascinated by its legendary past.
