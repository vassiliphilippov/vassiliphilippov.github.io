Kalaallit Nunaat

1. PROPN

/ kaˈlaːlit nuˈnaːt / (AmE), / kaˈlaːlit nuˈnaːt / (BrE)
The Greenlandic name for Greenland, the world's largest island and an autonomous territory within the Kingdom of Denmark.
Greenlandic name for Greenland

Examples

The people of Kalaallit Nunaat have a rich and unique cultural heritage.
Climate change has had a visible impact on the ice sheets surrounding Kalaallit Nunaat.
Kalaallit Nunaat gained greater self-government from Denmark in 2009.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greenland - the world's largest island, located between the North Atlantic and Arctic Ocean

Etymology

From Greenlandic 'Kalaallit' (Greenlandic people) + 'Nunaat' (land), meaning 'Land of the Greenlanders'.
