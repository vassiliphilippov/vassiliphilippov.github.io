good drunk

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd drʌŋk / (AmE), / ɡʊd drʌŋk / (BrE)
A person who, when intoxicated, behaves in a friendly, pleasant, or entertaining manner rather than causing trouble.
a well-behaved person while drunk
informal

Examples

At parties, he’s known as a good drunk and always makes everyone laugh.
Unlike some people, she’s a good drunk—always cheerful and never aggressive.
He prides himself on being a good drunk who’s fun to be around.

Synset Examples

At parties, he becomes a good drunk, entertaining everyone.
She's a good drunk and never causes trouble.
He's a good drunk, always friendly and fun.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad drunk - a person who becomes unpleasant or difficult when intoxicated
