cotton flannel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑt̬n ˈflæn(ə)l / (AmE), / ˈkɒtən ˈflæn(ə)l / (BrE)
a durable cotton fabric with a soft nap on one side, used especially for clothing and bedding
soft cotton fabric with nap on one side

Examples

She wore a comfortable cotton flannel shirt on the chilly morning.
The sheets were made of cotton flannel for extra warmth in winter.
I prefer pajamas made from cotton flannel because they feel so soft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flannel - a soft woven fabric, typically of wool or cotton
