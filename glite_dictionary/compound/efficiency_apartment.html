efficiency apartment

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfɪʃənsi əˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈfɪʃənsi əˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
A small, furnished apartment consisting of one main room with a kitchenette and a separate bathroom.
small furnished apartment with kitchenette and bathroom

Examples

She decided to rent an efficiency apartment to save money on rent.
The efficiency apartment comes fully furnished and is perfect for a single occupant.
After college, he lived in a downtown efficiency apartment for a year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio apartment - an apartment consisting of one main room serving as living, dining, and bedroom, with a kitchenette and bathroom
