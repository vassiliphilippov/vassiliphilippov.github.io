autocutie moment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtoʊˈkjuːti ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈkjuːti ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A moment that is especially adorable or charming, often causing delight or affection in others.
adorable or charming moment

She captured an autocutie moment on camera when her puppy fell asleep on her lap.

His autocutie moments are the highlight of our family gatherings.
