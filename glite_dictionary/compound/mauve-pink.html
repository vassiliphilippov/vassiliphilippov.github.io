mauve-pink

1. ADJ

/ moʊv pɪŋk / (AmE), / məʊv pɪŋk / (BrE)
A shade of pink tinged with mauve; a pink color with a slight purple hue.
pink with a mauve tint (color)

Examples

She wore a beautiful dress in a soft mauve-pink shade to the party.
The artist chose mauve-pink for the background of her latest painting.
A cluster of mauve-pink roses bloomed brightly in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pink - a pale red color
  • Synonym: mauve - a pale purple color
