card shark

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ʃɑrk / (AmE), / kɑːd ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a person skilled at playing cards, especially one who cheats professionally at card games
expert card player who cheats

Examples

The notorious card shark was finally caught cheating at the high-stakes poker table.
She suspected the newcomer was a card shark because he kept winning impossible hands.
Rumors spread quickly whenever a seasoned card shark entered the casino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: card sharp - a professional card player who cheats at card games
