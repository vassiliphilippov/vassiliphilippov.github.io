sense of resonance

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv ˈrɛzəˌnəns / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈrɛzənəns / (BrE)
a feeling of deep connection or emotional echo, often evoked by something such as music, words, or an experience
feeling of deep emotional connection or echo

Examples

Her words had a sense of resonance that lingered long after the meeting ended.
The novel gave him a sense of resonance with his own life experiences.
Art often creates a sense of resonance in viewers, reaching deeper than words alone can express.

Synset Examples

The music created a sense of resonance in the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resonance - the quality of being resonant; in physics or figuratively in emotion or meaning
