one-sixth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Each of six equal parts into which something is divided; 1/6.
one part out of six equal parts

Examples

She ate one-sixth of the chocolate cake at the party.
The pie was divided into six slices, and each person took one-sixth.
One-sixth of the population voted in the local election.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fraction - a numerical quantity that is not a whole number; expresses part of a whole
  • Degree Higher: one-third - each of three equal parts; 1/3
  • Degree Lower: one-twelfth - each of twelve equal parts; 1/12
