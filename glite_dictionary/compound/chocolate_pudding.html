chocolate pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet, creamy dessert made with chocolate and usually thickened with flour or cornstarch rather than eggs.
sweet, creamy chocolate dessert

Examples

She made a delicious chocolate pudding from scratch for dessert.
The chocolate pudding was rich, creamy, and perfectly sweet.
Would you like some chocolate pudding with your lunch today?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pudding - a sweet dessert; typically made by boiling or steaming
