Siberian crab apple

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈbɪriən kræb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / sɪˈbɪəriən kræb ˈæpəl / (BrE)
An Asian species of wild crab apple tree cultivated for its small, acidic, usually red fruit used in preserves.
species of crab apple tree with small acidic fruit

Examples

The Siberian crab apple produces small, tart fruit that are ideal for making jelly.
Gardeners appreciate the Siberian crab apple for its ornamental blossoms and hardy nature.
I planted a Siberian crab apple tree last spring, and it is already bearing fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - wild apple tree with small, sour fruit
