athletic supporter

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk səˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / əˈθlɛtɪk səˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
A special undergarment worn by men to support and protect the genitals during strenuous physical activity.
undergarment for protecting male genitals in sport

Examples

He wore an athletic supporter to prevent injury during the football game.
All male gymnasts are required to wear an athletic supporter during competitions.
The coach reminded the players to put on their athletic supporters before practice.
