subway fare

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌweɪ fɛr / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ feə / (BrE)
the amount of money required to travel on a subway train
price for subway train travel

Examples

The subway fare increased last year, making commuting slightly more expensive.
You can pay the subway fare with a contactless card at most stations.
Children under five ride for free and do not need to pay the subway fare.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: train fare - the fare charged for riding a train
  • Synonym: bus fare - the fare charged for riding a bus
  • Synonym: subway ticket - a ticket for travel on a subway
