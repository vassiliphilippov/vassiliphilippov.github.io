to untangle one's thoughts

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈtæŋɡəl wʌnz θɔts / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæŋɡl wʌnz θɔːts / (BrE)
to make one's ideas clear and organized, especially after feeling confused or overwhelmed
clarify and organize one's ideas

Examples

Before starting her essay, Maria took a walk to untangle her thoughts.
He found it helpful to write in his journal when trying to untangle his thoughts.
After the stressful meeting, John sat quietly to untangle his thoughts before responding.

Synset Examples

She needed to untangle her thoughts before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarify one's thoughts - to make one's ideas clearer
