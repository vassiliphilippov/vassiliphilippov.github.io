bon mot

1. NOUN

/ bɑn ˈmoʊ / (AmE), / bɒ̃ ˈmɒ / (BrE)
A clever or witty remark; a witticism.
clever or witty remark

Examples

Her acceptance speech was full of bon mots that had the audience laughing.
He is known for always having a bon mot ready in any situation.
The dinner conversation sparkled with bon mots from the guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witticism - a witty remark
  • Synonym: quip - a clever or witty remark

Etymology

From French 'bon mot' meaning 'good word' or 'witticism'.
