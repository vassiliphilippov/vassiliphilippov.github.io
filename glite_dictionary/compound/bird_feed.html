bird feed

1. NOUN

/ bɝd fid / (AmE), / bɜːd fiːd / (BrE)
food provided to birds, typically a mixture of seeds or grains, especially for domestic or wild birds kept outdoors
food mixture for feeding birds

Examples

She refilled the feeder with fresh bird feed every morning to attract songbirds.
Buying bird feed in bulk is more cost-effective if you have many feeders in your garden.
The pet store offers a wide selection of bird feed for different species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birdseed - a mixture of seeds given to birds as food
