cherry pepper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri ˈpɛpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
A type of small, round, usually pungent pepper plant (Capsicum annuum) cultivated for its bright red, cherry-shaped fruit.
small, round, pungent pepper plant

Examples

The salad was topped with sliced cherry peppers for an extra kick of heat.
She planted a cherry pepper bush in her vegetable garden last spring.
Pickled cherry peppers make a tasty addition to antipasto platters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimiento - large, mild, red chili pepper
  • Degree Higher: jalapeño - small, hot green or red chili pepper
