printing press

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪŋ prɛs / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ prɛs / (BrE)
a machine for printing text or images, especially on paper, typically in large quantities
machine for mass printing

Examples

The invention of the printing press revolutionized the spread of knowledge across Europe.
Many museums display replicas of the original Gutenberg printing press.
The local newspaper still uses an old-fashioned printing press for special editions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printer - a person or machine that prints; operator or device for printing
