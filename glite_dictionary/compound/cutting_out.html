cutting out

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
the surgical removal of a part of the body or tissue
surgical removal of body tissue
medical

Examples

The surgeon performed a cutting out of the tumor to prevent it from spreading.
A cutting out of the infected tissue was necessary to save the patient's life.
After the cutting out, the wound was carefully stitched and dressed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excision - the surgical removal of tissue
