seven seas

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛvən ˈsiz / (AmE), / ˌsɛv.ən ˈsiːz / (BrE)
the seas and oceans of the world collectively, often used to refer to all parts of the world that can be reached by sea
all the seas and oceans of the world
informal

Examples

He has traveled the seven seas during his long career as a merchant sailor.
Stories of pirates sailing the seven seas have fascinated readers for centuries.
After years exploring the seven seas, she finally returned home to tell her tales.

Synset Examples

the old salt had sailed the seven seas

Etymology

Originates from ancient and medieval literature, historically referring to various sets of seas in different eras; now used idiomatically for all oceans.
