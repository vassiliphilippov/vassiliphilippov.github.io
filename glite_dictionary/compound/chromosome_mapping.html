chromosome mapping

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɹoʊməˌsoʊm ˈmæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɹəʊməsəʊm ˈmæpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining the positions of genes on a chromosome
genetic process of locating genes on a chromosome
medical

Examples

Advancements in chromosome mapping have helped researchers identify genes linked to inherited diseases.
The team used modern techniques in chromosome mapping to analyze the fruit fly genome.
Accurate chromosome mapping is essential for studying complex genetic disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gene mapping - the determination of the location of genes on a chromosome
