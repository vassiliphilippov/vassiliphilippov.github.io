apple fritter

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl ˈfrɪt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ˈfrɪt.ə / (BrE)
A piece of batter containing sliced apple that is deep-fried and often sweetened, commonly eaten as a dessert or snack.
deep-fried batter with apple chunks

Examples

She bought a freshly baked apple fritter from the bakery for breakfast.
The apple fritter was dusted with cinnamon sugar and served warm.
Have you ever tried making apple fritters at home with your own apples?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fritter - a piece of food battered and fried
