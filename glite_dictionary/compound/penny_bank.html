penny bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛni bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈpɛni baŋk / (BrE)
a small savings container for coins, often used by children and sometimes shaped like a pig, for collecting and saving money
a child's coin bank

Examples

As a child, I loved dropping spare change into my penny bank every week.
Her grandmother gave her a porcelain penny bank shaped like a pig for her birthday.
The old-fashioned penny bank sat on the bookshelf, filled with coins collected over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piggy bank - a small container, often shaped like a pig, for saving coins, especially for children
