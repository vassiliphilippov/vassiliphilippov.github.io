liquid laugh

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd læf / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd lɑːf / (BrE)
a laugh that is smooth, cheerful, and easy-flowing, often perceived as pleasant or melodious
smooth, pleasant-sounding laugh

Examples

Her liquid laugh filled the room and put everyone at ease.
The child's liquid laugh was so sincere that it made me smile instantly.
He was known for his liquid laugh that could melt even the coldest heart.

Synset Examples
