phone plug

1. NOUN

/ foʊn plʌɡ / (AmE), / fəʊn plʌɡ / (BrE)
a plug used to connect a telephone or telephone line to a socket
plug for connecting a telephone

Examples

Make sure the phone plug is firmly inserted into the wall socket.
He replaced the old phone plug with a new one to fix the connection.
Some modern phones no longer require a traditional phone plug for operation.
