Savara language

1. NOUN

/ səˈvɑːrə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈvɑːrə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken by the Savara people in southeastern India, primarily north of Chennai (Madras)
Dravidian language of the Savara people

Examples

The Savara language is classified as part of the Dravidian language family.
Researchers are working to develop educational materials in the Savara language.
Many members of the Savara community strive to preserve the Savara language for future generations.

Etymology

Savara, the name of the ethnic group, and 'language'; Dravidian languages originate in South India.
