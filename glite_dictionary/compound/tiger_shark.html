tiger shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɡɚ ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, dangerous shark of warm seas, distinguished by dark vertical stripes or spots on its body
a large, striped shark species

Examples

The tiger shark is known for its aggressive nature and striped body pattern.
While diving off the coast, we spotted a massive tiger shark lurking near the reef.
Researchers tagged several tiger sharks to study their migration patterns.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: shark - a large fish known for its predatory habits
