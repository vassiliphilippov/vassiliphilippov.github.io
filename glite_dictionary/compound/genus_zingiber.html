genus Zingiber

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈzɪn.dʒɪ.bər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈzɪn.dʒɪ.bə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical Asiatic and Polynesian perennial plants, including the ginger plant, known for their aromatic rhizomes
genus of tropical plants including ginger

Examples

Many species within the genus Zingiber are cultivated for their aromatic rhizomes.
The genus Zingiber is native to tropical regions of Asia and Polynesia.
Ginger, a well-known spice, belongs to the genus Zingiber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ginger - an aromatic spice from the rhizome of Zingiber officinale

Etymology

From Latin 'Zingiber', from Greek 'zingiberis', from Sanskrit 'śṛṅgavera' (ginger).
