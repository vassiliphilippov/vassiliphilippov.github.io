seating room

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːtɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈsiːtɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
an area or space containing seats for several people
area with seats for people

Examples

Please take a seat in the seating room while you wait for your appointment.
The auditorium's seating room can accommodate up to two hundred guests.
There was little seating room available by the time we arrived at the event.

Synset Examples

there is seating for 40 students in this classroom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seating area - an area with seats for people
  • Antonym: standing room - space for people to stand; no seats available
