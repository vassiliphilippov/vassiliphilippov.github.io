hair metal days

1. NOUN

/ hɛr ˈmɛtəl deɪz / (AmE), / heə ˈmɛtəl deɪz / (BrE)
the period in the 1980s characterized by the popularity of glam metal music and bands known for their flashy appearance and elaborate hairstyles
the glam metal era of the 1980s

Examples

Many fans still fondly remember the outrageous fashion of the hair metal days.
MTV played nonstop videos from bands at the height of the hair metal days.
The hair metal days brought big hair, heavy makeup, and roaring guitar solos to the mainstream.

Synset Examples

Their concert was a nostalgic trip back to hair metal days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glam metal - a style of rock music noted for its flamboyant look and catchy hooks
  • Antonym: punk era - the period associated with the punk rock movement

Etymology

From 'hair metal', referring to the flamboyant hairstyles of bands, and 'days', meaning period of time.
