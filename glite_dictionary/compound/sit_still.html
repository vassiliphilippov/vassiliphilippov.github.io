to sit still

1. VERB

/ sɪt stɪl / (AmE), / sɪt stɪl / (BrE)
to remain motionless while sitting
remain motionless while sitting

Examples

The doctor asked the child to sit still while he examined her.
It was difficult for him to sit still during the long lecture.
Please sit still so I can finish tying your shoes.

Synset Examples

You need to sit still during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fidget - to make small movements nervously or restlessly
