rauli beech

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊli ˌbiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈraʊli ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
a large Chilean timber tree (Nothofagus alpina) that produces coarse lumber
Chilean timber tree; Nothofagus alpina

Examples

The furniture was crafted from rauli beech, giving it a unique Chilean character.
Many forests in southern Chile are dominated by the impressive rauli beech tree.
Due to its durability, rauli beech is often chosen for construction lumber in Chile.

Etymology

From Mapudungun (an indigenous language of Chile) 'rauli' for the tree, combined with 'beech' due to its similarity to European beeches.
