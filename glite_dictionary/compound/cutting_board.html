cutting board

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
a flat board, typically made of wood or plastic, used for cutting food such as meat or vegetables
board for cutting food

Examples

Please use the cutting board to slice the tomatoes.
She cleaned the cutting board thoroughly after chopping onions.
A wooden cutting board can last for years if properly maintained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chopping board - a board used for cutting food, especially in British English
