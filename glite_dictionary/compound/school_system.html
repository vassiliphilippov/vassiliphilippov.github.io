school system

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːl ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈskuːl ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The organization and facilities responsible for providing education from kindergarten through high school within a region or country.
organization providing primary and secondary education

Examples

The local school system is known for its high academic standards.
After moving to a new city, the family had to learn about the new school system.
Many parents are concerned about funding cuts in the public school system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: education system - the system or structure for providing education within a country or region
