desert paintbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛzɚt ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈdezət ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a plant of the western United States dry lands with erect stems and dense spikes of bright orange to red flowers, commonly known as a paintbrush
wildflower of western US deserts

Examples

We saw a field full of desert paintbrush during our hike in Nevada.
The desert paintbrush adds a splash of red to the arid landscape each spring.
Botanists study the desert paintbrush for its adaptation to dry climates.
