harbor of refuge

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑr.bɚ əv ˈrɛf.judʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.bə əv ˈrɛf.juːdʒ / (BrE)
a place offering safety or shelter from danger or trouble; a sanctuary
safe place or sanctuary in danger

Examples

During the storm, the town became a harbor of refuge for stranded travelers.
For many, the small chapel is a harbor of refuge in times of personal hardship.
The port was designed as a harbor of refuge for ships caught in sudden bad weather.

Synset Examples

During the storm, the bay became a harbor of refuge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haven - a place of safety or refuge
