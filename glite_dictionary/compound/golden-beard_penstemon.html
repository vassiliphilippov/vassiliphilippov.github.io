golden-beard penstemon

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldn bɪrd ˈpɛnstəmɒn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən bɪəd ˈpɛnstɪmɒn / (BrE)
a plant native to the southwestern United States, with long open clusters of scarlet flowers and yellow hairs on the lower lip
plant with scarlet flowers and yellow-haired lip

Examples

The golden-beard penstemon brightened the desert landscape with its vivid scarlet blooms.
Gardeners prize the golden-beard penstemon for attracting hummingbirds with its colorful flowers.
Hikers spotted several golden-beard penstemon along the rocky trail in New Mexico.
