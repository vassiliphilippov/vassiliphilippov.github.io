feminist movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛmənɪst ˈmuvmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪnɪst ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a social and political movement advocating for equal rights and opportunities for women
movement for women's equal rights

Examples

The feminist movement gained significant momentum during the 1960s and 1970s.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of the feminist movement in politics.
Many changes in workplace policies were driven by the feminist movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: women's liberation - movement for women's rights and freedom
