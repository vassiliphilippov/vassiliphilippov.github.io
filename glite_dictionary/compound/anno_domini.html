anno Domini

1. ADV

/ ˈænoʊ ˈdɑːmɪˌni / (AmE), / ˈænəʊ ˈdɒmɪni / (BrE)
used to indicate that a year is after the traditional date of the birth of Christ; often abbreviated as AD.
CE era dating term (after Christ's birth)

Examples

The temple was built in anno Domini 450.
Many historical documents are dated using the anno Domini system.
The year 2023 anno Domini marks two millennia since the birth of Christ.

Synset Examples

in AD 200

Related Terms

  • Antonym: before Christ - used to indicate years before the traditional date of Christ's birth (BC)
  • Synonym: Common Era - term equivalent to AD, used as a secular alternative

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'anno Domini' meaning 'in the year of the Lord'.
