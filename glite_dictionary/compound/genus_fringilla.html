genus Fringilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs frɪnˈdʒɪlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs frɪnˈdʒɪlə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Fringillidae, including birds such as the chaffinch and the brambling.
a bird genus (chaffinch, brambling, etc.)

Examples

The genus Fringilla includes birds commonly found in Europe and Asia.
Scientists recently described a new species within the genus Fringilla.
The chaffinch is perhaps the most well-known member of the genus Fringilla.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fringillidae - the biological family of true finches
  • Synonym: chaffinch - a small Old World bird of the genus Fringilla

Etymology

From Latin 'fringilla', meaning 'chaffinch'.
