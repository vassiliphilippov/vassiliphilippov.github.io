Bill W. group

1. NOUN

/ bɪl dʌbəl.juː ɡruːp / (AmE), / bɪl dʌbəl.juː ɡruːp / (BrE)
a support group for individuals struggling with addiction, especially Alcoholics Anonymous or similar 12-step programs
support group for addiction recovery

Examples

After years of denial, he finally joined a Bill W. group to seek help for his addiction.
Many people credit their recovery to the support they receive at a Bill W. group.
She attends a weekly Bill W. group to share her experiences and stay sober.

Synset Examples

She found support from her friend of Bill W. group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alcoholics Anonymous - an organization for people who want to stop drinking alcohol

Etymology

Refers to Bill W., the co-founder of Alcoholics Anonymous (Bill Wilson).
