filmy fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmi fɝn / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmi fɜːn / (BrE)
A tropical or subtropical fern of the genus Hymenophyllum, characterized by its very thin, translucent leaves.
tropical fern with translucent leaves

Examples

The filmy fern requires a humid environment to thrive indoors.
Botanists discovered a rare species of filmy fern deep in the rainforest.
Some filmy ferns grow on mossy branches, absorbing moisture from the air.

Etymology

From 'filmy' meaning thin and delicate, and 'fern'; refers to the plant's delicate translucent fronds.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmi fɝn / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmi fɜːn / (BrE)
Any fern of the genus Trichomanes, characterized by large, often translucent fronds, typically growing epiphytically on trees or terrestrially on mossy banks.
fern with translucent fronds, genus Trichomanes

Examples

The delicate fronds of the filmy fern glistened with moisture in the shaded forest.
Botanists admired how the filmy fern clung to the mossy branches high above the ground.
She carefully transplanted the rare filmy fern into a terrarium to observe its growth.
