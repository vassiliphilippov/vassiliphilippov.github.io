cleaning woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman employed to clean houses or offices, typically on a regular basis
woman who cleans homes or offices for pay

Examples

The cleaning woman arrives every Monday morning to tidy the house.
After everyone left, the cleaning woman began to sweep the floors and empty the bins.
They hired a cleaning woman to help keep the office neat and organized.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaning lady - woman employed to clean houses, informal term
  • Antonym: cleaning man - man employed to clean houses or offices
