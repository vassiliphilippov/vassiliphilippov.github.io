mountain daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A common name for certain species of boreal or alpine sandwort, small flowering plants found in mountainous or northern regions.
alpine or boreal sandwort plant

Examples

The mountain daisy thrives in cold, high-altitude meadows.
During the summer, mountain daisies carpet the slopes with tiny white flowers.
Hikers admired the resilience of the mountain daisy growing between rocks.
