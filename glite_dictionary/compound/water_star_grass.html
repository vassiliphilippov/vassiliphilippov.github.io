water star grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A North American aquatic plant with slender grassy leaves and yellow, star-shaped flowers.
aquatic plant with yellow star-shaped flowers

Examples

The pond was dotted with clusters of water star grass in full bloom.
We identified the bright yellow flowers as water star grass during our field survey.
Many aquatic birds nest among the thick stands of water star grass.
