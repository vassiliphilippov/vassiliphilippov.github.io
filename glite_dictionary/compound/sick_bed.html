sick bed

1. NOUN

/ sɪk ˌbɛd / (AmE), / sɪk ˌbɛd / (BrE)
the bed on which a person lies when they are ill or recovering from illness
bed used by a sick person

Examples

She spent two weeks confined to her sick bed after catching the flu.
From his sick bed, he dictated letters to his secretary.
The children visited their grandmother at her sick bed every afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deathbed - the bed in which someone dies or is dying
