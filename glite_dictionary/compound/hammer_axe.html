hammer axe

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmɚ æks / (AmE), / ˈhamə æks / (BrE)
a weapon featuring a hammer head on one side and an axe blade on the other, used historically in combat
weapon with a hammer and axe head

Examples

The medieval knight wielded a hammer axe to break through his opponent's armor.
Museum visitors admired the ornate hammer axe displayed among other ancient weapons.
A hammer axe combines the functionality of a smashing tool and a cutting blade.
