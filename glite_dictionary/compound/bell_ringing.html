bell ringing

1. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / bɛl ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the sound produced by the playing of a set of bells, especially as part of a musical or ceremonial performance
sound produced by playing bells

Examples

The bell ringing could be heard across the entire village during the festival.
She stopped to listen to the joyful bell ringing coming from the church tower.
At noon, the bell ringing signaled the end of the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiming - the ringing or sounding of bells
  • Antonym: silence - the complete absence of sound

2. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈɹɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / bɛl ˈɹɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or art of playing a set of bells, usually those hung in a tower
playing bells, usually in a tower

Examples

Every Sunday morning, the sound of bell ringing fills the village.
He has been passionate about bell ringing since his teenage years.
The church offers lessons in bell ringing for interested members of the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campanology - the study or art of bell ringing
