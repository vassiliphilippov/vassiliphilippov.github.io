weather deck

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ dɛk / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə dɛk / (BrE)
the upper deck of a ship that is exposed to the weather and protects the deck below
exposed upper deck of a ship

Examples

The crew quickly cleared the weather deck as the storm approached.
Passengers gathered on the weather deck to enjoy the sea breeze and sunshine.
Heavy cargo is often secured on the weather deck for easy access and ventilation.
