glorious sight

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɔriəs saɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːriəs saɪt / (BrE)
a view or scene that is extremely beautiful and impressive
beautiful and impressive view or scene

Examples

The city skyline at night was a glorious sight from the top of the hill.
After days of hiking, the waterfall was a glorious sight for the weary group.
The parade was a glorious sight, with colorful floats and lively music.

Synset Examples

The sunset was a glorious sight over the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacle - an impressive or remarkable sight
  • Antonym: eyesore - something very unpleasant or ugly to look at
