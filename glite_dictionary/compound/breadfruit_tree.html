breadfruit tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛdˌfrut ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌfruːt ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to the Pacific islands, producing large, starchy edible fruit with a texture similar to bread when cooked.
tropical tree with starchy edible fruit

Examples

The breadfruit tree supplied food for many generations of islanders.
We planted a breadfruit tree in our backyard to enjoy its nutritious fruit.
A mature breadfruit tree can yield hundreds of fruits each season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breadfruit - the large, starchy fruit of the breadfruit tree
