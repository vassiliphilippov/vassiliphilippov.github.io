casemaking clothes moth

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ kloʊðz mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsˌmeɪkɪŋ kləʊðz mɒθ / (BrE)
a small moth whose larvae live in tubes made of food material fastened with silk, often damaging fabrics
moth whose larvae damage fabrics with silk cases

Examples

The casemaking clothes moth is responsible for many holes found in wool sweaters and carpets.
Conservation labs use special techniques to protect textiles from the casemaking clothes moth.
A single casemaking clothes moth infestation can destroy valuable fabric collections if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes moth - moth that damages clothing and fabrics
