low price

1. NOUN

/ loʊ praɪs / (AmE), / ləʊ praɪs / (BrE)
a price that is lower than average or expected, making something affordable or cheap
an affordable or cheap price

Examples

During the holiday season, stores often advertise their low prices to attract more customers.
We chose this supermarket because of its consistently low prices on groceries.
Many travelers look for low price options when booking flights and hotels.

Synset Examples

Mallwart is known for its low prices and wide selection.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high price - a price that is higher than average or expected
  • Synonym: cheap - inexpensive; costing little money
