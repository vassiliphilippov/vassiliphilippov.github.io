travel kit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl kɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl kɪt / (BrE)
a small bag or case containing toiletries and personal care items for use while traveling
bag for toiletries when traveling

Examples

I always pack my travel kit before leaving for a trip.
She forgot her travel kit at the hotel, so she had to buy new toiletries.
His travel kit contains a toothbrush, toothpaste, and a razor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toiletry bag - a small container for holding toiletries
