bread board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛd bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛd bɔːd / (BrE)
a wooden or plastic board used for kneading dough or slicing bread
board used to knead or slice bread

Examples

She placed the warm loaf on the bread board before slicing it.
The bread board is covered in flour after kneading the dough.
Every kitchen should have a clean bread board ready for baking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting board - a board used as a surface for cutting food
