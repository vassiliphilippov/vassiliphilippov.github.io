guide dog

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪd dɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡaɪd dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to assist and lead people who are blind or visually impaired
dog trained to help the blind

Examples

The guide dog gently led its owner across the busy intersection.
After months of training, her guide dog was finally ready to help her navigate daily life.
Traveling with a guide dog gives people with vision loss much more independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seeing eye dog - dog trained to guide blind people (primarily US)
