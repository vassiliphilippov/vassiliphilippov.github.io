pumping station

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmpɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a building or facility in which pumps are installed and operated, typically to move water, sewage, or other fluids from one place to another
facility with pumps for moving fluids

Examples

The city built a new pumping station to improve its water supply system.
Heavy rain caused the pumping station to work at full capacity all night.
Engineers inspected the old pumping station for signs of corrosion and leaks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterworks - a building or system for supplying water
  • Synonym: power station - a facility for generating electricity
