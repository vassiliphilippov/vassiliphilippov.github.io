border district

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdər ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
an area or administrative district situated along or straddling the border between two countries or regions
district along a national or regional border

Examples

Tensions rose in the border district following the announcement of new trade policies.
Many families in the border district have relatives on both sides of the boundary.
Tourism is growing in the border district due to its unique blend of cultures.

Synset Examples

the Welsh marches between England and Wales

Related Terms

  • Synonym: border - the dividing line or frontier between two countries or regions
