sun-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌwɜːrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
The religious practice of worshipping the sun as a deity.
worship of the sun as a deity

Examples

Ancient Egyptians are well known for their sun-worship, especially the reverence for the god Ra.
The practice of sun-worship can be found in various ancient cultures around the world.
In early civilizations, sun-worship was often linked to agriculture and seasonal cycles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heliolatry - worship of the sun, especially in ancient religions
