mahoosive burger

1. NOUN

/ məˈhuːsɪv ˈbɝːɡɚ / (AmE), / məˈhuːsɪv ˈbɜːɡə / (BrE)
an extremely large burger, especially referred to humorously or in informal contexts
very large burger (informal)
informal british

Examples

The mahoosive burger at the fair comes with three beef patties and extra cheese.
He couldn't finish the mahoosive burger he ordered for lunch.
Their special this week is a mahoosive burger served with a mountain of fries.

Synset Examples

The mahoosive burger at the restaurant was too much to handle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat in a split bun
  • Synonym: massive - exceptionally large in size, quantity, or extent

Etymology

Blend of 'massive' and playful extension (originating as British slang 'mahoosive').
