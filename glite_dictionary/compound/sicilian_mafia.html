Sicilian Mafia

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈsɪliən ˈmɑːfiə / (AmE), / sɪˈsɪliən ˈmæfiə / (BrE)
an organized criminal society originating in Sicily, Italy, known for its secretive operations, influence, and illegal activities
Italian criminal organization from Sicily

Examples

The Sicilian Mafia has been the subject of movies and books for decades.
Law enforcement agencies have struggled to dismantle the Sicilian Mafia for many years.
Several witnesses testified about the power and influence of the Sicilian Mafia in Sicily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mafia - a criminal organization operating as a secret society

Etymology

From 'Sicilian', relating to Sicily, and Italian 'mafia', meaning organized crime syndicate.
