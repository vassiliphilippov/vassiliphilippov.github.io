brown thrasher

1. NOUN

/ braʊn ˈθræʃər / (AmE), / braʊn ˈθraʃə / (BrE)
a large songbird of eastern North America with reddish-brown plumage and a long tail
large reddish-brown songbird of North America

Examples

The brown thrasher is easily recognized by its bright yellow eyes and bold streaks on its chest.
A brown thrasher was singing loudly from the top of the hedge early this morning.
Many people attract the brown thrasher to their yards by planting dense shrubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrasher - A type of songbird related to the mockingbird
