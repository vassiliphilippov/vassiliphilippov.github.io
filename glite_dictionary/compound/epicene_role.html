epicene role

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪˌsin roʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌsiːn rəʊl / (BrE)
A role or character that is not specifically identified as male or female; gender-neutral.
gender-neutral role or character

Examples

The actor embraced the epicene role, making the character relatable to everyone.
Modern theater often includes at least one epicene role to challenge traditional gender norms.
Casting directors are increasingly interested in performers who can play an epicene role convincingly.

Synset Examples

The actor played an epicene role with great finesse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: androgynous - having both male and female characteristics or qualities

Etymology

From 'epicene', from Latin 'epicoenus', meaning of both sexes or neither.
