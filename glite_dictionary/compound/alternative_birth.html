alternative birth

1. NOUN

/ ɔlˈtɝnətɪv bɝθ / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv bɜːθ / (BrE)
a method of childbirth that avoids intrusive high-tech medical interventions in favor of more natural and home-like settings
natural childbirth method avoiding high-tech medicine

Examples

Some parents choose alternative birth options to have a more comfortable and less medicalized experience.
Midwives often support women seeking alternative birth in birth centers or at home.
The rise in alternative birth practices reflects growing interest in natural approaches to childbirth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: natural childbirth - childbirth with minimal medical intervention
