lamp housing

1. NOUN

/ læmp ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / læmp ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
a protective enclosure or frame that holds and supports a lamp, especially in equipment such as projectors or lighting fixtures
enclosure holding a lamp

Examples

The technician opened the lamp housing to replace the burnt-out bulb.
Dust inside the lamp housing can reduce the brightness of the projection.
Modern projectors feature an easily accessible lamp housing for quick maintenance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lampholder - a device that holds a lamp or light bulb
