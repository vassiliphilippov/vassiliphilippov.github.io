peelie snack

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːli snæk / (AmE), / ˈpiːli snæk / (BrE)
A snack, such as a fruit or packaged food, that is easy to peel or eat with little preparation.
snack easy to peel or eat
informal

Examples

Before going on a picnic, she packed several peelie snacks like bananas and oranges.
He prefers peelie snacks because they're less messy for school lunches.
Grapes and tangerines are my favorite peelie snacks during summer.

Synset Examples

She loves her peelie snacks for movie night.
