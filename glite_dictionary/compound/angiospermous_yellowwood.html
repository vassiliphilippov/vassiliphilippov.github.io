angiospermous yellowwood

1. NOUN

/ ˌændʒioʊˈspɝːməs ˈjɛloʊˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˌændʒɪəˈspɜːməs ˈjɛləʊˌwʊd / (BrE)
any of various angiospermous (flowering) trees that have yellow-colored wood, as distinguished from coniferous species
angiospermous tree with yellow wood

Examples

The angiospermous yellowwood is prized in carpentry for the warm hue of its timber.
Several species classified as angiospermous yellowwood grow in the southern hemisphere.
Botanists differentiate angiospermous yellowwood from coniferous yellowwoods based on reproductive traits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellowwood - any tree with yellow-colored wood

Etymology

From 'angiosperm' (flowering plant) + 'yellowwood' (tree with yellow timber).
