retail store

1. NOUN

/ ˈriː.teɪl stɔːr / (AmE), / ˈriː.teɪl stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or business where goods are sold directly to consumers, usually in small quantities.
shop selling goods to consumers

Examples

The new retail store on Main Street attracts hundreds of customers every day.
Many people prefer to shop in a retail store rather than online.
This company plans to open several new retail stores across the country next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shop - a place where goods are sold to customers
  • Degree Higher: department store - a large shop divided into departments selling a variety of goods
