screw auger

1. NOUN

/ skru ˈɔɡər / (AmE), / skruː ˈɔːɡə / (BrE)
a hand tool with a spiral blade used for boring holes in wood or other materials
hand tool for boring holes

Examples

He used a screw auger to bore a clean hole in the plank.
The carpenter kept his screw auger sharp for easy drilling.
Before power drills, a screw auger was an essential tool in woodworking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auger - a tool for boring holes, typically in wood
