Stanford University

1. PROPN

/ ˈstæn.fərd juˈnɪ.vɚ.sɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈstan.fəd juːˈnɪ.və.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
A prestigious private university located in Stanford, California, known for research and academic excellence.
prestigious university in California

Examples

Many students aspire to study at Stanford University because of its reputation for academic excellence.
After graduating from Stanford University, she began a successful career in technology.
The annual conference at Stanford University attracts researchers from around the world.

Etymology

Named after Leland Stanford, its founder.
