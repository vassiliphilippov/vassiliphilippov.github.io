dove's foot geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈdəʊvz ˌfʊt dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈdəʊvz fʊt dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a small-flowered western geranium (Geranium molle) with pink blossoms, commonly found as a weed in lawns and vacant lots
a small-flowered wild geranium weed

Examples

The dove's foot geranium quickly spread across the vacant lot, covering the ground with small pink flowers.
Gardeners often remove dove's foot geranium to keep their lawns tidy and free of weeds.
I found a patch of dove's foot geranium growing beside the footpath during my morning walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - a genus of plants with lobed leaves and typically pink or purple flowers
