main office

1. NOUN

/ meɪn ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / meɪn ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the office that serves as the administrative center or headquarters of an organization or enterprise
administrative headquarters of an organization

Examples

The main office relocated to the city center to be closer to clients.
If you need administrative support, please contact the main office.
The company's main office oversees all operations in the region.

Synset Examples

many companies have their headquarters in New York

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - the place where most of the important functions of an organization are coordinated
