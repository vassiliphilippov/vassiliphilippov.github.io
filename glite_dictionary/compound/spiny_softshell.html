spiny softshell

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪni ˈsɔftˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˈsɒftˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a freshwater turtle native to the western United States, recognized by its soft, warty shell and preference for quiet water
North American freshwater turtle with a warty shell

Examples

The spiny softshell is often found basking on sandy riverbanks during the summer.
We spotted a young spiny softshell swimming near the reeds in the slow-moving creek.
Unlike other turtles, the spiny softshell can move surprisingly fast on land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softshell turtle - any turtle with a soft, leathery shell
