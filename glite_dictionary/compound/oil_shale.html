oil shale

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ʃeɪl / (AmE), / ɔɪl ʃeɪl / (BrE)
a type of sedimentary rock from which oil can be obtained by heating
rock yielding oil when heated

Examples

Many countries are researching ways to extract oil from oil shale as an alternative energy source.
The region is known for its vast deposits of oil shale.
Processing oil shale can have significant environmental impacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shale - a fine-grained sedimentary rock that splits into thin layers
