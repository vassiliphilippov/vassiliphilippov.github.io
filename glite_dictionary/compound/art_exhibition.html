art exhibition

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˌɛksəˈbɪʃən / (AmE), / ɑːt ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən / (BrE)
an event where works of art, such as paintings or sculptures, are displayed for public viewing
event displaying works of art

Examples

The local gallery is hosting an art exhibition featuring contemporary paintings this weekend.
She submitted her sculpture to the annual art exhibition at the museum.
The art exhibition attracted visitors from all over the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhibition - a public display of items or works for viewing
