physical exertion

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ɪɡˈzɝːʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ɪɡˈzɜːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the use of physical effort or energy, especially to keep fit or through strenuous activity
use of physical effort or energy

Examples

After hours of physical exertion, she finally reached the top of the mountain.
The job involves a great deal of physical exertion every day.
He avoided any unnecessary physical exertion while recovering from surgery.

Synset Examples

the doctor recommended regular exercise
he did some exercising
the physical exertion required by his work kept him fit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - activity to improve physical fitness
  • Antonym: rest - state of relaxing and not using physical effort
