money plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌni plænt / (AmE), / ˈmʌni plɑːnt / (BrE)
a southeastern European plant (Lunaria annua) grown for its fragrant purplish flowers and distinctive round, flat, silver-white seedpods often used in decoration
ornamental plant with silvery seed pods

Examples

Money plant is popular among gardeners for its attractive, silvery seedpods used in dried floral arrangements.
The money plant blooms in late spring, displaying clusters of delicate purple flowers.
After harvesting the pods from the money plant, she used them to decorate her living room vase.
