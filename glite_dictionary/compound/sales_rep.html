sales rep

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz rɛp / (AmE), / seɪlz rɛp / (BrE)
A person who is employed to represent a company and sell its products or services to customers, either in person or by contacting them directly.
person who sells products for a company

Examples

The sales rep visited several clients in one afternoon.
If you have any questions, you can ask our sales rep for more information.
Each sales rep is assigned a specific territory to manage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales representative - a person who sells products for a company, formal form of sales rep
  • Antonym: buyer - a person who purchases goods or services, often from a sales rep

Etymology

Abbreviation of "sales representative".
