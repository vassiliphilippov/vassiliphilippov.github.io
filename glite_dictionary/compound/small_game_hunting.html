small game hunting

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ɡeɪm ˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / smɔːl ɡeɪm ˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the pursuit or shooting of small wild animals or birds for sport or food
hunting small wild animals or birds

Examples

The national park has designated areas for small game hunting during certain months.
Regulations around small game hunting are stricter to protect the wildlife population.
He took up small game hunting as a hobby after moving to the countryside.

Synset Examples

The area is known for its small game hunting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: big game hunting - the pursuit or killing of large wild animals for sport, often contrasted with small game hunting
