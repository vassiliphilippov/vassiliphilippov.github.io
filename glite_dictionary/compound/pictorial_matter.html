pictorial matter

1. NOUN

/ pɪkˈtɔriəl ˈmætər / (AmE), / pɪkˈtɔːriəl ˈmatə / (BrE)
illustrations, photographs, or other images included in a text to decorate or clarify it
images or illustrations in a text

Examples

The textbook contains a wealth of pictorial matter to support the written explanations.
Pictorial matter in the magazine made the articles more engaging for readers.
Authors often rely on pictorial matter to clarify complex scientific concepts.

Synset Examples

the dictionary had many pictures

Related Terms
