bottle opener

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑt̬l ˌoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˌəʊpənə / (BrE)
A device used to remove caps or corks from bottles.
tool for opening bottles

Examples

He used a bottle opener to pop the cap off the soda.
The drawer is full of kitchen tools like the bottle opener and corkscrew.
I couldn't find the bottle opener, so I had to improvise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corkscrew - a tool for pulling corks from bottles, especially wine bottles
  • Synonym: can opener - a device used to open tin cans
