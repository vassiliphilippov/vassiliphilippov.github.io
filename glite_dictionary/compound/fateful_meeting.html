fateful meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪtfəl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtfəl ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an encounter that has a significant or decisive impact on future events, often changing the course of one's life
significant or life-changing encounter

Examples

Their lives changed forever after that fateful meeting.
The story began with a fateful meeting in a quiet café.
Historians debate whether their fateful meeting was planned or accidental.

Synset Examples

Their lives became entangled after that fateful meeting.
