reigning beauty queen

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.nɪŋ ˈbjuː.ti kwiːn / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.nɪŋ ˈbjuː.ti kwiːn / (BrE)
the current titleholder of a beauty contest
current winner of a beauty contest

Examples

After winning the Miss Universe pageant, she became the reigning beauty queen of her country.
The reigning beauty queen will crown her successor at tomorrow's ceremony.
As the reigning beauty queen, she attended many charity events throughout the year.

Synset Examples

Everyone admired her, the reigning beauty queen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty queen - winner of a beauty contest
  • Antonym: runner-up - someone who finishes in second place in a contest
