parts inventory

1. NOUN

/ pɑrts ɪnˈvɛntɒri / (AmE), / pɑːts ɪnˈvɛntəri / (BrE)
A list or stock of replacement parts kept on hand, especially for equipment or machinery.
stock of replacement parts on hand

Examples

The manager checked the parts inventory before placing an order for new components.
Our company maintains a large parts inventory to minimize downtime during repairs.
You can access the parts inventory database to see what's available for your machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inventory - a complete list of items such as property, goods in stock, or the contents of a building
