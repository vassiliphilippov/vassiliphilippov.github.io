stocking filler

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑkɪŋ ˌfɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪŋ ˌfɪlə / (BrE)
a small present, typically inexpensive, placed in a Christmas stocking
small Christmas stocking gift

Examples

She bought several stocking fillers for her children to open on Christmas morning.
Chocolate coins are a classic stocking filler that children love.
We need to find some affordable stocking fillers before our holiday celebrations.
