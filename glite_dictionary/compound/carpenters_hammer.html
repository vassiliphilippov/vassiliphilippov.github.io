carpenter's hammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrpəntərz ˈhæmər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəntəz ˈhamə / (BrE)
a hammer with a split, curved end used for pulling nails
hammer with a split end for nails

Examples

He used a carpenter's hammer to pull the old nails from the floorboards.
A sturdy carpenter's hammer is essential for most home improvement projects.
The toolbox included a carpenter's hammer, a saw, and a tape measure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: claw hammer - a hammer with a curved, forked end used for pulling nails
