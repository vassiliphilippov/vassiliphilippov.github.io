sewer main

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːər ˌmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsjuːə ˌmeɪn / (BrE)
the principal pipe or conduit in a sewage system that carries wastewater to a treatment facility or disposal point
principal pipe in a sewage system

Examples

The city had to repair the sewer main after a major leak was discovered downtown.
A blockage in the sewer main caused flooding in several neighborhoods.
Engineers are inspecting the old sewer main to determine if it needs replacement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewer line - a pipe in a sewage system
