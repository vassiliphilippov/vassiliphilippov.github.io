extension course

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɛnʃən kɔrs / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən kɔːs / (BrE)
a course of study offered by a school or college, typically for adults, that extends education beyond the regular curriculum, often through evening or part-time classes
continuing education course for adults

Examples

She enrolled in an extension course at the local university to improve her writing skills.
The college offers a wide range of extension courses in the evenings for working adults.
After retiring, he decided to take an extension course in art history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adult education - educational programs intended for adults after formal schooling
