black economy

1. NOUN

/ blæk ɪˈkɑːnəmi / (AmE), / blæk ɪˈkɒnəmi / (BrE)
the part of a country's economic activity that is not recorded or regulated by the government, such as unreported cash transactions or illegal trade
unrecorded or illegal economic activity

Examples

Many small businesses operate partly within the black economy to avoid taxes.
The government introduced new policies to crack down on the black economy.
It is difficult to estimate the true size of the black economy in any country.

Synset Examples

no one knows how large the black economy really is

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underground economy - a synonym for the black economy; also refers to hidden or unreported economic activity
  • Antonym: official economy - the part of the economy that is regulated and recorded by the government
