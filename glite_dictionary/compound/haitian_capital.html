Haitian capital

1. NOUN

/ ˌheɪʃən ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌheɪʃən ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Haiti, known as Port-au-Prince
capital of Haiti

Examples

The Haitian capital was severely damaged by the earthquake in 2010.
Many international organizations are based in the Haitian capital.
Tourism in the Haitian capital has been increasing in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Port-au-Prince - the capital city of Haiti
