best-selling

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
Very popular and bought by many people, especially in reference to books, music, or other products.
popular and selling in great numbers

Examples

Her best-selling cookbook has been translated into several languages.
The author released a best-selling mystery novel last year.
That movie's soundtrack quickly became a best-selling album.

Synset Examples

a best-selling novel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bestseller - a product, especially a book, that sells in very large numbers

Etymology

Formed from 'best' + present participle 'selling', indicating the highest level of sales.
