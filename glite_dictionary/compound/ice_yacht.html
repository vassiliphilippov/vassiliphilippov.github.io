ice yacht

1. NOUN

/ aɪs jɑt / (AmE), / aɪs jɒt / (BrE)
a sailing vessel fitted with runners and a cross-shaped frame, designed for traveling across ice
sailing vessel for travel on ice

Examples

The crew assembled their ice yacht on the frozen lake before setting sail.
Racing enthusiasts gathered annually to compete with their sleek ice yachts.
He described the exhilarating speed he experienced while piloting an ice yacht across the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iceboat - a boat with runners for traveling over ice
