dress whites

1. NOUN

/ drɛs waɪts / (AmE), / drɛs waɪts / (BrE)
A formal white uniform worn by members of the military for ceremonial or formal occasions.
formal white military uniform

Examples

The officers arrived at the ceremony in their dress whites, looking sharp and dignified.
During the parade, each sailor wore his dress whites with pride.
She carefully pressed her husband's dress whites before his promotion ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dress blues - formal blue military uniform for ceremonial or formal occasions
