apple turnover

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl ˈtɝː.noʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ˈtɜː.nəʊ.və / (BrE)
A pastry made by wrapping apple filling in dough and baking it, typically in a triangular or semicircular shape.
pastry with apple filling

Examples

She picked up a warm apple turnover from the bakery for breakfast.
The apple turnover was filled with spiced apples and a flaky crust.
Would you like an apple turnover with your coffee this morning?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnover - a pastry with a sweet or savory filling folded over and baked
