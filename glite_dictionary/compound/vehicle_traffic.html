vehicle traffic

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːɪkəl ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / ˈviːɪkl̩ ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the flow or movement of vehicles coming and going in a particular area at a given time
the flow of vehicles in an area

Examples

Morning vehicle traffic often causes delays during rush hour in the city center.
New road construction aims to reduce heavy vehicle traffic on the main highway.
Due to the festival, vehicle traffic is restricted in the downtown area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - the movement of vehicles along roads
