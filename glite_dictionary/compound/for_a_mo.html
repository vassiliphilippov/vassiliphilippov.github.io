for a mo

1. ADP

/ fər ə moʊ / (AmE), / fə ə məʊ / (BrE)
for a short time; for a moment.
for a short time
informal british

Examples

I stepped outside for a mo to get some fresh air.
Could you watch my bag for a mo while I use the restroom?
He’ll be with you for a mo before heading off to the meeting.

Synset Examples

Let's take a break for a mo and regroup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for a moment - for a brief period of time

Etymology

Shortening of 'moment' to 'mo', informal British usage dating from the early 20th century.
