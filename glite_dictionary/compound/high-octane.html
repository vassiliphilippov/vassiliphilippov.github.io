high-octane

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈɑk.teɪn / (AmE), / haɪ ˈɒk.teɪn / (BrE)
Very energetic, forceful, or dynamic.
very energetic or forceful

Examples

Working with a high-octane team can be both thrilling and exhausting at times.
Her high-octane performance left the audience in awe.
The company launched a high-octane ad campaign to boost sales.

Synset Examples

a high-octane sales manager

high-powered executives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-powered - very influential or energetic
  • Synonym: high-voltage - very energetic or exciting

Etymology

Originally referring to fuel with a high octane rating (indicating power); used figuratively for energy or forcefulness.
