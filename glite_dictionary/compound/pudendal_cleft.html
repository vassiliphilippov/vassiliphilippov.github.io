pudendal cleft

1. NOUN

/ pjuːˈdɛndəl klɛft / (AmE), / pjuːˈdɛndəl klɛft / (BrE)
the anatomical fissure between the labia majora in the female external genitalia
fissure between the labia majora
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the pudendal cleft for any signs of irritation or infection.
Surgical procedures in the area of the pudendal cleft require careful handling to prevent injury.
Personal hygiene is especially important around the pudendal cleft to maintain health.

Etymology

From Latin 'pudenda' (external genital organs) and 'cleft' meaning a split or fissure.
