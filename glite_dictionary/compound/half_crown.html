half crown

1. NOUN

/ hæf kraʊn / (AmE), / hɑːf kraʊn / (BrE)
a former British coin worth two shillings and sixpence, equivalent to one-eighth of a pound
former British coin (2s 6d)
british archaic

Examples

My grandfather still keeps a half crown from his childhood as a memento.
In Victorian England, a half crown could buy a decent meal.
She discovered an old half crown while cleaning out her attic.
