liver sausage

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɚ ˌsɔsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˌsɒsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a type of sausage made with ground liver, often mixed with spices and other ingredients
sausage made with ground liver

Examples

She spread a thick slice of liver sausage on her rye bread for breakfast.
Many traditional German recipes include liver sausage as a key ingredient.
You can find liver sausage in the deli section of most supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liverwurst - a similar type of sausage made with liver, especially in German cuisine
