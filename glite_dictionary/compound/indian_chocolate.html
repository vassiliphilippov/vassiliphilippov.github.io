Indian chocolate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɑklət / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A perennial plant of northern temperate regions, having pinnate leaves and a few nodding flowers with a brown-purple calyx and orange and pink petals.
perennial plant with nodding brown-purple flowers

Examples

The garden featured a cluster of Indian chocolate blooming under the shade trees.
Botanists admire the unique color contrast in Indian chocolate flowers each spring.
We learned to identify Indian chocolate by its pinnate leaves and nodding blossoms.
