Libra the Scales

1. PROPN

/ ˈliː.brə ðə skeɪlz / (AmE), / ˈliː.brə ðə skeɪlz / (BrE)
the seventh sign of the zodiac, represented as a pair of scales, with the sun passing through it approximately from September 23 to October 22.
the seventh zodiac sign (the Scales)

Examples

People born under Libra the Scales are thought to seek harmony and balance in life.
The sun enters Libra the Scales around the end of September each year.
Astrology enthusiasts often discuss the qualities associated with Libra the Scales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Libra - the seventh sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin 'Libra' meaning 'scales' or 'balance', used in Roman astrology.
