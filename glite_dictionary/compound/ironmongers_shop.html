ironmonger's shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərnmʌŋɚz ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈaɪənmʌŋɡəz ʃɒp / (BrE)
(British) A shop that sells hardware such as tools, nails, and household metal goods.
British hardware store
british

Examples

My grandfather used to buy his tools from the local ironmonger's shop in the village.
While visiting England, we stopped at an ironmonger's shop to find a new hammer and some nails.
There is an old-fashioned ironmonger's shop on the corner that still sells traditional household items.

Synset Examples

in Great Britain they used to call a hardware store an ‘ironmonger's shop’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardware store - A shop selling tools and other equipment for home improvement

Etymology

From 'ironmonger' (dealer in iron goods) + 'shop'.
