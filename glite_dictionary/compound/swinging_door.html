swinging door

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔɚ / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
a door mounted on double hinges that allows it to open in both directions
door that opens in both directions

Examples

He pushed through the swinging door that led into the busy kitchen.
The saloon had classic wooden swinging doors at the entrance.
Waiters often prefer a swinging door for convenience when carrying trays.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: revolving door - a door that rotates around a vertical axis, allowing multiple people to enter or exit simultaneously
  • Antonym: sliding door - a door that opens by sliding rather than swinging
