building supply house

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldɪŋ səˈplaɪ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɪldɪŋ səˈplaɪ haʊs / (BrE)
A store where builders and contractors can buy materials and supplies for constructing houses and other structures.
store selling construction materials

Examples

The contractor visited the building supply house to purchase lumber and roofing materials for the new project.
Many local building supply houses offer delivery services for large orders.
She found the exact type of tiles she wanted at a building supply house downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hardware store - a shop selling tools, nails, and building equipment
