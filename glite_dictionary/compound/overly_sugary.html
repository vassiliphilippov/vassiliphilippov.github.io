overly sugary

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvɚli ˈsʌɡəɹi / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəli ˈsʌɡəri / (BrE)
Excessively sweet in taste or, figuratively, excessively sentimental in tone or style.
excessively sweet or sentimental

Examples

The cake was overly sugary, so I could only eat a few bites.
Some romantic movies are criticized for being overly sugary in their portrayal of love.
His comments became overly sugary, making everyone feel a bit uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

The coffee was overly sugary, making it hard to enjoy.
Her writing style is overly sweet, like treacle.
This dessert is too sugary for my taste.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sentimental - marked by excessive emotion or sentimentality
  • Degree Lower: sweet - having the taste of sugar; pleasingly sugary
