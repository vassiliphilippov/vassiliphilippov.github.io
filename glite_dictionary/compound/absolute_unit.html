absolute unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈæb.səˌlut ˈjun.ɪt / (AmE), / ˌæb.səˈluːt ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is remarkably large or impressive, especially in size, strength, or bulk (often used humorously or admiringly)
remarkably large or impressive person or thing
informal slang

Examples

Everyone at the gym calls him an absolute unit because of his incredible strength.
The dog was such an absolute unit that it barely fit through the door.
After seeing that giant pumpkin, people online kept sharing photos of the absolute unit.

Synset Examples

That guy is an absolute unit when he lifts weights.
He's become an absolute unit after months of training.
Look at that absolute unit devouring that pizza!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giant - very large person or thing
  • Antonym: lightweight - person or thing of small physical size or impact

Etymology

Humorous phrase that originated in internet memes (2017), popularized by Twitter.
