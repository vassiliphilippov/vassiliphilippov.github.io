sharptail mola

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑːrp.teɪl ˈmoʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːp.teɪl ˈməʊ.lə / (BrE)
a species of ocean sunfish distinguished by a caudal fin with a central projection
species of ocean sunfish

Examples

The sharptail mola can be identified by its unique fin shape.
Scientists recently observed a sharptail mola off the coast of Peru.
Unlike other sunfish, the sharptail mola has a distinct projection on its tail fin.
