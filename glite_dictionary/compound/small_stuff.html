small stuff

1. NOUN

/ smɔl stʌf / (AmE), / smɔːl stʌf / (BrE)
any light rope used on a ship, typically for minor tasks or lashing
light rope used on ships

Examples

The sailors used small stuff to secure the tarpaulin over the cargo.
He learned to splice small stuff as part of his training on board.
Some of the small stuff had worn out and needed replacing after the storm.
