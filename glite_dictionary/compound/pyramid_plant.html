pyramid plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪrəmɪd plænt / (AmE), / ˈpɪrəmɪd plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tall perennial herb of the genus Frasera, found in warm, dry upland areas of California, Oregon, and Washington.
a tall herb (Frasera genus) of western North America

Examples

Hikers in the Sierra Nevada often spot the distinctive pyramid plant growing along the sunlit slopes.
The pyramid plant is recognized by its tall stalks and clusters of pale green flowers.
Botanists are studying how the pyramid plant adapts to dry, upland environments.
