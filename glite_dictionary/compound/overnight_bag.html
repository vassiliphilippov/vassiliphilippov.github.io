overnight bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt bæg / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈnaɪt bæg / (BrE)
a small bag used for carrying clothing and personal items needed for an overnight stay
small travel bag for one-night trips

Examples

She packed her pajamas and toothbrush in her overnight bag before leaving for her friend's house.
His overnight bag was small enough to fit in the overhead compartment on the plane.
Don't forget your overnight bag if you're staying at the hotel tonight.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: weekend bag - a larger bag used for carrying clothing and personal items needed for a weekend trip
