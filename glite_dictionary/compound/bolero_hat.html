bolero hat

1. NOUN

/ boʊˈlɛɹoʊ hæt / (AmE), / bəˈlɛərəʊ hæt / (BrE)
A Spanish-style hat with a wide, flat brim and flat top, typically made of black felt.
Spanish flat-brimmed felt hat

Examples

She wore a stylish bolero hat to complete her Spanish-inspired outfit.
The actor's costume included a black bolero hat and a matching suit.
He tipped his bolero hat and smiled before stepping onto the stage.

Synset Examples

You look like Zorro in this bolero hat.

Etymology

From Spanish 'bolero', referring to both a style of music and dance, and to the costume traditionally worn by performers.
