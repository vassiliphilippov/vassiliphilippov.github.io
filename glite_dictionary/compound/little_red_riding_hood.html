Little Red Riding Hood

1. PROPN

/ ˌlɪt̬l ˌrɛd ˈraɪdɪŋ hʊd / (AmE), / ˌlɪtl ˌrɛd ˈraɪdɪŋ hʊd / (BrE)
a girl in a well-known fairy tale who encounters a wolf while visiting her grandmother
girl in fairy tale who meets a wolf

Examples

As a child, she loved reading about Little Red Riding Hood and her adventures in the forest.
The story of Little Red Riding Hood teaches children to be cautious with strangers.
For the school play, Emma dressed up as Little Red Riding Hood and carried a basket of treats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairy tale - a narrative featuring magical and imaginary beings and events
  • Antonym: wolf - the animal antagonist in the same fairy tale
