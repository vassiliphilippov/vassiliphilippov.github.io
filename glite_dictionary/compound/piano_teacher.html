piano teacher

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˈtitʃər / (AmE), / piˈænəʊ ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who teaches students how to play the piano
one who teaches piano playing

Examples

My piano teacher helped me prepare for my first recital.
After school, she takes lessons from a piano teacher downtown.
He has worked as a piano teacher for over twenty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: music teacher - a person who teaches music
