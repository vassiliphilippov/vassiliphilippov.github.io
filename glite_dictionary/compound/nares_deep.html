Nares Deep

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛrz diːp / (AmE), / ˈnɛəz diːp / (BrE)
a deep depression in the floor of the Atlantic Ocean located north of Haiti and Puerto Rico
oceanic depression north of Haiti and Puerto Rico

Examples

Scientists conducted research in the Nares Deep to study its unique marine life.
The Nares Deep is one of the lesser-known features of the Atlantic Ocean's floor.
Oceanographers have mapped the Nares Deep using advanced sonar technology.

Etymology

Named after the British explorer Sir George Nares.
