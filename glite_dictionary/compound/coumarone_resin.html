coumarone resin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːməˌroʊn ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ˈkuːməˌrəʊn ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a thermoplastic resin produced by the polymerization of indene and coumarone, used in coatings, paints, and asphalt tiles
thermoplastic resin used in coatings and tiles

Examples

The manufacturer uses coumarone resin to enhance the durability of road markings.
Certain industrial paints contain coumarone resin for improved weather resistance.
The company specializes in high-performance flooring made with coumarone resin.

Etymology

From 'coumarone', a chemical compound, and 'resin', indicating a synthetic resin.
