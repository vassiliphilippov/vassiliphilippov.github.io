tax boost

1. NOUN

/ tæks buːst / (AmE), / tæks buːst / (BrE)
an increase in the amount of tax that is collected
increase in taxes

Examples

The government announced a tax boost to raise additional revenue for infrastructure projects.
Many businesses are concerned about the impact of another tax boost next year.
Voters expressed their dissatisfaction with the proposed tax boost during the town hall meeting.

Synset Examples

a tax increase of 15 percent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax increase - an increase in the amount of tax
  • Antonym: tax cut - a reduction in the amount of tax
