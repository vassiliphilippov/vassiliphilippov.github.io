quadraphonic system

1. NOUN

/ kwɑː.drəˈfɑː.nɪk ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / kwæd.rəˈfɒn.ɪk ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a sound recording or playback system using four separate audio channels to create a more immersive audio experience
system with four audio channels

Examples

The concert was recorded using a quadraphonic system for a rich and immersive experience.
Audiophiles appreciated the unique sound separation offered by the quadraphonic system.
He upgraded his home theater to include a vintage quadraphonic system from the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: stereophonic system - a sound system using two separate channels for spatial audio

Etymology

From 'quadra-' meaning four, and 'phonic' relating to sound.
