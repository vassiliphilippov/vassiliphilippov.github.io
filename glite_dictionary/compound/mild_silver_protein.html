mild silver protein

1. NOUN

/ maɪld ˈsɪlvɚ ˈproʊtin / (AmE), / maɪld ˈsɪlvə ˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
an antiseptic compound of protein and silver, used for its germicidal properties, known by the trade name Argyrol
antiseptic compound of protein and silver
medical

Examples

The doctor applied mild silver protein to the wound to prevent infection.
Mild silver protein was once a common treatment for eye infections.
Because of its antiseptic qualities, mild silver protein was widely used in early 20th-century medicine.
