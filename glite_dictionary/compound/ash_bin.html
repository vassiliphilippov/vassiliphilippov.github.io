ash bin

1. NOUN

/ ˈæʃ bɪn / (AmE), / ˈæʃ bɪn / (BrE)
a container for holding rubbish, especially household ashes, until it is collected
container for rubbish or ashes

Examples

The ash bin is emptied every Monday morning by the council workers.
Please remember to put the cold ashes from the fireplace into the ash bin.
After the party, the ash bin was overflowing with trash and cigarette butts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustbin - a container for household waste; synonym to ash bin
  • Synonym: garbage can - a receptacle for rubbish, especially in North America
