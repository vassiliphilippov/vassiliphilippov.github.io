cigar cutter

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a small device used to cut the end off a cigar before smoking it
device for trimming cigar ends

Examples

He pulled out his cigar cutter before lighting up a new cigar.
A sharp cigar cutter ensures a clean cut every time.
She gave him an engraved cigar cutter as a birthday present.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutter - tool or device that cuts
