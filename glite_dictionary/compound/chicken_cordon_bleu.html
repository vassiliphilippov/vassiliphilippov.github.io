chicken cordon bleu

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ən ˌkɔr.dɑn ˈblu / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn ˌkɔː.dɒ̃ ˈbluː / (BrE)
A dish made of thin slices of chicken stuffed with cheese and ham, then sautéed or baked.
chicken stuffed with cheese and ham

Examples

Last night I ordered the chicken cordon bleu at the new restaurant downtown.
She prepared a homemade chicken cordon bleu for her family’s special dinner.
Many cooking classes teach students how to make chicken cordon bleu from scratch.

Etymology

French 'cordon bleu' meaning 'blue ribbon', indicating high culinary distinction.
