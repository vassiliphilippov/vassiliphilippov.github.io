vandyke beard

1. NOUN

/ vænˌdaɪk ˈbɪrd / (AmE), / vanˌdaɪk ˈbɪəd / (BrE)
A short, pointed beard resembling the style depicted in paintings by Anthony van Dyck.
short pointed beard style

Examples

The actor grew a vandyke beard for his role in the historical drama.
Portraits from the 17th century often feature men with a vandyke beard.
He carefully trimmed his vandyke beard before the costume party.

Etymology

Named after the Flemish painter Anthony van Dyck (1599–1641), known for depicting this beard style.
