spiny-edged

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪni ˌɛdʒd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˌɛdʒd / (BrE)
having a border or edge covered with spines or sharp projections
having a spiny border or edge

Examples

The spiny-edged leaves help protect the plant from herbivores.
He cut his finger on the spiny-edged shell while collecting seashells on the beach.
Several fish species have spiny-edged fins to deter predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiny - covered with spines or thorn-like projections
