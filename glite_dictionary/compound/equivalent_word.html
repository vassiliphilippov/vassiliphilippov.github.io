equivalent word

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪvələnt wɜrd / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪvələnt wɜːd / (BrE)
a word that has the same or nearly the same meaning as another word in a particular context; a synonym
a synonym; word of similar meaning

Examples

'Huge' is an equivalent word to 'enormous' in this context.
When translating, it's important to choose the most accurate equivalent word in the target language.
She searched for an equivalent word to express her feelings more precisely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synonym - a word having the same or nearly the same meaning as another
