loquat tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊˌkwɑt triː / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌkwæt triː / (BrE)
An evergreen tree native to China and Japan, cultivated in warm regions for its small, fuzzy, yellow fruit containing a large seed.
evergreen fruit tree native to East Asia

Examples

The loquat tree in their backyard produces fruit every spring.
We planted a loquat tree for its attractive foliage and sweet fruit.
A mature loquat tree can provide enough fruit for the whole family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loquat - the fruit of the loquat tree

Etymology

From Chinese 'lóu guǒ' (芦橘), via Japanese 'biwa'.
