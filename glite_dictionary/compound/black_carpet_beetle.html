black carpet beetle

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈkɑr.pɪt ˈbiː.təl / (AmE), / blæk ˈkɑː.pɪt ˈbiː.tl̩ / (BrE)
a species of carpet beetle that is entirely black in color, often found as a household pest damaging fabrics and stored products
an all-black species of carpet beetle

Examples

We found a black carpet beetle in the closet, which explained the holes in our wool sweaters.
The infestation of black carpet beetles required professional pest control treatment.
A single black carpet beetle larva can cause significant damage to household fabrics if left unchecked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet beetle - a small beetle whose larvae feed on natural fibers and damage carpets and other fabrics
