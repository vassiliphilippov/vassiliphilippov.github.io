car train

1. NOUN

/ kɑɹ treɪn / (AmE), / kɑː treɪn / (BrE)
a train that carries both passengers and their automobiles
train carrying people and their cars

Examples

We decided to take the car train so we could drive our own vehicle at our destination.
The car train provides a convenient way to travel long distances with your automobile.
Several European countries operate a car train service for holidaymakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorail - a service or train that carries cars and passengers
