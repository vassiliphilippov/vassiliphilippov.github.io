chrome horn

1. NOUN

/ kroʊm hɔrn / (AmE), / krəʊm hɔːn / (BrE)
A decorative horn made of chrome-plated metal, commonly installed on vehicles for both function and appearance.
decorative chrome vehicle horn

Examples

The classic car's chrome horn gleamed in the afternoon sun.
He pressed the chrome horn to alert the crowd during the parade.
Many truck owners add a chrome horn to enhance their vehicle's look.

Synset Examples

He installed a shiny chrome horn to impress his friends.
