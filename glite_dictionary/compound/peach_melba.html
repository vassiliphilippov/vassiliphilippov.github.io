peach melba

1. NOUN

/ pitʃ ˈmɛlbə / (AmE), / piːtʃ ˈmɛlbə / (BrE)
A dessert consisting of ice cream and peaches, typically served with a raspberry sauce.
dessert of ice cream and peaches

Examples

For dessert, she ordered a peach melba to satisfy her sweet tooth.
The chef's signature peach melba features homemade vanilla ice cream and fresh peaches.
During summer, I love to make peach melba with locally grown fruit.

Etymology

Named after the Australian soprano Nellie Melba and created by chef Auguste Escoffier.
