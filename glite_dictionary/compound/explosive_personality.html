explosive personality

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv pɚˌsænəˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪv pəˌsǽnəˈlɪti / (BrE)
a character or temperament that is extremely lively, energetic, and dynamic, often attracting attention or making a strong impression
very lively or dynamic character

Examples

Her explosive personality makes her the center of attention wherever she goes.
A teacher with an explosive personality can inspire and energize her students.
His explosive personality often surprises people who meet him for the first time.

Synset Examples

Her explosive personality lights up the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiery temperament - a personality showing intense and passionate emotion
  • Antonym: reserved personality - a character that is quiet and restrained
