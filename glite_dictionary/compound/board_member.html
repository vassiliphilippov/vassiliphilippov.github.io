board member

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / bɔːd ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who serves as a member of the governing board of an organization, company, or institution
member of a governing board

Examples

Each board member must review the financial report before the next meeting.
She was recently appointed as a board member of the local charity.
Many decisions require a vote from every board member present at the session.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: director - a member of the governing board of a company or organization
