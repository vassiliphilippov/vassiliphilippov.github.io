Brittany spaniel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪtəni ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtəni ˈspæniəl / (BrE)
A breed of medium-sized, energetic hunting dog from France, known for its short tail and usually orange- or liver-and-white coat.
French breed of hunting dog

Examples

The Brittany spaniel is admired for its agility and keen sense of smell while hunting.
Their Brittany spaniel won best in show at the national dog competition last weekend.
Veterinarians recommend regular exercise to keep a Brittany spaniel happy and healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaniel - a type of dog bred as a gun dog for flushing game
  • Synonym: setter - a breed of hunting dog trained to point and retrieve

Etymology

Named after the Brittany region of France, combined with 'spaniel' for its type.
