Hollywood moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑliˌwʊd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈhɒliˌwʊd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A dramatic, memorable, or emotionally charged event reminiscent of scenes in Hollywood movies.
memorable, cinematic event

Examples

The proposal atop the Eiffel Tower was truly a Hollywood moment for them.
Scoring the winning goal in the last seconds felt like a Hollywood moment.
When she walked into the party in that dress, it was her Hollywood moment.

Synset Examples

That sunset was a true Hollywood moment.
She felt like she was having her Hollywood moment on stage.
His speech was a Hollywood moment worthy of a movie.

Etymology

From Hollywood (the US film industry), referencing dramatic moments typical of movies.
