hand organ

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / hænd ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a portable musical instrument that produces music when a handle is turned to rotate a cylinder fitted with pins or pegs
portable music instrument with rotating cylinder

Examples

A street performer played an old hand organ outside the market.
Children gathered around to listen to the cheerful tunes of the hand organ.
He restored a 19th-century hand organ and often demonstrates it at local fairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel organ - a similar musical instrument operated by turning a handle, often used in the street
