steel guitar

1. NOUN

/ stiːl ɡɪˈtɑr / (AmE), / stiːl ɡɪˈtɑː / (BrE)
a guitar with steel strings played by sliding a steel bar over the strings to create a glissando effect, commonly used in country and Hawaiian music
guitar played with a steel bar for glissando

Examples

The musician performed a solo on his steel guitar, mesmerizing the audience with its shimmering sound.
She learned to play the steel guitar to add a unique touch to her country band.
A vintage steel guitar can be heard in many classic Hawaiian recordings.
