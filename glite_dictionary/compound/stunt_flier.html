stunt flier

1. NOUN

/ stʌnt ˈflaɪər / (AmE), / stʌnt ˈflaɪə / (BrE)
a pilot who performs aerobatic maneuvers and gives public displays of stunt flying and parachuting
pilot who performs aerobatic displays

Examples

The stunt flier amazed the crowd with daring loops and rolls during the air show.
As a stunt flier, she traveled to fairs across the country performing aerial tricks.
Many young pilots dream of becoming a stunt flier and thrilling spectators with their skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerobat - a pilot who performs acrobatic flying maneuvers
