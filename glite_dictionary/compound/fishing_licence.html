fishing licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
An official permit allowing a person to fish during a specified period, typically issued by a government authority.
official permit to fish

Examples

You must have a valid fishing licence to fish in this river.
She applied for a fishing licence before the start of the season.
Without a fishing licence, anglers can face heavy fines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: licence - an official permit granted by authorities
