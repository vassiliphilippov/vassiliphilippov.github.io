temperature reduction

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpɚətʃɚ rɪˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərətʃə rɪˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process of decreasing the temperature of something; a drop in temperature
decreasing of temperature

Examples

The air conditioner is designed for rapid temperature reduction during hot summer days.
A sudden temperature reduction can affect the performance of sensitive equipment.
Effective temperature reduction methods are essential in food preservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooling - the process of becoming cooler
  • Antonym: temperature increase - the process of becoming warmer
