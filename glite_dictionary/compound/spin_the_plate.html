spin the plate

1. NOUN

/ spɪn ðə pleɪt / (AmE), / spɪn ðə pleɪt / (BrE)
A children's game in which a plate or similar round object is spun on its edge and a player's name is called; the named player must catch it before it stops or pay a forfeit.
a children's game involving spinning a plate

Examples

At the party, the children decided to play spin the plate in the garden.
We used to spend hours playing spin the plate during our school breaks.
If you fail to catch the plate in spin the plate, you have to do a silly forfeit.
