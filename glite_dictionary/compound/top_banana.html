top banana

1. NOUN

/ tɑp bəˈnænə / (AmE), / tɒp bəˈnænə / (BrE)
the most important or leading person in a group or organization, especially the main comedian in a show
the main or most important person, esp. in entertainment
slang american

Examples

After years on the stage, she finally became the top banana in the comedy troupe.
The top banana always gets the last word in the show.
Despite his talent, he never aspired to be the top banana; he enjoyed supporting roles more.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: second banana - the assistant or subordinate comedian in a show

Etymology

American slang, originating from vaudeville and burlesque theatre in the 20th century.
