upcoming event

1. NOUN

/ ʌpˌkʌmɪŋ ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ʌpˌkʌmɪŋ ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an occasion or happening that is planned to take place in the near future
scheduled future happening

Examples

The school is preparing decorations for the upcoming event next week.
She sent out invitations to all her friends for the upcoming event.
Many people are excited to attend the upcoming event in the city center.

Synset Examples

We talked about the upcoming event yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: event - an occurrence, especially one of some importance
