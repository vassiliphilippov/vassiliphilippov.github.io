fenny aroma

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛni əˈroʊmə / (AmE), / ˈfɛni əˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
a sweet, earthy smell reminiscent of marshland or fenland
scent like marshland or fens

Examples

The old manor was filled with a fenny aroma after days of heavy rain.
He opened the greenhouse door and was greeted by the fenny aroma of wet soil and plants.
A fenny aroma drifted through the air, reminding her of walks near the marsh.

Synset Examples

Her garden had a fenny aroma from the flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthy aroma - an aroma suggestive of soil or earth
  • Degree Lower: musty smell - a stale or damp smell, often associated with enclosed spaces

Etymology

From 'fenny' (relating to fens or marshes) + 'aroma' (a pleasant smell, from Latin 'aroma').
