womanish attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmənɪʃ ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənɪʃ ˈatɪtjuːd / (BrE)
behavior regarded as characteristic of women, often implying weakness or excessive emotion (sometimes used disparagingly)
behavior stereotypically seen as feminine

Examples

His womanish attitude surprised everyone at the meeting.
She accused him of having a womanish attitude whenever he showed his feelings.
Some critics dismissed the artist's work as showing a womanish attitude toward life.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feminine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with women
  • Antonym: manly - having qualities traditionally associated with men; masculine
