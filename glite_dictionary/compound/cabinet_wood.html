cabinet wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪnət wʊd / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnɪt wʊd / (BrE)
wood of moderate density used for making cabinets and fine furniture
wood used in cabinetry

Examples

Teak is a popular cabinet wood due to its strength and attractive grain.
The craftsman selected a high-quality cabinet wood for the new bookcase.
Maple is often chosen as a cabinet wood for its smooth finish and durability.

Synset Examples

teak and other heavy cabinet wood
