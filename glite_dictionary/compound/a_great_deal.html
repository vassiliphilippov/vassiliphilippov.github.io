a great deal

1. ADV

/ ə ɡreɪt diːl / (AmE), / ə ɡreɪt diːl / (BrE)
to a large extent; in large quantities or very often
in large quantities or very often

Examples

I've learned a great deal from this experience over the last year.
He doesn't eat a great deal of meat anymore.

Synset Examples
