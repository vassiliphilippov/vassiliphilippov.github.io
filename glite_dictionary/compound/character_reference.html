character reference

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærɪktər ˈrɛfərəns / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə ˈrɛfərəns / (BrE)
a letter or statement written by someone who knows a person well, describing their qualities and reliability, typically provided to a potential employer
recommendation letter describing personal qualities

Examples

Applicants are often asked to provide a character reference from a previous employer or colleague.
Her former supervisor gave her an excellent character reference, highlighting her integrity and work ethic.
The school required a character reference as part of the admissions process.

Synset Examples

requests for character references are all too often answered evasively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reference - a letter or statement recommending someone for a job or position
  • Synonym: recommendation - a written or spoken statement praising someone's abilities or character
