spike arrester

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk əˈrɛstər / (AmE), / spaɪk əˈrestə / (BrE)
an electrical device installed in a power line to protect equipment from sudden surges or fluctuations in current
device protecting equipment from power surges

Examples

The electrician recommended installing a spike arrester to protect the sensitive equipment in the laboratory.
A sudden lightning strike caused a power surge, but the spike arrester absorbed the excess voltage.
Many modern appliances come with a built-in spike arrester to extend their lifespan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surge protector - device protecting electrical equipment from voltage spikes
