Murican style

1. NOUN

/ mɜrɪkən staɪl / (AmE), / mjʊərɪkən staɪl / (BrE)
a colloquial, exaggeratedly American style, often referencing stereotypical cultural traits or decor
exaggerated American style
informal slang

Examples

She decorated her house in true Murican style for the party.
The barbecue featured burgers, hot dogs, and plenty of soda in full Murican style.
His outfit—complete with cowboy boots and a flag bandana—was pure Murican style.
