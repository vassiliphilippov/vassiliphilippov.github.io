lemon-scented gum

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˌsɛntɪd ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˌsɛntɪd ɡʌm / (BrE)
an Australian eucalyptus tree (Corymbia citriodora) noted for its smooth bark and leaves with a strong lemon scent
Australian eucalyptus tree with lemon-scented leaves

Examples

The lemon-scented gum in the park released a fresh aroma after the rain.
Gardeners often plant lemon-scented gum for its fragrant foliage and attractive bark.
A row of lemon-scented gum lines the driveway, providing shade and a pleasant citrus scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eucalyptus - a genus of trees and shrubs native to Australia
