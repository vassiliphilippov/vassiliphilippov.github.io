real-estate business

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl əˌsteɪt ˈbɪznɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ɪsˌteɪt ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
The commercial activity of buying, selling, or renting properties such as land, houses, or buildings.
the business of real estate transactions

Examples

She decided to invest in the real-estate business after studying market trends for several years.
Many people made fortunes during the boom in the real-estate business in the early 2000s.
The real-estate business can be highly competitive, especially in large cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real estate - property consisting of land or buildings
  • Synonym: property business - the business of dealing in properties
