common privet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈprɪvɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈprɪvɪt / (BrE)
a deciduous or semi-evergreen shrub (Ligustrum vulgare), often used for hedges in gardens and parks
a shrub often used for hedges

Examples

The garden was bordered by a neatly trimmed common privet hedge.
Birds often use common privet for nesting because of its dense foliage.
She planted several common privet shrubs to create privacy around the patio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: privet - a shrub of the genus Ligustrum, especially used for hedges
