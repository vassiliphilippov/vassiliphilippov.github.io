all kinds

1. NOUN

/ ɔl kaɪndz / (AmE), / ɔːl kaɪndz / (BrE)
a wide variety of types or sorts; many different kinds
many different types or sorts

Examples

At the festival, there were all kinds of food from around the world.
She has read all kinds of books over the years.
We meet all kinds of people in our line of work.

Synset Examples

From artists to scientists, it takes all kinds to make a world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all sorts - many different types or sorts
