sports commentator

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts ˈkɑmənteɪtər / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈkɒmənteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who provides a spoken account and analysis of a sporting event for broadcast on television, radio, or online
person describing sports events for broadcast

Examples

The sports commentator explained the rules to viewers unfamiliar with the game.
Every sports commentator brings their own unique style to covering football matches.
As a former player, she became a respected sports commentator after retiring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commentator - a person who comments on events, especially on television or radio
