water biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
a thin, crisp biscuit made from flour and water, usually without fat, often eaten with cheese
thin, crisp biscuit made from flour and water
british

Examples

She served the cheese platter with a selection of water biscuits and grapes.
The water biscuit snapped easily between his fingers as he added a slice of cheddar.
For afternoon tea, we enjoyed water biscuits topped with smoked salmon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cracker - a thin, crisp baked bread or biscuit
