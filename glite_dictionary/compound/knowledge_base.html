knowledge base

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑlɪdʒ beɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɒlɪdʒ beɪs / (BrE)
a set of information or data about a particular subject, especially as used by a computer system or organization
collection of information on a subject

Examples

Our engineers regularly update the knowledge base to ensure all technical information is accurate.
The online knowledge base provides answers to frequently asked questions.
She consulted the company's knowledge base before reaching out to customer support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: database - an organized collection of data, especially in digital form
