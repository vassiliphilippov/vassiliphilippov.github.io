personal nirvana

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrsənəl nɚˈvɑnə / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl nɪəˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
a state of perfect happiness or inner peace experienced by an individual
a person's state of perfect happiness or peace

Examples

After years of turmoil, he finally found his personal nirvana in meditation and quiet reflection.
Traveling the world in a camper van is her idea of personal nirvana.
Some people believe that reaching personal nirvana is more important than achieving material success.

Synset Examples

Finding peace in nature was her personal nirvana.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nirvana - a transcendental state of liberation and freedom from suffering (from Buddhism)

Etymology

From 'nirvana' (Sanskrit: निर्वाण nirvāṇa, meaning 'extinction, blowing out', in Buddhism a state of liberation), with 'personal' indicating individual experience.
