battery charger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætəri ˌtʃɑrdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈbætəri ˌtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a device that restores energy to a rechargeable battery by passing an electric current through it
device for charging rechargeable batteries

Examples

Please remember to bring your battery charger when you travel abroad.
The phone won't work until you connect it to the battery charger.
He bought a universal battery charger that works for all his devices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charger - a device for charging, especially batteries
  • Synonym: power adapter - a device for converting electric current to power devices
