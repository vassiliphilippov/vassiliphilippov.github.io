Carson City

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːrsən ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈkɑːsən ˈsɪti / (BrE)
The capital city of the state of Nevada, located in the western part of Nevada, United States.
capital city of Nevada, USA

Examples

We visited Carson City last summer to learn about Nevada's history.
The legislature meets in Carson City to discuss state laws every year.
Carson City was founded during the Nevada silver boom.

Etymology

Named after the Carson River, which was named for frontiersman Kit Carson.
