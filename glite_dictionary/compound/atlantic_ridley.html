Atlantic ridley

1. NOUN

/ ətˈlæntɪk ˈrɪdli / (AmE), / ətˈlæntɪk ˈrɪdli / (BrE)
A species of grey sea turtle found along the Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of North America, also known as Kemp's ridley.
a sea turtle of Atlantic/Gulf Coasts

Examples

The endangered Atlantic ridley is the smallest species of sea turtle in the world.
Conservationists are working to protect the nesting sites of the Atlantic ridley along the Gulf Coast.
Sightings of the Atlantic ridley have increased due to successful conservation efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kemp's ridley - the common name and synonym for Atlantic ridley sea turtle

Etymology

Named after the Atlantic Ocean and American fisherman Richard M. Kemp, who helped identify the species.
