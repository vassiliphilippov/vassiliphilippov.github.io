translateresque phrase

1. NOUN

/ trænzˌleɪtəˈrɛsk freɪz / (AmE), / trænzˌleɪtəˈrɛsk freɪz / (BrE)
a phrase that resembles a poor or awkward translation, often sounding unnatural in the target language
awkward phrase like a poor translation

The report contained a translateresque phrase that made the instructions difficult to understand.
Her novel was criticized for its use of translateresque phrases, which made the prose seem unnatural.
Whenever software is poorly localized, you can spot a translateresque phrase in the dialog boxes.
