night watchwoman

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ˈwɑːtʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / naɪt ˈwɒtʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman employed to guard a building or premises during the night.
female night security guard

Examples

The night watchwoman made her rounds every hour to ensure the building was secure.
After midnight, the night watchwoman checked all the entry points of the warehouse.
The company hired a night watchwoman to patrol the premises when everyone else had gone home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night watchman - a guard employed at night
  • Synonym: watchwoman - a female guard or watchperson
