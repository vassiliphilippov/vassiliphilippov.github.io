history department

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪstəri dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈhɪstəri dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the academic division of a school, college, or university that is responsible for teaching and research in the field of history
academic division for teaching history

Examples

She decided to major in history after attending a seminar organized by the history department.
The history department is hosting a lecture series on ancient civilizations this semester.
Funding cuts have affected many programs in the history department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of an organization, such as a university
