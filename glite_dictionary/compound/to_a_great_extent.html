to a great extent

1. ADV

/ tə ə ˈɡreɪt ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / tə ə ˈɡreɪt ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)
To a considerable or significant degree; very much.
to a considerable degree

Examples

The policy changes have improved the situation to a great extent.
Her success was due to a great extent to her determination and hard work.
The city has modernized its public transport to a great extent over the last decade.

Synset Examples

he relied heavily on others' data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to a large extent - to a great or considerable degree
  • Degree Lower: to some extent - to a limited or moderate degree
  • Degree Lower: slightly - to a small degree
