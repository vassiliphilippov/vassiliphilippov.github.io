country box

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri bɑks / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri bɒks / (BrE)
a box containing local or artisanal foods, often prepared as a gift or for transport to outdoor events or picnics
box of local or artisanal foods

Examples

The country box arrived full of farm-fresh cheese, honey, and handmade jams.
For her birthday, she received a country box featuring gourmet snacks from local producers.
They brought a country box to the picnic, packed with homemade bread and preserves.

Synset Examples

She opened the country box and discovered handmade jams and cheeses.
