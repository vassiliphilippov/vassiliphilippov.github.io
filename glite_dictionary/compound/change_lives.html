to change lives

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ laɪvz / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ laɪvz / (BrE)
to have a significant positive or transformative effect on people's existence or well-being
make a significant positive impact on people

Examples

The new policy aims to change lives in disadvantaged communities across the country.
Her generosity has helped change lives for countless children in need.
Education has the power to change lives and open new opportunities.

Synset Examples

They believe Edison's medicine could change lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to transform - to make a thorough or dramatic change
