yellow star-thistle

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ ˈstɑr ˌθɪs.əl / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˈstɑː ˌθɪs.əl / (BrE)
A European weed with winged stems and hairy leaves, now found as an invasive plant in parts of the United States.
European weed with winged stems and hairy leaves

Examples

The field was overrun with yellow star-thistle, making it difficult for native plants to thrive.
Farmers are concerned about the impact of yellow star-thistle on their grazing lands.
Efforts to control yellow star-thistle have included introducing natural insect predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: star-thistle - any plant of the genus Centaurea with spiny flower heads
