piney vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪni vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈpaɪni vaɪb / (BrE)
A scent or atmosphere reminiscent of pine trees, often evoking freshness or wintertime.
atmosphere or scent like pine trees
informal

Examples

Her living room always has a piney vibe during the holidays, thanks to the real tree.
The candle gave the cabin a distinct piney vibe that reminded me of mountain lodges.
We tried to give the office a piney vibe with some evergreen branches and pine cones.

Synset Examples

The decorations had a piney vibe for winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine-scented - having the smell of pine
