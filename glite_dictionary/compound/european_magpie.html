European magpie

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈmægˌpaɪ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpi.ən ˈmæɡ.paɪ / (BrE)
a common black-and-white bird (Pica pica) found throughout Eurasia, known for its intelligence and distinctive long tail
a Eurasian black-and-white magpie

Examples

The European magpie is well known for its noisy chattering and bold behavior.
In folklore, the European magpie is often associated with superstition and symbolism.
We spotted a European magpie perched on the garden fence this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magpie - a long-tailed black and white bird of the crow family
