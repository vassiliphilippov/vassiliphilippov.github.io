looking glass tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkɪŋ ɡlæs tri / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪŋ ɡlɑːs triː / (BrE)
a large evergreen tree native to India and Burma, known for its leaves that are silvery on the underside
evergreen tree with silvery leaves, India/Burma

Examples

The leaves of the looking glass tree glimmer in the sunlight, creating a striking silver effect.
Botanists appreciate the looking glass tree for its distinctive foliage and sturdy trunk.
Villagers in India often rest under the shade of the looking glass tree during hot afternoons.
