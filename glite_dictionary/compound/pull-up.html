pull-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊl ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpʊl ʌp / (BrE)
A roadside café, especially one catering to lorry drivers.
roadside café for lorry drivers
british

Examples

We stopped at a pull-up to grab some breakfast on the way to London.
Most lorry drivers prefer a pull-up for a quick meal and a rest break.
There's a popular pull-up just off the motorway where truckers gather.
