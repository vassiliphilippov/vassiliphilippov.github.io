strip mall

1. NOUN

/ strɪp mɑl / (AmE), / strɪp mɔːl / (BrE)
a row of stores, businesses, and restaurants with a parking lot in front, typically situated along a road or busy street
row of shops with parking in front
american

Examples

There is a new strip mall opening near my neighborhood next month.
Most of the stores in the strip mall are small, family-owned businesses.
We stopped for lunch at a café in the strip mall on Main Street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping center - a group of retail stores and service establishments operated as a unit
  • Degree Higher: mall - a large building or series of connected buildings containing shops with interconnecting walkways
