round-the-clock

1. ADJ

/ ˌraʊnd.ðəˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˌraʊnd.ðəˈklɒk / (BrE)
happening or continuing all day and all night, without stopping
happening continuously, all day and night

Examples

The hospital provides round-the-clock emergency services for patients in need.
Security guards are stationed at the gate round-the-clock to ensure safety.
Thanks to round-the-clock monitoring, problems are detected before they become serious.

Synset Examples

around-the-clock nursing care

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuous - happening without interruption
  • Synonym: day-and-night - all the time; without stopping
