muster roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌstɚ roʊl / (AmE), / ˈmʌstə rəʊl / (BrE)
an official list of names of officers and enlisted personnel in a military unit or ship's company
official military personnel list

Examples

The captain checked the muster roll to ensure everyone was present before departure.
Every name on the muster roll must be accounted for during inspection.
The ship's muster roll was updated after the new recruits arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roster - a list of people's names, especially in a group or organization
  • Synonym: roll call - the act of reading aloud a list of names to determine attendance

Etymology

From 'muster', meaning an assembly of troops, and 'roll', meaning a list or register.
