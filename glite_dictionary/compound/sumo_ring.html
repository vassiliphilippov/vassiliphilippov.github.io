sumo ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːmoʊ rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuːməʊ rɪŋ / (BrE)
a circular area where sumo wrestling matches are held
circular area for sumo wrestling

Examples

The referee gestured for the wrestlers to enter the sumo ring.
A ceremonial purification is performed before each match inside the sumo ring.
Spectators gathered around the sumo ring to watch the intense bout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dohyō - the traditional Japanese term for the sumo ring

Etymology

From Japanese 'sumō' (sumo wrestling) and English 'ring'.
