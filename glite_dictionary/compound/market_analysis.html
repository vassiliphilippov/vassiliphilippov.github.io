market analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkɪt əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
a systematic examination of a market to gather information about its size, trends, competition, and customer needs, especially to inform business decisions
examination of a market for business purposes

Examples

The company's market analysis revealed a growing demand for eco-friendly products.
Before launching the product, the team conducted a thorough market analysis to understand potential customers.
Annual market analysis helps businesses stay ahead of industry trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market research - the process of gathering information about consumers' needs and preferences
