impregnable heart

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl hɑrt / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl hɑːt / (BrE)
a heart or emotional state that cannot be affected or hurt by feelings, especially love or pain
emotionally unaffected or unyielding heart

Examples

After years of disappointment, he developed an impregnable heart and stopped trusting others.
Her impregnable heart made it impossible for anyone to get close to her.
Despite all attempts to comfort him, his impregnable heart showed no sign of softening.

Synset Examples

She felt her heart was impregnable after the breakup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invulnerable - impossible to harm or damage; emotional meaning is similar
  • Synonym: unfeeling - lacking emotion or sensitivity; unable to be emotionally affected
  • Antonym: vulnerable - easily hurt or emotionally affected; opposite meaning

Etymology

From 'impregnable' (from Old French, from Latin 'impregnabilis', meaning unassailable) + 'heart'
