bastard yellowwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈjɛloʊˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈjɛləʊˌwʊd / (BrE)
A medium-sized tree native to South Africa, known for its yellowish wood.
medium-sized South African tree

Examples

The bastard yellowwood is often found in the coastal forests of South Africa.
