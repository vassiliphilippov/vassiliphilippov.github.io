repulsorlift engine

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpʌl.sɚ.lɪft ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌl.sə.lɪft ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
A fictional device in the Star Wars universe that enables vehicles to float and move by counteracting gravity.
anti-gravity device from Star Wars

Examples

The landspeeder hovered above the ground using its repulsorlift engine.
Technicians worked for hours to repair the damaged repulsorlift engine on the starship.
Many vehicles in Star Wars are powered by advanced repulsorlift engines that defy gravity.
