yellow pond lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ pɑnd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ pɒnd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a common water lily of eastern and central North America, with broad leaves and globe-shaped yellow flowers, typically found in sluggish fresh or slightly brackish water
a North American water lily with yellow flowers

Examples

The yellow pond lily blooms in late spring, providing color along the lake's edge.
We paddled through the marsh, where clusters of yellow pond lilies floated on the water.
Botanists studied the unique habitat requirements of the yellow pond lily in the region.
