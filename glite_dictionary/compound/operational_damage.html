operational damage

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənl ˈdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənl ˈdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
loss or destruction of military equipment that occurs during field operations, as opposed to accidents or maintenance failures
loss of military equipment in field operations

Examples

The report detailed the operational damage sustained by the tanks during the fierce fighting.
After the exercise, crews assessed the operational damage to their vehicles before returning to base.
Minimizing operational damage is critical to maintaining the effectiveness of any military unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battle damage - damage sustained as a result of combat actions
