Murray River

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɜri ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌri ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in southeast Australia, flowing west and south into the Indian Ocean near Adelaide
major river in southeast Australia

Examples

The Murray River is the longest river in Australia, stretching over 2,500 kilometers.
Many towns along the Murray River rely on it for irrigation and fresh water.
We enjoyed a cruise down the Murray River during our holiday in South Australia.

Etymology

Named after Sir George Murray, a British politician and colonial secretary in the early 19th century.
