Jamaica shorts

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪkə ʃɔrts / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪkə ʃɔːts / (BrE)
Short pants ending at the knee, typically worn for casual or athletic purposes.
short pants ending at the knee

Examples

She wore her favorite Jamaica shorts to the beach party last weekend.
During the summer, many athletes train in comfortable Jamaica shorts and T-shirts.
He prefers Jamaica shorts because they offer more freedom of movement than longer trousers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bermuda shorts - shorts that extend to just above the knee

Etymology

Named after Jamaica, possibly due to fashion origins or associations with tropical style.
