Coyote State

1. PROPN

/ kaɪˈoʊti steɪt / (AmE), / kɔɪˈəʊti steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the US state of South Dakota, referring to the coyote, the state animal
nickname for South Dakota, USA

Examples

South Dakota is often called the Coyote State because the coyote is its state animal.
I learned in school that the Coyote State is another name for South Dakota.
Travel brochures frequently refer to South Dakota as the Coyote State.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South Dakota - a state in the north-central United States
