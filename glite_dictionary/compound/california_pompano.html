California pompano

1. NOUN

/ kælɪˈfɔrnjə pɑmˈpɑnoʊ / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːnɪə pɒmˈpænəʊ / (BrE)
A species of pompano fish, smaller than the Florida pompano, commonly found in the West Indies.
species of pompano fish in the West Indies

Examples

Fishermen reported catching several California pompano off the coast last summer.
The restaurant served grilled California pompano as their specialty dish.
Biologists study the habitat of the California pompano to monitor changes in the West Indies ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pompano - a marine fish of the genus Trachinotus, known for its edible flesh
