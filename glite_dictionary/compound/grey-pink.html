grey-pink

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ɡreɪ pɪŋk / (BrE)
Of a pink color tinged with grey; a subdued or muted shade of pink with greyish tones.
pink with a hint of grey

Examples

Her room was decorated in soft grey-pink tones that created a calming atmosphere.
The morning sky glowed with a grey-pink hue as the sun began to rise.
She wore a delicate scarf in grey-pink, which matched her understated style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - color between black and white
  • Synonym: pink - pale red color
