Green Goblin

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrin ˈɡɑb.lɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˈɡɒb.lɪn / (BrE)
a fictional supervillain in the Marvel Comics universe, chiefly known as Spider-Man's enemy
Spider-Man's arch-enemy in Marvel Comics

Examples

For the superhero-themed party, he wore a detailed Green Goblin costume complete with a glider.
The Green Goblin first appeared in Marvel Comics as Spider-Man's formidable foe.
In the latest movie, the Green Goblin unveils a new set of dangerous gadgets.

Synset Examples

He dressed as the green goblin for Halloween.
The comic book featured the infamous green goblin as the villain.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Spider-Man - a fictional superhero in Marvel Comics
  • Synonym: villain - a character whose evil actions or motives are important to the plot

Etymology

From 'green', referring to the character's costume color, and 'goblin', a mythical mischievous creature.
