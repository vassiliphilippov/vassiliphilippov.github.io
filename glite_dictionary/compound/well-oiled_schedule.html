well-oiled schedule

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɛl ˈɔɪld ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˈɔɪld ˈʃɛdjuːl / (BrE)
A schedule that runs smoothly and efficiently due to good organization.
efficiently organized schedule

Examples

With a well-oiled schedule, the team completed every task on time without stress.
Her well-oiled schedule allowed her to juggle her job, studies, and hobbies effectively.
Event planners rely on a well-oiled schedule to ensure every detail is handled smoothly.

Synset Examples

She maintained a well-oiled schedule to balance work and life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-oiled machine - something that operates very smoothly and efficiently

Etymology

From the figurative phrase 'well-oiled,' referring to machinery that runs smoothly when properly lubricated.
