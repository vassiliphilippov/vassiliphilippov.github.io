today years old

1. ADJ

/ təˈdeɪ jɪərz oʊld / (AmE), / təˈdeɪ jɪəz əʊld / (BrE)
used humorously to indicate that one has just learned or realized something, typically something surprising or previously unknown
humorous phrase for just learning something
informal

Examples

I was today years old when I found out penguins have knees.
She realized she was today years old when she learned what DIY stands for.
He admitted he was today years old when he discovered tomatoes are fruit.

Synset Examples
