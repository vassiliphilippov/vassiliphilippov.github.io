African massage

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən məˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən məˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
A style of massage therapy originating in Africa, characterized by unique rhythmic techniques intended to relieve tension and promote relaxation.
massage therapy technique from Africa

Examples

During her trip to Cape Town, she experienced an African massage that left her feeling refreshed.
Many spas now offer African massage as part of their wellness packages.
He learned several African massage techniques during his training abroad.

Synset Examples

He raved about the benefits of an African massage.

Etymology

From 'African', referring to the continent of Africa, and 'massage', from French 'massage'.
