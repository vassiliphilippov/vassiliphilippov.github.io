shaving cutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪvɪŋ ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvɪŋ ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a high-precision tool used to finish gear tooth surfaces by removing small amounts of material after initial machining
precision tool for finishing gear teeth

Examples

The factory installed a new shaving cutter to improve the accuracy of their gear production.
After hobbing, the gears are finished with a shaving cutter to achieve the required surface quality.
Regular maintenance of the shaving cutter ensures consistently high-precision results in gear manufacturing.
