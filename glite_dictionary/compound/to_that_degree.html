to that degree

1. ADV

/ tə ðæt dɪˈɡri / (AmE), / tə ðæt dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
to the extent or level indicated; to that extent
to the indicated extent or level

Examples

Her explanation was convincing, but I didn't find it to that degree that I could ignore the evidence.
He was surprised that she cared to that degree about the outcome of the game.
The project needs to be detailed, but not to that degree of perfection.

Synset Examples

insofar as it can be ascertained, the horse lung is comparable to that of man
so far as it is reasonably practical he should practice restraint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insofar as - to the extent or degree that
