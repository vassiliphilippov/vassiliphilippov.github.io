precious moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛʃəs ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a time or occasion that is highly valued or cherished
highly valued or cherished time

Examples

She looked back on their wedding day as a precious moment in her life.
Don't rush through these precious moments; you'll miss out on memories you'll always cherish.
Every parent knows that bedtime stories are some of the most precious moments with their children.

Synset Examples

Those moments with family are truly precious.
Time spent with friends is precious to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasured memory - a fondly remembered and valued past experience
  • Degree Lower: fleeting moment - a brief or passing instant
