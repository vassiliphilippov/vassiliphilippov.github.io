right hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪt hænd / (AmE), / ˈraɪt hand / (BrE)
the hand on the right side of the body, typically the dominant hand in most people
the hand on the right side of the body

Examples

She raised her right hand and waved at the teacher.
He injured his right hand while playing basketball last week.
Most people prefer to write with their right hand.
