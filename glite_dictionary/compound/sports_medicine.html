sports medicine

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts ˈmɛdɪsən / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of injuries related to sports and exercise
medical field for sports injuries
medical

Examples

After his knee injury, he sought the advice of a sports medicine specialist.
Many universities now offer degrees in sports medicine for aspiring healthcare professionals.
The clinic’s sports medicine team helps athletes recover and prevent future injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthopedics - the branch of medicine dealing with bones and muscles
