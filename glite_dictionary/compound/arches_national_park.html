Arches National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑrtʃɪz ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃɪz ˌnæʃ(ə)nəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in eastern Utah, United States, famous for its natural sandstone arches and striking geological formations
national park in Utah known for sandstone arches

Examples

We spent our vacation exploring Arches National Park and marvelling at the rock formations.
Arches National Park attracts thousands of visitors each year from around the world.
The landscape of Arches National Park is dotted with more than 2,000 natural stone arches.
