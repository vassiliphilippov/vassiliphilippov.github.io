sport kite

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt kaɪt / (AmE), / spɔːt kaɪt / (BrE)
a maneuverable kite designed to be controlled by two lines and flown with both hands
a controllable two-line kite

Examples

Pilots use a sport kite to perform precise aerial maneuvers at festivals.
Learning to fly a sport kite requires good coordination and quick reflexes.
She bought a new sport kite for her summer trip to the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stunt kite - a similar kite designed for performing tricks and stunts
