mountain andromeda

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən ænˈdrɑː.mɪ.də / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn ænˈdrɒm.ɪ.də / (BrE)
an ornamental evergreen shrub of the southeastern United States, known for its small white bell-shaped flowers
a shrub with white bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The garden features a mountain andromeda that blooms beautifully every spring.
She planted a mountain andromeda along the walkway to add year-round greenery.
Botanists often study the mountain andromeda for its distinctive bell-shaped flowers.
