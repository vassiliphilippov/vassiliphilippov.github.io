credit account

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an arrangement with a business allowing a customer to purchase goods or services and pay for them later
purchase arrangement allowing payment later

Examples

She bought the sofa using her credit account at the furniture store.
The company allows customers to open a credit account for regular purchases.
Payments on your credit account are due by the 15th of each month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charge account - an account at a retail store that allows deferred payment for purchases
