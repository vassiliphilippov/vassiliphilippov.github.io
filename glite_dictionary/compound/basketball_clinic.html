basketball clinic

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a meeting or session where basketball players receive specialized instruction and evaluation from coaches or experts
session for basketball instruction and coaching

Examples

The local high school is hosting a basketball clinic for players of all ages next weekend.
She improved her shooting skills significantly after attending the summer basketball clinic.
Professional coaches were invited to teach at the annual youth basketball clinic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinic - a session for diagnosis or instruction, especially in sports or medicine
