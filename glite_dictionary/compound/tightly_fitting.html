tightly fitting

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtli ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtli ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fitting closely and firmly to the shape of something or someone; snug.
fitting closely and snugly

Examples

The biker wore a tightly fitting helmet for maximum protection.
She prefers tightly fitting dresses that show off her figure.
Make sure the lid is tightly fitting to keep the contents fresh.

Synset Examples

a tightly-fitting cover
tight-fitting clothes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose-fitting - not fitting closely; not snug
