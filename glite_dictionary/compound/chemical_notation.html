chemical notation

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl noʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl nəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a system of symbols and formulas used to represent chemical elements, compounds, and reactions in chemistry.
symbols/formulas used in chemistry

Examples

Students must learn how to interpret chemical notation for understanding chemical equations.
The periodic table uses a standard chemical notation for each element.
Accurate chemical notation is essential when writing research papers in chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molecular formula - a type of chemical notation showing the number and kinds of atoms in a molecule
