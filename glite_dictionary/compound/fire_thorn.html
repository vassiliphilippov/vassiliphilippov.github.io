fire thorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪər θɔrn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə θɔːn / (BrE)
A thorny shrub of the genus Pyracantha, with small white flowers and bright red or orange berries.
thorny shrub with red berries

Examples

The fire thorn is popular in gardens for its vibrant berries and dense foliage.
Birds are attracted to the bright berries of the fire thorn in autumn.
She planted a hedge of fire thorn to add color and security to her yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pyracantha - genus of thorny shrubs with bright berries

Etymology

From 'fire' (referring to the bright berries) and 'thorn' (the spiny branches).
