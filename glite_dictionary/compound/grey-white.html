grey-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ waɪt / (BrE)
Of a white color tinged with grey; having a slight greyish hue.
white tinged with grey

Examples

The puppy had a beautiful grey-white coat that shimmered in the sunlight.
Clouds drifted across the sky, casting a grey-white glow over the landscape.
Her painting used a subtle grey-white background to highlight the bright colors in the foreground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-white - a white color with a slight tint of another color
  • Degree Higher: gray - a color intermediate between black and white
  • Degree Lower: white - the lightest color, without hue
