all sides

1. NOUN

/ ɔl saɪdz / (AmE), / ɔːl saɪdz / (BrE)
all groups, viewpoints, or parties involved in a situation, discussion, or argument
all viewpoints or parties

Examples

The new policy was criticized from all sides during the debate.
During the negotiations, all sides agreed to compromise for peace.
The issue sparked intense reactions from all sides of the political spectrum.

Synset Examples

The scab union faced criticism from all sides.
