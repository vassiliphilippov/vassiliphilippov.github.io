prone to allergies

1. ADJ

/ proʊn tu əˈlɛrdʒiz / (AmE), / prəʊn tʊ əˈlɛdʒɪz / (BrE)
having a tendency to suffer from allergic reactions
tending to suffer from allergies

Examples

Many children who are prone to allergies need to take extra care during pollen season.
If you are prone to allergies, it's important to let your doctor know before starting new medication.
Her skin is prone to allergies, so she only uses fragrance-free products.

Synset Examples

She is prone to allergies in springtime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: allergy-prone - tending to suffer from allergies
