fifty-cent tour

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪfti sɛnt tʊr / (AmE), / ˌfɪfti sɛnt tʊə / (BrE)
a brief or basic guided tour, often inexpensive or introductory
economical or basic guided tour

Examples

The museum offers a fifty-cent tour for tourists on a tight budget.
After arriving at the university, we were taken on a fifty-cent tour of the campus.
If you're short on time, just ask for the fifty-cent tour of the factory.

Synset Examples

We opted for the fifty-cent tour to save money.
The guide offered a fifty-cent tour of the city.
