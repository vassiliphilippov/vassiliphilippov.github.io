web-spinning mite

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛb ˌspɪnɪŋ ˈmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛb ˌspɪnɪŋ ˈmaɪt / (BrE)
a type of mite that produces webs, often found on plants and known to cause damage by feeding on them
mite that produces webs

Examples

The rose bushes were infested with web-spinning mites that left a fine silk covering on the leaves.
Gardeners often struggle to control web-spinning mites on their tomato plants during hot, dry weather.
A magnifying glass revealed dozens of tiny web-spinning mites feeding on the undersides of the leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spider mite - small plant-feeding mite that spins webs
