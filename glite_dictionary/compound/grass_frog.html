grass frog

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs frɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs frɒɡ / (BrE)
a common European frog species that often lives both on land and in water, known for its green or brown coloration
a common European frog species

Examples

On our hike, we spotted a grass frog near the edge of the pond.
The grass frog is well adapted to both land and water environments.
Children caught a grass frog for the school biology project.
