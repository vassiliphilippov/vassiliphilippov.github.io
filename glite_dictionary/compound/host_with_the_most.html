host with the most

1. NOUN

/ hoʊst wɪð ðə moʊst / (AmE), / həʊst wɪð ðə məʊst / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally skilled or charismatic at hosting events, especially parties or gatherings
excellent, charismatic host
informal

Examples

Sarah is known as the host with the most because her parties are always unforgettable.
He prides himself on being the host with the most, ensuring every guest feels welcome.
Whenever we need a fun night out, we turn to the host with the most to organize it.

Synset Examples

As the host with the most, she never runs out of snacks.
Everyone loves him; he’s the host with the most.
He’s the host with the most, always throwing great parties.
