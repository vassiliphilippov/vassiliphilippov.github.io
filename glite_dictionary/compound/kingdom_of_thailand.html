Kingdom of Thailand

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈtaɪlænd / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈtaɪlænd / (BrE)
the official name of the country in Southeast Asia commonly known as Thailand, situated southward along the Isthmus of Kra to the Malay Peninsula
official name of Thailand; a country in Southeast Asia

Examples

The new ambassador has been stationed in the Kingdom of Thailand since January.
Many tourists visit the Kingdom of Thailand for its beaches and cultural heritage.
The Kingdom of Thailand was formerly known as Siam until 1939.

Synset Examples

Thailand is the official name of the former Siam

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thailand - the conventional short name for the country Kingdom of Thailand
