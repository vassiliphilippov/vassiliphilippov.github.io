Meccano set

1. NOUN

/ məˈkɑːnoʊ sɛt / (AmE), / mɪˈkɑːnəʊ sɛt / (BrE)
A construction toy consisting of metal strips, plates, wheels, and other parts that can be connected with nuts and bolts to build mechanical models.
metal construction toy set

Examples

As a child, he loved building cranes and bridges with his Meccano set.
The Meccano set encouraged her interest in engineering and design.
We spent rainy afternoons piecing together elaborate models from the Meccano set.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Erector set - a similar construction toy (US)
  • Synonym: construction set - toy set for building models

Etymology

From the trademark Meccano, coined by British inventor Frank Hornby in 1907.
