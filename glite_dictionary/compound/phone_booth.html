phone booth

1. NOUN

/ foʊn buθ / (AmE), / fəʊn buːð / (BrE)
a small structure with a public telephone inside, where people can make phone calls in private
enclosed structure for public telephone use

Examples

She stepped into the phone booth to make an urgent call.
In the city center, the old phone booth is now used as a mini library.
Superman famously changes his clothes in a phone booth before saving the day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone booth - a booth for using a telephone; alternative term for 'phone booth'
