pure cancer

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈkæn.sɚ / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈkæn.sə / (BrE)
Used figuratively to describe something that is extremely harmful or toxic in its influence.
something extremely harmful or toxic (figurative)
informal

Examples

The constant negativity in the forum is pure cancer and drives people away.
Unchecked gossip can be pure cancer for any organization.
Spreading misinformation is pure cancer to public trust.

Synset Examples

The rumors spreading around the office are pure cancer.
His attitude towards work is pure cancer for team morale.
Negative people can be pure cancer in any group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poison - something that causes harm or destruction
  • Synonym: toxic - very harmful or poisonous, especially in a figurative sense
