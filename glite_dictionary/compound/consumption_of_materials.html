consumption of materials

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌmpʃən əv məˈtɪriəlz / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmpʃən əv məˈtɪərɪəlz / (BrE)
the amount of material resources used up in the course of producing a specific unit of output, including materials, fuel, energy, and depreciation of equipment.
use of resources per production unit

Examples

The factory manager carefully tracked the consumption of materials to optimize efficiency and reduce waste.
Annual reports show a sharp increase in the consumption of materials in the automotive sector.
Reducing the consumption of materials can lower overall production costs and improve environmental sustainability.

Synset Examples

Within the national economy, in order to eliminate double counting, the consumption of materials must be calculated by the final product.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resource consumption - the process of using up resources, especially in production or daily life
