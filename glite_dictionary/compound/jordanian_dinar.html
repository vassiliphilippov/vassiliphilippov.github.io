Jordanian dinar

1. NOUN

/ dʒɔrˈdeɪniən dɪˈnɑr / (AmE), / dʒɔːˈdeɪniən dɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
the official currency of Jordan, equal to 1,000 fils
the currency of Jordan

Examples

He exchanged his dollars for Jordanian dinars before traveling to Amman.
The price was listed as twenty Jordanian dinars per person.
She collected coins, including a rare Jordanian dinar from the 1970s.
