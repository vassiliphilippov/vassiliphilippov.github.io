bourgeois lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ bʊrˈʒwɑː ˈlaɪfstɑɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʊəʒwɑː ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
a way of living typical of the middle class, often characterized by materialism, conventional attitudes, and concern for respectability and comfort
middle-class way of life, often materialistic

Examples

She abandoned her bourgeois lifestyle to travel the world as a backpacker.
The author critiqued the bourgeois lifestyle in her latest novel, calling it shallow and uninspiring.
Many aspire to a bourgeois lifestyle of home ownership and financial security.

Synset Examples

Her bourgeois lifestyle included fancy dinners and luxury cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle-class lifestyle - conventional way of life associated with the middle class
  • Antonym: bohemian lifestyle - way of life that is unconventional and artistic

Etymology

From French 'bourgeois' (middle class) and English 'lifestyle'.
