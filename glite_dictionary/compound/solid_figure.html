solid figure

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑlɪd ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
a three-dimensional geometric shape that has length, width, and height, such as a cube, sphere, or cylinder
three-dimensional geometric shape

Examples

In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the volume of a solid figure.
A cube is a simple example of a solid figure with six faces of equal size.
To understand surface area, you must first identify the faces of the solid figure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plane figure - a geometric shape that is two-dimensional
