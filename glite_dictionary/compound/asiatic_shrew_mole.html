Asiatic shrew mole

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ʃruː moʊl / (AmE), / ˌeɪʃiˈætɪk ʃruː məʊl / (BrE)
A small, burrowing insectivorous mammal found in eastern Asia, resembling both shrews and moles.
small insectivorous mammal of eastern Asia

Examples

The Asiatic shrew mole burrows through the forest floor in search of insects and worms.
Few people have seen a live Asiatic shrew mole due to its secretive underground lifestyle.
Researchers discovered a new population of Asiatic shrew moles in the mountain valleys of China.
