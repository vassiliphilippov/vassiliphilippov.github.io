motorcycle policeman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtərˌsaɪkəl ˈpɑlismən / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌsaɪkl ˈpɒlɪsmən / (BrE)
a male police officer who rides a motorcycle, often responsible for traffic enforcement and speed checks
male police officer who rides a motorcycle

Examples

The motorcycle policeman pulled over three cars for speeding in one hour.
A motorcycle policeman escorted the parade through the city centre.
I saw a motorcycle policeman checking drivers' licenses on the highway this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorcycle police officer - police officer who rides a motorcycle
  • Synonym: traffic cop - police officer responsible for traffic regulation
