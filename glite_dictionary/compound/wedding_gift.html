wedding gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ɡɪft / (BrE)
a present given to a couple on the occasion of their marriage
present given for a wedding

Examples

They chose a beautiful vase as their wedding gift to the newlyweds.
Guests usually bring a wedding gift to celebrate the couple's marriage.
We received many thoughtful wedding gifts from our friends and family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding present - a present given to someone getting married
  • Degree Lower: birthday gift - a present given to someone on their birthday
