Tampa Bay

1. PROPN

/ ˈtæmpə beɪ / (AmE), / ˈtæmpə beɪ / (BrE)
an inlet of the Gulf of Mexico located in west central Florida, known for its large natural harbor
inlet of the Gulf of Mexico in Florida
