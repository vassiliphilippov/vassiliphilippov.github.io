shit on toast

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪt ɑn toʊst / (AmE), / ʃɪt ɒn təʊst / (BrE)
An informal, derogatory term for something considered worthless or of very poor quality, especially bad food.
worthless thing or very bad food (informal)
informal vulgar

Examples

His homemade soup was so bad I had to call it shit on toast.
The critics agreed that the latest album was just shit on toast.
After tasting the cafeteria lunch, she muttered, 'This is shit on toast.'

Synset Examples

The review described the film as just shit on toast.
He called the meal shit on toast because it tasted awful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage - worthless or very poor quality thing
  • Synonym: crap - something considered poor in quality, especially informally
  • Antonym: delicacy - something considered special or high quality food
