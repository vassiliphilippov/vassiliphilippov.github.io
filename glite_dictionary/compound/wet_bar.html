wet bar

1. NOUN

/ wɛt bɑr / (AmE), / wɛt bɑː / (BrE)
a small bar or counter for mixing and serving drinks that includes a sink with running water
bar with sink for mixing drinks

Examples

We installed a wet bar in the basement for entertaining guests.
The new apartment comes with a wet bar next to the living room.
Having a wet bar makes it much easier to mix cocktails at parties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dry bar - a bar without a sink or running water
