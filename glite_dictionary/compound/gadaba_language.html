Gadaba language

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈdɑːbə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡəˈdɑːbə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Dravidian language spoken by the Gadaba people of eastern India.
Dravidian language of the Gadaba

Examples

Few people outside the region are familiar with the Gadaba language.
The Gadaba language is primarily spoken in Odisha and Andhra Pradesh.
Linguists have studied the Gadaba language to understand its Dravidian roots.
