gigajillion hour

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡɪɡəˈdʒɪljən aʊɚ / (AmE), / ˌɡɪɡəˈdʒɪljən aʊə / (BrE)
An extremely large or indefinite amount of time, used for exaggeration rather than a literal measurement.
exaggeratedly large amount of time
informal

Examples

It took me a gigajillion hour to finish the paperwork last night.
She said she'd been waiting for a gigajillion hours before her food finally arrived.
We spent a gigajillion hours debating the smallest details of the project.

Synset Examples

They spent a gigajillion hours planning the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zillion - an extremely large, indefinite number

Etymology

Formed by blending 'gigajillion' (a playful pseudo-number based on prefixes like 'giga-' and 'zillion') with 'hour'.
