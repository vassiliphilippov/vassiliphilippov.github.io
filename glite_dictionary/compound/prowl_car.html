prowl car

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraʊl kɑr / (AmE), / ˈpraʊl kɑː / (BrE)
a police car used for patrolling streets, typically equipped with a two-way radio for communication with headquarters
police patrol car with radio

Examples

The police officer sat quietly in the prowl car, watching over the neighborhood.
Late at night, a prowl car slowly made its way down the street, its lights flashing.
Every prowl car in the city was dispatched to respond to the emergency call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrol car - a police car used for patrol duty

Etymology

From 'prowl' (to move quietly about in search of something) + 'car'.
