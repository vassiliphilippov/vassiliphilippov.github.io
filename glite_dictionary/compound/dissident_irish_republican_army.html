Dissident Irish Republican Army

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɪsɪdənt ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːr ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˈdɪsɪdənt ˈaɪərɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑː ˈɛm / (BrE)
a radical paramilitary group that broke away from the Provisional IRA in 1997 and has opposed the Northern Ireland peace process through violent acts
Irish republican breakaway paramilitary group

Examples

The Dissident Irish Republican Army continues to oppose the peace process through violent actions.
Authorities are concerned about recent threats claimed by the Dissident Irish Republican Army.
Talks between the British government and the Dissident Irish Republican Army have failed to yield progress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Provisional Irish Republican Army - the main Irish republican paramilitary group from 1969 to 2005
