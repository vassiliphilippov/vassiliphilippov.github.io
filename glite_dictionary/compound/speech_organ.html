speech organ

1. NOUN

/ spiːtʃ ˈɔːrɡən / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
any of the organs, such as the tongue or lips, involved in producing speech sounds
organ involved in speech production

Examples

The tongue is perhaps the most versatile speech organ in the human body.
Damage to a speech organ can significantly affect a person's ability to speak clearly.
Linguists study how each speech organ contributes to the production of different sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: articulator - an organ involved in articulating speech sounds
