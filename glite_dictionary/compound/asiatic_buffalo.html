Asiatic buffalo

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ˈbʌfəloʊ / (AmE), / ˌeɪzɪˈatɪk ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
A large, typically domesticated buffalo native to Asia, often used as a draft animal.
large domesticated Asian buffalo

Examples

The Asiatic buffalo is an essential animal for farmers in many rural parts of Southeast Asia.
Unlike cattle, the Asiatic buffalo thrives in swampy fields and wet environments.
Many villages still rely on the strength of the Asiatic buffalo for plowing their rice paddies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water buffalo - Large domesticated buffalo native to Asia, also called Asiatic buffalo
  • Degree Lower: cow - Domesticated bovine animal
