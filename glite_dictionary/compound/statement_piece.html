statement piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtmənt pis / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtmənt piːs / (BrE)
an item of jewelry or clothing that is striking and designed to attract attention
eye-catching jewelry or fashion item

Examples

That bold red necklace is definitely a statement piece with her black dress.
She wore a simple outfit, letting her earrings serve as the statement piece.
The designer's latest collection includes a statement piece for every occasion.

Synset Examples

Her stackable rings made a beautiful statement piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessory - an item that is added to something else to make it more useful, attractive, or effective
