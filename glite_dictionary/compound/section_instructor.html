section instructor

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃən ɪnˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃən ɪnˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a person who teaches a particular section of a large college or university course, often responsible for a subset of students in lectures, labs, or discussions
teaches a section of a college course

Examples

The biology section instructor clarified complex topics during the weekly discussion group.
Each section instructor is responsible for grading assignments for their students.
Sophia became a section instructor for introductory chemistry while pursuing her doctorate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaching assistant - a person who assists with teaching duties in a college or university
  • Degree Higher: instructor - a person who teaches, especially in a college or university
