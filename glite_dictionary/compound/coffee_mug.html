coffee mug

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi mʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi mʌɡ / (BrE)
a large cup with a handle, designed for serving and drinking coffee
a mug for coffee

Examples

She poured hot tea into her favorite coffee mug instead of a teacup.
The company gave everyone a branded coffee mug as a holiday gift.
He accidentally dropped his coffee mug, and it shattered on the kitchen floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mug - a large, cylindrical drinking cup with a handle
