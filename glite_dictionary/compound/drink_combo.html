drink combo

1. NOUN

/ drɪŋk ˈkɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / drɪŋk ˈkɒmbəʊ / (BrE)
a combination of drinks offered together, usually at a special price or as part of a set
combination of drinks sold together

Examples

We ordered the drink combo to share at the movie theater.
The drink combo includes both a soda and a smoothie for one price.
Many cafes offer a drink combo with any sandwich purchase.

Synset Examples

I ordered a drink combo for a better deal.
