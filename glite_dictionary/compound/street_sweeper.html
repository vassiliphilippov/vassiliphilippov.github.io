street sweeper

1. NOUN

/ strit ˈswipər / (AmE), / striːt ˈswiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean streets, especially as employed by a city or local council
worker who cleans streets

Examples

The street sweeper began his route early in the morning to keep the neighborhood clean.
Sarah thanked the street sweeper for maintaining such tidy sidewalks.
As a child, he admired the street sweeper working diligently in all weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road sweeper - a machine for cleaning roads or streets
