United Nations Children's Fund

1. NOUN

/ juːˌnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz ˈtʃɪldrənz fʌnd / (AmE), / juːˌnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz ˈtʃɪldrənz fʌnd / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations that provides humanitarian and developmental assistance to children and mothers in developing countries
UN agency for children's aid and development

Examples

The United Nations Children's Fund provides vaccines and medical supplies to children in need around the world.
Many schools benefit from support given by the United Nations Children's Fund for their food and education programs.
The United Nations Children's Fund works to improve access to clean water in developing countries.
