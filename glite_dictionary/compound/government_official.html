government official

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌv(ə)nmənt əˈfɪʃ(ə)l / (BrE)
a person who is elected or appointed to administer government policies or functions
person administering government functions

Examples

Several government officials attended the international summit last week.
The new policy was announced by high-ranking government officials during a press conference.
Many government officials are required to disclose their financial interests to avoid conflicts of interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil servant - a person who works for a government department but is not elected
