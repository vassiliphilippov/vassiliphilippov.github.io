to steam clean

1. VERB

/ stiːm klin / (AmE), / stiːm kliːn / (BrE)
to clean something using steam, usually under pressure, to remove dirt or stains
clean using steam under pressure

Examples

We decided to steam clean the carpets to get rid of any lingering odors.
The company offers a service to steam clean your car’s interior.
After spilling coffee on the sofa, they had it steam cleaned by professionals.

Synset Examples

steam-clean the upholstered sofa

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep clean - to clean something very thoroughly
