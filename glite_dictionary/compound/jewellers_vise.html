jeweller's vise

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːələrz vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələz vaɪs / (BrE)
A small, specialized vise used for holding pieces securely while making or repairing jewelry.
small vise used in jewelry making

Examples

The goldsmith carefully secured the delicate ring in the jeweller's vise before engraving it.
A jeweller's vise is essential for working on intricate pieces without damaging them.
She bought a new jeweller's vise for her home workshop to make precise adjustments.
