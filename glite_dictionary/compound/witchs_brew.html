witch's brew

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪtʃɪz bru / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪz bruː / (BrE)
a dangerous or frightening mixture of things, especially a combination of elements that cause trouble or confusion
dangerous or disturbing mixture

Examples

The new law is a witch's brew of confusing regulations and harsh penalties.
Their financial crisis resulted from a witch's brew of bad decisions and dishonest advice.
The media created a witch's brew of fear and misinformation during the epidemic.

Synset Examples

a witches' brew of gangsters and terrorists
mixing dope and alcohol creates a witches' brew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things

Etymology

From the traditional image of a witch concocting magical, often sinister, mixtures in a cauldron.
