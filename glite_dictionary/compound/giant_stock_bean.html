giant stock bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt stɑk biːn / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt stɒk biːn / (BrE)
an annual, semi-erect bushy plant of tropical South America with long pods and white seeds, grown especially for animal forage
tropical South American forage plant

Examples

Farmers in the region began cultivating giant stock bean as a source of high-quality forage for their livestock.
The giant stock bean thrives in tropical climates and is valued for its long, nutritious pods.
Researchers are studying the giant stock bean for its potential to improve animal feed production.
