Southern Baptist Convention

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈbæptɪst kənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the largest association of Baptist churches in the United States, predominantly in the southern states
major U.S. Baptist church association

Examples

The Southern Baptist Convention convened its annual meeting in Nashville this year.
Many churches across the southern United States are affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention.
The Southern Baptist Convention has a major influence on religious life in the region.
