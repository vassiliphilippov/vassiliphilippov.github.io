Berkshire Hills

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝk.ʃɚ hɪlz / (AmE), / ˈbɜːk.ʃə hɪlz / (BrE)
a low mountain range in western Massachusetts known for its resorts and scenic beauty
mountain range and resort area in western Massachusetts

Examples

Many artists and writers find inspiration in the natural beauty of the Berkshire Hills.
Every summer, tourists flock to the Berkshire Hills for outdoor activities and music festivals.
The fall foliage in the Berkshire Hills attracts photographers from all over the country.

Etymology

Named after Berkshire County, itself named after Berkshire in England.
