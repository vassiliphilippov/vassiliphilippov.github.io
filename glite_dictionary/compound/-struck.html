-struck

1. ADJ

/ strʌk / (AmE), / strʌk / (BrE)
(in combination) deeply affected or overwhelmed by a particular emotion, event, or condition.
deeply affected by something (in compounds)

Examples

She was awe-struck by the beauty of the sunset.
After hearing the news, he stood thunderstruck and speechless.
Many fans felt star-struck when meeting their favorite actor.

Synset Examples

conscience-smitten
awe-struck

Related Terms

  • Synonym: -smitten - (in combination) deeply affected or overwhelmed by a particular emotion or condition

Etymology

From the past participle of 'strike', used in forming compounds such as 'awe-struck' or 'thunderstruck'.
