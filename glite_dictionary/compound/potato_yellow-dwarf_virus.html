potato yellow-dwarf virus

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈjɛloʊ daɪˈɔrf ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈjɛləʊ daɪˈdʒɔːf ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a virus that causes stunting and yellowing of the leaves of potato plants
virus affecting potato plants with yellowing and stunting
medical

Examples

Researchers are studying the effects of potato yellow-dwarf virus on crop yields.
Plants infected with potato yellow-dwarf virus exhibit noticeable stunting and yellow leaves.
Prevention measures can help protect fields from the spread of potato yellow-dwarf virus.
