bagel belt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪɡəl bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪɡl̩ bɛlt / (BrE)
A novelty belt designed to hold bagels or fashioned to resemble a bagel, often worn for humorous effect.
novelty belt for holding or resembling bagels

Examples

The comedian walked on stage wearing a bright bagel belt to get a laugh from the crowd.
At the breakfast-themed parade, several participants sported the iconic bagel belt.
He gifted his friend a homemade bagel belt as a quirky birthday present.
