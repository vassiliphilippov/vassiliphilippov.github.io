right-handed pitcher

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪtˈhændɪd ˈpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˌraɪtˈhændɪd ˈpɪtʃə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a pitcher who throws with their right hand.
baseball pitcher who throws right-handed

Examples

The team brought in a right-handed pitcher to face the next batter.
As a right-handed pitcher, he excelled at throwing fastballs to the outside corner.
Most of their right-handed pitchers have strong curveballs in their arsenal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: left-handed pitcher - (in baseball) a pitcher who throws with their left hand
