Great Eastern Islamic Raiders' Front

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt ˈiːstərn aɪˈslɑmɪk reɪˈdeɪərz frʌnt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈiːstən ɪˈslɑːmɪk reɪˈdeɪəz frʌnt / (BrE)
a Turkish Islamist militant group known for terrorist attacks, particularly in Istanbul, and opposition to Turkey's secular government and Western ties
Turkish Islamist militant organization
