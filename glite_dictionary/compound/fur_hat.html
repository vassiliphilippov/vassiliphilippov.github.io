fur hat

1. NOUN

/ fɝːˈhæt / (AmE), / fɜːˈhæt / (BrE)
A hat made from animal fur, typically worn for warmth or as a fashion accessory.
a hat made of animal fur

Examples

She wore a luxurious fur hat to keep warm during the snowstorm.
The old photograph showed men and women in heavy coats and fur hats.
He saved up for a soft fur hat before his winter trip to Moscow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ushanka - a Russian fur cap with ear flaps
