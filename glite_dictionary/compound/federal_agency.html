federal agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdəɹəl ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
A government organization or administrative unit established by a federal government to carry out specific functions or services.
organization within the federal government

Examples

The Environmental Protection Agency is a well-known federal agency responsible for protecting human health and the environment.
Each federal agency must submit its budget proposal to Congress every fiscal year.
A new federal agency was created to oversee national cybersecurity efforts.
