sweet-breathed

1. ADJ

/ swit ˈbrɛðd / (AmE), / swiːt ˈbrɛðd / (BrE)
having breath that is pleasantly fragrant or agreeable in smell
having pleasantly smelling breath

Examples

The poet described the child as sweet-breathed in his gentle lullaby.
She greeted him in the morning with her sweet-breathed presence, carrying the scent of mint.
Pastoral writers often praise the sweet-breathed lambs in the meadows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet-scented - having a sweet or pleasant smell
