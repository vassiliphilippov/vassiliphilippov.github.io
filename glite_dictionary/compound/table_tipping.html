table tipping

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˈtɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ ˈtɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the movement or manipulation of a table during a séance, attributed to the actions of spirits
the movement of a table in a séance

Examples

During the séance, the group claimed that table tipping allowed them to communicate with spirits.
Skeptics believe that table tipping is caused by unconscious movements rather than supernatural forces.
In the 19th century, table tipping became a popular tool among spiritualists.
