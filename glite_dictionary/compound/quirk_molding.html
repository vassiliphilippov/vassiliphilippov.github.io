quirk molding

1. NOUN

/ kwɝk ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɜːk ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
a molding that features a small, narrow groove as part of its design, often used as a decorative architectural detail
molding with a small groove

Examples

The intricate quirk molding along the ceiling added elegance to the room.
Restorers carefully repaired the damaged quirk molding around the old wooden door frame.
The cabinetmaker used quirk molding to create a subtle decorative effect on the panel edges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molding - a shaped strip of material used as a decoration in architecture or furniture
