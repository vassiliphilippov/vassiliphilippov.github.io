root beer float

1. NOUN

/ ˈrut ˌbɪr floʊt / (AmE), / ˈruːt ˌbɪə fləʊt / (BrE)
a drink made by placing a scoop of ice cream in a glass of root beer, typically served as a dessert
dessert drink: ice cream in root beer
american

Examples

I'd love a root beer float after dinner to cool off.
The diner served classic root beer floats with vanilla ice cream.
She made a root beer float for her little brother's birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice-cream soda - a drink made by mixing ice cream and soda
