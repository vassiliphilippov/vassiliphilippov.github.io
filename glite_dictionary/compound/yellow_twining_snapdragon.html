yellow twining snapdragon

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈtwaɪnɪŋ ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈtwaɪnɪŋ ˈsnæpˌdraɡən / (BrE)
A plant native to the southwestern United States with yellow flowers and stems that twist and twine through other vegetation.
plant with yellow twining flowers

Examples

The yellow twining snapdragon added a splash of color to the desert landscape.
We spotted a yellow twining snapdragon climbing through the brush on our hike.
Gardeners appreciate the resilience of the yellow twining snapdragon in dry regions.
