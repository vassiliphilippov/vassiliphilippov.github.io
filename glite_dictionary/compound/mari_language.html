Mari language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːri ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːri ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Mari people, chiefly in the Mari El republic of Russia
Uralic language of the Mari people

Examples

Many children in the Mari El republic learn the Mari language in school.
Linguists have documented traditional songs in the Mari language.
He is fluent in both Russian and the Mari language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnic - group of languages closely related to Mari within Uralic family
  • Synonym: Udmurt language - neighboring Uralic language spoken in the same region

Etymology

Mari, the self-designation of the Mari people; language from Latin 'lingua'.
