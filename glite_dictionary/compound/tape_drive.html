tape drive

1. NOUN

/ teɪp draɪv / (AmE), / teɪp draɪv / (BrE)
a device that reads from or writes data to magnetic tape, often used for data storage or backup
device for reading/writing data to magnetic tape

Examples

The backup system uses a tape drive to store copies of important files.
Many older computers relied on a tape drive for data storage.
She installed the new tape drive to archive the company's records.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard drive - device for storing data on a hard disk
  • Synonym: disk drive - device that reads from or writes to a disk
