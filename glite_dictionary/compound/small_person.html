small person

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is smaller than the average size in height or build
person of below average size

Examples

As a small person, she sometimes found it hard to reach the top shelves.
He was a small person but full of confidence and energy.
Designers now create clothes to better fit the needs of a small person.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tall person - a person who is taller than average
  • Synonym: short person - a person who is short in stature
