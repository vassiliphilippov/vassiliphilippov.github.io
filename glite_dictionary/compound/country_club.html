country club

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri klʌb / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri klʌb / (BrE)
a private club, usually located in the suburbs, that provides recreational and social activities, often including golf, tennis, and dining facilities
a private suburban recreational and social club

Examples

He spent his weekends at the country club playing golf with friends.
The new country club offers tennis courts and an outdoor pool for its members.
Joining a country club often requires an expensive membership fee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf club - a club where people play golf and socialize
  • Synonym: sports club - an organized group for playing various sports
