letter writer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtər ˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə ˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes letters to communicate, especially by mail or email
a person who writes letters

Examples

She has always been an enthusiastic letter writer, keeping in touch with friends around the world.
As a letter writer, he prefers handwritten notes to emails.
The editor published a response to the regular letter writer in the local newspaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: correspondent - a person who communicates by exchanging letters
