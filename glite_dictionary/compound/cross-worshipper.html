Cross-worshipper

1. NOUN

/ krɒs ˈwʌrʃəpər / (AmE), / krɒs ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who venerates or worships the Christian cross, typically used to describe Christians in a religious or historical context
person who venerates the Christian cross
