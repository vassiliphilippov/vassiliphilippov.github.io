station agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃən ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
the person responsible for managing and overseeing the operations at a railway station
person in charge of a railway station

Examples

The station agent greeted passengers and answered questions about train schedules.
As a station agent, she was responsible for both security and customer service at the depot.
After twenty years as a station agent, he knew every regular commuter by name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationmaster - person in charge of a railway station
