onion plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.jən plænt / (AmE), / ˈʌn.jən plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bulbous plant with hollow leaves, cultivated worldwide for its rounded edible bulb.
bulbous plant producing edible onions

Examples

The onion plant thrives in well-drained, fertile soils with plenty of sunlight.
Farmers harvest the bulbs from the onion plant when the leaves begin to yellow and fall over.
Growing an onion plant from seed can take several months before it's ready for harvest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion - the edible bulb produced by the onion plant
