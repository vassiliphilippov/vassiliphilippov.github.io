smooth lip fern

1. NOUN

/ smuð lɪp fɝːn / (AmE), / smuːð lɪp fəːn / (BrE)
A species of small, delicate fern native to the southeastern United States, northern Mexico, and Jamaica, known for its smooth-margined fronds.
a type of fern with smooth fronds

Examples

The smooth lip fern thrives in rocky woodlands and shaded limestone outcrops.
Gardeners often choose the smooth lip fern for its attractive, smooth-edged leaves.
Researchers studied the distribution of the smooth lip fern across different regions of Jamaica.
