friendly wave

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndli weɪv / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli weɪv / (BrE)
a gesture of waving the hand in a warm or welcoming manner as a greeting or sign of goodwill
a warm, welcoming hand wave

Examples

She greeted her neighbor with a friendly wave each morning.
A friendly wave from across the street made him feel welcomed.
He offered a friendly wave before leaving the party.

Synset Examples

He gave a friendly wave as he passed by.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wave - a movement of the hand or arm as a greeting or signal
