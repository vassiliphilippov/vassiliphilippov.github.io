antique vase

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtik veɪs / (AmE), / ænˈtiːk vɑːz / (BrE)
a valuable and usually decorative vase that is considered old and often prized for its age, beauty, or historical interest
old, valuable decorative vase

Examples

The museum displayed a stunning antique vase from the Ming dynasty.
During the auction, several bidders competed for the rare antique vase.
She carefully dusted the antique vase her grandmother had passed down to her.

Synset Examples

He found the antique vase, which was priceless.
I’ll take that antique vase for $500, Alex.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antique - an object that is old and often valuable
  • Synonym: vase - an open container used for holding flowers or as a decoration
