fencing sword

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnsɪŋ sɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfɛnsɪŋ sɔːd / (BrE)
a light, blunted sword used in the sport of fencing for practice and competition
sword used in fencing

Examples

She carefully selected her fencing sword before the tournament began.
The three main types of fencing sword are the foil, épée, and sabre.
You must wear protective clothing when using a fencing sword during practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foil - a type of fencing sword with a flexible rectangular blade
  • Synonym: épée - a fencing sword with a stiff, triangular blade
  • Synonym: sabre - a fencing sword with a curved, flat blade
