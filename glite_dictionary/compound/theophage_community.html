theophage community

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiːoʊˌfeɪdʒ kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈθiːə(ʊ)ˌfeɪdʒ kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group whose members share beliefs about the practice of consuming a deity, especially through ritual or symbolic means
group sharing belief in eating deities

Examples

Members of the theophage community participated in a symbolic ritual during the festival.
Scholars have studied the history and beliefs of the theophage community in ancient cultures.
The theophage community attracted controversy for its unconventional religious practices.

Synset Examples

The theophage community gathered to discuss their beliefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theophage - a person or group that consumes a deity, especially in ritual

Etymology

Formed from 'theophage' (from Greek 'theos' meaning 'god' and 'phagein' meaning 'to eat') and 'community' (from Latin 'communitas').
