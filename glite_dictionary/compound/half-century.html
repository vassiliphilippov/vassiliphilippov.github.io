half-century

1. NOUN

/ ˌhæfˈsɛntʃəri / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈsɛntʃəri / (BrE)
A period of fifty years.
a period of 50 years

Examples

The city has changed dramatically over the last half-century.
His research covers more than a half-century of scientific progress.
It took a half-century to complete the reconstruction after the war.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: century - a period of 100 years

Etymology

Formed from 'half' (meaning 50%) and 'century' (from Latin 'centuria', a group of 100).
