flame nettle

1. NOUN

/ fleɪm ˈnɛtəl / (AmE), / fleɪm ˈnɛtəl / (BrE)
any of several tropical plants of the genus Coleus, known for their brightly colored, variegated leaves and blue flower spikes, often grown as ornamentals
ornamental plant with multicolored leaves

Examples

The gardener planted a flame nettle for its strikingly colorful foliage.
A pot of flame nettle brightened up the dull corner of the patio.
Many people choose flame nettle as a decorative indoor plant due to its beautiful leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coleus - a genus of tropical plants with colorful leaves
