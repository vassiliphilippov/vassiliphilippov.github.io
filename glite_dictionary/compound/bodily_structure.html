bodily structure

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdɪli ˈstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a particular complex part of an organism's anatomy, relating to how its parts are constructed and arranged
complex anatomical part of a living thing

Examples

The physiologist explained how each bodily structure has a distinct function within the organism.
Genetic disorders can sometimes affect the development of a bodily structure.
The diagram highlighted the intricate bodily structures involved in digestion.

Synset Examples

he has good bone structure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anatomical structure - any part of the body or organism's physical form
