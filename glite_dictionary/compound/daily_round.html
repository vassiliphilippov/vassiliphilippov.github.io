daily round

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli raʊnd / (BrE)
the usual activities and tasks performed each day, especially as part of a person's routine
regular daily activities or routine

Examples

After a short vacation, returning to his daily round felt strangely comforting.
The nurse's daily round includes checking on each patient in the morning.
Despite the excitement of travel, she missed the familiarity of her daily round.

Synset Examples

the doctor made his rounds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: routine - a regular sequence of actions or procedures
  • Synonym: schedule - a plan of activities or events and when they will happen
