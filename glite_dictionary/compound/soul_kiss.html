soul kiss

1. NOUN

/ soʊl kɪs / (AmE), / səʊl kɪs / (BrE)
An open-mouthed kiss in which one's tongue is inserted into the other person's mouth; a French kiss.
open-mouthed kiss with tongues

Examples

She surprised him with a passionate soul kiss in the middle of the dance floor.
The movie's romantic scene ended with a long soul kiss beneath the rain.
They shared a soul kiss before saying goodbye at the airport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French kiss - an open-mouthed kiss with tongues inserted

2. NOUN

/ soʊl kɪs / (AmE), / səʊl kɪs / (BrE)
A deep, intimate kiss that expresses profound emotional connection between two people.
deep, intimate kiss

Examples

Their first soul kiss left them both breathless and deeply moved.
He leaned in for a soul kiss, holding her close in the quiet room.
Some believe that a true soul kiss only happens once in a lifetime.

Synset Examples

They shared a soul kiss that transcended words.
A soul kiss can change everything between two people.
Their connection felt like a soul kiss under the stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: french kiss - a kiss with the insertion of the tongue into the partner's mouth
  • Degree Lower: peck - a light, quick kiss
