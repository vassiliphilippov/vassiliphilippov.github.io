Bible thumper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪbəl ˌθʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl ˌθʌmpə / (BrE)
A person who is aggressively or ostentatiously fervent in their religious beliefs, especially Christianity, and often vocal about promoting them.
zealously outspoken religious person
informal

Examples

His grandmother was a devoted Bible thumper who never missed a chance to talk about faith.
The politician was accused of being a Bible thumper during his campaign speeches.
While some appreciated her passion, others found her Bible thumper ways a bit overwhelming.

Synset Examples

Her Bible thumper attitude makes conversations difficult.
He’s a Bible thumper who insists on strict morals.
Being a Bible thumper can alienate some friends.
The group’s Bible thumping turned off many attendees.
He was criticized for his constant Bible thumping.
Bible thumping has its place, but so does compassion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zealot - a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of their religious, political, or other ideals

Etymology

From 'Bible' (the Christian scriptures) and 'thumper' (someone who pounds or strikes), implying vocal and forceful advocacy of the Bible.
