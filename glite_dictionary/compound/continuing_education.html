continuing education

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɪnjuɪŋ ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / kənˈtɪnjʊɪŋ ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
education for adults that is taken after leaving formal schooling, often on a part-time basis, especially to further professional or personal development.
adult education taken after formal schooling

Examples

She decided to pursue continuing education to enhance her career prospects.
Many universities offer continuing education classes in the evenings and on weekends.
Employers often support continuing education as a way to keep their staff's skills up to date.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adult education - education provided to adults after leaving the formal school system
