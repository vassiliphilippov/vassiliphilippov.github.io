downy chess

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaʊni tʃɛs / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni tʃɛs / (BrE)
an annual or winter annual grass with softly hairy leaves, native to the Mediterranean region
hairy-leaved Mediterranean annual grass

Examples

The field was dotted with patches of downy chess among the other wild grasses.
Farmers consider downy chess a troublesome weed in some Mediterranean areas.
In spring, the softly hairy leaves of downy chess become easily recognizable.
