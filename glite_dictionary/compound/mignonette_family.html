mignonette family

1. NOUN

/ mɪn.jəˈnɛt ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / mɪn.jəˈnɛt ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of mainly Mediterranean herbs, including the mignonette, known scientifically as Resedaceae
family of Mediterranean herbs (Resedaceae)

Examples

The mignonette family is known for its aromatic herbs commonly found in Mediterranean regions.
Some species in the mignonette family are cultivated as ornamental plants.
Scientists identified a new genus within the mignonette family during their fieldwork in southern Europe.
