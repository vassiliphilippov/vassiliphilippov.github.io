Southern dewberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈduːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈdjuːˌbɛri / (BrE)
A trailing bramble plant native to southern North America, producing edible blackberry-like fruit.
fruiting bramble plant of southern North America

Examples

The Southern dewberry thrives in sandy soils throughout the Gulf Coast region.
She gathered ripe Southern dewberries for making jam.
Birds and small mammals feed on the fruit of the Southern dewberry in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dewberry - a type of bramble with edible fruit similar to a blackberry
