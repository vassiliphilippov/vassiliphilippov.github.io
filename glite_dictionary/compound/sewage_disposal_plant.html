sewage disposal plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuɪdʒ dɪˈspoʊzəl plænt / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪdʒ dɪˈspəʊzəl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility where sewage is treated and disposed of, typically by removing contaminants and processing the waste
facility for treating and disposing of sewage

Examples

The city recently upgraded its sewage disposal plant to handle a greater volume of waste.
Environmental regulations require careful monitoring of emissions from any sewage disposal plant.
Construction of the new sewage disposal plant is scheduled to begin next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wastewater treatment plant - a facility that treats wastewater, including sewage, before releasing it into the environment
