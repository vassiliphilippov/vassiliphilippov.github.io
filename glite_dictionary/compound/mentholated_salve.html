mentholated salve

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛnθəˌleɪtɪd sælv / (AmE), / ˈmɛnθəˌleɪtɪd sɑːlv / (BrE)
a medicinal ointment containing menthol, used to relieve pain or irritation of the skin or respiratory passages
ointment with menthol for pain/irritation relief
medical

Examples

He applied the mentholated salve to his chest to help relieve his cough.
The mentholated salve gave her a cooling sensation on the irritated skin.
Doctors sometimes recommend a mentholated salve for minor muscle aches and pains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ointment - a smooth substance applied to the skin for medicinal purposes
  • Synonym: balm - a soothing or healing substance used on the skin

Etymology

From 'menthol' (a compound from mint oils) + 'salve' (from Old English *sealf*, ointment).
