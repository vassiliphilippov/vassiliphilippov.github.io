on the turf

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə tɝf / (AmE), / ɒn ðə tɜːf / (BrE)
on a sports field or playing field, especially where grass or artificial turf is used
on a sports playing field

Examples

The players warmed up on the turf before the match began.
She scored the winning goal on the turf in front of thousands of fans.
After weeks of rain, the game was finally played on the turf as scheduled.

Synset Examples

The game is scheduled to begin on the turf.
They practiced hard on the turf before the finals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field - an area used for sporting events or games
