currant bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrənt bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈkʌrənt bʊʃ / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub of the genus Ribes that produces currants
shrub producing currants

Examples

The currant bush in our garden is heavy with ripe fruit every summer.
He pruned the currant bush to encourage new growth next year.
Birds often nest in the dense branches of the currant bush near the fence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: currant - small edible berry produced by currant bushes
