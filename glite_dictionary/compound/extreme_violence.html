extreme violence

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstrim vaɪˈə.ləns / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːm vaɪˈə.ləns / (BrE)
Acts of violence that are intense, severe, and often graphic or excessive.
very intense or graphic violence

Examples

The documentary was criticized for its depiction of extreme violence.
Some video games contain scenes of extreme violence that may not be suitable for children.
The rating was due to the film's use of extreme violence and disturbing imagery.

Synset Examples

The movie depicted extreme violence and gore.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: violence - the use of physical force to harm someone or something
