Asiatic black bear

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ˈblæk ˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˌeɪzɪˈætɪk ˈblæk ˌbeə / (BrE)
a medium-sized bear (Ursus thibetanus) with a black coat and a white V-shaped patch on the chest, native to central and eastern Asia
a bear species of central and eastern Asia

Examples

The Asiatic black bear is known for the distinctive white patch on its chest.
Deforestation has greatly affected the habitat of the Asiatic black bear in recent years.
Conservationists are working to protect the Asiatic black bear from illegal hunting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black bear - a species of bear native to North America
  • Degree Higher: brown bear - a large bear species found in Eurasia and North America
