department of anthropology

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑrtmənt əv ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
an academic division within a college or university that specializes in teaching and research in anthropology
university division for anthropology studies

Examples

She decided to apply to the department of anthropology at her university for graduate studies.
The department of anthropology is hosting a lecture on indigenous cultures next week.
After years of research, he became the head of the department of anthropology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - an administrative unit within an organization, especially a university or company
