coffee blend

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi blɛnd / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi blɛnd / (BrE)
a mixture of different varieties or origins of coffee beans combined to create a particular flavor profile
mixture of different coffee beans

Examples

The barista recommended their house coffee blend for a smooth and rich taste.
She prefers a medium-roast coffee blend in the morning to start her day.
The new coffee blend combines beans from Colombia and Ethiopia for a unique flavor.

Synset Examples

This new coffee blend is even gooder than the last one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: espresso blend - a blend of coffee beans designed for making espresso
