hen turkey

1. NOUN

/ hɛn ˈtɝki / (AmE), / hɛn ˈtɜːki / (BrE)
the meat of a female turkey, regarded as more tender or desirable than that of a male turkey
meat of a female turkey

Examples

This recipe calls for the use of hen turkey rather than a tom for a more tender roast.
Chefs often recommend hen turkey because of its fine texture and mild flavor.
We bought a fresh hen turkey for Thanksgiving dinner this year.

Synset Examples

Most experts agree that a hen turkey is better than a tom.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tom turkey - meat of a male turkey
