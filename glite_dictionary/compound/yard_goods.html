yard goods

1. NOUN

/ jɑrd ɡʊdz / (AmE), / jɑːd ɡʊdz / (BrE)
cloth or fabric that is sold by length, measured in yards.
fabrics sold by the yard

Examples

The store carries a wide selection of yard goods for quilting enthusiasts.
She purchased several yards of floral yard goods to make new curtains.
Special discounts are available on all yard goods this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fabric - cloth produced by weaving or knitting textile fibers
  • Synonym: textile - a type of cloth or woven fabric
