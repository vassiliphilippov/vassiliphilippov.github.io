bullsheet response

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlʃiːt rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈbʊlʃiːt rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
an answer or statement that is nonsensical or deliberately untruthful, often to avoid giving a straight answer
dishonest or nonsensical answer
informal

Examples

Whenever management is confronted, they give a bullsheet response that dodges the real issues.
I could tell his explanation was just a bullsheet response to hide his mistake.
Tired of the bullsheet responses in meetings, she demanded a clear answer.

Synset Examples

This is just another bullsheet response.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit response - an answer that is nonsense or deliberately untruthful

Etymology

Alteration of 'bullshit', using 'sheet' as a euphemism for 'shit' to avoid vulgarity.
