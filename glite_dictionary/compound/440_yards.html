440 yards

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːr ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɔːrti ˈjɑrdz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː ˈhʌndrəd ˈfɔːti ˈjɑːdz / (BrE)
A unit of length equal to a quarter of a mile, traditionally used in athletics and measurement.
unit of length; quarter mile

Examples

The old track was exactly 440 yards long, making it ideal for quarter-mile races.
She completed the swim, followed by a 440 yards run to the finish line.
Historically, the 440 yards was a standard event in athletics before being replaced by the 400 meters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarter mile - A unit of distance equal to a quarter of a mile (440 yards)
