cassette deck

1. NOUN

/ kəˈsɛt dɛk / (AmE), / kəˈsɛt dɛk / (BrE)
a machine for playing and recording audio on cassette tapes
device for playing/recording cassette tapes

Examples

He still prefers listening to music on his old cassette deck.
The cassette deck in my car stopped working last week.
She used a portable cassette deck to record interviews for her research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape recorder - a machine for recording and playing sound on magnetic tape
  • Antonym: turntable - a machine for playing vinyl records
