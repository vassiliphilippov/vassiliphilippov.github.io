orange tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ triː / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ triː / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Citrus that produces oranges, typically cultivated for its fruit
a citrus tree that produces oranges

Examples

The orange tree in our backyard is filled with ripe fruit every spring.
Ancient traders introduced the orange tree to Europe centuries ago.
She planted an orange tree to provide fresh fruit for her family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: citrus tree - a tree of the genus Citrus, bearing edible fruits such as oranges, lemons, limes
  • Degree Lower: lemon tree - a citrus tree that produces lemons
  • Degree Higher: grapefruit tree - a citrus tree that produces grapefruits
