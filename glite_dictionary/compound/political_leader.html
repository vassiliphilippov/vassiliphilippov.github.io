political leader

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who holds a prominent position or plays a leading role in political party activities or government affairs
important figure in politics or government

Examples

Many people look up to a political leader who acts with integrity.
The political leader addressed the crowd during the national celebration.
Every nation relies on the vision and skill of its political leaders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statesman - an experienced and respected political leader or figure
  • Synonym: politician - a person who is professionally involved in politics, especially as a holder of an elected office
