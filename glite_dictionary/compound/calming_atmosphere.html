calming atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmɪŋ ˈæθməˌsfɪr / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmɪŋ ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
an environment or setting that makes people feel relaxed and peaceful
soothing, peaceful environment

Examples

The spa is designed to provide a calming atmosphere for its clients.
Soft music and dim lights contributed to the calming atmosphere of the restaurant.
She decorated her office plants to help achieve a calming atmosphere at work.

Synset Examples

She lit incense to create a calming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxing atmosphere - an environment conducive to relaxation or comfort
  • Antonym: tense atmosphere - an environment filled with tension or unease
