wine vinegar

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈvɪnəɡɚ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈvɪnɪɡə / (BrE)
vinegar made from fermented wine, often used in cooking and salad dressings
vinegar made from wine

Examples

The salad dressing calls for a splash of wine vinegar to enhance the flavor.
She prefers using wine vinegar instead of balsamic in most of her recipes.
A bottle of red wine vinegar sat next to the olive oil on the kitchen shelf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vinegar - sour liquid produced by fermentation, used in cooking
