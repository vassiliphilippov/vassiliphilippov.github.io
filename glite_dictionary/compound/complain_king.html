complain king

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpleɪn kɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪn kɪŋ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who frequently complains, especially in an exaggerated or habitual manner
person who complains frequently
informal

Examples

Everyone avoids John at meetings because he's such a complain king.
Don't be a complain king—try to see the positive side of things!
As the self-proclaimed complain king, Mike finds fault with almost everything.

Synset Examples

He's known as the complain king of the office.
Even small issues turn him into the complain king.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whiner - a person who complains excessively
  • Synonym: grumbler - someone who grumbles or complains often
  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
