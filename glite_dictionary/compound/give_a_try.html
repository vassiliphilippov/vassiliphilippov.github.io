to give a try

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ə traɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə traɪ / (BrE)
to attempt to do something; to try doing something new or unfamiliar
make an attempt

Examples

I'm not sure I'll be any good at chess, but I want to give a try.
She decided to give a try at singing in the talent show.
If you haven't written poetry before, you should give a try.

Synset Examples

Why not give this new game a try?
I will give cooking a try this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - to make an attempt or effort to do something
  • Synonym: attempt - to make an effort to achieve or complete something
