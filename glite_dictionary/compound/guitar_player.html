guitar player

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪˈtɑr ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ɡɪˈtɑː ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the guitar, especially as a musician
someone who plays the guitar

Examples

He was an accomplished guitar player who performed in several jazz bands.
The guitar player took a solo that wowed the entire audience.
Many legendary songs owe their magic to a talented guitar player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guitarist - someone who plays the guitar
