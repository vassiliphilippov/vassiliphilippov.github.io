European blackbird

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌroʊpiən ˈblækˌbɝd / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiən ˈblækˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a common European thrush (Turdus merula) with all-black plumage in males and brownish plumage in females, known for its melodious song
a common European thrush with black plumage

Examples

The European blackbird is renowned for its rich and melodious singing at dawn.
I spotted a male European blackbird with glossy black feathers and a bright yellow beak.
Unlike its American namesake, the European blackbird is actually a species of thrush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blackbird - general name for black-plumaged thrushes, especially outside Europe
