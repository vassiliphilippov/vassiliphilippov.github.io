blue pimpernel

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈpɪmpərˌnɛl / (AmE), / bluː ˈpɪmpəˌnɛl / (BrE)
a North American mint plant (Pelletiera virginica) that produces a resinous substance once used as an antispasmodic
North American mint plant, former antispasmodic

Examples

The blue pimpernel was once widely used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic properties.
Researchers are studying the potential benefits of blue pimpernel in herbal treatments.
In the wild, the blue pimpernel can be recognized by its small blue flowers and minty aroma.
