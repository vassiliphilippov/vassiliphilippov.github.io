boron fertiliser

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɹˌɑn ˈfɝtəˌlaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrɒn ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
A fertilizer containing boron as an essential micronutrient to promote plant growth and development.
fertilizer containing boron for plants

Examples

Proper application of boron fertiliser can help prevent deficiencies in certain crops.
The farmers decided to use a boron fertiliser to improve their sunflower yield.
Too much boron fertiliser can harm plants, so careful dosage is important.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to enhance plant growth
