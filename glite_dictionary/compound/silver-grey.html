silver-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color that resembles silver; having a sheen or hue similar to that of silver.
grey color resembling silver

Examples

The old artist had silver-grey hair that gleamed in the sunlight.
Mist floated over the silver-grey lake at dawn, giving the scene an ethereal quality.
She chose a silver-grey dress for the evening gala, looking both elegant and sophisticated.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: grey - of a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: silver - a shiny, metallic grayish-white color
