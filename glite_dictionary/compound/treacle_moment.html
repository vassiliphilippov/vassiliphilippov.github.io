treacle moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈtriːkəl ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈtriːkəl ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a scene or passage in a book, film, or other work that is excessively sentimental or cloying
overly sentimental scene

Examples

Most romantic comedies have at least one treacle moment to tug at the audience's heartstrings.
The story could have been better without so many treacle moments near the end.
He rolled his eyes at yet another treacle moment during the award ceremony.

Synset Examples

The movie was filled with treacle moments that made me cringe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentimental - having or arousing feelings of tenderness or nostalgia
  • Synonym: cloying - disgust or sicken (someone) with an excess of sweetness
  • Antonym: hard-boiled - tough and unsentimental

Etymology

From 'treacle' meaning something overly sweet or sentimental, and 'moment.'
