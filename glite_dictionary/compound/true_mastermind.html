true mastermind

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈmæstərˌmaɪnd / (AmE), / truː ˈmɑːstəmaɪnd / (BrE)
a person who is exceptionally skilled and clever at planning and organizing complex activities, often behind the scenes
a highly skilled and clever planner

Examples

Detectives eventually discovered that the true mastermind behind the operation had never been seen at the scene.
Her colleagues often called her the true mastermind of the company's success.
Many believed he was simply a spokesperson, but in reality, he was the true mastermind of the plan.

Synset Examples

She is a true mastermind at organizing events seamlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mastermind - a person with outstanding intellect or skill who plans and directs a complex project or scheme
