waste of energy

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / weɪst əv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
an action or effort that does not achieve anything useful and therefore is considered pointless
futile or useless effort

Examples

Trying to change his mind will just be a waste of energy.
She realized cleaning the yard in the pouring rain was a complete waste of energy.
It's a waste of energy arguing over things that can't be changed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste of time - an action that achieves nothing useful
  • Degree Lower: effort - physical or mental energy used to achieve something
