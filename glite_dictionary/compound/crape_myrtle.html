crape myrtle

1. NOUN

/ kreɪp ˈmɜːrtəl / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈmɜːtəl / (BrE)
an ornamental shrub or small tree, native to eastern India and widely planted in the southern United States, known for its colorful summer flowers
ornamental flowering shrub or tree

Examples

The crape myrtle in our yard bursts into vibrant pink blooms every summer.
Homeowners often choose crape myrtle trees for their hardiness and lengthy flowering season.
We planted a crape myrtle along the driveway to add color to the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crepe myrtle - alternative spelling of 'crape myrtle', ornamental flowering shrub/tree
