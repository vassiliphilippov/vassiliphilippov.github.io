too large

1. ADJ

/ tuː lɑrdʒ / (AmE), / tuː lɑːdʒ / (BrE)
excessively large in size, amount, or degree; more than is needed or acceptable
excessively large; more than needed

Examples

The suitcase was too large to fit in the overhead compartment.
This shirt is too large for me; I need a smaller size.
Her excitement led her to bake a cake that was simply too large for the gathering.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: large - of considerable or relatively great size
  • Degree Higher: enormous - very large; much greater than usual
  • Antonym: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
