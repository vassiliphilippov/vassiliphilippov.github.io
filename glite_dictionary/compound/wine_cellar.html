wine cellar

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
A room or space, usually underground, used for storing wine, often in bottles or barrels, for aging and preservation.
room for storing wine

Examples

Their house has a beautifully restored wine cellar that keeps the wine at the perfect temperature.
He started collecting rare bottles to fill his wine cellar.
The chef led us downstairs to the restaurant's impressive wine cellar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cellar - an underground room used as a storage space
