apricot sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpɹɪˌkɑt sɔs / (AmE), / ˈæpɹɪkɒt sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweet sauce made from apricot preserves or puree, often used as an accompaniment to Chinese dishes or desserts.
sweet sauce made from apricots

Examples

The spring rolls were served with a tangy apricot sauce that perfectly complemented their flavor.
She drizzled warm apricot sauce over the ice cream for a unique dessert.
The chef recommended pairing the duck with a spicy apricot sauce for an Asian-inspired twist.
