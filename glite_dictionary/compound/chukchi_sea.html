Chukchi Sea

1. PROPN

/ ˈtʃʌktʃi siː / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌktʃi siː / (BrE)
a marginal sea of the Arctic Ocean located north of the Bering Strait, between Alaska and Russia
sea in the Arctic Ocean north of the Bering Strait

Examples

Scientists are studying the melting ice in the Chukchi Sea to understand climate change.
The Chukchi Sea is home to a diverse range of Arctic wildlife, including polar bears and walruses.
Shipping routes through the Chukchi Sea are becoming more accessible as the Arctic ice recedes.

Etymology

Named after the Chukchi people, indigenous to the region.
