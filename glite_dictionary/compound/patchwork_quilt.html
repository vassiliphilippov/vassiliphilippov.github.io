patchwork quilt

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætʃ.wɜrk kwɪlt / (AmE), / ˈpætʃ.wɜːk kwɪlt / (BrE)
a quilt made by sewing together pieces of fabric of different colors, patterns, or materials
quilt made from sewn fabric patches

Examples

She lovingly made a patchwork quilt for her granddaughter's bed.
The patchwork quilt displayed so many colors and patterns that it brightened up the entire room.
My grandmother taught me how to sew a patchwork quilt from old fabrics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quilt - a warm bed covering made of padding enclosed between layers of fabric
