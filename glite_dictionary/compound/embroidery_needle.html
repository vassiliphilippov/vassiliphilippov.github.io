embroidery needle

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈbroʊdəri ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri ˈniːdl / (BrE)
a long needle with a large eye, designed for threading heavy darning or embroidery thread
needle with large eye for embroidery

Examples

She carefully threaded the silk through the embroidery needle before starting her project.
An embroidery needle is essential for cross-stitch and other decorative sewing techniques.
I bought a set of embroidery needles with extra-large eyes for my thickest threads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle - a small slender instrument with a sharp point used for sewing
