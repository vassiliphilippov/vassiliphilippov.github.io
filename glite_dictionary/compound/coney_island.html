Coney Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈkoʊni ˌaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊni ˌaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
a section of Brooklyn, New York, situated on the Atlantic coast, famous for its amusement parks, boardwalk, and beach
amusement area in Brooklyn, New York

Examples

Many tourists visit Coney Island in the summer to enjoy rides and the beach.
The famous hot dog eating contest takes place every year at Coney Island.
After moving to Brooklyn, she spent her weekends exploring Coney Island's boardwalk and attractions.
