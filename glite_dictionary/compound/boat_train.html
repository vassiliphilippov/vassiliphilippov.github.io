boat train

1. NOUN

/ boʊt treɪn / (AmE), / bəʊt treɪn / (BrE)
a train provided for passengers traveling to or from a port to catch or disembark from a ship.
train to/from a port for ship passengers

Examples

The boat train left London early to meet the ferry at Dover.
Many travelers preferred the convenience of the boat train when heading to continental Europe.
She missed her boat train and had to wait for the next ferry.
