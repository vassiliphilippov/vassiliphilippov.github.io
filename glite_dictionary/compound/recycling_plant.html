recycling plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌsaɪklɪŋ plænt / (AmE), / ˈriːˌsaɪklɪŋ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a facility where used or discarded materials are reprocessed for reuse
facility for processing recyclable materials

Examples

The city built a new recycling plant to handle its growing waste problem.
Paper, glass, and plastics are sorted and processed at the recycling plant.
After collection, the old electronics were sent to a recycling plant for safe disposal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recycling center - a place where materials are collected and processed for recycling
