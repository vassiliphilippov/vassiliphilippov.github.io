status seeking

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtəs ˌsiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtəs ˌsiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the attempt or drive to gain social standing, approval, or influence; the pursuit of power or prestige
pursuit of social status or power

Examples

Her status seeking led her to join exclusive clubs and attend high-profile events.
The company's culture of status seeking created intense competition among employees.
In many societies, status seeking influences decisions about education and career choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve something, typically requiring determination and hard work
  • Antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest view of one's importance
