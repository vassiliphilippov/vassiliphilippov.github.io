skin irritation

1. NOUN

/ skɪn ˌɪɹɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / skɪn ˌɪɹɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A condition in which the skin becomes inflamed, itchy, or uncomfortable, often as a result of exposure to an irritant or allergen.
inflammation or discomfort of the skin
medical

Examples

If you develop skin irritation after using a new lotion, stop applying it immediately.
Doctors recommend avoiding harsh chemicals that might cause skin irritation.
Long exposure to the sun can sometimes lead to skin irritation.

Synset Examples

She applied a tincture to soothe her skin irritation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rash - an area of irritated or swollen skin
  • Synonym: eczema - a medical condition causing skin inflammation and irritation
