lemon yellow

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a strong, bright shade of yellow resembling the color of a lemon
strong, bright yellow color

Examples

She painted her kitchen a cheerful lemon yellow to brighten the space.
The artist used lemon yellow to highlight the sunflowers in the painting.
I bought a shirt in lemon yellow for the summer festival.
