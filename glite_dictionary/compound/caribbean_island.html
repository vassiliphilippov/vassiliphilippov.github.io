Caribbean island

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪbi.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / kəˈrɪb.i.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
any of the islands located in the Caribbean Sea, especially considered collectively as a region or with respect to culture, geography, or tourism.
island located in the Caribbean Sea

Examples

Jamaica is a popular Caribbean island known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture.
They spent their honeymoon on a small Caribbean island surrounded by turquoise waters.
The hurricane caused severe damage across several Caribbean islands last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caribbean - the region consisting of the Caribbean Sea and its islands
