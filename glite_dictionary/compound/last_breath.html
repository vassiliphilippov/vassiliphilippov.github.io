last breath

1. NOUN

/ læst brɛθ / (AmE), / lɑːst brɛθ / (BrE)
the moment of death; the final breath taken by a person before dying
moment of death; final breath

Examples

He was conscious until his last breath and spoke words of comfort to his family.
The nurse stayed by her side through her last breath.
Poets often write about love lasting until one's last breath.

Synset Examples

She held his hand as he drew his last breath.
He drew his last breath surrounded by loved ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final moments - the last moments before death
  • Synonym: death - the end of life
