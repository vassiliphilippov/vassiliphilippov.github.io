rain forest

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪn ˌfɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌfɒrɪst / (BrE)
a dense forest characterized by high annual rainfall, usually found in tropical regions
dense forest with high rainfall

Examples

The Amazon rain forest is home to thousands of species of plants and animals.
Many scientists are concerned about the rapid destruction of the rain forest.
Tourists visited the lush rain forest to experience its incredible biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jungle - a dense forest, often tropical, with tangled vegetation
