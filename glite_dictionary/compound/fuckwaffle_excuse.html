fuckwaffle excuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkˌwɑːfəl ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈfʌkˌwɒfəl ɪkˈskjuːs / (BrE)
A blatantly nonsensical or meaningless excuse, especially one delivered in a way that is irritating or contemptible.
vulgar term for a ridiculous excuse
vulgar informal

Examples

He offered a fuckwaffle excuse for missing the meeting, and nobody believed him.
After hearing yet another fuckwaffle excuse, I finally called him out on his lies.
Her fuckwaffle excuse about the project deadline was met with eye rolls from the entire team.

Synset Examples

I can't stand his fuckwaffle excuses anymore.
