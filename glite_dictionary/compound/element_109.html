element 109

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləmənt wʌn oʊ ˈnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈelɪmənt wʌn əʊ ˈnaɪn / (BrE)
a radioactive transuranic chemical element with atomic number 109, known as meitnerium
radioactive element, atomic number 109

Examples

Scientists were able to produce element 109 for the first time in 1982.
The decay of element 109 is studied to understand properties of superheavy elements.
Before being given its official name, element 109 was referred to by its atomic number.
