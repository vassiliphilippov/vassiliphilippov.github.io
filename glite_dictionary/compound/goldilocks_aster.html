goldilocks aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑks ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldɪˌlɒks ˈastə / (BrE)
An early-flowering perennial plant native to southern and southeastern Europe, with flower heads that resemble those of goldenrod.
European perennial plant with golden blooms

Examples

The goldilocks aster adds a splash of yellow to gardens in early spring.
She planted several goldilocks asters along the border for their bright, cheerful blooms.
The flowers of the goldilocks aster closely resemble those of the goldenrod.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - Plant with small bright yellow flowers in dense clusters
