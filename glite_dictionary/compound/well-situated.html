well-situated

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl ˈsɪt͡ʃuˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˈsɪtjuːeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having a favorable or comfortable position, especially in terms of financial circumstances; moderately wealthy or advantaged.
having a comfortable or favorable position, often financially

Examples

Her parents are well-situated and can afford to travel frequently.
The scholarship is intended for students who are not well-situated financially.
He grew up in a well-situated family, never lacking for anything.

  • Antonym: impoverished - having little or no money; poor
