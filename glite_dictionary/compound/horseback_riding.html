horseback riding

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌbæk ˌraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsbæk ˌraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding on the back of a horse while controlling its movements
the sport or activity of riding a horse

Examples

She has taken horseback riding lessons since she was a child.
Many people enjoy horseback riding through the countryside during the summer.
The camp offers horseback riding as one of its main outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrianism - the art or practice of riding horses
  • Degree Lower: bareback riding - riding a horse without a saddle
