low-budget

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
made, produced, or suitable for a small or limited amount of money; not expensive to fund or maintain
involving little money; inexpensive to produce

Examples

They created a low-budget film that became a big success at the box office.
The restaurant offers a low-budget menu perfect for students and young professionals.
She planned a low-budget wedding but made it memorable with creative ideas.

Synset Examples

a low-budget movie
a low-budget menu

Related Terms

  • Antonym: big-budget - involving a lot of money; expensive to produce
  • Synonym: low-cost - not costing much; inexpensive
