jaw-jutting confidence

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑː ˈdʒʌtɪŋ ˈkɑːnfɪdəns / (AmE), / dʒɔː ˈdʒʌtɪŋ ˈkɒnfɪdəns / (BrE)
A type of confidence that is especially assertive or bold, often visibly displayed in posture or demeanor.
bold, assertive confidence

Examples

Her jaw-jutting confidence made her the natural leader of the team.
He strode into the meeting with jaw-jutting confidence that silenced his critics.
The candidate’s jaw-jutting confidence was both admired and resented by his opponents.

Synset Examples

He had a jaw-jutting confidence that impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-assuredness - confident and sure behavior
  • Antonym: timidity - lack of self-confidence or boldness
