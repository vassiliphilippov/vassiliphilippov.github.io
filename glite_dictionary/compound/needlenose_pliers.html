needlenose pliers

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdəlˌnoʊz ˈplaɪərz / (AmE), / ˈniːdəlˌnəʊz ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
A small tool with long, thin jaws used for gripping, bending, or manipulating small objects, especially in fine or detailed work.
small pliers with long, thin jaws

Examples

He used his needlenose pliers to bend the tiny wire into place.
Needlenose pliers are essential for many types of electrical work.
She grabbed her needlenose pliers to reach a small screw in a tight space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pliers - A hand tool used for gripping or bending objects
