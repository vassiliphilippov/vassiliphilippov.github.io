hula technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːlə tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈhuːlə tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a method or style used in performing the hula dance
method for performing hula dance

Examples

She spent years perfecting her hula technique before joining the dance troupe.
The instructor corrected my hula technique so my movements would be more precise.
Learning a new hula technique can bring depth and variety to a dancer's performance.

Synset Examples

She demonstrated horizontal hula techniques during class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hula - a traditional Hawaiian dance
