mercy buckets

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜːrsi ˈbʌkɪts / (AmE), / ˈmɜːsi ˈbʌkɪts / (BrE)
a humorous or playful expression of gratitude, imitating the French phrase 'merci beaucoup'
playful phrase for 'thank you'
informal

Examples

After handing her the gift, he said, 'mercy buckets,' making everyone laugh.
Whenever her friends helped her move, she always responded with a cheerful 'mercy buckets!'
At the end of the show, the comedian thanked the crowd with a big 'mercy buckets.'

Synset Examples

They laughed and said 'mercy buckets' at his jokes.
After his performance, the audience shouted 'mercy buckets!'
The chef's dish was so good, they exclaimed 'mercy buckets!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thank you - a way of expressing gratitude
  • Synonym: thanks - an informal expression of gratitude

Etymology

Playful imitation of French 'merci beaucoup' (thank you very much).
