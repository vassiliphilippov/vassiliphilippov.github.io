French Canadian

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ kəˈneɪdiən / (AmE), / frɛntʃ kəˈneɪdiən / (BrE)
a Canadian whose ancestors were French settlers and whose native language is French
a Canadian of French descent

Examples

Many French Canadians celebrate their unique culture and traditions through music and festivals.
She grew up in Montreal as a proud French Canadian.
The history of French Canadians is an important part of Canada's heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Quebecois - a native or inhabitant of Quebec, especially one who speaks French
