peace offering

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈɑːfərɪŋ / (AmE), / piːs ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
a gift or gesture given to someone, especially an opponent or adversary, in an attempt to restore friendly relations or end a conflict
gesture or gift to restore peace

Examples

She brought him a peace offering in the form of homemade cookies after their argument.
He sent flowers as a peace offering to apologize for his harsh words.
They considered the generous donation a peace offering from the company after the dispute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive branch - a symbol or gesture of peace or reconciliation
  • Antonym: provocation - an action or speech that elicits anger or conflict; something that provokes

2. NOUN

/ pis ˈɑfərɪŋ / (AmE), / piːs ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
an item, gesture, or gift given to restore friendly relations or end a disagreement
item given to mend a relationship

Examples

The supervisor offered an extra day off as a peace offering to the team.
