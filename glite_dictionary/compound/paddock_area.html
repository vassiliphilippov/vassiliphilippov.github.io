paddock area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpædək ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpædək ˌeəriə / (BrE)
An enclosed area at a race event, especially for housing horses before a race.
enclosure for horses at races

Examples

Spectators gathered near the paddock area to see the horses before the big race.
Access to the paddock area is restricted to trainers and officials during the event.
After touring the stables, we visited the paddock area to watch the horses being prepared.

Synset Examples

We watched the race from the paddock area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paddock - an enclosed field or pasture for horses or other animals
