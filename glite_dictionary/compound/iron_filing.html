iron filing

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.ɚn ˈfaɪ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ən ˈfaɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a small piece or particle of iron that has been removed by filing, often used in scientific demonstrations
tiny particle of iron from filing

Examples

The students sprinkled iron filing around the magnet to trace its magnetic field.
A single iron filing may appear insignificant, but together they reveal magnetic force lines clearly.
He accidentally dropped an iron filing into the test tube during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filing - a small particle removed by filing a metal or other material
