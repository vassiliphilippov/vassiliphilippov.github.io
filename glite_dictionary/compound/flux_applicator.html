flux applicator

1. NOUN

/ flʌks ˌæplɪˈkeɪtɚ / (AmE), / flʌks ˌæplɪˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
a tool used to apply flux during soldering, brazing, or similar processes
tool for applying flux in soldering

Examples

Always use a flux applicator for even distribution when soldering electronic components.
The technician cleaned the tip of the flux applicator before beginning the repair.
You can buy a precision flux applicator for detailed circuit board work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applicator - a device used to apply a substance
