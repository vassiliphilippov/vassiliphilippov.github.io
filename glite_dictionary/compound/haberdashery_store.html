haberdashery store

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæbərˌdæʃəri stɔr / (AmE), / ˌhæbəˈdæʃəri stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells men's clothing and accessories, such as hats, shirts, and ties.
shop selling men's clothing and accessories

Examples

He bought a new suit at the downtown haberdashery store before his job interview.
Many traditional haberdashery stores also offer tailoring services.
The city center still has a classic haberdashery store run by the same family for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing store - a shop selling clothes or apparel

Etymology

From 'haberdashery' (store for small wares or men's clothing), itself from Anglo-Norman 'haberdas' (seller of small wares).
