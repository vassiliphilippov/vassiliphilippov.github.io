car insurance

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ɪnˈʃʊrəns / (AmE), / kɑː ɪnˈʃɔːrəns / (BrE)
insurance that covers vehicles and their owners against losses from theft, accidents, or damage involving cars
insurance for cars against theft or accidents

Examples

You are legally required to have car insurance if you want to drive on public roads.
After the accident, his car insurance covered the cost of repairs.
She compared several car insurance policies before choosing the one that best suited her needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motor insurance - insurance for vehicles against loss or damage
  • Antonym: home insurance - insurance covering loss or damage to a house
  • Antonym: life insurance - insurance paying money on the death of the insured person
