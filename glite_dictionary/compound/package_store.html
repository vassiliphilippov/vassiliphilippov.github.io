package store

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪdʒ stɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒ stɔː / (BrE)
a shop selling alcoholic beverages in sealed containers for consumption off the premises
shop selling packaged alcohol for off-site use
american

Examples

After work, John stopped by the package store to buy a bottle of wine for dinner.
State laws require customers to show ID when purchasing alcohol at the package store.
During the holidays, the local package store always has special offers on whiskey and gin.
