corn on the cob

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ɑn ðə kɑb / (AmE), / kɔːn ɒn ðə kɒb / (BrE)
corn cooked and served on the whole ear, typically eaten by holding the ends and biting off the kernels
whole ear of cooked corn

Examples

We had grilled corn on the cob at the summer picnic.
She brushed butter on the hot corn on the cob before serving it.
Children love to eat corn on the cob at fairs and festivals.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed fresh corn on the cob at the barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corn - a cereal plant that yields large grains, or kernels, set in rows on a cob
