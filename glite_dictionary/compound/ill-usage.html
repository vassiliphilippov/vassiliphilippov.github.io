ill-usage

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪlˈjuːsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɪlˈjuːsɪdʒ / (BrE)
Cruel or inhumane treatment; harsh or abusive conduct towards someone.
cruel or inhumane treatment

Examples

The animal was rescued after enduring years of ill-usage at the hands of its owner.
She never forgot the ill-usage she suffered as a child in the orphanage.
Reports of ill-usage at the factory prompted an investigation by the authorities.

Synset Examples

the child showed signs of physical abuse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abuse - cruel or violent treatment of someone
  • Synonym: mistreatment - the action of treating someone badly or unfairly

Etymology

From 'ill' (bad) + 'usage' (treatment or use), dating from the 17th century.
