scrub pine

1. NOUN

/ skrʌb paɪn / (AmE), / skrʌb paɪn / (BrE)
a small, shrubby pine tree of the eastern United States, with twisted branches and short needles in pairs
a small shrubby pine tree of eastern U.S.

Examples

The rocky hillside was dotted with scrub pine and low-lying bushes.
Many birds nest in the branches of the scrub pine each spring.
After the fire, only a few scrub pines remained standing in the barren field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine - a type of evergreen tree with needle-shaped leaves
