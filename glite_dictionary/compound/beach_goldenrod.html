beach goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ biːtʃ ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a vigorous, showy species of goldenrod found along the eastern and Gulf coasts of North America
a coastal North American goldenrod plant

Examples

The beach goldenrod adds a splash of color to the sandy dunes every summer.
Botanists studied the adaptations of beach goldenrod to coastal environments.
You can often spot beach goldenrod growing by the shorelines during late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago, known for its yellow flowers
