soft-shoe dancing

1. NOUN

/ sɑft ʃoʊ ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / sɒft ʃəʊ ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of tap dancing performed in shoes with soft soles, characterized by smooth and graceful movements.
tap dancing with soft-soled shoes

Examples

He performed soft-shoe dancing with effortless grace at the school talent show.
Many vaudeville performers were known for their soft-shoe dancing routines.
The old movie featured a charming scene of soft-shoe dancing on a wooden stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap dancing - A form of dance characterized by tapping sounds made with the feet
