Baic language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪk ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪɪk ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Tibeto-Burman language spoken in the Dali region of Yunnan province in China
language of the Dali region in Yunnan, China

Examples

The Baic language is primarily spoken in the mountainous regions near Dali.
Linguists have studied the Baic language to uncover its unique grammatical structures.
Efforts are underway to preserve the Baic language for future generations.
