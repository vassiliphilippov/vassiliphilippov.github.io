safe delivery

1. NOUN

/ seɪf dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / seɪf dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
the act or process of giving birth to a baby without medical complications or danger to mother or child
childbirth without complications

Examples

The hospital's excellent staff ensured a safe delivery for both mother and child.
Prenatal care is crucial to increase the chances of a safe delivery.
She felt relieved after experiencing a safe delivery with her second child.

Synset Examples

A skilled midwife is essential for a safe delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the process of giving birth to a baby
