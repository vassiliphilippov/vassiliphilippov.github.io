self-propelled

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or machine) able to move or drive itself by means of its own power, rather than being pushed or pulled.
able to move by its own power

Examples

The army deployed several self-propelled artillery units to the front lines.
Many modern lawn mowers are self-propelled, making them easier to use on large lawns.
The museum features a collection of early self-propelled vehicles and locomotives.

Synset Examples

a self-propelled vehicle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propelled - driven or moved forward by a specific force

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'propelled', meaning propelled by its own power.
