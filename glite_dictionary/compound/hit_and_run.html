hit and run

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪt ən ˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˌhɪt ən ˈrʌn / (BrE)
a road accident in which a driver involved leaves the scene without stopping to provide information or help
traffic accident where driver flees scene

Examples

The police are searching for the suspect involved in the hit and run last night.
After witnessing a hit and run, she immediately called emergency services.
The victim suffered minor injuries in the hit and run outside the supermarket.

Synset Examples

Witnesses reported seeing a hit and run at the intersection.
She was devastated after the hit and run left her car damaged.
The police are investigating a hit and run incident near the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit-and-run driver - a driver who leaves the scene of an accident without stopping
  • Synonym: traffic accident - an incident in which vehicles collide or cause injury/damage on the road

Etymology

From the actions described: 'hit' (to strike) and 'run' (to flee the scene).
