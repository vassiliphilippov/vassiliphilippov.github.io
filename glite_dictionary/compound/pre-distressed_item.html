pre-distressed item

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriː dɪˈstrest ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ˌpriː dɪˈstrest ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an item, such as furniture or clothing, that has been deliberately manufactured or altered to look aged, worn, or used
item made to look worn or aged

Examples

The shop window displayed a number of pre-distressed items, including chairs and tables with chipped paint.
Many fashion brands offer pre-distressed items to appeal to customers who like a vintage look.
He preferred buying pre-distressed items because they fit perfectly with his industrial-themed apartment.

Synset Examples

The store specializes in pre-distressed furniture for a rustic charm.
She bought a pre-distressed jacket that looked vintage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distressed - made to appear aged or worn
