art critic

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who evaluates and writes about works of art, especially paintings or sculpture
person who evaluates works of art

Examples

The art critic wrote a glowing review of the new exhibition.
Many artists value the perspective of an experienced art critic.
She became an influential art critic in the 20th century art world.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: critic - a person who judges the merits of literary or artistic works
