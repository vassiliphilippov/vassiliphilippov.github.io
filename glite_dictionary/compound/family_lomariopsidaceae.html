family Lomariopsidaceae

1. NOUN

/ fəˈmɪli loʊˌmɛəriˌɑːpsɪˈdeɪsiː / (AmE), / fəˈmɪli ləʊˌmarɪˌɒpsɪˈdiːsiː / (BrE)
A small family of usually climbing (scandent) ferns, found mainly in tropical regions.
family of mostly climbing ferns

Examples

Many tropical forests are home to ferns in the family Lomariopsidaceae.
The family Lomariopsidaceae comprises several genera of mostly climbing ferns.
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to the family Lomariopsidaceae in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From New Latin Lomariopsidaceae, based on the type genus Lomariopsis + family suffix -aceae.
