snuggle bunny

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌɡəl ˌbʌni / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡl ˌbʌni / (BrE)
An affectionate term for a person or pet who enjoys cuddling or being cuddled.
affectionate term for a cuddly person or pet
informal

Examples

On cold evenings, my cat becomes a real snuggle bunny and curls up next to me.
She called her boyfriend her snuggle bunny after he brought her breakfast in bed.
Their dog is the biggest snuggle bunny and loves to nap on laps.

Synset Examples

My boyfriend is such a snuggle bunny on chilly nights.
He loves being my snuggle bunny during movie nights.
She called her dog her little snuggle bunny.
