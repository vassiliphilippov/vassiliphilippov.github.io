the Old One

1. NOUN

/ ði oʊld wʌn / (AmE), / ði əʊld wʌn / (BrE)
a revered ancestral figure or deity, often regarded as a protector or source of wisdom in myth or legend
revered ancestral deity or figure

Examples

Many ancient tribes honored the Old One with elaborate ceremonies.
In the folklore of the region, the Old One is said to watch over the valley.
She believed that the Old One would answer their prayers during hard times.

Synset Examples

The villagers spoke of the Old One with reverence.
