miniature fan palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪniətʃɚ fæn pɑːm / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪətjʊə fæn pɑːm / (BrE)
A small, graceful palm species with slender, reedlike stems and leaf bases covered in loose, coarse fibers.
small palm with reedlike stems

Examples

The miniature fan palm is a popular choice for small tropical gardens.
I admired the delicate fibers along the stems of the miniature fan palm in the greenhouse.
Despite its small size, the miniature fan palm adds elegance to indoor spaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palm - a tree of the family Arecaceae, typically with an unbranched trunk and large leaves
