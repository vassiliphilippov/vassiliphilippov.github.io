gambling system

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæmblɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈɡamblɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of rules or a method designed to increase chances of winning when placing bets in gambling games
method for betting to win in gambling

Examples

After studying for months, he claimed to have invented a foolproof gambling system for blackjack.
Many people lose money believing in a gambling system that guarantees success at the casino.
She followed a popular gambling system but still left the roulette table empty-handed.

Synset Examples

he has a perfect gambling system at roulette

Related Terms

  • Synonym: system - a set of principles or procedures for achieving something
