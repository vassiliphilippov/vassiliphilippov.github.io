lady fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdi fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern (Athyrium filix-femina) widely cultivated for its delicate, feathery foliage
a widely grown delicate fern

Examples

The lady fern is popular in shaded garden borders due to its graceful leaves.
Many gardeners plant lady ferns beneath trees to add lush greenery.
I identified the lady fern by its finely divided fronds.
