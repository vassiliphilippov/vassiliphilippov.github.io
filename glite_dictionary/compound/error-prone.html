error-prone

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛrər ˌproʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛrə ˌprəʊn / (BrE)
likely to make mistakes or be affected by errors.
likely to make mistakes

Examples

The system was replaced because it was too error-prone to be reliable in critical situations.
Manual data entry is often error-prone, leading to costly mistakes.
As a beginner, her code was somewhat error-prone, but she improved with practice.
