Jersey girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜrzi ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːzi ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or woman from New Jersey, often associated with certain regional stereotypes such as being bold, fashionable, and outspoken
girl or woman from New Jersey
american informal

Examples

Everyone could tell she was a true Jersey girl by her big hair and confidence.
As a Jersey girl, she never missed a summer at the shore.
The movie features a tough, witty Jersey girl who stands up for her friends.

Synset Examples

She’s a classic Jersey girl, loud and proud.
Being a Jersey girl means loving the beach.

Etymology

From 'Jersey', referring to the U.S. state of New Jersey, and 'girl'.
