blue pea

1. NOUN

/ bluː piː / (AmE), / bluː piː / (BrE)
A tropical Asian vine (Clitoria ternatea) with pinnate leaves and bright blue flowers with yellow centers, commonly cultivated for ornamental or culinary use.
tropical vine with blue flowers

Examples

The blue pea is often used to make herbal tea with a striking blue color.
Gardeners admire the bright blossoms of the blue pea in tropical landscapes.
The chef decorated the dessert with petals from the blue pea flower for added color.
