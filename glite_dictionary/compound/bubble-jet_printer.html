bubble-jet printer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl ˌdʒɛt ˈprɪn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ˌdʒɛt ˈprɪn.tə / (BrE)
A type of inkjet printer in which tiny bubbles of ink are ejected onto paper to form images or text.
type of inkjet printer

Examples

Our office replaced its old laser printer with a new bubble-jet printer for higher-quality color prints.
The technician explained how the bubble-jet printer produces sharp images using tiny drops of ink.
Many home users prefer a bubble-jet printer for its affordability and quiet operation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inkjet printer - a printer that forms images by spraying tiny droplets of ink onto paper
