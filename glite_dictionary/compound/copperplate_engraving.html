copperplate engraving

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑpərˌpleɪt ɛnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəˌpleɪt ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An engraving made by etching or incising a design onto a smooth plate of copper, used in printmaking or illustration.
engraving made on copper plate

Examples

The museum displays a rare copperplate engraving from the eighteenth century.
She studied the art of copperplate engraving during her time at the academy.
Many old maps were produced using copperplate engraving techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etching - printmaking process and result using acid on metal plates
