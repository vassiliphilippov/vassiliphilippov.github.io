genus Lobotes

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs loʊˈboʊ.tiz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ləʊˈbəʊ.tiːz / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Lobotidae, comprising marine fish known as tripletails.
genus of marine fish (Lobotidae)
medical

Examples

The genus Lobotes includes species commonly known as tripletails.
Marine biologists study the genus Lobotes to understand their habitat and behavior.
Fishes of the genus Lobotes are recognized by their distinctive rounded fins.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, race) and Neo-Latin 'Lobotes' (name of the genus).
