indefinite quantity

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɛfɪnət ˈkwɑntəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛfɪnət ˈkwɒntɪti / (BrE)
an amount that is not precisely determined or specified
an unspecified or unknown amount

Examples

There was an indefinite quantity of flour left in the jar, so I had to measure it carefully.
The agreement allowed for the supply of an indefinite quantity of goods each year.
Scientists discovered an indefinite quantity of microbes in the new ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: certain quantity - a precisely determined amount
  • Synonym: approximate quantity - an amount that is estimated but not exact
