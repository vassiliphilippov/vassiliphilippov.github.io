touch of elegance

1. NOUN

/ tʌtʃ əv ˈɛlɪɡəns / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ɒv ˈɛlɪɡəns / (BrE)
a small detail or addition that enhances the beauty or refinement of something
small addition that enhances beauty

Examples

Fresh flowers always add a touch of elegance to a dining table.
The gold trim on the invitations gave them a touch of elegance.
Her simple, classic jewelry provided just the right touch of elegance to her look.

Synset Examples

The weskit added a touch of elegance to his outfit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish
  • Synonym: refinement - cultivated taste or elegance
