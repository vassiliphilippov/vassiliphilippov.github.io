round kumquat

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd ˈkʌmˌkwɑt / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈkʌmˌkwɒt / (BrE)
a shrub native to East Asia bearing small, round, orange citrus fruits, also known as kumquats
shrubs with round kumquat fruits

Examples

The round kumquat is prized for its sweet, edible peel and tart flesh.
Gardeners enjoy growing round kumquat trees for both their decorative and fruit-bearing qualities.
She made a delicious marmalade using freshly picked round kumquat fruits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kumquat - small, edible citrus fruit with sweet rind
