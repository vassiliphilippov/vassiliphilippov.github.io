red ginger

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈdʒɪndʒər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
an ornamental plant of the ginger family, native to Pacific islands, known for its bright red flower spikes
ornamental ginger plant with red flowers

Examples

The vibrant blooms of red ginger brighten many tropical gardens.
She arranged a bouquet that included freshly cut red ginger for the centerpiece.
Tourists admired the tall stalks of red ginger growing along the island pathways.
