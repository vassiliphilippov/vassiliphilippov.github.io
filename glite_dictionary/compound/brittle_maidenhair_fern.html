brittle maidenhair fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪt̬əl ˈmeɪdənˌhɛr fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.əl ˈmeɪdnˌheə fɜːn / (BrE)
A tropical American fern (Adiantum tenerum) with broad leaflets, widely cultivated as an ornamental plant.
a species of tropical maidenhair fern

Examples

The brittle maidenhair fern is often grown indoors for its attractive foliage.
Botanists discovered a wild population of brittle maidenhair fern in the rainforest.
He watered his brittle maidenhair fern regularly to keep it healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maidenhair fern - a common name for the genus Adiantum, known for its delicate fronds
