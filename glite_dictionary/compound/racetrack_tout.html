racetrack tout

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsˌtræk taʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪstræk taʊt / (BrE)
a person who offers tips or advice about betting on horse races, often for personal gain or to influence betting outcomes
someone who gives betting tips at horse races

Examples

The racetrack tout promised inside information on the next big winner for a small fee.
Every Saturday, the racetrack tout stood near the betting window, eagerly sharing tips with newcomers.
He realized too late that the racetrack tout had exaggerated his knowledge just to make money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tipster - a person who gives tips, especially about betting
  • Synonym: bookie - someone who takes bets, especially on horse racing
