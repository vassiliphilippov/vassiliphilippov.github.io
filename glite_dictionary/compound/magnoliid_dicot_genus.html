magnoliid dicot genus

1. NOUN

/ mægˈnoʊ.li.ɪd ˈdaɪ.kɑt ˈdʒi.nəs / (AmE), / mæɡˈnəʊ.li.ɪd ˈdaɪ.kɒt ˈdʒiː.nəs / (BrE)
A genus of dicotyledonous flowering plants considered among the most primitive living angiosperms.
primitive dicotyledonous plant genus

Examples

Researchers believe that the magnoliid dicot genus represents an early evolutionary branch of flowering plants.
The magnoliid dicot genus includes several species important in botany for understanding plant evolution.
Many ancient forests contained trees belonging to a magnoliid dicot genus.

Etymology

From 'Magnoliid', named after the magnolia, + 'dicot', short for dicotyledon, and Latin 'genus' (kind, type).
