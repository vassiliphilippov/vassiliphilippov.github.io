professional tennis player

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈtɛnɪs ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈtɛnɪs ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays tennis as a paid career, either in competitions or as a coach
person paid to play or teach tennis

Examples

After years of rigorous training, she finally became a professional tennis player and joined the international circuit.
He dreamed of making a living as a professional tennis player since he was a child.
Many professional tennis players travel the world to compete in major tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: tennis player - someone who plays tennis, amateur or professional
