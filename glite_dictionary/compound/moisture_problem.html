moisture problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔɪstʃɚ ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / ˈmɔɪstʃə ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
a situation in which excess water or dampness causes issues, often relating to buildings, materials, or other environments
issue caused by excess water or dampness

Examples

If left untreated, a moisture problem can lead to mold growth inside your home.
They discovered a serious moisture problem in the basement after the heavy rains.
Proper ventilation can help prevent a moisture problem from developing in the bathroom.

Synset Examples

The efflorescence on the wall indicated moisture problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dampness - slight wetness that causes discomfort or difficulty
  • Antonym: dryness - the state of being dry, free from moisture
