critical thinking

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪdɪkəl ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the objective analysis and evaluation of an issue or information in order to form a judgment
objective analysis and evaluation to form a judgment

Examples

The course curriculum emphasizes critical thinking and independent analysis of problems.
Employers value applicants who have demonstrated strong critical thinking skills.
She used critical thinking to evaluate all sides of the argument before responding.

Synset Examples

My professor always encourages critical thinking in discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analytical thinking - the ability to systematically analyze information
