blood lily

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / blʌd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Haemanthus, native to South Africa and Namibia, known for its bright red, spherical flowers.
plant with bright red flowers (Haemanthus genus)

Examples

The blood lily blooms in late spring, adding a splash of red to the garden.
Gardeners prize the blood lily for its striking, globe-shaped flowers.
After months of dormancy, the blood lily returns with vibrant color.
