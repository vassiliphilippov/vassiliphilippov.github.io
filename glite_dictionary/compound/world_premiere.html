world premiere

1. NOUN

/ wɝːld prəˈmɪr / (AmE), / wɜːld prəˈmɪə / (BrE)
the first public performance or showing of a play, film, piece of music, or other creative work anywhere in the world
first-ever public performance of a work

Examples

The symphony had its world premiere at Carnegie Hall last night.
Critics eagerly anticipated the world premiere of the new film at the festival.
The actor attended the world premiere of the stage production in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premiere - the first public performance of a play, film, or piece of music
