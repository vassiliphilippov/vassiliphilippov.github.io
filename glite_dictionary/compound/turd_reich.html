Turd Reich

1. NOUN

/ tɝd ɹaɪk / (AmE), / tɜːd ɹaɪk / (BrE)
A derogatory and vulgar term for a regime, group, or system perceived as corrupt, oppressive, or totalitarian, usually used in satire or mockery.
offensive satirical term for totalitarian regime
vulgar offensive informal

Examples

The comedian's routine criticized the political party by referring to them as the Turd Reich.
Online forums exploded in laughter when someone compared the new policy to the Turd Reich.
Some activists use the term Turd Reich to condemn groups they see as dangerously authoritarian.

Synset Examples

The group mocked him, calling his ideas the Turd Reich.
They compared his behavior to the Turd Reich's absurdity.
His outrageous claims made him sound like part of the Turd Reich.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Third Reich - Nazi Germany; the regime under Adolf Hitler from 1933 to 1945

Etymology

Formed satirically after "Third Reich", with "turd" (vulgar slang for feces) replacing "third" to insult or ridicule.
