posy sniffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊzi ˌsnɪfər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊzi ˌsnɪfə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys, identifies, or is particularly fond of flowers
person fond of or identifying flowers

Examples

The posy sniffer in our group can identify any flower.
Sarah proudly calls herself a posy sniffer and is always surrounded by bouquets.
Because he was a devoted posy sniffer, his house overflowed with fragrant blossoms.

Synset Examples

The posy sniffer in our group can identify any flower.
Being a posy sniffer, she always carries a bouquet.
He was called a posy sniffer for his flower obsession.

Etymology

From 'posy' (a small bouquet or flower) and 'sniffer' (one who smells or sniffs).
