long time

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ taɪm / (AmE), / lɒŋ taɪm / (BrE)
a period lasting a significant amount of time; a prolonged duration.
a lengthy period of time

Examples

We waited for a long time before the train finally arrived.
It's been a long time since we last saw each other.
He lived in that city for a long time before moving abroad.

Synset Examples

we've known each other for ages
I haven't been there for years and years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ages - a very long time
  • Antonym: short time - a brief period of time
