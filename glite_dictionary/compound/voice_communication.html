voice communication

1. NOUN

/ vɔɪs kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vɔɪs kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the exchange of information through spoken words; communication by talking rather than by writing or other means
spoken communication; verbal communication

Examples

Modern smartphones enable voice communication over long distances with minimal delay.
Pilots rely on radio for voice communication with air traffic control.
Effective voice communication is essential in emergency situations to coordinate response teams.

Synset Examples

his speech was garbled
he uttered harsh language
he recorded the spoken language of the streets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verbal communication - communication involving words, especially spoken rather than written
