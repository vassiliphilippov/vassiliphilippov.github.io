evening shift

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ ʃɪft / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ ʃɪft / (BrE)
A period of work that typically takes place during the evening, usually between 4 p.m. and midnight.
work period from late afternoon to midnight

Examples

She started working the evening shift after her classes ended each day.
The hospital relies on a dedicated evening shift to cover patient care.
Many restaurants need extra staff during the evening shift to handle dinner crowds.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: night shift - a period of work taking place during the night hours
  • Degree Lower: day shift - work period taking place during the daytime
