pleasure ground

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɛʒɚ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a park or area equipped with amusements and facilities for public entertainment; an amusement park
amusement park or recreational area

Examples

The children begged their parents to take them to the pleasure ground for the annual fair.
In Victorian England, a pleasure ground often featured music, games, and food stalls.
They spent the afternoon exploring the attractions at the city's oldest pleasure ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusement park - a park with rides and other entertainments
