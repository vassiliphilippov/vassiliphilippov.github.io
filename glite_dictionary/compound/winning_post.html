winning post

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪnɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
the post marking the end point at a racecourse, indicating the finish line.
post marking the finish line of a racecourse

Examples

The horse surged ahead in the final stretch to cross the winning post first.
Crowds cheered as the leading runner approached the winning post.
Jockeys urge their mounts towards the winning post with all their might.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish line - a line marking the end of a race
