international logistic support

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ləˈdʒɪstɪk səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃnəl ləˈdʒɪstɪk səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
arrangements made between countries to provide mutual assistance with transportation, supply, and resources, usually in a military or humanitarian context.
mutual assistance between countries with logistics

Examples

The mission depended heavily on international logistic support to deliver supplies and equipment across borders.
Effective international logistic support was crucial in responding quickly to the humanitarian crisis.
Many alliances are built on agreements concerning international logistic support during military operations.
