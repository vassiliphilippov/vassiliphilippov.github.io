swimming event

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ ɪˌvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ ɪˌvɛnt / (BrE)
a competition in which people or teams race or perform in swimming
athletic competition in swimming

Examples

The 100-meter freestyle is a popular swimming event at the Olympics.
She trained for months to compete in her first swimming event.
Each swimming event is carefully timed to determine the winner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition of speed, often track or swimming
  • Antonym: track event - an athletic event involving running on a track
