shopping list

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of items to be purchased, typically made before going shopping
list of items to buy

Examples

Before going to the supermarket, I made a shopping list so I wouldn't forget anything.
He found his mother's shopping list on the kitchen counter, filled with fruits and vegetables.
Writing a shopping list helps me stay organized and avoid unnecessary purchases.

Synset Examples

the union came to the table with a whole grocery list of demands

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery list - a list of items to buy, especially groceries

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a written or mental list of items to be bought, especially when shopping for groceries or other necessities
list of things to buy

Examples

Before going to the supermarket, I made a shopping list so I wouldn't forget anything.
Her shopping list included bread, milk, and eggs.
He checked each item off his shopping list as he walked through the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery list - a list of food items to buy; a specific type of shopping list

3. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a written or mental list of items that someone intends to buy, especially from a shop or supermarket
list of items to buy

Examples

She scribbled eggs and milk on her shopping list before heading to the store.
I always forget something if I go without a shopping list.
He checked his shopping list to make sure he hadn’t missed any ingredients for dinner.

Synset Examples

Don't forget your shopping list before you leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery list - a list of food and household items to buy at a grocery store
