polo shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊloʊ ʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A casual short-sleeved shirt with a collar, a placket neckline, and usually two or three buttons, typically made of cotton or a cotton blend.
casual collared short-sleeved shirt

Examples

He wore a clean polo shirt and jeans to the interview.
You can dress up a polo shirt with a blazer for a smart-casual look.
The school's uniform includes a navy blue polo shirt with the school logo.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: T-shirt - short-sleeved collarless casual shirt

Etymology

Named after the sport of polo, where this style of shirt was first popularized.
