to snatch the opportunity

1. VERB

/ snætʃ ði ɑːpərˈtuːnəti / (AmE), / snætʃ ði ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
to seize an opportunity quickly and eagerly
seize an opportunity quickly

Examples

When the job came up unexpectedly, she didn’t hesitate to snatch the opportunity.
They were able to snatch the opportunity and expand their business overseas.
He tried to snatch the opportunity before anyone else noticed it.

Synset Examples

He managed to snatch the opportunity for a promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to seize the opportunity - to take advantage of an opportunity
