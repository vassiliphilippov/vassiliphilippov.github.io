small white aster

1. NOUN

/ smɔl waɪt ˈæstər / (AmE), / smɔːl waɪt ˈæstə / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant of the aster family, Symphyotrichum racemosum, with small, white, daisy-like flowers.
a plant with small white aster flowers

Examples

The small white aster blooms in late summer, brightening gardens with its delicate flowers.
Botanists often recommend planting small white asters to attract bees and butterflies.
We spotted a small white aster along the hiking trail, nestled among the wild grasses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a genus of flowering plants with star-shaped flower heads
