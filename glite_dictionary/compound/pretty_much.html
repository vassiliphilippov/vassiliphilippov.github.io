pretty much

1. ADV

/ ˈprɪti mʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈprɪti mʌtʃ / (BrE)
almost; very nearly; to a large extent or degree.
almost; nearly
informal

Examples

I'm pretty much finished with the report, just a few more edits to go.
It was pretty much what we expected, so there were no surprises.
They've pretty much decided on which house to buy, but nothing is final yet.

Synset Examples

we were pretty much lost when we met the forest ranger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almost - very nearly but not exactly or entirely
  • Antonym: entirely - completely; wholly

2. ADV

/ ˈprɪti mʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈprɪti mʌtʃ / (BrE)
almost entirely; nearly; to a large extent, though not completely.
almost entirely; nearly
informal

Examples

I've pretty much finished the report, just a few edits left.
She was pretty much the only one who agreed with the plan.
It rained pretty much all day yesterday.

Synset Examples

This old phone is pretty much useless now.

Related Terms
