heather bell

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛðər ˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈhɛðə ˌbɛl / (BrE)
a common low European shrub with purple-red bell-shaped flowers, often found in heathland and moorland areas
European shrub with purple-red flowers

Examples

The hills were covered with blooming heather bell in late summer.
A sprig of heather bell was tucked into her lapel for good luck.
The heather bell attracts many bees with its vibrant flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heather - a low, spreading shrub with small, purple or pink flowers, common in Europe
