wild ass

1. NOUN

/ waɪld æs / (AmE), / waɪld ɑːs / (BrE)
a large, hoofed mammal of the horse family, found in Asia and northeast Africa, known for its untamed nature
a wild equine mammal of Asia and northeast Africa

Examples

The wild ass roams freely across the arid plains of Central Asia.
Conservation efforts aim to protect the endangered wild ass from poaching and habitat loss.
Unlike its domesticated relatives, the wild ass is rarely tamed and lives in herds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onager - a wild Asiatic ass, especially Equus hemionus
  • Antonym: domestic donkey - domesticated member of the horse family, Equus asinus
