spider orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdɚ ˌɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ˌɔːkɪd / (BrE)
any of several European orchids of the genus Ophrys, known for their flowers that resemble spiders.
European orchid with spider-like flowers

Examples

The spider orchid is prized by gardeners for its unique and delicate appearance.
We found a rare spider orchid while hiking in southern Europe.
Many spider orchids mimic the look of insects to attract pollinators.

3. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdər ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A Central American orchid with spiderlike flowers, often featuring prominent green warts.
Central American orchid with spiderlike flowers

Examples

The spider orchid is prized for its unusual, long-petaled flowers that resemble a spider.
Many collectors seek out rare species of spider orchid for their green, warty blooms.
In the rainforest, the spider orchid stands out due to its striking and unique flower shape.
