not acceptable

1. ADJ

/ nɑt əkˈsɛptəbəl / (AmE), / nɒt əkˈsɛptəbl / (BrE)
not allowed, permitted, or suitable; not meeting required standards or expectations
not allowed or suitable

Examples

Such behavior is not acceptable in our classroom.
The quality of this product is not acceptable to our standards.
Arriving late without any explanation is not acceptable at this company.

Synset Examples

Using that term is not acceptable anywhere.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acceptable - permitted or suitable
