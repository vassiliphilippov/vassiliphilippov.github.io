sinus headache

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪnəs ˌhɛdˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnəs ˌhɛd.eɪk / (BrE)
a headache caused by inflammation, congestion, or infection of the sinuses
headache from sinus congestion or infection
medical

Examples

My sinus headache gets worse whenever I have a cold or allergies.
Over-the-counter medication can help relieve a sinus headache.
He missed work because his sinus headache made it hard to concentrate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: migraine - severe headache usually accompanied by nausea or visual disturbance
  • Synonym: tension headache - headache caused by muscle tension in the head or neck
