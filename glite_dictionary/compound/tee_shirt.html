tee shirt

1. NOUN

/ ti ˌʃɝːt / (AmE), / ti ˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A casual, close-fitting pullover shirt with short sleeves, usually made of cotton.
casual short-sleeved pullover shirt

Examples

He wore a plain white tee shirt and jeans to the party.
The company printed its logo on hundreds of tee shirts for the event.
She bought a new tee shirt with a colorful design at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: T-shirt - alternate spelling of 'tee shirt', the same type of shirt

Etymology

Named for the T-shape of the body and sleeves.
