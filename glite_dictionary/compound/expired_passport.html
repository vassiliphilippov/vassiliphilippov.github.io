expired passport

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈspaɪɚd ˈpæspɔrt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspaɪəd ˈpɑːspɔːt / (BrE)
a passport that is no longer valid for travel or legal identification, usually because its period of validity has ended
passport that is no longer valid

Examples

She discovered that her expired passport would prevent her from traveling abroad until she renewed it.
The embassy requires you to turn in your expired passport when applying for a new one.
Travelers are advised to check their documents to avoid issues caused by an expired passport.

Synset Examples

His passport expired, so he couldn't board the flight.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: valid passport - a passport that is currently valid and can be used for travel
