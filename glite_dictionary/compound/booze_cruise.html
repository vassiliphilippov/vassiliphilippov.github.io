booze cruise

1. NOUN

/ buːz kruːz / (AmE), / buːz kruːz / (BrE)
an informal party or excursion on a boat, during which alcohol is served and consumed
party boat trip with alcohol
informal

Examples

We all met at the pier for the booze cruise before sunset.
After a long week at work, a booze cruise was just what they needed.
The company organized a booze cruise as part of their annual retreat.

Synset Examples

We're going on a booze cruise this weekend!
They enjoyed the sunset on their booze cruise.
The booze cruise promised endless drinks and fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party boat - a boat used for social gatherings and parties
  • Synonym: booze - alcoholic drink, especially in informal usage
  • Synonym: cruise - a journey on a ship or boat for pleasure

Etymology

From 'booze' (slang for alcoholic drink) + 'cruise' (a journey by boat).
