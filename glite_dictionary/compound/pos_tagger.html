POS tagger

1. NOUN

/ pi oʊ ɛs ˈtæɡɚ / (AmE), / piː əʊ ɛs ˈtæɡə / (BrE)
a computer program or tool that labels words in text with their corresponding parts of speech, such as noun, verb, or adjective
program labeling parts of speech in text

Examples

The researcher used a POS tagger to analyze the grammatical structure of sentences.
Many natural language processing tools include a built-in POS tagger for word classification.
To improve accuracy, they trained their own custom POS tagger on medical texts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: part of speech - a category to which a word is assigned in accordance with its syntactic functions

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'part of speech' (POS) + 'tagger' (one who tags).
