devil's fig

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz fɪɡ / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Argemone with large white or yellow flowers and prickly leaves, stems, and pods, found chiefly in tropical America.
a prickly plant of the genus Argemone

Examples

The field was overgrown with devil's fig, making it difficult to walk through.
Local farmers often remove devil's fig to prevent it from spreading to their crops.
The sharp thorns of devil's fig can injure livestock that graze nearby.
