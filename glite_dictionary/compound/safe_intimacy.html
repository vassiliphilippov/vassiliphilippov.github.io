safe intimacy

1. NOUN

/ seɪf ɪnˈtɪməsi / (AmE), / seɪf ɪnˈtɪməsi / (BrE)
intimate relations conducted in a way that minimizes the risk of sexually transmitted infections or other health concerns
intimate relations without health risk

Examples

Open communication is crucial for maintaining safe intimacy in any relationship.
Schools are increasingly offering workshops to educate young people about safe intimacy.
They both agreed to regular health screenings as part of their commitment to safe intimacy.

Synset Examples

Using a prophylactic is essential for safe intimacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe sex - sexual activity that is practiced in a way designed to reduce the risk of transmitting sexually transmitted infections
