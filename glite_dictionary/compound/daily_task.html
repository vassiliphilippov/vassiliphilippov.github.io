daily task

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli tæsk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli tɑːsk / (BrE)
a regular activity or chore that is performed every day
regular everyday activity or chore

Examples

I try to finish each daily task before noon to stay ahead of my schedule.
Making the bed is a simple daily task that helps keep the house tidy.
Some daily tasks, like taking out the trash, are shared among family members.

Synset Examples

These apps offer affordances that simplify daily tasks.
Zogbot is programmed to assist with our daily tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chore - a routine task, especially a household one
  • Synonym: routine - a usual set of activities or way of doing things
  • Degree Lower: weekly task - task performed every week
