toxic industrial waste

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑksɪk ɪnˈdʌstriəl weɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk ɪnˈdʌstriəl weɪst / (BrE)
poisonous waste materials produced by industrial processes, capable of causing injury or environmental harm, especially through chemical means
poisonous waste from industry

Examples

The factory was fined for illegally dumping toxic industrial waste into the river.
Strict regulations govern the disposal of toxic industrial waste to protect public health.
Scientists are developing new methods to safely treat toxic industrial waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazardous waste - waste that poses substantial or potential threats to public health or the environment
