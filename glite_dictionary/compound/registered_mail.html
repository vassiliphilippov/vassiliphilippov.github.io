registered mail

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd meɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd meɪl / (BrE)
Mail that is officially recorded by the postal service when sent, providing a record of delivery and added security.
secure, recorded postal service

Examples

I sent the important documents by registered mail to ensure they arrived safely.
When mailing valuable items, it's wise to use registered mail for extra security.
The package was shipped via registered mail and required a signature upon delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certified mail - mail for which the sender receives proof of delivery
