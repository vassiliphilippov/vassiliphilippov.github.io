to that extent

1. ADV

/ tə ðæt ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / tə ðat ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)
to the degree or level mentioned; as much as is stated or implied.
to the specified degree or level

Examples

She was angry, but I didn't think she would go to that extent to prove her point.
If you want to succeed to that extent, you must be prepared to work very hard.
He was surprised that she trusted him to that extent after everything that happened.

Synset Examples

insofar as it can be ascertained, the horse lung is comparable to that of man
so far as it is reasonably practical he should practice restraint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to that degree - to that extent; to the same level
