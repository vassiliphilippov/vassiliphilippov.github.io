Joe Average

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒoʊ ˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌdʒəʊ ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical person; the average member of society
an ordinary or typical person
informal

Examples

Many advertisements are designed to appeal to Joe Average rather than to specialists.
Although he's no superstar, Joe Average works hard and gets the job done.
In that survey, Joe Average reported spending about two hours online each day.

Synset Examples

Some politicians forget about the needs of Joe Average.
He's just a Joe Average, living a simple life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Average Joe - an ordinary or typical man; the average member of society

Etymology

Modelled after the given name 'Joe' as a generic male name, combined with 'average' to indicate typicality.
