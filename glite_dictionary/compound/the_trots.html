the trots

1. NOUN

/ ðə trɑts / (AmE), / ðə trɒts / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Diarrhea; an episode of frequent, loose, or watery bowel movements.
informal term for diarrhea
informal vulgar

Examples

He called in sick because he had the trots after eating at that restaurant.
Avoid drinking the tap water—you don't want to end up with the trots on your vacation.
After a questionable takeaway meal, she suffered from the trots all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - Frequent and watery bowel movements
