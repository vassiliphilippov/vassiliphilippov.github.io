cypress family

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪprəs ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪprəs ˌfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of coniferous trees and shrubs that includes cypresses, junipers, and many cedars
family of coniferous trees including cypresses and junipers

Examples

The cypress family includes species that are highly valued for their wood and ornamental qualities.
Junipers, which belong to the cypress family, produce distinctive blue berries.
Many members of the cypress family are adapted to dry, rocky soils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conifer - a tree that bears cones and evergreen needles or scales
