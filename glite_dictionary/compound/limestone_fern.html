limestone fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪmˌstoʊn fɝn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪmˌstəʊn fɜːn / (BrE)
A yellow-green fern found in rocky areas of the Northern Hemisphere, often growing on limestone.
yellow-green fern of rocky Northern Hemisphere areas

Examples

The limestone fern thrives in crevices of rocky hillsides across northern Europe.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the limestone fern in its natural limestone habitat.
We found several limestone ferns growing near the entrance to the cave.
