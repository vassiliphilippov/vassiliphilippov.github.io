to ooze with confidence

1. VERB

/ uz wɪð kənˈfɪdəns / (AmE), / uːz wɪð kənˈfɪd(ə)ns / (BrE)
to display or exude a strong sense of self-assurance or confidence in a noticeable way
display strong self-confidence

Examples

She seemed to ooze with confidence during the entire job interview.
Whenever he enters a room, he oozes with confidence and positivity.
The leader's ability to ooze with confidence inspired the whole team.

Synset Examples

Her confidence seemed to ooze with every word she spoke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiate confidence - show confidence very clearly or obviously
  • Antonym: lack confidence - display little or no self-assurance
