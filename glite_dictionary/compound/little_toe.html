little toe

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬.əl toʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl təʊ / (BrE)
the smallest and outermost toe on the human foot
smallest outermost toe

Examples

I stubbed my little toe on the corner of the table.
The shoe was so tight it squeezed her little toe.
Children sometimes call the little toe the 'pinky toe'.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: big toe - the largest and innermost toe on the human foot
