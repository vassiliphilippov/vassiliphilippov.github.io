fur-piece

1. NOUN

/ fɝː piːs / (AmE), / fɜː piːs / (BrE)
A scarf or collar made from animal fur, worn around the neck for warmth or as a fashion accessory.
neckpiece made of fur

Examples

She wore a luxurious fur-piece draped elegantly over her shoulders at the gala.
In the old photograph, her grandmother is dressed in a coat and a fur-piece.
Vintage stores occasionally offer a fur-piece, reminding shoppers of classic fashion trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stole - a woman's long scarf or shawl, often made of fur or similar material
