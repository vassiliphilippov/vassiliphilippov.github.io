street pharmacist

1. NOUN

/ stɹit ˈfɑːɹməˌsɪst / (AmE), / stɹiːt ˈfɑːməsɪst / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who sells drugs or remedies on the street, often referring to someone who deals in illegal substances
person illegally selling drugs on the street
slang informal

Examples

The city's street pharmacist was well-known for his discreet business practices.
Many neighborhoods struggle with problems caused by the local street pharmacist.
She joked that her cousin had become a street pharmacist after losing his job.

Synset Examples

The street pharmacist dispensed advice along with herbal cures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug dealer - person who sells illegal drugs
