moving picture

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a film or movie; a sequence of images shown on a screen that create the illusion of continuous motion, typically accompanied by sound, used for entertainment or storytelling
film/movie; sequence of moving images

Examples

The first moving picture ever made was only a few seconds long.
She has always been fascinated by the art of the moving picture.
Many classic moving pictures are now being restored for future generations.

Synset Examples

they went to a movie every Saturday night
the film was shot on location

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie - a story represented in motion by a sequence of images; film
  • Synonym: film - a motion picture; a sequence of moving images for entertainment

Etymology

Originally used in the early 20th century to describe films, from 'moving' (in motion) and 'picture' (image).
