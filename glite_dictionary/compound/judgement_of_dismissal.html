judgement of dismissal

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌdʒmənt əv dɪsˈmɪsəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒm(ə)nt əv dɪsˈmɪs(ə)l / (BrE)
a legal judgment that ends a case without proceeding to a trial
legal judgment ending a case without trial
legal

Examples

The court issued a judgement of dismissal after reviewing the submitted evidence.
A judgement of dismissal can prevent a lawsuit from advancing to a costly trial.
His lawyer successfully argued for a judgement of dismissal before the first hearing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismissal - the act of dismissing a case in a court of law
