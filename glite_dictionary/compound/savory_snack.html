savory snack

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪvəri snæk / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri snæk / (BrE)
a snack that is salty or spicy rather than sweet
salty or spicy snack food

Examples

She always chooses a savory snack like pretzels or crisps over cookies.
At the party, there was a table filled with different savory snacks for the guests.
If you're craving something salty, try a savory snack instead of chocolate.

Synset Examples

She prefers savory snacks over sweet ones.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sweet snack - a snack that is sweet in taste
