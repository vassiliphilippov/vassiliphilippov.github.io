ring ceremony

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / rɪŋ ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event in which graduates receive symbolic rings to mark their achievement, often held by schools or universities
formal event celebrating graduation with rings

Examples

The university held its annual ring ceremony for graduating seniors in the main auditorium.
Attending the ring ceremony was a proud moment for both students and their families.
She received her class ring at the ring ceremony, which marked the end of her college years.

Synset Examples

The class graduated with a special ring ceremony.
