to splash water

1. VERB

/ splæʃ ˈwɔtɚ / (AmE), / splæʃ ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
to make water scatter in drops or small waves by striking or moving through it suddenly
make water scatter by striking it

Examples

She laughed as she splash water on her friends at the pool.
The elephant loves to splash water over its back on hot days.
Please don't splash water on the bathroom floor.

Synset Examples

The kids splashed water everywhere while playing in the tub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splash - to scatter liquid in drops or small waves by striking it
