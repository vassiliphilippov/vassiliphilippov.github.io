total patzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈpæt.sər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈpæt.sə / (BrE)
An entirely unskilled or incompetent player, especially in games like chess.
completely unskilled player (esp. in chess)
informal

Examples

I'm a total patzer when it comes to playing bridge with my friends.
Despite practicing for years, she still considers herself a total patzer at chess.
He realized he was a total patzer after losing every match in the tournament.

Synset Examples

He joked that he was a total patzer at chess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patzer - An unskilled or incompetent player

Etymology

Patzer is from German 'patzen' (to botch), used informally in chess circles.
