local possie

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈpɒs.i / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈpɒs.i / (BrE)
a group of friends or associates from one's local area
group of local friends or associates
informal

Examples

After moving to town, she quickly became part of the local possie at the community center.
The local possie met every Friday night at the pub to catch up.
Everyone in the local possie pitched in to organize the charity fundraiser.

Synset Examples

The local possie is known for their annual cookout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: posse - a group of friends or associates

Etymology

"Possie" is a variant of "posse," from American English slang for a group of people; "local" meaning nearby or from the area.
