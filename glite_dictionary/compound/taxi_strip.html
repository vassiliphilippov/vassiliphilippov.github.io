taxi strip

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksi strɪp / (AmE), / ˈtæksi strɪp / (BrE)
A paved strip at an airport used by airplanes when taxiing to or from the runway.
paved strip for planes taxiing at airport

Examples

The pilot carefully guided the plane along the taxi strip before reaching the main runway.
Maintenance crews were repairing a section of the taxi strip after heavy use over the summer.
Please remain seated until the aircraft has stopped completely on the taxi strip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxiway - a path at an airport for aircraft to taxi to and from the runway
