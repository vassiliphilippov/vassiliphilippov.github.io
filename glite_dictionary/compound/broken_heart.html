broken heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊ.kən hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən hɑːt / (BrE)
A feeling of deep emotional pain or anguish, especially caused by the loss or rejection of a loved one.
intense sorrow from lost love

Examples

After the end of their long relationship, she suffered from a broken heart for months.
He wrote poetry to express the pain of his broken heart.
Time and support from friends eventually helped him heal his broken heart.

Synset Examples

he is recovering from a broken heart
a broken heart languishes here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartache - emotional anguish or grief, especially caused by loss or disappointment
  • Synonym: grief - intense sorrow, often due to loss
  • Antonym: joy - a feeling of great happiness
