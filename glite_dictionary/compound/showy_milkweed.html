showy milkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈmɪlkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈmɪlkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A species of milkweed native to southern North America, known for its large, star-shaped purple and pink flowers.
species of milkweed with large purple-pink flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with showy milkweed, attracting dozens of butterflies.
Conservationists planted showy milkweed to support the local monarch population.
Each stem of the showy milkweed bears clusters of vibrant, star-shaped flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkweed - a plant of the genus Asclepias, known for its milky sap and importance to butterflies
