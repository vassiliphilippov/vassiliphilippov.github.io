to wait until marriage

1. VERB

/ weɪt ənˈtɪl ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / weɪt ənˈtɪl ˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
to postpone engaging in sexual relations until after getting married, usually for religious or personal reasons
postpone sex until marriage

Examples

They chose to wait until marriage to have sex for the first time.
Some people wait until marriage because of religious beliefs or personal values.
She explained she wanted to wait until marriage before becoming sexually active.

Synset Examples

They decided to wait until marriage for coitus.
