printed symbol

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪntɪd ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪd ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
a character or mark produced in print or writing to represent information, such as a letter, numeral, or punctuation mark
a character or mark printed or written

Examples

Please write your name clearly, using each printed symbol in block letters.
A question mark is a common example of a printed symbol used in sentences.
Each printed symbol must be recognized by the scanning machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: character - a letter, symbol, or mark used in writing or printing
  • Synonym: glyph - a hieroglyphic character or symbol; a pictograph
