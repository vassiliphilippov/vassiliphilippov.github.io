for short

1. ADV

/ fər ˈʃɔrt / (AmE), / fə ˈʃɔːt / (BrE)
used to indicate that a shorter or simpler form of a name or phrase is being used instead of the full one
to indicate a shortened form of a name

Examples

Her name is Alexandra, but everyone calls her Alex for short.
The organisation is called the World Health Organization, or WHO for short.
You can refer to Professor Thompson as Prof. for short.

Synset Examples

His friends affectionately called him Albie for short.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nickname - an informal name given to someone or something
