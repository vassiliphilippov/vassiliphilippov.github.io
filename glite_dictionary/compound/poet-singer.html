poet-singer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊ.ɪt ˌsɪŋ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ɪt ˌsɪŋ.ə / (BrE)
a person who sings folk songs, often composing or performing them in a poetic style
folk song performer or composer

Examples

The ancient Greeks admired the poet-singer for preserving their history through song.
As a modern poet-singer, she writes and performs her own ballads.
Many cultures have some form of poet-singer who tells stories through music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk singer - a singer who performs folk songs
