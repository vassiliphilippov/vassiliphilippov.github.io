walking shoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkɪŋ ʃu / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ʃuː / (BrE)
a light, comfortable shoe designed specifically for walking, providing support and cushioning for the feet during vigorous or extended walks
shoe designed for vigorous walking

Examples

He bought a new pair of walking shoes before starting his hiking trip.
Comfortable walking shoes are essential for those who spend hours on their feet.
Many doctors recommend wearing proper walking shoes to avoid foot injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneaker - lightweight shoe worn for sports or casual occasions
  • Degree Higher: running shoe - shoe designed specifically for running
