state-of-the-art

1. ADJ

/ ˌsteɪt əv ði ˈɑrt / (AmE), / ˌsteɪt əv ði ˈɑːt / (BrE)
Incorporating or reflecting the most advanced techniques or methods available at the present time.
most advanced or up-to-date

Examples

This hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology.
Their new smartphone features a state-of-the-art camera system for stunning photos.
The research facility houses several state-of-the-art laboratories for innovative scientific work.

Synset Examples

state-of-the-art technology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting-edge - at the forefront of progress; extremely modern or advanced
  • Antonym: outdated - no longer fashionable or useful; obsolete

2. ADJ

/ steɪt əv ði ɑrt / (AmE), / steɪt əv ði ɑːt / (BrE)
incorporating the latest and most advanced technology or techniques available
most advanced or modern technology/technique

Examples

The new hospital boasts a state-of-the-art surgical center equipped with the latest technology.
Their state-of-the-art security system helps to prevent unauthorized access.
The company invested in state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment to improve efficiency.

Synset Examples

The lab's mass spec equipment is state-of-the-art.
They explored the ocean depths in a state-of-the-art submarine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting-edge - at the forefront of technological or scientific progress
  • Antonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date

Etymology

Formed from the phrase 'state of the art,' originally indicating the current level of development in a particular field or technology.
