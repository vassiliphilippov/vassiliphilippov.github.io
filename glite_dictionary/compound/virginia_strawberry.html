Virginia strawberry

1. NOUN

/ vərˈdʒɪnjə ˈstrɔːˌbɛri / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪni.ə ˈstrɒb.əri / (BrE)
a North American wild strawberry (Fragaria virginiana) with sweet red fruit, used as a source plant for many cultivated strawberries
North American wild strawberry species

Examples

The Virginia strawberry thrives in meadows and woodland edges across North America.
Some garden varieties were bred using the Virginia strawberry for its sweet flavor.
Wild patches of Virginia strawberry provide food for both wildlife and people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strawberry - a sweet, red, edible fruit produced by plants of the genus Fragaria
