to give pause

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv pɔz / (AmE), / ɡɪv pɔːz / (BrE)
to make someone hesitate or consider carefully before taking action
cause to hesitate or reconsider

Examples

The unexpected results may give pause to those considering similar investments.
Her strange behavior should give pause to anyone working with her closely.
The new evidence could give pause to even the most confident critics.

Synset Examples

This situation should give pause to everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cause to hesitate - to make someone pause before acting
  • Antonym: to reassure - to remove someone's hesitation or concern
