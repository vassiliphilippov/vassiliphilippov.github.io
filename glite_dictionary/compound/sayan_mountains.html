Sayan Mountains

1. PROPN

/ saɪˈæn ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / saɪˈan ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in southern Siberia, west of Lake Baikal, known for its important mineral deposits
mountain range in southern Siberia

Examples

The Sayan Mountains are famous for their rugged landscapes and rich wildlife.
Explorers were drawn to the Sayan Mountains because of the valuable minerals found there.
The Sayan Mountains form a natural border between Russia and Mongolia.
