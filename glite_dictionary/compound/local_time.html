local time

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl taɪm / (BrE)
the official time observed in a specific region, adjusted for its location relative to the Earth, and established by law or custom
official time in a specific region

Examples

Flight schedules are always listed in the local time of each city.
When we landed, it was already midnight local time.
Different countries may celebrate New Year's at different local times due to time zones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard time - the time of a specific time zone established by law or custom
