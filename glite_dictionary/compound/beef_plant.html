beef plant

1. NOUN

/ biːf plænt / (AmE), / biːf plɑːnt / (BrE)
A South American plant with green to purple or red branches, ornamental foliage, and spikes of small woolly flowers with dry membranous bracts.
South American ornamental plant with colorful foliage

Examples

Many gardeners grow the beef plant for its colorful leaves and striking appearance.
The beef plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
She added a beef plant to her collection of exotic ornamental foliage.
