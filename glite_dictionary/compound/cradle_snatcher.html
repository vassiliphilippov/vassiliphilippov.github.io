cradle snatcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪ.dl ˌsnæt͡ʃ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.dl ˌsnætʃ.ə / (BrE)
a person who is in a romantic or sexual relationship with someone much younger than themselves
person dating much younger partner

Examples

Some people wrongly judge Anna for being a cradle snatcher due to her young boyfriend.
The media often sensationalizes stories involving alleged cradle snatchers.
He hated being called a cradle snatcher just because his partner was a decade younger.

Synset Examples

Being a cradle snatcher, he faced backlash from his peers.
She defended herself against claims of being a cradle snatcher.
The tabloids labeled him a cradle snatcher after the news broke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cougar - an older woman who seeks relationships with much younger men
  • Synonym: age gap - a significant difference in age between partners
