night-stop

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪt.stɑp / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.stɒp / (BrE)
a break or stop in a journey for the night, typically involving an overnight stay at a location before resuming travel the next day.
overnight stop during a journey

Examples

The flight included a night-stop in Dubai before continuing to Australia.
Due to the long drive, we planned a night-stop halfway to our destination.
Our cruise itinerary features a night-stop at a small coastal village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stopover - a short stay somewhere between parts of a journey
