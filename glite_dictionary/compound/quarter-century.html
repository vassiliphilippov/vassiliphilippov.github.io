quarter-century

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtɚ ˈsɛn.tʃɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈkwɔː.tə ˈsɛn.tʃər.i / (BrE)
A period of twenty-five years.
period of 25 years

Examples

The old library building survived more than a quarter-century before it was finally rebuilt.
She has dedicated a quarter-century to researching climate change and its effects.
This technology, once revolutionary, is now a quarter-century old.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: century - a period of one hundred years
  • Degree Lower: decade - a period of ten years

Etymology

Formed from 'quarter' (one fourth) + 'century' (100 years).
