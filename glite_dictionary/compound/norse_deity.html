Norse deity

1. NOUN

/ nɔrs ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / nɔːs ˈdiːəti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the ancient Norse people, especially in Norse mythology
a god or goddess from Norse mythology

Examples

Odin is a powerful Norse deity associated with wisdom and war.
Many stories about Thor, a famous Norse deity, are told in the ancient sagas.
The museum's exhibit features statues of every major Norse deity from the Viking Age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Norse god - a male god from Norse mythology
  • Synonym: Norse goddess - a female deity from Norse mythology
