energy-storing

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi ˈstɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˈstɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
Relating to the storage of energy, especially in the context of biological or technical processes that accumulate energy for later use.
relating to storing energy

Examples

The liver plays a key role in many energy-storing metabolic processes.
Fat cells function as energy-storing compartments within animals' bodies.
Scientists are developing better energy-storing materials for use in batteries and other devices.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: energy-releasing - relating to processes that release energy (especially catabolism)
