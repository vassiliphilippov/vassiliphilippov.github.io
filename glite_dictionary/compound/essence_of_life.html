essence of life

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsəns əv laɪf / (AmE), / ˈɛsəns əv lʌɪf / (BrE)
the fundamental nature or most important quality that makes life meaningful or valuable
fundamental quality making life meaningful

Examples

For many people, creativity is the essence of life and brings them true happiness.
Some philosophers spend their whole careers searching for the essence of life.
He believes that connecting with others is the essence of life.

Synset Examples

Traveling around the world is the essence of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meaning of life - the purpose or significance of human existence
  • Synonym: life force - vital principle or energy believed to be at the core of living beings
