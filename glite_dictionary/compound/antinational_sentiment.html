antinational sentiment

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈsɛn.tə.mənt / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈsɛn.tɪ.mənt / (BrE)
opposition or hostility toward national interests or patriotism
opposition to national interests

Examples

The journalist's article was criticized for expressing antinational sentiment during a sensitive time.
Any form of antinational sentiment can be controversial, especially in times of conflict.
He was reprimanded after his social media post was deemed to reflect antinational sentiment.

Synset Examples

She was accused of antinational sentiments during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nationalism - strong identification with and support for one's nation
  • Antonym: patriotism - devotion to and vigorous support for one's country

Etymology

From anti- (against) + national (relating to a nation) + sentiment (feeling or opinion).
