newspaper critic

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who writes reviews or articles expressing opinions on various subjects, such as art, theater, or books, for a newspaper
journalist who reviews for a newspaper

Examples

The newspaper critic gave the play a glowing review in Sunday's edition.
As a newspaper critic, her columns influenced public opinion on new films.
Many readers look forward to the annual best-of list compiled by the newspaper critic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critic - a person who expresses opinions about the merits of something, especially art or literature
