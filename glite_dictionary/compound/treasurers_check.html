treasurer's check

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒərərz tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒ(ə)rəz tʃɛk / (BrE)
a check drawn by a bank officer on the bank's own funds rather than on a customer's account
bank check issued from bank's own funds

Examples

The payment must be made with a treasurer's check to ensure security.
He obtained a treasurer's check from his bank for the transaction.
Many institutions accept a treasurer's check as readily as cash.

Synset Examples

cashier's checks are as good as cash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cashier's check - a check drawn by a bank on its own funds and signed by a cashier
