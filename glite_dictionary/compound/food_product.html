food product

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / fuːd ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a substance or item that can be used or prepared for use as food, usually as a result of processing or manufacturing
item suitable for use as food

Examples

Many supermarkets offer a wide variety of food products from around the world.
The company introduced a new food product made from organic ingredients.
Labels on food products must list all the ingredients for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foodstuff - a substance suitable for consumption as food
