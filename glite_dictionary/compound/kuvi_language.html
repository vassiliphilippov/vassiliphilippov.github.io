Kuvi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːvi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkuːvi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken in parts of southeast India, particularly in Odisha and Andhra Pradesh.
Dravidian language of southeast India

Examples

Linguists have studied the Kuvi language to better understand Dravidian roots.
The Kuvi language is used by communities in Odisha and Andhra Pradesh.
There are ongoing efforts to preserve the Kuvi language among younger generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dravidian - a family of languages mainly spoken in southern India
