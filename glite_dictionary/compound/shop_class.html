shop class

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp klæs / (AmE), / ʃɒp klɑːs / (BrE)
a school course in which students learn practical skills such as woodworking, metalworking, or other trades
practical skills class (woodworking, etc.)
american

Examples

I learned how to use a lathe in shop class at school.
Many students look forward to shop class because it offers hands-on experience.
In shop class, we built our own bookshelves and painted them.

Synset Examples

I built a birdhouse in shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrial arts - a school subject teaching practical manual skills
