gut-scraper

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌtˌskreɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtˌskreɪpə / (BrE)
An experience that is extremely thrilling, intense, or frightening, often one that tests courage or endurance.
thrilling or intense experience

Examples

The roller coaster was a real gut-scraper from start to finish.
Climbing the mountain in bad weather turned out to be a gut-scraper for the whole team.
Watching the final moments of the championship game was a gut-scraper for the fans.

Synset Examples

The roller coaster was a gut-scraper of a ride.
The steep hike felt like a gut-scraper.
That horror movie was a real gut-scraper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail-biter - A suspenseful or tense situation or experience

Etymology

From 'gut' (meaning courage or stomach, symbolizing emotion or fear) + 'scraper' (something rough or intense). Patterned after similar compounds like 'nail-biter.'
