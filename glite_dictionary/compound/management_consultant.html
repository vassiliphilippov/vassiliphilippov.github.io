management consultant

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænədʒmənt kənˈsʌltənt / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒmənt kənˈsʌltənt / (BrE)
a professional who advises organizations on how to improve their management practices and business performance
business adviser specializing in management

Examples

The company hired a management consultant to streamline their operations and increase efficiency.
After years in finance, she became a management consultant, working with clients around the world.
The management consultant prepared a detailed report on improving employee productivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultant - a person who provides expert advice professionally
