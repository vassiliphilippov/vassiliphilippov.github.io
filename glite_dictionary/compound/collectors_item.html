collector's item

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛktərz ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktəz ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an object that is valued and sought after by collectors, especially because it is rare, unique, or regarded as the most important piece in a collection
object sought by collectors

Examples

That vintage comic book is considered a true collector's item among enthusiasts.
The car on display is a collector's item worth millions of dollars.
She was thrilled to add a rare stamp to her collection as her latest collector's item.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curio - an unusual or interesting object collected for its rarity or curiosity value

2. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛktɚz ˌaɪtəm / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktəz ˌaɪtəm / (BrE)
an object that is valued and sought after by collectors, often because it is rare, unique, or historically significant
object valued for rarity or significance

Examples

That limited edition comic book has become a real collector's item among fans.
Early vinyl records often turn into prized collector's items over time.
The signed poster is now considered a collector's item due to its rarity.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
