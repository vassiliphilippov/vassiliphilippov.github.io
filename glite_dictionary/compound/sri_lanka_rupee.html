Sri Lanka rupee

1. NOUN

/ sri ˈlæŋkə ruˈpi / (AmE), / sri ˈlæŋkə ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency of Sri Lanka, divided into 100 cents
currency of Sri Lanka

Examples

The price of tea is listed in Sri Lanka rupees at the local market.
She exchanged her dollars for Sri Lanka rupees before traveling to Colombo.
The Sri Lanka rupee has experienced fluctuations against the US dollar in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - the currency unit used in several South Asian countries
  • Degree Lower: cent - a monetary unit equal to one-hundredth of the basic currency

Etymology

From Hindi रुपया (rupayā) and Sinhalese රුපියල් (rupiyal), ultimately from Sanskrit रूप्य (rūpya, meaning 'wrought silver').
