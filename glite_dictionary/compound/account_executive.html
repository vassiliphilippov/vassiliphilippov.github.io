account executive

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊnt ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing a client's account in an advertising agency, brokerage, or similar service business
client manager in advertising or sales

Examples

The account executive met with the client to discuss the new advertising campaign.
As an account executive, she is responsible for maintaining relationships with major customers.
Many account executives work closely with the creative team to ensure client satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: account manager - a person responsible for managing clients' accounts, especially in sales or advertising
