administrative unit

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪsˈtreɪtɪv ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a division or part of an organization, government, or institution that is responsible for administration or management of certain functions or areas
division with administrative responsibilities

Examples

Each university administrative unit is responsible for overseeing its own budget and personnel.
The country is divided into several administrative units for more efficient governance.
Her new job involves managing an administrative unit within the health department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a distinct part of an organization with specific responsibilities
  • Synonym: division - a major administrative part of an organization or government
