red kauri

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈkaʊri / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈkɔːri / (BrE)
a New Zealand tree (Agathis australis) with glossy leaves and scaly reddish-brown bark
New Zealand tree with reddish-brown bark

Examples

The towering red kauri dominates parts of New Zealand's native forests.
Botanists studied the unique bark of the red kauri to better understand its adaptation to the local environment.
A fallen red kauri can provide a habitat for many forest creatures.
