heat exchanger

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːt ɪkˌsʧeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈhiːt ɪksˌʧeɪndʒə / (BrE)
a device that transfers heat from one fluid to another without the fluids mixing
device transferring heat between fluids

Examples

The air conditioning system uses a heat exchanger to cool the air efficiently.
Engineers inspected the heat exchanger for any signs of corrosion or leaks.
Improving the design of the heat exchanger can lead to significant energy savings.
