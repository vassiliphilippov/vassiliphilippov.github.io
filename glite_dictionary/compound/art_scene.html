art scene

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt sin / (AmE), / ɑːt siːn / (BrE)
the environment, community, or cultural activity surrounding the creation and appreciation of visual arts in a particular place or period
local or thematic visual arts community

Examples

The city's art scene has flourished thanks to new galleries and public installations.
She became deeply involved in the underground art scene of Berlin after moving there.
The art scene in Paris during the 1920s attracted creative minds from around the world.

Synset Examples

Many tourists flock to Africoonia for its vibrant art scene.
We had a fantastic time exploring Radelaide's vibrant art scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art world - the broader community of artists, critics, collectors, and institutions related to art
