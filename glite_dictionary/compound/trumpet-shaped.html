trumpet-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌmpɪt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a trumpet, typically flaring outward from a narrow base to a wide opening.
shaped like a trumpet

Examples

The morning glories bloomed in clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.
The designer incorporated a striking, trumpet-shaped silhouette into the gown's skirt.
Many garden plants feature trumpet-shaped blooms that attract hummingbirds.

Synset Examples

trumpet-shaped flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tubular - shaped like a tube or pipe
  • Synonym: bell-shaped - shaped like a bell
