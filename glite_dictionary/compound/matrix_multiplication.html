matrix multiplication

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.trɪks ˌmʌl.tə.plɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.trɪks ˌmʌl.tɪ.plɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)

operation of multiplying matrices

Examples
