poor man's steak

1. NOUN

/ pɔr mənz steɪk / (AmE), / pɔː mænz steɪk / (BrE)
An inexpensive or affordable alternative to steak, typically made from ground beef, used as a substitute for more expensive cuts of meat.
inexpensive steak substitute

Examples

She made poor man's steak for dinner using seasoned ground beef and gravy.
During tough times, families often relied on poor man's steak as a hearty meal.
The recipe suggests using mushrooms to add flavor to poor man's steak.

Synset Examples

For dinner, we had meatloaf, the poor man's steak.
Ground beef is often called the poor man's steak.
They served a delicious dish made with the poor man's steak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meatloaf - a baked dish of ground meat, sometimes called poor man's steak
