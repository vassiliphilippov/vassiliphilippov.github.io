written matter

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪtən ˈmætər / (AmE), / ˈrɪtən ˈmatə / (BrE)
textual content to be printed or published, excluding any graphical materials such as images or charts
textual content, not including graphics

Examples

The editor reviewed all the written matter before sending the magazine to print.
Please separate the written matter from the illustrations for the publisher.
Copyright law protects both written matter and artistic works.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: text - the main body of written or printed matter
