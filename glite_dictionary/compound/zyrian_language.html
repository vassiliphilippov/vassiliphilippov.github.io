Zyrian language

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪriən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈzɪəriən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Finno-Ugric language spoken by the Komi people of northeastern European Russia.
language of the Komi people

Examples

The Zyrian language is still spoken by many members of the Komi community today.
Scholars have published several dictionaries and grammars of the Zyrian language.
She is studying the Zyrian language to better understand the culture of the Komi people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Komi language - the language spoken by the Komi people, also known as Zyrian

Etymology

From Russian 'Zyryane' (the native name for Komi people) + 'language'.
