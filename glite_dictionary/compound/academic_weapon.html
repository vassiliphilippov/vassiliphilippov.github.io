academic weapon

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
a significant advantage, skill, or tool used to excel in academic discussions, debates, or work
significant academic advantage or tool

Examples

Her thorough understanding of statistics was her greatest academic weapon during research presentations.
He considers a well-organized bibliography to be an essential academic weapon.
With fluent writing skills as an academic weapon, she excelled in every class essay.

Synset Examples

Her extensive knowledge became an academic weapon in debates.
He used his thesis as an academic weapon against critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advantage - something that gives someone a better chance of success
  • Synonym: weapon - a means of gaining an advantage in a conflict or contest
