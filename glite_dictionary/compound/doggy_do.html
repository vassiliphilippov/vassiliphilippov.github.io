doggy do

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑɡi ˌdu / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡi ˌduː / (BrE)
feces left by a dog, especially regarded as an unpleasant or dirty substance to clean up
dog feces (informal)
informal

Examples

Please remember to pick up your doggy do when walking in the park.
There was doggy do all over the sidewalk after the storm.
She carried a bag to properly dispose of her dog's doggy do.
