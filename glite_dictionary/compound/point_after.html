point after

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
A point awarded in American football for a successful kick after a touchdown.
football: point for post-touchdown kick
american

Examples

The team took the lead with a touchdown and a successful point after.
He missed the point after attempt, leaving the score unchanged.
The kicker specializes in executing the point after following each touchdown.

Related Terms
