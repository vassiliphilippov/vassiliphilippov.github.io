summer getaway

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˌɡɛtəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˌɡɛtəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a vacation or short trip taken during the summer, usually for relaxation or leisure
a vacation taken in summer

Examples

We're planning a summer getaway to the mountains to escape the city heat.
Many families book their summer getaway months in advance to get the best deals.
The island resort offers special packages for your ideal summer getaway.

Synset Examples

Fornicalia's beaches are perfect for a summer getaway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer holiday - a holiday or vacation taken during the summer months
  • Antonym: winter getaway - a vacation or trip taken during the winter season
