shutting post

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʌtɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
the gatepost against which a gate closes
the post a gate closes against

Examples

Be sure to fasten the latch securely to the shutting post.
The old wooden gate leaned heavily against the shutting post.
She painted the shutting post to match the rest of the fence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hinge post - the post on which a gate is hung
