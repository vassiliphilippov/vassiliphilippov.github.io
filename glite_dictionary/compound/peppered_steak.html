peppered steak

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpɚd steɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəd steɪk / (BrE)
A steak that is coated with crushed peppercorns, pan-broiled, and often served with a sauce, typically made with brandy and butter.
steak coated with peppercorns and sauce

Examples

He ordered a peppered steak with a side of mashed potatoes at the restaurant.
The chef prepared a delicious peppered steak, coated generously with crushed black pepper.
For a special dinner, try making peppered steak with a brandy-and-butter sauce.
