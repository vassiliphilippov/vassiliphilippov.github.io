patchwork of ideas

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætʃˌwɜrk əv aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈpætʃˌwɜːk əv aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
A collection or mixture of different and diverse concepts, often combined in a way that lacks unity or consistency.
mixture of diverse concepts

Examples

The new policy was criticized as a patchwork of ideas lacking clear direction.
Her novel is a fascinating patchwork of ideas drawn from historical and futuristic themes.
The committee produced a patchwork of ideas that reflected the members' diverse backgrounds.

Synset Examples

The project was a patchwork of ideas from different team members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collection of ideas - a group or assortment of ideas
  • Synonym: jumble of ideas - a confused mixture of ideas

Etymology

Extension of 'patchwork', from 'patch' + 'work', originally meaning a fabric made of sewn patches.
