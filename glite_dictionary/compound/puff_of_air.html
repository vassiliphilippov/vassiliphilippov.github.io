puff of air

1. NOUN

/ pʌf əv ɛr / (AmE), / pʌf əv eə / (BrE)
a short, light movement or gust of air
brief light gust of air

Examples

A sudden puff of air blew the papers off the table.
She felt a cool puff of air on her neck as the window opened.
The candle flickered out with just a small puff of air.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gust - a strong, brief rush of wind
