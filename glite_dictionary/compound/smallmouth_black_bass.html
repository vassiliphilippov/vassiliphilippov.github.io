smallmouth black bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɔlˌmaʊθ blæk bæs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌmaʊθ blæk bɑːs / (BrE)
a species of freshwater fish in the sunfish family, noted for its relatively small mouth and angling popularity
species of freshwater fish

Examples

The smallmouth black bass is prized by anglers for its fighting spirit and agility.
He caught a large smallmouth black bass while fishing in the lake.
Unlike its cousin, the smallmouth black bass has a mouth that does not extend past the eye.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: largemouth bass - a related freshwater fish species with a much larger mouth
