cave man

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪv mæn / (AmE), / ˈkeɪv man / (BrE)
a prehistoric man who lived in caves, especially during the Stone Age
prehistoric man living in caves

Examples

Early humans often lived as cave men, seeking shelter in natural rock formations.
The cartoon depicted a cave man inventing the wheel for the first time.
Archaeologists uncovered ancient tools believed to be used by cave men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troglodyte - a person who lived in caves; a cave-dweller
