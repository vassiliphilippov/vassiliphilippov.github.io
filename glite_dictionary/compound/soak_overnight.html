to soak overnight

1. VERB

/ soʊk ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt / (AmE), / səʊk ˌəʊvəˈnaɪt / (BrE)
to immerse something in water or other liquid and leave it for an entire night, typically to soften or prepare it for use
immerse in liquid for a night

Examples

For this recipe, you need to soak overnight the dried chickpeas to make them tender.
If you soak overnight, the stains will come out more easily from the fabric.
The instructions say to soak overnight for best results before cooking the rice.

Synset Examples

Let the beans soak overnight for better cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soak - to immerse in liquid for a period of time
