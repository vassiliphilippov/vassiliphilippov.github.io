nutso idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtsoʊ aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈnʌtsəʊ aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
A crazy, eccentric, or wildly unconventional idea.
crazy or eccentric idea
informal slang

Examples

That was a nutso idea, but it turned out to be a big success.
She keeps coming up with nutso ideas that nobody else would even consider.
They dismissed his nutso idea at first, but later realized its value.

Synset Examples

His nutso ideas often lead to creative solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy idea - an idea that is bizarre or irrational

Etymology

From 'nutso' (slang for crazy, likely from 'nut' meaning crazy person) + 'idea'.
