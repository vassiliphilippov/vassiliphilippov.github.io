growing pains

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊ.ɪŋ ˌpeɪnz / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊ.ɪŋ ˌpeɪnz / (BrE)
aches or discomfort in muscles or joints, typically experienced by children and often believed to be related to rapid growth during development.
muscle or joint aches in growing children

Examples

Many children complain of growing pains in their legs at night.
The pediatrician explained that growing pains are a normal part of childhood.
Despite his growing pains, he played soccer enthusiastically every weekend.
