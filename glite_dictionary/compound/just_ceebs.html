just ceebs

1. X

/ dʒʌst sibz / (AmE), / dʒʌst siːbz / (BrE)
Used to express a lack of motivation or unwillingness to do something; feeling too lazy or can't be bothered.
slang: can't be bothered, unmotivated
informal slang

Examples

I was going to cook dinner, but just ceebs tonight.
We could go to the gym, but honestly, just ceebs.
Do you want to come out? Nah, just ceebs after that long meeting.

Synset Examples

After a long week, I feel like just ceebs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: can't be bothered - not willing to make the effort to do something

Etymology

Australian slang; 'ceebs' is a shortening of 'can't be bothered.'
