tonsorial artist

1. NOUN

/ tɑnˈsɔriəl ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / tɒnˈsɔːriəl ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
A barber or hairdresser; a person professionally engaged in cutting and styling hair.
barber; hairdresser

Examples

He scheduled an appointment with his trusted tonsorial artist every month.
The tonsorial artist skillfully transformed my unkempt hair into a sleek style.
At the new salon, the tonsorial artist offered expert advice on beard grooming.

Synset Examples

The tonsorial artist gave him a stylish new look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barber - a person whose job is cutting men's hair and shaving or trimming beards
  • Synonym: hairdresser - a person who cuts and styles hair as a profession

Etymology

From 'tonsorial' (relating to barbering, from Latin 'tonsor', barber) + 'artist'.
