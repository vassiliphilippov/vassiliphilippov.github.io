patrol boat

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊl boʊt / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊl bəʊt / (BrE)
a small naval vessel used to monitor and protect a specific area, especially for security or law enforcement purposes
small naval vessel for area patrol

Examples

The navy deployed a patrol boat to secure the harbor against intruders.
A patrol boat moves quickly along the coastline, keeping watch for illegal activities.
Several patrol boats were on duty during the maritime festival to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunboat - a small armed boat used for military purposes
