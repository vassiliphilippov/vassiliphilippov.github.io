chain saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪn ˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪn ˌsɔː / (BrE)
a portable power saw with a set of teeth attached to a rotating chain, used especially for cutting wood
portable saw with rotating chain

Examples

He used a chain saw to cut down the old tree in the backyard.
The loud noise from the chain saw startled the neighbors.
After sharpening the teeth, the chain saw worked much more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chainsaw - alternate spelling for chain saw
