trailer truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪlər ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a large road vehicle consisting of a powerful tractor unit pulling one or more long trailers, used for transporting goods
large vehicle consisting of a tractor and trailer

Examples

A trailer truck pulled up to the loading dock early in the morning.
He drives a trailer truck across several states every week.
The accident involved a trailer truck carrying fresh produce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tractor-trailer - a combination of a truck tractor and a trailer
  • Synonym: lorry - a large vehicle for transporting goods (British English)
