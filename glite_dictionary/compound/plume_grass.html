plume grass

1. NOUN

/ plum ɡræs / (AmE), / pluːm ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A tall, reedlike grass of the genus Erianthus, characterized by large, feathery plumes.
reedlike grass with large plumes

Examples

The plume grass swayed gently in the autumn breeze, its feathery tops shimmering in the sunlight.
Gardeners often use plume grass as an ornamental plant because of its attractive, fluffy seed heads.
Large stands of plume grass can be seen along riverbanks and wetlands throughout the southeastern United States.
