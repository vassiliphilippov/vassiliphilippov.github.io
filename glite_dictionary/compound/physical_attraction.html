physical attraction

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl əˈtrækʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl əˈtrækʃən / (BrE)
sexual or romantic interest in someone based on their physical appearance or bodily qualities
sexual or romantic interest based on looks

Examples

There was an undeniable physical attraction between them from the moment they met.
Some relationships are built on physical attraction, while others are based on emotional connection.
He realized their relationship lacked more than just physical attraction to be truly fulfilling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual attraction - attraction to others based on sexual desire
  • Antonym: emotional attraction - attraction based on emotional connection
