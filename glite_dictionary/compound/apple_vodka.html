apple vodka

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əl ˈvoʊd.kə / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl ˈvɒd.kə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic beverage made from vodka and flavored with apples.
vodka with apple flavor

Examples

She ordered a glass of apple vodka at the bar to try something new.
The cocktail menu includes a refreshing apple vodka martini.
You can really taste the crisp fruitiness in this brand of apple vodka.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear, strong alcoholic spirit from grain or potatoes
  • Degree Higher: apple brandy - brandy distilled from apples
