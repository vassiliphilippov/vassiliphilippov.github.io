green June beetle

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin dʒun ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ɡriːn dʒuːn ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a large greenish beetle (Cotinis nitida) commonly found in the southern United States, known for its metallic green color and appearance in early summer
large greenish beetle of the southern U.S.

Examples

In the garden, a green June beetle buzzed loudly among the blooming flowers.
The children were fascinated by the shiny shell of the green June beetle they found on the porch.
Farmers often find the green June beetle feeding on ripening fruit during the summer.
