helping hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (BrE)
assistance or support, especially given to someone in need
assistance; support

Examples

Whenever you need a helping hand, just call me.
The volunteers gave a helping hand to the disaster victims after the flood.
Jane always extends a helping hand to new employees in the office.

Synset Examples

She offered a helping hand to her neighbor.
He always has a helping hand when needed.
I appreciate your helping hand with the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistance - the act of helping or supporting someone
  • Synonym: aid - help or support, especially in difficult situations
  • Antonym: hindrance - something that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction
