shoulder holster

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər ˌhoʊlstər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə ˌhɒlstə / (BrE)
A holster designed to be worn over the shoulder, allowing a gun to be concealed under a jacket or coat.
holster worn over the shoulder

Examples

The detective reached into his shoulder holster and drew his pistol.
He preferred carrying a firearm in a shoulder holster for easier concealment.
A shoulder holster can be more comfortable than one worn on the hip, especially when seated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holster - a case for carrying a handgun
