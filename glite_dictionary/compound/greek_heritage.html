Greek heritage

1. NOUN

/ ɡrik ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡriːk ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the traditions, customs, history, and cultural legacy originating from Greece or Greek people
cultural legacy from Greece

Examples

Many people in the city celebrate festivals that honor their Greek heritage.
She takes pride in her Greek heritage and often shares traditional recipes with friends.
The museum exhibit showcased the influence of Greek heritage on modern civilization.
