acrobatic feat

1. NOUN

/ ˌækrəˈbætɪk fit / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈbætɪk fiːt / (BrE)
a difficult or impressive physical stunt performed by an acrobat
stunt by an acrobat

Examples

The gymnast amazed the audience with an acrobatic feat on the balance beam.
Performing such an acrobatic feat requires years of practice and training.
He held his breath as she completed an acrobatic feat high above the crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stunt - a dangerous or impressive trick or act, often in entertainment
