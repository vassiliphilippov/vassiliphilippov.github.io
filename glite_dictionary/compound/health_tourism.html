health tourism

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ˌtʊr.ɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ˌtʊə.rɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the act of traveling to another country or region to receive medical treatment or engage in wellness activities
travel for medical or wellness treatment

Examples

Many countries actively promote health tourism to attract international patients.
The rise in health tourism has led to new regulations for overseas clinics.
She booked a health tourism package that included surgery and a spa retreat.

Synset Examples

Many seek health tourism for dental work abroad.
Health tourism offers wellness retreats and surgeries.
Health tourism is booming in countries with affordable treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical tourism - traveling abroad for medical treatment
