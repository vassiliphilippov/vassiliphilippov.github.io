funnel-crest rosebud orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌnəl krɛst ˈroʊzbʌd ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌnəl krɛst ˈrəʊzbʌd ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to the northeastern United States, featuring magenta-pink flowers with a funnel-shaped lip; sometimes classified in the genus Pogonia.
orchid with magenta-pink, funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

The funnel-crest rosebud orchid blooms in late spring, adding a splash of magenta to the woodland floor.
Botanists found a population of funnel-crest rosebud orchids in a protected wetland area.
Gardeners value the funnel-crest rosebud orchid for its unusual funnel-shaped flowers and vibrant color.
