rib joint pliers

1. NOUN

/ rɪb dʒɔɪnt ˈplaɪɚz / (AmE), / rɪb dʒɔɪnt ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
a type of pliers with a movable pivot that allows the jaws to be adjusted for gripping different sizes of objects
pliers with adjustable pivot for varied grip

Examples

He used his rib joint pliers to loosen the stubborn pipe fitting.
Rib joint pliers are useful for gripping irregularly shaped objects during repairs.
When tackling plumbing tasks, many professionals rely on rib joint pliers for their versatility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slip-joint pliers - pliers with an adjustable pivot similar to rib joint pliers
