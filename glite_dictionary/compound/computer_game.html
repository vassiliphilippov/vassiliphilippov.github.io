computer game

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːt̬ɚ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played using a computer, typically involving interaction with software for entertainment or competition.
game played on a computer

Examples

He spent the entire afternoon playing his favorite computer game.
New releases in the computer game industry are often highly anticipated by fans.
A good computer game can be both entertaining and educational.

Related Terms
