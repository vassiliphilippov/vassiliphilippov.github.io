entertainment industry

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪn.mənt ˈɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪn.mənt ˈɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
The sector of business and society involved in producing and distributing entertainment, such as radio, television, films, and theater.
business producing and distributing entertainment

Examples

After college, she dreamed of working in the entertainment industry in Los Angeles.
Technological advances have significantly changed the entertainment industry over the past decade.
Many people underestimate how competitive the entertainment industry can be.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show business - the world of entertainment, especially the commercial, professional aspects
