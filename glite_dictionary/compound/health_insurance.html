health insurance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ɪnˌʃʊrəns / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ɪnˌʃɔːrəns / (BrE)
insurance covering the cost of medical treatment and healthcare expenses in the event of illness or injury
insurance for medical expenses

Examples

Many employers offer health insurance as part of their benefits package.
Without health insurance, medical bills can quickly become overwhelming.
She compared several health insurance plans before choosing the best one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical insurance - insurance covering medical expenses
  • Degree Lower: life insurance - insurance paying out on death of the insured
  • Synonym: disability insurance - insurance against loss of income due to disability

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθ ɪnˌʃʊrəns / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ ɪnˌʃɔːrəns / (BrE)
an arrangement by which a person or group receives financial coverage for medical expenses, usually by paying regular premiums to an insurance provider
coverage for medical expenses

Examples

Many employers offer health insurance as part of their benefits package.
After losing his job, he had to find private health insurance to cover his family's medical needs.
Travelers are often advised to purchase health insurance before visiting another country.

Synset Examples

Make sure you have health insurance before traveling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical insurance - insurance that covers the cost of medical care
