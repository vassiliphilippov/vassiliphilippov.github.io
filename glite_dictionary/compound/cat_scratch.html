cat scratch

1. NOUN

/ kæt skrætʃ / (AmE), / kæt skrætʃ / (BrE)
A scratch on the skin caused by a cat's claws.
scratch caused by a cat's claws

Examples

She noticed a cat scratch on her ankle after playing with her kitten.
The doctor examined the cat scratch to check for signs of infection.
A minor cat scratch can still cause irritation if not cleaned properly.

Synset Examples

She got a cat scratch on her arm while playing.
