skirt chaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɝt ˌtʃeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈskɜːt ˌtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
a man who habitually pursues or tries to attract women in an overt or aggressive manner
man who aggressively pursues women
informal

Examples

He had a reputation as a skirt chaser long before he moved to the city.
No one took his romantic advances seriously, knowing he was a notorious skirt chaser.
Her friends warned her that he was just another skirt chaser looking for attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women

Etymology

From 'skirt' (slang for woman) and 'chaser' (someone who actively pursues something), mid-20th century slang.

2. NOUN

/ skɝt ˈtʃeɪsɚ / (AmE), / skɜːt ˈtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
an informal term for a man who habitually pursues women in a flirtatious or promiscuous manner
man who persistently pursues women
informal

Examples

Everyone at the office knew that Tom was a notorious skirt chaser.
She warned her friend to steer clear of the skirt chaser at the club.
Rumors spread quickly about the new neighbor being a bit of a skirt chaser.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
