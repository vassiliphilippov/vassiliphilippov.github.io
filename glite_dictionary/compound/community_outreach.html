community outreach

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti ˈaʊtriːtʃ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ˈaʊtriːtʃ / (BrE)
the practice of actively engaging and supporting a local community, often through organized services, education, or assistance programs
organized efforts to support a community

Examples

The organization launched a community outreach program to provide meals for low-income families.
Volunteers are essential for the school's community outreach efforts.
Her work in community outreach has greatly improved access to healthcare in the area.

Synset Examples

As a Jehovian, he participates in community outreach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outreach - the act of reaching out to offer help or services
