happy murr

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpi mɜr / (AmE), / ˈhæpi məː / (BrE)
a soft sound expressing contentment or pleasure, typically made by an animal such as a cat
sound expressing contentment or pleasure

Examples

The kitten curled up in her lap with a happy murr as she stroked its fur.
A gentle happy murr escaped from the cat whenever it was fed its favorite food.
After a long nap in the sun, the old tomcat welcomed his owner with a soft happy murr.

Synset Examples

He let out a happy murr when he saw his favorite toy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purr - a low continuous vibratory sound expressing contentment, typically made by cats
