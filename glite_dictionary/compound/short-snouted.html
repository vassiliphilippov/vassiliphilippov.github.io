short-snouted

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌsnaʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌsnaʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having a snout that is shorter than average for the species.
having a shorter than average snout

Examples

The short-snouted dog breeds, like pugs and bulldogs, often have breathing difficulties.
A short-snouted crocodile is easier to distinguish from its longer-snouted relatives.
Scientists observed a short-snouted lizard thriving in the desert environment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-snouted - having a longer than average snout
