tankbuster capability

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋkˌbʌstər kəˌpeɪbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈtæŋkˌbʌstə kəˌpeɪbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the power or ability of a weapon, vehicle, or aircraft to destroy tanks
ability to destroy tanks

Examples

The new missile system gives the army significant tankbuster capability on the battlefield.
Many modern attack helicopters are valued for their tankbuster capability.
Improvements in technology have greatly enhanced the tankbuster capability of air support units.

Synset Examples

They equipped the aircraft with tankbuster capabilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-tank capability - ability to engage and destroy tanks or armored vehicles
