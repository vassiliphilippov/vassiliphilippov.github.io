Ozark Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ˈoʊzɑrk ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ˈəʊzɑːk ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a region of low mountains located in northwestern Arkansas, southeastern Missouri, and northeastern Oklahoma, known for its unique geology and natural beauty
low mountain region in central US

Examples

We spent our vacation hiking in the Ozark Mountains and exploring scenic trails.
The Ozark Mountains are famous for their clear rivers and abundant wildlife.
Many artists have found inspiration in the natural beauty of the Ozark Mountains.

Etymology

From the French 'Aux Arcs', referring to the people living in the area; 'mountains' from Old French 'montaine'.
