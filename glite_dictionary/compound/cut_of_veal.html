cut of veal

1. NOUN

/ kʌt əv ˈvil / (AmE), / kʌt əv ˈviːl / (BrE)
A piece or section of meat taken from a calf, used as food.
piece of meat from a calf

Examples

The chef prepared a delicious cut of veal with a rich mushroom sauce.
For tonight's dinner, we chose a tender cut of veal from the butcher.
Oven-roasting is a popular method for cooking many cuts of veal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut of beef - piece of meat from a cow
  • Synonym: veal - meat from a young calf
