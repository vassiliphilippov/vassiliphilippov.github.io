toaster oven

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊstər ˌʌvən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstə ˌʌvən / (BrE)
A small electric oven used for toasting, baking, or warming food, typically placed on a countertop.
small countertop oven for toasting food

Examples

She reheated her pizza slices in the toaster oven instead of using the microwave.
Many college students rely on a toaster oven to prepare quick meals in their dorm rooms.
The kitchen counter was crowded with a coffee maker, a blender, and a toaster oven.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toaster - appliance for making toasted bread
  • Synonym: oven - enclosed compartment for heating or cooking food
