booger sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊɡər ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʊɡə ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A humorous or joking term for an unappetizing or unpleasant meal, especially when referring to something that looks or sounds disgusting.
humorous term for an unappetizing meal
informal slang american

Examples

The kids giggled at the thought of a booger sandwich in their lunchboxes.
He referred to the cafeteria special as a booger sandwich because it looked so unappetizing.
She made a face when her brother called his creation a booger sandwich.

Synset Examples

The kids giggled at the thought of a booger sandwich.
He jokingly called his lunch a booger sandwich.
She laughed when he described his messy lunch as a booger sandwich.

Etymology

From 'booger', American slang for nasal mucus, combined with 'sandwich'.
