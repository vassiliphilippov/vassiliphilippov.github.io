charge account

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrdʒ əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account with a business that allows a customer to buy goods or services on credit and pay later
an account for buying goods on credit

Examples

She paid for the furniture on her charge account and promised to settle the bill next month.
Many department stores offer a charge account to encourage repeat customers.
After using his charge account frequently, he received a detailed monthly statement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credit account - an account allowing purchases on credit
