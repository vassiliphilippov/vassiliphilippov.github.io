safe-deposit box

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪf dɪˈpɑzɪt bɑks / (AmE), / ˈseɪf dɪˈpɒzɪt bɒks / (BrE)
a strong, secure metal box, usually kept in a bank, for storing valuable items or important documents
secure metal box for valuables (in a bank)

Examples

She keeps her family jewelry in a safe-deposit box at the bank.
Important documents like wills and deeds are often stored in a safe-deposit box.
After the move, they decided to rent a safe-deposit box for their valuables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safe - a strong container for protecting valuable items
