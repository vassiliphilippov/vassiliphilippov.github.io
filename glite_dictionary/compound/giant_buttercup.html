giant buttercup

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈbʌt̬ɚˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
a large perennial plant native to wet highland grasslands of Peru, formerly classified in the genus Ranunculus
large perennial plant of Peruvian highlands

Examples

The Andean meadows are home to the rare giant buttercup that blooms each spring.
Botanists were excited to find a patch of giant buttercup in the remote Peruvian highlands.
Unlike smaller varieties, the giant buttercup has impressive blooms and can grow over a meter tall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - a flowering plant of the genus Ranunculus, typically with yellow flowers
