east-central

1. ADJ

/ ˌistˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌiːstˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
Situated in or relating to the eastern central part of a region, country, or area.
in the eastern central part of a region

Examples

The east-central region of the country is known for its rich coal deposits.
She grew up in an east-central state, surrounded by rolling hills and rivers.
The east-central portion of the city experienced rapid growth during the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: north-central - in the northern central part of a region
  • Synonym: south-central - in the southern central part of a region
  • Synonym: west-central - in the western central part of a region
