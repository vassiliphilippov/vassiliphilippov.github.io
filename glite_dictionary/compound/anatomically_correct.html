anatomically correct

1. ADJ

/ əˌnæt̬.ə.mɪ.kli kəˈrɛkt / (AmE), / əˌnæt.ə.mɪ.kli kəˈrɛkt / (BrE)
Accurately representing the structure or appearance of human or animal anatomy, including sexual characteristics.
accurately representing real anatomy

Examples

The museum displayed an anatomically correct model of the human skeleton.
This doll is anatomically correct to help children learn about body parts.
Medical students used anatomically correct diagrams to study human physiology.

Synset Examples

The toy was designed to be anatomically correct for educational purposes.
