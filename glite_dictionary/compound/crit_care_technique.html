crit care technique

1. NOUN

/ krɪt kɛr tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / krɪt keə tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a specialized method or procedure used in critical care nursing to manage seriously ill patients
method used in critical care nursing
medical

Examples

The hospital is adopting new crit care techniques to improve patient outcomes.
She attended a workshop on advanced crit care techniques for emergency situations.
Mastering crit care techniques is essential for all intensive care nurses.

Synset Examples

They offer training for nurses in crit care techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensive care - specialized medical treatment for critically ill patients

Etymology

Shortened from 'critical care technique', with 'crit care' commonly used in medical contexts as an abbreviation for 'critical care'.
