rescue mission

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛsku ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɛskjuː ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an operation carried out to save people from danger or harm
operation to save people in danger

Examples

The rescue mission was launched at dawn after receiving a distress signal.
A joint rescue mission saved the stranded hikers from the flooded valley.
Hundreds of volunteers joined the rescue mission after the earthquake devastated the town.

Synset Examples

They launched a rescue mission after the storm hit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rescue operation - an organized activity to save people from danger
