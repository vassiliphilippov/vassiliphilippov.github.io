grass vetchling

1. NOUN

/ ɡræs ˈvɛtʃlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs ˈvɛtʃlɪŋ / (BrE)
An annual European plant of the vetch family with narrow leaves and typically red or pink flowers.
an annual European vetch with red flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with blooming grass vetchling among the wildflowers.
Botanists observed that grass vetchling thrives in moist, grassy areas across Europe.
She pressed a grass vetchling flower between the pages of her field guide.
