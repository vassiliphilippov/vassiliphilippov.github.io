junk pile

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌŋk paɪl / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk paɪl / (BrE)
an untidy pile or accumulation of discarded objects, rubbish, or unwanted material
an accumulation of discarded objects

Examples

The old car sat rusting in the junk pile behind the shed for years.
After cleaning the garage, we ended up with a huge junk pile in the driveway.
She found an antique mirror buried beneath the junk pile at the flea market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash heap - a pile of unwanted or discarded items
  • Synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of rubbish or waste
  • Degree Higher: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
