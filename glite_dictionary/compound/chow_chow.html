chow chow

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaʊ tʃaʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃaʊ tʃaʊ / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized dog with a thick double coat, curled fluffy tail, and a distinctive blue-black tongue, originating from northern China
breed of dog with thick coat and blue-black tongue

Examples

The chow chow is known for its lion-like mane and loyal temperament.
She adopted a chow chow puppy from a local animal shelter.
Many people admire the unique blue-black tongue of the chow chow.

Etymology

From Chinese 'chou', referring to various imported goods, later applied to the dog breed.
