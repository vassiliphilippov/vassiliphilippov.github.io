gala affair

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡælə əˈfɛr / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlə əˈfeə / (BrE)
an elaborate and often formal social event with entertainment, celebration, and festivities
formal festive social event

Examples

The museum hosted a gala affair to celebrate its grand reopening.
Everyone wore evening gowns and tuxedos to the annual gala affair.
Charities often raise funds by organizing a glamorous gala affair attended by celebrities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gala - a festive celebration or public entertainment

Etymology

From Italian 'gala' (festive dress) and English 'affair'.
