automobile mechanic

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtəmoʊˌbiːl məˈkænɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl mɪˈkænɪk / (BrE)
a person whose job is to repair and maintain automobiles
person who repairs automobiles

Examples

Mark has worked as an automobile mechanic for over twenty years.
When her car broke down, she took it to a trusted automobile mechanic.
The dealership is looking for an experienced automobile mechanic to join their team.
