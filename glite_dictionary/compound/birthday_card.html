birthday card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːθ.deɪ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ.deɪ kɑːd / (BrE)
a card sent or given to someone to convey birthday greetings
a greeting card for someone's birthday

Examples

She received a beautiful birthday card from her grandmother in the mail.
I forgot to buy a birthday card for my friend's party, so I made one myself.
The birthday card was filled with kind wishes from her coworkers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a card conveying a message of greeting
