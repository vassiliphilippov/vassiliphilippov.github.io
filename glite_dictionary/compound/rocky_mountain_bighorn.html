Rocky Mountain bighorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑki ˈmaʊntən ˈbɪɡˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈrɒki ˈmaʊntɪn ˈbɪɡˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a wild sheep native to the mountainous regions of western North America, noted for its massive curled horns
wild sheep of western North America

Examples

Hikers in the Rockies sometimes spot Rocky Mountain bighorn grazing on steep cliffs.
The horns of a mature Rocky Mountain bighorn can weigh up to 14 kilograms.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of the Rocky Mountain bighorn in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bighorn sheep - a North American wild sheep with large curved horns
