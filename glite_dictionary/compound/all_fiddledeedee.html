all fiddledeedee

1. NOUN

/ ɔl ˌfɪdəlˈdiː / (AmE), / ɔːl ˌfɪdəlˈdiː / (BrE)
Nonsense or something considered trivial or unimportant.
nonsense; trivial matter
archaic

Examples

He dismissed her concerns as all fiddledeedee and changed the subject immediately.
Don't believe everything you hear—most of it is all fiddledeedee anyway.
The story turned out to be all fiddledeedee, with no truth behind it.

Synset Examples

She said it was all fiddledeedee, not worth worrying about.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

Formed from the playful reduplicative word 'fiddle-dee-dee', used to express dismissal, combined with 'all' for emphasis.
