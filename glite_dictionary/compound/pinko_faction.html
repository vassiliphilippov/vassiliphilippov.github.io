pinko faction

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋkoʊ ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkəʊ ˈfækʃən / (BrE)
a group within a party or organization that has leftist or communist sympathies, often used disparagingly
group with leftist/communist sympathies
informal

Examples

After the election, the pinko faction pushed for more radical reforms within the union.
Her opponents accused her of supporting the pinko faction in parliament, though she denied it.
The party leader tried to distance himself from the pinko faction to appeal to moderate voters.

Synset Examples

The pinko faction was gaining influence in the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist faction - a group supporting communist views
  • Antonym: conservative faction - a group with conservative political views

Etymology

"Pinko" from "pink" (suggesting diluted "red", i.e., communism) + "faction".
