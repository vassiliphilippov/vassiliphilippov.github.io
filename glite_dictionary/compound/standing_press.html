standing press

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɪŋ prɛs / (AmE), / ˈstandɪŋ prɛs / (BrE)
a large printing press that applies pressure in a vertical direction, typically used in bookbinding and printing processes
vertical-action printing press

Examples

The old workshop still used a standing press for finishing handmade books.
After folding the pages, they placed them under the standing press to ensure flatness.
Restorers rely on a standing press to bind and repair antique volumes.
