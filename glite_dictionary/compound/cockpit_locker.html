cockpit locker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑk.pɪt ˈlɑ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.pɪt ˈlɒk.ə / (BrE)
(Nautical) a large storage compartment located under the seating area in a boat's cockpit.
a storage compartment under a boat's cockpit seating

Examples

The life jackets were stored safely in the cockpit locker before we set sail.
He opened the cockpit locker to grab a spare rope during the race.
It's important to keep the cockpit locker dry to protect equipment from water damage.
