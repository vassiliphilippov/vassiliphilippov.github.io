to tip your waiter

1. VERB

/ tɪp jʊr ˈweɪtɚ / (AmE), / tɪp jɔː ˈweɪtə / (BrE)
to give a gratuity to the person who serves you in a restaurant
give a gratuity to a restaurant server

Examples

Remember to tip your waiter if you are pleased with the service.
Many people wonder how much they should tip their waiter in foreign countries.
Always tip your waiter generously during the holidays.

Synset Examples

Don't forget to tip your waiter for great service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tip - to give a small sum of money as a reward for service
