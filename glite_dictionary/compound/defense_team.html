defense team

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns tim / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns tiːm / (BrE)
the lawyers and other legal professionals representing the defendant in a legal case, considered collectively
group of lawyers for the defendant

Examples

The defense team argued that the evidence was insufficient to convict their client.
His defense team included several experienced criminal attorneys and investigators.
After weeks of testimony, the defense team finally rested their case.

Synset Examples

the defense called for a mistrial

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prosecution - the legal party responsible for presenting the case against the defendant
