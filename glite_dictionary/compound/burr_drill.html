burr drill

1. NOUN

/ bɜr drɪl / (AmE), / bɜː drɪl / (BrE)
a high-speed rotary drill used by dentists to cut into or shape teeth
dental high-speed cutting drill
medical

Examples

The dentist selected a fine burr drill to carefully shape the tooth for the crown.
Modern burr drills make dental procedures faster and more precise for patients.
You may hear the sound of a burr drill during your filling appointment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dental drill - a drill used by dentists to remove tooth material
