government revenue

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈrɛvəˌnu / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈrɛvəˌnjuː / (BrE)
the income collected by a government from various sources, mainly used to fund public spending and services
income collected by a government

Examples

The decline in oil prices significantly reduced government revenue last year.
Improving tax collection is essential for increasing government revenue.
Public spending depends largely on the amount of government revenue available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax revenue - income collected by a government from taxes
