breaking off

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ ɔf / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ ɒf / (BrE)
a sudden interruption or termination of something, especially of an activity, relationship, or conversation
sudden interruption or termination

Examples

The breaking off of negotiations left both sides frustrated.
There was a sudden breaking off in the middle of his speech due to technical issues.
After a long friendship, the breaking off was unexpected and painful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interruption - a break in continuity or an instance of being interrupted
  • Antonym: continuation - the act of carrying on or resuming after a pause
