Republic of Guinea-Bissau

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌɡɪni bɪˈsaʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌɡɪni bɪˈsaʊ / (BrE)
A country in West Africa on the Atlantic coast, recognized as independent by Portugal in 1974.
country in West Africa

Examples

The Republic of Guinea-Bissau gained independence from Portugal in 1974.
She traveled to the Republic of Guinea-Bissau to conduct research on coastal ecosystems.
Many international organizations work in the Republic of Guinea-Bissau to support local development projects.

Etymology

'Guinea' from the Berber term 'Akal n-Iguinawen' (land of the black men); 'Bissau' after the capital city.
