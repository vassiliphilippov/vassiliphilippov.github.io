gymnastic apparatus

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪmˈnæstɪk əˈpærətəs / (AmE), / dʒɪmˈnastɪk əˈparətəs / (BrE)
a piece of equipment designed for use in gymnastic exercises
equipment for gymnastic exercises

Examples

The new gymnastic apparatus was installed in the school's sports hall last week.
Each gymnastic apparatus must meet strict safety standards during competitions.
She specialized in the balance beam, her favorite gymnastic apparatus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apparatus - a complex tool or piece of equipment
