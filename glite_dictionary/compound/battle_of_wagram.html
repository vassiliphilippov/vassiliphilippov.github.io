battle of Wagram

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv ˈwɑːɡrəm / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv ˈvɑːɡrəm / (BrE)
a major battle fought in 1809 during the Napoleonic Wars, in which Napoleon defeated the Austrian army near Wagram, Austria.
major 1809 Napoleonic battle in Austria

Examples

The battle of Wagram marked a turning point in Napoleon's campaign against Austria.
Historians often debate the tactics used at the battle of Wagram.
After the battle of Wagram, the political landscape of Europe changed significantly.
