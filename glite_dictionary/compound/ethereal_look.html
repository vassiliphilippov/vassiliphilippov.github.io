ethereal look

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈθɪriəl lʊk / (AmE), / ɪˈθɪəriəl lʊk / (BrE)
an appearance that is unusually delicate, light, or otherworldly
delicate, otherworldly appearance

Examples

Her dress, made of layers of tulle, gave her an ethereal look on stage.
Soft lighting in the photograph enhanced the model's ethereal look.
The misty morning lent the valley an ethereal look that made it seem magical.

Synset Examples

The gauzy fabric gave the outfit an ethereal look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethereal - extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world
