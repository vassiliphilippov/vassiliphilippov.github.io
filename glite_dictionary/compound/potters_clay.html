potter's clay

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑt̬ɚz kleɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒtəz kleɪ / (BrE)
a type of fine, iron-free clay used in making pottery and models
fine clay used in pottery and modeling

Examples

The artist preferred to use potter's clay for her delicate figurines.
Traditional vases are shaped from potter's clay before being fired in the kiln.
He ordered several bags of potter's clay for his new pottery studio.
