straw mushroom

1. NOUN

/ strɔ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (AmE), / strɔ ˈmʌʃrəm / (BrE)
A small, edible mushroom with a white cap and long stem, cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions and prized as a delicacy in Asian cuisine.
edible mushroom used in Asian cuisine

Examples

They added fresh straw mushrooms to the soup for a traditional flavor.
The market sells straw mushrooms imported from Thailand and Vietnam.
Cooks often use straw mushrooms in stir-fried vegetable dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: button mushroom - a common edible mushroom with a round white cap
