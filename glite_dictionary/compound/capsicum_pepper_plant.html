capsicum pepper plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpsɪkəm ˈpɛpər plænt / (AmE), / ˈkæpsɪkəm ˈpɛpə plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of various tropical plants of the genus Capsicum, bearing peppers used as vegetables or spices.
plant of the pepper genus Capsicum

Examples

The capsicum pepper plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires plenty of sunlight.
Farmers cultivate the capsicum pepper plant for its vibrant and flavorful peppers.
Each variety of capsicum pepper plant produces fruits of different shapes, colors, and levels of heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper - the spicy fruit of capsicum plants

Etymology

From Latin 'capsicum', the genus name for pepper plants.
