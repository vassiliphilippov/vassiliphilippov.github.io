Kavir Desert

1. PROPN

/ kɑːˈvɪr ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / kəˈvɪə ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large salt desert in north central Iran, known for its arid climate and salt flats.
salt desert in Iran

Examples

The harsh conditions of the Kavir Desert make it a challenging place for wildlife to survive.
Scientists often study the unique salt flats of the Kavir Desert to understand arid environments.
Tourists are advised to take precautions when exploring the vast Kavir Desert in Iran.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dasht-e Kavir - alternate name for the Kavir Desert
