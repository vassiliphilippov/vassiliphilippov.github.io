Federated States of Micronesia

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɛdərˌeɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv ˌmaɪkroʊˈniːʒə / (AmE), / ˌfɛdərˌeɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv ˌmɪkrəʊˈniːzɪə / (BrE)
a Pacific country consisting of over 600 islands in Micronesia, with a constitutional government in free association with the United States; gained independence in 1986
Pacific island nation in Micronesia

Examples

The Federated States of Micronesia became an independent nation in 1986.
Tourists are drawn to the tropical beauty of the Federated States of Micronesia.
The Federated States of Micronesia maintains close ties with the United States.
