dead march

1. NOUN

/ dɛd mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / dɛd mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
A slow, solemn piece of music played at funeral processions or ceremonies.
funeral procession music

Examples

The orchestra performed a dead march as the cortege approached the cemetery.
A somber dead march echoed through the hall during the memorial service.
The composer wrote a dead march to honor fallen soldiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funeral march - a slow piece of music played at funerals
