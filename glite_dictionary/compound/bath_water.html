bath water

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæθ ˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθ ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is used in a bath, especially referring to the water in a bathtub prepared for bathing or after use
water used for bathing

Examples

The children left their toys floating in the bath water after playing in the tub.
She accidentally dropped her phone into the bath water while taking a relaxing soak.
The bath water was too hot, so I added some cold water to cool it down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bath - a large container for water in which a person sits to wash their body
