Nepal trumpet flower

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈpɔl ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / nɪˈpɑːl ˈtrʌmpɪt ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
an evergreen woody climbing plant with large, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white, trumpet-shaped flowers, native to Nepal
woody climber with white trumpet-shaped flowers, Nepal

Examples

The Nepal trumpet flower is prized for its beautiful white blooms that fill gardens with a sweet scent.
Horticulturists often recommend the Nepal trumpet flower for shaded pergolas due to its vigorous climbing habit.
She photographed a cluster of Nepal trumpet flowers glistening after the rain in Katmandu.
