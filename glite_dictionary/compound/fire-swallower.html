fire-swallower

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪər ˌswɑːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌswɒlə / (BrE)
a performer who creates the illusion of swallowing fire as part of an entertainment act
performer who pretends to swallow fire

Examples

The circus hired a famous fire-swallower to amaze the audience with dangerous tricks.
Children watched in awe as the fire-swallower performed at the carnival.
After years of practice, she became a skilled fire-swallower touring with traveling shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire-eater - a performer who appears to swallow fire
