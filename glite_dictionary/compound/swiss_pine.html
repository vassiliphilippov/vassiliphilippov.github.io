Swiss pine

1. NOUN

/ swɪs paɪn / (AmE), / swɪs paɪn / (BrE)
a large European pine tree (Pinus cembra) with clusters of five needles, producing edible seeds called cembra nuts and a resinous sap
a European pine tree (Pinus cembra)

Examples

The Swiss pine is prized for its durable wood and edible seeds.
Many mountain slopes in the Alps are covered by dense Swiss pine forests.
Artisans often craft furniture from Swiss pine due to its pleasant fragrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cembra pine - another common name for the Swiss pine
