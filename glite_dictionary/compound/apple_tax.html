Apple tax

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl ˌtæks / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˌtæks / (BrE)
an informal term for the higher prices or additional costs associated with purchasing Apple products compared to similar alternatives
extra cost associated with Apple products
informal

Examples

Many tech enthusiasts begrudge paying the Apple tax when buying new devices.
The company’s accessories also come with an Apple tax that increases the total cost.
Some users try to avoid the Apple tax by purchasing third-party chargers.

Synset Examples

The Apple tax makes their products more expensive.
Consumers are wary of the Apple tax on accessories.
Many complain about the Apple tax on their devices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premium - an amount paid in addition to the standard price
  • Synonym: overcharge - an excessive or unfairly high price
