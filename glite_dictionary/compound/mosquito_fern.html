mosquito fern

1. NOUN

/ məˈskiːtoʊ fɜrn / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ fɜːn / (BrE)
A small free-floating aquatic fern native to the eastern United States and tropical America, often naturalized in other regions.
small aquatic floating fern plant

Examples

The pond was covered with a thick layer of mosquito fern during the summer.
Scientists are studying how mosquito fern can be used to improve water quality.
We removed some mosquito fern from the aquarium to give the fish more space.
