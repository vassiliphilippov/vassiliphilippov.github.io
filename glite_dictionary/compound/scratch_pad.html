scratch pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrætʃ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃ ˌpad / (BrE)
A pad of blank paper used for making quick notes, sketches, or calculations.
pad for quick notes or sketches

Examples

She grabbed her scratch pad to jot down a phone number before she forgot.
During the meeting, Mark filled his scratch pad with quick diagrams and notes.
A scratch pad is always handy when brainstorming new ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scribbling block - British term for a scratch pad
