tea-like drink

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌlaɪk drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌlaɪk drɪŋk / (BrE)
a beverage that resembles tea in appearance or taste but is not made from tea leaves
drink similar to tea, not made from tea leaves

Examples

He offered me a tea-like drink made from dried flowers rather than tea leaves.
Many people enjoy a tea-like drink in the evening because it contains no caffeine.
The café serves a variety of tea-like drinks, such as rooibos and chamomile infusions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbal tea - an infusion made from herbs, not from tea leaves
