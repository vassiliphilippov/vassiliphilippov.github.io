Kalka language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːlkə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkælkə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the standard variety of Mongolian spoken by the Khalkha people and serving as the official language of Mongolia
official language of Mongolia

Examples

The Kalka language is spoken by the majority of people in Mongolia.
Many historical documents from Mongolia are written in the Kalka language.
She is fluent in both Russian and the Kalka language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mongolian - the official language of Mongolia

Etymology

From the name of the Khalkha (Kalka) people, a major subgroup of Mongols.
