strong stomach

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the ability to tolerate unpleasant, disturbing, or nauseating things without being upset or made ill
ability to tolerate unpleasant things

Examples

You need a strong stomach to watch horror movies like that.
Nurses often develop a strong stomach from dealing with medical emergencies daily.
She proved she had a strong stomach during the dissection in biology class.

Synset Examples

He has a strong stomach for roller coasters.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: faint heart - a person with little courage or tolerance for unpleasantness
