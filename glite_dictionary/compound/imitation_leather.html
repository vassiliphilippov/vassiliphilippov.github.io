imitation leather

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪməˈteɪʃən ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃən ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
a fabric or material manufactured to resemble leather in appearance and texture
material made to look like leather

Examples

The couch was covered in imitation leather that looked remarkably real.
She prefers handbags made from imitation leather for ethical reasons.
After years of use, the imitation leather on the chair began to peel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faux leather - artificial leather used as a substitute for genuine leather
  • Antonym: leather - the tanned, tough material made from animal skin
