personal business

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
private matters or affairs that concern oneself and not others
private matters or concerns

Examples

He told his colleagues to stay out of his personal business.
She left early to take care of some personal business.
Please don't ask me about my personal business, as it's private.

Synset Examples

get his affairs in order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private affairs - matters that are personal and not public
  • Antonym: public business - matters that concern the wider community or the public
