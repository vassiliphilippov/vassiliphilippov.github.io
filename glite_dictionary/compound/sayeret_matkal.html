Sayeret Matkal

1. PROPN

/ saɪˈɛrɛt mætˈkɑl / (AmE), / saɪˈɛrɛt mætˈkæl / (BrE)
Israel's elite special forces unit specializing in counterterrorism, covert intelligence gathering, and hostage rescue missions.
Israeli special forces commando unit

Examples

The Sayeret Matkal is renowned for its high-risk operations behind enemy lines.
He was selected to join Sayeret Matkal after a rigorous training process.
News of the rescue mission credited Sayeret Matkal with saving dozens of hostages.

Etymology

Hebrew סיירת מטכ"ל (Sayeret Matkal), meaning 'General Staff Reconnaissance Unit'.
