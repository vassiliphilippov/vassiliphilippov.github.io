Sayeret Mat'kal

1. PROPN

/ saɪˌɛrɛt mɑtˈkɑl / (AmE), / saɪˌɛrɛt mætˈkæl / (BrE)
Israel's elite special forces unit specializing in counterterrorism, intelligence gathering, and hostage rescue missions.
Israeli elite commando unit

Examples

The Sayeret Mat'kal conducted a daring rescue operation behind enemy lines.
Many of Israel's top military leaders served in Sayeret Mat'kal during their careers.
The reputation of Sayeret Mat'kal attracts recruits from across the country.

Etymology

From Hebrew סיירת מטכ"ל (Sayeret Mat'kal), meaning 'General Staff Reconnaissance Unit'.
