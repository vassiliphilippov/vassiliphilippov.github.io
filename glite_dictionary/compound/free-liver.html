free-liver

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌlɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌlɪvə / (BrE)
a person who freely indulges in pleasures, especially food and drink, without restraint
someone who indulges freely in food and drink

Examples

Despite his reputation as a free-liver, he remained healthy well into old age.
The novel's main character is a notorious free-liver who hosts extravagant feasts.
Her friends called her a free-liver because she never passed up a good meal or drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hedonist - a person who pursues pleasure as the chief goal of life
  • Synonym: glutton - someone who eats and drinks excessively
  • Antonym: ascetic - a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention

Etymology

Compound of 'free' + 'liver' (in the sense of 'one who lives').
