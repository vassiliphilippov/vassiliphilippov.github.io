Northern dewberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈduːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈdjuːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a trailing bramble native to eastern North America (Rubus flagellaris) producing black edible berries similar to blackberries
trailing bramble bearing black berries

Examples

The woods behind our house are full of Northern dewberry plants in the summer.
Wildlife often snack on the berries of the Northern dewberry in eastern forests.
She went picking Northern dewberry for a homemade pie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dewberry - a trailing blackberry plant or its fruit
