senate campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnət kæmˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛnət kæmˌpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized effort by a candidate or political group to win election to a senate
effort to win election to a senate

Examples

She was the manager of his senate campaign last year.
Donations poured in from all over the country for her senate campaign.
Negative ads became a major feature of the heated senate campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: election campaign - an organized effort to win an election to public office
