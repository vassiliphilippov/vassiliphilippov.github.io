purple-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəlˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplˌrɛd / (BrE)
Having a red color tinged or mixed with purple.
red with a purple tinge

Examples

The artist chose a purple-red shade to highlight the flowers in her painting.
His tie was a striking purple-red that stood out against his gray suit.
She loved painting her nails with a deep purple-red polish for special occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
