birth defect

1. NOUN

/ bɝθ dɪˌfɛkt / (AmE), / bɜːθ dɪˌfɛkt / (BrE)
an abnormality or problem that is present in a baby at birth, affecting appearance, structure, or function
abnormality present at birth
medical

Examples

The doctors discovered a birth defect in the baby's heart shortly after delivery.
Certain vitamins can reduce the risk of birth defects during pregnancy.
She founded a charity to help children living with birth defects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congenital disorder - a disorder present from birth, either inherited or caused by environmental factors
