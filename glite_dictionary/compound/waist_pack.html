waist pack

1. NOUN

/ weɪst pæk / (AmE), / weɪst pæk / (BrE)
A small pouch, usually with a zipper, attached to a belt and worn around the waist to carry personal items.
pouch worn on a waist belt

Examples

She kept her passport and money in a waist pack while traveling through Europe.
Runners often use a waist pack to store keys and energy gels during a race.
Instead of carrying a purse, he wore a waist pack for convenience at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanny pack - a small zippered pouch worn at the waist, especially in American English
  • Synonym: belt bag - a pouch attached to a belt and worn around the waist
