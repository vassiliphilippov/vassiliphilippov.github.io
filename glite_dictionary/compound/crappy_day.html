crappy day

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræp.i deɪ / (AmE), / ˈkræp.i deɪ / (BrE)
A very bad or unpleasant day.
a very bad or unpleasant day
informal

Examples

After missing my train and losing my wallet, it truly was a crappy day.
She just wants to go home after such a crappy day at the office.
A crappy day can make even the smallest problems feel overwhelming.

Synset Examples

He had a crappy day at work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bad day - a day marked by misfortune or difficulty
  • Antonym: great day - a day marked by good fortune or positive experiences
