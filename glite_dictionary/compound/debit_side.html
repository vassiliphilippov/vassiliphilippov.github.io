debit side

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛbɪt saɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛbɪt saɪd / (BrE)
the left side of a financial statement or account, showing amounts owed or recorded as debits
left side of an account statement

Examples

The accountant checked the debit side of the ledger for any discrepancies.
Make sure all expenses are listed on the debit side of your financial statement.
At the end of the month, he balanced the debit side against the credit side.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: credit side - the right side of a financial statement, showing amounts credited

Etymology

From 'debit', from Latin debitus (something owed), and 'side', referring to the layout of financial statements.
