itchy all over

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪtʃi ɔl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃi ɔːl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
experiencing itchiness across the whole body
feeling itchy everywhere on the body

Examples

After spending time in the field, she became itchy all over from insect bites.
The allergic reaction made him itchy all over despite taking medication.
Wearing the wool sweater left me feeling itchy all over for hours.

Synset Examples

He felt itchy all over after rolling in the grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: itchy - experiencing an unpleasant skin sensation causing the desire to scratch
