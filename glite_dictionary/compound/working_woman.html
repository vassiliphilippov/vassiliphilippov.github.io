working woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkɪŋ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is employed in manual or industrial labor, especially outside the home
female manual or industrial worker

Examples

The factory hired several working women to help meet production demands.
As a working woman, she takes pride in providing for her family.
Labor unions have long advocated for the rights of the working woman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: working man - a man who performs manual or industrial labor
  • Antonym: housewife - a married woman whose main occupation is caring for her family and home
