cup and saucer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌp ənd ˈsɔsɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌp ənd ˈsɔːsə / (BrE)
a European biennial plant (Campanula medium) widely cultivated for its blue, violet, or white bell-shaped flowers
European biennial plant with showy flowers

Examples

The cup and saucer brightened the garden with its beautiful blue blooms.
She planted several cup and saucer seedlings along the fence for a splash of color.
Many gardeners prize the cup and saucer for its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers.
