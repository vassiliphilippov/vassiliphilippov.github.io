eggshake

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌʃeɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌʃeɪk / (BrE)
A milkshake made with the addition of raw or cooked egg, often for extra richness or protein.
milkshake with added egg

Examples

He ordered an eggshake at the diner to go with his burger.
Adding honey to your eggshake can make it taste even better.
Some athletes drink an eggshake after workouts for extra protein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkshake - a sweet drink made by mixing milk, ice cream, and flavorings
