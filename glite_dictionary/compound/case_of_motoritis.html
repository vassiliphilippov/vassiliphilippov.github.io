case of motoritis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪs əv ˌmoʊ.təˈraɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪs əv ˌməʊ.təˈraɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
a state of tiredness resulting from prolonged periods of driving
fatigue from long driving

Examples

After driving six hours without a break, I developed a case of motoritis.
Many long-distance truckers have experienced a case of motoritis after a full day's drive.
She warned him to take breaks, or he would end up with a case of motoritis.

Synset Examples

After hours of driving, I felt a case of motoritis coming on.
