suicide disease

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɪˌsaɪd dɪˈziːz / (AmE), / ˈsuːɪsʌɪd dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
an informal term for trigeminal neuralgia, a debilitating nerve disorder characterized by intense facial pain that may lead sufferers to suicidal thoughts or actions
informal term for painful nerve disorder (trigeminal neuralgia)
informal medical

Examples

She was diagnosed with suicide disease after months of suffering from excruciating facial pain.
Doctors now recognize suicide disease as one of the most severe forms of nerve pain.
Many patients with suicide disease struggle to find effective treatment for their symptoms.

Synset Examples

He was diagnosed with a suicide disease that devastated his mental health.
The community rallied to support those suffering from the suicide disease.
Awareness campaigns are crucial for understanding the suicide disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trigeminal neuralgia - a chronic pain condition affecting the trigeminal nerve in the face
