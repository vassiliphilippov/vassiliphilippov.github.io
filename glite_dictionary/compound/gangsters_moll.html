gangster's moll

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæŋstərz mɑl / (AmE), / ˈɡæŋstəz mɒl / (BrE)
the female companion or girlfriend of a gangster, especially in the context of organized crime
girlfriend of a gangster

Examples

In the film, the gangster's moll wore glamorous dresses and stood by her dangerous partner.
Rumors spread quickly about the identity of the gangster's moll after the infamous bank robbery.
The police suspected the gangster's moll knew more about the crime than she admitted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gun moll - female companion of a gangster
  • Synonym: gangster - member of an organized criminal group

Etymology

From 'gangster' (criminal member of a gang) + 'moll,' from Yiddish *mol* (girl), originally from Latin *mollis* (soft).
