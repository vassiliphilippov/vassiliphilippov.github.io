Musgoi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsɡɔɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsɡɔɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in regions south of Lake Chad in Cameroon and neighboring areas
Chadic language of Cameroon region

Examples

The Musgoi language is spoken by thousands of people in northern Cameroon.
She conducted linguistic research on the Musgoi language for her dissertation.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Musgoi language among younger generations.
