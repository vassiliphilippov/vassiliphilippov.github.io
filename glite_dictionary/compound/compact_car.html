compact car

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmˌpækt kɑr / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpækt kɑː / (BrE)
a small, fuel-efficient automobile, typically designed for use in cities and offering seating for up to five passengers
small, economical automobile

Examples

She decided to buy a compact car because it was easier to park in the city.
Many students opt for a compact car due to its affordable price and efficiency.
The rental agency offered a variety of compact cars for customers traveling alone or in small groups.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: subcompact car - an even smaller and more economical car than a compact car
  • Degree Higher: mid-size car - a car larger than a compact, but smaller than a full-size
