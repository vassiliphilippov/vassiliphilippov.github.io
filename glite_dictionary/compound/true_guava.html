true guava

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈɡwɑvə / (AmE), / truː ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree, widely grown in warm regions for its sweet, round yellow fruit.
tropical tree with edible yellow fruit

Examples

The orchard includes a row of true guava trees, which bear fruit in early summer.
She enjoyed the sweet flavor of true guava in her morning smoothie.
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate true guava for commercial purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guava - the fruit of the guava tree; also the tree itself
