heartfelt obituary

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌfɛlt oʊˈbɪtʃuˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtfɛlt əʊˈbɪtjʊəri / (BrE)
an obituary written or expressed with genuine emotion and sincerity
genuinely emotional obituary

Examples

The newspaper published a heartfelt obituary that moved many readers to tears.
After her father's passing, Maria wrote a heartfelt obituary celebrating his generosity and achievements.
The family appreciated the heartfelt obituary that captured the essence of their loved one so honestly.

Synset Examples

He was moved by the heartfelt obituary in the paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obituary - notice of a death, often with a brief biography
