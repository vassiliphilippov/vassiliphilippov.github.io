side order

1. NOUN

/ saɪd ˈɔɹdɚ / (AmE), / saɪd ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a small dish served in addition to the main course of a meal, usually as an accompaniment
dish served with a main course

Examples

She ordered a side order of fries with her burger.
Would you like a side order of vegetables with your steak?
The restaurant offers a side order of salad with every meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: side dish - a food item served as an accompaniment to the main course

2. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪd ˌɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd ˌɔːdə / (BrE)
An additional dish ordered and served alongside a main course.
additional dish with a main course

Examples

He requested a side order of garlic bread with his pasta.
Would you like a side order of salad with your steak?
I usually get a side order of fries when I order a burger.

Synset Examples
