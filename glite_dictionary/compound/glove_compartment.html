glove compartment

1. NOUN

/ ɡlʌv kəmˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ɡlʌv kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a small storage compartment in the dashboard of a car, typically used for storing gloves, documents, and other small items
dashboard storage compartment in a car

Examples

She opened the glove compartment to retrieve her sunglasses before driving off.
The registration papers are usually kept in the glove compartment of the car.
After parking, he checked the glove compartment to make sure nothing valuable was left inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glovebox - another term for glove compartment

Etymology

Refers to its original purpose of storing driving gloves; 'glove' + 'compartment'.
