dumb bomb

1. NOUN

/ dʌm bɑm / (AmE), / dʌm bɒm / (BrE)
an aerial bomb that is not guided and relies on gravity to reach its target
unguided aerial bomb

Examples

The air force used dumb bombs in the attack due to their lower cost.
Unlike a smart bomb, a dumb bomb lacks any guidance system.
Pilots must be more accurate when dropping dumb bombs for effective targeting.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smart bomb - a guided bomb that uses built-in technology to hit a target precisely
