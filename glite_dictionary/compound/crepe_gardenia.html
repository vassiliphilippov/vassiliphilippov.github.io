crepe gardenia

1. NOUN

/ kreɪp ɡɑrˈdiːniə / (AmE), / kreɪp ɡɑːˈdiːniə / (BrE)
a tropical shrub native to northern India and Thailand, known for its glossy leaves and fragrant, crimped white flowers that bloom at night
tropical shrub with fragrant crimped flowers

Examples

The crepe gardenia is prized for its intensely fragrant nocturnal blossoms.
Gardeners in subtropical regions often plant the crepe gardenia for its decorative foliage and flowers.
At dusk, the crepe gardenia fills the garden with a sweet scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardenia - any of various shrubs and trees in the genus Gardenia, noted for their fragrant white flowers
