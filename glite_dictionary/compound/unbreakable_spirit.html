unbreakable spirit

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈbreɪkəbəl ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbreɪkəbl ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a person's strong resilience and unwavering determination, especially when facing difficulties or adversity
strong resilience and determination

Examples

Despite repeated setbacks, her unbreakable spirit inspired everyone around her.
The team showed an unbreakable spirit in the face of fierce competition.
His unbreakable spirit helped him overcome every obstacle in his path.

Synset Examples

She wore an unbreakable spirit despite the challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resilience - the ability to recover quickly from difficulties
  • Synonym: determination - firmness of purpose; resolve
