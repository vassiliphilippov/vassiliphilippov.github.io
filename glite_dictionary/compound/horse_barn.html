horse barn

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs bɑrn / (AmE), / hɔːs bɑːn / (BrE)
a building on a farm used for housing horses or other livestock
farm building for horses

Examples

The farm had a large horse barn at the end of the lane.
After the storm, they checked the horse barn to make sure all the animals were safe.
She spent hours each weekend cleaning stalls in the old horse barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stable - a building in which horses are kept
