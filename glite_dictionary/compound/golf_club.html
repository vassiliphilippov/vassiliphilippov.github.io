golf club

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf klʌb / (AmE), / ɡɒlf klʌb / (BrE)
A long, thin implement with a handle and a heavy head, used by golfers to hit the ball in the game of golf.
implement used to hit a golf ball

Examples

He bought a new golf club to improve his game.
Each golfer carries several golf clubs for different types of shots.
She accidentally left her favorite golf club at the course after the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: club - a heavy stick or bat used for hitting a ball in games
