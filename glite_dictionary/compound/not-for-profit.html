not-for-profit

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑt fər ˈprɑfɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒt fə ˈprɒfɪt / (BrE)
An organization established for purposes other than making a profit, often to further a social cause or community goal.
organization not aimed at making profit

Examples

She established a not-for-profit dedicated to providing educational resources for underserved communities.
Many health care clinics operate as not-for-profit organizations, reinvesting any surplus into services.
He volunteers at a local not-for-profit that supports animal welfare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonprofit - organization not operated for profit
