Member of Parliament

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛmbər əv ˈpɑrləmənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛmbə əv ˈpɑːləmənt / (BrE)
an elected member of the British Parliament, especially the House of Commons
elected representative in the British Parliament
british

Examples

The Member of Parliament for our district will be addressing the public tonight.
Several Members of Parliament have voiced concerns about the proposed legislation.
She served as a Member of Parliament for more than twenty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MP - abbreviation for Member of Parliament
  • Antonym: senator - a member of the senate in various countries, especially the US
