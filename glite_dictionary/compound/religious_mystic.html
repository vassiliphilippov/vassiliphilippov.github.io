religious mystic

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈmɪstɪk / (BrE)
a person who seeks to experience direct spiritual connection with a divine or transcendent reality, often believing in truths beyond ordinary human understanding
someone seeking direct spiritual connection beyond ordinary understanding

Examples

The writings of a religious mystic often focus on personal experiences of divine revelation.
Many people looked to the religious mystic for spiritual guidance and enlightenment.
Throughout history, every major tradition has produced at least one notable religious mystic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mystic - a person who seeks unity with the divine or spiritual truths beyond ordinary understanding
  • Antonym: skeptic - a person inclined to doubt or question accepted beliefs
