paytriot shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌtraɪət ʃɝːt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌtrʌɪət ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A T-shirt or garment displaying patriotic slogans or symbols, often worn to express patriotism in a way that is intentionally ironic or critical of commercialized patriotism.
ironic patriotic shirt, often critical
informal

Examples

He wore his paytriot shirt with a giant eagle on the front, even though he meant it sarcastically.
The group handed out paytriot shirts mocking over-the-top displays of nationalism at the event.
Her paytriot shirt featured a flag and a snarky message about buying freedom.

Synset Examples

He proudly wore his paytriot shirt to the rally.

Etymology

Blend of 'patriot' and 'pay', alluding to 'patriot shirt' and the commercialization of patriotism.
