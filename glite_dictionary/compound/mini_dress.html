mini dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪni ˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɪni ˌdrɛs / (BrE)
A very short dress, typically ending well above the knees.
very short dress above knees

Examples

She wore a sparkling mini dress to the party, catching everyone's eye.
The new fashion collection features a bold red mini dress perfect for summer.
A mini dress can be paired with tights and boots for a chic look in cooler weather.

Synset Examples

A mini dress is not appropriate for a formal meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miniskirt - a very short skirt
  • Degree Lower: midi dress - a dress with a hemline at mid-calf
  • Degree Lower: maxi dress - a dress with an ankle-length hem

Etymology

From 'mini', meaning small or short, and 'dress'. 'Mini' became popular in the 1960s to describe the mini skirt.
