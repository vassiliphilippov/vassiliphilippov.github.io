genus Parophrys

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs pəˈrɑːfrɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs pəˈrɒfrɪs / (BrE)
A genus of flatfish in the family Soleidae, including the English sole (Parophrys).
genus of flatfish including the English sole

Examples

The genus Parophrys is best known for the English sole, a common Pacific flatfish.
Researchers studied the morphological features of fish within the genus Parophrys.
The genus Parophrys contains only a single recognized species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English sole - a species of flatfish in the genus Parophrys

Etymology

From Neo-Latin 'Parophrys', the formal biological genus name, from Greek 'para' (beside) + 'ophrys' (brow, eyebrow).
