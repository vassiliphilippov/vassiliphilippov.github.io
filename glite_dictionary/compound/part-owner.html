part-owner

1. NOUN

/ pɑrt ˈoʊnɚ / (AmE), / pɑːt ˈəʊnə / (BrE)
A person who owns something jointly with one or more other people.
person who owns something jointly

Examples

She is a part-owner of the company and helps make important decisions.
After years of investing, he finally became a part-owner of a local restaurant.
Both sisters are part-owners of the family farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: co-owner - a person who owns something jointly with another
