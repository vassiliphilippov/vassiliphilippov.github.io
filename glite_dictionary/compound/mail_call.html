mail call

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪl kɔl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl kɔːl / (BrE)
an occasion, especially in military or institutional settings, when the names of people receiving mail are called out and mail is distributed
occasion when mail is distributed

Examples

Every evening at the army barracks, the soldiers eagerly awaited mail call.
She felt a rush of excitement whenever her name was announced during mail call.
At prison mail call, inmates gathered in hopes of hearing from loved ones.
