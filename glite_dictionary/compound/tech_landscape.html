tech landscape

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈlændskeɪp / (AmE), / tɛk ˈlændskeɪp / (BrE)
the current state and structure of the technology industry, including trends, companies, and innovations
the state of the technology industry

Examples

The rapid rise of artificial intelligence is reshaping the tech landscape worldwide.
Startups are emerging everywhere, making the tech landscape more competitive than ever.
Investors are closely watching how regulation will affect the tech landscape in coming years.

Synset Examples

Microhoo's merger changed the tech landscape significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology sector - businesses and organizations involved in technology industries
  • Synonym: business landscape - the overall state of the business environment
