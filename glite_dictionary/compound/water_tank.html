water tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə tæŋk / (BrE)
a container for holding the water used to flush a toilet
container for flushing water in toilet

Examples

The water tank behind the toilet was leaking, so the plumber replaced it.
She lifted the lid of the water tank to check the flushing mechanism when the toilet wouldn’t work.
Older homes may have a separate water tank installed above the toilet for flushing.
