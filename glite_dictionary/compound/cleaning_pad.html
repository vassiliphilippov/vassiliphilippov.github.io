cleaning pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ pæd / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ pad / (BrE)
a small flat piece of material used for cleaning surfaces by rubbing
tool for cleaning surfaces

Examples

Please use a cleaning pad to wipe down the stove before you leave.
She bought a new cleaning pad to scrub the bathroom tiles more effectively.
After each use, he rinsed the cleaning pad to keep it fresh for next time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scouring pad - a rough pad for scrubbing stubborn dirt or stains
