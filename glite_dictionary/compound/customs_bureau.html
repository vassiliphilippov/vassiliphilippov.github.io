Customs Bureau

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəmz bjʊˈroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmz bjʊəˈrəʊ / (BrE)
a government agency responsible for enforcing import tariffs and customs regulations
agency enforcing import tariffs

Examples

The Customs Bureau announced new regulations on imported electronics this year.
Every shipment entering the country is inspected by the Customs Bureau.
Officers from the Customs Bureau seized several illegal items during the border check.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customs - the official department that collects tariffs on imported goods
  • Synonym: border control - governmental measures that regulate or restrict the flow of people and goods across borders
