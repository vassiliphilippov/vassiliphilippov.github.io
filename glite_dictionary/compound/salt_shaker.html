salt shaker

1. NOUN

/ sɔlt ˈʃeɪkər / (AmE), / sɒlt ˈʃeɪkə / (BrE)
A container with a perforated top, used for sprinkling salt onto food.
container for sprinkling salt

Examples

Please pass me the salt shaker so I can season my food.
The salt shaker on the table was nearly empty after dinner.
She accidentally dropped the salt shaker and spilled salt everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper shaker - a container with a perforated top for sprinkling pepper
