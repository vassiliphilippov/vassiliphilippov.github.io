coffee cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˌkrim / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌkriːm / (BrE)
cream with at least 18% butterfat, typically used to lighten coffee
cream for coffee with 18% butterfat

Examples

She added a splash of coffee cream to her morning mug for extra richness.
The recipe called for coffee cream instead of milk to achieve a smoother texture.
In some countries, coffee cream is sold alongside milk at the supermarket.

Synset Examples

in England they call light cream ‘single cream’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: single cream - British term for light cream with similar fat content
  • Degree Higher: heavy cream - cream with higher fat content (about 36%)
  • Degree Lower: half-and-half - American cream product with lower fat content (10-12%)
