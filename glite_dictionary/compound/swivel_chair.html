swivel chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪvəl tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈswɪvəl tʃeə / (BrE)
a chair with a seat that can turn freely around a central point on its base
a rotating chair

Examples

She adjusted her swivel chair before starting work at the computer.
The manager spun around in his swivel chair to face the window.
Children love to play with the spinning seat of a swivel chair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office chair - a chair designed for use at a desk in an office, often with wheels and height adjustment
