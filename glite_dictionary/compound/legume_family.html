legume family

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛɡ.jum ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡ.juːm ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a large family of plants (Fabaceae) including trees, shrubs, vines, and herbs that bear bean pods, such as peas and beans
family of plants bearing bean pods (Fabaceae)

Examples

The legume family includes beans, peas, lentils, and hundreds of other species.
Members of the legume family are valued for their ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
Acacia trees, which belong to the legume family, are common in tropical regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Fabaceae - the botanical family comprising legumes
  • Synonym: pea family - another name for the legume family
