too whorey

1. ADJ

/ tu ˈhɔːri / (AmE), / tuː ˈhɔːri / (BrE)
(Of clothing or appearance) excessively revealing or provocative in a way considered indecent or inappropriate, often used in a derogatory or offensive manner.
excessively revealing or provocative (offensive/vulgar)
offensive vulgar informal

Examples

Some people thought her dress was too whorey for the school dance.
He made a rude comment about her outfit being too whorey for a business meeting.
She worried her new top looked too whorey, so she chose something more modest.

Synset Examples

Her outfit was too whorey for the family gathering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slutty - (of clothing or behavior) sexually provocative or promiscuous, usually considered offensive
  • Degree Lower: provocative - intended to arouse sexual interest or attention
