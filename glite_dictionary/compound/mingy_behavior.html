mingy behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪŋi bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋi bɪˈheɪviə / (BrE)
Actions or conduct showing stinginess or reluctance to share or spend resources.
actions showing stinginess

Examples

Everyone noticed her mingy behavior when she refused to contribute to the group gift.
His mingy behavior at dinner left the waitstaff with a disappointingly small tip.
People were offended by his mingy behavior, especially when he avoided chipping in for shared expenses.

Synset Examples

Her mingy behavior at the potluck disappointed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stinginess - the quality of being unwilling to spend or give; meanness
  • Antonym: generous behavior - actions showing readiness to give or share; kindness or liberality
