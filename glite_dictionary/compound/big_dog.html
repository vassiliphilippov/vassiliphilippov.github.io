big dog

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ dɔɡ / (AmE), / bɪɡ dɒɡ / (BrE)
an important or influential person; someone with prominence or authority in a particular group or sphere
influential or prominent person
informal

Examples

After the promotion, he became the big dog in the company.
They want to prove themselves against the big dog in the industry.
At the meeting, the big dog made all the important decisions.

Synset Examples
