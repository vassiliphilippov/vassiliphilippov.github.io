chocolate cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət keɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət keɪk / (BrE)
A cake with chocolate as a main ingredient, often baked with cocoa or melted chocolate and served as a dessert.
a cake made with chocolate

Examples

I baked a chocolate cake for her birthday party last Saturday.
He ordered a slice of chocolate cake with extra whipped cream at the café.
Nothing beats a rich chocolate cake when you're craving a sweet treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake - a sweet baked food made from flour, sugar, eggs, and other ingredients
