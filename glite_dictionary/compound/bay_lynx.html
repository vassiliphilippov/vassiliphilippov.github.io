bay lynx

1. NOUN

/ beɪ lɪŋks / (AmE), / beɪ lɪŋks / (BrE)
a small wild cat of North America, also known as the bobcat, distinguished by its short tail and spotted coat
North American wild cat, bobcat

Examples

The bay lynx is well adapted to woodland and semi-desert environments across the continent.
A bay lynx was spotted near the edge of the forest, hunting for small mammals.
Unlike the Canadian lynx, the bay lynx does not depend solely on snowshoe hares for food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobcat - a North American wild cat with a short tail and spotted fur
  • Antonym: Canadian lynx - a wild cat of northern North America with longer legs and less spotting than the bobcat
