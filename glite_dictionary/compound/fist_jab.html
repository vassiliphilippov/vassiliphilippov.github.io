fist jab

1. NOUN

/ fɪst dʒæb / (AmE), / fɪst dʒæb / (BrE)
A gesture of greeting, celebration, or solidarity made by briefly touching the knuckles of two closed fists together.
gesture involving touching knuckles in greeting

Examples

After the win, the two players exchanged a quick fist jab and smiled.
As a sign of support, she gave her friend a fist jab before the exam.
Instead of shaking hands, they greeted each other with a fist jab.

Synset Examples

The photo of the terrorist fist jab became a viral meme.
They shared a friendly terrorist fist jab after the match.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fist bump - A gesture where two people touch fists lightly together, similar in meaning to 'fist jab'
  • Synonym: high five - A gesture involving two people slapping open palms together in celebration or greeting
