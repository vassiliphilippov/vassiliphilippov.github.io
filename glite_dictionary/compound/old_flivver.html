old flivver

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈflɪvɚ / (AmE), / əʊld ˈflɪvə / (BrE)
An old, often unreliable car, especially one considered out-of-date or in poor condition.
old, unreliable car
informal american

Examples

My grandfather still drives his old flivver on country roads every weekend.
We barely made it to the concert in that old flivver of yours.
The mechanic laughed when he saw the old flivver sputtering into the garage.

Synset Examples

That old flivver is still running after all these years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated car

Etymology

'Flivver' is an American slang term from the early 20th century, possibly of imitative origin, referring to a cheap or poorly made car.
