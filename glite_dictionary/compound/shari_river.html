Shari River

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃɑːri ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːri ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in central Africa that flows northwest into Lake Chad.
a river in Africa flowing into Lake Chad

Examples

The Shari River forms part of the border between Chad and Cameroon.
Seasonal floods in the Shari River basin support local agriculture and fishing.
Many settlements have developed along the banks of the Shari River over the centuries.
