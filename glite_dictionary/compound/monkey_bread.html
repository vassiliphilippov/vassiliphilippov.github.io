monkey bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki brɛd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki brɛd / (BrE)
the large, gourd-like fruit of the African baobab tree, with an edible, pulpy interior
African fruit with edible pulp

Examples

The villagers gathered monkey bread from the baobab trees for their evening meal.
The tangy flavor of monkey bread is popular in many West African desserts.
Researchers studied the nutritional value of monkey bread in the local diet.
