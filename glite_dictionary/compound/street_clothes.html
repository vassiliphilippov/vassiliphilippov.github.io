street clothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈstriːt kloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈstriːt kləʊðz / (BrE)
ordinary clothes worn in public, as opposed to uniforms, costumes, or sportswear
ordinary clothes for public wear

Examples

After the game, the players changed back into their street clothes before heading home.
She arrived in street clothes instead of her work uniform for the party.
The actors wore street clothes during the rehearsal to keep things casual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: costume - a set of clothes worn for a particular occasion or purpose
  • Antonym: uniform - a special set of clothes worn by members of an organization
