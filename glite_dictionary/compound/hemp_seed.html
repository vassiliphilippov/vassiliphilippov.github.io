hemp seed

1. NOUN

/ hɛmp siːd / (AmE), / hɛmp siːd / (BrE)
the edible seed of the hemp (cannabis) plant, used as a food or ingredient in various products
edible seed of the hemp plant

Examples

Many health food stores now sell hemp seed as a source of protein and omega fatty acids.
He likes to sprinkle hemp seed on his morning oatmeal for extra nutrition.
The recipe called for ground hemp seed to add a nutty flavor to the bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemp - fiber plant, source of cannabis products
