pine box

1. NOUN

/ paɪn bɑks / (AmE), / paɪn bɒks / (BrE)
A simple coffin, especially one made from pine wood.
simple coffin made of pine

Examples

The old man requested to be laid to rest in a plain pine box.
In many movies, outlaws end up in a pine box after a final showdown.
The carpenter built a simple pine box for the funeral.

Synset Examples

They buried the treasure in a sturdy pine box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffin - A box or container for a dead body; a casket
