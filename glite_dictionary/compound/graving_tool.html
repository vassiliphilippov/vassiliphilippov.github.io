graving tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪvɪŋ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvɪŋ tuːl / (BrE)
A tool used for engraving designs or patterns on a surface, typically metal, wood, or stone.
tool for engraving designs

Examples

The artist used a finely honed graving tool to create intricate details on the silver plate.
She inherited her grandfather's collection of graving tools for engraving wood and metal.
Careful control of the graving tool is essential for producing clean lines in the design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burin - a steel cutting tool used in engraving and carving

Etymology

From 'grave' (to carve, engrave) + 'tool'.
