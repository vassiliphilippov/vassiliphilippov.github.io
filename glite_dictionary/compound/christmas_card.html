Christmas card

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs kɑːd / (BrE)
a decorative card sent as a greeting at Christmas, typically featuring holiday scenes or messages
decorative card sent at Christmas

Examples

Each December, I send a Christmas card to all my relatives and friends.
We received a beautiful Christmas card with a snowy village on the front.
She handmade every Christmas card she gave out last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a card sent to convey good wishes on a special occasion
