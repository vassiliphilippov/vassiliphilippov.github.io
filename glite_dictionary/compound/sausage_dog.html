sausage dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɪdʒ dɒɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ dɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a dachshund, a breed of dog with a long body and short legs
informal term for a dachshund
informal

Examples

As a child, I always wanted a sausage dog as a pet.
The sausage dog waddled across the park, attracting smiles from everyone.
Her grandparents' sausage dog sleeps curled up on the sofa every night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dachshund - a breed of dog with a long body and short legs
