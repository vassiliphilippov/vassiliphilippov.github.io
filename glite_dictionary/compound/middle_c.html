middle C

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪdəl ˈsi / (AmE), / ˌmɪdəl ˈsiː / (BrE)
the musical note C located near the center of a piano keyboard, corresponding to 261.63 hertz, and used as a reference point in music.
central note of the piano keyboard

Examples

To start the scale, place your thumb on middle C and play upwards.
The piano tuner struck middle C to check the instrument's pitch.
She learned to read sheet music by first finding middle C on the staff.
