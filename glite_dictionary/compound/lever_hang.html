lever hang

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːvər hæŋ / (AmE), / ˈliːvə hæŋ / (BrE)
A gymnastic position performed on the rings, where the body is held stationary and horizontal to the ground.
horizontal hold on rings in gymnastics

Examples

The gymnast held a perfect lever hang during his routine.
Practicing the lever hang is essential for building core strength in gymnastics.
She struggled at first to master the lever hang on the rings but improved with time.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: planche - an advanced gymnastic position with the body parallel to the ground, supported by the hands
