Gaza Strip

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑzə strɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːzə strɪp / (BrE)
a coastal region in the Middle East, situated at the southeastern edge of the Mediterranean Sea, bordering Israel and Egypt
territory bordering Israel and Egypt

Examples

The Gaza Strip has been at the center of many political conflicts in recent decades.
Many humanitarian organizations operate within the Gaza Strip to assist the local population.
Border crossings between Israel and the Gaza Strip are often tightly controlled.

Synset Examples

he is a Palestinian from Gaza

Etymology

From the name of the city Gaza plus 'strip', describing its narrow territorial shape.
