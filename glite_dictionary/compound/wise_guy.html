wise guy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪz ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a man who makes clever, sarcastic, or impudent remarks, often trying to appear smart or witty
man who makes sarcastic or impudent remarks
informal

Examples

Stop being a wise guy and take this seriously for once.
The teacher quickly silenced the wise guy at the back of the classroom.
Every group has a wise guy who always cracks jokes during meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart aleck - a person who behaves as if they know everything or are cleverer than others

2. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who makes sarcastic or clever remarks, often in an irritating or annoying way
person making sarcastic or clever remarks
informal

Examples

Don't be a wise guy; just help me with the project.
Every group has at least one wise guy who always has a comeback.
He acted like a wise guy in the meeting, interrupting with sarcastic jokes.

Synset Examples

Stop being a wise guy and just answer the question.
The wise guy in the group always cracks jokes.
He thinks he's a wise guy, but he makes silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritating because they act as if they know everything
  • Synonym: know-it-all - a person who behaves as if they know everything
