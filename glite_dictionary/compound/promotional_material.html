promotional material

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈmoʊʃənəl məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃənəl məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
any item or message created to advertise or promote a product, service, event, or idea.
material for advertising or promotion

Examples

The company distributed promotional material at the trade show to attract new clients.
Most of the promotional material featured the new product’s innovative design.
Please recycle any unwanted promotional material you receive in the mail.

Synset Examples

the packaging of new ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising material - items or messages specifically intended to advertise
  • Antonym: informational material - materials aimed at informing rather than promoting
