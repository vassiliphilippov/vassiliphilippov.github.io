cedar nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːdər nʌt / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə nʌt / (BrE)
the edible seed of certain pine species, especially the Swiss pine, often used in cooking or as a snack
edible seed of Swiss pine

Examples

The chef added toasted cedar nuts to the salad for a delicious crunch.
I bought a bag of cedar nuts at the health food market.
Cedar nuts are high in healthy fats and a good source of protein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine nut - the edible seed of any tree in the genus Pinus
