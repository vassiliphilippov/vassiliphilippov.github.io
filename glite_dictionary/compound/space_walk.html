space walk

1. NOUN

/ speɪs wɔk / (AmE), / speɪs wɔːk / (BrE)
an activity in which an astronaut leaves the confines of a spacecraft and works outside in space
activity performed by an astronaut outside a spacecraft

Examples

The astronauts prepared for their space walk by carefully checking their suits.
She completed her first space walk during the International Space Station mission.
A televised space walk gave people on Earth a live view of the repairs being made.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extravehicular activity - activity performed by an astronaut outside a spacecraft
