cure for cancer

1. NOUN

/ kjʊr fər ˈkænsɚ / (AmE), / kjʊə fə ˈkænsə / (BrE)
a treatment or remedy that completely eliminates cancer from the body
treatment that eliminates cancer

Examples

Many scientists are dedicating their lives to discovering a cure for cancer.
If a universal cure for cancer were found, it would change the world forever.
People donate millions each year to support research aimed at a cure for cancer.

Synset Examples

She hopes to find a cure for cancer in her lifetime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cure - a treatment that restores health
  • Synonym: cancer - a serious disease caused by uncontrolled cell growth
