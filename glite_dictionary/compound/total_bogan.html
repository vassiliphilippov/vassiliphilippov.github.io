total bogan

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈboʊ.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈbəʊ.ɡən / (BrE)
An unsophisticated or unrefined person, especially one stereotyped as lacking in social graces or education (Austral., N.Z. informal).
unsophisticated or unrefined person (Austral., N.Z. slang)
informal slang

Examples

Some people at the party acted like a total bogan and embarrassed their friends.
You don't have to dress fancy, but don't be a total bogan at the restaurant.
When she called him a total bogan, he just laughed it off.

Synset Examples

She laughed and said he was a total bogan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bogan - an unsophisticated or uncultured person (Austral., N.Z. slang)
  • Antonym: snob - a person with an exaggerated respect for high social position or wealth; an antonym in context

Etymology

From Australian/New Zealand slang "bogan" (unsophisticated person); "total" used for emphasis.
