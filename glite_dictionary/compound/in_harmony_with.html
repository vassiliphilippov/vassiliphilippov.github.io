in harmony with

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈhɑːrməni wɪθ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈhɑːməni wɪð / (BrE)
in agreement or consistent with someone or something
in agreement or alignment with

Examples

Her beliefs are always in harmony with her actions.
The architect designed the building in harmony with its natural surroundings.
Their proposal was drafted in harmony with the new policies.

Synset Examples

They made decisions in harmony with the Lord's teachings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in accordance with - in a way that agrees with a rule or request
