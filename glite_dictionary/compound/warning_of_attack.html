warning of attack

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrnɪŋ əv əˈtæk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ əv əˈtæk / (BrE)
an official notification given to policy-makers that an enemy is about to launch an attack
official notification of impending attack

Examples

The government issued a warning of attack after intercepting enemy communications.
Military leaders rely on a warning of attack to make rapid strategic decisions.
A false warning of attack could trigger unnecessary mobilization of national forces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alert - official warning of possible danger or attack
  • Synonym: threat - an indication of possible harm or danger
