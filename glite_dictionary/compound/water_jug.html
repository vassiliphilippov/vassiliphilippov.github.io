water jug

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ər dʒʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə dʒʌɡ / (BrE)
A jug used for holding or serving water.
jug for holding water

Examples

She poured fresh lemonade from the water jug into our glasses.
Please refill the water jug before the meeting starts.
The ceramic water jug sat in the middle of the kitchen table.

Related Terms
