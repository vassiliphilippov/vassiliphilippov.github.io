snake fence

1. NOUN

/ sneɪk fɛns / (AmE), / sneɪk fɛns / (BrE)
a rail fence constructed in a zigzag pattern with interlocking rails, common in rural North America
zigzag rail fence

Examples

The old farmhouse was surrounded by a snake fence that had weathered many winters.
They decided to build a snake fence around their property using local timber.
A traditional snake fence adds rustic charm to the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: split-rail fence - a type of fence made with wooden rails, often zigzagged; another term for a snake fence

Etymology

Named for its zigzag pattern, resembling the movement of a snake.
