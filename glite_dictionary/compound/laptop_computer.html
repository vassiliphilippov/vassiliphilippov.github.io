laptop computer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæp.tɑp kəmˈpju.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈlæp.tɒp kəmˈpjuː.tə / (BrE)
A portable personal computer small and light enough to be used on one's lap.
portable personal computer

Examples

She bought a new laptop computer for work so she could travel easily.
Many students take notes in class using their laptop computer.
The laptop computer fits easily into a backpack, making it ideal for commuting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laptop - portable personal computer

Etymology

From 'laptop' (usable on the lap) + 'computer'.
