ten-day fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛn deɪ fɝːn / (AmE), / ˈtɛn deɪ fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical Southern Hemisphere fern with leathery, pinnatifid fronds, widely distributed in its habitat
widely distributed tropical fern

Examples

During our hike, we saw a lush patch of ten-day fern growing by the riverbank.
The leathery fronds of the ten-day fern make it easy to identify among other ferns.
Botanists often study the ten-day fern to learn about tropical plant adaptation.
