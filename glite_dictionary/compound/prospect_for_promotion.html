prospect for promotion

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.spɛkt fər prəˈmoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.pɛkt fə prəˈməʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
the likelihood or chance of being advanced to a higher position or rank at work
chance of job advancement

Examples

After the restructuring, her prospects for promotion improved significantly within the company.
Many employees attend training sessions to enhance their prospects for promotion.
Due to his excellent work performance, John's prospects for promotion were very strong.

Synset Examples

His prospects for promotion seemed dim after the review.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promotion - the act of raising someone to a higher position or rank
