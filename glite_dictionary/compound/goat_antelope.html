goat antelope

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊt ˈæntəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ɡəʊt ˈæntɪˌləʊp / (BrE)
a bovid mammal related to goats but having antelope-like features, including various species such as mountain goats, gorals, serows, and chamois
a bovid with goat and antelope features

Examples

The chamois is a well-known type of goat antelope found in European mountains.
Researchers studied the adaptation of various goat antelopes to cold, mountainous environments.
The serow, a rare goat antelope, lives in the forests of Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bovid - any member of the Bovidae family, including cattle, goats, sheep, and antelopes
  • Synonym: antelope - a swift-running wild animal with hollow horns in the Bovidae family
  • Synonym: goat - a domesticated ruminant mammal with backward curving horns
