swallow shrike

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑːloʊ ˌʃraɪk / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ ˌʃraɪk / (BrE)
An Australasian and Asian bird related to shrikes, with a swallow-like appearance.
Australasian and Asian bird resembling a swallow

Examples

The swallow shrike is often seen darting through the forests of Southeast Asia.
A swallow shrike can be identified by its long wings and hooked beak.
Researchers observed the nesting habits of the swallow shrike in northern Australia.
