Father Brown

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɑðər braʊn / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə braʊn / (BrE)
the Catholic priest who serves as the main character and amateur detective in stories by G. K. Chesterton
fictional detective priest by G. K. Chesterton

Examples

My favorite detective stories are the Father Brown mysteries written by Chesterton.
The character of Father Brown uses intuition and a deep understanding of human nature to solve crimes.
Many adaptations of Father Brown have been made for television and radio.
