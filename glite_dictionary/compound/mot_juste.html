mot juste

1. NOUN

/ moʊ ˈʒust / (AmE), / məʊ ˈʒuːst / (BrE)
the exactly right word or expression for a particular situation or idea
the exactly right word or expression

Examples

She searched her mind, trying to find the mot juste to express her feelings.
The novelist is admired for always choosing the mot juste in his writing.
Sometimes it takes hours to come up with the mot juste for a poem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exact word - the precise or most suitable word for something
  • Antonym: misnomer - a word or name that is wrong or not appropriate

Etymology

From French, literally 'exact word'.
