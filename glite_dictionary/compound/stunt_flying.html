stunt flying

1. NOUN

/ stʌnt ˈflaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / stʌnt ˈflaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of performing aerobatic maneuvers or stunts while flying an aircraft.
aerobatic flying in aircraft

Examples

The airshow featured incredible stunt flying that thrilled the audience.
She trained for years to master the art of stunt flying in vintage planes.
Stunt flying requires incredible skill, courage, and quick reflexes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerobatics - the practice of flying maneuvers involving aircraft attitudes not used in normal flight
