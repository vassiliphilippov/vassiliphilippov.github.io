champagne cup

1. NOUN

/ ʃæmˈpeɪn kʌp / (AmE), / ʃamˈpeɪn kʌp / (BrE)
A punch or mixed drink made with sparkling wine, often combined with other ingredients such as fruit and liqueurs.
punch made with sparkling wine

Examples

She served a refreshing champagne cup at the garden party.
The recipe for the champagne cup includes strawberries and a splash of brandy.
Guests praised the host's signature champagne cup during the wedding toast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champagne cocktail - a cocktail made with champagne, bitters, and sugar
