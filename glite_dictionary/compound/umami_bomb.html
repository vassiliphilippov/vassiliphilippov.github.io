umami bomb

1. NOUN

/ uˈmɑːmi bɑm / (AmE), / uˈmɑːmi bɒm / (BrE)
a dish or food with an exceptionally intense and rich umami (savory) flavor
dish with intense umami flavor

Examples

The mushroom risotto was an umami bomb that delighted everyone at the table.
That miso-glazed cod is an umami bomb with every bite packed full of savory flavor.
He called the ramen broth an umami bomb, thanks to the combination of soy and mushrooms.

Synset Examples

This dish is an umami bomb of flavors.
He described the sauce as an umami bomb for the taste buds.
The stew was an umami bomb that satisfied everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: umami - a category of taste corresponding to the flavor of glutamates; savory taste

Etymology

From Japanese ‘umami’ (savory taste) + English ‘bomb’ (something explosive), indicating a powerful burst of savory flavor.
