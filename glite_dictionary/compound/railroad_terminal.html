railroad terminal

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɑd ˈtɝmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɒd ˈtɜːmɪnəl / (BrE)
a station at the end of a railway line where trains load or unload passengers or goods
end station for trains

Examples

The city finished renovating its historic railroad terminal last year.
Hundreds of commuters pass through the railroad terminal every morning.
Freight is transferred from the railroad terminal to trucks for local delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway station - a place where trains stop to pick up or drop off passengers or goods
  • Degree Lower: bus terminal - a station at the end of a bus route
