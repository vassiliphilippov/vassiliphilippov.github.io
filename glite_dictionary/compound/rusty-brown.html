rusty-brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstiˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈrʌstiˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Having the brownish-red color characteristic of rust.
reddish-brown color like rust

Examples

The leaves turned a rusty-brown color in the autumn sunlight.
She painted the door a rusty-brown to match the old brickwork.
The rusty-brown stains on the metal showed years of exposure to rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - having a brown color tinged with red
