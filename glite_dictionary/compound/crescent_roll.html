crescent roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛsənt roʊl / (AmE), / ˈkrɛsənt rəʊl / (BrE)
A rich, flaky bread roll shaped like a crescent.
flaky, crescent-shaped bread roll

Examples

She served warm crescent rolls with melted butter for breakfast.
The recipe calls for a can of refrigerated crescent rolls to make the crust.
He bought a package of crescent rolls to go with the soup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: croissant - a buttery, crescent-shaped French pastry
