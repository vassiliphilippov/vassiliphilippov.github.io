exotic dancer

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɑːtɪk ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person, typically a woman, who performs erotic dances by undressing to music as a form of entertainment
performer who dances erotically, undressing to music

Examples

The club featured an exotic dancer who performed to lively jazz music every Friday night.
She worked as an exotic dancer to pay her college tuition.
Some people see the profession of exotic dancer as empowering, while others criticize it.
