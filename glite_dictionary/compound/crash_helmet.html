crash helmet

1. NOUN

/ kræʃ ˈhɛlmɪt / (AmE), / kræʃ ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
A padded helmet worn by riders of bicycles or motorcycles to protect the head in case of accidents.
protective helmet for cyclists and motorcyclists

Examples

All motorcycle riders are required by law to wear a crash helmet for their safety.
She bought a brightly colored crash helmet before taking up cycling in the city.
Fortunately, his crash helmet prevented serious injury during the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - protective head covering, especially made of hard material
