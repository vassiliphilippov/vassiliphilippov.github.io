feature film

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːtʃɚ fɪlm / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə fɪlm / (BrE)
A full-length film that is the main film in a cinema program, typically with a running time long enough to be considered the primary attraction.
main full-length film in a cinema program

Examples

The cinema is showing a new feature film every Friday night.
Winning an award for his first feature film launched his directing career.
We watched a short documentary before the feature film started.

Synset Examples

the feature tonight is ‘Casablanca’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie - a motion picture; a film shown in a cinema
  • Antonym: short film - a film shorter than a feature film, often shown before the main movie

Etymology

From 'feature' meaning the main attraction, + 'film', from early 20th century cinema usage.
