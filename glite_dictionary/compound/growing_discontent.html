growing discontent

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊɪŋ dɪsˈkɑntɛnt / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ dɪsˈkɒntɛnt / (BrE)
increasing dissatisfaction or unhappiness within a group, organization, or society
increasing dissatisfaction

Examples

There is growing discontent among employees over pay cuts and layoffs.
The government tried to ignore the growing discontent in the countryside.
Growing discontent was evident in the protests that spread across the city.

Synset Examples

The election revealed a growing discontent with the uniparty system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discontent - a dissatisfied or restless feeling
  • Synonym: growing unease - increasing anxiety or worry
