stem turn

1. NOUN

/ stɛm tɝn / (AmE), / stɛm tɜːn / (BrE)
a skiing turn in which the tail of one ski is pushed outward before bringing the other ski parallel, used to control direction and speed
a type of skiing turn

Examples

She mastered the stem turn before moving on to more advanced skiing techniques.
The instructor demonstrated how to do a proper stem turn on the beginner slope.
Many beginners rely on the stem turn to navigate gentle hills safely.
