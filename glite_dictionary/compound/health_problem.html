health problem

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / hɛlθ ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
A physical or mental condition that prevents someone from functioning normally or causes pain or discomfort.
physical or mental medical condition

Examples

Regular exercise can help prevent many health problems later in life.
She missed work last week because of a serious health problem.
Doctors are working together to find solutions for common health problems in the community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illness - a disease or period of sickness affecting the body or mind
  • Synonym: condition - a particular state of health, often used for persistent diseases or medical problems
  • Synonym: disease - a disorder of structure or function in a human, animal, or plant
