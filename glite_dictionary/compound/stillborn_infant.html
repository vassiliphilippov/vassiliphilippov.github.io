stillborn infant

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪlˌbɔrn ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / ˈstɪlbɔːn ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
an infant who is born without signs of life
infant born without signs of life
medical

Examples

The hospital has a private room for families who have suffered the loss of a stillborn infant.
Special counseling services are offered to parents grieving a stillborn infant.
Documentation is required if a stillborn infant is to be named and recorded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stillbirth - the birth of a baby who is born without signs of life
