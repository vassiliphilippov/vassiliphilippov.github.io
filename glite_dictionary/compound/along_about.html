along about

1. ADV

/ əˈlɔŋ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
at approximately or around a particular time
at approximately a certain time
informal

Examples

We should arrive along about noon, so please be ready.
The meeting is scheduled to start along about three o'clock.
She usually gets home along about sunset each evening.

Synset Examples

We should arrive along about noon, just in time for lunch.
They left the house along about eight in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about - approximately; around a time
