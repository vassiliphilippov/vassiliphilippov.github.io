bag of dicks

1. NOUN

/ bæɡ əv dɪks / (AmE), / bæɡ əv dɪks / (BrE)
(vulgar, slang) A very unpleasant, annoying, or frustrating situation or experience.
vulgar term for a bad situation
slang vulgar
