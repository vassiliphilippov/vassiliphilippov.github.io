genus Urophycis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌjʊrəˈfaɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌjʊərəʊˈfaɪsɪs / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish in the family Phycidae, commonly known as hakes, comprising eight recognized species.
genus of marine hakes (fish)

Examples

The genus Urophycis includes several species of hakes found along the Atlantic coast.
Scientists have recently identified a new species within the genus Urophycis.
Fisheries often target members of the genus Urophycis due to their commercial value.

Etymology

From Greek 'oura' (tail) + 'phykis', relating to the fish's morphology.
