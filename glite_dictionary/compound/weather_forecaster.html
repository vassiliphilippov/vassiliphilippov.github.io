weather forecaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ fəɹˌkæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə fəˌkɑːstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to predict and report future weather conditions
person who predicts the weather

Examples

The weather forecaster predicted rain for the weekend, so we postponed our picnic.
Every evening, the weather forecaster appears on television to update the public about tomorrow's weather.
As a weather forecaster, she analyzes complex data to provide accurate short-term forecasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorologist - an expert in or student of meteorology; one who studies weather and climate
