bang-up job

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋ ʌp dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈbæŋ ʌp dʒɒb / (BrE)
an excellent or outstanding piece of work; a job done extremely well
excellent or outstanding job
informal

Examples

You did a bang-up job organizing the event; everyone had a fantastic time.
The mechanic did a bang-up job fixing my car, and it runs better than ever.
She gave a bang-up job on her final project for art class.

Synset Examples

That was a bang-up job on the presentation!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent job - a job done extremely well
  • Antonym: poor job - a job done badly or with poor quality

Etymology

Likely from the adjective 'bang-up' (meaning excellent), used colloquially since the 19th century.
