processing time

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑː.sɛsɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.ɛsɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time required to complete a specified process or procedure
duration to complete a procedure

Examples

The new software significantly reduced the processing time for customer orders.
Manufacturers are always looking for ways to shorten processing time without sacrificing quality.
Due to the high volume of applications, the processing time may be longer than usual.

Synset Examples

they increased output by decreasing processing time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnaround time - the time taken to complete a process or fulfill a request
