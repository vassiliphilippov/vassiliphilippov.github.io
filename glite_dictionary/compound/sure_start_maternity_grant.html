Sure Start Maternity Grant

1. NOUN

/ ʃʊr stɑrt məˈtɜrnɪti ɡrænt / (AmE), / ʃɔː stɑːt məˈtɜːnɪti ɡrɑːnt / (BrE)
a one-time payment by the UK government to help with the costs of a new child, available to eligible parents
UK government payment for new child
british

Examples

After the birth of her first child, Maria applied for the Sure Start Maternity Grant.
Eligibility for the Sure Start Maternity Grant depends on the family's financial circumstances.
Many families rely on the Sure Start Maternity Grant to help cover initial baby expenses.
