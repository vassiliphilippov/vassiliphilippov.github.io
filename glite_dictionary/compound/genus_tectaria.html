genus Tectaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs tɛkˈtɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs tɛkˈtɛː.ri.ə / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial or epilithic ferns native to tropical rain forests.
genus of tropical rain forest ferns
