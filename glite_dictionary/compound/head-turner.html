head-turner

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛd ˌtɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌtɜːnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is strikingly attractive or noticeable and draws people's attention
someone or something very attractive or noticeable

Examples

That sports car is a real head-turner on the highway.
She wore a stunning dress that made her the head-turner of the evening.
The new store design is a head-turner for everyone passing by.

Synset Examples

His turboslut upgrade made the car a real head-turner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye-catcher - something particularly attractive or noticeable
