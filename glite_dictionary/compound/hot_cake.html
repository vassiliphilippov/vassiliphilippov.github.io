hot cake

1. NOUN

/ hɑt keɪk / (AmE), / hɒt keɪk / (BrE)
A flat, round cake made from a thin batter and cooked on both sides on a griddle; also known as a pancake.
flat cake fried on a griddle

Examples

On Sunday mornings, my grandmother would make stacks of delicious hot cakes for breakfast.
He drizzled maple syrup over a warm hot cake, savoring each bite.
The smell of hot cakes cooking filled the entire house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pancake - a thin, flat cake of batter fried on both sides
