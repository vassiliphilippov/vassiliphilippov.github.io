American harvest mouse

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈhɑr.vəst maʊs / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈhɑː.vɪst maʊs / (BrE)
a small, grayish mouse native to North and Central America, often found in grain fields and grassy areas
a small North American field mouse

Examples

The American harvest mouse is rarely seen during the day as it hides in tall grass.
Researchers discovered a nest of American harvest mice in the abandoned field.
Farmers often encounter the American harvest mouse when harvesting grains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest mouse - a mouse species living in fields and grasslands
