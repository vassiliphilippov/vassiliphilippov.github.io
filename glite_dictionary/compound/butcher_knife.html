butcher knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊtʃɚ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə naɪf / (BrE)
a large, sharp knife used for cutting or trimming meat, especially by butchers
large knife for cutting meat

Examples

The chef skillfully used a butcher knife to carve the large roast.
Every butcher keeps a butcher knife ready for preparing big cuts of meat.
Always handle a butcher knife carefully to avoid accidental injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaver - a heavy broad-bladed knife used for cutting through meat and bone
