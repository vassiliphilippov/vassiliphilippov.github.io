Hani language

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːni ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːni ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The language of the Hani people, belonging to the Loloish branch of the Sino-Tibetan family, spoken in southern Yunnan Province, China, and surrounding areas.
a Loloish language of southern China

Examples

Many people in Yunnan Province speak the Hani language as their mother tongue.
Researchers have compiled a dictionary of the Hani language to preserve its unique vocabulary.
She is fluent in both Mandarin and the Hani language thanks to her multicultural upbringing.
