glasses case

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsɪz keɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsɪz keɪs / (BrE)
a small, usually rigid container for storing and protecting eyeglasses or spectacles
container for eyeglasses or spectacles

Examples

She keeps her new spectacles safe in a sturdy glasses case.
He searched his backpack but couldn't find his glasses case.
My glasses case is lined with soft fabric to prevent scratches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacle case - a case for carrying spectacles
