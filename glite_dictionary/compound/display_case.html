display case

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (BrE)
a glass container with transparent sides used to display and protect items in shops, museums, or homes
glass container for displaying items

Examples

The museum's most valuable artifacts are locked in a display case for security.
She arranged her collectibles neatly inside a glass display case in the living room.
Jewelry sparkled under the bright lights of the store's main display case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showcase - a glass case used for displaying items

2. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ keɪs / (BrE)
A glass container or cabinet used for exhibiting and protecting items such as collectibles, merchandise, or artifacts.
glass container for showing items

Examples

The antique coins were arranged in a display case at the museum.
She admired the jewelry sparkling behind the glass display case in the store.
Each trophy stood proudly in the school's main display case near the entrance.

Synset Examples

The exhibition showcases butterflies under glass display cases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showcase - a glass container for exhibiting items
