their specialty

1. NOUN

/ ðɛr ˈspɛʃəlti / (AmE), / ðeə ˈspɛʃəlti / (BrE)
a dish or item that a particular place, especially a restaurant, is known for or does especially well
signature dish or item of a place

Examples

Whenever I visit that cafe, I always order their specialty, the homemade apple pie.
The waiter recommended their specialty, which is a spicy seafood stew unlike anything I've tasted before.
Many people travel miles just to try their specialty at this famous diner.

Synset Examples

You must try the veal; it’s their specialty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: house specialty - the main or best-known dish of a restaurant
  • Synonym: signature dish - the dish that a chef or restaurant is known for
