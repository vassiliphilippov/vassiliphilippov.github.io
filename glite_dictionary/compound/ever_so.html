ever so

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvɚ soʊ / (AmE), / ˈɛvə səʊ / (BrE)
Used to emphasize the degree of an adjective or adverb; very or extremely.
very; to a great degree

Examples

The room was ever so quiet after everyone left.
I'm ever so grateful for your help with the project.
That dress looks ever so lovely on you.

Synset Examples

she was ever so friendly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: very - to a high degree; extremely
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small degree
