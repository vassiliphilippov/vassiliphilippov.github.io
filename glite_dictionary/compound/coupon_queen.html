coupon queen

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːpɑːn kwiːn / (AmE), / ˈkuːpɒn kwiːn / (BrE)
a woman who is extremely skilled and enthusiastic at finding and using coupons to obtain discounts when shopping
woman skilled at using coupons for discounts
informal

Examples

My sister is a real coupon queen and saves our family a lot of money.
As the office's coupon queen, she always knows when the best sales are happening.
He joked that his wife was the ultimate coupon queen after she bought a week's groceries for half price.

Synset Examples

She’s the coupon queen, always finding the best deals.
Everyone knows her as the coupon queen at the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain hunter - someone who looks for the best discounts or deals
