pater of the family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪtər əv ðə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtə əv ðə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
the father or male head of a family, especially in a household
male head of a family

Examples

As the pater of the family, he made all major decisions for the household.
At family gatherings, the pater of the family often gave the opening toast.
The pater of the family was respected for his wisdom and leadership.

Synset Examples

The pater of the family shared his wisdom at dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patriarch - the male head of a family or tribe

Etymology

From Latin 'pater' (father), used in English to evoke authority or formality.
