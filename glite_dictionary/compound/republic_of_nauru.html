Republic of Nauru

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv nɑˈuːruː / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv nɑːˈuːruː / (BrE)
a small island country in the Pacific Ocean, located on Nauru Island, whose economy is largely supported by phosphate exports
island country in the Pacific Ocean

Examples

The Republic of Nauru is one of the world's smallest independent nations.
Phosphate mining has significantly influenced the economy of the Republic of Nauru.
The Republic of Nauru became a member of the United Nations in 1999.
