blue grama

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈɡræmə / (AmE), / blu ˈɡræmə / (BrE)
a perennial pasture grass native to western North America, known for its blue-green leaves and importance as forage for livestock
a pasture grass native to western North America

Examples

Blue grama is an important forage grass for cattle in the Great Plains.
The lawn was planted with blue grama to withstand drought conditions.
Many native grasslands feature blue grama alongside other resilient species.
