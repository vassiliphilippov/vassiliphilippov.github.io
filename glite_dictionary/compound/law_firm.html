law firm

1. NOUN

/ lɔ ˌfɝm / (AmE), / lɔː ˌfɜːm / (BrE)
A business organization formed by lawyers to practice law and provide legal services to clients.
business of lawyers; legal services company

Examples

She joined a prestigious law firm after graduating from law school.
The law firm specializes in corporate and intellectual property cases.
Many clients rely on the expertise of a law firm for legal advice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal practice - the professional work done by lawyers
