roofing tile

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfɪŋ taɪl / (AmE), / ˈruːfɪŋ tʌɪl / (BrE)
a thin, flat or curved slab, usually made of fired clay or concrete, used to cover roofs for protection from the weather
thin slab for covering roofs

Examples

The house was built in the traditional style with red roofing tiles.
He replaced several broken roofing tiles after the storm damaged the roof.
Clay roofing tiles are known for their durability and classic appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tile - a thin slab of hard material used for covering surfaces
