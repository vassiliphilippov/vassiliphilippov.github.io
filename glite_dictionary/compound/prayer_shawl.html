prayer shawl

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛr ʃɔl / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌʃɔːl / (BrE)
a shawl with ritually knotted fringes on each corner, worn by Jewish people during morning prayers
shawl worn by Jews during prayer

Examples

He wrapped himself in a prayer shawl before beginning his morning prayers.
The rabbi explained the significance of the knotted fringes on the prayer shawl.
Many families pass down a special prayer shawl from one generation to the next.
