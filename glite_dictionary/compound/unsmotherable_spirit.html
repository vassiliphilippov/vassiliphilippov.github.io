unsmotherable spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnˈsmʌðərəbəl ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsmʌðərəbəl ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A spirit, character, or determination that cannot be suppressed, subdued, or extinguished.
spirit that cannot be subdued or extinguished

Examples

Despite years of adversity, his unsmotherable spirit inspired everyone around him.
The team's unsmotherable spirit pushed them to victory against all odds.
Even in defeat, she maintained an unsmotherable spirit and encouraged her teammates to persevere.

Synset Examples

Her spirit was unsmotherable, no matter the challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indomitable spirit - an unyielding or impossible-to-defeat spirit
  • Synonym: unquenchable spirit - a spirit that cannot be extinguished or stifled
