bus company

1. NOUN

/ bʌs ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / bʌs ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that provides local transportation services by bus
company providing bus transportation

Examples

The bus company announced new routes starting next month.
After the merger, the small town's bus company expanded its fleet.
Her brother works as a driver for a local bus company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus service - the operation of buses providing transport to the public
  • Antonym: train company - company operating trains for public transport
