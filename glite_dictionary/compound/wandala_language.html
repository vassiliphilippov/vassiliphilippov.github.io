Wandala language

1. NOUN

/ wɑnˈdɑːlə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / wænˈdɑːlə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Mandara mountains of Cameroon, noted for having only two vowels
Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

Linguists are fascinated by the Wandala language because it has only two vowel sounds.
The Wandala language is spoken primarily in the Mandara mountains of northern Cameroon.
She is studying the phonology of the Wandala language for her doctoral dissertation.
