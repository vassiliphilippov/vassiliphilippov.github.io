battle-tested skill

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætl ˌtɛstɪd skɪl / (AmE), / ˈbætəl ˌtɛstɪd skɪl / (BrE)
A skill that has been proven effective and reliable through challenging or demanding experiences.
skill proven by experience

Examples

Her battle-tested skills made her a valuable asset during the crisis.
The company's battle-tested skills were evident in how efficiently they handled the unexpected problems.
Relying on his battle-tested skills, he navigated the negotiation with confidence.

Synset Examples

She brought her battle-tested skills to the new team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: experienced - having knowledge or skill from doing something for a long time
  • Antonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
