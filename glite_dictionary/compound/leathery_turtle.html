leathery turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðəri ˈtɝːtəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəri ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
A large marine turtle with a flexible, leathery shell, known for being the largest living turtle species.
largest marine turtle with leathery shell

Examples

The leathery turtle can weigh up to 700 kilograms, making it the heaviest turtle in the world.
Scientists are concerned about declining populations of the leathery turtle due to habitat loss and hunting.
A distinctive feature of the leathery turtle is its soft, leathery shell instead of a hard, bony one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leatherback turtle - Another name for the leathery turtle, the largest living sea turtle
