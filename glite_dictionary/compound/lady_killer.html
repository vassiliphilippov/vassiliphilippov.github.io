lady killer

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdi ˌkɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi ˌkɪlə / (BrE)
a man who is very attractive to women and often seduces or exploits them
man who seduces or manipulates women

Examples

Everyone at the party knew Jake was a bit of a lady killer, charming women with ease.
Despite his reputation as a lady killer, he longed for a meaningful relationship.
That old actor was once a famous lady killer in his day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
  • Antonym: gentleman - a courteous or honorable man
