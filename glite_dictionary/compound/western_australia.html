Western Australia

1. PROPN

/ ˌwɛstɚn ɔːˈstreɪliə / (AmE), / ˌwɛstən ɔːˈstreɪliə / (BrE)
a state occupying the western third of Australia, with Perth as its capital
Australia's largest western state

Examples

Many rare species can only be found in Western Australia.
My cousin is moving to Western Australia for work next month.
The weather in Western Australia is usually warm and dry.
