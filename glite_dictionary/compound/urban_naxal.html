urban Naxal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːbən ˈnʌksəl / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˈnæksəl / (BrE)
a derogatory term for a leftist activist or dissenter in an urban area, especially one accused of supporting Naxalite or Maoist causes
urban leftist activist (derogatory)
informal offensive

Examples

Many activists were labeled urban Naxals after criticizing government policies online.
Some politicians use the term urban Naxal to discredit their opponents.
The film explored the challenges faced by those branded as urban Naxals in India.

Synset Examples

She was accused of being an urban Naxal for her protests.
The term 'urban Naxal' is often used in political debates.
Some journalists label activists as urban Naxals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Naxalite - member of a Maoist political group in India

Etymology

Blend of 'urban' and 'Naxal', from 'Naxalite' (a member of a Maoist political movement in India, originating from Naxalbari).
