high-vitamin diet

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈvaɪ.tə.mɪn ˈdaɪ.ət / (AmE), / haɪ ˈvɪ.tə.mɪn ˈdaɪ.ət / (BrE)
a diet that contains increased levels of vitamins, intended especially for individuals with vitamin deficiencies
diet rich in vitamins

Examples

After being diagnosed with a deficiency, her doctor recommended a high-vitamin diet to improve her health.
Many nutritionists agree that a high-vitamin diet can help boost the immune system.
Children with growth problems sometimes benefit from following a high-vitamin diet for several months.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-vitamin diet - a diet lacking in sufficient vitamins
  • Synonym: balanced diet - a diet containing all nutrients in the right proportions
