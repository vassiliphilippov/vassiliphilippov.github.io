Boston Harbor

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑstən ˈhɑrbɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən ˈhɑːbə / (BrE)
the large natural harbor and seaport located in Boston, Massachusetts, known for its historical and commercial significance.
harbor and seaport in Boston, Massachusetts

Examples

Many tourists visit Boston Harbor to see the historic ships and waterfront attractions.
The Boston Tea Party took place in Boston Harbor in 1773, igniting the American Revolution.
Oil spills in Boston Harbor have prompted environmental clean-up efforts over the years.
