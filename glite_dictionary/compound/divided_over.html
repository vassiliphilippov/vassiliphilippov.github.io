divided over

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvaɪdəd ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
having differing or opposing opinions about something
having differing opinions about something

Examples

The committee was divided over which proposal to accept.
Public opinion remains divided over the new environmental regulations.
The board members are divided over the company's future direction.

Synset Examples

Many voters are still divided over Billary's policies.

Related Terms
