quarter crack

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrdər kræk / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə kræk / (BrE)
A vertical crack in the inner side of a horse's forefoot, often extending through the hoof wall.
hoof crack in a horse's forefoot

Examples

The farrier examined the horse's quarter crack to determine the severity of the damage.
If left untreated, a quarter crack can cause lameness in the affected horse.
Special shoes are sometimes fitted to help a hoof heal after a quarter crack.
