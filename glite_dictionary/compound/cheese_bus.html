cheese bus

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiz bʌs / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːz bʌs / (BrE)
A brightly colored school bus, especially one painted yellow; used humorously.
humorous term for a yellow school bus
informal american

Examples

Every morning, the children waited at the corner for the cheese bus to arrive.
I rode the cheese bus to school throughout elementary and middle school.
He joked that his first car was actually a retired cheese bus.

Synset Examples

The kids loved riding the cheese bus to school.
We painted the old van to look like a cheese bus.

Etymology

Formed from 'cheese' (alluding to the yellow color) and 'bus'.
