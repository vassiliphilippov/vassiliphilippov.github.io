lemon cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən tʃiːz / (BrE)
a sweet, thick spread made from lemons, butter, eggs, and sugar, served as a conserve or dessert topping
a sweet lemon-flavored spread
british

Examples

She spread lemon cheese on her toast for breakfast.
The homemade lemon cheese gave the cake a delightful tangy flavor.
Would you like a scone with some lemon cheese?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon curd - a sweet, creamy spread made with lemons, eggs, butter, and sugar
