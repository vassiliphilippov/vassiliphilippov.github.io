family movie

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
A film intended to be suitable for children and adults, featuring content appropriate for all ages.
film suitable for all ages

Examples

We watched a family movie together on Friday night with popcorn and snacks.
The studio specializes in producing family movies that parents and children can enjoy together.
Finding a good family movie can make weekend evenings both fun and relaxing.

Synset Examples

According to Bazalgette and Staples, a fine example of a family film is Honey, I Shrunk the Kids (1989), which if it was a European children's film with a similar plot, the title would be Sis, Dad Shrunk Us, explaining that European children's films are told from the child's perspective, portraying the story through the various emotions and experiences of the child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family film - film genre suitable for all ages
