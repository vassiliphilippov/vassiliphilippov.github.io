strip alert

1. NOUN

/ strɪp əˈlɝːt / (AmE), / strɪp əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
a state of readiness in which aircraft and their crews are prepared for immediate takeoff, typically for domestic defense purposes
military aircraft state of immediate readiness

Examples

The base maintained two fighter jets on strip alert throughout the weekend.
Due to increased tensions, the military implemented a strip alert protocol at several airfields.
Pilots on strip alert must be ready to scramble at a moment's notice.

Synset Examples

the Air Force will keep fighters on strip alert at bases around the country
