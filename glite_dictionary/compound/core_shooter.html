core shooter

1. NOUN

/ kɔr ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / kɔː ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
a machine used in foundries to manufacture sand cores by injecting or blowing sand into a core box
machine for making sand cores in foundries

Examples

The foundry upgraded to a new core shooter to improve its sand molding process.
After the core shooter malfunctioned, production of cylinder heads was delayed for several hours.
Training workers to operate the core shooter safely is essential for efficient foundry operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: core box - a box or mold used to form sand cores
