subway token

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌweɪ ˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈsʌbweɪ ˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
a coin-shaped token used to pay for entry into a subway system, especially before the introduction of electronic fare cards
coin used for subway fare

Examples

He handed the booth clerk a subway token and hurried to catch the train.
Before MetroCards, every New Yorker carried at least one subway token in their pocket.
The museum displays a collection of vintage subway tokens from different decades.
