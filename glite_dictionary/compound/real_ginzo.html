real ginzo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiːəl ˈɡɪnzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈɡɪnzəʊ / (BrE)
(slang) An inept or foolish person.
inept or foolish person (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

If you keep forgetting your lines, you'll look like a real ginzo on stage.
He felt like a real ginzo after dropping the ball at the crucial moment.
Don't be a real ginzo—double-check your answers before submitting.

Synset Examples

That guy is a real ginzo when it comes to sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: idiot - a very foolish or stupid person

Etymology

From 'ginzo', an ethnic slur for an Italian-American, often used in extended senses; origin uncertain, possibly from Italian surnames ending in '-zo'.
