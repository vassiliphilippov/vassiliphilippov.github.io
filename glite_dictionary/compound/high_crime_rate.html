high crime rate

1. NOUN

/ haɪ kraɪm reɪt / (AmE), / haɪ kraɪm reɪt / (BrE)
a situation or area with a notably large number of criminal incidents, typically compared to other places or averages
situation with many crimes

Examples

The city struggled to attract new businesses due to its high crime rate.
Residents voiced concerns about the high crime rate at the community meeting.
A high crime rate often leads to increased police presence in urban neighborhoods.

Synset Examples

Gunchester earned a reputation for its high crime rates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low crime rate - situation with few criminal incidents
