wall socket

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈsɑkɪt / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈsɒkɪt / (BrE)
a device set into a wall, forming a point where the plug of an electrical appliance can be connected to the electricity supply
electrical outlet set in a wall

Examples

I plugged my phone charger into the wall socket next to the bed.
The electrician repaired the faulty wall socket in the living room.
You should never touch a wall socket with wet hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power outlet - a device for connecting electrical equipment to the power supply
  • Antonym: plug - object inserted into a socket to obtain electric current
