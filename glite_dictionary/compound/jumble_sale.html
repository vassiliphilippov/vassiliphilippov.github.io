jumble sale

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌm.bəl seɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bəl seɪl / (BrE)
an event where second-hand or donated goods are sold, typically to raise money for a charity or organization
charity sale of donated goods
british

Examples

The local church held a jumble sale to raise funds for the new roof.
She donated all her old clothes to the annual jumble sale.
We found some great bargains at the jumble sale last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rummage sale - charity sale of donated goods (chiefly US term)
