tender groin

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛndɚ ˈɡrɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə ˈɡrɔɪn / (BrE)
a painful or sensitive area in the groin, often indicating injury or inflammation
painful or sensitive groin area
medical

Examples

The player missed several games because of a tender groin after training.
A tender groin can be a sign of a muscle strain or hernia.
His doctor advised rest and ice to reduce the pain caused by a tender groin.

Synset Examples

The athlete had to sit out due to a persistent tendergroin.
He complained of a tendergroin after the intense workout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groin strain - an injury involving torn or stretched groin muscles
