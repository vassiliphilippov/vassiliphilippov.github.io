sailors choice

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlərz ˌtʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈseɪləz ˌtʃɔɪs / (BrE)

a tropical grunt fish species

The sailors choice is commonly seen by snorkelers along the coral reefs of the Florida Keys.
