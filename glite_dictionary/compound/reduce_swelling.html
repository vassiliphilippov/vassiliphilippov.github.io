to reduce swelling

1. VERB

/ rɪˈduːs ˈswɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːs ˈswɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
to make swelling become smaller or less severe in an injured or affected area of the body
make swelling smaller or less severe
medical

Examples

Applying a cold compress immediately can help reduce swelling after an injury.
Doctors gave him medication to reduce swelling in his ankle.
Elevating the leg may also assist in reducing swelling caused by a sprain.

Synset Examples

She applied ice to her bruise to reduce swelling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to alleviate - to make pain or suffering less severe
  • Antonym: exacerbate swelling - to make swelling worse or more severe
