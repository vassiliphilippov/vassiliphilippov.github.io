Texpatriate community

1. NOUN

/ tɛksˈpætɹi.eɪt kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / tɛksˈpætɹi.eɪt kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
A group of people originally from Texas who are living outside the state of Texas.
Texans living outside Texas

Examples

The Texpatriate community in New York holds annual cultural festivals to celebrate Texas traditions.
During the holiday season, the Texpatriate community gathered to share memories of home-cooked Texan meals.
She joined a Texpatriate community group online to connect with fellow Texans living overseas.

Synset Examples

The Texpatriate community organized a barbecue last weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expatriate community - community of people from the same country living abroad
  • Degree Lower: Texan - a person from Texas

Etymology

Blend of 'Texan' and 'expatriate'; 'expatriate' from Medieval Latin (ex- 'out' + patria 'native country').
