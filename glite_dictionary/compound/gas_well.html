gas well

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs wɛl / (AmE), / ɡas wɛl / (BrE)
A well drilled into the earth to extract natural gas.
well for obtaining natural gas

Examples

The company drilled a new gas well in the northern region last year.
Many gas wells require regular maintenance to keep them producing efficiently.
After several years, the gas well yielded less fuel and was eventually sealed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil well - a well drilled to obtain petroleum oil
