toilet table

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt ˌteɪbl / (BrE)
a low table fitted with a mirror or mirrors, used when sitting to dress or apply makeup
dressing table with mirror(s)

Examples

She sat at her toilet table to apply her makeup before the party.
The antique toilet table had an ornate mirror and delicate carvings.
Each guest room in the hotel includes a spacious toilet table for personal grooming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressing table - a table with a mirror for dressing or grooming
  • Synonym: vanity - a piece of bedroom furniture with a mirror and drawers, used for grooming

Etymology

From 'toilet' (French toilette, meaning 'dressing, grooming') and 'table' (from Old French table, from Latin tabula).
