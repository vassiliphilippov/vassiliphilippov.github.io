fir cone

1. NOUN

/ fɝː koʊn / (AmE), / fɜː kəʊn / (BrE)
the cone produced by a fir tree, containing its seeds
cone containing seeds of a fir tree

Examples

The forest floor was scattered with fir cones after the strong winds last night.
A squirrel picked up a fir cone and began nibbling on its seeds.
She collected fir cones to use in her holiday decorations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine cone - the cone produced by a pine tree, containing its seeds
