pencil cedar tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnsəl ˌsiːdər triː / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəl ˌsiːdə triː / (BrE)
a type of juniper whose wood is suitable for making pencils
juniper tree used for pencil wood

Examples

The wood of the pencil cedar tree is prized for its fine grain and aroma.
Historically, pencil cedar trees were harvested extensively to supply the pencil industry.
Botanists study the various species referred to as pencil cedar tree for their ecological significance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juniper - an evergreen shrub or small tree with berry-like cones
