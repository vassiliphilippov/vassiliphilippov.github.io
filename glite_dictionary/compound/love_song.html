love song

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
A song about love or expressing love for another person, often romantic in nature.
song about love

Examples

He wrote a beautiful love song for his girlfriend's birthday.
Their favorite love song played as they danced together at the wedding.
The album is filled with heartfelt love songs that capture different emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballad - a song or poem narrating a story in short stanzas, often about love
