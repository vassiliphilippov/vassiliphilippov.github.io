property settlement

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpərti ˈsɛtl̩mənt / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəti ˈsɛtlmənt / (BrE)
(law) the formal arrangement dividing property and assets between marriage partners upon divorce or separation.
legal division of assets after divorce
legal

Examples

The court ordered a property settlement that divided their assets equally.
Negotiating a fair property settlement was the most challenging part of the divorce.
They reached a property settlement without having to go to court.
