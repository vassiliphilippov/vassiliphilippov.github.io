gold of pleasure

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊld əv ˈplɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ɡəʊld əv ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
an annual plant (Camelina sativa) of the mustard family, with small yellowish flowers, cultivated for its oil-rich seeds and fiber
oil-seed plant (Camelina sativa)

Examples

Farmers in Europe have grown gold of pleasure for its valuable oil since ancient times.
Recently, gold of pleasure has gained popularity as a sustainable crop for biofuel production.
The seeds of gold of pleasure are processed to produce a nutritious oil used in cooking and cosmetics.
