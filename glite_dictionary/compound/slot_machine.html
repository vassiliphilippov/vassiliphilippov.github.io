slot machine

1. NOUN

/ slɑt məˈʃin / (AmE), / slɒt məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a gambling machine operated by inserting coins, in which a player wins money if particular symbols line up after spinning
coin-operated gambling machine

Examples

He lost all his coins playing the slot machine at the casino.
Bright lights and spinning reels drew her to the slot machine by the entrance.
Many tourists try their luck on a slot machine hoping to win a jackpot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit machine - British term for a slot machine

Etymology

From 'slot' meaning a narrow opening and 'machine'.
