sunny day

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌni deɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌni deɪ / (BrE)
A day when the sun is shining brightly and there are few or no clouds.
day with bright sunshine

Examples

We went hiking because it was such a beautiful sunny day.
Children love to play outside on a sunny day.
After a week of rain, a sunny day finally arrived.

Synset Examples

It's a sunny day, perfect for a picnic.
The garden looks so cheerful on a sunshiny day.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rainy day - a day when it rains
  • Antonym: cloudy day - a day with many clouds, little or no sunshine
