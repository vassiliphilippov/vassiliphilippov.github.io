clever-clever

1. ADJ

/ ˈklɛvɚ ˈklɛvɚ / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə ˈklɛvə / (BrE)
Deliberately or ostentatiously clever, often with the implication of being smug and self-satisfied.
ostentatiously or smugly clever
british

Examples

He made a clever-clever remark that annoyed everyone in the meeting.
Sometimes her clever-clever attitude puts people off.
They didn't appreciate his clever-clever jokes during the serious discussion.
