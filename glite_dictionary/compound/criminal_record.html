criminal record

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnl ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
an official record of a person's convictions for criminal offenses
list of a person's criminal convictions

Examples

If you have a criminal record, it may be harder to find employment.
The company refused to hire applicants with any criminal record.
After the trial, his criminal record was expunged.

Synset Examples

he ruled that the criminal record of the defendant could not be disclosed to the court
the prostitute had a record a mile long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - an official written account of something
