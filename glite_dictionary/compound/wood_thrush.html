wood thrush

1. NOUN

/ wʊd θrʌʃ / (AmE), / wʊd θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a large North American songbird of the thrush family, noted for its melodious song and commonly found in eastern woodlands
a North American woodland songbird

Examples

The distinctive song of the wood thrush echoes through the forest in the early morning.
A wood thrush built its nest among the branches of a maple tree in our backyard.
Many birdwatchers visit the park in hopes of spotting a wood thrush among the foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrush - a songbird of the family Turdidae
