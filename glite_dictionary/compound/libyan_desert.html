Libyan Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪbiən ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ˈlɪbiən ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
the northeastern part of the Sahara Desert, located in Libya, Egypt, and Sudan
northeastern part of the Sahara Desert

Examples

The Libyan Desert is known for its arid climate and vast stretches of sand dunes.
Archaeologists have discovered ancient rock art in the remote areas of the Libyan Desert.
The western oases provide a stark contrast to the surrounding barrenness of the Libyan Desert.
