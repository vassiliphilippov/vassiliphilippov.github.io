golden thread

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən θrɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən θrɛd / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant native to North American woodlands, known for its trifoliate leaves, yellow rootstock, and white flowers.
a North American woodland perennial plant

Examples

The herbalist identified the plant as golden thread, prized for its medicinal root.
We found patches of golden thread blooming along the shaded forest trail.
Golden thread thrives best in the cool, damp soils of northern woodlands.
