total nozzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.təl ˈnɒz.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈnɒz.ə / (BrE)
(British slang) A completely foolish or incompetent person.
foolish or incompetent person (slang)
slang british

Examples

He was a total nozzer for forgetting his own birthday.
Everyone in the office thinks Kevin is a total nozzer after the presentation.
She called her brother a total nozzer when he tried to fix the TV and broke it.

Synset Examples

She laughed at his mistake, calling him a total nozzer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking sense or judgment
  • Antonym: genius - a very intelligent or talented person
