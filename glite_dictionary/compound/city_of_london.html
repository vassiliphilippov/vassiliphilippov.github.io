City of London

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɪti əv ˈlʌndən / (AmE), / ˌsɪti əv ˈlʌndən / (BrE)
the area of central London within its original ancient boundaries, known as a major commercial and financial centre, distinct from Greater London
financial district in central London

Examples

Many banks have their headquarters in the City of London.
The City of London has its own police force and governance system.
The financial heart of the UK is located in the City of London.
