dinner theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌθiətər / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
A theater in which a meal is served to the audience as part of the admission price, typically while a play or performance is presented.
theater where dinner is served with the show

Examples

We booked tickets at the local dinner theater for a fun night out.
The dinner theater offers a three-course meal served during the performance.
Many tourists enjoy experiencing a dinner theater while visiting the city.
