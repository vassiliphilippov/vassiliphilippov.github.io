yellowish green

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.ɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green that is tinged with yellow; a color between green and yellow
a shade between green and yellow

Examples

The walls were painted a soft yellowish green that made the room feel fresh and lively.
I chose a dress in yellowish green for the spring wedding.
The artist mixed blue, yellow, and a hint of white to achieve the perfect yellowish green shade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chartreuse - a yellowish-green color
  • Degree Lower: green - the color between blue and yellow on the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: yellow - the color between green and orange in the visible spectrum
