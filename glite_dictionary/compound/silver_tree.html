silver tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər triː / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə triː / (BrE)
a small South African tree (Leucadendron argenteum) with long, silky, silvery leaves
South African tree with silvery leaves

Examples

The slopes of Table Mountain are home to the silver tree, famous for its shimmering foliage.
Tourists are often captivated by the beauty of the silver tree in the Cape region.
Botanists study the silver tree for its unique adaptation to South Africa's climate.
