stern look

1. NOUN

/ stɝn lʊk / (AmE), / stɜːn lʊk / (BrE)
a serious or disapproving facial expression, often used to show displeasure or strictness
serious or disapproving expression

Examples

The teacher gave me a stern look when I arrived late to class.
With a stern look, the principal silenced the noisy students in the hall.
She fixed him with a stern look to show she disapproved of his joke.

Synset Examples

His dwimmery during the lecture earned him a stern look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glare - an angry or fierce stare
  • Antonym: smile - an expression of happiness or friendliness
