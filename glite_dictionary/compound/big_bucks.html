big bucks

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ bʌks / (AmE), / bɪɡ bʌks / (BrE)
a large amount of money, especially as income or profit
a large sum of money
informal

Examples

She earned big bucks working in the tech industry.
People will pay big bucks for rare collectibles.
He made big bucks after his company went public.
