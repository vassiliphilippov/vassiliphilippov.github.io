almond crescent

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːmənd ˈkrɛsənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ˈkrɛsənt / (BrE)
A rich, crescent-shaped cookie made with ground almonds.
crescent-shaped almond cookie

Examples

My grandmother makes delicious almond crescents every Christmas.
The bakery displays trays of freshly baked almond crescents near the window.
I love the nutty flavor and buttery texture of an almond crescent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kipferl - A similar crescent-shaped pastry of Austrian origin
