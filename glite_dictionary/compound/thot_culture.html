thot culture

1. NOUN

/ θɑt ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / θɒt ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
A subculture characterized by the open display of promiscuity and materialism, often associated with certain online and social media behaviors.
subculture tied to promiscuity and materialism
slang

Examples

Many critics argue that thot culture promotes unhealthy views on relationships and self-worth.
She wrote an article discussing the influence of thot culture on fashion and music.
Some people blame social media for the rise of thot culture among young adults.

Synset Examples

The internet is filled with thot culture nowadays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hookup culture - culture of casual sexual encounters

Etymology

'Thot' is a slang acronym for 'that ho over there', which originated in African American Vernacular English (AAVE); 'culture' from Latin 'cultura'.
