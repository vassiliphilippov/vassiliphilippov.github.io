Liberian dollar

1. NOUN

/ laɪˈbɪriən ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / laɪˈbɪəriən ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official basic unit of currency in Liberia
currency of Liberia

Examples

Most prices in Monrovia are listed in Liberian dollars.
She exchanged her US dollars for Liberian dollars at the border.
The government announced the introduction of new Liberian dollar banknotes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: US dollar - currency of the United States
