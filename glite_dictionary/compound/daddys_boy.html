daddy's boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdædiz bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈdædiz bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or man who is excessively attached to or dependent on his father
a male overly attached to his father
informal

Examples

Everyone at school called him a daddy's boy because he always sided with his father.
Despite being an adult, Mark is still a daddy's boy at heart.
His friends teased him for being a daddy's boy whenever he quoted his father.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows he’s a bit of a daddy's boy.
Being a daddy's boy has its perks, like new games.
He’s such a daddy's boy; he follows him everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mama's boy - a boy or man excessively attached to his mother
