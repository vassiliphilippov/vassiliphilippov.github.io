call centre

1. NOUN

/ kɔl ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a place where large numbers of telephone calls are handled, especially for taking orders or providing customer service
place handling large numbers of phone calls

Examples

I called the company's call centre to ask about my recent order.
Many businesses outsource their call centre operations to cut costs.
Working in a call centre requires strong communication skills and patience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contact center - a center that manages customer contacts through various channels including phone, email, and chat
