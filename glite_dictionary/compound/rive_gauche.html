Rive Gauche

1. PROPN

/ ʁiv ɡoʃ / (AmE), / ʁiv ɡoʃ / (BrE)
the southern bank of the River Seine in Paris, known as a historic center of artistic and student life
the southern bank of the Seine in Paris

Examples

Many famous writers and artists made the Rive Gauche their home in the twentieth century.
Walking along the Rive Gauche, you'll find charming cafes and bookshops.
The Rive Gauche is often associated with Paris’s bohemian lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Rive Droite - the northern bank of the River Seine in Paris

Etymology

French, literally ‘left bank’.
