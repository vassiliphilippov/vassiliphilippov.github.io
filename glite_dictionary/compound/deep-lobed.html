deep-lobed

1. ADJ

/ diːp loʊbd / (AmE), / diːp ləʊbd / (BrE)
(especially of leaves or body parts) having lobes that are markedly deep or pronounced.
having very deep lobes

Examples

The oak tree has deep-lobed leaves that are easy to identify.
The surgeon examined the patient's deep-lobed liver for any abnormalities.
A deep-lobed petal structure is characteristic of this flower species.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lobed - having lobes or rounded projections
