love-potion

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv ˌpoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌpəʊʃən / (BrE)
A drink believed to have magical properties that can cause someone to fall in love with the person who gives it to them.
magical drink inducing love

Examples

The witch brewed a mysterious love-potion to help the young man win his beloved's heart.
Legends often warn of the dangers in using a love-potion to manipulate someone's feelings.
Shakespeare's plays sometimes feature characters who fall victim to a love-potion and act against their will.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potion - a liquid with supposed magical powers
