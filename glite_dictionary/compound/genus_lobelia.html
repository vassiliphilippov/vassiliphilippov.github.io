genus Lobelia

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiː.nəs loʊˈbiː.li.ə / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.nəs ləʊˈbiː.li.ə / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants, sometimes placed in the family Lobeliaceae, including many species known for their bright, tubular flowers.
genus of flowering plants, including lobelias

Examples

The genus Lobelia includes many species widely cultivated for their colorful garden flowers.
Botanists debate whether the genus Lobelia should be placed in the family Campanulaceae or in a separate family, Lobeliaceae.
Some members of the genus Lobelia are known for their medicinal properties, though others can be toxic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lobelia - any plant of the genus Lobelia

Etymology

Lobelia named after Flemish botanist Matthias de l'Obel (1538–1616).
