shoulder patch

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldɚ ˌpætʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə ˌpætʃ / (BrE)
a patch worn on the shoulder of a military uniform to indicate rank or affiliation
patch for military rank or unit on uniform

Examples

The soldier's shoulder patch showed he belonged to an elite unit.
Each division in the army has a distinctive shoulder patch on their uniforms.
She sewed a new shoulder patch onto her jacket after her promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badge - a piece of metal or cloth worn to show rank, membership, or achievement
  • Synonym: insignia - a badge or distinguishing mark of military rank, office, or membership
