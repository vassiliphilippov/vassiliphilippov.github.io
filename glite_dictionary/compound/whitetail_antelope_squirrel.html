whitetail antelope squirrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪtˌteɪl ˈæntɪˌloʊp ˈskwɝːrəl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtteɪl ˈæntɪləʊp ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
a small species of ground squirrel native to the western United States, characterized by its white tail and quick, agile movements
small ground squirrel of western US

Examples

The whitetail antelope squirrel is often seen darting among rocks and shrubs in arid regions.
Unlike many other squirrels, the whitetail antelope squirrel is active during the hottest part of the day.
We spotted a family of whitetail antelope squirrels while hiking in Nevada.
