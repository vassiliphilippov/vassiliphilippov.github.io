stock farmer

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / stɒk ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
a farmer who breeds or raises livestock such as cattle, sheep, or pigs
farmer who raises livestock

Examples

The stock farmer checks on his herd of cattle every morning before sunrise.
Becoming a successful stock farmer requires knowledge of animal health and nutrition.
Many stock farmers also sell dairy products made from their livestock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livestock farmer - farmer who raises livestock; synonym of stock farmer
  • Antonym: arable farmer - farmer who grows crops, as opposed to raising animals
