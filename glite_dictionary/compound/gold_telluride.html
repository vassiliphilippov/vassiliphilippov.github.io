gold telluride

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊld tɛlˈjʊəˌraɪd / (AmE), / ɡəʊld tɛlˈjʊəraɪd / (BrE)
a compound of gold and tellurium with the chemical formula AuTe₂, occurring naturally as the mineral calaverite
compound of gold and tellurium (AuTe₂)

Examples

Calaverite is a naturally occurring form of gold telluride found in some gold deposits.
Scientists analyzed the properties of synthetic gold telluride in the laboratory.
The extraction of gold from gold telluride ores requires special techniques due to its chemical bonding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calaverite - a mineral form of gold telluride
  • Degree Lower: telluride - a compound of tellurium with another element
