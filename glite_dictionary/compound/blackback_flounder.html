blackback flounder

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbæk ˈflaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈblækˌbæk ˈflaʊndə / (BrE)
An edible flatfish found in North American waters, especially valued as a food fish during winter months.
a North American edible flatfish

Examples

The blackback flounder is especially prized by local fishermen in the winter months.
Many restaurants in New England feature dishes made with fresh blackback flounder.
He caught a large blackback flounder during their fishing trip off the coast of Maine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flounder - a type of flatfish from coastal waters
