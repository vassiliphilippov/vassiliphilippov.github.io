garlic chewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrlɪk ˈtʃuːər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk ˈtʃuːə / (BrE)
a person who frequently eats garlic, often resulting in strong breath
person who often eats garlic
