to illuminate the issue

1. VERB

/ ɪˈluːmɪˌneɪt ði ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪneɪt ði ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
to clarify or make a topic or problem easier to understand by providing explanation or insight
clarify or explain a topic

Examples

The professor's detailed analysis helped to illuminate the issue for the students.
Her report did much to illuminate the issue of climate change in the region.
Experts were invited to illuminate the issue during the panel discussion.

Synset Examples

Her speech helped to illuminate the issue for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to clarify - to make something clearer or easier to understand
  • Antonym: obscure the issue - to make a topic or problem less clear or harder to understand
