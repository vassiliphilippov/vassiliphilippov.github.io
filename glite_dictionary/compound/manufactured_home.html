manufactured home

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn.jəˌfæk.tʃɚd ˈhoʊm / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃəd ˈhəʊm / (BrE)
A large prefabricated house, built in a factory and transported to a site where it can be connected to utilities and used as permanent housing.
prefabricated house, movable and permanent

Examples

They decided to buy a manufactured home because it was more affordable than a traditional house.
The community consists mostly of manufactured homes and has a friendly atmosphere.
Their new manufactured home was delivered and set up within a single week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mobile home - a prefabricated home that can be transported but is often not as permanent as a manufactured home
  • Synonym: modular home - a prefabricated building consisting of repeated sections called modules
