fire door

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ dɔːr / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə dɔː / (BrE)
A door made of fire-resistant material, designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke between sections of a building.
fire-resistant door for blocking fire spread

Examples

Please ensure the fire door remains closed at all times for safety reasons.
The new building is equipped with an automatic fire door system on every floor.
During the inspection, he checked that each fire door was properly labeled and functional.
