mantis prawn

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn.tɪs prɔn / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tɪs prɑːn / (BrE)
A large, predatory marine crustacean with distinctive raptorial claws, also known as a mantis shrimp.
a type of large marine crustacean

Examples

Fishermen in Southeast Asia often catch mantis prawns for local markets.
The scientist studied the hunting behavior of the mantis prawn in its natural habitat.
Several restaurants feature dishes made with fresh mantis prawns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mantis shrimp - large predatory marine crustacean with powerful claws
