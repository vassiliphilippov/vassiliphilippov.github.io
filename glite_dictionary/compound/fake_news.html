fake news

1. NOUN

/ feɪk nuːz / (AmE), / feɪk njuːz / (BrE)
false or misleading information presented as news, often with the intention of deceiving people
false or misleading news reports

Examples

Many people were misled after reading the fake news article online.
During the election, several websites spread fake news to influence public opinion.
He accused the television channel of broadcasting fake news about the event.

Synset Examples

He joked that MSDNC was just fake news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disinformation - false information deliberately spread to deceive people
  • Antonym: news - newly received or noteworthy information, especially about recent events
