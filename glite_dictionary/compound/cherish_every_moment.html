to cherish every moment

1. VERB

/ ˈtʃɛrɪʃ ˈɛvri ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrɪʃ ˈɛvri ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
to value and appreciate each moment greatly, often with affection or gratitude
value each moment highly

Examples

He learned to cherish every moment with his family after his illness.
We should cherish every moment because life passes quickly.
She tried to cherish every moment during her vacation abroad.

Synset Examples

She cherished every moment of their last dance together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasure every moment - regard every moment as precious
