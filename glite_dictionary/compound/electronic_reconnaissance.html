electronic reconnaissance

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˌrɛkəˈnɑsəns / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˌrɛkəˈnɒsəns / (BrE)
The detection, identification, evaluation, and location of foreign electromagnetic emissions (excluding radioactive sources), particularly for military or intelligence purposes.
detection and analysis of foreign electromagnetic signals

Examples

The aircraft was equipped with sophisticated electronic reconnaissance systems to monitor enemy communications.
Advances in electronic reconnaissance have greatly improved the detection of radar installations.
During the operation, the team relied on electronic reconnaissance to map out hostile forces' positions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconnaissance - preliminary survey or exploration, especially for military purposes
