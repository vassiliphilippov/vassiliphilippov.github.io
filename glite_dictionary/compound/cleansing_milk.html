cleansing milk

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛnzɪŋ mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈklɛnzɪŋ mɪlk / (BrE)
A thick, gentle liquid used for cleaning the face, especially to remove makeup or dirt.
gentle liquid face cleanser

Examples

She uses cleansing milk every night to gently remove her makeup.
Always apply cleansing milk to damp skin for better results.
Many people with sensitive skin prefer cleansing milk over foaming cleansers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleanser - a substance for cleaning the skin, especially the face
