sun rose

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn ˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌrəʊz / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Helianthemum, typically hardy flowering plants found in rocky or dry regions.
flowering plant of genus Helianthemum

Examples

The sun rose adds a splash of yellow to the rocky hillside each spring.
Several varieties of sun rose thrive in well-drained, sandy soil.
Gardeners often choose sun rose for its drought resistance and long blooming season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rockrose - another common name for plants of the genus Helianthemum
