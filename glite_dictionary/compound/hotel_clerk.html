hotel clerk

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl klɜrk / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl klɑːk / (BrE)
a person who works at the front desk of a hotel, greeting guests and handling reservations, check-ins, and check-outs
front desk worker in a hotel

Examples

The hotel clerk greeted us with a warm smile when we arrived late at night.
She asked the hotel clerk for directions to the nearest restaurant.
A friendly hotel clerk helped us check in and answered all our questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptionist - person who greets visitors and answers phones in an office or hotel
