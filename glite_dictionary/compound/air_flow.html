air flow

1. NOUN

/ ˈer ˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌfləʊ / (BrE)
the movement of air, especially as controlled or measured in a particular space or system
movement of air

Examples

The air flow in the room was improved by opening a window.
Engineers tested the air flow to ensure proper ventilation in the new building.
A blocked vent can reduce air flow and make the system less efficient.

Synset Examples

she adjusted the fan so that the airflow was directed right at her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air current - a flow of air, especially a steady movement of air in one direction
