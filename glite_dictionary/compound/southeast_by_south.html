southeast by south

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˈist baɪ ˈsaʊθ / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːst baɪ ˈsaʊθ / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point south of southeast, corresponding to a direction of 157°30′ clockwise from due north
compass point between southeast and south-southeast

Examples

The ship altered its course to southeast by south to avoid the storm.
The wind was blowing steadily from the southeast by south.
He consulted his compass and set a bearing of southeast by south for the journey.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: southeast - the compass point midway between south and east
  • Degree Higher: south-southeast - the compass point between south and southeast
