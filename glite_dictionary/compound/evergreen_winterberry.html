evergreen winterberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡrin ˈwɪntərˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌɡriːn ˈwɪntəbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen holly shrub of eastern North America with oblong leathery leaves and small black berries
evergreen North American holly with black berries

Examples

The evergreen winterberry provides year-round greenery in many gardens across eastern North America.
Birds are attracted to the small black berries of the evergreen winterberry during the colder months.
We planted an evergreen winterberry hedge along the back fence for privacy and decoration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - a type of shrub or tree with glossy leaves and berries, genus Ilex
