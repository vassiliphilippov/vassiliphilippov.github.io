personal identification number

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɜrsənl aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
A secret numerical code chosen by an individual and used for verifying identity to access accounts or conduct electronic transactions.
secret code for account access

Examples

Please enter your personal identification number to access your bank account.
Never share your personal identification number with anyone to keep your information secure.
I forgot my personal identification number and had to reset it online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PIN - abbreviation for personal identification number

Etymology

Formed from 'personal', 'identification', and 'number'; commonly abbreviated as 'PIN'.
