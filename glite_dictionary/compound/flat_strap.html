flat strap

1. NOUN

/ flæt stræp / (AmE), / flæt strap / (BrE)
a strap that is thin and smooth, with a flat rather than rounded or padded shape
a thin, flat-shaped strap

Examples

The backpack features a comfortable flat strap that doesn't dig into your shoulder.
Her watch has a sleek flat strap made of black leather.
He replaced the worn-out handle with a sturdy flat strap for easier carrying.

Synset Examples

I prefer a flat strap over bulky options.
The bag has a flat strap that's easy to carry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strap - a strip of material used for fastening or holding
  • Antonym: padded strap - a strap with added cushioning for comfort
