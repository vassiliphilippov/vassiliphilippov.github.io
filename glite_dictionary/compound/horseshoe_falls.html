Horseshoe Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɔrsoʊ ˈfɔlz / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsʃuː ˈfɔːlz / (BrE)
the largest section of Niagara Falls, located primarily on the Canadian side in Ontario and known for its distinctive horseshoe shape
main section of Niagara Falls in Ontario

Examples

Millions of tourists visit Horseshoe Falls every year to witness its stunning beauty.
The mist rising from Horseshoe Falls can be seen from miles away on a sunny day.
During our trip to Canada, we took a boat tour that got close to Horseshoe Falls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Niagara Falls - the collective waterfalls on the border of Ontario, Canada, and New York, USA
