Mexican black cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛksɪkən blæk ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən blæk ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a species of cherry tree native to Mexico, noted for its dark-colored fruit
species of cherry tree with dark fruit native to Mexico

Examples

The Mexican black cherry thrives in warm climates and produces deep purple, almost black, fruit.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of the Mexican black cherry tree.
The market sells preserves made from Mexican black cherry each summer.
