feather-brain idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðɚ ˌbreɪn aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə ˌbreɪn aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
A silly or foolish thought or notion.
foolish or silly idea
informal

Examples

She came up with another feather-brain idea that made everyone laugh.
His feather-brain idea was quickly dismissed by the team.
Every time we try to plan something, he suggests some feather-brain idea.

Synset Examples

His feather-brain ideas often lead to silly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silly idea - an idea that lacks sense or seriousness

Etymology

From 'feather-brain', meaning a frivolous or scatterbrained person, combined with 'idea'.
