hollow ache

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑloʊ eɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɒləʊ eɪk / (BrE)
a deep, persistent pain that feels empty or desolate, often emotional rather than physical
deep emotional pain or emptiness

Examples

Even years later, a hollow ache remained whenever she thought of her childhood home.
He tried to smile, but the hollow ache in his chest lingered.
Hearing the old song brought back a hollow ache he thought he had forgotten.

Synset Examples

She felt a hollow ache in her heart after the breakup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ache - a continuous or prolonged dull pain
