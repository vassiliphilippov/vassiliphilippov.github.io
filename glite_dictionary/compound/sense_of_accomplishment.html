sense of accomplishment

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv əˈkɑmplɪʃmənt / (AmE), / sɛns əv əˈkɒmplɪʃmənt / (BrE)
a feeling of pride and satisfaction that comes after achieving something
feeling of pride after achievement

Examples

Many athletes experience a sense of accomplishment when they break their personal records.
After finishing the marathon, she was overwhelmed by a sense of accomplishment.
Learning a new language can give you a strong sense of accomplishment.

Synset Examples

He felt a sense of accomplishment after the gor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pride - a feeling of deep pleasure or satisfaction derived from achievements
  • Antonym: sense of failure - a feeling of disappointment from not achieving something
