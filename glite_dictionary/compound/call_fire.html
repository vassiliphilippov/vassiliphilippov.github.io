call fire

1. NOUN

/ kɔl faɪɚ / (AmE), / kɔːl faɪə / (BrE)
fire directed at a specific target in response to a request from a military unit
requested military fire on a target

Examples

The commander requested call fire to suppress the enemy position.
Effective call fire was essential during the battle to support advancing troops.
The unit relied on call fire whenever they encountered unexpected resistance.
