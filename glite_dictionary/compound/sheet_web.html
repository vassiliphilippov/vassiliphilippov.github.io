sheet web

1. NOUN

/ ʃit wɛb / (AmE), / ʃiːt wɛb / (BrE)
A spider web that forms a flat, irregular sheet, often resembling a flattened hammock.
flat, irregular spider web

Examples

The garden hedge was covered in sheet webs glistening with morning dew.
Some spiders construct sheet webs close to the ground to catch crawling insects.
I noticed a sheet web stretched between the branches, looking like a delicate hammock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cobweb - a spider web, especially when old and dusty
