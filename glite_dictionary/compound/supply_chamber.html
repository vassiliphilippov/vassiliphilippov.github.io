supply chamber

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪ ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a compartment or device designed to hold something and release or supply it as needed, especially in machinery or equipment
compartment for storing and supplying material

Examples

The printer's ink supply chamber needs to be refilled regularly for smooth operation.
The water flowed from the reservoir into a supply chamber before being distributed.
During maintenance, technicians inspected the supply chamber for blockages or leaks.
