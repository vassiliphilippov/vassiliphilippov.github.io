white goods

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ɡʊdz / (AmE), / waɪt ɡʊdz / (BrE)
Household linen or textiles, typically made of white cloth, such as sheets, towels, and tablecloths.
household linens made of white cloth

Examples

The store offers a wide selection of white goods including bed sheets and pillowcases.
After the wedding, they received several sets of white goods as gifts.
She washed all the white goods before putting them in the linen closet.

/ waɪt ɡʊdz / (AmE), / waɪt ɡʊdz / (BrE)

Examples
