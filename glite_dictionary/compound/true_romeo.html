true Romeo

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈroʊmiˌoʊ / (AmE), / truː ˈrəʊmiəʊ / (BrE)
A man who is a passionate or ideal lover, especially one who pursues romantic love with devotion, referencing Shakespeare's character Romeo.
passionate or ideal lover

Examples

Everyone at school knew he was a true Romeo who left love notes in every locker.
She found her true Romeo during a summer trip to France.
Despite his reputation, Mark proved to be a true Romeo when he serenaded Emily under her window.

Synset Examples

He declared his love for her like a true Romeo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romeo - an ideal male lover or suitor, especially one who is passionate

Etymology

Reference to Romeo, the romantic hero in Shakespeare's play 'Romeo and Juliet' (1597).
