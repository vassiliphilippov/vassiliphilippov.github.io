baby rabies

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˈreɪ.biz / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˈreɪ.biːz / (BrE)
an exaggerated, humorous term for an intense or overwhelming desire to have a baby or to show affection toward babies
humorous term for intense baby affection or desire
informal

Examples

Every time she sees a toddler, she jokes that she has baby rabies.
His friends teased him about having baby rabies after he spent all afternoon playing with his niece.
After visiting the maternity ward, she admitted she was struck by a bout of baby rabies.
