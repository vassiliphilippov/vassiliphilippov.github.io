business relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs rɪˌleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs rɪˌleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a formal arrangement between two or more parties to provide regular professional or commercial services, often governed by a contract
formal commercial arrangement

Examples

Our company established a business relationship with a major European supplier last year.
Trust is essential in any successful business relationship.
They decided to formalize their business relationship with a written contract.

Synset Examples

he asked to see the executive who handled his account

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partnership - a business owned by two or more people
  • Synonym: client relationship - the association between a service provider and its client
