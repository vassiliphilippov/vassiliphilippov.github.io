computer circuit

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtər ˈsɜːrkɪt / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
an electrical circuit that is part of a computer and responsible for performing specific functions such as processing, memory, or input/output operations
electrical circuit in a computer

Examples

The motherboard contains several computer circuits responsible for managing data flow.
Many computer circuits are integrated into a single chip for efficiency.
A malfunction in one computer circuit can affect the entire system's performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electronic circuit - a circuit consisting of electronic components
