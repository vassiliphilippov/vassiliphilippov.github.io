shotgun shell

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn ʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtɡʌn ʃɛl / (BrE)
A cartridge containing lead shot or other projectiles, designed to be fired from a shotgun.
ammunition cartridge for a shotgun

Examples

He loaded a shotgun shell into his old hunting rifle before heading into the woods.
The shotgun shells were scattered across the floor after target practice.
Manufacturers are developing non-lead shotgun shells to be more environmentally friendly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartridge - a casing containing a charge and a bullet or shot for firearms
