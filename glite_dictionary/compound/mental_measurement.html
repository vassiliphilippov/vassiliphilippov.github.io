mental measurement

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.təl ˈmɛʒ.ər.mənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl ˈmɛʒ.ə.mənt / (BrE)
the process or technique of quantifying mental functions or abilities, such as intelligence, memory, or aptitude, using standardized tests or assessments
quantification of mental abilities or functions

Examples

Psychologists rely on mental measurement to assess cognitive development in children.
Modern mental measurement techniques have improved the accuracy of personality assessments.
The field of psychometrics is devoted to refining mental measurement tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychometrics - the science of measuring mental capacities and processes
