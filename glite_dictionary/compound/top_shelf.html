top shelf

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ʃɛlf / (AmE), / tɒp ʃɛlf / (BrE)
the highest shelf in a storage space; (informal) of the highest quality or class
the highest shelf; the best quality

Examples

The cookies are on the top shelf, out of reach of the children.
He always buys top shelf whiskey for special occasions.
Her art collection is truly top shelf, featuring works by renowned masters.

Synset Examples

She climbed the ladder to reach the top shelf.
Her top shelf collection includes many rare wines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premium - of superior quality or value
  • Antonym: second-rate - of inferior or lesser quality
