vanilla ice cream

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɪlə aɪs ˈkrim / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlə aɪs ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A sweet frozen food made from cream or milk and flavored with vanilla extract.
ice cream flavored with vanilla

Examples

He ordered a bowl of vanilla ice cream for dessert.
Would you like some vanilla ice cream on your apple pie?
The chef made homemade vanilla ice cream using fresh ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: chocolate ice cream - ice cream flavored with chocolate
  • Antonym: strawberry ice cream - ice cream flavored with strawberry
