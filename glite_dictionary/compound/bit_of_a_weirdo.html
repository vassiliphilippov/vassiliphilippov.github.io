bit of a weirdo

1. NOUN

/ bɪt əv ə ˈwɪɚdoʊ / (AmE), / bɪt əv ə ˈwɪədəʊ / (BrE)
A person who is somewhat strange or unconventional.
somewhat strange or unconventional person
informal

Examples

My brother is a bit of a weirdo when it comes to his food choices.
People say Tom is a bit of a weirdo, but I think he's just creative.
She always thought of herself as a bit of a weirdo for liking old horror movies.

Synset Examples

Everyone in the class thought she was a bit of a weirdo.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: weirdo - a person who is very strange or eccentric
