medicinal drug

1. NOUN

/ məˈdɪsənl ˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / mɪˈdɪsɪn(ə)l ˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
a substance used to treat, prevent, or alleviate the symptoms of disease
substance for medical treatment
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed a new medicinal drug to help control the patient's blood pressure.
Many medicinal drugs undergo years of testing before becoming available to the public.
This medicinal drug is used mainly to alleviate symptoms of seasonal allergies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine - substance used to treat or prevent disease
  • Synonym: drug - any substance used in diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease
