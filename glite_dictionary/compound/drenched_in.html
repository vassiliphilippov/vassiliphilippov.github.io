drenched in

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrɛntʃt ɪn / (AmE), / ˈdrɛntʃt ɪn / (BrE)
completely covered or filled with a particular quality, substance, or thing, often used to describe something being enveloped or suffused
completely covered or filled with something

Examples

The city was drenched in color during the vibrant festival.
