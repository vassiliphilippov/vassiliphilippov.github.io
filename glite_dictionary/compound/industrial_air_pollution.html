industrial air pollution

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl ɛr pəˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl eə pəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
The contamination of the atmosphere caused by emissions from factories and industrial plants.
pollution from factories in the air

Examples

Many cities struggle with industrial air pollution due to the presence of factories and power plants.
Regulations were introduced to limit industrial air pollution in order to protect public health.
The smog was largely caused by industrial air pollution from nearby manufacturing facilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air pollution - the contamination of the atmosphere by harmful substances
