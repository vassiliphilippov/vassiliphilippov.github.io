strong antagonist

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ ænˈtæɡənɪst / (AmE), / strɒŋ ænˈtæɡənɪst / (BrE)
a character in a story, film, or play who powerfully opposes the hero
powerful opponent of the hero

Examples

The film's strong antagonist kept the audience on edge until the final scene.
Every great hero needs a strong antagonist to test their resolve.
The novel's plot was driven by a strong antagonist with complex motives.

Synset Examples

The story featured a strong antagonist who challenged the hero.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antagonist - a character who opposes the main character
  • Antonym: protagonist - the main or leading character, often the hero
