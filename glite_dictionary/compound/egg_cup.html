egg cup

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡ kʌp / (AmE), / ɛɡ kʌp / (BrE)
a small cup-shaped dish used for holding and serving a boiled egg, usually upright in its shell
small cup for a boiled egg

Examples

She placed the soft-boiled egg in an egg cup before serving breakfast.
The antique porcelain egg cup was part of her grandmother's collection.
I bought a set of colorful egg cups for our brunch table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup - a small round container used for drinking or serving food
