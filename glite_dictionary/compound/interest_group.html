interest group

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntrəst ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəst ɡruːp / (BrE)
an organized group of people who share common aims and seek to influence public policy or decisions related to their field of activity
group seeking to influence policy

Examples

The environmental interest group organized a rally to push for new climate legislation.
Several interest groups submitted proposals to the government regarding healthcare reform.
Education policy was heavily influenced by teacher interest groups during the debate.

Synset Examples

the iron interests stepped up production

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pressure group - a group that tries to influence public policy in the interest of a particular cause
  • Synonym: lobby - a group of people seeking to influence legislators
