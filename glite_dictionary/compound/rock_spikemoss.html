rock spikemoss

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈspaɪkˌmɔs / (AmE), / rɒk ˈspaɪkˌmɒs / (BrE)
A tufted spikemoss (Selaginella rupestris) that forms loose, spreading mats, native to eastern North America.
tufted spikemoss of eastern North America

Examples

The dry cliffside was covered with patches of rock spikemoss.
Botanists identified the dense green mats as rock spikemoss during their field trip.
Because rock spikemoss prefers rocky habitats, it's rarely found in wooded areas.
