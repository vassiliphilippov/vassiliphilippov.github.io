quantum bogosort

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɑn.təm ˈboʊ.ɡoʊ.sɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.təm ˈbəʊ.ɡəʊ.sɔːt / (BrE)
A fictitious or humorously named extremely inefficient sorting algorithm, often mentioned in programming discussions as a parody of impractical computational approaches.
humorous inefficient sorting algorithm
informal

Etymology

From 'quantum', referencing quantum computation, and 'bogosort', a joke name for inefficient sorting algorithms.
