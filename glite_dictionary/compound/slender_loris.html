slender loris

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɛndər ˈlɔrɪs / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˈlɒrɪs / (BrE)
a small, slim-bodied primate native to southern India and Sri Lanka, known for its large eyes and slow movements
slim-bodied primate of India and Sri Lanka

Examples

The slender loris spends most of its life in trees, hunting insects at night.
Deforestation is threatening the habitat of the slender loris in southern India.
Children were fascinated by the big-eyed slender loris at the wildlife sanctuary.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: loris - a small, slow-moving primate of South and Southeast Asia
