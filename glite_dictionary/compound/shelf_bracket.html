shelf bracket

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛlf ˈbræk.ɪt / (AmE), / ʃɛlf ˈbræk.ɪt / (BrE)
a metal or wooden support fixed to a wall to hold up a shelf
support for a shelf

Examples

He used a sturdy shelf bracket to mount the wooden shelf on the wall.
The instructions recommend installing two shelf brackets for extra stability.
After replacing the old shelf bracket, the shelf no longer sagged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracket - a support projecting from a wall to hold a shelf or other weight
