stomach sweetbread

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌmək ˈswiːtbɹɛd / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək ˈswiːtbɹɛd / (BrE)
the edible pancreas of an animal, often used as food
edible animal pancreas (food)

Examples

She ordered stomach sweetbread at the upscale French restaurant.
Chefs often soak stomach sweetbread in milk before cooking to enhance its delicate flavor.
The recipe calls for fresh stomach sweetbread, sautéed with herbs and white wine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweetbread - the edible thymus or pancreas of an animal

Etymology

From 'sweetbread'—a culinary term referring to the edible glands of animals, traditionally the thymus or pancreas.
