acetate rayon

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsəˌteɪt ˈreɪ.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈæsɪteɪt ˈreɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
a synthetic fabric made from cellulose acetate fibers, known for its smooth texture and lustrous appearance
synthetic fabric made from cellulose acetate

Examples

Her evening gown was made of shimmering acetate rayon that caught the light beautifully.
Manufacturers often choose acetate rayon for linings due to its silky feel and affordable cost.
The label indicated that the scarf was composed entirely of acetate rayon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rayon - a synthetic fiber made from regenerated cellulose

Etymology

From 'acetate', referring to cellulose acetate, and 'rayon', from French 'rayon' (ray, beam).
