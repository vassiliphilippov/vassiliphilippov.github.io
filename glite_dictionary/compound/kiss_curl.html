kiss curl

1. NOUN

/ kɪs kɝl / (AmE), / kɪs kɜːl / (BrE)
A small lock of hair curled and styled to lie flat against the forehead or cheek, often as a decorative feature.
small curled lock of hair on forehead or cheek

Examples

She styled a kiss curl on her forehead for the vintage party.
The actress was known for the kiss curls that framed her face in old photographs.
He used a little gel to create a perfect kiss curl above his eyebrow.
