stabbingly harsh

1. ADJ

/ ˈstæbɪŋli hɑrʃ / (AmE), / ˈstæbɪŋli hɑːʃ / (BrE)
Extremely critical or severe in a way that feels piercing or acutely painful.
extremely critical or piercingly severe

Examples

The critic's stabbingly harsh comments left the actor devastated.
She remembered her teacher's stabbingly harsh words for years.
The stabbingly harsh winter wind made walking almost unbearable.

Synset Examples

The stabbingly harsh review surprised the author.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scathing - severely critical
  • Antonym: gentle - kind or mild, not harsh
