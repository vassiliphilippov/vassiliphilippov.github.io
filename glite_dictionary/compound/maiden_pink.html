maiden pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdn pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn pɪŋk / (BrE)
A small, low-growing Eurasian plant of the pink family, noted for its single pale pink flowers with crimson centers.
a Eurasian pink-flowered plant

Examples

The rock garden featured a cluster of maiden pink blooming among the stones.
Botanists admire the delicate charm of maiden pink for ornamental use in gardens.
The maiden pink thrives in well-drained, sandy soils with plenty of sunlight.
