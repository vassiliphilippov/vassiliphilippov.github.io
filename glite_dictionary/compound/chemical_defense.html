chemical defense

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
measures or procedures taken to protect against or respond to attacks or hazards involving chemical agents
protective actions against chemical agents

Examples

The army implemented a new chemical defense strategy to protect its soldiers from toxic gas attacks.
Training in chemical defense is mandatory for all laboratory personnel working with hazardous substances.
The city upgraded its chemical defense systems in response to concerns about industrial accidents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological defense - measures taken to protect against biological agents
  • Synonym: nuclear defense - measures taken to protect against nuclear threats
