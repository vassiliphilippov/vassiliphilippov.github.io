health benefit

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ ˈbɛnəfɪt / (AmE), / hɛlθ ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
a positive effect or advantage that something has on a person's physical or mental well-being
positive effect on health

Examples

Eating more vegetables can provide significant health benefits over time.
One of the main health benefits of regular exercise is improved cardiovascular function.
Green tea is often praised for its numerous health benefits in popular media.

Synset Examples

She prefers brown bread for its health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: health risk - possibility of harm to health
  • Synonym: benefit - an advantage or profit gained from something
