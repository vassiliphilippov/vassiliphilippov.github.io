Welsh comb

1. NOUN

/ wɛlʃ kʌm / (AmE), / wɛlʃ kɒm / (BrE)
a type of wide-toothed comb, traditionally from Wales, used for untangling hair, especially curls
wide-toothed hair comb from Wales

Examples

The Welsh comb is perfect for detangling curls.
She inherited a beautiful antique Welsh comb from her grandmother.
He always uses a Welsh comb to keep his thick hair tangle-free.

Synset Examples

The Welsh comb is perfect for detangling curls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - a toothed device for arranging or smoothing hair

Etymology

Named for its origin in Wales, where such combs were traditionally used.
