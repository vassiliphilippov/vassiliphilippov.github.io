pointing out

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔɪntɪŋ ˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˌpɔɪntɪŋ ˈaʊt / (BrE)
the act of drawing attention to something by indicating or demonstrating it
the act of indicating or demonstrating

Examples

The pointing out of the error saved the company from making a costly mistake.
She appreciated his pointing out the quickest route to the station.
Without his pointing out, I would have missed the relevant details in the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indication - the act of pointing out or making known
