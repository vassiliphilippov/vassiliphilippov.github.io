stone breaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊn ˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstəʊn ˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to break stones, especially as part of construction or road work
person who breaks stones for a living

Examples

The stone breaker spent hours each day smashing rocks at the construction site.
In the old photograph, a stone breaker is seen working with a hammer beside a gravel road.
Many stone breakers worked under the hot sun to prepare the foundation for the new railway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarryman - a person who works in a quarry extracting stone
