deep kiss

1. NOUN

/ dip kɪs / (AmE), / diːp kɪs / (BrE)
An open-mouthed kiss in which one's tongue is inserted into the partner's mouth.
passionate open-mouthed kiss with tongues

Examples

Their romantic evening ended with a long, lingering deep kiss by the fireplace.
She leaned in and surprised him with a passionate deep kiss during the movie.
A deep kiss can be an intimate way to show affection between lovers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French kiss - an open-mouthed kiss in which the tongues touch; synonym of deep kiss
