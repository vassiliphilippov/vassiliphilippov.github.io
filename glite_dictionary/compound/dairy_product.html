dairy product

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛri ˌprɑdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈdeəri ˌprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
A food produced from the milk of mammals, such as butter, cheese, or yogurt.
food made from milk

Examples

People who are lactose intolerant should avoid most dairy products.
Yogurt is a popular dairy product enjoyed around the world.
Many recipes call for at least one dairy product, such as cheese or cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk product - food produced from milk
  • Antonym: non-dairy - not containing milk or milk products
