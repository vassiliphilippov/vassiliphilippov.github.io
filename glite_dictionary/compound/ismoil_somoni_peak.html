Ismoil Somoni Peak

1. PROPN

/ isˈmoʊil soʊˈmoʊni piːk / (AmE), / ɪsˈmɔɪl səˈmɒni piːk / (BrE)
the highest mountain in the Pamir Mountains, located in northeastern Tajikistan near the Chinese border, with an elevation of 24,590 feet (7,495 meters)
highest peak in the Pamir Mountains, Tajikistan

Examples

Climbers from around the world have attempted to summit Ismoil Somoni Peak since its discovery.
The breathtaking landscape around Ismoil Somoni Peak attracts adventurers and scientists alike.
He trained for years before finally reaching the top of Ismoil Somoni Peak in Tajikistan.

Etymology

Named after Ismoil Somoni, a prominent Tajik national hero and founder of the Samanid dynasty.
