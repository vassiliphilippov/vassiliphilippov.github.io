total shitsack

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.təl ˈʃɪt.sæk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈʃɪt.sæk / (BrE)
(Vulgar, offensive, informal) An extremely unpleasant, obnoxious, or contemptible person.
contemptible or obnoxious person (vulgar)
informal vulgar offensive

Everyone at the party thought Mark was being a total shitsack after he insulted the host.
Don't be a total shitsack just because things didn't go your way.
Even his friends admitted he could act like a total shitsack when he was in a bad mood.
