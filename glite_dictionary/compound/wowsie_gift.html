wowsie gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaʊzi ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈwaʊzi ɡɪft / (BrE)
An impressive or surprising gift, often causing excitement or astonishment.
impressive or surprising gift
informal

Examples

When I opened the box, I couldn't believe such a wowsie gift was waiting for me.
He surprised his wife with a wowsie gift for their anniversary.
That graduation party became unforgettable when her parents presented her with a wowsie gift.

She just received a wowsie gift from her best friend.
