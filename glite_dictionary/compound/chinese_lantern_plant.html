Chinese lantern plant

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃaɪniz ˈlæntɚn ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˌtʃaɪniːz ˈlæntən ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
an Old World perennial plant (Physalis alkekengi) grown for its ornamental, inflated, papery, orange-red calyx resembling a lantern
plant with lantern-like orange-red calyx

Examples

In the garden, the Chinese lantern plant adds a splash of vibrant orange among the greenery.
She dried the Chinese lantern plant to use its calyces in an autumn floral arrangement.
The unique appearance of the Chinese lantern plant makes it a favorite for ornamental gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Physalis - genus to which the Chinese lantern plant belongs
  • Synonym: bladder cherry - another common name for the Chinese lantern plant
