extraterrestrial being

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛk.strə.təˈrɛs.tri.əl ˈbiː.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɛk.strə.təˈrɛs.tri.əl ˈbiː.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A living creature believed or assumed to exist outside the Earth or its atmosphere.
life form from outside Earth

Examples

Many scientists believe it is possible that extraterrestrial beings exist somewhere in the universe.
The movie featured several kinds of extraterrestrial beings visiting Earth.
Reports of encounters with extraterrestrial beings have fascinated the public for decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alien - a being from another world

Etymology

From Latin 'extra' (outside) + 'terrestris' (of the earth), plus 'being'.
