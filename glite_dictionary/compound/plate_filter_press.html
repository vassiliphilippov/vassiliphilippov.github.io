plate filter press

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt ˈfɪltər prɛs / (AmE), / pleɪt ˈfɪltə prɛs / (BrE)
a type of filter press consisting of a series of plates and frames assembled alternately, used to separate solids from liquids through filtration
type of filter press with plates and frames

Examples

The plate filter press is widely used in industrial wastewater treatment for efficient dewatering of sludge.
During the sugaring process, a plate filter press can remove fine particles from maple syrup.
Engineers recommended installing a modern plate filter press to improve the filtration process in the factory.

Synset Examples

Plate filter press is extensively used in sugaring operations such as the production of maple syrup in Canada, since it offers very high efficiency and reliability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter press - an industrial machine used to separate solids and liquids using pressure filtration
