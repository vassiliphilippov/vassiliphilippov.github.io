drug company

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈkʌm.pəni / (BrE)

pharmaceutical manufacturing company

Examples

The drug company announced a breakthrough in cancer treatment yesterday.

After a merger, the new drug company quickly became an industry leader.
