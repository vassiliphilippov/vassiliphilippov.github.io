dance move

1. NOUN

/ dæns muːv / (AmE), / dɑːns muːv / (BrE)
a specific movement or step performed as part of a dance
a step or movement in a dance

Examples

She impressed everyone at the party with her new dance move.
He practiced the complicated dance move until he could do it perfectly.
The instructor demonstrated each dance move slowly for the beginners.

Synset Examples

The kids laughed at Og's silly dance moves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance step - a movement or sequence of movements making up a dance
