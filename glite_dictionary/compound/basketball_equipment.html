basketball equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪtˌbɔl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the objects and gear used when playing basketball, such as balls, hoops, and uniforms
objects used for playing basketball

Examples

The school purchased new basketball equipment for the team, including nets and basketballs.
Proper basketball equipment is required for safety and optimal performance during games.
She stored all her basketball equipment in a large duffel bag after practice.
