Battle of the Coral Sea

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ðə ˈkɔɹəl si / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ðə ˈkɒrəl siː / (BrE)
A major naval battle in May 1942 during World War II, marking the first clash fought entirely by aircraft launched from carriers, resulting in a Japanese defeat.
World War II naval battle, 1942

Examples

The Battle of the Coral Sea marked a turning point in the Pacific War.
Many historians study the strategy used in the Battle of the Coral Sea.
The outcome of the Battle of the Coral Sea prevented a Japanese invasion of Port Moresby.
