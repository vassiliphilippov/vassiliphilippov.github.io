bulletproof vest

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlɪtˌpruf vɛst / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːf vest / (BrE)
a sleeveless protective garment worn to absorb the impact of bullets or other projectiles
protective garment against bullets

Examples

The officer put on a bulletproof vest before heading out on duty.
Many security guards are required to wear a bulletproof vest while working in high-risk areas.
The suspect’s bulletproof vest saved his life during the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body armor - clothing designed to absorb or deflect physical attacks
