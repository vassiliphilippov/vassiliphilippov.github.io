straight off

1. ADV

/ streɪt ɑf / (AmE), / streɪt ɒf / (BrE)
immediately, without delay or hesitation.
immediately; without delay

Examples

She told me straight off that she wasn't interested in the job.
I recognized the address straight off when I saw the envelope.
If you have any questions, please ask straight off rather than waiting.
