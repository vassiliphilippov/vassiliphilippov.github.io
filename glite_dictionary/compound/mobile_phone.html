mobile phone

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.bəl foʊn / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.baɪl fəʊn / (BrE)
A portable telephone that connects to a cellular network, allowing calls and other communications while on the move.
portable cellular telephone

Examples

She forgot her mobile phone at home and missed several important calls.
Many people use their mobile phone to browse the internet and check emails.
His mobile phone battery died just as he was about to send a message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cell phone - a portable telephone that uses a cellular network
