film writer

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈraɪtər / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈraɪtə / (BrE)
a person who writes screenplays or scripts for films
person who writes film scripts

Examples

The film writer spent months developing the script for the new blockbuster.
After winning the award, she became one of Hollywood's most respected film writers.
Every aspiring film writer dreams of seeing their story on the big screen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screenwriter - a person who writes scripts for films or television
  • Synonym: playwright - a person who writes plays for the theater
