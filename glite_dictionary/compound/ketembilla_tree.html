ketembilla tree

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈtɛmbɪlə tri / (AmE), / kɪˈtɛmbɪlə triː / (BrE)
a small, spiny shrub native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, gooseberry-like pulp
tropical shrub with edible maroon-purple fruit

Examples

The ketembilla tree thrives in warm climates and produces attractive purple fruit.
Farmers in Sri Lanka often cultivate the ketembilla tree for its delicious berries.
The sweet pulp of the ketembilla tree is enjoyed both fresh and in preserves.
