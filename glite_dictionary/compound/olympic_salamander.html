olympic salamander

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɪmpɪk səˈlæməndɚ / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpɪk səˈlæməndə / (BrE)
a small, large-eyed, semiaquatic salamander native to the northwestern United States
a small salamander from the US Northwest

Examples

The olympic salamander is often found in moist forests near streams in Washington State.
Scientists are studying how habitat loss affects the olympic salamander population.
We spotted several olympic salamanders hiding under logs during our hike.
