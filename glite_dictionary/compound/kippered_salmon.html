kippered salmon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪpɚd ˈsæmən / (AmE), / ˈkɪpəd ˈsæmən / (BrE)
Salmon that has been cured by salting and smoking.
smoked and salted salmon

Examples

We served kippered salmon with scrambled eggs for breakfast.
The flavor of kippered salmon is richer than that of fresh fish.
She bought a package of kippered salmon at the gourmet market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoked salmon - salmon that has been smoked, typically not salted
  • Antonym: fresh salmon - salmon that has not been smoked or cured

Etymology

From 'kipper' (to cure fish by salting and smoking) + 'salmon'. 'Kipper' from Old English 'cypera', meaning to preserve fish.
