flat cap

1. NOUN

/ flæt kæp / (AmE), / flæt kap / (BrE)
a rounded cap with a small stiff brim in front, typically made of wool or tweed.
a rounded cap with a stiff front brim

Examples

He wore a flat cap and a tweed jacket to complete the look.
Many older men in the countryside still favor the traditional flat cap.
A flat cap is often associated with classic British style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsboy cap - a similar rounded cap with a fuller body and button top
