coffee rose

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi roʊz / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi rəʊz / (BrE)
A tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant, night-blooming flowers with wavy petals, native to northern India and Thailand.
tropical shrub with fragrant, wavy-petaled flowers

Examples

The coffee rose is prized in gardens for its fragrant nocturnal blossoms.
Botanists documented a new variety of coffee rose in northern Thailand.
The glossy leaves of the coffee rose make it attractive even when not in bloom.
