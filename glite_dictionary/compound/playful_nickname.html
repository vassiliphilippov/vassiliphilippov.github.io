playful nickname

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl ˈnɪkneɪm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl ˈnɪkneɪm / (BrE)
a friendly or humorous name given to someone, often used lightheartedly rather than formally
humorous or friendly informal name

Examples

He always uses a playful nickname for his teammates during practice.
Her playful nickname at work made everyone smile.
When they were kids, their father had a playful nickname for each one.

Synset Examples

He calls his friend Hymie as a playful nickname.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nickname - an informal name given to someone or something, often descriptive
