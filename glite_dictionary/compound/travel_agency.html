travel agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a business that arranges transportation, accommodations, and itineraries for travelers
business arranging travel for people

Examples

She booked her vacation through a travel agency and found the process very convenient.
Most travel agencies offer package tours that include flights and hotels.
The couple visited a travel agency to arrange their honeymoon trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel agent - a person who works for a travel agency arranging journeys for customers
