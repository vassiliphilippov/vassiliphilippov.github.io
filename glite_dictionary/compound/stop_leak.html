stop leak

1. NOUN

/ stɑp lik / (AmE), / stɒp liːk / (BrE)
a liquid additive used to seal leaks in a vehicle's radiator temporarily
liquid for sealing radiator leaks

Examples

The mechanic suggested adding stop leak to the radiator to fix the problem temporarily.
Many drivers keep a bottle of stop leak in their trunk in case of emergencies.
Although stop leak can help with minor leaks, a damaged radiator may still need professional repair.

Synset Examples

Using stop leak is a quick solution to the problem, not a permanent repair.
