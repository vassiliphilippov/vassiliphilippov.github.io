Marshall Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑrʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃəl ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of islands and atolls in the central Pacific Ocean, forming an independent country in Micronesia
country in the central Pacific Ocean

Examples

She traveled to the Marshall Islands to study coral reefs and marine biodiversity.
The Marshall Islands gained independence from the United States in 1986.
Rising sea levels threaten the very existence of the Marshall Islands.

Etymology

Named after British explorer John Marshall.
