galactic spaceship

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈlæktɪk ˈspeɪsˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ɡəˈlæktɪk ˈspeɪsʃɪp / (BrE)
a spacecraft designed or imagined for travel between galaxies or through the galaxy, often found in science fiction
spacecraft for intergalactic travel (sci-fi)

Examples

The movie featured a massive galactic spaceship traveling at the speed of light.
Scientists often debate the feasibility of building a real galactic spaceship in the future.
He drew sketches of an advanced galactic spaceship in his notebook during class.

Synset Examples

He dreams of traveling on a galactic spaceship one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spaceship - a vehicle used for travel in outer space
