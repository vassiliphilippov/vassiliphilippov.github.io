number one wood

1. NOUN

/ ˌnʌmbɚ ˈwʌn wʊd / (AmE), / ˌnʌmbə ˈwʌn wʊd / (BrE)
a golf club (the driver) with a near-vertical face, used for hitting the longest shots from the tee
golf club for long tee shots (driver)

Examples

He pulled out his number one wood to start the tournament with a powerful drive.
The number one wood is often called the driver by modern golfers.
She hit the ball straight down the fairway with her number one wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driver - the main golf club used for long tee shots
