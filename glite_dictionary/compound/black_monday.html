Black Monday

1. PROPN

/ blæk ˈmʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / blæk ˈmʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
the name given to October 19, 1987, when global stock markets crashed sharply, marking a significant event in financial history
the 1987 stock market crash

Examples

The crash of Black Monday had lasting effects on the world economy.
Financial analysts still study Black Monday for clues about market instability.
Many investors lost confidence after experiencing Black Monday.
