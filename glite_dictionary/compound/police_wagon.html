police wagon

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
A van or vehicle used by the police to transport prisoners.
police vehicle for transporting prisoners

Examples

The police wagon arrived at the scene to take the suspects into custody.
After the protest, several people were loaded into the police wagon.
The old police wagon was parked in front of the courthouse, waiting to transport prisoners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paddy wagon - van used by police to transport prisoners
  • Synonym: prison van - vehicle used to transfer prisoners
