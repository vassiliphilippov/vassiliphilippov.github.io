nalidixic acid

1. NOUN

/ ˌnælɪˈdɪksɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌnælɪˈdɪksɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
an antibacterial agent used especially to treat infections of the urinary tract
antibacterial agent for urinary infections
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed nalidixic acid to treat the patient's urinary tract infection.
Resistance to nalidixic acid has been reported in some bacterial strains.
Pharmacists must label nalidixic acid carefully due to potential side effects.
