carbohydrate intake

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑr.boʊˈhaɪ.dreɪt ˈɪn.teɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.bəˈhaɪ.dreɪt ˈɪn.teɪk / (BrE)
the amount of carbohydrates consumed as part of a diet, usually measured to monitor or manage nutrition, health, or performance
the amount of carbohydrates consumed

Examples

She carefully tracks her carbohydrate intake to help manage her blood sugar levels.
Athletes often adjust their carbohydrate intake before major competitions to optimize performance.
Nutritionists recommend moderating your carbohydrate intake as part of a balanced diet.

Synset Examples

She counts her carbohydrate intake while dieting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carbohydrate - a type of nutrient found in foods; a source of energy
  • Synonym: protein intake - the amount of protein consumed
  • Synonym: daily allowance - recommended daily limit or amount of a nutrient
