element of surprise

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləmənt əv səˈpraɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪmənt əv səˈprʌɪz / (BrE)
an unexpected feature or factor that makes something surprising or catches people off guard
unexpected feature that causes surprise

Examples

The sudden thunderstorm added an element of surprise to the summer picnic.
The chef introduced an element of surprise by including an unusual ingredient in the meal.
Using misdirection, the director maintained an element of surprise throughout the entire film.

Synset Examples

She planned the party with an element of surprise for everyone.
The magician's trick was all about the element of surprise.
The twist at the end added an element of surprise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surprise - the feeling caused by something unexpected
