police department

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis dɪˌpɑɹtmənt / (AmE), / pəˈliːs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of a local government responsible for enforcing laws and preventing crime in an area
local law enforcement agency

Examples

The city established a new police department to better serve its residents.
The police department held a press conference about the recent crime wave.
She applied for a job at the local police department after graduating college.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police force - an organized body of police officers
