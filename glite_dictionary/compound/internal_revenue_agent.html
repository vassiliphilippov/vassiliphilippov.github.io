internal revenue agent

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌtɝːnəl ˈrɛvənju ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜːnəl ˈrɛvənjuː ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official whose job is to collect taxes on behalf of the government, especially as an employee of the U.S. Internal Revenue Service.
government tax collector (especially US)
american

Examples

After years as an internal revenue agent, she became an expert in tax law.
The internal revenue agent reviewed the company’s financial records for discrepancies.
If you receive a letter from an internal revenue agent, you should respond promptly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax collector - a person whose job is to collect taxes
