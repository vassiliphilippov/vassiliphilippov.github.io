golden gram

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ɡræm / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ɡræm / (BrE)
the seed of the mung bean plant, used as food in various cuisines
seed of the mung bean plant used as food

Examples

In many Asian recipes, golden gram is sprouted and used in salads or stir-fries.
The farmer harvested several bags of golden gram after the rainy season.
Health enthusiasts often add golden gram to their soups for extra protein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the seed of Vigna radiata used in cooking, also known as green gram
  • Synonym: green gram - another name for the mung bean or its seed
