written agreement

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪt̬.ən əˈɡriː.mənt / (AmE), / ˈrɪt.ən əˈɡriː.mənt / (BrE)
a document that records the terms agreed upon between two or more parties, typically legally binding
a document formalizing an agreement

Examples

Both companies signed a written agreement outlining the terms of their partnership.
The lease is a written agreement between the landlord and the tenant.
A written agreement can help prevent misunderstandings in a business deal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contract - a written or spoken agreement that is enforceable by law
