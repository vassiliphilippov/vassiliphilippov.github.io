the outside world

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈaʊtsaɪd wɜrld / (AmE), / ði ˈaʊtsaɪd wɜːld / (BrE)
the world outside one's immediate environment, especially beyond a place of seclusion or isolation
the world beyond one’s immediate surroundings

Examples

During her long recovery, she felt completely disconnected from the outside world.
News from the outside world made him eager to travel again.
Children in remote villages often have limited contact with the outside world.

Synset Examples

After months of isolation, he finally ventured into the outside world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: society - people in general considered as a community
  • Synonym: the public - ordinary people not involved in a particular situation
