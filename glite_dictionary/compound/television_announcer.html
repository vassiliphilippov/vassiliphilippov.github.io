television announcer

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən əˈnaʊnsər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən əˈnaʊnsə / (BrE)
a person who introduces television programs or provides commentary or information on television
person who announces on television

Examples

The television announcer introduced the evening news with a calm and professional tone.
During the awards ceremony, the television announcer kept the audience informed about the upcoming presentations.
Many viewers recognize the television announcer by his signature catchphrase at the end of each broadcast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio announcer - a person who introduces radio programs or provides commentary or information on radio
