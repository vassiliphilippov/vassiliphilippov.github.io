domestic sheep

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ʃip / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ʃiːp / (BrE)
a species of sheep domesticated and raised for wool, meat, or skin
domesticated species of sheep

Examples

There are many breeds of domestic sheep kept on farms around the world.
The wool from domestic sheep is used to make clothing and textiles.
Veterinarians study the health of domestic sheep to improve livestock care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheep - a domesticated ruminant animal with a thick woolly coat
