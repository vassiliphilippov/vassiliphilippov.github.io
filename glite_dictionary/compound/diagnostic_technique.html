diagnostic technique

1. NOUN

/ daɪ.əɡˈnɑs.tɪk tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əɡˈnɒs.tɪk tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a method or procedure used to determine the nature or cause of a medical condition through observation, measurement, or testing
method or procedure to diagnose medical conditions
medical

Examples

This new diagnostic technique allows doctors to detect infections in their earliest stages.
The hospital invested in advanced diagnostic techniques to improve patient outcomes.
Accurate diagnostic technique is essential for effective treatment of many diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagnostic test - a test performed to identify a disease or condition
  • Antonym: treatment - medical care given to manage or cure an illness
