housing estate

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪŋ ɪˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ ɪˌsteɪt / (BrE)
a group of houses or apartments, usually built at the same time and forming a single planned residential area
group of planned houses or apartments
british

Examples

They bought a house on a new housing estate just outside the city.
Many housing estates were built in the 1960s to provide affordable homes.
The local council plans to improve security across the housing estate.
