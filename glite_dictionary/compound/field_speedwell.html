field speedwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈfild ˈspidˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˈspiːdwɛl / (BrE)
A European plant with small blue flowers borne on long stalks, commonly found in fields and widely naturalized in North America.
a small-flowered blue field plant

Examples

A patch of field speedwell brightened the edge of the path with its tiny blue flowers.
Gardeners often struggle to control field speedwell in their lawns due to its rapid spread.
The botanist identified the bluish petals as those of field speedwell growing among the crops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speedwell - a plant of the genus Veronica, with small blue or white flowers
