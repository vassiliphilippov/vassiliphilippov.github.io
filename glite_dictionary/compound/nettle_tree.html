nettle tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛtl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈnɛtl ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Celtis, typically bearing inconspicuous flowers and small, berrylike fruits.
tree of the genus Celtis

Examples

The nettle tree produces small, dark berries that attract birds in late summer.
Some gardeners plant a nettle tree for its ornamental value and hardiness.
The leaves of the nettle tree are rough to the touch and somewhat resemble those of the common nettle.
