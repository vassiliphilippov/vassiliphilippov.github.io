olive family

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.lɪv ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ɪv ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of flowering trees and shrubs, including olives, ashes, jasmines, privets, and lilacs, usually producing berries, drupes, or capsules as fruit
family of trees and shrubs including olive and jasmine

Examples

The olive family includes familiar plants such as the lilac and the ash tree.
Many members of the olive family are valued for their ornamental flowers.
Jasmine, part of the olive family, is prized for its fragrant blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oleaceae - the scientific name for the olive family
