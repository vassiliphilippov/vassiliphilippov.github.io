phantasy world

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tə.si wɝːld / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si wɜːld / (BrE)
A world or reality that exists only in one's imagination, often believed to be real by the person experiencing it.
imaginary or illusory world

Examples

As a child, Sarah often retreated into her own phantasy world to escape reality.
He lives in a phantasy world, unable to accept the truth about his situation.
The novel explores a phantasy world created by the protagonist's vivid imagination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy world - an imaginary universe, especially in literature or games

Etymology

From 'phantasy', a variant of 'fantasy', ultimately from Greek 'phantasia' (appearance, imagination), and 'world'.
