redeye flight

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌaɪ ˌflaɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌaɪ ˌflaɪt / (BrE)
A late-night or overnight flight, typically arriving the next morning, that often leaves passengers tired or sleepless.
overnight flight causing sleep loss

Examples

I booked a redeye flight to save money and arrive early the next day.
Taking the redeye flight left me exhausted for my morning meeting.
She often prefers the redeye flight so she doesn't waste a whole day traveling.

Synset Examples

he took the redeye in order to get home the next morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: night flight - a flight that takes place at night

Etymology

From 'red-eye' (referring to red eyes from fatigue) + 'flight.'
