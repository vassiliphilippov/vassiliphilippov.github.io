camping bus

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpɪŋ bʌs / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪŋ bʌs / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle equipped with sleeping and cooking facilities, used for camping while traveling
large vehicle for camping travel

Examples

We drove across the country in a camping bus, stopping at different campsites each night.
The camping bus has enough beds and a kitchen area for the whole family.
They rented a camping bus for their summer road trip through the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camper - a motor vehicle or trailer equipped for camping
  • Synonym: recreational vehicle - a motor vehicle equipped with living space for recreation
