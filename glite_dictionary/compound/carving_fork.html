carving fork

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrvɪŋ fɔrk / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvɪŋ fɔːk / (BrE)
a large, two-pronged fork used to hold meat steady while carving it
large fork for carving meat

Examples

Please use the carving fork to hold the roast steady while I slice it.
He brought out a gleaming carving fork and a sharp knife to prepare the turkey.
The set includes a chef's knife and a matching carving fork for special occasions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fork - an eating or serving utensil with two or more prongs
