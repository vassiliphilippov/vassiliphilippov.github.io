pillow sham

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪloʊ ʃæm / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ ʃam / (BrE)
a decorative cover for a pillow, typically used as part of bedding to enhance appearance
decorative cover for a pillow

Examples

The decorative pillow sham matched the quilt on the guest bed.
She removed the pillow shams before settling in for the night.
Each bed was neatly made with a white pillow sham placed on top of the pillows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pillowcase - a removable cloth cover for a pillow, usually for protection and washing

Etymology

From sham, meaning 'false' or 'imitation', indicating a cover that is not part of the functional bedding.
