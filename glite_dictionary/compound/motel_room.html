motel room

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈtɛl ruːm / (AmE), / məʊˈtɛl ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a motel, typically furnished for sleeping and lodging guests temporarily
sleeping room in a motel

Examples

We reserved a motel room near the highway for our overnight stop.
The motel room was clean and had a comfortable bed.
She forgot her phone charger in the motel room and had to return to get it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hotel room - a room in a hotel for guests to stay in
  • Degree Higher: suite - a set of connected rooms, often in a hotel or motel
  • Degree Lower: room - a separate enclosed space within a building

Etymology

From 'motel', a blend of 'motor' and 'hotel', and 'room'.
