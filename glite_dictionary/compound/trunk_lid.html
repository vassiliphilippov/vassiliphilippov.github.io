trunk lid

1. NOUN

/ trʌŋk lɪd / (AmE), / trʌŋk lɪd / (BrE)
a hinged cover for the trunk of a vehicle or container, allowing access to the storage area
hinged cover for the trunk of a vehicle or container

Examples

She opened the trunk lid to put her groceries inside.
The mechanic fixed the hinge on the trunk lid so it would close properly.
Rainwater leaked through a gap in the trunk lid and soaked the spare tire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boot lid - cover for the storage compartment of a car in British English
