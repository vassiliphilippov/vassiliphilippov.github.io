drawing power

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪŋ ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
the ability to attract a large number of people, especially customers or supporters.
capacity to attract people

Examples

Despite his age, the actor's drawing power remains strong in the film industry.
The team's drawing power increased after they signed two star players.
The new restaurant quickly established its drawing power with delicious food and excellent service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appeal - the quality of being attractive or interesting
  • Synonym: pull - the power to attract people
