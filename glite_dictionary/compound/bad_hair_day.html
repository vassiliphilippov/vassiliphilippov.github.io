bad hair day

1. NOUN

/ bæd hɛr deɪ / (AmE), / bad hɛː deɪ / (BrE)
A day when one's hair is difficult to manage or looks untidy, often affecting one's mood or overall sense of well-being.
a day with unmanageable or untidy hair

Examples

I just threw on a cap because I was having a bad hair day.
Everyone has a bad hair day now and then, so don't feel embarrassed.
Despite her bad hair day, she still managed to look confident at the interview.

Synset Examples

On a bad hair day, I just tie it up.
She decided to wear a hat because it was a bad hair day.
I woke up late and had a bad hair day.
