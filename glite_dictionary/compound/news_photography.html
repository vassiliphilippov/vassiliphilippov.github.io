news photography

1. NOUN

/ nuz fəˈtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / njuːz fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the activity or practice of taking photographs that document newsworthy events, typically for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
photography documenting news events

Examples

He has worked in news photography for more than twenty years, covering major international events.
Thanks to advancements in technology, news photography can now instantly reach global audiences.
She won an award for excellence in news photography after capturing the aftermath of the hurricane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photojournalism - the practice of communicating news stories through photographs
