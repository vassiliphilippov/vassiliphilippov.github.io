cheese plane

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz pleɪn / (AmE), / tʃiːz pleɪn / (BrE)
a kitchen tool with a flat blade for producing thin, even slices of semi-hard or hard cheese
kitchen tool for slicing cheese thinly

Examples

She used a cheese plane to add thin slices to the platter.
A cheese plane is perfect for serving semi-hard cheeses like Gouda.
They bought a new cheese plane for their kitchen after the old one broke.

Synset Examples

A cheese slicer can be also used for slicing cold butter, zucchini, cucumber or cabbage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese slicer - a tool for slicing cheese, often with a similar design to a cheese plane
