loading zone

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊdɪŋ zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈləʊdɪŋ zəʊn / (BrE)
An area designated for vehicles to stop temporarily in order to load or unload goods or passengers.
area for temporary loading/unloading

Examples

Delivery trucks may use the loading zone outside the store from 8 AM to 6 PM.
The taxi waited for her in the loading zone near the airport entrance.
Parking in a loading zone is not allowed unless you are actively loading or unloading.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: no parking - area where parking is not permitted
  • Synonym: unloading zone - area designated mainly for unloading goods or passengers
