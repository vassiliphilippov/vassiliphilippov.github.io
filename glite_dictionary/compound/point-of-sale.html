point-of-sale

1. ADJ

/ pɔɪnt əv seɪl / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv seɪl / (BrE)
relating to the place where a retail transaction is completed, especially where payment is made and receipts are issued.
relating to a retail transaction location

Examples

The store upgraded its point-of-sale systems to speed up customer transactions.
Many retailers use digital displays for advertising at the point-of-sale.
A point-of-sale terminal can process multiple forms of electronic payment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: checkout - the place in a shop where you pay for your goods
  • Synonym: cash register - a machine used at the point of sale to record sales and handle cash
