ultimately expugnable

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌltɪmətli ɪkˈspjuːnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmətli ɪkˈspjuːnəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able, in the end or ultimately, to be defeated, disproved, or shown to be false or invalid.
able to be defeated or disproved in the end

Examples

Despite its apparent strength, the theory remained ultimately expugnable in light of new evidence.
No matter how compelling, the defense's case was deemed ultimately expugnable by the court.
Her reasoning sounded solid at first but turned out to be ultimately expugnable upon closer analysis.

Synset Examples

His arguments were strong but ultimately expugnable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expugnable - capable of being overcome or refuted
  • Antonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome

Etymology

From Latin 'expugnabilis' meaning 'that can be taken by storm or overcome', plus 'ultimately'.
