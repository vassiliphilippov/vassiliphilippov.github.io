gum accroides

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌm əˈkrɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ɡʌm əˈkrɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
an alcohol-soluble resin obtained from certain Australian trees, used in varnishes and in manufacturing paper
Australian tree resin used in varnish, papermaking
