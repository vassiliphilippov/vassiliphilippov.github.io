turkey drumstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrki ˈdrʌmstɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki ˈdrʌmstɪk / (BrE)
the lower part of a turkey's leg, typically cooked and eaten as food
lower leg portion of a turkey

Examples

He grabbed a turkey drumstick from the platter and started eating.
Roasting a turkey drumstick takes less time than cooking a whole bird.
At the fair, I bought a giant turkey drumstick for lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drumstick - the lower joint of the leg of a chicken, turkey, or similar bird
