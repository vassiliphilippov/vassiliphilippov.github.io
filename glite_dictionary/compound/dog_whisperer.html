dog whisperer

1. NOUN

/ dɔg ˈwɪspɚɚ / (AmE), / dɒg ˈwɪspərə / (BrE)
a person who is especially skilled at training and handling dogs, often using gentle, intuitive methods
expert in dog training

Examples

People often call her a dog whisperer because even the most stubborn dogs listen to her.
After watching the dog whisperer work, I was amazed at how quickly the puppy learned.
The neighborhood dog whisperer has helped dozens of owners with their unruly pets.

Synset Examples

He’s known as a dog whisperer, training pets effortlessly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal trainer - a person who trains animals

Etymology

Modeled after 'horse whisperer'; 'whisperer' meaning one who can communicate with animals in a special way.
