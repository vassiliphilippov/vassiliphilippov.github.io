bluethroat pikeblenny

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌθroʊt ˈpaɪkˌblɛni / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌθrəʊt ˈpaɪkˌblɛni / (BrE)
a small species of pikeblenny fish native to the western Atlantic, found from Florida to Cuba
a species of pikeblenny fish

Examples

The bluethroat pikeblenny is typically found in shallow coastal waters off Florida.
Scientists observed a bluethroat pikeblenny nesting among the rocks near Cuba.
