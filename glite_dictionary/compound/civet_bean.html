civet bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪvət biːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪt biːn / (BrE)
A small, flat green bean similar to the lima bean, sometimes used as food.
a small, flat green bean like a lima bean

Examples

The farmer harvested several civet beans from his garden for tonight's stew.
I've never tasted civet beans before, but I've heard they're quite similar to lima beans.
Cooks often substitute civet beans when they can't find lima beans at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lima bean - a large, flat, edible green or whitish seed of a legume
