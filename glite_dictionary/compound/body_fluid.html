body fluid

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌfluɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌfluːɪd / (BrE)
any of the liquids produced by or found in the human or animal body, such as blood, saliva, or urine.
liquid produced by the body
medical

Examples

The presence of a virus in a body fluid can help diagnose certain diseases.
Doctors collected samples of body fluid for laboratory analysis.
Some infections are spread through contact with body fluid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluid - a substance that flows easily and is not solid or gas
