tag end

1. NOUN

/ tæg ɛnd / (AmE), / tæɡ end / (BrE)
a small remaining part of something, especially a piece of cloth; the very end or scrap that is left over
small remainder, especially of cloth

Examples

She used the tag end of the fabric to patch a hole in her shirt.
Only a tag end of the ribbon was visible after the gift was unwrapped.
At the tag end of the curtain, there was just enough material left for a small pouch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remnant - a small part remaining after the main part has gone

Etymology

From 'tag' (short piece or attachment) + 'end' (last or final part).
