white pages

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈpeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / waɪt ˈpeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
a telephone directory, usually printed on white paper, listing residential names alphabetically with their telephone numbers
residential telephone directory

Examples

She searched the white pages to find her friend's new address.
Many households used the white pages to look up unfamiliar phone numbers.
The white pages are rarely used today due to the rise of digital directories.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: yellow pages - a business telephone directory printed on yellow paper
