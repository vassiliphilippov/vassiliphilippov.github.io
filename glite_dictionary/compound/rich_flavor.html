rich flavor

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ ˈfleɪvər / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
an intense and deeply pleasing taste, especially in food or drink
intense, pleasing taste in food or drink

Examples

The soup had a rich flavor that lingered pleasantly on my palate.
Aged cheeses often develop a rich flavor that food lovers appreciate.
Chocolate cake with a rich flavor is perfect for special occasions.

Synset Examples

This dish has a rich flavor, like a gourmet-ino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strong flavor - taste that is easily detected and impactful
  • Antonym: bland flavor - lacking strong taste or character
