screwbean mesquite

1. NOUN

/ ˈskruːˌbin məˈskiːt / (AmE), / ˈskruːˌbiːn mɛˈskiːt / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico, notable for its spirally twisted seed pods
shrublike tree with spirally twisted pods

Examples

The screwbean mesquite thrives in desert washes and produces unique spirally twisted seed pods.
Wildlife in the region often feed on the pods of the screwbean mesquite.
Conservationists are working to preserve the habitats of the screwbean mesquite in the southwestern U.S.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mesquite - a type of tree or shrub found in arid regions of the Americas
