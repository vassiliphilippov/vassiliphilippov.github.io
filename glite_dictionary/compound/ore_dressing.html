ore dressing

1. NOUN

/ ɔr ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔː ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of crushing and separating ore into valuable substances or waste using various techniques
process of extracting valuable materials from ore

Examples

The development of new ore dressing techniques improved metal recovery rates.
Advances in ore dressing have made mining operations more efficient and environmentally friendly.
He specialized in ore dressing during his studies in mining engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mineral processing - the process of separating valuable minerals from their ores

Etymology

From 'ore' (rock containing metal) and 'dressing' (preparation or treatment).
