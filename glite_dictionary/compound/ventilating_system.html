ventilating system

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a mechanical system in a building that supplies fresh air and removes stale air
air-circulating system in buildings

Examples

The building's ventilating system keeps the air fresh even when the windows are closed.
A modern ventilating system can help prevent the buildup of moisture and mold.
They upgraded the ventilating system to improve air quality throughout the office.
