spice rack

1. NOUN

/ spaɪs ræk / (AmE), / spaɪs ræk / (BrE)
a rack or stand used to organize and display jars or containers of spices, typically for use in a kitchen
rack for organizing spices

Examples

She arranged the new jars neatly on her spice rack.
The spice rack was mounted on the kitchen wall for easy access.
A well-organized spice rack makes cooking much more efficient.
