health spa

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ spɑ / (AmE), / hɛlθ spɑː / (BrE)
A place where people go to exercise, relax, and improve their health, often with fitness facilities, pools, and saunas.
place for exercise and relaxation

Examples

After a stressful week at work, she decided to visit a health spa for a massage and yoga session.
Many modern health spas offer a variety of treatments, from facials to hydrotherapy.
Joining a health spa helped him maintain a regular fitness routine and manage stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spa - a place offering health and relaxation treatments
