wrong-site surgery

1. NOUN

/ rɔŋ saɪt ˈsɝdʒəri / (AmE), / rɒŋ saɪt ˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
A surgical procedure performed on the wrong part of the body.
surgery on the incorrect body part
medical

Examples

A hospital implemented strict protocols to prevent wrong-site surgery after a serious incident last year.
Victims of wrong-site surgery often suffer both physical and emotional trauma as a result of the mistake.
The medical team reviewed the case to understand how wrong-site surgery occurred despite the safety checklist.
