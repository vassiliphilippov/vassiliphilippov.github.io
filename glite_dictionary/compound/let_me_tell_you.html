let me tell you

1. X

/ lɛt mi tɛl ju / (AmE), / lɛt mi tɛl ju / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement or story, especially to emphasize what follows or to grab the listener's attention
used to emphasize a statement or story

Examples

Let me tell you, that movie was not what I expected at all.
Let me tell you about the time I got lost in Paris.
Let me tell you, it's not easy running a business these days.

Synset Examples

Let me tell you about my incredible trip last summer.
Let me tell you, that was the best meal ever.
Let me tell you what, I’ve never seen anything like it.
