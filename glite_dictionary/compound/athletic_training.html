athletic training

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / æθˈlɛtɪk ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a planned program of practice, exercise, and diet designed to improve an athlete's physical performance
exercise and diet regimen for athletes

Examples

Many runners follow strict athletic training programs to prepare for marathons.
Nutrition plays a crucial role in effective athletic training.
Her commitment to athletic training helped her win the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training - the process of learning the skills needed for a particular job or activity
