oval kumquat

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvəl kʌmˌkwɑt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəl ˈkʌmkwɒt / (BrE)
a small evergreen shrub (Fortunella margarita) bearing oval-shaped orange fruits, commonly grown for its edible fruit and ornamental value
shrubs bearing oval kumquats

Examples

The oval kumquat is prized for its sweet, edible rind and tart flesh.
Gardeners often plant the oval kumquat for its attractive foliage and colorful fruit.
She made a marmalade using freshly picked oval kumquat from her backyard shrub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kumquat - a small, orange-like citrus fruit and the plant bearing it
