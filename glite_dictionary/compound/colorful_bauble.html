colorful bauble

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚfəl ˈbɑbəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl ˈbɔːbəl / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored ornamental ball, often used to decorate a Christmas tree
brightly colored tree ornament

Examples

The children carefully hung each colorful bauble on the tree's branches.
A single colorful bauble caught the light and sparkled in the room.
Shops were filled with every size and shape of colorful bauble for the holiday season.

Synset Examples

The Christmas tree was adorned with colorful baubles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bauble - a small, showy trinket or decoration
