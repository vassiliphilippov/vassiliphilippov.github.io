registered representative

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person authorized to manage clients' accounts and provide advice at a brokerage, advertising agency, or other service business
authorized account manager in finance or services

Examples

Only a registered representative can make investment recommendations to retail clients.
She became a registered representative after passing the required licensing exams.
If you have questions about your account, please contact your registered representative.
