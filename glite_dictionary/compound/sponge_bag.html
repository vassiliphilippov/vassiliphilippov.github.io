sponge bag

1. NOUN

/ spʌndʒ bæɡ / (AmE), / spʌndʒ bæɡ / (BrE)
a small waterproof bag used for carrying toiletries such as soap and toothpaste while travelling
waterproof bag for toiletries when travelling
british

Examples

He packed his razor and toothbrush into his sponge bag before leaving for the airport.
Remember to bring your sponge bag, or you'll have no soap at the hotel.
Her sponge bag has separate pockets for toothpaste and shampoo bottles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toiletry bag - a small bag for carrying personal grooming items
  • Synonym: wash bag - a bag for holding toiletries, often used in travel
