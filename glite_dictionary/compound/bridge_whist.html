bridge whist

1. NOUN

/ brɪdʒ wɪst / (AmE), / brɪdʒ wɪst / (BrE)
an early form of the card game bridge in which the dealer could choose the trump suit
early form of bridge card game

Examples

My grandfather used to play bridge whist before the modern game of bridge became popular.
Bridge whist allows the dealer to select the trump suit, unlike later versions of bridge.
The rules of bridge whist differ slightly from those in contract bridge.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bridge - a popular card game derived from whist
  • Degree Lower: whist - a classic trick-taking card game, predecessor to bridge
