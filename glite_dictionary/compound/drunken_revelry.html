drunken revelry

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌŋkən ˈrɛvəlri / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋkən ˈrɛvəlri / (BrE)
noisy and lively celebration or party characterized by excessive drinking, often with wild or uninhibited behavior.
wild partying with excessive drinking

Examples

The students' drunken revelry after exams kept the neighbors awake all night.
Years later, he still remembered the drunken revelry of his youth with a mix of nostalgia and regret.
Police responded to complaints about drunken revelry spilling out onto the streets after midnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacchanalia - an occasion of wild and drunken revelry
