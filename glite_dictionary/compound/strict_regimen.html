strict regimen

1. NOUN

/ strɪkt ˈrɛdʒəˌmɛn / (AmE), / strɪkt ˈrɛdʒɪmən / (BrE)
a carefully controlled and rigorous routine or system, often involving diet, exercise, or training
rigorous routine or system

Examples

After his surgery, he adhered to a strict regimen of physical therapy and medication.
The athlete credits his success to a strict regimen of early mornings and disciplined workouts.
Her doctor recommended a strict regimen to help control her diabetes.

Synset Examples

He follows a strict regimen for home training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regimen - a prescribed way of life, especially as relates to diet or exercise
