computer expert

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈɛkspɝt / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
A person who has specialized knowledge and skills in computers and computing.
person with expertise in computers

Examples

As a computer expert, she was able to fix the network issues in no time.
The school hired a computer expert to set up their new lab.
If you have trouble with your device, ask a computer expert for help.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computer specialist - an expert in the field of computers
  • Antonym: novice - a person who is new to using computers
