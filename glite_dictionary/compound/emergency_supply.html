emergency supply

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi səˈplaɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi səˈplaɪ / (BrE)
a stock of items kept for use in unexpected or dangerous situations, such as disasters or accidents
items kept for emergencies or disasters

Examples

Every household should have an emergency supply of food and water in case of natural disasters.
He kept a small emergency supply of medicine in his car for unexpected situations.
During the storm, the hotel distributed its emergency supply kits to all the guests.

Synset Examples

Tampon Tim always carries extra supplies for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provision - an amount of something supplied or provided
