showy lady's-slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ.i ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪp.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪp.ə / (BrE)
a North American wild orchid with pale pink and white flowers featuring a large, inflated pouch-like lip
North American wild orchid

Examples

We spotted a showy lady's-slipper blooming along the edge of the wetland trail.
The showy lady's-slipper is protected in many states due to its rarity.
Photographers travel each June to capture the beauty of the showy lady's-slipper in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's-slipper - any orchid with a pouch-shaped lip
