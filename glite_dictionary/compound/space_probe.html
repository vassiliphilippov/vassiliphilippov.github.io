space probe

1. NOUN

/ speɪs proʊb / (AmE), / speɪs prəʊb / (BrE)
an unmanned spacecraft designed to explore outer space and gather scientific information beyond Earth's atmosphere
unmanned spacecraft for space exploration

Examples

The space probe sent back detailed images of Jupiter's rings.
Scientists celebrated as the space probe successfully entered Mars' orbit.
Data from the space probe revealed new information about Pluto's surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacecraft - a vehicle designed for travel or operation in outer space
  • Degree Lower: satellite - an artificial body placed in orbit around the earth or another planet

Etymology

space (from Latin spatium, 'room, extent') + probe (from Latin probare, 'to test, prove')
