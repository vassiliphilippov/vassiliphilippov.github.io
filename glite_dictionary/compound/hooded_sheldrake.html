hooded sheldrake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊdəd ˈʃɛlˌdreɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪd ˈʃɛldrɛɪk / (BrE)
a small North American duck (Lophodytes cucullatus), the male distinguished by a large, fan-shaped crest on its head
a small North American duck with a large crest

Examples

We spotted a hooded sheldrake gliding quietly across the pond at dawn.
The male hooded sheldrake is famous for its striking black-and-white crest.
During migration season, several hooded sheldrakes visit the wetlands near our town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hooded merganser - a small North American duck with a fan-shaped crest (Lophodytes cucullatus)
