sweet lime

1. NOUN

/ swit laɪm / (AmE), / swiːt laɪm / (BrE)
a citrus fruit with a sweet, mildly flavored pulp, resembling a lemon but lacking its sharp acidity
a citrus fruit with sweet mild pulp

Examples

She offered me a slice of sweet lime to refresh my palate on a hot day.
Unlike regular lemons, sweet lime has a mild flavor that's less tart and more subtle.
Sweet lime juice is a popular drink in many tropical countries during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon - a yellow citrus fruit with acidic juice
  • Synonym: lime - a small, green, acidic citrus fruit
