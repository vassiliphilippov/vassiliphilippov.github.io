American crab apple

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən kræb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən kræb ˈapəl / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to the eastern United States, with pink blossoms and small yellow fruit
tree with pink blossoms and yellow fruit

Examples

The American crab apple is known for its beautiful springtime blossoms.
In the park, I saw several American crab apple trees in full bloom.
Local wildlife often feed on the fruit of the American crab apple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a small, sour apple or the tree that bears it
