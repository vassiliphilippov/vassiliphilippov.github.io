high-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪ ˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ ˌɡreɪd / (BrE)
of superior quality; of a better or higher standard than others of its kind.
of superior quality

Examples

The company only uses high-grade materials in its products.
Doctors recommend consuming high-grade olive oil for its health benefits.
The laboratory tested a sample of high-grade steel for structural use.

Synset Examples

top-grade ore

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low-grade - of inferior quality or lower standard
  • Synonym: top-quality - of the very best quality
