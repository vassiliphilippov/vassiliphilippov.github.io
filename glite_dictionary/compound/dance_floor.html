dance floor

1. NOUN

/ dæns flɔr / (AmE), / dɑːns flɔː / (BrE)
a floor or designated area used for dancing, often found in clubs, parties, or events
area designated for dancing

Examples

The crowd quickly filled the dance floor as the music began to play.
They installed new lighting above the dance floor to create a better atmosphere.
She spent hours on the dance floor at her friend's wedding.
