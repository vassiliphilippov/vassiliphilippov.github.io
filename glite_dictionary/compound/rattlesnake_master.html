rattlesnake master

1. NOUN

/ ˈrætəlˌsneɪk ˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈrætəlˌsneɪk ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a coarse, prickly perennial plant of the United States (Eryngium yuccifolium), traditionally believed to cure rattlesnake bites
prickly American plant; folk remedy for snakebite

Examples

The prairie was dotted with tall, spiky rattlesnake master plants.
He collected rattlesnake master to display its unusual spherical flowers in his garden.
Native Americans once used rattlesnake master as a folk remedy for snakebites.
