hospital ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːspɪtl ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtəl ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship equipped and staffed to serve as a hospital, especially for treating wounded personnel during wartime
ship used as a hospital, often in war

Examples

During the war, many wounded soldiers were transported to the hospital ship for treatment.
A hospital ship is equipped with medical staff and facilities to care for the injured at sea.
The government deployed a hospital ship to assist in disaster relief after the hurricane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambulance - vehicle equipped to transport sick or injured people
