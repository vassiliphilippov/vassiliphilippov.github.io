anchovy butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈænt͡ʃoʊvi ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈantʃəvi ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
butter blended with mashed anchovies, used as a savory spread or sauce
butter mixed with mashed anchovies

Examples

He spread a generous layer of anchovy butter on his toast before serving the soup.
The recipe calls for topping the grilled steak with a slice of anchovy butter.
Chefs often use anchovy butter to add a savory, umami flavor to vegetables and fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compound butter - butter mixed with other ingredients, often herbs or seafood, for flavor
