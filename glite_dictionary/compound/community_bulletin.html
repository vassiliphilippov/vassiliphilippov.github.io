community bulletin

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti bəˈlɪtən / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti bʊˈlɪtɪn / (BrE)
a notice board or publication used to display announcements, news, or information about local events and activities within a community
notice board for local events

Examples

The upcoming charity drive was listed on the community bulletin at the library entrance.
Check the community bulletin for updates on local events and activities.
Residents often post lost pet notices on the community bulletin near the town hall.

Synset Examples

I saw the event details on the community bulletin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulletin board - a board for posting public notices
  • Synonym: notice board - a board for displaying notices and announcements
