sweet roll

1. NOUN

/ swit roʊl / (AmE), / swiːt rəʊl / (BrE)
A small, yeast-leavened bread roll that is sweetened, often containing raisins, nuts, spices, or a glaze.
a small sweet yeast bread roll

Examples

She enjoyed a warm sweet roll with her morning coffee.
The bakery offers sweet rolls filled with cinnamon and nuts.
Would you like to try a sweet roll or a croissant for breakfast?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - small, round bread or cake, often sweet or spiced
  • Degree Higher: pastry - dough-based baked foods, often sweet
