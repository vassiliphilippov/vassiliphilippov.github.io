distribution agreement

1. NOUN

/ dɪstrɪˈbjuʃən əˌɡriːmənt / (AmE), / dɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən əˌɡriːmənt / (BrE)
a formal contract outlining the terms under which one party is permitted to market and sell goods or products on behalf of another
contract for marketing and selling goods
legal

Examples

The company signed a distribution agreement with a local wholesaler to expand its reach.
Before launching the new product, they negotiated a distribution agreement with several retailers.
A well-drafted distribution agreement can help prevent disputes between manufacturers and distributors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franchise agreement - contract authorizing use of a business's name and methods
