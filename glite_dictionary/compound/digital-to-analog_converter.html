digital-to-analog converter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl tu ˈænəlˌɑːɡ kənˈvɝːt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl tə ˈænəlɒɡ kənˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a device that converts digital signals into analogue signals, often used in audio and video equipment
device converting digital to analogue signals

Examples

The new stereo system includes a high-quality digital-to-analog converter for improved sound reproduction.
Some music enthusiasts invest in a separate digital-to-analog converter to enhance audio fidelity.
A digital-to-analog converter is essential in devices that play digital audio files through traditional speakers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: analog-to-digital converter - a device for converting analogue signals into digital signals
