Belgian sheepdog

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈʃip.dɔg / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈʃiːp.dɒg / (BrE)
A hardy breed of dog developed in Belgium for herding sheep, known for its intelligence and versatility.
breed of herding dog from Belgium

Examples

The Belgian sheepdog excels at agility and obedience competitions.
A Belgian sheepdog requires regular exercise and mental stimulation to be happy.
The police force trained a Belgian sheepdog to assist in search and rescue operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belgian Malinois - a related Belgian breed used for herding and protection
  • Synonym: German shepherd - another popular working and herding dog breed
