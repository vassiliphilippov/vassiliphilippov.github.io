on fleek

1. ADJ

/ ɑn fliːk / (AmE), / ɒn fliːk / (BrE)
extremely attractive, stylish, or perfectly executed; exactly right
very stylish or perfectly done (slang)
slang informal american

Examples

Her makeup was on fleek all night at the party.
The chef’s plating skills are totally on fleek in this restaurant.
You have to see his new sneakers—they’re absolutely on fleek!

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on point - very good, precise, or stylish
  • Synonym: stylish - fashionable and attractive
  • Antonym: outdated - no longer fashionable or current

Etymology

Popularized in American English slang in the early 2010s; origin unknown, first viral use attributed to a 2014 Vine video.
