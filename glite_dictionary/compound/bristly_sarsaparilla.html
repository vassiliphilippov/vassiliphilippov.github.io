bristly sarsaparilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪsli ˌsɑrspəˈrɪlə / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsli ˌsɑːspəˈrɪlə / (BrE)
a bristly herb native to eastern and central North America, bearing black fruit and valued for its medicinal bark
North American herb with medicinal bark

Examples

The bristly sarsaparilla is often found growing in hardwood forests throughout the region.
Traditional remedies sometimes use the bark of bristly sarsaparilla for its medicinal properties.
In autumn, the bristly sarsaparilla produces clusters of dark, almost black berries.
