goats' milk

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊts mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊts mɪlk / (BrE)
milk produced by goats, often consumed as a beverage or used in making cheese and other dairy products
milk from goats

Examples

Many people who are lactose intolerant can drink goats' milk without any problems.
The artisan cheese is made from fresh goats' milk collected locally.
Some parents prefer to feed their children goats' milk rather than cow's milk.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cow's milk - milk produced by cows
  • Synonym: sheep's milk - milk produced by sheep
