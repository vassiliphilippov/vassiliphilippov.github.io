theatrical production

1. NOUN

/ θiˈætrɪkəl prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the staging and performance of a play or drama in a theatre, including all aspects of its presentation
the staging of a play or drama

Examples

The school is holding its annual theatrical production in the auditorium next month.
This year's theatrical production of 'Hamlet' received enthusiastic reviews from critics.
She has been involved in every theatrical production the company has staged over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play - a dramatic work intended for performance by actors on a stage
