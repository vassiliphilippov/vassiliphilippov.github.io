blushing mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌʃɪŋ ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃɪŋ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
an edible yellowish mushroom that often turns red when bruised or touched, commonly found in woodlands
an edible mushroom that reddens when handled

Examples

The blushing mushroom is a favorite among foragers due to its distinct color change.
When handling the blushing mushroom, you’ll notice its flesh turns a reddish hue.
Many chefs appreciate the blushing mushroom for its mild, nutty flavor in dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bolete - any mushroom of the family Boletaceae, many with pores instead of gills
