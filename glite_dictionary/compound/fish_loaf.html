fish loaf

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ loʊf / (AmE), / fɪʃ ləʊf / (BrE)
A dish consisting of flaked fish baked in a loaf shape with bread crumbs and various seasonings.
loaf-shaped dish made with flaked fish

Examples

My grandmother used to make a delicious fish loaf every Friday during Lent.
The restaurant offered a special fish loaf served with a tangy tomato sauce.
For a quick dinner, you can prepare a simple fish loaf using canned tuna and breadcrumbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meatloaf - loaf-shaped dish of ground meat, bread crumbs, and seasonings
