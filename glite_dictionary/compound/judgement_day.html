Judgement Day

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌdʒmənt deɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt deɪ / (BrE)
(in Christianity) the day at the end of the world when God judges the actions of all people and determines their eternal destiny.
final day of divine judgement

Examples

Many believers await Judgement Day with hope and trepidation.
The preacher spoke about the importance of preparing for Judgement Day.
Stories and paintings often depict Judgement Day as a dramatic event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doomsday - the day of the Last Judgment in Christian belief

Etymology

From Old French *jugement* (judgement) and Old English *dæg* (day).
