cowardly chickenfucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊərdli ˈtʃɪkɪnfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈkaʊədli ˈtʃɪkɪnfʌkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive insult directed at someone considered extremely cowardly, often used to express utter contempt or derision.
offensive insult for extreme cowardice
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

He called his opponent a cowardly chickenfucker during the heated argument.
No one wanted to be labeled a cowardly chickenfucker in front of the group.
The gang leader shouted, "Don't be a cowardly chickenfucker, stand your ground!"
