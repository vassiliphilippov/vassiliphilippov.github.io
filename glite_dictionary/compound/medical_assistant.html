medical assistant

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a person trained to help medical professionals with clinical and administrative tasks in healthcare settings
healthcare worker aiding medical staff
medical

Examples

The clinic hired a new medical assistant to help manage patient appointments and records.
As a medical assistant, your duties may include taking vital signs and preparing examination rooms.
After completing her training, she found work as a medical assistant in a family practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick or infirm
