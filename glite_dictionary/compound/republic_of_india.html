Republic of India

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɪndiə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɪndiə / (BrE)
The official name of India, a country in South Asia that became an independent republic in 1947.
official name of India, country in South Asia

Examples

The constitution of the Republic of India was adopted on January 26, 1950.
The Republic of India is known for its cultural diversity and economic growth.
After independence in 1947, the nation officially became the Republic of India.
