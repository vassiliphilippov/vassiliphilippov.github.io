spiral notebook

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪrəl ˈnoʊtbʊk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl ˈnəʊtbʊk / (BrE)
A notebook bound with a metal or plastic spiral coil, allowing the pages to be turned easily.
notebook with spiral coil binding

Examples

She took out her spiral notebook to write down the lecture notes.
He carries a spiral notebook for sketching ideas whenever inspiration strikes.
The teacher asked everyone to bring a spiral notebook to class next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notebook - a book of blank pages for writing notes
