pit bull terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪt bʊl təˈrɪr.i / (AmE), / ˈpɪt bʊl tɛˈrɪə / (BrE)
An American breed of muscular terriers with a short, stiff, close-lying coat, known for their strength and agility.
breed of muscular American terrier

Examples

The pit bull terrier is often admired for its loyalty and courage.
She adopted a pit bull terrier from the local animal shelter last year.
Due to their strength, pit bull terriers require experienced owners who can train them properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Staffordshire bull terrier - a similar but distinct British breed of short-haired terrier

Etymology

From 'pit bull', originally referring to dogs used in pit fighting, and 'terrier', a type of dog breed.
