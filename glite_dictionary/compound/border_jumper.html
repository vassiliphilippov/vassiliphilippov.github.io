border jumper

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdɚ ˌdʒʌmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə ˌdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who illegally enters a country by crossing its border without authorization.
person illegally crossing a border

Examples

Authorities arrested several border jumpers attempting to cross the frontier at night.
The documentary focuses on the dangers faced by border jumpers seeking a better life.
Local residents reported sightings of border jumpers near the river.

Synset Examples

The border jumper was apprehended by authorities.
The news reported on a notorious border jumper.
They hired a lawyer for the border jumper's case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal immigrant - a person who enters or stays in a country without legal permission
  • Synonym: smuggler - a person who illegally transports people or goods across borders
