toilet bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt baɡ / (BrE)
a small waterproof bag for carrying toiletries such as soap, toothpaste, and toothbrush when travelling
bag for toiletries when travelling

Examples

He packed his toothbrush and razor into his toilet bag before leaving for the airport.
A new toilet bag makes it easier to organize your grooming essentials while traveling.
I realized I had forgotten my toilet bag after we had already checked into the hotel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wash bag - a small bag for toiletries, especially for travel
  • Synonym: toiletry bag - a bag for carrying personal hygiene items when travelling
