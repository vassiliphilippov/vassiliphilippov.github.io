prompt copy

1. NOUN

/ prɑmpt ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / prɒmpt ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
the version of a play's script used by the prompter, typically annotated with cues and stage directions
annotated script used by a theatre prompter

Examples

The stage manager triple-checked the prompt copy before the first rehearsal began.
Her meticulous notes in the prompt copy helped the cast stay in sync during the performance.
Without the prompt copy, the crew struggled to coordinate lighting and sound cues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prompt book - the annotated script of a stage play used by the prompter
