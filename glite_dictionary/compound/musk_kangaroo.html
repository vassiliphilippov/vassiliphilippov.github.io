musk kangaroo

1. NOUN

/ mʌsk kæŋˈɡæroʊ / (AmE), / mʌsk kæŋˈɡærəʊ / (BrE)
a small species of kangaroo native to northeastern Australia, known for its musky odor
small kangaroo of northeastern Australia

Examples

The musk kangaroo is rarely seen outside its natural habitat in northeastern Australia.
Researchers are studying the diet of the musk kangaroo to better understand its ecological role.
I spotted a musk kangaroo while hiking in the Queensland rainforest.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: kangaroo - a large Australian marsupial with strong hind legs
