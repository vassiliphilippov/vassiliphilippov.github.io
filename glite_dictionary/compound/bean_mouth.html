bean mouth

1. NOUN

/ bin maʊθ / (AmE), / biːn maʊθ / (BrE)
a person who habitually talks too much or gossips about others
someone who talks too much; a gossip
informal

Examples

She has a bean mouth when it comes to gossip.
Everyone knows he's a bean mouth about the latest news.
Don't be such a bean mouth; try to keep some things private.

Synset Examples

She has a bean mouth when it comes to gossip.

I don't trust her; she's always been a bean mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gossip - a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others
  • Synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about trivial matters
