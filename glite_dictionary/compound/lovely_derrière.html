lovely derrière

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvli dɛrˈjɛr / (AmE), / ˈlʌvli dɛrˈjɛə / (BrE)
an attractive or shapely buttocks, often referred to in an elegant or playful way
attractive backside or buttocks

Examples

He couldn't help but notice her lovely derrière as she walked by.
The dress complemented her lovely derrière perfectly.
She joked about doing extra squats to keep her lovely derrière in shape.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology
