friendly insult

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndli ˈɪnsʌlt / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli ˈɪnsʌlt / (BrE)
A playful or teasing remark intended without harm, often exchanged between friends.
playful, teasing remark between friends

Examples

She called him a genius in a friendly insult about his clumsy cooking.
They exchanged friendly insults while playing cards last night.
A friendly insult can strengthen a bond if everyone knows it’s a joke.

Synset Examples

He threw the friendly insult back at you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tease - to make fun of or attempt to provoke in a playful way
  • Synonym: joke - something said or done to cause laughter
  • Degree Higher: insult - a remark or action that is rude or offensive
