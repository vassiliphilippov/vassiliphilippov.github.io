ad blitz

1. NOUN

/ æd blɪts / (AmE), / æd blɪts / (BrE)
a sudden, intensive campaign of advertisements, especially over a short period to achieve maximum impact
intensive advertising campaign

Examples

The company launched an ad blitz to promote its new smartphone model.
During the election season, voters were overwhelmed by an ad blitz from both parties.
The ad blitz helped increase the brand’s market share in just a few weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - a series of coordinated advertisements to promote a product or idea
