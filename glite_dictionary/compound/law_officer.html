law officer

1. NOUN

/ lɔ ˈɑfɪsər / (AmE), / lɔː ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to enforce the law, such as a police officer or sheriff
person who enforces the law

Examples

The law officer questioned all witnesses thoroughly before closing the investigation.
Several law officers responded quickly to the emergency call downtown.
As a law officer, she was dedicated to maintaining peace in her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police officer - person employed to enforce the law
  • Antonym: criminal - person who breaks the law
