golden ager

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈeɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈeɪdʒə / (BrE)
an elderly person, especially one who is retired or in their later years of life
an elderly person

Examples

The community center offers dance classes specifically for golden ager members.
As a golden ager, he volunteers his time to mentor young people.
Discounts are available for golden agers at many local theaters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senior citizen - an older person, especially over the age of retirement
  • Synonym: elderly - old; past middle age
  • Antonym: youth - the period between childhood and adult age; a young person

Etymology

From 'golden age' (meaning an ideal or flourishing period) plus '-er' indicating a person.
