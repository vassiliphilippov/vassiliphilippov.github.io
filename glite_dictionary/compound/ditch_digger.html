ditch digger

1. NOUN

/ dɪtʃ ˈdɪɡər / (AmE), / dɪtʃ ˈdɪɡə / (BrE)
a laborer whose job is to dig ditches, often using hand tools
manual laborer who digs ditches

Examples

He worked as a ditch digger before saving enough money for college.
The crew hired several ditch diggers to complete the project on schedule.
Being a ditch digger in the summer heat was exhausting work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
