Ionian Sea

1. PROPN

/ aɪˈoʊniən si / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊniən siː / (BrE)
An arm of the Mediterranean Sea located between western Greece and southern Italy.
sea between Greece and Italy

Examples

Many cruise ships travel through the Ionian Sea during the summer months.
The islands of Corfu and Kefalonia are located in the Ionian Sea.
The deep blue waters of the Ionian Sea are famous among divers and sailors.

Etymology

From Latin 'Ionium mare', from Greek 'Iónios Pélagos' (Ionian Sea).
