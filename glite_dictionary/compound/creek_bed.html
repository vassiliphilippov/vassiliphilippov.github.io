creek bed

1. NOUN

/ krik bɛd / (AmE), / kriːk bɛd / (BrE)
the bottom of a creek, typically the channel where water flows or has flowed before
channel at the bottom of a creek

Examples

During the drought, the creek bed was completely dry and covered in cracked mud.
We followed the winding creek bed through the forest to find the old campsite.
After heavy rains, the creek bed quickly filled with rushing water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riverbed - the bed or channel in which a river flows
