Ruhr River

1. PROPN

/ rʊr ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / rʊə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in western Germany, a tributary of the Rhine, flowing through the Ruhr industrial region
river in western Germany, tributary of the Rhine

Examples

The Ruhr River is an important waterway for Germany's industrial heartland.
Many towns along the Ruhr River flourished during the industrial revolution.
Heavy rainfall caused the Ruhr River to overflow its banks last year.
