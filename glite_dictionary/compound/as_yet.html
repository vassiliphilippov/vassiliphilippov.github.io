as yet

1. ADV

/ æz ˈjɛt / (AmE), / æz ˈjɛt / (BrE)
up to the present time; so far (used mainly in negative statements or questions)
up to now; so far (mainly negative contexts)

Examples

We have received no news as yet about the results of the test.
The reasons for his decision remain unclear as yet.
There have been no reported cases as yet in this region.

Synset Examples

So far he hasn't called
the sun isn't up yet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: so far - up to this point in time
