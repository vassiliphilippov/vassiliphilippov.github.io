house trailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs ˌtreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs ˌtreɪlə / (BrE)
A vehicle equipped for living in, intended to be towed by a car or truck.
vehicle for living, towable by car or truck

Examples

They decided to live in a house trailer while building their new home.
The campground has spaces equipped with utilities for house trailer parking.
When the storm hit, several house trailers were damaged by high winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caravan - vehicle equipped for living, often towed by a car (mainly British)
  • Synonym: mobile home - a large trailer or transportable structure used as a permanent living accommodation
