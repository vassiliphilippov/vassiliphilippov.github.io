Economic and Social Council

1. PROPN

/ ɪˌkɑnəmɪk ənd səˈsoʊʃəl ˈkaʊnsəl / (AmE), / iːˌkɒnəmɪk ənd səˈsəʊʃəl ˈkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
a permanent council of the United Nations responsible for coordinating economic and social policies and activities
UN body for economic and social coordination

Examples

The Economic and Social Council oversees the work of many UN specialized agencies.
At yesterday’s summit, the Economic and Social Council discussed global strategies for poverty reduction.
Membership of the Economic and Social Council rotates among United Nations member states.
