immediate apprehension

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmiːdiət əˌprɛˈhɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈmiːdiət əˌprɪˈhɛnʃən / (BrE)
an intuitive understanding or awareness that occurs instantly, without the need for conscious reasoning or inference
instant intuitive understanding

Examples

His immediate apprehension of the solution surprised even the experts at the conference.
Children often demonstrate an immediate apprehension of basic moral concepts before being taught.
She had an immediate apprehension of the risks involved, without needing further explanation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intuition - the ability to understand something immediately, without conscious reasoning
