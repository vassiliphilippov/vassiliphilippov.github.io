barley water

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrli ˌwɑtər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːli ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
A drink made by boiling barley in water, often used as a nourishing liquid for infants or as a mild beverage.
drink made by boiling barley in water

Examples

After her illness, the doctor recommended giving the baby barley water to help her recover.
My grandmother would always make fresh barley water during the hot summer months.
Some parents prefer to use barley water as a gentle supplement for infants.
