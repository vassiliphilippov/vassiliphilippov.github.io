brief fling

1. NOUN

/ bɹif flɪŋ / (AmE), / briːf flɪŋ / (BrE)
a short and casual romantic or sexual relationship, usually not lasting long
short casual romantic relationship

Examples

They had a brief fling when they met at university, but went their separate ways.
After her divorce, she wasn't interested in anything serious and enjoyed a brief fling over the holidays.
The rumors about his brief fling with a colleague quickly died down.

Synset Examples

They had a brief fling during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affair - a secret or romantic relationship, often extramarital
