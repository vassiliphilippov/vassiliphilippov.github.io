black-backed jackal

1. NOUN

/ ˌblækˈbækt ˈdʒækəl / (AmE), / ˌblækˈbækt ˈdʒækəl / (BrE)
a species of jackal native to Africa, distinguished by a black fur along its back and shoulders
African jackal species with black back

Examples

The black-backed jackal is known for its cunning hunting strategies on the African savanna.
We observed a pack of black-backed jackals scavenging near the river at dawn.
Unlike other jackals, the black-backed jackal has distinct coloration that sets it apart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackal - a wild dog native to Africa and Asia
