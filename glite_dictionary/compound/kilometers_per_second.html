kilometers per second

1. NOUN

/ kɪˈlɑːmɪtɚz pɚ ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtəz pə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
A unit of speed equal to the number of kilometers traveled in one second.
unit of speed (km/s)

Examples

The spacecraft was traveling at nearly 12 kilometers per second on its approach to Mars.
Astronomers measured the star's movement at 30 kilometers per second.
Sound in air travels much slower, at about 0.3 kilometers per second.

Synset Examples

Light travels in vacuum at the speed of almost 300,000 kilometers per second.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meters per second - unit of speed equal to the number of meters traveled in one second
  • Synonym: miles per hour - unit of speed in miles traveled per hour
