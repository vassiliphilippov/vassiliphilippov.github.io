moment of muppetry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənt əv ˈmʌpətri / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt əv ˈmʌpətri / (BrE)
an episode or situation characterized by silliness or foolishness
a silly or foolish situation
informal british

Examples

After forgetting his keys for the third time this week, he laughed about his moment of muppetry.
Everyone cringed at Sarah's moment of muppetry during the company presentation.
Looking back, that disastrous cake-baking attempt was a classic moment of muppetry.

Synset Examples

They regarded that incident as a moment of muppetry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: moment of madness - a brief period of recklessness or loss of control

Etymology

Informal use of 'muppetry,' inspired by the TV show 'The Muppets,' symbolizing silly behavior.
