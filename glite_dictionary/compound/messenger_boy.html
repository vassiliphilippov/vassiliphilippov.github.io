messenger boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsɪndʒɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪndʒə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy employed to carry messages or run errands, especially for a business or organization
boy who carries messages or runs errands

Examples

The company employed a messenger boy to deliver documents across town.
In the early 1900s, a messenger boy could be seen dashing between offices.
He started his career as a messenger boy before working his way up in the company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courier - a person who delivers messages, packages, or mail
