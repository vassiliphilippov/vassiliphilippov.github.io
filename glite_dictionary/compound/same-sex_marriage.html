same-sex marriage

1. NOUN

/ ˌseɪm sɛks ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌseɪm sɛks ˈmarɪdʒ / (BrE)
A legally or socially recognized marriage between two people of the same sex.
marriage between two people of the same sex

Examples

The country legalized same-sex marriage in 2015, making headlines around the world.
Debates over same-sex marriage often involve questions of equal rights and religious beliefs.
Their 10th wedding anniversary was a celebration of their same-sex marriage.

Synset Examples

the legal status of same-sex marriages has been hotly debated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriage - the legally or formally recognized union of two people as partners
  • Antonym: opposite-sex marriage - marriage between two people of different sexes
