global empire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊbəl ˈɛmˌpaɪr / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəl ˈɛmpaɪə / (BrE)
an organization or entity that has widespread influence or control across the world, especially in business or politics
worldwide powerful organization

Examples

She built a global empire from her small online store in just ten years.
The company's global empire stretches from Asia to South America.
He hopes to expand his business into a global empire one day.

Synset Examples

Their goal is to establish a global empire through innovation.
He dreams of creating a global empire in tech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empire - a group of nations or peoples ruled over by an emperor, empress, or other powerful government
  • Synonym: multinational - a company or organization operating in several countries
