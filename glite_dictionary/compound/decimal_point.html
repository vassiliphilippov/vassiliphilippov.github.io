decimal point

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛsɪməl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈdɛsɪməl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the dot used to separate the whole number part from the fractional part in a decimal number
dot in a decimal number

Examples

Please write the number with a decimal point between the whole number and the fraction.
The amount is correct up to two places after the decimal point.
In some European countries, a comma is used instead of a decimal point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decimal - a number written in the decimal system

Etymology

From Latin 'decimus' (tenth) and English 'point' (dot or mark).
