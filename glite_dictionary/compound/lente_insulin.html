Lente Insulin

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛn.teɪ ɪnˈs(j)uː.lɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.teɪ ɪnˈs(j)uː.lɪn / (BrE)
A brand name for an intermediate-acting form of insulin used in the treatment of diabetes mellitus.
intermediate-acting insulin for diabetes
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Lente Insulin to help manage her blood sugar levels.
Patients receiving Lente Insulin often inject it once or twice daily as recommended.
Lente Insulin was once a popular choice before newer insulin analogs became available.
