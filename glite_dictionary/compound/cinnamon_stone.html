cinnamon stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪnəmən stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən stəʊn / (BrE)
A variety of garnet that ranges in color from yellow to brown.
yellow to brown garnet variety

Examples

The jeweler showcased a beautiful cinnamon stone in the center of the ring.
Her pendant featured a rare cinnamon stone that glittered in the sunlight.
Collectors prize the cinnamon stone for its warm, golden-brown hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garnet - a group of silicate minerals used as gemstones and abrasives

Etymology

Refers to the gem's cinnamon-like color.
