defensive structure

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛnsɪv ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsɪv ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a building or other construction built to provide protection against attack
structure built for defense

Examples

The ancient city was surrounded by a massive defensive structure to deter invaders.
Engineers inspected the defensive structure for weaknesses before the enemy approached.
During the siege, the soldiers took cover behind the defensive structure to protect themselves from arrows.

Synset Examples

the artillery battered down the defenses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortification - a military construction or building designed for defense
  • Synonym: fortress - a large, strong building or group of buildings used as a military defense
