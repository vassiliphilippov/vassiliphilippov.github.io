constricting corset

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrɪktɪŋ ˈkɔr.sət / (AmE), / kənˈstrɪktɪŋ ˈkɔː.sɪt / (BrE)
a tightly fitting garment designed to compress and shape the waist
tight-fitting waist-shaping garment

Examples

Many women in the nineteenth century wore a constricting corset to achieve a fashionable silhouette.
The actress found it difficult to breathe while wearing the constricting corset for her period drama.
She discarded her constricting corset in favor of more comfortable modern clothing.

Victorian fashions often featured a constricting corset.
