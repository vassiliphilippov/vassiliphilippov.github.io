walnut family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔlnʌt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlnʌt ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of trees, Juglandaceae, that includes walnuts, butternuts, hickories, and pecans, typically bearing edible nuts
family of nut-bearing trees (Juglandaceae)

Examples

The walnut family includes both pecans and hickories, not just walnuts.
Trees in the walnut family produce nuts that are valued for both flavor and nutrition.
Botanists classify the butternut tree as part of the walnut family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walnut - edible nut from trees of the genus Juglans
  • Synonym: hickory - American tree yielding nuts, related to walnuts
  • Synonym: pecan - tree or nut from Carya illinoinensis, in the same family
