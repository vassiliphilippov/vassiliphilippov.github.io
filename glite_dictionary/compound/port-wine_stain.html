port-wine stain

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrt ˌwaɪn steɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɔːt ˌwaɪn steɪn / (BrE)
A flat, pink to purple birthmark caused by a concentration of dilated blood vessels in the skin.
pink or purple flat birthmark
medical

Examples

The baby was born with a noticeable port-wine stain on her left cheek.
Laser treatments can help reduce the appearance of a port-wine stain.
He felt self-conscious about the port-wine stain on his arm.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to the color of port wine.
