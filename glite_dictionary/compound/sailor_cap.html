sailor cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlər kæp / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə kap / (BrE)
a flat, round cap with a small brim or no brim, traditionally worn by sailors as part of their uniform
a flat cap worn by sailors

Examples

The little boy wore a sailor cap to the party.
During the parade, veterans marched in navy uniforms and sailor caps.
She found a vintage sailor cap at the costume shop.
