honeydew melon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌdu ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈhʌnidjuː ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
A large, smooth-skinned melon with pale green flesh and a greenish-white rind, known for its sweet flavor.
a sweet, pale green-fleshed melon

Examples

She served chilled honeydew melon slices at the picnic.
A ripe honeydew melon should feel heavy for its size and smell fragrant.
He enjoys putting cubes of honeydew melon in his fruit salad.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: melon - a large fruit with a hard rind and sweet flesh, typically eaten as food

2. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌduː ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪdjuː ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
a type of sweet, round melon with smooth pale skin and light green or white flesh, lacking a musky smell
a sweet, smooth-skinned melon

Examples

She enjoyed a slice of honeydew melon with her breakfast.
The fruit salad was filled with cubes of juicy honeydew melon.
At the market, the honeydew melon looked perfectly ripe and sweet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watermelon - a large, juicy fruit with green rind and red or yellow flesh
