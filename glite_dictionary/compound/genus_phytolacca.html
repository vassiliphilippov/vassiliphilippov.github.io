genus Phytolacca

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌfaɪtəˈlækə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌfaɪtəˈlækə / (BrE)
The genus of flowering plants in the family Phytolaccaceae, commonly known as pokeweeds.
genus of pokeweeds (plants)

Examples

The genus Phytolacca includes several species of pokeweed found in North America.
Researchers are studying alkaloids present in the genus Phytolacca for their medicinal properties.
Fruit from plants of the genus Phytolacca can be toxic if ingested by humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pokeweed - a North American plant of the genus Phytolacca with poisonous berries

Etymology

From Greek 'phytón' (plant) and 'lákka' (a kind of berry).
