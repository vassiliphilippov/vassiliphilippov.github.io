healthcare facility

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlθˌkɛr fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθkeə fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a place such as a hospital, clinic, or nursing home where medical care is provided to patients
place where medical care is provided

Examples

The city built a new healthcare facility to serve the growing community.
Each healthcare facility must comply with strict safety and sanitation regulations.
She works as a nurse in a rural healthcare facility that treats both children and adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospital - an institution for medical treatment and care
  • Synonym: clinic - a healthcare center for outpatient care
