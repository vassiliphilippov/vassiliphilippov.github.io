more or less

1. ADV

/ mɔɹ ər lɛs / (AmE), / mɔː ə lɛs / (BrE)
to some extent; slightly or approximately, but not completely or exactly
to some extent; approximately

Examples

The project is more or less finished, but we still have a few details to check.
She understood the instructions, more or less, and managed to complete the task.
The weather stayed sunny more or less all week, except for some light rain on Wednesday.

Synset Examples

his arguments were somewhat self-contradictory
the children argued because one slice of cake was slightly larger than the other

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somewhat - to some degree or extent
  • Synonym: slightly - to a small degree; not much
