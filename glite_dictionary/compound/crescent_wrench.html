crescent wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛsənt rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrɛsənt rɛntʃ / (BrE)
an adjustable spanner with a movable jaw, used to grip and turn hexagonal nuts and bolts
adjustable spanner for nuts and bolts
american

Examples

He tightened the bolt with a crescent wrench from his toolkit.
The mechanic always keeps a crescent wrench handy for quick adjustments.
Make sure to use a crescent wrench that fits snugly around the nut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjustable spanner - a spanner with a movable jaw for different sizes of nuts

Etymology

Named after the Crescent Tool Company, which popularized this type of wrench.
