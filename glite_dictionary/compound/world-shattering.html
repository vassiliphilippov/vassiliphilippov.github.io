world-shattering

1. ADJ

/ wɝld ˈʃætəɹɪŋ / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈʃætərɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely significant or important, having a major impact as if affecting the whole world
of enormous significance or impact

Examples

The journalist described the event as a world-shattering discovery that could change science forever.
She waited anxiously for world-shattering news but received only a routine update.
Not every decision at work needs to be a world-shattering one.

Synset Examples

earthshaking proposals
the contest was no world-shaking affair
the conversation … could hardly be called world-shattering

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthshaking - extremely significant; having a dramatic impact
  • Synonym: world-shaking - of great importance; extremely significant
  • Antonym: insignificant - not important or having little effect
