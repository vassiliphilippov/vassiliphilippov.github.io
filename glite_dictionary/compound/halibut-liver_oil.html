halibut-liver oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæləˌbʌt ˈlɪvɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈhælɪbət ˈlɪvə ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty oil extracted from the livers of halibut, used as a source of vitamin A
oil from halibut livers, vitamin A source

Examples

Many nutritionists recommend halibut-liver oil as an excellent source of vitamin A.
The doctor advised him to take halibut-liver oil daily to support his eye health.
Some people prefer halibut-liver oil capsules over cod-liver oil due to its mild flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cod-liver oil - oil from cod livers, another source of vitamin A and D
