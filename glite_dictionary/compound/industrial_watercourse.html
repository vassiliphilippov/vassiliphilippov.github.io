industrial watercourse

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl ˈwɔtɚˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ɪnˌdʌstriəl ˈwɔːtəkɔːs / (BrE)
a canal or waterway constructed and used for industrial purposes, typically operated by one or more industries
canal used by industries

Examples

The factory relies on the industrial watercourse for transporting raw materials to its main facility.
Maintenance of the industrial watercourse is essential to ensure continuous operation of nearby manufacturing plants.
Several companies share rights to use the industrial watercourse that flows through the industrial park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canal - an artificial waterway for navigation or irrigation
