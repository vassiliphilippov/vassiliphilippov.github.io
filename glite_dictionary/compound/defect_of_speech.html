defect of speech

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛkt əv spiːtʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛkt əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
a disorder or abnormality that affects a person's ability to speak clearly or correctly
speech disorder or abnormality
medical

Examples

After his accident, he developed a defect of speech that made it hard to pronounce certain words.
Therapy can help children overcome a defect of speech and communicate more effectively.
A defect of speech such as stammering often requires professional intervention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speech disorder - an impairment of the ability to speak clearly
