Peninsular Malaysia

1. PROPN

/ pəˈnɪnsələr məˈleɪziə / (AmE), / pɛnˈɪnsjʊlə məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the part of Malaysia located on the Malay Peninsula, sharing a land border with Thailand to the north
region of Malaysia on the Malay Peninsula

Examples

Many tourists visit Peninsular Malaysia for its beaches and cultural attractions.
The climate of Peninsular Malaysia is typically humid and tropical throughout the year.
A train journey from Bangkok to Peninsular Malaysia offers stunning views of the countryside.
