youth gang

1. NOUN

/ juːθ gæŋ / (AmE), / juːθ ɡæŋ / (BrE)
a group of young people, typically teenagers, who band together and may engage in criminal or antisocial activities
a gang of teenagers

Examples

Several neighborhoods have been affected by youth gangs involved in vandalism and theft.
The police organized outreach programs to prevent youth gang activity in the city.
Parents worry about their children being recruited into a youth gang.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gang - an organized group of criminals or troublemakers
