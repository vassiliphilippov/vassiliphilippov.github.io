Isle of Wight

1. PROPN

/ ˌaɪl əv ˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌaɪl əv ˈwaɪt / (BrE)
an island and county in southern England, located in the English Channel
island and county in southern England
british

Examples

We spent our summer holiday on the Isle of Wight exploring its beautiful beaches and historic sites.
The Isle of Wight festival attracts thousands of music fans every year.
After taking the ferry, they arrived on the Isle of Wight to visit friends.

Etymology

From Old English *wit* (possibly 'raised' or a reference to its chalk cliffs); 'isle' from French *île*.
