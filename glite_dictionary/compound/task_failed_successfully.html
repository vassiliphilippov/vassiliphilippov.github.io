task failed successfully

1. X

/ tæsk feɪld səkˈsɛsfəli / (AmE), / tɑːsk feɪld səkˈsɛsfəli / (BrE)
an ironic phrase used to indicate a humorous or contradictory situation where a failure has strangely resulted in a positive or intended outcome
ironic phrase about failing in a successful way

Examples

When he saw the error message saying task failed successfully, he couldn't help but laugh.
The software update crashed, but my files were restored — task failed successfully.
Her attempt to fix the bug didn't work, but the glitch disappeared anyway: task failed successfully.

Synset Examples

His computer displayed 'task failed successfully' after the glitch.
He didn't know whether to celebrate the 'task failed successfully' message.
She laughed when the program showed 'task failed successfully'.
