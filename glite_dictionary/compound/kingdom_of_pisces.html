kingdom of Pisces

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈpaɪsiz / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈpiːsiːz / (BrE)
a metaphorical realm representing creative inspiration, artistic sensitivity, and intuition, associated with the astrological sign Pisces
realm of Pisces-inspired creativity and sensitivity

Examples

She often retreats to the kingdom of Pisces when working on her art.
Writers say that in the kingdom of Pisces, imagination knows no bounds.
Many musicians find solace and ideas in the kingdom of Pisces.

Synset Examples

She feels at home in the kingdom of Pisces.
In the kingdom of Pisces, creativity flows freely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pisces - the twelfth sign of the zodiac, symbolized by fish
