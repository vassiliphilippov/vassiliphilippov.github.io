Guadalupe Mountains

1. PROPN

/ ɡwɑdəˈlup ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ɡwɑːdəˈluːp ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in southern New Mexico and western Texas, forming the southern extension of the Sacramento Mountains
mountain range in New Mexico and Texas

Examples

The Guadalupe Mountains are home to the highest peak in Texas, Guadalupe Peak.
Many hikers are drawn to the Guadalupe Mountains for their scenic trails and diverse wildlife.
Pine Springs is a popular gateway to the Guadalupe Mountains National Park.

Etymology

Named after the Spanish word 'Guadalupe', itself of Arabic origin, and the river/valley in Spain.
