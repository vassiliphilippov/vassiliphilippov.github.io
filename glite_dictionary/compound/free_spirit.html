free spirit

1. NOUN

/ fri ˈspɪrət / (AmE), / friː ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a person who acts independently, unconventionally, or without regard for social norms or expectations
an independent, unconventional person

Examples

Despite the pressures from her peers, she remained a free spirit and did things her own way.
He was known as a free spirit, never letting anyone dictate his choices.
Many artists are seen as free spirits, pursuing creativity above convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonconformist - a person who does not follow established customs or beliefs
  • Synonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control
  • Antonym: conformist - a person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices
