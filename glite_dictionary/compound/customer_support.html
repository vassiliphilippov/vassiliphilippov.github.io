customer support

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌs.tə.mɚ səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.tə.mə səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
assistance and advice provided by a company to those who buy or use its products or services
service providing assistance to customers

Examples

If you have any issues with your order, please contact customer support for help.
Our customer support team is available 24/7 to answer your questions.
She called customer support when her new phone stopped working.

Synset Examples

She wasn’t getting any answers from customer support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customer service - assistance provided by a company to its customers
