check-out procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛk aʊt prəˈsiːdʒər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk aʊt prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a series of steps or actions for inspecting or verifying something, especially before completion or departure
process for verifying or inspecting

Examples

The technician followed the check-out procedure carefully before starting the machine.
Please review the check-out procedure before leaving your hotel room.
A thorough check-out procedure helps prevent costly mistakes in the lab.

Synset Examples

they made a check of their equipment
the pilot ran through the check-out procedure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procedure - an established or official way of doing something
  • Synonym: inspection - the act of closely examining something
