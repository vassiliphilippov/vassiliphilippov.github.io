to plaster the ceiling

1. VERB

/ ˈplæstɚ ðə ˈsiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstə ðə ˈsiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
to apply plaster to the ceiling in order to create a smooth or even surface
apply plaster to a ceiling

Examples

It took an entire day to plaster the ceiling of the living room.
Before painting, make sure to plaster the ceiling for a flawless finish.
