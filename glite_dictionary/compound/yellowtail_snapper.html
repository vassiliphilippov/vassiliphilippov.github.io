yellowtail snapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌteɪl ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊteɪl ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
a brightly colored fish of the tropical Atlantic and Caribbean, notable for its broad yellow stripe along the side and tail, valued as a food fish
a colorful tropical food fish with a yellow stripe

Examples

The yellowtail snapper is a popular catch among recreational fishermen in Florida.
Grilled yellowtail snapper with herbs is a delicacy in many Caribbean restaurants.
Divers often spot schools of yellowtail snapper swimming near coral reefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapper - any of various marine fish of the family Lutjanidae
