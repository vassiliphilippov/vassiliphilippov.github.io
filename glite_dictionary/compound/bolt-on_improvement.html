bolt-on improvement

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlt ɑn ɪmˈpruvmənt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlt ɒn ɪmˈpruːvmənt / (BrE)
an additional feature or enhancement that can be added to an existing system without being integral to its original design, intended to increase its usefulness or performance
non-integral enhancement or add-on

Examples

The latest smartphone offers several bolt-on improvements to boost customer satisfaction.
Many companies use bolt-on improvements to update their products without major redesigns.
Our software package includes multiple bolt-on improvements for increased flexibility.

Synset Examples

The new features are just bolt-on improvements to the existing software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: add-on - an extra part or feature that can be added

Etymology

From 'bolt-on', meaning something that can be attached using bolts; applied figuratively to enhancements.
