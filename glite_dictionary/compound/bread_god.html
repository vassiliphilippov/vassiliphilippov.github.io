bread god

1. NOUN

/ brɛd ɡɑd / (AmE), / brɛd ɡɒd / (BrE)
An expert or highly skilled baker, often used humorously or informally.
expert or master baker (humorous)
informal

Examples

Since opening her bakery, everyone in town calls her the bread god.
If you watched him knead dough, you’d think he was a bread god.
After perfecting sourdough, Marcus liked to joke that he had become a true bread god.

Synset Examples

He thinks he’s the bread god after winning the baking contest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: master baker - a baker of the highest skill and status
  • Antonym: novice baker - a baker with little experience
