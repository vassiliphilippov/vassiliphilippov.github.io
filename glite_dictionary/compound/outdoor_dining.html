outdoor dining

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔr ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of eating meals outdoors at a restaurant or café, often on a patio, terrace, or sidewalk area
eating meals outdoors at a restaurant

Examples

Many restaurants have expanded their outdoor dining spaces to accommodate more guests during warm weather.
We enjoyed outdoor dining under twinkling lights on the patio last night.
Some cities have passed new regulations to support outdoor dining throughout the year.

Synset Examples

We wondered if outdoor dining would still be a thing next summer.
