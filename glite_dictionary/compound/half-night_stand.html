half-night stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf ˌnaɪt stænd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf ˌnaɪt stænd / (BrE)
a brief and non-committal romantic or sexual encounter, typically lasting only part of a night
very brief romantic or sexual encounter
informal

Examples

She joked that it was just a half-night stand, not the start of something serious.
Their meeting ended in a half-night stand, with both leaving before midnight.
He laughed and admitted it was only a half-night stand and nothing more.

Synset Examples

She laughed, saying it was more of a half-night stand than anything serious.
Their relationship turned out to be just a half-night stand.
He admitted it was only meant to be a half-night stand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one-night stand - a sexual relationship lasting only one night

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'one-night stand.'
