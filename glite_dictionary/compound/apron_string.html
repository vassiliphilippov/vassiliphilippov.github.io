apron string

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.prən ˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.prən ˌstrɪŋ / (BrE)
a cord or tie attached to an apron and used to fasten it around the waist, usually referred to in the plural
cord for tying an apron at the waist

Examples

She tied the apron strings tightly around her waist before starting to cook.
The apron string broke, and her apron fell to the floor.
He tugged at the apron strings as a way to get his mother's attention while she was baking.
