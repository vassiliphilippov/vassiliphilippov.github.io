red-spotted purple

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈspɑtɪd ˈpɜrt͡ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈspɒtɪd ˈpɜːpl̩ / (BrE)
a North American butterfly (Limenitis arthemis astyanax) similar to the banded purple but distinguished by red spots on the underwing surfaces
North American butterfly with red spots under wings

Examples

The red-spotted purple is often seen fluttering through wooded areas in summer.
I spotted a red-spotted purple resting with its wings closed, showing the distinctive red markings.
Unlike the banded purple, the red-spotted purple has bright red spots on the underside of its wings.
