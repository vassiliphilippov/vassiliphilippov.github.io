prairie aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛri ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɛəri ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a violet-flowered perennial plant of central United States, with solitary flower heads, belonging to the aster family
violet-flowered perennial aster of central US

Examples

We found a cluster of prairie aster blooming along the trail in early summer.
The prairie aster thrives in open grasslands across the central United States.
Bees are attracted to the vibrant flowers of the prairie aster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the daisy family with star-shaped flower heads
