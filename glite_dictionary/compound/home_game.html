home game

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ɡeɪm / (AmE), / həʊm ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a sports game played at the team's own venue rather than at their opponent's venue
game played at team's own venue

Examples

We have five home games left before the playoffs begin.
The team always plays better during a home game.
Attendance was higher than expected at last night's home game.

2. NOUN

/ hoʊm ɡeɪm / (AmE), / həʊm ɡeɪm / (BrE)

game played at a team's own venue

Examples

Tickets for the next home game sold out within hours.
Players often feel more confident during a home game because of local support.
