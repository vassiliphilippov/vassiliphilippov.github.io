exponential expression

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˌspoʊnɛnʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˌspəʊnɛnʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a mathematical expression in which a constant, especially the base e, is raised to a power
mathematical expression with a base raised to a power

Examples

The equation contains an exponential expression involving the constant e.
In advanced calculus, we often deal with exponential expressions when solving differential equations.
To simplify the formula, rewrite the exponential expression as a single power.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exponential function - a function of the form f(x) = aᵡ, especially with base e
