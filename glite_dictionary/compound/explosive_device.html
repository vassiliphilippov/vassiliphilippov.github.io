explosive device

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsploʊsɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a device that is designed to burst or detonate violently due to internal energy, typically as a weapon or for demolition
a device designed to explode

Examples

The security team found an explosive device hidden under the bridge during their inspection.
A homemade explosive device was safely defused by the bomb squad.
Police are investigating the origins of the explosive device discovered in the abandoned warehouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomb - an explosive weapon that is detonated under specific conditions
  • Synonym: detonator - a device used to set off an explosive device
