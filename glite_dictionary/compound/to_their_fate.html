to their fate

1. ADV

/ tə ðɛr feɪt / (AmE), / tə ðɛə feɪt / (BrE)
left without help or support, allowing circumstances to determine what happens to them
left without help or support

Examples

The explorers were left to their fate when the rescue team failed to arrive.
Fearing capture, the soldiers abandoned the prisoners to their fate.
Rather than intervene, she allowed the plans to unravel and left everyone to their fate.

Synset Examples

The group left the weak member to their fate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abandon - to leave someone or something completely and forever
