legal opinion

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːɡəl əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
a formal written statement by a judge or court explaining the reasoning behind a judicial decision
court's written explanation of a decision
legal

Examples

The judge issued a detailed legal opinion outlining the reasons for her ruling.
Law students are often required to analyze past legal opinions to understand how courts interpret statutes.
The Supreme Court's legal opinion set an important precedent for future cases.

Synset Examples

opinions are usually written by a single judge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgment - the official decision of a court in a case
