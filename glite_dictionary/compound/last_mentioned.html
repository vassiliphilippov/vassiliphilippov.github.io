last mentioned

1. ADJ

/ læst ˈmɛnʃənd / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɛnʃənd / (BrE)
referring to the person or thing most recently mentioned or stated
most recently cited person or thing

Examples

Of the two presidents discussed, the last mentioned served during a major economic crisis.
If you have questions about the painters, contact the last mentioned for more details.
The last mentioned proposal was favored by most members of the committee.

Synset Examples

the last mentioned pianist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aforementioned - mentioned previously
  • Antonym: former - the first of two people or things previously mentioned
  • Synonym: latter - the second of two people or things previously mentioned
