pipe fitter

1. NOUN

/ paɪp ˈfɪtər / (AmE), / paɪp ˈfɪtə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who installs and repairs pipes, fixtures, and related appliances, especially for water, gas, or steam systems
worker who installs and repairs pipes

Examples

The factory hired a pipe fitter to overhaul their steam system.
After completing her apprenticeship, she became a certified pipe fitter.
If there is a gas leak, you should call a professional pipe fitter immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumber - a person who installs and repairs water pipes and fittings
