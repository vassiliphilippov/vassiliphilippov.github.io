big time

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ taɪm / (AmE), / bɪɡ taɪm / (BrE)
the highest or most successful level of an activity or profession, especially in entertainment or sports.
the highest level of success in a field
informal

Examples

She finally made it to the big time after years of hard work in small clubs.
Many dream of reaching the big time in Hollywood, but few succeed.
After his latest hit, he's definitely playing in the big time now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limelight - the center of public attention or success
