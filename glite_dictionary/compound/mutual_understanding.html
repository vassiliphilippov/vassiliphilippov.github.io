mutual understanding

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːtʃuəl ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtʃu.əl ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more people share similar feelings or opinions and are able to sympathize with each other
shared sympathy or agreement

Examples

After a long conversation, they finally reached a mutual understanding about the issue.
Building mutual understanding is essential for successful teamwork.
Our friendship is based on mutual understanding and respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapport - a close and harmonious relationship
  • Antonym: misunderstanding - a failure to understand correctly
