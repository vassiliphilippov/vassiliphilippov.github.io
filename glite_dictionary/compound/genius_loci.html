genius loci

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːni.əs ˈloʊ.saɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːni.əs ˈləʊ.sɪ / (BrE)
the distinctive spirit or atmosphere of a particular place
the spirit or atmosphere of a place

Examples

The old library has a genius loci that inspires quiet study and reflection.
Architects strive to preserve the genius loci when designing new buildings in historic neighborhoods.
Each city has its own genius loci, shaped by its people and history.

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'spirit of the place'. In Roman mythology, the protective spirit of a place.
