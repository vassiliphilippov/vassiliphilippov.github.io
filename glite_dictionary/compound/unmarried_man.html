unmarried man

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈmærid mæn / (AmE), / ʌnˈmærid man / (BrE)
a man who has never been married
man who has never married

Examples

He is an unmarried man who focuses entirely on his career.
In some cultures, an unmarried man may face social pressure to marry by a certain age.
The invitation was addressed to all unmarried men in the village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bachelor - an unmarried man, typically young
