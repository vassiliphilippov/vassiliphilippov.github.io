soft-shoe shuffle

1. NOUN

/ sɔft ʃu ˈʃʌfəl / (AmE), / sɒft ʃuː ˈʃʌf(ə)l / (BrE)
a style of tap dancing performed with soft-soled shoes, characterized by smooth, gliding movements and subtle footwork
a style of tap dancing with soft shoes

Examples

The old movie star performed a graceful soft-shoe shuffle for the delighted audience.
He learned the soft-shoe shuffle as part of his tap dance training.
The comedian ended his act with a classic soft-shoe shuffle across the stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap dance - a dance performed in tap shoes with rhythmic tapping sounds
