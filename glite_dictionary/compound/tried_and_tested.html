tried and tested

1. ADJ

/ traɪd ænd ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / traɪd ənd ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
proven to be effective or reliable after being used or examined over a period of time
proven effective or reliable

Examples

These tried and tested techniques have been used successfully for decades.
We always rely on tried and tested equipment for our expeditions.
She prefers a tried and tested solution rather than experimenting with something new.

Synset Examples

I prefer using tried and tested methods for my projects.
This recipe is tried and tested by many chefs.
Her tried and tested strategies always yield great results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proven - shown by evidence or experience to be true or reliable
  • Synonym: trusted - regarded as reliable or dependable
  • Antonym: untested - not tried or proven; lacking experience
