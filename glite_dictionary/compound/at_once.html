at once

1. ADV

/ ət ˈwʌns / (AmE), / ət ˈwʌns / (BrE)
At the same time; simultaneously.
simultaneously, at the same time

Examples

She managed to answer both questions at once without hesitation.
The twins spoke at once, making it hard to understand them.
It's difficult to concentrate on two things at once.

Synset Examples

Her ability to sing and dance at once? That's something.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneously - occurring at the same time
  • Synonym: concurrently - happening or existing together in time
