necklace tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛk.ləs triː / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.lɪs triː / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Ormosia, whose brightly colored seeds are often used as beads in necklaces and other jewelry
tree with seeds used as beads

Examples

The seeds of the necklace tree are prized for making colorful jewelry.
In the rainforest, I saw a necklace tree laden with bright red seeds.
Many local artisans use necklace tree seeds in their handcrafted beadwork.
