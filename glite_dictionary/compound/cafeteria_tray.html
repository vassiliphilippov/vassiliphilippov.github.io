cafeteria tray

1. NOUN

/ kəˌfɪˈtɪriə treɪ / (AmE), / kəˌfiːˈtɪəriə treɪ / (BrE)
a flat, rectangular tray used for carrying food and drink in a cafeteria
tray for carrying food in a cafeteria

Examples

He carried his lunch on a cafeteria tray to the nearest table.
Please return your cafeteria tray to the designated rack after you finish eating.
The cafeteria tray was stacked with plates, drinks, and utensils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tray - a flat, shallow container used for carrying or holding things
