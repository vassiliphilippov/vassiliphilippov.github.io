raisin-nut cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.zən nʌt ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.zɪn nʌt ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
a cookie made with a filling or mixture of raisins and nuts, often forming a paste inside the dough
cookie with raisin and nut filling

Examples

My grandmother bakes delicious raisin-nut cookies for the holidays every year.
Would you like a cup of tea with a homemade raisin-nut cookie?
The bakery's raisin-nut cookies are filled with a sweet, chewy paste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raisin cookie - cookie containing raisins, sometimes without nuts
  • Degree Lower: oatmeal raisin cookie - cookie made with oatmeal and raisins
