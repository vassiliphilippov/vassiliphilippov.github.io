organic brain syndrome

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈɡænɪk breɪn ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡanɪk breɪn ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
A mental disorder caused by physical injury or disease affecting the structure or function of the brain.
mental disorder from brain damage or disease
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the patient's confusion was due to organic brain syndrome following a head injury.
People with organic brain syndrome may experience memory loss and difficulty thinking clearly.
Chronic alcoholism can sometimes result in organic brain syndrome if the brain tissue is damaged.
