hand puppet

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈpʌp.ɪt / (AmE), / hænd ˈpʌp.ɪt / (BrE)
a puppet made of fabric and a hollow head that is worn over the hand and manipulated by the fingers
puppet worn on the hand

Examples

The children laughed as the hand puppet danced around the stage.
She made a tiger hand puppet in her art class.
A hand puppet can be used to tell stories in a creative way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppet - a movable model of a person or animal controlled by strings, rods, or by hand
