family dinner

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæmɪli ˈdɪnər / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈdɪnə / (BrE)
A meal, typically dinner, that is shared by members of a family, often as a regular social event.
shared evening meal with family

Examples

We always try to have a family dinner every Sunday night, no matter how busy we are.
The children enjoy helping to prepare the family dinner on holidays.
A weekly family dinner gives everyone a chance to reconnect and share stories.

Synset Examples

Poppa always tells the best stories during family dinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinner - the main meal of the day, usually eaten in the evening
