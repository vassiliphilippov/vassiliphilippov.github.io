to respecting the boundary

1. VERB

/ rɪˈspɛktɪŋ ðə ˈbaʊndəri / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktɪŋ ðə ˈbaʊndəri / (BrE)
recognizing and honoring personal or interpersonal limits, especially in social or emotional contexts
acknowledging personal or interpersonal limits

Examples

In healthy relationships, respecting the boundary helps maintain trust and comfort.
The counselor emphasized the importance of respecting the boundary of others during group sessions.
By respecting the boundary, she showed empathy and understanding for her friend's needs.

Synset Examples

Respecting the boundary is crucial in any friendship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundaries - limits that define acceptable behavior or personal space
  • Antonym: crossing the line - overstepping accepted limits or boundaries
