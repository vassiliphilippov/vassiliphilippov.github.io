hog plum bush

1. NOUN

/ hɑɡ plʌm bʊʃ / (AmE), / hɒɡ plʌm bʊʃ / (BrE)
a small North American shrubby tree that bears small edible yellow to reddish fruits, commonly called hog plums
small American tree with edible yellow-red fruit

Examples

The hog plum bush thrives in sandy soils and produces fruit in late summer.
Birds are often attracted to the hog plum bush for its sweet, yellow fruit.
She planted a hog plum bush in her garden to attract wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hog plum - the edible fruit of certain trees and shrubs called hog plums
