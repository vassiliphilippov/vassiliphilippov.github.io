University of Cambridge

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnəˈvɜrsəti əv ˈkæmbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti əv ˈkæmbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a prestigious university located in Cambridge, England, known for its academic excellence and rich history
prestigious university in Cambridge, England

Examples

She was accepted to study law at the University of Cambridge last year.
Many notable scientists have been affiliated with the University of Cambridge.
The University of Cambridge is one of the oldest universities in the world.

Etymology

Founded in 1209, named after the city of Cambridge, England.
