Italian rice

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən ɹaɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈtalɪən rʌɪs / (BrE)
rice cooked with broth and often finished with grated cheese, in the Italian style; commonly known as risotto
rice dish cooked in Italian style

Examples

The chef recommended trying the Italian rice with wild mushrooms as his specialty dish.
At the party, everyone loved the creamy Italian rice topped with Parmesan cheese.
She learned to make authentic Italian rice during her trip to Milan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risotto - Italian dish of rice cooked in broth to a creamy consistency
