chunky heel

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌŋki hiːl / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋki hiːl / (BrE)
A wide, often square-shaped heel on a shoe or boot, designed for stability and available in various heights.
wide, square-shaped shoe heel

Examples

She prefers shoes with a chunky heel for added comfort during long days at work.
The designer's new collection features sandals with a bold chunky heel.
A chunky heel provides better balance than a stiletto.

Synset Examples

Shoes with block heels will give you a few additional inches while still being fairly comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: block heel - a thick, block-shaped heel on shoes
