Nutmeg State

1. PROPN

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Connecticut, one of the original 13 colonies in the United States.
nickname for Connecticut, USA

Examples

She grew up in the Nutmeg State and still visits Connecticut every summer.
The Nutmeg State played a crucial role in the American Revolution.
Many people from the Nutmeg State enjoy visiting the scenic coastline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Connecticut - a state in the northeastern United States, one of the original thirteen colonies

Etymology

Refers to the historical association of Connecticut with the trade of nutmeg, and possibly to the state's reputation for shrewdness.
