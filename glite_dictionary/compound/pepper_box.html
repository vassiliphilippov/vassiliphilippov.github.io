pepper box

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ˌbɒks / (BrE)
A small container with a perforated top used for sprinkling ground pepper onto food.
container for sprinkling pepper

Examples

She reached for the pepper box to add some spice to her soup.
The vintage pepper box matched the old-fashioned salt cellar on the dinner table.
After refilling the pepper box, he placed it neatly next to the napkins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper shaker - a container with a perforated top for sprinkling pepper
  • Synonym: salt shaker - a container with a perforated top for sprinkling salt
