guitar-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ɡɪˈtɑr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ɡɪˈtɑː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a guitar.
in the form of a guitar

Examples

The artist displayed a large guitar-shaped sculpture in the center of the gallery.
She bought a guitar-shaped birthday cake for her brother, who is a musician.
The hotel features a unique, guitar-shaped swimming pool that attracts many guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guitar - a musical instrument with six strings, played by plucking or strumming
