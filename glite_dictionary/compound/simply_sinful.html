simply sinful

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪmpli ˈsɪnfəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpli ˈsɪnfʊl / (BrE)
Extremely delicious or indulgent, especially in a way that feels slightly excessive or decadent.
extremely delicious or indulgent
informal

Examples

That cheesecake is simply sinful—I couldn't resist a second slice.
The ice cream sundae was simply sinful with all its rich toppings.
She described the handmade truffles as simply sinful and absolutely addictive.
