bronzing powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːnzɪŋ ˌpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɒnzɪŋ ˌpaʊdə / (BrE)
A cosmetic powder in shades of brown, applied to the skin to give the appearance of a tan.
cosmetic powder for a tanned look

Examples

She dusted a little bronzing powder on her cheeks for a sun-kissed glow.
A good bronzing powder can make your skin look radiant all year round.
Avoid applying too much bronzing powder, as it can create an unnatural effect.

Synset Examples

Sculpting your face with a bronzing powder will make it appear slimmer.
If you don't like sunbathing, you can try a temporary tanner, like a bronzer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronzer - cosmetic used to create a tanned appearance
