all age

1. ADJ

/ ɔl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɔːl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
Suitable for people of every age group; appropriate or intended for both children and adults.
appropriate for all ages

Examples

The new theater policy makes all performances all age events.
This board game is truly all age and fun for the whole family.
The festival is designed to be all age, so everyone can attend.

Synset Examples

The humor in 'Urinal of the Planets' appeals to all ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family-friendly - suitable for both children and adults
