guidance device

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdəns dɪˌvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdəns dɪˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a system or piece of equipment used for automatically controlling and directing the path of a vehicle, especially a missile or aircraft
system for automatic vehicle control

Examples

The missile's guidance device ensures it stays on target during its flight.
Modern aircraft rely on an advanced guidance device for safe and precise landings.
Scientists tested the guidance device in various weather conditions to verify its reliability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guidance system - system for controlling and directing the path of a vehicle
