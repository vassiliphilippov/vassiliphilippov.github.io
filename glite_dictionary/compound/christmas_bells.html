Christmas bells

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs bɛlz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs bɛlz / (BrE)
any of several Australian plants of the genus Blandfordia, with large, bell-shaped orange or red flowers, often blooming around Christmas.
Australian plant with bell-shaped red or orange flowers

Examples

The vibrant Christmas bells added a splash of color to the Australian bushland in December.
Gardeners often plant Christmas bells for their striking blooms around the holiday season.
We admired the clusters of Christmas bells along the walking trail, glowing in the summer sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Blandfordia - genus of Australian plants with bell-shaped flowers

Etymology

Named for its bright, bell-shaped flowers and typical blooming period near Christmas in Australia.
