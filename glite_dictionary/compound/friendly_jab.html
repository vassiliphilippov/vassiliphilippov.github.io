friendly jab

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndli dʒæb / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli dʒæb / (BrE)
a light, playful poke or touch, usually given as a gesture of camaraderie or affection
a playful poke or touch

Examples

She laughed and gave him a friendly jab in the ribs.
His friendly jab was meant as a sign of affection, not annoyance.
With a friendly jab, she jokingly reminded her friend to stay awake.

Synset Examples

He gave me a friendly jab on the shoulder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poke - a quick push with a finger or object
  • Degree Higher: punch - a blow with a closed fist, usually harder than a jab
