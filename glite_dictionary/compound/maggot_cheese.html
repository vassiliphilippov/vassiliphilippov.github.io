maggot cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæɡət tʃiːz / (AmE), / ˈmæɡət tʃiːz / (BrE)
A type of cheese that contains live maggots, regarded as a delicacy in some cultures.
cheese with live maggots, a delicacy

Examples

Many tourists are surprised to learn that maggot cheese is eaten as a specialty in certain parts of Italy.
The importation of maggot cheese is banned in some countries due to strict food safety regulations.
At the tasting event, she hesitated before trying a slice of maggot cheese.

Synset Examples

Maggot cheese is considered a delicacy in some cultures.
They served maggot cheese at the exotic food festival.
