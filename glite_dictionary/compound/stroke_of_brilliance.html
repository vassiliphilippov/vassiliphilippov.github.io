stroke of brilliance

1. NOUN

/ stroʊk əv ˈbrɪljəns / (AmE), / strəʊk əv ˈbrɪliəns / (BrE)
a sudden instance of exceptional creativity, intelligence, or cleverness
sudden flash of creative genius

Examples

Winning the competition was a stroke of brilliance on her part, thanks to her unique approach.
His solution to the complex problem was a real stroke of brilliance that impressed everyone.
The architect's design showed a stroke of brilliance that set the building apart from others.

Synset Examples

His latest painting was a stroke of brilliance.
Her innovative idea was a stroke of business brilliance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flash of genius - sudden creative inspiration or intelligence
  • Antonym: stroke of luck - a sudden, unexpected event that brings good fortune
