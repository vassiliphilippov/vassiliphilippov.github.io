star city

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈsɪti / (AmE), / stɑː ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a city that is highly prominent, popular, or renowned, often as a travel, cultural, or economic destination
highly prominent or popular city

Examples

Many aspiring actors head to a star city in hopes of making it big.
Paris is often considered a star city for fashion enthusiasts.
Hosting the Olympics brought even more attention to the star city.

Synset Examples

She dreams of moving to a star city like Los Angeles.
Tourists flock to the star city for its beaches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metropolis - a large, important city, often the chief city of a region
  • Antonym: backwater - a place or situation regarded as isolated or less significant
