construction project

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a planned and organized building or engineering task, usually involving the creation or renovation of structures or infrastructure
planned building or engineering task

Examples

The company won a large contract for a new construction project downtown.
Any delays in material delivery can set the entire construction project back by weeks.
She was hired as the manager for a hospital construction project in the city.

Synset Examples

The unsifted sand caused issues in the construction project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: project - an organized planned undertaking
  • Synonym: construction - the act or process of building something
