Unbrako wrench

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈbreɪkoʊ rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrɑːkəʊ rɛntʃ / (BrE)
A brand-named wrench with a hexagonal cross-section used for driving bolts and screws with hexagonal sockets, especially Allen screws.
hexagonal wrench for Allen screws

Examples

He tightened the bolts with an Unbrako wrench to ensure a secure fit.
This machine requires an Unbrako wrench for most of its assembly.
Do you have an Unbrako wrench to adjust this Allen screw?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Allen wrench - A wrench used to turn bolts and screws with hexagonal sockets
  • Synonym: hex key - A tool for hexagonal socket screws

Etymology

‘Unbrako’ is a trademark originally from the Unbrako company, known for producing socket screws and related tools.
