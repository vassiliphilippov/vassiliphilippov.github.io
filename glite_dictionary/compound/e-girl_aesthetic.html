e-girl aesthetic

1. NOUN

/ ˈi ɡɜrl ɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˈiː ɡɜːl ɛsˈθetɪk / (BrE)
a style of fashion and makeup characterized by bright colors, playful motifs, and influences from internet and alternative youth culture
fashion style with bright, playful look
informal

Examples

The e-girl aesthetic features bold eyeliner, dyed hair, and playful accessories.
Many teenagers on social media embrace the e-girl aesthetic to express their individuality.
She revamped her wardrobe to adopt a more distinct e-girl aesthetic.

Synset Examples

The e-girl aesthetic is all about vibrant colors.

Etymology

From 'e-girl', a blend of 'electronic' and 'girl', denoting internet-based youth subculture, and 'aesthetic', from Greek aisthētikos (perceptible by the senses).
