warm sunlight

1. NOUN

/ wɔrm ˈsʌnˌlaɪt / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈsʌnlaɪt / (BrE)
sunlight that feels pleasantly warm to the skin
pleasantly warm rays of the sun

Examples

She enjoyed the warm sunlight as she sat on the park bench.
The cat stretched out in a patch of warm sunlight on the living room floor.
After days of rain, the warm sunlight lifted everyone's mood.

Synset Examples

She read her book in the warm sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunshine - direct sunlight, especially when bright and pleasant
