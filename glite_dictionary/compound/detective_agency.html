detective agency

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɛktɪv ˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪv ˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a business that investigates and gathers information for clients, often regarding personal, legal, or financial matters
business that investigates for clients

Examples

After noticing suspicious activity, she hired a detective agency to look into the matter.
The detective agency specializes in background checks and surveillance for its clients.
He opened his own detective agency after years of working in law enforcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private investigator - a person hired to undertake investigatory services
  • Antonym: police department - a government organization responsible for enforcing laws and investigating crimes
