tax collection

1. NOUN

/ tæks kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / tæks kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of collecting taxes, typically by a government authority
the collection of taxes

Examples

The government improved its tax collection methods to increase public revenue.
I work in the tax collection department at the city council.
Delays in tax collection can affect the funding of public services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxation - the system of levying taxes
  • Antonym: tax evasion - the illegal non-payment or underpayment of taxes
