to trace the origins

1. VERB

/ treɪs ði ˈɔrɪdʒənz / (AmE), / treɪs ði ˈɒrɪdʒɪnz / (BrE)
to investigate and determine the beginnings or sources of something
investigate the beginnings or sources

Examples

Scientists worked tirelessly to trace the origins of the mysterious illness.
The historian was able to trace the origins of the tradition back several centuries.
Experts used genetic markers to trace the origins of the invasive species.

Synset Examples

They traced the origins back to a Chinese virus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trace the source - investigate the source of something
