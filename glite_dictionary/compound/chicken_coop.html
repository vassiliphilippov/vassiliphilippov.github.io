chicken coop

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn kuːp / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn kuːp / (BrE)
a small building or enclosure where chickens or other poultry are kept
shelter for chickens or poultry

Examples

The old chicken coop in the backyard needed repairs before new hens could move in.
Every morning, she collected eggs from the chicken coop behind the barn.
A fox broke into the chicken coop and scared the flock last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: henhouse - a shelter where hens are kept
