old bag

1. NOUN

/ oʊld bæg / (AmE), / əʊld bæɡ / (BrE)
an insulting term for an unattractive or bad-tempered woman, especially an older woman
offensive term for an unpleasant woman
slang offensive

Examples

He called her an old bag after their argument, which shocked everyone in the room.
In the film, the villain is portrayed as a bitter old bag whom nobody likes.
It's rude and disrespectful to refer to someone as an old bag.

2. NOUN

/ oʊld bæg / (AmE), / əʊld bæɡ / (BrE)
an unpleasant or annoying person, especially an older woman who is regarded as old-fashioned or overly serious (informal, derogatory)
a derogatory name for an unpleasant person
informal offensive british

Examples

She was such an old bag that everyone tried to avoid her at work.
The kids in the neighborhood always complain about the old bag who scolds them for playing too loudly.
Don’t be an old bag and just let people have some fun.
