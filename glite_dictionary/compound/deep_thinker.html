deep thinker

1. NOUN

/ diːp ˈθɪŋkər / (AmE), / diːp ˈθɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who reflects seriously and profoundly about complex or abstract topics
person who thinks profoundly about complex issues

Examples

She is regarded as a deep thinker who often challenges conventional ideas.
Many writers are deep thinkers, constantly exploring the meaning of life.
His reputation as a deep thinker made others frequently seek his advice.

Synset Examples

She’s known as a deep thinker among her peers.
Being a deep thinker, he often surprises us with insights.
As a deep thinker, he often contemplates life's mysteries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philosopher - a person engaged or learned in philosophy, especially as an academic discipline
