dormitory room

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔːr.məˌtɔːr.i ruːm / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.mɪ.tər.i ruːm / (BrE)
A large sleeping room, especially in a school or institution, containing several beds.
a large sleeping room with several beds

Examples

The students shared a dormitory room during their first year at university.
Lights out in the dormitory room was strictly enforced at 10 p.m.
There were six beds in the dormitory room, providing space for all the camp counselors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dormitory - a building or part of a building where people sleep, especially at a school or college
  • Synonym: barracks - a building or group of buildings used to house soldiers

Etymology

From Latin 'dormitorium' (sleeping place) + 'room'.
