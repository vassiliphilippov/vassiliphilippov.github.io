blueberry soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌbɛri suːp / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri suːp / (BrE)
A sweet or savory soup made primarily from blueberries, often served chilled as a dessert or appetizer.
soup made from blueberries

Examples

On hot days, blueberry soup makes a refreshing start to a meal.
The chef garnished the blueberry soup with a swirl of cream and mint leaves.
You can serve blueberry soup either warm or cold, depending on your preference.

Synset Examples

Blueberry soup is perfect for summer.
