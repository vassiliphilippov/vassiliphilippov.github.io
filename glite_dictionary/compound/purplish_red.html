purplish red

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːplɪʃ ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ ˈrɛd / (BrE)
a shade of red with a tinge or hue of purple
red color with a hint of purple

Examples

The artist chose a purplish red for the background of the painting.
She wore a dress in a striking purplish red that matched her lipstick.
The sunset filled the sky with hues of purplish red and orange.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - a rich deep red color inclining to purple
  • Degree Lower: maroon - a brownish-red color
