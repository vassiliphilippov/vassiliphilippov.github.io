wine taster

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈteɪstər / (AmE), / waɪn ˈteɪstə / (BrE)
a person who professionally evaluates the quality and characteristics of wines
person who evaluates wine quality

Examples

The renowned wine taster gave high marks to the new vintage.
As a wine taster, she developed an exceptional palate for subtle flavors in wine.
Each wine taster at the event shared their personal tasting notes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sommelier - a wine steward or wine expert, especially in a restaurant
  • Antonym: wine maker - a person who makes wine
