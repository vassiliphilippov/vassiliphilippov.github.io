city man

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti mæn / (AmE), / ˈsɪti mæn / (BrE)
A male financier who works in one of the banks or financial institutions in the City of London.
male financier in London's financial district
british

Examples

After graduating from university, he dreamed of becoming a city man in London.
The boardroom was filled with experienced city men discussing the merger.
As a senior city man, he managed high-profile investment portfolios.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banker - a person who works in banking, especially at a high level
  • Synonym: financier - a person concerned with the management of large amounts of money
  • Antonym: countryman - a person from the countryside, sometimes contrasted with city dwellers
