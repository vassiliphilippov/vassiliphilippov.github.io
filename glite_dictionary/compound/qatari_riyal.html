Qatari riyal

1. NOUN

/ kəˈtɑːri riːˈjɑːl / (AmE), / kəˈtɑːri rɪˈjɑːl / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of Qatar
currency of Qatar

Examples

The exchange rate for the Qatari riyal has remained stable against the US dollar.
She exchanged her dollars for Qatari riyal at the airport in Doha.
All prices in Qatar are marked in Qatari riyal, making shopping straightforward for visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riyal - the monetary unit used in several Middle Eastern countries
  • Degree Lower: Qatari dirham - a former subunit of currency in Qatar

Etymology

From Arabic 'riyāl', influenced by the Spanish 'real' (royal), with 'Qatari' denoting relation to Qatar.
