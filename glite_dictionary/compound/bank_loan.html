bank loan

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk loʊn / (AmE), / baŋk ləʊn / (BrE)
A sum of money borrowed from a bank, to be paid back with interest by an agreed date.
money borrowed from a bank with interest

Examples

He applied for a bank loan to help start his business.
The bank loan must be repaid within five years, along with the interest.
They used a bank loan to finance their new home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan - an amount of money that is borrowed
