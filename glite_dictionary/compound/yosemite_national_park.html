Yosemite National Park

1. PROPN

/ joʊˈsɛmɪti ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / jəʊˈsɛmɪti ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in California known for its spectacular waterfalls, granite cliffs, and diverse natural scenery
famous national park in California

Examples

We spent our summer vacation hiking in Yosemite National Park and marveled at the waterfalls.
Every year, millions of tourists visit Yosemite National Park to explore its stunning natural beauty.
Ansel Adams became famous for his black-and-white photographs of Yosemite National Park.

Etymology

Named after the Yosemite Valley, itself named from a Native American (Southern Miwok) word for 'killer' used for the region's indigenous people.
