mumpish look

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌmpɪʃ lʊk / (AmE), / ˈmʌmpɪʃ lʊk / (BrE)
a sullen or sulky facial expression
sullen or sulky expression

Examples

She sat in the corner with a mumpish look that lasted all evening.
His mumpish look told everyone he was upset about missing the party.
A mumpish look crossed her face when her idea was rejected.

Synset Examples

She had a mumpish look after hearing the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sullen - bad-tempered and sulky
  • Antonym: cheerful look - a bright or happy facial expression

Etymology

From 'mumpish', probably from 'mump' (to sulk or pout) + '-ish' suffix, meaning inclined to be sullen.
