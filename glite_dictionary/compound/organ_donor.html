organ donor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɡən ˈdoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən ˈdəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who gives one of their organs, either during life or after death, to be transplanted into another person
person who donates an organ for transplant
medical

Examples

The hospital thanked the organ donor whose kidney saved the patient's life.
After her accident, she became an organ donor and helped several people.
Being an organ donor can give others a second chance at life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donor - a person who gives something, especially blood or a body part
