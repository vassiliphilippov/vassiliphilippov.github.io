Dixie cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪksi ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈdɪksi ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a small, disposable paper cup typically used for drinking water or other beverages
small disposable paper cup

Examples

She grabbed a Dixie cup from the dispenser to get a drink of water.
Please throw your used Dixie cup in the trash after the party.
At the doctor's office, they gave me a Dixie cup to rinse my mouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paper cup - a cup made of paper, usually disposable

Etymology

Dixie was a trademark of the American Cup Company for disposable paper cups, popularized in the early 20th century.
