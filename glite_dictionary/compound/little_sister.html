little sister

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əl ˈsɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a sister who is younger than oneself
younger sister

Examples

Even though my little sister is younger, she is much taller than I am now.
I used to read bedtime stories to my little sister when we were kids.
My little sister starts high school next year, and I'm so proud of her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: younger sister - a sister who is younger than oneself
  • Antonym: big sister - a sister who is older than oneself
