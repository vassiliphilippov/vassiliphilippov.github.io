tough experience

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / tʌf ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
a situation that is difficult, stressful, or challenging to endure
difficult or challenging situation

Examples

Losing his job last year was a tough experience that changed his outlook on life.
Training for the marathon in winter conditions was a tough experience for all the runners.
She learned a lot from her tough experience moving to a new country alone.

Synset Examples

David's miscarriage was a tough experience for us all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard time - a period of difficulty or hardship
