liver pudding

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvər ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a type of sausage made from ground pork liver and typically seasoned and cooked in a loaf or sausage form
pork liver sausage (Southern US dish)
american

Examples

Many people in the South enjoy slices of liver pudding served with eggs for breakfast.
My grandmother makes her own liver pudding using an old family recipe.
You can find liver pudding in most grocery stores across North Carolina.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liverwurst - a type of sausage made with pork liver
