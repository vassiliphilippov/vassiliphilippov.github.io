dust-collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌst kəˌlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈdʌst kəˌlɛktə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to capture and remove dust and debris from the air, especially in workshops or industrial settings
device for capturing dust and debris

Examples

The new workshop has a powerful dust-collector to ensure a clean working environment.
Without a dust-collector, sawdust quickly accumulates around the machinery.
They upgraded their dust-collector to handle finer particles more efficiently.

Synset Examples

Every woodworking shop needs a good dust-collector.
He installed a dust-collector to keep the workshop clean.
The dust-collector saved us hours of cleaning time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical appliance that sucks up dust and dirt, especially from floors
