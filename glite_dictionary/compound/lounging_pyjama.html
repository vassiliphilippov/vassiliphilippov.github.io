lounging pyjama

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ pəˈdʒɑmə / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ pəˈdʒɑːmə / (BrE)
A type of loose-fitting clothing, similar to pajamas, designed to be worn while relaxing at home.
relaxed-fit pajamas for lounging

Examples

On weekends, she prefers to stay home in her lounging pyjama and read a good book.
He bought a new set of lounging pyjamas for the winter evenings at home.
After work, I change into my lounging pyjama to unwind and watch TV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pajamas - lightweight loose-fitting trousers worn for sleeping or relaxing

Etymology

From 'lounge' (to relax) and 'pyjama', from Hindi 'pajama' (leg covering).
