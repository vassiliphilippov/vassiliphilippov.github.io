stopped up

1. ADJ

/ stɑpt ʌp / (AmE), / stɒpt ʌp / (BrE)
(of a nose or passage) blocked so that nothing can pass through, especially due to illness or congestion.
blocked, especially of a nose

Examples

My nose has been stopped up all week because of allergies.
If your ears are stopped up, it can be hard to hear clearly.
She spoke with a stopped up nose, her voice sounding congested.

Synset Examples

a stopped (or stopped-up) nose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blocked - obstructed so that passage is prevented
