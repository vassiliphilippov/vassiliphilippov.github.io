iron eagle

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn ˈiːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a sculpture or artistic representation of an eagle made of iron, often used as a symbol of strength, power, and liberty
an eagle figure made of iron symbolizing power

Examples

The town square features an impressive iron eagle perched atop the war memorial.
Collectors value antique iron eagles for their craftsmanship and symbolism.
The dictator adopted the iron eagle as the national emblem to project authority.

Synset Examples

The iron eagle symbolizes strength and freedom.
He admired the iron eagle sculpture in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eagle - a large bird of prey noted for its strength and keen vision
