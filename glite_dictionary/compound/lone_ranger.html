Lone Ranger

1. NOUN

/ loʊn ˈreɪndʒər / (AmE), / ləʊn ˈreɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person who acts independently and solves problems or takes action alone, often regarded as heroic or solitary
person who acts independently

Examples

After the company merged, he felt like the Lone Ranger, working on projects without any partners.
She tends to act as a Lone Ranger rather than collaborating with her colleagues.
In every crisis, he takes on the role of Lone Ranger and handles matters by himself.

Synset Examples

He feels like the Lone Ranger, always handling problems himself.
In the meeting, she played the Lone Ranger, tackling issues solo.
He always comes to the rescue like the Lone Ranger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maverick - a person who is independent and does not go along with a group
  • Antonym: team player - someone who works well as a member of a group

Etymology

From the name of a fictional hero in U.S. radio, TV, and film, known for working alone.
