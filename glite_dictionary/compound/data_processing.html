data processing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.tə ˌprɑː.sɛs.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə ˌprəʊ.ses.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(computer science) the sequence of operations performed on data by a computer in order to retrieve, transform, or classify information.
computer operations on data

Examples

Many businesses rely on data processing to manage large amounts of information efficiently.
Improvements in data processing have transformed the field of scientific research.
The company invested in faster computers to speed up data processing tasks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data analysis - the process of inspecting, cleaning, and modeling data to discover information
