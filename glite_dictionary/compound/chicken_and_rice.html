chicken and rice

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ənd ˈraɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ənd ˈraɪs / (BrE)
A dish consisting of rice and chicken cooked together, often seasoned and sometimes combined with other ingredients.
dish of chicken cooked with rice

Examples

We enjoyed a warm bowl of chicken and rice on a cold evening.
Her favorite comfort food is homemade chicken and rice.
The restaurant serves a special chicken and rice every Sunday.
