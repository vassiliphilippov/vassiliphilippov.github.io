Celtic deity

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛltɪk ˈdiəti / (AmE), / ˈkɛltɪk ˈdiːəti / (BrE)
a god or goddess worshipped by the ancient Celts
a god or goddess of the Celts

Examples

The sacred groves were believed to be the dwelling places of a Celtic deity.
Lugh is a prominent Celtic deity associated with skill and craftsmanship.
Artifacts depicting the image of a Celtic deity have been found in ancient burial sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Celtic god - a male deity worshipped by the Celts
  • Synonym: Celtic goddess - a female deity worshipped by the Celts
