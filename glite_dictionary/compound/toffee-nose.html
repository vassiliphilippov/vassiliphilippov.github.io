toffee-nose

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑfi ˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈtɒfi ˌnəʊz / (BrE)
a person who is pretentious, snobbish, or thinks themselves superior to others
pretentious or snobbish person
british informal

Examples

She dismissed him as a toffee-nose who looked down on everyone at the dinner.
Stop acting like a toffee-nose just because you got a promotion.
Nobody likes a toffee-nose who boasts about their expensive holidays.

Synset Examples

Don't be such a toffee-nose about the party.
His toffee-nose attitude made him unpopular.
That toffee-nose thinks she's better than everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snob - a person who believes their tastes are superior to others

Etymology

British slang, possibly from the phrase 'to be toffy' (high-class) or 'toff' (aristocrat), with 'nose' suggesting looking down on others.
