snuff user

1. NOUN

/ snʌf ˈjuːzɚ / (AmE), / snʌf ˈjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who habitually uses snuff (powdered tobacco) by inhaling it through the nose
person who uses snuff tobacco

Examples

He became a habitual snuff user after moving to the countryside.
In the 18th century, being a snuff user was considered fashionable among the elite.
The doctor advised the snuff user to quit for health reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoker - a person who smokes tobacco regularly
  • Antonym: non-user - a person who does not use a particular substance
