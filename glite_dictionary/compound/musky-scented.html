musky-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌski ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌski ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having the scent or smell of musk
having the smell of musk

Examples

The musky-scented cologne lingered on his jacket all night.
She preferred musky-scented candles over floral ones for her living room.
The forest air was thick and musky-scented after the rain.
