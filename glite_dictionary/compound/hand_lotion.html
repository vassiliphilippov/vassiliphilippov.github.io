hand lotion

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / hænd ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
A lotion applied to the hands to moisturize and soften the skin.
moisturizing lotion for hands

Examples

She keeps a bottle of hand lotion on her desk to prevent her skin from drying out.
After washing my hands, I always apply hand lotion to keep them soft.
He bought a scented hand lotion as a gift for his mother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moisturizer - a substance for moisturizing the skin
  • Synonym: hand cream - a thick cream for moisturizing the hands
