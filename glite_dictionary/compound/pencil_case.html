pencil case

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnsl keɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəl keɪs / (BrE)
A small container or box used for storing pencils, pens, and other writing instruments.
container for storing pencils and pens

Examples

The student neatly arranged her colored pencils in her pencil case.
He forgot his pencil case at home and had to borrow a pen for the exam.
This pencil case has several compartments for organizing markers and erasers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pencil box - a box or container for holding pencils and writing utensils
