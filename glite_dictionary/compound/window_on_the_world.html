window on the world

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ɑn ðə wɜrld / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ɒn ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
an opportunity, means, or perspective that gives insight into global events, experiences, or cultures
means of gaining global perspective

Examples

Her volunteer work became a window on the world, opening her eyes to new challenges and opportunities.
The internet acts as a window on the world, connecting people to news and cultures across the globe.
For many students, studying abroad is a valuable window on the world.

Synset Examples

Books offer a window on the world beyond our own.
The documentary served as a window on the world for viewers.
Traveling provides a window on the world and its cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window - an opening in a wall fitted with glass to admit light or air and allow people to see out
  • Synonym: perspective - a particular attitude toward or way of regarding something; a point of view
