heterofascist movement

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛtəroʊˈfæʃɪst ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈfæʃɪst ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A political movement characterized by extreme nationalism that is aligned with or promotes heteronormativity.
extreme nationalist, heteronormative political movement

Examples

The political party has been accused of supporting a heterofascist movement that excludes minorities.
Activists protested against the new laws, calling them part of a rising heterofascist movement in the country.
Several scholars are publishing articles that analyze the rhetoric of the heterofascist movement online.

Synset Examples

The documentary explored the dangers of heterofascist movements.

Etymology

Formed from 'hetero-' (meaning different or heterosexual) + 'fascist' (referring to authoritarian, nationalistic ideologies).
