Simpson Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪmpsən ˈdez.ɚt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpsən ˈdez.ət / (BrE)
a large desert region in central Australia, known for its extensive sand dunes
desert region in central Australia

Examples

The Simpson Desert is famous for its red sand dunes and extreme arid conditions.
Crossing the Simpson Desert requires careful planning and plenty of water.
Scientists study rare plants and animals adapted to survive in the Simpson Desert.

Etymology

Named after Alfred Allen Simpson, an Australian philanthropist and geographer.
