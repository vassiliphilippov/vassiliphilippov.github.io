horse chestnut cream

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈtʃɛs.nət kriːm / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈtʃɛs.nət kriːm / (BrE)
a cream or ointment containing horse chestnut extract, used to relieve muscle or joint pain
ointment with horse chestnut extract for pain relief

Examples

She applied horse chestnut cream to her aching knees after her morning run.
Many athletes use horse chestnut cream to help soothe tired muscles.
The pharmacist recommended horse chestnut cream for relief from joint pain.

Synset Examples

I use horse balsam after working out to soothe muscle pain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arnica cream - a cream made from arnica, used to treat bruises and muscle pain

Etymology

From 'horse chestnut', the common name for Aesculus hippocastanum, combined with 'cream' to indicate a topical preparation.
