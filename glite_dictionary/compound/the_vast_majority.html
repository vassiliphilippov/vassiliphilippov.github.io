the vast majority

1. NOUN

/ ðə væst məˈdʒɔrəti / (AmE), / ðə vɑːst məˈdʒɒrəti / (BrE)
nearly all of a particular group; an overwhelming majority
nearly all; overwhelming majority

Examples

The vast majority of students completed the assignments on time.
We found that the vast majority of people supported the new policy.
Despite a few complaints, the vast majority were very satisfied with the service.

Synset Examples

The vast majority agreed that change was necessary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: most - the largest part or majority
  • Antonym: minority - the smaller part or number; less than half
