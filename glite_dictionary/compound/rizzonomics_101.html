rizzonomics 101

1. NOUN

/ ˌɹɪzəˈnɑmɪks wʌn oʊ wʌn / (AmE), / ˌɹɪzəˈnɒmɪks wʌn əʊ wʌn / (BrE)
a humorous or ironic term for the study or discussion of effective dating tactics, especially involving charisma or flirting skills
humorous term for dating tactics
informal slang

Examples

He claims to be so good at flirting that he could teach rizzonomics 101 to others.
Her advice on dating feels like an entire class in rizzonomics 101.
After my last date went well, my friends joked that I must have studied rizzonomics 101.

Synset Examples

He jokingly called his dating strategy rizzonomics 101.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rizz - charisma or skill in flirting

Etymology

Blend of 'rizz' (slang for charisma or flirting skill) and 'economics 101' (introductory university course).
