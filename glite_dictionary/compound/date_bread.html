date bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪt brɛd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt brɛd / (BrE)
A sweet bread made with chopped dates mixed into the dough.
sweet bread with chopped dates

Examples

She baked a fresh loaf of date bread for the family breakfast.
Many people enjoy date bread with a cup of tea in the afternoon.
The date bread was moist and filled with pieces of sweet fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana bread - sweet bread made with mashed bananas
