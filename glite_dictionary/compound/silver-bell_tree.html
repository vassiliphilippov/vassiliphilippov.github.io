silver-bell tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər bɛl tri / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə bɛl triː / (BrE)
A medium-sized deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States, noted for its bell-shaped white flowers.
North American tree with bell-shaped flowers

Examples

A silver-bell tree adds lovely white flowers to the garden each spring.
In the southern states, many parks have planted the silver-bell tree for its ornamental value.
Experts recommend the silver-bell tree for gardens that receive partial shade.
