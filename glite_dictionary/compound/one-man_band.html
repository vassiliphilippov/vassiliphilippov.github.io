one-man band

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌn mæn bænd / (AmE), / ˈwʌn mæn bænd / (BrE)
a person who performs multiple roles or works independently, especially by taking care of many different tasks without assistance
person who does everything alone

Examples

Since the staff was short, Olivia handled the phone, emails, and deliveries like a one-man band.
As a freelance designer, Daniel was a true one-man band managing clients, billing, and marketing himself.
Onstage, he amazed the audience by playing multiple instruments as a one-man band.

Synset Examples

She runs her business like a one-man band, doing it all herself.
As a one-man band, he handled every aspect of the project.
He's a talented musician, a true one-man band on stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soloist - a person who works or performs alone
  • Antonym: team player - a person who works collaboratively with others
