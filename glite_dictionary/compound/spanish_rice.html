Spanish rice

1. NOUN

/ ˈspænɪʃ raɪs / (AmE), / ˈspanɪʃ raɪs / (BrE)
A spicy rice dish cooked with tomatoes, onions, and green peppers, commonly served as a side in Mexican and Southwestern cuisine.
spicy rice dish with tomatoes and vegetables

Examples

We always serve Spanish rice alongside grilled chicken at family dinners.
The recipe calls for bell peppers and onions to add flavor to the Spanish rice.
She ordered a plate of tacos with a side of Spanish rice and beans.
