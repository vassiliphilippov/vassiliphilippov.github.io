writing skill

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ skɪl / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ skɪl / (BrE)
the ability to write clearly, correctly, and effectively in a given language
ability to write effectively

Examples

Employers often look for strong writing skill when hiring for office jobs.
Her writing skill improved dramatically after she took the creative writing course.
Good writing skill is essential for clear communication in business.

Synset Examples

She studied grammar to improve her writing skills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: literacy - ability to read and write
