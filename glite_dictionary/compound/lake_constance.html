Lake Constance

1. PROPN

/ leɪk ˈkɑnstəns / (AmE), / leɪk ˈkɒnstəns / (BrE)
a large lake on the border of Germany, Switzerland, and Austria, part of the Rhine River system
lake in Central Europe, on German-Swiss-Austrian border

Examples

Many tourists visit Lake Constance every summer to enjoy its scenic views and water sports.
The Rhine River flows through Lake Constance before continuing its journey northward.
Several picturesque towns are located along the shores of Lake Constance.

Etymology

From the German name 'Bodensee', the English form influenced by the nearby city of Konstanz (Constance).
