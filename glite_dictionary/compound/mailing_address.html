mailing address

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlɪŋ əˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlɪŋ əˌdrɛs / (BrE)
the address to which mail is sent to reach a person or organization
address for receiving mail

Examples

Please provide your mailing address so we can send you the documents.
He recently moved and forgot to update his mailing address with the bank.
Your package will be delivered to the mailing address you listed on the form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postal address - the address where post can be delivered
