to call for support

1. VERB

/ kɔl fər səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / kɔːl fə səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to request or seek help or assistance, often in a time of need or difficulty
ask for help or assistance

Examples

During the crisis, the organization didn't hesitate to call for support from international partners.
If you ever feel overwhelmed, it's important to call for support from friends and family.
The leader called for support to implement the new changes across the company.

Synset Examples

She didn’t hesitate to call her friend for support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to ask for help - to request assistance
