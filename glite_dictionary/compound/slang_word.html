slang word

1. NOUN

/ slæŋ wɝːd / (AmE), / slæŋ wɜːd / (BrE)
A word or expression used in very informal language, often connected to a particular group or context and not considered part of standard language.
informal, nonstandard word or phrase

Examples

The teenagers invented a new slang word to describe something cool.
Many older people struggle to keep up with every slang word their children use.
Some slang words quickly become outdated as language changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slang - very informal language, often used in casual speech
