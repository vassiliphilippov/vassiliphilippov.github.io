to giggle uncontrollably

1. VERB

/ ˈɡɪɡəl ˌʌn.kənˈtroʊləbli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡl ˌʌn.kənˈtrəʊləbli / (BrE)
to laugh in a silly and uncontrolled way, often because something is very funny or embarrassing
laugh in an uncontrollable, silly way

Examples

The children started to giggle uncontrollably during the play when the actor slipped on a banana peel.
She tried to stay serious during the meeting but ended up giggling uncontrollably at her friend's joke.
Whenever he sees a funny cat video, he can't help but giggle uncontrollably.

Synset Examples

That naughty bit made the kids giggle uncontrollably.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giggle - to laugh lightly in a nervous or silly way
  • Degree Lower: chuckle - to laugh quietly or to oneself
