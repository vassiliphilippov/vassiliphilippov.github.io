loaf of bread

1. NOUN

/ loʊf əv brɛd / (AmE), / ləʊf əv brɛd / (BrE)
A shaped mass of bread baked as a single piece, usually sliced before being eaten.
a shaped mass of bread

Examples

She bought a fresh loaf of bread from the bakery this morning.
He sliced the loaf of bread and made sandwiches for lunch.
The recipe calls for one loaf of bread cut into cubes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loaf - a shaped mass of baked bread
