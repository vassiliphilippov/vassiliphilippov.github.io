loading dock

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.dɪŋ dɑk / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.dɪŋ dɒk / (BrE)
a platform at a building where trucks or trains are loaded or unloaded
platform for loading or unloading vehicles

Examples

The truck pulled up to the loading dock to begin unloading its cargo.
Workers were busy moving boxes from the loading dock into the warehouse.
Please make sure the loading dock is clear before the shipment arrives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dock - a platform for loading or unloading freight
  • Synonym: platform - a raised level surface used for a particular purpose
