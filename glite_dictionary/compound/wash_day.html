wash day

1. NOUN

/ wɑʃ deɪ / (AmE), / wɒʃ deɪ / (BrE)
a day designated for doing the household laundry
day set aside for laundry

Examples

Saturday is usually our family's wash day.
After a long wash day, she finally sat down to relax.
People used to hang clothes outside to dry on wash day.

Synset Examples

I love the fresh scent after wash day.
