the l-word

1. NOUN

/ ði ˈɛl wɝd / (AmE), / ði ˈɛl wɜːd / (BrE)
Used as a euphemism for the word 'love', especially when referring to the act or difficulty of saying it aloud.
euphemism for 'love'

Examples

After months of dating, he still felt awkward bringing up the l-word.
It took her a while to say the l-word to her partner.
Some people find it difficult to use the l-word in conversation.

Synset Examples

Saying the l-word took their relationship to the next level.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: love - deep affection or romantic attachment

Etymology

Patterned after phrases like 'the F-word', using the initial letter as a euphemism.
