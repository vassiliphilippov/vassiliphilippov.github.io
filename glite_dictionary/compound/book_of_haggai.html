Book of Haggai

1. PROPN

/ bʊk əv ˈhæɡaɪ / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈhæɡaɪ / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament recounting the prophecies of Haggai, mainly about the rebuilding of the Temple after the Babylonian exile
Old Testament book of prophecies by Haggai

Examples

The Book of Haggai urges the people to rebuild the Temple in Jerusalem.
Many scholars study the Book of Haggai for its historical and theological significance.
In Sunday school, we discussed the teachings found in the Book of Haggai.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Haggai - Hebrew prophet in the Old Testament
  • Degree Higher: Book of Zechariah - Old Testament book of prophecies after the exile
