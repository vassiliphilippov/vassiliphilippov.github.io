all-weather

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlˌwɛðər / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌwɛðə / (BrE)
designed to be used or effective in all kinds of weather conditions.
suitable for any weather conditions

Examples

The new all-weather running track allows athletes to train year-round.
They purchased all-weather tires for their car before the winter started.
The club’s all-weather pitch was still playable despite the heavy rain.

Synset Examples

a good all-weather road
all-weather flying

2. ADJ

/ ˌɔlˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈwɛðə / (BrE)
suitable for use in any kind of weather or climate conditions.
for any weather conditions

Examples

We bought an all-weather jacket for our hiking trip in the mountains.
The stadium was upgraded with an all-weather running track to allow year-round use.
These all-weather tires provide good grip whether it rains or snows.

Synset Examples

These shoes are perfect for all-weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weatherproof - designed to resist the effects of weather
  • Synonym: waterproof - not allowing water to pass through
  • Synonym: all-purpose - suitable for all uses
