language barrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈbæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈbæriə / (BrE)
a difficulty or obstacle to communication caused by people not sharing a common language
obstacle to communication from different languages

Examples

When traveling abroad, the language barrier can make it hard to order food or ask for directions.
Misunderstandings often occur in business meetings when there is a language barrier between participants.
She overcame the language barrier quickly and was soon fluent in English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication barrier - an obstacle to effective communication
