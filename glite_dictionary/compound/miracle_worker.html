miracle worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪrəkəl ˌwɝkər / (AmE), / ˈmɪrəkəl ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who is believed to perform miracles or extraordinary feats, often with supernatural or divine power
person who performs miracles

Examples

Many regarded the saint as a miracle worker after several accounts of unexplained healings.
The villagers turned to the miracle worker in times of great need and despair.
Skeptics doubted whether the so-called miracle worker truly possessed any magical powers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healer - a person who heals others, especially by supernatural means
  • Antonym: charlatan - a person falsely claiming to have a special skill; a fraud
