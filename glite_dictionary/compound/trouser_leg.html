trouser leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraʊzɚ lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə lɛɡ / (BrE)
the part of a pair of trousers that covers one leg
one leg portion of trousers

Examples

She noticed a rip in her trouser leg after walking through the bushes.
His right trouser leg got caught in the bicycle chain.
The tailor measured the length of each trouser leg before making adjustments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trouser - an outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, with a separate part for each leg
