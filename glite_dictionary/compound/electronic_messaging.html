electronic messaging

1. NOUN

/ əˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the sending and receiving of messages by electronic means, especially by computer networks such as email
sending messages by computer or electronic means

Examples

Many businesses now rely on electronic messaging to communicate with employees and clients.
Advances in electronic messaging have made it easier to collaborate across different countries.
Teachers use electronic messaging to send assignments and announcements to their students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - messages sent electronically over a computer network
