Cascade Range

1. PROPN

/ kæsˈkeɪd reɪndʒ / (AmE), / kæsˈkeɪd reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a major mountain range in the northwestern United States, stretching through Washington, Oregon, and northern California
mountain range in the northwestern United States

Examples

We spent our summer vacation hiking in the Cascade Range and exploring its volcanic peaks.
The Cascade Range is known for its beautiful waterfalls and diverse wildlife.
Several major rivers originate in the Cascade Range before flowing into the Pacific Ocean.

Etymology

Named after the cascades (waterfalls) in the region; 'Cascade' from French 'cascade' meaning 'waterfall'.
