genus Aristotelia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˌærɪstəˈtiːliə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌærɪstəˈtiːliə / (BrE)
A small genus of shrubs or small trees native to Australia, New Zealand, and western South America.
genus of shrubs or small trees

Examples

Botanists recently discovered a new species belonging to the genus Aristotelia in Chile.
The genus Aristotelia includes several plants prized for their ornamental value.
Native forests in New Zealand often feature members of the genus Aristotelia.

Etymology

Genus name from the Greek philosopher Aristotle, commonly used in scientific naming.
