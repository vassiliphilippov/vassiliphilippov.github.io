Eyetie culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.taɪ ˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.taɪ ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) Italian culture; relating to customs, arts, and traditions of Italy or Italians.
Italian culture (slang, offensive)
slang offensive

Examples

The movie explores Eyetie culture through food and family, though the term is outdated and can be seen as offensive.
He claimed to know a lot about Eyetie culture, but his remarks were full of stereotypes.
While studying abroad, she initially struggled to adapt to Eyetie culture and customs.

Synset Examples

She loved the Eyetie culture and its rich traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Italian culture - the culture of Italy and Italians

Etymology

Slang alteration of 'Italian', based on imitation of Italian pronunciation of 'Italian'.
