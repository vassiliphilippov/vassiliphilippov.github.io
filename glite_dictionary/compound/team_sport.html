team sport

1. NOUN

/ tim spɔrt / (AmE), / tiːm spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport in which groups of players form teams that compete against other teams.
sport involving teams competing

Examples

Football is a popular team sport played all over the world.
She prefers playing team sports because she enjoys working with others.
Hockey and volleyball are both examples of a team sport.

Synset Examples

baseball is a team sport but golf is not

Related Terms

  • Antonym: individual sport - a sport in which participants compete as individuals, not as members of a team
