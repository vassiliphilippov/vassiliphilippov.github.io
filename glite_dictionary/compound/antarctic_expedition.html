Antarctic expedition

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtɑːrktɪk ˌɛkspəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ænˈtɑːktɪk ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a journey to the Antarctic region, especially for purposes of exploration, research, or discovery
journey to the Antarctic for exploration or research

Examples

The team faced harsh conditions on their Antarctic expedition but collected valuable scientific data.
Her dream was to join an Antarctic expedition and study penguin colonies firsthand.
The discovery of the lost hut was the highlight of the latest Antarctic expedition.

Synset Examples

We saw penguins during our Antarctic expedition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expedition - a journey undertaken for a specific purpose
