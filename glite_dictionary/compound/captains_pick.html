captain's pick

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp.tənz pɪk / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪnz pɪk / (BrE)
a participant or player chosen by a team's captain, especially in sports or competitions
player chosen by a team captain

Examples

Being the captain's pick can come with added pressure to perform well.
He was thrilled to be selected as the captain's pick for the finals.
The team relied on the captain's pick to secure a victory.

Synset Examples

Being the captain's pick often guarantees a spot on the team.
Her selection was the captain's pick for the tournament.
