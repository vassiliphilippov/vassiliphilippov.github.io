tech gadget

1. NOUN

/ tɛk ˈɡædʒət / (AmE), / tɛk ˈɡadʒɪt / (BrE)
a small technological device or tool, especially one regarded as new, clever, or useful
small technological device or tool

Examples

She always wants the latest tech gadget as soon as it hits the market.
The company is known for inventing innovative tech gadgets that make life easier.
Many people can't leave home without their favorite tech gadget, like a smartwatch or wireless earbuds.

Synset Examples

The new tech gadget came in a simple beige box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device
  • Synonym: device - an object or machine invented for a particular purpose
