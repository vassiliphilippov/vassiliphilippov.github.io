toothed spurge

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːt̬t spɝːdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtuːt spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
an annual weed of northeastern North America, characterized by leaves with toothed (dentate) edges
annual North American weed with toothed leaves

Examples

The field was overrun by toothed spurge, making it hard for crops to grow.
Gardeners often remove toothed spurge to keep their lawns weed-free.
You can recognize toothed spurge by its distinctive toothed leaves and small green flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spurge - any plant of the genus Euphorbia
