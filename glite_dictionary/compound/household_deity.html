household deity

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌhoʊld ˈdiːɪti / (AmE), / ˈhaʊshəʊld ˈdiːɪti / (BrE)
A spirit or god believed to protect and watch over a home, worshipped or honored within a household.
spirit or god worshipped in a home

Examples

Many families place offerings to their household deity to ensure safety and prosperity.
The image of the household deity was kept in a special shrine near the kitchen.
As tradition dictates, every festival begins with prayers to the household deity.

Synset Examples

They have a household deity that protects their home.
She lights incense for their household deity every morning.
In many cultures, a household deity is honored during festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic god - a god associated with the home
