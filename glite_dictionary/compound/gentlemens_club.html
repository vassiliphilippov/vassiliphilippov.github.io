gentlemen's club

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntəlmənz klʌb / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmənz klʌb / (BrE)
a private social club for men, often with exclusive membership and facilities
private social club for men

Examples

He spent his evenings at the gentlemen's club playing cards with old friends.
Membership to the gentlemen's club was considered a mark of high social status.
The gentlemen's club offered its members a library, dining rooms, and billiard tables.
