computer hardware

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtər ˈhɑrˌdwɛr / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈhɑːdweə / (BrE)
The mechanical, electronic, magnetic, and electrical components that make up a computer system, such as the central processing unit, memory, and drives.
physical components of a computer system

Examples

Our IT department is upgrading the computer hardware to improve system performance.
She specializes in repairing and maintaining computer hardware for local businesses.
The company donated old computer hardware to local schools.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: computer software - the programs and operating information used by a computer
