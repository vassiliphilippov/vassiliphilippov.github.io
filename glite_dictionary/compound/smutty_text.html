smutty text

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmʌti tɛkst / (AmE), / ˈsmʌti tɛkst / (BrE)
written content that contains explicit or obscene sexual material, often considered offensive or inappropriate
text with explicit sexual content

Examples

He was caught reading a smutty text during class, much to the teacher’s dismay.
Their inbox was full of smutty texts they had sent each other as jokes.
The publisher refused to print any smutty text for fear of controversy.

Synset Examples
