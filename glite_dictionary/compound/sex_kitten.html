sex kitten

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ˈkɪtn̩ / (AmE), / sɛks ˈkɪtn̩ / (BrE)
A young woman who is considered very sexually attractive and seductive.
young woman with strong sex appeal

Examples

She was labeled a sex kitten by the press after her breakthrough movie role.
The actress hated being typecast as a sex kitten instead of being seen for her talent.
The magazine often featured portraits of Hollywood’s most famous sex kittens from the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bombshell - a very attractive or seductive woman
  • Synonym: sex symbol - a person widely considered sexually attractive
