variously-leaved pondweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛɹiəsli liːvd ˈpɑndwiːd / (AmE), / ˈvɛːriəsli liːvd ˈpɒndwiːd / (BrE)
a species of pondweed native to most of Europe (except the Mediterranean) and northern United States, known for having leaves of varying shapes
species of pondweed with variable leaves

Examples

The variously-leaved pondweed is easily identified by the diversity of its leaf shapes.
In northern U.S. lakes, variously-leaved pondweed provides important habitat for aquatic wildlife.
Scientists studied the adaptability of variously-leaved pondweed across its wide geographical range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pondweed - aquatic plant of the genus Potamogeton
