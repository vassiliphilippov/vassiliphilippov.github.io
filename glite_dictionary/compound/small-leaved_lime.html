small-leaved lime

1. NOUN

/ smɔl livd laɪm / (AmE), / smɔːl liːvd laɪm / (BrE)
A large European linden tree (Tilia cordata) with small, dark green leaves, often grown as an ornamental.
European linden tree species with small leaves

Examples

The small-leaved lime is popular in city parks for its attractive foliage.
Several small-leaved lime trees line the avenue, providing thick shade in summer.
The botanist identified the tree as a small-leaved lime by its distinctive leaves.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: large-leaved lime - A European linden species with large leaves (Tilia platyphyllos)
  • Synonym: linden - Tree of the genus Tilia, known as lime or linden
