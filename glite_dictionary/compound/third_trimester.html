third trimester

1. NOUN

/ θɝd traɪˈmɛstɚ / (AmE), / θɜːd traɪˈmɛstə / (BrE)
the period from the 28th week of pregnancy until birth
final stage of pregnancy (28 weeks to birth)
medical

Examples

During her third trimester, she began attending childbirth classes to prepare for the baby's arrival.
Fatigue and back pain are common complaints in the third trimester of pregnancy.
The doctor scheduled more frequent checkups during the third trimester to monitor the baby's development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trimester - a period of three months, especially in pregnancy
