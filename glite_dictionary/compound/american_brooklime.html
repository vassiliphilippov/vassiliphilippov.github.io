American brooklime

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈbrʊkˌlaɪm / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈbrʊk.laɪm / (BrE)
a plant of western North America and northeastern Asia with prostrate stems and dense racemes of pale violet to lilac flowers
plant with lilac flowers native to North America and Asia

Examples

The wetland was filled with patches of American brooklime along the water’s edge.
Botanists surveyed the area and found several rare species, including American brooklime.
In spring, the delicate flowers of American brooklime add a touch of color to the streams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brooklime - a spreading aquatic plant with blue flowers
