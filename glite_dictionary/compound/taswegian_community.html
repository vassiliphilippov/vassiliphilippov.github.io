Taswegian community

1. NOUN

/ tæzˈwiːdʒən kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / tæzˈwiːdʒiən kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group of people who are from or closely connected to Tasmania, often sharing distinct cultural characteristics.
group of people from Tasmania

Examples

The Taswegian community held a gathering to promote local traditions and cuisine.
During the celebrations, the Taswegian community welcomed visitors with music and storytelling.
Members of the Taswegian community living abroad often organize events to stay connected to their roots.

Synset Examples

The Taswegian community celebrated their unique culture at the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tasmanian - person from Tasmania

Etymology

Taswegian: from Tasmania + '-wegian' (after Norwegian); community: from Latin 'communitas'
