breakdown in communication

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkˌdaʊn ɪn kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk.daʊn ɪn kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a failure to exchange information or express ideas effectively, leading to misunderstanding or confusion
failure to effectively exchange information

Examples

A breakdown in communication between doctors and nurses caused delays in patient care.
Without regular team meetings, a breakdown in communication can easily happen.
The company lost the contract due to a breakdown in communication with the client.

Synset Examples

The team's breakdown in communication led to failure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscommunication - failure to communicate information accurately
