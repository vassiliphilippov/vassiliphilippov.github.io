mountain climate

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntn̩ ˈklaɪmət / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntən ˈklaɪmət / (BrE)
the typical weather patterns and atmospheric conditions found in mountainous regions
weather conditions in mountainous areas

Examples

Travelers are advised to prepare for sudden changes typical of a mountain climate.
The region's unique ecosystem is shaped by its harsh mountain climate.
Living in a mountain climate requires adapting to colder temperatures and frequent weather changes.

Synset Examples

As a lowlander, he found the mountain climate challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climate - the general weather conditions of a region
