Florida smoothhound

1. NOUN

/ flɔːrɪdə ˈsmuːðhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪdə ˈsmuːðhaʊnd / (BrE)
a species of smoothhound shark (Mustelus norrisi) found in the western Atlantic from the northern Gulf of Mexico to Brazil
a shark species from the Atlantic coast of the Americas

Examples

The Florida smoothhound inhabits shallow coastal waters and is rarely seen far offshore.
Marine biologists study the diet of the Florida smoothhound along the Brazilian coastline.
Unlike larger sharks, the Florida smoothhound poses no danger to humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothhound - a type of shark in the genus Mustelus
