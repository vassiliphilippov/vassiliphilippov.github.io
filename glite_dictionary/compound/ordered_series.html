ordered series

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdɚd ˈsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəd ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
a sequence of items arranged in a specific order, often used as a reference standard for comparison or measurement
sequence arranged by order, often for comparison

Examples

The results were ranked according to an ordered series from best to worst.
We used an ordered series to classify the quality of responses.
The survey asked participants to rate their satisfaction on an ordered series of numbers from 1 to 10.

Synset Examples

judging on a scale of 1 to 10

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sequence - a set of things in a particular order
