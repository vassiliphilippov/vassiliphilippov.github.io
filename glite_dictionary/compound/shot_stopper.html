shot stopper

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑt ˈstɑpɚ / (AmE), / ʃɒt ˈstɒpə / (BrE)
a goalkeeper who is particularly skilled at saving shots on goal, especially in football or soccer
goalkeeper skilled at saving shots

Examples

The team's new shot stopper made several impressive saves during the match.
As a shot stopper, she has earned praise for her quick reflexes.
Many consider him the best shot stopper in the league this season.

Synset Examples

The new recruit proved to be a great shot stopper.
He was known as a shot stopper during his playing days.
The goalkeeper is a fantastic shot stopper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goalkeeper - player who guards the goal in football/soccer
