weighted average

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪtɪd ˈævɚɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪd ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
An average in which each value contributes in proportion to a designated weight representing its importance or frequency.
an average where values have different weights

Examples

To find the final grade, the teacher calculated a weighted average of all the assignments and exams.
A weighted average takes into account the relative importance of each data point, unlike a simple average.
The performance of each stock in the index is reflected in the weighted average based on its market capitalization.

Synset Examples

This simple mechanical model is now a familiar representation of the weighted arithmetic mean of a set of observations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arithmetic mean - an average calculated as the sum of quantities divided by the number of quantities
