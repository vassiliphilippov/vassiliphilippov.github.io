cosplay outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːsˌpleɪ ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒsˌpleɪ ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
A costume worn, especially by fans, to represent a character from fiction, often at conventions or events.
costume of a fictional character, worn by fans

Examples

She spent weeks designing her cosplay outfit for the upcoming convention.
His elaborate cosplay outfit won first prize at the costume contest.
Many attendees admired the detail in her cosplay outfit inspired by her favorite anime character.

Synset Examples

The convention was filled with amazing cosplay outfits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: costume - an outfit worn to imitate a particular character or period
  • Synonym: disguise - a set of clothes worn to make someone look like another person

Etymology

From Japanese 'kosupure' (costume play) + English 'outfit'.
