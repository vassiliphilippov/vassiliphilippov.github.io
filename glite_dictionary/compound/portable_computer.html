portable computer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrtəbəl kəmˈpjuːt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
a personal computer designed to be easily carried and used in different locations
computer easily carried by hand

Examples

She bought a portable computer for traveling so she could work from anywhere.
Engineers often rely on a portable computer to access their software at remote sites.
The development of the portable computer revolutionized the way people work outside the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laptop - a portable computer small enough to be used on the user's lap
  • Antonym: desktop computer - a personal computer designed to fit on a desk and not intended for portability
