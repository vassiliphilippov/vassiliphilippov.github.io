bike rack

1. NOUN

/ baɪk ræk / (AmE), / baɪk ræk / (BrE)
a metal frame or device used for parking and securing bicycles
rack for parking bicycles

Examples

I locked my bicycle to the bike rack outside the library.
The new park was equipped with a bike rack for visitors.
Please make sure your bike rack is installed securely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicycle stand - a stand for parking bicycles
