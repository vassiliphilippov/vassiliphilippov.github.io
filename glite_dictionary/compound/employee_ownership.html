employee ownership

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˌplɔɪiː ˈoʊnərʃɪp / (AmE), / ɪmˌplɔɪiː ˈəʊnəʃɪp / (BrE)
A system in which the employees of a company own shares or a controlling stake in the business for which they work.
ownership of a business by its employees

Examples

The company adopted employee ownership to give workers a greater stake in its success.
Studies suggest that employee ownership can improve job satisfaction and productivity.
After years of planning, the bakery finally transitioned to employee ownership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooperative - A business or organization owned and managed by a group of individuals for their mutual benefit
