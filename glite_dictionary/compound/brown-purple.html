brown-purple

1. ADJ

/ braʊn ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / braʊn ˈpɜːpəl / (BrE)
of a dark purple color tinged with brown; having a purple shade mixed with brown.
dark purple tinged with brown

Examples

The artist used a brown-purple hue to paint the shadowy corners of the room.
She wore a brown-purple scarf that brought out the warm tones in her complexion.
The sky turned a brown-purple as the sun set behind the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple-brown - a brownish color with a hint of purple
