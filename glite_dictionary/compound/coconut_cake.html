coconut cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkəˌnʌt keɪk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkənʌt keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made with shredded coconut mixed into the batter and often in the frosting.
cake with shredded coconut

Examples

For her birthday, she requested a homemade coconut cake with creamy frosting.
The tropical flavor of coconut cake always reminds me of summer holidays.
He baked a delicious coconut cake topped with toasted coconut flakes for dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrot cake - a cake containing grated carrot, often with frosting
