Otaheite arrowroot

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊtəˈhiːti ˈæroʊˌruːt / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəˈhiːti ˈærəˌruːt / (BrE)
a starch obtained from the root of the pia plant, used as food or in cooking
starch from the pia plant root

Examples

The baker used Otaheite arrowroot to thicken the delicate pudding.
In some tropical regions, Otaheite arrowroot is favored for its smooth texture in desserts.
She purchased a packet of Otaheite arrowroot at the specialty food store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrowroot - a starch obtained from the root of several tropical plants

Etymology

Otaheite is an older name for Tahiti; arrowroot refers to the starchy root used for food.
