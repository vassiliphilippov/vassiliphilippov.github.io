circulating decimal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.kjəˌleɪ.t̬ɪŋ ˈdɛs.ɪ.məl / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kjʊ.leɪ.tɪŋ ˈdɛs.ɪ.məl / (BrE)
a decimal fraction in which a sequence of digits repeats itself indefinitely
decimal with repeated digits indefinitely

Examples

The fraction one third is written as a circulating decimal: 0.333...
When converted to a decimal, 2/7 produces a circulating decimal sequence.
He explained that a circulating decimal has a set of digits that never ends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recurring decimal - decimal fraction in which one or more digits repeat endlessly
