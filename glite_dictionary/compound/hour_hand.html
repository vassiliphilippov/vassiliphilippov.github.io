hour hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊɚ hænd / (AmE), / ˈaʊə hænd / (BrE)
the shorter hand of a clock or watch that indicates the hours
shorter clock hand showing hours

Examples

The hour hand moved slowly as the morning passed.
If the hour hand points to 3 and the minute hand to 12, it is three o'clock.
She glanced at the hour hand to see how late it was.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: minute hand - the longer hand of a clock that points to the minutes
