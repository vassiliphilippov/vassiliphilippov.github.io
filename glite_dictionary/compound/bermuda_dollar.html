Bermuda dollar

1. NOUN

/ bɚˈmjuːdə ˈdɑːlər / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money used in Bermuda, equal to 100 cents
currency unit of Bermuda

Examples

The price of a meal in Bermuda is usually quoted in Bermuda dollars.
He exchanged his US dollars for Bermuda dollars at the airport.
The Bermuda dollar is pegged at par with the US dollar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - the basic monetary unit in several countries, including the US and Canada
