beach waggon

1. NOUN

/ biːtʃ ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a car with a long body and a rear door, featuring extra space behind the rear seat for cargo or passengers
car with extra rear space and rear door

Examples

They packed all their luggage into the beach waggon before heading to the seaside.
The family's old beach waggon was perfect for summer road trips.
We rented a beach waggon to drive along the coast with our surfboards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: station wagon - a car with a long body, rear door, and extended boot space
