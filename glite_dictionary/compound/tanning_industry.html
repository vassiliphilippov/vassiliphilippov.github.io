tanning industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈtænɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈtænɪŋ ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector concerned with the processing of animal hides into leather
sector processing animal hides

Examples

Working conditions in the tanning industry have improved significantly over the past decade.
Environmental regulations have greatly affected how the tanning industry disposes of waste.
Many rural towns relied on jobs provided by the tanning industry.

Synset Examples

Leather poisoning is a risk in the tanning industry.
