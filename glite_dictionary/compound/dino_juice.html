dino juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnoʊ ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəʊ ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
A playful, informal term for a drink associated with dinosaurs, especially a children's beverage, an energy drink, or a green smoothie.
informal term for a children's drink, energy drink, or green smoothie
informal

Examples

The kids giggled as they drank their dino juice at the party.
He grabs a can of his favorite energy drink and calls it dino juice.
Every morning, she blends spinach and fruit to make her special dino juice.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinosaur juice - playful drink name for a green smoothie or kid's drink

Etymology

Formed from "dino" (short for dinosaur) and "juice".
