ballyhoo artist

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæliˌhu ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈbæliˌhu ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses exaggerated or sensational material to attract public attention, especially in publicity or promotion
person who uses sensational publicity methods

Examples

The ballyhoo artist drew crowds with his outrageous claims and elaborate stunts.
Many viewed him as a talented ballyhoo artist rather than a serious journalist due to his flashy headlines.
The circus hired a skilled ballyhoo artist to boost ticket sales with wild promotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promoter - a person who publicizes or organizes events or products
  • Synonym: showman - person skilled at attracting public attention

Etymology

From 'ballyhoo' (extravagant publicity) + 'artist'. 'Ballyhoo' is possibly from early 20th-century American slang.
