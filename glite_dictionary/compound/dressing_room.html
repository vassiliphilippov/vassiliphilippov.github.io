dressing room

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ rʊm / (BrE)
a room in which people change their clothes, especially in theaters, sports venues, or shops
room for changing clothes

Examples

The actors hurried to the dressing room to prepare for the final scene.
After the game, the team gathered in their dressing room to talk strategy.
She tried on several outfits in the dressing room before choosing her dress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changing room - a room for changing clothes, often at a gym or pool
