zamia family

1. NOUN

/ ˈzeɪmiə ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈzeɪmɪə ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of tropical and subtropical cycads, often included in the family Cycadaceae, consisting of plants known as zamias.
family of cycads; zamias

Examples

The zamia family contains several species that are popular as ornamental plants.
Scientists studied the evolutionary relationship between members of the zamia family and other cycads.
Many plants in the zamia family are native to the Americas and prefer tropical climates.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Cycadaceae - family including cycads related to zamias
