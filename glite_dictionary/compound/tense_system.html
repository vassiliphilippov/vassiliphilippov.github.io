tense system

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛns ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈtɛns ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
the set of verb tenses and their usage patterns in a particular language
set of verb tenses in a language

Examples

Different languages may have a more complex tense system than English.
Learning the tense system is essential for mastering French grammar.
The tense system in this language includes past, present, and future forms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tense - a form of a verb showing time of action
