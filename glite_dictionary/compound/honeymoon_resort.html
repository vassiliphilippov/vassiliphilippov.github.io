honeymoon resort

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌmun rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌmuːn rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
a resort that is designed to cater to newlyweds, often offering romantic amenities and packages for couples celebrating their honeymoon
resort for newlyweds celebrating their honeymoon

Examples

They spent a week at an exclusive honeymoon resort in the Maldives.
Hawaii is famous for its beautiful honeymoon resorts that offer special packages for couples.
The honeymoon resort provided private villas and candlelit dinners for the newlyweds.

Synset Examples

Niagara Falls is a well-known honeymoon resort

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resort - a place where people go to relax and have fun
