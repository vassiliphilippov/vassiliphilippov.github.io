cheese tray

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiz treɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːz treɪ / (BrE)
A flat serving dish or tray used for displaying and serving a selection of cheeses.
tray for serving cheese

Examples

We ordered a cheese tray for the party, which featured several local varieties.
The restaurant's cheese tray included goat cheese, brie, and sharp cheddar.
She arranged grapes and crackers alongside the cheese tray before her guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serving tray - a tray for carrying or serving food and drinks
