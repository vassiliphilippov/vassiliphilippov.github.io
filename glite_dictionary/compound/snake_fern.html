snake fern

1. NOUN

/ sneɪk fɝn / (AmE), / sneɪk fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical fern, widespread in the Old World, that has become naturalized in places such as Jamaica and Florida
a tropical fern species

Examples

The snake fern thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight.
Scientists found a large patch of snake fern growing wild in Florida.
Gardeners admire the exotic appearance of the snake fern in tropical landscapes.
