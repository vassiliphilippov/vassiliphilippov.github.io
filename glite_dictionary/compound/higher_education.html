higher education

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ.ɚ ˌɛdʒ.ʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.ə ˌed.jʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
education provided by a college, university, or other institution that awards academic degrees or professional qualifications beyond secondary school level.
education at college or university level

Examples

Many young people choose to pursue higher education to improve their career prospects.
The government is increasing funding for higher education institutions this year.
Access to higher education is considered crucial for social mobility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tertiary education - education provided after secondary school, often at college or university
