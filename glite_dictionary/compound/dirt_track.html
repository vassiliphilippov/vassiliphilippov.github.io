dirt track

1. NOUN

/ dɝt træk / (AmE), / dɜːt træ k / (BrE)
a racetrack with a surface made of packed earth rather than being paved with asphalt or concrete
unpaved racetrack for racing

Examples

The race was held on a challenging dirt track that became muddy after the rain.
She prefers riding motorcycles on a dirt track because it tests her skills more than paved roads.
Many rural towns host weekly car races at their local dirt track during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racetrack - a track where races are held
