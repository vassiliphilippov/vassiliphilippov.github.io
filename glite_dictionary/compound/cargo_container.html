cargo container

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ kənˈteɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ kənˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a large, standardized container used for transporting goods, especially by ship, train, or truck
large container for transporting goods

Examples

The ship was loaded with dozens of cargo containers bound for Europe.
Each cargo container is inspected carefully before it leaves the port.
Farm equipment was stacked inside a giant cargo container for overseas delivery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping container - a large standardized metal box for transporting goods
