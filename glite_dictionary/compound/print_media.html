print media

1. NOUN

/ prɪnt ˈmidiə / (AmE), / prɪnt ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
Newspapers, magazines, and other publications that are printed on paper, as opposed to being published electronically.
newspapers and magazines in printed form

Examples

Many believe that print media is losing ground to online news sources.
Advertising agencies still consider print media an effective channel for reaching certain audiences.
The decline of print media has led to significant changes in the journalism industry.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: broadcast media - television and radio as means of communication
  • Antonym: digital media - media content shared via electronic or digital channels
