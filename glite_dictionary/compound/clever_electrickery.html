clever electrickery

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvɚ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪkəri / (BrE)
skillful or ingenious use of technical tricks or deception, often in a playful or impressive way
ingenious or technical tricks
informal

Examples

The magician amazed everyone with his clever electrickery during the performance.
Her repair job was a bit of clever electrickery and some duct tape.
They suspected some clever electrickery was behind the seemingly impossible device.

Synset Examples

His magic tricks relied on clever electrickery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trickery - the use of clever schemes or deceit

Etymology

Blend of 'electricity' or 'electric' and 'trickery', used humorously.
