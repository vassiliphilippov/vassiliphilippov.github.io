oil stain

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl steɪn / (AmE), / ɔɪl steɪn / (BrE)
a mark or discoloration caused by oil, often difficult to remove from fabrics, surfaces, or other materials
mark or stain caused by oil

Examples

Be careful not to spill your salad dressing or you might get an oil stain on your shirt.
The mechanic tried to clean the oil stain from the garage floor, but it wouldn’t come off.
An oil stain on the sofa can be tricky to remove without the right cleaner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stain - a mark that is difficult to remove
