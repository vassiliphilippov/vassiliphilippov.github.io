mountain male fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊn.tən meɪl fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn meɪl fɜːn / (BrE)
a species of fern belonging to the genus Dryopteris, typically found in mountainous regions
a fern species of the genus Dryopteris

Examples

The mountain male fern thrives in the shaded, damp forests of alpine regions.
Botanists collected specimens of the mountain male fern for research on native plant diversity.
Unlike the common male fern, the mountain male fern prefers higher altitudes and rocky soils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: male fern - a related fern species also in the genus Dryopteris
