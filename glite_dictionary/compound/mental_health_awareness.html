mental health awareness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ əˈwɛrnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ əˈwɛənəs / (BrE)
the knowledge and understanding of mental health issues and the importance of promoting mental well-being
understanding of mental health issues

Examples

Promoting mental health awareness helps reduce the stigma associated with mental illness.
Schools and workplaces now offer programs to improve mental health awareness among their members.
Raising mental health awareness can encourage people to seek help when needed.

Synset Examples

Mental health awareness is crucial in today's society.

Related Terms
