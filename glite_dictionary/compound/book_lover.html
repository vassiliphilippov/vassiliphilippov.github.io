book lover

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˌlʌvər / (AmE), / bʊk ˌlʌvə / (BrE)
a person who loves and often collects books
person who loves books

Examples

She has always been a passionate book lover, spending hours reading every day.
As a book lover, he enjoys browsing old bookstores for rare finds.
Every book lover dreams of having a personal library filled with treasured volumes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bibliophile - a person who loves or collects books
