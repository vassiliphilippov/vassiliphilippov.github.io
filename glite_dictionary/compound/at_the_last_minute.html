at the last minute

1. ADV

/ ət ðə læst ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ət ðə lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
just before the latest possible time or deadline; at the final opportunity to act or decide.
just before a deadline or event

Examples

He finished preparing his presentation at the last minute and barely made it in time.
They decided at the last minute to join the party, surprising everyone.
The hotel room was booked at the last minute, so there weren't many options left.

Synset Examples

He canceled the trip at the last minute.

She submitted her report at the last minute.
He handed in the report to-do at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at the eleventh hour - just in time; at the last possible moment
  • Antonym: in advance - ahead of time; before a deadline
