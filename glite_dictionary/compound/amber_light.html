amber light

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmbɚ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈæmbə laɪt / (BrE)
the yellow signal on a traffic light, indicating that drivers should prepare to stop or proceed with caution
yellow caution signal on a traffic light

Examples

The car slowed down as the amber light appeared ahead.
You should stop when you see an amber light, unless it's unsafe to do so.
He drove through the intersection just as the amber light began to flash.

Synset Examples

The amber light flashed, signaling the end of the session.
He hesitated at the amber light, unsure whether to stop or go.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow light - a yellow traffic signal indicating caution or prepare to stop
  • Antonym: green light - a green signal on a traffic light meaning go
  • Antonym: red light - a red signal on a traffic light meaning stop
