feather ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðər bɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə bɔːl / (BrE)
a low, tuberculate cactus native to northeastern Mexico, notable for its white, feathery spines
a cactus with feathery spines

Examples

The feather ball is prized by collectors for its delicate appearance and unusual spines.
We found several feather balls thriving in the rocky soil of northern Mexico.
Unlike many desert plants, the feather ball is surprisingly easy to grow in a home garden.
