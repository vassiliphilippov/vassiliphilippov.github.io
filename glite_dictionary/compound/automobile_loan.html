automobile loan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.t̬ə.moʊˌbiːl loʊn / (AmE), / ˈɔː.tə.məˌbiːl ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan provided to an individual to finance the purchase of an automobile, typically repaid with interest over a fixed period
loan for buying a car

Examples

He applied for an automobile loan to help pay for his new car.
Many banks offer competitive rates on automobile loans to attract more customers.
Before accepting an automobile loan, it's important to compare interest rates from different lenders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car loan - loan used to buy a car
  • Antonym: mortgage - loan to buy property or real estate
