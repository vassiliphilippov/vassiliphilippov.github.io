skirting board

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɝːtɪŋ bɔːrd / (AmE), / ˈskɜːtɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A narrow, usually wooden or plastic board fixed to the bottom of an interior wall to cover the joint between the wall and the floor.
board covering wall-floor joint
british

Examples

We need to replace the broken skirting board in the living room before painting.
Dust tends to collect along the skirting board in older houses.
He accidentally scuffed the white skirting board while moving the sofa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseboard - a board covering the joint between the wall and the floor in a room (US usage)
