open invitation

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpən ˌɪnvəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an offer or chance to communicate or participate freely, available to anyone without restriction
unrestricted offer to participate or communicate

Examples

Anyone who wishes to join the club has an open invitation to attend our weekly meetings.
The conference issued an open invitation to all interested researchers around the world.
Her home was always an open invitation for friends and family to stop by.

Synset Examples

He felt relieved with her open invitation to discuss anything.
The gallery offers an open invitation for artists to submit their work.
She sent out an open invitation to her birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invitation - a written or spoken request to participate or attend
