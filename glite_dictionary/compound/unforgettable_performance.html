unforgettable performance

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.fərˈɡɛt.ə.bəl pərˈfɔr.məns / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fəˈɡɛt.ə.bəl pəˈfɔː.məns / (BrE)
a performance that is so impressive or remarkable that it is remembered for a long time
memorable, remarkable performance

Examples

Her stunning acting resulted in an unforgettable performance that moved the entire audience.
The concert last night was an unforgettable performance by the jazz band.
Critics described his debut as an unforgettable performance that will be remembered for years.

Synset Examples

The choreography made the performance unforgettable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable performance - a performance that is worth remembering
