beer mug

1. NOUN

/ bɪr mʌɡ / (AmE), / bɪə mʌɡ / (BrE)
A large, sturdy drinking vessel with a handle, designed for serving beer.
large drinking vessel for beer

Examples

He lifted his beer mug and toasted his friends at the bar.
She collected old-fashioned beer mugs from different countries.
The waiter set a frosty beer mug in front of me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beer stein - a traditional German beer mug, often made of stoneware and with a hinged lid
  • Synonym: tankard - a large drinking vessel for beer, typically with a handle and sometimes a lid
