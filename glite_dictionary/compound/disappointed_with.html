disappointed with

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd wɪð / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd wɪð / (BrE)
feeling sad or displeased because something did not meet your expectations or hopes
feeling sad or displeased by something

Examples

She felt disappointed with her exam results despite studying hard.
Many fans were disappointed with the ending of the movie.
I'm really disappointed with the service we received at the restaurant.

Synset Examples

He was disappointed with his C in chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disappointed in - feeling sad or displeased about someone's actions or qualities
