bean dip

1. NOUN

/ bin dɪp / (AmE), / biːn dɪp / (BrE)
A creamy dip made from cooked beans, usually blended with spices and other ingredients, and served as a condiment or appetizer.
dip made from cooked beans

Examples

She brought a bowl of bean dip to the party, and it was gone in minutes.
This spicy bean dip goes perfectly with tortilla chips and fresh veggies.
You can make your own bean dip by blending cooked beans with garlic, lime juice, and herbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hummus - a Middle Eastern dip made from chickpeas
  • Degree Lower: salsa - a spicy tomato-based dip or sauce
  • Degree Higher: guacamole - a creamy avocado-based dip
