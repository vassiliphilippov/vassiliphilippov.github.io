fish finger

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ ˈfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / fɪʃ ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
A long, thin piece of fish coated in breadcrumbs and fried or baked, typically served as a convenience food.
breaded, fried fish stick
british

Examples

My son loves eating fish fingers with mashed potatoes for dinner.
They served homemade fish fingers at the school's cafeteria today.
You can bake fish fingers in the oven for a healthier option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish stick - breaded, fried fish fillet (American English)
