television program

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˈvɪʒən ˈproʊˌgræm / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈvɪʒən ˈprəʊˌgræm / (BrE)
A broadcasted content segment, such as a show or series, presented on television.
show or series broadcast on television

Examples

I never miss my favorite television program on Friday nights.
The awards ceremony honored the best television programs of the year.
She was invited to appear as a guest on a popular television program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: TV show - a show broadcast on television
