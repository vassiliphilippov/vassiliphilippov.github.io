drop zone

1. NOUN

/ drɑp zoʊn / (AmE), / drɒp zəʊn / (BrE)
an area designated for the air delivery of supplies or troops, where materials are dropped by parachute to people on the ground
military area for aerial drops

Examples

The supply plane dropped its cargo over the drop zone at dawn.
Paratroopers jumped into the drop zone during the training exercise.
Accurate marking of the drop zone is crucial for a successful mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landing zone - an area designated for aircraft to land, especially helicopters
