quirky personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɝːki pɚˌsænəˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈkwəːki pəˌsænəˈlɪti / (BrE)
an unusual or unconventional set of character traits that make someone distinctive
distinctive, unconventional character traits

Examples

Her quirky personality always brings a smile to our faces at work.
The actor is famous for playing characters with a quirky personality in his movies.
Even as a child, his quirky personality set him apart from his peers.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves Kramer's quirky personality on the show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange (of a person or their behavior)
  • Antonym: conventional personality - having typical, usual, or traditional traits
