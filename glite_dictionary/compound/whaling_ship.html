whaling ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪlɪŋ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈweɪlɪŋ ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship designed or used for hunting and processing whales
ship used for whale hunting

Examples

The old whaling ship has been turned into a museum on the waterfront.
During the 19th century, a whaling ship could be at sea for years at a time.
They spotted a whaling ship sailing through the icy Arctic waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whaler - a ship or person that hunts whales
