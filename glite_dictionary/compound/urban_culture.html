urban culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːbən ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the customs, traditions, and social behaviors typically found in city environments
customs and practices of city life

Examples

The city's vibrant music scene is a hallmark of urban culture.
Many fashion trends originate in urban culture before becoming mainstream.
The documentary explores how urban culture shapes the identities of young people.

Synset Examples

The artist's new line is called Foxxo, celebrating urban culture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rural culture - customs and practices characteristic of the countryside
