red admiral

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd ˈædmərəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈædmɪrəl / (BrE)
a butterfly of temperate Europe and Asia, noted for its black wings with striking red and white markings
a butterfly with red and white markings

Examples

A red admiral fluttered gracefully among the garden flowers, displaying its vivid colors.
We spotted a red admiral basking in the sunlight on the stone wall.
The children watched as a red admiral landed gently on a dandelion.
