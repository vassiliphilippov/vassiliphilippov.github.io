pansyish demeanor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænzi.ɪʃ dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈpænzi.ɪʃ dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
A manner or way of behaving that is perceived as weak or effeminate, often used disparagingly.
weak or effeminate behavior
slang offensive

Examples

His pansyish demeanor made him stand out in the group of aggressive athletes.
The movie was criticized for portraying the villain with a pansyish demeanor.
Despite his pansyish demeanor, he proved to be very strong and courageous under pressure.

Synset Examples

She found his pansyish attitude refreshing and endearing.
His pansyish demeanor often drew teasing from friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
  • Antonym: masculine - having qualities traditionally associated with men

Etymology

From 'pansy' (slang for an effeminate man), plus '-ish' (having the qualities of), and 'demeanor'. 'Pansy' as slang derives from the flower, symbolically linked to effeminacy.
