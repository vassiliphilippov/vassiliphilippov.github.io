tennis match

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs matʃ / (BrE)
a game of tennis played between two players (singles) or two pairs of players (doubles), typically as part of a tournament or competition
a game played between tennis players

Examples

The final tennis match of the tournament will be played on Sunday.
She won her first professional tennis match at the age of seventeen.
Rain delayed the start of the tennis match for over an hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: match - a contest or game between opponents
  • Degree Lower: tennis game - a segment of play in tennis claimed by a player or side
