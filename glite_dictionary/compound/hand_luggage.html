hand luggage

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈlʌɡɪdʒ / (AmE), / hænd ˈlʌɡɪdʒ / (BrE)
small bags or cases that you can carry with you onto an aircraft or other means of travel, rather than checking in as cargo or hold luggage.
small carry-on luggage for travel

Examples

You are only allowed one piece of hand luggage on this flight.
She packed her laptop and documents in her hand luggage for easy access.
The airline has strict size limits for hand luggage carried onto the plane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carry-on - a bag you can take onto an aircraft
  • Antonym: checked luggage - luggage put in the hold of an aircraft
