summer sweet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ swiːt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə swiːt / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to eastern and southern coastal United States, known for its fragrant white flowers that bloom in racemes during the summer.
fragrant flowering shrub of eastern US

Examples

The summer sweet adds a delightful fragrance to the garden each July.
Many pollinators are attracted to the white blossoms of the summer sweet.
We planted a row of summer sweet along the walkway for its beautiful summer blooms.
