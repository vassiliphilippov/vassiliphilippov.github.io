adjoining room

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / əˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a hotel room that shares a wall with another room, typically with or without a connecting door
hotel room sharing a wall with another

Examples

The family requested adjoining rooms so they could stay close together during their holiday.
Our reservation includes two adjoining rooms for a more convenient stay.
If you need more space, ask the front desk about available adjoining rooms.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: connecting room - hotel rooms that are joined by a private interior door
