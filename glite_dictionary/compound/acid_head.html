acid head

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsɪd hɛd / (AmE), / ˈæsɪd hɛd / (BrE)
a person who habitually takes LSD; a regular user of the drug LSD
person who regularly takes LSD
slang

Examples

In the late 1960s, many young people were labeled acid heads by the media.
She talked about her wild years as an acid head during the psychedelic era.
Some acid heads claim that LSD expands their mind and changes their perspective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acid freak - person addicted to or enthusiastic about LSD

Etymology

From 'acid' (slang for LSD, short for lysergic acid diethylamide) + 'head' (slang for habitual user).
