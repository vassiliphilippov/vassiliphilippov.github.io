animal distribution

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ˌdɪstrəˈbjuʃən / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the geographical spread or arrangement of animal species across different regions of the world
geographical spread of animal species

Examples

Climate change can drastically alter the animal distribution in affected regions.
Recent studies show shifts in animal distribution because of urban development.
Mapping patterns of animal distribution helps scientists understand biodiversity hotspots.

Synset Examples

The study of vicariance reveals patterns in animal distribution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distribution - the way something is shared out or spread over an area
