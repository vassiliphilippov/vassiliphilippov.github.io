screw eye

1. NOUN

/ skruː aɪ / (AmE), / skruː aɪ / (BrE)
A screw with a shank bent into a ring or loop at one end, used for attaching wires, ropes, or cables to surfaces.
screw with a ring-shaped end

Examples

He installed a screw eye in the ceiling to hang the plant safely.
Thread the cord through the screw eye to secure the frame to the wall.
The curtain was held up by several sturdy screw eyes screwed into the window frame.
