hard palate

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd ˈpælət / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈpælət / (BrE)
the bony front part of the roof of the mouth, separating the oral and nasal cavities
bony part of the roof of the mouth
medical

Examples

The dentist examined her hard palate for any signs of infection.
A cleft in the hard palate can cause difficulties with eating and speech.
The hard palate forms a barrier between the mouth and nasal passages.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft palate - the fleshy, flexible part toward the back of the roof of the mouth
