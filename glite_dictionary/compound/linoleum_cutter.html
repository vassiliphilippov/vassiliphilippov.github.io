linoleum cutter

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈnoʊliəm ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / lɪˈnəʊliəm ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
A small knife with a short, stiff blade and a curved point, used for cutting linoleum, especially in printmaking or flooring work.
knife for cutting linoleum

Examples

She used a linoleum cutter to carefully carve designs into the soft block.
Before installing the tiles, he trimmed the edges with his linoleum cutter.
A linoleum cutter is essential for preparing detailed printing blocks in art class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility knife - a general-purpose cutting tool with a retractable blade

Etymology

From linoleum (a type of floor covering) + cutter (one that cuts).
