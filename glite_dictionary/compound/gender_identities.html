gender identities

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛndɚ aɪˈdɛntɪtiz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndə aɪˈdɛntɪtiz / (BrE)
The various ways in which people identify or express themselves in relation to gender.
ways of identifying or expressing gender

Examples

There are many gender identities represented in modern society, each with its own unique experiences.
Discussions about gender identities have become increasingly common in recent years.
Educational programs are helping people understand different gender identities and respect personal preferences.

Synset Examples

Being a transphile means celebrating diversity in gender identities.

Related Terms
