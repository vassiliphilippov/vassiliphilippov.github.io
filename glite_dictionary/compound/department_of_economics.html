department of economics

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks / (BrE)
an academic division in a college or university responsible for teaching and research in economics
academic division for economics studies

Examples

She applied to the department of economics for a graduate teaching assistantship.
The university's department of economics has produced several Nobel laureates.
Our department of economics offers courses in both microeconomics and macroeconomics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faculty of economics - an academic group or teaching staff in economics at a university
  • Antonym: department of history - academic division for teaching and research in history
