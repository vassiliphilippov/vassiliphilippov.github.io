dog-sized

1. ADJ

/ dɔg saɪzd / (AmE), / dɒg saɪzd / (BrE)
Having the approximate size of an average dog.
of a size similar to a dog

Examples

The strange animal was dog-sized and moved quickly through the bushes.
They sell dog-sized beds for large pets.
A dog-sized statue greeted visitors at the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cat-sized - having the approximate size of an average cat
