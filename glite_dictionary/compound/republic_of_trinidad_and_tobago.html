Republic of Trinidad and Tobago

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌtrɪnɪˈdæd ənd təˈbeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌtrɪnɪˈdad ənd təˈbeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a sovereign island country in the southern Caribbean, off the northeastern coast of Venezuela, comprising the islands of Trinidad and Tobago; independent from the United Kingdom since 1962.
island country in the Caribbean

Examples

The Republic of Trinidad and Tobago became an independent nation in 1962.
Tourism plays a vital role in the economy of the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago.
She moved to the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago for work after graduating from university.
