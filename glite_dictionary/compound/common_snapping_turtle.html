common snapping turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈsnæpɪŋ ˌtɝtəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈsnæpɪŋ ˌtɜːtəl / (BrE)
a large North American freshwater turtle with a powerful hooked jaw, known for its aggressive nature and tendency to bite when threatened
large, aggressive freshwater turtle of North America

Examples

The common snapping turtle can deliver a painful bite if provoked.
We spotted a common snapping turtle basking on a log in the pond.
Many people are wary of swimming where common snapping turtles are known to live.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapping turtle - freshwater turtle known for its aggressive, biting behavior
