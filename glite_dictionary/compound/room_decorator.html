room decorator

1. NOUN

/ ruːm ˈdɛkəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ruːm ˈdɛkəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is to decorate the interiors of rooms, arranging furnishings, colors, and accessories for aesthetic and functional effect
person specializing in decorating interiors

Examples

The room decorator chose pastel shades to create a calm atmosphere in the bedroom.
As a professional room decorator, she always keeps up with the latest trends in interior design.
They hired a room decorator to transform their living room into a more inviting space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior decorator - a person whose job is to decorate and arrange the inside of buildings
