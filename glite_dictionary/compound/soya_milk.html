soya milk

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪ.ə mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪ.ə mɪlk / (BrE)
a plant-based milk substitute made from soybeans, often used as an alternative to dairy milk
plant-based milk from soybeans

Examples

Many people choose soya milk as a dairy-free alternative for their cereal.
You can make tofu at home using soya milk and coagulant.
The café offers both cow's milk and soya milk for coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soy milk - plant-based milk substitute from soybeans
  • Degree Lower: almond milk - plant-based milk substitute from almonds
  • Antonym: dairy milk - milk from cows or other animals

Etymology

From 'soya', an alternative spelling of 'soy', from Japanese 'shōyu' (soy sauce), and 'milk'.
