English muffin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈmʌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈmʌfɪn / (BrE)
a small, round, flat yeast-leavened bread, typically sliced horizontally, toasted, and served with butter or toppings
small, round, flat bread; often sliced and toasted

Examples

I had an English muffin with jam for breakfast this morning.
She toasted an English muffin and topped it with poached eggs and hollandaise sauce.
Would you like your burger served on an English muffin instead of a regular bun?

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: muffin - a small, round, sweet or savory quick bread
