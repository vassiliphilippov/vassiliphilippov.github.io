air rage

1. NOUN

/ ɛr reɪdʒ / (AmE), / eə reɪdʒ / (BrE)
violent or aggressive behavior by airline passengers, usually as a result of stress or frustration during a flight
aggressive behavior by airline passengers

Examples

Incidents of air rage are increasing as flights become more crowded.
The airline staff handled the air rage situation calmly and professionally.
Many airlines now have strict policies to deal with passengers displaying air rage.

Synset Examples

His air rage was evident during the flight delay.
She witnessed air rage when the flight was overbooked.
Passengers experienced air rage due to cramped conditions.
