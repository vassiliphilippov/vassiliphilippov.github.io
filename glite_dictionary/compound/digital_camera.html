digital camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl ˈkæm(ə)ɹə / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈkæm(ə)rə / (BrE)
A camera that records images in digital form, allowing them to be stored and viewed electronically.
camera that stores images digitally

Examples

She bought a new digital camera to take photos on her vacation.
Most smartphones now function as a digital camera as well as a phone.
The digital camera allows you to preview pictures instantly on the screen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camera - device for capturing photographs
