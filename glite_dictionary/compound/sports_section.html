sports section

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌsɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the part of a newspaper or magazine devoted to news and reports about sports
part of a publication covering sports news

Examples

He always reads the sports section first to check the latest football results.
The article about the new swimmer appeared in the sports section of the Sunday paper.
She clipped her favorite team's photo from the sports section for her scrapbook.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports page - a single page in a publication covering sports news
