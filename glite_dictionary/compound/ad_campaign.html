ad campaign

1. NOUN

/ æd kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / æd kämˈpeɪn / (BrE)
a series of coordinated advertisements and promotional activities designed to achieve a specific marketing goal
series of coordinated advertisements

Examples

The company's new ad campaign targets young adults with catchy slogans and colorful visuals.
She was hired to oversee the launch of their international ad campaign next quarter.
After the ad campaign ended, sales of the product increased significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - an organized program of advertisements or marketing efforts

2. NOUN

/ æd kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / æd kəmˈpeɪn / (BrE)
a coordinated series of advertisements promoting a particular product, service, or message
a marketing series of advertisements

Examples

The ad campaign for the new smartphone featured celebrities and catchy slogans.
After launching a creative ad campaign, sales for the brand quickly doubled.
They hired a top agency to design their upcoming ad campaign.

Synset Examples

The new ad campaign feels too salesy for my taste.
The new Talcum X ad campaign is really catchy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - a coordinated marketing effort using advertisements to promote something

Etymology

Shortened from 'advertising campaign'; 'ad' is a common abbreviation of 'advertisement', from Latin 'advertere' (to turn toward).
