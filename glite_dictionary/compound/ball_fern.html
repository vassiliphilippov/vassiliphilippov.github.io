ball fern

1. NOUN

/ bɔl fɝn / (AmE), / bɔːl fɜːn / (BrE)
A feathery fern native to tropical Asia and Malaysia.
feathery fern of tropical Asia and Malaysia

Examples

The ball fern thrives in humid, shaded forests throughout Malaysia.
Gardeners often choose the ball fern for its delicate, attractive foliage.
Several species of ball fern are popular in Asian ornamental gardening.
