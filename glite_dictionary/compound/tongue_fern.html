tongue fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌŋ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ fɜːn / (BrE)
An East Asian fern with fronds shaped like tongues, sometimes classified in the genus Cyclophorus.
an East Asian fern with tongue-shaped fronds

Examples

The tongue fern is popular among collectors for its unique frond shape.
In shaded gardens, tongue ferns thrive and add a lush, exotic touch.
She identified the plant as a tongue fern due to its distinctive leaf form.
