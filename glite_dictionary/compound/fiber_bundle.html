fiber bundle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪbɚ ˌbʌndl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə ˌbʌndl̩ / (BrE)
a bundle of fibers, especially referring to groups of nerve fibers in the body
group of fibers, esp. nerve fibers

Examples

The optic nerve is made up of a thick fiber bundle connecting the eye to the brain.
Damage to the spinal cord's fiber bundle can result in loss of sensation below the injury site.
Scientists studied how signals travel quickly through each fiber bundle in the nervous system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerve bundle - a bundle of nerve fibers
  • Synonym: fascia - a band or sheet of connective tissue, such as bundles of nerve fibers
