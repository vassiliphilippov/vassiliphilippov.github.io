symbolic logician

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈbɑlɪk ləˈɡɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɒlɪk ləˈɡɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in symbolic logic, often engaging in the study or teaching of formal symbolic systems in logic
expert in symbolic logic

Examples

As a symbolic logician, she contributed several innovative concepts to the study of formal logic.
The conference attracted mathematicians and symbolic logicians from around the world.
He found the work of the 20th-century symbolic logician particularly inspiring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logician - an expert in logic
