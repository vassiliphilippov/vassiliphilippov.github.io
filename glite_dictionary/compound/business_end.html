business end

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs end / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs end / (BrE)
the practical, important, or most serious part of something, especially the part directly involved in achieving a result
most important or practical part

Examples

Please be careful when handling the business end of a knife.
In negotiations, she takes care of the business end with great skill.
We need more people who can handle the business end of our operations.

Synset Examples

He knows the business end of negotiations very well.
Make sure to handle the business end of the project first.
Always focus on the business end to ensure success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heart - the central or most important part of something
  • Synonym: tip - the pointed or end part of an object, often where the action happens
