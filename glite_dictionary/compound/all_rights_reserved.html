all rights reserved

1. X

/ ˌɔl ˈraɪts rɪˈzɝvd / (AmE), / ˌɔːl ˈraɪts rɪˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
A legal phrase indicating that the copyright holder grants no permission for reproduction or use without explicit consent.
legal notice of full copyright protection
legal

Examples

The new photography book had all rights reserved printed on the copyright page.
At the end of the film's credits, the phrase all rights reserved appeared to assert the company's copyright.
Whenever you publish original work, it's common to include all rights reserved as part of your copyright statement.

Synset Examples

The movie poster stated all rights reserved at the bottom.
Please note that all rights reserved for this artwork.
The book cover read all rights reserved, no copying allowed.
