to bake bread

1. VERB

/ beɪk brɛd / (AmE), / beɪk brɛd / (BrE)
to make bread by cooking dough in an oven or similar appliance
make bread by baking dough

Examples

She likes to bake bread at home every Saturday morning.
Many families bake bread together as a weekend tradition.
During the lockdown, more people started to bake bread from scratch.

Synset Examples

They love to bake bread on weekends.

Related Terms
