up-to-the-minute

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌp tə ðə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌp tə ðə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
up to the present moment; the very latest or most current.
most recent; very up-to-date

Examples

The website provides up-to-the-minute updates on the weather in your area.
She always wears up-to-the-minute fashions straight from the runway.
This report includes up-to-the-minute data collected just hours ago.

Synset Examples

the news is up-to-the-minute
the very latest scientific discoveries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latest - most recent; newest
