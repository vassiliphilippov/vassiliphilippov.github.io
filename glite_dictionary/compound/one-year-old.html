one-year-old

1. ADJ

/ wʌn jɪr oʊld / (AmE), / wʌn jɪə əʊld / (BrE)
a person or animal that is one year old, or describing someone or something that is one year of age
a person or animal aged one year

Examples

Our one-year-old daughter took her first steps yesterday.
The zoo welcomed a one-year-old panda from China.
Please keep small objects away from your one-year-old.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: toddler - a young child who is just beginning to walk
