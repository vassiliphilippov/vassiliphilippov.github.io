order form

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə fɔːm / (BrE)
a printed or digital document used to request goods or services from a supplier or company
document for placing an order

Examples

Please fill out the order form and submit it to complete your purchase.
The company sent me an order form by email for the new catalog items.
He accidentally left one item off the order form before sending it in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purchase order - an official document authorizing a purchase transaction
