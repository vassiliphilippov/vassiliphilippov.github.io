restorative experience

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈstɔːrətɪv ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / rɪˈstɒrətɪv ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an experience that has a healing or refreshing effect, helping to restore health, energy, or well-being
healing or refreshing experience

Examples

The yoga retreat provided a truly restorative experience for all participants.
Spending time in nature often gives me a restorative experience when I'm stressed.
After a long week at work, a quiet evening at home can be a restorative experience.

Synset Examples

The spa offers a restorative experience for tired souls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healing - making or becoming healthy again
  • Synonym: refreshing experience - an experience that makes someone feel refreshed

Etymology

From Latin restaurare (to restore) and Latin experientia (trial, experience).
