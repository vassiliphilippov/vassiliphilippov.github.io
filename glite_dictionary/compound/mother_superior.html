mother superior

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðɚ səˈpɪriɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə səˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
the woman in charge of a convent or community of nuns
woman head of a convent of nuns

Examples

The new mother superior welcomed the novices to the convent with kindness and grace.
During the meeting, the mother superior discussed the plans for the upcoming charity event.
Sister Agnes was appointed mother superior after decades of devoted service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abbess - a woman who is the head of an abbey of nuns
