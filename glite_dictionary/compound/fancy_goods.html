fancy goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænsi ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈfænsi ɡʊdz / (BrE)
articles that are mainly decorative or ornamental, rather than practical or functional, often sold as gifts or trinkets.
decorative or ornamental items

Examples

The shop's window was filled with fancy goods for the holiday season.
She specialized in selling imported fancy goods such as porcelain figurines and decorative vases.
The gift store offers a wide range of fancy goods to suit every taste.
