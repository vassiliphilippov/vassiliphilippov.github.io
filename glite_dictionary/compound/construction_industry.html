construction industry

1. NOUN

/ kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃən ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy involved in building houses, commercial buildings, and other structures
sector building houses and structures

Examples

The construction industry recovered steadily after the financial crisis of 2008.
New technologies are transforming the construction industry by making building processes more efficient.
Many people are employed in the construction industry, working on infrastructure projects nationwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: building industry - the industry concerned with the construction of buildings
