feeding chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfidɪŋ ˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈfiːdɪŋ ˌtʃɛə / (BrE)
a high chair designed for feeding an infant or very young child, typically with long legs, a footrest, and a detachable tray
high chair for feeding infants

Examples

The baby sat happily in her feeding chair while her mother prepared breakfast.
We bought a sturdy feeding chair for our twins to make mealtime easier.
The restaurant provided a clean feeding chair for families with young children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high chair - a tall chair for feeding young children
