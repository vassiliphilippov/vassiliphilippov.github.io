one thousand

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈθaʊzənd / (AmE), / wʌn ˈθaʊzənd / (BrE)
the number 1,000; the cardinal number equal to ten times one hundred
the number 1,000

Examples

There are one thousand seats in the concert hall.
He ran one thousand meters in the race.
The company donated one thousand dollars to charity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thousand - the number 1,000
  • Degree Lower: nine hundred - the number 900
  • Degree Higher: one thousand one - the number 1,001
