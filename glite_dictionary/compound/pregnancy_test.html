pregnancy test

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛɡnənsi tɛst / (AmE), / ˈprɛɡnənsi tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test to determine if a woman is pregnant, usually by detecting hormone levels in urine or blood
test to determine pregnancy
medical

Examples

She anxiously waited for the pregnancy test results to appear.
A pregnancy test can usually detect pregnancy as early as a few days after a missed period.
He bought a pregnancy test from the pharmacy for his wife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - an examination or procedure for evaluating something (general sense)
