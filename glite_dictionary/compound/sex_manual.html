sex manual

1. NOUN

/ sɛks ˈmænjuəl / (AmE), / seks ˈmanjuəl / (BrE)
a book that provides instruction in sexual techniques, intended to enhance the reader's sex life
book of sexual techniques

Examples

He bought a sex manual to improve his relationship with his partner.
The sex manual offered practical advice that was both informative and respectful.
Many couples find that reading a sex manual helps them communicate more openly about their needs.
