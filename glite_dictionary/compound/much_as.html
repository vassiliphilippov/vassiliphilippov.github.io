much as

1. ADV

/ mʌtʃ æz / (AmE), / mʌtʃ az / (BrE)
In a similar way; in much the same manner.
in a similar way

Examples

Much as her brother did, she decided to pursue a career in engineering.
The team responded much as they had the previous year, showing resilience in the face of difficulty.
He approached the challenge much as his mentor would have, with patience and determination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: similarly - in a similar manner
  • Synonym: like - in the same way as
