broadcast station

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːst ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A station equipped to transmit radio or television programs to the public.
a station transmitting radio or TV programs

Examples

The local broadcast station aired breaking news about the approaching storm.
Each broadcast station must comply with government regulations regarding content.
She got a job working at a broadcast station as a technical engineer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio station - a station for transmitting radio broadcasts
  • Synonym: television station - a station for transmitting television broadcasts
