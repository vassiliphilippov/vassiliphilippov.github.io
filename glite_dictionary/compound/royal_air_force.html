Royal Air Force

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɔɪəl ˈɛr fɔrs / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl ˈeə fɔːs / (BrE)
the air force of the United Kingdom, responsible for aerial military operations.
UK's aerial military force

Examples

My grandfather served in the Royal Air Force during World War II.
The Royal Air Force played a crucial role in the Battle of Britain.
Each year, the Royal Air Force conducts an air show at its main base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: RAF - abbreviation for Royal Air Force
