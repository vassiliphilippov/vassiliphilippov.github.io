silk-stocking dress

1. NOUN

/ sɪlk ˈstɑkɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / sɪlk ˈstɒkɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
A formal and stylish dress made of silk, typically worn for special occasions.
formal silk dress

Examples

She wore a silk-stocking dress to the gala and stood out among the guests.
The bride chose an elegant silk-stocking dress for her wedding ceremony.
Many celebrities appeared on the red carpet in luxurious silk-stocking dresses this year.
