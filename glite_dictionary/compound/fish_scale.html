fish scale

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ skeɪl / (AmE), / fɪʃ skeɪl / (BrE)
A small, thin, flat plate forming part of the covering of a fish's body.
a thin plate covering a fish's body

Examples

The shimmering fish scale reflected the sunlight brilliantly underwater.
She collected a fish scale from the shore and examined its structure under a microscope.
Many artists are inspired by the iridescent colors of a fish scale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scale - a thin, flat, hard plate forming part of the external covering of certain animals
