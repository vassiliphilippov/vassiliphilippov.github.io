pathway to recovery

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæθˌweɪ tu ɹɪˈkʌvɚi / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθweɪ tʊ rɪˈkʌvəri / (BrE)
the process or course of actions by which someone heals or regains health after illness, injury, or hardship
process of returning to health

Examples

After her surgery, the pathway to recovery included months of physical therapy and rest.
Positive thinking and support from family can greatly ease one's pathway to recovery.
Doctors emphasized that every pathway to recovery is unique and depends on the individual.

Synset Examples

The pathway to recovery can be difficult but rewarding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recovery - the process of becoming well again after an illness or injury
  • Antonym: relapse - a return to a worse state after improvement, especially in health
