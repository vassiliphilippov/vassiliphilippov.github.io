tree surgeon

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈsɝdʒən / (AmE), / tri ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A specialist who treats and cares for damaged or diseased trees, often performing pruning, surgery, or removal of affected parts.
specialist in treating damaged trees

Examples

We called a tree surgeon to assess the old oak after the storm.
The tree surgeon carefully removed diseased branches from the maple in our garden.
Many parks hire a tree surgeon to maintain the health and safety of their trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborist - a professional in the cultivation, management, and study of trees
