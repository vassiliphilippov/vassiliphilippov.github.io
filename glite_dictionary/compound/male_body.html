male body

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / meɪl ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
the physical body of a male human being, as distinguished from that of a female
body of a male human

Examples

The male body develops broader shoulders during puberty compared to the female body.
Athletes often train to increase the strength and endurance of the male body.
Different hormones influence fat distribution in the male body and the female body.
