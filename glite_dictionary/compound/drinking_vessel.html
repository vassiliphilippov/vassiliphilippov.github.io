drinking vessel

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌvɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌvɛsl̩ / (BrE)
a container designed for holding liquids to be drunk, such as a cup, glass, or mug
container used for drinking

Examples

The museum displayed an ancient drinking vessel made of bronze.
He accidentally knocked over the drinking vessel and spilled his water.
Many cultures have their own traditional drinking vessel styles and designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup - a small open container for drinking
  • Synonym: glass - a drinking container made of glass
