Quadrigesima Sunday

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɑːdrɪˈdʒɛsəmə ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˌkwɒdrɪˈdʒɛsɪmə ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
the first Sunday in Lent in the Christian calendar
first Sunday in Lent

Examples

Many churches hold special services on Quadrigesima Sunday to mark the beginning of Lent.
On Quadrigesima Sunday, the congregation reflected on the themes of repentance and renewal.
The tradition of fasting often begins on Quadrigesima Sunday in some Christian denominations.

Etymology

From Latin 'quadragesima' (fortieth), as Lent lasts forty days.
