best seller

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛst ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌbɛst ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
A book or other publication that sells in large numbers, especially over a short period.
book or publication with high sales

Examples

Her first novel quickly became a best seller and was translated into twenty languages.
The author dreamed of writing a best seller that would top the charts for weeks.
Despite little publicity, the cookbook turned out to be a surprise best seller last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockbuster - something, especially a film or book, that is a great commercial success
