pub quiz

1. NOUN

/ pʌb kwɪz / (AmE), / pʌb kwɪz / (BrE)
an informal competition in which teams or individuals answer questions on general knowledge, typically held in a pub
a trivia competition held in a pub

Examples

We decided to enter the pub quiz to test our general knowledge skills.
Every Thursday night, the local pub hosts a lively pub quiz.
Winning the pub quiz earned them a free round of drinks.

Synset Examples

That was a tough pub quiz question last night.
He answered the pub quiz question before I could.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trivia night - an event where people compete in answering trivia questions, often in a bar setting
