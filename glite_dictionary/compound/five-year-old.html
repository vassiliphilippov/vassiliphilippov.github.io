five-year-old

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪv jɪr oʊld / (AmE), / ˈfaɪv jɪə əʊld / (BrE)
a person or thing that is five years old
person or thing aged five years

Examples

The kindergarten class is full of energetic five-year-olds.
Her daughter is a clever five-year-old who loves to read.
It is rare for a five-year-old car to be in perfect condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child - a young person between infancy and puberty
  • Degree Higher: six-year-old - a person or thing that is six years old
  • Degree Lower: four-year-old - a person or thing that is four years old
