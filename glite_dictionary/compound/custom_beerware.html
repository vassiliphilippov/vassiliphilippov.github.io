custom beerware

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəm ˈbɪrˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm ˈbɪəˌwɛə / (BrE)
Drinking vessels for beer that are personalized or specially made to order.
personalized beer drinking vessels

Examples

The brewery offers custom beerware featuring your name or logo.
She collects custom beerware from local artists wherever she travels.
Many groomsmen receive custom beerware as a wedding gift.

Synset Examples

They sell custom beerware at the local craft fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beerware - drinking vessels designed for beer
