athletic wear

1. NOUN

/ əθˈlɛtɪk wɛr / (AmE), / əθˈlɛtɪk wɛə / (BrE)
clothing designed for sports or physical exercise, often also worn for casual occasions
clothing for sports or casual wear

Examples

He prefers to wear athletic wear even when he's not working out.
Many people choose athletic wear for its comfort and style.
The store specializes in selling the latest athletic wear from major brands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sportswear - clothing designed specifically for sports and physical activities
