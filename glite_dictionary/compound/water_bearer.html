Water Bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ər ˌbɛr.ər / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.tə ˌbeə.rə / (BrE)
The eleventh sign of the zodiac, Aquarius; the sun is in this sign from about January 20 to February 18.
zodiac sign Aquarius

Examples

She was born under the sign of the Water Bearer, making her an Aquarius.
According to astrologers, people of the Water Bearer sign are independent and inventive.
The Water Bearer is often depicted holding a jar pouring water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aquarius - the eleventh sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: Pisces - the twelfth sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Lower: Capricorn - the tenth sign of the zodiac
