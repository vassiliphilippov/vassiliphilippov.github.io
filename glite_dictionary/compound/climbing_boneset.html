climbing boneset

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈboʊnˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈbəʊnˌsɛt / (BrE)
a tropical American herb with vanilla-scented flowers that climbs up trees
tropical American climbing herb

Examples

The fragrant flowers of the climbing boneset attract many butterflies in the rainforest.
Gardeners admire the climbing boneset for its ability to grow up tree trunks and trellises.
Some traditional healers use climbing boneset in remedies for its pleasant scent and medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boneset - a name for several plants of the genus Eupatorium, used in herbal medicine
