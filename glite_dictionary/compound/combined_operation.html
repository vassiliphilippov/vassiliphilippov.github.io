combined operation

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈbaɪnd ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / kəmˈbaɪnd ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a military action carried out jointly by two or more allied nations or by coordinating land, sea, and air forces
joint military action by allies or coordinated forces

Examples

The two armies planned a combined operation to retake the city from enemy forces.
Successful combined operations require careful coordination between all branches of the military.
NATO executed a combined operation involving land, sea, and air units during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint operation - a military operation involving cooperation between different services or forces
