fostering equality

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑstərɪŋ ɪˈkwɑləti / (AmE), / ˈfɒstərɪŋ ɪˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
The act of encouraging and supporting equal rights and opportunities for all people.
promoting equal rights

Examples

Educational programs play a key role in fostering equality within diverse communities.
The organization is dedicated to fostering equality in the workplace.
Policies aimed at fostering equality can reduce social and economic disparities.

Synset Examples

Understanding LGBTism is crucial for fostering equality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promoting equality - Supporting or advancing equal rights for all groups
  • Antonym: discrimination - Unjust treatment of different groups, opposite of equality
