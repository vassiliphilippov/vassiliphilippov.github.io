steel factory

1. NOUN

/ stiːl ˈfæktəri / (AmE), / stiːl ˈfæktəri / (BrE)
A factory where steel is manufactured from iron and other materials.
factory where steel is made

Examples

The city grew rapidly after a large steel factory was built nearby.
Hundreds of workers are employed at the steel factory on the outskirts of town.
Dust and noise from the steel factory can be a problem for local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steel mill - factory for producing steel
  • Synonym: foundry - workshop or factory for casting metal
