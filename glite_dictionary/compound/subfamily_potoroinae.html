subfamily Potoroinae

1. NOUN

/ sʌbˌfæmɪli poʊˌtɔːroʊˈini / (AmE), / sʌbˌfæmɪli pəˌtɒrəʊˈiːniː / (BrE)
A subfamily of marsupials in the family Potoroidae, commonly known as rat kangaroos, native to Australia.
a subfamily of marsupials (rat kangaroos)

Examples

The subfamily Potoroinae includes small marsupials known as rat kangaroos.
Scientists are studying the evolutionary history of the subfamily Potoroinae in Australia.
Several endangered species belong to the subfamily Potoroinae.

Etymology

From New Latin Potoroinae, based on the genus Potoroos.
