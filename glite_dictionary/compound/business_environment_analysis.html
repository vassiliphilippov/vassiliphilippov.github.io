business environment analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ɪnˈvaɪərənmənt əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the systematic process of identifying and assessing external factors affecting a business, and developing strategies in response
analysis of external factors affecting a business

Examples

Our company commissioned a business environment analysis before expanding into the Southeast Asian market.
A thorough business environment analysis can reveal opportunities and threats for both new and established firms.
Consultants often emphasize the importance of business environment analysis in strategic planning.

Synset Examples

There are also many other business environment reforms that have taken place over the past two years, having their genesis in the IDB's business environment analysis of nearly a decade ago, but these will require another article to document.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmental analysis - the process of assessing environmental factors affecting an organization
