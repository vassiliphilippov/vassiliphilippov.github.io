line drawing

1. NOUN

/ laɪn ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / laɪn ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
A drawing that consists only of lines, showing the outlines of forms or objects without shading or color.
drawing using outlines only

Examples

He created a beautiful line drawing of the city skyline.
The artist's line drawings capture the essence of her subjects with remarkable simplicity.
Students learned to make a line drawing before adding any colors or shading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketch - a rough or unfinished drawing or painting, often made to assist in making a more finished picture
  • Antonym: painting - a picture made using paint
