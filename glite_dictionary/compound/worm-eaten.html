worm-eaten

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɜrm ˌitn̩ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːm ˌiːtn̩ / (BrE)
Infested with or damaged (as if eaten) by worms; marked by holes or decay caused by worms.
damaged or infested by worms

Examples

The old bookshelf was so worm-eaten that it crumbled when touched.
They discovered a worm-eaten apple at the bottom of the crate.
The document’s worm-eaten edges made it difficult to handle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotten - decayed or decomposed, especially by the action of bacteria or fungi
  • Antonym: pristine - in its original condition; unspoiled
