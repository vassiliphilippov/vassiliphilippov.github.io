old times

1. NOUN

/ oʊld taɪmz / (AmE), / əʊld taɪmz / (BrE)
the past, especially as remembered with fondness or nostalgia
the past, remembered with nostalgia

Examples

My grandparents often tell stories about the old times when they were young.
He loves looking through photos from the old times and recalling happy memories.
Sometimes, hearing that song makes me long for the old times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good old days - a time in the past remembered as better or happier than the present
