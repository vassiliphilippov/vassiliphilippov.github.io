shoe repairing

1. NOUN

/ ʃu rɪˈpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃuː rɪˈpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or occupation of mending and restoring shoes
the trade of mending shoes

Examples

He learned the art of shoe repairing from his father, who was a skilled cobbler.
Local businesses like shoe repairing shops are disappearing from many towns.
After the flood, the shoe repairing shop was busier than ever with damaged footwear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cobbling - the craft of mending shoes
  • Degree Higher: shoemaking - the craft or trade of making shoes
