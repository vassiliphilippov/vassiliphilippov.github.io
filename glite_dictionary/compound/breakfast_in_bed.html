breakfast in bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkfəst ɪn bɛd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst ɪn bɛd / (BrE)
a meal, typically breakfast, served and eaten while a person remains in bed
breakfast served and eaten in bed

Examples

She surprised her husband with breakfast in bed on his birthday.
On lazy Sundays, he loves having breakfast in bed while reading the newspaper.
The hotel offered complimentary breakfast in bed for honeymoon couples.

Synset Examples

He enjoys breakfast in bed on special occasions.
