caught up

1. ADJ

/ kɔt ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːt ʌp / (BrE)
Involved in something, often without intending or wanting to be; absorbed or entangled, especially emotionally or involuntarily.
involved involuntarily or emotionally

Examples

She got caught up in office politics and found it hard to escape.
He was so caught up in the story that he forgot about the time.
Many people were caught up in the panic during the evacuation.

Synset Examples

caught up in the excitement of the crowd
caught up in the scandal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: involved - taking part in or connected with something
  • Synonym: entangled - caught in or involved in a difficult situation
