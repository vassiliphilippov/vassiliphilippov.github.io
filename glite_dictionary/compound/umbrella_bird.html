umbrella bird

1. NOUN

/ ʌmˈbrɛl.ə bɝd / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə bɜːd / (BrE)
a large black tropical American bird with a prominent overhanging crest and a long, feathered wattle hanging from its neck
large black bird with crest and wattle

Examples

The umbrella bird is known for its unusual crest and deep booming calls in the rainforest.
During their expedition, they were thrilled to spot a rare umbrella bird perched on a branch.
The male umbrella bird uses its wattle and crest to attract a mate during the breeding season.
