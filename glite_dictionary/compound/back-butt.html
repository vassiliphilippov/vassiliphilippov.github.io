back-butt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæk ˌbʌt / (AmE), / ˈbæk ˌbʌt / (BrE)
the area where the lower back meets the buttocks
area between lower back and buttocks
informal

Examples

He sat down hard on his back-butt after slipping on the ice.
After the long bike ride, her back-butt was sore.
The doctor asked if he felt discomfort in his lower back or back-butt.

Synset Examples

He fell and landed right on his back-butt.
She complained about pain in her back-butt after the hike.
