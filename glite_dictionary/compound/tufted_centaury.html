tufted centaury

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌftɪd sɛnˈtɔri / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd ˈsɛntʃʊəri / (BrE)
a tufted perennial plant of western Europe and the Azores, with bright pink to white flowers
a European perennial plant with pink or white flowers

Examples

The meadows were dotted with blooming tufted centaury in early summer.
Gardeners often plant tufted centaury to attract butterflies with its colorful flowers.
The tufted centaury thrives in well-drained soil and sunny locations.
