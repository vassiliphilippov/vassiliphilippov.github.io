barley-broth

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrli ˌbrɔθ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːli ˌbrɒθ / (BrE)
a soup made with barley, often hearty and comforting, typically using vegetables or meat stock
soup made with barley

Examples

After hiking all afternoon, a bowl of barley-broth was exactly what they needed.
My mother taught me how to make traditional Scottish barley-broth for winter dinners.
The restaurant's menu featured a hearty lamb and barley-broth that was perfect for cold evenings.

Synset Examples

The warm barley-broth was comforting after a long day.
She served a hearty barley-broth on the cold winter night.
His grandmother's recipe for barley-broth was a family favorite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barley soup - soup in which barley is the main ingredient

Etymology

barley: from Old English *bærlic* (of barley); broth: from Old English *broth* (liquid in which meat has been boiled).
