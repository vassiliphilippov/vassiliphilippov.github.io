boot maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːt ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbuːt ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes boots, especially by hand or as a skilled trade
someone who makes boots

Examples

The boot maker handcrafted a pair of leather boots for the client.
In the small town, the local boot maker was famous for his sturdy work.
Apprentices watched closely as the experienced boot maker demonstrated his techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoemaker - a person who makes or repairs shoes
