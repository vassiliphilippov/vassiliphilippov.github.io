scoring system

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɔrɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈskɔːrɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of rules or methods used to assign points, grades, or rankings based on quality, merit, or quantity.
system for assigning points or grades

Examples

The competition uses a complex scoring system to ensure fairness among all participants.
We had to adjust our scoring system due to changes in the rules.
The new scoring system rewards creativity and originality more than before.
