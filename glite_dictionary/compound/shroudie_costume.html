shroudie costume

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃraʊdi ˈkɑːstum / (AmE), / ˈʃraʊdi ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
A costume designed to resemble a burial shroud, typically worn for spooky or ghost-like effect.
costume resembling a burial shroud

Examples

He wore a shroudie costume to the Halloween party, complete with tattered white cloth and eerie makeup.
The children laughed as they tried to make their own shroudie costume from an old sheet.
Her shroudie costume stood out among the other outfits at the haunted house event.

Synset Examples

The shroudie costume was both spooky and stylish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghost costume - costume that looks like a ghost
