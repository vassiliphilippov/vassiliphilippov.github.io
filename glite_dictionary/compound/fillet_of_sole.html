fillet of sole

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlət əv ˈsoʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪt əv ˈsəʊl / (BrE)
a fillet cut of any of several flatfish known as sole, used as a lean, delicate food
flatfish fillet (sole), used in cooking

Examples

The chef served a delicious fillet of sole with lemon and capers.
At the restaurant, I ordered the grilled fillet of sole for my main course.
She pan-fried a fillet of sole to make a light, healthy dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sole - flatfish species valued as food fish
  • Synonym: fillet - a boneless strip of meat or fish

Etymology

From French 'filet' (a thread, strip) and 'sole' (a type of flatfish).
