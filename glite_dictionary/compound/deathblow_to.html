deathblow to

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθˌbloʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈdɛθˌbləʊ tʊ / (BrE)
an event or action that completely destroys, defeats, or puts an end to something
decisive end or destruction of something

Examples

The resignation of the CEO was the deathblow to the troubled company.
The new law delivered a deathblow to the outdated policy.
Losing their biggest sponsor proved to be the deathblow to the team's championship hopes.

Synset Examples

The scandal delivered a deathblow to the politician's career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final blow - a blow that ends something, often used figuratively
  • Synonym: coup de grâce - a final blow or shot given to kill a wounded person or animal, also used figuratively
  • Antonym: lifeline - something that gives help or support in a desperate situation
