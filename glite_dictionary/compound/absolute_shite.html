absolute shite

1. NOUN

/ ˈæb.sə.luːt ʃaɪt / (AmE), / ˈæb.sə.luːt ʃaɪt / (BrE)
something that is completely worthless, very poor in quality, or an utter failure
something extremely bad or worthless
informal vulgar british

Examples

The referee's decision was absolute shite according to the fans.
Our new phone system turned out to be absolute shite and broke down immediately.
He thought the latest episode was absolute shite, with terrible acting and a predictable plot.

Synset Examples

The performance was absolute shite from start to finish.
That movie was total shite, I regret watching it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolute rubbish - something completely worthless or of very poor quality
  • Synonym: absolute trash - something entirely useless or without value

Etymology

British and Irish slang; 'shite' from Old English 'scītan' (to defecate), parallel to 'shit'.
