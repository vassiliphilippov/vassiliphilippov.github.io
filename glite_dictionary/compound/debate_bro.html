debate bro

1. NOUN

/ dəˈbeɪt broʊ / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪt brəʊ / (BrE)
a person, typically a young man, who is excessively enthusiastic about debating and often engages in argumentative behavior, especially online
an excessively argumentative person (usually male)
slang

Examples

The debate bro couldn't handle losing the argument.
In every online forum, there's always a debate bro looking to stir up controversy.
She rolled her eyes as the debate bro tried to dominate the discussion with endless points.

Synset Examples

The debate bro couldn't handle losing the argument.
He's a typical debate bro, always trying to outsmart others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argumentative - given to expressing divergent or opposite views
  • Degree Higher: troll - someone who posts inflammatory or off-topic messages online

Etymology

From "debate" + slang term "bro" (short for "brother"), indicating a young man.
