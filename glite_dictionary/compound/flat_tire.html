flat tire

1. NOUN

/ flæt ˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / flæt ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
A pneumatic tire that has lost all or most of its air, making it unable to function properly.
a tire that has lost air

Examples

We had to pull over on the highway because of a flat tire.
She learned how to change a flat tire during her first driving lesson.
Driving on a flat tire can damage your wheel and is dangerous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puncture - a small hole that lets air out of a tire, often causing a flat tire
