golf range

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a place where people practice golf shots, especially driving the ball over long distances
place to practice golf shots

Examples

She practiced her swing at the golf range every Saturday morning.
They opened a new golf range just outside the city limits.
After work, he likes to relax at the golf range with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving range - an area for practicing long golf shots
