roof rabbit

1. NOUN

/ ɹuːf ˈræbɪt / (AmE), / ɹuːf ˈræbɪt / (BrE)
An informal or playful term for a rabbit, especially one living in urban areas or seen on rooftops; sometimes used humorously for similar small animals.
urban rabbit; playful small animal term
informal

Examples

We watched the roof rabbit hop gracefully from one ledge to another.
After sunset, a curious roof rabbit appeared outside my apartment window.
City dwellers often call these urban creatures roof rabbits as a joke.

Synset Examples

She laughed as the roof rabbit darted away from the noise.
We spotted a roof rabbit hopping across the lawn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit - a small furry animal with long ears and short tail
