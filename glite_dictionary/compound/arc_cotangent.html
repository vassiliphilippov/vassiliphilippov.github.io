arc cotangent

1. NOUN

/ ɑrk ˈkoʊtəˌdʒɛnt / (AmE), / ɑːk ˈkəʊtændʒənt / (BrE)
The inverse function of the cotangent; the angle whose cotangent is a given number.
inverse cotangent function

Examples

Calculating the arc cotangent of a number is often necessary in higher-level mathematics.
On the exam, you may be asked to find the arc cotangent of certain trigonometric values.
The graph of the arc cotangent function has a distinct shape compared to other inverse trigonometric functions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arccot - abbreviation for arc cotangent
  • Degree Lower: cotangent - trigonometric function; ratio of adjacent to opposite side
  • Synonym: arctangent - inverse function of the tangent

Etymology

From 'arc', meaning angle in trigonometric context, and 'cotangent', from Latin 'cotangens'.
