photographic paper

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪk ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪk ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
paper coated with a light-sensitive chemical, used for making photographic prints
light-sensitive paper for making photographs

Examples

He placed the photographic paper into the developer solution under a red safelight.
Old family portraits were carefully printed onto high-quality photographic paper.
With the rise of digital photography, fewer people use traditional photographic paper nowadays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photo paper - short form of 'photographic paper', used especially for digital prints
