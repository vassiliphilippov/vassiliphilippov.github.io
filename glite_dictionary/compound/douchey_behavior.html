douchey behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈduʃi bəˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈduːʃi bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
obnoxiously arrogant or rude conduct, often perceived as unnecessarily self-important or inconsiderate
arrogant or rude conduct
informal slang american

Examples

His douchey behavior at the meeting irritated everyone.
She couldn't stand his douchey behavior during the party.
People tend to avoid those who often display douchey behavior.

Synset Examples

His douchey behavior turned everyone away from him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • Synonym: rude - offensively impolite or ill-mannered

Etymology

From slang 'douchey', based on 'douche' (originally from French, literally meaning 'shower'), used pejoratively in American slang; attached to 'behavior'.
