bicycle traffic

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.sɪ.kəl ˈtræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.sɪ.kəl ˈtræf.ɪk / (BrE)
the movement or flow of bicycles along a road or path; the bicycles moving in an area at a given time
flow or presence of bicycles

Examples

There was heavy bicycle traffic on the main street during the morning commute.
City planners are working to improve safety for bicycle traffic at intersections.
After the new bike lane opened, bicycle traffic increased significantly in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic - vehicles or people moving along a route
