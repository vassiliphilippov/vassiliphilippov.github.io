Barbados-gooseberry vine

1. NOUN

/ bɑrˈbeɪdoʊs ˈɡuːsbɛri vaɪn / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdəʊs ˈɡuːsbəri vaɪn / (BrE)
a West Indian woody climbing plant with spiny stems, fragrant white flowers in clusters, and small yellow to orange fruits
West Indian spiny climbing plant

Examples

The Barbados-gooseberry vine grows rapidly in tropical gardens, twining over fences and trellises.
She admired the clusters of fragrant white flowers on the Barbados-gooseberry vine at the botanical garden.
Small yellow fruits hung from the spiny stems of the old Barbados-gooseberry vine by the wall.
