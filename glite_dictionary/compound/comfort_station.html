comfort station

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmfərt ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfət ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a public toilet; a restroom or facility with toilets available for general use
public toilet; restroom

Examples

The park map shows several comfort stations near the picnic areas.
We stopped at a comfort station during our road trip for a quick break.
The only public comfort station was closed for cleaning.

Related Terms
