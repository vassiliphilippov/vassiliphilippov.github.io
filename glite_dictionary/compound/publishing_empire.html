publishing empire

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ ˈɛmpaɪər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ ˈɛmpaɪə / (BrE)
A large conglomerate of publishing companies under single ownership or management.
group of publishing companies

Examples

He built a publishing empire that spanned magazines, newspapers, and digital platforms.
After the merger, the company became the world's largest publishing empire.
Rupert Murdoch's publishing empire includes some of the most influential newspapers in the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: media empire - a large group of media businesses owned by one person or organization
