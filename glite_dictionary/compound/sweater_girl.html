sweater girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtər ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈswɛtə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman with an attractive bust who wears tight-fitting sweaters, often for emphasis or style
woman in tight sweater emphasizing bust

Examples

Movie magazines in the 1940s often featured the latest sweater girl on their covers.
The look of a sweater girl became iconic during Hollywood's golden age.
She wore a tight cardigan, reminiscent of the classic sweater girl style.

Etymology

Originated in 1940s America, associated with actresses who popularized the style of tight sweaters.
