black and gold garden spider

1. NOUN

/ blæk ənd ɡoʊld ˈɡɑrdən ˈspaɪdər / (AmE), / blæk ənd ɡəʊld ˈɡɑːdən ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
a large North American spider (Argiope aurantia) with distinctive black and yellow markings, often found in gardens
a large North American spider with black and yellow markings

Examples

We spotted a black and gold garden spider weaving its web between the tomato plants.
The black and gold garden spider is harmless to humans but can look quite startling up close.
Every summer, a black and gold garden spider builds its zigzag web near our back porch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Argiope aurantia - the scientific (Latin) name for the black and gold garden spider
