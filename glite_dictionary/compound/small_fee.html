small fee

1. NOUN

/ smɔl fi / (AmE), / smɔːl fiː / (BrE)
a minor charge or payment required for a service or privilege
a minor charge for a service

Examples

We charge a small fee for shipping and handling.
There is a small fee to access the museum's special exhibition.
A small fee applies if you want to upgrade your seat.

Synset Examples

They charged a small fee for the service.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: large fee - a significant or major charge for a service
