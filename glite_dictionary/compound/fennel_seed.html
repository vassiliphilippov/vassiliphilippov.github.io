fennel seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnəl siːd / (AmE), / ˈfɛnəl siːd / (BrE)
the small, aromatic seeds of the fennel plant, used as a spice or flavoring in cooking and medicine
aromatic spice seeds from the fennel plant

Examples

The recipe calls for a teaspoon of fennel seed to enhance the flavor of the sauce.
Some herbal teas include fennel seed for its soothing properties.
She toasted fennel seed before grinding it to add to the bread dough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aniseed - the seed of the anise plant, with a similar licorice-like flavor
