total dorkazoid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈdɔrkəˌzɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈdɔːkəzɔɪd / (BrE)
An informal and humorous term for a person who is socially awkward or unfashionable.
socially awkward, unfashionable person (slang)
informal slang

Examples

My brother can be a total dorkazoid at family parties, always telling weird jokes.
Despite being a total dorkazoid in high school, he eventually found his crowd in college.
She called herself a total dorkazoid after tripping over her own backpack in class.

Synset Examples

He's a total dorkazoid when it comes to fashion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dork - a socially awkward or unfashionable person
  • Synonym: nerd - someone highly intellectual but socially awkward
  • Antonym: cool - fashionable or socially adept person

Etymology

Formed from 'dork' (slang for 'socially awkward person') plus the playful suffix '-azoid'; 'total' used for emphasis.
