largemouthed bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑrdʒˌmaʊθt bæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒˌmaʊθt bæs / (BrE)
a large North American freshwater fish, noted for its broad mouth, widely sought after as a game fish
a large North American freshwater game fish

Examples

The lake is famous for its largemouthed bass that attract anglers from across the country.
He caught a record-breaking largemouthed bass during the annual fishing tournament.
Many wildlife programs focus on the habitat of the largemouthed bass due to its ecological importance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smallmouthed bass - a related species of bass with a smaller mouth
