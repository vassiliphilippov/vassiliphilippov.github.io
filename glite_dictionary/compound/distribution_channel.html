distribution channel

1. NOUN

/ dɪs.trɪˈbju.ʃən ˈtʃæn.əl / (AmE), / dɪs.trɪˈbjuː.ʃən ˈtʃæn.əl / (BrE)
a method or route for making a company's products available to customers, either directly or through intermediaries such as wholesalers or retailers
route or method for getting products to customers

Examples

The company expanded its distribution channels to include both online retailers and traditional stores.
Choosing the right distribution channel can significantly affect a product's market success.
Direct sales are often considered the most efficient distribution channel for startup businesses.

Synset Examples

possible distribution channels are wholesalers or small retailers or retail chains or direct mailers or your own stores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales channel - a means of selling a product to customers
