soda can

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə kæn / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə kæn / (BrE)
a metal container designed to hold a single serving of a carbonated soft drink
metal container for soft drinks

Examples

She opened a soda can and took a refreshing sip.
Please recycle your empty soda can after you finish.
The picnic table was covered with plates, napkins, and a few soda cans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft drink can - a can for containing a non-alcoholic, carbonated beverage
