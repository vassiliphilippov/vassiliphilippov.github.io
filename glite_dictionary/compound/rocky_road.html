rocky road

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑki roʊd / (AmE), / ˈrɒki rəʊd / (BrE)
a situation or period that is full of difficulties or challenges
difficult or challenging situation

Examples

After the merger, the company faced a rocky road as it reorganized.
Their marriage has been a rocky road, but they are committed to making it work.
Starting a new business is often a rocky road with many unexpected challenges.

Synset Examples

Her journey to success was a long, rocky road.
They faced a rocky road before achieving their dreams.
Life can be a rocky road filled with challenges.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth sailing - an easy or trouble-free situation
