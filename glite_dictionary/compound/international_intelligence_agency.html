international intelligence agency

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
an intelligence agency that operates in or belongs to a country other than the United States
foreign or non-U.S. intelligence agency

Examples

The report mentioned an international intelligence agency had warned about possible terrorist threats.
He used to work for an international intelligence agency before starting his own consulting firm.
Several countries' representatives met to coordinate efforts between their international intelligence agencies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intelligence agency - an organization responsible for obtaining and analyzing information, especially secret information, for government or military purposes
  • Antonym: domestic intelligence agency - an intelligence agency that operates mainly within its own country
