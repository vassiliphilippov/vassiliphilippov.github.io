fire-breathing skill

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌbriðɪŋ skɪl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌbriːðɪŋ skɪl / (BrE)
the ability or art of creating a dramatic flame by breathing out fuel and igniting it, typically as a performance
ability to breathe fire as a performance

Examples

He spent years perfecting his fire-breathing skill before joining the circus troupe.
Learning fire-breathing skill requires patience, safety precautions, and expert guidance.
Her impressive fire-breathing skill amazed the audience during the festival.

Synset Examples

He practiced his fire-breathing skills every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire eater - a performer who breathes or consumes fire as entertainment
  • Degree Lower: juggling - the act of keeping several objects in motion in the air as a performance
