laughing jackass

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæfɪŋ ˈdʒækˌæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ ˈdʒækˌæs / (BrE)
an almost extinct owl of New Zealand, known for its distinctive call
almost extinct owl of New Zealand

Examples

The laughing jackass was once common in the forests of New Zealand.
Researchers are working to save the laughing jackass from extinction.
Very few people today have ever seen a laughing jackass in the wild.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlæfɪŋ ˈdʒækˌæs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋ ˈdʒækˌæs / (BrE)
an Australian kingfisher, also known as the laughing kookaburra, notable for its loud, cackling call
Australian kingfisher with a cackling call

Examples

The laughing jackass is famous for its distinctive call, which sounds like loud, raucous laughter.
We spotted a laughing jackass perched on a eucalyptus branch while hiking in Australia.
Early settlers often woke to the sound of the laughing jackass at dawn.
