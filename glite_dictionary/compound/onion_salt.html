onion salt

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən sɒlt / (BrE)
a condiment made from ground dried onion mixed with salt, used to flavor food
seasoned salt with onion flavor

Examples

I sprinkled some onion salt on the fries for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of onion salt.
She always keeps a shaker of onion salt in her spice rack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garlic salt - a seasoning made of ground garlic and salt
