herbal concoction

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜrbəl kənˈkɑkʃən / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl kənˈkɒkʃən / (BrE)
a mixture made from herbs, usually prepared for medicinal purposes or as a remedy
mixture of herbs for medicine

Examples

The healer prepared a special herbal concoction to soothe my sore throat.
Drinking a warm herbal concoction before bed can help you relax.
They sold a variety of herbal concoctions at the health food store.

Synset Examples

The herbal concoction promised to boost immunity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbal remedy - a treatment using herbs instead of or alongside conventional medicine
  • Synonym: potion - a liquid with healing, magical, or poisonous properties, especially in folklore
