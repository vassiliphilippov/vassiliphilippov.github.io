rose bed

1. NOUN

/ roʊz bɛd / (AmE), / rəʊz bɛd / (BrE)
an area of soil in a garden, especially prepared and planted with rose bushes
garden area planted with roses

Examples

Her favorite part of the garden was the rose bed near the fountain.
Every spring, he carefully pruned the bushes in the rose bed.
They admired the vibrant colors in the newly planted rose bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower bed - a prepared area of ground for growing flowers
