Tangier pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈtændʒɪr piː / (AmE), / ˈtændʒɪə piː / (BrE)
A North African annual plant similar to the sweet pea, with showy but scentless flowers.
North African annual plant with showy flowers

Examples

The Tangier pea is prized for its vibrant, odorless blooms in Mediterranean gardens.
Unlike the sweet pea, the Tangier pea does not have a fragrant scent.
Gardeners appreciate the Tangier pea because it adds a splash of color in spring.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sweet pea - a climbing plant with fragrant flowers, related species
