West Bank

1. PROPN

/ wɛst bæŋk / (AmE), / wɛst bæŋk / (BrE)
an area of land in the Middle East, situated on the west side of the Jordan River between Israel and Jordan, largely inhabited by Palestinians
territory west of the Jordan River

Examples

The West Bank has been at the center of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict for decades.
International aid organizations operate in both Gaza and the West Bank.
He traveled to the West Bank to report on local politics and daily life.
