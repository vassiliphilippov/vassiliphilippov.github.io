cancer survivor

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænsɚ səˈvaɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkænsə səˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who has recovered from or is living after cancer
someone who has survived cancer

Examples

As a cancer survivor, she now works to support others facing the disease.
The conference featured stories from several cancer survivors of all ages.
He proudly calls himself a cancer survivor after battling illness for two years.

Synset Examples

He is a cancer survivor who shares his story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: survivor - a person who continues to live despite hardship or danger
