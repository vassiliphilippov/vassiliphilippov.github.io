regional independence

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːdʒənl ˌɪndəˈpɛndəns / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəl ˌɪndɪˈpendəns / (BrE)
the condition or demand of a region having political autonomy or self-government separate from a central authority
political autonomy of a specific region

Examples

The debate over regional independence has intensified in recent years across several European countries.
Many activists organized rallies in support of regional independence for the province.
Some fear that regional independence could lead to economic instability in the nation.

Synset Examples

As a franquist, he advocated for regional independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autonomy - the right or condition of self-government
  • Synonym: secession - the action of withdrawing formally from a federation or body, especially a political state
