chess move

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛs muːv / (AmE), / tʃɛs muːv / (BrE)
an action in which a chess piece is moved according to the rules of the game
an action of moving a chess piece

Examples

He planned his next chess move carefully to avoid losing his queen.
A single brilliant chess move turned the game in her favor.
Every chess move must follow strict rules to be considered valid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: move - an action of changing the position of something
