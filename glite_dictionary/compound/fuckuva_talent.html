fuckuva talent

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌkəvə ˈtælənt / (AmE), / ˈfʌkəvə ˈtælənt / (BrE)
An exceptionally impressive or extraordinary talent; used for strong emphasis, often in informal or vulgar speech.
extraordinary talent (emphatic, vulgar)
informal vulgar

Examples

She has a fuckuva talent for making people laugh, everyone in the room was in stitches.
That's a fuckuva talent you've got there—don't waste it!
He showed a fuckuva talent for playing the guitar at such a young age.
