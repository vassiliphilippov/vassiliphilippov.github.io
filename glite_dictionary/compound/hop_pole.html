hop pole

1. NOUN

/ hɑp poʊl / (AmE), / hɒp pəʊl / (BrE)
a tall pole used to support the wires on which hop plants are trained to grow
pole supporting hop plants

Examples

During the spring, workers set up each hop pole by hand throughout the field.
Old photographs show rows of hop poles standing tall above the growing plants.
A sturdy hop pole is essential for supporting heavy vines as the hops mature.
