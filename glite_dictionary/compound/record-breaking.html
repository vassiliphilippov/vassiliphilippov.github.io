record-breaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛkərd ˌbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd ˌbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Surpassing any previously established record; better, higher, or greater than all previous examples.
surpassing any previous record

Examples

The athlete achieved a record-breaking time in the 100-meter dash.
Last year saw a record-breaking number of visitors to the museum.
Temperatures reached a record-breaking high during the summer heatwave.

Synset Examples

a record-breaking high jump
record-breaking crowds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record-setting - establishing a new record
  • Synonym: unprecedented - never done or known before
