deep dive

1. NOUN

/ diːp daɪv / (AmE), / diːp daɪv / (BrE)
a thorough and detailed examination or analysis of a subject or topic
thorough examination or analysis

Examples

Her report includes a deep dive into the company’s financial history.
The course will begin with a deep dive into modern data analytics.
We decided to take a deep dive into the software’s security features.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: overview - a general summary or review of a subject
