Great Wall

1. PROPN

/ ɡreɪt ˈwɔl / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈwɔːl / (BrE)
a massive ancient fortification built across northern China, originally constructed to protect against invasions.
ancient Chinese defensive wall

Examples

The Great Wall is one of the most famous landmarks in the world.
She visited the Great Wall during her trip to China last year.
Satellite images show the remarkable extent of the Great Wall stretching across the countryside.
