Siege of Lucknow

1. NOUN

/ sidʒ əv ləkˈnaʊ / (AmE), / siːdʒ əv lʌkˈnaʊ / (BrE)
the prolonged military blockade and attack on British residents and troops in Lucknow by Indian insurgents during the Indian Mutiny of 1857
historic 1857 siege in Lucknow, India

Examples

The Siege of Lucknow was one of the most dramatic events of the Indian Mutiny.
Many civilians and soldiers suffered hardships during the Siege of Lucknow in 1857.
The Siege of Lucknow is frequently mentioned in British colonial history.

Etymology

From 'siege' (military blockade) and 'Lucknow', the Indian city; refers to a key event of the 1857 Indian Mutiny.
