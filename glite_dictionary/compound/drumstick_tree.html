drumstick tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌmˌstɪk tri / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌstɪk triː / (BrE)
a tropical tree (Moringa oleifera) with feathery leaves, fragrant yellowish flowers in drooping clusters, and long seed pods, commonly used in cooking and traditional medicine
tropical tree with edible pods and medicinal uses

Examples

The drumstick tree is prized in many regions for its nutritious leaves and pods.
She planted a drumstick tree in her backyard to use the pods in curries.
Extracts from the drumstick tree are often used in traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Moringa - genus of trees and shrubs including Moringa oleifera

Etymology

Named for its seed pods, which resemble drumsticks.
