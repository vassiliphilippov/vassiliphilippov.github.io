staghorn fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæɡˌhɔrn fɝn / (AmE), / ˈstæɡˌhɔːn fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical fern of the genus Platycerium, known for its large, flat, lobed fronds that resemble stag antlers
tropical fern with antler-like fronds

Examples

The staghorn fern is often mounted on wood and displayed as a piece of living art.
She watered her staghorn fern every week to keep it healthy in the humid environment.
Their greenhouse features a stunning collection of staghorn ferns from Southeast Asia.

Etymology

Named for the resemblance of its fronds to the antlers (horns) of a stag (male deer).
