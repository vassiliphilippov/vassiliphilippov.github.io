sheet anchor

1. NOUN

/ ʃit ˈæŋkɚ / (AmE), / ʃiːt ˈæŋkə / (BrE)
a spare anchor kept onboard a ship for use in emergencies
emergency spare anchor on a ship

Examples

The crew deployed the sheet anchor after the main anchor was lost in the storm.
Every well-equipped ship carries a sheet anchor for emergencies at sea.
In the old days, a sheet anchor was a vital safeguard for long ocean voyages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchor - a heavy object used to moor a vessel
