Gladstone bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlædˌstoʊn ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡlæd.stən ˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a large, rigid leather traveling bag with a hinged opening at the top
large, stiff leather traveling bag

Examples

He packed his clothes neatly into a Gladstone bag before leaving for the train station.
The vintage Gladstone bag was handed down from my grandfather.
Doctors often carried their instruments in a Gladstone bag during house calls in the 19th century.

Etymology

Named after William Ewart Gladstone (1809–1898), British Prime Minister.
