charlotte russe

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrˈloʊt ˈrus / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈlɒt ˈruːs / (BrE)
A dessert made of ladyfingers lined around a mold and filled with Bavarian cream.
dessert of ladyfingers and Bavarian cream

Examples

For dessert, she served a homemade charlotte russe topped with fresh berries.
The bakery's signature charlotte russe is both elegant and delicious.
He learned to prepare a classic charlotte russe in his French pastry class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charlotte - a dessert with a bread or cake outer layer and fruit or cream filling

Etymology

From French Charlotte (the name of the dessert) + russe (Russian), reflecting its supposed creation for a Russian dignitary.
