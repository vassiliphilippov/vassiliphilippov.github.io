day labourer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌleɪbərər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌleɪbərə / (BrE)
a person who works for daily wages, usually engaged in manual or unskilled labor
a manual worker paid by the day

Examples

Many day labourers gather at the market each morning, hoping to find construction work.
The government is planning support programs for day labourers affected by the lockdown.
As a day labourer, he never knows if there will be work for him tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
