male monarch

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈmɑːnɑrk / (AmE), / meɪl ˈmɒnək / (BrE)
A male sovereign who rules a kingdom; a king.
male ruler of a kingdom

Examples

The male monarch presided over the ceremony with great dignity.
Throughout history, many countries have been governed by a male monarch.
After the long reign of the previous male monarch, the succession passed to his daughter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: queen - female monarch; a woman who rules a kingdom
  • Synonym: king - male monarch; a man who rules a kingdom
