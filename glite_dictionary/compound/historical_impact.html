historical impact

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈstɔrɪkəl ˈɪmpækt / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl ˈɪmpækt / (BrE)
the significant and lasting effect that an event, action, or phenomenon has on history or historical development
significant effect on history

Examples

The historical impact of the Civil Rights Movement continues to influence society today.
Many historians debate the historical impact of technological advances on human progress.
Her research examines the historical impact of colonial rule in Africa.

Synset Examples

They discussed the historical impact of the atomic bomb on world politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: historical significance - the importance assigned to an event or person in history
