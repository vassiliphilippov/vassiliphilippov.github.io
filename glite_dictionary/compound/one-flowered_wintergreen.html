one-flowered wintergreen

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈflaʊɚd ˈwɪntɚˌgrin / (AmE), / wʌn ˈflaʊəd ˈwɪntəˌɡriːn / (BrE)
a delicate evergreen herb of northern temperate regions, bearing a single white flower at the tip of the stem; sometimes classified in the genus Pyrola
delicate evergreen herb with a single white flower

Examples

The one-flowered wintergreen can be found growing in moist, shady forests throughout the northern hemisphere.
Botanists admire the solitary white blossom of the one-flowered wintergreen each spring.
She photographed a rare patch of one-flowered wintergreen during her hike in the Canadian woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wintergreen - any plant with aromatic leaves of the genus Gaultheria or Pyrola
