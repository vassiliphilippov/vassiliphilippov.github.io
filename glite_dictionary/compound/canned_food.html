canned food

1. NOUN

/ kænd fuːd / (AmE), / kænd fuːd / (BrE)
Food that has been preserved and sealed in a can to extend its shelf life.
food preserved in a can

Examples

She opened a can of canned food for a quick and easy dinner.
After the hurricane, they relied on canned food for several days.
The supermarket has an entire aisle dedicated to canned food products.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fresh food - food that is not preserved or processed
  • Synonym: tinned food - food preserved in a can (British term)
