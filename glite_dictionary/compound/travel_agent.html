travel agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company that arranges tickets, accommodation, and transport for people going on holiday or making journeys
person who arranges trips and tours

Examples

She went to the travel agent to book her summer vacation.
A good travel agent can save you money on flights and hotels.
Many people now book online instead of using a travel agent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tour operator - a person or company that organizes and sells package holidays or tours
