tea maker

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / tiː ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
a small, usually metal, container or spoon with a perforated cover used to hold tea leaves while brewing in hot water
perforated utensil for brewing loose tea

Examples

I use a tea maker to brew my favorite loose-leaf blends every morning.
She bought a new tea maker shaped like a small, decorative spoon with holes.
Instead of tea bags, he prefers a tea maker for a fresher taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea infuser - a device for holding loose tea leaves while steeping
