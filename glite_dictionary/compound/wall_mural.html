wall mural

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈmjʊərəl / (BrE)
A large decorative artwork, often painted or printed, applied directly to a wall to enhance its appearance.
decorative artwork applied to a wall

Examples

The living room was transformed by a striking wall mural depicting a forest scene.
They hired an artist to paint a custom wall mural in the nursery.
A colorful wall mural can make a small space feel more vibrant and dynamic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mural - A painting or artwork applied directly to a wall or ceiling.
