spitting snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪtɪŋ sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈspɪtɪŋ sneɪk / (BrE)
a highly venomous snake of southern Africa that can eject venom toward its target, often as a defense mechanism
venomous snake that spits venom

Examples

The spitting snake can accurately aim its venom at the eyes of a threat from several feet away.
Conservationists observed a spitting snake exhibiting its unique defensive behavior in the wild.
Children were warned not to approach the spitting snake found near the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spitting cobra - a venomous African or Asian cobra able to eject venom at a target
