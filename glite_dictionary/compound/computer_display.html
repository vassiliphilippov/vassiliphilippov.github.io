computer display

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjutər dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
A screen that shows the visual output from a computer to the user.
screen for viewing a computer's output

Examples

The new computer display has a higher resolution and brighter colors than the old one.
I connected my laptop to a larger computer display for easier multitasking.
Touchscreen computer displays are becoming more popular in schools and offices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - an electronic device for displaying images and text from a computer
