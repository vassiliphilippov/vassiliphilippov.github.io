name tag

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪm tæɡ / (AmE), / ˈneɪm taɡ / (BrE)
a small label or badge displaying the name of the person wearing it, usually for identification purposes
label showing a person's name

Examples

At the conference, everyone was given a name tag to wear.
Please remember to put on your name tag before entering the event hall.
Her name tag had her full name and department printed clearly.
