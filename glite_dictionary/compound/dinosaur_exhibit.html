dinosaur exhibit

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr ɪgˈzɪbɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəˌsɔː ɪgˈzɪbɪt / (BrE)
an exhibition or display featuring models, fossils, or reconstructions of dinosaurs, usually in a museum setting
exhibition featuring dinosaurs

Examples

The museum unveiled a new dinosaur exhibit that attracted thousands of visitors over the weekend.
Children were amazed by the life-sized models in the dinosaur exhibit.
The dinosaur exhibit features interactive activities and educational displays for all ages.

Synset Examples

The natural history museum has a fascinating dinosaur exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinosaur display - an arrangement or presentation of dinosaur-related items or models
