zig-zag fold

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ foʊld / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡ fəʊld / (BrE)
a pattern of parallel folds made alternately in opposite directions, creating a series of sharp angles like the letter Z
series of alternating sharp-angle folds

Examples

He carefully created a zig-zag fold in the brochure to make it compact and easy to carry.
The artist used a zig-zag fold technique to achieve a three-dimensional effect in her paper sculpture.
A zig-zag fold can be seen in the way the map opens and closes along its creases.

Synset Examples

Seen from above, concertina folds resemble a Z or M or series of zigs and zags.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concertina fold - a similar accordion-like folding pattern
