lanjiao gesture

1. NOUN

/ lænˈdʒjaʊ ˈdʒɛstʃər / (AmE), / lanˈdʒaʊ ˈdʒɛstə / (BrE)
A Chinese slang gesture, typically considered offensive, used to signify annoyance or frustration.
Chinese gesture expressing annoyance
offensive slang

Examples

He made a lanjiao gesture to express his frustration.
In online videos, you might see someone perform the lanjiao gesture as an insult.
Some people find the lanjiao gesture deeply disrespectful and offensive.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese 'lánjiāo' (literally 'blue penis'), slang in several Chinese dialects.
