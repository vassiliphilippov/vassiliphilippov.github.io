resplendent trogon

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsplɛndənt ˈtroʊɡɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈsplɛndənt ˈtrəʊɡən / (BrE)
a very rare and brightly colored bird native to Central America, known as the national bird of Guatemala
brightly colored Central American bird

Examples

The resplendent trogon is admired for its vivid green and red plumage.
Many birdwatchers travel to Guatemala hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive resplendent trogon.
The image of the resplendent trogon appears on the national emblem of Guatemala.

Etymology

From Latin 'resplendens' (shining brilliantly) and Spanish 'trogón' (a kind of bird, from Greek 'trōgōn', gnawer).
