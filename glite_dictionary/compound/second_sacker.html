second sacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈsækər / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈsakə / (BrE)
(Baseball) The player who plays the position of second base.
baseball player at second base

Examples

The team's second sacker made a spectacular diving catch to end the inning.
As a rookie second sacker, he quickly impressed everyone with his range and arm.
The veteran second sacker has played over a thousand games at his position.

Related Terms
