cooking pan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ pan / (BrE)
a wide, shallow metal vessel used for cooking food, typically on a stovetop
wide metal vessel for cooking

Examples

She placed the vegetables in the cooking pan and turned on the stove.
Every kitchen should have at least one good cooking pan for daily meals.
After washing the cooking pan, he hung it back on the rack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frying pan - a shallow pan used for frying food
  • Synonym: saucepan - a deep cooking pan with a handle and often a lid
