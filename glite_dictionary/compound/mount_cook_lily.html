Mount Cook lily

1. NOUN

/ maʊnt kʊk ˈlɪli / (AmE), / maʊnt kʊk ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a perennial plant of New Zealand with large, showy white flowers, found especially in alpine regions
New Zealand perennial with white flowers

Examples

The Mount Cook lily is renowned for its large and striking white blossoms.
While hiking in New Zealand, I photographed a blooming Mount Cook lily near the mountain trails.
Botanists study the Mount Cook lily for its adaptability to alpine environments.

Etymology

Named after Mount Cook, New Zealand's highest peak, where the plant is often found.
