callout for attention

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlˌaʊt fər əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlˌaʊt fə əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
a request or exclamation made to prompt students or listeners to focus or pay attention
request for listeners to focus

Examples

The teacher's callout for attention was met with silence.
She used a quick callout for attention before starting the lesson.
During assemblies, a simple callout for attention can help quiet a noisy crowd.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention-getter - something used to attract attention
