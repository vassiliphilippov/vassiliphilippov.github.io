snow lily

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a dogtooth violet found in western North America, notable for its bright yellow flowers; Erythronium grandiflorum
a yellow-flowered wildflower of western North America

Examples

We saw a cluster of snow lilies blooming near the creek in late spring.
The snow lily is known for its delicate yellow petals and slender leaves.
Hikers often take photographs of the beautiful snow lily in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogtooth violet - a plant of the genus Erythronium, related wildflower with similar appearance
