copper glance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːpɚ ɡlæns / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
a heavy, grey mineral consisting mainly of copper(I) sulfide, used as an ore of copper
a grey mineral copper ore (chalcocite)

Examples

The geologists discovered a vein of copper glance in the mountain.
Pure copper glance is a valuable source of copper for the mining industry.
Early miners often recognized copper glance by its metallic luster and grey color.

Related Terms
