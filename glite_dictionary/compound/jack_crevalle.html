jack crevalle

1. NOUN

/ dʒæk krəˈvæli / (AmE), / dʒæk krəˈvæli / (BrE)
a large game fish of the western Atlantic and Gulf of Mexico, also known as the common jack (Caranx hippos)
a large Atlantic and Gulf game fish

Examples

Fishermen in Florida often catch jack crevalle near the shoreline.
The jack crevalle is known for its strength and aggressive strikes when hooked.
Many anglers consider the jack crevalle a challenging sport fish due to its fighting ability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trevally - any of several fishes in the genus Caranx, related to the jack crevalle
