practical application

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræk.tɪ.kəl ˌæp.lɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪ.kəl ˌæp.lɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of putting something such as a theory or idea into effective use in real situations
putting something to use in practice

Examples

The practical application of this technology could transform everyday life.
She enjoys studying science, but she's most interested in its practical application.
The course emphasizes both theory and practical application through hands-on projects.

Synset Examples

he advocated the application of statistics to the problem
a novel application of electronics to medical diagnosis

  • Synonym: application - the act of putting something to use
