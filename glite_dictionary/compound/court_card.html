court card

1. NOUN

/ kɔrt kɑrd / (AmE), / kɔːt kɑːd / (BrE)
a playing card in a deck that depicts a person, such as a king, queen, or jack
playing card showing a face (king, queen, or jack)

Examples

You must play a court card to win this round.
She collected all the court cards from her favorite deck.
The king is considered the highest court card in many games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face card - a playing card with a king, queen, or jack
