clock time

1. NOUN

/ klɑk taɪm / (AmE), / klɒk taɪm / (BrE)
the time shown by a clock, as opposed to time measured or experienced otherwise
time as shown on a clock

Examples

According to the clock time, the meeting starts at 3:00 PM sharp.
The results were adjusted for clock time to ensure accuracy across all locations.
His sense of clock time was disrupted after traveling across several time zones.

Synset Examples

do you know what time it is?
the time is 10 o'clock

Related Terms

  • Antonym: solar time - time measured by the position of the sun
  • Degree Higher: real time - actual time during which a process occurs
