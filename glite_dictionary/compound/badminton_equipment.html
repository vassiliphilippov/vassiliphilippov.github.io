badminton equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbædˌmɪtn̩ ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈbædmɪntən ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the specialized equipment, such as rackets, shuttlecocks, and nets, used for playing badminton
gear used for playing badminton

Examples

Players are required to bring their own badminton equipment to the tournament.
The school invested in new badminton equipment, including rackets and nets, for the sports department.
Proper care of your badminton equipment ensures better performance and longer-lasting gear.
