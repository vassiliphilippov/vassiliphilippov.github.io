budget constraint

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdʒɪt kənˌstreɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt kənˌstreɪnt / (BrE)
a limit on the amount of money that can be spent, often because of available financial resources
limit on money that can be spent

Examples

The team had to cancel the project due to a budget constraint.
Even with a tight budget constraint, they managed to organize the event successfully.
Many families face at least one budget constraint when planning their summer vacation.

Synset Examples

We must take into account the budget constraints.
