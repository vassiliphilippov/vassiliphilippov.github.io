Lisu language

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːsuː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈliːsuː ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Lisu people, a Loloish language spoken in parts of Southwest China and neighboring regions
language of the Lisu people in Southwest China

Examples

The Lisu language has its own unique writing system developed in the early 20th century.
Many Lisu people are bilingual, speaking both Chinese and the Lisu language.
Researchers have published a dictionary of the Lisu language for linguistic studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yi language - a related Loloish language of Southwest China

Etymology

Lisu is the name of the ethnic group; 'language' from Middle English 'langage', from Old French, from Latin 'lingua'.
