canvas tent

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.vəs tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkæn.vəs tɛnt / (BrE)
a tent made from strong, heavy cloth called canvas, used especially for camping or outdoor shelter
tent made of canvas fabric

Examples

We set up our canvas tent by the lake and enjoyed the fresh air.
The old canvas tent provided shelter during the sudden rainstorm.
They chose a canvas tent for their camping trip because it is durable and weather-resistant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tent - a portable shelter made of cloth, supported by poles and ropes
