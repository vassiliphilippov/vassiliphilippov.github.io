golden years

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˌjɪrz / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˌjɪəz / (BrE)
the period of life after retirement, often viewed as a time of relaxation and enjoyment
time after retirement

Examples

Many people look forward to their golden years as a time to travel and pursue hobbies.
After retiring, she spent her golden years volunteering at the local library.
They moved to a quieter town to enjoy their golden years in peace.
