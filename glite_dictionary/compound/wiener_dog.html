wiener dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːnər dɔːɡ / (AmE), / ˈwiːnə dɒɡ / (BrE)
An informal term for a dachshund, a small dog breed with a long body and short legs.
informal term for dachshund
informal

Examples

Her wiener dog likes to burrow under blankets when it sleeps.
They dressed their wiener dog in a tiny sweater for the winter.
A wiener dog won first prize at the local pet costume contest.

Synset Examples

She took her wiener dog for a walk in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dachshund - A small breed of dog with a long body and short legs

Etymology

Refers to the resemblance of the dachshund to a wiener sausage (hot dog), 'wiener' from Vienna sausage.
