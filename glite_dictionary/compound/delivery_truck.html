delivery truck

1. NOUN

/ dəˈlɪvəri trʌk / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri trʌk / (BrE)
a vehicle, typically a van or small truck, designed for transporting goods or services to customers
vehicle for delivering goods to customers

Examples

A delivery truck pulled up in front of the bakery early this morning.
The company invested in a new fleet of delivery trucks to improve service efficiency.
The furniture was transported directly to her house in a large delivery truck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: van - a medium-sized vehicle for transporting goods or people
