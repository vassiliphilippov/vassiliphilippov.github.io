section gang

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃən ˌɡæŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃən ˌɡæŋ / (BrE)
a group of workers responsible for maintaining a particular section of a railway line
railroad maintenance work crew

Examples

The section gang started repairs on the damaged rails early in the morning.
For years, the section gang kept this stretch of track in excellent condition.
During the summer, my uncle worked with a section gang along the old railway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gang - a group of people working together
