slide bolt

1. NOUN

/ slaɪd boʊlt / (AmE), / slaɪd bəʊlt / (BrE)
A type of lock with a horizontal metal bar that is moved manually to secure a door or gate.
manually operated horizontal locking bar

Examples

The shed door was secured with a slide bolt to keep it from opening in the wind.
He installed a new slide bolt on the garden gate for extra security.
Before leaving the cottage, she made sure the slide bolt was fastened tightly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadbolt - a lock bolt that is moved by turning a knob or key, not spring-actuated
