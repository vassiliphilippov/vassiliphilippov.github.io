Lake Clark National Park

1. PROPN

/ leɪk klɑrk næʃəˌnæl pɑrk / (AmE), / leɪk klɑːk næʃəˌnal pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Alaska known for its natural scenery and significant archeological sites related to Eskimo and Athapaskan cultures
national park in Alaska, USA

Examples

Many visitors travel to Lake Clark National Park to experience Alaska's pristine wilderness.
Researchers found ancient tools at Lake Clark National Park that shed light on indigenous cultures.
The breathtaking landscapes of Lake Clark National Park attract photographers from around the world.
