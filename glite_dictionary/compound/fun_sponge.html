fun sponge

1. NOUN

/ fʌn spʌndʒ / (AmE), / fʌn spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a person who spoils the enjoyment of others; someone who dampens fun or excitement
someone who spoils others' fun
informal

Examples

Don't be such a fun sponge—let's dance and have a good time!
Everyone stopped laughing when the fun sponge walked into the room.
His constant complaining made him the fun sponge of the group.

Synset Examples

His negativity makes him a real fun sponge.
She always acts like a fun sponge at parties.
Don't be a fun sponge; join us for karaoke!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment of others
  • Synonym: party pooper - someone who refuses to join in the fun at a social gathering
