bubble gum

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl ˌɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ˌɡʌm / (BrE)
A type of chewing gum that is designed to be blown into bubbles.
chewing gum for blowing bubbles

Examples

He gave the children each a piece of bubble gum as a treat.
She blew a huge bubble with her bubble gum during class and it popped loudly.
The store sells a variety of flavors of bubble gum near the checkout counter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chewing gum - sweetened gum for chewing
