high stool

1. NOUN

/ haɪ stuːl / (AmE), / haɪ stʃuːl / (BrE)
a tall seat, typically without a back or with minimal support, often used at bars or kitchen counters
tall seat for a bar or counter

Examples

She sat on a high stool at the kitchen island while sipping her coffee.
The cafe features high stools along the window for people watching.
He nearly lost his balance while climbing onto the high stool at the bar.

Synset Examples

The bar had high stools for patrons to enjoy their drinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barstool - a tall stool used at a bar
  • Degree Lower: stool - a simple seat without a back or arms
