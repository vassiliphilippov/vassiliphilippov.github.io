evil spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvəl ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːvəl ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A supernatural being or force believed to cause harm, misfortune, or evil.
malevolent supernatural being

Examples

Some cultures believe that an evil spirit can cause illness or misfortune.
The priest performed a ritual to drive out the evil spirit from the old house.
She said she felt the presence of an evil spirit in the abandoned building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demon - An evil supernatural being or spirit
  • Synonym: ghost - The soul of a dead person, believed to appear to the living
  • Antonym: good spirit - A supernatural being associated with virtue or positivity
