George Burns

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ bɝːnz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ bɜːnz / (BrE)
(1896–1996) A United States comedian, actor, and writer known for his long career in vaudeville, radio, film, and television, often performing with his wife, Gracie Allen.
American comedian and actor (1896–1996)

Examples

George Burns was famous for his witty one-liners and impeccable comedic timing.
The old movie featured George Burns in one of his most memorable roles.
Many fans remember George Burns for his partnership with Gracie Allen.
