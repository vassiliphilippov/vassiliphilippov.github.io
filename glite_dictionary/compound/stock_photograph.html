stock photograph

1. NOUN

/ stɑk ˈfoʊ.t̬ə.græf / (AmE), / stɒk ˈfəʊ.tə.grɑːf / (BrE)
a photograph that already exists and is licensed for specific use, often in publications or advertising
existing photo licensed for use

Examples

The magazine chose a stock photograph for its cover instead of commissioning a new image.
You can purchase a stock photograph online for your website project.
Many advertisements rely on a stock photograph to convey the desired message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock photo - photograph available for licensing; alternative term for stock photograph
