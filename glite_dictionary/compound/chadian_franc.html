Chadian franc

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃædiən fræŋk / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪdiən fræŋk / (BrE)
the standard unit of currency used in Chad
currency of Chad

Examples

The exchange rate for the Chadian franc has remained stable this year.
She converted her dollars into Chadian francs before traveling to N'Djamena.
Most local purchases in Chad are made using the Chadian franc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CFA franc - currency used by several central African countries including Chad
