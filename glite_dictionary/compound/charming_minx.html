charming minx

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑrmɪŋ mɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ mɪŋks / (BrE)
a woman who is playfully mischievous or cunning in an appealing or attractive way
playfully mischievous or cunning woman

Examples

She played the role of a charming minx in the romantic comedy, delighting audiences with her wit.
Despite her innocent appearance, everyone knew she was quite the charming minx when it came to getting what she wanted.
At the party, the charming minx managed to win over even the most skeptical guests with her laughter and charm.

Synset Examples

Everyone knew her as the charming minx of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minx - a boldly flirtatious or impudent woman
