travel iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl ˌaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a small, lightweight iron designed for use while traveling
portable iron for travel

Examples

She packed her travel iron to keep her clothes wrinkle-free during the business trip.
A compact travel iron is perfect for ironing shirts in hotel rooms.
He forgot his travel iron and struggled to keep his suit looking neat while abroad.
