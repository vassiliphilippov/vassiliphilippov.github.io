buffing wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌfɪŋ wiːl / (AmE), / ˈbʌfɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
a wheel covered with soft material, used with abrasive compounds for polishing or smoothing surfaces
tool for polishing or smoothing surfaces

Examples

The jeweler used a buffing wheel to bring out the shine in the silver bracelet.
Always wear eye protection when operating a buffing wheel in the workshop.
With the right compound and a buffing wheel, even old furniture can regain its original luster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polishing wheel - wheel used for polishing surfaces
