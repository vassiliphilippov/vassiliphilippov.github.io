in stock

1. ADJ

/ ɪn stɑk / (AmE), / ɪn stɒk / (BrE)
available for use or sale; present in the inventory of a shop or supplier
available for sale or use

Examples

The item you ordered is currently in stock and ready to ship.
We only display products on our website that are in stock.
The store manager assured me that new laptops would be in stock by next week.

Synset Examples

they have plenty of stonewashed jeans in stock

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of stock - not available for sale or use
