large civet

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈsɪvət / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈsɪvɪt / (BrE)
a common species of civet found in India and southeast Asia, known for its relatively large size
a large civet animal of Asia

Examples

The large civet is often found in forests across southeast Asia and India.
Researchers tracked several large civets to study their habitats and behaviors.
A large civet was spotted by the river during a late-night wildlife survey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civet - a small, mostly nocturnal mammal, often with a musky scent
