French kiss

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ kɪs / (AmE), / frɛntʃ kɪs / (BrE)
An open-mouthed kiss in which one person inserts their tongue into the other's mouth.
passionate kiss with tongues

Examples

The couple shared a long French kiss at the end of the movie.
He leaned in to give her a gentle French kiss, surprising her.
Their first French kiss was awkward but memorable.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: kiss - To touch someone with your lips as a sign of love or greeting

Etymology

Named after the association of passionate kissing with French culture in early 20th-century English.
