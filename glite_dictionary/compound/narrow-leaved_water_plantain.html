narrow-leaved water plantain

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛroʊ ˌlivid ˈwɔtər ˌplæntɪn / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ ˌliːvd ˈwɔːtə ˌplæntɪn / (BrE)
a species of aquatic plant (Alisma lanceolatum) with narrow, lance-shaped leaves, found in shallow water and wetlands
an aquatic plant with narrow, lance-shaped leaves

Examples

The pond was surrounded by narrow-leaved water plantain and tall reeds.
Scientists studied the habitat preferences of narrow-leaved water plantain in European wetlands.
We identified several specimens of narrow-leaved water plantain growing along the riverbank.
