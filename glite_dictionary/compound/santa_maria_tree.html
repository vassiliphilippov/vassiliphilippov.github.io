Santa Maria tree

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæn.tə məˈriː.ə triː / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə məˈriː.ə triː / (BrE)
A West Indian tree with racemes of fragrant white flowers, valued for its durable timber and resinous juice.
West Indian tree with fragrant flowers and durable timber

Examples

The furniture was crafted from Santa Maria tree wood, prized for its durability and beauty.
Local artisans collect resin from the Santa Maria tree for traditional uses.
Each spring, the Santa Maria tree blossoms with clusters of fragrant white flowers.
