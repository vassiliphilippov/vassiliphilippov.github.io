head lineswoman

1. NOUN

/ hɛd ˈlaɪnzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / hɛd ˈlaɪnzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female official in American football who is responsible for recording yardage gained or lost
female football official recording yardage

Examples

Sarah was promoted to head lineswoman after several years of officiating football games.
The head lineswoman quickly signaled the penalty to the referee during the match.
As a head lineswoman, she maintained precise records of every play's yardage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head linesman - male or generic official with the same football role
