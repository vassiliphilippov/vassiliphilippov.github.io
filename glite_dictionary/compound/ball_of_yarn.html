ball of yarn

1. NOUN

/ bɔl əv jɑrn / (AmE), / bɔːl əv jɑːn / (BrE)
a round mass of yarn wound into a ball, typically used for knitting or crocheting
round mass of yarn for knitting

Examples

The cat batted at the ball of yarn, unraveling it across the floor.
She bought a bright red ball of yarn for her next knitting project.
A single ball of yarn was all she needed to finish the scarf.

Synset Examples

The kitten chased after the ball of yarn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skein - a length of yarn or thread wound in a loose, long coil
