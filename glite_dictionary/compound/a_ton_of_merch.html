a ton of merch

1. NOUN

/ ə tʌn əv mɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ə tʌn əv mɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a large quantity of merchandise, especially in the context of branded goods, such as souvenirs, apparel, or collectibles
large amount of merchandise (informal)
informal

Examples

After the concert, she bought a ton of merch to remember the night.
Their closets are filled with a ton of merch from different fandoms.
He spent all his savings on a ton of merch during the convention.

Synset Examples

He bought a ton of merch because he’s a total weeaboo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merch - informal term for merchandise
  • Synonym: a ton of - informal phrase meaning 'a large amount of'
