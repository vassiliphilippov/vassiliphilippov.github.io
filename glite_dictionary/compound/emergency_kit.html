emergency kit

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɜrdʒənsi kɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi kɪt / (BrE)
a set of essential supplies and equipment prepared for use in unexpected or urgent situations, such as accidents or natural disasters
set of supplies for emergencies

Examples

All hikers are required to carry an emergency kit when exploring the national park.
Our family keeps an emergency kit with flashlights, batteries, and canned food in the basement.
After the earthquake, she was grateful to have a well-stocked emergency kit at home.

Synset Examples

Always keep an emergency kit in your car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first aid kit - set of medical supplies for basic treatment
