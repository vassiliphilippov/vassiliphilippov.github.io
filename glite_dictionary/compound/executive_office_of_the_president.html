Executive Office of the President

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv ˈɔfɪs əv ðə ˈprɛzɪdənt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv ˈɒfɪs əv ðə ˈprɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
the group of federal agencies and offices that assist the President of the United States in carrying out government duties and policies
agencies assisting the U.S. President
american

Examples

The Executive Office of the President plays a vital role in shaping national policy decisions.
Many key advisors to the president work within the Executive Office of the President.
The budget proposal was reviewed by the Executive Office of the President before being sent to Congress.

Related Terms
