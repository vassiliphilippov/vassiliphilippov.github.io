mundane routine

1. NOUN

/ mʌnˈdeɪn ruːˈtiːn / (AmE), / mʌnˈdeɪn ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
an ordinary and unexciting daily pattern of activities or schedule
ordinary, boring daily schedule or pattern

Examples

She was determined to break free from her mundane routine and see the world.
After months of following the same mundane routine, he longed for some excitement.
Many people struggle to find meaning in their mundane routine of work and chores.

Synset Examples

She craved adventure, tired of her mundane routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monotonous routine - a daily schedule lacking in variety, boring and repetitive
  • Antonym: exciting schedule - a daily plan that is stimulating and interesting
