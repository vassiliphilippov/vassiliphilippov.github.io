eating place

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːtɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈiːtɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
A place, such as a restaurant or café, where people go to eat meals.
place where people eat meals

Examples

We decided to try the new eating place near the train station.
There are several eating places in the center of town, ranging from fast food to fine dining.
The guidebook recommends this eating place for its local specialties and friendly service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals
  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant serving light meals and drinks
