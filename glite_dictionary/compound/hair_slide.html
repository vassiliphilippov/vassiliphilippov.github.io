hair slide

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr slaɪd / (AmE), / ˈheə slaɪd / (BrE)
a decorative hinged clip used by girls and women to hold hair in place
decorative hair-holding clip

Examples

She used a hair slide to keep her bangs out of her eyes.
The silver hair slide matched her elegant dress perfectly.
My sister collects hair slides in all shapes and colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrette - a clasp or pin for holding hair in place
