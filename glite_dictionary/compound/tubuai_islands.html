Tubuai Islands

1. PROPN

/ tuːˈbuː.aɪ ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / tuːˈbuː.aɪ ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
a chain of small islands in French Polynesia, located in the South Pacific Ocean, southwest of Tahiti
island chain in French Polynesia

Examples
