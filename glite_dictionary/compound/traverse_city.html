Traverse City

1. PROPN

/ ˈtrævərs ˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈtrævəs ˌsɪti / (BrE)
A city in northern Michigan, United States, located on an arm of Lake Michigan, known for its tourism and cherry production.
a city in northern Michigan, US

Examples

Each summer, thousands of tourists visit Traverse City to enjoy its beaches and cherry festival.
She grew up in Traverse City before moving to Chicago for college.
The picturesque views of Lake Michigan make Traverse City a popular destination for photographers.

Etymology

Named after Grand Traverse Bay, from French *la grande traverse* (the long crossing).
