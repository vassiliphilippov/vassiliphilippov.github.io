gossip columnist

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑsəp ˈkɑləmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsɪp ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
a journalist who writes a regular column reporting news and stories, often about celebrities and social events, based on rumors or personal information
journalist who writes celebrity gossip columns

Examples

The gossip columnist published a story about the actor's new relationship.
As a gossip columnist, she attends numerous celebrity parties to gather information for her column.
Many celebrities dislike having their private lives exposed by a persistent gossip columnist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columnist - a journalist who writes regular articles for a newspaper or magazine
