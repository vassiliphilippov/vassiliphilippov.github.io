East River

1. PROPN

/ ˈist ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈiːst ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a tidal strait in New York City separating Manhattan and the Bronx from Queens and Brooklyn
tidal strait in New York City

Examples

The East River flows between Manhattan and Brooklyn, providing stunning city views.
Several iconic bridges cross the East River, including the Brooklyn Bridge and the Manhattan Bridge.
Ferries regularly operate on the East River, connecting different parts of New York City.
