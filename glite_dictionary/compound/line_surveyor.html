line surveyor

1. NOUN

/ laɪn səˈvɛr jɚ / (AmE), / laɪn səˈvɛə rə / (BrE)
a surveyor responsible for marking positions and measuring straight lines, often using a range pole, in land surveying projects.
surveyor who marks positions with a range pole

Examples

The line surveyor carefully marked the boundary with a range pole before construction began.
As a line surveyor, she spent hours aligning reference points in the field.
The project required a skilled line surveyor to ensure the property lines were accurate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surveyor - a person who measures and maps land
