China virus

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.nə ˈvaɪ.rəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.nə ˈvaɪ.rəs / (BrE)
a controversial and widely criticized term referring to the COVID-19 virus, highlighting its origin in China; considered offensive due to its potential to stigmatize people of Chinese or Asian descent
offensive term for COVID-19
offensive

Examples

The use of the term China virus has sparked controversy around the world.
Many medical experts advise against calling COVID-19 the China virus because it fuels discrimination.
International organizations have condemned the phrase China virus for promoting stigma against Asian communities.
