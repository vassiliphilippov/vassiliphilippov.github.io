robot pilot

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊˌbɑt ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbɒt ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
an automatic navigational device that controls the course of a ship, plane, or spacecraft without human intervention
automatic device for steering vehicles

Examples

The ship was guided smoothly across the Atlantic by its robot pilot.
Modern aircraft rely on a robot pilot to maintain a set altitude and direction.
When the robot pilot malfunctioned, manual control had to be restored immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autopilot - device that automatically controls the path of a vehicle
