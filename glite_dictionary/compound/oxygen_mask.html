oxygen mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑksɪdʒən mæsk / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdʒ(ə)n mɑːsk / (BrE)
a device worn over the nose and mouth to supply oxygen from a storage tank, used especially in emergencies or medical situations
device supplying oxygen to nose and mouth

Examples

The flight attendant demonstrated how to use the oxygen mask in case of an emergency.
She placed an oxygen mask over the patient's face to help him breathe.
Pilots are trained to put on their oxygen masks quickly at high altitudes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breathing mask - mask worn over the nose and mouth to facilitate breathing in special situations
