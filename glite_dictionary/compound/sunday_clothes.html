Sunday clothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.deɪ kloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ kləʊðz / (BrE)
the best or most formal clothes that someone wears, especially to church on Sunday or on special occasions
best or formal clothes worn for church or special occasions

Examples

After ironing his Sunday clothes, he carefully hung them by the door for the morning service.
She always made sure her children wore their Sunday clothes when visiting their grandparents.
Back then, families reserved their Sunday clothes for church and important gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finery - expensive or formal clothes, especially for a special occasion

Etymology

From the practice of wearing one's finest clothing to church on Sundays.
