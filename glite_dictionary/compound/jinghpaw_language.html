Jinghpaw language

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪŋpɔː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋpɔː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Jinghpaw people, belonging to the Kachinic branch of the Tibeto-Burman language family, spoken mainly in northern Myanmar
language of the Jinghpaw people in Myanmar

Examples

She is fluent in both Burmese and Jinghpaw language.
The Jinghpaw language is an important marker of cultural identity for its speakers.
Linguists have studied the Jinghpaw language to understand its structure and history.
