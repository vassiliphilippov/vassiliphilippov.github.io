meat packing

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈpækɪŋ / (AmE), / miːt ˈpakɪŋ / (BrE)
the wholesale slaughtering, processing, and packaging of meat for distribution and sale to retailers
wholesale processing and packaging of meat

Examples

The city became famous for its meat packing industry in the early twentieth century.
Modern meat packing plants adhere to strict hygiene regulations to ensure food safety.
He works in meat packing, overseeing the distribution of processed beef to supermarkets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meat processing - the handling and preparation of meat for sale, including cutting, curing, and packaging
