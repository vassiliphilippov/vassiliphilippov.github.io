smooth aster

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈæstər / (AmE), / smuːð ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
a species of North American perennial plant of the aster family, with smooth leaves and pale blue to violet flowers (Symphyotrichum laeve)
species of North American aster

Examples

The smooth aster blooms in late summer with striking violet flowers.
Gardeners often plant smooth asters to attract butterflies and bees.
You can find the smooth aster growing wild in prairies and open woodlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - any of various chiefly fall-blooming herbaceous plants of the family Asteraceae
