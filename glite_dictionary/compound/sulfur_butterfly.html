sulfur butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌlfər ˈbʌtɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
Any of various butterflies of the subfamily Coliadinae, usually yellow or orange in color.
a yellow or orange butterfly, Coliadinae subfamily

Examples

A sulfur butterfly fluttered among the wildflowers in the sunny meadow.
The children chased a bright yellow sulfur butterfly through the garden.
Some species of sulfur butterfly migrate long distances during the changing seasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterfly - an insect with two pairs of large wings covered with scales, usually brightly colored
