eye condition

1. NOUN

/ aɪ kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / aɪ kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
the state or quality of the optical properties or health of the eye, especially in relation to vision
state of the eye's optical properties

Examples

He was diagnosed with a rare eye condition that affected his vision.
Regular check-ups can help detect any eye condition early.
Wearing proper eyewear may prevent some eye conditions from worsening.
