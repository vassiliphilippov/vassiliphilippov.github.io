high-tension environment

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈtɛnʃən ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / haɪ ˈtɛnʃən ɪnˈvʌɪrənmənt / (BrE)
a situation or setting characterized by a lot of stress, anxiety, or pressure
a situation with a lot of stress or pressure

Examples

Working in a high-tension environment can be exhausting if you don't find ways to manage stress.
The negotiations took place in a high-tension environment where every word mattered.
Some people perform best in a high-tension environment because it keeps them alert and focused.

Synset Examples

They thrive in high-tension environments like emergency rooms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stressful environment - a situation or place causing a lot of stress
