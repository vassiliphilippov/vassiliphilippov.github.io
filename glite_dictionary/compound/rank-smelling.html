rank-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ræŋkˈsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ræŋkˈsmɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
having a strong, unpleasant, and offensive odor; rancid-smelling
having an offensive, rancid odor

Examples

The trash cans behind the restaurant were rank-smelling after a week without being emptied.
He wrinkled his nose as a rank-smelling cheese was brought to the table.
We had to air out the room because of the rank-smelling socks left on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having a very unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
