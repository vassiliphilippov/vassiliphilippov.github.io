land development

1. NOUN

/ lænd dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / lænd dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of making an area of land more useful by preparing it for buildings, agriculture, or other purposes
process of preparing land for use

Examples

The city council approved new land development projects in the western district.
Local farmers are concerned about the impact of land development on wildlife habitats.
Regulations on land development aim to manage population growth while protecting natural resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urbanization - the process by which cities grow or land becomes urban
