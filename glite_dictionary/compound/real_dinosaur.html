real dinosaur

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹiəl ˈdaɪnəˌsɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈɹɪəl ˈdaɪnəˌsɔː / (BrE)
an object or person that is very old-fashioned or outdated, especially compared to current standards
very old-fashioned or outdated thing

Examples

My old flip phone is a real dinosaur compared to modern smartphones.
In the world of technology, that software is a real dinosaur now.
Some say printed newspapers are becoming a real dinosaur in the digital age.

Synset Examples

My old laptop is a real dinosaur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinosaur - something that is old-fashioned or obsolete
