cheeko pek

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃiːkoʊ pɛk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkəʊ pɛk / (BrE)
An informal term for a cowardly or overly cautious person.
cowardly or overly cautious person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a cheeko pek when it's your turn to speak.
His friends teased him for acting like a cheeko pek during the haunted house tour.
If you keep being a cheeko pek, you'll miss out on all the fun.
