respiratory disorder

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈspɪrəˌtɔri dɪsˈɔrdər / (AmE), / rɪˈspɪrətri dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a disease or condition that affects the organs involved in breathing, such as the lungs, bronchi, or trachea
disease affecting the respiratory system
medical

Examples

Asthma is a common respiratory disorder that affects people of all ages.
Exposure to air pollution can increase the risk of developing a respiratory disorder.
Chronic bronchitis is considered a serious respiratory disorder requiring long-term treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respiratory disease - disease affecting the respiratory system
