gooseberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡusˌbɛri lɪˈkɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊzbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic drink made by infusing or distilling gooseberries, often sweet and served as a dessert liqueur.
alcoholic drink made from gooseberries

Examples

After dinner, she offered her guests a glass of gooseberry liqueur to complement the dessert.
The homemade gooseberry liqueur had a distinctive tartness that everyone enjoyed.
They used fresh berries from the garden to create a unique gooseberry liqueur for the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a strong, sweet alcoholic drink flavored with fruits, herbs, or spices
