small misunderstanding

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ˌmɪsəndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / smɔːl ˌmɪsəndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a minor instance of miscommunication or confusion between people, usually not serious
minor miscommunication

Examples

There was a small misunderstanding about the meeting time, but it was quickly resolved.
It was only a small misunderstanding, so there's no need to apologize so much.
After a small misunderstanding with his friend, Tom cleared things up and they laughed about it.

Synset Examples

Don't shout; it's just a small misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misunderstanding - a failure to understand; a misinterpretation
  • Degree Higher: big misunderstanding - a major or serious miscommunication or confusion
