for certain

1. ADV

/ fər ˈsɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / fə ˈsɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
without any doubt; definitely or surely.
without a doubt; definitely

Examples

No one knows for certain where the ancient manuscript came from.
I can't say for certain if he'll arrive on time, but he promised he would.
We will find out for certain after the test results are announced.

Related Terms
