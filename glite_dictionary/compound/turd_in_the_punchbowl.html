turd in the punchbowl

1. NOUN

/ tɝd ɪn ðə ˈpʌntʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / tɜːd ɪn ðə ˈpʌntʃbəʊl / (BrE)
an element or person that disrupts or spoils an otherwise pleasant situation; something that causes discomfort or embarrassment in a social setting
disruptive or unwelcome element
informal vulgar

Examples

His negative attitude was a real turd in the punchbowl during the celebration.
Bringing up politics at dinner was like dropping a turd in the punchbowl.
The technical glitch served as a turd in the punchbowl in an otherwise smooth event.

Synset Examples

The unexpected news felt like a turd in the punchbowl.
His rude comment was a turd in the punchbowl at the party.
Her negativity was a turd in the punchbowl during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoilsport - someone who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment of others
