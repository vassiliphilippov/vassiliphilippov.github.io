state of petrification

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ˌpɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˌpɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a condition of being so shocked or frightened as to be temporarily unable to move or react
condition of shock or paralysis

Examples

When the intruder appeared, he froze in a state of petrification unable to call for help.
The team was so stunned by their loss that they sat in a state of petrification for several minutes.
The earthquake struck so suddenly that many people were left in a state of petrification.

Synset Examples

The sudden news left her in a state of petrification.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paralysis - inability to move or feel part or most of the body
  • Synonym: stupor - a state of near-unconsciousness or insensibility

Etymology

From 'petrify', from Latin petrificare (to turn into stone).
