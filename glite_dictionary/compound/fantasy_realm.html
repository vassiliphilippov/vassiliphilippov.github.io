fantasy realm

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.tə.si rɛlm / (AmE), / ˈfan.tə.si rɛlm / (BrE)
an imagined world with magical or supernatural elements, often used as a setting in literature, games, or film
imagined world with fantastical elements

Examples

As a child, she often imagined living in a fantasy realm inhabited by dragons and wizards.
Many novels transport readers to a fantasy realm where the impossible becomes possible.
The film's elaborate set design vividly recreated a magical fantasy realm on screen.

Synset Examples

He escaped into a fangtasy realm of adventure.
In her fangtasy world, anything was possible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy world - an invented world characterized by magical or supernatural elements
  • Antonym: real world - the actual world as opposed to an imagined or fictional one
