pressure sensation

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛʃɚ sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the tactile perception experienced when force is applied to an area of skin
sense of touch from applied force
medical

Examples

After the surgery, her pressure sensation in the affected arm was reduced.
The doctor tested his pressure sensation to evaluate nerve function.
Loss of pressure sensation can indicate damage to sensory nerves.

Synset Examples

the sensitivity of his skin to pressure and temperature was normal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touch - the sense that enables perception of contact or pressure on the skin
