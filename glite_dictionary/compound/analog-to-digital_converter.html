analog-to-digital converter

1. NOUN

/ ˌænəlɔɡ tə ˈdɪdʒɪtəl kənˈvɝtɚ / (AmE), / ˌænəlɒɡ tə ˈdɪdʒɪtəl kənˈvəːtə / (BrE)
A device that converts analogue signals into digital signals.
device converting analogue to digital signals

Examples

The analog-to-digital converter is essential for transforming sound waves into a digital format.
Many audio interfaces contain a high-quality analog-to-digital converter to ensure accurate sound reproduction.
Upgrading the analog-to-digital converter improved the clarity and detail of the digital recordings.
