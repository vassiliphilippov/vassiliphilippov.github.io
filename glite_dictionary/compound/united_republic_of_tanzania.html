United Republic of Tanzania

1. PROPN

/ juːˈnaɪt̬ɪd rəˈpʌblɪk əv tənˈzæniə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd rɪˈpʌblɪk əv tænˈzɑːnɪə / (BrE)
the official name for the country of Tanzania, located in eastern Africa
official name of Tanzania (country, eastern Africa)

Examples

Many international organizations recognize the United Republic of Tanzania as the country's official name.
The United Republic of Tanzania gained independence in the 1960s through the union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar.
On official documents, citizens list their nationality as belonging to the United Republic of Tanzania.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tanzania - country in eastern Africa
