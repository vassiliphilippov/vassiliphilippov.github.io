to call 999

1. VERB

/ kɑl naɪn naɪn naɪn / (AmE), / kɔːl naɪn naɪn naɪn / (BrE)
to dial the emergency services telephone number 999 in the United Kingdom
dial UK emergency services (999)
british

Examples

If you see a serious accident, you should call 999 without hesitation.
She quickly called 999 when she noticed smoke coming from the building next door.
They advised everyone to call 999 in case of any medical emergency.

Synset Examples

He called 999 for assistance during the fire.
If in danger, remember to call 999 immediately.
