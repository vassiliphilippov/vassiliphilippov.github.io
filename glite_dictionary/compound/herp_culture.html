herp culture

1. NOUN

/ hɜrp ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / hɜːp ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the hobby or practice of keeping reptiles and amphibians as pets
the keeping of reptiles and amphibians as a hobby

Examples

Many educational programs have introduced children to herp culture through interactive exhibits.
The magazine focuses on all aspects of herp culture, from terrarium design to breeding.
As herp culture grows in popularity, more resources are available for responsible pet owners.

Synset Examples

Herp culture has become increasingly popular among young enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herpetology - the scientific study of reptiles and amphibians
  • Synonym: aquarium keeping - the hobby of maintaining aquariums and aquatic life

Etymology

From 'herp', short for herpetology (the study of reptiles and amphibians), plus 'culture' (way of life or practice).
