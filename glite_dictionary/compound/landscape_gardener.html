landscape gardener

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænd.skeɪp ˈɡɑːr.dn̩.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈlænd.skeɪp ˈɡɑː.dn.ə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design and arrange gardens and outdoor spaces in an attractive way
person who designs and arranges gardens

Examples

They hired a landscape gardener to transform their backyard into a tranquil retreat.
After consulting with a landscape gardener, we decided to add a water feature and new flower beds.
The landscape gardener carefully selected plants that would thrive in our local climate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landscaper - a person who makes changes to outdoor land for aesthetic or practical reasons
