anthem trance

1. NOUN

/ ˈænθəm træns / (AmE), / ˈænθəm trɑːns / (BrE)
A subgenre of trance music characterized by uplifting melodies, major chord progressions, and a euphoric or 'rush'-like feeling.
uplifting subgenre of trance music

Examples

The DJ played a classic anthem trance track that had the entire crowd dancing and cheering.
Many fans of electronic dance music love the soaring melodies found in anthem trance.
He started his set with a few techno tracks before switching to his favorite style, anthem trance.

Synset Examples

Instead of the darker tone of Goa, uplifting trance uses similar chord progressions as progressive trance, but tracks' chord progressions usually rest on a major chord, and the balance between major and minor chords in a progression will determine how ‘happy’ or ‘sad’ the progression sounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uplifting trance - trance music subgenre known for positive, energetic mood
  • Degree Lower: progressive trance - related trance music subgenre with smoother transitions and buildups

Etymology

Formed from 'anthem' (a rousing or uplifting song) and 'trance' (an electronic music genre).
