schmancy place

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃmænsi pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈʃmænsi pleɪs / (BrE)
an informal term for a fancy or upscale establishment, often used humorously
fancy or upscale establishment (informal, humorous)
informal

Examples

They held the award ceremony at some schmancy place downtown.
Whenever his friends visit, he insists on taking them to a schmancy place for dinner.
I'm not dressed for a schmancy place like this; let's go somewhere more casual.

Synset Examples

He likes to dine at schmancy places to impress his dates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fancy restaurant - restaurant that is stylish and high quality
  • Synonym: upscale establishment - a business or venue of high quality and status

Etymology

Formed by adding the playful slang modifier 'schmancy' to 'place,' with 'schmancy' a humorous alteration of 'fancy.'
