flatwork ironer

1. NOUN

/ ˈflætˌwɜrk ˈaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈflætˌwɜːk ˈaɪnə / (BrE)
a large machine with a heated roller used for ironing and drying sheets, table linens, pillow cases, and similar items, typically in commercial laundries.
commercial laundry ironing machine

Examples

The hotel invested in a new flatwork ironer to handle its high volume of bed linens.
After washing, the sheets were fed into the flatwork ironer for a crisp finish.
A flatwork ironer is essential equipment in many commercial laundry operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironer - a device used for ironing fabric
