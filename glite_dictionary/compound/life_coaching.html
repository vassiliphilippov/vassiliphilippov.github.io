life coaching

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈkoʊ.tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / laɪf ˈkəʊ.tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of assisting people to identify and achieve their personal or professional goals through guidance and support.
guidance in achieving personal goals

Examples

She started life coaching after leaving her job as a manager.
Many people turn to life coaching when they feel stuck in their careers.
Life coaching sessions helped him gain more confidence and direction.

Synset Examples

We specialize in transition life coaching for personal and professional empowerment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coaching - the process of training or guiding someone to achieve a goal

Etymology

Formed from "life" + "coaching," modelled after terms like "sports coaching"; gained use in the late 20th century.
