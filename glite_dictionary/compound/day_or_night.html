day or night

1. NOUN

/ deɪ ɔɹ naɪt / (AmE), / deɪ ɔː naɪt / (BrE)
at any time; continuously or whenever needed, without restriction to the time of day
at any time; always available

Examples

You can reach the hotel reception day or night if you need assistance.
The helpline operates day or night to support people in crisis.
He promised to be there for her day or night if she ever needed help.

Synset Examples

She would help him day or night without hesitation.
The café is open day or night for customers.
You can call me day or night if needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: around the clock - all day and all night; continuously
