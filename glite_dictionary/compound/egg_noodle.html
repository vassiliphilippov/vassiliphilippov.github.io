egg noodle

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡ ˈnuː.dl̩ / (AmE), / ɛɡ ˈnuː.dl̩ / (BrE)
a type of noodle made from wheat flour and eggs, usually cut into narrow strips and used in soups or main dishes
noodle made with eggs and flour

Examples

She cooked a pot of egg noodles to serve with the chicken stew.
For this recipe, add the egg noodles during the last five minutes of cooking.
Chinese cuisine often features stir-fried egg noodles with vegetables and meat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noodle - a strip or tube of pasta or similar dough, often boiled and served in a sauce or soup
