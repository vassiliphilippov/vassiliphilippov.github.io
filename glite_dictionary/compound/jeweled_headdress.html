jeweled headdress

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːəld ˈhɛdˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəld ˈhɛdˌdrɛs / (BrE)
A decorative head covering that is adorned with jewels or precious stones.
headdress decorated with jewels

Examples

The queen wore a stunning jeweled headdress during the coronation ceremony.
Her elaborate costume included a silk robe and a sparkling jeweled headdress.
Antique dealers admired the intricate work on the ancient jeweled headdress found in the tomb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiara - a jeweled ornamental band worn on the front of a woman's hair
  • Synonym: diadem - a jeweled crown or headband worn as a symbol of sovereignty
