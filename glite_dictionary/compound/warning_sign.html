warning sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrnɪŋ saɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ saɪn / (BrE)
an event, condition, or signal that suggests potential danger or trouble
indicator of possible danger or trouble

Examples

The doctor told her that frequent headaches could be a warning sign of a more serious condition.
The flashing light on the dashboard is a warning sign that something is wrong with the car.
She took his sudden lack of communication as a warning sign in their relationship.

Synset Examples

Ignoring the warning sign could lead to serious consequences.
The storm clouds were a clear warning sign of rain.
A mini stroke can be a warning sign of bigger issues.
She took his silence as a warning sign to leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warning - a statement or event that indicates possible or impending danger
