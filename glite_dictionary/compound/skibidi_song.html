Skibidi song

1. NOUN

/ skɪˈbiːdi sɔŋ / (AmE), / skɪˈbiːdi sɒŋ / (BrE)
a popular dance song known for its viral online dance challenge, originally by the Russian band Little Big (2018)
a viral dance song by Little Big

Examples

People of all ages took part in the Skibidi song challenge on social media.
The catchy rhythm of the Skibidi song made it a global sensation.
Whenever the DJ played the Skibidi song, the dance floor filled up instantly.

Synset Examples

Everyone was dancing to the skibidi song at the party.

Etymology

From the refrain 'Skibidi' in the 2018 song by Russian band Little Big.
