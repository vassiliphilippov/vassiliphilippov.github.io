day school

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ skuːl / (BrE)
A school that operates during the daytime and does not have boarding facilities for its students.
school without boarding, held during the day

Examples

Many parents prefer a day school so their children can be at home each evening.
She attended a local day school before transferring to a boarding school.
The day school offers aftercare programs for working parents.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boarding school - a school where pupils live during term time
