flinty gaze

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɪnti ɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˈflɪnti ɡeɪz / (BrE)
a stern, hard, or unyielding look, often showing little emotion or sympathy
a hard, unyielding look

Examples

The detective fixed him with a flinty gaze that betrayed no emotion.
Her flinty gaze silenced the room immediately.
He responded to the criticism with nothing but a flinty gaze and pressed on.

Synset Examples

His flinty gaze made everyone uneasy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold stare - an emotionless or unfriendly look
  • Antonym: gentle look - a kind, soft, or tender glance

Etymology

From 'flinty', meaning hard like flint, emphasizing the unyielding nature of the gaze.
