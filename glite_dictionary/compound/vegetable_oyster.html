vegetable oyster

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈɔɪstər / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈɔɪstə / (BrE)
A Mediterranean herb (salsify) with an edible root that is said to taste like oysters.
herb with edible root tasting like oyster

Examples

We roasted the vegetable oyster and served it with a creamy sauce.
The roots of the vegetable oyster are a popular delicacy in some regions.
Many gardeners grow vegetable oyster for its unique oyster-like flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salsify - a plant with an edible root, also called vegetable oyster

Etymology

Named for its oyster-like flavor when cooked.

2. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈɔɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈɔɪstə / (BrE)
A plant with a long, white edible root (salsify), whose flavor is said to resemble oysters.
edible root plant (salsify)

Examples

The chef used vegetable oyster in the soup to give it a delicate, seafood-like flavor.
She roasted slices of vegetable oyster with herbs and olive oil.
At the farmers' market, I bought fresh vegetable oyster to try in a new recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salsify - a plant with a long, white root, also called vegetable oyster
