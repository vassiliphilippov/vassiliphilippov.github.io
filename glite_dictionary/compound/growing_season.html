growing season

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊɪŋ ˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ ˌsiːzən / (BrE)
the part of the year when conditions are most favorable for the growth of crops or plants
period when crops grow best

Examples

The region has a long growing season because of its mild climate.
Farmers must plant their crops at the beginning of the growing season to ensure a good harvest.
Some vegetables can only be grown during the warmer growing season months.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops
  • Antonym: dormant period - period when plants are not actively growing
