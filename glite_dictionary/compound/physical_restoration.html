physical restoration

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of helping disabled persons by removing or reducing physical disabilities; rehabilitation to restore physical function
process of removing or reducing disabilities
medical

Examples

After his accident, he underwent physical restoration to regain movement in his legs.
The center specializes in the physical restoration of patients with spinal injuries.
Advances in medicine have improved the techniques used in physical restoration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rehabilitation - the process of restoring someone to health or normal life
