credit card debt

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt kɑrd dɛt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt kɑːd dɛt / (BrE)
the amount of money that a person owes to a financial institution as a result of using credit cards to make purchases
money owed on credit cards

Examples

Her credit card debt grew quickly after she lost her job.
Managing credit card debt effectively is important for financial health.
Many young adults struggle to pay off their credit card debt.

Synset Examples

Don't let your credit card debt run up too high.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debt - money that is owed
