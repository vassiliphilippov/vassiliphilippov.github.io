Second Battle of El Alamein

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈbætəl əv ɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsekənd ˈbætəl əv ɛl ˌæləˈmeɪn / (BrE)
an important World War II battle in 1942 in which Allied forces, led by Montgomery, defeated German troops under Rommel in Egypt
key World War II battle in Egypt, 1942

Examples

The Second Battle of El Alamein marked a turning point in the North African campaign during WWII.
Historians often cite the Second Battle of El Alamein as a decisive defeat for German forces.
Montgomery's leadership was crucial to the Allied victory at the Second Battle of El Alamein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Battle of El Alamein - the general name for the battles fought at El Alamein in WWII
  • Degree Lower: First Battle of El Alamein - an earlier WWII battle at El Alamein in 1942
