rip-snorting mad

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪp ˌsnɔrtɪŋ mæd / (AmE), / ˈrɪp ˌsnɔːtɪŋ mad / (BrE)
Extremely angry or furious; in a state of intense rage.
extremely angry; furious
informal american

Examples

He became rip-snorting mad when he saw the damage to his new car.
The coach was rip-snorting mad after the team lost by a single point.
If you keep lying to her, she'll be rip-snorting mad in no time.

Synset Examples

He gets rip-snorting mad every time someone interrupts him.
She was rip-snorting mad when she found her car missing.
The manager was rip-snorting mad over the missed deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furious - extremely angry
  • Degree Higher: enraged - filled with intense anger
  • Degree Lower: angry - having strong feelings of displeasure or hostility

Etymology

Compound of 'rip-snorting', an American colloquialism meaning vigorous or intense, and 'mad' meaning angry.
