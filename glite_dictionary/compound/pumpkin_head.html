pumpkin head

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌmpkɪn hɛd / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpkɪn hɛd / (BrE)
A pumpkin that has been hollowed out and carved to resemble a head, typically used as a decoration for Halloween; also, a head or person resembling a pumpkin.
carved Halloween pumpkin or pumpkin-like head

Examples

Every year, children place a pumpkin head on their porch for Halloween.
The costume was complete with a giant pumpkin head and a tattered cloak.
He looked so ridiculous with that oversized pumpkin head mask at the party.

Synset Examples

She carved a scary face on her pumpkin head for Halloween.
The pumpkin-headed characters in the movie were hilarious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack-o'-lantern - A carved pumpkin lantern, especially for Halloween
