blade roast

1. NOUN

/ bleɪd roʊst / (AmE), / bleɪd rəʊst / (BrE)
A cut of meat for roasting, taken from the blade section of the animal, usually the shoulder.
roast cut from the shoulder blade

Examples

The chef recommended a blade roast for making a tender pot roast.
She cooked the blade roast slowly to bring out its full flavor.
When shopping for beef, he looked for a well-marbled blade roast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chuck roast - a cut of beef from the upper shoulder and lower neck
