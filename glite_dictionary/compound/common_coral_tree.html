common coral tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈkɔrəl tri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈkɒrəl triː / (BrE)
a small South American spiny tree with dark crimson and scarlet flowers, borne either solitary or in clusters
South American tree with crimson flowers

Examples

The common coral tree is admired for its brilliant scarlet blooms in botanical gardens.
Farmers often plant the common coral tree as a windbreak around their fields.
In spring, the common coral tree becomes a striking focal point with its showy flowers.
