sense impression

1. NOUN

/ sɛns ɪmˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / sɛns ɪmˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
An elementary awareness or immediate feeling resulting from the stimulation of a sense organ.
immediate sensory awareness

Examples

A sudden sense impression startled him as he touched the cold surface.
The artist tried to capture every sense impression from his daily walk.
Early sense impressions can shape how we perceive the world as adults.

Synset Examples

a sensation of touch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensation - a physical feeling or perception resulting from something that happens to or comes into contact with the body
