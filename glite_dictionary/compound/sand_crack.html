sand crack

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænd ˌkræk / (AmE), / ˈsænd ˌkræk / (BrE)
a vertical fissure in the wall of a horse's hoof, often resulting in lameness or discomfort
vertical fissure in a horse's hoof

Examples

The farrier treated the horse's sand crack to prevent further injury.
A severe sand crack can cause considerable pain and lameness in a horse.
Proper hoof care reduces the risk of sand cracks developing.
