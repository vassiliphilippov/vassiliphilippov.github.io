bass horn

1. NOUN

/ beɪs hɔrn / (AmE), / beɪs hɔːn / (BrE)
a brass wind instrument with the lowest pitch in its family, used especially in orchestras and bands
lowest brass wind instrument

Examples

The orchestra added a bass horn to enrich the depth of its sound.
He learned to play the bass horn for the school's marching band.
Some composers write specific parts for the bass horn in their musical scores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuba - a large brass wind instrument with a deep sound
