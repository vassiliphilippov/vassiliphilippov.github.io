large order

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a task, demand, or requirement that is difficult to achieve or fulfill; a formidable challenge
a difficult or formidable task

Examples

Securing that multi-million dollar contract is certainly a large order for any new business.
Achieving world peace in our lifetime remains a large order for humanity.
Solving this complex equation by hand is quite a large order for most students.

Synset Examples

finishing in time was a tall order but we did it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tall order - a task or request that is difficult to accomplish
