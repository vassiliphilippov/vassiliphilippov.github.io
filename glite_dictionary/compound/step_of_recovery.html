step of recovery

1. NOUN

/ stɛp əv rɪˈkʌvəri / (AmE), / stɛp əv rɪˈkʌvəri / (BrE)
a distinct stage or phase in the process of recovering from illness, addiction, or hardship
a stage in the process of recovery

Examples

Reaching the final step of recovery took him several years of hard work and dedication.
Each step of recovery brought her closer to her goal of sobriety.
Therapists often discuss the importance of every step of recovery with their patients.

Synset Examples

He was nervous as he approached the 13th step of recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage of recovery - a phase in the recovery process
