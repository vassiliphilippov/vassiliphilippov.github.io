yellow-bellied terrapin

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌbɛlid ˈtɛrəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌbɛlɪd ˈtɛrəpɪn / (BrE)
a freshwater turtle native to the United States and South America, often kept as a pet and sometimes raised commercially
a type of freshwater turtle

Examples

The yellow-bellied terrapin is a popular choice for pet owners due to its attractive markings.
Many pet stores offer young yellow-bellied terrapins for sale to customers.
She learned about the yellow-bellied terrapin during her biology class on aquatic reptiles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrapin - a small freshwater turtle
