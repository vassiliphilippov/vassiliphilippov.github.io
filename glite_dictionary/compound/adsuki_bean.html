adsuki bean

1. NOUN

/ ædˈzuːki ˌbiːn / (AmE), / adˈzuːki ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a small, reddish-brown bean widely cultivated in East Asia, especially in China and Japan, often used to make sweet or savory dishes
small reddish-brown Asian bean

Examples

Red bean paste is often made from adsuki beans in Japanese desserts.
You can find adsuki beans in many Asian grocery stores.
She added cooked adsuki beans to the salad for extra protein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adzuki bean - alternative spelling for 'adsuki bean', same plant and seed

Etymology

From Japanese 'azuki', meaning 'red bean'.
