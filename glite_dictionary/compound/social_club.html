social club

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl klʌb / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl klʌb / (BrE)
a formal organization or group where people with common interests meet for social activities
formal group for social activities

Examples

He became a member of the local social club to meet new people.
Many social clubs organize events and outings for their members.
The university has a variety of social clubs catering to different interests.

Synset Examples

he joined a golf club
they formed a small lunch society
men from the fraternal order will staff the soup kitchen today

Related Terms

  • Synonym: club - an association dedicated to a particular interest or activity
