snot rag

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnɑt ˌræɡ / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌræɡ / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory term for a cloth or tissue used to wipe nasal mucus
slang for tissue or handkerchief for the nose
informal slang

Examples

He reached into his pocket for a snot rag when he felt a sneeze coming on.
She refused to touch the dirty snot rag on the table.
During the cold, I went through an entire box of snot rags in three days.

Synset Examples

He grabbed a snot rag to clean up his mess.
I always keep a snot rag handy during allergy season.
Don't use that old snot rag; it's disgusting!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handkerchief - a square piece of cloth used for wiping the face or nose
  • Synonym: tissue - a disposable piece of soft absorbent paper for wiping the nose

Etymology

From 'snot' (nasal mucus) and 'rag' (piece of cloth).
