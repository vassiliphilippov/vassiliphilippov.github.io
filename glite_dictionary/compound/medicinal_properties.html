medicinal properties

1. NOUN

/ məˈdɪsɪnəl ˈprɑpɚtiz / (AmE), / məˈdɪsɪnəl ˈprɒpətiz / (BrE)
the qualities of a substance that have beneficial effects on health or are used to treat illness or disease
health benefits of a substance

Examples

Many herbs are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional remedies.
Scientists are researching the medicinal properties of this rare fungus to develop new treatments.
The plant’s medicinal properties have been recognized by local communities for generations.

Synset Examples

Saxifrax is known for its medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therapeutic - having healing properties; relating to the treatment of illness
