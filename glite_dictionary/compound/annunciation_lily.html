Annunciation lily

1. NOUN

/ əˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən ˈlɪli / (AmE), / əˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃ(ə)n ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A lily of the eastern Mediterranean and the Balkans, with broad, funnel-shaped white flowers, often associated with the Annunciation in Christian tradition.
lily with white funnel-shaped flowers

Examples

The Annunciation lily blooms every spring in our garden.
Artists often depict the Virgin Mary holding an Annunciation lily in religious paintings.
The delicate fragrance of the Annunciation lily filled the church during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Madonna lily - A synonym referring to the same species of lily with white flowers

Etymology

From Annunciation, referring to the Christian event, and 'lily' from Old English 'lilie'.
