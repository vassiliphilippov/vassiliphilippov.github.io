appetite for success

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəˌtaɪt fər səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈapɪtʌɪt fə səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
a strong desire or ambition to achieve success; intense motivation to reach one's goals
strong ambition to achieve success

Examples

Her appetite for success drove her to work late into the night every day.
Many athletes possess a relentless appetite for success that motivates them to train harder than their peers.
Despite facing setbacks, his appetite for success only grew stronger.

Synset Examples

Her rapacious appetite for success knew no bounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve something
  • Synonym: drive - strong determination to achieve something
