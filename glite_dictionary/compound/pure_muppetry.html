pure muppetry

1. NOUN

/ pjʊr ˈmʌpətri / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈmʌpətri / (BrE)
behavior that is extremely foolish or ridiculous
foolish or ridiculous behavior
informal british

Examples

His latest proposal was dismissed as pure muppetry by his colleagues.
The meeting descended into pure muppetry when no one could agree on a plan.
Blaming the weather for their failure was pure muppetry in my opinion.

Synset Examples

His constant distractions were pure muppetry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgement
  • Synonym: idiocy - extremely stupid behavior
  • Antonym: wisdom - soundness of judgement

Etymology

Formed from 'pure' (utter, complete) and 'muppetry' (derived from 'muppet', British slang for a fool).
