gelatin dessert

1. NOUN

/ ˈʤɛlətɪn dɪˈzɜrt / (AmE), / ˈʤɛlətən dɪˈzɜːt / (BrE)
A sweet, jellied dessert made by dissolving gelatin in water or fruit juice, often served chilled.
sweet jellied dessert made with gelatin

Examples

For her birthday party, she made a colorful gelatin dessert with layers of fruit.
Kids often enjoy eating a wobbly gelatin dessert after lunch.
The recipe calls for orange juice as the base for the gelatin dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jelly - A sweet dessert made by boiling fruit juice with sugar and gelatin, then allowing it to set
