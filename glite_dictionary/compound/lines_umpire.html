lines umpire

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪnz ˌʌmpaɪər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnz ˌʌmpaɪə / (BrE)
an official in sports such as tennis and soccer who assists the referee by watching for balls or players going out of bounds or offside
official who assists referee by watching lines

Examples

The lines umpire raised his flag to signal the ball was out.
In professional tennis, the lines umpire sits at the edge of the court to monitor the baselines.
After a heated discussion, the referee consulted the lines umpire before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: line judge - official who determines whether a ball lands within bounds
