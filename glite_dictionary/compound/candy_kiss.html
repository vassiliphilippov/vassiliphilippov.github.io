candy kiss

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændi kɪs / (AmE), / ˈkændi kɪs / (BrE)
A small, bite-sized piece of candy, especially a chocolate confection that is individually wrapped.
bite-sized candy confection

Examples

She handed me a candy kiss as a sweet treat after lunch.
The bowl was full of assorted candy kisses in shiny wrappers.
He always keeps a candy kiss in his pocket for his granddaughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chocolate - a food made from roasted and ground cacao seeds
