Hindoo ceremony

1. NOUN

/ hɪnˈduː ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / hɪnˈduː ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a religious event or festival celebrated within Hindu tradition, especially as referred to using older spellings
a Hindu religious event or festival (archaic spelling)
archaic

Examples

Many British colonists wrote about their experiences witnessing a Hindoo ceremony during the 1800s.
The book describes the rituals observed at a traditional Hindoo ceremony held in Bengal.
He studied the customs associated with each Hindoo ceremony in ancient texts.

Synset Examples

She attended a Hindoo ceremony last weekend.
The Hindoo festival was celebrated with great enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hindu ceremony - a religious event or festival in Hindu tradition

Etymology

Archaic spelling of 'Hindu', from Persian 'Hindu', used in English during the 18th–19th centuries.
