hit single

1. NOUN

/ hɪt ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / hɪt ˈsɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
a musical recording released as a single that becomes extremely popular or successful, often reaching a high position on music charts
popularly successful music recording

Examples

Their latest hit single was played on every radio station last summer.
The band achieved fame with their first hit single in the early 2000s.
She dreams of releasing a hit single that will make her a star.

Synset Examples

They expect this hit single to top the charts soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chart-topper - a song or album that reaches the top position in music charts
  • Antonym: flop - a song or album that is not successful
