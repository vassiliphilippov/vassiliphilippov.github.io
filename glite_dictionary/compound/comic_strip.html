comic strip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmɪk ˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪk ˌstrɪp / (BrE)
a sequence of drawings, typically accompanied by text, that tells a story or presents a joke, often appearing in newspapers or comic books
narrative series of comic drawings

Examples

He reads the comic strip in the newspaper every morning before breakfast.
The artist created a new comic strip featuring talking animals and witty humor.
Many childhood memories are tied to the colorful comic strip adventures on Sunday mornings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartoon - a drawing or series of drawings, often humorous, in a newspaper or magazine
