to spray-dry

1. VERB

/ spreɪ ˈdraɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ ˈdraɪ / (BrE)
to dry a liquid or slurry by atomizing it into a fine spray and contacting it with a hot gas, producing a powder or dry particles
dry by spraying into hot gas

Examples

The company uses large machines to spray-dry milk into a fine powder for easy packaging.
Instant coffee is often made by spray-drying liquid coffee extract under high heat.
After they spray-dried the fruit juice, only a fragrant powder remained.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry - to remove moisture from something
