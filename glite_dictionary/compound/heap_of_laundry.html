heap of laundry

1. NOUN

/ hip əv ˈlɔndri / (AmE), / hiːp əv ˈlɔːndri / (BrE)
a large pile of laundry, typically referring to dirty clothes waiting to be washed
large pile of dirty clothes

Examples

After the trip, there was a heap of laundry to sort and wash.
She sighed at the heap of laundry on the bathroom floor.
A heap of laundry blocked the doorway, making it hard to get in.

Synset Examples

There's a heap of laundry waiting to be done.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pile of laundry - a heap or collection of laundry
