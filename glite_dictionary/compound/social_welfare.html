social welfare

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊʃəl ˈwɛlfɛr / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəl ˈwɛlfɛə / (BrE)
Governmental provision of financial or other assistance to individuals or groups in need.
government aid for people in need

Examples

The new government prioritized improving social welfare programs to reduce poverty.
Many communities rely on social welfare for support during times of economic crisis.
Debates about the effectiveness of social welfare continue in many countries.

Synset Examples

she lives on welfare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare - financial or other assistance provided by the government to those in need
