grey-green

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪˈɡrin / (AmE), / ɡreɪˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a color that is green tinged with grey, or intermediate between grey and green.
green tinged with grey (color)

Examples

The old statue had a grey-green patina from years of exposure to the weather.
She wore a beautiful scarf in soft grey-green tones that matched her eyes.
As the storm approached, the sky turned a strange grey-green color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenish - having a green tinge or hue
