hooded ladies' tresses

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊdəd ˈleɪdiz ˈtrɛsɪz / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪd ˈleɪdɪz ˈtrɛsɪz / (BrE)
a North American orchid with dense clusters of spiraling creamy white flowers, two upper petals forming a hood
a North American orchid species

Examples

We spotted a cluster of hooded ladies' tresses growing near the stream during our hike.
Botanists were excited to find rare hooded ladies' tresses blooming in the meadow.
The creamy white flowers of hooded ladies' tresses are arranged in a gentle spiral.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ladies' tresses - any orchid of the genus Spiranthes, known for spiraled flowers
