porpoise oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔr.pəs ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.pəs ɔɪl / (BrE)
A yellow fatty oil obtained from porpoises, formerly used as a fine lubricant.
oil from porpoises, used as lubricant

Examples

Before synthetic alternatives, porpoise oil was prized for its lubricating qualities in fine machinery.
The museum displayed a container that once held porpoise oil used in old clocks.
Restrictions on whaling led to the decline in the use of porpoise oil in industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sperm oil - oil obtained from the head cavities of sperm whales, former lubricant
  • Synonym: whale oil - oil obtained from the blubber of whales
