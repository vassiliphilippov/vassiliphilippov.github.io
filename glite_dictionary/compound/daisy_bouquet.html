daisy bouquet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪzi boʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzi bəʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
A group of daisies arranged together in an attractive way, often given as a gift or used for decoration.
decorative bunch of daisies

Examples

She placed the daisy bouquet in a vase on the dining table.
The florist created a beautiful daisy bouquet for the wedding.
A simple daisy bouquet can brighten up any room.

Synset Examples

He gifted her a daisy bouquet on her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bouquet - an attractively arranged bunch of flowers
