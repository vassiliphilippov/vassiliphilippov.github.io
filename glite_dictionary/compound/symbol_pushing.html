symbol pushing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəl ˌpʊʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəl ˌpʊʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of actively promoting or emphasizing particular symbols, concepts, or ideas, often to influence perception or communication.
actively promoting symbols or ideas

Examples

The company’s relentless symbol pushing helped its logo become instantly recognizable worldwide.
Critics argue that excessive symbol pushing can overshadow substantive ideas in political campaigns.
Her approach to marketing focuses heavily on symbol pushing rather than on the details of the product.

Synset Examples

Symbol pushing can sometimes lead to misunderstandings in branding.
