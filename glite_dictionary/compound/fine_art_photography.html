fine art photography

1. NOUN

/ faɪn ɑrt fəˈtɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / faɪn ɑːt fəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
photography created primarily as an expression of the photographer’s artistic vision, rather than for commercial or documentary purposes.
artistic photography created to express vision

Examples

She specializes in fine art photography, using unusual techniques to create images that evoke strong emotions.
Many galleries now exhibit fine art photography alongside paintings and sculptures.
His work in fine art photography explores the relationship between urban spaces and natural landscapes.

Synset Examples

From there on his interest steadily transformed towards fine art photography of landscapes, both natural and manmade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: art photography - photography created as art, rather than for commercial purposes
  • Antonym: documentary photography - photography intended to document reality, often for journalistic purposes
