Reserve Officers Training Corps

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈzɜrv ˈɑfəsɚz ˈtreɪnɪŋ kɔrps / (AmE), / rɪˈzɜːv ˈɒfɪsəz ˈtreɪnɪŋ kɔːps / (BrE)
a college program that trains students to become commissioned officers in the armed forces upon graduation
college officer training program (US military)
american

Examples

He joined the Reserve Officers Training Corps to pursue a military career after college.
Many universities offer scholarships to students in the Reserve Officers Training Corps.
Participation in the Reserve Officers Training Corps requires a commitment to serve after graduation.
