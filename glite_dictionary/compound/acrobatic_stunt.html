acrobatic stunt

1. NOUN

/ ˌækrəˈbætɪk stʌnt / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈbætɪk stʌnt / (BrE)
an impressive or daring physical movement or feat performed by an acrobat, often involving balance, agility, and coordination
daring acrobat's movement or feat

Examples

The circus performer amazed the audience with a breathtaking acrobatic stunt high above the ground.
She spent years perfecting the acrobatic stunt for her gymnastics routine.
Pulling off such an acrobatic stunt requires strength, flexibility, and intense training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stunt - a feat displaying skill or daring, especially in performance
