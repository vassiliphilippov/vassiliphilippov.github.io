elephantiasis scroti

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛlɪfænˈtaɪəsɪs ˈskroʊtaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪfænˈtaɪəsɪs ˈskrɔːti / (BrE)
a condition in which the scrotum becomes abnormally swollen due to chronic obstruction of the lymphatic vessels
swelling of the scrotum from lymphatic obstruction
medical

Examples

Severe cases of elephantiasis scroti may require surgical intervention to relieve the swelling.
The prevalence of elephantiasis scroti is highest in regions where filarial infections are common.
Proper hygiene and early treatment can help prevent elephantiasis scroti in affected individuals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elephantiasis - abnormal enlargement of body parts caused by lymphatic obstruction

Etymology

From 'elephantiasis' (from Greek elephas, elephant-) and 'scroti,' genitive of Latin 'scrotum' (scrotum, pouch).
