corporate chaos

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːr.pə.rət ˈkeɪ.ɑs / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.pər.ət ˈkeɪ.ɒs / (BrE)
A situation of disorder or confusion within a business or corporate organization, often due to mismanagement or crisis.
business-related disorder or confusion

Examples

When the CEO resigned unexpectedly, the company descended into corporate chaos for several weeks.
Many employees left the firm, unable to cope with the constant corporate chaos.
Effective leadership is essential to prevent corporate chaos and maintain productivity.

Synset Examples

He preferred a genteel business atmosphere over corporate chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusion - a state of being bewildered or unclear
  • Synonym: disorder - lack of organization or order
