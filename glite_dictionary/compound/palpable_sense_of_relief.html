palpable sense of relief

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæl.pə.bəl sɛns əv rɪˈlif / (AmE), / ˈpæl.pə.bəl sɛns əv rɪˈliːf / (BrE)
a strong feeling of relief that is so obvious it can be almost physically felt
strong, obvious feeling of relief

Examples

When the exam results were posted, there was a palpable sense of relief throughout the class.
After the storm passed, a palpable sense of relief settled over the entire town.
You could feel a palpable sense of relief in the meeting room once the good news was announced.

Synset Examples

There was a palpable sense of relief after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: palpable - so intense as to seem almost tangible
  • Synonym: relief - the alleviation of pain or distress; a feeling of reassurance
