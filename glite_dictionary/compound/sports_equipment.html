sports equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ɪˌkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ɪˌkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the objects and gear needed to take part in a particular sport, such as balls, protective clothing, or racquets
objects used for playing sports

Examples

The school purchased new sports equipment for the athletics program.
Proper sports equipment is essential to ensure player safety during games.
He keeps all his sports equipment organized in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gear - equipment or apparatus used for a particular purpose
