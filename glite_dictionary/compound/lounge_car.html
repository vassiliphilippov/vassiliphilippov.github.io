lounge car

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒ kɑː / (BrE)
a railroad car equipped with a bar, tables, and lounge chairs, designed for passenger relaxation and socializing
railroad car with bar and lounge seating

Examples

We spent most of the journey relaxing in the lounge car and chatting with fellow passengers.
The lounge car offered scenic views and refreshments during the long train ride.
Passengers gathered in the lounge car to enjoy drinks and conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar car - railroad car serving alcoholic drinks
  • Synonym: dining car - railroad car where meals are served
