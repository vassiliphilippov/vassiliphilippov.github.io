crown roast

1. NOUN

/ kraʊn roʊst / (AmE), / kraʊn rəʊst / (BrE)
a roast made from the rib section of lamb (or occasionally pork), arranged in a circle like a crown for roasting and serving
circular roast of rib meat, usually lamb

Examples

The chef prepared a beautiful crown roast of lamb for the holiday dinner.
She ordered a crown roast from the butcher for her special occasion.
The crown roast was filled with stuffing and garnished with fresh herbs.
