spearmint oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrˌmɪnt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈspɪə.mɪnt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an aromatic essential oil extracted from the spearmint plant, used in flavoring, perfumery, and aromatherapy
essential oil from the spearmint plant

Examples

Add a few drops of spearmint oil to your diffuser for a refreshing scent.
The toothpaste contains natural spearmint oil for flavor and breath freshening.
Some people use spearmint oil to ease minor digestive discomforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peppermint oil - an aromatic oil obtained from the peppermint plant
