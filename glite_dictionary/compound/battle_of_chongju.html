Battle of Chongju

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l ʌv tʃʌŋˈdʒu / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv tʃɒŋˈdʒuː / (BrE)
a battle during the Korean War in November 1950, in which UN forces advancing towards the Yalu River were met and driven back by 200,000 Chinese troops
1950 Korean War battle involving UN and Chinese forces

Examples

The Battle of Chongju marked a significant turning point in the Korean War.
Historians often analyze the tactics used during the Battle of Chongju.
Commemorations are held each year to remember those who fought in the Battle of Chongju.
