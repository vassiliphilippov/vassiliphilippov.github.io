communist bandit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmjənɪst ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnɪst ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
(derogatory, historical) A person, especially a guerrilla or rebel, associated with communist movements and accused of revolutionary theft or violence.
derogatory term for a communist guerrilla or rebel
offensive

Examples

The legend of the communist bandit grew over time.
He was captured and tried as a communist bandit by the authorities.
Some villagers secretly supported the communist bandit movement.
