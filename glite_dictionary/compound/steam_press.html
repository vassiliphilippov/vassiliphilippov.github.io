steam press

1. NOUN

/ stiːm prɛs / (AmE), / stiːm prɛs / (BrE)
a home appliance used for pressing clothes with heat and steam to remove wrinkles
appliance for pressing clothes

Examples

She used the steam press to quickly smooth out her skirt before heading to work.
The hotel offers a steam press in every room for guests' convenience.
After washing his shirts, he put them through the steam press to keep them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iron - a handheld device for pressing clothes with heat
