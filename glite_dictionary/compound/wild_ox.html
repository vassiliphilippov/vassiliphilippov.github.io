wild ox

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ɑks / (AmE), / waɪld ɒks / (BrE)
A large, undomesticated bovine animal, especially one belonging to the genera Bos or Bibos.
undomesticated bovine animal

Examples

The gaur is a species of wild ox found in South and Southeast Asia.
For centuries, the wild ox roamed the grasslands in large herds.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitats of the wild ox.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: domestic ox - a bovine animal that has been domesticated, such as cattle used in farming
