force of nature

1. NOUN

/ fɔrs əv ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / fɔːs əv ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing with unstoppable power or influence, often likened to powerful natural phenomena
unstoppable power or influence

Examples

The hurricane was a true force of nature, leaving a trail of destruction in its path.
Her passion and drive make her a force of nature in the industry.
His innovative ideas hit the market like a force of nature.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerhouse - a person or thing of great energy, strength, or power
  • Antonym: weakling - a person lacking strength or power
