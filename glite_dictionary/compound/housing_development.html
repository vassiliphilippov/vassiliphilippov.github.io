housing development

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪŋ dɪˌvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ dɪˌvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
an area where a group of similar homes are built together by property developers, typically managed as a single community
area of newly built homes

Examples

They bought a new home in the housing development on the edge of town.
The city approved plans for a large housing development with parks and shops.
Many families were attracted to the housing development by its affordable prices and modern amenities.

Synset Examples

they live in the new housing development

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subdivision - an area of land divided into plots for homes
  • Synonym: apartment complex - a group of apartment buildings managed as a single entity
