made of glass

1. ADJ

/ meɪd əv ɡlæs / (AmE), / meɪd əv ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
constructed or formed from glass; often used metaphorically to suggest transparency or vulnerability.
constructed from glass; transparent or vulnerable

Examples

The antique vase is made of glass and must be handled carefully.
During the interview, she felt as if she were made of glass, unable to hide her emotions.
The conservatory was made of glass, flooding the room with natural light.

Synset Examples

He realized the closet is made of glass during the awkward meeting.
She felt exposed, as if the closet is made of glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • Synonym: transparent - allowing light to pass through so that objects can be seen clearly
