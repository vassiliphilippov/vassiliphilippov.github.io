potato yellow dwarf

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ ˈjɛloʊ ˈdaɪɚf / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˈjɛləʊ ˈdʌɪəf / (BrE)
A viral disease of potato plants characterized by yellowing and dwarfing of the foliage and stems.
disease of potato plants causing yellowing and dwarfing

Examples

Fields affected by potato yellow dwarf often show stunted plants with yellowed leaves.
Agronomists are researching new ways to control potato yellow dwarf in susceptible regions.
Early detection of potato yellow dwarf can help reduce losses in commercial potato crops.
