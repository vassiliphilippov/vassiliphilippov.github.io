uranium ore

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈreɪniəm ɔr / (AmE), / jʊˈreɪnɪəm ɔː / (BrE)
a naturally occurring mineral or rock from which uranium can be extracted
ore containing uranium

Examples

The company discovered a rich deposit of uranium ore in the desert.
Processing uranium ore requires strict safety protocols to limit radiation exposure.
Large quantities of uranium ore are needed to produce nuclear fuel.
