old man of the mountain

1. NOUN

/ oʊld mæn əv ðə ˈmaʊntən / (AmE), / əʊld mæn əv ðə ˈmaʊntɪn / (BrE)
A plant with hairy, featherlike leaves and tall stems, each bearing a large yellow flower head; found in mountainous regions of north-central United States.
a yellow-flowered mountain plant of the U.S.

Examples

The old man of the mountain is admired for its striking yellow blooms each spring.
We spotted an old man of the mountain while hiking in the Rockies.
Botanists study the old man of the mountain to learn how it thrives in harsh mountain climates.
