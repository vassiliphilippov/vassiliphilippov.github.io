shop assistant

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ʃɒp əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to help customers and sell goods in a shop or store
someone who helps customers in a shop

Examples

The shop assistant helped me find the right size for my shoes.
I asked the shop assistant if they had the dress in blue.
Several shop assistants were busy arranging products on the shelves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesperson - a person whose job is selling things, especially in a shop
