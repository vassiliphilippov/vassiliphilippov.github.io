teaching quota

1. NOUN

/ ˈtitʃɪŋ ˈkwoʊtə / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ ˈkwəʊtə / (BrE)
the minimum number of classes or instructional hours a teacher is required to conduct as specified in their contract or by institutional policy
minimum required teaching load

Examples

The university increased the teaching quota for each lecturer this semester.
Many teachers argue that a lower teaching quota improves instructional quality.
Under her contract, she has already met her annual teaching quota.
