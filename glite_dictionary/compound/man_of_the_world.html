man of the world

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn əv ðə ˈwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈmæn əv ðə ˈwɜːld / (BrE)
a man who is experienced and knowledgeable about life, especially about social and practical matters
experienced and worldly man

Examples

He was a true man of the world, comfortable in any society and always at ease.
Considered a man of the world, he handled negotiations with remarkable tact.
After years of travel and business, he had become a seasoned man of the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woman of the world - an experienced and sophisticated woman
  • Antonym: innocent - a person lacking experience or worldly wisdom
