American plaice

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈpleɪs / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈpleɪs / (BrE)
a large flatfish (Hippoglossoides platessoides) native to the North Atlantic, valued as a food fish in North America.
a flatfish of the North Atlantic, used as food

Examples

The American plaice is widely harvested by commercial fisheries along the North Atlantic coast.
Grilled American plaice is a popular dish in many seaside restaurants.
Researchers are studying the population decline of the American plaice.
