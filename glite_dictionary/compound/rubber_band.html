rubber band

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbər bænd / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə bænd / (BrE)
a narrow loop of elastic rubber used to hold objects together
elastic loop for holding things together

Examples

She wrapped a rubber band around the stack of envelopes to keep them together.
The package was secured with a rubber band so the contents wouldn't spill out.
He shot a rubber band across the room as a joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic band - a similar elastic band used to hold things together
  • Degree Lower: hair tie - an elastic band used to secure hair

2. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbər bænd / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə bænd / (BrE)
A loop of rubber, usually in the form of a thin ring, used for holding objects together by its elasticity.
loop of rubber for holding things

Examples

She gathered her hair into a ponytail with a rubber band.
I always keep a spare rubber band on my desk in case I need to bundle documents.
The stack of postcards was secured with a red rubber band.

Synset Examples

He used a rubber band to bind the papers neatly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic band - a similar loop of elastic material for holding things together
