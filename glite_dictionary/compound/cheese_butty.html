cheese butty

1. NOUN

/ tʃiz ˈbʌti / (AmE), / tʃiːz ˈbʌti / (BrE)
A sandwich made with cheese, typically buttered bread, commonly eaten in Britain.
sandwich made with cheese
british

Examples

A simple cheese butty is a staple of many British packed lunches.
Would you like a cheese butty with your tea?
She made herself a cheese butty before heading out to work.

Synset Examples

She made a delicious cheese butty for her snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese sandwich - a sandwich made with cheese between slices of bread

Etymology

'Butty' is British slang for 'sandwich', from Northern English dialect, possibly from 'butter'.
