opera company

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.pɚ ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈɒp.rə ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)

organization producing and performing operas

Examples

Our local opera company is performing Verdi’s 'La Traviata' this season.
