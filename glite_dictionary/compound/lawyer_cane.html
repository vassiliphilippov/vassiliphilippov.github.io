lawyer cane

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔjər keɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɔːjə keɪn / (BrE)
a tall, scrambling, spiny palm native to northeastern Queensland, Australia
scrambling spiny palm of Queensland

Examples

The forest was thick with lawyer cane, making it hard to move through without getting scratched.
Botanists surveyed several types of palms, including the lawyer cane, during their expedition.
Aboriginal Australians traditionally used the strong fibers of lawyer cane to weave baskets.
