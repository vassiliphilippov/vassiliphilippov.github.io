embryonic stem-cell research

1. NOUN

/ ɛmˌbraɪɑnɪk stɛm sɛl rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ɛmˌbrɪɒnɪk stɛm sɛl rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
Research involving stem cells derived from embryos, focusing on their characteristics and potential use in medicine.
research on stem cells from embryos
medical

Examples

Embryonic stem-cell research has the potential to revolutionize treatments for diseases like Parkinson's and diabetes.
There are ethical debates surrounding embryonic stem-cell research due to its use of human embryos.
Many medical advances have been made possible through embryonic stem-cell research in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stem-cell research - research involving stem cells and their potential medical uses
