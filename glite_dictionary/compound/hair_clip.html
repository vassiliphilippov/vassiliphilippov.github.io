hair clip

1. NOUN

/ hɛɹ klɪp / (AmE), / heə klɪp / (BrE)
A small device for holding hair in place, often made of metal or plastic.
device for holding hair in place

Examples

She used a hair clip to keep her bangs out of her eyes while studying.
The silver hair clip added a touch of elegance to her hairstyle.
After losing her favorite hair clip, she had to tie her hair with a rubber band instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrette - a bar-shaped clip or clasp for holding hair in place
  • Synonym: hairpin - a thin piece of metal used to hold hair in place
