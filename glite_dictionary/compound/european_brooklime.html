European brooklime

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈɹoʊpiən ˈbɹʊkˌlaɪm / (AmE), / jʊəˈɹəʊpiən ˈbɹʊkˌlaɪm / (BrE)
a low-growing European plant with blue flowers, sometimes naturalized in North America
European plant with blue flowers

Examples

The stream was bordered with patches of European brooklime, its delicate blue flowers catching the sunlight.
Gardeners often value European brooklime as a ground cover for wet, shady areas.
In North America, European brooklime can be found naturalized in damp meadows and along riverbanks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brooklime - a creeping plant with blue flowers found near water
