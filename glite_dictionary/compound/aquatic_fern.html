aquatic fern

1. NOUN

/ əˈkwɑːtɪk fɝːn / (AmE), / əˈkwɒtɪk fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern that grows in water or very wet places
fern that grows in water

Examples

Some species of aquatic fern help reduce mosquito breeding by covering the water surface.
We added a beautiful aquatic fern to our garden pond last spring.
The aquatic fern Azolla is known for its ability to fix nitrogen.
