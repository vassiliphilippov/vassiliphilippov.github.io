rose periwinkle

1. NOUN

/ roʊz ˈperɪˌwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈperɪˌwɪŋkl̩ / (BrE)
A tropical, evergreen plant with woody stems and large pink or red flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental.
tropical plant with pink or red flowers

Examples

The rose periwinkle produces beautiful flowers that brighten up gardens throughout the summer.
Scientists discovered important medicinal compounds in the rose periwinkle plant.
A cluster of pink rose periwinkle blooms lined the path to the entrance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periwinkle - a plant of the genus Vinca or Catharanthus, with trailing stems and blue or white flowers
