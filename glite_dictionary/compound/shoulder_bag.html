shoulder bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə bæɡ / (BrE)
a large handbag with a long strap, designed to be carried by hanging from the shoulder
handbag with shoulder strap

Examples

She slung her shoulder bag over one arm before leaving the house.
He bought his sister a new leather shoulder bag for her birthday.
The pickpocket tried to steal the tourist's shoulder bag in the crowded market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbag - a bag carried by women to hold personal items
  • Antonym: backpack - a bag with shoulder straps, carried on the back
