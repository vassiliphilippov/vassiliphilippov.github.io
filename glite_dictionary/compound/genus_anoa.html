genus Anoa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs əˈnoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈnəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of small, wild buffalo in the family Bovidae, native to Indonesia, especially Sulawesi.
genus of dwarf buffalo in Sulawesi

Examples

Scientists are studying the genus Anoa to better understand the evolution of dwarf buffalo.
The genus Anoa includes some of the world's smallest wild cattle species.
Habitat loss is a significant threat to members of the genus Anoa in Sulawesi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anoa - A species of small buffalo native to Sulawesi

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and local Indonesian name 'anoa'.
