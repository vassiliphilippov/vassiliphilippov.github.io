home stand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm ˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌstænd / (BrE)
A series of successive games played by a sports team at its home field or court.
series of home games (sports)

Examples

The baseball team started their home stand with three consecutive wins.
After a long road trip, the players were relieved to return for a ten-game home stand.
Fans are hoping the upcoming home stand will improve the team's standing in the league.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: road trip - a series of successive games played away from a team's home field or court
