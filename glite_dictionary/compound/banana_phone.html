banana phone

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə foʊn / (AmE), / bəˈnænə fəʊn / (BrE)
a toy shaped like a banana that functions as a phone, or a banana used humorously as a pretend phone
novelty phone shaped like a banana

Examples

The children burst into laughter when their teacher answered the banana phone during story time.
She bought her nephew a banana phone as a quirky birthday present.
At parties, people sometimes use a real banana as a banana phone for silly photos.

Synset Examples

The kids played with a toy shaped like a banana phone.
He pretended to talk on a banana phone for laughs.
