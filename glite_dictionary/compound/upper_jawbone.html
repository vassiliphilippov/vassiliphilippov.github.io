upper jawbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpɚ ˈdʒɔˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈʌpə ˈdʒɔːbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone in vertebrates forming the upper part of the jaw, typically fused to the skull
bone forming the upper jaw

Examples

The dentist examined the patient’s upper jawbone for signs of infection.
In birds, the upper jawbone is specially adapted for feeding.
The x-ray revealed a fracture in her upper jawbone after the accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maxilla - the technical term for the upper jawbone in vertebrates
