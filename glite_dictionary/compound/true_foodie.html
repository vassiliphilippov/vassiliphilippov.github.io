true foodie

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈfudi / (AmE), / truː ˈfuːdi / (BrE)
A person who is genuinely very enthusiastic and passionate about food, especially about trying new and gourmet foods.
genuine food enthusiast

Examples

She considers herself a true foodie and loves exploring new restaurants every weekend.
Only a true foodie would travel to another city just for a special meal.
If you're a true foodie, this market is a must-visit destination.

Synset Examples

He's a true foodie, always seeking gourmet experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foodie - a person with a particular interest in food; a gourmet
