wintergreen oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚɡrin ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəɡriːn ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fragrant oil obtained from the leaves of the wintergreen plant, used as a flavoring and in medicine
oil from the wintergreen plant

Examples

Some toothpaste contains wintergreen oil for its refreshing flavor.
The pharmacist recommended wintergreen oil to help relieve muscle aches.
You can smell the distinct aroma of wintergreen oil in this ointment.
