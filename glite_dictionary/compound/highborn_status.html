highborn status

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌbɔrn ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌbɔːn ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the condition of being born into a noble or aristocratic family
noble or aristocratic origin

Examples

Her highborn status was evident from the way people addressed her at the gala.
Despite his highborn status, he always treated everyone with respect and kindness.
The novel explores how highborn status shapes the opportunities available to its characters.

Synset Examples

His highborn status opened many doors for him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noble birth - being born into nobility
  • Antonym: commoner status - the condition of not being of noble birth
