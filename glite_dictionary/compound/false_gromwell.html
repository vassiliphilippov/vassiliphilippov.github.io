false gromwell

1. NOUN

/ fɔls ˈɡrɑmˌwɛl / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈɡrɒm.wɛl / (BrE)
Any of several North American perennial herbs with hairy leaves and small yellowish or greenish flowers, especially of the genus Onosmodium.
North American perennial herb

Examples

The prairie was dotted with false gromwell among the native grasses and flowers.
Botanists collected samples of false gromwell to study its unique hairy leaves.
We spotted a patch of false gromwell growing near the edge of the woodland.
