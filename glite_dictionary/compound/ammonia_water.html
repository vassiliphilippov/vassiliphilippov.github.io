ammonia water

1. NOUN

/ əˈmoʊ.ni.ə ˈwɔ.tɚ / (AmE), / əˈməʊ.ni.ə ˈwɔː.tə / (BrE)
a solution of ammonia gas dissolved in water, commonly used as a cleaning agent or in chemical applications
solution of ammonia in water

Examples

Always use ammonia water in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling fumes.
She mixed ammonia water with detergent to clean the windows.
Industrial labs often store ammonia water in tightly sealed containers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammonium hydroxide - another term for ammonia dissolved in water, emphasizing its chemical form
