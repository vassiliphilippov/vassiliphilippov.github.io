axe head

1. NOUN

/ ˈæks hɛd / (AmE), / ˈaks hɛd / (BrE)
the metal cutting part of an axe, typically attached to the handle
cutting part of an axe

Examples

The blacksmith forged a new axe head for the broken tool.
He accidentally chipped the edge of his axe head while chopping wood.
The old axe head was rusty but still very sharp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axe - a tool with a heavy bladed head mounted crosswise on a handle
