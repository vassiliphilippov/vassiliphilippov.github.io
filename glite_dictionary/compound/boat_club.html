boat club

1. NOUN

/ boʊt klʌb / (AmE), / bəʊt klʌb / (BrE)
an organization that promotes and supports boating and yachting activities, often providing facilities or organizing events for members
organization supporting boating or yachting

Examples

She joined the local boat club to improve her sailing skills and meet other boating enthusiasts.
The annual regatta is organized by the city's oldest boat club.
Members of the boat club can store their boats at the marina and participate in special events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yacht club - organization supporting yachting activities
