motor horn

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtɚ hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə hɔːn / (BrE)
a device fitted to a motor vehicle that emits a loud sound to warn or alert others
vehicle warning sound device

Examples

Every car must be equipped with a motor horn for safety reasons.
He pressed the motor horn to alert the cyclist ahead.
The sound of the motor horn startled the pedestrians crossing the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn - a device that makes a loud warning sound, especially on vehicles
