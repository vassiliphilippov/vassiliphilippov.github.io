common question

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
A question that is frequently asked, often by many people or in a particular context.
a frequently asked question

Examples

If you have a common question, please check our FAQ page first.
The teacher addressed each common question at the beginning of the lesson.
One common question is how to reset your password.

Synset Examples

The Xbot in our chat helps answer common questions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frequently asked question - A question that many people ask, often answered in a FAQ list
