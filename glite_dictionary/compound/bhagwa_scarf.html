bhagwa scarf

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːɡwə skɑrf / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡwə skɑːf / (BrE)
a saffron-colored scarf worn as a symbol of Hindu identity or affiliation
saffron scarf symbolizing Hindu identity

Examples

At the rally, many people waved flags and donned a bhagwa scarf to express their unity.
He tied a bhagwa scarf around his neck as a sign of devotion.
Some political parties use the bhagwa scarf as part of their official attire.

Synset Examples

He wore a bhagwa scarf to show his support.

Etymology

From Hindi 'bhagwā', meaning 'saffron', referring to the color of the scarf.
