vertical bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɝː.t̬ɪ.kəl bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tɪ.kəl bæŋk / (BrE)
a steep banking maneuver in which an aircraft's lateral axis is nearly vertical to the ground
steep aircraft banking maneuver

Examples

During the airshow, the pilot executed a vertical bank to impress the crowd.
A sharp vertical bank can put significant stress on an airplane's structure.
He lost altitude quickly after entering a vertical bank at high speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - to tilt an aircraft laterally during a turn
