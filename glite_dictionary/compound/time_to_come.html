time to come

1. NOUN

/ taɪm tə kʌm / (AmE), / taɪm tə kʌm / (BrE)
the period of time that is still in the future; the future.
the future; the time ahead

Examples

In the time to come, technology will continue to shape our lives.
We must plan now to avoid problems in the time to come.
Historians will study these events for years in the time to come.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: future - the time after the present; what is still to happen
