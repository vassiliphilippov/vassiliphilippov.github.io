bacon burger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkən ˌbɜːrɡər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən ˌbɜːɡə / (BrE)
A hamburger that includes bacon as one of its toppings.
hamburger with bacon

Examples

I ordered a bacon burger with extra cheese for lunch.
Many fast-food restaurants offer a signature bacon burger on their menu.
The bacon burger at that new place is juicy and full of flavor.

Synset Examples

They serve a delicious bacon burger at that diner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheeseburger - a hamburger topped with cheese
