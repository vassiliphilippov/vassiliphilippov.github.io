lanseh tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæn.seɪ tri / (AmE), / ˈlan.seɪ triː / (BrE)
An East Indian tree (Lansium parasiticum) that bears edible yellow berries.
East Indian tree with edible yellow berries

Examples

The lanseh tree produces fruit that is popular in many Southeast Asian markets.
Farmers carefully tend to the lanseh tree during the fruiting season.
We sampled the sweet, yellow berries from a lanseh tree while traveling in Indonesia.
