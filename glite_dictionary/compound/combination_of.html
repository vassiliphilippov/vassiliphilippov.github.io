combination of

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃən əv / (AmE), / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən əv / (BrE)
the act or result of joining two or more elements together
joining two or more things

Examples

The combination of flavors in this dish creates a unique taste experience.
The combination of exercise and diet helped him lose weight.
Success often results from a combination of hard work and good luck.

Synset Examples

The combination of gammon and pickles is delightful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things blended together
  • Synonym: blend - a mixture of different things or qualities

Etymology

From Latin 'combinatio' (a joining together) + 'of' (Old English 'of', expressing the relationship).
