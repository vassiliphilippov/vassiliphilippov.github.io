large-flowering magnolia

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈflaʊərɪŋ mæɡˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈflaʊərɪŋ mæɡˈnəʊlɪə / (BrE)
an evergreen tree of the southern United States with large, stiff, glossy leaves and huge, fragrant white flowers
evergreen tree with large white flowers

Examples

The large-flowering magnolia is admired for its beautiful blossoms and glossy leaves.
Many southern gardens feature a large-flowering magnolia as a centerpiece.
The scent of the large-flowering magnolia filled the warm, humid air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: southern magnolia - another name for the large-flowering magnolia tree
