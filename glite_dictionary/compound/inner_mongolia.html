Inner Mongolia

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪnɚ mɑŋˈgoʊliə / (AmE), / ˌɪnə mɒŋˈɡəʊliə / (BrE)
an autonomous region in northern China, bordering Mongolia, known for its diverse ethnic groups and vast grasslands.
autonomous region in northern China

Examples

She traveled to Inner Mongolia to experience the unique culture and landscapes of the region.
The climate of Inner Mongolia can be harsh, with cold winters and hot summers.
Many ethnic Mongols still maintain traditional herding lifestyles in Inner Mongolia.
