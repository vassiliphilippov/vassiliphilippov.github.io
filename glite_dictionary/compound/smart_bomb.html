smart bomb

1. NOUN

/ smɑrt bɑm / (AmE), / smɑːt bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb equipped with guidance systems, such as laser or radio controls, allowing it to accurately reach a specific target
precision-guided bomb

Examples

The military used a smart bomb to strike the carefully chosen target.
Modern air strikes often rely on smart bombs to minimize collateral damage.
The introduction of the smart bomb marked a turning point in aerial warfare.

Synset Examples

smart bombs have revolutionized aerial bombardment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guided missile - missile steered by remote control or onboard guidance
