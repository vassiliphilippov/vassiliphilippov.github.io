differently able worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt.li ˈeɪ.bəl ˈwɝː.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɪf.ərənt.li ˈeɪ.bəl ˈwɜː.kə / (BrE)
A person with a disability who is employed or seeking work, emphasizing their different abilities rather than limitations.
employee with a disability

Examples

The company makes a point to employ differently able workers in various departments.
Many differently able workers bring unique skills and fresh viewpoints to their jobs.
She advocates for better workplace accommodations for differently able workers.

Synset Examples

They hire differently able workers for their unique perspectives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disabled worker - an employee with a disability
