mercury thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜrkjəri θərˈmɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmɜːkjʊri θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A thermometer that uses mercury in a glass tube to measure temperature, with the mercury rising and falling as temperature changes.
thermometer using mercury in a glass tube

Examples

The nurse checked my temperature with a mercury thermometer under my tongue.
Many schools have replaced the old mercury thermometer with electronic versions for safety reasons.
A mercury thermometer can provide accurate readings but must be handled with care due to the toxic metal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcohol thermometer - thermometer using alcohol instead of mercury
  • Synonym: digital thermometer - thermometer providing a digital temperature readout
