knife thrust

1. NOUN

/ naɪf θrʌst / (AmE), / naɪf θrʌst / (BrE)
a forceful stab or blow with a knife or other pointed weapon
forceful stab with a knife

Examples

The victim suffered a fatal knife thrust to the chest.
He parried the opponent's knife thrust just in time.
The police found a deep knife thrust wound at the scene.

Synset Examples

one strong stab to the heart killed him

Related Terms
