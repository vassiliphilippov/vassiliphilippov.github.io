color guard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a group of people who serve as a ceremonial escort for the regimental or national flag, especially in military contexts
ceremonial flag escort (military)

Examples

The color guard marched at the front of the parade, carrying the national flag with great pride.
During the ceremony, the color guard presented the regimental colors with precision and respect.
Each branch of the military selects members for its prestigious color guard units.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honor guard - a group that serves as a ceremonial guard, often for important events or individuals
