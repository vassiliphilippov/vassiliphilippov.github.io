quiche-eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːʃ ˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈkiːʃ ˌiːtə / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who enjoys eating quiche, often used to stereotype someone as trendy or sophisticated.
person who enjoys eating quiche; trendy stereotype
informal

Examples

She's a proud quiche-eater who can recommend the best bakeries in town.
He was teased for being a quiche-eater after bringing one to the office party.
The magazine joked about the city’s population of quiche-eaters and their favorite brunch spots.

Synset Examples

As a quiche-eater, she enjoys trying new flavors.
