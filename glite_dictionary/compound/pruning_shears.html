pruning shears

1. NOUN

/ ˈpruːnɪŋ ʃɪrz / (AmE), / ˈpruːnɪŋ ʃɪəz / (BrE)
A type of strong, hand-held scissors used for cutting small branches and stems of woody plants.
garden tool for cutting plants

Examples

She trimmed the roses with her pruning shears before arranging them in a vase.
The gardener sharpened his pruning shears to make the work easier.
Always clean your pruning shears after use to prevent plant diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secateurs - garden clippers for cutting plants, especially in British English
