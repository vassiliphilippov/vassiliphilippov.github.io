spike heath

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk hiːθ / (AmE), / spaɪk hiːθ / (BrE)
a small, evergreen, mat-forming shrub of southern Europe and Asia Minor, with stiff stems and terminal clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers
small evergreen shrub of southern Europe and Asia Minor

Examples

The spike heath is valued in rock gardens for its attractive clusters of flowers.
Botanists discovered a new population of spike heath on the Mediterranean hillside.
Gardeners often use spike heath for groundcover because of its dense, mat-forming growth.
