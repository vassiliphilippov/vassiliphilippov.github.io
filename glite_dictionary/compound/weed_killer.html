weed killer

1. NOUN

/ wid ˈkɪlɚ / (AmE), / wiːd ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
a chemical substance used to destroy unwanted plants, especially weeds
chemical for destroying weeds

Examples

Many gardeners use weed killer to keep their lawns looking tidy.
Be careful not to spray weed killer near your vegetable plants.
The store had several types of weed killer on sale this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbicide - a substance used to destroy unwanted vegetation
