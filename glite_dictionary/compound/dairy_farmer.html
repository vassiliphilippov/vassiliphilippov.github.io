dairy farmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛri ˌfɑrmər / (AmE), / ˈdeəri ˌfɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a farm that produces milk and dairy products
owner or manager of a dairy farm

Examples

The dairy farmer rises early every morning to milk the cows and check on the herd.
Many dairy farmers rely on modern technology to increase milk production and efficiency.
After years of experience, she became a respected dairy farmer in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
