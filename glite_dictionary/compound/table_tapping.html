table tapping

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˌtæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl ˌtapɪŋ / (BrE)
An alleged method of communicating with spirits, in which messages are conveyed by tapping on a table during a séance.
spiritualist method of spirit communication

Examples

Many Victorians believed that table tapping could help them speak to the spirits of departed loved ones.
The séance featured dramatic table tapping that left the participants both frightened and fascinated.
Researchers debunked the claims of table tapping by revealing clever tricks hidden under the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table turning - a related spiritualist practice involving a table that rotates or moves during a séance
