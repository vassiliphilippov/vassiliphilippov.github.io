life-support

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪf səˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔːt / (BrE)
relating to equipment or methods that are used to keep someone alive when they are seriously ill or injured and unable to survive without help.
relating to sustaining life medically

Examples

The patient was transferred to the ICU and placed on life-support systems immediately.
Advances in life-support technology have improved survival rates for critically ill patients.
Doctors debated whether it was ethical to withdraw life-support measures in this situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life-sustaining - maintaining or supporting life
