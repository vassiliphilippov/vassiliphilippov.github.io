propeller head

1. NOUN

/ prəˈpɛlər hɛd / (AmE), / prəˈpɛlə hɛd / (BrE)
an enthusiastic or obsessive person about technology or gadgets, especially computers
person overly enthusiastic about technology
informal

Examples

The new software instantly attracted every propeller head in the company.
He's known as a propeller head who can fix any computer problem you have.
Even at the party, the conversation among the propeller heads turned to the latest tech gadgets.

Synset Examples

Every propeller head in the room was captivated.
As a propeller head, she loves discussing tech innovations.
He's a real propeller head, always fixing gadgets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: techie - someone who is proficient or enthusiastic about technology
  • Synonym: geek - a person with an obsessive interest in a particular subject, especially technology

Etymology

Originally American slang; from the image of a person with a 'propeller beanie' cap, symbolizing nerdiness.
