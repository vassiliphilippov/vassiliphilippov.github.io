late-night hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪt ˌnaɪt ˈaʊər / (AmE), / ˈleɪt ˌnaɪt ˈaʊə / (BrE)
the latter part of the night, typically when most people are asleep
the latter part of the night

Examples

She was still working at the office during the late-night hour when everyone else had gone home.
Many creative people say their best ideas come in a late-night hour of quiet.
During that late-night hour, the streets were almost completely empty.
