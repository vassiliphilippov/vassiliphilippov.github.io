trouser cough

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraʊzɚ kɔf / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə kɒf / (BrE)
an informal term for the sound produced when someone passes gas, especially while sitting; a euphemistic term for flatulence
euphemism for flatulence
informal slang

Examples

He tried to stifle a trouser cough during the silent exam room.
Her loud trouser cough caused everyone at the dinner table to giggle.
Nobody admitted to the trouser cough that echoed in the quiet office.

Synset Examples

His trouser cough embarrassed him in front of everyone.
He tried to hide his trouser cough during the meeting.
She laughed when he blamed the trouser cough on his new pants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatulence - the accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal; the act of passing gas
  • Synonym: fart - an informal term for passing gas from the anus
