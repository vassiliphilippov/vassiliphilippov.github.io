call up

1. NOUN

/ kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
an official order instructing someone to report for military service
military conscription order

Examples

After receiving his call up, he packed his bags and prepared to leave for basic training.
Many young men dreaded the moment when a call up letter would arrive in the mail.
During wartime, a call up often meant putting one's education and career on hold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draft - the practice of ordering people into military service
  • Synonym: conscription - compulsory enlistment for state military service
  • Antonym: discharge - the release from military service
