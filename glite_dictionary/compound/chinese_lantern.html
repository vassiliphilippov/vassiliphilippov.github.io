Chinese lantern

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪˈniz ˈlæntərn / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˈlɑːntən / (BrE)
a decorative, collapsible lantern made of brightly colored paper, typically used for festive occasions.
decorative paper lantern

Examples

During the festival, colorful Chinese lanterns hung across the street and swayed in the wind.
We decorated the garden with glowing Chinese lanterns for the evening party.
A single Chinese lantern lit up the dark balcony, casting intricate shadows on the wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lantern - a portable light with a protective case
