weather condition

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðər kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a particular state of the atmosphere at a specific time and place, such as temperature, wind, clouds, and precipitation
state of the atmosphere (temperature, wind, etc.)

Examples

The severe weather condition made it dangerous to drive on the mountain roads.
Meteorologists monitor every weather condition before making their predictions.
Farmers depend on favorable weather conditions for a successful harvest.

Synset Examples

they were hoping for good weather
every day we have weather conditions and yesterday was no exception
the conditions were too rainy for playing in the snow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather - the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time
