order Chelonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər kəˈloʊniə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kɛˈləʊnɪə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order comprising tortoises and turtles
order of tortoises and turtles

Examples

Scientists classify both land-dwelling tortoises and aquatic turtles within the order Chelonia.
The anatomy of animals in the order Chelonia has fascinated biologists for centuries.
She specialized in the study of the order Chelonia during her doctoral research.

Etymology

From Greek khélōnē (tortoise), used as a scientific taxonomic name.
