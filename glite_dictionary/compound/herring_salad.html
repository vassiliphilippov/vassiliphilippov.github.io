herring salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛr.ɪŋ ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / ˈhɛr.ɪŋ ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
A salad made with pickled herring, often combined with vegetables, eggs, or potatoes and served cold.
salad featuring pickled herring

Examples

For the holiday dinner, she served a traditional herring salad with beets and apples.
The menu included a creamy herring salad as an appetizer.
My grandmother taught me how to prepare her favorite herring salad recipe.
