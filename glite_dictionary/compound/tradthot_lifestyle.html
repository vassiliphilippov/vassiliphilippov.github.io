tradthot lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ træd θɑt ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / træd θɒt ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A lifestyle characterized by combining traditional or conservative feminine aesthetics with behavior intended to attract attention, often in a provocative manner, especially on social media.
provocative lifestyle blending traditional and attention-seeking elements
slang informal

Examples

Many influencers have adopted a tradthot lifestyle to increase their visibility online.
Her photos reflect a tradthot lifestyle, mixing modest dresses with suggestive poses.
Critics argue that the tradthot lifestyle promotes contradictory values in young women.

Synset Examples

His girlfriend embraced the tradthot lifestyle to attract attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egirl - a girl who uses social media to attract online attention, often with alternative or provocative aesthetic
  • Synonym: influencer lifestyle - a lifestyle focused on creating an online persona to attract followers and attention

Etymology

Blend of 'trad' (short for 'traditional') and 'thot' (slang for 'that ho over there'), with 'lifestyle'.
