pill head

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪl ˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪl ˌhɛd / (BrE)
A person who habitually consumes amphetamine pills, especially as a form of drug abuse.
habitual user of amphetamine pills
slang

Examples

After struggling with addiction for years, he finally admitted he had become a pill head.
The police warned that the neighborhood was becoming a hotspot for pill heads.
In her memoir, she spoke candidly about her past as a pill head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed freak - A person addicted to or habitually taking amphetamines
