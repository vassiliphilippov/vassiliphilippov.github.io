black horehound

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈhɔrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / blak ˈhɔː(h)aʊnd / (BrE)
A European herb with wrinkled leaves and whorls of dark purple, ill-smelling flowers (Ballota nigra).
European herb with purple, smelly flowers

Examples

The strong odor of black horehound makes it easy to identify in the wild.
Herbalists once used black horehound for digestive ailments despite its unpleasant smell.
Clusters of black horehound bloomed along the edge of the old garden wall.
