schizoid tendencies

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪt.sɔɪd tɛnˈdən.siz / (AmE), / ˈskɪt.sɔɪd tɛnˈdən.siz / (BrE)
a pattern of behavior marked by social withdrawal, emotional detachment, and little interest in forming close relationships
tendency toward social withdrawal and detachment
medical

Examples

His schizoid tendencies led him to avoid most social gatherings.
Doctors noticed that her schizoid tendencies became more pronounced over time.
The patient exhibited several schizoid tendencies, including a lack of interest in personal relationships.

Synset Examples

Her schizoid tendencies made her an introvert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schizoid - a person with a personality type characterized by emotional aloofness and solitary habits

Etymology

From schizo- (split), modeled after 'schizoid' (relating to a personality type), based on Greek skhizein (to split).
