checkered lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkərd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Fritillaria, especially Fritillaria meleagris, having nodding, bell-shaped flowers marked with a checkered pattern
plant with checkered-pattern flowers, genus Fritillaria

Examples

Each spring, the checkered lily blooms in the meadow with its striking purple and white pattern.
Gardeners often choose the checkered lily for its unique, nodding flowers and ornamental value.
The checkered lily thrives in moist soil and partial shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fritillary - any plant of the genus Fritillaria with similar bell-shaped flowers
