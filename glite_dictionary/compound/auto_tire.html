auto tire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ ˌtaɪr / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌtaɪə / (BrE)
A tire consisting of a rubber ring that fits around the rim of an automobile wheel, providing a cushion and traction for the vehicle.
a tire for an automobile wheel

Examples

The mechanic replaced the worn auto tire with a brand-new one.
You should always check your auto tire pressure before a long trip.
A sharp nail punctured my auto tire on the highway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car tire - a tire used on a car
  • Antonym: bicycle tire - a tire for a bicycle wheel
  • Antonym: truck tire - a tire for a truck wheel

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'automobile tire'. 'Tire' is from Middle English 'tyre', shortened from 'attire'.
