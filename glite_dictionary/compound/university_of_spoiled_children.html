University of Spoiled Children

1. NOUN

/ juːˈnɪvɚsəti əv spɔɪld ˈtʃɪldrən / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəti əv spɔɪld ˈtʃɪldrən / (BrE)
A derogatory nickname for the University of Southern California (USC), implying the institution caters primarily to privileged or wealthy students.
derogatory nickname for USC (University of Southern California)
offensive american

Examples

Some critics derisively call USC the University of Spoiled Children due to perceptions of student privilege.
He joked that he couldn't afford to attend the University of Spoiled Children.
The nickname University of Spoiled Children is considered offensive by many associated with USC.

Synset Examples

Critics label it the University of Spoiled Children due to its reputation.
