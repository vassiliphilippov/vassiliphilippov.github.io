fire pit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌpɪt / (BrE)
A pit or depression, especially at a volcano's crater, whose floor consists of incandescent lava.
pit with molten lava at its base

Examples

Scientists observed the fire pit glowing intensely at the bottom of the volcano.
The explorer carefully approached the fire pit to study the lava flow.
At night, the fire pit lit up the whole crater with an orange-red glow.

Synset Examples

the fire pit of the crater
