boxing ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksɪŋ rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒksɪŋ rɪŋ / (BrE)
a square area with ropes on which boxers compete in matches
square area for boxing matches

Examples

The championship fight will take place in a boxing ring at Madison Square Garden.
She stepped confidently into the boxing ring for her first professional match.
The referee inspected the boxing ring before the event began to ensure safety standards were met.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ring - enclosed area for sports or entertainment
