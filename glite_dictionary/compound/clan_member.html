clan member

1. NOUN

/ klæn ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / klæn ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a clan, especially one united by ancestry or shared interests
person belonging to a clan

Examples

Every clan member was expected to attend the annual gathering.
As a respected clan member, she helped settle many disputes within the group.
The rights of each clan member were protected under traditional laws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clansman - a male member of a clan
  • Degree Higher: clan - a group of close-knit and interrelated families
