breakfast food

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkfəst fuːd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst fuːd / (BrE)
a food customarily eaten for breakfast, such as cereal, eggs, toast, or pancakes
food typically eaten for breakfast

Examples

I prefer something light for breakfast food, like fruit and yogurt.
This restaurant serves classic breakfast foods all day long.
She always stocks her pantry with a variety of breakfast food options.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cereal - a common breakfast food made of processed grains
