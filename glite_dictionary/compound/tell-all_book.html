tell-all book

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlˌɔl ˈbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlˌɔːl ˈbʊk / (BrE)
a book in which the author reveals previously private or secret personal information, often about themselves or others
book revealing private or secret information

Examples

Her tell-all book shocked the public with its revelations about the celebrity's private life.
The politician's former aide published a tell-all book after resigning from the campaign.
Despite threats of legal action, he went ahead and released his tell-all book last year.

Synset Examples

She decided to write a tell-all book about her experiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memoir - a historical account or biography written from personal knowledge
  • Synonym: biography - an account of someone's life written by someone else
