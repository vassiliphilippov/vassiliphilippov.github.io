spider mite

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdər ˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ˌmaɪt / (BrE)
A tiny, web-spinning mite that feeds on and damages garden plants and fruit trees.
web-spinning plant pest mite

Examples

The leaves turned yellow after a spider mite infestation in the greenhouse.
A spider mite spins fine webs on the undersides of the leaves it feeds on.
Gardeners often struggle to control spider mite populations during hot, dry weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mite - a small arachnid; a tiny creature related to spiders and ticks
