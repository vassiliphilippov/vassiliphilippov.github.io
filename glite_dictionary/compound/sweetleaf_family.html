sweetleaf family

1. NOUN

/ ˈswitˌlif ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌliːf ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of dicotyledonous flowering plants in the order Ebenales, including shrubs and small trees such as sweetleaf (Symplocos).
botanical family of shrubs and small trees

Examples

The sweetleaf family includes several species of shrubs found in Asia and the Americas.
Researchers discovered a new member of the sweetleaf family in the rainforest.
Many gardeners appreciate plants from the sweetleaf family for their ornamental value.
