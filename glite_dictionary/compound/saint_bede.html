Saint Bede

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt biːd / (AmE), / seɪnt biːd / (BrE)
an English monk and scholar (672–735), also known as the Venerable Bede, celebrated for his historical and theological writings.
English monk and scholar (672–735)

Examples

Many historians regard Saint Bede as the father of English history.
The writings of Saint Bede continue to influence medieval scholarship.
I visited the monastery where Saint Bede spent much of his life.
