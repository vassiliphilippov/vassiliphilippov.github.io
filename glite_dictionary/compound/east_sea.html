East Sea

1. PROPN

/ ist si / (AmE), / iːst siː / (BrE)
the sea between Japan and the eastern coasts of Korea and Russia, also known as the Sea of Japan
sea between Japan and Korea/Russia

Examples

Many Koreans refer to the body of water as the East Sea instead of the Sea of Japan.
Disputes over the naming of the East Sea have appeared in international forums.
Ships crossed the East Sea during the research expedition last spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sea of Japan - another name for the same body of water
