dust devil

1. NOUN

/ dʌst ˈdɛvəl / (AmE), / dʌst ˈdɛvəl / (BrE)
A small, rapidly rotating column of air that lifts dust, leaves, and other debris from the ground, typically occurring in dry, sunny locations.
miniature whirlwind or air vortex

Examples

A dust devil spun across the empty field, scattering leaves and twigs into the air.
While hiking in the desert, we watched a dust devil twist its way across the sand.
Children ran after the dust devil, laughing as it picked up bits of paper from the ground.
