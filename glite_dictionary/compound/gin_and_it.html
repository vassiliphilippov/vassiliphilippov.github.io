gin and it

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪn ənd ɪt / (AmE), / dʒɪn ənd ɪt / (BrE)
a cocktail made with gin and sweet vermouth
cocktail of gin and sweet vermouth

Examples

After work, he ordered a gin and it at the bar.
During the 1920s, a gin and it was a popular choice for an evening drink.
She prefers a gin and it over a classic martini because of the sweeter vermouth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: martini - cocktail made with gin and dry vermouth

Etymology

'It' is a shortening of 'Italian vermouth'.
