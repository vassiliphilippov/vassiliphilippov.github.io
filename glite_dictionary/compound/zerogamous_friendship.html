zerogamous friendship

1. NOUN

/ zɪroʊˈɡæməs ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəʊˈɡaməs ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (BrE)
A friendship that is not characterized by any romantic or sexual commitments.
friendship without romantic or sexual commitments

Examples

Some people prefer a zerogamous friendship over romantic relationships.
She treasures her zerogamous friendship because it focuses purely on mutual support.
A zerogamous friendship can provide deep emotional connection without romantic expectations.

Synset Examples

Zerogamous friendships can be just as fulfilling.

Etymology

Formed from 'zero-' (none) and '-gamous' (related to marriage or sexual union).
