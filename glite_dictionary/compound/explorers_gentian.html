explorer's gentian

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsplɔrərz ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɔːrəz ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to western North America, with blue flowers spotted with green, sometimes growing in tufts or sprawling
a blue-flowered perennial plant of western North America

Examples

Many hikers admire the vibrant colors of the explorer's gentian in mountain meadows.
The explorer's gentian thrives in moist, rocky soils above the tree line.
Botanists catalogued several varieties of explorer's gentian during their surveys of the alpine regions.
