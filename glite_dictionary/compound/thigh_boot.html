thigh boot

1. NOUN

/ θaɪ boʊt / (AmE), / θaɪ bəʊt / (BrE)
A very high boot that extends to the thigh, often used for fishing or as a fashion item.
a very high boot reaching the thigh

Examples

The fisherman wore thigh boots to wade into the river safely.
She paired her dress with sleek black thigh boots for a bold look.
Thigh boots are ideal for keeping your legs dry when working in wet areas.
