letter carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚ ˌkæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə ˌkæriə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to deliver mail and packages to homes and businesses
person who delivers mail

Examples

The letter carrier brought several packages to our doorstep this morning.
As a letter carrier, she walks miles every day delivering mail.
Children waved to the letter carrier as he passed by their house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailman - a person who delivers mail
  • Synonym: postman - person who delivers mail (especially British English)
