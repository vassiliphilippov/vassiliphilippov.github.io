political climate

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪd.ɪ.kəl ˈklaɪ.mət / (AmE), / pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl ˈklaɪ.mət / (BrE)
the general attitudes, mood, and state of affairs relating to politics at a particular time or place
prevailing political mood or situation

Examples

The political climate in the country has become increasingly polarized over the past few years.
Businesses are closely monitoring the political climate before making new investments.
The university remained neutral despite the tense political climate surrounding the election.

Synset Examples

The political climate felt like a tinderbox waiting to ignite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the general mood or environment of a situation
