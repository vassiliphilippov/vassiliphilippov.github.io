German pancake

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝː.mən ˈpænˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜː.mən ˈpænˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A thick, puffy, mildly sweet oven-baked pancake, usually flavored with lemon and served with confectioners' sugar, jam, or fruit sauce.
oven-baked puffy pancake, often lemon-flavored

Examples

She served a delicious German pancake topped with fresh strawberries and powdered sugar for brunch.
A German pancake puffs up beautifully in the oven and deflates once removed, creating a light and tender texture.
For a special treat, I like to drizzle lemon juice over my German pancake and add a dollop of berry jam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dutch baby - another name for German pancake
  • Degree Lower: pancake - flat, thin cake made from batter and cooked on a griddle or frying pan
