order Pezizales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdɚ ˌpɛzɪˈzeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌpɛzɪˈzeɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of mostly saprophytic fungi characterized by cup-shaped ascocarps
taxonomic order of cup-shaped fungi
medical

Examples

Many species within the order Pezizales thrive on decaying organic matter in temperate forests.
Researchers collected several unusual specimens belonging to the order Pezizales during the field study.
The order Pezizales includes fungi that are distinguished by their characteristic cup-shaped fruiting bodies.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Pezizales', based on 'Peziza' (a genus of fungi) and '-ales' (indicating order).
