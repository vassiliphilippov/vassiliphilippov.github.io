road rage

1. NOUN

/ roʊd reɪdʒ / (AmE), / rəʊd reɪdʒ / (BrE)
violent or aggressive behavior exhibited by drivers towards other road users, often caused by frustration or anger in traffic
violent anger by drivers in traffic

Examples

A surge in road rage incidents has prompted new safety campaigns.
He apologized after his road rage outburst at the intersection.
Videos of road rage are often shared on social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggression - hostile or violent behavior
  • Antonym: calmness - the state of being calm or peaceful, opposite of road rage
