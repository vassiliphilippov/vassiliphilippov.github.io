object ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑb.dʒɛkt bɔl / (AmE), / ˈɒb.dʒɪkt bɔːl / (BrE)
In billiards and similar cue sports, the ball that is intended to be struck first by the cue ball.
the ball to be struck by the cue ball in billiards

Examples

He focused carefully on the object ball before taking his shot.
If the cue ball doesn't hit the object ball first, it's considered a foul.
She lined up the pool cue, aiming for the red object ball in the corner pocket.
