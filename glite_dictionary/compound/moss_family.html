moss family

1. NOUN

/ mɑs ˈfæməli / (AmE), / mɒs ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a group of related moss species classified as a family in botanical taxonomy
a group of related mosses (taxonomy)

Examples

Many biologists study the unique features found in the moss family.
The Polytrichaceae is an important moss family in northern regions.
Botanists recently discovered a new species belonging to the moss family in the rainforest.
