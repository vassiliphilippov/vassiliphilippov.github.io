big family

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈfæmɪli / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family with a large number of members, typically including several children or extended relatives
family with many members

Examples

Growing up in a big family taught me how to share and cooperate with others.
During the holidays, the house is full because our big family comes together to celebrate.
She always dreamed of having a big family with lots of children.

Synset Examples

He has four siblings, making for a big family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: large family - a family with many members
