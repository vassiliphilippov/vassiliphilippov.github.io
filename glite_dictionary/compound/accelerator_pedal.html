accelerator pedal

1. NOUN

/ əkˈsɛlɚˌreɪtɚ ˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / əkˈsɛləˌreɪtə ˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
the pedal in a vehicle that controls the speed of the engine by regulating the throttle
pedal in a vehicle to control speed

Examples

He pressed the accelerator pedal to overtake the slow-moving truck.
When you take your foot off the accelerator pedal, the car begins to slow down.
The mechanic advised me to be gentle with the accelerator pedal to improve fuel efficiency.

Synset Examples

he stepped on the gas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas pedal - alternative term for accelerator pedal
