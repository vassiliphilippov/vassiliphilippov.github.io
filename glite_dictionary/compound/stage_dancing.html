stage dancing

1. NOUN

/ steɪdʒ ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / steɪdʒ ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
artistic dancing performed before an audience, typically as part of a theatrical show
artistic theatrical dancing

Examples

The troupe specializes in stage dancing that blends classical and modern styles.
She has trained in stage dancing since childhood and has performed internationally.
Many Broadway musicals feature elaborate stage dancing sequences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballet - a highly stylized form of stage dancing
  • Synonym: modern dance - contemporary form of artistic stage dancing
