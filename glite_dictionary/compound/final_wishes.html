final wishes

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnəl ˈwɪʃɪz / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnəl ˈwɪʃɪz / (BrE)
a person's last requests or desires, especially those expressed near the end of life or regarding matters after their death
last requests or desires at end of life

Examples

She made sure to discuss her final wishes with her family before her surgery.
The lawyer asked if he had any final wishes he wanted to add to his will.
It is important to document your final wishes to avoid confusion among relatives.

Synset Examples

She shared her final wishes on her deathbed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last will - a legal document outlining final wishes regarding property after death
