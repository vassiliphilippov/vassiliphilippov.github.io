Indian rhododendron

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˌroʊdəˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˌrəʊdəˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub native to India and Southeast Asia, known for its large purple flowers
evergreen shrub with large purple flowers

Examples

The Indian rhododendron blooms each spring, filling the garden with vibrant purple flowers.
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of the Indian rhododendron in remote regions of Assam.
We planted an Indian rhododendron in our backyard to add some color and greenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rhododendron - a genus of flowering shrubs and small trees
