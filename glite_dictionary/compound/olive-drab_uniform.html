olive-drab uniform

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑlɪv dræb juˈnɪfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv dræb juˈnɪfɔːm / (BrE)
a military uniform of the United States Army, made from cloth of a dull olive color
US Army uniform of dull olive color

Examples

The soldiers marched in their olive-drab uniforms during the ceremony.
His grandfather kept an old olive-drab uniform from his days in the Army.
In the 1940s, the olive-drab uniform was standard issue for all enlisted personnel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatigues - military clothing worn for manual labor or field work

Etymology

From 'olive-drab,' referring to the dull olive-green color, and 'uniform'.
