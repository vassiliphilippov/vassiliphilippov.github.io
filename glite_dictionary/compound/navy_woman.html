navy woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvi ˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈneɪvi ˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is a member of a country's navy, especially as a uniformed service member.
female member of the navy

Examples

The navy woman received an award for her outstanding service.
During the ceremony, several navy women were promoted to higher ranks.
She was proud to be a navy woman serving her country at sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailor - member of a navy or seafaring crew
  • Antonym: navyman - a male serviceman in the navy
