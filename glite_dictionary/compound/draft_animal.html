draft animal

1. NOUN

/ dræft ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / drɑːft ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
an animal, such as a horse or ox, used for pulling heavy loads or doing farm work
animal used for pulling loads

Examples

The farmer hitched his oxen as draft animals to plow the field.
In some regions, camels serve as draft animals for transportation and agriculture.
Before the invention of tractors, horses were the most common draft animals on farms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beast of burden - an animal used for carrying loads
  • Degree Lower: pack animal - an animal used for carrying loads on its back
