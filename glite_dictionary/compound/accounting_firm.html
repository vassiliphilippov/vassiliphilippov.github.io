accounting firm

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊntɪŋ fɜrm / (AmE), / əˈkaʊntɪŋ fɜːm / (BrE)
A company that provides accounting and auditing services to clients for a fee.
company providing accounting and auditing services

Examples

She accepted a position at a prestigious accounting firm after graduating from university.
Many small businesses hire an accounting firm to handle their taxes every year.
The accounting firm was audited to ensure their practices met industry standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accountant - a person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts
