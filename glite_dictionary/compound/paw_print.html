paw print

1. NOUN

/ pɔ ˌprɪnt / (AmE), / pɔː ˌprɪnt / (BrE)
A mark or impression left by an animal's foot, especially one with claws or pads.
mark made by an animal's paw

Examples

The muddy trail was covered with paw prints left by a stray dog.
She found a paw print on the kitchen floor after her cat came in from outside.
The biologist used the paw prints to identify which animals were present in the area.

Synset Examples

He noticed a print of a paw in the mud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footprint - a mark made by a foot on a surface
