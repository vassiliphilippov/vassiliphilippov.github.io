leather carp

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛðər kɑrp / (AmE), / ˈlɛðə kɑːp / (BrE)
A variety of domestic carp with no scales or only a few scales, distinguished by its smooth skin.
scaleless variety of domestic carp

Examples

The leather carp is prized in some regions for its smooth, almost scaleless body.
Unlike other domesticated carp, the leather carp lacks most of its scales.
Farm ponds often contain leather carp alongside other varieties such as mirror carp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mirror carp - a variety of carp with irregular, patchy scales
