lying supine

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪɪŋ səˈpaɪn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪɪŋ sjʊˈpaɪn / (BrE)
Lying on one's back, facing upward.
lying on one's back

Examples

After the race, she rested lying supine on the grass, staring at the clouds.
The doctor asked the patient to remain lying supine for the examination.
He watched the stars while lying supine on the warm sand.

Synset Examples

After the workout, he felt great lying supine on the mat.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lying prone - lying on one's front, face downward

Etymology

From Latin 'supinus' (bent backward, lying on the back).
