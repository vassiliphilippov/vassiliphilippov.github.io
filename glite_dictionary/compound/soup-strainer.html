soup-strainer

1. NOUN

/ suːp ˈstreɪnər / (AmE), / suːp ˈstreɪnə / (BrE)
(slang) A mustache, especially a large or bushy one that might catch food or soup.
slang term for a mustache
slang

Examples

I couldn't help but notice the impressive soup-strainer he was sporting at the party.
After growing his soup-strainer, he had to wipe his mouth more carefully after meals.
That old photo shows my grandfather with a thick soup-strainer above his lip.
