German-speaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɝːmən ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to speak or communicate in the German language; relating to people or regions that use German as a spoken language.
able to speak German or using German language

Examples

Many German-speaking tourists visit the city each year.
She grew up in a German-speaking household.
The conference attracted attendees from several German-speaking countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English-speaking - able to speak or communicate in English
