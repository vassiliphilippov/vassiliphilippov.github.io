music school

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk skuːl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk skuːl / (BrE)
a school where students are taught music, either as the main focus or as a specialized subject
a school focusing on music education

Examples

She has been attending a music school since she was six years old.
The music school offers lessons in piano, violin, and voice.
Many famous musicians trained at a prestigious music school in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservatory - a college for the study of classical music or other arts

2. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk skuːl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk skuːl / (BrE)
a school where students study music, often offering classes in performance, theory, and composition
school for the study of music

Examples

She attended a prestigious music school to study piano and composition.
Many talented musicians start their training at a local music school.
The music school offers after-school programs for children of all ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservatory - a college for the study of classical music or other arts
  • Synonym: art school - a school specializing in visual or performing arts
