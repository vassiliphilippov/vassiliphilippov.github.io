to build character

1. VERB

/ bɪld ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / bɪld ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
to develop moral and personal qualities such as integrity, resilience, or discipline through experience or challenge
develop moral or personal strengths

Examples

Many people believe that overcoming adversity can build character and inner strength.
Community service is often recommended as a way to build character in teenagers.
Losing gracefully in competition can help children build character and resilience.

Synset Examples

Traveling can help build character and broaden your perspective.
Sports can teach discipline and build character in children.
Volunteering at the shelter helps build character in young people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: develop character - to cultivate personal and moral qualities
