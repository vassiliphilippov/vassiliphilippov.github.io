bullyboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊliˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʊliˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a man who acts in an aggressive, intimidating way, especially as an agent for a political group or cause
aggressive or intimidating man, often in politics

Examples

The politician used a group of bullyboys to intimidate voters at the polling station.
Several bullyboys stormed into the meeting, shouting and threatening the organizers.
He acts like a bullyboy whenever he doesn't get his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially one involved in crime
  • Synonym: henchman - a faithful follower or supporter, especially one prepared to engage in crime or violence by way of service
