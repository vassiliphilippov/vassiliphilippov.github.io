reckless driving

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛkləs ˈdraɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkləs ˈdraɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of operating a vehicle without regard for the safety of people or property.
dangerous or careless driving

Examples

He was arrested for reckless driving after speeding through the intersection.
The new law aims to reduce reckless driving by increasing penalties.
Reckless driving is a leading cause of road accidents in many countries.

Synset Examples

His reckless driving became a liability for the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: careless driving - driving a vehicle with less than the proper amount of care and attention
