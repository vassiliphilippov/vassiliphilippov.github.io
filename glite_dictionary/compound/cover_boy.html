cover boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A young man or boy whose image appears on the front cover of a magazine, especially as a model representing attractiveness or fashion.
male model featured on a magazine cover

Examples

The fashion magazine chose a new cover boy for its summer issue.
He dreamed of becoming a cover boy after winning the modeling competition.
As a teenager, he was discovered and soon became a popular cover boy in Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover girl - female model featured on a magazine cover

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'cover girl' (originally 20th century); 'cover' refers to magazines' front pages.
