dinosaur of a car

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr əv ə kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnəsɔːr əv ə kɑː / (BrE)
an old or outdated car, especially one that is considered large, inefficient, or obsolete
an old or outdated car

Examples

He finally traded in his dinosaur of a car for something more reliable.
Everyone could hear the engine of that dinosaur of a car coming down the street.
Her first vehicle was such a dinosaur of a car that it barely started in the morning.

Synset Examples

He still drives that dinosaur of a car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clunker - an old, run-down car
  • Synonym: old banger - an old, worn-out car (British English)

Etymology

Metaphoric use of 'dinosaur' for something outdated; 'dinosaur' from Greek deinos ('terrible') + sauros ('lizard').
