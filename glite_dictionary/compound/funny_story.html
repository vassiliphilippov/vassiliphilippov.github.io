funny story

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an account of an amusing or humorous incident, often including a punchline
an amusing anecdote

Examples

She told a funny story at the dinner table and everyone laughed.
Whenever Mark starts a funny story, you know it's going to end with a punchline.
The comedian opened his set with a funny story about his childhood.

Synset Examples

she told a funny story
she made a funny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joke - something said or done to cause amusement or laughter
  • Synonym: anecdote - a short and amusing or interesting story about a real incident or person
