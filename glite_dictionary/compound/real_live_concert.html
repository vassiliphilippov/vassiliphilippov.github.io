real live concert

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəl ˈlaɪv ˈkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ˌrɪəl ˈlaɪv ˈkɒnsət / (BrE)
an actual in-person musical performance, as opposed to a recorded or virtual event
in-person musical performance

Examples

After two years of cancellations, it felt amazing to be at a real live concert again.
Tickets for the real live concert sold out within minutes of going on sale.
Many fans prefer the energy of a real live concert over watching virtual performances online.

Synset Examples

We attended a real live concert last night.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: virtual concert - a musical performance delivered via the internet, not in-person
