Vandyke brown

1. NOUN

/ vænˌdaɪk ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / vanˌdaɪk ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
a deep, moderate brown color used in painting and artistic work
a deep, moderate brown color

Examples

The artist mixed Vandyke brown with other colors to achieve a natural effect.
The frame was stained in Vandyke brown to match the vintage décor.
She prefers Vandyke brown for shading in her portraits.

Etymology

Named after the painter Anthony van Dyck, who frequently used the color.
