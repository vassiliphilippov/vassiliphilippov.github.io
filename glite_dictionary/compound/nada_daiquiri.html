NADA daiquiri

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːdə daɪˈkɪri / (AmE), / ˈnɑːdə daɪˈkɪəri / (BrE)
A non-alcoholic version of a daiquiri, made with fruit juice and other ingredients but without alcohol.
non-alcoholic daiquiri cocktail
american

Examples

She ordered a NADA daiquiri at the bar so she could enjoy the taste without the alcohol.
The restaurant's NADA daiquiri was just as refreshing as the classic version.
At the party, many guests chose a NADA daiquiri for a fun, alcohol-free option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mocktail - a non-alcoholic mixed drink
  • Degree Higher: daiquiri - an alcoholic cocktail made with rum, citrus juice, and sugar

Etymology

NADA as an acronym for 'No Alcohol Drinks Allowed' in American English, combined with 'daiquiri', a cocktail originally from Cuba.
