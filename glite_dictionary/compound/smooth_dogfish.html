smooth dogfish

1. NOUN

/ smuːð ˈdɔgˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / smuːð ˈdɒgˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, bottom-dwelling shark (Mustelus canis) found along the Atlantic coasts of North and South America
a small Atlantic coastal shark

Examples

The smooth dogfish is commonly found in shallow coastal waters along the eastern United States.
Fishermen often catch smooth dogfish by accident while looking for other species.
The skin of the smooth dogfish is notably smooth compared to other sharks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogfish - a small shark of several species
