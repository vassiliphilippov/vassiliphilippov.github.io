leave of absence

1. NOUN

/ liv əv ˈæbsəns / (AmE), / liːv əv ˈæbsəns / (BrE)
a period of time when an employee or student is officially allowed to be away from work or duty
authorized time away from work or duty

Examples

She took a leave of absence from her job to care for her family.
The university granted him a leave of absence for medical reasons.
After her leave of absence, she returned to work with renewed energy.

Synset Examples

a ten day's leave to visit his mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sabbatical - a period of paid leave granted for study or travel
  • Degree Lower: break - a short period of rest or interruption from duty
  • Degree Higher: retirement - permanent withdrawal from work or duty
