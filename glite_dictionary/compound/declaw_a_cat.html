to declaw a cat

1. VERB

/ diˈklɔː ə kæt / (AmE), / diˈklɔː ə kæt / (BrE)
to remove a cat's claws, typically by surgical means
surgically remove a cat's claws

Examples

It is now illegal in some countries to declaw a cat.
Many animal rights groups argue that it is inhumane to declaw a cat.
Some owners regret their decision to declaw a cat after learning about possible complications.

Synset Examples

It's controversial to declaw cats, as it alters their behavior.
Many pet owners choose to declaw their cats for safety.
Veterinarians often advise against declawing cats for ethical reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to declaw - to remove the claws of an animal, especially a cat

Etymology

From 'de-' (removal) + 'claw' + 'cat'.
