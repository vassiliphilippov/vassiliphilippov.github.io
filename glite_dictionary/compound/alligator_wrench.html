alligator wrench

1. NOUN

/ ˈæləˌɡeɪtər rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtə rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a wrench with a V-shaped jaw and serrations on one side, resembling the open jaws of an alligator, used for gripping and turning objects
tool with V-shaped, serrated jaw

Examples

He used an alligator wrench to loosen the rusted pipe fitting.
The mechanic reached for the alligator wrench in his toolbox.
Unlike other tools, an alligator wrench grips objects with its serrated jaw.

Etymology

Named for its resemblance to an alligator's open jaws.
