fire station

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A building where firefighters and fire engines are based and from which they operate.
building for firefighters and fire engines

Examples

The town's fire station is located near the city center.
All the local firefighters gathered at the fire station for training.
A new fire station will be built to replace the old one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firehouse - Another term for fire station, especially in North America
