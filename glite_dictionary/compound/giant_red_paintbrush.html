giant red paintbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt rɛd ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt rɛd ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a wildflower native to western North America, characterized by ragged clusters of bright crimson or scarlet flowers
wildflower of western North America with crimson flowers

Examples

We spotted a giant red paintbrush growing beside the hiking trail.
The meadow was dotted with blooming giant red paintbrushes in early summer.
Many photographers are drawn to the vibrant hues of the giant red paintbrush.
