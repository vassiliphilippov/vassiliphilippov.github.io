hired hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪɚd hænd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd hænd / (BrE)
a person employed to do manual labor, especially on a farm or ranch
farm or ranch laborer for hire

Examples

The hired hand repaired the fence after the storm damaged it.
They relied on a hired hand to help with the harvest each year.
As a hired hand, he lived in a small cabin near the barn.

Synset Examples

the hired hand fixed the railing
a ranch hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranch hand - a laborer working on a ranch
  • Synonym: farmhand - a person employed to work on a farm
