fuel level

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuəl ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
the amount of fuel remaining in a tank or system
amount of fuel remaining

Examples

The fuel level in the tank was dangerously low after the long drive.
Can you check the fuel level before we leave for the trip?
Modern cars often display the fuel level digitally on the dashboard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel gauge - an instrument for measuring fuel level
