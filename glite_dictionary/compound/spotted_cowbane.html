spotted cowbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑːtɪd ˈkaʊˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈkaʊˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a tall, poisonous plant of northeastern North America with purple-spotted stems and clusters of toxic tuberous roots resembling small sweet potatoes
poisonous North American plant

Examples

The wet meadows were thick with spotted cowbane, which is highly toxic to livestock.
Naturalists warn hikers to avoid touching spotted cowbane when exploring marshy areas.
The roots of spotted cowbane can be fatal if mistakenly eaten.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water hemlock - a group of extremely poisonous plants of the genus Cicuta
  • Synonym: cowbane - a common name for several poisonous plants of the genus Cicuta
