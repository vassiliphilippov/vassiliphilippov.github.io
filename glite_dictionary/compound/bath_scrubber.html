bath scrubber

1. NOUN

/ bæθ ˈskrʌbər / (AmE), / bɑːθ ˈskrʌbə / (BrE)
A washing implement, typically made of nylon mesh, used to wash and scrub the body while bathing or showering.
tool for scrubbing the body in the bath or shower

Examples

I use a bath scrubber every night in the shower to exfoliate my skin.
The nylon mesh of the bath scrubber helps create a rich lather with soap.
After a week of travel, she was grateful for a hot bath and her favorite bath scrubber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loofah - a natural sponge used for bathing and scrubbing the skin
  • Synonym: sponge - a soft, porous material used for washing the body
