high fidelity sound system

1. NOUN

/ haɪ fəˈdɛlɪti saʊnd ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / haɪ fɪˈdɛlɪti saʊnd ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
An audio system designed to reproduce sound with very high accuracy and minimal distortion.
audio system with high accuracy

Examples

He upgraded to a high fidelity sound system to enjoy his music collection with better clarity.
The high fidelity sound system filled the room with rich, clear audio during the movie night.
Many audiophiles invest in a high fidelity sound system for the best possible listening experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stereo system - audio equipment with two or more channels for sound reproduction

Etymology

High fidelity from Latin 'fidelitas' (faithfulness), used in audio since 1930s.
