hotel plan

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl plæn / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl plæn / (BrE)
a pricing arrangement at a hotel that includes accommodation and specified meals for guests
type of hotel pricing with meals included

Examples

When booking, you can choose a hotel plan that includes breakfast and dinner.
The resort offers several hotel plan options depending on the number of meals you want.
She compared each hotel plan carefully to find the one that best suited her needs.
