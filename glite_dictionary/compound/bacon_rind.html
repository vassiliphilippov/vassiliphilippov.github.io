bacon rind

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkən raɪnd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən raɪnd / (BrE)
the outer skin or layer of bacon, often removed before cooking or used to add flavor to dishes
outer skin of bacon

Examples

Please remove the bacon rind before adding the meat to the stew.
Crispy bacon rind can be a delicious snack or garnish.
He bought some bacon rind to flavor his homemade beans.
