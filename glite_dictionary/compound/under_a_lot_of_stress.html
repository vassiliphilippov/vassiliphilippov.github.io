under a lot of stress

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndɚ ə lɔt əv strɛs / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ə lɒt əv strɛs / (BrE)
experiencing significant emotional or psychological pressure or tension, often due to demanding situations
experiencing significant emotional pressure

Examples

She's been under a lot of stress at work lately, trying to meet all the deadlines.
When people are under a lot of stress, it's important to find healthy ways to cope.
He became ill after being under a lot of stress during the move.

Synset Examples

In his defense, he was under a lot of stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under pressure - experiencing stress or demands from external factors
  • Antonym: relaxed - free from stress or anxiety
