Mount Rushmore

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ˈrʌʃ.mɔr / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈrʌʃ.mɔː / (BrE)
a mountain in South Dakota featuring the carved faces of four U.S. presidents: Washington, Jefferson, Lincoln, and Roosevelt
monumental mountain with presidents' faces, USA

Examples

Many tourists visit Mount Rushmore each year to see the famous presidential carvings.
The construction of Mount Rushmore took over a decade to complete.
In school, we learned about the history behind Mount Rushmore and its significance to American culture.
