nomad lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊ.mæd ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.mad ˈlaɪf.staɪl / (BrE)
a way of life characterized by frequent travel and not settling permanently in one place
constantly traveling way of life

Examples

Many people are attracted to the nomad lifestyle because of its sense of freedom.
After quitting his job, he embraced a nomad lifestyle and began exploring new countries each month.
The rise of remote work has made the nomad lifestyle more accessible than ever before.

Synset Examples

The nomad lifestyle suits those who love adventure.
They live a nomadic life, moving from place to place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nomadic lifestyle - lifestyle of moving from place to place
