soft flag

1. NOUN

/ sɔft flæɡ / (AmE), / sɒft flæɡ / (BrE)
a common name for plants of the genus Typha, also known as reed maces or cattails, found in wetlands of America, Europe, North Africa, and Asia
plant; Typha/reed mace/cattail

Examples

The wetland was full of soft flag and other aquatic plants.
Biologists studied the growth patterns of soft flag along the riverbank.
Early settlers sometimes used the leaves of soft flag for weaving mats and baskets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cattail - another common name for Typha plants
