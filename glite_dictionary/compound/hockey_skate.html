hockey skate

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɒki skeɪt / (BrE)
an ice skate designed for playing hockey, featuring a short blade and a strong boot to protect the feet and ankles
ice skate for hockey, with strong boot

Examples

He quickly laced up his hockey skate before heading onto the rink.
The design of a hockey skate offers extra support for sudden stops and turns.
Can you recommend a good brand of hockey skate for beginners?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice skate - a boot with a blade for skating on ice
