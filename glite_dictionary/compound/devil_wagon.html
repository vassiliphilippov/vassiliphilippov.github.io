devil wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəl ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
A vehicle, ride, or contraption that moves dangerously fast or recklessly.
fast or dangerous ride or vehicle

Examples

The kids built a makeshift devil wagon and took turns racing down the driveway.
He sped through the neighborhood in his old devil wagon, startling everyone he passed.
They warned us not to ride the devil wagon without helmets or safety gear.

Synset Examples

They rode the devil wagon down the steep hill, laughing all the way.
