European mountain ash

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈmaʊntən æʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈmaʊntɪn æʃ / (BrE)
A small Eurasian tree (Sorbus aucuparia) with pinnate leaves and clusters of orange-red berrylike fruits.
Eurasian tree with orange-red berries

Examples

The berries of the European mountain ash attract many birds during the autumn months.
We planted a European mountain ash in our garden for its attractive foliage and fruit.
In folklore, branches of the European mountain ash were thought to offer protection from evil spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rowan - Eurasian tree with pinnate leaves and bright red berries
