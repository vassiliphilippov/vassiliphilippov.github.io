rara avis

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɑːrə ˈeɪvɪs / (AmE), / ˌrɑːrə ˈɑːvɪs / (BrE)
A rare or unique person or thing.
rare or unique person or thing

Examples

She is a true rara avis in the academic world, combining brilliance and humility.
Finding such integrity in politics is a rara avis these days.
He was considered a rara avis—an artist untouched by commercial pressures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rarity - something that is rare

Etymology

From Latin 'rara avis', meaning 'rare bird'.
