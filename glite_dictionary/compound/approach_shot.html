approach shot

1. NOUN

/ əˈproʊtʃ ʃɑt / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ ʃɒt / (BrE)
(in golf) a relatively short shot intended to land the ball on the putting green.
golf shot to land ball on green

Examples

After a perfect drive, she hit an approach shot that landed just feet from the hole.
His approach shot was too strong and rolled past the putting green.
Choosing the right club for an approach shot is crucial in professional golf.

Synset Examples

he lost the hole when his approach rolled over the green

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chip shot - a short, low golf shot played from near the green
  • Synonym: putt - a gentle golf stroke on the green to roll the ball in the hole
