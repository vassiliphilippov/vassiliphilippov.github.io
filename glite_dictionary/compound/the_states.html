the States

1. NOUN

/ ðə steɪts / (AmE), / ðə steɪts / (BrE)
An informal term for the United States of America, especially used by people from outside the country.
informal term for the USA
informal british

Examples

She spent a year studying in the States before returning home.
Many British musicians dream of making it big in the States.
I'm planning a holiday to the States next spring to visit my cousins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United States - the country commonly referred to as 'the States'
