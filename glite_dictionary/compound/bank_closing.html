bank closing

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ˈkloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈkləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the shutting down of a bank, usually due to financial problems or insolvency
closure of a bank due to financial crisis

Examples

The sudden bank closing left many customers unable to access their savings.
Government officials investigated the reasons behind the bank closing last Friday.
The city was shocked by the unexpected bank closing, which affected hundreds of local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank failure - a bank's collapse due to inability to meet obligations
