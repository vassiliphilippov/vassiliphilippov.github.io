sea chub

1. NOUN

/ si tʃʌb / (AmE), / si tʃʌb / (BrE)
a schooling fish found mostly in the Indian and western Pacific oceans, with some species also present in the western Atlantic
a type of schooling fish found in tropical oceans

Examples

The sea chub is often seen swimming in large groups near coral reefs.
Fishermen in the Pacific region sometimes catch sea chub while netting other species.
Some sea chubs have adapted to brackish waters as well as marine environments.
