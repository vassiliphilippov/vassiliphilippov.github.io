genus Fregata

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs frɪˈɡeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs frɪˈɡɑːtə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Fregatidae, comprising frigatebirds.
frigatebird genus

Examples

The genus Fregata includes some of the most skilled aerial seabirds in the world.
Male birds of the genus Fregata are known for their distinctive red throat pouches.
Researchers observed nesting behavior unique to the genus Fregata on the remote island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frigatebird - a large tropical seabird of the genus Fregata

Etymology

From Latin 'fregata' (frigate), used in taxonomy for these seabirds.
