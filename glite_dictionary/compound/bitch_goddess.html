bitch goddess

1. NOUN

/ bɪtʃ ˈɡɑːdəs / (AmE), / bɪtʃ ˈɡɒdɪs / (BrE)
A powerful, assertive woman who is often regarded as domineering or intimidating, especially in contexts such as fashion or business.
powerful, domineering woman (esp. fashion/business)
informal offensive

Examples

She quickly became known as the bitch goddess in her fiercely competitive industry.
Some people admired her success, while others called her a bitch goddess behind her back.
Despite the negativity, she proudly owned her reputation as the bitch goddess of the fashion world.

Synset Examples
