soybean flour

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌbin flaʊr / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
flour made by grinding soybeans, used in baking and cooking as a source of protein and as a gluten-free alternative to wheat flour.
flour made from ground soybeans

Examples

Bakers often add soybean flour to bread recipes to increase protein content.
Gluten-free cookies made with soybean flour have a nutty flavor and moist texture.
Using soybean flour is a good alternative for those who are allergic to wheat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soy flour - flour made from ground soybeans
