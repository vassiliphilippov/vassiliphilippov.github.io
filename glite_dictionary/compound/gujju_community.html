Gujju community

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʊdʒu kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈɡʊdʒu kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
the community of people of Gujarati descent, especially those living outside Gujarat
Gujarati ethnic community
informal

Examples

The Gujju community in London hosts an annual Diwali celebration that attracts hundreds of attendees.
Many members of the Gujju community speak both Gujarati and English fluently.
The Gujju community has made significant contributions to local businesses in the city.

Synset Examples

The Gujju community organized a festival to celebrate their culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gujarati community - community of Gujarati people

Etymology

'Gujju' is an informal term derived from 'Gujarati,' referring to people from Gujarat, India.
