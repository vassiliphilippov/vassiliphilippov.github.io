vegetable patch

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl pætʃ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl patʃ / (BrE)
a small area of ground in a garden where vegetables are grown
small garden area for vegetables

Examples

She spends an hour every morning tending to her vegetable patch.
This year, we planted tomatoes and carrots in our vegetable patch.
The children learned about growing food by helping out in the school’s vegetable patch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitchen garden - a garden where vegetables, herbs, and fruit are grown for household use
