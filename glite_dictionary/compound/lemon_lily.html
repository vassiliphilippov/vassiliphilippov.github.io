lemon lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛm.ən ˈlɪl.i / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.ən ˈlɪl.i / (BrE)
A species of day lily with fragrant yellow flowers, native to eastern North America.
yellow-flowered day lily

Examples

A single lemon lily brightened the corner of the garden with its delicate yellow petals.
The lemon lily thrives in moist meadows and woodlands in spring and early summer.
Botanists studied the fragrance compounds produced by the lemon lily.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day lily - any plant of the genus Hemerocallis, known for its edible flowers and day-blooming blooms
