golden chain

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a small ornamental tree or shrub of the genus Laburnum, known for its clusters of bright yellow flowers
ornamental tree or shrub with yellow flowers

Examples

The golden chain in our garden is covered with vivid yellow blossoms every spring.
Many people use branches of golden chain to decorate their homes for Easter.
The poisonous seeds of the golden chain make it important to keep children away from the plant.
