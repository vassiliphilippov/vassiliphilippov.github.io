Purple Heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl hɑːrt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl hɑːt / (BrE)
A United States military decoration awarded to any member of the armed forces who is wounded or killed in action.
U.S. military award for wounded or killed in action
american

Examples

He was awarded the Purple Heart after being wounded in combat.
She displayed her late grandfather's Purple Heart on the mantelpiece with pride.
Many veterans honor those who received the Purple Heart for their bravery.
