movie industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business of producing, distributing, and exhibiting films for public entertainment
business of making and distributing films

Examples

Many people dream of working in the movie industry as actors or directors.
The movie industry generates billions of dollars each year worldwide.
Technology has rapidly changed how the movie industry produces and distributes films.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: film industry - the business of making movies
