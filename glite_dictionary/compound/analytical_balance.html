analytical balance

1. NOUN

/ əˌnæl.ɪˈtɪk.əl ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / əˌnæl.ɪˈtɪk.əl ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
a highly precise balance used in laboratories, especially for quantitative chemical analysis
precise laboratory weighing instrument

Examples

The laboratory technician carefully weighed the sample on the analytical balance.
Every university chemistry lab is equipped with at least one analytical balance for precise measurements.
Before using the analytical balance, she calibrated it to ensure accurate results.
