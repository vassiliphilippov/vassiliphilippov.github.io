daily smoko

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli ˈsmoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˈsməʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a regular break taken each day for refreshments, smoking, or relaxation, especially at work
regular daily break for snacks or relaxation
informal british

Examples

Most workers look forward to their daily smoko as a chance to relax.
During their daily smoko, the team usually discusses the day's news.
He always brings his own biscuits to enjoy with his daily smoko.

Synset Examples

He enjoys a good chat during his daily smoko.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoko - a break from work for snacks or a cigarette

Etymology

From Australian/New Zealand English 'smoko', meaning a break for smoking or refreshment, combined with 'daily'.
