armoured car

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmɚd kɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd kɑː / (BrE)
a strong vehicle with armor plating, used to safely transport money or valuable items
vehicle for secure transport of valuables

Examples

Security guards loaded the money into the armoured car before driving to the bank.
The armoured car was parked outside the jewelry store for extra protection.
A new fleet of armoured cars was purchased to ensure the safe delivery of valuables.

Synset Examples

the paintings were delivered to the museum in an air-conditioned armored car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored truck - vehicle for transporting valuables, especially money

2. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.mɚd kɑːr / (AmE), / ˈɑː.məd kɑː / (BrE)
A military combat vehicle with wheels, light armor, and usually armed with a machine gun.
military wheeled vehicle with light armor

Examples

The convoy was escorted by an armoured car equipped with a mounted machine gun.
During the battle, the armoured car provided covering fire for the infantry.
Most armoured cars are designed for speed and reconnaissance rather than direct assault.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: tank - heavily armored and armed military vehicle on tracks

Etymology

From 'armoured' (protected with armor) and 'car' (vehicle), reflecting its purpose as a lightly armored, mobile combat vehicle.
