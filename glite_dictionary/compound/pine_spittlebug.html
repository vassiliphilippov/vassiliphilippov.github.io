pine spittlebug

1. NOUN

/ paɪn ˈspɪtəlˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / paɪn ˈspɪtəlˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a North American insect that feeds on pine trees, often producing frothy spittle-like masses on the plant
North American pest insect on pines

Examples

The pine spittlebug can cause significant damage to pine trees in forested areas.
We found several masses of frothy foam, a sign of the pine spittlebug, on the branches.
Foresters monitor pine spittlebug infestations to protect young pine plantations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spittlebug - insect that produces frothy spittle masses, family Cercopidae
