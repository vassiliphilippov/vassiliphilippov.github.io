intercom speaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntərˌkɑm ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˈɪntəkɒm ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
the loudspeaker component of an intercom or public address system, used for transmitting sound or announcements
loudspeaker on an intercom system

Examples

The principal's announcement came through the intercom speaker in every classroom.
She heard her name called over the intercom speaker as she waited in the lobby.
A crackling sound on the intercom speaker signaled that someone was about to speak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loudspeaker - an apparatus that converts electrical signals into sound
  • Antonym: microphone - a device that converts sound into electrical signals
