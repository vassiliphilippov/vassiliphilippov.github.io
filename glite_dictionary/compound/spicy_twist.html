spicy twist

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsi twɪst / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi twɪst / (BrE)
an unexpected or distinctive spicy flavor added to a dish or recipe
unexpected addition of spicy flavor

Examples

The chef gave the classic pasta sauce a spicy twist by adding chili flakes.
Her chocolate cake had a spicy twist with a hint of cayenne pepper.
These chips come with a spicy twist that fans of bold flavors will appreciate.

Synset Examples

He likes to give curry a spicy twist.
