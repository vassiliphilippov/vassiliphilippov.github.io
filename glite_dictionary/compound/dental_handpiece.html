dental handpiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl ˈhændˌpis / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl ˈhændˌpiːs / (BrE)
a powered dental instrument that holds and rotates disks, cups, or burs for preparing, contouring, cleaning, or polishing teeth or restorations
powered dental instrument for dental procedures
medical

Examples

The dentist picked up the dental handpiece to begin polishing the patient's teeth.
Modern dental handpieces offer variable speeds for different procedures.
Sterilizing the dental handpiece is essential to prevent cross-contamination.
