poker-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊkər feɪst / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə feɪst / (BrE)
deliberately showing no emotion, especially to avoid revealing thoughts or feelings
showing no emotion; expressionless

Examples

She remained poker-faced throughout the entire meeting, betraying no reaction to the news.
Even when he won the lottery, his poker-faced demeanor gave nothing away.
The comedian told the joke with a completely poker-faced expression, making it even funnier.

Synset Examples

deadpan humor
his face remained expressionless as the verdict was read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadpan - deliberately impassive or expressionless
  • Synonym: expressionless - without expression or emotion on the face

Etymology

From the practice in the card game poker of keeping a neutral facial expression to hide emotions or intentions.
