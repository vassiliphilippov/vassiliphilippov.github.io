class fellow

1. NOUN

/ klæs ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / klɑːs ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
a person who attends the same class as you at school, college, or university
person in same class as you

Examples

He became good friends with his class fellow during their first year at university.
Each class fellow was invited to the reunion ten years after graduation.
She helped her class fellow catch up on the assignments after he was absent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classmate - person who is in the same class at school or college
