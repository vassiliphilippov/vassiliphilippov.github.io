railroad man

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌroʊd mæn / (AmE), / ˈreɪlrəʊd man / (BrE)
A man who is employed to work on or for a railroad, especially in operational or maintenance roles.
male railway employee

Examples

His grandfather was a proud railroad man for nearly forty years.
The railroad man checked every car before the train left the station.
Many towns were built up because a railroad man decided to lay new tracks there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railwayman - a man employed to work for a railway
  • Synonym: railroader - a person employed by a railroad
