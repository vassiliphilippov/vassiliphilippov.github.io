ex-spouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksˌspaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɛksˌspaʊs / (BrE)
a person to whom one was formerly married; a former husband or wife
a former husband or wife

Examples

After the divorce, she maintained a friendly relationship with her ex-spouse.
The lawyer advised him to communicate with his ex-spouse only in writing.
Many people attend family events with their children and their ex-spouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ex-husband - a man who was formerly a husband
  • Synonym: ex-wife - a woman who was formerly a wife

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'ex-' (Latin 'ex-', meaning 'out of, from, former') and 'spouse' (from Latin 'sponsus', betrothed).
