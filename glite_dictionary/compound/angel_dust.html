angel dust

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl dʌst / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl dʌst / (BrE)
A slang term for the drug phencyclidine (PCP), originally used as a veterinary anesthetic, taken illicitly for its hallucinogenic effects.
slang for phencyclidine (PCP), hallucinogenic drug
slang

Examples

The police found a bag of angel dust hidden in the suspect's jacket.
She was hospitalized after taking angel dust at a party, experiencing severe hallucinations.
Years ago, angel dust was sometimes used as an anesthetic for animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PCP - phencyclidine, chemical name for angel dust
