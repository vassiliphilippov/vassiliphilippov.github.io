to clear (someone's) inbox

1. VERB

/ klɪr maɪ ˈɪn.bɑks / (AmE), / klɪə maɪ ˈɪn.bɒks / (BrE)
to remove, deal with, or respond to all emails in one's electronic inbox so it is empty or manageable
remove or respond to all emails in inbox

Examples

I always try to clear my inbox before the end of the workday.
She spent her lunch break trying to clear her inbox of unread messages.
It's a great feeling to finally clear your inbox after a busy week.

Synset Examples

I need to clear my inbox before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empty (someone's) inbox - remove all emails from the inbox
