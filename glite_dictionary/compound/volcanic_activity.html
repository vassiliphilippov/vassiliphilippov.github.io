volcanic activity

1. NOUN

/ vɑlˈkænɪk ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪk ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the process or occurrence of eruptions and other geological phenomena involving volcanoes
eruptions and processes involving volcanoes

Examples

Scientists monitor volcanic activity to predict possible eruptions and protect local communities.
Recent volcanic activity has resulted in the formation of new islands in the region.
The increase in volcanic activity caused ash clouds to disrupt air travel.

Synset Examples

The region is known for its volcanic activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eruption - a violent discharge of material from a volcano
