horned owl

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd aʊl / (AmE), / hɔːnd aʊl / (BrE)
a large owl characterized by prominent ear tufts, found in various regions and including species such as the great horned owl
large owl with ear tufts

Examples

The horned owl is known for its distinctive feather tufts that look like ears.
We spotted a horned owl perched silently on a tree branch at dusk.
Several species of horned owl inhabit forests across North America.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: great horned owl - a specific species of horned owl native to the Americas
