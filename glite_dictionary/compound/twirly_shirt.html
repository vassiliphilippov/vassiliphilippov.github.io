twirly shirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɝːli ʃɝːt / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːli ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A shirt designed to twirl or spin outward when the wearer spins, often brightly colored and favored by children or in playful contexts.
shirt that twirls when spun

Examples

She loved spinning in her new twirly shirt at the party.
A pink twirly shirt is her favorite thing to wear on sunny days.
The shop displayed several colorful twirly shirts near the window.

Synset Examples

He spun in his twirly shirt, feeling carefree.
