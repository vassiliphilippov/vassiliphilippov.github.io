Isle of Man

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪl əv ˈmæn / (AmE), / ˈaɪl əv ˈmæn / (BrE)
an island in the Irish Sea between Great Britain and Ireland, known for its self-governing status and annual motorcycle races
self-governing island in the Irish Sea

Examples

The Isle of Man is famous for its annual TT motorcycle races, attracting visitors from around the world.
She spent her summer holiday exploring the scenic coastline of the Isle of Man.
The government of the Isle of Man maintains its own legal and political systems, separate from the United Kingdom.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Mǫn' or Manx Gaelic 'Ellan Vannin', meaning 'island of Man'.
