business department

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs dɪˌpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a division or section of a business firm responsible for a particular function or area
section of a business firm for a specific function

Examples

She works in the business department handling client accounts and contracts.
Many companies have a separate business department dedicated to marketing and sales.
The university's business department offers courses in management and finance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of an organization focusing on a specific activity
