alphabetic character

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.fəˈbɛ.tɪk ˈkɛr.ək.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌæl.fəˈbɛt.ɪk ˈkæ.rək.tə / (BrE)
A letter or symbol used as one of the basic written elements in an alphabet to represent sounds of speech.
letter of the alphabet

Examples

Each alphabetic character corresponds to a particular sound in the language.
Children learn to write by practicing each alphabetic character repeatedly.
The password must include at least one alphabetic character and one number.

Synset Examples

his grandmother taught him his letters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter - A single symbol in an alphabet, especially as a unit of written language.
