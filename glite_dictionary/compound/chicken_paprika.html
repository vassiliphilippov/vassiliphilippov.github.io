chicken paprika

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən păˌpriːkə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn pəˈpriːkə / (BrE)
A dish consisting of chicken simmered in broth with onions and paprika, often finished with sour cream.
chicken dish with paprika and sour cream

Examples

We ordered the chicken paprika at the restaurant, and it was full of flavor.
Her grandmother taught her how to prepare traditional chicken paprika with lots of onions.
The recipe calls for simmering the chicken paprika until the meat is tender and juicy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paprikash - A Hungarian dish made with meat and paprika, often with sour cream

Etymology

From chicken + paprika, the Hungarian name for a type of red pepper used in the dish.
