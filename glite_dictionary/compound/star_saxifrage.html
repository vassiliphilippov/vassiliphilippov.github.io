star saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈsæksɪˌfrædʒ / (AmE), / stɑː ˈsæksɪˌfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A small, often mat-forming alpine plant of Europe, with small star-shaped white flowers.
alpine plant with star-like white flowers

Examples

The star saxifrage blooms across rocky alpine meadows in late spring.
Gardeners admire the delicate beauty of the star saxifrage in shaded rock gardens.
We spotted clusters of star saxifrage among the moss-covered stones during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxifrage - a plant of the genus Saxifraga, often found in alpine habitats

Etymology

From Latin 'saxifraga', meaning 'stone-breaker', and 'star' referring to the flower's shape.
