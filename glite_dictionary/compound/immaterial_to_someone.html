immaterial to (someone)

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪməˈtɪriəl tə ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈtɪəriəl tə ˈsʌmwən / (BrE)
Not relevant or significant to a particular person; having no importance for someone.
not relevant or significant to someone

Examples

Whether the meeting is at 10 or 11 is immaterial to me since I can't attend either time.
The manager's explanation was immaterial to the outcome of the negotiation.
Your personal preference is immaterial to this decision; we must follow company policy.

Synset Examples

Their opinion on the matter is immaterial to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrelevant to (someone) - not connected with or relevant to someone
