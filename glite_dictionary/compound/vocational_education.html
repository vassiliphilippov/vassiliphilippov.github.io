vocational education

1. NOUN

/ voʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
Education and training that prepares people for a specific trade, craft, or career, typically in industry, agriculture, or the trades.
education for specific trades or careers

Examples

After completing his vocational education, he started working as an electrician.
The government is investing more in vocational education to address the skill gap in the workforce.
Many students prefer vocational education programs because they offer hands-on training and faster entry into employment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical education - education relating to applied sciences or skills for industry
  • Antonym: academic education - education focused on general knowledge and theoretical learning
