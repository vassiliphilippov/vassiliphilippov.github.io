paper cutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ.pɚ ˌkʌ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.pə ˌkʌ.tə / (BrE)
a device with a straight blade for cutting sheets of paper to the desired size
device for cutting paper

Examples

The teacher used the paper cutter to trim the handouts for the class.
Please be careful when operating the paper cutter in the office.
After aligning the sheets, he brought down the blade of the paper cutter swiftly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guillotine - a device for cutting paper, often with a lever blade

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a device with a blade used for cutting sheets of paper straight and cleanly
tool for cutting paper

Examples

The office purchased a new paper cutter to help with preparing reports.
Be careful when using the paper cutter so you don't injure your hand.
She aligned the stack of flyers before slicing them with the paper cutter.

Synset Examples

The paper cutter made my work much easier.
