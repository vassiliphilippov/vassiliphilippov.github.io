rental collection

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛn.təl kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɛntəl kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a collection of books available for readers to rent for a small daily fee
books available for rental

Examples

The library added a new rental collection for popular fiction titles.
Customers often visit the bookstore's rental collection to borrow recent bestsellers.
She borrowed three novels from the rental collection and returned them the next week.
