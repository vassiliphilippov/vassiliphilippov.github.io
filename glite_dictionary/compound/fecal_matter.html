fecal matter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːkəl ˈmætər / (AmE), / ˈfiːkəl ˈmætə / (BrE)
Solid waste excreted from the bowels; feces.
solid waste from the bowels

Examples

The laboratory is testing the fecal matter for signs of infection.
Please dispose of fecal matter properly when cleaning up after your dog.
Doctors examine fecal matter samples to diagnose digestive disorders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feces - solid waste discharged from the body

Etymology

From Latin 'faex' (dregs) and English 'matter', meaning substance.
