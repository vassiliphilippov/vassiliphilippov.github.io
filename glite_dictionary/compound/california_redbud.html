California redbud

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɪfərniə ˈrɛdbʌd / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈniːə ˈrɛdbʌd / (BrE)
a shrub native to the western United States, known for its pink or crimson flowers and tendency to form dense thickets
western US shrub with pink or crimson flowers

Examples

The California redbud adds a splash of color to the spring landscape.
Many gardeners plant California redbud for its striking crimson blossoms.
A dense thicket of California redbud lines the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: redbud - any of several North American shrubs or small trees with pink or purple flowers
