Hank Marvin

1. ADJ

/ hæŋk ˈmɑrvɪn / (AmE), / hæŋk ˈmɑːvɪn / (BrE)
British slang for extremely or very hungry; rhyming slang (from 'starving').
British slang: very hungry
british informal slang

Examples

After missing lunch, I was absolutely Hank Marvin by dinnertime.
We finished the game and everyone said they were Hank Marvin.
He told me he was Hank Marvin and needed food right away.

Synset Examples

He arrived home as Hank Marvin, ready for dinner.
After hiking all day, I felt as Hank Marvin for a snack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: starving - very hungry; suffering from extreme hunger

Etymology

Cockney rhyming slang; 'Hank Marvin' rhymes with 'starving'.
