mental institution

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl ˌɪn.stəˈtu.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl ˌɪn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
a hospital or facility for the care and treatment of people with mental illnesses or disorders
hospital for people with mental illnesses

Examples

After his breakdown, he spent several months in a mental institution receiving treatment.
The stigma surrounding mental institutions has lessened in recent years as mental health awareness has grown.
She works as a nurse at a mental institution on the outskirts of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatric hospital - a hospital specializing in the treatment of serious mental disorders
