deer hunting

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪr ˌhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or sport of hunting deer for food, sport, or population control
hunting of deer

Examples

He goes deer hunting every autumn with his family.
Licenses are required in many states for deer hunting season.
She learned the basics of deer hunting from her grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting - the activity of pursuing and killing wild animals
