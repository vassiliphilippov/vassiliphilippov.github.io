bridal-wreath

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪdəl riθ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪdl riːθ / (BrE)
A type of ornamental shrub, especially Spiraea species, having many small white flowers that bloom in spring.
a flowering shrub with white spring flowers

Examples

The garden was lined with blooming bridal-wreath in early April.
She chose to plant bridal-wreath for its elegant arching branches and spring blossoms.
A hedge of bridal-wreath adds a delicate touch of white to the landscape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiraea - a genus of shrubs with clusters of small flowers
