jim crow

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm ˈkroʊ / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈkrəʊ / (BrE)
A crowbar fitted with a claw for pulling nails.
crowbar with a claw for pulling nails

Examples

The carpenter used a jim crow to pry up the old floorboards.
You can pull nails out easily with a jim crow thanks to its built-in claw.
She found an old jim crow in her grandfather's toolbox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowbar - a long metal bar used as a lever
