market keeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkɪt ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages a shop or stall in a market, often responsible for selling goods directly to customers
person who manages a shop or stall in a market

Examples

The market keeper greeted customers warmly as they browsed the fresh produce.
Each market keeper is responsible for keeping their stall clean and inviting.
She became a market keeper after inheriting her family's spice shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a small shop
