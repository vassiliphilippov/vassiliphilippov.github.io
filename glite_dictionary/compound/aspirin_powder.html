aspirin powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈæspɚɪn ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈasp(ə)rɪn ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a powdered form of aspirin, usually used for faster absorption or ease of use
aspirin in powder form
medical

Examples

Some people prefer aspirin powder because it dissolves quickly in water.
The pharmacist recommended aspirin powder for my headache since I can't swallow pills.
Always check the dosage instructions when using aspirin powder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspirin - a common pain-relieving and anti-inflammatory drug
