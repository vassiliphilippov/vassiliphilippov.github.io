cyclone cellar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkloʊn ˈsɛlər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkləʊn ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
an underground shelter designed to provide safety during storms, especially cyclones or tornadoes
underground storm shelter

Examples

They waited out the tornado in the cyclone cellar until it was safe to come out.
Every spring, my grandparents made sure their cyclone cellar was stocked with supplies in case of storms.
The farm's cyclone cellar was built deep enough to withstand the strongest winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storm cellar - an underground shelter for protection from storms
