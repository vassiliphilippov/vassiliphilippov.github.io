nose test

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊz tɛst / (AmE), / ˈnəʊz tɛst / (BrE)
an informal assessment made by smelling something or by relying on one's intuition, especially to judge freshness, quality, or authenticity
quick judgment by smell or intuition

Examples

I always give leftovers the nose test before deciding whether they’re safe to eat.
The wine passed the nose test, smelling fresh and fruity.
If an idea doesn’t pass the nose test, it’s probably not worth pursuing.

Synset Examples

If it doesn’t pass the nose test, I won’t eat it.
His cooking failed the nose test, smelling awful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sniff test - a judgment made by smelling something to determine its quality or suitability
