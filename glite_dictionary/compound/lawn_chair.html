lawn chair

1. NOUN

/ lɔn tʃɛr / (AmE), / lɔːn tʃɛə / (BrE)
a light chair, often folding, designed for use outdoors on lawns or in gardens
light chair for outdoor use

Examples

He spent the afternoon reading in his lawn chair under the shade of a tree.
Please bring your own lawn chair to the barbecue.
We sat side by side in lawn chairs watching the sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deck chair - a folding chair for use outdoors, typically on a deck or at the beach
