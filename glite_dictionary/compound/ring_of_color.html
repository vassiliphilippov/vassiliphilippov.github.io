ring of color

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋ əv ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / rɪŋ əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a small circular area of color, such as that around the human nipple or an inflamed ring around a pimple or insect bite
small circular colored area on the body

Examples

A red ring of color appeared around the mosquito bite.
Doctors noted a ring of color circling the infected area.
The ring of color around the nipple became more prominent.
