green soybean

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈsoʊˌbiːn / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈsəʊˌbiːn / (BrE)
a soybean harvested while still green and eaten as a vegetable, often shelled and cooked
immature edible soybean

Examples

I ordered a bowl of green soybeans as a healthy appetizer.
Stir-fried green soybean pairs well with garlic and ginger.
The salad includes fresh green soybean for added protein.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edamame - immature, green soybean, often served in the pod; alternative term
