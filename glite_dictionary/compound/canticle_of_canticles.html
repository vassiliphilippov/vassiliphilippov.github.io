Canticle of Canticles

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæn.tɪ.kəl əv ˈkæn.tɪ.kəlz / (AmE), / ˈkan.tɪ.kl əv ˈkan.tɪ.klz / (BrE)
another name for 'Song of Songs', an Old Testament book consisting of a collection of love poems, traditionally attributed to Solomon but written later.
biblical book of love poems (Song of Songs)

Examples

The Canticle of Canticles is often read during Jewish Passover celebrations.
Many theologians have analyzed the symbolism in the Canticle of Canticles.
The lush imagery of the Canticle of Canticles has inspired artists and poets through the centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Song of Songs - another name for the biblical book, Canticle of Canticles
  • Synonym: Song of Solomon - another English name for Canticle of Canticles

Etymology

From Latin 'Canticum Canticorum' meaning 'Song of Songs.'
