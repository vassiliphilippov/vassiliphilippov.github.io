airtight bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.taɪt bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈeə.taɪt bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag designed to prevent air from entering or escaping, ensuring its contents remain protected from exposure to air
bag that prevents air entry or escape

Examples

Please store the leftover food in an airtight bag to keep it fresh.
The scientist placed the sample inside an airtight bag for transportation.
Make sure the device is wrapped in an airtight bag before shipping it overseas.

Synset Examples

They sealed the documents in an airtight bag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum bag - a bag from which air is removed to create a vacuum
