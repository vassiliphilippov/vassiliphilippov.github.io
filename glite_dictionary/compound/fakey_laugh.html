fakey laugh

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪki læf / (AmE), / ˈfeɪki lɑːf / (BrE)
A laugh that is insincere or artificially produced, not genuine.
insincere or artificial laugh
informal

Examples

She gave a fakey laugh when her boss made a terrible joke.
Everyone could tell by his fakey laugh that he wasn't amused.
The interviewer's fakey laugh made the conversation feel awkward.

Synset Examples

He always had a fakey laugh that seemed forced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fake laugh - a laugh that is not genuine; acted or pretended
  • Antonym: genuine laugh - a laugh that is real or sincere
