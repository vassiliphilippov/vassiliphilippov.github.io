china stone

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪnə stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnə stəʊn / (BrE)
a fine, usually white clay formed by the weathering of aluminous minerals, used in ceramics and as an absorbent or filler
type of white clay used in ceramics

Examples

The porcelain artist selected a high-quality china stone for her latest pottery designs.
Paper manufacturers sometimes add china stone as a filler to improve texture and appearance.
The factory imports china stone in bulk for use in its ceramic products.
