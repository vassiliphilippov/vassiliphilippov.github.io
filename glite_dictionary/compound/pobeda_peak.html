Pobeda Peak

1. PROPN

/ poʊˈbɛdə ˈpiːk / (AmE), / pəʊˈbɛdə ˈpiːk / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Tien Shan mountain range, one of the highest in Central Asia, with an elevation of 24,406 feet (7,439 meters)
mountain peak in Tien Shan, Central Asia

Examples

Climbers from around the world attempt to summit Pobeda Peak each year despite its challenging conditions.
He took breathtaking photographs of the sunrise illuminating Pobeda Peak from the base camp.
The expedition faced severe weather on their way to Pobeda Peak in the Tien Shan mountains.

Etymology

From Russian 'Pik Pobedy,' meaning 'Victory Peak.'
