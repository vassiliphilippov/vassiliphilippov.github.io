beef fondue

1. NOUN

/ biːf fɑnˈdu / (AmE), / biːf fɒnˈdjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of cubes of beef cooked in hot oil at the table and served with assorted dipping sauces.
dish of beef cubes cooked in hot oil with sauces

Examples

They ordered beef fondue for two and enjoyed cooking the meat at their table.
The restaurant specializes in traditional Swiss dishes like beef fondue and raclette.
At the party, guests gathered around the table to dip steak into the bubbling oil for beef fondue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondue - dish in which small pieces of food are cooked or dipped in a hot liquid

Etymology

Fondue from French *fondre* (to melt), originally referring to the melted cheese version.
