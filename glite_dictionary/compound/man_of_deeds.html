man of deeds

1. NOUN

/ mæn əv didz / (AmE), / man əv diːdz / (BrE)
a man who is inclined to act rather than reflect; a person known for taking action rather than contemplating
action-oriented man

Examples

He was respected as a man of deeds rather than empty promises.
The company needed a man of deeds to lead it through the crisis.
As a man of deeds, he preferred solving problems over discussing them at length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: man of action - a man who prefers to act quickly and decisively
  • Antonym: man of thought - a man who is more contemplative than active
