solar array

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlɚ əˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə əˈreɪ / (BrE)
An assembly of multiple connected solar cells that converts sunlight into electricity.
group of connected solar cells

Examples

The new community center is powered by a large solar array installed on its roof.
Engineers checked each solar array to ensure it was operating at maximum efficiency.
The Mars rover relies on its solar array to recharge its batteries during the mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solar panel - a single flat panel containing solar cells for converting sunlight into electricity
