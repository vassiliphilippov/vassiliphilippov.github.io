loufah sponge

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːfə ˌspʌndʒ / (AmE), / ˈluːfə ˌspʌndʒ / (BrE)
the dried, fibrous skeleton of the fruit of a luffa plant, used as a natural sponge or strainer, especially for washing or scrubbing
dried luffa fruit used as a sponge

Examples

She uses a loufah sponge to exfoliate her skin in the shower every morning.
The loufah sponge is biodegradable, making it an eco-friendly choice for cleaning.
After soaking the dried loufah sponge, it becomes soft and expands, perfect for household chores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loofah - the fibrous fruit of a luffa, used as a washing sponge

Etymology

From Arabic *lūfah*, the plant's name; through modern scientific Latin *Luffa*.
