parts catalog

1. NOUN

/ pɑrts ˈkæt̬əlɔɡ / (AmE), / pɑːts ˈkatəlɒɡ / (BrE)
a publication listing the parts of machinery, vehicles, or equipment, usually with prices and identifying information
list of machinery parts with prices

Examples

The mechanic ordered a replacement part after consulting the parts catalog.
Many auto shops rely on a comprehensive parts catalog to serve their customers efficiently.
You can search for compatible components using the online parts catalog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalog - a systematic list of items, typically in a book or pamphlet
