Atlantic Coast

1. NOUN

/ əˈtlæntɪk koʊst / (AmE), / ətˈlæntɪk kəʊst / (BrE)
the region of land bordering the Atlantic Ocean, especially the eastern coast of North America or other continents facing the Atlantic
coast along the Atlantic Ocean

Examples

They spent their summer vacation traveling along the Atlantic Coast of the United States.
Many major cities are located on the Atlantic Coast due to its historic importance for trade.
The hurricanes often make landfall near the Atlantic Coast during the storm season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Pacific Coast - the region of land bordering the Pacific Ocean
