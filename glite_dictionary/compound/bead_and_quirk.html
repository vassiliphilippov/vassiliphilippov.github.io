bead and quirk

1. NOUN

/ bid ənd kwɝk / (AmE), / biːd ənd kwɜːk / (BrE)
a decorative molding featuring a beading separated from the surrounding surface by a narrow groove
a molding with a beading and separating groove

Examples

The cabinet doors were finished with a bead and quirk detail along the edges.
Traditional frames often display a bead and quirk to enhance their profile.
Restoring the old wainscoting required matching the original bead and quirk molding.
