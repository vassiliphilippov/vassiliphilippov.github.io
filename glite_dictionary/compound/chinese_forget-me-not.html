Chinese forget-me-not

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz fərˈɡɛt mi nɑt / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz fəˈɡɛt mi nɒt / (BrE)
A biennial herb native to East Asia, grown for its bright blue flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
East Asian herb with bright blue flowers

Examples

She planted Chinese forget-me-nots along the border of her garden for a splash of color.
Chinese forget-me-not blooms attract bees and butterflies in the spring.
The florist recommended Chinese forget-me-not for its long-lasting bright blue flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget-me-not - a small flowering plant of the genus Myosotis known for its blue flowers
