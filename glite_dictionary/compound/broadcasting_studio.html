broadcasting studio

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔdˌkæstɪŋ ˈstuːdiˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ ˈstjuːdiəʊ / (BrE)
a room or building equipped for radio or television broadcasts to originate and be produced
room for making radio or TV broadcasts

Examples

The news anchor went live from the broadcasting studio at 6 p.m.
Each broadcasting studio is soundproof to ensure high-quality audio recordings.
During the tour, students visited the broadcasting studio to see how a radio show is produced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a room where artists, musicians, or broadcasters work
