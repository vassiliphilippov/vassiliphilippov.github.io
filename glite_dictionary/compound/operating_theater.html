operating theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˈθiːətər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
a room in a hospital that is specially equipped for surgical operations
room equipped for surgery in a hospital
british

Examples

The patient was taken directly to the operating theater for emergency surgery.
All the equipment in the operating theater must be sterilized before each procedure.
The hospital recently built a state-of-the-art operating theater to improve surgical care.

Synset Examples

great care is taken to keep the operating rooms aseptic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operating room - a room in a hospital equipped for the performance of surgical operations (esp. US English)

Etymology

From Latin 'operari' (to work) and Greek 'theatron' (a place for viewing).
