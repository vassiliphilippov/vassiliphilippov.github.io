quick dash

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk dæʃ / (AmE), / kwɪk dæʃ / (BrE)
A brief and fast trip to a place, usually for a specific purpose.
a fast brief trip to a place

Examples

After dinner, Sarah made a quick dash to the pharmacy before it closed.
He left the meeting for a quick dash to grab a coffee.
I need to do a quick dash to the post office during my lunch break.

Synset Examples

She took a quick dash to the store for milk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: errand - a short journey to carry out a particular purpose
