flowering tobacco

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚɪŋ təˈbækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ təˈbækəʊ / (BrE)
A South American perennial plant of the Nicotiana genus, often grown as an ornamental for its nocturnally fragrant greenish-white flowers.
ornamental plant with fragrant flowers

Examples

The flowering tobacco added a sweet scent to the evening air in their garden.
She planted flowering tobacco alongside roses for its striking, night-blooming flowers.
Gardeners recommend flowering tobacco as an easy-to-grow ornamental plant with fragrant blossoms.
