Twitter freak

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪtər ˌfriːk / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtə ˌfriːk / (BrE)
a person who is extremely enthusiastic or obsessed with using Twitter
person obsessed with using Twitter
informal

Examples

He's considered a Twitter freak because he spends hours every day online.
Being a Twitter freak sometimes means tweeting about even the smallest events in life.
People called her a Twitter freak because she knew every viral trend instantly.

Synset Examples

He’s such a Twitter freak, always tweeting his thoughts.
Being a Twitter freak means never missing a beat online.
As a Twitter freak, she follows every trending topic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Twitter addict - someone addicted to using Twitter
  • Degree Lower: Twitter user - someone who uses Twitter
