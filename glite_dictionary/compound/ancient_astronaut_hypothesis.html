ancient astronaut hypothesis

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪnʃənt ˈæstrəˌnɑːt haɪˈpɑθəsɪs / (AmE), / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈæstrənɔːt hʌɪˈpɒθəsɪs / (BrE)
the pseudo-scientific theory that intelligent extraterrestrial beings visited Earth and made contact with humans in ancient times
theory of extraterrestrials contacting ancient humans

Examples

The ancient astronaut hypothesis suggests that advanced aliens played a role in shaping early human civilizations.
Many mainstream scientists reject the ancient astronaut hypothesis due to a lack of credible evidence.
Television series and books have popularized the ancient astronaut hypothesis among enthusiasts worldwide.

Synset Examples

Sagan argued that while many legends, artifacts and purported out-of-place artifacts were cited in support of ancient astronaut theories, ‘very few require more than passing mention’ and could be easily explained with more conventional theories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paleocontact hypothesis - the hypothesis that ancient humans were contacted by extraterrestrial beings
