jewelled headdress

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːəld ˈhɛdˌrɛs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəld ˈhɛdˌrɛs / (BrE)
A decorative headdress adorned with jewels or precious stones, often worn for ceremonial or special occasions.
headdress decorated with jewels

Examples

The queen wore a stunning jewelled headdress at the coronation ceremony.
Museum visitors marveled at the ancient jewelled headdress displayed in the glass case.
Her costume included a golden dress and an elaborate jewelled headdress sparkling under the lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiara - semi-circular jeweled headpiece, often worn by women
  • Synonym: crown - ornamental headpiece worn by royalty, often set with jewels
