backup system

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæk.ʌp ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈbæk.ʌp ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a computer system designed to make and store copies of data for recovery in case of loss or damage
system for making computer data backups

Examples

Our company uses a robust backup system to protect all important customer information.
After the server crashed, the IT team restored the files using the backup system.
It is essential to regularly test your backup system to ensure it works as expected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: backup - a copy of data made to protect against loss
