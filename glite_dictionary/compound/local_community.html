local community

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people living in the same area, especially considered as sharing common interests or identity
group of people living in the same area

Examples

The local community organized a charity drive to support families in need.
Members of the local community voiced their concerns at the town hall meeting.
The new park quickly became a meeting spot for the entire local community.

Synset Examples

The local coonmunity gathered for the annual festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: community - a group of people living together or having interests in common
