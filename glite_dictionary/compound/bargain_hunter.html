bargain hunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrɡən ˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡɪn ˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who searches for goods that are being sold at lower than usual prices
person seeking discounted goods

Examples

Every Saturday, the bargain hunter scours flea markets looking for hidden treasures.
Holiday sales always attract at least one savvy bargain hunter in every family.
As a true bargain hunter, she rarely buys anything at full price.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopper - a person who is shopping

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrɡən ˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡən ˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who looks for goods that are sold at a cheaper price than usual, especially by searching for discounts and special offers
person seeking discounts or deals

Examples

As a bargain hunter, she never pays full price for anything.
The bargain hunter scored a designer jacket for half the original price.
Many bargain hunters wake up early to shop at holiday sales.
