psychiatric hospital

1. NOUN

/ saɪˌkaɪ.əˈtrɪk ˈhɑːspɪtəl / (AmE), / saɪˌkiː.əˈtrɪk ˈhɒspɪtəl / (BrE)
a hospital specializing in the treatment of people with mental health disorders
hospital for mental health treatment

Examples

After being diagnosed with severe depression, he was admitted to a psychiatric hospital for treatment.
Many psychiatric hospitals offer outpatient programs as well as inpatient care.
The documentary explored daily life inside a psychiatric hospital in the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental hospital - hospital for treating mental illnesses
  • Antonym: general hospital - hospital for general medical treatment
