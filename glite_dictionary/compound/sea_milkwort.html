sea milkwort

1. NOUN

/ si ˈmɪlkˌwɝt / (AmE), / si ˈmɪlkˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a small fleshy herb found along North American seashores and in brackish marshes, with pink or white flowers
North American coastal herb with pink or white flowers

Examples

We found several sea milkwort plants growing along the edge of the marsh.
The delicate pink flowers of the sea milkwort add color to coastal salt marshes in the summer.
Botanists are studying how sea milkwort adapts to salty environments near the shore.
