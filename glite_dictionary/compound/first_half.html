first half

1. NOUN

/ fɝst hæf / (AmE), / fɜːst hɑːf / (BrE)
the initial period of play or activity when it is divided into two equal parts, especially in sports.
the initial of two equal periods, esp. in sports

Examples

The team scored three goals in the first half and secured their lead.
She was substituted just before the end of the first half.
The pace of the game slowed down after a fast first half.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: second half - the latter of two halves of play or activity
