Brexshit discussion

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkʃɪt dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkʃɪt dɪˈskʌʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A frustrating or tiresome conversation or debate about Brexit, often expressing annoyance at the topic's persistence.
frustrating talk about Brexit
informal slang vulgar british

Examples

I'm exhausted by the office's daily Brexshit discussion—can't we talk about something else for once?
Social media is flooded with yet another pointless Brexshit discussion every week.
He rolled his eyes when the meeting descended into a predictable Brexshit discussion again.

Synset Examples

Everyone is tired of the endless Brexshit discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brexit discussion - a conversation about Brexit
  • Synonym: Brexit debate - a debate about Brexit

Etymology

Blend of 'Brexit' and 'shit', used derogatorily to express frustration with Brexit-related talk.
