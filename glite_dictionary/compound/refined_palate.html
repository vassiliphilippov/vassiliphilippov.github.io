refined palate

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfaɪnd ˈpælət / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnd ˈpælət / (BrE)
A highly developed or discriminating taste, especially for fine food or drink.
sophisticated taste in food or drink

Examples

Only someone with a refined palate could appreciate the subtle flavors in this wine.
Her refined palate made her an exceptional food critic.
Developing a refined palate takes years of tasting and exposure to different cuisines.

Synset Examples

The chef has a refined palate for gourmet food.
She has a refined taste in music and art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discerning palate - an ability to distinguish subtle qualities in taste
  • Antonym: unrefined palate - a less sophisticated or undeveloped sense of taste
