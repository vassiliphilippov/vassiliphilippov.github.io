eighter from Decatur

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪtər frəm dɪˈkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈeɪtə frəm dɪˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
(US slang) A roll of eight in the game of craps, often called out by players; sometimes more generally, the number eight.
slang term for the number eight in craps
slang american

Examples

When he rolled an eighter from Decatur, the table erupted in cheers.
Old-timers at the casino loved to shout 'eighter from Decatur!' whenever someone rolled an eight.
He hoped for an eighter from Decatur, but rolled snake eyes instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the number between seven and nine

Etymology

Possibly from a rhyming phrase used by dice players in the American South and Midwest, linking 'eight' with 'Decatur' for its rhyme.
