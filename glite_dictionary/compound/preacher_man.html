preacher man

1. NOUN

/ ˈpritʃər mæn / (AmE), / ˈpriːtʃə mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to preach the Christian gospel, especially in a colloquial or informal context
male preacher (colloquial)
informal

Examples

The preacher man spoke passionately at the revival meeting last night.
As a child, she would often listen to the preacher man on Sunday mornings.
Rumors spread that the preacher man was moving to another town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preacher - a person who preaches, especially a minister in a church
  • Synonym: pastor - a minister in charge of a Christian church
