balsam family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔl.səm ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.səm ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Balsaminaceae) distinguished from Geraniaceae by their irregular flowers
family of flowering plants (Balsaminaceae)

Examples

The balsam family includes popular garden plants like impatiens and jewelweed.
Members of the balsam family are known for their bright, showy flowers.
Botanists distinguish the balsam family from related plant families by examining the structure of their flowers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Geraniaceae - a family of flowering plants commonly known as the geranium family
  • Synonym: Balsaminaceae - the scientific name for the balsam family
