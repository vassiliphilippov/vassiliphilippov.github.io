supernacular style

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːpərˈnækjələr staɪl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈnækjələ staɪl / (BrE)
an outstanding or exceptional way of doing something
exceptional manner of doing something

Examples

The painter's supernacular style earned him international recognition.
She delivered her speech in a supernacular style that captivated the audience.
Critics praised the chef's supernacular style in presentation and flavor.

Synset Examples

His supernacular style impressed all the critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the highest quality; excellent
  • Antonym: mediocre style - an ordinary or average manner of doing something

Etymology

From 'supernacular', meaning of the highest quality, from Latin 'supernaculum' (to the last drop), plus 'style'.
