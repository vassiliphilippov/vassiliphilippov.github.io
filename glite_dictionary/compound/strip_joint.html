strip joint

1. NOUN

/ strɪp dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / strɪp dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a bar or nightclub where striptease performances are given for entertainment
bar or club with striptease shows

Some people associate strip joints with bachelor parties and nightlife.
