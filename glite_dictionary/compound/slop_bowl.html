slop bowl

1. NOUN

/ slɑp boʊl / (AmE), / slɒp bəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl, often part of a tea set, used to collect the dregs and remains from teacups or coffee cups at the table
bowl for tea or coffee dregs
archaic

Examples

After finishing their tea, the guests emptied the remains into the slop bowl provided.
The antique slop bowl was an essential part of the Victorian tea set.
She explained the purpose of the slop bowl to her curious grandchildren.

2. NOUN

/ slɑp boʊl / (AmE), / slɒp bəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl used for holding animal feed, kitchen scraps, or waste, especially for pigs or in the context of waste disposal
bowl for animal feed or waste

Examples

She poured the leftover soup into the slop bowl for the pigs.
After clearing the table, he emptied the scraps into the slop bowl.
The old farmhouse kitchen had a large slop bowl by the door.

Synset Examples

She filled the slop bowl with leftover scraps.
