bumper-to-bumper

1. ADJ

/ ˌbʌmpər tə ˈbʌmpər / (AmE), / ˌbʌmpə tə ˈbʌmpə / (BrE)
(of traffic) moving very slowly or not at all, with vehicles so close together that their bumpers nearly touch.
very dense, slow-moving traffic

Examples

Traffic on the highway was bumper-to-bumper during rush hour.
After the concert, we sat in bumper-to-bumper traffic for nearly an hour.
The roadwork caused a bumper-to-bumper jam all the way across town.

Synset Examples

bumper-to-bumper traffic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gridlocked - completely blocked traffic, so that vehicles cannot move
