beer glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A relatively large glass used for serving beer.
glass container for serving beer

Examples

The bartender poured the ale into a chilled beer glass and handed it to the customer.
She collects different types of beer glasses from around the world as souvenirs.
A proper beer glass enhances both the flavor and aroma of the drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pint glass - a type of glass specifically for serving a pint of beer
