lavish hotel

1. NOUN

/ ˈlævɪʃ hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˈlævɪʃ həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A hotel that is luxurious and extravagantly furnished or equipped.
extravagant and luxurious hotel

Examples

They spent their honeymoon in a lavish hotel overlooking the ocean.
The awards ceremony will take place in a lavish hotel downtown.
Even celebrities were impressed by the opulence of the lavish hotel they visited.

Synset Examples

The corporate event was held in a lavish hotel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxury hotel - a hotel offering high-quality accommodation and services
  • Antonym: budget hotel - a hotel offering basic, inexpensive accommodation
