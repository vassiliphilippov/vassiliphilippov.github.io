artworld

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrt wɜrld / (AmE), / ˈɑːt wɜːld / (BrE)
the community or sphere of people, institutions, and activities involved in the visual arts
community engaged in the visual arts

Examples

The artworld has seen dramatic changes with the rise of digital media and online exhibitions.
She quickly became a notable figure in the artworld after her first solo exhibition.
Critics play a crucial role in shaping trends within the artworld.

Synset Examples

Firstly, it is not a coincidence that the loss of interest in the inquiry into artistic beauty coincides with a sea change in the artworld, transformed by new art movements as well as struggles in defining art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arts community - community interested in or involved in arts
