baseball mitt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl mɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl mɪt / (BrE)
A large, padded glove worn by fielders in baseball to catch and field balls.
large glove used in baseball

Examples

The catcher slid on his baseball mitt before stepping onto the field.
She signed his old baseball mitt as a souvenir from the game.
Without a baseball mitt, it's hard to catch fast pitches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball glove - Glove worn by baseball fielders to catch the ball
