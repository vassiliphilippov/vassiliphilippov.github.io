bog rein orchid

1. NOUN

/ bɑg raɪn ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / bɒg raɪn ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
an orchid with spikes of many fragrant white flowers on erect leafy stems, growing in wet or boggy areas of western and northern North America.
a North American bog-dwelling orchid

Examples

We spotted a beautiful bog rein orchid blooming beside the wetland trail.
The bog rein orchid thrives in moist, peaty soils and is known for its sweet fragrance.
Botanists conducted a survey of native plants and found several bog rein orchids in the area.
