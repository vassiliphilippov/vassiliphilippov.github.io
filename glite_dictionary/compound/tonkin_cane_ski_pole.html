Tonkin cane ski pole

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑn.kɪn keɪn skaɪ poʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɒn.kɪn keɪn skaɪ pəʊl / (BrE)
A ski pole made from Tonkin cane, a strong species of bamboo historically used for its durability.
ski pole made from Tonkin cane bamboo

Examples

Vintage Tonkin cane ski poles are popular among collectors for their craftsmanship and style.
She replaced her aluminum poles with sturdy Tonkin cane ski poles before hitting the slopes.
Traditional Tonkin cane ski poles are valued for their flexibility and classic look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski pole - A pole used by skiers for balance and propulsion

Etymology

Tonkin cane refers to a type of bamboo from the Tonkin region of northern Vietnam.
