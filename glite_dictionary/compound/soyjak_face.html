soyjak face

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌdʒæk feɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌdʒæk feɪs / (BrE)
an exaggerated, cartoonish facial expression characterized by an open mouth and wide eyes, used in internet memes to represent excitement or mock enthusiasm
cartoonish facial expression in memes
slang

Examples

The cartoon featured a character with a prominent soyjak face reacting to the news.
She used a soyjak face meme in response to the announcement.
Several online communities parody trends using images of the soyjak face.

Synset Examples

The meme with the soyjak face went viral online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wojak - a popular meme character with a sad or resigned expression

Etymology

Blend of 'soy' (from 'soylent', internet slang linked to perceived enthusiasm for trends) and 'wojak' (a popular meme character); 'face' refers to the expression itself.
