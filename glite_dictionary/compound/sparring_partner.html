sparring partner

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑrɪŋ ˌpɑrtnər / (AmE), / ˈspɑːrɪŋ ˌpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person who practices boxing with another as part of their training, especially to help prepare for a competition or important fight
a training companion in boxing

Examples

Every champion needs a skilled sparring partner to prepare for big matches.
Her sparring partner helped her perfect her defensive skills before the competition.
He traveled abroad to train with a new sparring partner ahead of the title fight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training partner - a person who participates in training exercises with another, in sports or fitness
