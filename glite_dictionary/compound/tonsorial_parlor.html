tonsorial parlor

1. NOUN

/ tɑnˈsɔriəl ˈpɑrlər / (AmE), / tɒnˈsɔːriəl ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A barber shop; a place where men's hair is cut and groomed.
barber shop (formal/old-fashioned)

Examples

After a long week, Sam treated himself to a relaxing shave at the local tonsorial parlor.
The old photograph showed men chatting in a bustling tonsorial parlor downtown.
He opened a tonsorial parlor in the city, offering classic haircuts and hot towel shaves.

Synset Examples

He frequents a tonsorial parlor for the best haircuts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barber shop - A place where men's hair is cut and groomed

Etymology

From 'tonsorial', relating to barbers (from Latin tonsor, 'barber'), and 'parlor', meaning a room for reception or business.
