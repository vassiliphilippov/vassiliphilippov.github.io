blue-gray

1. ADJ

/ bluː ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / bluː ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a gray color with a hint of blue; tinged with blue.
gray tinged with blue

Examples

The sky turned a blue-gray as the storm approached.
Her eyes were a striking blue-gray color that seemed to change with the light.
He painted the walls a soft blue-gray to create a calming atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - A color intermediate between black and white
  • Degree Higher: blue - A color resembling the clear sky
