tree farming

1. NOUN

/ tri ˈfɑrmɪŋ / (AmE), / triː ˈfɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of cultivating and managing trees for commercial or environmental purposes
cultivation and management of trees

Examples

Many landowners have turned to tree farming as a sustainable source of income.
The government offers grants for tree farming to promote reforestation.
Modern tree farming involves careful planning to maximize both yield and conservation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forestry - the science and practice of managing forests
