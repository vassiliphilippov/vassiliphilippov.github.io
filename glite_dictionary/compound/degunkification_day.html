degunkification day

1. NOUN

/ diˌɡʌŋkəfɪˈkeɪʃən deɪ / (AmE), / diˌɡʌŋkɪfɪˈkeɪʃən deɪ / (BrE)
An informal day set aside for cleaning, organizing, or removing accumulated mess or clutter from a space.
day for cleaning and decluttering
informal

Examples

Our team holds an annual degunkification day to tidy up the office and recycle old equipment.
After months of neglect, we finally declared Saturday as degunkification day for the garage.
The company encouraged all staff to participate in degunkification day to create a cleaner and more pleasant workspace.

Synset Examples

They scheduled a degunkification day for the kitchen equipment.

Etymology

Formed from 'degunk', an informal blend of 'de-' (remove) and 'gunk' (dirt or grime), with '-ification'; combined with 'day'.
