bulblet bladder fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌlb.lət ˈblæd.ɚ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbʌlb.lət ˈblæd.ə fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern (Cystopteris bulbifera) often bearing small bulbils on its leaflets.
North American fern with bulbils

Examples

The bulblet bladder fern is easily identified by the small bulbils growing on its fronds.
Gardeners often cultivate bulblet bladder fern in shaded rock gardens for its delicate appearance.
Hikers spotted a patch of bulblet bladder fern near the creek during their afternoon walk.
