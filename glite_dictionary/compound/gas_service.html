gas service

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ɡas ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a public utility that provides and distributes gas for use in homes, businesses, or industries
utility supplying gas to buildings

Examples

Our gas service was interrupted due to maintenance work on the pipeline.
You need to set up the gas service before moving into your new apartment.
The city recently improved its gas service network to ensure safer supply.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electricity service - a utility that provides electricity
  • Synonym: water service - a utility that provides water
  • Synonym: gas company - a company supplying gas to consumers
