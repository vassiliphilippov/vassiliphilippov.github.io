age of consent

1. NOUN

/ eɪdʒ əv kənˈsɛnt / (AmE), / eɪdʒ əv kənˈsɛnt / (BrE)
the minimum age at which a person is considered legally competent to consent to marriage or sexual activity
legal minimum age for marriage or sexual activity
legal

Examples

The age of consent varies from state to state in the United States.
He faced charges because the girl was below the age of consent.
Many activists are calling to raise the age of consent to protect minors.
