cyclohexanol phthalate

1. NOUN

/ saɪ.kloʊˈhɛk.sə.nɑl ˈθæl.eɪt / (AmE), / sɪ.kləʊˈhɛk.sə.nɒl ˈθæl.eɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound that is the ester formed from cyclohexanol and phthalic acid, often used as a plasticizer
ester of cyclohexanol and phthalic acid

Examples

The factory switched to using cyclohexanol phthalate as a safer plasticizer in its products.
Researchers found that exposure to cyclohexanol phthalate can be detected in certain environmental samples.
New safety regulations now restrict the use of cyclohexanol phthalate in children's toys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phthalate - a salt or ester of phthalic acid
  • Synonym: cyclohexanol - an alcohol derived from cyclohexane

Etymology

From 'cyclohexanol' (a cyclohexane alcohol) and 'phthalate' (from phthalic acid).
