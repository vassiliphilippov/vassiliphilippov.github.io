Energy Secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnərdʒi ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the head of the Department of Energy in certain governments, responsible for national energy policy and administration
head of the Department of Energy

Examples

The President nominated a new Energy Secretary to address the country's rising energy demands.
After years as a senator, she took office as Energy Secretary in the new administration.
The Energy Secretary announced a major investment in renewable energy sources last month.

Synset Examples

the first Secretary of Energy was James R. Schlesinger who was appointed by Carter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secretary of Energy - official title for the head of the Department of Energy
