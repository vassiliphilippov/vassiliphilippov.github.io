spike mike

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk maɪk / (AmE), / spaɪk maɪk / (BrE)
a contact microphone with a pointed end, used for listening through walls.
microphone for listening through walls

Examples

The detective placed a spike mike against the wall to eavesdrop on the conversation.
Security agents sometimes use a spike mike to gather evidence discreetly.
A spike mike can transmit even faint sounds from the adjacent room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contact microphone - a microphone that detects audio vibrations from physical contact, not air
