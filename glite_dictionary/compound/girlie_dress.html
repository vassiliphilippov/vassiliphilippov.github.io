girlie dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɝːli ˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli ˌdrɛs / (BrE)
A dress that is especially feminine in style, often playful, decorative, or dainty.
feminine, playful dress style

Examples

She wore a very girlie dress to the wedding.
The boutique specializes in selling girlie dresses with ruffles and ribbons.
Her girlie dress featured pastel colors and delicate lace, perfect for the spring party.

Synset Examples

She wore a very girlie dress to the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frock - a woman's or girl's dress
  • Antonym: tomboyish clothes - clothing styles that are not feminine or girlish
