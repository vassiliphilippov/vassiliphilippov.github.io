clustered poppy mallow

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌstɚd ˈpɑpi ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəd ˈpɒpi ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
a perennial plant with densely hairy stems, mostly triangular basal leaves, and clusters of rose-purple flowers
a perennial plant with clustered rose-purple flowers

Examples

The clustered poppy mallow adds vibrant color to prairie wildflower gardens.
Botanists identified the rare clustered poppy mallow growing along the rocky hillside.
The leaves of the clustered poppy mallow are notably triangular and densely hairy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy mallow - a plant of the genus Callirhoe, also known for mallow-like flowers
