coal miner

1. NOUN

/ koʊl ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to extract coal from underground mines
someone who works in a coal mine

Examples

My grandfather was a proud coal miner for over thirty years.
Several coal miners were honored for their bravery during the rescue operation.
The documentary follows the daily lives of a group of coal miners in West Virginia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miner - a person who works in a mine
