claim agent

1. NOUN

/ kleɪm ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / kleɪm ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who investigates insurance claims or claims for damages and recommends or arranges settlements
insurance claims investigator/settlement officer

Examples

The claim agent interviewed the policyholder and inspected the damaged property.
After the accident, the insurance company sent a claim agent to assess the situation.
Our office hired an experienced claim agent to expedite claims settlements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: claims adjuster - a person who investigates and settles insurance claims
