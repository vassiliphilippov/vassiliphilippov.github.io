whisky on the rocks

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski ɑn ðə rɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ɒn ðə rɒks / (BrE)
whisky served poured over ice cubes
whisky served with ice

Examples

He ordered a whisky on the rocks after a long day at work.
Would you like your Scotch neat or whisky on the rocks?
She prefers whisky on the rocks to cocktails.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neat - (of a drink) served plain, without ice or mixers
  • Degree Lower: whisky - a strong alcoholic drink distilled from grain mash
