to trace family history

1. VERB

/ treɪs ˈfæmli ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / treɪs ˈfæmli ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
to investigate and discover information about one's ancestors and lineage
research one's ancestry or lineage

Examples

Many people hire professionals to trace family history when records are difficult to find.
She used online archives to trace family history on her father's side.
It's easier than ever to trace family history thanks to digital databases.

Synset Examples

She managed to trace her family's history back to the 1800s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research ancestry - to investigate information about ancestors
