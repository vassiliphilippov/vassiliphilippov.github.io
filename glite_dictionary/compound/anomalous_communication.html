anomalous communication

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑː.mə.ləs kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˈnɒm.ə.ləs kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
communication that occurs by paranormal means, such as telepathy or clairvoyance, outside normal sensory channels
communication by paranormal means

Examples

Many researchers study anomalous communication to understand if telepathy is real.
The phenomenon of anomalous communication challenges our understanding of human perception.
Some claim to have experienced anomalous communication during séances or dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telepathy - communication between minds by psychic means
  • Synonym: clairvoyance - the supposed faculty of perceiving things beyond normal sensory contact

Etymology

From anomalous (irregular, abnormal) + communication; 'anomalous' from Greek anōmalos (uneven, irregular).
