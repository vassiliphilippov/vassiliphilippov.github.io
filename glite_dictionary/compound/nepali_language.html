Nepali language

1. NOUN

/ nɛˈpɑli ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / nɪˈpɑːli ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Nepal, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European language family
official language of Nepal

Examples

She is fluent in both Hindi and Nepali language.
The constitution of Nepal recognizes the Nepali language as the official language.
Many people in northern India can also speak the Nepali language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nepali - the language spoken in Nepal

Etymology

From Nepal, the country name, with English language.
