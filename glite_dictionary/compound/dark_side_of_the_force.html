dark side of the Force

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk saɪd əv ðə fɔrs / (AmE), / dɑːk saɪd əv ðə fɔːs / (BrE)
the evil or malevolent aspect of a mystical power, especially as depicted in the Star Wars universe
evil aspect of a mystical power (esp. Star Wars)

Examples

Many characters in the Star Wars saga struggle with the temptation of the dark side of the Force.
The emperor tries to lure Luke to the dark side of the Force.
She believes that anger and hate can lead people toward the dark side of the Force.

Synset Examples

She warned him about the dark side of the Force.
He was tempted by the dark side of the Force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the Force - a mystical energy field in the Star Wars universe
  • Antonym: light side of the Force - the good or benevolent aspect of the Force in Star Wars
