present times

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛzənt taɪmz / (AmE), / ˈprɛzənt taɪmz / (BrE)
the circumstances, ideas, or general conditions of the current age or period
the current age; the present era

Examples

Many challenges faced by young people in the present times are vastly different from those in the past.
The rapid advancement of technology is a hallmark of the present times.
Writers often reflect on how present times influence the values of society.

Synset Examples

in modern times like these

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modern times - the current or most recent period, especially in contrast to the past
  • Antonym: olden days - times long past, particularly in contrast to the present
