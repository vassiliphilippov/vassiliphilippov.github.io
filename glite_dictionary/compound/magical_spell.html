magical spell

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪkəl spɛl / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪkəl spɛl / (BrE)
a set of words or a verbal formula believed to have magical power to produce a supernatural effect
verbal formula with magical power

Examples

The wizard cast a magical spell to open the hidden door.
Stories of witches and their magical spells have fascinated people for centuries.
The children pretended to chant a magical spell while playing in the garden.

Synset Examples

he whispered a spell as he moved his hands
inscribed around its base is a charm in Balinese

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incantation - the chanting or uttering of words purporting to have magical power
  • Antonym: curse - a solemn utterance intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm
