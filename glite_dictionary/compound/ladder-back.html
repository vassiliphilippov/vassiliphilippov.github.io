ladder-back

1. NOUN

/ ˈlædɚˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈladəˌbak / (BrE)
the backrest of a chair consisting of two vertical uprights connected by multiple horizontal slats
chair back with horizontal slats

Examples

She admired the antique ladder-back in the corner of the dining room.
The craftsman restored the worn slats of the ladder-back to their original beauty.
Many rustic kitchens feature a traditional ladder-back as a focal point.
