pizza bagel

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtsə ˈbeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə ˈbeɪɡl̩ / (BrE)
A bagel that is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and other pizza ingredients, then baked or toasted.
bagel with pizza-style toppings

Examples

She likes to make pizza bagels as a simple after-school snack.
If you want something fast, try heating a pizza bagel in the toaster oven.
Pizza bagel parties are popular because everyone can choose their own toppings.

Synset Examples

I grabbed a pizza bagel for a quick lunch.
He ordered a pizza bagel, craving something cheesy.
For snacks, we had pizza bagels while watching the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagel - ring-shaped bread roll
  • Synonym: pizza - flat bread with savory toppings, typically cheese and tomato sauce
