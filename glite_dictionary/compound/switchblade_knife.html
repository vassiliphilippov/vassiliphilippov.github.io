switchblade knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃˌbleɪd naɪf / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃˌbleɪd naɪf / (BrE)
a pocketknife with a blade that opens automatically when a button or lever is pressed
spring-operated pocketknife

Examples

He pulled a switchblade knife from his pocket and flicked it open.
Carrying a switchblade knife is illegal in some countries due to safety concerns.
The detective found a switchblade knife at the crime scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switchblade - a knife with a blade that springs out from the handle when a button is pressed
  • Degree Lower: pocketknife - a small folding knife carried in a pocket

Etymology

From 'switchblade' (combining 'switch' and 'blade') and 'knife'.
