job opportunity

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb ˌɑpərˈtunəti / (AmE), / dʒɒb ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
a chance or prospect for employment, especially for a particular position or role
a chance for employment

Examples

I'm searching for a job opportunity that matches my skills and interests.
The economic downturn has made every job opportunity more competitive than ever.
He decided to relocate abroad when a promising job opportunity arose.

Synset Examples

She moved to town for a job opportunity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career opportunity - a possibility for advancement or employment in one's career
