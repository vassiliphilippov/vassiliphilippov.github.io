incumbent president

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkʌmbənt ˈprɛzɪdənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌmbənt ˈprɛzɪdənt / (BrE)
the president who is currently holding office
current president in office

Examples

The incumbent president addressed the nation following the election results.
Many voters felt the incumbent president had not fulfilled his campaign promises.
A fierce debate arose between the challenger and the incumbent president during the televised event.

Synset Examples

The incumbent president announced his re-election campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republic
  • Synonym: incumbent - currently holding a specified office
  • Antonym: former president - a president who previously held office

Etymology

Incumbent from Latin incumbēns, 'lying in or on', used for someone holding an office.
