dead person

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / dɛd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is no longer alive; a human who has died
someone who has died

Examples

The police needed to identify the dead person before contacting the family.
She inherited the house from a dead person she had never met.
The will of the dead person was read aloud to the relatives.

Synset Examples

I wonder what the dead person would have done

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deceased - a person who has died
  • Synonym: corpse - the dead body of a human being
  • Antonym: living person - a person who is alive
