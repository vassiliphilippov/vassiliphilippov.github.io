name calling

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪm ˌkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈneɪm ˌkɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of abusive or insulting language to attack or criticize someone, rather than addressing the issue
verbal abuse; using insults

Examples

The debate quickly descended into name calling instead of a respectful discussion.
She was hurt by the name calling from her classmates at school.
Instead of responding logically, he resorted to name calling on social media.

Synset Examples

sticks and stones may break my bones but names can never hurt me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - an offensive remark or action
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
