2 Chronicles

1. PROPN

/ tu ˈkrɑnɪklz / (AmE), / tuː ˈkrɒnɪklz / (BrE)
the second book of Chronicles in the Old Testament, recounting the history of Judah and Israel up to the return from the Babylonian Captivity
Old Testament book; history of Judah and Israel

Examples

Many of the genealogies can be found in 2 Chronicles in the Old Testament.
The reign of King Solomon is described in detail in 2 Chronicles.
He referenced 2 Chronicles during his lecture on ancient Israel.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 1 Chronicles - the first book of Chronicles in the Old Testament, covering the history of Israel

Etymology

'Chronicles' from Greek 'chronika' (annals, historical records).
