standard fare

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɚd fɛr / (AmE), / ˈstændəd feə / (BrE)
food or offerings that are typical, usual, or commonly expected in a particular context
typical or expected food or offerings

Examples

At most fast food restaurants, standard fare includes burgers and fries.
Complimentary breakfast is standard fare at many hotels.
Vegetarian options have become standard fare at business lunches.

Synset Examples

For lunch, pizza was the standard fare at the office.
Free coffee and snacks were standard fare at the conference.
The menu offered burgers as standard fare for guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regular fare - food or items that are regularly provided or served
