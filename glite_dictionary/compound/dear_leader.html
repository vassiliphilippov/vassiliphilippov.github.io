Dear Leader

1. NOUN

/ dɪr ˈliːdər / (AmE), / dɪə ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a title or term used, often satirically or in official propaganda, to refer to an authoritarian leader, especially in certain countries
title for an authoritarian leader, often satirical

Examples

Many citizens gave speeches praising their Dear Leader during the national celebration.
The newspaper was filled with glowing articles about the wisdom of the Dear Leader.
Journalists sometimes use the phrase Dear Leader sarcastically when discussing totalitarian regimes.

Synset Examples

They wrote a letter addressed to 'Dear Leader' for support.
The citizens rallied behind their 'Dear Leader' during the speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Great Leader - a similarly styled honorific used to refer to a supreme leader, often in North Korean context
  • Synonym: Supreme Leader - another honorific for authoritative rulers, often in a propagandistic sense
