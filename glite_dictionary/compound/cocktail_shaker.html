cocktail shaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑk.teɪl ˌʃeɪ.kər / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl ˌʃeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a container with a tight-fitting lid used for mixing cocktails by shaking the ingredients together with ice
container for mixing cocktails

Examples

She poured the ingredients into the cocktail shaker and gave it a vigorous shake.
Every well-stocked bar should have a good cocktail shaker on hand.
He bought a stainless steel cocktail shaker as a gift for his friend who loves mixing drinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaker - a container used for mixing or shaking ingredients
