film industry

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of business and organizations involved in producing, distributing, and exhibiting movies
business of producing and distributing movies

Examples

She has worked in the film industry for more than twenty years.
Many small towns benefit economically from the film industry choosing them as shooting locations.
The conference brought together experts from all areas of the film industry to discuss current trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: movie industry - the business of making and distributing movies
  • Degree Lower: television industry - the business involved in producing and broadcasting television programs
