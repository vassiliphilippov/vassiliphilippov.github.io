spinal curvature

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪnəl ˈkɜrvətʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl ˈkɜːvətʃə / (BrE)
an abnormal or exaggerated bending or curve in the spinal column, especially one causing a medical condition.
abnormal curve of the spine
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with a mild spinal curvature after examining her X-rays.
Physical therapy can help manage pain caused by spinal curvature.
He wore a brace for years to correct his abnormal spinal curvature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoliosis - a sideways curvature of the spine
  • Synonym: kyphosis - excessive outward curvature, causing hunching of the back
  • Synonym: lordosis - excessive inward curvature of the spine
