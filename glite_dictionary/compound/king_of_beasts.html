king of beasts

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ əv bists / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv biːsts / (BrE)
a figurative name for the lion, regarded as the most majestic of animals
a figurative term for the lion

Examples

Many ancient cultures depicted the king of beasts as a symbol of power and nobility.
The fierce roar of the king of beasts echoed across the savanna.
In mythology, the king of beasts was often seen as a guardian of the animal kingdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lion - large, tawny predatory feline of Africa and India
