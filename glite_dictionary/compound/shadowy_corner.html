shadowy corner

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃædoʊ.i ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊ.i ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
a place that is dimly lit or secluded, often away from direct view
dimly lit or secluded place

Examples

They met secretly in a shadowy corner of the room.
A stranger watched from a shadowy corner, nearly invisible to the guests.
The detective searched every shadowy corner for evidence.

Synset Examples

They met in a shadowy corner of the bar.
