people of color

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəl əv ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
People who are not classified as white, especially those who are Black, Asian, Indigenous, or from other non-white groups.
non-white people; racial minorities

Examples

The organization supports people of color in pursuing careers in technology.
Many activists are working to amplify the voices of people of color in politics.
The city’s history is shaped by the contributions of people of color from many backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: white people - People identified as belonging to the white race; people of European descent
  • Synonym: person of color - A single person who is not classified as white
