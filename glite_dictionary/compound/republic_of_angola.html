Republic of Angola

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv æŋˈɡoʊlə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv æŋˈɡəʊlə / (BrE)
The official name of Angola, a country in southwestern Africa on the Atlantic coast, which became independent from Portugal in 1975.
official name of Angola (country in Africa)

Examples

The capital and largest city of the Republic of Angola is Luanda.
After years of conflict, the Republic of Angola has made significant progress in economic development.
The Republic of Angola joined the United Nations shortly after its independence in 1975.
