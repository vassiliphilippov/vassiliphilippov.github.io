Long Island

1. PROPN

/ lɔːŋ ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
a large island in southeastern New York State, forming part of the New York metropolitan area; Brooklyn and Queens occupy its western end
island in southeastern New York State

Examples

They spent the summer on Long Island, enjoying the beaches and quaint towns.
Many commuters travel from Long Island to Manhattan every day for work.
The western end of Long Island includes both Brooklyn and Queens.
