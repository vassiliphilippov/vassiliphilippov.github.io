wind cone

1. NOUN

/ wɪnd koʊn / (AmE), / wɪnd kəʊn / (BrE)
a truncated cloth cone mounted on a mast, used (especially at airports) to indicate the direction and strength of the wind
cone-shaped wind direction indicator

Examples

The airport installed a new wind cone near the runway for better visibility.
Pilots check the wind cone before takeoff to determine wind direction and speed.
During the storm, the wind cone was blown nearly horizontal by the strong gusts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: windsock - a similar fabric indicator for wind direction, especially used at airfields
