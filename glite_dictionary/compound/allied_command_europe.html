Allied Command Europe

1. PROPN

/ ˈælaɪd kəˈmænd juˈroʊp / (AmE), / ˈæl.aɪd kəˈmɑːnd juˈrəʊp / (BrE)
the major strategic military headquarters of NATO, responsible for safeguarding the area from Norway to Turkey
NATO's major European military command

Examples

During the Cold War, Allied Command Europe played a crucial role in NATO's defense strategy.
The headquarters of Allied Command Europe was located in Mons, Belgium.
Military officials met at Allied Command Europe to discuss security arrangements for member states.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NATO - North Atlantic Treaty Organization, an intergovernmental military alliance
