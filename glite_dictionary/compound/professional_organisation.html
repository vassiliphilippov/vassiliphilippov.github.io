professional organisation

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənl ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃn / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
An organization formed by and for people working in a particular profession, often to set standards, support members, or represent their interests.
group for people in a profession

Examples

Membership in a professional organisation can help advance your career through networking opportunities.
The professional organisation sets guidelines and ethical standards for its members.
Joining a professional organisation often provides access to academic journals and training seminars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional association - an organization of professionals for mutual benefit
  • Antonym: trade union - an organization of workers formed to protect their rights and interests
