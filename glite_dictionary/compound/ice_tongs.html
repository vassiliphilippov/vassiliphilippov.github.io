ice tongs

1. NOUN

/ aɪs tɑŋz / (AmE), / aɪs tɒŋz / (BrE)
a tool with two long arms and gripping ends, used for lifting and carrying blocks of ice
tool for lifting blocks of ice

Examples

The deliveryman picked up the heavy block with ice tongs and carried it into the kitchen.
Grandfather showed us his old ice tongs from when he used to deliver ice before refrigerators were common.
Using ice tongs, she lifted the slippery chunk into the cooler without touching it.
