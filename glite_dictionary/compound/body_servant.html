body servant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌsɝvənt / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌsɜːvənt / (BrE)
a personal attendant, such as a valet or maid, responsible for assisting someone with their daily personal needs and care
personal attendant or valet/maid

Examples

He relied on his body servant to help him dress every morning before work.
Throughout history, nobles often had a body servant to attend to their daily needs.
The body servant's duties included preparing baths and managing clothing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valet - a man's personal male attendant, responsible for clothes and appearance
  • Synonym: maid - a female domestic servant
