a bit tetched

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt tɛtʃt / (AmE), / ə bɪt tɛtʃt / (BrE)
Slightly crazy or eccentric; showing odd or unusual behavior, often in a harmless way.
slightly crazy or eccentric
informal

Examples

Everyone in the village thought old Mr. Jenkins was a bit tetched after he painted his house purple.
You have to be a bit tetched to swim in the river during winter, if you ask me.
The way she talks to her plants makes me think she's a bit tetched.

Synset Examples

After years of stress, he seemed a bit tetched.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - unconventional and slightly strange
  • Degree Higher: crazy - mentally deranged; insane
  • Antonym: sane - mentally healthy and reasonable

Etymology

Dialect alteration of 'touched,' meaning mentally affected, especially in rural or Southern American English.
