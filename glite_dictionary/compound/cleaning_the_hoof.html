cleaning the hoof

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ ðə hʊf / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ ðə hʊf / (BrE)
the act of removing dirt or debris from a horse's foot, especially as part of routine horse care
removing dirt from a horse's hoof

Examples

Proper cleaning the hoof is essential to maintain a horse's health and prevent infections.
Before every ride, stable hands spend time cleaning the hoof of each horse.
The farrier showed us the correct method for cleaning the hoof during the workshop.

Synset Examples

He found a horseshoe while cleaning the hoof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoof picking - removing debris from the sole of a horse's hoof with a hoof pick
