open-air marketplace

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpən ɛr ˈmɑrkɪtˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən eə ˈmɑːkɪtˌpleɪs / (BrE)
A public market located outdoors where food and goods are sold.
outdoor public market

Examples

We bought fresh vegetables at the open-air marketplace in the city center.
Local artisans sell their crafts every weekend at the open-air marketplace.
A band was playing lively music near the entrance to the open-air marketplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market - A regular gathering for the purchase and sale of goods; a marketplace.
