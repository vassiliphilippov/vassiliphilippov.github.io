financial forecast

1. NOUN

/ fəˈnænʃəl ˈfɔrˌkæst / (AmE), / faɪˈnanʃəl ˈfɔːkɑːst / (BrE)
an estimate of a company's future financial position, income, expenses, and cash flow based on expected conditions and assumptions
estimate of future financial performance

Examples

The company's financial forecast for the next quarter predicts moderate growth in revenue.
Before seeking investors, the startup prepared a detailed financial forecast.
Uncertainty in the market made their financial forecast less reliable than usual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budget - plan for allocating financial resources
