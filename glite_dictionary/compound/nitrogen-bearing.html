nitrogen-bearing

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪtroʊdʒən ˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌɪtrədʒən ˌbɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a substance or compound) containing or relating to nitrogen.
containing or relating to nitrogen

Examples

The soil is rich in nitrogen-bearing minerals that promote healthy plant growth.
Ammonia is a common nitrogen-bearing compound used in fertilizers.
Scientists are studying nitrogen-bearing gases in the atmosphere to understand environmental changes.
