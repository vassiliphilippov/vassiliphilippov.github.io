clothing store

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊðɪŋ stɔːr / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðɪŋ stɔː / (BrE)
A shop or store that sells clothes, typically for men, women, or both.
shop selling clothing

Examples

He found a great suit at the new clothing store downtown.
Many clothing stores are offering sales this weekend.
She manages a clothing store that specializes in casual wear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boutique - a small shop that sells fashionable clothes or accessories
  • Synonym: department store - a large shop selling a wide variety of goods including clothing
