elitist crowd

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlitɪst kraʊd / (AmE), / ɪˈliːtɪst kraʊd / (BrE)
a group of people regarded as exclusive, snobbish, or superior to others
exclusive or snobbish group of people

Examples

Many students felt intimidated by the elitist crowd in the honors club.
She was never interested in joining the elitist crowd at work, preferring more down-to-earth colleagues.
Rumors spread quickly among the elitist crowd at the private party.

Synset Examples

She avoided the elitist crowd at the gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elite - a select group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities
  • Synonym: snobbish - having or showing the attitude of people who think they are superior to others
