white turnip

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈtɝː.nɪp / (AmE), / waɪt ˈtɜː.nɪp / (BrE)
A round, white root vegetable of the turnip plant, commonly eaten cooked or raw.
round white root vegetable

Examples

She added diced white turnip to the vegetable soup for extra flavor.
Roasted white turnip makes a delicious and healthy side dish.
The market had fresh white turnips on sale during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turnip - a round root vegetable with white flesh

2. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈtɝː.nɪp / (AmE), / waɪt ˈtɜː.nɪp / (BrE)
A widely cultivated plant with a large, round, white or yellow edible root, commonly used as a vegetable.
edible root vegetable; cultivated plant

Examples

She sliced a white turnip and added it to the stew for some extra flavor.
Farmers in this region often grow white turnips because they thrive in cool climates.
Roasted white turnip makes a delicious side dish with grilled meat.
