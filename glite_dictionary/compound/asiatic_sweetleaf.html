Asiatic sweetleaf

1. NOUN

/ əˈziː.æt̬.ɪk ˈswiːt.liːf / (AmE), / əˈziː.æt.ɪk ˈswiːt.liːf / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia, known for its decorative bright blue fruit.
Asian shrub with blue fruit

Examples

The gardener planted an Asiatic sweetleaf near the pond for its striking blue berries.
In spring, the Asiatic sweetleaf produces small flowers before developing its signature fruit.
Asiatic sweetleaf is highly valued in landscaping for its unusual foliage and colorful berries.
