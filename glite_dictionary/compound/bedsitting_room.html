bedsitting room

1. NOUN

/ bɛdˈsɪtɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / bɛdˈsɪtɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
A furnished sitting room that also serves as a bedroom, typically with some basic kitchen or washing facilities.
studio room serving as sitting and bedroom
british

Examples

He lived in a small bedsitting room above the corner shop.
The university offers affordable bedsitting rooms for students on a tight budget.
Her bedsitting room was cozy but a bit cramped when visitors stayed over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio apartment - a small apartment combining living room, bedroom, and kitchenette

Etymology

Blend of 'bed' and 'sitting room', primarily British usage.
