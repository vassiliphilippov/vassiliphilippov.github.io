ton capacity

1. NOUN

/ tʌn kəˈpæsəti / (AmE), / tʌn kəˈpæsəti / (BrE)
the maximum weight, measured in tons, that a piece of equipment such as a crane is designed to lift or carry safely
maximum weight a machine can lift, in tons

Examples

The new crane has a ton capacity that meets our construction needs.
Always check the ton capacity before operating heavy machinery.
This truck's ton capacity allows it to transport large industrial equipment.

Synset Examples

This rough terrain crane has a lift capacity of 60 tons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lifting capacity - maximum load a machine can raise
  • Synonym: load limit - maximum permissible load for a machine or structure
