amusement park

1. NOUN

/ əˈmjuzmənt ˌpɑrk / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzmənt ˌpɑːk / (BrE)
A large outdoor area with rides, shows, games, and other forms of entertainment, operated as a commercial business.
commercial entertainment venue with rides and shows

Examples

We spent the whole day at the amusement park enjoying roller coasters and games.
The city's new amusement park is expected to attract thousands of tourists each year.
Children were excited to see the parade of characters at the amusement park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theme park - an amusement park organized around a central theme or subject
