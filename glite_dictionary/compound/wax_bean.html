wax bean

1. NOUN

/ wæks biːn / (AmE), / wæks biːn / (BrE)
A variety of snap bean with yellow, waxy pods, eaten as a vegetable.
yellow-podded snap bean

Examples

I bought a pound of wax beans for tonight's stir-fry.
The garden's wax beans turned a bright yellow as they ripened.
You can substitute wax beans for green beans in most recipes.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: green bean - a variety of snap bean with green pods

2. NOUN

/ wæks bin / (AmE), / waks biːn / (BrE)
a variety of common bean plant grown for its edible yellow pods, which are eaten as a vegetable
a type of bean with yellow edible pods

Examples

We added some freshly picked wax beans to the salad for extra color.
The farmer's market had both green and wax beans available this week.
She prefers the mild flavor of wax beans in her vegetable stir-fry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green bean - a common bean plant grown for its edible green pods
