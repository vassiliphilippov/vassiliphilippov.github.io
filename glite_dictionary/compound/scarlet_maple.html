scarlet maple

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑr.lət ˈmeɪ.pəl / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt ˈmeɪ.pəl / (BrE)
a maple tree of eastern and central North America, with five-lobed leaves that turn scarlet and yellow in autumn
North American maple tree with scarlet autumn leaves

Examples

The scarlet maple in the front yard blazed with color as autumn arrived.
Nature lovers flocked to see the scarlet maple leaves turn red and gold each fall.
A scarlet maple was planted in the park to provide shade and vibrant autumn scenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red maple - a maple tree known for its bright red autumn foliage
