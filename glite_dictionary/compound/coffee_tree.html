coffee tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi triː / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi triː / (BrE)
a small tropical tree or shrub of the genus Coffea, cultivated for its seeds (coffee beans) used to produce coffee
tree or shrub yielding coffee beans

Examples

The coffee tree thrives in tropical climates with rich, well-drained soil.
Farmers harvest the red cherries from the coffee tree to obtain coffee beans.
A single coffee tree can produce enough beans for several pounds of coffee each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee plant - the plant that produces coffee beans; another term for coffee tree
  • Degree Lower: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree, often related in plant taxonomy
