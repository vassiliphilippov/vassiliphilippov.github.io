temporal arrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpɚəl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the organization or sequence of events in time
sequence of events in time

Examples

The temporal arrangement of the novel's chapters creates suspense for the reader.
Researchers studied the temporal arrangement of activities throughout the day.
Effective temporal arrangement of events is essential when planning a conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sequence - a set of related things or events arranged in a particular order

Etymology

From Latin 'temporalis' (of time) and Old French 'arrangement' (putting in order).
