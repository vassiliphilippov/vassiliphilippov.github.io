personalized party favor

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɝː.sən.əˌlaɪzd ˈpɑːr.ti ˈfeɪ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.sən.əˌlaɪzd ˈpɑː.ti ˈfeɪ.vər / (BrE)
a small custom gift prepared for guests at a party, often featuring their names or a personal touch
custom gift for event guests

Examples

Each child at the birthday received a personalized party favor with their name on it.
Guests were delighted to find a personalized party favor waiting at their seat.
The hosts spent hours creating personalized party favors to make the event memorable.

Synset Examples

We gave out personalized party favors at the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party favor - a small gift given to guests at a party
