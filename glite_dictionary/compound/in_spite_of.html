in spite of

1. ADP

/ ɪn spaɪt ʌv / (AmE), / ɪn spaɪt ɒv / (BrE)
despite the presence or effect of something; regardless of.
despite; regardless of

Examples

He decided to join the trip in spite of his fear of flying.
In spite of the bad weather, the concert was held outdoors.
She finished the marathon in spite of an ankle injury.

Synset Examples

He smiled in spite of his disappointment.
She succeeded in spite of all the obstacles.
In spite of the rain, the picnic went on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despite - without being affected by; regardless of
