key palm

1. NOUN

/ ki pɑm / (AmE), / kiː pɑːm / (BrE)
a small, stocky fan palm native to southern Florida and Cuba
small palm tree of Florida and Cuba

Examples

The key palm is well adapted to the coastal winds of Florida.
Some botanists study the unique characteristics of the key palm.
We spotted several key palms growing near the beach on our trip to Cuba.
