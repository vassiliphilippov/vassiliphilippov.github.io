encounter group

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkaʊntɚ ɡruːp / (AmE), / ɪnˈkaʊntə ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group therapy session in which participants express their feelings openly to develop self-awareness and mutual understanding
group therapy session for open emotional expression

Examples

The therapist suggested joining an encounter group to help him express his emotions more honestly.
Many people found the encounter group a transformative experience, breaking down emotional barriers.
She attended an encounter group every week to gain insight into her relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: support group - a group that offers emotional or practical support for a shared experience
