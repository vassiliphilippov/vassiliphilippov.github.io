gas station

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɡas ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where fuel for vehicles, especially gasoline, is sold and where other services for motorists are provided.
place for refueling vehicles
american

Examples

We stopped at a gas station to fill up the car before continuing our journey.
She works at a gas station just outside of town.
The closest gas station was closed, so we had to drive further to find one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petrol station - British term for a place where fuel for vehicles is sold
