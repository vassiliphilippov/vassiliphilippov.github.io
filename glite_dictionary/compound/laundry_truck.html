laundry truck

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔndri ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndri ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a van or truck used to collect and deliver laundry to and from customers
vehicle for laundry collection and delivery

Examples

The laundry truck comes every morning to pick up dirty linens from the hotel.
Our uniforms are delivered fresh every Monday by the laundry truck.
I waved to the driver as the laundry truck pulled up outside our building.

Synset Examples
