classic pothead

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪk ˈpɑtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪk ˈpɒtˌhɛd / (BrE)
A person who habitually uses marijuana and fits the stereotype of a marijuana user.
habitual marijuana user (stereotypical)
informal slang

Examples

Everyone knew Jake was a classic pothead who never turned down a joint.
She's worried her roommate is turning into a classic pothead with all the late-night snacks and movies.
In college, Mark had the reputation of being the classic pothead in his dorm.

Synset Examples

She joked that he was a classic pothead, always munching snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pothead - a person who habitually uses marijuana
