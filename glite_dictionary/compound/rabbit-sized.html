rabbit-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈræbɪt saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt saɪzd / (BrE)
about the same size as a rabbit; having approximately the dimensions of a rabbit.
about the size of a rabbit

Examples

The children spotted a rabbit-sized creature darting through the bushes.
Scientists discovered a rabbit-sized dinosaur fossil in the remote region.
She adopted a rabbit-sized dog that fit comfortably in her arms.
