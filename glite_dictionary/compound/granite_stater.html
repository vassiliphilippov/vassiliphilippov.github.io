Granite Stater

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænɪt ˌsteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡranɪt ˌsteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is a native or resident of New Hampshire, USA
person from New Hampshire

Examples

She's proud to be a lifelong Granite Stater and supports local businesses in her community.
Every Granite Stater knows that autumn is the best season to hike in the White Mountains.
The governor addressed concerns raised by many Granite Staters during the recent town hall meeting.

Etymology

From 'Granite State,' a nickname for New Hampshire referring to its granite formations, plus the suffix '-er'.
