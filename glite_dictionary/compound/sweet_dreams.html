sweet dreams

1. X

/ swit drimz / (AmE), / swiːt driːmz / (BrE)
used to wish someone pleasant or happy dreams before they go to sleep
wish for pleasant dreams

Examples

She kissed her son goodnight and whispered, sweet dreams.
Before turning off the lights, he said, "Sweet dreams!" to his wife.
It's a lovely gesture to wish your friends sweet dreams before bed.

Synset Examples

She whispered sweet dreams to her child before bed.
He always says sweet dreams before hanging up the phone.
Goodnight! I hope you have sweet dreams tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good night - a polite phrase used to express good wishes before sleeping
