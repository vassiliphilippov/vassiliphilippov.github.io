rate of pay

1. NOUN

/ reɪt əv peɪ / (AmE), / reɪt əv peɪ / (BrE)
the amount of money that is paid or received for work, usually calculated per hour, week, or other unit of time
amount of money paid per unit of time

Examples

The company increased the rate of pay for night shift employees to attract more workers.
Teachers are demanding a higher rate of pay to reflect the cost of living increases.
Her rate of pay went up after she gained certification in her field.

Synset Examples

women's pay rate is lower than men's

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - a fixed regular payment for work, usually paid on a daily or weekly basis
  • Synonym: salary - a fixed regular payment, typically paid monthly or annually but often expressed as an annual sum
