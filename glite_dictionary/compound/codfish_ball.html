codfish ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑdˌfɪʃ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒdˌfɪʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
A small ball of seasoned mashed potatoes mixed with flaked salt cod, usually breaded and fried.
fried ball of cod and potatoes

Examples

She prepared a batch of crisp codfish balls for Sunday brunch.
My grandmother's recipe for codfish balls is a family favorite.
The diner serves delicious homemade codfish balls with tartar sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishcake - a patty of flaked fish and potato
