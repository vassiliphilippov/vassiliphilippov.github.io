public transit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˈtrænzɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtrænsɪt / (BrE)
A system of shared transportation, such as buses and trains, available for use by the general public.
system of public passenger transport

Examples

Many cities are investing heavily in public transit to reduce road congestion.
She prefers taking public transit to driving her car to work every day.
The government announced a new plan to improve public transit in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public transportation - transportation systems designed for use by the general public
