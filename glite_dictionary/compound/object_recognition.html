object recognition

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːb.dʒɛkt ˌrɛk.əɡˈnɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈɒb.dʒɪkt ˌrɛk.əɡˈnɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the process by which the visual system identifies and interprets familiar objects in the environment
identifying objects by sight

Examples

Researchers have made significant advances in object recognition for self-driving cars.
Children develop object recognition skills as they grow and learn to identify familiar items.
Modern smartphones use object recognition to sort photos automatically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pattern recognition - the identification of patterns in data or signals, often by machines or the brain
