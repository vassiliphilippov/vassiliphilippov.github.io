electronic instrument

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
a musical instrument that produces sound by means of electronic circuits and technology, rather than acoustic or mechanical means.
musical instrument using electronic sound production

Examples

The musician created unique sounds using an electronic instrument during the performance.
Many modern bands incorporate at least one electronic instrument into their music.
She prefers playing an electronic instrument because of the wide range of tones it can produce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synthesizer - an electronic musical instrument that generates audio signals
