arrow grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈæroʊ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈærəʊ ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tufted perennial plant found in shallow water or marshland, sometimes known to poison livestock
marsh plant, can poison livestock

Examples

The marsh was thick with arrow grass and other aquatic plants.
Farmers warn that arrow grass can be dangerous to grazing animals.
We studied the ecology of arrow grass during the field trip.
