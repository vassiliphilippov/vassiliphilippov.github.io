striking portrait

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪkɪŋ ˈpɔrtrət / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkɪŋ ˈpɔːtrət / (BrE)
A painting or image of a person that is especially impressive or visually arresting.
impressive or eye-catching painting of a person

Examples

The gallery featured a striking portrait of a woman in vibrant colors.
Many visitors paused to admire the artist's striking portrait at the entrance.
She commissioned a striking portrait to commemorate her grandfather's legacy.

Synset Examples

The artist displayed a striking portrait at the gallery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portrait - a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person
