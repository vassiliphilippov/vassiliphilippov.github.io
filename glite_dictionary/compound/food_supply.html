food supply

1. NOUN

/ fuːd səˈplaɪ / (AmE), / fuːd səˈplaɪ / (BrE)
the amount or stock of food available for use, especially within a particular area or population
amount of food available

Examples

After the storm, the town's food supply was dangerously low.
They worked together to ensure a steady food supply for the orphanage.
A reliable food supply is crucial for any large city.

Synset Examples

The white maggot infestation ruined the food supply.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provision - supply of necessary materials, especially food
  • Antonym: food shortage - lack or insufficiency of available food
