pressure gauge

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛʃər ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure and display the pressure of a fluid, such as a gas or liquid, within a system
instrument for measuring fluid pressure

Examples

The engineer used a pressure gauge to check the system for any leaks.
Please make sure the pressure gauge is calibrated before running the experiment.
Many industrial machines have a pressure gauge attached for safety monitoring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manometer - device for measuring fluid pressure
