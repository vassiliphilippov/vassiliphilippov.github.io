coal-fired

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊl ˌfaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊl ˌfaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a machine, vehicle, or power station) powered by burning coal as a fuel.
powered by burning coal

Examples

The city still relies on a coal-fired power plant to generate electricity.
Regulations on coal-fired boilers have become stricter in recent years.
The museum features a restored coal-fired locomotive from the 19th century.

Synset Examples

a coal-fired ship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil-fired - powered by burning oil as a fuel
  • Synonym: gas-fired - powered by burning gas as a fuel
