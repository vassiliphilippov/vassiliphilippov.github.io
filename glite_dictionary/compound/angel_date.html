angel date

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪn.dʒəl deɪt / (AmE), / ˈeɪn.dʒəl deɪt / (BrE)
A romantic outing or engagement that is described as exceptionally pleasant, perfect, or heavenly.
exceptionally pleasant romantic outing

Examples

Our anniversary dinner was such an angel date that I didn't want the night to end.
He surprised her with an angel date at her favorite rooftop restaurant.
They remembered their first angel date as the moment they truly fell in love.

Synset Examples

They planned an angel date under the stars.
Her birthday dinner felt like an angel date.
