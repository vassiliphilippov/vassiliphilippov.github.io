Nilgiri Hills

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɪlˈɡɪri ˌhɪlz / (AmE), / ˌnɪlˈɡɪəri ˌhɪlz / (BrE)
a range of hills in southern India, known for their scenic beauty and tea plantations
hills in southern India

Examples

The Nilgiri Hills are famous for their lush tea estates and cool climate.
Many tourists visit the Nilgiri Hills to enjoy trekking and picturesque views.
The biodiversity of the Nilgiri Hills attracts researchers from around the world.

Etymology

From Tamil and Sanskrit: 'nil' (blue) and 'giri' (mountain).
