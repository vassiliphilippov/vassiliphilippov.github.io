shoddy product

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑdi ˈprɑdəkt / (AmE), / ˈʃɒdi ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
an item that is poorly made or of inferior quality
poorly made or inferior item

Examples

The company recalled the shoddy product after receiving hundreds of complaints about its durability.
We were disappointed to find that the toy was a shoddy product that broke within a day.
Customers should not have to pay full price for a shoddy product with obvious defects.

Synset Examples

She returned the shoddy product to the store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defective product - item with flaws or faults
  • Antonym: high-quality product - item that is well-made or superior in quality

Etymology

From 'shoddy', originally referring to recycled wool of poor quality in 19th-century Britain.
