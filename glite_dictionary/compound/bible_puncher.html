Bible puncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪbəl ˌpʌntʃər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl ˌpʌntʃə / (BrE)
A person who enthusiastically promotes or aggressively advocates religious beliefs, often in a forceful or intrusive manner.
aggressive advocate of religious beliefs

Examples

Many people found the Bible puncher at the street corner hard to ignore.
She was criticized as a Bible puncher for her relentless religious lectures at work.
The meeting grew tense when a well-known Bible puncher began dominating the conversation.

Synset Examples

He’s often seen as a Bible puncher in discussions.
The debate turned heated with a Bible puncher on stage.
She avoided the Bible puncher’s aggressive style of preaching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible thumper - a person who vigorously promotes Christian beliefs

Etymology

Formed from 'Bible' referring to the Christian holy book, and 'puncher' in the sense of one who forcefully promotes an idea. Compare to 'Bible thumper.'
