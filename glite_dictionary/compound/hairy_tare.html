hairy tare

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛri tɛr / (AmE), / ˈheəri teə / (BrE)
A European vetch plant (Vicia hirsuta) commonly grown for forage or as a cover crop.
a European vetch cultivated as forage

Examples

Farmers sometimes plant hairy tare to improve soil fertility and prevent erosion.
The field was covered with hairy tare to provide green fodder for the livestock.
Many gardeners use hairy tare as a natural cover crop in the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vetch - any climbing plant of the genus Vicia
