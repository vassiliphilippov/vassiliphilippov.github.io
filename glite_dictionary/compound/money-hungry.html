money-hungry

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌni ˌhʌŋɡri / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌhʌŋɡri / (BrE)
having a strong desire for money, often to the point of greed
greedy for money or profit

Examples

The money-hungry corporation cut employee benefits to increase profits.
People accused the developer of being money-hungry after he demolished the historical building.
She refused to work with money-hungry clients who only cared about their own gain.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greedy - having an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
