command post

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmænd poʊst / (AmE), / kəˈmɑːnd pəʊst / (BrE)
a military headquarters from which a commander directs and controls forces during operations
military headquarters for directing operations

Examples

The general set up a command post near the front line to oversee the operation.
Radio communications between the command post and the troops were maintained throughout the night.
After the attack, everyone was ordered to report immediately to the command post.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - main office or center from which operations are directed
