steering system

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪrɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The mechanism that allows a vehicle or other object to be directed or guided along a desired course, especially in motor vehicles.
mechanism for directing a vehicle

Examples

The car's steering system was upgraded for better control and safety.
After the accident, mechanics checked the steering system for any damage.
A faulty steering system can make it difficult to control the vehicle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steering wheel - the wheel used by the driver to steer a vehicle
