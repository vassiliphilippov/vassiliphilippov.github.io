James Bond

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒeɪmz bɑnd / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪmz bɒnd / (BrE)
A fictional British secret agent, also known by his code number 007, created by writer Ian Fleming and featured in numerous novels and films.
fictional British secret agent 007

Examples

He dreamed of being as suave and daring as James Bond when he was a child.
The latest James Bond film broke box office records worldwide.
She read every James Bond novel that Ian Fleming wrote.

Etymology

Created by Ian Fleming for his 1953 novel Casino Royale; the name was inspired by the ornithologist James Bond.
