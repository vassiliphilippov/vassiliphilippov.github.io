gas mileage

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the number of miles a vehicle can travel on one gallon of gasoline; a measure of fuel efficiency.
miles traveled per gallon of gasoline
american

Examples

My new car has really good gas mileage, so I rarely have to fill up the tank.
Drivers often compare models based on their gas mileage to save money on fuel costs.
The manufacturer advertises improved gas mileage for their latest hybrid vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel economy - the efficiency with which a vehicle converts fuel into distance traveled
  • Antonym: fuel consumption - the amount of fuel used by a vehicle over a distance
