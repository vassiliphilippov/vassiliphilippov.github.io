Republic of Estonia

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈstoʊniə / (AmE), / ɪˈstəʊniə / (BrE)
a country in northeastern Europe, situated on the Baltic Sea, with Tallinn as its capital
country in northeastern Europe

Examples

The Republic of Estonia declared independence in 1991 after the collapse of the Soviet Union.
Tallinn, the capital of the Republic of Estonia, is known for its medieval old town.
The Republic of Estonia is a member of the European Union and NATO.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Estonia - country in northeastern Europe

Etymology

From 'Estonia', which comes from medieval Latin Estia, possibly ultimately from an ancient local word.
