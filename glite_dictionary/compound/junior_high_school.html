junior high school

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒuːniər ˈhaɪ skuːl / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːniə ˈhaɪ skuːl / (BrE)
a school for students in the early years of secondary education, typically including grades 7 and 8, and sometimes grade 9
school for grades 7 and 8 (sometimes 9)
american

Examples

After graduating from elementary school, she attended junior high school in her hometown.
Many students join sports teams when they first enter junior high school.
His younger brother will start junior high school next September.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: middle school - school for grades between elementary and high school, often 6–8
