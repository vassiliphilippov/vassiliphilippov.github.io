blonde lilian

1. NOUN

/ blɑnd ˈlɪliən / (AmE), / blɒnd ˈlɪliən / (BrE)
A North American dogtooth plant with solitary white flowers that have yellow centers and blue or pink exteriors.
flowering North American dogtooth plant

Examples

The garden was filled with the delicate blooms of blonde lilian in early spring.
Botanists admired the unique coloration of the blonde lilian during their field study.
He planted several blonde lilians along the woodland trail for their striking beauty.
