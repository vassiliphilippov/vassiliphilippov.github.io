black opal

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈoʊ.pəl / (AmE), / blæk ˈəʊ.pəl / (BrE)
a variety of opal that has a dark body color and displays vibrant internal flashes of green, red, or other colors
dark opal with colorful internal flashes

Examples

The jeweler showed me a stunning black opal set in a gold ring.
Black opals are highly prized for their brilliant play of color.
She inherited a rare black opal pendant from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opal - a gemstone consisting of hydrated silica, known for its play of color
