red raspberry

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / rɛd ˈrazˌbɛri / (BrE)
a raspberry plant or its fruit that is red in color, belonging to several related species, especially Rubus idaeus.
a raspberry with red fruit

Examples

She picked a bowl of fresh red raspberries from the garden for dessert.
Red raspberry leaves are sometimes used to make herbal tea.
The pie was filled with sweet red raspberries and topped with whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: black raspberry - a raspberry species or variety with black fruit
