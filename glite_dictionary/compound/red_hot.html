red hot

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd hɑt / (AmE), / ˈrɛd hɒt / (BrE)
(US, regional) A hot dog or frankfurter served hot in a bun, especially in some parts of the United States.
hot dog or frankfurter in a bun
american

Examples

He ordered a red hot with mustard and onions at the diner.
Vendors at the baseball game were selling red hots to the crowd.
The red hot is a popular snack at fairs across upstate New York.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot dog - a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun
  • Synonym: frankfurter - a cooked sausage of beef or pork in a bun
