bad joke

1. NOUN

/ bæd dʒoʊk / (AmE), / bæd dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is unfunny, falls flat, or is inappropriate in a given context.
unfunny or inappropriate joke

Examples

Everyone was silent after he told a bad joke at the dinner table.
She knew it was a bad joke, but she laughed anyway to be polite.
That comedian is usually funny, but last night he made a really bad joke.

Synset Examples

I laughed politely, but it was a bad joke.
He told a bad joke that nobody found funny.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: off-color joke - a joke that is likely to be offensive or indecent
  • Antonym: good joke - a joke that is genuinely funny or well-received
