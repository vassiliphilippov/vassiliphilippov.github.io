rock snake

1. NOUN

/ rɑk seɪk / (AmE), / rɒk seɪk / (BrE)
a very large python (Python sebae) found in tropical and southern Africa
large African python

Examples

During their safari, they spotted a massive rock snake coiled around a tree branch.
A rock snake can grow over six meters long and is considered one of Africa's largest snakes.
The villagers called for help after discovering a rock snake near their homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: python - large nonvenomous constricting snake of the family Pythonidae
