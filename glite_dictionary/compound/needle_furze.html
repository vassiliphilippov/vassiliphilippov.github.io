needle furze

1. NOUN

/ ˈnidl ˌfɜrz / (AmE), / ˈniːdl ˌfɜːz / (BrE)
a prickly shrub with yellow flowers, found on moors in New England and Europe
prickly yellow-flowered shrub of moors

Examples

The fields were dotted with patches of needle furze swaying in the breeze.
Walking through the moor, she pricked her hand on a needle furze bush.
In spring, needle furze covers the landscape with bright yellow blossoms.
