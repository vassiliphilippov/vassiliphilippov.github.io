landlord special

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændˌlɔrd ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)lɔːd ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
an apartment or rental property with minimal upgrades or improvements, often offered at a lower rent
rental property with minimal improvements

When I moved into the landlord special, I noticed the paint was peeling and the appliances were outdated.
Many students choose a landlord special because the rent is lower than in renovated units.
The real estate agent warned us that the apartment was a classic landlord special with only cosmetic fixes.
