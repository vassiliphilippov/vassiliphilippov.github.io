continent-wide

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑn.tɪ.nəntˈwaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪ.nəntˈwaɪd / (BrE)
Affecting or involving the whole of a continent.
affecting the entire continent

Examples

The summit led to a continent-wide agreement on trade and environmental standards.
A continent-wide drought has affected millions of people this year.
The organization launched a continent-wide campaign to improve education across Africa.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: worldwide - affecting or relating to the whole world
  • Degree Lower: regional - relating to or characteristic of a particular region
