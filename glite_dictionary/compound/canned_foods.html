canned foods

1. NOUN

/ kænd fəʊdz / (AmE), / kænd fəʊdz / (BrE)
Foods that have been preserved by being sealed in airtight cans, typically for long-term storage.
foods preserved in cans

Examples

Many people keep canned foods in their pantry for emergencies.
The supermarket has an entire aisle dedicated to canned foods.
Because they last a long time, canned foods are popular for camping trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tinned food - food preserved in tins or cans (chiefly British)
