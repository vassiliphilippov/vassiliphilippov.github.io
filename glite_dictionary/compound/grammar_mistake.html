grammar mistake

1. NOUN

/ ˈgræmər mɪˈsteɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡræmə mɪˈsteɪk / (BrE)
an error in the use of grammar while speaking or writing a language
an error in grammatical usage

Examples

His presentation contained several grammar mistakes that made it hard to follow.
She often corrects her students when they make a grammar mistake.
It's important to review your writing for grammar mistakes before submitting your assignment.

Synset Examples

His grammar mistakes were distracting during the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grammatical error - an error in the application of grammar rules
