ionic medication

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈɑnɪk ˌmɛdəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / aɪˈɒnɪk ˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a medical treatment that uses a local electric current to introduce medicinal ions into body tissues
medical treatment using electric current to deliver medicine
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended ionic medication to reduce inflammation in the injured shoulder.
Many physical therapy clinics offer ionic medication as part of their pain management techniques.
By applying ionic medication, the medicine can reach deeper tissues than with traditional ointments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iontophoresis - process of introducing ionized substances through the skin by applying electric current
