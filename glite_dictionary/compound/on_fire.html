on fire

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ɒn ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
burning with flames; lighted up by fire.
burning with flames

Examples

The entire building was on fire by the time the firefighters arrived.
After the lightning strike, the massive tree was suddenly on fire.
Candles on fire decorated the tables at the party.
