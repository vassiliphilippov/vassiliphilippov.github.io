stone splitting machine

1. NOUN

/ stoʊn ˈsplɪtɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈsplɪtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used for splitting natural stone such as granite, marble, and sandstone into pieces
machine for splitting stone

Examples

The quarry invested in a new stone splitting machine to increase production efficiency.
Workers feed large blocks of granite into the stone splitting machine for processing.
Modern stone splitting machines have made custom paving stones more widely available.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone cutter - a machine or tool for cutting stone
