rock formation

1. NOUN

/ rɑk fɔrˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɒk fɔːˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a natural structure formed by rocks, typically as a result of geological processes
natural structure formed by rocks

Examples

The Grand Canyon is famous for its unique rock formations and vivid colors.
Hikers stopped to admire the unusual rock formation along the trail.
Scientists are studying the ancient rock formations to learn more about Earth's history.

Synset Examples

The geologist explained the rock formations in detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outcrop - a visible exposure of rock at the Earth's surface
