line of merchandise

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈmɜːtʃəndaɪs / (BrE)
a particular type or range of products offered for sale by a business or manufacturer
range of products sold by a business

Examples

The store introduced a new line of merchandise aimed at young adults.
We expanded our line of merchandise to include eco-friendly products.
Their popular line of merchandise features stylish, affordable clothing.

Synset Examples

a nice line of shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: product line - a set of related products sold by the same company
