English iris

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈaɪrɪs / (BrE)
a bulbous iris species native to the Pyrenees, cultivated for its large, delicate flowers in various colors except yellow
a cultivated iris with showy flowers

Examples

The English iris is favored by gardeners for its elegant, colorful blooms.
Unlike many other varieties, the English iris does not produce yellow flowers.
In early summer, the English iris adds a splash of color to flower beds.
