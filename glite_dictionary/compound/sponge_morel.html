sponge morel

1. NOUN

/ spʌndʒ məˈrɛl / (AmE), / spʌndʒ məˈrɛl / (BrE)
an edible mushroom with a rounded to elongated cap marked by an irregular pattern of pits and ridges, valued for cooking
an edible morel mushroom species

Examples

She was delighted to find a sponge morel growing beneath the old pine tree.
The chef included sautéed sponge morels as a delicacy in the spring tasting menu.
Many foragers consider the sponge morel one of the best wild mushrooms to eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morel - an edible mushroom with a honeycomb appearance
