to no degree

1. ADV

/ tə ˈnoʊ dɪˈɡri / (AmE), / tə ˈnəʊ dɪˈɡriː / (BrE)
not at all; in no way or manner
not at all; in no way

Examples

The report was to no degree influenced by outside opinions.
She was to no degree responsible for the outcome of the project.
His apology was to no degree convincing to the audience.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
