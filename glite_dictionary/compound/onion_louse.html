onion louse

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən laʊs / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən laʊs / (BrE)
a small insect that is harmful to onion plants and sometimes tobacco, typically referring to certain species of lice or thrips that infest these crops
insect pest of onions and tobacco

Examples

The farmer discovered an infestation of onion louse in his crop, threatening the harvest.
Applying organic pesticides helped control the spread of the onion louse in the field.
If left untreated, the onion louse can cause significant damage to both onions and tobacco plants.
