Eastern pasque flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈistərn ˈpæsk ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈpɑːsk ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a short, hairy perennial plant with early spring blue-violet or lilac flowers, found in North America and Siberia
a perennial plant with spring blue-violet flowers

Examples

The Eastern pasque flower blooms in early spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
Botanists studied the habitat of the rare Eastern pasque flower in Siberian meadows.
