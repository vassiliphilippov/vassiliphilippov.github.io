to dance all night

1. VERB

/ dæns ɔl naɪt / (AmE), / dɑːns ɔːl naɪt / (BrE)
to dance continuously for the entire night, often as part of a celebration or party
to dance the whole night

Examples

We danced all night at the wedding and didn't get home until sunrise.
She loves to dance all night whenever her favorite songs are playing.
They were exhausted after dancing all night at the music festival.

Synset Examples

They danced all night to the beats of Tranada.
The party girl danced all night without a care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party all night - to celebrate or socialize throughout the night
