sadospiritual side

1. NOUN

/ ˌsædoʊˈspɪrɪtʃuəl saɪd / (AmE), / ˌsædəʊˈspɪrɪtjʊəl saɪd / (BrE)
a personal aspect or inclination that combines elements of sadism and spirituality, often explored in self-development or alternative spiritual practices
blend of sadism and spirituality

Examples

Many participants came to terms with their sadospiritual side during the intensive workshop.
She wrote a memoir about accepting and integrating her sadospiritual side.
His sadospiritual side emerged through a mixture of meditation and ritualistic pain.

Synset Examples

They explored their sadospiritual side during the retreat.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sadism - the tendency to derive pleasure, especially sexual gratification, from inflicting pain or humiliation on others
  • Degree Lower: spiritually - relating to or affecting the human spirit or soul

Etymology

Blend of 'sadism' (from Marquis de Sade) and 'spiritual,' combining psychological and spiritual themes.
