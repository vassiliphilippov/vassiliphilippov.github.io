toilet-trained

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔɪlət treɪnd / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt treɪnd / (BrE)
(of a child or animal) having been taught to use the toilet instead of a diaper or similar.
having learned to use the toilet

Examples

By the age of three, most children are toilet-trained and no longer need diapers.
She was relieved when her puppy became toilet-trained after just a few weeks.
The preschool requires all children to be toilet-trained before enrollment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potty-trained - having learned to use the toilet (informal, especially for young children)
