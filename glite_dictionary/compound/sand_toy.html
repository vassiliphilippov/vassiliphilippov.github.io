sand toy

1. NOUN

/ sænd tɔɪ / (AmE), / sænd tɔɪ / (BrE)
A toy designed for playing with sand, especially at a beach or in a sandpit.
toy for playing with sand

Examples

Their children spent hours building castles with their sand toy at the beach.
The store sells every kind of sand toy, from buckets to small cranes.
He forgot to bring his favorite sand toy to the playground's sandpit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beach toy - toy made to be used at the beach
  • Synonym: sandbox - a box or area filled with sand for children to play in
