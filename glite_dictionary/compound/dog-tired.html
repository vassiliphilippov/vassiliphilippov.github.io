dog-tired

1. ADJ

/ dɔɡˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / dɒɡˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; exhausted.
extremely tired

Examples

After a long day at work, I was dog-tired and went straight to bed.
She felt dog-tired after running the marathon in the heat.
He came home dog-tired but still managed to prepare dinner for the family.

Synset Examples

After the hike, I was dog-tired and ready to sleep.
He collapsed on the couch, dog-tired from his workout.
She felt dog-tired after working all weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausted - very tired; completely drained of energy
  • Antonym: refreshed - no longer tired; full of energy

Etymology

Likely from comparison to the exhaustion of a working dog; first recorded in the late 19th century.
