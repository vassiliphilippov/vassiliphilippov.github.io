industry leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndəstri ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈɪndəstri ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person or company that is prominent or most influential in a particular industry
leading person or company in an industry

Examples

She is considered an industry leader in sustainable fashion.
The technology industry leader announced a groundbreaking new product today.
Many startups aspire to become an industry leader in their chosen field.

Synset Examples

The event was an exclusive gathering of industry leaders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market leader - company with the largest market share in an industry
