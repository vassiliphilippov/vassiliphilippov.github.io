idle words

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪdəl wɝdz / (AmE), / ˈaɪdl wɜːdz / (BrE)
statements or language that are empty, insincere, or lack meaningful content; talk without sincerity or substance
insincere or empty talk

Examples

Don't waste my time with your idle words; I want to hear the truth.
The politician's speech was full of idle words but lacked real solutions.
She dismissed his apology as nothing more than idle words.

Synset Examples

that's a lot of wind
don't give me any of that jazz

Related Terms

  • Synonym: empty words - statements that have no meaning or substance
  • Synonym: idle chatter - light or trivial conversation without serious meaning
  • Antonym: meaningful words - words that have substance or genuineness
