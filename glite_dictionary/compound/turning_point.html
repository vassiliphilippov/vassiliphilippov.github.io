turning point

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝnɪŋ ˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ ˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
an event or moment at which a significant change occurs, especially one that affects the future course of events
critical moment of significant change

Examples

Winning the championship was the turning point in her athletic career.
The discovery of penicillin marked a turning point in medical history.
The company's decision to invest in technology became a turning point for its future success.

Synset Examples

the agreement was a watershed in the history of both nations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watershed - crucial dividing point or landmark event
  • Synonym: milestone - important event marking progress or change

2. NOUN
