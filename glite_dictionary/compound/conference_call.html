conference call

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns kɔːl / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fər.əns kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call in which more than two people take part, often for business discussions
telephone call with multiple participants

Examples

We scheduled a conference call with the marketing team to discuss the new campaign.
During the conference call, each manager gave an update on their region's performance.
She joined the conference call from her home office in London.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teleconference - a conference involving participants in different locations linked by telecommunications devices
