crystal gazing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstl ˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstl ˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of staring into a crystal ball in an attempt to see visions of future or distant events.
fortune-telling using a crystal ball

Examples

The fortune teller practiced crystal gazing to predict her client's future.
Some people view crystal gazing as mere superstition rather than a scientific method.
During the séance, she tried crystal gazing to contact spirits from afar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrying - using a reflective surface to foresee future events
  • Synonym: divination - the practice of seeking knowledge of the future
