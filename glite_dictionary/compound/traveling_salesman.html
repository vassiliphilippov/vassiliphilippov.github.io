traveling salesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəlɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɪŋ ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
a salesperson whose job involves visiting customers in different locations, typically over a wide area, to sell goods or services
a salesperson who travels to see customers

Examples

Her father was a traveling salesman who spent most weeks on the road.
The traveling salesman visited each store in his region every month.
I met a traveling salesman who sold high-end kitchen appliances across three states.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commercial traveler - a salesperson who travels to visit clients, especially in British English
