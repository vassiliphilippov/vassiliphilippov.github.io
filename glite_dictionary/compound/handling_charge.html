handling charge

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændlɪŋ tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a fee charged for the packaging, processing, or mailing of an order
fee for packaging or mailing an order

Examples

Please note that a handling charge will be added to your total bill.
The company applies a small handling charge for online purchases.
Customers often complain about the unexpected handling charge at checkout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping charge - fee charged for sending goods by mail or courier
  • Synonym: service charge - an additional fee for a service provided
