to make a decision

1. VERB

/ meɪk ə dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / meɪk ə dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
to choose what should be done after considering different possibilities or options
to choose what to do after considering options

Examples

It's important to make a decision before the deadline passes.
After hours of discussion, the committee finally made a decision.
She found it difficult to make a decision when both offers were tempting.

Synset Examples

I need to decide where to go for vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decide - to choose or come to a resolution
  • Antonym: to hesitate - to pause before deciding
