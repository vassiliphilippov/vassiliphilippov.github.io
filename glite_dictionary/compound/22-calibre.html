.22-calibre

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛnti tuː ˈkæləbər / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti tuː ˈkalɪbə / (BrE)
Relating to a firearm bore or its ammunition that measures 0.22 inches (twenty-two hundredths of an inch) in diameter.
of a 0.22 inch caliber (firearms)

Examples

He used a .22-calibre rifle for target practice at the shooting range.
The police recovered a .22-calibre pistol near the crime scene.
My grandfather gave me his old .22-calibre hunting rifle when I turned eighteen.

Synset Examples

a .22 caliber pistol

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calibre - the internal diameter of a gun barrel

Etymology

From the numerical designation for bore diameter, using the decimal for inch measurement.
