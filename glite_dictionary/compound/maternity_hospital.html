maternity hospital

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɝː.nə.ti ˌhɑː.spɪ.təl / (AmE), / məˈtɜː.nɪ.ti ˌhɒs.pɪ.təl / (BrE)
a hospital specializing in the care of women during pregnancy and childbirth and in the care of newborn infants
hospital for childbirth and newborn care

Examples

She was admitted to the maternity hospital two weeks before her due date.
The city is planning to build a new maternity hospital to serve the growing population.
After delivery, both mother and baby stayed at the maternity hospital for several days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospital - a health care institution providing patient treatment
