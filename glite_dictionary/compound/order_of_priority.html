order of priority

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər əv praɪˈɔrɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əv praɪˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the arrangement of tasks or items according to their relative importance or urgency
ranking by importance or urgency

Examples

The manager reviewed all project requests and arranged them in order of priority.
Please complete the tasks in order of priority to ensure deadlines are met.
We must decide the order of priority before allocating resources to each department.

Synset Examples

Her tasks were listed in order of priority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: priority - the fact or condition of being regarded or treated as more important
