front entrance

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt ˈɛntrəns / (AmE), / frʌnt ˈɛntrəns / (BrE)
the main door or opening at the front of a building, providing access from outside
main door at the front of a building

Examples

We met by the front entrance of the museum before the tour started.
Please use the front entrance when you arrive for your appointment.
The front entrance was beautifully decorated for the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: back entrance - door or opening at the back of a building
