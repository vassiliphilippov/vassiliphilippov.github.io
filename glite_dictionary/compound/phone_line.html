phone line

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn laɪn / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn laɪn / (BrE)
a connection that allows telephone signals to be transmitted between two locations
telephone connection

Examples

The company installed a new phone line in our office last week.
Please do not use the internet while I'm on the phone line, as it slows down the connection.
We called the technician because there was static on the phone line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone line - a wire or circuit connecting telephones
