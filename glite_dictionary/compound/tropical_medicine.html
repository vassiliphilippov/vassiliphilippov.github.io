tropical medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑːpɪkəl ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl ˈmɛdəsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases commonly found in tropical regions
branch of medicine for tropical diseases
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in tropical medicine after volunteering in West Africa.
Advances in tropical medicine have reduced the impact of malaria and dengue fever.
The institute is renowned for its research in tropical medicine and public health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infectious disease - illnesses caused by pathogenic microorganisms
