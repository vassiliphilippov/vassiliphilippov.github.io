kid-glove precision

1. NOUN

/ kɪd ɡlʌv prɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / kɪd ɡlʌv prɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a level of precision that is extremely careful, gentle, or delicate
extremely careful precision

Examples

Every operation in the laboratory requires kid-glove precision to avoid contamination.
The watchmaker handled the tiny gears with kid-glove precision.
He edited the manuscript with kid-glove precision, catching even the smallest errors.

Synset Examples

She gave her presentation with kid-glove precision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicacy - the quality of being delicate or gentle
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or precision

Etymology

From 'kid-glove', originally referring to gloves made of kid leather and symbolizing gentleness or delicacy.
