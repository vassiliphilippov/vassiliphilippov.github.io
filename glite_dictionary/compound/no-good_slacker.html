no-good slacker

1. NOUN

/ ˌnoʊ ˈɡʊd ˈslækər / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈɡʊd ˈslækə / (BrE)
an extremely lazy and irresponsible person with no positive qualities
lazy, irresponsible person
informal

Examples

Everyone in the office thinks he's a no-good slacker who never gets anything done.
After years of being called a no-good slacker, he finally decided to prove everyone wrong.
She refused to date a no-good slacker and wanted someone with ambition.

Synset Examples

He’s a no-good slacker who never helps out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work
