bicycle wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkəl wiːl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ wiːl / (BrE)
the wheel that is part of a bicycle, usually consisting of a rim, hub, and spokes
the wheel of a bicycle

Examples

He replaced the broken bicycle wheel before heading out for his ride.
I need to pump more air into the front bicycle wheel.
The mechanic expertly trued the bicycle wheel to make sure it spun smoothly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheel - a circular object that turns on an axle and is used to move vehicles
  • Degree Lower: bicycle tire - the rubber tire mounted on a bicycle wheel
