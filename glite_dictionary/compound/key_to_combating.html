key to combating

1. NOUN

/ ki tə kəmˈbætɪŋ / (AmE), / kiː tə kəmˈbætɪŋ / (BrE)
essential factor or method for successfully dealing with a particular problem or challenge
essential factor for solving a problem

Examples

Education is the key to combating poverty in many communities.
Improved hygiene is a key to combating the spread of viruses.
Collaboration among nations is a key to combating global warming.

Synset Examples

Prevention is key to combating infectious disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: key to - something that is essential for achieving or understanding something
