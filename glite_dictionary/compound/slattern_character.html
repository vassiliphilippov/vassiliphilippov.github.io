slattern character

1. NOUN

/ ˈslæt̬ɚn ˈkɛrɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˈslætən ˈkærɪktə / (BrE)
A literary or dramatic character who is depicted as a promiscuous or slovenly woman.
a promiscuous/slovenly female character

Examples

The author used a slattern character to highlight themes of social decay in the novel.
Critics noted that the slattern character was a cliché, lacking depth in her portrayal.
Her role as the slattern character created conflict among the other figures in the play.

Synset Examples

In the novel, the slattern character adds drama to the plot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slattern - a slovenly, untidy woman, often with promiscuous connotation

Etymology

From 'slattern' (origin: possibly from Middle English meaning slovenly woman) + 'character'.
