horrendous traffic

1. NOUN

/ həˈrɛndəs ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / hɒˈrɛndəs ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
very severe or extremely bad traffic congestion.
extremely bad traffic congestion

Examples

We were late to the meeting because of the horrendous traffic on the highway.
After the concert, horrendous traffic made it difficult to leave the city center.
People often complain about horrendous traffic during the holiday season.

Synset Examples

Traffic in Suckramento was horrendous today.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: heavy traffic - traffic that is unusually slow or congested
  • Antonym: light traffic - traffic with few vehicles and little congestion
