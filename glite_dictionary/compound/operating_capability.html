operating capability

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpəˌreɪtɪŋ kəˌpeɪbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ kəˌpeɪbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the ability of a technological system to function as designed or intended
ability of a system to function as designed

Examples

The company's new aircraft has a greater operating capability than previous models.
Regular maintenance is essential to preserve the operating capability of complex machinery.
The upgrade improved the operating capability of the production line significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performance - the effectiveness with which something functions
