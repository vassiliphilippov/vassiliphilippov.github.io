indented cylinder

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɛntɪd sɪˈlɪndər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛntɪd sɪˈlɪndə / (BrE)
a rotating machine used for separating grain by kernel length, consisting of a cylinder with indented pockets that collect kernels of specific sizes.
machine for separating grain by kernel length

Examples

The grain was processed through an indented cylinder to remove broken kernels.
Modern mills often use an indented cylinder to improve the quality of their seed separation.
After calibration, the indented cylinder efficiently sorted the seeds by size.
