half dollar

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
A United States coin with a value of fifty cents, equal to half of a dollar.
U.S. fifty-cent coin

Examples

The cashier handed me a shiny half dollar as part of my change.
Collectors often seek rare half dollar coins from the early 20th century.
He saved every half dollar he received in a glass jar on his desk.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quarter - a United States coin worth one fourth of a dollar
  • Degree Higher: dollar - a United States coin worth one dollar
