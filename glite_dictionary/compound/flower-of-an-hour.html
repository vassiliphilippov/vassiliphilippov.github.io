flower-of-an-hour

1. NOUN

/ ˌflaʊɚ əv ən ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˌflaʊə əv ən ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A small annual herb native to the Old World tropics, known for its yellow, purple-centered flowers that last for a very short time; often found as a weed in North America.
a tropical annual with short-lived flowers

Examples

The delicate flower-of-an-hour blooms quickly fade by midday.
Gardeners often appreciate the fleeting beauty of the flower-of-an-hour in their summer beds.
We spotted several flowers-of-an-hour growing wild along the roadside.
