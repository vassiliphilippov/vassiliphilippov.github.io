air of gravitas

1. NOUN

/ ɛr əv ɡræv.ə.tɑs / (AmE), / eə əv ˈɡræv.ɪ.tæs / (BrE)
a quality or atmosphere of seriousness and dignity
serious and dignified atmosphere

Examples

The new CEO addressed the employees with an air of gravitas that immediately commanded respect.
The old library has an air of gravitas that makes visitors whisper upon entering.
His speech, delivered with an air of gravitas, moved the audience to silence.

Synset Examples

The ceremony had an air of gravitas that was palpable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gravitas - seriousness and dignity
  • Synonym: air of authority - appearance or manner suggesting authority

Etymology

From Latin 'gravitas' (weight, seriousness), paired with 'air' meaning manner or impression.
