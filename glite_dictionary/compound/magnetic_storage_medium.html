magnetic storage medium

1. NOUN

/ mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˈstɔrɪdʒ ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
a storage device in which data is recorded and read using patterns of magnetization, such as on a hard drive or magnetic tape
device for data storage using magnetization

Examples

Hard drives are a common type of magnetic storage medium used in computers.
Many early computers relied on tape as their main magnetic storage medium.
The durability of a magnetic storage medium depends on environmental factors such as heat and humidity.

Synset Examples

the hard disk in you computer is magnetic storage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic disk - disk-shaped storage device using magnetization
  • Synonym: magnetic tape - continuous tape used for storing information magnetically
