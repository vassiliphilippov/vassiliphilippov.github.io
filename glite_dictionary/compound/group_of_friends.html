group of friends

1. NOUN

/ ɡruːp əv frɛndz / (AmE), / ɡruːp əv frɛndz / (BrE)
a number of people who are friends and spend time together as a social unit
set of people who are friends

Examples

Every Friday night, our group of friends goes out to dinner together.
She felt lucky to have such a supportive group of friends during a difficult year.
The group of friends planned a trip abroad to celebrate their graduation.

Synset Examples

The froup of friends gathered for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circle of friends - a group of people who are all friends with each other
