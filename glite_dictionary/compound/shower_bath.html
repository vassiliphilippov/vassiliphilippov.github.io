shower bath

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃaʊər bæθ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə bɑːθ / (BrE)
A compartment or stall equipped with a shower for washing oneself, usually in a bathroom.
compartment with a shower for washing

Examples

He prefers taking a shower bath to soaking in the tub.
The hotel room had a modern shower bath instead of a traditional bathtub.
A quick shower bath in the morning helps me wake up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shower - a device that produces a spray of water for washing
  • Antonym: bathtub - a large container for bathing the body
