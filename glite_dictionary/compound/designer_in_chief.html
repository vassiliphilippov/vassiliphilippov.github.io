designer in chief

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzaɪnər ɪn tʃiːf / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪnə ɪn tʃiːf / (BrE)
the primary designer responsible for overseeing and directing a project or creative endeavor
chief designer on a project

Examples

As designer in chief, he set the creative direction for the entire architecture firm.
The designer in chief presented her vision for the new technology product at the launch event.
Appointed designer in chief, she coordinated all aspects of the fashion collection's development.

Synset Examples

She was the designer in chief for the new line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief designer - the main designer of something
  • Synonym: editor in chief - the most senior editor of a publication
