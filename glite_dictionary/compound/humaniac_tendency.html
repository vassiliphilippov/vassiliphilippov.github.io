humaniac tendency

1. NOUN

/ hjuˈmeɪniæk ˈtɛndənsi / (AmE), / hjuːˈmæniæk ˈtɛndənsi / (BrE)
an inclination or disposition toward performing humanitarian actions or supporting humanitarian causes
inclination toward humanitarian actions

Examples

Her humaniac tendency made her the first to help whenever disaster struck.
Many people admired his humaniac tendency to always think of others' needs before his own.
The organization sought volunteers with a humaniac tendency to lead their outreach programs.

Synset Examples

His humaniac tendencies often led him to volunteer for various causes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanitarian tendency - inclination to act in a humanitarian way

Etymology

Formed from 'humaniac' (blend of 'humanitarian' and '-maniac') and 'tendency'.
