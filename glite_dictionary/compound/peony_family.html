peony family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːəni ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpiːəni ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
A family of perennial rhizomatous herbs and shrubs, known scientifically as Paeoniaceae, native to temperate regions of Europe and North America.
family of perennial flowering plants (Paeoniaceae)

Examples

The peony family includes many popular garden plants prized for their large, showy blooms.
Many members of the peony family thrive in temperate climates around the world.
Botanists reclassified several species when the peony family was recognized as distinct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peony - a type of flowering plant with large showy flowers
