garbage disposal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ dɪˌspoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ dɪˌspəʊzəl / (BrE)
A device installed under a kitchen sink that grinds food waste so it can be washed away through the plumbing.
kitchen appliance for food waste disposal

Examples

We had a garbage disposal installed to make cleaning up after dinner easier.
Don't put eggshells in the garbage disposal or it might get clogged.
Our old garbage disposal finally broke, so we had to buy a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste disposer - another term for garbage disposal
