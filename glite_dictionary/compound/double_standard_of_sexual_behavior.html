double standard of sexual behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌb.əl ˈstæn.dɚd əv ˈsɛk.ʃu.əl bɪˈheɪ.vjɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl ˈstæn.dəd əv ˈsɛk.ʃu.əl bɪˈheɪ.vjə / (BrE)
a social code that permits greater sexual freedom for men than for women, often reflecting or reinforcing the subordination of women
different sexual norms for men and women

Examples

The double standard of sexual behavior has been challenged by feminist movements around the world.
Many films still portray a double standard of sexual behavior, judging women more harshly than men.
Society's double standard of sexual behavior often impacts young people as they form their identities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double standard - the application of different sets of principles for similar situations
  • Synonym: sexual double standard - a code allowing different sexual behavior for men and women
