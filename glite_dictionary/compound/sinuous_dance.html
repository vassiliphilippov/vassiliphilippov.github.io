sinuous dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪn.ju.əs dæns / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.ju.əs dɑːns / (BrE)
a dance characterized by graceful, flowing, and curving movements
graceful, flowing dance movement

Examples

The ballerina's sinuous dance told a story through each delicate gesture and curve.
Audiences were mesmerized by the sinuous dance performed under the moonlight.
Critics admired the sinuous dance for its seamless blend of strength and elegance.

Synset Examples

Her sinuous dance captivated everyone in the audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graceful dance - dance performed with elegance and smoothness
