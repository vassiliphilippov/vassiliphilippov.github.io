black marlin

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈmɑːrlɪn / (AmE), / blæk ˈmɑːlɪn / (BrE)
a large species of marlin found in the Pacific Ocean, known for its size and speed, and highly prized in game fishing
large game fish of the Pacific Ocean

Examples

The fisherman was thrilled to catch a black marlin off the coast of Australia.
Black marlin are among the fastest fish in the ocean, reaching impressive speeds when chasing prey.
Scientists are studying the migration patterns of the black marlin in the Pacific.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marlin - a large game fish with an elongated upper jaw
