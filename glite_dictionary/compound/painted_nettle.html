painted nettle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntɪd ˈnɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd ˈnɛtəl / (BrE)
a tropical perennial herb with brightly colored or variegated leaves, often grown as an ornamental plant; also known as coleus
ornamental plant with colorful leaves

Examples

The garden was filled with painted nettle, adding splashes of pink and purple to the borders.
She enjoys growing painted nettle indoors because of its attractive, variegated foliage.
Many landscapers recommend painted nettle for creating vibrant container displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coleus - another name for the painted nettle; a group of ornamental plants known for colorful foliage
