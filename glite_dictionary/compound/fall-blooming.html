fall-blooming

1. ADJ

/ fɔl ˈbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming or flowering during the autumn season.
blooming in autumn

Examples

The fall-blooming asters add vibrant color to the garden after summer flowers fade.
Many gardeners appreciate fall-blooming plants for their late-season interest.
Chrysanthemums are a classic example of a fall-blooming perennial.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spring-blooming - (of plants) blooming in the spring
  • Antonym: summer-blooming - (of plants) blooming in the summer
  • Antonym: winter-blooming - (of plants) blooming in the winter
