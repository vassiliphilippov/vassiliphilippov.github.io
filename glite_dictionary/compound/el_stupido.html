el stupido

1. NOUN

/ ɛl stuˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ɛl stjuːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
A humorous or self-deprecating term for someone acting foolish or making a silly mistake.
humorous term for foolishness
informal

Examples

He forgot where he parked his car again and muttered, "Nice going, el stupido."
After tripping over his own shoelaces, he laughed and called himself el stupido.
She texted the wrong person and joked, "Well done, el stupido strikes again!"

Synset Examples

After forgetting his keys again, he called himself el stupido.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or who makes foolish decisions
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently

Etymology

Formed humorously using Spanish articles ('el') and English-based mock-Spanish morphology ('stupido', mimicking Spanish 'estúpido').
