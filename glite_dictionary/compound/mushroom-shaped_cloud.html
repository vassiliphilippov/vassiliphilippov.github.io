mushroom-shaped cloud

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌʃˌruːm ʃeɪpt klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃrʊm ʃeɪpt klaʊd / (BrE)
a large cloud, shaped like a mushroom, formed by the aftermath of a powerful explosion, especially a nuclear bomb, consisting of dust, smoke, and debris rising high into the sky
cloud formed by a large explosion, mushroom-shaped

Examples

The atomic explosion created a massive mushroom-shaped cloud visible for miles around.
Photographs of the nuclear test show a distinct mushroom-shaped cloud towering over the desert.
Witnesses described the mushroom-shaped cloud rising rapidly after the bomb detonated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mushroom cloud - the iconic mushroom-shaped cloud produced by a nuclear explosion
