sea turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi ˌtɝː.təl / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌtɜː.tl̩ / (BrE)
Any of several large marine turtles with limbs adapted into flippers, typically found in warm seas.
large marine turtle with flipper-like limbs

Examples

The biologists observed a sea turtle laying eggs on the sandy beach.
Many species of sea turtle are threatened due to pollution and fishing nets.
She spotted a large sea turtle swimming gracefully near the coral reef.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: turtle - a reptile with a hard shell that lives in or near water
  • Degree Lower: freshwater turtle - turtle species that live in freshwater habitats
