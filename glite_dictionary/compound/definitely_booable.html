definitely booable

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛf.ɪ.nət.li ˈbuː.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈdef.ɪ.nɪt.li ˈbuː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a performance, action, etc.) completely deserving of being booed by an audience for being poor or unacceptable.
fully deserving to be booed

Examples

Many critics found the film definitely booable given its weak plot and lackluster acting.
His rude comments at the ceremony were definitely booable and drew negative reactions from the crowd.
If an athlete cheats during a game, that behavior is definitely booable by any standard.

Synset Examples

The performance was barely entertaining, definitely booable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: booable - deserving to be booed
  • Synonym: unimpressive - not deserving admiration or approval
