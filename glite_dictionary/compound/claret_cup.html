claret cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛrət kʌp / (AmE), / ˈklærət kʌp / (BrE)
a chilled punch made from claret wine, brandy, lemon juice, sugar, and sometimes sherry or curaçao, often garnished with fresh fruit
a type of wine-based punch

Examples

For the summer party, we served a refreshing claret cup to our guests.
Her recipe for claret cup includes orange slices and a splash of curaçao for extra flavor.
A chilled claret cup is a classic Victorian drink perfect for picnics and garden gatherings.

Etymology

From 'claret' meaning a type of red Bordeaux wine and 'cup' referring to a punch or mixed drink.
