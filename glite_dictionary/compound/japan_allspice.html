Japan allspice

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæn ˈɔlˌspaɪs / (AmE), / dʒəˈpan ˈɔːlˌspaɪs / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub from Japan, cultivated for its fragrant yellow flowers
Japanese shrub with fragrant yellow flowers

Examples

The Japan allspice in my garden fills the air with a sweet fragrance every winter.
She planted a Japan allspice near the patio for its lovely yellow blooms.
Botanists admire Japan allspice for its early flowering and pleasant scent.
