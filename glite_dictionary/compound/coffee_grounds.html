coffee grounds

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi ɡraʊndz / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ɡraʊndz / (BrE)
the particles of ground coffee remaining after the coffee has been brewed
the dregs left after brewing coffee

Examples

After brewing a fresh pot, she used the coffee grounds to fertilize her garden.
The filter caught all the coffee grounds, leaving the drink smooth and clear.
He accidentally spilled coffee grounds all over the counter while cleaning out the machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grounds - solid particles left after liquid has been filtered through a substance
