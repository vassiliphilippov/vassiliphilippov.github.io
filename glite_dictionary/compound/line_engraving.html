line engraving

1. NOUN

/ laɪn ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / laɪn ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
A print made from an engraved metal plate with lines incised into its surface, typically used for illustrations and reproductions.
print made from an engraved metal plate

Examples

The museum displayed a rare line engraving from the eighteenth century.
He studied the details in the line engraving, admiring the precision of each mark.
Many classic books were once illustrated with line engravings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engraving - an image or design printed from an engraved surface

an engraving made with incised lines

Examples
