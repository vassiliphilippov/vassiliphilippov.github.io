arctic poppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrktɪk ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈɑːktɪk ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
a subarctic perennial poppy of both hemispheres, with fragrant white, yellow, orange, or peach flowers
a subarctic flowering poppy species

Examples

The arctic poppy blooms early in the summer in the tundra regions.
We admired the pale yellow flowers of the arctic poppy while hiking in northern Alaska.
Botanists study how the arctic poppy survives such extreme cold environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poppy - flowering plant of the genus Papaver
