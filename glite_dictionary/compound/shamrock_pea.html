shamrock pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæm.rɑk pi / (AmE), / ˈʃæm.rɒk piː / (BrE)
a trailing plant native to Asia and Africa, with three-part leaves and bright cobalt blue flowers
a trailing plant with blue flowers

Examples

The garden was covered with a carpet of shamrock pea vines in full bloom.
She admired the vibrant cobalt flowers of the shamrock pea during her hike in Africa.
Botanists have noted the resilience of the shamrock pea in diverse climates.
