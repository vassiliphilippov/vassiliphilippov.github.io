Aussie kiss

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔzi kɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒzi kɪs / (BrE)
a kiss involving the touching of noses or performed with eyes open, associated with Australian custom
kiss involving noses or open eyes
informal

Examples

They shared an Aussie kiss before saying goodbye at the airport.
He gave her a playful Aussie kiss, brushing his nose against hers.
During the game, couples had to demonstrate the perfect Aussie kiss.
