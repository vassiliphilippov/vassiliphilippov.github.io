washing day

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
a day designated for doing household laundry, typically on a regular basis
day for doing household laundry

Examples

Monday was always washing day in my grandmother's house.
Rain can be a real nuisance on washing day.
They assigned Thursday as their regular washing day to keep the routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laundry day - a day dedicated to washing clothes
