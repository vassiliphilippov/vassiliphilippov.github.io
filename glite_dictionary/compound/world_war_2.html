World War 2

1. PROPN

/ wɝrld wɑr tuː / (AmE), / wɜːld wɔː tuː / (BrE)
The global war fought from 1939 to 1945 between the Allies and the Axis powers, resulting in significant worldwide impact.
global war from 1939 to 1945

Examples

Many families were separated during World War 2 as soldiers left to fight overseas.
The devastation caused by World War 2 led to major changes in international relations.
Some of the greatest advancements in technology came as a result of World War 2.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: World War I - the global war fought from 1914 to 1918; also called the Great War
  • Degree Higher: Cold War - the period of political tension following World War 2, primarily between the US and the USSR
