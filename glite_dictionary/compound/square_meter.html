square meter

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ˈmiːtɚ / (AmE), / skwɛə ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to the area of a square with sides measuring one meter; used in the metric system
unit of area (metric system)

Examples

The apartment has a total area of 85 square meters.
One square meter of fabric was needed to make the dress.
Prices are often listed per square meter when buying carpet.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: square foot - a unit of area equal to a square with sides of one foot

Etymology

Formed from 'square' (shape) and 'meter' (unit of length), used together since adoption of the metric system.
