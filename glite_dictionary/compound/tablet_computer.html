tablet computer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæblət kəmˈpjuːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtæblət kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
a portable computing device with a touchscreen interface, designed to be operated primarily by touch
portable touchscreen computing device

Examples

Many students now use a tablet computer instead of carrying heavy textbooks.
She drew the design on her tablet computer during the meeting.
The new tablet computer is lightweight and easy to carry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablet - a portable touch-screen device
  • Synonym: laptop - a portable computer with a keyboard and screen attached
