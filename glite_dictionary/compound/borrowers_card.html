borrower's card

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːr.oʊ.ɚz kɑːrd / (AmE), / ˈbɒr.əʊ.əz kɑːd / (BrE)
a card issued by a library certifying that its holder has the right to borrow books or other materials
library card granting borrowing rights

Examples

You need your borrower's card to check out books from the library.
She forgot her borrower's card at home and couldn't borrow any magazines.
A valid borrower's card is required to access digital resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: library card - a card that identifies a library user and enables borrowing rights
