scale leaf

1. NOUN

/ skeɪl lif / (AmE), / skeɪl liːf / (BrE)
a specialized leaf or bract in certain plants that protects a bud or catkin
protective leaf in some plants

Examples

The scale leaf covers the bud, protecting it from harsh winter weather.
As the bud opens in spring, the scale leaf falls away.
The scale leaves of the pine tree are tough and overlap closely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bract - a specialized leaf associated with a reproductive structure
  • Synonym: bud scale - a scale-like structure protecting a plant bud
