grayish brown

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ braʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ braʊn / (BrE)
a color or pigment that is a mixture of grey and brown, resulting in a light dull brown shade
a light grey-brown color

Examples

The bird's feathers were a soft grayish brown that helped it blend with the woodland floor.
She chose a grayish brown paint for the living room to create a warm atmosphere.
His jacket was a faded grayish brown, showing years of use under the sun.

Synset Examples

she wore dun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dun - a dull grayish-brown color
