congou tea

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑŋ.ɡuː ti / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋ.ɡuː tiː / (BrE)
A variety of Chinese black tea made with carefully rolled leaves, known for its smooth and mellow flavor.
variety of Chinese black tea

Examples

She served a pot of congou tea with delicate pastries in the afternoon.
The shop imports rare congou tea directly from China.
He preferred congou tea for its rich aroma and smooth taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black tea - tea that is more oxidized than green, oolong, and white teas

Etymology

From Chinese (Mandarin) gōngfu chá, meaning 'skillfully made tea'.
