bad looker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæd ˈlʊkər / (AmE), / ˈbad ˈlʊkə / (BrE)
a person who is considered unattractive in appearance
an unattractive person

Examples

Despite being called a bad looker, he had many friends because of his sense of humor.
She didn't care that some considered her a bad looker; she was confident in herself.
He may be a bad looker, but his kindness makes him appealing to everyone.

Synset Examples

He's a bad looker, but his personality shines.
She said he wasn't a bad looker, just not her type.
He’s not a bad looker, but he lacks confidence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good looker - an attractive person
