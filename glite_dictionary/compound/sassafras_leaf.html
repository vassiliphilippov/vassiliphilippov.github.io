sassafras leaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæsəˌfræs lif / (AmE), / ˈsasəˌfras liːf / (BrE)
a leaf from the sassafras tree, used as a flavoring in cooking and herbal drinks
leaf of the sassafras tree used as flavoring

Examples

She added a sassafras leaf to the stew to give it a unique flavor.
The herbalist recommended brewing tea with dried sassafras leaves for a soothing drink.
Gumbo recipes in Louisiana often call for ground sassafras leaves as a thickener and spice.

Synset Examples

She brewed a tea from sassafrac leaves for flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bay leaf - aromatic leaf used for flavoring in cooking

Etymology

From Spanish *sassafrás*, via French, ultimately from a Native American language.
