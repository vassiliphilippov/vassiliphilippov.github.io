partial denture

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrʃəl ˈdɛn.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːʃəl ˈdɛn.tʃə / (BrE)
a removable dental appliance that replaces one or more missing teeth in a dental arch
removable dental appliance for missing teeth
medical

Examples

My grandmother got a new partial denture after losing two of her back teeth.
The dentist recommended a partial denture to fill the gap left by my missing molar.
Wearing a partial denture improved his ability to chew and speak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: denture - a removable plate or frame holding one or more artificial teeth
