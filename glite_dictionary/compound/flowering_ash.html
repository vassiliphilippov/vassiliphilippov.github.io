flowering ash

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ˌæʃ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˌaʃ / (BrE)
A southern Mediterranean ash tree with fragrant white flowers in dense clusters, known for yielding manna.
southern European ash tree with white flowers

Examples

The flowering ash is admired for its masses of white blossoms in late spring.
Beekeepers appreciate the flowering ash because its flowers attract pollinators.
In southern Italy, manna is harvested from the sap of the flowering ash.
