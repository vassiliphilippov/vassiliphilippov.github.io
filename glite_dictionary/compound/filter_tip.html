filter tip

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪl.tɚ tɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.tə tɪp / (BrE)
a small device at the end of a cigarette containing material that filters smoke before it is inhaled
device at end of cigarette to filter smoke

Examples

He only smokes cigarettes with a filter tip to reduce the harshness of the smoke.
The filter tip became standard on most cigarettes during the 20th century.
Scientists debate how effective the filter tip actually is at removing toxins from cigarette smoke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette filter - small filter fitted to a cigarette to reduce intake of harmful substances
