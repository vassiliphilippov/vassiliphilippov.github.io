nurse practitioner

1. NOUN

/ nɜrs prækˈtɪʃənər / (AmE), / nɜːs prækˈtɪʃənə / (BrE)
a registered nurse who has received advanced training and is qualified to perform many of the duties of a physician, including diagnosing and treating illnesses
advanced practice registered nurse
medical

Examples

After her appointment with the nurse practitioner, Maria felt confident about her treatment plan.
A nurse practitioner can prescribe medication and order diagnostic tests in many states.
The clinic hired an experienced nurse practitioner to expand its primary care services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physician assistant - a trained medical professional who assists physicians but is not a doctor
