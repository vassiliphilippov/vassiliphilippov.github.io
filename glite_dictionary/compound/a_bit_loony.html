a bit loony

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈluːni / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈluːni / (BrE)
Somewhat crazy or silly; slightly eccentric or unbalanced.
somewhat crazy or silly
informal

Examples

Sometimes my uncle acts a bit loony when he's excited.
That movie was a bit loony, but I enjoyed its humor.
His plan sounded a bit loony, but it just might work.
