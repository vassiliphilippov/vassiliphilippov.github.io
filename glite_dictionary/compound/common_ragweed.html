common ragweed

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmən ˈrægˌwid / (AmE), / ˌkɒmən ˈræɡˌwiːd / (BrE)
an annual North American weed with finely divided leaves and spikes of small green flowers, often causing hay fever
annual North American weed, causes hay fever

Examples

Many people develop allergies in late summer due to common ragweed.
We found common ragweed growing along the edge of the field.
Common ragweed spreads easily and can be difficult to eradicate from gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ragweed - any of various weedy plants of the genus Ambrosia
