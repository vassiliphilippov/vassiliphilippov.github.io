bicycle pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkəl pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl̩ pʌmp / (BrE)
a device for inflating the tires of a bicycle by forcing air into them
device for inflating bicycle tires

Examples

He used a bicycle pump to inflate his flat tire before the ride.
My old bicycle pump broke, so I had to buy a new one.
Don't forget to bring your bicycle pump on the cycling trip in case someone gets a flat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air pump - a device for pumping air into something
