Saturday night special

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæɾɚdeɪ ˌnaɪt ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsætədeɪ ˌnaɪt ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
A small, inexpensive handgun that is easy to acquire, often associated with criminal activity.
cheap, easily obtained handgun

Examples

He was arrested with a Saturday night special hidden in his jacket pocket.
Many cities have laws restricting the sale of Saturday night specials.
The police believe the shooting was carried out with a Saturday night special.

Etymology

Refers to the idea of such guns being bought cheaply for criminal acts typically committed on a Saturday night.
