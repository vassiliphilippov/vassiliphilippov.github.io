1-1-8

1. NOUN

/ wʌn wʌn eɪt / (AmE), / wʌn wʌn eɪt / (BrE)
An emergency telephone number used in some countries to contact emergency services such as police, fire, or ambulance.
emergency telephone number

Examples

If you witness an accident, dial 1-1-8 immediately for help.
In Italy, 1-1-8 is the number to call for an ambulance.
The child was taught how to contact emergency services by calling 1-1-8.
