personal check

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənəl tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl tʃɛk / (BrE)
a cheque written by an individual that is drawn on funds from their personal bank account
a cheque from an individual's personal bank account

Examples

You can pay with a personal check or by credit card at the front desk.
The landlord does not accept personal checks for the security deposit, only cashier’s checks or money orders.
Her personal check was returned due to insufficient funds.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cashier's check - a check guaranteed by a bank, drawn on the bank's own funds
  • Antonym: certified check - a check guaranteed by a bank as to payment
