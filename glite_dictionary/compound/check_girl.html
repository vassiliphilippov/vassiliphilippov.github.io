check girl

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛk ɡɝːl / (AmE), / tʃɛk ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female attendant responsible for checking coats, parcels, or other items at the entrance of a public place such as a restaurant or theater
female coat or parcel checker

Examples

The check girl handed me a ticket for my coat when I entered the restaurant.
She worked as a check girl at the theater throughout college.
The check girl smiled as she returned my umbrella at the end of the evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat check - an area or counter where coats and parcels are left temporarily
  • Synonym: checkroom attendant - a person who works in a coat check area
