large purchase

1. NOUN

/ lɑrdʒ ˈpɝːtʃəs / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ ˈpɜːtʃəs / (BrE)
an item or group of items bought that involve a substantial amount of money
a significant, costly transaction

Examples

After saving for months, Luke finally made a large purchase and bought himself a new car.
It's important to think carefully before committing to a large purchase like a house.
Many people take out loans to finance large purchases such as appliances or furniture.

Synset Examples

She made a large purchase at the electronics store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: major purchase - an important or significant purchase
