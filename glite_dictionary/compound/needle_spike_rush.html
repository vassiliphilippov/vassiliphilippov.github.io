needle spike rush

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdl̩ spaɪk ˌrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈniːdl spaɪk ˌrʌʃ / (BrE)
A fine-leaved aquatic plant of the spike rush genus, commonly used as an aerator in aquariums.
fine-leaved aquatic plant used in aquariums

Examples

The aquarium looked healthier after we added needle spike rush to help oxygenate the water.
Many aquarists prefer needle spike rush because of its attractive, slender leaves.
She planted several needle spike rushes along the pond's edge for a natural look.
