scrap metal

1. NOUN

/ skræp ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / skræp ˈmet.əl / (BrE)
Metal that has been discarded or is no longer wanted, typically collected and sold to be melted down and reused.
discarded metal for recycling

Examples

The old factory was filled with piles of scrap metal waiting to be collected.
She collects scrap metal from construction sites and sells it to recycling centers.
After the storm, broken fences and other scrap metal were left lying in the street.

Synset Examples

he finally sold the car for scrap metal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junk - old or discarded articles; useless waste
  • Synonym: waste metal - metal that is no longer useful except for being recycled
