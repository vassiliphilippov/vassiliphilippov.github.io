bean blight

1. NOUN

/ bin blaɪt / (AmE), / biːn blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease of bean plants caused by various fungi or bacteria, resulting in spotting, wilting, or death of the plants
disease affecting bean plants

Examples

The crop yield was reduced significantly due to bean blight spreading across the fields.
Farmers are advised to look for early symptoms of bean blight during the rainy season.
Applying the recommended fungicide can help prevent the onset of bean blight in your garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blight - a plant disease, especially one caused by fungi, resulting in withering or death
