spaghetti and meatballs

1. NOUN

/ spəˈɡɛti ənd ˈmitˌbɔlz / (AmE), / spəˈɡɛti ənd ˈmiːtbɔːlz / (BrE)
An Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti served with meatballs in a tomato sauce.
Italian-American pasta dish with meatballs

Examples

Every Sunday, my grandmother would cook spaghetti and meatballs for the whole family.
The restaurant is famous for its homemade spaghetti and meatballs.
For dinner tonight, I’m craving classic spaghetti and meatballs with garlic bread.
