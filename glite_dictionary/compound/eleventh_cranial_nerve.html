eleventh cranial nerve

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜrv / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜːv / (BrE)
the accessory nerve; the eleventh cranial nerve that arises from cranial and spinal roots and controls certain neck and shoulder muscles
accessory nerve; eleventh cranial nerve
medical

Examples

Damage to the eleventh cranial nerve can lead to weakness in the shoulder muscles.
The surgeon identified the eleventh cranial nerve during the neck dissection.
Injury to the eleventh cranial nerve may affect head rotation and shoulder elevation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessory nerve - another name for the eleventh cranial nerve; nerve controlling neck/shoulder muscles
