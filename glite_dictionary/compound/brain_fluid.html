brain fluid

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈfluɪd / (AmE), / breɪn ˈfluːɪd / (BrE)
the fluid present in the brain that helps protect it and supports cognitive function
fluid in the brain providing protection and function

Examples

The researcher measured the level of brain fluid in the laboratory mice.
An imbalance of brain fluid can cause pressure on the brain.
During the procedure, a sample of brain fluid was collected for testing.
