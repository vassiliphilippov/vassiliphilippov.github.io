pepper vodka

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛpər ˈvɑdə / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ˈvɒdkə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic beverage, specifically vodka, that is flavored with pepper to give it a spicy taste.
vodka flavored with pepper

Examples

The bartender recommended the house-made pepper vodka for my Bloody Mary.
She enjoys experimenting with different infused spirits, especially pepper vodka.
A shot of pepper vodka adds a fiery twist to classic cocktails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage from eastern Europe, usually made from grain or potatoes
