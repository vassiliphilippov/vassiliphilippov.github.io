Pokomo language

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈkoʊmoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / pəˈkəʊməʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken by the Pokomo people in the coastal areas of Kenya
Bantu language of Kenyan coast

Examples

The Pokomo language is taught in some primary schools in coastal Kenya.
Researchers have published a dictionary of the Pokomo language.
She is fluent in both Swahili and the Pokomo language.

Etymology

From 'Pokomo', the ethnic group that speaks the language, plus 'language'.
