Hawkeye State

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑːkˌaɪ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkˌaɪ steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Iowa in the Midwestern United States
nickname for the state of Iowa

Examples

After moving to the Hawkeye State, she quickly adapted to life in Iowa.
The Hawkeye State is known for its rolling plains and agricultural heritage.
Many presidential candidates visit the Hawkeye State early in the election season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iowa - a state in the Midwestern United States

Etymology

Refers to Chief Black Hawk, a leader of the Sauk Native American tribe; adopted as Iowa's nickname in the 19th century.
