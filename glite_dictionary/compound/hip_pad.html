hip pad

1. NOUN

/ hɪp pæd / (AmE), / hɪp pad / (BrE)
a protective pad worn on the hip, especially by football and hockey players, to cushion against impacts
protective garment for hips in sports

Examples

Each player is required to wear a hip pad for extra protection during the game.
The hip pad absorbs some of the impact when a player falls to the side.
He checked his hip pad before stepping onto the ice to make sure it was secure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoulder pad - protective pad worn on the shoulder, often in sports
  • Synonym: knee pad - protective pad worn on the knee, typically in sports or work
