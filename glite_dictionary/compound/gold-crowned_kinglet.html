gold-crowned kinglet

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡoʊldˈkraʊnd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldˈkraʊnd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
A small North American songbird (Regulus satrapa) with a distinct golden crest on its head.
a North American songbird with a golden crown

Examples

A gold-crowned kinglet flitted among the pine branches, searching for tiny insects.
Birdwatchers in the park were thrilled to spot a gold-crowned kinglet during the migration season.
The distinctive golden crown helps to easily identify the gold-crowned kinglet from other small songbirds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruby-crowned kinglet - A similar North American kinglet species with a red crown
