tramp's spurge

1. NOUN

/ træmps spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / træmps spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
A common perennial plant of the United States, known for its showy white petal-like bracts.
U.S. perennial plant with white bracts

Examples

Tramp's spurge is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive white bracts.
We spotted a cluster of tramp's spurge growing along the roadside.
The unique appearance of tramp's spurge makes it easy to identify in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Euphorbia marginata - Scientific name for tramp's spurge
