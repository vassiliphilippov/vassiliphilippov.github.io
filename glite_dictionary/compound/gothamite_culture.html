Gothamite culture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːðəmaɪt ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθəmaɪt ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The distinctive cultural practices, traditions, and social behaviors of the residents of Gotham City, often referring to the fictional setting in comic books and related media.
culture of Gotham City residents

Examples

The Gothamite culture is known for its blend of resilience and creativity in the face of adversity.
Many movies explore how Gothamite culture responds to crime and heroism in the city.
Art festivals are a cherished tradition in Gothamite culture, reflecting the city's diversity.

Synset Examples

The Gothamite culture is vibrant and full of life.

Etymology

From 'Gotham', the fictional city in DC Comics, and '-ite' (denoting inhabitants of a place), plus 'culture'.
