bank manager

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ˈmænɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person in charge of the operations and staff at a branch of a bank
manager of a bank branch

Examples

The bank manager approved her loan application after reviewing her financial history.
If you have any issues with your account, please ask to speak to the bank manager.
After ten years as a teller, she was promoted to bank manager at a local branch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: branch manager - manager of a branch office in any business
