baby grand piano

1. NOUN

/ ˌbeɪ.bi ˌɡrænd piˈænoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbeɪ.bi ˌɡrænd piˈænəʊ / (BrE)
a small grand piano, typically shorter in length than a standard grand piano but with a similar shape
a small grand piano

Examples

Their living room was dominated by a beautiful baby grand piano near the window.
She took lessons on a baby grand piano, which fit perfectly in her apartment.
The concert hall displayed both a full grand and a baby grand piano for performances.
