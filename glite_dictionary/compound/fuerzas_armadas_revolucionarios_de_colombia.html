Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarios de Colombia

1. PROPN

/ ˌfwɛr.sɑs ɑrˈmɑːðɑs ɾeˌβoluθjoˈnarjos ðe koˈlombja / (AmE), / ˌfjʊə.sɑs ɑːˈmɑːdɑs rɛˌvɒljuˈnɑːrɪɒs deɪ kɒˈlɒmbiə / (BrE)
a Marxist-Leninist guerrilla organization in Colombia, founded in 1957, known for armed struggle, political activism, and involvement with drug trafficking; commonly known as FARC.
Colombian guerrilla group known as FARC

Examples

The Colombian government held peace talks with the Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarios de Colombia to end decades of conflict.
Many rural areas were under the control of the Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarios de Colombia for years.
Reports linked the Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarios de Colombia to various drug trafficking operations.

Etymology

Spanish for 'Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia'.
