vintage cookbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ ˈkʊk.bʊk / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ ˈkʊk.bʊk / (BrE)
a cookbook that is old, classic, or from a previous era, often valued for its age and historical significance
old or classic cookbook

Examples

She collects vintage cookbooks to learn about traditional recipes from different cultures.
The local library hosted a display of rare vintage cookbooks from the 19th century.
A vintage cookbook can offer unique insight into the eating habits of previous generations.

Synset Examples

He gifted her a vintage cookbook for her birthday.
