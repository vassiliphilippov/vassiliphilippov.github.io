enclosed space

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkloʊzd speɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊzd speɪs / (BrE)
an area or room that is completely or mostly surrounded by walls, barriers, or other boundaries
area surrounded by boundaries

Examples

The chemicals should always be handled carefully, especially in an enclosed space without proper ventilation.
The cat slept comfortably in a small enclosed space she found under the stairs.
Firefighters are trained to rescue people trapped in an enclosed space.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open space - an area that is not surrounded or confined by boundaries
