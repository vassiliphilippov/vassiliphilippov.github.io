half snipe

1. NOUN

/ hæf snaɪp / (AmE), / hɑːf snaɪp / (BrE)
a small short-billed snipe found in the Old World, especially referring to the Jack snipe (Lymnocryptes minimus).
a small Old World snipe bird

Examples

The wetland reserve is a perfect habitat for the elusive half snipe during migration season.
Birdwatchers were thrilled to spot a half snipe feeding along the muddy banks.
Unlike its larger relatives, the half snipe is noted for its shorter bill and secretive nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jack snipe - a small species of snipe found in Eurasia (Lymnocryptes minimus)
