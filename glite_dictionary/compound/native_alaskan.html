Native Alaskan

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.tɪv əˈlæs.kən / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv əˈlæ.skən / (BrE)
a member or descendant of any of the aboriginal peoples of Alaska
indigenous person of Alaska

Examples

Many Native Alaskan communities are located in remote regions accessible only by boat or plane.
The museum features artwork created by Native Alaskan artists.
She is proud of her heritage as a Native Alaskan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alaska Native - an indigenous person of Alaska; often used interchangeably
