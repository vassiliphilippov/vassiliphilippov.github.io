little angel

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (BrE)
An affectionate term used to refer to a well-behaved or cherished child.
affectionate term for a child

Examples

My son was such a little angel at the wedding, charming everyone he met.
After cleaning her room without being asked, her mother called her a little angel.
The teacher praised the little angel who helped a classmate with his homework.

Synset Examples

He calls his daughter his little angel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angel - a person regarded as good or innocent
