Italian bee

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæljən biː / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliən biː / (BrE)
a yellowish variety of honeybee known for its gentle nature and high productivity, similar in behavior to the Carniolan bee
a yellowish breed of honeybee

Examples

Many beekeepers prefer raising Italian bees because of their gentle temperament.
The Italian bee is famous for its excellent honey production throughout the summer.
Our hive contains mostly Italian bees, which are easy to manage and highly productive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Carniolan bee - a breed of honeybee similar in behavior to the Italian bee
