cradle robber

1. NOUN

/ ˈkreɪ.dl ˌrɑː.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.dl ˌrɒ.bə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who dates or forms romantic relationships with someone significantly younger than themselves
person who dates much younger partners
informal slang

Examples

Many people whispered that he was a cradle robber because his girlfriend was so much younger.
Her family disapproved, claiming she was dating a cradle robber.
Tabloids often speculate about celebrities who are labeled as cradle robbers.

Synset Examples

They joked that her new boyfriend was a cradle robber.
She was criticized for dating a cradle robber in her social circle.
His reputation as a cradle robber made people uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cougar - an older woman who seeks out younger partners, typically men
  • Synonym: sugar daddy - an older man who gives money or gifts to a younger partner in return for companionship or relationship

Etymology

From 'cradle' (a baby's bed) and 'robber', figuratively suggesting someone who 'steals' from the cradle.
