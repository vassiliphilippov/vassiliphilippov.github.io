carina fornicis

1. NOUN

/ kəˈriːnə fɔrˈnaɪsɪs / (AmE), / kəˈriːnə fɔːˈnaɪsɪs / (BrE)
A ridge found on the lower surface of the fornix in the brain.
ridge on lower surface of brain fornix
medical

Examples

The carina fornicis is visible during detailed anatomical dissections of the brain.
Damage to the carina fornicis can be associated with certain neurological disorders.
Researchers identified changes in the carina fornicis in patients with memory impairments.

Etymology

From Latin 'carina' (keel, ridge) and 'fornix' (arch, vault).
