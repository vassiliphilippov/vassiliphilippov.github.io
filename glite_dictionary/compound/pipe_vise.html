pipe vise

1. NOUN

/ paɪp vaɪs / (AmE), / paɪp vaɪs / (BrE)
a clamp designed to hold a pipe firmly in place while it is being cut or threaded
clamp for holding pipe during work

Examples

The plumber secured the pipe in a pipe vise before cutting it to length.
A pipe vise is essential when threading new plumbing lines.
He clamped the metal tubing into the pipe vise to keep it steady.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vise - a clamping device for holding work in place
