zip gun

1. NOUN

/ zɪp ɡʌn / (AmE), / zɪp ɡʌn / (BrE)
a crudely made homemade pistol, typically assembled from spare or improvised parts
crude homemade pistol

Examples

The police confiscated a zip gun that had been hidden under the suspect's jacket.
In desperate times, some gang members would resort to making a zip gun out of spare parts.
Possession of a zip gun is illegal in most jurisdictions due to safety concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firearm - a portable gun; weapon that fires projectiles
  • Synonym: pistol - a small firearm designed to be held and operated with one hand
