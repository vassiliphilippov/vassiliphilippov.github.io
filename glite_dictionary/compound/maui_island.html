Maui Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈmaʊ.i ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊ.i ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
the second largest of the Hawaiian Islands, known for its scenic beauty and popular tourist destinations
the second largest Hawaiian island

Examples

We spent our honeymoon on Maui Island, enjoying the beaches and volcanic landscapes.
The Road to Hana is one of the most famous drives on Maui Island.
Every year, thousands of tourists visit Maui Island for its natural beauty and warm climate.

Etymology

Named after the legendary Polynesian navigator Māui, a figure in Hawaiian mythology.
