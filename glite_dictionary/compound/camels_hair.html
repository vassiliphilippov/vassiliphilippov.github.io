camel's hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæməlz hɛr / (AmE), / ˈkæməlz heə / (BrE)
a soft, tan fabric made from the hair of a camel, often used in coats and other garments
fabric made from camel hair

Examples

The designer coat was made of luxurious camel's hair.
He bought a soft camel's hair sweater for the winter.
Camel's hair is prized for its warmth and lightweight feel.
