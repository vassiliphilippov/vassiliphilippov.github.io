grinding wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraɪndɪŋ wiːl / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
A wheel made of abrasive materials, used for cutting, grinding, or finishing metal or other hard materials.
abrasive wheel for grinding or cutting

Examples

The machinist attached a new grinding wheel to the bench grinder before starting his work.
Always wear goggles when using a grinding wheel to shape metal objects.
The grinding wheel made quick work of smoothing the rough edges on the steel plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abrasive wheel - a wheel made of abrasive particles for cutting or grinding
