shot putter

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑt ˈpʊtər / (AmE), / ʃɒt ˈpʊtə / (BrE)
an athlete who competes in the shot put event
athlete in the shot put event

Examples

The Olympic gold medalist is a world-class shot putter with a powerful technique.
As a shot putter, she trains daily to improve her strength and accuracy.
In high school, he was both a shot putter and a discus thrower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who participates in sports or physical exercise
  • Synonym: shot put - an athletics (track and field) event in which a heavy spherical object is thrown as far as possible

Etymology

From 'shot put' (the event) + 'er' (agent noun suffix).
