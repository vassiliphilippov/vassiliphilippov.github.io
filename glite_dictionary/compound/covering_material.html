covering material

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ məˈtɪr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ məˈtɪə.ri.əl / (BrE)
a material used by builders to cover or protect surfaces, such as floors, walls, or roofs
building material for covering surfaces

Examples

The kitchen floor was finished with a durable covering material that resists stains and moisture.
Contractors must select the appropriate covering material for exterior walls to ensure weather resistance.
Before painting, a special covering material was applied to protect the wood from damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coating - a layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection or decoration
  • Antonym: substrate - underlying layer or surface to which a covering is applied
