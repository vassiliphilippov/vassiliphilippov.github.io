Namib Desert

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɑːmɪb ˈdɛzɚt / (AmE), / ˈnæmɪb ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a large desert in Namibia, running along the southwestern coast between the high plateau and the Atlantic Ocean
a coastal desert in Namibia

Examples

The Namib Desert is known for its towering sand dunes and unique flora and fauna.
Many tourists visit the Namib Desert to see the breathtaking scenery and stark landscapes.
The cool, foggy mornings in the Namib Desert create an unusual climate for such an arid region.

Etymology

From Nama (Khoekhoe) 'namib', meaning 'vast place'.
