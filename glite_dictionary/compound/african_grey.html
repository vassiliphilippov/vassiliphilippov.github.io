African grey

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a medium-sized, grey parrot native to equatorial Africa, known for its intelligence and ability to mimic human speech
a grey parrot from Africa, popular as a pet

Examples

The African grey is renowned for its remarkable talking ability.
She adopted an African grey that quickly learned dozens of words.
Veterinarians recommend a varied diet for African grey parrots to maintain their health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parrot - a bird, often brightly colored, known for its ability to mimic sounds
