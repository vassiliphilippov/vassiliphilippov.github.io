pancake day

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænˌkeɪk deɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæn.keɪk deɪ / (BrE)
The day before Lent, traditionally marked by eating pancakes, also known as Shrove Tuesday.
the day before Lent, marked by eating pancakes
british

Examples

Many families celebrate pancake day by making stacks of pancakes together.
At school, the children enjoyed races on pancake day.
British people often use pancake day as an excuse to try new pancake toppings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrove tuesday - the last day before Lent, traditionally celebrated with pancakes
