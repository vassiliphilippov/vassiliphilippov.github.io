bitchy-pants

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪtʃi pænts / (AmE), / ˈbɪtʃi pænts / (BrE)
(slang) A person who acts in a petty, negative, overly dramatic, or whiny manner.
someone petty or overly dramatic (slang)
slang informal

Examples

Don't be such a bitchy-pants about losing the game.
Whenever she doesn't get her way, she turns into a real bitchy-pants.
Calling your boss a bitchy-pants is probably not the best career move.

Synset Examples

Stop being a bitchy-pants and just enjoy the party!
He’s such a bitchy-pants when he doesn’t get his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drama queen - a person given to exaggerated emotional reactions
  • Synonym: whiner - a person who complains a lot or in an annoying way
  • Antonym: easygoing - relaxed and not easily upset or annoyed

Etymology

Formed from 'bitchy' (petty, mean-spirited) and the playful suffix '-pants', as in 'smarty-pants'.
