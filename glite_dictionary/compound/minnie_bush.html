minnie bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪni bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪni bʊʃ / (BrE)
a low shrub native to the eastern United States, characterized by downy twigs
low shrub with downy twigs, eastern US

Examples

We found a cluster of minnie bush growing near the forest edge.
The minnie bush is easy to recognize because of its soft, hairy twigs.
Birds often build their nests in minnie bush thickets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree with multiple stems
