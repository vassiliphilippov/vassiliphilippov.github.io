beer ticket

1. NOUN

/ bɪr ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / bɪə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a voucher or token exchanged for a beer or alcoholic drink, typically at events or festivals
voucher for a beer or drink

Examples

At the music festival, each guest received a beer ticket with their entry wristband.
Make sure you don't lose your beer ticket or you won't be able to get a free drink.
She traded her extra beer ticket for a slice of pizza.

Synset Examples

He handed me a beer ticket at the festival.
I lost my beer ticket and couldn't get my drink.
They gave us a beer ticket for each entry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drink ticket - a voucher exchanged for an alcoholic drink
