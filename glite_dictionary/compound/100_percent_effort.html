100 percent effort

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd pərˈsɛnt ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd pəˈsɛnt ˈɛfət / (BrE)
complete commitment or hard work, leaving nothing in reserve
total commitment or hard work

Examples

If you want to succeed, you must give 100 percent effort every single day.
