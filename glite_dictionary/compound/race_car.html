race car

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪs kɑr / (AmE), / ˈreɪs kɑː / (BrE)
a car designed and built for competing in motor races, typically featuring high speed and special engineering
a car built for motor racing

Examples

The race car sped past the finish line in a blur of bright colors.
He dreamed of driving a professional race car on a famous circuit.
The engineers spent months perfecting the design of their new race car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports car - a car designed for high speed and maneuverability, often sporty but not necessarily for racing
