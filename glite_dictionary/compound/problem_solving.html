problem solving

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːbləm ˌsɑːlvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒbləm ˌsɒlvɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of finding solutions to difficult or complex issues
the process of finding solutions to problems

Examples

Effective problem solving requires both analytical and creative thinking.
The workshop helped students improve their problem solving skills.
Teamwork can often lead to more efficient problem solving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision making - the process of making choices, especially important ones
