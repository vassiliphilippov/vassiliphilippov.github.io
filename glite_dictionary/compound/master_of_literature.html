Master of Literature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.tɚ əv ˈlɪt.əˌrɛ.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stər əv ˈlɪt.ər.ə.tʃə / (BrE)
A postgraduate academic degree awarded in the field of literature.
advanced degree in literature

Examples

She earned her Master of Literature after two years of postgraduate study.
Applicants for the Master of Literature program must have an undergraduate degree in a related field.
With a Master of Literature, he qualified for advanced academic positions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Master of Arts - a postgraduate degree in arts or humanities
  • Synonym: Master of Science - a postgraduate degree in science disciplines
