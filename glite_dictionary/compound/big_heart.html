big heart

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ hɑrt / (AmE), / bɪɡ hɑːt / (BrE)
a generous and kind nature; compassion and willingness to help others
generous, kind nature

Examples

Despite her busy schedule, she always finds time for others—she truly has a big heart.
The community rallied around him because of his big heart and generosity.
You need a big heart to forgive so easily and help those in need.

Synset Examples

She is a little woman with a big heart.
He has a big heart and helps everyone in need.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kind-hearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Synonym: generous - willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected
  • Antonym: cold-hearted - lacking sympathy or compassion
