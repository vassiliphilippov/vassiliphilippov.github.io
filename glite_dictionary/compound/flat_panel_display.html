flat panel display

1. NOUN

/ flæt ˈpænəl dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / flæt ˈpænəl dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
a video screen that is thin and flat, often used in computers and televisions
thin, flat video display screen

Examples

Most modern laptops are equipped with a flat panel display for a lighter, sleeker design.
She replaced her bulky old monitor with a new flat panel display.
The office upgraded all their computers to models with flat panel displays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monitor - an electronic visual display for computers
  • Antonym: cathode ray tube - an older, bulkier technology for video displays
