channel capacity

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃænəl kəˈpæsəti / (AmE), / ˈtʃænəl kəˈpæsəti / (BrE)
the highest amount of data that can be transmitted over a communication channel without error, typically measured in bits per second.
maximum data rate of a channel

Examples

The channel capacity of this fiber-optic link allows the transmission of huge volumes of data.
Improvements in coding techniques can help approach the theoretical channel capacity.
Engineers are working to maximize channel capacity for wireless networks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bandwidth - the range of frequencies or data rate a channel can handle
