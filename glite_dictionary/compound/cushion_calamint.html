cushion calamint

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊʃən ˈkæləˌmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃən ˈkæləmɪnt / (BrE)
an aromatic herb with clusters of small pink or whitish flowers, found widely in the United States, Europe, and Asia
aromatic herb with pink or whitish flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the fragrance of blooming cushion calamint in summer.
She planted cushion calamint alongside the lavender to attract pollinators.
Botanists noted the resilience of cushion calamint in rocky soils.
