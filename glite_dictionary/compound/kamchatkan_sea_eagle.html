Kamchatkan sea eagle

1. NOUN

/ kæmˈtʃætkən si ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / kæmˈtʃætkən siː ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large sea eagle native to the coasts of the northwestern Pacific, especially around the Kamchatka Peninsula
large sea eagle of the northwestern Pacific

Examples

The Kamchatkan sea eagle is renowned for its massive wingspan and striking appearance.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered Kamchatkan sea eagle.
We spotted a Kamchatkan sea eagle perched on a cliff overlooking the Pacific Ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Steller's sea eagle - a large sea eagle of northeast Asia, especially Kamchatka

Etymology

From Kamchatka, the peninsula in eastern Russia, and 'sea eagle'.
