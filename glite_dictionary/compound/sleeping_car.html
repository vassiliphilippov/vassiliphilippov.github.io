sleeping car

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car equipped with beds or berths for sleeping during overnight journeys
railway car with beds for sleeping

Examples

The overnight train included a sleeping car for passengers traveling long distances.
We reserved a berth in the sleeping car to ensure a comfortable night's rest on our journey.
The sleeping car was quiet and dimly lit, making it easy to fall asleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabin car - a railway car with private accommodations
  • Antonym: dining car - a railway passenger car where meals are served
