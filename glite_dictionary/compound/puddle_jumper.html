puddle jumper

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌdəl ˌdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈpʌdəl ˌdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
A small, lightweight boat designed for use in shallow water, or a playful person who is skilled at jumping over puddles.
small boat for shallow water or playful person

Examples

The children used a puddle jumper to cross the shallow creek during the hike.
Everyone admired her as a puddle jumper when she gracefully skipped over every puddle on the playground.
We rented a puddle jumper for an afternoon exploring the quiet backwaters.

Synset Examples

We called him a puddle jumper for his playful spirit.
