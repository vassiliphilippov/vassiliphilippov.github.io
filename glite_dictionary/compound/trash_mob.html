trash mob

1. NOUN

/ træʃ mɑb / (AmE), / træʃ mɒb / (BrE)
A group of people who assemble, often suddenly and informally, to clean up litter in a public place.
group organized for litter cleanup

Examples

A trash mob descended on the city park, picking up every piece of litter they could find.
The local environmental group organized a trash mob to clean the riverbank.
Social media was used to coordinate a spontaneous trash mob in the downtown area.

Synset Examples

They formed a trash mob to tackle litter in the neighborhood.
The community organized a trash mob to clean up the beach.
The park was cleared after the trash mob left.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flash mob - a large group that assembles suddenly to perform an unusual act
