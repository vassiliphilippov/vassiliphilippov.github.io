drug traffic

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈtræfɪk / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈtræfɪk / (BrE)
the illegal trade or exchange of drugs, especially narcotic drugs, for profit
illegal trade of narcotic drugs

Examples

The police made several arrests connected to the drug traffic in the city.
Efforts to stop drug traffic require cooperation between different countries.
Many laws target individuals involved in the drug traffic rather than users.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug trade - the business of buying and selling illegal drugs
  • Synonym: drug trafficking - the act of dealing or trading in illegal drugs
  • Antonym: drug enforcement - action or policy of preventing illegal drug trade
