canal boat

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnæl ˌboʊt / (AmE), / kəˈnæl ˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a long, narrow boat designed for carrying freight or passengers on canals
long, narrow boat for canals

Examples

They hired a canal boat to explore the old waterways in England.
Freight was once commonly transported by canal boats before the rise of the railway.
A canal boat slowly passed under the stone bridge, its engine barely making a sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narrowboat - a type of very narrow canal boat used on British canals
