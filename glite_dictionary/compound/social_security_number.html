Social Security number

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊ.ʃəl səˈkjʊr.ɪ.ti ˈnʌm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.ʃəl sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti ˈnʌm.bə / (BrE)
an official number assigned to an individual in the United States, used for Social Security and identification purposes
U.S. personal identification number
american

Examples

You should never share your Social Security number with strangers to prevent identity theft.
The job application form requires your Social Security number for background checks.
She discovered someone had illegally used her Social Security number to open several accounts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: national insurance number - official number for social security in the UK
