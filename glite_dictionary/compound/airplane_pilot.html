airplane pilot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpleɪn ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
a person who is licensed to operate an aircraft during flight
person licensed to fly an aircraft

Examples

The airplane pilot checked all systems before taking off.
During the storm, the experienced airplane pilot managed to land safely.
She dreams of becoming an airplane pilot and traveling the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the controls of an aircraft
