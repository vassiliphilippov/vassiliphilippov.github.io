lavender-tinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈlævəndɚ tɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈlævəndə tɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having a slight tint or shade of the color lavender.
tinged with lavender color

Examples

As the sun set, the sky became lavender-tinged with streaks of purple and pink.
She wore a lavender-tinged dress that complemented her calm demeanor.
The artist used a lavender-tinged wash to give the painting a dreamy atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavender - a pale purple color
  • Synonym: purple-tinged - tinged with the color purple
