commencement exercise

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛnsmənt ˌɛksɚˌsaɪz / (AmE), / kəˈmɛns.mənt ˌɛk.sə.saɪz / (BrE)
a formal academic ceremony at which degrees or diplomas are awarded to graduating students
graduation ceremony for awarding diplomas
american

Examples

Family members traveled from out of state to attend her commencement exercise last spring.
The university's commencement exercise will be held outdoors, weather permitting.
Many students feel a great sense of accomplishment as they participate in their commencement exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graduation ceremony - the ceremony at which academic degrees or diplomas are awarded

Etymology

From 'commencement' (Middle English from Old French, 'beginning'; in universities, the ceremony marking the beginning of a graduate's next phase) plus 'exercise' (from Latin 'exercitium').
