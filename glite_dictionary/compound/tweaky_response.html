tweaky response

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwiːki rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈtwiːki rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
an odd, eccentric, or unusual answer, especially one that seems slightly off or peculiar
odd or unusual answer
informal

Examples

Her tweaky response made everyone in the room pause and wonder what she meant.
The interviewee gave a tweaky response that left the panel slightly confused.
Whenever asked about his hobbies, he always offers a tweaky response instead of a straightforward answer.

Synset Examples

That was a tweaky response to the question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric reply - an answer that is unusual or unconventional
  • Antonym: normal response - an answer that is typical or expected

Etymology

Formed from "tweaky" (informal, meaning odd or eccentric, originally from "tweak") + "response".
