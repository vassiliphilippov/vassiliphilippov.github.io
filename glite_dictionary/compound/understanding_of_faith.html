understanding of faith

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndɚˈstændɪŋ əv feɪθ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ əv feɪθ / (BrE)
knowledge or interpretation regarding religious concepts, especially beliefs and doctrines
comprehension of religious beliefs

Examples

Her understanding of faith grew deeper after years of study and reflection.
The seminar aimed to broaden participants' understanding of faith from different religious perspectives.
His understanding of faith was shaped largely by his upbringing and personal experiences.

Synset Examples

He studied Christianity to deepen his understanding of faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faith - strong belief in religious doctrines
  • Synonym: religious belief - belief in religious doctrines or teachings
