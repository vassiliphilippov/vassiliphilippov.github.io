cliff brake

1. NOUN

/ klɪf breɪk / (AmE), / klɪf breɪk / (BrE)
any of several small ferns that grow on rocks or cliffs, typically in tropical and warm temperate regions
type of small rock-growing fern

Examples

We found a rare cliff brake growing between the stones along the hiking trail.
Botanists are studying several species of cliff brake in the region to understand their adaptation to rocky environments.
The delicate fronds of the cliff brake make it a unique addition to shade gardens.
