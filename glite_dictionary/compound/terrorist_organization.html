terrorist organization

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrərɪst ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
An organized group that uses violence, intimidation, or terror, especially against civilians, to achieve political aims.
group using terror for political ends

Examples

The government placed the terrorist organization on its list of banned groups.
Several countries cooperated to dismantle the terrorist organization operating in the region.
Funding for the terrorist organization was traced to overseas accounts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrorist group - an alternative term for a group using terror for political aims
