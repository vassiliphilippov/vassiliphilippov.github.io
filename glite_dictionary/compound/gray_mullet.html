gray mullet

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a species of freshwater or coastal fish with a spindle-shaped body, found worldwide and often used as food.
a widely distributed food fish

Examples

Fishermen caught a large gray mullet near the shore this morning.
Grilled gray mullet is a popular dish in many Mediterranean countries.
The gray mullet is valued for both its meat and roe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mullet - a family of fish of which the gray mullet is a type

2. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
the lean flesh of a marine or freshwater mullet, highly valued as food
valued fish used as food

Examples

The chef prepared grilled gray mullet with lemon and herbs for the evening special.
Fishermen caught plenty of gray mullet along the coast this morning.
I ordered a dish of smoked gray mullet at the seafood restaurant.

Related Terms
