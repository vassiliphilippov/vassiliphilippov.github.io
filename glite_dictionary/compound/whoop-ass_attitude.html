whoop-ass attitude

1. NOUN

/ wupˌæs ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / wʊpˌæs ˈatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An aggressively assertive or confrontational demeanor, especially one that suggests readiness to take decisive or forceful action.
aggressive or assertive demeanor
informal slang american

Examples

Her whoop-ass attitude inspired her teammates to push harder than ever before.
He walked into the meeting with a whoop-ass attitude that intimidated his competitors.
Their whoop-ass attitude made a real difference during the high-stakes negotiation.

Synset Examples

He brought his whoop-ass attitude to the competition.
She tackled the project with a whoop-ass mentality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressive - behaving in an angry or forceful way
  • Synonym: assertive - having or showing a confident and forceful personality

Etymology

From 'whoop-ass', a slang term meaning to show force or dominance, originally from American vernacular.
