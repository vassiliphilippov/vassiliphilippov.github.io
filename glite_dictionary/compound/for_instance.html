for instance

1. ADV

/ fər ˈɪnstəns / (AmE), / fə ˈɪnstəns / (BrE)
used to introduce a specific example that illustrates a point just made
used to introduce an example

Examples

Many countries, for instance, Canada and Japan, have adapted to changing technologies.
There are many ways to solve the equation; for instance, you could simplify both sides first.
Some fruits, for instance bananas and apples, are high in potassium.

Synset Examples

take ribbon snakes, for example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for example - used to introduce something chosen as a typical case
