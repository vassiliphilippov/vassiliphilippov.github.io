homo politicus

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmoʊ pəˈlɪtɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ pɒˈlɪtɪkəs / (BrE)
A person who is actively and deeply involved in politics, often advocating for social or public issues.
person deeply involved in politics

Examples

Despite his career in engineering, Mark is known as a homo politicus who never shies away from debate.
Many protesters at the rally identified themselves as homo politicus, fighting for social justice.
To be a homo politicus in today's world requires resilience and commitment.

Synset Examples

As a homo politicus, he fights for equality.
The homo politicus advocates for LGBTQ+ rights.
Being a homo politicus means navigating complex social issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political animal - a person who is naturally interested in politics

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'political man'.
