Republic of Croatia

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv kroʊˈeɪʃə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv krəʊˈeɪʃə / (BrE)
the official name of Croatia, a country in the western Balkans of south-central Europe, on the eastern Adriatic coast, independent since 1991
official name for Croatia (country)

Examples

The Republic of Croatia declared its independence from Yugoslavia in 1991.
Tourism is a major industry in the Republic of Croatia due to its beautiful Adriatic coastline.
The president of the Republic of Croatia addressed the nation in a televised speech.
