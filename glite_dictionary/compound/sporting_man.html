sporting man

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtɪŋ mæn / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ man / (BrE)
a man who enjoys outdoor activities, especially sports and recreation
man enthusiastic about outdoor activities

Examples

As a true sporting man, he spent his weekends hiking, fishing, and playing tennis.
The club attracted every sporting man in town during the hunting season.
He prided himself on being a sporting man and never shied away from a physical challenge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sportsman - a person, especially a man, who takes part in sports

2. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtɪŋ mæn / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ man / (BrE)
a man who lives a lively, pleasure-seeking life, especially one who frequently gambles on sporting events
a man who lives for pleasure and gambling, esp. on sports

Examples

He developed the reputation of a sporting man who frequented horse races and betting parlors.
The novel depicts the extravagant lifestyle of a sporting man in 19th-century London.
Despite being a sporting man, he maintained a sense of fairness in all his wagers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who bets money on games or events
