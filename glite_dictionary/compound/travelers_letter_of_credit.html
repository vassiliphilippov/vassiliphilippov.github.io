traveler's letter of credit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævələrz ˈlɛtər əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrævələz ˈlɛtə əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
a bank-issued letter of credit provided to a traveler as a guarantee of payment, allowing them to obtain funds or make payments while abroad.
bank letter of credit for travelers

Examples

Before international ATMs were common, many tourists relied on a traveler's letter of credit to access funds abroad.
The bank issued him a traveler's letter of credit before his business trip to Asia.
A traveler's letter of credit provides security for both the traveler and the institutions they deal with overseas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter of credit - an official bank document guaranteeing payment to a seller
