home banking

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈbæŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / həʊm ˈbæŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of conducting banking transactions from home using electronic communication such as a telephone or computer
banking from home via electronic communication

Examples

With home banking, you can check your account balance anytime from your living room.
Many people find home banking more convenient than visiting a branch in person.
The rise of home banking has changed the way customers interact with their banks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: online banking - the use of the internet to perform banking transactions
