star sapphire

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈsæfˌaɪər / (AmE), / stɑː ˈsæfaɪə / (BrE)
A sapphire gemstone that, when cut, displays a starlike pattern on its surface due to its crystalline structure.
sapphire gemstone with a starlike pattern

Examples

She admired the star sapphire in her grandmother's antique ring.
The unique luster of a star sapphire makes it highly prized among collectors.
A jeweler explained how the light creates a star effect in the star sapphire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sapphire - a precious blue gemstone
