soft-cover book

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑft ˌkʌvɚ ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌkʌvə ˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book bound with flexible paper covers rather than rigid ones
a book with paper covers

Examples

She prefers to read soft-cover books because they are lighter to carry.
The latest novel is only available as a soft-cover book at the moment.
Many students buy soft-cover books to save money compared to hardcovers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paperback - a book bound in thick paper or paperboard
  • Antonym: hardcover - a book bound with rigid protective covers
