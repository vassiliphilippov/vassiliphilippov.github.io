throne sniffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈθroʊn ˌsnɪfɚ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊn ˌsnɪfə / (BrE)
A person who excessively flatters or fawns over those in positions of authority or power in order to gain favor.
sycophant to powerful people

Examples

He gained a reputation as a throne sniffer after always agreeing with the boss.
People disliked the throne sniffer for always flattering those in charge.
Rather than speaking his mind, he chose the path of a throne sniffer at court.

Synset Examples

He was called a throne sniffer for his flattery.
Being a throne sniffer won't win you real friends.
The throne sniffer always sought the king's favor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important; a flatterer
  • Synonym: brown-noser - someone who flatters a superior in order to gain favor
