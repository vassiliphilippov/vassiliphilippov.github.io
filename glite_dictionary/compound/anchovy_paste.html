anchovy paste

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.tʃoʊ.vi peɪst / (AmE), / ˈan.tʃə.vi peɪst / (BrE)
A savory paste made primarily from anchovies, used to add flavor to sauces, spreads, and various dishes.
savory paste made from anchovies

Examples

The chef added a teaspoon of anchovy paste to the salad dressing for extra depth of flavor.
Many Italian recipes call for anchovy paste as a secret umami ingredient.
She spread a thin layer of anchovy paste on the toast before serving it with tomatoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchovy - A small saltwater fish used for food
