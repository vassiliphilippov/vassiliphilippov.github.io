Sinhala language

1. NOUN

/ sɪnˈhɑːlə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / sɪnˈhɑːlə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Sri Lanka, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European language family
official language of Sri Lanka

Examples

Many Sri Lankans speak Sinhala language as their mother tongue.
She is studying the Sinhala language at university.
The official documents are translated into both Tamil and Sinhala language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sinhalese - a native or inhabitant of Sri Lanka, or the language they speak

Etymology

From Sinhala, the native term for the language, from Sanskrit Siṃhala (name for Sri Lanka).
