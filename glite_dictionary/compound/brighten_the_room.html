to brighten the room

1. VERB

/ ˈbraɪtn̩ ðə ruːm / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtn ðə ruːm / (BrE)
to make the atmosphere in a room more cheerful and lively, especially by one's presence or actions
make a room's atmosphere cheerful

Examples

Her infectious smile always brightens the room whenever she enters.
His cheerful attitude brightened the room during the tense meeting.
You could see her laughter brightening the room at the family gathering.

Synset Examples

Annie always brightens the room with her laughter.
Swedey's laughter is contagious and brightens the room.
She has a nice smile that brightens the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to light up the room - to make the atmosphere more happy or lively in a room
