dog turd

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ tɜrd / (AmE), / dɒɡ tɜːd / (BrE)
a piece of solid waste excreted by a dog
solid dog feces

Examples

Be careful not to step in a dog turd while walking in the park.
She was annoyed to find a dog turd on her front lawn.
The tired jogger narrowly missed a dog turd on the sidewalk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turd - a piece of solid human or animal excrement
