on the offensive

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ði əˈfɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ɒn ði əˈfɛnsɪv / (BrE)
actively attacking or taking action against an opponent rather than defending
actively attacking; not defensive

Examples

After weeks of retreat, the army finally went on the offensive against its enemies.
During the debate, she decided to go on the offensive instead of defending her views.
The company is now on the offensive, launching several new products this year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on the defensive - primarily concerned with defending against attacks
