to walk a dog

1. VERB

/ wɔk ə dɔɡ / (AmE), / wɔːk ə dɒɡ / (BrE)
to take a dog outdoors for exercise, usually by walking on a leash
take a dog outside for exercise

Examples

Every morning, she sets aside time to walk a dog in the nearby park.
Some teenagers earn money by walking a dog for neighbors while they're at work.
It's important to walk a dog regularly to keep it healthy and happy.

Synset Examples

She walked her dog in the long paddock near the farm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walk - move at a regular and fairly slow pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
