caravan inn

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærəˌvæn ɪn / (AmE), / ˈkærəvan ɪn / (BrE)
an inn, especially in eastern countries, with a large courtyard, providing accommodation for caravans and travelers
inn for caravans with a courtyard

Examples

The ancient traders rested at a caravan inn on their long journey through the desert.
A typical caravan inn offered shelter for both people and animals.
Many old caravan inns still stand along the Silk Road as historical monuments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caravanserai - an inn with a courtyard for travelers in Asia or North Africa
