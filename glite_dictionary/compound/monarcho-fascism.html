monarcho-fascism

1. NOUN

/ məˌnɑːrkoʊˈfæʃɪzəm / (AmE), / məˌnɑːkəʊˈfæʃɪzəm / (BrE)
A political ideology that combines elements of monarchy and fascism.
blend of monarchy and fascism

Examples

The historian wrote extensively on the consequences of monarcho-fascism in the early 20th century.
Concerns about monarcho-fascism resurfaced during the recent surge in right-wing politics.
Some regimes have displayed characteristics that closely align with monarcho-fascism.

Synset Examples

He critiqued the rise of monarcho-fascism in recent politics.
The documentary explored the dangers of monarcho-fascism.
The group's ideology leaned towards monarcho-fascism, as discussed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascism - an authoritarian and nationalistic right-wing system of government and social organization
  • Synonym: monarchy - a form of government with a monarch at the head

Etymology

Formed from 'monarch-' (relating to monarchy) and 'fascism'.
