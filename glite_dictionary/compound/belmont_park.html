Belmont Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛlˌmɑnt pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbɛlmɒnt pɑːk / (BrE)
A racetrack for thoroughbred horse racing located in Elmont, on Long Island, New York, and the venue for the Belmont Stakes.
racetrack in New York; site of the Belmont Stakes

Examples

The final leg of the Triple Crown is held at Belmont Park every June.
Many legendary racehorses have competed at Belmont Park over the years.
Visitors from around the world travel to Belmont Park to witness the famous Belmont Stakes.
