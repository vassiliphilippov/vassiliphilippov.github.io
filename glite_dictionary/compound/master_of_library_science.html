Master of Library Science

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstɚ əv ˈlaɪbrɛri ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə əv ˈlaɪbrəri ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
A postgraduate academic degree in library science, typically required for professional librarianship.
postgraduate degree in library science

Examples

She earned her Master of Library Science before becoming the head librarian.
Many universities now offer an online Master of Library Science program.
A Master of Library Science is often required for library management positions.
