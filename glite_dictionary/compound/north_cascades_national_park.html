North Cascades National Park

1. PROPN

/ nɔrθ kæˈskeɪdz ˌnæʃəˈnæl pɑrk / (AmE), / nɔːθ kæsˈkeɪdz ˌnæʃəˈnæl pɑːk / (BrE)
A national park in northern Washington State, USA, known for its alpine wilderness and historic sites from the gold rush and logging eras.
national park in Washington State, USA

We spent a week hiking in North Cascades National Park and were amazed by the rugged scenery.
