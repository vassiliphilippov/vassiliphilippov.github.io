grey hen

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪ hɛn / (AmE), / ɡreɪ hɛn / (BrE)
the female of the black grouse, a large game bird found in Europe and Asia, noted for its greyish-brown plumage
female black grouse

Examples

The grey hen is typically less brightly colored than the male black grouse.
We spotted a grey hen with her chicks in the heathland.
Unlike the blackcock, the grey hen blends in with the surrounding vegetation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blackcock - the male black grouse
  • Synonym: black grouse - a large game bird of Europe and Asia
