red helleborine

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˌhɛləˈbɔraɪn / (AmE), / rɛd ˌhɛlɪˈbɔːraɪn / (BrE)
an orchid found in the Mediterranean and Asia, with a loose spike of bright rose-pink flowers
Mediterranean/Asian orchid with pink flowers

Examples

The red helleborine is rare and protected in several European countries.
Botanists were delighted to discover a population of red helleborine near the forest edge.
The delicate pink blooms of the red helleborine add color to Mediterranean woodlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helleborine - any of various wild orchids of the genus Epipactis or Cephalanthera

Etymology

From 'red' for its flower color, and 'helleborine,' a term for various wild orchids, from Latin 'helleborine'.
