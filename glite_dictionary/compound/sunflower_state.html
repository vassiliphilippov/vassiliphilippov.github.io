Sunflower State

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ɚ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌflaʊ.ə steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Kansas, known for its abundance of sunflowers
nickname for Kansas, USA

Examples

Every summer, tourists visit the Sunflower State to admire its fields of wild sunflowers.
She grew up in the Sunflower State and still calls Kansas home.
The Sunflower State is known for its rich history and prairies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kansas - a state in the Midwestern United States with capital Topeka

Etymology

Named after the wild sunflowers common in Kansas.
