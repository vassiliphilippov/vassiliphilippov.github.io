total fuckbucket

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈfʌkˌbʌk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈfʌkˌbʌk.ɪt / (BrE)
A crude and highly insulting slang term for a person considered worthless, contemptible, or disgusting.
crude insult for a worthless or contemptible person
slang offensive vulgar

Everyone at the party thought he was being a total fuckbucket after his rude comments.
Even her closest friends agreed that acting like a total fuckbucket wasn't helping her reputation.
The politician was called a total fuckbucket by his harshest critics.
