animal fat

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl ˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl ˌfæt / (BrE)
fat obtained from the tissues of animals, used in cooking and food production or for industrial purposes
fat derived from animals

Examples

Excessive consumption of animal fat can increase the risk of heart disease.
Some traditional dishes are cooked with animal fat instead of vegetable oil.
The soap was made using animal fat as a primary ingredient.

Synset Examples

animal fat is high in saturated fatty acids

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vegetable fat - fat obtained from plants
