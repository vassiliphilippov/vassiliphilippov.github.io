sticky-outy hairdo

1. NOUN

/ ˌstɪki ˈaʊti ˈhɛrdu / (AmE), / ˌstɪki ˈaʊti ˈheəduː / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the hair sticks out prominently or noticeably
a hairstyle where hair sticks out
informal

Examples

Her sticky-outy hairdo made her easy to spot in the crowd.
He laughed when his sister showed up with a wildly sticky-outy hairdo.
The wind was not responsible for my sticky-outy hairdo—that style was intentional.

Synset Examples

The sticky-outy hairdo made her stand out at the party.
