payment rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪmənt reɪt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪmənt reɪt / (BrE)
the amount of money paid for work or service within a fixed period, such as per hour, day, or month.
amount of money paid per unit time

Examples

The company increased the payment rate for overtime work to attract more employees.
She negotiated her payment rate before accepting the consulting assignment.
If the payment rate is too low, workers may seek other opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - fixed regular payment for work
  • Synonym: salary - regular payment, typically monthly, for employment
  • Synonym: hourly rate - rate of payment per hour worked
