fresh perspective

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / frɛʃ pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
a new and original way of considering or approaching something
a new and original way of looking at something

Examples

The new manager brought a fresh perspective to solving old problems in the department.
After years of routine, the committee needed someone with a fresh perspective to inspire change.
Sometimes traveling abroad can give you a fresh perspective on your own country.

Synset Examples

The statesider brought a fresh perspective to the team.
His cleversome ideas always bring fresh perspectives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: new outlook - a different or changed attitude or viewpoint
  • Antonym: stale viewpoint - an old or unoriginal way of seeing things
