woman chaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊmən ˌtʃeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən ˌtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
a man who persistently and aggressively pursues romantic or sexual relationships with women
man persistently seeking women

Examples

Everyone at the party knew Tom was a notorious woman chaser who couldn't pass up a flirt.
The novel revolves around a charming woman chaser with a reputation for broken hearts.
Though he tried to change his ways, his past as a woman chaser still followed him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who habitually seduces or tries to seduce women
