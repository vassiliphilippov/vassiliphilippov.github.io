with conviction

1. ADV

/ wɪð kənˈvɪkʃən / (AmE), / wɪð kənˈvɪkʃən / (BrE)
In a way that shows strong belief or confidence.
with strong belief or confidence

Examples

She argued with conviction during the debate, impressing everyone in the audience.
He delivered his speech with conviction and inspired those who heard him.
The witness testified with conviction, making her statement very compelling.

Synset Examples

He spoke with coonviction about his beliefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confidently - in a way that shows confidence
