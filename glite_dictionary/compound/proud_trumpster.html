proud Trumpster

1. NOUN

/ praʊd ˈtrʌmp.stɚ / (AmE), / praʊd ˈtrʌmp.stə / (BrE)
A person who strongly and openly supports Donald Trump, especially in politics.
an open supporter of Donald Trump
informal american

Examples

He described himself as a proud Trumpster during the interview.
Several proud Trumpsters gathered outside the rally to show their support.
It's not uncommon to see a proud Trumpster wearing campaign merchandise.

Synset Examples

She identified as a proud Trumpster at the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trump supporter - a person who supports Donald Trump
  • Antonym: Never Trumper - a Republican opposed to Donald Trump

Etymology

Formed from 'proud' and 'Trumpster', the latter derived from the surname Trump with the suffix '-ster' denoting an adherent or enthusiast.
