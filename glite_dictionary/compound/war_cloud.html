war cloud

1. NOUN

/ wɔr klaʊd / (AmE), / wɔː klaʊd / (BrE)
a sign or indication suggesting that war is likely or imminent
ominous sign that war threatens

Examples

Diplomatic tensions are rising, and war clouds are gathering over the region.
The speech was seen as a sign of the war clouds on the horizon.
In 1939, war clouds loomed ominously over Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threat - danger or sign of possible harm
  • Antonym: peace - a state of harmony free from war
