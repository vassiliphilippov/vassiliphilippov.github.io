second stomach

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
the second of the four compartments in the stomach of a ruminant, located between the rumen and the omasum, also known as the reticulum.
the second compartment of a ruminant's stomach

Examples

The cow's second stomach plays a vital role in digesting plant fibers.
Food passes from the rumen into the second stomach for further breakdown.
Veterinary students studied the structure of the second stomach in sheep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reticulum - the anatomical name for the second stomach of a ruminant
