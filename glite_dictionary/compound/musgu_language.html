Musgu language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsɡu ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌsɡu ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, particularly in the Far North Region of Cameroon
Chadic language of Cameroon

Examples

The Musgu language is spoken by communities in northern Cameroon.
She is researching the structure of the Musgu language for her doctoral thesis.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Musgu language among younger generations.
