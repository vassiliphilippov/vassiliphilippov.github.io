city-born

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪti bɔrn / (AmE), / ˈsɪti bɔːn / (BrE)
Having the customs, manners, or characteristics typical of someone raised in a city.
having characteristics of someone from a city

Examples

As a city-born child, she was used to a fast-paced life.
His city-born mannerisms stood out in the quiet village.
The city-born manager had little patience for rural traditions.
