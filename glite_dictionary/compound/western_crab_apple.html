western crab apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstɚn kræb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən kræb ˈæpəl / (BrE)
a species of wild crab apple native to the western United States, noted for its fragrant pink flowers
wild crab apple tree of western US

Examples

The western crab apple grows abundantly along the coastal forests of Oregon and Washington.
In spring, the western crab apple produces clusters of beautiful, fragrant pink blossoms.
Wildlife often feed on the tart fruit of the western crab apple during autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a small, sour wild apple
