Ways and Means Committee

1. NOUN

/ weɪz ənd ˈminz kəˌmɪti / (AmE), / weɪz ənd ˈmiːnz kəˌmɪti / (BrE)
a permanent committee of the United States House of Representatives responsible for making recommendations on all bills related to raising revenue
US House committee on revenue bills
american

Examples

The Ways and Means Committee will hold a hearing to discuss the proposed tax legislation next week.
Many influential financial laws have originated in the Ways and Means Committee over the years.
She was recently appointed to the powerful Ways and Means Committee in Congress.
