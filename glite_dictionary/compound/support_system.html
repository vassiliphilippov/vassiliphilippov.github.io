support system

1. NOUN

/ səˈpɔrt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A group of people or organizations that provide help, encouragement, or practical assistance to someone, especially during difficult times.
network providing help and encouragement

Examples

After her surgery, Maria relied heavily on her support system of family and friends.
A strong support system is essential for coping with stress at work.
Many students benefit from having a support system on campus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: network - an interconnected group or system
  • Synonym: lifeline - a source of critical support or help

2. NOUN

/ səˈpɔrt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a group of people who provide emotional, psychological, or practical help to someone, especially during times of difficulty
network of people providing help

Examples

She leaned on her support system after her surgery, finding strength in her friends and family.
A strong support system can make all the difference during stressful life transitions.
Without his support system, he would have struggled much more with the move to a new city.

Synset Examples

She considers her monthly friend a vital part of her support system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: network - a group or system of interconnected people or things
  • Synonym: safety net - something that provides security against misfortune
