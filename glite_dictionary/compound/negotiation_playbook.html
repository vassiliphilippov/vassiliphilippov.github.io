negotiation playbook

1. NOUN

/ nəˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən ˈpleɪˌbʊk / (AmE), / nɪˌɡəʊʃɪˈeɪʃən ˈpleɪˌbʊk / (BrE)
a set of strategies or guidelines outlining how to approach and conduct negotiations
guide or strategy for negotiations

Examples

The team developed a negotiation playbook to ensure they were prepared for every scenario.
Consultants often recommend having a negotiation playbook before entering major business talks.
Her success was due, in part, to the detailed negotiation playbook she had assembled.

Synset Examples

She followed the negotiation playbook meticulously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term or overall aim
  • Synonym: tactic - a specific action or method used to achieve a short-term goal
