divine guidance

1. NOUN

/ dəˈvaɪn ˈɡaɪ.dəns / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn ˈɡaɪ.dəns / (BrE)
special influence or direction from a deity believed to guide a person's thoughts or actions, often in a religious context
influence or direction from a deity

Examples

Many believers pray for divine guidance when facing challenging decisions in life.
She attributed her discovery to divine guidance rather than luck or coincidence.
The prophets claimed to receive divine guidance in their visions and teachings.

Synset Examples

they believe that the books of Scripture were written under divine guidance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiration - divine influence believed to guide action or thought
