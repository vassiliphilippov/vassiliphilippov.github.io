lion marmoset

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ən ˈmɑr.moʊˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ən ˈmɑː.məˌsɛt / (BrE)
a small South American marmoset with silky fur and a long nonprehensile tail, known for its mane-like facial hair
South American marmoset with a mane-like face

Examples

The lion marmoset has a striking golden mane around its face.
Researchers spotted a group of lion marmosets moving through the rainforest canopy.
Efforts are underway to protect the habitat of the endangered lion marmoset in Brazil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamarin - another type of small South American monkey, closely related to marmosets
