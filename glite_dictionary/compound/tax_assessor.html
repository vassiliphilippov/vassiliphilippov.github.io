tax assessor

1. NOUN

/ tæks əˈsɛsər / (AmE), / tæks əˈsɛsə / (BrE)
an official responsible for determining the value of property for tax purposes
official who assesses property for taxation

Examples

The tax assessor visited our neighborhood to determine property values.
If you disagree with the tax assessor's valuation, you can file an appeal.
Every city employs a tax assessor to ensure property taxes are fairly applied.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assessor - an official who evaluates or estimates the nature, ability, or quality of something
