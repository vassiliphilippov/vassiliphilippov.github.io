Toby Fillpot jug

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.bi ˈfɪl.pɒt dʒʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.bi ˈfɪl.pɒt dʒʌɡ / (BrE)
a drinking jug, typically ceramic, in the form of a stout man wearing a tricorn hat, often used as a decorative mug
jug shaped like a stout man with a tricorn hat

Examples

The antique shop displayed a rare Toby Fillpot jug among its collection of ceramics.
He drank ale from a Toby Fillpot jug modeled after a merrily smiling man.
Collectors often seek out Toby Fillpot jugs because of their unique designs and historical value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Toby jug - ceramic jug in the form of a seated person, usually a jovial man

Etymology

Named after Toby Fillpot, a character in an 18th-century English drinking song; 'Toby jug' became a generic term for such mugs.
