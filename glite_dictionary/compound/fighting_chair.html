fighting chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪtɪŋ tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtɪŋ tʃeə / (BrE)
a sturdy, fixed chair on a fishing boat, equipped for an angler to securely fight large hooked fish, especially in saltwater game fishing.
chair for fighting large hooked fish (fishing)

Examples

The marlin put up an incredible fight as I sat strapped into the fighting chair.
A well-designed fighting chair makes it easier to land giant tuna safely.
He settled into the fighting chair and prepared for the hours-long battle with the fish.
