learning ability

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɜrnɪŋ əˌbɪləti / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnɪŋ əˌbɪləti / (BrE)
the capacity to acquire knowledge, skills, or understanding through study or experience
capacity to learn or acquire skills

Examples

Despite her young age, her learning ability impressed all of her teachers.
The job requires strong learning ability to master complex new technologies quickly.
Differences in learning ability can make standardized education challenging for some students.

Synset Examples

he's got plenty of brains but no common sense

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aptitude - natural ability to do something or to learn
  • Synonym: intelligence - ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills
  • Antonym: inability - lack of ability or power to do something
