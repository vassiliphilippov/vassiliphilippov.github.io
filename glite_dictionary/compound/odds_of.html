odds of

1. NOUN

/ ɑdz əv / (AmE), / ɒdz əv / (BrE)
the probability or likelihood that a particular event will occur
probability of an event

Examples

The odds of being struck by lightning are extremely low.
She calculated the odds of drawing the winning ticket before entering the contest.
Scientists study the odds of various diseases occurring in the population.

Synset Examples

What are the odds of winning the lottery twice?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probability - the likelihood or chance of something happening

Etymology

From the noun 'odds' (probability or likelihood), used in English since the 16th century.
