torch song

1. NOUN

/ tɔrtʃ sɔŋ / (AmE), / tɔːtʃ sɒŋ / (BrE)
a sentimental popular song that expresses disappointment or unrequited love
sentimental song about lost love

Examples

She performed a moving torch song that brought the entire audience to tears.
Many classic jazz singers were famous for their emotional torch songs.
His latest album includes a beautiful torch song about longing and heartbreak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ballad - a slow, sentimental song

Etymology

From the phrase 'to carry a torch (for someone)', meaning to suffer unrequited love.
