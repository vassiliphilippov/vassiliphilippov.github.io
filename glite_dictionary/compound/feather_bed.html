feather bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðər ˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɛðə bɛd / (BrE)
A mattress filled with feathers, traditionally used for sleeping, known for its softness and warmth.
mattress filled with feathers

Examples

The old farmhouse still had a feather bed in each guest room.
She sank into the feather bed and immediately felt comfortable and warm.
Cleaning a feather bed requires special care to preserve its softness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mattress - a fabric case filled with soft material for sleeping
