hot topic

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / hɒt ˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
A subject that generates a lot of discussion or is of current interest, especially because it is controversial or important.
subject generating much interest or debate

Examples

Climate change remains a hot topic in global politics and media coverage.
Remote work has become a hot topic among business leaders since the pandemic began.
Artificial intelligence is a hot topic in the technology industry these days.

Synset Examples

Bidenflation has become a hot topic in political debates.
Canadian healthcare is a hot topic in political debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversial issue - subject causing public disagreement or debate
  • Synonym: debate - discussion on a particular topic involving opposing viewpoints
  • Antonym: nonissue - topic not considered important or interesting
