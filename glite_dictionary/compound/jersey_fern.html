Jersey fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːzi fɝːn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːzi fɜːn / (BrE)
a small, short-lived fern native to Central and South America
a small Central/South American fern

Examples

The Jersey fern thrives in moist, shaded areas of Central America.
Researchers discovered a previously unknown colony of Jersey fern in the rainforest.
Unlike many perennial plants, the Jersey fern completes its life cycle within a single season.
