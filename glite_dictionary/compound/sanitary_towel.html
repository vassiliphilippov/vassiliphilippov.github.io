sanitary towel

1. NOUN

/ ˈsænəˌtɛri ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / ˈsænɪtəri ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
A disposable absorbent pad worn inside underwear to absorb menstrual blood.
disposable pad for menstrual flow

Examples

She always carries a sanitary towel in her bag just in case.
The nurse handed her a sanitary towel from the supply cabinet.
Many brands offer a sanitary towel designed for overnight use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanitary napkin - another term for a disposable absorbent menstrual pad
