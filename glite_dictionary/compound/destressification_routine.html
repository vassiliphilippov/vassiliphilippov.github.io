destressification routine

1. NOUN

/ diˌstrɛsəˌfɪˈkeɪʃən ruˈtin / (AmE), / diˌstrɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
a personal set of activities or practices followed to reduce stress and promote relaxation
set of activities to reduce stress

Examples

Every evening, she follows her destressification routine to unwind after work.
A warm bath is an essential part of my destressification routine.
Many people develop a personalized destressification routine during stressful periods.

Synset Examples

Her destressification routine includes reading and tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxation technique - a method or exercise intended to reduce tension and stress

Etymology

Formed from 'de-' (remove), 'stress,' '-ification' (making something), and 'routine' (regular practice).
