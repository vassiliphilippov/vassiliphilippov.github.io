doorframe

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌfreɪm / (BrE)
the structural frame surrounding and supporting a door within a wall or partition
frame surrounding a door

Examples

The old wooden doorframe was cracked and needed to be replaced.
She painted the doorframe white to match the rest of the room.
Carefully measure the doorframe before ordering a new door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door - a movable barrier used to open and close an entrance
  • Synonym: window frame - the structure that supports a window
