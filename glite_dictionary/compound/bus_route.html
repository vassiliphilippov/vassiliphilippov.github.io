bus route

1. NOUN

/ bʌs ruːt / (AmE), / bʌs ruːt / (BrE)
a regular path or course that a passenger bus follows between specific destinations
a regular path followed by a passenger bus

Examples

The number 15 bus route runs through the city center every fifteen minutes.
Many commuters rely on this bus route to get to work each day.
They're planning to add a new bus route connecting the suburbs and the airport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus line - a system or company operating buses along set routes
  • Synonym: rail route - the path followed by trains between stations
