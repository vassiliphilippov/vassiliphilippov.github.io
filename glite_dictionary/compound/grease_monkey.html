grease monkey

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡris ˌmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈɡriːs ˌmʌŋki / (BrE)
an informal term for a mechanic, especially one who repairs and maintains automobiles
slang for automobile mechanic
slang

Examples

His father worked as a grease monkey at the local garage for over twenty years.
Don't underestimate the skills of a grease monkey; automotive work requires both knowledge and finesse.
She became a certified grease monkey right after high school and quickly earned the respect of her peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanic - someone who repairs and maintains machinery, especially automobiles

2. NOUN

/ ɡris ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ɡriːs ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
an informal term for a mechanic or someone skilled at repairing machinery, especially motor vehicles
informal term for a mechanic
informal

Examples

Jake always dreamed of becoming a grease monkey and opening his own auto repair shop.
Even as a teenager, she was a natural grease monkey in her father's garage.
Many grease monkeys now use advanced computer diagnostics alongside traditional tools.

Synset Examples

The garage is filled with tools for the grease monkey at heart.
Every weekend, the grease monkey works on his motorcycle.
He's a grease monkey who loves fixing old cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanic - a person who repairs and maintains machinery

Etymology

Refers to mechanics frequently getting covered in grease while working on engines; 'monkey' humorously suggests agility.
