needle bush

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːdəl bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈniːdl bʊʃ / (BrE)
A shrub with sharp, rigid, needle-shaped leaves and white flowers, native to eastern Australia.
Australian shrub with needle-shaped leaves

Examples

Many gardens in Australia feature the needle bush for its attractive white flowers and hardy nature.
The leaves of the needle bush are sharp and can deter animals from eating them.
Scientists are studying the needle bush to better understand its adaptation to dry climates.
