Olympic National Park

1. PROPN

/ əˈlɪmpɪk ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpɪk ˌnæʃnəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Washington State, USA, known for its diverse ecosystems including temperate rainforests and mountain ranges
national park in Washington State, USA

Examples

We spent a week hiking in Olympic National Park and marveled at its lush rainforests.
The diverse wildlife of Olympic National Park attracts visitors from around the world.
Photographers are drawn to Olympic National Park for its breathtaking scenery and unique ecosystems.

Etymology

Named after the Olympic Mountains, which in turn are named after Mount Olympus of Greek mythology.
