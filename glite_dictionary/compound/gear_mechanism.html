gear mechanism

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪr ˈmɛk.əˌnɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ɡɪə ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
a system of gears designed to transmit motion and force for a specific mechanical purpose
system of gears transmitting motion

Examples

The gear mechanism inside the clock keeps perfect time.
Engineers designed a new gear mechanism for the car's steering system.
The failure of the gear mechanism caused the machine to stop working.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gear train - a set of gears arranged to transmit motion
  • Synonym: mechanism - a system of parts working together in a machine
