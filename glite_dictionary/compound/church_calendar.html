church calendar

1. NOUN

/ tʃɜrtʃ ˈkæləndɚ / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈkælɪndə / (BrE)
A calendar of the Christian year that shows the dates of Christian fasts, festivals, and other religious observances.
Christian calendar of religious observances

Examples

The church calendar lists all the major Christian holidays and observances.
According to the church calendar, Easter is celebrated in the spring each year.
The priest explained the significance of each holy day on the church calendar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liturgical year - The annual cycle of Christian seasons and festivals
