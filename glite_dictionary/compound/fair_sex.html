fair sex

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛr ˌsɛks / (AmE), / ˈfeə ˌsɛks / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term used to refer to women as a group, often with a sense of admiration or gallantry
old-fashioned term for women as a group
archaic

Examples

Many poets have celebrated the virtues and charms of the fair sex in their writings.
He always spoke highly of the fair sex and treated all women with respect.
Such an old-fashioned view of the fair sex is rarely found in modern society.
