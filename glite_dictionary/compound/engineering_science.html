engineering science

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr.ɪŋ ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪər.ɪŋ ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The branch of knowledge concerned with applying scientific principles to practical engineering problems and the design of structures, machines, and systems.
application of science to engineering

Examples

She decided to specialize in engineering science at university.
Advances in engineering science have led to more efficient renewable energy systems.
The conference attracted experts from all fields of engineering science.

Synset Examples

he had trouble deciding which branch of engineering to study

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engineering - the branch of science and technology concerned with design and building

Etymology

Engineering from Medieval Latin 'ingeniator' (contriver, inventor) and science from Latin 'scientia' (knowledge).
