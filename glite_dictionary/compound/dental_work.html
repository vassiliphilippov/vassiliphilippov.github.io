dental work

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl wɝk / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl wɜːk / (BrE)
procedures and treatments performed by a dentist to maintain or restore the health of teeth and gums
procedures performed by a dentist

Examples

She had to schedule her dental work around her vacation plans.
Many people are nervous about getting dental work done at the dentist's office.
After his accident, he required extensive dental work to repair his broken teeth.

Synset Examples

Midaz is often used to help patients during dental work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dental procedure - a single operation or treatment done by a dentist
  • Degree Higher: oral surgery - surgical procedures performed in the mouth
