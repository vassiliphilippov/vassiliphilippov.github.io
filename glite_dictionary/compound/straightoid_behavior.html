straightoid behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪtoɪd bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtɔɪd bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Behavior that is reminiscent of, or mimics, the attitudes, mannerisms, or lifestyle typically associated with heterosexual individuals, often used in contrast with behaviors seen as characteristic of LGBTQ+ communities.
behavior mimicking heterosexual norms
informal slang

Examples

Her friends joked about her straightoid behavior during the office party.
Some members of the group criticized his straightoid behavior as conformist.
They felt pressured to adopt straightoid behavior to fit in at family gatherings.

Synset Examples

His straightoid behavior often alienates his more liberal friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heteronormative - relating to the belief that heterosexuality is the normal or preferred sexual orientation
  • Antonym: queer behavior - behavior perceived as characteristic of LGBTQ+ people

Etymology

Formed from 'straight' (slang for heterosexual) and the suffix '-oid' (resembling), plus 'behavior'.
