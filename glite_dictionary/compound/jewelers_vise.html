jeweler's vise

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːələrz vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːələz vaɪs / (BrE)
A small, precise vise used by jewelers to hold pieces while working on them.
small vise for jewelry making

Examples

The jeweler's vise kept the tiny ring steady as she set the new stone.
He carefully tightened the jeweler's vise to avoid damaging the delicate gold bracelet.
Every professional workshop is equipped with a reliable jeweler's vise for intricate repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vise - a mechanical tool for holding an object firmly in place
