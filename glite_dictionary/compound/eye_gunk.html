eye gunk

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ɡʌŋk / (AmE), / aɪ ɡʌŋk / (BrE)
a sticky or crusty substance that collects in the corners of the eyes, especially after sleep
sticky discharge from the eyes
informal

Examples

When I woke up, I found some eye gunk in the corners of my eyes.
Doctors say eye gunk can sometimes indicate an infection if it changes color.
She wiped the eye gunk away from her baby's lashes gently.

Synset Examples

I woke up with eye gunk stuck in my lashes.
Make sure to clean the eye gunk before bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye discharge - fluid or matter discharged from the eyes
  • Synonym: sleep - the crusty substance that forms in the eyes during sleep
