to feel like a boofhead

1. VERB

/ fil laɪk ə ˈbʊfhɛd / (AmE), / fiːl laɪk ə ˈbʊfhɛd / (BrE)
to feel foolish or stupid, especially after making a mistake; chiefly Australian slang
feel foolish or stupid (Australian slang)
informal slang

Examples

After tripping over her own feet in front of the crowd, she felt like a boofhead.
I totally felt like a boofhead when I called him by the wrong name.
Missing such an easy question made me feel like a boofhead.

Synset Examples

He felt like a boofhead after missing the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel foolish - to experience a sense of embarrassment or stupidity
  • Synonym: boofhead - a foolish or stupid person (Australian slang)

Etymology

From Australian slang 'boofhead' (a foolish person), itself possibly from 'boof' (a blow or bump) + 'head'.
