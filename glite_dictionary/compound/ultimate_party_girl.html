ultimate party girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltɪmət ˈpɑːrti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ˈpɑːti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who is highly sociable, frequently attends parties, and is known for her enthusiasm for nightlife
woman known for partying and nightlife
informal

Examples

Everyone knew Jenna as the ultimate party girl, always ready for a night out.
College was her time to shine as the ultimate party girl in her group of friends.
After settling down, she no longer fit the image of the ultimate party girl.

Synset Examples

She was the ultimate party girl, always the last to leave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party animal - a person who loves parties and social gatherings
