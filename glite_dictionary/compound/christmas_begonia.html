Christmas begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs bəˈgoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs bɪˈɡəʊnɪə / (BrE)
a hybrid begonia grown in winter for its many large pink flowers, often kept as a decorative houseplant
winter-blooming begonia with pink flowers

Examples

The Christmas begonia adds a splash of color to indoor spaces during the winter months.
My grandmother always places a Christmas begonia on her windowsill for the holidays.
Gardeners appreciate the vibrant blooms of the Christmas begonia when most other flowers are dormant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of perennial flowering plants with attractive foliage and flowers
