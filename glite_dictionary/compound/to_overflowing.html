to overflowing

1. ADV

/ tu ˌoʊvɚˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / tə ˌəʊvəˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
so full that nothing more can be contained; filled beyond capacity.
filled beyond capacity

Examples

The theater was packed to overflowing on opening night.
She stuffed her suitcase to overflowing with clothes for the trip.
The donation box was filled to overflowing by generous supporters.

Synset Examples

The cup was poured to overflowing with hot coffee.
The basket was filled to overflowing with fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overflowing - so full that contents spill over
