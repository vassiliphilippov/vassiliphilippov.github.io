steel-wool pad

1. NOUN

/ stiːl wʊl pæd / (AmE), / stiːl wʊl pad / (BrE)
a pad made of steel wool, used for polishing, cleaning, or smoothing surfaces
pad of steel wool for polishing or cleaning

Examples

She used a steel-wool pad to scrub the burnt pan clean.
The old paint came off easily with a steel-wool pad and some solvent.
Always wear gloves when using a steel-wool pad to avoid hurting your hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scouring pad - a pad used for scouring or cleaning surfaces, often made from materials other than steel wool
