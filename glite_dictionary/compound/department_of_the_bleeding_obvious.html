Department of the Bleeding Obvious

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɑːrtmənt əv ðə ˈblidɪŋ əbˈviəs / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt əv ðə ˈbliːdɪŋ əbˈviəs / (BrE)
a humorous or sarcastic term for a statement that is extremely obvious or self-evident
sarcastic way to state something obvious
informal british

Examples

Her comment about water being wet came directly from the Department of the Bleeding Obvious.
If you just realized you need a ticket to travel, that's a finding worthy of the Department of the Bleeding Obvious.
His report, stating that fire is hot, belongs in the Department of the Bleeding Obvious.
