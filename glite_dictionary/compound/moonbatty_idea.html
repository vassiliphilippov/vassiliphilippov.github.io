moonbatty idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnbæti aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈmuːnbæti aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
An idea that is considered crazy, unrealistic, or wildly eccentric.
crazy or unrealistic idea
informal slang

Examples

Her moonbatty idea of building a castle in her backyard made everyone laugh.
Don't dismiss every moonbatty idea; sometimes the wildest concepts lead to real breakthroughs.
He is always coming up with some moonbatty idea that has no chance of working.

Synset Examples

She laughed at his moonbatty ideas about time travel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crackpot idea - an idea considered foolish or absurd
  • Antonym: sensible idea - an idea that is reasonable, practical, or wise

Etymology

From 'moonbat' (slang for an eccentric or crazy person) + '-y' (forming adjectives), plus 'idea'.
