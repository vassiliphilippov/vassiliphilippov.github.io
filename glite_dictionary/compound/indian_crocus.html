Indian crocus

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈkroʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈkrəʊkəs / (BrE)
a small orchid of the genus Pleione, native to Asia, bearing one or two slender-stalked flowers in shades of white, pink, magenta, or occasionally yellow
a dwarf orchid of the genus Pleione

Examples

The Indian crocus blooms in early spring, adding vibrant color to shady woodland gardens.
She carefully transplanted the delicate bulbs of the Indian crocus into her greenhouse collection.
The Indian crocus is prized for its striking magenta flowers and easy cultivation.
