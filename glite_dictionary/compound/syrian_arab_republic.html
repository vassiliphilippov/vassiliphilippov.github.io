Syrian Arab Republic

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪriən ˈærəb rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriən ˈærəb rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name of Syria, a country in the Middle East at the eastern end of the Mediterranean, known for its ancient civilizations
official name of the country Syria
