even so

1. ADV

/ ˌivən ˈsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌiːvən ˈsəʊ / (BrE)
used to introduce a statement that contrasts with or seems to contradict something that has just been said; nevertheless.
nevertheless; in spite of that

Examples

She knew it would be risky, but even so, she decided to proceed.
The weather was terrible; even so, the match continued as planned.
He may not have much experience, but even so, he's a quick learner.

Synset Examples

although I'm a little afraid, however I'd like to try it
while we disliked each other, nevertheless we agreed
he was a stern yet fair master
granted that it is dangerous, all the same I still want to go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nevertheless - in spite of that; nonetheless
  • Synonym: nonetheless - in spite of that; anyway
  • Synonym: however - used to introduce a statement in contrast with something previously mentioned
