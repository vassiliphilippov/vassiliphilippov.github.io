red baneberry

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈbeɪnbɛri / (AmE), / rɛd ˈbeɪnbəri / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with compound leaves, small white flowers in clusters, and bright red poisonous berries.
North American plant with red poisonous berries

Examples

Many hikers avoid touching the red baneberry because its berries are highly poisonous.
In the shaded forest, clusters of red baneberry stood out among the greenery.
Children must be taught to recognize red baneberry and not to eat its tempting fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baneberry - a group of poisonous plants with berries

Etymology

From 'red' + 'baneberry', the latter meaning 'poisonous berry'.
