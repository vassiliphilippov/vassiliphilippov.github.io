to grab coffee

1. VERB

/ ɡræb ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / ɡræb ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to quickly get or buy a cup of coffee, often casually or when in a hurry
quickly get coffee
informal

Examples

Do you want to grab coffee with me after class?
I usually grab coffee before I start work in the morning.
They decided to grab coffee together and catch up on life.

Synset Examples

Let's grab some java before heading to the meeting.
We grabbed coffee at Tim Whoreton's this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get coffee - to obtain a cup of coffee
