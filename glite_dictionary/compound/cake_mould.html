cake mould

1. NOUN

/ keɪk moʊld / (AmE), / keɪk məʊld / (BrE)
a container made of metal, silicone, or other materials, used to shape a cake as it bakes
container for shaping cake during baking
british

Examples

She poured the batter into the cake mould before placing it in the oven.
Different shapes of cake moulds can make your desserts more decorative.
The recipe recommends using a round cake mould for even baking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake pan - a metal or silicone container for baking cakes (American English)
