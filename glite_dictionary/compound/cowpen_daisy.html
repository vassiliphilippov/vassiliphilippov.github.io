cowpen daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊˌpɛn ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌpɛn ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a coarse, greyish-green annual herb with yellow flowers, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.
a yellow-flowered annual herb of the southwest US and Mexico

Examples

The field was speckled with blooming cowpen daisy during the late summer months.
Ranchers often find cowpen daisy growing near livestock enclosures.
Some gardeners cultivate cowpen daisy for its bright yellow flowers and hardiness.
