flowerbed

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ.bɛd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə.bɛd / (BrE)
A section of ground, often in a garden, specially prepared for growing flowers.
garden area for growing flowers

Examples

Tulips bloomed in the flowerbed along the front walkway.
They spent Saturday afternoon planting bulbs in the new flowerbed.
Weeds had started to grow around the edges of the flowerbed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower garden - a garden or part of a garden where flowers are grown
