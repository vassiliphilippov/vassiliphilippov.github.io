stage door

1. NOUN

/ steɪdʒ dɔr / (AmE), / steɪdʒ dɔː / (BrE)
an entrance to the backstage area of a theater, used by performers and theater staff
entrance to backstage area of a theater

Examples

The actors waited by the stage door for their cues before entering the scene.
Fans often gather outside the stage door hoping for autographs from the performers.
Only authorized personnel are allowed through the stage door during a show.
