consulting service

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌltɪŋ ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / kənˈsʌltɪŋ ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a service provided by a professional advisor, such as a lawyer, doctor, or consultant, offering expert advice to clients
service offering expert professional advice

Examples

The company hired a consulting service to help improve its organizational structure.
Many small businesses rely on external consulting services for legal and financial advice.
She launched her own consulting service specializing in environmental regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultancy - the business of giving expert advice to other professionals
