chicken drumstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˈdrʌmˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈdrʌmstɪk / (BrE)
the lower part of a chicken's leg, especially as cooked and served as food
lower leg of a chicken (food)

Examples

She served fried chicken drumsticks with mashed potatoes for dinner.
The picnic basket was filled with cold chicken drumsticks and sandwiches.
He prefers the juicy meat of the chicken drumstick over the breast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drumstick - the lower part of a bird's leg, especially as food
