ink bottle

1. NOUN

/ ɪŋk ˈbɑːt̬əl / (AmE), / ɪŋk ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
A bottle that contains ink, typically used for refilling pens or for writing and drawing.
bottle containing ink

Examples

He accidentally knocked over the ink bottle, spilling black ink all over his desk.
She dipped her old-fashioned pen into the ink bottle before starting to write the letter.
The artist kept a blue ink bottle next to his sketchbook for quick access while drawing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inkwell - a small container for ink used in writing with a dip pen
