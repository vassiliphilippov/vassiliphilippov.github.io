skank behavior

1. NOUN

/ skæŋk bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / skæŋk bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Disreputable, immoral, or deliberately provocative behavior considered low or contemptible, especially in a sexual context.
disreputable or immoral behavior
informal slang offensive

Examples

Rumors about her skank behavior spread quickly through the small town.
He wanted to distance himself from the skank behavior he saw at the party.
The manager warned employees that skank behavior would not be tolerated in the workplace.

Synset Examples

She avoided the skank behavior that others displayed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleazy behavior - immoral or sordid behavior
  • Antonym: respectable behavior - proper or socially approved behavior

Etymology

From "skank," a slang term for a promiscuous or contemptible person, combined with "behavior."
