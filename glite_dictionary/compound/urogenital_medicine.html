urogenital medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊroʊˈdʒɛnɪtl ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪtl ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the urinary tract and the urogenital system
medical field for urinary and urogenital disorders
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in urogenital medicine after completing her medical degree.
Advances in urogenital medicine have greatly improved treatment options for kidney and bladder issues.
The hospital's urogenital medicine department offers comprehensive care for both men and women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urology - the branch of medicine dealing with the urinary system

Etymology

From Latin 'uro-' (urine) + 'genital' (relating to reproduction).
