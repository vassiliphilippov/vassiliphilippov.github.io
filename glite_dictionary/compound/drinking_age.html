drinking age

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the minimum legal age at which a person is allowed to purchase or consume alcoholic beverages
minimum legal age for alcohol purchase or consumption

Examples

The drinking age in most states is 21 years old.
Many people debate whether the drinking age should be lowered to 18.
She celebrated her birthday by legally buying a drink on reaching the drinking age.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal age - the age at which a person is legally recognized as an adult or eligible for a right
