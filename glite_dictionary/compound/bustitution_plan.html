bustitution plan

1. NOUN

/ bʌsˈtɪtʃuːʃən plæn / (AmE), / bʌsˈtɪtjuːʃən plæn / (BrE)
a plan arranging for buses to temporarily replace train service, usually during track maintenance or disruptions
plan to use buses instead of trains temporarily

Examples

The railway announced a bustitution plan for the weekend while the tracks are repaired.
Commuters should review the bustitution plan before planning their journeys next week.
A detailed bustitution plan was shared online ahead of the scheduled subway closure.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replacement service - a service that stands in for another, especially public transit

Etymology

Blend of 'bus' and 'substitution', modeling after terms like 'substitution', plus 'plan.'
