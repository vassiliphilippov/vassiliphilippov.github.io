faggot stitch

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæɡət stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfæɡət stɪtʃ / (BrE)
A decorative sewing stitch used to join groups of parallel threads or edges in an openwork pattern, often in embroidery or fagoting.
sewing stitch for joining threads in openwork

Examples

She used a faggot stitch to create a delicate border along the edge of the pillowcase.
The dress features panels connected by intricate faggot stitches.
Learning the faggot stitch is essential for certain types of heirloom embroidery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fagoting - an embroidery technique joining edges with open stitches

Etymology

From 'faggot' (a bundle of sticks) in the sense of a bundle, plus 'stitch'.
