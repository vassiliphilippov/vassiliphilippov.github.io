sense of smell

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛns əv smɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɛns əv smɛl / (BrE)
the ability or faculty to perceive and distinguish odors through the nose
ability to perceive odors

Examples

Dogs have a remarkable sense of smell that allows them to detect even faint scents.
Losing her sense of smell made it difficult to enjoy her favorite foods.
The perfume was so strong that it overpowered my sense of smell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smell - the faculty or act of perceiving odors
