street credibility

1. NOUN

/ stɹit krəˌdɪbɪlɪti / (AmE), / stɹiːt krəˌdɪbɪlɪti / (BrE)
respect and acceptance among young, fashionable, or urban people, especially those involved in popular culture or subcultures.
respect among urban or fashionable youth

Examples

The new rapper quickly gained street credibility after performing in several underground clubs.
Wearing that brand helps boost your street credibility among teenagers.
He lost some street credibility when people learned he grew up in the suburbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cred - informal abbreviation for credibility, especially among young people
  • Synonym: street cred - informal, shortened form meaning the same as street credibility
