pedo van

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːdoʊ væn / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəʊ væn / (BrE)
(slang) A van, typically unmarked and plain, that is associated with or suspected of being used by child predators.
slang for van associated with child predators
slang

Examples

The rumor about a pedo van in the area made parents uneasy.
Some memes jokingly depict a plain white van as a pedo van.
He crossed the street when he saw a suspicious pedo van parked near the playground.

Synset Examples

A pedo van was reported lurking in the school area.
Parents were alerted to watch for a pedo van in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white van - a plain, unmarked van often associated with suspicious activity

Etymology

From "pedo", abbreviation of "pedophile", and "van"; "pedo van" popularized through internet slang and memes.
