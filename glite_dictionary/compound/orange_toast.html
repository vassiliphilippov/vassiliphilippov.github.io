orange toast

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrɪndʒ toʊst / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ təʊst / (BrE)
toasted bread spread with butter, sugar, grated orange rind, and a little orange juice
toast with orange-flavored butter and sugar

Examples

She prepared a plate of orange toast for breakfast to surprise her guests.
The unique flavor of orange toast makes it a delightful morning treat.
You can easily make orange toast by mixing sugar and orange zest into the butter.
