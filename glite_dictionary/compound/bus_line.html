bus line

1. NOUN

/ bʌs laɪn / (AmE), / bʌs laɪn / (BrE)
an organization that operates buses on fixed routes for public transportation
company operating a bus transportation system

Examples

The city awarded the contract to a new bus line to improve public transport services.
After the bus line went out of business, many commuters had to find alternative transportation.
She worked for a bus line coordinating schedules and assisting passengers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus company - a company providing bus services
  • Degree Lower: bus route - a fixed path traveled by buses
