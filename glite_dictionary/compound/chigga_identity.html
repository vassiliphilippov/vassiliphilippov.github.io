chigga identity

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪɡə aɪˈdɛntɪti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪɡə aɪˈdɛntɪti / (BrE)
A cultural identity that blends African American and Chinese influences, especially in style, language, and behavior.
African American–Chinese blended cultural identity
informal slang

Examples

The rapper's lyrics reflect his chigga identity.
Many young artists embrace a chigga identity in their music and fashion choices.
Discussions about cultural appropriation often mention the concept of chigga identity.

Synset Examples

The rapper's lyrics reflect his chigga identity.

Etymology

Blend of 'Chinese' and a reclaimed slang term from African American English, used controversially.
