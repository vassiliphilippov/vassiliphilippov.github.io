sharp riposte

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp ɹɪˈpoʊst / (AmE), / ʃɑːp rɪˈpɒst / (BrE)
A quick and witty reply, especially one that counters a criticism or insult.
quick, witty reply

Examples

Her sharp riposte silenced the critic instantly.
He always has a sharp riposte ready during debates.
The comedian's sharp riposte drew laughter from the crowd.

Synset Examples

She had a sharp riposte ready for his criticism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - a quick, witty, or sharp reply
  • Synonym: comeback - a witty or sharp reply to criticism
  • Synonym: rejoinder - a reply, especially a sharp or witty one

Etymology

From French 'riposte', meaning a quick counter in fencing or dialogue.
