hedge sparrow

1. NOUN

/ hɛdʒ ˈspæroʊ / (AmE), / hɛdʒ ˈspærəʊ / (BrE)
a small brownish European songbird (Prunella modularis), commonly found in hedgerows and gardens, also known as the dunnock.
small brown European songbird, the dunnock

Examples

A hedge sparrow was singing in the bushes near the garden gate.
During spring, you can spot the hedge sparrow darting among the hedgerows.
The hedge sparrow is often mistaken for a female house sparrow due to its similar coloring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dunnock - common alternative name for the hedge sparrow
