how-do-you-do

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaʊ duː ju duː / (AmE), / ˌhaʊ dɪ juː ˈduː / (BrE)
A formal expression of greeting or polite acknowledgement when meeting someone, especially for the first time.
formal greeting or salutation

Examples

She greeted her new colleague with a polite how-do-you-do and a handshake.
When introduced to the ambassador, he offered a formal how-do-you-do.
Their conversation started with the usual how-do-you-do before moving on to business.

Synset Examples

every morning they exchanged polite hellos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hello - informal greeting
  • Synonym: hi - informal greeting
  • Antonym: goodbye - expression used to bid farewell
