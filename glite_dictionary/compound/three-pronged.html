three-pronged

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriˌprɑŋd / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌprɒŋd / (BrE)
having three projecting parts or points, typically used for tools, utensils, or approaches
having three prongs or points

Examples

She used a three-pronged fork to pick up the salad.
The company announced a three-pronged strategy to increase sales.
The farmer repaired his old three-pronged pitchfork before working in the field.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: two-pronged - having two projecting prongs or points
  • Degree Higher: four-pronged - having four projecting prongs or points
