plastering trowel

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstərɪŋ ˈtraʊəl / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstərɪŋ ˈtraʊəl / (BrE)
a flat-bladed hand tool used to spread and smooth plaster on walls and ceilings
tool for spreading and smoothing plaster

Examples

The mason used a plastering trowel to create a smooth finish on the wall.
After dipping the plastering trowel into the mixture, she spread it evenly across the ceiling.
It's important to keep your plastering trowel clean for the best results while plastering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trowel - a small hand tool with a handle and flat blade, used for applying and smoothing mortar or plaster
