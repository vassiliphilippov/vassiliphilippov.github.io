epacris family

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈpækrɪs ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ɪˈpakrɪs ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of Australasian shrubs and small trees, scientifically known as Epacridaceae
family of Australasian shrubs and trees

Examples

Many native Australian gardens feature members of the epacris family for their delicate flowers.
Botanists studied several rare species within the epacris family during their fieldwork.
The epacris family is closely related to the heather family but primarily found in Australasia.

Etymology

Named from the type genus Epacris, from Greek 'epakris' meaning 'on the summit', referring to its typical habitat.
