lady of pleasure

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdi əv ˈplɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi əv ˈplɛʒə / (BrE)
a woman who works as a prostitute, especially in historical or literary contexts
prostitute (euphemistic/historical)

Examples

In Victorian novels, a lady of pleasure was often depicted as a tragic figure.
He was warned that he should not associate with any ladies of pleasure in the city.
The old inn was rumored to have once been frequented by ladies of pleasure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostitute - a person who engages in sexual activity for payment
