hairy darling pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛri ˈdɑrlɪŋ pi / (AmE), / ˈheəri ˈdɑːlɪŋ piː / (BrE)
A shrubby perennial plant native to southern Australia, with downy or woolly stems, hairy undersides of leaves, and racemes of red to pink flowers.
Australian shrubby perennial plant

Examples

The hairy darling pea brightens the garden with its vibrant pink blooms in the spring.
Botanists studied the hairy darling pea for its adaptation to dry Australian climates.
Several hairy darling peas were planted along the walking trail to showcase native flora.
