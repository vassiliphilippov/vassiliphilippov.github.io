aronia liqueur

1. NOUN

/ əˈroʊniə lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / əˈrəʊnɪə lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic liqueur made from aronia berries, known for its deep color and tart flavor.
strong liqueur made from aronia berries

Examples

Many artisan distilleries now produce aronia liqueur for local markets and tourists.
The dessert was drizzled with a rich aronia liqueur to enhance its flavor.
She brought a bottle of aronia liqueur back from her trip to Eastern Europe.
