naked-muzzled

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪkɪdˌmʌzəld / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪdˌmʌzəld / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a hairless muzzle.
having a hairless muzzle (animal term)

Examples

The tapir is a naked-muzzled mammal native to Central and South America.
Certain breeds of dogs are known for their naked-muzzled appearance.
The zoologist noted the naked-muzzled characteristic among the primates in the region.
