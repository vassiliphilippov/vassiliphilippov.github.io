cab fare

1. NOUN

/ kæb fɛr / (AmE), / kæb feə / (BrE)
the amount of money charged for a ride in a taxicab
taxi ride charge

Examples

He handed the driver some cash to cover the cab fare.
The company reimbursed her for the cab fare to the airport.
I forgot my wallet and couldn't pay the cab fare after my ride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi fare - the fare charged for a ride in a taxi
