phone book

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn bʊk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn bʊk / (BrE)
a book containing an alphabetical list of telephone subscribers, with their addresses and telephone numbers
directory of telephone numbers and addresses

Examples

She searched the phone book for his number but couldn't find it.
A thick phone book sat unused on the doorstep for weeks.
Many people now use online directories instead of a traditional phone book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone directory - a book listing telephone subscribers and their numbers
