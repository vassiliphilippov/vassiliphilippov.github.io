Tower of Pharos

1. PROPN

/ ˈtaʊɚ əv ˈfeɪrɒs / (AmE), / ˈtaʊə əv ˈfærɒs / (BrE)
the ancient lighthouse built on the island of Pharos at Alexandria, regarded as one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World
ancient lighthouse at Alexandria

Examples

The Tower of Pharos guided countless sailors safely to the port of Alexandria.
Ancient texts describe the Tower of Pharos as a marvel of engineering.
Archaeologists still debate the exact appearance of the legendary Tower of Pharos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lighthouse of Alexandria - another name for the Tower of Pharos, the ancient lighthouse

Etymology

Named after Pharos, the island near Alexandria, Egypt, where the lighthouse was built.
