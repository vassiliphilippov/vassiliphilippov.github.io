book jacket

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈdʒækət / (AmE), / bʊk ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a removable paper cover for a book, typically printed with promotional information and artwork
paper cover for a book

Examples

She carefully removed the book jacket to keep it from getting torn.
The book jacket featured a glowing review from a famous author.
Collectors often value first editions with the original book jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust jacket - removable paper cover of a book
  • Degree Lower: book cover - the protective cover of a book
