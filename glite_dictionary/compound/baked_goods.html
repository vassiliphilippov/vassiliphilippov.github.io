baked goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkt ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkt ɡʊdz / (BrE)
foods such as breads, cakes, and pastries that are cooked by baking, especially in an oven
foods baked in an oven, like bread and cakes

Examples

The bakery on Main Street is famous for its delicious baked goods.
She brought a variety of homemade baked goods to the school fundraiser.
Many people enjoy baked goods with their morning coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry - a type of sweet baked food made of dough, such as a tart or pie
  • Synonym: confectionery - sweets and candies collectively
