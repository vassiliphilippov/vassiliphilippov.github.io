felt fern

1. NOUN

/ fɛlt fɝn / (AmE), / fɛlt fɜːn / (BrE)
An East Asian fern with fronds shaped like tongues, sometimes classified in the genus Cyclophorus.
East Asian fern with tongue-shaped fronds

Examples

The unique appearance of the felt fern makes it popular among indoor plant enthusiasts.
Botanists sometimes debate whether the felt fern should be placed in the genus Cyclophorus.
In its native habitat, the felt fern grows on mossy rocks and shaded forest floors.
