broad-leaved everlasting pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔd ˌlivd ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ pi / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd ˌliːvd ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋ piː / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant native to central and southern Europe, with broad leaves and purple, pink, or white flowers, often naturalized in North America.
a perennial climbing plant with broad leaves and colorful flowers

Examples

The broad-leaved everlasting pea produces vibrant blooms that brighten any garden in mid-summer.
Gardeners value the broad-leaved everlasting pea for its ability to quickly cover trellises and fences.
Many wild areas in North America now host naturalized populations of the broad-leaved everlasting pea.
