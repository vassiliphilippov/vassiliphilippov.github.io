celestial globe

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛstʃl ɡloʊb / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstɪəl ɡləʊb / (BrE)
a spherical model that represents the apparent positions of the stars and constellations in the sky
spherical model of the heavens

Examples

The museum displayed a rare celestial globe from the 17th century.
He studied the stars with the help of a detailed celestial globe.
An ornate celestial globe decorated the astronomer's study.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: terrestrial globe - a globe representing the Earth
