side-look

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪd lʊk / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd lʊk / (BrE)
A glance to the side, especially with a particular emotion or intention.
a glance to the side

Examples

She shot him a sharp side-look before answering the question.
With a sly side-look, he revealed the secret to his friend.
A cold side-look from her was enough to stop his laughter.

Synset Examples

she shot him an impatient side-glance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: side-glance - a quick look to the side, especially with emotion or intent
