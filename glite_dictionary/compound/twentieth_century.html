twentieth century

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛn.ti.əθ ˈsɛn.tʃə.ri / (AmE), / ˈtwɛntiəθ ˈsɛn.tʃʊ.ri / (BrE)
the period of time from the year 1901 to the year 2000
the period from 1901 to 2000

Examples

Many technological advances were made during the twentieth century.
The twentieth century witnessed two world wars and rapid social change.
Art deco became popular in the early twentieth century.
