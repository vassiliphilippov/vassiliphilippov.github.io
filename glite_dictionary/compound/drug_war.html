drug war

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ wɔr / (AmE), / drʌɡ wɔː / (BrE)
the ongoing conflict or efforts by law enforcement agencies to combat the production, distribution, and sale of illegal drugs
conflict over illegal drugs

Examples

Many politicians have debated the effectiveness of the drug war in reducing crime rates.
Thousands of people have been affected by the violence resulting from the drug war in various countries.
The government increased funding for new initiatives to fight the drug war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war on drugs - official campaign against illegal drugs, especially in the U.S.
