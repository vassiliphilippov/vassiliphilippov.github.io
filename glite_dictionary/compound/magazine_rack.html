magazine rack

1. NOUN

/ məˈɡæzˌin ræk / (AmE), / məˈɡæz.iːn ræk / (BrE)
a frame or stand used for holding and displaying magazines
stand for holding magazines

Examples

She placed the latest issue of the journal in the magazine rack by the sofa.
The waiting room was neatly organized, with a magazine rack full of entertainment and news periodicals.
After assembling the magazine rack, he arranged the family's favorite publications inside it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rack - a framework, stand, or shelf for holding things
