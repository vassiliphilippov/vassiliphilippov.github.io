clouding up

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaʊdɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the process of clouds forming and becoming visible in the sky, often indicating an upcoming change in weather
formation of visible clouds in the sky

Examples

Within a few minutes, the sky began clouding up as a storm approached.
We canceled our picnic because of the sudden clouding up in the afternoon.
The weather report mentioned the clouding up would precede rain by an hour or two.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloud formation - the act or process of clouds being created in the atmosphere
