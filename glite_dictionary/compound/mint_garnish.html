mint garnish

1. NOUN

/ mɪnt ˈɡɑrnɪʃ / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈɡɑːnɪʃ / (BrE)
a sprig or leaf of mint used to decorate and enhance the appearance and flavor of food or drinks
sprig of mint for decoration

Examples

The chocolate cake was finished with a mint garnish for a fresh and colorful look.
She placed a mint garnish on top of the fruit salad before serving.
A cool drink with a mint garnish is especially refreshing on a hot day.

Synset Examples

They added a mint garnish to the dessert for decoration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garnish - an item used to decorate food or drink
