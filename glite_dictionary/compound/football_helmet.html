football helmet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtbɔl ˌhɛlmɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ˌhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
A hard, padded helmet with a face mask, worn to protect the head and face of players in American football.
protective helmet for American football

Examples

Every player is required to wear a football helmet during the game for safety.
He decorated his old football helmet with stickers from each season.
The team's new football helmets feature an upgraded face mask for better protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmet - a protective head covering
