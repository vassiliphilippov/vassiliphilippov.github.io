amateurish attempt

1. NOUN

/ ˌæmətɚɪʃ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˌamətʃʊərɪʃ əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an effort or attempt that shows a lack of skill or professionalism, typically made by someone inexperienced
inexperienced or unskilled attempt

Examples

His amateurish attempt at painting the kitchen ended with paint splattered all over the floor.
The team's amateurish attempt at singing the national anthem was met with polite applause.
Despite her amateurish attempt at baking, the cake turned out surprisingly tasty.

Synset Examples

His amateurish attempts at fixing the car only made things worse.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: professional attempt - an effort performed with the skill and standards expected of a professional
  • Synonym: amateur effort - an effort made by someone who is not a professional
