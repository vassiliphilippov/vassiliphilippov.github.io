future progressive tense

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuː.tʃɚ prəˈɡrɛs.ɪv tɛns / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.tʃə prəˈɡrɛs.ɪv tɛns / (BrE)
the verb tense used to refer to an action that will be ongoing at a particular time in the future
tense for ongoing future actions

Examples

By this time tomorrow, I will be working at my new job; this is the future progressive tense.
The future progressive tense is used in 'She will be studying all night.'
Teachers often explain the future progressive tense when discussing English verb forms.

Synset Examples

‘I will be running’ is an example of the future progressive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: present progressive tense - tense for ongoing present actions
  • Synonym: past progressive tense - tense for ongoing past actions
