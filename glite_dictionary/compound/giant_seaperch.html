giant seaperch

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsiːˌpɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsiːˌpɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A large species of perch known for its sporting and eating qualities, found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater environments.
large edible sporting fish species

Examples

Anglers come from around the world to catch the giant seaperch in these waters.
The restaurant is famous for its grilled giant seaperch dishes.
Juvenile giant seaperch can live in both fresh and brackish water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barramundi - another large, edible Asian-Australian fish species often called 'giant perch'
