quintessential sarong party girl

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɪn.tɪˈsɛn.ʃəl səˈrɔŋ ˈpɑːr.ti ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˌkwɪn.tɪˈsen.ʃəl səˈrɒŋ ˈpɑː.ti ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A typical or perfect example of a sociable young woman, especially of Southeast Asian origin, who enjoys parties, nightlife, and the beach and is often seen wearing a sarong.
typical fun-loving, beachgoing party girl stereotype

Examples

She's known around the city as the quintessential sarong party girl, always ready for the next event.
At the party, everyone agreed she was the quintessential sarong party girl with her vibrant style and outgoing personality.
The movie features a quintessential sarong party girl character who loves the sun, sand, and socializing.

Synset Examples

At the beach, she was the quintessential sarong party girl.

Etymology

Sarong from Malay 'sarung' (covering), 'party girl' from English, 'quintessential' from Latin 'quintus' (fifth) and 'essentia' (essence).
