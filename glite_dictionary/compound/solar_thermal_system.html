solar thermal system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlɚ ˈθɝːməl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈθɜːməl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system that uses sunlight to produce heat, often for heating water or buildings, or generating electricity.
system using sunlight to produce heat

Examples

The new building uses a solar thermal system to supply hot water to all the apartments.
Installing a solar thermal system can reduce your home's energy costs significantly.
The engineer explained how the solar thermal system captures sunlight to heat the swimming pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solar panel - a device that converts sunlight into electricity
