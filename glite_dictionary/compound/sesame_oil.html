sesame oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛsəmi ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəmi ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow to dark brown oil obtained from sesame seeds, used in cooking and as a flavoring
oil from sesame seeds used for cooking

Examples

She drizzled sesame oil over the salad for extra flavor.
Authentic Asian dishes often call for a splash of sesame oil.
He prefers to cook his stir-fry vegetables using sesame oil instead of butter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable oil - oil derived from plants used in cooking
