a bit hokey

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈhoʊki / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈhəʊki / (BrE)
somewhat cheesy or corny; slightly sentimental, inauthentic, or lacking in sophistication
somewhat cheesy or corny
informal

Examples

I found the ending of the film a bit hokey, but it still made me smile.
Her speech was a bit hokey, with all those clichés, but it came from the heart.
Some guests thought the party decorations were a bit hokey for such a formal occasion.

Synset Examples

The decorations were a bit hokey for the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheesy - tastelessly sentimental or insincere
  • Synonym: corny - unoriginal, overly sentimental

Etymology

From 'hokey', an American colloquialism possibly from 'hokum' (stage nonsense) + the intensifier phrase 'a bit.'
