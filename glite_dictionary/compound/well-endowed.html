well-endowed

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl ɪnˈdaʊd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ɪnˈdaʊd / (BrE)
having a large amount of a particular desirable quality, resource, or attribute; richly supplied or equipped
richly provided or equipped

Examples

The university is well-endowed with libraries, making it an excellent place for research.
Thanks to her generous sponsors, the artist found herself well-endowed with materials to complete her project.
He considered himself well-endowed with patience and resilience, which helped him in his career.

Synset Examples

He was well-endowed with both talent and charm.
She felt well-endowed with opportunities in her career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endowed - provided or supplied with a particular quality, ability, or asset
