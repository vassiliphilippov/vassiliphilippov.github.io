filter bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪltɚ bɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɪltə bɛd / (BrE)
a layer of sand or gravel used to filter water in water treatment systems
layer of sand/gravel for water filtration

Examples

The wastewater treatment plant uses a filter bed to remove impurities before discharging water into the river.
Replacing the sand in the filter bed improved the filtration process significantly.
Every six months, the engineers inspect each filter bed for signs of clogging or contamination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - device or substance used to remove impurities or particles
