temple orange tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpəl ˈɔrɪndʒ tri / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpəl ˈɒrɪndʒ triː / (BrE)
A large citrus tree known for producing large, sweet, deep-orange fruit that is easy to peel, widely grown in Florida.
citrus tree with sweet, easy-to-peel orange fruit

Examples

The temple orange tree thrives in the warm climate of central Florida.
Farmers prefer the temple orange tree for its sweet, easy-to-peel fruit.
She planted a temple orange tree in her backyard to enjoy fresh citrus each season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange tree - tree producing oranges, a common citrus tree
