rampant cronyism

1. NOUN

/ ˈræmpənt ˈkroʊniˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈræmpənt ˈkrəʊniˌɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice of widespread favoritism in appointments or awarding advantages, especially to friends or associates, often disregarding merit
widespread favoritism, especially among friends or associates

Examples

Accusations of rampant cronyism led to public outrage and demands for reform.
The company was plagued by rampant cronyism, making it hard for qualified outsiders to advance.
Political analysts warned that rampant cronyism could undermine public trust in the government.

Synset Examples

The project suffered due to rampant cronyism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nepotism - favoritism shown to relatives, especially in job appointments
  • Synonym: favoritism - the practice of giving unfair preferential treatment
