northern lights

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðɚn ˌlaɪts / (AmE), / ˈnɔːð(ə)n ˌlaɪts / (BrE)
the aurora borealis; a natural display of colorful lights in the sky, usually seen in the northern polar regions
aurora borealis; lights in the northern sky

Examples

On our trip to Norway, we were lucky enough to see the northern lights dance across the sky.
Many travelers visit Iceland hoping to catch a glimpse of the spectacular northern lights.
The northern lights are a breathtaking natural phenomenon visible in some high-latitude regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aurora - a natural light display in the sky, especially near the polar regions
  • Antonym: southern lights - the aurora australis; similar phenomenon in the Southern Hemisphere
