raw milk

1. NOUN

/ rɔ mɪlk / (AmE), / rɔː mɪlk / (BrE)
milk that has not been pasteurized to kill bacteria or pathogens
unpasteurized milk

Examples

Some people believe that raw milk has more health benefits than pasteurized milk.
It is illegal to sell raw milk in some countries due to health concerns.
The farm offers bottles of fresh raw milk each morning to local customers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pasteurized milk - milk that has been heat-treated to kill bacteria
