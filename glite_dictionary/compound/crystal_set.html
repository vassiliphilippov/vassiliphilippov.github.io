crystal set

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstəl sɛt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəl sɛt / (BrE)
an early type of simple radio receiver that uses a crystal detector to receive radio signals without external power
early simple radio receiver using a crystal

Examples

He built a crystal set from a kit when he was a child.
Listening to distant stations on a crystal set was a popular hobby in the 1920s.
The museum displayed an antique crystal set alongside other early radio equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystal radio - simple radio receiver using a crystal detector
