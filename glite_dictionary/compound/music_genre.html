music genre

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuː.zɪk ˈʒɑn.rə / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.zɪk ˈʒɒn.rə / (BrE)
A category of music distinguished by a particular style, form, or content.
category or style of music

Examples

My favorite music genre is jazz because of its improvisational style.
Rock became a popular music genre in the 1960s and 70s.
Each music genre attracts different kinds of listeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musical style - particular manner or way of expressing music

Etymology

Genre from French, originally from Latin genus (kind or type).
