tough going

1. NOUN

/ tʌf ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / tʌf ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation or task that is difficult or requires a lot of effort to complete
difficult or challenging situation

Examples

The first few miles of the marathon were tough going due to the steep hills.
It's tough going managing work and studying at the same time.
After the storm, clearing the fallen trees was really tough going.

Synset Examples

It’s been tough going with all the setbacks.
The hike was tough going but worth the view.
The project has been tough going since the start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard going - a situation or task that is challenging or strenuous
  • Antonym: smooth sailing - a situation or process that is easy and without problems
