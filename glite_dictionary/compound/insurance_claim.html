insurance claim

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊrəns kleɪm / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərəns kleɪm / (BrE)
a request made for payment under the terms of an insurance policy, usually due to loss or damage
request for payment from insurance

Examples

She filed an insurance claim after her car was damaged in the storm.
The company rejected his insurance claim due to insufficient evidence.
Processing an insurance claim can sometimes take several weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: claim - a demand for something as rightful
