particle physics

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑɹ.tɪ.kəl ˈfɪz.ɪks / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.tɪ.kəl ˈfɪz.ɪks / (BrE)
the branch of physics concerned with the study of subatomic particles and their interactions
branch of physics studying subatomic particles

Examples

She decided to study particle physics at university after reading about the Higgs boson.
Advancements in particle physics have deepened our understanding of the fundamental forces of nature.
Many countries collaborate on particle physics experiments at facilities like CERN.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-energy physics - another term for particle physics, emphasizing high-energy processes
