death cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθ kæp / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ kap / (BrE)
An extremely poisonous white mushroom (Amanita phalloides) with a cup-shaped base and white gills, often mistaken for edible varieties.
poisonous white mushroom (Amanita phalloides)

Examples

The death cap is responsible for the majority of fatal mushroom poisonings worldwide.
Foragers are warned never to consume wild mushrooms unless they can distinguish a death cap from edible species.
Even a small bite of a death cap can cause severe liver damage and potentially death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Amanita - genus of mushrooms including several poisonous species
  • Synonym: toadstool - a poisonous mushroom
