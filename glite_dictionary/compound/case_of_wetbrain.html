case of wetbrain

1. NOUN

/ keɪs əv ˈwɛtbreɪn / (AmE), / keɪs ɒv ˈwɛtbreɪn / (BrE)
A condition of mental dullness or cognitive impairment caused by excessive and prolonged alcohol consumption.
mental impairment after excessive drinking
informal

Examples

After his fourth night out, he said he had a case of wetbrain and couldn't focus at work.
You don't want to end up with a case of wetbrain after drinking that much every weekend.
She joked about having a case of wetbrain, but her friends were worried about her heavy drinking.

Synset Examples

After the binge, he felt like he had a case of wetbrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wet brain - Severe brain damage (Wernicke–Korsakoff syndrome) due to chronic alcoholism
