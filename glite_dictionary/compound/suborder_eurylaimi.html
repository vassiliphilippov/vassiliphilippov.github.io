suborder Eurylaimi

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbˈɔrdər ˌjʊrɪˈleɪmaɪ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈɔːdə ˌjʊərɪˈleɪmɪ / (BrE)
A suborder of passerine birds known as broadbills, found mainly in tropical Asia and Africa.
a bird suborder: broadbills
medical

Examples

The suborder Eurylaimi contains several species of broadbills native to tropical forests.
Recent studies offer new insights into the evolution of the suborder Eurylaimi.
Members of the suborder Eurylaimi are known for their striking plumage and large, broad bills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadbill - A group of birds belonging to the suborder Eurylaimi

Etymology

From New Latin 'Eurylaimus', the type genus, itself from Greek 'eurys' (broad) and 'laimos' (throat).
