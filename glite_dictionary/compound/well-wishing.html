well-wishing

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛlˌwɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌwɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing or showing good wishes for someone's success or happiness
expressing good wishes

Examples

Our well-wishing neighbors sent us a card for our anniversary.
He thanked his well-wishing colleagues for their support during a difficult time.
The bride was surrounded by well-wishing friends and family at the wedding.

Synset Examples

his well-wishing supporters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodwill - friendly or helpful feelings toward others
  • Antonym: ill will - hostile or unfriendly feelings

Etymology

Formed from 'well' (good) + 'wishing' (the act of wishing).
