marching music

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrtʃɪŋ ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtʃɪŋ ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music composed for or suited to being played by marching bands, typically with a strong, regular rhythm for marching
music written for marching bands

Examples

The parade was accompanied by lively marching music played by the local band.
He collects records of classic marching music from the early 20th century.
Sousa's compositions are some of the most famous examples of marching music.

Synset Examples

Sousa wrote the best marches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: march - a piece of music with a strong, regular rhythm, intended for marching
