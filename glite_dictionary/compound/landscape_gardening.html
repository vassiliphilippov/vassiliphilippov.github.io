landscape gardening

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændskeɪp ˈɡɑrdn̩ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæn(d)skeɪp ˈɡɑːdn̩ɪŋ / (BrE)
the art and profession of designing, arranging, and maintaining gardens and outdoor areas to create attractive landscapes
designing and maintaining gardens and grounds

Examples

She studied landscape gardening before starting her own design business.
The park's beauty is a testament to skilled landscape gardening.
Many colleges now offer courses in landscape gardening as part of environmental studies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gardening - the practice of cultivating and managing a garden

Etymology

Formed from 'landscape' and 'gardening'; 'landscape' from Dutch 'landschap' (region, scenery).
