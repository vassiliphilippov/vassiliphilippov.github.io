natural persuader

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚl pɚˈsweɪdɚ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl pəˈsweɪdə / (BrE)
a person who has an innate ability to convince others easily
someone skilled at convincing others

Examples

With his charisma and charm, John is a natural persuader who easily inspires confidence in others.
She proved to be a natural persuader during the negotiations, winning over even the most reluctant team members.
It takes a natural persuader to sell such a difficult idea to the board.

Synset Examples

He's a natural persuader, always getting his way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persuader - someone who persuades
