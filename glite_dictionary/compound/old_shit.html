old shit

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ʃɪt / (AmE), / əʊld ʃɪt / (BrE)
past issues, problems, or grievances that are considered tiresome, repetitive, or better left in the past, often used informally or with annoyance.
past issues or annoying problems
informal slang vulgar

Examples

Please stop talking about old shit; I'm ready to move on with my life.
Every time we argue, she brings up the same old shit from years ago.
I'm tired of dealing with old shit that should have been resolved long ago.

Synset Examples

Let’s not bring up that old shit from last year.
I can't deal with this old shit anymore, it’s exhausting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old news - information or problems that have already been discussed and are no longer relevant
