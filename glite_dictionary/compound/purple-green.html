purple-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl ɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color tinged or shaded with purple.
green tinged with purple

Examples

The leaves turned a deep purple-green as autumn approached.
She wore a striking purple-green scarf that complemented her dress.
The artist blended purple-green hues to create a unique background for the painting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: green - A color between blue and yellow in the spectrum; colored like grass or emeralds
  • Degree Higher: purple - A color intermediate between red and blue
