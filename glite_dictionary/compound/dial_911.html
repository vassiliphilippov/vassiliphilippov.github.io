to dial 911

1. VERB

/ daɪəl naɪn wʌn wʌn / (AmE), / daɪəl naɪn wʌn wʌn / (BrE)
to call emergency services by entering the telephone number 911, especially in the United States
call emergency services (US)
american

Examples

If you see smoke coming from the building, you should dial 911 immediately.
She didn't hesitate to dial 911 when she saw the man collapse in the park.
In case of a fire or other emergency, always remember to dial 911 for help.

Synset Examples

They had to dial 911 after the car accident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to call 911 - to telephone emergency services using 911 (US equivalent usage)

Etymology

911 became the universal emergency telephone number in the United States in 1968.
