General Custer

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛnɚəl ˈkʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl ˈkʌstə / (BrE)
(1839–1876) A United States Army general who was killed with all his command by the Sioux at the Battle of Little Bighorn.
U.S. general killed at Little Bighorn

Examples

Many historians still debate whether General Custer made crucial tactical errors at Little Bighorn.
The life and legacy of General Custer remain controversial subjects in American history.
A statue of General Custer stands in his hometown as a tribute to his military career.
