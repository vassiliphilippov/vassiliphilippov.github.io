Great Firewall of China

1. PROPN

/ greɪt ˈfaɪərwɔl əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / greɪt ˈfaɪəwɔːl ɒv ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
the system of internet censorship and surveillance implemented by the Chinese government to restrict access to foreign websites and control online information within China
China's internet censorship system

Examples

Many expatriates use VPNs to bypass the Great Firewall of China.
The Great Firewall of China blocks access to several popular social media platforms.
Researchers study the impact of the Great Firewall of China on digital freedom.

Synset Examples

The Great Firewall of China restricts many foreign websites.
She learned to navigate the Great Firewall of China while traveling.
Accessing social media in China means bypassing the Great Firewall of China.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet censorship - control or suppression of what can be accessed, published, or viewed on the internet
  • Degree Higher: Great Wall of China - ancient defensive wall in northern China; name inspiration

Etymology

Modeled on the term 'firewall' (computer security) and the 'Great Wall of China'.
