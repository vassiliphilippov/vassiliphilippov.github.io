wood pigeon

1. NOUN

/ wʊd ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / wʊd ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
A large Eurasian pigeon with grey plumage and distinctive white patches on its wings and neck.
a large Eurasian pigeon species

Examples

A wood pigeon landed on the garden fence, cooing softly as it looked for food.
We spotted several wood pigeons nesting high in the oak trees.
The distinctive white collar makes the wood pigeon easy to identify among other birds.
