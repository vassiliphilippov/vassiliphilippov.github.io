undreamt of

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdɹɛmpt ʌv / (AmE), / ʌnˈdɹɛmt ɒv / (BrE)
not thought of or imagined as possible; beyond what has been conceived beforehand
not imagined as possible

Examples

The scientists achieved results undreamt of just a decade ago.
We inherited a fortune undreamt of by our ancestors.
Technology has advanced at a pace undreamt of in previous generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginable - impossible to imagine; inconceivable
