Indonesian Borneo

1. PROPN

/ ɪnˌdoʊˈniʒən bɔːrˈnioʊ / (AmE), / ɪnˌdəʊˈniːziən bɔːˈniːəʊ / (BrE)
the southern part of the island of Borneo that is part of Indonesia, also known as Kalimantan
Indonesian part of the island of Borneo

Examples

The forests of Indonesian Borneo are home to many endangered species.
International conservation efforts focus heavily on Indonesian Borneo due to deforestation.
He traveled to Indonesian Borneo to study local traditions and wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kalimantan - the Indonesian portion of Borneo
