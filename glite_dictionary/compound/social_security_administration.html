Social Security Administration

1. PROPN

/ ˌsoʊʃəl səˈkjʊrəti ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəl sɪˈkjʊərəti ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
an independent U.S. government agency responsible for administering the Social Security program, which provides retirement, disability, and survivors' benefits
U.S. agency managing Social Security benefits
american

Examples
