lilac-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪ.læk pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.læk pɪŋk / (BrE)
A shade of pink that is tinged with a hint of lavender or lilac.
pink tinged with lavender

Examples

She wore a delicate dress in lilac-pink that complemented her complexion.
The sky turned a beautiful lilac-pink at dusk, just before the stars appeared.
The artist mixed paint to capture the unique shade of lilac-pink seen in the flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rose-pink - a shade of pink with a rose-like hue
  • Degree Higher: lilac - a pale violet color
  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red color
