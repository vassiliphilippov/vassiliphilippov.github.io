fencer's mask

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnsərz mæsk / (AmE), / ˈfɛnsəz mɑːsk / (BrE)
a protective face covering made of fine mesh, worn over the face during fencing to guard against injury
protective mesh mask for fencing

Examples

Before each bout, she carefully adjusted her fencer's mask for maximum comfort and safety.
The mesh of a fencer's mask allows clear vision while protecting the face from sword strikes.
It is mandatory to wear a fencer's mask in all official fencing competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face
