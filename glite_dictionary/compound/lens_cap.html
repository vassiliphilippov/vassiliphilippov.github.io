lens cap

1. NOUN

/ lɛnz kæp / (AmE), / lɛnz kap / (BrE)
A cover placed over a camera or optical lens to protect it from dust, dirt, and damage when not in use.
protective cover for a lens

Examples

He forgot to replace the lens cap after taking the picture, and the lens got dusty.
Always put the lens cap back on to protect your camera when it's not in use.
The lens cap fell off in my bag, so now my photos look blurry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lens - A piece of glass or other transparent material used to focus or disperse light
