skilled worker

1. NOUN

/ skɪld ˈwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / skɪld ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a worker who has specialized training or expertise in a particular field, trade, or occupation
employee with specialized skills or training

Examples

After completing her apprenticeship, she became a skilled worker in the automotive industry.
Many factories are struggling to find enough skilled workers to operate advanced machinery.
A skilled worker usually earns higher wages than someone without specialized training.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unskilled worker - a worker who does not have specialized skills or training
  • Synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a particular craft
