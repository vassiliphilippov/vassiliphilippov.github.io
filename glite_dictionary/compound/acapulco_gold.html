Acapulco gold

1. NOUN

/ əˌkɑːˈpʊlkoʊ ɡoʊld / (AmE), / əˌkæpʊlˈkəʊ ɡəʊld / (BrE)
a highly potent, sought-after variety of marijuana originally grown in the region around Acapulco, Mexico
potent strain of marijuana from Acapulco

Examples

Rumors spread quickly when people found out he was selling Acapulco gold at the concert.
Some musicians in the 1970s famously referenced Acapulco gold in their songs.
Because of its reputation, Acapulco gold became a highly prized strain among cannabis enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - a drug made from the dried leaves and flowers of the cannabis plant

Etymology

Named after Acapulco, a city in Mexico, and its gold-colored marijuana plants.
