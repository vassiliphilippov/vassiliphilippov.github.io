bubble pack

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl pæk / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl pæk / (BrE)
A type of packaging in which a product is sealed between a clear plastic cover with bubble-shaped protrusions and a backing made of cardboard or similar material.
packaging with plastic bubbles and cardboard backing

Examples

The batteries came in a bubble pack, making them easy to display and hard to tamper with.
I struggled to open the bubble pack without damaging the product inside.
Manufacturers prefer using a bubble pack for small electronics because it protects the item from damage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blister pack - packaging with a pre-formed plastic cavity and a backing, commonly used for pills
