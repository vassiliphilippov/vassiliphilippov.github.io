pen-and-ink

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛn ən ˈɪŋk / (AmE), / ˌpɛn ən ˈɪŋk / (BrE)
a drawing or illustration created using pen and ink, characterized by lines and shading without the use of color
drawing made with pen and ink

Examples

The artist completed a beautiful pen-and-ink of the old church.
She specializes in pen-and-ink illustrations for children's books.
Many classic comic strips were originally drawn in pen-and-ink.
