gas cooker

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈkʊkər / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈkʊkə / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance with gas burners and an oven, used for cooking with gas as a fuel source.
kitchen appliance for cooking with gas

Examples

We replaced our old oven with a new gas cooker for better temperature control.
Many people prefer to use a gas cooker because it heats up quickly.
The landlord supplied a modern gas cooker in the renovated kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - a kitchen appliance used for cooking food
  • Antonym: electric cooker - a kitchen appliance for cooking powered by electricity
