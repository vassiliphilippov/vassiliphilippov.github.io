employee turnover

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪi ˈtɜrnˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪi ˈtɜːnˌəʊvə / (BrE)
the rate at which employees leave a company and are replaced by new employees within a given period
rate at which employees leave and are replaced

Examples

High employee turnover can disrupt a company's operations and reduce overall productivity.
Many managers are concerned about the costs associated with frequent employee turnover.
A positive work environment can help reduce employee turnover in most organizations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attrition - reduction in staff by employees leaving and not being replaced
