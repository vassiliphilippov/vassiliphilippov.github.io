place mat

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪs ˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪs ˌmat / (BrE)
A small mat placed at an individual table setting to protect the surface and serve as decoration during a meal.
individual table mat for dining

Examples

She chose a colorful place mat to brighten up her breakfast table.
Each guest had a bamboo place mat at their setting for the dinner party.
The children spilled juice, but luckily the place mat absorbed most of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablecloth - a cloth spread over a table, especially during meals
