attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfəsɪt ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvəti dɪsˌɔrdər / (AmE), / əˈtɛnʃən ˈdɛfɪsɪt ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪvɪti dɪsˌɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition, especially in children, characterized by difficulty in maintaining attention, hyperactivity, and impulsive behavior.
condition involving hyperactivity and attention difficulties
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that attention deficit hyperactivity disorder can affect both learning and behavior in children.
Teachers have developed new methods to assist students diagnosed with attention deficit hyperactivity disorder.
Many parents seek information about attention deficit hyperactivity disorder after noticing symptoms in their children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ADHD - abbreviation for attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

Etymology

Formed from medical terms: 'attention deficit' (lack of concentration) + 'hyperactivity' (excess movement) + 'disorder'.
