Kunlun Mountains

1. PROPN

/ kuːnˈluːn ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / kuːnˈluːn ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a major mountain range in western China, extending eastward for about 1,000 miles from the Indian border
mountain range in western China

Examples

The Kunlun Mountains are among the longest mountain chains in Asia.
Snow leopards inhabit the high peaks of the Kunlun Mountains.
Travelers cross the Kunlun Mountains to reach the vast deserts of western China.
