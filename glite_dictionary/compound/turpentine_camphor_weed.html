turpentine camphor weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːpənˌtaɪn ˈkæmfər wiːd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːp(ə)nˌtaɪn ˈkæmfə wiːd / (BrE)
an aromatic plant native to the western United States, known for its strong scent and resinous leaves
