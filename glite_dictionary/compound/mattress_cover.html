mattress cover

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætrəs ˌkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈmætrəs ˌkʌvə / (BrE)
a removable protective covering for a mattress, often made of fabric or plastic, used to keep the mattress clean and prolong its life
removable protective covering for a mattress

Examples

She bought a new mattress cover to keep her bed clean and dust-free.
It's a good idea to wash your mattress cover regularly to maintain hygiene.
The mattress cover fits snugly and protects against spills and stains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mattress protector - a specific type of mattress cover designed for added protection, often waterproof
