finishing line

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ laɪn / (BrE)
a line marking the end point of a race or competition
line marking the end of a race

Examples

The runner crossed the finishing line just seconds ahead of his closest rival.
Spectators cheered loudly as each athlete reached the finishing line.
She collapsed with relief after she finally passed the finishing line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish line - a line marking the end of a race (alternative form)
