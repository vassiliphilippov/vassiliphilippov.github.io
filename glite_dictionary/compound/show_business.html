show business

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊ ˌbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ ˌbɪznəs / (BrE)
the world of entertainment, including theater, film, television, and radio, and the people who work in it
the entertainment industry

Examples

After years of working backstage, she finally found her place in show business as an actress.
Many people are drawn to show business by the promise of fame and glamour.
The documentary provides an inside look at the challenges of succeeding in show business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertainment industry - businesses and people involved in entertainment, especially professionally
