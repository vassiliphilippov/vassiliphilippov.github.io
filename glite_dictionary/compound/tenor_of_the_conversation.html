tenor of the conversation

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɚ əv ðə ˌkɑnvɚˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɛnə əv ðə ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the general character, mood, or tone of a conversation
general character or mood of a conversation

Examples

The tenor of the conversation suddenly became more serious as they discussed the accident.
She tried to lighten the tenor of the conversation with a joke.
You could tell from the tenor of the conversation that everyone was worried about the upcoming changes.

Synset Examples

The tenor of the conversation shifted to politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tone of the conversation - the general feeling or attitude expressed in a discussion
  • Synonym: mood of the conversation - the emotional atmosphere present in a discussion

Etymology

From 'tenor' (meaning 'general course or character') + 'conversation.'
