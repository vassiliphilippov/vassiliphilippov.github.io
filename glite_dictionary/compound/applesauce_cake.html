applesauce cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈæp.əlˌsɔs keɪk / (AmE), / ˈæp.əlˌsɔːs keɪk / (BrE)
A moist, spiced cake made with applesauce as a key ingredient.
moist spicy cake with applesauce

Examples

My grandmother always bakes an applesauce cake for the holidays, and it fills the house with a wonderful aroma.
The applesauce cake was both moist and flavorful, thanks to the generous amount of cinnamon and nutmeg.
You can add walnuts or raisins to your applesauce cake recipe for extra texture and taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spice cake - a cake flavored with spices, often similar in style
