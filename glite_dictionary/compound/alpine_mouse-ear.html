Alpine mouse-ear

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.paɪn ˈmaʊs ɪr / (AmE), / ˈæl.paɪn ˈmaʊs ɪə / (BrE)
a small, white-flowered perennial plant (Cerastium alpinum) native to the Arctic and mountainous regions of Europe
mountain and Arctic flowering plant

Examples

The Alpine mouse-ear blooms early in the brief Arctic summer.
Hikers admired the delicate white petals of the Alpine mouse-ear along the mountain trail.
Alpine mouse-ear is well adapted to cold, rocky habitats at high elevations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cerastium - genus of small flowering plants including mouse-ear chickweed
