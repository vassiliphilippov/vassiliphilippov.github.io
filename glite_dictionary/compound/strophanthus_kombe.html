Strophanthus kombe

1. NOUN

/ stroʊˈfænθəs ˈkɒm.bi / (AmE), / strəʊˈfænθəs ˈkɒm.bi / (BrE)
a tropical African climbing plant whose seeds are a source of the cardiac glycoside strophanthin
African plant, source of strophanthin

Examples

The seeds of Strophanthus kombe have been used as a source of heart medication.
Researchers studied the chemical compounds found in Strophanthus kombe for pharmaceutical use.
Local communities have long utilized Strophanthus kombe for its medicinal properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strophanthin - a cardiac glycoside obtained from Strophanthus species

Etymology

From modern Latin (genus Strophanthus) and a local African name (kombe).
