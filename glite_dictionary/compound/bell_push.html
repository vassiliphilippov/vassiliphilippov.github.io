bell push

1. NOUN

/ bɛl pʊʃ / (AmE), / bɛl pʊʃ / (BrE)
a button mounted on a wall or door that you press to ring a bell, especially at an entrance
button for ringing a bell
british

Examples

She pressed the bell push and waited for someone to answer the door.
The old-fashioned bell push beside the gate had stopped working.
Please use the bell push if the front door is closed when you arrive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorbell - an electric bell installed at an entrance, activated by a button
