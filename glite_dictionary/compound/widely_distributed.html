widely distributed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪdli dɪˈstrɪbjutɪd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdli dɪsˈtrɪbjuːtɪd / (BrE)
growing or occurring in many different areas around the world
found in many parts of the world

Examples

The plant is widely distributed across North America, Europe, and Asia.
This species of bird is widely distributed in tropical and subtropical regions.
The virus became widely distributed following the increase in international travel.

Synset Examples

a cosmopolitan herb
cosmopolitan in distribution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmopolitan - found all over the world or in many places; having a worldwide spread
