self-registering thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf rɛˈdʒɪstərɪŋ θərˈmɑmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf rɪˈdʒɪstərɪŋ θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a thermometer that records readings of temperature automatically, without the need for constant human observation
thermometer that records temperature automatically

Examples

The laboratory installed a self-registering thermometer to monitor temperature changes throughout the night.
A self-registering thermometer provides accurate records without manual intervention.
The weather station relied on a self-registering thermometer for continuous data collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermograph - a device that automatically records temperature readings
