official document

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɪʃəl ˈdɑːkjumənt / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃəl ˈdɒkjumənt / (BrE)
a document that states a contractual relationship or grants a right, often issued by an authority or recognized body
formal document granting rights or relationships
legal

Examples

He needed an official document to prove his citizenship status.
The bank will only accept an official document as proof of address.
Her lawyer prepared an official document outlining the terms of the agreement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certificate - an official document attesting to a fact or qualification
  • Synonym: deed - a legal document concerning property or rights
  • Synonym: contract - a written agreement enforceable by law
