Ivory Coast franc

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪvəri koʊst ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / ˈaɪvəri kəʊst ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in the Ivory Coast, replaced by the CFA franc in 1945.
former currency unit of the Ivory Coast

Examples

Before independence, the Ivory Coast franc was used in all official transactions.
Historical documents often mention prices in Ivory Coast francs before the adoption of the CFA franc.
Collectors sometimes search for old Ivory Coast franc coins at auctions.
