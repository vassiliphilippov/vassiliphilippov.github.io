preterm baby

1. NOUN

/ ˌpriːtɜrm ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˌpriːtɜːm ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
an infant born before 37 weeks of pregnancy have been completed
infant born before 37 weeks gestation
medical

Examples

The hospital has a specialized unit for caring for preterm babies.
Advances in medical technology have greatly improved outcomes for preterm babies.
Parents of a preterm baby often face unique challenges in the first weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premature baby - an infant born before the normal period of gestation

Etymology

From 'preterm' (from Latin 'prae-' meaning 'before' + 'term') and 'baby.'
