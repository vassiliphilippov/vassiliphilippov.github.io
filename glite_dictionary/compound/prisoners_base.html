prisoner's base

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzənərz beɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɪzənəz beɪs / (BrE)
A children's game in which two teams try to capture members of the opposing team by tagging them and bringing them to their own base.
children's game involving team capture

Examples

The children played prisoner's base in the park all afternoon.
When it started to rain, the group reluctantly abandoned their game of prisoner's base.
She remembered playing prisoner's base with her friends every summer during her childhood.
