exotic belly dancer

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɑːtɪk ˈbɛli ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk ˈbɛli ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a woman who performs belly dancing, often emphasizing foreign or sensual style
female performer of belly dance

Examples

The club hired an exotic belly dancer to entertain the guests during the festival.
Crowds gathered in the square to watch the exotic belly dancer perform traditional Middle Eastern routines.
As an exotic belly dancer, she wore vibrant costumes and captivated audiences with her expressive movements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly dancer - a person who performs belly dancing
