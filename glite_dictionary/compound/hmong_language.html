Hmong language

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔŋ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Hmong people, belonging to the Hmong-Mien language family, primarily in Southeast Asia and by diaspora communities
language spoken by the Hmong people

Examples

Many members of the community speak Hmong language as their first language at home.
She is studying the Hmong language to communicate with her grandparents in Laos.
A dictionary of the Hmong language has recently been published to help preserve it.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Hmong - an ethnic group native to Southeast Asia
  • Synonym: Hmong-Mien languages - language family including Hmong spoken in Southeast Asia and China

Etymology

From Hmong, the name of the people, plus English 'language'.
