grass-covered

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræs ˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːs ˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
covered with grass; having a surface largely or entirely covered by grass.
covered with grass

Examples

The hills were grass-covered and shone in the morning light.
They picnicked on a grass-covered field near the river.
Old ruins stood among the grass-covered mounds.

Synset Examples

wide grass-covered plains as far as the eye could see

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grassy - covered with or resembling grass
