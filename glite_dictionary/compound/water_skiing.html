water skiing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌskiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which a person is pulled over water on skis by a motorboat
sport of being pulled on skis by a boat

Examples

He took up water skiing during his summer vacation at the lake.
Competition-level water skiing requires strength, balance, and precise control.
The resort offers lessons for beginners who want to try water skiing for the first time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterski - a ski used for water skiing
