cleaning lady

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɪŋ ˌleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɪŋ ˌleɪdi / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to clean people's houses or offices
woman employed as a cleaner

Examples

The cleaning lady comes every Monday to tidy the house and do the laundry.
After the party, we hired a cleaning lady to help restore order to our apartment.
Their cleaning lady has worked for the family for more than ten years.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaner - person whose job is to clean, regardless of gender
  • Synonym: cleaning man - a man whose job is to clean people's houses or offices
  • Synonym: maid - female domestic worker
