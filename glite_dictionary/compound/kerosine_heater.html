kerosine heater

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrəˌsin ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈkɛrəsiːn ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A portable heater that burns kerosene oil to produce heat for warming spaces or for cooking.
heater powered by kerosene oil

Examples

The old cabin was kept warm by a kerosine heater during the harsh winter nights.
We used a kerosine heater to cook food when the electricity went out.
Always ensure proper ventilation when operating a kerosine heater indoors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil heater - heater that burns oil for heat
