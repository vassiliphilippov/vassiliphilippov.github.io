Chinese scholartree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz ˈskɑːlər ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈskɒlə ˌtriː / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to China and Japan, with compound dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant creamy-white flowers.
tree with fragrant white flowers, native to China/Japan

Examples

The Chinese scholartree blooms in midsummer, filling the garden with a sweet fragrance.
They planted a row of Chinese scholartrees along the avenue for their beautiful white flowers.
The Chinese scholartree is valued in landscape design for its graceful form and dense foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pagodatree - another name for the Chinese scholartree
  • Synonym: Sophora japonica - scientific name of the Chinese scholartree
