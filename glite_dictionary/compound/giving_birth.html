giving birth

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvɪŋ bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ bɜːθ / (BrE)
the process of bringing a baby or young into the world from the body of its mother
the act of having a baby

Examples

Many women choose to have a partner present when giving birth.
She experienced some complications while giving birth to her first child.
Mammals differ from reptiles in that they are capable of giving birth to live young.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the act or process of giving birth to a child
