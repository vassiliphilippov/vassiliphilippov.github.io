black bread

1. NOUN

/ blæk brɛd / (AmE), / blak brɛd / (BrE)
Bread made from coarse rye flour, typically dark in color and with a dense texture.
dark rye flour bread

Examples

In Russia, black bread is often served with soup and butter.
She bought a loaf of black bread from the local bakery for breakfast.
Traditional black bread has a rich, earthy flavor that pairs well with cheese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rye bread - bread made chiefly of rye flour
