common yellowthroat

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑmən ˈjɛloʊˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˌkɒmən ˈjɛləʊˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
a small American warbler with a bright yellow throat, found in marshy or open wet areas across North America
a small North American warbler bird

Examples

A flash of bright yellow revealed a common yellowthroat darting through the reeds by the pond.
We spotted several common yellowthroats during our morning birdwatching walk.
The distinctive song of the common yellowthroat is often heard in wetland habitats throughout spring and summer.
