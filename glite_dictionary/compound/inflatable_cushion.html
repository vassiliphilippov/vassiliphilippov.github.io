inflatable cushion

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfleɪtəbl ˈkʊʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtəbl ˈkʊʃən / (BrE)
A cushion, usually made of rubber or plastic, that can be filled with air for use and deflated for storage.
cushion filled with air for use

Examples

She packed an inflatable cushion for extra comfort on the flight.
The picnic was much more enjoyable thanks to my inflatable cushion.
After the long hike, he rested on an inflatable cushion by the lake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air mattress - an inflatable mattress filled with air
