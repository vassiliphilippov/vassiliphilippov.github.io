flowering spurge

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚɪŋ spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Euphorbia corollata) with showy white petal-like bracts, commonly found in dry fields and prairies
a perennial plant with white bracts

Examples

The flowering spurge brightened the prairie with its delicate white bracts.
Gardeners often plant flowering spurge to attract butterflies to their landscapes.
We found a cluster of flowering spurge along the edge of the old field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Euphorbia - a large genus of plants commonly known as spurges
