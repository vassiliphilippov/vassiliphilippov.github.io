buckthorn family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkˌθɔrn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌθɔːn ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of usually thorny trees and shrubs bearing drupe-like fruit, many of which have medicinal value; the botanical family Rhamnaceae
botanical family of thorny trees and shrubs

Examples

The buckthorn family includes many plants valued for their medicinal properties.
Some species in the buckthorn family are used as ornamental shrubs in gardens.
Researchers have studied the fruits of the buckthorn family for their health benefits.
