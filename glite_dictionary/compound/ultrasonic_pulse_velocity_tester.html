ultrasonic pulse velocity tester

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌltrəˈsɑnɪk pʌls vəˈlɑsəti ˈtɛstər / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɒnɪk pʌls vɪˈlɒsəti ˈtɛstə / (BrE)
a piece of equipment that measures the speed of ultrasonic pulses through concrete to evaluate its strength and quality
device for measuring concrete quality using ultrasound

Examples

The engineer used an ultrasonic pulse velocity tester to assess the strength of the repaired concrete slab.
Modern construction sites often rely on an ultrasonic pulse velocity tester for non-destructive testing of materials.
Before the building inspection, the team calibrated their ultrasonic pulse velocity tester to ensure accurate results.
