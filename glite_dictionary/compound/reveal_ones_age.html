to reveal one's age

1. VERB

/ rɪˈvil wʌnz eɪdʒ / (AmE), / rɪˈviːl wʌnz eɪdʒ / (BrE)
to disclose how old one is, often in situations where people prefer to keep their age private
disclose one's age

Examples

She never reveals her age at parties, preferring to keep it a secret.
It's impolite to ask someone to reveal their age in some cultures.
He joked but refused to reveal his age during the interview.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disclose one's age - to make one's age known to others
