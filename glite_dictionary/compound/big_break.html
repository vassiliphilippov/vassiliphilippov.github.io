big break

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ breɪk / (AmE), / bɪɡ breɪk / (BrE)
an important opportunity or success that marks a significant advancement in one's career or ambitions
major opportunity or success

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally got her big break with a leading role in a major movie.
Many artists wait for a big break but success often comes gradually.
His big break happened when a famous director noticed his performance.

Synset Examples

She finally got her big break in the film industry.
Everyone waits for their big break in life.
His big break came when he won the talent show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opportunity - chance to do something or succeed
  • Synonym: lucky break - a sudden unexpected chance; stroke of good luck
