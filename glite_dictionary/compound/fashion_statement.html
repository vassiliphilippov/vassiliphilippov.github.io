fashion statement

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃən ˌsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈfæʃən ˌsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an item of clothing, accessory, or style that is intended to express a particular trend or personal identity
clothing or style expressing identity

Examples

Her bright red boots made a bold fashion statement at the party.
Wearing mismatched earrings can be quite a unique fashion statement.
Many celebrities use their outfits to make a fashion statement on the red carpet.

Synset Examples

The four-eyed glasses were a fashion statement.
The squaretoes of his shoes were a fashion statement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads fashion trends
