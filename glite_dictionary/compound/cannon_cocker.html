cannon cocker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænən ˈkɑkər / (AmE), / ˈkænən ˈkɒkə / (BrE)
a person who operates a cannon, especially in a military or reenactment context
person who operates a cannon

Examples

During the battle reenactment, the cannon cocker loaded the gunpowder with practiced skill.
Many artillery units rely on an experienced cannon cocker to ensure safe and accurate firing.
The cannon cocker's uniform was covered in soot after a long day of firing exercises.

Synset Examples

The cannon cocker was ready to fire the historic replica.

As a cannon cocker, he had to follow strict safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artilleryman - a soldier who operates large guns
  • Synonym: gunner - a person who operates or fires a gun or cannon

Etymology

From 'cannon', denoting a large gun, and 'cocker', an informal term for one who handles or fires something.
