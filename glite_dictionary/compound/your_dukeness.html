Your Dukeness

1. NOUN

/ jɚr ˈduːk.nəs / (AmE), / jɔː ˈdjuːk.nəs / (BrE)
A humorous or respectful form of address used to refer to or address a person as if they were a duke.
playful/respectful address for a duke

Examples

Everyone laughed when he referred to the guest of honor as Your Dukeness during the speech.
If Your Dukeness requires anything else, please let the staff know immediately.
She curtseyed deeply and greeted him with a mischievous smile, "Good morning, Your Dukeness."

Synset Examples

I must address Your Dukeness with utmost respect.
As Your Dukeness wishes, we shall proceed with the plan.
Thank you, Your Dukeness, for your generous support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Your Highness - formal address for royalty or high nobility

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'Your Highness' and 'duke.'
