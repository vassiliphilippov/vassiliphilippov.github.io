tongue twister

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌŋ ˌtwɪstər / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ ˌtwɪstə / (BrE)
A phrase or expression that is difficult to pronounce clearly, especially because of a sequence of similar sounds.
phrase that's hard to pronounce

Examples

The teacher gave the students a new tongue twister to practice their pronunciation skills.
She struggled to say the tongue twister without stumbling over the words.
Trying to recite a tongue twister quickly is both fun and challenging.

Synset Examples

‘rubber baby buggy bumper’ is a tongue twister

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twister - a word or phrase that twists the tongue; a difficult sequence of sounds
