baking hot

1. ADJ

/ ˈbeɪkɪŋ hɑt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ hɒt / (BrE)
Extremely hot, as if one were in an oven.
extremely hot (like an oven)

Examples

It was baking hot on the beach, so we sought shade under an umbrella.
The kitchen gets baking hot in the afternoon sun.
Everyone complained about the baking hot temperatures during the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scorching - extremely hot
  • Antonym: freezing - extremely cold
