bucket list

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (BrE)
A list of experiences or achievements that a person hopes to accomplish during their lifetime, especially before they die.
things to do before you die

Examples

Visiting the Grand Canyon has always been at the top of my bucket list.
He made a bucket list after his diagnosis and started traveling the world.
Writing a novel was one of the few things still left on her bucket list.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wish list - a list of desired items or experiences

Etymology

From the phrase 'kick the bucket' (to die) + 'list'. Popularized in the early 21st century.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt lɪst / (BrE)
a list of experiences or achievements a person wants to accomplish during their lifetime, especially before they die
list of desired life experiences

Examples

Visiting Antarctica is at the top of my bucket list.
He made a bucket list of things he wants to do after retiring.
Writing a novel is something I hope to check off my bucket list one day.

Synset Examples

She crossed off scuba diving from her bucket list.
Skydiving is on my bucket list for this year.
Living in has-been-dom isn't on her bucket list.
Hiking the Appalachian Trail is on my bucket list.
Traveling to Japan is a must on my bucket list.

Etymology

From the phrase 'kick the bucket' (to die) combined with 'list'; popularized by the 2007 film 'The Bucket List'.
