seafood Newburg

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiˌfud ˈnuːbɝɡ / (AmE), / ˈsiːfuːd ˈnjuːbəːɡ / (BrE)
A dish consisting of seafood served in a rich, creamy Newburg sauce, typically on toast or rice.
seafood in creamy Newburg sauce

Examples

We enjoyed a delicious seafood Newburg at the restaurant last night.
The chef recommended serving seafood Newburg over buttered toast for an authentic experience.
If you love creamy sauces, you should definitely try the seafood Newburg on the menu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lobster Newburg - lobster cooked in Newburg sauce
  • Synonym: Newburg sauce - rich, creamy sauce with butter, cream, eggs, and sherry

Etymology

From 'seafood' + 'Newburg', with 'Newburg' referring to a rich cream sauce named after Lobster Newburg, an American dish from the late 19th century.
