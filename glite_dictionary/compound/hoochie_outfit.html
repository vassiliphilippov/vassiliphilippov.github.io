hoochie outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːtʃi ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈhuːtʃi ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
A set of clothing that is provocative or reveals a lot of skin, often worn to appear sexually attractive.
provocative or revealing clothing
slang american

Examples

She definitely turned heads when she arrived in a hoochie outfit at the club.
His parents disapproved of his sister's hoochie outfit for her birthday party.
The magazine criticized celebrities for wearing hoochie outfits on the red carpet.

Synset Examples

She wore a hoochie outfit to the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provocative clothing - clothing intended to attract attention or arouse sexual interest
  • Antonym: conservative outfit - clothing that covers the body modestly and is not provocative

Etymology

From 'hoochie', American slang for a sexually provocative woman, of unknown origin.
