steel band

1. NOUN

/ stil bænd / (AmE), / stiːl band / (BrE)
A musical group, especially from the Caribbean, that plays music using instruments made from the heads of oil drums (steel pans).
group playing steel pan instruments (Caribbean)

Examples

The steel band performed lively calypso tunes at the festival.
We hired a steel band for the wedding to create a tropical atmosphere.
Children learned to play the pans in their school's steel band.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steel drum - a musical instrument made from a steel oil drum, played especially in steel bands

Etymology

From 'steel', referring to the material of the instruments, and 'band', meaning a musical group.
