luxury liner

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌkʃəri ˈlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʌkʃ(ə)ri ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a large passenger ship equipped with luxurious accommodations and amenities
large, opulent passenger ship

Examples

The Titanic was a famous luxury liner that promised unmatched elegance and comfort at sea.
Many travelers dreamed of cruising the world on a luxury liner during the golden age of transatlantic travel.
The luxury liner offered gourmet dining, a ballroom, and spacious staterooms for its wealthy guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocean liner - a large passenger ship for long voyages
