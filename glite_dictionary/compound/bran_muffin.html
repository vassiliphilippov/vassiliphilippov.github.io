bran muffin

1. NOUN

/ bræn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (AmE), / bræn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (BrE)
a muffin made with bran, usually wheat bran, often considered a healthier baked good due to its high fiber content
muffin made with wheat bran

Examples

Every morning, she enjoys a warm bran muffin with her coffee.
The bakery had several flavors, but the bran muffin was the healthiest option.
He baked a batch of bran muffins to bring to the office breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muffin - a small domed cake, often sweetened and baked in a cup-shaped pan
