breaking wind

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ wɪnd / (BrE)
the act of expelling intestinal gas through the anus, usually making a sound; to fart
expelling intestinal gas (flatulence)

Examples

He tried to hide the fact that he was breaking wind during the meeting.
Some people find breaking wind in public embarrassing, while others think it's funny.
It's normal for the human body to be breaking wind several times a day.
