machine-displayable text

1. NOUN

/ məˈʃin dɪˈspleɪ.ə.bəl tɛkst / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn dɪˈspleɪ.ə.bəl tɛkst / (BrE)
Electronic text stored and presented in a format that can be viewed by a computer or digital device, often as a digital image.
text viewable by a computer

Examples

The scanned contract was saved as machine-displayable text for easier archiving.
Modern libraries often store rare manuscripts as machine-displayable text to preserve their contents digitally.
You can search for keywords within any machine-displayable text using specific software.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electronic text - text encoded for storage or processing by computers
