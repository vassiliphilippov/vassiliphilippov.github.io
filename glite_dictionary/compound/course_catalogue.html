course catalogue

1. NOUN

/ kɔrs ˈkætəlˌɒg / (AmE), / kɔːs ˈkætəlɒg / (BrE)
an official publication listing all the courses offered by a college or university, often with descriptions and requirements
list of courses at a college or university

Examples

You can find the full list of classes in the university’s course catalogue for this semester.
The course catalogue provides detailed information about all available majors and electives.
Before registering, students are encouraged to consult the course catalogue carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: course catalog - alternative spelling (American English) of 'course catalogue'
