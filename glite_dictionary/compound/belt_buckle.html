belt buckle

1. NOUN

/ bɛlt ˈbʌkəl / (AmE), / bɛlt ˈbʌkəl / (BrE)
a flat or curved piece of metal or other material, usually attached to one end of a belt, used to fasten the belt around the waist
a clasp used to fasten a belt

Examples

He polished the silver belt buckle on his uniform before the ceremony.
Her belt buckle was decorated with intricate designs.
The cowboy wore a large, shiny belt buckle engraved with his initials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buckle - a clasp used for fastening two ends, especially of a belt or strap
