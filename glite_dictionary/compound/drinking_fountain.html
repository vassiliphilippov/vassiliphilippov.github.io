drinking fountain

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌfaʊntən / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkɪŋ ˌfaʊntən / (BrE)
a device in a public place that provides a jet of water for people to drink from directly
public fixture for drinking water

Examples

She stopped at the drinking fountain to take a sip of water after her jog.
Children gathered around the drinking fountain in the park on the hot afternoon.
Please do not wash your hands in the drinking fountain—use the bathroom sinks instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water fountain - a fountain designed for drinking water
