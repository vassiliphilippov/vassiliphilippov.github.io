figure eight

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ ˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə ˈeɪt / (BrE)
A two-dimensional shape resembling the numeral eight, formed by two loops joined together.
shape resembling the numeral 8

Examples

The children traced a figure eight in the dirt with a stick.
She skated a perfect figure eight on the ice during her routine.
The racetrack was designed in the shape of a figure eight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eight - the number 8

3. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə eɪt / (BrE)
a knot shaped like the number eight, tied in a rope to prevent it from slipping through a hole or pulley
knot shaped like the numeral eight

Examples

The climber used a figure eight knot to secure his rope before starting the ascent.
Always tie a figure eight to prevent the rope from slipping through the pulley.
She learned how to make a figure eight while practicing knots for her sailing course.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: overhand knot - a simple knot made by passing the end of a rope over itself
