oil geologist

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl dʒiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɔɪl dʒɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a specialist who studies the geology of areas to locate and evaluate oil reserves
specialist in petroleum geology

Examples

The oil geologist discovered a promising new field off the coast of Brazil.
As an oil geologist, her expertise was critical during the drilling project.
Many oil geologists spend months surveying remote regions for underground oil reserves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum geologist - a geologist specializing in the exploration and extraction of petroleum
