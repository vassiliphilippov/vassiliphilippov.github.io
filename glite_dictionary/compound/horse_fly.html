horse fly

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs flaɪ / (AmE), / hɔːs flaɪ / (BrE)
A large, fast-flying fly whose female sucks the blood of various animals, often causing painful bites.
large biting fly

Examples

A horse fly bit me while I was walking in the field.
Farmers often use sprays to keep horse flies away from their livestock.
The painful bite of a horse fly can cause swelling and irritation.
