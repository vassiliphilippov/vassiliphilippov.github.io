smartphone zombie

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɑrtˌfoʊn ˈzɑmbi / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtfəʊn ˈzɒmbi / (BrE)
a person who is so absorbed in using their smartphone that they are oblivious to their surroundings, especially while walking
person distracted by their smartphone
informal

Examples

There are so many smartphone zombies bumping into people on the sidewalk.
The city launched a campaign to warn smartphone zombies about road safety.
Don't be a smartphone zombie when you cross the street.

Synset Examples

He walked into the street like a smartphone zombie.
She felt like a smartphone zombie, glued to her screen.
Smartphone zombies are everywhere at the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phubber - a person who snubs others in favor of their mobile phone
