chicken yard

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkɪn jɑrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn jɑːd / (BrE)
an enclosed area or yard where chickens or other poultry are kept
enclosed area for keeping poultry

Examples

The hens spend most of their day pecking around the chicken yard.
We built a fence to keep predators out of the chicken yard.
Children love to watch the chicks play in the chicken yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken run - an enclosed outdoor area where chickens can move about freely
