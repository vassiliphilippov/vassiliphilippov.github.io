LGBTQ+ community

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛlˌdʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˈkjuː ˈkəmjunəti / (AmE), / ˌɛlˌdʒiːˌbiːˌtiːˈkjuː ˈkɒmjʊnəti / (BrE)
the collective group of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other sexual or gender minority individuals
group of LGBTQ+ people

Examples

Many members of the LGBTQ+ community celebrated the Supreme Court's decision on same-sex marriage.
The festival aims to provide a safe space for the LGBTQ+ community to express themselves freely.
Healthcare providers are working to address the unique needs of the LGBTQ+ community.

Synset Examples

The term faggot was reclaimed by the LGBTQ+ community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay community - the community of people who identify as gay

Etymology

Acronym formed from lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer (or questioning), and the plus sign for inclusivity.
