garbage pickup

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˈpɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˈpɪkʌp / (BrE)
the service or process of collecting and removing household or commercial waste from a location
collection and removal of waste
american

Examples

The city schedules garbage pickup twice a week for all residential areas.
Due to the holiday, garbage pickup has been delayed by one day.
Please place your bins at the curb before garbage pickup in the morning.
