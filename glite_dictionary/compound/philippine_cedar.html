Philippine cedar

1. NOUN

/ fɪˈlɪpɪn ˈsiːdər / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪpɪn ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a Philippine timber tree with hard, red, fragrant wood, used in construction and furniture-making
a Philippine timber tree with fragrant, red wood

Examples

The wood from the Philippine cedar is prized for its durability and fragrance.
Our dining table was handcrafted from solid Philippine cedar.
Illegal logging has threatened the survival of the Philippine cedar in some areas.
