midsummer-men

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪdˌsʌmɚˈmɛn / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˌsʌməˈmɛn / (BrE)
A Eurasian mountain plant with fleshy pink-tipped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers, also known for its ornamental value.
Eurasian mountain plant with yellow flowers

Examples

In the garden, a patch of midsummer-men bloomed brightly among the rocks.
Hikers admired the unique foliage and yellow blossoms of the midsummer-men along the mountain trail.
Botanists collect seeds from midsummer-men to help conserve rare alpine species.
