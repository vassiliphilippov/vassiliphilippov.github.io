fitted sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt / (BrE)
A bed sheet with elastic edges designed to fit snugly over a mattress.
sheet with elastic edges for a mattress

Examples

The new set came with one fitted sheet and two pillowcases.
Changing the fitted sheet on a large mattress can be tricky.
She prefers a fitted sheet because it stays in place better than a flat sheet.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flat sheet - a rectangular sheet without elastic placed over the fitted sheet
