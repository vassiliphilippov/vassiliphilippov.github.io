sports jacket

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts ˈdʒækət / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A lightweight, single-breasted jacket, often in bold patterns or colors, typically worn as informal or smart casual attire.
lightweight informal jacket

Examples

He wore a navy blue sports jacket to the dinner party.
The college's traditional sports jacket had red and yellow stripes.
In cooler weather, she prefers a sports jacket over a sweater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazer - a type of smart jacket similar to a sports jacket, often part of a uniform
  • Degree Lower: jacket - a short coat typically extending to the hips
