faculty member

1. NOUN

/ ˈfækəlti ˌmɛmbər / (AmE), / ˈfækəlti ˌmɛmbə / (BrE)
an educator who is part of the academic staff at a college or university
university or college academic staff member

Examples

Every faculty member is required to attend the staff meeting on Monday.
As a new faculty member, she quickly became involved in campus life.
The university honored one faculty member from each department for outstanding teaching.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professor - a senior academic at a college or university
  • Synonym: lecturer - an academic who teaches at a college or university
  • Antonym: student - a person enrolled in a college or university for study
