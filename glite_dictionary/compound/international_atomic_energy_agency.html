International Atomic Energy Agency

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪntərˈnæʃənl əˈtɑmɪk ˈɛnərdʒi ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənl əˈtɒmɪk ˈɛnədʒi ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
an international organization under the United Nations that promotes safe, secure, and peaceful use of atomic energy
UN agency for atomic energy regulation

Examples

The International Atomic Energy Agency monitors nuclear facilities worldwide to ensure compliance with international regulations.
In 2005, the International Atomic Energy Agency and its director were awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
Delegates from over 170 countries attended the International Atomic Energy Agency conference on nuclear security.
