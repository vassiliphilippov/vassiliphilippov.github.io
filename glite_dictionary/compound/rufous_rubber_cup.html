rufous rubber cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfəs ˈrʌbər kʌp / (AmE), / ˈruːfəs ˈrʌbə kʌp / (BrE)
a small, cup-shaped fungus of the family Sarcosomataceae (Galiella rufa), typically reddish-brown in color and found on decaying wood.
a reddish-brown cup-shaped fungus

Examples

We spotted a rufous rubber cup growing on the fallen log in the forest.
The rufous rubber cup is easily recognized by its reddish-brown, rubbery appearance.
Fungi enthusiasts enjoy searching for the elusive rufous rubber cup during autumn walks.
