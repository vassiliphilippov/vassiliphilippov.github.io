spenno budget

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛnoʊ ˈbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɛnəʊ ˈbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
an extremely limited or strict budget, allowing for minimal expenditure
very tight or limited budget
informal slang

Examples

They managed to throw a party on a spenno budget, using only homemade decorations and snacks.
After losing his job, he had to live according to a spenno budget for several months.
Traveling abroad on a spenno budget requires careful planning and lots of research.

Synset Examples

Living on a spenno budget can be challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoestring budget - a very small amount of money available for spending
  • Synonym: tight budget - a limited or restricted amount of money to spend

Etymology

From Australian slang 'spenno' meaning 'having little money'; 'budget' from Middle French 'bougette' (small bag).
