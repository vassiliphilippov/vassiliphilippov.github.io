Chinese pea tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪ.niːz piː triː / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz piː triː / (BrE)
A shrub native to northern China, having dark-green glossy leaves and solitary pale yellow flowers.
shrub native to northern China

Examples

The Chinese pea tree thrives in well-drained soil and sunny locations.
We planted a Chinese pea tree in the botanical garden last spring.
The glossy foliage of the Chinese pea tree adds beauty to ornamental landscapes.
