telephone service

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a public utility that provides facilities and connections for people to make and receive telephone calls
public utility for phone calls

Examples

The city's telephone service was interrupted by the strong storm last night.
Many rural areas still lack access to reliable telephone service.
After moving to a new apartment, she quickly arranged for telephone service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telecommunications - the transmission of information over distances for communication
  • Synonym: telephone company - a business that provides telephone services to customers
