sales campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlz kæmˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈseɪlz kæmˌpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized series of advertising or promotional activities designed to increase sales of a product or service
advertising effort to boost sales

Examples

The company launched a new sales campaign to introduce its latest smartphone model.
After reviewing last quarter’s results, the team decided to revamp their sales campaign.
A successful sales campaign can dramatically increase a brand’s market share.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - a series of advertisements with a particular theme
  • Synonym: marketing campaign - a coordinated set of marketing activities
