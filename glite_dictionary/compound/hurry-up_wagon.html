hurry-up wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɜriˈʌp ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ˌhʌriˈʌp ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
A rapid or urgent approach to completing a task or activity.
rapid/urgent approach or method

Examples

With the deadline looming, we had no choice but to hop on the hurry-up wagon.
Management wants everyone on the hurry-up wagon until the project is finished.
After weeks of procrastination, she finally jumped on the hurry-up wagon and completed her report.

Synset Examples

She realized she had to join the hurry-up wagon to finish on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush - to move or act with great speed
  • Antonym: to dawdle - to move slowly and waste time
