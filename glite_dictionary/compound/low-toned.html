low-toned

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ toʊnd / (AmE), / ˈləʊ təʊnd / (BrE)
(of a sound or voice) very low in volume; quiet or subdued in tone.
very low in volume

Examples

The low-toned conversation in the library was barely audible from across the room.
She spoke in a low-toned whisper so as not to wake the baby.
The low-toned music created a calm and relaxing atmosphere at the café.

Synset Examples

a low murmur
the low-toned murmur of the surf

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft - (of sound) quiet, not loud
  • Antonym: loud - making or able to make much noise
