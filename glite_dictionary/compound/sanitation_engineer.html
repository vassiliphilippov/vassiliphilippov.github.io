sanitation engineer

1. NOUN

/ ˌsænəˈteɪʃən ˌendʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌsænɪˈteɪʃən ˌendʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
an engineer specializing in designing and implementing public health and hygiene solutions, such as waste management and sanitation systems
engineer specializing in sanitation and public health

Examples

The city hired a sanitation engineer to oversee improvements to its waste disposal system.
As a sanitation engineer, he designs facilities that promote public health and hygiene.
Many sanitation engineers work closely with environmental agencies on water treatment projects.

Synset Examples

The sanitation engineer was praised for her innovative solutions.
The sanitation engineer ensures the city's waste is managed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmental engineer - engineer focusing on improving environmental quality
