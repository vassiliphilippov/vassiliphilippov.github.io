Directorate for Inter-Services Intelligence

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈrɛktɚət fɔr ˌɪntɚˈsɜrvəs ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktərət fɔː ˌɪntəˈsɜːvɪs ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns / (BrE)
the principal intelligence agency of Pakistan, involved in political and military operations and widely known for its influence within the country and involvement in regional affairs.
Pakistan's main intelligence agency

Examples

The Directorate for Inter-Services Intelligence is often cited for its significant influence in Pakistan's domestic affairs.
International media have reported alleged links between the Directorate for Inter-Services Intelligence and various political groups.
The Directorate for Inter-Services Intelligence is frequently abbreviated as ISI in global security analyses.
