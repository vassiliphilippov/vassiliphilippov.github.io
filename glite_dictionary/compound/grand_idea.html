grand idea

1. NOUN

/ ɡrænd aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ɡrænd aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an ambitious or impressive concept, often involving bold plans or aspirations
ambitious or impressive concept

Examples

The company invested millions in his grand idea to revolutionize urban transportation.
Although her grand idea sounded amazing, it was difficult to put into practice.
Many inventions begin as a grand idea before they are refined into reality.

Synset Examples

His speech was filled with grand ideas and visions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big idea - a significant or important concept or plan
  • Antonym: modest idea - a less ambitious or unassuming concept or plan
