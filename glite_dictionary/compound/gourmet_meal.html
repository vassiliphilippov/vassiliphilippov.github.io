gourmet meal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʊr.meɪ ˈmiːl / (AmE), / ˈɡɔː.meɪ ˈmiːl / (BrE)
a meal prepared with high-quality ingredients and exceptional skill, often reflecting sophistication and culinary artistry
high-quality, sophisticated meal

Examples

The chef surprised everyone with a stunning gourmet meal featuring locally sourced ingredients.
They celebrated their anniversary by enjoying a five-course gourmet meal at an exclusive restaurant.
Even accomplished cooks can find preparing a true gourmet meal to be a rewarding challenge.

Synset Examples

She prepared a gourmet meal for the special occasion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fine dining - eating at high-quality, elegant restaurants
  • Antonym: simple meal - a basic or plain meal without sophistication

Etymology

From French 'gourmet' (expert in food or wine) + English 'meal'.
