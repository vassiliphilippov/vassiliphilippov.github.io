cabinet minister

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪnət ˈmɪnɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnət ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a senior government official who is a member of the cabinet and responsible for leading a specific government department or portfolio
senior government official in a cabinet

Examples

The Prime Minister consulted the cabinet minister on the proposed policy changes.
Several cabinet ministers resigned over the controversial decision.
As a cabinet minister, she is responsible for national education policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minister - a government official in charge of a department

2. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪnət ˈmɪnɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæbɪnɪt ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a member of the cabinet in a government, typically responsible for leading a government department
senior government official in cabinet

Examples

The cabinet minister announced several new policy changes during the press conference.
She became the youngest cabinet minister in the country's history in 2015.
Each cabinet minister is responsible for a different government department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minister - a senior member of government in charge of a department
  • Synonym: cabinet - the group of most important government ministers
