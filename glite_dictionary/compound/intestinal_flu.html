intestinal flu

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl fluː / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl fluː / (BrE)
A viral or bacterial infection causing inflammation of the stomach and intestines, often resulting in vomiting and diarrhea.
infection of stomach and intestines

Examples

The doctor diagnosed his illness as intestinal flu after he developed severe stomach cramps and vomiting.
During the outbreak, many children missed school due to intestinal flu.
She avoided eating out for a month after catching intestinal flu on holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastroenteritis - inflammation of the stomach and intestines
