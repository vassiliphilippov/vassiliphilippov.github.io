God tree

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑd triː / (AmE), / ɡɒd triː / (BrE)
a large tropical tree with a massive, ridged trunk and large seed pods covered in silky floss; the source of kapok fiber
large tropical tree; kapok source

Examples

The God tree provides the silky kapok fiber used for stuffing pillows and mattresses.
In the rainforest, a single God tree can tower above the surrounding canopy.
Many birds nest in the high branches of the God tree, taking advantage of its vast size.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kapok tree - tropical tree producing kapok fiber
