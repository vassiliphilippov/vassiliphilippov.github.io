South Korea

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ kəˈriːə / (AmE), / saʊθ kəˈriːə / (BrE)
a country in the southern part of the Korean Peninsula, officially known as the Republic of Korea, established in 1948
country in East Asia, south of North Korea

Examples

Many students dream of traveling to South Korea to experience its culture and cuisine.
The economy of South Korea has grown rapidly over the past decades.
After the war, South Korea emerged as a separate nation from its northern neighbor.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: North Korea - a country in the northern part of the Korean Peninsula
