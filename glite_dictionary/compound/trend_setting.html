trend setting

1. NOUN

/ trɛnd ˌsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / trɛnd ˌsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of initiating or popularizing a new trend or fashion
initiating or popularizing a trend or fashion

Examples

Her innovative designs played a major role in trend setting within the fashion industry.
The company's focus on trend setting technology gave it an edge over competitors.
Social media influencers are often credited with trend setting in lifestyle and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trendsetter - a person who leads the way in fashion or ideas
