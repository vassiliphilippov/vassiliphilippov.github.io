dog show

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ʃoʊ / (AmE), / dɒɡ ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a competitive event in which dogs are judged for conformity to breed standards and other qualities
competitive exhibition of dogs

Examples

She spent months preparing her poodle for the dog show in hopes of winning a prize.
The dog show attracted breeders from all over the country this year.
At the dog show, judges evaluated each breed based on specific standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat show - competitive exhibition of cats
