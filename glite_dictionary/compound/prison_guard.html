prison guard

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzən ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈprɪzən ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a person whose job is to supervise and guard prisoners in a jail or prison
person who guards prisoners in a prison

Examples

The prison guard checked each cell carefully during his nightly rounds.
After the riot, every prison guard was put on high alert.
She became a prison guard after attending the training academy last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard - a person who keeps watch or protects something
  • Synonym: warden - the head official in charge of a prison
