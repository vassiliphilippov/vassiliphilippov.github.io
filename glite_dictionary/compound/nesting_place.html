nesting place

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place that is suitable or used for animals to build their nest and lay eggs
a place suitable for nesting

Examples

The old oak tree has long been a favorite nesting place for local birds.
Conservationists are working to protect the turtles' nesting place along the coast.
They identified a secluded cliff as the perfect nesting place for the endangered falcons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure built by a bird or other animal to hold its eggs or young
