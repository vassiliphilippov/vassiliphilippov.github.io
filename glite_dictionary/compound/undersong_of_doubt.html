undersong of doubt

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌsɔŋ əv daʊt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəsɒŋ əv daʊt / (BrE)
a subtle and persistent feeling of uncertainty that exists in the background of one's thoughts or emotions
a subtle underlying feeling of uncertainty

Examples

Despite her confident speech, an undersong of doubt ran beneath her words.
There was an undersong of doubt in his mind as he made the decision.
His optimism was genuine, but an undersong of doubt colored his thoughts throughout the day.

Synset Examples

Her undersong of doubt lingered in the back of my mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doubt - a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction

Etymology

Formed from 'undersong' (meaning underlying melody or theme) and 'doubt'.
