pen-tailed tree shrew

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛn ˌteɪld ˈtriː ˌʃruː / (AmE), / ˈpɛn ˌteɪld ˈtriː ˌʃruː / (BrE)
a small brown tree shrew native to Malaysia, characterized by a naked tail with long stiff hairs along the distal third
Malaysian tree shrew with a distinctive tail

Examples

The pen-tailed tree shrew has a unique tail that distinguishes it from other small mammals in Malaysia.
Researchers observed the nocturnal behavior of a pen-tailed tree shrew in its natural habitat.
Unlike many rodents, the pen-tailed tree shrew includes nectar in its regular diet.
