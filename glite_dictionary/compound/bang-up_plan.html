bang-up plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋ.ʌp plæn / (AmE), / ˈbaŋ.ʌp plan / (BrE)
an excellent or impressive plan
excellent or impressive plan
informal american

Examples

That was a bang-up plan you came up with to solve the problem.
With his bang-up plan, the team finished the project ahead of schedule.
We need a bang-up plan if we want to impress the investors.

Synset Examples

Everyone agreed it was a bang-up plan for the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliant plan - a very clever or impressive plan
  • Antonym: terrible plan - a very bad or ill-conceived plan

Etymology

The adjective 'bang-up' meaning 'excellent' is originally American slang from the 19th century.
