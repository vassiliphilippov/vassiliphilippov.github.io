hedge maple

1. NOUN

/ hɛdʒ ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / hɛdʒ ˈmeɪpl / (BrE)
a small Eurasian maple (Acer campestre) often grown as a shrub or for hedging purposes
a small Eurasian maple shrub used for hedges

Examples

The hedge maple is popular in gardens for its ability to form dense, attractive hedges.
She planted a row of hedge maples along the driveway to provide privacy.
The leaves of the hedge maple turn golden yellow in the autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maple - a tree or shrub of the genus Acer, typically having lobed leaves
