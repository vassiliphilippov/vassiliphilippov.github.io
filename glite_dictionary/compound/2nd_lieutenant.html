2nd lieutenant

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd luːˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd lɛfˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
a commissioned officer of the lowest rank in the Army, Air Force, or Marine Corps
lowest commissioned officer rank

Examples

After completing training, she was commissioned as a 2nd lieutenant in the Air Force.
The 2nd lieutenant was assigned to lead a new platoon.
Many officers begin their military careers as 2nd lieutenants.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: first lieutenant - commissioned officer immediately above '2nd lieutenant'

Etymology

'Lieutenant' from French 'lieu tenant' (place holder); 'second' indicates rank.
