Hungarian partridge

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋˈɡɛriən ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛːrɪən ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ / (BrE)
a common European game bird, the partridge (Perdix perdix), introduced to North America for sport hunting
a species of European game bird

Examples

Hunters often seek the Hungarian partridge because of its swift flight and elusive behavior.
The Hungarian partridge population has increased in several North American regions due to conservation efforts.
I spotted a covey of Hungarian partridges in the grasslands early this morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partridge - a medium-sized game bird
