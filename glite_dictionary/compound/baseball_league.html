baseball league

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl liːɡ / (BrE)
an organized association of baseball teams that compete against each other in a series of scheduled games
group of competing baseball teams

Examples

My son just joined a local baseball league for kids aged 8 to 12.
The National Baseball League announced new rules for the upcoming season.
Several new teams will enter the city's baseball league next year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: league - an organized group of sports teams that compete against each other
