weather sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðɚ ʃit / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ʃiːt / (BrE)
(Nautical) A rope or chain used to regulate the angle at which a sail is set in relation to the wind.
nautical rope adjusting sail angle

Examples

The crew quickly tightened the weather sheet to keep the sail steady during the storm.
When tacking, always check that the weather sheet is properly secured.
Old sailing ships required a strong weather sheet to maintain course in rough winds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheet - A rope or chain attached to the lower corner of a sail for controlling its position
