butt floss

1. NOUN

/ bʌt flɔs / (AmE), / bʌt flɒs / (BrE)
(slang, humorous) A very revealing swimsuit, shorts, or underwear, especially one that consists of a thin strip of cloth passing between the buttocks.
revealing swimwear or underwear (humorous, slang)
slang informal

Examples

He joked that his new swimsuit was nothing more than butt floss.
The dancer's costume was so minimal it was practically butt floss.
She confidently wore her butt floss bikini at the pool party.
