cup of coffee

1. NOUN

/ kʌp əv ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / kʌp əv ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
a serving of coffee, typically in a cup, often enjoyed as a beverage
serving of coffee in a cup

Examples

He always starts his morning with a cup of coffee and the newspaper.
Do you want to join me for a cup of coffee after lunch?
Nothing beats a hot cup of coffee on a cold day.

Synset Examples

I need a cup of coffee to start my day.
She enjoyed a cup of coffee while reading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a drink made from roasted coffee beans
