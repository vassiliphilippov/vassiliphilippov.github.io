giant granadilla

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˌɡrænəˈdɪlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˌɡranəˈdɪlə / (BrE)
a tropical American passionflower (Passiflora quadrangularis) that yields a large, edible fruit
tropical passionfruit plant and its large fruit

Examples

The giant granadilla is prized for its enormous, sweet-scented fruit.
After tasting the giant granadilla, she decided to plant one in her garden.
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate the giant granadilla for both its flowers and fruit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: granadilla - a passionfruit, especially the fruit of Passiflora species
