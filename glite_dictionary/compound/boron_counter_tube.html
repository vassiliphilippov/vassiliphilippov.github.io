boron counter tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔːrɑn ˈkaʊntər tuːb / (AmE), / ˈbɒrən ˈkaʊntə tjuːb / (BrE)
a detector tube filled with boron used for counting neutrons, especially in nuclear experiments
neutron-counting detector tube with boron

Examples

The laboratory installed a boron counter tube to monitor neutron emissions from the reactor.
Accurate measurements were obtained using a boron counter tube during the experiment.
A boron counter tube is essential equipment for detecting low levels of neutron radiation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proportional counter - a type of gas-filled radiation detector
