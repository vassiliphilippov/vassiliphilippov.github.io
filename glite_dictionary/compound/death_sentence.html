death sentence

1. NOUN

/ dɛθ ˈsɛntəns / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈsɛntəns / (BrE)
an official punishment in which a person is sentenced by a court to be put to death for a crime
a legal sentence of execution
legal

Examples

The court handed down a death sentence for the convicted murderer.
Many countries have abolished the death sentence due to concerns about human rights.
The possibility of a death sentence caused widespread debate among legal experts.

Synset Examples

The jury delivered a death sentence after the trial concluded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capital punishment - the legally authorized killing of someone as punishment for a crime
  • Antonym: life sentence - a punishment of a convicted person to remain in prison for life
