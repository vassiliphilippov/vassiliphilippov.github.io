in retreat

1. ADJ

/ ɪn rɪˈtrit / (AmE), / ɪn rɪˈtriːt / (BrE)
withdrawing from a position or moving back, especially after a defeat or setback
withdrawing; moving back

Examples

After facing strong opposition, the protestors were in retreat by nightfall.
Sales figures show the company is in retreat in several key markets.
The troops were visibly in retreat as they abandoned their positions.

Synset Examples

The army was in retreat after the surprise attack.
The company is in retreat after the poor sales report.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: advance - a forward movement; especially in military operations
