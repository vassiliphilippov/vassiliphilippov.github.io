evil-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈiː.vəl ˌlʊk.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiː.vəl ˌlʊk.ɪŋ / (BrE)
having an appearance that suggests evil or malice
having an appearance suggesting evil

Examples

The dog was hunched in the corner, its evil-looking eyes fixed on the strangers.
She avoided the evil-looking man in the alley late at night.
An evil-looking storm cloud rolled across the sky, blocking out the sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sinister - giving the impression of something harmful or evil
  • Antonym: innocent-looking - having an appearance that suggests innocence
