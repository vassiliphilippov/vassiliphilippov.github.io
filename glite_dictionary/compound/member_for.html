member for

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛmbɚ fɔr / (AmE), / ˈmɛmbə fə / (BrE)
a person elected to represent a particular area in a legislative body, especially in Commonwealth countries
elected legislative representative for a specific area

Examples

The member for Newtown voiced concerns about the proposed legislation.
As member for her district, she attended every council meeting.
The member for Easton submitted a petition signed by hundreds of residents.

Synset Examples

As member for Barkshire, she fought for local businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: representative - person chosen to act or speak for others
