no doubt

1. ADV

/ noʊ ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something is certainly true or very likely, often to concede a point before adding a contrasting opinion.
certainly; admittedly; without question

Examples

No doubt, this report will cause some controversy among the experts.
No doubt, she's a talented musician, but her live performances need improvement.
He will, no doubt, want to discuss the matter with you soon.

Synset Examples

to be sure, he is no Einstein

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; definitely
  • Synonym: doubtless - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: perhaps - possibly but not definitely
