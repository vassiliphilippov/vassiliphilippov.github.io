watch case

1. NOUN

/ wɑtʃ keɪs / (AmE), / wɒtʃ keɪs / (BrE)
the metal or plastic outer shell that encloses and protects the internal mechanism of a watch
outer shell of a watch

Examples

He replaced the scratched watch case with a new one to restore the timepiece's appearance.
The watch case is made of stainless steel to protect the delicate inner workings.
Collectors often look for vintage watches with their original watch case intact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bezel - the ring surrounding the crystal of a watch or clock
