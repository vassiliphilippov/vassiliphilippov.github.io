insulating tape

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnsəˌleɪtɪŋ teɪp / (AmE), / ˈɪnsjʊleɪtɪŋ teɪp / (BrE)
a water-resistant adhesive tape used to insulate exposed electrical conductors
tape for insulating electrical wires

Examples

Always use insulating tape to cover bare wires after making a connection.
He repaired the damaged cord with a piece of insulating tape.
Make sure the insulating tape is wrapped tightly to prevent electrical hazards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrical tape - adhesive tape used for insulating electrical wires
