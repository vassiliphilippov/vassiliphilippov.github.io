to flush out toxins

1. VERB

/ flʌʃ aʊt ˈtɑksɪnz / (AmE), / flʌʃ aʊt ˈtɒksɪnz / (BrE)
to remove harmful substances from the body, typically through increased fluid intake or detoxification processes
remove harmful substances from the body

Examples

Nutritionists recommend drinking plenty of water to flush out toxins from your system.
Some people believe herbal teas can help flush out toxins naturally.
Regular exercise and hydration are said to flush out toxins and improve overall health.

Synset Examples

Drinking water helps flush out toxins from the body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to detoxify - to remove toxic substances or qualities
  • Antonym: accumulate toxins - to collect or build up harmful substances in the body
