faint footstep

1. NOUN

/ feɪnt ˈfʊtˌstɛp / (AmE), / feɪnt ˈfʊt.stɛp / (BrE)
a quiet or soft sound made by a person's step while walking
quiet or soft sound of a step

Examples

A faint footstep alerted her to someone approaching from behind.
In the darkness, only the faint footsteps could be heard as he crept down the hall.
She paused, listening for any faint footstep in the empty corridor.

Synset Examples

I heard a faint footstep behind me in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footstep - the sound or mark made by a foot when walking
