well-tried

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈtraɪd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈtraɪd / (BrE)
(of a method, system, etc.) thoroughly tested and proven to be effective or reliable.
thoroughly tested and proven

We used a well-tried recipe that has never failed us.
Her well-tried methods brought consistent success.
