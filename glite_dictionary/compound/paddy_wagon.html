paddy wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈpædi ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈpædi ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
a police van used to transport prisoners
police van for transporting prisoners
informal

Examples

The suspects were handcuffed and loaded into the paddy wagon after the raid.
After the protest, several people were taken away in a paddy wagon.
The old paddy wagon rattled as it drove through the city streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police van - a van used by police, especially for transporting prisoners

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from "Paddy," a slang term for an Irish person, reflecting the large number of Irish police officers in 19th-century America, and "wagon" for a vehicle.
