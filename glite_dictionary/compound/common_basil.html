common basil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈbæzəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈbæzɪl / (BrE)
A tropical Asian herb with aromatic leaves and small white flowers, widely used as a culinary herb in salads, sauces, and other dishes.
aromatic herb used in cooking

Examples

Fresh common basil leaves add a fragrant flavor to tomato sauce.
He grows common basil in his kitchen garden for cooking.
Many Mediterranean dishes call for common basil as a key ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basil - a culinary herb with aromatic leaves
