name dropper

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪm ˌdrɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈneɪm ˌdrɒpə / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be familiar with famous or important people, often to impress others
person who pretends to know famous people

Examples

Everyone knows Tom is a name dropper who loves to mention celebrities in every conversation.
She annoyed her colleagues by being a constant name dropper at office parties.
You don't have to be a name dropper to make a good impression.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • Antonym: modest person - a person who does not boast or seek attention
