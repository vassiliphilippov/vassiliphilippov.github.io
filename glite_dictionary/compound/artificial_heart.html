artificial heart

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːɹtɪˈfɪʃəl hɑɹt / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃl hɑːt / (BrE)
a mechanical device that is implanted to replace the function of a natural human heart
mechanical heart replacement device
medical

Examples

After suffering from severe heart disease, she received an artificial heart during surgery.
Scientists continue to improve artificial heart technology to increase patient survival rates.
The patient's artificial heart kept him alive while he waited for a transplant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pacemaker - an electronic device that helps regulate heart rhythm
