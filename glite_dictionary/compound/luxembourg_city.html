Luxembourg City

1. PROPN

/ ˈlʌksəmˌbɜːrɡ ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈlʌksəmbɜːɡ ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Luxembourg, situated in the southern part of the country
capital city of Luxembourg

Examples

Many international organizations have their headquarters in Luxembourg City.
The old quarters and fortifications of Luxembourg City are UNESCO World Heritage sites.
Tourists are often impressed by the dramatic cliffs surrounding Luxembourg City.

Etymology

Named after the country Luxembourg, from Latin 'Lucilinburhuc' (little fortress).
