speed cop

1. NOUN

/ spiːd kɑp / (AmE), / spiːd kɒp / (BrE)
a police officer who patrols roads, often on a motorcycle, to monitor and enforce speed limits
policeman who enforces traffic speed limits

Examples

The speed cop signaled for the driver to pull over after clocking him above the speed limit.
Local speed cops have increased patrols on the highway during the holiday weekend.
She slowed down immediately when she spotted a speed cop ahead on his motorcycle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic officer - police officer responsible for road safety and traffic regulation
