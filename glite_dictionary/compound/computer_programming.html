computer programming

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjutɚ ˈproʊɡræmɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˌpjuːtə ˈprəʊɡramɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating sequences of instructions for a computer to perform specific tasks
writing instructions for computers

Examples

She decided to take a class in computer programming to learn how to build her own applications.
Many jobs in the technology industry require knowledge of computer programming.
Computer programming can be both challenging and rewarding, especially when solving complex problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coding - the act of writing code for computers
  • Synonym: software development - the overall process of designing, creating, and maintaining software
