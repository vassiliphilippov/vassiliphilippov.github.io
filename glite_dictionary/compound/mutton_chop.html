mutton chop

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtən tʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈmʌtən tʃɒp / (BrE)
a chop cut from the meat of a mature sheep, especially as prepared for cooking
a chop from a mature sheep

Examples

He ordered a mutton chop with mashed potatoes for dinner.
The chef grilled the mutton chop to perfection, adding rosemary for extra flavor.
Martha prefers lamb but sometimes enjoys a well-cooked mutton chop.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lamb chop - a chop cut from a young sheep (lamb)
