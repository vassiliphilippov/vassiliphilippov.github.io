civil order

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪvəl ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
the system of rules and organization by which a society is governed
system by which a society is governed

Examples

The collapse of the civil order led to widespread unrest in the region.
A stable civil order is necessary for economic development.
Throughout history, philosophers have debated the best form of civil order for a just society.
