connecting room

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛktɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a hotel room that is directly linked to an adjoining room by a private interior door, allowing access between the two rooms.
hotel room with an interior door to another room

Examples

We requested a connecting room so that our children could stay next door and still have easy access.
The hotel offered us a connecting room to make our family vacation more convenient.
If you need extra space, you can book a suite with a connecting room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjoining room - a hotel room adjacent to another but not necessarily connected by a door
