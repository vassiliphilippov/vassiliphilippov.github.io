fresh manicure

1. NOUN

/ frɛʃ ˌmænɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˌmanɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a manicure that has been done very recently, leaving the nails looking newly groomed and polished
recently done nail treatment

Examples

She showed off her fresh manicure at the party last night.
A fresh manicure can make your hands look elegant and well-groomed.
After a stressful week, getting a fresh manicure was exactly what she needed.

Synset Examples

A fresh manicure always boosts her confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manicure - a cosmetic treatment for the hands and fingernails
