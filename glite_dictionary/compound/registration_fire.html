registration fire

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən faɪr / (AmE), / ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən faɪə / (BrE)
artillery fire delivered to obtain accurate data for subsequent effective engagement of targets
artillery fire to gather targeting data

Examples

The artillery unit used registration fire to ensure their subsequent barrage would hit the intended target.
Before the main attack, commanders ordered registration fire to calibrate their guns.
Accurate registration fire is crucial for successful battlefield operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranging fire - artillery fire to determine range and accuracy
