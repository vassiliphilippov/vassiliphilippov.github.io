to scrub the kitchen

1. VERB

/ skrʌb ðə ˈkɪtʃən / (AmE), / skrʌb ðə ˈkɪtʃɪn / (BrE)
to clean the kitchen thoroughly by scrubbing surfaces to remove dirt or stains
thoroughly clean the kitchen

Examples

After cooking dinner, she always scrubs the kitchen to keep it spotless.
We spent the afternoon scrubbing the kitchen from top to bottom.
Before the inspection, they had to scrub the kitchen thoroughly.

Synset Examples

I need to scrub the kitchen before guests arrive.
