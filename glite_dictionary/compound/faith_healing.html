faith healing

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪθ ˌhiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθ ˌhiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of curing illnesses through prayer, faith, or spiritual means rather than medical treatment
curing illness by prayer or faith

Examples

She believes strongly in faith healing and attends special services for prayer and recovery.
Some people turn to faith healing when conventional medicine offers little hope.
The documentary explored stories of faith healing in different cultures around the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: medicine - the science or practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing illness
