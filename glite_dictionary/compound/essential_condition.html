essential condition

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəl kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃəl kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a necessary requirement or prerequisite that must be met for something else to occur or exist
a necessary requirement; prerequisite

Examples

Trust is an essential condition for any successful relationship.
Having a valid passport is an essential condition for international travel.
Health and safety are an essential condition for working in this factory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prerequisite - something required as a prior condition
