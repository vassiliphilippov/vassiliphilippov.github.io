man's clothing

1. NOUN

/ mænz ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / manz ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
clothing designed specifically for men to wear.
clothing for men

Examples

The store offers a wide selection of man's clothing for both formal and casual occasions.
He decided to donate all his old man's clothing to charity.
Fashion magazines often feature the latest trends in man's clothing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menswear - clothing designed for men
  • Antonym: women's clothing - clothing designed for women
