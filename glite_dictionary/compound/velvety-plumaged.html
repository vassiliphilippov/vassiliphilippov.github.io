velvety-plumaged

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛl.vɪ.ti ˈplʌ.mɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈvɛl.vɪ.ti ˈplʌ.mɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of a bird) having plumage that is soft and smooth in appearance or texture, like velvet.
having plumage like velvet

Examples

The rare parrot, velvety-plumaged and bright, attracts attention from birdwatchers worldwide.
She marveled at the velvety-plumaged finch perched delicately on the branch.
Among the flock, the velvety-plumaged bird stood out for its deep, lustrous feathers.
