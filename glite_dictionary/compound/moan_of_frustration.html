moan of frustration

1. NOUN

/ moʊn əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / məʊn əv frəˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a low sound made expressing annoyance or dissatisfaction, especially when upset or disappointed
sound expressing annoyance or dissatisfaction

Examples

He gave a loud moan of frustration when he couldn't solve the puzzle.
The audience let out a collective moan of frustration as the power went out.
She stifled a moan of frustration while searching for her lost keys.

Synset Examples

She let out a moan of frustration after the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groan - a low sound expressing pain or despair
