boxing match

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑksɪŋ mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɒksɪŋ mætʃ / (BrE)
a contest between two boxers following the rules of boxing, usually held in a boxing ring
contest between boxers

Examples

The championship boxing match drew a large crowd to the arena.
He trained for months to prepare for his first professional boxing match.
Last night's boxing match was broadcast live on national television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fight - a violent confrontation or contest between people
