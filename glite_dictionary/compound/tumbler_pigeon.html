tumbler pigeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌmblər ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈtʌmblə ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a breed of domestic pigeon that performs backward somersaults in flight or on the ground
pigeon breed that somersaults in flight

Examples

The tumbler pigeon is admired for its ability to flip backward in midair.
Many breeders train their tumbler pigeons for aerial performances and competitions.
She watched a tumbler pigeon perform acrobatic somersaults above the rooftops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roller pigeon - a breed of pigeon known for doing backward somersaults in rapid succession
