urban living

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝbən ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the lifestyle or way of life typically associated with living in a city, often involving fast pace, diversity, and access to amenities
city-based lifestyle

Examples

Many young professionals are drawn to urban living for its excitement and opportunities.
The high cost of urban living often makes housing unaffordable for many families.
He prefers the peace of the countryside to the hustle and bustle of urban living.

Synset Examples

Parklife captures the essence of urban living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: city life - life in a city or urban area
