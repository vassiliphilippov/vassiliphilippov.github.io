fashion collection

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃ.ən kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
A designer's group of clothing items created for a specific season or show.
designer's group of seasonal clothes

Examples

The new fashion collection was unveiled during Paris Fashion Week and received widespread acclaim.
She spent weeks designing her spring fashion collection for the runway.
Many magazines feature photos from the latest fashion collection launches each season.

Synset Examples

Our latest fashion collections for Autumn 2014 have just arrived!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collection - a set of items considered as a group
