Republic of the Sudan

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə suːˈdɑːn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə suːˈdɑːn / (BrE)
The official name of Sudan, a country in northeastern Africa bordering the Red Sea, which gained independence from Egypt and the United Kingdom in 1956.
official name of Sudan (country in northeastern Africa)

Examples

The president of the Republic of the Sudan addressed the United Nations General Assembly.
Trade agreements between Egypt and the Republic of the Sudan have increased significantly in recent years.
The Republic of the Sudan shares its eastern border with the Red Sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sudan - common short name for Republic of the Sudan
