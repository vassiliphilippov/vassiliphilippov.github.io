letter paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
paper that is cut to a standard size for writing letters, often supplied with matching envelopes
paper for writing letters

Examples

She chose a beautiful set of letter paper for her thank-you notes.
The shop sells high-quality letter paper with matching envelopes.
Please use the official letter paper for all correspondence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationery - materials for writing, such as paper and envelopes
  • Synonym: notepaper - paper used for writing notes or letters

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtər ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
writing paper of a standard size, used for writing letters or correspondence
writing paper for correspondence

Examples

She wrote her grandmother a note on elegant letter paper she had bought at the shop.
The office ordered new boxes of letter paper for official correspondence.
Please use letter paper rather than scrap paper when sending formal letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationery - writing materials, especially paper and envelopes
