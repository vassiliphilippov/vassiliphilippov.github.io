bear's foot

1. NOUN

/ bɛrz fʊt / (AmE), / beəz fʊt / (BrE)
A hellebore plant with digitate (finger-like) leaves, known for its unpleasant odor and irritant properties if ingested.
a hellebore with finger-like leaves and bad odor

Examples

The garden's shade was dominated by a large bear's foot with its striking leaves.
She warned that bear's foot should not be ingested because of its irritant qualities.
Botanists studied the unusual smell of the bear's foot growing wild in the woods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hellebore - a genus of poisonous plants sometimes used medicinally
