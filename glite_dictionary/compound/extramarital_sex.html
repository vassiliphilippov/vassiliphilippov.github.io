extramarital sex

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkstrəˈmærɪtəl sɛks / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈmærɪtəl sɛks / (BrE)
sexual intercourse between people, at least one of whom is married to someone else
sex outside marriage

Examples

Rumors about his involvement in extramarital sex caused a scandal in the community.
Many cultures have strict taboos against extramarital sex.
She divorced her husband after discovering his extramarital sex with a coworker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adultery - voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and someone who is not their spouse

Etymology

From 'extra-' (Latin, meaning 'outside') + 'marital' (from Latin 'maritalis', 'of marriage').
