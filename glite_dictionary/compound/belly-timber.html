belly-timber

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛli ˌtɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛli ˌtɪmbə / (BrE)
food or sustenance, especially hearty meals or snacks.
food or sustenance (archaic)
archaic

Examples

He packed enough belly-timber to last them through the long journey.
The old sailor asked for some belly-timber before heading out to sea.
After hours of work, the men sat down for a meal of hearty belly-timber.

Synset Examples

She always carries belly-timber for road trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: food - substance consumed for nutritional support
  • Synonym: sustenance - food and drink regarded as a source of strength

Etymology

Formed from 'belly' (stomach) + 'timber' (wood, in archaic sense meaning material or matter).
