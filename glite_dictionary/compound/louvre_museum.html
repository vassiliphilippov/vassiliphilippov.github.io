Louvre Museum

1. PROPN

/ ˈluːvɚ mjuˈziːəm / (AmE), / ˈluːv(ə) mjuˈziːəm / (BrE)
A world-famous art museum and historic monument located in Paris, known for its extensive collection including the Mona Lisa.
famous art museum in Paris

Examples

The Louvre Museum attracts millions of visitors from around the world every year.
She spent an entire afternoon exploring the Louvre Museum in Paris.
The Mona Lisa is displayed in the Louvre Museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: museum - a building where objects of historical, artistic, or scientific interest are exhibited

Etymology

From French 'Louvre', the name of the royal palace where the museum is housed.
