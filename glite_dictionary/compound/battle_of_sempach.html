battle of Sempach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬əl əv sɛmˈpɑk / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv sɛmˈpak / (BrE)
a decisive battle in 1386 in which the Swiss Confederation defeated the Hapsburgs, securing their independence
1386 Swiss victory over the Hapsburgs

Examples

The battle of Sempach marked a turning point in Swiss history.
Many historians regard the battle of Sempach as crucial in ending Hapsburg rule over Switzerland.
Commemorations of the battle of Sempach are still held annually in the region.
