no matter the outcome

1. X

/ noʊ ˈmædər ði ˈaʊtˌkʌm / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈmætə ði ˈaʊtkʌm / (BrE)
used to emphasize that what happens or is decided will not affect someone's actions or attitude; regardless of the result
regardless of the result

Examples

I'll support you no matter the outcome of the competition.
They promised to remain friends no matter the outcome of their disagreement.
We have to try our best no matter the outcome.

Synset Examples

I will stand with you no matter the outcome.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regardless of the outcome - without considering what the result will be
  • Synonym: no matter what - regardless of what happens
