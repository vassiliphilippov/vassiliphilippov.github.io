sweet young thing

1. NOUN

/ swit jʌŋ θɪŋ / (AmE), / swiːt jʌŋ θɪŋ / (BrE)
an attractive and youthful person, especially a young woman, often spoken of in a patronizing or affectionate way
an attractive and youthful person

Examples

The sweet young thing walked into the room and immediately attracted everyone's attention.
He couldn't help but smile at the sweet young thing behind the counter.
Some people use the term sweet young thing in a condescending way.

Synset Examples

The sweet young thing charmed everyone with her smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingenue - an innocent or naive young woman (often in theater or film)
