with a view to

1. ADP

/ wɪð ə vjuː tu / (AmE), / wɪð ə vjuː tʊ / (BrE)
with the intention or aim of achieving something
with the intention of

Examples

She is taking evening classes with a view to starting her own business.
They moved to the city with a view to finding better job opportunities.
He saved money with a view to traveling around the world.

Synset Examples

She enrolled in classes with a view to advancing her career.
They are saving money with a view to buying a house.
He approached the project with a view to improving efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in order to - with the aim or purpose of doing something
