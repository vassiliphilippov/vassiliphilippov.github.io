green pea

1. NOUN

/ ɡriːn piː / (AmE), / ɡriːn piː / (BrE)
a small, round, green seed eaten as a vegetable, picked while immature and fresh from the pod
a small, fresh green legume seed

Examples

She added fresh green peas to the risotto for extra color and sweetness.
Frozen green peas are convenient for quick meals and salads.
The soup was made with a handful of young green peas and mint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea - the round seed of a leguminous plant, eaten as a vegetable
