black-necked cobra

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæk ˌnɛkt ˈkoʊbrə / (AmE), / ˈblæk ˌnɛkt ˈkəʊbrə / (BrE)
an aggressive species of cobra native to Africa, which rarely bites but can spit venom that may cause blindness
African cobra species known for spitting venom

Examples

The black-necked cobra is feared for its ability to spit venom at the eyes of predators.
Wildlife experts warn that the black-necked cobra should not be approached, despite its tendency to avoid biting.
Photographers often use protective gear when filming the black-necked cobra in its natural habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spitting cobra - cobra species that eject venom toward the eyes of predators
