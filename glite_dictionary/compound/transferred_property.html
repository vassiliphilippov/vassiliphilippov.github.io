transferred property

1. NOUN

/ trænsˈfɝd ˈprɑːpɚti / (AmE), / trænsˈfɜːd ˈprɒpəti / (BrE)
property whose ownership has been transferred from one person or entity to another.
property after ownership transfer
legal

Examples

The house became transferred property after the sale was finalized.
Inheritance laws determine how transferred property is distributed among heirs.
He was required to declare all transferred property received in the past year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: property - something owned; a possession
