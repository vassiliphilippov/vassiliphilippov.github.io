kicking and screaming

1. X

/ ˈkɪkɪŋ ənd ˈskriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪkɪŋ ənd ˈskriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
protesting very loudly and physically, often in a very upset or angry manner, especially when being forced to do something against one's will
protesting loudly and physically

Examples

Despite his parents' pleas, the child was taken to the dentist kicking and screaming.
She was dragged out of the meeting kicking and screaming after her heated outburst.
He went to summer camp kicking and screaming, but ended up loving it.

Synset Examples

The toddler was kicking and screaming when it was time to leave.
He left the party kicking and screaming after losing the game.
She went to bed kicking and screaming, refusing to sleep.
