impossible dream

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɑːsəbəl driːm / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɒsəbl driːm / (BrE)
an aspiration or goal that cannot be achieved; something one hopes for but is unattainable
unattainable aspiration or goal

Examples

Chasing that impossible dream kept him motivated.
She pursued her impossible dream of becoming an astronaut.
Many people secretly hold on to an impossible dream despite all odds.
