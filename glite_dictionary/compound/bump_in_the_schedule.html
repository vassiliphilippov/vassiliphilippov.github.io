bump in the schedule

1. NOUN

/ bʌmp ɪn ðə ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / bʌmp ɪn ðə ˈʃedjuːl / (BrE)
an unexpected disruption or delay in a planned timetable or sequence of events
unexpected delay or disruption in a schedule

Examples

We encountered a bump in the schedule when the speaker's flight was canceled.
Even a minor bump in the schedule can cause the project to run late.
The festival had a bump in the schedule due to unexpected technical issues.

Synset Examples

There was a bump in the schedule due to rain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delay - a period of time when something is postponed
  • Synonym: setback - a reversal or check in progress
