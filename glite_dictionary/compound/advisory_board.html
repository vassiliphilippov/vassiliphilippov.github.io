advisory board

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvaɪzəri bɔrd / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzəri bɔːd / (BrE)
a group of people appointed to provide expert advice to an organization or chief administrator
group providing expert advice

Examples

She served on the advisory board for several nonprofit organizations.
The university established an advisory board to guide its research priorities.
Our advisory board meets quarterly to review strategic plans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: board of directors - a group of people elected to manage the affairs of a corporation
