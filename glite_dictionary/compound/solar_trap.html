solar trap

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlɚ træp / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə træp / (BrE)
a terrace or garden arranged to capture maximum sunlight while being shielded from cold winds
sunny, sheltered terrace or garden

Examples

The house was built with a solar trap to provide warmth during winter afternoons.
Their garden includes a small solar trap where they enjoy morning coffee, even on chilly days.
Designers recommend adding a solar trap to enhance outdoor comfort in colder climates.
