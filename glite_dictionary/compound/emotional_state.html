emotional state

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənl steɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl steɪt / (BrE)
a person's condition with respect to their feelings and emotions at a particular time, especially regarding pleasure or dejection
a person's condition of emotions at a time

Examples

His emotional state improved after he spoke to a therapist.
The news had a significant impact on her emotional state.
It's important to be aware of your emotional state during stressful situations.

Synset Examples

his emotional state depended on her opinion
he was in good spirits
his spirit rose

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mood - a temporary state of mind or feeling
  • Synonym: frame of mind - a person's general attitude or feelings at a particular time
