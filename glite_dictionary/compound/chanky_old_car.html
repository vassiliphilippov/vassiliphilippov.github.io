chanky old car

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃæŋki oʊld kɑr / (AmE), / ˈtʃæŋki əʊld kɑː / (BrE)
A car that is unreliable, poorly maintained, and prone to mechanical problems.
unreliable, worn-out car
informal

Examples

The chanky old car rattled noisily down the street, leaving a trail of smoke behind.
Every winter, his chanky old car struggles to start in the cold mornings.
She refused to go on a road trip in that chanky old car, fearing it would break down.

Synset Examples

That chanky old car broke down again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated car
  • Synonym: lemon - a car that is defective or constantly breaks down
