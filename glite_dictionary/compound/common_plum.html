common plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən plʌm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən plʌm / (BrE)
a widely cultivated species of plum (Prunus domestica) found in cooler temperate regions, bearing edible fruit
a widely grown plum species

Examples

The common plum is popular in home gardens due to its sweet fruit and hardiness.
Farmers in Europe often grow several varieties of common plum for both fresh eating and preserves.
The orchard was filled with mature common plum trees, their branches heavy with ripe fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - a type of sweet, edible fruit
