game time

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm taɪm / (AmE), / ɡeɪm taɪm / (BrE)
the moment when action, a challenge, or an important event is about to begin
moment to begin a challenge or event

Examples

The team gathered as the coach shouted, "It's game time! Let's give it our best."
Everyone triple-checked their gear; game time was only minutes away.
When the boss entered the room, the staff knew it was game time for the big presentation.

Synset Examples

It’s game time, ready or not here we come!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crunch time - a critical moment when decisive action is needed
  • Synonym: kickoff - the beginning of an event or activity
