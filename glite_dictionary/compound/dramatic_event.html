dramatic event

1. NOUN

/ drəˈmæt̬ɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an incident or occurrence marked by intense emotion or turbulence
turbulent or highly emotional incident

Examples

The sudden resignation of the CEO was a dramatic event that shook the company.
The play is inspired by a dramatic event from the author's childhood.
After the crash, the community rallied together in response to the dramatic event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incident - an event or occurrence, often with notable consequences
