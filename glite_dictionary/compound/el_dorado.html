El Dorado

1. NOUN

/ ɛl dɔˈrɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ɛl dəˈrɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
An imaginary place of great wealth and opportunity, originally believed by early explorers to exist in South America.
legendary place of wealth and opportunity

Examples

For centuries, explorers searched the Amazon for El Dorado, hoping to find untold riches.
The tech industry is often seen as the modern El Dorado by ambitious entrepreneurs.
Investors flocked to the city, believing it to be an economic El Dorado.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utopia - an imagined perfect place or state of things
  • Synonym: paradise - an ideal or idyllic place

Etymology

From Spanish 'el dorado' meaning 'the gilded one'.
