psychic phenomenon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkɪk fəˈnɑːmənən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkɪk fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
An event or occurrence that appears to defy physical laws and is believed to be caused by extra-sensory mental processes.
an event believed caused by psychic or mental powers

Examples

Many people have tried to study the psychic phenomenon of telepathy under controlled conditions.
She claimed that the strange noises were a psychic phenomenon caused by spirits.
Scientists generally remain skeptical about the existence of any true psychic phenomenon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paranormal - events outside the range of normal experience or scientific explanation
  • Synonym: supernatural - attributed to some force beyond scientific understanding or laws of nature
