Staffordshire bull terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæfərdʃɪr bʊl təˈrɪr / (AmE), / ˈstæfədʃə bʊl təˈrɪə / (BrE)
A strong, medium-sized breed of dog from England, known for its stocky build, broad skull, and smooth coat.
a strong, medium-sized English dog breed

Examples

The Staffordshire bull terrier is known for its loyalty and affectionate nature.
At the dog show, her Staffordshire bull terrier won first place in the agility competition.
Many families choose a Staffordshire bull terrier because of its friendly temperament with children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bull terrier - a breed of dog developed by crossing bulldogs and terriers

Etymology

Named after Staffordshire, the county in England where the breed originated, combined with 'bull terrier'.
