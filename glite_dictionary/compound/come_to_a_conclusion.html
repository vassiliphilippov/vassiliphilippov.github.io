to come to a conclusion

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə ə kənˈkluʒən / (AmE), / kʌm tʊ ə kənˈkluːʒən / (BrE)
to reach a final decision or judgment after consideration
reach a final decision or judgment

Examples

After reviewing all the evidence, the jury finally came to a conclusion.
It took us several hours to come to a conclusion about which candidate to hire.
I haven't come to a conclusion yet about which university to attend.

Synset Examples

It took him a while to come to that conclusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrive at a conclusion - reach a decision or judgment
  • Synonym: draw a conclusion - form an opinion or judgment after considering the evidence
