vertical circle

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɝːtɪkəl ˈsɝːkəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl ˈsɜːkl̩ / (BrE)
A great circle on the celestial sphere that passes through the zenith and is perpendicular to the horizon.
great circle passing through zenith and perpendicular to horizon

Examples

The star crossed the vertical circle at midnight, making it easy to determine its altitude.
Astronomers observed the planet as it moved past the vertical circle through the zenith.
He used the vertical circle to calculate the exact time of the star's culmination.
