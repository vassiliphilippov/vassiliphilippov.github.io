telephone jack

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn dʒæk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn dʒæk / (BrE)
a socket into which a telephone can be plugged to connect it to a telephone network
socket for connecting a telephone

Examples

The new apartment had only one telephone jack in the living room.
She needed an adapter to connect her modem to the telephone jack.
They discovered the telephone jack was faulty and called a technician to repair it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone jack - a socket for connecting a telephone or similar device
  • Antonym: telephone plug - the plug that connects a telephone to a socket
