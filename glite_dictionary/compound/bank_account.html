bank account

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋk əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈbæŋk əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
An arrangement with a bank in which the customer deposits and withdraws money and keeps financial records.
an account with a bank for depositing and withdrawing money

Examples

She opened a new bank account to manage her savings.
All payments will be transferred directly to your bank account.
He checked his bank account balance online every week.

Synset Examples

he moved his bank account to a new bank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: account - a record or arrangement for keeping financial information with an institution
