fishing gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ ɡɪɹ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
the equipment used for catching fish, such as rods, nets, lines, and hooks
equipment used for fishing

Examples

He packed all his fishing gear before heading to the lake.
The store sells a wide range of fishing gear for both beginners and experts.
She bought new fishing gear for her summer trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tackle - equipment used for fishing
