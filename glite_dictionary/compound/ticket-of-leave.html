ticket-of-leave

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt əv liv / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt əv liːv / (BrE)
a permit formerly issued to convicts allowing them conditional release from prison under specific restrictions
permit for conditional release of convicts
legal archaic

Examples

After serving ten years, the convict was granted a ticket-of-leave and allowed to work outside the prison.
Many former prisoners struggled to find employment, even with a ticket-of-leave.
His ticket-of-leave was revoked when he violated the conditions of his release.
