chunky sweater

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃʌŋki ˈswɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋki ˈswɛtə / (BrE)
a thick, knitted sweater made with bulky yarn, often worn for warmth
thick, knitted sweater

Examples

She wrapped herself in a soft chunky sweater on the chilly evening.
His favorite chunky sweater is knitted by his grandmother.
When autumn arrives, I always reach for a cozy chunky sweater.

Synset Examples

She loves chunky sweaters in the winter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jumper - a knitted garment covering the upper body, mostly used in British English
