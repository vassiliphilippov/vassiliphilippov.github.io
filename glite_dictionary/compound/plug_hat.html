plug hat

1. NOUN

/ plʌɡ hæt / (AmE), / plʌɡ hæt / (BrE)
a man's formal felt hat that is round and stiff with a narrow brim, popular in the 19th and early 20th centuries; also known as a top hat
formal round stiff felt hat

Examples

The distinguished gentleman wore a plug hat to the evening gala.
In old photographs, you can often spot men in plug hats and tailcoats.
He tipped his plug hat politely as he entered the theatre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top hat - a tall, flat-crowned, broad-brimmed hat; synonym for plug hat
