rate of payment

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪt əv ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈreɪt əv ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the amount of money paid per unit of time or specified service period
amount paid per unit time

Examples

Her rate of payment increased after she received her certification.
The company offers a competitive rate of payment for overtime work.
You should always confirm your rate of payment before starting a new job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - payment for work, usually hourly or weekly
  • Synonym: salary - regular payment, typically monthly or biweekly, for employment
