scarlet clematis

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːɹ.lət kləˈmæt.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt klɪˈmæ.tɪs / (BrE)
a woody vine native to Texas, known for its showy, solitary, nodding scarlet flowers; a species of clematis.
a Texan vine with scarlet flowers

Examples

The garden featured a beautiful scarlet clematis climbing along the fence.
She planted a scarlet clematis to add vibrant color to her backyard.
Wild scarlet clematis can be seen blooming in parts of Texas each spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clematis - woody climbing plant of the buttercup family with showy flowers
