undiluted honesty

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.daɪˈluː.tɪd ˈɑː.nə.sti / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈljuː.tɪd ˈɒn.ə.sti / (BrE)
complete and unfiltered truthfulness; honesty without any attempt to soften or disguise the truth.
complete and unfiltered truthfulness

Examples

She admired his undiluted honesty when discussing difficult subjects.
Undiluted honesty is sometimes hard to hear but necessary for growth.
His report was marked by undiluted honesty, even when the findings were controversial.

Synset Examples

She appreciated the undiluted honesty in his feedback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candor - the quality of being open and honest; frankness
  • Synonym: frankness - the quality of being open, honest, and direct
  • Antonym: dishonesty - the quality of being untruthful or deceitful
