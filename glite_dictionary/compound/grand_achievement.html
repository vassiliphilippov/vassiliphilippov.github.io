grand achievement

1. NOUN

/ ɡrænd əˈtʃivmənt / (AmE), / ɡrænd əˈtʃiːvmənt / (BrE)
a significant or outstanding accomplishment, often marking an important milestone or success
significant accomplishment or success

Examples

Winning the championship was considered his grand achievement of the year.
The scientist's grand achievement changed the course of medical research.
They regarded the completion of the bridge as a grand achievement in engineering.

Synset Examples

They celebrated her grand achievement with a party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milestone - an important event or turning point
  • Synonym: feat - an impressive or difficult achievement
