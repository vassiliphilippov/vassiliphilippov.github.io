Denali Fault

1. PROPN

/ dəˈnɑːli fɔlt / (AmE), / dəˈnɑːli fɔːlt / (BrE)
a major geological fault extending through central Alaska, known for its seismic activity
major geological fault in Alaska

Examples

The Denali Fault is responsible for some of the largest earthquakes in Alaska's history.
Geologists have studied the Denali Fault to better understand regional seismic risks.
The 2002 earthquake significantly shifted parts of the Denali Fault system.
