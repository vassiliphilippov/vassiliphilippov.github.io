to deliver package

1. VERB

/ dɪˈlɪvɚ ˈpækɪdʒ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə ˈpækɪdʒ / (BrE)
to transport and hand over a parcel to a designated recipient or location
bring and hand over a parcel

Examples

The company now uses electric vans to deliver packages throughout the city.
She signed for the parcel after the courier delivered the package to her office.
Drones might soon deliver packages to rural homes more quickly than trucks.

Synset Examples

They watched the drone deliver packages to their doorstep.

Related Terms
