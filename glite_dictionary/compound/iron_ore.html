iron ore

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn ɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ɔː / (BrE)
A naturally occurring mineral or rock from which iron can be profitably extracted.
rock or mineral for extracting iron

Examples

The company specializes in mining iron ore for use in steel production.
Large deposits of iron ore have been found in the region.
The ship was loaded with tons of iron ore heading for the factory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ore - rock or mineral from which metal can be extracted
