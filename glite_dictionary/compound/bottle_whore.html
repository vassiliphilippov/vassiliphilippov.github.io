bottle whore

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬l wɔːr / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl wɔː / (BrE)
A slang, derogatory term for a person, typically a woman, who habitually seeks free alcoholic drinks or attention at bars or clubs, often by associating with others who are buying.
person who seeks free drinks, especially at bars (slang, derogatory)
slang offensive

Examples

She didn't want to be called a bottle whore just because she accepted a few free drinks.
He accused his ex-girlfriend of acting like a bottle whore at the nightclub.
Some people criticize the way bars encourage bottle whores to attract big spenders.

Synset Examples

She was labeled a bottle whore for always seeking free drinks.
Being a bottle whore doesn't impress anyone at the bar.
He jokingly referred to her as a bottle whore among friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold digger - a person who forms relationships for financial gain
  • Degree Lower: party animal - someone who enjoys going to parties frequently
