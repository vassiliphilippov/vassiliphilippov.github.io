coal seller

1. NOUN

/ koʊl ˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to deliver or sell coal, especially for household or industrial use
person who sells or delivers coal

Examples

The coal seller arrived at dawn, bringing a fresh load for the fireplace.
In the past, every neighborhood had its own coal seller who knew all the families.
She remembered the days when the coal seller would deliver bags of coal to their doorstep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coalman - a person who delivers coal to customers
