favorite son

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt sʌn / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt sʌn / (BrE)
A politician who is strongly supported in their home state, especially when nominated for national office chiefly to control the state's delegates.
politician supported in home state
american

Examples

The party nominated a favorite son to ensure the state's delegates' loyalty.
As a favorite son, he received overwhelming support in the primary.
The term favorite son is often used in American political reporting during election seasons.
