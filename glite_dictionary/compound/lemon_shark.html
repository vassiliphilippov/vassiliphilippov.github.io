lemon shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛm.ən ˌʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.ən ˌʃɑːk / (BrE)
a species of large, yellowish-brown shark commonly found in shallow coastal waters of the Atlantic, known for its schooling behavior
a species of large, yellowish-brown shark

Examples

The lemon shark is often seen swimming in shallow tropical waters near the coast.
Researchers tagged several lemon sharks to study their migratory patterns.
The distinctive yellowish color helps the lemon shark blend in with sandy ocean floors.
