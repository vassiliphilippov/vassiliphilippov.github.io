Brazos River

1. PROPN

/ ˈbræzəs ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈbræzɒs ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in the southern United States, rising in New Mexico and flowing across Texas into the Gulf of Mexico.
river in Texas, USA

Examples

The Brazos River flooded after the heavy rains last spring.
Many Texas towns are situated along the Brazos River.
We went canoeing on the Brazos River during our summer vacation.

Etymology

From Spanish 'brazos', meaning 'arms'.
