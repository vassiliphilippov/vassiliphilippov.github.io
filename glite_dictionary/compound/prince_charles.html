Prince Charles

1. PROPN

/ prɪns ˈʧɑrlz / (AmE), / prɪns ˈtʃɑːlz / (BrE)
the eldest son of Queen Elizabeth II and heir to the British throne, born in 1948, later known as King Charles III.
the former Prince of Wales, son of Queen Elizabeth II

Examples

Prince Charles attended the opening ceremony of the new hospital.
Many books document the life and achievements of Prince Charles.
After the passing of Queen Elizabeth II, Prince Charles became King Charles III.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: King Charles III - monarch of the United Kingdom, formerly Prince Charles
