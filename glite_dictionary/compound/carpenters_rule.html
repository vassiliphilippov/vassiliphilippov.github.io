carpenter's rule

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz ruːl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pən.təz ruːl / (BrE)
a measuring rule used by carpenters, typically flexible and marked with units for measuring lengths and drawing straight lines
measuring rule used by carpenters

Examples

He used a carpenter's rule to measure the length of the wooden plank.
A carpenter's rule is an essential tool in any woodworking shop.
Before cutting the beams, she double-checked her measurements with her carpenter's rule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape measure - a flexible ruler used to measure size or distance
