universal resource locator

1. NOUN

/ juːˈnɪvɚsəl rɪˈsɔrs ləˈkeɪtɚ / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəl rɪˈsɔːs ləʊˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
the address of a specific resource, such as a web page, on the World Wide Web, commonly known as a URL
address for a web page or online resource

Examples

To access the document, you need to enter its universal resource locator into your web browser.
Every image on the website has its own unique universal resource locator.
She emailed me the universal resource locator so I could download the report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: URL - abbreviation of universal resource locator
  • Synonym: web address - an informal term for the address of a web page

Etymology

Formed from 'universal' + 'resource' + 'locator', coined in the early 1990s as a technical standard in computing.
