batting glove

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɾɪŋ ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ ɡlʌv / (BrE)
a glove worn by a batter in baseball or softball to improve grip on the bat and protect the hands
glove for batters in baseball/softball

Examples

He always wears his batting glove during games for better grip and comfort.
Her batting glove ripped after a season of heavy use at the plate.
The rookie adjusted his batting glove before stepping up to bat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batting helmet - helmet worn by a batter in baseball or softball for protection
  • Synonym: bat - a smooth club used in baseball and softball to hit the ball
