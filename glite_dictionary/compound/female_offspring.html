female offspring

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːˌmeɪl ˈɔfˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl ˈɒfˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
A child of a person or animal that is female; a daughter.
female child (daughter)

Examples

The scientist recorded the number of female offspring produced by each pair of mice.
It is customary in some cultures for female offspring to care for elderly parents.
The study showed that female offspring were more likely to inherit certain traits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daughter - a person's female child
  • Antonym: male offspring - a child who is male
