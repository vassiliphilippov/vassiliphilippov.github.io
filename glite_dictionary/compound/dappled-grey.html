dappled-grey

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæpəld ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈdæpəld ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
A horse with a grey coat marked with lighter and darker areas, giving a mottled appearance.
grey color with a mottled pattern (esp. horse)

Examples

The magnificent dappled-grey stood out among the other horses in the field.
She had always dreamed of riding a dappled-grey through the countryside.
The artist painted a stunning portrait of a dappled-grey in motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - a color between black and white

Etymology

From 'dapple' (mottled or spotted marking) and 'grey' (color).
