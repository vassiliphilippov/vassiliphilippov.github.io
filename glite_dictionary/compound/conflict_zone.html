conflict zone

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.flɪkt zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.flɪkt zəʊn / (BrE)
an area where there is ongoing or active military fighting or armed conflict
area with active military fighting

Examples

International aid workers entered the conflict zone to provide assistance to civilians.
Journalists often face great risks when reporting from a conflict zone.
The government declared the region a conflict zone after renewed fighting broke out.

Synset Examples

The military deployed antipersonnel mines in the conflict zone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war zone - an area where a war is being fought
