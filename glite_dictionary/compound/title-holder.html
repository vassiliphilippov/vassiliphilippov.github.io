title-holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪt̬lˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtlˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who currently holds first place or a championship in a competition
person holding a championship or first place

Examples

As the reigning title-holder, she must defend her championship next month.
The title-holder was upset by a newcomer in a dramatic final round.
Every title-holder faces the challenge of living up to high expectations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champion - someone who has won a competition or contest
  • Antonym: challenger - a person competing against the title-holder for first place or a championship
