full of energy

1. ADJ

/ fʊl əv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / fʊl əv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
having a lot of physical or mental vitality and enthusiasm
having a lot of vigor or enthusiasm

Examples

The children were full of energy after their afternoon nap and wanted to go outside and play.
She always feels full of energy in the mornings and gets a lot of work done before noon.
Despite his age, Mr. Jones remains full of energy and enthusiasm for life.

Synset Examples

My neighbor's tyke is adorable and full of energy.
That kid is always full of energy and curiosity.
That blacky dog is full of energy and mischief.
My brother is the live one in our family, full of energy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality
  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Antonym: tired - in need of sleep or rest; weary
