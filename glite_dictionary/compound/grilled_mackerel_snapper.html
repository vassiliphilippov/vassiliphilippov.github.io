grilled mackerel snapper

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪld ˈmækərəl ˈsnæpər / (AmE), / ɡrɪld ˈmækərəl ˈsnæpə / (BrE)
A dish consisting of mackerel snapper fish that has been cooked by grilling, often served with seasonings or sauces.
dish of grilled mackerel snapper fish

Examples

The restaurant's signature dish was the grilled mackerel snapper served with rice and vegetables.
I enjoyed the delicate flavors of the grilled mackerel snapper on my trip to the Caribbean.
At dinner, she recommended the grilled mackerel snapper for those who prefer lighter, healthier options.

Synset Examples

He ordered the grilled mackerel snapper with lemon butter sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled fish - fish that has been cooked by grilling
  • Antonym: fried snapper - snapper fish dish prepared by frying
