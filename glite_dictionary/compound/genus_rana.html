genus Rana

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈreɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈrɑː.nə / (BrE)
A genus of frogs in the family Ranidae, commonly known as the true frogs.
a genus of true frogs

Examples

Scientists have discovered a new species within the genus Rana in Southeast Asia.
The genus Rana includes several widely distributed and familiar frog species.
Researchers compared the habitat preferences of frogs from the genus Rana and other genera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frog - A tailless amphibian with long hind legs, commonly of the family Ranidae

Etymology

From Latin 'rana' meaning 'frog'.
