coffee can

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi kæn / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi kæn / (BrE)
A metal or plastic container for storing ground coffee.
container for ground coffee

Examples

She keeps her spare change in an old coffee can on the shelf.
After finishing the grounds, he repurposed the coffee can for storing nails and screws.
The recipe calls for mixing the cake batter in a clean coffee can.
