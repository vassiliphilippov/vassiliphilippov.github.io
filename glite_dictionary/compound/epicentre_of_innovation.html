epicentre of innovation

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪˌsɛntər əv ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌsɛntə əv ˌɪnəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the main centre or focal point where new ideas or technological advancements originate or concentrate
main hub of new ideas/advancements

Examples

The city became the epicentre of innovation in technology.
During the 19th century, Paris was considered the epicentre of innovation in art and culture.
The research park quickly established itself as the epicentre of innovation for clean energy solutions.

Synset Examples

The city became the epicentre of innovation in technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hub - a central or main place of activity or interest
  • Synonym: frontier of innovation - the leading or most advanced position in new developments

Etymology

Borrowed metaphorically from 'epicentre,' originally from Greek 'epikentros' meaning 'situated at the centre.'
