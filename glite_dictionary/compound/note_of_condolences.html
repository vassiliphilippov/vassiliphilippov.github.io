note of condolences

1. NOUN

/ noʊt əv kənˈdoʊlənzɪz / (AmE), / nəʊt əv kənˈdəʊlənzɪz / (BrE)
a written message expressing sympathy to someone who has experienced a loss, especially a bereavement
written message of sympathy

Examples

She sent a heartfelt note of condolences to her friend after hearing the sad news.
After the funeral, many people wrote a note of condolences to the grieving family.
He received a note of condolences from his colleagues expressing their deepest sympathy.

Synset Examples

Her note of congratudolences touched my heart.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter of sympathy - a written communication expressing sorrow for someone's loss
