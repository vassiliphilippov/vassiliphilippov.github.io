tourist attraction

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪst əˌtrækʃən / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst əˌtrækʃən / (BrE)
a place or feature that draws tourists because of its interest, beauty, or significance
place or feature attracting tourists

Examples

The Eiffel Tower is the most famous tourist attraction in Paris.
The city invested heavily to improve its main tourist attractions.
Natural wonders often become popular tourist attractions for visitors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sight - notable place of interest, especially for tourists
  • Synonym: landmark - historically or culturally important place, often visited by tourists
