well-knit

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛl ˌnɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛl ˌnɪt / (BrE)
strongly and firmly constructed or well organized; compact or cohesive in structure or form
strongly constructed; cohesive

Examples

The team presented a well-knit plan that addressed every potential issue.
Her novel is a well-knit story, with no loose ends left unexplained.
His well-knit physique was the result of years of dedication to fitness.
