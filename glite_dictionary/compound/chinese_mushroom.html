Chinese mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪˌniz ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪˌniːz ˈmʌʃˌrʊm / (BrE)
A small, edible mushroom with a white cap and long stem, regarded as a delicacy in China and other Asian countries.
edible mushroom used in Asian cuisine

Examples

The soup was garnished with Chinese mushroom slices for extra flavor.
He bought fresh Chinese mushrooms from the Asian market for the stir-fry.
Vegetarians often use Chinese mushroom as a meat substitute in their dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straw mushroom - small edible mushroom commonly used in Asian cuisine
