home truth

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm truːθ / (AmE), / həʊm truːθ / (BrE)
a true statement about someone or something that is unpleasant to acknowledge, especially about oneself
unpleasant but true statement

Examples

During the meeting, she told him a home truth about his work habits.
It can be painful to hear a home truth, but sometimes it's necessary for personal growth.
He was shocked when his best friend delivered a few home truths about his attitude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard truth - an uncomfortable truth that is difficult to accept
