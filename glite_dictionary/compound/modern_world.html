modern world

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdərn wɜrld / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən wɜːld / (BrE)
the society, culture, and conditions of the present age, characterized by contemporary ideas, technology, and ways of life
the present-day society and conditions

Examples

Many people struggle to keep up with the pace of change in the modern world.
Technology plays a crucial role in shaping the modern world.
Environmental concerns are a major issue facing the modern world today.
