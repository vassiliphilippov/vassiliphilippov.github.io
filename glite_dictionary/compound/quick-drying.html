quick-drying

1. ADJ

/ kwɪkˈdraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɪkˈdraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially a liquid or coating) drying rapidly after being applied.
drying quickly after application

Examples

This quick-drying paint is perfect for small home improvement projects.
The quick-drying glue allowed her to finish the craft in minutes.
He prefers quick-drying towels when traveling because they save time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast-drying - dries in a short amount of time
  • Antonym: slow-drying - takes a long time to dry
