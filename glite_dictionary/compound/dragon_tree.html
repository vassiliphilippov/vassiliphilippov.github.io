dragon tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræɡən tri / (AmE), / ˈdræɡən triː / (BrE)
A tall, subtropical tree native to the Canary Islands, known for its thick trunk and red resin called dragon's blood.
tree of the Canary Islands; source of dragon's blood

Examples

The famous dragon tree in Tenerife is several centuries old.
Botanists are studying the medicinal properties of resin from the dragon tree.
The dragon tree is admired for its unique umbrella-shaped canopy and thick trunk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dragon's blood - red resin from certain plants, including the dragon tree
