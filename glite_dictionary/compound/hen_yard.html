hen yard

1. NOUN

/ hɛn jɑrd / (AmE), / hɛn jɑːd / (BrE)
an enclosed area or yard where hens or other poultry are kept
enclosed yard for keeping poultry

Examples

Every morning, she fed the chickens in the hen yard behind the barn.
The old hen yard needed repairs after last winter’s storms.
Fresh eggs are collected daily from the hen yard in the back garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - a small structure where chickens are housed
