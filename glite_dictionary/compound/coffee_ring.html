coffee ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi rɪŋ / (BrE)
A ring-shaped coffeecake, often containing fruits or nuts and sometimes topped with icing.
ring-shaped coffeecake

Examples

My grandmother baked a delicious coffee ring filled with cinnamon and walnuts for breakfast.
We served slices of coffee ring at the brunch party alongside fresh fruit and tea.
The bakery's signature coffee ring is topped with a sweet glaze and dried cherries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffeecake - a sweet, cake-like bread often served with coffee
