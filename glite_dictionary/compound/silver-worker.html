silver-worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects made of silver
person who works with silver

Examples

The silver-worker repaired the delicate cup with great care.
As a skilled silver-worker, she crafted beautiful jewelry for her clients.
The town became famous for its talented silver-workers in the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silversmith - a person who makes objects from silver
