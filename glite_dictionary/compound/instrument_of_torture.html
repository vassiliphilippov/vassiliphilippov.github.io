instrument of torture

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstrəmənt əv ˈtɔrtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrʊmənt ɒv ˈtɔːtʃə / (BrE)
A device specifically designed and used to inflict severe pain or suffering as a form of punishment or coercion.
device for inflicting torture

Examples

The museum displayed a gruesome instrument of torture from the medieval era.
He was falsely accused of designing an instrument of torture for the regime.
Historical records describe many forms of instrument of torture used to extract confessions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: torture device - a device designed for inflicting torture
