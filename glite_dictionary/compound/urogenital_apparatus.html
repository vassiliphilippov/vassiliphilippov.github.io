urogenital apparatus

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊroʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl əˈpærətəs / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnɪtəl əˈparətəs / (BrE)
the combined organ system responsible for both reproduction and the formation and excretion of urine in the body
combined reproductive and urinary organ system
medical

Examples

The urogenital apparatus plays a crucial role in both urinary and reproductive functions.
Doctors examined the patient's urogenital apparatus for signs of infection.
Damage to the urogenital apparatus can lead to significant health complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genitourinary system - organ system comprising reproductive and urinary organs

Etymology

From Greek 'ouron' (urine) and Latin 'genitalis' (pertaining to reproduction), plus Latin 'apparatus' (equipment, system).
