stocking stuffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑkɪŋ ˌstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪŋ ˌstʌfə / (BrE)
a small gift, typically inexpensive, placed in a Christmas stocking as part of holiday traditions
small gift for a Christmas stocking

Examples

Each child received a stocking stuffer along with their larger presents on Christmas morning.
I usually buy candy and small toys as stocking stuffers for my family.
She found a tiny book in her stocking stuffer that she read right away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stocking filler - British term for a small present put in a Christmas stocking
