grippy sock

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrɪpi sɑk / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpi sɒk / (BrE)
a sock with a textured or rubberized sole designed to prevent slipping on smooth surfaces
sock with non-slip sole

Examples

The hospital provided each patient with a pair of grippy socks for safety.
My son loves running around the house in his colorful grippy socks.
Yoga studios often recommend grippy socks for better stability during practice.

Synset Examples

The grippy sock design prevents slipping on smooth surfaces.
He slipped on his grippy socks to avoid falling.
The toddler wore her grippy socks while playing on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: non-slip sock - a sock designed to prevent slipping
  • Degree Lower: compression sock - a sock designed to apply pressure to the legs
