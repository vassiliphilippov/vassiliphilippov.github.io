certain of

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɝː.tən əv / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tən ɒv / (BrE)
confident that something is true or will happen; having no doubt about something
confident; having no doubt

Examples

Maria felt certain of her decision, despite others' doubts.
The scientists are certain of the accuracy of their results.
He was certain of his ability to finish the marathon.

Synset Examples

She was certain of winning the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sure of - confident or convinced about something
  • Antonym: uncertain of - not confident or unsure about something
