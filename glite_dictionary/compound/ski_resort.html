ski resort

1. NOUN

/ ski rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / ski rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
A place where people can stay while enjoying skiing, often offering accommodation, equipment rental, and other facilities.
place offering lodging and facilities for skiing

Examples

They booked a week at a ski resort in the Alps for their winter vacation.
This ski resort has trails suitable for both beginners and experts.
The new ski resort features luxury chalets and state-of-the-art lifts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resort - a place where people go for holidays or recreation

2. NOUN

/ ski ɹɪˈzɔɹt / (AmE), / ski ɹɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
A place or area equipped for skiing and other winter sports, often including accommodation and other facilities for visitors.
place for skiing and winter sports

Examples

We're planning to spend our vacation at a luxury ski resort in the Alps.
The ski resort offers lessons for both beginners and advanced skiers.
By early December, fresh snow had transformed the town into a bustling ski resort.
