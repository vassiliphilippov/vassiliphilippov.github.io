police headquarters

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the central office or main station for police in a particular city, district, or jurisdiction, from which officers are dispatched and administrative activities are managed
main police office in an area

Examples

After the robbery, the suspects were taken to police headquarters for questioning.
The mayor is scheduled to visit the new police headquarters next week.
Reporters gathered outside the police headquarters awaiting an official statement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police station - a building where police officers work and where people can report crimes
