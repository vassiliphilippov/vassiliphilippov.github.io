blue false indigo

1. NOUN

/ blu fɔls ˈɪndɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / bluː fɔːls ˈɪndɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Baptisia australis) of the pea family, with spikes of blue flowers and gray-green leaves
a North American plant with blue flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming blue false indigo in early summer.
Native pollinators are attracted to the vibrant flowers of blue false indigo.
She planted blue false indigo along the fence for its striking color and resilience.
