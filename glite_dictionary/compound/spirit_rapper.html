spirit rapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrət ˌræpər / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌrapə / (BrE)
a person who claims to communicate with the dead by producing mysterious knocking sounds, especially during séances
person claiming to receive supernatural knocks from spirits

Examples

Many people in the 19th century attended séances led by a spirit rapper.
The spirit rapper claimed that the knocks heard on the table were messages from beyond the grave.
Skeptics often tried to expose fraudulent spirit rappers during public demonstrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medium - person claiming to communicate with the spirits of the dead

Etymology

From 'spirit' (supernatural being) and 'rapper' (one who knocks), originally used in 19th-century spiritualism.
