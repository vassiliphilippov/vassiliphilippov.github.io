rising slope

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪzɪŋ sloʊp / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ sləʊp / (BrE)
a slope or surface that ascends; an upward incline
an upward-inclining surface

Examples

The hikers struggled up the rising slope as the sun began to set.
Heavy rain caused erosion on the rising slope behind the old house.
A rising slope makes this road challenging for inexperienced cyclists.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: descent - a downward slope or the act of going down
  • Synonym: incline - a surface that deviates from the horizontal; a sloping surface
