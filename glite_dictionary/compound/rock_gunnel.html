rock gunnel

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈɡʌnəl / (AmE), / rɒk ˈɡʌnəl / (BrE)
A slippery, scaleless food fish of the northern Atlantic coastal waters, known for its elongated body.
northern Atlantic coastal fish

Examples

Fishermen often catch rock gunnel while trawling along the northern Atlantic coast.
The rock gunnel can be recognized by its long, slender, and scaleless body.
Many coastal communities use rock gunnel as an ingredient in traditional seafood dishes.
