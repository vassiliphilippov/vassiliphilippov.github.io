horse pucky

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈpʌki / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈpʌki / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Nonsense; foolish or untrue talk or ideas.
nonsense; foolish talk (slang)
informal slang american

Examples

I knew everything he said about the project was just horse pucky.
Don't tell me that winning is all about luck; that's pure horse pucky.
She dismissed his complaints as nothing but horse pucky.

Synset Examples

That excuse sounds like horse pucky to me.
His story is just horse pucky to cover his mistakes.
Don’t give me that horse pucky; I see through it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are silly or have no meaning

Etymology

American slang; 'pucky' is a euphemism for excrement, origin mid-20th century.
