ten point

1. NOUN

/ tɛn pɔɪnt / (AmE), / tɛn pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a score or mark of ten out of a given total, often used in grading systems or competitions
a score or mark of ten

Examples

The essay received ten points out of a possible twenty.
She awarded ten points for creativity in the project.
Only teams scoring ten points or more advanced to the next round.

Synset Examples
