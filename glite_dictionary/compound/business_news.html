business news

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs nuːz / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs njuːz / (BrE)
news relating to companies, finance, and the economy
news about companies and finance

Examples

She reads the business news every morning to stay informed about the market.
The network launched a new show focused on business news and investment tips.
Recent business news has highlighted the rapid growth of the tech sector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: financial news - news covering finance and economic matters
