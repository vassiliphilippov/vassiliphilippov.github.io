jewel casket

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːəl ˌkæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːəl ˌkɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A small, often decorative box used for holding jewels or other valuables.
ornate box for storing jewels

Examples

She kept her grandmother’s pearls in a beautiful jewel casket on her dresser.
The ornate jewel casket was lined with blue velvet and decorated with silver filigree.
They presented the queen with a golden jewel casket on the occasion of her coronation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jewel box - a container for jewels or valuables
