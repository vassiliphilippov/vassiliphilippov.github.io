dustcart

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌst.kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈdʌst.kɑːt / (BrE)
(British) A vehicle used for collecting household garbage and refuse.
British refuse collection vehicle
british

Examples

The dustcart comes down our street every Friday morning to collect rubbish.
Residents are asked to leave their bins out for the dustcart before 7 a.m.
An orange dustcart blocked the road as workers emptied the wheelie bins.

Synset Examples

in Britain a garbage truck is called a ‘dustcart’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage truck - vehicle for collecting domestic refuse (American)

Etymology

Compound of ‘dust’ and ‘cart’, originally referring to a horse-drawn vehicle for collecting household ashes and rubbish.
