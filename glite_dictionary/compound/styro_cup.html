styro cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪroʊ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈstaɪrəʊ ˌkʌp / (BrE)
A disposable drinking cup made from Styrofoam, often used for serving hot or cold beverages.
disposable cup made of Styrofoam

Examples

She poured her tea into a styro cup before heading out the door.
After the meeting, empty styro cups were scattered around the table.
He prefers using a styro cup for his morning coffee because it keeps the drink hot.

Synset Examples

He bought a styro cup for his coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Styrofoam cup - a disposable cup made from Styrofoam

Etymology

Styrofoam is a trademarked brand of polystyrene foam. 'Styro cup' derives from the brand name.
