art department

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ɑːt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a school, college, or university responsible for teaching art and art appreciation
school or college division teaching art

Examples

After graduation, she took a job in the art department at the local university.
The school’s art department organizes regular exhibitions for students' work.
Funding for the art department was increased to improve facilities and resources.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: science department - the academic division responsible for teaching science subjects
