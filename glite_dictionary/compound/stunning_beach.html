stunning beach

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ ˌbiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
a beach that is extremely beautiful and impressive
exceptionally beautiful beach

Examples

We spent the afternoon relaxing on the stunning beach overlooking the turquoise sea.
Her photographs feature a stunning beach with white sand and palm trees.
Every year, tourists flock to the island's stunning beaches to enjoy the scenery.

Synset Examples

The Island is known for its stunning beaches.

Related Terms
