sea elephant

1. NOUN

/ si ˈɛlɪfənt / (AmE), / si ˈɛlɪfənt / (BrE)
Either of two large seal species with a trunk-like snout, found in northern Atlantic and southern waters, also known as elephant seals.
large seal with a trunk-like snout

Examples

The massive sea elephant hauled itself onto the shore to rest among the rocks.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of the sea elephant using satellite tags.
A male sea elephant can reach weights of up to 4,000 kilograms during mating season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elephant seal - large seal with trunk-like snout (Mirounga spp.)
