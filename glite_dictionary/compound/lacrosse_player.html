lacrosse player

1. NOUN

/ ləˈkrɑs ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ləˈkrɒs ˌpleɪə / (BrE)

athlete who plays lacrosse

Examples

The lacrosse player scored the winning goal in the final seconds of the match.
As a former lacrosse player, she now coaches the local youth team.
The university recruited a talented lacrosse player from out of state.
