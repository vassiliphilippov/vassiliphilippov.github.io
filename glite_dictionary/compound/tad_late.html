tad late

1. ADJ

/ tæd leɪt / (AmE), / tæd leɪt / (BrE)
Slightly late; a little late.
slightly late
informal

Examples

He apologized for being tad late to dinner, but the host was understanding.
Sorry, I’m just a tad late for our reservation.
The train was only a tad late this morning, which surprised everyone.

Synset Examples

He was a tad late to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a little late - slightly late

Etymology

From 'tad' (a small amount) with American English origins, combined with 'late.'
