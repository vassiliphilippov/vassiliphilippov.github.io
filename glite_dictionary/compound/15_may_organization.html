15 May Organization

1. PROPN

/ fɪfˈtiːn meɪ ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːn meɪ ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization formed in 1979 as a splinter group from the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, disbanded in the 1980s when members joined al-Fatah
Palestinian militant group (1979–1980s)

Examples

The 15 May Organization was responsible for several attacks during its brief existence in the late 1970s and early 1980s.
After the dissolution of the 15 May Organization, its members joined other Palestinian factions.
Historical accounts often mention the 15 May Organization when discussing militant groups in the Middle East.
