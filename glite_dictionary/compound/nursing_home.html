nursing home

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːsɪŋ hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsɪŋ həʊm / (BrE)
an institution providing residential care for elderly or disabled people who need assistance with daily activities and medical supervision
institution for elderly or disabled care

Examples

After her surgery, she stayed in a nursing home to recover and receive physical therapy.
Many families find it difficult to choose the right nursing home for their loved ones.
The new nursing home offers recreational activities and round-the-clock medical care.

Synset Examples

a home for the elderly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rest home - a place for people who need care but not intensive medical treatment
  • Antonym: hospital - institution for medical and surgical treatment
