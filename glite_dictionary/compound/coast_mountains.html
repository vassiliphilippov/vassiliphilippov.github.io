Coast Mountains

1. PROPN

/ koʊst ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / kəʊst ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a range of mountain ranges along the Pacific coast of North America, extending from southeastern Alaska through British Columbia to the interior of British Columbia
mountain range along Pacific coast of North America

Examples

The Coast Mountains are known for their stunning glaciers and rugged terrain.
Many rivers in British Columbia originate in the Coast Mountains.
Winter sports enthusiasts visit the Coast Mountains for excellent skiing opportunities.
