pitch shot

1. NOUN

/ pɪtʃ ʃɑt / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ʃɒt / (BrE)
a high, short approach shot in golf, intended to land softly on the green.
high approach golf shot

Examples

She used a pitch shot to get the ball over the bunker and onto the green.
A successful pitch shot can help save par in a difficult situation.
With only 30 yards to the flag, he chose a pitch shot rather than a chip.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: chip shot - a low, short golf shot close to the green
