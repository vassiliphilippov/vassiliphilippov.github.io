boner-killer

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊnɚ ˌkɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnə ˌkɪlə / (BrE)
Something that completely ruins excitement, pleasure, or enthusiasm, especially in an abrupt or disappointing way; a mood killer.
something that ruins excitement or pleasure
slang

Examples

The awkward ending to the movie was a real boner-killer for the audience.
His rude comment acted as a boner-killer during our celebration.
That news was such a boner-killer for everyone's mood.

Synset Examples

The awkward silence was a real boner-killer.
Her constant interruptions were a major boner-killer.
The sudden rain was a total boner-killer for our picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mood killer - something that spoils the mood or atmosphere
  • Synonym: buzzkill - a person or thing that ruins enjoyment

Etymology

From slang 'boner' (erection or blunder) + 'killer', indicating something that ends excitement.
