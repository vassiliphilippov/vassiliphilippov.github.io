too uptight

1. ADJ

/ tu ʌpˈtaɪt / (AmE), / tuː ʌpˈtaɪt / (BrE)
Excessively tense, nervous, or anxious, often to an extent that is noticeable or counterproductive.
excessively tense or anxious

Examples

She was too uptight to speak comfortably during the interview.
Don’t be too uptight about making mistakes when learning something new.
He gets too uptight about minor details at work.

Synset Examples

He was too uptight to enjoy the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uptight - tense, nervous, or overly formal
