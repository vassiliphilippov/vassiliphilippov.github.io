Sep 11

1. PROPN

/ sɛp ɪˈlɛvən / (AmE), / sɛp ɪˈlɛvən / (BrE)
September 11, 2001, the day when terrorist attacks were carried out in the United States by hijacked airliners.
the day of the 2001 terrorist attacks in the U.S.

Examples

The country commemorates Sep 11 with memorial services each year.
Security policies changed dramatically after Sep 11.
Many people remember exactly where they were on Sep 11.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 9/11 - the terrorist attacks on September 11, 2001, in the U.S.
  • Synonym: September 11 - the date of the 2001 terrorist attacks in the U.S.
