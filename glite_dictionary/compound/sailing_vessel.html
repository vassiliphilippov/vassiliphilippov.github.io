sailing vessel

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlɪŋ ˈvɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪŋ ˈvɛsəl / (BrE)
A boat or ship that is propelled by sails using the power of the wind, often with several masts.
boat or ship propelled by sails

Examples

The sailing vessel cut gracefully through the waves as the wind filled its sails.
Centuries ago, explorers crossed vast oceans in wooden sailing vessels.
The museum features a restored sailing vessel from the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sailboat - a small boat propelled by sails
