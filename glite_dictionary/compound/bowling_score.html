bowling score

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊlɪŋ skɔr / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋ skɔː / (BrE)
the total number of points achieved by a player or team in a game of bowling
total points in a bowling game

Examples

She was thrilled when her bowling score broke two hundred for the first time.
Every player's bowling score is displayed on the overhead monitor.
His low bowling score was a disappointment after such a strong start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score - the number of points won in a game or test
