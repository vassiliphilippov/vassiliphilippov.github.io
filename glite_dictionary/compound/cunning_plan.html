cunning plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌnɪŋ plæn / (AmE), / ˈkʌnɪŋ plan / (BrE)
a clever scheme or strategy, often designed to deceive or outwit someone
clever and usually deceitful scheme

Examples

Her cunning plan ensured she got the promotion she wanted.
The detective quickly saw through their cunning plan to steal the jewels.
Every good villain needs a cunning plan to carry out their scheme.

Synset Examples

Her cunning plan surprised everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scheme - a plan, often secret, to achieve something, especially by cheating or deceiving others
  • Synonym: plot - a secret plan to achieve something, especially one that is illegal or dishonest
