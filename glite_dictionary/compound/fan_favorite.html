fan favorite

1. NOUN

/ fæn ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / fan ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is especially popular or well-liked among fans, often more than others in the same context
person or thing especially liked by fans

Examples

The underdog quickly became a fan favorite after delivering a stunning performance in the semifinals.
That particular flavor is a fan favorite at the local ice cream shop.
The actor’s portrayal of the villain made him a surprise fan favorite on the show.

Synset Examples

The character named 'Peeniss' was a fan favorite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowd pleaser - something or someone that delights a large group of people
  • Synonym: underdog - competitor thought to have little chance of winning, but popular with fans
