calves' feet

1. NOUN

/ kævz fit / (AmE), / kɑːvz fiːt / (BrE)
the feet of calves, used in cooking, often prepared as a jelly or in soups
feet of calves used as a food

Examples

She made a traditional dish using calves' feet for the family gathering.
The butcher sold calves' feet to customers preparing jelly.
Recipes for calves' feet jelly were once common in old English cookbooks.
