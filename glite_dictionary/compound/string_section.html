string section

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɪŋ ˌsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈstrɪŋ ˌsɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the group of musicians in an orchestra who play stringed instruments such as violins, violas, cellos, and double basses
section of orchestra playing stringed instruments

Examples

The conductor gestured for the string section to begin the piece softly.
Several members of the string section practiced together before the rehearsal started.
The lush sound was achieved by having a large string section in the orchestra.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brass section - section of an orchestra that plays brass instruments
  • Antonym: woodwind section - section of an orchestra that plays woodwind instruments
