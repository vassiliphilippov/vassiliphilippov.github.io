dramatic production

1. NOUN

/ drəˈmætɪk pɹəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk pɹəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of staging and performing a play or drama, typically involving actors, direction, and other theatrical elements
the performance of a play or drama

Examples

The school is preparing a dramatic production of Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' this fall.
She was cast in the leading role of the community theater's latest dramatic production.
Years of experience in dramatic production gave him a deep appreciation of stagecraft.

Synset Examples

the group joined together in a dramatic production

Related Terms

  • Synonym: play - a dramatic work performed by actors
