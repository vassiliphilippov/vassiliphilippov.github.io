devil's weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz ˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ˌwiːd / (BrE)
a tropical poisonous plant with sprawling stems, known for its spiny fruit and as a harmful pasture weed
tropical poisonous plant; pasture weed

Examples

Ranchers have to watch out for devil's weed invading their pastures.
The fruit of devil's weed splits into five sharp, spiny parts.
Devil's weed can poison livestock if it's not removed from grazing areas.
