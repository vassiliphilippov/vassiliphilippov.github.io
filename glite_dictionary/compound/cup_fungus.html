cup fungus

1. NOUN

/ kʌp ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / kʌp ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
Any of a group of fungi with a cup-shaped fruiting body, belonging to the subclass Discomycetes or related groups.
fungus with a cup-shaped fruiting body

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with cup fungi after the heavy rains.
Some cup fungus species are brightly colored and easy to spot in leaf litter.
Scientists study the unique spores produced by cup fungus in various ecosystems.
