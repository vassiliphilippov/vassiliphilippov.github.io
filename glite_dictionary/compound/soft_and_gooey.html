soft and gooey

1. ADJ

/ sɑft ənd ˈɡu.i / (AmE), / sɒft ənd ˈɡuː.i / (BrE)
having a texture that is both easily yielding to pressure and sticky or viscous, often used to describe foods
soft and sticky texture

Examples

The brownies came out soft and gooey just the way I like them.
She loves her cookies soft and gooey in the middle.
The cheese was soft and gooey after being melted on the pizza.

Synset Examples

The chocolate cake was soft and gooey in the center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gooey - sticky and soft; viscous
