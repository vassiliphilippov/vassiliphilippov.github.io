most especially

1. ADV

/ moʊst ɪˈspɛʃəli / (AmE), / məʊst ɪˈspɛʃəli / (BrE)
Used to emphasize something as being more important or true than anything else.
emphasizing something above all else

Examples

He enjoys all kinds of music, but he loves jazz most especially.
Everyone should be careful, most especially children crossing the road.
I am grateful for all your help, most especially during the difficult times.

Synset Examples

above all, you must be independent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: especially - to an exceptional degree; particularly
  • Synonym: above all - more than anything else or anyone else
