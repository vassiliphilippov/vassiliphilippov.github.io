five-year term

1. NOUN

/ faɪv jɪr tɜrm / (AmE), / faɪv jɪə tɜːm / (BrE)
a period of five years specified as the length of a contract, lease, or agreement
a five-year period in a contract or lease

Examples

The lease includes a five-year term with the option to renew afterward.
Her contract specifies a five-year term starting on the date of signature.
We agreed to a five-year term for the loan to secure better rates.

Synset Examples

The lease has a five-year term.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: term - a fixed period of time for which something lasts
