inch tracker

1. NOUN

/ ɪntʃ ˈtrækər / (AmE), / ɪntʃ ˈtrækə / (BrE)
a tool or device used to measure or monitor small increments of progress, especially in activities like fitness or personal goals
tool for measuring small progress

Examples

The new software update includes an inch tracker to help users visualize their daily productivity.
I rely on my inch tracker to keep motivated when improvements are small but steady.
Their inch tracker provided visual encouragement as she worked toward her long-term goal.

Synset Examples

The fitness app acts as an inch tracker for my progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: progress tracker - a tool to monitor progress
  • Degree Lower: pedometer - device measuring number of steps taken
  • Degree Higher: milestone tracker - tool for recording major achievements
