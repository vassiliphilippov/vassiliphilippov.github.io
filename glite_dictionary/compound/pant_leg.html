pant leg

1. NOUN

/ pænt lɛɡ / (AmE), / pænt leɡ / (BrE)
the part of a pair of trousers or pants that covers one leg
one leg portion of trousers or pants

Examples

There's a tear in the pant leg that needs to be mended.
After walking through the mud, his pant leg was completely dirty.
She rolled up her pant leg to wade through the stream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trouser leg - the leg portion of a pair of trousers
