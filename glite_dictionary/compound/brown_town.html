brown town

1. NOUN

/ braʊn taʊn / (AmE), / braʊn taʊn / (BrE)
(slang) A humorous metaphor for a state of gastrointestinal distress, such as diarrhea, or a figurative place representing an embarrassing or unpleasant situation.
slang for gastrointestinal distress or bad situation
slang

Examples

After eating those questionable tacos, he was afraid he'd end up in brown town all night.
The comedian made everyone laugh by describing his trip to brown town after the chili contest.
Whenever my nerves get the better of me, I find myself visiting brown town before big presentations.

Synset Examples

He joked that the roller coaster would take them to brown town.
The spicy curry might take you to brown town later.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - frequent loose or liquid bowel movements
  • Synonym: upset stomach - condition of digestive discomfort, often leading to diarrhea
