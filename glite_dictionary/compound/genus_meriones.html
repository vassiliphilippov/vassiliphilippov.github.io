genus Meriones

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːnəs məˈraɪəˌniːz / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs mɛˈrɪəˌniːz / (BrE)
A genus of rodents in the family Cricetidae, commonly known as jirds.
a genus of rodents (jirds)

Examples

The genus Meriones includes several species of desert-dwelling rodents known as jirds.
Biologists have identified numerous adaptations in the genus Meriones for surviving arid environments.
Research on the genus Meriones has contributed to our understanding of rodent evolution in Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jird - a type of rodent belonging to the genus Meriones
  • Synonym: gerbil - a small rodent related to jirds

Etymology

From New Latin Meriones, the genus name, after the Greek name ‘Meriones’.
