Billy the Kid

1. PROPN

/ ˌbɪli ðə ˈkɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɪli ðə ˈkɪd / (BrE)
A famous American outlaw of the Old West, reputed to have killed 21 men, active during the late 19th century.
notorious American outlaw (1859–1881)

Examples

The legend of Billy the Kid has inspired countless books and films about the Wild West.
Some historians question whether Billy the Kid really killed as many people as claimed.
Tourists often visit sites in New Mexico associated with Billy the Kid.
