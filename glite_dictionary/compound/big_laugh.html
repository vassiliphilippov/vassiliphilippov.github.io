big laugh

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ læf / (AmE), / bɪɡ lɑːf / (BrE)
a loud, hearty, or very noticeable laugh, often in reaction to something funny
a loud or hearty laugh

Examples

His joke got a big laugh from everyone in the room.
She burst out with a big laugh at the surprising ending.
The kids gave a big laugh when the clown slipped on the banana peel.

Synset Examples

The comedian got a big laugh after the rimshot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly laugh - a loud, deep and hearty laugh
