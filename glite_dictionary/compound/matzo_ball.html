matzo ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːtsoʊ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈmæt.səʊ bɔːl / (BrE)
A small dumpling made from matzo meal, traditionally served in soup as part of Jewish cuisine.
Jewish dumpling made from matzo meal

Examples

Grandma always makes her famous matzo ball soup for Passover dinner.
The restaurant offers chicken soup with a large matzo ball floating on top.
I've never tried a matzo ball before, but it was delicious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dumpling - small ball of dough often cooked in liquid or soup

Etymology

From Yiddish 'matse' or 'matzo' (unleavened bread) and English 'ball'.
