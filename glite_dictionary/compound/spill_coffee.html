to spill coffee

1. VERB

/ spɪl ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / spɪl ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to accidentally pour coffee out of a cup or container, often causing a stain or mess
to accidentally pour coffee and cause a mess

Examples

Be careful not to spill coffee on the laptop during the meeting.
She groaned when she spilled coffee all over her desk.
It’s easy to spill coffee if your cup is too full.

Synset Examples

She managed to spill coffee on her favorite shirt.
He used a rag to wipe the spilled coffee.
She spilled coffee on her shirt, dag!
Goddessdammit, I spilled coffee all over my shirt!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spill - to accidentally pour liquid out of a container
