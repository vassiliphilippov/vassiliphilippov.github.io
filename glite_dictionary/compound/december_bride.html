December bride

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsɛmbɚ braɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛmbə braɪd / (BrE)
a woman who gets married in the month of December
bride married in December

Examples

The December bride looked radiant as she walked down the aisle with snow falling gently outside.
Many couples dream of becoming December brides to have a magical winter wedding.
As a December bride, she loved the festive atmosphere and holiday decorations at her reception.
