dampener for morale

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæmpənər fər məˈræl / (AmE), / ˈdampənə fə məˈrɑːl / (BrE)
something that reduces the enthusiasm, spirit, or confidence of a group or person
something that reduces morale

Examples

The sudden resignation of the team leader was a real dampener for morale.
Budget cuts have acted as a dampener for morale throughout the department.
Constant criticism can become a dampener for morale in any workplace.

Synset Examples

The news of the layoffs was a dampener for morale.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boost for morale - something that increases morale or confidence
  • Synonym: blow to morale - an event that badly affects morale
