party liner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti ˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a member of a political party who strictly adheres to the official policies and positions of that party
person who follows party line strictly

Examples

He was known as a party liner, never opposing the party's policies.
The debate featured a mix of independent thinkers and strict party liners.
Critics accused her of being a party liner who never questioned leadership decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partisan - a strong supporter of a party, cause, or person
