local agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
an organization or business operating in a specific area and providing services to people in that community
organization providing local services

Examples

She contacted a local agency to help her find a job.
Many families adopt pets through a local agency rather than a national group.
The local agency offers support to small businesses in the area.

Synset Examples

They found a reliable nanny through a local agency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agency - an organization that provides a particular service
