Swiss canton

1. NOUN

/ swɪs ˈkæn.tɑn / (AmE), / swɪs ˈkæn.tɒn / (BrE)
one of the administrative divisions, called cantons, that make up the federation of Switzerland
administrative division of Switzerland

Examples

Geneva is a well-known Swiss canton famous for international organizations and diplomacy.
Each Swiss canton has its own constitution and considerable autonomy from the federal government.
The tradition of neutrality is deeply rooted in every Swiss canton across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canton - an administrative division of a country, especially in Switzerland
