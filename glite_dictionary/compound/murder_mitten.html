murder mitten

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdɚ ˈmɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə ˈmɪtn̩ / (BrE)
a glove linked to the commission of a murder or associated with a criminal act
glove used in a murder or crime

Examples

The detective carefully placed the murder mitten into an evidence bag at the scene.
Police believe the murder mitten may contain vital fingerprints.
The suspect tried to dispose of the murder mitten after committing the crime.

Synset Examples
