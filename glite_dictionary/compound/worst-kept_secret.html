worst-kept secret

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɝːst ˈkɛpt ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˌwɜːst ˈkɛpt ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
A fact that is supposed to be secret but is actually widely known by many people.
widely known supposed secret

Examples

The promotion was the company's worst-kept secret long before it was officially announced.
It became the worst-kept secret in the office that he was applying for another job.
Their relationship was the worst-kept secret in our friend group.

Synset Examples

It’s the worst-kept secret that she’s leaving the company.
Their engagement was the worst-kept secret in town.
This party is the worst-kept secret among colleagues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: open secret - something that is supposed to be secret but is widely known
