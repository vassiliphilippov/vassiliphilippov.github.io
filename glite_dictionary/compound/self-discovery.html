self-discovery

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf dɪˈskʌvəri / (AmE), / sɛlf dɪˈskʌvəri / (BrE)
the process of gaining an understanding of your own character, feelings, or individuality
process of understanding oneself

Examples

Traveling alone was an important journey of self-discovery for her.
Many people believe that meditation can aid in self-discovery.
Writing in a journal has helped him with his own self-discovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-knowledge - understanding of oneself or one's own motives or character
