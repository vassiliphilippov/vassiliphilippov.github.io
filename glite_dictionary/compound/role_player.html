role player

1. NOUN

/ roʊl ˈpleɪ.ər / (AmE), / rəʊl ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who performs a role in a play, film, or other dramatic production
theatrical performer or actor

Examples

After years as a role player in community theater, he finally landed a leading part.
Each role player brings something unique to the ensemble cast.
Aspiring actors often start as a role player before taking on starring roles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - person who performs in plays or movies
  • Synonym: actress - female performer in plays or movies
