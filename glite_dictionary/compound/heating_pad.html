heating pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtɪŋ pæd / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ pad / (BrE)
A flexible pad containing electrical heating elements, used to apply heat to a part of the body for warmth or pain relief.
electrically heated flexible pad

Examples

She applied a heating pad to her sore back for relief.
During the winter, I use a heating pad to keep my feet warm at night.
The doctor recommended using a heating pad for muscle spasms.
