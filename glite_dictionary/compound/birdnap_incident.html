birdnap incident

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌnæp ˈɪn.sɪ.dənt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌnap ˈɪn.sɪ.dənt / (BrE)
an incident involving the illegal capture of birds
an event of illegal bird capture

Examples

The reserve increased security after a birdnap incident threatened the local parrot population.
Authorities launched an investigation following a birdnap incident at the aviary.
News of the recent birdnap incident alarmed conservationists across the country.

Synset Examples

The wildlife group was outraged by the birdnap incident last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poaching - illegal hunting or capturing of wild animals
  • Synonym: kidnap incident - an incident involving the abduction of a person

Etymology

Blend of 'bird' and 'kidnap'; 'incident' from Latin 'incidentem' (falling upon).
