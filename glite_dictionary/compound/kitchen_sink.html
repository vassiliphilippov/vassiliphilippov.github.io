kitchen sink

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
A large basin with a water supply, installed in a kitchen and used for washing dishes and other kitchen tasks.
basin for washing in a kitchen

Examples

The plumber came to fix the leaking kitchen sink yesterday.
She rinsed the vegetables in the kitchen sink before preparing dinner.
Dishes piled up in the kitchen sink after the party.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
a large or exhaustive variety of things, covering every possible option or element, often to excess
everything possible; a wide variety

Examples

The recipe includes spices, herbs, and even fruit—she really put the kitchen sink into it.
His essay tried to cover every argument, throwing in the kitchen sink for good measure.
They packed the car with clothes, snacks, and electronics—almost the whole kitchen sink.

Synset Examples

His presentation had the kitchen sink of ideas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everything but the kitchen sink - almost everything imaginable; nearly every possible thing has been included
