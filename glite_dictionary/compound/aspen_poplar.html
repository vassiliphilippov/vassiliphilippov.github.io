aspen poplar

1. NOUN

/ ˈæspən ˈpɑplɚ / (AmE), / ˈæspən ˈpɒplə / (BrE)
a species of poplar tree widely cultivated in North America, characterized by white bark and leaves with pale undersides
a poplar tree with white bark and pale leaves

Examples

The aspen poplar is known for its smooth, white bark and delicate, trembling leaves.
In autumn, the aspen poplar displays brilliant yellow foliage along the mountain roads.
Many birds build their nests in the branches of the aspen poplar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poplar - a type of tall tree with light, soft wood and leaves that tremble in the wind
  • Synonym: aspen - a type of tree with leaves that move easily in the wind, especially Populus tremula or Populus tremuloides
