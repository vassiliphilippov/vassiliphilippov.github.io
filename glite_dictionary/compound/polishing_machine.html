polishing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑlɪʃɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine equipped with an abrasive wheel for polishing the surfaces of various materials and objects
machine for polishing surfaces

Examples

The technician used a polishing machine to give the metal surface a smooth, shiny finish.
Marble floors are often restored with a commercial polishing machine.
Operating a polishing machine requires safety equipment to protect against flying debris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffer - a machine for smoothing or shining surfaces, often used synonymously with a polishing machine
