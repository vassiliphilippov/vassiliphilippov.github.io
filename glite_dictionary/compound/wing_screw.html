wing screw

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋ skuː / (AmE), / wɪŋ skuː / (BrE)
a screw with flat wing-like projections to enable turning by hand without tools
screw with winged projections for hand turning

Examples

Tighten the panel securely with a wing screw so it can be removed without tools.
He replaced the lost wing screw to keep the battery compartment closed.
The wing screw design makes adjustments quick and easy on the go.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wing nut - a nut with projections for turning by hand
