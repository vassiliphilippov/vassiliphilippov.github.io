dressing case

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsɪŋ keɪs / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ keɪs / (BrE)
a small suitcase or container designed to hold brushes, bottles, and toiletries for travel
small luggage for toiletries

Examples

She packed her dressing case with all the essentials for her business trip.
Victorian travelers often carried a dressing case made of leather and silver fittings.
His antique dressing case included a set of engraved brushes and glass bottles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vanity case - small container for holding cosmetics and personal items
