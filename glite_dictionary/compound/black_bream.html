black bream

1. NOUN

/ blæk briːm / (AmE), / blæk briːm / (BrE)
an important dark-colored edible fish of Australia, valued as both food and game
an edible fish native to Australia

Examples

The black bream is a popular catch among anglers in southern Australia.
Many restaurants serve fresh black bream with seasonal vegetables.
Researchers are studying the breeding habits of the black bream in local rivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bream - a species of freshwater or marine fish
