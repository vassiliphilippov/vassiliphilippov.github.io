spurred gentian

1. NOUN

/ spɝd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / spɜːd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Halenia, noted for their flowers with spurred lobes.
plant of the genus Halenia with spurred flowers

Examples

We spotted a rare spurred gentian growing by the edge of the forest trail.
The unique shape of the spurred gentian's flowers makes it easy to identify in the wild.
Several species of spurred gentian are found in mountainous regions of North America.
