billiard player

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪljɚd ˌpleɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays billiards, either recreationally or professionally
a person who plays billiards

Examples

She is a skilled billiard player who has won several local tournaments.
As a billiard player, he practices for hours to perfect his shots.
Many famous billiard players started their careers in small, neighborhood clubs.

Related Terms
