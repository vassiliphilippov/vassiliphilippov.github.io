masquerade ball

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæskəˌreɪd ˈbɔl / (AmE), / ˌmɑːskəˌreɪd ˈbɔːl / (BrE)
a formal party or dance at which guests wear costumes and masks to conceal their identities
costumed ball with masks

Examples

The count hosted a lavish masquerade ball in his ancestral mansion.
Many guests arrived in elaborate costumes at the annual masquerade ball.
She lost her mask during the masquerade ball, revealing her identity to everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: costume party - a party where guests wear costumes

Etymology

From Italian *mascherata* (masked entertainment) and French *bal* (dance).
