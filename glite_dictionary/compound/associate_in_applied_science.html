Associate in Applied Science

1. NOUN

/ əˈsoʊʃiət ɪn əˈplaɪd ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / əˈsəʊʃiət ɪn əˈplaɪd ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
a two-year academic degree awarded by colleges and universities for studies in applied science, typically focusing on practical and technical skills
two-year applied science degree

Examples

After earning her Associate in Applied Science, she found a job as a lab technician.
Many community colleges offer an Associate in Applied Science in nursing.
He completed an Associate in Applied Science before transferring to a four-year university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Associate's degree - a two-year undergraduate degree awarded by colleges and universities
