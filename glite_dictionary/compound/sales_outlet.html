sales outlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlz ˌaʊt.lət / (AmE), / ˈseɪlz ˌaʊt.lət / (BrE)
a place where goods are sold directly to customers, typically a shop or store
retail shop or place for selling goods

Examples

The company opened a new sales outlet in the city center last month.
Many customers prefer to see products in a sales outlet before buying online.
The supermarket chain has over fifty sales outlets across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: store - a place where goods are sold to the public
  • Synonym: shop - British term for a retail place selling goods
