Lebanese pound

1. NOUN

/ ˌlɛbəˈniz paʊnd / (AmE), / ˌliːbəˈniːz paʊnd / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Lebanon, equal to 100 piasters
currency of Lebanon

Examples

The exchange rate for the Lebanese pound has fluctuated dramatically in recent years.
He paid the taxi driver ten thousand Lebanese pounds for the short ride.
Travelers are advised to carry cash in Lebanese pounds when visiting rural areas of Lebanon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lira - another name for the Lebanese pound
