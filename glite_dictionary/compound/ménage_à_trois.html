ménage à trois

1. NOUN

/ meɪˌnɑːʒ ɑ ˈtrwɑː / (AmE), / meɪˌnɑːʒ ɑ ˈtrwɑː / (BrE)
an arrangement in which three people, typically a married couple and another person, live together and share sexual relations
relationship involving three cohabiting people

Examples

The novel explores the complexities of a ménage à trois and challenges traditional notions of marriage.
Rumors about the celebrity's alleged ménage à trois quickly spread in the tabloids.
They entered into a ménage à trois, attempting to balance love, jealousy, and open communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threesome - a sexual activity involving three people

Etymology

From French, literally 'household of three'.
