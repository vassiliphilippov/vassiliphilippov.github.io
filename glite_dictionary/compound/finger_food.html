finger food

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡər fuːd / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə fuːd / (BrE)
food that is designed to be eaten with the fingers, rather than with utensils
food eaten with the fingers

Examples

At the party, a variety of finger food was served on colorful platters.
Small sandwiches and spring rolls are popular types of finger food at receptions.
He prefers to serve only finger food at his gatherings for easy cleanup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hors d'oeuvre - a small dish served before the main part of a meal, often eaten with fingers
