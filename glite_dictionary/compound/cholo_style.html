cholo style

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃoʊ.loʊ staɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊ.ləʊ staɪl / (BrE)
A distinctive fashion and aesthetic associated with Mexican-American urban culture, often featuring baggy clothing, bandanas, tattoos, and specific hairstyles.
Mexican-American urban fashion style
informal

Examples

His cholo style was evident in the crisp white T-shirt, baggy khakis, and carefully groomed mustache.
The film showcased the cholo style popular in Los Angeles during the 1990s.
She added elements of cholo style to her wardrobe, inspired by lowrider culture.

Synset Examples

She admired his cholo style, especially his tattoos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cholo - member of a Mexican-American urban subculture

Etymology

‘Cholo’ from Mexican Spanish, originally a term for people of mixed Indigenous and European descent; style refers to the fashion and aesthetics developed within this cultural context.
