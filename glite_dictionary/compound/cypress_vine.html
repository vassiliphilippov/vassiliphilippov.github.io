cypress vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪprəs vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪprəs vaɪn / (BrE)
a tropical American climbing plant with finely divided leaves and red or sometimes white, star-shaped flowers, often grown as an ornamental
ornamental climbing plant with red flowers

Examples

Every summer, the cypress vine covers the trellis in a mass of bright red blooms.
She planted cypress vine along the fence to add some color to her garden.
The finely cut leaves of the cypress vine make it a favorite among gardeners.
