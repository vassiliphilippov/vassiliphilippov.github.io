domestic partner

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈpɑːrtnər / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person with whom one lives and shares a long-term intimate relationship, without being married
person in a long-term cohabiting relationship

Examples

He added his domestic partner to his health insurance plan.
Many companies provide benefits for employees and their domestic partners.
After living together for five years, they registered as domestic partners under state law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spouse - a husband or wife
  • Degree Lower: boyfriend - male partner in a romantic or sexual relationship
  • Degree Lower: girlfriend - female partner in a romantic or sexual relationship
