Elliott's goldenrod

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛliəts ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪəts ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
a species of goldenrod (Solidago latissimifolia), a flowering plant native to North America
a type of goldenrod plant

Examples

We saw several clusters of Elliott's goldenrod growing near the edge of the wetland.
The botanist explained how Elliott's goldenrod differs from other goldenrod species.
Gardeners appreciate Elliott's goldenrod for its showy yellow blooms in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldenrod - a plant of the genus Solidago with yellow flowers

Etymology

Named after Stephen Elliott, an American botanist.
