orange grove

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ɡroʊv / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ɡrəʊv / (BrE)
A piece of land planted with orange trees, usually cultivated for commercial fruit production.
area planted with orange trees

Examples

They bought an old orange grove and now sell fresh juice at a local market.
The air was filled with the scent of blossoms in the springtime orange grove.
A tour of the orange grove showed us how the fruit is harvested and packed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grove - a small group of trees
