law court

1. NOUN

/ lɔ kɔrt / (AmE), / lɔː kɔːt / (BrE)
A tribunal where legal cases are heard and justice is administered by a judge or judges.
tribunal where legal cases are heard

Examples

The criminal was brought before the law court to face charges against him.
All disputes over land ownership must be settled by a law court.
She works as a clerk in the local law court downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court of law - A formal legal tribunal where cases are adjudicated
  • Synonym: tribunal - A type of court or assembly with judicial authority
