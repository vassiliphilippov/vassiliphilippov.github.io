spastic gesture

1. NOUN

/ ˈspæstɪk ˈdʒɛstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈspæstɪk ˈdʒɛstʃə / (BrE)
an uncontrolled, awkward bodily movement, typically characterized by sudden or jerky motions
uncontrolled, awkward movement

Examples

The comedian's spastic gestures made everyone laugh.
The patient's spastic gestures were a result of his neurological condition.
She tried to control her spastic gesture, but her hand jerked unexpectedly.

Synset Examples
