sporting goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtɪŋ ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ ɡʊdz / (BrE)
equipment and gear used for sports or physical activities, typically sold in stores as merchandise
equipment and gear for sports

Examples

The store offers a wide selection of sporting goods, from tennis rackets to footballs.
He spent the afternoon browsing for new sporting goods at the mall.
Many sporting goods manufacturers now produce eco-friendly equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports equipment - items used in playing sports
  • Synonym: athletic gear - clothing and equipment for athletic activities
