easy chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːzi tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈiːzi tʃɛː / (BrE)
A large, comfortable, upholstered armchair designed for relaxation.
comfortable upholstered armchair

Examples

He settled into his easy chair with a good book after a long day.
The living room featured a plush sofa and an easy chair by the fireplace.
She bought a new easy chair to make her reading nook more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
