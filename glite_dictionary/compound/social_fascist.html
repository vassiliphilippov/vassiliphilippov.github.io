social fascist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates or enforces extremely authoritarian or intolerant social policies or views
person with extremely authoritarian social views

Examples

Many activists accused the new legislator of being a social fascist because of her rigid policies.
Critics often brand those opposing social freedoms as social fascists in heated political debates.
He resented being called a social fascist for holding traditional views on these issues.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a social fascist for his extreme views.
Many dismissed his arguments as the rhetoric of a social fascist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authoritarian - favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority
  • Antonym: liberal - open to new behavior or opinions, opposite of intolerant

Etymology

Formed from 'social' and 'fascist', originally political jargon, with 'fascist' from Italian fascista (member of a fascio).
