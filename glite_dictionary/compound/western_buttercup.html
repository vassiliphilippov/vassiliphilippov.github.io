western buttercup

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn ˈbʌtərˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈbʌtəkʌp / (BrE)
a perennial wildflower of western North America, belonging to the buttercup family, with glossy yellow flowers
perennial wildflower of western North America

Examples

The western buttercup blooms in early spring across meadows in California.
Hikers admired the bright yellow petals of the western buttercup along the trail.
Many gardeners plant western buttercups to attract bees and other pollinators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttercup - any of various plants of the genus Ranunculus, typically with yellow flowers
