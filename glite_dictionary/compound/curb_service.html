curb service

1. NOUN

/ kɝb ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / kɜːb ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
A service provided to customers who remain in their vehicles, typically where orders are taken and delivered to the car curbside.
service for customers in their vehicles

Examples

Some diners still offer curb service for customers who prefer to stay in their cars.
During the pandemic, many restaurants expanded curb service to reduce contact inside the building.
We enjoyed classic burgers with curb service at the retro drive-in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive-in - a place where customers are served while staying in their car
  • Antonym: takeout - food purchased to be eaten away from the place of sale
