laying on of hands

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪɪŋ ɑn əv hændz / (AmE), / ˈleɪɪŋ ɒn əv hændz / (BrE)
the ritual act of placing one's hands on a person's head to invoke spiritual blessing, especially during Christian ordination or healing ceremonies
ritual act of blessing by touch in Christianity

Examples

During the ordination, the priest performed the laying on of hands to bless the new minister.
The laying on of hands is a tradition in many Christian denominations for granting authority or healing.
Many believers attribute miraculous recoveries to the laying on of hands during prayer services.
