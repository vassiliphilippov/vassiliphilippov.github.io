genus Solanum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs soʊˈleɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs səˈleɪnəm / (BrE)
the type genus of the Solanaceae family, including plants such as nightshade, potato, and eggplant
plant genus in the nightshade family

Examples

The genus Solanum includes both the common potato and the eggplant.
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to the genus Solanum in South America.
Many plants in the genus Solanum contain alkaloids that can be toxic to humans.

Etymology

From Latin 'Solanum', the genus name for nightshades.
