shoe shop

1. NOUN

/ ʃu ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃuː ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where shoes are sold.
a store selling shoes

Examples

I went to the shoe shop to buy a new pair of sneakers.
Many shoe shops offer discounts during holiday sales.
The shoe shop on Main Street has a great selection of boots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoe store - a store selling shoes, mainly American English
