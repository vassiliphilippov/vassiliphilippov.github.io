squash ball

1. NOUN

/ skwɑʃ bɔl / (AmE), / skwɒʃ bɔːl / (BrE)
a small rubber ball used in the game of squash
ball used in squash

Examples

He forgot to pack his squash ball before heading to the courts.
The squash ball bounced erratically off the back wall during the match.
She bought a new squash ball because the old one had lost its bounce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squash - a fast-paced racquet sport played by two players in a four-walled court
