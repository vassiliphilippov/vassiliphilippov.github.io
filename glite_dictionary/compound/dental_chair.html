dental chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl tʃɛə / (BrE)
A specialized chair in a dentist's office designed to position patients comfortably during dental procedures.
special chair used by dentists for patients

Examples

The hygienist asked me to sit in the dental chair before the examination began.
The dental chair can be adjusted to help the dentist reach different areas of your mouth.
Modern dental chairs often come equipped with built-in lights and sinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentist's chair - chair used by a dentist for patient treatment
