inspiring speech

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈspaɪɚɪŋ spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ spiːtʃ / (BrE)
a speech that motivates, encourages, or uplifts listeners
motivational or uplifting speech

Examples

The coach gave an inspiring speech before the team went out to play.
Her inspiring speech brought the audience to its feet in applause.
He remembered the inspiring speech his teacher gave him at graduation.

Synset Examples

JFK was known for his inspiring speeches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motivational speech - a speech designed to motivate or inspire an audience
  • Antonym: discouraging speech - a speech that demotivates or disheartens listeners
