Republic of Finland

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈfɪnlænd / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈfɪnlənd / (BrE)
the official name of Finland, a republic in northern Europe that gained independence from Russia in 1917
official name of Finland (country in northern Europe)

Examples

The Republic of Finland celebrates its Independence Day on December 6th each year.
After declaring independence in 1917, the Republic of Finland established its own government and constitution.
The Republic of Finland is known for its high standard of living and beautiful natural landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finland - country in northern Europe
