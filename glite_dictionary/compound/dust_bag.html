dust bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌst bӕɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʌst bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag in a vacuum cleaner that collects dust and dirt sucked in during cleaning
bag in vacuum cleaner for dust

Examples

The dust bag in my vacuum cleaner needs to be changed regularly.
She replaced the full dust bag before cleaning the carpet again.
Many modern vacuums have a removable dust bag for easy disposal of dirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter bag - a bag that filters particles from air or gas, often used in machinery
