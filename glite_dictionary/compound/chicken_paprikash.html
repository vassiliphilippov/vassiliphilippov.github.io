chicken paprikash

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɪk.ɪn ˈpɑː.prɪ.kæʃ / (AmE), / ˌtʃɪk.ɪn ˈpæp.rɪ.kæʃ / (BrE)
a Hungarian dish of chicken simmered in broth with onions and paprika, typically finished with sour cream
Hungarian chicken dish with paprika and sour cream

Examples

We ordered chicken paprikash at the new Hungarian restaurant downtown.
Her grandmother's recipe for chicken paprikash uses extra paprika for a richer flavor.
I've never tried making chicken paprikash before, but it looks delicious.

Etymology

Hungarian 'paprikás', from 'paprika' (the spice), reflecting the dish's key ingredient.
