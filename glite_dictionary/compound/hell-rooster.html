hell-rooster

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlˌɹuːstər / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌɹuːstə / (BrE)
a person who is extremely aggressive or fierce in fighting
very fierce fighter

Examples

The boxer gained a reputation as a hell-rooster in the ring, never backing down from a challenge.
During the debate, she became a verbal hell-rooster, fiercely defending her opinions.
They hired him for security because his hell-rooster attitude discouraged any troublemakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warrior - a brave or experienced fighter, especially in past societies
  • Antonym: pacifist - someone who is opposed to violence, especially war
