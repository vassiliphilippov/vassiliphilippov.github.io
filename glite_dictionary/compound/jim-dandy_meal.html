jim-dandy meal

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm ˈdændi miːl / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈdændi miːl / (BrE)
An excellent or outstanding meal.
outstanding meal

Examples

After a long day of hiking, we sat down to a jim-dandy meal by the campfire.
She prepared a jim-dandy meal for her parents' anniversary celebration.
That restaurant always serves a jim-dandy meal whenever we visit.

Synset Examples

He served a jim-dandy meal that impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feast - a large meal, typically one in celebration
  • Synonym: banquet - an elaborate and formal meal for many people

Etymology

From "jim-dandy" (an excellent person or thing), origin uncertain but attested in 19th-century American English.
