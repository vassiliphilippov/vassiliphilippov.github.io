pole jumping

1. NOUN

/ poʊl ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / pəʊl ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or competition of jumping over a high crossbar using a long pole for assistance
jumping over a bar with a pole (sport)

Examples

He decided to take up pole jumping after being inspired by the Olympics.
The school offers lessons in pole jumping for students interested in track and field events.
Mastering the technique of pole jumping requires strength, agility, and precise timing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vault - the athletic event of jumping over a high bar using a pole
