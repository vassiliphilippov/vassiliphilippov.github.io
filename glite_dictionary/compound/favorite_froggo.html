favorite froggo

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərət ˈfrɑːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt ˈfrɒɡəʊ / (BrE)
A playful and affectionate term for someone who is endearing or funny, often used among friends or children.
playful term for an endearing or funny person
slang informal

Examples

Whenever she made everyone laugh, she became their favorite froggo for the day.
They affectionately called their class clown the favorite froggo of second grade.
You're my favorite froggo whenever you do that silly dance.

Synset Examples

The kids giggled and called him their favorite froggo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: froggo - slang term for a frog or endearing person

Etymology

Formed from 'favorite' and 'froggo', the latter being a slang diminutive or playful variant of 'frog' in internet culture.
