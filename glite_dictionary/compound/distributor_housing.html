distributor housing

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstrɪb.jəˌtɚ ˈhaʊ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪb.jʊ.tə ˈhaʊ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
the metal casing or enclosure that supports and protects the distributor cam in an internal combustion engine
part of an engine that holds the distributor cam

Examples

The mechanic removed the distributor housing to inspect the cam for wear.
A crack in the distributor housing can lead to ignition problems in your vehicle.
Always ensure the distributor housing is properly sealed to prevent moisture from entering the engine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distributor - device in an engine that directs high voltage to the spark plugs
