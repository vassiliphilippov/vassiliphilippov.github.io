ketchup bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛtʃʌp ˌbɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛtʃəp ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a bottle designed to hold and dispense ketchup
bottle for holding ketchup

Examples

He grabbed the ketchup bottle from the table and squeezed some onto his fries.
The restaurant refilled each ketchup bottle every morning before opening.
She accidentally dropped the ketchup bottle, and it spilled all over the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle - a container with a narrow neck used for storing liquids
