zipless design

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪpləs dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈzɪpləs dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style of clothing or accessory made without zippers, often intended to look elegant or streamlined
design without zippers

Examples

The zipless design adds elegance to the outfit.
Her dress features a zipless design, making it easy to slip on and off.
Many modern handbags now use a zipless design for a minimalist look.
