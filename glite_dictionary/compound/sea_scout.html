Sea Scout

1. NOUN

/ si skaʊt / (AmE), / si skaʊt / (BrE)
a member of a branch of the Boy Scouts or similar organization that specializes in boating and water-based activities
member of water-focused Scout branch

Examples

After joining the Sea Scouts, she quickly learned how to sail and navigate.
The Sea Scouts took part in a weekend canoeing trip along the coast.
As a Sea Scout, he earned badges for swimming and boating activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scout - a member of the Scout movement, especially a Boy Scout or Girl Scout
