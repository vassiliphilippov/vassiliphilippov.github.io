pipe vine

1. NOUN

/ paɪp vaɪn / (AmE), / paɪp vaɪn / (BrE)
A hardy deciduous vine with large leaves and curved, pipe-shaped flowers.
vine with pipe-shaped flowers

Examples

The pipe vine in her garden attracted dozens of butterflies during the summer.
Gardeners appreciate the pipe vine for its unique, curved flowers and rapid growth.
He planted a pipe vine along the fence to create a natural screen.
