blooming-fool begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːmɪŋ fuːl bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈbluːmɪŋ fuːl bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
A hybrid variety of winter-blooming begonia cultivated for its numerous large pink flowers.
hybrid winter-blooming begonia with large pink flowers

Examples

The blooming-fool begonia is prized by gardeners for its striking pink blooms during winter.
She added a blooming-fool begonia to her collection to brighten up the greenhouse.
Each year, the blooming-fool begonia displays a spectacular array of flowers despite the cold weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of perennial flowering plants often grown as ornamentals
