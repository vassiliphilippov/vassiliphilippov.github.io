western skink

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərn skɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən skɪŋk / (BrE)
a small, smooth-scaled lizard (Plestiodon skiltonianus) found in grasslands and open woodlands of western North America
a lizard species of western North America

Examples

We spotted a western skink basking on a sunny rock during our hike.
The western skink is known for its distinctive blue tail, especially in juveniles.
Biologists are studying how the habitat changes affect the western skink population.
