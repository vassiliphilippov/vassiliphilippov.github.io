ethnic minority

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛθnɪk məˈnɔrəti / (AmE), / ˈɛθnɪk mɪˈnɒrɪti / (BrE)
A group within a population that has different national or cultural traditions from the majority.
group with distinct cultural or national background

Examples

Government initiatives aim to support ethnic minority communities across the country.
She grew up as part of an ethnic minority in a small town.
The university is committed to increasing the enrollment of ethnic minority students.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: majority - the greater part or number; larger group compared to a smaller one
  • Synonym: minority - a smaller group or number within a larger population
