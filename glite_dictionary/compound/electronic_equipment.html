electronic equipment

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
Devices or apparatus that use the controlled movement of electrons, especially through gases, vacuums, or semiconductors, for their operation.
devices using controlled electron flow

Examples

Many modern offices are filled with electronic equipment such as computers and printers.
The technician repaired the faulty electronic equipment in the laboratory.
She is responsible for maintaining all of the electronic equipment at the television studio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electronic device - a device that operates by controlling electron flow
