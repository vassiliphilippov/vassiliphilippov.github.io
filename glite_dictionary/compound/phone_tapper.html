phone tapper

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌtæpər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌtæpə / (BrE)
a person who secretly listens to telephone conversations by means of an electronic device connected to the telephone line
person who wiretaps telephones

Examples

The police arrested a suspected phone tapper who had been monitoring private calls for months.
She hired a security expert to check for any phone tapper activity on her business lines.
Modern encryption makes it much harder for a phone tapper to eavesdrop undetected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wiretapper - a person who unlawfully listens to private telephone conversations
