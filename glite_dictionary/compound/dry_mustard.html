dry mustard

1. NOUN

/ draɪ ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / draɪ ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
Powdered mustard seeds used as a spice or condiment, often added to foods or used in cooking.
powdered mustard used in cooking

Examples

She added a teaspoon of dry mustard to the salad dressing for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for dry mustard rather than prepared mustard.
Some homemade remedies suggest using dry mustard mixed with warm water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mustard powder - Powdered form of mustard seeds used for seasoning
