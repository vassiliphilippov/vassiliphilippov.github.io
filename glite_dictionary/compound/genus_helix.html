genus Helix

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈhiː.lɪks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈhiː.lɪks / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Helicidae, comprising various species of land snails, including the common edible snail.
type genus of Helicidae (snails)

Examples

The genus Helix includes the well-known edible snail Helix pomatia.
Many species in the genus Helix are native to Europe and parts of Asia.
The genus Helix has been studied extensively for its adaptation to terrestrial environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Helix - genus of land snails in Helicidae

Etymology

From Latin 'helix' meaning spiral or coil.
