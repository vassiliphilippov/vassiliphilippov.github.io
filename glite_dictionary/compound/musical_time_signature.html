musical time signature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl ˈtaɪm ˈsɪgnətʃər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈtaɪm ˈsɪgnətʃə / (BrE)
a symbol in musical notation indicating how many beats are in each measure and which note value receives one beat
musical notation showing beats per measure

Examples

The composer chose a 3/4 musical time signature for the waltz.
Learning to read a musical time signature is essential for playing in an orchestra.
The song shifts to a different musical time signature halfway through.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time signature - mark in music notation showing meter; e.g., 4/4, 3/4
