ink cartridge

1. NOUN

/ ɪŋk ˈkɑːrtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪŋk ˈkɑːtɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small container that holds ink, designed to be inserted into a printer or pen and replaced when empty
replaceable container of ink for printer or pen

Examples

The printer stopped working because the ink cartridge ran out of ink.
Please replace the ink cartridge if you notice faded prints.
She bought a new ink cartridge so she could finish her school project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cartridge - a container holding a supply of something, usually for insertion into a device
