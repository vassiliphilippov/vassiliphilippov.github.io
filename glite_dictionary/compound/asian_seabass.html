Asian seabass

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən ˈsiːˌbæs / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃən ˈsiːˌbæs / (BrE)
a large species of perch (Lates calcarifer), valued as a game and food fish, found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater habitats of the Indo-Pacific region
a large edible Indo-Pacific fish species

Examples

The chef grilled the Asian seabass to perfection, pairing it with a tangy mango salsa.
Anglers often travel to Southeast Asia specifically to catch Asian seabass in the wild.
The aquarium showcases a large tank dedicated to Asian seabass and other regional fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barramundi - another name for the Asian seabass (Lates calcarifer)
