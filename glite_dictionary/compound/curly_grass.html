curly grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrli ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a rare small fern of northeastern North America with many slender spiraling fronds, forming dense tufts
rare fern with spiraling fronds

Examples

The wetland was dotted with curly grass growing in small, dense clusters.
Botanists were excited to find curly grass thriving in the cool, moist soil of the region.
Because of its spiraling fronds, curly grass is easily distinguished from other ferns.
