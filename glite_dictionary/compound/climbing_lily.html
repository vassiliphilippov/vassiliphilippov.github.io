climbing lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant of the genus Gloriosa, native to tropical Africa and Asia, with tendrils at leaf tips and showy yellow, red, or purple flowers; all parts are poisonous.
tropical climbing plant with showy flowers

Examples

The climbing lily adds vibrant color to gardens, but gardeners must handle it carefully due to its toxic parts.
We saw a climbing lily with striking red petals during our hike in the botanical reserve.
All parts of the climbing lily are poisonous, so it's important to keep it away from pets and children.
