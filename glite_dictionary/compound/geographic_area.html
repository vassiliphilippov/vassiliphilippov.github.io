geographic area

1. NOUN

/ dʒiˈɑɡrəfɪk ˈɛəriə / (AmE), / dʒiˈɒɡrəfɪk ˈeəriə / (BrE)
A region of the Earth's surface that has defined boundaries or characteristics.
region with defined boundaries

Examples

The census collects information about every geographic area in the country.
Environmental laws often vary depending on the geographic area concerned.
Species diversity can change greatly from one geographic area to another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: region - an area, especially part of a country or the world, having definable characteristics
  • Synonym: territory - an area of land under the jurisdiction of a government or authority
