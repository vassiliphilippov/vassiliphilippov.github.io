apparatus urogenitalis

1. NOUN

/ ˌæpəˌreɪtəs ˌjuːroʊˌdʒɛnɪˈtælɪs / (AmE), / ˌæpəˌreɪtəs ˌjʊərəʊˌdʒɛnɪˈtælɪs / (BrE)
the system of organs involved in reproduction and the formation and excretion of urine; the urogenital system
reproductive and urinary organ system
medical

Examples

The apparatus urogenitalis includes both the kidneys and reproductive organs in mammals.
Diseases affecting the apparatus urogenitalis can impact fertility and urinary function.
She studied the development of the apparatus urogenitalis during her medical research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urogenital system - system of organs including urinary and reproductive organs

Etymology

From Latin apparatus (equipment) and urogenitalis (relating to urine and reproduction).
