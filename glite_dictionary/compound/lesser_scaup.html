lesser scaup

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈskɑp / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈskɔːp / (BrE)
a North American diving duck (Aythya affinis), the male of which is recognized by its purplish head; smaller than the greater scaup
North American diving duck species

Examples

We spotted a flock of lesser scaup resting on the lake this morning.
Male lesser scaup are easily identified by their glossy purplish heads.
During migration, many lesser scaup stop to feed in local wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: greater scaup - a similar but larger diving duck species
