horse tick

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs tɪk / (AmE), / hɔːs tɪk / (BrE)
a winged parasitic fly that feeds on the blood of horses
parasitic winged fly on horses

Examples

The veterinarian found a horse tick attached to the mare's mane after the ride.
Horse ticks can cause irritation and transmit diseases to horses if not treated promptly.
Groomers recommend checking for horse ticks during regular horse care routines.
