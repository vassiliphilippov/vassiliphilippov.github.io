wearing apparel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛrɪŋ əˌpærəl / (AmE), / ˈwɛːrɪŋ əˈparəl / (BrE)
clothing of any kind; garments collectively.
clothing; garments collectively

Examples

She packed her finest wearing apparel for the gala event.
Regulations require all employees to use appropriate wearing apparel in the laboratory.
The store specializes in affordable wearing apparel for children and teenagers.

Synset Examples

she was refined in her choice of apparel
he always bought his clothes at the same store
fastidious about his dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing - items worn to cover the body
  • Synonym: attire - clothes, especially fine or formal ones
  • Antonym: nudity - the state of not wearing any clothing

Etymology

From 'wearing' (present participle of 'wear') + Middle English 'apparel', from Old French 'apparer' (to prepare).
