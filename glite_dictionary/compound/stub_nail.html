stub nail

1. NOUN

/ stʌb neɪl / (AmE), / stʌb neɪl / (BrE)
A short, thick nail used especially for securing objects where length is not required.
short, thick type of nail

Examples

He used a stub nail to attach the bracket to the wall, as it did not require deep fastening.
A stub nail is ideal for projects where a standard nail would be too long or cumbersome.
You can find a variety of stub nails at the local hardware store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nail - a thin, pointed metal pin used to fasten things together
