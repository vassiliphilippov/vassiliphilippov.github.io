gasoline tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæsəˌlin tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡasəliːn taŋk / (BrE)
a container in a vehicle for storing gasoline to supply its engine
container in a vehicle for gasoline

Examples

The mechanic inspected the gasoline tank for any signs of leakage.
After the accident, the gasoline tank was found to be completely empty.
He filled the gasoline tank before setting off on his long road trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuel tank - a container for storing fuel in a vehicle or machine
