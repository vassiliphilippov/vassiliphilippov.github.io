professional dancer

1. NOUN

/ prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈdænsər / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who performs dance as a paid occupation, especially as a career
a person who dances for a living

Examples

She became a professional dancer after years of rigorous training.
The competition was judged by a panel of professional dancers.
He aspired to be a professional dancer on Broadway.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: dancer - a person who dances, either professionally or as a hobby
