zebrawood tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈziː.brəˌwʊd triː / (AmE), / ˈziː.brəˌwʊd triː / (BrE)
Any of several trees or shrubs whose wood is marked with dark and light stripes resembling those of a zebra.
tree with striped wood

Examples

High-quality furniture is sometimes made from zebrawood tree due to its distinctive appearance.
The zebrawood tree is prized for its bold, striped wood pattern.
Some guitar manufacturers use wood from the zebrawood tree for decorative inlays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zebrawood - wood with dark and light stripes used in decorative woodworking

Etymology

Formed from 'zebrawood' (so named for its striped appearance) + 'tree'.
