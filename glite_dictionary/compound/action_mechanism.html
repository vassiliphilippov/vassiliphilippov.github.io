action mechanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.ʃən ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈæk.ʃən ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the mechanical part of a device that transmits power and initiates movement, especially in musical instruments or machinery
part transmitting power in a mechanism

Examples

The action mechanism of the old piano required extensive repairs before it would play properly.
A well-designed action mechanism ensures smooth movement in mechanical toys.
Engineers examined the action mechanism to determine the source of the malfunction.

Synset Examples

the piano had a very stiff action
