milk float

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk floʊt / (AmE), / mɪlk fləʊt / (BrE)
a small vehicle, typically powered by electricity, with an open side for delivering milk to homes
electric vehicle for home milk delivery
british

Examples

The milk float quietly made its way down the street early in the morning.
In the 1970s, many neighborhoods relied on a milk float for their daily deliveries.
She fondly remembers the cheerful clinking of bottles on the milk float outside her house.
