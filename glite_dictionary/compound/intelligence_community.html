Intelligence Community

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
The group of U.S. government agencies and organizations responsible for carrying out intelligence activities, coordinated under the Director of National Intelligence.
U.S. government intelligence agencies group
american

Examples

The Intelligence Community plays a crucial role in advising government leaders on matters of national security.
After the September 11 attacks, the Intelligence Community underwent significant reforms to improve coordination.
Leaked documents revealed how the Intelligence Community monitors foreign threats and cyber activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Central Intelligence Agency - the main civilian foreign intelligence service of the U.S. government
  • Synonym: National Security Agency - U.S. agency responsible for signals intelligence and information assurance
