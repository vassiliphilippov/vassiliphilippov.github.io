constant twitteration

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnstənt twɪtəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt twɪtəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
frequent and lively communication on Twitter, especially through continuous posting or engagement
frequent, lively Twitter activity

Examples

Her constant twitteration kept her followers engaged throughout the day.
The politician's constant twitteration made headlines every week.
Some users find constant twitteration overwhelming and prefer quieter social media feeds.

Synset Examples

Her constant twitteration kept her followers engaged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tweeting - posting messages on Twitter
  • Antonym: silence - absence of communication or tweets

Etymology

Blend of 'constant' and 'twitteration' (from 'Twitter' + '-ation' forming a noun of action).
