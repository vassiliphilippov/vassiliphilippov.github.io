community center

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunəti ˌsɛntər / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
a building or facility where members of a community gather for social, cultural, or recreational activities
place for community activities and gatherings

Examples

Many local events are hosted at the community center throughout the year.
She volunteered to teach art classes at the community center every weekend.
The new community center features a gym, a library, and several meeting rooms.

place for community activities and services

Examples

A new playground is being built next to the community center to encourage more family visits.
