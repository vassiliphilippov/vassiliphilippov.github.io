Canis Minor

1. PROPN

/ ˈkeɪ.nɪs ˈmaɪ.nər / (AmE), / ˈkaː.nɪs ˈmiː.nɔː / (BrE)
a small constellation in the northern sky, located east of Orion and containing the bright star Procyon.
a constellation east of Orion

Examples

The constellation Canis Minor is easy to find by locating its brightest star, Procyon.
My astronomy class studied Canis Minor as part of the winter sky.
Many ancient cultures included Canis Minor in their star maps.

Etymology

Latin for 'lesser dog'.
