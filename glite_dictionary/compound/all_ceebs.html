all ceebs

1. NOUN

/ ɔl sibz / (AmE), / ɔːl siːbz / (BrE)
Used to describe a strong state of laziness or lack of motivation to do anything.
state of laziness or disinterest
informal slang

Examples

After a long week at work, I'm just all ceebs this weekend.
She didn't do her assignments because it was all ceebs last night.
It's raining outside, and I'm feeling all ceebs about going to the gym.

Synset Examples

He was in such a bad mood, it was all ceebs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy
  • Antonym: motivated - having a reason or enthusiasm to do something

Etymology

From the slang abbreviation 'ceebs', itself from the phrase 'can't be bothered'.
