Siamese cat

1. NOUN

/ saɪəˈmiz kæt / (AmE), / saɪəˈmiːz kæt / (BrE)
A slender, short-haired breed of cat with blue eyes, a pale body, and darker coloring on the ears, face, paws, and tail tip.
slender blue-eyed cat breed with color points

Examples

The Siamese cat sat gracefully on the windowsill, observing everything with its striking blue eyes.
She has owned a Siamese cat since she was a child and adores its affectionate nature.
Their Siamese cat is known for its loud, distinctive meow and playful personality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cat - a small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur

Etymology

Named after Siam (now Thailand), where the breed originated.
