training college

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ˌkɑː.lɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ˌkɒl.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a college that offers courses and instruction for people preparing for a particular profession or field
college for professional training

Examples

After graduating from high school, she enrolled in a training college to become a primary school teacher.
Many nurses receive their qualifications from a training college specializing in healthcare.
He attended a training college to prepare for a career in engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vocational school - a school that teaches skills needed for a particular job or profession
