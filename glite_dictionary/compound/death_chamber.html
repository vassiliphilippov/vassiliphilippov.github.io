death chamber

1. NOUN

/ dɛθ ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a sealed room used for executions, into which poison gas is released to kill people or animals
room for execution by poison gas

Examples

The prisoner was led to the death chamber for the execution.
Many museums exhibit reconstructions of a death chamber as part of history displays.
Use of the death chamber has been banned in several states.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas chamber - a sealed chamber used to kill people or animals with poisonous gas
