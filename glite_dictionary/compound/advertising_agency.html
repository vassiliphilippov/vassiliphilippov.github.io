advertising agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vɚˌtaɪ.zɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪ.zɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
A company that creates advertisements and develops advertising campaigns for other businesses or organizations.
company that creates advertisements

Examples

The company hired an advertising agency to improve its brand image.
She works as a creative director at a well-known advertising agency in London.
Many small businesses cannot afford the high fees of a large advertising agency.

  • Synonym: ad agency - informal term for advertising agency
