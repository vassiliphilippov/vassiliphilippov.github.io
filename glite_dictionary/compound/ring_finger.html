ring finger

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋ ˌfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ ˌfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
the third finger of the hand, located between the middle finger and little finger, often the finger on which a wedding ring is worn
the third finger of the hand

Examples

She wore her grandmother's diamond on her ring finger for special occasions.
In many cultures, people place their wedding bands on the left ring finger.
He accidentally cut his ring finger while chopping vegetables.
