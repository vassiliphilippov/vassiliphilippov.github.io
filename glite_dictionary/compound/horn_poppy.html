horn poppy

1. NOUN

/ hɔrn ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / hɔːn ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered Eurasian plant of the genus Glaucium, naturalized along sandy shores in eastern North America.
Eurasian yellow-flowered plant (Glaucium)

Examples

The sandy dunes were covered in a scattering of horn poppy with their bright yellow blooms.
Botanists studied the spread of horn poppy along the North American coastline.
The distinctive seed pods of the horn poppy give the plant its common name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea poppy - A synonym for horn poppy, referring to the same plant genus.
