beach grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbitʃ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃ ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tough grass with strong roots that grows on exposed sandy shores, helping to stabilize dunes
tough grass found on sandy beaches

Examples

The dunes were covered in beach grass, protecting the shoreline from erosion.
Biologists study how beach grass stabilizes coastal sand dunes.
We walked barefoot along the beach grass, careful not to damage its roots.
