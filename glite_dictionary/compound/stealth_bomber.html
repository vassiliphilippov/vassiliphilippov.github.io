stealth bomber

1. NOUN

/ stɛlθ ˈbɑːmər / (AmE), / stɛlθ ˈbɒmə / (BrE)
a military aircraft designed to avoid detection by radar, used for dropping bombs on targets
radar-evading military bomber aircraft

Examples

The stealth bomber flew undetected across the border to carry out its mission.
Advancements in technology have made the stealth bomber one of the most effective tools in modern warfare.
The military unveiled a new stealth bomber capable of evading enemy radar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bomber - a military aircraft that drops bombs
