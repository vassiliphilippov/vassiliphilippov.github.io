watermelon begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtərˌmɛlən bəˈɡoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəmɛlən bɪˈɡəʊniə / (BrE)
a tropical houseplant from South America, known for its silvery striped, fleshy leaves resembling a watermelon rind
houseplant with striped, fleshy leaves

Examples

The watermelon begonia thrives best in indirect light and humid conditions.
Many people choose the watermelon begonia for its attractive, patterned foliage.
She placed the potted watermelon begonia on her kitchen windowsill for a splash of greenery.
