cap band

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæp bænd / (AmE), / ˈkap band / (BrE)
a band encircling the lower part of a cap, especially on military or official headgear, often in a contrasting color or with decoration
decorative band on a cap, often military

Examples

The officer's cap band was adorned with golden embroidery.
You can identify the rank by the color of the cap band.
The cap band on her uniform featured intricate patterns.

Synset Examples

General officers' caps are similar to those of field-grade officers, but the cap band is dark blue and embroidered with gold oak leaf motifs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatband - a decorative band around a hat
