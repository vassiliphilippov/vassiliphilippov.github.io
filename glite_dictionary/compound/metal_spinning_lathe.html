metal spinning lathe

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬l ˈspɪnɪŋ læθ / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl ˈspɪnɪŋ lɑːθ / (BrE)
a lathe specifically designed for shaping metal by spinning it at high speed against a forming tool
lathe for shaping metal by spinning

Examples

The craftsman demonstrated how a metal spinning lathe can create symmetrical metal bowls.
Modern factories often rely on a metal spinning lathe for producing automotive parts.
Before using the metal spinning lathe, always check that all safety guards are in place.

Synset Examples

Metal spinning lathes are almost as simple as wood turning lathes, and usually are.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lathe - a machine for shaping wood, metal, or other material by means of a rotating drive
