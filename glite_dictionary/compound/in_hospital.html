in hospital

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈhɑːspɪtəl / (AmE), / ɪn ˈhɒspɪtəl / (BrE)
receiving medical treatment as a patient in a hospital
receiving treatment as a hospital patient

Examples

After her surgery, she remained in hospital for another week to recover.
He was in hospital for observation following a minor heart attack.
The news reported that five people are still in hospital after the fire.

Synset Examples
