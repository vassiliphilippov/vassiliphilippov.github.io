pawpaw fruit

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑː.pɔː ˌfruːt / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.pɔː ˌfruːt / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with orange flesh, also known as papaya, valued for its sweet taste and nutritional benefits.
tropical fruit, also called papaya

Examples

Ripe pawpaw fruit is delicious in salads or eaten fresh on its own.
The market has a variety of tropical fruits, including pawpaw fruit and mangoes.
She made a smoothie with pawpaw fruit, banana, and yogurt.

Synset Examples

He always brings pawpaw fruit to the picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papaya - another name for pawpaw fruit, a tropical fruit with orange flesh

Etymology

From Carib *papaya*, adopted widely via Spanish and Portuguese.
