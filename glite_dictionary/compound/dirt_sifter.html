dirt sifter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝt ˌsɪftɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːt ˌsɪftə / (BrE)
a gardening tool used to separate soil from rocks, debris, or other unwanted materials
gardening tool for sifting soil

Examples

After filling the raised bed, she used a dirt sifter to remove small stones and debris.
The dirt sifter made it easy to prepare fine soil for planting seeds.
Gardeners often rely on a dirt sifter to ensure that their soil is free of unwanted materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sieve - a utensil for sifting or straining
  • Degree Lower: soil - the top layer of earth
