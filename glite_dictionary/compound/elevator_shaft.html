elevator shaft

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtər ʃæft / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə ʃɑːft / (BrE)
a vertical passage in a building that contains and allows the movement of an elevator between floors
vertical passage for an elevator in a building

Examples

The maintenance crew inspected the elevator shaft for any signs of structural damage.
Firefighters lowered their equipment down the elevator shaft to reach those trapped below.
Debris was found clogging the bottom of the elevator shaft after the renovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shaft - a vertical or sloping passageway
