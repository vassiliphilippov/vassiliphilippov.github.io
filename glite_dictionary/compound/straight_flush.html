straight flush

1. NOUN

/ streɪt flʌʃ / (AmE), / streɪt flʌʃ / (BrE)
A poker hand consisting of five consecutive cards of the same suit.
poker hand; five consecutive suited cards

Examples

He was ecstatic when he revealed his straight flush to win the tournament.
A straight flush is one of the rarest hands in poker.
She couldn't believe her luck when she drew a straight flush in the final round.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: royal flush - highest straight flush; ace-high sequence in poker
  • Degree Lower: flush - poker hand with five cards of the same suit, not in sequence
