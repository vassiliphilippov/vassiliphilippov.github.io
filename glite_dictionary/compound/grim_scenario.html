grim scenario

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪm səˈnɛrioʊ / (AmE), / ɡrɪm səˈnɑːriəʊ / (BrE)
a situation or sequence of events that is extremely serious, bleak, or likely to result in a negative outcome
serious or bleak situation

Examples

The government is preparing for a grim scenario in case the disease spreads further.
Economists painted a grim scenario of soaring unemployment and inflation.
The film imagines a grim scenario where technology takes control of society.

Synset Examples

The novel depicted a grim scenario of xenocide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dire situation - a situation of extreme danger or difficulty
  • Antonym: optimistic scenario - a situation likely to have a positive outcome
