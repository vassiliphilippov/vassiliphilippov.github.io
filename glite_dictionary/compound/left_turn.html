left turn

1. NOUN

/ lɛft tɝn / (AmE), / lɛft tɜːn / (BrE)
an act of turning toward the left side, especially while driving or moving forward
a turn toward the left side

Examples

At the next intersection, make a left turn onto Maple Street.
The driver signaled before taking a left turn at the traffic light.
You must yield to pedestrians when making a left turn.

Synset Examples

He took a left turn into the parking lot.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right turn - an act of turning toward the right side, especially while driving or moving forward
