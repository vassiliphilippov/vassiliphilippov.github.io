dental floss

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl flɔs / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl flɒs / (BrE)
a soft, thin thread used to clean between the teeth to remove food particles and plaque
thin thread for cleaning between teeth

Examples

Using dental floss daily helps prevent gum disease and cavities.
He forgot to pack his dental floss when he traveled abroad.
The hygienist recommended that I use dental floss after every meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floss - soft thread used for cleaning teeth or for embroidery

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntəl flɑs / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl flɒs / (BrE)
A thin cord of nylon or similar material used to clean between the teeth.
thin cord for cleaning between teeth

Examples

She always carries dental floss in her purse in case she needs it after meals.
Dentists recommend using dental floss at least once a day for best oral hygiene.
He ran out of dental floss and had to use a toothpick instead.
