globe lily

1. NOUN

/ ɡloʊb ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ɡləʊb ˈlɪli / (BrE)
any of several North American plants of the genus Calochortus that have egg-shaped or globe-shaped flowers, often grown for their ornamental value
plant with globe-shaped flowers (genus Calochortus)

Examples

The globe lily is prized by gardeners for its delicate, globe-shaped blossoms.
Hiking in California, we spotted several globe lily plants in bloom along the trail.
Botanists studied the habitat preferences of the rare white globe lily in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Calochortus - a genus of North American plants with showy, tulip-like flowers
