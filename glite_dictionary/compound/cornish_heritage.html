Cornish heritage

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɪʃ ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ ˈhɛrɪtɪdʒ / (BrE)
the traditions, culture, and ancestry related to Cornwall and its people
traditions and ancestry from Cornwall

Examples

She researches her Cornish heritage to learn more about her ancestors.
The museum showcases artifacts that celebrate Cornish heritage and traditional crafts.
He is proud of his Cornish heritage and often visits Cornwall to reconnect with his roots.

As a Janner, he takes pride in his Cornish heritage.
