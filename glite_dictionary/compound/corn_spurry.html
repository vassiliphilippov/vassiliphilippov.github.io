corn spurry

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈspɜri / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈspʌri / (BrE)
A small European weed with whorled leaves and white flowers, commonly found in cultivated fields.
a small European weed with whorled leaves

Examples

The farmer was concerned about the spread of corn spurry in his wheat fields.
Weeds like corn spurry can compete with crops for nutrients and sunlight.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of corn spurry in different soil types.
