drug abuse

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ əˈbjus / (AmE), / drʌɡ əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
the habitual or excessive use of drugs, especially illegal or harmful ones, that leads to addiction or other negative consequences
excessive or harmful use of drugs
medical

Examples

The community is working together to combat drug abuse among teenagers.
Many families have been affected by drug abuse and its consequences.
Education programs aim to prevent drug abuse in schools across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substance abuse - the harmful or hazardous use of psychoactive substances, including alcohol and illicit drugs
