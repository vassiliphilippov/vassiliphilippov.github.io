apple-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈæpəl saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈæpəl saɪzd / (BrE)
having the approximate size of an apple
about the size of an apple

Examples

The doctor found an apple-sized lump during the examination.
She picked up an apple-sized stone from the riverbank and tossed it into the water.
The hailstones that fell last night were almost apple-sized, causing damage to cars.
