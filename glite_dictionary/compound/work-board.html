work-board

1. NOUN

/ wɝk bɔrd / (AmE), / wɜːk bɔːd / (BrE)
A flat, horizontal board used as a surface to support manual work such as sewing, carving, or other crafts.
horizontal board for manual work

Examples

The tailor cut the fabric precisely on the work-board in her studio.
He placed the wooden planks on the work-board before starting to carve.
Artists often use a work-board to keep their tools and materials organized while they create.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - A sturdy table at which manual work is done
