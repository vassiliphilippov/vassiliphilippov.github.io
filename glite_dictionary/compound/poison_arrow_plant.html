poison arrow plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪzən ˈɛroʊ plænt / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən ˈærəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
A medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa, with thick leathery leaves, white or pink flowers, and purplish black two-seeded fruits.
South African evergreen shrub or tree

Examples

Local communities have traditionally used the poison arrow plant for its medicinal properties.
The garden now features a healthy poison arrow plant near the southern fence.
Berries from the poison arrow plant are easily recognized by their purplish black color.
