bread sauce

1. NOUN

/ brɛd sɔs / (AmE), / brɛd sɔːs / (BrE)
a creamy white sauce made from bread, typically flavored with cloves and onion, usually served with poultry
traditional sauce made with bread, clove, and onion
british

Examples

Roast chicken is often served with bread sauce during traditional British dinners.
She made a delicious bread sauce infused with cloves and onion to complement the turkey.
Despite its simplicity, bread sauce adds a comforting touch to holiday meals.
