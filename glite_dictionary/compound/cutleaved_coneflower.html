cutleaved coneflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtˌlivd ˈkɑnˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌliːvd ˈkɒnˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A tall plant native to North America, with deeply cut leaves and large yellow flower heads whose rays arch downward.
North American plant with deeply cut leaves and yellow flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with cutleaved coneflower blooming in late summer.
We spotted a patch of cutleaved coneflower while hiking in the Rockies.
Botanists often study the unique leaves of the cutleaved coneflower.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coneflower - any of several plants with cone-shaped flower heads
