tax program

1. NOUN

/ tæks ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / tæks ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a plan or system for determining and implementing taxes in a government or organization
plan for setting and collecting taxes

Examples

The government introduced a new tax program to simplify the tax code.
Critics argued that the tax program would benefit the wealthy most.
Each political party presented its own tax program during the election campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax policy - government guidelines for taxation
