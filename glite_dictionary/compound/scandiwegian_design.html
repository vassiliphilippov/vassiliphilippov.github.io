Scandiwegian design

1. NOUN

/ ˌskændiˈwiːdʒi.ən dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌskændɪˈwiːdʒi.ən dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style of design originating in Scandinavian countries, characterized by minimalism, simplicity, and functionality
minimalist Scandinavian design style
informal

Examples

She decorated her new apartment in a Scandiwegian design to achieve a clean and modern look.
The living room features elements of Scandiwegian design, such as light wood and neutral colors.
Many people admire Scandiwegian design for its balance between style and practicality.

Synset Examples

She loves the minimalistic style of Scandiwegian design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scandinavian design - design style from Scandinavia, known for minimalism and function

Etymology

Blend of 'Scandinavian' and 'Norwegian'.
