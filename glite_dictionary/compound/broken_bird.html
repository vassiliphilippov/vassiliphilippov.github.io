broken bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈbroʊ.kən bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən bɜːd / (BrE)
A person who is emotionally hurt or vulnerable; someone who has been deeply affected by hardship or sorrow.
emotionally hurt or vulnerable person

Examples

After the harsh breakup, he felt like a broken bird, unable to trust anyone.
The novel explores the life of a broken bird trying to find hope again.
Sometimes, her fragile smile revealed the broken bird hidden beneath her confidence.

Synset Examples

She felt like a broken bird after the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wounded soul - a person deeply emotionally hurt
  • Synonym: lost soul - a person who feels emotionally lost or troubled
