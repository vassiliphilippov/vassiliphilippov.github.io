white tie and tails

1. NOUN

/ waɪt taɪ ənd teɪlz / (AmE), / waɪt taɪ ənd teɪlz / (BrE)
a man's most formal evening dress, consisting of a white bow tie, white waistcoat, and a black tailcoat with matching trousers
formal men's evening dress with white bow tie and tailcoat

Examples

He arrived at the gala in white tie and tails, looking every bit the distinguished gentleman.
The invitation specified white tie and tails, so everyone dressed in their finest attire.
Performers at the concert were required to wear white tie and tails for the prestigious event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white tie - the most formal type of evening dress for men
  • Degree Lower: black tie - a semi-formal evening dress code for men
