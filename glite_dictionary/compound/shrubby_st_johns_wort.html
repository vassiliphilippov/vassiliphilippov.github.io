shrubby St John's wort

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrʌbi ˌseɪnt ˈdʒɒnz wɜrt / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌbi seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz wɜːt / (BrE)
A stiff shrub with oblong, entire leaves and dense clusters of yellow flowers, belonging to the genus Hypericum.
a stiff shrub with yellow flowers

Examples

The shrubby St John's wort blooms in late summer with bright yellow flowers.
Gardeners often choose shrubby St John's wort for its hardiness and ornamental value.
I planted a shrubby St John's wort to add color to my garden borders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: St John's wort - common name for plants of the genus Hypericum
