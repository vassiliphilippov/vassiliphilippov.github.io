gopher tortoise

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊfər ˌtɔːrtəs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊfə ˌtɔːtəs / (BrE)
a large, burrowing land tortoise native to the southeastern United States, known for digging extensive burrows
burrowing land tortoise of southeastern US

Examples

The gopher tortoise digs deep burrows that provide shelter for many other animals.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the gopher tortoise in Florida.
We spotted a gopher tortoise crossing the sandy trail during our hike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortoise - a land-dwelling reptile with a hard shell
