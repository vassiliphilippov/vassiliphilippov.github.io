Arab chief

1. NOUN

/ ˈærəb tʃiːf / (AmE), / ˈærəb tʃiːf / (BrE)
the leader of an Arab village, tribe, or family, often holding authority and social status within the community
leader of an Arab village or tribe

Examples

The Arab chief welcomed the foreign visitors to his village with great hospitality.
Negotiations were often conducted between the colonial authorities and the local Arab chief.
As an Arab chief, he was responsible for settling disputes within his tribe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheikh - an Arab leader, particularly of a tribe or village
