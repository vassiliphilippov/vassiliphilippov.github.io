tent stitch

1. NOUN

/ tɛnt stɪtʃ / (AmE), / tɛnt stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a small diagonal embroidery stitch used in needlepoint
needlepoint embroidery stitch

Examples

She used a tent stitch to create the background of her tapestry.
Learning the tent stitch is essential for beginners in needlepoint.
The design combined cross-stitching and the classic tent stitch.
