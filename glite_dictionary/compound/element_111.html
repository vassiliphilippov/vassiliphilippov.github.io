element 111

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛləmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈwʌn / (BrE)
a radioactive transuranic chemical element with atomic number 111, also known as roentgenium.
the element roentgenium; atomic number 111

Examples

Scientists have successfully synthesized element 111 in the laboratory.
The chemical properties of element 111 are not fully understood due to its instability.
Few atoms of element 111 have ever been observed because of its short half-life.
