vintage garageware

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪntɪdʒ ɡəˈrɑːʒˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈvɪntɪdʒ ɡəˈrɑːʒˌweə / (BrE)
old or collectible household items, especially those typically found in garages, such as tools, signs, or equipment, from a previous era considered valuable or desirable by collectors
collectible old garage-related items

Examples

She found some vintage garageware at the flea market.
Collectors often search estate sales for rare pieces of vintage garageware.
His shelves were lined with vintage garageware, including old oil cans and metal toolboxes.

Synset Examples
