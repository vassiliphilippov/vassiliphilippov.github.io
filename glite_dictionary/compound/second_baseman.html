second baseman

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈbeɪsmən / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈbeɪsmən / (BrE)
(Baseball) the player who fields the position near second base and is responsible for plays at that base.
baseball player at second base

Examples

The second baseman made an impressive diving catch during the game.
After the starter was injured, a rookie took over as second baseman.
She practiced hard to improve her fielding skills as a second baseman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shortstop - the player positioned between second and third base in baseball
