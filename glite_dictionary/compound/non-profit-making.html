non-profit-making

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑːn ˈprɑːfɪt ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ˈprɒfɪt ˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Not operated or intended to make a profit; not commercially motivated.
not intended to make a profit

Examples

The organization is strictly non-profit-making and supports local community projects.
They run a non-profit-making theater dedicated to educating young people about the arts.
This non-profit-making scheme relies on donations from the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonprofit - not established for the purpose of making a profit
