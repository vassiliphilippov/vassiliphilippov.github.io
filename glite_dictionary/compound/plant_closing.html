plant closing

1. NOUN

/ plænt ˈkloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈkləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the permanent or temporary shutting down of all operations at a manufacturing or business facility
shutting down of a business or factory

Examples

The sudden plant closing left hundreds of workers without jobs in the small town.
Union members protested the announced plant closing scheduled for the end of the year.
The government offered assistance to those affected by the recent plant closing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: closure - the act or process of closing something, especially permanently
  • Synonym: shutdown - a closure of a factory, business, or system, especially temporarily
