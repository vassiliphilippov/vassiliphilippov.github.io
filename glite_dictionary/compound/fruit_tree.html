fruit tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrut ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈfruːt ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree that produces edible fruit.
tree that produces edible fruit

Examples

We planted a fruit tree in our backyard to enjoy fresh apples each autumn.
Each year, the fruit tree was covered with blossoms that promised a good harvest.
Pruning a fruit tree helps encourage better-quality fruit production.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: fruit - the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food
