physical goods

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl gʊdz / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ɡʊdz / (BrE)
Products or items that can be physically touched and possessed, as opposed to intangible goods such as services or digital content.
tangible products or items

Examples

The company specializes in the distribution of physical goods such as electronics and furniture.
Unlike digital products, physical goods require shipping and inventory management.
Retail stores typically focus on selling physical goods that customers can examine before purchasing.

Synset Examples

A physical good may cost more than an intangible good, but is easier to return or track.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangible goods - goods that have a physical form and can be touched
  • Antonym: intangible goods - products such as services or digital content that do not have a physical form
