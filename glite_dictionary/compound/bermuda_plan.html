Bermuda plan

1. NOUN

/ bərˈmjuːdə plæn / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə plan / (BrE)
a hotel rate plan that includes a full breakfast each day
hotel plan with daily full breakfast

Examples

The resort offered the Bermuda plan, which included a hearty breakfast every morning.
Many tourists choose the Bermuda plan for the convenience of having breakfast included in their stay.
When booking a room, ask if the Bermuda plan is available to save on meal costs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continental plan - hotel plan providing only a light breakfast
