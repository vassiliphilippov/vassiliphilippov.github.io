silky terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlki ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
An Australian breed of small toy dogs having a silky blue-and-tan coat.
small Australian toy dog breed

Examples

The silky terrier pranced proudly around the dog show ring.
She adopted a playful silky terrier from the animal shelter last year.
With its long, shiny coat, the silky terrier requires regular grooming to stay healthy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yorkshire terrier - a similar small breed with a long, silky coat, originally from England
