leaf shape

1. NOUN

/ lif ʃeɪp / (AmE), / liːf ʃeɪp / (BrE)
the form or outline of a leaf, especially as classified in botany
form or outline of a plant leaf

Examples

The leaf shape can help botanists identify different plant species.
Maple trees are easily recognized by their distinctive leaf shape.
Changes in leaf shape may indicate environmental adaptation.
