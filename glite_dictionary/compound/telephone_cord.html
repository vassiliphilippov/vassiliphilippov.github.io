telephone cord

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌfəʊn kɔːd / (BrE)
a flexible insulated wire or cable that connects a telephone handset to its base or to a wall outlet
wire connecting a telephone handset

Examples

He tripped over the telephone cord on his way out of the office.
The telephone cord was tangled and needed to be straightened.
She unplugged the telephone cord before moving the desk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cord - a long, thin, flexible string or rope made from twisted strands
