Wolverine State

1. PROPN

/ ˈwʊlvəˌrin steɪt / (AmE), / ˈwʊlvəriːn steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the US state of Michigan, located in the Great Lakes region with its capital at Lansing
nickname for the state of Michigan, US

Examples

She grew up in the Wolverine State and still supports local Michigan teams.
The Wolverine State is famous for its beautiful lakes and auto industry.
Many tourists visit the Wolverine State for its vibrant autumn colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Michigan - a state in the Great Lakes region of the United States

Etymology

Refers to the wolverine, once believed to inhabit Michigan; nickname dates to the 19th century.
