three o'clock sharp

1. ADV

/ θri əˈklɑk ʃɑrp / (AmE), / θri əˈklɒk ʃɑːp / (BrE)
at exactly three o'clock; precisely at the hour of three
exactly at 3:00

Examples

Please arrive at three o'clock sharp to avoid missing any important information.
The train is scheduled to depart at three o'clock sharp every afternoon.
She stepped into the room at three o'clock sharp, just as planned.

Synset Examples

The meeting starts at three o'clock sharp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the dot - precisely at the stated time
  • Antonym: about three o'clock - around the time of three o'clock
