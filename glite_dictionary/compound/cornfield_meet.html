cornfield meet

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌfild mit / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnfɪəld miːt / (BrE)
an event or gathering held in a cornfield, often for social or community purposes
gathering held in a cornfield

Examples

The local community organizes a cornfield meet every summer to celebrate the harvest.
This year’s cornfield meet featured live music and traditional games for all ages.
She enjoyed catching up with old friends at the annual cornfield meet.
