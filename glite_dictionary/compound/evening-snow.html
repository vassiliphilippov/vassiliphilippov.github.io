evening-snow

1. NOUN

/ ˈivnɪŋ snoʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ snəʊ / (BrE)
A small annual plant native to California, known for its white flowers.
California annual plant with white flowers

Examples

The garden was dotted with delicate evening-snow blooming beside the pathway.
Botanists observed a cluster of evening-snows on the hillside during their field study.
Planting evening-snow can add a touch of elegance with its bright white flowers.
