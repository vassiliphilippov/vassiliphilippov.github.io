all over the world

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈoʊvɚ ðə wɜːrld / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈəʊvə ðə wɜːld / (BrE)
in or from many different countries across the globe; everywhere in the world
everywhere in the world

Examples

Her artwork has been exhibited all over the world and praised by international critics.
People all over the world celebrate New Year's Eve in different ways.
Our products are shipped to customers all over the world every day.

Synset Examples

The entrepôt handled goods from all over the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldwide - extending or reaching throughout the world
  • Synonym: international - involving more than one country
