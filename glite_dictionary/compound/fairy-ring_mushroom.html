fairy-ring mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛri rɪŋ ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈfeəri rɪŋ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
A type of mushroom that grows in a circular pattern known as a fairy ring, typically in grassy areas.
mushroom growing in a ring

Examples

We spotted a circle of fairy-ring mushrooms in the park after the rain.
The fairy-ring mushroom is known for its distinctive growth pattern.
Some people collect fairy-ring mushrooms for culinary purposes, but caution is advised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairy ring - a naturally occurring circular ring of mushrooms
  • Synonym: mushroom - a type of fungus with a stem and cap
