flower bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊər bɛd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə bɛd / (BrE)
An area of ground in a garden or park where flowers are planted and grown.
area for growing flowers

Examples

She spent the morning planting tulips in the new flower bed.
The flower bed by the front door was filled with bright daffodils.
After a week away, weeds had started to grow in the flower bed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden bed - an area of ground in a garden for growing plants

2. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ bɛd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə bɛd / (BrE)
a piece of ground where flowers are planted and grown, often arranged for decorative purposes in a garden
a garden area for growing flowers

Examples

The roses in the flower bed bloom beautifully each spring.
She spent her morning planting tulips in the flower bed by the patio.
Our flower bed needs to be weeded before we add new blooms.

Synset Examples

Everyone admires Shrubya's vibrant flower beds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garden bed - a prepared area in a garden for planting, not limited to flowers
  • Synonym: border - a narrow strip of ground along the edge of a garden where flowers may be grown
