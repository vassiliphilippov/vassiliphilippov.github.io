shop mechanic

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp məˈkænɪk / (AmE), / ʃɒp mɪˈkænɪk / (BrE)
a skilled worker who maintains and repairs machinery, typically in a workshop or industrial setting
workshop machinery repairer

Examples

The shop mechanic quickly diagnosed the problem with the engine and made the necessary repairs.
She worked for several years as a shop mechanic in a busy manufacturing plant.
When the factory equipment broke down, the shop mechanic was called in to fix it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mechanic - a person who repairs and maintains machinery
