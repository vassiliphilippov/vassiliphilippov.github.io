Minato Ohashi Bridge

1. PROPN

/ minɑːtoʊ oʊˈhɑːʃi ˈbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɪˈnɑːtəʊ əʊˈhæʃi ˈbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a cantilever bridge located in Osaka, Japan, known for its distinctive structure and significance in transportation
cantilever bridge in Osaka, Japan

Examples

The Minato Ohashi Bridge is an iconic sight along the skyline of Osaka.
Several tourists stopped to take photos of the Minato Ohashi Bridge at sunset.
Construction of the Minato Ohashi Bridge helped ease traffic congestion in the city.

Etymology

Japanese, from 'minato' (harbor) and 'ōhashi' (large bridge).
