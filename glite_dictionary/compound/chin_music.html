chin music

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪn ˈmjuzɪk / (AmE), / tʃɪn ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
foolish or idle talk; chatter that is unimportant or irrelevant
idle or foolish talk

Examples

Stop with the chin music and get to the point.
All this chin music isn't helping solve the problem.
He filled the afternoon with endless chin music about his travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatter - rapid, trivial, or foolish talk
  • Synonym: prattle - foolish or inconsequential talk

Etymology

From the image of moving one's chin while talking, likened to musical noise.
