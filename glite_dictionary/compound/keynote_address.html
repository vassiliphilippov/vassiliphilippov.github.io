keynote address

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːˌnoʊt əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌnəʊt əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
a main speech at a formal gathering, especially one that sets the underlying tone or main theme of the event
main speech setting the event's theme

Examples

The conference will begin with a keynote address by the CEO of the company.
She was honored to give the keynote address at the university's annual gala.
His inspiring keynote address set the tone for the entire event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keynote speech - a main speech outlining the central theme at an event
