British Honduras

1. PROPN

/ ˌbrɪtɪʃ hɒnˈdʊrəs / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ hɒnˈdjʊərəs / (BrE)
the former name of Belize, a country on the northeastern coast of Central America, which was under British colonial rule until 1981.
former name of Belize (country in Central America)

Examples

Many archival documents from the colonial period refer to the territory as British Honduras.
He was born in British Honduras before it became known as Belize.
During the 1960s, British Honduras experienced significant political changes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Belize - modern name for what was formerly British Honduras

Etymology

British, referring to the United Kingdom, combined with Honduras, from the adjacent region; formal name until the country was renamed Belize in 1973.
