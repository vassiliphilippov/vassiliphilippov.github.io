Tupi language

1. NOUN

/ tuˈpi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tuːˈpiː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Tupi people of Brazil and Paraguay
language of the Tupi people of Brazil and Paraguay

Examples

Many indigenous words in Portuguese come from the Tupi language.
Anthropologists are studying the Tupi language to better understand the region's history.
He is fluent in both Portuguese and the Tupi language.

Etymology

From Tupi, the name of the indigenous people and their language of Brazil.
