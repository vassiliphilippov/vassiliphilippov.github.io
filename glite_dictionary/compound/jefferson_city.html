Jefferson City

1. PROPN

/ ˌdʒɛfərsən ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛfəsən ˈsɪti / (BrE)
The capital city of the U.S. state of Missouri, located in central Missouri on the Missouri River.
capital city of Missouri, USA

Examples

The governor delivered a speech at Jefferson City to announce the new education policy.
After moving to Missouri, they decided to settle in Jefferson City to be closer to the government offices.
Many historic buildings in Jefferson City attract tourists from all over the state.

Etymology

Named after Thomas Jefferson, the third President of the United States.
