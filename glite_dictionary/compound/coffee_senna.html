coffee senna

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˈsɛnə / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˈsɛnə / (BrE)
a leafy, malodorous tropical shrub (Senna occidentalis) whose seeds have been used as a coffee adulterant; sometimes classified in the genus Cassia
tropical shrub with seeds used as coffee adulterant

Examples

The seeds of coffee senna were once used as a cheap substitute in ground coffee.
Botanists debate whether coffee senna belongs to the genus Senna or Cassia.
The weedy shrub known as coffee senna can be found throughout tropical regions.
