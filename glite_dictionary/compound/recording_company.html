recording company

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A business that produces and distributes musical recordings.
company producing and selling music recordings

Examples

She signed a contract with a major recording company to release her debut album.
The recording company was responsible for promoting the band's new single worldwide.
After years of producing music independently, he finally attracted the attention of a recording company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record label - label or brand identifying the company that releases music
