creepy-crawly

1. NOUN

/ ˈkripi ˈkrɔli / (AmE), / ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli / (BrE)
A small insect or creature, especially one that causes discomfort or fear.
small insect or unpleasant creature
informal

Examples

My daughter screamed when she saw a creepy-crawly in the bathtub.
After turning over the log, we found several creepy-crawlies scurrying away.
He hates camping because of all the creepy-crawlies that come out at night.

Synset Examples

Everyone in town knows Spiders Georg for his creepy crawlies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insect - a small arthropod animal with six legs
  • Synonym: bug - informal term for an insect
