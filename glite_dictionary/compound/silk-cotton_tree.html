silk-cotton tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlk ˌkɑtən ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈsɪlk ˌkɒtən ˌtriː / (BrE)
A massive tropical tree with a deeply ridged trunk that bears large pods filled with seeds covered in silky floss, and is the source of kapok fiber.
tropical tree; source of kapok fiber

Examples

The silk-cotton tree towered over the village, its trunk marked with deep grooves and spines.
Farmers harvest the fluffy fiber from the pods of the silk-cotton tree to use as stuffing.
A row of silk-cotton trees stood alongside the tropical highway, their seed pods bursting open in the sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kapok tree - tropical tree noted for its kapok fiber
