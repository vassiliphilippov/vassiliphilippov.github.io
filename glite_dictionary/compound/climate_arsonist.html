climate arsonist

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmət ˈɑrsənɪst / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət ˈɑːsənɪst / (BrE)
a person who deliberately damages or destroys the environment, especially through actions that contribute to climate change
person who intentionally harms the environment

Examples

Environmental activists condemned the politician as a climate arsonist after his anti-regulation statements.
Some argue that corporate leaders who ignore ecological warnings are modern climate arsonists.
The term climate arsonist has been used to describe those responsible for severe environmental degradation.

Synset Examples

Every climate arsonist should face severe penalties.
They labeled him a climate arsonist after the wildfires.
The authorities arrested a suspected climate arsonist last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsonist - a person who intentionally sets fire to property

Etymology

Formed from 'climate' (environmental conditions) and 'arsonist' (one who commits arson).
