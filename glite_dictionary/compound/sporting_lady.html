sporting lady

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtɪŋ ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
(archaic) A woman who engages in sexual activity for money; a prostitute.
archaic term for prostitute
archaic

Examples

In many old novels, a sporting lady would be found working in city saloons.
Some towns in the Wild West were famous for their sporting ladies and lively nightlife.
The detective suspected the elegant guest was actually a sporting lady in disguise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostitute - a person who engages in sexual activity for payment
  • Synonym: call girl - a female prostitute who arranges meetings with clients by phone

Etymology

Formed from 'sporting' (relating to amusements, here euphemistically) + 'lady'.
