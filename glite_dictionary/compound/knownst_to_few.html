knownst to few

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊnst tu ˈfju / (AmE), / ˈnəʊnst tə ˈfjuː / (BrE)
Known by only a small number of people; not widely known or recognized.
known by few people
archaic

Examples

Knownst to few, the lake is actually fed by a network of underground springs.
The artist’s early work, knownst to few, was completely different from his later style.
Knownst to few, the cellar once served as a meeting place for revolutionaries.

Synset Examples

What is knownst to few is that he was once a famous artist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unknown to most - not known by most people

Etymology

From archaic 'knownst', a past participle form of 'know' patterned after forms like 'unknownst'.
