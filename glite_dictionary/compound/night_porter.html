night porter

1. NOUN

/ naɪt ˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / naɪt ˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a porter who works during the night, especially at a hotel or similar establishment
porter working at night (esp. in hotels)

Examples

The night porter greeted guests arriving after midnight at the hotel.
As night porter, his duties included keeping the lobby secure and helping late arrivals.
She worked as a night porter while studying at university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porter - a person employed to carry luggage or attend to guests, especially at hotels
  • Synonym: concierge - a hotel employee who assists guests with various tasks
