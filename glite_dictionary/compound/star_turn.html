star turn

1. NOUN

/ stɑr tɜrn / (AmE), / stɑː tɜːn / (BrE)
an outstanding or especially impressive performance, particularly by an actor or entertainer, that attracts special attention
outstanding performance (entertainment)

Examples

The young actor's star turn in the musical earned him a nomination for best newcomer.
Critics praised her star turn as the lead detective, calling it riveting and unforgettable.
His unexpected star turn at the awards show brought the audience to their feet.

Synset Examples

His star turn in the play earned him rave reviews.
The actress delivered a star turn that left the audience speechless.
Her star turn in the movie was a career highlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: showstopper - a performance or performer that wins enthusiastic or prolonged applause
  • Antonym: supporting role - a less prominent acting role in a play or film
