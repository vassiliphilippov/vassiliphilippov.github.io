Jefferson Davis' Birthday

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛfɚsən ˈdeɪvɪs ˈbɜrθˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛfəsən ˈdeɪvɪs ˈbɜːθˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A holiday commemorating the birth of Jefferson Davis, observed on June 3, mainly in some southern states of the United States.
U.S. holiday marking Jefferson Davis's birth

Examples

Some southern states still recognize Jefferson Davis' Birthday as a public holiday.
Jefferson Davis' Birthday is celebrated with parades and ceremonies in parts of Mississippi and Alabama.
The office will be closed in observance of Jefferson Davis' Birthday next week.
