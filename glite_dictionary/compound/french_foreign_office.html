French Foreign Office

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈfɔrən ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈfɒrɪn ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the French government department responsible for foreign affairs, officially known as the Ministry for Europe and Foreign Affairs
French department for foreign affairs

Examples

The ambassador met officials from the French Foreign Office to discuss the new trade agreement.
Journalists gathered outside the French Foreign Office waiting for a statement on the crisis.
The French Foreign Office issued a travel advisory for citizens planning to visit the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ministry for Europe and Foreign Affairs - official name of the French Foreign Office
  • Synonym: Foreign Office - UK government department responsible for foreign affairs
