angled loofah

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡəld ˈluːfə / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡld ˈluːfə / (BrE)
a plant of the gourd family, widely cultivated in tropical regions, whose young fruit is eaten as a vegetable or used to make natural sponges
tropical gourd plant; edible fruit and sponge source

Examples

She added chopped angled loofah to the curry for a delicate, tender texture.
In many Asian markets, you can find fresh angled loofah among the vegetables.
The young fruit of the angled loofah is prized for its mild flavor and versatility in cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loofah - fibrous gourd fruit used as a sponge
