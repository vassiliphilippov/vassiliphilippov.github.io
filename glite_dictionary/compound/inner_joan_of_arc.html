inner Joan of Arc

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnər ʤoʊn əv ɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɪnə ˌdʒəʊn əv ɑːk / (BrE)
A person's inner strength, courage, or resolve, likened to the bravery of Joan of Arc.
courage or resolve inspired by Joan of Arc

Examples

Whenever I face a challenge at work, I try to find my inner Joan of Arc.
She drew on her inner Joan of Arc to stand up for what she believed was right.
He encouraged his team to summon their inner Joan of Arc before the big presentation.

Synset Examples

In times of trouble, she channels her inner Joan of Arc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one; bravery
  • Synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • Antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery; fearfulness

Etymology

Alludes to Joan of Arc, the French heroine and symbol of courage.
