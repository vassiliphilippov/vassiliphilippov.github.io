Little Rhody

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪtl ˈroʊdi / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈrəʊdi / (BrE)
A nickname for the U.S. state of Rhode Island, the smallest state in the United States and one of the original 13 colonies.
nickname for Rhode Island, US state

Examples

Many people are surprised at how much history Little Rhody holds despite its size.
She grew up in Little Rhody and loves its coastal scenery.
The food festivals in Little Rhody are famous throughout New England.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rhode Island - A state in the northeastern United States; the smallest US state

Etymology

Derived from 'Rhody', an informal diminutive of 'Rhode Island'.
