Japanese apricot

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz ˈæp.raɪ.kɑt / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt / (BrE)
an ornamental tree native to East Asia, having fragrant white or pink blossoms and small yellow fruit, often cultivated for its flowers or fruit
an East Asian ornamental tree with fragrant blossoms

Examples

The Japanese apricot blossoms early in spring, brightening gardens with its delicate pink flowers.
She planted a Japanese apricot in her backyard to enjoy its fragrant blossoms every year.
The fruit of the Japanese apricot is often used to make traditional East Asian preserves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apricot - a small, soft, orange fruit or the tree it grows on
