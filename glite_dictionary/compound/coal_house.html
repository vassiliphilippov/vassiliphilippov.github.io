coal house

1. NOUN

/ koʊl haʊs / (AmE), / kəʊl haʊs / (BrE)
A small building or shed used for storing coal, typically near a house or building.
a shed for storing coal

Examples

The old coal house still stands behind the cottage, though it hasn't been used in years.
They filled the coal house every autumn in preparation for the cold winter months.
Grandfather remembered carrying buckets of coal from the coal house to the kitchen stove.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coal shed - a shed for storing coal
