service of process

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrvɪs əv ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs əv ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
the formal act of delivering legal documents such as a writ, summons, or complaint to a party in a legal proceeding
formal delivery of legal documents
legal

Examples

The defendant claimed that service of process had not been properly completed.
Without service of process, the lawsuit cannot officially proceed.
He hired a professional to ensure service of process was executed correctly.

Synset Examples

he accepted service of the subpoena

Etymology

From the legal meaning of 'service' (delivery of legal documents) and 'process' (legal proceedings).
