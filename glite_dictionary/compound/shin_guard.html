shin guard

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪn ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ʃɪn ɡɑːd / (BrE)
A protective pad or covering worn over the front of the lower leg to shield the shin from injury, especially in sports such as soccer, hockey, or baseball.
protective pad for the shin in sports

Examples

Hockey players are required to wear shin guards during every game to prevent injuries.
He forgot his shin guards and had to borrow a pair from a teammate before the soccer match.
The catcher made sure his shin guards were tightly secured before crouching behind home plate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pad - a protective cushion used for safety or comfort
