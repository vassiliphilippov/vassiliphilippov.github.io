Port Sudan

1. PROPN

/ pɔrt suˈdæn / (AmE), / pɔːt suːˈdæn / (BrE)
A port city in Sudan on the Red Sea, serving as the country's main seaport.
port city in Sudan on the Red Sea

Examples

Port Sudan plays a vital role in Sudan's international trade, handling most of the country's imports and exports.
Many humanitarian organizations operate from Port Sudan to provide aid to remote regions.
The port facilities at Port Sudan have been expanded to accommodate larger cargo ships.
