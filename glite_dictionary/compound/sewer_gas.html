sewer gas

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɚ ˌɡæs / (AmE), / ˈsjuːə ˌɡas / (BrE)
A foul-smelling gas produced in sewers, typically consisting of various gases from decomposing organic matter.
foul-smelling gas in sewers

Examples

A strong odor of sewer gas filled the basement after the heavy rain.
Long exposure to sewer gas can be hazardous to your health.
The plumber discovered a leak that was allowing sewer gas to enter the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methane - a colorless, odorless, flammable gas that is a major component of sewer gas
