genus Lates

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈleɪ.tiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈleɪ.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of large percoid fishes found in fresh and brackish waters, including the Nile perch.
a genus of large freshwater/brackish fishes

Examples

The genus Lates includes several commercially important fish species.
Scientists have been studying the ecological impact of fish within the genus Lates on local waterways.
One well-known member of the genus Lates is the Nile perch, found in African lakes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nile perch - a large freshwater fish belonging to genus Lates

Etymology

From Latin 'Lates', the type genus name for this group of fishes.
