walk-to

1. ADJ

/ wɔk tuː / (AmE), / wɔːk tuː / (BrE)
Situated close enough to be easily reached on foot.
able to be reached by walking

Examples

The apartment is in a walk-to neighborhood with shops and restaurants nearby.
We prefer a walk-to school for our children, so they don't need a bus.
Many buyers search for walk-to amenities like parks and supermarkets.

Synset Examples

walking distance
the factory with the big parking lot … is more convenient than the walk-to factory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: walking distance - distance close enough to be walked
