annual salt-marsh aster

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ju.əl sɔlt mɑrʃ ˈæs.tər / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl sɔːlt mɑːʃ ˈæs.tə / (BrE)
a North American plant of the aster family (Symphyotrichum subulatum), typically found in salt marshes and coastal areas, bearing small pale purple or white flowers.
a plant species found in salt marshes

Examples

The annual salt-marsh aster grows abundantly along the mid-Atlantic coastline.
Wildlife often rely on the annual salt-marsh aster for nectar in late summer.
We spotted several annual salt-marsh asters blooming in the tidal wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - any plant of the genus Aster, known for star-shaped flower heads
