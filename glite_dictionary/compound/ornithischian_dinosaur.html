ornithischian dinosaur

1. NOUN

/ ɔːrˌnɪˈθɪskiən ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ɔːˌnɪˈθɪskɪən ˈdaɪnəˌsɔː / (BrE)
a herbivorous dinosaur of the order Ornithischia, characterized by a pelvis structure similar to that of birds
herbivorous dinosaur with a bird-like pelvis

Examples

The Stegosaurus is a well-known ornithischian dinosaur from the Jurassic period.
Fossils of an early ornithischian dinosaur were recently discovered in South America.
Unlike their saurischian relatives, ornithischian dinosaurs had a pelvis that pointed backward.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saurischian dinosaur - dinosaur with a lizard-like pelvis, another major dinosaur group

Etymology

From Greek ornith- (bird) + ischion (hip joint), referring to the bird-like pelvis.
