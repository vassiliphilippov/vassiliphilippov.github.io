stage name

1. NOUN

/ steɪdʒ neɪm / (AmE), / steɪdʒ neɪm / (BrE)
A pseudonym used by an actor, performer, or entertainer instead of their real name.
pseudonym used by a performer

Examples

Many famous musicians perform under a stage name instead of their birth name.
She adopted a stage name to create a more memorable public persona.
The credits list both his real name and his stage name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name, especially used by an author
  • Antonym: real name - the actual name given at birth or legal name
