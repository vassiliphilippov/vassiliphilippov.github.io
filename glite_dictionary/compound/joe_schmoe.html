Joe Schmoe

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊ ʃmoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəʊ ʃməʊ / (BrE)
a name used to refer to an average or ordinary person, especially a typical, unremarkable man
average or ordinary person (esp. man)
informal american

Examples

The company wants to create a product that appeals to the average Joe Schmoe on the street.
He's just a regular Joe Schmoe trying to make ends meet like everyone else.
That movie wasn't made for critics; it was made for the typical Joe Schmoe.

Synset Examples

They hired Joe Schmoe to help with marketing.
Even Joe Schmoe can see the flaws in this plan.
Joe Schmoe won't mind if we skip the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: John Doe - an anonymous or typical person, especially in legal contexts

Etymology

Probably derived from Jewish (Yiddish) surname 'Schmo' as a humorous generic addition to the common American first name 'Joe'.
