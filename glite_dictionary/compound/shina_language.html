Shina language

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃiːnə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːnə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Dardic language spoken mainly in northern Kashmir and parts of northern Pakistan
a Dardic language of northern Kashmir

Examples

There are several dialects of the Shina language found throughout Gilgit-Baltistan.
Linguists have documented many oral traditions in Shina language.
Her family still speaks the Shina language at home despite living in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dardic - a subgroup of the Indo-Aryan languages spoken mainly in northern Pakistan and surrounding regions

Etymology

From Shina, the name used by its speakers for both the people and the language.
