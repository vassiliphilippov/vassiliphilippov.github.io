rice weevil

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪs ˌwiːvəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪs ˌwiːvəl / (BrE)
a small brown beetle that infests stored grains, especially rice, and is considered a pest
pest beetle that infests stored grain

Examples

The warehouse lost much of its rice stock to a rice weevil infestation.
Proper storage methods can help prevent rice weevils from destroying grain supplies.
A single rice weevil can lay hundreds of eggs inside grains of rice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weevil - any small beetle of the Curculionidae family, many of which are pests
