genus Galago

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡəˈleɪ.ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡəˈleɪ.ɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of small nocturnal primates known as bush babies, native to Africa.
a genus of African bush babies (primates)

Examples

The genus Galago includes several species of agile bush babies found throughout sub-Saharan Africa.
Researchers studying nocturnal primates often focus on the genus Galago for their unique behaviors.
Members of the genus Galago are known for their large eyes and remarkable leaping abilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bush baby - a small, nocturnal African primate of the Galago genus

Etymology

From Latin 'Galago', the scientific name assigned to this genus.
