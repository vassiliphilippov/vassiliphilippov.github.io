belladonna lily

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛləˈdɑnə ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˌbɛləˈdɒnə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a South African plant of the amaryllis family, often cultivated for its fragrant white or rose-colored flowers
South African flowering plant, amaryllis family

Examples

The belladonna lily blooms in late summer, adding a splash of color to the garden.
She planted a row of belladonna lily bulbs along the walkway last spring.
After a week in the vase, the belladonna lily still filled the room with its sweet fragrance.

Etymology

From Italian 'belladonna' (beautiful lady) and 'lily', named for its beauty.
