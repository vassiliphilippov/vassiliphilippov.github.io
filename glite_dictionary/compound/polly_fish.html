polly fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑli fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒli fɪʃ / (BrE)
A brightly colored tropical fish with a beak-like mouth formed by fused teeth, commonly known as a parrotfish.
colorful tropical fish with beak-like mouth

Examples

The coral reef was full of vibrant polly fish darting among the rocks.
A polly fish uses its beak-like mouth to scrape algae from coral.
Children loved watching the colorful polly fishes at the aquarium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parrotfish - brightly colored tropical fish with fused teeth forming a beak-like mouth
