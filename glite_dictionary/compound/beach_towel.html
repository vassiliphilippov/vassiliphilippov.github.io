beach towel

1. NOUN

/ bitʃ ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
a large towel used to dry oneself after swimming, especially at the beach
large towel for drying after swimming

Examples

She spread her beach towel on the sand and lay down to sunbathe.
After swimming, he wrapped himself in a colorful beach towel.
Every year, we buy a new beach towel for our summer holiday.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: towel - a piece of absorbent cloth for drying or wiping
