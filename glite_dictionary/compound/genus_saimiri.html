genus Saimiri

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs saɪˈmiː.ri / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs saɪˈmiː.ri / (BrE)
A genus of small New World monkeys known as squirrel monkeys, native to Central and South America.
a genus of squirrel monkeys

Examples

Researchers observed the behavior of the genus Saimiri in its natural rainforest habitat.
The genus Saimiri includes several species of squirrel monkeys.
Characteristics of the genus Saimiri distinguish it from other New World monkey genera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squirrel monkey - a small monkey of tropical America belonging to the genus Saimiri

Etymology

From Latin 'Saimiri', the genus name, used in taxonomic classification.
