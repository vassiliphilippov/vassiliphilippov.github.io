committal service

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪt̬.əl ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / kəˈmɪt.əl ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a ceremony at which a body is committed to its final resting place, such as a grave or cremation site
funeral ceremony for burial or cremation

Examples

The committal service will be held at the cemetery following the funeral.
Family and friends gathered for the committal service to say their final goodbyes.
The priest spoke a few words during the committal service as the casket was lowered.

Synset Examples

the committal service will be held next Monday

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funeral - a ceremony in which a deceased person is buried or cremated
  • Synonym: burial - the act or ceremony of placing a dead body in a grave

Etymology

From 'committal' (act of committing) and 'service' (ceremony or ritual).
