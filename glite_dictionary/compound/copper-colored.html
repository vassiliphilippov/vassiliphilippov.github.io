copper-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑpɚˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəˌkʌləd / (BrE)
Having the reddish-brown color of copper metal.
having the color of copper

Examples

The statue had a copper-colored patina from years of exposure to the elements.
She wore a stunning copper-colored dress to the gala.
The bird's copper-colored feathers shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronze - a metallic brownish color similar to copper but usually darker
