old geezer

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈɡizər / (AmE), / əʊld ˈɡiːzə / (BrE)
an informal and often humorous or mildly disrespectful term for an elderly man
elderly man (informal, humorous)
informal

Examples

Everyone at the café knows the old geezer who sits by the window every morning.
My neighbor is a real old geezer with plenty of funny stories to tell.
They laughed when the old geezer tried to show off his dance moves at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: codger - an old man (informal, often humorous)
  • Synonym: old fogey - an old-fashioned, conservative, or fussy elderly man

2. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈɡizər / (AmE), / əʊld ˈɡiːzə / (BrE)
an elderly man, often described in a humorous or slightly dismissive way
an elderly man (humorous, informal)
informal

Examples

My grandfather may be an old geezer, but he still goes hiking every weekend.
The old geezer across the street always waves at the children as they pass by.
Don't underestimate that old geezer; he used to be a champion boxer.

Synset Examples

That old geezer always has the best stories to tell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geezer - an old man, especially one who is odd or eccentric
