Facebook slut

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪsˌbʊk slʌt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsbʊk slʌt / (BrE)
A person who frequently posts content on Facebook, often to an excessive degree; used informally and sometimes as a joke or insult.
someone who posts excessively on Facebook
slang informal offensive

Examples

Don't be such a Facebook slut—you've posted three status updates just this morning.
He joked that his daily photo uploads made him a true Facebook slut.
She laughed at being called a Facebook slut by her friends after posting vacation albums every day.

Synset Examples

She called herself a Facebook slut for posting daily.
People often joke about being a Facebook slut online.
His friends teased him for being a Facebook slut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oversharer - a person who reveals too much personal information online
