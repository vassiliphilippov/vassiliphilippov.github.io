rutilant hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈruː.tɪ.lənt hɛr / (AmE), / ˈruː.tɪ.lənt heə / (BrE)
Hair that shines with a reddish or golden color.
hair shining reddish or golden

Examples

Her rutilant hair shimmered in the late afternoon light.
He admired the rutilant hair of the old portrait, catching subtle copper highlights.
The actress was known for her striking rutilant hair, which glowed beneath the stage lights.

Synset Examples

Her rutilant hair shimmered in the sunlight.

Etymology

From Latin 'rutilans', present participle of 'rutilare' (to redden).
