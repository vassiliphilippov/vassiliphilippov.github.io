Joe Blow

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒoʊ ˈbloʊ / (AmE), / ˌdʒəʊ ˈbləʊ / (BrE)
an ordinary or average man; a person representing the typical, everyday individual
average or ordinary man
informal american

Examples

It's not something Joe Blow off the street would understand.
The government needs to make policies that help Joe Blow, not just the wealthy.
Joe Blow might not read the fine print before signing a contract.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Joe Public - an ordinary or average person, especially in the UK
  • Synonym: John Doe - a generic name used for an unidentified or average man

Etymology

American English, with 'Joe' as a generic first name and 'Blow' as an arbitrary surname.
