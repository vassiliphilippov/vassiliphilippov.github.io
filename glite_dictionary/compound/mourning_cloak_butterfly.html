mourning cloak butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ kloʊk ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ kləʊk ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
A large butterfly found in temperate regions, with dark purple wings bordered by yellow.
large butterfly with dark wings and yellow edges

Examples

The mourning cloak butterfly is often seen in early spring before most other butterflies emerge.
With its dark wings trimmed in yellow, the mourning cloak butterfly is easy to identify.
Children watched as a mourning cloak butterfly landed on a sunlit tree branch.
