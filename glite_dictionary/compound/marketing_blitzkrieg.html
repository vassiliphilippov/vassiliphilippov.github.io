marketing blitzkrieg

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkətɪŋ ˈblɪtskriɡ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ ˈblɪtskriːɡ / (BrE)
an intense and rapid marketing campaign aimed at quickly achieving maximum impact
an intense, fast marketing campaign

Examples

The company's marketing blitzkrieg flooded social media with advertisements in just a few days.
After the marketing blitzkrieg, product sales soared well beyond expectations.
Their latest marketing blitzkrieg left competitors scrambling to keep up.

Synset Examples

The marketing blitzkrieg launched last week was impressive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing campaign - a coordinated marketing effort to promote a product

Etymology

'Blitzkrieg' is from German, meaning 'lightning war', originally a military term for fast, overwhelming attack. Applied metaphorically to marketing.
