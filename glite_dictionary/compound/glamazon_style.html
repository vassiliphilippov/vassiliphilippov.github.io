glamazon style

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæm.əˌzɑn staɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡlæm.əˌzɒn staɪl / (BrE)
A fashionable and striking manner of dress or appearance, especially one that exudes glamor and confidence.
strikingly fashionable appearance

Examples

Her glamazon style made her stand out on the red carpet.
Many models in the fashion show embraced a bold glamazon style.
He admired her confidence and her sense of glamazon style.

Synset Examples

Her glamazon style turned heads wherever she went.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glamorous - full of glamor; elegant and attractive

Etymology

Blend of 'glamorous' and 'Amazon' (mythical race of warrior women), popularized in fashion and entertainment contexts.
