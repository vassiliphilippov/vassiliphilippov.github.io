people's republic

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəlz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈpiːplz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
(often People's Republic) a state that is officially governed by representatives elected by the people, typically with a socialist or communist political system.
state governed by elected representatives, often socialist/communist

Examples

The establishment of a people's republic marked a new political era in the country.
After years of struggle, they proclaimed the region a people's republic.
Several countries styled themselves as a people's republic during the twentieth century.

Synset Examples

The people's republic declared independence last year.
The people's republic was established after the revolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: republic - a country governed by elected representatives and a president rather than a monarch
  • Synonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members
