insurance premium

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊr.əns ˈpriː.mi.əm / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns ˈpriː.mi.əm / (BrE)
a regular payment made to an insurance company for insurance coverage
payment for insurance coverage

Examples

Your insurance premium may increase if you make frequent claims.
We pay our insurance premium annually to keep our coverage active.
She compared several companies to find the lowest insurance premium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premium - an amount to be paid for an insurance policy
