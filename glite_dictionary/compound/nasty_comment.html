nasty comment

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæsti ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti ˈkɒmənt / (BrE)
an unpleasant or offensive remark intended to hurt or upset someone
unpleasant or offensive remark

Examples

She made a nasty comment about his appearance during the meeting.
The internet is full of people leaving nasty comments on videos and posts.
He regretted his nasty comment as soon as he saw her reaction.

Synset Examples

That was a nasty comment to make in public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insult - a remark or action that is rude or offensive
  • Antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
