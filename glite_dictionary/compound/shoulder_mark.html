shoulder mark

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə mɑːk / (BrE)
an ornamental badge or piece of fabric worn on the shoulder of a uniform to indicate rank
badge on shoulder indicating rank

Examples

The officer's shoulder mark displayed his new rank clearly.
All members of the regiment were required to sew a shoulder mark onto their uniforms.
She noticed the gold stripes on his shoulder mark as he entered the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epaulet - an ornamental shoulder piece on a uniform
