mushroom wine sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌʃruːm waɪn sɔs / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃrəm waɪn sɔːs / (BrE)
a brown sauce made with mushrooms and red wine or Madeira, often served with meats
sauce of mushrooms and red wine

Examples

The filet mignon was topped with a rich mushroom wine sauce that enhanced its flavor.
She learned to make mushroom wine sauce for chicken after reading a French cookbook.
A classic beef dish often features mushroom wine sauce poured generously over the tender meat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauce - a typically liquid or semi-liquid substance served with food to add moistness and flavor
