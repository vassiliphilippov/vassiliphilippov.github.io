musical theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪkəl ˈθiətər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
A form of theatre that combines songs, spoken dialogue, acting, and dance to tell a story.
theatre combining song, dance, and dialogue

Examples

She has always dreamed of performing in musical theater on Broadway.
Many high schools put on a musical theater production each year to showcase their students' talents.
The city is known for its vibrant musical theater scene, attracting visitors from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musical - a play or film with songs and dancing as part of the story
