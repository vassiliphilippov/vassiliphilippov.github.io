practice of law

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræktɪs əv lɔ / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪs əv lɔː / (BrE)
the professional work carried out by lawyers, involving the application of legal knowledge in advising clients, preparing legal documents, and representing clients in court
work and profession of lawyers
legal

Examples

After ten years in accounting, she finally began the practice of law in her hometown.
Regulating the practice of law helps to maintain public trust in the legal system.
Many law graduates aspire to enter the practice of law after passing the bar exam.

Synset Examples

he studied law at Yale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: legal profession - the profession involving lawyers and judges
