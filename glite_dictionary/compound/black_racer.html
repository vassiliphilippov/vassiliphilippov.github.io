black racer

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈreɪsər / (AmE), / blæk ˈreɪsə / (BrE)
a nonvenomous, fast-moving, black-colored snake found in the eastern United States, typically growing up to six feet long
a large, fast black snake of eastern US

Examples

A black racer darted across the trail and disappeared into the underbrush.
Despite its size, the black racer poses no threat to humans.
We spotted a young black racer sunning itself on the stone wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racer - any of several fast-moving, slender snakes
