undreamed of

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdriːmd əv / (AmE), / ʌnˈdriːmd ɒv / (BrE)
so remarkable or extraordinary as not to have been imagined ever before
unimaginably extraordinary or unexpected

Examples

The invention brought undreamed of changes to people’s lives.
She achieved success undreamed of by previous generations.
The explorers discovered riches undreamed of in the remote valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginable - impossible to think of or believe; inconceivable
