army unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmi ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a group that forms an organized subdivision of an army, such as a platoon, company, battalion, or regiment
group forming part of an army

Examples

The government deployed an army unit to assist in disaster relief after the hurricane.
Each army unit received its orders before the start of the operation.
He served in an elite army unit specializing in reconnaissance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military unit - a group, subdivision, or formation within the military
