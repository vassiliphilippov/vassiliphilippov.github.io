volleyball tournament

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɑliˌbɔl ˈtʊrnəmənt / (AmE), / ˈvɒlibɔːl ˈtʊənəmənt / (BrE)
a sporting competition in which several teams or individuals play volleyball matches to determine an overall winner
competitive volleyball event

Examples

Our school is hosting a volleyball tournament next weekend and expects teams from all over the city.
They won first place in the annual volleyball tournament held at the beach.
The company organized a volleyball tournament to raise funds for charity.

Synset Examples

They organized a bolleyball tournament for the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tournament - a sporting competition involving a series of games or matches
