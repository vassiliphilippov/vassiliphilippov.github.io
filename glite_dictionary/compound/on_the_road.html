on the road

1. NOUN

/ ɑn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / ɒn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
travelling from place to place, especially as part of one's work or while touring
travelling from place to place (touring)

Examples

After two weeks on the road, the band was ready for a break.
The team will be on the road for the next five games.
She loves the freedom of being on the road and discovering new places.

Synset Examples

they took the show on the road
they lost all their games on the road

Related Terms

  • Synonym: touring - travelling, especially for performances or events
  • Antonym: at home - in one's usual place; not travelling
