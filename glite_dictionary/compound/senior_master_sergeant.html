senior master sergeant

1. NOUN

/ ˌsiːniɚ ˈmæstɚ ˌsɝːdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌsiːniə ˈmɑːstə ˌsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
a senior noncommissioned officer rank in the US Air Force, above master sergeant and below chief master sergeant
Air Force noncommissioned officer rank

Examples

After twenty years of service, he was promoted to senior master sergeant in the Air Force.
The senior master sergeant led his squad with years of expertise and authority.
Several senior master sergeants attended the leadership training session this year.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: master sergeant - noncommissioned officer rank in the US Army and Air Force
  • Degree Higher: chief master sergeant - highest noncommissioned officer rank in the US Air Force
