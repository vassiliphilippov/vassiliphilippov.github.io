main entry word

1. NOUN

/ meɪn ˈɛntri wɝd / (AmE), / meɪn ˈɛntri wɜːd / (BrE)
The form of a word that appears as the heading in a dictionary entry, used for alphabetizing and organizing definitions.
canonical dictionary headword

Examples

Every dictionary lists the main entry word in bold for easy reference.
Sometimes the spelling of the main entry word differs from less common variant forms.
Children can quickly find information by scanning for the main entry word on each page.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headword - word or phrase that serves as the heading of a dictionary entry
