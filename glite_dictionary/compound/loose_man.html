loose man

1. NOUN

/ lus mæn / (AmE), / luːs mæn / (BrE)
A man who is sexually promiscuous or unfaithful to his partner; a male adulterer.
sexually unfaithful man; male adulterer

Examples

Rumors began to spread when people started calling him a loose man after his affair was discovered.
His reputation as a loose man made it difficult for anyone to trust him in relationships.
She confronted her husband, accusing him of being a loose man who could not be faithful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philanderer - a man who engages in love affairs frivolously or casually
  • Synonym: adulterer - a person who commits adultery
