radio transmitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ trænzˈmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ tranzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a device in a radio system that sends out radio signals or broadcasts
device that sends out radio signals

Examples

The engineer repaired the radio transmitter after it stopped sending signals to the tower.
A powerful radio transmitter can broadcast messages over long distances.
All emergency vehicles are equipped with a radio transmitter for communication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmitter - device that sends out signals or messages
