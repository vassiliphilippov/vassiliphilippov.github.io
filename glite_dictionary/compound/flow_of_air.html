flow of air

1. NOUN

/ floʊ əv ɛr / (AmE), / fləʊ əv eə / (BrE)
the movement or circulation of air, especially as directed or affected by natural forces or mechanical devices
movement or circulation of air

Examples

The engineer measured the flow of air through the ventilation system to ensure proper circulation.
She placed the plant near the window to benefit from the gentle flow of air.
Reducing obstructions in the duct will improve the flow of air throughout the building.

Synset Examples

she adjusted the fan so that the airflow was directed right at her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airflow - movement of air, especially directed by a fan or vent
  • Synonym: draft - a current of air, especially one flowing indoors
