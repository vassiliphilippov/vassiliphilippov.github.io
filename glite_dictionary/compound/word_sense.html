word sense

1. NOUN

/ wɝːd sɛns / (AmE), / wɜːd sɛns / (BrE)
a distinct meaning accepted as one possible interpretation of a word
distinct accepted meaning of a word

Examples

Linguists often study each word sense separately to avoid confusion.
The dictionary lists several word senses for the verb 'run.'
Understanding the correct word sense depends on the context of the sentence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meaning - the idea or concept that a word expresses
  • Synonym: sense - one of the meanings of a word or phrase
