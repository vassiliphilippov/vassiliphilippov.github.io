flight simulator

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪt ˌsɪmjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪt ˌsɪmjʊˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a machine used on the ground to imitate the experience and conditions of flying an aircraft, often for training purposes
machine for training pilots by simulating flight

Examples

The pilot spent hours practicing emergency landings in the flight simulator.
Using a flight simulator, students can learn to handle challenging weather without leaving the ground.
Modern flight simulators provide highly realistic visuals and motion feedback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simulator - a device that simulates conditions for training or research purposes
