feeling of being untethered

1. NOUN

/ ˈfilɪŋ əv ˈbiɪŋ ʌnˈtɛðɚd / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ əv ˈbiːɪŋ ʌnˈtɛðəd / (BrE)
a sense of freedom and absence of restrictions or limitations
sense of freedom and lack of restraint

Examples

She experienced a feeling of being untethered after leaving her demanding job.
There is a unique feeling of being untethered when you travel alone in a new country.
The sudden move to a new city gave him a feeling of being untethered, both exciting and daunting.

Synset Examples

She enjoyed the feeling of being untethered during her travels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liberation - the act of setting someone free; release from restriction
  • Antonym: restriction - a limitation or control placed over actions or conditions
