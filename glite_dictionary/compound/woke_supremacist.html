woke supremacist

1. NOUN

/ woʊk səˈprɛməsɪst / (AmE), / wəʊk səˈprɛməsɪst / (BrE)
a person who strongly promotes progressive social views, often to the extent of believing these views should dominate social or political discourse
strong advocate for progressive social views

Examples

Some critics labeled her a woke supremacist after her passionate speech on social justice.
The phrase woke supremacist has appeared in online debates about political polarization.
He was called a woke supremacist for insisting only his perspective on social issues was acceptable.

Synset Examples

He was accused of being a woke supremacist for his radical views.
The term woke supremacist is often used in heated political debates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: progressive - favoring or advocating social reform
  • Degree Higher: supremacist - a person who believes in the superiority of a particular group

Etymology

From 'woke' (alert to social injustice) and 'supremacist' (one who believes in the superiority of a particular group).
