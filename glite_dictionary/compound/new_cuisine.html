new cuisine

1. NOUN

/ nu kuˈzin / (AmE), / njuː kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
food or culinary styles from cultures that one has not experienced before, especially when referring to trying dishes from around the world
food from unfamiliar cultures

Examples

Every time I travel, I make it a point to sample the new cuisine of the region.
She loves cooking and is always searching for recipes from new cuisine to try at home.
Our city offers food festivals that showcase new cuisine from around the globe.

Synset Examples

They delight in exploring new cuisines on their travels.
