Jesus junk

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːzəs dʒʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs dʒʌŋk / (BrE)
Christian-themed trinkets or memorabilia, often considered kitschy or in poor taste.
kitschy Christian-themed trinkets
informal

Examples

His grandmother's shelves are packed with Jesus junk from decades of church bazaars.
They laughed at the collection of Jesus junk on sale at the roadside stand.
She regretted buying so much Jesus junk on her vacation, but the souvenirs made her smile.

Synset Examples

He collects all sorts of Jesus junk for his display.
Her house is filled with Jesus junk from thrift stores.
I found some hilarious Jesus junk at the flea market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitsch - Art, objects, or design considered to be in poor taste because of excessive garishness or sentimentality
  • Synonym: memorabilia - Objects kept or collected because of their historical interest, especially those associated with memorable people or events
