true identity

1. NOUN

/ tru aɪˈdɛntɪti / (AmE), / tru ˈaɪdɛntɪti / (BrE)
a person's real or genuine self, as opposed to any false or superficial persona
a person's real or genuine self

Examples

After years of hiding, she finally revealed her true identity to her friends.
The superhero kept his true identity a secret from the world.
It took time and reflection for him to understand his true identity.

Synset Examples

Being a transgenderist means embracing one's true identity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: false identity - an assumed or fake persona, not one's real self
