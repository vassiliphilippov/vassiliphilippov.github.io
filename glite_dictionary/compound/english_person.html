English person

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of England
someone from England

Examples

An English person is typically proud of their country's rich history.
The museum guide was an English person who spoke fluent French.
She met an English person while traveling in Italy, and they became friends.
