coffee filter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.fi ˌfɪl.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒf.i ˌfɪl.tə / (BrE)
A paper or mesh filter used to separate coffee grounds from liquid when brewing coffee.
paper or mesh filter for brewing coffee

Examples

He placed a fresh coffee filter in the machine before adding the grounds.
Most coffee filters are made of paper, but some are reusable metal types.
Don't forget to change the coffee filter every day to keep your coffee tasting fresh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter - device or material for removing impurities or particles
