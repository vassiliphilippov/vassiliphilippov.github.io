New Year

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈjɪr / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɪə / (BrE)
the calendar year that has just started
the year just begun

Examples

Everyone makes resolutions at the start of the New Year.
The New Year brings a sense of renewal and hope for many people.
He returned to work refreshed and optimistic for the New Year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: old year - the calendar year that has just ended
