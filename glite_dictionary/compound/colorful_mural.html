colorful mural

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚfəl ˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl ˈmjʊərəl / (BrE)
a large painted picture on a wall featuring many bright or varied colors
large, brightly colored wall painting

Examples

A colorful mural covered the entire side of the elementary school.
The artist was commissioned to create a colorful mural depicting the community's history.
Tourists often stop to photograph the colorful mural in the city center.

Synset Examples

A colorful mural formed the backdrop for the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mural - a painting or artwork painted directly on a wall
