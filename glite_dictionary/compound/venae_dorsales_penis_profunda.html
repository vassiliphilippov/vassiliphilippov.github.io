venae dorsales penis profunda

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːniː dɔrˈseɪliz ˈpiːnɪs prəˈfʊndə / (AmE), / ˈviːniː dɔːˈseɪliːz ˈpiːnɪs prəˈfʊndə / (BrE)
The deep dorsal vein of the penis, which drains blood from the penis and is a tributary of the prostatic plexus.
deep dorsal vein of the penis (anatomy)
medical

Examples

During the surgical procedure, the venae dorsales penis profunda was carefully preserved to prevent excessive bleeding.
Damage to the venae dorsales penis profunda can lead to complications such as impaired venous drainage.
The urologist examined the patient's venae dorsales penis profunda for signs of thrombosis.

Etymology

Latin: 'venae dorsales' (dorsal veins) + 'penis' (penis) + 'profunda' (deep).
