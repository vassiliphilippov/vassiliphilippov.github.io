toy box

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box or chest for storing children's toys
box for storing toys

Examples

She asked her son to put his blocks in the toy box before dinner.
The brightly painted toy box sat in the corner of the nursery.
After the playdate, the children helped tidy up by filling the toy box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toy chest - a chest for storing toys
