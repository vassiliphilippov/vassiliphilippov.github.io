orange mushroom pimple

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɪndʒ ˈmʌʃˌɹʊm ˈpɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈmʌʃˌrʊm ˈpɪmpəl / (BrE)
A type of parasitic fungus (Hypomyces lactifluorum) that grows on certain mushrooms, turning them orange and sometimes giving them a pimpled appearance. Also known as lobster mushroom.
a fungus that parasitizes mushrooms, turning them orange

Examples

The forager was excited to find an orange mushroom pimple nestled among the forest leaves.
Chefs prize the orange mushroom pimple for its unique flavor and striking appearance.
The guidebook describes how Hypomyces lactifluorum creates the distinctive orange mushroom pimple on its host.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobster mushroom - an edible fungus formed by Hypomyces lactifluorum infecting certain mushrooms
