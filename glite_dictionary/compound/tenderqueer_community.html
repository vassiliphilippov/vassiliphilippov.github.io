tenderqueer community

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛndɚˌkwɪr kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəˌkwɪə kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group of queer individuals characterized by sensitivity and empathy, often emphasizing emotional openness and mutual support.
group of empathetic queer individuals

Examples

The tenderqueer community often organizes support groups to create safe spaces for sharing emotions.
Many young people find acceptance within the tenderqueer community because of its focus on kindness and understanding.
Workshops led by the tenderqueer community highlight the value of emotional expression.

Synset Examples

The tenderqueer community shares their stories to foster understanding.

Etymology

Blend of 'tender' (gentle, sensitive) and 'queer' (relating to LGBTQ+ identity), combined with 'community'.
