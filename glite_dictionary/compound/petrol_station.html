petrol station

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtɹəl ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɹəl ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where petrol and often other vehicle fuels are sold and where vehicles can be refueled.
place for purchasing petrol for vehicles
british

Examples

I stopped at a petrol station to fill up the car before the long drive.
They built a new petrol station just outside the village.
The nearest petrol station is ten miles from here, so plan your trip carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas station - place where gasoline is sold, chiefly in American English
