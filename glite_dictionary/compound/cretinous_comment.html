cretinous comment

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛt.nəs ˈkɑː.mɛnt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛt.ɪ.nəs ˈkɒm.ənt / (BrE)
a remark that is extremely foolish or stupid
foolish or stupid remark

Examples

He made a cretinous comment during the meeting that left everyone speechless.
The politician's cretinous comment was widely criticized by the media.
Her cretinous comments only served to embarrass her further.

Synset Examples

His cretinous comments only made the situation worse.
The cretinous remarks from the crowd were shocking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid remark - a remark lacking intelligence or sense
  • Antonym: insightful comment - a remark showing deep understanding

Etymology

From 'cretinous', related to ‘cretin’ (from French *crétin*, originally meaning a person with a certain medical condition, later a general insult), combined with 'comment'.
