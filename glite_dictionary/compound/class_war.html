class war

1. NOUN

/ klæs wɔr / (AmE), / klɑːs wɔː / (BrE)
conflict between social or economic classes, especially between the working class and the ruling or capitalist class
conflict between social or economic classes

Examples

The novel depicts a class war erupting in the aftermath of economic crisis.
Debates about taxation and social policy often reflect an underlying class war.
Many historians argue that the industrial revolution intensified the class war in Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: class struggle - conflict between different classes in society
