pocket calculator

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈkælkjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
a small electronic calculator designed to be held in the hand or carried in a pocket
small portable calculator

Examples

He pulled a pocket calculator from his briefcase to check the figures.
With the rise of smartphones, the traditional pocket calculator has become less common.
Students are allowed to use a pocket calculator during this math exam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculator - an electronic or mechanical device for making mathematical calculations
