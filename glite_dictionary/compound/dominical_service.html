dominical service

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɪnɪkəl ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / dəˈmɪnɪkəl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
A Christian worship service held on Sunday.
Christian worship on Sunday

Examples

She found comfort in attending the dominical service each week.
The community gathered for the dominical service at the old church.
Holiday travelers missed their usual dominical service while abroad.

Synset Examples

They attended the dominical service every Sunday.

Etymology

From Latin 'dominicalis', meaning 'of the Lord', referring to Sunday.
