hand mixer

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈmɪksɚ / (AmE), / hænd ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a handheld electric or manual device used for mixing or beating food ingredients
handheld kitchen mixing device

Examples

She used her hand mixer to quickly whip the cream for the dessert.
A hand mixer makes it easy to blend cake batter evenly.
After the old hand mixer broke, they bought a new one with more speed settings.

Synset Examples

The motor of a hand mixer must be lightweight as it is supported by the user during use.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stand mixer - a kitchen appliance with a stationary base used for mixing food
