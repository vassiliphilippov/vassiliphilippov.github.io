alimony drone

1. NOUN

/ ˈælɪˌmoʊni droʊn / (AmE), / ˈælɪˌməʊni drəʊn / (BrE)
a person who is dependent on or burdened by alimony payments, often feeling trapped or obligated
person dependent on alimony payments
informal

Examples

After his divorce, he often complained about being an alimony drone to his friends.
She refused to become an alimony drone, determined to regain her independence.
The movie depicted the character as an alimony drone stuck in a monotonous routine.

Synset Examples
