sunburst pleat

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌbɝst pliːt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnbɜːst pliːt / (BrE)
A knife pleat cut on the bias of fabric to create a flared, radiating effect, often used in skirts or dresses.
a pleat creating a flared, radiating effect

Examples

The designer added a sunburst pleat to the skirt for dramatic flair.
Her dress featured elegant sunburst pleat details that caught the light beautifully.
A sunburst pleat can transform a simple fabric into a stunning garment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleat - a fold in cloth that is sewn in place
