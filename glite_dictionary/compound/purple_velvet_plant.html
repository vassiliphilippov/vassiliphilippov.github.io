purple velvet plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈvɛlvət plænt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈvɛlvɪt plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical foliage plant native to Java, valued for its attractive velvety leaves covered with violet-purple hairs.
ornamental plant with velvety purple leaves

Examples

The purple velvet plant adds a splash of color to indoor gardens with its vivid leaves.
Gardeners admire the purple velvet plant for its soft, hairy foliage.
He placed a purple velvet plant on the windowsill to brighten up the room.
