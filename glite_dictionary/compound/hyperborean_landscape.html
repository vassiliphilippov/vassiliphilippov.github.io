hyperborean landscape

1. NOUN

/ haɪ.pərˈbɔː.ri.ən ˈlænd.skeɪp / (AmE), / haɪ.pəˈbɔː.ri.ən ˈlan(d).skeɪp / (BrE)
an extremely cold or icy landscape, typically found in far northern or Arctic regions
icy, extremely cold landscape

Examples

The expedition trekked across the hyperborean landscape, braving snowstorms and subzero temperatures.
A sense of endless isolation hung over the hyperborean landscape stretching beyond the icebound horizon.
Photographers were drawn to the eerie light and stark beauty of the hyperborean landscape.

Synset Examples

The explorers found hyperborean landscapes in the Arctic.
She described her trip to the hyperborean region with awe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arctic landscape - landscape of the Arctic region
  • Synonym: tundra - a flat, treeless Arctic region

Etymology

From Greek Hyperborea, a mythical land 'beyond the North Wind', plus 'landscape'.
