Peach State

1. PROPN

/ pitʃ steɪt / (AmE), / piːtʃ steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the U.S. state of Georgia, in the southeastern United States
nickname for Georgia, USA

Examples

Atlanta is the capital city of the Peach State.
Tourists flock to the Peach State in spring to see the peach blossoms.
She moved from New York to the Peach State for a warmer climate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Georgia - a state in the southeastern United States

Etymology

Named for Georgia's reputation for producing peaches.
