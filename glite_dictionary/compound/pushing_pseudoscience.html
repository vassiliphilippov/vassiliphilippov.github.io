to pushing pseudoscience

1. VERB

/ ˈpʊʃɪŋ ˌsuːdoʊˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃɪŋ ˌsjuːdəʊˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
actively promoting or supporting scientific claims or theories that are false or not supported by evidence
promoting false scientific claims

Examples

Some social media influencers have been pushing pseudoscience about health supplements.
The scientist was criticized for pushing pseudoscience in his public talks.
Activists accused the company of pushing pseudoscience to boost product sales.

Synset Examples

The conference was filled with quackademics pushing pseudoscience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudoscience - a system of beliefs mistaken as being based on scientific method
