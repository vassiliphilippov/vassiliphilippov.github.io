drummy texture

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌmi ˈtɛkstʃər / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmi ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
A texture that is dry and crumbly, often used to describe baked goods or food items.
dry and crumbly texture

Examples

The bread from that bakery often has a drummy texture, making it perfect for dipping in soup.
After baking too long, the cookies developed a drummy texture and crumbled easily in the hand.
Her favorite type of shortbread has a drummy texture that melts in the mouth.

Synset Examples

The cake had a drummy texture that everyone loved.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crumbly - breaking easily into small pieces
