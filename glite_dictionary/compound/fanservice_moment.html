fanservice moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌsɝːvɪs ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈfanˌsɜːvɪs ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a scene or element in a work of fiction added specifically to please or attract the audience, often by appealing to fans' interests or desires
scene added for fan appeal

Examples

The show included a fanservice moment that excited many longtime viewers.
Critics argued that the movie's fanservice moments distracted from the main plot.
Some anime series are known for featuring at least one fanservice moment in every episode.

Synset Examples

They added fanservice moments to attract a larger audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanservice - the practice of adding material to please fans

Etymology

Blend of 'fan' (enthusiast) and 'service,' originating in Japanese pop culture (fan sābisu).
