chem lab

1. NOUN

/ kɛm læb / (AmE), / kɛm læb / (BrE)
a laboratory where chemistry experiments or research are conducted, especially in educational settings
chemistry laboratory; room for chemistry experiments

Examples

The students spent the afternoon mixing solutions in the chem lab.
Safety goggles must be worn at all times in the chem lab.
She developed a passion for science while working in the college chem lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments, research, or teaching
  • Synonym: chemistry laboratory - a laboratory dedicated to experiments and research in chemistry
