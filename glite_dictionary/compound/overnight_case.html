overnight case

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt keɪs / (AmE), / ˈəʊvənaɪt keɪs / (BrE)
a small suitcase or bag designed to carry clothing and essentials for an overnight trip
small bag for overnight travel

Examples

She packed her overnight case with toiletries and a change of clothes.
His overnight case fit perfectly under the seat on the train.
I grabbed my overnight case for the business trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overnight bag - a bag for carrying items needed for an overnight stay
