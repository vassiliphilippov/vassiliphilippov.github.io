wedding present

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌprɛzənt / (BrE)
a gift given to someone on the occasion of their marriage
gift for a wedding

Examples

They bought a beautiful vase as a wedding present for the newlyweds.
Receiving a wedding present from her grandparents was very touching.
The couple wrote thank-you notes for every wedding present they received.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding gift - a gift given to a couple at their wedding
  • Degree Lower: birthday present - a gift given for someone's birthday
