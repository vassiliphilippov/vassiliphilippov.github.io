mouth organ

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊθ ˌɔr.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθ ˌɔː.ɡən / (BrE)
a small rectangular musical instrument with a row of free reeds, played by blowing or drawing air through openings
small instrument played by blowing into reeds

Examples

He learned to play the mouth organ as a child during long train journeys.
She bought a new mouth organ at the music shop downtown.
The sound of his mouth organ echoed through the quiet campground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonica - a musical instrument with metal reeds played by blowing or drawing air
