Christian Bible

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstʃən ˈbaɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən ˈbaɪbəl / (BrE)
the collection of sacred scriptures of Christianity, comprising the Old Testament and the New Testament
the holy scriptures of Christianity

Examples

Many families keep a Christian Bible displayed in their home.
She read passages from the Christian Bible during the ceremony.
The Christian Bible has influenced Western literature and art for centuries.

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible - the holy scriptures of the Christian religion
  • Synonym: Scripture - the sacred writings of Christianity contained in the Bible
  • Antonym: Qur'an - the Islamic sacred book

Etymology

Bible from Latin 'Biblia', meaning 'books'; 'Christian' from Latin 'Christianus', follower of Christ.
