Slip Slop Slap

1. NOUN

/ slɪp slɑp slæp / (AmE), / slɪp slɒp slæp / (BrE)
a slogan used to promote sun protection, advising people to slip on a shirt, slop on sunscreen, and slap on a hat
sun protection campaign slogan

Examples

Australians are reminded every summer to Slip Slop Slap before going outdoors.
The Slip Slop Slap message has helped reduce rates of skin cancer.
Teachers taught the children to Slip Slop Slap before recess.

Synset Examples

Remember to Slip Slop Slap to protect your skin!

Etymology

Australian campaign slogan coined in the early 1980s for skin cancer prevention.
