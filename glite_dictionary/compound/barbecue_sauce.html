barbecue sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːrbɪkjuː sɔs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪkjuː sɔːs / (BrE)
a spicy, sweet, and tangy sauce, typically tomato-based, used for flavoring and serving with grilled or smoked foods, especially meat
spicy, sweet sauce for grilled foods

Examples

He brushed barbecue sauce over the ribs before putting them on the grill.
Some people prefer their chicken wings tossed in spicy barbecue sauce.
We ran out of barbecue sauce, so I made some from scratch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ketchup - a seasoned tomato sauce typically used as a condiment
