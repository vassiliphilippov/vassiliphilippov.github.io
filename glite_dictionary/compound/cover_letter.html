cover letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚ ˌlɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə ˌlɛtə / (BrE)
A letter sent with other documents, especially a résumé or application, providing additional information about the sender.
letter sent with documents for extra information

Examples

Please include a cover letter with your job application to introduce yourself.
Her cover letter highlighted her achievements and explained why she was interested in the position.
The recruiter appreciated receiving a detailed cover letter along with the applicant's résumé.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: application letter - a letter sent to request employment
