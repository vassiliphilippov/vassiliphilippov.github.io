climbing equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ɪˌkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ɪˌkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the specialized equipment or gear used for rock climbing, mountaineering, or similar climbing activities
equipment used for climbing

Examples

Proper climbing equipment is essential for ensuring your safety when ascending steep rock faces.
