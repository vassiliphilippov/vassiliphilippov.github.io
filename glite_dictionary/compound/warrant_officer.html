warrant officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrənt ˌɑfəsər / (AmE), / ˈwɒrənt ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a military officer who holds rank by virtue of a warrant, ranking above enlisted personnel and below commissioned officers
military officer of intermediate rank

Examples

He served as a warrant officer in the Navy for over a decade.
The warrant officer led the inspection of the barracks this morning.
After years of service, she was promoted to the rank of warrant officer.
