Pierre Laporte Bridge

1. PROPN

/ piːˌɛr lɑːˈpɔːrt brɪdʒ / (AmE), / piːˈɛə ləˈpɔːt brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a suspension bridge across the Saint Lawrence River in Quebec, Canada, named after Pierre Laporte
suspension bridge in Quebec, Canada

Examples

The Pierre Laporte Bridge is the longest suspension bridge in Canada.
Traffic was delayed on the Pierre Laporte Bridge due to strong winds.
The Pierre Laporte Bridge connects Quebec City to Lévis over the Saint Lawrence River.

Etymology

Named after Pierre Laporte, a Quebec government minister.
