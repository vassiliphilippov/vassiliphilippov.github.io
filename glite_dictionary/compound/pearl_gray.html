pearl gray

1. ADJ

/ pɜrl ɡreɪ / (AmE), / pɜːl ɡreɪ / (BrE)
A pale grey color with a subtle pearly or silvery sheen.
a pale grey with a pearly sheen

Examples

The walls were painted a delicate pearl gray to create a calm atmosphere.
She wore a pearl gray dress that shimmered under the lights.
His car is a sleek pearl gray, making it look both modern and elegant.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: gray - a color between black and white
  • Degree Higher: silver - a shiny grey-white color similar to that of silver metal
