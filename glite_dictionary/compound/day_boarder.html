day boarder

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌbɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌbɔːdə / (BrE)
A schoolchild who attends a boarding school and has meals at school but returns home to sleep each night.
boarding school pupil who eats at school but sleeps at home

Examples

As a day boarder, she spends most of her time at school but goes home every evening.
The school offers facilities for both day boarders and full boarders.
My parents chose to make me a day boarder so I could enjoy school life but still live at home.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: boarder - a pupil who lives at school during the term
