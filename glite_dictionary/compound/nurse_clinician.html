nurse clinician

1. NOUN

/ nɜrs klɪˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / nɜːs klɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a registered nurse who has had advanced training and is qualified to perform many tasks normally done by a physician
advanced practice registered nurse
medical

Examples

After years of experience, she became a nurse clinician and started seeing patients independently.
The hospital employs several nurse clinicians to help manage chronic illness clinics.
As a nurse clinician, he is qualified to diagnose conditions and prescribe certain medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nurse practitioner - an advanced practice registered nurse authorized to diagnose and treat illness
  • Degree Higher: physician - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • Degree Lower: registered nurse - a nurse who has completed the necessary education and licensure
