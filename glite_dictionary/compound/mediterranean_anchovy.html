Mediterranean anchovy

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈæntʃəvi / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪnɪən ˈan.tʃə.vi / (BrE)
a small fish from the Mediterranean region, highly valued for its strong flavor and commonly used preserved or in sauces and relishes
small, flavorful Mediterranean fish used in cooking

Examples

The chef added finely chopped Mediterranean anchovy to the salad for extra depth of flavor.
Imported Mediterranean anchovies are often used as a gourmet ingredient in Italian and Spanish dishes.
The distinctive taste of Mediterranean anchovy elevates simple sauces and dressings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anchovy - a small, strongly flavored fish typically used in cooking
