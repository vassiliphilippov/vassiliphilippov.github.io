ticket-stamping machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt ˌstæmpɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt ˌstæmpɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an automatic device that validates a ticket by stamping it with the location and time information
machine that stamps and validates tickets

Examples

Before boarding the train, she inserted her ticket into the ticket-stamping machine to validate it.
Many stations have replaced the old ticket-stamping machines with digital scanners.
If you forget to use the ticket-stamping machine, you might receive a fine during inspection.
