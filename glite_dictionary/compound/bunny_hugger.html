bunny hugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌni ˌhʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈbʌni ˌhʌɡə / (BrE)
a person who advocates for animal rights or is very fond of animals, especially rabbits
animal rights advocate or animal lover

Examples

Many people called her a bunny hugger after she started campaigning for animal welfare laws.
He was criticized as a bunny hugger for opposing the hunting proposal.
The bunny hugger group staged a peaceful protest outside the fur store.

Synset Examples

As a bunny hugger, she volunteers at the animal shelter.
The bunny hugger organized a fundraiser for rabbit rescues.
He's known as a bunny hugger for his love of animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animal lover - a person who loves animals; someone fond of animals
