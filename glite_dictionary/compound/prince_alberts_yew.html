Prince Albert's yew

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɪns ælˈbɜrtz juː / (AmE), / ˌprɪns ælˈbɜːts juː / (BrE)
A small ornamental yew tree with attractive foliage and partially weeping branches, native to the mountains of southern Chile.
ornamental yew tree from Chile

Examples

Gardeners value the Prince Albert's yew for its elegant, cascading branches.
The mild climate in parts of California allows the Prince Albert's yew to thrive in gardens.
We planted a Prince Albert's yew near the pond to add year-round greenery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yew - a type of evergreen tree or bush
