November 11

1. NOUN

/ noʊˈvɛmbɚ ɪˈlɛvənθ / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛmbə ɪˈlɛvənθ / (BrE)
In the United States, a legal holiday known as Veterans' Day since 1954, formerly called Armistice Day, observed in honor of military veterans.
U.S. holiday; Veterans' Day, November 11
american

Examples

Every year on November 11, ceremonies are held to honor veterans across the United States.
The school will be closed on November 11 in observance of Veterans' Day.
Many families visit memorials together on November 11 to pay their respects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Veterans' Day - an American holiday honoring military veterans, observed on November 11
  • Synonym: Armistice Day - the former name of Veterans' Day, marking the anniversary of the end of World War I
