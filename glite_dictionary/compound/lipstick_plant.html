lipstick plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪpˌstɪk plænt / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.stɪk plɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical plant with fleshy leaves and bright red, tube-shaped flowers, often grown as a houseplant
tropical plant with red flowers

Examples

The lipstick plant on her windowsill is covered in vibrant red flowers.
Gardeners often choose a lipstick plant for its ease of care and striking appearance.
He bought a blooming lipstick plant to brighten up the office.
