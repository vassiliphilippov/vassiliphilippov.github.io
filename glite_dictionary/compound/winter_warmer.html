winter warmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈwɔr.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈwɔː.mə / (BrE)
a hot drink, dish, or other thing that brings comfort and warmth during cold winter weather
a comforting hot drink or food for winter

Examples

A steaming mug of hot chocolate is my favorite winter warmer.
She made a hearty stew as a winter warmer for the family.
Many pubs offer a special winter warmer menu featuring soups and hot drinks.

Synset Examples

A good book is the best winter warmer, don’t you think?
This soup is a perfect winter warmer on cold days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfort food - food that provides consolation or a feeling of well-being
