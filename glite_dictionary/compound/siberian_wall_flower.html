Siberian wall flower

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈbɪriən wɔl ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / saɪˈbɪəriən wɔːl ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A showy biennial or short-lived perennial plant (Erysimum hieraciifolium) cultivated for its terminal clusters of orange-yellow flowers, sometimes placed in the genus Cheiranthus.
ornamental plant with orange-yellow flowers

Examples

The Siberian wall flower adds a vibrant splash of color to spring gardens.
She planted Siberian wall flowers along the pathway to attract bees and butterflies.
Many gardeners appreciate the fragrance of the Siberian wall flower in bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallflower - any plant of the genus Erysimum, often with yellow or orange flowers, commonly grown on walls or rocky places
