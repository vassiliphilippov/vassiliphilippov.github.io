local spot

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl spɑt / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl spɒt / (BrE)
a place within a particular area that is known for being popular, interesting, or favored by local people
a popular or notable place in an area

Examples

Tourists often ask locals to recommend a good local spot for authentic food.
We discovered a charming local spot that serves the best coffee in town.
She prefers to unwind at a quiet local spot after work.

Synset Examples

Ah Tiong always knows the best local spots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangout - a place where people regularly spend time
  • Synonym: hotspot - a place of significant activity or popularity
