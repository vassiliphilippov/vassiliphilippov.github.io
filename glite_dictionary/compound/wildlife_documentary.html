wildlife documentary

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪldˌlaɪf ˌdɑkjəˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlaɪf ˌdɒkjʊˈmɛntri / (BrE)
A film or television program that features animals in their natural habitats and explores aspects of nature and the environment.
film or TV about animals and nature

Examples

The BBC released a new wildlife documentary showcasing the life of polar bears in the Arctic.
She was inspired to become a biologist after watching a wildlife documentary on African elephants.
Last night, I stayed up late to finish a fascinating wildlife documentary about endangered species.

Synset Examples

I read about the asp in a wildlife documentary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nature documentary - a documentary focusing on natural environments and wildlife
