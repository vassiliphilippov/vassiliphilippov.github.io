big sister

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈsɪstər / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
an older female sibling
older female sibling

Examples

My big sister helped me with my homework when I was struggling.
Whenever I had a problem growing up, I could always count on my big sister.
Her big sister taught her how to ride a bike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elder sister - an older female sibling; especially formal or British usage
  • Antonym: younger sister - a female sibling who is younger than oneself
