iced coffee

1. NOUN

/ aɪst ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / aɪst ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
A cold beverage made from coffee served over ice, often sweetened and with added cream or milk.
cold coffee drink served over ice

Examples

On a hot afternoon, nothing is more refreshing than a glass of iced coffee by the pool.
She prefers her iced coffee sweetened with vanilla syrup and topped with whipped cream.
They ordered iced coffee instead of hot drinks at the café during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold brew - coffee brewed with cold water, not just served over ice
  • Degree Lower: coffee - a drink made from roasted and ground coffee beans

2. NOUN

/ aɪst ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / aɪst ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee that is served cold, often poured over ice and sometimes sweetened or flavored.
cold coffee served with ice

Examples

She ordered an iced coffee instead of her usual hot drink.
On a warm afternoon, nothing is more refreshing than a glass of iced coffee.
Many people enjoy adding milk or flavors to their iced coffee.

Synset Examples

He loves adding syrup to his iced coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold brew - coffee brewed with cold water and served chilled
