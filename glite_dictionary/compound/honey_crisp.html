honey crisp

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni krɪsp / (AmE), / ˈhʌni krɪsp / (BrE)
a type of crisp candy made with honey, often characterized by a light, brittle texture
crisp candy made with honey

Examples

The bakery was famous for its homemade honey crisp treats.
She offered him a honey crisp with his tea after dinner.
The recipe calls for real honey to get the perfect texture in a honey crisp.
