poop machine

1. NOUN

/ pup məˈʃin / (AmE), / puːp məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a humorous and informal term for an animal or baby that frequently defecates or produces large amounts of feces
someone or something that defecates frequently (humorous, informal)
informal

Examples

After becoming a parent, I realized my newborn was a real poop machine.
The vet joked that our new puppy was a poop machine during house training.
My sister warned me that toddlers can be total poop machines when they’re sick.

Synset Examples

The puppy acted like a poop machine after its meal.
She called her baby a poop machine after changing three diapers.
My son is a poop machine; he's always in the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defecator - a person or animal that defecates
