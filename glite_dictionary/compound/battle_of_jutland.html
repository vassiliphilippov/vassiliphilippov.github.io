Battle of Jutland

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv ˈdʒʌtlənd / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv ˈdʒʌtlənd / (BrE)
a major but indecisive naval battle of World War I, fought between British and German fleets off the northwestern coast of Denmark in 1916
decisive WWI naval battle (1916)

Examples

The Battle of Jutland remains one of the largest naval battles in history.
Historians still debate the strategic outcome of the Battle of Jutland.
A memorial commemorates those who lost their lives at the Battle of Jutland.
