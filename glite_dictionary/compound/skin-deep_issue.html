skin-deep issue

1. NOUN

/ skɪn dip ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / skɪn diːp ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
a problem that is superficial and does not address underlying causes or seriousness
a superficial problem; not serious

Examples

The report only touched on skin-deep issues without analyzing the root causes.
They realized their arguments were just skin-deep issues and resolved to discuss their real concerns.
Focusing on skin-deep issues won't lead to lasting improvements in the organization.

Synset Examples

They realized their arguments were just skin-deep issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superficial - existing or occurring at or on the surface, not deep or thorough
