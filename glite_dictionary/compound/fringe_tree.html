fringe tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɪndʒ ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈfrɪndʒ ˌtriː / (BrE)
A small ornamental tree or shrub of the genus Chionanthus, known for its clusters of fragrant, white, fringe-like flowers.
ornamental tree with fringe-like flowers

Examples

The fringe tree in our garden is covered with delicate, white blossoms each spring.
Gardeners often plant the fringe tree for its showy flowers and pleasant fragrance.
We admired the feathery blooms on the old fringe tree by the fence.
