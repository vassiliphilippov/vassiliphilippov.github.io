Sunday best

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.deɪ ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ ˈbɛst / (BrE)
one's best clothes, traditionally worn to church on Sunday or for special occasions
one's best clothes for Sunday or special occasions

Examples

She wore her Sunday best to the wedding.
The children were all dressed in their Sunday best for the family photo.
He saves his Sunday best for important events.

2. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.deɪ bɛst / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ bɛst / (BrE)
one's finest clothes, typically worn on special occasions or for church services
one's best clothes for special occasions

Examples

He always saves his Sunday best for weddings and other important ceremonies.
The children were dressed in their Sunday best for the school photo.
She took extra care choosing her Sunday best before attending the holiday banquet.

Synset Examples

He decided to dress in his Sunday best for the occasion.
They pulled out their Sunday best for the family gathering.
She wore her Sunday best to church this week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finery - expensive or elaborate clothes and accessories
  • Antonym: everyday clothes - clothing worn on ordinary days, not for special occasions

Etymology

Refers to the tradition of wearing one's best clothes to church on Sundays.
