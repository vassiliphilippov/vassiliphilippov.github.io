bar soap

1. NOUN

/ bɑr soʊp / (AmE), / bɑː səʊp / (BrE)
Soap that is shaped and sold as a solid bar, typically used for washing the body or hands.
solid soap in bar form

Examples

She always prefers to use bar soap instead of liquid soap in the shower.
The hotel bathroom was stocked with tiny bar soap for guests to use.
Many dermatologists recommend gentle bar soap for people with sensitive skin.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: liquid soap - Soap in liquid form, typically used for washing hands or body
