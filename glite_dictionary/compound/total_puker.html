total puker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈpjuː.kər / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈpjuː.kə / (BrE)
a person who vomits frequently, especially in situations involving motion or discomfort
person prone to frequent vomiting
informal

Examples

My little brother is a total puker whenever we go on a boat trip.
Unfortunately, I became known as the total puker during our school bus rides.
She avoided roller coasters because she was a total puker even on the gentlest rides.

Synset Examples

That ride made him a total puker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puker - a person who vomits
  • Degree Lower: nauseous - feeling sick and likely to vomit
