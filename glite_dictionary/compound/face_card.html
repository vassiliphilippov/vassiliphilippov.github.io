face card

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs kɑːd / (BrE)
one of the playing cards in each suit that bear a picture of a king, queen, or jack
playing card with a king, queen, or jack

Examples

He drew a face card and doubled his bet.
There are twelve face cards in a standard deck of playing cards.
Pulling a face card can be advantageous in blackjack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court card - playing card with a king, queen, or jack; British term
