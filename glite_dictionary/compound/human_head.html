human head

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən hɛd / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən hɛd / (BrE)
the upper part of the human body that contains the brain, eyes, ears, nose, and mouth
upper part of the human body

Examples

A strong blow to the human head can cause serious injury.
The human head contains important organs such as the brain and eyes.
Ancient sculptures often depict the human head in great detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head - the upper or front part of the body in animals, typically containing sensory organs
