banana family

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of treelike tropical Asian herbs, including the banana and related plants, scientifically known as Musaceae
family of tropical herbs including bananas

Examples

The banana family includes not only edible bananas but also ornamental plants with large leaves.
Many species in the banana family are cultivated for their fruits throughout the tropics.
Scientists study the banana family to understand disease resistance in bananas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Musaceae - the botanical family comprising bananas and their relatives
