hearty stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrti stu / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti stjuː / (BrE)
A thick, filling, and nutritious soup or dish, often containing meat, vegetables, and sometimes grains or beans.
filling, nutritious soup or dish

Examples

Nothing warms me up on a cold day like a bowl of hearty stew.
The restaurant's hearty stew is their most popular winter dish.
After hiking all afternoon, they enjoyed a hearty stew by the campfire.

Synset Examples

She made a hearty stew for dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
