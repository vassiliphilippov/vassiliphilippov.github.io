chicken casserole

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkən ˌkæsəˈroʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˌkæsəˈrəʊl / (BrE)
A baked dish made with pieces of chicken and other ingredients, typically cooked together in a deep dish in the oven.
baked chicken dish with other ingredients

Examples

Her favorite comfort food is chicken casserole with mushrooms and rice.
We brought a homemade chicken casserole to the family potluck.
The restaurant serves a delicious chicken casserole every Wednesday.
