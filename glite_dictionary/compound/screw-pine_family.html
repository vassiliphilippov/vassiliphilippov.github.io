screw-pine family

1. NOUN

/ skruː paɪn ˈfæməli / (AmE), / skruː paɪn ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of woody plants in the order Pandanales, including the genus Pandanus and related species
family of woody plants including pandanus

Examples

Many species in the screw-pine family are known for their long, fibrous leaves.
The screw-pine family is found mainly in tropical and subtropical regions.
Pandanus is one of the best-known genera in the screw-pine family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pandanus - a genus of tropical plants in the screw-pine family
