to mix colors

1. VERB

/ mɪks ˈkʌlɚz / (AmE), / mɪks ˈkʌləz / (BrE)
to combine different paints or pigments in order to produce desired shades or hues
combine paints to make new shades

Examples

Art teachers often show students how to mix colors to achieve a wide variety of tones.
She learned to mix colors to create realistic landscapes in her paintings.
If you mix colors like blue and yellow, you'll get green.

Synset Examples

As a painter, she spends hours mixing colors to get it right.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blend - to mix different things together to become one
