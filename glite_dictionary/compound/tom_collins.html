Tom Collins

1. NOUN

/ tɑm ˈkɑlɪnz / (AmE), / tɒm ˈkɒlɪnz / (BrE)
a tall cocktail made with gin, lemon juice, sugar, and carbonated water, served over ice
gin-based cocktail with lemon and soda

Examples

She ordered a Tom Collins at the bar and sipped it by the window.
A classic Tom Collins is refreshing on a hot summer day.
He mixed up a batch of Tom Collinses for their cocktail party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gin fizz - cocktail similar to a Tom Collins, made with gin, lemon juice, sugar, and soda water

Etymology

Said to be named after a fictitious person from an American hoax in the 1870s.
