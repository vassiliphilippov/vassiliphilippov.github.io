mental testing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl̩ ˈtɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl ˈtɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of administering standardized tests to measure cognitive abilities, memory, intelligence, aptitude, or personality
administering psychological tests

Examples

The school conducted mental testing to assess students' learning needs.
Recent advances have improved the reliability of mental testing in children.
Applicants had to undergo mental testing as part of the job selection process.

Synset Examples

the test was standardized on a large sample of students

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychometric test - test designed to measure psychological attributes
