short story

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
A story in prose that is shorter than a novel and typically focuses on a single theme or incident.
prose narrative shorter than a novel

Examples

She published her first short story in a national magazine at the age of eighteen.
The collection features several short stories exploring different aspects of human nature.
His latest short story deals with themes of loss and forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: novel - a long work of fiction in prose
  • Degree Higher: novella - a prose narrative longer than a short story but shorter than a novel
