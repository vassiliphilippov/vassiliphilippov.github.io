meat crayon

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈkreɪ.ən / (AmE), / miːt ˈkreɪ.ən / (BrE)
a humorous term for a sausage or a sandwich containing processed meat
humorous term for sausage or processed meat sandwich
informal

Examples

The kids laughed when their dad called the hot dogs meat crayons.
She packed a meat crayon sandwich in her lunch for work.
The deli advertised their sausage rolls as gourmet meat crayons.

Synset Examples

The chef called the sausage a fancy meat crayon.
She joked about her lunch being a meat crayon sandwich.
