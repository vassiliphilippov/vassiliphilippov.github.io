beacon of hope

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːkən əv hoʊp / (AmE), / ˈbiːkən əv həʊp / (BrE)
A person, thing, or idea that serves as a symbol or source of optimism or inspiration, especially in challenging times.
symbol or source of optimism or inspiration

Examples

The new hospital became a beacon of hope for people in the area.
Her achievements are seen as a beacon of hope for aspiring scientists.
After the disaster, the volunteers acted as a beacon of hope for the struggling community.

Synset Examples

The new park is a beacon of hope for the community.
The charity serves as a beacon of hope for the needy.
Her recovery became a beacon of hope for others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiration - the process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something
  • Antonym: hopelessness - the state of having no hope
