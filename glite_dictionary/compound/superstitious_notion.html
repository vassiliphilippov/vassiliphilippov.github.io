superstitious notion

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈstɪʃ.əs ˈnoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈstɪʃ.əs ˈnəʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
an irrational belief based on superstition, often arising from ignorance or fear
irrational belief based on superstition

Examples

She dismissed the story as just another superstitious notion without any basis in fact.
Some sailors refused to go to sea on Fridays because of an old superstitious notion.
It's a superstitious notion that breaking a mirror will bring seven years of bad luck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superstition - irrational belief in supernatural influences
