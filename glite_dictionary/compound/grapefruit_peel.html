grapefruit peel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt pil / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpˌfruːt piːl / (BrE)
strips of grapefruit skin, often cooked in sugar syrup and coated with sugar for use as a sweet or garnish
candied strips of grapefruit skin

Examples

She decorated the cake with finely sliced grapefruit peel for a burst of color.
Candied grapefruit peel adds a tangy sweetness to holiday desserts.
The chef recommended using grapefruit peel as a unique garnish for cocktails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candied peel - fruit peel cooked with sugar, used as a sweet or in baking
