safety concern

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti kənˈsɝːn / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a matter or issue that may pose a risk to health, safety, or well-being
issue posing a risk to safety or well-being

Examples

The playground was closed due to safety concerns raised by parents.
During the inspection, several safety concerns were identified and documented.
He reported a safety concern to his supervisor after noticing exposed wires.

Synset Examples

They decided to stop the job due to safety concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risk - possibility of harm or danger
  • Synonym: hazard - potential source of harm or danger
