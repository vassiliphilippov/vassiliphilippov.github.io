basket hilt

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskət hɪlt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt hɪlt / (BrE)
a type of sword hilt featuring a basket-shaped guard that protects the hand
sword hilt with basket-shaped hand guard

Examples

The Scottish broadsword is famous for its distinctive basket hilt.
Collectors prize antique swords with ornate basket hilts for their craftsmanship.
The basket hilt offers excellent protection for the wielder’s hand during combat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilt - the handle of a sword or dagger
