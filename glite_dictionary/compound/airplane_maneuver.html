airplane maneuver

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌpleɪn məˈnuːvɚ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)
a movement or series of movements carried out by an aircraft to change its position or direction
movement performed by an aircraft

Examples

The pilot demonstrated a complex airplane maneuver during the air show.
Every airplane maneuver must be executed with precision and care.
Learning advanced airplane maneuvers is essential for all fighter pilots.
