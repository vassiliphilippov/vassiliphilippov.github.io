zingy taste

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪŋi teɪst / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋi teɪst / (BrE)
a sharp, lively, and pleasantly tangy flavor, often with a hint of citrus or spice
sharp, lively flavor

Examples

Lime juice gives the salsa a zingy taste that brightens up the dish.
The salad dressing had a zingy taste because of the addition of mustard and vinegar.
Kids often enjoy the zingy taste of sour candies.

Synset Examples

He loves the zingy taste of fresh ginger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangy - having a strong, piquant flavor or smell
