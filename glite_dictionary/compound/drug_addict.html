drug addict

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈædɪkt / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈadɪkt / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to illegal or controlled drugs, especially narcotics or other substances recognized as having potential for abuse
person addicted to drugs

Examples

She struggled for years as a drug addict before seeking help.
The shelter provides services for homeless drug addicts.
His experience as a drug addict inspired him to help others recover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to a particular substance or activity
