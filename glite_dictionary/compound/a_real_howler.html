a real howler

1. NOUN

/ ə ɹiːəl ˈhaʊlər / (AmE), / ə ˈrɪəl ˈhaʊlə / (BrE)
something extremely funny or ridiculous, often used to describe a joke, mistake, or situation that causes people to laugh a lot
very funny or ridiculous thing

Examples

Last night's comedy show featured a real howler that had the entire audience in stitches.
He made a real howler during the meeting, and everyone burst out laughing.
The typo in the headline was a real howler and quickly went viral online.

Synset Examples

That movie was a real howler; I laughed so much.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: howler - an amusing or ridiculous mistake
