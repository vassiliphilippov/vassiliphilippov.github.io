deep red

1. NOUN

/ diːp rɛd / (AmE), / diːp rɛd / (BrE)
a strong and vivid shade of red, darker than ordinary red
a vivid, dark shade of red

Examples

The artist chose a deep red for the background of the painting.
She wore a dress in deep red that stood out in the crowd.
The walls were painted a warm deep red to create a cozy atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: scarlet - a brilliant red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • Synonym: crimson - a rich, deep red color inclining to purple
  • Degree Higher: burgundy - a dark red or purple-red color
