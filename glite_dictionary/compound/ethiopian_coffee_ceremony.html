Ethiopian coffee ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːθiˈoʊpiən ˈkɔfi ˌsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈəʊpiən ˈkɒfi ˌsɛrəˈməʊni / (BrE)
a traditional Ethiopian ritual involving the preparation, serving, and drinking of coffee, often performed as a social or ceremonial event
traditional Ethiopian ritual of coffee preparation

Examples

We were honored to participate in an authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony during our visit to Addis Ababa.
The Ethiopian coffee ceremony is an important social event that brings families and friends together.
At the cultural festival, a woman in traditional dress prepared coffee for guests as part of the Ethiopian coffee ceremony.

Synset Examples

The Ethiopian coffee ceremony was a highlight of the event.
