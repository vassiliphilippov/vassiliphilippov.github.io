keyboard shelf

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːbɔrd ʃɛlf / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd ʃɛlf / (BrE)
a sliding or pull-out tray attached to a desk or workstation, designed to hold a computer keyboard
sliding tray for computer keyboard

Examples

My new desk has a keyboard shelf that makes typing much more comfortable.
He accidentally broke the keyboard shelf while adjusting the height of his workstation.
The keyboard shelf slides smoothly in and out beneath the main desktop.

Synset Examples

The most common form of the computer desk is a variant of the ergonomic desk, which has an adjustable keyboard tray and sufficient desktop space for handwriting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard tray - a tray attached to a desk for holding a keyboard
