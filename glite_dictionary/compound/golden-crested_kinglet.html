golden-crested kinglet

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡoʊldən ˈkrɛstɪd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldən ˈkrɛstɪd ˈkɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a small European songbird (Regulus regulus) with a black-bordered yellow crown patch; also called the goldcrest.
small European bird with a yellow crown patch

Examples

The golden-crested kinglet darted among the branches, its yellow crown patch flashing in the sunlight.
Birdwatchers often hope to spot a golden-crested kinglet during their walks in European woodlands.
Despite its tiny size, the golden-crested kinglet is known for its lively song.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldcrest - another name for the same bird species
  • Degree Higher: kinglet - a small songbird in the family Regulidae
