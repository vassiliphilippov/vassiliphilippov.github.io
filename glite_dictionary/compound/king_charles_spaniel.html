King Charles spaniel

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ tʃɑrlz spæn.i.əl / (AmE), / kɪŋ tʃɑːlz spæn.jəl / (BrE)
A small English toy spaniel breed with a black-and-tan coat, named after King Charles II who popularized it.
a small English toy spaniel breed

Examples

The King Charles spaniel was a favorite companion of the English aristocracy.
She adopted a King Charles spaniel because of its affectionate nature and elegant appearance.
At the dog show, his King Charles spaniel won a prize for best in breed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cavalier King Charles spaniel - a related but distinct breed of toy spaniel

Etymology

Named after King Charles II of England, who was fond of the breed.
