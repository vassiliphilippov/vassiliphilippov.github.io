aqueous solution

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.kwi.əs səˈluː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.kwɪ.əs səˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
A solution in which water is the solvent.
solution with water as solvent

Examples

The salt was dissolved in an aqueous solution before the experiment began.
Sugar forms an aqueous solution when stirred into water.
They tested the acidity of the aqueous solution using litmus paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solution - a homogeneous mixture of two or more substances

Etymology

From Latin 'aqua' (water), with 'aqueous' meaning 'of or like water'.
