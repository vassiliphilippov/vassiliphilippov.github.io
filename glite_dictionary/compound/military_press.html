military press

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri prɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri prɛs / (BrE)
An exercise in weightlifting where a barbell is lifted first to shoulder height and then pressed overhead in a controlled movement.
barbell exercise lifted overhead

Examples

She added the military press to her workout routine to strengthen her shoulders.
He struggled to complete five reps of the military press with proper form.
The military press is recommended for building upper body strength.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoulder press - a similar exercise pressing weights above the head
