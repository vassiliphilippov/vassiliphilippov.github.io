head teacher

1. NOUN

/ hɛd ˈtitʃɚ / (AmE), / hɛd ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
the teacher who is the leader and has overall responsibility for a school
the principal of a school (chief educator)
british

Examples

The head teacher addressed the assembly with an inspiring speech about school values.
Parents are encouraged to meet with the head teacher if they have concerns about their child.
After the incident, the head teacher implemented new guidelines to ensure student safety.

Synset Examples

she sent unruly pupils to see the principal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: principal - the administrator in charge of a school, especially in American English
