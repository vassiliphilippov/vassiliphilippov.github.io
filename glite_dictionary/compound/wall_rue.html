wall rue

1. NOUN

/ wɔl ru / (AmE), / wɔːl ruː / (BrE)
A small, delicate fern (Asplenium ruta-muraria) that grows on walls and rocky slopes in Eurasia and North America.
species of small fern on walls or cliffs

Examples

The ancient stone bridge was covered in wall rue, adding a touch of green to the gray stones.
Botanists discovered several clusters of wall rue thriving on the abandoned castle walls.
If you look closely at rocky outcrops in the area, you might spot some wall rue growing there.

Etymology

From the resemblance of its fronds to those of rue and its fondness for growing on walls.
