oyster stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər stu / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə stjuː / (BrE)
A soup or stew made with oysters, typically cooked in milk or cream and often seasoned with butter and spices.
stew made with oysters and cream

Examples

We always serve oyster stew as a special treat on Christmas Eve.
The chef's signature oyster stew is creamy and packed with flavor.
She learned to make a delicious oyster stew from her grandmother's recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chowder - a thick soup or stew, often containing seafood
