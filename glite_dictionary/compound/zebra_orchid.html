zebra orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈzibrə ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈziːbrə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
an orchid of southwestern Australia with reddish linear leaves and panicles of pale yellow flowers marked with purple, featuring a deep red or purple lip
orchid species with purple-marked yellow flowers

Examples

The zebra orchid is native to the woodlands of southwestern Australia.
Botanists admire the delicate patterns on the zebra orchid’s pale yellow petals.
Gardeners often cultivate zebra orchids for their unique and colorful flowers.
