smooth darling pea

1. NOUN

/ smuð ˈdɑrlɪŋ pi / (AmE), / smuːð ˈdɑːlɪŋ piː / (BrE)
an erect or trailing perennial plant of eastern Australia, known for its axillary racemes of blue, purple, or red flowers
Australian perennial plant with colorful racemes

Examples

The smooth darling pea adds vibrant color to many gardens in eastern Australia.
Botanists documented a new population of smooth darling pea in the region last year.
Some gardeners favor the smooth darling pea for its showy blue and purple flowers.
