film maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlm ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlm ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes motion pictures, especially as a producer or director
person who makes films

Examples

The acclaimed film maker won numerous awards for his innovative documentaries.
She's an aspiring film maker who hopes to direct her first feature next year.
Many independent film makers struggle to secure funding for their projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: director - a person who supervises the actors and directs the action in the production of a show
  • Synonym: producer - a person responsible for the financial and managerial aspects of making a film
