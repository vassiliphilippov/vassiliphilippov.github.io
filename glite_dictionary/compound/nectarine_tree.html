nectarine tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛktəriːn ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈnɛktəriːn ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree that produces nectarines, having fruit with smooth skin and usually yellow flesh, regarded as a variety or mutation of the peach tree.
tree producing smooth-skinned nectarines

Examples

The nectarine tree in our backyard produces juicy, sweet fruit every summer.
Unlike its fuzzy relative, the nectarine tree bears fruit with smooth skin.
She pruned the nectarine tree in early spring to ensure a healthy harvest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peach tree - a tree that bears peaches
