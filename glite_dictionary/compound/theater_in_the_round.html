theater in the round

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiətər ɪn ðə raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə ɪn ðə raʊnd / (BrE)
a type of theater in which the audience surrounds the stage on at least three sides
theater with audience around the stage

Examples

The new play was performed in a theater in the round, allowing the actors to engage with the audience from all sides.
She prefers acting in a theater in the round because it creates a more intimate setting.
Several community centers have converted their spaces into a theater in the round for experimental productions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arena stage - a stage placed in the center of a room, with audience on all sides
