long underwear

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ˈʌndərˌwɛr / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈʌndəˌwɛə / (BrE)
A warm undergarment consisting of a shirt and drawers, usually worn as a single piece, that covers the arms and legs.
warm one-piece undergarment

Examples

During the harsh winters, he always wore long underwear beneath his clothes.
She packed several pairs of long underwear for their camping trip in the mountains.
Children often complain about wearing long underwear, but it keeps them warm in the snow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal underwear - undergarment designed to retain body heat, especially in cold weather
