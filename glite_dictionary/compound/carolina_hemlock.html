Carolina hemlock

1. NOUN

/ kæ.rəˈlaɪ.nə ˈhem.lɑk / (AmE), / kær.əˈlaɪ.nə ˈhem.lɒk / (BrE)
a medium-sized evergreen tree of the southeastern United States, with spreading branches and widely diverging cone scales
a southeastern U.S. evergreen tree

Examples

The Carolina hemlock is native to the southern Appalachian Mountains.
Botanists are concerned about the declining number of Carolina hemlocks due to pests.
We saw a grove of Carolina hemlock trees during our hike in North Carolina.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemlock - a type of evergreen tree in the genus Tsuga
