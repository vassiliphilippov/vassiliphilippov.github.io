summer holiday

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˌhɑːlɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˌhɒlɪdeɪ / (BrE)
a holiday or vacation taken during the summer, often the period when schools are closed for the summer season
vacation period during summer
british

Examples

We're planning a trip to Italy for our summer holiday.
Many families spend their summer holiday by the seaside.
Schoolchildren look forward to the long summer holiday every year.

Synset Examples

The kids are excited for their summer holiday next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summer vacation - holiday or break from work or school during the summer months (especially American English)
