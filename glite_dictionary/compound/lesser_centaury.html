lesser centaury

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər sɛnˈtɔri / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə sɛnˈtɔːri / (BrE)
a small European annual plant (Centaurium pulchellum) of the gentian family, with clusters of pink flowers in dense cymes
a small European annual plant with pink flowers

Examples

The bright pink flowers of the lesser centaury are easy to spot in open grasslands.
She carefully identified the lesser centaury by its cluster of tiny blooms.
In summer, lesser centaury often carpets meadows across parts of Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a plant of the genus Centaurium, to which lesser centaury belongs

Etymology

From 'lesser' (smaller) and Middle English 'centaure', from Latin 'centaurea', a kind of plant.
