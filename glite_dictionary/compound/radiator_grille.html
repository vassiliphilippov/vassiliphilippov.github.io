radiator grille

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdiˌeɪtɚ ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪeɪtə ɡrɪl / (BrE)
A framework of slats or mesh at the front of a vehicle that allows air to reach the engine's radiator for cooling.
front grating allowing airflow to a car's radiator

Examples

The radiator grille gives the car its distinctive front appearance.
Debris caught in the radiator grille can block airflow and cause the engine to overheat.
He polished the chrome radiator grille until it gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grille - a framework of metal bars protecting or allowing passage of air or light
