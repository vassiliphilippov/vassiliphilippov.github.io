Eagle Scout

1. NOUN

/ ˈiɡəl skaʊt / (AmE), / ˈiːɡl skaʊt / (BrE)
a Boy Scout who has achieved the highest rank in the Boy Scouts by earning a required number of merit badges and fulfilling additional leadership and service requirements
highest rank in Boy Scouts

Examples

After years of dedication, he finally became an Eagle Scout at age seventeen.
Colleges often recognize the leadership skills of an Eagle Scout as a sign of commitment and achievement.
She listed her status as an Eagle Scout on her job application.
