genus Gloxinia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡlɒkˈsɪn.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ɡlɒkˈsɪn.i.ə / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical American herbaceous plants with leafy stems and axillary flowers, belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.
genus of tropical American plants

Examples

Botanists have recently reclassified several species in the genus Gloxinia.
The stunning flowers of the genus Gloxinia attract many plant enthusiasts.
She specializes in the cultivation of rare plants, particularly those from the genus Gloxinia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gloxinia - a plant of the genus Gloxinia, especially cultivated for its attractive flowers

Etymology

From Latin 'gloxinia', named after Benjamin Peter Gloxin, an 18th-century German physician and botanist.
