Sea of Marmara

1. PROPN

/ ˈsi əv ˈmɑrmərə / (AmE), / ˈsiː əv ˈmɑːmərə / (BrE)
an inland sea in northwestern Turkey, connected to the Black Sea by the Bosporus and to the Aegean Sea by the Dardanelles
inland sea in northwestern Turkey

Examples

The Sea of Marmara separates the Asian and European parts of Turkey.
Ships pass through the Sea of Marmara when traveling from the Black Sea to the Aegean.
Many coastal cities line the shores of the Sea of Marmara.

Etymology

From Turkish 'Marmara', derived from Marmara Island; ultimately from Greek 'marmaron' (marble), due to marble sources on the island.
