Andrew's clintonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.druːz klɪnˈtoʊ.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈæn.druːz klɪnˈtəʊ.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
a plant of western North America, with nearly leafless stalks topped by clusters of red or reddish lavender flowers, found from California to Oregon
plant native to California and Oregon

Examples

The rare Andrew's clintonia blooms beautifully along forest edges in northern California.
Hikers spotted a patch of Andrew's clintonia near the Oregon border.
Botanists are working to protect the habitat of Andrew's clintonia from development.
