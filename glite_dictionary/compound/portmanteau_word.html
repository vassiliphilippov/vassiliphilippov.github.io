portmanteau word

1. NOUN

/ pɔrtˈmæntoʊ wɝːd / (AmE), / pɔːtˈmæntəʊ wɜːd / (BrE)
A word formed by blending parts of two or more other words and combining their meanings.
word formed by blending two words

Examples

The word brunch is a classic portmanteau word combining 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.
Many modern portmanteau words like 'smog' and 'motel' have become common in English.
She explained that 'infomercial' is a portmanteau word blending 'information' and 'commercial'.

Synset Examples

‘smog’ is a blend of ‘smoke’ and ‘fog’
‘motel’ is a portmanteau word made by combining ‘motor’ and ‘hotel’
‘brunch’ is a well-known portmanteau

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blend - word formed by blending parts of two words
  • Synonym: compound word - word formed from two or more whole words

Etymology

From French 'portemanteau' (coat rack, suitcase); first used by Lewis Carroll for blended words.
