laboratory bench

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæbrəˌtɔri bɛntʃ / (AmE), / ləˈbɒrətəri bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A sturdy table or surface in a laboratory used for scientific experiments, research, or practical work.
work surface in a laboratory

Examples

The scientist placed her samples on the laboratory bench before beginning the experiment.
Please ensure that all chemicals are stored safely when you leave the laboratory bench.
Each student was assigned to a laboratory bench for the duration of the biology lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a strong table for manual work
