mother's daughter

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðərz ˈdɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəz ˈdɔːtə / (BrE)
a daughter who is favored by and similar to her mother, often reflecting her mother's traits or preferences
favored, similar daughter

Examples

People say Emily is truly her mother's daughter, with the same quick wit and determination.
As a mother's daughter, she developed a love for gardening from an early age.
Everyone noticed that Lisa, a mother's daughter, always supported her mom's opinions at family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: father's son - a son who is favored by and similar to his father
