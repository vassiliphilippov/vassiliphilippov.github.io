cranberry juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrænˌbɛri dʒus / (AmE), / ˈkræn.bəri dʒuːs / (BrE)
The juice extracted from cranberries, usually diluted and sweetened for drinking.
juice made from cranberries

Examples

She drank a glass of cranberry juice with her breakfast every morning.
Some people believe that cranberry juice can help prevent urinary tract infections.
We served cranberry juice at the party as a non-alcoholic option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranberry - a small, red, tart berry
