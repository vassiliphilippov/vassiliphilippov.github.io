your all's

1. PRON

/ jʊr ɔlz / (AmE), / jɔːr ɔːlz / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) Used as the possessive form of 'you all,' indicating something belonging to a group of people being addressed.
possessive of 'you all' (informal, US)
informal american

Examples

Could you write your all's names on the sign-up sheet?
We appreciated your all's help with the event last weekend.
Is this your all's car parked outside?

Synset Examples

Can I get your all's opinion on this matter?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: y'all's - alternative possessive form of 'y'all' (you all)

2. PRON

/ jʊr ɔlz / (AmE), / jɔː ɔːlz / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) Belonging to or associated with you all (plural you); the possessive form of 'you all.'
possessive form of 'you all' (informal, US)
informal american

Examples

Thank you for your all's hard work on this project.
The teacher recognized your all's contributions to the fundraiser.
We truly appreciate your all's patience during this process.

Synset Examples

I appreciate your all's effort in making this happen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: y'all's - possessive form of 'y'all' (you all)

Etymology

Chiefly Southern and Midland American English dialect formation from 'you all' plus possessive 's.
