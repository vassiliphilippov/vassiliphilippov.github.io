New Hampshire

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈhæmpʃɚ / (AmE), / njuː ˈhæmpʃə / (BrE)
a state of the northeastern US in New England, bordered by Canada, Vermont, Maine, Massachusetts, and the Atlantic Ocean; capital: Concord.
a state in northeastern US, capital Concord

Examples

Many tourists visit New Hampshire in the autumn to see the colorful foliage.
The presidential primary in New Hampshire is closely watched during election years.
She moved to New Hampshire for its beautiful mountains and small towns.

Etymology

From the English county of Hampshire, reflecting early colonial naming.
