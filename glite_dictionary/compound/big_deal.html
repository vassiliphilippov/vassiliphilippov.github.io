big deal

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈdiːl / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈdiːl / (BrE)
a thing of great importance or consequence
something important or significant

Examples

Winning the award was a big deal for her career.
He acts like it's a big deal, but it's actually quite routine.
Securing that contract is a really big deal for our firm.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: triviality - something of little value or importance
  • Synonym: significant event - an important or notable occurrence
