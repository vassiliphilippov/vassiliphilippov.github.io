druggie friend

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌɡi frɛnd / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡi frɛnd / (BrE)
a friend who regularly uses illegal drugs
friend who uses drugs
informal

Examples

My parents were worried that I might become a druggie friend in high school.
She realized she needed to distance herself from her druggie friend after seeing him struggle with addiction.
People often judge you by the company you keep, especially if you have a druggie friend.

Synset Examples

She avoided hanging out with her old druggie friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: druggie - a person who habitually takes illegal drugs

Etymology

Formed from 'druggie' (informal for person who uses drugs) and 'friend'.
