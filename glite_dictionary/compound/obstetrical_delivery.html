obstetrical delivery

1. NOUN

/ əbˈstɛtrɪkəl dɪˈlɪvəri / (AmE), / ɒbˈstɛtrɪkəl dɪˈlɪvəri / (BrE)
the act or process of delivering a baby, especially as assisted by a medical professional
the process of delivering a baby
medical

Examples

The doctor performed the obstetrical delivery with great care and attention to both mother and child.
Complications during obstetrical delivery can sometimes require an emergency cesarean section.
She specialized in obstetrical delivery and worked in the maternity ward for over twenty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: childbirth - the process of giving birth to a baby
  • Synonym: labor - the process of childbirth, especially the period of contractions leading to delivery

Etymology

From Latin obstetricia (midwifery) and delivery (from Old French delivrer, to set free).
