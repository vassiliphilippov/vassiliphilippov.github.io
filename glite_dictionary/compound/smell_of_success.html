smell of success

1. NOUN

/ smɛl əv səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / smɛl əv səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
a feeling or experience of having achieved success, often accompanied by satisfaction or fulfillment
a feeling of achievement or triumph

Examples

After years of hard work, she finally enjoyed the smell of success.
His first novel brought him the smell of success he had always dreamed of.
Investors flocked to the company, sensing the smell of success in its recent profits.

Synset Examples

She savored the sweet smell of success after her promotion.
After years of hard work, he finally experienced the sweet smell of success.
Winning the award was the sweet smell of success for the team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taste of victory - a feeling of having won or succeeded
