vacuum bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæk.juːm bæg / (AmE), / ˈvæk.juːm bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag that collects dust and dirt sucked up by a vacuum cleaner
bag for collecting dust in a vacuum cleaner

Examples

I need to replace the vacuum bag because it's full of dust.
Most upright vacuum cleaners use a disposable vacuum bag.
She accidentally tore the vacuum bag while trying to empty it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacuum cleaner - an electrical appliance that cleans surfaces by suction
