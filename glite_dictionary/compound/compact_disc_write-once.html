compact disc write-once

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpækt dɪsk ˈraɪt ˌwʌns / (AmE), / kəmˈpækt dɪsk ˈraɪt ˌwʌns / (BrE)
a type of compact disc that can be recorded on only once and then functions as a read-only memory
CD that can be written once

Examples

After burning the files onto a compact disc write-once, he could not make any changes to it.
The album was released on compact disc write-once to prevent unauthorized editing.
Be sure to verify your data before using a compact disc write-once, since you can't erase or rewrite it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CD-R - a recordable compact disc; abbreviation for 'compact disc recordable'
