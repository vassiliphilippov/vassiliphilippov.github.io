air mile

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌmaɪl / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌmaɪl / (BrE)
a unit of distance in aviation and navigation, equal to exactly 1,852 meters (one nautical mile)
unit of distance in aviation (1,852 meters)

Examples

Pilots use the air mile to measure distances between airports.
The journey covered almost five hundred air miles.
One air mile is standardized at 1,852 meters for navigational purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nautical mile - a unit of distance used in navigation, equal to 1,852 meters
