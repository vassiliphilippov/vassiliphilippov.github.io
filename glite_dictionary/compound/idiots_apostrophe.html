idiot's apostrophe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪdiəts əˈpɑstrəfi / (AmE), / ˈɪdɪəts əˈpɒstrəfi / (BrE)
an incorrect use of an apostrophe to form a plural, regarded as a sign of ignorance or poor understanding of punctuation
misuse of apostrophe for plurals

Examples

The sign outside the café had an idiot's apostrophe in "cappuccino's".
She rolled her eyes at the idiot's apostrophe on the menu.
Forum users debated whether the mistake was an idiot's apostrophe or just a typo.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
