to light up the night sky

1. VERB

/ laɪt ʌp ðə naɪt skaɪ / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp ðə naɪt skaɪ / (BrE)
to make the night sky bright or illuminated, especially with lights or fireworks.
illuminate the night sky

Examples

The fireworks will light up the night sky during the festival.
Lanterns released into the air beautifully light up the night sky every New Year's Eve.
Lasers and searchlights light up the night sky above the stadium for special events.
