percent sign

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsɛnt saɪn / (AmE), / pəˈsɛnt saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol (%) used to show that a number is a proportion out of 100; used after a number to indicate 'per hundred'
the symbol % showing a number out of 100

Examples

The percent sign is commonly used in mathematics to indicate percentages.
Please write the answer using a percent sign instead of the word 'percent'.
On the label, the percent sign follows the number showing the daily value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - one part in every hundred

Etymology

From Latin 'per centum' (by the hundred), combined with 'sign' for symbol.
