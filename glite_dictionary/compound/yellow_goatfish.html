yellow goatfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈɡoʊtˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈɡəʊtˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a species of goatfish with a greyish body and a yellow stripe, often found schooling in tropical and subtropical waters
a goatfish species with a yellow stripe

Examples

The yellow goatfish is easily recognized by the bright stripe running along its side.
A school of yellow goatfish swam near the coral reef as divers passed by.
Fishermen often spot yellow goatfish in shallow, sandy lagoons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatfish - any marine fish of the family Mullidae, known for their chin barbels and bright colors
