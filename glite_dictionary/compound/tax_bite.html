tax bite

1. NOUN

/ tæks baɪt / (AmE), / tæks baɪt / (BrE)
the amount of tax deducted or owed, or the impact of taxes on one's income
the portion of income taken as tax

Examples

The tax bite took a larger chunk out of his bonus than he anticipated.
Many families are concerned about the tax bite they face each April.
After looking at her pay stub, she was shocked by the tax bite on her salary.

Synset Examples

The tax bite this year was larger than expected.
They felt the tax bite after their income increased.
She was surprised by the tax bite on her paycheck.
