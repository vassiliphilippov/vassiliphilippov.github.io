moving wallpaper

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvɪŋ ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
decorative wallpaper, usually digital, that gives the impression of movement or is visually animated
animated or dynamic decorative wallpaper

Examples

The new restaurant features moving wallpaper that shifts patterns throughout the day.
He installed a moving wallpaper in his living room for a more immersive experience.
Guests were fascinated by the moving wallpaper projected onto the walls during the party.

Synset Examples

The exhibit featured moving wallpaper that mesmerized visitors.
