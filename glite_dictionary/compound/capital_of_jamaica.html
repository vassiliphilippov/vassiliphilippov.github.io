capital of Jamaica

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæpɪtəl əv dʒəˈmeɪkə / (AmE), / ˈkapɪt(ə)l əv dʒəˈmeɪkə / (BrE)

the capital city of Jamaica

Examples

Kingston is known as the capital of Jamaica and is famous for its vibrant music scene.
