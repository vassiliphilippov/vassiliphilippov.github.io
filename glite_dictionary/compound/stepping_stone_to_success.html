stepping stone to success

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpɪŋ stoʊn tə səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈstɛpɪŋ stəʊn tə səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
an event or experience that helps one make progress towards achieving success
experience aiding progress to success

Examples

Every small achievement became a stepping stone to success in her career.
He viewed each setback as a stepping stone to success, rather than a defeat.
Internships can serve as a valuable stepping stone to success for students entering the job market.

Synset Examples

Each failure is a stepping stone to success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milestone - a significant stage or event in progress
  • Antonym: obstacle - something that hinders progress

Etymology

From the literal use of 'stepping stone' (a stone on which one steps to cross a stream), used metaphorically for progress.
