plains inhabitant

1. NOUN

/ pleɪnz ɪnˈhæbɪtənt / (AmE), / pleɪnz ɪnˈhæbɪtənt / (BrE)
a person who lives in a plains region, especially the Great Plains of North America
person living in a plains region

Examples

The plains inhabitant often relied on buffalo for food and clothing.
Researchers studied the lifestyle of the plains inhabitant during the 19th century.
As a plains inhabitant, she was familiar with the vast open landscapes and harsh weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plainsman - a person who lives on the plains, especially in North America
