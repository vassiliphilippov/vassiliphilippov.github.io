order Bennettitales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər bəˌnɛtɪˈteɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə bɛˌnɛtɪˈteɪliːz / (BrE)
an extinct order of gymnospermous plants known from the Carboniferous period, characterized by their unique reproductive structures; known only in fossil form.
extinct order of fossil gymnosperm plants

Examples

Fossils of the order Bennettitales reveal details about plant evolution during the Jurassic period.
Researchers discovered new specimens of the order Bennettitales in sedimentary rock layers.
The order Bennettitales is now extinct, but once thrived during the Mesozoic era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bennettites - a genus of extinct seed plants within Bennettitales

Etymology

From the genus Bennettites (named after British botanist J.J. Bennett) + -ales (taxonomic suffix).
