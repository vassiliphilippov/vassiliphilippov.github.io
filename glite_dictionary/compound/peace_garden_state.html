Peace Garden State

1. PROPN

/ pis ˈɡɑrdən steɪt / (AmE), / piːs ˈɡɑːdən steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of North Dakota, USA
nickname for North Dakota, USA

Examples

North Dakota is often called the Peace Garden State because of the International Peace Garden on its border.
The license plates in the Peace Garden State proudly display the state's nickname.
Tourists flock to the Peace Garden State to explore its natural beauty and friendly communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Dakota - a state in north central United States bordering Canada

Etymology

Refers to the International Peace Garden on the North Dakota–Manitoba border.
