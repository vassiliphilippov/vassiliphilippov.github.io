sugar water

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃʊɡɚ ˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
a solution of water sweetened with sugar, often used as a simple drink or as food for animals and plants
water sweetened with sugar

Examples

The hummingbird was attracted to the sugar water in the feeder.
She gave her friend a glass of sugar water to soothe her sore throat.
Adding sugar water to the vase helps keep the flowers fresh longer.
