transportation system

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrænspərˈteɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A facility comprising the means and equipment necessary for the movement of passengers or goods.
means and infrastructure for moving people or goods

Examples

The city’s transportation system was upgraded to include more efficient buses and trains.
A reliable transportation system is essential for economic growth and connectivity.
Many people depend on the public transportation system to commute to work every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public transport - system of buses, trains, etc. available for everyone to use
  • Synonym: infrastructure - the basic physical and organizational structures needed for operation of a society or enterprise
