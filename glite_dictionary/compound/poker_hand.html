poker hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊkər hænd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə hænd / (BrE)
A set of five cards dealt to a player in a game of poker, used to compete against other players' sets based on their values.
five cards dealt in poker to one player

Examples

She was disappointed with her poker hand, as it contained no pairs or high cards.
Winning the round required a strong poker hand like a straight or a flush.
Each player examined their poker hand before deciding how much to bet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand - the collection of cards dealt to a player in a card game
