smooth-shelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmuð ˌʃɛld / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð ˌʃɛld / (BrE)
having a shell that is even and free from ridges, bumps, or roughness
having an even, untextured shell

Examples

The smooth-shelled turtle was easily distinguished from its rougher cousins.
Collectors prized the smooth-shelled eggs for their unique appearance.
Some snails have smooth-shelled forms, while others display intricate patterns and textures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rough-shelled - having a shell that is not smooth, often with ridges or bumps
