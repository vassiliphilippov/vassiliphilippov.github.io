symphony orchestra

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪm.fə.ni ɔɹˈkɛs.tɹə / (AmE), / ˈsɪm.fə.ni ɔːˈkɛs.trə / (BrE)
a large orchestra made up of string, woodwind, brass, and percussion instruments, which performs symphonic and other large-scale works
large orchestra performing symphonic music

Examples

The symphony orchestra will be performing Beethoven’s Ninth Symphony this Saturday.
She has played the violin in the symphony orchestra for over ten years.
Tickets for the symphony orchestra's concert sold out within hours.

Synset Examples

we heard the Vienna symphony

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestra - a group of musicians playing together
