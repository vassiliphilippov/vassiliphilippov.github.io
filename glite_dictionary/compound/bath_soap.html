bath soap

1. NOUN

/ bæθ soʊp / (AmE), / bɑːθ səʊp / (BrE)
a bar or type of soap designed for washing the body during bathing or showering
soap for washing the body

Examples

She always chooses a lavender-scented bath soap for her evening showers.
The hotel provided complimentary bath soap and shampoo in each room.
My skin feels softer since I switched to a moisturizing bath soap last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toilet soap - soap specially made for washing the body
