high heel

1. NOUN

/ haɪ hil / (AmE), / haɪ hiːl / (BrE)
A type of women's shoe with a tall, raised heel.
woman's shoe with tall heel

Examples

She wore her favorite high heels to the party, despite knowing her feet would hurt.
The store sells elegant high heels in various colors and designs.
After dancing all night in high heels, she looked forward to slipping into comfortable sneakers.

Synset Examples

The clack of high heels echoed in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stiletto - a woman's shoe with a very high, thin heel
  • Antonym: flat - a shoe with little or no heel
