hand wedge

1. NOUN

/ hænd wɛdʒ / (AmE), / hænd wɛdʒ / (BrE)
a small manual tool or support, typically wedge-shaped, used to secure, stabilize, or support objects temporarily
manual wedge-shaped support or tool

Examples

He slid a hand wedge beneath the door to keep it from closing.
The technician used a hand wedge to stabilize the equipment during the repair.
After assembling the shelf, she inserted a hand wedge for extra support.

Synset Examples

He used a hand wedge to keep the door open.
She placed a hand wedge under the tabletop for support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedge - a piece of wood, metal, or other material having one thick end and tapering to a thin edge, used to split things apart or secure them
