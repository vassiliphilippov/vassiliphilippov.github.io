King Arthur's Round Table

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ˈɑrθərz raʊnd ˈteɪbl̩ / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈɑːθəz raʊnd ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
the legendary circular table at which King Arthur and his knights sat as equals in Arthurian legend
legendary table of King Arthur and his knights

Examples

Many stories describe the legendary King Arthur's Round Table as a symbol of equality among the knights.
Tourists often visit the site in Winchester believed to display King Arthur's Round Table.
Discussions at King Arthur's Round Table supposedly decided the fate of the entire kingdom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: King Arthur - legendary king of the Britons in medieval legend
  • Synonym: Camelot - legendary castle and court associated with King Arthur

Etymology

From Arthurian legend; 'Round Table' represents equality among knights.
