snapshottery style

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnæp.ʃɒt.ər.i staɪl / (AmE), / ˈsnæp.ʃɒt.ər.i staɪl / (BrE)
a style of photography or art focused on capturing spontaneous moments or scenes, often with an informal or candid approach
art style capturing candid moments

Examples

The gallery's latest show highlighted the artist's snapshottery style in every photograph.
Critics praised her snapshottery style for its sense of immediacy and authenticity.
Many modern photographers adopt a snapshottery style to convey the spontaneity of everyday life.

Synset Examples

The exhibition showcased his unique snapshottery style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapshot - an informal photograph, especially one taken quickly

Etymology

From 'snapshot' (informal photograph) + '-ery' (indicating quality or style) + 'style'.
