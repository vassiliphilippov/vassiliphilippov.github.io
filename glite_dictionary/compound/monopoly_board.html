Monopoly board

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɑː.pə.li bɔrd / (AmE), / məˈnɒp.ə.li bɔːd / (BrE)
the playing board used in the game of Monopoly, marked with properties, spaces, and instructions essential to gameplay
board for the game Monopoly

Examples

She carefully unfolded the Monopoly board and arranged all the houses and hotels before the game started.
Whenever we lost the Monopoly board, we improvised with scraps of paper and markers.
The corners of their old Monopoly board were worn from years of family game nights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Monopoly - the board game involving property trading and management

Etymology

From the board game 'Monopoly,' first published in 1935.
