analog-digital converter

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ə.lɔg ˈdɪdʒ.ɪ.t̬əl kənˈvɝ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈæn.əlɒg ˈdɪdʒ.ɪ.təl kənˈvɜː.tə / (BrE)
a device that converts analogue signals into digital signals
device converting analogue to digital signals

Examples

The analog-digital converter in your phone changes sensor data into a format computers can process.
Modern audio equipment often includes a high-precision analog-digital converter to improve sound quality.
Engineers tested the new analog-digital converter to ensure it accurately captured voltage fluctuations.
