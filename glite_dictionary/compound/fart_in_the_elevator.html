fart in the elevator

1. NOUN

/ fɑrt ɪn ði ˈɛlɪˌveɪtɚ / (AmE), / fɑːt ɪn ði ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə / (BrE)
a humorous or embarrassing incident in which someone passes gas in a confined space, especially when others are present
embarrassing incident of flatulence in a confined space
informal slang

Examples

It was the classic case of a fart in the elevator—no one wanted to take credit.
The awkward silence after his fart in the elevator made everyone laugh.
Telling a story about a fart in the elevator broke the ice at the party.

Synset Examples

He quickly exited after a fart in the elevator.
It was awkward when someone farted in the elevator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatulence - the accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal
  • Synonym: bathroom humor - humor involving bodily functions, especially excretion
