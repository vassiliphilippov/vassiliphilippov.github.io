eagle freak

1. NOUN

/ ˈiɡəl friːk / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəl friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic about the Eagles (especially the Philadelphia Eagles football team), or who is passionate about eagle-related activities.
enthusiastic fan of the Eagles or eagles
informal

Examples

As an eagle freak, she owns every team jersey ever released.
He was called an eagle freak because he never missed a single game.
The club for eagle freaks organizes trips to every home and away match.

Synset Examples

She’s an eagle freak, rarely missing a game.
As an eagle freak, he knows all the players' stats.
Being an eagle freak, he collects memorabilia from every game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanatic - A person with extreme enthusiasm for a subject or activity
  • Synonym: aficionado - An enthusiastic fan or devotee of a particular activity or subject
