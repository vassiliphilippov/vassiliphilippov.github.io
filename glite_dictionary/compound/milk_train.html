milk train

1. NOUN

/ mɪlk treɪn / (AmE), / mɪlk treɪn / (BrE)
a train used for transporting milk and dairy products, especially from rural to urban areas
train for transporting milk and dairy products

Examples

The milk train delivered fresh milk to the city every morning.
Old photographs show the milk train passing through the countryside at dawn.
The local dairy relied on the milk train for its daily shipments.

Synset Examples
