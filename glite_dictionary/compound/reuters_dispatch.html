Reuters dispatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtərz dɪˈspætʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪtəz dɪˈspætʃ / (BrE)
a news report issued by the international news agency Reuters
news report from Reuters agency

Examples

The front page carried a Reuters dispatch about the latest developments in the Middle East.
According to a Reuters dispatch, the summit concluded with no major agreements.
A breaking Reuters dispatch alerted investors worldwide to the unexpected market changes.

Synset Examples

A Reuters dispatch the next day left the clear impression that the attack might have something to do with opponents of embryonic stem cell research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news dispatch - a report sent to a news agency or newspaper

Etymology

Reuters from founder's name, Paul Reuter (1816–1899), and dispatch meaning 'news message'.
