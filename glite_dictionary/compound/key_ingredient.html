key ingredient

1. NOUN

/ ki ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (AmE), / ki ɪnˈɡriːdiənt / (BrE)
the most important or essential component of something, especially in food or in any process, situation, or thing
most important component

Examples

Trust is a key ingredient in any successful relationship.
Patience is often the key ingredient to learning a new skill.
Garlic is a key ingredient in this pasta sauce recipe.

Synset Examples

Fresh herbs are the key ingredient for flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main ingredient - the primary or chief component of a mixture or recipe
  • Degree Lower: secret ingredient - an ingredient kept hidden to enhance a dish or result
