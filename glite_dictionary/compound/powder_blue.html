powder blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊdɚ bluː / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə bluː / (BrE)
a pale blue color that has a slight greyish tint
a pale, greyish-blue color

Examples

She wore a beautiful dress in powder blue to the wedding.
The painter chose powder blue for the walls to create a calming effect.
His tie was a subtle shade of powder blue that matched his shirt.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sky blue - a bright, light shade of blue, like a clear sky

Etymology

From the color of powdered blue dye used in laundering and tinting during the 17th century.

2. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊdɚ bluː / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə bluː / (BrE)
of a moderate to pale blue color, sometimes with a slight purplish tint
pale blue color

Examples

She chose a powder blue dress for the wedding ceremony.
The walls were painted in a soft powder blue to create a calming atmosphere.
A powder blue sky stretched above the tranquil countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sky blue - a light blue color like that of a clear sky
  • Degree Lower: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
  • Synonym: azure - bright blue color like a clear sky
