twist drill

1. NOUN

/ twɪst drɪl / (AmE), / twɪst drɪl / (BrE)
A drill bit with deep helical grooves running along its length, used for cutting holes in materials.
drill bit with helical grooves

Examples

The carpenter chose a twist drill to bore holes in the wooden panel.
You should always use a sharp twist drill when working with metal to achieve clean results.
Different sizes of twist drills are available for various types of projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill bit - a tool for cutting cylindrical holes, typically with a rotating motion
