ice cream cone

1. NOUN

/ aɪs krim koʊn / (AmE), / aɪs kriːm kəʊn / (BrE)
A cone-shaped baked wafer used as a container for serving ice cream.
cone-shaped container for ice cream

Examples

She gave her daughter an ice cream cone as a treat after dinner.
The chocolate ice cream cone melted quickly in the summer sun.
He dropped his ice cream cone on the pavement and looked disappointed.

Synset Examples

The ice cream cone remained unlicked on the table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waffle cone - a cone for ice cream with a crisp, waffle-like texture
  • Synonym: ice cream - a sweet frozen food made from milk or cream
