dinner napkin

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌnæpkɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌnapkɪn / (BrE)
a large cloth or paper napkin used at dinner to protect clothes and wipe the mouth and hands
a large napkin for use at dinner

Examples

She placed a crisp white dinner napkin on each plate before the guests arrived.
The restaurant provided a cloth dinner napkin for every customer.
He unfolded his dinner napkin and set it across his lap before starting to eat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: napkin - piece of cloth or paper for wiping the mouth and hands during a meal
