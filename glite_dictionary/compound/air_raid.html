air raid

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr reɪd / (AmE), / ˈeə reɪd / (BrE)
an attack in which military aircraft drop bombs or shoot at a target on the ground
attack by aircraft with bombs or guns

Examples

The city was heavily damaged during the air raid last night.
Sirens warned of an incoming air raid, and everyone rushed to shelters.
During World War II, civilians often took cover in basements during an air raid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bombing - an attack in which bombs are dropped on a target
  • Antonym: ground assault - a military attack carried out by troops on the ground
