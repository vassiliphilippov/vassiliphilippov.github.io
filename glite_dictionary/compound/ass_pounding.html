ass pounding

1. NOUN

/ æs ˈpaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / æs ˈpaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
A severe defeat, overwhelming loss, or physically punishing experience, often used in informal or vulgar contexts.
severe defeat or physically punishing experience (slang, vulgar)
