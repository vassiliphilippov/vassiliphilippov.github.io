dry cleaners

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌklinɚz / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnəz / (BrE)
a shop where clothes and other fabrics are cleaned with chemicals rather than water
shop for cleaning clothes with chemicals

Examples

I'll pick up my suit from the dry cleaners after work tonight.
She dropped off her wedding dress at the dry cleaners to have it cleaned.
We need to find a reliable dry cleaners in this neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: launderette - self-service laundry shop with washing machines and dryers
