anxiety disorder

1. NOUN

/ ænˈzaɪ.ə.ti dɪsˌɔɹ.dɚ / (AmE), / æŋˈzaɪ.ə.ti dɪsˌɔː.də / (BrE)
A mental health disorder characterized by excessive or persistent anxiety as a central symptom, affecting daily functioning.
mental disorder marked by severe anxiety
medical

Examples

She was diagnosed with an anxiety disorder after months of constant worry and restlessness.
People with an anxiety disorder may experience panic attacks in stressful situations.
Treatment for an anxiety disorder often includes therapy, medication, or both.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depression - mental disorder marked by persistent sadness
  • Synonym: phobia - type of anxiety disorder marked by irrational fear
