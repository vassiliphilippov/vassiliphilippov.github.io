pilgrim's journey

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪlɡrəmz ˈdʒɜrni / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɡrɪmz ˈdʒɜːni / (BrE)
a journey taken by a pilgrim to a sacred place for religious reasons
journey to a sacred place

Examples

Many faithful people embark on a pilgrim's journey to Mecca each year.
His pilgrim's journey to Jerusalem changed his life profoundly.
Undertaking a pilgrim's journey is seen as an act of devotion in many religions.
