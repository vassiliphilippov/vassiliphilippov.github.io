stem lettuce

1. NOUN

/ stɛm ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / stɛm ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a variety of lettuce grown mainly for its thick, edible stem rather than its leaves
lettuce grown for its edible stem

Examples

Stem lettuce is popular in Asian cuisine for its crunchy texture and mild flavor.
Farmers harvested rows of stem lettuce to sell at the local market.
The recipe calls for sliced stem lettuce as the main ingredient in the salad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celtuce - another name for stem lettuce
  • Degree Higher: lettuce - a leafy vegetable commonly used in salads
