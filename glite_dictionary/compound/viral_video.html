viral video

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪrəl ˈvɪdioʊ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəl ˈvɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
A video that quickly becomes popular by being widely shared, especially on the internet.
popular, rapidly shared internet video

Examples

The cat's viral video was viewed by millions within a day of being uploaded.
She became famous overnight after her dance viral video swept the internet.
Companies hope their commercials will turn into the next big viral video.

Synset Examples

Did you see Shitney's latest viral video?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - an idea, behavior, or style that spreads rapidly online

Etymology

Formed from 'viral' (in reference to rapid spread, as with a virus) and 'video'.
