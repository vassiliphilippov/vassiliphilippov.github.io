heart-healthy

1. ADJ

/ hɑrt ˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
(of food) beneficial to heart health; low in fats, sodium, and other ingredients that may contribute to heart disease.
beneficial to heart health (low in fats, sodium, etc.)

Examples

Doctors often recommend eating a heart-healthy diet rich in vegetables and whole grains.
This cereal is marketed as heart-healthy because it contains oats and very little sodium.
Making heart-healthy choices at the grocery store can help prevent cardiovascular disease.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unhealthy - harmful to health
  • Synonym: low-fat - containing little fat
