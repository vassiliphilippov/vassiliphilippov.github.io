family circle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈsɜrkl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
the highest or rearmost area in a theater balcony, where the least expensive seats are located
cheapest section of theater balcony

Examples

Tickets in the family circle sold out quickly for the opening night of the musical.
She could only afford seats in the family circle, but was delighted just to be at the theater.
From the family circle, the stage looked distant, but the atmosphere was still exciting.
