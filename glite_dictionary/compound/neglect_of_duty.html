neglect of duty

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈɡlɛkt əv ˈduːti / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛkt əv ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
failure to perform one's responsibilities or obligations, especially in a professional or official capacity; breach of duty.
failure to fulfill responsibilities
legal

Examples

The officer was dismissed for neglect of duty after failing to respond to the emergency call.
Repeated neglect of duty can result in disciplinary action or even termination.
She was accused of neglect of duty when important safety checks were missed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dereliction of duty - intentional or serious neglect of official responsibilities
  • Antonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort
