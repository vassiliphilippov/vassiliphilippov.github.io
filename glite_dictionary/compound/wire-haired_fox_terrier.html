wire-haired fox terrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪr ˌhɛrd fɑks təˈrɪər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə hɛəd fɒks ˈtɛrɪə / (BrE)
a breed of fox terrier with a dense, wiry coat and distinctive facial hair, originally bred for fox hunting
breed of dog with wiry hair

Examples

The wire-haired fox terrier is known for its playful and energetic temperament.
She entered her wire-haired fox terrier in the national dog show and won first prize.
Many owners appreciate how little a wire-haired fox terrier sheds compared to other breeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fox terrier - a breed of terrier originally bred for fox hunting
