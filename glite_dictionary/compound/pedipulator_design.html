pedipulator design

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛdəˈpjʊlətɚ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌpɛdɪˈpjʊlətə dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a robotics design or mechanism that enables precise movement control, typically modeled after leg or arm movements
robotics design for precise movement control
medical

Examples

The pedipulator design greatly improves the robot's ability to handle delicate tasks in surgery.
Researchers are developing a new pedipulator design for use in space exploration robots.
Our company patented a novel pedipulator design to enhance industrial automation.

Synset Examples

The pedipulator design allows for precise movements in robotics.

Etymology

From 'pedipulator' (from Latin 'pes/pedis' meaning 'foot' and 'manipulator') + 'design'.
