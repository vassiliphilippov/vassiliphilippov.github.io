solant goose

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlənt ɡus / (AmE), / ˈsɒlənt ɡuːs / (BrE)
a very large white seabird with black wing tips, also known as the northern gannet
large white seabird (northern gannet)

Examples

The cliffs were home to hundreds of solant geese nesting on narrow ledges.
A solant goose soared above the waves, searching for fish.
Fishermen often spotted the distinctive black wingtips of the solant goose from afar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gannet - a large seabird with white plumage and black wing tips
