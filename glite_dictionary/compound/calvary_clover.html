Calvary clover

1. NOUN

/ ˈkælvəri ˌkloʊvər / (AmE), / ˈkælvəri ˌkləʊvə / (BrE)
an annual plant of the Mediterranean region, notable for its spiny seed pods and leaves marked with dark spots
Mediterranean annual plant with spiny seed pods

Examples

The fields were dotted with Calvary clover, its leaves flecked with distinctive dark spots.
Botanists studied the Calvary clover to understand the adaptation of its spiny seed pods.
In Mediterranean gardens, Calvary clover often appears as a hardy, self-seeding annual.
