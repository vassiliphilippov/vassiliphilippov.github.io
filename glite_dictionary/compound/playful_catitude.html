playful catitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪfəl ˈkætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈpleɪfl ˈkatɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
an attitude that is carefree, mischievous, and playful, like that often associated with cats
carefree, playful attitude like a cat's
informal slang

Examples

She faced every obstacle with a playful catitude that made her popular among her friends.
His playful catitude brought laughter to even the most serious meetings.
Approaching tasks with playful catitude helped her stay calm and creative under pressure.

Synset Examples

He approached the challenge with a playful catitude.

Etymology

Blend of 'cat' and 'attitude', modeled after 'catitude' (cat + attitude).
