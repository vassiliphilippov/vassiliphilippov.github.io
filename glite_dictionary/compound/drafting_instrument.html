drafting instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræftɪŋ ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftɪŋ ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
a tool used by a draftsman for making precise technical drawings or plans
tool for technical drawing

Examples

He carefully selected a drafting instrument from the case before starting his architectural plan.
The engineer relied on several drafting instruments to complete the detailed blueprint.
Modern computers have replaced many traditional drafting instruments in design studios.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drawing instrument - tool used for making drawings
