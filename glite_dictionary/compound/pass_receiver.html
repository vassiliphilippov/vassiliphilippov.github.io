pass receiver

1. NOUN

/ pæs rɪˈsiːvər / (AmE), / pɑːs rɪˈsiːvə / (BrE)
a football player whose role is to catch forward passes during a game
football player who catches forward passes

Examples

The quarterback threw a perfect spiral to his pass receiver in the end zone.
Every successful team needs at least one outstanding pass receiver on the roster.
During practice, the coach worked with each pass receiver to improve their route running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receiver - football player who catches a pass
