Secretary of Agriculture

1. NOUN

/ sɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i əv æ.ɡrɪˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / sɛk.rɪˌteə.ri əv æ.ɡrɪˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the official who serves as the head of the Department of Agriculture in the government, responsible for overseeing national agricultural policy
government minister for agriculture

Examples

The Secretary of Agriculture announced new policies to support sustainable farming this year.
Every Secretary of Agriculture faces unique challenges depending on market conditions and climate.
She was sworn in as the new Secretary of Agriculture after being confirmed by the Senate.

Synset Examples

the post of Secretary of Agriculture was established in 1889

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agriculture - the science or practice of farming
