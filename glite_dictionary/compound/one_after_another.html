one after another

1. ADV

/ wʌn ˈæftɚ əˈnʌðɚ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈɑːftə əˈnʌðə / (BrE)
(of things or events) following each other in quick or uninterrupted succession.
in quick succession, sequentially

Examples

The students entered the classroom one after another, filling every seat.
Failures kept coming one after another, but he refused to give up.
The fireworks exploded one after another, lighting up the night sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in succession - one after the other, in a consecutive order
  • Synonym: one by one - each in turn, individually
