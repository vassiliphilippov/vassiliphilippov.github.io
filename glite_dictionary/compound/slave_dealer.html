slave dealer

1. NOUN

/ sleɪv ˈdiːlɚ / (AmE), / sleɪv ˈdiːlə / (BrE)
a person who buys and sells enslaved people as part of the slave trade
person who trades enslaved people

Examples

The museum display included records kept by a notorious slave dealer from the 1800s.
He made a fortune as a slave dealer before the abolition of slavery.
The ship was chartered by a wealthy slave dealer operating along the coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slave trader - person engaged in the slave trade
  • Antonym: abolitionist - person who advocates for the abolition of slavery
