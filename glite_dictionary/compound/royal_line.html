royal line

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪəl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl laɪn / (BrE)
the family line or lineage descended from a sovereign or royal family, especially considered as a group
family lineage of royalty

Examples

The royal line can be traced back to the 12th century in this country.
With no heirs left in the royal line, succession became a disputed matter.
Many treasures were passed down through generations of the royal line.

Synset Examples

the wedding was attended by royalty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: royalty - members of a royal family; royal persons
  • Antonym: commoner - a person who is not of noble rank
