zerogamous lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˌzɪəroʊˈɡæməs ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəʊˈɡaməs ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of life characterized by the absence of romantic commitments or exclusive relationships.
lifestyle without romantic commitments

Examples

After years of heartbreak, he embraced a zerogamous lifestyle focused solely on personal growth and friendships.
Some people find a zerogamous lifestyle frees them from the pressures of dating and relationships.
A zerogamous lifestyle is becoming more common among those prioritizing career or personal goals over romance.

Synset Examples

They chose a zerogamous lifestyle to avoid commitments.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monogamous lifestyle - lifestyle involving a single long-term romantic partner
  • Antonym: polyamorous lifestyle - lifestyle involving multiple romantic partners with consent

Etymology

Formed from 'zero-' (none) and '-gamous' (from Greek 'gamos', meaning marriage), drawing analogy from terms like 'monogamous'.
