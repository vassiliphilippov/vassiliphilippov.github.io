computer programmer

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpjuːtər ˈproʊɡræmər / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈprəʊɡræmə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to design, write, and test computer programs.
person who writes computer programs

Examples

She worked as a computer programmer for a major software company in Silicon Valley.
The company is looking to hire an experienced computer programmer to update its database system.
As a computer programmer, he's skilled in several coding languages including Python and Java.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: software developer - a person who creates and maintains computer software
