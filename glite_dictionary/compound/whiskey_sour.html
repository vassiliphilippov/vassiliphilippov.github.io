whiskey sour

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski ˌsaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ˌsaʊə / (BrE)
a cocktail made with whiskey, lemon or lime juice, sugar, and sometimes egg white, typically served over ice
cocktail with whiskey, citrus, and sugar

Examples

He ordered a whiskey sour at the bar to start the evening.
The bartender made the perfect whiskey sour with just the right amount of lemon juice.
Some people prefer their whiskey sour with a splash of egg white for a smooth texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sour - a family of cocktails made with a base liquor, citrus, and sweetener
