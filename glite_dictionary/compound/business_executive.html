business executive

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
a person who holds a senior managerial position in a business corporation, responsible for making key decisions and overseeing operations
senior manager in a corporation

Examples

The business executive presented the company's new strategy at the annual meeting.
After twenty years, she became a highly respected business executive in the tech industry.
Many business executives travel frequently to negotiate major deals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executive - person with senior managerial responsibilities in an organization
