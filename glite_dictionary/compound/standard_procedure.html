standard procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændərd pɹəˈsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈstændəd prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
A prescribed or established way of doing something that is routinely followed in a particular situation.
routine method or practice

Examples

Following the standard procedure ensured that the experiment was conducted correctly and safely.
It is standard procedure to check identification before allowing entry into the building.
During emergencies, staff are expected to adhere to the standard procedure without hesitation.

Synset Examples

rote memorization has been the educator's standard operating procedure for centuries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protocol - official rules or procedures to be followed
  • Synonym: custom - a traditional practice or usual way of behaving
