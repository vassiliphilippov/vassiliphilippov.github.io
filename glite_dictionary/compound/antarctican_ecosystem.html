Antarctican ecosystem

1. NOUN

/ ænˈtɑːrk.tɪ.kən ˈiːˌkoʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ænˈtɑːktɪkən ˈiːkəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
the interconnected community of living organisms and their environment in Antarctica
ecological system of Antarctica

Examples

The Antarctican ecosystem is uniquely adapted to extreme cold and limited sunlight.
Climate change poses a serious threat to the delicate balance of the Antarctican ecosystem.
Researchers are mapping the biodiversity within the Antarctican ecosystem to monitor changes over time.

Synset Examples

He studied the Antartican ecosystem for his thesis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecosystem - a biological community of interacting organisms and their environment
