prairie rocket

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛri ˈrɑkət / (AmE), / ˈpreəri ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
any of several western North American plants of the genus Cheiranthus, especially those with large yellow flowers.
western North American flowering plant, genus Cheiranthus

Examples

The prairie rocket blooms in the early spring across the western plains.
Gardeners admire the bright yellow flowers of the prairie rocket.
Hikers spotted patches of prairie rocket along the trail in Colorado.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wallflower - a plant of the same genus Cheiranthus, often with yellow or orange flowers
