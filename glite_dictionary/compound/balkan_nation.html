Balkan nation

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæl.kən ˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈbæl.kən ˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a country located on the Balkan Peninsula in southeastern Europe
country on the Balkan Peninsula

Examples

Greece is a well-known Balkan nation with a rich cultural heritage.
The political tensions among several Balkan nations have shaped the region's recent history.
Tourists often visit more than one Balkan nation during a trip through southeastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Balkan country - country situated on the Balkan Peninsula

Etymology

Balkan from Turkish 'balkan' (mountain), nation from Latin 'natio' (birth, people).
