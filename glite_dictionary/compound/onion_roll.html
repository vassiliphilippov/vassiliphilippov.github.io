onion roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌnjən roʊl / (AmE), / ˈʌnjən rəʊl / (BrE)
A soft, yeast-raised bread roll flavored with onions, often with pieces of onion inside or on top.
yeast-raised bread roll with onion flavor

Examples

I ordered an onion roll with my soup at the deli.
The bakery makes fresh onion rolls every morning, topped with caramelized onions.
She prefers her sandwich on an onion roll instead of plain white bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagel - chewy bread roll, often round with a hole
