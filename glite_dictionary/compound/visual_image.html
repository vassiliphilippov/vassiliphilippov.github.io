visual image

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪd͡ʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuːəl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
an image produced in the mind or visual system as a result of visual perception
image formed by visual perception

Examples

The artist closed her eyes and tried to hold the visual image of the sunset in her mind.
After the flash of lightning, a visual image lingered before his eyes.
Children often remember vocabulary words better when they associate each with a clear visual image.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental image - an image that occurs in the mind, including those not originating from visual input

2. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a mental picture or impression that resembles or is based on what is seen by the eyes
a mental picture resembling what is seen

Examples

The author used vivid words to create a visual image in the reader's mind.
She closed her eyes and tried to recall the visual image of her childhood home.
A strong visual image can help people remember information more effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental image - an image formed in the mind, not necessarily visual
