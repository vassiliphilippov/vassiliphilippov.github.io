on tour

1. NOUN

/ ɑn tʊr / (AmE), / ɒn tʊə / (BrE)
traveling from place to place, especially as part of a group’s series of scheduled events or performances
traveling for a series of events

Examples

The band will be on tour in Europe throughout the summer.
The comedian spent two months on tour promoting his new material.
After winning the championship, the team went on tour to celebrate with fans in other cities.

Synset Examples

they took the show on the road
they lost all their games on the road

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the road - traveling, especially as part of a performance or sports series
