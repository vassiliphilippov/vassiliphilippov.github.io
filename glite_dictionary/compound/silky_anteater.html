silky anteater

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlki ˈæntiˌitər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ˈæntiːtə / (BrE)
a small, squirrel-sized anteater native to Central and South America, with long, silky golden fur and no teeth
small Central and South American anteater

Examples

The silky anteater is rarely seen because it lives high in the rainforest canopy.
With its long golden fur, the silky anteater blends perfectly into its surroundings.
Scientists observed a silky anteater feeding at night in the Amazon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anteater - a mammal that feeds mainly on ants and termites
