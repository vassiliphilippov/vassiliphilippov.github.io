hair care

1. NOUN

/ hɛr kɛr / (AmE), / heə keə / (BrE)
the practice of keeping hair clean, healthy, and well-groomed, including washing, cutting, styling, or conditioning it
maintenance and treatment of hair

Examples

Proper hair care can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
She follows a strict hair care routine, using only salon-quality products.
Men are becoming more interested in hair care and grooming products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairdressing - the activity or occupation of styling or arranging hair
  • Synonym: hairstyling - the art or practice of styling hair
