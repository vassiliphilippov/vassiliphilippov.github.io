Haleakala National Park

1. PROPN

/ hɑːleɪˌɑːkəˈlɑː ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑːrk / (AmE), / hɑːliːˌæləkəˈlɑː ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Hawaii, known for the Haleakala dormant volcano and diverse landscapes
a national park in Hawaii

Examples

Every year, thousands of tourists visit Haleakala National Park to watch the sunrise from the summit.
The unique wildlife and breathtaking views make Haleakala National Park a must-see destination in Hawaii.
Scientists often study the rare plants found only in Haleakala National Park.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'Haleakalā' meaning 'house of the sun' and English 'National Park'.
