stormy petrel

1. NOUN

/ ˌstɔrmi ˈpɛtrəl / (AmE), / ˌstɔːmi ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
A small, sooty-black seabird with white markings found in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean, known for flying close to the surface of the sea during storms.
a small Atlantic and Mediterranean seabird

Examples

A stormy petrel darted over the crests of the waves as the wind picked up.
Many sailors consider the stormy petrel a sign of approaching rough weather.
The biography described him as a political stormy petrel, always stirring trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petrel - a type of seabird in the family Hydrobatidae
