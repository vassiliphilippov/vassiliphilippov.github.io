loyal companion

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔɪ.əl kəmˈpæn.jən / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪ.əl kəmˈpæn.jən / (BrE)
a person or animal who remains steadfastly supportive and faithful to someone, especially as a friend or partner
faithful friend or partner

Examples

After her surgery, her dog was her constant loyal companion during recovery.
Traveling the world feels less lonely with a loyal companion by your side.
The story is about an adventurer and his loyal companion facing challenges together.

Synset Examples

He considered his cat a loyal companion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faithful companion - trusted and dependable friend or partner
