to soothe a sore throat

1. VERB

/ suð ə sɔr θroʊt / (AmE), / suːð ə sɔː θrəʊt / (BrE)
to relieve or lessen the discomfort, pain, or irritation in one's throat
relieve throat discomfort

Examples

Drinking warm tea may help soothe a sore throat after a long day of speaking.
Many people use honey and lemon to soothe a sore throat naturally.
Doctors often recommend lozenges to soothe a sore throat during a cold.

Synset Examples

Honey is great for soothing a sore throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ease a sore throat - to make throat pain less severe
