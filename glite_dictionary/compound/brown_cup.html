brown cup

1. NOUN

/ braʊn kʌp / (AmE), / braʊn kʌp / (BrE)
a type of fungus in the genus Sclerotinia, characterized by a cup-shaped, brown fruiting body
a type of cup-shaped brown fungus

Examples

The brown cup is commonly found on decaying wood in damp forests.
Scientists studied the brown cup to understand its role in plant disease.
Some gardeners remove brown cups to prevent the spread of fungal infections.
