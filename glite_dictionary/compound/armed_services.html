armed services

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmd ˈsɝvəsɪz / (AmE), / ɑːmd ˈsɜːvɪsɪz / (BrE)
the military forces of a country, including the army, navy, air force, and sometimes other branches
a country's military forces

Examples

After college, he decided to join the armed services to serve his country.
The government increased funding for the armed services in response to rising tensions.
Members of the armed services are honored on Veterans Day for their dedication and bravery.

Synset Examples

their military is the largest in the region
the military machine is the same one we faced in 1991 but now it is weaker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - the armed forces as a whole
  • Antonym: civilian - a person who is not a member of the armed services

Etymology

From 'armed', meaning equipped with weapons, and 'services', referring to branches of organised activity.
