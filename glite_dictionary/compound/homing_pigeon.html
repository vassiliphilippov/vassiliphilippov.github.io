homing pigeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmɪŋ ˌpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmɪŋ ˌpɪdʒən / (BrE)
a pigeon trained to return to its home after being released from a distant location, often used to carry messages
pigeon trained to return home

Examples

The army used homing pigeons to deliver secret messages during the war.
After the race, her prize homing pigeon returned to its loft within hours.
Many bird enthusiasts keep homing pigeons and train them for competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carrier pigeon - a pigeon trained to carry messages
