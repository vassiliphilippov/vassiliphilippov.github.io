Osaka Bay

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈsɑːkə beɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈsɑːkə beɪ / (BrE)
a bay of the western Pacific Ocean located in southern Honshu, Japan, near the cities of Osaka and Kobe
bay in southern Honshu, Japan

Examples

The city of Kobe is situated on the coast of Osaka Bay.
Numerous ships pass through Osaka Bay each day due to its busy ports.
During our trip to Japan, we enjoyed a cruise along Osaka Bay at sunset.
