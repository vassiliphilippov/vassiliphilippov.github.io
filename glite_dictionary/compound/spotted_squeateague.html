spotted squeateague

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd ˌskwiːəˈtiːɡ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˌskwiːəˈtiːɡ / (BrE)
A species of weakfish found along the southern Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of the United States, known for its spotted appearance.
a weakfish of southern US coasts

Examples

Fishermen hope to catch a spotted squeateague during their trips to the Gulf Coast.
The spotted squeateague is known for the distinctive spots along its silvery sides.
Many anglers prize the spotted squeateague for its fighting spirit and delicate flavor.
