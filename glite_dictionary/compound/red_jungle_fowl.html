red jungle fowl

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈdʒʌŋɡəl faʊl / (AmE), / rɛd ˈdʒʌŋɡl faʊl / (BrE)
a wild bird of southeastern Asia, considered to be the ancestor of the domestic chicken
wild ancestor of the domestic chicken

Examples

The red jungle fowl can still be found in the forests of Southeast Asia.
Scientists believe the red jungle fowl is the primary ancestor of modern chickens.
Wild populations of red jungle fowl exhibit colorful plumage and distinctive calls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jungle fowl - any of several wild Asian birds related to domestic chickens
  • Degree Higher: domestic chicken - common domesticated fowl descended from the red jungle fowl
