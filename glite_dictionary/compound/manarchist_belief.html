manarchist belief

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɑrkɪst bəˈlif / (AmE), / ˈmænɑːkɪst bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
a political ideology that advocates for male dominance and the prioritization of men's interests
ideology favoring male dominance

Examples

Some online forums attract users who openly discuss their manarchist beliefs.
His manarchist belief was evident in the way he dismissed women's opinions.
Critics argue that manarchist beliefs undermine gender equality efforts.

Synset Examples

His views were influenced by manarchist beliefs.

Etymology

Blend of 'man' and 'anarchist', with '-ist' for adherent and 'belief' for ideology.
