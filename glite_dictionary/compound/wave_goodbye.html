to wave goodbye

1. VERB

/ weɪv ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / weɪv ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to say farewell by moving your hand back and forth, usually as a gesture to someone leaving
to bid farewell with a waving gesture

Examples

They stood at the gate to wave goodbye to their grandparents as the taxi drove away.
She smiled and waved goodbye before boarding the airplane.
He felt a little sad as he waved goodbye to his friends at the end of the summer camp.

Synset Examples

It's hard to wave goodbye to childhood friends.
I had to wave goodbye as the train pulled away.
She waved goodbye as the trucker left the depot.
He waved goodbye as they drove off down the road.
She waved goodbye, adding a cheerful 'Gracias!' as she left.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to say goodbye - to express farewell, usually verbally
