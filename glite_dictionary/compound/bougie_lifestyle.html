bougie lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːʒi ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈbuːʒi ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living characterized by luxury, extravagance, or association with the upper class, often with a sense of conspicuous consumption or aspiration.
extravagant, upper-class way of living
slang

Examples

She documented her bougie lifestyle on social media, posting photos of gourmet meals and exotic holidays.
Critics accused the influencer of promoting a bougie lifestyle that most of her followers could not afford.
Despite his humble beginnings, he now enjoys a distinctly bougie lifestyle filled with designer clothing and exclusive parties.

Synset Examples

His bougie lifestyle included fine dining and luxury cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious lifestyle - living with great wealth and comfort

Etymology

'Bougie' is a slang shortening of 'bourgeois,' originally from French 'bourgeois' (middle-class).
