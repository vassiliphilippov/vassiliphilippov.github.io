burn mark

1. NOUN

/ bɝn mɑrk / (AmE), / bɜːn mɑːk / (BrE)
a visible area or spot on a surface or skin where burning has occurred, leaving a mark or discoloration
mark left by burning

Examples

The hot iron left a burn mark on his shirt sleeve.
She has a small burn mark on her hand from touching the stove.
There was a visible burn mark on the old wooden table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scar - a mark left on the skin after a wound has healed
  • Synonym: blemish - a small mark or flaw that spoils the appearance of something
