social scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
an expert in the study of human society and social relationships
an expert in social sciences

Examples

The social scientist presented her research on community dynamics at the international conference.
As a social scientist, he often collaborates with psychologists and economists.
Many social scientists are interested in understanding how cultural norms shape behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociologist - an expert in sociology
