jungle gym

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋɡəl dʒɪm / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl dʒɪm / (BrE)
A structure consisting of interconnected bars and rods, designed for children to climb and play on in outdoor playgrounds.
playground climbing structure for children

Examples

The kids spent hours climbing on the jungle gym at the park.
After school, she likes to hang upside down from the jungle gym.
They installed a new jungle gym in the community playground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climbing frame - playground structure for climbing, chiefly British term

Etymology

Coined in early 20th century America, possibly from the idea of climbing like in a jungle.
