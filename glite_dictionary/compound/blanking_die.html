blanking die

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæŋkɪŋ daɪ / (AmE), / ˈblæŋkɪŋ daɪ / (BrE)
a tool used in manufacturing that cuts a flat piece of material to a specific shape in a single operation
tool for cutting material to shape in one step

Examples

A blanking die is commonly used in the automotive industry to create precise body panel shapes.
The engineer carefully adjusted the blanking die before starting the production process.
This factory uses a sophisticated blanking die to cut metal sheets quickly and accurately.

Synset Examples

Generally a blanking die may only cut the outside contour of a part, often used for parts with no internal features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - a specialized tool used to cut or shape material
