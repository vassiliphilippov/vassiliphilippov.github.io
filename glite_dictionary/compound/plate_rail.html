plate rail

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt reɪl / (AmE), / pleɪt reɪl / (BrE)
a narrow rail or shelf attached to a wall, designed to display plates or other decorative objects
narrow wall shelf for displaying plates

Examples

She placed her grandmother's china on the plate rail above the fireplace.
Victorian homes often feature a plate rail in the dining room for decorative display.
The carpenter installed a custom plate rail to showcase the antique plates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picture rail - a molding attached to a wall from which pictures can be hung
