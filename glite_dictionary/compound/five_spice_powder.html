five spice powder

1. NOUN

/ faɪv spaɪs ˈpaʊdər / (AmE), / faɪv spaɪs ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
a Chinese seasoning made by grinding star anise, fennel, pepper, cloves, and cinnamon
Chinese spice blend

Examples

She added a teaspoon of five spice powder to the marinade for extra flavor.
The aroma of five spice powder filled the kitchen as the duck was roasting.
Many Asian markets sell five spice powder in both small and large packages.
