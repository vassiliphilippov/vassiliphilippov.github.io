genus Turreae

1. NOUN

/ ˈd͡ʒiːnəs tʊˈriːiː / (AmE), / ˈd͡ʒiːnəs tjʊˈriːiː / (BrE)
A genus of trees and shrubs found in tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.
genus of tropical trees and shrubs

Examples

The genus Turreae includes several species native to tropical forests.
Botanists recently discovered a new member of the genus Turreae in northern Australia.
Research on the genus Turreae has advanced our understanding of plant evolution in tropical regions.

Etymology

From Latin 'genus' (kind, type) and 'Turreae,' the taxonomic name of the group.
