ivy arum

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪvi ˈɛərəm / (AmE), / ˈaɪvi ˈɑːrəm / (BrE)
An evergreen climbing plant, widely grown for its variegated leaves, commonly kept as an indoor ornamental.
ornamental evergreen climbing plant

Examples

Many people grow ivy arum in hanging baskets because of its attractive trailing vines.
The leaves of ivy arum display unique patterns of green and yellow.
Ivy arum is easy to care for and thrives in indoor environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Epipremnum aureum - the scientific name for ivy arum
  • Synonym: pothos - another common name for ivy arum
