deep freezer

1. NOUN

/ diːp ˈfriːzər / (AmE), / diːp ˈfriːzə / (BrE)
A large electric freezer for storing food at very low temperatures for a long period of time.
large freezer for long-term storage

Examples

We bought a new deep freezer to store meat and vegetables for the winter.
Our old deep freezer stopped working during the storm, and all the food thawed out.
He keeps ice cream and frozen meals in the deep freezer in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freezer - an appliance used to keep food at very low temperatures
