excellent service

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksələnt ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈɛksələnt ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
Assistance or help provided of very high quality, especially in a professional or customer-service context.
high-quality assistance or help

Examples

The restaurant is famous for its excellent service as well as its delicious cuisine.
We received excellent service from the staff throughout our stay at the hotel.
Customers often return because they value the excellent service they consistently experience.

Synset Examples

I tipped the drinks waiter generously for his excellent service.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: good service - assistance provided that meets expectations
  • Degree Higher: outstanding service - assistance or help that is exceptionally good or remarkable
