electrostatic precipitator

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtə / (BrE)
A device that removes dust and other fine particles from gases using electrostatic charges.
device removing dust from gases by electric charge

Examples

The factory installed a new electrostatic precipitator to reduce air pollution from its smokestacks.
Routine maintenance of the electrostatic precipitator is crucial for efficient operation.
Many power plants use an electrostatic precipitator to comply with environmental regulations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust collector - device used to improve air quality by collecting airborne particles

Etymology

From electrostatic (relating to stationary electric charges) and precipitator (device that causes precipitation).
