spicy dipping sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsi ˈdɪpɪŋ sɔs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi ˈdɪpɪŋ sɔːs / (BrE)
a sauce with a spicy flavor served as an accompaniment for dipping food
spicy sauce for dipping food

Examples

The spring rolls are even better with the spicy dipping sauce on the side.
He prepared a homemade spicy dipping sauce to serve with the chicken wings.
Many Asian restaurants offer a spicy dipping sauce for dumplings and fried snacks.
