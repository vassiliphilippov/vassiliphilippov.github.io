record-keeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛkərd ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːd ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to keep and maintain records, especially official or systematic ones
person responsible for keeping records

Examples

The record-keeper made sure every transaction was documented accurately.
Our organization’s record-keeper maintains files dating back to the 1950s.
As the team’s record-keeper, she updated the membership list every month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archivist - a person responsible for preserving and organizing archives
  • Synonym: registrar - an official responsible for keeping records, especially of births, deaths, and marriages
