nether cheek

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛðər tʃik / (AmE), / ˈnɛðə tʃiːk / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic term for the buttocks or the rear end
buttocks; rear end (euphemistic)

Examples

The chair was so uncomfortable, it bruised my nether cheek.
He slipped on the ice and landed squarely on his nether cheek.
After hours of cycling, her nether cheek was sore.

Synset Examples

The chair was so uncomfortable, it bruised my nether cheek.
He fell and landed hard on his nether cheek.
She winced after landing on her nether cheek during the fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buttocks - the fleshy part of the body on which a person sits

Etymology

From 'nether' meaning 'lower' + 'cheek', suggesting the cheeks of the buttocks.
