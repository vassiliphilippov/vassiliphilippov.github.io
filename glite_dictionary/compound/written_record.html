written record

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪtn ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈrɪtn ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a document in written form that preserves evidence or knowledge of facts or events
document preserving evidence or information

Examples

The contract served as a written record of their agreement.
It is important to keep a written record of all financial transactions.
The ancient scrolls are a valuable written record of early civilization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record - an account or document preserving information
  • Antonym: oral record - knowledge or history preserved by word of mouth
