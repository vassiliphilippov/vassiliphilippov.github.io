tap wrench

1. NOUN

/ tæp ˌrɛntʃ / (AmE), / tæp ˌrɛntʃ / (BrE)
a tool used to hold and turn a tap for cutting internal screw threads in a hole
tool for turning a tap to cut internal threads

Examples

The mechanic selected a tap wrench to cut threads inside the new metal part.
You must use a tap wrench carefully to avoid breaking the tap during threading.
He inserted the tap into the tap wrench and began threading the drilled hole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrench - tool for gripping and turning objects
