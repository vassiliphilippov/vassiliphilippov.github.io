component part

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpoʊ.nənt pɑːrt / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊ.nənt pɑːt / (BrE)
a single element or unit that forms part of a larger whole
an element of a larger whole

Examples

Every component part of the clock must be cleaned carefully during restoration.
The engine failed because a crucial component part was missing.
Learning each component part of the process helped me understand the whole system.

Synset Examples

he wanted to feel a part of something bigger than himself

the smaller component is hard to reach
the animal constituent of plankton

Related Terms

  • Synonym: component - a part or element of a larger whole
  • Synonym: constituent - an essential part or element
  • Antonym: whole - something that is complete in itself
