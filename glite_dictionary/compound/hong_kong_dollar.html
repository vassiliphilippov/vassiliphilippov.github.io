Hong Kong dollar

1. NOUN

/ hɑŋ kɔŋ ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / hɒŋ kɒŋ ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of Hong Kong
currency of Hong Kong

Examples

She exchanged her US dollars for Hong Kong dollars at the airport.
The price is listed in Hong Kong dollars on the website.
Travelers often need to purchase Hong Kong dollars before visiting the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - a unit of currency used in several countries
