military machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
the organized armed forces of a nation, considered as a powerful and coordinated system, especially in terms of its ability to wage war
the armed forces of a nation

Examples

The country invested heavily in its military machine to strengthen national defense.
During the war, the military machine operated with remarkable efficiency and discipline.
Many historians debate the true power of the Roman military machine in ancient times.

Synset Examples

their military is the largest in the region
the military machine is the same one we faced in 1991 but now it is weaker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - the armed forces of a country
  • Synonym: war machine - the military organization and resources for waging war
