nutmeg-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a nutmeg.
shaped like a nutmeg

Examples

The seeds are nutmeg-shaped and brownish in color.
She found a nutmeg-shaped stone on the beach.
The artist crafted a small, nutmeg-shaped ornament from wood.
