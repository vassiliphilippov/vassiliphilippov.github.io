Fort George Gordon Meade

1. PROPN

/ fɔrt ʤɔrdʒ ˈɡɔrdən mid / (AmE), / fɔːt ˌʤɔːdʒ ˈɡɔːdən miːd / (BrE)
A United States Army base in Maryland that serves as the headquarters of the National Security Agency.
U.S. Army base in Maryland, NSA headquarters

Examples

Many intelligence operations are overseen from Fort George Gordon Meade in Maryland.
The National Security Agency is headquartered at Fort George Gordon Meade.
She was stationed at Fort George Gordon Meade during her military service.

Etymology

Named after Union Army General George Gordon Meade, noted for his victory at the Battle of Gettysburg.
