wishing cap

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪʃɪŋ kæp / (AmE), / ˈwɪʃɪŋ kap / (BrE)
a magical cap in folklore that grants the wearer whatever they wish for
magical cap granting wishes (folklore)

Examples

The hero placed the wishing cap on his head and immediately found himself in a faraway land.
Legends say that whoever possesses the wishing cap can fulfill any desire.
Children listened with wide eyes as the storyteller described the magical powers of the wishing cap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic hat - a hat with magical powers
  • Synonym: wishing well - a well thought to grant wishes to those who toss a coin into it
