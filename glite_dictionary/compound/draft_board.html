draft board

1. NOUN

/ dræft bɔrd / (AmE), / drɑːft bɔːd / (BrE)
a local committee or agency responsible for selecting individuals for compulsory military service, especially during times of conscription.
agency selecting people for military conscription

Examples

The draft board notified him that he was to report for military service.
Many young men anxiously awaited their letters from the draft board during the Vietnam War.
She appealed her case to the draft board, hoping for a deferment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conscription - compulsory enlistment for state service, typically into the armed forces
