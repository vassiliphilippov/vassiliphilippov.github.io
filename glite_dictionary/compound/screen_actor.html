screen actor

1. NOUN

/ skrin ˈæktɚ / (AmE), / skriːn ˈæktə / (BrE)
an actor who appears in films or on television, rather than on stage.
actor in films or TV

Examples

After years as a stage performer, he became a screen actor in Hollywood.
Many screen actors start out in television commercials or minor film roles.
She won an award for her work as a screen actor in independent movies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stage actor - an actor who performs in the theater, on stage
  • Synonym: film actor - an actor who performs in movies
