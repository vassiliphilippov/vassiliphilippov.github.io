sky rat

1. NOUN

/ skaɪ ræt / (AmE), / skaɪ ræt / (BrE)
(informal, slang) An urban pigeon or similar bird, often regarded as a nuisance or pest.
informal term for a city pigeon
informal slang

Examples

The park bench was surrounded by sky rats searching for crumbs.
Newspapers have debated whether feeding sky rats encourages their overpopulation in the city.
After lunch, we watched sky rats swoop down to snatch discarded chips.

Synset Examples

The city’s skyline was marred by a few sky rats nesting on the ledges.
He called the pigeons sky rats as they scavenged for food.
The tourists were amused by the sky rats hovering near the cafes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigeon - a bird commonly found in city areas
  • Synonym: rat - rodent known as a pest, referenced for comparison
