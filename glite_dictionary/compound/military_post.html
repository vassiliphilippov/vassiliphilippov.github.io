military post

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri poʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pəʊst / (BrE)
A military installation where a unit or body of troops is stationed for defense, operations, or administration.
military installation for stationed troops

Examples

The town grew rapidly after the nearby military post was established.
Soldiers at the military post train daily for upcoming missions.
Families of officers live on the military post during assignments abroad.

Synset Examples

this military post provides an important source of income for the town nearby
there is an officer's club on the post

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military base - a facility for storing troops and equipment; closely related military installation
