intricate carving

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntrɪkət ˈkɑrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪntrɪkət ˈkɑːvɪŋ / (BrE)
a carving featuring elaborate and detailed patterns or designs, often requiring great skill
very detailed carving

Examples

The museum's exhibits include an intricate carving of a dragon from the Ming dynasty.
She admired the intricate carving on the antique wooden chair in the study.
Restorers worked carefully to preserve the intricate carving around the entrance to the historic cathedral.

Synset Examples

The church's altar was adorned with intricate carvings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carving - the act of shaping something from a material by cutting away parts
