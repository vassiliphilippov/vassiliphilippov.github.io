soda bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌbɑt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle designed for containing soft drinks such as soda.
bottle for soft drinks

Examples

She recycled the empty soda bottle after finishing her drink.
The picnic table was covered with plates and a large soda bottle.
Please grab a soda bottle from the fridge before you sit down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft drink bottle - a bottle for any non-alcoholic, carbonated beverage
