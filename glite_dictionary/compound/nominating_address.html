nominating address

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːmɪˌneɪtɪŋ əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈnɒmɪˌneɪtɪŋ əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
a formal speech, typically at a political convention, proposing someone as a candidate for election
proposing speech at a political convention

Examples

The senator delivered a stirring nominating address that rallied the delegates to her cause.
His nominating address was eloquent and highlighted the candidate's lifelong dedication to public service.
During the convention, she was chosen to give the nominating address for her party's presidential hopeful.

Synset Examples

the nomination was brief and to the point

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nomination speech - a speech nominating someone for office
