business enterprise

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ˌɛntərˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˌɛntəˌprʌɪz / (BrE)
an organization or activity involved in the provision of goods or services for profit, including financial, commercial, and industrial aspects
an organization involved in commercial activities

Examples

The government is encouraging small business enterprises by offering tax incentives.
Starting a business enterprise requires careful planning and a clear understanding of the market.
The city attracts investment from a variety of business enterprises, ranging from technology to retail.

Synset Examples

computers are now widely used in business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company - a commercial business
