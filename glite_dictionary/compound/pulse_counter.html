pulse counter

1. NOUN

/ pʌls ˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / pʌls ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
an electronic device that counts the number of electrical pulses it receives
electronic device for counting electrical pulses

Examples

The pulse counter in the system accurately records every incoming electrical signal.
Technicians used a pulse counter to monitor the output from the generator.
A simple pulse counter can help automate various industrial processes.
