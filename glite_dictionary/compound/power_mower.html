power mower

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌmoʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌməʊ.ə / (BrE)
a lawn mower operated by a gasoline engine rather than manually
gasoline-powered lawn mower

Examples

He cut the grass in the backyard with his new power mower.
The noise from the neighbor's power mower woke me up early on Saturday morning.
Before starting the power mower, make sure there is enough gasoline in the tank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lawn mower - a machine for cutting grass
