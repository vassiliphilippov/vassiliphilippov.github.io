prompter's box

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːmptərz bɑːks / (AmE), / ˈprɒmptəz bɒks / (BrE)
A small booth projecting above the floor in front of a theater stage, where the prompter sits to cue actors during a performance.
booth in front of stage for theater prompter

Examples

The actor glanced toward the prompter's box to catch his forgotten line.
During the play, the prompter's box was discreetly positioned at the edge of the stage.
In older theaters, you can still see the original prompter's box used by the stage staff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prompter - person who cues actors during a performance
