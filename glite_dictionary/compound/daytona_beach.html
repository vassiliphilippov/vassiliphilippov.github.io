Daytona Beach

1. PROPN

/ deɪˈtoʊnə ˌbiːtʃ / (AmE), / deɪˈtəʊnə ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
A city and resort town in northeastern Florida, known for its beaches and role in automobile racing history.
city and resort in Florida, USA

Examples

Every spring, thousands of students travel to Daytona Beach for vacation.
The annual car race in Daytona Beach attracts motorsport fans from around the world.
Daytona Beach is famous for its long stretches of hard, white sand.
