brain fart

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪn fɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn fɑːt / (BrE)
a temporary lapse in memory or concentration, often causing forgetfulness or confusion
temporary lapse in memory or focus
informal

Examples

I had a total brain fart and left my keys in the car.
She called it a brain fart when she forgot the answer during the test.
Don't worry, everyone has a brain fart every now and then.

Synset Examples

During the meeting, he experienced a brain fart.
It was just a brain fart; I’ll remember later.
I had a brain fart and forgot her name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memory lapse - a brief period of forgetting something
  • Synonym: senior moment - a lapse in memory, often associated with aging
