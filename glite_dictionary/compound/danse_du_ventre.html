danse du ventre

1. NOUN

/ dɑ̃s du ˈvɑ̃tɹə / (AmE), / dɑ̃s dy ˈvɑ̃tɾə / (BrE)
A Middle Eastern dance characterized by sensuous movements of the hips and abdomen; also known as belly dance.
sensuous Middle Eastern dance

Examples

She learned the danse du ventre while traveling in Egypt.
The performance featured a traditional danse du ventre with colorful costumes and lively music.
During the festival, an instructor offered a workshop on danse du ventre techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly dance - A style of dance performing sensuous abdominal movements, common in Middle Eastern cultures

Etymology

French, literally 'dance of the belly'.
