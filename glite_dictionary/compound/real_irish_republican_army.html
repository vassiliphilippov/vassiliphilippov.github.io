Real Irish Republican Army

1. PROPN

/ ɹiːl ˌaɪɹɪʃ ɹɪˌpʌblɪkən ˈɑːɹmi / (AmE), / ɹiːl ˌaɪɹɪʃ ɹɪˌpʌblɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a dissident Irish republican paramilitary group, formed in 1997 as a breakaway from the Provisional IRA, known for opposing peace agreements and carrying out terrorist attacks
Irish republican dissident paramilitary group (formed 1997)

Examples

The Real Irish Republican Army claimed responsibility for the 1998 Omagh bombing.
After splitting from the main group, the Real Irish Republican Army continued to oppose the peace process.
Authorities believe the Real Irish Republican Army remains active despite years of security crackdowns.
