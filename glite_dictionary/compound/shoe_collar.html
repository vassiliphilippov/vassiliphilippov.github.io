shoe collar

1. NOUN

/ ʃu ˈkɑlər / (AmE), / ʃu ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
The padded or stitched rim at the opening of a shoe or boot, often providing comfort and support around the ankle.
rim at the opening of a shoe or boot

Examples

The padded shoe collar adds extra comfort for runners during long workouts.
Make sure the shoe collar fits snugly around your ankle to prevent blisters.
Dirt collects easily along the shoe collar, so it's important to clean it regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoe tongue - strip under the laces of a shoe
