gym mat

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm mæt / (AmE), / dʒɪm mæt / (BrE)
a thick, cushioned pad placed on the floor to provide safety and support during gymnastics, exercise, or sports activities
thick padded mat for gymnastic sports

Examples

The children practiced their rolls on the gym mat to ensure a soft landing.
Please put the gym mat back in the storage room after your workout.
A thick gym mat is essential for safe tumbling exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mat - a small piece of material placed on a floor for protection or comfort
