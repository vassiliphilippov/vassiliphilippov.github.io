blackberry-lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbɛri ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈblækbəri ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A perennial garden plant (Iris domestica) whose ripe seed capsule opens to reveal a mass of seeds resembling a blackberry.
garden plant with blackberry-like seeds

Examples

We planted a blackberry-lily in the flowerbed for its striking orange flowers and unique seed pods.
After the capsule splits, the seeds of the blackberry-lily look just like a cluster of blackberries.
Gardeners admire the blackberry-lily both for its attractive foliage and for the showy seeds it produces.
