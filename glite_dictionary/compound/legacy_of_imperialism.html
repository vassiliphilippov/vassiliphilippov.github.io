legacy of imperialism

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛɡəsi əv ɪmˈpɪriəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ɪmˈpɪəriəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the enduring political, economic, social, and cultural consequences resulting from a period of colonial rule and exploitation by imperial powers
lasting impact of colonial rule

Examples

The legacy of imperialism is evident in the political boundaries drawn in many African countries.
Educational inequality in former colonies is often traced back to the legacy of imperialism.
Debates over reparations are frequently linked to the legacy of imperialism.

Synset Examples

Many debates focus on the legacy of imperialism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colonialism - policy or practice of acquiring full or partial control over another country
  • Synonym: imperialism - policy of extending a country's power and influence through colonization or use of military force
