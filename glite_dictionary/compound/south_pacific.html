South Pacific

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ pəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / saʊθ pəˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
the region of the Pacific Ocean located south of the equator, often including the islands and countries situated there
region of the Pacific Ocean south of the equator

Examples

The islands of the South Pacific attract tourists with their stunning beaches and diverse cultures.
Many species unique to the South Pacific face threats due to climate change.
She spent two years working on marine conservation projects in the South Pacific.
