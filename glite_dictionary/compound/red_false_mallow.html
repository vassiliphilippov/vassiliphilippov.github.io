red false mallow

1. NOUN

/ rɛd fɔls ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / rɛd fɔːls ˈmaləʊ / (BrE)
A plant of the western United States with racemose red flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Malvastrum.
plant with red racemose flowers (western US)

Examples

The red false mallow blooms vibrantly in the spring along canyon trails in Utah.
Gardeners admire the striking color of the red false mallow's flowers.
Some botanists have debated whether the red false mallow belongs in the genus Malvastrum.
