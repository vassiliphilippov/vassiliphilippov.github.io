star billing

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈbɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / stɑː ˈbɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
prominent credit or position given to a leading actor or performer in a production
prominent credit for a leading performer

Examples

The leading actress was thrilled to have star billing in the new production.
Despite being a newcomer, he received star billing alongside the veterans.
Directors sometimes argue over which performer deserves star billing in ensemble films.

Synset Examples

The movie featured several big names, but he got star billing.
She received star billing for her outstanding performance.
With star billing, the actors are guaranteed more screen time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top billing - the highest credit or most prominent position given to a performer

Etymology

Metaphorically uses 'star' for a famous performer and 'billing' for the order of names on a bill or poster.
