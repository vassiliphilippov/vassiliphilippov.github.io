ball game

1. NOUN

/ bɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / bɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played on a field or court in which a ball is used, such as baseball or football.
a sport played with a ball

Examples

The children played a friendly ball game in the park after school.
Baseball is the most popular ball game in some countries.
She joined a local ball game league to stay active.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sport - an activity involving physical exertion and skill
  • Synonym: game - an activity that people do for fun, often following rules
