honey fungus

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌni ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈhʌni ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
an edible mushroom with honey-colored caps, commonly growing at the base of trees, especially in late summer and autumn; should not be eaten raw
an edible honey-colored mushroom found near trees

Examples

We found several honey fungus clusters growing at the base of the old oak tree.
Although honey fungus is edible, experts recommend cooking it thoroughly before eating.
Gardeners often worry when honey fungus appears, as it can spread and damage trees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mushroom - a type of fungus, typically with a stem and a cap
  • Antonym: toadstool - a wild mushroom, especially one considered inedible or poisonous
