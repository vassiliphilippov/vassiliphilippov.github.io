steel plant

1. NOUN

/ stil plænt / (AmE), / stiːl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a factory where steel is produced from raw materials or semi-finished products
factory where steel is made

Examples

The new steel plant will create thousands of jobs in the region.
Strict safety protocols are enforced throughout the steel plant to protect workers.
Environmental regulations require the steel plant to reduce its emissions significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steel mill - a factory for making steel
