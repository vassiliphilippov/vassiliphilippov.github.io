square measure

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / skwɛə ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a unit or system of units used to measure area, such as square meter, square foot, or square mile
units for measuring area

Examples

Land is usually calculated using square measure to determine its total area.
The architect converted all the dimensions to square measure for the building plans.
Many countries use the metric system for square measure instead of imperial units.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: area - the amount of surface covered by a shape or object
