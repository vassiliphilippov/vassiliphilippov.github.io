way out

1. NOUN

/ weɪ aʊt / (AmE), / weɪ aʊt / (BrE)
an exit or opening through which one can leave or escape from a place
an exit or means of escape

Examples

The security guard pointed to the way out at the end of the corridor.
In an emergency, always look for the nearest way out.
He searched the cave for any possible way out but found none.

Synset Examples

he blocked the way out
the canyon had only one issue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exit - a way of leaving a place
