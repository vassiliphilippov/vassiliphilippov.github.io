subversive activity

1. NOUN

/ səbˈvɜrsɪv ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / səbˈvɜːsɪv ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
Actions intended to undermine, overthrow, or destroy an established authority or government, especially by covert or illegal means.
actions aimed at overthrowing authority

Examples

The government cracked down on subversive activity to prevent the spread of rebellion.
He was jailed for his involvement in subversive activity against the state.
Intelligence agencies are constantly monitoring for signs of subversive activity within the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sedition - conduct or speech inciting rebellion against authority
  • Synonym: treason - the crime of betraying one's country, especially by attempting to overthrow the government
