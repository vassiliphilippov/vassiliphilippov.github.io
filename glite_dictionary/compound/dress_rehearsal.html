dress rehearsal

1. NOUN

/ drɛs rɪˈhɜrsəl / (AmE), / drɛs rɪˈhɜːsəl / (BrE)
a full, uninterrupted practice of a play or performance in complete costume, carried out shortly before the first public show
final rehearsal in full costume

Examples

The cast gathered on stage for the final dress rehearsal before opening night.
A technical mishap during the dress rehearsal helped the crew prepare for the real show.
She felt nervous during the dress rehearsal, but performing in costume made it real.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rehearsal - a practice or trial performance of a play or other work
