on one's knees

1. ADP

/ ɑn wʌnz niz / (AmE), / ɒn wʌnz niːz / (BrE)
in a kneeling position, or in a state of submission or humility
in a kneeling posture or state of submission

Examples

The team was on its knees after a string of defeats, desperately hoping for a win.
He dropped on his knees to beg for forgiveness.
She was on her knees in the garden, pulling out weeds.

Synset Examples

After the fall, she was on her knees checking for injuries.
The child was on his knees, searching for the toy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to kneel - to be in or get into a position where your body is supported on your knees
