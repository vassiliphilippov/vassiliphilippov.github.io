Philippine peso

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlɪpɪn ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪpɪn ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
the official currency of the Philippines, equal to 100 centavos
currency of the Philippines

Examples

The price was 500 Philippine pesos, which is affordable for most locals.
She exchanged her dollars for Philippine pesos at the Manila airport.
You can pay in Philippine pesos using cash or a credit card.

Etymology

From Spanish 'peso' meaning 'weight.'
