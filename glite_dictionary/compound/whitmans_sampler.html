Whitman's sampler

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪtmənz ˈsæmplɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪtmənz ˈsɑːmplə / (BrE)
A diverse selection or varied mixture of different items or experiences.
varied selection or assortment

Examples

Her poetry collection was a true Whitman's sampler of emotions.
The travel show presents a Whitman's sampler of destinations from around the globe.
The festival offered a Whitman's sampler of local cuisines, music, and crafts.

Synset Examples

Her poetry collection was a true Whitman's sampler of emotions.
He described his musical style as a Whitman's sampler of genres.
The art exhibition felt like a Whitman's sampler of creativity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assortment - a collection containing a variety of things
  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things

Etymology

From 'Whitman's Sampler', a popular assortment box of chocolates introduced by the Whitman's candy company in 1912 (U.S.), publicized as a variety selection.
