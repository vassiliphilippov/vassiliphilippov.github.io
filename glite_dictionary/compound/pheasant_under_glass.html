pheasant under glass

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛzənt ˌʌndɚ ˈɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈfɛznt ˌʌndə ˈɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A dish consisting of roast pheasant served, typically in fine restaurants, under a glass cover.
roast pheasant dish served under glass

Examples

The restaurant was famous for its luxurious pheasant under glass and attentive service.
On special occasions, my grandfather would prepare pheasant under glass as a family treat.
Many consider pheasant under glass to be a symbol of classic haute cuisine from the early twentieth century.
