deer grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪr ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several perennial plants of the genus Rhexia, typically with pink-purple to magenta flowers, native to eastern North America
a genus of flowering plants from eastern North America

Examples

The deer grass in the meadow bloomed in shades of magenta throughout the summer.
Gardeners often plant deer grass to attract pollinators to their yards.
We identified several varieties of deer grass during our hike in the national park.
