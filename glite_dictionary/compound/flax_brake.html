flax brake

1. NOUN

/ flæks breɪk / (AmE), / flæks breɪk / (BrE)
a machine used to separate the woody part of flax stems from the fibrous material.
machine for separating flax fibers

Examples

The old farmer demonstrated how to use the flax brake to process raw flax.
Many museums display a flax brake as part of their exhibits on traditional textile production.
The flax brake played a crucial role in turning flax plants into usable fiber for linen.
