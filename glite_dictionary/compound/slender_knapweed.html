slender knapweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɛndɚ ˈnæpˌwid / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˈnæpwiːd / (BrE)
A species of knapweed (Centaurea debeauxii) distinguished by its slender form and purple flower heads, native to parts of Europe.
a species of knapweed with slender stems

Examples

The meadow was filled with wildflowers, including bright purple slender knapweed swaying in the breeze.
Botanists identified the rare slender knapweed growing along the edge of the woodland.
Gardeners appreciate slender knapweed for its delicate stems and long blooming period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knapweed - a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family
