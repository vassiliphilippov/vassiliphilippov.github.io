over a lakh

1. X

/ ˈoʊvər ə læk / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə ə læk / (BrE)
more than one hundred thousand, usually referring to money or counted items, especially in South Asian contexts
more than one hundred thousand (esp. money, S. Asia)

Examples

The damage was estimated to be over a lakh in losses.
He donated over a lakh rupees to the charity.
Each car in the fleet cost over a lakh.

Synset Examples

The project cost over a lakh to complete.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lakh - one hundred thousand (100,000) in South Asian numbering

Etymology

From Hindi लाख (lākh), meaning 'hundred thousand', commonly used in South Asian English.
