open-armed hug

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpən ˈɑrmd hʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈɑːmd hʌɡ / (BrE)
a warm and enthusiastic embrace that is given with arms outstretched in welcome or affection
a warm, welcoming embrace

Examples

She greeted her family with an open-armed hug after her long trip abroad.
He offered an open-armed hug to anyone who needed comfort.
Nothing compares to the feeling of an open-armed hug from a close friend.

Synset Examples

She welcomed her friends back with open-armed hugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embrace - an act of holding someone closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection
  • Synonym: hug - an act of holding someone tightly in one's arms to express affection
