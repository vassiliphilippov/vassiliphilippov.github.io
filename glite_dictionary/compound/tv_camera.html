TV camera

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː ˈkæm.ɚ.ə / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː ˈkæm.ər.ə / (BrE)
A television camera; a device for capturing moving images for television broadcasting by converting them into electronic signals.
camera for television broadcasting

Examples

The news crew set up their TV camera on the street to film the event.
He operates the TV camera during the live sports broadcast.
The studio invested in a new high-definition TV camera to improve picture quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television camera - camera used to capture images for television

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'television' (TV) combined with 'camera'.
