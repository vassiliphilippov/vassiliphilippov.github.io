mint raita

1. NOUN

/ mɪnt ˈraɪətə / (AmE), / mɪnt ˈraɪətə / (BrE)
A yogurt-based sauce flavored with mint, commonly served as a condiment in Indian cuisine.
Indian yogurt-mint sauce

Examples

She made a refreshing mint raita for the summer picnic.
A bowl of mint raita was served alongside the spicy curry.
To cool down the meal, they added spoonfuls of mint raita to their plates.

Synset Examples

She made a refreshing mint raita for the summer picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raita - an Indian condiment of yogurt and spices, sometimes with vegetables or herbs

Etymology

From Hindi 'raita', a yogurt-based condiment, and English 'mint'.
