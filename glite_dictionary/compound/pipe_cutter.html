pipe cutter

1. NOUN

/ paɪp ˈkʌtɚ / (AmE), / paɪp ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a hand tool designed for cutting pipes cleanly and accurately
tool for cutting pipes

Examples

The plumber used a pipe cutter to neatly trim the copper tubing.
A pipe cutter provides a smoother edge than a hacksaw when cutting metal pipes.
You should always measure the pipe before using a pipe cutter to ensure the perfect length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe wrench - a wrench for gripping and turning pipes
