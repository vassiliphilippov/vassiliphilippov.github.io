horsetail lichen

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrˌsteɪl ˈlaɪkən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌsteɪl ˈlɪkən / (BrE)
a lichen of the genus Alectoria, characterized by a thallus made up of hair-like filaments
a lichen with hair-like filaments

Examples

The tangled mass of horsetail lichen hung from the old spruce branches.
Scientists study horsetail lichen to learn more about air quality in northern forests.
Hikers often mistake horsetail lichen for moss due to its fine filaments.
