outdoor plans

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔɹ plænz / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.dɔː plænz / (BrE)
activities or events arranged to take place outdoors
events arranged to take place outside

Examples

We had to cancel our outdoor plans because it started to rain heavily.
Their outdoor plans for a picnic were disrupted by sudden strong winds.
It is wise to check the weather forecast before making outdoor plans.

Synset Examples

The thunderstorm might kill our outdoor plans.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indoor plans - activities scheduled to take place inside
