drill rig

1. NOUN

/ drɪl rɪɡ / (AmE), / drɪl rɪɡ / (BrE)
A large machine or structure used to drill wells for the extraction of oil or gas.
machine for drilling oil or gas wells

Examples

The workers assembled the drill rig on the new oil field.
A powerful drill rig is essential for offshore drilling operations.
The company invested in a state-of-the-art drill rig to speed up exploration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drilling rig - machine that creates holes in the earth's surface
