Isthmus of Kra

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪsθ.məs əv krɑː / (AmE), / ˈɪsθ.məs əv krɑː / (BrE)
a narrow isthmus in southern Thailand linking the Malay Peninsula to the Asian mainland
narrow isthmus in southern Thailand

Examples

The Isthmus of Kra has long been considered a potential site for a canal connecting the Gulf of Thailand with the Andaman Sea.
Several trade routes pass through the narrow Isthmus of Kra in southern Thailand.
Efforts to build a canal across the Isthmus of Kra have been proposed for centuries.

Etymology

Isthmus from Greek isthmos (neck of land), Kra from local Thai designation.
