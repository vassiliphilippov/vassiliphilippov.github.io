over there

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvɚ ˈðɛr / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ˈðeə / (BrE)
at or in a place or direction that is distant from the speaker
in a location away from the speaker

Examples

The museum is over there, across the river bridge.
If you need a chair, there's one over there by the window.
He pointed over there where the children were playing.

Synset Examples

The bakery is over there, just past the park.
You can find my car parked over there.
She waved at me from over there.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: here - in this place; close to the speaker
  • Synonym: there - in or at that place; farther from the speaker
