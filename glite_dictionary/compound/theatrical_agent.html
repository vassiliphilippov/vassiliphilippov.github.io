theatrical agent

1. NOUN

/ θiˈætrɪkəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company who represents actors and finds them work in theatre, film, or television
person who represents actors in show business

Examples

After signing with a new theatrical agent, she landed several roles in major stage productions.
A good theatrical agent can help actors secure auditions and negotiate contracts.
He worked as a theatrical agent in London, representing both established stars and newcomers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talent agent - a person who finds jobs for actors, musicians, etc.
  • Synonym: booking agent - an agent who arranges performances for artists
