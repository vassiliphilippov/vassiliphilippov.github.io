armored vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmɚd ˈviːəkəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈviːɪkl / (BrE)
a vehicle equipped with protective armor plating to defend against attacks or hazards
vehicle fitted with armor plating

Examples

The convoy included several armored vehicles for protection against roadside bombs.
Police deployed an armored vehicle to help disperse the crowd safely.
The museum displays an old armored vehicle used during World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - a heavily armored fighting vehicle with tracks and a large gun
  • Degree Lower: armored car - a lighter armored vehicle, often used to transport valuables or personnel
