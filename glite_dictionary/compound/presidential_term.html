presidential term

1. NOUN

/ ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl tɜrm / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl tɜːm / (BrE)
the length of time during which a president holds office
duration of a presidency

Examples

He was re-elected for a second presidential term in 2004.
The constitution limits the presidential term to four years.
Her reforms were implemented during her presidential term.

Synset Examples

things were quiet during the Eisenhower administration

Related Terms

  • Synonym: term of office - the period during which a person holds a particular official position
