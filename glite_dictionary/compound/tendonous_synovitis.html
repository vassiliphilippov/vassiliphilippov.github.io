tendonous synovitis

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛn.də.nəs ˌsɪn.əˈvaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.də.nəs ˌsɪn.əˈvaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a tendon and its surrounding sheath, often causing pain and swelling.
inflammation of a tendon sheath
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with tendonous synovitis after experiencing severe wrist pain for weeks.
Physical therapy can help in treating mild cases of tendonous synovitis.
Chronic tendonous synovitis may require anti-inflammatory medication to reduce swelling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenosynovitis - medical term for inflammation of a tendon sheath

Etymology

From Latin 'tendo' (tendon) and Greek 'syn-' (together) + 'ov' (egg, sheath) + '-itis' (inflammation).
