black margate

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈmɑrˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / blæk ˈmɑːˌɡeɪt / (BrE)
a dusky grey fish (Anisotremus surinamensis) found in warm western Atlantic waters from Louisiana and Florida southward, valued as food
a dusky grey food fish of the western Atlantic

Examples

Fishermen in the Gulf of Mexico often catch black margate along coral reefs.
We grilled fresh black margate for dinner after returning from our trip to Florida.
The black margate is easily identified by its dusky grey coloring and deep body.
