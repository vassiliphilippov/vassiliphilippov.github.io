rich with

1. ADJ

/ rɪtʃ wɪð / (AmE), / rɪtʃ wɪð / (BrE)
containing a large amount of a particular quality, ingredient, or feature; abundant in.
abundant in; full of (something)

Examples

The garden was rich with blooming flowers in early spring.
Her voice was rich with emotion as she told her story.
This chocolate cake is rich with the flavor of dark cocoa.

Synset Examples

The soup was rich with marrow, enhancing its flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rich in - containing a lot of something; abundant in
