Schrödinger's douchebag

1. NOUN

/ ʃroʊˈdɪŋərz ˈduːʃˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ʃrəʊˈdɪŋəz ˈduːʃˌbaɡ / (BrE)
A person whose actual character, often whether they are rude or decent, remains uncertain until assessed or observed more closely.
person of uncertain character until assessed
informal slang

Examples

You never know with him; he's a Schrödinger's douchebag until you see how he acts in different situations.
Some people thought Mark was charming, but others suspected he was just a Schrödinger's douchebag.
Until she revealed her true colors, we all considered her a Schrödinger's douchebag in the group.

Synset Examples

He's a Schrödinger's douchebag until you get to know him.
Her behavior makes her a Schrödinger's douchebag in the group.
You can’t tell if he’s nice or rude; he’s a Schrödinger's douchebag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: two-faced - person who presents different characters in different situations
  • Synonym: pretender - person who pretends to be something they're not

Etymology

From Schrödinger (referring to Schrödinger's cat thought experiment, representing uncertainty) + douchebag (slang for an obnoxious person).
