knife edge

1. NOUN

/ naɪf ɛdʒ / (AmE), / naɪf ɛdʒ / (BrE)
the sharp cutting side of the blade of a knife
the sharp edge of a knife blade

Examples

Be careful not to touch the knife edge when washing the blade.
The knife edge was sharp enough to slice through the tomato with ease.
After years of use, the knife edge had become dull and needed sharpening.
