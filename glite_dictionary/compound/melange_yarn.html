melange yarn

1. NOUN

/ meɪˈlɑːnʒ jɑrn / (AmE), / meɪˈlɒ̃ʒ jɑːn / (BrE)
A type of yarn made by combining at least two fibers, which may differ in type or color, resulting in a blended appearance.
yarn made from blended fibers or colors

Examples

The soft sweater was crafted from melange yarn to create a subtle, multi-tone look.
Designers often use melange yarn to add depth and interest to knitted garments.
The rug features melange yarn blends, giving it a rich and textured appearance.

Synset Examples

This shirt is defined by a lightly brushed twill weave in deep colors woven using melange yarn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blended yarn - yarn made by spinning together different types of fibers

Etymology

Melange from French 'mélange' (mixture); 'yarn' from Old English 'gearn'.
