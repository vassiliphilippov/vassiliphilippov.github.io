fencing stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnsɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɛnsɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
a stick used in place of a sword for practicing fencing or swordplay
stick used for fencing practice

Examples

The instructor handed each student a fencing stick to practice their parries and thrusts safely.
Before using real swords, children often begin with a fencing stick to learn the basics of the sport.
He fashioned a fencing stick out of a sturdy branch to simulate sword fighting with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foil - a light fencing sword with a flexible blade
