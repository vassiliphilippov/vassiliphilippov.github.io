climate arson

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmət ˈɑrsən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət ˈɑːsən / (BrE)
the deliberate destruction or endangerment of the environment, especially through actions that contribute to climate change
deliberate environmental destruction

Examples

The government was accused of climate arson after rolling back environmental protections.
International leaders warned that climate arson threatens the future of the planet.
Laws aimed to prevent climate arson have been proposed by several environmental organizations.

Synset Examples

The report highlighted the dangers of climate arson.
Activists protested against climate arson in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmental destruction - the act of damaging or ruining the natural environment
