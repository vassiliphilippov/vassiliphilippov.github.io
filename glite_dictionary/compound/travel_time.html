travel time

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl taɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time spent traveling from one place to another
the time spent traveling

Examples

The company will reimburse employees for travel time during official business trips.
He calculated his travel time to work to see if moving closer would save him hours each week.
Allow extra travel time during rush hour due to expected heavy traffic.

Synset Examples

workers were not paid for their travel time between home and factory

Related Terms
