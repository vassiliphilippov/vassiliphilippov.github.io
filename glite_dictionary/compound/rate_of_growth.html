rate of growth

1. NOUN

/ reɪt əv ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / reɪt əv ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
the speed at which something increases in size or quantity over a period of time
speed at which something grows

Examples

The rate of growth for the company doubled last year, exceeding all expectations.
Agricultural scientists measure the rate of growth of crops under different conditions.
Slower job creation has resulted in a lower rate of growth for the national economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: growth rate - rate at which something grows
