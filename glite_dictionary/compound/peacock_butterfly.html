peacock butterfly

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːkɑk ˈbʌtɚflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈpiːkɒk ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
A European butterfly with reddish-brown wings, each marked with a striking purple eyespot.
European butterfly with eyespot-marked wings

Examples

We spotted a beautiful peacock butterfly resting on the garden flowers.
The vivid eyespots on the peacock butterfly help it to deter predators.
Every spring, peacock butterflies emerge in large numbers across the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butterfly - an insect with large colorful wings and a slender body
