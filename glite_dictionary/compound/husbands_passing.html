husband's passing

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌzbəndz ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndz ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
the death of a woman's husband, often used as a gentle or euphemistic expression
the death of one's husband

Examples

She struggled with grief in the months following her husband's passing.
After her husband's passing, friends and family offered their support and condolences.
Many legal and financial matters had to be settled after her husband's passing.

Synset Examples

She became a widow after her husband's passing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: husband's death - the death of a woman's husband
  • Antonym: wife's passing - the death of a man's wife
