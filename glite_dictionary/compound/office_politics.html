office politics

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɪs pəˈlɪ.tɪks / (AmE), / ˈɒ.fɪs pəˈlɪ.tɪks / (BrE)
the strategies and manipulative tactics people use to gain advantage or influence within a workplace setting
manipulative behavior in the workplace

Examples

She excelled at navigating office politics to advance her career.
Many employees try to avoid office politics whenever possible.
Good leaders strive to reduce the negative impact of office politics on their teams.

Synset Examples

Avoiding office politics is crucial for a peaceful workplace.
She navigated office politics to secure a promotion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workplace politics - strategies and behavior related to power and influence in a work environment
