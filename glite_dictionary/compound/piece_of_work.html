piece of work

1. NOUN

/ pis əv wɝk / (AmE), / piːs əv wɜːk / (BrE)
a creation or achievement that is notable for being challenging, impressive, or remarkable, sometimes used sarcastically to describe someone or something unusual or difficult
impressive or remarkable creation or achievement

Examples

That novel is truly a piece of work—its structure is unlike anything I've read before.
Everyone agreed the new bridge was a massive piece of work for the engineers involved.
She can be such a piece of work sometimes, always finding new ways to surprise us.

Synset Examples

That sculpture is a remarkable piece of work.
This report is a real piece of work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - an outstanding work of art or skill
  • Synonym: achievement - something accomplished successfully
