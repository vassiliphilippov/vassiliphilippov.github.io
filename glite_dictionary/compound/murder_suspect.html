murder suspect

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜrdər səˌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə səˌspɛkt / (BrE)
a person who is believed to have committed murder but has not yet been proven guilty
person suspected of murder

Examples

The police arrested a murder suspect after finding new evidence.
News outlets reported that the murder suspect had fled the country.
The court will determine whether the murder suspect is guilty or innocent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspect - a person thought to be guilty of a crime or offense
  • Degree Higher: murderer - someone who has committed murder
