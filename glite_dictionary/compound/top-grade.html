top-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑp ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɒp ɡreɪd / (BrE)
Of the highest quality or standard; superior to others of its kind.
of the highest quality

Examples

Only top-grade steel is used in the construction of this bridge.
She prefers to buy top-grade coffee for her café.
