corn chip

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn tʃɪp / (AmE), / kɔːn tʃɪp / (BrE)
A thin, crisp snack made from fried cornmeal dough, usually served salted or flavored.
thin crisp snack made from fried cornmeal

Examples

She grabbed a handful of corn chips to go with her salsa.
The party guests munched on corn chips and guacamole all night.
He opened a bag of corn chips for a quick snack while watching TV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tortilla chip - a similar snack typically made from corn tortillas
