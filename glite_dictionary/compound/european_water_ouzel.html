European water ouzel

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈwɔtər ˈaʊzəl / (AmE), / jʊˈrəʊpiən ˈwɔːtə ˈaʊzəl / (BrE)
a small, aquatic songbird of European rivers and streams, also known as the white-throated dipper.
European aquatic songbird (white-throated dipper)

Examples

The European water ouzel is often seen bobbing along fast-flowing rivers in the Alps.
We spotted a European water ouzel diving for insects beneath the icy water.
The distinctive song of the European water ouzel can be heard early in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dipper - an aquatic songbird feeding in or near streams
