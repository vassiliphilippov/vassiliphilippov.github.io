sulfur mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌl.fɚ ˌmaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.fə ˌmaɪn / (BrE)
a mine where sulfur is extracted from the ground
mine for extracting sulfur

Examples

The workers at the sulfur mine had to wear special masks to avoid inhaling toxic gases.
Several towns in the region depend economically on a large sulfur mine nearby.
After years of operation, the sulfur mine was shut down due to environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mine - an excavation in the earth for extracting minerals
