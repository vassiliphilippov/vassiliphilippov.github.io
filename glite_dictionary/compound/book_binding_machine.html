book binding machine

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / bʊk ˈbaɪndɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used to fasten together the pages and cover of a book.
machine for binding books

Examples

The publishing company recently purchased a new book binding machine to increase production efficiency.
She watched as the book binding machine neatly attached the covers to the pages.
Modern book binding machines can handle hundreds of books per hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookbinder - a person who binds books as a profession
