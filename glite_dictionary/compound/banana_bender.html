banana bender

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə ˈbɛndər / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˈbɛndə / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes Australian) A lively, wild party, often involving excessive drinking; also, a quirky or eccentric person.
wild party; quirky person (slang)
informal slang british

Examples

They threw a banana bender to celebrate the end of exams.
The night quickly became a banana bender after everyone showed up with drinks.
He's always the life of the party, a real banana bender among his friends.

Synset Examples

The party turned into a banana bender as the drinks flowed.
They celebrated the win with a banana bender at the bar.
After the long hike, he was a complete banana bender.

Etymology

Australian slang, originally a jocular term for a person from Queensland (a state known for banana production).
