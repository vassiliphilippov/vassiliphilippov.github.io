Clinton News Network

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪntən nuz ˈnɛtwɝk / (AmE), / ˈklɪntən njuːz ˈnɛtwɜːk / (BrE)
A satirical nickname for the cable news network CNN, implying that it is biased in favor of Bill or Hillary Clinton or the Democratic Party.
satirical nickname for CNN, implying partiality
informal slang

Examples

Some commentators derisively refer to CNN as the Clinton News Network during election coverage.
After hearing their segment, he joked about tuning into the Clinton News Network.
The accusation that it is the Clinton News Network is often repeated in online forums.

Synset Examples

He jokingly referred to it as the Clinton News Network.
Critics often call it the Clinton News Network for bias.
Many believe the Clinton News Network has a political agenda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CNN - Cable News Network, an American television news channel
