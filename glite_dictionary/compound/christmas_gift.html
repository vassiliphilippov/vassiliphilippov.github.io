Christmas gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs ɡɪft / (BrE)
A present that is given on or around Christmas Day, typically exchanged as part of the Christmas celebration.
present given at Christmas

Examples

Every year, children eagerly await their Christmas gifts under the tree.
She wrapped each Christmas gift with colorful paper and shiny ribbons.
He received a wonderful Christmas gift from his grandparents this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas present - a present given at Christmas time
