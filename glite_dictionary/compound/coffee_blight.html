coffee blight

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi blaɪt / (BrE)
A disease caused by fungi or bacteria that affects coffee plants, leading to reduced yield and quality of coffee beans.
disease affecting coffee plants

Examples

The outbreak of coffee blight devastated plantations across Central America, threatening farmers' livelihoods.
Scientists are racing to develop coffee varieties resistant to coffee blight.
Losses caused by coffee blight have led to increased global coffee prices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blight - a plant disease or any cause of decay or destruction in plants
