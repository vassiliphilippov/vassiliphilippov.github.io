bedroom furniture

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌruːm ˈfɝːnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbɛdruːm ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
furniture designed for use in a bedroom, such as beds, dressers, and nightstands.
furniture used in a bedroom

Examples

We shopped for bedroom furniture at the new store downtown.
Her bedroom furniture includes a queen-sized bed and a matching dresser.
Modern bedroom furniture often combines style with practical storage solutions.
