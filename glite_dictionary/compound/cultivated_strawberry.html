cultivated strawberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltɪˌveɪtɪd ˈstrɔːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd ˈstrɒbəri / (BrE)
A species of strawberry (Fragaria × ananassa) that is widely grown for its edible fruit.
widely grown species of strawberry

Examples

The cultivated strawberry is commonly sold in supermarkets around the world.
Farmers prefer to grow the cultivated strawberry because of its large fruit size and high yield.
The flavor of the cultivated strawberry differs from that of many wild varieties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wild strawberry - species of strawberry found in the wild
