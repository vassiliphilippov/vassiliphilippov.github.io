libshit policy

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbʃɪt ˈpɑːləsi / (AmE), / ˈlɪbʃɪt ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
A derogatory slang term for a policy that is considered excessively progressive or politically correct.
derogatory term for politically correct policy
slang offensive

Examples

Many employees criticized the new libshit policy implemented by management.
He accused the school board of enforcing yet another libshit policy this year.
Social media debates often spiral into name-calling when someone brings up a libshit policy.

Synset Examples

The company's libshit policies led to low morale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woke policy - policy criticized as overly progressive or politically correct

Etymology

Blend of 'liberal' and vulgar 'shit', used as an insult toward liberal or progressive policies.
