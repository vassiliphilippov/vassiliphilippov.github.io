Weser River

1. PROPN

/ ˈveɪzər ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈveːzə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in northwestern Germany that flows northward to the North Sea near Bremerhaven
river in northwestern Germany

Examples

The Weser River passes through several important German cities before reaching the North Sea.
They enjoyed a scenic cruise along the Weser River during their stay in Bremen.
The Weser River is famous for its picturesque landscapes and historical towns along its banks.

Etymology

From German 'Weser', tracing back to the Latin 'Visurgis'.
