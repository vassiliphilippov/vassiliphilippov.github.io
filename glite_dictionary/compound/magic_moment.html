magic moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪk ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a special or memorable occasion, often marked by strong emotion or significance
memorable or special time

Examples

Their wedding day was truly a magic moment for the whole family.
He looked back on the championship win as a magic moment in his career.
Watching the first snowfall of the year was a magic moment for the children.

Synset Examples

The sunset created a magic moment for everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable moment - an occasion that is easily remembered, usually because it is special or significant
