man-month

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn ˌmʌnθ / (AmE), / ˈmæn ˌmʌnθ / (BrE)
A unit of measurement representing the amount of work one person can complete in one month, used to estimate the labor required for a project or task.
unit of a month's work by one person

Examples

The project was estimated to require fifty man-months of work.
We completed the software upgrade two man-months ahead of schedule.
Accurately calculating man-months is essential for project budgeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: man-hour - a unit representing one person's work in one hour
