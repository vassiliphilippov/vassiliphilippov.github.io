trans cult

1. NOUN

/ trænz kʌlt / (AmE), / tranz kʌlt / (BrE)
a group or movement, often regarded as extreme or controversial, that is centered around transgender identity and issues
controversial group focused on transgender identity
informal

Examples

Some activists have warned about the influence of a trans cult on young people online.
The book examines claims about the so-called trans cult from multiple perspectives.
Critics argue that labeling supportive communities as a trans cult is misleading and inflammatory.

Synset Examples

Some believe that the online group resembles a trans cult.
The documentary exposed the practices of a notorious trans cult.
