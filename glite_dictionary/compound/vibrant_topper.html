vibrant topper

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbrənt ˈtɑpər / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈtɒpə / (BrE)
a bright and stylish hat or accessory, especially one worn as a striking addition to an outfit
bright and stylish hat or accessory

Examples

She drew attention at the party with her vibrant topper perched stylishly on her head.
Every spring, shop windows display at least one vibrant topper as the highlight of their new collections.
His red feathered vibrant topper made his costume unforgettable.

Synset Examples

The fashionista chose a vibrant topper for her outfit.
