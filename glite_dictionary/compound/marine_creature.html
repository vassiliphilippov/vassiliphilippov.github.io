marine creature

1. NOUN

/ məˈrin ˈkritʃɚ / (AmE), / məˈriːn ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
any animal that lives in the sea, such as fish, molluscs, or marine mammals.
an animal that lives in the sea

Examples

The octopus is a fascinating marine creature known for its intelligence and camouflage skills.
Divers were amazed by the variety of marine creatures they encountered on the reef.
Pollution poses a serious threat to every marine creature in the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sea creature - an animal that lives in the sea
  • Antonym: land animal - an animal that lives primarily on land
