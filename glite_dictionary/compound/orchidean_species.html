orchidean species

1. NOUN

/ ɔrˈkidiən ˈspiʃiz / (AmE), / ɔːˈkɪdɪən ˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
A species belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).
species of the orchid family

Examples

The rare orchidean species bloomed for the first time in the greenhouse this year.
Scientists discovered several new orchidean species in the mountainous region.
Many orchidean species have intricate pollination mechanisms involving specific insects.

Synset Examples

He admired the orchidean species at the botanical garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a plant of the orchid family, typically with showy flowers

Etymology

From 'orchidean', formed from Latin 'orchis' (orchid) and the suffix '-ean', meaning 'relating to'.
