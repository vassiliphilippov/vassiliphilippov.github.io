Book of Habakkuk

1. PROPN

/ bʊk əv həˈbækək / (AmE), / bʊk əv həˈbækək / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament containing the prophecies attributed to the prophet Habakkuk
Old Testament book of prophecies

Examples

Many scholars have analyzed the themes found in the Book of Habakkuk.
The Book of Habakkuk addresses the problem of injustice in the world.
She referenced the Book of Habakkuk during her lecture on biblical prophecy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Habakkuk - a Hebrew prophet of the Old Testament

Etymology

Habakkuk is a Hebrew name; the book is named after the prophet whose prophecies it records.
