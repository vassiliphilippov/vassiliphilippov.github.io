touch for

1. NOUN

/ tʌtʃ fɔr / (AmE), / tʌtʃ fə / (BrE)
a natural skill or special ability for something
natural talent for something

Examples

Her touch for languages impressed everyone at the interview.
He has a touch for making people feel comfortable.
The chef's touch for seasoning sets her dishes apart.

Synset Examples
