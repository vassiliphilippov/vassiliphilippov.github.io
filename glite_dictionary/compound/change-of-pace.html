change-of-pace

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv peɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv peɪs / (BrE)
A slow pitch in baseball thrown to trick the batter, especially when a fastball is expected.
slow baseball pitch to deceive batter

Examples

The pitcher used a change-of-pace to throw off the experienced batter.
Her unexpected change-of-pace landed perfectly in the strike zone.
He mixes in a change-of-pace after several fastballs to keep hitters guessing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fastball - a pitched baseball thrown at high speed
