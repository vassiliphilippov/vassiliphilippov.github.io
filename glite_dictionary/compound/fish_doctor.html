fish doctor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃ ˌdɑktɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌdɒktə / (BrE)
A brightly colored, scaleless Arctic eelpout (Lycodes reticulatus), found in cold northern waters.
a brightly colored Arctic eelpout

Examples

The fish doctor is known for its brilliant colors and unique appearance.
Researchers studied the habitat of the fish doctor in Arctic waters.
Anglers rarely encounter a fish doctor outside its icy native range.
