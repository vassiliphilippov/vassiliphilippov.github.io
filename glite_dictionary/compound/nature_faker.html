nature faker

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪtʃər ˈfeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈneɪtʃə ˈfeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who falsely claims to appreciate or love nature
someone who pretends to love nature

Examples

Some influencers are criticized as nature fakers because they only take staged photos outdoors.
Don't be a nature faker; try truly connecting with the environment instead.
He was exposed as a nature faker when he admitted he hated hiking.

Synset Examples

Don’t be a nature faker; get outside and explore.
She’s a nature faker, only pretending to love camping.
He’s such a nature faker; he never hikes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poser - a person who pretends to be something they are not
