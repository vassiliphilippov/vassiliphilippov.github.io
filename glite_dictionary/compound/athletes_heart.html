athlete's heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈæθliːts hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈæθliːts hɑːt / (BrE)
an enlarged heart, especially the left ventricle, commonly found among athletes trained for endurance due to prolonged and intensive physical activity
enlarged heart in endurance athletes
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that athlete's heart is a normal adaptation to intense physical training.
A routine checkup revealed signs of athlete's heart in several members of the cycling team.
She was relieved to learn her enlarged heart was just athlete's heart and not a disease.
