B-flat clarinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈbi flæt klæˈrɪnɛt / (AmE), / ˈbiː flæt klæˈrɪnɪt / (BrE)
a clarinet pitched in B-flat, commonly used as the standard form of the instrument in bands and orchestras
clarinet pitched in B-flat

Examples

The orchestra featured a beautiful solo performed on the B-flat clarinet.
Sarah switched from the alto saxophone to the B-flat clarinet in high school band.
Many beginner method books are written specifically for the B-flat clarinet variety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarinet - a woodwind instrument with a single-reed mouthpiece
