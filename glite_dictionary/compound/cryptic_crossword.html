cryptic crossword

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪptɪk ˈkrɔsˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪptɪk ˈkrɒsˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A type of crossword puzzle in which each clue is itself a word puzzle, often involving wordplay, puns, or hidden meanings.
crossword using word puzzles as clues

Examples

He enjoys solving cryptic crosswords every morning with his coffee.
The newspaper features a challenging cryptic crossword in its weekend edition.
Many people find the clues in a cryptic crossword much trickier than those in regular puzzles.

Synset Examples

Many Canadian newspapers, including the Ottawa Citizen, Toronto Star and the Globe and Mail, carry cryptic crosswords.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crossword - a puzzle in which words are filled into a grid based on clues
