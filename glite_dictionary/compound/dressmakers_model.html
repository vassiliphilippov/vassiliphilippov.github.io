dressmaker's model

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkərz ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsˌmeɪkəz ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a person employed to display dresses, especially for fittings or fashion shows
person who models dresses for fittings or shows

Examples

The dressmaker's model stood patiently as the designer adjusted the hem of the gown.
As a dressmaker's model, she often spent hours being fitted for new collections.
The young woman dreamed of becoming a famous dressmaker's model in Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person who wears and displays clothes for fashion purposes
