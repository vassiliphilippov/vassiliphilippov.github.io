senior hurling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniɚ ˈhɝlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə ˈhɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the highest level of competitive hurling, typically for adult players rather than juniors or minors
top-level adult hurling competition

Examples

He played senior hurling for his county for over a decade.
The club won their first senior hurling title in 20 years.
Attending senior hurling matches is a popular tradition in Ireland.

Synset Examples

The seniors won the championship in senior hurling last year.
He played senior hurling for the local club every weekend.
She loved attending senior hurling matches to cheer on her team.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: junior hurling - a lower level of hurling competition for younger or less experienced players
