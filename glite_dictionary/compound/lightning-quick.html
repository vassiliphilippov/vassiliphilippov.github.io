lightning-quick

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪtnɪŋ ˌkwɪk / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ ˌkwɪk / (BrE)
Extremely fast or immediate; happening with the speed of lightning.
extremely fast; very rapid

Examples

His lightning-quick response prevented a major accident.
The player is known for her lightning-quick footwork on the court.
The chef's lightning-quick hands chopped the vegetables in seconds.

Synset Examples

He made a lightning-quick decision to change careers.
Her lightning-quick reflexes saved her from falling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instantaneous - Occurring or done instantly, without any delay
  • Antonym: slow - Taking a long time; not fast

Etymology

Formed by combining 'lightning' (fast natural phenomenon) and 'quick'.
