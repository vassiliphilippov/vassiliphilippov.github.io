double crochet

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌbəl kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a crochet stitch made by making one yarn over, inserting the hook into the stitch, drawing the yarn through, pulling through two loops twice to complete the stitch
a type of crochet stitch

Examples

She learned how to make a double crochet after mastering the basic chain stitch.
The scarf pattern calls for alternating rows of single and double crochet.
He struggled to remember the steps for a proper double crochet stitch.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: treble crochet - a different crochet stitch, known as 'double crochet' in US terminology
