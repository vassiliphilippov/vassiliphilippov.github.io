fifty dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪfti ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɪfti ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A United States banknote with a face value of fifty dollars.
U.S. $50 banknote
american

Examples

She handed the cashier a fifty dollar bill to pay for her groceries.
The ATM only dispenses fifty dollar bills and twenty dollar bills.
He was surprised to find a fifty dollar bill in the pocket of his old jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banknote - a piece of paper money issued by a central bank
  • Degree Higher: hundred dollar bill - a United States bill worth 100 dollars
  • Degree Lower: twenty dollar bill - a United States bill worth 20 dollars
