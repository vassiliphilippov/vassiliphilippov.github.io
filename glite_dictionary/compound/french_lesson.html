French lesson

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈlɛsən / (AmE), / frɛnʃ ˈlɛsən / (BrE)
an occasion or period of instruction in the French language
instruction in the French language

Examples

She attends a French lesson every Tuesday evening at the community center.
During the French lesson, students practiced introducing themselves and asking for directions.
My favorite part of the French lesson is learning new vocabulary through games.
