piano music

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / piˈanəʊ ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music that is played on a piano.
music played on a piano

Examples

She drifted off to sleep while listening to soft piano music in the background.
The restaurant hired a pianist to provide live piano music on Friday evenings.
Hearing piano music coming from the open window, she paused to listen.

Examples
