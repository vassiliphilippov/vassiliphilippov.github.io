African green monkey

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfrɪkən ˌɡrin ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˌæfrɪkən ˌɡriːn ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
a common savanna-dwelling monkey of Africa with a greenish-grey back and a yellow tail
African savanna monkey with green back and yellow tail

Examples

The African green monkey is often seen near rivers on the savanna.
Scientists have studied the behavior of the African green monkey in its natural habitat.
The African green monkey has fur that appears greenish because of its yellow and black hairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vervet - another name for the African green monkey
