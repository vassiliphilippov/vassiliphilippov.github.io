plume thistle

1. NOUN

/ plum ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / pljuːm ˈθɪsəl / (BrE)
Any of various biennial or perennial thistles with attractive purple, yellow, or occasionally white flower heads.
a group of thistle plants with showy flower heads

Examples

The plume thistle is popular in wildflower gardens for its striking purple blooms.
Many insects are attracted to the plume thistle due to its abundant nectar.
While hiking, we spotted a plume thistle growing by the path, its flowers bright against the green grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thistle - a prickly plant of the family Asteraceae
