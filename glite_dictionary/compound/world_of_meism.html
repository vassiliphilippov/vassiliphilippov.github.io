world of meism

1. NOUN

/ wɝld əv ˈmiːɪzəm / (AmE), / wɜːld əv ˈmiːɪzəm / (BrE)
A society or environment characterized by a strong emphasis on individualism and self-interest, often at the expense of community values.
society focused on individualism and self-interest

Examples

In today's world of meism, building meaningful connections is more important than ever.
Her book critiques the values promoted by a world of meism and self-promotion.
Living in a world of meism can make people feel isolated despite being constantly connected.

Synset Examples

In a world of meism, teamwork is essential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: individualism - the pursuit of individual rather than collective interests

Etymology

Formed from 'me' (referring to oneself) and the suffix '-ism' (denoting a belief system or practice).
