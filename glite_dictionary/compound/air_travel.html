air travel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌtrævəl / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌtrævəl / (BrE)
the act or process of traveling by aircraft, especially as a form of transportation for people or goods
travel by aircraft

Examples

Air travel has become much more affordable over the last decade.
Concerns about the environmental impact of air travel have led to increased interest in alternative transportation.
She prefers air travel over long road trips when visiting her relatives abroad.
