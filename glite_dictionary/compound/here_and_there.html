here and there

1. ADV

/ hɪr ənd ðɛr / (AmE), / hɪə ənd ðeə / (BrE)
in various places; from one place to another without a fixed path or pattern.
in various places

Examples

Books were scattered here and there across the floor.
We wandered here and there looking for a place to eat.
His belongings were packed here and there in different boxes.

Synset Examples

he worked here and there but never for long in one town
we drove here and there in the darkness

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: everywhere - in all places
  • Degree Lower: somewhere - in or at an unspecified place
