working group

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrkɪŋ ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people assigned to work together temporarily to achieve a specific goal or complete a particular task
temporary team for a specific task

Examples

The company formed a working group to improve its sustainability practices.
After months of meetings, the working group finally presented its recommendations.
Several departments sent representatives to join the working group on digital transformation.

Synset Examples

the working group was supposed to report back in two weeks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
