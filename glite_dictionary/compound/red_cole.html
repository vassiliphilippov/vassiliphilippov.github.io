red cole

1. NOUN

/ rɛd koʊl / (AmE), / rɛd kəʊl / (BrE)
a coarse Eurasian plant cultivated for its thick, white, pungent root, commonly known as horseradish
coarse Eurasian plant with pungent root

Examples

Freshly grated red cole adds a spicy kick to roasted meats.
She grew red cole in her garden to make homemade condiments.
The pungent aroma of red cole filled the kitchen as he prepared the sauce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseradish - a plant with a pungent root used as a condiment
