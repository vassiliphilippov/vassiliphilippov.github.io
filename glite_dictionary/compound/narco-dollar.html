narco-dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑrkoʊ ˌdɑlər / (AmE), / ˈnɑːkəʊ ˌdɒlə / (BrE)
a US dollar obtained from the illegal drug trade
money from narcotics trafficking

Examples

Authorities seized millions in narco-dollars during the raid on the cartel's headquarters.
The flow of narco-dollars has profound effects on the local economy.
Money laundering schemes are often used to legitimize narco-dollars in the financial system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug money - money obtained from selling illegal drugs

Etymology

Blend of 'narco' (relating to narcotics) and 'dollar'.
