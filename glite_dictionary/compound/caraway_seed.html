caraway seed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærəˌweɪ sid / (AmE), / ˈkarəweɪ siːd / (BrE)
The aromatic seed of the caraway plant, used as a seasoning in cooking and baking.
aromatic spice seed used in cooking

Examples

The recipe called for a teaspoon of caraway seed to add flavor to the bread.
Traditional rye bread often contains caraway seed for its distinctive taste.
She sprinkled some caraway seed over the roasted vegetables before serving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caraway - the caraway plant, from which the seeds come
