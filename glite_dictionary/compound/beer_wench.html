beer wench

1. NOUN

/ bɪr wɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɪə wɛntʃ / (BrE)
a woman who serves beer in a tavern or pub, often in a traditional or costume setting
female server in a tavern or pub
informal

Examples

The beer wench carried a tray piled high with frothy mugs for the patrons.
During Oktoberfest, many women dress up as a beer wench to serve drinks.
He laughed as the beer wench balanced five pints at once.

Synset Examples

Dressed as a beer wench, she served everyone with a smile.
He joked that the beer wench would take our orders all night.
The beer wench brought us another round of drinks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barmaid - a female bartender or server in a bar or pub
