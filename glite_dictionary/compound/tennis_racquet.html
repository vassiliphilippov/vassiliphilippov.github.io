tennis racquet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs ˌrækət / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌrækɪt / (BrE)
a light, handled racket with an open hoop across which a network of strings is tightly stretched, used to hit the ball in tennis
racket used in tennis

Examples

She bought a new tennis racquet before the tournament started.
His tennis racquet has a larger head size for better control.
Every professional player chooses their tennis racquet very carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racket - a sports implement used to hit a ball or shuttlecock
