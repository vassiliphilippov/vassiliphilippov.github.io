groundwater pump

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡraʊndˌwɔtɚ pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtə pʌmp / (BrE)
a device designed to extract or sample water from underground sources
device for extracting underground water

Examples

The technician used a groundwater pump to collect samples for testing.
Installing a reliable groundwater pump is crucial for accessing clean water in rural areas.
They replaced the old groundwater pump to improve the efficiency of the well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water pump - a device for moving or raising water
