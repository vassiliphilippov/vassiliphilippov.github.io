vaultingly ambitious

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɔltɪŋli æmˈbɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˈvɔːltɪŋli æmˈbɪʃəs / (BrE)
Having an extremely high or excessive level of ambition.
excessively ambitious

Examples

The young CEO had vaultingly ambitious plans to transform the entire industry within five years.
Critics described the project as vaultingly ambitious, questioning whether it could ever be completed.
Her vaultingly ambitious goals propelled her to the top of her field.

Synset Examples

His vaultingly ambitious plans surprised everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ambitious - having a strong desire to succeed or achieve

Etymology

From 'vaulting' (leaping, striving ambitiously) + '-ly' + 'ambitious'.
