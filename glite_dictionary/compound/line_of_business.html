line of business

1. NOUN

/ laɪn əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a particular variety or range of products or merchandise offered by a business
range of products or merchandise

Examples

The company has introduced a new line of business featuring sustainable materials.
Shoes are their most successful line of business this season.
We're expanding our line of business to include electronics.

Synset Examples

a nice line of shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: product line - a related group of products sold by the same company

2. NOUN

/ laɪn əv ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / laɪn əv ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
a particular kind or area of commercial or professional activity
specific type of business activity

Examples

She works in the pharmaceutical line of business and attends medical conferences regularly.
Each line of business within the company has its own management structure and goals.
After years in retail, he decided to enter a new line of business focused on technology solutions.
