blue joke

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈdʒoʊk / (AmE), / bluː ˈdʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that is indecent or risqué, often referring to sexual matters
indecent or risqué joke

Examples

He made everyone uncomfortable by telling a blue joke at the dinner table.
The comedian was known for slipping a blue joke into every set.
Some audiences prefer clean humor, while others don't mind a blue joke now and then.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty joke - a joke that deals with sexual content or is considered indecent

Etymology

Likely from 'blue' meaning indecent or obscene, possibly from the phrase 'blue laws', meaning laws prohibiting immorality.
