euthanasia debate

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuθəˈneɪʒə dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˌjuːθəˈneɪzi.ə dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
a public discussion or controversy surrounding the ethics and legality of assisted dying or euthanasia
public discussion about assisted dying

Examples

The euthanasia debate often centers on questions of personal autonomy and dignity.
Recent legislation reignited the national euthanasia debate in parliament.
Medical professionals play a key role in the euthanasia debate worldwide.

Synset Examples

The euthanasia debate continues to stir emotions globally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assisted suicide debate - public discourse on assisted suicide
  • Synonym: abortion debate - public discussion on the ethics of abortion

Etymology

‘Euthanasia’ from Greek ‘eu’ (good) + ‘thanatos’ (death); ‘debate’ from Old French ‘debatre’.
