order Heterotrichales

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛt.ə.roʊˌtrɪk.eɪlz / (AmE), / ˈhɛt.ərəʊˌtrɪk.eɪlz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of yellow-green algae with simple or branching filaments, comprising the single family Tribonemaceae.
order of yellow-green algae

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species within the order Heterotrichales in a freshwater lake.
Members of the order Heterotrichales are studied for their unique filament structures.
Algae in the order Heterotrichales are important to aquatic ecosystems due to their photosynthetic role.

Etymology

From New Latin, combining 'hetero-' (different) and 'trich-' (hair) with the taxonomic suffix '-ales'.
