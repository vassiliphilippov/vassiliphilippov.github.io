flying coffin

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkɑfɪn / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkɒfɪn / (BrE)
an unsafe vehicle or aircraft, especially one perceived as dangerously likely to crash
unsafe, dangerous vehicle or aircraft
informal

Examples

The old bomber was nicknamed a flying coffin by its nervous crew.
No one wanted to ride in that flying coffin after the last accident.
Many vintage airplanes earn the reputation of being a flying coffin due to their poor safety records.

Synset Examples

That old car is a flying coffin; it's so unsafe.
The pilot referred to his plane as a flying coffin.
Many consider those outdated models to be flying coffins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: death trap - a vehicle or place considered extremely dangerous
