pearl diver

1. NOUN

/ pɜrl ˈdaɪvər / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈdaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who dives underwater to search for oysters or other mollusks in order to collect pearls
person who dives for pearls

Examples

The pearl diver carefully scanned the seabed for oysters containing valuable gems.
For centuries, the island was home to skilled pearl divers who supplied the world with precious pearls.
A pearl diver must have excellent breath control and a keen eye for hidden treasures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diver - a person who swims or works underwater, often using special breathing equipment
