Mexican flameleaf

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛksɪkən ˈfleɪmˌliːf / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈfleɪmˌliːf / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with poisonous sap and bright scarlet, leaf-like bracts surrounding small yellow flowers.
tropical plant with scarlet leaf-like bracts

Examples

The garden was ablaze with the brilliant red of the Mexican flameleaf.
Caution is advised, as the sap of the Mexican flameleaf can be toxic if handled carelessly.
The Mexican flameleaf attracts butterflies with its colorful bracts.
