wire service

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪɚ ˌsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə ˌsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
a news agency that gathers and distributes news reports to newspapers, broadcasters, and other media outlets, typically electronically
news agency distributing reports electronically

Examples

The story was distributed nationwide by a wire service.
Many local papers rely on wire services for international news coverage.
She works as a reporter for a major wire service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news agency - organization that collects and supplies news reports

Etymology

From 'wire' (referring to telegraph wires) and 'service' (agency or system providing news). The term originated in the era of telegraphic communication.
