crib lizard

1. NOUN

/ krɪb ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / krɪb ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
A person who prefers to stay at home rather than go out socially.
person who prefers staying at home
informal slang american

Examples

I've always been a bit of a crib lizard, preferring books and movies to parties.
He called himself a crib lizard and happily spent weekends in pajamas at home.
Even when invited out, she chooses to be a crib lizard and relax on her couch.

Synset Examples

Being a crib lizard suits him just fine.
As a crib lizard, she prefers Netflix over parties.
He's such a crib lizard, always hanging out at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homebody - person who likes staying at home
  • Synonym: couch potato - person who spends a lot of time sitting at home, especially watching TV

Etymology

‘Crib’ is slang for ‘home’ (American English). ‘Lizard’ used humorously to evoke an image of lounging around.
