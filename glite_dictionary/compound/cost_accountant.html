cost accountant

1. NOUN

/ kɑst əˈkaʊn.tənt / (AmE), / kɒst əˈkaʊn.tənt / (BrE)
a person who specializes in recording, analyzing, and managing the costs related to the production of goods or services
a specialist in cost analysis for production

Examples

The cost accountant prepared a detailed report on the factory's monthly expenses.
As a cost accountant, she implemented new systems to reduce manufacturing costs.
The company hired an experienced cost accountant to improve their budgeting processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accountant - a person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts
