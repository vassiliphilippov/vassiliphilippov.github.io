shaded gazebo

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪdɪd ɡəˈzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪdɪd ɡəˈzəʊ / (BrE)
a roofed outdoor structure, typically found in gardens or parks, designed to provide shade and shelter
roofed outdoor structure for shade

Examples

The children enjoyed a picnic beneath the shaded gazebo on a hot afternoon.
We reserved the shaded gazebo for our family reunion in the park.
After jogging, she rested in the shaded gazebo to cool off.

Synset Examples

The kids played under the shaded gazebo in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gazebo - a freestanding, roofed outdoor structure, often in a garden or park
