yellow cleavers

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈkliːvərz / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈkliːvəz / (BrE)
a common perennial plant with yellow flowers, found in North America, Europe, and Asia, belonging to the bedstraw family
a yellow-flowered perennial bedstraw plant

Examples

The field was covered in bright yellow cleavers during the peak of summer.
Gardeners often appreciate yellow cleavers for their resilience and attractive yellow blossoms.
In some regions, yellow cleavers are valued for their use in traditional herbal remedies.
