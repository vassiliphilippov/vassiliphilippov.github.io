South Sea Islands

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθ ˈsi ˈaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθ ˈsiː ˈaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
any of the islands in the southern or southwestern parts of the central Pacific Ocean, especially those of Polynesia, Melanesia, and Micronesia.
islands in the southern Pacific Ocean

Examples

Many explorers set sail in search of the South Sea Islands during the Age of Discovery.
The climate of the South Sea Islands is tropical and ideal for beach vacations.
Stories about the South Sea Islands often feature lush landscapes and exotic wildlife.
