boeuf Bourguignonne

1. NOUN

/ bœf bʊrɡiˈnjɔn / (AmE), / bœf bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
A French dish of beef stewed with mushrooms, onions, and red wine, often flavored with various seasonings.
French beef stew with red wine

Examples

The chef recommended the boeuf Bourguignonne for its rich flavors and tender beef.
We enjoyed a hearty serving of boeuf Bourguignonne during our trip to Paris.
She decided to make boeuf Bourguignonne for the dinner party, impressing all her guests.

Etymology

From French 'boeuf Bourguignonne,' meaning 'beef in the style of Burgundy.'
