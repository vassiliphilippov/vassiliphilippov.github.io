behind its time

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈhaɪnd ɪts taɪm / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ɪts taɪm / (BrE)
outdated or not current; no longer in keeping with modern standards or trends
outdated; not current

Examples

Many of the museum's exhibits are behind its time and need to be updated.
The school's teaching methods are considered behind its time by many educators.
Some critics said the novel was behind its time and failed to address current issues.

Synset Examples

The technology feels behind its time now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdated - no longer modern or useful
  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
