troglodyte lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑːɡləˌdaɪt ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtrɒɡləˌdaɪt ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living that is extremely simple and primitive, often reminiscent of early humans living in caves.
simple, primitive way of living

Examples

Some people are attracted to a troglodyte lifestyle in order to escape the pressures of modern society.
After moving to the remote mountain cabin, she embraced a troglodyte lifestyle with no electricity or running water.
The novel explores the challenges of adopting a troglodyte lifestyle in the heart of the wilderness.

Synset Examples

The troglodyte lifestyle appealed to those seeking simplicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primitive lifestyle - a way of living without modern comforts or technology

Etymology

From Greek 'trōglodutēs' (cave-dweller) and English 'lifestyle'.
