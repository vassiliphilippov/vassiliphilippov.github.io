horny layer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrni ˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːni ˌleɪə / (BrE)
the outermost layer of the epidermis, made up of dead cells that are gradually shed
outermost layer of epidermis, dead cells
medical

Examples

The horny layer acts as a barrier to protect underlying tissues from infection and dehydration.
As we age, the horny layer can become thicker or thinner depending on skin health.
Daily exfoliation helps remove dead cells from the horny layer of the skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stratum corneum - outermost layer of the epidermis
