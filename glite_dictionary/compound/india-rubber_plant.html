India-rubber plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiə ˌrʌbər plænt / (AmE), / ˈɪndiə ˌrʌbə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A large tropical Asian tree, often grown indoors as a houseplant, that is a source of Assam rubber.
tropical tree grown as a houseplant

Examples

The India-rubber plant is a popular choice for people looking to add greenery to their homes.
In its native habitat, the India-rubber plant can grow into a large tree.
She placed her new India-rubber plant near the window where it would get enough sunlight.
