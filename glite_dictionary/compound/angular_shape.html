angular shape

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋɡjələr ʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡjʊlə ʃeɪp / (BrE)
a shape that has one or more sharp angles
shape with sharp angles

Examples

The modern building was notable for its angular shape and glass walls.
Artists often contrast a soft background with an angular shape in the foreground.
The sculpture's angular shape gave it a striking and bold presence.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rounded shape - a shape with smooth, curved edges or corners
