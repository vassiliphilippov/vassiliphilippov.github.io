hearing-impaired

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪrɪŋ ɪmˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˈhɪə.rɪŋ ɪmˈpeəd / (BrE)
having reduced or limited ability to hear; experiencing partial or total hearing loss.
having reduced or no hearing

Examples

Special devices can help hearing-impaired students participate more fully in class discussions.
He works as an advocate for hearing-impaired people across the country.
The hotel offers services for guests who are hearing-impaired.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: deaf - unable to hear at all or almost at all
  • Degree Lower: hard of hearing - having some but limited ability to hear
