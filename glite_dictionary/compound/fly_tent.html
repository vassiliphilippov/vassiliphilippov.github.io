fly tent

1. NOUN

/ flaɪ tɛnt / (AmE), / flaɪ tɛnt / (BrE)
A tent that has an outer covering (fly) or a front extension providing extra protection from weather.
tent with a fly front or outer covering

Examples

We pitched our fly tent by the river to have extra cover from the rain.
The campers appreciated the shade their fly tent provided during the hot afternoon.
A fly tent is ideal for areas with unpredictable weather, offering added protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flysheet - a piece of material forming an outer covering for a tent

Etymology

From 'fly' (outer covering of a tent) + 'tent'.
