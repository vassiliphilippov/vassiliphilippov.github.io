coefficient of concordance

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈɪfɪʃənt əv kənˈkɔrdəns / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪfɪʃənt ɒv kənˈkɔːdəns / (BrE)
a statistical measure that quantifies the agreement between different sets of rankings of the same set of items
statistical measure of ranking agreement

Examples

The coefficient of concordance was calculated to assess the agreement among the judges’ rankings.
A high coefficient of concordance suggests strong consensus among the different raters.
Researchers used the coefficient of concordance to evaluate consistency in peer review scores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rank correlation coefficient - a measure of the degree of association between two rankings

Etymology

From 'coefficient', meaning a multiplying factor, and Latin 'concordare' (to agree, to be harmonious).
