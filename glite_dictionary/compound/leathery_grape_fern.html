leathery grape fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛð.ə.ri ɡreɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈlɛð.əri ɡreɪp fəːn / (BrE)
A European fern with leathery, sparsely hairy fronds, belonging to the Botrychium genus.
European fern with leathery fronds

Examples

We spotted a leathery grape fern growing along the shaded woodland path.
The leathery grape fern is known for its tough, evergreen fronds.
Botanists are studying the distribution of the leathery grape fern in various European habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grape fern - a group of small ferns in the genus Botrychium
