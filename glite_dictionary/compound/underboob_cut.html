underboob cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌbuːb kʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəbʊːb kʌt / (BrE)
a style of clothing or garment designed to reveal the lower part of the breasts
clothing design showing lower breasts
informal

Examples

The model's dress had an underboob cut that caught everyone's attention on the runway.
Some swimwear brands are now featuring daring underboob cut designs for the summer collection.
She wasn't sure if her new shirt's underboob cut was too revealing for the party.

Synset Examples

Her outfit featured an edgy underboob cut.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: crop top - a short top, often exposing the midriff

Etymology

From 'underboob' (under + boob, slang for breast) and 'cut' (style or shape in clothing).
