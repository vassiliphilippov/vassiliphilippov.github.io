Kingdom of Cambodia

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv kæmˈboʊdiə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv kæmˈbəʊdiə / (BrE)
The official name for the country of Cambodia, a nation in Southeast Asia formerly part of French Indochina.
the official name of Cambodia, country in Southeast Asia

Examples

The government of the Kingdom of Cambodia has invested in improving infrastructure.
After traveling through Vietnam, they crossed the border into the Kingdom of Cambodia.
The official language of the Kingdom of Cambodia is Khmer.
