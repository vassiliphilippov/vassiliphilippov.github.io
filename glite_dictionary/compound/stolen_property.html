stolen property

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊlən ˈprɑːpɚti / (AmE), / ˈstəʊlən ˈprɒpəti / (BrE)
goods or belongings that have been taken without the owner's permission; things that have been stolen
goods taken without permission

Examples

The police recovered the stolen property after an extensive investigation.
Anyone found in possession of stolen property can be arrested and charged.
He was accused of buying stolen property at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraband - goods that are illegally imported or exported
