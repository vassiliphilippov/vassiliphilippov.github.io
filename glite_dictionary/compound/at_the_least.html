at the least

1. ADV

/ ət ðə list / (AmE), / ət ðə liːst / (BrE)
No less than; used to indicate the minimum amount, degree, or number possible.
not less than; minimum amount

Examples

You will need to wait at the least two weeks for your application to be processed.
She earns $40,000 a year, at the least.
He should have apologized, at the least, for his mistake.

Synset Examples

at least two hours studying the manual
a tumor at least as big as an orange

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at least - no less than; minimum amount
