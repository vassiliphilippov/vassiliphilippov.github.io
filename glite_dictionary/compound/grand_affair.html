grand affair

1. NOUN

/ ɡrænd əˈfɛr / (AmE), / ɡrænd əˈfeə / (BrE)
an important or elaborate event, often marked by ceremony or celebration
important or elaborate event

Examples

Their wedding was a grand affair, attended by hundreds of guests.
The launch of the new product turned out to be quite a grand affair.
In that town, the annual parade is always a grand affair with music and fireworks.

Synset Examples

The anointment of the new CEO was a grand affair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: event - a planned occasion or occurrence
  • Synonym: gala - a social occasion with special entertainments or performances
  • Antonym: non-event - an event that is less significant than expected
