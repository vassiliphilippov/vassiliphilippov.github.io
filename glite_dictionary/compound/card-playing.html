card-playing

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑrd ˌpleɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːd ˌpleɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Devoted to or frequently engaged in playing cards, especially as a form of pleasure or gambling.
fond of playing cards, esp. gambling

Examples

His father was a notorious card-playing gambler who often stayed out all night.
She disapproved of her brother's card-playing friends and their late-night poker sessions.
The novel tells the story of a card-playing aristocrat who loses his fortune.
