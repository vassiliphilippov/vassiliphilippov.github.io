deep cut

1. NOUN

/ dip kʌt / (AmE), / diːp kʌt / (BrE)
a song by an artist or on an album that is not widely known or released as a single, often appreciated by dedicated fans
lesser-known song by an artist or on an album

Examples

That concert included a deep cut that true fans immediately recognized.
If you want to impress them, request a deep cut instead of just the big hits.
Collectors often enjoy exploring the deep cuts in an artist’s discography.

Synset Examples

This album has some real deep cuts worth listening to.
Fans appreciate the deep cuts that showcase their talent.
They played a deep cut from the band’s early days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hidden gem - a song or work that is particularly good but not widely known
