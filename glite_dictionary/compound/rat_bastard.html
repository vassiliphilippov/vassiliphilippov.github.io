rat bastard

1. NOUN

/ ræt ˈbæstərd / (AmE), / ræt ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
(offensive, vulgar) An insulting term for a person considered to be contemptible or despicable.
contemptible or despicable person (insult)
offensive vulgar

Examples

After stealing from his own friend, everyone started calling him a rat bastard.
She glared at him, calling him a rat bastard for lying to her.
The coach was furious, yelling that only a rat bastard would cheat during the game.

Synset Examples

He called him a rat bastard for betraying the team.
She couldn't believe he was such a rat bastard.
Everyone knew he was a rat bastard after the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - insulting term for a despicable or unpleasant person
  • Synonym: rat - a person regarded as a traitor or informant
