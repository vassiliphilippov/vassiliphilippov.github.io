credit hour

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌaʊə / (BrE)
A unit of academic credit representing one hour of classroom instruction per week over a semester.
unit of academic credit

Examples

Each math class is worth three credit hours toward graduation.
To be a full-time student, you must enroll in at least twelve credit hours per semester.
She transferred her credit hours from her previous university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: course credit - unit used to measure educational coursework, often equivalent to a credit hour
