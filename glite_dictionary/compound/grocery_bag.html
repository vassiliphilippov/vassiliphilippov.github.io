grocery bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊsəri bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag, typically made of paper or plastic, used by customers to carry groceries from a store.
bag for carrying groceries

Examples

She packed all the vegetables into a large grocery bag before leaving the supermarket.
After the store banned plastic, customers had to bring their own grocery bags.
The paper grocery bag ripped because it was too heavy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping bag - a bag for carrying purchased goods
  • Synonym: paper bag - a bag made of paper, often used to carry groceries
