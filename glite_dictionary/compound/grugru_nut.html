grugru nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruːɡruː nʌt / (AmE), / ˈɡruːɡruː nʌt / (BrE)
the edible nut of certain Brazilian or West Indian palm trees
nut from West Indian or Brazilian palms

Examples

The grugru nut is often gathered and eaten by locals in the Caribbean.
She tasted a grugru nut for the first time during her trip to Brazil.
After the harvest, they sold bags of grugru nuts at the market.
