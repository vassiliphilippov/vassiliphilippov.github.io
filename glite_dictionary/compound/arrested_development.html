arrested development

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɛstɪd dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛnt / (AmE), / əˈrɛstɪd dɪˌvɛləpˈmənt / (BrE)
a condition in which normal physical or mental development stops or is halted before completion
premature halt in normal development
medical

Examples

The child suffered from arrested development due to a lack of essential nutrients in early childhood.
Some psychological disorders are characterized by arrested development of emotional maturity.
Medical experts studied the causes of arrested development in patients exposed to environmental toxins.
