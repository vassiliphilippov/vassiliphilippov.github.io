elf cup

1. NOUN

/ ɛlf kʌp / (AmE), / ɛlf kʌp / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored cup-shaped fungus of the family Pezizaceae
a cup-shaped fungus

Examples

We discovered a cluster of elf cups growing on the damp forest floor.
The distinctive red color of the elf cup makes it easy to spot among the leaves.
Photographers often seek out the elf cup because of its unusual appearance and bright hue.
