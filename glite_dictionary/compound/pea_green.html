pea green

1. NOUN

/ pi ɡrin / (AmE), / piː ɡriːn / (BrE)
a shade of green with a yellowish tinge, similar to the color of fresh peas
yellowish-green color

Examples

She painted the kitchen walls a lovely pea green to give the room a fresh look.
The decorator suggested adding accents in pea green for a natural touch.
His favorite sweater is a soft wool in pea green.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: olive green - a dark yellowish-green color
  • Degree Higher: lime green - a vivid yellowish-green color, brighter than pea green

Etymology

Named after the typical color of fresh green peas.
