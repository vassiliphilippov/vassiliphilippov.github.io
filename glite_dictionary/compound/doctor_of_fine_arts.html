Doctor of Fine Arts

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktɚ əv faɪn ˈɑrts / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv faɪn ˈɑːts / (BrE)
an honorary degree awarded for achievements in the fine arts
honorary degree in fine arts

Examples

She was awarded a Doctor of Fine Arts by the university for her contributions to sculpture.
The artist received a Doctor of Fine Arts at the commencement ceremony last spring.
Some notable actors and musicians have been honored with a Doctor of Fine Arts degree for their creative work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Doctor of Letters - an honorary degree in literature or related fields
