catchy hook

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃi hʊk / (AmE), / ˈkætʃi hʊk / (BrE)
a short, memorable musical phrase or refrain that is easily remembered and often repeated in a song
memorable musical phrase in a song

Examples

The song's catchy hook made it a summer hit that everyone sang along to.
That band is known for creating a catchy hook in almost every single they release.
After hearing the catchy hook once, it was stuck in my head all day.

Synset Examples

She loves the catchy hooks of corporation pop songs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hook - a musical phrase designed to catch the listener's attention
