squeeze box

1. NOUN

/ skwiz bɑks / (AmE), / skwiːz bɒks / (BrE)
a portable, box-shaped musical instrument with bellows and free reeds, such as an accordion or concertina, that produces sound when air is pushed or pulled through the reeds
portable bellows-driven musical instrument

Examples

He played an old folk tune on his squeeze box, filling the room with lively music.
She bought a vintage squeeze box at the flea market for her collection of musical instruments.
The village band featured a talented musician on the squeeze box during the festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accordion - a portable musical instrument with a keyboard, bellows, and reeds
  • Synonym: concertina - a small, hexagonal, bellows-driven free-reed instrument
