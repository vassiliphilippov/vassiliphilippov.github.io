nursing care

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːsɪŋ kɛr / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsɪŋ keə / (BrE)
professional care given by a trained nurse to support a patient's health and recovery
professional care provided by a nurse

Examples

The hospital provides excellent nursing care for all its patients.
After his surgery, his recovery depended on round-the-clock nursing care.
Families often appreciate the compassionate nursing care given to their loved ones in hospices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical care - treatment and attention received to maintain or improve health
