high-low

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ ˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ ˌləʊ / (BrE)
A form of poker in which the pot is split between the highest and lowest hand.
poker game where high and low hands split pot

Examples

We're playing high-low tonight, so remember the low hand is just as important.
He won the pot in high-low with both the highest and lowest hands.
In high-low poker, strategy changes since players aim for both extremes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud poker - variant of poker where some cards are dealt face-up
