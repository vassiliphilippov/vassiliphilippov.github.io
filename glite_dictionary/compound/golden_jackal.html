golden jackal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈdʒækəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈdʒækəl / (BrE)
a medium-sized jackal native to Europe and Asia, known for its golden-yellow coat
a jackal species with a golden coat, native to Eurasia

Examples

The golden jackal is found throughout southeastern Europe and southern Asia.
Farmers often hear the howling of a golden jackal at night near their villages.
Unlike its African relatives, the golden jackal has adapted well to living close to humans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jackal - a wild dog of Africa and Asia
