space vehicle

1. NOUN

/ speɪs ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / speɪs ˈviːɪkəl / (BrE)
a craft designed to travel in outer space, such as a spacecraft or satellite
craft designed for space travel

Examples

The company announced plans to launch a new space vehicle into orbit next year.
Scientists are developing a space vehicle capable of reaching Mars.
A space vehicle must withstand extreme temperatures when re-entering Earth's atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacecraft - a vehicle or device designed for travel or operation in outer space
  • Synonym: satellite - an artificial object placed in orbit around a planet
