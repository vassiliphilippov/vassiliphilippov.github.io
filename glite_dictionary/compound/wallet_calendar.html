wallet calendar

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑlət kəˌlændɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɒlɪt kəˌlændə / (BrE)
A small calendar made to fit inside a pocket or wallet, often in the form of a card, booklet, or small notebook.
small calendar for a wallet or pocket

Examples

He kept a wallet calendar in his pocket to remember important dates.
The company distributed a branded wallet calendar to its clients as a promotional gift.
Despite using a smartphone, she still prefers her paper wallet calendar for jotting down appointments.

Synset Examples

My grandfather still uses pocket calendars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocket calendar - a small calendar designed to be carried in a pocket
