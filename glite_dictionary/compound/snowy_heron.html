snowy heron

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊ.i ˈhɛr.ən / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ.i ˈher.ən / (BrE)
a small white egret (Egretta thula) of the Americas, noted for its slender black bill, black legs, and yellow feet
small white egret of the Americas

Examples

The snowy heron is easily recognized by its bright white feathers and distinctive yellow feet.
While birdwatching in Florida, she spotted a snowy heron along the marshy shoreline.
The wetland reserve provides an important habitat for the snowy heron and many other waterfowl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egret - a type of heron with white plumage
