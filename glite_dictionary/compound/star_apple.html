star apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑr ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈstɑː ˌæpəl / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to the West Indies and Central America, bearing edible purple fruit that is star-shaped in cross section
tropical tree with edible star-shaped fruit

Examples

The star apple is prized for its sweet, juicy pulp in many Caribbean countries.
She sliced the star apple open to reveal its distinctive star-shaped pattern inside.
Farmers often cultivate star apples alongside mangos and avocados in tropical orchards.
