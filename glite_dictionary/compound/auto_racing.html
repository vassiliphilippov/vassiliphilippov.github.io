auto racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːtoʊ ˌreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which cars compete against each other on a race track or course, typically for speed over a set distance or time.
sport of racing automobiles

Examples

Many fans travel across the country to attend auto racing events each year.
The history of auto racing dates back to the late 19th century.
She dreams of becoming a professional auto racing driver someday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motor racing - the sport of racing motor vehicles
  • Synonym: car racing - the sport of racing cars

Etymology

From 'auto', short for 'automobile', and 'racing'.
