Continuity Irish Republican Army

1. PROPN

/ kənˈtɪnjuəti ˌaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑrmi / (AmE), / kənˈtɪnjʊəti ˌaɪərɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
an Irish republican paramilitary organization formed in 1994 opposing the peace process in Northern Ireland, considered a terrorist group by the UK and others
Irish republican paramilitary group (since 1994)

Examples

The Continuity Irish Republican Army claimed responsibility for the recent attack in Northern Ireland.
The government has repeatedly condemned the activities of the Continuity Irish Republican Army.
Security forces are on high alert due to threats from the Continuity Irish Republican Army.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Irish Republican Army - main Irish republican paramilitary organization active during the 20th century
  • Synonym: Provisional Irish Republican Army - splinter group of the IRA active from 1969-2005
