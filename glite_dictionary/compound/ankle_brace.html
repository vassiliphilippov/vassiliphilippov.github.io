ankle brace

1. NOUN

/ ˈæŋ.kəl breɪs / (AmE), / ˈæŋ.kl̩ breɪs / (BrE)
a supportive device worn around the ankle to stabilize or protect it, especially after injury
supportive device for the ankle
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended an ankle brace after I sprained my ankle during a soccer match.
She wore an ankle brace to provide extra support while hiking on uneven ground.
After his surgery, he needed to use an ankle brace for several weeks.
