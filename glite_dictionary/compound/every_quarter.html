every quarter

1. ADV

/ ˈɛv.ri ˈkwɔr.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ri ˈkwɔː.tə / (BrE)
at intervals of three months; once during each period of three months in a year
once every three months

Examples

The company releases its financial reports every quarter to keep shareholders informed.
I schedule a meeting with my mentor every quarter to discuss my progress.
We inspect the fire alarms every quarter to ensure they are working properly.

Synset Examples

interest is compounded quarterly
