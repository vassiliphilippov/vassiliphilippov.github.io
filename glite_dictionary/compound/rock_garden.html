rock garden

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈɡɑrdn̩ / (AmE), / rɒk ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A garden area designed with a prominent display of rocks, often planted with alpine or other low-growing plants.
garden with rocks and alpine plants

Examples

The couple spent the weekend creating a beautiful rock garden in their backyard.
Several rare alpine flowers bloomed between the stones in her rock garden.
A well-designed rock garden adds texture and seasonal interest to your landscaping.
