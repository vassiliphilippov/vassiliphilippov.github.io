garden party

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn̩ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering held outdoors, typically in a garden, for conversation, entertainment, and refreshments
outdoor social gathering in a garden

Examples

We were invited to a garden party last Saturday to celebrate Emily's birthday.
The garden party featured live music, delicious food, and games for the children.
They set up canopies and fairy lights to prepare for the evening garden party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests, typically for celebration or recreation
