chair car

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛr kɑr / (AmE), / tʃɛː kɑː / (BrE)
A railway passenger car designed for daytime travel, offering individual seats for which an extra fare is charged.
railway car with individual seats for extra fare

Examples

The train has a chair car for passengers who want more comfort during the journey.
I reserved a seat in the chair car to enjoy the larger, reclining chairs.
Passengers in the chair car paid a little extra for added amenities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - a railway passenger car, usually less luxurious
