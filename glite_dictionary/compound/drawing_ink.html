drawing ink

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪɪŋ ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ɪŋk / (BrE)
a black liquid ink specially prepared for use in drawing, printing, or writing
black ink for drawing or writing

Examples

The artist carefully dipped his pen into the drawing ink to begin his sketch.
I prefer using drawing ink because it produces bold, crisp lines on the paper.
Good quality drawing ink dries quickly and resists fading over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ink - colored liquid used for writing, printing, or drawing
