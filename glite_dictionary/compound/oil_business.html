oil business

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
the industry involved in the production, processing, and distribution of oil and oil products
industry producing and trading oil

Examples

The oil business is a major contributor to the country's economy.
She has worked in the oil business for over fifteen years.
Environmental regulations have significantly impacted the oil business in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil industry - the collective businesses and activities involved in the extraction, production, and marketing of oil
  • Degree Higher: energy sector - all industries involved in production and supply of energy, including oil, gas, and electricity
