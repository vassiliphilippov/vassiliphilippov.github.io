plainclotheswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female police officer or detective who wears ordinary clothes instead of a uniform while on duty
female detective in civilian clothes

Examples

The plainclotheswoman observed the suspect from across the crowded café.
It was a plainclotheswoman who made the undercover arrest last night.
Reporters mistook the plainclotheswoman for a bystander at the scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plainclothesman - a male detective or police officer in civilian clothes
  • Synonym: detective - a police officer whose job is to investigate crimes

Etymology

From 'plainclothes' (civilian clothing worn by police) + 'woman'. Formed by analogy with 'plainclothesman'.
