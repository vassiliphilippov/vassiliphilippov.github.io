tilled land

1. NOUN

/ tɪld lænd / (AmE), / tɪld lænd / (BrE)
Land that is cultivated by plowing, sowing, and raising crops.
land cultivated for crops

Examples

Farmers prepare the tilled land each spring by turning over the soil.
After months of drought, the tilled land finally received much-needed rain.
The region is known for its vast stretches of fertile tilled land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arable land - land suitable for growing crops
