special education

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛʃəl ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
Education designed for students with physical, mental, or learning disabilities whose needs cannot be met by a typical classroom program.
education for students with disabilities

Examples

After his diagnosis, Tom was placed in special education to better support his learning needs.
The school employs several teachers who are trained in special education.
Many parents advocate for improved funding for special education programs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mainstream education - education in regular classrooms for students without special needs
