angel cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl keɪk / (BrE)
A light, airy sponge cake made without egg yolks, typically using only egg whites, flour, and sugar.
light sponge cake without egg yolks

Examples

She baked an angel cake for the family gathering, serving it with fresh strawberries and cream.
The angel cake was so light and fluffy that it almost melted in our mouths.
Have you ever tried making an angel cake from scratch without using any egg yolks?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sponge cake - a light cake made with eggs, sugar, and flour

Etymology

From 'angel' (suggesting lightness/purity) + 'cake'; popularized in the United States in the late 19th century.
