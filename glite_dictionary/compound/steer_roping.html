steer roping

1. NOUN

/ stɪr ˈroʊpɪŋ / (AmE), / stɪə ˈrəʊpɪŋ / (BrE)
a rodeo event in which a participant attempts to capture a steer by lassoing it
rodeo event lassoing a steer

Examples

He watched the steer roping competition at the county fair.
Steer roping requires skill and precision from both horse and rider.
Many spectators gathered to see the thrilling steer roping event at the rodeo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calf roping - a rodeo event involving lassoing and tying a calf
