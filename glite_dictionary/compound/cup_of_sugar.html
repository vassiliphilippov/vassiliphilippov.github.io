cup of sugar

1. NOUN

/ kʌp əv ˈʃʌɡɚ / (AmE), / kʌp əv ˈʃʌɡə / (BrE)
a standard unit of sugar measurement in cooking, equal to the volume of one cup
unit of sugar measurement (one cup)

Examples

The recipe calls for one cup of sugar to be added to the cake batter.
Can you give me half a cup of sugar for my coffee?
She measured out two cups of sugar before starting to bake.

Synset Examples

I borrowed a cup of sugar from Mrs. Jones next door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cup - a unit of volume used in cooking, equal to about 240 milliliters
