glimpse into

1. NOUN

/ ɡlɪmps ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ɡlɪmps ˈɪntu / (BrE)
a brief or partial view or insight into something, especially something not normally seen or understood
brief view or insight into something

Examples

The documentary gives viewers a glimpse into the daily lives of mountain villagers.
Reading her diary provided a rare glimpse into her thoughts and feelings.
The tour offers a glimpse into how ancient civilizations built their cities.

Synset Examples

She offered a glimpse into her creative process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peek into - to look quickly or briefly into something
