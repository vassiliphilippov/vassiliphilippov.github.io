bed of flowers

1. NOUN

/ bɛd əv ˈflaʊɚz / (AmE), / bɛd əv ˈflaʊəz / (BrE)
an area of ground in a garden or park where flowers are planted and grown
an area for growing flowers

Examples

The bed of flowers by the walkway was full of bright tulips.
She spent the afternoon weeding the bed of flowers in her backyard.
A bed of flowers adds color and beauty to any garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowerbed - an area of ground in a garden planted with flowers
