gun for hire

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn fər ˈhaɪɚ / (AmE), / ɡʌn fə ˈhaɪə / (BrE)
a professional killer who is hired to murder someone, typically using a gun
hired professional killer

Examples

The mafia hired a gun for hire to eliminate their rival.
Detectives believed the crime was committed by a gun for hire with no personal motive.
In the movie, the main character is a ruthless gun for hire seeking redemption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitman - a hired killer, an assassin
  • Synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone for political or hired reasons
