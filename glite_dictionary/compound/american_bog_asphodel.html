American bog asphodel

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən bɑɡ ˈæsfəˌdɛl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən bɒɡ ˈæs.fə.dɛl / (BrE)
A perennial wetland plant (Narthecium americanum) native to the eastern United States, from New Jersey to South Carolina.
wetland plant of eastern US

Examples

The rare American bog asphodel blooms in the marshes of New Jersey each spring.
Conservationists are working to protect the remaining habitats of the American bog asphodel.
I spotted a cluster of American bog asphodel while hiking in a South Carolina wetland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bog asphodel - a species of flowering plant growing in wetlands
