longcase clock

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔːŋˌkeɪs klɑːk / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋkeɪs klɒk / (BrE)
A tall, freestanding pendulum clock enclosed in a narrow, upright wooden case, often called a grandfather clock.
tall pendulum clock in a wooden case

Examples

The antique shop displayed a magnificent longcase clock crafted in the eighteenth century.
His grandfather polished the old longcase clock every Sunday morning.
The steady ticking of the longcase clock echoed through the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather clock - a tall, freestanding pendulum clock
