Nenets language

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛnɛts ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈnɛnɛts ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Nenets people of northern Siberia
Uralic language of the Nenets in Siberia

Examples

Linguists have documented many stories told in the Nenets language.
The preservation of the Nenets language is important for indigenous culture in Siberia.
She is fluent in both Russian and the Nenets language.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Uralic language - a member of the Uralic family of languages

Etymology

'Nenets' from the ethnonym of the Nenets people; 'language' from Latin 'lingua'.
