drug peddler

1. NOUN

/ drʌɡ ˈpɛd.lɚ / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈpɛd.lə / (BrE)
a person who illegally sells drugs without a license
illegal drug seller

Examples

The police arrested a notorious drug peddler in the city center last night.
She warned her children to stay away from drug peddlers near the school.
The neighborhood had a serious problem with drug peddler activity before the new patrols began.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug dealer - someone who sells illegal drugs
