silk-stocking lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ sɪlk ˈstɑːkɪŋ ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / sɪlk ˈstɒkɪŋ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
a way of living characterized by wealth, privilege, and luxurious comforts
wealthy or privileged way of living

Examples

He enjoyed a silk-stocking lifestyle with private jets and five-star hotels.
Despite her humble beginnings, she quickly adapted to a silk-stocking lifestyle after her marriage.
Many novels depict the silk-stocking lifestyle of the social elite in the early twentieth century.

Synset Examples

His silk-stocking lifestyle was evident in his lavish home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opulent lifestyle - a life marked by lavishness and luxury
  • Antonym: simple lifestyle - a life without luxury or extravagance

Etymology

From 'silk-stocking', historically referring to the luxury of silk stockings, symbolizing wealth and upper-class status.
