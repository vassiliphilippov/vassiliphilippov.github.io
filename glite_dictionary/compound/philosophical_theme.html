philosophical theme

1. NOUN

/ fəˌlɑːsəˈfɪkəl θiːm / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒsəˈfɪkəl θiːm / (BrE)
an abstract idea or underlying subject related to philosophy, often explored in literature, art, or discussion
idea or subject relating to philosophy

Examples

The novel explores several philosophical themes, including the nature of reality and personal identity.
Her essay examines the philosophical theme of free will versus determinism.
Many science fiction films are rich in philosophical themes about human existence and morality.

Synset Examples

They discussed the philosophical themes in The Matrix.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theme - main subject or idea in a piece of writing, discussion, or work of art
