land rail

1. NOUN

/ ˈlænd ˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˌreɪl / (BrE)
a common Eurasian bird (the corncrake) of the rail family, often found in grain fields and meadows
Eurasian rail bird, the corncrake

Examples

We heard the distinctive call of the land rail hidden in the tall grass.
The land rail is most active at dusk during the spring and summer.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the land rail in Eastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corncrake - another name for the land rail, a Eurasian bird (Crex crex)
