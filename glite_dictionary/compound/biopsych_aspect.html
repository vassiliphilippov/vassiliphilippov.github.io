biopsych aspect

1. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈsaɪk ˈæs.pɛkt / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əˈsʌɪk ˈæs.pɛkt / (BrE)
An aspect concerning the interaction between biological and psychological factors.
biological and psychological interrelation

Examples

The therapist considered the biopsych aspect of the patient's condition before suggesting a treatment plan.
Medical students learn about the biopsych aspect of chronic illnesses.
Researchers are increasingly studying the biopsych aspect of anxiety disorders.

Synset Examples

Her research focused on the biopsych aspects of stress management.
They explored the biopsych relationship between genetics and behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biopsychosocial - relating to the interconnection of biological, psychological, and social factors

Etymology

Formed from 'bio-' (life, living organisms) and 'psych' (mind).
