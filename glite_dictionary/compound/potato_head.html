potato head

1. NOUN

/ pəˈteɪtoʊ hɛd / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ hɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a foolish or silly person
foolish or silly person (informal)
informal

Examples

He accidentally locked himself out again—what a potato head!
You'd be a potato head to forget your keys on such an important day.
They called him a potato head after he told that ridiculous joke.

Synset Examples

I felt like a potato head for forgetting her birthday.
Don't be such a potato head, think before you speak!
He called himself a potato head after losing the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
