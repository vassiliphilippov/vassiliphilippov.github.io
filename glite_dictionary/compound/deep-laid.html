deep-laid

1. ADJ

/ ˈdip ˌleɪd / (AmE), / ˈdiːp ˌleɪd / (BrE)
(of a plan or scheme) secretly and carefully arranged or planned to achieve an objective, especially in a concealed manner.
secretly and carefully planned

Examples

Their deep-laid strategies only became clear after the merger was complete.
He was shocked to discover the deep-laid plot against him.
She admired the deep-laid ingenuity of the rival team's approach.

Synset Examples

deep-laid plans

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secret - kept hidden or not intended to be known by others
  • Antonym: open - not secret or hidden
