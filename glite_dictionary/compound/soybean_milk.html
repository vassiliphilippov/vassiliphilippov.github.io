soybean milk

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪˌbin mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn mɪlk / (BrE)
A white liquid made from ground soybeans and water, used as a substitute for cow's milk and in products like tofu and infant formula.
plant-based milk from soybeans

Examples

Many people who are lactose intolerant choose to drink soybean milk with their breakfast cereal.
The recipe calls for a cup of soybean milk instead of regular milk.
She adds soybean milk to her coffee for a creamy texture without dairy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soy milk - a milk substitute made from soybeans; alternative name for soybean milk
