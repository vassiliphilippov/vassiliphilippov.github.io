fame whore

1. NOUN

/ feɪm wɔːr / (AmE), / feɪm wɔː / (BrE)
A person who seeks attention and notoriety in an excessive or shameless manner.
someone who craves public attention
slang

Examples

The reality TV contestant was often labeled a fame whore by critics.
Many celebrities are accused of being fame whores when they share every detail of their lives online.
He acted like a fame whore at the event, desperate to be noticed by the press.

Synset Examples

She was often called a fame whore for her publicity stunts.
His behavior last night made him look like a fame whore.
The tabloids love to chase after fame whores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention seeker - a person who behaves in a way to attract attention
