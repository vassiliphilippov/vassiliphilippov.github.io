face soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs ˌsoʊp / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌsəʊp / (BrE)
A soap formulated specifically for cleansing the face, typically milder than regular soap.
soap for washing the face

Examples

She always uses face soap to keep her skin clean and healthy.
Gentle face soap is recommended for people with sensitive skin.
I bought a new face soap that smells like lavender.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: facial cleanser - a product for cleaning the face
  • Degree Lower: body soap - soap used for washing the body
