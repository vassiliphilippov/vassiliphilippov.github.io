mother-in-law

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðər ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ɪn lɔː / (BrE)
the mother of one's spouse
one's spouse's mother

Examples

My mother-in-law is visiting us this weekend.
She gets along very well with her mother-in-law.
After the wedding, he called his mother-in-law to thank her for everything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: father-in-law - the father of one's spouse
