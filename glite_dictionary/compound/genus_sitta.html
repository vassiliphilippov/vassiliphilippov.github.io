genus Sitta

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːtə / (AmE), / ˈsɪtə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Sittidae, comprising the nuthatches, a group of small passerine birds known for climbing trees headfirst.
type genus of the nuthatch family

Examples

The genus Sitta includes the Eurasian nuthatch and other related species.
Researchers studied the behavior of several birds belonging to the genus Sitta.
Members of the genus Sitta are known for their ability to climb down tree trunks headfirst.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Sittidae - the family of birds known as nuthatches
  • Synonym: nuthatch - a common term for birds in genus Sitta

Etymology

From Latin 'sitta', from Ancient Greek 'σίττη' (sittē), a kind of bird (nuthatch).
