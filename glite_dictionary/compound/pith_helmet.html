pith helmet

1. NOUN

/ pɪθ ˈhɛlmət / (AmE), / pɪθ ˈhɛlmɪt / (BrE)
a lightweight, hard sun hat made from pith or similar material, traditionally worn in hot climates for protection from the sun
lightweight sun hat worn in hot climates

Examples

The explorer wore a pith helmet to shield himself from the harsh desert sun.
Vintage photographs often show colonial officers in pith helmets.
She found an old pith helmet among her grandfather's travel memorabilia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sun hat - a hat worn for protection from the sun

Etymology

From 'pith,' referring to the soft, spongy tissue of certain plants, used as the material, and 'helmet.'
