bastard turtle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstərd ˈtɝt̬l / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd ˈtɜːtl / (BrE)
A grey sea turtle found along the Atlantic and Gulf Coasts of North America.
grey sea turtle of North America

Examples

The bastard turtle is often seen nesting on sandy beaches along the Gulf Coast.
Conservationists work to protect the eggs of the endangered bastard turtle.
The bastard turtle is also called the Atlantic ridley or Kemp’s ridley turtle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ridley turtle - a small sea turtle native to the Atlantic; also called ridley
