broccoli raab

1. NOUN

/ ˌbrɑːkəli ˈrɑːb / (AmE), / ˌbrɒkəli ˈrɑːb / (BrE)
A vegetable with slightly bitter dark green leaves and clustered flower buds, often used in Italian cooking.
edible green leafy vegetable

Examples

She sautéed the broccoli raab with garlic and olive oil for a simple side dish.
Broccoli raab has a distinctive bitter flavor that pairs well with pasta dishes.
Farmers’ markets often feature fresh broccoli raab in the spring and fall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapini - another name for broccoli raab
