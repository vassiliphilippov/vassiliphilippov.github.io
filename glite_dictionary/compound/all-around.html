all-around

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˌɔːl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
Having many different skills, qualities, or abilities; versatile or comprehensive.
having many abilities; versatile

Examples

She is an all-around performer, excelling in singing, dancing, and acting.
The team needed someone with all-around skills to fill multiple positions.
His all-around knowledge of the business helped him solve complex problems.

Synset Examples

an all-around athlete
a well-rounded curriculum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: versatile - able to do many different things well
