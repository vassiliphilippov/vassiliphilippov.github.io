a thousand pardons

1. NOUN

/ ə ˈθaʊzənd ˈpɑrdənz / (AmE), / ə ˈθaʊzənd ˈpɑːdənz / (BrE)
a very polite or formal expression used to apologize or ask forgiveness
very polite or formal apology

Examples

A thousand pardons, I didn't realize you were standing there.
Please accept a thousand pardons for this unfortunate oversight.
A thousand pardons, but I seem to have misplaced your letter.

Synset Examples

A thousand pardons, but could you repeat that?
I ask for a thousand pardons for my mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorry - used to express apology or regret
