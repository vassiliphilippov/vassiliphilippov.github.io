woke mind virus

1. NOUN

/ woʊk maɪnd ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / wəʊk maɪnd ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a derogatory term for perceived excessive political correctness or over-awareness of social justice issues, often implying that such attitudes 'infect' individuals or discourse
derogatory term for excessive political correctness
informal slang

Examples

Some politicians blame the rise of identity politics on the so-called woke mind virus.
He claimed that schools were being overtaken by the woke mind virus and demanded change.
The columnist dismissed their concerns as symptoms of the woke mind virus spreading online.

Synset Examples

He joked that the woke mind virus was spreading faster than ever.
Some believe the woke mind virus has infected many cultural discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political correctness - the avoidance of language or behavior that may offend particular groups of people
  • Synonym: cancel culture - the practice of withdrawing support from public figures after they have done or said something considered objectionable
  • Antonym: conservative backlash - opposition to progressive social changes

Etymology

Coined in the early 2020s, combining 'woke' (slang for social awareness), 'mind', and 'virus' (used metaphorically for rapid, spreading influence).
