film festival

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈfɛstɪvəl / (BrE)
an event at which a selection of films is shown to an audience, often including premieres and awards, typically held annually.
event showcasing a selection of films

Examples

The Sundance film festival attracts independent filmmakers from all over the world.
Her documentary won an award at a prestigious film festival in Europe.
They are planning to premiere their new movie at the Toronto film festival next month.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: festival - an event or series of events celebrating a particular theme or subject
