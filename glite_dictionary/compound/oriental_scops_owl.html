Oriental scops owl

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈriː.ən.tl skɑps aʊl / (AmE), / ˌɒr.iˈɛn.tl skɒps aʊl / (BrE)
A small species of owl (Otus sunia) native to Asia, recognized by its compact size and distinctive call.
a small Asian owl species

Examples

The Oriental scops owl is known for its soft, whistling call at night.
Birdwatchers travel to Southeast Asia to catch a glimpse of the elusive Oriental scops owl.
Recent studies have revealed the migratory patterns of the Oriental scops owl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scops owl - a type of small owl with prominent ear tufts
