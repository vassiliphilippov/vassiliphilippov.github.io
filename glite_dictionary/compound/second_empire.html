Second Empire

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈɛmpaɪər / (AmE), / ˌsekənd ˈempaɪə / (BrE)
the imperial government of France under Napoleon III, reigning from 1852 to 1870.
the French imperial regime under Napoleon III (1852–1870)

Examples

The Second Empire was marked by ambitious urban renewal projects in Paris.
Many 19th-century buildings in the United States were inspired by Second Empire architectural style.
The defeat in the Franco-Prussian War led to the collapse of the Second Empire.
