netted melon

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛtɪd ˌmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈnɛtɪd ˌmɛlən / (BrE)
A variety of muskmelon with a thin net-like patterned rind and sweet green flesh.
muskmelon with net-like rind and green flesh

Examples

The farmer harvested several netted melons from his field in late summer.
I prefer the sweet, juicy taste of a netted melon to regular cantaloupe.
After slicing the netted melon, she served it as a refreshing dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cantaloupe - a type of muskmelon with orange flesh and a netted rind
