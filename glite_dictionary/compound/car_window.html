car window

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / kɑː ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window that is part of a car, typically used for ventilation or visibility
window in a car

Examples

The driver rolled down the car window to enjoy the fresh air.
Rain was tapping lightly against the back car window as they drove.
She stuck her hand out of the car window to feel the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window - an opening in a wall or vehicle fitted with glass
