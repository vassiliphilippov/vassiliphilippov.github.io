order Ulvales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ʌlˈveɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ʌlˈveɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of green algae in the class Ulvophyceae, including the genus Ulva (sea lettuce) and related forms.
order of green algae (Ulvophyceae)
medical

Examples

The order Ulvales includes several species commonly known as sea lettuce.
Researchers are studying the ecological role of the order Ulvales in coastal waters.
Members of the order Ulvales are important for both marine habitats and the food industry.

Etymology

From Latin 'Ulva' (a genus of seaweed) and modern taxonomic suffix '-ales' (order).
