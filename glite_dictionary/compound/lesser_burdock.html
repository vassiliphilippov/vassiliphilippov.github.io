lesser burdock

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈbɜrdɑk / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈbɜːdɒk / (BrE)
a coarse biennial plant (Arctium minus) of the daisy family, widespread in Europe and North Africa, with large leaves and purple flower heads.
a widespread European burdock plant

Examples

The fields were overgrown with lesser burdock, making it difficult to walk through.
Gardeners often remove lesser burdock because it spreads quickly and crowds out native plants.
The large, rough leaves of lesser burdock are easily recognized by most botanists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burdock - any of several coarse weedy plants of the genus Arctium
