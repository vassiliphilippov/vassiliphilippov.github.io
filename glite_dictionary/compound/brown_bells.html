brown bells

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊn ˌbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn ˌbɛlz / (BrE)
A California herb with brownish-purple or greenish bell-shaped flowers.
California herb with bell-shaped flowers

She identified several wildflowers, including the brown bells, during her hike.
