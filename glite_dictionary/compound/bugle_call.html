bugle call

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuɡəl kɔl / (AmE), / ˈbjuːɡl kɔːl / (BrE)
a signal or tune played on a bugle, especially to mark a specific time or event, especially in the military
signal tune played on a bugle

Examples

The sound of the bugle call echoed through the camp at sunrise.
At the end of the ceremony, a solemn bugle call was played to honor the fallen soldiers.
Each morning, troops assembled promptly in response to the familiar bugle call.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reveille - a bugle call played to wake military personnel
  • Synonym: taps - a bugle call played at military funerals or to signal lights out
