Baluchi language

1. NOUN

/ bəˈluːtʃi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bəˈluːtʃi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken mainly in Pakistan, Iran, Afghanistan, and some other regions
Iranian language of Pakistan and Iran

Examples

The Baluchi language has several dialects spoken across different provinces.
He is conducting research on the grammar of the Baluchi language.
Many traditional Baluch poems are preserved in the Baluchi language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Balochi - variant spelling of Baluchi
  • Degree Higher: Iranian language - branch of the Indo-Iranian languages, including Baluchi

Etymology

From Persian *Balōčī*, referring to the Baloch people and their language.
