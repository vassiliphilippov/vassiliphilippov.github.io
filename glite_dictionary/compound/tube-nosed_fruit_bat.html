tube-nosed fruit bat

1. NOUN

/ tuːb noʊzd fruːt bæt / (AmE), / tjuːb nəʊzd fruːt bæt / (BrE)
a type of fruit bat of the genus Nyctimene, characterized by nostrils that form distinct diverging tubes
bat with tubular nostrils (genus Nyctimene)

Examples

The tube-nosed fruit bat is easily recognized by its unusually shaped nostrils.
Scientists discovered a new species of tube-nosed fruit bat in the rainforest.
Many tube-nosed fruit bats play a crucial role in pollinating tropical plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit bat - a bat that feeds primarily on fruit
