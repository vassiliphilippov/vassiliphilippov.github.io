cry-baby tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraɪ ˌbeɪbi ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkraɪ ˌbeɪbi ˌtriː / (BrE)
a small spiny tree native to South America, known for its dark crimson and scarlet flowers, usually solitary or in clusters
South American spiny tree with crimson flowers

Examples

The cry-baby tree is prized for its beautiful scarlet flowers in many South American gardens.
Botanists have studied the cry-baby tree for its unique spiny branches and striking blooms.
You can spot a cry-baby tree by its solitary clusters of deep crimson flowers.
