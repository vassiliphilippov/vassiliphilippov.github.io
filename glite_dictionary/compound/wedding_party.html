wedding party

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
the group of people who take part in a wedding ceremony, such as the bride, groom, bridesmaids, and groomsmen
group involved in a wedding ceremony

Examples

The wedding party posed for photos in the garden after the ceremony.
Every member of the wedding party had a special role to play on the big day.
The hotel reserved a private room for the wedding party to celebrate after the reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bridal party - the group of people chosen by the couple to participate in a wedding ceremony
