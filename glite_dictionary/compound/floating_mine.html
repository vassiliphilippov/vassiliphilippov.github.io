floating mine

1. NOUN

/ ˈfloʊtɪŋ maɪn / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtɪŋ maɪn / (BrE)
an explosive device designed to float on water and detonate when a ship collides with it
explosive device floating on water

Examples

During the war, several ships were damaged by a floating mine lurking near the harbor.
The navy dispatched a vessel to safely detonate a floating mine spotted off the coast.
Fishermen reported the presence of a floating mine drifting dangerously close to their boats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mine - an explosive device placed to destroy vehicles or ships
