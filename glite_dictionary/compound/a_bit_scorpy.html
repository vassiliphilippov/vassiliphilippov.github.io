a bit scorpy

1. ADJ

/ ə bɪt ˈskɔːrpi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈskɔːpi / (BrE)
Slightly aggressive or somewhat prickly in manner or attitude.
slightly aggressive or prickly
informal

Examples

After missing her morning coffee, she was a bit scorpy during the meeting.
He tends to get a bit scorpy when people question his work.
The team found their coach a bit scorpy after the disappointing match.

Synset Examples

She described his attitude as a bit scorpy today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prickly - easily irritated or likely to become annoyed
  • Degree Higher: hostile - unfriendly; antagonistic
