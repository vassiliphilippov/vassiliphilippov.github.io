sanctuary for injured wild animal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæŋktʃuˌɛri fɔr ˈɪndʒərd waɪld ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈsaŋktjʊəri fə ˈɪndʒəd waɪld ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
a place where injured wild animals are brought to receive care and recover in safety
a safe place for recovering wild animals

Examples

After a car accident, the fox was taken to a sanctuary for injured wild animals for treatment.
Many volunteers work at the sanctuary for injured wild animals to help animals recover.
The new fundraising campaign supports the construction of a sanctuary for injured wild animals in the region.

Synset Examples

She volunteers at a sanctuary for injured wild animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildlife sanctuary - a protected area established for the conservation of wild animals
  • Degree Lower: animal shelter - a place giving temporary protection to animals, often domesticated
