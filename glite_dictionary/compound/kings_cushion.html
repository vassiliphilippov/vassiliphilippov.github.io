king's cushion

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋz ˈkʊʃən / (AmE), / kɪŋz ˈkʊʃən / (BrE)
a luxurious or comfortable cushion, often specially designed for a king's throne
luxurious cushion for a king's throne

After the coronation, the new monarch sat proudly on the king's cushion atop the throne.

In the museum, a replica king's cushion adds authenticity to the royal chamber exhibit.
