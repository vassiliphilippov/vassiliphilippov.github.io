key issues

1. NOUN

/ ki ˈɪʃuz / (AmE), / ki ˈɪsjuːz / (BrE)
the most important or central problems in a discussion, situation, or context
most important problems or topics

Examples

The committee spent hours debating the key issues before voting on the proposal.
During the interview, she addressed all the key issues concerning the company's future.
Let's focus our discussion on the key issues that affect our community.

Synset Examples

She perspicaciously identified the key issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main issues - the chief or principal problems in a discussion or situation
  • Antonym: minor issues - less important or secondary problems
