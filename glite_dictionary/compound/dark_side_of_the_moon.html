dark side of the moon

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk saɪd əv ðə mun / (AmE), / dɑːk saɪd əv ðə muːn / (BrE)
the hemisphere of the moon that is never visible from Earth due to tidal locking
side of the moon not visible from Earth

Examples

Scientists have been studying the dark side of the moon using satellites and robotic probes.
Many people mistakenly believe that the dark side of the moon is always in darkness.
The spacecraft sent back detailed images of the dark side of the moon for the first time.
