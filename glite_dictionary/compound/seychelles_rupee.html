Seychelles rupee

1. NOUN

/ seɪˈʃɛlz ruˈpi / (AmE), / seɪˈʃɛlz ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency of Seychelles, equal to 100 cents.
currency of Seychelles

Examples

The price of the ticket is 150 Seychelles rupees, payable at the entrance.
Tourists should exchange their dollars for Seychelles rupee upon arrival in the country.
The value of the Seychelles rupee has remained stable against major foreign currencies this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - the basic monetary unit of several South Asian countries

Etymology

From French 'Seychelles' (the country name) and Hindi 'rūpiyā' (silver coin).
