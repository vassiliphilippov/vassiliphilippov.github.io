ditty bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪti bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɪti baɡ / (BrE)
a small bag used by sailors or soldiers to hold personal articles such as sewing kits and toiletries
small bag for sailors' or soldiers' personal items

Examples

Every sailor kept his sewing kit inside his ditty bag during the voyage.
She found her grandfather’s old ditty bag among his navy memorabilia.
The recruits were told to stow their toiletries in their ditty bag before inspection.

Etymology

Originated in the mid 19th century; possibly from dialect ditt ('anything small').
