paisa vasool

1. ADJ

/ ˌpaɪsə vəˈsuːl / (AmE), / ˌpaɪsə vəˈsuːl / (BrE)
(Indian English) Worth the amount of money spent; giving good value for money.
good value for money (Indian English)

Examples

This budget smartphone is real paisa vasool for students on a tight budget.
The show was complete paisa vasool—every minute was enjoyable and worth the ticket price.
Many street food stalls offer meals that are truly paisa vasool.

Synset Examples

He believes every meal should be a paisa vasool effort.
For the price, this hotel is truly paisa vasool.
The movie was a complete paisa vasool experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: value for money - the quality of being worth what you pay for it

Etymology

From Hindi/Urdu ‘paisa’ (money, coin) + ‘vasool’ (collect, recover).
