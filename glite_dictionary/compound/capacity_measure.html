capacity measure

1. NOUN

/ kəˈpæsəti ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / kəˈpæsəti ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a unit used to measure volume or capacity, such as a liter, gallon, or quart
unit of measurement for volume

Examples

The liter is a common capacity measure used around the world.
Bakers often use cups and pints as capacity measures in their recipes.
When converting recipes, knowing each capacity measure is essential for accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volume - the amount of space that a substance or object occupies
  • Synonym: capacity - the maximum amount that something can contain
