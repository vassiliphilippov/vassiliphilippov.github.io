mortal enemy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrtl̩ ˈɛnəmi / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtl ˈɛnəmi / (BrE)
an enemy who wishes to cause your death or completely destroy you
an enemy wanting to kill or destroy you

Examples

For years, he regarded his rival as his mortal enemy and trusted no one else.
The hero faced his mortal enemy in the epic final battle.
Despite their history, she never imagined her former friend would become her mortal enemy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: archenemy - a principal or main enemy
