poo pirate

1. NOUN

/ puː ˈpaɪrət / (AmE), / puː ˈpaɪərət / (BrE)
a humorous or playful term for a person who collects animal waste, especially dog feces, or for a dog that buries its waste
humorous term for animal-waste collector or a dog that buries waste
informal

Examples

The new employee was jokingly nicknamed the poo pirate after he cleaned up the dog park.
Every morning, my dog becomes a poo pirate, secretly hiding his mess in the backyard.
They laughed and called their friend a poo pirate because he always volunteered to walk the neighborhood dogs.

Synset Examples

Being a poo pirate is not a fun job at the park.
My dog acts like a poo pirate, burying his treasures.
The kids called him the poo pirate for his messy habits.
