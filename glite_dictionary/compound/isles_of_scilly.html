Isles of Scilly

1. PROPN

/ aɪlz əv ˈsɪli / (AmE), / aɪlz əv ˈsɪli / (BrE)
an archipelago of small islands off the southwestern coast of England, near the entrance to the English Channel
archipelago off southwest England
