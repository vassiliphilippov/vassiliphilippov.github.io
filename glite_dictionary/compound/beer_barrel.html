beer barrel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ˌbærəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌbærəl / (BrE)
a barrel specifically made or used for storing or transporting beer
barrel for storing beer

Examples

The old pub displayed a large beer barrel in the corner as a decoration.
Workers rolled the heavy beer barrel into the cellar for storage.
During the festival, each table had its own beer barrel for guests to fill their mugs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keg - a small barrel, especially one for beer
