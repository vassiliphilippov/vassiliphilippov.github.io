world organization

1. NOUN

/ wɜrld ˌɔrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / wɜːld ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an international alliance that includes many different countries, formed to promote cooperation on global issues
international alliance of many countries

Examples

After World War II, the United Nations became the most prominent world organization dedicated to maintaining peace.
The World Health Organization is a world organization focused on improving global health.
Many countries joined the world organization to tackle climate change together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: international organization - an organization with members from several countries

2. NOUN

/ wɝːld ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / wɜːld ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an international alliance or group involving many different countries, especially one set up to promote cooperation or resolve global issues
international alliance of many countries

Examples

The United Nations is a well-known world organization that works to maintain international peace.
Membership in a world organization can help smaller countries have a global voice.
Many global health initiatives are coordinated through a world organization such as the World Health Organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: international organization - an alliance of various nations or political entities for common goals
