burglar alarm

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːɡlɚ əˌlɑːrm / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡlə əˌlɑːm / (BrE)
a device that makes a loud noise or sends a signal to warn of an attempted burglary
device for warning of burglary

Examples

The store installed a modern burglar alarm to protect against theft at night.
When the burglar alarm went off, the neighbors immediately called the police.
She forgot to set the burglar alarm before leaving the house for vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security alarm - device for alerting to unauthorized entry

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːɡlɚ əˌlɑrm / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡlə əˌlɑːm / (BrE)
a device that makes a loud sound to warn of unauthorized entry, especially into a building
device that warns of break-ins

Examples

As soon as the window was forced open, the burglar alarm went off, scaring the intruder away.
The new burglar alarm can be controlled remotely from a smartphone app.
Installing a burglar alarm is a good way to protect your home from break-ins.

Synset Examples

they could hear the burglar alarm a mile away

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security system - system for protecting property from intrusion
