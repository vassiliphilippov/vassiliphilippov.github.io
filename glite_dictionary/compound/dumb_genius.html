dumb genius

1. NOUN

/ dʌm ˈdʒiːniəs / (AmE), / dʌm ˈdʒiːniəs / (BrE)
a person who is highly intelligent but lacks common sense or social skills
very smart person lacking common sense
informal

Examples

Although he's a dumb genius at math, he often forgets where he left his keys.
The new intern is a classic dumb genius—brilliant ideas but zero social awareness.
People call him a dumb genius because he excels in theory but struggles with everyday life.

Synset Examples

Being a dumb genius means he struggles with everyday tasks.
He's a dumb genius, brilliant but totally clueless socially.
Sometimes, the dumb genius surprises us with his insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absent-minded professor - an academic who is brilliant but forgetful or oblivious to surroundings
