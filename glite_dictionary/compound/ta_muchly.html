ta muchly

1. INTJ

/ tɑː ˈmʌtʃli / (AmE), / tɑː ˈmʌtʃli / (BrE)
(informal, British) Used to express thanks or gratitude, often in a light-hearted or humorous way.
informal British expression of thanks
informal british

Examples

I forgot my umbrella, but someone lent me one—ta muchly to them!
Ta muchly for picking me up from the station last night.
She handed me the report with a smile, and I said, 'ta muchly!'

Synset Examples

He replied with a smile, ta muchly!
She nodded, saying ta muchly for the advice.
Ta muchly for your assistance today!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thanks - an expression of gratitude
  • Synonym: cheers - British informal way of saying thanks

Etymology

From British slang 'ta' (thank you), combined with 'muchly' (much, in a playful form).
