iron-tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərn ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˌtri / (BrE)
a small, slow-growing deciduous tree native to northern Iran, recognized for its low domed shape and hard wood.
hard-wooded tree of northern Iran

Examples

The iron-tree is valued for its exceptionally hard wood and resilience to harsh climates.
In the botanical gardens, visitors admired the unique shape of the iron-tree.
Efforts are underway to protect the dwindling populations of the iron-tree in its native habitat.
