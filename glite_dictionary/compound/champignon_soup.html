champignon soup

1. NOUN

/ ʃæmˈpɪnjɒn suːp / (AmE), / ʃæmˈpiːnjɒ̃ suːp / (BrE)
A usually creamy soup made with champignon mushrooms, commonly served as an appetizer or light meal.
creamy soup made with champignon mushrooms

Examples

She ordered a bowl of champignon soup to start her meal at the restaurant.
The chef garnished the champignon soup with fresh parsley and a drizzle of cream.
On cold days, I love making homemade champignon soup for my family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mushroom soup - Soup made with mushrooms as the primary ingredient

Etymology

Champignon from French 'champignon' (mushroom); soup from Old French 'soupe'.
