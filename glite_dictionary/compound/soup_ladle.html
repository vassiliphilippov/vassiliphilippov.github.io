soup ladle

1. NOUN

/ suːp ˈleɪdəl / (AmE), / suːp ˈleɪdəl / (BrE)
a large, deep spoon with a long handle used for serving soup
spoon for serving soup

Examples

She used a soup ladle to fill the bowls at dinner.
The restaurant keeps a soup ladle hanging above the stove for convenience.
He accidentally dropped the soup ladle into the pot while serving guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladle - a large, deep spoon with a long handle for serving liquids
