frankfurter bun

1. NOUN

/ ˈfræŋkfɚtɚ bʌn / (AmE), / ˈfræŋkfətə bʌn / (BrE)
a long, soft bread roll shaped to hold a frankfurter or hot dog sausage
long roll for frankfurters or hot dogs

Examples

She placed the steaming sausage into a frankfurter bun and topped it with mustard and onions.
The bakery sells fresh frankfurter buns every morning for people preparing hot dogs.
I prefer my hot dogs in a whole wheat frankfurter bun for a healthier option.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot dog bun - a long bread roll for holding a hot dog
