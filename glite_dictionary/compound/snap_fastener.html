snap fastener

1. NOUN

/ snæp ˈfæstənər / (AmE), / snæp ˈfɑːstənə / (BrE)
a small device for fastening clothing, consisting of two interlocking discs that close with a snapping sound
clothing fastener with snapping action

Examples

The jacket is secured with a snap fastener instead of buttons.
You can easily close your bag using the snap fastener at the top.
Many baby clothes use a snap fastener for quick dressing and undressing.

Synset Examples

children can manage snaps better than buttons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press stud - a fastener used on clothing; British equivalent of snap fastener
  • Antonym: button - a small round fastener sewn on clothing
