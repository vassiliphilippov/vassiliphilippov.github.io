Japanese andromeda

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz ænˈdrɑːmədə / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪz ænˈdrɒmɪdə / (BrE)
A broad-leaved evergreen shrub native to Asia, with glossy leaves and drooping clusters of white flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.
Asian evergreen shrub with white flowers

Examples

The Japanese andromeda in her garden was covered in fragrant white blossoms in early spring.
We planted a Japanese andromeda because it stays green all year and thrives in shade.
The nursery recommended Japanese andromeda for its resistance to deer and attractive foliage.
