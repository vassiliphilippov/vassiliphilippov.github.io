dwarf sperm whale

1. NOUN

/ dwɔrf spɜrm weɪl / (AmE), / dwɔːf spɜːm weɪl / (BrE)
a very small species of sperm whale (Kogia sima) found in tropical and temperate oceans, growing up to about 8 feet in length
small species of sperm whale

Examples

A dwarf sperm whale was spotted near the coastline by marine biologists yesterday.
Unlike larger whales, the dwarf sperm whale is rarely seen at the ocean’s surface.
The dwarf sperm whale can release a cloud of ink to escape predators, similar to a squid.

Related Terms
