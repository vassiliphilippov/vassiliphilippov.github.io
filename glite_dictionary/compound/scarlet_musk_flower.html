scarlet musk flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑrlət mʌsk ˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈskɑːlɪt mʌsk ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, notable for its sticky branches, tuberous roots, and deep red flowers.
a plant with deep red flowers, native to southwestern US and northern Mexico

Examples

The scarlet musk flower blooms in late spring, adding vibrant color to the desert landscape.
Botanists studied the tuberous roots of the scarlet musk flower to understand its drought resistance.
Gardeners in arid regions often cultivate the scarlet musk flower for its striking deep red blossoms.
