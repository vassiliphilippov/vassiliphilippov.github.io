global health alert

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊbl hɛlθ əˈlɝt / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbl hɛlθ əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
an official warning issued to the public or health authorities about potential health risks affecting populations worldwide
international health risk warning

Examples

The WHO issued a global health alert after the new virus spread across continents.
A global health alert was announced in response to the outbreak of Ebola.
Travelers received a global health alert about rising dengue fever cases worldwide.

Synset Examples

The Wuhan coronavirus outbreak prompted global health alerts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health alert - an official warning about a health risk
