verbal creation

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrbəl kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəl kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of producing or inventing something through speech or the use of language
creation by means of speech or language

Examples

Her novel is a stunning example of verbal creation that captivates readers from start to finish.
The poet's genius lies in his effortless verbal creation of vivid imagery.
Many speeches have changed history through powerful verbal creation and persuasive language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utterance - something that is said; a spoken word, statement, or vocal sound
  • Synonym: speech - the expression of thoughts and feelings by spoken words
