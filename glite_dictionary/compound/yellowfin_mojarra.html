yellowfin mojarra

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊˌfɪn moʊˈhɑrə / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌfɪn məʊˈhɑːrə / (BrE)
a species of small fish (Gerres cinereus) found from Bermuda and the Gulf of Mexico to Brazil, popular as a panfish
a small panfish found in western Atlantic waters

Examples

The yellowfin mojarra is a common catch for anglers along the Gulf Coast.
Fishermen often pan-fry yellowfin mojarra because of their mild flavor.
In shallow waters, schools of yellowfin mojarra can be seen darting over sandy bottoms.
