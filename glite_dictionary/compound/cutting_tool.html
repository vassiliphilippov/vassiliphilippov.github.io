cutting tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌt̬ɪŋ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ tʊːl / (BrE)
A tool or implement designed specifically for cutting, such as a knife, scissors, or chisel.
tool designed for cutting

Examples

The chef reached for his sharpest cutting tool to slice the vegetables precisely.
A lathe uses a rotating cutting tool to shape pieces of metal or wood.
Always store each cutting tool properly to prevent accidents in the workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool - An implement used to carry out a particular function
