fag hag

1. NOUN

/ fæɡ hæɡ / (AmE), / fæɡ hæɡ / (BrE)
a woman who forms close friendships with gay men, often enjoying their company and participating in events or communities connected to gay culture; considered slang and sometimes offensive
woman close friends with gay men (slang, sometimes offensive)
slang offensive

Examples

She embraced her role as a fag hag and proudly attended every pride parade with her friends.
Some people call her a fag hag, but she just enjoys being around vibrant, creative individuals.
Despite the label fag hag, she views her friendships with gay men as mutually supportive and valuable.

Synset Examples

Being a fag hag means she always supports LGBTQ rights.
Her reputation as a fag hag made her the life of the party.
As a fag hag, she enjoyed the company of her gay friends.

Etymology

From slang 'fag' (offensive for gay man) and 'hag' (pejorative term for an old woman), first attested in the 1970s in gay culture.
