sexual love

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl lʌv / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl lʌv / (BrE)
a deep romantic and physical attraction involving sexual desire
romantic feeling with sexual desire

Examples

The novel explores the theme of sexual love between its two main characters.
He realized the difference between friendship and sexual love only after he fell in love for the first time.
Their sexual love was passionate but eventually faded over time.

Synset Examples

their love left them indifferent to their surroundings
she was his first love

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic love - love characterized by emotional closeness and romance, not necessarily sexual
  • Antonym: platonic love - love without sexual desire

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl lʌv / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl lʌv / (BrE)
sexual activities, often including sexual intercourse, between two people in a romantic relationship
sexual activities between lovers

Examples

After years together, their sexual love remained strong and passionate.
She believed that sexual love should be an expression of deep emotional connection.
Their relationship struggled when sexual love became less frequent and less fulfilling.
