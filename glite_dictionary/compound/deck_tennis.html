deck tennis

1. NOUN

/ dɛk ˈtɛnɪs / (AmE), / dɛk ˈtɛnɪs / (BrE)
a game, usually played on the deck of a ship, in which players toss a ring over a net stretched across a small court
game tossing ring over net on ship deck

Examples

Many passengers enjoyed playing deck tennis during the long voyage.
She learned the rules of deck tennis while traveling on an ocean liner.
Deck tennis is a fun way to stay active on a cruise ship.
