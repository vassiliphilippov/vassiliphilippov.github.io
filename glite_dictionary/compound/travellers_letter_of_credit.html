traveller's letter of credit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævələrz ˈlɛtər əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrævələz ˈlɛtə əv ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
a letter of credit issued by a bank to a traveler, allowing them to obtain money while traveling through various banks or branches.
financial document for travelers to access funds

Examples

The businesswoman relied on her traveller's letter of credit to withdraw funds while touring Europe.
In the past, a traveller's letter of credit was an essential tool for international travelers.
He showed the bank clerk his traveller's letter of credit to access money in a foreign country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter of credit - a document from a bank guaranteeing payment
