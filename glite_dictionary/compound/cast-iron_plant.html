cast-iron plant

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæstˈaɪərn plænt / (AmE), / ˌkɑːstˈaɪən plɑːnt / (BrE)
an evergreen perennial plant with large tough basal leaves, widely grown as a foliage houseplant, especially for its resilience
tough evergreen houseplant (Aspidistra elatior)

Examples

The cast-iron plant can thrive in low light and requires minimal care.
She decorated the office with a large cast-iron plant in the corner for a touch of greenery.
Gardeners recommend the cast-iron plant for those new to indoor gardening due to its hardiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspidistra - another name for the cast-iron plant

Etymology

Named for its reputation for being as tough and resilient as cast iron.
