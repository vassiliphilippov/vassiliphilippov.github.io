chemical formula

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈfɔrmjələ / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈfɔːmjʊlə / (BrE)
A notation that uses symbols to represent the elements present in a substance and the proportions in which they occur.
notation for a substance using element symbols

Examples

The chemical formula for water is H₂O.
Scientists use the chemical formula to identify and communicate about substances.
Every compound is described by a unique chemical formula.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molecular formula - notation showing the exact number of atoms of each element in a molecule
