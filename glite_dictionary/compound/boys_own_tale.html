Boy's Own tale

1. NOUN

/ bɔɪz oʊn teɪl / (AmE), / bɔɪz əʊn teɪl / (BrE)
An exciting adventure story, especially one typical of old-fashioned children's boys' magazines or books, featuring heroic deeds and daring exploits.
exciting childhood adventure story

Examples

His journey across the desert turned into a real Boy's Own tale filled with danger and heroism.
The exploits of explorers in the nineteenth century often read like a Boy's Own tale.
The movie had all the elements of a classic Boy's Own tale, from hidden treasure to daring rescues.

Synset Examples

That adventure felt like a Boy's Own tale come to life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure story - A story featuring exciting or unusual experiences

Etymology

From 'The Boy's Own Paper', a popular British boys' magazine (1879–1967) known for adventure stories.
