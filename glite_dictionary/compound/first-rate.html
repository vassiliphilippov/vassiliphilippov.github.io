first-rate

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɝst ˈreɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈreɪt / (BrE)
of the highest quality; excellent
of the best quality

Examples

The chef at that restaurant prepares first-rate meals every night.
Her skills as a negotiator are first-rate, making her highly sought after.
We stayed in a first-rate hotel during our vacation.

Synset Examples

His presentation was first-rate, impressing everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - extremely good; outstanding
  • Synonym: superb - of impressive quality; magnificent
  • Antonym: second-rate - of mediocre or inferior quality

Etymology

From nautical terminology, originally referring to the largest and most powerful class of British warships in the 17th–18th centuries.
