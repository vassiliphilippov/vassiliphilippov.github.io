oyster crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪstər kræb / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə kræb / (BrE)
a small soft-bodied crab that lives inside the mantle cavity of oysters
crab living inside oysters

Examples

The oyster crab is considered a delicacy in some coastal cuisines.
Marine biologists discovered a tiny oyster crab hidden inside the shell.
You might occasionally find an oyster crab when opening fresh oysters.
