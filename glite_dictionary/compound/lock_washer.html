lock washer

1. NOUN

/ lɑk ˈwɑʃɚ / (AmE), / lɒk ˈwɒʃə / (BrE)
A washer designed to prevent a nut from loosening.
washer that prevents a nut from loosening

Examples

The mechanic used a lock washer to secure the bolt and prevent it from loosening over time.
Be sure to include a lock washer when assembling the parts to ensure a tighter fit.
Without a lock washer, the vibrations caused the nuts to loosen and fall off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washer - a flat ring used to give tightness to a joint, to prevent leakage, or to distribute pressure
