cracker crumbs

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækɚ krʌmz / (AmE), / ˈkrækə krʌmz / (BrE)
Small pieces or fragments of crackers, used especially as a coating for food or as a thickening agent.
small pieces of crackers (used in cooking)

Examples

She coated the chicken in cracker crumbs before frying it for a crisp finish.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of cracker crumbs to thicken the soup.
He sprinkled cracker crumbs over the casserole for a crunchy topping.
