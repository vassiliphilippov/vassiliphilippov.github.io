pocket mirror

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑk.ɪt ˈmɪr.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɒk.ɪt ˈmɪə.rə / (BrE)
A small, portable mirror designed to be carried in a handbag or pocket, typically used for personal grooming.
small portable mirror for personal use

Examples

She always keeps a pocket mirror in her bag for quick touch-ups.
The pocket mirror slipped out of his jacket when he bent over.
After lunch, Kate discreetly checked her lipstick in her pocket mirror.

Synset Examples

I bought a new compact mirror today, it has an additional magnifying mirror and battery-powered LED lights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compact mirror - a small mirror in a case, often with powder or make-up
