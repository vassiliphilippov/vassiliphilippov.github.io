pair of pliers

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛr əv ˈplaɪərz / (AmE), / ˈpeə əv ˈplaɪəz / (BrE)
A hand tool with two hinged arms and serrated jaws, used for gripping, bending, or cutting objects.
gripping hand tool with hinged arms

Examples

He fixed the wire using a pair of pliers from his toolbox.
Can you hand me that pair of pliers to remove this nail?
Every mechanic's kit contains at least one pair of pliers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pliers - a hand tool with two handles and jaws for gripping things
