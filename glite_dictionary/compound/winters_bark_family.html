winter's bark family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntərz bɑrk ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəz bɑːk ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a small family of chiefly tropical shrubs and trees, including genera Drimys and Pseudowintera, sometimes classified under Magnoliaceae
family of tropical shrubs and trees (Drimys, Pseudowintera)

Examples

The winter's bark family includes several species known for their aromatic bark.
Botanists sometimes group the winter's bark family with Magnoliaceae due to similar floral structures.
Pseudowintera is a notable genus within the winter's bark family.
