pie chart

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a circular graph divided into sections to show the relative sizes of data as parts of a whole
circular graph showing proportions

Examples

The sales team presented their results using a pie chart for easier comparison.
Each segment of the pie chart shows a department's percentage of the annual budget.
She used a pie chart to illustrate the survey responses in her report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bar chart - a chart with rectangular bars representing values

Etymology

From 'pie', referring to a resemblance to a sliced pie, and 'chart', meaning a visual representation of data.
