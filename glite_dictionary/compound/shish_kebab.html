shish kebab

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪʃ kəˈbɑb / (AmE), / ʃɪʃ kəˈbæb / (BrE)
A dish of marinated cubes of meat, usually with vegetables, cooked on a skewer.
skewered grilled meat dish

Examples

We ordered chicken shish kebab and rice for dinner at the Turkish restaurant.
Each shish kebab was loaded with juicy meat, onions, and green peppers.
He learned to make a delicious shish kebab on the backyard grill last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kebab - dish of pieces of meat roasted or grilled, sometimes on skewers

Etymology

From Turkish 'şiş kebap', meaning 'skewer' (şiş) and 'roast meat' (kebap).
