love seat

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv siːt / (AmE), / ˈlʌv siːt / (BrE)
A small sofa designed to seat two people comfortably.
small sofa for two people

Examples

They bought a new love seat to fit in their small living room.
The cozy love seat was the perfect spot for the couple to relax together.
She recovered the old love seat with a stylish blue fabric.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa - a long, upholstered seat for more than one person
  • Synonym: couch - a long, comfortable seat for sitting or lying on
