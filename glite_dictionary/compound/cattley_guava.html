cattley guava

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætli ˈɡwɑvə / (AmE), / ˈkætli ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
a small tropical shrubby tree (Psidium cattleyanum) that bears deep red or sometimes yellow oval fruit, also called strawberry guava
a tropical shrub with red or yellow fruit

Examples

The cattley guava produces small, dark red fruits that are sweet and tangy.
Farmers in Hawaii often grow cattley guava for its hardy nature and tasty fruit.
She made a delicious jam from fresh cattley guava picked from the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guava - a tropical fruit and the tree it comes from (genus Psidium)

Etymology

Named after English horticulturist William Cattley (1788–1835).
