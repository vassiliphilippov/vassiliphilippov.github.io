bang-up performance

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋ ˌʌp pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈbæŋ ˌʌp pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an excellent or impressive performance
excellent or impressive performance
informal

Examples

The actor gave a bang-up performance in the lead role, earning a standing ovation.
Everyone agreed that the choir's bang-up performance made the evening unforgettable.
She was nervous beforehand, but she delivered a bang-up performance at the piano recital.

Synset Examples

He gave a bang-up performance last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outstanding performance - a performance that is exceptionally good or impressive
  • Antonym: mediocre performance - a performance that is average or uninspired
