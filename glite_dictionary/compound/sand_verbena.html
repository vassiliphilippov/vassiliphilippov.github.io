sand verbena

1. NOUN

/ sænd vərˈbiːnə / (AmE), / sand vəˈbiːnə / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Abronia, native to western North America and Mexico, with clusters of flowers resembling verbena
a flowering plant of the genus Abronia

Examples

The bright purple flowers of the sand verbena dot the desert landscape in spring.
Some species of sand verbena are used in native plant gardens for their vivid blooms.
We spotted a field covered in blooming sand verbena during our hike in Baja California.
