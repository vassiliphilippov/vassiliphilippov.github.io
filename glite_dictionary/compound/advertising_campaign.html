advertising campaign

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vərˌtaɪ.zɪŋ kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪ.zɪŋ kamˈpeɪn / (BrE)
a series of coordinated advertisements and promotional activities designed to achieve a specific marketing objective
coordinated marketing advertisements

Examples

The company's new advertising campaign was launched during the holiday season to boost sales.
A well-designed advertising campaign can help a brand reach a wider audience and increase its market share.
They spent millions of dollars on their latest advertising campaign, hoping to attract more customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing campaign - a coordinated set of actions to promote a product or service
