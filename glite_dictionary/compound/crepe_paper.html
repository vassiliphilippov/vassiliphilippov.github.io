crepe paper

1. NOUN

/ kreɪp ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A thin, crinkled paper, usually colored, used for decorations and crafts.
crinkled decorative paper

Examples

We decorated the entire room with crepe paper streamers in bright colors.
She used pink crepe paper to make paper flowers for the party.
Children love crafting with crepe paper because of its texture and vibrant hues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tissue paper - a thin, soft paper used for wrapping or crafts

Etymology

From French 'crêpe' (creases, wrinkles) referring to the paper's texture.
