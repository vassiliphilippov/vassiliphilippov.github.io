air spring

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
a mechanical device that uses compressed air to absorb shocks and vibrations in vehicles or machinery
mechanical shock absorber using compressed air

Examples

The new car model features an advanced air spring system for a smoother ride.
If the air spring fails, you may notice increased bumpiness while driving.
Heavy trucks often use an air spring to improve load handling and comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shock absorber - a device for absorbing and dampening shock impulses
