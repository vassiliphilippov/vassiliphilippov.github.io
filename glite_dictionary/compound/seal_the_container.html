to seal the container

1. VERB

/ sil ðə kənˈteɪnɚ / (AmE), / siːl ðə kənˈteɪnə / (BrE)
to close a container tightly so that nothing can enter or escape
close a container securely

Examples

Please seal the container before transporting any chemicals to prevent spills.
After placing the leftovers inside, make sure to seal the container tightly.
The instructions say to seal the container and keep it in a cool place.

Synset Examples

We need to seal the container before storing it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seal - to close tightly with a seal
