her Romeo

1. NOUN

/ hɚ roʊˈmiːoʊ / (AmE), / hə ˈrəʊmiəʊ / (BrE)
A woman's romantic partner, likened to the passionate and devoted character Romeo from Shakespeare's play.
a woman's devoted romantic partner

Examples

She always said her husband was her Romeo because of his thoughtful gestures.
After the proposal under the stars, her friends joked that she had finally found her Romeo.
Whenever he writes her poems, she smiles and calls him her Romeo.

Synset Examples

She called him her Romeo after he surprised her with flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Romeo - A passionate male lover; an allusion to Shakespeare's Romeo

Etymology

From Romeo, the male protagonist in Shakespeare's 'Romeo and Juliet', symbolizing a passionate lover.
