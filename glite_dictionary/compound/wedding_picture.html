wedding picture

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a photograph taken at a wedding, usually showing the bride, groom, and their guests
photograph from a wedding

Examples

Their wedding picture was framed and placed on the mantelpiece.
She showed her friends the wedding pictures from her daughter's ceremony.
Every wedding picture captured a special moment of joy and celebration.
