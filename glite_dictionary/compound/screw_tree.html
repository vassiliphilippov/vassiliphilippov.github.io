screw tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈskruː ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈskruː ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical tree or shrub of the genus Helicteres, known for its spirally twisted fruit
tropical tree or shrub (Helicteres)

Examples

The screw tree is easily recognized by its unique, twisted seed pods.
Farmers in India sometimes plant the screw tree along field borders for its medicinal value.
Many herbal remedies use the bark of the screw tree to treat coughs.
