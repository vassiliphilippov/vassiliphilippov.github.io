round scad

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd skæd / (AmE), / raʊnd skad / (BrE)
A small, spindle-shaped fish of the western Atlantic, belonging to the family Carangidae, commonly found in coastal waters.
small fusiform Atlantic fish

Examples

Fishermen caught a large shoal of round scad near the coral reef.
Grilled round scad is a popular dish in some Caribbean cuisines.
The biology class studied the habitat of the round scad during their field trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scad - any of various small fish in the family Carangidae
