coal burning

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊl ˌbɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊl ˌbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of burning coal, especially to produce energy
burning of coal for energy

Examples

The power plant's reliance on coal burning has serious environmental consequences.
Efforts to limit coal burning are key to reducing air pollution.
Many countries are shifting away from coal burning to cleaner energy sources.

Synset Examples

We need to reduce coal burning for environmental reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combustion - the process of burning something
