Great Plains paintbrush

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt pleɪnz ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ɡreɪt pleɪnz ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
A hairy plant with pinkish flowers native to the Great Plains region extending to northern Mexico.
hairy plant with pinkish flowers

Examples

The Great Plains paintbrush brightens prairies with its distinctive pinkish blooms every spring.
Researchers studied how the Great Plains paintbrush adapts to varying soil conditions across its range.
Hikers admired clusters of Great Plains paintbrush along the rocky trail in northern Mexico.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paintbrush - any plant of the genus Castilleja known for its colorful bracts
