hot plate

1. NOUN

/ hɑt pleɪt / (AmE), / hɒt pleɪt / (BrE)
a portable electric device used for heating, cooking, or keeping food warm, typically with one or two burners
portable electric cooking device

Examples

The students used a hot plate to cook pasta in their dormitory.
She left the soup simmering on the hot plate during the meeting.
Because the kitchen was being renovated, a hot plate was their only cooking appliance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - a device used for cooking or heating, typically larger and stationary
  • Synonym: burner - a part of a stove or a portable device for producing heat
