Union flag

1. NOUN

/ ˈjun.jən flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈjuː.njən flæɡ / (BrE)
the national flag of the United Kingdom, commonly known as the Union Jack
national flag of the United Kingdom

Examples

The Union flag was flown above the palace during the official ceremony.
Each corner of the Union flag represents a different part of the United Kingdom.
Tourists often buy souvenirs decorated with the Union flag as a symbol of Britain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Union Jack - the national flag of the United Kingdom, often used interchangeably with 'Union flag'
