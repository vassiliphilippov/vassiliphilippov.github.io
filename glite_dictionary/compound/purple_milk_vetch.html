purple milk vetch

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl mɪlk vɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl mɪlk vɛtʃ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of southern and western Europe, characterized by dense clusters of purple or violet flowers.
European perennial plant with purple flowers

Examples

The hillside was covered in blooming purple milk vetch during spring.
Botanists recorded the presence of purple milk vetch in the region's grasslands.
She admired a cluster of purple milk vetch growing near the old stone wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkvetch - Any of numerous leguminous plants of the genus Astragalus
