tobacco user

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ ˈjuːzər / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ ˈjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who uses tobacco products, especially by smoking, chewing, or snuffing
person who uses tobacco

Examples

The doctor explained the health risks associated with being a tobacco user.
In some workplaces, tobacco users are required to pay higher insurance premiums.
As a former tobacco user, he now advocates for quitting smoking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoker - a person who smokes tobacco products
  • Antonym: nonsmoker - a person who does not use tobacco products
