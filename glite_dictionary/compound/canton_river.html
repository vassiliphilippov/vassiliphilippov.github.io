Canton River

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæn.tən ˈrɪv.ər / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tən ˈrɪv.ə / (BrE)
a river in southeast China, also known as the Pearl River, that flows into the South China Sea
river in southeast China

Examples

The Canton River is vital to the region's economy, supporting shipping and trade.
Many bridges span the Canton River as it winds through Guangzhou.
Pollution remains a concern along the lower stretches of the Canton River.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pearl River - another name for the Canton River
