step dancing

1. NOUN

/ stɛp ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / stɛp ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dance in which precise, rhythmic steps are more important than gestures or postures.
dance emphasizing rhythmic steps

Examples

The group performed traditional step dancing at the festival, captivating the audience with their intricate footwork.
She has been taking step dancing lessons since she was a child, specializing in Irish styles.
Competitions for step dancing often focus on the precision and clarity of each step.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap dancing - dancing involving rhythmical tapping of the toes and heels
