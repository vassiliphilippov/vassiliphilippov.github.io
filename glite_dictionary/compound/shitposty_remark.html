shitposty remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌpoʊsti rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌpəʊsti rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment that is deliberately nonsensical, absurd, or intended to provoke, especially online
deliberately nonsensical or provocative comment
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He made a shitposty remark during the meeting that left everyone confused.
Try not to engage when someone leaves a shitposty remark in the comment section.
Her shitposty remark went over most people's heads, but a few users found it hilarious.

Synset Examples

Don't take his shitposty remarks seriously.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troll - someone who intentionally provokes or upsets others online
  • Degree Lower: banter - playful or teasing remarks

Etymology

From 'shitpost', a blend of vulgar 'shit' and 'post' (an online message), plus '-y' (having the qualities of).
