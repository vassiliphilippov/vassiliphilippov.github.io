bike parking rack

1. NOUN

/ baɪk ˈpɑrkɪŋ ræk / (AmE), / baɪk ˈpɑːkɪŋ ræk / (BrE)
a metal frame or structure where bicycles can be securely parked and often locked
a rack for parking bicycles

Examples

I locked my bike to the bike parking rack outside the library.
The city installed a new bike parking rack near the bus station for commuters.
Several bicycles were neatly lined up at the bike parking rack in the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicycle rack - a rack used for parking or storing bicycles
