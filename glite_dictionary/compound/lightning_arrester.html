lightning arrester

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪtnɪŋ əˌrɛstər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ əˌrɛstə / (BrE)
a device connected to a power line to protect electrical equipment from damage caused by lightning or sudden voltage surges
device protecting from lightning surges

Examples

The building was equipped with a lightning arrester to safeguard sensitive electronics during storms.
After the power surge, the lightning arrester prevented any damage to the transformer.
Engineers recommended installing a new lightning arrester on the main power line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surge protector - device that protects electrical devices from voltage spikes
