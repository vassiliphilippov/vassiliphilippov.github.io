constant blather

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnstənt ˈblæðər / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt ˈblɑːðə / (BrE)
continuous or endless talk, often trivial or meaningless.
endless meaningless chatter

Examples

The meeting was filled with constant blather that didn't address the real issues.
She grew impatient with his constant blather and asked him to get to the point.
After an hour of constant blather, everyone was ready to leave the party.

Synset Examples

I can't stand his constant blather about politics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: babble - incessant or foolish talk
  • Synonym: prattle - silly, idle, or meaningless talk
  • Antonym: silence - absence of sound or speech
