basement-dwelling existence

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsmənt ˌdwɛlɪŋ ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsmənt ˌdwɛlɪŋ ɪɡˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
a lifestyle characterized by living in a basement, often implying social isolation, modest means, or withdrawal from mainstream life
lifestyle of living in a basement, often isolated

Examples

After years of working remotely, he settled into a basement-dwelling existence filled with online meetings and solitude.
Some critics argue that a basement-dwelling existence can lead to social withdrawal and missed opportunities.
She joked about her basement-dwelling existence while showing off her collection of vintage comics online.

Synset Examples

After college, she embraced a basement-dwelling existence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hermit - a person living in solitude, often for religious reasons
  • Synonym: recluse - a person who lives a solitary life and tends to avoid other people
