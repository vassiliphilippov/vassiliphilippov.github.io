lemon drop

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən drɑp / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən drɒp / (BrE)
A small, hard, lemon-flavored candy, typically yellow and shaped like a lemon.
lemon-flavored hard candy

Examples

She offered me a lemon drop from the jar on her desk.
The children loved the sweet and tangy taste of lemon drops.
I hadn't tasted a lemon drop since my childhood visits to the candy store.
