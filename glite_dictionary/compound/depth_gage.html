depth gage

1. NOUN

/ dɛpθ ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / dɛpθ ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the depth of grooves, holes, or other recesses
instrument for measuring depth of grooves or holes

Examples

The machinist used a depth gage to check the depth of the drilled hole.
Make sure the depth gage is properly calibrated before taking any measurements.
She bought a digital depth gage for her woodworking projects.
