bridge hand

1. NOUN

/ brɪdʒ hænd / (AmE), / brɪdʒ hand / (BrE)
the set of cards dealt to and held by a single player in the card game of bridge
set of cards held by a bridge player

Examples

She looked at her bridge hand and realized she had a strong chance to win the game.
Each bridge hand presents a new challenge and requires careful strategy.
After the deal, he examined his bridge hand and planned his opening bid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand - set of cards dealt to a player in card games
