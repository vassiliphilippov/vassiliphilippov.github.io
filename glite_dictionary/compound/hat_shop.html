hat shop

1. NOUN

/ hæt ʃɑp / (AmE), / hæt ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop that sells hats, especially women's hats.
shop selling hats, esp. for women

Examples

She found the perfect summer hat at the new hat shop downtown.
The hat shop displayed an elegant collection of fascinators for the upcoming wedding season.
After years in fashion, she finally opened her own hat shop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millinery - shop or business making and selling women's hats
