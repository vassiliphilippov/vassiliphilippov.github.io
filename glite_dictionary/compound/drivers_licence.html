driver's licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvərz ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvəz ˌlaɪsns / (BrE)
An official document permitting a person to drive a motor vehicle.
official permit to drive a vehicle

Examples

You need a valid driver's licence to rent a car in this country.
She finally passed her test and received her driver's licence last week.
The officer asked to see his driver's licence during the traffic stop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving licence - British English term for driver's licence
  • Synonym: driver's license - American English spelling of driver's licence
