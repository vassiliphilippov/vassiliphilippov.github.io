glamazonian figure

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæm.əˈzoʊ.ni.ən ˈfɪɡ.jɚ / (AmE), / ɡlæm.əˈzəʊ.ni.ən ˈfɪɡ.jə / (BrE)
a woman's body shape that is notably tall and strikingly glamorous
tall, glamorous woman's figure
informal

Examples

Her glamazonian figure caught everyone's attention at the gala.
In the world of fashion, a glamazonian figure is often seen striding down the runways.
She embraced her glamazonian figure and used it to inspire confidence in others.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statuesque - (of a woman) attractively tall and dignified
  • Antonym: petite - (of a woman) attractively small and dainty

Etymology

Blend of 'glamorous' and 'Amazonian', the latter alluding to the tall, strong women of Greek mythology.
