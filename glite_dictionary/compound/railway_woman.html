railway woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌweɪ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈreɪlweɪ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works as an employee of a railway
female railway employee

Examples

The railway woman greeted passengers as they boarded the morning train.
As a railway woman, she worked tirelessly to ensure the safety of the tracks.
Several railway women were honored at the ceremony for outstanding service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railwayman - a man who works as an employee of a railway
