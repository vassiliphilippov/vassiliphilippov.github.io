cost analysis

1. NOUN

/ kɑst əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (AmE), / kɒst əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (BrE)
the process of examining and reporting the separate costs involved in a particular operation or activity
assessment of detailed cost factors

Examples

The company conducted a thorough cost analysis before launching the new product.
A detailed cost analysis revealed unexpected expenses during construction.
Performing a cost analysis helped the team identify areas where they could save money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cost calculation - determining the total cost of a project or operation
