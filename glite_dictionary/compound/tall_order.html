tall order

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a task or requirement that is particularly difficult or challenging to achieve
formidable or difficult task

Examples

Finishing the project by Friday is a tall order, but the team is determined to try.
Convincing him to change his mind will be a tall order after everything that's happened.
Scoring three goals in five minutes was a tall order, but the team almost managed it.

Synset Examples

finishing in time was a tall order but we did it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: challenge - something difficult that requires effort
  • Antonym: easy task - something simple or not challenging to do
