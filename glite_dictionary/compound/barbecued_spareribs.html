barbecued spareribs

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjud ˈspɛr.rɪbz / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuːd ˈspɛə.rɪbz / (BrE)
Pork ribs that are baked or roasted with a spicy barbecue sauce.
pork ribs cooked with barbecue sauce

Examples

We ordered a plate of barbecued spareribs with extra sauce for dinner.
The restaurant is famous for its tender barbecued spareribs and homemade coleslaw.
She loves to prepare barbecued spareribs on the grill for family gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbecue - a meal or gathering at which meat or other food is cooked outdoors on a rack over an open fire or on a grill
