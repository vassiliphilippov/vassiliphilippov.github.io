table salt

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl sɒlt / (BrE)
A white, crystalline substance consisting mainly of sodium chloride, used for seasoning and preserving food.
common edible salt for seasoning food

Examples

She asked him to pass the table salt during dinner.
Most recipes call for a pinch of table salt to enhance the flavor.
I forgot to buy table salt at the grocery store.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salt - a white crystalline substance used for seasoning
