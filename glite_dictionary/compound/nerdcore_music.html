nerdcore music

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːdˌkɔːr ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɜːdˌkɔː mjuːzɪk / (BrE)
a genre of music characterized by lyrics and themes centered around nerd culture, such as gaming, technology, and geek interests
music genre focused on nerd/geek themes

Examples

Many artists create nerdcore music inspired by their love of video games and science fiction.
The convention featured a live performance of nerdcore music that thrilled the tech-savvy audience.
In recent years, nerdcore music has gained popularity on streaming platforms among fans of geek culture.

Synset Examples

He loves nerdcore music; it's quirky and fun.
They performed a nerdcore rap at the convention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chiptune - a style of synthesized electronic music often associated with video games
  • Synonym: geek rock - rock music with themes and lyrics relating to geek culture

Etymology

Blend of 'nerd', meaning an enthusiastic and knowledgeable person about computing or pop culture, and 'hardcore music'.
