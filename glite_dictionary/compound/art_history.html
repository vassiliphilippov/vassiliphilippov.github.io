art history

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
the academic study of the history and development of painting, sculpture, and other visual arts
study of the history of visual arts

Examples

She decided to major in art history at university because of her passion for Renaissance paintings.
The exhibition offers valuable insights for students of art history as well as casual visitors.
His research in art history focuses on the evolution of sculpture in ancient Greece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: history of art - alternative term for art history
