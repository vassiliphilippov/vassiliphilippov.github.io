engineering school

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr.ɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a college or institution that offers courses and degrees in engineering and related technical fields
school specializing in engineering education

Examples

He decided to apply to an engineering school to study mechanical engineering.
Many successful inventors attended an engineering school during their early careers.
The engineering school offers specialized programs in robotics and civil engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical college - a college providing courses in technical and vocational subjects
