shit box

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪt bɑks / (AmE), / ʃɪt bɒks / (BrE)
A very poorly maintained or undesirable place or thing, especially an old or low-quality vehicle or living situation.
very poor-quality place or thing
informal slang vulgar

Examples

No one wanted to buy his shit box of a car, even at a low price.
They moved out of their old shit box as soon as they could afford something better.
The landlord is asking too much rent for that rundown shit box downtown.

Synset Examples

She referred to her tiny apartment as a shit box.
His old car was just a shit box that barely ran.
The rental was a total shit box, falling apart everywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dump - A very dirty or run-down place, often used for homes or buildings
  • Synonym: jalopy - An old, dilapidated car
