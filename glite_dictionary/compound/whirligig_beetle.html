whirligig beetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːlɪˌɡɪɡ ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlɪˌɡɪɡ ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small aquatic beetle that swims rapidly in circles on the surface of water, belonging to the family Gyrinidae
aquatic beetle that circles on water

Examples

The whirligig beetle darted swiftly in circles across the pond's surface.
Children watched a group of whirligig beetles spinning like tiny boats on the lake.
A whirligig beetle can evade predators by rapidly changing direction on the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gyrinidae - family of aquatic beetles including whirligig beetles

Etymology

From 'whirligig', referring to rapid spinning movement, and 'beetle'.
