needle palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈnidəl pɑm / (AmE), / ˈniːdl pɑːm / (BrE)
A small palm tree with long, stiff, needle-like leaves, native to the southeastern United States.
a palm with stiff needle-like leaves

Examples

The needle palm thrives in subtropical gardens because of its hardiness.
Rarely growing taller than five feet, the needle palm is ideal for small landscapes.
Botanists studied the resilience of the needle palm in freezing temperatures.
