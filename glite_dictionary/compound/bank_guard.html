bank guard

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ɡɑrd / (AmE), / bæŋk ɡɑːd / (BrE)
A security guard employed to protect a bank and its customers.
security guard at a bank

Examples

The bank guard noticed suspicious activity near the entrance.
Every bank guard receives special training in emergency procedures.
During the robbery, the bank guard activated the silent alarm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: security guard - someone employed to protect a building or area
