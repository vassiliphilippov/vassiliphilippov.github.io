credit application

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal request submitted to a lender seeking approval for a line of credit or borrowing arrangement
request for a line of credit

Examples

The bank requires customers to fill out a credit application before opening a new credit card account.
She submitted her credit application online and received a response within minutes.
A rejected credit application can impact your credit score if not handled carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan application - an application submitted to request a loan
  • Degree Higher: credit approval - authorization for granting a line of credit
