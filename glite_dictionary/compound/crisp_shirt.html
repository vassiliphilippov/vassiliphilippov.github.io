crisp shirt

1. NOUN

/ krɪsp ʃɝt / (AmE), / krɪsp ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A shirt that is neatly pressed and looks clean and fresh, often with a stiff or sharp appearance.
neatly pressed, fresh-looking shirt

Examples

He always starts the day in a crisp shirt and polished shoes.
A crisp shirt makes a great first impression at interviews.
After ironing, she hung the crisp shirt in the closet.

Synset Examples

He wore a crisp shirt with a stylish collar.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wrinkled shirt - a shirt that is creased and looks untidy
