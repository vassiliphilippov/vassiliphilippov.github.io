to etch a design

1. VERB

/ ɛtʃ ə dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ɛtʃ ə dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
to engrave or carve a pattern or image onto a surface, typically by using acid, a sharp tool, or another method
to engrave a pattern or image onto a surface

Examples

The artist used a needle to etch a design into the glass pane.
They plan to etch a design onto the metal for the jewelry piece.
You need special tools if you want to etch a design by hand on wood.
