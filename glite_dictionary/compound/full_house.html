full house

1. NOUN

/ fʊl haʊs / (AmE), / fʊl haʊs / (BrE)
a hand in poker consisting of three cards of one rank and two cards of another rank
poker hand: three of a kind and a pair

Examples

He won the game with a full house, beating everyone at the table.
When I saw my hand, I realized I had a full house with kings over tens.
A full house usually beats a flush in standard poker rules.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: straight flush - a poker hand containing five cards of sequential rank, all of the same suit
  • Degree Lower: flush - a poker hand of five cards of the same suit, not in sequence
