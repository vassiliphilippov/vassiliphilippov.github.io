corrugated board

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔr.əˌɡeɪ.tɪd bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ʊ.ɡeɪ.tɪd bɔːd / (BrE)
strong cardboard consisting of a fluted corrugated sheet and one or two flat linerboards, commonly used in packaging and boxes
cardboard with a fluted inner layer

Examples

Most shipping boxes are made from corrugated board because of its strength and durability.
The architect used recycled corrugated board for her eco-friendly project.
You can make lightweight furniture out of corrugated board for temporary use.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cardboard - stiff, thick paper-based material used for boxes and packaging
