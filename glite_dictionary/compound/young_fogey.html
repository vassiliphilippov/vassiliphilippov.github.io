young fogey

1. NOUN

/ jʌŋ ˈfoʊgi / (AmE), / jʌŋ ˈfəʊgi / (BrE)
A young person with old-fashioned or conservative views and tastes, particularly in matters of style or behavior.
a young person with old-fashioned views

Examples

Don’t be a young fogey; try something different!
My dad is a young fogey; he refuses to use apps.
He's such a young fogey, always resisting new technology.
