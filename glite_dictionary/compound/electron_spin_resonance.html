electron spin resonance

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛk.trɑn spɪn ˈrɛz.ən.əns / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛk.trɒn spɪn ˈrɛz.ən.əns / (BrE)
a technique in which microwaves are absorbed by paramagnetic substances to study the magnetic properties of electrons
microwave technique for studying paramagnetic substances
medical

Examples

Scientists used electron spin resonance to analyze the free radicals in the sample.
Electron spin resonance provides valuable information about the structure of paramagnetic materials.
The discovery of electron spin resonance has greatly advanced research in molecular biology and chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electron paramagnetic resonance - alternative name for electron spin resonance

Etymology

electron + spin + resonance, reflecting its use in detecting resonant absorption linked to electron spins.
