disc space

1. NOUN

/ dɪsk speɪs / (AmE), / dɪsk speɪs / (BrE)
the amount of storage available for data on a magnetic disk in a computer system
available storage on a disk

Examples

I need to delete some old files to free up disc space on my computer.
The software requires at least 5 gigabytes of disc space to install.
Running out of disc space can cause your system to slow down or crash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disk space - the amount of storage available on a computer disk; alternate spelling
  • Antonym: memory - the part of a computer where data is stored or processed
