game room

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪm ruːm / (AmE), / ɡeɪm ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or other building set aside for amusements and recreational activities, such as playing games, table tennis, or hosting parties
room for games and recreation

Examples

The children spent their afternoon in the game room playing table tennis.
They transformed the basement into a spacious game room for family and friends.
The hotel offers a game room with arcade machines, pool tables, and board games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recreation room - a room for relaxing and recreational activities
