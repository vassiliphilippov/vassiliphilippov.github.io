vitamin-deficiency diet

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪtəmɪn dɪˈfɪʃənsi ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈvɪtəmɪn dɪˈfɪʃənsi ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet formulated to provide adequate vitamins for individuals with vitamin deficiencies
diet for correcting vitamin deficiencies
medical

Examples

After her diagnosis, the doctor recommended a vitamin-deficiency diet to restore her nutrient balance.
Hospitals often prepare a special vitamin-deficiency diet for patients who lack essential nutrients.
A carefully planned vitamin-deficiency diet can help reverse the effects of long-term malnutrition.
