Secretary of State for the Home Department

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkrəˌtɛri əv steɪt fɔr ðə hoʊm dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌsɛkrəˌtəri əv steɪt fə ðə həʊm dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the British cabinet minister who heads the Home Office, responsible for internal affairs such as security, immigration, and law and order
British cabinet minister for home affairs
british

Examples

The Secretary of State for the Home Department announced new measures to tackle online crime.
In Parliament, the Secretary of State for the Home Department faced questions about immigration policy.
The Prime Minister met with the Secretary of State for the Home Department to discuss national security matters.
