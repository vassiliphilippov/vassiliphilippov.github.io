crown beard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraʊn ˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊn ˌbɪəd / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Verbesina, typically having clusters of white or yellow flower heads
plant of the genus Verbesina

Examples

The meadow was full of crown beard blooming in the summer sun.
We identified several species of crown beard during our nature walk.
Gardeners often plant crown beard for its attractive yellow flowers.
