pig's ears

1. NOUN

/ pɪɡz ɪrz / (AmE), / pɪɡz ɪəz / (BrE)
an edible mushroom with a typically brown, often compound fruiting body resembling the shape of a pig's ear
edible brown mushroom

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with pig's ears after the heavy rain.
She foraged for pig's ears to add to her mushroom stew.
Experts warn that some lookalikes of pig's ears are not edible.
