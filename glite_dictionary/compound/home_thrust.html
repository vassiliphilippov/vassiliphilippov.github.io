home thrust

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm ˌθrʌst / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌθrʌst / (BrE)
the main point or decisive focus of an argument, particularly when presented forcefully or effectively
decisive point of an argument

The lawyer's closing argument contained a home thrust that left a lasting impression on the jury.
After circling around the topic for minutes, she finally delivered the home thrust of her presentation.
Despite several distractions, the senator returned to his home thrust about tax reform.
