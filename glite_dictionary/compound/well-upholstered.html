well-upholstered

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛl əpˈhoʊlstərd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ʌpˈhɒlstəd / (BrE)
(of furniture) furnished with high-quality padding and covering, making it comfortable and visually appealing.
furnished with high-quality padding

Examples

The guest room had a well-upholstered armchair that was perfect for reading.
We chose a well-upholstered bench for the hallway to add both comfort and style.
The well-upholstered seats in the theater made the long performance much more enjoyable.

Synset Examples
