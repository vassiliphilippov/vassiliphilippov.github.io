hidden fishhole

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən ˈfɪʃˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən ˈfɪʃˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a secret or concealed place where one goes to fish, known only to select people
secret fishing location

Examples

Few people outside the village know about the hidden fishhole by the riverbend.
Discovering a hidden fishhole can make a fishing trip unforgettable.
