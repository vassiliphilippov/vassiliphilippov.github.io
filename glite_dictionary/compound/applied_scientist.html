applied scientist

1. NOUN

/ əˈplaɪd ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / əˈplaɪd ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who uses scientific knowledge to address practical or real-world problems
scientist applying science to practical issues

Examples

The company hired an applied scientist to improve the efficiency of its manufacturing process.
As an applied scientist, she bridges the gap between research and practical application.
Many applied scientists work closely with engineers to develop new technologies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: theoretical scientist - a scientist focused on developing theories rather than practical applications
