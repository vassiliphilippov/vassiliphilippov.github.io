chess match

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛs mætʃ / (AmE), / tʃɛs matʃ / (BrE)
A formal contest or game played between two chess players, usually as part of a tournament or competition.
contest between chess players

Examples

After hours of intense play, the chess match finally ended in a draw.
Millions watched the world championship chess match unfold online.
He trained for months in preparation for his upcoming chess match against the reigning champion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chess game - an individual game of chess, not necessarily part of a formal match
