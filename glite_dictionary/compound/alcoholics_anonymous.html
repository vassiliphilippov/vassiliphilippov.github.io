Alcoholics Anonymous

1. PROPN

/ ˌælkəˌhɑlɪks əˈnɑnəməs / (AmE), / ˌælkəhɒlɪks əˈnɒnɪməs / (BrE)
an international organization that provides support groups for people seeking to overcome alcoholism
international support group for alcoholism recovery

Examples

After years of struggling with addiction, he joined Alcoholics Anonymous for support and guidance.
Many members credit Alcoholics Anonymous with helping them achieve long-term sobriety.
The principles of Alcoholics Anonymous are based on mutual aid and personal accountability.

Etymology

From 'alcoholic' + 'anonymous'; organization founded in the United States in 1935.
