decision-making process

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ ˈproʊ.ses / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪʒ.ən ˌmeɪ.kɪŋ ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
the series of steps or stages through which a decision is reached, especially in an organization or group
steps taken to make a decision

Examples

The company revised its decision-making process to include feedback from all employees.
Transparency is essential at every stage of the decision-making process.
Her research focused on how culture influences the decision-making process.

Synset Examples

We need to involve everyone in the decision-making process.
Several factors underly the decision-making process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision making - the process of making decisions
