in detail

1. ADV

/ ɪn dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ɪn dɪˈteɪl / (BrE)
thoroughly and including all important particulars; with all the facts, features, or circumstances described individually.
thoroughly; including all particulars

Examples

The report explains the project in detail, covering every aspect from start to finish.
She described her experience in detail so everyone could understand what happened.
The process was not just mentioned but discussed in detail during the meeting.

Synset Examples

he studied the snake in detail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - in a thorough manner; completely

2. ADV

/ ɪn dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ɪn ˈdiːteɪl / (BrE)
with thoroughness and including many specific facts, steps, or features; not generally or superficially
thoroughly; with specifics

Examples

The witness described the incident in detail to the police.
Before making a decision, let's discuss each option in detail.
The instructions are written in detail to ensure no steps are missed.

Synset Examples

The report outlines the findings in detail.
She explained the project requirements in detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in depth - in a thorough and detailed manner
  • Antonym: superficially - in a way that is not thorough or detailed
