cum dumpster

1. NOUN

/ kʌm ˈdʌmpstɚ / (AmE), / kʌm ˈdʌmpstə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar slang term used to insult or degrade someone, typically a woman, by implying extreme promiscuity or that they are an object for sexual use.
offensive slang insult implying promiscuity
slang offensive vulgar

That movie received backlash for referring to a female character as a cum dumpster.
Using the term cum dumpster is deeply disrespectful and should be avoided in any conversation.
He was criticized for posting a comment calling someone a cum dumpster online.
