shark-liver oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑrk ˌlɪvɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːk ˌlɪvə ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty yellow to brown oil obtained from the livers of sharks, used in leather dressing and as a source of vitamin A
oil from shark livers, source of vitamin A

Examples

Some traditional remedies use shark-liver oil for its supposed health benefits.
The tanner applied shark-liver oil to the leather to make it softer.
Supplements containing shark-liver oil are marketed as a source of vitamin A.
