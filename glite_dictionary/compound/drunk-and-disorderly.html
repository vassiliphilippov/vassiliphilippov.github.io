drunk-and-disorderly

1. NOUN

/ drʌŋk ənd dɪsˈɔrdərli / (AmE), / drʌŋk ənd dɪsˈɔːdəli / (BrE)
a person who has been arrested for being drunk and behaving disruptively in a public place
person arrested for drunken, disruptive behavior
informal legal

Examples

The police rounded up three drunk-and-disorderlies outside the bar last night.
After the parade, several drunk-and-disorderlies spent the night in the county jail.
She was charged as a drunk-and-disorderly after causing a scene in the park.

Synset Examples

they delivered the drunk-and-disorderlies to the county jail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public intoxication - the offense of being drunk in a public place
