recreational facility

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkrieɪʃənəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a public place or building designed for people to take part in leisure and relaxation activities
public place for leisure or relaxation

Examples

The city council plans to build a new recreational facility near the river for local residents.
Children spent their afternoons at the recreational facility playing basketball and swimming.
Many neighborhoods lack a modern recreational facility where people can exercise and socialize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leisure center - a building with facilities for indoor sports, exercise, and relaxation
  • Synonym: community center - a place where people in a local area can take part in activities
