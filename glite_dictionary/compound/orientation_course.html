orientation course

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən kɔːrs / (AmE), / ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən kɔːs / (BrE)
a course designed to introduce newcomers to a new situation, environment, or organization
introductory course for newcomers

Examples

All new employees must attend the orientation course before starting their regular duties.
The university offers an orientation course for international students to help them adjust to campus life.
Her orientation course covered safety guidelines, workplace policies, and team introductions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: induction course - a course introducing someone to an organization or new job
