plastic blow molding machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk bloʊ ˈməʊldɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk bləʊ ˈməʊldɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to form hollow plastic objects by inflating heated plastic into a mold
machine for forming hollow plastic objects

Examples

The new factory invested in a state-of-the-art plastic blow molding machine for bottling production.
A technician adjusted the settings on the plastic blow molding machine to improve product quality.
She specializes in repairing industrial equipment such as the plastic blow molding machine.

Synset Examples

The injection blow molding machine is based on an extruder barrel and screw assembly which melts the polymer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: injection molding machine - machine used to shape plastic by injecting molten material into a mold
