wing tip

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋ tɪp / (AmE), / wɪŋ tɪp / (BrE)
A decorative piece of leather on the toe of a shoe with a point extending backward toward the middle of the shoe, often forming a 'W' shape.
decorative toecap on shoes

Examples

He polished his favorite pair of wing tips before the big interview.
The classic wing tip design adds elegance to formal footwear.
Many vintage shoes feature a wing tip for a distinctive look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brogue - a strong outdoor shoe with decorative perforations, often featuring a wing tip

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɪŋ tɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋ tɪp / (BrE)
A style of shoe featuring a pointed toecap with extensions, often in the shape of a 'W', running along both sides of the shoe.
dress shoe with pointed toecap design

Examples

He wore polished wing tip shoes to the interview, making a smart first impression.
Those vintage wing tip shoes have stylish perforations along the edges.
My grandfather always paired his suits with brown leather wing tips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brogue - a strong outdoor shoe with decorative perforations and pinking

Etymology

From 'wing' for the extended toecap shape and 'tip' for the end part of the shoe.
