payroll check

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌroʊl tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌrəʊl tʃɛk / (BrE)
a check issued to an employee as payment of wages or salary
check for payment of wages or salary

Examples

Every Friday, employees receive their payroll check from the accounting department.
She deposited her payroll check at the bank after work.
The payroll checks were delayed due to a technical issue with the company's system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paycheck - a check given to an employee for their salary or wages
