in one sitting

1. ADV

/ ɪn wʌn ˈsɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn wʌn ˈsɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an activity) completed at one time without stopping or taking breaks.
done without breaks or interruption

Examples

She managed to finish the entire puzzle in one sitting.
He ate the whole pizza in one sitting, which surprised everyone.
Many people can't watch an entire season of a show in one sitting.

Synset Examples

He read the whole book in one sitting.
