Kongo language

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑŋɡoʊ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Bantu language spoken by the Kongo people in the tropical regions of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Republic of the Congo, and Angola
Bantu language of the Kongo people

Examples

She is fluent in both French and Kongo language.
The Kongo language has several dialects spoken throughout Central Africa.
He studied the Kongo language for his research on Bantu linguistics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lingala - a Bantu language spoken in the northwestern Democratic Republic of the Congo and Republic of the Congo

Etymology

From Kongo, the native name for the people and language.
