something queer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ kwɪr / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ kwɪə / (BrE)
a thing or event that is strange or unusual
something strange or unusual

Examples

As soon as we arrived at the old house, I felt something queer in the air.
If you notice something queer happening, let me know immediately.
There's something queer about this whole situation that makes me uneasy.

Synset Examples

There's something queer about the way he tells stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queer - strange or unusual (adjective sense)
  • Synonym: odd - strange or different from what is expected
