nervous impulse

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrvəs ˈɪmpʌls / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs ˈɪmpʌls / (BrE)
an electrical signal that travels along a nerve fiber, allowing the transmission of information within the nervous system
electrical signal in a nerve fiber

Examples

A nervous impulse travels rapidly along the axon to reach the next neuron.
The touching of a hot object triggers a nervous impulse that causes you to withdraw your hand.
Certain drugs can block the passage of a nervous impulse and prevent muscle contraction.

Synset Examples

they demonstrated the transmission of impulses from the cortex to the hypothalamus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerve impulse - an electrical signal traveling along a nerve fiber
