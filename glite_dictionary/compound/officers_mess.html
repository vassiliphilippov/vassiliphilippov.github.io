officer's mess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑfəsərz mɛs / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪsəz mes / (BrE)
A dining facility reserved exclusively for officers in the armed forces.
military dining facility for officers

Examples

The regiment's officer's mess is renowned for its excellent food and historic atmosphere.
Only commissioned personnel are permitted to dine in the officer's mess.
After the ceremony, the guests were invited to a reception at the officer's mess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mess - a place where members of the armed forces eat together
