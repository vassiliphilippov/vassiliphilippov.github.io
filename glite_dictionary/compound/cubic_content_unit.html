cubic content unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːbɪk ˈkɑnˌtɛnt ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪk ˈkɒn.tɛnt ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for determining the volume or capacity of an object, typically expressed in cubic meters, cubic centimeters, etc.
unit for measuring volume or capacity

Examples

The dimensions of the container were measured in cubic content units to ensure it met shipping requirements.
Liquid volume is often recorded using standard cubic content units such as cubic centimeters or cubic meters.
Architects frequently calculate the cubic content unit of a structure when designing buildings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volume unit - a unit for measuring the amount of three-dimensional space
