unlicked envelope

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈlɪkt ˈɛn.vəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪkt ˈɛn.və.ləʊp / (BrE)
an envelope that has not been sealed by licking the adhesive flap
envelope not sealed by licking

Examples

She found an unlicked envelope among the pile of mail on her desk.
He handed her the unlicked envelope, asking her to seal it and send it out.
An unlicked envelope may indicate an unread or unsent letter.

Synset Examples

The unlicked envelope sat unopened for days.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sealed envelope - an envelope that has been closed and secured
