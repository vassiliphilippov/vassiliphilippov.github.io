to think too much

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk tuː mʌtʃ / (AmE), / θɪŋk tʃuː mʌtʃ / (BrE)
to overanalyze or dwell excessively on thoughts, often leading to unnecessary worry or difficulty making decisions
dwell excessively on thoughts

Examples

She tends to think too much before making even simple decisions.
If you think too much about the future, it can make you anxious.
I realized I was thinking too much about what others might say.

Synset Examples

Sometimes I worry I just think too much.
He tends to think too much about minor details.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to overthink - to think about something too much or for too long
