redleg cricket

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌlɛɡ ˈkrɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌlɛɡ ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
a species of cricket distinguished by its red legs, commonly found in gardens and grassy areas
a cricket species with red legs

Examples

The redleg cricket is often heard chirping at dusk in rural areas.
Gardeners sometimes find redleg crickets hiding beneath flower pots.
A distinctive feature of the redleg cricket is its bright red legs.

Synset Examples

The redleg cricket is often seen in summer gardens.
