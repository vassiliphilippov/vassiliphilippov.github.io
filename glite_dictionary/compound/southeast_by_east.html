southeast by east

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaʊθˌist baɪ ˈist / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˌiːst baɪ ˈiːst / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point east of southeast, equal to 123.75° clockwise from due north
compass point between southeast and east-southeast

Examples

The ship adjusted its course to southeast by east to avoid the storm.
At sunrise, the wind began to blow from the southeast by east.
On the old map, the treasure is marked southeast by east of the main bay.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: southeast - the compass point midway between south and east (135°)
  • Degree Higher: east-southeast - the compass point between east and southeast (112.5°)
