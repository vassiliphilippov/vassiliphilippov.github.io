point of departure

1. NOUN

/ pɔɪnt əv dɪˈpɑːrtʃər / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv dɪˈpɑːtʃə / (BrE)
the place or starting position from which a journey, enterprise, or process begins
starting place for a journey or process

Examples

San Francisco was the point of departure for our round-the-world cruise.
The meeting served as a point of departure for future negotiations.
Her childhood experiences became the point of departure for her later artistic work.

Synset Examples
