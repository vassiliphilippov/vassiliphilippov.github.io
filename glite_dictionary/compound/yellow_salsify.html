yellow salsify

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈsælsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈsælsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
a European perennial plant (Tragopogon dubius) with hollow stems, long narrow leaves, and pale yellow flowers, naturalized in North America.
European plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with the bright blooms of yellow salsify among the grasses.
Gardeners sometimes mistake yellow salsify for dandelion because of its yellow flower.
Yellow salsify thrives in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salsify - an edible root vegetable related to yellow salsify
