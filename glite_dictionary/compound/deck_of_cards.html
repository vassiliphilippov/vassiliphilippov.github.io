deck of cards

1. NOUN

/ dɛk əv kɑrdz / (AmE), / dɛk əv kɑːdz / (BrE)
a complete set of 52 playing cards used in games such as poker and bridge
set of 52 playing cards

Examples

She shuffled the deck of cards before dealing the first hand.
A magician can amaze an audience with just a deck of cards.
We played several rounds of gin rummy using an old deck of cards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pack of cards - another term for a set of 52 playing cards
