hurricane roof

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜːrɪkeɪn ˌruːf / (AmE), / ˈhʌrɪkən ˌruːf / (BrE)
the uppermost deck on a passenger ship, usually open and exposed to the weather
uppermost deck of a passenger ship

Examples

The captain invited us up to the hurricane roof to view the sunset.
Crew members secured loose items on the hurricane roof before the storm approached.
Passengers enjoyed the fresh sea breeze while relaxing on the hurricane roof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promenade deck - a deck on a ship for passengers to walk on
