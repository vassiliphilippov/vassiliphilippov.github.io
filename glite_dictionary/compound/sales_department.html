sales department

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / seɪlz dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a business responsible for selling its products or services
division responsible for sales in a business

Examples

The sales department exceeded its quarterly targets by introducing a new incentive program.
She applied for a job in the sales department at a large software company.
Our company's sales department works closely with the marketing team to attract new customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing department - division responsible for promoting and advertising products
