warm personality

1. NOUN

/ wɔrm pɚˌsænəˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / wɔːm pɜːsəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
a kind and friendly nature that makes others feel comfortable
kind and friendly nature

Examples

Her warm personality made newcomers feel immediately welcome at the party.
Interviewers are often impressed by candidates who display a warm personality.
Despite her senior position, she is known throughout the company for her warm personality.
