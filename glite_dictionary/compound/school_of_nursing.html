school of nursing

1. NOUN

/ skuːl əv ˈnɜrsɪŋ / (AmE), / skuːl ɒv ˈnɜːsɪŋ / (BrE)
a college or other educational institution where people are trained to become nurses
institution for training nurses

Examples

She was accepted into the school of nursing at her local university.
The school of nursing offers various programs for future healthcare professionals.
After graduating from the school of nursing, he started working at a major hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursing school - institution where nurses receive their training
  • Degree Lower: nurse - person trained to care for the sick or infirm
