Magdalena River

1. PROPN

/ ˌmæɡdəˈleɪnə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌmæɡdəˈleɪnə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in Colombia that flows north from the Andes to the Caribbean Sea
major river in Colombia

Examples

The Magdalena River is vital to transportation and commerce in Colombia.
Many cities along the Magdalena River depend on its waters for agriculture.
The journey up the Magdalena River offers stunning views of the Colombian landscape.

Etymology

Named after Mary Magdalene, reflecting Spanish colonial influence in Colombia.
