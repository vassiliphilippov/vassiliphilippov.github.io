pizza pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpitsə paɪ / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə paɪ / (BrE)
An Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of baked dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients.
Italian dish with dough, sauce, and cheese
american

Examples

We ordered a large pizza pie for the party last night.
Every Sunday night, our family enjoys a homemade pizza pie together.
The restaurant is famous for its thin-crust pizza pie topped with fresh basil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pizza - Italian dish with dough, tomato sauce, and cheese

Etymology

Formed from 'pizza' (from Italian) and 'pie' (English for baked dish with crust).
