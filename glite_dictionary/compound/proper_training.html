proper training

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpər ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒpə ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
instruction or education that is appropriate or sufficient for a particular purpose or task
adequate instruction for a purpose

Examples

The company invested heavily in proper training for all staff members.
Without proper training, employees are more likely to make costly mistakes.
Before using the new machinery, you must complete proper training.

Synset Examples

They provided proper training for new employees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training - the process of teaching or learning a skill or job
  • Antonym: improper training - inadequate or unsuitable instruction for a purpose
