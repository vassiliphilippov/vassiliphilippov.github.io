buried booty

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛr.id ˈbuː.ti / (AmE), / ˈbɛr.id ˈbuː.ti / (BrE)
treasure or valuables that have been hidden underground, often in the context of pirates or secret hoards.
treasure hidden underground

Examples

The pirates searched the island for the legendary buried booty.
According to the legend, a chest of buried booty lies beneath the old oak tree.
Many adventurers dream of discovering buried booty on deserted beaches.

Synset Examples

The treasure map led them to the buried booty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasure - a quantity of precious metals, gems, or other valuable objects
