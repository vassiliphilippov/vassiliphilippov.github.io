Your Maj

1. NOUN

/ jʊr mædʒ / (AmE), / jɔː mædʒ / (BrE)
An informal or humorous way of addressing a monarch, as a shortened form of 'Your Majesty.'
informal address to a monarch
informal

Examples

It's always a pleasure to serve you, Your Maj.
Do you require anything else, Your Maj, before the banquet begins?
Everyone bowed as Your Maj entered the grand hall.

Synset Examples

It's an honor to be here, Your Maj.
Your Maj, the people await your address.
May I present the report to you, Your Maj?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Your Majesty - formal address to a monarch

Etymology

Shortened from 'Your Majesty.' 'Maj' is an abbreviation of 'Majesty.'
