lady-killer

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiˌkɪlər / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiˌkɪlə / (BrE)
a man who is very charming or sexually attractive to women; a man who is notably successful in attracting women
man very attractive to women
informal

Examples

The charming actor was known throughout Hollywood as a true lady-killer.
Despite his reputation as a lady-killer, he was actually quite shy with women.
Rumors spread quickly that the new professor was something of a lady-killer among the students.

Synset Examples

With his charisma, he was a real lady-killer.
He had a reputation as a lady-killer in high school.
The new guy at work is quite the lady-killer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
  • Synonym: heartthrob - a man who is popular with women because of his looks or charm
