Queensland grass-cloth plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwiːnzlænd ˈɡræs klɑθ plænt / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnzlənd ˈɡrɑːs klɒθ plɑːnt / (BrE)
An Australian plant of the genus Pipturus, whose fiber is used for making cloth.
Australian plant used for fiber

Examples

The fiber from the Queensland grass-cloth plant is prized for its strength and durability.
Traditional weavers in Australia have long used the Queensland grass-cloth plant to make cloth.
Botanists study the Queensland grass-cloth plant for its unique properties and ecological value.
