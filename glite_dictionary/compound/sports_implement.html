sports implement

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌɪmpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌɪmplɪmənt / (BrE)
an object or tool used to play a particular sport or carry out a sporting activity
object used in a sport

Examples

The tennis racket is a common sports implement used by players in matches.
He forgot to bring his main sports implement to the cricket game and had to borrow a bat.
Proper care of your sports implement can improve your performance on the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports equipment - items or apparatus used for sporting activities
