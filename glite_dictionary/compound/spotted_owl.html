spotted owl

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑt̬ɪd aʊl / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd aʊl / (BrE)
a large North American owl (Strix occidentalis) with dark brown plumage, a heavily spotted chest, and native to forests from British Columbia to central Mexico
North American owl species with spotted chest

Examples

The spotted owl is often discussed in debates about forest conservation in the Pacific Northwest.
Scientists are monitoring spotted owl populations to track the health of old-growth forests.
A wildlife photographer captured a rare image of a spotted owl perched on a mossy branch.
