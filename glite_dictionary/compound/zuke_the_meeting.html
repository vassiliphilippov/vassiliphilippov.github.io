to zuke the meeting

1. VERB

/ zuːk ðə ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / zuːk ðə ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
to skip or leave a meeting early, often without official permission or notice
leave a meeting early; skip a meeting
informal

Examples

He decided to zuke the meeting to avoid conflict.
I can't believe you just zuked the meeting right after lunch.
Some employees were caught zukeing the meeting during the weekly review.
