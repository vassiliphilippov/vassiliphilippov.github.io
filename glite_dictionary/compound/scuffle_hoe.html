scuffle hoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌfəl hoʊ / (AmE), / ˈskʌfl həʊ / (BrE)
a hoe designed to cut weeds or break up soil by being pushed rather than pulled
a hoe used by pushing, not pulling

Examples

The gardener used a scuffle hoe to remove weeds quickly from the flower bed.
A scuffle hoe is more efficient in loose soil compared to a traditional hoe.
With her new scuffle hoe, she found gardening much easier and less tiring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoe - a garden tool used for weeding or breaking up soil
