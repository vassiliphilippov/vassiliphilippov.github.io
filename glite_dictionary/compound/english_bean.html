English bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ biːn / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ biːn / (BrE)
An Old World upright plant, Vicia faba, grown for its large, flat, edible seeds and sometimes as fodder; also known as broad bean or fava bean.
upright plant grown for flat edible seeds

Examples

The farmer harvested a field full of English beans for the local market.
We served a stew made with fresh English beans and herbs.
English bean plants thrive in temperate climates and provide nutritious seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broad bean - large edible-seeded legume, alternative name for English bean
  • Synonym: fava bean - common edible bean, another synonym for English bean
