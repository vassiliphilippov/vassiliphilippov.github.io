light bulb

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪt ˌbʌlb / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌbʌlb / (BrE)
An electric lamp with a glass housing and a wire filament that produces light when heated by electricity.
electric lamp with a filament

Examples

Please turn off the light bulb when you leave the room to save electricity.
The old light bulb stopped working, so I had to replace it with a new one.
She accidentally broke the light bulb while cleaning the lamp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulb - rounded glass container for lighting
