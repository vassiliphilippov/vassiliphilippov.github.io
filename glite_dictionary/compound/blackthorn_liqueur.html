blackthorn liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌθɔrn lɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈblækˌθɔːn lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic drink made from blackthorn fruit (sloes), typically sweet and flavored
alcoholic drink made from blackthorn fruit

Examples

He offered us a glass of homemade blackthorn liqueur after dinner.
The recipe calls for ripe sloes to infuse the blackthorn liqueur with rich flavor.
They toasted the holiday with a bottle of traditional blackthorn liqueur from the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sloe gin - a liqueur made by infusing gin with blackthorn fruit (sloes)
