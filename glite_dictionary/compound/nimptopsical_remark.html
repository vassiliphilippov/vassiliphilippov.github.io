nimptopsical remark

1. NOUN

/ nɪmpˈtɑpsɪkəl rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / nɪmpˈtɒpsɪkəl rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
An absurd or nonsensical comment, often made humorously or without sense.
absurd or nonsensical comment

Examples

The professor's lecture was disrupted by a nimptopsical remark from a student in the back.
Whenever he's nervous, he tends to make a nimptopsical remark to break the tension.
Her nimptopsical remarks added a sense of playfulness to the otherwise serious discussion.

Synset Examples

His nimptopsical remarks made everyone laugh during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that has no meaning or makes no sense
