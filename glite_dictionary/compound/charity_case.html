charity case

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃær.ə.ti keɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑː.rɪ.ti keɪs / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as needing or receiving charity or assistance from others
person considered in need of charity

Examples

After the accident, he hated being treated like a charity case by his colleagues.
I just want a job; I don't want to be anyone's charity case.
The organization helped her, but she refused to see herself as a charity case.

Synset Examples

He felt like a charity case after losing his job.
She didn’t want to be seen as a charity case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare case - person receiving government welfare support
  • Synonym: beneficiary - person who receives benefits or help
