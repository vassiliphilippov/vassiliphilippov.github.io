giddy limit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪdi ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪdi ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
the highest or most excessive point of excitement, dizziness, or enthusiasm
peak of excitement or dizziness

Examples

After three roller coasters in a row, I had definitely reached my giddy limit.
Her laughter soon pushed the children to their giddy limit.
The constant surprises at the party brought excitement to the giddy limit.

Synset Examples

The party pushed his energy to the giddy limit.
We reached our giddy limit after that roller coaster ride.
