Karenlike behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrən laɪk bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈkærən laɪk bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Entitled or demanding behavior, especially in public, often characterized by excessive complaints or requests to speak with managers.
entitled, demanding public behavior
informal slang

Examples

Her Karenlike behavior at the restaurant included loudly asking to see the manager.
Some people online criticize Karenlike behavior when they see others making unreasonable demands.
His Karenlike behavior embarrassed everyone when he kept complaining about the service.

Synset Examples

He had a Karenlike attitude when asking for manager.
Her Karenlike behavior at the store was embarrassing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entitled - feeling or acting as if deserving special privileges
  • Synonym: demanding - making excessive requests or requirements

Etymology

Formed from the given name 'Karen' (used as slang for an entitled woman) and 'like', meaning 'resembling'.
