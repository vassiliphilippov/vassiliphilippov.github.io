Jamaica apple

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪkə ˈæpəl / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪkə ˈæpəl / (BrE)
a large, heart-shaped tropical fruit with soft, acidic pulp, native to parts of the Caribbean and Central America
a tropical fruit with soft, acid pulp

Examples

The market had baskets of fresh Jamaica apples imported from the Caribbean.
He sliced the ripe Jamaica apple for dessert, enjoying its tangy flavor.
Many tropical fruit salads include Jamaica apple for a unique taste and texture.
