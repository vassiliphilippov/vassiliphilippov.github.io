greyish-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ pɪŋk / (BrE)
Of a colour that is primarily pink with a tinge of grey.
pink tinged with grey

Examples

She chose a greyish-pink dress for the wedding, which suited the pastel theme perfectly.
The sky was painted with beautiful greyish-pink hues at dawn.
The walls in the living room are a soft greyish-pink, creating a calm and elegant atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pink - a pale red colour
  • Degree Higher: grey - a colour between black and white
