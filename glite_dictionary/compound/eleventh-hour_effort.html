eleventh-hour effort

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈaʊɚ ˈɛfɚt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈaʊə ˈɛfət / (BrE)
a last-minute attempt to do something, especially when time is almost out
last-minute attempt

Examples

Despite the challenging circumstances, they mounted an eleventh-hour effort to win the race.
The project was finished on time thanks to an eleventh-hour effort by the whole team.
Her eleventh-hour effort convinced the board to approve the proposal.

Synset Examples

They made an eleventh-hour effort to save the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last-ditch effort - a final, desperate attempt to achieve something
  • Synonym: last-minute - happening or done at the latest possible time before an event

Etymology

From the phrase 'eleventh hour', meaning the latest possible time, from the biblical parable in Matthew 20:1–16.
