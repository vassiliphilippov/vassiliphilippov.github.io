rest day

1. NOUN

/ rɛst deɪ / (AmE), / rɛst deɪ / (BrE)
a day when one does not work, usually set aside for relaxation or recovery
day off for relaxation or recovery

Examples

After a week of intense training, the coach scheduled a rest day for the team.
Sunday is traditionally considered a rest day in many cultures.
She used her rest day to read, nap, and recharge for the coming week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day off - a day when one does not go to work or have duties
