tank driver

1. NOUN

/ tæŋk ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / tæŋk ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a soldier trained to operate and drive a military tank
soldier who operates a tank

Examples

The tank driver expertly maneuvered the vehicle through the rugged terrain.
After his training, he was assigned as a tank driver in the armored division.
The success of the mission depended on the skills of the tank driver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank crewman - soldier who is a member of a tank crew
