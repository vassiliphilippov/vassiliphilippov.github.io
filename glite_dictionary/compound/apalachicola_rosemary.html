Apalachicola rosemary

1. NOUN

/ əˌpælətʃɪˈkoʊlə ˈroʊzmɛri / (AmE), / əˌpælətʃɪˈkəʊlə ˈrəʊzməri / (BrE)
a small, aromatic shrub native to the Apalachicola River area in the southeastern United States, with pinkish flowers and listed as a threatened species
aromatic shrub native to southeastern US

Examples

The Apalachicola rosemary can be found only in certain sandy soils near the Apalachicola River.
Conservationists are working to protect the threatened Apalachicola rosemary from habitat destruction.
The distinctive aroma of the Apalachicola rosemary makes it easily identifiable in the wild.
