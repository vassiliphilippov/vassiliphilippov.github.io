growth rate

1. NOUN

/ ɡroʊθ reɪt / (AmE), / ɡrəʊθ reɪt / (BrE)
The speed at which something increases in size during a particular period of time.
rate of increase in size over time

Examples

The company's annual growth rate reached a new high this year.
Scientists monitor the growth rate of bacteria in the laboratory.
A tree's growth rate can reveal a lot about its environment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decline rate - the rate at which something decreases in size over time
  • Synonym: increase - becoming larger or greater
