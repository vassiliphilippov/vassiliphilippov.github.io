spotted lynx

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑːtɪd ˈlɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈlɪŋks / (BrE)
a wildcat of southern Europe with a short tail and a spotted coat, also known as the Iberian lynx (Lynx pardinus)
wildcat of southern Europe; Iberian lynx

Examples

The endangered spotted lynx is protected in national parks across Spain and Portugal.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of the spotted lynx in Andalusia.
Unlike the Eurasian lynx, the spotted lynx has distinctive dark spots on its fur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iberian lynx - wildcat native to the Iberian Peninsula; same species as spotted lynx
