Kingdom of Morocco

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv məˈroʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv məˈrəʊkəʊ / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, officially a constitutional monarchy, with a predominantly Muslim population; gained independence from France in 1956.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

The Kingdom of Morocco is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes.
After years of colonial rule, the Kingdom of Morocco achieved independence in 1956.
Tourists from around the world travel to the Kingdom of Morocco to visit cities like Marrakech and Casablanca.
