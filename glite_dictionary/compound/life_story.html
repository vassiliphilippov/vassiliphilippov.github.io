life story

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / laɪf ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an account of the events and experiences that make up a person's life
account of a person's life events

Examples

She told me the fascinating life story of her grandfather, who lived through two world wars.
The magazine published an article sharing the life story of a famous athlete.
He decided to write his life story to inspire others facing similar challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biography - a detailed description of someone's life
  • Synonym: autobiography - an account of a person's life written by themselves

2. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈstɔri / (AmE), / laɪf ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
the account or narrative of the significant events and experiences in a person's life, often given in detail
the narrative of a person's life

Examples

Her life story is filled with remarkable achievements and hardships.
He wrote a book detailing the life story of his grandfather.
Many people enjoy hearing the life story of someone who has overcome great challenges.

Synset Examples

Her autobiography tells the life story of a survivor.
He often shares his life story to inspire others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biography - an account of a person's life written by someone else
  • Synonym: autobiography - an account of a person's life written by that person
