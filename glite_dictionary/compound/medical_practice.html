medical practice

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
the professional work of a doctor or group of doctors in providing medical care to patients
the practice of medicine

Examples

After many years of study, she finally opened her own medical practice.
The government is investigating improvements to rural medical practice.
He has retired from medical practice but still advises hospitals as a consultant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine - the science or practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing disease
