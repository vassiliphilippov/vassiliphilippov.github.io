mud shark

1. NOUN

/ mʌd ʃɑrk / (AmE), / mʌd ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a type of fish found in muddy or silty waters, notable for its distinctive appearance
a fish found in muddy waters

Examples

The fisherman was proud when he reeled in a large mud shark from the river.
Local legends tell of a mysterious mud shark lurking in the murky lake.
During their expedition, the team discovered several mud sharks near the delta.

Synset Examples

He caught a mud shark during his fishing trip.
We spotted a mud shark while snorkeling in the lagoon.
