safe hands

1. NOUN

/ seɪf hændz / (AmE), / seɪf hændz / (BrE)
used to refer to a person or group who can be trusted to look after someone or something carefully and responsibly
trustworthy or reliable care; capable guardianship

Examples

You can relax knowing the company is in safe hands with the new manager.
I'm leaving my dog with Julia; I know he's in safe hands.
The project was transferred to another department to ensure it was in safe hands.

Synset Examples

I ensured the documents were in safe hands.
Your pets are in safe hands while you're away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
  • Synonym: reliable - able to be trusted to do what is expected or to look after something
