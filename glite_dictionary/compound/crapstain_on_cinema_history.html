crapstain on cinema history

1. NOUN

/ ˈkræpˌsteɪn ɑn ˈsɪnəmə hɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈkræpˌsteɪn ɒn ˈsɪnəmə hɪstəri / (BrE)
An extremely derogatory term for a movie considered to be of very poor quality and damaging to the reputation of cinema.
derogatory term for a terrible film
slang informal

Examples

Many critics consider that sequel to be a crapstain on cinema history.
Years later, the director still regrets making what he calls a crapstain on cinema history.
Some films are so bad they become memorable as a crapstain on cinema history.

Synset Examples

The movie was a crapstain on cinema history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turkey - a film that is unsuccessful or of poor quality
  • Antonym: masterpiece - an outstanding work of art or craft, especially a film of great quality

Etymology

From 'crap' (slang for something worthless) + 'stain', metaphorically marking a blemish on cinema history.
