genus Aranea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs əˈreɪniə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈreɪniə / (BrE)
A genus of orb-weaving spiders that includes common garden spiders and barn spiders.
genus of orb-weaving spiders

Examples

The genus Aranea contains several species that are commonly found in gardens.
Researchers studied the web patterns of spiders classified under the genus Aranea.
A barn spider is one notable member of the genus Aranea.

Etymology

From Latin 'aranea' (spider) used in taxonomic naming.
