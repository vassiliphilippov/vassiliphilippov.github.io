precious item

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛʃəs ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
an object of great value or sentimental importance, often kept safe or cherished
valued or cherished object

Examples

The museum displayed several precious items from ancient civilizations.
She kept her grandmother's brooch as a precious item in her jewelry box.
Collectors are always on the lookout for rare and precious items at estate sales.

Synset Examples

She kept her grandmother's ring as a precious heirloom.
The ring was the most precious jewel he owned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasure - something very valuable or greatly prized
  • Synonym: heirloom - a valuable object handed down through generations
