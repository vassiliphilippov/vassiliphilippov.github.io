local secret

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊkəl ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
A place, establishment, or fact known primarily to people living in a particular area and not widely known by outsiders.
hidden or little-known place in a local area

Examples

The tiny café down the alley is a local secret beloved by neighborhood residents.
Only the locals know about this beach; it's a true local secret.
During my trip, I found a local secret that wasn't mentioned in any travel guide.

Synset Examples

The shop is hidden in a backstreet, making it a local secret.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hidden gem - a place or thing valued for its quality but not widely known
