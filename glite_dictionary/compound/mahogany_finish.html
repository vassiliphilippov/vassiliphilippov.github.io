mahogany finish

1. NOUN

/ məˈhɑːɡəni ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / məˈhɒɡəni ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
a wood finish that imitates or resembles the appearance and color of mahogany wood
wood finish resembling mahogany

Examples

The coffee table has a deep mahogany finish that matches the other pieces in the living room.
Many antique dressers are restored with a high-gloss mahogany finish to enhance their elegance.
A mahogany finish gives the bookshelf a classic, rich appearance.

Synset Examples

She chose a mahogany finish for her new cabinets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oak finish - a wood finish with the appearance of oak
