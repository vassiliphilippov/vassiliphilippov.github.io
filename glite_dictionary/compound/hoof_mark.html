hoof mark

1. NOUN

/ hʊf mɑrk / (AmE), / hʊf mɑːk / (BrE)
A visible impression made on a surface by the hoof of an animal.
impression made by an animal’s hoof

Examples

The muddy path was covered with hoof marks after the horses passed by.
She identified the animal by the hoof marks left in the soft earth.
Rain had filled the hoof marks with small pools of water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoofprint - a mark or impression left by an animal's hoof
