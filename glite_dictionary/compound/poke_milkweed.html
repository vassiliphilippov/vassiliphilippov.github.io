poke milkweed

1. NOUN

/ poʊk ˈmɪlkwid / (AmE), / pəʊk ˈmɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
A species of milkweed native to eastern North America, noted for its leaves that resemble those of pokeweed.
a species of milkweed with pokeweed-like leaves

Examples

The poke milkweed blooms in shady woodland areas each summer.
Gardeners appreciate poke milkweed for its attractive white and green flowers.
Biologists studied how monarch butterflies lay eggs on poke milkweed rather than other species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkweed - a plant of the genus Asclepias, known for its milky sap
  • Synonym: pokeweed - a North American plant with large leaves and purple-black berries
