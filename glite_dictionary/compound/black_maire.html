black maire

1. NOUN

/ blæk ˈmaɪər / (AmE), / blæk ˈmeə / (BrE)
a northern New Zealand tree (Nestegis cunninghamii) with dense, hard light-brown wood, valued for timber
NZ tree with dense hard wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from the durable wood of the black maire.
A mature black maire can reach impressive heights in New Zealand forests.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the rare black maire from overharvesting.
