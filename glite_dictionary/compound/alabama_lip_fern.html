Alabama lip fern

1. NOUN

/ əˈlæbəmə lɪp fɜrn / (AmE), / əˈlæbəmə lɪp fəːn / (BrE)
a species of fern native to the southeastern United States, northern Mexico, and Jamaica, known for its delicate, narrow fronds
species of fern native to southeastern US

Examples

The Alabama lip fern thrives in rocky woodlands across the southeastern United States.
I spotted an Alabama lip fern while hiking near the Mexican border last spring.
Botanists study the Alabama lip fern for its unique adaptation to dry, shaded habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fern - a nonflowering plant with feathery fronds
