glamazonian traits

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæməˈzoʊniən treɪts / (AmE), / ɡlæməˈzəʊniən treɪts / (BrE)
Characteristics that combine glamorous appeal with strength or power, typically associated with confident, striking women.
traits showing glamour and strength

Examples

She proudly displayed her glamazonian traits on the runway, captivating everyone in the room.
The character's glamazonian traits made her a role model for young girls who value both beauty and strength.
In her latest role, she channels her glamazonian traits into playing a superhero.

Synset Examples

She embraced her glamazonian traits with pride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glamazon - A glamorous, tall, and strong woman
  • Synonym: feminine strength - Qualities or characteristics denoting strength combined with traditional femininity

Etymology

Formed from 'glamazon' (a blend of 'glamorous' and 'Amazon') plus 'traits'.
