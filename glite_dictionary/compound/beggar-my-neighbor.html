beggar-my-neighbor

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛɡər maɪ ˈneɪbər / (AmE), / ˌbɛɡə maɪ ˈneɪbə / (BrE)
A card game for two players in which the object is to win all of the other player's cards.
a two-player card game

Examples

When we were children, we often played beggar-my-neighbor on rainy afternoons.
The rules of beggar-my-neighbor are simple enough for young kids to understand.
After dinner, the siblings decided to have a quick game of beggar-my-neighbor.
