daily bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli brɛd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli brɛd / (BrE)
the basic necessities of life, especially the food needed to sustain oneself each day
food and other basic necessities for daily life

Examples

He works long hours to provide his family with their daily bread.
Farmers depend on the harvest for their daily bread.
Many people struggle to earn their daily bread in difficult economic conditions.

Synset Examples

He earns his daily bread as a tourist guide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustenance - means of sustaining life, especially food
  • Synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life

Etymology

From the phrase in the Christian Lord's Prayer, "Give us this day our daily bread", using "bread" to symbolize all life-sustaining foods.
