mulberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌlbɛri lɪˈkɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌlbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A sweet alcoholic drink made by infusing mulberries in strong alcohol, often consumed as a dessert beverage.
liqueur made from mulberries

Examples

She poured a glass of mulberry liqueur to accompany the dessert.
Homemade mulberry liqueur makes a thoughtful and delicious gift.
They served chilled mulberry liqueur at the end of the meal.

Synset Examples

My aunt makes the best mulberry liqueur.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a sweet alcoholic beverage, often flavored with fruits, herbs, or spices
