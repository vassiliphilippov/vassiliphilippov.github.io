Khalka language

1. NOUN

/ ˈxɑlkə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkælkə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Mongolic language spoken by the Khalkha people, serving as the official language of Mongolia
official language of Mongolia

Examples

The government uses the Khalka language as the medium of instruction in schools.
Scholars have published many works on the grammar of the Khalka language.
She learned to speak the Khalka language after moving to Ulaanbaatar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mongolian - the language of Mongolia, also called Mongolian

Etymology

From Mongolian Халх (Khalh), the name of the largest subgroup of Mongols.
