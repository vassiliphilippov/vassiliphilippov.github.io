Andhra Pradesh

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːndrə prəˈdɛʃ / (AmE), / ˌændrə prəˈdɛʃ / (BrE)
a state in southeastern India, located on the Bay of Bengal
state in southeastern India

Examples

She was born in Andhra Pradesh, which is known for its rich cultural heritage.
Many tourists visit Andhra Pradesh to experience its famous temples and beaches.
The economy of Andhra Pradesh is largely based on agriculture and information technology.

Etymology

From Telugu 'Āndhra Pradeś', with 'Andhra' referring to the people and 'Pradesh' meaning 'region' or 'state'.
