one day

1. ADV

/ wʌn deɪ / (AmE), / wʌn deɪ / (BrE)
At some unspecified time in the future.
at an unspecified future time

Examples

I hope to travel around the world one day.
She promised herself that she would write a novel one day.
Maybe one day we'll meet again under different circumstances.

Synset Examples

He dreams of becoming a successful banker one day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: someday - at some unknown time in the future
