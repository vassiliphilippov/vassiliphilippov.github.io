cultish following

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌltɪʃ ˈfɑloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪʃ ˈfɒl.əʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
A group of people who show intense and uncritical devotion resembling that of a cult, often to a person, idea, or product.
devoted group resembling a cult

Examples

The indie film developed a cultish following after its release on streaming platforms.
She has attracted a cultish following for her unconventional style and outspoken views.
The product's cultish following drove sales far beyond industry expectations.

Synset Examples

The group had a cultish following among young adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cult following - a group of dedicated fans
