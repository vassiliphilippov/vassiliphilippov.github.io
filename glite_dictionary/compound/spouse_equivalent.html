spouse equivalent

1. NOUN

/ spaʊs ɪˈkwɪvələnt / (AmE), / spaʊs ɪˈkwɪvələnt / (BrE)
a person who is in a long-term cohabiting sexual relationship with another, but is not legally married to them
long-term cohabiting partner, not married

Examples

Some employers offer health benefits to the spouse equivalent of an employee.
He referred to his long-term partner as his spouse equivalent when filling out official forms.
Laws regarding the rights of a spouse equivalent can vary widely between countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic partner - a person with whom one shares an ongoing domestic life, especially without formal marriage
