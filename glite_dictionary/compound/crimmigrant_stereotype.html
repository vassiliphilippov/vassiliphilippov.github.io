crimmigrant stereotype

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪˌɡrænt ˈsteriəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪˌɡrænt ˈstɪəriəˌtaɪp / (BrE)
A negative stereotype that associates immigrants with criminal behavior, often used in media or political discourse.
stereotype linking immigrants and crime

Examples

The politician's speech was criticized for reinforcing the crimmigrant stereotype.
Media outlets often face backlash for perpetuating the crimmigrant stereotype.
Scholars are researching the impact of the crimmigrant stereotype on public perception of immigration.

Synset Examples

He was angered by the crimmigrant stereotype in the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xenophobia - fear or dislike of people from other countries
  • Synonym: prejudice - preconceived judgement or opinion, often negative

Etymology

Blend of 'criminal' and 'immigrant', plus 'stereotype'.
