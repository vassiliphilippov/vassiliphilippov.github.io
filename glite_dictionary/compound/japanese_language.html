Japanese language

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Japanese people, primarily in Japan
the main language of Japan

Examples

She is studying Japanese language at university to become a translator.
The Japanese language uses three different scripts: kanji, hiragana, and katakana.
Fluency in the Japanese language is required for this job in Tokyo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese - the language spoken in Japan
  • Antonym: English language - the language spoken in England, the US, and other countries
