founding anniversary

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaʊndɪŋ ænɪˌvɝːsəri / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndɪŋ ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri / (BrE)
the date or celebration marking the establishment of an organization, institution, city, or similar entity
date marking establishment of something

Examples

The school organized a concert to celebrate its founding anniversary.
Several dignitaries attended the company's founding anniversary ceremony.
Every year, the museum hosts special events on its founding anniversary.

Synset Examples

The city held a jubilee for its founding anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anniversary - the annual recurrence of a date marking a notable event
