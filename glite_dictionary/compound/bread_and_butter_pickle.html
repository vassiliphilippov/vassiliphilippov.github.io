bread and butter pickle

1. NOUN

/ brɛd ənd ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˈpɪkəl / (AmE), / brɛd ənd ˈbʌtə ˈpɪkəl / (BrE)
A type of sweet pickle made from thinly sliced cucumbers, typically pickled with onions, vinegar, and sugar.
sweet pickle of sliced cucumbers

Examples

I love adding bread and butter pickles to my burgers for a sweet crunch.
She made a large batch of bread and butter pickles from her garden cucumbers.
The sandwich was served with a side of bread and butter pickle slices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gherkin - a small pickled cucumber

Etymology

Named for the belief that a simple sandwich of these pickles with bread and butter was once a staple meal.
