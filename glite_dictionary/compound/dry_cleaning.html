dry cleaning

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌkliːnɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of cleaning clothes or fabrics using a solvent other than water.
cleaning clothes with chemical solvent

Examples

I dropped my suit off for dry cleaning because it can't be washed in water.
The dry cleaning was ready for pickup in two days.
Some stains are difficult to remove without professional dry cleaning services.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: laundering - the act of washing clothes with water and detergent
