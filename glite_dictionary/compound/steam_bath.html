steam bath

1. NOUN

/ stim bæθ / (AmE), / stiːm bɑːθ / (BrE)
A bath in which the body is exposed to steam in order to induce sweating, often as part of a health or cleansing ritual.
bath involving exposure to steam

Examples

After a long workout, he relaxed in the steam bath to ease his sore muscles.
The hotel spa offers a traditional steam bath experience for its guests.
Many people enjoy a steam bath as part of their weekly self-care routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauna - a room used as a hot-air or steam bath for cleaning and refreshing the body

Many people believe that taking a steam bath helps to cleanse the skin and improve circulation.
