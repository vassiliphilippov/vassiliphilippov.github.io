real clanker

1. NOUN

/ ˈriəl ˈklæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ˈklæŋkə / (BrE)
an old, noisy, and unreliable vehicle, especially a car that frequently breaks down
noisy, unreliable vehicle
informal

Examples

My neighbor drives a real clanker that rattles every time it starts.
Despite the fancy paint job, it's actually a real clanker under the hood.
She was embarrassed to show up in such a real clanker at the reunion.

Synset Examples

The old car was a real clanker, always breaking down.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated car
  • Synonym: lemon - a car that is defective or constantly gives problems
