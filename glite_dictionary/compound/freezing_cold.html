freezing cold

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriːzɪŋ koʊld / (AmE), / ˈfriːzɪŋ kəʊld / (BrE)
extremely or unpleasantly cold, as if at or below the freezing point
extremely cold temperature

Examples

Despite his thick jacket, the freezing cold wind cut right through him.
We woke up to a freezing cold morning with frost covering the ground.
She dipped her hand into the freezing cold water and gasped in shock.

Synset Examples

The water was freezing cold when I jumped in.
He forgot his coat in the freezing cold weather.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cold - having a low temperature, especially when compared to the temperature of the human body
  • Synonym: icy - very cold, covered with or resembling ice
