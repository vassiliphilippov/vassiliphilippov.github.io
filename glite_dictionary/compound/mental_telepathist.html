mental telepathist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛntəl təˈlɛpəθɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl tɛˈlɛpəθɪst / (BrE)
a person who claims to have the ability to communicate thoughts directly from mind to mind, without using normal senses
person claiming telepathic ability

Examples

The book tells the story of a mental telepathist who solves mysteries using her unique abilities.
Many doubted his claim to be a mental telepathist, but he insisted he could read minds.
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a mental telepathist and communicating silently with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telepath - a person who claims to have telepathic powers
  • Synonym: mind reader - a person who can supposedly read another's thoughts
  • Synonym: clairvoyant - a person who claims to have a supernatural ability to perceive events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact

Etymology

From 'mental' (relating to the mind) and 'telepathist' (from 'telepathy', influenced by Greek 'tele' meaning 'distant' and 'pathos' meaning 'feeling').
