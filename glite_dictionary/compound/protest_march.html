protest march

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.tɛst mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tɛst mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an organized public march by a group of people to express opposition or demand change, usually directed at an authority or government
organized march to express opposition

Examples

Thousands took part in the protest march to demand fair wages.
A protest march was held outside the parliament building last weekend.
The city issued permits for the planned protest march through downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demonstration - a public meeting or march protesting against something or expressing views on a political issue
