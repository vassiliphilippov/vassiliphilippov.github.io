cellular phone

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛljələr foʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɛljʊlə fəʊn / (BrE)
a portable telephone that connects to a network via radio signals, allowing for use over a wide area
portable telephone using cellular network

Examples

She bought a new cellular phone because her old one stopped working.
It is now common for children to carry a cellular phone for safety reasons.
The cellular phone rang loudly during the meeting, causing a distraction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mobile phone - portable telephone using a mobile network

Etymology

From 'cellular', referring to the network of 'cells' (small geographic areas) that make up the service area, combined with 'phone' (short for 'telephone').
