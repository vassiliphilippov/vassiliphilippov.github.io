saddle soap

1. NOUN

/ ˈsædl ˌsoʊp / (AmE), / ˈsædl ˌsəʊp / (BrE)
a mild soap used to cleanse and condition leather, especially saddles and other leather goods
soap for cleaning and conditioning leather

Examples

He used a damp cloth and some saddle soap to clean his riding boots.
To keep the leather supple, apply saddle soap regularly after brushing off any dirt.
The instructions recommend using saddle soap before polishing your leather belt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leather conditioner - a substance for softening and preserving leather
  • Degree Lower: soap - a substance used for washing and cleaning
