dietary intake

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪəˌtɛri ˈɪnˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪətəri ˈɪnteɪk / (BrE)
the types and amounts of food and drink consumed by a person or group
types and amounts of food consumed

Examples

Monitoring your dietary intake is important for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
Children's dietary intake should include a variety of fruits and vegetables.
She kept a journal of her dietary intake to help identify food allergies.

Synset Examples

The nutritionist reviewed my dietary intake closely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition - the process of obtaining the food necessary for health and growth
  • Synonym: diet - the kinds of food that a person or animal habitually eats
