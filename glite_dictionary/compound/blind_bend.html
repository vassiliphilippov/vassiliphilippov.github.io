blind bend

1. NOUN

/ blaɪnd bɛnd / (AmE), / blaɪnd bend / (BrE)
A bend or curve in a road that is impossible or very difficult to see around while driving, making it dangerous to proceed without caution.
a curve in a road with obscured visibility

Examples

Be extra careful when approaching the blind bend on the country lane.
A car suddenly appeared as I rounded the blind bend near the village.
They installed mirrors to improve visibility at the dangerous blind bend by the school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharp bend - a bend in the road with a very tight curve
  • Synonym: blind corner - a turn in the road where oncoming traffic is hidden from view
