carrot cruncher

1. NOUN

/ ˈkærət ˈkrʌntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkærət ˈkrʌntʃə / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who eats mainly healthy food, especially vegetables
person who eats a lot of vegetables
informal

Examples

The new nutritionist is a real carrot cruncher who recommends lots of vegetables.
Since turning vegan, he's become quite the carrot cruncher.
They teased her for being a carrot cruncher at the barbecue.

Synset Examples

As a carrot cruncher, she avoids junk food entirely.
The carrot cruncher shared her favorite salad recipe with us.
The carrot cruncher in our group loves healthy snacks.
