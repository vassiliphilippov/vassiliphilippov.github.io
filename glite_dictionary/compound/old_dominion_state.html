Old Dominion State

1. PROPN

/ ˌoʊld dəˈmɪnjən steɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊld dəˈmɪnjən steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the US state of Virginia, one of the original 13 colonies and a member of the former Confederate States.
nickname for Virginia (US state)
american

Examples

She was born and raised in the Old Dominion State, enjoying the rich history and beautiful landscapes.
Many Civil War battles took place in the Old Dominion State.
The university attracts students from all across the Old Dominion State.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Virginia - a state in the eastern United States; one of the original 13 colonies

Etymology

Refers to Virginia's status as the oldest of the overseas dominions of the English crown.
