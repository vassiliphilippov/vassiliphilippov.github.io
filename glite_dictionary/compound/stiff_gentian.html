stiff gentian

1. NOUN

/ stɪf ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / stɪf ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a gentian plant of eastern North America with clusters of bristly blue flowers
a North American gentian with blue flowers

Examples

We spotted a beautiful stiff gentian growing near the edge of the forest.
The stiff gentian blooms late in the season, adding bright color to autumn meadows.
Gardeners often seek out stiff gentians for their striking blue clusters of flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentian - a plant of the genus Gentiana, known for its bright blue or purple flowers
