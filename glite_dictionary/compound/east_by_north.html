east by north

1. NOUN

/ ist baɪ nɔrθ / (AmE), / iːst baɪ nɔːθ / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point north of due east, or at an angle of 78°45′ clockwise from due north
compass point between east and east-northeast

Examples

The ship set its course to east by north to avoid the rocky coastline.
According to the old map, the treasure is buried three miles east by north of the lighthouse.
The wind shifted suddenly from east to east by north, changing the direction of the sails.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: east - the cardinal compass point at 90°, directly opposite west
  • Degree Higher: east-northeast - the compass point one point farther north than east by north
