sawing wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔɪŋ wʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːɪŋ wʊd / (BrE)
the act of cutting wood with a saw
cutting wood with a saw

Examples

He spent all morning in the garage sawing wood for the new bookshelf.
The steady sound of someone sawing wood came from next door.
After gathering the planks, she began sawing wood to build the frame.

Synset Examples

We spent the afternoon in the workshop, sawing wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saw - a hand tool for cutting wood or other materials
