hunting knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌn.tɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈhʌn.tɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
a large, sharp knife with a sturdy blade and a handle shaped to fit the grip, designed primarily for use while hunting
large knife for hunting tasks

Examples

He always carried a hunting knife when he went camping in the woods.
She used her hunting knife to prepare food and cut kindling for the fire.
A sturdy hunting knife can be a valuable tool in a survival situation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowie knife - a large hunting knife with a single-edged blade
