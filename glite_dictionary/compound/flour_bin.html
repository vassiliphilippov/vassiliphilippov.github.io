flour bin

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊɚ bɪn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə bɪn / (BrE)
a container or storage bin used for holding flour, typically in a kitchen or bakery
container for storing flour

Examples

She scooped a cup of flour from the flour bin to start baking the bread.
The bakery refills the large flour bin every morning before opening.
Please make sure the flour bin is tightly closed to keep out moisture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bin - a container for storage
