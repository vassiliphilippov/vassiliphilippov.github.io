enlisted man

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈlɪstɪd mæn / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪd man / (BrE)
a male member of the armed forces who is not a commissioned officer
male non-officer in the military

Examples

After serving as an enlisted man for four years, he decided to apply to officer training school.
The army relies on the dedication and hard work of every enlisted man and woman.
During the ceremony, several enlisted men were commended for their bravery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlisted person - a non-officer member of the armed forces (any gender)
  • Antonym: officer - a person holding a position of authority in the armed forces
