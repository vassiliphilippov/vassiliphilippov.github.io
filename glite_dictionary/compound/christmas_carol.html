Christmas carol

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs ˈkærəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˈkærəl / (BrE)
a joyful religious song sung at Christmas to celebrate the birth of Jesus Christ
religious song sung at Christmas

Examples

We sang our favorite Christmas carol by the fireplace on Christmas Eve.
A group of children performed a traditional Christmas carol outside the church.
Hearing a Christmas carol always puts me in the holiday spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carol - a religious song or hymn, especially one sung at Christmas
