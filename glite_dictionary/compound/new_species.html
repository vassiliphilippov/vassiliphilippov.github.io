new species

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈspiːʃiz / (AmE), / njuː ˈspiːsiːz / (BrE)
a group of organisms that has not previously been identified or classified by scientists
previously unidentified biological group

Examples

Scientists recently discovered a new species of frog in the Amazon rainforest.
Every year, marine biologists identify dozens of new species in the deep sea.
The new species was named after its unique striped coloration.

Synset Examples

Antarctican researchers are uncovering new species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: species - a group of living organisms consisting of similar individuals
