laugh a minute

1. NOUN

/ læf ə ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / lɑːf ə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
a situation, event, or person that is very amusing or entertaining
very funny or entertaining situation

Examples

The new sitcom is a laugh a minute and had the whole family in stitches.
Traveling with him is guaranteed to be a laugh a minute experience.
She’s such a laugh a minute that every party feels exciting when she’s there.

Synset Examples

With friends like these, it’s always laugh a minute.
The comedy show was laugh a minute from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riot - a person or thing that is very amusing
