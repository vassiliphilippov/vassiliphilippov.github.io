royally offended

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɔɪəli əˈfɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəli əˈfɛndɪd / (BrE)
extremely offended; deeply hurt or insulted, often to an exaggerated or dramatic degree
extremely offended; deeply insulted
informal

Examples

She was royally offended when her ideas were dismissed so quickly.
After being kept waiting for hours, the guest felt royally offended by the staff's attitude.
If you forget his birthday, he'll be royally offended and probably won't speak to you for days.

Synset Examples

He was royally offended by the unfair comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deeply offended - very strongly offended
  • Degree Lower: mildly offended - slightly or only a little offended
