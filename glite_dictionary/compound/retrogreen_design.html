retrogreen design

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛtroʊˈɡrin dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈɡriːn dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a style of design that combines vintage or retro aesthetics with eco-friendly, sustainable practices and materials
eco-friendly design with retro style

Examples

The new café features a retrogreen design with upcycled furniture and energy-efficient lighting.
Architects are embracing retrogreen design to merge classic looks with sustainability.
Her apartment's retrogreen design impressed guests by blending vintage charm and eco-friendly innovation.

Synset Examples

His retrogreen design uses sustainable materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green design - design focused on environmental sustainability

Etymology

Formed from 'retro' (referring to styles from the past) and 'green' (meaning environmentally friendly).
