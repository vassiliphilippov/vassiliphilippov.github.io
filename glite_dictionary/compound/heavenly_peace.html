heavenly peace

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvənli pis / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli piːs / (BrE)
A state of perfect tranquility, calmness, or bliss, as if in heaven.
perfect tranquility or bliss

Examples

After a long day, Sarah found heavenly peace while sitting by the tranquil lake.
Many seek heavenly peace through meditation and quiet reflection.
The gentle music brought a sense of heavenly peace to the entire room.

Synset Examples

The children sang a lullaby, wishing him to sleep in heavenly peace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tranquility - a state of calm and peacefulness
  • Synonym: bliss - perfect happiness; great joy
  • Antonym: turmoil - a state of great disturbance or confusion
