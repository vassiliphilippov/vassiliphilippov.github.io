nude statue

1. NOUN

/ nud ˈstætʃu / (AmE), / njuːd ˈstætʃuː / (BrE)
a statue representing a naked human figure
sculpture of a naked human

Examples

The art museum recently acquired a beautiful nude statue from the 19th century.
Tourists gathered in the square to take photos of the famous nude statue in the fountain.
Some people consider the display of a nude statue in public spaces to be controversial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statue - a carved or cast figure of a person or animal, especially one that is life-size or larger
  • Synonym: nude - a naked human figure, especially as the subject of a painting, sculpture, or photograph
