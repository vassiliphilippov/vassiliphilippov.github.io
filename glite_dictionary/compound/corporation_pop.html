corporation pop

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɔrpəˈreɪʃən pɑp / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃən pɒp / (BrE)
mainstream music produced by major companies, often characterized by commercial appeal and lack of artistic originality
mainstream music by major companies

Examples

Many critics argue that corporation pop values profit over artistic innovation.
She prefers indie rock to corporation pop, which she finds formulaic.
The radio station only plays the latest corporation pop singles.

Synset Examples

The latest trends in music are all about corporation pop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop music - popular music genre with broad appeal
