slough grass

1. NOUN

/ slaʊ ɡræs / (AmE), / slaʊ ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A species of North American cordgrass with leaves that have dry, membranous margins and glumes bearing long awns.
a North American cordgrass species

Examples

The marsh was covered in dense patches of slough grass swaying in the breeze.
Biologists studied the habitat preferences of slough grass in the wetland ecosystem.
We walked along the riverbank, noting the tall slough grass growing near the water's edge.
