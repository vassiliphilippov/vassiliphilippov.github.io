oil painter

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
an artist who creates paintings using oil-based paints
an artist who uses oil paints

Examples

The famous oil painter captured the beauty of the landscape in vivid colors.
As an aspiring oil painter, she spent years perfecting her technique.
The exhibition featured works by a renowned nineteenth-century oil painter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: watercolorist - an artist who paints with watercolors
  • Synonym: painter - an artist who paints pictures
