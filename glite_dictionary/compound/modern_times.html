modern times

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɚn taɪmz / (AmE), / ˈmɒdn taɪmz / (BrE)
the period characterized by the conditions, values, and ideas of the present age; contemporary era
the present age or contemporary era

Examples

Many inventions that we depend on every day did not exist in modern times.
Discussions about climate change have become more urgent in modern times.
In modern times, people rely heavily on technology to stay connected.

Synset Examples

in modern times like these

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ancient times - the distant past, especially before the Middle Ages
  • Antonym: medieval times - the period in European history from about the 5th to the late 15th centuries
  • Synonym: contemporary - belonging to or occurring in the present
