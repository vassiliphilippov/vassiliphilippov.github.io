personal organiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrsənl ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈɔːɡənaɪzə / (BrE)
a small portable electronic device used to manage personal information such as appointments, contacts, and notes; can also function as an alarm clock, calculator, and more
hand-held electronic device for managing personal information

Examples

I rely on my personal organiser to keep track of meetings and deadlines.
She set a reminder on her personal organiser so she wouldn't forget her doctor's appointment.
Before smartphones became common, many people used a personal organiser to store their contacts and notes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PDA - portable digital assistant; another term for a personal organiser
