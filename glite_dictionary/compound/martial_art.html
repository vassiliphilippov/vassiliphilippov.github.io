martial art

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrtʃəl ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃəl ɑːt / (BrE)
any of various traditional systems of combat and self-defense, often originating in Asia, that are practiced as sports or for self-improvement
combat system or practice, often Asian

Examples

He has been practicing martial art forms since he was a child.
The discipline required for martial art training helps students both physically and mentally.
Many films showcase spectacular martial art choreography during fight scenes.

Synset Examples

he had a black belt in the martial arts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: karate - a Japanese martial art using striking techniques
  • Synonym: judo - a Japanese martial art focusing on throws and holds
  • Synonym: boxing - a combat sport with punches

Etymology

From Latin 'martialis' (of Mars, the Roman god of war) + English 'art.'
