huge gulf

1. NOUN

/ hjudʒ ɡʌlf / (AmE), / hjuːdʒ ɡʌlf / (BrE)
A large or significant difference or separation between two people, groups, ideas, or situations.
a large difference or separation

Examples

The report revealed a huge gulf between the incomes of the wealthy and the poor.
There is still a huge gulf between what the politicians promise and what they deliver.
A huge gulf exists between the management and the workers regarding company policy.

Synset Examples

There's a huge gulf between our expectations and reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chasm - a profound difference or gap between viewpoints or feelings
  • Synonym: divide - a clear separation or difference between two groups or ideas
