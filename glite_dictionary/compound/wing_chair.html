wing chair

1. NOUN

/ wɪŋ tʃɛr / (AmE), / wɪŋ tʃɛː / (BrE)
An easy chair with side extensions (wings) on either side of a high back, designed to shield the sitter from drafts or trap heat from a fireplace.
large easy chair with side wings

Examples

She settled into the wing chair by the fireplace with a good book.
The antique wing chair added elegance to the library.
He dozed off in the old, comfortable wing chair after dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
