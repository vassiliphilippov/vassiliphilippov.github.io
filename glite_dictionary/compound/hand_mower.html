hand mower

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈmoʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / hænd ˈməʊ.ə / (BrE)
a lawn mower that is operated manually by pushing it, without a motor
manual lawn mower

Examples

He prefers using a hand mower because it's quieter and better for the environment.
I spent the afternoon pushing the hand mower across the backyard.
Our old hand mower is still sharp and works well for small lawns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lawn mower - a machine for cutting the grass on a lawn
  • Antonym: power mower - a lawn mower powered by a motor
