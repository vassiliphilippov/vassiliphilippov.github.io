rolling pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlɪŋ pɪn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ pɪn / (BrE)
A kitchen utensil consisting of a cylindrical rod with handles at each end, used for rolling out dough.
utensil for rolling out dough

Examples

She used a rolling pin to flatten the cookie dough before cutting out shapes.
The old rolling pin belonged to her grandmother and was made of solid wood.
If you don't have a rolling pin, you can use a clean wine bottle as a substitute.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry board - a flat board on which pastry dough is rolled out
