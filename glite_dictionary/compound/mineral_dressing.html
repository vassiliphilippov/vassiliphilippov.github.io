mineral dressing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnərəl ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of crushing and separating ore to extract valuable minerals or remove waste material, using various techniques
ore processing for valuable minerals

Examples

Modern mineral dressing techniques include crushing, grinding, and flotation to separate valuable materials from ore.
He specialized in mineral dressing and introduced efficient methods in the mining industry.
Proper mineral dressing ensures that waste is minimized and precious metals are recovered effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ore dressing - the process of separating metal ore from other material
  • Synonym: beneficiation - the process of improving the concentration of valuable minerals in ore
