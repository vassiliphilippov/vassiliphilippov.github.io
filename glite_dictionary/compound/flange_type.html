flange type

1. NOUN

/ flændʒ taɪp / (AmE), / flændʒ taɪp / (BrE)
a particular design or style of a flange, defined by its shape, dimensions, or method of attachment
specific design or style of a flange

Examples

Each application may require a different flange type depending on the operating conditions.
Weld neck is a common flange type used in high-pressure systems.
It's important to select the correct flange type for compatibility with existing equipment.
