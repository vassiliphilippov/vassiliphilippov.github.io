Prince of Darkness

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪns əv ˈdɑːrknəs / (AmE), / ˈprɪns əv ˈdɑːknəs / (BrE)
(in Judeo-Christian and Islamic belief) the chief spirit of evil; another name for Satan, the adversary of God and master of Hell.
another name for Satan, the chief evil spirit

Examples

In the novel, the Prince of Darkness manipulates humans to fulfill his evil plans.
Medieval art often depicts the Prince of Darkness as a terrifying and powerful figure.
Some people believe the Prince of Darkness tempts individuals to stray from the path of good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Satan - the supreme evil spirit and adversary of God in Christian and Islamic belief
  • Synonym: devil - evil spirit; especially the chief evil spirit in Christianity and Islam
  • Antonym: God - the supreme being, creator and ruler of the universe in monotheistic religions
