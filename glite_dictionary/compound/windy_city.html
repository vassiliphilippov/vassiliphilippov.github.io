Windy City

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪndi ˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈwɪndi ˌsɪti / (BrE)
a popular nickname for Chicago, the largest city in Illinois, known for its bustling activity and proximity to Lake Michigan
nickname for Chicago

Examples

Many tourists visit the Windy City to experience its fascinating museums and deep-dish pizza.
After moving to the Windy City, she quickly adapted to the vibrant urban lifestyle.
The Windy City hosted a major international music festival last summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Chicago - the largest city in Illinois, also called the Windy City

Etymology

Refers to Chicago, possibly referencing both its weather and political climate; popularized in the late 19th century.
