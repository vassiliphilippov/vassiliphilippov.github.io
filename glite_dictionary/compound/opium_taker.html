opium taker

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpiəm ˈteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈəʊpiəm ˈteɪkə / (BrE)
a person who habitually uses or is addicted to opium
person addicted to opium

Examples

In the novel, the mysterious opium taker spends his days wandering the foggy streets of London.
After years as an opium taker, he struggled to regain his health and relationships.
Authorities tried to help habitual opium takers break their dangerous addiction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opium addict - a person addicted to opium
