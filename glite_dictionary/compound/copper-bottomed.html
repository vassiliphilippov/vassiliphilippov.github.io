copper-bottomed

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːpɚˈbɑːt̬əmd / (AmE), / ˌkɒpəˈbɒtənd / (BrE)
Having a bottom made of or sheathed with copper.
having a bottom of copper

Examples

The chef preferred copper-bottomed saucepans for their excellent heat distribution.
A copper-bottomed ship was more resistant to biofouling in the 19th century.
She admired the vintage copper-bottomed kettle on the stove.
