single prop

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl prɑp / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl prɒp / (BrE)
an aircraft powered by a single propeller
single-propeller aircraft

Examples

He learned to fly in a single prop before moving on to larger aircraft.
The airfield was busy, with several single props taking off for short training hops.
For their weekend trip, they rented a small single prop to reach the remote island.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: twin prop - an aircraft powered by two propellers
