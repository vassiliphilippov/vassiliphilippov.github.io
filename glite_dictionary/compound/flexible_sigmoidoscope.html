flexible sigmoidoscope

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɛksəbəl sɪɡˈmɔɪdoʊˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈflɛksɪbəl sɪɡˈmɔɪdəskəʊp / (BrE)
a thin, flexible instrument for examining the interior of the sigmoid colon, typically used in medical procedures
medical instrument for examining the sigmoid colon
medical

Examples

The doctor used a flexible sigmoidoscope to investigate the source of the patient's rectal bleeding.
A routine screening may involve the insertion of a flexible sigmoidoscope to examine the lower part of the colon.
With proper training, nurses can operate a flexible sigmoidoscope safely and effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sigmoidoscope - an instrument for examining the sigmoid colon

Etymology

From 'flexible' (bendable) and 'sigmoidoscope' (from 'sigmoid', S-shaped part of the colon, and Greek 'skopein', to look).
