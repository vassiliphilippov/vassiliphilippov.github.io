grooming trend

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruː.mɪŋ trɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡruː.mɪŋ trɛnd / (BrE)
a fashionable style or practice currently popular in pet grooming
current style in pet grooming

Examples

She likes to follow every new grooming trend for her show dogs.
The groomer suggested a grooming trend that would suit our schnauzer perfectly.
Social media has made it easier for pet owners to learn about the latest grooming trends.

Synset Examples

He styled his poodle with the latest grooming trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trend - a general direction in which something is developing or changing
