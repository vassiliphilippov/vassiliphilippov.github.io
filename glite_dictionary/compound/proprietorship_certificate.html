proprietorship certificate

1. NOUN

/ prəˈpraɪətərˌʃɪp ˌsɝː.tɪ.fɪ.kət / (AmE), / prəˈprʌɪətəˌʃɪp ˌsɜː.tɪ.fɪ.kət / (BrE)
an official document that states the owner of a sole proprietorship and confirms responsibility for the business
certificate naming sole business owner

Examples

The bank required a proprietorship certificate before approving the loan application.
To register a small business, a proprietorship certificate must be submitted to the authorities.
She displayed her proprietorship certificate on the wall of her new bakery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sole proprietorship - a business owned and run by one person
