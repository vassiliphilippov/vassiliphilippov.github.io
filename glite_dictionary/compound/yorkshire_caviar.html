Yorkshire caviar

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɔrkʃər ˈkæviɑr / (AmE), / ˈjɔːkʃə ˈkæviɑː / (BrE)
A local delicacy from Yorkshire made from fish roe, typically regarded as a gourmet dish.
gourmet fish roe delicacy from Yorkshire

Examples

We were served Yorkshire caviar as a starter at the countryside inn.
Many food critics praise the intense flavor of Yorkshire caviar.
Some locals say no celebration is complete without a dish of Yorkshire caviar.
