nickel alloy

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪkəl ˌælɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɪkəl ˌalɔɪ / (BrE)
an alloy in which nickel is the principal component, often combined with other elements to enhance specific properties
alloy with nickel as main component

Examples

High-performance turbine blades are often made from nickel alloy due to its heat resistance.
The company developed a new nickel alloy that is both strong and lightweight.
Engineers chose a nickel alloy for the pipes because of its excellent corrosion resistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alloy - a metal made by combining two or more metallic elements
