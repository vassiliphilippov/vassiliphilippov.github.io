gentlemen's attire

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntləmɪnz əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmɪnz əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
Formal clothing typically worn by men, especially on special or ceremonial occasions.
formal men's clothing

Examples

He arrived at the wedding in immaculate gentlemen's attire, complete with a top hat and gloves.
The invitation specified that guests should wear gentlemen's attire for the gala dinner.
Renting gentlemen's attire is common for proms and black-tie events.

Synset Examples

The gentlemen's attire was formal and impeccable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formalwear - formal clothing for special occasions
  • Synonym: eveningwear - clothing suitable for formal evening events
  • Antonym: casual wear - clothing suitable for informal occasions
