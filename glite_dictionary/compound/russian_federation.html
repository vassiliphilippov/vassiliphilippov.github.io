Russian Federation

1. PROPN

/ ˈrʌʃən ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the official name for Russia, a country in northeastern Europe and northern Asia, formerly part of the Soviet Union
official name for the country Russia

Examples

The president of the Russian Federation addressed the United Nations yesterday.
After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, the Russian Federation became an independent nation.
Many people still refer to the Russian Federation simply as Russia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Russia - common name for the Russian Federation
  • Degree Lower: Soviet Union - former federation that included Russia and other republics
