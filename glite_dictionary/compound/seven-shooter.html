seven-shooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvən ˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈsɛvən ˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a firearm, especially a revolver, that has seven chambers or is capable of holding and firing seven bullets without reloading
revolver with seven chambers

Examples

The old cowboy proudly displayed his antique seven-shooter in a glass case.
During the card game, he revealed a hidden seven-shooter strapped to his belt.
Collectors often search for rare models of the seven-shooter at auctions.

Synset Examples

In the movie, the hero used a seven-shooter to save the day.
He carried a seven-shooter for protection during his travels.
The old cowboy proudly displayed his antique seven-shooter.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: six-shooter - a revolver with six chambers for bullets
  • Synonym: revolver - a type of handgun with a revolving cylinder holding multiple chambers

Etymology

Formed from 'seven' and 'shooter', referring to the number of chambers for bullets.
