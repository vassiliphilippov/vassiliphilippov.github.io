love child

1. NOUN

/ lʌv tʃaɪld / (AmE), / lʌv tʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child born to parents who are not married to each other.
child born out of wedlock

Examples

Rumors spread quickly when the politician's love child was mentioned in the press.
She grew up knowing she was a love child, but her family always supported her.
The story focuses on the challenges faced by a love child in a traditional society.
