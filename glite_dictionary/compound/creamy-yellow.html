creamy-yellow

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrimi ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkriːmi ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
having a shade of yellow that is mixed with a creamy or pale tint, resembling the color of cream.
pale yellow with a creamy tint

Examples

She wore a creamy-yellow dress that complemented her complexion perfectly.
The kitchen walls were painted a soft creamy-yellow, creating a warm atmosphere.
I love the delicate creamy-yellow petals of these spring tulips.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum
