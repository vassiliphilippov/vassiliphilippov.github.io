genus Strelitzia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs strɛˈlɪtsiə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs strɛˈlɪtsiə / (BrE)
A small genus of large perennial evergreen plants with banana-like leaves, sometimes classified in the family Musaceae.
genus of large evergreen plants

Examples

The genus Strelitzia includes several striking plant species native to South Africa.
Horticulturists often cultivate members of the genus Strelitzia for their exotic flowers.
Botanists sometimes debate whether the genus Strelitzia should be placed in the family Musaceae.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bird of paradise - Common name for Strelitzia reginae, a species of this genus

Etymology

Genus named after Queen Charlotte of Mecklenburg-Strelitz, wife of King George III.
