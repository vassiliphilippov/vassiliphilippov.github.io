common wolffia

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən wʊlˈfiə / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən wʊlˈfiə / (BrE)
A very small aquatic plant of the Americas, known as the smallest flowering plant in the world.
the smallest flowering plant

Examples

The common wolffia is so tiny it can float on a raindrop.
Biologists study the common wolffia to understand how the smallest flowers develop.
In wetlands across the Americas, common wolffia often forms green mats on the water's surface.
