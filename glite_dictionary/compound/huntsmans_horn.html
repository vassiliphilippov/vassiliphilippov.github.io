huntsman's horn

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntsmənz hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsmənz hɔːn / (BrE)
a pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, with erect yellow trumpet-shaped pitchers, wide mouths, and erect lids
a southeastern US pitcher plant

Examples

The huntsman's horn grows naturally in boggy areas of the southeastern United States.
Botanists are fascinated by the unique shape of the huntsman's horn's pitcher.
We spotted a rare huntsman's horn while hiking through the wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher plant - a carnivorous plant with tube-shaped leaves for trapping insects
