dill seed

1. NOUN

/ dɪl siːd / (AmE), / dɪl siːd / (BrE)
The seed of the dill plant, used as a seasoning in cooking.
seed of dill plant used as seasoning

Examples

The recipe calls for a teaspoon of dill seed to enhance the flavor.
Pickled cucumbers are often made with garlic and dill seed for extra aroma.
She sprinkled some dill seed over the roasted potatoes before serving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dill - an aromatic plant whose leaves and seeds are used for flavoring food
