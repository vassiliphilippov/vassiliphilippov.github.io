queer duck

1. NOUN

/ kwɪr dʌk / (AmE), / kwɪə dʌk / (BrE)
an unconventional, eccentric, or odd person; someone who stands out for unusual or peculiar behavior
eccentric person; odd individual
informal

Examples

My uncle is a real queer duck, always inventing strange gadgets in his garage.
You could tell he was a queer duck by the way he dressed at the party.
Although she was considered a queer duck, everyone admired her creativity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odd duck - an unusual or quirky person
  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional and slightly strange views or behavior
