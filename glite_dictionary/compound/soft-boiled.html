soft-boiled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔft ˌbɔɪld / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌbɔɪld / (BrE)
(of an egg) cooked so that the white is set but the yolk remains liquid.
egg with liquid yolk

Examples

She prefers her eggs soft-boiled for breakfast every morning.
The chef recommended serving asparagus with a soft-boiled egg on the side.
To make a perfect soft-boiled egg, time the cooking carefully.

Synset Examples

soft-boiled eggs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hard-boiled - (of an egg) cooked so that both the white and yolk are solid
