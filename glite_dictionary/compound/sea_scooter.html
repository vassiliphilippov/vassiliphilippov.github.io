sea scooter

1. NOUN

/ si ˈskuːtər / (AmE), / siː ˈskuːtə / (BrE)
a small motorized watercraft designed for individual use, often resembling a scooter and used for recreation on water
motorized watercraft resembling a scooter

Examples

He rented a sea scooter for the afternoon and rode around the lake.
The new sea scooter is easy to maneuver and perfect for beginners.
A sea scooter can reach impressive speeds on calm water.
