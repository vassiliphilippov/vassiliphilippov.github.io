Aku language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːku ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɑːku ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Kwa language spoken by the Yoruba people in southwestern Nigeria
a language spoken by the Yoruba in Nigeria

Examples

Linguists have documented the Aku language as a distinct member of the Kwa branch.
Many cultural traditions are preserved through storytelling in the Aku language.
The Aku language is closely associated with the Yoruba people of southwestern Nigeria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yoruba language - principal language spoken by the Yoruba people of Nigeria
