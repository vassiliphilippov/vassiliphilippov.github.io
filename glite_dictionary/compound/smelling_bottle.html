smelling bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɛlɪŋ ˌbɑːtəl / (AmE), / ˈsmelɪŋ ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a small bottle containing smelling salts, used especially to revive someone who has fainted or feels faint
bottle for smelling salts

Examples

She carried a smelling bottle in her purse in case she felt faint during the long carriage ride.
The doctor offered her a smelling bottle when she began to swoon in the hot room.
In Victorian times, it was common for ladies to use a smelling bottle at social gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smelling salts - aromatic compounds used to revive those who have fainted
