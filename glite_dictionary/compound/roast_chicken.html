roast chicken

1. NOUN

/ roʊst ˈtʃɪk.ən / (AmE), / rəʊst ˈtʃɪk.ɪn / (BrE)
Chicken cooked by roasting, typically in an oven, often served as a main course.
chicken cooked by roasting

Examples

We had roast chicken and vegetables for Sunday lunch.
The aroma of roast chicken filled the entire kitchen.
She seasoned the roast chicken with fresh herbs before placing it in the oven.

Synset Examples

He asked for a roast chicken for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grilled chicken - chicken cooked by grilling
  • Synonym: roast beef - beef cooked by roasting
