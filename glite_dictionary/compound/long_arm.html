long arm

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ɑrm / (AmE), / lɒŋ ɑːm / (BrE)
the ability or power of a person or organization to affect people or events far away; extensive influence or reach
far-reaching influence or power

Examples

The long arm of the law eventually caught up with the fugitive.
Her long arm in the industry helped many young artists succeed.
Critics claim that the company's long arm reaches well beyond the market into politics.

Synset Examples

His influence had a long arm that reached far and wide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reach - range or extent of influence or power
  • Synonym: authority - the power to give orders or make decisions
