coal mine

1. NOUN

/ koʊl maɪn / (AmE), / kəʊl maɪn / (BrE)
a place underground where coal is extracted from the earth
an underground place where coal is dug

Examples

The workers spent twelve hours a day in the coal mine extracting fuel for the power plant.
Many communities in the region were built around the local coal mine.
Modern safety equipment has made working in a coal mine far less dangerous than in the past.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mine - an excavation from which minerals and other materials are extracted
