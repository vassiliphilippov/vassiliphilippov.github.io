Amerikkkan dream

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪk.ən driːm / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪk.ən driːm / (BrE)
A critical reinterpretation of the American dream that highlights systemic racism, inequality, and the exclusion of marginalized groups in the United States.
critical take on the American dream focused on inequality

Examples

His book argues that the Amerikkkan dream is built on the suffering of marginalized people.
The rapper's lyrics denounce the false promises of the Amerikkkan dream.
Many activists use the phrase Amerikkkan dream to highlight ongoing social and racial injustice.

Synset Examples

The Amerikkkan dream often feels out of reach for many.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: American dream - the ideal that every US citizen has an equal opportunity to achieve success and prosperity

Etymology

Modification of 'American dream,' with 'KKK' referencing the Ku Klux Klan to emphasize racism and injustice.
