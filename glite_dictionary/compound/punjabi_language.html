Punjabi language

1. NOUN

/ pʌnˈdʒɑːbi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Punjab region, spoken mainly in Pakistan and India
language spoken in Punjab region

Examples

Many people in Canada speak Punjabi language as their first language.
She is fluent in both English and Punjabi language.
The movie was dubbed into the Punjabi language for local audiences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hindi - the main language of northern India and an Indo-Aryan language related to Punjabi

Etymology

From Persian 'Punjābī', meaning 'of Punjab'; 'Punjab' from Persian 'panj' (five) and 'āb' (water), referring to the land of five rivers.
