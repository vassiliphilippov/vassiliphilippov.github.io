giant bamboo

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt bæmˈbu / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt bamˈbuː / (BrE)
a very large tropical bamboo native to Southeast Asia, noted for its tough hollow stems that can resemble tree trunks
very large tropical bamboo plant

Examples

The giant bamboo in their backyard reached over twenty meters and provided shade for the entire garden.
Some construction projects in Asia use giant bamboo for building due to its strength and flexibility.
Botanists studied the rapid growth rate of giant bamboo after the monsoon season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bamboo - tall tropical plant with hollow woody stems
