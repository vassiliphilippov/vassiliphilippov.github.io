windmill grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndˌmɪl ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌmɪl ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A perennial Australian grass with long spikes radiating from the stalk like the vanes of a windmill.
Australian grass with radiating spikes

Examples

The unique shape of the windmill grass makes it easy to spot in the Australian outback.
Farmers often appreciate windmill grass for its drought resistance and decorative appeal.
As we walked through the field, the windmill grass swayed gently in the breeze.
