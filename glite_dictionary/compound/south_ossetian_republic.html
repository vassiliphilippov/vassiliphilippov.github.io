South Ossetian Republic

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ ɔˈsiːtiən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / saʊθ ɒˈsiːtiən rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
a disputed region in the South Caucasus that declared independence from Georgia, recognized by a few countries but considered by most of the international community to be part of Georgia
disputed region in the South Caucasus

Examples

The independence of the South Ossetian Republic is recognized by only a few countries around the world.
Conflict erupted in the region after the South Ossetian Republic declared independence from Georgia.
Many international organizations do not recognize the South Ossetian Republic as a sovereign state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abkhazia - another disputed region in the South Caucasus
  • Antonym: Georgia - the country claiming sovereignty over South Ossetia
