service area

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌɛəriə / (BrE)
a place on a highway where travelers can stop for fuel, food, restrooms, and other services
roadside rest and service stop

Examples

We decided to stop at the service area to get some coffee and stretch our legs.
The next service area is 20 miles ahead, so make sure you have enough fuel.
Many service areas now offer electric vehicle charging stations in addition to traditional gas pumps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rest stop - a designated place along a highway for resting and facilities
  • Synonym: service station - a place offering fuel and basic vehicle services
