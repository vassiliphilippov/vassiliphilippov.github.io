mental note

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl̩ noʊt / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl̩ nəʊt / (BrE)
an act of deliberately remembering something in one's mind to recall it later
an intention to remember something

Examples

He made a mental note to call his friend after work.
During the presentation, I took a mental note of the key points.
She always makes a mental note of people's birthdays so she doesn't forget.

Synset Examples

he made a mental note to send her flowers
