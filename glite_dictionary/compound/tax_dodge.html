tax dodge

1. NOUN

/ tæks dɑdʒ / (AmE), / tæks dɒdʒ / (BrE)
an illegal method or scheme for avoiding the payment of taxes
illegal way to avoid paying taxes

Examples

The company was fined heavily for its tax dodge involving fake invoices.
Authorities uncovered a complex tax dodge that saved millions for the wealthy businessman.
Despite the risks, some firms still attempt a tax dodge to reduce their financial liabilities.

Synset Examples

Using offshore accounts can sometimes lead to a tax dodge.
He was accused of a tax dodge to avoid payments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tax evasion - the illegal act of not paying taxes owed
  • Antonym: tax avoidance - legal means of minimizing tax liability
