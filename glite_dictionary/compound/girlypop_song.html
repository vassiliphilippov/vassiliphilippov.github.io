girlypop song

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɝːli pɑp sɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli pɒp sɒŋ / (BrE)
An upbeat pop song characterized by a style and themes aimed primarily at young girls.
upbeat pop song for young girls

Examples

The crowd went wild when the DJ played their favorite girlypop song.
She loves singing along to every new girlypop song that appears on the charts.
Their latest album is filled with catchy girlypop songs perfect for summer parties.

Synset Examples

They danced to girlypop songs at the party all night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bubblegum pop - a subgenre of pop music with catchy melodies and simple lyrics, often aimed at young audiences

Etymology

Blend of 'girly' and 'pop (music)'.
