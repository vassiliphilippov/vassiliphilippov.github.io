young bird

1. NOUN

/ jʌŋ bɝd / (AmE), / jʌŋ bɜːd / (BrE)
a bird that has not yet reached adulthood; a fledgling or chick
a bird that is not yet adult

Examples

The young bird waited in the nest for its mother to bring food.
It is important not to touch a young bird if you find one on the ground.
Scientists studied how the diet of a young bird affects its growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chick - a young bird, especially just hatched
  • Synonym: fledgling - a young bird just learning to fly
  • Antonym: adult bird - a fully grown bird
