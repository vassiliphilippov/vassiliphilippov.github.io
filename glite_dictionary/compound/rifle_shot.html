rifle shot

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪfəl ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl ʃɒt / (BrE)
the maximum distance that a bullet fired from a rifle can travel effectively
distance a rifle bullet can travel

Examples

The deer was just beyond rifle shot, so the hunter waited.
At that range, the cabin was well within rifle shot.
The officer estimated the intruder was only a rifle shot away from their position.

Synset Examples

the target was out of rifle range

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rifle range - the maximum effective distance of a rifle
