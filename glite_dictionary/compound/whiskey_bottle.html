whiskey bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski ˌbɑːtəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a glass bottle used for storing or serving whiskey
bottle for storing whiskey

Examples

He placed the empty whiskey bottle on the table and sighed deeply.
The label on the whiskey bottle was faded and hard to read.
Please recycle your whiskey bottle after finishing the drink.
