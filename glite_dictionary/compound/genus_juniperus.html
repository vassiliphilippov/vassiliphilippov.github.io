genus Juniperus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs dʒuːˈnɪ.pər.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs dʒuːˈnɪ.pər.əs / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees and shrubs known as junipers, widely distributed in the Northern Hemisphere.
genus of juniper trees and shrubs

Examples

The genus Juniperus includes species that provide the berries used to flavor gin.
Botanists are still discovering new species within the genus Juniperus in remote regions.
Many members of the genus Juniperus are popular as ornamental plants in gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juniper - any tree or shrub of the genus Juniperus

Etymology

From Latin 'Juniperus', the classical name for the juniper tree.
