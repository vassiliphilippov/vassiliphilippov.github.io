trash can

1. NOUN

/ træʃ ˌkæn / (AmE), / træʃ ˌkan / (BrE)
A container for holding waste until it is collected.
container for holding waste

Examples

Please put your empty cup in the trash can before you leave the room.
The wind blew the trash can over, scattering rubbish everywhere.
Every week, the trash can outside our house is emptied by the garbage collectors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bin - A receptacle for storing waste or other materials
  • Synonym: dustbin - A British term for a container for household waste
  • Synonym: recycling bin - A container for materials to be recycled
