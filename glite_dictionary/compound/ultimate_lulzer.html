ultimate lulzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌltəmɪt ˈlʌlzər / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ˈlʌlzə / (BrE)
A person who is exceptionally skilled at making others laugh by telling funny stories, especially online.
person skilled at telling funny stories
slang informal

Examples

Everyone agreed that Jenna was the ultimate lulzer at the party last night.
You have to hear his jokes—he's a true ultimate lulzer!
In our group chat, she's known as the ultimate lulzer because of her hilarious messages.

Synset Examples

Her stories made her the ultimate lulzer among friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comedian - A professional entertainer who tells jokes or amusing stories

Etymology

Formed from 'ultimate' and 'lulzer', where 'lulz' is an internet slang variation of 'lols' (laughs).
