raisin cookie

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.zən ˈkʊk.i / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.zɪn ˈkʊk.i / (BrE)
A cookie containing raisins.
cookie with raisins

Examples

She offered me a homemade raisin cookie with my tea.
The bakery sells delicious oatmeal and raisin cookies every morning.
I prefer a raisin cookie to a chocolate chip one when I want something less sweet.

Related Terms
