good boy

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd bɔɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊd bɔɪ / (BrE)
a term of affection or praise for a well-behaved child or dog
term of praise for child or dog

Examples

You’re such a good boy, helping your sister with her homework.
The trainer smiled and said, 'What a good boy!' after the dog obeyed the command.
Parents often reward their child for being a good boy at the dentist.

Synset Examples

He praised his son, calling him a good boy.
The dog sat patiently; what a good boy!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good girl - term of praise for a well-behaved girl or female animal
