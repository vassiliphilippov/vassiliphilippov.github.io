house wren

1. NOUN

/ haʊs rɛn / (AmE), / haʊs rɛn / (BrE)
a small North and Central American songbird (Troglodytes aedon), often found nesting near human dwellings
small American songbird that nests near houses

Examples

A house wren built its nest in the mailbox outside our kitchen door this spring.
The cheerful song of the house wren is a familiar sound in suburban gardens across the United States.
We watched a pair of house wrens feeding their chicks on the back porch.
