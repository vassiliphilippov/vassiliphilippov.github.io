gig mill

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪɡ mɪl / (AmE), / ɡɪɡ mɪl / (BrE)
a textile mill that uses rotary wire cylinders to raise the nap on fabric.
textile mill with rotary wire napping cylinders

Examples

The factory installed a new gig mill to improve the softness of its blankets.
Workers in the gig mill monitored the fabric closely as it passed over the wire cylinders.
During the nineteenth century, the gig mill was essential in finishing woolen textiles.
