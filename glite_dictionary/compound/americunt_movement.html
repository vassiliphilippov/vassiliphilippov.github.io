Americunt movement

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkʌnt ˈmuvmənt / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkʌnt ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
A highly offensive and derogatory term referring to American cultural trends, often used disparagingly.
vulgar slur for American cultural trends
offensive vulgar

Examples

His essay harshly critiqued what he called the Americunt movement in contemporary culture.
She refused to support the Americunt movement and its shallow values.
Use of the phrase Americunt movement is deeply offensive and controversial.

Synset Examples

The article discussed the implications of the Americunt movement.

Etymology

Blend of 'America' and a highly offensive slang word for the vulva.
