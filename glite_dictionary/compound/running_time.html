running time

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ tʌɪm / (BrE)
the length of time that a film, television show, or performance lasts from beginning to end
duration of film or show

Examples

The running time of the movie is just under two hours.
Always check the running time before booking tickets to a theater performance.
With an extended running time, the documentary covers every aspect of the subject.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duration - the length of time that something continues
