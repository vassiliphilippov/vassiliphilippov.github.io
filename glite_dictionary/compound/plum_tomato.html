plum tomato

1. NOUN

/ plʌm təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / plʌm təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a variety of tomato that is oval-shaped, has fewer seeds and fleshier pulp than other types, and is commonly used in cooking, especially for making sauces
oval tomato used in cooking

Examples

We bought several plum tomatoes to make homemade pasta sauce.
The recipe calls for chopped plum tomatoes instead of standard ones.
Plum tomatoes are preferred for canning due to their firm texture.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cherry tomato - a small, round variety of tomato, typically eaten raw

2. NOUN

/ plʌm təˈmeɪɾoʊ / (AmE), / plʌm təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
A variety of tomato with an oval shape, resembling a plum, often used for sauces and canning.
oval-shaped tomato variety

Examples

She prefers plum tomatoes for making her homemade pasta sauce.
The salad was topped with sliced plum tomato and fresh basil.
Canned plum tomatoes are ideal for stews and soups.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: cherry tomato - small, round tomato variety
  • Degree Higher: beefsteak tomato - large, fleshy tomato variety
