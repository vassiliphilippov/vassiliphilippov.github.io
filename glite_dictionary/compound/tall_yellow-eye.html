tall yellow-eye

1. NOUN

/ tɔl ˈjɛloʊ aɪ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈjɛləʊ aɪ / (BrE)
an Australian perennial herb with yellow flowers belonging to the genus Xyris
Australian herb with yellow flowers

Examples
