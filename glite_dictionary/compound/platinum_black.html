platinum black

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæt̬ənəm blæk / (AmE), / ˈplætɪnəm blæk / (BrE)
a fine black powder made of platinum, used as a catalyst in chemical reactions
black platinum powder catalyst

Examples

The chemist used platinum black to speed up the reaction in the laboratory.
Fuel cells often use platinum black as an efficient catalyst at the electrode.
To purify hydrogen, engineers passed the gas over heated platinum black.
