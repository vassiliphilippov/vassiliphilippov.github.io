plate and frame filter press

1. NOUN

/ pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltər prɛs / (AmE), / pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltə pres / (BrE)
a type of filter press consisting of a series of plates and frames assembled alternately between supports, used for separating solids from liquids in industrial processes
industrial filter press with plates and frames

Examples

The plate and frame filter press is commonly used in the chemical industry for efficient solid-liquid separation.
Technicians inspected the plate and frame filter press to ensure it was operating correctly during the filtration process.
A plate and frame filter press can handle large volumes of material, making it ideal for wastewater treatment plants.

Synset Examples

Plate filter press is extensively used in sugaring operations such as the production of maple syrup in Canada, since it offers very high efficiency and reliability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filter press - a machine used to separate solids and liquids in industrial processes
