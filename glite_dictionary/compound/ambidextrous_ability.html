ambidextrous ability

1. NOUN

/ ˌæm.bɪˈdɛk.strəs əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌam.bɪˈdɛk.strəs əˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the capability to use both hands with equal skill or facility
skill using both hands equally well

Examples

Her ambidextrous ability impressed the judges during the talent competition.
Having ambidextrous ability can be a significant advantage for musicians, surgeons, and athletes.
She developed her ambidextrous ability through years of practice in sports and art.

Synset Examples

Her ambidextrous abilities made her a great athlete.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambidexterity - the quality of being ambidextrous
  • Synonym: ambidextrous - able to use both hands with equal skill (adjective)
