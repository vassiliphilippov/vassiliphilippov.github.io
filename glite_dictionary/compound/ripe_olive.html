ripe olive

1. NOUN

/ raɪp ˈɑːlɪv / (AmE), / raɪp ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
an olive that is harvested when fully mature and preserved by curing, pickling, drying, or canning
fully mature, preserved olive

Examples

The salad was topped with slices of ripe olive for a rich, robust flavor.
She prefers ripe olives to green ones because of their smooth texture and deep taste.
You can buy ripe olive paste at many gourmet shops for spreading on bread.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: green olive - an olive that is harvested before ripening and usually pickled or stuffed
