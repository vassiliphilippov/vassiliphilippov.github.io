chess club

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛs klʌb / (AmE), / tʃɛs klʌb / (BrE)
an organized group of people who meet regularly to play chess
group for playing chess

Examples

He joined the chess club at university to improve his skills.
The school's chess club meets every Thursday after classes.
As president of the chess club, Maria organized a tournament for new members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chess - a board game for two players
  • Synonym: club - an organization of people with a common interest
