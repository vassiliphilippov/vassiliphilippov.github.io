poor relation

1. NOUN

/ pɔr rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / pɔː rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
A person or thing that is considered inferior in status, importance, or quality compared to others in the same group.
inferior person or thing in a group

Examples

Despite its long history, the club is often viewed as the poor relation of the league.
In this country, public transport is the poor relation compared to private cars.
She always felt like the poor relation at family gatherings because of her modest background.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underdog - a person or group expected to lose or be at a disadvantage
  • Antonym: favorite - a person or thing regarded as the best or most likely to succeed
