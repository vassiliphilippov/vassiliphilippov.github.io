Capitol Reef National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæpɪtəl ˌriːf ˌnæʃənl ˈpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl ˌriːf ˌnæʃənəl ˈpɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in Utah known for its colorful rock formations, desert landscape, and diverse plant and animal life
national park in Utah, USA

Examples

We spent three days hiking in Capitol Reef National Park and marveled at the stunning landscapes.
Capitol Reef National Park offers unique opportunities to explore canyons, cliffs, and natural arches.
Her favorite vacation was camping under the stars in Capitol Reef National Park.

Etymology

Named for white domes of Navajo Sandstone that resemble the United States Capitol building and for the rocky ridges called 'reefs' by explorers.
