home wrecker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm ˌrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌrɛkə / (BrE)
a person who is thought to be responsible for the breakup of someone else's marriage or committed relationship, often by having an affair with one of the partners
person causing a breakup of a marriage or relationship
informal

Examples

Many tabloid magazines unfairly called her a home wrecker after the affair went public.
He never intended to be a home wrecker, but his relationship with Jane changed everything.
Rumors circulated that the actress was a home wrecker, despite her denials.

Synset Examples

The rumors painted her as a home wrecker.
He felt like a home wrecker when he intervened.
She was labeled a home wrecker after the scandal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulterer - a person who commits adultery
