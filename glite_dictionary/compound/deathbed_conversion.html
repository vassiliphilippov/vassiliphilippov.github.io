deathbed conversion

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛθˌbɛd kənˈvɝʒən / (AmE), / ˈdɛθbɛd kənˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
a change in religious beliefs or values that occurs when someone is near death, often involving adopting a faith or repenting previous beliefs
change of beliefs near death

Examples

Many skeptics doubt the sincerity of a deathbed conversion when it occurs so late in life.
His deathbed conversion to Christianity became a topic of intense public interest.
The priest was called to witness the billionaire's deathbed conversion in his final hours.

Synset Examples

She had a deathbed conversion and embraced faith.
A deathbed conversion is often a dramatic moment.
His deathbed conversion surprised everyone in the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversion - a change in belief, typically religious
