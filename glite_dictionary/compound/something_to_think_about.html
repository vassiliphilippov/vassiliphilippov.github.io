something to think about

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə ˈθɪŋk əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmθɪŋ tə ˈθɪŋk əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
an idea or issue that deserves serious consideration or reflection
an idea worth considering

Examples

Her advice certainly gave me something to think about before making my decision.
The documentary offers viewers something to think about regarding climate change.
After hearing his story, I found myself with something to think about for days.

Synset Examples

His speech gave everyone something to think about.
That article really gave me something to think about.
