voiceless community

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔɪsləs kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪsləs kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people who lack power, representation, or the means to express their concerns in society or politics
group lacking power or representation

Examples

The new policy aims to empower the voiceless community that has long been neglected.
Activists spoke on behalf of the voiceless community at the rally.
Social workers often advocate for the rights of the voiceless community.

Synset Examples

The documentary highlighted the plight of voiceless communities.
Many voiceless individuals need our support and advocacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marginalized group - group relegated to the fringes of society, lacking power or influence
