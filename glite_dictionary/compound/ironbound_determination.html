ironbound determination

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərnbaʊnd ˌdɛtərməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈaɪəbaʊnd ˌdɛtəmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
An unwavering and unbreakable determination; a very strong will to achieve something despite obstacles.
unbreakable, firm determination

Examples

Her ironbound determination inspired everyone on her team to keep pushing through the challenges.
With ironbound determination, he overcame every setback and reached his goal.
The company's success was driven by the founder's ironbound determination to never give up.

Synset Examples

Her ironbound determination impressed everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolve - firm determination to do something
  • Synonym: tenacity - the quality of being very determined
  • Antonym: wavering - lack of firmness or determination

Etymology

Formed from 'ironbound' (bound or reinforced with iron, hence very strong) and 'determination.'
