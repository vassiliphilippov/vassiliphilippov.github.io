quarter pound

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtər paʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə paʊnd / (BrE)
A unit of weight equal to a quarter of a pound (4 ounces avoirdupois).
unit of weight; 4 ounces

Examples

She bought a quarter pound of cheese from the market.
The recipe calls for a quarter pound of butter.
He ordered a quarter pound steak at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pound - a unit of weight equal to 16 ounces
  • Degree Lower: ounce - a unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound
