last-ditch attempt

1. NOUN

/ læst dɪtʃ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / lɑːst dɪtʃ əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
a final and desperate effort to achieve something when all else has failed
final, desperate effort

Examples

In a last-ditch attempt, the team worked through the night to finish the project.
Her last-ditch attempt to convince the jury was passionate but ultimately unsuccessful.
He made a last-ditch attempt to save the business from bankruptcy.

Synset Examples

The last-ditch attempt failed to persuade the board.
They made a last-ditch effort to save the deal.
In a last-ditch move, they offered a better price.
They made a last-gasp attempt to win the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last-ditch effort - a final attempt to succeed

Etymology

From the military phrase 'last ditch,' meaning the last line of defense.
