picture taking

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ ˌteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of taking photographs, especially as an activity or hobby
the act of taking photographs

Examples

The museum strictly prohibits picture taking inside its galleries.
She has developed a real passion for picture taking since getting her new camera.
Their vacation included plenty of sightseeing and picture taking along the way.

Related Terms
