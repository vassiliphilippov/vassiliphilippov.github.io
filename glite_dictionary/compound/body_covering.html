body covering

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdi ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A covering for the body or a part of it, such as skin, scales, fur, clothing, or a protective layer.
covering for the body or a body part

Examples

Feathers serve as a body covering that helps birds stay warm.
The snake's scales are its natural body covering and offer protection.
He wore a thick body covering to shield himself from the cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: covering - something that covers or protects
