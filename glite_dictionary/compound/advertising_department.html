advertising department

1. NOUN

/ ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈadvətaɪzɪŋ dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the division of a company or organization responsible for planning and managing advertisements
division responsible for advertising

Examples

She applied for a position in the advertising department at a major publishing house.
The advertising department launched a new campaign for the summer season.
Meetings between the advertising department and design team are held every Monday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing department - division responsible for marketing activities
