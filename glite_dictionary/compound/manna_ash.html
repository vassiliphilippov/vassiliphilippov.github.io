manna ash

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænə æʃ / (AmE), / ˈmænə æʃ / (BrE)
a southern Mediterranean ash tree with fragrant white flowers in dense clusters, which yields a sweet sap called manna
southern Mediterranean ash tree yielding manna

Examples

The manna ash is commonly found in southern Italy and Sicily, where its sweet sap is harvested.
We admired the fragrant white blossoms of the manna ash during our walk through the Mediterranean garden.
Products made from manna ash sap have been used as natural sweeteners and traditional medicines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ash tree - a tree of the genus Fraxinus, including many species grown for timber or ornamental use
