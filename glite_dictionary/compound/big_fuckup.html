big fuckup

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈfʌkʌp / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈfʌkʌp / (BrE)
A serious or disastrous mistake, especially one caused by carelessness or incompetence.
serious or disastrous mistake
informal vulgar

Examples

Losing the client was a big fuckup on his part.
After that big fuckup, the team had to work all weekend to fix things.
Calling the wrong number during the interview was my biggest big fuckup of the day.

Synset Examples

I made a big fuckup on that project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • Degree Lower: minor error - a small or insignificant mistake
