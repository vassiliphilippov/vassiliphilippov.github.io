personal effects

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːsənl ɪˈfɛkts / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ɪˈfɛkts / (BrE)
someone's portable belongings, especially those of a personal nature, typically excluding items used in business
someone's personal portable belongings

Examples

She kept all her personal effects in a small suitcase beside her bed.
After his uncle passed away, he was asked to collect the personal effects from the nursing home.
Travelers are advised not to leave their personal effects unattended in public places.

Synset Examples

she left some of her personal effects in the house
