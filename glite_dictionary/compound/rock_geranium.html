rock geranium

1. NOUN

/ rɑk dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / rɒk dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a perennial plant with basal leaves mottled with white and flowers in lax panicles on erect stems, often found in rocky areas
a plant with white-mottled leaves and lax flower clusters

Examples

She planted a rock geranium in the shaded corner of her garden for its attractive foliage.
The rock geranium thrives in rocky soil and produces clusters of delicate flowers in spring.
Gardeners often choose the rock geranium for borders due to its resilience and beautiful blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - a genus of plants with lobed leaves and typically pink or purple flowers
