lynx caracal

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋks ˈkærəkæl / (AmE), / lɪŋks ˈkærəkæl / (BrE)
a wild cat species found in deserts of northern Africa and southern Asia, also known as the caracal
wild cat of northern Africa and southern Asia

Examples

The lynx caracal is renowned for its distinctive tufted black ears and agility.
Researchers observed a lynx caracal hunting small mammals in the Sahara desert.
Many zoos house the lynx caracal because of its striking appearance and adaptability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caracal - medium-sized wild cat with long black ear tufts

Etymology

From Greek λύγξ (lynx) and Turkish kara kulak (black ear).
