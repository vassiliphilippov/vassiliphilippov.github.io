Goodenia family

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈdiːniə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ɡəˈdiːniə ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
A family of sappy, mainly herbaceous plants native to Australasia and southeast China, scientifically known as Goodeniaceae.
Australasian plant family (Goodeniaceae)

Examples

The Goodenia family includes over 400 species of plants found mainly in Australia.
Many members of the Goodenia family are popular in native garden landscaping due to their vibrant flowers.
She studied the unique seed dispersal methods of plants in the Goodenia family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Goodeniaceae - the botanical name for the Goodenia family

Etymology

From the genus name Goodenia, named after Samuel Goodenough, English bishop and botanist.
