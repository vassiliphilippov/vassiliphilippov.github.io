leafy-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ ˈliːfi stɛmd / (AmE), / ˈliːfi stɛmd / (BrE)
Having a stem that bears many leaves or is covered with foliage.
having a stem with many leaves

Examples

The leafy-stemmed plant thrived in the damp sunlight near the pond.
Botanists studied the leafy-stemmed species to understand its adaptation mechanisms.
A leafy-stemmed herb can often be distinguished by the dense foliage along its stalk.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leafless - having no leaves; lacking foliage
