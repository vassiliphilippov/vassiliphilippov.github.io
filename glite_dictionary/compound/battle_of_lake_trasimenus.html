Battle of Lake Trasimenus

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv leɪk træˈsɪmənəs / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv leɪk træˈsɪmɪnəs / (BrE)
a battle in 217 BC during the Second Punic War in which Hannibal ambushed and defeated a Roman army led by Flaminius at Lake Trasimene in Italy
historic 217 BC battle in Italy in Second Punic War

Examples

The Battle of Lake Trasimenus is considered one of Hannibal's greatest tactical victories.
Historians have studied the Battle of Lake Trasimenus to understand ancient military strategy.
The defeat at the Battle of Lake Trasimenus shocked the Roman Republic and prompted changes in their command.
