international sea and swell scale

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntɚˈnæʃənəl siː ənd swɛl skeɪl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl siː ənd swɛl skeɪl / (BrE)
an official scale used for measuring and reporting the height of sea waves and swell
scale for measuring wave heights at sea

Examples

Mariners regularly use the international sea and swell scale to report wave conditions.
The weather forecast included the international sea and swell scale to inform sailors of possible dangers.
Accurate readings from the international sea and swell scale are essential for safe navigation during storms.
