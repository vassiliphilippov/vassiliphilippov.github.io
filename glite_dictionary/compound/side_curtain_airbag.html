side curtain airbag

1. NOUN

/ saɪd ˈkɝː.tn ˈɛr.bæɡ / (AmE), / saɪd ˈkɜː.tən ˈeə.bæɡ / (BrE)
a safety device in vehicles that deploys from above the side windows to protect the head during side impacts or rollovers
side-deploying safety airbag in cars

Examples

The new model comes equipped with a side curtain airbag for added passenger safety.
In the event of a rollover, the side curtain airbag deploys instantly to protect occupants’ heads.
Many modern cars feature a side curtain airbag as standard equipment to reduce injury during side collisions.

Synset Examples

Curtain airbags have been said to reduce brain injury or fatalities by up to 45% in a side impact with an SUV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: airbag - an inflatable safety device in vehicles
