french-fried potatoes

1. NOUN

/ ˌfrɛntʃ ˌfraɪd pəˈteɪtoʊz / (AmE), / ˌfrɛntʃ ˌfraɪd pəˈteɪtəʊz / (BrE)
Strips of potato deep-fried in oil, typically served hot as a snack or side dish.
deep-fried strips of potato

Examples

Would you like some french-fried potatoes with your burger?
The restaurant serves their french-fried potatoes with a side of garlic aioli.
Crispy french-fried potatoes are a favorite choice at barbecues and picnics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French fries - thin strips of potato deep-fried and eaten hot
  • Synonym: chips - fried potato slices or strips (British English for 'French fries')

Etymology

Named for the French style of cutting and frying potatoes.
