red eft

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ɛft / (AmE), / rɛd ɛft / (BrE)
the reddish juvenile terrestrial stage of the eastern newt, a common North American amphibian
juvenile terrestrial stage of a North American newt

Examples

We spotted a tiny red eft on the forest trail after yesterday’s rain.
The red eft is known for its bright orange-red coloration and prefers moist, wooded habitats.
During its terrestrial stage, the eastern newt is called a red eft and can travel far from water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eft - the terrestrial juvenile stage of a newt
  • Synonym: eastern newt - a common North American newt species (Notophthalmus viridescens)
