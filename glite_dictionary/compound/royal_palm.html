royal palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɔɪ.əl pɑm / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl pɑːm / (BrE)
a tall palm tree native to southern Florida and Cuba, characterized by its feather-like leaves and smooth, straight trunk
tall palm tree of Florida and Cuba

Examples

The royal palm lined the avenue, creating a tropical and elegant atmosphere.
Many tourists admired the height and beauty of the royal palm in the botanical gardens.
In southern Florida, the royal palm is a common sight in public parks.
