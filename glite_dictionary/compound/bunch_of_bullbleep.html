bunch of bullbleep

1. NOUN

/ bʌntʃ əv ˈbʊlˌbliːp / (AmE), / bʌntʃ əv ˈbʊlˌbliːp / (BrE)
used to describe something that is nonsense, untrue, or not to be believed
nonsense; false information
informal

Examples

Many people dismissed the rumor as just a bunch of bullbleep.
He claimed he could fix everything, but it sounded like a bunch of bullbleep to me.
Don't believe his excuses; they're a bunch of bullbleep.

Synset Examples

That report is just a bunch of bullbleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - ideas, statements, or beliefs that are foolish or untrue
  • Synonym: bullshit - coarse slang for something that is nonsense or false
