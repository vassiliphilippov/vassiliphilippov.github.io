traditional dish

1. NOUN

/ trəˈdɪʃənl dɪʃ / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəl dɪʃ / (BrE)
A food item that is characteristic of and often long-established in a particular culture or region.
food from a specific culture

Examples

Paella is a traditional dish commonly prepared and enjoyed throughout Spain.
During the festival, families gather to share a traditional dish passed down from their ancestors.
The chef specializes in recreating every traditional dish from her hometown.

Synset Examples

Kishke is a traditional dish that everyone loves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicacy - A rare or luxurious food, often from a specific region
