inner resource

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnər rɪˈsɔrs / (AmE), / ˈɪnə rɪˈzɔːs / (BrE)
a mental or emotional ability or strength that helps one deal with difficult situations
mental or emotional capacity or strength

Examples

She relied on her inner resource to cope with months of isolation.
His inner resource helped him manage the stress of leadership.
When faced with adversity, she drew upon her inner resource for strength.

Synset Examples

to have an inner resource against loneliness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resilience - the ability to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness
  • Synonym: fortitude - courage and strength in facing adversity
