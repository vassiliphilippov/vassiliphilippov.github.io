blackcurrant liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌkɝːənt lɪˈkɪr / (AmE), / ˌblækˈkʌrənt lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a sweet alcoholic drink made from blackcurrants, usually served as a dessert liqueur or used in cocktails
sweet alcoholic drink from blackcurrants

Examples

We added a splash of blackcurrant liqueur to the champagne for a classic Kir Royale.
This dessert is topped with a drizzle of blackcurrant liqueur for extra flavor.
He brought back a bottle of French blackcurrant liqueur as a gift from his trip.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: liqueur - a sweet alcoholic beverage flavored with fruit, herbs, or spices
