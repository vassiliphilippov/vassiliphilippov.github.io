cockatoo parrot

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːkəˈtuː ˈpærət / (AmE), / ˌkɒkəˈtuː ˈpærət / (BrE)
a small grey Australian parrot noted for its yellow crested head
small grey Australian parrot with yellow crest

Examples

The cockatoo parrot is easily recognized by its striking yellow crest and playful nature.
During our visit to Australia, we spotted a beautiful cockatoo parrot perched on a branch.
Zookeepers provide enrichment activities to keep the cockatoo parrot mentally stimulated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockatoo - a parrot with an erectile crest, native to Australia and nearby islands
