indicator lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndɪˌkeɪtɚ læmp / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkeɪtə lamp / (BrE)
A small electric light that shows whether a device is switched on or operating.
light showing device status

Examples

The red indicator lamp on the dashboard began to blink rapidly.
If the indicator lamp is off, the device is not receiving power.
Please check that the indicator lamp is illuminated before operating the machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator light - a light that indicates the status of a device
