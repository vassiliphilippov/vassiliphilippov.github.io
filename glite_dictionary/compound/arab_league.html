Arab League

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛˌræb liːɡ / (AmE), / ˈærəb liːɡ / (BrE)
an international organization of Arab states founded in 1945 to enhance political, economic, cultural, and social cooperation among its members
international organization of Arab states

Examples

The Arab League held an emergency summit to discuss regional conflicts.
Membership in the Arab League gives countries a platform for diplomatic collaboration.
The Arab League was instrumental in coordinating economic policies among its members.
