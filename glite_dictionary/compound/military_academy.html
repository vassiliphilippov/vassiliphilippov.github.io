military academy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪl.əˌtɛr.i əˈkæd.ə.mi / (AmE), / ˈmɪl.ɪ.t(ə)r.i əˈkæd.ə.mi / (BrE)
A school or college where future military officers are trained in both academic subjects and military practices.
a school for training military officers

Examples

He graduated from a prestigious military academy before joining the army.
Applicants to the military academy must pass a rigorous entrance exam.
The tradition at the military academy includes daily drills and early morning exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military school - a school for basic military training, sometimes not for officers
