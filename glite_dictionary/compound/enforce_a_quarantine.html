to enforce a quarantine

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈfɔrs ə ˈkwɔrənˌtin / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːs ə ˈkwɒrəntiːn / (BrE)
to impose and maintain isolation of people or areas to prevent the spread of disease or contamination
impose isolation for disease control

Examples

During the outbreak, the government decided to enforce a quarantine in the affected neighborhoods.
Health authorities may enforce a quarantine to stop the disease from spreading further.
After detecting the virus, officials were quick to enforce a quarantine on travelers entering the country.

Synset Examples

The city enforced a quarantine during the outbreak.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarantine - a period or state of enforced isolation
  • Antonym: lift a quarantine - end an enforced isolation period
