face saver

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪs ˌseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌseɪvə / (BrE)
an action or thing that helps someone avoid embarrassment or loss of dignity or prestige
something that preserves dignity or prestige

Examples

Announcing a joint decision was a face saver for both parties involved.
The compromise offered a face saver after hours of heated debate.
He proposed an alternative plan as a face saver to avoid admitting his mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: face-saving - preserving dignity or preventing embarrassment
