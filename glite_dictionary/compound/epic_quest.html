epic quest

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪk kwɛst / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪk kwɛst / (BrE)
A long and adventurous journey, often involving great challenges or heroic deeds.
long and adventurous journey

Examples

The novel follows a young hero on an epic quest to save his kingdom.
Many video games center around the idea of an epic quest filled with danger and discovery.
The film's characters embark on an epic quest across treacherous mountains and dark forests.

Synset Examples

The movie featured a hobbit on an epic quest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quest - a long search or pursuit to achieve a goal
