boob tube

1. NOUN

/ bub tub / (AmE), / buːb tjuːb / (BrE)
an informal term for a television set
informal term for television set
informal slang american

Examples

Every night, he sits in front of the boob tube for hours.
Some parents worry that their kids spend too much time glued to the boob tube.
We watched the football game on the boob tube last Sunday.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a ‘telly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telly - British informal term for television set
  • Synonym: television - device for receiving and displaying TV signals

Etymology

Originated in 1960s American slang. 'Boob' meaning 'fool' and 'tube' referring to the cathode-ray tube of early televisions.

2. NOUN

/ buːb tuːb / (AmE), / buːb tjuːb / (BrE)
A television set, especially referred to informally or with mild derision.
television (informal/derogatory)
informal slang american

Examples

After work, she likes to unwind in front of the boob tube with some comedy shows.
Instead of studying, he wasted hours glued to the boob tube.
Many parents worry their children spend too much time watching the boob tube.

Synset Examples

After a long day, I just want to relax in front of the boob tube.
He spent the weekend binge-watching shows on the boob tube.
The kids love their cartoons on the boob tube.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - an electronic device for receiving television broadcasts; also the medium itself

Etymology

American slang from mid-20th century; 'boob' meaning 'fool' + 'tube' (cathode ray tube).
