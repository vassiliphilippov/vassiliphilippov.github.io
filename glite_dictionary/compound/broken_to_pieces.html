broken to pieces

1. ADJ

/ ˈbroʊ.kən tə ˈpiː.sɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən tə ˈpiː.sɪz / (BrE)
shattered or destroyed completely; reduced to fragments
shattered or destroyed; in many pieces

Examples

After the earthquake, many homes were broken to pieces and impossible to repair.
When the vase fell off the table, it was broken to pieces in an instant.
Her trust in him was broken to pieces by his constant lies.

Synset Examples

The child's toy was broken to pieces after falling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shattered - completely broken or smashed into pieces
  • Antonym: intact - whole; not broken or damaged
