horseshoe whipsnake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌʃu ˈwɪpˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsʃuː ˈwɪpsneɪk / (BrE)
a slender, fast-moving Eurasian snake with a characteristic horseshoe-shaped marking on its head
slender Eurasian snake with horseshoe marking

Examples

The horseshoe whipsnake is often seen darting quickly across rocky hillsides in southern Europe.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the horseshoe whipsnake in Mediterranean grasslands.
A horseshoe whipsnake was spotted basking on a stone wall during the early morning hours.
