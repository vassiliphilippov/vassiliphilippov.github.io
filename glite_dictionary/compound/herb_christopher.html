herb Christopher

1. NOUN

/ hɜrb ˈkrɪstəfər / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈkrɪstəfə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Actaea, known for its acrid, poisonous berries.
poisonous plant of genus Actaea

Examples

The berries of herb Christopher are highly poisonous if ingested by humans.
In the shady parts of the forest, herb Christopher can often be found growing wild.
Gardeners are warned to avoid planting herb Christopher where children play due to its toxic berries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baneberry - another name for plants in the genus Actaea, known for toxic berries
