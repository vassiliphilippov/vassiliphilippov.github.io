Swiss franc

1. NOUN

/ swɪs fræŋk / (AmE), / swɪs fræŋk / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Switzerland and Liechtenstein
currency of Switzerland and Liechtenstein

Examples

Switzerland uses the Swiss franc rather than the euro as its official currency.
The value of the Swiss franc rose sharply against the dollar last year.
Tourists often need to exchange their money for Swiss francs when visiting Zurich.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franc - the basic monetary unit of several countries, formerly used in France
