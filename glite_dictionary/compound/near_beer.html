near beer

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪr ˌbɪr / (AmE), / ˈnɪə ˌbɪə / (BrE)
a beverage that tastes like beer but contains very little alcohol, usually less than 0.5%
beer-like drink with very low alcohol

Examples

The bar offers near beer for customers who want the taste without the alcohol.
During her pregnancy, she occasionally enjoyed a cold near beer with dinner.
Many sports events serve near beer to accommodate those avoiding alcohol.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: non-alcoholic beer - beer-like beverage with little or no alcohol
