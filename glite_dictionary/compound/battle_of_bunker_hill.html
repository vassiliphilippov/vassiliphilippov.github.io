Battle of Bunker Hill

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˈbʌŋkər hɪl / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈbʌŋkə hɪl / (BrE)
a key early battle of the American War of Independence, fought in 1775 near Boston, in which British forces defeated the American colonists
first major battle of the American Revolution

Examples

The Battle of Bunker Hill was a turning point in the early stages of the American Revolution.
Many tourists visit the monument commemorating the Battle of Bunker Hill in Boston.
Although the British won, the high casualties at the Battle of Bunker Hill showed that the colonists would resist fiercely.
