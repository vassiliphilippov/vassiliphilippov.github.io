topic of debate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑː.pɪk əv dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.ɪk əv dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
a subject or issue that is widely discussed, especially one that provokes disagreement
subject widely discussed or disputed

Examples

The ethics of genetic engineering remain a topic of debate in the scientific community.
Climate change has become a major topic of debate among world leaders.
Whether homework is effective for students is regularly a topic of debate among educators.

Synset Examples

Castration is often a topic of debate among animal rights groups.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversy - prolonged public disagreement or heated discussion
  • Antonym: consensus - general agreement
