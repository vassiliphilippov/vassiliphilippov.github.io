true lovers' knot

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈlʌvərz nɑt / (AmE), / tru ˈlʌvəz nɒt / (BrE)
A stylized, decorative knot used as a symbol or emblem of love or affection, often seen in jewelry or artwork.
decorative knot symbolizing love

Examples

She wore a silver pendant shaped like a true lovers' knot around her neck.
The true lovers' knot has long been a symbol in romantic poetry and art.
They exchanged rings engraved with a delicate true lovers' knot to mark their commitment.
