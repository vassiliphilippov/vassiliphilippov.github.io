tortoise plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔːr.t̬əs plænt / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.tɔɪs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a South African vine with a large, woody rootstock covered in deeply fissured bark
South African vine with large, fissured rootstock

Examples

The tortoise plant is prized for the unusual appearance of its massive rootstock.
Botanists studied the tortoise plant to better understand its drought resistance.
Gardeners often cultivate the tortoise plant for its unique, sculpted base.
