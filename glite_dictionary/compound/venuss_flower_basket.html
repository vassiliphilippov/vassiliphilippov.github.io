Venus's flower basket

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈflaʊər ˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs ɪz ˈflaʊə ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a deep-water marine sponge with a cylindrical skeleton made of intricate glassy latticework, found in the waters of the East Indies and eastern Asia
marine sponge with glassy lattice skeleton

Examples

The Venus's flower basket is admired for its delicate, glass-like skeleton.
Collectors often display the Venus's flower basket as a natural piece of art.
