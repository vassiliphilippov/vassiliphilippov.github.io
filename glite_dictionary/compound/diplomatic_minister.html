diplomatic minister

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈmɪnɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk ˈmɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a diplomatic official representing one government to another, ranking below an ambassador
diplomat ranking below ambassador

Examples

The country appointed a new diplomatic minister to strengthen its relations with the neighboring state.
Before she became ambassador, she served as the diplomatic minister to France for five years.
A diplomatic minister often handles negotiations when an ambassador is unavailable.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ambassador - a diplomatic official of the highest rank
