Palm Beach

1. PROPN

/ pɑm ˈbiːtʃ / (AmE), / pɑːm ˈbiːtʃ / (BrE)
A resort town on an island off the Atlantic coast in southeast Florida, known for its beaches and luxury lifestyle.
resort town in southeast Florida

Examples

She spent her winter vacation relaxing in Palm Beach and enjoying the warm weather.
Many celebrities have homes in Palm Beach because of its luxury and privacy.
The annual charity gala at Palm Beach attracts visitors from around the world.
