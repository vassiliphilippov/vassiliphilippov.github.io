Dirty Harry

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɝːti ˈhæri / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti ˈhæri / (BrE)
the nickname of a fictional hard-edged police inspector played by Clint Eastwood in several films; used to refer metaphorically to a tough, uncompromising law enforcement officer
famous tough police inspector in film

Examples

He always admired Dirty Harry for his unwavering sense of justice in the face of danger.
The rookie cop was nicknamed Dirty Harry for his bold and fearless attitude.
After watching Dirty Harry, she became fascinated by detective films.

Synset Examples

Watching Dirty Harry inspired him to become a cop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police officer - a member of a police force
