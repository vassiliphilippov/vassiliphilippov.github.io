gardening knife

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdnɪŋ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːd(ə)nɪŋ naɪf / (BrE)
A knife specially designed or used for gardening tasks, such as cutting plants or roots.
knife used for gardening

Examples

She used a gardening knife to trim the overgrown roots of the plant.
A gardening knife is an essential tool for any serious gardener.
With a sharp gardening knife, he cut back the old branches before spring arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pruning knife - a small knife used for pruning plants
