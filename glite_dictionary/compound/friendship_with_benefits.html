friendship with benefits

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛndʃɪp wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndʃɪp wɪð ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (BrE)
a friendly relationship that also includes sexual activity without the commitment of a romantic partnership
casual sexual friendship
informal

Examples

After their breakups, they agreed to a friendship with benefits without any pressure for commitment.
Some people find a friendship with benefits easier than a traditional relationship.
Maintaining a friendship with benefits can be complicated if one person develops deeper feelings.

Synset Examples

They decided to keep their friendship with benefits casual.
Their friendship with benefits is working out well so far.
She was clear about wanting a friendship with benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friends with benefits - friends who engage in casual sexual activity
