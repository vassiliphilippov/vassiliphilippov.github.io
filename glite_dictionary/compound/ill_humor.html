ill humor

1. NOUN

/ ɪl ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / ɪl ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
a state of feeling angry, irritable, or disagreeable
angry or disagreeable mood

Examples

Despite his ill humor, he managed to apologize to his colleagues before leaving.
The child's ill humor after the argument lasted the entire afternoon.
She tried to cheer him up, but his ill humor persisted throughout dinner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good humor - a cheerful and pleasant mood
  • Synonym: irritability - the quality of being easily annoyed or angered
