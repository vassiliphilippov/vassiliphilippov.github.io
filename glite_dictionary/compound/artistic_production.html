artistic production

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪk prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of creating works of art or objects with aesthetic value
creation of works of art

Examples

The museum showcases a wide range of artistic production from local artists.
Her research focuses on the influence of social change on artistic production during the 20th century.
Funding for artistic production has increased significantly over the last decade.
