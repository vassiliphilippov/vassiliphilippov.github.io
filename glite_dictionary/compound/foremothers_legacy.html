foremother's legacy

1. NOUN

/ fɔrˌmʌðɚz ˈlɛɡəsi / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌmʌðəz ˈlɛɡəsi / (BrE)
the heritage, traditions, or values passed down from a female ancestor
heritage from a female ancestor

Examples

Her sense of justice is part of her foremother's legacy that guides her decisions.
The activist was determined to protect the land as a tribute to her foremother's legacy.
They celebrated their foremother's legacy by retelling old family stories and cooking traditional meals.

Synset Examples

She honored her foremother's legacy during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inheritance - property or traits received from ancestors
  • Antonym: patrimony - heritage from a male ancestor (father's side)
