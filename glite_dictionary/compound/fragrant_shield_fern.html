fragrant shield fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt ʃild fɝn / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt ʃiːld fɜːn / (BrE)
A species of fern found in northern Eurasia and North America, known for its pleasantly scented fronds.
fern with fragrant fronds

Examples

The fragrant shield fern is popular in gardens for its sweet-smelling leaves.
During our hike, we spotted a cluster of fragrant shield ferns along the shaded stream.
Botanists study the fragrant shield fern to learn about its unique aroma and resilience.
