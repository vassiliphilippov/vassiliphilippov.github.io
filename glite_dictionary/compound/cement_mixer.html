cement mixer

1. NOUN

/ səˈmɛnt ˌmɪksər / (AmE), / sɪˈment ˌmɪksə / (BrE)
a machine with a large rotating drum used to mix cement, sand, gravel, and water to make concrete
machine for mixing cement and concrete

Examples

The construction site was busy with workers and a cement mixer churning in the background.
We rented a cement mixer to help pour the foundation for the new house.
After the cement mixer broke down, the crew had to mix the concrete by hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concrete mixer - machine used to mix concrete
