round dance

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd dæns / (AmE), / raʊnd dɑːns / (BrE)
a ballroom dance in which the dancers perform revolving movements, usually in a circle or with partners rotating around a center
a ballroom dance with revolving movements

Examples

The couples gracefully performed the round dance at the evening ball.
She enjoys learning various types of round dance at her local dance club.
The round dance requires coordination and an awareness of your partner's movements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waltz - a ballroom dance in triple time with revolving movements

2. NOUN

/ ˈraʊnd ˌdæns / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌdɑːns / (BrE)
a folk dance in which the dancers form or move in a circle
folk dance in a circle

Examples

A group performed a traditional round dance at the festival, holding hands in a large circle.
The children enjoyed learning a simple round dance at summer camp.
Each village contributed its own unique style to the evening's round dance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circle dance - folk dance performed in a circle
