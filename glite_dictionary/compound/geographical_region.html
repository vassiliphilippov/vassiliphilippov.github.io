geographical region

1. NOUN

/ dʒiˌɑːɡrəˈfɪkəl ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / dʒiˌɒɡrəˈfɪkəl ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
an area of the Earth's surface that is clearly defined or demarcated for geographical, political, or cultural reasons
area defined for geographical or political reasons

Examples

The Amazon rainforest is the largest geographical region of its kind in the world.
This policy only applies within a specific geographical region of the country.
Many languages are spoken within the geographical region of Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: region - a particular area or part of the world or a country
