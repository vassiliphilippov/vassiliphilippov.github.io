Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈsaʊdi əˈreɪbiə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv ˈsaʊdi əˈreɪbɪə / (BrE)
a country in southwest Asia occupying most of the Arabian Peninsula, governed as an absolute monarchy and known for its vast oil reserves
country in southwest Asia; absolute monarchy

Examples

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is the largest country in the Middle East by land area.
Many pilgrims visit the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia each year for the Hajj pilgrimage.
Oil exports play a crucial role in the economy of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Saudi Arabia - a country in the Arabian Peninsula; alternative official name
