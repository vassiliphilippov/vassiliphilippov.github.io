in one bite

1. ADV

/ ɪn wʌn baɪt / (AmE), / ɪn wʌn baɪt / (BrE)
consumed completely or very quickly in a single mouthful or action
completely eaten in a single mouthful

Examples

He devoured the enormous sandwich in one bite.
She finished her dessert in one bite, impressing everyone at the table.
The snake swallowed the mouse in one bite.

Synset Examples

He finished the burger in one bite.
