Mack truck

1. NOUN

/ mæk trʌk / (AmE), / mæk trʌk / (BrE)
a large, heavy-duty truck manufactured by the Mack Trucks company, often used for long-distance hauling and freight transport
brand of heavy-duty truck

Examples

He proudly drove his new Mack truck down the interstate.
The company uses a fleet of Mack trucks to transport goods across the country.
She recognized the distinctive bulldog hood ornament of the Mack truck immediately.

Synset Examples

He drove a Mack truck across the country last summer.

Etymology

Named after the Mack Trucks company, founded by John M. Mack and Augustus F. Mack in the United States.
