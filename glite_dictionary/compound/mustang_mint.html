mustang mint

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.tæŋ mɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.tæŋ mɪnt / (BrE)
A fragrant annual herb native to California, with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of rose-purple flowers.
fragrant California annual herb

Examples

The hillside was covered in blooming mustang mint after the spring rains.
Gardeners prize mustang mint for its vibrant rose-purple flowers and strong fragrance.
We identified several wildflowers, including mustang mint, during our California hike.
