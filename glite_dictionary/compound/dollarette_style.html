dollarette style

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɑːləˈrɛt staɪl / (AmE), / ˌdɒləˈrɛt staɪl / (BrE)
a style of dress for young girls characterized by youthful and often decorative designs, reminiscent of dolls' clothing
style of dress for young girls

Examples

The fashion designer launched a new collection inspired by the dollarette style.
Many parents choose the dollarette style for their daughters' party dresses.
The boutique specializes in clothes that reflect the dollarette style popular among young girls.

Synset Examples

The dollarette style is perfect for little girls.

Etymology

From 'dollarette' (a diminutive of 'doll'), combined with 'style.'
