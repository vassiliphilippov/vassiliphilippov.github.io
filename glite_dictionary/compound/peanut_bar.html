peanut bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːnʌt bɑr / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt bɑː / (BrE)
A sweet confection made from peanuts held together with taffy or syrup, usually shaped into a bar.
a bar-shaped peanut confection

Examples

I bought a peanut bar at the convenience store for a quick snack.
The homemade peanut bar was crunchy and sweet, perfect with a cup of coffee.
She packed a peanut bar in her lunchbox for dessert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: candy bar - a sweet bar-shaped confection
