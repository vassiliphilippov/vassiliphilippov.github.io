food processor

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ˈprɑː.sɛs.ɚ / (AmE), / fuːd ˈprəʊ.ses.ə / (BrE)
a kitchen appliance with interchangeable blades, used for shredding, blending, chopping, or slicing food
kitchen appliance for chopping or blending food

Examples

She used the food processor to quickly chop vegetables for the salad.
A food processor makes preparing dough much easier and faster.
After buying a food processor, he started making homemade pesto regularly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blender - electric kitchen appliance for mixing or puréeing foods
  • Synonym: mixer - kitchen appliance for mixing ingredients
