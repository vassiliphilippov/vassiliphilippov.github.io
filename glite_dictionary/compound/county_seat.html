county seat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊnti siːt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊnti siːt / (BrE)
The town or city where the government offices of a county are situated.
administrative center of a county

Examples

Springfield is the county seat of Greene County in Missouri.
The courthouse is located in the county seat, making it a hub for local government affairs.
Many residents travel to the county seat for official business and public meetings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capital - a city where government of a country, region, or state is based
