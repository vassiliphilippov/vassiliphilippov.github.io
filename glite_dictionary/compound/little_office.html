Little Office

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a Roman Catholic devotional service honoring the Virgin Mary, similar to but shorter than the Divine Office
Catholic devotional service for the Virgin Mary

Examples

The monks gathered every evening to pray the Little Office together in the chapel.
She carries a copy of the Little Office for her daily prayers.
During Lent, many Catholics choose to recite the Little Office in addition to their usual devotions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Divine Office - the set of daily prayers prescribed by the Roman Catholic Church
