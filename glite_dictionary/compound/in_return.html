in return

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɹɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / ɪn ɹɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
as an exchange or as a response to something given or done
as an exchange or reciprocation

Examples

She helped me with my homework, and I baked cookies for her in return.
Businesses expect some benefits in return for their investments.
He offered advice and, in return, received gratitude from the team.

Synset Examples

gave up our seats on the plane and in return received several hundred dollars and seats on the next plane out
we get many benefits in return for our taxes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in exchange - as something given in place of something else
