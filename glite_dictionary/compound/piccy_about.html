piccy about

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪki əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈpɪki əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
Very particular or fussy about details or choices, especially in what one accepts or prefers.
very particular or fussy
informal

Examples

My sister is always so piccy about what she wears, it's hard to shop with her.
He’s piccy about the companies he applies to, only choosing those with a strong reputation.
They can be quite piccy about where they go on vacation, wanting only the best.

Synset Examples

He's so piccy about his food, it drives me crazy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picky - very particular about choices, especially food
