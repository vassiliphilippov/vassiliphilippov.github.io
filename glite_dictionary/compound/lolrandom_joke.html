lolrandom joke

1. NOUN

/ loʊl ˈrændəm dʒoʊk / (AmE), / lɒl ˈrændəm dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is intentionally absurd, nonsensical, or unexpected, designed to be funny through randomness or surprise.
absurd or nonsensical joke
slang

Examples

He loves to tell lolrandom jokes that make everyone at the table burst into laughter.
During the stream, the chat filled up with lolrandom jokes and memes.
Her talent for inventing lolrandom jokes brings a quirky charm to the group.

Synset Examples

She has a talent for making lolrandom jokes that lighten the mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense joke - a joke with no logical sense or meaning
  • Degree Higher: random - lacking a definite plan, purpose, or pattern

Etymology

Blend of 'LOL' (laugh out loud) from internet slang and 'random'; 'joke' meaning something said or done to provoke laughter.
