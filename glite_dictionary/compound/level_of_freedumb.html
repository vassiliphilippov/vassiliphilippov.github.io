level of freedumb

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛvəl əv ˈfriː.dʌm / (AmE), / ˈlɛv.əl əv ˈfriː.dʌm / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) A term used to describe a particularly foolish or senseless attitude or action, often in the context of someone championing 'freedom' in an ignorant or misguided way.
humorous term for foolishness or lack of sense
informal slang

Examples

It's hard to imagine the level of freedumb on display in some online forums.
The politician's comments reached a new level of freedumb that left everyone speechless.
People often laugh at the sheer level of freedumb in conspiracy theories.

Synset Examples

She couldn't believe the level of freedumb during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgment

Etymology

Humorous alteration of 'freedom' with 'dumb' to imply foolishness.
