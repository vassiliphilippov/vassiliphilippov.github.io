European sandpiper

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpi.ən ˈsændˌpaɪpər / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpi.ən ˈsændˌpaɪpə / (BrE)
A species of small wading bird native to Europe, belonging to the sandpiper family.
European species of sandpiper bird

Examples

The European sandpiper is often seen along estuaries and mudflats during migration season.
Researchers observed several European sandpipers feeding in the shallow waters of the river.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare European sandpiper in the coastal reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandpiper - a small wading bird of the family Scolopacidae
