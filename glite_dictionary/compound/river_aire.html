River Aire

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɪvər ˈɛər / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˈɛə / (BrE)
a river in northern England that flows southeast through West Yorkshire, eventually joining the River Ouse.
river in northern England

Examples

The River Aire is prone to flooding after heavy rainfall in West Yorkshire.
Many towns, including Leeds, are built along the banks of the River Aire.
Pollution levels in the River Aire have decreased significantly in recent years.
