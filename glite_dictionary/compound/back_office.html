back office

1. NOUN

/ bæk ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / bæk ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the department of a business or organization that undertakes administrative and support tasks not directly involving customers
administrative/support area of a business

Examples

The back office is responsible for processing invoices and payroll for the company.
Many employees in the back office never interact directly with clients.
After several years on the sales team, he transferred to a back office position in accounting.

Synset Examples

The back office handles all the administrative tasks.
The customer service team collaborates with the back office.
She works in the back office managing finances.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: front office - the part of a business that interacts directly with customers or clients
