genus Sula

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsuːlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈsuːlə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Sulidae, comprising the gannets and boobies, seabirds found in tropical and subtropical oceans.
type genus of Sulidae family (gannets, boobies)

Examples

The genus Sula includes several species of seabirds known as boobies.
Scientists discovered a new species in the genus Sula off the Pacific coast.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the populations of birds in the genus Sula.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sulidae - the biological family to which genus Sula belongs
  • Synonym: booby - a seabird in genus Sula

Etymology

From Latin 'sula', meaning 'gannet'.
