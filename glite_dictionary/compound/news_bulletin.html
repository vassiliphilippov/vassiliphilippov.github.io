news bulletin

1. NOUN

/ nuz ˈbʊlət̬ɪn / (AmE), / njuːz ˈbʊlɪtɪn / (BrE)
A short official announcement of the latest news, often broadcast on radio or television.
brief official news announcement

Examples

The television interrupted its regular program to broadcast a news bulletin about the earthquake.
We first heard about the presidential resignation on the morning news bulletin.
A breaking news bulletin updated viewers about the hurricane's path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news flash - a short, urgent news report on an important event
