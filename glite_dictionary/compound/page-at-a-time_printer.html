page-at-a-time printer

1. NOUN

/ ˌpeɪdʒ ət ə ˈtaɪm ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ˌpeɪdʒ ət ə ˈtaɪm ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
A printer that produces one page at a time rather than printing continuously.
printer that prints one page at a time

Examples

This old office still uses a page-at-a-time printer for all of its hard copies.
The technician explained that a page-at-a-time printer is slower than modern laser printers.
Many early computer labs relied on the reliability of a page-at-a-time printer.
