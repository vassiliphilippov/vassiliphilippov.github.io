age group

1. NOUN

/ eɪdʒ ɡruːp / (AmE), / eɪdʒ ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people who are approximately the same age, often considered together for statistical or social purposes
people of a similar age

Examples

Children in the 5–10 age group need parental supervision when swimming.
This movie is not suitable for any age group under 16.
The survey divided respondents into different age groups for analysis.
