Congressional Medal of Honor

1. NOUN

/ kənˌɡrɛʃənəl ˈmɛdəl əv ˈɑnər / (AmE), / kənˌɡrɛʃənəl ˈmɛdəl əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
the highest United States military decoration, awarded for extraordinary acts of bravery and valor above and beyond the call of duty
highest U.S. military valor award

Examples

He received the Congressional Medal of Honor for his heroic actions during the battle.
Only a few soldiers have ever been awarded the Congressional Medal of Honor.
The ceremony for the Congressional Medal of Honor was broadcast live on national television.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Medal of Honor - the official name of the highest U.S. military award for valor
