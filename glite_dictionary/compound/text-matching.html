text-matching

1. NOUN

/ tɛkst ˈmætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tɛkst ˈmatʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or application of searching for text strings in data that match a specified pattern or query
searching for matching text strings in data

Examples

Many plagiarism detection tools rely on text-matching algorithms to compare student assignments.
Effective text-matching can help companies filter out spam emails efficiently.
The search engine improved its text-matching to provide more relevant results.

Synset Examples

Google uses text-matching to find web pages containing a particular text

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pattern-matching - the process of checking if a certain pattern is present
