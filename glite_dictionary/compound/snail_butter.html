snail butter

1. NOUN

/ sneɪl ˈbʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / sneɪl ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
A seasoned butter mixture with shallots, garlic, and parsley, used especially for cooking snails.
butter mix for cooking snails

Examples

The chef prepared a rich snail butter to serve with freshly baked bread.
Add a dollop of snail butter to each shell before baking the escargot.
Her recipe for snail butter calls for extra parsley and a hint of lemon zest.
