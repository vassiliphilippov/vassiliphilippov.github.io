redheaded woodpecker

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdˌhɛdɪd ˈwʊdˌpɛkər / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˌhɛdɪd ˈwʊdˌpɛkə / (BrE)
a medium-sized North American woodpecker with striking black and white plumage and a bright red head and neck
North American woodpecker with a red head

Examples

We spotted a redheaded woodpecker perched on the edge of the forest.
The redheaded woodpecker is known for its bold coloring and loud calls.
Local birdwatchers often record sightings of the redheaded woodpecker during the spring migration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodpecker - a bird that pecks at tree bark to find insects
