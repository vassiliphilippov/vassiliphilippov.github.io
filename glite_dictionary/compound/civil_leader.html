civil leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪvəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who plays a prominent role in the administration or affairs of a city or community
leader in community or city affairs

Examples

The civil leader addressed the town hall meeting about upcoming changes to public transportation.
As a respected civil leader, she advocated for better education policies in the local government.
Many citizens look to the civil leader for guidance during community crises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: community leader - a leader in local community matters
  • Degree Higher: political leader - someone who leads or is prominent in politics
