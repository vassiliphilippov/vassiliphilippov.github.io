Texas accent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksəs ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
the distinctive way of speaking English that is characteristic of people from Texas
regional English accent of Texas

Examples

Her Texas accent became more pronounced after she moved back to Dallas.
People often comment on his strong Texas accent during business calls.
The movie character's Texas accent added authenticity to the role.

Synset Examples

Her friends teased her for her Texass accent.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Southern accent - the English accent characteristic of the Southern United States
