slender centaury

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɛndər sɛnˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˈsɛntʃʊəri / (BrE)
a slender species of centaury, an annual herb with small pink flowers, found in grassy places and meadows
a slender annual flowering herb

Examples

The slender centaury thrives in sandy soils and open meadows.
Botanists identified several specimens of slender centaury along the nature trail.
The fields were dotted with the delicate pink flowers of slender centaury during summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a genus of small herbs with pink flowers
