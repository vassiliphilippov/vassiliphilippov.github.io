Multikulti festival

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʊltiˈkʌlti ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌlti ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
a festival that promotes or celebrates multiculturalism and cultural diversity
festival celebrating multiculturalism

Examples

The city's annual Multikulti festival attracts thousands of visitors from around the world.
Local musicians from diverse backgrounds performed together at the Multikulti festival last weekend.
She volunteered to coordinate art workshops at the Multikulti festival this summer.

Synset Examples

The Multikulti festival celebrates cultural diversity in the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiculturalism - the presence or support of several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society

Etymology

From German 'Multikulti', a shortening of 'multikulturell' (multicultural).
