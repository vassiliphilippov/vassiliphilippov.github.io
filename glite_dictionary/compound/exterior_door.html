exterior door

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɪriɚ dɔr / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪəriə dɔː / (BrE)
a door that provides entrance to or exit from the outside of a building
door leading to the outside

Examples

Make sure the exterior door is locked before you leave the house.
The new house features a sturdy exterior door with a modern design.
He forgot his keys and was locked out, standing in front of the exterior door.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interior door - a door located inside a building, not leading outside
