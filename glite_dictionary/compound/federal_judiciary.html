Federal Judiciary

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl dʒuːˈdɪʃiˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl dʒuːˈdɪʃəri / (BrE)

U.S. national court system
american legal

Examples

Cases involving constitutional issues often progress to the highest levels of the Federal Judiciary.
