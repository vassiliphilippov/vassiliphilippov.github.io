piece of furniture

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈfɜrnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
an individual item such as a chair, table, or bed that is part of the furnishings in a room or building
individual item of furniture

Examples

There was only one piece of furniture in the room: an old wooden chair.
After moving in, they bought each piece of furniture separately to match their style.
Can you help me move this heavy piece of furniture into the living room?

Synset Examples

they had too much furniture for the small apartment
there was only one piece of furniture in the room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: furniture - large movable equipment, such as tables and chairs, used to make a room suitable for living or working
