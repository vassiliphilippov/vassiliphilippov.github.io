elevator girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtɚ ɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman employed to operate an elevator, especially in a department store or office building
a female elevator operator
archaic

Examples

She worked as an elevator girl in a busy downtown department store during the 1950s.
The uniformed elevator girl greeted each passenger with a polite smile.
Stories of the elevator girl capture a vanished era of city life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elevator operator - a person employed to operate an elevator
