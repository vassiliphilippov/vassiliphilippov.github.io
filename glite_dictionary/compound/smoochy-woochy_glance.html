smoochy-woochy glance

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuːtʃi ˈwuːtʃi ɡlæns / (AmE), / ˈsmuːtʃi ˈwuːtʃi ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
an affectionate or flirtatious look, often exchanged playfully or romantically
affectionate or flirtatious look
informal

Examples

They exchanged smoochy-woochy glances across the dimly lit café.
His smoochy-woochy glance made her blush instantly.
Their playful banter was often accompanied by a smoochy-woochy glance.

Synset Examples

They exchanged smoochy-woochy glances across the room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flirtatious glance - an amorous, inviting look
  • Antonym: stare - an intent, prolonged look

Etymology

Formed from 'smoochy-woochy' (playful reduplication from 'smooch', meaning 'kiss') + 'glance'.
