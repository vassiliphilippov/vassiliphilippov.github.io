commercial activity

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmɝʃəl ækˈtɪvɪti / (AmE), / kəˌmɜːʃl ækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
activity carried out as part of a business with the aim of making a profit.
business-related activity for profit

Examples

The government has introduced new regulations affecting commercial activity in the region.
Many cities rely on commercial activity to boost their local economies.
Any commercial activity conducted on public land requires a special permit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business - the practice of making one's living by engaging in commerce
