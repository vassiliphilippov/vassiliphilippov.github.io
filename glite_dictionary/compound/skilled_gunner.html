skilled gunner

1. NOUN

/ skɪld ˈɡʌnər / (AmE), / skɪld ˈɡʌnə / (BrE)
a person who is highly proficient in operating and firing guns, especially in a military or competitive context
a highly proficient gun operator or marksman

Examples

The army relied on their skilled gunner to protect the frontline during battle.
As a skilled gunner, she participated in national shooting competitions and often won medals.
A skilled gunner can make the difference between victory and defeat in a heated exchange.

Synset Examples

He's a skilled gunner with years of experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marksman - a person skilled in shooting
  • Synonym: sharpshooter - an expert shooter
