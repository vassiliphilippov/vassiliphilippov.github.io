bull nettle

1. NOUN

/ bʊl ˈnɛt̬əl / (AmE), / bʊl ˈnɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a coarse, prickly North American plant with pale yellow flowers and berrylike fruit, commonly found in the southern and eastern United States
a prickly North American plant

Examples

The field was dotted with bull nettle, making it difficult to walk barefoot.
Gardeners in the South often struggle to remove bull nettle from their lawns.
You should wear gloves when handling bull nettle because of its sharp spines.
