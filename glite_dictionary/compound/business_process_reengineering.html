business process reengineering

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˈprɑːsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈprəʊsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the examination and redesign of business workflows and processes to achieve significant improvements in performance, efficiency, and quality
redesign of business workflows for improvement

Examples

Many companies undertake business process reengineering to boost efficiency and remain competitive.
The firm's success was largely attributed to strategic business process reengineering.
He led several business process reengineering initiatives during his time as operations manager.

Synset Examples

This is part of an effort by the DoD to provide an overarching methodology for improvement projects, and it includes business process reengineering as one of the key methods to achieve process improvement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: process improvement - the practice of identifying, analyzing, and improving business processes

Etymology

From 'business process' + 'reengineering,' coined in management literature in the early 1990s.
