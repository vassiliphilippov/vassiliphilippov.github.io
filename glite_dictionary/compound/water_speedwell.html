water speedwell

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈspiːdˌwɛl / (BrE)
a plant with spikes of small blue flowers, typically found in wet places in Eurasia and America (Veronica anagallis-aquatica)
plant of wet habitats, Veronica anagallis-aquatica

Examples

The water speedwell thrives along the edges of slow-moving streams and ponds.
During our hike, we spotted clusters of water speedwell blooming near the riverbank.
Gardeners often choose water speedwell for wetland restoration projects due to its adaptability.
