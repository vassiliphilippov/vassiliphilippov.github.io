sword lily

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / sɔːd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Gladiolus, having sword-shaped leaves and tall flower spikes with bright, showy blooms.
plant with sword-shaped leaves and striking flowers

Examples

The sword lily adds vibrant color to summer gardens with its tall, graceful spikes of flowers.
She arranged a bouquet of sword lilies for the dining table, their blooms opening in the afternoon sunlight.
Gardeners often plant sword lilies for their dramatic foliage and long-lasting flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gladiolus - a plant of the genus Gladiolus, commonly called sword lily

Etymology

Named for its sword-like leaves; 'sword' (from Old English 'sweord') + 'lily'.

2. NOUN

/ sɔrd ˈlɪli / (AmE), / sɔːd ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Gladiolus, with sword-shaped leaves and spikes of brightly colored flowers, widely cultivated.
a plant of the genus Gladiolus

Examples

The sword lily is a popular choice for brightening up summer gardens.
She arranged a bouquet of sword lilies for the table centerpiece.
Gardeners often plant sword lily bulbs in the spring for a vibrant display.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gladiolus - a plant of the genus Gladiolus, also known as sword lily

Etymology

From the plant's sword-shaped (linear) leaves; 'sword' + 'lily'.
