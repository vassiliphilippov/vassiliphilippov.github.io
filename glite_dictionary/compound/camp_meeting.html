camp meeting

1. NOUN

/ kæmp ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / kæmp ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a large outdoor or tent-based religious meeting, typically evangelical, lasting several days
large outdoor religious gathering

Examples

The community gathered for the annual camp meeting that lasted four days and drew hundreds of people.
Many people found spiritual inspiration during the revival-style camp meeting under the open sky.
She attended a historic camp meeting where preachers spoke from a wooden platform to a large audience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revival - an evangelistic meeting or series of meetings for spiritual renewal
