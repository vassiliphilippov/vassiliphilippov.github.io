yuppity neighborhood

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʌpɪti ˈneɪbərˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈjʌpɪti ˈneɪbəhʊd / (BrE)
An informal term for a fashionable and upscale area that is popular among young professionals.
upscale area popular with young professionals
informal

Examples

She moved into a yuppity neighborhood with coffee shops and yoga studios on every corner.
The city's most yuppity neighborhood attracts tech workers and creative professionals alike.
Housing prices in the yuppity neighborhood have soared due to its growing popularity.

Synset Examples

The yuppity neighborhood was filled with trendy boutiques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yuppie - a young urban professional
  • Synonym: upscale neighborhood - a wealthy and fashionable area

Etymology

Formed from 'yuppie' (young urban professional) and 'neighborhood'.
