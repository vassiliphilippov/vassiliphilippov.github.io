gravida II

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈviːdə ˈtuː / (AmE), / ɡrəˈviːdə ˈtuː / (BrE)
A woman who is pregnant for the second time.
woman pregnant for the second time
medical

Examples

The medical chart indicated she was a gravida II with no previous complications.
As a gravida II, she knew what to expect during her second pregnancy.
The study focused on the outcomes of gravida II and multiparous women.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gravida I - a woman pregnant for the first time
  • Degree Higher: gravida III - a woman pregnant for the third time

Etymology

From Latin 'gravida' (pregnant) and Roman numeral II (two), used in medical terminology to denote pregnancy order.
