Saale River

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɑl.ə ˈrɪv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.lə ˈrɪv.ə / (BrE)
a river in central Germany that flows north to join the Elbe River
river in central Germany

Examples

The Saale River passes through several historic cities in Germany.
During our trip to Saxony, we enjoyed a picnic by the Saale River.
The Saale River eventually merges with the Elbe, one of Germany's great waterways.

Etymology

From German 'Saale,' the name of the river.
