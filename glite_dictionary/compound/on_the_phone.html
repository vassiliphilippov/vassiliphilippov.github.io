on the phone

1. ADP

/ ɑn ðə foʊn / (AmE), / ɒn ðə fəʊn / (BrE)
using a telephone to talk to someone; engaged in a telephone conversation
using a telephone to talk

Examples

She can't come to the door right now; she's on the phone with her boss.
I waited patiently while he was on the phone making a reservation.
It's difficult to concentrate when you're on the phone and someone is talking loudly nearby.

Synset Examples

She was on the phone when I arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the line - talking on the telephone (informal)
