ear specialist

1. NOUN

/ ɪr ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ɪə ˈspɛʃəlɪst / (BrE)
a physician who specializes in treating diseases and disorders of the ear
doctor specializing in ear disorders
medical

Examples

My mother made an appointment with an ear specialist to check her hearing problems.
After months of discomfort, John finally saw an ear specialist who diagnosed an inner ear infection.
The ear specialist recommended a series of hearing tests for the patient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otologist - a physician specializing in diseases of the ear
  • Synonym: audiologist - a specialist in the assessment and management of hearing disorders
