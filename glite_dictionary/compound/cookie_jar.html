cookie jar

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊki dʒɑr / (AmE), / ˈkʊki dʒɑː / (BrE)
A jar or container used for storing cookies, and sometimes used for concealing money or other items.
jar for storing cookies

Examples

She reached into the cookie jar and grabbed two chocolate chip cookies for herself.
The children were caught with their hands in the cookie jar before dinner.
He kept his spare change hidden in a cookie jar on the kitchen shelf.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkʊki ˌdʒɑr / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ˌdʒɑː / (BrE)
A jar used for storing cookies, often kept in the kitchen; sometimes also used to conceal money or valuables.
jar for storing cookies (or money)

Examples

He reached into the cookie jar and grabbed a handful of chocolate chip cookies.
They kept emergency money hidden in an old cookie jar on the top shelf.
As children, we used to sneak extra treats from the cookie jar when Mom wasn't looking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biscuit tin - a container for keeping biscuits, especially in British English
