definite quantity

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛfɪnət ˈkwɑntəti / (AmE), / ˈdɛfɪnət ˈkwɒntəti / (BrE)
a specific, clearly defined measure or amount of something
a specific, set amount

Examples

The recipe calls for a definite quantity of sugar to ensure the cake rises properly.
Scientists need a definite quantity of the chemical to run the experiment accurately.
He asked for a definite quantity of water, not just an estimate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indefinite quantity - an unspecified or not clearly defined amount
