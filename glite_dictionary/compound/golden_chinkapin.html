golden chinkapin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈtʃɪŋkəˌpɪn / (BrE)
a small evergreen tree of the Pacific Coast of North America, with glossy yellow-green leaves and edible nuts
Pacific Coast evergreen tree with edible nuts

Examples

The golden chinkapin thrives in the moist forests along the Pacific coastline.
In autumn, hikers often search for the edible nuts of the golden chinkapin.
The glossy leaves of the golden chinkapin add beauty to any garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chinkapin - a type of tree or shrub producing edible nuts
