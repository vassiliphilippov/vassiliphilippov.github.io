baby boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi bɔɪ / (BrE)
a very young male child; an infant son
very young male child

Examples

The proud parents held their baby boy for the first time at the hospital.
After months of anticipation, they welcomed a healthy baby boy into their family.
The blue blanket indicated that the newborn was a baby boy.

Synset Examples

Every parent loves their baby boy unconditionally.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: baby girl - a very young female child; an infant daughter
