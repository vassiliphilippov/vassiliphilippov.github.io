tomato juice

1. NOUN

/ təˈmeɪɾoʊ dʒus / (AmE), / təˈmɑːtəʊ dʒuːs / (BrE)
the liquid extracted from tomatoes, commonly consumed as a beverage or used in cooking
liquid from tomatoes, often a drink

Examples

She ordered a glass of tomato juice with her breakfast.
The recipe calls for two cups of tomato juice as the base for the soup.
Many people enjoy drinking tomato juice on long flights.
