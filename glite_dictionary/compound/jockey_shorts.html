jockey shorts

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑki ˌʃɔrts / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒki ˌʃɔːts / (BrE)
A style of short, tight-fitting men's underpants, originally trademarked but now used generically.
men's short, tight-fitting underpants

Examples

He bought several pairs of jockey shorts for his trip.
Many athletes prefer to wear jockey shorts for comfort during training.
His jockey shorts were visible above his jeans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: briefs - short, close-fitting underpants

Etymology

Originally a trademark from Jockey, an American underwear brand, first used in the 1930s.
