old folks' home

1. NOUN

/ oʊld foʊks hoʊm / (AmE), / əʊld fəʊks həʊm / (BrE)
a residential facility where elderly people live and receive care
residential facility for elderly people
informal

Examples

Every weekend, she brings homemade cookies to the old folks' home in her neighborhood.
After his fall, John moved into an old folks' home where he could get daily assistance.
The city council is planning to renovate the local old folks' home to improve living conditions.

Synset Examples

They decided to visit the old folks' home on Sunday.
The community organized a charity event for the old folks' home.
He volunteers every week at the local old folks' home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursing home - a residential facility for people who need constant medical care
  • Synonym: retirement home - a place where retired people live and may receive some care
