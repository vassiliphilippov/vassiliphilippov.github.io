soft ice cream

1. NOUN

/ sɔft aɪs krim / (AmE), / sɒft aɪs kriːm / (BrE)
a frozen dessert similar to traditional ice cream, but softer and typically made with a boiled custard base, often served directly from a machine
soft frozen ice cream dessert

Examples

After lunch, we treated ourselves to soft ice cream at the park.
The new café serves delicious vanilla soft ice cream in a cone.
Kids lined up outside the truck waiting for their favorite soft ice cream flavors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice cream - a sweet frozen dessert made from cream or milk and sugar
