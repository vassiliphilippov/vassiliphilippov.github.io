dining compartment

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a section of a passenger train where meals are served to travelers during the journey
train car where meals are served

Examples

We enjoyed breakfast in the dining compartment as the train rolled through the countryside.
The dining compartment was beautifully set up, offering a delicious menu to all passengers.
After a long journey, they headed to the dining compartment for a hot meal and some conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining car - a railway passenger car where food is served
