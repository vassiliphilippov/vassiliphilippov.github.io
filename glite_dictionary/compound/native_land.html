native land

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.tɪv lænd / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv lænd / (BrE)
the country where a person was born, regarded emotionally or personally as their homeland
country of one's birth

Examples

After many years abroad, he longed to return to his native land.
She always speaks fondly of her native land and its traditions.
Many immigrants never forget the taste of food from their native land.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homeland - a person's native country or region; the place where one belongs
  • Antonym: foreign land - a country other than one's own
