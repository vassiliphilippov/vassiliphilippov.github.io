tonsorial skill

1. NOUN

/ tɑnˈsɔriəl skɪl / (AmE), / tɒnˈsɔːriəl skɪl / (BrE)
the ability or expertise in cutting, shaping, and styling hair
skill in cutting and styling hair

Examples

The celebrity sought out a salon renowned for its tonsorial skill.
Decades of tonsorial skill have made her the most respected stylist in town.
His tonsorial skill transformed my unruly hair into a work of art.

Synset Examples

She appreciated the tonsorial skills of her barber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbering - the profession or activity of cutting and styling hair, especially men's

Etymology

From Latin tonsorius (of a barber), from tondere (to shear or clip); skill from Old Norse skil (distinction, discernment).
