educational activity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃənl ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌedjʊˈkeɪʃənl ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
an activity designed to teach knowledge or skills, especially in a formal or organized setting
activity designed to teach knowledge or skills

Examples
