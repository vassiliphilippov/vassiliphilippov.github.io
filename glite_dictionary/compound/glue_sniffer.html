glue sniffer

1. NOUN

/ ɡlu snɪfɚ / (AmE), / ɡluː ˈsnɪfə / (BrE)
a person who inhales fumes from glue or similar substances to get intoxicated
person who abuses inhalants like glue
offensive

Examples

The school launched a campaign to discourage students from becoming glue sniffers.
Local authorities are concerned about the rising number of glue sniffers in the city.
He denied ever being a glue sniffer despite the rumors.

Synset Examples

Don’t be a glue sniffer; focus on your work instead.
The kid was acting like a glue sniffer, overly hyper and unpredictable.
He was labeled a glue sniffer for his odd behaviors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhalant abuser - a person who abuses inhalants for intoxication
