town planning

1. NOUN

/ taʊn ˈplænɪŋ / (AmE), / taʊn ˈplænɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or profession of designing and organizing the development and arrangement of towns and cities
design and organization of urban areas

Examples

She studied town planning at university before working for the city council.
Good town planning helps create attractive, functional, and sustainable communities.
Many cities hire specialists in town planning to manage growth and development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urban planning - the planning and design of urban environments
