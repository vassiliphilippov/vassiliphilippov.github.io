raving mad

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪvɪŋ mæd / (AmE), / ˈreɪvɪŋ mad / (BrE)
wildly or uncontrollably insane; behaving or speaking in a completely irrational way
wildly insane or irrational

Examples

After days without sleep, he seemed raving mad to his friends.
The suspect was shouting in the street like someone raving mad.
Many dismissed her predictions as the cries of a raving mad woman.

Synset Examples

a raving lunatic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mad - mentally ill; insane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mentally ill
