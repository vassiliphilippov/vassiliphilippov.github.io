churchy attire

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɝːtʃi əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃi əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
clothing that is considered appropriate or suitable for attending church services
clothing suitable for church

Examples

She put on her churchy attire before heading out to Sunday morning service.
His grandmother always insisted on churchy attire for important religious holidays.
Many people reserve special clothes as their churchy attire to show respect during worship.

Synset Examples

His churchy attire included a suit and tie for Sunday service.
