plumbing system

1. NOUN

/ ˈplʌmɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈplʌmɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A network of pipes and fixtures in a building that supplies water and gas and removes waste.
network of pipes and fixtures in a building

Examples

The house needed a new plumbing system after the old pipes began to leak.
Modern buildings are designed with an efficient plumbing system to provide clean water and remove waste.
He called a contractor to inspect the plumbing system before moving into his new apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumbing - the work or system of installing and maintaining pipes and fixtures
