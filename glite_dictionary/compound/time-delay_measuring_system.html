time-delay measuring system

1. NOUN

/ taɪm dɪˈleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / taɪm dɪˈleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system or device used to measure the time interval between two separate events, commonly used in scientific and technical fields
device for measuring time intervals

Examples

A time-delay measuring system was used to record the precise interval between the two signals.
Engineers relied on a time-delay measuring system to synchronize the equipment accurately.
The laboratory invested in a new time-delay measuring system to improve the accuracy of their experiments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chronoscope - an instrument used to measure short time intervals accurately
