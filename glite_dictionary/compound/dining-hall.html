dining-hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪnɪŋ hɔl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room in a college or university where meals are served and eaten
large college or university dining room

Examples

We always meet for lunch in the dining-hall after our morning lectures.
The university's main dining-hall can accommodate hundreds of students.
During exam week, the dining-hall stays open late to serve stressed students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafeteria - a place where people eat, often referring to a dining area offering food for purchase
