leg curling

1. NOUN

/ lɛg ˈkɜːrlɪŋ / (AmE), / lɛg ˈkɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an exercise in which the flexor muscles at the back of the thigh are strengthened by bending the knee against resistance
exercise for strengthening leg flexors

Examples

Doing leg curling regularly can help improve your hamstring strength.
She added leg curling to her workout to prevent muscle imbalances.
Most gyms have machines specifically designed for leg curling exercises.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leg curl - exercise targeting the hamstring muscles
