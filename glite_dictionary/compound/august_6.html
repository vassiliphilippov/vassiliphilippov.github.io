August 6

1. PROPN

/ ˈɔɡəst sɪks / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡəst sɪks / (BrE)
A Christian church festival held on August 6 in commemoration of the Transfiguration of Jesus.
Christian festival marking the Transfiguration

Examples

Many churches hold special services on August 6 to mark the Transfiguration of Jesus.
The feast of August 6 is observed annually by several Christian denominations.
Pilgrims gather in Jerusalem every August 6 to celebrate this important event.
