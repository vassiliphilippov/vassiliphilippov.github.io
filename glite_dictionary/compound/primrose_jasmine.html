primrose jasmine

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪmˌroʊz ˈdʒæzmɪn / (AmE), / ˈprɪmˌrəʊz ˈdʒæzmɪn / (BrE)
an evergreen climbing shrub native to western China, with yellow flowers resembling those of a primrose
evergreen yellow-flowered shrub of western China

Examples

The primrose jasmine adds a cheerful splash of yellow to gardens in early spring.
We planted a primrose jasmine along the fence for its beautiful, arching branches.
In western China, the primrose jasmine can be seen growing wild on the hillsides.
