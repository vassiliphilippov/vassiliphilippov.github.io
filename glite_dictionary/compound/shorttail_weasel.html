shorttail weasel

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔːrt.teɪl ˈwiː.zəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.teɪl ˈwiː.zəl / (BrE)
A small carnivorous mammal of the Northern Hemisphere, especially in its white winter coat; also known as an ermine or stoat.
small mustelid, white in winter

Examples

The fur of the shorttail weasel turns pure white during winter, helping it blend into the snow.
A shorttail weasel darted across the forest floor, its white coat barely visible against the snow.
Farmers appreciate the shorttail weasel for keeping the rodent population under control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ermine - Stoat or shorttail weasel in its white winter coat
  • Synonym: stoat - Small mustelid, brown in summer and white in winter
