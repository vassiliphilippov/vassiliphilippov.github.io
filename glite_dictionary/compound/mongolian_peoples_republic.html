Mongolian People's Republic

1. PROPN

/ mɑŋˈɡoʊliən ˈpiːpəlz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / mɒŋˈɡəʊliən ˈpiːp(ə)lz rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the former name of Mongolia when it was a socialist republic in central Asia from 1924 to 1992
historical name for Mongolia (1924–1992)

Examples

The Mongolian People's Republic was established in 1924 with Soviet support.
During the era of the Mongolian People's Republic, the country followed a socialist political system.
After 1992, the Mongolian People's Republic became known simply as Mongolia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mongolia - a country in east-central Asia, formerly the Mongolian People's Republic
