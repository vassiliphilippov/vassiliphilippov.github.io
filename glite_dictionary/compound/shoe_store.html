shoe store

1. NOUN

/ ʃuː stɔr / (AmE), / ʃuː stɔː / (BrE)
a shop or store that sells shoes to customers
a store where shoes are sold

Examples

She went to the shoe store to buy a new pair of sneakers.
The shoe store in the mall is having a big sale this weekend.
He got a job working at a local shoe store after school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoe shop - a shop where shoes are sold (British English)
