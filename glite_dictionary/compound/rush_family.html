rush family

1. NOUN

/ rʌʃ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / rʌʃ ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of tufted, grass-like herbs known as rushes (family Juncaceae).
family of grass-like herbs (Juncaceae)

Examples

The rush family includes many species found in wetlands around the world.
Botanists classify true rushes as members of the rush family.
Unlike grasses, the rush family features round, unjointed stems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush - a grass-like plant of the genus Juncus
