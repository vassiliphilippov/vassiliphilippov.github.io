minor league

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪnɚ liɡ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə liːɡ / (BrE)
a sports league made up of teams that are not part of the major league, especially in baseball
sports league below the major league

Examples

He started his career in the minor league before moving up to the majors.
Many talented players spend several years in the minor league developing their skills.
The team was promoted from the minor league after an excellent season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: major league - the principal or highest-level sports league
