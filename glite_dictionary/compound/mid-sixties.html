mid-sixties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈsɪkstiːz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈsɪkstiːz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between about 64 and 66 years of age
age from about 64 to 66 years

Examples

After retiring in his mid-sixties, he traveled around the world.
Many people in their mid-sixties remain active and involved in the community.
She started painting as a hobby in her mid-sixties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sixties - the decade from age 60 to 69
  • Degree Lower: early sixties - the period from age 60 to 63
  • Degree Higher: late sixties - the period from age 67 to 69
