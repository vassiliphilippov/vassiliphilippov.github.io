hoop ash

1. NOUN

/ huːp æʃ / (AmE), / huːp æʃ / (BrE)
A vigorous, spreading North American tree with dark brown heavy wood and leaves that turn gold in autumn; a type of ash tree.
species of North American ash tree

Examples

The hoop ash is admired for its golden foliage in the fall.
Woodworkers value the hoop ash for its strong and durable timber.
We planted a hoop ash in the backyard to provide shade and color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ash - a type of tree in the genus Fraxinus
