under-the-counter

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndər ðə ˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / ˌʌndə ðə ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
(of goods or transactions) done or sold illicitly and in secret, typically to avoid legal restrictions.
sold or done illicitly and secretly

Examples

Some pharmacies were caught selling under-the-counter drugs without prescriptions.
The antique store sometimes sold under-the-counter items to avoid paying taxes.
He was fired for arranging under-the-counter deals at his previous job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal - not allowed by law
  • Synonym: black market - illegal traffic or trade in officially controlled or scarce commodities
  • Antonym: above board - legal and open; not secretive or deceptive
