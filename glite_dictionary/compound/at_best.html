at best

1. ADV

/ ət ˈbɛst / (AmE), / ət ˈbɛst / (BrE)
Used to indicate the most favorable interpretation, outcome, or circumstance; only under the most favorable conditions.
in the most favorable scenario

Examples

At best, the plan will only break even, not turn a profit.
She will, at best, recover partially from the injury.
This solution is, at best, a temporary fix to a much deeper problem.

Synset Examples

at best we'll lose only the money

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at worst - used to indicate the most unfavorable scenario
