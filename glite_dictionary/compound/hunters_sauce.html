hunter's sauce

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɚz sɔs / (AmE), / ˈhʌntəz sɔːs / (BrE)
A French-style brown sauce made with tomato purée, onions, mushrooms, and dry white wine, typically served with meats.
sauce with wine, mushrooms, and tomato

Examples

The chicken was served with a rich hunter's sauce that complemented the flavors perfectly.
Many French recipes for game meats call for a traditional hunter's sauce.
Adding mushrooms and wine gives hunter's sauce its distinctive taste.

Etymology

From French *sauce chasseur*, literally 'hunter's sauce'.
