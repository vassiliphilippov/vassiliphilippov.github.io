City of San Marino

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪti əv sæn məˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɪti əv san məˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and only city of the Republic of San Marino, located in southern Europe
capital city of San Marino

Examples

The City of San Marino is famous for its medieval architecture and historic fortresses.
During their European tour, they spent a day exploring the streets of the City of San Marino.
The population of the City of San Marino is smaller than that of many small towns.

Etymology

San Marino is named after Saint Marinus, a Christian stonemason and founder of the republic.
