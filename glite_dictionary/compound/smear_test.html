smear test

1. NOUN

/ smɪr tɛst / (AmE), / smɪə tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test in which cells taken from a woman's cervix are examined for signs of cervical cancer or other abnormalities
medical test for cervical cancer screening
medical british

Examples

The doctor recommended a smear test as part of her routine health check-up.
She was anxious while waiting for her smear test results to come back from the lab.
Regular smear tests are important for early detection of cervical cancer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pap test - a test for cervical cancer in women, also called Pap smear
