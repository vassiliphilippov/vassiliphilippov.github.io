Foggy Bottom

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɑɡi ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡi ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
A nickname for the United States Department of State, originating from the name of the area in Washington, D.C. where the department is located.
nickname for the U.S. State Department

Examples

Many policy decisions are made quietly in Foggy Bottom before reaching the public.
Congress questioned Foggy Bottom officials about the new diplomatic strategy.
The ambassador's appointment was announced by Foggy Bottom late last night.

Etymology

Named after the Foggy Bottom area of Washington, D.C.
