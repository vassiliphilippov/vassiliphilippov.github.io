zinc supplement

1. NOUN

/ zɪŋk ˈsʌpləmənt / (AmE), / zɪŋk ˈsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
a dietary product taken to increase the amount of zinc in the diet, typically as a tablet, capsule, or liquid
dietary product with zinc

Examples

My doctor recommended a zinc supplement to help improve my immune system.
You can find zinc supplements at most pharmacies and health food stores.
Some people experience digestive discomfort after taking a zinc supplement on an empty stomach.

Synset Examples

She takes a zinc supplement for her immune health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dietary supplement - a product intended to supplement the diet
