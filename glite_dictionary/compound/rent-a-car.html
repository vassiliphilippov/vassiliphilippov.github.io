rent-a-car

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛnt ə kɑr / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt ə kɑː / (BrE)
a car that is rented for a short period, especially by travelers or visitors
a car available to rent

Examples

We arrived late at night and picked up our rent-a-car from the airport.
After comparing prices, she decided on a compact rent-a-car for the weekend trip.
Many tourists choose a rent-a-car to explore the surrounding area at their own pace.

Synset Examples

she picked up a hire car at the airport and drove to her hotel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rental car - a car that is rented, usually for a short time
  • Synonym: hire car - British term for a rented car
