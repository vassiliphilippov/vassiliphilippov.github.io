yellow-orange

1. ADJ

/ ˌjɛloʊ ˈɔːrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊ ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
of a color that is predominantly orange with a tinged or mixture of yellow; situated between yellow and orange in the color spectrum
orange tinged with yellow color

Examples

The walls of the dining room were painted a cheerful yellow-orange shade.
She chose a yellow-orange apple out of the basket for her snack.
At sunset, the sky glowed with streaks of yellow-orange and red.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: orange - a bright reddish-yellow color
  • Degree Higher: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum, a primary color
