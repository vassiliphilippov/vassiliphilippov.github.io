Kingdom of Sweden

1. PROPN

/ ˈswidn̩ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdn̩ / (BrE)
The official name of Sweden, a Scandinavian kingdom occupying the eastern part of the Scandinavian Peninsula in northern Europe.
the official name of Sweden, a Scandinavian kingdom

Examples

The constitution of the Kingdom of Sweden outlines the framework of its parliamentary democracy.
Official documents refer to the country as the Kingdom of Sweden.
The Kingdom of Sweden is known for its high standard of living and beautiful natural landscapes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sweden - A country in northern Europe, occupying most of the Scandinavian Peninsula; the common name for the Kingdom of Sweden.
