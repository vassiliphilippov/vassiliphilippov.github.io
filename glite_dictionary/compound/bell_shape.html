bell shape

1. NOUN

/ bɛl ʃeɪp / (AmE), / bɛl ʃeɪp / (BrE)
A shape that is rounded and wider at the bottom, tapering smoothly to a narrower top, resembling a bell.
shape resembling a bell

Examples

The flower has a distinct bell shape that makes it easy to identify.
The architect designed the roof with a gentle bell shape to give the building a unique profile.
Clouds sometimes form a bell shape when weather patterns shift rapidly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bell-shaped - having the form of a bell
