rego status

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛɡoʊ ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈriːɡəʊ ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
the current registration status of a vehicle, typically in Australia or New Zealand
vehicle registration status (Australasia)
informal

Examples

You can look up your rego status online to see if your car is registered.
The police officer asked to see proof of the vehicle's rego status.
Before renewing your insurance, check the rego status of your vehicle.

Synset Examples

She checked her rego status online yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registration status - the official record of whether something (especially a vehicle) is registered

Etymology

Shortened from 'registration' (rego) in Australian/New Zealand English, combined with 'status'.
