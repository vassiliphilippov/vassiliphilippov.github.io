Economic and Social Council commission

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌkɑnəmɪk ənd səˈsoʊʃəl ˈkaˌmaɪʃən / (AmE), / iˌkɒnəmɪk ənd səˈsəʊʃl ˈkəmɪʃən / (BrE)
a commission established by the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations to address specific economic and social issues
UN Economic and Social Council commission

Examples

The Economic and Social Council commission on human rights meets annually to review global progress.
A new Economic and Social Council commission was formed to study environmental challenges.
She was appointed as a member of an Economic and Social Council commission focused on gender equality.
