magical ability

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædʒɪkəl əˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪkəl əˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the power to perform magic or supernatural feats
the power to perform magic

Examples

The wizard's magical ability allowed him to transform stones into gold.
She discovered her magical ability at an early age, astonishing her friends and family.
Many stories feature characters who possess a hidden magical ability that shapes their destiny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magic - the power of apparently influencing events by mysterious or supernatural forces
  • Synonym: supernatural power - a force beyond scientific understanding or the laws of nature
