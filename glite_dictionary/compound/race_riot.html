race riot

1. NOUN

/ reɪs ˈraɪ.ət / (AmE), / reɪs ˈraɪ.ət / (BrE)
a violent public disturbance resulting from conflict between people of different racial groups
violent riot between racial groups

Examples

The city was scarred by a race riot that erupted in 1968.
Several neighborhoods suffered extensive damage following the race riot.
Historians have studied the causes and consequences of each race riot in detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riot - a violent public disturbance involving a group of people

2. NOUN

/ ˈreɪs ˌraɪət / (AmE), / ˈreɪs ˌraɪət / (BrE)
A violent public disturbance caused by racial tensions, often involving clashes between groups of different ethnic backgrounds.
violent disturbance caused by racial tensions

Examples

The city was thrown into chaos by the sudden race riot that erupted downtown.
Following the verdict, a massive race riot broke out in several neighborhoods.
Many historians have studied the causes and effects of each major race riot in the twentieth century.

Synset Examples

The city witnessed a race riot after the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: riot - a violent public disorder or disturbance
