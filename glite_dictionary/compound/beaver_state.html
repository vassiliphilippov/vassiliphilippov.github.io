Beaver State

1. PROPN

/ ˈbiːvər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈbiːvə steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the state of Oregon, located in the northwestern United States
nickname for Oregon, US state

Examples

While traveling through the Beaver State, we visited several beautiful national parks.
The Beaver State is known for its scenic coastline and lush forests.
Many people move to the Beaver State for its vibrant cultural scene and outdoor lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oregon - a state in the northwestern United States

Etymology

Refers to Oregon's historical association with beaver populations and the fur trade.
