mallee hen

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæli hɛn / (AmE), / ˈmali hɛn / (BrE)
an adult female mallee fowl, a large ground-dwelling bird native to Australia
adult female mallee fowl (bird)

Examples

The mallee hen is renowned for building large nesting mounds of leaf litter and sand.
During the breeding season, the mallee hen diligently tends to her eggs.
We spotted a mallee hen in the bush while hiking through the reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallee fowl - a large ground-dwelling bird native to Australia

Etymology

From Australian English "mallee" (a type of eucalyptus scrub) and "hen" (female bird).
