meclizine hydrochloride

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛkləˌzin ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛklɪziːn ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a drug used to prevent and treat nausea, vomiting, and dizziness caused by motion sickness or vertigo
drug for motion sickness and vertigo
medical

Examples

If you suffer from motion sickness, your doctor may prescribe meclizine hydrochloride before you travel.
She took meclizine hydrochloride to prevent dizziness during her boat trip.
Some people find meclizine hydrochloride effective in managing the symptoms of vertigo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antihistamine - a drug used to counteract the effects of histamine
