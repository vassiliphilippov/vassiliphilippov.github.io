every last penny

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛv.ri læst ˈpɛni / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ri lɑːst ˈpɛni / (BrE)
the whole amount of one's money or resources; every single bit of what someone has
all of one's money or resources

Examples

She spent every last penny she had on her new business venture.
After paying the bills, there wasn't every last penny left for entertainment.
He donated every last penny of his savings to the charity.

Synset Examples

She saved every last penny for her trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: every penny - all of one's money
  • Antonym: not a penny - no money at all
