horizontally challenged

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) Used as a euphemism for overweight or obese.
humorous euphemism for overweight
informal
