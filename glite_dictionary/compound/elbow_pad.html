elbow pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlboʊ pæd / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊ pæd / (BrE)
a protective pad worn over the elbow to prevent injury, especially while playing sports such as football or hockey
protective pad worn over the elbow

Examples

He put on his elbow pad before heading onto the hockey rink.
Wearing an elbow pad can help prevent bruises during sports.
The skater bought a new set of elbow pads for protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knee pad - a protective pad worn on the knee
