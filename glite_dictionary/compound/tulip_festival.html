tulip festival

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlɪp ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪp ˈfɛstɪvəl / (BrE)
an event held to celebrate and display tulip flowers, often featuring gardens, parades, and related festivities
event celebrating tulip flowers

Examples

Every spring, thousands of tourists flock to Ottawa for the famous tulip festival.
The tulip festival attracts garden enthusiasts from around the world.
She took hundreds of photos at the tulip festival last year.
