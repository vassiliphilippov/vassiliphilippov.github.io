homeless person

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmləs ˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmləs ˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person without a home or permanent place to live, especially someone living on the streets
someone without permanent housing

Examples

The city has opened new shelters to support the homeless person population during the winter months.
She volunteers every weekend to provide meals to at least one homeless person in her neighborhood.
We must do more to address the needs of every homeless person in our community.

Synset Examples

a homeless was found murdered in Central Park

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vagrant - a person without a settled home or regular work who wanders from place to place
  • Synonym: rough sleeper - a person who sleeps outside because they are homeless (mainly British)
