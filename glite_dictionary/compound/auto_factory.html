auto factory

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ ˌfæktəɹi / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌfæktəri / (BrE)
A factory where automobiles are manufactured.
a factory for making cars

Examples

The new auto factory opened last year, bringing hundreds of jobs to the region.
She works as an engineer at an auto factory just outside the city.
Many auto factories are now using robots to speed up production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile plant - a factory where automobiles are manufactured
