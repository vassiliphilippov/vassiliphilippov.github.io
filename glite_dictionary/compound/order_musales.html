order Musales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər mjuˈseɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə mjuːˈseɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of tropical flowering plants, including bananas and related species
order of tropical plants (bananas, etc.)

Examples

The order Musales includes important food crops like bananas and plantains.
Botanists classify these tropical species under the order Musales.
Studies of the order Musales reveal a diverse range of fruit-bearing plants.

Etymology

From Latin 'Musa' (the genus for banana) plus order suffix '-ales' indicating a plant order.
