clubmoss family

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌbˌmɑs ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈklʌbˌmɒs ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of spore-producing plants in the order Lycopodiales, commonly known as clubmosses.
family of spore-producing plants (clubmosses)

Examples

The clubmoss family includes several species found in temperate forests.
Scientists study the clubmoss family to understand plant evolution.
Members of the clubmoss family reproduce using spores rather than seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lycopodiaceae - the scientific name for the clubmoss family
