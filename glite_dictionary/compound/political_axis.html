political axis

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈæksɪs / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈæksɪs / (BrE)
An imaginary line or spectrum used to represent different political beliefs, attitudes, or positions, often from one extreme to another.
spectrum of political beliefs or positions

Examples

The new party positioned itself at a unique point on the political axis.
Debates often revolve around where policies fall along the political axis.
The left-right political axis does not capture all ideological differences.

Synset Examples

They debated the political axis of the new law.
