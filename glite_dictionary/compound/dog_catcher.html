dog catcher

1. NOUN

/ dɔɡ ˈkætʃɚ / (AmE), / dɒɡ ˈkætʃə / (BrE)
a person employed by a municipality to capture and impound stray dogs and other animals
municipal employee who catches stray dogs

Examples

The dog catcher collected several stray dogs from the park this morning.
Residents called the dog catcher when they noticed a pack of strays roaming the neighborhood.
As a dog catcher, he often rescued lost pets and returned them to their owners.
