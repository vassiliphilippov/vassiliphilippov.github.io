split-pea soup

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplɪt piː suːp / (AmE), / ˈsplɪt piː suːp / (BrE)
A thick soup made with stock and split peas, typically including onions, carrots, and celery.
soup with split peas and vegetables

Examples

My grandmother always makes split-pea soup on cold winter days.
The restaurant's split-pea soup is famous for its rich, hearty flavor.
I prepared a pot of split-pea soup with ham and fresh vegetables for dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pea soup - soup made from peas, especially with a thicker consistency
