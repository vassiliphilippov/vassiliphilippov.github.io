clasp knife

1. NOUN

/ klæsp naɪf / (AmE), / klɑːsp naɪf / (BrE)
a large folding knife with one or more blades that fold into the handle
large folding knife with blades

Examples

He took his clasp knife from his pocket to cut the rope.
The old sailor always carried a sturdy clasp knife for emergencies.
She used a clasp knife to whittle a small figure out of wood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocketknife - small knife with one or more folding blades, carried in a pocket
