pudendal cleavage

1. NOUN

/ pjuːˈdɛndl ˈkliːvɪdʒ / (AmE), / pjuːˈdɛndəl ˈkliːvɪdʒ / (BrE)
the anatomical fissure or groove between the labia majora in the female genitalia
fissure between the labia majora
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor inspected the pudendal cleavage for signs of irritation.
Certain types of clothing may reveal more of the pudendal cleavage than intended.
Medical textbooks detail the anatomy of the pudendal cleavage as part of the external genitalia.

Etymology

From Latin 'pudenda' (external genitals) and 'cleavage' (a split or division).
