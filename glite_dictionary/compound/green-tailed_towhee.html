green-tailed towhee

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡriːn ˈteɪld ˈtoʊ.i / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈteɪld ˈtəʊ.i / (BrE)
A species of towhee with greenish upperparts and a distinctive yellowish-green tail, found in the Rocky Mountains and western North America.
bird species of the Rocky Mountains

Examples

We spotted a green-tailed towhee perched on a low shrub near the trail.
The unmistakable call of the green-tailed towhee can often be heard in the mountain foothills.
Birdwatchers hope to see a green-tailed towhee during migration season in Colorado.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towhee - a type of North American sparrow-like bird
