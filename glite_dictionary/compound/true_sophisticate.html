true sophisticate

1. NOUN

/ tru səˈfɪstɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / tru səˈfɪstɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
A person who is genuinely knowledgeable, refined, and discerning in matters of taste, culture, or style.
genuinely refined and knowledgeable person

Examples

Among the guests, he stood out as a true sophisticate who appreciated fine art and wine.
It takes years of experience to become a true sophisticate in the world of gourmet cuisine.
She is a true sophisticate, able to discuss both classical music and modern art with ease.

Synset Examples

Her fashion sense makes her a true sophisticate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sophisticate - a person with much worldly experience and knowledge of fashion and culture
