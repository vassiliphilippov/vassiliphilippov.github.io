daughter of Eve

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔt̬ɚ əv ˈiːv / (AmE), / ˈdɔːtə əv ˈiːv / (BrE)
a woman; a female human being, especially in allusion to the biblical Eve as the first woman
a woman (biblical reference)

Examples

She grew up believing that, as a daughter of Eve, she could accomplish anything.
Every daughter of Eve must make her own choices in life.
The novel described the trials faced by each daughter of Eve in her family.

Synset Examples

She proudly identifies as a daughter of Eve.
Every daughter of Eve carries a unique story.
As a daughter of Eve, she embraced her femininity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woman - an adult female human
  • Antonym: man - an adult male human

Etymology

From Eve, the first woman in the biblical Book of Genesis; the phrase signifies ancestry from Eve.
