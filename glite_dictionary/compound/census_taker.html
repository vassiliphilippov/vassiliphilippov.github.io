census taker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛnsəs ˌteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsəs ˌteɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect census data by visiting individual households and recording information
person who collects census data

Examples

The census taker visited every house in the neighborhood to gather information about the residents.
After completing her training, she worked as a census taker during the national population survey.
We were asked detailed questions by a census taker who came to our door last week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enumerator - a person employed to take a census
