pea flour

1. NOUN

/ pi ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / pi ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a type of flour made by grinding dried peas, used in cooking and baking
flour made from dried peas

Examples

Gluten-free bread can be made with pea flour as a nutritious alternative.
He bought a bag of pea flour to try new plant-based recipes at home.
Adding pea flour to soups helps to thicken them and increase protein content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chickpea flour - flour made from ground chickpeas, also known as gram flour
