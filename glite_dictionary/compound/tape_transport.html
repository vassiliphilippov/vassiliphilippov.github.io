tape transport

1. NOUN

/ teɪp ˈtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / teɪp ˈtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
the mechanism in a tape recorder or player that moves the magnetic tape across the read/write heads
mechanism moving tape across heads

Examples

The tape transport in this cassette player is making a strange noise.
A faulty tape transport can cause uneven playback and damage the tape.
He repaired the tape transport mechanism to restore the recorder's functionality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tape deck - audio equipment for playing magnetic tape
