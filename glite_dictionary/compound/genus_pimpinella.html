genus Pimpinella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs pɪmˌpɪˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs pɪmˌpɪˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the parsley family (Apiaceae), which includes anise and other aromatic herbs.
genus of plants including anise

Examples

The species classified under genus Pimpinella are known for their aromatic properties.
Botanists have long studied the genus Pimpinella for its medicinal uses.
Anise, a member of the genus Pimpinella, is valued both as a spice and for its health benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anise - an aromatic plant of the same genus

Etymology

From Latin 'Pimpinella', the botanical name for this genus.
