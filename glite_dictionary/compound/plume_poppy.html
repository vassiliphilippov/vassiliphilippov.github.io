plume poppy

1. NOUN

/ plum ˈpɑːpi / (AmE), / pluːm ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant native to China and Japan, widely grown for its large, feathery clusters of creamy white flowers.
Asian perennial with feathery white flowers

Examples

The plume poppy added dramatic height to the back of the garden border.
They planted a plume poppy near the fence to showcase its spectacular flowers.
Although beautiful, the plume poppy can be invasive if not carefully managed.
