Trumplican faction

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmplɪkən ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmplɪkən ˈfækʃən / (BrE)
A group within a political party or movement that strongly supports Donald Trump and his policies.
pro-Trump political group
informal american

Examples

The Trumplican faction is pushing for more aggressive policies in Congress.
Some members of the party distanced themselves from the Trumplican faction during the election campaign.
The rise of the Trumplican faction has changed the party's priorities in recent years.

Synset Examples

The Trumplican faction is pushing for more aggressive policies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Republican faction - A political subgroup within the Republican Party

Etymology

Blend of 'Trump' (surname of Donald Trump, former U.S. president) and 'Republican', plus 'faction'.
