spurge family

1. NOUN

/ spɝdʒ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / spɜːdʒ ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, scientifically known as Euphorbiaceae, including spurges and related species.
family of flowering plants (Euphorbiaceae)

Examples

The spurge family includes many plants that produce a milky sap.
Some species in the spurge family are used for medicinal purposes.
Poinsettias belong to the spurge family and are popular holiday plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Euphorbiaceae - scientific name of the spurge family of plants
