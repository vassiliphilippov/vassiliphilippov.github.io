overseas telegram

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚˈsiz ˈtɛləˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsiːz ˈtɛlɪɡræm / (BrE)
a telegram sent to a recipient in a foreign country
telegram sent abroad

Examples

He received an overseas telegram from his sister in Australia.
During the war, families often awaited news through an overseas telegram.
The company relied on overseas telegrams to communicate with their international branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telegram - a message sent by telegraph and then delivered in written or printed form
