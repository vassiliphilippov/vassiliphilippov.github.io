naval equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvəl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈneɪvəl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the machines, tools, and devices specifically used by a navy for its operations at sea
machines and devices used by a navy

Examples

The new aircraft carrier was fitted with state-of-the-art naval equipment before its maiden voyage.
Advancements in naval equipment have greatly improved the country's maritime defense capabilities.
The museum displayed several pieces of historic naval equipment from World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military equipment - equipment used by armed forces in general
