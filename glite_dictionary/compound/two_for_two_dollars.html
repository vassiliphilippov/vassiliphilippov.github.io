two for two dollars

1. NOUN

/ tuː fər tuː ˈdɑlɚz / (AmE), / tuː fə tuː ˈdɒləz / (BrE)
a promotional offer where two items are sold together for the total price of two dollars
promotional deal; two items for two dollars

Examples

The store is having a two for two dollars sale on energy drinks this week.
I picked up two packs of gum for two for two dollars at the checkout counter.
Many convenience stores offer snacks two for two dollars as a limited-time promotion.

Synset Examples

I bought two sandwiches, two for two dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: two for one - a promotional offer where two items are sold for the price of one
