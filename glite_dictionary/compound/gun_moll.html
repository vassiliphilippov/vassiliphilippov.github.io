gun moll

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn mɑl / (AmE), / ɡʌn mɒl / (BrE)
A woman who is the companion or girlfriend of a gangster.
gangster's female companion

Examples

The famous gangster was always seen with his gun moll by his side.
In old crime movies, the gun moll often had a dramatic role alongside the mob boss.
She was mistaken for a mere gun moll, but she was actually involved in the gang's operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moll - a gangster's girlfriend or a woman associate in crime

Etymology

From American slang 'gun' (firearm) + archaic 'moll' (woman, girlfriend), from Molly (pet form of Mary).
