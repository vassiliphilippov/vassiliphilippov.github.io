open-armed hospitality

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpən ˈɑrmd hɑːˌspɪˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈɑːmd hɒspɪˈtalɪti / (BrE)
a reception or welcome characterized by warmth, friendliness, and eagerness to receive guests or newcomers
warm and friendly welcome

Examples

The local villagers showed open-armed hospitality to the volunteers helping after the flood.
She was touched by the open-armed hospitality offered by her in-laws during her first visit.
Tourists often praise the region for its open-armed hospitality and generous spirit.

Synset Examples

The community received the newcomers with open-armed hospitality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospitality - friendly and generous reception of guests
  • Antonym: cold welcome - an unwelcoming or unfriendly reception
