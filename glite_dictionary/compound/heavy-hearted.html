heavy-hearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛviˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Feeling sad, sorrowful, or weighed down by unhappiness.
feeling sad or sorrowful

Examples

She felt heavy-hearted after saying goodbye to her childhood home.
His heavy-hearted apology showed how much the mistake meant to him.
The rainy weather made everyone seem a little more heavy-hearted than usual.

Synset Examples

He wrote a heavy-hearted letter to his friend.
The heavy-hearted song resonated with many listeners.
After the news, she felt heavy-hearted all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downcast - feeling sad or discouraged
  • Synonym: dejected - feeling sad and lacking hope or enthusiasm
  • Antonym: light-hearted - cheerful and carefree
