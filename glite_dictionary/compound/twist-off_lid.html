twist-off lid

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪst ˌɔf lɪd / (AmE), / ˈtwɪst ˌɒf lɪd / (BrE)
A lid or cap designed to be removed from a container by turning it, commonly found on jars or bottles.
removable lid for jars or bottles

Examples

She struggled to open the twist-off lid on the jar of pickles.
Most glass bottles today come with a twist-off lid for convenience.
To preserve the contents, make sure the twist-off lid is closed tightly after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screw cap - a cap that is screwed onto the neck of a bottle or jar
