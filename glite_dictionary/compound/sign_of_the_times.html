sign of the times

1. NOUN

/ saɪn əv ðə taɪmz / (AmE), / saɪn əv ðə taɪmz / (BrE)
an event, development, or feature that indicates the prevailing attitudes or conditions of a particular period
indicator of current societal trends

Examples

The popularity of streaming services is a clear sign of the times.
Empty offices in city centers are a sign of the times after the shift to remote work.
Public concern about climate change is another sign of the times.

Synset Examples

Fast fashion's popularity is a troubling sign of the times.
Social media's influence is a clear sign of the times.
The rise of remote work is a sign of the times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: symptom - an indicator or sign of something, often of an undesirable situation
