rifle butt

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪfəl bʌt / (AmE), / ˈraɪfl bʌt / (BrE)
the thick end of a rifle, which is held against the shoulder when firing
the thick end of a rifle held against the shoulder

Examples

He placed the rifle butt firmly against his shoulder before taking aim.
The old rifle butt showed signs of wear after years of use.
She was injured when the rifle butt accidentally struck her arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butt - the thick end of something, especially a tool or weapon
