open air

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊpən ˈɛr / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈeə / (BrE)
the outside environment; a place or space that is not enclosed or covered, especially the outdoors
the outdoors; outside, not enclosed

Examples

We ate our lunch in the open air and enjoyed the sunshine.
Many festivals are held in the open air during the summer months.
She felt revitalized after spending a weekend in the open air.

Synset Examples

he wanted to get outdoors a little
the concert was held in the open air
camping in the open

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdoors - the open air; outside, not in a building
  • Antonym: indoors - inside a building; not in the open air
