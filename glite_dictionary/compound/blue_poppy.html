blue poppy

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / blu ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
a perennial flowering plant native to China, noted for its drooping clusters of mauve-pink to bright blue flowers
Chinese perennial with blue flowers

Examples

Tourists visit the Himalayas each year to catch a glimpse of the rare blue poppy.
The blue poppy thrives in cool, mountainous regions with moist soil.
She was delighted when the blue poppy in her garden finally bloomed.
