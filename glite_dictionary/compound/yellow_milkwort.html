yellow milkwort

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈmɪlkˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈmɪlkwɜːt / (BrE)
a bog plant of the pine barrens in the southeastern United States, noted for its spikes of irregular yellow-orange flowers
bog plant with yellow-orange flowers

Examples

The yellow milkwort thrives in the acidic soils of the southeastern pine barrens.
We spotted a cluster of yellow milkwort while hiking through the swampy meadow.
Botanists are keen to protect habitats where the yellow milkwort naturally occurs.
