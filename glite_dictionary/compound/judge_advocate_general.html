judge advocate general

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒʌdʒ ˌædvəkeɪt dʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒʌdʒ ˌædvəkət ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
the highest-ranking legal officer who serves as the senior legal advisor to a branch of the armed forces
senior legal officer in the military
legal

Examples

The judge advocate general oversees all legal matters for the military branch.
She was appointed as the judge advocate general after years of distinguished service.
The judge advocate general provided guidance on military justice procedures.
