breathing apparatus

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriðɪŋ əˈpærəˌtəs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ ˌapəˈreɪtəs / (BrE)
a device designed to assist or enable a person to breathe, especially in situations where normal breathing is impaired or impossible
device for assisting breathing

Examples

All firefighters were equipped with breathing apparatus before entering the burning building.
The patient required a breathing apparatus following respiratory failure in the emergency room.
Divers rely on a specialized breathing apparatus to stay underwater for extended periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respirator - a device worn over the mouth and nose to assist breathing
  • Synonym: oxygen mask - a mask that supplies oxygen to assist breathing
