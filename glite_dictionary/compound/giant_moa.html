giant moa

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈmoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈməʊ.ə / (BrE)
an extinct, flightless bird from New Zealand, known as the largest species of moa, reaching up to 12 feet in height
largest extinct flightless bird of New Zealand

Examples

Fossils of the giant moa have been found throughout the South Island of New Zealand.
The giant moa stood taller than any other known bird species.
Early Māori settlers likely encountered the giant moa before its extinction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moa - any of several extinct flightless birds from New Zealand

Etymology

From Māori 'moa' (name for the bird).
