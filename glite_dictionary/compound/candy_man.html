candy man

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændi mæn / (AmE), / ˈkændi mæn / (BrE)
a man who sells candy, especially to children, often used affectionately
man who sells candy

Examples

After school, the children would gather around the candy man for a treat.
The fair's candy man wore a bright red hat and greeted everyone cheerfully.
Every Saturday, the local candy man set up his cart near the playground.

Synset Examples

Kids rushed to the candy man after school.
The candy man always had a smile and treats.
The candy man at the fair had all my favorites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet seller - a person who sells sweets; chiefly British equivalent
