combination salad

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmbəˈneɪʃən ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A salad made with greens and vegetables, to which meat, chicken, or cheese is added.
salad with greens plus meat, chicken, or cheese

Examples

She ordered a combination salad with grilled chicken for lunch.
The restaurant's combination salad includes ham, cheese, and a variety of fresh vegetables.
Would you like your combination salad with turkey or roast beef?
