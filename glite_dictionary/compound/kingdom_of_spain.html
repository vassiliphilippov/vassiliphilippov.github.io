Kingdom of Spain

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm əv speɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv speɪn / (BrE)
The official name of Spain, a parliamentary monarchy in southwestern Europe occupying most of the Iberian Peninsula, noted for its historical colonial empire.
official name of the country Spain

Examples

The Kingdom of Spain is a member of the European Union and has a constitutional monarch.
During the Age of Exploration, the Kingdom of Spain established colonies across the Americas and Asia.
The official language of the Kingdom of Spain is Spanish, but several regional languages are also spoken.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Spain - A country in southwestern Europe, commonly referred to by its short name
