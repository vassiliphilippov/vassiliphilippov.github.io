wild Shitmas party

1. NOUN

/ waɪld ˈʃɪtməs ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / waɪld ˈʃɪtməs ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A raucous and excessive Christmas celebration, often marked by rowdy or unconventional behavior.
outrageous or wild Christmas party
informal slang vulgar

Examples

Every year, their wild Shitmas party is the talk of the neighborhood.
We ended up at a wild Shitmas party that lasted until dawn.
He wore the ugliest sweater he owned to the wild Shitmas party.

Synset Examples

They always throw a wild Shitmas party every year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas party - a party held to celebrate Christmas

Etymology

Formed from 'wild' and the slang variant 'Shitmas' (a vulgar blend of 'shit' and 'Christmas' used to mock or deride the holiday).
