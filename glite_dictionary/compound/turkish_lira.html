Turkish lira

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜr.kɪʃ ˈliː.rə / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.kɪʃ ˈlɪə.rə / (BrE)
the official basic unit of currency in Turkey
currency unit of Turkey

Examples

The value of the Turkish lira has fluctuated significantly against the dollar in recent years.
Many tourists exchange their euros for Turkish lira upon arriving in Istanbul.
She paid for the souvenirs using Turkish lira instead of her credit card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lira - a former or existing currency unit in several countries

Etymology

From Turkish 'lira', from Italian 'lira', itself from Latin 'libra' (pound, balance).
