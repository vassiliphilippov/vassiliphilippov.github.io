knife blade

1. NOUN

/ naɪf bleɪd / (AmE), / naɪf bleɪd / (BrE)
the flat, sharp-edged part of a knife used for cutting
the cutting part of a knife

Examples

Be careful when cleaning the knife blade so you don't cut yourself.
The chef prefers a knife blade made of high-carbon steel for durability.
She replaced the old knife blade with a sharper one before chopping vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blade - the flat cutting part of a tool or weapon
