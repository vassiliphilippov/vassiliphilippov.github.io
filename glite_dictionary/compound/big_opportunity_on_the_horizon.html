big opportunity on the horizon

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ɑːpərˈtunəti ɑn ðə həˈraɪzn̩ / (AmE), / bɪɡ ɒpəˈtjuːnəti ɒn ðə həˈraɪzn̩ / (BrE)
a significant chance for success or advancement that is likely to arrive soon
a major upcoming chance for success

Examples

She believes there’s a big opportunity on the horizon for her career.
The company is preparing for a big opportunity on the horizon in the tech industry.
With the new partnership, a big opportunity on the horizon could change everything.

Synset Examples

There's a big opportunity on the horizon for us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opportunity - a set of circumstances that makes it possible to do something
