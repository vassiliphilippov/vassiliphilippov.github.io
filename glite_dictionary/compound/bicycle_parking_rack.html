bicycle parking rack

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkl ˈpɑrkɪŋ ræk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl ˈpɑːkɪŋ ræk / (BrE)
a metal framework or structure designed for securely parking and locking bicycles in public or private spaces
structure for parking and locking bicycles

Examples

The city installed a new bicycle parking rack outside the library.
Students often lock their bikes to the bicycle parking rack near the school entrance.
Please make sure to secure your bike at the bicycle parking rack before coming into the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bike rack - a rack for holding bicycles; often used as a synonym
