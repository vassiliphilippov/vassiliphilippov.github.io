homemade nog

1. NOUN

/ hoʊmˌmeɪd nɑɡ / (AmE), / həʊmˌmeɪd nɒɡ / (BrE)
Eggnog that is made at home, typically enjoyed as a festive or holiday drink.
egg-based festive drink made at home

Examples

She brought a pitcher of homemade nog to the holiday party, and everyone loved it.
On Christmas Eve, we always enjoy a glass of homemade nog by the fireplace.
His secret recipe for homemade nog includes a dash of nutmeg and a splash of bourbon.

Synset Examples

Nothing beats homemade nog on a cold winter night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eggnog - a rich, sweet, creamy holiday drink made with eggs, milk, sugar, and often alcohol
