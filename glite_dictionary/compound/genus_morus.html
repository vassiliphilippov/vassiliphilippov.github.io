genus Morus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmɔːrəs / (BrE)
the genus of plants in the family Moraceae that includes the mulberries
genus including mulberries

Examples

The genus Morus contains several species commonly known as mulberries.
Botanists classify new mulberry species under the genus Morus.
Fossil records indicate that the genus Morus originated in Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mulberry - a deciduous tree of the genus Morus, known for its edible fruit

Etymology

From Latin 'morus' (mulberry), used in botanical Latin classification.
