top piece

1. NOUN

/ tɑp pis / (AmE), / tɒp piːs / (BrE)
the most outstanding or valuable item in a collection or group
best or most valuable item

Examples

The top piece in his stamp collection is a rare 19th-century issue.
Everyone admired the top piece of the jewelry display, a diamond necklace.
At the auction, bidders competed fiercely for the top piece among the antiques.

Synset Examples

Her top piece in the collection is a rare sculpture.
This painting is considered the top piece of the exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centerpiece - the most important or decorative item, especially in a display
  • Synonym: highlight - the best or most interesting part of something
