silver grey

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
a light shade of grey with a silvery, metallic appearance
a light silvery-grey color

Examples

The car was painted a stunning silver grey that shimmered in the sunlight.
Her hair gradually turned silver grey as she got older, giving her a distinguished look.
They chose silver grey curtains to match the modern décor of the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silver - a precious shiny grey-white metal
  • Synonym: grey - a color between black and white
