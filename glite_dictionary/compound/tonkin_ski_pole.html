Tonkin ski pole

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑn.kɪn skaɪ poʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɒŋ.kɪn skaɪ pəʊl / (BrE)
A ski pole made from Tonkin Cane, a strong species of bamboo used for its durability and flexibility.
ski pole made from Tonkin Cane bamboo

Examples

In the early days of alpine skiing, many competitors preferred a Tonkin ski pole for its flexibility and strength.
The collector proudly displayed a vintage Tonkin ski pole alongside other classic skiing gear.
Some traditionalists still choose a Tonkin ski pole over modern metal or composite designs for its unique feel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ski pole - a pole held in one hand and used to aid balance when skiing

Etymology

Named after Tonkin Cane, a variety of bamboo native to the Tonkin region in northern Vietnam.
