sign of the zodiac

1. NOUN

/ saɪn əv ðə ˈzoʊdiˌæk / (AmE), / saɪn ɒv ðə ˈzəʊdiˌæk / (BrE)
one of the twelve equal divisions of the zodiac, each named after a constellation, used in astrology to represent personality traits and forecast events.
one of twelve astrological divisions of the zodiac

Examples

She was born in July, so her sign of the zodiac is Cancer.
Many people believe each sign of the zodiac influences personality traits.
The horoscope section lists daily advice for every sign of the zodiac.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zodiac - an imaginary belt in the sky divided into twelve parts, used in astrology

Etymology

From the astrological use of 'sign' and 'zodiac,' the latter from Greek zōidiakos, meaning 'circle of animals.'
