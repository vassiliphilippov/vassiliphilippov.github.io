fraternization at work

1. NOUN

/ fræˌtɜrnəˌzeɪʃən æt wɜrk / (AmE), / frəˌtɜːnɪˈzeɪʃən æt wɜːk / (BrE)
the act of engaging in social or friendly relations with colleagues in the workplace, which may be discouraged or cause complications
socializing with colleagues in a workplace context

Examples

Many companies have strict policies about fraternization at work to prevent favoritism and conflicts.
Rumors about fraternization at work spread quickly through the office.
Some employees believe that fraternization at work can improve team morale, while others see it as a risk.

Synset Examples

Fraternization at work can lead to conflicts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workplace romance - a romantic relationship between colleagues at work
  • Antonym: nepotism - favoritism granted to relatives or friends in the workplace
