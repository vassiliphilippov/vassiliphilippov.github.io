lesser anteater

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈæntiˌitər / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈæntiːtə / (BrE)
A small anteater with a prehensile tail and four-clawed forelimbs, native to tropical South and Central America.
small anteater native to South and Central America

Examples

The lesser anteater is known for its agile climbing abilities in the rainforests.
We spotted a lesser anteater searching for ants among the branches during our trip to Costa Rica.
Researchers observed the lesser anteater using its long tongue to extract insects from tree bark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamandua - a small anteater of South and Central America, also known as the lesser anteater
