blood pudding

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈpʊd.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a type of dark sausage typically made from pig's blood, fat, and oatmeal or other grain, often served cooked or fried
sausage made from pig's blood

Examples

In England, blood pudding is a traditional part of a full breakfast.
She refused to try blood pudding because of its unusual ingredients.
The chef prepared homemade blood pudding using a classic family recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black pudding - British term for a similar dark sausage made from blood
