water-shield family

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌʃild ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌʃiːld ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of aquatic flowering plants, sometimes considered separate from true water lilies, that includes the genera Cabomba and Brasenia.
a family of aquatic flowering plants (Cabombaceae)

Examples

The water-shield family consists of aquatic plants commonly found in still or slow-moving waters.
Brasenia, a genus within the water-shield family, is known for its floating leaves.
Botanists sometimes debate whether the water-shield family should be grouped with true water lilies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lily family - the plant family Nymphaeaceae, closely related to water-shield family
