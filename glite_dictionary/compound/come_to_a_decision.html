to come to a decision

1. VERB

/ kʌm tu ə dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / kʌm tʊ ə dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
to reach a conclusion after considering the options
reach a conclusion

Examples

After weeks of discussion, the team finally came to a decision about the project.
It's important to gather all the facts before you come to a decision.
She was relieved once she had come to a decision about her future.

Synset Examples

After much thought, she came to a decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decide - to make a choice or come to a conclusion
  • Synonym: to arrive at a decision - reach a decision after consideration
