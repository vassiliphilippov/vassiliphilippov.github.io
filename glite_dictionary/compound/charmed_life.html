charmed life

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrmd laɪf / (AmE), / tʃɑːmd laɪf / (BrE)
a life that seems to be unusually lucky or protected from harm or misfortune
life marked by great luck or ease

Examples

Many believed she led a charmed life after surviving so many close calls.
From an early age, he seemed destined for a charmed life.
The novel tells the story of a boy who unexpectedly finds himself living a charmed life.

Synset Examples

Despite hardships, she had a charmed life overall.
He lived a charmed life filled with adventures.
Winning the lottery gave him a charmed life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunate - receiving good from uncertain or accidental events
  • Antonym: unlucky - having bad luck
