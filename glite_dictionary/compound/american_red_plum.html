American red plum

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən ɹɛd plʌm / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən rɛd plʌm / (BrE)
A species of wild plum tree native to eastern and central North America, known for its red-orange fruit with yellow flesh.
tree with red-orange fruit, native to North America

Examples

The American red plum is prized for its tart, flavorful fruit.
We found a grove of American red plum trees along the riverbank.
Jam made from American red plum is a delicacy in some rural communities.
