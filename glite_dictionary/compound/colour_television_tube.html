colour television tube

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tuːb / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən tjuːb / (BrE)
a cathode ray tube in a television set that displays images in full colour, as opposed to black and white
a TV tube for displaying full-colour images
british

Examples

The old living room set still has a colour television tube that works perfectly.
Early models with a colour television tube were considered a luxury.
He replaced the faulty colour television tube instead of buying a new TV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cathode ray tube - an electronic display device used in televisions and monitors
  • Antonym: black-and-white television tube - a television tube that displays images only in black, white, and shades of grey
