sewage system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuɪdʒ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪdʒ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a network of pipes and facilities for collecting and carrying away waste water and sewage from buildings and streets
network for transporting sewage

Examples

The city invested millions to upgrade its aging sewage system and prevent pollution.
Heavy rainfall overwhelmed the old sewage system, causing flooding in several neighborhoods.
A well-designed sewage system is essential for public health and sanitary conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewerage - system or process of removing sewage
