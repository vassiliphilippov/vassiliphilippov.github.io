American Samoa

1. PROPN

/ əˌmɛrɪkən səˈmoʊə / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪkən səˈməʊə / (BrE)
a United States territory consisting of islands in the South Pacific Ocean, east of the independent state of Samoa
U.S. territory in the South Pacific
american

Examples

The people of American Samoa have a unique cultural identity and U.S. national status.
Tourists are drawn to the natural beauty of American Samoa's islands and coral reefs.
In 2021, American Samoa participated in the Summer Olympic Games.

Etymology

Named after the U.S.A. and the indigenous region of Samoa.
