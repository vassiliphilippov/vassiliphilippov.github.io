disco biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪskoʊ ˈbɪskət / (AmE), / ˈdɪskəʊ ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A slang term for the drug MDMA (ecstasy), especially used in the context of club or rave culture.
