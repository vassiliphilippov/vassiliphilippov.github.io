prestigious university

1. NOUN

/ prəˈstɪdʒəs ˌjuːnɪˈvɝːsəti / (AmE), / prɛˈstɪdʒəs ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)

well-respected university

Examples

Many employers value degrees from a prestigious university when hiring new staff.
