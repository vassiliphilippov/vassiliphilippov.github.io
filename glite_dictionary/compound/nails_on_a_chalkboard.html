nails on a chalkboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪlz ɑn ə ˈtʃɔkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈneɪlz ɒn ə ˈtʃɔːkbɔːd / (BrE)
a sound that is extremely unpleasant, often used to describe something that provokes a strong feeling of discomfort or irritation, especially a high-pitched screeching noise.
extremely unpleasant, piercing sound

Examples

The screech of the brakes was like nails on a chalkboard to her sensitive ears.
For some people, the way he whistles is worse than nails on a chalkboard.
The teacher's old microphone produced a feedback noise that sounded just like nails on a chalkboard.

Synset Examples

He can't stand the sound of nails on a chalkboard.
The sound of nails on a chalkboard makes my teeth itch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grating - harsh and unpleasant in sound
  • Antonym: soothing - having a calming or pleasing sound
