red-necked grebe

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdˈnɛkt ˈɡrib / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈnɛkt ˈɡriːb / (BrE)
a large, stocky waterbird found in circumpolar regions, characterized by a distinctive dark or reddish neck
large waterbird with a reddish neck

Examples

We spotted a red-necked grebe swimming quietly near the shore.
During migration season, several red-necked grebes can be seen on the lake.
The distinctive call of the red-necked grebe echoed across the wetlands.
