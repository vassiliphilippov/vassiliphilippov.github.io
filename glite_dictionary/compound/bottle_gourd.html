bottle gourd

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːt̬l ˌɡɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˌɡɔːd / (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant, Lagenaria siceraria, grown for its hard-shelled, bottle-shaped fruit, which is used for food or as containers when dried.
climbing plant with bottle-shaped gourds

Examples

The bottle gourd is often used as a vegetable in Asian cuisines.
After drying, a bottle gourd can be turned into a useful water container.
Gardeners sometimes grow bottle gourd for both its edible fruit and ornamental value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calabash - another name for the bottle gourd or its dried shell; synonym for bottle gourd
