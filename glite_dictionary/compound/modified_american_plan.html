modified American plan

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːdɪˌfaɪd əˈmɛrɪkən plæn / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪˌfaɪd əˈmɛrɪkən plan / (BrE)
a hotel arrangement in which the price includes breakfast and dinner, but not lunch.
hotel plan with breakfast and dinner included

Examples

Many resorts offer a modified American plan to give guests more flexibility with lunch options.
The modified American plan usually appeals to travelers who plan to be out during the day.
With the modified American plan, breakfast and dinner are included in your stay, while lunch is not.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: American plan - hotel price plan including all meals
  • Degree Lower: European plan - hotel price plan with no meals included
