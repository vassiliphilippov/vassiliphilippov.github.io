prairie crab

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛəri kræb / (AmE), / ˈpreəri kræb / (BrE)
A wild crab apple tree native to the western United States, known for its fragrant pink flowers.
wild crab apple tree with pink flowers

Examples

The prairie crab adds a splash of pink to the grasslands each spring.
Botanists study the prairie crab for its resilience in harsh climates.
We planted a prairie crab in our garden for its beautiful flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - tree producing small, sour apples, genus Malus
