Resurrection of Christ

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən əv kraɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən əv krʌɪst / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the event in which Jesus Christ rose from the dead on the third day after his crucifixion.
Christian event: Christ's rising from the dead

Examples

Belief in the Resurrection of Christ is central to Christian faith and theology.
Every year, Christians around the world celebrate the Resurrection of Christ at Easter.
The story of the Resurrection of Christ offers hope and renewal to many believers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Easter - Christian festival celebrating the Resurrection of Christ
  • Antonym: crucifixion - execution of Jesus Christ by crucifixion
