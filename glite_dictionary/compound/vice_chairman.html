vice chairman

1. NOUN

/ vaɪs ˈtʃɛrmæn / (AmE), / vaɪs ˈtʃɛəmən / (BrE)
A man who holds a position directly below the chairman and may act as the chairman's deputy or substitute.
male deputy to the chairman

Examples

He served as vice chairman of the board for five years before becoming chairman.
The vice chairman chaired the meeting in the chairman’s absence.
During the press conference, the vice chairman addressed questions about the company’s direction.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: chairman - person presiding over a meeting or organization
  • Synonym: vice chairwoman - female deputy to the chairman or chairwoman
  • Synonym: deputy chairman - alternate term for vice chairman (gender-neutral)

Etymology

From Latin 'vice' (in place of) and 'chairman' (presiding officer).
