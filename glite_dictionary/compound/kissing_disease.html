kissing disease

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪsɪŋ dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
An informal term for infectious mononucleosis, a disease marked by fever, swollen lymph nodes, and increased mononuclear cells, often transmitted through saliva.
informal name for infectious mononucleosis
informal medical

Examples

The doctor said her symptoms were typical of the kissing disease.
Because the kissing disease spreads through saliva, teenagers are often affected.
He missed two weeks of school after being diagnosed with the kissing disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mononucleosis - an infectious disease marked by an increase of mononuclear leukocytes

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪsɪŋ dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
An informal term for mononucleosis, a viral infection often spread through saliva, notably by kissing.
colloquial term for mononucleosis
informal

Examples

Her doctor explained that the kissing disease is simply another name for mononucleosis.
Despite its nickname, the kissing disease can also be spread by sharing drinks.
