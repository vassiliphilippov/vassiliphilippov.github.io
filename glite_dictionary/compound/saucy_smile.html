saucy smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsi smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsi smaɪl / (BrE)
a playful or cheeky smile, often suggesting confidence or flirtatiousness
a playful, cheeky smile

Examples

She gave him a saucy smile to show she was only teasing.
His saucy smile made everyone in the room laugh.
With a saucy smile, she accepted the invitation to dance.

Synset Examples

She gave him a saucy smile at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheeky - impudently bold or playful
  • Degree Higher: insolent - showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect
