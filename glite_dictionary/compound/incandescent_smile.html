incandescent smile

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.kænˈdɛs.ənt smaɪl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kænˈdɛs.ənt smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile that is notably bright, glowing, or radiant in appearance or effect.
a glowing, radiant smile

Examples

Her incandescent smile lit up the entire room, making everyone feel welcome.
Even in difficult times, his incandescent smile gave hope to those around him.
The bride's incandescent smile was captured in every photograph from the ceremony.

Synset Examples

Her incandescent smile brightened everyone's day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant smile - a bright and joyful smile

Etymology

From 'incandescent', from Latin 'incandescere' meaning 'to glow' and 'smile'.
