ex-boyfriend

1. NOUN

/ ɛks ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ɛks ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a man who was formerly a woman's boyfriend
a former male romantic partner

Examples

She ran into her ex-boyfriend at the coffee shop yesterday.
After the breakup, her ex-boyfriend moved to another city.
My ex-boyfriend and I are still good friends despite our past.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boyfriend - a man who is a woman's romantic partner
  • Synonym: ex-girlfriend - a woman who is no longer a man's girlfriend

Etymology

Formed from 'ex-' (former) and 'boyfriend'. 'Ex-' from Latin 'ex-' (out of, from).
