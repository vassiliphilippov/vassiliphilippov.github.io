to search for answers

1. VERB

/ sɜrtʃ fər ˈænsərz / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ fə ˈɑːnsəz / (BrE)
to look for solutions or information in order to solve a problem or satisfy curiosity
look for solutions or information

Examples

After hours of trying, the scientists continued to search for answers to the mysterious phenomenon.
Many people search for answers online when they encounter a difficult problem.
The documentary follows families who search for answers about their origins.

Synset Examples

They decided to search for answers somewhere else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seek answers - attempt to find solutions or information
  • Synonym: look for answers - try to discover solutions or information
