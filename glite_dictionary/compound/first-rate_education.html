first-rate education

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɝst ˈreɪt ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃn̩ / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈreɪt ˌɛdjuːˈkeɪʃn̩ / (BrE)
an education of the highest quality; excellent academic training
high-quality or excellent education

Examples

Many parents strive to provide their children with a first-rate education at any cost.
A first-rate education can open doors to better career opportunities.
The scholarship allowed her to pursue a first-rate education abroad.

Synset Examples

She received a first-rate education at that prestigious university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top-notch - of the highest quality
  • Antonym: mediocre education - education of only average quality
