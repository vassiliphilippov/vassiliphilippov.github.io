skinny legend

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪni ˈlɛdʒənd / (AmE), / ˈskɪni ˈlɛdʒənd / (BrE)
A person who is highly admired or idolized, often for their style, persona, or notably slim figure, especially in online culture.
admired, stylish or slim person (slang, online)
slang

Examples

After her iconic performance, fans all over Twitter started calling her a skinny legend.
Despite the joke, he was dubbed a skinny legend by his friends for his confidence and sense of style.
The term skinny legend has become a popular compliment on social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok.

Synset Examples

He called her a skinny legend for her achievements.
