refuse heap

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛfjuːs hiːp / (AmE), / ˈrɛfjuːs hiːp / (BrE)
a pile or accumulation of refuse and discarded material
pile of discarded waste

Examples

The old refuse heap behind the factory grew larger every week.
Children were warned not to play near the refuse heap at the edge of the village.
The city organized a cleanup to remove the refuse heap from the vacant lot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish heap - a pile of household or other waste
  • Synonym: dump - a place where waste is disposed of
