American wormseed

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɜrmˌsid / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈwɜːmsiːd / (BrE)
a rank-smelling tropical American plant of the goosefoot family, commonly known as a pigweed
rank-smelling tropical American plant

Examples

The herbalist recommended using American wormseed for its traditional medicinal properties.
We found patches of American wormseed growing wild near the riverbank.
The strong odor of American wormseed made it easy to identify among other weeds.
