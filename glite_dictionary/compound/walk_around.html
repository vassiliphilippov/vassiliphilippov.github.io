walk around

1. NOUN

/ wɑk əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / wɔːk əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
a leisurely or casual stroll through an area, usually without a specific destination
casual stroll through an area

Examples

We suggested taking a walk around the city center before dinner.
After lunch, he enjoyed a quiet walk around the park to clear his mind.
Their walk around the museum lasted over an hour as they took in the exhibits.

Synset Examples

They met each other in a walk around the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroll - a slow, relaxed walk, especially for pleasure
