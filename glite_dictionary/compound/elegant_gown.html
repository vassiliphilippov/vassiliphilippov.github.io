elegant gown

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛlɪɡənt ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪɡənt ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a formal dress that is stylish, graceful, and attractive
stylish and graceful formal dress

Examples

She attended the charity gala in an elegant gown decorated with sequins.
The designer showcased a new elegant gown collection during fashion week.
Her elegant gown flowed beautifully as she walked across the stage.

Synset Examples

The bride looked stunning in her elegant gown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening gown - a long formal dress worn for evening events
  • Synonym: ball gown - a very formal dress for dances or balls
