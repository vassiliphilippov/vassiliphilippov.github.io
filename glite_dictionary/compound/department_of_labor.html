Department of Labor

1. PROPN

/ dɪˌpɑɹtmənt əv ˈleɪbɚ / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtmənt əv ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
the federal department of the United States government responsible for promoting and improving the welfare and working conditions of wage earners
U.S. government department for labor issues
american

Examples

The Department of Labor announced new regulations to protect workers' rights across the country.
She applied for a grant from the Department of Labor to support her workforce training program.
The Department of Labor publishes important statistics on employment and unemployment rates each month.
