nettle family

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛt̬l ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈnɛtl ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Urticaceae) that includes many species of nettles, often characterized by stinging hairs on the leaves and stems
the plant family Urticaceae, including nettles

Examples

The nettle family includes a variety of plants that often have stinging hairs.
Many edible greens, such as stinging nettle, belong to the nettle family.
Research on the nettle family has revealed important medicinal properties among its members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Urticaceae - the botanical family of nettles
