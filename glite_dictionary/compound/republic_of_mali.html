Republic of Mali

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈmɑli / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈmɑːli / (BrE)
A landlocked country in northwestern Africa, formerly a French colony, which achieved independence in 1960.
country in northwestern Africa

Examples

The Republic of Mali declared its independence from France in 1960.
Many ancient empires flourished where the Republic of Mali now stands.
The Niger River is vital to agriculture in the Republic of Mali.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mali - another name for the Republic of Mali; also refers to the historical realm
