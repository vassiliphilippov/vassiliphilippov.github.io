tobacco mosaic

1. NOUN

/ təˈbækoʊ məˈzeɪɪk / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ məˈzeɪɪk / (BrE)
a viral plant disease causing mottled discoloration of the leaves, especially in tobacco plants
disease causing mottled leaves in tobacco

Examples

The spread of tobacco mosaic devastated crops across the region last year.
Scientists are studying ways to prevent tobacco mosaic from infecting other susceptible plants.
Symptoms of tobacco mosaic include yellowed, mottled patches on the leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tobacco mosaic virus - the virus that causes tobacco mosaic disease
