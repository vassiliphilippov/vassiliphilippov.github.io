coco palm

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌpɑːm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌpɑːm / (BrE)
A tall tropical palm tree that produces coconuts as its fruit, widely grown throughout tropical regions.
tropical palm tree producing coconuts

Examples

The coco palm thrives along sandy beaches in warm, humid climates.
Farmers harvest coconuts from the coco palm for food and oil.
The island was lined with tall coco palms swaying gently in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coconut palm - another term for coco palm, palm tree that bears coconuts
