letter opener

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛt̬ɚ ˈoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / ˈletə ˈəʊpənə / (BrE)
a small tool with a thin, dull blade, used for opening envelopes or slitting uncut pages of books
tool for opening envelopes

Examples

She picked up the letter opener to slice through the envelope neatly.
The antique silver letter opener on his desk was a family heirloom.
For years, he used the same letter opener to open his daily mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paper knife - tool for cutting open envelopes or uncut pages
