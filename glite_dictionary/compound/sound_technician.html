sound technician

1. NOUN

/ saʊnd tɛkˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / saʊnd tɛkˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a person responsible for operating and controlling sound equipment, especially for amplifying sound or producing sound effects in media such as TV, radio, or live performances
person who manages sound equipment

Examples

The sound technician adjusted the microphone levels before the concert started.
During the live broadcast, the sound technician was responsible for balancing all the audio.
Without a skilled sound technician, the play's sound effects would not have been as effective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audio engineer - person who operates and manages audio equipment and recording technology
