local hooer

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.kəl ˈhuː.ər / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl ˈhuː.ə / (BrE)
(slang, sometimes vulgar) A term used to refer to a person in a locality who is considered to be promiscuous.
local slang for a promiscuous person
slang vulgar

Examples

Everyone in town gossiped about the local hooer and her latest affairs.
Some people unfairly judge the local hooer without knowing their story.
Calling someone a local hooer can be hurtful and offensive.

Synset Examples

Everyone knows he's the local hooer of the group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuous - having or characterized by many transient sexual relationships
  • Synonym: whore - a person who has sex for money; pejorative slang for a promiscuous person

Etymology

From Irish/Scottish English pronunciation of 'whore' as 'hooer', with 'local' to specify someone from the area.
