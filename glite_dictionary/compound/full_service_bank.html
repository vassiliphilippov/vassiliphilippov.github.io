full service bank

1. NOUN

/ fʊl ˈsɝː.vɪs bæŋk / (AmE), / fʊl ˈsɜː.vɪs bæŋk / (BrE)
a bank that offers a wide range of financial services to customers, including accepting deposits, providing loans, and other related services
bank offering complete financial services

Examples

Many customers prefer a full service bank for the convenience of accessing all their financial needs in one place.
The community’s new full service bank offers mortgages, business loans, and personal checking accounts.
After moving to a rural area, she found there was no full service bank nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commercial bank - a bank that offers services to the general public and to companies
