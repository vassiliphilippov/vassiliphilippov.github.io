data communication

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.tə kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the electronic transmission of digitally encoded information, especially for processing or storage by computers
transmission of digital information

Examples

Data communication plays a crucial role in the functioning of computer networks.
The efficiency of data communication affects the speed of online transactions.
Modern industries rely heavily on secure data communication systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telecommunication - communication at a distance by electronic means
