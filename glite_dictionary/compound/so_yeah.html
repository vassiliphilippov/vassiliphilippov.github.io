so yeah

1. ADV

/ soʊ ˈjɛə / (AmE), / səʊ ˈjɛə / (BrE)
Used in informal speech or writing to summarize, conclude, or indicate a result or consequence of what has just been said.
phrase to summarize or indicate result
informal

Examples

We've finished the project, so yeah, we're ready to present tomorrow.
She didn't call me back, so yeah, I guess she's not interested.
The restaurant was closed when we arrived, so yeah, we had to find somewhere else to eat.
