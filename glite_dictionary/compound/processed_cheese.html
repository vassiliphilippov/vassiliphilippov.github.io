processed cheese

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑsɛst tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈprɒsɛst tʃiːz / (BrE)
cheese made by blending several types of cheese with emulsifiers and other ingredients to produce a uniform, shelf-stable product.
cheese product made by blending cheeses

Examples

I prefer natural cheddar to processed cheese because it has a richer flavor.
Sandwiches made with processed cheese are popular in school cafeterias.
Some people avoid processed cheese due to its artificial ingredients and additives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheese - a solid food made from the milk of cows, goats, or sheep
