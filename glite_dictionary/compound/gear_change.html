gear change

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪr tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɡɪə tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the action of shifting gears in a vehicle, especially to change speed or torque
action of shifting gears in a vehicle

Examples

The gear change was barely noticeable as the car accelerated on the highway.
She practiced making a smooth gear change during her driving lesson.
A mistimed gear change can cause the engine to stall.

Synset Examples

The truck driver's gear change was smooth and quick.
She learned the truck driver's gear change on her first day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gear shift - the mechanism used to change gears in a vehicle
  • Synonym: shift - the act of changing gears in a vehicle
