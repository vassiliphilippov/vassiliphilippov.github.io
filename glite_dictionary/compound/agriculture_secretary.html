Agriculture Secretary

1. NOUN

/ ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃɚ səˌkrɛˈtɛri / (AmE), / ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃə səˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
the head of a government's Department of Agriculture, responsible for agricultural policy and administration.
head of the Department of Agriculture

Examples

The Agriculture Secretary announced new subsidies to support local farmers this year.
After the disaster, the Agriculture Secretary visited the affected rural communities.
Each country has its own Agriculture Secretary or equivalent position overseeing farming policies.

Synset Examples

the first Secretary of Agriculture was Norman J. Colman, who was appointed by Cleveland

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secretary of Agriculture - alternative title for Agriculture Secretary; government official responsible for agriculture

2. NOUN

/ ˈæɡrɪˌkʌltʃɚ səˈkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ˈaɡrɪˌkʌltʃəri səˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
the head of the government department responsible for agriculture, usually called the Department of Agriculture
head of Department of Agriculture

Examples

The new Agriculture Secretary announced major reforms to the subsidy system.
She was the first woman ever appointed as Agriculture Secretary in her country.
The Agriculture Secretary met with farmers to discuss the impact of recent droughts.
