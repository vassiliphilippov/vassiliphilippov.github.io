column inch

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːləm ɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒləm ɪntʃ / (BrE)
a unit of measurement of printed text or advertising space, equal to one column wide and one inch deep
unit of measurement for printed space

Examples

The price of the ad is calculated per column inch.
Newspapers often charge advertisers by the column inch.
The article covered nearly thirty column inches in the local paper.
