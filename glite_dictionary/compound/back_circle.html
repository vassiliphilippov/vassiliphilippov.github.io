back circle

1. NOUN

/ bæk ˈsɜːrkəl / (AmE), / bæk ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
a gymnastic feat in which an acrobat arches their back from a lying position and bends their knees until their toes touch their head
gymnastic feat bending back until toes touch head

Examples

The gymnast performed a perfect back circle during her floor routine, impressing the judges.
Learning the back circle requires a great deal of flexibility and practice.
She amazed her classmates by demonstrating a back circle in gym class.
