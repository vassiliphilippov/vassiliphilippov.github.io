moonshine distiller

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnˌʃaɪn dɪˈstɪlər / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌʃaɪn dɪˈstɪlə / (BrE)
a person who produces illegal distilled alcohol, typically known as moonshine
person making illegal distilled alcohol

Examples

Local authorities arrested the moonshine distiller after discovering his hidden still in the woods.
The old cabin once belonged to a famous moonshine distiller during Prohibition.
Many moonshine distillers passed down their secret recipes through generations.

Synset Examples

The festival featured local moonshine distillers showcasing their craft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bootlegger - person who makes or sells illegal alcohol
