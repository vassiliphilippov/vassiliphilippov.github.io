coming together

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmɪŋ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
the act or process of joining as one group, unit, or entity
act of uniting or joining together

Examples

The coming together of the two departments improved communication across the company.
Their coming together marked a significant change in the direction of the movement.
A successful project often depends on the coming together of talented individuals.

Synset Examples

the merging of the two groups occurred quickly
there was no meeting of minds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merging - the act of combining or joining together
  • Synonym: union - the act of uniting or joining two or more things
  • Antonym: separation - the act of moving apart or dividing

2. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmɪŋ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmɪŋ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
an occasion when people gather for a common purpose; a meeting or assembly
an occasion of gathering or assembly

Examples

The annual coming together of the villagers is a cherished tradition.
The coming together of different cultures can lead to greater understanding.
Their coming together was marked by lively discussions and shared goals.
