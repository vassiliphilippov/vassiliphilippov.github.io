people in power

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəl ɪn ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl ɪn ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
the group of individuals who hold positions of authority or control within a government, organization, or society
those holding authority or control

Examples

The people in power have the resources to shape public policy.
Artists often question the actions of people in power through their work.
Reforms are difficult to implement when people in power resist change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authority - the power or right to give orders and make decisions
  • Synonym: power elite - a small group of people who hold a disproportionate amount of power
