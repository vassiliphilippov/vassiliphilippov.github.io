house paint

1. NOUN

/ haʊs peɪnt / (AmE), / haʊs peɪnt / (BrE)
paint specially formulated for covering the exterior woodwork or surfaces of a house
paint for a house's exterior

Examples

We chose a bright blue house paint to refresh the exterior of our cottage.
Before you apply house paint, make sure the wood is dry and clean.
The store carries a wide variety of house paint in both oil and latex formulas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paint - a colored substance spread over a surface to leave a thin decorative or protective coating
