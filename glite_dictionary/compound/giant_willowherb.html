giant willowherb

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɪloʊˌhɜrb / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɪləʊˌhɜːb / (BrE)
A tall North American perennial plant with creeping rootstocks, narrow leaves, and abundant spikes of pinkish-purple flowers, often found in burned or recently cleared areas; valued as a honey plant.
a tall flowering North American perennial plant

Examples

The giant willowherb flourished quickly in the areas that had been recently cleared by wildfire.
Bees are attracted to the giant willowherb due to its rich supply of nectar.
Local beekeepers plant giant willowherb near their hives to boost honey production.
