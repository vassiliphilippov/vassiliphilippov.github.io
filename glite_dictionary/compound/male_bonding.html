male bonding

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈbɑndɪŋ / (AmE), / meɪl ˈbɒndɪŋ / (BrE)
the formation of a close personal relationship or connection between men, often through shared activities or experiences
close relationship between men

Examples

The hiking trip provided plenty of opportunities for male bonding among the coworkers.
Many movies depict male bonding through scenes of shared challenges and adventures.
Some believe that sports teams foster strong male bonding that can last a lifetime.

Synset Examples

the rituals known as male bonding do not necessarily involve drinking beer together

Related Terms
