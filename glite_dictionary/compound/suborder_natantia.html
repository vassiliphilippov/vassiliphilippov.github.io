suborder Natantia

1. NOUN

/ sʌbˈɔrdɚ næˈtænʃiə / (AmE), / sʌbˈɔːdə næˈtanʃɪə / (BrE)
A former taxonomic suborder of decapod crustaceans, including shrimp and prawns.
former decapod suborder (shrimp, prawns)

Examples

The classification of shrimp under the suborder Natantia has been revised in recent years.
Many marine biologists once grouped prawns and similar species into the suborder Natantia.
Research into the suborder Natantia highlighted the diversity of swimming crustaceans.

Etymology

From Latin 'natantia', plural of 'natans' (swimming), referring to the swimming nature of included species.
