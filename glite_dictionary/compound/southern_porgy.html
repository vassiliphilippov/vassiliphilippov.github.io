southern porgy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈpɔrɡi / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈpɔːɡi / (BrE)
a species of porgy fish found in the southern Atlantic coastal waters of North America
a porgy fish from southern Atlantic North America

Examples

Fishermen often catch southern porgy along the coasts of Florida and Georgia.
The southern porgy is prized for its mild, white flesh in local seafood markets.
Scientists are studying the population of southern porgy to monitor changes in the coastal ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porgy - a fish of the Sparidae family, found in coastal waters
