golden jubilee

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən dʒuːˈbɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən dʒuːˈbiːliː / (BrE)
The celebration of a 50th anniversary, especially of an important event such as a marriage, organization, or reign.
50th anniversary celebration

Examples

The city organized a parade to commemorate its golden jubilee of independence.
Their company held a special dinner in honor of its golden jubilee this year.
The couple received a certificate from the mayor for their golden jubilee wedding anniversary.

Synset Examples

They celebrated their golden jubilee with a grand party.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: silver jubilee - 25th anniversary celebration
  • Degree Higher: diamond jubilee - 60th or 75th anniversary celebration

Etymology

From 'golden' (symbolizing the 50th year) + 'jubilee', from Latin 'jubilæus' (year of jubilation).
