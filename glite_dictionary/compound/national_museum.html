national museum

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃənl məˈzuːəm / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl mjuˈziːəm / (BrE)
A museum established to represent and preserve the cultural, historical, or artistic heritage of a nation, often housing significant national artifacts and exhibits.
principal museum representing a nation

Examples

The country's national museum houses some of its most treasured historical artifacts.
We spent the afternoon exploring the art collection at the national museum.
Several schools organized trips to the national museum during history month.

Synset Examples

She visited Canberra for the national museum.
