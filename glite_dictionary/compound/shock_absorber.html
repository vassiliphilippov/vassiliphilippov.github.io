shock absorber

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑk əbˌzɔrbɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒk əbˌzɔːbə / (BrE)
A device fitted to a vehicle's suspension to absorb and damp sudden shocks or impulses.
device for absorbing shocks on vehicles

Examples

The shock absorber in my bike helps make the ride much smoother.
If a shock absorber wears out, it can affect your car’s handling and safety.
After driving over the pothole, the mechanic inspected the shock absorber for damage.

Synset Examples

the old car needed a new set of shocks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damper - a device that deadens, restrains, or depresses
