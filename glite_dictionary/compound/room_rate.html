room rate

1. NOUN

/ ruːm reɪt / (AmE), / ruːm reɪt / (BrE)
the amount charged per night for staying in a hotel room
daily price for a hotel room

Examples

The hotel’s room rate includes breakfast and free Wi-Fi.
During the holiday season, room rates typically increase due to high demand.
She called several hotels to compare their room rates before booking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rate - a fixed price or charge for something
