intra vires

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə ˈvaɪriːz / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə ˈvaɪəriːz / (BrE)
(of an act or decision) within the legal power or authority of a person, official, or body.
within legal power or authority
legal

Examples

The new regulations were found to be intra vires by the court.
The board acted intra vires when approving the merger within its defined authority.
This decision is intra vires the powers granted to the committee by the company's charter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ultra vires - outside legal authority; beyond one's legal power

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'within the powers.'
