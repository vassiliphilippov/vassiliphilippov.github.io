devil lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəl ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl ˌlɪli / (BrE)
an East Asian perennial plant with large, reddish-orange, black-spotted flowers and reflexed petals
East Asian flower with spotted, reflexed petals

Examples

The devil lily brightened the shaded corner of the garden with its orange-hued blossoms.
Gardeners prize the devil lily for its striking, spotted petals and elegant appearance.
In late summer, the devil lily blooms, attracting butterflies and admirers alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lily - a plant with large, prominent flowers
