piked reverse hang

1. NOUN

/ paɪkt rɪˈvɜrs hæŋ / (AmE), / paɪkt rɪˈvɜːs hæŋ / (BrE)
A gymnastic position on rings where the gymnast hangs upside down with the body bent at the hips and legs kept straight.
gymnastics move: inverted bent-body hang

Examples

The gymnast held a perfect piked reverse hang during the routine.
She practiced her piked reverse hang to improve her ring performance.
Judges awarded high marks for his stable piked reverse hang position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reverse hang - hanging upside down on rings

Etymology

From 'piked', meaning bent at the hips, and 'reverse hang', a gymnastic term for hanging upside down.
