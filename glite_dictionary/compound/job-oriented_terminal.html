job-oriented terminal

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑb ɔˈriən.tɪd ˈtɝː.mɪ.nəl / (AmE), / dʒɒb ɔˈrɪən.tɪd ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəl / (BrE)
A computer terminal designed for a particular application or specific task.
terminal designed for a particular application

Examples

The company installed a job-oriented terminal to streamline order processing for the warehouse staff.
Each job-oriented terminal is configured with software tailored to specific business functions.
They replaced the multipurpose computers with job-oriented terminals at customer service points.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dedicated terminal - terminal used for a specific purpose
