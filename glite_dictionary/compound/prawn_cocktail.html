prawn cocktail

1. NOUN

/ prɔn ˈkɑk.teɪl / (AmE), / prɔːn ˈkɒk.teɪl / (BrE)
a cold dish consisting of prawns served in a spiced sauce, often as a starter
cold dish of prawns in sauce

Examples

For starters, I ordered the prawn cocktail, which was served with a creamy Marie Rose sauce.
The restaurant is famous for its classic prawn cocktail appetizer.
She always chooses prawn cocktail when it's available on the menu.

Synset Examples

She criticized the prawn cocktail offensive for being outdated and elitist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrimp cocktail - a similar dish with shrimp, often found in American cuisine
