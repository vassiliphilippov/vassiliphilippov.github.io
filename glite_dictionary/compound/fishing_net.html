fishing net

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ nɛt / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ nɛt / (BrE)
a net used for catching or enclosing fish, typically by pulling it through the water or drawing it in.
net used for catching fish

Examples

He cast the fishing net into the lake early in the morning.
The villagers mended their fishing nets before heading out to sea.
A large fishing net lay drying on the dock after a long day's work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: net - a piece of meshed fabric used for catching fish, animals, or objects
