toilet paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlət ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
soft, thin, absorbent paper used for cleaning oneself after using the toilet
soft paper for use in toilets

Examples

Could you please pass me the toilet paper from the shelf?
During the shortages, many stores quickly ran out of toilet paper.
He bought a pack of recycled toilet paper to reduce waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tissue - thin, soft paper used for cleaning or wiping
