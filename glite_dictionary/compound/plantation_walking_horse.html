plantation walking horse

1. NOUN

/ plænˈteɪʃən ˈwɔkɪŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / plænˈteɪʃ(ə)n ˈwɔːkɪŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse breed or type notable for stamina and trained to perform a fast running walk
a gaited horse known for its running walk

Examples

The plantation walking horse is admired for its endurance and smooth gait over long distances.
She trained her plantation walking horse to excel in competitive trail riding events.
Many riders appreciate the plantation walking horse for its comfortable running walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tennessee walking horse - an American breed of gaited horse known for its distinctive running walk
