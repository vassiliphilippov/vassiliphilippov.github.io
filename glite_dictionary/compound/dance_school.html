dance school

1. NOUN

/ dæns skuːl / (AmE), / dɑːns skuːl / (BrE)
a school where people receive instruction and training in dancing
school for learning dance

Examples

She enrolled at a prestigious dance school to improve her ballet technique.
Many children attend dance schools after regular classes to learn different styles.
The dance school organized an annual performance for the students' families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance studio - a place where dance is practiced or taught
