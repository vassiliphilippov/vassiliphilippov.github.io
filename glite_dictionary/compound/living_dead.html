living dead

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ dɛd / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ dɛd / (BrE)
a dead body that has been supernaturally reanimated, especially as an undead creature such as a zombie or vampire
reanimated dead body; undead

Examples

The ancient curse unleashed an army of the living dead on the village.
Fans of horror movies are fascinated by stories featuring the living dead.
According to legend, the living dead roam the graveyard at midnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zombie - a reanimated corpse, especially in popular fiction
  • Synonym: undead - a fictional being that is dead yet behaves as if alive
