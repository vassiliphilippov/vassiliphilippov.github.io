union member

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːniən ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
A person who belongs to a trade union, typically a worker represented by the union.
worker belonging to a trade union

Examples

She has been a union member for over twenty years.
All union members are expected to attend the meeting next week.
Becoming a union member provided him with better job security and benefits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade unionist - a member or supporter of a trade union
