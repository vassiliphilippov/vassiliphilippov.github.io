four-year-old

1. NOUN

/ fɔr jɪr oʊld / (AmE), / fɔː jɪə əʊld / (BrE)
A person, animal, or thing that is four years old.
child, animal, or thing aged four years

Examples

The four-year-old recited the entire alphabet without any help.
Our family adopted a four-year-old dog from the shelter.
She enrolled her four-year-old in swimming lessons this summer.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: five-year-old - a person, animal, or thing that is five years old
  • Degree Lower: three-year-old - a person, animal, or thing that is three years old
