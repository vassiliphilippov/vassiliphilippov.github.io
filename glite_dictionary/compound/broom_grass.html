broom grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrum ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbruːm ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several tall grasses, especially of the genus Andropogon, used in making brooms
tall grass used for making brooms

Examples

Traditional brooms made from broom grass are still used in many rural communities.
The farmer harvested broom grass to sell at the local market.
Some species of broom grass can grow over six feet tall.
