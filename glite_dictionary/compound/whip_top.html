whip top

1. NOUN

/ wɪp tɑp / (AmE), / wɪp tɒp / (BrE)
A spinning toy that is set in motion by striking it with a whip or lash.
spinning toy spun by whipping

Examples

He showed the children how to spin the whip top by lashing it with a string.
My grandfather used to make wooden whip tops for us when we were kids.
The museum displayed an old-fashioned whip top among other vintage toys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: top - a toy that spins on a point when set in motion
