ethnic joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛθ.nɪk dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk dʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that targets or makes fun of a particular ethnic group, often considered offensive or prejudiced.
joke targeting an ethnic group
offensive

Examples

He was criticized for telling an ethnic joke at the party.
Many people find ethnic jokes inappropriate and offensive in any social setting.
She regretted laughing at an ethnic joke after realizing its impact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racist joke - a joke expressing or promoting racism
  • Degree Lower: joke - something said or done to provoke laughter or amusement
