indigo bird

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.dɪˌɡoʊ bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.ɡəʊ bɜːd / (BrE)
A small North American songbird, especially the indigo bunting, with deep blue plumage in the male.
small deep blue North American songbird

Examples

We spotted an indigo bird perched on the fence, its feathers shining brightly in the morning sun.
Every summer, the indigo bird returns to our backyard to nest among the shrubs.
The vibrant blue color makes the indigo bird easy to spot among other common songbirds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indigo bunting - small North American songbird with brilliant blue plumage
