emerald creeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmɚəld ˈkriːpɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛməɹəld ˈkɹiːpə / (BrE)
a vigorous evergreen climbing plant native to the Philippines, grown for its striking green, claw-shaped flowers
Philippine climbing plant with green flowers

Examples

The emerald creeper produces dazzling cascades of green flowers in tropical gardens.
Botanists are fascinated by the unique color of the emerald creeper's blossoms.
She planted an emerald creeper by the trellis to attract more butterflies to the yard.
