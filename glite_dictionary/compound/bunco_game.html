bunco game

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌnkoʊ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkəʊ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A swindle involving cheating at gambling or convincing someone to buy worthless property.
a type of cheating swindle

Examples

The detective warned the community about the bunco game targeting elderly residents.
He lost his savings in a bunco game run by a clever con artist.
Authorities are cracking down on bunco games that trick people into buying fake properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunco - a swindle or confidence trick
  • Synonym: con game - a trick to cheat someone out of money or property

Etymology

From American English "bunco" (a confidence trick), possibly from Spanish "banco" (bench, banking table).
