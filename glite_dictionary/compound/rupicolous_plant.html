rupicolous plant

1. NOUN

/ ruːˈpɪkələs plænt / (AmE), / ruːˈpɪkələs plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that grows among or on rocks.
plant that grows among rocks

Examples

The rupicolous plant species thrived in the cracks of the mountain cliffs.
This botanical garden features many unique rupicolous plants from arid regions.
A rupicolous plant typically adapts to harsh, rocky environments with minimal soil.

Etymology

From Latin 'rupes' (rock) and '-colous' (inhabiting).
