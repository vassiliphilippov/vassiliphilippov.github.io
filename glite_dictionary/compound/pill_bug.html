pill bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪl ˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl ˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
A small terrestrial isopod with a convex, segmented body, known for its ability to roll into a ball when disturbed.
small isopod that curls into a ball

Examples

When touched, the pill bug instantly curled into a tight ball.
Children often find pill bugs under rocks in the garden after rain.
Scientists study the pill bug's defense mechanism for inspiration in robotics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodlouse - a terrestrial isopod found in damp places
