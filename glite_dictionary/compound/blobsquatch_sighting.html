blobsquatch sighting

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑbˌskwɑtʃ ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɒbˌskwɒtʃ ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
An instance of someone claiming to have seen a blobsquatch, often based on unclear or ambiguous photographic evidence.
reported sighting of a blobsquatch

Examples

The documentary featured a supposed blobsquatch sighting.
After camping in the woods, they claimed to witness a blobsquatch sighting near the lake.
Online forums are full of blurry photos labeled as the latest blobsquatch sighting.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bigfoot sighting - a reported sighting of Bigfoot, a mythical ape-like creature

Etymology

From 'blob' (indistinct shape) + 'Sasquatch' (mythical ape-like creature of North American folklore).
