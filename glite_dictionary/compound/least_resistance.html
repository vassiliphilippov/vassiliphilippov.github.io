least resistance

1. NOUN

/ list rɪˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / liːst rɪˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the easiest or least effortful way to do something, often chosen to avoid difficulty or confrontation
easiest way to do something

Examples

He always chooses the least resistance when solving problems, preferring easy shortcuts.
Some students seek the least resistance to get through assignments without much effort.
The company took the path of least resistance by accepting the first offer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: path of least resistance - course of action that avoids difficulty; the easiest way
