America's Hat

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛɹɪkəz hæt / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkəz hæt / (BrE)
a humorous or facetious nickname for Canada, referencing its geographic position north of the United States
humorous nickname for Canada
informal
