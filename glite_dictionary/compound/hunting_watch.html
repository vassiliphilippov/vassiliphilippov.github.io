hunting watch

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ wɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a pocket watch with a hinged metal cover to protect the crystal
pocket watch with protective lid

Examples

He inherited his grandfather's hunting watch, carefully opening the lid to check the time.
Collectors prize a hunting watch for its ornate case and protective cover.
The hunting watch was designed so riders could check the time without damaging the glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocket watch - a watch carried in the pocket and usually attached to a chain

Etymology

Named for its original use by hunters to protect the watch during outdoor activities.
