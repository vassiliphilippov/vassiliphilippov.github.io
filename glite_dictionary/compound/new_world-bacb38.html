new world

1. NOUN

/ nu ˈwɝld / (AmE), / njuː ˈwɜːld / (BrE)
a situation or realm offering new opportunities, experiences, or possibilities, especially as something unknown or untried before
a realm of new opportunities or possibilities

Examples

The discovery of the internet created a new world for communication and business.
Graduating from college felt like stepping into a new world full of possibilities.
Virtual reality technology is opening a new world for gaming enthusiasts.

Synset Examples

Columbus' journey opened new worlds for exploration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncharted territory - an area, situation, or subject that has not been experienced before
