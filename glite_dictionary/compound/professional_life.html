professional life

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl laɪf / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃ(ə)nəl laɪf / (BrE)
the period of a person's life spent working in a profession or career, especially in industry, business, or a specialized field
a person's career or working life

Examples

She devoted most of her professional life to teaching at the university.
In his professional life, he worked as a doctor for over forty years.
Balancing family and professional life can be challenging for many people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career - an occupation or profession followed as a life's work
