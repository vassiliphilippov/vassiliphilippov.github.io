colorful blinkie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚfəl ˈblɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl ˈblɪŋki / (BrE)
an animated graphic, usually small and decorative, featuring bright colors and used to embellish online profiles or websites
decorative animated graphic for online use

Examples

She added a colorful blinkie to her profile page to make it more eye-catching.
Many users decorate their blogs with colorful blinkies that flash and sparkle.
A colorful blinkie can be a fun way to express your mood online.
