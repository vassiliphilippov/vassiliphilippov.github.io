juvenile wart

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuːvəˌnaɪl wɔrt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːvɪnaɪl wɔːt / (BrE)
a small, benign wart occurring on the face or hands of children
a benign wart found on children
medical

Examples

The doctor identified the growth on her hand as a juvenile wart and reassured the family it was benign.
Many children develop a juvenile wart on their fingers, but these often disappear without treatment.
He was embarrassed by the juvenile wart on his face, but the dermatologist said it was harmless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verruca - a wart, especially on the hand or foot
  • Synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin
