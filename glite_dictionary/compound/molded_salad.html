molded salad

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊl.dɪd ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / ˈməʊl.dɪd ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
a salad made by setting meats, vegetables, or other ingredients in gelatin, then shaping them in a mold
gelatin-based salad set in a mold

Examples

My grandmother always prepared a molded salad with vegetables and ham for our holiday dinners.
The molded salad wobbled slightly as it was brought to the table, its colorful layers clearly visible.
Retro cookbooks often feature recipes for molded salad made with fruit and whipped cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gelatin salad - a salad made primarily with gelatin and various ingredients
