back brace

1. NOUN

/ bæk breɪs / (AmE), / bæk breɪs / (BrE)
a supportive device worn around the back to provide stability or correct posture, typically used to treat or prevent back injuries
supportive device for the back

Examples

After her surgery, she had to wear a back brace for several months.
The doctor recommended a back brace to help improve his posture while working.
He found that using a back brace reduced his lower back pain during long periods of standing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brace - a device that supports or holds something in place
