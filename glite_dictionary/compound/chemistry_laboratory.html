chemistry laboratory

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪstri ˈlæbrəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈkemɪstri ˈlæbrətəri / (BrE)
A room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching in chemistry.
a lab for chemical experiments or research

Examples

She works in a state-of-the-art chemistry laboratory at the university.
The students conducted their experiments in the chemistry laboratory.
Safety procedures are very important in any chemistry laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments
  • Degree Lower: science laboratory - a laboratory used for scientific research in general
