Chesterbelloc manner

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʃɛstərˈbɛlɒk ˈmænər / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛstəˈbɛlək ˈmænə / (BrE)
In a style reminiscent of Chesterbelloc, often charming, witty, or marked by cleverness and lively argument.
style reminiscent of Chesterbelloc: witty, charming

Examples

The author's critique was written in a Chesterbelloc manner, blending humor with incisive commentary.
He always debates in a Chesterbelloc manner, full of wit and playful logic.
Her essays, delivered in a Chesterbelloc manner, captivated readers with their charm and insight.

Synset Examples

She delivered her opinion in a Chesterbelloc manner, charming everyone.

Etymology

Blend of the surnames Chesterton and Belloc, referring to writers G. K. Chesterton and Hilaire Belloc.
