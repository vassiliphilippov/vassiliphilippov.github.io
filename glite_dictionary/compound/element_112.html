element 112

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛləmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈtu / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪmənt wʌn ˈwʌn ˈtjuː / (BrE)
a synthetic radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 112, also known as copernicium
synthetic chemical element, atomic number 112

Examples

Scientists synthesized element 112 for the first time in a laboratory in Germany.
The chemical properties of element 112 are similar to those of mercury.
Researchers continue to study the behavior of element 112 because of its instability.
