notebook computer

1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊtbʊk kəmˈpjuːtər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtbʊk kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
a small, lightweight portable computer, similar to a laptop
small portable computer

Examples

She brought her notebook computer to the meeting for taking notes and accessing documents.
A notebook computer is ideal for students who need to work in multiple locations.
Technological advances have made the notebook computer more powerful and affordable than ever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laptop - a portable computer that is small enough to use on one's lap

Etymology

Formed from 'notebook' (referring to the compact size) and 'computer'.
