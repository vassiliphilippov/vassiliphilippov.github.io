dusky shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌski ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈdʌski ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, slender blue-grey shark (Carcharhinus obscurus) found in tropical and temperate waters worldwide
a species of blue-grey shark

Examples

The dusky shark is often found in warm coastal waters around the world.
A researcher tagged several dusky sharks to track their migration patterns.
The dusky shark can be identified by its slender, blue-grey body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Carcharhinus obscurus - scientific name of the dusky shark
