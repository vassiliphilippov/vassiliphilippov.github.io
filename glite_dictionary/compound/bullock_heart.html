bullock heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlək hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlək hɑːt / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Annona reticulata) that bears a bristly, heart-shaped, acid tropical fruit.
tropical fruit tree, Annona reticulata

Examples

The farmer sold ripe bullock hearts at the local market.
Have you ever tasted a bullock heart fruit while traveling in the Caribbean?
The bullock heart tree thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custard apple - another tropical fruit from the Annona genus, similar to bullock heart
