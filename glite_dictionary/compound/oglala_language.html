Oglala language

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈɡlɑː.lə ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əʊˈɡlɑː.lə ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Siouan language spoken by the Oglala people, a subgroup of the Lakota Sioux
language of the Oglala (Siouan family)

Examples

The Oglala language is an important part of the tribe's cultural heritage.
Many community programs work to preserve the Oglala language among younger generations.
He studied the Oglala language to better understand Lakota traditions and stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lakota - a North American Indian language of the Sioux group closely related to Oglala

Etymology

From Oglala, a Lakota tribal name, and 'language'.
