marketing information

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːrkət̬ɪŋ ˌɪnfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ ˌɪnfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
Data and insights obtained from marketing research, used to inform marketing strategies and product development.
data from marketing research

Examples

The company relies on accurate marketing information to develop its advertising strategy.
Our team analyzed the latest marketing information before launching the new product line.
Good decisions are based on up-to-date marketing information collected from various sources.

Synset Examples

Smart investors want marketing information about what consumers want to view and the public's opinion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: market research - The process of gathering information about consumers' needs and preferences.
