bustling with

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌs.lɪŋ wɪð / (AmE), / ˈbʌs.lɪŋ wɪð / (BrE)
(of a place) full of energetic activity or movement, usually involving many people.
full of energetic activity

Examples

The city center is always bustling with tourists during the summer months.
The restaurant was bustling with waiters and customers eager to be served.
By midday, the square was bustling with street performers, vendors, and shoppers.

Synset Examples
