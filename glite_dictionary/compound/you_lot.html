you lot

1. PRON

/ ju lɑt / (AmE), / juː lɒt / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) Used to address or refer to a group of people.
informal plural form of 'you', chiefly British
informal british

Examples

You lot always find a way to make everyone laugh at family gatherings.
Where are you lot off to so early in the morning?
I can't believe you lot finished the project ahead of schedule!

Synset Examples

Hey, you lot are invited to the party this weekend.
I can't believe you lot made such a mess!
