frozen foods

1. NOUN

/ ˈfroʊzən fuːdz / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən fuːdz / (BrE)
foods that have been preserved by freezing to maintain freshness and prolong shelf life
foods preserved by freezing

Examples

Most supermarkets have a wide selection of frozen foods for busy families.
He prefers to cook with fresh ingredients rather than relying on frozen foods.
The company exports its frozen foods to markets around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frozen food - food that has been preserved by freezing (singular)
  • Antonym: fresh food - food not preserved by freezing; not processed
