state of excitement

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / steɪt əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
a condition of being very excited or emotionally aroused
condition of being very excited

Examples

The children were in a state of excitement as they waited for the parade to begin.
After hearing the good news, she entered a state of excitement and could not sit still.
The team's victory put the entire town into a state of excitement.

Synset Examples

The dog was in a state of excitement during the walk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitement - a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • Antonym: calm - a state of peace and quiet, without excitement
