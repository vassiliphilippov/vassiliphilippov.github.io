pipe major

1. NOUN

/ paɪp ˈmeɪdʒɚ / (AmE), / paɪp ˈmeɪdʒə / (BrE)
the chief piper in a bagpipe band, responsible for leading the musicians and arranging music
chief piper in a bagpipe band

Examples

The pipe major led the band onto the parade field with a flourish.
After years of dedication, he was promoted to pipe major of the regiment's pipe band.
The competition was fierce, and each pipe major hoped to impress the judges with their band's performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drum major - the leader of a marching band or drum corps

Etymology

From 'pipe' (bagpipes) + 'major' (from Latin 'maior', greater or leader).
