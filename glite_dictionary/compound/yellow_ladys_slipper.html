yellow lady's slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
a species of orchid with a yellow, slipper-shaped lip and maroon or purple-brown petals, native to Europe, North America, and Japan
a yellow-flowered orchid species

Examples

The yellow lady's slipper is a rare and beautiful orchid found in woodlands.
Some gardeners protect wild yellow lady's slipper orchids in nature reserves.
Photographers travel each spring to capture the yellow lady's slipper in bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - any orchid of the genus Cypripedium, with slipper-shaped pouches
