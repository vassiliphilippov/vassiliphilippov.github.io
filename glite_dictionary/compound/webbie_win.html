webbie win

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛbi wɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɛbi wɪn / (BrE)
an instance of winning a Webby Award, an accolade for excellence on the internet
winning a Webby Award

Examples

After their webbie win, the design team gained international recognition.
This year's webbie win meant a lot to our marketing department.
They proudly displayed their webbie win certificate in the office lobby.

Synset Examples

They celebrated the webbie win with a small party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Webby Award - an annual award honoring excellence on the internet

Etymology

From 'Webby' (name of the Webby Awards for internet excellence) + 'win'.
