unfathomable mystery

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfæðəməbəl ˈmɪstəri / (AmE), / ʌnˈfaðəməbl ˈmɪstəri / (BrE)
A mystery that is impossible to understand or explain.
mystery that cannot be understood

Examples

The disappearance of the colony remains an unfathomable mystery centuries later.
Some believe the origins of consciousness are an unfathomable mystery beyond human understanding.
For generations, the strange lights in the sky were considered an unfathomable mystery.

Synset Examples

The universe holds unfathomable mysteries yet to be discovered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enigma - something that is mysterious or difficult to understand
