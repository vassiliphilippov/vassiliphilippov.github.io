table saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl sɔ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl sɔː / (BrE)
A circular saw mounted below a table or bench, with its blade projecting up through a slot, used for cutting wood and other materials.
bench-mounted circular saw

Examples

Always use a push stick when operating the table saw for added safety.
He built a custom bookshelf with the help of his table saw.
The carpenter adjusted the height of the blade on the table saw before making the cut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular saw - power saw with a rapidly rotating toothed disk
