Secretary of Defense

1. NOUN

/ səˈkrɛ.t̬ə.ri əv dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / sɪˈkrɛt.əri əv dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
the head of the United States Department of Defense, responsible for overseeing the armed forces and advising the President on military matters
head of the U.S. Department of Defense
american

Examples

He was appointed Secretary of Defense by the President in 2019.
The Secretary of Defense held a press conference to address the crisis.
Many former Secretaries of Defense have transitioned to roles in the private sector after their service.

Synset Examples

the position of Defense Secretary was created in 1947

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Defense Secretary - alternative title for the Secretary of Defense
  • Antonym: Secretary of State - head of the U.S. Department of State
