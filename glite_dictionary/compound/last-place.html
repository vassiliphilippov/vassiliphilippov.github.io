last-place

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæst ˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːst ˌpleɪs / (BrE)
Lowest in rank, position, or importance; coming or placed at the end in a series.
lowest in rank or importance

Examples

Despite their efforts, the team finished in last-place again this season.
He was disappointed to receive the last-place award at the science fair.
The company is determined to avoid a last-place ranking in customer satisfaction surveys.

Synset Examples

last prize
in last place

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first-place - highest in rank or importance
