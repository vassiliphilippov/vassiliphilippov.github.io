burn plant

1. NOUN

/ bɝn plænt / (AmE), / bɜːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
A succulent plant with thick, spiny-margined leaves containing a soothing gel used to treat burns, commonly known as Aloe vera.
succulent used for soothing burns (Aloe vera)

Examples

The gel from the burn plant is often used to soothe sunburns.
Many people keep a burn plant in their kitchen for minor skin irritations.
She learned that the burn plant thrives best in bright, indirect sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aloe vera - succulent plant used for its soothing gel
