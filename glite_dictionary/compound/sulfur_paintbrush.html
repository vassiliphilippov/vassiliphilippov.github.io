sulfur paintbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌl.fɚ ˈpeɪnt.brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.fə ˈpeɪnt.brʌʃ / (BrE)
A plant found in moist highland meadows, known for its clusters of ragged pale yellow flowers.
plant with pale yellow flower clusters

Examples

The sulfur paintbrush brightened the alpine meadow with its pale yellow blooms.
Botanists surveyed the area to study the unique population of sulfur paintbrush.
Hikers admired the ragged clusters of flowers on the sulfur paintbrush along the trail.
