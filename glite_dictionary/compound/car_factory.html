car factory

1. NOUN

/ kɑr ˈfæktəri / (AmE), / kɑː ˈfæktəri / (BrE)
A factory where automobiles are manufactured.
a factory making cars

Examples

The city is famous for its car factory that produces thousands of vehicles every year.
After graduating, she found a job at a car factory as an engineer.
Strikes at the car factory led to a slowdown in production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile plant - a plant where automobiles are manufactured
