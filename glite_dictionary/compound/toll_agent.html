toll agent

1. NOUN

/ toʊl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / təʊl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person employed to collect tolls, usually at a toll booth or toll plaza
person who collects tolls

Examples

The toll agent greeted each driver with a friendly smile as they stopped to pay.
As a toll agent, she worked long hours on the busy expressway.
The company announced plans to automate booths, reducing the need for toll agents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toll collector - a person who collects tolls
  • Degree Lower: cashier - a person handling payments in various transactions
