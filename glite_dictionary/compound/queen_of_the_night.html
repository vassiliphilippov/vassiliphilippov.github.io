queen of the night

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwiːn əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌkwiːn əv ðə ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
A tropical American climbing cactus, especially Selenicereus or Epiphyllum species, cultivated for its large, fragrant, night-blooming flowers and colorful fruit.
tropical cactus with night-blooming flowers

Examples

The queen of the night bloomed in our garden, filling the air with a sweet fragrance.
Gardeners often stay up late just to witness the queen of the night flower open for a few hours.
My grandmother cherishes her queen of the night plant and proudly shows its rare blossoms to visitors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cereus - a genus of cacti including several night-blooming species
