loony lefty

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni ˈlɛfti / (AmE), / ˈluːni ˈlɛfti / (BrE)
A disparaging term for someone with extreme or eccentric left-wing political views.
derogatory term for extreme left-wing person
informal offensive

Examples

Some newspapers dismiss protesters as loony lefties without listening to their arguments.
He was labeled a loony lefty after proposing radical changes at the council meeting.
Online debates often devolve into name-calling, with opponents quick to use terms like loony lefty.

Synset Examples

He called her a loony lefty for her radical ideas.
The loony lefty always brings up controversial topics.
They often argue with that loony lefty on social media.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lefty - left-wing person (informal)
  • Antonym: right-winger - person with right-wing political views

Etymology

Formed from 'loony' (slang for crazy) and 'lefty' (slang for left-wing person).
