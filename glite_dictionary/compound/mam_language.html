Mam language

1. NOUN

/ mɑm ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mam ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Mayan language spoken by the Mam people of Guatemala and southern Mexico
Mayan language of the Mam people

Examples

Many people in western Guatemala speak the Mam language in their daily lives.
Researchers are working to document and preserve the Mam language for future generations.
He is fluent in both Spanish and the Mam language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mayan language - any of the languages belonging to the Mayan family

Etymology

Mam from the indigenous name of the people who speak the language.
