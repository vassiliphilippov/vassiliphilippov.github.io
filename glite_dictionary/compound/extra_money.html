extra money

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkstrə ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈɛkstrə ˈmʌni / (BrE)
money received in addition to one's regular income or earnings
additional income beyond regular earnings

Examples

She took a part-time job to earn some extra money for her summer vacation.
Many people look for ways to make extra money outside their main profession.
Selling homemade crafts online provided him with some extra money each month.

Synset Examples

He made extra money by renting out his car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bonus - an extra payment in addition to what is usual or expected
  • Synonym: side income - income earned in addition to a main job, often from another source
