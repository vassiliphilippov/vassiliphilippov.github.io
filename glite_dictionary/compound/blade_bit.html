blade bit

1. NOUN

/ bleɪd bɪt / (AmE), / bleɪd bɪt / (BrE)
A type of drilling bit featuring hardened cutting edges for boring into materials.
drilling bit with hardened cutting edges

Examples

The blade bit quickly bored through the wooden plank with little resistance.
He replaced his old drill attachment with a new blade bit for better performance.
Make sure your blade bit is properly hardened before using it on metal surfaces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill bit - cutting tool for making holes, used in drills
