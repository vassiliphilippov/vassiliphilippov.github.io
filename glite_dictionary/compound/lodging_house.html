lodging house

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːdʒɪŋ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪŋ haʊs / (BrE)
A house in which rooms are rented to tenants, typically for short or long stays.
house with rooms for rent

Examples

They stayed in a small lodging house near the train station during their trip.
The lodging house offers affordable rooms for students and travelers.
After moving to the city, he found a room in a lodging house until he could rent an apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boarding house - a house where meals and lodging are provided for paying guests
