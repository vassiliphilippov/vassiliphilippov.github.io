fish knife

1. NOUN

/ fɪʃ naɪf / (AmE), / fɪʃ naɪf / (BrE)
A small table knife with a broad, spatula-shaped blade, designed for eating fish.
table knife used for eating fish

Examples

The waiter placed a fish knife and fork beside my plate for the main course.
She found a vintage silver fish knife at the antique market downtown.
It's customary to use a fish knife when serving grilled trout at formal dinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish fork - a fork designed for eating fish
