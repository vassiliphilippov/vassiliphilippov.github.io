purple sage

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl seɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl seɪdʒ / (BrE)
a silvery-leaved herb native to California, bearing clusters of purple flowers
California herb with purple flowers

Examples

The hillside was covered in blooming purple sage during the spring.
Gardeners often choose purple sage for its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
She brushed her fingers against the fragrant leaves of the purple sage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sage - an aromatic plant with greyish-green leaves used in cooking and herbal medicine
