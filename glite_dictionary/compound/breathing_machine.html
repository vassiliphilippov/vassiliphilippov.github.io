breathing machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriːðɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine that helps a person to breathe, especially when they are unable to do so unaided due to illness or injury
device that assists breathing

Examples

The patient was put on a breathing machine after the surgery to assist with her recovery.
When COVID-19 cases surged, there was a shortage of breathing machines in many hospitals.
His condition improved, and eventually he no longer needed the breathing machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ventilator - a machine that provides artificial respiration
