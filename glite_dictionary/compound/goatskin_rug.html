goatskin rug

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊtskɪn rʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtskɪn rʌɡ / (BrE)
a rug made from the hide or skin of a goat, often used as a decorative floor covering
rug made from goat skin

Examples

The luxurious goatskin rug gave the room a warm, rustic charm.
After skiing, she relaxed by the fire on a soft goatskin rug.
They bought an antique goatskin rug at the market to decorate their living room.

Synset Examples

He admired the texture of the goatskin rug.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheepskin rug - a rug made from the skin of a sheep
