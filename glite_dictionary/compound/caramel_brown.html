caramel brown

1. ADJ

/ ˈkærəmɛl braʊn, ˈkɑːrməl braʊn / (AmE), / ˈkærəmɛl braʊn / (BrE)
a moderate yellow-brown color resembling that of caramel
moderate yellow-brown color

Examples

She chose a caramel brown paint for her living room walls, creating a warm atmosphere.
The horse's coat was a striking caramel brown with a glossy sheen under the sunlight.
He wore a caramel brown jacket that matched perfectly with his autumn wardrobe.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: beige - a pale sandy yellow-brown color
  • Degree Higher: chestnut - a dark brown color
  • Synonym: caramel - a light brown color, originally from burnt sugar
