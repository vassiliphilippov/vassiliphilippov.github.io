litter problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtər ˈprɑːbləm / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
A situation in which waste or trash accumulates in an area, causing environmental or social concerns.
issue of trash accumulation

Examples

The city has been working to address its litter problem by increasing the number of trash cans in public areas.
Tourists have complained about the litter problem on the popular beaches.
Community groups often organize clean-up events to tackle the local litter problem.

Synset Examples

Garbage Grove is infamous for its litter problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: litter - waste material carelessly discarded in public places
