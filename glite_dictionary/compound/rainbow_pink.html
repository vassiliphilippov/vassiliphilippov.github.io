rainbow pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌboʊ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ pɪŋk / (BrE)
a type of flowering plant (Dianthus chinensis) with deeply toothed rose-lilac flowers, often grown as an annual
a garden flower with toothed rose-lilac petals

Examples

She planted a cluster of rainbow pink in her border to add vibrant color to the garden.
The rainbow pink blooms early in the summer, attracting butterflies with its striking petals.
Gardeners often choose rainbow pink for its delicate, frilled flowers and easy cultivation.
