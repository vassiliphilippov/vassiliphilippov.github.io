cod-liver oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑd ˌlɪvər ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒd ˌlɪvə ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil taken from the livers of cod and related fish, used especially as a dietary supplement rich in vitamins A and D
oil from cod livers, dietary supplement

Examples

Many parents give their children cod-liver oil during the winter for its health benefits.
She remembers the strong taste of cod-liver oil from her childhood.
Doctors sometimes recommend cod-liver oil to help prevent vitamin D deficiency.
