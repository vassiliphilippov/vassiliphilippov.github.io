Operation Desert Storm

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑː.pəˈreɪ.ʃən ˈdɛz.ɚt stɔrm / (AmE), / ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən ˈdez.ət stɔːm / (BrE)
the codename for the U.S.-led coalition military campaign during the 1991 Gulf War that resulted in the rapid defeat of Iraqi forces in Kuwait
1991 Gulf War coalition military campaign

Examples

Operation Desert Storm marked a turning point in modern military strategy during the Gulf War.
Thousands of troops were deployed as part of Operation Desert Storm in early 1991.
The swift victory of Operation Desert Storm was widely covered by the international media.
