biggest bleeder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɡəst ˈbliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡɪst ˈbliːdə / (BrE)
the person who is most significantly affected by losses, especially in a business, team, or financial context.
person most affected by losses

Examples

During the layoffs, John turned out to be the biggest bleeder in terms of lost revenue.
If our company continues this trend, marketing will be the biggest bleeder next quarter.
When the budget cuts hit, the department with the most staff became the biggest bleeder.

Synset Examples

In our team, she’s the biggest bleeder when it comes to losses.
