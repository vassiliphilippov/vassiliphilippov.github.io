crotch rocket

1. NOUN

/ krɑtʃ ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / krɒtʃ ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
A slang term for a fast, high-performance motorcycle designed for speed and agility.
slang for high-performance motorcycle
