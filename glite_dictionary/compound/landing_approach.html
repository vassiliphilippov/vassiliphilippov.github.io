landing approach

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændɪŋ əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
the phase of flight in which an aircraft prepares to land, typically following a specific path to the landing area
aircraft phase before landing

Examples

The pilot carefully executed the landing approach despite the strong crosswinds.
During the landing approach, passengers are advised to remain seated with their seatbelts fastened.
The control tower gave instructions for the landing approach as the runway came into view.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final approach - the last stage of an aircraft's approach before landing
