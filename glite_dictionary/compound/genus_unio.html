genus Unio

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈjuː.ni.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈjuː.ni.əʊ / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Unionidae, comprising certain freshwater mussels found in rivers and lakes
genus of freshwater mussels

Examples

The genus Unio includes several species of freshwater mussels important to aquatic ecosystems.
Researchers studied the shell characteristics unique to the genus Unio.
Conservation efforts have focused on preserving habitats that support populations of genus Unio.

Etymology

From Latin 'Unio', the genus name, and 'genus', meaning 'kind' or 'type'.
