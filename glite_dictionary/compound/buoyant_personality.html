buoyant personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɪənt pərˌsæn.əˈlɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪənt pəˌsæ.nəˈlɪt.i / (BrE)
a cheerful and lively character that is optimistic and full of energy
cheerful, lively character

Examples

Even under pressure, her buoyant personality helped keep the team's spirits high.
People are naturally drawn to his buoyant personality at social gatherings.
Despite the setbacks, Tom's buoyant personality never faltered.

Synset Examples

His buoyant personality makes him fun to be around.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful disposition - having a happy and positive attitude
  • Antonym: sullen personality - a gloomy and uncommunicative character
