caraway seed bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛr.əˌweɪ ˌsiːd brɛd / (AmE), / ˈkar.ə.weɪ ˌsiːd brɛd / (BrE)
bread made with or containing caraway seeds, which impart a distinctive aromatic flavor
bread with caraway seeds

Examples

The bakery's caraway seed bread has a robust flavor that pairs well with savory spreads.
I prefer caraway seed bread for my sandwiches because of its unique aroma.
She baked fresh caraway seed bread to serve with cheese and soup.
