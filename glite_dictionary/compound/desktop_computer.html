desktop computer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛskˌtɑp kəmˈpjutɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɛsktɒp kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
A personal computer designed to be used at a desk, typically with a separate monitor, keyboard, and mouse.
personal computer for use at a desk

Examples

She set up her new desktop computer in the home office.
Many graphic designers prefer using a desktop computer for its greater processing power.
The company's IT department upgraded all staff to desktop computers last year.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: laptop - a portable personal computer that can be used on one's lap
  • Synonym: personal computer - a general-purpose computer for individual use
