Church of Christ Scientist

1. PROPN

/ tʃɝtʃ əv kraɪst ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ əv kraɪst ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
A Protestant denomination founded by Mary Baker Eddy in 1866, emphasizing spiritual healing and the teachings of Christian Science.
Protestant denomination founded by Mary Baker Eddy

Examples

She has been a member of the Church of Christ Scientist for over twenty years.
The Church of Christ Scientist was established with a focus on healing through prayer.
In some cities, the Church of Christ Scientist buildings are architectural landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christian Science - religious movement emphasizing spiritual healing, founded by Mary Baker Eddy
