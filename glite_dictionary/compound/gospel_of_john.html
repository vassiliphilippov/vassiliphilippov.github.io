Gospel of John

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑspəl əv dʒɑn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒspəl əv dʒɒn / (BrE)
the fourth and last of the four canonical Gospels in the New Testament, recounting the life and teachings of Jesus Christ
the fourth Gospel in the New Testament
