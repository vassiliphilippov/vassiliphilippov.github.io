homemade dog biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊmˌmeɪd dɔɡ ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌmeɪd dɒɡ ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A biscuit for dogs made at home rather than bought commercially.
a biscuit for dogs made at home

Examples

She baked a fresh batch of homemade dog biscuits for her puppy's birthday.
Many pet owners prefer giving their dogs homemade dog biscuits to ensure healthy ingredients.
The recipe for homemade dog biscuits includes oats, peanut butter, and pumpkin puree.

Synset Examples

The bakery sells homemade dog biscuits for pets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog biscuit - a hard biscuit for dogs to eat as food or a treat
  • Synonym: homemade - made at home, not bought from a store
