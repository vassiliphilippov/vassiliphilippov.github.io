float-type rain gage

1. NOUN

/ floʊt taɪp reɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / fləʊt taɪp reɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A rain gauge in which a float rests on the collected water to measure the amount of rainfall.
rain gauge with float measuring collected rain

Examples

The float-type rain gage recorded higher rainfall levels after last night's storm.
Scientists rely on a float-type rain gage for accurate precipitation measurements at the field station.
Maintenance of the float-type rain gage includes checking the float for debris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rain gauge - an instrument for measuring the amount of rain
