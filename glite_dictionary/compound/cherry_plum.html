cherry plum

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri ˌplʌm / (BrE)
a small tree native to Asia, bearing edible red or yellow fruit similar to cherries or plums
a tree with edible red or yellow fruit

Examples

The cherry plum produces fruit early in the season, making it popular among gardeners.
Ripe cherry plums can be red or yellow, depending on the variety.
They planted a cherry plum tree at the edge of the orchard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - a sweet edible fruit or tree of the genus Prunus

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri plʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri plʌm / (BrE)
A small, round, edible fruit that resembles a cherry but is actually a type of plum, typically from the Prunus cerasifera tree.
small edible fruit, type of plum

Examples

The garden was full of ripe cherry plums ready to be picked.
She made delicious jam from a basket of fresh cherry plums.
A single cherry plum can fit easily in the palm of your hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plum - a sweet, fleshy, oval fruit with a smooth skin and a single stone
  • Degree Lower: cherry - a small, round, typically red fruit with a stone
