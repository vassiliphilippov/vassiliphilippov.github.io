sought after

1. ADJ

/ sɔt ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / sɔːt ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
Highly desired or in demand; much wanted by many people.
highly desired or in demand

Examples

The chef is known for creating sought after dishes that attract food lovers from across the city.
Tickets to the concert are sought after and sell out within minutes.
That artist's paintings have become highly sought after by collectors worldwide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desirable - wanted or wished for as being attractive, useful, or necessary
  • Synonym: coveted - greatly desired or envied
  • Antonym: unwanted - not or no longer desired

Etymology

Past participle 'sought' (from 'seek') + 'after'.
