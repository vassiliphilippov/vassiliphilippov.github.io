Algerian capital

1. NOUN

/ ælˈdʒɪəriən ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ælˈdʒɪəriən ˈkæpɪtəl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Algeria, located on the Mediterranean coast; also known as Algiers.
capital city of Algeria

Examples

The Algerian capital is known for its beautiful white buildings overlooking the bay.
Several international conferences have been held in the Algerian capital in recent years.
Tourists are drawn to the rich cultural heritage found in the Algerian capital.
