price bracket

1. NOUN

/ praɪs ˈbrækət / (AmE), / praɪs ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
a range or category of prices within which products or services are grouped
range or category of prices

Examples

Most cars in this price bracket come with advanced safety features.
We are looking for a hotel in a lower price bracket for the trip.
The new phone falls into a higher price bracket than its predecessor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: price range - a range of prices within certain limits
