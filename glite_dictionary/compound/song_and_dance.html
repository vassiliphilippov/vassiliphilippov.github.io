song and dance

1. NOUN

/ sɔŋ ənd dæns / (AmE), / sɒŋ ənd dɑːns / (BrE)
a theatrical performance that features both singing and dancing
theatrical act with singing and dancing

Examples

The musical included a spectacular song and dance in the second act.
Her Broadway debut featured a solo song and dance performance.
They attended a song and dance show at the historic theater.
