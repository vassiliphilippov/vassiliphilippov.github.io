oven thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌvən θərˈmɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈʌv.ən θəˈmɒm.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
a thermometer used to measure the temperature inside an oven
thermometer for measuring oven temperature

Examples

The oven thermometer showed that the temperature was higher than the dial indicated.
She bought an oven thermometer to ensure her cakes baked evenly.
Without an oven thermometer, it's hard to know if your oven is heating correctly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermometer - an instrument for measuring temperature
