male berry

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪl ˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl ˌbɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous, much-branched shrub with dense, downy clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers.
deciduous shrub with white bell-shaped flowers

Examples

The male berry blooms in early summer, adding bright spots of white to the garden.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of male berry leaves in traditional remedies.
I planted a male berry in the corner of my yard to attract pollinators.
