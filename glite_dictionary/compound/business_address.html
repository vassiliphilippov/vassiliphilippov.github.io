business address

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
the address where a business is physically located or officially registered
address where a business is located

Examples

Please send all invoices to our business address listed at the bottom of the letterhead.
He updated his business address after moving the company to a new office.
The business address must be provided when registering the company with the authorities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailing address - the address to which mail is sent
