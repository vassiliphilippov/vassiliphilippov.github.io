refrigerator cookie

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtər ˈkʊki / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtə ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A cookie made from dough that is shaped into a roll, chilled until firm, and then sliced and baked.
cookie made from chilled roll of dough

Examples

My grandmother always kept a batch of refrigerator cookies in the freezer for unexpected guests.
You just slice the refrigerator cookie dough and bake it when you want fresh cookies.
The recipe calls for chilling the refrigerator cookie dough overnight before slicing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: icebox cookie - a cookie made from chilled dough, synonym for refrigerator cookie
