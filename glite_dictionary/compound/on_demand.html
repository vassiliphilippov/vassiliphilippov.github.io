on demand

1. ADV

/ ɑn dɪˈmænd / (AmE), / ɒn dɪˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
available for use or access at any time, whenever requested or needed
available whenever needed

Examples

The new movie can be watched on demand from your smart TV.
Many cloud services allow you to scale resources on demand.
With online courses, you can learn new skills on demand at your own pace.

Synset Examples

They provide support on demand through their app.
The content is streamed on demand at any time.
The service is available on demand for all users.
