pay as you earn

1. NOUN

/ peɪ əz ju jɝn / (AmE), / peɪ əz juː jɜːn / (BrE)
the British system of collecting income tax directly from employees' salaries by deduction before the wages are paid
British income tax withholding system
british

Examples

In the UK, most employees have their taxes deducted through pay as you earn.
The pay as you earn system simplifies the tax process for both employers and workers.
He checked his pay as you earn deductions on his monthly payslip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: withholding tax - tax deducted at source from income
