good price

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd praɪs / (AmE), / ɡʊd praɪs / (BrE)
an amount of money that is favorable or advantageous, especially in buying or selling
favorable or advantageous amount of money

Examples

She bought the antique chair at a good price during the clearance sale.
The store is known for offering electronics at a good price.
If you wait for the right moment, you can get airline tickets at a good price.

Synset Examples

He managed to sell his old car for a good price.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bargain - something bought or offered for sale at a price advantageous to the buyer
