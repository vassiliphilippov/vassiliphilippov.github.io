buybull mentality

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ bʊl mənˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ bʊl mɛnˈtaləti / (BrE)
an optimistic attitude among investors, characterized by a strong belief that markets will continue to rise, leading to aggressive buying
overly optimistic investor attitude
informal

Examples

The buybull mentality among tech investors fueled another round of record highs.
Caution is needed when a buybull mentality sweeps through the market, as risks are sometimes overlooked.
After a year of steady gains, his buybull mentality became even more pronounced.

Synset Examples

His buybull mentality led to risky investment choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bull market - a market characterized by rising prices
  • Antonym: bearish mentality - a pessimistic belief in falling markets

Etymology

Blend of 'buy' and 'bull' (from 'bull market', meaning a rising market), plus 'mentality'.
