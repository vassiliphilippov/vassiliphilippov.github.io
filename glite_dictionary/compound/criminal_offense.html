criminal offense

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns / (BrE)
An act that is forbidden by law and punishable as a crime.
an act punishable as a crime
legal

Examples

Committing a criminal offense can result in imprisonment or heavy fines.
He was charged with a criminal offense for his actions during the protest.
Forging documents is considered a criminal offense in many countries.

Synset Examples

a long record of crimes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crime - An act forbidden by law or harmful to society
  • Antonym: civil offense - An act violating civil law, not criminal law
