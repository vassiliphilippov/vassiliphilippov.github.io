polo stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊloʊ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ stɪk / (BrE)
a long-handled mallet used to strike the ball in the game of polo
mallet used in polo

Examples

Each player swung their polo stick with impressive skill during the match.
He forgot to bring his polo stick, so he had to borrow one from the club.
The polo stick must be both strong and flexible to withstand the impact of the game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallet - a hammer with a large, usually wooden head used for striking things without damaging them
