homework problem

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm.wɝːk ˈprɑː.bləm / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.wɜːk ˈprɒb.ləm / (BrE)
a problem or exercise assigned to students to be completed outside regular classroom hours, typically as part of their homework
problem assigned as homework

Examples

Each night, the teacher assigned a new homework problem for the class to solve.
I struggled with the last homework problem because it was much harder than the others.
The math homework problem required us to use two different formulas to find the answer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignment - a task or piece of work assigned to someone as part of a job or course of study
  • Synonym: exercise - a task or activity designed to practice or test a skill
