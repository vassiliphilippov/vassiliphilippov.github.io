off-colour

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔf ˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈɒf ˌkʌlə / (BrE)
(of a joke or remark) slightly indecent or in poor taste; verging on the inappropriate.
slightly indecent or inappropriate

Examples

That comedian is known for making off-colour jokes that sometimes shock the audience.
Her off-colour comment at dinner made everyone uncomfortable.
The movie contains some off-colour humor that may not be suitable for children.

Synset Examples

an indelicate remark
an off-color joke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indelicate - lacking decorum; somewhat indecent, especially in speech
  • Antonym: clean - not offensive or indecent in content (used of jokes or remarks)
