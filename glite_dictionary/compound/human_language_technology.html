human language technology

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ tɛkˌnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ tɛkˌnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The field of information science concerned with the processing and analysis of natural language by computers.
processing natural language by computers

Examples

Recent advances in human language technology have made voice-activated assistants more accurate and responsive.
The conference included sessions on the ethical implications of human language technology.
Companies are investing heavily in human language technology to improve communication with customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: computational linguistics - scientific study of language from a computational perspective
