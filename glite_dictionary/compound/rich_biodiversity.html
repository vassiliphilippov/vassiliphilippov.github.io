rich biodiversity

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ ˌbaɪ.oʊ.daɪˈvɝː.sə.ti / (AmE), / rɪtʃ ˌbaɪ.əʊ.daɪˈvɜː.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
a high level of variety in the types of plants, animals, and other organisms in a particular area
high variety of life in a region

Examples

The Amazon rainforest is famous for its rich biodiversity and unique species.
Efforts are being made to protect the rich biodiversity of coral reefs worldwide.
Regions with rich biodiversity attract scientists and tourists alike.

Synset Examples

Guinea is known for its rich biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biodiversity - the variety of plant and animal life in a particular habitat
