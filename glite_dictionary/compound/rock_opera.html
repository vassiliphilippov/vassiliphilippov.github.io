rock opera

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈɑpɚə / (AmE), / rɒk ˈɒpərə / (BrE)
A work of musical theatre that combines the structure or dramatic elements of opera with the musical style of rock music.
opera featuring rock music

Examples

The band's latest album is a rock opera that tells a story from beginning to end.
I saw a live performance of a rock opera last night, and it was fantastic.
Famous musicians have experimented with the rock opera format since the late 1960s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opera - a theatrical work combining drama, music, song, and sometimes dance
