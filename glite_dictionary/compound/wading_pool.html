wading pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪdɪŋ puːl / (AmE), / ˈweɪdɪŋ puːl / (BrE)
a shallow pool of water, especially for children to play in
shallow pool for children

Examples

The children splashed happily in the wading pool on a hot summer day.
Parents should supervise their kids when they are playing in the wading pool.
After setting up the wading pool in the backyard, Sarah invited her friends over to cool off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paddling pool - a shallow pool for children, chiefly British term
