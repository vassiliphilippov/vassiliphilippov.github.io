sport fish

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt fɪʃ / (AmE), / spɔːt fɪʃ / (BrE)
a fish prized for recreational fishing, typically because it puts up a fight when caught
fish sought after for sport fishing

Examples

Anglers travel from all over the world to catch sport fish like marlin and tuna.
Conservation efforts often focus on protecting native sport fish populations in local lakes.
Some regions restrict fishing methods to ensure that sport fish are not overharvested.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game fish - fish caught for sport rather than food
