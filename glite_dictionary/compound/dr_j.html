Dr. J

1. PROPN

/ ˌdɒktɚ ˈdʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɒktə ˈdʒeɪ / (BrE)
the nickname of Julius Erving, a celebrated American basketball forward known for his exceptional skill and style
nickname of basketball player Julius Erving
american

Examples

Dr. J revolutionized the way basketball was played with his acrobatic moves and slam dunks.
Many aspiring players look up to Dr. J as a role model for his style and sportsmanship.
In the 1980s, fans crowded arenas just to catch a glimpse of Dr. J in action.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Julius Erving - famous American basketball player known as Dr. J

Etymology

Nickname formed from 'Doctor' and the initial 'J' of Julius.
