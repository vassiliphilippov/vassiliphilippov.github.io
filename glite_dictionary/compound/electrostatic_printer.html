electrostatic printer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
a printer that forms images on paper by using an electric charge to transfer toner or ink
printer using electric charge for imaging

Examples

The office upgraded to an electrostatic printer for faster and clearer printouts.
An electrostatic printer can produce high-quality images using very fine toner particles.
Technicians recommend cleaning the electrostatic printer regularly to maintain print quality.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: laser printer - a printer that uses a laser beam and electrostatic process to print images

Etymology

From electrostatic (relating to stationary electric charges) + printer.
