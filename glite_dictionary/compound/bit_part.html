bit part

1. NOUN

/ bɪt pɑrt / (AmE), / bɪt pɑːt / (BrE)
a very small acting role with few lines in a play, film, or television show
small acting role

Examples

After years of only landing bit parts, she finally got her big break as a lead actress.
He was grateful even for a bit part in the popular television series.
Many famous actors started their careers with small bit parts before becoming stars.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leading role - the main role in a play, film, or television show
  • Synonym: cameo - a small but special part, often played by a well-known person
