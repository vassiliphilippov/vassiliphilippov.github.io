mix of peaches and cream

1. NOUN

/ mɪks əv pitʃɪz ənd kriːm / (AmE), / mɪks əv piːtʃɪz ənd kriːm / (BrE)
a dish or dessert made by combining peaches and cream, often sweetened; typically served chilled
peaches and cream dessert

Examples

For dessert, we enjoyed a mix of peaches and cream served in glass bowls.
She prepared a mix of peaches and cream for the summer picnic.
Nothing says summer like a fresh mix of peaches and cream on a hot afternoon.

Synset Examples

The dessert was a delightful mix of peaches and cream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peaches and cream - a dish of sliced peaches with cream
