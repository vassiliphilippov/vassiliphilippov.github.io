maximum and minimum thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæksɪməm ənd ˈmɪnɪməm θərˈmɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪməm ənd ˈmɪnɪməm θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a thermometer that records the highest and lowest temperatures reached over a period of time, often used in meteorology
device recording high and low temperatures

Examples

The maximum and minimum thermometer in the greenhouse recorded a high of 30°C and a low of 10°C overnight.
Farmers rely on a maximum and minimum thermometer to monitor temperature changes in their fields.
Our weather station includes a maximum and minimum thermometer for tracking daily temperature extremes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermometer - an instrument for measuring temperature
