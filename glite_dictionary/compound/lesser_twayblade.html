lesser twayblade

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˈtweɪˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈtweɪbleɪd / (BrE)
a small orchid with two triangular leaves and a short, loose cluster of green to reddish flowers, found in Europe, Asia, North America, and Greenland
a small orchid with two leaves and greenish or reddish flowers

Examples

We spotted a rare lesser twayblade growing in the damp woodland soil.
The lesser twayblade is often overlooked due to its small size and greenish flowers.
Botanists recorded the presence of lesser twayblades across the forest floor in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twayblade - a type of orchid with two leaves
