hotel bill

1. NOUN

/ hoʊˈtɛl bɪl / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl bɪl / (BrE)
a statement listing charges for a guest's stay at a hotel, including room rates and any additional services used
statement of hotel charges

Examples

She examined the hotel bill carefully to make sure there were no extra charges.
At checkout, the receptionist handed me my hotel bill for the three-night stay.
Please keep your hotel bill if you need to claim expenses from your company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bill - a statement of charges for goods or services
