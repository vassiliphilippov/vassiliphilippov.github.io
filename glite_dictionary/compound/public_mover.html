public mover

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˈmuːvər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈmuːvə / (BrE)
a company that transports household or business possessions from one location to another, typically for a fee
company that transports possessions between sites

Examples

We hired a public mover to handle our relocation across town.
The public mover arrived early to begin packing up the office furniture.
Choosing a reliable public mover made our move much less stressful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moving company - a company that transports goods for customers between locations
