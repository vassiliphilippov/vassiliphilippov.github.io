family Mylodontidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌmaɪloʊˈdɒntɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌmaɪlɒˈdɪntɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of extinct South American edentate mammals closely related to ground sloths
family of extinct South American edentates

Examples

New fossils have shed light on the evolutionary history of the family Mylodontidae.
The family Mylodontidae included some of the largest prehistoric ground sloths ever discovered.
Scientists continue to debate the diet and habits of the extinct family Mylodontidae.

Etymology

Mylodontidae from modern Latin, based on genus Mylodon + -idae (zoological family suffix)
