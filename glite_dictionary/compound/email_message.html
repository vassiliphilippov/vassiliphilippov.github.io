email message

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːˌmeɪl ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈiːˌmeɪl ˈmɛsɪdʒ / (BrE)
a message that is sent electronically using the email system over the internet
message sent via email system

Examples

Please let me know if you receive my email message by tomorrow morning.
She deleted the suspicious email message without opening it.
It is important to proofread your email message for spelling errors before sending.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: email - system for sending electronic messages over the internet
  • Degree Lower: message - a communicated piece of information
