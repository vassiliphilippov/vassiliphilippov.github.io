California coffee

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfɔrnjə ˈkɔfi / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəˈnɪə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub of the western United States that produces small red or black fruits
evergreen shrub native to the western US

Examples

The hiker paused to admire a California coffee bush in bloom along the trail.
Birds are often attracted to the bright fruits of the California coffee.
Gardeners sometimes plant California coffee for its drought tolerance and attractive berries.
