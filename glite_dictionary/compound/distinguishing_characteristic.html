distinguishing characteristic

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ ˌkɛrəktəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪŋ ˌkærɪktəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
a quality or feature that makes someone or something different from others
unique identifying feature

Examples

The bird's red crest is its most obvious distinguishing characteristic.
One distinguishing characteristic of this painting style is its use of vibrant colors.
Her sense of humor became a distinguishing characteristic among her friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trait - a distinguishing quality or characteristic
  • Synonym: feature - an important or notable part of something
