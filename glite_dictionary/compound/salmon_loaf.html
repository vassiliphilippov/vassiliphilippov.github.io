salmon loaf

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæmən ˌloʊf / (AmE), / ˈsæmən ləʊf / (BrE)
A baked dish made with flaked salmon, often combined with breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings, shaped into a loaf.
baked dish of flaked salmon shaped into a loaf

Examples

My grandmother makes a delicious salmon loaf every holiday season.
The leftover salmon loaf was perfect for sandwiches the next day.
You can serve salmon loaf with a side of steamed vegetables for a complete meal.
