good looks

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd lʊks / (AmE), / ɡʊd lʊks / (BrE)
attractive and pleasing physical appearance, especially of facial features
attractive physical appearance

Examples

His good looks made him very popular in school.
She admired his charm and good looks.
Despite his good looks, he always remained humble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handsomeness - the quality of being good-looking, especially in men
  • Synonym: beauty - a combination of qualities that pleases the senses, especially sight
  • Antonym: plainness - lack of special physical attractiveness
