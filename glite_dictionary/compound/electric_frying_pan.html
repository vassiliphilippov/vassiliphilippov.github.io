electric frying pan

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈfraɪɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈfraɪɪŋ pæn / (BrE)
A frying pan with its own built-in electric heating element, used for cooking food without the need for a stovetop.
frying pan with electric heating

Examples

We cooked pancakes on the electric frying pan during our camping trip.
She prefers using an electric frying pan because it maintains a steady temperature.
His old electric frying pan still works after twenty years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillet - a frying pan, especially in American English
