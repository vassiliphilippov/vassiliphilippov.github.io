advanced technology

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvænst tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːnst tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
Modern and highly developed tools, equipment, or systems, especially those using the latest scientific and engineering advances.
modern, sophisticated tools or systems

Examples

The new hospital is equipped with advanced technology to provide better patient care.
Many jobs now require workers to be familiar with advanced technology and automated processes.
Space exploration relies on advanced technology to reach distant planets and collect data.

Synset Examples

The lab was filled with advanced technology and eager researchers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technology - application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes
