jointer plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɔɪntər pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntə pleɪn / (BrE)
a long carpenter's plane used to smooth and straighten the edges of boards so they fit together accurately
long tool for straightening board edges

Examples

The carpenter used a jointer plane to create a perfectly straight edge on the board.
A well-tuned jointer plane makes joining pieces of wood much easier and more accurate.
He spent several minutes adjusting the blade before working with his jointer plane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plane - a carpenter's tool for smoothing or shaping wood

Etymology

From 'jointer' (something that joins) + 'plane' (carpenter's tool for shaping wood).
