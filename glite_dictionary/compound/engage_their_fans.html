to engage their fans

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ ðɛr fænz / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ ðeə fænz / (BrE)
to interact with or connect meaningfully with one's supporters, especially through communication or activities
interact or connect with supporters

Examples

Many celebrities use live streams to engage their fans on various social media platforms.
The musician tried to engage their fans by responding to comments during the concert.
Posting behind-the-scenes photos is one way to engage their fans and build loyalty.
