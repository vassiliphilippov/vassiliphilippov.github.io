brain tumour

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈtumɚ / (AmE), / breɪn ˈtjuːmə / (BrE)
An abnormal growth of tissue forming a mass in the brain, which can be benign or malignant.
abnormal growth in the brain
medical british

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with a brain tumour after experiencing severe headaches and vision problems.
Doctors performed surgery to remove the brain tumour and reduce swelling.
She has been undergoing treatment for a brain tumour since last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain tumor - abnormal growth in the brain (US spelling)
