God squad

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑd skwɑd / (AmE), / ɡɒd skwɒd / (BrE)
an informal group of people organized to carry out religious or charitable activities
informal group for religious or charitable work
informal

Examples

The God squad volunteers regularly at the local food bank.
He started a God squad at his college to support community outreach projects.
Some students jokingly referred to their prayer group as the God squad.

Synset Examples

The God squad organized a charity run this weekend.
