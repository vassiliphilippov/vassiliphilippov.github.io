too-generous

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈdʒɛnərəs / (AmE), / tuː ˈdʒɛnərəs / (BrE)
Giving more than is necessary or appropriate in generosity; excessively generous.
excessively generous

Examples

Her too-generous donations often left her with little money for herself.
Some people found his too-generous spirit overwhelming at times.
The company’s too-generous return policy began to hurt its profits.
