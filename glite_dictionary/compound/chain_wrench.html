chain wrench

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪn rɛntʃ / (AmE), / tʃeɪn rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a hand tool with a chain loop used for gripping and turning pipes or other cylindrical objects
hand tool with a chain for gripping pipes

Examples

You need a chain wrench to loosen that stubborn pipe fitting.
The mechanic reached for a chain wrench to grip the large metal valve.
Always position the chain wrench securely before applying force to avoid slippage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipe wrench - tool for gripping and turning pipes
