order Haplosporidia

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər hæpˌloʊspəˈrɪdiə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə hæpˌləʊspəˈrɪdiə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of single-celled parasites within the subclass Acnidosporidia.
order of single-celled parasites

Examples

Several important pathogens of marine invertebrates belong to the order Haplosporidia.
Researchers are studying the life cycle of organisms in the order Haplosporidia.
The order Haplosporidia includes parasites that affect shellfish populations.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Haplosporidia,' combining Greek 'haplo-' (single) + 'spora' (spore).
