stash of booze

1. NOUN

/ stæʃ əv buːz / (AmE), / stæʃ əv buːz / (BrE)
a hidden supply of alcoholic drinks, kept secretly for personal use or later consumption
hidden supply of alcoholic drinks
informal

Examples

He keeps a stash of booze in his garage for special occasions.
They found an old stash of booze hidden behind the basement wall.
Whenever friends visit, her secret stash of booze comes out.

Synset Examples

He always keeps a stash of booze in his fridge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liquor stash - hidden supply of liquor
  • Synonym: hoard of alcohol - hidden accumulation of alcoholic beverages
