unfortunate person

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfɔɹtʃənət ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːtʃənət ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who suffers from bad luck or misfortune
a person suffering from misfortune

Examples

The charity provides food and shelter to every unfortunate person who comes through their doors.
She felt sympathy for the unfortunate person caught in the accident on the news.
Sometimes, all it takes is one bad decision to become an unfortunate person facing hardship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victim - a person harmed, injured, or killed as a result of an event or action
