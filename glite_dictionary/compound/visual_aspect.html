visual aspect

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl ˈæspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒu.əl ˈæspɛkt / (BrE)
the outward or visible appearance of a person or thing; what can be seen visually
outward or visible appearance

Examples

The architect focused on the visual aspect of the building to make it more appealing.
Social media often emphasizes the visual aspect of personal branding.
Lighting plays a significant role in enhancing the visual aspect of a performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appearance - the way someone or something looks
  • Synonym: aspect - a particular part or feature of something, especially as seen or considered
