land cress

1. NOUN

/ lænd krɛs / (AmE), / lænd krɛs / (BrE)
a leafy plant native to southwestern Europe and cultivated in various regions, whose leaves are used as a salad green or garnish
edible leafy plant used in salads

Examples

She added fresh land cress to the salad for extra flavor.
The garden's land cress has a mildly peppery taste similar to watercress.
In some regions, land cress is preferred over watercress for its hardiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watercress - an edible aquatic plant with pungent leaves
