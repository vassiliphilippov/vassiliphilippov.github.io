library catalogue

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪˌbrɛri ˈkætəlɔg / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri ˈkætəlɒg / (BrE)
a complete list of all the resources available in a library, typically organized systematically to aid users in finding materials
a list of all resources in a library

Examples

The new online system makes it easier to search the library catalogue for books and articles.
Please check the library catalogue to see if the novel is available.
She spent hours browsing the library catalogue for research material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalogue - a complete list of items, often systematically arranged
