fat finger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæt ˌfɪŋɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæt ˌfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
a clumsy mistake made by pressing the wrong button or key, often due to hitting more than one by accident
clumsy error pressing wrong button or key

Examples

A single fat finger caused millions to be lost when the wrong order was placed.
He blamed the error on a fat finger during a rushed call.
I've made more than one fat finger mistake when texting quickly.

Synset Examples

His fat finger pressed the wrong button.
I hit send with my fat finger by mistake.
Be careful not to fat-finger the settings on your phone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typo - a typographical error
