Venus' slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnəs ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈviːnəs ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
Any of various orchids of the genus Paphiopedilum, having slender flower stalks bearing one to several waxy flowers with pouch-like lips.
orchid of the genus Paphiopedilum

Examples

The Venus' slipper is prized among orchid enthusiasts for its unusual pouch-shaped flower.
Gardeners often admire the striking colors and elegant form of the Venus' slipper.
A rare Venus' slipper was on display at the botanical garden's annual orchid show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady's slipper - any orchid of the genus Cypripedium or related genera with a distinctive pouch-like labellum

Etymology

Named after Venus, the Roman goddess of love, and the slipper-like shape of the flower's labellum.
