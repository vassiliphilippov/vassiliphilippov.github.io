African tulip

1. NOUN

/ ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈtu.lɪp / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən ˈtjuː.lɪp / (BrE)
a tropical African tree with bright green evergreen leaves and clusters of showy orange-red or sometimes violet-blue flowers
a tropical African flowering tree

Examples

The African tulip is often grown as an ornamental tree in tropical gardens.
Clusters of African tulip flowers attract a variety of birds and insects.
An avenue lined with African tulip trees was striking with its brilliant blooms.
