Pine Tree State

1. PROPN

/ ˌpaɪn ˈtri ˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˌpaɪn ˈtriː ˌsteɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for the US state of Maine, located in the northeastern region of the country
nickname for the US state of Maine

Examples

Maine is popularly known as the Pine Tree State because of its vast forests.
We spent our summer vacation traveling through the Pine Tree State.
The Pine Tree State is famous for its beautiful coastlines and national parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Maine - a state in the northeastern United States; the Pine Tree State

Etymology

Refers to Maine's extensive pine forests and its official state tree, the Eastern White Pine.
