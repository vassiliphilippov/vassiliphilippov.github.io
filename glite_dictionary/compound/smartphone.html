smartphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɑrtˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtˌfəʊn / (BrE)
A mobile phone that combines advanced computing capability with connectivity, allowing installation of applications and access to the internet.
advanced mobile phone with computing features

Examples

She upgraded to a smartphone so she could check her emails on the go.
Most smartphones now have excellent cameras and powerful processors.
He uses his smartphone to navigate while driving in unfamiliar cities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: feature phone - a mobile phone with basic functions, lacking advanced computing features

Etymology

Formed from 'smart' (suggesting intelligence or advanced features) and 'phone' (short for telephone).
