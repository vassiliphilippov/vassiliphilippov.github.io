Equality State

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈkwɑlɪti steɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɒlɪti steɪt / (BrE)
a nickname for Wyoming, a state in the western United States known for its mountainous regions and the Great Plains
nickname for Wyoming, U.S. state

Examples

Wyoming is often called the Equality State due to its pioneering role in women's suffrage.
Many tourists visit the Equality State to explore Yellowstone National Park.
The nickname 'Equality State' reflects Wyoming's history of promoting equal rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wyoming - a state in the western United States

Etymology

From Wyoming's early adoption of women's suffrage, symbolizing equality.
