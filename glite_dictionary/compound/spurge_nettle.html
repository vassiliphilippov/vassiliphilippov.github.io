spurge nettle

1. NOUN

/ spɜrdʒ ˈnɛtəl / (AmE), / spɜːdʒ ˈnɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a tropical American herb with stinging hairs that can cause skin irritation
tropical stinging herb

Examples

The leaves of the spurge nettle can cause a painful rash if touched.
Farmers often warn children to stay away from spurge nettle growing near the fields.
After brushing against a spurge nettle, she experienced a tingling sensation on her skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nettle - herbaceous plant with stinging hairs
