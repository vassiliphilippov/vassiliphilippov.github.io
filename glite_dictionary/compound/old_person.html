old person

1. NOUN

/ oʊld ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / əʊld ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is advanced in age; an elderly individual
an elderly person

Examples

The community center offers many activities for the old person living nearby.
It can be challenging for an old person to navigate public transportation alone.
She volunteers her time to help take care of an old person who lives on her street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elderly - a polite or formal word for an old person
  • Antonym: young person - a person who is not yet old; youth
