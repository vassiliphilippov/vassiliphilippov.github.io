Bahasa Melayu

1. NOUN

/ bɑːˈhɑː.sə məˈluː.juː / (AmE), / bɑːˈhɑː.sə məˈluː.juː / (BrE)
the Malay language, especially as spoken in Malaysia and neighboring countries
the Malay language in Malaysia and nearby regions

Examples

She is fluent in Bahasa Melayu as well as English.
The official documents were translated into Bahasa Melayu for the conference.
Learning Bahasa Melayu helped him connect with local communities in Malaysia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Malay - the Austronesian language of the Malay people

Etymology

From Malay 'bahasa' (language) + 'Melayu' (Malay).
