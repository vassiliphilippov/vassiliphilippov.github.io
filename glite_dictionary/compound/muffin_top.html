muffin top

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌfɪn tɑp / (AmE), / ˈmʌfɪn tɒp / (BrE)
a layer of fat that spills over the top of the waistband of tight trousers or skirts, resembling the shape of a muffin top
fat spilling over waistband
informal

Examples

He wore a tight shirt that highlighted his muffin top.
The jeans were too small, giving her a muffin top.
She decided to wear looser clothes to hide her muffin top.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spare tire - a ring of excess fat around a person's waist

Etymology

From the resemblance to the rounded top of a muffin that spills over the edge of a muffin case.
