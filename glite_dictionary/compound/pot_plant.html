pot plant

1. NOUN

/ pɑt plænt / (AmE), / pɒt plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant grown in a flowerpot, especially one kept indoors as a decoration.
plant grown in a pot indoors
british

Examples

She placed a beautiful pot plant on the windowsill to brighten up the room.
Caring for a pot plant requires regular watering and occasional feeding.
Many offices use a pot plant to create a more inviting atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houseplant - a plant grown indoors, usually in a pot
