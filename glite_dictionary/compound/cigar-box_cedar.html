cigar-box cedar

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr bɑks ˈsiːdɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː bɒks ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
the fragrant, light wood commonly used for making cigar boxes, usually obtained from certain species of cedar or related trees
fragrant wood used for cigar boxes

Examples

The humidifier in the cigar-box cedar helps preserve the quality of the cigars.
Many luxury cigar boxes are made from cigar-box cedar for its distinctive aroma.
Collectors often seek boxes made of cigar-box cedar because it protects cigars from pests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cedar - a type of fragrant, durable wood from the cedar tree
