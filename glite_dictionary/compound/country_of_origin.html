country of origin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri əv ˈɔrɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri əv ˈɒrɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
the country where a person was born or from which a product or thing comes
the country where someone or something comes from

Examples

Immigrants are sometimes asked to declare their country of origin on official forms.
All imported goods must be labeled with their country of origin.
She has visited her parents' country of origin several times.
