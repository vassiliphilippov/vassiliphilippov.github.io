purple emperor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈɛmpɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈɛmpərər / (BrE)
a large European butterfly, the male of which has wings with a purple sheen
large European butterfly with purple-shaded wings

Examples

The sighting of a purple emperor in the woods thrilled the butterfly enthusiasts.
Male purple emperors are known for their strikingly iridescent wings.
Children tried to photograph the elusive purple emperor as it basked in the sunlight.
