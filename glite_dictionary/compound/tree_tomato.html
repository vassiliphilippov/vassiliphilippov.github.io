tree tomato

1. NOUN

/ tri təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / tri təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
A South American shrub or small tree with edible egg-shaped reddish-brown fruit that resembles a tomato in flavor and appearance.
South American fruit-bearing shrub or its fruit

Examples

The farmers harvested tree tomatoes from the slope early in the morning.
I tasted a tree tomato for the first time on my trip to Ecuador.
Tree tomato juice is popular in some Andean countries.
