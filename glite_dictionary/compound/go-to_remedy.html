go-to remedy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊ tu ˈrɛmədi / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ tuː ˈrɛmədi / (BrE)
A preferred or reliable method or solution for dealing with a particular problem or situation.
preferred or reliable solution

Examples

Chicken soup is my go-to remedy when I have a cold.
Meditation has become her go-to remedy for managing stress.
For headaches, his go-to remedy is a cup of strong coffee.

Synset Examples

The local street pharmacist knew everyone’s go-to remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remedy - a medicine or treatment for a disease or injury; a solution to a problem
