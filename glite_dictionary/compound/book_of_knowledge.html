book of knowledge

1. NOUN

/ bʊk əv ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a general reference book or elementary encyclopedia that provides basic information on a wide range of subjects
elementary encyclopedia; general reference book

Examples

As a child, Sarah would spend hours reading the book of knowledge in her school library.
The old book of knowledge on the shelf contains articles about history, science, and art.
Parents often buy a book of knowledge to support their children's curiosity about the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encyclopedia - a book or set of books giving information about many subjects
