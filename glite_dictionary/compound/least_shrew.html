least shrew

1. NOUN

/ list ʃru / (AmE), / liːst ʃruː / (BrE)
a very small brown shrew (Cryptotis parva) found in grassy areas of the eastern United States
a tiny brown shrew of eastern US

Examples

The least shrew is notable for its tiny size and rapid movements in grassy fields.
Researchers tracked the population of the least shrew throughout several counties in the eastern United States.
Predators such as owls and snakes often hunt the least shrew at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrew - a small, mouse-like insectivorous mammal
