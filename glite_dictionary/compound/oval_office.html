Oval Office

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.vəl ˈɑː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəl ˈɒ.fɪs / (BrE)
the official office of the President of the United States, located in the White House
office of the U.S. President in the White House

Examples

The president addressed the nation from the Oval Office last night.
Tourists visiting the White House often hope to catch a glimpse of the Oval Office.
Major policy decisions are frequently made within the Oval Office.

Etymology

Named for the distinctive oval shape of the room in the White House.
