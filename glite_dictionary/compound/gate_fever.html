gate fever

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪt ˈfiːvər / (AmE), / ɡeɪt ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
a feeling of anxiety or excitement experienced just before boarding a flight
anxiety or excitement before a flight

Examples

She tried to calm her gate fever while waiting at the departure gate.
Every time he travels, his gate fever makes him double-check his boarding pass.
Long security lines usually heighten my gate fever before a flight.

Synset Examples

The long lines at security triggered her gate fever.
His gate fever made him anxious to board the plane.
She felt gate fever as she waited for her flight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerves - nervousness or anxiety, especially before an important event
  • Synonym: excitement - a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness
