carrot juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæɹət dʒus / (AmE), / ˈkærət dʒuːs / (BrE)
juice obtained by extracting liquid from carrots, often consumed as a beverage, usually freshly squeezed.
juice made from carrots

Examples

Many people drink carrot juice for its health benefits.
She made a fresh glass of carrot juice every morning.
The café serves organic carrot juice along with other vegetable drinks.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: vegetable juice - juice extracted from vegetables, often used as a beverage or ingredient
  • Synonym: orange juice - juice extracted from oranges, commonly consumed as a beverage
