backpack vacuum cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌpæk ˈvæk.juːm ˈkliː.nər / (AmE), / ˈbæk.pæk ˈvæk.juːm ˈkliː.nə / (BrE)
a portable vacuum cleaner designed to be carried on the user's back using straps, allowing greater mobility and ease during cleaning, especially in commercial settings.
vacuum cleaner worn on the back

Examples

The janitor preferred using a backpack vacuum cleaner for its convenience in tight hallways.
Many custodians find that a backpack vacuum cleaner reduces fatigue during long shifts.
After switching to a backpack vacuum cleaner, the cleaning crew was able to work more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canister vacuum cleaner - a vacuum cleaner with a separate canister unit, typically pulled along the floor
