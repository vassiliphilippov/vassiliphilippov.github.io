Paiwanic language

1. NOUN

/ paɪˈwɑːnɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / paɪˈwɑːnɪk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Formosan language spoken by the Paiwan people of Taiwan
Formosan language of the Paiwan in Taiwan

Examples

The Paiwanic language is spoken by the Paiwan people in southern Taiwan.
Scholars are working to document and preserve the Paiwanic language for future generations.
Many traditional songs are performed in the Paiwanic language during cultural festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Paiwan - a member of an indigenous people of Taiwan
  • Synonym: Formosan language - any of the languages spoken by the indigenous peoples of Taiwan

Etymology

From 'Paiwan', the indigenous group in Taiwan, plus '-ic', forming an adjective, and 'language'.
