star vehicle

1. NOUN

/ stɑr ˈviː.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / stɑː ˈviː.ɪ.kl̩ / (BrE)
a film, play, or television show created primarily to display the talents of a particular star actor or celebrity
entertainment created to showcase a star

Examples

The director was searching for a star vehicle to feature the studio's latest leading actress.
Critics dismissed the movie as just another star vehicle without much substance.
Studio executives hoped the musical would become a major star vehicle for its charismatic lead.

Synset Examples

She landed a role in a star vehicle that was highly anticipated.
The new release is a star vehicle for an up-and-coming actor.
The film was a star vehicle designed to showcase his talents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vehicle - a medium for the expression or achievement of something; in entertainment, a work for showcasing talent
