stamping machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæmpɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈstæmpɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A power-driven machine used for shaping or marking materials by pressing or stamping them, typically used in manufacturing.
power tool used for stamping

Examples

The new factory installed a stamping machine to increase production speed.
Each stamping machine must be carefully calibrated to ensure precision.
The operator checked the stamping machine for any signs of wear before starting his shift.

Synset Examples

a metal stamper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stamper - a machine or tool for stamping
