Kauai Island

1. PROPN

/ kaʊˈaɪ ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / kaʊˈaɪ ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
an island of Hawaii located northwest of Oahu, known for its lush landscapes and beaches
Hawaiian island northwest of Oahu

Examples

We spent our honeymoon exploring the beautiful beaches of Kauai Island.
Kauai Island is often called the 'Garden Island' due to its lush vegetation.
Hikers travel to Kauai Island for the famous Na Pali Coast trails.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Oahu - an island of Hawaii located southeast of Kauai

Etymology

From Hawaiian Kauaʻi, the indigenous name of the island.
