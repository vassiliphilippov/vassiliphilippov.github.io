applied anatomy

1. NOUN

/ əˈplaɪd əˈnæt̬.ə.mi / (AmE), / əˈplaɪd əˈnæt.ə.mi / (BrE)
the practical application of anatomical knowledge to medical diagnosis and treatment
practical use of anatomy in medicine
medical

Examples

Medical students often study applied anatomy to understand how anatomical structures relate to clinical procedures.
Knowing applied anatomy is essential for surgeons performing complex operations.
The lecturer emphasized applied anatomy during the case presentation to illustrate the importance of anatomical knowledge in patient care.
