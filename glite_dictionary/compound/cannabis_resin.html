cannabis resin

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænəbɪs ˈrɛzɪn / (AmE), / ˈkanəbɪs ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a sticky resinous substance obtained from the cannabis plant, considered to contain the primary psychoactive components of marijuana and hashish
resin from the cannabis plant, used as a drug

Examples

The police seized several kilograms of cannabis resin during the drug bust.
Many hashish products are derived directly from cannabis resin collected from the plant.
Importing cannabis resin is illegal in many countries due to its psychoactive properties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hashish - a drug made from the concentrated resin of cannabis
  • Synonym: marijuana - a psychoactive drug from the leaves and flowers of cannabis
