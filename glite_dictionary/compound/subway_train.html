subway train

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌb.weɪ treɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ treɪn / (BrE)
A train designed to operate within an underground urban railway system, commonly known as a subway.
train running in an underground subway system

Examples

The subway train arrived just as I reached the platform.
During rush hour, the subway train was packed with commuters heading downtown.
She missed her stop and had to wait for the next subway train.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subway - an underground electric railway system
  • Synonym: underground train - a train running in an underground railway system
  • Antonym: surface train - a railway train running above ground
