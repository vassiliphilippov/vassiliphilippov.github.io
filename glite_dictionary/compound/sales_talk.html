sales talk

1. NOUN

/ seɪlz tɔk / (AmE), / seɪlz tɔːk / (BrE)
speech or conversation intended to persuade someone to buy something, often by emphasizing the benefits of the product or service
persuasive talk to sell something

Examples

The car dealer's sales talk was so convincing that I almost bought a new model on the spot.
Despite all the sales talk, I wasn't persuaded to sign the contract.
She was put off by the aggressive sales talk and decided to shop elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitch - a speech or argument intended to persuade someone to buy or accept something
