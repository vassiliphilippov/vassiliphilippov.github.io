case of thumbitis

1. NOUN

/ keɪs əv θʌmˈaɪtɪs / (AmE), / keɪs əv θʌmˈaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A condition involving pain or discomfort in the thumbs resulting from excessive texting or use of handheld devices.
pain in thumbs from texting
informal

Examples

After a weekend marathon of video games, he ended up with a case of thumbitis.
Sarah visited the doctor when her case of thumbitis became too painful to ignore.
Doctors are seeing more patients with a case of thumbitis due to increased smartphone use.

Synset Examples

Her constant texting led to a case of thumbitis.

Etymology

Blend of 'thumb' and '-itis' (suffix denoting inflammation), modeled on medical terminology.
