tip-and-run

1. ADJ

/ tɪp ənd rʌn / (AmE), / tɪp ənd rʌn / (BrE)
(of an attack or tactic) involving a brief assault followed by a rapid retreat or withdrawal.
brief attack followed by quick escape

Examples

The rebels used tip-and-run tactics to frustrate the larger army.
Their victory was credited to a series of swift tip-and-run assaults.
The commander praised the effectiveness of our tip-and-run operations.

Synset Examples

hit-and-run units
tip-and-run assaults

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit-and-run - brief attack followed by flight
