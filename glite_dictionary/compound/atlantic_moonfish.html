Atlantic moonfish

1. NOUN

/ əˈtlæntɪk ˈmuːnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈtlæntɪk ˈmuːnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a silvery marine fish with a very flat body, found in the Atlantic Ocean
a flat-bodied silvery fish of the Atlantic

Examples

Fishermen sometimes catch Atlantic moonfish near the coastal reefs.
The Atlantic moonfish is known for its distinctive, flattened shape and bright, silvery color.
Marine biologists study the habits of Atlantic moonfishes in shallow waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lookdown - a silvery marine fish with a similarly flat body
