selling agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company authorized to sell goods or property on behalf of another, usually for a commission
person who sells for others on commission

Examples

The company hired a selling agent to market their products overseas.
As a selling agent, she earns commission on every transaction she facilitates.
A selling agent can help property owners find qualified buyers quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sales agent - person who sells goods or services on behalf of others
  • Antonym: buying agent - person who purchases goods or services on behalf of others
