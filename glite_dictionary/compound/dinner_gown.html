dinner gown

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnɚ ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a formal dress worn for evening occasions, especially at dinner events
formal evening dress for dinner events

Examples

She wore a sparkling dinner gown to the charity gala last night.
The boutique specializes in elegant dinner gowns for black-tie affairs.
Her red velvet dinner gown caught the attention of everyone at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evening gown - a long dress for formal evening events
