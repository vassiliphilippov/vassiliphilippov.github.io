musk turtle

1. NOUN

/ mʌsk ˈtɝːtəl / (AmE), / mʌsk ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small freshwater turtle, native to North America, noted for producing a strong musky odor as a defense mechanism
small North American freshwater turtle with a musky odor

Examples

The musk turtle released a strong odor when it was handled by the biologist.
We spotted several musk turtles basking on logs along the riverbank.
Because of its scent, the musk turtle is sometimes called a stinkpot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stinkpot - another name for the musk turtle, especially in North America
