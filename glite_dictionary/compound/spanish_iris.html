Spanish iris

1. NOUN

/ ˈspænɪʃ ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ ˈaɪərɪs / (BrE)
a bulbous iris native to the western Mediterranean region, typically bearing violet-purple flowers
iris plant from western Mediterranean

Examples

The Spanish iris is prized by gardeners for its vibrant violet blooms.
She planted several Spanish irises along the edge of the path for a touch of Mediterranean color.
The delicate petals of the Spanish iris opened in late spring, brightening the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iris - a genus of flowering plants with showy flowers
