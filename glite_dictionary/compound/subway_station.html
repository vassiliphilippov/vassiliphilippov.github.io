subway station

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌweɪ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ ˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a place where subway trains stop to pick up or drop off passengers
place where subway trains stop for passengers

Examples

She met her friend at the subway station before heading downtown together.
The subway station was crowded during the morning rush hour.
A map at the subway station helped him find the right train to his destination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train station - a place where trains stop to load or unload passengers
  • Synonym: bus station - a terminal where buses pick up or drop off passengers
