little helper

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtl̩ ˈhɛlpɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ˈhɛlpə / (BrE)
something or someone that provides assistance, typically in a minor or auxiliary role
minor assistant or aid

Examples

Whenever I'm baking, my daughter is my little helper in the kitchen.
A cup of coffee in the morning can be a real little helper for getting started.
He referred to his notes as his little helper during the speech.

Synset Examples

The herbal tea is my mother's little helper for stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistant - a person who helps or supports someone else
