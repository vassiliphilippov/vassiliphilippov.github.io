summer harvest

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ ˈhɑrvəst / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈhɑːvɪst / (BrE)
The crops or produce gathered during the summer season.
crops gathered in summer

Examples

The summer harvest was particularly abundant this year, providing plenty of fresh fruit and vegetables.
Farmers celebrated the successful summer harvest by organizing a community feast.
Many families rely on the summer harvest to store food for the colder months.

Synset Examples

They stored the summer harvest in the icehouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops
