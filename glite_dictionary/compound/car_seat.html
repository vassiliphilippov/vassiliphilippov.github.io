car seat

1. NOUN

/ kɑɹ siːt / (AmE), / kɑː siːt / (BrE)
a seat designed for use in a motor vehicle, typically for passengers or the driver
seat in a car

Examples

Please make sure your child is properly strapped into the car seat before we leave.
The car seat was adjusted to provide more legroom for the driver.
She wiped the crumbs off the car seat before her friend got in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seat - a thing made or used for sitting on
