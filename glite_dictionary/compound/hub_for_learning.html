hub for learning

1. NOUN

/ hʌb fɚ ˈlɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / hʌb fə ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a central place or facility where educational activities and resources are concentrated or coordinated
central educational facility or center

Examples

The community center was transformed into a hub for learning during the summer months.
Universities often serve as a hub for learning and cultural exchange in their cities.
Their online platform has become a virtual hub for learning for students worldwide.

Synset Examples

Our town's library is a valuable Hub for learning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: learning center - a place or facility designed for educational activities
