genus Nopalea

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs noʊˈpeɪliə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs nəʊˈpeɪlɪə / (BrE)
A genus of cacti native to Mexico and Central America, characterized by flat, fleshy stems and scarlet flowers.
genus of cacti with scarlet flowers

Examples

The genus Nopalea is easily identified by its bright scarlet flowers and flattened stems.
Some species of the genus Nopalea are cultivated as ornamental plants in arid regions.
Researchers discovered a new species belonging to the genus Nopalea in the deserts of Mexico.

Etymology

From Latin 'Nopalea', adopted in scientific classification.
