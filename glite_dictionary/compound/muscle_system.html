muscle system

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəl ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl̩ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
The system of muscles and associated tissues that enables movement in a living organism.
system of muscles in an organism

Examples

The human muscle system consists of over 600 individual muscles that control movement.
A healthy diet and regular exercise are essential for maintaining a strong muscle system.
Doctors study the muscle system to understand how injuries impact mobility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular system - the system of muscles enabling movement in the body
