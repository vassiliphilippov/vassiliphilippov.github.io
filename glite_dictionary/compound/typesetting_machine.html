typesetting machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌsɛtɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌsɛtɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used to arrange text for printing by setting it into type
machine for setting text into type

Examples

The old newspaper office still displayed an antique typesetting machine from the early twentieth century.
Before computers, a typesetting machine was essential to produce books and magazines efficiently.
She studied the operation of the typesetting machine during her training as a printer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linotype - a brand and type of hot metal typesetting machine
  • Synonym: typesetter - a person or machine that sets type for printing

Etymology

From 'type' (printed characters), 'setting', and 'machine'.
