pipewort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpwɝt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical aquatic or bog herbs, including the pipeworts (Eriocaulaceae)
a family of aquatic or bog herbs

Examples

The pipewort family contains many species found in tropical wetlands.
Some members of the pipewort family are cultivated in aquatic gardens for their distinctive appearance.
Botanists study the pipewort family to understand plant adaptations to marshy environments.
