bubble bath

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌbəl bæθ / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl bɑːθ / (BrE)
A bath in which a special liquid or powder is added to the water to produce foam and scent.
foam-filled scented bath

Examples

After a long day, I love to relax in a bubble bath with candles and music.
She poured the lavender-scented bubble bath into the hot water and watched the foam grow.
Children often enjoy a bubble bath because of all the colorful bubbles it creates.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌb.əl bæθ / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl bɑːθ / (BrE)
a liquid, powder, or crystal product added to bathwater to create foam and scent, often softening the water
product used to create foam in bathwater

Examples

She added some bubble bath to the tub and soon the water was covered in fluffy white foam.
Children love playing with the bubbles after pouring in their favorite bubble bath.
The lavender-scented bubble bath made her evening soak even more relaxing.
