chemical terrorism

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈtɛrərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ˈtɛrərɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the use of toxic chemical substances as weapons by terrorists to threaten, harm, or disrupt societies
terrorism involving toxic chemical agents

Examples

The international community is working to prevent chemical terrorism by controlling access to dangerous substances.
Fears of chemical terrorism have led to increased security measures at chemical plants.
Training for first responders now includes strategies for dealing with the aftermath of chemical terrorism.

Synset Examples

a good agent for chemical terrorism should be colorless and odorless and inexpensive and readily available and not detectable until symptoms are experienced

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bioterrorism - terrorism involving the use of biological agents or toxins
  • Synonym: chemical warfare - the use of chemical substances in war to harm or kill enemy forces
