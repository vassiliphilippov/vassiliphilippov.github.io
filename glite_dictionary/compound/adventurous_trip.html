adventurous trip

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɛntʃərəs trɪp / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃ(ə)rəs trɪp / (BrE)
A journey or excursion that involves exciting experiences, often including some element of risk or uncertainty.
journey involving excitement and risk

Examples

They dreamed of taking an adventurous trip through the Amazon rainforest.
Her friends organized an adventurous trip that included rock climbing and white-water rafting.
An adventurous trip can help you break out of your comfort zone and discover new things.

Synset Examples

They planned an adventurous trip to Siberia next spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adventure - An unusual and exciting or daring experience
