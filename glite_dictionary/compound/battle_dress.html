battle dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætl drɛs / (AmE), / ˈbætl drɛs / (BrE)
a military uniform designed for wear in the field or during combat, as opposed to ceremonial or everyday dress
military uniform for field use

Examples

The soldiers changed into their battle dress before heading out on the mission.
During the parade, they wore ceremonial uniforms rather than their usual battle dress.
His old battle dress was stored carefully in a trunk after he left the army.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatigues - a type of clothing worn by soldiers for labor or field work
