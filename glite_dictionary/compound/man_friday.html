man Friday

1. NOUN

/ mæn ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (AmE), / man ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
A male assistant who is especially reliable and helpful in carrying out various tasks.
especially helpful male assistant

Examples

After the manager left, Tom became the office's man Friday and handled all emergencies.
Steve is not just my assistant; he's my man Friday who I can count on for anything.
Every successful leader needs a dependable man Friday to manage the details.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: girl Friday - female assistant who is very helpful and reliable

Etymology

From the character Friday, a loyal servant to Robinson Crusoe in Daniel Defoe's 1719 novel *Robinson Crusoe*.

/ mæn ˈfraɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / mæn ˈfraɪ.deɪ / (BrE)

reliable male assistant

Examples

As her trusted man Friday, he arranged all the meetings and handled logistics with ease.
Whenever there was a problem, everyone turned to Mark, their dependable man Friday.
