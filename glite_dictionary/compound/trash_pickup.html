trash pickup

1. NOUN

/ træʃ ˈpɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / træʃ ˈpɪkˌʌp / (BrE)
the collection and removal of garbage from homes or businesses by a public or private service
collection and removal of garbage
american

Examples

Please remember that trash pickup is on Mondays in our neighborhood.
The city announced changes to the trash pickup schedule for the holiday week.
Missed trash pickup can cause unpleasant odors and litter problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage collection - the act of collecting household waste; also used widely in British English
