piano player

1. NOUN

/ piˈænoʊ ˈpleɪɚ / (AmE), / piˈanəʊ ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the piano, especially as a performer or musician
person who plays the piano

Examples

The piano player filled the room with beautiful music every evening.
She is an accomplished piano player and has won several competitions.
The jazz band hired a new piano player for their upcoming tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pianist - a professional or highly skilled piano player
