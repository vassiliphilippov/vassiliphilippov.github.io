full moon maple

1. NOUN

/ fʊl muːn ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / fʊl muːn ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
A Japanese maple tree with deeply incised leaves that turn bright red in autumn, cultivated for its ornamental value.
Japanese maple with deeply cut, bright red autumn leaves

Examples

The leaves of the full moon maple turn a brilliant scarlet in the fall.
Gardeners prize the full moon maple for its delicate, deeply lobed foliage.
We planted a full moon maple in the backyard to add vibrant color each autumn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Acer japonicum - scientific name for full moon maple
