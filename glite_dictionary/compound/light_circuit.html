light circuit

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ˈsɝː.kɪt / (AmE), / laɪt ˈsɜː.kɪt / (BrE)
a circuit consisting of wiring and equipment that supplies electrical power to lights in a building or area
electrical circuit for lights

Examples

The electrician inspected the light circuit to determine why the living room lights were flickering.
Before replacing the ceiling fixture, he made sure to turn off the light circuit at the breaker box.
Older houses may have only one light circuit serving all the rooms upstairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighting circuit - an electrical circuit that powers lighting
