Abor language

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːbɔːr ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɑːbɔː ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a group of little-known Kamarupan languages spoken in northeastern India
Kamarupan languages of northeastern India

Examples

The Abor language is spoken by some indigenous groups in northeastern India.
Researchers have found few written records of the Abor language.
Linguists are working to document the Abor language before it becomes extinct.

Etymology

Abor, a designation for certain tribes in Arunachal Pradesh, India.
