pink sand verbena

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk sænd vərˈbinə / (AmE), / pɪŋk sand vəˈbiːnə / (BrE)
A prostrate herb with clusters of deep pink to white flowers, native to coastal dunes from British Columbia to Baja California.
coastal plant with pink or white flowers

Examples

We spotted a cluster of pink sand verbena blooming along the beach trail.
The pink sand verbena thrives in sandy soils near the coast.
Botanists conducted a survey of pink sand verbena populations in California dunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Abronia umbellata - the scientific name for pink sand verbena
