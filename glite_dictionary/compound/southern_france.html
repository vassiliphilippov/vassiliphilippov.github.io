Southern France

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌðərn fræns / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən frɑːns / (BrE)
the southern part of France, typically considered to include regions such as Provence, Occitania, and the French Riviera
southern region of France

Examples

Many tourists flock to Southern France for its stunning Mediterranean coastline.
Wines from Southern France are renowned for their rich flavors and variety.
The mild climate in Southern France makes it a popular place for retirement.
