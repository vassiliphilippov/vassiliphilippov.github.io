kidney fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdni fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni fɜːn / (BrE)
a large fern native to New Zealand with distinctive kidney-shaped fronds
New Zealand fern with kidney-shaped fronds

Examples

The kidney fern is one of the most distinctive plants found in New Zealand's rainforests.
We spotted a patch of kidney ferns growing along the shaded forest floor.
Botanists admire the unique frond shape of the kidney fern during their studies.
