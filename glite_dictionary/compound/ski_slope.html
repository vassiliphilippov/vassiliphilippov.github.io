ski slope

1. NOUN

/ ski sloʊp / (AmE), / ski sləʊp / (BrE)
a snow-covered hill or area prepared and used for skiing
snow-covered hill for skiing

Examples

The resort boasts a challenging ski slope that attracts professionals from around the world.
Children were learning to ski on the beginner ski slope near the lodge.
After the snowfall, the ski slopes were groomed and ready for the weekend crowd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piste - a marked ski run or path down a mountain for skiing
