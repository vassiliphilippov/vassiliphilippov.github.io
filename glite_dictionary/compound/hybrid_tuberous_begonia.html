hybrid tuberous begonia

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪ.brɪd tuːˈbɛr.əs bɪˈɡoʊ.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪ.brɪd tjuːˈbɪə.rəs bɪˈɡəʊ.ni.ə / (BrE)
A begonia plant grown from a tuber, developed as a hybrid, with showy, variously colored flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental.
ornamental hybrid begonia with tuberous roots

Examples

The hybrid tuberous begonia blooms with magnificent colors in summer gardens and window boxes.
Gardeners prize the hybrid tuberous begonia for its large flowers and ability to thrive in partial shade.
She planted several varieties of hybrid tuberous begonias along the shady path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants, many cultivated as ornamentals
