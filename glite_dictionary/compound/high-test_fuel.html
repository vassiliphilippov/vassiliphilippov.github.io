high-test fuel

1. NOUN

/ haɪ tɛst ˈfjuːl / (AmE), / haɪ tɛst ˈfjʊəl / (BrE)
fuel, especially gasoline, that has a high octane rating and is suitable for high-performance engines
fuel with a high octane rating

Examples

Many classic car owners prefer high-test fuel to keep their engines running smoothly.
Some gas stations still offer high-test fuel for performance cars.
Jet engines require high-test fuel to operate efficiently at high altitudes.

Synset Examples

The high-test fuel improved the engine's performance.
He only uses high-test gasoline for his sports car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-octane - (of fuel) having a high octane rating

Etymology

From 'high-test', meaning high in tested quality or standard, especially relating to octane rating in fuel.
