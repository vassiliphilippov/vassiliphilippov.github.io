manner of speaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɚ əv ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmænə əv ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
a person's characteristic way of expressing themselves orally; their style of speaking.
way of expressing oneself orally

Examples

His manner of speaking was calm and deliberate, which helped set everyone at ease.
You could tell she was nervous from her manner of speaking during the interview.
Over the years, his manner of speaking has grown more confident and persuasive.

Synset Examples

his manner of speaking was quite abrupt
her speech was barren of southernisms
I detected a slight accent in his speech

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speaking style - a particular style or pattern of speaking
  • Synonym: speech - the expression of thoughts and feelings by spoken words
