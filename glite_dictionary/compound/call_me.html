call me

1. X

/ kɔl mi / (AmE), / kɔːl miː / (BrE)
used to ask someone to contact you by telephone
ask someone to phone you

Examples

If you have any questions, just call me and I’ll explain.
Feel free to call me whenever you want to talk.
She told him to call me when he arrived.

Synset Examples

If you need help, just call me.
