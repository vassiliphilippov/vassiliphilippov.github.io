axial rotation

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksiəl roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈæksiəl rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the rotation of an object around its own axis
rotation around an axis

Examples

The Earth's axial rotation is responsible for the cycle of day and night.
A wheel's axial rotation needs to be smooth to ensure proper vehicle handling.
In anatomy, axial rotation of the spine allows for twisting movements.

Synset Examples

wheels in axial rotation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotation - the action of rotating around a center or axis

Etymology

From Latin axis (axle) and rotatio (a turning), used in technical contexts since the 19th century.
