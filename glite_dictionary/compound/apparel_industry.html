apparel industry

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɛrəl ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / əˈparəl ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in the design, manufacture, and sale of clothing and fashion items
clothing and fashion business sector

Examples

Employment in the apparel industry has increased steadily over the past decade.
Environmental concerns are pushing the apparel industry to adopt more sustainable practices.
The apparel industry often responds quickly to changing fashion trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fashion industry - the sector related to creating and selling fashionable clothing and accessories
