bacon strip

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkən strɪp / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkən strɪp / (BrE)
A thin slice of bacon, usually cooked until crisp and served as a food item.
thin slice of bacon

Examples

I added a crispy bacon strip to my sandwich for extra flavor.
She cooked several bacon strips for breakfast each morning.
Each salad comes topped with a generous bacon strip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacon - Cured and smoked meat from the sides of a pig
