City of Brotherly Love

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɪti əv ˈbrʌðɚli lʌv / (AmE), / ˌsɪti əv ˈbrʌðəli lʌv / (BrE)
a nickname for Philadelphia, the largest city in Pennsylvania, United States
nickname for Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Examples

Many historic events took place in the City of Brotherly Love, especially during the American Revolution.
She's moving to the City of Brotherly Love to start her new job at the university.
The nickname City of Brotherly Love reflects Philadelphia's origins and welcoming spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Philadelphia - the largest city in Pennsylvania, USA

Etymology

From Philadelphia's Greek roots: 'philos' (love) and 'adelphos' (brother).
