livery driver

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvəri ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəri ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a taxi or other vehicle for hire
professional taxi or hired vehicle driver
american

Examples

The city requires every livery driver to display their license in the car.
She worked as a livery driver for several years before getting an office job.
Many livery drivers prefer airport runs because of the reliable fares.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi driver - a person who drives a taxi for passengers

Etymology

From 'livery' (a vehicle for hire) and 'driver'.
