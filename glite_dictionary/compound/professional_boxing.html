professional boxing

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈbɑksɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈbɒksɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of boxing in which participants compete for money as their occupation, rather than as amateurs.
boxing as a paid sport

Examples

He decided to pursue a career in professional boxing after winning several amateur titles.
Professional boxing is often more heavily regulated than its amateur counterpart.
Many athletes transition from amateur to professional boxing to compete on a larger stage and earn prize money.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur boxing - boxing as a non-professional sport or hobby
