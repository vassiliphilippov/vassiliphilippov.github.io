betting shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛtɪŋ ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛtɪŋ ʃɒp / (BrE)
a commercial establishment where people can legally place bets, especially on sporting events, away from the event venue
place to place legal bets (esp. UK)
british

Examples

He frequently visited the betting shop to place wagers on horse races.
All betting shops were closed during the lockdown period.
She walked past the betting shop without being tempted to go in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bookmaker - a person or organization that takes bets, especially on sporting events
