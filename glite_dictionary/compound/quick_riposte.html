quick riposte

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk rɪˈpoʊst / (AmE), / kwɪk rɪˈpɒst / (BrE)
A swift and witty reply, especially in a conversation or argument.
swift and witty reply

Examples

Her quick riposte silenced everyone in the room.
He admired her quick riposte to the challenging question.
A quick riposte often requires both humor and intelligence.

Synset Examples

Her quick riposte turned the argument in her favor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - a sharp, witty reply
  • Synonym: comeback - a quick, clever reply to criticism

Etymology

Riposte from French 'riposte' (retort, reply), from Italian 'risposta' (reply).
