devil's tongue

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəlz tʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz tʌŋ / (BrE)
A tropical plant of southeastern Asia with a foul odor, large leaves, and a showy dark red spathe, grown for its edible corms or as an ornamental.
tropical plant with foul odor and edible corms

Examples

The greenhouse displayed a rare devil's tongue with its impressive dark red spathe.
Some people grow devil's tongue for its unusual appearance and edible corms.
The foul odor emitted by the devil's tongue attracts pollinators in its native habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: konjac - plant also known as devil's tongue, valued for its starchy corm
