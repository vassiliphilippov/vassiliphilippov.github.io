frozen orange juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈfroʊzən ˈɔrɪndʒ ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊz(ə)n ˈɒrɪndʒ ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
orange juice that has been concentrated and preserved by freezing, typically sold in cans to be reconstituted with water
concentrated orange juice preserved by freezing

Examples

She bought a can of frozen orange juice to make breakfast easier.
Mix the frozen orange juice concentrate with three cans of cold water.
Frozen orange juice is a convenient option for busy families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange juice - juice obtained by pressing or squeezing oranges
