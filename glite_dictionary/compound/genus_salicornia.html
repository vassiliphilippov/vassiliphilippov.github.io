genus Salicornia

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs sæl.ɪˈkɔːr.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs sæl.ɪˈkɔː.nɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of succulent, salt-tolerant plants known as glassworts, found in coastal salt marshes and mudflats.
genus of salt-tolerant plants (glassworts)

Examples

The genus Salicornia is commonly found in salt marshes along the coast.
Researchers are studying the unique salt tolerance of plants in the genus Salicornia.
Glassworts from the genus Salicornia are sometimes used in gourmet cooking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glasswort - a common name for plants in the genus Salicornia

Etymology

From Latin 'sal' (salt) and 'cornu' (horn), referring to the appearance of the plant.
