sparring mate

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑːrɪŋ meɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɑːrɪŋ meɪt / (BrE)
a boxer who trains with another boxer, often helping them prepare for an upcoming fight by simulating an opponent
boxer who trains with another for practice

Examples

He hired an experienced sparring mate to help him prepare for the championship fight.
During the training camp, her sparring mate pushed her to improve her defense.
The coach selected a new sparring mate who closely matched the style of the upcoming opponent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparring partner - a person who spars with another for practice

Etymology

From 'spar' (to box for practice) + 'mate' (companion, partner).
