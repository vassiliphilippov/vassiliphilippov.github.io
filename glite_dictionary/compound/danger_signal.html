danger signal

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪndʒɚ ˌsɪgnəl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒə ˌsɪgnəl / (BrE)
a sign, warning, or indication that something harmful or risky may occur
warning of potential harm or risk

Examples

Ignoring the danger signal could lead to serious consequences.
The flashing light served as a danger signal for everyone nearby.
A sudden drop in temperature can act as a danger signal when hiking in the mountains.

Synset Examples

Ignoring the danger signal could lead to serious consequences.
The flashing light was a danger signal to all drivers.
Her sudden silence was a danger signal for all of us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warning sign - a sign that indicates a possible danger or problem
