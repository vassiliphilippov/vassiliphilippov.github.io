moss green

1. ADJ

/ mɑs ˈɡrin / (AmE), / mɒs ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
A moderate, somewhat dull yellow-green color, resembling the color of moss.
dull yellow-green color

Examples

The living room was painted a soft moss green to create a calming atmosphere.
She wore a moss green dress that complemented her hazel eyes perfectly.
The artist mixed yellow and blue to achieve the exact shade of moss green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: olive green - a dark yellowish-green color similar to that of green olives
  • Degree Higher: forest green - a dark green color resembling that of the forest
  • Degree Lower: chartreuse - a brighter, more vivid yellow-green color
