paper cup

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɚ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˌkʌp / (BrE)
A disposable drinking cup made from paper, typically used for beverages and thrown away after use.
disposable cup made from paper

Examples

She drank her coffee from a paper cup while waiting at the station.
Please recycle your paper cup after use to help reduce waste.
At the picnic, all the drinks were served in paper cups for convenience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plastic cup - a disposable cup made of plastic
  • Antonym: mug - a large cup with a handle, usually used for hot drinks
