ash grey

1. NOUN

/ æʃ ɡreɪ / (AmE), / æʃ ɡreɪ / (BrE)
a light grey color, resembling the color of ash
a light grey color

Examples

She painted her living room walls a soft ash grey to create a calm atmosphere.
His shirt was a pale ash grey that matched his tie.
The sky turned an ash grey before the storm rolled in.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: grey - the color between black and white
  • Degree Lower: off-white - a color very close to white with a slight tint
