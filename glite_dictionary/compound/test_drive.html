test drive

1. NOUN

/ tɛst draɪv / (AmE), / tɛst draɪv / (BrE)
a trial run of a vehicle, especially a car, to assess its performance before making a purchase
trial run of a vehicle before buying

Examples

Before buying a new car, Sarah scheduled a test drive to make sure it met her expectations.
The dealership allows customers to take any vehicle out for a test drive.
His first test drive convinced him that the SUV was the right choice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: road test - an examination or trial of a vehicle on the road to assess its performance

2. NOUN

/ tɛst draɪv / (AmE), / tɛst draɪv / (BrE)
an act of driving a vehicle to evaluate its performance before making a purchase
trial run of a vehicle before buying

Examples

He scheduled a test drive to see how the car handled on the highway.
Before making a decision, they took several vehicles out for a test drive.
The dealership encourages customers to take a test drive before purchasing any vehicle.

Synset Examples

Let's take the new car for a test drive.
She went on a test drive before buying the sedan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trial run - an experiment or test to try or evaluate something
