pea pod

1. NOUN

/ pi pɑd / (AmE), / pi pɒd / (BrE)
the elongated, green, seed case of a pea plant containing the peas; edible in some varieties
seed case of a pea plant

Examples

She picked a fresh pea pod from the garden and snapped it open to eat the sweet peas inside.
Stir-fried vegetables often include sliced pea pods for added texture and flavor.
Snow peas are a type of pea pod that are eaten whole, including the tender husk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pod - seed case of a leguminous plant
