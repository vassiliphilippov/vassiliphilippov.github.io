genus Hypopitys

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs haɪˈpɑː.pɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs hɪˈpɒ.pɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A former botanical genus name used for pinesaps, now usually included in the genus Monotropa.
former genus of pinesaps (plants)

Examples

Some botanists previously classified these plants under genus Hypopitys instead of Monotropa.
The genus Hypopitys includes non-photosynthetic plants often found in pine forests.
Recent studies have merged plants from genus Hypopitys into the genus Monotropa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Monotropa - genus that now includes pinesaps previously classified as Hypopitys

Etymology

From Greek 'hypo-' (under) + 'pitys' (pine), referring to its habitat under pines.
