murder log

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝdɚ lɔg / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə lɒg / (BrE)
a written or digital record used to track details of murders, events, activities, or suspects, often in the context of crime investigations or fiction
record of murders and related events

Examples

The detective updated the murder log after each new clue was uncovered.
Players are encouraged to keep a detailed murder log to solve the game quickly.
She referred to her old murder log when writing her latest mystery novel.

Synset Examples

In the game, players maintained a murder log of events.
He kept a murder log to track the suspects.
The author referred to her murder log for inspiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: log - a written record of events, usually in chronological order
