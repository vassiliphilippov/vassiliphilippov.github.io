pork roast

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk roʊst / (AmE), / pɔːk rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of pork that is suitable for cooking by roasting
cut of pork for roasting

Examples

She prepared a delicious pork roast for Sunday dinner.
The pork roast needs to cook for several hours to become tender.
Their special today is a herb-crusted pork roast with seasonal vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roast pork - pork that has been roasted, or a dish made by roasting pork
