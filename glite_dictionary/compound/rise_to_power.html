rise to power

1. NOUN

/ raɪz tə ˈpaʊɚ / (AmE), / raɪz tə ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
the process or act of attaining a position of authority or control, especially in government or leadership
attainment of authority or high office

Examples

After years of political turmoil, his unexpected rise to power changed the nation overnight.
The documentary explores the general's rise to power and the impact on his country.
Historians often debate the factors that contributed to her rise to power in the 16th century.

Synset Examples

Elizabeth's accession in 1558

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accession - the attainment of a position of rank or power, typically a throne
