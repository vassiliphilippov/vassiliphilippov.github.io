true to life

1. ADJ

/ truː tə laɪf / (AmE), / truː tə laɪf / (BrE)
closely resembling or representing real life; accurate or realistic in detail and presentation
realistic; resembling real life

Examples

The documentary provided a true to life look at the struggles faced by local fishermen.
Her paintings are so true to life that they almost look like photographs.
The actor's performance in the film was remarkably true to life and moving.

Synset Examples

the novel about ghetto life was true to life
true-to-life duck decoys

Related Terms

  • Synonym: realistic - representing things in a way that is true to life
  • Synonym: lifelike - very similar to a real person or thing
  • Antonym: unrealistic - not resembling or representing real life
