fruit grower

1. NOUN

/ fruːt ˈɡroʊɚ / (AmE), / fruːt ˈɡrəʊə / (BrE)
a person or business that cultivates fruit for commercial sale
person who grows fruit commercially

Examples

The fruit grower inspected his orchard daily to ensure the apples were healthy.
Many fruit growers are adopting organic farming practices to meet market demand.
The region is known for its skilled fruit growers and high-quality produce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchardist - a person who owns or manages an orchard
