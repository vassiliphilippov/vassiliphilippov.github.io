objet d'art

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑbʒɛ dɑr / (AmE), / ˌɒbʒeɪ ˈdɑː / (BrE)
an article of artistic value, especially a small decorative work of art
article of artistic or decorative value

Examples

The gallery's collection includes an exquisite objet d'art from the 18th century.
She admired the finely crafted objet d'art on the shelf.
At the auction, several rare objets d'art attracted eager bidders.

Synset Examples

this store sells only objets d'art
it is not known who created this piece

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artwork - a work of art, especially a painting, drawing, or sculpture

Etymology

From French, meaning 'object of art'.
