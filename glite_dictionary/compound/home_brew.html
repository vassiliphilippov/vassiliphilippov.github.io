home brew

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm bruː / (AmE), / həʊm bruː / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink, especially beer, made at home rather than bought from a shop or bar.
beer or other alcohol made at home

Examples

He offered me a bottle of his home brew at the party.
Many people started making home brew during the lockdown.
Her father’s home brew was surprisingly good for a homemade beer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homebrewed - prepared or brewed at home
