Leo the Lion

1. NOUN

/ ˌliːoʊ ðə ˈlaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˌliːəʊ ðə ˈlaɪ.ən / (BrE)
the fifth sign of the zodiac, represented as a lion; the sun is in this sign from about July 23 to August 22
zodiac sign; the Lion

Examples

People born under Leo the Lion are said to be natural leaders with strong personalities.
Astrological charts place Leo the Lion between Cancer and Virgo in the zodiac.
The symbol for Leo the Lion is often depicted as a majestic lion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Leo - the zodiac sign Leo, the Lion
  • Degree Lower: Cancer - the zodiac sign before Leo
  • Degree Higher: Virgo - the zodiac sign after Leo

Etymology

From Latin 'leo' meaning 'lion,' representing the constellation of Leo.
