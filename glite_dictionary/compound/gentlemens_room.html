gentlemen's room

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntəlmɪnz ruːm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnt(ə)lmɪnz ruːm / (BrE)
a room with toilet facilities intended for use by men
men's restroom; toilet for men

Examples

The hotel lobby had a sign directing guests to the gentlemen's room.
He excused himself and went to the gentlemen's room during dinner.
The gentlemen's room was located at the end of the hallway.

Synset Examples

The restroom was labeled as the gentlemen's room.
He waited in the gentlemen's room before the event started.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: men's room - a restroom for men; toilet for men
  • Antonym: ladies' room - a restroom for women; toilet for women
