gray-pink

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ pɪŋk / (BrE)
A shade of pink that is tinged with gray.
pink with a hint of gray

Examples

She chose a gray-pink paint for the living room walls, creating a soft and elegant atmosphere.
The evening sky was streaked with a beautiful gray-pink hue as the sun set.
He wore a suit with a gray-pink tie, adding a subtle touch of color to his outfit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: rose - a shade of pink or red
  • Degree Higher: mauve - a pale purple or pinkish color with grayish tones
