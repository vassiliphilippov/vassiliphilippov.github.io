top gear

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ɡɪr / (AmE), / tɒp ɡɪə / (BrE)
a state of maximum efficiency, energy, or performance; the highest or fastest level of operation
maximum efficiency or performance

Examples

The team shifted into top gear as the deadline approached.
When the competition heated up, she went into top gear to win.
Production is now running in top gear to meet the increase in demand.

Synset Examples

He always drives in top gear on the highway.
The team is in top gear for the upcoming launch.
She shifted into top gear during the final stretch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overdrive - a state or condition of heightened activity or performance
  • Synonym: full throttle - operating at maximum speed or capacity
  • Antonym: low gear - a gear providing low speed but higher power, opposite end of the performance spectrum

Etymology

From the literal term in automobiles, where 'top gear' refers to the highest gear ratio used for maximum speed or efficiency.
