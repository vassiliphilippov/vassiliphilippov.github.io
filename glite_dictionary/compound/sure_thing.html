sure thing

1. NOUN

/ ʃʊr θɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃɔː θɪŋ / (BrE)
something that is certain to happen or succeed; a certainty.
something certain to happen
informal

Examples

Her promotion is a sure thing after all her hard work.
The team's victory was seen as a sure thing by most fans.
Don't treat any investment as a sure thing, because nothing is guaranteed.

Synset Examples

his victory is a certainty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainty - something that is certain
  • Antonym: possibility - something that may happen or be true
