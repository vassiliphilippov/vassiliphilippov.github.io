glee club

1. NOUN

/ ɡliː klʌb / (AmE), / ɡliː klʌb / (BrE)
a group or club organized to sing together, especially performing choral arrangements for amusement or social events
club for group singing

Examples

She joined the glee club in high school and performed at every concert.
The glee club practices every Wednesday after school.
Their college glee club has won several competitions for their choral performances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choir - a group of people who sing together, typically in church services

Etymology

From 'glee' (a type of song) + 'club'; 'glee' from Old English 'glēo' (entertainment, music).
