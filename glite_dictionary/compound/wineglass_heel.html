wineglass heel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪnˌɡlæs hiːl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌɡlɑːs hiːl / (BrE)
A woman's shoe heel shaped like a wineglass, typically narrow at the base, widening in the middle, and narrowing again at the top.
woman's shoe heel shaped like a wineglass

Examples

She admired the delicate wineglass heel on her new designer shoes.
The shoe store just got a fresh collection featuring wineglass heels in various colors.
Walking on a wineglass heel can require some practice, especially for beginners.
