autumn romance

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtəm roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
a romantic relationship that develops or occurs during the autumn season, often characterized by a sense of nostalgia or fleeting beauty
romantic relationship in autumn

Examples

Their autumn romance blossomed as they walked through the colorful park each evening.
She often reminisced about the autumn romance that changed her life forever.
The film tells the story of an autumn romance that begins at a harvest festival.

Synset Examples
