journey cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝni keɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːni keɪk / (BrE)
A type of cornbread, usually cooked pancake-style on a griddle, especially popular in New England.
griddled cornbread, chiefly New England
american

Examples

On our hiking trip, we made journey cake for a quick and hearty breakfast.
Grandma served hot journey cake with maple syrup on cold winter mornings.
Locals claim their journey cake recipe has been handed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: johnnycake - cornbread usually cooked flat on a griddle or pan, variant spelling/pronunciation

Etymology

Alteration of 'johnnycake', which is of uncertain origin but attested in American English since the 18th century.
