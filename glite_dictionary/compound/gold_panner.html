gold panner

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊld ˈpænər / (AmE), / ɡəʊld ˈpænə / (BrE)
a person who extracts gold from alluvial deposits by washing soil or gravel in a pan
person who pans for gold

Examples

The gold panner patiently sifted through the gravel, hoping to find a few precious flakes.
In the nineteenth century, many gold panners traveled west in search of fortune.
A seasoned gold panner can spot promising spots along a riverbank with ease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prospector - person who searches for mineral deposits
