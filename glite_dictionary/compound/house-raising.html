house-raising

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
an event in which neighbors or members of a community gather to help build a house, typically as a collective effort
community event for building a house

Examples

The entire village attended the house-raising to help the newlyweds start their life together.
A house-raising brought everyone closer, with neighbors working side by side from dawn till dusk.
They planned a house-raising this weekend, hoping many hands would make light work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barn raising - a community event where neighbors help build a barn
