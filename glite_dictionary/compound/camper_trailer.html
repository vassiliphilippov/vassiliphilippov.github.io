camper trailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpər ˌtreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈkæmpə ˌtreɪlə / (BrE)
a trailer fitted with living and sleeping facilities, used for holiday trips or camping
trailer equipped for camping or travel

Examples

We spent our summer vacation traveling across the country in a camper trailer.
The camper trailer was fully equipped with a small kitchen and comfortable beds.
After setting up the camper trailer at the campsite, the family enjoyed a relaxing evening outdoors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caravan - vehicle equipped for living, towed by a car (British English)
  • Synonym: recreational vehicle - motor vehicle or trailer equipped with living space and amenities
