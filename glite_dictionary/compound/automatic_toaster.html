automatic toaster

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈtoʊstər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmatɪk ˈtəʊstə / (BrE)
An electric toaster in which bread slices are placed vertically in slots and automatically pop up when toasting is finished.
electric toaster with automatic pop-up function

Examples

I bought an automatic toaster that pops up the bread perfectly every time.
The kitchen counter was cluttered with an automatic toaster, a coffee maker, and a blender.
She prefers an automatic toaster because it saves her time during busy mornings.

Synset Examples

My pop-up toaster is terrible, it always throws my toast on the ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop-up toaster - a toaster in which the slices pop up automatically after toasting
