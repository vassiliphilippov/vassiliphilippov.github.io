k-word

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ wɝd / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ wɜːd / (BrE)
a euphemistic way to refer to a highly offensive racial slur beginning with the letter 'k'
euphemism for racial slur (offensive term)
offensive

Examples

He was careful never to say the k-word in public discussions.
The teacher addressed the history and impact of the k-word without repeating the slur itself.
Media outlets often avoid printing the k-word due to its offensive nature.

Synset Examples

He got in trouble for saying the k-word.
Using the k-word in conversation is unacceptable.
The k-word has a painful history for many.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: n-word - euphemism for a racial slur beginning with the letter 'n'
