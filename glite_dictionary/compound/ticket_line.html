ticket line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪkɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt laɪn / (BrE)
a queue of people waiting to purchase tickets
a queue waiting to buy tickets

Examples

We stood in the ticket line for nearly an hour before the concert.
A long ticket line stretched around the block on opening night.
She joined the ticket line early to make sure she got a seat.

Related Terms
