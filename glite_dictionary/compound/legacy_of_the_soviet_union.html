legacy of the Soviet Union

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛɡəsi əv ðə ˌsoʊviət juˈniən / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ðə ˌsəʊviət juːˈniːən / (BrE)
the lasting historical, political, and cultural effects resulting from the existence and policies of the Soviet Union.
historical and cultural effects of Soviet rule

Examples

Many Eastern European countries are still dealing with the legacy of the Soviet Union in their political systems.
The educational reforms were partly shaped by the legacy of the Soviet Union.
Her grandparents often discussed how the legacy of the Soviet Union affected their lives.

Synset Examples

The artist's work was influenced by the legacy of the Soviet Jewnion.

  • Synonym: legacy - something handed down from the past
