Guyana dollar

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪˈænə ˈdɑlər / (AmE), / ɡaɪˈænə ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Guyana.
currency of Guyana

Examples

Many tourists exchange their US dollars for Guyana dollars upon arriving in Georgetown.
The value of the Guyana dollar has fluctuated against the euro this year.
Banks in Guyana often issue new Guyana dollars to replace old or damaged notes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar - the name of various units of currency, especially in the US, Canada, and others
