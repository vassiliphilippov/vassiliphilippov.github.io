voltaic system

1. NOUN

/ voʊlˈteɪɪk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / vɒlˈteɪɪk ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system that generates electricity from solar energy, typically using photovoltaic cells.
system for generating solar electricity

Examples

The company installed a voltaic system on the roof to supply renewable energy.
Investing in a voltaic system can significantly decrease household electricity bills.
Many remote villages are powered by a voltaic system due to lack of a central power grid.

Synset Examples

They installed a voltaic system to reduce energy costs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photovoltaic system - system generating electricity from light using solar cells

Etymology

From 'voltaic', relating to Alessandro Volta, an Italian physicist, combined with 'system'.
