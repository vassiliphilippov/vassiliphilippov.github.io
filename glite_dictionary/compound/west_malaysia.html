West Malaysia

1. PROPN

/ wɛst məˈleɪʒə / (AmE), / west məˈleɪziə / (BrE)
the part of Malaysia located on the Malay Peninsula, bordering Thailand to the north and separated from East Malaysia by the South China Sea
region of Malaysia on the Malay Peninsula

Examples

Many tourists visit West Malaysia for its vibrant cities and beautiful beaches.
The capital city of Kuala Lumpur is located in West Malaysia.
Trade between Thailand and West Malaysia has increased over the years.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: East Malaysia - the part of Malaysia on the island of Borneo
