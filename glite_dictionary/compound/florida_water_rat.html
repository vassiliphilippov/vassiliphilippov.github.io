Florida water rat

1. NOUN

/ flɔːrɪdə ˈwɔːtər ræt / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪdə ˈwɔːtə ræt / (BrE)
a semiaquatic rodent found in the wetlands of Florida, known for its adapted lifestyle in watery habitats
semiaquatic rodent native to Florida wetlands

Examples

The Florida water rat is rarely seen outside its preferred marsh habitats.
Researchers tracked the movement of the Florida water rat to study wetland health.
Conservation efforts have helped to stabilize Florida water rat populations in recent years.
