so much floof

1. NOUN

/ soʊ mʌtʃ fluːf / (AmE), / səʊ mʌtʃ fluːf / (BrE)
(informal) Used to describe an animal with an abundance of fluffy fur, typically in an affectionate or playful way.
abundance of fluffy fur (informal)
informal slang

Examples

That Pomeranian has so much floof that it looks like a tiny cloud.
People on social media love posting photos of cats with so much floof.
The bunny’s so much floof made everyone want to pet it.

Synset Examples

That dog has so much floof, it's adorable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluffy - covered with soft, light fur or feathers
  • Synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, or unkempt fur or hair

Etymology

From 'floof', a playful variant of 'fluff', used in internet slang to describe fluffy animals.
