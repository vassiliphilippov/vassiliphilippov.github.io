hit list

1. NOUN

/ hɪt lɪst / (AmE), / hɪt lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people who are to be targeted, harmed, or killed, especially by a criminal or terrorist organization
list of targeted victims for harm

Examples

The police found a hit list containing the names of several local officials.
Journalists suspected they were on the hit list after receiving threats.
The gang leader allegedly ordered attacks on everyone named on the hit list.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: death list - a list of people to be killed
