mathy brain

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæθi breɪn / (AmE), / ˈmæθi breɪn / (BrE)
a mind that is naturally inclined towards mathematical thinking or problem-solving
a mind oriented towards mathematics
informal

Examples

Ever since childhood, she has had a mathy brain that quickly grasps numbers and logic.
If you’ve got a mathy brain, this career path could be a great fit for you.
He may not love writing essays, but his mathy brain excels at solving complex problems.

Synset Examples

She has a mathy brain that loves solving equations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematical mind - a mind adept at understanding mathematical concepts
