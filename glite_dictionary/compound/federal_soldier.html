Federal soldier

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)

Union Army member (Civil War)

Examples

The museum features the uniform of a Federal soldier from the Civil War.
A Federal soldier marched alongside his comrades through the battle-scarred fields.
Many letters written by Federal soldiers have been preserved in historical archives.
