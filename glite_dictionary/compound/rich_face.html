rich face

1. NOUN

/ rɪtʃ feɪs / (AmE), / rɪtʃ feɪs / (BrE)
a face that shows depth, character, or expressiveness, often used in artistic or literary contexts
face full of character or depth

The photographer sought subjects with a rich face to add depth to his project.
