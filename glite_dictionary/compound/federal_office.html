federal office

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛdərəl ˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a government department or agency that is part of the federal government of the United States
department or agency in US federal government
american

Examples

She works in a federal office overseeing environmental regulations.
The new policy must be approved by the appropriate federal office before implementation.
Several federal offices were relocated to a different state last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government agency - an organization that is part of the government
