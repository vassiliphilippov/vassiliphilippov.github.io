Clinton administration

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪn.tən ædˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈklɪn.tən ədˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the executive branch of the United States government during the presidency of Bill Clinton (1993–2001)
U.S. executive branch under President Clinton

Examples

Many welfare reforms were enacted during the Clinton administration.
The Clinton administration faced several major international crises.
Economic growth was robust throughout the Clinton administration.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Bush administration - the executive branch under President George W. Bush
  • Antonym: Obama administration - the executive branch under President Barack Obama
