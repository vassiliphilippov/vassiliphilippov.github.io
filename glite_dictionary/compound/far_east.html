Far East

1. NOUN

/ fɑr ist / (AmE), / fɑː ˈiːst / (BrE)
the countries of eastern Asia, especially China, Japan, Korea, and surrounding regions, often considered as a cultural or geographic grouping
eastern Asia region (China, Japan, etc.)

Examples

He spent several years working in the Far East before returning home.
Many ancient trade routes connected Europe with the Far East.
The company plans to expand its business operations into the Far East.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: East Asia - the eastern region of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea

Etymology

From English 'far' + 'east', referring to its distance from Western Europe.
