letter of the alphabet

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtər əv ði ˈælfəˌbɛt / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə əv ði ˈælfəbɛt / (BrE)
a written symbol used to represent a speech sound in a systematic set of characters, such as those in the English alphabet
character representing a speech sound

Examples

Every English word is made up of one or more letters of the alphabet.
Children often learn the letters of the alphabet before they start reading.
The teacher pointed to each letter of the alphabet on the board.

Synset Examples

his grandmother taught him his letters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter - an individual written or printed character of the alphabet
  • Synonym: character - a printed or written symbol used in writing or printing
