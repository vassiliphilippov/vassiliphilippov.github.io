Ukie community

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːki kəˈmjuːnəti / (AmE), / ˈjuːki kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
An informal term for the Ukrainian community, especially as recognized in diaspora contexts.
informal term for the Ukrainian community
informal

Examples

The Ukie community in Toronto is known for its vibrant cultural events.
Members of the Ukie community gathered to celebrate Independence Day.
She volunteers regularly within the local Ukie community.

Synset Examples

The Ukie community organized a festival downtown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ukrainian community - the community formed by Ukrainians, especially abroad

Etymology

From 'Ukie', a colloquial abbreviation of 'Ukrainian', plus 'community'.
