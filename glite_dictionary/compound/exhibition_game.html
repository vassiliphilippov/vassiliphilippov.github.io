exhibition game

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛɡ.zɪˈbɪʃ.ən ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˌɛk.sɪˈbɪʃ.ən ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a sports game played for practice or entertainment, not counted in official standings
sports game not counted in standings

Examples

The teams will play an exhibition game before the official season begins.
Last night's exhibition game gave new players a chance to showcase their skills.
The score in an exhibition game doesn't affect the league rankings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendly - a game played for practice or entertainment rather than competition
