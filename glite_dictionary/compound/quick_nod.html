quick nod

1. NOUN

/ kwɪk nɑd / (AmE), / kwɪk nɒd / (BrE)
A brief and informal gesture of agreement or acknowledgment by moving the head up and down quickly.
a brief gesture of agreement or acknowledgment

Examples

She acknowledged the offer with a quick nod and a smile.
A quick nod from the manager meant we could proceed.
With a quick nod, he signaled his approval to the team.

Synset Examples

She gave a quick nod to acknowledge his suggestion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nod - an act of moving your head up and down to show agreement or acknowledgment
