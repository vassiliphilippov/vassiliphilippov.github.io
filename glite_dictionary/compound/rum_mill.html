rum mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌm mɪl / (AmE), / ˈrʌm mɪl / (BrE)
A source or origin of gossip or rumors.
source of gossip or rumors

Examples

The rum mill at her office spreads news faster than any official memo.
He didn't believe anything he heard from the local rum mill.
Before long, the rum mill had twisted the story into something completely different.

Synset Examples

She heard the news from the rum mill before anyone confirmed it.
The rumor spread quickly through the local rum mill.
The town was buzzing with the latest gossip from the rum mill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grapevine - means or source of unofficial information or gossip
