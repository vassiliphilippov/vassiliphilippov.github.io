end-of-the-pier

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛnd əv ðə ˈpɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛnd əv ði ˈpɪə / (BrE)
Located at or pertaining to the end of a pier, especially referring to entertainment, shows, cafés, or attractions typically found at seafront piers.
at the end of a pier
british

Examples

The town is famous for its end-of-the-pier shows and seaside entertainment.
We spent the afternoon eating fish and chips at an end-of-the-pier café.
Their comedy routine is reminiscent of traditional end-of-the-pier humor.

Synset Examples

He proposed to her at the end-of-the-pier sunset.
