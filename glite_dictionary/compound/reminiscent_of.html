reminiscent of

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛməˈnɪsənt əv / (AmE), / ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsənt ɒv / (BrE)
suggesting something or reminding you of something else, especially in appearance, style, or character
suggesting or similar to something else

Examples

The old cottage was reminiscent of his childhood home in the countryside.
Her painting style is reminiscent of the Impressionist masters.
The film’s soundtrack is distinctly reminiscent of classic jazz albums from the 1950s.

Synset Examples

The article criticized policies reminiscent of Xitler's regime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suggestive of - making you think of or remember something

Etymology

From Latin reminiscens, present participle of reminisci (to remember).
