two-way street

1. NOUN

/ ˌtuː weɪ ˈstriːt / (AmE), / ˌtuː weɪ ˈstriːt / (BrE)
A street on which vehicles are allowed to travel in both directions.
street allowing two-way traffic

Examples

Be careful when crossing a two-way street, as cars can come from both directions.
Parking is allowed on only one side of the two-way street.
City planners decided to convert the two-way street into a one-way for better traffic flow.

Synset Examples

you have to look both ways crossing a two-way street
