strawberry shrub

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ʃrʌb / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbəri ʃrʌb / (BrE)
a hardy shrub native to the southeastern United States, known for its clove-scented wood and fragrant reddish-brown flowers
a clove-scented flowering shrub of the southeastern U.S.

Examples

The strawberry shrub fills the garden with its spicy fragrance each spring.
Botanists in the southeastern states often study the strawberry shrub for its unique reddish-brown flowers.
Their yard features a mature strawberry shrub that attracts visitors every blooming season.
