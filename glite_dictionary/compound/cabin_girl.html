cabin girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæbɪn ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈkabɪn ɡəːl / (BrE)
A young woman employed as a servant on a ship, often assisting with duties in the passenger or officer cabins.
young female servant on a ship

Examples

The cabin girl brought fresh towels to the passengers each morning.
During the long voyage, the cabin girl became friends with the crew.
A cabin girl was responsible for keeping the officers' quarters tidy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabin boy - a young man employed as a servant on a ship
