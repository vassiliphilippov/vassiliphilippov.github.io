ab crack

1. NOUN

/ æb kræk / (AmE), / ab kræk / (BrE)
a visible vertical line or groove that appears in the middle of the abdomen, usually due to prominent or well-defined abdominal muscles
visible line between abdominal muscles

Examples

She spent months working out to achieve her ab crack before her holiday.
His personal trainer said an ab crack is the result of hard work and genetics.
Photos of celebrities with a prominent ab crack are often shared on social media.

Synset Examples

She showed off her ab crack at the beach.
His workout routine helped him develop an ab crack.
I aspire to have an ab crack like hers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: six-pack - well-defined abdominal muscles
