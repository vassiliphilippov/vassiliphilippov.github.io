storm door

1. NOUN

/ stɔrm dɔr / (AmE), / stɔːm dɔː / (BrE)
an additional outer door installed in front of an exterior door to protect against severe weather or provide insulation during winter
outer door for weather protection

Examples

We added a storm door to help keep the house warm during the cold months.
The old storm door rattled in the wind every time a storm blew through.
She replaced the screen door with a storm door before winter arrived.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: screen door - a door with wire mesh or screening to keep out insects while allowing air flow
