salto mortale

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑltoʊ mɔrˈtɑleɪ / (AmE), / ˌsæltoʊ mɔːˈtɑːleɪ / (BrE)
a daring acrobatic somersault, often performed in gymnastics or circus acts
acrobatic somersault leap

Examples

The gymnast's salto mortale thrilled everyone in the stadium.
He ended his routine with a difficult salto mortale that drew applause from the crowd.
Few performers dare attempt the double salto mortale in competition.

Synset Examples

The gymnast's salto mortale brought the audience to cheers.
Her salto mortale impressed the judges at the competition.
He executed a perfect salto mortale during the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement in which a person turns head over heels in the air or on the ground

Etymology

From Italian 'salto mortale' meaning 'deadly jump' or 'leap of death'.
