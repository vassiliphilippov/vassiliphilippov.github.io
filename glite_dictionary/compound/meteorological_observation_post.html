meteorological observation post

1. NOUN

/ ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˌɑbzɚˈveɪʃən poʊst / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃən pəʊst / (BrE)
a site equipped for the systematic collection and recording of meteorological data
site for gathering weather data

Examples

The country's highest meteorological observation post recorded record low temperatures last winter.
Data from a remote meteorological observation post helped meteorologists track the approaching storm.
Scientists visited the meteorological observation post daily to calibrate the instruments and collect readings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weather station - a place where weather conditions are measured and recorded
