clarinet section

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛr.ɪ.nɛt ˌsɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈklær.ɪ.nɛt ˌsɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the group of clarinet players in a band or orchestra
group of clarinet players in an ensemble

Examples

The clarinet section played a beautiful solo during the symphony.
She was thrilled to be chosen as the leader of the clarinet section.
The conductor asked the clarinet section to play more softly in the next movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: string section - the section of a band or orchestra that plays string instruments
  • Synonym: brass section - the section of a band or orchestra that plays brass instruments
