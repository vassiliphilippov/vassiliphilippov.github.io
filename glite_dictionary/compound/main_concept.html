main concept

1. NOUN

/ meɪn ˈkɑnsɛpt / (AmE), / meɪn ˈkɒnsɛpt / (BrE)
the primary idea or most important theme in a discussion, text, or situation.
primary idea or theme

Examples

The teacher asked the students to summarize the main concept of the article in their own words.
Understanding the main concept will help you answer the questions about the story.
Before you start writing, make sure you identify the main concept you want to discuss.

Synset Examples

This diagram will help illustrate the main concept.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central idea - the most important idea in a text or discussion
  • Antonym: minor detail - a less important or secondary point in a text or situation
