black and tan

1. NOUN

/ blæk ənd tæn / (AmE), / blæk ənd tæn / (BrE)
a drink consisting of a mixture of dark stout and pale ale, typically poured so the two layers remain separate in the glass
beer cocktail of stout and pale ale

Examples

I'll have a black and tan with my meal, please.
The bartender expertly poured a black and tan for the customer.
Many pubs in the UK and Ireland serve a traditional black and tan.

Synset Examples

I prefer a black and tan over a regular beer.
