sea lane

1. NOUN

/ si leɪn / (AmE), / siː leɪn / (BrE)
a regularly used route at sea followed by ships or other vessels
a regular shipping route at sea

Examples

The cargo ship followed the sea lane to ensure a safe and efficient journey.
International authorities monitor each sea lane to prevent collisions and piracy.
Heavy storms forced several vessels to deviate from the main sea lane yesterday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping lane - a route regularly used by ships for passage
