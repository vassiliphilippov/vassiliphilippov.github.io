sought-after

1. ADJ

/ sɔt ˈæf.tɚ / (AmE), / sɔːt ˈɑːf.tə / (BrE)
Greatly desired or in demand; wanted by many people.
greatly desired or in demand

Examples

That restaurant is one of the most sought-after places in town for dining.
Her paintings have become highly sought-after by collectors worldwide.
Tickets for the concert were sought-after and sold out within minutes.

Synset Examples

the most sought-after item was the silver candelabrum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in demand - widely desired or required
  • Antonym: unwanted - not desired or sought for

Etymology

From 'sought', the past participle of 'seek', and 'after', used together since the late 19th century.
