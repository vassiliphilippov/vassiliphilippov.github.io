nature worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪtʃɚ ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈneɪtʃə ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
a religious system in which natural forces and phenomena are regarded as deities and are worshipped
worship of natural forces or phenomena

Examples

Many ancient cultures practiced nature worship, seeing divinity in rivers, mountains, and the sun.
The festival is rooted in centuries-old traditions of nature worship.
Modern environmental movements sometimes draw inspiration from nature worship philosophies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animism - the belief that natural objects and the universe possess souls
