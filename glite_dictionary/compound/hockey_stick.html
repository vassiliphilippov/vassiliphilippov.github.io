hockey stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki stɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɒki stɪk / (BrE)
A long stick with a curved or angled blade at one end, used by players to hit and control the puck or ball in hockey.
curved stick for playing hockey

Examples

The player gripped his hockey stick tightly as he prepared to take the shot.
She bought a new hockey stick before the start of the season.
Broken hockey sticks lay scattered beside the rink after the match.
