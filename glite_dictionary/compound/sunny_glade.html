sunny glade

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌni ɡleɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌni ˈɡleɪd / (BrE)
An open, grassy area within a forest or woodland that receives plentiful sunlight.
open sunlit area in woodland

Examples

We stopped for a picnic in a sunny glade surrounded by tall pines.
A deer paused quietly in the sunny glade, searching for food.
Wildflowers bloom abundantly each spring in the sunny glade near the river.

Synset Examples

The children played in the sunny glade near the stream.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glade - an open space in a forest

Etymology

From 'sunny' (full of sunlight) + 'glade' (open space in a forest).
