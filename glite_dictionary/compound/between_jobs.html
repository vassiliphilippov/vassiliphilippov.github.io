between jobs

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈtwin dʒɑbz / (AmE), / bɪˈtwiːn dʒɒbz / (BrE)
temporarily unemployed or in the process of looking for work
temporarily unemployed

Examples

After being laid off, he found himself between jobs for several months.
Many people use the time between jobs to learn new skills or travel.
She reassured her parents that being between jobs was only temporary.

Synset Examples

I'm currently between jobs and looking for new opportunities.
She decided to travel while she was between jobs.
Being between jobs can be a stressful time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unemployed - not having a paid job but available to work
