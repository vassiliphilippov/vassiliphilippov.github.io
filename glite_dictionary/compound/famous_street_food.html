famous street food

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪməs strit fud / (AmE), / ˈfeɪməs striːt fuːd / (BrE)
a well-known local food, typically sold by vendors outdoors in public places, often considered representative of a city or region's culture
well-known local outdoor food

Examples

The city is renowned for its famous street food, attracting foodies from around the world.
Sampling famous street food is a must-do activity for visitors eager to experience authentic local flavors.
Many people associate Bangkok with its vibrant and famous street food scene.

Synset Examples

Tourists flock to Slumbai for its famous street food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: street food - food sold by vendors in public places outdoors
