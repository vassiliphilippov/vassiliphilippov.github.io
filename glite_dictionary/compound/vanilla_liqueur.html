vanilla liqueur

1. NOUN

/ vəˈnɪlə lɪˈkɜr / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlə lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A sweet alcoholic beverage flavored with vanilla.
vanilla-flavored alcoholic liqueur

Examples

Many dessert recipes call for a splash of vanilla liqueur to enhance the flavor.
She served hot chocolate with a shot of vanilla liqueur at the holiday party.
The bartender recommended a cocktail made with vanilla liqueur and espresso.

Synset Examples

I take my coffee with a drop of vanilla liqueur to taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a sweet, flavored alcoholic beverage
