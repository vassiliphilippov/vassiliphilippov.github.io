ready and waiting

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdi ənd ˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi ənd ˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fully prepared and available to be used or to act immediately
fully prepared and available

Examples

The emergency medical team was ready and waiting when the injured hikers arrived.
She kept her bags ready and waiting by the door, just in case she needed to leave quickly.
A hot meal was ready and waiting for us when we returned from our long journey.

2. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdi ənd ˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi ənd ˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
fully prepared and anticipating the start of something or expected action
fully prepared and anticipating action

Examples

The rescue crew was ready and waiting when the call for help came in.
Dinner will be served soon, so please be ready and waiting at the table.
She was ready and waiting by the phone for news of the competition.

Synset Examples

The guests were ready and waiting for the party to start.
I was ready and waiting when the delivery arrived.
The team was ready and waiting for the signal to begin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prepared - having made all the necessary arrangements or plans
  • Synonym: waiting - staying in a place until an expected event happens
