Old World least weasel

1. NOUN

/ oʊld wɜrld list ˈwiːzəl / (AmE), / əʊld wɜːld liːst ˈwiːzəl / (BrE)
a small species of weasel (Mustela nivalis) native to Europe and Asia, notable for its slender body and short legs
small European and Asian weasel species

Examples

The Old World least weasel is the smallest carnivorous mammal in the world.
Farmers often appreciate the presence of the Old World least weasel for its role in controlling rodent populations.
During winter, the fur of the Old World least weasel turns white, aiding its camouflage in snowy environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weasel - small, slender carnivorous mammal in the genus Mustela
