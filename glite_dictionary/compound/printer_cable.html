printer cable

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪn.tɚ ˌkeɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tə ˌkeɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a cable used to connect a computer to a printer for transmitting data
cable for connecting computer and printer

Examples

He realized he couldn't print his document because the printer cable was missing.
This printer cable is compatible with most modern printers and computers.
Please check if the printer cable is securely connected before troubleshooting further.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: USB cable - a cable with a USB connector used for data transfer or charging
  • Synonym: parallel cable - an older type of cable used to connect computers and printers
