telephone booth

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn buθ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌfəʊn buːθ / (BrE)
A small enclosed structure in a public place where a public telephone is installed.
small structure with a public telephone

Examples

He stepped into the telephone booth to make a call on his lunch break.
Old telephone booths are now rare in most cities due to the rise of mobile phones.
She found shelter from the rain inside a red telephone booth near the station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone box - British term for a public telephone booth
  • Synonym: call box - Another term for a public telephone booth
