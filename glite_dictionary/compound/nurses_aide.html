nurse's aide

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɝːsɪz eɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɜːsɪz eɪd / (BrE)
a person who assists a nurse with basic patient care tasks that require little formal training
person assisting a nurse with basic care

Examples

The nurse's aide helped the patient get out of bed and brought him breakfast.
She worked as a nurse's aide in the hospital during her summer break.
A nurse's aide is responsible for assisting nurses with routine patient care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orderly - hospital worker assisting with basic care and maintenance tasks
