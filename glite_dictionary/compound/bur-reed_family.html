bur-reed family

1. NOUN

/ bɝ ˈrid ˈfæməli / (AmE), / bɜː ˈriːd ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of aquatic or marsh plants comprising only the genus Sparganium, characterized by grass-like leaves and spherical flower heads
family of aquatic plants (Sparganium)

Examples

The bur-reed family includes various species found in marshes and shallow waters worldwide.
Researchers studied the bur-reed family to understand the ecological role of Sparganium plants.
With its distinctive spherical flower heads, the bur-reed family is easily recognized by botanists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sparganium - the only genus in the bur-reed family; aquatic plants with spherical flower clusters
