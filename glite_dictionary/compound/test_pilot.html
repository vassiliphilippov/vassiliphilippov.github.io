test pilot

1. NOUN

/ tɛst ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / tɛst ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
A pilot who operates experimental aircraft in order to evaluate their performance under various conditions.
pilot of experimental aircraft

Examples

Becoming a test pilot requires courage, exceptional flying skills, and a deep understanding of aeronautics.
The test pilot reported back with valuable feedback after pushing the new jet to its limits.
As a test pilot, she was the first to fly the prototype through advanced maneuvers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the flying controls of an aircraft
