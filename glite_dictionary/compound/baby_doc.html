Baby Doc

1. PROPN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi dɑk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi dɒk / (BrE)
the nickname of Jean-Claude Duvalier (1951–2014), son of François Duvalier and president of Haiti from 1971 to 1986, overthrown by a mass uprising
nickname of Haitian president Jean-Claude Duvalier

Examples

After years in power, Baby Doc was forced to flee Haiti in 1986.
Many Haitians still remember the harsh rule of Baby Doc.
The lavish lifestyle of Baby Doc contrasted sharply with the poverty in Haiti.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Papa Doc - the nickname of François Duvalier, father of Jean-Claude Duvalier and former president of Haiti

Etymology

Formed from 'baby' and doc, echoing his father's nickname 'Papa Doc'.
