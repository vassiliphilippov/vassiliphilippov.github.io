same old story

1. NOUN

/ seɪm oʊld ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / seɪm əʊld ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a situation or set of events that happens repeatedly, especially in a way that is predictable or tiresome
a familiar or repetitive situation

Examples

Every time he talks about quitting smoking, it's the same old story with no results.
It's the same old story at work—late nights and tight deadlines.
Whenever I ask her for help, I hear the same old story about how busy she is.

Synset Examples

It's the same old story every time we argue.
She sighed, knowing it was the same old story.
He’s late again; it’s the same old story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliché - an overused phrase or idea; something unoriginal and predictable
