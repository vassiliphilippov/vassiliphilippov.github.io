natural enclosure

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃɚl ɪnˈkloʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
an area of land or space that is surrounded or closed off by natural features such as hills, trees, or rocks
space naturally surrounded by features

Examples

The herd of deer rested safely within the natural enclosure formed by the river and the steep cliffs.
They discovered a small natural enclosure in the forest, perfect for a campsite.
The garden was bordered by rocks and trees, creating a peaceful natural enclosure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enclosure - an area sealed off with barriers
