gun smoke

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn smoʊk / (AmE), / ɡʌn sməʊk / (BrE)
the smoke produced when a gun is fired
smoke from firing a gun

Examples

A thick cloud of gun smoke hung in the air after the battle.
The sharp smell of gun smoke lingered long after the shots were fired.
He coughed as gun smoke drifted across the shooting range.
