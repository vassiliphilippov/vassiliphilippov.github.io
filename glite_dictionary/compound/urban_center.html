urban center

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrbən ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən ˈsentə / (BrE)
A large and densely populated city or metropolitan area, often including multiple administrative districts.
large and densely populated city area

Examples

New York is often considered the most influential urban center in the United States.
Many people migrate from rural villages to the nearest urban center seeking better job opportunities.
The development of transportation systems has connected previously isolated towns to the main urban center.

Synset Examples

Ancient Troy was a great city

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metropolis - a very large and busy city
  • Synonym: city - a large town
  • Antonym: rural area - the countryside, as opposed to a city or town
