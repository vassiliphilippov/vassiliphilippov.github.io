lever lock

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛvɚ lɑk / (AmE), / ˈliːvə lɒk / (BrE)
a lock operated by a set of levers that must be raised to specific heights to allow the bolt to move
type of lock with lever tumblers

Examples

The security of the vault depends on a complex lever lock mechanism.
A skilled locksmith was able to pick the old lever lock with ease.
Many antique safes are equipped with a sturdy lever lock for extra protection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tumbler lock - a type of lock mechanism using tumblers
