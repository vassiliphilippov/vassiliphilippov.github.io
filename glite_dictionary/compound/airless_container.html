airless container

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr.ləs kənˈteɪ.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈeə.ləs kənˈteɪ.nə / (BrE)
a sealed container from which air has been removed or excluded
sealed container without air

Examples

The airless container kept the food fresh much longer than a regular jar.
Please store the chemicals in an airless container to prevent oxidation.
After sealing the painting in an airless container, it was protected from environmental damage.
