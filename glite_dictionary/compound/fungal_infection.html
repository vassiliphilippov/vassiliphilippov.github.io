fungal infection

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌŋɡəl ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəl ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
An infection or inflammatory condition in the body caused by a fungus.
infection caused by a fungus
medical

Examples

Athlete's foot is a common fungal infection that affects the skin between the toes.
People with weakened immune systems are more susceptible to fungal infection.
The doctor prescribed antifungal medication to treat the stubborn fungal infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mycosis - fungal infection; medical term for infection caused by fungi
