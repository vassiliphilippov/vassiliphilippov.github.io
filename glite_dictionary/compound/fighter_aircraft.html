fighter aircraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪtər ˈɛr.kræft / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtə ˈeə.krɑːft / (BrE)
A high-speed military airplane designed for air-to-air combat against enemy aircraft.
military airplane for air-to-air combat

Examples

The new fighter aircraft can reach speeds of over Mach 2.
During the airshow, several fighter aircraft performed complex maneuvers.
The military invested heavily in developing advanced fighter aircraft for national defense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter jet - a jet-powered military aircraft designed for air combat
  • Antonym: bomber - a military aircraft designed to drop bombs
