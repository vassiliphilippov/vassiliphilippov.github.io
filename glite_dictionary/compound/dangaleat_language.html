Dangaleat language

1. NOUN

/ ˌdæŋɡəˈleɪət ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌdaŋɡəˈleɪət ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in Chad, notable for its seven vowels and distinctions in vowel length.
Chadic language of Chad

Examples

Linguists have studied the Dangaleat language for its unique vowel system.
There are efforts to preserve the Dangaleat language among younger generations in Chad.
The research paper presents a detailed phonological analysis of the Dangaleat language.
