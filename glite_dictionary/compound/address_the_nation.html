to address the nation

1. VERB

/ əˈdrɛs ðə ˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈdrɛs ðə ˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
to make a formal speech to the citizens of a country, usually delivered by a leader or official
make a formal speech to a nation's citizens

Examples

The president will address the nation tonight regarding the new policy changes.
After the disaster, the governor decided to address the nation to provide reassurance.
In times of crisis, it is common for leaders to address the nation on television.

Synset Examples

The Prime Minister addressed the nation from Westminster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: address the public - make a formal speech to the general public
