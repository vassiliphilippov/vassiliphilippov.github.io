Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons

1. PROPN

/ ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən fər ðə ˌprəˌhɪbɪˈʃən əv ˈkemɪkəl ˈwɛpənz / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən fə ðə ˌprəˌhɪbɪˈʃən əv ˈkemɪkəl ˈwɛpənz / (BrE)
an international organization established to promote and verify adherence to the Chemical Weapons Convention, which prohibits the use and production of chemical weapons
international body administering chemical weapons prohibition

Examples

The Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons won the Nobel Peace Prize in 2013 for its efforts to eliminate chemical arms.
Many countries collaborate with the Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons to comply with international disarmament obligations.
The Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons regularly inspects facilities to ensure the destruction of chemical stockpiles.
