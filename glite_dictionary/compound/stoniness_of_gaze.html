stoniness of gaze

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊni.nəs əv ɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˈstəʊni.nəs əv ɡeɪz / (BrE)
an unyielding or emotionless facial expression, especially as shown by one's eyes or way of looking
an unyielding or emotionless look

Examples

Her voice faltered when she met the stoniness of his gaze across the table.
The stoniness of her gaze made it clear that she would not compromise.
Under the stoniness of his gaze, everyone in the room fell silent.

Synset Examples

She noted the stoniness of his gaze as he listened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold stare - an unfriendly or emotionless look
  • Synonym: blank expression - a face showing no emotion
