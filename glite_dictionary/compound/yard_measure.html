yard measure

1. NOUN

/ jɑrd ˈmɛʒər / (AmE), / jɑːd ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a ruler or tape that is exactly one yard (three feet) in length, used to measure distance or fabric
a ruler or tape one yard long

Examples

The tailor used a yard measure to check the length of the fabric.
Every classroom should have a yard measure for math and craft projects.
She bought a new yard measure for her sewing business.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yardstick - a measuring stick one yard in length
