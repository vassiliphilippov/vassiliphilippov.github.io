meat rack

1. NOUN

/ mit ræk / (AmE), / miːt ræk / (BrE)
a display area or structure for holding and presenting meat products, typically in a butcher's shop or supermarket
display area for meat products

Examples

The butcher arranged fresh steaks carefully on the meat rack every morning.
Shoppers browsed the meat rack for the best cuts of beef and pork.
Due to a refrigeration fault, the meat rack had to be emptied and cleaned.

Synset Examples

There was an impressive selection on the meat rack today.
