statistical procedure

1. NOUN

/ stəˈtɪstɪkəl prəˈsiːdʒər / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a method or set of steps for analyzing or representing statistical data
method for analyzing statistical data

Examples

The researcher used a statistical procedure to interpret the survey results.
Understanding the appropriate statistical procedure is essential for accurate data analysis.
Multiple statistical procedures were applied to ensure the validity of the findings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: statistical method - a technique for collecting, analyzing, interpreting, or presenting statistical data
  • Synonym: statistical analysis - the process of analyzing data using statistics
