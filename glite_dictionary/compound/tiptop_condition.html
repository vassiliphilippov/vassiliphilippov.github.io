tiptop condition

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪpˌtɑp kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɪptɒp kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
in a state that is excellent or at its best possible quality or working order.
excellent or optimal state

Examples

After years of restoration, the classic car is now in tiptop condition.
She keeps her bicycle in tiptop condition with frequent tune-ups.
The hotel room was clean and in tiptop condition when we arrived.

Synset Examples

He keeps his car in tiptop condition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pristine condition - in perfect, untouched state
  • Antonym: poor condition - in a bad or deteriorated state
