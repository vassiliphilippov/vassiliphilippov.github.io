power drill

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər drɪl / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə drɪl / (BrE)
an electric or battery-powered tool used for making holes in hard materials such as wood, metal, or stone
electric tool for drilling holes

Examples

He used a power drill to assemble the furniture quickly and efficiently.
Make sure the power drill is fully charged before starting the project.
The carpenter reached for his power drill to bore holes in the wooden plank.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drill - a tool or machine with a rotating cutting tip for making holes
