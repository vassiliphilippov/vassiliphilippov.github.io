wide awake

1. ADJ

/ waɪd əˈweɪk / (AmE), / waɪd əˈweɪk / (BrE)
fully alert and not at all sleepy
fully alert; not sleepy

Examples

After the loud thunderstorm, I was wide awake for hours.
He drank a cup of strong tea to stay wide awake during the meeting.
Children are often wide awake early on Christmas morning.

Synset Examples

After that coffee, I'm wide awake now.

I was wide awake after that strong coffee.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: asleep - in a state of sleep
