glove puppet

1. NOUN

/ ɡlʌv ˈpʌp.ɪt / (AmE), / ɡlʌv ˈpʌp.ɪt / (BrE)
A puppet with a cloth body and a hollow head that is worn over the hand, controlled by the fingers inside.
puppet worn over the hand

Examples

The children enjoyed putting on a show with their glove puppet of a dragon.
She made a glove puppet from felt and buttons for the school play.
Each glove puppet had a different face, making the performance lively and fun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hand puppet - puppet controlled by hand inside
