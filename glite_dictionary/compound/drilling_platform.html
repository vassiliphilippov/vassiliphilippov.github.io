drilling platform

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪlɪŋ ˌplæt.fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈdrɪlɪŋ ˌplæt.fɔːm / (BrE)
an offshore structure, either floating or fixed to the seabed, used to support the equipment and personnel needed for drilling oil wells.
offshore structure for drilling oil wells

Examples

The workers spent months on the drilling platform extracting oil from beneath the seabed.
A new drilling platform was installed off the coast to boost production.
Safety regulations on a drilling platform are extremely strict due to the hazardous environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil rig - a structure with equipment for drilling oil wells
