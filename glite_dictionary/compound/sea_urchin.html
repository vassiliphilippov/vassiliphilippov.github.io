sea urchin

1. NOUN

/ si ˈɝː.tʃɪn / (AmE), / si ˈɜː.tʃɪn / (BrE)
A small, round marine animal with a spiny shell, commonly found in shallow waters.
spiny-shelled marine animal

Examples

While snorkeling, we spotted a colorful sea urchin nestled among the rocks.
Many species of sea urchin feed on algae growing on the ocean floor.
Some coastal cuisines serve dishes featuring raw sea urchin as a delicacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urchin - a small marine animal with a spiny shell
