velvety-furred

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɛlvəti fɜrd / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvəti fɜːd / (BrE)
(of an animal or object) having fur that feels soft and smooth like velvet.
having soft, velvet-like fur

Examples

The velvety-furred rabbit sat quietly in the grass, barely making a sound.
Many people are drawn to chinchillas because of their velvety-furred coats.
Scientists observed a rare, velvety-furred mole emerging from its burrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: velvety - having a soft and smooth texture like velvet
