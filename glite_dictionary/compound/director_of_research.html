director of research

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛktɚ əv rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktə əv rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a person responsible for overseeing and managing research activities within an organization or research center
supervisor in a research center

Examples

She was appointed as the director of research at the institute, tasked with leading several scientific projects.
The director of research oversees the work of dozens of scientists and ensures the quality of their findings.
As director of research, he set the strategic direction for the company's innovation efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: research director - person in charge of research activities
