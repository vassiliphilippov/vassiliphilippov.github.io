social issue

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈɪʃuː / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
a problem or concern that influences and is opposed by a considerable number of people within a society
problem affecting society

Examples

Poverty is often considered a major social issue that needs urgent attention.
Many politicians debate social issues such as healthcare and education reform.
The documentary raised awareness about environmental protection as a pressing social issue.

Synset Examples

Leftbook is a great platform for discussing social issues.
The gaytheist group met to discuss social issues impacting their community.
The article criticized the conservaloon's stance on social issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social problem - another term for a problem that affects many people in society
