soda fountain

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌfaʊntən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌfaʊntən / (BrE)
a counter or small establishment where ice cream, sodas, and sundaes are prepared and served, especially popular in the United States in the 20th century
counter serving ice cream and sodas
american

Examples

We sat at the soda fountain and ordered chocolate sundaes after school.
The old-fashioned soda fountain is now a nostalgic attraction in many small towns.
Children used to gather at the local soda fountain for milkshakes and friendly conversation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice cream parlor - a shop specializing in selling ice cream and related desserts

2. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊdə ˌfaʊntən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌfaʊntən / (BrE)
a device or counter for dispensing carbonated drinks, typically found in cafes or drugstores
apparatus for dispensing carbonated drinks

Examples

The old pharmacy still has a soda fountain where customers can order milkshakes and root beer floats.
Children gathered around the soda fountain on hot summer afternoons for a cool drink.
He took a job at the local soda fountain, serving sodas and ice cream to customers.

Related Terms
