pink fivecorner

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈfaɪvkɔːrnər / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈfaɪvkɔːnə / (BrE)
A heathlike shrub native to southwestern Australia, cultivated for its sharply scented foliage and pink flowers, followed by pentagonal fruit.
Australian shrub with pink flowers and five-sided fruit

Examples

The botanical garden features a beautiful display of pink fivecorner in bloom each spring.
Gardeners prize the pink fivecorner for its uniquely shaped fruit and fragrant leaves.
During our trip to Western Australia, we spotted several wild pink fivecorner shrubs along the trail.
