G-woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female special agent of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)
female FBI special agent

Examples

The story follows a brave G-woman as she investigates a high-profile case.
Aspiring to be a G-woman, she enrolled in criminology courses at university.
After years of hard work, she became the first G-woman in her unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: G-man - male FBI special agent

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'G-man', where 'G' stands for 'government'.
