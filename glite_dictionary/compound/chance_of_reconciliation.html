chance of reconciliation

1. NOUN

/ tʃæns əv ˌrɛkənˌsɪliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / tʃɑːns əv ˌrɛkənsɪliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the likelihood or possibility that a relationship can be restored or repaired after a disagreement or estrangement
possibility of restoring a relationship

Examples

After the heated argument, there seemed to be little chance of reconciliation between the two friends.
Couples therapy improved their chance of reconciliation despite years of misunderstanding.
His apology raised everyone's chance of reconciliation in the office.

Synset Examples

His anger could obliterate any chance of reconciliation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconciliation - the restoration of friendly relations
