turn of phrase

1. NOUN

/ tɝn əv freɪz / (AmE), / tɜːn əv freɪz / (BrE)
a particular or distinctive way of expressing something, especially in speaking or writing
distinctive expression or wording

Examples

She has a clever turn of phrase that makes her writing enjoyable to read.
The politician's unique turn of phrase captured the attention of the audience.
His witty turn of phrase often brings humor to serious discussions.

Synset Examples

John's succinct turn of phrase persuaded her that it would not be a good idea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - a particular word or phrase used to convey an idea
