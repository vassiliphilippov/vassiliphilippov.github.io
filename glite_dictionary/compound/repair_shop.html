repair shop

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛr ʃɑp / (AmE), / rɪˈpeə ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where repairs and maintenance are carried out, especially on machinery, vehicles, or appliances.
shop for repairs and maintenance

Examples

My car broke down, so I took it to the repair shop for fixing.
The repair shop fixed my broken laptop in just one day.
During the storm, several bicycles were lined up outside the repair shop waiting for new parts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workshop - a room or building where things are manufactured or repaired
  • Synonym: garage - a place where vehicles are repaired and serviced
