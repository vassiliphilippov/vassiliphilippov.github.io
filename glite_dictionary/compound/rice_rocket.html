rice rocket

1. NOUN

/ raɪs ˈrɑk.ɪt / (AmE), / raɪs ˈrɒk.ɪt / (BrE)
(slang) A fast, small, typically Asian-manufactured car, often modified for speed and performance.
slang for a fast, small Asian car
slang american

Examples

Jake customized his rice rocket to reach impressive speeds on the highway.
The parking lot was filled with brightly painted rice rockets on Saturday night.
He takes pride in his rice rocket, which he tunes for every street race.

Synset Examples

He built a rice rocket to help transport harvests quickly.
She called her car a rice rocket because of its speed.
The rice rocket zoomed past the fields, loaded with fresh produce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hot rod - a car modified for high speed
  • Degree Higher: muscle car - a powerful American car with a large engine

Etymology

From 'rice' (referring to Asian origin, stereotypically linked to the staple food) + 'rocket' (a fast vehicle).
