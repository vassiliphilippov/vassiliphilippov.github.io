data conversion

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə kənˈvɝːʒən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə kənˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the process of changing information from one format or encoding to another, typically as part of computing or electronic data management
changing data from one format to another

Examples

The IT department performed a data conversion to move old records into the new database.
Effective data conversion ensures that information remains accurate after switching systems.
When updating the software, they encountered errors during the data conversion process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data migration - moving data between storage types, formats, or computer systems
