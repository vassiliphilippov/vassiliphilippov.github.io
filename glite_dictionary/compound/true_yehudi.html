true Yehudi

1. NOUN

/ truː jəˈhuːdi / (AmE), / truː jəˈhuːdi / (BrE)
an outstanding or master violinist, referencing Yehudi Menuhin
exceptional violinist (after Yehudi Menuhin)

Examples

Despite his youth, she called him a true Yehudi after hearing his solo performance.
Many aspire to be a true Yehudi, but few reach such mastery on the violin.
The critics unanimously agreed he played like a true Yehudi at the international music festival.

Synset Examples

He plays the violin like a true Yehudi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virtuoso - a person highly skilled in a specific artistic pursuit, especially music
  • Antonym: amateur - a person who is not highly skilled or professional, especially in the arts

Etymology

Refers to Yehudi Menuhin (1916–1999), a renowned violinist.
