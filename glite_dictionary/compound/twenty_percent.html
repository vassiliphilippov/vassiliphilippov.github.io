twenty percent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛnti pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
An amount or portion equal to one part out of every one hundred, representing 20% of a total.
twentieth part; 20% of a whole

Examples

Only twenty percent of the students passed the difficult final exam.
The company is offering employees a twenty percent discount on all products.
About twenty percent of the world's population lacks access to clean water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fifth - one part in five equal parts; 1/5
  • Degree Lower: ten percent - an amount equal to 10% of a whole
  • Degree Higher: twenty-five percent - an amount equal to 25% of a whole
