wormseed mustard

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrmˌsid ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːmsiːd ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
a slender yellow-flowered European mustard (Erysimum cheiranthoides), sometimes considered a troublesome weed and formerly used to expel intestinal worms
a yellow-flowered European mustard plant

Examples

The fields were overrun with wormseed mustard after a wet spring.
Ancient herbalists once recommended wormseed mustard as a treatment for intestinal parasites.
Gardeners struggle to eliminate wormseed mustard because it spreads quickly among crops.
