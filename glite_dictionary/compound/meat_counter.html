meat counter

1. NOUN

/ mit ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / miːt ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a counter in a store where various types of meat are displayed for sale
counter displaying meat for sale

Examples

I asked the butcher at the meat counter for a pound of fresh chicken.
She walked straight to the meat counter to buy steaks for the barbecue.
The supermarket's meat counter always has special deals on weekends.
