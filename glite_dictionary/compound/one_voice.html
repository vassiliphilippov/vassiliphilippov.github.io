one voice

1. NOUN

/ wʌn vɔɪs / (AmE), / wʌn vɔɪs / (BrE)
used to refer to agreement or unity among a group of people, especially when expressing the same opinion or decision
agreement or unity among people

Examples

The committee presented their recommendations with one voice to the board.
Citizens from all backgrounds spoke with one voice in calling for reform.
The team responded to criticism as one voice, showing their solidarity.

Synset Examples

They spoke out against injustice with one voice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unanimous - fully in agreement
