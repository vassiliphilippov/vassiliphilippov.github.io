shoestring budget

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuˌstrɪŋ ˈbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌstrɪŋ ˈbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
A budget that is very small; limited financial resources available for a project or activity.
very limited budget or resources

Examples

They launched the film on a shoestring budget, relying mostly on volunteers.
Even on a shoestring budget, the event was a huge success.
She traveled across Europe with only a shoestring budget and a backpack.

Synset Examples

They managed to start the business on a shoestring budget.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tight budget - budget with very little money available
