trans rights

1. NOUN

/ trænz raɪts / (AmE), / trænz raɪts / (BrE)
the rights and protections that ensure equal treatment and non-discrimination for transgender people
rights ensuring equality for transgender people

Examples

Many activists are campaigning for stronger trans rights legislation in their country.
She attended the march to show her support for trans rights.
Discussions about trans rights are increasingly prominent in politics and the media.

Synset Examples

The Trantifa group organized a rally to support trans rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gay rights - rights and protections for gay people
  • Synonym: civil rights - rights guaranteeing personal liberties to all citizens

Etymology

Shortened from 'transgender rights', where 'trans' is an abbreviation of 'transgender.'
