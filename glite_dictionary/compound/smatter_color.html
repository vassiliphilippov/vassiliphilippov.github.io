to smatter color

1. VERB

/ ˈsmæt̬ɚ ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsmætə ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
to apply colors in a scattered or irregular manner, typically over a surface.
apply colors in a scattered way

Examples

She decided to smatter color on the canvas to create a lively effect.
At the children's festival, volunteers smattered color over the white t-shirts for fun.
The artist likes to smatter color across the wall to make the mural more dynamic.

Synset Examples
