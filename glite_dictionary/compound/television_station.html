television station

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪvɪʒən ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A company or place that produces and transmits television broadcasts to the public.
company or place broadcasting television

Examples

She interned at a local television station during her summer break.
The television station aired the news live from the scene of the accident.
Many popular hosts started their careers at regional television stations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: TV channel - a particular frequency or virtual channel for broadcasting television programs
  • Antonym: radio station - a station for producing and transmitting radio broadcasts
