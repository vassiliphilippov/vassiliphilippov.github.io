healing herb

1. NOUN

/ ˈhilɪŋ hɝb / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪŋ hɜːb / (BrE)
a plant whose leaves are used for their medicinal properties, often as a tisane or in salads and cooking
plant with medicinal leaves; culinary uses

Examples

The local healer made a tea from a healing herb to soothe headaches.
Fresh healing herbs were added to the salad for both flavor and health benefits.
Many people grow at least one healing herb in their kitchen garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicinal herb - a plant valued for its healing or health-promoting properties
