army tank

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmi ˌtæŋk / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˌtaŋk / (BrE)
an enclosed armored military vehicle with a cannon, designed to move on caterpillar tracks and used in combat
armored military vehicle with a cannon

Examples

The old army tank was put on display at the military museum.
During the battle, an army tank rolled across the muddy field.
Children crowded around the massive army tank at the parade, marveling at its size.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - a heavy armored fighting vehicle with caterpillar tracks and a large gun
