purplish-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːplɪʃ ˈɡrin / (AmE), / ˈpɜːplɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Green in color tinged or shaded with purple.
green tinged with purple

Examples

The lighting in the room gave the walls a purplish-green hue.
She wore a scarf of shimmering purplish-green silk to the gala.
Some plants display a purplish-green tint on their leaves during early spring.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: green - a color between blue and yellow on the spectrum
  • Degree Higher: purple - a color intermediate between red and blue
