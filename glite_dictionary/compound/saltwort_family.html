saltwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌwɔrt ˈfæmɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌwəːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of plants consisting only of the genus Batis, known as saltworts, found in saline environments
family of plants (genus Batis) called saltworts

Examples

The saltwort family includes hardy plants that thrive in salty marsh environments.
Researchers are studying the distribution of the saltwort family along coastal regions.
Batis maritima is the only species in the saltwort family found in North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Batis - a genus of saltworts native to saline habitats
