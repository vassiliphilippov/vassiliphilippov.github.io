tea parlour

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈpɑːrlər / (AmE), / ti ˈpɑːlə / (BrE)
A restaurant or café where tea and light refreshments are served, typically in a relaxed setting.
café serving tea and light meals

Examples

We met at the tea parlour for afternoon snacks and a chat.
The city's oldest tea parlour still attracts tourists and locals alike.
She decorates her tea parlour with vintage china and lace tablecloths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearoom - a room or establishment where tea and light meals are served
