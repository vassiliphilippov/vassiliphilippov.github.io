sexual marketplace

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈmɑːrkɪtˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl ˈmɑːkɪtˌpleɪs / (BrE)
the environment or context in which people seek romantic or sexual partners, viewed as involving social dynamics of exchange and competition
dating and sexual relationship environment

Examples

Sociologists have studied how age and social status affect the sexual marketplace.
She wrote a book examining how technology shapes the modern sexual marketplace.
Competition can be intense in the sexual marketplace, especially in large cities.

Synset Examples

Online dating has transformed the sexual marketplace.
