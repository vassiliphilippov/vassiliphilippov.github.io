selector switch

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɛktɚ swɪtʃ / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktə swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a switch used to choose between different options or settings in a device or system
a switch for choosing among options

Examples

The technician turned the selector switch to the 'manual' setting.
You must use the selector switch on the control panel to change the operating mode.
Each channel is activated by rotating the selector switch to the desired number.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
