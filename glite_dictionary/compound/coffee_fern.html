coffee fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfi fɝn / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi fɜːn / (BrE)
an evergreen fern native to California and Baja California, known for its coffee-colored leaf stems
evergreen fern of California and Baja California

Examples

The coffee fern thrives in well-drained soils along the California coast.
Botanists noted that the coffee fern can survive drought conditions.
The glossy leaves of the coffee fern add year-round greenery to gardens.
