blueberry liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌbɛri lɪˈkɝ / (AmE), / ˈbluːbəri lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
A sweet, alcoholic beverage flavored with blueberries, blueberry juice, or blueberry extract.
sweet alcoholic drink with blueberry flavor

Examples

She added a splash of blueberry liqueur to the cocktail for a fruity twist.
The dessert was topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of blueberry liqueur.
Bartenders often use blueberry liqueur to create vibrant, flavorful mixed drinks.
