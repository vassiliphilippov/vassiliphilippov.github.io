psychical communication

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkɪkəl kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkɪkəl kəmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the transmission of information or thoughts by paranormal means, such as telepathy or extrasensory perception
paranormal transmission of information

Examples

Some researchers claim that psychical communication can occur between twins even when they are far apart.
The book explores the possibility of psychical communication with deceased loved ones through séances.
Despite numerous studies, evidence for psychical communication remains highly controversial among scientists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telepathy - direct communication of thoughts or feelings from one person to another without using speech, writing, or other normal signals
  • Synonym: extrasensory perception - perception that occurs independently of the known senses

Etymology

From Greek psykhe (soul, mind) + English communication. The term is common in parapsychology.
