oil worker

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who works in the production, extraction, or sale of petroleum
person working in petroleum industry

Examples

After graduating, he became an oil worker at a rig in the North Sea.
Many oil workers face challenging conditions during offshore drilling operations.
The company is hiring experienced oil workers to expand its production capacity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum worker - person employed in the petroleum industry
