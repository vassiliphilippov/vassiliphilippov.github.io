church bench

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ bɛntʃ / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A long bench with a back, used for seating members of the congregation in a church.
bench in a church for the congregation

Examples

The old wooden church bench creaked as the congregation stood up to sing.
She sat quietly on the church bench, lost in thought.
The craftsman restored each church bench with great care and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pew - a long bench with a back in a church for the congregation
