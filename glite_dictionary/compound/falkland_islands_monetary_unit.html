Falkland Islands monetary unit

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːklənd ˈaɪləndz məˌnɛtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːklənd ˈaɪləndz məˌnɛtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit used in the Falkland Islands, equal in value to the British pound sterling
currency unit of the Falkland Islands

Examples

The Falkland Islands monetary unit is pegged at parity with the British pound sterling.
Tourists can exchange their currency for the Falkland Islands monetary unit at local banks.
All transactions on the islands are conducted using the Falkland Islands monetary unit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Falkland Islands pound - official currency of the Falkland Islands
  • Synonym: British pound sterling - official currency of the United Kingdom, to which the Falkland Islands pound is pegged
