sea gull

1. NOUN

/ ˈsi ˌɡʌl / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌɡʌl / (BrE)
a mostly white aquatic bird with long pointed wings and short legs, commonly found near coasts and inland waters
aquatic bird with long wings, short legs

Examples

A flock of sea gulls circled above the fishing boats near the harbor.
The picnic was interrupted when a curious sea gull swooped down to steal some food.
Children enjoy feeding sea gulls at the beach during the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gull - another term for sea gull; aquatic bird of the family Laridae
