German short-haired pointer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜːrmən ˈʃɔrt heɚd ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈʃɔːt hɛəd ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
a breed of medium to large hunting dog with a short coat, originating in Germany, known for its versatility and intelligence
a breed of German hunting dog with a short coat

Examples

The German short-haired pointer is prized for its versatility in both field and water.
Her family adopted a German short-haired pointer from the animal shelter last year.
At the dog show, several German short-haired pointers competed in the sporting group.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointer - a breed of hunting dog known for pointing game birds
