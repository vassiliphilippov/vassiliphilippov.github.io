diplomatic corps

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk kɔr / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk kɔː / (BrE)
The collective body of diplomats representing foreign countries in a host nation.
body of foreign diplomats in a country

Examples

The annual reception for the diplomatic corps was attended by ambassadors from across the globe.
A new ambassador has recently joined the diplomatic corps in Washington, D.C.
The president addressed the diplomatic corps during the state banquet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomat - an official representing a country abroad

Etymology

From French corps diplomatique, with 'corps' meaning 'body'.
