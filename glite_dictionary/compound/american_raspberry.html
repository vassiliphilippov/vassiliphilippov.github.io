American raspberry

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈrazbəri / (BrE)
A raspberry plant native to North America, producing red edible berries.
North American red raspberry plant

Examples

The American raspberry thrives in temperate regions across the United States and Canada.
She added fresh American raspberries to the fruit salad for a touch of tartness.
Farmers often plant American raspberry bushes for their hardy growth and delicious fruit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - A small red edible berry and the plant producing it
