air force officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr fɔrs ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / ˈeə fɔːs ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a commissioned officer serving in the air force of a country
commissioned officer in the air force

Examples

She became the youngest air force officer to be promoted to captain.
The air force officer was responsible for overseeing flight operations at the base.
Several air force officers attended the international defense conference.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: airman - enlisted or non-commissioned member of an air force
  • Degree Higher: general - a high-ranking military officer
