sense of fakiness

1. NOUN

/ sɛns əv ˈfeɪkinəs / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈfeɪkinɪs / (BrE)
a feeling that something is insincere, unnatural, or artificial
feeling of artificiality or insincerity

Examples

There was a sense of fakiness in the way she smiled at everyone.
Despite the impressive production, a sense of fakiness lingered throughout the performance.
His apology had a sense of fakiness that made it hard to believe.

Synset Examples

His charm was overshadowed by a sense of fakiness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sense of authenticity - a feeling that something is genuine or real
  • Synonym: artificiality - the quality of being artificial or insincere
