pool player

1. NOUN

/ puːl ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / puːl ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of pool, especially one who plays skillfully or professionally
person who plays pool (cue game)

Examples

The pool player sank the eight ball with a perfect shot.
She became a skilled pool player after years of practice at the local club.
Many pool players compete in tournaments for cash prizes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billiards player - a person who plays any of the billiards games
