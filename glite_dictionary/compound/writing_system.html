writing system

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A method of representing the sounds or words of a language by means of written or printed symbols.
method of written language representation

Examples

The Chinese writing system is known for its thousands of unique characters.
English uses an alphabetic writing system based on the Latin script.
Scholars study how each writing system reflects the culture that developed it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphabet - A standard set of letters representing the basic sounds of a language
  • Synonym: orthography - The conventional spelling system of a language
