butcher boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊtʃɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a young male who works in a butcher's shop, often as an apprentice or assistant
young assistant in butcher's shop

Examples

The butcher boy wrapped up the steaks and handed them to the customer with a smile.
As a butcher boy, he started learning how to cut meat at a very young age.
The restaurant hired a new butcher boy to help with the early morning deliveries.

Synset Examples

The local butcher boy is known for his friendly service.
The butcher boy delivered fresh meat to our restaurant.
As a butcher boy, he learned the trade from his father.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: butcher - a person who sells or cuts meat
