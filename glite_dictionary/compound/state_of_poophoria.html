state of poophoria

1. NOUN

/ steɪt əv puːˈfɔːriə / (AmE), / steɪt əv puːˈfɔːriə / (BrE)
A euphoric feeling of joy or excitement, especially after defecating.
euphoria after defecation
informal slang

Examples

After finally finishing his morning routine, Tom was in a state of poophoria that lasted for hours.
She joked that nothing compares to the state of poophoria after a big meal.
Some people describe a surprising sense of relief and joy—a state of poophoria—after going to the bathroom.

Synset Examples

The team celebrated in a state of poophoria after the victory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: euphoria - a feeling of intense happiness or excitement

Etymology

Blend of 'poop' and 'euphoria', modeled on the pattern 'state of euphoria'.
