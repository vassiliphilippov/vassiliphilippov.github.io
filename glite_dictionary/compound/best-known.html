best-known

1. ADJ

/ bɛst ˈnoʊn / (AmE), / bɛst ˈnəʊn / (BrE)
most famous or widely recognized; most familiar to the public
most famous or widely recognized

Examples

The Mona Lisa is Leonardo da Vinci's best-known painting.
Her best-known novel won several literary awards.
The scientist is best-known for his research on renewable energy.
