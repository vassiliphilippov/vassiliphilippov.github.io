bush tit

1. NOUN

/ bʊʃ tɪt / (AmE), / bʊʃ tɪt / (BrE)
a small, active grey songbird of western North America, belonging to the long-tailed tit family (Aegithalidae)
small grey songbird of western North America

Examples

A flock of bush tits darted among the branches, searching for insects.
The bush tit is known for building elaborate hanging nests.
I spotted a pair of bush tits while hiking in California.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tit - small songbird of the family Paridae or related families
