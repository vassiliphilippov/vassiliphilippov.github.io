Livonian-speaking

1. ADJ

/ lɪˈvoʊniən ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / lɪˈvəʊniən ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to communicate in the Livonian language, either as a native or learned speaker.
able to speak Livonian

Examples

Only a handful of Livonian-speaking people remain in Latvia today.
The museum features audio recordings made by Livonian-speaking elders.
He grew up in a Livonian-speaking village along the Baltic Sea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Livonian - the Uralic language of the Livonian people
