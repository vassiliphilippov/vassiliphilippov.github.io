office block

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɪs blɑːk / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ɪs blɒk / (BrE)
A large building that contains many offices, usually rented out to different businesses or organizations.
building containing many offices

Examples

The company moved its headquarters to a modern office block in the city center.
Several tech startups occupy the office block across the street from the station.
The architect designed the office block with environmentally-friendly features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office building - a building containing offices where work is done
