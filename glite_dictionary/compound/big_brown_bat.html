big brown bat

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ braʊn bæt / (AmE), / bɪɡ braʊn bæt / (BrE)
A rather large, widely distributed species of brown bat found in North America (Eptesicus fuscus).
large North American brown bat species

Examples

The big brown bat is often seen hunting insects around streetlights at dusk.
Researchers tracked the migration patterns of the big brown bat using radio transmitters.
A big brown bat can eat up to half its body weight in insects in one night.
