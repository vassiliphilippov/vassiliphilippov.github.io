golden hamster

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈhæmstɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈhæmstə / (BrE)
a small, light-colored species of hamster, originally from Syria, often kept as a pet
a small pet hamster species

Examples

My daughter loves taking care of her golden hamster and cleaning its cage every week.
The golden hamster is popular in many households because of its friendly temperament.
Some science classes use the golden hamster to teach students about animal behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamster - a small rodent often kept as a pet
