bread-and-butter issue

1. NOUN

/ ˌbrɛd ən ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˌbrɛd ən ˈbʌtə ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
an issue that has a direct impact on people's financial situation or everyday living expenses
issue affecting financial well-being

Examples

Healthcare is a bread-and-butter issue for many voters in this election.
The candidates focused on bread-and-butter issues like jobs and housing costs during the debate.
Rising energy bills have become a significant bread-and-butter issue for families across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economic issue - a topic relating to the economy or finances
  • Antonym: luxury issue - an issue dealing with non-essential or extravagant matters

Etymology

From the phrase 'bread and butter', referring to basic means of living or livelihood.
