Christmas holly

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˈhɒli / (BrE)
A species of evergreen tree (Ilex opaca) with spiny leaves and red berries, commonly used as a decoration during Christmas.
evergreen tree used in Christmas decorations

Examples

We hung Christmas holly above the fireplace to add a festive touch to our living room.
The bright red berries of Christmas holly stand out beautifully against its glossy green leaves.
Many people use branches of Christmas holly to decorate their homes during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: holly - evergreen shrub or tree of the genus Ilex
