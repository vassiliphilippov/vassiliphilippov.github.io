architectural plan

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːrkəˈtɛktʃərəl plæn / (AmE), / ˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃərəl plæn / (BrE)
a detailed, scaled drawing showing the design and layout of a building or structure
scale drawing of a building's design

Examples

The city council reviewed the architectural plan before approving the new library project.
She studied the architectural plan to understand how natural light would enter each room.
Copies of the architectural plan were distributed to all contractors involved in the renovation.

Synset Examples

the plans for City Hall were on file

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blueprint - a detailed technical drawing, especially of an architectural or engineering design
