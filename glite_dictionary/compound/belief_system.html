belief system

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈlif ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / bɪˈliːf ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of principles or tenets that form the basis of a particular philosophy, religion, or perspective.
set of guiding principles or beliefs

Examples

Her political opinions are influenced by her belief system.
Different cultures often have distinct belief systems shaping their values and customs.
He questioned every assumption within his own belief system.

Synset Examples

Historians study pseudoreligion to understand modern belief systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldview - overall perspective from which one sees and interprets the world
