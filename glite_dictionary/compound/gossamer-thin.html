gossamer-thin

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑːsəmər θɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsəmə θɪn / (BrE)
extremely thin, light, and delicate, like gossamer
extremely thin and delicate

Examples

She chose a gossamer-thin shawl that barely provided any warmth.
The document was protected by a gossamer-thin layer of plastic.
His argument for leaving early was gossamer-thin, lacking any solid justification.

Synset Examples

His gossamer-thin excuses didn't convince anyone.
She wore a gossamer-thin dress that fluttered in the breeze.
The spider's web was gossamer-thin and shimmering in the light.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filmy - very thin and semi-transparent
  • Synonym: sheer - very thin, fine, and almost transparent
  • Antonym: thick - having a large distance between two opposite sides

Etymology

From 'gossamer', meaning a fine, filmy substance like a spider's web, + 'thin'.
