incurable condition

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkjʊrəbəl kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈkjʊərəbəl kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a health problem or illness that cannot be cured or permanently resolved
illness that cannot be cured

Examples

After several tests, the specialist confirmed it was an incurable condition.
Living with an incurable condition can be emotionally challenging for families.
Medical research continues to search for treatments for many incurable conditions.

Synset Examples

The doctors called it an incurable condition despite their efforts.
He was diagnosed with an incurable illness last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terminal illness - an illness that is expected to lead to death and cannot be cured
