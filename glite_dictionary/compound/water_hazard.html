water hazard

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌhæzərd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌhazəd / (BrE)
in golf, an obstacle consisting of a pond, stream, or other body of water that players must avoid
golf obstacle of a body of water

Examples

The golfer carefully aimed to avoid the water hazard near the green.
Several balls landed in the water hazard during the tournament.
A new water hazard has been added to the redesigned course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazard - danger or obstacle in a game, especially golf
