prisonbreak

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzənˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈprɪznˌbreɪk / (BrE)
an act of escaping from prison
an escape from prison

Examples

The detectives investigated the elaborate prisonbreak that occurred last night.
After the prisonbreak, police searched the surrounding area for the fugitives.
The movie is based on a daring prisonbreak that shocked the nation.

Synset Examples

the breakout was carefully planned

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakout - a forcible escape from prison or confinement
  • Degree Lower: prison - a place where people are confined as punishment for crimes

Etymology

From 'prison' (a place of confinement) and 'break' (an act of breaking out).
