tenpin bowling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnˌpɪn ˈboʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌpɪn ˈbəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
A game in which players roll a heavy ball down a lane to knock over ten wooden pins arranged in a triangular pattern.
game of rolling a ball to knock over ten pins

Examples

Many families enjoy going tenpin bowling together on weekends.
He set a new record for the highest score in tenpin bowling at the local alley.
Tenpin bowling has become a popular activity for children's birthday parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowling - The general game of rolling balls to knock down pins
