hot shit

1. NOUN

/ hɑt ʃɪt / (AmE), / hɒt ʃɪt / (BrE)
A person who is arrogant or overly self-confident, often believing themselves to be more important or impressive than others.
arrogant or self-important person (slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

Ever since he won that award, he acts like he's hot shit at the office.
She walked in as if she were hot shit and everyone should pay attention to her.
Don't let his attitude fool you; he's not as hot shit as he thinks.

Synset Examples

He thinks he's hot shit now that he got promoted.
Don’t let him act like he’s hot shit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big shot - an important or influential person
  • Antonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence
