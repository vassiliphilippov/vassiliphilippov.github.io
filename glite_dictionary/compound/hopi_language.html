Hopi language

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊpi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Uto-Aztecan language spoken by the Hopi people of northeastern Arizona
indigenous language of the Hopi people

Examples

There are ongoing efforts to preserve the Hopi language among younger generations.
She is fluent in both English and the Hopi language.
Researchers have compiled a dictionary of the Hopi language to aid in revitalization programs.

Etymology

From 'Hopi', the name of the Hopi people, plus 'language'.
