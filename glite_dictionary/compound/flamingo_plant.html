flamingo plant

1. NOUN

/ fləˈmɪŋɡoʊ plænt / (AmE), / fləˈmɪŋɡəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical plant, often grown as a houseplant, known for its shiny leaves and bright scarlet spathe and spadix; also called anthurium.
tropical plant with bright scarlet spathe (anthurium)

Examples

The flamingo plant adds a vibrant splash of color to any living room decor.
She carefully watered her flamingo plant to keep its bright red flowers healthy.
Many people admire the glossy leaves and distinctive spathe of the flamingo plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthurium - a type of tropical plant with characteristic spathes and spadices
