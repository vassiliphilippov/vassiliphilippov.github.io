utility woman

1. NOUN

/ juˈtɪləti ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / juːˈtɪləti ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a female baseball player valued for her ability to play at several different positions
versatile female baseball player

Examples

As a utility woman, she filled in wherever the team needed her most.
The coach praised her for becoming an exceptional utility woman over the season.
Many utility women in the league are known for their adaptability and skill set.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility man - a male baseball player valued for the ability to play at several positions

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'utility man,' combining 'utility' (usefulness, versatility) and 'woman.'

2. NOUN

/ juˈtɪlɪti ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / juːˈtɪlɪti ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a female worker who is expected to perform any of various jobs as needed
female worker serving various roles

Examples

Maria was hired as the team's utility woman, ready to assist wherever she was needed.
As a utility woman, she covered for absent staff and filled gaps in multiple departments.
The company values a good utility woman who is adaptable and willing to take on new challenges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility man - a male worker expected to serve in any capacity when needed

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'utility man', combining 'utility' and 'woman'.
