pickled herring

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪkəld ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪkld ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a herring preserved in a pickling solution, typically brine or vinegar, often served as food
herring preserved in brine or vinegar

Examples

During the holidays, her grandmother would always serve pickled herring as an appetizer.
A traditional Scandinavian breakfast often includes pickled herring with rye bread.
He tried pickled herring for the first time while visiting Amsterdam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herring - an edible fish of the family Clupeidae
  • Synonym: soused herring - herring preserved in a vinegar marinade
