close up

1. ADV

/ kloʊs ʌp / (AmE), / kləʊs ʌp / (BrE)
at or from a very short distance; very near
at very short distance; very near

Examples

Without my reading glasses, I can't read anything close up.
The photographer prefers to shoot his subjects close up for dramatic effect.
Some insects are fascinating when viewed close up under a microscope.

Synset Examples

without my reading glasses I can hardly see things close up
even firing at close range he missed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearby - close in distance; not far away
  • Antonym: distant - far away in space
