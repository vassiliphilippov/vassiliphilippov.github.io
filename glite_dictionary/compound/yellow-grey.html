yellow-grey

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɛloʊˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of grey tinged with yellow; having a grey color mixed with yellow.
grey color mixed with yellow

Examples

The morning sky had a yellow-grey hue after the storm passed.
She painted the walls a subtle yellow-grey to create a calm atmosphere.
The old photograph had faded to a soft yellow-grey over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - a color between black and white
  • Synonym: yellow - the color of a ripe lemon or egg yolk
  • Degree Higher: greenish-grey - grey tinged with green
  • Degree Lower: off-white - a color close to white with a yellow or grey tinge
