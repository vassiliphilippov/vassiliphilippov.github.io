little hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l hænd / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl hand / (BrE)
the shorter hand of a clock or watch that indicates the hours
the hour hand of a clock or watch

Examples

The little hand is pointing at three, so it's three o'clock.
She taught her daughter to tell time by following the little hand on the clock.
If the little hand is between eight and nine, it's almost nine o'clock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hour hand - the hand of a clock or watch that indicates hours

2. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l hænd / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl hand / (BrE)
a small hand, especially that of a child or someone regarded with affection or innocence
a small or childlike hand

Examples

She wrapped her little hand around her father's finger for comfort.
With a little hand wave, the boy said goodbye to his friends.
His little hand barely reached the top of the kitchen counter.

Synset Examples

He gently held her little hand during the scary movie.
The toddler reached out with her little hand to grab the toy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: small hand - a hand that is small in size
