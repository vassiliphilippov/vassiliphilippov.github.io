Sweeney Todd

1. PROPN

/ ˈswiːni tɑd / (AmE), / ˈswiːni tɒd / (BrE)
a fictional barber, originally from a play, who murdered his customers; featured in literature and popular culture
fictional murderous barber character

Examples

The story of Sweeney Todd has inspired countless adaptations in theatre and film.
Many people were horrified by the gruesome deeds attributed to Sweeney Todd.
Sweeney Todd is often referenced as a classic example of a villainous antihero.
