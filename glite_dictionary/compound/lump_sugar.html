lump sugar

1. NOUN

/ lʌmp ˈʃʊɡɚ / (AmE), / lʌmp ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
refined sugar pressed into small rectangular or cube shapes, typically used for sweetening drinks
refined sugar in cube or lump form

Examples

She dropped a lump sugar into her tea and stirred it gently.
The café only served coffee with lump sugar on the side.
He prefers lump sugar to granulated sugar when he has his morning coffee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cube sugar - sugar in the form of cubes for individual use
  • Antonym: granulated sugar - sugar in the form of small loose crystals
