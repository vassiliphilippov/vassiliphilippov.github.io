web TV

1. NOUN

/ wɛb ti ˈvi / (AmE), / wɛb tiː ˈviː / (BrE)
Original television content produced for broadcast exclusively through the internet rather than traditional television channels.
original TV content broadcast on the internet

Examples

Many independent creators now release their web TV series directly on YouTube and streaming platforms.
The rise of web TV has changed how people discover new shows and genres.
She was cast in a popular web TV drama after her video went viral online.

Synset Examples

The Guild is an award winning web television series about the lives of an online guild called The Knights of Good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internet television - television content delivered via the internet
