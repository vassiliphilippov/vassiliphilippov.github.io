conference room

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns ruːm / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fər.əns ruːm / (BrE)
a room designed or used for holding conferences, meetings, or discussions, especially in an office or business setting
room used for meetings or conferences

Examples

The manager reserved the conference room for the weekly team meeting.
Please make sure to clean up the conference room after your presentation.
The company's annual review was held in their largest conference room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting room - a room for holding meetings
