Albanian language

1. NOUN

/ ælˈbeɪniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ælˈbeɪniən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The Indo-European language spoken mainly in Albania and by Albanian communities in other parts of the Balkans.
language spoken in Albania

Examples

He is fluent in both Italian and Albanian language.
Learning the Albanian language can be challenging for English speakers.
The Albanian language has its own unique alphabet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Albanian - The native or a citizen of Albania; also the language spoken there
