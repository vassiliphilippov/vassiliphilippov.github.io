corn-cracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrn ˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːn ˌkrækə / (BrE)
a device used to break or crush corn kernels, typically for food preparation or animal feed
device for crushing corn kernels

Examples

She bought a corn-cracker to make fresh corn dishes.
The farmer used a corn-cracker to prepare feed for his livestock.
Older country kitchens often included a hand-operated corn-cracker for daily use.
