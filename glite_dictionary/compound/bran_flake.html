bran flake

1. NOUN

/ bræn fleɪk / (AmE), / bræn fleɪk / (BrE)
a thin, crisp flake of breakfast cereal made from whole wheat, including the bran
wheat breakfast cereal flake

Examples

I usually eat a bowl of bran flakes with milk every morning.
The company introduced a new variety of bran flakes with added fruit.
Some people prefer bran flakes because they are high in dietary fiber.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornflake - a flake-shaped breakfast cereal made from toasted corn
