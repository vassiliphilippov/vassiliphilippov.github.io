Chrismahanukwanzakah celebration

1. NOUN

/ krɪs.mə.hɑ.nə.kwɑn.zə.kə ˌsɛl.əˈbreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / krɪs.mə.hæn.ʊ.kwæn.zə.kə ˌsɛl.ɪˈbreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a festive event that combines elements of Christmas, Hanukkah, and Kwanzaa, often intended to inclusively celebrate various winter holidays
combined celebration of Christmas, Hanukkah, and Kwanzaa
informal

Examples

This year, our office is hosting a Chrismahanukwanzakah celebration to include everyone in the festivities.
Their family holds a Chrismahanukwanzakah celebration every December to honor multiple traditions.
The school's Chrismahanukwanzakah celebration featured songs and symbols from all three holidays.

Synset Examples

The event was a vibrant Chrismahanukwanzakah celebration for all.

Etymology

Coined in early 2000s from a blend of 'Christmas,' 'Hanukkah,' and 'Kwanzaa,' with 'celebration.'
