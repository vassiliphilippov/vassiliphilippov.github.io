organ loft

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrɡən lɔft / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən lɒft / (BrE)
A raised gallery or platform in a church where the organ is placed and usually played.
raised gallery in a church for an organ

Examples

The choir sang from the organ loft at the back of the church.
The majestic sound filled the nave as the organist played from the organ loft.
Visitors admired the ornate woodwork of the old organ loft during the tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choir loft - a gallery in a church for the choir
