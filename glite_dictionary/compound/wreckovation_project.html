wreckovation project

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkəˈveɪʃən ˈprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəˈveɪʃn ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
A renovation project involving the restoration or improvement of a damaged or wrecked structure.
renovation of a damaged structure

Examples

The city launched a wreckovation project to save the old railway station from collapse.
After the hurricane, the government funded a major wreckovation project in the region.
Our team is participating in a wreckovation project to breathe new life into abandoned buildings.

Synset Examples

They started a wreckovation project to restore the historic site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renovation - the process of restoring something to a good state

Etymology

Blend of 'wreck' and 'renovation'.
