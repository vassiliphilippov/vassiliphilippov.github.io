trail riding

1. NOUN

/ treɪl ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / treɪl ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of riding a horse, bicycle, or other animal along trails or rough paths, especially for pleasure
riding on trails or rough paths

Examples

They spent the weekend trail riding through the national park.
Many people enjoy trail riding as a way to experience nature on horseback.
She joined a club dedicated to mountain bike trail riding adventures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horseback riding - riding on a horse for sport or pleasure
