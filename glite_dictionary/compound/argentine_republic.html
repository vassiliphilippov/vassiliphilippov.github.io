Argentine Republic

1. PROPN

/ ɑːrˈʤɛntaɪn rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈʤɛntaɪn rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
the official name of Argentina, a country in southern South America and the continent's second largest nation
official name for the country of Argentina

Examples

The Argentine Republic is famous for its tango music and dance.
After gaining independence, the Argentine Republic established a representative government.
Trade between Brazil and the Argentine Republic has increased significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Argentina - country in South America

Etymology

From Spanish 'República Argentina', from Latin 'argentum' (silver), referencing the Río de la Plata.
