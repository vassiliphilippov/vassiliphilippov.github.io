gigacycle per second

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪɡəˌsaɪkəl pɚ ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡəˌsaɪkəl pə ˈsekənd / (BrE)
A unit of frequency equal to one billion cycles occurring each second; equivalent to one gigahertz.
unit of frequency; one billion cycles per second

Examples

The new processor operates at a speed of 2 gigacycles per second.
Data transmission rates can now reach several gigacycles per second in advanced networks.
A frequency of one gigacycle per second is commonly referred to as one gigahertz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gigahertz - a unit of frequency equal to one billion hertz

Etymology

From giga- (prefix meaning 'billion'), cycle (from Greek 'kyklos', circle), and per second, denoting rate.
