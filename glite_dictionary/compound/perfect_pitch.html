perfect pitch

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜːrfɪkt pɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪkt pɪtʃ / (BrE)
The ability to identify or reproduce a musical note accurately without a reference tone.
ability to identify musical notes by ear

Examples

Few musicians possess perfect pitch, and most rely on relative pitch to identify notes.
Her remarkable perfect pitch allowed her to tune any instrument by ear.
Children are more likely to develop perfect pitch if they receive early musical training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolute pitch - the technical term for perfect pitch in music psychology
