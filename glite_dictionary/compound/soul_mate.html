soul mate

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊl ˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl ˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person ideally suited to another as a close friend or romantic partner, based on a profound or natural affinity
person with deep or natural affinity

Examples

He always felt that Anna was his soul mate from the moment they met.
It can take years to find your true soul mate.
Many novels tell stories of lovers searching for their soul mates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: companion - a person who spends a lot of time with another
  • Synonym: partner - a person with whom one shares an intimate relationship
  • Antonym: stranger - a person whom one does not know

Etymology

From 'soul' (the spiritual or immaterial part of a human being) + 'mate' (companion).
