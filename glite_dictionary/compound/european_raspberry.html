European raspberry

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈræzˌbɛri / (BrE)
A shrub native to Europe that produces red or orange edible berries; commonly referred to as the raspberry.
shrub with edible red or orange berries

Examples

The European raspberry thrives in temperate climates and is widely cultivated for its sweet fruit.
She picked a handful of European raspberry canes from the garden to transplant to her allotment.
Jams made from the European raspberry have a distinctive flavor much loved in desserts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raspberry - a small, soft red or black fruit
