Sultanate of Oman

1. PROPN

/ ˈsʌltəˌneɪt əv oʊˈmɑn / (AmE), / ˈsʌltənət əv əʊˈmɑːn / (BrE)
A country in the Middle East located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, known for its strategic position and oil-based economy.
country in the Middle East, Arabian Peninsula

Examples

The Sultanate of Oman has invested heavily in modernizing its infrastructure in recent years.
Many tourists visit the Sultanate of Oman to experience its diverse landscapes and historic sites.
Diplomatic relations between the Sultanate of Oman and its neighbors have remained largely stable.
