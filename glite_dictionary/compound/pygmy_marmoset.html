pygmy marmoset

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡmi ˈmɑr.moʊ.sɛt / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi ˈmɑː.mə.sɛt / (BrE)
A very small monkey native to the tropical forests of the Amazon, known as the smallest monkey species in the world.
the smallest monkey species

Examples

The pygmy marmoset can fit comfortably in the palm of your hand due to its tiny size.
Biologists observed a family of pygmy marmosets feeding on tree sap in the rainforest.
Children at the zoo were fascinated by the playful pygmy marmoset in its enclosure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marmoset - a small, tree-dwelling New World monkey
