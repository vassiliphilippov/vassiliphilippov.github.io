opera house

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpərə haʊs / (AmE), / ˈɒpərə haʊs / (BrE)
A theatre or building designed for the performance of operas and other musical dramas.
theatre for opera performances

Examples

The Sydney opera house is known for its distinctive architecture and world-class performances.
She attended an unforgettable performance at the city's historic opera house.
Most large European cities have at least one opera house that hosts international artists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatre - a building where plays and other performances are staged
