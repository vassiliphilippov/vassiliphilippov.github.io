scientific discipline

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈɛntɪfɪk ˈdɪsəˌplɪn / (AmE), / saɪˈɛntɪfɪk ˈdɪsɪplɪn / (BrE)
A particular branch of scientific study or knowledge, such as physics, chemistry, or biology.
branch of scientific study

Examples

Biology is a scientific discipline that explores living organisms and ecosystems.

The conference brought together experts from every major scientific discipline.
