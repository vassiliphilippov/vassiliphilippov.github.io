cat's feet

1. NOUN

/ kæts fit / (AmE), / kæts fiːt / (BrE)
a low-growing perennial herb with leaves covered in whitish down and clusters of small white flowers
a perennial herb with white-downy leaves and small white flowers

Examples

The meadow was dotted with patches of cat's feet among the grasses.
Gardeners often plant cat's feet as a ground cover for its attractive foliage.
She bent down to study the cluster of cat's feet blooming near the path.
