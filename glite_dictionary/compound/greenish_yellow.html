greenish yellow

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnɪʃ ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnɪʃ ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a shade of yellow that is tinged with green
yellow with a hint of green

Examples

The walls of the kitchen were painted a soft greenish yellow that brightened the space.
She wore a scarf in greenish yellow, which complemented her olive complexion.
Leaves turning greenish yellow often indicate a lack of nutrients in the soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow-green - a color between yellow and green
