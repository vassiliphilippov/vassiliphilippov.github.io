papish remark

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpɪʃ rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpɪʃ rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
A comment that resembles or relates to papists (Roman Catholics), often used in a derogatory or disparaging way.
derogatory comment about papists/Catholics
offensive archaic

Examples

His papish remark drew criticism from both sides of the political spectrum.
Avoid making papish remarks during discussions about religion.
The novel was filled with papish remarks reflecting the prejudices of that era.

Synset Examples

Her papish remarks made the debate uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: papist - a derogatory term for a Roman Catholic

Etymology

From 'papist', a derogatory term for Roman Catholics; 'papish' is the adjectival form.
