Hershey bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɜːrʃi bɑr / (AmE), / ˈhɜːʃi bɑː / (BrE)
A rectangular bar of milk chocolate manufactured by the Hershey company, popular as a confectionery treat.
chocolate bar by Hershey

Examples

She handed me a Hershey bar as a snack during our road trip.
The recipe calls for two Hershey bars melted into the mixture.
He loves to enjoy a Hershey bar with a glass of milk in the evening.

Etymology

Named after the Hershey Company, founded by Milton S. Hershey.
