trumpet tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌmpət tri / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪt triː / (BrE)
a tropical American tree with large shield-shaped leaves and hollow stems, often found in rainforests
tropical American tree with hollow stems

Examples

The trumpet tree is valued for its lightweight, hollow wood, which is sometimes used to make musical instruments.
After the storm, a large branch fell from the old trumpet tree in the village square.
Many species of birds nest in the broad leaves of the trumpet tree.
