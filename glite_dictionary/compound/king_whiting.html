king whiting

1. NOUN

/ kɪŋ ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈwaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a species of whiting found along the southeastern coast of North America, valued as a food fish
a whiting fish of the southeastern US coast

Examples

Fishermen along the Atlantic coast often catch king whiting during the summer months.
The restaurant's special today is grilled king whiting with lemon and herbs.
The biologist identified the specimen as a young king whiting by its distinct coloring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whiting - a common name for various species of fish in the cod family
