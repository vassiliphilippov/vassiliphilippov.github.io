diplomatic pouch

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk paʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a container used by diplomats for official correspondence and documents, which is protected from search under international law
secure bag for diplomatic mail

Examples

The ambassador received vital instructions in a diplomatic pouch from his government.
All contents of a diplomatic pouch are protected by international law.
The courier discreetly carried the diplomatic pouch through customs without inspection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomatic bag - alternative term for diplomatic pouch
