in condition

1. ADJ

/ ɪn kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪn kənˈdɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
physically fit and healthy, especially as a result of exercise; in good physical shape
physically fit; in good shape

Examples

After several weeks of regular training, he felt in condition for the marathon.
Athletes must stay in condition if they wish to perform at their best.
She goes jogging every morning to keep herself in condition.

Synset Examples

exercised daily to keep herself in condition

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of condition - not physically fit or healthy
