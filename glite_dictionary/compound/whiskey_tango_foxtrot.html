whiskey tango foxtrot

1. NOUN

/ ˌwɪski ˌtæŋɡoʊ ˈfɑkstrɑt / (AmE), / ˌwɪski ˌtæŋɡəʊ ˈfɒkstrɒt / (BrE)
an exclamation expressing disbelief, confusion, or astonishment, often as a humorous substitute for 'WTF'
expression of disbelief or confusion
informal american

Examples

When I saw the bill for dinner, all I could think was whiskey tango foxtrot.
He muttered 'whiskey tango foxtrot' under his breath when he saw the mess in the kitchen.
The plot twist at the end had the audience whispering 'whiskey tango foxtrot' to each other.

Related Terms

Etymology

From the NATO phonetic alphabet for the letters W, T, and F, representing the initialism 'WTF.'
