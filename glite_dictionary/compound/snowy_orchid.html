snowy orchid

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊ.i ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊ.i ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
a slender orchid of eastern North America with white, fringed flowers
a white-flowered North American orchid

Examples

The snowy orchid blooms in the wet meadows every summer, attracting many pollinators.
Botanists are working to protect the habitat of the rare snowy orchid.
We spotted a beautiful cluster of snowy orchids along the woodland trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fringed orchid - an orchid with fringed petals, related group
