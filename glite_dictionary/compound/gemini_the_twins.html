Gemini the Twins

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛmɪnaɪ ðə twɪnz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪniː ðə twɪnz / (BrE)
the third sign of the zodiac, represented by twins; the sun is in this sign from about May 21 to June 20
third sign of the zodiac

Examples

People born under Gemini the Twins are said to be curious and adaptable.
The symbol for Gemini the Twins is two figures representing twins.
She often checks her horoscope for Gemini the Twins to see what the stars predict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gemini - the third sign of the zodiac; also simply called Gemini

Etymology

From Latin 'Gemini,' meaning 'twins.'
