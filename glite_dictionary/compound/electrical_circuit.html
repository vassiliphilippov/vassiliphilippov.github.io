electrical circuit

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈsɜrkɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
A closed path or network through which electric current flows, typically consisting of wires and electrical components.
closed path for electric current

Examples

The light bulb will only turn on if the electrical circuit is complete.
Faulty wiring can cause an electrical circuit to short and become dangerous.
He studied the electrical circuit diagram before repairing the appliance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circuit - a complete path for an electric current
