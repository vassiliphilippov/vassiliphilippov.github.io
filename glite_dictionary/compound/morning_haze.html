morning haze

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ heɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ heɪz / (BrE)
a thin fog or mist that appears in the early hours of the day
fog or mist in the early morning

Examples

The morning haze lingered over the hills, giving the landscape a dreamy quality.
Driving was difficult because of the thick morning haze covering the highway.
As the sun rose higher, the morning haze gradually disappeared, revealing a clear blue sky.

Synset Examples

The morning haze made it hard to see the road.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fog - thick cloud of tiny water droplets near the ground
  • Synonym: mist - thin fog with condensation near the earth's surface
