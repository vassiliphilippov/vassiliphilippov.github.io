street shitter

1. NOUN

/ stɹit ˈʃɪtɚ / (AmE), / stɹiːt ˈʃɪtə / (BrE)
A vulgar term for a person who defecates in public places, often used as an insult.
person who defecates in public (offensive, vulgar)
offensive vulgar

Examples

The police finally caught the street shitter who had been fouling the park.
Neighbors were disgusted by the antics of the local street shitter.
No one wanted to be known as the street shitter in town.

Synset Examples

He was notorious as the street shitter in the neighborhood.
