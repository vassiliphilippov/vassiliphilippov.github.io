in reality

1. ADV

/ ɪn riˈæləti / (AmE), / ɪn riˈælɪti / (BrE)
used to emphasize the true situation as opposed to what has been assumed or appears to be the case, often introducing a contrast with expectations.
actually; in fact; true situation

Examples

Although he seems confident, in reality he feels quite insecure.
Many people thought the book was fiction, but in reality it was based on true events.
The restaurant looks expensive, but in reality the prices are quite reasonable.

Synset Examples

you may actually be doing the right thing by walking out
she actually spoke Latin
they thought they made the rules but in reality they were only puppets
people who seem stand-offish are in reality often simply nervous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actually - used to emphasize what is really true or to express surprise
  • Synonym: in fact - used to introduce the truth of a statement, often in contrast with what has been implied
