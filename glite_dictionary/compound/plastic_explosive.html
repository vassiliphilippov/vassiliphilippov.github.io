plastic explosive

1. NOUN

/ ˌplæstɪk ɪkˈsploʊsɪv / (AmE), / ˌplæstɪk ɪkˈspləʊsɪv / (BrE)
A powerful explosive material that is soft and can be easily shaped by hand to fit around an object.
moldable, powerful explosive material

Examples

The bomb squad discovered a quantity of plastic explosive hidden in the abandoned vehicle.
They used plastic explosive to demolish the old bridge safely and efficiently.
Smugglers attempted to conceal the plastic explosive inside electronic equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semtex - A well-known type of plastic explosive
  • Synonym: C-4 - A widely used type of plastic explosive, particularly by military forces
