box coat

1. NOUN

/ bɑks koʊt / (AmE), / bɒks kəʊt / (BrE)
a short, loose-fitting coat that hangs straight from the shoulders
a loose, short coat

Examples

He preferred to wear a box coat for its comfort and classic style.
The box coat was popular among coachmen in the nineteenth century.
She found a vintage box coat at the thrift store.
