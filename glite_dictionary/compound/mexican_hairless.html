Mexican hairless

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛksɪkən ˈhɛərləs / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈhɛəlɪs / (BrE)
a small, nearly hairless breed of dog native to Mexico, known for its smooth skin and ancient origins
hairless dog breed from Mexico

Examples

The Mexican hairless is known for its lack of fur and affectionate nature.
Many people with allergies prefer the Mexican hairless over other breeds.
A Mexican hairless requires special care to protect its skin from the sun.
