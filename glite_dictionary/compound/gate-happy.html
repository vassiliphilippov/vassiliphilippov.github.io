gate-happy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪt ˌhæpi / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪt ˌhæpi / (BrE)
Very excited or cheerful about traveling, especially when at an airport or embarking on a journey.
excited or cheerful about traveling
informal

His gate-happy grin made the long layover much more enjoyable for everyone.
