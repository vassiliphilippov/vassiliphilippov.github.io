priceless moment

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪsləs ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈpraɪsləs ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a moment of such great value or significance that it cannot be measured in terms of money
a moment of great value or significance

Examples

Graduating from college was a priceless moment for her and her family.
Catching the first glimpse of his newborn son was a priceless moment he would never forget.
Their laughter together on that rainy afternoon became a priceless moment in her memory.

Synset Examples

That moment was truly priceless to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memorable moment - an event or moment that is especially memorable
