tip truck

1. NOUN

/ tɪp tɹʌk / (AmE), / tɪp trʌk / (BrE)
A truck with a platform that can be tilted to unload its contents by gravity, usually used for transporting loose materials.
truck with a tilting platform for unloading

Examples

The construction site needed a tip truck to move the gravel quickly.
They hired several tip trucks to remove debris after the storm.
A driver operated the tip truck with care while unloading the sand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dump truck - a truck designed to carry and empty loose materials by tilting its platform
