Timor Sea

1. PROPN

/ ˈtiːmɔr si / (AmE), / ˈtiːmɔː siː / (BrE)
an arm of the eastern Indian Ocean located between Timor and northern Australia
sea between Timor and northern Australia

Examples

The oil reserves in the Timor Sea have sparked various economic and political disputes.
A major cyclone developed rapidly over the Timor Sea before making landfall in northern Australia.
Many migratory birds can be observed along the coasts bordering the Timor Sea.
