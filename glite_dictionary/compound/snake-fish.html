snake-fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈsneɪk ˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsneɪk ˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A tropical fish with a large mouth and a lizard-like head, found in various regions worldwide.
tropical fish with a lizard-like head

Examples

The aquarium had a snake-fish gliding slowly near the rocks.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of the snake-fish in tropical rivers.
A snake-fish can often be identified by its large mouth and elongated head.
