bellflower family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlˌflaʊər ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbɛlˌflaʊə ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants (Campanulaceae) that includes bellflowers and similar species, sometimes including the lobelia family
a family of flowering plants (Campanulaceae)

Examples

The bellflower family contains many species with striking blue or purple flowers.
Lobelia is sometimes included within the bellflower family in certain plant classifications.
Botanists study the bellflower family for its wide variety of floral structures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Campanulaceae - the scientific name of the bellflower family
