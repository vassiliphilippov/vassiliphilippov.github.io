French bracken

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈbræk.ən / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈbræk.ən / (BrE)
A large fern with deeply rooted, upright, bipinnate compound fronds, found worldwide.
species of large fern

Examples

The hillside was covered in dense French bracken.
Gardeners often struggle to remove French bracken due to its deep roots.
Botanists documented the spread of French bracken into new regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bracken - a genus of large, coarse ferns
