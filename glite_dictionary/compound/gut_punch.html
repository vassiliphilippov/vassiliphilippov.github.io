gut punch

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ɡʌt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
a sudden feeling of shock or disappointment caused by unexpected bad news or events
sudden emotional shock or disappointment
informal

Examples

The announcement of the layoffs was a gut punch to everyone in the office.
It was a real gut punch when her promotion fell through at the last minute.
Hearing about his friend's accident gave him a sudden gut punch of dread.

Synset Examples

His betrayal was a real gut punch.
Losing the match felt like a gut punch.
The news was a gut punch to the entire team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blow - a sudden shock or disappointment; setback
