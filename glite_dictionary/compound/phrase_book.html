phrase book

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪz ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈfreɪz ˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing common expressions in a foreign language with their translations, used to help travelers communicate.
book of translated foreign phrases

Examples

I carried a phrase book to help me order food in French restaurants.
Before traveling to Japan, he bought a phrase book to learn basic greetings.
Her phrase book proved invaluable when asking for directions abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictionary - a reference book containing words and their meanings or translations
