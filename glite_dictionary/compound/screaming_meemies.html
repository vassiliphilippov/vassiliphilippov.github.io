screaming meemies

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɹimiŋ ˈmiːmiz / (AmE), / ˈskɹiːmɪŋ ˈmiːmiz / (BrE)
A state of extreme nervousness, fear, or panic, often marked by uncontrolled agitation or distress.
extreme nervousness or panic
informal american

Examples

She got the screaming meemies right before her big presentation.
The horror movie gave him the screaming meemies for days afterward.
Any mention of spiders is enough to bring on the screaming meemies for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jitters - a feeling of nervousness or anxiety
  • Synonym: panic - sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety
  • Antonym: composure - the state of being calm or in control of oneself

Etymology

Originated in American English, possibly from World War I slang for shells ('screaming Mimis') causing panic.
