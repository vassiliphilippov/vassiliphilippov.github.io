local area network

1. NOUN

/ ˌloʊ.kəl ˈɛɹ.i.ə ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˌləʊ.kəl ˈeə.ri.ə ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)
A computer network that connects devices within a limited area, such as an office, school, or building.
computer network for a limited area

Examples

All the computers in the office are connected to the same local area network.
A local area network allows employees to easily share files and printers.
The IT team upgraded the local area network to improve data transfer speeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: LAN - Acronym for local area network in computing

Etymology

Formed from 'local' + 'area network', first used in computing in the late 20th century.
