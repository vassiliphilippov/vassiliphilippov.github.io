pair of tongs

1. NOUN

/ pɛr əv tɑŋz / (AmE), / peə əv tɒŋz / (BrE)
A hand tool with two hinged arms and handles, used for gripping and lifting objects, especially hot or delicate items.
tool for gripping and lifting objects

Examples

She used the pair of tongs to remove the hot tray from the oven.
A pair of tongs is essential for flipping food on the grill safely.
The blacksmith picked up the glowing metal with a sturdy pair of tongs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tongs - a tool with two arms for gripping and lifting
