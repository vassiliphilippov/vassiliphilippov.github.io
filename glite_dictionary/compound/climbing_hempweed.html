climbing hempweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmpˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈhɛmpˌwiːd / (BrE)
A tropical American herb with vanilla-scented flowers that climbs up trees.
tropical vine with vanilla-scented flowers

Examples

The climbing hempweed quickly covered the trunk of the old mango tree.
Botanists are fascinated by the sweet scent of the climbing hempweed flowers.
Gardeners often plant climbing hempweed to add fragrance and greenery to their landscapes.
