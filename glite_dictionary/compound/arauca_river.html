Arauca River

1. PROPN

/ əˈraʊkə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / əˈraʊkə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in northern South America that rises in Colombia and flows eastward into the Orinoco River in central Venezuela.
river in Colombia and Venezuela

Examples

The Arauca River forms part of the border between Colombia and Venezuela.
During the rainy season, the Arauca River is prone to flooding its banks.
Many bird species inhabit the wetlands near the Arauca River.
