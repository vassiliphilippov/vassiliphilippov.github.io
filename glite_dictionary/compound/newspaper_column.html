newspaper column

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈkɑːləm / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈkɒləm / (BrE)
an article in a newspaper, usually appearing regularly, in which a writer expresses their opinions or provides commentary on various topics
regular opinion or commentary piece in a newspaper

Examples

She publishes a weekly newspaper column on environmental issues.
His newspaper column has sparked debate among readers across the country.
Many people look forward to reading her newspaper column every Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editorial - an article giving the editor's opinion in a newspaper
  • Synonym: feature article - a longer article in a newspaper or magazine, often focused on a particular topic
