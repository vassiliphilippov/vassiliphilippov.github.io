heavily traveled

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛvəli ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈhɛvɪli ˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a road, route, etc.) used by a large number of vehicles or people; experiencing much traffic.
used by many travelers or vehicles

Examples

The heavily traveled highway was backed up for miles after the accident.
Many tourists avoid the heavily traveled areas of the city during peak season.
Repairs are scheduled for the heavily traveled bridge to minimize disruption to commuters.

Synset Examples

the region's most heavily traveled highways

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busy - (of a street, etc.) full of activity or people
  • Synonym: congested - (of roads or areas) too crowded with traffic or people
