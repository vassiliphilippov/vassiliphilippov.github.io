African golden wolf

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡoʊldən wʊlf / (AmE), / ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡəʊldən wʊlf / (BrE)
a species of canid native to northern Africa, closely related to the golden jackal and the grey wolf
canid species of northern Africa

Examples

The African golden wolf inhabits arid regions and savannas across northern Africa.
Recent studies have shown that the African golden wolf is genetically distinct from the golden jackal.
Researchers tracked the movements of several African golden wolves to better understand their hunting behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden jackal - a medium-sized canid native to southeastern Europe and Southeast Asia
  • Synonym: grey wolf - large canid native to Eurasia and North America, ancestor of the domestic dog
