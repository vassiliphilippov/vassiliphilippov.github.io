yacht race

1. NOUN

/ jɑt reɪs / (AmE), / jɒt reɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which yachts, crewed by people, race against each other over a set course
competition between racing yachts

Examples

She trained for months to compete in the annual yacht race around the island.
The yacht race attracted teams from over 20 different countries.
Weather conditions can greatly affect the outcome of a yacht race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regatta - a series of boat races, especially with yachts
