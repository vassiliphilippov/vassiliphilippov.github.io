brazen behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪzən bɪˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪzn bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
actions that are boldly shameless, without regard for normal standards of propriety or shame
shamelessly bold actions

Examples

The manager was appalled by his employee's brazen behavior during the staff meeting.
She shocked the witnesses with her brazen behavior in court.
Despite being warned, his brazen behavior continued to draw criticism from colleagues.

Synset Examples

Her brazen behavior shocked everyone at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shameless - without shame or embarrassment; bold in an improper way
