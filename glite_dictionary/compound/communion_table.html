communion table

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjun.jən ˌteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / kəˈmjuː.njən ˌteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
A table in a Christian church where the bread and wine are placed for the sacrament of Communion.
table for Communion in churches

Examples

The communion table was beautifully decorated with fresh flowers for the Sunday service.
During the ceremony, the pastor stood behind the communion table to break the bread.
Several elders gathered around the communion table to assist with distributing the elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altar - A table or flat-topped block used as the focus for a religious ritual, especially for making sacrifices or offerings
