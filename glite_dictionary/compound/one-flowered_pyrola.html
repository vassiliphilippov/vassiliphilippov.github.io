one-flowered pyrola

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌnˌflaʊərd paɪˈroʊlə / (AmE), / ˈwʌnˌflaʊəd paɪˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a small evergreen herb of northern temperate regions, with a single white flower at the top of the stem; sometimes classified in the genus Pyrola
evergreen herb with one white flower

Examples

The delicate one-flowered pyrola can be spotted in shaded northern forests during early summer.
Botanists studied a patch of one-flowered pyrola thriving along the woodland trail.
Due to its solitary bloom, the one-flowered pyrola is easy to distinguish from other Pyrola species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrola - evergreen herbaceous plant of the heath family
