team collaboration

1. NOUN

/ tim ˌkæl.əˈbɚˌeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / tiːm ˌkæl.əˈbɒɹ.eɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of working together effectively as a group to achieve shared goals
working together as a group

Examples

Effective team collaboration was crucial in completing the project ahead of schedule.
Many companies invest in tools to encourage better team collaboration among employees.
Without strong team collaboration, even skilled individuals may struggle to achieve the desired results.

Synset Examples

Using TEHM, we improved team collaboration significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common purpose
  • Synonym: teamwork - the combined actions of a group, especially when effective and efficient
