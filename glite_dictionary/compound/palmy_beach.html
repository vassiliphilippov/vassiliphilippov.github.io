palmy beach

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːlmi ˌbitʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːlmi ˌbiːtʃ / (BrE)
a beach characterized by an abundance of palm trees, often suggesting a lush and tropical environment
beach with many palm trees

Examples

We spent our afternoons relaxing on the palmy beach with a cold drink in hand.
The hotel promoted its palmy beach as the perfect getaway for honeymooners.
After days of hiking, they finally arrived at a secluded palmy beach with clear blue water.

Synset Examples

Their vacation was filled with palmy beaches and sunny skies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tropical beach - a warm, lush, sandy shoreline near the tropics

Etymology

From 'palmy', meaning 'abundant in palm trees', derived from 'palm' (tree) + '-y' (adjective suffix), and 'beach'.
