nutmeg melon

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
a variety of muskmelon with netlike markings on its rind and deep green flesh
a type of muskmelon with green flesh

Examples

The farmer grew several varieties of nutmeg melon in his greenhouse.
A ripe nutmeg melon has a sweet aroma and green, juicy flesh.
She sliced a nutmeg melon for dessert on a hot summer day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muskmelon - a species of melon with sweet, juicy flesh

2. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ˈmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌmɛɡ ˈmɛlən / (BrE)
A variety of muskmelon with a thin, netted rind and sweet green flesh.
a type of sweet, green-fleshed muskmelon

Examples

The nutmeg melon was perfectly ripe, filling the kitchen with a sweet aroma.
She sliced some nutmeg melon for the fruit salad, adding a refreshing flavor.
Farmers at the market displayed baskets of green-fleshed nutmeg melon alongside other summer fruits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muskmelon - a sweet melon with a netted rind, including cantaloupe and similar varieties
