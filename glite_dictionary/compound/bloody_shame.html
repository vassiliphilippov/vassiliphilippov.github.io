bloody shame

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌdi ʃeɪm / (AmE), / ˈblʌdi ʃeɪm / (BrE)
A non-alcoholic version of a Bloody Mary cocktail, typically made with tomato juice and standard Bloody Mary seasonings, but without alcohol.
non-alcoholic Bloody Mary

Examples

She ordered a bloody shame so she could enjoy brunch without the alcohol.
If you want a spicy drink but don't want to drink, try a bloody shame.
The bartender offered to make me a bloody shame since I was the designated driver.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Bloody Mary - a cocktail made with vodka, tomato juice, and various spices

Etymology

Adaptation of 'Bloody Mary' (the cocktail) with 'shame' added as humorous commentary on the absence of alcohol.
