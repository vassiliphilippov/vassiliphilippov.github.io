flower gardening

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ ˌɡɑr.dn̩.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ˌɡɑː.dən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of growing and caring for flowering plants, often as a hobby or for decorative purposes.
cultivation of flowering plants

Examples

She finds flower gardening to be a relaxing and rewarding hobby.
Many people take up flower gardening in the spring to brighten their yards.
Courses on flower gardening are offered at the local community center.
