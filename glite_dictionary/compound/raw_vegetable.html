raw vegetable

1. NOUN

/ rɔ ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / rɔː ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (BrE)
a vegetable that has not been cooked
uncooked vegetable

Examples

Eating a raw vegetable like carrot can provide more vitamins than its cooked form.
She prefers a salad with only raw vegetables and a light dressing.
Doctors recommend including at least one raw vegetable in your daily meals for better nutrition.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cooked vegetable - a vegetable that has been cooked
