covering letter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ ˈlɛt.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ ˈlet.ə / (BrE)
a letter sent with other documents to give additional information or explanation
letter accompanying documents

Examples

Please remember to include a covering letter with your application form.
She attached a covering letter explaining her interest in the position.
A well-written covering letter can help your CV stand out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover letter - letter accompanying a job or application; US usage
