extension ladder

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˌlædər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən ˌlædə / (BrE)
A ladder consisting of two or more sections that can be slid to increase its length.
adjustable-length ladder

Examples

The firefighter quickly raised the extension ladder to reach the second-story window.
We used an extension ladder to paint the higher parts of the house.
Always make sure the extension ladder is securely locked before climbing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ladder - a structure for climbing consisting of two side pieces and rungs
