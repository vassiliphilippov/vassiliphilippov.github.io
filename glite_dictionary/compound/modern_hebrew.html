Modern Hebrew

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɑdərn ˈhiˌbru / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən ˈhiːbruː / (BrE)
the form of the Hebrew language spoken and written in Israel today, revived from ancient Hebrew
contemporary Hebrew language of Israel

Examples

Many immigrants to Israel learn Modern Hebrew to communicate in their new country.
Modern Hebrew has incorporated words from many different languages.
She studied Modern Hebrew at university as part of her linguistics degree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hebrew - the Semitic language of the ancient Hebrews and modern Israelis
