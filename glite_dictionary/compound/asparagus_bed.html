asparagus bed

1. NOUN

/ əˈspærəɡəs bɛd / (AmE), / əˈspærəɡəs bɛd / (BrE)
a section of soil prepared and used specifically for growing asparagus plants
garden bed for growing asparagus

Examples

The old asparagus bed in the corner still produces fresh spears every spring.
She prepared the soil carefully before planting the new asparagus bed behind the shed.
Proper drainage is essential for a healthy asparagus bed.
