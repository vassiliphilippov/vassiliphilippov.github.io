Barry the Fairy

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæri ðə ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈbæri ðə ˈfeəri / (BrE)
A playful nickname for a man characterized by whimsical, magical, or flamboyant qualities, especially in performance or entertainment.
nickname for a flamboyant or magical man

Examples

Everyone called him Barry the Fairy because of his energetic and mesmerizing performances.
After his stunning show, Barry the Fairy became the star of the night.
At the children's party, Barry the Fairy entertained the crowd with tricks and jokes.

Synset Examples

Everyone called him Barry the Fairy for his magical tricks.
At the party, Barry the Fairy dazzled everyone with his show.
