cut price

1. NOUN

/ kʌt praɪs / (AmE), / kʌt prʌɪs / (BrE)
a price that is lower than the usual or standard price
reduced or discounted price

Examples

The store is offering many products at cut price this week.
After the holidays, unsold goods are often sold at a cut price.
Many shoppers wait for the cut price sales before buying electronics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount price - a price lower than the regular price
  • Antonym: full price - the normal or original price before any reductions
