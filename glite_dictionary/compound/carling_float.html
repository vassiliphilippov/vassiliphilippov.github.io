Carling float

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrlɪŋ floʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːlɪŋ fləʊt / (BrE)
a type of emergency raft used if a ship must be abandoned at sea
emergency raft for ship abandonment

Examples

The crew prepared the Carling float as a precaution during the storm.
In case of shipwreck, the Carling float could support several people.
The captain ordered the men to launch the Carling float immediately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life raft - a buoyant raft used for saving lives at sea
