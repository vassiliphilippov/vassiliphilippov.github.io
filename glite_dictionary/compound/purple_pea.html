purple pea

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl pi / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl piː / (BrE)
any of several evergreen Australian shrubs grown for their glossy green leaves and vibrant blue or violet flowers
Australian evergreen shrub with violet flowers

Examples

The purple pea is popular in gardens for its bright violet flowers.
Several species of purple pea are native to Australia and thrive in well-drained soil.
We planted a purple pea near the patio to add color all year round.
