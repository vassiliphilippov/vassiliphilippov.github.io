towel horse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊəl hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəl hɔːs / (BrE)
a rack made up of one or more bars for hanging towels
rack for hanging towels

Examples

She put the wet bathroom towels on the towel horse to dry.
The antique wooden towel horse stood next to the bathtub.
Please hang your hand towel on the towel horse after use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothes horse - a frame on which clothes are hung to dry or air
