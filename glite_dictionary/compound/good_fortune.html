good fortune

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈfɔɹ.tʃən / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈfɔː.tʃuːn / (BrE)
luck or a stroke of luck that brings positive outcomes or success.
luck; positive twist of fate

Examples

Winning the lottery was truly a stroke of good fortune for Sarah.
He attributed his success more to good fortune than to hard work.
After several setbacks, some good fortune finally came their way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luck - success or failure apparently brought by chance
  • Antonym: misfortune - bad luck or an unlucky event

2. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈfɔr.tʃuːn / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈfɔː.tʃuːn / (BrE)
luck or success resulting from favorable circumstances or chance; an auspicious state caused by positive outcomes.
luck or favorable outcomes

Examples

She attributed her promotion to a stroke of good fortune rather than just hard work.
He wished his friends good fortune on their journey across the mountains.
Winning the lottery was a sudden burst of good fortune for them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luck - success or failure apparently brought by chance rather than through one's own actions
  • Antonym: misfortune - bad luck; an unfortunate state resulting from unfavorable outcomes
