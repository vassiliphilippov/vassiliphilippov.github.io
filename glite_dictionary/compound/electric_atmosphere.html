electric atmosphere

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈat.mə.sfɪə / (BrE)
an environment or mood that is highly charged with excitement or tension
exciting or tense environment

Examples

The crowd buzzed with anticipation in the electric atmosphere before the match began.
There was an electric atmosphere in the courtroom as the verdict was about to be announced.
Her performance created an electric atmosphere that the audience will never forget.

Synset Examples

The concert created an electric atmosphere that energized everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charged atmosphere - an environment full of emotional intensity or excitement
