bummery vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌməri vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈbʌməri vaɪb / (BrE)
An atmosphere or feeling that is gloomy, depressing, or negative.
gloomy or negative atmosphere
informal

Examples

The team's bummery vibe seemed to bring down everyone's spirits during the meeting.
He tried to lighten the bummery vibe by telling a few jokes.
Despite the decorations, there was a bummery vibe at the event after the bad news.

Synset Examples

The group's bummery vibe turned the party sour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downer - Something or someone that makes people feel sad or depressed
  • Antonym: positive vibe - An atmosphere that is optimistic or cheerful
