military strength

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈstrɛŋθ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈstrɛŋθ / (BrE)
the overall capability of a country's armed forces, including personnel, equipment, and resources, to conduct military operations or wage war
the capability of a country's armed forces

Examples

The country's military strength has increased dramatically over the last decade.
Comparing military strength is essential in international relations and defense planning.
They invested in new technology to boost their overall military strength.

Synset Examples

we faced an army of great strength
politicians have neglected our military posture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military power - the capability of a nation's armed forces; similar meaning
  • Antonym: military weakness - lack of strength or capacity in military terms; opposite meaning
