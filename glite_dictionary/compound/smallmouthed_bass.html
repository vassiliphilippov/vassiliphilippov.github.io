smallmouthed bass

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɔlˌmaʊθt ˌbæs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌmaʊθt ˌbæs / (BrE)
A North American freshwater fish of the sunfish family, distinguished by a relatively small mouth and commonly sought after by anglers.
species of North American freshwater fish

Examples

The smallmouthed bass prefers clear, cool streams and lakes.
She caught a smallmouthed bass during her fishing trip last summer.
Smallmouthed bass are popular targets among sport fishermen.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: largemouth bass - a related black bass species with a larger mouth
