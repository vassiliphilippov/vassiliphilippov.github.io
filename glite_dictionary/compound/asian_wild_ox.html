Asian wild ox

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪʒən waɪld ɑks / (AmE), / ˈeɪʃ(ə)n waɪld ɒks / (BrE)
Any of several large wild oxen native to Asia, such as the gaur or banteng, belonging to the genus Bos.
a large wild ox native to Asia

Examples

The Asian wild ox is known for its massive size and curved horns.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered Asian wild ox.
You can find the Asian wild ox in the forests and grasslands of Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaur - a large wild ox of South and Southeast Asia
  • Synonym: banteng - a species of wild cattle found in Southeast Asia
