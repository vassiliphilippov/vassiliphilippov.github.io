white fringed orchid

1. NOUN

/ waɪt frɪndʒd ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / waɪt frɪndʒd ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
a bog orchid native to eastern North America, known for its spike of pure white, fringed flowers
North American orchid with white fringed flowers

Examples

The white fringed orchid blooms in wet meadows and bogs each summer.
We spotted a rare white fringed orchid while hiking through the marshland.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the white fringed orchid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a plant of the family Orchidaceae, known for its complex flowers
