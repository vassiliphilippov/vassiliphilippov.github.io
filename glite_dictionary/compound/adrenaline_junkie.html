adrenaline junkie

1. NOUN

/ əˈdrɛnəlɪn ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / əˈdrɛnəlɪn ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to excitement and seeks out thrilling activities or situations
person addicted to excitement
informal

Examples

My brother is a true adrenaline junkie who loves skydiving and mountain biking.
The movie is perfect for any adrenaline junkie craving action and suspense.
After bungee jumping, the adrenaline junkie started planning his next adventure.

Synset Examples

He's such an adrenaline junkie, always seeking the next thrill.
Being an adrenaline junkie, she loves extreme sports.
The adrenaline junkie signed up for skydiving lessons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrill-seeker - a person who enjoys taking part in exciting or dangerous activities

Etymology

From 'adrenaline' (a hormone linked to excitement) + slang use of 'junkie' (addict).
