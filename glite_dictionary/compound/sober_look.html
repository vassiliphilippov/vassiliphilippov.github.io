sober look

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊbɚ lʊk / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə lʊk / (BrE)
a facial expression that is serious or grave, showing little or no amusement or emotion
a serious or grave facial expression

Examples

The teacher gave the noisy students a sober look to quiet them down.
After hearing the news, her face took on a sober look.
He met her question with a sober look, refusing to smile or laugh.

Synset Examples

She gave him a sober look after his joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solemn look - a facial expression showing seriousness or formality
