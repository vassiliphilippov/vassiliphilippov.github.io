BW defense

1. NOUN

/ biː ˈdʌfɛns / (AmE), / biː ˈdɪˌfɛns / (BrE)
protective measures and strategies used to defend against biological warfare
defense against biological warfare

Examples

The country's military invested heavily in BW defense to protect its troops from biological attacks.
Recent research has focused on improving BW defense capabilities using advanced detection technology.
International cooperation is essential for effective BW defense against emerging bio-threats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biological warfare - the use of biological toxins or infectious agents as weapons
  • Synonym: chemical defense - defense against chemical warfare

Etymology

Abbreviation 'BW' for 'biological warfare'.
