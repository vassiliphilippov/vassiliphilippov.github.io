beaver eater

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːvər ˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːvə ˌiːtə / (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for someone who eats beaver meat or is jokingly associated with doing so
person who eats beaver meat (informal, humorous)
informal

Examples

The comedian called himself a beaver eater to get a laugh from the audience.
During the camping trip, he proudly declared he was a real beaver eater.
She brought a T-shirt that read ‘Certified Beaver Eater’ to the party.

Synset Examples

The term beaver eater made everyone laugh during lunch.
He joked about being a beaver eater at the picnic.
As a beaver eater, he loved all things related to beavers.
