grain merchant

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪn ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / ɡreɪn ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells food grains such as wheat, rice, or corn
person or business selling food grains

Examples

The grain merchant supplied local bakeries with high-quality wheat every week.
After many years as a grain merchant, he was familiar with the ups and downs of harvest seasons.
Farmers negotiated prices with the grain merchant at the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merchant - a person involved in trade or commerce
