devil tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛvəl triː / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəl triː / (BrE)
an evergreen tree of eastern Asia and the Philippines, Alstonia scholaris, with large leathery leaves and small green-white flowers in compact clusters; its bark was formerly used medicinally.
evergreen Asian tree, Alstonia scholaris

Examples

The bark of the devil tree was once valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Asian medicine.
In the garden stood a tall devil tree with glossy green leaves and delicate flowers.
Botanists studied the devil tree to learn more about its unique adaptation to tropical climates.
