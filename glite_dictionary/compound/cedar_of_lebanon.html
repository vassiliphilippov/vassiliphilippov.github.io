cedar of Lebanon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːdɚ əv ˈlɛbənən / (AmE), / ˈsiːdə əv ˈlɛbənən / (BrE)
a large cedar tree (Cedrus libani) native to Lebanon and northwestern Syria, known for attaining great age and height
a large cedar tree native to Lebanon and Syria

Examples

The ancient cedar of Lebanon stood tall against the blue sky.
Many historical buildings were constructed with wood from the cedar of Lebanon.
Conservationists are working to protect the remaining cedar of Lebanon forests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cedar - a type of coniferous tree with aromatic wood
