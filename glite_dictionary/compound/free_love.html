free love

1. NOUN

/ fri lʌv / (AmE), / friː lʌv / (BrE)
sexual relations between people who are not married to each other, especially as part of a belief in the right to sexual freedom
sexual relations outside marriage

Examples

The hippie movement of the 1960s promoted free love as part of its countercultural values.
Some people believe that free love undermines traditional family structures.
The concept of free love was considered controversial in many societies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuity - having sexual relationships with many people
  • Antonym: monogamy - having a sexual relationship with only one partner
