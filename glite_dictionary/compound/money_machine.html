money machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌni məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈmʌni məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
an enterprise or source that generates a large amount of profit with little effort or risk
highly profitable business or source of income

Examples

Thanks to its popular franchise, the studio became a true money machine in the film industry.
The social media platform turned into a money machine after introducing advertising.
Everyone wanted to invest in the tech company because it was seen as a potential money machine.

Synset Examples

His startup became a money machine almost overnight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cash cow - a product or business that consistently earns a lot of money
