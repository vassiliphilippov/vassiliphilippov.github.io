common vetchling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈvɛtʃ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a perennial Eurasian wild pea plant (Lathyrus pratensis) with yellow flowers and compressed seed pods, sometimes cultivated as forage
a Eurasian wild pea plant with yellow flowers

Examples

The field was dotted with bright yellow common vetchling among the tall grass.
Farmers sometimes grow common vetchling as a forage crop for livestock.
She identified the common vetchling by its slender stems and distinctive yellow flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meadow vetchling - another common name for Lathyrus pratensis
  • Synonym: lathyrus - genus of plants including vetchlings and sweet peas
