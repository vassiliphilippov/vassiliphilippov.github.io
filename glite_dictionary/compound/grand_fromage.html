grand fromage

1. NOUN

/ ɡræn frəˈmɑʒ / (AmE), / ɡɹɑ̃ fɹəˈmɑːʒ / (BrE)
an important or influential person; a big boss or someone in charge (often used humorously)
important or influential person
informal

Examples

After his promotion, everyone started treating him like the grand fromage at the office.
She doesn't act like the grand fromage, even though she's in charge.
Whenever there's a problem, the grand fromage is always called to solve it.

Synset Examples

He loves being the grand fromage in social gatherings.
At the meeting, he acted like the grand fromage.
Everyone knew she was the grand fromage of the organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: big cheese - an important or influential person

Etymology

French, literally 'big cheese'.
