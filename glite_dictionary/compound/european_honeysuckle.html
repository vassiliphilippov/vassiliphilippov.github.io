European honeysuckle

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈhʌniˌsʌkl / (BrE)
a Eurasian shrub (Lonicera periclymenum) cultivated for its fragrant twin yellowish-white flowers and scarlet fruit
a flowering Eurasian shrub with fragrant flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the sweet scent of European honeysuckle in bloom.
She planted a European honeysuckle near the fence to attract butterflies.
Clusters of scarlet fruit appear on the European honeysuckle after its flowers fade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeysuckle - a group of shrubs or vines with sweet-smelling flowers
