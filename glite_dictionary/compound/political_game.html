political game

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
the strategic and competitive maneuvering characteristic of politics
competitive aspect of politics

Examples

She quickly learned how to navigate the political game in her new job at city hall.
Many politicians focus more on playing the political game than serving the public interest.
The rise and fall of leaders often depend on their skill in the political game.

Synset Examples

He felt like a running dog in the political game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power play - a strategic move to gain advantage, especially in politics
