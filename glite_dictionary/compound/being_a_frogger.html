being a frogger

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːɪŋ ə ˈfrɑːɡər / (AmE), / ˈbiːɪŋ ə ˈfrɒɡə / (BrE)
Participating in a game or activity that involves crossing roads or obstacles, especially in the style of the video game 'Frogger'.
taking part in a road-crossing game
informal

Examples

After school, we pretended to be traffic and took turns being a frogger on the playground.
City safety week included activities like being a frogger to teach children about road safety.
She laughed at the challenge of being a frogger during the outdoor festival.

Synset Examples

The game was fun; I enjoyed being a frogger for a day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Frogger - classic arcade video game involving road crossing

Etymology

From the arcade game 'Frogger', first released in 1981, where the player guides a frog across a road.
