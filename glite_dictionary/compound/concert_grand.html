concert grand

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.sɚt ɡrænd / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sət ɡrænd / (BrE)
A large grand piano designed and built for professional concert performances, typically offering the highest quality and volume.
large grand piano for concerts

Examples

The pianist performed on a concert grand that filled the hall with rich, resonant tones.
A concert grand is often the centerpiece of a symphony stage due to its powerful sound.
She saved for years to purchase a used concert grand for her music studio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grand piano - a large, wing-shaped piano with horizontal strings and a large resonant soundboard
