swallow-tailed hawk

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑloʊ teɪld hɑk / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ teɪld hɔːk / (BrE)
a graceful North American kite with distinctive black-and-white plumage and a deeply forked tail
North American kite with a forked tail

Examples

We spotted a swallow-tailed hawk gliding effortlessly above the river.
The distinctive forked tail makes the swallow-tailed hawk easy to identify from a distance.
Swallow-tailed hawks migrate to South America during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kite - a bird of prey related to hawks with long wings and tail
