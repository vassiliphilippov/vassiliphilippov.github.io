corn chowder

1. NOUN

/ kɔrn ˈtʃaʊdɚ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈtʃaʊdə / (BrE)
A thick soup made with corn, milk or cream, and often other ingredients such as potatoes and onions.
thick soup with corn, often creamy

Examples

She made a delicious corn chowder for dinner using fresh ingredients from the farmers' market.
During the cold winter months, a bowl of corn chowder is especially comforting.
The restaurant's signature corn chowder is made with bacon, potatoes, and sweet corn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chowder - a thick soup, usually containing seafood or vegetables and potatoes
