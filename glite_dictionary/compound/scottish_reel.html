Scottish reel

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑːtɪʃ ril / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ riːl / (BrE)
a lively traditional Scottish dance characterized by circular movements and gliding steps, often performed to fast-paced music
lively traditional Scottish dance

Examples

The dancers performed a traditional Scottish reel at the celebration.
Learning the steps of a Scottish reel can be challenging for beginners.
A Scottish reel is often the highlight of Highland gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reel - a lively Scottish folk dance

Etymology

From 'Scottish' (of or relating to Scotland) + 'reel', which refers to both a type of lively folk dance and the music for such dances.
