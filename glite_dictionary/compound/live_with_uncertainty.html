to live with uncertainty

1. VERB

/ lɪv wɪð ʌnˈsɝtnti / (AmE), / lɪv wɪð ʌnˈsɜːtnti / (BrE)
to continue life or cope while not knowing what will happen in the future
cope without knowing the outcome

Examples

Many people have had to live with uncertainty during difficult economic times.
She learned to live with uncertainty after her diagnosis.
Entrepreneurs often live with uncertainty as they build their businesses.

Synset Examples

It's hard to live with such uncertainty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accept uncertainty - to acknowledge and be at peace with not knowing outcomes
  • Synonym: live in fear - to continue one's life while feeling afraid of future events
