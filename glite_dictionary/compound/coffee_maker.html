coffee maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfi ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to brew coffee automatically, typically in a kitchen setting
machine for brewing coffee

Examples

I bought a new coffee maker that can brew two pots at once.
Every morning, the smell of fresh coffee from the coffee maker fills the kitchen.
She set the coffee maker to start brewing before she woke up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee machine - a machine for making coffee, especially commercially
