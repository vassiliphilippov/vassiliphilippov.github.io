foot doctor

1. NOUN

/ fʊt ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / fʊt ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions of the feet; an informal term for a podiatrist
doctor specializing in foot care
informal medical

Examples

After twisting his ankle, Mark went to see a foot doctor for treatment.
My grandmother regularly visits a foot doctor to check on her bunions.
The foot doctor prescribed special orthotics to help with my flat feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: podiatrist - a medical specialist in foot care
