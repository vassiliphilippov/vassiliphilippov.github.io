horse blanket

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈblæŋkɪt / (BrE)
A large, heavy blanket used to cover a horse, often placed under the saddle or over the horse for warmth or protection.
large blanket placed on a horse

Examples

The stable hand carefully placed the horse blanket under the saddle before tacking up.
During the winter, the trainer always uses a thick horse blanket to keep the mare warm.
After a long ride, she removed the horse blanket to let the horse cool off.
