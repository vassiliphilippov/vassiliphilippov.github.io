killer bee

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪlər biː / (AmE), / ˈkɪlə biː / (BrE)
an investment banker who devises strategies to make a company less attractive to hostile takeovers
anti-takeover specialist in finance

Examples

Several top firms hired killer bees to help fend off aggressive takeover bids.
By bringing in a killer bee, the company managed to stay independent.
The board relied on a team of killer bees to develop complex defense mechanisms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white knight - a person or company that rescues a company from a hostile takeover
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪlər biː / (AmE), / ˈkɪlə biː / (BrE)
a hybrid strain of bee, originally from Brazil, known for its aggressive behavior, produced by crossing African bees with European honeybees
aggressive hybrid bee species

Examples

Many people are frightened when they hear reports of killer bees moving into new areas.
Researchers are studying how killer bees impact local ecosystems and native bee populations.
After being attacked by a swarm of killer bees, the farmer needed medical attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Africanized bee - another name for the killer bee, especially in scientific contexts
