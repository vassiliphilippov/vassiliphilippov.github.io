athletic contest

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪk ˈkɑnˌtɛst / (AmE), / əˈθletɪk ˈkɒntest / (BrE)
a competition in which athletes or teams compete in physical events or sports
a competition between athletes or teams

Examples

The city will host a major athletic contest next summer, attracting participants from all over the world.
Winning an athletic contest requires dedication, training, and teamwork.
Many schools encourage students to join at least one athletic contest each year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports competition - an event in which people compete in sports
  • Synonym: game - a competitive activity with rules, especially in sports or athletics
