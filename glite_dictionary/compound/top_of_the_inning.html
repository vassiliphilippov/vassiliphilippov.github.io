top of the inning

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɒp əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the first half of an inning in baseball or softball, when the visiting team is at bat
first half of an inning in baseball/softball

Examples
