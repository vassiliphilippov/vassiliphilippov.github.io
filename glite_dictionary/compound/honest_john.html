honest John

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑnəst ˈdʒɑn / (AmE), / ˌɒnɪst ˈdʒɒn / (BrE)
A nickname or informal term for a person who is regarded as honest and trustworthy.
a trustworthy person (nickname)
informal

Examples

Everyone knew him as honest John because he never cheated anyone.
She trusted honest John to deliver the package safely.
In our neighborhood, being called honest John is the highest compliment.

Synset Examples

Honest John always returned the lost wallets.
They called him honest John for his integrity.
Everyone trusted him; he was an honest John.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straight shooter - a person who is honest and direct
  • Antonym: dishonest - not honest; likely to lie, cheat, or steal
