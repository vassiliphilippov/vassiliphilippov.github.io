baking tray

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.kɪŋ treɪ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.kɪŋ treɪ / (BrE)
A flat, rectangular metal sheet used in an oven for baking cookies, biscuits, or other foods.
flat metal sheet for baking

Examples

Carefully place the cookies on the baking tray before putting them in the oven.
She lined the baking tray with parchment paper to prevent sticking.
After removing the baking tray from the oven, let the pastries cool for a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baking sheet - flat oven pan for baking; American English equivalent
  • Synonym: cookie sheet - flat oven pan for cookies; especially American English
