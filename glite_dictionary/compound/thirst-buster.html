thirst-buster

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜrst ˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈθɜːst ˌbʌstə / (BrE)
a drink that effectively quenches thirst, especially on a hot day or after exertion
a drink that quenches thirst

Examples

After an hour in the sun, lemonade is a perfect thirst-buster.
She grabbed a cold soda from the fridge, hoping it would be a real thirst-buster.
Many athletes reach for sports drinks as a thirst-buster during intense workouts.

Synset Examples

This drink is a great thirst-buster on hot days.
They advertised their new soda as the best thirst-buster.
Ice water is the ultimate thirst-buster after running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refreshment - a drink or snack that restores strength or energy
  • Synonym: thirst-quencher - a drink that satisfies or relieves thirst
