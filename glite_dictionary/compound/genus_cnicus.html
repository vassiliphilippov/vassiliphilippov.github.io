genus Cnicus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsaɪ.nɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsniː.kəs / (BrE)
A genus of plants in the family Asteraceae, containing a single species, blessed thistle (Cnicus benedictus).
genus of plants (blessed thistle)

Examples

The herbalist recommended using genus Cnicus for its medicinal properties.
Researchers classified blessed thistle under the genus Cnicus.
Botanical texts often reference genus Cnicus when discussing thistles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessed thistle - A plant species (Cnicus benedictus) in the genus Cnicus, known for its medicinal use

Etymology

From Latin genus (kind, type) and Cnicus, from Greek knikos (thistle).
