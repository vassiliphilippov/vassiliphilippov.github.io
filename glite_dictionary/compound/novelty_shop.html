novelty shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːvəlti ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈnɒvəlti ʃɒp / (BrE)
a shop that sells a variety of small, unusual, or amusing items, especially suitable as gifts
shop selling small gifts or curiosities

Examples

Alice bought a quirky mug at the novelty shop downtown.
Most novelty shops carry a wide range of humorous greeting cards and toys.
He runs a small novelty shop that specializes in unusual souvenirs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift shop - a shop selling items appropriate for gifts, usually souvenirs or decorative items
