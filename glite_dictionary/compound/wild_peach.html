wild peach

1. NOUN

/ waɪld pitʃ / (AmE), / waɪld piːtʃ / (BrE)
a large, much-branched shrub primarily grown for its evergreen foliage, not the true peach tree
evergreen shrub known as wild peach

Examples

The wild peach is commonly used as an ornamental plant in gardens.
Many birds are attracted to the dense foliage of the wild peach.
The wild peach shrub retains its green leaves throughout the year.
