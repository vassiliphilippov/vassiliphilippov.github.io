Asian Russia

1. PROPN

/ ˈeɪʒən ˈrʌʃə / (AmE), / ˈeɪʒ(ɪ)ən ˈrʌʃə / (BrE)
the part of Russia located in the continent of Asia, east of the Ural Mountains
the Asian part of Russia

Examples

Much of Asian Russia is covered by vast forests known as the taiga.
The population density in Asian Russia is much lower than in European Russia.
Major cities in Asian Russia include Novosibirsk, Omsk, and Vladivostok.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Siberia - the vast region of north-central and eastern Russia in Asia
