hidden booty

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪdən ˈbuːti / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən ˈbuːti / (BrE)
treasure or valuable items that have been concealed or hidden
concealed treasure or valuables

Examples

Legend says there is hidden booty in the old pirate cave.
The adventurers searched the ruins for hidden booty left behind by ancient kings.
After hours of digging, the group finally uncovered the hidden booty beneath the oak tree.

Synset Examples

They were excited to find hidden booty at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buried treasure - treasure that has been hidden in the ground
