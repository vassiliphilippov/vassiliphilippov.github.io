academic costume

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmɪk ˈkɑstum / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪk ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
A traditional formal outfit worn by faculty or students at universities or colleges during ceremonies such as graduations or inaugurations.
formal attire for academic ceremonies

Examples

The university requires all graduates to wear academic costume at the commencement ceremony.
Professors appeared in full academic costume for the annual convocation.
She rented her academic costume before the graduation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academic dress - formal clothing worn for academic ceremonies
