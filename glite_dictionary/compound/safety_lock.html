safety lock

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti lɑk / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti lɒk / (BrE)
a device fitted to a weapon to prevent it from being fired accidentally or unintentionally
locking device on a weapon for safety

Examples

Always engage the safety lock when you are not using your firearm.
The revolver's safety lock prevented the weapon from discharging by accident.
He inspected the safety lock to ensure the gun was secure before handling it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety catch - a device on a gun which prevents accidental firing
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04133474-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti lɑk / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti lɒk / (BrE)
A bolt or mechanism that prevents a door or gate from being opened from the outside, enhancing security.
bolt preventing opening from outside

Examples

Please make sure the safety lock is engaged before you leave the house.
The apartment door has a sturdy safety lock for additional protection.
He forgot to release the safety lock, so the gate wouldn't open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadbolt - a strong lock with a sliding metal bolt, often for doors
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-04133345-n
Synset POS: n
