short-run

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrt ˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌrʌn / (BrE)
relating to or lasting for a short period of time; not long-term.
lasting a short period

Examples

The company only considered short-run profits rather than long-term sustainability.
In the short-run, these policies may help reduce unemployment.
The effects of the medication are mostly short-run, lasting only a few hours.

Synset Examples

short-run planning
a short-term lease
short-term credit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-run - lasting over a long period of time
  • Synonym: short-term - lasting for a short period of time
