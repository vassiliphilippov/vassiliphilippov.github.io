daily order

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪli ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
an official command or set of commands given each day by a military commander to personnel under their authority
official daily military command(s)

Examples

The colonel reviewed the daily orders every morning with his staff.
Sailors are expected to check the daily orders for their assigned duties.
The daily order included instructions about training exercises and meal times.

Synset Examples

They ran everything and issued daily orders from the occupied Headquarters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: general order - a command issued by a military authority, usually of continuing importance
  • Synonym: special order - an order issued for a specific case, as distinguished from daily or general orders
