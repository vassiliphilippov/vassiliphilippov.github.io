wooden-top island

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdən tɑp ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈwʊd(ə)n tɒp ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
a kitchen island that has a wooden surface or countertop, often used for food preparation or dining
kitchen island with wooden surface

Examples

The centerpiece of their new kitchen is a large wooden-top island that doubles as a dining table.
She prepared bread dough on the smooth surface of the wooden-top island.
A wooden-top island adds warmth and charm to a modern, minimalist kitchen.

Synset Examples

They chose a rustic style for the kitchen with a wooden-top island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitchen island - a free-standing counter in a kitchen that is accessible from all sides
