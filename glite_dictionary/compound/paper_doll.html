paper doll

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər dɑl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə dɒl / (BrE)
a doll made from paper, typically cut or folded into the shape of a person, often used as a child's toy
a doll made from paper

Examples

When I was a child, I used to spend hours playing with my paper dolls.
She carefully cut out a paper doll from the magazine to add to her collection.
The art project for the kids involved making a colorful paper doll.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doll - a small model of a human figure used as a child's toy
