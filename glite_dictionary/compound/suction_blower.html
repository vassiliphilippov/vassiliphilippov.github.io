suction blower

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌkʃən ˈbloʊər / (AmE), / ˈsʌkʃən ˈbləʊə / (BrE)
a device that uses suction and blowing action to transport materials, especially grain, through pneumatic systems
device for pneumatic grain transport

Examples

The new suction blower increased efficiency in the grain storage facility.
Farmers often use a suction blower to transfer wheat into silos quickly and safely.
A malfunctioning suction blower can disrupt the entire pneumatic transport process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blower - a machine or device for producing a current of air
