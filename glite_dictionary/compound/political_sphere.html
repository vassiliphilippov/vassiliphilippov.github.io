political sphere

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl sfɪr / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl sfɪə / (BrE)
the domain or environment in which political activity, debate, and decision-making take place
domain of political activity

Examples

Many young people are showing renewed interest in the political sphere these days.
Debates in the political sphere often influence social attitudes and government policies.
Activists hope to bring new issues to the forefront of the political sphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political arena - a place or situation of political activity or competition
