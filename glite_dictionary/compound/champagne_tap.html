champagne tap

1. NOUN

/ ʃæmˈpeɪn tæp / (AmE), / ʃæmˈpeɪn tap / (BrE)
a device designed for dispensing champagne from a bottle or container in a controlled manner
device for dispensing champagne

Examples

The hotel banquet featured a champagne tap for the guests to enjoy unlimited refills.
He expertly attached the champagne tap to the bottle before starting the celebration.
Several elegant champagne taps lined the bar at the New Year's party.
