cock's eggs

1. NOUN

/ kɑks ɛgz / (AmE), / kɒks ɛgz / (BrE)
a weedy vine native to Argentina, with solitary white flowers and egg-shaped white or yellow fruit
Argentinian vine with egg-shaped fruit

Examples

The garden was taken over by the invasive cock's eggs, which produced unusual yellow fruit.
Local farmers warned that cock's eggs could easily spread and choke out other plants.
We examined the white blossoms of the cock's eggs vine growing along the fence.
