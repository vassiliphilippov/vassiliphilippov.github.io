unicorn startup

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːnɪˌkɔrn ˈstɑːrtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪˌkɔːn ˈstɑːtʌp / (BrE)
A privately held startup company valued at over one billion US dollars.
private startup valued over $1 billion

Examples

Many investors are eager to identify the next unicorn startup before it achieves a billion-dollar valuation.
She landed a job at a rapidly growing unicorn startup in Silicon Valley.
Becoming a unicorn startup has become a benchmark for success among tech entrepreneurs.

Synset Examples

She joined the unicorn startup that was valued at M$FT.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: decacorn - A startup valued at over $10 billion
  • Synonym: startup - A newly established business

Etymology

Unicorn, here figurative for rarity, was popularized in this sense by venture capitalist Aileen Lee in 2013.
