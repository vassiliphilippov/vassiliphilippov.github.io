blend of

1. NOUN

/ blɛnd ʌv / (AmE), / blɛnd ɒv / (BrE)
a mixture that consists of different elements or components combined together
mixture of different elements

Examples

The restaurant offers a blend of traditional and modern flavors on its menu.
His music is a unique blend of jazz and electronic sounds.
The policy is a blend of economic and environmental concerns.

Synset Examples

The movie's dialogue was a perfect blend of Hinglish humor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things
  • Synonym: combination - a result of putting things together
