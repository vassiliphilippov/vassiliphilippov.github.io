watermelon rind

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚˌmɛlən raɪnd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəmɛlən raɪnd / (BrE)
the thick outer skin of a watermelon, typically green and white, that surrounds the sweet, edible flesh
outer skin of a watermelon

Examples

After eating the sweet fruit, she threw the watermelon rind into the trash.
Pickled watermelon rind is a delicacy in some cultures.
He carefully carved a face into the watermelon rind for a summer party.

Synset Examples

He tossed the rind of the watermelon into the compost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rind - the tough outer layer of various fruits, especially citrus or melon
