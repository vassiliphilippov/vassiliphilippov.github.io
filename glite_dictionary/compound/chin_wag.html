chin wag

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪn wæɡ / (AmE), / tʃɪn waɡ / (BrE)
an informal, light conversation, especially for social occasions; a chat
informal social conversation
informal british

Examples

They sat down for a chin wag over a cup of tea.
Whenever we meet, we end up having a long chin wag.
After the meeting, everyone stayed behind for a quick chin wag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chat - an informal conversation
  • Synonym: conversation - an oral exchange of sentiments or ideas

Etymology

From 'chin' (referring to talking) + 'wag' (movement), reflecting the motion of talking.
