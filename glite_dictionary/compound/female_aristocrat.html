female aristocrat

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːˌmeɪl ˌærɪsˈtɒkrət / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl ˌærɪsˈtɒkrət / (BrE)
A woman who belongs to the aristocracy; a female member of the nobility.
woman who is an aristocrat

Examples

The novel follows a female aristocrat who defies tradition to pursue her own ambitions.
As a female aristocrat, she was expected to uphold her family's social standing.
Many paintings from the period depict female aristocrats in elaborate gowns at royal courts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aristocrat - a member of the aristocracy
  • Synonym: noblewoman - a woman of noble rank
  • Antonym: male aristocrat - a man who is an aristocrat
