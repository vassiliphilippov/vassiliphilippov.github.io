chauvinistic environment

1. NOUN

/ ʃoʊˈvɪnɪstɪk ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (AmE), / ʃəʊˈvɪnɪstɪk ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt / (BrE)
A social setting or atmosphere where attitudes or practices favoring male dominance and sexism prevail.
sexist or male-dominated setting

Examples

Many women left the company because of its chauvinistic environment.
In a chauvinistic environment, female employees may struggle to have their ideas taken seriously.
The university worked hard to change its reputation for having a chauvinistic environment.

Synset Examples

The chauvinistic environment made it hard for women to succeed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexist - displaying prejudice or discrimination based on sex, especially against women
  • Antonym: egalitarian - favoring equal rights and opportunities for all people
