family line

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli laɪn / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli laɪn / (BrE)
a group of people descended from a common ancestor; a lineage or ancestral line
line of descent from a common ancestor

Examples

The royal family line can be traced back several centuries.
She is the last surviving member of her family line.
Many traditions were passed down through generations in their family line.

Synset Examples

his family has lived in Massachusetts since the Mayflower

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lineage - direct descent from an ancestor; ancestry or pedigree
  • Synonym: bloodline - the descendants of one individual; family lineage
  • Synonym: ancestry - one's family or ethnic descent
