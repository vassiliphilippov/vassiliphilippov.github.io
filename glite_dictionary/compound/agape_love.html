agape love

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡɑːpeɪ lʌv / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːpeɪ lʌv / (BrE)
selfless, unconditional love for others, especially of a spiritual or altruistic nature, without sexual implications
spiritual or selfless love

Examples

Many religious texts discuss agape love as the highest form of devotion to others.
The charity's mission is rooted in agape love, serving people without expecting anything in return.
He strives to show agape love towards everyone, regardless of their background or beliefs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philia - affectionate regard or friendship, usually between equals

Etymology

From Greek ἀγάπη (agápē), meaning 'love' in a spiritual or selfless sense.
