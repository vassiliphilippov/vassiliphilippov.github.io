climbing onion

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈʌn.jən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈʌn.jən / (BrE)
a much-branched, leafless, twining South African plant with bright green stems and large aboveground bulbs, often grown as an ornamental
South African ornamental plant with bulbous stems

Examples

The climbing onion makes an attractive addition to a sunroom with its vivid green stems.
Gardeners often grow the climbing onion for its unusual, bulbous appearance.
Unlike true onions, the climbing onion is prized for its ornamental value rather than its culinary use.
