exchange rate

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ reɪt / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ reɪt / (BrE)
the value at which the currency of one country can be exchanged for that of another country
price for exchanging currencies

Examples

The exchange rate between the dollar and the euro has fluctuated this year.
Travelers often check the exchange rate before converting their money.
Our company lost money due to an unfavorable exchange rate last quarter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversion rate - the rate at which one currency can be exchanged for another
