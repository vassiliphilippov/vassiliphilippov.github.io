infantilizer approach

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfæntəˌlaɪzər əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfæntəlʌɪzə əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or practice of treating someone as if they are a child, often inappropriately or unnecessarily
method of treating someone as a child

Examples

The new manager avoided the infantilizer approach to encourage more independence among her staff.
Many critics argue that the infantilizer approach in elder care can reduce patients' autonomy.
Teachers who use an infantilizer approach may undermine students' confidence.

Synset Examples

She rejected the infantilizer approach in her parenting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infantilize - to treat someone as if they are a child

Etymology

From 'infantilize' (from Latin 'infantilis' meaning 'of a child') + 'approach'.
