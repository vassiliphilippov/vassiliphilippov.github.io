self-contemplation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf.kənˌtɛm.pləˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.kɒnˌtɛmplɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of examining or reflecting on one's own thoughts, desires, and behavior.
examination of one's own thoughts and feelings

Examples

Through regular self-contemplation, she gained a deeper understanding of her motivations and fears.
Many philosophers believe that self-contemplation is necessary for personal growth and moral development.
After the argument, he spent an evening in self-contemplation to reconsider his actions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-reflection - careful thought about your own behavior and beliefs
  • Synonym: introspection - the examination or observation of one's own mental processes

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' + 'contemplation', from Latin contemplatio (meditation).
