autist perspective

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔː.tɪst pɚˈspɛk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈɔː.tɪst pəˈspɛk.tɪv / (BrE)
the viewpoint or way of understanding things typical of or influenced by someone with autism
viewpoint of someone with autism

Examples
