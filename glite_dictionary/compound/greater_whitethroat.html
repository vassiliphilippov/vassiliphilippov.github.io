greater whitethroat

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪtər ˈwaɪtˌθroʊt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈwaɪtˌθrəʊt / (BrE)
a greyish-brown songbird of the warbler family, with a white throat and underparts, found in Europe and western Asia (Sylvia communis)
a greyish-brown Old World warbler with a white throat

Examples

We spotted a greater whitethroat singing in the hedgerow during our morning walk.
The greater whitethroat is known for its distinctive, scratchy song.
During migration, large numbers of greater whitethroats can be seen in southern Europe.
