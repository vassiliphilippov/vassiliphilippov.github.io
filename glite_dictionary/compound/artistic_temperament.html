artistic temperament

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪk ˈtɛmpərəmənt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪk ˈtɛmpərəmənt / (BrE)
A personality type marked by creativity, sensitivity, and emotional expressiveness, often associated with artists and creative individuals.
creative and sensitive personality type

Examples

Many famous composers were known for their artistic temperament, which sometimes created tension in their relationships.
Living with someone who has an artistic temperament can be both inspiring and challenging.
She credits her artistic temperament for enabling her to see beauty in everyday life.

Synset Examples

The director's artistic temperament made filming challenging.
He has an artistic temperament that fuels his creativity.
Her artistic temperament often led to passionate outbursts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive - easily affected emotionally
  • Synonym: creative - having the ability to create
  • Antonym: pragmatic - dealing with things realistically; practical
