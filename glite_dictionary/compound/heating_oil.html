heating oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈhitɪŋ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈhiːtɪŋ ɔɪl / (BrE)
A petroleum product used as fuel in furnaces or boilers for heating buildings.
petroleum fuel for heating

Examples

Many homes in rural areas rely on heating oil to keep warm during winter.
The price of heating oil often rises as temperatures drop.
We had the heating oil tank refilled before the first snowfall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diesel fuel - a type of fuel derived from petroleum, similar to heating oil
