turkey stew

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.ki stuː / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.ki stjuː / (BrE)
A stew prepared with turkey meat as the main ingredient, often including vegetables and seasonings.
stew made with turkey meat

Examples

My grandmother always makes turkey stew with leftover meat after Thanksgiving.
On a cold winter night, nothing is more comforting than a bowl of turkey stew.
The chef recommended serving the turkey stew with fresh bread.
