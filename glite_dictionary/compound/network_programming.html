network programming

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛtˌwɝk ˈproʊɡræmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛtwɜːk ˈprəʊɡramɪŋ / (BrE)
the schedule or lineup of television or radio programs to be broadcast by a network.
schedule of programs broadcast by a network

Examples

Prime time network programming attracts the largest audience of the evening.
The station adjusts its network programming each season to appeal to viewers.
Due to breaking news, the regular network programming was interrupted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schedule - a plan of intended events or broadcast times
