transphilic theme

1. NOUN

/ trænsˈfɪlɪk θiːm / (AmE), / tranzˈfɪlɪk θiːm / (BrE)
a topic that promotes love and acceptance of transgender individuals
topic supporting transgender acceptance

Examples

The conference included several transphilic themes to create a welcoming environment for everyone.
Her presentation addressed transphilic theme in films and literature.
Schools are beginning to adopt transphilic themes in their diversity education programs.

Synset Examples

The event focused on transphilic themes, fostering acceptance and love.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: transphobia - dislike or prejudice against transgender people
  • Synonym: transgender - identifying with a gender different from one's birth sex

Etymology

Formed from 'trans-' (across, referring to transgender) and '-philic' (loving, from Greek 'philos'), plus 'theme'.
