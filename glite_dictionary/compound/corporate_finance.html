corporate finance

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrpərət fəˈnaɪns / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərət faɪˈnæns / (BrE)
the area of finance dealing with the financial activities, strategies, and decisions of corporations, including funding, capital structure, and investment decisions
finance of corporations

Examples

She pursued a career in corporate finance after earning her MBA.
The company hired a new director to oversee corporate finance operations.
Understanding corporate finance is essential for making sound business decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance - the management of large amounts of money, especially by governments or large companies
