abdominal discomfort

1. NOUN

/ æbˈdɑmɪnəl dɪsˈkʌmfərt / (AmE), / æbˈdɒmɪnəl dɪsˈkʌmfət / (BrE)
a feeling of pain or unease in the abdomen
pain or unease in the abdomen
medical

Examples

Persistent abdominal discomfort can sometimes indicate a more serious health issue.
If you experience abdominal discomfort, it's important to consult your doctor for proper diagnosis.
