plastic wrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk ræp / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstɪk ræp / (BrE)
A very thin, transparent sheet of plastic used for covering and storing food to keep it fresh.
thin plastic sheet for food storage

Examples

She covered the leftovers with plastic wrap before putting them in the refrigerator.
It's best to use plastic wrap to keep cut fruit from drying out.
He wrapped the sandwiches tightly in plastic wrap for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cling film - thin plastic film used for sealing food items
