United States of America

1. PROPN

/ ˌjuːnaɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / juːˌnaɪ.tɪd ˈsteɪts əv əˈmer.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
a country in North America consisting of 50 states, including the contiguous states, Alaska, and Hawaii; declared independence from Britain in 1776
country in North America (50 states)

Examples

The United States of America declared its independence from Britain in 1776.
The United States of America is known for its diverse population and culture.
She traveled across the United States of America last summer, visiting several major cities.
