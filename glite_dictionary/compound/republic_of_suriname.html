Republic of Suriname

1. PROPN

/ rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsʊrəˈnɑːm / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌsʊərɪˈnɑːm / (BrE)
a country in northeastern South America, on the Atlantic coast, which gained independence from the Netherlands in 1975
country in northeastern South America

Examples

The Republic of Suriname became independent from the Netherlands in 1975.
Rainforests cover much of the Republic of Suriname, making it one of South America's most biodiverse countries.
Dutch is the official language of the Republic of Suriname.
