three times

1. ADV

/ θɹi taɪmz / (AmE), / θɹi taɪmz / (BrE)
By a factor of three; multiplied by three.
by a factor of three

Examples

The price of gasoline has increased three times since last year.
If you flip the coin three times, what are the chances of getting heads every time?
Her score was three times higher than the previous record.

Synset Examples

our rent increased threefold in the past five years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threefold - by three times as much; triply
