key staging post

1. NOUN

/ ki ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ki ˈsteɪdʒɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
an important stop or location, especially one crucial for progress in a journey or process
important stop or location

Examples

The small town became a key staging post for relief supplies after the storm.
The village acted as a key staging post for migrating birds on their long journey.
In the plan, this checkpoint was identified as a key staging post before the final ascent.

Synset Examples

This village is a key staging post on our journey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waypoint - a stopping place along a route
  • Synonym: milestone - an important event in the development of something
