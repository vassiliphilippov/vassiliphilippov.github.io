trash collection

1. NOUN

/ træʃ kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / træʃ kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of collecting and removing garbage or waste from homes, businesses, or public areas
the removal of garbage or waste
american

Examples

The city schedules trash collection every Monday morning for residents.
After the holiday, extra bags were left out for trash collection.
Residents must place their bins on the curb before trash collection begins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish collection - the process of collecting and removing waste (British equivalent)
