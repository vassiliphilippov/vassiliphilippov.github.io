tight cap

1. NOUN

/ taɪt kæp / (AmE), / taɪt kap / (BrE)
a cap that fits securely to prevent leakage or spillage
cap that seals securely

Examples

Make sure the jar has a tight cap before storing it in the cupboard.
A tight cap keeps the shampoo from spilling in your travel bag.
The medicine container came with a tight cap for child safety.

Synset Examples

The bottle had a tight cap to prevent leaks.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose cap - a cap that does not fit tightly and may allow leakage
