arty vibe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrti vaɪb / (AmE), / ˈɑːti vaɪb / (BrE)
a creative, artistic, or unconventional atmosphere or feeling often associated with art, artists, or artistic activity
creative or artistic atmosphere
informal

Examples

The downtown cafe has an arty vibe that attracts local painters and musicians.
Visitors love the arty vibe of her apartment, filled with handmade sculptures and vibrant paintings.
He prefers bars with an arty vibe rather than loud nightclubs.
