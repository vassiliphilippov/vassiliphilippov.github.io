embryonic development

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm.briˈɑn.ɪk dɪˈvɛl.əp.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪˈɒn.ɪk dɪˈvɛl.əp.mənt / (BrE)
the process by which an embryo forms and develops from fertilization to the fetal stage
biological process of embryo formation and growth
medical

Examples

Embryonic development in mammals involves several complex stages, beginning with fertilization.
Scientists are interested in how environmental factors influence embryonic development.
Abnormalities during embryonic development can result in birth defects.

Synset Examples

Researchers are studying embryonic development in frogs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embryogenesis - the formation and development of an embryo
