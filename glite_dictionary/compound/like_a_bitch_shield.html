like a bitch shield

1. ADV

/ laɪk ə bɪtʃ ʃiːld / (AmE), / laɪk ə bɪtʃ ʃiːld / (BrE)
used to describe a display or manner of strong, bold confidence that acts as a social barrier or defense.
as a display of strong confidence
informal

Examples

She enters every negotiation like a bitch shield, undaunted by criticism or resistance.
His arrogance stood out like a bitch shield among his more reserved colleagues.
Whenever she faces the press, she puts on her confidence like a bitch shield to deflect tough questions.

Synset Examples

He wore his confidence like a bitch shield.
