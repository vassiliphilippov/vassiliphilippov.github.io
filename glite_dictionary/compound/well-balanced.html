well-balanced

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈbælənst / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈbælənst / (BrE)
In an optimal state of balance or equilibrium; properly proportioned or stable.
in an optimal state of balance

Examples

A well-balanced team is essential for a successful project.
