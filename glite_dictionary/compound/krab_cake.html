krab cake

1. NOUN

/ kræb keɪk / (AmE), / kræb keɪk / (BrE)
a dish made from imitation crab meat, typically mixed with other ingredients and formed into a patty
patties made of imitation crab meat

Examples

Many diners prefer a krab cake because it is more affordable than a traditional crab cake.
The chef served the krab cake with a spicy remoulade sauce.
She learned how to make homemade krab cakes using canned imitation crab meat.

Synset Examples

He ordered a krab cake as an appetizer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab cake - a patty made from real crab meat

Etymology

Influenced by 'crab cake'; 'krab' is a brand-influenced spelling for imitation crab meat, often surimi.
