Commission on Narcotic Drugs

1. PROPN

/ kəˈmɪʃ.ən ɑn nɑrˈkɑtɪk drʌɡz / (AmE), / kəˈmɪʃ.ən ɒn nɑːˈkɒt.ɪk drʌɡz / (BrE)
the commission of the United Nations Economic and Social Council responsible for supervising international drug control and policies concerning narcotic drugs
UN body for international drug control

Examples

The Commission on Narcotic Drugs holds annual meetings to discuss global drug policy.
As a member state, Canada reports its narcotics statistics to the Commission on Narcotic Drugs.
The Commission on Narcotic Drugs recently approved new regulations on opioid medication control.
