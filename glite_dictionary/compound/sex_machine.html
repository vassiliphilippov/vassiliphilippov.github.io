sex machine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛks məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈseks məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a person who is highly sexual or attractive, especially one who is regarded as skillful or confident in sexual contexts
a highly sexual or attractive person
slang

Examples

Despite his reputation as a sex machine, he is actually quite shy in private.
She dressed up and danced like a total sex machine at the club.
He enjoys being called a sex machine, but he laughs it off among friends.
