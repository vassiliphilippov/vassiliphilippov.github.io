run-of-the-mill

1. ADJ

/ ˌrʌn əv ðə ˈmɪl / (AmE), / ˌrʌn əv ðə ˈmɪl / (BrE)
ordinary and not special in any way; lacking any distinctive features or qualities.
ordinary; not special

Examples

The restaurant's food was run-of-the-mill, nothing to get excited about.
It was just another run-of-the-mill day at the office.
His performance was entirely run-of-the-mill, with nothing to set it apart from the rest.

Synset Examples

run-of-the-mill boxing
your run-of-the-mine college graduate
as unexceptional an incident as can be found in a lawyer's career

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features
  • Antonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking, unusual

Etymology

Originally from the milling industry, referring to products as they come from the mill, before any selection or grading.
