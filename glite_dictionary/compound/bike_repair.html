bike repair

1. NOUN

/ baɪk rɪˈpɛr / (AmE), / baɪk rɪˈpeə / (BrE)
the process or activity of maintaining and fixing bicycles
maintenance and fixing of bicycles

Examples

After my chain broke, I watched a video on bike repair to fix it myself.
There's a bike repair workshop every Saturday at the park.
Good bike repair skills can save you time and money on the road.

Synset Examples

He always carries some lube for bike repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bicycle maintenance - the care and upkeep of a bicycle
  • Antonym: car repair - maintenance and fixing of motor vehicles
