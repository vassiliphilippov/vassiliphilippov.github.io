Moreton Bay tulipwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrtən beɪ ˈtuːlɪpˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtən beɪ ˈtjuːlɪpˌwʊd / (BrE)
an Australian tree (Harpullia pendula) known for yielding variegated tulipwood used in woodworking and furniture-making
Australian tree yielding ornamental wood

Examples

The Moreton Bay tulipwood is prized for its beautiful, streaked timber.
Carpenters often seek out Moreton Bay tulipwood for intricate inlay work.
You can find Moreton Bay tulipwood growing along the coasts of eastern Australia.
