bare feet

1. NOUN

/ bɛr fit / (AmE), / beə fiːt / (BrE)
feet that are not covered by socks, shoes, or any other footwear
feet without footwear

Examples

The cold tiles sent a chill through my bare feet as I walked into the kitchen.
After a long hike, she enjoyed feeling the sand between her bare feet.
Children love to run around in the backyard with bare feet during summer.

Synset Examples

The dewey grass felt cool under my bare feet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barefoot - with no shoes or other covering on the feet
