batty idea

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæti aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈbæti aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
An idea that is considered crazy, eccentric, or slightly absurd.
crazy or eccentric idea
informal

Examples

She came up with another batty idea for a business this morning.
Some people laughed at his batty idea to build a house in a tree.
Despite being a batty idea, her plan actually worked out in the end.

Synset Examples

Her batty ideas often surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy idea - an idea that is wild or improbable

Etymology

From 'batty,' meaning crazy, probably from 'bats in the belfry.'
