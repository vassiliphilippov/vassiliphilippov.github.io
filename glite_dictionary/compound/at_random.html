at random

1. ADV

/ ət ˈrændəm / (AmE), / ət ˈrændəm / (BrE)
Without a specific pattern, order, or purpose; in a way that is unpredictable or arbitrary.
without a specific pattern or plan

Examples

The police stopped cars at random to check for valid licenses.
He picked a book at random from the shelf and started reading.
Names were drawn at random to decide the winner of the prize.

Synset Examples

the houses were randomly scattered
bullets were fired into the crowd at random

Related Terms

  • Synonym: randomly - in a random manner; without a specific order or plan
