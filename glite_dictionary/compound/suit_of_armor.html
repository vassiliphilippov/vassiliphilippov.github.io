suit of armor

1. NOUN

/ suːt əv ˈɑːrmər / (AmE), / suːt əv ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
a complete set of metal coverings worn to protect the whole body in battle, especially by soldiers in the past
complete protective metal coverings for the body

Examples

The knight donned his suit of armor before entering the battlefield.
A suit of armor is displayed in the castle museum.
It took several hours to clean and polish the suit of armor after the tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armor - protective covering made of metal or other material
