tv-antenna

1. NOUN

/ ˌtiːˈviː ænˈtɛnə / (AmE), / ˌtiːˈviː ænˈtɛnə / (BrE)
An aerial or device designed to receive broadcast television signals.
device for receiving TV signals

Examples

The technician climbed onto the roof to adjust the tv-antenna for better reception.
Many older homes still have a tv-antenna mounted above the chimney.
She bought a new indoor tv-antenna to watch free local channels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aerial - a device for receiving or transmitting radio or television signals
  • Synonym: antenna - a structure for receiving or transmitting electromagnetic waves
