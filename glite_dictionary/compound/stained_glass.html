stained glass

1. NOUN

/ steɪnd ɡlæs / (AmE), / steɪnd ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
glass that has been colored, often by adding metallic salts during manufacture or by painting, and used especially for decorative windows.
colored glass for decorative windows

Examples

The church was filled with light streaming through its beautiful stained glass windows.
Artists carefully crafted the new stained glass panels for the museum entryway.
Sunlight shining through stained glass creates colorful patterns on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window - an opening in a wall fitted with glass
