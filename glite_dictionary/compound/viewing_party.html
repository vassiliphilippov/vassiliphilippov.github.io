viewing party

1. NOUN

/ ˈvjuɪ.ɪŋ ˌpɑr.ti / (AmE), / ˈvjuː.ɪŋ ˌpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a social gathering in which people assemble to watch a specific program, event, or broadcast together
gathering to watch an event or show

Examples

Sarah is hosting a viewing party for the season finale of their favorite show.
They organized a viewing party to watch the World Cup final together.
Every year, fans hold a viewing party for the Oscars and make their own ballots.

Synset Examples

We always have a viewing party for Shark Week.
