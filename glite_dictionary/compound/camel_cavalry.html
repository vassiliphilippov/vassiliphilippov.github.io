camel cavalry

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæməl ˈkævəlri / (AmE), / ˈkæməl ˈkævəlri / (BrE)
Soldiers mounted on camels, used especially in historical armies of Arab, Afghan, Persian, and Indian regions.
military unit: soldiers mounted on camels

Examples

The camel cavalry played a crucial role in desert warfare due to the animals' endurance.
Several ancient armies relied on camel cavalry to outmaneuver their enemies in arid regions.
Historians have documented the tactics used by camel cavalry during important battles in the Middle East.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cavalry - soldiers who fight mounted on horseback or other animals
