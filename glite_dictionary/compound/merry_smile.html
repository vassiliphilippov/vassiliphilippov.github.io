merry smile

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛri smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɛri smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile that expresses cheerfulness or joy.
cheerful, joyful smile

Examples

She greeted the guests with a merry smile that instantly lifted their spirits.
A merry smile played on his lips as he listened to the children laugh.
Even in difficult times, her merry smile never seemed to fade.

Synset Examples

She wore a merry smile all day long.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smile - a facial expression showing pleasure or amusement
  • Synonym: grin - a broad smile
