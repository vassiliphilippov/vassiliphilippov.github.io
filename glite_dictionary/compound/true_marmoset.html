true marmoset

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈmɑrmoʊˌzɛt / (AmE), / truː ˈmɑːməzɛt / (BrE)
A species of small South American monkeys of the genus Callithrix, distinguished from closely related tamarins.
a small South American monkey (Callithrix genus)

Examples

The true marmoset is native to the forests of Brazil.
Researchers observed a group of true marmosets foraging for insects in the trees.
Unlike tamarins, the true marmoset typically gives birth to twins.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marmoset - a small South American monkey of the family Callitrichidae

Etymology

Formed from 'true' and 'marmoset' to distinguish species of genus Callithrix.
