winter flowering cherry

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚ ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˈflaʊərɪŋ ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
a shrub or tree native to Japan, cultivated as an ornamental for its rose-pink flowers that bloom in winter
Japanese tree or shrub with winter-blooming pink flowers

Examples

The winter flowering cherry brightens up the garden even on the coldest days.
Every January, our local park is filled with winter flowering cherry blossoms.
She planted a winter flowering cherry in her yard for its delicate pink flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry tree - a tree that bears cherries
