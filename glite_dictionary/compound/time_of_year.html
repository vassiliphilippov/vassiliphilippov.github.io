time of year

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv jɪr / (AmE), / taɪm əv jɪə / (BrE)
a particular period in the yearly cycle, especially as divided by the seasons or typical weather conditions
period in the yearly cycle or season

Examples

This is my favorite time of year because the trees are in full bloom.
Retail stores are especially busy at this time of year.
Many people travel abroad during this time of year to escape the cold.

Synset Examples

the regular sequence of the seasons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: season - one of the four main divisions of the year (spring, summer, autumn, winter)
