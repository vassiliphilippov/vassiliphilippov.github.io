payroll department

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌroʊl dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪˌrəʊl dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of an organization responsible for calculating and managing the wages or salaries of employees
department managing employee pay

Examples

The payroll department ensures all employees receive their paychecks on time.
She contacted the payroll department to correct an error in her salary.
Every month, the payroll department processes bonuses and deductions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: human resources department - department responsible for recruiting, training, employee relations
