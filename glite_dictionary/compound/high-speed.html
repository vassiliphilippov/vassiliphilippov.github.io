high-speed

1. ADJ

/ haɪ spiːd / (AmE), / haɪ spiːd / (BrE)
Operating, moving, or designed to operate at a very fast rate.
operating or moving very fast

Examples

The new train service runs on a high-speed rail line between the two cities.
This laboratory uses high-speed centrifuges to separate biological samples.
Our company invested in high-speed internet to support remote work and video conferencing.

Synset Examples

a high-speed food processor
a high-velocity shell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fast - moving or operating quickly
  • Antonym: slow - moving or operating at a low speed
