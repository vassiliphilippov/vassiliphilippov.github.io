Kurdistan Workers' Party

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɜːrdɪˈstɑːn ˈwɝːkɚz ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌkɜːdɪˈstɑːn ˈwɜːkəz ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a Kurdish militant and political organization, designated as a terrorist group by several countries, seeking autonomy or independence for Kurds in southeastern Turkey
Kurdish militant/political organization in Turkey

Examples

The Turkish government considers the Kurdistan Workers' Party a terrorist organization.
Founded in 1978, the Kurdistan Workers' Party has been involved in an armed struggle for Kurdish rights.
Negotiations between Turkish authorities and the Kurdistan Workers' Party have repeatedly broken down.
