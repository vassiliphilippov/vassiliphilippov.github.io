plant substance

1. NOUN

/ plænt ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
a material that is derived from plants, such as a part, product, or extract of a plant
material derived from plants

Examples

Many modern medicines are made from plant substance found in rainforests.
The scientist isolated a rare plant substance that may help treat infections.
Some cleaning products use only natural plant substance instead of synthetic chemicals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plant material - matter originating from or consisting of plants
  • Antonym: animal substance - material derived from animals
