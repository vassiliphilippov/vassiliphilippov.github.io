bold claret

1. NOUN

/ boʊld ˈklærət / (AmE), / bəʊld ˈklærət / (BrE)
A strong, rich red wine, especially one noted for its robust flavor and depth.
strong, rich red wine

Examples

The waiter recommended a bold claret to complement the steak.
She prefers a bold claret over lighter reds for special occasions.
A glass of bold claret brought out the flavors in the cheese platter.

Synset Examples

The meal was enhanced by a bold claret.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: claret - dry red wine, especially from Bordeaux, France
