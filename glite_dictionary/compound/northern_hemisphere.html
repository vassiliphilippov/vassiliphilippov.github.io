northern hemisphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrðərn ˈhɛmɪsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˌnɔːðən ˈhemɪsfɪə / (BrE)
the half of the Earth that lies north of the equator
half of Earth north of the equator

Examples

The northern hemisphere typically experiences colder winters than the southern hemisphere.
Many famous cities, such as London and Beijing, are located in the northern hemisphere.
Birds migrate from the northern hemisphere to warmer regions during autumn.

Synset Examples

The northern hemisphere experiences summer in June.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: southern hemisphere - the half of the Earth that lies south of the equator
