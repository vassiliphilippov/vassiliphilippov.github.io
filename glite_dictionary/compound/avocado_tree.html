avocado tree

1. NOUN

/ əˈvɑːkəˌdoʊ triː / (AmE), / əˈvɑːkədəʊ triː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree that bears large, pulpy green fruit known as avocados.
tree that produces avocados

Examples

The avocado tree in her backyard produces dozens of fruits every year.
Farmers often graft avocado trees to improve fruit quality and yield.
After five years, the young avocado tree finally began to blossom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avocado - the large, green fruit of the avocado tree
