mineral processing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnərəl ˌprɑsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl ˌprɒsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of crushing and separating ore into valuable substances or waste using various techniques
extraction of valuable materials from ore

Examples

Mineral processing is essential to extract metals efficiently from mined ore.
New flotation techniques have improved the efficiency of mineral processing plants worldwide.
The study focused on environmentally friendly approaches to mineral processing in developing countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ore dressing - the process of separating valuable minerals from their ores
