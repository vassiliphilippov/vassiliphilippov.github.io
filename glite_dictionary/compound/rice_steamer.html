rice steamer

1. NOUN

/ raɪs ˈstiːmər / (AmE), / raɪs ˈstiːmə / (BrE)
an electric kitchen appliance used for boiling or steaming rice automatically
kitchen appliance for cooking rice

Examples

She bought a new rice steamer to make dinner preparation easier.
With the rice steamer, you can cook perfect rice every time without watching the stove.
After washing the grains, he placed them into the rice steamer and pressed the start button.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rice cooker - an electric appliance for cooking rice; often used interchangeably with rice steamer
