cognitive state

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːɡnətɪv steɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv steɪt / (BrE)
a particular condition or status of a person's mental processes, such as attention, memory, or perception
mental condition or status

Examples

Her cognitive state was affected by the medication, making it difficult to focus.
Regular exercise has been shown to improve a person's cognitive state over time.
After the accident, doctors monitored his cognitive state to assess potential brain injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: state of mind - the particular mental or emotional condition someone is in
  • Synonym: mood - a temporary state of mind or feeling

Etymology

From Latin 'cognitio' (knowing) and English 'state'.
