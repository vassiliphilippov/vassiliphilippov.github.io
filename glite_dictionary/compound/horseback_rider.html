horseback rider

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌbæk ˈraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsbæk ˈraɪdə / (BrE)
a person who rides horses, especially one skilled in equitation
person who rides horses

Examples

The experienced horseback rider guided her horse across the muddy riverbank without hesitation.
As a horseback rider, he has competed in several national equestrian events.
Every horseback rider must develop a strong bond with their horse for optimal performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrian - a person who rides horses, especially as a sport
