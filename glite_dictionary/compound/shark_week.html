Shark Week

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrk wik / (AmE), / ʃɑːk wiːk / (BrE)
an annual week-long television programming event focused on sharks, usually featuring documentaries and specials about shark species and behavior
annual TV event about sharks

Examples

Every summer, my family looks forward to Shark Week on television.
The network releases new documentaries for Shark Week each year.
During Shark Week, social media is full of shark facts and videos.

Synset Examples

Shark Week brings out the inner ocean lover in everyone.
