family name

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli neɪm / (AmE), / ˈfæməli neɪm / (BrE)
The part of a person's name that is shared with other members of their family, as distinguished from the given name; a surname.
surname; shared family name

Examples

In many cultures, the family name is passed down from one generation to the next.
She changed her family name after getting married.
Your family name comes after your given name in some countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surname - a hereditary name shared by family members
