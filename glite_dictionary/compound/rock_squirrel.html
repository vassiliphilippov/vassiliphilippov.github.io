rock squirrel

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈskwɝl / (AmE), / rɒk ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
a large gray ground squirrel living in rocky areas of the southwestern United States
large gray ground squirrel of the southwestern US

Examples

We spotted a rock squirrel sunning itself on a boulder during our hike.
The rock squirrel is well-adapted to the arid environment of the Southwest.
You can often see rock squirrels darting between rocks in national parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ground squirrel - a type of squirrel that lives on or in the ground rather than in trees
