tension ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnʃən rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnʃən rɪŋ / (BrE)
A ring placed around the base of the penis, usually to help maintain an erection by restricting blood flow.
ring worn to help maintain an erection

Examples

The doctor recommended using a tension ring as part of the treatment for erectile dysfunction.
He purchased a silicone tension ring to improve sexual performance.
Proper use of a tension ring requires following safety guidelines to prevent injury.
