seizure-alert dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiʒɚ əˈlɝt dɔg / (AmE), / ˈsiːʒə əˈlɜːt dɒg / (BrE)
A dog trained to recognize and respond to signs of an oncoming seizure in a person, often by alerting or assisting them.
dog trained to detect and respond to seizures

Examples

Her seizure-alert dog warned her minutes before she had an episode, allowing her to get to a safe place.
The hospital offers training programs for seizure-alert dogs to help patients with epilepsy.
Families with children suffering from seizures are increasingly turning to seizure-alert dogs for support and safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service dog - a dog trained to help people with disabilities
