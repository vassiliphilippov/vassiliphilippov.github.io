live axle

1. NOUN

/ laɪv ˈæksəl / (AmE), / laɪv ˈæksəl / (BrE)
an axle in a self-propelled vehicle that transmits driving power to the wheels
driving axle in a vehicle

Examples

The car's live axle is responsible for transmitting power from the engine to the wheels.
Older trucks often use a live axle design for better durability under heavy loads.
Technicians inspected the live axle for signs of wear after hearing strange noises during acceleration.
