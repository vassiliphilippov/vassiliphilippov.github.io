mechanical routine

1. NOUN

/ məˈkænɪkəl ruˈtin / (AmE), / mɪˈkanɪkəl ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
a set of actions or procedures performed regularly in an unthinking or automatic way
repetitive unthinking procedure

Examples

Her morning mechanical routine never varied, even on weekends.
Employees sometimes fall into a mechanical routine when performing repetitive tasks.
He realized that his daily commute had become a mechanical routine requiring no real thought.

Synset Examples

She followed a mechanical routine every morning without fail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: habit - a regular practice or tendency, especially one that is hard to give up
  • Synonym: ritual - a series of actions regularly and invariably followed
