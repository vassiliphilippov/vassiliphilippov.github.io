ventilation system

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
A mechanical system in a building designed to provide fresh air and remove stale air.
mechanical system for air circulation

Examples

The building's ventilation system was upgraded to improve air quality and comfort.
During the renovation, they installed a new ventilation system to ensure proper airflow.
Employees complained that the faulty ventilation system made the office stuffy and uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

she was continually adjusting the ventilation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air conditioning - system for controlling the temperature and humidity of air
