Carolina wren

1. NOUN

/ kəˈraɪlɪnə rɛn / (AmE), / kəˈrɛlɪnə rɛn / (BrE)
a large wren native to the southeastern United States, known for its distinctive musical call and reddish-brown plumage
large North American songbird

Examples

The Carolina wren built its nest near our porch last spring.
You can often hear the loud song of a Carolina wren on warm southern mornings.
We spotted a Carolina wren hopping among the garden shrubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wren - small songbird of the family Troglodytidae
