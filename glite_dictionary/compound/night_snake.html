night snake

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪt sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, nocturnal snake native to the western United States and Mexico, known for its secretive habits
a nocturnal snake of North America

Examples

The night snake is rarely seen during the day due to its nocturnal nature.
Herpetologists conducted a study on the habitat of the night snake in Arizona.
A night snake slithered quietly across the desert floor under the cover of darkness.
