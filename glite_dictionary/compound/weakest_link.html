weakest link

1. NOUN

/ ˈwikəst lɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈwiːkɪst lɪŋk / (BrE)
the least effective or most vulnerable part of a group, system, or process
most vulnerable or ineffective part

Examples

The team's defense was considered their weakest link, often allowing crucial goals.
In any security system, humans are frequently the weakest link.
She realized that her lack of experience was the weakest link in her otherwise impressive application.

Synset Examples

He was the weakest link in the team.
Don't be the weakest link; contribute more.
Every chain has its weakest link, and he was ours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Achilles' heel - a weakness in spite of overall strength

Etymology

From the metaphor of a chain being only as strong as its weakest link.
