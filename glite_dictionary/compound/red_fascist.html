red fascist

1. NOUN

/ rɛd ˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / rɛd ˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a person or policy espousing far-left authoritarian or oppressive views, often equating them with fascism.
person or policy with far-left authoritarian views

Examples

Some accused the government of acting like a red fascist regime during the crackdown.
His opponents derided him as a red fascist because of his uncompromising policies.
She rejected the label of red fascist, insisting her actions were in defense of democracy.

Synset Examples

Some see him as a red fascist due to his harsh rhetoric.
He was labeled a red fascist for his extreme views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascist - person with extreme authoritarian, nationalist views
  • Synonym: communist - supporter of communist ideology or system

Etymology

Formed from 'red' (color associated with communism) and 'fascist' (authoritarian nationalist).
