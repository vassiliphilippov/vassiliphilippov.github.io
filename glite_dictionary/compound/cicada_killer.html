cicada killer

1. NOUN

/ səˈkeɪdə ˈkɪlɚ / (AmE), / sɪˈkɑːdə ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
a large wasp, usually black or rust-colored, that hunts and preys on cicadas
large wasp that hunts cicadas

Examples

The cicada killer is known for its impressive size and solitary hunting behavior.
I saw a cicada killer dragging its prey across the yard.
Gardeners sometimes mistake the cicada killer for a hornet due to its large, intimidating appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wasp - a winged insect with a narrow waist and a sting
