golden saxifrage

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldən ˈsæksɪˌfrædʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈsæksɪfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of several low-growing aquatic or semi-aquatic plants of the genus Chrysosplenium, with small yellowish-green flowers
a low aquatic or semi-aquatic plant, genus Chrysosplenium

Examples

The damp woodland was carpeted with golden saxifrage after the spring rains.
She identified the delicate plant as golden saxifrage growing near the stream.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate golden saxifrage in shady, moist borders for its foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxifrage - any plant of the genus Saxifraga, often with small flowers

Etymology

From 'golden' (referring to the flower color) + 'saxifrage', from Latin saxifraga (rock-breaker), the generic name for these plants.
