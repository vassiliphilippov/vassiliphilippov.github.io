American lime

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən laɪm / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən laɪm / (BrE)
A large deciduous tree native to North America, with dark green leaves and a rounded crown, often planted for shade.
large North American shade tree

Examples

The park was lined with towering American lime trees, providing cool shade in the summer.
An American lime in our backyard attracts many birds during the spring.
The gardener recommended planting an American lime for its dense, attractive foliage.
