grooming tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡruːmɪŋ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈɡruːmɪŋ tuːl / (BrE)
an instrument or device used for personal care, hygiene, or beautification
tool for personal care or hygiene

Examples

He keeps a variety of grooming tools in his bathroom drawer.
A good grooming tool can make pet care much easier.
Before his interview, he carefully selected each grooming tool to look his best.

Synset Examples

His collection of grooming tools includes a Welsh comb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comb - toothed device for arranging or cleaning hair
  • Synonym: brush - tool with bristles used for cleaning or grooming
