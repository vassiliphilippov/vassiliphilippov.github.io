electric mixer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmɪksər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a kitchen appliance used for mixing food ingredients, powered by an electric motor
kitchen appliance for mixing food

Examples

She used an electric mixer to whip the cream until it was light and fluffy.
The recipe suggested beating the eggs by hand, but I prefer using my electric mixer.
Our old electric mixer finally broke, so we had to buy a new one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixer - a device for mixing ingredients
