to bottle beer

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːt̬l ˈbɪr / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˈbɪə / (BrE)
to package or put beer into bottles, especially for storage, selling, or distribution
put beer into bottles for storage or sale

Examples

Many craft breweries bottle beer as well as selling it on draft.
He learned how to bottle beer during his brewing course.
They spent the afternoon bottling beer for the local festival.

Synset Examples

He decided to bottle his homemade beer this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: can beer - to package beer in cans
