viva voce

1. NOUN

/ ˌviːvə ˈvoʊtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌviːvə ˈvəʊtʃiː / (BrE)
an examination conducted orally, especially as part of an academic assessment
oral examination (academic)

Examples

She passed her viva voce for the PhD with flying colors.
The final assessment included both written and viva voce examinations.
Candidates found the viva voce more challenging than the written test.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oral examination - an exam conducted by spoken questions and answers

Etymology

From Latin 'viva voce' meaning 'with the living voice'.
