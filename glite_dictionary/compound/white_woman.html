white woman

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / waɪt ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is identified as White, typically referring to a person of European descent or having light skin pigmentation
woman identified as racially White

Examples

The police were looking for a white woman last seen in the area on Tuesday.
She is the first white woman to win the award in more than a decade.
In the photograph, a white woman sits next to her colleagues at the conference table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: White man - a man who is White
  • Antonym: Black woman - a woman who is Black
