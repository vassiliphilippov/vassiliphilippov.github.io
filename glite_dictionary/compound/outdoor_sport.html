outdoor sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔɹ spɔɹt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport that is played outside rather than indoors.
sport played outdoors

Examples

Tennis is often considered an outdoor sport, although it can be played indoors as well.
Hiking is an outdoor sport that attracts nature lovers from all over the world.
Schools usually encourage participation in at least one outdoor sport each semester.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indoor sport - a sport that is played indoors
