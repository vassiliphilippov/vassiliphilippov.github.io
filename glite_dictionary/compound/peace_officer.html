peace officer

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / piːs ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
A person with legal authority to enforce the law, such as a police officer or sheriff.
law enforcement officer
legal

Examples

The sheriff is considered a peace officer under the law.
Only a certified peace officer may issue a traffic citation in this state.
Many peace officers receive special training for handling dangerous situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police officer - a member of a police force
  • Synonym: officer of the law - a person who is responsible for enforcing the law
