butcher shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊtʃər ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where fresh meat and poultry are sold, and sometimes fish.
shop selling meat and poultry

Examples

We bought some sausages and steaks from the local butcher shop for the barbecue.
After closing time, the lights in the butcher shop were still on as the staff cleaned up.
He worked in his father's butcher shop every summer during high school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butcher - a person who sells or cuts meat
  • Synonym: meat market - a market or shop selling meat
