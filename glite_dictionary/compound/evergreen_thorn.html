evergreen thorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvərˌɡriːn θɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɛvəɡriːn θɔːn / (BrE)
a species of evergreen hawthorn native to southeastern Europe, known for its sharp spines and persistent green leaves
evergreen hawthorn of southeastern Europe

Examples

The garden features several evergreen thorn shrubs imported from southeastern Europe.
Experts recommend pruning the evergreen thorn to maintain its compact shape.
The evergreen thorn provides year-round foliage and sharp protection for nesting birds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hawthorn - a shrub or tree of the genus Crataegus, typically with spiny branches and white or pink flowers
