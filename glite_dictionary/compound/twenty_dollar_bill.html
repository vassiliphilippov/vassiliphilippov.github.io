twenty dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɛnti ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A United States banknote with a value of twenty dollars.
U.S. $20 banknote
american

Examples

He handed the cashier a twenty dollar bill and waited for his change.
The vending machine only accepts coins and twenty dollar bills.
She found a crumpled twenty dollar bill in her coat pocket.
