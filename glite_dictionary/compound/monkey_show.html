monkey show

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a performance, often entertaining, in which monkeys are trained to perform tricks or acts for an audience
performance featuring trained monkeys

Examples

The children laughed and clapped during the monkey show at the fair.
A travelling circus brought a famous monkey show to our town last summer.
Many tourists gathered in the park to watch the lively monkey show.

Synset Examples

They organized a monkey show for the children's party.
