order Siluriformes

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər sɪˌljʊrəˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sɪˌljʊərɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of fish within the superorder Malacopterygii, comprising catfishes and related species
taxonomic order of catfishes
medical

Examples

Many species in the order Siluriformes are found in freshwater habitats worldwide.
The order Siluriformes encompasses a diverse range of catfish.
Scientists study the order Siluriformes to learn about fish adaptation and evolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catfish - common name for fish of the order Siluriformes

Etymology

From modern Latin *Siluriformes*, from *Silurus* (a type of catfish) + *-formes* (having the form of).
