armed service

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmd ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / ɑːmd ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a branch of a country's military forces, such as the army, navy, or air force
military branch (army, navy, etc.)

Examples

He announced his intention to join the armed service after finishing school.
Each armed service has its own traditions and history.
Veterans from every armed service were honored at the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armed forces - the military forces of a country
