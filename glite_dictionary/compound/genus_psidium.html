genus Psidium

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs saɪˈdiːəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs sɪˈdiːəm / (BrE)
A genus of tropical shrubs and small trees in the myrtle family, including guavas.
genus of tropical guava plants

Examples

The genus Psidium contains several species of plants known for producing edible guava fruit.
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate members of the genus Psidium for their sweet fruit.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of plants in the genus Psidium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guava - the edible fruit of several species in the genus Psidium

Etymology

From Latin 'psidium', ultimately from Greek 'psidion' (a type of pomegranate).
