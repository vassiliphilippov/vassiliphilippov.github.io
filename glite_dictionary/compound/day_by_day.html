day by day

1. ADV

/ ˈdeɪ baɪ ˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ baɪ ˈdeɪ / (BrE)
gradually and progressively as time passes; with each succeeding day
progressively, with each day

Examples

Her strength improved day by day after the operation.
We watched the seedlings grow day by day through the spring.
His confidence increased day by day as he practiced speaking English.

Synset Examples

his health weakened day by day
