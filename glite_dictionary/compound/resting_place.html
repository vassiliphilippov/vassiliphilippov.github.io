resting place

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈrɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place where someone is buried after death; a grave or burial site.
burial place; grave

Examples

She laid flowers at her grandfather's resting place every year on his birthday.
The authors' final resting place is a small cemetery overlooking the sea.
After a long search, the soldier's resting place was finally discovered.

Synset Examples

He visits her resting place every Sunday to pay his respects.
The cemetery is a peaceful resting place for many.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grave - a place in the ground where a dead person is buried
  • Synonym: tomb - a large grave, typically an underground one marked by a monument
