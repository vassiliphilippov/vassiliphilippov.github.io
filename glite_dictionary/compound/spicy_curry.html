spicy curry

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪsi ˈkɜri / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi ˈkʌri / (BrE)
A hot, highly seasoned dish prepared with curry spices and known for its strong, spicy flavor.
hot, seasoned dish with curry spices

Examples

He ordered a spicy curry to warm himself on the cold night.
Many people find it difficult to eat a spicy curry without drinking plenty of water.
Aromatic rice pairs perfectly with a spicy curry for a satisfying meal.

Synset Examples

He loves Indish food, especially spicy curries.
They served this rice with a spicy curry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curry - a dish of meat, vegetables, etc. cooked in an Indian-style sauce of strong spices
