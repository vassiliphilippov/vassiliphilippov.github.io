beefsteak geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːfˌsteɪk dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈbiːfˌsteɪk dʒəˈriːniəm / (BrE)
A type of hybrid begonia, often with rough-textured, colorful leaves patterned in silver, bronze, purple, and red-brown, and small inconspicuous flowers, widely grown as a houseplant.
a hybrid begonia with patterned leaves

Examples

The beefsteak geranium is prized for its dramatic, colorful foliage in home gardens.
She placed a beefsteak geranium in the sunniest window to encourage healthy growth.
Collectors often seek rare varieties of beefsteak geranium for their unique leaf patterns.
