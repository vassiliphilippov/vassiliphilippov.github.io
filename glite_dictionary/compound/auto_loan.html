auto loan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ ˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ ˌləʊn / (BrE)
a loan taken out to purchase an automobile, typically repaid over a fixed period with interest
loan used to buy a car

Examples

He applied for an auto loan to buy his first car.
Interest rates for an auto loan are usually lower than for other types of personal loans.
Many dealerships offer special deals if you finance your purchase with their auto loan programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car loan - a loan used to buy a car
  • Degree Higher: mortgage - a loan for purchasing property
  • Degree Higher: personal loan - a loan issued for personal use
