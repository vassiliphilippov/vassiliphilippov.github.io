touchy-feely conversation

1. NOUN

/ ˌtʌtʃiˈfili ˌkɑnvərˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtʌtʃiˈfiːli ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a conversation characterized by overt emotional expression or sentimentality
emotionally expressive discussion
informal

Examples

I find it difficult to engage in touchy-feely conversation with people I hardly know.
Their meetings often included some touchy-feely conversation to build trust among team members.
Not everyone is comfortable with a touchy-feely conversation, especially in the workplace.

Synset Examples

She prefers straightforward discussions over touchy-feely conversations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotional conversation - a discussion focusing on emotions or feelings

Etymology

Formed from 'touchy-feely' (informal for openly emotional) + 'conversation'.
