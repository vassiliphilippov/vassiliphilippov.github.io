booty chaser

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːti ˌtʃeɪsər / (AmE), / ˈbuːti ˌtʃeɪsə / (BrE)
A person, typically a man, who habitually pursues casual sexual relationships.
person who pursues casual sexual encounters
informal slang

Examples

He quickly gained a reputation as a booty chaser among his friends.
She warned her roommate about dating a known booty chaser.
The movie portrays the main character as a classic booty chaser.

Synset Examples

I can't stand that he's become a total booty chaser.
He's known as a booty chaser at the club.
She avoids guys who are just booty chasers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
  • Synonym: cad - a man who behaves dishonorably, especially toward women

Etymology

From 'booty' (slang for buttocks or sexual partner) + 'chaser' (one who pursues).
