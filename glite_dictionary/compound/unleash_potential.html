to unleash potential

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈliʃ pəˈtɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈliːʃ pəˈtɛnʃəl / (BrE)
to allow someone's abilities or talents to be fully expressed or utilized
to fully realize abilities or talents

Examples

The new training program helped employees unleash potential they didn’t know they had.
Great leaders inspire their teams to unleash potential and achieve extraordinary results.
With the right support, she was finally able to unleash potential and thrive in her career.

Synset Examples

It's time to unleash your potential and follow your dreams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fulfil potential - to develop abilities to the fullest extent
