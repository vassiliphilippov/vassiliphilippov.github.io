ex-husband

1. NOUN

/ ɛks ˈhʌzbənd / (AmE), / ɛks ˈhʌzbənd / (BrE)
a man who was formerly married to a particular woman
former husband

Examples

Her ex-husband called to discuss the arrangements for their daughter's graduation.
After the divorce, Sarah and her ex-husband remained friends.
The story focused on the relationship between the main character and her ex-husband.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ex-wife - a woman who was formerly a particular man's wife
  • Degree Lower: husband - a married man; a woman's spouse

Etymology

Formed from 'ex-' (Latin, meaning 'former') and 'husband'.
