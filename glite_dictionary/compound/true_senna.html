true senna

1. NOUN

/ tru ˈsɛnə / (AmE), / truː ˈsɛnə / (BrE)
an erect shrub (Senna alexandrina) with racemes of tawny yellow flowers, whose dried leaves are used medicinally as a laxative
medicinal shrub; source of cathartic leaves

Examples

The dried leaves of the true senna plant are commonly used in herbal teas for their laxative effects.
Researchers studied the cultivation requirements of true senna to increase medicinal yields.
In traditional medicine, extracts from true senna are prescribed for constipation and digestive issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senna - any plant of the genus Senna, many with medicinal value
