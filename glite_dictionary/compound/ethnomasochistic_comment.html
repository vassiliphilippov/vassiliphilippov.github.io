ethnomasochistic comment

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛθnoʊməˈsoʊʃɪstɪk ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊməˈsəʊʃɪstɪk ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a comment expressing self-deprecating or disparaging views about one's own ethnic group
self-deprecating comment about one’s own ethnicity

Examples

Many interpreted her ethnomasochistic comment as a reflection of internalized bias.
The article was criticized for containing several ethnomasochistic comments about the author's cultural background.
He regretted making such an ethnomasochistic comment after reflecting on its impact.

Synset Examples

Many felt his ethnomasochistic comments were out of touch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-deprecating remark - a comment belittling oneself or one's group

Etymology

From 'ethno-' (relating to ethnicity) and 'masochistic' (deriving pleasure from self-denigration), coined in sociological discourse.
