labia minora

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bi.ə mɪˈnɔɹ.ə / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bɪ.ə mɪˈnɔː.rə / (BrE)
the two thin, inner folds of skin on either side of the vaginal opening in the vulva
inner folds of the vulva
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor inspected the labia minora for any signs of irritation.
The labia minora can vary greatly in size and shape from person to person.
Proper hygiene of the labia minora is important for overall vulvar health.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: labia majora - the larger, outer folds of the vulva

Etymology

From Latin labia (lips) + minora (smaller), distinguishing them from labia majora (larger lips).
