mid-seventies

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈsɛv(ə)nˌtiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈsɛv(ə)ntiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of about 70 and 80
age between 70 and 80

Examples

My grandmother remained very active into her mid-seventies.
Many people travel more in their mid-seventies after retiring.
He learned to paint when he was in his mid-seventies.
