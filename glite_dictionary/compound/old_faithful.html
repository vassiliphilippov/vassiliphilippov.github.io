Old Faithful

1. PROPN

/ oʊld ˈfeɪθ.fəl / (AmE), / əʊld ˈfeɪθ.fəl / (BrE)
a famous geyser in Yellowstone National Park known for erupting at regular intervals, approximately every 65 minutes
famous Yellowstone geyser

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Old Faithful each year to witness its regular eruptions.
During our trip, we made sure to arrive early to see Old Faithful in action.
Old Faithful is one of the main attractions of Yellowstone National Park.

Etymology

Named for its reliability as one of Yellowstone’s most dependable geysers.
