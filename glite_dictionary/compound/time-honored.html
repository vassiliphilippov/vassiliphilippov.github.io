time-honored

1. ADJ

/ taɪm ˈɑnɚd / (AmE), / taɪm ˈɒnəd / (BrE)
Respected or valued because it has existed for a long time and is upheld by tradition.
respected due to long tradition

Examples

The company follows a time-honored approach to customer service.
Every year, they celebrate with a time-honored festival in the town square.
He cooked the meal using his grandmother’s time-honored recipes.

Synset Examples

time-honored institutions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traditional - existing in or as part of a tradition; long-established
