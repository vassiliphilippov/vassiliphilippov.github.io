theater stage

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̬ɚ steɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə steɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a theater where actors perform for an audience
raised platform for performances in a theater

Examples

The crew prepared the theater stage for tonight's performance with elaborate props and lighting.
She felt nervous as she stepped onto the theater stage for the first time in front of an audience.
The new theater stage was designed to enhance acoustics and visibility for both performers and spectators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage - a raised platform for performances
