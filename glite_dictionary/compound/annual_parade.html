annual parade

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.ju.əl pəˈreɪd / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl pəˈreɪd / (BrE)
a public procession or celebration held once every year, often with music, costumes, and displays
yearly public procession or celebration

Examples

The city's annual parade is a beloved tradition attended by the whole community.
Every summer, an annual parade winds through the streets with colorful floats and marching bands.
The annual parade was canceled this year due to bad weather, disappointing many residents.

Synset Examples

The annual parade drew thousands of spectators.
