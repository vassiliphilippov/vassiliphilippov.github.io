yellow paper daisy

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˈpeɪpər ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈpeɪpə ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
an Australian plant with flowers ranging from lemon yellow to deep gold, commonly used in dried flower arrangements
Australian plant with yellow flowers, used in dried arrangements

Examples

The yellow paper daisy thrives in dry, sunny gardens across Australia.
Many people choose the yellow paper daisy for its long-lasting beauty in dried floral displays.
Gardeners appreciate the resilience of the yellow paper daisy during periods of drought.
