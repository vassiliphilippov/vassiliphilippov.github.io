nine iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪn ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn ˌaɪən / (BrE)
A golf club with a high loft, typically used for short approach shots and shots requiring height.
a type of high-loft golf club

Examples

She selected her nine iron to hit the ball over the bunker and onto the green.
Many golfers find that a nine iron is perfect for short approach shots.
He practiced chipping with his nine iron to improve his control around the greens.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: pitching wedge - a golf club with even greater loft used for very short shots
  • Degree Lower: eight iron - a golf club with slightly less loft than a nine iron
