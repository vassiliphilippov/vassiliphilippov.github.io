ant shrike

1. NOUN

/ ænt ʃraɪk / (AmE), / ænt ʃraɪk / (BrE)
A mainly tropical American bird of the antbird family (Thamnophilidae), superficially resembling shrikes.
a tropical American bird, antbird family

Examples

The dense Amazon forests are home to many species of ant shrike.
We spotted an ant shrike perched quietly among the branches.
Some ant shrikes are known for following ants to find food.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antbird - a bird of the family Thamnophilidae that typically feeds on ants
  • Synonym: shrike - a predatory songbird of the family Laniidae
