Qassam Brigades

1. PROPN

/ kɑˈsɑm brɪˈɡeɪdz / (AmE), / kaˈsɑːm brɪˈɡeɪdz / (BrE)
the military wing of Hamas, responsible for organizing and carrying out armed attacks against Israel, including suicide bombings
military wing of Hamas

Examples

The Qassam Brigades claimed responsibility for the attack on the checkpoint.
Israel has often targeted leaders of the Qassam Brigades in airstrikes.
Members of the Qassam Brigades are trained in guerrilla warfare and explosives.

Etymology

Named after Izz ad-Din al-Qassam (1882–1935), a Syrian Muslim preacher and leader.
