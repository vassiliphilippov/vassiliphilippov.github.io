bullock's heart

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊləkz hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈbʊləkz hɑːt / (BrE)
A small tropical American tree (Annona reticulata) bearing a bristly, heart-shaped acidic fruit.
tropical American tree with heart-shaped fruit

Examples

The ripe bullock's heart was sliced and served as dessert after dinner.
Local farmers cultivate bullock's heart trees for their nutritious fruit.
You can recognize a bullock's heart by its unique heart-shaped and slightly bristly exterior.
