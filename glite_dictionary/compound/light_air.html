light air

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ɛr / (AmE), / laɪt eə / (BrE)
A very light wind with a speed of 1–3 knots, corresponding to force 1 on the Beaufort scale.
very light wind (Beaufort scale 1)

Examples

A light air rippled the surface of the lake, barely disturbing the water.
Sailing was slow in the light air that morning, requiring patience and skill.
Even a light air can help a sailboat move gently along the coast.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: light breeze - wind on the Beaufort scale, slightly stronger than light air
  • Degree Lower: calm - no wind movement, Beaufort scale 0
