cape forget-me-not

1. NOUN

/ keɪp fərˈɡɛt mi nɑt / (AmE), / keɪp fəˈɡɛt mi nɒt / (BrE)
A plant of southern Africa (Anchusa capensis), bearing blue flowers with white throats, often grown as an ornamental.
southern African plant with blue, white-throated flowers

Examples

The cape forget-me-not adds a splash of bright blue to many spring gardens.
Gardeners often choose the cape forget-me-not for its vibrant flowers and easy maintenance.
I planted several cape forget-me-nots along the walkway for a colorful border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forget-me-not - a small plant with blue flowers in the genus Myosotis

Etymology

Named for the Cape region of South Africa and the similarity to the forget-me-not flower.
