sweetest human bean

1. NOUN

/ ˈswiːtəst ˈhjuːmən ˈbiːn / (AmE), / ˈswiːtɪst ˈhjuːmən ˈbiːn / (BrE)
A humorous or affectionate way to refer to someone as exceptionally kind or pleasant.
very kind, pleasant person (humorous)
informal

Examples

Everyone loves Maria—she's truly the sweetest human bean you could ever hope to meet.
After helping me with my project, my friend called me the sweetest human bean.
You brought cookies for everyone? You are the sweetest human bean!

Synset Examples

She's the sweetest human bean you'll ever meet.

Etymology

Playful alteration of 'human being,' substituting 'being' with 'bean' for humorous effect.
