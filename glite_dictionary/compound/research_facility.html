research facility

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ fəˌsɪlɪti / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
A place or building equipped for scientific, academic, or other research activities.
place equipped for research

Examples

The new research facility will focus on developing renewable energy sources.
She was offered a position at a prestigious research facility overseas.
Scientists at the research facility made a breakthrough in cancer treatment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building equipped for scientific experiments
