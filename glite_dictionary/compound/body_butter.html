body butter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
A thick, creamy moisturizer containing a high proportion of oils, used to hydrate and nourish the skin.
rich, oily skin moisturizer

Examples

Applying body butter after a shower can help seal in moisture for softer skin.
She prefers using body butter over lotions during winter months.
Many body butters are scented with natural extracts like cocoa or shea.

Synset Examples

Body butter is recommended for dry skin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moisturizer - a substance used for keeping the skin moist
