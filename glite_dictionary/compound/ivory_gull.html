ivory gull

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.vəri ɡʌl / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.vəri ɡʌl / (BrE)
a white gull of the Arctic regions that migrates south as far as England and New Brunswick
a white Arctic gull species

Examples

During his expedition in Greenland, he spotted an ivory gull perched on the drifting ice.
Climate change is affecting the habitat of the ivory gull more severely each decade.
The pure white feathers of the ivory gull make it easy to distinguish from other Arctic birds.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gull - a seabird with long wings and usually white or gray plumage
