Japanese-speaking

1. ADJ

/ dʒəˌpæniz ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒəˌpæniːz ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to communicate in Japanese; speaking the Japanese language.
able to speak Japanese

Examples

All the tour guides on this trip are Japanese-speaking and can assist international visitors.
We need to hire a Japanese-speaking teacher for our language program.
The company is looking for Japanese-speaking staff to expand its business in Tokyo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese - the language spoken in Japan
