calorie chart

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæləri tʃɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkæləri tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a table or list showing the caloric content of various foods
list of foods with calorie content

Examples

The calorie chart on the restaurant menu helped me choose a healthier option.
Before starting her diet, she printed a calorie chart to track her daily intake.
A calorie chart can be a useful tool for anyone managing their weight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition chart - a chart showing various nutritional values of foods
