party killer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrti ˌkɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti ˌkɪlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that spoils the atmosphere or enjoyment at a party or social gathering
something that spoils a party's mood

Examples

Bringing up politics at dinner was a real party killer last night.
Her negative attitude quickly became a party killer for everyone else.
Nothing is more of a party killer than a noise complaint from the neighbors.

Synset Examples

He joked that discussing the dismal science was a party killer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
  • Synonym: spoilsport - someone who stops others from enjoying something
