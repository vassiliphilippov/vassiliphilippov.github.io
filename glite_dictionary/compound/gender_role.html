gender role

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛndɚ ˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndə ˌrəʊl / (BrE)
the public expression of attitudes and behaviors that are culturally associated with being male or female
public expression of male or female identity

Examples

Many cultures have traditional gender roles that define how men and women are expected to behave.
She challenged the gender role assigned to her by choosing a career in engineering.
Modern discussions on equality often focus on breaking down outdated gender roles.

Synset Examples

your gender role is the public expression of your gender identity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gender identity - an individual's personal sense of their gender
  • Synonym: sex role - the role or behavior learned by a person as appropriate to their sex
