lantern jaw

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæntərn dʒɔ / (AmE), / ˈlæntən dʒɔː / (BrE)
A long, thin, and often prominent lower jaw or chin.
long, thin lower jaw

Examples

His lantern jaw gave him a striking and memorable profile.
She described the suspect as tall, with a lantern jaw and piercing eyes.
The actor's lantern jaw made him a favorite for heroic roles.

Etymology

From the resemblance to the shape or projection of a lantern.
