black bead

1. NOUN

/ blæk bid / (AmE), / blæk biːd / (BrE)
a shrub native to the West Indies and Florida, with few spines, clusters of white to yellow flowers, and curved pods containing black shiny seeds
a shrub with black shiny seeds, native to West Indies and Florida

Examples

The black bead shrub grows in tropical regions of Florida and the Caribbean.
Gardeners value the black bead for its attractive flowers and glossy seeds.
Locals use the pods from the black bead in traditional crafts and jewelry.
