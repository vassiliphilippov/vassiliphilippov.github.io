starving artist

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrvɪŋ ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈstɑːvɪŋ ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
an artist who lives in poverty or has little financial success, often due to focusing on art rather than commercial gain
artist struggling financially

Examples

After years as a starving artist, she finally sold enough paintings to support herself.
Many college graduates fear becoming a starving artist when pursuing their dreams in creative fields.
He wrote a memoir about his experience as a starving artist in Paris.

Synset Examples

He embraced the life of a starving artist to follow his passion.
Many people romanticize the concept of the starving artist.
The starving artist finally got recognition for his work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impoverished - lacking money or resources
  • Antonym: wealthy artist - an artist who is financially successful
