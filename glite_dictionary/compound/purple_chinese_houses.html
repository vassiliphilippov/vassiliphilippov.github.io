purple Chinese houses

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈtʃaɪniz ˈhaʊzɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈhaʊzɪz / (BrE)
a plant native to California with white and lavender to pale-blue flowers arranged in rings around the stem, resembling a pagoda
California plant with tiered, pale-blue flowers

Examples

Purple Chinese houses bloom in the spring across hillsides in California.
Gardeners appreciate the unique architecture of purple Chinese houses in their native plant gardens.
While hiking, we spotted several clusters of purple Chinese houses along the trail.
