two-piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuˌpis / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌpiːs / (BrE)
a business suit consisting of a matching jacket and skirt or trousers
business suit with matching jacket and skirt or trousers

Examples

She wore a classic two-piece to the interview, looking both professional and elegant.
His new two-piece was tailored to fit perfectly.
Many women prefer a two-piece with trousers instead of a skirt for business meetings.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: three-piece - a suit with jacket, trousers, and waistcoat
