tea cloth

1. NOUN

/ tiː klɔθ / (AmE), / tiː klɒθ / (BrE)
A small cloth used to cover a table, typically when serving tea.
small tablecloth for serving tea

Examples

She laid a clean tea cloth on the table before serving the cakes.
The embroidered tea cloth gave the tea party a charming touch.
After washing the dishes, she folded the tea cloth and put it away in the drawer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablecloth - a cloth spread over a table, especially during meals
