dry-stone wall

1. NOUN

/ ˌdraɪ stoʊn ˈwɔl / (AmE), / ˌdraɪ stəʊn ˈwɔːl / (BrE)
A wall built from stones carefully fitted together without the use of mortar.
stone wall built without mortar

Examples

They restored the old dry-stone wall that separated the fields.
Building a dry-stone wall requires patience and skill with fitting the stones.
In rural England, dry-stone walls are a traditional feature of the landscape.
