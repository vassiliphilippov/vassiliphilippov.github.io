motor hotel

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
A hotel designed for motorists, typically providing rooms with direct access to the parking area.
hotel for motorists with direct parking access

Examples

We decided to stay at a motor hotel so we could park right outside our room.
The motor hotel offered convenient access to the highway for travelers.
Many classic American road trips feature nights spent in a motor hotel along the route.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motel - a roadside hotel designed for motorists

Etymology

Blend of 'motor' and 'hotel'; precursor to the modern word 'motel'.
