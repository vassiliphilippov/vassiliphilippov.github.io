broom beard grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːm bɛrd ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbruːm bɪəd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A hardy North American grass known for its attractive foliage, which turns pale bronze in autumn.
a hardy North American grass

Examples

The foliage of broom beard grass turns a striking bronze color in the autumn months.
Gardeners often choose broom beard grass for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
In many prairie restorations, broom beard grass provides both beauty and resilience.
