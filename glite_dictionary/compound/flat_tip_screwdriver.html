flat tip screwdriver

1. NOUN

/ flæt tɪp ˈskruːˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / flæt tɪp ˈskruːˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
A screwdriver with a flat, wedge-shaped tip designed to fit into the straight slot of a screw head.
screwdriver with a flat, wedge-shaped tip

Examples

He used a flat tip screwdriver to tighten the loose screws on the door hinge.
Make sure you select the correct size flat tip screwdriver for the job.
The instruction manual recommends a flat tip screwdriver for assembling the furniture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slotted screwdriver - screwdriver with a flat blade for slotted screws
  • Antonym: Phillips screwdriver - screwdriver with a cross-shaped tip for Phillips screws
