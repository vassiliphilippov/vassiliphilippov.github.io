rock licker

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑk ˌlɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌlɪkə / (BrE)
A person who flatters others excessively or acts overly subservient to gain personal advantage.
sycophant; excessive flatterer
slang

Trying to be a rock licker didn’t help her career as much as she hoped.
