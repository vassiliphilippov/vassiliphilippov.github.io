part-time girlfriend

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑrtˌtaɪm ˈɡɜrlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtˌtaɪm ˈɡɜːlˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a woman who is someone's romantic partner with only limited commitment or availability, often in a casual or non-exclusive relationship
casual or non-exclusive female romantic partner

Examples

He introduced her as his part-time girlfriend when people asked about their relationship.
With his demanding job, she often felt like a part-time girlfriend rather than a real partner.
They both agreed to keep things casual, so she became his part-time girlfriend.

Synset Examples

He's been seeing her as a part-time girlfriend for months.
She felt like a part-time girlfriend with his busy schedule.
He introduced her as his part-time girlfriend at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girlfriend - a female romantic partner
  • Antonym: boyfriend - a male romantic partner
  • Degree Higher: full-time girlfriend - a girlfriend with full commitment or availability
