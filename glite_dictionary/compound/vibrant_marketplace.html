vibrant marketplace

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪbrənt ˈmɑrkətˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈmɑːkɪtpleɪs / (BrE)
a market that is full of energy, activity, and vivid colors, often bustling with people and trade
lively, colorful market

Examples

Tourists are often drawn to the vibrant marketplace for its unique souvenirs and lively atmosphere.
The vibrant marketplace was filled with colorful stalls, music, and the aroma of street food.
She set up her jewelry booth in the vibrant marketplace downtown, hoping to attract new customers.

Synset Examples

She loves exploring the vibrant marketplace on weekends.
