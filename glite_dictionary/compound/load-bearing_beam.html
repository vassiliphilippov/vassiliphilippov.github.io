load-bearing beam

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊd ˌbɛrɪŋ ˈbiːm / (AmE), / ˈləʊd ˌbɛərɪŋ ˈbiːm / (BrE)
a structural beam designed to support and transmit weight from above to the foundation or supporting structure
structural beam that carries weight

Examples

The contractor checked each load-bearing beam to ensure it was properly installed.
Renovating the old house required reinforcing several load-bearing beams.
If you remove a load-bearing beam, you risk collapsing part of the structure.

Synset Examples

Inspect the load-bearing beams for any damage.
She studied the load-bearing structures in her engineering class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girder - a large, main supporting beam used in construction
  • Synonym: joist - a supporting beam running horizontally between foundations or walls
