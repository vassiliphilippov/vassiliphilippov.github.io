religious ceremony

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event or ritual performed as part of a religion
a formal ritual with religious meaning

Examples

The wedding was a beautiful religious ceremony held in the old church.
Attending a religious ceremony can give insight into a culture's values and beliefs.
The priest led the religious ceremony with prayers and hymns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - a religious or solemn ceremony consisting of a series of actions performed according to a prescribed order
  • Antonym: secular ceremony - a ceremony with no religious significance
