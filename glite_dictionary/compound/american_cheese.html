American cheese

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛrɪkən tʃiz / (AmE), / əˈmerɪkən tʃiːz / (BrE)
a processed cheese with a mild flavor and smooth, creamy texture, commonly used in sandwiches and burgers in the United States
processed cheese popular in the US
american

Examples

She added a slice of American cheese to her burger for extra flavor.
Many kids enjoy grilled cheese sandwiches made with American cheese.
The recipe calls for shredded American cheese to make the sauce creamy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cheddar - a hard, smooth-textured cheese, originally from Cheddar, England
