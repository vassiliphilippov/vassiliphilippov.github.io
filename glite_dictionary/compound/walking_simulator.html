walking simulator

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑkɪŋ ˌsɪmjəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ˌsɪmjʊˌleɪtə / (BrE)
a type of video game emphasizing exploration and interaction with the environment rather than traditional gameplay elements such as combat or puzzles
exploration-focused video game genre

Examples

Many players enjoy a walking simulator for its storytelling and immersive environments rather than action-packed gameplay.
She spent the afternoon exploring an atmospheric walking simulator set in an abandoned town.
Critics praised the walking simulator for its detailed scenery and emotional narrative.

Synset Examples

This game is more of a walking simulator than an action game.
