Kingdom of Iberia

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɪŋdəm ʌv aɪˈbɪriə / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋdəm əv aɪˈbɪəriə / (BrE)
an ancient kingdom located south of the Caucasus Mountains, corresponding roughly to modern-day eastern and southern Georgia
ancient kingdom in present-day Georgia

Examples

Archaeologists have uncovered relics from the Kingdom of Iberia in several parts of eastern Georgia.
The Kingdom of Iberia played a significant role in the spread of Christianity in the Caucasus region.
Historians believe that the ancient rulers of the Kingdom of Iberia established early forms of statehood in Georgia.

Etymology

'Iberia' from Latin, from Ancient Greek Ἰβηρία (Ibería), referring to the ancient region in the Caucasus.
