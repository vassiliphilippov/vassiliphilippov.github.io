silver grass

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a type of grass native to Australia and New Zealand, notable for its silvery appearance
Australian or New Zealand grass with a silvery sheen

Examples

The rolling hills were covered in silver grass that shimmered under the morning sun.
During our hike in New Zealand, we passed through fields of silver grass swaying in the breeze.
Gardeners often choose silver grass for its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
