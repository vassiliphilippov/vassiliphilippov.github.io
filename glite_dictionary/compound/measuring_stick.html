measuring stick

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛʒɚɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
A ruler or rod marked with units for measuring length or distance.
marked tool for measuring length

Examples

The technician used a measuring stick to ensure the shelves were the correct height.
Every classroom keeps a measuring stick for math and science projects.
They marked the garden rows with a measuring stick to plant the seeds evenly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruler - an instrument used to draw straight lines and measure length
  • Synonym: yardstick - a measuring rod a yard long, used as a standard for measurement
