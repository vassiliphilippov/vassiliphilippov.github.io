male answer syndrome

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈænsər ˈsɪndroʊm / (AmE), / meɪl ˈɑːnsə ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
the tendency for men to answer questions with confidence, even when they may lack sufficient knowledge or expertise
tendency for men to answer regardless of knowledge

Examples

Her friend joked about his male answer syndrome during trivia night.
Many people criticize male answer syndrome as a barrier to effective group discussions.
The interviewer noticed a case of male answer syndrome when he answered every question, even outside his field.
