trollface meme

1. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊlˌfeɪs miːm / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊlˌfeɪs miːm / (BrE)
an internet meme featuring a simplistic, grinning face, used to represent trolling or humorous mischief online
internet meme depicting trolling or mischief

Examples

He posted a trollface meme after winning the argument.
The trollface meme quickly became popular among internet users for its mischievous grin.
Whenever someone made a joke in the group chat, someone would reply with a trollface meme.

Synset Examples

He posted a trollface meme after winning the argument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meme - an image or phrase that spreads rapidly online
  • Synonym: troll - to intentionally provoke or annoy on the internet

Etymology

From 'trollface' (the name of the distinctive cartoon face created in 2008 by Carlos Ramirez) and 'meme' (from Greek 'mimema', meaning 'that which is imitated').
