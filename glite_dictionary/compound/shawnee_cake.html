Shawnee cake

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑːˈniː keɪk / (AmE), / ʃɔːˈniː keɪk / (BrE)
A type of johnnycake, traditionally made by Native Americans, particularly the Shawnee, consisting of cornmeal flatbread.
cornmeal flatbread; type of johnnycake

Examples

She prepared a traditional Shawnee cake using freshly ground cornmeal and honey.
During the festival, vendors sold delicious hot Shawnee cakes to visitors.
The recipe for Shawnee cake has been passed down through generations in her family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: johnnycake - a flatbread made from cornmeal, originally American

Etymology

From Shawnee, the Native American tribe, with 'cake' denoting a baked or fried bread.
