striped drum

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪpt drʌm / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt drʌm / (BrE)
a species of drumfish distinguished by horizontal stripes along its body, found in coastal waters.
a type of drumfish with stripes

Examples

The striped drum is often seen in shallow waters along the coast.
Fishermen sometimes catch a striped drum when netting near the reef.
The aquarium's newest exhibit features a beautiful striped drum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drumfish - a family of fish known for the drumming sound they produce
