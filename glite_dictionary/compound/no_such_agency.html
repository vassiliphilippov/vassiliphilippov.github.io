No Such Agency

1. NOUN

/ noʊ sʌtʃ ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / nəʊ sʌtʃ ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a humorous or euphemistic nickname for the National Security Agency (NSA), often used to imply secrecy or denial of its existence
