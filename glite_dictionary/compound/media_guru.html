media guru

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːdiə ˈɡʊru / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə ˈɡʊəruː / (BrE)
an expert who gives advice or guidance on the use of communication media, especially in marketing or public relations
expert adviser on communication media

Examples

The company hired a media guru to revamp its public image online.
As a respected media guru, she regularly appears on business news programs.
Every startup dreams of finding a media guru to boost its brand awareness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: media consultant - an adviser specializing in communication media strategies
