common sickle pine

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈsɪkəl paɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈsɪkəl paɪn / (BrE)
A small tropical rainforest tree native to Indonesia and Malaysia, known for its sickle-shaped leaves.
small rainforest tree of Indonesia and Malaysia

Examples

The common sickle pine is valued for its distinctive foliage and attractive shape.
Botanists discovered a rare common sickle pine in the heart of the Malaysian rainforest.
Efforts to conserve the common sickle pine have increased due to habitat loss.
