Wikipedia page

1. NOUN

/ wɪˈkipədiə peɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɪˈkiːpɪdiə peɪdʒ / (BrE)
A web page on the Wikipedia platform containing information on a particular topic.
a page on Wikipedia about a topic

Examples

She discovered a fascinating fact on the Wikipedia page about ancient Egypt.
The Wikipedia page needs to be updated with the latest information.
Each Wikipedia page should cite reliable sources to support its claims.

Synset Examples

He edited the Wikipedia page to correct misinformation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wikipedia article - a written entry or article on Wikipedia

Etymology

From the website name 'Wikipedia' (a blend of 'wiki' and 'encyclopedia') and 'page.'
