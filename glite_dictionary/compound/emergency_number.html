emergency number

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a telephone number designated for people to call for help in emergencies, such as police, fire, or medical services
telephone number for emergencies

Examples

In case of fire, dial the emergency number immediately.
Travelers are advised to memorize the local emergency number when visiting a new country.
The emergency number connects callers with police, ambulance, and fire services.

Synset Examples

The emergency number in this area is 1-1-8.
