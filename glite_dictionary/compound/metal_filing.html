metal filing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛt̬.əl ˈfaɪ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmet.əl ˈfaɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a small piece of metal removed from a surface by the use of a file
a small piece of metal produced by filing

Examples

After filing the bracket, tiny metal filings covered the workbench.
Always wear goggles to prevent metal filings from getting into your eyes.
The magnet quickly collected all the metal filings scattered on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filing - a small particle or shaving removed by filing
