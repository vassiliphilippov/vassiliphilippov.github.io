big daddy

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ ˈdædi / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈdædi / (BrE)
an important or influential person, especially the most powerful in a particular group or field
the most important or influential person
informal

Examples

Everyone knew he was the big daddy of the boardroom decisions.
She called him the big daddy of the neighborhood after all he had done.
The big daddy of fast food chains is opening a new branch downtown.

Synset Examples

The big daddy of tech companies is still leading.
He's the big daddy of the local music scene.
In the office, he acts like the big daddy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kingpin - a person who is essential or important in an organization or activity
  • Synonym: top dog - the most important or dominant person in a group
  • Antonym: underdog - a person or group expected to lose or be at a disadvantage
