a few

1. DET

/ ə fju / (AmE), / ə fjuː / (BrE)
a small number of people or things; more than one but not many
a small number; not many

Examples

Only a few people showed up for the meeting despite the invitation.
She brought a few cookies to share with everyone at lunch.
If you have a few minutes, I'd like to ask you something.

Synset Examples

a few roses
a couple of roses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: few - not many; a small number
  • Degree Higher: several - more than two but not many
  • Degree Lower: a couple - two; a small number, usually two
