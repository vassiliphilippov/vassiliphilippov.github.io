modern man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑdɚn mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən mæn / (BrE)
a term used to refer to the human species Homo sapiens, including all existing human populations
the human species Homo sapiens

Examples

Many scientists believe that modern man originated in Africa around 200,000 years ago.
Unlike Neanderthals, modern man developed advanced language and art.
The remains suggest that modern man and archaic humans may have interbred in ancient times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Homo sapiens - the scientific name for the human species
