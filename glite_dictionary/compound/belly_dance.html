belly dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛli dæns / (AmE), / ˈbɛli dɑːns / (BrE)
a form of Middle Eastern dance characterized by sensual movements of the hips and abdomen
Middle Eastern dance with hip and abdominal movements

Examples

She has been learning to belly dance for three years and now performs in local shows.
The festival featured a stunning belly dance performed by international artists.
Many people take belly dance classes for exercise and cultural appreciation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oriental dance - another name for belly dance, especially in Western contexts

2. VERB

/ ˈbɛli dæns / (AmE), / ˈbɛli dɑːns / (BrE)
to perform a traditional Middle Eastern dance characterized by sinuous movements of the hips and abdomen
perform a Middle Eastern hip-and-abdomen dance

Examples

She learned to belly dance while living in Cairo for a year.
Many people belly dance as a fun way to exercise and express themselves.
He was amazed when his grandmother offered to belly dance at the family party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belly dancer - a person who performs belly dance
