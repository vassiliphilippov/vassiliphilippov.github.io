night-light

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌlaɪt / (BrE)
A small light, such as a lamp or bulb, left on at night, especially in a bedroom, to provide dim illumination.
small lamp kept on at night

Examples

The child could not sleep without his night-light glowing softly in the corner.
I bought a new night-light for the guest room to help visitors find their way at night.
The night-light automatically turns on when it gets dark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamp - device for giving light
  • Synonym: candle - stick of wax with a wick that burns to provide light
