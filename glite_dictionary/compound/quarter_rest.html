quarter rest

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrt̬ɚ rɛst / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə rɛst / (BrE)
A musical symbol indicating a silence or pause equal in duration to one quarter of a whole rest.
musical rest equal to a quarter of a whole rest

Examples

The composer indicated a quarter rest at the end of the measure.
Please remember to count the quarter rest when playing your part.
He mistakenly played through the quarter rest instead of pausing.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: whole rest - a musical rest with the time value of a whole note
  • Degree Lower: eighth rest - a musical rest with half the duration of a quarter rest
