three-ring circus

1. NOUN

/ ˌθriː rɪŋ ˈsɝːkəs / (AmE), / ˌθriː rɪŋ ˈsɜːkəs / (BrE)
A circus show featuring simultaneous performances in three separate rings, often characterized by multiple acts happening at once.
circus with three simultaneous performance rings

Examples

The performers dazzled the audience at the three-ring circus with their acrobatics, clowns, and animal acts all at once.
Organizing the event felt as chaotic as a three-ring circus with dozens of things happening at the same time.
He took his children to their first three-ring circus and they were amazed by the lively spectacle.
