talker identification

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔkər aɪˌdɛntəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkə aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of identifying a person from the sound of their voice
identification by voice analysis

Examples

Forensic analysts used talker identification techniques to determine the speaker in the recording.
Advances in machine learning have improved the accuracy of talker identification systems.
In court, an expert testified about the reliability of talker identification for criminal investigations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voice identification - identification of a person from their voice
