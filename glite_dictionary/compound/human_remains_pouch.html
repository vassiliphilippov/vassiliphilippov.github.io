human remains pouch

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən rɪˈmeɪnz paʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən rɪˈmeɪnz paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a bag designed for containing and transporting the body of a deceased person, especially a soldier
bag for transporting a dead body

Examples

The recovery team placed the victim's body in a human remains pouch before transporting it to the morgue.
Each soldier's human remains pouch was clearly labeled for identification purposes.
The military issued new human remains pouches with improved sealing features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: body bag - a bag for transporting a corpse
