football league

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊt.bɔl liɡ / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl liːɡ / (BrE)
an organized competition in which football teams play against each other over a period of time
organized football competition

Examples

The city has its own football league that runs from September to December.
He was promoted to the national football league after an impressive season.
Many young athletes dream of playing in a professional football league one day.
