animal foot

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɪməl fʊt / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl fʊt / (BrE)
the foot of a non-human animal; the lowest part of the leg of a vertebrate animal, used for standing or walking
foot of a non-human animal

Examples

The veterinarian examined the injured animal foot carefully before treating the wound.
Tracks left in the snow revealed the distinct shape of an animal foot.
Some animals have pads on their animal feet to help them walk quietly through the forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paw - the foot of an animal having claws or pads
  • Synonym: foot - the lower extremity of the leg below the ankle, on which a person stands or walks
