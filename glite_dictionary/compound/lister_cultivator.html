lister cultivator

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪstər kəlˈtɪveɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlɪstə kʌlˈtɪvətə / (BrE)
a type of cultivator with blades designed to throw soil toward the roots of crops, used in agricultural practices
agricultural tool for directing soil

Examples

The farmer used a lister cultivator to prepare the soil for planting corn.
Modern lister cultivators can be adjusted to suit different row widths.
After a heavy rain, a lister cultivator helps restore the ridges around the plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultivator - a tool or machine used to loosen soil or remove weeds

Etymology

From 'lister', a type of plow, combined with 'cultivator'.
