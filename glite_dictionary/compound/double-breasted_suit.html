double-breasted suit

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌb.əl ˈbrɛs.tɪd ˌsut / (AmE), / ˌdʌb.əl ˈbrɛs.tɪd ˌsuːt / (BrE)
A suit featuring a jacket with wide, overlapping front flaps and two parallel columns of buttons.
suit with a double-breasted jacket

Examples

He wore a navy double-breasted suit to the wedding, looking very distinguished.
Fashion designers are bringing back the classic double-breasted suit this season.
A well-tailored double-breasted suit can make a strong impression at formal events.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-breasted suit - a suit with a jacket having a single row of buttons
