quarter-ass job

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrdər ˌæs dʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə ˌæs dʒɒb / (BrE)
An effort or piece of work done in a careless, insufficient, or substandard way.
carelessly done job; substandard effort
slang informal vulgar

Examples

I'm disappointed because he gave the report a quarter-ass job and didn't check any of the facts.
You can't expect great results from a quarter-ass job like that.
The repairs were so poorly done, it was clearly a quarter-ass job.

Synset Examples

He did a quarter-ass job on the presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: half-assed job - a job done with minimal effort or care
  • Antonym: good job - a job done well or carefully

Etymology

Modeled after 'half-ass(ed)' (slang for 'insufficient'), with 'quarter' signifying an even lower degree of effort. 'Ass' here is used in the sense of 'bottom' or 'effort'.
