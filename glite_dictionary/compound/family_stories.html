family stories

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈstɔriz / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈstɔːriz / (BrE)
accounts or narratives about the history, experiences, or events of a particular family, often passed down orally or in writing
narratives about family history or experiences

Examples

Whenever we visit our grandparents, we hear new family stories about their childhood adventures.
He recorded several family stories so future generations would know their heritage.
Sharing family stories at reunions helps strengthen the bond between relatives.

Synset Examples

Zio always tells the best family stories during gatherings.
My grannam always shares the best family stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anecdote - a short, amusing or interesting story about a real incident or person
  • Degree Higher: folklore - the traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community, passed through generations
