flower store

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ stɔr / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə stɔː / (BrE)
a shop that sells flowers and ornamental plants
shop selling flowers and plants

Examples

I stopped by the flower store to buy a bouquet for my friend's birthday.
The flower store on Main Street always has beautiful arrangements in the window.
She has dreamed of owning her own flower store since she was a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: florist - a person or shop selling and arranging flowers
