hoarded wealth

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrdɪd wɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːdɪd wɛlθ / (BrE)
Wealth that has been accumulated and kept, especially secretly or for a long period, often in the form of money, valuables, or jewels.
accumulated and kept wealth

Examples

The king's hoarded wealth was hidden deep within the castle walls.
During times of uncertainty, people often resort to hoarded wealth for security.
The authorities finally uncovered the hoarded wealth amassed by the corrupt official.

Synset Examples

2. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrdɪd wɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːdɪd wɛlθ / (BrE)
Money or valuables that have been accumulated and kept in reserve, often selfishly or secretly.
accumulated money or valuables kept in reserve

Examples

Rumors spread about the king's hoarded wealth hidden in a secret vault beneath the palace.
After years of hard work, her hoarded wealth allowed her to retire comfortably.
The philanthropist decided to share his hoarded wealth with local charities before he passed away.

Synset Examples
