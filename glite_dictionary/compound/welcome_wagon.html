welcome wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlkəm ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəm ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
a vehicle or service that delivers information and gifts to new residents, typically provided by local businesses to welcome them to the community
service delivering gifts to new residents

Examples

The neighborhood's welcome wagon arrived with a basket of treats and helpful local coupons.
As a new family in town, we were pleasantly surprised by a visit from the welcome wagon.
Volunteers organized a welcome wagon to greet new homeowners in the area.

Etymology

Originates from a U.S. company called 'Welcome Wagon', founded in 1928, which initiated this custom.
