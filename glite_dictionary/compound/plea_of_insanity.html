plea of insanity

1. NOUN

/ pli əv ɪnˈsænəti / (AmE), / pliː əv ɪnˈsænəti / (BrE)
a legal defense in which a defendant claims not to be responsible for their actions due to mental illness at the time of the alleged crime
legal defense claiming mental illness
legal

Examples

The defendant entered a plea of insanity at the start of the trial.
Her lawyer advised her to consider a plea of insanity due to her medical history.
The jury was unconvinced by his plea of insanity and returned a guilty verdict.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insanity defense - legal defense based on mental incapacity
