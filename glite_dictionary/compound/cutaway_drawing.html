cutaway drawing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌt̬əˌweɪ ˈdrɔɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌtəweɪ ˈdrɔːɪŋ / (BrE)
a drawing or model that shows the inside of an object by removing or omitting some of its exterior parts
drawing showing the inside of something

Examples

The textbook included a cutaway drawing of the engine to illustrate how it works.
A cutaway drawing helped us understand the complex inner workings of the building's ventilation system.
They used a detailed cutaway drawing in the presentation to show the computer's internal components.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cross section - a representation showing a slice or section through an object
