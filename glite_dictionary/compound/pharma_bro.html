Pharma Bro

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrmə broʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmə brəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a man in the pharmaceutical industry who is considered unethical or exploits drug pricing for profit
man in pharma industry with questionable ethics
slang informal

Examples

The CEO was quickly dubbed a Pharma Bro after his company's controversial price hike.
Many view Pharma Bros as symbols of corporate greed in the healthcare industry.
After the news broke, social media erupted with jokes about the latest Pharma Bro scandal.

Synset Examples

He was labeled a Pharma Bro for his tactics.
The Pharma Bro raised prices without remorse.
The media criticized the Pharma Bro for greed.

Etymology

Blend of 'pharma' (pharmaceutical) and 'bro' (slang for 'brother', informal term for a man).
