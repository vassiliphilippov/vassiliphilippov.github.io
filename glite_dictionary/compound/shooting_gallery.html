shooting gallery

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtɪŋ ˌɡæləri / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ ˌɡæləri / (BrE)
a place, often indoors, where people can practice shooting at targets with rifles or handguns
indoor range for shooting practice

Examples

The police trained regularly at the shooting gallery downtown.
She perfected her aim at the local shooting gallery before joining the competition.
Children lined up to try their luck at the shooting gallery at the fair.

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃu.tɪŋ ˌɡæl.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈʃuː.tɪŋ ˌɡæl.ə.ri / (BrE)
a place, often an abandoned building, where drug users go to buy and inject heroin or other narcotics
place where addicts buy and use heroin

Examples

The police raided the abandoned warehouse, which had become a notorious shooting gallery for local addicts.
Neighbors complained about the increase in litter from syringes near the old shooting gallery.
He described his time using heroin in a dark, crowded shooting gallery on the outskirts of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opium den - place where opium is smoked, often illicit

Etymology

Extension of 'shooting' (injecting drugs) and 'gallery' (a place for activity).
