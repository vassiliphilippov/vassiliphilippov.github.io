the Smokies

1. PROPN

/ ðə ˈsmoʊkiz / (AmE), / ðə ˈsməʊkiz / (BrE)
an informal name for the Great Smoky Mountains, a mountain range on the border of North Carolina and Tennessee in the southeastern United States
the Great Smoky Mountains (informal name)
informal american

Examples

We plan to visit the Smokies this summer for a camping trip.
The breathtaking views from the Smokies attract tourists every year.
Many species of wildlife thrive in the Smokies' diverse habitats.

Synset Examples

We spent the weekend hiking in the Smokies.
We saw a bear while driving through the Smokies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Great Smoky Mountains - mountain range on the North Carolina–Tennessee border, US

Etymology

From 'smoky' referring to the natural mist that often appears over the mountains.
