drive-by killing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv baɪ ˈkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
A homicide committed by shooting at someone from a moving automobile.
shooting murder from a moving vehicle

Examples

The police are investigating the latest drive-by killing in the city.
Residents are fearful after several drive-by killings occurred in their neighborhood.
The prosecutor described the drive-by killing as an act of gang violence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive-by shooting - an attack involving shooting from a moving vehicle

Etymology

Formed from 'drive-by' (shooting from a moving car) and 'killing'.
