serious violation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəs vaɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs vaɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
A significant or major breach of legal rules or regulations.
major breach of legal rules

Examples

Any employee found guilty of a serious violation may face immediate dismissal.
Drunk driving is considered a serious violation of traffic regulations in most countries.
The company was fined for a serious violation of environmental safety standards.

Synset Examples

Speeding is a serious violation of traffic laws.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: minor violation - a lesser or less severe breach of rules
  • Synonym: offense - an act that breaks a law or rule
