single-propeller plane

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl prəˈpɛlɚ pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl prəˈpelə pleɪn / (BrE)
An aircraft that is powered by one propeller.
airplane with one propeller

Examples

The pilot completed several training flights in a single-propeller plane.
They spotted a small single-propeller plane flying low over the countryside.
A single-propeller plane can be less expensive to operate than larger aircraft.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: twin-propeller plane - airplane with two propellers
