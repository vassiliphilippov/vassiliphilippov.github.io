arty approach

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrti əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɑːti əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a method or style that is noticeably creative or self-consciously artistic, often in a way that draws attention to its artistry
notably creative or artistic style

Examples

Her arty approach to interior design transformed the space into a creative haven.
The event had an arty approach that appealed to a younger, more innovative crowd.
With his arty approach to photography, every picture tells a unique story.

Synset Examples

He has an arty approach to fashion that stands out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artistic approach - a style or method characterized by artistic quality or creativity
