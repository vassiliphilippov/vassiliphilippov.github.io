human being

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a person; any individual member of the species Homo sapiens, distinguished by intelligence, articulate speech, and upright posture
a person; member of the human species

Examples

Every human being deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
Scientists are always searching for what makes a human being unique among animals.
The rights of every human being should be protected by law.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: person - an individual human

Etymology

From Latin humanus (human) + Old English beon (to be), meaning "a human person."
