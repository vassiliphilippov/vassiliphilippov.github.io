spike lavender

1. NOUN

/ spaɪk ˈlævəndɚ / (AmE), / spaɪk ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant with pale purple flowers, known for yielding spike lavender oil used in perfumery and aromatherapy.
Mediterranean plant with purple flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with the scent of spike lavender blooming in early summer.
Essential oils are often extracted from spike lavender for use in aromatherapy treatment.
In Mediterranean climates, spike lavender thrives in rocky, sunlit locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lavender - a genus of aromatic shrubs with purple flowers
