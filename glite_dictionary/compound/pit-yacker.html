pit-yacker

1. NOUN

/ pɪtˈjækər / (AmE), / pɪtˈjakə / (BrE)
A person who tells stories or shares gossip informally, often in a humorous way, especially relating to mining circles.
informal storyteller or gossip, often in mining

Examples

The old miners loved hearing the pit-yacker spin tales about their days underground.
Every village had at least one pit-yacker who knew all the latest happenings.
She became a well-known pit-yacker after years of sharing stories at the pub.

Synset Examples

Her tales as a pit-yacker kept everyone entertained.
As a pit-yacker, he knew all the local gossip.
The pit-yacker shared wild stories from the mines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storyteller - someone who tells stories
  • Synonym: gossip - a person who enjoys talking about others, often spreading rumors

Etymology

From 'pit' (coal mine) and dialect word 'yacker' (talker, one who chats).
