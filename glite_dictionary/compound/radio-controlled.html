radio-controlled

1. ADJ

/ ˌreɪdioʊ kənˈtroʊld / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊ kənˈtrəʊld / (BrE)
(of a device) operated or guided by remote control using radio signals.
operated by radio signals

Examples

The children played in the park with a radio-controlled car.
Scientists used a radio-controlled drone to observe the animals without disturbing them.
My father taught me how to build a radio-controlled boat when I was young.

Synset Examples

a radio-controlled airplane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remote-controlled - operated from a distance using a remote control
