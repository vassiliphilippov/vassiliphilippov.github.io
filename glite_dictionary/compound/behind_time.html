behind time

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈhaɪnd taɪm / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd taɪm / (BrE)
not arriving or happening at the arranged or expected time; late or delayed
late; not punctual

Examples

The train was behind time due to heavy snowfall on the tracks.
Sorry, I’m behind time and missed the first part of your presentation.
Deliveries are often behind time during the holiday season.

Synset Examples

I'm always behind time when it comes to meetings.
He arrived behind time, missing the beginning of the lecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: late - after the expected or usual time
  • Antonym: ahead of time - earlier than the expected or arranged time
