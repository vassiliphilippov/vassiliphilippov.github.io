flower arrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ əˌreɪndʒ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə əˌreɪndʒ.mənt / (BrE)
A decorative grouping or display of flowers, often designed artistically for aesthetic effect.
decorative display of flowers

Examples

The centerpiece was a beautiful flower arrangement of roses and lilies.
She took a course in flower arrangement to improve her decorating skills.
The hotel lobby featured an elaborate flower arrangement that welcomed guests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bouquet - an attractively arranged bunch of flowers, often given as a present
