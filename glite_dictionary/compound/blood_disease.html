blood disease

1. NOUN

/ blʌd dɪˈziz / (AmE), / blʌd dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease or disorder that affects the blood.
disease of the blood
medical

Examples

Sickle cell anemia is a type of blood disease that primarily affects people of African descent.
After several tests, the doctor diagnosed her with a rare blood disease.
Researchers are developing new treatments for various blood diseases such as leukemia and hemophilia.
