staff sergeant

1. NOUN

/ stæf ˈsɑrdʒənt / (AmE), / stɑːf ˈsɑːdʒənt / (BrE)
A noncommissioned officer ranking above corporal and below sergeant first class in the Army or Marines, or above airman first class in the Air Force.
noncommissioned military officer rank

Examples

After years of service, he was promoted to staff sergeant in the Army.
The staff sergeant led the team through the training exercise with confidence.
She reported her concerns directly to the staff sergeant.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sergeant - noncommissioned officer above corporal
  • Degree Lower: corporal - noncommissioned officer below sergeant
