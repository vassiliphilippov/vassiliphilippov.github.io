jungle of city life

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋɡəl əv ˈsɪti laɪf / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl əv ˈsɪti laɪf / (BrE)
a metaphor for the chaotic, confusing, and overwhelming nature of urban environments
chaotic urban environment (metaphor)

Examples

After moving from a small town, she found the jungle of city life hard to navigate.
The book explores how to survive the jungle of city life and build meaningful connections.
He missed the peace of the countryside after years in the jungle of city life.
