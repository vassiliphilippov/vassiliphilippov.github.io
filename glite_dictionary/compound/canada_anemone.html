Canada anemone

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnædə əˈnɛmoʊni / (AmE), / kəˈnædə əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a perennial woodland herb with white flowers, native to North America, found from Labrador to Colorado
North American woodland herb with white flowers

Examples

The Canada anemone blooms in late spring along shaded woodland streams.
Hikers admired the delicate white flowers of the Canada anemone bordering the trail.
Gardeners appreciate the hardiness of Canada anemone in natural gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anemone - a genus of flowering plants known as windflowers
