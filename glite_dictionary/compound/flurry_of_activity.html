flurry of activity

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɝːi əv ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈflʌri əv ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
a sudden and intense period of energetic actions or events
sudden burst of energetic activity

Examples

Following the manager's sudden resignation, there was a flurry of activity in the HR department.
A flurry of activity erupted in the kitchen as dinner time approached.
The new product launch sparked a flurry of activity among the marketing team.

Synset Examples

The announcement caused a flurry of activity in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burst of activity - a sudden, brief period of energetic actions
