lobed spleenwort

1. NOUN

/ loʊbd ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / ləʊbd ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A species of spleenwort fern native to eastern and southern regions of the United States.
a species of spleenwort fern

Examples

The lobed spleenwort is often found growing on shaded limestone rocks.
Botanists conducted a survey of lobed spleenwort populations in Georgia.
Many gardeners value the lobed spleenwort for its attractive fronds and hardy nature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spleenwort - a type of fern belonging to the genus Asplenium
