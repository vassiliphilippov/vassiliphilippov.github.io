street cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrit ˌklinər / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌkliːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean public streets, typically working for a city or municipal department
person who cleans public streets

Examples

A street cleaner swept the roads early in the morning before traffic began.
The city employs dozens of street cleaners to keep neighborhoods tidy.
She worked as a street cleaner for several years before changing careers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanitation worker - person employed to clean and maintain public spaces
