green smoothie

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈsmuði / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈsmuːði / (BrE)
a blended drink made primarily from leafy green vegetables and fruit, often consumed for its health benefits
smoothie with leafy greens and fruit

Examples

She makes a green smoothie every morning for breakfast to boost her energy.
Adding spinach to your green smoothie is a great way to increase your daily intake of vegetables.
He offered me a taste of his homemade green smoothie, which included kale and pineapple.

Synset Examples

He loves to start his day with a green smoothie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothie - a thick, smooth drink made from pureed raw fruit and/or vegetables
