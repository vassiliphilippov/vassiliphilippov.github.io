cricket bat

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪkət bæt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt bæt / (BrE)
A flat, broad-bladed wooden club used to hit the ball in the game of cricket.
bat used in cricket

Examples

He tightened his grip on the cricket bat before stepping up to face the bowler.
The store sells a variety of cricket bats suitable for beginners and professionals alike.
After the match, she proudly displayed her signed cricket bat in her room.

Synset Examples

a cricket bat has a narrow handle and a broad flat end for hitting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bat - an implement with a handle and solid surface, used to hit the ball in sports
