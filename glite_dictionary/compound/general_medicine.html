general medicine

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnərəl ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and nonsurgical treatment of diseases affecting internal organs, especially in adults
medical field treating internal diseases
medical

Examples

After completing his studies, he decided to specialize in general medicine.
Many patients with chronic conditions are referred to the department of general medicine.
She works as a consultant in general medicine at the city hospital.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internal medicine - the medical specialty dealing with nonsurgical treatment of internal diseases
  • Antonym: surgery - the branch of medicine concerned with surgical procedures
