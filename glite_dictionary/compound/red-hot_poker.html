red-hot poker

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛd hɑt ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / ˌrɛd hɒt ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
a tall flowering plant of the genus Kniphofia, grown for its spike of bright red, yellow, or orange tubular flowers resembling a red-hot iron poker
a tall plant with brightly colored flower spikes

Examples

The red-hot poker brightened up the garden with its vibrant flower spikes.
Gardeners often choose red-hot pokers for their ability to attract hummingbirds and butterflies.
A row of red-hot poker plants lined the pathway, creating a striking summer display.
