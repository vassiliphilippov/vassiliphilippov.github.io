furniture maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜrnɪtʃər ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪtʃə ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes furniture, especially as a profession
person who makes furniture

Examples

The furniture maker crafted a beautiful oak dining table for the client.
After years as a furniture maker, she opened her own workshop in the city.
Each furniture maker at the exhibition displayed unique handcrafted pieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpenter - a person who makes or repairs wooden objects and structures
  • Synonym: upholsterer - a person who puts padding, springs, and covers on furniture
