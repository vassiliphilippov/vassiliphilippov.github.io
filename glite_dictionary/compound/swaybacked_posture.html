swaybacked posture

1. NOUN

/ ˈsweɪˌbækt ˈpɑstʃər / (AmE), / ˈsweɪˌbækt ˈpɒstjə / (BrE)
A posture in which the back curves inward excessively, resulting in a concave appearance.
concave-curved back posture

Examples

Years of horseback riding had given her a noticeable swaybacked posture.
The doctor explained that a swaybacked posture could lead to back pain if left untreated.
Correcting his swaybacked posture required regular physical therapy sessions.

Synset Examples

Her swaybacked posture indicated a long day of labor.
