sheep frog

1. NOUN

/ ʃip frɑɡ / (AmE), / ʃiːp frɒɡ / (BrE)
a small terrestrial frog of Central America, known for its bleating call that resembles the sound of a sheep
a Central American frog with a sheep-like call

Examples

The distinctive bleating call of the sheep frog can often be heard during the rainy season.
Researchers are studying the habitat preferences of the sheep frog in Central America.
At night, a chorus of sheep frogs filled the air near the pond.
