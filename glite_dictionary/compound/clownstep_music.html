clownstep music

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaʊnˌstɛp ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈklaʊnˌstɛp ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
a subgenre of drum and bass music characterized by playful or bouncy rhythms and often distinctive, 'clownish' sound effects.
bouncy subgenre of drum and bass music

Examples

The DJ played a set of clownstep music that had the whole crowd jumping.
Some critics argue that clownstep music is a playful offshoot of traditional drum and bass.
I discovered clownstep music at a music festival and instantly loved its quirky rhythms.

Synset Examples

Clownstep music got everyone on the dance floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drum and bass - a genre of electronic dance music with fast beats and heavy bass

Etymology

Formed from 'clown' (referring to the playful sound) and 'step' (as in '2-step' or 'dubstep', music genres).
