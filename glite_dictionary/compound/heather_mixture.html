heather mixture

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛðər ˈmɪkstʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɛðə ˈmɪkstʃə / (BrE)
Yarn or fabric made by interweaving mixed colors, creating a muted, greyish appearance with subtle flecks of color.
muted, flecked mixed-color yarn or fabric

Examples

She chose a sweater knitted from heather mixture for its soft, subtle tones.
The jacket's heather mixture gives it a unique, understated style.
Many scarves this season feature a heather mixture that pairs easily with different outfits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heather - yarn or fabric with interwoven colors resembling heather plant tones

Etymology

From 'heather', referencing the colors of the heather plant, plus 'mixture', meaning a combination of elements.
