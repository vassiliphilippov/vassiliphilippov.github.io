beach house

1. NOUN

/ biːt͡ʃ haʊs / (AmE), / biːtʃ haʊs / (BrE)
a house located on or near a beach, typically used as a vacation home
house on or near a beach

Examples

We rented a beach house for our summer holiday in Florida.
The hurricane caused significant damage to their beach house last year.
Owning a beach house is a dream for many people who love the ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cottage - a small, simple house, typically in the countryside or by the sea
