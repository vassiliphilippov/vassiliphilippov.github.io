artificial script

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːr.təˈfɪʃ.əl skrɪpt / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl skrɪpt / (BrE)
a writing system that is deliberately created by an individual or group, rather than naturally evolving as part of a language or culture
a deliberately created writing system

Examples

The linguist designed an artificial script to accompany her newly invented language.
Many fantasy novels include an artificial script for the fictional races depicted in their stories.
Learning an artificial script can be challenging due to its unfamiliar structure and symbols.

Synset Examples

Balin-Silel is a constructed script invented by Aayush Chaturvedi in November 2014 to write his conlang by the same name.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: natural script - a writing system that has evolved naturally within a culture or language
  • Synonym: constructed script - synonym for artificially created writing system
