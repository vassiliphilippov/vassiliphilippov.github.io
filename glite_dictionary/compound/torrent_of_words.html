torrent of words

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔr.ənt əv wɝdz / (AmE), / ˈtɒr.ənt əv wɜːdz / (BrE)
a rapid and continuous flow of speech or talk, often delivered with great intensity or emotion
rapid, continuous speech

Examples

The politician unleashed a torrent of words during the heated debate.
Caught up in excitement, she spilled out a torrent of words about her adventure.
As soon as the meeting began, a torrent of words poured from the anxious presenter.

Synset Examples

He spoke in a torrent of words, barely pausing for breath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flood of words - an overwhelming amount of speech
