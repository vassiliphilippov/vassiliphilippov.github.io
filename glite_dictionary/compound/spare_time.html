spare time

1. NOUN

/ spɛr taɪm / (AmE), / spɛə taɪm / (BrE)
time when you are not working and can do what you enjoy
free time for leisure activities

Examples

I like to read novels in my spare time.
She spends her spare time volunteering at the animal shelter.
What do you usually do with your spare time?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free time - time when you are not busy working or doing essential tasks
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-15162377-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ spɛr taɪm / (AmE), / spɛː taɪm / (BrE)
time when you are not working or busy with other responsibilities
free time; time not occupied by duties

Examples

She enjoys reading novels in her spare time.
I learned to play guitar during my spare time after work.
What do you usually do with your spare time?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free time - time when you are not required to work or fulfill responsibilities
