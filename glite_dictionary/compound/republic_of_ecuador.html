Republic of Ecuador

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈekwədɔr / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈekwədɔː / (BrE)
a country in northwestern South America, bordered by Colombia, Peru, and the Pacific Ocean; its capital is Quito.
country in northwestern South America

Examples

The Republic of Ecuador is famous for its breathtaking Galápagos Islands and diverse wildlife.
After traveling through Colombia, she entered the Republic of Ecuador by bus.
The Andes Mountains traverse the Republic of Ecuador, offering stunning landscapes and hiking opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ecuador - a country in northwestern South America (short form)

Etymology

Ecuador from Spanish 'República del Ecuador', meaning 'Republic of the Equator', as the country is named after the equator line.
