Ave Maria

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑː.veɪ məˈriː.ə / (AmE), / ˌɑː.veɪ məˈrɪə / (BrE)
a prayer or salutation to the Virgin Mary, traditionally used in Christian liturgy and devotion
prayer to the Virgin Mary

Examples

During the wedding ceremony, a choir sang the Ave Maria beautifully.
Many Catholics recite the Ave Maria daily as part of their prayers.
Schubert's version of Ave Maria is popular at both weddings and funerals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hail Mary - a prayer asking for the intercession of the Virgin Mary, especially in the Roman Catholic tradition

Etymology

From Latin, meaning 'Hail Mary'.
