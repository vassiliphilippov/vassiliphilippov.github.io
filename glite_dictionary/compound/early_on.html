early on

1. ADV

/ ˈɝːli ɑn / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ɒn / (BrE)
at or during an early stage in a process or period of time
at an early stage

Examples

It’s hard to judge the project’s success early on, but the signs are positive.
He realized early on that learning a new language would require patience.
