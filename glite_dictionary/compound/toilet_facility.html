toilet facility

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔɪlət fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
A room or building containing a toilet or toilets available for public use.
public restroom/toilet

Examples

The museum has a toilet facility near the main entrance for visitors.
Many parks offer a toilet facility that is wheelchair accessible.
The festival organizers installed an extra toilet facility to accommodate the large crowds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public toilet - a toilet accessible to the public
