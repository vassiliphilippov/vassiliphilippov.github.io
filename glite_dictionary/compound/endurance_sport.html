endurance sport

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʊr.əns spɔrt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjʊə.rəns spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport that requires prolonged physical effort and stamina over an extended period of time.
sport requiring prolonged physical effort

Examples

Triathlon is a challenging endurance sport that combines swimming, cycling, and running.
Many athletes prefer endurance sports because they test both mental and physical stamina.
Training for an endurance sport requires careful planning and commitment over many months.

Synset Examples

Despite being unmuscular, he excelled in endurance sports.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: team sport - sport played by teams rather than individuals
  • Degree Lower: strength sport - sport focused on physical power, such as weightlifting
