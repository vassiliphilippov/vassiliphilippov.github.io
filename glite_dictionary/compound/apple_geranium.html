apple geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpəl dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈæpl dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a type of geranium with round, fragrant leaves and small white flowers
geranium with fragrant round leaves

Examples

I planted an apple geranium in my kitchen window because it releases a sweet aroma.
The apple geranium is popular for its rounded, fragrant leaves and delicate white flowers.
She crushed a leaf from the apple geranium to add a fresh scent to the room.
