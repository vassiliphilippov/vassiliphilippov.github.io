Vietnamese language

1. NOUN

/ viˌɛtnəˈmiz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / viˌɛtnəˈmiːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language of Vietnam, belonging to the Austroasiatic language family
official language of Vietnam

Examples

She is fluent in both English and Vietnamese language.
The Vietnamese language has six tones that can change the meaning of a word.
They offer Vietnamese language classes at the local community center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vietnamese - a person from Vietnam or relating to Vietnam

Etymology

From 'Vietnamese' + 'language'; 'Vietnamese' from French 'Vietnamien,' from 'Việt Nam.'
