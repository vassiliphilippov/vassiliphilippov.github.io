swim meet

1. NOUN

/ swɪm mit / (AmE), / swɪm miːt / (BrE)
an event in which swimmers from two or more teams compete against each other across various races
competitive swimming event

Examples

Our school is hosting a swim meet this Saturday, and everyone is encouraged to attend.
She qualified for the finals during last weekend’s swim meet in Dallas.
The team has been training hard all season to prepare for the regional swim meet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming competition - a contest in which swimmers race against each other
