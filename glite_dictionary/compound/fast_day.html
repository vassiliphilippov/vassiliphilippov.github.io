fast day

1. NOUN

/ fæst deɪ / (AmE), / fɑːst deɪ / (BrE)
a day set aside for abstaining from food, typically for religious or spiritual reasons
day designated for fasting

Examples

Many people observe a fast day as part of their religious tradition.
The church announced that next Friday will be a fast day for the entire community.
During Lent, several fast days are observed by the faithful.
