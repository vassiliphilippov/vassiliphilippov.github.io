piling hammer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪlɪŋ ˌhæmɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlɪŋ ˌhamə / (BrE)
A heavy machine used for driving piles into the ground, typically during construction work.
machine for driving piles into ground

Examples

The crew used a piling hammer to drive the steel beams deep into the riverbed.
A hydraulic piling hammer can make the process much faster and more efficient.
The noise from the piling hammer could be heard across the construction site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pile driver - a machine that drives piles into the ground
