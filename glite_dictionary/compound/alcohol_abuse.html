alcohol abuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈæl.kəˌhɑl əˈbjus / (AmE), / ˈæl.kəˌhɒl əˈbjuːs / (BrE)

excessive use of alcohol

Examples

Programs for treating alcohol abuse are available in most cities.
