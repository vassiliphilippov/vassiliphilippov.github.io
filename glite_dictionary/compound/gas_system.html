gas system

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ɡas ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a facility or network of equipment for supplying natural gas to homes, businesses, or industrial clients
facility for supplying natural gas

Examples

The city's gas system was upgraded to handle increased demand during the winter months.
Routine maintenance ensures that the gas system operates safely and efficiently.
A faulty gas system can pose serious risks to public safety if not properly managed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas network - system of pipes for the distribution of natural gas
