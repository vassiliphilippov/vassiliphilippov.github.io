Hebrew text

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːbruː tɛkst / (AmE), / ˈhiːbruː tɛkst / (BrE)
written material composed in the Hebrew language, typically using the Hebrew alphabet
written material in Hebrew

Examples

He spent hours studying the Hebrew text of the manuscript.
The prayer was recited in both English and Hebrew text.
Scholars compared the ancient Hebrew text with its modern translation.

Synset Examples

He read the Hebrew text with great enthusiasm.
