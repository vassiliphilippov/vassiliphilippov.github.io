Icelandic-speaking

1. ADJ

/ aɪˈslændɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪˈslændɪk ˌspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
able to speak or communicate in the Icelandic language
able to speak Icelandic

Examples

Many Icelandic-speaking tourists visit the old town during the summer months.
The seminar will be conducted in English, but Icelandic-speaking staff will be available to help with translation.
She joined an Icelandic-speaking club to improve her language skills after moving to Reykjavik.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: English-speaking - able to communicate in English
  • Antonym: non-Icelandic-speaking - not able to communicate in Icelandic
