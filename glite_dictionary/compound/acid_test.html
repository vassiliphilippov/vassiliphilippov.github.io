acid test

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsɪd tɛst / (AmE), / ˈæsɪd tɛst / (BrE)
a decisive test that proves the value, quality, or truth of something
decisive or crucial test

Examples

Passing this exam is the acid test for her programming skills.
The new smartphone will face its acid test when released to the public.
Winning over skeptical investors was the acid test for the startup team.

Synset Examples

Passing this exam is the acid test for her skills.
The new device will undergo an acid test next week.
The project’s success will be the acid test of their teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: litmus test - a test that determines the true character or quality of something

Etymology

Originally referring to the use of acid to test for gold's authenticity in the 19th century.
