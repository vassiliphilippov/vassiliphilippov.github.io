barking deer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrkɪŋ ˌdɪr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkɪŋ ˌdɪə / (BrE)
a small Asian deer, also known as a muntjac, characterized by short antlers and a barking call
small Asian deer with a barking call

Examples

The barking deer is known for its loud, dog-like cries in the jungle.
Researchers spotted a barking deer near the forest edge at dawn.
Unlike other deer, the barking deer is often heard before it is seen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muntjac - another name for barking deer; a small Asian deer with short antlers and a barking cry
