Inspector Maigret

1. PROPN

/ ɪnˈspɛktər maɪˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛktə ˈmaːɡreɪ / (BrE)
a fictional French detective created by the writer Georges Simenon, known for his calm and methodical investigations
fictional detective by Simenon

Examples

Many readers admire how Inspector Maigret remains so calm under pressure during his investigations.
The latest television adaptation brought Inspector Maigret to life with remarkable accuracy.
Georges Simenon's novels featuring Inspector Maigret are considered classics of detective fiction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sherlock Holmes - a famous fictional British detective created by Arthur Conan Doyle

Etymology

From French, character created by Belgian author Georges Simenon.
