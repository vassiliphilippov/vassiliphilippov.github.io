Cornhusker State

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɔrnˌhʌskər steɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌhʌskə steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for Nebraska, a Midwestern state located on the Great Plains of the United States.
nickname for the state of Nebraska

Examples

Many people travel across the Cornhusker State to experience its scenic plains and friendly towns.
The University of Nebraska, located in the Cornhusker State, has a strong football tradition.
Agriculture plays a major role in the economy of the Cornhusker State.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nebraska - A Midwestern state in the U.S. known as the Cornhusker State

Etymology

Named after the University of Nebraska's athletic teams, the 'Cornhuskers', referring to the state's corn production.
