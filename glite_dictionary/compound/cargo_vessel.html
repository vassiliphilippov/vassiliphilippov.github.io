cargo vessel

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ ˈvɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ ˈvɛsəl / (BrE)
A ship specially designed to transport goods and cargo.
ship for carrying cargo

Examples

The port handles hundreds of cargo vessels every year.
A massive cargo vessel docked at the harbor early this morning.
It took days to unload all the containers from the cargo vessel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freighter - a ship used for carrying freight
  • Antonym: passenger ship - a ship designed to carry people rather than goods
