Turd World

1. NOUN

/ tɜrd wɝld / (AmE), / tɜːd wɜːld / (BrE)
(Slang, offensive) A derogatory term used to refer to a chaotic, dysfunctional, or undesirable situation, often as a play on 'Third World'.
slang for a chaotic or dysfunctional situation
slang offensive

Examples

After the power outage and flooding, the city felt like Turd World for days.
He joked that his cluttered garage had become Turd World overnight.
Some critics describe online comment sections as pure Turd World.

Synset Examples

After that argument, it felt like we were in Turd World.
Some people think social media is like living in Turd World.
He jokingly referred to his messy apartment as Turd World.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Third World - Term for developing countries, often used disparagingly

Etymology

Formed as a pun on 'Third World', replacing 'Third' with 'Turd' (slang for feces).
