Unbrako key

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈbreɪkoʊ kiː / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrɑːkəʊ kiː / (BrE)
A type of wrench with a hexagonal cross-section, used for driving Allen screws.
hexagonal wrench for Allen screws

Examples

He always keeps an Unbrako key in his toolbox for assembling furniture.
The technician needed an Unbrako key to tighten the machine's bolts.
Could you pass me the 5mm Unbrako key?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Allen key - a wrench with a hexagonal cross-section, also used for Allen screws

Etymology

From 'Unbrako,' a trademark for a brand of fasteners, combined with 'key.'
