fire opal

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌoʊpəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌəʊpəl / (BrE)
A variety of opal gemstone with bright, fiery orange, yellow, and red colors.
opal gemstone with fiery colors

Examples

The jeweler showed me a beautiful fire opal set in a gold ring.
She admired the fire opal for its vivid orange and red hues.
Collectors often seek out the rarest fire opals for their gemstone collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opal - a translucent mineral of hydrated silica, often showing iridescent colors
