steak sauce

1. NOUN

/ steɪk sɔs / (AmE), / steɪk sɔːs / (BrE)
a savory, often pungent bottled sauce served as a condiment for steak and other meats
savory sauce served with steak

Examples

He poured some steak sauce over his grilled sirloin to enhance the flavor.
The restaurant makes its own signature steak sauce with herbs and spices.
Do you prefer your steak sauce spicy or mild?
