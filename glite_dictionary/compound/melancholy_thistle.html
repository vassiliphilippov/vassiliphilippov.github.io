melancholy thistle

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛl.ənˌkɑ.li ˈθɪs.əl / (AmE), / ˈmɛl.ən.kəli ˈθɪs.əl / (BrE)
a perennial thistle of northern Europe with lance-shaped leaves and typically solitary reddish-purple flower heads
a European thistle with reddish-purple flowers

Examples

The melancholy thistle can often be found blooming in Scottish meadows during summer.
Botanists admire the unique reddish-purple flowers of the melancholy thistle.
Local folklore attributes special healing properties to the melancholy thistle.
