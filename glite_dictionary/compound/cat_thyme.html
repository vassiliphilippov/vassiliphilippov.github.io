cat thyme

1. NOUN

/ kæt θaɪm / (AmE), / kæt θaɪm / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant (Teucrium marum) with small hairy leaves and reddish-purple flowers, known for attracting cats.
Mediterranean plant attractive to cats

Examples

My garden's cat thyme attracts every stray feline in the neighborhood.
Botanists often recommend cat thyme for people wanting a natural way to entertain their cats.
The cat thyme bloomed with vibrant reddish-purple flowers this spring.
