poster boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstər bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A man or boy who is seen as a perfect example of something, often used as a symbol for a cause or movement.
male symbol or prime example

Examples

He became the poster boy for environmental activism after his campaign went viral.
That athlete is often called the poster boy for clean living and hard work.
In the media, he was unfairly portrayed as the poster boy for corporate greed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poster child - a person who epitomizes or represents a cause or quality, often in publicity or fundraising

Etymology

Based on 'poster child' (originally referring to children featured on posters for charitable campaigns).
