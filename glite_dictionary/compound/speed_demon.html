speed demon

1. NOUN

/ spid ˈdimən / (AmE), / spiːd ˈdiːmən / (BrE)
a person who habitually drives a vehicle at dangerously high speeds, often exceeding speed limits
reckless fast driver

Examples

He drives like a speed demon whenever he's late for work.
The police stopped a speed demon on the highway for going 100 miles per hour.
Don't be a speed demon—safety is more important than arriving a few minutes early.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reckless driver - a person who drives dangerously without regard for safety
  • Antonym: slowpoke - a person who drives or moves very slowly
