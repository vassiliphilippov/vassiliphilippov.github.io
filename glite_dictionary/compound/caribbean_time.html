Caribbean time

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪbiən taɪm / (AmE), / kəˈrɪbi.ən taɪm / (BrE)
a relaxed or flexible approach to punctuality and time management, often associated with Caribbean cultures
relaxed attitude toward punctuality

Examples

Many islanders live by Caribbean time, never rushing to meet strict deadlines.
On Caribbean time, being an hour late to a party isn't unusual at all.
After moving to Jamaica, he quickly adapted to the local Caribbean time mentality.

Synset Examples

He always jokes about his friends’ Caribbean time habits.
