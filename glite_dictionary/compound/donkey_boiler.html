donkey boiler

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑŋki ˈbɔɪlər / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki ˈbɔɪlə / (BrE)
a small auxiliary boiler on a ship, used especially when the vessel is in port
auxiliary ship boiler used in port

Examples

The chief engineer inspected the donkey boiler before the ship docked for repairs.
While in port, the crew relied on the donkey boiler for steam and heating.
Some older vessels still have a functional donkey boiler for auxiliary power.

Etymology

From 'donkey' (suggesting something auxiliary or secondary) and 'boiler'.
