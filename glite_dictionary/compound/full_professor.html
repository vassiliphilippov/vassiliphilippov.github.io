full professor

1. NOUN

/ fʊl prəˈfɛsɚ / (AmE), / fʊl prəˈfɛsə / (BrE)
a professor who has attained the highest academic rank at a college or university
professor of the highest academic rank

Examples

After years of research and teaching, Dr. Lee was promoted to full professor.
Becoming a full professor requires outstanding achievements in both scholarship and teaching.
He aspired to be a full professor before the age of fifty.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: associate professor - an academic rank below full professor
