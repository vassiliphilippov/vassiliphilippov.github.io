wolf-sized

1. ADJ

/ wʊlf saɪzd / (AmE), / wʊlf saɪzd / (BrE)
having the approximate size of a wolf
about the size of a wolf

Examples

The animal in the photograph appeared to be wolf-sized, much larger than a typical dog.
We encountered a wolf-sized creature prowling by the campsite at dusk.
Some prehistoric mammals were wolf-sized but had completely different features.
