dark side of technology

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk saɪd əv tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / dɑːk saɪd əv tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The negative or harmful aspects of technological advancements or use.
harmful aspects of technology

Examples

Recent documentaries have highlighted the dark side of technology, including privacy invasion and social isolation.
Many parents worry about the dark side of technology when raising children in a digital age.
The conference included a panel discussing the dark side of technology and its impact on mental health.

Synset Examples

Black Mirror explores the dark side of technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downsides of technology - the disadvantages of technology
  • Antonym: benefits of technology - the positive aspects of technology
