to do you for

1. VERB

/ du ˈju fɔr / (AmE), / duː ˈjuː fə / (BrE)
(humorous, informal) Used to ask someone what service or assistance they require; a playful variant of 'what can I do for you?'
humorous way to ask how to help someone
informal

Examples

The shopkeeper smiled and said, "What can I do you for today?"
As the bartender approached, he asked, "Evening! What can I do you for?"
She greeted me with a grin and said, "Morning! What can I do you for on this beautiful day?"

Synset Examples

Welcome! What can I do you for today?
Hello! What can I do you for on this fine day?
Hi there! What can I do you for this afternoon?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: do for - to serve or provide for someone; to do something that benefits someone
