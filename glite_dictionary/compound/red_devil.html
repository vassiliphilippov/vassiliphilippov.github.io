red devil

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd ˌdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˌdɛvəl / (BrE)
A barbiturate drug (trade name Seconal), used as a sodium salt for sedation and treating convulsions.
barbiturate drug (Seconal) used for sedation
medical

Examples

The doctor warned him about the risks of taking red devil without a prescription.
In the 1960s, red devil was sometimes misused as a recreational drug.
She was given a dose of red devil to calm her before the procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Seconal - a brand name of the barbiturate drug secobarbital sodium
  • Degree Lower: barbiturate - a class of drugs acting as central nervous system depressants
