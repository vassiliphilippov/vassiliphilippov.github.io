sewage disposal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɪdʒ dɪˈspoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪdʒ dɪˈspəʊzəl / (BrE)
the process of removing and treating waste water and excrement from homes and other buildings
removal and treatment of waste water

Examples

Many cities invest heavily in sewage disposal systems to protect public health.
Improper sewage disposal can lead to serious environmental problems.
Upgrades to the sewage disposal plant reduced river pollution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste disposal - the act of getting rid of unwanted materials
