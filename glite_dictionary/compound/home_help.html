home help

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm hɛlp / (AmE), / həʊm hɛlp / (BrE)
a person employed to assist with household tasks, especially by a local authority to support the elderly or infirm in their own home
domestic assistant for the elderly or infirm
british

Examples

Mrs. Brown relies on a home help to keep her apartment tidy and do the shopping.
The council arranged for a home help to visit my grandmother twice a week.
Many elderly people value the independence that a home help allows them to maintain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carer - a person who provides care for someone else, especially the elderly or disabled
