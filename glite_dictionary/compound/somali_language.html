Somali language

1. NOUN

/ səˈmɑːli ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈmɑːli ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Cushitic language spoken by the Somali people, chiefly in Somalia and neighboring regions.
language spoken in Somalia

Examples

Many people in the Horn of Africa speak the Somali language as their mother tongue.
She is fluent in both English and the Somali language.
The Somali language uses the Latin alphabet for its written form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cushitic - a branch of the Afroasiatic languages

Etymology

Named after the Somali people; 'Somali' possibly from the native ethnonym Soomaal.
