Turkmen language

1. NOUN

/ tɝːkˈmɛn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɜːkˈmen ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Turkic language spoken by the Turkmen people, primarily in Turkmenistan and surrounding regions
the Turkic language of the Turkmen people

Examples

She is fluent in both Russian and Turkmen language.
The Turkmen language uses a Latin-based alphabet in Turkmenistan.
This dictionary provides translations between English and the Turkmen language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - the Turkic language of Turkey
  • Synonym: Uzbek - the Turkic language spoken by Uzbeks

Etymology

Turkmen from Persian 'Turkman,' language from Latin 'lingua' (tongue, speech).
