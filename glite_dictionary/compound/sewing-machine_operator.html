sewing-machine operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊɪŋ məˌʃin ˈɑpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ məˌʃiːn ˈɒpəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sew fabrics or materials by using a sewing machine, typically in a factory or workshop
person who operates a sewing machine

Examples

The sewing-machine operator carefully stitched the pieces of fabric together on the factory floor.
Experienced sewing-machine operators are in high demand in the garment industry.
She worked as a sewing-machine operator for over ten years before she became a supervisor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seamstress - a woman whose occupation is sewing
  • Synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters garments professionally
