year-round

1. ADJ

/ jɪr ˈraʊnd / (AmE), / jɪə ˈraʊnd / (BrE)
happening, used, or available throughout the entire year.
all year long

Examples

The indoor pool is open to guests year-round no matter the weather.
Many residents enjoy the mild climate, which allows for year-round gardening.
This museum offers year-round programs for children and families.

Synset Examples

They built a greenhouse to grow vegetables year-round.
The altitudinous peaks were covered in snow year-round.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seasonal - available or occurring only during a particular season
