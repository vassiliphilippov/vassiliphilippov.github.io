sport jacket

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt ˈdʒækət / (AmE), / spɔːt ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A lightweight, single-breasted jacket, often with stripes or patterns, worn for informal or semi-formal occasions.
lightweight, single-breasted informal jacket

Examples

He wore a navy sport jacket to the dinner party.
A striped sport jacket can add color to your wardrobe.
The university crest was embroidered on his sport jacket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazer - a type of smart jacket, often with a naval or club association
  • Degree Higher: suit jacket - a formal jacket that is part of a matching suit
