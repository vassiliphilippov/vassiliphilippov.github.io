taig community

1. NOUN

/ taɪɡ kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / taɪɡ kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
A group of people who identify with Irish nationalism, particularly in a Northern Irish context.
group identifying with Irish nationalism
offensive british

Examples

The taig community has faced significant challenges throughout history.
Many members of the taig community participate in cultural festivals each year.
Discussions about the taig community often arise in the context of Northern Irish politics.

Synset Examples

The taig community celebrates its heritage proudly.

Etymology

From 'taig', an offensive term for an Irish Catholic, chiefly in Northern Ireland, and 'community'.
