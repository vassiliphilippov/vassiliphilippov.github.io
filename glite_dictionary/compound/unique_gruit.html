unique gruit

1. NOUN

/ juˈnik ˈɡruːɪt / (AmE), / juːˈniːk ˈɡruːɪt / (BrE)
A type of herbal beer brewed with a distinct combination of herbs and spices, differing from traditional, hop-based ales.
distinct herbal beer

Examples

At the tasting event, I discovered a unique gruit with hints of rosemary and orange peel.
The brewery is famous for its unique gruit that stands out among craft beers.
Many beer enthusiasts appreciate trying a unique gruit for its complex and aromatic flavors.

Synset Examples

I tried a unique gruit that had a floral taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gruit - herbal mixture used for brewing beer
  • Antonym: ale - type of beer brewed using a warm fermentation method

Etymology

Gruit: from Dutch gruit (herb mixture used in brewing).
