summer camp

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmɚ kæmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə kæmp / (BrE)
a place where children are cared for and participate in organized activities during the summer months, typically away from home
place for children with activities during the summer

Examples

My children spent two weeks at summer camp and loved every minute of it.
She made many lifelong friends during her time at summer camp.
Every July, the school organizes a summer camp for young learners.

Synset Examples

city kids get to see the country at a summer camp

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camp - a place where people stay temporarily with tents or cabins
