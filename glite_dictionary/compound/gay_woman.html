gay woman

1. NOUN

/ ɡeɪ ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ɡeɪ ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who is attracted romantically or sexually to other women; a female homosexual.
a female homosexual

Examples

She proudly identifies as a gay woman and supports LGBTQ+ rights.
The documentary featured the life stories of several gay women from around the world.
As a gay woman, she found comfort and acceptance within her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lesbian - a woman who is homosexual
