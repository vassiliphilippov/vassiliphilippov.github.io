eye tooth

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ tuθ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ tuːθ / (BrE)
one of the four pointed teeth (canines) between the incisors and premolars in each jaw, particularly the upper canine
canine tooth between incisors and premolars

Examples

The dentist explained that my eye tooth was causing the discomfort in my upper jaw.
Losing an eye tooth can affect the alignment of your other teeth.
She needed braces because her eye teeth were not properly aligned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canine - a pointed tooth situated between the incisors and the premolars

Etymology

From the belief that the upper canine is situated directly beneath the eye.
