first attempt

1. NOUN

/ fɝst əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / fɜːst əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
the initial effort or try made to do something, especially when doing it for the first time
the initial effort or try

Examples

His first attempt at painting was surprisingly good for a beginner.
After her first attempt to climb the mountain failed, she trained even harder.
The cookies didn't turn out well on my first attempt, but I kept trying.

Synset Examples

This is her first attempt at baking bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attempt - an act of trying to do something, especially something difficult
