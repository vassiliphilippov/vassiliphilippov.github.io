smoky quartz

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmoʊki kwɔrts / (AmE), / ˈsməʊki kwɔːts / (BrE)
a variety of quartz that is smoky yellow or brown in color, often used as a gemstone
a brown or smoky yellow variety of quartz

Examples

The pendant featured a beautiful smoky quartz set in a silver frame.
Collectors prize high-quality smoky quartz for its unique coloration.
He bought a ring adorned with a large piece of smoky quartz.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: quartz - a hard, crystalline mineral composed of silicon dioxide
