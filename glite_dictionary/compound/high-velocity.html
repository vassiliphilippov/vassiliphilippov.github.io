high-velocity

1. ADJ

/ haɪ vəˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / haɪ vəˈlɒsəti / (BrE)
Operating or moving at a very high speed, especially compared to similar things.
operating or moving at high speed

Examples

She ducked as a high-velocity tennis ball whizzed past her head.
Engineers developed high-velocity trains to cut travel times between major cities.
