reception desk

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɛpʃən dɛsk / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpʃən dɛsk / (BrE)
a counter or area at the entrance of a hotel, office, or other building where visitors are greeted and assisted
counter where visitors are greeted

Examples

Please check in at the reception desk when you arrive at the hotel.
The reception desk staff can answer any of your questions about the building.
A vase of fresh flowers brightened up the reception desk in the lobby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: front desk - main service counter in a hotel or building lobby
