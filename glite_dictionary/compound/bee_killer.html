bee killer

1. NOUN

/ biː ˈkɪlɚ / (AmE), / biː ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
A swift predatory fly with a robust, bee-like body and a hardened proboscis for sucking the juices of other insects it catches in flight.
predatory fly resembling a bee

Examples

The bee killer hovers patiently before darting out to catch its prey.
Many gardeners mistake the bee killer for a bumblebee due to its similar appearance.
A bee killer can immobilize its victim with a single swift attack in mid-air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: robber fly - a large predatory fly of the family Asilidae
