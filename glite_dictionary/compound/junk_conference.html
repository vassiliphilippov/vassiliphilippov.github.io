junk conference

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌŋk ˈkɑːnfərəns / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk ˈkɒnfərəns / (BrE)
A conference that is considered to have little value, substance, or relevance, often seen as a waste of time or resources.
conference of little value or relevance

Examples

Many academics are frustrated by having their work presented at a junk conference.
She realized too late that the junk conference had no notable speakers or genuine sessions.
The company wasted thousands sending employees to a junk conference with barely any practical takeaways.

Synset Examples

He regretted going to the junk conference instead of staying home.
Attending the junk conference didn't yield any useful contacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion
