Battle of Cowpens

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv ˈkaʊpɛnz / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈkaʊpɛnz / (BrE)
A battle during the American Revolutionary War in 1781, where American forces under Daniel Morgan decisively defeated the British.
decisive 1781 battle in the American Revolution

Examples

The Battle of Cowpens was a turning point in the Southern campaign of the American Revolution.
Many historians study the tactics used at the Battle of Cowpens to understand Daniel Morgan's leadership.
A reenactment of the Battle of Cowpens takes place every year at the battlefield site in South Carolina.
