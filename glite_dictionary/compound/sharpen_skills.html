to sharpen skills

1. VERB

/ ˈʃɑːr.pən skɪlz / (AmE), / ˈʃɑː.pən skɪlz / (BrE)
to improve or enhance one's abilities or expertise in a particular area
improve one's abilities

Examples

He attended several workshops to sharpen skills crucial for his new job.
Athletes train regularly to sharpen skills and stay competitive.
Reading widely can help you sharpen skills in critical thinking and analysis.

Synset Examples

He took time to sharpen his skills during practice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to improve - to make or become better
  • Synonym: hone skills - to refine or perfect abilities
