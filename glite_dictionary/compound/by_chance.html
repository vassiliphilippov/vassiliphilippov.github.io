by chance

1. ADV

/ baɪ tʃæns / (AmE), / baɪ tʃɑːns / (BrE)
without planning or intention; accidentally.
without planning or accidentally

Examples

The discovery was made by chance during routine research.
They ran into each other by chance while shopping downtown.
