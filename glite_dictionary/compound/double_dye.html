to double dye

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌbəl daɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl daɪ / (BrE)
to dye (fabric or material) twice, often to achieve a particular color or effect
dye (something) twice

Examples

To achieve a deeper shade, the artisan decided to double dye the fabric.
Some manufacturers double dye garments to produce more vibrant colors.
The wool was double dyed to enhance its richness and uniformity.
