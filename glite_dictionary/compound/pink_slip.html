pink slip

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk slɪp / (AmE), / pɪŋk slɪp / (BrE)
an official notice given to an employee to inform them that they have been dismissed from their job
notice of dismissal from a job

Examples

After ten years at the company, Sarah was shocked to receive a pink slip without any warning.
Many workers feared they would find a pink slip in their mailboxes after the restructuring.
The company issued hundreds of pink slips during the economic downturn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layoff notice - notice informing an employee that they are being laid off
  • Synonym: termination notice - official notice stating that employment is ending

Etymology

Originally referring to the color of the paper used for such notices in the United States.
