social gathering

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈɡæðərɪŋ / (BrE)
an event where people assemble for social interaction, often in a formal or informal setting
an event where people meet socially

Examples

The annual social gathering brought colleagues from across the country together.
She was nervous about attending her first formal social gathering at work.
Due to the pandemic, large social gatherings were temporarily prohibited.

Synset Examples

the party was quite an affair
an occasion arranged to honor the president
a seemingly endless round of social functions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social event where people meet to enjoy themselves
  • Synonym: get-together - an informal meeting or gathering of people
