Militant Tendency

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪlɪtənt tɛnˈdɛnsi / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtənt tɛnˈdənsɪ / (BrE)
a Trotskyist political organization formed within the British Labour Party in 1964, known for its activities during the late 20th century
Trotskyist group in UK Labour Party (1964–1991)
british

Examples

Many Labour Party members were expelled due to their association with the Militant Tendency.
The Militant Tendency campaigned for socialism through the Labour Party during the 1970s and 1980s.
Debates about the legacy of the Militant Tendency still occur among British political historians.
