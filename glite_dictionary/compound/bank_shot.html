bank shot

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk ʃɑt / (AmE), / baŋk ʃɒt / (BrE)
a shot in basketball in which the ball is bounced off the backboard before passing through the hoop
a basketball shot rebounding off the backboard

Examples

She made a difficult bank shot to win the game at the buzzer.
Practicing the bank shot helped him improve his accuracy near the basket.
The point guard used a bank shot after driving past his defender.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: swish - a basketball shot that goes through the hoop without touching the backboard or rim
