book of instructions

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊk əv ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (AmE), / ˈbʊk əv ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (BrE)
a manual that accompanies a device or product, explaining how to install, operate, or use it
manual explaining use of a device

Examples

The vacuum cleaner came with a book of instructions that explains every feature in detail.
Before assembling the furniture, she carefully read the book of instructions to avoid mistakes.
He misplaced the book of instructions and had trouble figuring out how to set up the printer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a handbook or guide providing instructions
  • Synonym: guidebook - a book giving information or instructions about something
