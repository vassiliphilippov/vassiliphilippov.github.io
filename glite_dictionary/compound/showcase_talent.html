to showcase talent

1. VERB

/ ˈʃoʊˌkeɪs ˈtælənt / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊkeɪs ˈtælənt / (BrE)
to display or demonstrate one's skills or abilities, often in public or for evaluation
display one's skills or abilities

Examples

The festival allows emerging artists to showcase talent in front of industry professionals.
At the audition, he hoped to showcase talent that would set him apart from the rest.
Social media platforms have become popular places to showcase talent to a global audience.

Synset Examples

She showcased her talent on the drum at the concert.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to demonstrate - to show clearly by action or proof
  • Antonym: hide - to conceal or keep secret (one's abilities)
