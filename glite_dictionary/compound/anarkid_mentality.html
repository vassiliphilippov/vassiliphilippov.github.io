anarkid mentality

1. NOUN

/ ˈænɑr.kɪd mɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˈanəkɪd mɛnˈtalɪti / (BrE)
A mindset characterized by rejecting authority, societal rules, or established conventions, often linked to young people or subcultures with anarchist ideals.
mindset rejecting authority and rules
informal
