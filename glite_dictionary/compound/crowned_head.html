crowned head

1. NOUN

/ kraʊnd hɛd / (AmE), / kraʊnd hɛd / (BrE)
a monarch or sovereign ruler, especially one who holds their position by hereditary right
hereditary monarch or sovereign ruler

Examples

Several crowned heads attended the royal wedding in London.
The museum displays portraits of Europe's most powerful crowned heads from past centuries.
Despite the revolution, some crowned heads still managed to hold onto their thrones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monarch - a sovereign head of state, especially a king, queen, or emperor
