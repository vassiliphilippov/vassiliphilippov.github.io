water carpet

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˈkɑːr.pət / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.tə ˈkɑː.pɪt / (BrE)
An aquatic herb with yellowish flowers, found in central and western United States.
aquatic herb with yellowish flowers

Examples

A thick layer of water carpet covered the surface of the shallow pond.
Botanists identified the yellowish flowers as belonging to the water carpet species native to the region.
The rapid spread of water carpet has altered the ecosystem of several wetlands in the Midwest.
