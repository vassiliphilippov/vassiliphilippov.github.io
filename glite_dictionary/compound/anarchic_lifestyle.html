anarchic lifestyle

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑːrkɪk ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / əˈnɑːkɪk ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (BrE)
a way of living that rejects or operates without any recognized authority or governing structure
lifestyle without recognized authority

Examples

After moving to the remote island, they adopted an anarchic lifestyle far from any rules or laws.
The commune’s anarchic lifestyle attracted people disillusioned with conventional society.
Some critics saw the artist's anarchic lifestyle as a form of protest against societal norms.

Synset Examples

They embraced an anarchic lifestyle, rejecting all authority.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lifestyle - a way of living or existing
  • Synonym: anarchy - absence of government and absolute freedom of the individual
