whip hand

1. NOUN

/ wɪp hænd / (AmE), / wɪp hand / (BrE)
A position of advantage, power, or control over others.
position of advantage or control

Examples

After years of negotiation, the company finally gained the whip hand in the deal.
With the evidence in her favor, she had the whip hand in the dispute.
The government lost the whip hand after the scandal became public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advantage - a condition or circumstance that puts one in a favorable or superior position
  • Synonym: control - the power to influence or direct people's behavior or the course of events

Etymology

Originally referring to the hand holding the whip when driving horses, indicating control over them.
