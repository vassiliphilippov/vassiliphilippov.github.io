shin splints

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪn splɪnts / (AmE), / ʃɪn splɪnts / (BrE)
pain and inflammation in the front part of the lower leg, especially along the shinbone, commonly caused by exercise such as running
painful inflammation in the lower leg (shin) area
medical

Examples

After her first week of marathon training, she developed shin splints that made it hard to walk.
Doctors recommend rest and ice for treating shin splints in amateur athletes.
Wearing proper running shoes can help prevent shin splints during intense workouts.
