criminal court

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl kɔːt / (BrE)
a court of law that deals with cases involving crimes and criminal offenses
court for criminal cases
legal

Examples

The defendant appeared before the criminal court to answer charges of theft.
She works as a prosecutor in the criminal court downtown.
The criminal court found him guilty and sentenced him to five years in prison.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: civil court - a court dealing with non-criminal cases
