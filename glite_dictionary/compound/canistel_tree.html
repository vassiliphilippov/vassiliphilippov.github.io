canistel tree

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɪstəl triː / (AmE), / kəˈnɪstəl triː / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to Florida and the West Indies that yields edible yellow fruit, also known as eggfruit.
tropical tree with edible yellow fruit

Examples

The canistel tree produces fruit that is often called eggfruit because of its texture.
Farmers in the West Indies cultivate the canistel tree for its nutritious yellow fruit.
Botanists are studying how the canistel tree adapts to different tropical climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eggfruit - the fruit produced by the canistel tree, with yellow flesh
