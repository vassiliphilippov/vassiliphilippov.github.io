bad egg

1. NOUN

/ bæd ɛɡ / (AmE), / bæd eɡ / (BrE)
an untrustworthy or dishonest person
dishonest or untrustworthy person
archaic

Examples

Nobody trusted Jack after his scheme was revealed; he was known around town as a bad egg.
The company had to let go of a few bad eggs to restore its reputation.
You can tell he's a bad egg just by the way he talks to people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrongdoer - a person who does something morally or legally wrong
  • Synonym: villain - a wicked or evil person
  • Antonym: good egg - a kind or trustworthy person
