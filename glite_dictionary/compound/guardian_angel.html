guardian angel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːrdiən ˈeɪndʒəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdiən ˈeɪndʒəl / (BrE)
an angel believed to protect and guide a particular person
an angel assigned to protect a person

Examples

She believes that her guardian angel has protected her from harm many times.
In times of trouble, he felt as if a guardian angel was watching over him.
The nurse was like a guardian angel to the frightened children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angel - a spiritual being regarded as a messenger or servant of God

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːrdiən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdiən ˈeɪn.dʒəl / (BrE)
a spirit or person believed to protect, help, or give guidance to someone
protective spirit or helper

Examples

She always felt that her grandmother was her guardian angel, watching over her in difficult times.
After the accident, he believed a guardian angel had saved his life.
During the storm, a stranger appeared like a guardian angel, helping them reach safety.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protector - someone who defends or shelters another
  • Synonym: guide - a person who leads or advises others

Etymology

From guardian (protector) + angel (spiritual being; from Latin angelus, Greek angelos meaning 'messenger').
