seven iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvən ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈsɛvən ˈaɪən / (BrE)
A type of golf club with a lofted face, used for hitting high shots toward the green.
golf club for high shots to the green

Examples

He chose a seven iron to hit his approach shot to the green.
Most players can hit a seven iron between 130 and 160 yards, depending on their skill.
The coach advised practicing with a seven iron for better control on short holes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iron - a type of golf club with a metal head
