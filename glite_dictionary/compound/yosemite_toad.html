Yosemite toad

1. NOUN

/ joʊˈsɛmɪti toʊd / (AmE), / jəʊˈsɛmɪti təʊd / (BrE)
a species of toad native to high meadows and forest borders of the Sierra Nevada in California
a toad species of the Sierra Nevada

Examples

The Yosemite toad is an endangered species native to California's Sierra Nevada mountains.
During our hike, we were lucky to spot a Yosemite toad near a mountain stream.
Scientists are monitoring Yosemite toads to study the effects of climate change on their population.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toad - a tailless amphibian of the family Bufonidae
