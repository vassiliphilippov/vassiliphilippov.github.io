International Labor Organization

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪntəɹˈnæʃənəl ˈleɪbɚ ˌɔːɹɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˈleɪbə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations that promotes labor rights and seeks to improve working conditions globally
UN agency promoting labor rights

Examples

The International Labor Organization released a new report on child labor worldwide.
She works as a consultant for the International Labor Organization in Geneva.
Membership in the International Labor Organization is considered important for countries seeking to improve labor standards.
