inner strength

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnɚ strɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ˈɪnə strɛŋθ / (BrE)
the mental or emotional resilience and determination that helps someone overcome difficulties or adversity
personal resilience and determination

Examples

Her inner strength kept her going even when things seemed hopeless.
Many people underestimate their own inner strength until faced with true hardship.
Meditation and reflection can help you cultivate inner strength in challenging times.

Synset Examples

She showed her inner strength during the crisis.
He discovered his inner strength through adversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resilience - the ability to recover from setbacks or difficulties
  • Synonym: fortitude - courage and strength in facing adversity
