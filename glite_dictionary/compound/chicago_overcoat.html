Chicago overcoat

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪˈkɑːɡoʊ ˈoʊvərˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪˈkɑːɡəʊ ˈəʊvəkəʊt / (BrE)
a heavy, often stylish overcoat associated with Chicago, typically worn in cold weather
heavy overcoat associated with Chicago
