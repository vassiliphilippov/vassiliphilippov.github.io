golf-club head

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑlf klʌb hɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlf klʌb hɛd / (BrE)
the part of a golf club that strikes the ball
the striking part of a golf club

Examples

The golf-club head must be cleaned after every shot to ensure accuracy.
She replaced her old golf-club head with a new titanium one.
The weight of the golf-club head affects the distance you can hit the ball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clubhead - the head of any club, especially in golf
