real treat

1. NOUN

/ riəl trit / (AmE), / rɪəl triːt / (BrE)
something that is especially enjoyable or gives great pleasure
something very enjoyable

Examples

Dinner at that restaurant was a real treat for us.
Her homemade cookies are always a real treat.
Watching the sunrise over the mountains is a real treat.

Synset Examples

You should visit Bathhouse Barry for a real treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delight - something that gives great pleasure
  • Synonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
