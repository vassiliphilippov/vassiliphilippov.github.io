right on time

1. ADV

/ raɪt ɑn taɪm / (AmE), / raɪt ɒn taɪm / (BrE)
at exactly the planned or scheduled time; punctually
punctually; at the exact time

Examples

The train arrived right on time, just as the schedule predicted.
If you finish your work right on time, you'll impress your manager.
She always makes sure to be right on time for her classes.

Synset Examples

The babysitter arrived right on time for the kids.
He was right on time for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on time - punctual; at the scheduled time
  • Antonym: late - after the scheduled or expected time
