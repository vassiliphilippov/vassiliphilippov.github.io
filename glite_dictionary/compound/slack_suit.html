slack suit

1. NOUN

/ slæk suːt / (AmE), / slæk suːt / (BrE)
a casual outfit consisting of slacks and a matching jacket, typically worn for informal occasions
casual outfit with slacks and jacket

Examples

She wore a navy slack suit to the luncheon, looking effortlessly chic.
He preferred a comfortable slack suit for weekend outings instead of a formal suit.
The store has a new collection of slack suits perfect for spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - a set of outer clothes made of the same fabric and designed to be worn together
  • Synonym: sportswear - casual clothing designed for informal, everyday wear
