googolplexian wealth

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡuːɡəlˌplɛksiən ˈwɛlθ / (AmE), / ˌɡuːɡəlˌplɛksiən ˈwɛlθ / (BrE)
humorous or exaggerated term for extremely vast wealth, often used hyperbolically
exaggerated term for immense wealth
informal

Examples

He joked that his lottery winnings would soon make him the owner of googolplexian wealth.
Stories often exaggerate with phrases like googolplexian wealth to describe a billionaire's riches.
She claimed, with a grin, that she was planning her retirement thanks to her googolplexian wealth.

Synset Examples

She humorously referred to her savings as googolplexian wealth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortune - a large amount of money or valuable possessions
  • Antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor

Etymology

Googolplexian combines 'googolplex' (a 1 followed by a googol of zeros) with the suffix '-ian'; 'wealth' from Old English 'wela'.
