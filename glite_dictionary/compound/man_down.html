man down

1. NOUN

/ mæn daʊn / (AmE), / mæn daʊn / (BrE)
a person who has been injured or incapacitated, especially in a team, military, or emergency context
person who is injured/incapacitated

Examples

During the rescue mission, they suddenly had a man down and needed immediate backup.
The coach paused the game when he saw a man down on the field.
A man down in our squad meant we had to adjust our entire strategy.

Synset Examples

He called for help after a man down in the game.
We had a man down during the intense training session.
The team rallied quickly after a man down on the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casualty - a person who is injured or killed in a serious accident or war
