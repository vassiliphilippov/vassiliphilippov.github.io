garlic bread

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrlɪk brɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk brɛd / (BrE)
A type of bread, usually French or Italian, sliced and spread with garlic-flavored butter and toasted until crisp.
bread with garlic butter, toasted

Examples

She ordered a basket of garlic bread to share with the table.
The aroma of freshly baked garlic bread filled the kitchen.
Would you like some garlic bread with your pasta?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bruschetta - Italian toasted bread with toppings, often including garlic and tomatoes
