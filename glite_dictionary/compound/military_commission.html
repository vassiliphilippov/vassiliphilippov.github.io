military commission

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪləˌtɛri kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪt(ə)ri kəˈmɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an official document by which a person is made an officer in the armed forces
document granting officer rank in the military

Examples

She received her military commission after graduating from the academy.
His military commission was signed by the president himself.
Without a military commission, he could not serve as an officer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commission - an official document giving authority to act
