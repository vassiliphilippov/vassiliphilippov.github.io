vital for

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪtəl fɔr / (AmE), / ˈvaɪtəl fəː / (BrE)
absolutely necessary or extremely important with respect to something
essential or crucial for something

Examples

Clear communication is vital for avoiding misunderstandings in any workplace.
Regular exercise is vital for maintaining good physical and mental health.
Honest feedback is vital for improving the quality of our work.

Synset Examples

Understanding CR is vital for our team’s success.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: essential for - completely necessary for
  • Degree Lower: important for - of great significance for
