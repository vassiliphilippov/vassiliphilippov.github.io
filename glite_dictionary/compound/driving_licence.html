driving licence

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˈlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official document permitting someone to drive a motor vehicle
official permit to drive a vehicle

Examples

She passed her test and finally got her driving licence last week.
You must carry your driving licence with you when operating a vehicle.
His driving licence was revoked after the speeding incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driver's license - official document allowing a person to drive a motor vehicle (US English)
