service road

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝː.vɪs roʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs rəʊd / (BrE)
A local road running parallel to a major road or highway, intended to provide access to properties and businesses along the route.
local road parallel to a highway

Examples

The shopping center can only be accessed from the service road that runs beside the highway.
After the accident, traffic was diverted onto the service road for several hours.
Many houses are located along the service road next to the motorway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frontage road - a local road parallel to a highway, providing access to properties
