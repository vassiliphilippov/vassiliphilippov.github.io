great synergy

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪt ˈsɪnərdʒi / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈsɪnədʒi / (BrE)
a highly effective collaboration or interaction where combined efforts produce better results than individual contributions
highly effective collaboration

Examples

Their partnership resulted in great synergy, boosting the company's performance.
We witnessed great synergy between the two departments during the merger.
The project succeeded because there was great synergy among all team members.

Synset Examples

The team achieved great synergy during the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synergy - the interaction of elements that produces a greater effect
