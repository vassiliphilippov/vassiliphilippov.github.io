halieutically relevant

1. ADJ

/ ˌheɪliˈutɪkli rɪˈlɛvənt / (AmE), / ˌhalɪˈjuːtɪkli rɪˈlɛvənt / (BrE)
of or pertaining to matters significant in the study or management of fishery science
related to fishery science

Examples

The researchers focused on halieutically relevant data to advise sustainable fishing quotas.
Only halieutically relevant factors were considered in the environmental impact study.
This report summarizes the most halieutically relevant trends observed over the past decade.

Synset Examples

The team conducted halieutically relevant surveys to assess fish populations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fisheries-related - relating to fisheries or fishery science
  • Antonym: irrelevant - not connected or relevant to something

Etymology

From Greek halieutikos (of or belonging to fishing), from halieus (fisherman); relevant from Latin relevare (to raise up, make light).
