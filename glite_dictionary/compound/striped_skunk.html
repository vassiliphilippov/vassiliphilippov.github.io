striped skunk

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪpt skʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈstraɪpt skʌŋk / (BrE)
a North American skunk species (Mephitis mephitis) known for its black fur and distinctive white stripes running from head to tail
common North American skunk species

Examples

We saw a striped skunk crossing the road last night on our way home.
The striped skunk uses its distinctive white stripes to warn predators of its foul-smelling spray.
A striped skunk visited our backyard and left a strong odor behind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skunk - a small North American mammal known for its strong-smelling defensive spray
