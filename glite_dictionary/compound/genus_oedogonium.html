genus Oedogonium

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊdəˈɡoʊniəm / (AmE), / ˌiːdəˈɡəʊniəm / (BrE)
the type genus of the family Oedogoniaceae, consisting of freshwater green algae with long, unbranched filaments, usually free-floating when mature
type genus of green algae Oedogoniaceae

Examples

Many freshwater ponds are home to species belonging to the genus Oedogonium.
Botanists studied the structure of the genus Oedogonium to understand its reproduction.
The genus Oedogonium is frequently found as long, green filaments floating in still water.

Etymology

From Greek 'oidos' (swelling) + 'gonos' (seed), referencing aspects of the organism's reproductive structure.
