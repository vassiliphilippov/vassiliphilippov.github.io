handball court

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbɔl kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhan(d)ˌbɔːl kɔːt / (BrE)
an indoor or outdoor court used for playing handball, with walls against which the ball is hit
court for playing handball

Examples

The new sports center has a state-of-the-art handball court with glass walls.
Players must wear protective gear when using the handball court to prevent injuries.
Several high schools are building outdoor handball courts to encourage student participation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handball - ball game played by hitting a ball against a wall with the hand
