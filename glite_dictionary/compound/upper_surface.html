upper surface

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpər ˈsɜrfɪs / (AmE), / ˈʌpə ˈsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
the side or part of something that is on or nearest the top
top side of something

Examples

The upper surface of the leaf was shiny and smooth to the touch.
Dust tends to settle on the upper surface of shelves and cabinets.
Painters carefully cleaned the upper surface of the wooden table before applying varnish.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underside - the lower surface or bottom of something
