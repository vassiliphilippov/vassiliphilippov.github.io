epic tale

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪk teɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪk teɪl / (BrE)
a long narrative story that celebrates heroic deeds or extraordinary adventures, often involving legendary figures
long narrative of heroic deeds

Examples

The novel unfolds like an epic tale, weaving history, myth, and adventure together.
She was captivated by the epic tale of the hero's perilous journey home.
The director adapted the ancient epic tale for the big screen, thrilling a new generation.

Synset Examples

He told an epic tale of adventure and bravery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saga - a long story of heroic achievement, especially a medieval prose narrative
  • Synonym: legend - a traditional story sometimes popularly regarded as historical but unauthenticated

Etymology

Epic: from Latin 'epicus', from Greek 'epikos' (from 'epos', word, song); Tale: from Old English 'talu' (story, tale).
