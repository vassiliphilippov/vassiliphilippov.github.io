home-cured

1. ADJ

/ hoʊm kjʊrd / (AmE), / həʊm kjʊəd / (BrE)
(of food, especially meat or fish) preserved or processed by curing at home rather than commercially.
preserved by curing at home

Examples

We enjoyed some home-cured bacon for breakfast this morning.
She takes pride in making home-cured salmon for family gatherings.
The butcher compared his home-cured sausages to the store-bought version.

Synset Examples

home-cured hams

Related Terms

  • Antonym: commercially cured - preserved by curing in a commercial setting
