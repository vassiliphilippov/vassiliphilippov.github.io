a cappella singing

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑː kəˈpɛl ə ˈsɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæ kəˈpel ə ˈsɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
Singing performed without instrumental accompaniment.
singing without instruments

Examples

The choir's a cappella singing filled the cathedral with pure harmonies.
He prefers a cappella singing because it highlights the voices' natural beauty.
Groups specializing in a cappella singing often perform pop songs without any instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a cappella - without instrumental accompaniment

Etymology

From Italian 'a cappella' meaning 'in the manner of the chapel'.
