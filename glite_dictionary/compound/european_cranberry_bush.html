European cranberry bush

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈkrænˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈkrænˌbɛri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to Europe and Asia, with clusters of white flowers and bright red berries, often grown as an ornamental plant.
deciduous shrub with red berries

Examples

The European cranberry bush brightens gardens in autumn with its stunning red berries.
Birds are often attracted to the fruit of the European cranberry bush in winter.
She decided to plant a European cranberry bush along the garden path for its colorful display.
