highborn guest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌbɔrn ɡɛst / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌbɔːn ɡɛst / (BrE)
A guest who is of noble birth or high social status.
guest of noble or high social status

Examples

The king welcomed the highborn guest with special honors at the royal banquet.
Only a highborn guest would have been given such a lavish suite in the palace.
Each highborn guest was expected to present a gift to the host upon arrival.

Synset Examples

The highborn guests were dressed in elegant attire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noble - a person of high rank or noble birth
