cable tramway

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪbəl ˈtræmˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbl ˈtræm.weɪ / (BrE)
A system for transporting passengers or freight using carriers suspended from moving cables, usually supported by a series of towers.
carrier system suspended from cables

Examples

The city built a cable tramway across the valley to connect the two mountain towns.
Tourists took the cable tramway to enjoy panoramic views of the landscape below.
Repairs to the cable tramway caused delays for regular commuters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cable car - vehicle suspended from or pulled by a cable, often used in hilly areas
