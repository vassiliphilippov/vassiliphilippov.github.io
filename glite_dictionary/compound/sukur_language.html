Sukur language

1. NOUN

/ suːˈkuːr ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / suːˈkuːə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly in the Mandara mountains region of northeastern Nigeria, especially in Adamawa State
Chadic language of Adamawa State, Nigeria

Examples

The Sukur language is still actively spoken among the people of the Mandara mountains.
Linguists have studied the Sukur language for its unique phonetic features.
Efforts are being made to preserve the Sukur language for future generations.
