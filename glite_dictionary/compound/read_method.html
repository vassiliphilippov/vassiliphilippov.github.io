Read method

1. NOUN

/ riːd ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / riːd ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)
a method of natural childbirth emphasizing the normalcy of birth and the psychological nature of pain, using education and breathing techniques to encourage relaxation
method of natural childbirth using breathing and relaxation

Examples

Many women choose the Read method to cope with the pain of natural childbirth.
The prenatal classes introduced us to the Read method and its focus on relaxation and education.
She credited the Read method with helping her remain calm throughout labor.

Etymology

Named after British obstetrician Grantly Dick-Read (1890–1959), who developed and promoted the method.
