great with child

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪt wɪð tʃaɪld / (AmE), / ɡreɪt wɪð tʃaɪld / (BrE)
(archaic) In an advanced stage of pregnancy; heavily pregnant.
heavily pregnant (archaic)
archaic

Examples

In the old portrait, the lady is great with child and delicately holding her abdomen.
She was great with child when her husband left for the war.
Biblical texts often mention women who were great with child during important journeys.

Synset Examples

was big with child
was great with child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pregnant - having a baby developing inside the womb
