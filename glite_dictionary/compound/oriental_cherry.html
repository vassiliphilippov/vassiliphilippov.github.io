oriental cherry

1. NOUN

/ ɔːr.iˈɛn.t̬əl ˈtʃɛr.i / (AmE), / ɔː.riˈɛn.təl ˈtʃɛr.i / (BrE)
an ornamental tree (Prunus serrulata) native to East Asia, widely cultivated for its white or pink blossoms and small inedible fruits
ornamental cherry tree with showy blossoms

Examples

Every spring, tourists flock to the park to see the oriental cherry trees in full bloom.
She planted an oriental cherry in her garden for its beautiful blossoms.
The delicate flowers of the oriental cherry symbolize renewal and new beginnings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherry blossom - the flower of the cherry tree, especially ornamental varieties

Etymology

From 'oriental' (eastern) referring to its Asian origin, and 'cherry' for the genus Prunus.
