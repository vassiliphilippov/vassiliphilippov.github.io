ten-cent trinket

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛn ˈsɛnt ˈtrɪŋkɪt / (AmE), / ˌtɛn ˈsɛnt ˈtrɪŋkɪt / (BrE)
A small item of little value; something that is cheap or insignificant.
small, cheap or insignificant item

Examples

He gave her a ten-cent trinket as a souvenir from his trip.
The shop is full of ten-cent trinkets and novelty gifts.
She didn't want a ten-cent trinket; she wanted something meaningful.

Synset Examples

This toy is just a ten-cent trinket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trinket - a small ornament or item of little value
