purple-tinted

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɝːpəl ˈtɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈtɪntɪd / (BrE)
Having or showing a slight or faint purple coloration or hue.
tinged with purple color

Examples

The artist used a purple-tinted wash to create a mood of twilight in the painting.
She wore sunglasses with purple-tinted lenses that softened the bright sunlight.
Clouds with a purple-tinted edge floated across the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purplish - having a slight purple color
