hunting license

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official permit that allows a person to hunt and kill specific animals within a designated period and area
permit to hunt specified animals

Examples

You must obtain a hunting license before you can legally hunt deer in this state.
The wildlife officer checked his hunting license during their patrol in the forest.
She proudly displayed her first hunting license after completing the required safety course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: permit - an official document granting permission
