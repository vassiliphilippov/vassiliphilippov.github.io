peasant life

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛzənt laɪf / (AmE), / ˈpɛznt lʌɪf / (BrE)
the way of life, conditions, and daily activities of rural, working-class people, especially peasants in historical or traditional societies
lifestyle of rural, working-class peasants

Examples

Many novels set in medieval Europe depict the hardships and joys of peasant life.
She became fascinated by peasant life after visiting rural villages during her travels.
The museum exhibit showcased artifacts used in everyday peasant life centuries ago.

Synset Examples

The peasant life was simple yet fulfilling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peasant - a member of a traditional class of rural, agricultural laborers
