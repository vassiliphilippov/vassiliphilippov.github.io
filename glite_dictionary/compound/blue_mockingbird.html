blue mockingbird

1. NOUN

/ bluː ˈmɑkɪŋˌbɝd / (AmE), / bluː ˈmɒkɪŋˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a species of mockingbird native to Mexico, known for its blue-grey plumage and melodious song
a Mexican species of mockingbird

Examples

The blue mockingbird is frequently spotted in the wooded regions of western Mexico.
Birdwatchers travel to see the striking blue mockingbird during the spring migration.
Unlike other species, the blue mockingbird prefers dense underbrush for nesting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mockingbird - a long-tailed grey songbird known for imitating sounds
