twenty-two pistol

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈtu ˈpɪstl̩ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈpɪstl̩ / (BrE)
A pistol that uses .22-caliber ammunition, known for its low recoil and common use in target shooting or training.
.22-caliber pistol

Examples

He took his twenty-two pistol to the shooting range for some target practice.
The twenty-two pistol is often recommended for beginner shooters because of its light recoil.
She inherited her grandfather's old twenty-two pistol as part of his collection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pistol - a small firearm designed to be held in one hand
