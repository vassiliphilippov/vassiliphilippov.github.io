singing voice

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɪŋ vɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɪŋ vɔɪs / (BrE)
the quality or tone of a person's voice when singing, as opposed to speaking
voice quality when singing

Examples

She lost her singing voice after the illness and could no longer perform on stage.
His singing voice is much deeper than his speaking voice, surprising many audience members.
The teacher complimented Lily on the strength of her singing voice during the audition.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: speaking voice - the quality of the voice when speaking, not singing
