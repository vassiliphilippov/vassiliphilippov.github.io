effusion of tears

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈfjuʒən ʌv tɪrz / (AmE), / ɪˈfjʊʒən əv tɪəz / (BrE)
a sudden or abundant outpouring of tears, typically as an expression of strong emotion
a sudden outpouring of tears

Examples

Moved by the speech, she broke into an effusion of tears.
The film's ending brought an effusion of tears from the audience.
After receiving the news, his effusion of tears touched everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

There was an effusion of tears at the farewell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outburst - a sudden release of strong emotion
