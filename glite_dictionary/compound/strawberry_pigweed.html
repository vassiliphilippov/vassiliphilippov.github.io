strawberry pigweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔːˌbɛri ˈpɪɡˌwid / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbɛri ˈpɪɡwiːd / (BrE)
A European annual plant with clusters of greenish flowers, followed by red, pulpy, berrylike fruit; naturalized in North America.
a European annual plant with red berry-like fruit

Examples

The strawberry pigweed thrived in the disturbed soil near the edge of the field.
Gardeners are advised to remove strawberry pigweed to prevent it from spreading.
The red, berrylike fruit of the strawberry pigweed is a distinctive feature of this plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pigweed - a group of annual herbs of the Amaranth family
