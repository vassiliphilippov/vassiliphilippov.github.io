one knee

1. NOUN

/ wʌn niː / (AmE), / wʌn niː / (BrE)
the position of kneeling with one knee on the ground, especially when proposing marriage
kneeling with one knee on the ground

Examples

Many people get down on one knee to propose marriage.
He dropped to one knee in front of the crowd, holding out the ring.
Taking a moment, she bent on one knee to tie her shoe.

Synset Examples

He knelt down on one knee to propose.
