Rhodie spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdi ˌspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdi ˌspɪrɪt / (BrE)
The strong enthusiasm and sense of community associated with Rhododendron University events or traditions.
enthusiasm for Rhododendron University events

Examples

The Rhodie spirit was evident as students decorated the campus for the big game.
Alumni often return to campus to recapture the Rhodie spirit during reunion weekend.
Wearing school colors is one way to show your Rhodie spirit at university events.

Synset Examples

Everyone loves the Rhodie spirit during homecoming.

Etymology

From 'Rhodie', a shortened form of 'Rhododendron', and 'spirit' (enthusiasm or morale).
