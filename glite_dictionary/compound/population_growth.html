population growth

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑpjəˈleɪʃən ɡroʊθ / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən ɡrəʊθ / (BrE)
an increase in the number of people living in a particular area, country, or the world
increase in number of inhabitants

Examples

Rapid population growth is putting pressure on the region's natural resources.
The country has implemented policies to slow its population growth.
Urban areas have experienced significant population growth over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: population decline - a decrease in the number of people living in a particular area, country, or the world
