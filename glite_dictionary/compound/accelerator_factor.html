accelerator factor

1. NOUN

/ əkˌsɛləˈreɪtər ˈfæktər / (AmE), / əkˌsɛləˈreɪtə ˈfæktə / (BrE)
a protein in the blood that acts as a coagulation factor, helping to speed up the clotting process
blood protein aiding clotting
medical

Examples
