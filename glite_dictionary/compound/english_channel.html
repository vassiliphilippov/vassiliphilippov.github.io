English Channel

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈtʃanəl / (BrE)
the arm of the Atlantic Ocean that separates southern England from northern France
channel between England and France

Examples

Many ships cross the English Channel every day, connecting Britain with continental Europe.
He swam across the English Channel to set a new world record.
The English Channel is known for its unpredictable weather and strong currents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: La Manche - French name for the English Channel
