mourning band

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌbænd / (BrE)
a black band worn on the arm, hat, or uniform as a symbol of mourning for someone who has died
black arm or hat band worn in mourning

Examples

Police officers wore mourning bands over their badges in memory of their fallen colleague.
He placed a mourning band on his arm to show respect at the funeral.
The tradition of wearing a mourning band dates back centuries in some cultures.
