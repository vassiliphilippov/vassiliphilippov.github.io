way to contact

1. NOUN

/ weɪ tə ˈkɑnˌtækt / (AmE), / weɪ tə ˈkɒnˌtækt / (BrE)
a method used to communicate or get in touch with someone
method of reaching someone

Examples

Email is the fastest way to contact our support team.
He didn't leave any way to contact him after the meeting.
Social media has become a popular way to contact friends and family.

Synset Examples

We have no ways to contact him right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: means of communication - a channel or method for exchanging information
