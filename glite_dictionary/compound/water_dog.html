water dog

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌdɒɡ / (BrE)
a person who greatly enjoys spending time in or on the water, especially swimming or boating
person fond of water activities

Examples

As a true water dog, she spends every summer weekend at the lake.
My brother is such a water dog that he learned how to surf before he could ride a bike.
You can always spot a water dog by their constant enthusiasm for the beach or swimming pool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water lover - a person who loves the water or water activities
Source: WordNet

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to work in or around water, especially for retrieving waterfowl during hunting
dog trained for retrieving in water

Examples

The hunter relied on his water dog to fetch ducks from the lake.
Portuguese water dogs are known for their swimming skills and intelligence.
After the shot, the water dog swiftly dove in to retrieve the bird.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retriever - a dog breed trained to retrieve game for hunters
