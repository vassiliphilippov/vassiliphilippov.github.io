service line

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪs laɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs laɪn / (BrE)
the back line marking each end of a tennis or handball court, which the server must not cross when serving
back line in tennis or handball court

Examples

The player made sure not to step over the service line when serving the ball.
A foot fault was called because she crossed the service line during her serve.
Proper positioning behind the service line is crucial in both tennis and handball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseline - the line at each end of a tennis or badminton court
