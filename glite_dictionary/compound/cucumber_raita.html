cucumber raita

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjuːkʌmbɚ raɪˈeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈkjuːkʌmbə raɪˈɑːtə / (BrE)
A yogurt-based condiment or side dish mixed with chopped cucumber and various spices, typically served with Indian meals.
yogurt and cucumber condiment

Examples

The meal was served with a bowl of cucumber raita to cool the palate.
Adding cucumber raita provided a refreshing contrast to the spicy dishes.
She prepared cucumber raita as a side for the chicken biryani.

Synset Examples

We enjoyed cucumber raita alongside the spicy curry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raita - an Indian yogurt-based condiment with assorted ingredients

Etymology

From Hindi 'raita', a yoghurt-based dish, combined with English 'cucumber.'
