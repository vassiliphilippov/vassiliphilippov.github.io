horsetail milkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrˌsteɪl ˈmɪlkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌsteɪl ˈmɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
a species of milkweed native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, known to be poisonous to livestock
a poisonous milkweed plant of the southwest US and Mexico

Examples

Cattle can become ill after grazing on horsetail milkweed in contaminated pastures.
Horsetail milkweed blooms in the arid regions of New Mexico every summer.
Farmers are advised to remove horsetail milkweed from grazing areas to protect their livestock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkweed - any of various plants of the genus Asclepias known for their milky sap
