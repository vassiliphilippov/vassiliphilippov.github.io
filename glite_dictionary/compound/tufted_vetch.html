tufted vetch

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌftɪd vɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd vɛtʃ / (BrE)
a perennial climbing plant (Vicia cracca) of temperate regions in Eurasia and North America, having dense clusters of bluish-purple flowers
perennial climbing plant with dense bluish-purple flower clusters

Examples

The meadows were dotted with bright clusters of tufted vetch among the tall grasses.
The gardener planted tufted vetch to attract bees and butterflies to the plot.
Hikers admired the climbing stems of tufted vetch weaving through the hedgerows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vetch - any of various climbing plants of the genus Vicia
