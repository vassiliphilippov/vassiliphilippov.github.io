Roman nettle

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊ.mən ˈnɛt̬.əl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən ˈnɛt.əl / (BrE)
An annual European nettle with stinging leaves and small clusters of green flowers.
annual European stinging nettle

Examples

The gardener pulled out a Roman nettle growing near the roses.
Roman nettles can sometimes be found along the edges of European fields.
Wearing gloves is advised when handling the leaves of a Roman nettle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nettle - any of various plants of the genus Urtica, typically with stinging hairs
