big-chested

1. ADJ

/ bɪɡˈtʃɛstɪd / (AmE), / bɪɡˈtʃɛstɪd / (BrE)
having a large or well-developed chest, especially in reference to the human body.
having a large chest

Examples

The big-chested swimmer cut through the water with powerful strokes.
She admired the big-chested statue in the ancient hall.
Years of rowing left him big-chested and strong.

Synset Examples

he was big-chested, big-shouldered and heavy-armed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broad-chested - having a broad chest
