Oregon crab apple

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrɪɡən kræb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪɡən kræb ˈæpəl / (BrE)
A small tree or shrub native to the western United States, with white blossoms and small yellow or red fruit.
small tree with white blossoms, western U.S.

Examples

The Oregon crab apple is often used in landscaping for its beautiful spring blossoms.
Wildlife in the region feed on the fruit of the Oregon crab apple during late autumn.
Many gardeners appreciate the hardiness of the Oregon crab apple in challenging climates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab apple - a small, sour apple or the tree that bears it
