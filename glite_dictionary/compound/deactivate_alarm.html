to deactivate alarm

1. VERB

/ diˈæktɪˌveɪt əˈlɑrm / (AmE), / diˈæktɪˌveɪt əˈlɑːm / (BrE)
to turn off or disable an alarm system, making it inactive
turn off an alarm system

Examples

Before unlocking the door, remember to deactivate alarm to avoid triggering it.
She quickly deactivated alarm when she arrived at the office early in the morning.
You must deactivate alarm after entering your security code on the panel.

Synset Examples

Please deactivate the alarm before entering the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disarm alarm - make an alarm system unable to activate or sound
  • Antonym: activate alarm - turn on or enable an alarm system
