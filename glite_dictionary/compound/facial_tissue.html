facial tissue

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪʃəl ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈtɪsjuː / (BrE)
A soft, absorbent paper tissue designed for wiping or cleaning the face or nose.
soft tissue paper for the face

Examples

She grabbed a facial tissue to wipe her nose.
The box of facial tissues sat on the coffee table.
Always carry a facial tissue with you during allergy season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tissue - thin piece of soft, absorbent paper
