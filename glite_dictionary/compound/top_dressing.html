top dressing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɒp ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A layer of fertilizer or manure applied to the surface of soil, without being plowed in.
surface application of fertilizer

Examples

The gardener applied a top dressing of compost to improve the soil quality.
Regular top dressing helps lawns remain lush and healthy throughout the summer.
After reseeding, he recommended adding a top dressing to encourage grass growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fertilizer - a substance added to soil to improve plant growth
