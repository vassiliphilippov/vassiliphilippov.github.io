verbal intelligence

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrbəl ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəl ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns / (BrE)
the ability to use language effectively and to understand spoken or written words
ability to use and understand language

Examples

Her verbal intelligence helped her excel in debates and storytelling competitions.
A high level of verbal intelligence is often linked to strong reading comprehension skills.
Teachers use various tests to measure students' verbal intelligence during assessments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguistic intelligence - intelligence relating to spoken or written language ability
