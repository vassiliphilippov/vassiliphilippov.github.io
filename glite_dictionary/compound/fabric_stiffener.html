fabric stiffener

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæbrɪk ˈstɪfənər / (AmE), / ˈfæbrɪk ˈstɪfənə / (BrE)
a chemical or substance applied to fabric to make it stiffer and less flexible, especially in laundry or craft use
chemical for making fabric stiffer

Examples

Add fabric stiffener to your craft project if you want the lace to hold its shape.
She prefers using a homemade fabric stiffener when working with delicate materials.
The instructions recommended applying fabric stiffener to achieve crisp folds in the fabric.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fabric softener - a substance used to make fabric softer after washing
