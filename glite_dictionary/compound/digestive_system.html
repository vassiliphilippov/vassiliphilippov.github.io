digestive system

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈdʒɛstɪv ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛstɪv ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs in the body responsible for breaking down food and absorbing nutrients
system for digesting food
medical

Examples

The digestive system breaks down food so our bodies can use the nutrients.
Problems with the digestive system can cause discomfort and illness.
Doctors often study the digestive system to better understand how nutrition affects health.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastrointestinal tract - the passage through which food passes in the body
