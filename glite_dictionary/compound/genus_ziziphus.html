genus Ziziphus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs ˈzɪzɪfəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈzɪzɪfəs / (BrE)
A genus of spiny shrubs found chiefly in tropical regions of America and Asia, including the jujubes.
genus of spiny tropical shrubs (jujubes)

Examples

The genus Ziziphus includes several species valued for their edible fruit.
Botanists studied the distribution of the genus Ziziphus across Asian countries.
Many medicinal plants belong to the genus Ziziphus, notably the well-known jujube.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jujube - the fruit or shrub of Ziziphus species

Etymology

From Latin genus (kind) and Ziziphus, the Latin name for the jujube tree, from Greek zizuphos.
