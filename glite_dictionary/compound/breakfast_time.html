breakfast time

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkfəst taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst taɪm / (BrE)
the time of day when breakfast is usually eaten
the usual time for eating breakfast

Examples

We always gather around the kitchen table at breakfast time.
At breakfast time, the café is filled with people grabbing coffee and pastries.
He missed breakfast time because he overslept.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dinnertime - the usual time for eating dinner
  • Synonym: lunchtime - the usual time for eating lunch
