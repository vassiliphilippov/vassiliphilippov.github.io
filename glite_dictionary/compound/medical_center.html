medical center

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
an area within a city where medical facilities, such as hospitals and clinics, are concentrated
area with concentrated medical facilities

Examples

The hospital is located in the downtown medical center near several specialty clinics.
New developments in the city's medical center have improved access to healthcare.
Many students choose apartments close to the medical center for convenience.
