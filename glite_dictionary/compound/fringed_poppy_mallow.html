fringed poppy mallow

1. NOUN

/ frɪndʒd ˈpɑpi ˈmæloʊ / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈpɒpi ˈmæləʊ / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to the southern plains of the United States, with rose-red or rose-purple, fringed flowers
perennial US wildflower with fringed red-purple flowers

Examples

The fringed poppy mallow is prized in gardens for its striking rose-red flowers.
We spotted a patch of fringed poppy mallow during our hike in Texas.
Wildlife enthusiasts often photograph the blooming fringed poppy mallow in late spring.
