safety island

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪfti ˌaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˌaɪlənd / (BrE)
A raised, curbed area in a roadway, separated from traffic, that serves as a safe waiting place for pedestrians.
raised area in road for pedestrian safety

Examples

The city installed a safety island at the busy intersection to help pedestrians cross the street safely.
She waited on the safety island until the signal changed before crossing to the other side.
Buses now stop near the safety island so passengers can board more safely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traffic island - a defined area in a road for channeling traffic or aiding pedestrians
