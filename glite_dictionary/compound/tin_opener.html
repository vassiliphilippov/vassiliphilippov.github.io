tin opener

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪn ˌoʊpənər / (AmE), / ˈtɪn ˌəʊpənə / (BrE)

device for opening cans
british

Examples

This tin opener is much easier to use than the old one we had.
Make sure to pack a tin opener when you go camping, or you'll struggle with canned food.
