deep-dive analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːp daɪv əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈdiːp daɪv əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
a thorough and detailed examination of a particular subject or issue
thorough examination of a subject

Examples

The team presented a deep-dive analysis of customer behavior over the past year.
Her deep-dive analysis identified several key issues that others had overlooked.
Before making a decision, the board requested a deep-dive analysis on potential risks and opportunities.

Synset Examples

She offered a deep-dive analysis of the market trends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investigation - the action of investigating something or someone; formal or systematic examination

Etymology

From 'deep dive', originally an idiom meaning a thorough investigation, and 'analysis', from Greek 'analusis' (a breaking up).
