worldly possessions

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜrldli pəˈzɛʃənz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli pəˈzɛʃənz / (BrE)
all the material property or belongings that a person owns, as opposed to spiritual or intangible assets
material belongings owned by a person

Examples

He sold all his worldly possessions before moving abroad to start a new life.
After the fire, she was left with only a few worldly possessions.
Monks are known for renouncing their worldly possessions to focus on spiritual growth.

Synset Examples

he left all his worldly possessions to his daughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessions - things that one owns
