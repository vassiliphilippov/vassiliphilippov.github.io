marsh light

1. NOUN

/ mɑrʃ laɪt / (AmE), / mɑːʃ laɪt / (BrE)
a faint or mysterious light sometimes seen over marshy ground, often associated with folklore or unexplained natural phenomena
mysterious light seen in marshes

Examples

As we crossed the bog, a pale marsh light hovered near the reeds.
Local legends claim that a marsh light lured travelers off the safe path.
Some believe a marsh light is caused by natural gases igniting above the wetlands.

Synset Examples

We saw the marsh light flickering in the distance.
The legend spoke of a mysterious marsh light guiding lost travelers.
The marsh light disappeared as we approached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: will-o'-the-wisp - a phosphorescent light seen at night, especially over marshy ground
