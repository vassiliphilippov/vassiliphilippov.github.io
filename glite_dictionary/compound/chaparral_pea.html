chaparral pea

1. NOUN

/ tʃəˈpæɹəl pi / (AmE), / tʃəˈpærəl piː / (BrE)
a spiny evergreen shrub native to dry, rocky mountain slopes of California, with showy rose and purple flowers, forming dense thickets
spiny California shrub with rose-purple flowers

Examples

The chaparral pea is often found on dry, rocky slopes throughout coastal California.
Hikers were delighted by the clusters of bright flowers on the chaparral pea during early spring.
Dense thickets of chaparral pea provide shelter for many small animals in mountain habitats.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pickeringia - the botanical genus of the chaparral pea
