price list

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪs ˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈpraɪs ˌlɪst / (BrE)
A written or printed list showing the prices charged for different goods or services.
list showing prices of goods or services

Examples

The company updated its price list to reflect the new discounts.
Please ask for a price list before making your order.
The hotel’s price list is available online for guests to view.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catalogue - a complete list of items, often with descriptions and prices
