cinnamon-scented

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən ˌsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən ˌsɛntɪd / (BrE)
Having a smell or aroma of cinnamon.
having the smell of cinnamon

Examples

The kitchen was filled with the cinnamon-scented aroma of freshly baked rolls.
She bought a cinnamon-scented candle to give her living room a cozy feel.
Guests were welcomed with bowls of cinnamon-scented potpourri throughout the house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spicy - having a strong flavor or aroma from spices
