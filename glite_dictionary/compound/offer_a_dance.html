to offer a dance

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfər ə dæns / (AmE), / ˈɒfə ə dɑːns / (BrE)
to invite someone to dance with you, especially in a formal or social setting
invite someone to dance

Examples

He nervously approached Sophia and offered her a dance at the party.
Would you mind if I offer you a dance before the music ends?
At the wedding, several guests eagerly offered a dance to the bride.

Synset Examples

Milady, may I offer you a dance?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ask someone to dance - to request that someone dance with you
  • Antonym: accept a dance - to agree to dance when offered or invited
