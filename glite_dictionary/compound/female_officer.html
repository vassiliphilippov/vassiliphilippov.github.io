female officer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiː.meɪl ˈɑː.fɪ.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈfiː.meɪl ˈɒ.fɪ.sə / (BrE)
A woman who holds an official position of authority, especially in the armed forces, police, or other organizations.
woman in an officer position

Examples

She was the first female officer to lead the unit in its entire history.
The police department hired several female officers to promote diversity on the force.
As a female officer in the navy, she encountered both challenges and opportunities.

Synset Examples

She was celebrated as the first female officeress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: officer - a person holding a position of authority, especially in the armed forces or police
  • Antonym: male officer - a man holding an officer position
