soft and squishy

1. ADJ

/ sɔft ənd ˈskwɪʃi / (AmE), / sɒft ənd ˈskwɪʃi / (BrE)
pleasantly yielding to pressure; having a soft, compressible texture
having a soft, yielding texture

Examples

The cake turned out perfectly soft and squishy.
She loves stuffed animals that feel soft and squishy when you hug them.
After the rain, the ground was soft and squishy beneath our feet.
