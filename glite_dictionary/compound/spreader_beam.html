spreader beam

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛdər biːm / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdə ˌbiːm / (BrE)
a beam used in lifting operations, placed below the crane hook and fitted with ropes or slings at various points to distribute the load evenly
lifting beam for distributing loads

Examples

The workers attached the heavy load to the spreader beam before lifting it with the crane.
Using a spreader beam helped prevent the steel pipes from bending during the lift.
Safety regulations require a certified spreader beam when hoisting oversized equipment.
