pain sensation

1. NOUN

/ peɪn sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / peɪn sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the physical feeling of discomfort or suffering caused by injury, illness, or other stimuli affecting the body
physical feeling of discomfort or pain
medical

Examples

The pain sensation in her leg intensified after the injury.
Researchers are studying how the brain processes the pain sensation caused by heat or cold.
Some medications are designed to block the pain sensation without affecting other senses.

Synset Examples

as the intensity increased, the sensation changed from tickle to pain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pain - physical or emotional suffering or discomfort
  • Degree Lower: sensation - a physical feeling or perception resulting from something that happens to or comes into contact with the body
