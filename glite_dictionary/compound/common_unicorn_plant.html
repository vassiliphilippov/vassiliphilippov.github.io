common unicorn plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈjuːnɪˌkɔrn ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈjuːnɪˌkɔːn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
an annual plant native to the southern United States and Mexico, with large whitish or yellowish flowers mottled with purple and a distinctive long, curving beak-like fruit
annual plant with curved beak-like fruit

Examples

The common unicorn plant is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks in Texas.
Gardeners admire the unique fruit of the common unicorn plant for its long, curving shape.
Some artists use the dried seed pods of the common unicorn plant in their sculptures.
