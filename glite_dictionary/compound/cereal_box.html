cereal box

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪriəl bɑks / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl bɒks / (BrE)
a cardboard box used to package and sell breakfast cereals
cardboard box for breakfast cereals

Examples

He went to the pantry and grabbed a cereal box for breakfast.
The prize was hidden inside the cereal box as a surprise for children.
Please recycle the empty cereal box after you finish the cereal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carton - a box made from thick paper or cardboard
