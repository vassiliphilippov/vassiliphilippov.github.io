Green Party

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrin ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a political party that advocates for environmental protection and sustainability, often with left-leaning social policies
political party focused on environmental issues

Examples

The Green Party gained several seats in the last national election.
She joined the Green Party to support environmental and social justice causes.
Voters concerned about climate change are likely to support the Green Party candidates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environmentalist party - a political party concerned primarily with environmental issues
  • Antonym: Labour Party - major left-leaning political party
  • Antonym: Conservative Party - major right-leaning political party
