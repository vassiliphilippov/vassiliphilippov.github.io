harvest time

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvəst taɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst taɪm / (BrE)
the time of year when crops are gathered from the fields
season for gathering crops

Examples

Every year, the village holds a festival to celebrate harvest time.
Farmers worked tirelessly during harvest time to collect all their crops before the rain.
As harvest time approached, the fields turned golden with ripe wheat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops
