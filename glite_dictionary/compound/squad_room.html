squad room

1. NOUN

/ skwɑd ruːm / (AmE), / skwɒd ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a barracks where a group of soldiers are housed together
room in a barracks for soldiers

Examples

The new recruits kept their belongings neat and tidy in the squad room.
After evening roll call, the soldiers gathered in the squad room to relax.
Each squad room was inspected daily by the sergeant.

2. NOUN

/ skwɑd ruːm / (AmE), / skwɒd ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a police station where police officers gather for roll call and duty assignments
police station gathering room

Examples

The officers met in the squad room to receive their assignments for the night shift.
She decorated the squad room with banners after the team's big case was closed.
Tension filled the squad room as the detectives waited for news about their missing colleague.
