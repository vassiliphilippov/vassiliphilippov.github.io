horse doctor

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs ˈdɑktər / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a veterinarian who specializes in treating horses
veterinarian for horses

Examples

The injured stallion recovered quickly after the horse doctor treated its leg.
When her mare fell ill, she called for the best horse doctor in the county.
In the old West, a horse doctor was as important as a human physician.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: veterinarian - animal doctor; medical professional for animals
