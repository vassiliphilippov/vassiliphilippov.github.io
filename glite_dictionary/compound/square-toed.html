square-toed

1. ADJ

/ skwɛr toʊd / (AmE), / skwɛə təʊd / (BrE)
(of a shoe or a person's toes) having a flat, square-shaped front rather than rounded or pointed.
having a square-shaped toe

Examples

He bought a pair of square-toed leather boots for the wedding.
The square-toed style became popular again in the early 2000s.
Her square-toed sandals gave her a unique and stylish look.

Synset Examples

He prefers his shoes to be square-toed for comfort.
The square-toed design is very trendy this season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pointed-toe - having a shoe or toe shape with a pointed front
  • Antonym: round-toed - having a shoe or toe shape with a rounded front
