without scathe

1. ADP

/ wɪˈðaʊt skæθ / (AmE), / wɪˈðaʊt skeɪθ / (BrE)
without experiencing criticism, harm, or injury
without harm or criticism

Examples

The athlete completed the grueling race without scathe and celebrated his victory.
Despite the heated debate, she emerged from the meeting without scathe.
He navigated the risky business deal without scathe, surprising his colleagues.

Synset Examples

He spoke without scathe, choosing his words carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unscathed - not injured or harmed

Etymology

From 'without' (outside; lacking) and 'scathe' (harm; injury), with 'scathe' from Old Norse 'skaði' (harm, damage).
