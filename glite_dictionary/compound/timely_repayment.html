timely repayment

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmli rɪˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmli rɪˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying back money by the agreed-upon deadline or within the scheduled period
repayment made on time

Examples

Maintaining a good credit score requires timely repayment of all your loans.
The contract offers a discount for timely repayment of the principal amount.
Banks often reward customers who have a record of timely repayment.

Synset Examples

He planned his budget to ensure timely repayment of debts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: late payment - payment made after the agreed deadline
  • Synonym: punctual payment - payment made exactly at the scheduled time
