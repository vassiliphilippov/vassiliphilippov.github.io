idiot sandwich

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪdiət ˌsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət ˌsændwɪtʃ / (BrE)
a humorous insult or self-deprecating phrase meaning someone who feels extremely foolish or stupid
humorous insult for foolishness
informal slang

Examples

After forgetting his keys for the third time, he called himself an idiot sandwich in frustration.
The internet meme turned her embarrassing moment into the perfect idiot sandwich joke.
During the game show blunder, the contestant felt like a complete idiot sandwich.

Synset Examples

He felt like an idiot sandwich after that embarrassing mistake.
Making that choice left him in an idiot sandwich of trouble.
She called him an idiot sandwich for his foolish decision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or acting foolishly
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment

Etymology

Popularized by a comedic sketch involving chef Gordon Ramsay, combining 'idiot' and 'sandwich' for comic effect.
