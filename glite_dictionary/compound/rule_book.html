rule book

1. NOUN

/ ruːl bʊk / (AmE), / ruːl bʊk / (BrE)
a book or document that contains the rules and regulations for a particular activity, organization, or system
book of rules or regulations

Examples

Every team member should read the rule book before the season starts.
The rule book clearly states what penalties apply for late submissions.
Some people prefer to improvise rather than stick to the rule book.

Synset Examples

they run things by the book around here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: code - a set of rules or principles
  • Synonym: handbook - a book giving information such as facts or instructions
