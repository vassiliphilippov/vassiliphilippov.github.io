pedestrian crossing

1. NOUN

/ pəˈdɛstriən ˈkrɔsɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪˈdɛstrɪən ˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
a designated place for pedestrians to cross a road, typically marked with lines or stripes where they have the right of way
marked place for pedestrians to cross a road

Examples

The children waited patiently at the pedestrian crossing for the signal to turn green.
A new pedestrian crossing was installed near the school to improve safety.
Drivers must always stop for people using the pedestrian crossing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crosswalk - a place designated for people to cross a street, especially in North America
