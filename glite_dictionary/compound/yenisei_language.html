Yenisei language

1. NOUN

/ jɛnɪˌseɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / jɛnɪˌseɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extinct language of the Yeniseian people, formerly spoken along the Yenisei River in Siberia
extinct Siberian language

Examples

The Yenisei language is of great interest to linguists studying ancient Siberian cultures.
Most information about the Yenisei language comes from early explorers' records.
Few people today can read the surviving texts in the Yenisei language.

Etymology

From the Yenisei River, Siberia, with 'Yenisei' possibly from a local word meaning 'big water.'
