hoochie dance

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːtʃi dæns / (AmE), / ˈhuːtʃi dɑːns / (BrE)
A provocative or playful dance, often performed in a flirtatious or exaggerated manner.
provocative or playful dance
informal slang

Examples

Her hoochie dance at the party drew plenty of attention from the crowd.
He tried to copy the hoochie dance but ended up looking awkward and silly.
The music video featured a flashy hoochie dance during the chorus.

Synset Examples

That hoochie dance made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to twerk - A dance involving thrusting hip movements

Etymology

From 'hoochie', likely derived from 'hoochie coochie', an American term for a sexually suggestive dance (late 19th century).
