school crossing

1. NOUN

/ skuːl ˈkrɔsɪŋ / (AmE), / skuːl ˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
a designated place on a road where school children can cross safely, usually marked and supervised
crosswalk for school children

Examples

The guard helped the children cross safely at the school crossing each morning.
Parents are encouraged to volunteer at the school crossing near the primary school.
Drivers must slow down when approaching a school crossing during school hours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crosswalk - a place marked on a road where pedestrians may cross
