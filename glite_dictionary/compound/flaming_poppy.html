flaming poppy

1. NOUN

/ ˈfleɪmɪŋ ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋ ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A variety of California poppy distinguished by its bright red flowers.
California poppy with bright red flowers

Examples

The flaming poppy lit up the garden with its vibrant red blossoms.
Horticulturists admired the rare flaming poppy for its intense color.
She planted a flaming poppy among the traditional orange California poppies for contrast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: California poppy - A species of poppy native to California, typically with orange flowers
