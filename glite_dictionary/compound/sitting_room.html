sitting room

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪtɪŋ ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈsɪtɪŋ ˌruːm / (BrE)
a room in a private house or establishment where people can sit, talk, and relax
room in a house for sitting and relaxing

Examples

They gathered in the sitting room to enjoy a cup of tea and conversation.
The sitting room was filled with sunlight streaming in through the large windows.
We have decorated our sitting room with comfortable sofas and colorful cushions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living room - a room in a house for general everyday use
  • Synonym: parlor - a room in a private house for sitting and entertaining guests
