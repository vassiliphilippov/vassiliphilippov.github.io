stickup woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪk.ʌp ˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈstɪk.ʌp ˌwʊm.ən / (BrE)
A woman who commits armed robbery, especially by threatening violence with a weapon.
female armed robber

Examples

The police are searching for the stickup woman who robbed three banks last month.
Newspaper headlines described her as the notorious stickup woman behind the city’s crime spree.
Witnesses said the stickup woman threatened the clerk with a pistol and demanded all the money in the register.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stickup man - a man who commits armed robbery
  • Synonym: robber - a person who steals, especially by force
  • Antonym: victim - a person harmed by a crime

Etymology

From 'stickup' (armed robbery) + 'woman'. Patterned after 'stickup man'.
