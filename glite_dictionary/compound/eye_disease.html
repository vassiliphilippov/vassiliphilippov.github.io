eye disease

1. NOUN

/ aɪ dɪˈziz / (AmE), / aɪ dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
Any disease that affects the eye, including conditions impairing vision or eye health.
a disease affecting the eye
medical

Examples

Regular eye exams help detect eye disease in its early stages.
Glaucoma is a common eye disease that can lead to vision loss if untreated.
Her research focuses on genetic factors contributing to eye disease in children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocular disease - any disease of the eye
