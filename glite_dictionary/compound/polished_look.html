polished look

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːlɪʃt lʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃt lʊk / (BrE)
a refined and elegant appearance, suggesting careful grooming or design
refined, elegant appearance

Examples

Her simple black dress and understated jewelry gave her a truly polished look.
The new software interface has a much more polished look than the previous version.
A quick swipe of red lipstick can add a polished look to any casual outfit.

Synset Examples

She added a gloss to the photo for a polished look.
He admired her sleek hairstyle and polished look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleek - smooth and glossy in appearance, stylish
  • Antonym: shabby - in poor condition, not polished or refined in appearance
