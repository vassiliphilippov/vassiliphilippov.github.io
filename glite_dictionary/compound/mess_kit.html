mess kit

1. NOUN

/ mɛs kɪt / (AmE), / mɛs kɪt / (BrE)
a set of metal dishes and eating utensils, typically carried by soldiers or campers for eating meals outdoors
portable dishware set for eating outdoors

Examples

Every soldier was issued a mess kit before departing for the field.
The Boy Scouts learned how to clean their mess kits after dinner.
We packed our mess kit in the backpack for the camping trip.
