Pakistani rupee

1. NOUN

/ pəˈkɪstəni ruˈpi / (AmE), / pækɪˈstɑːni ruːˈpiː / (BrE)
the official currency of Pakistan, equal to 100 paisa
currency of Pakistan

Examples

The value of the Pakistani rupee fell sharply against the US dollar last year.
He exchanged his dollars for Pakistani rupees at the airport currency counter.
Tourists should check the current Pakistani rupee exchange rate before traveling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rupee - the basic monetary unit in several South Asian countries
