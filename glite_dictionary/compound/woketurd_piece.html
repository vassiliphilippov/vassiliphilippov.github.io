woketurd piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈwoʊkˌtɝd pis / (AmE), / ˈwəʊkˌtɜːd piːs / (BrE)
A derogatory term for an article, essay, or written work perceived as superficially progressive or insincerely advocating 'woke' views, lacking in genuine substance.
derogatory term for superficial progressive article
offensive slang

Examples

Many readers dismissed the editorial as just another woketurd piece with no original insight.
He accused the magazine of publishing a woketurd piece to appeal to a certain audience.
Some found her article thoughtful, while others labeled it a woketurd piece online.

Synset Examples

Critics called the article a woketurd piece lacking substance.
