violet-black

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌblæk / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˌblæk / (BrE)
of a black color having a tinge of violet; a shade of black with a purplish hue.
black tinged with violet

Examples

The artist chose a violet-black pigment to depict the twilight sky in her painting.
His jacket looked almost violet-black under the dim club lights.
Examination under the microscope revealed violet-black crystals scattered throughout the sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purple-black - black with a tinge of purple
