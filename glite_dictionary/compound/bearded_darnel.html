bearded darnel

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪrdɪd ˈdɑrnəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪədɪd ˈdɑːnəl / (BrE)
a weedy annual grass, often found in grainfields, with seeds that are sometimes considered poisonous
a poisonous weedy grass

Examples

Farmers take care to remove bearded darnel from their wheat fields to protect the crop.
The seeds of bearded darnel can be toxic to livestock if ingested in large quantities.
Historically, the presence of bearded darnel in bread flour was believed to cause dizziness and nausea.
