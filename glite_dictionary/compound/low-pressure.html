low-pressure

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌprɛʃər / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌprɛʃə / (BrE)
Not forceful or aggressive; gentle or relaxed in approach.
gentle; not forceful or aggressive

Examples

The company prefers a low-pressure work environment that reduces stress for employees.
She appreciated the low-pressure attitude of the yoga instructor, who encouraged students to move at their own pace.
It's easier to make decisions when you are not in a low-pressure sales situation.

Synset Examples

a low-pressure salesman
a low-pressure campaign

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-pressure - forceful or aggressive in approach
  • Synonym: gentle - having a mild or kind character
