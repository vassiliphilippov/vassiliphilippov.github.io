South of Houston

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ əv ˈhaʊstən / (AmE), / saʊθ əv ˈhaʊstən / (BrE)
a district in southwestern Manhattan, New York City, known for its shops, restaurants, art galleries, and artist lofts; commonly known as SoHo.
district in Manhattan, New York City

Examples

Many artists moved their studios to South of Houston in the 1970s.
Tourists flock to South of Houston for its boutique shopping and trendy restaurants.
The South of Houston area is famous for its cast iron architecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: SoHo - district in Manhattan, known for art and fashion; alternate name for South of Houston

Etymology

Named for its location south of Houston Street in Manhattan; the abbreviation SoHo is modeled after London's Soho district.
