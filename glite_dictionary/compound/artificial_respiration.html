artificial respiration

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːr.t̬əˈfɪʃ.əl ˌrɛs.pəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl ˌrɛs.pɪˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an emergency procedure that maintains breathing by artificial means when natural respiration has stopped or is inadequate
emergency procedure to maintain breathing
medical

Examples

The lifeguard performed artificial respiration until the paramedics arrived.
If someone stops breathing, you may need to give them artificial respiration to save their life.
Training in artificial respiration is essential for all first aid responders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mouth-to-mouth resuscitation - method of artificial respiration using a rescuer's breath
  • Synonym: cardiopulmonary resuscitation - emergency procedure combining artificial respiration and chest compressions
