total no-life

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.t̬əl ˈnoʊ laɪf / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈnəʊ laɪf / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who has no social life or engaging activities
person with no social life
informal

Examples

After staying inside playing games all summer, he felt like a total no-life.
She called herself a total no-life because she never went out with friends.
Everyone else was at the party while I just stayed home like a total no-life.

Synset Examples

Sometimes I feel like a total no-life on weekends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loner - a person who prefers not to associate with others
  • Antonym: social butterfly - a person who is socially active and outgoing
