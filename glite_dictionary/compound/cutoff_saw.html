cutoff saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌtˌɔf sɑ / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɒf sɔː / (BrE)
a handsaw designed to cut wood at right angles to the grain or major axis
a saw for cutting at right angles

Examples

The carpenter used a cutoff saw to trim the wooden planks precisely at right angles.
Always ensure your cutoff saw blade is sharp for accurate cuts across the grain.
A cutoff saw is essential for making crosscuts in woodworking projects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crosscut saw - a saw designed for cutting across the grain of wood
