zinc-rich

1. ADJ

/ zɪŋk rɪtʃ / (AmE), / zɪŋk rɪtʃ / (BrE)
containing a high amount of zinc, especially as a component of food, materials, or supplements
containing a large amount of zinc

Examples

Leafy green vegetables are often considered zinc-rich foods.
Doctors recommend eating zinc-rich snacks during cold season for immune support.
This supplement provides a zinc-rich boost to your daily diet.

Synset Examples

The recipe called for a pinch of zinc-rich pumpkin seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iron-rich - containing a high amount of iron
