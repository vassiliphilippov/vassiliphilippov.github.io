Americunt policies

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kʌnt ˈpɑːl.ə.siz / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kʌnt ˈpɒl.ɪ.siːz / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term used to describe U.S. government policies, expressing intense anger or contempt.
derogatory term for U.S. policies
offensive vulgar slang

Examples

Many activists rallied against the latest Americunt policies on immigration.
Social media was flooded with outrage at what users called the discriminatory Americunt policies.
Some critics use the term Americunt policies to express their disdain for controversial U.S. actions abroad.

Synset Examples

He criticized the Americunt policies that affected the working class.

Etymology

Formed by blending 'America' and an obscene insult to intensify criticism of U.S. policies.
