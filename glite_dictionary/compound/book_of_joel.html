Book of Joel

1. NOUN

/ bʊk əv ˈdʒoʊəl / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈdʒəʊəl / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament containing the prophecies of Joel
Old Testament book of prophecies

Examples

Many scholars have analyzed the prophetic themes found in the Book of Joel.
The Book of Joel is often cited for its vivid imagery of locusts and restoration.
During the sermon, the pastor quoted from the Book of Joel to illustrate hope after disaster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Joel - a prophet in the Old Testament and the supposed author of the Book of Joel
  • Degree Higher: Book of Amos - another prophetic book in the Old Testament
  • Degree Lower: Book of Hosea - Old Testament prophetic book preceding Joel
