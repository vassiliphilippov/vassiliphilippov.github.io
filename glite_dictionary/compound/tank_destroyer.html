tank destroyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæŋk dɪˌstrɔɪər / (AmE), / ˈtaŋk dɪˌstrɔɪə / (BrE)
a lightly armored military vehicle equipped with a powerful antitank gun and designed to destroy enemy tanks
armored vehicle with antitank gun

Examples

The army deployed several tank destroyers to halt the enemy's armored advance.
During the fierce battle, a tank destroyer took out two enemy tanks with ease.
Modern tank destroyers are equipped with advanced targeting systems for increased accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - an armored fighting vehicle with tracks and a large gun
  • Synonym: armored vehicle - a vehicle protected by armor, used in military operations
