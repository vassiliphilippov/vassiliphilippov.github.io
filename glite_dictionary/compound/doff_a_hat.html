to doff a hat

1. VERB

/ dɑf ə hæt / (AmE), / dɒf ə hæt / (BrE)
to remove one's hat as a gesture of respect or greeting
remove hat as a sign of respect

Examples

As the procession passed, he doffed his hat in a show of respect.
It was customary to doff a hat upon entering the church.
She smiled when the gentleman politely doffed his hat to greet her.

Synset Examples

He doffed his hat in respect as she passed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: don a hat - to put on a hat

Etymology

From 'doff', a contraction of 'do off', meaning 'to take off'.
