Union of Burma

1. PROPN

/ ˈjun.jən əv ˈbɝː.mə / (AmE), / ˈjuː.njən əv ˈbɜː.mə / (BrE)
the former official name of Myanmar, a country in southeastern Asia bordering the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea
former official name of Myanmar

Examples

The country was officially called the Union of Burma before it became Myanmar.
Several treaties were signed by the Union of Burma government in the 20th century.
She studied the post-colonial history of the Union of Burma in her master's thesis.

Synset Examples

much opium is grown in Myanmar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Myanmar - the current official name for the same country

Etymology

The name 'Union of Burma' was adopted after the country's independence from British colonial rule in 1948; 'Burma' derives from the Burmese word 'Bamar,' the majority ethnic group.
