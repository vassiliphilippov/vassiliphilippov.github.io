Tajik language

1. NOUN

/ tɑːˈdʒiːk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɑːˈdʒiːk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken mainly in Tajikistan and parts of Uzbekistan, closely related to Persian (Farsi)
Iranian language of Tajikistan

Examples

Many students travel to Dushanbe to study the Tajik language in an immersive environment.
Although similar to Persian, the Tajik language uses the Cyrillic alphabet in Tajikistan.
She was proud of her fluency in Russian, Uzbek, and the Tajik language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Iranian language spoken in Iran and understood to be closely related to Tajik
  • Synonym: Farsi - the modern Persian language as spoken in Iran

Etymology

From Persian تاجیکی (Tâjiki), relating to Tajik people and their language.
