soot-black

1. ADJ

/ sʊt blæk / (AmE), / sʊt blæk / (BrE)
as black as soot; having a deep, dark black color similar to soot.
deep black color, like soot

Examples

The artist used soot-black paint to create dramatic contrast in her work.
His coat was soot-black after he crawled through the old chimney.
The cat's soot-black fur shimmered in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-black - as black as jet; very deep black color
