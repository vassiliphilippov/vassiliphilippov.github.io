horned lizard

1. NOUN

/ hɔrnd ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, spiny lizard found in western North America, with hornlike projections on its head and spiny scales on its body
spiny lizard with hornlike head projections

Examples

The horned lizard relies on camouflage to avoid predators in its desert habitat.
We spotted a horned lizard basking in the sun near the hiking trail.
Many people mistakenly call the horned lizard a toad because of its stout body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horned toad - another term for horned lizard
