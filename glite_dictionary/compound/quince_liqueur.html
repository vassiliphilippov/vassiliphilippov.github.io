quince liqueur

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪns lɪˈkɜr / (AmE), / ˈkwɪns lɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
a strong alcoholic liqueur made from the fruit of the quince
liqueur made from quince fruit

Examples

At the end of the meal, she offered her guests a glass of quince liqueur as a digestif.
Some traditional recipes call for a splash of quince liqueur to enhance fruity desserts.
He bought a bottle of local quince liqueur during his trip to Eastern Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liqueur - a sweet, strong alcoholic drink flavored with fruit, herbs, or spices
