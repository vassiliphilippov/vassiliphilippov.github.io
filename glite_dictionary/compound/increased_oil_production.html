increased oil production

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkrist ɔɪl prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːst ɔɪl prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
a rise in the amount of oil extracted or produced, often as a result of technology or policy changes
a rise in the amount of oil produced

Examples

The country benefited from increased oil production, boosting its economy.
Technological advances have led to increased oil production in the region.
Environmentalists are concerned about the effects of increased oil production on climate change.

Synset Examples

Fracking has increased oil production dramatically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil production - the process or amount of extracting oil
