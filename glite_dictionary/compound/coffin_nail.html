coffin nail

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.fɪn ˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.fɪn ˌneɪl / (BrE)
(slang) A cigarette; so called because of its association with health risks.
slang for a cigarette
slang

Examples

He stepped outside to have a quick coffin nail during the break.
The doctor warned him that every coffin nail reduces his life expectancy.
Back in the day, people used to light up a coffin nail almost anywhere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette - a small cylinder of finely cut tobacco rolled in paper for smoking
  • Synonym: smoke - another informal term for cigarette

Etymology

Figurative use, likening cigarettes to 'nails' put into one's coffin (i.e., bringing death closer).
