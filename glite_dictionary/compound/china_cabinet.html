china cabinet

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪnə ˌkæbənɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnə ˌkabɪnɪt / (BrE)
a cabinet, typically with glass doors, used for storing and displaying china or other fine dinnerware
cabinet for displaying china

Examples

The antique china cabinet in the dining room is filled with porcelain dishes.
She proudly arranged her collection of teacups in the new china cabinet.
We inherited a mahogany china cabinet from our grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display cabinet - a cabinet designed to display objects
