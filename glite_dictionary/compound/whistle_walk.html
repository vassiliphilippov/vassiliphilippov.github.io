whistle walk

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪsəl wɑk / (AmE), / ˈwɪsəl wɔːk / (BrE)
A casual or leisurely walk, often taken while whistling.
casual walk with whistling

Examples

Every morning, I start my day with a peaceful whistle walk around the neighborhood.
She suggested a whistle walk to help relieve some of his stress.
They enjoyed a long whistle walk through the woods, whistling old tunes.

Synset Examples

Every Sunday, he takes a whistle walk to clear his mind.
She enjoyed a leisurely whistle walk through the quiet park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stroll - a leisurely walk
  • Synonym: saunter - to walk in a slow, relaxed manner
