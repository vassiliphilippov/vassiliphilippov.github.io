Uruguay potato

1. NOUN

/ ˌjʊrəˌɡwaɪ pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˌɡwaɪ pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
A variety of potato similar to the common potato, originating from or associated with Uruguay.
a potato variety from Uruguay

Examples

The chef used a Uruguay potato to prepare an authentic South American dish.
Farmers in the region recently harvested several tons of Uruguay potatoes.
The texture of the Uruguay potato makes it ideal for mashing or roasting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potato - a starchy tuberous crop, staple food
