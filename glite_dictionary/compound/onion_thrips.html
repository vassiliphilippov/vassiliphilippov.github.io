onion thrips

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌn.jən ˌθɹɪps / (AmE), / ˈʌn.jən ˌθrɪps / (BrE)
a small insect pest (Thrips tabaci) that infests onion plants and sometimes tobacco, causing damage by feeding on their tissues
insect pest harmful to onions and tobacco

Examples

Onion thrips can cause serious crop losses if not properly controlled.
Farmers monitor their fields regularly for signs of onion thrips infestation.
Onion thrips are also known to damage tobacco crops under certain conditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrips - any of a group of tiny, slender insects of the order Thysanoptera
