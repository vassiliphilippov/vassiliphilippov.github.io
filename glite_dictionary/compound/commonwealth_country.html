Commonwealth country

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmənˌwɛlθ ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənˌwɛlθ ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
a country that is a member of the British Commonwealth of Nations
member nation of the British Commonwealth

Examples

Australia is a Commonwealth country and participates in international events with other member nations.
Citizens of a Commonwealth country may have special privileges when traveling to the United Kingdom.
She studied the legal systems that exist across various Commonwealth countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Commonwealth - the organization of independent nations, mostly former British colonies

Etymology

From 'Commonwealth' (referring to the British Commonwealth) + 'country'.
