Mother Thumb

1. PROPN

/ ˈmʌðɚ θʌm / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə θʌm / (BrE)
a character from a traditional nursery rhyme, often representing the thumb in fingerplay songs
character in a nursery rhyme

Examples

As a child, she loved the nursery rhyme about Mother Thumb and her daughters.
During storytime, the teacher wiggled her thumb and called it Mother Thumb.
The song featuring Mother Thumb helps children learn the names of their fingers.

Synset Examples

The nursery rhyme about Mother Thumb and her four daughters is fun.
