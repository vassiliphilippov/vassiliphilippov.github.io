aldermanic meeting

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔldɚˈmænɪk ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɔːldəˈmænɪk ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting held by aldermen, typically to discuss and decide on matters of local government
meeting of aldermen for local government business

Examples

The city council scheduled an aldermanic meeting to vote on the new zoning proposal.
At last night's aldermanic meeting, citizens voiced concerns about increased traffic.
Public attendance is encouraged at the monthly aldermanic meeting.

Synset Examples

The aldermanic meeting addressed community safety issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: council meeting - a formal meeting of a local government council

Etymology

From 'alderman', from Old English 'ealdorman' (elder, chief) + 'meeting'.
