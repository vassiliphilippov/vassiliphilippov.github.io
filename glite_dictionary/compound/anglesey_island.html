Anglesey Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈæŋɡəlˌsi ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡləsi ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
a large island off the northwest coast of Wales, separated from the mainland by the Menai Strait
island off northwest Wales

Examples

We spent our holiday exploring Anglesey Island and its beautiful coastal villages.
The bridge over the Menai Strait connects mainland Wales to Anglesey Island.
Many rare bird species can be found nesting on Anglesey Island during spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wales - a country in the southwest of Great Britain

Etymology

From Welsh 'Ynys Môn' (Môn Island); 'Anglesey' possibly from Old Norse or Latin forms.
