change of life

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (BrE)
the time in a woman's life when menstruation ends and she is no longer able to have children; menopause
menopause; end of menstrual cycles

Examples

Many women experience symptoms such as hot flashes during the change of life.
Her doctor explained that the change of life is a natural biological process.
Some women find the change of life challenging, both physically and emotionally.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menopause - the period in a woman's life when menstruation ceases

2. NOUN

/ tʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (BrE)
a significant or transformative period or event that greatly affects someone's way of living or perspective
major transformative event or period

Examples

Moving to another country was a true change of life for our family.
Winning the scholarship became the change of life she had been hoping for.
For many people, retirement marks a change of life filled with new opportunities.

Synset Examples

That trip was a real change of life for me.

Adopting a pet was her change of life moment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turning point - a decisive moment of change or transition
