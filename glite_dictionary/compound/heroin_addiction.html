heroin addiction

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛroʊɪn əˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈhɛrəʊɪn əˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
A strong and harmful dependence on heroin, characterized by compulsive drug use despite harmful consequences.
dependence on heroin
medical

Examples

Many families have been affected by heroin addiction in their communities.
Overcoming heroin addiction often requires medical and psychological support.
His struggle with heroin addiction lasted for more than a decade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addiction - compulsive need for and use of a habit-forming substance
