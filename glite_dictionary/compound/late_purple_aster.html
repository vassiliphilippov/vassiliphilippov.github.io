late purple aster

1. NOUN

/ leɪt ˈpɝpl̩ ˈæstɚ / (AmE), / leɪt ˈpɜːpl ˈæstə / (BrE)
a North American wildflower (Symphyotrichum patens) bearing lavender to purple daisy-like flowers in late summer and fall
a wildflower with purple blooms (Symphyotrichum patens)

Examples

The late purple aster blooms beautifully in the fall along woodland edges.
Gardeners often plant late purple aster for its vibrant autumn color.
We identified several late purple aster growing in the nature reserve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aster - a plant of the daisy family with star-shaped flower heads
