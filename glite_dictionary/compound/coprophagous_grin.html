coprophagous grin

1. NOUN

/ kəˈprɑf.ə.ɡəs ɡrɪn / (AmE), / kəˈprɒf.ə.ɡəs ɡrɪn / (BrE)
a grin that suggests dark humor, grotesque amusement, or inappropriate shock
grotesque or darkly humorous grin

Examples

The villain's coprophagous grin unsettled everyone in the room.
He flashed a coprophagous grin when recounting the morbid tale.
Her coprophagous grin was both captivating and deeply disturbing.

Synset Examples

The comedian's coprophagous grin made the audience laugh.
She gave a coprophagous grin when sharing a shocking joke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grimace - a twisted facial expression, often showing disgust or pain

Etymology

From 'coprophagous', meaning 'feeding on feces', used metaphorically for extreme unpleasantness or shock.
