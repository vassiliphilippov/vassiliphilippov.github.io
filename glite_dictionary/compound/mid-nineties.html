mid-nineties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈnaɪtiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈnaɪtiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life when they are between 90 and 100 years old
age between 90 and 100

Examples

Her grandmother remained active well into her mid-nineties.
Many people in their mid-nineties continue to live independently.
He celebrated his birthday in the mid-nineties with all his great-grandchildren.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nineties - the period of life from age 90 to 99
