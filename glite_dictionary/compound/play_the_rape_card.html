to play the rape card

1. VERB

/ pleɪ ðə reɪp kɑrd / (AmE), / pleɪ ðə reɪp kɑːd / (BrE)
To use the topic or accusation of rape, especially in a manipulative way, to gain sympathy or advantage in a situation.
exploit rape accusations for advantage
offensive

Examples

During the trial, the defense accused the plaintiff of playing the rape card to influence the jury.
Some critics argued that the politician was playing the rape card to distract from the real issues.
It's irresponsible to play the rape card in a debate just to win public support.

Synset Examples

He accused her of playing the 'rape card' during the debate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to play the race card - to exploit one's race or accusations of racism for personal advantage

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'play the race card' (to invoke race for advantage).
