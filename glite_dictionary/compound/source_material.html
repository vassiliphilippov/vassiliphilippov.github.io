source material

1. NOUN

/ sɔrs məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / sɔːs məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
documents or publications that provide information used as a basis for research, study, or creative work
original documents used for research or study

Examples

Researchers must cite the source materials they use in their papers.
The historian spent years analyzing source materials from the eighteenth century.
Good source materials are essential for an accurate report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reference material - materials used for consultation to obtain information
  • Synonym: primary source - an original document or firsthand evidence
  • Antonym: secondary source - work that discusses or analyzes primary sources
