Association of Islamic Groups and Communities

1. PROPN

/ əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən əv ɪzˈlɑmɪk ˈɡruːps ənd kəˈmjunɪtiz / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən ɒv ɪzˈlɑːmɪk ˈɡruːps ənd kəˈmjuːnɪtiz / (BrE)
A Turkish Islamist militant organization with ties to al-Qaeda, active primarily in Germany, seeking the violent overthrow of the Turkish government to establish an Islamic state modeled on Iran.
Turkish militant Islamist organization

Examples

The Association of Islamic Groups and Communities was implicated in several plots against the Turkish government.
German authorities cracked down on activities linked to the Association of Islamic Groups and Communities.
Security experts monitor the Association of Islamic Groups and Communities for its connections to international extremist networks.
