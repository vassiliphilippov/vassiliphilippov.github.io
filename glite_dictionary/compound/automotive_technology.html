automotive technology

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmoʊtɪv tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈməʊtɪv tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or application of the design, development, and construction of automobiles and their systems
technology and engineering related to automobiles

Examples

Advances in automotive technology have made cars safer and more efficient than ever before.
He decided to study automotive technology at the local community college.
The latest trends in automotive technology focus on electric and autonomous vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automobile engineering - the branch of engineering that deals with automobiles

Etymology

From 'automotive' (relating to self-propelled vehicles) and 'technology' (systematic application of knowledge).
