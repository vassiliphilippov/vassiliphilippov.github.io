to look so douchey

1. VERB

/ lʊk soʊ ˈduʃi / (AmE), / lʊk səʊ ˈduːʃi / (BrE)
to appear obnoxious, arrogant, or socially unacceptable in manner or style
appear obnoxious or arrogant
informal slang offensive american

Examples

Wearing sunglasses indoors makes him look so douchey.
She didn't want to look so douchey at the party, so she changed her outfit.
That flashy sports car really makes you look so douchey to everyone.

Synset Examples

Wearing that outfit makes him look so douchey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look like a jerk - to appear rude or unpleasant
  • Antonym: look respectable - to appear proper or acceptable

Etymology

From 'douche,' an insult in American English (possibly from French 'douche' meaning 'shower'). 'Douchey' is a derived slang adjective.
