plume of feathers

1. NOUN

/ plum əv ˈfɛð.ɚz / (AmE), / pluːm əv ˈfɛð.əz / (BrE)
a long, soft, or showy feather or a group of feathers, often used for decoration or adornment
decorative feather or cluster of feathers

Examples

The dancer wore a sparkling costume topped with a plume of feathers.
A majestic plume of feathers adorned the warrior's helmet.
She decorated the hat with a large, colorful plume of feathers.

Synset Examples

The artist painted a plume of feathers gracefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feather - one of the flat, light structures covering the bodies of birds
