silver wedding anniversary

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌænɪˈvɝːsɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈwɛdɪŋ ˌænɪˈvɜːsəri / (BrE)
The 25th anniversary of a marriage, often celebrated as a significant milestone.
25th wedding anniversary

Examples

They celebrated their silver wedding anniversary with a large family gathering.
Their children threw a surprise party for their parents' silver wedding anniversary.
This year marks the silver wedding anniversary of the couple who married in 1999.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: golden wedding anniversary - the 50th anniversary of a marriage
  • Degree Lower: wedding anniversary - the anniversary of the date on which a wedding took place

Etymology

From the tradition of celebrating the 25th wedding anniversary with gifts of silver, dating back to European customs.
