bead-rattler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːd ˌræt.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈbiːd ˌræt.lə / (BrE)
a person who makes noise or draws attention, especially in a festive or celebratory setting
person making noise in festive context

Examples

During Carnival, the most energetic bead-rattler led the parade with his infectious enthusiasm.
The children took turns playing the role of bead-rattler in their school performance.
Everyone watched as the bead-rattler danced through the streets, shaking maracas and cheering.

Synset Examples

He's known as a bead-rattler, always making noise.
She played the role of a bead-rattler in the parade.
The bead-rattler at the festival got everyone's attention.
