aircrew member

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkru ˈmɛmbər / (AmE), / ˈeəˌkruː ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who is part of the operational staff serving on an aircraft during a flight
member of an aircraft's operational staff

Examples

Every aircrew member must pass a medical examination before flying.
She worked as an aircrew member on international flights for over a decade.
The training program is mandatory for each new aircrew member.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crew member - a person who is part of a working team, especially on a vehicle
  • Antonym: passenger - a person who is not part of the crew but travels in a vehicle
