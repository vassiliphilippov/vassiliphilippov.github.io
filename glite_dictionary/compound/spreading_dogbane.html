spreading dogbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈdɒɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Apocynum androsaemifolium) with pinkish flowers in loose clusters, traditionally used in folk medicine for joint pain and inflammation
North American plant with pinkish flowers; used in folk medicine

Examples

Hikers in the region often pass by spreading dogbane growing along the trail.
Traditional remedies sometimes used the roots of spreading dogbane to ease aching joints.
The pinkish blossoms of spreading dogbane attract a variety of pollinators in the summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dogbane - another plant of the genus Apocynum, closely related species
