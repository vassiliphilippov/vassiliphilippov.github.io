cisoid perspective

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪsɔɪd pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɪsɔɪd pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
an approach or viewpoint based on the properties or concepts of a cisoid, a specific mathematical curve
approach based on a mathematical curve (cisoid)

Examples

The architect's design was inspired by a cisoid perspective to create unique curved structures.
Researchers adopted a cisoid perspective when analyzing the complex wave patterns.
By applying a cisoid perspective, the mathematician revealed unexpected properties of the system.

Synset Examples

She approached the problem with a cisoid perspective.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sinusoidal perspective - an approach based on or involving sinusoidal curves

Etymology

From 'cisoid', a term in geometry from Greek 'cis-', meaning 'on this side', and 'oid', meaning 'form' or 'resemblance'.
