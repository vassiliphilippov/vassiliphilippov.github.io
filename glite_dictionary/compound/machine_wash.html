to machine wash

1. VERB

/ məˈʃin wɑʃ / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn wɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash (clothes or fabric) using a washing machine rather than by hand
wash with a washing machine

Examples

Most delicate items should not be machine washed to prevent damage.
You can machine wash these curtains in cold water.
She always checks if her clothes are safe to machine wash before doing laundry.

Synset Examples

Can these shirts be machine-washed?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hand wash - to wash (clothes, etc.) by hand rather than by machine
