pink-lavender

1. ADJ

/ pɪŋk ˈlævəndər / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈlævəndə / (BrE)
A shade of lavender with a pinkish tint.
lavender tinged with pink

Examples

She chose pink-lavender curtains to add a soft touch to the room.
The sky was painted with hues of pink-lavender at sunrise.
Her dress was a delicate shade of pink-lavender, perfect for the spring wedding.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lavender - a pale purple color
  • Degree Higher: mauve - a pale purple color, pinker than lavender
