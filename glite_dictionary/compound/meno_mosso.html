meno mosso

1. ADJ

/ ˌmeɪnoʊ ˈmoʊsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmeɪnəʊ ˈməʊsəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) played at a reduced speed; less rapid.
played at a reduced speed (music)

Examples

The composer marked the next section meno mosso to create a gentle contrast.
As the piece approached the coda, the tempo shifted to meno mosso.
The transition was highlighted by a sudden change to meno mosso, giving the music a calmer feel.

Etymology

Italian, literally 'less moved.'
