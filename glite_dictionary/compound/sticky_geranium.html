sticky geranium

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪki dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˈstɪki dʒəˈreɪniəm / (BrE)
a wildflower native to western North America, with hairy, sticky stems and pinkish-purple flowers in loose clusters
a pink-flowered North American wild geranium

Examples

The sticky geranium thrives in the meadows of the Pacific Northwest.
We stopped to admire a cluster of blooming sticky geraniums along the trail.
Gardeners sometimes plant sticky geranium for its attractive, long-lasting flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geranium - any plant of the genus Geranium, commonly called cranesbills
