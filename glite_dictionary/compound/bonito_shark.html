bonito shark

1. NOUN

/ bəˈniːtoʊ ʃɑrk / (AmE), / bəˈniːtəʊ ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a common blue-grey shark of the southwest Pacific, valued as both a sport and food fish.
blue-grey shark of the southwest Pacific

Examples

Fishermen in New Zealand often seek the bonito shark for both its size and its challenge.
The bonito shark is recognized by its distinctive blue-grey coloration.
Some coastal restaurants in Australia serve dishes featuring bonito shark as a specialty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue shark - a large, slender blue shark common in temperate waters
