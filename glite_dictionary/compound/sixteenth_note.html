sixteenth note

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪksˌtinθ ˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪksˌtiːnθ ˌnəʊt / (BrE)
a musical note with a duration equal to one sixteenth of a whole note
musical note, one-sixteenth duration

Examples

The melody became more lively when the composer added several sixteenth notes to the rhythm.
She practiced playing sixteenth notes until she could perform them smoothly and accurately.
In this piece, the left hand plays a series of rapid sixteenth notes throughout the measure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semidemiquaver - a musical note with the same time value as a sixteenth note (British English)
