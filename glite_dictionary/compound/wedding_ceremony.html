wedding ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
a formal event at which a marriage is officially celebrated and the vows are exchanged
event where marriage ceremony takes place

Examples

They invited all their family and friends to their wedding ceremony last summer.
The couple exchanged vows during a beautiful wedding ceremony in the garden.
A traditional wedding ceremony often includes music, readings, and a formal exchange of rings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony and the events associated with it
