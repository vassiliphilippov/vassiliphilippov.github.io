boat racing

1. NOUN

/ boʊt ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / bəʊt ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sport in which boats are raced against each other on water, typically using oars, sails, or engines.
the sport of racing boats

Examples

Every summer, the town hosts an annual boat racing event on the river.
He developed a passion for boat racing after joining the university rowing club.
Boat racing requires both physical strength and teamwork to succeed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rowing - the sport or activity of propelling a boat using oars
  • Synonym: sailing - the sport of using sails to move a boat
