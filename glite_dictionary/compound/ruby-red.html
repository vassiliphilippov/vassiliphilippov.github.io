ruby-red

1. ADJ

/ ˈruːbi rɛd / (AmE), / ˈruːbi rɛd / (BrE)
Having the deep red color characteristic of a ruby; vividly red.
deep, vivid red color

Examples

The artist chose a ruby-red for the flowers in her painting.
He wore a stunning ruby-red tie to the formal dinner.
The setting sun cast a ruby-red glow over the city skyline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarlet - bright red color with a slightly orange tint
  • Synonym: crimson - rich deep red color inclining to purple

Etymology

From 'ruby' (the gemstone, from Latin 'rubinus' meaning 'red') and 'red'.
