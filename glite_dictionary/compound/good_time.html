good time

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
an enjoyable or exciting experience; a period during which one has fun
enjoyable or exciting experience

Examples

We had a good time hiking in the mountains last weekend.
She always knows how to turn any gathering into a good time.
Everyone agreed that the concert was a good time from start to finish.
