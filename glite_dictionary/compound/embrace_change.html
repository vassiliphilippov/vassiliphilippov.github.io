to embrace change

1. VERB

/ ɪmˈbreɪs tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
to willingly accept and adapt to new circumstances or transformations
willingly accept and adapt to change

Examples

Leaders must encourage employees to embrace change in a rapidly evolving industry.
After moving to a new city, she decided to embrace change rather than resist it.
Technology requires us to constantly embrace change and stay adaptable.

Synset Examples

It's important to embrace change in our lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to adapt - to adjust to new conditions
  • Antonym: resist change - to refuse to accept new circumstances or transformations
