Green Line

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡrin laɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn laɪn / (BrE)
the demarcation line that marked the boundaries between Israel and its neighbors established after the 1948 Arab-Israeli War
Israeli 1948 armistice border

Examples

Tourists can visit the historic Green Line that separates West Jerusalem from the West Bank.
International talks often focus on changes to the Green Line established in 1949.
The neighborhood lies just a few blocks from the original Green Line between Israel and its neighbors.
