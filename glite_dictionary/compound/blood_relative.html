blood relative

1. NOUN

/ blʌd ˈrɛlətɪv / (AmE), / blʌd ˈrɛlətɪv / (BrE)
A person related to someone by birth rather than by marriage or adoption; a person sharing a common ancestor.
person related by birth or origin

Examples

She donated her kidney to a blood relative who was in urgent need.
Only blood relatives were allowed to attend the family reunion.
He discovered he had a blood relative living in another country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kin - one's family and relations
  • Antonym: relative by marriage - a person related to someone by marriage
