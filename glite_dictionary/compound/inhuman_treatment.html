inhuman treatment

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈhjuːmən ˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈhjuːmən ˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the act of deliberately causing great pain and suffering to someone in a cruel way
deliberately cruel treatment

Examples

The prisoners complained of inhuman treatment while in detention.
International law prohibits the use of inhuman treatment against anyone in custody.
Journalists reported shocking cases of inhuman treatment in the refugee camps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruelty - deliberate infliction of pain and suffering
