sanghi community

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːŋgi kəˈmjuːnəti / (AmE), / ˈsæŋgi kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people associated with or supporting the Hindu nationalist ideology in India, especially those linked to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) or similar organizations
group supporting Hindu nationalist ideology in India

Examples

The sanghi community organized a charity event in support of education for underprivileged children.
Debates between liberals and the sanghi community often become heated on social media platforms.
Members of the sanghi community regularly take part in religious and cultural processions throughout the year.

Synset Examples

The sanghi community organized a charity event.

Etymology

From Hindi 'Sanghī', derived from 'Sangh', meaning (association), typically referring to members of the RSS.
