lesser spotted woodpecker

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛsər ˌspɑːtɪd ˈwʊdˌpɛkər / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˌspɒtɪd ˈwʊdpɛkə / (BrE)
a small species of woodpecker found in Europe and parts of Asia, with distinctive black and white markings and a barred back
a small European woodpecker species

Examples

The lesser spotted woodpecker is quite rare in this area.
The distinctive call of the lesser spotted woodpecker can often be heard in old woodlands.
We were excited to spot a pair of lesser spotted woodpeckers nesting near the river.

Synset Examples
