sleeping accommodation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
a room or space primarily intended for sleeping, especially in a hotel, train, or other temporary setting
room or space for sleeping

Examples

The train offers sleeping accommodation for overnight journeys.
We need to book sleeping accommodation for all the conference attendees.
The cabin contains basic sleeping accommodation but no private bathroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedroom - a room used for sleeping in a house or apartment
