meal prep

1. NOUN

/ mil prɛp / (AmE), / miːl prɛp / (BrE)
the act of preparing meals in advance, often for the coming days or week, to save time and support healthy eating
preparing meals in advance

Examples

Proper meal prep can help you stick to a healthy diet even with a busy schedule.
She spends her Sunday afternoons doing meal prep for the entire week.
Investing in good containers makes meal prep much more convenient and efficient.

Synset Examples

A good mincer makes meal prep much easier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meal planning - organizing and planning meals ahead of time
