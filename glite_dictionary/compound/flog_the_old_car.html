to flog the old car

1. VERB

/ flɑg ði oʊld kɑr / (AmE), / flɒg ði əʊld kɑː / (BrE)
to sell your old car, especially in an informal or casual way
informal: sell an old car
informal british

Examples

I'm thinking I'll flog the old car and put the money towards a new one.
After months of repairs, he finally decided to flog the old car to a neighbor.
She managed to flog the old car online for a decent price.

Synset Examples

He had to flog the old car to make space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sell - to exchange something for money

Etymology

From British informal 'flog' meaning 'to sell,' of uncertain origin.
