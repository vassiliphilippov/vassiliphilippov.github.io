baseball equipment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪsˌbɔl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsbɔːl ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
the equipment, such as bats, balls, gloves, and protective gear, used in playing baseball
items used in playing baseball

Examples

The coach made sure all the baseball equipment was ready for the first day of practice.
She bought new baseball equipment for her son, including a glove and a helmet.
Proper baseball equipment is essential to ensure player safety during games.

Related Terms
