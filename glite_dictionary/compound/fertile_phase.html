fertile phase

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝː.təl feɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.taɪl feɪz / (BrE)
the period in the menstrual cycle when a woman is most likely to conceive, typically spanning several days around ovulation
period of highest fertility in menstrual cycle
medical

Examples

Couples trying to conceive often track the fertile phase of the menstrual cycle.
Understanding your fertile phase can increase your chances of becoming pregnant.
Doctors recommend using ovulation tests to help identify the fertile phase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ovulation - release of an egg from the ovary
  • Antonym: infertile period - time in the menstrual cycle when conception is unlikely
