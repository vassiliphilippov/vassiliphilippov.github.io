leaf blight

1. NOUN

/ lif blaɪt / (AmE), / liːf blaɪt / (BrE)
a plant disease causing browning and premature falling of leaves
plant disease affecting leaves

Examples

The tomato crop was severely damaged by leaf blight last summer.
Farmers are taking measures to prevent leaf blight from spreading in their fields.
Symptoms of leaf blight include brown spots and early leaf drop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blight - a plant disease or injury resulting in withering or death
  • Synonym: fungal infection - an infection in plants caused by fungi
