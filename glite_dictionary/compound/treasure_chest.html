treasure chest

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒər tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə tʃɛst / (BrE)
A large strong box, typically used to store money, jewels, or other valuables, often associated with pirates or hidden riches.
box for storing valuables or treasure

Examples

The pirates buried their treasure chest on a deserted island before sailing away.
Legend says a treasure chest is hidden somewhere in the old castle's basement.
He opened the dusty treasure chest and found it filled with gold coins and jewels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chest - a large, strong box for storage or transport
