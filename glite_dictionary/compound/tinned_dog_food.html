tinned dog food

1. NOUN

/ tɪnd dɔɡ fuːd / (AmE), / tɪnd dɒɡ fuːd / (BrE)
dog food preserved and packaged in a tin can; canned food for dogs
dog food in a can

Examples

She always keeps a tinned dog food supply ready.
The shelter provided tinned dog food for the animals.
He opened a tinned dog food for his pet.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dry dog food - dog food that is dry and not canned
  • Synonym: canned food - food preserved and sold in a can

Etymology

From 'tinned', meaning preserved or packaged in a tin can, and 'dog food'.
