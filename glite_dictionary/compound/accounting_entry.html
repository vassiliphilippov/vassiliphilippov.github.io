accounting entry

1. NOUN

/ əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈɛntri / (AmE), / əˈkaʊntɪŋ ˈɛntri / (BrE)
a record of a financial transaction in the books of accounts
record of a financial transaction

Examples

Each accounting entry must have both a debit and a credit side for the books to balance.
The auditor reviewed every accounting entry made during the fiscal year.
Incorrect accounting entries can lead to significant financial reporting errors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journal entry - an entry recording a transaction in accounting journals
