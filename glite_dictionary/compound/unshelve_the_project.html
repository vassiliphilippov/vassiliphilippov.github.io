to unshelve the project

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈʃɛlv ðə ˈprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛlv ðə ˈprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
to revive or resume work on a project that was previously paused, postponed, or abandoned
resume a previously paused project

Examples

After several years on hold, the committee decided to unshelve the project and move forward with development.
The company may unshelve the project if market conditions improve next year.
We are excited to finally unshelve the project that has been gathering dust for so long.

Synset Examples

They plan to unshelve the project after the funding comes through.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shelve the project - to postpone or suspend work on a project
  • Synonym: to revive - to bring something back into use or attention
