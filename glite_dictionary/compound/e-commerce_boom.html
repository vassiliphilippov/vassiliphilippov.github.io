e-commerce boom

1. NOUN

/ ˌiː ˈkɑː.mɚs buːm / (AmE), / ˌiː ˈkɒ.mɜːs buːm / (BrE)
a period of rapid growth and expansion in online shopping and internet-based commercial activities
rapid growth of online shopping

Examples

The e-commerce boom has transformed the way businesses reach and serve their customers.
Analysts credit the e-commerce boom for the surge in online retail sales over the past decade.
During the pandemic, the e-commerce boom accelerated as more people turned to online shopping.

Synset Examples

The e-commerce boom changed how we shop forever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: e-commerce - buying and selling of goods or services via the internet
