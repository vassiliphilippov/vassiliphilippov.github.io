price reduction

1. NOUN

/ praɪs rɪˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / praɪs rɪˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of lowering the selling price of goods or services
a decrease in selling price

Examples

The store announced a price reduction on all electronic items this weekend.
After the price reduction, sales of the new smartphone increased significantly.
Customers are always looking for a price reduction before making a big purchase.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual price of something
