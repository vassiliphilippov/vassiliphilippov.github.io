cup of java

1. NOUN

/ kʌp əv ˈdʒɑːvə / (AmE), / kʌp əv ˈdʒɑːvə / (BrE)
a cup of coffee; an informal way to refer to a serving of coffee
informal term for a cup of coffee
informal american

Examples

He grabbed a cup of java before heading to work.
Would you like a cup of java while we chat?
Nothing wakes me up like a hot cup of java in the morning.

Synset Examples

I need a cup of java to start my day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - a hot drink made from roasted and ground coffee beans

Etymology

'Java' as slang for coffee originates from the Indonesian island of Java, a major coffee producer.
