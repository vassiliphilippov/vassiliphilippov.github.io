redbird cactus

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌbɝd ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbɜːd ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A low tropical American shrub known for its powerful emetic properties, often found in Central and South America.
tropical American shrub with emetic properties

Examples

The redbird cactus is valued in traditional medicine for its strong emetic qualities.
Gardeners should be cautious when handling the redbird cactus due to its toxicity.
Many South American cultures have used the redbird cactus in herbal remedies for centuries.
