tape recorder

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪp rɪˌkɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈteɪp rɪˌkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device used for recording and playing back sound by means of magnetic tape
device for recording/playback on magnetic tape

Examples

She carefully set up the tape recorder to capture the entire interview.
My father still has an old tape recorder in his study filled with classic jazz recordings.
The meeting was documented with a tape recorder so that no details would be missed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cassette recorder - a tape recorder that uses cassette tapes instead of open reels
