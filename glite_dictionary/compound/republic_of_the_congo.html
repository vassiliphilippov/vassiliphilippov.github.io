Republic of the Congo

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈkɑ̃ɡoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a country in west-central Africa, with Brazzaville as its capital; achieved independence from France in 1960
country in west-central Africa

Examples

The Republic of the Congo is known for its rich rainforests and diverse wildlife.
After independence, the Republic of the Congo established diplomatic relations with many countries.
Brazzaville, the capital city of the Republic of the Congo, sits on the banks of the Congo River.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Democratic Republic of the Congo - adjacent country in central Africa, sometimes confused due to similar names
