earless lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrləs ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈɪəlɪs ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a slender lizard found in the plains of the western United States and Mexico, notable for lacking external ear openings
slender lizard without external ear openings

Examples

The earless lizard is well adapted to the sandy soils of the southwestern deserts.
Unlike other lizards, the earless lizard lacks external ear openings.
Researchers observed several earless lizards basking on rocks in the midday sun.
