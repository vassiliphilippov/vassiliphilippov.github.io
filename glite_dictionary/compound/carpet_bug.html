carpet bug

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pət bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt bʌɡ / (BrE)
a small beetle, especially the larvae of certain species, which are household pests feeding on woolen fabrics
small beetle pest attacking woolens

Examples

We discovered a carpet bug infestation in the guest room last spring.
The larvae of the carpet bug can cause significant damage to wool sweaters and rugs.
A pest control expert identified the small insects as carpet bugs feeding on our carpets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet beetle - a beetle whose larvae eat natural fibers in carpets and clothing
