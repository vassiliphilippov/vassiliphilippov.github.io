Belgian shepherd

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈʃɛp.ərd / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.dʒən ˈʃɛp.əd / (BrE)
A hardy breed of working dog developed in Belgium for herding sheep, known for intelligence and versatility.
breed of herding dog from Belgium

Examples

The Belgian shepherd is admired for its intelligence and obedience.
Police teams often use a Belgian shepherd for tracking and protection work.
Farmers relied on their loyal Belgian shepherds to herd sheep across the fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: German shepherd - a large breed of working dog from Germany
  • Synonym: collie - a breed of herding dog known for intelligence
