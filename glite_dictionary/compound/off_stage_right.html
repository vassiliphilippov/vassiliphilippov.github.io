off stage right

1. ADV

/ ˌɔf steɪdʒ ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ˌɒf steɪdʒ ˈraɪt / (BrE)
To or located on the area to the right side of the stage, out of the view of the audience.
to the right side off the stage

Examples

The lead actor waited off stage right until her cue to enter.
You could hear someone laughing off stage right during the performance.
The director signaled for the dancer to exit off stage right.

Synset Examples

The props were moved off stage right to clear the space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage right - the area of the stage to the actor's right
  • Antonym: off stage left - to the left side of the stage, out of audience view
