Battle of Bataan

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæt̬l əv bəˈtæn / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv bəˈtæn / (BrE)
a major military engagement in World War II (1942), where Japanese forces besieged and defeated American and Filipino troops on the Bataan Peninsula in the Philippines
World War II battle in the Philippines (1942)

Examples

The Battle of Bataan was one of the most significant early defeats for Allied forces in the Pacific.
Many soldiers suffered during the grueling Battle of Bataan before surrendering to Japanese forces in 1942.
Historians often discuss the strategic importance of the Battle of Bataan in the context of World War II.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Battle of Corregidor - subsequent World War II battle on a nearby island fortress in the Philippines
  • Synonym: Battle of Midway - pivotal naval battle in the Pacific during WWII
