square inch

1. NOUN

/ skwɛr ɪntʃ / (AmE), / skweə ɪntʃ / (BrE)
a unit of area measurement equal to the area of a square with sides one inch long
unit of area (one inch by one inch)

Examples

The fabric sample measures exactly one square inch in size.
Please mark off a square inch on the blueprint for reference.
He calculated the pressure in pounds per square inch using the formula.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: square foot - a unit of area equal to a square with sides one foot long
  • Degree Lower: square centimeter - a unit of area equal to a square with sides one centimeter long
