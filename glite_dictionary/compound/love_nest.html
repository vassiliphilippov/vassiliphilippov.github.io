love nest

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌv nɛst / (AmE), / ˈlʌv nɛst / (BrE)
a cozy or private place where a couple spend romantic time together, often as their home or a getaway
a romantic home or retreat for couples

Examples

They decorated their love nest with fairy lights and fresh flowers.
After a long week, they escaped to their love nest in the countryside.
Rumors spread about the celebrity couple's secret love nest by the sea.

Synset Examples

They turned their apartment into a cozy love nest.
Their love nest was filled with candles and soft music.
The couple enjoyed a weekend getaway at their love nest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hideaway - a place used as a retreat or hiding place, often for privacy
  • Synonym: honeymoon suite - a hotel room designed for newlyweds or romantic couples
