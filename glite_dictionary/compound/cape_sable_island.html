Cape Sable Island

1. PROPN

/ keɪp ˈseɪbəl ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / keɪp ˈseɪbəl ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
a promontory located at the southern tip of Nova Scotia, Canada, known for its historical significance and wild bird habitats
promontory at southern tip of Nova Scotia, Canada

Examples

Many migratory birds stop at Cape Sable Island on their journey north every spring.
Tourists often visit Cape Sable Island to enjoy its scenic coastal views and rich maritime history.
Storms off the coast of Cape Sable Island can be powerful during the winter months.
