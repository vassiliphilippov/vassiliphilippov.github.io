second best

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛk.ənd bɛst / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd best / (BrE)
the person or team that finishes in second place in a competition.
the one who finishes second

Examples

Although he tried his hardest, he had to settle for second best in the race.
She was disappointed to finish second best, but proud of her performance nonetheless.
Even as second best, he received a silver medal for his efforts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winner - the person who comes first in a competition
  • Synonym: runner-up - a person who finishes second in a competition
