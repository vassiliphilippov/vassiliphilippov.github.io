genus Ameiurus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs əˈmaɪərəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈmaɪərəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Ameiuridae, comprising the bullhead catfishes commonly found in North America.
type genus of bullhead catfishes

Examples

The genus Ameiurus includes several species of bullhead catfish found across North America.
Researchers collected data on different members of the genus Ameiurus to study their habitats.
The genus Ameiurus is distinguished by its rounded tail and stout body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullhead catfish - a common name for catfish in the genus Ameiurus

Etymology

From New Latin 'Ameiurus', denoting the genus of certain North American freshwater catfishes.
