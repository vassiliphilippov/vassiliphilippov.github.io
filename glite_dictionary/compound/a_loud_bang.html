a loud bang

1. NOUN

/ ə laʊd bæŋ / (AmE), / ə laʊd baŋ / (BrE)
a sudden, sharp, and very loud noise, often caused by something exploding or crashing
sudden, very loud noise

Examples

Everyone jumped when a loud bang echoed through the hallway.
We were startled by a loud bang coming from the garage.
Just as I opened the door, a loud bang made me freeze in place.

Synset Examples

She heard a loud bang outside her window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise
