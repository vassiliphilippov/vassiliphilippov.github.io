American dog violet

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən dɔɡ ˈvaɪ.əl.ət / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən dɒɡ ˈvaɪ.əl.ət / (BrE)
a violet of eastern North America with pale violet to white flowers
a North American violet with pale flowers

Examples

We found an American dog violet blooming near the edge of the forest trail.
The American dog violet is known for its delicate pale petals and heart-shaped leaves.
Gardeners often seek the American dog violet because it thrives in shaded woodland gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - any small plant of the genus Viola with purple, blue, or white flowers
