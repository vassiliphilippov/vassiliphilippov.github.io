shrink ray

1. NOUN

/ ʃrɪŋk reɪ / (AmE), / ʃrɪŋk reɪ / (BrE)
a fictional device used to make objects or people smaller in size
fictional size-reducing device

Examples

In the film, the villain used a shrink ray to reduce the size of the city.
She joked that her laundry must have gone through a shrink ray because her clothes no longer fit.
The concept of a shrink ray often appears in cartoons and science fiction stories.

Synset Examples

I swear my grocery shrink ray has made my snacks disappear.
It's like a grocery shrink ray took half my items!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enlarger ray - a fictional device that makes things larger
