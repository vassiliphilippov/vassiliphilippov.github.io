figure loom

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ ˌluːm / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə ˌluːm / (BrE)
a loom designed for weaving fabrics with patterns or figures
loom for weaving patterned fabrics

Examples

The textile museum features a restored figure loom used in the 19th century.
Manufacturers often rely on a figure loom to produce intricate brocade fabrics.
Learning to operate a figure loom requires both technical skill and artistic sense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jacquard loom - a loom with an automatic patterning mechanism for complex designs
