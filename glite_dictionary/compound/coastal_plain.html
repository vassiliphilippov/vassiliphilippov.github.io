coastal plain

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊstl pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstl pleɪn / (BrE)
an area of flat, low-lying land adjacent to a sea coast
low, flat land near a coast

Examples

The Atlantic coastal plain stretches from New Jersey to Florida.
Rice is often grown on the fertile coastal plain of Vietnam.
Wildlife thrives on the coastal plain due to its rich wetlands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - a large area of flat land with few trees
