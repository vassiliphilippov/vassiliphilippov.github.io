calendar week

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.ən.dɚ ˌwiːk / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ən.də ˌwiːk / (BrE)
A period of seven consecutive days as defined by the calendar, usually from Sunday to Saturday or from Monday to Sunday.
seven-day period in the calendar

Examples

The project must be finished by the end of the current calendar week.
Most companies start their calendar week on a Monday.
He worked overtime for three calendar weeks in a row.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: week - a period of seven consecutive days
