French knot

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ nɑt / (AmE), / frɛntʃ nɒt / (BrE)
an embroidery stitch in which the thread is wound around the needle and stitched into the fabric to form a small, raised knot
embroidery stitch forming a small knot

Examples

To create a textured center for the flower, use a French knot with yellow thread.
Her embroidered handkerchief was adorned with delicate French knots along the border.
The instructor demonstrated how to make a perfect French knot during the embroidery class.
