garbage collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect and dispose of refuse from homes and businesses
person who collects and disposes of refuse

Examples

The garbage collector picked up the bins early in the morning.
Many cities depend on garbage collectors to keep their streets clean.
As a child, he admired the hard work of the local garbage collector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refuse collector - person whose job is to collect refuse
  • Synonym: dustman - person who collects household rubbish (British English)
