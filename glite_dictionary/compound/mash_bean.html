mash bean

1. NOUN

/ mæʃ biːn / (AmE), / mæʃ biːn / (BrE)
the seed of the mung bean plant, used as food
seed of the mung bean plant (food)

Examples

High in protein, mash bean is a staple ingredient in many Asian dishes.
Soaked mash bean makes a nutritious base for soups and salads.
The market sells dried mash bean alongside lentils and chickpeas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mung bean - the seed or plant of Vigna radiata, an Asian legume used as food
