virgin vault

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɝːdʒɪn vɔlt / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn vɔːlt / (BrE)
a storage space or compartment, especially for valuables, that has not been accessed, used, or explored before
untouched or unexplored storage area

Examples

The archaeologist was overjoyed to find a virgin vault beneath the ruins.
Rumors spread quickly about the virgin vault rumored to contain priceless jewels.
They used cutting-edge equipment to survey the virgin vault before opening it.

Synset Examples

He discovered a virgin vault filled with ancient artifacts.
Accessing the virgin vault required a special key.
The treasure was hidden within a virgin vault deep underground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vault - a secure room or compartment for storing valuables
