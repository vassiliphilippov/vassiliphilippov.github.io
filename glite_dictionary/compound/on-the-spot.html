on-the-spot

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑn ðə ˈspɑt / (AmE), / ˌɒn ðə ˈspɒt / (BrE)
happening or done at the place where an event is occurring or has occurred; at the scene of action
at the scene of action

Examples

The police conducted an on-the-spot investigation after the incident was reported.
She made an on-the-spot decision to help the injured man.
Our journalist provided on-the-spot coverage of the unfolding events.

Synset Examples

an on-the-spot reporter
on-the-spot inspections
an on-the scene newscast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediate - done at once or without delay
