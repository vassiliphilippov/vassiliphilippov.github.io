fantastic job

1. NOUN

/ fænˈtæstɪk dʒɑb / (AmE), / fanˈtastɪk dʒɒb / (BrE)
an excellent or very impressive performance or piece of work
excellent or impressive performance

Examples

She did a fantastic job organizing the conference, and everyone praised her efforts.
The chef did a fantastic job preparing the meal for all the guests.
You've done a fantastic job with your presentation; it was both clear and engaging.

Synset Examples

The pet groomer did a fantastic job on my dog.
You all did a fantastic job on the project!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent job - performance or work done extremely well; synonymic phrase
  • Antonym: terrible job - very poor or unsatisfactory performance; antonymic phrase
