baby bed

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi bɛd / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi bɛd / (BrE)
a small bed with high sides, designed to keep a baby safe and prevent falling out
small bed for babies with sides

Examples

We assembled the baby bed in the nursery before our daughter was born.
The store sells a variety of baby beds with different designs and features.
He gently placed his son in the baby bed and turned on the mobile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crib - a small bed with high sides for a baby or young child
  • Synonym: cot - (British) a small bed with high sides for a baby
