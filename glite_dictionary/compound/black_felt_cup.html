black felt cup

1. NOUN

/ blæk fɛlt kʌp / (AmE), / blæk fɛlt kʌp / (BrE)
a small black cup-shaped fungus of the family Sarcosomataceae, scientifically known as Plectania nannfeldtii.
a cup-shaped black fungus

Examples

The black felt cup is often found in damp forests during early spring.
Mycologists are excited to find a rare black felt cup growing on decaying wood.
He photographed a cluster of black felt cups along the shaded forest path.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Plectania nannfeldtii - scientific name for black felt cup fungus
