purple-flowering raspberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrpl ˌflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˌflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ ˈræzˌbəri / (BrE)
a shrubby plant of eastern North America, Rubus odoratus, known for its showy rose to purplish flowers and red or orange thimble-shaped fruit
North American shrub with purple flowers and thimble-shaped fruit

Examples

The purple-flowering raspberry adds a splash of color to forest edges in early summer.
Gardeners appreciate the purple-flowering raspberry for both its ornamental blooms and edible fruit.
Unlike other raspberries, the purple-flowering raspberry thrives in shady woodland settings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rubus odoratus - the scientific name for purple-flowering raspberry
  • Degree Higher: raspberry - a prickly shrub that bears edible red fruit
