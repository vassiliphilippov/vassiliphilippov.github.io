Carlos the Jackal

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑrloʊs ðə ˈdʒækəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːlɒs ðə ˈdʒækəl / (BrE)
the nickname of Ilich Ramírez Sánchez (born 1949), a Venezuelan militant and international terrorist, active in the late 20th century
notorious Venezuelan militant (born 1949)

Examples

The international authorities finally captured Carlos the Jackal after years of pursuit.
Many documentaries have explored the activities of Carlos the Jackal throughout the 1970s and 1980s.
Few figures in modern history are as infamous as Carlos the Jackal for global terrorism.

Etymology

“Jackal” refers to his notoriety and was popularized by the media following the novel and film 'The Day of the Jackal'.
