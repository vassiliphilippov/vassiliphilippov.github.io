central office

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntrəl ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
the main office that acts as the administrative center of an organization or company
main administrative center of an organization

Examples

All important decisions are made at the central office.
The central office coordinates activities among all regional branches.
He called the central office to ask about his job application.

Synset Examples

many companies have their headquarters in New York

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - main office or center of operations of an organization
