Salafist Group for Call and Combat

1. PROPN

/ sæˈlæfɪst ɡruːp fəɹ kɔl ənd kəmˈbæt / (AmE), / səˈlafɪst ɡruːp fə kɔːl ənd kəmˈbat / (BrE)
an Algerian extremist Islamist organization that split from the Armed Islamic Group, known for seeking to overthrow the Algerian government and supporting al-Qaeda activities in Europe and North Africa
Algerian Islamist extremist group

Examples

The Salafist Group for Call and Combat was responsible for several high-profile attacks in Algeria.
International authorities are concerned about the recruitment activities of the Salafist Group for Call and Combat across North Africa.
The alliance between the Salafist Group for Call and Combat and al-Qaeda has increased regional instability.
