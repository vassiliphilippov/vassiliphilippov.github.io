constant admiration

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnstənt ædˌmɪˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt ædˌmɪəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an ongoing and unceasing need for praise and approval from others
ongoing need for praise and approval

Examples

Being a celebrity often comes with the expectation of constant admiration from fans.
His ego demanded constant admiration, leaving little room for genuine friendships.
A leader who seeks constant admiration may struggle to accept constructive criticism.

Synset Examples

Being a narcissist, he craves constant admiration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admiration - a feeling of respect and approval
