sugar bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːɡɚ bɑːm / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə bɒm / (BrE)
A food or drink containing a very high amount of sugar, often considered excessively sweet.
high-sugar food or drink
informal

Examples

My breakfast cereal is a total sugar bomb, but I love how sweet it is.
That milkshake was such a sugar bomb that I couldn't finish it.
Kids often crave sugar bombs like candy bars and frosted pastries.

Synset Examples

That soda is a real sugar bomb, loaded with calories.
I can't resist this sugar bomb of a cupcake.
The dessert was a sugar bomb, too sweet for me.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sweet - having a taste similar to sugar; not bitter or salty
  • Degree Higher: sickly sweet - so sweet as to be unpleasant or overwhelming
