art director

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / ɑːt dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
the person responsible for the visual aspects of a theatrical, film, or television production, including scenery, costumes, and lighting
person overseeing visual aspects of productions

Examples

The art director collaborated closely with the costume designer to create a striking stage presence.
Our film's art director transformed the set into a realistic New York City street.
The award went to the art director for outstanding achievement in theatrical design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: production designer - person responsible for the overall visual concept of a film or theater production
