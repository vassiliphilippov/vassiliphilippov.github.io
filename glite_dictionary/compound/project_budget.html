project budget

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑdʒɛkt ˈbʌdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɒdʒɛkt ˈbʌdʒɪt / (BrE)
an estimate of the total costs and resources needed for a particular project, typically detailing allocations for different expenses
financial plan for a specific project

Examples

The team submitted the project budget to management for approval before work began.
Unexpected expenses forced us to revise the project budget halfway through construction.
Keeping track of every cost helped the organization stay within the project budget.

Synset Examples

The project budget is M$ for the entire year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: budget - an estimate of income and expenditure for a set period of time
