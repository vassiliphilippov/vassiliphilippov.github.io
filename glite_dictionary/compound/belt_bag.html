belt bag

1. NOUN

/ bɛlt bæɡ / (AmE), / bɛlt bæɡ / (BrE)
a small pouch with a zipper that is attached to a belt and worn around the waist for carrying personal items
small pouch worn on a belt

Examples

She kept her phone and keys in her belt bag while jogging in the park.
The travel guide recommended wearing a belt bag to keep valuables secure.
He bought a new belt bag for the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fanny pack - a similar pouch worn around the waist
