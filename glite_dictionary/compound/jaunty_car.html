jaunty car

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑnti kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːnti kɑː / (BrE)
an open, two-wheeled horse-drawn cart, formerly widely used in Ireland, with seats facing outward
Irish two-wheeled horse-drawn cart

Examples

The tourists enjoyed a ride in a jaunty car through the Irish countryside.
In old photographs, you can see families traveling together by jaunty car.
The museum displays a restored jaunty car from the late nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jaunting car - similar Irish two-wheeled cart with side-facing seats
