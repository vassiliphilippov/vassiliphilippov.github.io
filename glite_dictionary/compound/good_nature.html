good nature

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a cheerful and helpful disposition; a tendency to be pleasant and obliging toward others
cheerful and obliging disposition

Examples

Her good nature made her popular with everyone at the office.
People often took advantage of his good nature and willingness to help.
Even under stress, her good nature never faltered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-natured - having or showing a cheerful, pleasant disposition
  • Antonym: ill nature - an unpleasant or unkind disposition
