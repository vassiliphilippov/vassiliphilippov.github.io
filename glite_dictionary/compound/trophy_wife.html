trophy wife

1. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊ.fi waɪf / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊ.fi waɪf / (BrE)
a young and attractive wife, typically of an affluent older man, seen as a status symbol rather than as a partner in a traditional marriage.
attractive young wife of a wealthy older man

Examples

At the gala, many whispered about the CEO and his trophy wife attending together.
He faced criticism for marrying a trophy wife half his age after his divorce.
Although some call her a trophy wife, she manages several charitable foundations herself.

Synset Examples

his trophy wife was an asset to his business

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold digger - someone who forms relationships to extract money or gifts

Etymology

From 'trophy' (prize or symbol of achievement) + 'wife,' first popularized in the late 20th century.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊ.fi waɪf / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊ.fi waɪf / (BrE)
an attractive younger wife regarded primarily as a status symbol for her usually older, wealthy husband
attractive wife seen as a status symbol

Examples

Some people assumed she was just a trophy wife, but she runs her own business.
He was criticized for divorcing his first wife and marrying a trophy wife half his age.
The gossip magazines often speculate about which celebrities have a trophy wife.

Synset Examples

Her friends called her a trophy wife due to her beauty.
He married a trophy wife, showcasing his success.
The media loves to highlight the lives of trophy wives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arm candy - an attractive companion who is mainly for display
