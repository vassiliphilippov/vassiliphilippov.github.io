monkey man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki mæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki mæn / (BrE)
A person who acts in a playful, silly, or mischievous way.
playful or silly person
informal

Examples

Everyone at the party knew Tom as the monkey man because of his constant jokes and pranks.
As a child, she was quite the monkey man, always climbing trees and making funny faces.
His friends called him monkey man due to his energetic and silly behavior.

Synset Examples

They dubbed him the monkey man for his antics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clown - a person who behaves in a comical or foolish way
