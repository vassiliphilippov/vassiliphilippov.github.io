men's furnishings

1. NOUN

/ mɛnz ˈfɝːnɪʃɪŋz / (AmE), / mɛnz ˈfɜːnɪʃɪŋz / (BrE)
items such as shirts, ties, socks, and other accessories for men's dress, especially as sold by a haberdasher
men's clothing accessories

Examples

The store has a wide selection of men's furnishings, including silk ties and cufflinks.
He opened his own shop specializing in quality men's furnishings on Main Street.
Traditional men's furnishings like handkerchiefs have become less common in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haberdashery - items and shop dealing with men's clothing accessories
