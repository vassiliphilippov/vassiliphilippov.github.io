slebs and influencers

1. NOUN

/ slɛbz ənd ɪnˈfluənsərz / (AmE), / slɛbz ənd ɪnˈfluənsəz / (BrE)
A colloquial phrase referring to celebrities and social media personalities, often used together to denote well-known figures in entertainment and online spaces.
celebrities and social media personalities
informal

Examples

The guest list for the launch included several slebs and influencers from the fashion world.
In today's media, brands often collaborate with slebs and influencers to reach a broader audience.
Rumors about the event spread quickly after it was attended by numerous slebs and influencers.

Synset Examples

The party was full of slebs and influencers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment
  • Synonym: influencer - a person who influences others, especially via social media

Etymology

‘Slebs’ is an informal shortening of ‘celebrities’; ‘influencers’ refers to people with influence on social media.
