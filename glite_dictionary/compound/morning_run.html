morning run

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔrnɪŋ rʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ rʌn / (BrE)
a run taken in the morning, typically for exercise or fitness.
run taken in the morning

Examples

She never skips her morning run, even on weekends.
A short morning run along the river helps me clear my mind before work.
He met his neighbor during his daily morning run through the park.

Synset Examples

She felt awake and energized after the morning run.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jog - to run at a steady, gentle pace for exercise
  • Antonym: evening run - a run taken in the evening, typically for exercise
