order Aristolochiales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ˌærɪstəˌlɒkiaˈliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌærɪstəˌlɒkɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of plants characterized by a tubular, petal-like perianth and an inferior ovary
an order of flowering plants
medical

Examples

The order Aristolochiales includes several species known for their unique floral structures.
Researchers studied the evolutionary relationships within the order Aristolochiales.
Many medicinal plants belong to the order Aristolochiales.

Etymology

From Neo-Latin Aristolochia (the type genus) + -ales (order suffix in taxonomy).
