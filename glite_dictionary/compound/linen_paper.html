linen paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪnən ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈlɪnɪn ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
A high-quality paper made from linen fibers or given a texture resembling linen, often used for formal documents or stationery.
high-quality paper with linen fibers or finish

Examples

The wedding invitations were printed on elegant linen paper to give them a sophisticated touch.
He chose linen paper for his résumé because of its professional appearance and texture.
The artist preferred sketching on linen paper for its durability and unique feel.
