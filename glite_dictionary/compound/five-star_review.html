five-star review

1. NOUN

/ faɪv stɑr rɪˈvju / (AmE), / faɪv stɑː rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
an assessment or rating indicating the highest possible level of quality or approval
top-quality rating or review

Examples

The restaurant received a five-star review from a respected food critic.
Her novel's first five-star review appeared in a leading magazine last week.
Many customers rely on five-star reviews before making a purchase online.

Synset Examples

Her performance earned her a five-star review.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: five-star - indicating the highest quality level
