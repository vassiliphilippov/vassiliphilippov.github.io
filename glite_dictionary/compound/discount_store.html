discount store

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪskaʊnt stɔɹ / (AmE), / ˈdɪskaʊnt stɔː / (BrE)
A retail store that sells goods at prices lower than those typically charged by traditional retail outlets.
shop selling goods at reduced prices

Examples

Many people shop at the local discount store to save money on groceries and household items.
The new discount store offers great deals on electronics and clothing.
After comparing prices, she realized the discount store had the best bargains in town.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department store - a large store stocking many varieties of goods in different departments
