swinging post

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪŋɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
the gatepost to which the gate is attached and about which it swings open or closed
gatepost supporting the gate for swinging

Examples

The heavy wooden swinging post needed replacing after decades of use.
Make sure the swinging post is set deep enough to support the weight of the gate.
Without a sturdy swinging post, the gate will not function properly.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: latch post - the gatepost on which a gate fastens
