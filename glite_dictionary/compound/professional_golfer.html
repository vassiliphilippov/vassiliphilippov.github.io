professional golfer

1. NOUN

/ prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈɡɑlfər / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈɡɒlfə / (BrE)
A person who earns a living by playing golf or teaching the game professionally.
person who plays golf as a profession

Examples

After years of amateur tournaments, she finally became a professional golfer on the tour.
Being a professional golfer requires skill, discipline, and the ability to handle pressure.
Many professional golfers offer private coaching sessions to beginners.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur golfer - someone who plays golf for recreation, not for a living
