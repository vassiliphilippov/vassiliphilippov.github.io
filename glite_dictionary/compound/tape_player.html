tape player

1. NOUN

/ teɪp ˈpleɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / teɪp ˈpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
an electronic device used to play audio from magnetic tapes, such as cassettes or reels
device for playing audio tapes

Examples

He found an old tape player in his attic and played his childhood mixtapes.
Many cars from the 1980s and 1990s were equipped with a tape player.
The band released their album on cassette, so you'll need a tape player to listen to it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cassette player - an electronic device for playing compact audio cassettes
  • Synonym: tape recorder - a device that records and plays back sound using magnetic tape
