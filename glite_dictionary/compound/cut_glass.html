cut glass

1. NOUN

/ kʌt ɡlæs / (AmE), / kʌt ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
glass intricately decorated by cutting or grinding facets into its surface, often used for ornamental purposes
glass with decorative cut or ground facets

Examples

The chandelier sparkled brilliantly, its cut glass pendants scattering light across the room.
She inherited a collection of antique cut glass vases from her grandmother.
The craftsman demonstrated how to shape and polish cut glass to achieve intricate designs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystal - high-quality, clear, and often cut glass used for tableware and ornaments
