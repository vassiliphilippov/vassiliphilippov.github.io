bicycle-built-for-two

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪsɪkl bɪlt fər tuː / (AmE), / ˈbaɪsɪkl bɪlt fə tuː / (BrE)
a bicycle designed for two riders, with two seats and two sets of pedals, one behind the other
bicycle for two riders

Examples

They rented a bicycle-built-for-two and rode around the park together.
A bicycle-built-for-two requires both riders to pedal in sync for the best experience.
I'd always wanted to try riding a bicycle-built-for-two on a summer afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tandem - a bicycle with two seats and two sets of pedals, one behind the other
