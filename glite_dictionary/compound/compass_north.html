compass north

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌm.pəs nɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pəs nɔːθ / (BrE)
the direction indicated by the north-pointing end of a magnetic compass, which aligns with the Earth's magnetic north
direction indicated by a compass needle

Examples

The hikers adjusted their route to head toward compass north rather than true north.
In navigation, compass north can differ from true north due to magnetic declination.
When you use a standard compass, the needle always aligns with compass north.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetic north - the direction toward the Earth's magnetic north pole
