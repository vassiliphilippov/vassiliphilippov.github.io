factory price

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæk.tɚ.i praɪs / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tər.i praɪs / (BrE)
the price at which goods are sold by the manufacturer, not including transport or additional costs
manufacturer's sale price without extra costs

Examples

The company offered the new products at factory price for a limited time.
Buying directly from the source allowed customers to pay only the factory price.
The factory price does not include shipping or handling fees.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: retail price - the price at which goods are sold to the public
