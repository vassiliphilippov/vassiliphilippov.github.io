bit of a thembo

1. NOUN

/ bɪt əv ə ˈθɛmboʊ / (AmE), / bɪt əv ə ˈθɛmbəʊ / (BrE)
An attractive man who is not very intelligent.
attractive but unintelligent man (slang)
slang

Examples

Everyone thinks Jake is a bit of a thembo because of his looks and goofy comments.
He may look like a bit of a thembo, but he has a kind heart.
She laughed and called him a bit of a thembo after he forgot his keys again.

Synset Examples

He’s a bit of a thembo, always trying to impress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: himbo - attractive but unintelligent man (slang)
  • Synonym: bimbo - attractive but unintelligent person (often woman)

Etymology

Blend of 'them' (gender-neutral pronoun) and 'bimbo', modeled after 'himbo'.
