rug beater

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡ ˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡ ˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a handheld implement with a wire or cane frame used for beating dust and dirt out of carpets or rugs
hand tool for beating dust from carpets

Examples

Before vacuum cleaners were common, people used a rug beater to clean their carpets.
She took the old rug beater off the hook and went outside to clean the mat.
The antique shop had a collection of vintage rug beaters hanging on the wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet - a thick woven floor covering
  • Synonym: broom - a brush with a long handle for sweeping
