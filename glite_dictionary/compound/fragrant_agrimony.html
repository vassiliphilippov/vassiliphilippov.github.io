fragrant agrimony

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt əˈɡrɪməni / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt əˈɡrɪməni / (BrE)
a fragrant European perennial herb (Agrimonia procera) typically found at woodland margins on moist soils
a fragrant European perennial herb

Examples

The woodland was dotted with fragrant agrimony along the damp edges of the path.
Botanists identified the plant as fragrant agrimony, known for its pleasant aroma.
We found a patch of fragrant agrimony growing beside the forest stream.
