wellspring of joy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌsprɪŋ əv dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɛl.sprɪŋ əv dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
a source or origin that continually produces happiness or pleasure
source of happiness or pleasure

Examples

Her children are a wellspring of joy that brightens her everyday life.
Traveling to new places is often a wellspring of joy for adventurous people.
The puppy became a wellspring of joy during their difficult times.

Synset Examples

Their friendship is a wellspring of joy in my life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fount of joy - a source of happiness or pleasure

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'wellspring' (from Old English 'wella' and 'springan', meaning a natural source of water).
