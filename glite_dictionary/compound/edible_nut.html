edible nut

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdəbəl nʌt / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl̩ nʌt / (BrE)
a hard-shelled seed with an edible kernel inside, enclosed by a woody or leathery shell
hard-shelled edible seed

Examples

Almonds are a popular type of edible nut often used in baking and snacks.
Some people are allergic to more than one kind of edible nut.
The museum displayed various shells of ancient edible nuts found at archaeological sites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nut - a dry fruit consisting of a hard shell enclosing a seed
