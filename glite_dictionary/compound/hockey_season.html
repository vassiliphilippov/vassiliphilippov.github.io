hockey season

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑki ˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˌsiːzən / (BrE)
The period of each year when hockey games are officially played in a league or competition.
period when hockey is played

Examples

Tickets for the new hockey season sold out within days.
The team hopes to win more games this hockey season than last year.
Every hockey season attracts thousands of devoted fans to the stadium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: season - a period of the year when a particular activity is performed
