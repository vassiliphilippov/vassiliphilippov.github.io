tap dance performance

1. NOUN

/ tæp dæns pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / tæp dɑːns pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
a dance performance featuring rhythmic tapping sounds made with the feet, especially using special shoes
a performance of tap dancing

Examples

The theater was filled with excitement during the tap dance performance.
She prepared for months for her first tap dance performance on stage.
Every year, the festival features a dazzling tap dance performance by local students.

Synset Examples

He enjoys watching tap dance performances on TV.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tap dancing - style of dance using rhythmic tapping sounds
