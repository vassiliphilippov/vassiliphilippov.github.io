anonymous benefactor

1. NOUN

/ əˈnɑː.nə.məs bəˈnɛf.æk.tər / (AmE), / əˈnɒn.ɪ.məs bɪˈnɛf.æk.tə / (BrE)
a person who donates money or resources without revealing their identity
unidentified donor of aid or money

Examples

The scholarships were made possible by an anonymous benefactor who wished to help local students.
After the museum received a large gift from an anonymous benefactor, it was able to expand its collection.
The hospital thanked the anonymous benefactor for the generous donation that funded new equipment.

Synset Examples

The donation came from an anonymous benefactor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or other help to a person or cause
