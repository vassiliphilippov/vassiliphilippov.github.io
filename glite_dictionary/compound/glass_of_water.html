glass of water

1. NOUN

/ ɡlæs əv ˈwɔːtɚ / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs əv ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
a drinking glass that is filled with water, usually for drinking
drinking glass containing water

Examples

Could you bring me a glass of water with my meal?
After her run, she drank a cold glass of water to rehydrate.
He accidentally knocked over his glass of water onto the floor.

Synset Examples

Please pour me a glass of water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glass of juice - a drinking glass filled with juice
  • Synonym: cup of water - a cup containing water
