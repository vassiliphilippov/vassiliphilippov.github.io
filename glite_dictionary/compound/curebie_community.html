curebie community

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjʊrbi kəˌmjunəti / (AmE), / ˈkjʊəbi kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group or community of people who promote or are interested in alternative health remedies, often outside mainstream medical practice
group focused on alternative health remedies

Examples

The curebie community often gathers at wellness fairs to exchange ideas about nontraditional therapies.
Social media has enabled the curebie community to grow and connect people with similar interests from around the world.
Some critics argue that the curebie community sometimes promotes unproven or potentially dangerous health practices.

Synset Examples

The curebie community gathered to share their favorite treatments.
