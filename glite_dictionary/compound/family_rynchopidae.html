family Rynchopidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli raɪnˈkɒpɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli rɪnˈkɒpɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of birds, coextensive with the genus Rynchops, known as skimmers.
a family of skimmer birds
medical

Examples

The family Rynchopidae consists solely of the skimmers, which are known for their unique feeding behavior.
Ornithologists have long studied the migration patterns within the family Rynchopidae.
Members of the family Rynchopidae are easily recognized by their distinctive elongated lower mandibles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skimmer - a bird of the genus Rynchops, member of family Rynchopidae

Etymology

From New Latin *Rynchopidae*, based on the genus *Rynchops* and the suffix *-idae* used for animal family names.
