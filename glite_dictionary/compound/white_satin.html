white satin

1. NOUN

/ waɪt ˈsæt̬.ən / (AmE), / waɪt ˈsæt.ɪn / (BrE)
a glossy, smooth fabric with a shiny surface, in a pure white color, often used for elegant clothing or decorations
glossy white fabric

Examples

She wore a stunning dress made of white satin.
The ribbons were tied in neat bows of white satin.
A pair of white satin shoes completed her bridal ensemble.

Synset Examples
