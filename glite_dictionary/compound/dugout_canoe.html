dugout canoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌɡaʊt kəˈnu / (AmE), / ˈdʌɡaʊt kəˈnuː / (BrE)
a canoe made from a large log that has been hollowed out and shaped
canoe made by hollowing out a log

Examples

The villagers traveled down the river in a dugout canoe to reach the market.
She admired the craftsmanship of the dugout canoe on display at the museum.
Building a dugout canoe requires patience and skilled hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canoe - a small, slender boat pointed at both ends and propelled by paddles
