People against Gangsterism and Drugs

1. PROPN

/ ˈpipəl əˈɡɛnst ˈɡæŋstərɪzəm ənd drʌɡz / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl əˈɡenst ˈɡæŋstəˌrɪzəm ənd drʌɡz / (BrE)
A South African organization formed in 1996 to oppose drug lords and gangsterism, later accused of vigilante activities and linked to Islamic extremist groups.
vigilante group in South Africa opposing drugs and gangs

Examples

The South African government took measures after People against Gangsterism and Drugs began its campaign against local crime.
Many community members supported People against Gangsterism and Drugs in their early efforts to reduce drug-related violence.
Reports linked People against Gangsterism and Drugs to a series of bombings in Cape Town.
