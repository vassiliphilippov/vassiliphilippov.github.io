blended whisky

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (BrE)
a whisky made by mixing two or more different whiskies or by combining whisky with neutral spirits
whisky made from a blend of whiskies or spirits

Examples

He prefers blended whisky over single malt because of its smoother taste.
The bar stocked several varieties of blended whisky from Scotland and Ireland.
A good blended whisky often combines the flavors of different distilleries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single malt whisky - whisky made from malted barley at a single distillery
