psychological science

1. NOUN

/ ˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the scientific study of mental processes and behavior, encompassing aspects of cognition, emotion, and social interaction
science of mental processes and behavior

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in psychological science to better understand human behavior.
Recent advances in psychological science have shed new light on memory and perception.
The journal publishes cutting-edge research in the field of psychological science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychology - the scientific study of the human mind and its functions
