credit bureau

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˈbjʊroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˈbjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a private organization that collects and maintains information on consumers' credit histories and provides this information to lenders and other authorized users
organization maintaining consumer credit records

Examples

Before approving the loan, the bank checked his record at the credit bureau.
Many people request a copy of their credit bureau report each year to confirm its accuracy.
If you find an error on your file, you should contact the credit bureau directly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credit agency - organization that provides credit reports
