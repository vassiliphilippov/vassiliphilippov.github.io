camera care

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmərə kɛr / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə keə / (BrE)
the act or practice of maintaining a camera in good working condition through proper handling and regular maintenance
maintenance and upkeep of a camera

Examples

Proper camera care includes cleaning the lens and storing your equipment in a dry place.
Photographers who practice regular camera care experience fewer technical issues on shoots.
Reading the manual is an important first step to good camera care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maintenance - the process of keeping something in good condition
  • Antonym: neglect - lack of proper care or attention
