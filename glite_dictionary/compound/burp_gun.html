burp gun

1. NOUN

/ bɝːp ɡʌn / (AmE), / bɜːp ɡʌn / (BrE)
A fully automatic pistol or a small submachine gun, especially one that fires rapidly.
small automatic submachine gun

Examples

The soldier carried a burp gun slung low across his chest.
Soviet troops in World War II often used the PPSh-41, a famous burp gun.
During the raid, the sharp rattle of a burp gun echoed through the alley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submachine gun - a lightweight automatic gun that fires pistol ammunition

Etymology

Imitative of the rapid firing sound ('burp') plus 'gun'.
