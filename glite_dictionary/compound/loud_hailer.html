loud hailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊd ˌheɪlər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊd ˌheɪlə / (BrE)
a portable device consisting of a loudspeaker with a built-in microphone and amplifier, used to amplify a person's voice
portable device for amplifying speech

Examples

The coach used a loud hailer to give instructions across the field.
During the protest, a volunteer spoke through a loud hailer to address the crowd.
She always brings her loud hailer to rallies so everyone can hear her message.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: megaphone - a handheld device used to amplify the voice, often cone-shaped
