trust and transparency

1. NOUN

/ trʌst ənd trænsˈpærənsi / (AmE), / trʌst ənd trænsˈpærənsi / (BrE)
a state or policy of open and reliable communication, fostering confidence and honesty between parties
open, reliable communication and honesty

Examples

The company is committed to operating with trust and transparency in all its dealings.
Effective leadership requires a culture of trust and transparency within the team.
Customer loyalty grows when businesses demonstrate trust and transparency in their practices.

Synset Examples

Our discussions will run on trust and transparency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openness - willingness to share information freely
  • Synonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful
  • Antonym: secrecy - the state of keeping information hidden
