loddon pondweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑd.ən ˈpɑndˌwid / (AmE), / ˈlɒd.ən ˈpɒnd.wiːd / (BrE)
A species of pondweed with floating leaves, found in the northern United States and Europe.
species of pondweed with floating leaves

Examples

The shallow lake was covered in patches of loddon pondweed, making it a haven for wildlife.
Scientists discovered loddon pondweed thriving in the wetlands during their ecological survey.
Gardeners often use loddon pondweed to improve the habitat in natural ponds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pondweed - an aquatic plant of the genus Potamogeton
