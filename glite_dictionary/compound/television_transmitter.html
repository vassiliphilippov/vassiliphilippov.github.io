television transmitter

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˌvɪʒən trænzˈmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən trænzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a device that sends out television signals for broadcast to television receivers
device that broadcasts television signals

Examples

The new television transmitter improved the clarity of the broadcast across the entire city.
After the storm, engineers had to repair the damaged television transmitter to restore service.
A powerful television transmitter is required to reach remote areas with the television signal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radio transmitter - device that sends out radio signals
  • Antonym: television receiver - device that receives television signals for display
