professional tennis

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfəʃənəl ˈtɛnɪs / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈtɛnɪs / (BrE)
the sport of playing tennis as a paid profession, rather than as an amateur
playing tennis as a paid profession

Examples

He has been competing in professional tennis tournaments since he was eighteen.
The prize money in professional tennis has increased significantly in the last decade.
Her dream is to make a career in professional tennis and play in Grand Slam events.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: amateur tennis - playing tennis without receiving payment
