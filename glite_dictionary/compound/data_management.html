data management

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.t̬ə ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.tə ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt / (BrE)
the process of organizing, storing, and maintaining data so it can be efficiently and securely used and accessed
the organization and maintenance of data

Examples

Good data management practices are essential for ensuring data integrity in scientific research.
The company invested in software to automate its data management workflows.
Effective data management helps businesses comply with government regulations and protect sensitive information.

Synset Examples

The passoid structure helps in data management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: database management - the process or software that controls the storage, organization, and retrieval of data in a database
  • Synonym: file management - the process of organizing and managing computer files
