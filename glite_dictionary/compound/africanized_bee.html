Africanized bee

1. NOUN

/ əˈfrɪkənaɪzd bi / (AmE), / əˈfrɪkənaɪzd biː / (BrE)
a hybrid bee originally bred in Brazil from African and European honeybees, known for its aggressive behavior and rapid spread through the Americas
a hybrid aggressive bee species

Examples

Africanized bee populations have spread rapidly across South and Central America since their introduction.
Farmers are cautious when working near hives thought to belong to the Africanized bee.
The distinct aggressiveness of the Africanized bee makes it more dangerous than other honeybees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honeybee - a bee that produces honey and lives in large colonies

Etymology

Formed from African + -ized, referring to bees with African ancestry.
