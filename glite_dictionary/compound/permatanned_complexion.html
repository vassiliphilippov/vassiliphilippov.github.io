permatanned complexion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrməˌtænd kəmˈplɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɜːməˌtænd kəmˈplɛkʃən / (BrE)
an artificially tanned skin tone that appears sun-kissed throughout the year
artificially tanned skin tone all year

Examples

His permatanned complexion made him look like he'd just returned from vacation, even in the middle of winter.
She admired her friend's permatanned complexion, wondering how much time and effort it must take to maintain it.
The actor's permatanned complexion became part of his signature Hollywood look.

Synset Examples

With his permatanned complexion, he always looked sun-kissed.
Her permatanned skin made her stand out in winter.

Etymology

Blend of 'perma(nent)' and 'tanned', plus 'complexion' from Latin 'complexio'.
