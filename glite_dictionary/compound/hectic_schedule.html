hectic schedule

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛktɪk ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈhɛktɪk ˈʃɛdjuːl / (BrE)
a period of time filled with many activities or appointments, leaving little time for rest or organization
a period of constant, busy activity

Examples

After returning from vacation, I found myself with a hectic schedule at work.
She struggled to balance her hectic schedule and her personal life.
Despite his hectic schedule, he always makes time for exercise.

Synset Examples

My schedule is really hectic this month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busy schedule - a schedule with many activities or appointments
