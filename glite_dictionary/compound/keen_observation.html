keen observation

1. NOUN

/ kin ɑbˌzɚˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / kiːn ɒbˌzɜːˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the ability to notice and interpret details or situations with sharp insight or perceptiveness
sharp or insightful perception

Examples

Her keen observation of human behavior helped her write realistic characters in her novels.
The scientist's keen observation led to an important discovery in the laboratory.
Detectives rely on keen observation to solve complex cases.

Synset Examples

They admired Sherlock Holmes for his keen observations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perceptiveness - the quality of having sharp insight or understanding
  • Synonym: insight - the ability to gain an accurate understanding of something
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or consideration
