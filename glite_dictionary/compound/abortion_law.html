abortion law

1. NOUN

/ əˈbɔɹ.ʃən lɔ / (AmE), / əˈbɔː.ʃən lɔː / (BrE)
The set of legal regulations and rules governing the practice and legality of abortion in a specific place.
law governing abortion
legal

Examples

Recent changes in abortion law have sparked widespread political debate.
The clinic is required to comply with state abortion law.
Countries differ greatly in their approach to abortion law.

Synset Examples

Abortion laws vary significantly across different states.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproductive rights - legal rights relating to reproduction and reproductive health
  • Degree Higher: abortion ban - a legal prohibition of abortion
