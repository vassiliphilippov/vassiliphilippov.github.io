city skyline

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˈskaɪˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˈskaɪlaɪn / (BrE)
the outline or profile of a city's buildings as seen against the sky, especially when distinctive or recognizable
outline of a city's buildings against the sky

Examples

The city skyline was breathtaking when seen from the river at night.
He took dozens of photos of the city skyline during his trip to New York.
After the skyscraper was built, the city skyline changed dramatically.

Synset Examples

The city skyline appeared kaleidoscopic during sunset.
The new skyscraper will dominate the city's skyline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skyline - the outline of objects seen against the sky, especially buildings or mountains
