textile mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛkstaɪl mɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstaɪl mɪl / (BrE)
A factory where textiles such as cloth or fabric are produced, especially on a large scale.
factory producing textiles

Examples

The old textile mill by the river has been converted into apartments.
Many workers found employment at the textile mill during the Industrial Revolution.
A new textile mill opened in the city, boosting the local economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mill - a building with machinery for manufacturing
