automobile tire

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːtəmoʊˌbiːl ˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəməˌbiːl ˈtaɪə / (BrE)
A rubber covering fitted around the rim of a car wheel, providing traction and absorbing shock while driving.
rubber covering for a car wheel

Examples

He replaced the worn-out automobile tire before setting off on his road trip.
A punctured automobile tire can be repaired if the damage is not severe.
The mechanic checked the pressure in each automobile tire to ensure they were properly inflated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car tire - a tire for a car wheel
  • Degree Lower: bicycle tire - the tire of a bicycle wheel
  • Degree Higher: truck tire - the tire of a truck wheel
