ninth-inning

1. NOUN

/ naɪnθ ˈɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / naɪnθ ˈɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the final inning in a standard baseball game, or the last stage of an activity or event
final inning in baseball; last stage

Examples

The team pulled off a dramatic win in the ninth-inning.
We made a ninth-inning effort to finish the project on time.
Their hope for victory faded during the ninth-inning of the match.

Synset Examples

The game was intense, especially in the ninth-inning.
In the ninth-inning, the team made a stunning comeback.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finale - the last part of a performance or event
  • Antonym: beginning - the start or first part of something
