luggage compartment

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌɡɪdʒ kəmˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ kəmˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
a compartment in a vehicle, especially an automobile, where luggage, shopping, or tools are stored
compartment in a vehicle for storing luggage

Examples

He opened the luggage compartment to load their suitcases before the trip.
The spare tire is often stored in the luggage compartment of the car.
Please check that you haven't left anything behind in the luggage compartment before you leave the taxi.

Synset Examples

he put his golf bag in the trunk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trunk - the main storage compartment at the back of an automobile (American English)
  • Synonym: boot - the storage compartment at the back of a car (British English)
