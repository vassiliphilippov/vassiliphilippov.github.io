silver ash

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvər æʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə æʃ / (BrE)
any of various Australian timber trees of the genus Flindersia, valued for their light-colored wood
Australian timber tree of the genus Flindersia

Examples

The wood from the silver ash is prized for making fine furniture and flooring.
Botanists studied the unique leaf patterns of the silver ash in the rainforest.
Planting a silver ash in your garden can provide ample shade and attractive foliage.
