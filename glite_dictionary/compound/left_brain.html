left brain

1. NOUN

/ lɛft breɪn / (AmE), / lɛft breɪn / (BrE)
the left hemisphere of the brain, which controls the right side of the body and is associated with logical and analytical functions
the left hemisphere of the brain

Examples

Damage to the left brain can affect a person's ability to speak clearly.
Some people believe the left brain is responsible for logic and reasoning.
She studied how the left brain processes language differently from the right.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: right brain - the right hemisphere of the brain
