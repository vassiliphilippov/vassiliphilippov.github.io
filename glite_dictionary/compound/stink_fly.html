stink fly

1. NOUN

/ stɪŋk flaɪ / (AmE), / stɪŋk flaɪ / (BrE)
A pale green lacewing fly known for producing an unpleasant smell, whose larvae are carnivorous.
a smelly pale green lacewing fly

Examples

The stink fly releases a strong odor to deter predators.
Farmers appreciate the stink fly because its larvae feed on crop pests.
You can often find a stink fly near garden plants in the summer.
