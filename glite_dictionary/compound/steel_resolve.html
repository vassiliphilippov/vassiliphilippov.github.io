steel resolve

1. NOUN

/ stil rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / stiːl rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
firm determination and unyielding resilience, especially in the face of adversity or difficulty
firm determination; unyielding resilience

Examples

Despite the many setbacks, her steel resolve pushed her forward to success.
The coach's steel resolve inspired the team to never give up.
He faced the challenging situation with steel resolve, refusing to be discouraged.

Synset Examples

He showed his steel resolve during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determination - the quality of being firm in purpose
  • Synonym: resilience - the ability to recover from difficulties

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'steel' to suggest strength and toughness.
