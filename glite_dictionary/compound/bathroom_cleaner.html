bathroom cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæθˌrum ˈkliːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a cleaning product formulated for removing dirt, grime, and stains from bathroom surfaces
cleaning product for bathrooms

Examples

I need to buy some bathroom cleaner to scrub the tiles and sink.
She sprayed the bathroom cleaner on the shower walls and let it sit for a minute.
Always use gloves when handling strong bathroom cleaner to protect your skin.
