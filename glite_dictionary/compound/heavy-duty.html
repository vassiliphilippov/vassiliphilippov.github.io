heavy-duty

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛvi ˈduːti / (AmE), / ˌhɛvi ˈdjuːti / (BrE)
(of equipment or materials) designed to withstand hard use or demanding work.
designed for hard or demanding work

Examples

These heavy-duty boots are perfect for hiking on rough terrain.
I bought a heavy-duty blender that can crush ice effortlessly.
The workers used heavy-duty cables to lift the machinery.

Synset Examples

a heavy-duty detergent
heavy-duty gloves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: durable - able to withstand wear, pressure, or damage
