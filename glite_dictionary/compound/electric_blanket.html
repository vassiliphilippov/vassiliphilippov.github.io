electric blanket

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈblæŋkɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
a blanket equipped with an electric heating element that allows the user to control its temperature
blanket with built-in electric heater

Examples

During the winter, I love curling up under my electric blanket to keep warm at night.
She forgot to turn off her electric blanket before leaving for work.
An electric blanket can help reduce heating costs by providing direct warmth.
