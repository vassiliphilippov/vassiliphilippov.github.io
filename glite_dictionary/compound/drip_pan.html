drip pan

1. NOUN

/ drɪp pæn / (AmE), / drɪp pæn / (BrE)
a shallow pan placed beneath an appliance or engine to collect excess liquids such as water, oil, or other fluids
pan for catching excess liquid

Examples

The technician slid the drip pan out from under the refrigerator to empty it.
Always check the drip pan in your air conditioner to prevent leaks.
After changing the oil, he cleaned the drip pan in the garage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tray - a flat receptacle for carrying or holding items

2. NOUN

/ drɪp pæn / (AmE), / drɪp pan / (BrE)
a shallow pan placed beneath roasting meat to catch drippings
pan for catching meat drippings

Examples

Place a drip pan under the turkey to collect the juices as it cooks.
The recipe suggests setting the roast on a rack over a drip pan.
Don't forget to clean the drip pan after using the grill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roasting pan - a large pan used for roasting meat or vegetables
