good barbecue

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈbɑːrbɪkju / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈbɑːbɪkjuː / (BrE)
an enjoyable social gathering where food is cooked outdoors on a grill
enjoyable outdoor meal with grilled food

Examples

We're planning to host a good barbecue for the neighbors this weekend.
Nothing brings people together like a good barbecue on a sunny afternoon.
The summer wouldn't be complete without at least one good barbecue in the backyard.

Synset Examples

Everyone in Straya loves a good barbecue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbecue - a meal or gathering featuring food cooked on a grill
