by the piece

1. ADV

/ baɪ ðə pis / (AmE), / baɪ ðə piːs / (BrE)
individually; one item at a time, rather than in bulk or as a whole.
individually; one at a time

Examples

The store sells fabric by the piece, not by the yard.
She prefers to buy dishes by the piece so she can select each one.
Artisans often create their pottery by the piece, making each item unique.

Synset Examples

she sold the plates by the piece
