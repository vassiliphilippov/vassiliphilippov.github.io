armoured combat vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmərd ˈkɑːmbət ˈviːəkl̩ / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd ˈkɒmbət ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a military vehicle that is enclosed, protected by armor, equipped with a cannon, and moves on caterpillar tracks
armored military vehicle with cannon and tracks

Examples

The army deployed several armoured combat vehicles near the border to strengthen their defenses.
The development of the first armoured combat vehicle revolutionized modern warfare.
Soldiers took cover behind a armoured combat vehicle as enemy fire intensified.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tank - a heavily armored fighting vehicle carrying guns and moving on caterpillar tracks
