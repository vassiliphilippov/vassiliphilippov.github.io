bone hard

1. ADJ

/ boʊn hɑrd / (AmE), / bəʊn hɑːd / (BrE)
extremely tough, resilient, determined, or harsh; especially of a person, condition, or material
extremely tough or resolute

Examples

The mountain trail was bone hard after the long, freezing winter.
After years in the military, his attitude was bone hard and unbreakable.
Her determination was bone hard, refusing to let any setback stop her.

Synset Examples

The winter was bone hard, making it tough to stay warm.
He was bone hard after weeks of intense training.
She was bone hard in her resolve to finish the project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard - tough, resilient, not easy to break or affect
  • Synonym: unyielding - not giving way to pressure; resolute
