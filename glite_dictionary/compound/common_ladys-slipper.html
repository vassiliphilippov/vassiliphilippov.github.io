common lady's-slipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈleɪdiz ˌslɪpə / (BrE)
a pale pink wild orchid of northeastern North America, with an inflated, pouch-like petal
pale pink North American wild orchid

Examples

We saw a field of common lady's-slippers blooming beside the trail in June.
Botanists study the common lady's-slipper because of its distinctive pouch-shaped flower.
The common lady's-slipper is protected in some regions due to its rarity.

Related Terms
