latte art

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːteɪ ɑːrt / (AmE), / ˈlæteɪ ɑːt / (BrE)
Decorative patterns or designs made in the foam on top of a latte, usually created by a skilled barista pouring steamed milk into espresso.
decorative milk designs on a latte

Examples

She spent hours perfecting her latte art before entering the national competition.
The café is famous for its intricate latte art that delights every customer.
He posted a picture of the heart-shaped latte art on his social media profile.

Synset Examples

The foamer at the coffee shop loves to talk about latte art.
He practiced latte art for one's foams at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barista - a person who prepares and serves coffee drinks, especially espressos and lattes

Etymology

From Italian 'latte' meaning 'milk', and English 'art'.
