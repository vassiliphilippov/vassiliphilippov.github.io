Hawaiian steak

1. NOUN

/ həˈwaɪ.i.ən steɪk / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i.ən steɪk / (BrE)
A steak dish prepared with tropical flavors or in a style associated with Hawaiian cuisine, typically featuring pineapple or sweet-sour sauces.
steak with tropical flavors or Hawaiian style

Examples

The Hawaiian steak was served with a sweet pineapple glaze and grilled vegetables.
If you like sweet and savory combinations, you should try their house-special Hawaiian steak.
Many summer barbecues feature Hawaiian steak as a crowd-pleasing main course.

Synset Examples

We ordered the Hawaiian steak for dinner tonight.
The restaurant is famous for its delicious Hawaiian steak.
