wisdom tooth

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪzdəm tuθ / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm tuːθ / (BrE)
one of the four last molars, usually appearing in late adolescence or early adulthood, located at the back of the upper and lower jaws
last molar to erupt in adulthood

Examples

My wisdom tooth started causing pain, so I scheduled an appointment with the dentist.
The dentist recommended removing all four wisdom teeth to prevent future problems.
After her wisdom tooth extraction, she needed a few days to recover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molar - a large, flat tooth at the back of the mouth used for grinding food
