religious service

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a public act of worship conducted according to prescribed rules, especially in a church, temple, or other place of worship
public act of worship

Examples

The family attends a religious service every Sunday morning.
A special religious service was held to celebrate the anniversary of the church's founding.
Many people gather for a religious service during major holidays such as Christmas or Passover.

Synset Examples

the Sunday service

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service - a formal act of worship
