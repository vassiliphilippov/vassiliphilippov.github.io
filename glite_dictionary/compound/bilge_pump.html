bilge pump

1. NOUN

/ bɪldʒ pʌmp / (AmE), / bɪldʒ pʌmp / (BrE)
A pump used to remove bilgewater from the bottom of a ship or boat.
pump for removing bilgewater

Examples

The bilge pump activated automatically when water started collecting below the deck.
Modern yachts are often equipped with an electric bilge pump for safety.
He checked the bilge pump regularly to prevent flooding in the engine room.

Etymology

From 'bilge' (the lowest internal part of a ship) and 'pump'.
