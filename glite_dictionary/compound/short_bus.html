short bus

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt bʌs / (AmE), / ʃɔːt bʌs / (BrE)
a small school bus, especially one used to transport students with special needs
small school bus for special needs students

Examples

He used to ride the short bus every morning to elementary school.
The district purchased a new short bus for students with mobility challenges.
After the main buses left, the short bus arrived to pick up a few remaining students.

Synset Examples

He rode the short bus to school every day.
The short bus is often used for special needs students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school bus - a bus that transports children to and from school
