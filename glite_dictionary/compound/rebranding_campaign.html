rebranding campaign

1. NOUN

/ riˈbrændɪŋ kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / riːˈbrændɪŋ kæmˈpeɪn / (BrE)
an organized effort by a company or organization to change its public image or brand identity
effort to change a brand's image

Examples

The company launched a rebranding campaign to update its outdated logo and public image.
After negative publicity, the airline initiated a major rebranding campaign to rebuild customer trust.
A successful rebranding campaign can revitalize a business and attract new customers.

Synset Examples

They designed a swooshtika for their brand's rebranding campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rebranding - changing a brand's corporate image or identity
