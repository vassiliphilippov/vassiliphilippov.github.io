breakfast table

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛkfəst ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst ˌteɪbl / (BrE)
a table at which breakfast is served or eaten
table used for eating breakfast

Examples

They gathered around the breakfast table to share morning stories and laughter.
A vase of fresh flowers sat in the center of the breakfast table, brightening the room.
Children rushed to the breakfast table as the smell of pancakes filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining table - a table at which meals are eaten
