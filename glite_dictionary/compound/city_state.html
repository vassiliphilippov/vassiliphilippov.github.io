city state

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌsteɪt / (BrE)
an independent state consisting of a single city and its surrounding territory
an independent sovereign city and territory

Examples

Venice was a powerful city state during the Middle Ages.
Singapore is often described as a modern city state.
Ancient Greece was home to several city states, such as Athens and Sparta.
