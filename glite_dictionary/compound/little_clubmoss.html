little clubmoss

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪtəl ˈklʌbˌmɑs / (AmE), / ˈlɪt(ə)l ˈklʌbmɒs / (BrE)
A small perennial plant of northern Europe and America, resembling a miniature fir, belonging to the clubmoss family.
a small clubmoss of northern regions

Examples

We found a patch of little clubmoss growing near the edge of the forest.
The little clubmoss is often mistaken for a young fir tree due to its shape.
Hikers in Scandinavia sometimes spot little clubmoss carpeting the forest floor.
