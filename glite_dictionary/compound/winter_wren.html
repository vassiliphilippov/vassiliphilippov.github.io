winter wren

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚ rɛn / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə rɛn / (BrE)
a small species of wren found in coniferous forests of the Northern Hemisphere
small northern hemisphere wren

Examples

We spotted a winter wren flitting among the pine branches.
The song of the winter wren is surprisingly loud for such a tiny bird.
Winter wrens often nest close to the ground in dense undergrowth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wren - a small songbird of the family Troglodytidae
