pikey fashion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪki ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / ˈpaɪki ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
A style of dress associated with the 'pikey' stereotype, often viewed negatively and linked to certain marginalized groups in Britain.
derogatory style associated with 'pikey' stereotype
offensive british

Examples

His parents disapproved of his pikey fashion and told him to dress differently for the party.
She was judged unfairly for her pikey fashion, though she simply liked bold clothing.
Some tabloids criticized celebrities for adopting a so-called pikey fashion on the red carpet.

Synset Examples

They were dressed in a pikey fashion that caught my eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chav - a young person characterized by brash and loutish behavior and clothing, associated with certain British stereotypes

Etymology

'Pikey' is British slang, possibly from 'pike' (a road) and used pejoratively for itinerant people.
