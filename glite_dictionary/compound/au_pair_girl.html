au pair girl

1. NOUN

/ oʊ pɛr ɡɜrl / (AmE), / əʊ pɛə ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a foreign young woman who lives with a family and helps with childcare and housework in exchange for room, board, and a small payment
foreign young woman helping in a home

Examples

The family hired an au pair girl from France to help with their children.
As an au pair girl, she attended language classes and helped after school.
Many au pair girls use their free time to explore the local culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: au pair - a young foreign person who helps with housework or childcare in exchange for board and lodging

Etymology

From French au pair (on equal terms) + English girl.
