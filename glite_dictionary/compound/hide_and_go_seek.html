hide and go seek

1. NOUN

/ haɪd ənd ɡoʊ sik / (AmE), / haɪd ənd ɡəʊ siːk / (BrE)
a children's game in which one player closes their eyes and counts while the others hide, then tries to find them
children's hiding game

Examples

We played hide and go seek in the backyard until it got dark.
The children squealed with laughter while playing hide and go seek.
At the party, everyone joined a big game of hide and go seek after cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hide-and-seek - a children's game in which players hide and one player seeks
