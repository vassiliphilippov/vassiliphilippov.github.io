it bag

1. NOUN

/ ɪt bæg / (AmE), / ɪt bæɡ / (BrE)
A handbag that is highly fashionable and sought after, especially one that has achieved iconic status for a season or period.
trendy, sought-after handbag

Examples

She proudly displayed her new it bag at the party.
The designer's release quickly became the season's must-have it bag.
Celebrities were photographed carrying the latest it bag on the red carpet.

Synset Examples

She carried her it bag everywhere she went.
Everyone wants to own the new it bag from the fashion show.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbag - a small bag carried by women to hold personal items

Etymology

The 'it' in 'it bag' refers to the concept of 'it' meaning something or someone highly fashionable and in demand, an extension of the expression 'It girl' from early 20th-century English.
