place of origin

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs əv ˈɔrɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / pleɪs əv ˈɒrɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
the location where something or someone was born, created, or first began to exist
location where something began

Examples

Italy is considered the place of origin for many popular pasta dishes.
The museum displayed artifacts found near the place of origin of the ancient civilization.
When importing goods, the place of origin must be declared on the shipping documents.

Synset Examples

the birthplace of civilization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthplace - the place where someone was born
