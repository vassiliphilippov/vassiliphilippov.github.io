butterfly orchis

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ ˈɔr.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ ˈɔː.kɪs / (BrE)
A Mexican epiphytic orchid with pale green or yellow-green flowers and a white, purple-veined lip.
a Mexican epiphytic orchid species

Examples

The butterfly orchis thrives naturally in the humid forests of Mexico.
Horticulturists admire the unique coloration of the butterfly orchis flowers.
She added a butterfly orchis to her collection of rare orchids.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərflaɪ ˈɔrkɪs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ ˈɔːkɪs / (BrE)
A Mediterranean orchid species, typically bearing purple flowers with a fan-shaped, spotted or striped rose-red lip.
Mediterranean orchid with purple flowers

Examples

The butterfly orchis is admired for its delicate, fan-shaped flowers in Mediterranean gardens.
We spotted a rare butterfly orchis blooming among the wild grasses during our hike.
Botanists study the pollination habits of the butterfly orchis because of its unique flower structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchid - a plant of the family Orchidaceae, with complex flowers
