gambling house

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæm.blɪŋ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡam.blɪŋ haʊs / (BrE)
a public building where people play games of chance for money, often operated as a business
place for playing games of chance for money

Examples

The police raided the gambling house for operating without a license.
He lost all his savings at a notorious gambling house downtown.
Many people visit the gambling house hoping to get lucky and win big.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - a facility for gambling, often with additional entertainment
