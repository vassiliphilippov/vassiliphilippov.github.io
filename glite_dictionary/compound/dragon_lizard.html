dragon lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræɡən ˌlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈdræɡən ˌlɪzəd / (BrE)
a very large lizard species, especially the Komodo dragon, native to Indonesian islands
very large lizard, e.g. Komodo dragon

Examples

The dragon lizard is renowned for its impressive size and predatory skills.
Scientists studied the habitat of the dragon lizard on several Indonesian islands.
Visitors to Komodo National Park hope to catch a glimpse of a wild dragon lizard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Komodo dragon - the largest living lizard, native to Indonesia
