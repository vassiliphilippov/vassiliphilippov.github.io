schmucky attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude that is foolish, obnoxious, or contemptible in a way that is likely to irritate others.
foolish or obnoxious attitude
informal american

Examples

If you keep that schmucky attitude, no one will want to work with you.
He apologized for his schmucky attitude at the meeting last week.
Her schmucky attitude made everyone feel uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

Her schmucky attitude ruined the group's vibe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schmucky - foolish or contemptible; annoying

Etymology

Schmucky from Yiddish 'shmok' (penis, contemptible person) + attitude.
