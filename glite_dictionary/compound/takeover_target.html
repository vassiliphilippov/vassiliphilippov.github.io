takeover target

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪkˌoʊvɚ ˈtɑːrɡɪt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkˌəʊvə ˈtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
a company regarded as attractive for acquisition by another company or investor
company attractive for acquisition

Examples

The tech giant became a takeover target after reporting record profits this year.
Several investors view the struggling retailer as a potential takeover target.
When its stock price dropped significantly, the company was seen as a prime takeover target.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of getting or acquiring something, especially another company
