far post

1. NOUN

/ fɑr poʊst / (AmE), / fɑː pəʊst / (BrE)
the goalpost that is farthest from the player on the field, especially when referring to aiming or positioning in sports such as football (soccer) or hockey
goalpost farthest from the player

Examples

He curled the ball perfectly toward the far post, but the goalkeeper made a brilliant save.
The striker sprinted into the box and tapped the ball in at the far post.
Players often try to cross the ball to a teammate waiting at the far post.

Synset Examples

The ball sailed past the defender to the far post.
He scored a goal by shooting to the far post.
She aimed for the far post with precision.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: near post - the goalpost closest to the player
