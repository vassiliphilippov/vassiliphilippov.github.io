genus Sturnella

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒinəs stərˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs stɜːˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A genus of passerine birds in the family Icteridae, which includes the meadowlarks.
genus of birds including meadowlarks

Examples

The genus Sturnella includes several species known as meadowlarks.
Ornithologists have long studied the genus Sturnella for its distinctive songs.
Members of the genus Sturnella are common across open grasslands in the Americas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meadowlark - North American songbird of the genus Sturnella

Etymology

From Latin 'Sturnella', diminutive of 'sturnus' meaning starling.
