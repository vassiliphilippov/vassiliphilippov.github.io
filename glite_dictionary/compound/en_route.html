en route

1. ADV

/ ɑn ˈrut / (AmE), / ɒn ˈruːt / (BrE)
on the way to a particular destination; while traveling to a place.
on the way to a place

Examples

The ambulance was already en route to the accident scene when we called 911.
We made a quick stop for coffee en route to the airport.
The team encountered heavy traffic en route to the championship game.

Synset Examples

help is on the way
we saw him on the way to California

Related Terms

  • Synonym: en route to - on the way toward a place; variant stressing the destination

Etymology

From French 'en route' (on the way, on the road).
