fluorescent fixture

1. NOUN

/ fluˈrɛsnt ˈfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɛsnt ˈfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
A lighting unit designed to hold and power one or more fluorescent lamps.
light fitting for fluorescent lamps

Examples

The office is illuminated by a fluorescent fixture hanging from the ceiling.
We need to replace the fluorescent fixture in the kitchen because it's flickering.
Installing a new fluorescent fixture can greatly improve the lighting in this room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: light fixture - an electrical device for holding and powering lamps
