total suckhole

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.təl ˈsʌkˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈsʌkˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a person who is excessively obsequious or sycophantic, especially in a way that is insincere or self-serving
excessively sycophantic person (slang, insulting)
slang offensive

Examples

I can't stand working with Greg; he's such a total suckhole when the boss is around.
The media portrayed her as a total suckhole for always agreeing with the CEO.
Calling someone a total suckhole is pretty harsh, even if they are being obsequious.

Synset Examples

That politician is just a total suckhole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important in order to gain advantage
  • Synonym: bootlicker - a person who behaves in an excessively servile or obsequious manner
  • Synonym: brown-noser - someone who tries too hard to please those in authority
