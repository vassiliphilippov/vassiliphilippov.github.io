low-density

1. ADJ

/ loʊ ˈdɛnsəti / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈdɛnsəti / (BrE)
having or characterized by a low concentration or number of people, things, or particles within a given area or volume.
having a low concentration

Examples

These apartments are located in a low-density residential neighborhood with plenty of open space.
The company developed a low-density foam for lightweight packaging solutions.
Urban planners aim to preserve green spaces by supporting low-density housing developments.

Synset Examples

low-density urban areas

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-density - having a high concentration or number per area or volume
