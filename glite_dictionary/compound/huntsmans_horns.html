huntsman's horns

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntsmənz hɔrnz / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsmənz hɔːnz / (BrE)
A pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its erect yellow trumpet-shaped pitchers with wide mouths and erect lids.
North American pitcher plant with trumpet-shaped yellow pitchers

Examples

The wetlands are home to the rare huntsman's horns, which attract botanists from across the country.
In spring, the huntsman's horns display striking yellow pitchers that stand tall among the grasses.
Conservation efforts have been launched to protect the habitat of huntsman's horns from urban development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sarracenia flava - Scientific name for huntsman's horns, the yellow pitcher plant
  • Synonym: pitcher plant - Carnivorous plant with pitcher-shaped leaves, general term
