halter-sack dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔltɚ ˈsæk drɛs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltə ˈsæk drɛs / (BrE)
a dress style featuring a halter neckline and a loose, sack-like silhouette, typically suitable for warm weather
loose, halter-neck summer dress

Examples

She wore a halter-sack dress to the garden party on a hot afternoon.
Many fashion designers included the halter-sack dress in their summer collections this year.
The lightweight fabric of the halter-sack dress makes it perfect for beach vacations.

Synset Examples

She wore a halter-sack dress that was perfect for summer.
