Patent Office

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætənt ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈpætənt ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
the government agency responsible for examining, granting, and maintaining patents and trademarks
government agency for patents and trademarks

Examples

She submitted her invention to the Patent Office for review and approval.
The Patent Office rejected the application due to insufficient documentation.
After a lengthy process, the Patent Office finally granted him a patent for his design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trademark office - government office handling trademarks
  • Synonym: patent - a government authority or license conferring a right or title
