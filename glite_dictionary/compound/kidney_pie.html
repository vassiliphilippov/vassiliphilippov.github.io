kidney pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪdni paɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni paɪ / (BrE)
A savory pie filled with beef kidneys, typically baked with a pastry crust.
pie made with beef kidneys

Examples

She ordered a kidney pie with mashed potatoes for lunch.
Traditional kidney pie is a staple in some British pubs.
After tasting the kidney pie, he was surprised by its rich flavor.
