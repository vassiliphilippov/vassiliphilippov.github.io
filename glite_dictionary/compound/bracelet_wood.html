bracelet wood

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪslət wʊd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪslət wʊd / (BrE)
A small West Indian shrub or tree whose hard, glossy yellow and brown patterned seeds are used to make bracelets.
West Indian shrub/tree with decorative seeds

Examples

The locals collect the seeds of the bracelet wood to craft colorful jewelry.
She wore a necklace made from the patterned seeds of the bracelet wood.
The botanical garden features a rare specimen of bracelet wood near the entrance.
