tung tree

1. NOUN

/ tʌŋ tri / (AmE), / tʌŋ triː / (BrE)
A tree native to China whose seeds produce tung oil, used in wood finishing and paints.
Chinese tree producing tung oil

Examples

The plantation included hundreds of tung trees for harvesting tung oil.
Leaves from the tung tree are broad and heart-shaped, distinguishing it from other native species.
Tung oil, extracted from the seeds of the tung tree, is highly valued by woodworkers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tung oil - a drying oil obtained from the seeds of the tung tree
