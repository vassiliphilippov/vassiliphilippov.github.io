employment contract

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈkɑntrækt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈkɒntrækt / (BrE)
a formal agreement between an employer and an employee outlining the terms and conditions of employment
agreement between employer and employee
legal

Examples

She asked her lawyer to review the employment contract before signing it.
The employment contract specifies the employee's working hours and benefits.
Breaking an employment contract without notice can have legal consequences.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contract - a legally binding agreement between two or more parties
