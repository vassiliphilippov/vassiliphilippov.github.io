life scientist

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / laɪf ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies living organisms and life processes
scientist who studies living organisms

Examples

She became a life scientist because she was fascinated by how living things function.
As a life scientist, he often conducts research on cellular processes.
Many life scientists collaborate across disciplines to solve complex biological problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
