good weather

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
pleasant weather conditions suitable for outdoor activities, typically dry, mild, and sunny.
pleasant weather for outdoor activities

Examples

We're hoping for good weather during our vacation so we can spend time at the beach.
The festival was a great success, thanks in part to the unusually good weather.
If the good weather continues, we might go hiking this weekend.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad weather - weather that is unpleasant or unsuitable for outdoor activities
