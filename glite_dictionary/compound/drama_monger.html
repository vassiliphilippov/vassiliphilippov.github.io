drama monger

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑːmə ˌmʌŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːmə ˌmʌŋə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately stirs up drama, trouble, or conflict among others
person who stirs up drama or trouble
informal

Examples

Sarah doesn't like hanging out with a drama monger because things always get tense.
Social media tends to reward the loudest drama mongers with the most attention.
Instead of solving the issue, he acted like a drama monger and made everything worse.

Synset Examples

Avoid the dramamonger; he loves to create chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scaremonger - a person who spreads frightening or alarming reports
  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person who habitually causes difficulty or conflict

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'scaremonger' and 'warmonger': 'drama' + 'monger' (dealer or promoter).
