eye opener

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / aɪ ˈəʊpənə / (BrE)
an event, experience, or fact that is surprising and reveals something previously unknown or unexpected
something that is surprising and revealing

Examples

The trip to the developing world was a real eye opener for me.
Reading the report was an eye opener that changed her perspective on the issue.
His frank confession proved to be an eye opener for the entire team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revelation - a surprising fact that is made known
  • Degree Higher: shock - a sudden upsetting event or fact

2. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ oʊpənɚ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ əʊpənə / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink taken early in the morning to stimulate or revive one.
morning alcoholic drink

Examples

He poured himself an eye opener before heading out to work.
Some people claim that an eye opener can help cure a hangover.
His morning ritual included reading the news and having an eye opener.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair of the dog - an alcoholic drink taken to relieve a hangover
