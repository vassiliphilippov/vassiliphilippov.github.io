lowbush cranberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊˌbʊʃ ˈkrænˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbʊʃ ˈkrænˌbəri / (BrE)
A small, tart red berry similar to the American cranberry but smaller, produced by certain northern shrubs of the genus Vaccinium or Viburnum.
a small, tart red berry resembling a cranberry

Examples

Wild lowbush cranberry grows in abundance throughout northern Canada and Alaska.
The tartness of lowbush cranberry makes it ideal for sauces and jams.
She picked a handful of lowbush cranberries on her hike through the wetland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranberry - a tart red berry from certain shrubs, used in cooking
