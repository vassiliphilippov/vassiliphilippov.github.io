lead glass

1. NOUN

/ lɛd ɡlæs / (AmE), / lɛd ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A type of glass containing lead oxide, notable for its high refractive index, brilliance, and use in fine glassware and optical instruments.
glass made with lead oxide

Examples

The chandelier was studded with lead glass crystals that sparkled under the lights.
Optical instruments often use lead glass lenses to improve image clarity.
The artisan specializes in decorative items crafted from fine lead glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crystal - high-quality glass with a high refractive index, typically containing lead oxide
