French people

1. NOUN

/ frɛntʃ ˈpipəl / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈpiːpl / (BrE)
the citizens or natives of France collectively; the people who are from France.
the people of France

Examples

The French people are known for their rich culinary traditions and appreciation of fine art.
During the national holiday, millions of French people gather to celebrate Bastille Day.
The history of the French people is deeply intertwined with European culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Frenchman - a male person from France
  • Synonym: Frenchwoman - a female person from France
  • Antonym: Britons - the people of Britain
