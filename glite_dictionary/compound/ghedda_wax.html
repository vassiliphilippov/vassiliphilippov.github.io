ghedda wax

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛdə ˌwæks / (AmE), / ˈɡɛdə ˌwaks / (BrE)
a wax produced by Indian and African bees, used in various industrial and domestic applications
wax from Indian and African bees

Examples

Artisans often prefer ghedda wax for crafting candles due to its particular consistency.
The export market for ghedda wax has expanded with the rise of natural cosmetics.
Traditional Indian sculpture sometimes uses ghedda wax as a modeling material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beeswax - a natural wax produced by honey bees
