pronghorn antelope

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɔŋ.hɔrn ˈæn.təˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈprɒŋ.hɔːn ˈæn.tɪˌləʊp / (BrE)
a fast-running, antelope-like ruminant of western North American plains, characterized by small forked horns
North American hoofed mammal with forked horns

Examples

The pronghorn antelope can outrun most predators on the open plains.
We spotted a herd of pronghorn antelope grazing near the foothills.
Despite its name, the pronghorn antelope is not a true antelope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pronghorn - North American mammal with forked horns
