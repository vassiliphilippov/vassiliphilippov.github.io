pair of pincers

1. NOUN

/ per əv ˈpɪnsərz / (AmE), / peə r əv ˈpɪnsəz / (BrE)
a hand tool used for gripping or holding objects, consisting of two levers joined at a pivot
hand tool for gripping, two levers

Examples

The mechanic reached for a pair of pincers to pull out the stuck nail.
Please hand me that pair of pincers from the toolbox, would you?
She used a pair of pincers to bend the wire into shape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pincers - a hand tool for gripping or pulling objects
  • Synonym: pliers - a hand tool used for holding or bending objects
