Book of Hosea

1. PROPN

/ ˌbʊk əv hoʊˈziə / (AmE), / ˌbʊk əv həʊˈziːə / (BrE)
A book of the Old Testament, recounting the prophecies and teachings of Hosea.
Old Testament book of Hosea's prophecies

Examples

Many scholars study the Book of Hosea to understand ancient Israelite prophecy.
The message of forgiveness is central in the Book of Hosea.
In her sermon, she quoted verses from the Book of Hosea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hosea - a Hebrew prophet, the main figure in the Book of Hosea

Etymology

Hosea from Hebrew 'Hôšēaʿ, a prophet in Israel.
