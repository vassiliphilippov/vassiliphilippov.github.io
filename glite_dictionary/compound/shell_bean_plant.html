shell bean plant

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛl bin plænt / (AmE), / ʃɛl biːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
a bean plant cultivated mainly for its edible seeds rather than for its pod
bean plant grown for edible seeds

Examples

This region is known for its high-yield shell bean plant varieties.
Farmers often rotate shell bean plants with other crops to maintain soil health.
The shell bean plant requires well-drained soil and full sunlight to thrive.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: string bean - a type of bean grown for its edible pods
