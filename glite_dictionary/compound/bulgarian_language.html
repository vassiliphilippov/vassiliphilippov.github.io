Bulgarian language

1. NOUN

/ bʌlˈɡɛriən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bʌlˈɡɛːriən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Slavic language spoken primarily in Bulgaria and by Bulgarian communities worldwide
Slavic language of Bulgaria

Examples

He is fluent in both English and Bulgarian language.
The Bulgarian language uses the Cyrillic script.
Many words in the Bulgarian language have Slavic roots.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bulgarian - a native or inhabitant of Bulgaria, or relating to Bulgaria or its language
  • Degree Higher: Slavic language - any of the languages belonging to the Slavic branch of the Indo-European family

Etymology

From 'Bulgarian', referring to Bulgaria, and 'language'.
