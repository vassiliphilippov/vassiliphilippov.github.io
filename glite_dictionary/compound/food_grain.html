food grain

1. NOUN

/ fuːd ɡreɪn / (AmE), / fuːd ɡreɪn / (BrE)
a grain from a cereal plant that is used as food, such as wheat, rice, or corn
grain from cereal plants used for food

Examples

The price of food grain has risen sharply in recent months due to drought.
Wheat is considered the most important food grain in many countries.
The government imported large quantities of food grain to prevent shortages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cereal grain - grain from grass plants, generally used as food
