American toad

1. NOUN

/ əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən toʊd / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən təʊd / (BrE)
a common species of toad (Anaxyrus americanus) found in North America, with warty skin and typically brown or grey coloring
a common North American toad species

Examples

While hiking, we spotted an American toad near the edge of the pond.
The call of the American toad is a familiar sound in early spring.
Young children enjoyed observing the American toad in the school garden.
