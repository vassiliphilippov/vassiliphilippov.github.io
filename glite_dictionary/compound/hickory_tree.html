hickory tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪkəri triː / (AmE), / ˈhɪkəri triː / (BrE)
An American hardwood tree of the genus Carya, bearing edible nuts and valued for its strong wood.
American hardwood tree with edible nuts

Examples

The hickory tree in our backyard produces delicious nuts every autumn.
Furniture made from hickory tree wood is prized for its strength and durability.
They sat under a large hickory tree to escape the midday sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pecan - a type of hickory tree with edible nuts

Etymology

From American English 'hickory', from Virginia Algonquian (Powhatan) 'pohickory'.
