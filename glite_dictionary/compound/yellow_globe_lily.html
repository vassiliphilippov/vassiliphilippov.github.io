yellow globe lily

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ɡloʊb ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ɡləʊb ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a globe lily native to northern California, with open branched clusters of clear yellow, egg-shaped flowers
a globe lily with yellow flowers, native to northern California

Examples

The yellow globe lily blooms in early spring along the hillsides of northern California.
Botanists studied the unique habitat of the yellow globe lily during their field research.
Many hikers stop to photograph the striking yellow globe lily on their trail.
