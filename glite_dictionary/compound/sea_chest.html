sea chest

1. NOUN

/ si tʃɛst / (AmE), / siː tʃɛst / (BrE)
a sailor's sturdy storage chest for keeping personal belongings while at sea
storage chest used by sailors

Examples

The old sea chest in the attic still contained letters and maps from long-ago voyages.
Each sailor kept his clothes and valuables locked away in a sea chest beneath his bunk.
He traced his family history through possessions found in his great-grandfather’s sea chest.
