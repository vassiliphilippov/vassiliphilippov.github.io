axillary fossa

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɪˌlɛri ˈfɒsə / (AmE), / ˈæksɪləri ˈfɒsə / (BrE)
the hollow area beneath the arm at the shoulder, commonly known as the armpit
hollow under the arm at the shoulder
medical

Examples

The doctor examined the patient's axillary fossa for signs of swelling.
Excessive sweat can accumulate in the axillary fossa during hot weather.
A visible lump in the axillary fossa may require further medical evaluation.

Synset Examples

they were up to their armpits in water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armpit - the hollow under the arm at the shoulder

Etymology

From Latin 'axilla' (armpit) and 'fossa' (ditch, hollow).
