early-to-bedder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɜrli tə ˈbɛdər / (AmE), / ˈɜːli tə ˈbɛdə / (BrE)
a person who habitually goes to bed early
person who goes to bed early

Examples

My roommate is an early-to-bedder who is usually asleep by 9 p.m.
As an early-to-bedder, she wakes up refreshed every morning.
I admire people who are early-to-bedders, but I prefer to stay up late.

Synset Examples

Being an early-to-bedder helps me stay productive.
As an early-to-bedder, he never stays out late.
She’s an early-to-bedder who wakes up before dawn.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: night owl - person who stays up late at night
