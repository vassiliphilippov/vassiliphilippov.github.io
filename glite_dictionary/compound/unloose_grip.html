to unloose grip

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈlus ɡɹɪp / (AmE), / ʌnˈluːs ɡrɪp / (BrE)
to relax or release one's hold or control on something
release one's hold or control

Examples

She decided to unloose grip on old resentments and move forward with her life.
If you unloose grip on the steering wheel, the car may veer off course.
He tried to unloose grip, but the handle was slippery from the rain.

Synset Examples

You should unloose your grip on the rope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loosen grip - to make one's grip less tight
  • Antonym: tighten grip - to hold or control more firmly
