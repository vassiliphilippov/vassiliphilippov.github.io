water intake

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ɪnˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɪnˌteɪk / (BrE)
the amount of water a person or animal consumes, typically within a given period
amount of water consumed

Examples

It's important to track your water intake to stay properly hydrated.
Doctors recommend increasing water intake during exercise or in hot climates.
Her daily water intake exceeded two liters.

Synset Examples

She monitored her water intake during the hot summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluid intake - the amount of all fluids consumed
