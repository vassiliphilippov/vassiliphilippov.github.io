bin liner

1. NOUN

/ bɪn ˈlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / bɪn ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a plastic bag placed inside a bin to keep it clean and contain waste
plastic bag for lining a bin
british

Examples

Please remember to put a fresh bin liner in after you empty the bin.
She pulled the bin liner out by the handles and tied it up before taking it outside.
Buying biodegradable bin liners is a simple way to reduce plastic waste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage bag - a plastic bag used for holding waste, common term in American English
