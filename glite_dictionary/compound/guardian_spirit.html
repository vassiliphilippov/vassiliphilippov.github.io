guardian spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdiən ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdɪən ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A spirit or angel believed to protect or watch over a particular person.
a protective spirit or angel

Examples

Many people believe that everyone has a guardian spirit watching over them.
The legend says her guardian spirit saved her from danger several times.
He often prayed to his guardian spirit for protection and guidance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guardian angel - an angelic being assigned to protect and guide a particular person
