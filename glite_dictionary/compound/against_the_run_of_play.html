against the run of play

1. NOUN

/ əˈɡɛnst ðə rʌn əv pleɪ / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst ðə rʌn əv pleɪ / (BrE)
used in sports to describe a situation where a team scores or succeeds unexpectedly while being dominated by the opposing team
scoring unexpectedly when being dominated

Examples

The underdogs managed to score against the run of play and shocked the leaders.
Their goal came against the run of play, as the opposition had dominated most of the match.
Fans cheered when their team equalized against the run of play in the final minutes.

Synset Examples

His team conceded a goal against the run of play.
It was surprising to see them score against the run of play.
They scored a goal against the run of play.
