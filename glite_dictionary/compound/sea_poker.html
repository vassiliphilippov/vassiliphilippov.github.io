sea poker

1. NOUN

/ si ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / si ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
a small, slender fish up to 8 inches long, covered with bony plates, found mainly in deeper waters of the northern Pacific Ocean
a small, bony-plated northern Pacific fish

Examples

The diver spotted a sea poker hiding among the rocks on the ocean floor.
Local fishermen sometimes catch sea pokers in their deep-water nets.
A sea poker is easily recognized by its bony plates and slender body.
