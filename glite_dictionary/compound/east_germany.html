East Germany

1. PROPN

/ ist ˈdʒɜːrməni / (AmE), / iːst ˈdʒɜːməni / (BrE)
a former country in north central Europe, established in 1949 under Soviet control and reunified with West Germany in 1990
a former country in north central Europe (1949–1990)

Examples

Many families were separated when East Germany built the Berlin Wall in 1961.
After the fall of the Berlin Wall, East Germany and West Germany were reunified.
He visited East Germany as a student in the 1980s before the reunification.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: West Germany - the former federal republic in western Germany (1949–1990)
  • Degree Higher: Germany - the modern unified country in central Europe
