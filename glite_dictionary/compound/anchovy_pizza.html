anchovy pizza

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.tʃoʊ.vi ˈpiː.t̬ə / (AmE), / ˈæn.tʃəʊ.vi ˈpiː.t.sə / (BrE)
A pizza topped with anchovies, tomato sauce, and cheese.
pizza with anchovies as a topping

Examples

She ordered an anchovy pizza for dinner despite her friends' protests.
Some people love the salty flavor of anchovy pizza while others avoid it.
The new restaurant offers a delicious anchovy pizza on its Italian menu.

Related Terms
