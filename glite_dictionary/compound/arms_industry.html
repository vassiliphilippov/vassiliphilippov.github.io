arms industry

1. NOUN

/ ɑrmz ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ɑːmz ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in the production and sale of weapons and military equipment
sector producing and selling weapons

Examples

Global debates often focus on the role of the arms industry in international conflicts.
The arms industry employs thousands of people in manufacturing, research, and development.
New government regulations aim to increase transparency in the arms industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defense industry - industry producing defense-related equipment and services
  • Synonym: military-industrial complex - the relationship between a country's military and the industry that supplies it
