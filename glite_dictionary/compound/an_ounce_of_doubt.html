an ounce of doubt

1. NOUN

/ æn aʊns əv daʊt / (AmE), / ən aʊns əv daʊt / (BrE)
a very small amount of uncertainty or suspicion
a very small amount of uncertainty

Examples

I don't have an ounce of doubt about his intentions.
She completed the exam without an ounce of doubt in her answers.
He trusted her so completely that there was not an ounce of doubt in his mind.

Synset Examples

I wouldn't give an ounce of doubt to her honesty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: a shred of doubt - an even smaller or finer amount of doubt
