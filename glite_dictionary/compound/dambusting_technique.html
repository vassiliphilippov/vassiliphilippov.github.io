dambusting technique

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæmˌbʌstɪŋ tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈdæmˌbʌstɪŋ tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a method or approach used to destroy or breach a dam, often for military or engineering purposes
method for destroying dams
