New Zealand beech

1. NOUN

/ nuː ˈziːlənd biːtʃ / (AmE), / njuː ˈziːlənd biːtʃ / (BrE)
Any of several tall New Zealand trees of the genus Nothofagus, some of which yield useful timber.
New Zealand tree of genus Nothofagus

Examples

We saw a magnificent New Zealand beech towering above the rest of the forest.
The furniture was made from durable New Zealand beech wood.
Several species of New Zealand beech are important for the country's timber industry.
