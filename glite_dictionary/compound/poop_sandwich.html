poop sandwich

1. NOUN

/ pup ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / puːp ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a humorous and informal term for a very unpleasant or undesirable situation or experience
an unpleasant situation (humorous, slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

The morning shift at the restaurant turned into a total poop sandwich when three employees called in sick.
He described dealing with the paperwork as a poop sandwich he couldn't avoid.
Sometimes life hands you a poop sandwich and you just have to get through it.

Synset Examples

The meeting was a complete poop sandwich, full of complaints.
This homework feels like a poop sandwich; it's so hard!
He described his day as a poop sandwich filled with stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog's breakfast - a confused or messy situation; a mess
  • Synonym: nightmare - a very unpleasant or difficult situation
