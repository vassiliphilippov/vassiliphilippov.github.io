Scouse culture

1. NOUN

/ skaʊs ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / skaʊs ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
The distinctive culture associated with the people of Liverpool, characterized by its unique dialect, traditions, and social customs.
culture and customs of Liverpool
british

Examples

Many musicians and bands have been influenced by Scouse culture over the years.
Learning about Scouse culture helped me understand the history and humor of Liverpool.
Television shows often highlight the friendliness and energy typical of Scouse culture.

Synset Examples

I learned about Scouse culture during my trip to Liverpool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liverpool - major city in northwest England with distinctive culture and history
  • Synonym: Scouse - the dialect or accent of people from Liverpool

Etymology

Scouse from the local dialect term for people from Liverpool, originally referring to 'lobscouse', a type of stew eaten by sailors.
