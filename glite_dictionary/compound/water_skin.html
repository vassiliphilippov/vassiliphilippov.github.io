water skin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ skɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə skɪn / (BrE)
a traditional container made from animal skin, used for carrying water, especially in arid regions
container for water made from animal skin

Examples

The desert travelers filled their water skin before setting out at dawn.
In ancient times, a water skin was essential for long journeys across dry landscapes.
He drank from his water skin to quench his thirst under the hot sun.
