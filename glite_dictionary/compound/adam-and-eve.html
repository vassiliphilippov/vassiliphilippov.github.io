Adam-and-Eve

1. NOUN

/ ˌædəm ən ˈiv / (AmE), / ˌædəm ən ˈiːv / (BrE)
A North American orchid (Aplectrum hyemale) that has a single leaf and produces yellowish-brown flowers.
North American orchid with single leaf

Examples

The forest floor was covered with Adam-and-Eve, their yellowish-brown flowers standing out among the leaves.
Gardeners prize Adam-and-Eve for its unusual appearance and resilience in shade.
On our hike, we spotted a rare Adam-and-Eve orchid blooming near the old oak tree.
