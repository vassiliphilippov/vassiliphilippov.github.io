tired and emotional

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪərd ənd ɪˈmoʊʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəd ənd ɪˈməʊʃənl / (BrE)
feeling physically exhausted and emotionally sensitive, often as a result of strain or stress
exhausted and emotionally sensitive

Examples

After hearing the bad news, she felt tired and emotional and needed some time alone.
He became tired and emotional following weeks of intense work without rest.
By the end of the marathon, everyone was tired and emotional but grateful for the experience.

Synset Examples

He was tired and emotional after the breakup.
After the long meeting, she was tired and emotional.
