kerosene lamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrəˌsin læmp / (AmE), / ˈkɛrəˌsiːn læmp / (BrE)
A lamp that produces light by burning kerosene as fuel.
lamp burning kerosene for light

Examples

During the power outage, they used a kerosene lamp to light the living room.
The old cottage still has a kerosene lamp on the mantelpiece for emergencies.
She cleaned the glass chimney of the kerosene lamp to make the flame brighter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil lamp - a lamp that burns oil for light, of which a kerosene lamp is a type
