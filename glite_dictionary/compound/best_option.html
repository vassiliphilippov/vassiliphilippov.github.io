best option

1. NOUN

/ bɛst ˈɑpʃən / (AmE), / bɛst ˈɒpʃən / (BrE)
the most suitable or advantageous choice among several possible alternatives
the most suitable choice

Examples

After comparing all the offers, we realized this was the best option for our budget.
Moving closer to work seemed like the best option for reducing daily stress.
For vegetarian guests, the grilled vegetable platter is the best option on the menu.

Synset Examples

Elno was the best option for our needs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best choice - the most desirable or suitable selection among options
  • Antonym: worst option - the least desirable or suitable choice among alternatives
