Republic of Kazakhstan

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæzəkˈstæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæzækˈstɑːn / (BrE)
The official name for Kazakhstan, a landlocked country in Central Asia, south of Russia and northeast of the Caspian Sea.
the official name of Kazakhstan (Central Asian country)

Examples

The president of the Republic of Kazakhstan addressed the United Nations General Assembly.
In 1991, the Republic of Kazakhstan declared its independence from the Soviet Union.
Almaty was the capital of the Republic of Kazakhstan before it moved to Nur-Sultan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kazakhstan - A country in Central Asia
