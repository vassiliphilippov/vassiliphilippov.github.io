roseate outlook

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊziˌeɪt ˈaʊtlʊk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzɪət ˈaʊtlʊk / (BrE)
an optimistic or positive perspective on situations, often unrealistically so
optimistic perspective

Examples

Even in difficult times, her roseate outlook inspired those around her to keep going.
Critics accused the administration of having a roseate outlook on the country's economic prospects.
His roseate outlook made him popular, but sometimes led to disappointment when reality set in.

Synset Examples

Despite setbacks, his roseate outlook never wavered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimistic outlook - expecting the best; having a positive view
  • Antonym: pessimistic outlook - expecting the worst; having a negative view

Etymology

From 'roseate', meaning rose-colored or optimistic (from Latin 'roseus'), and 'outlook', meaning perspective.
