molasses kiss

1. NOUN

/ məˈlæs.ɪs kɪs / (AmE), / məˈlæs.ɪs kɪs / (BrE)
A chewy confection made with molasses, similar in texture to toffee, often individually wrapped.
molasses-flavored chewy candy

Examples

Every Halloween, my grandmother would hand out homemade molasses kisses to the neighborhood children.
The wrapper of the molasses kiss stuck slightly, but the candy was worth the effort.
He reminisced about the molasses kiss treats he enjoyed during his childhood in Canada.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toffee - a hard, chewy candy made by boiling sugar or molasses with butter
