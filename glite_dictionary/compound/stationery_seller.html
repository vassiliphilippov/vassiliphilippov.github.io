stationery seller

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃəˌnɛri ˈsɛlər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃənəri ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a person or business that sells writing materials and office supplies
merchant selling writing materials and office supplies

Examples

The stationery seller on Main Street offers a wide selection of notebooks and pens.
We stopped by the local stationery seller to pick up envelopes for the invitations.
Many students rely on a nearby stationery seller for their school supplies at the start of term.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stationer - a person or shop that sells stationery
