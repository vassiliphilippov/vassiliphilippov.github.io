toss of a coin

1. NOUN

/ tɑs əv ə kɔɪn / (AmE), / tɒs əv ə kɔɪn / (BrE)
an act of deciding something by flipping a coin to produce a random result
random choice by flipping a coin

Examples

To break the tie, the judges resorted to a toss of a coin.
Our group leader was chosen with a simple toss of a coin.
Sometimes important decisions come down to a toss of a coin.

Synset Examples

They decided the winner on the toss of a coin.
Their fate was sealed on the toss of a coin.
It came down to the toss of a coin for the last seat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flip of a coin - an act of choosing by flipping a coin
