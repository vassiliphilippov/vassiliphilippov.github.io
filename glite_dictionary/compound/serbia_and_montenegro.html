Serbia and Montenegro

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɜrbiə ənd ˌmɒntəˈniːɡroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːbiə ənd ˌmɒntəˈniːɡrəʊ / (BrE)
a former country in southeastern Europe, bordering the Adriatic Sea, formed by the republics of Serbia and Montenegro after the breakup of Yugoslavia; officially existed from 2003 to 2006
former country in southeastern Europe (2003–2006)

Examples

During its short existence, Serbia and Montenegro was known for its complex political relationship between the two republics.
In 2006, Montenegro voted for independence, dissolving the union of Serbia and Montenegro.
The national team of Serbia and Montenegro participated in the 2006 FIFA World Cup.
