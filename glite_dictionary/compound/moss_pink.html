moss pink

1. NOUN

/ mɑs pɪŋk / (AmE), / mɒs pɪŋk / (BrE)
A low, spreading plant native to North America, with wiry stems and clusters of pink, fringed flowers; also known as creeping phlox.
flowering plant with pink blooms

Examples

The garden was carpeted with blooming moss pink in early spring.
We planted moss pink along the edge of the walkway for a splash of color.
The delicate flowers of the moss pink attract butterflies and bees.

2. NOUN

/ mɑs pɪŋk / (AmE), / mɒs pɪŋk / (BrE)
a low, spreading perennial plant (Phlox subulata) with needle-like evergreen leaves and pink or white flowers, often grown as a ground cover
a low-growing flowering plant (Phlox subulata)

Examples

The garden path was bordered by a vibrant carpet of moss pink.
In spring, moss pink blooms cover the hillside in shades of pink and white.
We chose moss pink to fill the sunny spots between the rocks.
