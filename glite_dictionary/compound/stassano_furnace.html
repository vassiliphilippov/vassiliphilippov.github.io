Stassano furnace

1. NOUN

/ stəˈsɑːnoʊ ˈfɜrnɪs / (AmE), / stəˈsɑːnəʊ ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
an electric furnace in which an electric arc is used to heat and make steel
electric arc furnace for making steel

Examples

The steel plant installed a new Stassano furnace to increase production efficiency.
Early experiments with the Stassano furnace paved the way for modern electric steelmaking.
A Stassano furnace uses an electric arc to achieve the high temperatures needed for refining steel.

Etymology

Named after Giovanni Battista Stassano, Italian engineer who invented the furnace.
