personal finance tip

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɜrsənl faɪˈnæns tɪp / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl faɪˈnæns tɪp / (BrE)
a piece of advice on managing one's own money, budgeting, or financial planning
advice on handling one's own finances

Examples

She shared a helpful personal finance tip about tracking expenses daily.
The magazine publishes a personal finance tip every week for its readers.
I learned an invaluable personal finance tip at yesterday's seminar.

Synset Examples

He is drafting a blogpost on personal finance tips.

Related Terms
