ladies' room

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪdiz ruːm / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz rʊm / (BrE)
A public toilet or restroom designated for use by women.
women's public restroom

Examples

She excused herself to go to the ladies' room before dinner was served.
The ladies' room is located at the end of the hallway on the right.
There was a long line outside the ladies' room during intermission.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: men's room - restroom for men in a public building
