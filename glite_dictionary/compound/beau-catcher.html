beau-catcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌkatʃə / (BrE)
a person, typically a woman, who attracts many admirers or romantic interests
person attracting admirers

Examples

Everyone at school considered Julie a true beau-catcher because of her lively personality.
With his new haircut and confidence, Adam became the office beau-catcher.
In the novel, the main character is a notorious beau-catcher who leaves a trail of broken hearts.

Synset Examples

As a beau-catcher, she always had admirers.
He's quite the beau-catcher at parties.
Her charm made her a natural beau-catcher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmer - a person who delights or fascinates others
  • Synonym: heartthrob - a person who is very attractive and who attracts a lot of admiration

Etymology

From 'beau' (French for 'admirer, suitor') + 'catcher'.
