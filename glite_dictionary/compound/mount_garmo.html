Mount Garmo

1. PROPN

/ maʊnt ˈɡɑːrmoʊ / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈɡɑːməʊ / (BrE)
the highest mountain peak in the Pamir Mountains, located near the Chinese border in northeastern Tajikistan, with an elevation of 24,590 feet (7,495 meters)
highest peak in the Pamir Mountains, Tajikistan

Examples

Climbers from around the world travel to Tajikistan to ascend Mount Garmo.
The view from the summit of Mount Garmo is said to be breathtaking.
Weather conditions on Mount Garmo can change rapidly, making expeditions challenging.
