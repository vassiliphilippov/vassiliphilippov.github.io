order of the day

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdɚ əv ðə deɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əv ðə deɪ / (BrE)
the list of matters to be discussed or dealt with by an assembly or organization on a particular day
agenda for an assembly or meeting

Examples

The chairman reviewed the order of the day before the meeting began.
Approval of last meeting's minutes was first on the order of the day.
The debate on education reform was moved to the order of the day for next week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agenda - a list of items to be discussed at a formal meeting
