snout beetle

1. NOUN

/ snaʊt ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / snaʊt ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
A small beetle with a long, projecting snout, often destructive to crops such as grains and nuts; commonly known as a weevil.
small beetle with a long snout; a weevil

Examples

The grain harvest was threatened by an infestation of snout beetles.
Many species of snout beetle cause serious damage to stored food products.
A snout beetle can be recognized by its distinctive elongated nose.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weevil - a small beetle with a snout, often a crop pest
