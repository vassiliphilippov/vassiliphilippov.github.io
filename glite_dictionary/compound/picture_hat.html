picture hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ hæt / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə hæt / (BrE)
a woman's fashionable hat with a wide brim, often elaborately decorated
woman's wide-brimmed decorative hat

Examples

She wore a stunning picture hat adorned with silk flowers to the garden party.
The old photograph shows ladies in elegant dresses and large picture hats.
A picture hat can add glamour to any formal occasion.

Etymology

From 'picture' (referring to hats worn by women in portrait paintings of the 18th and 19th centuries) and 'hat'.
