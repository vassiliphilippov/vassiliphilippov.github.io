Swedish iron

1. NOUN

/ ˈswidɪʃ ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ ˈaɪən / (BrE)
High-purity wrought iron historically produced in Sweden, valued for its quality and use in specialized applications.
high-purity wrought iron from Sweden

Examples

The blacksmith insisted on using only Swedish iron for crafting delicate tools.
In the nineteenth century, Swedish iron was highly sought after across Europe.
Many famous bridges were built using Swedish iron due to its strength and purity.
