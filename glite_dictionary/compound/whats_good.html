what's good

1. X

/ wʌts ɡʊd / (AmE), / wɒts ɡʊd / (BrE)
(informal) A casual greeting or inquiry, often used to ask if anything positive or noteworthy is happening.
informal greeting or inquiry
informal

Examples

As soon as he walked in, I said, 'what's good?' to greet him.
People often text 'what's good?' to check in with their friends.
When she answered the phone, her first words were 'what's good?'.

Synset Examples

In our meeting, I asked, 'So, what's good today?'
I greeted him with a smile, 'What's good?'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: what's up - informal greeting or inquiry about recent events
