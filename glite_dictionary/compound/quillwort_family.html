quillwort family

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪlˌwɝt ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈkwɪlˌwɜːt ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of aquatic or marsh plants known as quillworts, corresponding to the genus Isoetes.
family of aquatic plants (Isoetes)

Examples

The quillwort family contains only the genus Isoetes, which thrives in aquatic environments.
Botanists study the quillwort family to better understand the evolution of early vascular plants.
Some members of the quillwort family are found in temporary or seasonal ponds.

Related Terms
