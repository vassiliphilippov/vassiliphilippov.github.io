folk art

1. NOUN

/ foʊk ɑrt / (AmE), / fəʊk ɑːt / (BrE)
Art of unknown or collective origin, typically reflecting the traditional values, customs, and skills of a community or culture.
art reflecting traditional culture

Examples

The museum features a stunning collection of folk art from rural communities.
He specializes in the restoration of folk art pieces passed down through generations.
Many festivals celebrate the region’s unique folk art traditions and crafts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fine art - art created primarily for aesthetic and intellectual purposes
