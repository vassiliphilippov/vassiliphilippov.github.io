flower garden

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ ˌɡɑr.dn̩ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ˌɡɑː.dn̩ / (BrE)
A garden cultivated primarily with flowering plants for decorative purposes.
garden with flowering plants

Examples

In spring, the flower garden bursts into color with tulips and daffodils.
She spends hours every weekend tending her flower garden behind the house.
A well-designed flower garden can attract bees and butterflies to your yard.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vegetable garden - a garden for growing vegetables
