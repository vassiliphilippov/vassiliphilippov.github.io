paid vacation

1. NOUN

/ peɪd veɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / peɪd vəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A period of absence from work for rest or recreation during which an employee receives regular pay.
time off work with pay

Examples

All employees are entitled to two weeks of paid vacation each year.
She traveled to Italy during her paid vacation from work.
Some companies offer more paid vacation days as a benefit to long-term staff.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpaid leave - absence from work without pay
  • Synonym: holiday - a period of time spent away from work or school for rest or recreation
