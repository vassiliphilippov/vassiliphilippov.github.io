water-rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌreɪt / (BrE)
the regular charge paid for water supplied by a public utility, often calculated quarterly
charge for water supply
british

Examples

The quarterly water-rate is included in our household bills.
Many residents are concerned about an increase in their water-rate this year.
If you believe your water-rate is too high, contact your local council for assistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water bill - a bill for water supplied by a utility company
