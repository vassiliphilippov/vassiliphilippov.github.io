squirrel cage

1. NOUN

/ ˈskwɝːrəl keɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl keɪdʒ / (BrE)
a cylindrical cage that rotates as a small animal, such as a hamster or squirrel, runs inside it, typically used for exercise in pet enclosures
rotating exercise cage for small animals

Examples

The squirrel cage allows small pets to run and exercise indoors.
My hamster spends hours running in his squirrel cage every night.
The pet store displayed several types of squirrel cages for different animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamster wheel - rotating exercise wheel for small rodents
