attic fan

1. NOUN

/ ˈætɪk fæn / (AmE), / ˈætɪk fan / (BrE)
a fan installed in the attic of a building to expel hot air and improve ventilation
fan for expelling hot air from attics

Examples

The attic fan keeps the upstairs much cooler during hot summer days.
After installing an attic fan, their energy costs dropped noticeably.
A noisy attic fan could indicate a problem that needs repair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ventilation fan - a fan for providing fresh air or expelling stale air
