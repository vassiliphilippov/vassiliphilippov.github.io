purple silkweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrpl ˈsɪlˌkwid / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈsɪlˌkwiːd / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to eastern North America, characterized by its pink-purple flowers.
North American perennial plant with pink-purple flowers

Examples

The garden was filled with purple silkweed, attracting dozens of butterflies.
Researchers study purple silkweed for its role in supporting monarch caterpillars.
We spotted a field of blooming purple silkweed along the roadside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkweed - plant of the genus Asclepias, with milky sap
