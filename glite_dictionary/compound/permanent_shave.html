permanent shave

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːmənənt ʃeɪv / (AmE), / ˈpɜːmənənt ʃeɪv / (BrE)
a cosmetic procedure which removes facial hair in a way that lasts over time and requires minimal ongoing maintenance
lasting facial hair removal

Examples

After years of daily shaving, he opted for a permanent shave to simplify his routine.
A permanent shave can save time and effort for those who dislike facial hair.
Many grooming salons now offer permanent shave options using advanced techniques.

Synset Examples

The barber recommended a permanent shave for easy maintenance.
He decided on a permanent shave for a fresh look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair removal - the act of removing unwanted hair from the body
