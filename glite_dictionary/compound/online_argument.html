online argument

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑnˌlaɪn ˈɑrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlaɪn ˈɑːɡjəmənt / (BrE)
a disagreement, discussion, or debate that takes place on the internet, typically in forums, social media, or comment sections
disagreement happening on the internet

Examples

It is easy to be misunderstood in an online argument because of the lack of tone.
She spent hours responding to comments in an online argument about climate change.
After a heated online argument, they both decided to log off and take a break.

Synset Examples

The term cuck is often misused in online arguments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argument - a verbal exchange expressing differing viewpoints
  • Synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
