mithridate mustard

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθrɪˌdeɪt ˈmʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈmɪθrɪˌdeɪt ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
A fetid Eurasian weed with round, flat pods, now naturalized throughout North America.
fetid Eurasian weed with flat pods

Examples

The garden was overrun with mithridate mustard, its pungent smell unmistakable.
Botanists have noted the rapid spread of mithridate mustard across North American meadows.
Many farmers consider mithridate mustard to be a troublesome invasive species.
