employment agency

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˌeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˌeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
a business or organization that matches people seeking jobs with employers looking for workers
business that finds jobs for people

Examples

After months of searching, she finally found a position through the employment agency.
The company used an employment agency to recruit new staff quickly.
Many graduates register with an employment agency to increase their chances of finding work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recruitment agency - a business that helps companies find employees
