Kammon Strait Bridge

1. PROPN

/ kɑːmˌmoʊn streɪt brɪdʒ / (AmE), / kamˌmɒn streɪt brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A suspension bridge connecting the Japanese islands of Kyushu and Honshu across the Kanmon Strait.
suspension bridge between Kyushu and Honshu in Japan

Examples

Many tourists visit the Kammon Strait Bridge to admire its impressive engineering and scenic views.
The Kammon Strait Bridge is a vital part of the transportation link between Kyushu and Honshu.
Photographs of the Kammon Strait Bridge often appear in travel guides to Japan.

Etymology

Named from 'Kanmon' (a combination of 'Kan' from Shimonoseki and 'Mon' from Moji), the two cities the bridge connects.
