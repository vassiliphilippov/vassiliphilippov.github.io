first gear

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ɡɪə / (BrE)
the lowest forward gear in a vehicle's gearbox, used to start the vehicle moving from rest
lowest forward gear in a vehicle

Examples

You should always start off in first gear when driving a manual car.
He shifted from first gear to second as the car picked up speed.
The engine makes a loud noise if you stay too long in first gear.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: second gear - the next higher gear after first, allowing higher speed
