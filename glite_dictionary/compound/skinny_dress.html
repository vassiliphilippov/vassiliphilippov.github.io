skinny dress

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪni drɛs / (AmE), / ˈskɪni drɛs / (BrE)
a tight-fitting dress designed to emphasize the contours of the body
tight-fitting, body-hugging dress

Examples

She wore a skinny dress to the party, turning heads wherever she went.
A skinny dress is perfect if you want to show off your figure.
Retailers are showcasing more skinny dresses in their spring collections this year.

Synset Examples

She wore a skinny dress that accentuated her figure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodycon dress - a tight-fitting dress that hugs the figure
