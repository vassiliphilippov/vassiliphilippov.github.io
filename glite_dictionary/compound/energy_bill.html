energy bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnɚdʒi bɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi bɪl / (BrE)
a statement showing the amount of money owed for energy consumed, such as electricity or gas, over a particular period
statement of amount owed for energy use

Examples

Our energy bill was much higher this winter due to the cold weather.
She set up automatic payments for her energy bill every month.
With better insulation, their energy bills dropped by nearly 30%.

Synset Examples

The floor temperature challenge made our energy bills soar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utility bill - statement of charges for utilities such as water, gas, or electricity
