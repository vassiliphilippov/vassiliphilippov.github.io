vapor escape

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpər ɪˈskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈveɪpə ɪsˈkeɪp / (BrE)
the release or emission of steam or gas from a confined space into the air
release of steam or gas

Examples

The mechanic checked the engine for any signs of vapor escape before restarting it.
Sudden vapor escape from the container caused a loud hissing sound.
Proper ventilation is important to control vapor escape in chemical laboratories.

Synset Examples

He saw vapor escape when he opened the kettle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vent - an opening through which gas or steam can escape
