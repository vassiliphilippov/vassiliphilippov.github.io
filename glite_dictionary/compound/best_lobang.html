best lobang

1. NOUN

/ bɛst loʊˈbɑŋ / (AmE), / bɛst ləʊˈbæŋ / (BrE)
The best opportunity, bargain, or deal available, especially in shopping or other consumer contexts.
the best bargain or opportunity
informal

Examples

If you want the best lobang on electronics, check out the sale this weekend.
He always manages to find the best lobang whenever we're looking for travel deals.
Ask her about the best lobang in town—she knows all the secret spots.

Synset Examples

She always knows the best lobang for discounts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deal - an advantageous purchase or agreement
  • Synonym: opportunity - a favorable situation or chance

Etymology

From Malay 'lobang' meaning 'hole', by extension 'opportunity' in colloquial use in Singapore and Malaysia. 'Best' added for emphasis.
