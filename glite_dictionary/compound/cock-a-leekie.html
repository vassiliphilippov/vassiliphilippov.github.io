cock-a-leekie

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːk ə ˈliːki / (AmE), / ˌkɒk ə ˈliːki / (BrE)
A traditional Scottish soup made from chicken boiled with leeks, often including prunes and sometimes rice or barley.
Scottish chicken and leek soup

Examples

For the starter, we served traditional cock-a-leekie to our guests.
On a cold day, nothing warms me up like a bowl of cock-a-leekie.
He learned the recipe for cock-a-leekie from his Scottish grandmother.

Etymology

Scottish English, from 'cock' (chicken) and 'leek'.
