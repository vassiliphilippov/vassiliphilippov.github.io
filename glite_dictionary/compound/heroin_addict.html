heroin addict

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛroʊn ˈædɪkt / (AmE), / ˈhɛrəʊɪn ˈadɪkt / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to heroin
person addicted to heroin

Examples

The clinic offers support groups for heroin addicts trying to overcome their dependency.
After years as a heroin addict, he finally sought help and entered rehabilitation.
Many heroin addicts struggle with both physical and psychological challenges during recovery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to a substance, especially drugs
