Sierra Madre Oriental

1. PROPN

/ siˈɛrə ˈmɑdreɪ ɔrɪˈɛntəl / (AmE), / siˈɛərə ˈmɑːdreɪ ɔːrɪˈɛntəl / (BrE)
a mountain range in northeastern Mexico that runs parallel to the Gulf of Mexico coastline
mountain range in northeastern Mexico

Examples

The Sierra Madre Oriental is known for its diverse plant and animal life.
Much of the rainfall on Mexico's east coast is influenced by the Sierra Madre Oriental mountains.
Scientists are studying climate change effects in the Sierra Madre Oriental region.

Etymology

From Spanish Sierra Madre Oriental, meaning 'Eastern Mother Range'.
