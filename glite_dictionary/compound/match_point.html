match point

1. NOUN

/ mætʃ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / matʃ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
(in tennis and other sports) a point which, if won by a player or team, secures victory in the match.
the point that secures victory in a match

Examples

She served an ace on match point to win the championship.
The crowd grew tense as Federer faced match point in the final set.
After saving three match points, he finally lost the game.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: game point - the point which, if won, wins the game for a player or team
  • Degree Lower: set point - a point which, if won, wins the set for a player or team
