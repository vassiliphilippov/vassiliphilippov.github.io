reddish blue

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdɪʃ bluː / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ bluː / (BrE)
a color lying between blue and violet in the spectrum, with a slight red tint
bluish-violet color with a red tint

Examples

The sky at dusk sometimes takes on a reddish blue hue before nightfall.
She wore a dress of reddish blue that complemented her complexion perfectly.
The artist mixed several paints to create a unique reddish blue shade for the portrait.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: violet - a bluish-purple color at the end of the visible spectrum
  • Degree Lower: blue - a primary color between green and violet in the spectrum
  • Synonym: purplish blue - a blue with a purple or red tint
