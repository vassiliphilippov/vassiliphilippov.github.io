family Fissurellidae

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˌfɪʃəˈrɛlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌfɪsjʊˈrɛlɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of marine gastropod mollusks, which includes keyhole limpets and slit limpets
a family of marine gastropod mollusks

Examples

The family Fissurellidae includes a variety of keyhole limpets found in oceans worldwide.
Researchers discovered a new species within the family Fissurellidae during the marine survey.
Many members of the family Fissurellidae are recognized by the distinctive hole or slit in their shells.

Etymology

From New Latin 'Fissurellidae', based on the type genus 'Fissurella'.
