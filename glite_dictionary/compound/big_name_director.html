big name director

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡ neɪm dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / bɪɡ neɪm dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
a famous or well-known director, especially in film, television, or theater
famous director

Examples

She was thrilled to work with a big name director on her first film.
Landing a role in a big name director's movie can launch an actor's career.
Critics were surprised when the big name director agreed to take on the independent project.

Synset Examples
