CalArts style

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl ɑrts staɪl / (AmE), / ˈkæl ɑːts staɪl / (BrE)
a distinctive style of animation characterized by simple shapes, expressive features, and a quirky, informal aesthetic, associated with the California Institute of the Arts
distinctive animation style linked to CalArts

Examples

The new cartoon series boldly uses the CalArts style to appeal to a younger audience.
Many independent animators criticize the overuse of the CalArts style in mainstream projects.
She experimented with different techniques before settling on the CalArts style for her graduation film.

Synset Examples

They embraced the quirky CalArts style in their project.
Her animation clearly reflects that CalArts style.
The film showcased a vibrant CalArts style aesthetic.

Etymology

From 'CalArts', the abbreviation of California Institute of the Arts, where the style originated.
