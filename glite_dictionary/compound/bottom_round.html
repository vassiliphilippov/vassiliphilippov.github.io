bottom round

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑtəm raʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm raʊnd / (BrE)
a cut of beef taken from the round, typically suitable for pot roast
cut of beef from the round

Examples

The chef recommended using bottom round for making a flavorful pot roast.
She bought a large bottom round from the butcher for Sunday dinner.
To achieve tenderness, bottom round should be cooked slowly with moist heat.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: top round - a cut of beef from the upper part of the round
