sword bean

1. NOUN

/ sɔrd ˌbiːn / (AmE), / sɔːd ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant (Canavalia gladiata) with long pods containing red or brown beans, cultivated in Asia as a food crop.
tropical plant with edible long pods

Examples

The farmer planted sword bean to diversify his crops this year.
In traditional Asian cooking, sword bean is often used in stews and soups.
Botanists studied the growth patterns of the sword bean in tropical climates.
