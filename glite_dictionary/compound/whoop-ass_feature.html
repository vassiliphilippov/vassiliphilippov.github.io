whoop-ass feature

1. NOUN

/ ˈwup æs ˈfiːt͡ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈwʊp æs ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
an impressive or exceptionally powerful feature, especially in a device or product
impressive or powerful feature
informal slang

Examples

The phone's camera is a real whoop-ass feature compared to other models.
Gamers praised the console's graphics engine as its biggest whoop-ass feature.
Adding wireless charging was the whoop-ass feature that convinced me to upgrade.

Synset Examples

This new device has some serious whoop-ass features.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer feature - a feature that gives a product a significant advantage
  • Synonym: standout feature - a feature that is notably impressive or distinguishing

Etymology

From slang 'whoop-ass' (meaning forceful or impressive) + 'feature'.
