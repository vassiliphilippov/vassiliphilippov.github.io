soup spoon

1. NOUN

/ suːp spuːn / (AmE), / suːp spuːn / (BrE)
A spoon with a large, rounded bowl, used for eating soup.
spoon for eating soup

Examples

She picked up her soup spoon and began tasting the vegetable broth.
Please use the soup spoon rather than the dessert spoon for the first course.
The soup spoon has a rounder, deeper bowl than other spoons in the set.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tablespoon - a large spoon used for serving or eating food
  • Antonym: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring tea or coffee
