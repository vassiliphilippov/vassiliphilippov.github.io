complete and utter joy

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈplit ənd ˈʌtɚ dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ənd ˈʌtə dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
a feeling of absolute and overwhelming happiness
total happiness; absolute joy

Examples

The crowd erupted in complete and utter joy when the team won the championship.
He was filled with complete and utter joy upon holding his newborn daughter for the first time.
The news of her recovery brought complete and utter joy to the entire family.

Synset Examples

She felt complete and utter joy at the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - an intense feeling of joy or delight
  • Synonym: bliss - perfect happiness; great joy
  • Antonym: misery - a state of great unhappiness or suffering
