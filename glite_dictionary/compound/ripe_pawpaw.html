ripe pawpaw

1. NOUN

/ raɪp ˈpɑːˌpɑː / (AmE), / raɪp ˈpɔːˌpɔː / (BrE)
A pawpaw fruit that is fully matured and sweet, typically eaten fresh or used in desserts and smoothies.
fully matured, sweet pawpaw fruit

Examples

I sliced a ripe pawpaw and added it to my morning yogurt.
The scent of a ripe pawpaw filled the kitchen.
You can tell a ripe pawpaw by its soft skin and fragrant aroma.

Synset Examples

She made a delicious smoothie with ripe pawpaw.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unripe pawpaw - pawpaw fruit that is not yet fully matured or sweet
