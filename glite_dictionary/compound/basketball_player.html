basketball player

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
A person who plays basketball, especially as a member of a team or professionally.
athlete who plays basketball

Examples

She has dreamed of becoming a professional basketball player since she was a child.
The team's star basketball player scored thirty points in last night’s game.
Many college students hope to be drafted as a basketball player in the national league.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise
