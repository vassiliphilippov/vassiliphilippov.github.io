business loan

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs loʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan provided by a bank or other financial institution specifically for business purposes
loan for business purposes

Examples

The company applied for a business loan to expand its operations.
Many small enterprises rely on a business loan to cover startup expenses.
The business loan was approved after the owners submitted their financial statements.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: personal loan - a loan intended for personal use rather than business
