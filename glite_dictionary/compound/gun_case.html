gun case

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn keɪs / (AmE), / ɡʌn keɪs / (BrE)
a container designed to hold and protect a gun, often used for transport or storage
container for storing a gun

Examples

He stored his rifle in a locked gun case for safety.
The airport requires travelers to check any firearms inside a secure gun case.
She bought a padded gun case to protect her shotgun during transport.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gun safe - a secure storage container for firearms
