major leaguer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪ.dʒɚ ˌliː.ɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.dʒə ˌliː.ɡə / (BrE)
A professional baseball player who is a member of a major-league team.
professional baseball player in a major league

Examples

He dreamed of becoming a major leaguer ever since he was a child.
The team signed three new major leaguers during the off-season.
As a major leaguer, she quickly earned respect for her talent and dedication.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: minor leaguer - a player who is a member of a minor-league baseball team
