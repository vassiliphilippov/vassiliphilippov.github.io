cress plant

1. NOUN

/ krɛs plænt / (AmE), / krɛs plɑːnt / (BrE)
any of several plants in the mustard family (Cruciferae) with edible, pungent-tasting leaves, often used in salads or as garnish
edible, pungent plant in the mustard family

Examples

Gardeners often grow cress plant for its quick harvest and spicy flavor.
The chef added fresh leaves from the cress plant to the salad for a peppery taste.
As a child, I enjoyed watching the cress plant sprout on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cress - edible plant with pungent leaves, typically used in salads
