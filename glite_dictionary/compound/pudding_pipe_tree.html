pudding pipe tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʊdɪŋ paɪp triː / (AmE), / ˈpʊdɪŋ paɪp triː / (BrE)
A deciduous or semi-evergreen tropical tree with scented sepia to yellow flowers in drooping clusters and pods whose pulp is used medicinally; found in tropical Asia, Central and South America, and Australia.
tropical tree with medicinal pods

Examples

The pudding pipe tree is often cultivated for its medicinal properties in southern India.
Drooping clusters of yellow flowers make the pudding pipe tree stand out during the flowering season.
Traditional healers sometimes use the pulp from the pudding pipe tree pods in herbal remedies.
