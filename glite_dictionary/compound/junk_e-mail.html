junk e-mail

1. NOUN

/ dʒʌŋk ˈiːmeɪl / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk ˈiːmeɪl / (BrE)
unwanted or unsolicited e-mail messages, typically of a commercial nature, sent out in bulk to multiple recipients
unwanted or bulk e-mail messages

Examples

I set up filters to automatically move all junk e-mail to a separate folder.
She receives so much junk e-mail that it clutters her inbox every day.
The company's aggressive marketing campaign resulted in a flood of junk e-mail to customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spam - unsolicited bulk e-mail, especially advertising
