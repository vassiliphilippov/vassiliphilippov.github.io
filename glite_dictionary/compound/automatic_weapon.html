automatic weapon

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
a firearm that automatically reloads and continues to fire as long as the trigger is pressed
firearm that fires continuously while the trigger is held

Examples

The soldier was armed with an automatic weapon during the operation.
Owning an automatic weapon is heavily regulated in many countries.
He demonstrated how quickly an automatic weapon could fire multiple rounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: machine gun - a fully automatic firearm, often mounted or portable
