stonelike demeanor

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnˌlaɪk dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌlaɪk dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
an emotionless or unresponsive appearance, resembling the qualities of stone
emotionless or unresponsive appearance

Examples

Despite the bad news, he maintained a stonelike demeanor throughout the meeting.
Her stonelike demeanor concealed any sign of excitement or disappointment.
Interviewers found his stonelike demeanor intimidating and hard to interpret.

Synset Examples

Her stonelike demeanor made it hard to read her emotions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassive - showing no emotion or feeling
  • Synonym: expressionless - showing no emotion through facial expression
