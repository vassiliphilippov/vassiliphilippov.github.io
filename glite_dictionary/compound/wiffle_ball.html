Wiffle Ball

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪfəl bɔl / (AmE), / ˈwɪf(ə)l bɔːl / (BrE)
A hollow plastic ball with perforated sides, used especially for a bat-and-ball game of the same name.
hollow plastic ball with holes

Examples

We played a lively game of Wiffle Ball in the backyard after lunch.
The lightweight design of the Wiffle Ball makes it perfect for safe indoor use.
He bought a pack of Wiffle Balls and bats for the neighborhood kids.

Etymology

Trademark, originally from the name of the manufacturer; possibly from 'whiff', meaning to swing and miss.
