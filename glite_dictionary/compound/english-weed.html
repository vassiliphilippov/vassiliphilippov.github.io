English-weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ wiːd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ wiːd / (BrE)
A South African bulbous wood sorrel with conspicuous yellow flowers, often found as a garden weed.
South African bulbous wood sorrel with yellow flowers

Examples

English-weed is commonly found growing in gardens throughout South Africa.
The bright yellow flowers of English-weed brighten the landscape during spring.
Some gardeners try to remove English-weed because it spreads quickly among other plants.
