tara vine

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːrə vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɑːrə vaɪn / (BrE)
A climbing Asian vine with long, finely serrated leaves, white flowers in clusters, and greenish-yellow edible fruit.
Asian climbing vine with edible fruit

Examples

The tara vine grows rapidly in the warm, humid climate of Southeast Asia.
Many villagers harvest the edible fruit of the tara vine to use in local cuisine.
Clusters of white flowers appear on the tara vine each spring before fruiting.
