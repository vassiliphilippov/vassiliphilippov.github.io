violin maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪə.lɪn ˌmeɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.ə.lɪn ˌmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs violins
person who makes violins

Examples

The violin maker carefully selected the wood for his next instrument.
She apprenticed with a renowned violin maker in Italy for two years.
Every violin maker develops a signature sound in their instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luthier - a person who makes or repairs stringed instruments
