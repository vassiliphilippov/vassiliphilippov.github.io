ratty curtain

1. NOUN

/ ˈræti ˈkɝː.tən / (AmE), / ˈræti ˈkɜː.tən / (BrE)
A curtain that is worn-out, shabby, or in poor condition.
worn-out or shabby curtain

Examples

The ratty curtain barely kept the sunlight out of the room.
She decided to replace the ratty curtain with something more cheerful.
A ratty curtain hung limply over the broken window.

Synset Examples

The ratty curtains needed replacing badly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curtain - piece of cloth that hangs across a window or opening
  • Synonym: ratty - worn-out or shabby in appearance
