guard dog

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑrd dɔɡ / (AmE), / ɡɑːd dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog trained to protect property or people by warning of or attacking intruders
dog trained to guard property

Examples

The warehouse keeps a fierce guard dog on the premises overnight.
Their property is protected by an electronic fence and a trained guard dog.
The family adopted a guard dog to help ensure their safety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchdog - a dog kept to guard property and give warning of intruders
  • Synonym: attack dog - a dog trained to attack on command
  • Antonym: pet - an animal kept for companionship rather than security
