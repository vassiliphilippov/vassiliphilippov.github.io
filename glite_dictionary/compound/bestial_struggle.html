bestial struggle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛstiəl ˈstrʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪəl ˈstrʌɡl / (BrE)
a brutal or savage fight characterized by animal-like violence or lack of restraint
brutal or savage fight

Examples

The novel describes a bestial struggle between the two rival gangs.
There was a bestial struggle for food in the aftermath of the disaster.
The documentary showed the bestial struggle of animals competing for territory.

Synset Examples

The film depicted a bestial struggle for survival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brawl - a noisy fight, often in a public place
  • Synonym: melee - a confused fight or scuffle
