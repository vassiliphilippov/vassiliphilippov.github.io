tone-beginning

1. NOUN

/ toʊn bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / təʊn bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the manner in which a musical tone or phrase is started, especially in terms of its decisiveness or clarity
start of a musical tone or phrase

Examples

The pianist's tone-beginning was so precise that each note stood out clearly.
Mastering the art of tone-beginning can greatly enhance a singer's performance.
Different instruments may require different techniques for a clean tone-beginning.
