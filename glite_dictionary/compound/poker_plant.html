poker plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊkər plænt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A clump-forming plant native to South Africa, grown for its tall spikes of scarlet flowers, also known as red hot poker.
plant with tall scarlet flower spikes

Examples

The poker plant adds a vivid splash of color to any summer garden.
Gardeners often choose the poker plant for its striking flower spikes.
In full bloom, the poker plant attracts hummingbirds and butterflies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: red hot poker - Common name for the poker plant (Kniphofia), a clump-forming plant with red or orange flower spikes

Etymology

Named for its flower spikes' resemblance to red-hot pokers used in fireplaces.
