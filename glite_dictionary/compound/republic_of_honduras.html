Republic of Honduras

1. PROPN

/ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv hɒnˈdʊrəs / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv hɒnˈdjʊərəs / (BrE)
A country in Central America, formerly part of the Spanish Empire, which became independent in 1821; known for its early Mayan centers.
country in Central America

Examples

The Republic of Honduras is known for its Mayan ruins and diverse wildlife.
In 1821, the Republic of Honduras gained independence from Spanish colonial rule.
Many tourists visit the Republic of Honduras to explore its beaches and rainforests.
