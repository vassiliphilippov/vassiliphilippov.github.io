hay-scented fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪ ˌsɛntɪd fɝn / (AmE), / ˈheɪ ˌsɛntɪd fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern native to eastern North America, with pale green fronds and a scent resembling that of hay
a North American fern with a hay-like scent

Examples

A patch of hay-scented fern covered the forest floor near the edge of the clearing.
The aroma of hay-scented fern is released when you brush past its delicate fronds.
Gardeners enjoy planting hay-scented fern for its lush appearance and pleasant smell.
