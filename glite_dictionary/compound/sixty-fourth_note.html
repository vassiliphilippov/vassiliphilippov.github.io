sixty-fourth note

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪksti ˈfɔrθ noʊt / (AmE), / ˌsɪksti ˈfɔːθ nəʊt / (BrE)
a musical note with a time value equal to one sixty-fourth of a whole note
very short musical note

Examples

The piece was played so fast that the sixty-fourth note passages were barely audible.
She practiced the sixty-fourth note runs until they sounded clear and precise.
Modern compositions sometimes make use of the sixty-fourth note to achieve rapid ornamentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hemidemisemiquaver - a musical note with the same time value as a sixty-fourth note, chiefly in British English
