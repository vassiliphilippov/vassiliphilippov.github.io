favourable position

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvərəbəl pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbl pəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation in which someone or something has an advantage over others or holds a beneficial status
advantageous situation or status

Examples

The company secured a favourable position in the market by launching an innovative product.
Her years of experience put her in a favourable position for the promotion.
By arriving early, we were able to get a favourable position near the stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advantage - a condition or circumstance that puts one in a superior or more favorable position
  • Antonym: disadvantage - an unfavorable circumstance or condition that reduces the chances of success or effectiveness
