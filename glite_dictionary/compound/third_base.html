third base

1. NOUN

/ θɝd beɪs / (AmE), / θɜːd beɪs / (BrE)
the fielding position on a baseball team located near the third base, occupied by a defensive player in the infield
baseball fielding position near third base

Examples

She has played third base for the team for three years.
The ball was hit sharply toward third base, but the fielder made an excellent stop.
He switched from playing shortstop to third base last season.

Synset Examples

he is playing third

Related Terms

  • Synonym: third baseman - the player who occupies third base on a baseball team

2. NOUN

/ θɝd beɪs / (AmE), / θɜːd beɪs / (BrE)
the third of four bases that a runner must touch in order to score a run in baseball
the third base in baseball

Examples

The runner slid safely into third base after a wild throw from the outfield.
He played third base for his high school baseball team.
After stealing third base, she was just one step away from scoring a run.

Synset Examples

he was cut down on a close play at third

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first base - first of four bases a runner must touch in baseball
  • Synonym: second base - second of four bases a runner must touch in baseball
  • Synonym: home plate - final base that a runner must touch to score in baseball
