fly frame

1. NOUN

/ flaɪ freɪm / (AmE), / flaɪ freɪm / (BrE)
a machine in textile manufacturing that converts drawn sliver into roving by adding a slight twist
machine making roving from sliver

Examples

The old spinning mill still has an original fly frame in its equipment collection.
Technicians monitor the fly frame to ensure consistent production of high-quality roving.
Proper maintenance of the fly frame is essential for avoiding breakdowns during textile processing.

Synset Examples

Simplex machines give the attenuated sliver a small amount of twist to ensure the required strength of the roving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roving frame - a machine for producing roving in textile manufacturing
