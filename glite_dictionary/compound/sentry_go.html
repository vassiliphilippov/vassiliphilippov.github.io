sentry go

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntri ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntri ɡəʊ / (BrE)
a period or duty of standing guard as a sentry, especially in military contexts
period of sentry duty

Examples

He was assigned the sentry go for the first half of the night.
After his sentry go, he returned to the barracks to rest.
During his sentry go, he kept a careful watch over the entrance.

Synset Examples

he was on guard that night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard duty - the job of guarding a place or person
