cultivated parsnip

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌl.tɪˌveɪ.tɪd ˈpɑr.snɪp / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tɪ.veɪ.tɪd ˈpɑː.snɪp / (BrE)
A biennial European plant (Pastinaca sativa) grown for its long, edible, tapering root.
edible root vegetable; cultivated plant

Examples

The chef roasted cultivated parsnip with honey and thyme for a sweet, earthy flavor.
Farmers harvest cultivated parsnip in the autumn when the roots are most tender.
Adding slices of cultivated parsnip to stews brings out a pleasant, nutty taste.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parsnip - the plant Pastinaca sativa, grown for its edible root
