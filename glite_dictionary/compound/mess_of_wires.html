mess of wires

1. NOUN

/ mɛs əv waɪɚz / (AmE), / mɛs əv waɪəz / (BrE)
a disorganized or tangled collection of wires
tangled collection of wires

Examples

A technician spent hours sorting out the mess of wires behind the server rack.
The entertainment system left a mess of wires sprawled across the floor.
After moving his desk, he was surprised by the mess of wires tangled underneath.

Synset Examples

She tried to untangle the mess of wires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tangle - a confused or intricate mass; a snarl, especially of threads or lines
