September 11

1. PROPN

/ sɛpˈtɛmbɚ ɪˈlɛvən / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɛmbə ɪˈlɛvən / (BrE)
the day in 2001 when coordinated terrorist attacks were carried out in the United States, notably the destruction of the World Trade Center in New York City and damage to the Pentagon
date of major terrorist attacks in 2001

Examples

The tragic events of September 11 changed the world forever.
Many memorial services are held every year on September 11.
After September 11, airport security measures became much more stringent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 9/11 - abbreviation for the terrorist attacks that occurred on September 11, 2001
