half title

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈtaɪtəl / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈtaɪtəl / (BrE)
the first page of some books containing only the title and no other details
first page with only the book title

Examples

Before the main title page, the book features a half title printed in simple type.
Collectors look for rare editions with an intact half title.
The publisher requested a revised half title page for the second printing.
