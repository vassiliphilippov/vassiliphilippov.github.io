transmitting aerial

1. NOUN

/ trænzˈmɪtɪŋ ˈɛriəl / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtɪŋ ˈeəriəl / (BrE)
an antenna designed to transmit radio or television signals
antenna for sending radio/TV signals

Examples

The radio station upgraded its transmitting aerial to improve signal strength across the county.
Engineers climbed the tower to inspect the transmitting aerial after the storm damaged several components.
A faulty transmitting aerial can cause disruptions in live television broadcasts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antenna - a device that transmits or receives radio waves
