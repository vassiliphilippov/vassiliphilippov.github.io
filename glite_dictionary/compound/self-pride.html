self-pride

1. NOUN

/ sɛlfˈpraɪd / (AmE), / sɛlfˈpraɪd / (BrE)
a feeling of satisfaction and confidence in oneself or one's own worth
pride in oneself

Examples

He carried himself with a quiet self-pride that impressed everyone he met.
Developing a sense of self-pride can help children build resilience against peer pressure.
Her self-pride never turned into arrogance; she remained humble despite her achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pride - a feeling of satisfaction derived from one's own achievements
  • Antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's own importance
