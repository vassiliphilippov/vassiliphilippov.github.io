bulletin board

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlətɪn bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlətɪn bɔːd / (BrE)
A board mounted on a wall for posting public notices, messages, or announcements.
board for posting notices

Examples

Please check the bulletin board for new announcements before leaving the room.
She pinned the missing dog poster to the bulletin board at the community center.
The teacher updates the classroom bulletin board every month with student artwork.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noticeboard - a board for displaying notices or announcements (chiefly British term)
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02919884-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlətɪn ˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlətɪn ˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a computer system running software that allows users to post messages and access shared information, often forming an online community
online message board system

Examples

Many enthusiasts still use bulletin boards to exchange technical information about vintage computers.
Before the internet was widely available, people joined bulletin boards to discuss shared interests.
The local computer club hosts a bulletin board where members can post announcements and files.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forum - an online discussion site where people can hold conversations in the form of posted messages
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02920030-n
Synset POS: n
