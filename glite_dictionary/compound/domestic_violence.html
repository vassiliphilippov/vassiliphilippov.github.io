domestic violence

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈvaɪələns / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈvaɪələns / (BrE)
violent or abusive behavior directed at a spouse, partner, or other household member, typically in the home
violence against family members or partners

Examples

She left her husband after years of domestic violence.
The shelter provides support for victims of domestic violence.
Laws against domestic violence have become much stricter in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abuse - wrong or cruel treatment
