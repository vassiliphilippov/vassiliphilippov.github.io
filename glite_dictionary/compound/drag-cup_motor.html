drag-cup motor

1. NOUN

/ dræɡ kʌp ˈmoʊtɚ / (AmE), / dræɡ kʌp ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
an induction motor featuring a cup-shaped rotor made of conducting material, with a stationary magnetic core inside it.
type of induction motor with a cup-shaped rotor

Examples

The engineer inspected the drag-cup motor to diagnose the unusual noise.
A drag-cup motor is often used in sensitive measurement devices because of its low inertia.
Replacing the drag-cup motor improved the instrument's response time.
