trampy outfit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræmpi ˈaʊtfɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrampi ˈaʊtfɪt / (BrE)
An outfit considered sexually provocative or inappropriate, often viewed as lacking modesty or good taste.
provocative or inappropriate clothing
informal

Examples

The tabloid criticized the celebrity for wearing a trampy outfit on the red carpet.
Her mother disapproved of the trampy outfit she wore to the club.
He joked that his Halloween costume was just an excuse to wear a trampy outfit.

Synset Examples

She wore a trampy outfit for the Halloween party.
They criticized her for dressing in a trampy style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provocative outfit - clothing designed to attract sexual attention
