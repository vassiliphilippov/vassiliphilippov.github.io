kee siao attitude

1. NOUN

/ ki siɑʊ ˈætɪtud / (AmE), / kiː siːaʊ ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a demeanor or manner of behaving that is silly, eccentric, or seemingly crazy, often in a playful or humorous way
silly or crazy demeanor
informal

Examples

Her kee siao attitude made everyone laugh during the meeting.
You need a bit of a kee siao attitude to survive in this hectic office.
His kee siao attitude often lightens the mood among his friends.

Synset Examples

Her kee siao attitude made everyone laugh.

Etymology

From Singaporean English, with 'kee siao' derived from Hokkien dialect, where 'siao' means 'crazy'.
