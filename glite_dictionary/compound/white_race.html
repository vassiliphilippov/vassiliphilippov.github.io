White race

1. NOUN

/ waɪt reɪs / (AmE), / waɪt reɪs / (BrE)
A group of people identified as having light skin, typically of European descent.
group of people with light skin

Examples

He taught a class on the history of the White race in Europe.
Some genetic studies examine the origins of the White race.
The term White race has evolved in its meaning over time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caucasian - person of white descent
  • Antonym: Black race - group of people with dark skin
