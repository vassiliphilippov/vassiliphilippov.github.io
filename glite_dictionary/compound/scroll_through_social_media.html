to scroll through social media

1. VERB

/ skroʊl θruː ˌsoʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / skrəʊl θruː ˌsəʊʃəl ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
to move through content on social media platforms by continuously sliding or flicking a screen, typically to browse posts or updates
browse social media by scrolling

Examples

It's easy to lose track of time when you scroll through social media before bed.
She likes to scroll through social media in the morning to catch up on news and updates.
Many people scroll through social media during their commute to work.

Synset Examples

He spends hours scrolling through Fakebook instead of engaging.
She spends hours scrolling through twitface every day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: browse - to look through information or goods casually or leisurely
