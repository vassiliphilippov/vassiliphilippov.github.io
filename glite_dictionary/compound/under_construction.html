under construction

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndɚ kənˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˈʌndə kənˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
in the process of being built or developed; not yet finished and not ready for use
in progress; being built or developed

Examples

The new bridge downtown is still under construction and will open next year.
The restaurant was under construction for several months before it finally opened.
Their online store remains under construction, so you can't place orders yet.

Synset Examples

The website is under construction; please check back later.
The new library is still under construction downtown.
