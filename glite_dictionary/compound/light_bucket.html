light bucket

1. NOUN

/ laɪt ˈbʌkɪt / (AmE), / laɪt ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
a bucket that is lightweight, designed for easy lifting or safety and ease of use
lightweight bucket for easy lifting

Examples

The contractor chose a light bucket so he could move materials quickly around the site.
Using a light bucket reduced the strain on the workers' backs during cleanup.
She found that the light bucket made lifting debris much easier than the regular metal one.

Synset Examples

The contractor used a light bucket for easy access to the roof.
He operated the light bucket with precision to reach the high branches.
A light bucket makes tree trimming much safer.
