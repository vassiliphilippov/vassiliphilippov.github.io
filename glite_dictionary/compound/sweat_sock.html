sweat sock

1. NOUN

/ swɛt sɑk / (AmE), / swɛt sɒk / (BrE)
A thick, absorbent sock typically worn during athletic activities to absorb sweat and provide comfort.
athletic sock for absorbing sweat

Examples

He grabbed a clean sweat sock before heading to basketball practice.
The sweat sock prevented blisters during his morning run.
After the game, his sweat sock was soaked through from exertion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletic sock - sock designed for sports or physical activity
