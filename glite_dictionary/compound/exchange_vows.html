to exchange vows

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈsʧeɪndʒ vaʊz / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ vaʊz / (BrE)
to make mutual promises of love and commitment during a wedding ceremony
make mutual promises in a wedding

Examples

They decided to exchange vows in a small garden surrounded by family and friends.
The couple exchanged vows beneath a canopy of twinkling fairy lights.
Many people dream of the day they will exchange vows with the one they love.

Synset Examples

They exchanged vows at a beautiful altar decorated with flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to get married - to become husband and wife or formally join in marriage
