chavette trend

1. NOUN

/ tʃæˈvɛt trɛnd / (AmE), / tʃaˈvɛt trɛnd / (BrE)
a style associated with young, trendy women in the UK, often characterized by bold and flashy fashion choices
fashion style of young trendy UK women
british informal

Examples

The chavette trend influenced high street fashion with its use of bold colors and statement jewellery.
She adopted the chavette trend by pairing large hoop earrings with tracksuits and flashy trainers.
In the early 2000s, the chavette trend became popular among teenagers across the UK.

Synset Examples

The chavette trend was all about bold fashion choices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chav - British slang for a young person regarded as displaying brash and loutish behavior and style

Etymology

From British slang 'chavette' (female variant of 'chav') plus 'trend'.
