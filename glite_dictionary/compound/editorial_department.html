editorial department

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdəˈtɔriəl dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the section of a publishing business responsible for editing material before publication
section that edits material for publication

Examples

The editorial department is reviewing the final draft of the novel before it goes to print.
She applied for an internship in the editorial department of a major magazine publisher.
Direct communication with the editorial department can help authors clarify submission guidelines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editorial - relating to the preparation of material for publication
