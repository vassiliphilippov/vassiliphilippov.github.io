scheme arch

1. NOUN

/ skiːm ɑːrtʃ / (AmE), / skiːm ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an arch whose height is less than half its width
arch with height less than half its width

Examples

The old bridge was notable for its low scheme arch, which provided a unique silhouette.
When renovating the facade, the architect preserved the original scheme arch above the doorway.
A scheme arch distributes weight differently than a semicircular arch, requiring careful engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: segmental arch - an arch whose curve is a segment of a circle
