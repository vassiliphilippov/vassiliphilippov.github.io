almighty being

1. NOUN

/ ɑlˈmaɪti ˈbiɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒlˈmaɪti ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
a deity or god considered to have unlimited power and authority
a deity with unlimited power

Examples

Many cultures have myths about an almighty being who created the universe.
She prayed every night, believing an almighty being could guide her through life.
Philosophers have long debated the nature of an almighty being and its influence on the world.

Synset Examples

They believed in an almighty being watching over them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess; a divine being
  • Synonym: god - a being believed to have supernatural powers
