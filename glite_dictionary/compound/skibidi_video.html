skibidi video

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɪbɪdi ˈvɪdioʊ / (AmE), / ˈskɪbɪdi ˈvɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
a short, humorous video, especially one in a meme style, inspired by or imitating the 'Skibidi' meme trend
meme-inspired humorous short video
informal slang

Examples

The group spent hours making their own skibidi video to share online.
My younger cousin is obsessed with every new skibidi video that comes out on social media.
That skibidi video went viral because it was so bizarre and funny.

Synset Examples

We laughed while watching the latest skibidi videos.

Etymology

From the viral internet meme 'Skibidi', itself derived from the 2018 Little Big song 'Skibidi'.
