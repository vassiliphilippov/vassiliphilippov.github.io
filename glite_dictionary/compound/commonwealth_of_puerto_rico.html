Commonwealth of Puerto Rico

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑmənˌwɛlθ əv ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənˌwɛlθ əv ˌpwɛətəʊ ˈriːkəʊ / (BrE)
A self-governing territory associated with the United States, occupying the island of Puerto Rico.
self-governing U.S. territory in the Caribbean

Examples

The Commonwealth of Puerto Rico has its own constitution and elected government.
Tourism is a major industry in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
Residents of the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico are U.S. citizens but cannot vote in presidential elections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puerto Rico - an island and unincorporated territory of the U.S. in the Caribbean
