porn merchant

1. NOUN

/ pɔrn ˈmɜrtʃənt / (AmE), / pɔːn ˈmɜːtʃənt / (BrE)
a person who presents, deals in, or sells sexually explicit materials, especially in violation of accepted social standards
seller or presenter of sexually explicit material

Examples

The new law targets any porn merchant who tries to sell explicit material to minors.
Local authorities cracked down on a notorious porn merchant operating in the city center.
After years as a porn merchant, he decided to leave the adult entertainment industry behind.
