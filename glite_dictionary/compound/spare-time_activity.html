spare-time activity

1. NOUN

/ ˌspɛr taɪm ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌspɛə taɪm ækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
an activity that a person does in their free time, especially for enjoyment or relaxation, apart from work or main responsibilities
activity done in free time

Examples

Gardening is her favorite spare-time activity after a long week at work.
Many people choose reading as a relaxing spare-time activity.
He started learning guitar as a spare-time activity to unwind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
