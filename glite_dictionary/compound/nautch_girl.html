nautch girl

1. NOUN

/ nɑtʃ ɡɜrl / (AmE), / nɔːtʃ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female professional dancer in India, especially during the British colonial period, who performed traditional dances for entertainment.
a female professional dancer in India

Examples

During the festival, a famous nautch girl performed classical dances for the audience.
Colonial officers often hired a nautch girl to entertain guests at formal events.
The novel describes the life of a nautch girl in nineteenth-century India.

Etymology

From Hindi 'nāc' (dance) and English 'girl'.
