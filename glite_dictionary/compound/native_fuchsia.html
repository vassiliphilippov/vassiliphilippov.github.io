native fuchsia

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪt̬ɪv ˈfuːʃə / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪv ˈfjuːʃə / (BrE)
an erect deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand, with maroon flowers and growing up to 10 feet tall
New Zealand shrub or tree with maroon flowers

Examples

The native fuchsia attracts native birds in many New Zealand gardens.
During our hike, we spotted a tall native fuchsia covered in maroon blooms.
Conservationists are working to protect the native fuchsia from invasive pests.
