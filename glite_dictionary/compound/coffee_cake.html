coffee cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A cake or sweet bread, often flavored with cinnamon or nuts, typically served with coffee.
a cake served with coffee

Examples

She brought a delicious coffee cake to the brunch.
Would you like a slice of coffee cake with your tea?
The coffee cake was topped with a crumbly streusel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake - a sweet baked food
