continuous receiver watch

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɪn.ju.əs rɪˈsiː.vɚ wɑːtʃ / (AmE), / kənˈtɪn.ju.əs rɪˈsiː.və wɒtʃ / (BrE)
the maintenance of constant monitoring to receive communications or signals of interest by a receiving unit
constant monitoring for received signals

Examples

The operator was responsible for maintaining a continuous receiver watch throughout his shift.
Regulations require that ships at sea keep a continuous receiver watch on emergency frequencies.
Disruptions can occur if a continuous receiver watch is not properly established during critical operations.
