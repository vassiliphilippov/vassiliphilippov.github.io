global economic strategy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡloʊ.bəl iˈkɑː.nɪk ˈstræt.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊ.bəl iˈkɒn.ə.mɪk ˈstræt.ɪ.dʒi / (BrE)
an international plan or approach for managing and developing economic policy across countries or worldwide
worldwide economic policy plan

Examples

Many governments are rethinking their global economic strategy in response to recent financial crises.
China's global economic strategy includes investing heavily in international infrastructure projects.
A successful global economic strategy often relies on cooperation among major world economies.

Synset Examples

Neoliberalism has reshaped global economic strategies significantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economic policy - the course of action adopted by a government regarding its economy
