contraceptive method

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.trəˈsɛp.tɪv ˈmɛθ.əd / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trəˈsɛp.tɪv ˈmɛθ.əd / (BrE)
a technique or device used to prevent pregnancy, such as condoms, intrauterine devices, or birth control pills
technique or device to prevent pregnancy

Examples

She discussed different contraceptive methods with her doctor before making a decision.
Using a reliable contraceptive method is important for family planning.
Many contraceptive methods also help prevent the transmission of certain diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contraception - the deliberate prevention of pregnancy using various methods or devices
  • Antonym: fertility treatment - medical treatment to assist pregnancy
