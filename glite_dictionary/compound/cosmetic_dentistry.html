cosmetic dentistry

1. NOUN

/ kɑzˈmɛt̬ɪk ˈdɛn.tɪs.tri / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɛt.ɪk ˈdɛn.tɪ.stri / (BrE)
The branch of dentistry that focuses on improving the appearance of teeth, gums, and smile.
branch of dentistry improving dental appearance
medical

Examples

She decided to undergo cosmetic dentistry to enhance her smile before her wedding.
Advancements in cosmetic dentistry now allow for natural-looking dental implants.
Many people seek cosmetic dentistry for procedures like teeth whitening and veneers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dentistry - the science or practice of the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases of the teeth

Etymology

From 'cosmetic' (from Greek kosmetikos, relating to adornment) and 'dentistry' (from Latin dens, tooth).
