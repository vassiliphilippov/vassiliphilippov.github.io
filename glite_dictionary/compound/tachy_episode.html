tachy episode

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæki ˌɛpɪˌsoʊd / (AmE), / ˈtæki ˌɛpɪˌsəʊd / (BrE)
A period during which the heart beats unusually quickly; an episode of rapid heartbeat.
episode of rapid heartbeat
medical

Examples

If you notice any tachy episode while resting, you should contact your doctor.
Patients with arrhythmias may experience a tachy episode triggered by stress or exertion.
She wore a monitor for a week to track any tachy episode that might occur.

Synset Examples

Tachy episodes can be concerning if they occur frequently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tachycardia - abnormally fast heart rate
  • Antonym: brady episode - an episode of unusually slow heartbeat

Etymology

From 'tachy-' (short for tachycardia, from Greek takhys 'swift') + 'episode'.
