European sanicle

1. NOUN

/ jʊˈroʊpiən ˈsænɪkəl / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈsanɪkəl / (BrE)
a plant native to Europe and Asia, with white to pale pink flowers, belonging to the genus Sanicula
European and Asian plant with pale flowers

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with European sanicle blooming among the ferns.
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of European sanicle in the region.
The delicate white and pink flowers of European sanicle attract many pollinators in spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanicle - a plant of the genus Sanicula, known for its medicinal uses
