Chinese wistaria

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪniz wɪˈstɛriə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz wɪˈstɑːrɪə / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to China, known for its deep purple, fragrant flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental vine.
climbing plant with deep purple flowers

Examples

The Chinese wistaria bloomed beautifully along the old garden wall this spring.
We planted a young Chinese wistaria to provide shade and color for our pergola.
A vine of Chinese wistaria can quickly cover fences and trellises with its cascading flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wisteria - a genus of flowering vines with long clusters of flowers
