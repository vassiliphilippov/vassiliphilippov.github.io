pole jumper

1. NOUN

/ poʊl ˈdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / pəʊl ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
an athlete who jumps over a high crossbar using a long pole, especially in the sport of pole vault
athlete using a pole to jump over a high bar

Examples

The pole jumper cleared a new personal record at the state championships.
As a pole jumper, she trains every day to improve her technique and height.
The crowd cheered as the pole jumper soared gracefully over the bar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pole vaulter - an athlete who competes in pole vault
