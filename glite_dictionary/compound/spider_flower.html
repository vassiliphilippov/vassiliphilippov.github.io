spider flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪdɚ ˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a tall annual plant native to South America, cultivated for its long-lasting, spider-shaped white to pink-purple flowers
tall annual plant with spider-shaped flowers

Examples

The spider flower added striking color to the summer garden with its pink blooms.
Gardeners often choose the spider flower for its unusual, spidery petals and long blooming season.
The tall stems of the spider flower sway gently in the warm breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleome - genus of flowering plants to which the spider flower belongs
