tufted pansy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʌftɪd ˈpænzi / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd ˈpænzi / (BrE)
A European viola plant with an unusually long corolla spur, known for its attractive flowers.
European viola with a long corolla spur

Examples

The tufted pansy adds a splash of color to many European gardens.
She planted tufted pansies along the garden path for their unusual flower shape.
Horticulturists admire the tufted pansy for its long corolla spur and vibrant petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pansy - a garden plant with brightly colored flowers, of the genus Viola
