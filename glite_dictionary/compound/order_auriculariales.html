order Auriculariales

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔrdər ɔˌrɪkjəˌlɛr.iˈeɪliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ɔːˌrɪkjʊˌlɛəriˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of fungi, coextensive with the family Auriculariaceae, sometimes classified within the order Tremellales
order of fungi in the Auriculariaceae family

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species in the order Auriculariales during their rainforest expedition.
Many edible jelly fungi, such as wood ear mushrooms, belong to the order Auriculariales.
The classification of the order Auriculariales has changed as molecular studies have provided new insights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Auriculariaceae - a family of fungi within the order Auriculariales
  • Degree Higher: order Tremellales - a taxonomic order of fungi, closely related to Auriculariales

Etymology

From New Latin 'Auriculariales', named after the type genus Auricularia.
