practice game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpræk.tɪs ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪs ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game played to improve skills or test strategies, whose result does not affect official standings
non-official preparatory sports match

Examples

The basketball team scheduled a practice game to prepare for the upcoming tournament.
Coaches often use a practice game to try out new players in different positions.
Although they lost the practice game, the players learned valuable strategies for the regular season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrimmage - an informal game or practice match, especially in American football
