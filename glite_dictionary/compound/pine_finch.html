pine finch

1. NOUN

/ paɪn fɪntʃ / (AmE), / paɪn fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small finch of North American coniferous forests, also known as the pine siskin
a small North American finch

Examples

A pine finch perched on the branch, blending in with the evergreen needles.
We spotted a flock of pine finches feeding on sunflower seeds in the backyard.
The pine finch is often confused with other finches due to its streaked plumage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pine siskin - a small brown-streaked finch found in North America
