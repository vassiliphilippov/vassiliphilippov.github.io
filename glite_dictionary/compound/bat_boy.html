bat boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbæt bɔɪ / (BrE)
A boy whose job is to take care of bats and other equipment for a baseball team.
boy assisting a baseball team

Examples

The bat boy quickly handed a new bat to the player at the plate.
Every major league team has at least one bat boy during games.
As a teenager, he worked as a bat boy for the local minor league team.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batgirl - a girl who assists a baseball team in a similar role as a bat boy
