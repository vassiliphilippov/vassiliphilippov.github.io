market research

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkɪt rɪˌsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt rɪˌsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the process of collecting and analyzing information about consumers' needs and preferences, and the movement of goods or services from producer to consumer
analysis of consumers and markets

Examples

The company invested heavily in market research to better understand its customers' needs.
Accurate market research helped them decide where to launch their new product.
Before expanding internationally, they conducted thorough market research on local buying habits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marketing - the promotion and selling of products or services
