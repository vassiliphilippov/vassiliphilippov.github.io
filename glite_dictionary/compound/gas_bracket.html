gas bracket

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs ˈbrækət / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
a pipe fixture with one or more burners, attached to a wall, used for gas lighting
wall-mounted gas lighting fixture

Examples

The old house still had a gas bracket in the hallway, though it was no longer in use.
During renovations, they discovered a hidden gas bracket behind a wooden panel.
Antique dealers often look for original gas brackets from Victorian homes.
