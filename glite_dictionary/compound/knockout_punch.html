knockout punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːk.aʊt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈnɒk.aʊt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
a punch that hits with enough force to make an opponent unable to continue, typically ending a boxing match
punch that ends a boxing match

Examples

The challenger delivered a knockout punch in the final round, securing his victory.
That right hook was a knockout punch that left the crowd stunned.
He trained for months to perfect his knockout punch before the championship bout.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knockout - a blow or event in boxing causing an opponent to be unable to continue
