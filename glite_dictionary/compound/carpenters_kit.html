carpenter's kit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːr.pən.tɚz kɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəntəz kɪt / (BrE)
a set of tools used by a carpenter for woodworking tasks
set of tools for a carpenter

Examples

He opened his carpenter's kit and selected a hammer for the job.
A good carpenter's kit should include a saw, measuring tape, and chisel.
After years of work, his old carpenter's kit still served him well.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool kit - a set of tools kept together for a specific purpose
