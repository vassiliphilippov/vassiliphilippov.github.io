blatant ecopornography

1. NOUN

/ ˈbleɪtənt ˌiːkoʊˌpɔrnɑːˈɡræfi / (AmE), / ˈbleɪtənt ˌiːkəʊˌpɔːnɒˈɡræfi / (BrE)
overtly exploitative or misleading environmental advertising designed to appeal to ecological concerns while lacking genuine commitment
exploitative 'green' advertising

Examples

The commercial's shallow use of environmental themes was dismissed as blatant ecopornography by critics.
Many activists are calling out brands for their blatant ecopornography during Earth Day campaigns.
Her article exposes the blatant ecopornography that often accompanies corporate sustainability reports.

Synset Examples

She described the ad campaign as blatant ecopornography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greenwashing - the practice of making misleading claims about environmental benefits

Etymology

Blend of 'eco-' (relating to ecology) and 'pornography,' used figuratively to criticize insincere environmental appeals.
