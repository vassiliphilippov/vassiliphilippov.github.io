dress suit

1. NOUN

/ drɛs suːt / (AmE), / drɛs sjuːt / (BrE)
a formal outfit for men consisting of a tailcoat and trousers, typically worn for evening occasions
men's formal evening attire

Examples

He wore a dress suit to the charity ball last night.
Only guests in a dress suit or evening gown were allowed entry to the gala.
His dress suit included a black tailcoat, white shirt, and bow tie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuxedo - a formal men's jacket and trousers for evening wear
  • Antonym: morning coat - a formal men's coat for daytime events
