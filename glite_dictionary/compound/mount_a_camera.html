to mount a camera

1. VERB

/ maʊnt ə ˈkæmɚ / (AmE), / maʊnt ə ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
to attach a camera securely to a support or fixture
attach a camera to a support

Examples

Many wildlife photographers mount a camera on a tree to capture animal behavior.
Before the interview, the technician will mount a camera above the stage to film the event.
Make sure you mount a camera securely so it doesn't shake during recording.

Synset Examples

He decided to mount his camera on the tripod.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to install - to place or fix equipment in position for use
