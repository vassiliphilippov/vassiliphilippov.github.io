heartfelt speech

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrtˌfɛlt spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtfɛlt spiːtʃ / (BrE)
a spoken address or expression that is sincere and full of deep emotion
sincere, deeply emotional speech

Examples

She delivered a heartfelt speech at her friend's wedding, moving everyone to tears.
The mayor's heartfelt speech addressed the concerns of the grieving community.
After winning the award, he gave a heartfelt speech thanking his family and supporters.

Synset Examples

Her heartfelt speech could easily make the bald man cry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassioned speech - speech delivered with strong emotion or passion
  • Antonym: impersonal speech - speech that is detached or lacking emotion
