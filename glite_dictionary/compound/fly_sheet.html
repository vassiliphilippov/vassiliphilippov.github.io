fly sheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪ ˌʃit / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ ˌʃiːt / (BrE)
a piece of canvas or similar material attached to a tent entrance, used as a flap that can be drawn back to allow entry
tent entrance flap of canvas or fabric

Examples

He held open the fly sheet so we could slip quietly into the tent.
The fly sheet was flapping in the wind after the storm passed.
Make sure to fasten the fly sheet tightly to keep out insects and rain.
