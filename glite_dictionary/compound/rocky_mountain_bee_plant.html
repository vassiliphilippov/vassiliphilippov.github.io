Rocky Mountain bee plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑki ˈmaʊntən biː plænt / (AmE), / ˈrɒki ˈmaʊntɪn biː plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant native to western North America, recognized for its trifoliate leaves and white or pink spider-shaped flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental.
plant with white or pink spider-shaped flowers

Examples

Many gardeners choose to plant Rocky Mountain bee plant to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
The Rocky Mountain bee plant can brighten any landscape with its vibrant pink flowers.
I noticed a cluster of Rocky Mountain bee plant growing wild along the hiking trail.
