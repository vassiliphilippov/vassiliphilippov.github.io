clammy chickweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæmi ˈtʃɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈklæmi ˈtʃɪkwiːd / (BrE)
a common name for several plants related to the common chickweed, especially those with sticky or moist foliage
a plant related to common chickweed

Examples

The leaves of the clammy chickweed often feel sticky to the touch.
Gardeners sometimes mistake clammy chickweed for the more common chickweed species.
Hikers may encounter patches of clammy chickweed growing in moist, shady areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: common chickweed - a low-growing annual plant with small white flowers, also called Stellaria media
