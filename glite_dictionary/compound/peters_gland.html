Peter's gland

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtərz ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtəz ɡlænd / (BrE)
any of several lymph nodes located in the intestinal wall near the junction of the ileum and colon
lymph nodes in intestinal wall near ileum and colon
medical

Examples

During surgery, the doctor found swelling near the Peter's gland in the lower intestine.
Some diseases can affect the Peter's glands and cause abdominal discomfort.
Inflammation of a Peter's gland is rare but can indicate an underlying infection.
