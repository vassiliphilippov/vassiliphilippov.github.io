libertard approach

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbərˌtɑrd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɪbətɑːd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A derogatory slang term for a policy or attitude associated with libertarianism that is considered by the speaker to be foolish or misguided.
derogatory term for a misguided libertarian policy
offensive slang

Examples

Many critics dismissed the reform as just another libertard approach that ignored real-world consequences.
His opponents accused him of taking a libertard approach to healthcare regulation.
The commentator's article harshly criticized what he saw as a libertard approach to economic policy.

Synset Examples

He called the policy a libertard approach to governance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libertarian - a person who advocates minimal state intervention in the lives of citizens
  • Antonym: authoritarian approach - favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority

Etymology

Blend of 'libertarian' and the offensive suffix '-tard' (from 'retard').
