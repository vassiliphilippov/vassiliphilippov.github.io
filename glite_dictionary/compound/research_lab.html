research lab

1. NOUN

/ rəˈsɝtʃ læb / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ læb / (BrE)
a place or facility where scientific research and experiments are carried out
place for scientific research

Examples

She works in a research lab studying the effects of new medicines.
The university opened a new research lab dedicated to renewable energy.
Safety protocols must be followed at all times in the research lab.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments, research, or teaching
