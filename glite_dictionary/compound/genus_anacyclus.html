genus Anacyclus

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːnəs əˈneɪsɪkləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əˈnæsɪkləs / (BrE)
A genus of plants in the daisy family, known for including Spanish pellitory.
a plant genus in the daisy family

Examples

Various herbs belonging to the genus Anacyclus are native to the Mediterranean region.
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of the genus Anacyclus for centuries.
The genus Anacyclus includes species famous for their use in traditional herbal remedies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Anacyclus - the genus Anacyclus, a group of flowering plants

Etymology

From Latin *Anacyclus*, from Greek *Anakyklos*, a name for Spanish pellitory.
