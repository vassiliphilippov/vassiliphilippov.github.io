section hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃən hænd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃən hand / (BrE)
a worker employed as part of a crew assigned to maintain a section of a railroad track
railroad track maintenance worker

Examples

The section hand reported for duty every morning to inspect the rails.
In the early 1900s, being a section hand was a tough but steady job.
A photograph of a section hand repairing tracks hangs in the old railway station.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trackman - a person who maintains railroad tracks
  • Synonym: section gang - a crew assigned to maintain a section of railroad track
