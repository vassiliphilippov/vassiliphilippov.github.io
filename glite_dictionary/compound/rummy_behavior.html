rummy behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌmi bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈrʌmi bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
unusual or quirky actions; behavior that seems odd or eccentric
quirky or eccentric actions
informal

Examples

Her rummy behavior at the meeting caught everyone's attention.
The child's rummy behavior made her teachers both smile and worry.
People were puzzled by his rummy behavior during the ceremony.

Synset Examples

Her rummy behavior made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric behavior - unconventional or slightly strange behavior
  • Antonym: normal behavior - typical or usual behavior, not odd

Etymology

Probably from 'rummy' (slang for odd or strange), itself of unknown origin, possibly related to 'rum' (1800s British slang for odd).
