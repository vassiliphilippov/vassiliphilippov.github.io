government man

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnmənt mæn / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvəmənt mæn / (BrE)
a male law-enforcement agent of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), especially as referred to informally or historically
male FBI law-enforcement agent

Examples

Several witnesses saw a government man questioning people outside the bank.
The old movies often feature a government man on the trail of notorious criminals.
He suspected that his neighbor was, in fact, a government man keeping a close watch.

  • Synonym: G-man - special agent of the FBI, government man
