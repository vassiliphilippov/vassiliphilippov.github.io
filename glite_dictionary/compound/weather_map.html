weather map

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðər mæp / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə mæp / (BrE)
a map that displays meteorological data such as temperature, precipitation, and atmospheric pressure for a specific time across a region
map showing meteorological data

Examples

The meteorologist pointed to the weather map to explain the approaching storm.
Students learned how to read a weather map in their science class.
By looking at the weather map, she could plan the best days for their outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synoptic chart - a chart showing the meteorological conditions over a wide area at a particular time
