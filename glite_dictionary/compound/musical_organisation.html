musical organisation

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˌɔːɹɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization consisting of musicians who perform together as a group
group of musicians performing together
british

Examples

She joined a local musical organisation to pursue her passion for singing and performing.
The musical organisation held a concert to raise funds for charity last weekend.
A well-managed musical organisation can help young musicians develop their talents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestra - a large group of musicians who play together, especially classical music
  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians who play popular music together
