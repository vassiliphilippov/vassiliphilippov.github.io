Lassen Volcanic National Park

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæs.ən voʊlˈkæn.ɪk ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈlæs.ən vɒlˈkæn.ɪk ˌnæʃ.ən.əl pɑːk / (BrE)
a national park in northern California known for its volcanic landscapes, including mountains, lakes, and hot springs
national park in northern California

Examples

We spent our summer vacation hiking in Lassen Volcanic National Park and exploring its geothermal features.
The bubbling mud pots and steaming fumaroles make Lassen Volcanic National Park a fascinating place for geology enthusiasts.
Wildflowers bloom throughout Lassen Volcanic National Park each spring, attracting visitors from across California.

Etymology

Named after Mount Lassen, itself named for Peter Lassen, a Danish-American pioneer.
