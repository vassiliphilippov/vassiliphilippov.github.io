cigarette lighter

1. NOUN

/ sɪɡəˌrɛt ˈlaɪtər / (AmE), / sɪɡəˌrɛt ˈlaɪtə / (BrE)
A small device used to create a flame for lighting cigarettes, cigars, or similar items.
device for lighting cigarettes

Examples

He reached into his pocket and pulled out a cigarette lighter to ignite the candle.
She bought her father a silver cigarette lighter for his birthday.
The sign at the gas station warned customers not to use a cigarette lighter near the pumps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighter - a device producing a small flame
