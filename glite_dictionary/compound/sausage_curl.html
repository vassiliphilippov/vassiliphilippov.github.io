sausage curl

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔsɪdʒ kɝl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ kɜːl / (BrE)
a thick, cylindrical curl of hair resembling a sausage, typically styled as a ringlet
a thick sausage-shaped hair curl

Examples

She wore her hair in elaborate sausage curls for the Victorian-themed party.
The child’s golden sausage curl bounced with every step she took.
He admired the portrait, noting the precise sausage curls framing the subject’s face.
