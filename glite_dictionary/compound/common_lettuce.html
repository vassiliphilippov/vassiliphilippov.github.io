common lettuce

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmən ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A widely cultivated leafy garden plant (Lactuca sativa) with succulent leaves, often used in salads.
a widely grown salad plant

Examples

She bought a fresh head of common lettuce at the farmer's market for her salad.
The common lettuce grows best in cool, well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.
Gardeners often choose common lettuce for its crisp texture and mild flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lettuce - a leafy salad vegetable, Lactuca sativa
