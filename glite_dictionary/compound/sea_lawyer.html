sea lawyer

1. NOUN

/ si ˈlɔjər / (AmE), / si ˈlɔːjə / (BrE)
a sailor who is argumentative and prone to disputing orders, often citing rules or regulations in a contentious way
an argumentative or contentious seaman
slang

Examples

The captain grew tired of the sea lawyer constantly arguing about every order given.
Every crew has at least one sea lawyer who knows all the rules but never follows them.
He quickly gained a reputation as a sea lawyer for questioning the officers at every turn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person who habitually causes difficulty or arguments
  • Antonym: peacemaker - a person who helps others resolve conflicts and keeps peace
