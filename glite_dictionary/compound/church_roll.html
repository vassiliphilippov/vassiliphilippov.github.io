church roll

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ roʊl / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ rəʊl / (BrE)
a list of the members belonging to a particular church
list of church members

Examples

The pastor updated the church roll to include new members after the baptism ceremony.
Every year, the secretary checks the church roll for accuracy and makes necessary corrections.
Many families have been listed on the church roll for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: membership roll - list of members in any organization
