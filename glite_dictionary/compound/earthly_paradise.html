earthly paradise

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːθli ˌpærəˈdaɪs / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli ˌpærəˈdaɪs / (BrE)
a place on earth that is ideal, beautiful, and seemingly perfect in every way; often associated with delight and happiness
a perfect, idyllic place on earth

Examples

Many writers have described the secluded valley as an earthly paradise.
They hoped to turn the old farmhouse into an earthly paradise of peace and tranquility.
Tourists see the remote beach as something close to an earthly paradise.

Synset Examples

In her garden, she created an earthly paradise filled with flowers.
The tropical island felt like an earthly paradise.
They dreamed of building their earthly paradise in the mountains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - a perfect place or state of happiness
  • Synonym: heaven on earth - a state or place of perfect happiness on earth
