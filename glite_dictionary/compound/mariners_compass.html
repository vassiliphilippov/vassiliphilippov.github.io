mariner's compass

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɪnərz ˈkʌmpəs / (AmE), / məˈrɪnəz ˈkʌmpəs / (BrE)
a magnetic compass with a circular card that rotates so that its north point aligns with magnetic north, used especially by sailors for navigation
magnetic compass used in navigation at sea

Examples

The captain checked the mariner's compass before changing the ship's course.
Antique dealers often search for a well-preserved mariner's compass from the 18th century.
Without a mariner's compass, early explorers would have struggled to cross the open ocean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compass - an instrument for determining direction, using a magnetic needle
