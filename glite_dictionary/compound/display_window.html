display window

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspleɪ ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a large window at the front of a shop, used for showing goods to attract customers
shop window for displaying goods

Examples

The new dresses are featured in the display window to attract more customers.
She stopped to admire the artwork showcased in the gallery's display window.
During the holidays, the department store creates elaborate scenes in its display window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shop window - a window at the front of a shop displaying goods for sale
