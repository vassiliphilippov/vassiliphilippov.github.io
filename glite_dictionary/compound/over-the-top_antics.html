over-the-top antics

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvɚ ðə ˈtɑp ˈæntɪks / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈtɒp ˈæntɪks / (BrE)
exaggerated or excessively theatrical behavior, often intended to attract attention or amuse others
exaggerated or excessive behavior

Examples

His over-the-top antics at the party had everyone talking the next day.
Some comedians rely on over-the-top antics rather than subtle humor to get laughs.
The actor was criticized for his over-the-top antics during the award ceremony.

The group laughed at his over-the-top fagmo antics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antics - amusing, foolish, or capricious actions
