ballpoint pen

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌpɔɪnt pɛn / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlpɔɪnt pɛn / (BrE)
A pen that uses a small rotating metal ball at its tip to transfer ink onto paper.
pen with a small rotating ball tip

Examples

She wrote her name neatly with a ballpoint pen on the application form.
I always carry a ballpoint pen in my bag for taking notes.
The ballpoint pen ran out of ink just before he finished the letter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rollerball pen - pen using a ball tip but with water-based ink
  • Antonym: fountain pen - pen with a metal nib, using liquid ink

Etymology

From 'ballpoint' (referring to the rotating ball tip) + 'pen'.
