cigar box

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr bɑks / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː bɒks / (BrE)
a box designed for storing or holding cigars
box for storing cigars

Examples

He kept his grandfather's letters safe in an old cigar box on the shelf.
The artisan crafted a guitar from a discarded cigar box and some wire.
She placed the collection of rare coins into a velvet-lined cigar box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humidor - a container for keeping cigars or tobacco moist
