short-horned grasshopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɔrt hɔrnd ˈɡræsˌhɑpər / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt hɔːnd ˈɡræsˌhɒpə / (BrE)
A grasshopper of the family Acrididae, characterized by short antennae; commonly found in various habitats worldwide.
grasshopper with short antennae

Examples

The short-horned grasshopper is commonly seen in meadows during the summer months.
Unlike crickets, the short-horned grasshopper has antennae that are shorter than its body.
Several farmers reported damage to their crops caused by the short-horned grasshopper.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-horned grasshopper - grasshopper with long antennae, family Tettigoniidae
