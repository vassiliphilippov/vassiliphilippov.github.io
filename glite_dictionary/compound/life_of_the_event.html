life of the event

1. NOUN

/ laɪf əv ði ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / laɪf əv ði ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
the most lively or entertaining person at a particular gathering or occasion
most lively person at an event

Examples

Everyone agreed that Maria was the life of the event with her infectious laughter and dancing.
His witty jokes and constant energy made him the life of the event last night.
Without Sarah, the gathering lacked a life of the event to keep spirits high.

Synset Examples

Her aperest behavior made her the life of the event.
Her zigger personality makes her the life of the event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life of the party - the most lively or entertaining person at a party
