heart cherry

1. NOUN

/ hɑrt ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A variety of sweet cherry with juicy, heart-shaped fruit, often cultivated for eating.
a type of sweet, heart-shaped cherry

Examples

The orchard was full of ripe heart cherries ready to be picked.
She topped her cake with a handful of fresh heart cherries.
Many people prefer the sweet taste of heart cherry over sour varieties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet cherry - a type of cherry with sweet edible fruits
