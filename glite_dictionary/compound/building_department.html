building department

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪl.dɪŋ dɪˌpɑrt.mənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.dɪŋ dɪˌpɑːt.mənt / (BrE)
the division or section of an organization responsible for constructing, maintaining, and repairing its physical premises or facilities
division managing construction and maintenance

Examples

The building department oversees all renovations and repairs in the office complex.
If you have a maintenance request, please contact the building department directly.
The building department approved the plans for the new research facility.
