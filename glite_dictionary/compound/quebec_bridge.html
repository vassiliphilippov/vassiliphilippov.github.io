Quebec Bridge

1. PROPN

/ kwəˈbɛk brɪdʒ / (AmE), / kwɪˈbɛk brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a large cantilever bridge spanning the Saint Lawrence River near Quebec City, Canada
cantilever bridge in Quebec, Canada

Examples

The collapse of the Quebec Bridge during construction led to major engineering reforms.
The Quebec Bridge is considered a marvel of early 20th-century civil engineering.
Tourists often visit the Quebec Bridge for its impressive design and historical significance.

Etymology

Named after Quebec City, near which it is located.
