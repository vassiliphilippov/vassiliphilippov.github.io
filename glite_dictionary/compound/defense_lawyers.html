defense lawyers

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈlɔjɚz / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˈlɔːjəz / (BrE)
lawyers who represent the accused in a court of law
lawyers representing the accused in court

Examples

The defense lawyers argued that the evidence was not admissible in court.
After a long cross-examination, the defense lawyers rested their case.
Her defense lawyer was known for winning difficult cases.

Synset Examples

the defense called for a mistrial

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defense attorney - lawyer representing the accused in a court case
  • Antonym: prosecutor - lawyer representing the state or prosecution in a criminal case
