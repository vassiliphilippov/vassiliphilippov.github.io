an hour and a half

1. NOUN

/ ən ˈaʊɚ ənd ə hæf / (AmE), / ən ˈaʊə ənd ə hɑːf / (BrE)
A period of time lasting for 90 minutes.
duration of 90 minutes

Examples

The meeting lasted an hour and a half and covered all agenda items.
It takes me an hour and a half to get to work every morning.
She practiced the piano for an hour and a half before dinner.

Synset Examples

I waited for an hour and a half for you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ninety minutes - a time interval lasting 90 minutes
