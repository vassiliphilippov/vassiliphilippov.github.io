multicultural performance

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl pərˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
a performance that features or represents aspects of multiple cultures, often through music, dance, or drama
performance representing multiple cultures

Examples

The city hosted a multicultural performance to celebrate its diversity.
Students participated in a multicultural performance during International Week at the school.
The multicultural performance included dances from Africa, Asia, and Latin America.

Synset Examples

The festival showcased multicultural performances and cuisines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performance - an act of staging or presenting a play, concert, or other form of entertainment
