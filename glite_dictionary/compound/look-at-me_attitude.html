look-at-me attitude

1. NOUN

/ ˌlʊk æt ˈmiː ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˌlʊk æt ˈmiː ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a manner or behavior intended to attract attention to oneself
behavior meant to attract attention

Examples

Her look-at-me attitude made her the center of attention in every room.
He was criticized for his look-at-me attitude during the meeting, which distracted from the discussion.
People with a look-at-me attitude often crave recognition and praise from others.

Synset Examples

Her look-at-me attitude made her the center of attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention-seeking - seeking to attract attention or publicity
