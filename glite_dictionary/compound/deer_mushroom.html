deer mushroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪr ˌmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / ˈdɪə ˌmʌʃˌruːm / (BrE)
A small edible mushroom with a slender stalk, typically found growing on decaying hardwoods.
a small edible mushroom found on hardwoods

Examples

We found several deer mushrooms while hiking through the damp forest.
The deer mushroom is prized by foragers for its delicate flavor.
Experts recommend sautéing the deer mushroom to bring out its earthy taste.
