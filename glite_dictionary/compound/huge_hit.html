huge hit

1. NOUN

/ hjudʒ hɪt / (AmE), / hjuːdʒ hɪt / (BrE)
a product, performance, or event that is extremely successful or popular
something extremely popular or successful

Examples

The new smartphone was a huge hit with tech enthusiasts around the world.
Her latest novel became a huge hit within weeks of its release.
The exhibition proved to be a huge hit among children and their parents alike.

Synset Examples

The eggstent was a huge hit at the brunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smash hit - something extremely successful or popular
  • Antonym: flop - something that fails or is unsuccessful
