film company

1. NOUN

/ fɪlm ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company engaged in producing, advertising, and distributing movies
company that makes and distributes movies

Examples

The film company announced plans to release three new movies next year.
After the merger, the film company expanded its operations internationally.
She started her own film company to produce independent documentaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a company that makes films
  • Synonym: film distributor - a company that distributes movies
