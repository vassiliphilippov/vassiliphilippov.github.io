luggage van

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌɡɪdʒ væn / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ van / (BrE)
a railway car used for carrying passengers' luggage
railway car for passengers' luggage

Examples

The train's luggage van was packed with suitcases and parcels.
Please deposit your heavy bags in the luggage van before finding your seat.
Porters quickly loaded the trunks into the luggage van as the passengers boarded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baggage car - a railway car for carrying luggage (American English term)
