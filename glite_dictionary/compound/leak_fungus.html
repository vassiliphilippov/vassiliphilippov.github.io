leak fungus

1. NOUN

/ liːk ˈfʌŋ.ɡəs / (AmE), / liːk ˈfʌŋ.ɡəs / (BrE)
A fungus that causes soft watery rot in fruits and vegetables and dry rot rings around the roots of sweet potatoes.
fungus causing rot in produce

Examples

The crops were ruined by a severe outbreak of leak fungus after heavy rains.
Farmers must store sweet potatoes carefully to prevent leak fungus from developing.
Leak fungus can spread rapidly in humid environments, causing significant postharvest losses.
