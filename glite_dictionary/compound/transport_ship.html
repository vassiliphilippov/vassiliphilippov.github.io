transport ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrænspɔrt ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtrænspɔːt ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship designed or used for carrying soldiers or military equipment
military ship for carrying troops/equipment

Examples

The army loaded tanks onto the transport ship before the mission began.
During the war, several transport ships crossed the ocean carrying vital supplies.
They watched a transport ship dock at the military harbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troopship - a ship designed to transport soldiers
