pubic hair

1. NOUN

/ ˈpjuːbɪk hɛr / (AmE), / ˈpjuːbɪk heə / (BrE)
The hair that grows in the area around the genitals after puberty.
hair in the genital area

Examples

Some people choose to remove their pubic hair for personal or cultural reasons.
The growth of pubic hair usually begins during puberty.
Wearing a swimsuit, she carefully checked that her pubic hair wasn't visible.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: body hair - hair growing on any part of the human body

Etymology

From Latin 'pubes' (the hair covering the lower abdomen at puberty) + English 'hair'.
