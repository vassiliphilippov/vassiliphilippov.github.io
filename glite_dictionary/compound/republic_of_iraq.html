Republic of Iraq

1. PROPN

/ ɹəˈpʌblɪk əv aɪˈɹæk / (AmE), / rɪˌpʌblɪk əv aɪˈræk / (BrE)
The official name of Iraq, a country in the Middle East in western Asia, historically known as Mesopotamia.
official name of Iraq (country)

Examples

The official documents were stamped with the seal of the Republic of Iraq.
She traveled to the Republic of Iraq to study Mesopotamian history.
Diplomatic relations were established between Canada and the Republic of Iraq in the 20th century.

Related Terms
