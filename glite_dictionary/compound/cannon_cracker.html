cannon cracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.ɪn ˌkræk.er / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ən ˌkræk.ə / (BrE)
A type of large firecracker designed to produce a loud explosion.
large firecracker

Examples

The children watched in awe as the cannon cracker exploded with a deafening bang.
He bought a huge cannon cracker for the festival fireworks display.
Local laws prohibit the use of cannon crackers because of their power and loud noise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firecracker - an explosive device for making noise, especially at celebrations
