interior decorator

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɪriər ˈdɛkəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə ˈdɛkəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose profession is planning and designing the decoration and furnishings of the inside of buildings, especially homes and offices.
person who decorates interiors professionally

Examples

The couple hired an interior decorator to give their home a modern and stylish look.
As an interior decorator, she loves working with color schemes and fabrics.
The office's new appearance was the work of a talented interior decorator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior designer - a person who plans the layout and functional design of interior spaces
