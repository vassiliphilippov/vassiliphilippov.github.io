moment of joy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊmənt əv dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈməʊmənt əv dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
a brief period or instance of happiness
a short period of happiness

Examples

Even a difficult life will have its moments of joy.
She cherished every moment of joy with her family during the holidays.
In tough times, it's important to remember each moment of joy.

Synset Examples

Even the worst days can have its moments of joy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: moment of sorrow - a brief period or instance of sadness
