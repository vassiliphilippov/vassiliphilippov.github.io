xerox copy

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɪrɑks ˌkɑpi / (AmE), / ˈzɪərɒks ˌkɒpi / (BrE)
a copy of a document or image produced using a xerographic copying machine, especially a photocopier
copy made with a photocopier

Examples

She handed me a xerox copy of the report for my records.
Please make sure you submit a xerox copy of your passport along with the application.
The xerox copy was barely legible, so he asked for the original.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photocopy - a copy made using a photocopier

Etymology

From 'Xerox', a trademark based on Greek 'xeros' (dry), referring to the dry process of xerography, and 'copy'.
