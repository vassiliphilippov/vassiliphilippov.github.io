pikey behavior

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪki bəˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪki bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
offensive behavior stereotypically attributed to certain travelling communities, often implying criminality or lack of social norms (derogatory and offensive)
offensive stereotype of traveler behavior
offensive slang

Examples

The council received complaints about pikey behavior near the fairground.
He was accused of pikey behavior after vandalizing the property.
Some newspapers have been criticized for using the term pikey behavior in their coverage.

Synset Examples

He warned us about the pikey behavior in that area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pikey - offensive slang term for a member of the traveller community

Etymology

From 'pikey', a derogatory slang term, possibly from 'pike' (road), referring to travelers; usage considered offensive.
