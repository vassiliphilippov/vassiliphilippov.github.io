tea biscuit

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈbɪskɪt / (AmE), / tiː ˈbɪskɪt / (BrE)
A flat, lightly sweet biscuit or cookie, often served with tea.
flat, mildly sweet biscuit or cookie

Examples

She served freshly baked tea biscuits with raspberry jam for the afternoon guests.
A warm cup of Earl Grey and a tea biscuit make the perfect snack.
For the picnic, I packed a tin of homemade tea biscuits to share.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biscuit - A small baked unleavened cake, crisp and flat
  • Synonym: cookie - A small sweet biscuit
