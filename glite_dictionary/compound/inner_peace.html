inner peace

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnɚ pis / (AmE), / ˈɪnə piːs / (BrE)
a state of mental and emotional calm, free from stress or worry, often achieved through personal growth or spiritual practice
state of mental and emotional calm

Examples

After years of meditation, she finally discovered inner peace.
Many people travel in search of inner peace and self-discovery.
Practicing gratitude daily can help you maintain inner peace during challenging times.

Synset Examples

Finding inner peace is a journey, like Buddha taught.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serenity - the state of being calm, peaceful, and untroubled
  • Synonym: tranquility - a state of peace and quiet
  • Antonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
