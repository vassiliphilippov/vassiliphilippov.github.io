lord of gingerbread

1. NOUN

/ lɔrd əv ˈdʒɪndʒərˌbrɛd / (AmE), / lɔːd əv ˈdʒɪndʒəˌbrɛd / (BrE)
a playful or honorary title for someone considered to be the best maker of gingerbread, or an expert in creating gingerbread
expert or champion gingerbread maker

Examples

She is known as the lord of gingerbread at the annual holiday bake-off.
After winning three years in a row, he was undisputed lord of gingerbread.
Their daughter strives every year to become the family's lord of gingerbread.

Synset Examples

The bakery crowns its best creations the lord of gingerbread.
He fancied himself the lord of gingerbread at the festival.
Every Christmas, she becomes the lord of gingerbread in her kitchen.
