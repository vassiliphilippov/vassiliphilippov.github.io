reconnaissance vehicle

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɑnəsəns ˌviːɪkəl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒnɪsəns ˌviːɪkəl / (BrE)
a fast, lightly armored military vehicle with four-wheel drive, designed for scouting and observation missions, often with an open top
fast armored military vehicle for scouting

Examples

The army deployed a reconnaissance vehicle ahead of the main convoy to scout for enemy positions.
During the exercise, the squad traveled in a reconnaissance vehicle to gather information about the terrain.
The reconnaissance vehicle was equipped with advanced sensors to monitor movement along the border.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scout car - lightly armored vehicle used for reconnaissance

Etymology

From French 'reconnaissance' (recognition, exploration) + English 'vehicle'.
