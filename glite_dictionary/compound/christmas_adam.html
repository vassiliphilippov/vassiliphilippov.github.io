Christmas Adam

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs·məs ˈæd.əm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs ˈæd.əm / (BrE)
an informal term for the day before Christmas Eve, celebrated by some as a lighthearted counterpart to Christmas Eve
the day before Christmas Eve
informal

Examples

Every year, my family bakes cookies together on Christmas Adam.
Some friends get together for a movie marathon on Christmas Adam.
We always clean the house and prepare decorations on Christmas Adam before the big celebrations.

Synset Examples

Christmas Adam is a fun tradition in our home.
On Christmas Adam, we exchange small gifts.
We always celebrate Christmas Adam with a family dinner.

Etymology

A play on 'Christmas Eve', referencing the biblical order of Adam before Eve.
