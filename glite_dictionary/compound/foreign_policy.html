foreign policy

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrən ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪn ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
A government's strategy in dealing with other countries and managing its international relations.
a government's strategy towards other countries

Examples

The president outlined his administration's foreign policy priorities in the speech.
Many voters are concerned about how foreign policy decisions affect national security.
China's new foreign policy focuses on strengthening economic partnerships.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: domestic policy - a government's strategy in dealing with internal affairs
