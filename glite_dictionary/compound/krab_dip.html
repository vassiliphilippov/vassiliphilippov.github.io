krab dip

1. NOUN

/ kræb dɪp / (AmE), / kræb dɪp / (BrE)
A creamy dip made with imitation crab meat, often served as a party appetizer.
dip made with imitation crab meat

Examples

I brought my favorite krab dip to the potluck, and everyone loved it.
This recipe calls for cream cheese, green onions, and flaked krab dip.
Store-bought krab dip is a quick solution when you need an easy appetizer.

Synset Examples

They served a spicy krab dip at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crab dip - a dip made with real crab meat

Etymology

‘Krab’ is a commercial spelling indicating imitation crab, made from processed fish.
