green lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrin ˌlɪzɚd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌlɪzəd / (BrE)
a common Eurasian lizard, typically about a foot long, known for its bright green coloration
Eurasian bright green lizard species

Examples

A green lizard basked in the sunlight on the garden wall.
We spotted a green lizard during our hike in southern France.
The green lizard is well-camouflaged among the leaves.
