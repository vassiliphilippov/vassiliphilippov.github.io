dirty look

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti lʊk / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti lʊk / (BrE)
a look or glance expressing disapproval, dislike, or anger
disapproving or angry glance

Examples

She shot her colleague a dirty look after his rude comment in the meeting.
When he forgot their anniversary, his wife gave him a dirty look that said it all.
The teacher gave the students a dirty look for talking during the exam.

Synset Examples

He received a dirty look for being late.
I gave her a dirty look for interrupting me.
She shot him a dirty look after the rude comment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glare - an angry or fierce look
  • Synonym: scowl - an angry or displeased expression
