cookie cutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊki ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
A tool for cutting dough into shapes before baking cookies.
kitchen tool for shaping cookies

Examples

She used a star-shaped cookie cutter to make festive holiday treats.
Before baking, he pressed a cookie cutter into the rolled dough to create heart shapes.
The children enjoyed using the animal-shaped cookie cutters for their baking project.
