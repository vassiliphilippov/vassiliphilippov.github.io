Brummagem antique

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrʌmədʒəm ænˈtik / (AmE), / ˈbrʌmɪdʒəm ænˈtiːk / (BrE)
An antique object from Birmingham, especially one that is imitative or of dubious authenticity.
antique from Birmingham, often imitative
british

Examples

The collector was wary of buying a Brummagem antique without proper authentication.
Some shops on the outskirts sold Brummagem antiques made to look like rare Victorian pieces.
She discovered that the vase was a cleverly crafted Brummagem antique, not a genuine Georgian artifact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antique - an old collectible object, valued for its age or quality

Etymology

Brummagem is an informal, dialectal name for Birmingham, England, historically associated with imitation goods.
