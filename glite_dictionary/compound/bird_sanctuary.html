bird sanctuary

1. NOUN

/ bɝːd ˈsæŋktʃuˌɛri / (AmE), / bɜːd ˈsaŋktjʊəri / (BrE)
A building or area where birds are kept and protected, often for conservation, research, or public viewing.
a place where birds are kept and protected

Examples

The city has established a bird sanctuary to protect endangered species.
We visited a bird sanctuary and learned about native birds.
The bird sanctuary houses exotic birds from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aviary - a large enclosure for keeping birds
