runner-up

1. NOUN

/ ˌrʌnər ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌrʌnə ˈʌp / (BrE)
the competitor or team that finishes in second place in a race, competition, or contest
person or team that finishes second

Examples

She was named the runner-up in the international piano competition.
After a close match, the team finished as runner-up in the tournament.
Despite being the runner-up, he was proud of his performance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: winner - the competitor who finishes first
