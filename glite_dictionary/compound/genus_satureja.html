genus Satureja

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒiːnəs sæˈtʊəriə / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs sæˈtjʊərɪə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic herbs in the mint family, commonly known as savory, including species used as culinary herbs.
a genus of herbs (savory) in the mint family

Examples

The genus Satureja includes both summer and winter savory, widely used in Mediterranean cuisine.
Botanists are studying the medicinal properties of plants from the genus Satureja.
Members of the genus Satureja thrive in well-drained, sunny locations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savory - an aromatic herb used in cooking, especially of the genus Satureja

Etymology

From Latin 'Satureja', the Latin name for savory.
