fragrant wood fern

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt wʊd fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt wʊd fɜːn / (BrE)
a fern native to northern Eurasia and North America, noted for its fragrant fronds
fern with fragrant fronds

Examples

The fragrant wood fern grows well in shaded forest gardens.
Many gardeners appreciate the subtle scent of the fragrant wood fern's foliage.
The fragrant wood fern can thrive in both North American and Eurasian climates.
