maternity ward

1. NOUN

/ məˈtɝːnɪti wɔrd / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnɪti wɔːd / (BrE)
a hospital ward where care is provided for women during pregnancy, childbirth, and for newborn infants
hospital ward for childbirth and maternity care

Examples

She was admitted to the maternity ward after going into labor.
The hospital has a newly renovated maternity ward for expecting mothers and their babies.
After delivery, both mother and child stayed in the maternity ward for observation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nursery - a room in a hospital where newborn babies are cared for
