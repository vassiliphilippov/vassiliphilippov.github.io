stately home

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪtli hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtli həʊm / (BrE)
A large and impressive country house, especially one that was formerly or is currently occupied by an aristocratic family.
large aristocratic country mansion
british

Examples

They spent the weekend touring a beautiful stately home in the English countryside.
Several television dramas have been filmed in that famous stately home.
The gardens surrounding the stately home are open to visitors every summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mansion - a large, impressive house
  • Degree Higher: palace - official residence of royalty or high dignitaries
