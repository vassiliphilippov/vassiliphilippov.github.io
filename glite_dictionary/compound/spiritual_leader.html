spiritual leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrətʃuəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who leads or guides others in religious or spiritual matters
person guiding others in religion

Examples

The Dalai Lama is often regarded as a spiritual leader by millions around the world.
Many people sought guidance from the spiritual leader during times of crisis.
She became a respected spiritual leader in her community after years of study and meditation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religious leader - a person who leads others in organized religion
  • Antonym: follower - a person who is led or guided by another
