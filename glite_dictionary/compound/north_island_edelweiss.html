North Island edelweiss

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɔrθ ˈaɪlənd ˌɛdəlˌwaɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθ ˈaɪlənd ˌɛdəlˌvaɪs / (BrE)
a perennial herb native to New Zealand, closely resembling the European edelweiss, with white woolly flower heads
perennial herb, New Zealand, similar to edelweiss

Examples

The North Island edelweiss grows in high-altitude regions of New Zealand.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate North Island edelweiss for its resemblance to its European namesake.
Unlike its European counterpart, the North Island edelweiss thrives in the unique climate of New Zealand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edelweiss - alpine plant with white woolly flowers, native to Europe
