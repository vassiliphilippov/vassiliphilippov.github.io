ammunition chest

1. NOUN

/ ˌæmjəˈnɪʃən tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˌamjʊˈnɪʃən tʃɛst / (BrE)
a strong container used to store and transport ammunition, especially for military use
container for storing ammunition

Examples

The soldiers kept extra rounds in the ammunition chest during the battle.
He opened the ammunition chest to check the supply of bullets and shells.
Each ammunition chest was carefully labeled and stored below deck on the warship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammunition box - box used for holding ammunition
