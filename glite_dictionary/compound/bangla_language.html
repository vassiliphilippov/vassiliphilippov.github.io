Bangla language

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑŋɡlə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbaŋlə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Magadhan language spoken by the Bengali people, and the official language of Bangladesh and the Indian state of West Bengal
official language of Bangladesh and West Bengal

Examples

Many poets have written masterpieces in Bangla language.
She is fluent in both English and Bangla language.
The government declared Bangla language a national language after independence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bengali language - another common name for Bangla language

Etymology

From Bengali বাংলা (Bāṅlā), the name of the language and people.
