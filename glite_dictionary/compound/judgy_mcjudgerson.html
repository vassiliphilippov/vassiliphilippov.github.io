Judgy McJudgerson

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌdʒi məkˈdʒʌdʒərsən / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒi məkˈdʒʌdʒəsən / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) A person who is overly judgmental or habitually judges others harshly.
overly judgmental person (humorous term)
informal slang

Examples

Stop being such a Judgy McJudgerson and let people enjoy themselves.
Every group has a Judgy McJudgerson who always has an opinion about everything.
When I wore my bright jacket, my sister turned into a Judgy McJudgerson and rolled her eyes.

Synset Examples

She was acting like a real Judgy McJudgerson today.
Don't be a Judgy McJudgerson at the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgmental - tending to judge people too quickly or harshly

Etymology

A humorous formation based on the pattern of surnames (e.g., "Mc-" prefix), common in internet slang to playfully label traits.
