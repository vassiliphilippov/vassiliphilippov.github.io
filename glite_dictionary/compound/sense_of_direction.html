sense of direction

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛns əv dəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛns əv dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the ability to know where you are and to find your way to other places, especially without maps or other aids
ability to orient oneself geographically

Examples

Despite never visiting the city before, Mia's sense of direction helped her easily find the museum.
He has such a poor sense of direction that he often gets lost, even with a GPS.
Explorers often rely on their natural sense of direction when they are deep in unfamiliar terrain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orientation - awareness of one's environment and position
