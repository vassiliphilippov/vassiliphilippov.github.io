farm machine

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm məˈʃin / (AmE), / fɑːm məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine used on a farm to aid in agricultural tasks such as plowing, planting, or harvesting crops
machine used in farming

Examples

The new farm machine made harvesting much faster for local farmers.
He invested in a high-tech farm machine to improve crop yields.
Older farm machines can sometimes be found at agricultural museums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farm equipment - tools and machines used on a farm
