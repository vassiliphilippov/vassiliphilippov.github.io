fecal occult test

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːkəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (AmE), / ˈfiːkəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test that checks stool samples for hidden blood, often used to detect colorectal cancers
test for hidden blood in stool
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended a fecal occult test as part of my annual check-up.
A fecal occult test can help detect early signs of colorectal cancer.
You can perform a fecal occult test at home by following the instructions provided in the kit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fecal occult blood test - a laboratory test to find hidden blood in the stool

Etymology

From Latin 'faecalis' (of dregs or sediment) and 'occultus' (hidden).
