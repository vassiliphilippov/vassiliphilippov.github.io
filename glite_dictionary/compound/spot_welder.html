spot welder

1. NOUN

/ spɑt ˈwɛl.dər / (AmE), / spɒt ˈwɛl.də / (BrE)
A worker or technician who performs spot welding, especially in industrial or manufacturing settings.
person who performs spot welding

Examples

The spot welder carefully joined the metal sheets along the assembly line.
She trained as a spot welder after completing her technical education.
Modern factories rely on both robots and skilled spot welders to assemble vehicles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welder - a person whose job is to weld materials together
