English toy spaniel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ tɔɪ spæniəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ tɔɪ ˈspænɪəl / (BrE)
A small British breed of dog with a long silky coat, rounded head, and short upturned muzzle.
small British dog breed

Examples

The English toy spaniel is known for its gentle temperament and affectionate nature.
She brought her English toy spaniel to the dog show, where it won first place in its group.
Many families choose the English toy spaniel as a companion because of its friendly personality.
