hip tile

1. NOUN

/ hɪp taɪl / (AmE), / hɪp taɪl / (BrE)
a roof tile specially shaped to cover and protect the external angle (hip) formed by the meeting of two sloping sides of a roof
roof tile for covering a roof hip

Examples

The roofer carefully installed each hip tile along the sloping edges of the roof.
Choosing the right hip tile can enhance both the durability and appearance of your home.
The architect specified clay hip tiles to match the traditional style of the building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tile - a thin, flat slab used for covering roofs, floors, or walls
