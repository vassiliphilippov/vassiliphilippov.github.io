Bohai Sea

1. PROPN

/ boʊˈhaɪ siː / (AmE), / bəʊˈhaɪ siː / (BrE)
an inlet of the Yellow Sea on the northeast coast of China, near Beijing and Tianjin
inlet of the Yellow Sea, China

Examples

The Bohai Sea is a critical region for shipping routes connecting northern China to the world.
Winter ice in the Bohai Sea can disrupt fishing and transportation.
Many industrial cities are located along the coast of the Bohai Sea.
