wood chisel

1. NOUN

/ wʊd ˈtʃɪzl̩ / (AmE), / wʊd ˈtʃɪzl̩ / (BrE)
a hand tool with a sharp blade used for shaping or carving wood, typically by being struck with a mallet or pushed by hand
hand tool for carving or shaping wood

Examples

He carefully used a wood chisel to carve intricate patterns into the door.
Before assembling the shelf, she trimmed the edges with a wood chisel.
The carpenter sharpened his wood chisel before starting the restoration work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chisel - a tool with a cutting edge at the end of a metal blade; used for shaping wood, stone, or metal
