music paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuːzɪk ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper printed with staff lines for writing music notation.
paper with musical staff lines

Examples

She bought a new notebook of music paper before her composition class.
Please write your melody on music paper and hand it in next week.
I always keep extra music paper in my bag for when inspiration strikes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manuscript paper - paper with printed staves for musical notation
  • Degree Higher: sheet music - printed music scores
