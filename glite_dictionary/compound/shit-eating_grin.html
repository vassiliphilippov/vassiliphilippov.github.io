shit-eating grin

1. NOUN

/ ˌʃɪtˈiːtɪŋ ˈɡrɪn / (AmE), / ˌʃɪtˈiːtɪŋ ˈɡrɪn / (BrE)
a smug, self-satisfied, or annoyingly knowing smile or facial expression
smug or self-satisfied smile
vulgar informal
