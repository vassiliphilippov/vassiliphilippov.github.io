Chilean nut

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪˈliən nʌt / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən nʌt / (BrE)
A Chilean shrub (Gevuina avellana) that produces a coral-red fruit with an edible seed similar to a hazelnut.
edible seed from Chilean shrub

Examples

The oil from the Chilean nut is highly prized in gourmet cooking.
She tasted the roasted Chilean nut and compared it to a hazelnut.
Farmers in southern Chile cultivate the Chilean nut for both export and local consumption.
