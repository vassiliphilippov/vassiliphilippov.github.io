anal personality

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.nəl pərˌsɑːn.əˈlɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.nəl pəˌsɒn.əˈlɪ.ti / (BrE)
(in psychoanalysis) a personality type characterized by excessive orderliness, meticulousness, stubbornness, and reserved behavior, believed to originate from fixation during the anal stage of childhood development.
personality type marked by orderliness and reserve (psychoanalysis)

Examples

The psychologist described him as having an anal personality, obsessed with organization and control.
Many psychoanalysts believed that an anal personality could develop from strict toilet training in childhood.
Her anal personality traits made her extremely meticulous about keeping her workspace orderly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anal-retentive - a person showing excessive orderliness and perfectionism, stemming from fixation during the anal stage

Etymology

From 'anal' (psychoanalytic reference to the anal stage) + 'personality'; originates in early 20th-century Freudian theory.
