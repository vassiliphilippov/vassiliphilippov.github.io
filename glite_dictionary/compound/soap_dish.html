soap dish

1. NOUN

/ soʊp dɪʃ / (AmE), / səʊp dɪʃ / (BrE)
A small tray or receptacle for holding a bar of soap, typically found in a bathroom or kitchen.
tray for holding soap

Examples

She placed the bar of soap back on the soap dish near the sink.
The ceramic soap dish matched the rest of the bathroom accessories.
Please wash the soap dish regularly to prevent soap scum buildup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soap holder - a container or device for holding soap
