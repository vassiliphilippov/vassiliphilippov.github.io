link whoring

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋk ˈhɔːrɪŋ / (AmE), / lɪŋk ˈhɔː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of excessively promoting links for personal gain, especially in online communities, often regarded as unethical or annoying
excessive self-promotion via online links
informal slang offensive

Examples

Many forums have strict rules against link whoring to maintain the quality of discussions.
He was accused of link whoring when he posted his website in every thread.
Repeated link whoring can result in being banned from online communities.

Synset Examples

She stopped link whoring after realizing it hurt her reputation.

Link whoring is frowned upon in most communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spamming - sending unwanted or irrelevant messages online
