blue copperas

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈkɑpərəs / (AmE), / blu ˈkɒpərəs / (BrE)
a hydrated blue crystalline form of copper sulfate, used especially in chemistry and industry
hydrated blue crystalline copper sulfate

Examples

Large blue crystals of blue copperas formed on the bottom of the beaker.
