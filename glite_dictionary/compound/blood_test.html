blood test

1. NOUN

/ blʌd tɛst / (AmE), / blʌd tɛst / (BrE)
a medical examination of a sample of blood to obtain information about a person's health
medical analysis of a blood sample
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered a blood test to check for any infections.
Routine blood tests are important for monitoring your overall health.
After the blood test, she received her results within two days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urine test - a medical test of a urine sample
