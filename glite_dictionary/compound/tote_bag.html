tote bag

1. NOUN

/ toʊt bæg / (AmE), / təʊt bæɡ / (BrE)
a large, sturdy bag with parallel handles, typically used for carrying shopping or personal items
large, sturdy carry bag with handles

Examples

She carried her groceries home in a reusable tote bag.
A canvas tote bag is ideal for trips to the farmer’s market.
The library sells tote bags to support its programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopping bag - a bag used for carrying shopping

Etymology

From the verb "tote" (to carry) + "bag."
