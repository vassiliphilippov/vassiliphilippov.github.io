entire leaf

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtaɪər liːf / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪə liːf / (BrE)
a leaf with a smooth margin, lacking notches or indentations
leaf with smooth, unnotched margin

Examples

The botanist identified the plant by its entire leaf and smooth stem.
Maple trees typically have lobed leaves, but this species has an entire leaf margin.
You can distinguish the species by checking if it has an entire leaf or a serrated one.
