invaluable experience

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈvæljuəbəl ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaljuəbl ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
Knowledge or skills that are extremely useful and cannot be measured by value.
extremely useful knowledge or skills

Examples

Her years volunteering abroad gave her invaluable experience working with different cultures.
The advice from my mentor was an invaluable experience that shaped my career.
Internships provide students with invaluable experience before entering the workforce.

Synset Examples

The pylote's experience was invaluable during the storm.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: valuable experience - experience that is useful or beneficial
  • Synonym: expertise - advanced knowledge or skill in a particular area
