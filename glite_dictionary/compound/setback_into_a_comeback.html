setback into a comeback

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛtˌbæk ˈɪntu ˈkʌmbæk / (AmE), / ˈsɛtbæk ˈɪntuː ˈkʌmbæk / (BrE)
the process of turning a failure or defeat into a later success or recovery
turning failure into success

Examples

After her injury, she turned her setback into a comeback and won the championship.
Companies often use stories of setback into a comeback to inspire employees during tough times.
His career is a classic setback into a comeback tale that motivates young athletes.

Synset Examples

They managed to turn a setback into a comeback after the loss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comeback - a return to success after a failure or decline
  • Antonym: setback - a reversal or check in progress
