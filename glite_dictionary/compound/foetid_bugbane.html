foetid bugbane

1. NOUN

/ ˈfidɪd ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A plant native to Siberia and eastern Asia, known for its ill-smelling green-white flowers; a species of bugbane.
ill-smelling plant of Siberia and eastern Asia

Examples

The garden's wild corner is dominated by foetid bugbane, whose pungent blossoms deter visitors.
Hikers recognized foetid bugbane by the strong odor that surrounded the plants in bloom.
In Siberian forests, foetid bugbane thrives in damp, shady areas near streams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bugbane - any plant of the genus Cimicifuga, known for insect-repelling properties

Etymology

Foetid from Latin foetidus (stinking); bugbane is a common name for plants in the genus Cimicifuga.
