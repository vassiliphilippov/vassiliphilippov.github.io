iron foundry

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪɚn ˈfaʊndɹi / (AmE), / ˈaɪən ˈfaʊndɹi / (BrE)
A factory or workshop where cast iron is melted and shaped into products.
factory producing cast iron

Examples

The old iron foundry was converted into an art museum.
Workers at the iron foundry operated the heavy machinery with great skill.
Air pollution regulations have changed the way iron foundries operate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundry - a workshop or factory for casting metal
