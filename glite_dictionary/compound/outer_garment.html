outer garment

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtɚ ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə ˈɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
a piece of clothing worn over other clothes, especially for warmth or protection
clothing worn over other clothes

Examples

She put on her outer garment before stepping out into the cold evening.
Rain had soaked through his outer garment but his clothes underneath remained dry.
In winter, a heavy outer garment is essential for keeping warm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat - an outer garment with sleeves, worn outdoors
  • Synonym: jacket - a short, outer garment covering the upper body
