West Coast

1. PROPN

/ wɛst koʊst / (AmE), / wɛst kəʊst / (BrE)
the western coastal region of the United States, typically including the states of California, Oregon, and Washington
western coastal region of the US

Examples

Many people dream of living on the West Coast because of its mild climate and natural beauty.
Tech companies often have headquarters on the West Coast, especially in Silicon Valley.
The earthquake affected several cities along the West Coast.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: East Coast - the eastern seaboard of the United States
