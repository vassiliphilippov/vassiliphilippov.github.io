clear-eyed analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪr aɪd əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈklɪə aɪd əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
an objective and honest evaluation, free from bias or wishful thinking
objective, honest evaluation

Examples

The report offers a clear-eyed analysis of the current economic trends.
Her advice was based on a clear-eyed analysis rather than wishful thinking.
A clear-eyed analysis helps organizations avoid costly mistakes.

Synset Examples

A clear-eyed analysis of the situation is needed.
She approached the challenge with a clear-eyed perspective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: objective analysis - evaluation based only on facts, not feelings
  • Synonym: critical analysis - analytical evaluation involving careful judgment
  • Antonym: biased analysis - evaluation affected by prejudice or subjectivity
