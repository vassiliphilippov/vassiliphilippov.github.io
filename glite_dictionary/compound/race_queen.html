race queen

1. NOUN

/ reɪs kwin / (AmE), / reɪs kwiːn / (BrE)
a female model or promotional figure who participates in motorsport events, often serving as an ambassador or award presenter, and sometimes chosen in a beauty competition
female model at motorsport events

Examples

The race queen wore a stunning outfit as she greeted fans at the Grand Prix.
Several race queens posed with the winning car during the trophy ceremony.
She dreamed of becoming a race queen and representing her favorite racing team.

Synset Examples

The race queen wore a stunning gown at the event.
She was crowned race queen after winning the competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grid girl - female promotional model at motorsport events
  • Synonym: beauty queen - winner of a beauty pageant
