cattle farm

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætl fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈkætl fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where cattle are bred and raised for meat, milk, or other products.
a farm for raising cattle

Examples

Jake grew up working on his family's cattle farm in Texas.
The cattle farm expanded by purchasing more land for grazing.
Modern technology has improved productivity on every cattle farm in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairy farm - A farm that specializes in milk production from cows
  • Synonym: ranch - A large farm, especially in North America, where cattle or other animals are bred
