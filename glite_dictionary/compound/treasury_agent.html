treasury agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛʒəri ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒəri ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a special law-enforcement agent employed by the United States Treasury Department, responsible for investigating federal financial crimes such as counterfeiting and tax evasion
US Treasury law-enforcement agent
american

Examples

A treasury agent investigated the case of suspected tax fraud in the corporation.
The movie's main character poses as a treasury agent to infiltrate a criminal network.
As a treasury agent, she received specialized training in detecting counterfeit currency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: federal agent - a law enforcement agent of the federal government
