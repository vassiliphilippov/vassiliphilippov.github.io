navel orange

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪvəl ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈneɪv(ə)l ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A variety of seedless orange with a small secondary fruit at its apex, recognized by its navel-like formation.
a type of seedless orange

Examples

The navel orange is popular because it is easy to peel and contains no seeds.
Many grocers recommend the navel orange for making fresh juice at home.
She added slices of navel orange to the salad for a burst of sweetness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orange - a round citrus fruit with a tough bright reddish-yellow rind

Etymology

Named for the navel-like formation at the blossom end of the fruit.
