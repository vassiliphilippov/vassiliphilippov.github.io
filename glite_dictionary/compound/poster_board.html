poster board

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstər bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstə bɔːd / (BrE)
A stiff, smooth cardboard used especially for making posters, displays, or signs.
stiff cardboard for posters or displays

Examples

She mounted her science project on a large sheet of poster board.
We need to buy some poster board for the classroom presentation.
The sign was made with bright markers on yellow poster board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardboard - a stiff, heavy paper used to make boxes and signs
