burn bag

1. NOUN

/ bɝn bæɡ / (AmE), / bɜːn bæɡ / (BrE)
a secure bag used to collect and transport classified or sensitive documents for destruction by burning
container for documents to be destroyed by burning

Examples

All classified reports must be placed in the burn bag at the end of the meeting.
The secretary carefully checked the burn bag to ensure no documents were left behind.
Security protocol requires that the burn bag be locked until it reaches the incinerator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shredder - a machine used to destroy documents by cutting them into small pieces
