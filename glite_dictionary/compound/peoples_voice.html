people's voice

1. NOUN

/ ˈpipəlz vɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəlz vɔɪs / (BrE)
the collective opinion or will of the general population, especially as expressed in public debate or voting
opinion of the general populace

Examples

Political leaders must listen to people's voice when making important decisions.
The film reflects the people's voice on issues of social justice.
During the protests, the people's voice grew stronger each day demanding change.

Synset Examples

The people's voice should always be heard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public opinion - the views prevalent among the general public
  • Synonym: majority opinion - the opinion held by most people
