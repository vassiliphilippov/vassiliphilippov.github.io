kosher product

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊʃər ˈprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊʃə ˈprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
A food item prepared according to Jewish dietary laws, making it permissible for consumption by those observing kashrut.
food meeting Jewish dietary laws

Examples

More supermarkets now stock kosher products to meet customer demand.
Before Passover, the family buys only certified kosher products.
Some people choose kosher products for reasons of food safety or religious observance.

Synset Examples

Many people prefer kosher products for dietary reasons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kosher - food that complies with Jewish dietary laws
  • Synonym: halal product - food prepared according to Islamic dietary laws

Etymology

Kosher: from Yiddish *kosher*, from Hebrew *kashēr* (fit, proper).
