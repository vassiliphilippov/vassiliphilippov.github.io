loan application

1. NOUN

/ loʊn ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ləʊn ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal request for a loan, typically submitted to a bank or financial institution
request to borrow money

Examples

She submitted her loan application to the bank yesterday.
The loan application process includes checking your credit history and financial documents.
Many people find the loan application paperwork complicated and time-consuming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loan - sum of money borrowed
