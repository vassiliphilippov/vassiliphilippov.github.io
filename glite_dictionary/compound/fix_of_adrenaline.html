fix of adrenaline

1. NOUN

/ fɪks əv əˈdrɛnəlɪn / (AmE), / fɪks ɒv əˈdrɛnəlɪn / (BrE)
a sudden burst of excitement or energy, often felt in thrilling or stressful situations
burst of excitement or energy
informal

Examples

Extreme sports enthusiasts constantly seek their next fix of adrenaline.
