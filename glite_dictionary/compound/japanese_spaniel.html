Japanese spaniel

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈpæniz ˈspænl̩ / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈspænɪl / (BrE)
a small breed of toy dog originating in Japan, with a silky black-and-white or red-and-white coat and a short, broad muzzle
small breed of toy dog from Japan

Examples

The Japanese spaniel is known for its affectionate nature and elegant appearance.
She adopted a rescue Japanese spaniel from the local animal shelter.
Many royal courts prized the Japanese spaniel as a companion dog.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Japanese Chin - another name for the Japanese spaniel breed

Etymology

From 'Japanese' (relating to Japan) and 'spaniel' (a type of dog breed).
