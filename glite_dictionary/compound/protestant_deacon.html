Protestant deacon

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊt̬əstənt ˈdiːkən / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtɛstənt ˈdiːkən / (BrE)
A lay member of a Protestant church who assists the minister in various capacities.
Protestant church lay assistant

Examples

The Protestant deacon helped organize the community outreach event at the church.
As a Protestant deacon, she supports the minister by visiting the elderly and the sick.
At Sunday service, the Protestant deacon assisted in distributing communion to the congregation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deacon - An ordained or appointed church member who assists in religious services and duties
