hip boot

1. NOUN

/ hɪp buːt / (AmE), / hɪp buːt / (BrE)
A very high waterproof boot that reaches up to the hip, typically worn for fishing or wading in water.
very high waterproof boot, often for fishing

Examples

He carefully pulled on his hip boots before wading into the river to fish.
The flooded field was so deep that only hip boots kept her legs dry.
Hunters and anglers often wear hip boots to cross streams or marshes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wader - a waterproof boot or garment for keeping legs and feet dry in water
  • Degree Lower: boot - a type of footwear that covers the foot and ankle and sometimes the leg
