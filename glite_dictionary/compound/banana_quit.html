banana quit

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə kwɪt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə kwɪt / (BrE)
A small tropical American bird of the honeycreeper family, typically having a curved bill and feeding on nectar.
a tropical American honeycreeper bird

Examples

The banana quit is often seen flitting among the flowers in Caribbean gardens.
We spotted a colorful banana quit drinking nectar from hibiscus blossoms.
A banana quit built its nest near our porch last spring.

Related Terms
