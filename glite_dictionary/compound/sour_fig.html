sour fig

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊɚ fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊə fɪɡ / (BrE)
a low-growing South African succulent plant, Carpobrotus edulis, that produces a capsular fruit with edible pulp
South African succulent with edible fruit

Examples

The sour fig is often used to make jams in South Africa.
We tasted the tangy fruit of the sour fig while hiking along the coast.
A patch of sour fig covered the rocky ground, its pink flowers in bloom.
