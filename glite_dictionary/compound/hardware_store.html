hardware store

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrd.wɛr stɔr / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd.wɛə stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells tools, building supplies, and other hardware for home improvement and repair.
shop selling tools and supplies

Examples

He went to the hardware store to buy some nails and screws.
There is a hardware store just around the corner from my house.
Many hardware stores offer advice on home improvement projects.

Synset Examples

in Great Britain they used to call a hardware store an ‘ironmonger's shop’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironmonger's - a British term for a shop selling hardware
