Battle of St Mihiel

1. PROPN

/ ˈbætəl əv sənt miˈjɛl / (AmE), / ˈbæt(ə)l əv sənt miˈjɛl / (BrE)
a major World War I battle in 1918 during which American troops launched their first independent offensive in France, as part of the Meuse-Argonne operation
major 1918 World War I battle in France

Examples

The Battle of St Mihiel marked a turning point in the involvement of American forces in World War I.
Historians often highlight the significance of the Battle of St Mihiel in military strategy discussions.
My great-grandfather served during the Battle of St Mihiel in France.

Related Terms
