one-seventh

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈsɛvənθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsɛvənθ / (BrE)
Each of the seven equal parts into which something is divided.
one of seven equal parts

Examples

About one-seventh of the total budget was allocated to education.
The landowner sold one-seventh of his property to his neighbor.
Each child received one-seventh of the chocolate bar in the class.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: one-sixth - one of six equal parts
  • Degree Lower: one-eighth - one of eight equal parts
