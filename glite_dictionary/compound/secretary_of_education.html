Secretary of Education

1. NOUN

/ sɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i əv ˌɛdʒ.əˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.rə.təri əv ˌɛd.jʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the government official who is the head of the Department of Education and responsible for national education policy
head of the Education Department (government)

Examples

The Secretary of Education announced new reforms to the national curriculum yesterday.
Each President nominates a new Secretary of Education to join the cabinet.
The responsibilities of the Secretary of Education include overseeing federal education funding and policy.

Synset Examples

the post of Education Secretary was created in 1979

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Education Secretary - official leading the Education Department
  • Synonym: Secretary of State - high-ranking government official, typically foreign affairs or state policy

head of the Department of Education (US government)

Examples

After a lengthy debate, the president nominated Miguel Cardona as Secretary of Education.
The Secretary of Education announced new policies to improve literacy rates across the country.
As Secretary of Education, she was known for advocating increased funding for public schools.
