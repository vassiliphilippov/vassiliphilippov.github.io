St. Kitts and Nevis

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt kɪts ənd ˈnivɪs / (AmE), / seɪnt kɪts ənd ˈniːvɪs / (BrE)
An island country in the Caribbean Sea, consisting chiefly of the islands of Saint Kitts and Nevis, east-southeast of Puerto Rico; gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1983.
Caribbean island country; independent since 1983

Examples

She spent her honeymoon in St. Kitts and Nevis, enjoying the beautiful beaches and coral reefs.
Citizens of St. Kitts and Nevis celebrate their independence every year in September.
The government of St. Kitts and Nevis has implemented new environmental protection laws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caribbean - The region of the Caribbean Sea, its islands and surrounding coasts

Etymology

St. Kitts is a shortened form of 'Saint Christopher'; Nevis from Spanish 'Nuestra Señora de las Nieves' (Our Lady of the Snows).
