painful sensation

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnfəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnfəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
a physical feeling characterized by acute discomfort or pain
a feeling of acute pain or discomfort

Examples

The patient reported a painful sensation in her left foot after the surgery.
Touching the hot stove caused a sudden painful sensation in my hand.
After the injection, I felt a brief painful sensation in my arm.

Synset Examples

as the intensity increased, the sensation changed from tickle to pain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pain - physical suffering or discomfort
  • Degree Lower: ache - a continuous or prolonged dull pain
