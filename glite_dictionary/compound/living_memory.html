living memory

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
the period of time within a person's or group's life that can be remembered by people still alive
time period people can still recall

Examples

The floods were the worst disaster in living memory for the region.
In living memory, there has never been as much snow on the ground.
That tradition has been maintained throughout living memory in our village.

Synset Examples

This is the worst storm in living memory for the town.
Such changes have not occurred within living memory.
In living memory, we have never faced such challenges.
