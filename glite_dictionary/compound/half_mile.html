half mile

1. NOUN

/ hæf maɪl / (AmE), / hɑːf maɪl / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to half of one mile, or 880 yards (approximately 804.67 metres)
unit of length equal to 0.5 mile

Examples

It is only a half mile from the train station to my house.
She jogged a half mile before taking a break to catch her breath.
The school is located about a half mile east of the post office.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mile - a unit of length equal to 1,760 yards or approximately 1,609 metres
  • Degree Lower: quarter mile - a unit of length equal to a quarter of a mile
