budget-friendly alternative

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌdʒət ˌfrɛn(d)li ɔlˈtɝnətɪv / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt ˌfrɛndli ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv / (BrE)
an option or substitute that is affordable and does not cost much money compared to others
affordable option or substitute

Examples

Many students opt for a budget-friendly alternative to expensive textbooks.
We chose the second venue because it was a budget-friendly alternative to the hotel.
The company introduced a budget-friendly alternative that quickly became popular with cost-conscious customers.

Synset Examples

They marketed the Euroclone as a budget-friendly alternative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affordable alternative - an alternative that can be bought within one's means
  • Antonym: costly alternative - an alternative that is expensive
