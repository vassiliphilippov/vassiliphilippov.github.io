sideline interest

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪd.laɪn ˈɪn.trəst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd.laɪn ˈɪn.trəst / (BrE)
A hobby, activity, or secondary job pursued in addition to one's main occupation.
secondary job or hobby

Examples

She spends her evenings painting as a sideline interest apart from her accounting career.
Many teachers have a sideline interest to supplement their incomes.
Writing fiction became his sideline interest after years of working as a journalist.

Synset Examples

They encouraged her to pursue her sideline interests.
She decided to take a sideline job to earn extra money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hobby - activity done regularly for enjoyment; not a main job
  • Antonym: main job - a person's primary or principal employment
