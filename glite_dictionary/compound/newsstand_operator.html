newsstand operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌstænd ˌɑːpəˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌstænd ˌɒpəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to sell newspapers and magazines from a newsstand
person who sells newspapers at a newsstand

Examples

The newsstand operator greeted customers with a smile every morning at the station.
She worked as a newsstand operator for over twenty years in the busy downtown area.
After his shift ended, the newsstand operator closed up the kiosk and headed home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newsagent - person who sells newspapers and magazines (esp. British)
