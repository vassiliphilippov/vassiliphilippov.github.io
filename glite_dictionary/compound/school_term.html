school term

1. NOUN

/ skuːl tɝːm / (AmE), / skuːl tɜːm / (BrE)
A period during which a school or educational institution holds regular classes.
the period classes are held in school

Examples

The school term begins in early September and ends in December.
Many families plan their vacations around the school term.
The new library was completed in time for the start of the school term.

Synset Examples

they had to shorten the school term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semester - half-year term in a school or college
  • Synonym: quarter - a division of the academic year into four terms
