dirty linen

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti ˈlɪnən / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti ˈlɪnɪn / (BrE)
personal or family matters that are private and potentially embarrassing if discussed in public
private, embarrassing matters

Examples

No one wanted to air their dirty linen in front of the press.
It's best not to discuss dirty linen at work meetings.
The politician feared that his dirty linen would be exposed during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dirty laundry - private matters that could cause embarrassment if revealed
