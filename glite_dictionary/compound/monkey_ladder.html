monkey ladder

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌŋki ˌlædər / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌlædə / (BrE)
a light, narrow ladder giving access to the monkey bridge on a ship
narrow ship's ladder to monkey bridge

Examples

The crew climbed the monkey ladder up to the monkey bridge for a better view of the harbor.
Make sure the monkey ladder is securely fastened before you start your ascent.
He lost his grip as he was going down the monkey ladder in rough seas.
