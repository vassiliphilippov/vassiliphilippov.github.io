general headquarters

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnɚəl ˌhɛd.kwɔːr.tɚz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl ˌhɛd.kwɔː.təz / (BrE)
the central military headquarters from which a commander controls and organizes armed forces during operations
main military command center

Examples

The army's general headquarters was heavily guarded throughout the campaign.
Messages from general headquarters coordinated the movements of all the different units.
After the briefing at general headquarters, the officers returned to their field positions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headquarters - a place from which operations are directed
