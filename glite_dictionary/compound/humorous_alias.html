humorous alias

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmərəs ˈeɪliəs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəs ˈeɪliəs / (BrE)
a name or pseudonym adopted for amusement, often to conceal one's identity in a playful or comical way
a funny pseudonym or fake name

Examples

He often comments on forums using a humorous alias to make people laugh.
The author published the satirical article under a humorous alias to avoid being recognized.
Choosing a humorous alias boosted her confidence to join online games with strangers.

Synset Examples

He used 'Peeniss' as a humorous alias online.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name used by someone, often to conceal identity
  • Synonym: alias - an assumed name, especially as used to hide one's true identity
