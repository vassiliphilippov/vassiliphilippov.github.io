barrel roll

1. NOUN

/ ˈbærəl roʊl / (AmE), / ˈbærəl rəʊl / (BrE)
an aerobatic maneuver in which an aircraft makes a complete rotation about its longitudinal axis while following a helical or spiral flight path
aerobatic aircraft maneuver

Examples

The pilot executed a perfect barrel roll during the airshow, thrilling the spectators.
Learning how to perform a barrel roll is essential for advanced aerobatic training.
The stunt involved a series of rapid barrel rolls at high altitude.
