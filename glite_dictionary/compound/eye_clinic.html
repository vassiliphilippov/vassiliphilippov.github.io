eye clinic

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / aɪ ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
A medical facility where specialists diagnose and treat eye conditions.
medical facility for eye care

Examples

She visited the eye clinic for her annual vision checkup.
The city is opening a new eye clinic to serve local residents.
After the surgery, he had regular follow-up visits at the eye clinic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye hospital - a hospital specializing in eye care and treatment
  • Degree Lower: clinic - a facility for diagnosis and treatment of outpatients
