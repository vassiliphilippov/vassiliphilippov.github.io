mat rep

1. NOUN

/ mæt ɹɛp / (AmE), / mæt rɛp / (BrE)
an individual repetition of an exercise performed on a mat
repetition of an exercise done on a mat

Examples

She completed fifteen mat reps of crunches as part of her workout.
It's important to use proper form for every mat rep to avoid injury.
His trainer asked him to increase the number of mat reps this week.

Synset Examples

He counted his mat rep carefully to track his progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rep - one complete motion of an exercise
