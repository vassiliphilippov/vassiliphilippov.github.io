purslane family

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜrs.leɪn ˈfæm.əl.i / (AmE), / ˈpɜːs.leɪn ˈfæm.əl.i / (BrE)
a family of usually succulent herbaceous plants, cosmopolitan but especially common in the Americas, known botanically as Portulacaceae.
family of succulent herbaceous plants (Portulacaceae)

Examples

Many species in the purslane family thrive in arid regions thanks to their succulent leaves.
The purslane family contains both edible and ornamental plants valued worldwide.
Botanists study the purslane family to understand how plants adapt to dry environments.
