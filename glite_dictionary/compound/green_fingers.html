green fingers

1. NOUN

/ ɡriːn ˈfɪŋɡɚz / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈfɪŋɡəz / (BrE)
An unusual ability to make plants grow well and keep them healthy.
ability to grow plants well
british

Examples

My grandmother definitely has green fingers; her garden is the envy of the neighborhood.
Anyone with green fingers will love our new range of flower seeds.
Despite my efforts, I don’t have green fingers and all my houseplants keep dying.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: green thumb - special ability to make plants grow (American English equivalent)
