California quail

1. NOUN

/ kəˌlɪfɚniə ˈkwaɪl / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəniə ˈkwaɪl / (BrE)
a plump, short-tailed bird native to coastal California and Oregon, known for its distinctive forward-drooping head plume
a plump coastal bird of California and Oregon

Examples

We spotted a flock of California quail in the park this morning.
The distinctive head plume makes the California quail easy to identify.
Habitat loss has reduced the number of California quail in some regions.
