stunt woman

1. NOUN

/ stʌnt ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / stʌnt ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman employed in films or television as a substitute for an actor to perform dangerous or physically demanding stunts.
female performer of dangerous stunts in films

Examples

The film's car chase scenes were so risky that a stunt woman was hired to perform them.
She always admired the bravery and skill of a professional stunt woman.
A talented stunt woman doubled for the lead actress during the high-wire action sequence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuntman - a man who performs dangerous stunts, especially in films
