man-eating shark

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn ˌiːtɪŋ ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈmæn ˌiːtɪŋ ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, aggressive shark found in warm seas, known for occasionally attacking humans
aggressive shark that attacks humans

Examples

Many swimmers fear man-eating sharks when entering tropical waters.
The documentary featured the great white, a notorious man-eating shark.
Despite popular myths, attacks by man-eating sharks are actually quite rare.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shark - a large fish with sharp teeth and cartilage skeleton
