lemon juice

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən ˌdʒus / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən ˌdʒuːs / (BrE)
the liquid obtained from lemons, usually by squeezing, used in food, drinks, and as a cleaning agent
juice extracted from lemons

Examples

Add some lemon juice to the dressing for extra flavor.
He drank hot water with lemon juice every morning.
Fresh lemon juice can help prevent apples from turning brown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lime juice - juice extracted from limes
