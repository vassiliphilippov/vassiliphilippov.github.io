personal estate

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɜr.sən.əl ɪˈsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.sən.əl ɪˈsteɪt / (BrE)
movable property owned by an individual, as opposed to real estate such as land or buildings.
movable property (not land or buildings)
legal

Examples

Under her will, all of her personal estate was left to her grandchildren.
The inventory includes everything from vehicles to jewelry as part of the personal estate.
Debts must be paid from the personal estate before the remainder is distributed to heirs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: real estate - property in the form of land or buildings
