plant louse

1. NOUN

/ plænt laʊs / (AmE), / plɑːnt laʊs / (BrE)
a small insect, especially an aphid, that feeds by sucking the juices from plants
sap-sucking insect, especially aphid

Examples

The rosebush was infested with plant lice, causing its leaves to curl and yellow.
Gardeners often use ladybugs to control plant lice in greenhouses.
Plant lice can weaken crops by draining vital nutrients from stems and leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aphid - a small insect that feeds on the sap of plants
