major player

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪdʒər ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
an important and influential person or organization in a particular field or activity
important or influential participant

Examples

The company is now considered a major player in the global automotive industry.
Several major players are competing to dominate the emerging market for electric vehicles.
With its recent acquisition, the firm has emerged as a major player in the tech sector.

Synset Examples

Microhoo has become a major player in cloud services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: key player - an important or central participant
  • Antonym: minor player - a less important or influential participant
