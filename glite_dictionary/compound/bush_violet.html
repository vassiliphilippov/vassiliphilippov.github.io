bush violet

1. NOUN

/ bʊʃ ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / bʊʃ ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
Any of several herbaceous plants of the genus Browallia, often grown for their blue, violet, or white flowers.
herbaceous plant grown for blue or violet flowers

Examples

The bush violet is popular for its bright, colorful blooms in summer gardens.
She planted several bush violets along the walkway for a splash of blue and purple.
Although the bush violet prefers partial shade, it will tolerate some sun.
