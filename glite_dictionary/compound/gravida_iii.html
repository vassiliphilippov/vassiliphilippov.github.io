gravida III

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈviːdə ˈθriː / (AmE), / ɡrəˈviːdə ˈθriː / (BrE)
a woman who is pregnant for the third time
woman pregnant for the third time
medical

Examples

The doctor noted that the patient was a gravida III with no complications in her previous pregnancies.
As a gravida III, she was familiar with the procedures of prenatal care.
The patient’s status as gravida III was recorded in her medical chart for reference.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gravida I - a woman who is pregnant for the first time
  • Degree Lower: gravida II - a woman who is pregnant for the second time
  • Degree Higher: gravida IV - a woman who is pregnant for the fourth time

Etymology

From Latin 'gravida' (pregnant), with Roman numeral III indicating the third pregnancy.
