bass part

1. NOUN

/ beɪs pɑrt / (AmE), / beɪs pɑːt / (BrE)
the lowest vocal or instrumental part in polyphonic music, forming the harmonic foundation of a piece
lowest part in polyphonic music

Examples

The choir's bass part added depth to the performance.
He enjoys singing the bass part in his a cappella group.
Learning the bass part requires a good ear for harmony.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: alto part - the second highest part in choral or polyphonic music
