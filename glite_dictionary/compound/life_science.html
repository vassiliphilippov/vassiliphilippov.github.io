life science

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪəns / (BrE)
any of the sciences that deal with living organisms and their life processes, such as biology, botany, and zoology.
branch of science studying living organisms

Examples

She decided to major in life science because she was fascinated by living organisms.
The university offers a wide range of courses in life science, including genetics and microbiology.
Careers in life science can include research, teaching, and roles in the healthcare industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biology - the scientific study of life and living organisms
