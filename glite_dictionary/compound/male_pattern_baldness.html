male pattern baldness

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈpætərn ˈbɔldnɪs / (AmE), / meɪl ˈpætn ˈbɔːldnəs / (BrE)
a common type of hair loss in men, characterized by thinning hair and receding hairline at the crown and temples
type of hair loss in men
medical

Examples

His father also suffered from male pattern baldness, so he expected to lose his hair early.
A variety of treatments claim to slow the progression of male pattern baldness.
He noticed the first signs of male pattern baldness in his late twenties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alopecia - medical term for hair loss
