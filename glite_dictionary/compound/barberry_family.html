barberry family

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrˌbɛri ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəri ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of shrubs or herbs, scientifically known as Berberidaceae, including barberry and related plants
plant family (Berberidaceae)

Examples

The barberry family contains many species used in traditional medicine and ornamental gardening.
Several members of the barberry family thrive in temperate regions worldwide.
Botanists are studying the unique characteristics of plants within the barberry family.
