duck hunting

1. NOUN

/ dʌk ˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / dʌk ˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of hunting ducks, typically with firearms
the sport of hunting ducks

Examples

Every fall, my family goes duck hunting at the nearby lake.
Some people view duck hunting as a traditional sport, while others oppose it on ethical grounds.
Proper clothing and equipment are essential for a successful duck hunting trip.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunting - the act of pursuing and killing wild animals
