air transport

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
the conveyance of passengers or cargo by aircraft
transportation by aircraft

Examples

Air transport has revolutionized global trade and allowed for faster movement of goods.
Many companies rely on air transport to deliver their products internationally.
The demand for air transport increased rapidly after the invention of jet engines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air travel - traveling from place to place by airplane
  • Antonym: sea transport - transport of goods or passengers by sea
