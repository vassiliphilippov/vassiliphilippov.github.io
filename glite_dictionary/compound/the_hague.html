The Hague

1. PROPN

/ ðə ˈheɪɡ / (AmE), / ðə ˈheɪɡ / (BrE)
A city in the western Netherlands that is the seat of the Dutch government and parliament, the Supreme Court, and the Council of State, and hosts the International Court of Justice.
city in the Netherlands; Dutch seat of government

Examples

Many international organizations have their headquarters in The Hague.
The peace talks were held in The Hague last summer.
As the seat of government, The Hague is home to the Dutch parliament.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Den Haag', short for '’s-Gravenhage', meaning 'the Count’s hedge or enclosure'.
