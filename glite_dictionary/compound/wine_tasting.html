wine tasting

1. NOUN

/ waɪn ˈteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / waɪn ˈteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
a social event where people taste and evaluate different wines
event to sample and compare wines

Examples

We attended a wine tasting at the local vineyard last weekend.
The wine tasting featured selections from several different countries.
She arranged a wine tasting to celebrate her birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tasting - a session of sampling and evaluating food or drink
