esculent plant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛskjʊlənt plænt / (AmE), / ˈɛskjʊlənt plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that is suitable for eating; an edible plant or vegetable
an edible plant or vegetable

Examples

The garden is full of esculent plants ready for harvest.
Many wild esculent plants can provide nutrition in times of scarcity.
Researchers are cataloging native esculent plants to promote sustainable agriculture.

Synset Examples

The garden is full of esculent plants ready for harvest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable - a plant or part of a plant used as food
  • Antonym: inedible plant - a plant that is not suitable for eating

Etymology

From Latin 'esculentus' (edible), from 'escā' (food) + 'plant.'
