false teeth

1. NOUN

/ fɔls tiθ / (AmE), / fɔːls tiːθ / (BrE)
artificial teeth, typically removable, used to replace missing natural teeth
removable artificial teeth (dentures)

Examples

My grandmother has worn false teeth since her early forties.
He placed his false teeth in a glass of water before going to bed.
After losing a tooth, she considered getting false teeth to improve her smile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: denture - a removable plate or frame holding artificial teeth
