Sudanese pound

1. NOUN

/ suːˈdæniz paʊnd / (AmE), / suːˈdɑːniːz paʊnd / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Sudan, equal to 100 piasters
currency unit of Sudan

Examples

The value of the Sudanese pound has fluctuated significantly in recent years.
He exchanged his dollars for Sudanese pounds at the local bank.
Many shops in Khartoum only accept payment in Sudanese pounds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pound - a basic unit of currency in various countries
