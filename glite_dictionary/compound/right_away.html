right away

1. ADV

/ raɪt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / raɪt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
immediately; without delay or hesitation.
immediately, without delay

Examples

I'll call you right away if there are any updates.
She left the room right away when the meeting ended.
The doctor saw the patient right away because it was an emergency.

Synset Examples

he answered immediately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immediately - without any delay
  • Synonym: straightaway - at once; without delay
  • Antonym: eventually - after a period of time; in the end
