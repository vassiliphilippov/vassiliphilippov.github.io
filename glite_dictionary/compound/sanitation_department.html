sanitation department

1. NOUN

/ ˌsænɪˈteɪʃən dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˌsænɪˈteɪʃən dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the department of a local government that is responsible for collecting and disposing of garbage and maintaining cleanliness in public areas
department responsible for public waste collection

Examples

The sanitation department collected the household trash early this morning.
Problems with garbage overflow were reported to the sanitation department.
Many cities rely on the sanitation department to keep streets clean and safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public works department - department in charge of public services and infrastructure
