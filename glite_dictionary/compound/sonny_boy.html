sonny boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌni bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌni bɔɪ / (BrE)
A familiar or affectionate term of address for a young boy.
affectionate term for a young boy
informal

Examples

Come here, sonny boy, and let me take a look at that scrape.
The old man ruffled the hair of the neighborhood sonny boy with a laugh.
Don't worry, sonny boy, everything will work out in the end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lad - another informal word for a boy
  • Synonym: boy - a male child
