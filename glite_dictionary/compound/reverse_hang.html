reverse hang

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvɜrs hæŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs hæŋ / (BrE)
A position or movement in which a person hangs with their arms extended backwards, often used in gymnastics or fitness activities.
hang with arms extended backwards

Examples

She demonstrated a reverse hang on the pull-up bar as part of her training routine.
The gymnast held a reverse hang before transitioning into her next move.
Performing a reverse hang requires strong shoulder flexibility and control.
