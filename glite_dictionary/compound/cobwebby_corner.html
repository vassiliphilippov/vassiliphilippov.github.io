cobwebby corner

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑbˌwɛbi ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / ˈkɒbwɛbi ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
a corner or recess that is filled with cobwebs, often suggesting neglect or disuse
corner filled with cobwebs

Examples

She swept the cobwebby corner behind the old bookcase to make the room look tidier.
A thick layer of dust had settled in the cobwebby corner of the attic.
He nervously glanced toward the cobwebby corner, half expecting something to crawl out.

Synset Examples

The cobwebby corners of the house needed a thorough cleaning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cobweb - a fine, tangled web spun by a spider
