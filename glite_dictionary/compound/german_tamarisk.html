German tamarisk

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɜrmən ˈtæməˌrɪsk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈtæmərɪsk / (BrE)
a Eurasian shrub resembling the tamarisk, known for its slender branches and small scale-like leaves
a Eurasian shrub similar to tamarisk

Examples

The German tamarisk thrives in sandy soils along riverbanks and coastal areas.
Conservationists are monitoring the spread of German tamarisk to protect native plant species.
She planted a German tamarisk in her garden for its delicate pink flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamarisk - a genus of shrubs and small trees with slender branches and small leaves
