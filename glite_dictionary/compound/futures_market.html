futures market

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuːtʃərz ˌmɑɹkɪt / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃəz ˌmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A market where standardized futures contracts are bought and sold.
market for trading futures contracts

Examples

She invested heavily in the futures market to hedge against price fluctuations.
The Chicago Board of Trade is one of the oldest futures markets in the world.
Companies use the futures market to lock in prices for essential raw materials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commodities market - market for trading raw materials or primary products
