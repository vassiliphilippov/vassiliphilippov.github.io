papaya juice

1. NOUN

/ pəˈpaɪə dʒus / (AmE), / pəˈpaɪə dʒuːs / (BrE)
the liquid obtained by extracting or blending the flesh of papayas, often consumed as a beverage
juice made from papayas

Examples

She ordered a glass of papaya juice with her breakfast.
Fresh papaya juice is a popular drink in many tropical countries.
He blended ripe papayas to make homemade papaya juice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruit juice - juice extracted from the flesh of fruit
