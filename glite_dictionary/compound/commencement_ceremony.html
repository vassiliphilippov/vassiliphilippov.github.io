commencement ceremony

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛnsmənt ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / kəˈmɛnsmənt ˈsɛrəˌməʊni / (BrE)
a formal academic event at which degrees or diplomas are officially conferred to graduates
formal academic graduation event

Examples

The university's commencement ceremony drew proud families from across the country.
She delivered the valedictory address at her college's commencement ceremony.
After the commencement ceremony, the graduates celebrated with friends and family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graduation - completion of an academic degree or course of study
  • Synonym: convocation - a formal assembly, often for the conferring of academic degrees

Etymology

From 'commencement' (beginning), referencing the start of a graduate's new life stage, and 'ceremony', from Latin 'caerimonia'.
