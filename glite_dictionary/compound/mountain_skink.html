mountain skink

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntən skɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn skɪŋk / (BrE)
a small lizard found in rocky, mountainous areas of the southwestern United States and Mexico, frequenting oak and pine habitats
a small lizard of southwestern US and Mexico

Examples

The mountain skink is well adapted to life among the rocky slopes of Arizona.
We spotted a mountain skink basking on a stone in the cool morning sun.
Researchers are studying the mountain skink to learn more about its habitat preferences.
