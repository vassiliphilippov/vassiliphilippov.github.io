lounging robe

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ roʊb / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒɪŋ rəʊb / (BrE)
a comfortable robe worn before dressing or while relaxing at home
robe for relaxing or before dressing

Examples

After her shower, she wrapped herself in a soft lounging robe and made coffee.
He spent his Sunday morning reading the newspaper in his favorite lounging robe.
A plush lounging robe makes a thoughtful and practical gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressing gown - a robe worn typically over nightwear
  • Synonym: bathrobe - a robe worn after bathing
