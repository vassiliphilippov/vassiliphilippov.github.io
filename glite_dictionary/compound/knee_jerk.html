knee jerk

1. NOUN

/ ni dʒɜrk / (AmE), / niː dʒɜːk / (BrE)
an involuntary reflex in which the lower leg suddenly kicks out after the patellar tendon just below the knee is sharply tapped
automatic leg reflex caused by knee tap

Examples

The doctor tested the knee jerk response during the physical examination.
A sharp tap below the kneecap usually triggers a noticeable knee jerk.
Her knee jerk was normal, indicating healthy nerve function.
