dirty joke

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that contains sexual or indecent subject matter, often considered rude or offensive
sexual or indecent joke

Examples

He told a dirty joke at the party, and everyone burst out laughing.
Some people find dirty jokes offensive, while others find them funny.
She asked him to stop making dirty jokes in the office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blue joke - a joke that is risqué or obscene
  • Antonym: clean joke - a joke without sexual or indecent content
