total shitfuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈʃɪtˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈʃɪtfʌk / (BrE)
A situation that is extremely chaotic, disastrous, or poorly managed.
utterly chaotic or disastrous situation
vulgar slang

Examples

After the merger, the company became a total shitfuck of mismanagement and confusion.
His attempt to organize the event was a total shitfuck from start to finish.
The meeting descended into a total shitfuck when tempers flared and everyone started yelling.

Synset Examples

This project turned into a total shitfuck overnight.
