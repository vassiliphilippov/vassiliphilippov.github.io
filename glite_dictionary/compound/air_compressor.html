air compressor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr kəmˌprɛsər / (AmE), / ˈeə kəmˌprɛsə / (BrE)
A machine that takes in air at atmospheric pressure and delivers it at a higher pressure, often for powering tools or inflating objects.
device that compresses air to higher pressure

Examples

The mechanic used an air compressor to inflate the car tires quickly and efficiently.
Before painting the fence, she cleaned it with an air compressor to remove all the dust.
Industrial workshops often have a large air compressor to power various pneumatic tools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compressor - a machine that compresses gases
