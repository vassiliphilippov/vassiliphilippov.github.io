Last Supper

1. PROPN

/ læst ˈsʌpɚ / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈsʌpə / (BrE)
The final meal that Jesus shared with his disciples before his crucifixion, traditionally regarded as a Passover supper.
Jesus's final meal with his disciples

Examples

The Last Supper has been depicted by many famous artists throughout history.
Christians commemorate the Last Supper during the celebration of the Eucharist.
Leonardo da Vinci's painting of the Last Supper is one of the most renowned artworks in the world.
