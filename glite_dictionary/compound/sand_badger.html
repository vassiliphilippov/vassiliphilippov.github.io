sand badger

1. NOUN

/ sænd ˈbædʒər / (AmE), / sand ˈbadʒə / (BrE)
a Southeast Asian badger with a pig-like snout, found in sandy habitats
Southeast Asian badger with a pig-like snout

Examples

The sand badger is known for its distinctive snout and burrowing habits.
Researchers observed a family of sand badgers digging for insects in the forest.
Unlike other species, the sand badger is adapted to sandy environments.
