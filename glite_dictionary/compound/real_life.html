real life

1. NOUN

/ ˈriəl laɪf / (AmE), / ˈrɪəɫ laɪf / (BrE)
the practical or actual world, as distinct from the academic, theoretical, or fictional world
the practical world, not academic or fictional

Examples

After graduating, she found that real life was more challenging than she expected.
Many theories sound great in class, but real life is much more complicated.
In real life, solutions are rarely as neat as they seem in textbooks.

Synset Examples

a good consultant must have a lot of experience in the real world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: real world - the practical world as opposed to an idealized or academic one
