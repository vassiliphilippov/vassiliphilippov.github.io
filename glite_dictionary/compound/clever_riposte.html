clever riposte

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɛvɚ rɪˈpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə rɪˈpɒst / (BrE)
a quick, witty, and effective reply to a comment or criticism
quick, witty reply

Examples

Her clever riposte caught everyone by surprise and defused the tension instantly.
During the debate, his clever riposte silenced his opponent.
The comedian was known for delivering a clever riposte to every heckler in the audience.

Synset Examples

His clever riposte left everyone in the room laughing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retort - a quick, witty, or sharp reply
  • Synonym: rejoinder - a reply, especially a sharp or witty one
