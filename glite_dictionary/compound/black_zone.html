black zone

1. NOUN

/ blæk zoʊn / (AmE), / blæk zəʊn / (BrE)
an area regarded as dangerous or unsafe, especially one with high levels of crime or risk
dangerous or high-crime area

Examples

After dark, the alley was labeled a black zone by the local police.
Travelers are advised to steer clear of the black zone downtown.
The government allocated more resources to improve safety in the designated black zones.

Synset Examples

We avoid the black zone due to safety concerns.
The area has become a black zone for crime rates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: danger zone - an area perceived as risky or unsafe
  • Antonym: safe zone - an area considered free from danger
