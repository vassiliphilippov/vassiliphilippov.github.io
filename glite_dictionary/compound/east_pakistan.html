East Pakistan

1. PROPN

/ ist pɑːˈkɪstæn / (AmE), / iːst pækɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
the former eastern provincial region of Pakistan, which became the independent country of Bangladesh in 1971
former name for modern Bangladesh (1947–1971)

Examples

My grandfather was born in East Pakistan before it became Bangladesh.
The events of 1971 led to the independence of East Pakistan.
Many families migrated from East Pakistan to other regions during the Partition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bangladesh - South Asian country; former East Pakistan after 1971
  • Antonym: West Pakistan - the western part of Pakistan before 1971; now simply Pakistan
