poor white trash

1. NOUN

/ pɔr waɪt træʃ / (AmE), / pɔː waɪt træʃ / (BrE)
(offensive, slang) A derogatory term for White people who are impoverished or considered to be of low social status.
offensive term for poor White people
slang offensive

Examples

He was insulted when someone called him poor white trash at the gathering.
The term poor white trash appears in older novels depicting class prejudice in the rural South.
Using poor white trash is both derogatory and offensive in modern conversation.
