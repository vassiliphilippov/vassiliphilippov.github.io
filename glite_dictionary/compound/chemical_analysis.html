chemical analysis

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛmɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɛmɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of determining the chemical components or elements present in a substance by separating or identifying them
identification of elements or compounds in a substance

Examples

The laboratory performed a thorough chemical analysis on the new compound.
She specializes in chemical analysis to determine the purity of pharmaceuticals.
A chemical analysis revealed the presence of several toxic metals in the water sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analysis - detailed examination of the elements or structure of something
