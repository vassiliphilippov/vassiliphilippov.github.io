dumb blonde

1. NOUN

/ dʌm blɑnd / (AmE), / dʌm blɒnd / (BrE)
(offensive) A stereotype of a blonde-haired woman considered as lacking intelligence or common sense.
stereotype of unintelligent blonde woman
offensive

Examples

The movie featured a dumb blonde character who provided comic relief.
He was criticized for calling his coworker a dumb blonde during the meeting.
She spoke out against the dumb blonde stereotype, saying it was outdated and offensive.

Synset Examples

The movie featured a classic dumb blonde character.
He teased her, calling her a dumb blonde.
She laughed at the dumb blonde stereotype.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blonde - person, especially a woman, with light-colored hair
