cognitive scientist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːɡnətɪv ˈsaɪəntɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnətɪv ˈsaɪəntɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies mental processes such as perception, memory, reasoning, and language.
scientist studying mental processes

Examples

As a cognitive scientist, she is interested in how people learn new languages.
The conference brought together cognitive scientists from universities around the world.
Many cognitive scientists collaborate with computer engineers to study artificial intelligence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychologist - an expert in psychology or the study of mind and behavior
  • Synonym: neuroscientist - a scientist who studies the nervous system, especially the brain
