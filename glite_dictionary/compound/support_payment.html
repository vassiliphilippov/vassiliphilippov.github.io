support payment

1. NOUN

/ səˈpɔrt ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
a payment made regularly by one person for the maintenance of another, especially as ordered by a court
payment for maintenance of another person
legal

Examples

After the divorce, he was required to make a monthly support payment to his ex-wife.
Failing to pay the court-ordered support payment can have serious legal consequences.
She relies on the support payment to help cover her children's expenses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alimony - court-ordered financial support paid to a spouse after separation or divorce
  • Synonym: child support - regular payments made to support a child after divorce or separation
