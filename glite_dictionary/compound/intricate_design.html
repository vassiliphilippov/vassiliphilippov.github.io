intricate design

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntrɪkət dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɪntrɪkət dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design or pattern that is very detailed and complex, with many interconnected parts
detailed and complex pattern or arrangement

Examples

The artist spent months creating an intricate design on the ceramic vase.
Each page of the book featured an intricate design in the corners, adding elegance to the text.
The jeweler was known for his intricate design work, making each piece unique and beautiful.

Synset Examples

He admired the intricate design of the teacup and saucer.
He admired the intricate design of the fabric on the sofa.
She admired the intricate design of his baldric.
She admired the intricate designs on the phylactery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elaborate design - design that is highly detailed and carefully planned
  • Antonym: simple design - design that is plain and lacks complexity
