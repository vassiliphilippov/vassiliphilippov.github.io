motherfunking late

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌðərˌfʌŋkɪŋ ˈleɪt / (AmE), / ˌmʌðəˌfʌŋkɪŋ ˈleɪt / (BrE)
Extremely late; arriving at a much later time than expected, used for emphasis (informal, vulgar slang).
extremely late (slang, vulgar)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He was motherfunking late again for the meeting.
The bus was motherfunking late and everyone was furious.
She made a motherfunking late entrance to the party, turning every head.

Synset Examples

He was motherfunking late again for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motherfucking late - arriving much later than expected, strongest vulgar intensifier version
  • Degree Lower: late - arriving after the expected time
  • Degree Higher: extremely late - very much after the expected time, even stronger degree

Etymology

Formed by blending 'motherfucking' (from 'mother' + 'fucking') with 'funk', as a euphemism for the vulgar intensifier.
