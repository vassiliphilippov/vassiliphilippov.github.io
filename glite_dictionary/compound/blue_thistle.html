blue thistle

1. NOUN

/ blu ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / bluː ˈθɪsl / (BrE)
a prickly European plant with spiky blue flowers, often considered a weed; now also found in the United States
a prickly plant with blue flowers

Examples

The fields were dotted with blue thistle, adding a splash of color among the green grass.
Gardeners sometimes remove blue thistle because its sharp leaves can make it unwanted in lawns.
I spotted a beautiful blue thistle growing by the roadside during our hike.
