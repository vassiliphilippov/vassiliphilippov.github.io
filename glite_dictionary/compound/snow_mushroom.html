snow mushroom

1. NOUN

/ snoʊ ˈmʌʃˌrum / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
an edible, gelatinous white fungus, popular in East Asian cuisine, often sold dried and used in soups and desserts
edible gelatinous white fungus used in Asian cuisine

Examples

The snow mushroom is prized for its ability to absorb flavors in sweet and savory dishes.
Many traditional Chinese desserts include dried snow mushroom for its unique texture.
Before cooking, soak the snow mushroom in warm water to rehydrate it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tremella - a genus of fungi that includes species called snow mushrooms
  • Synonym: white fungus - another common name for the snow mushroom in English
