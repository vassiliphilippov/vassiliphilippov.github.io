cordial reception

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔr.di.əl rɪˈsɛp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.di.əl rɪˈsɛp.ʃən / (BrE)
a warm and friendly welcome given to guests or strangers
warm and friendly welcome

Examples

The ambassador received a cordial reception upon arriving at the conference.
Everyone who visited the countryside village commented on the cordial reception they were given.
We were grateful for the cordial reception extended by our gracious hosts.
