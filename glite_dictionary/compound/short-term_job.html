short-term job

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrt tɝm dʒɑb / (AmE), / ʃɔːt tɜːm dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job that is expected to last for a limited or brief period of time, often for a few weeks or months
temporary job for a limited period

Examples

Many students take on short-term jobs during their summer holidays to earn extra money.
Due to the project’s limited duration, only short-term jobs were offered to the applicants.
After being laid off, he relied on a series of short-term jobs to support his family.

Synset Examples

The itinerant worker found short-term jobs wherever he could.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temporary job - a job that is not permanent and only lasts for a short period
