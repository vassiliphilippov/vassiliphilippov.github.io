carillon playing

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɪl.ɒn ˈpleɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈrɪl.ɒn ˈpleɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or practice of performing music by playing a set of tower-hung bells known as a carillon
playing music on a set of tower bells

Examples

She has specialized in carillon playing since she joined the university's bell tower society.
The intricate art of carillon playing requires both strength and musical sensitivity.
Every Sunday, his beautiful carillon playing echoes across the city square.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carillon - a set of stationary bells hung in a tower and played using a keyboard
