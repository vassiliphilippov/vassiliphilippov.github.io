court-ordered

1. ADJ

/ kɔrt ˈɔrdərd / (AmE), / kɔːt ˈɔːdəd / (BrE)
Ordered by a court of law; mandated by legal authority.
ordered by a court of law

Examples

The company complied with the court-ordered compensation for its employees.
He was placed under court-ordered supervision after his release.
