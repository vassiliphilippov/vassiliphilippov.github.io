bank clerk

1. NOUN

/ bæŋk klɝk / (AmE), / bæŋk klɑːk / (BrE)
a person employed by a bank who handles customer transactions such as receiving and paying out money
bank employee handling transactions

Examples

The bank clerk helped me deposit a check into my account.
As a bank clerk, she handles many transactions every day.
I asked the bank clerk about opening a new savings account.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teller - a person who receives and pays out money at a bank
