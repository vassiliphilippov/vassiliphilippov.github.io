fen orchid

1. NOUN

/ fɛn ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / fɛn ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
a small orchid found in wetland areas of eastern North America and Europe, with two nearly basal leaves and dull yellow-green flowers
a wetland orchid with yellow-green flowers

Examples

The fen orchid is now considered endangered in many parts of its native range.
Botanists surveyed the marsh to find specimens of the rare fen orchid.
The dull yellow-green flowers of the fen orchid help it blend into the surrounding grasses.
