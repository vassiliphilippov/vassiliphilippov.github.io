silver spruce

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˌsprus / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌspruːs / (BrE)
A tall spruce tree with blue-green needles and a dense conical crown, becoming columnar with age as lower branches sweep downward.
a spruce tree with blue-green needles

Examples

The silver spruce in their backyard provided welcome shade during the summer months.
Many landscapers value the silver spruce for its distinctive blue-green foliage and elegant shape.
As it matured, the silver spruce developed sweeping lower branches that nearly touched the ground.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Colorado blue spruce - a North American species of spruce tree known for its bluish needles
