party theme

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːrti θiːm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti θiːm / (BrE)
the central idea or concept around which the decorations, activities, or style of a party are organized
central idea or concept for a party

Examples

My sister picked a tropical party theme for her birthday this year.
The party theme was superheroes, so everyone dressed accordingly.
Choosing the right party theme can make planning much easier.
