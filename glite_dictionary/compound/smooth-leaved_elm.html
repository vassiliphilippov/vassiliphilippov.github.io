smooth-leaved elm

1. NOUN

/ smuð livd ɛlm / (AmE), / smuːð liːvd elm / (BrE)
A European elm tree noted for its shiny, smooth leaves, often grown as an ornamental plant.
European elm with smooth, shiny leaves

Examples

The smooth-leaved elm is prized for its attractive foliage in urban landscapes.
After the storm, only the smooth-leaved elm in the garden remained unscathed.
He planted a smooth-leaved elm near the entrance to enhance the property's appearance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: elm - a type of tree with broad leaves, belonging to the genus Ulmus
