Hupa language

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːpə ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhuːpə ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Athabaskan language spoken by the Hupa people of northwestern California
Athabaskan language of the Hupa people

Examples

The Hupa language is traditionally spoken in the Hoopa Valley of northwestern California.
Efforts are underway to preserve and revitalize the Hupa language among younger generations.
She learned the Hupa language to connect with her ancestral heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Athabaskan language - a family of Native American languages spoken in North America

Etymology

Hupa, referring to the Native American people of northwestern California, combined with 'language'.
