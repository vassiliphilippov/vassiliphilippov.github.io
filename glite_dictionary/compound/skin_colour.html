skin colour

1. NOUN

/ skɪn ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / skɪn ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
the shade or pigmentation of a person's skin, especially the face
the shade or pigmentation of skin

Examples

Her skin colour changed slightly after spending the summer outdoors.
Many cosmetics are available in different shades to match every skin colour.
He had a fair skin colour with light freckles across his cheeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complexion - the natural color and appearance of the skin, especially the face
