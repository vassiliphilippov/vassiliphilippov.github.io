Office of Intelligence Support

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːfɪs əv ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs əv ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
an agency within the U.S. Department of the Treasury that manages intelligence relationships and connects the Intelligence Community with officials handling international economic policy
U.S. Treasury agency overseeing intelligence links

Examples

The Office of Intelligence Support plays a key role in coordinating economic intelligence activities for the Treasury Department.
Officials at the Office of Intelligence Support briefed policymakers on recent international financial threats.
Changes in leadership at the Office of Intelligence Support often reflect shifts in U.S. economic security priorities.
