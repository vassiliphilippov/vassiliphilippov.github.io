Begonia cheimantha

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡoʊniə kaɪˈmænθə / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə kaɪˈmænθə / (BrE)
A hybrid winter-blooming begonia cultivated for its many large pink flowers.
a hybrid winter-blooming begonia

Examples

The Begonia cheimantha produces abundant pink blooms even in the cold months of winter.
She decorated her living room with pots of Begonia cheimantha to brighten the space during the holidays.
Gardeners appreciate Begonia cheimantha for its resilience and vibrant winter flowers.

Etymology

From New Latin, combining 'Begonia' (the genus, named after Michel Bégon) with likely Greek 'cheim-' (winter) and '-anthos' (flower).
