house centipede

1. NOUN

/ haʊs ˈsɛn.tɪ.piːd / (AmE), / haʊs ˈsɛn.tɪ.piːd / (BrE)
a long-legged centipede often found in damp indoor places such as cellars and bathrooms
long-legged centipede found indoors

Examples

We found a house centipede hiding in the basement yesterday.
The bathroom attracts house centipedes because of the moisture.
A house centipede can move very quickly across the wall when disturbed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centipede - a predatory arthropod with many legs, related to the house centipede
