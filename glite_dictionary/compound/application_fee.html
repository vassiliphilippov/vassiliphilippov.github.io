application fee

1. NOUN

/ ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən fiː / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən fiː / (BrE)
a sum of money paid when requesting admission, permission, or consideration for something, such as a job, school, or service
fee paid when applying for something

Examples

Most universities require an application fee when you submit your admission form.
Payment of the application fee does not guarantee acceptance into the program.
She forgot to include the application fee with her documents, so her application was delayed.

Synset Examples

Make sure to include the application fee with submission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registration fee - fee paid to register for something
