gas stove

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs stoʊv / (AmE), / ɡæs stəʊv / (BrE)
A stove that uses gas as a fuel for cooking, typically equipped with burners (gas rings) and an oven.
stove using gas for cooking

Examples

She prefers cooking on a gas stove because it heats up quickly.
Their old apartment came with a beautiful vintage gas stove.
Always make sure the gas stove is turned off before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: electric stove - a stove powered by electricity
