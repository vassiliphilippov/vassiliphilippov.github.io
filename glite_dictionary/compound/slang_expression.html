slang expression

1. NOUN

/ slæŋ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / slæŋ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
an informal word or phrase that is used in place of more standard terms, often belonging to a particular group or subculture
informal, nonstandard word or phrase

Examples

She used a slang expression that I had never heard before.
Teenagers often invent new slang expressions to communicate with each other.
The teacher asked the students not to use any slang expressions in their essays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slang - very informal language, especially words and expressions that are used by particular groups of people

2. NOUN

/ slæŋ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / slæŋ ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
an informal word or phrase used in casual speech, not usually suitable for formal situations
informal word or phrase

Examples

She used a slang expression that confused her grandmother.
Many teenagers invent new slang expressions to describe their experiences.
That slang expression is common in American movies but not used in formal writing.

Synset Examples

their speech was full of slang expressions
