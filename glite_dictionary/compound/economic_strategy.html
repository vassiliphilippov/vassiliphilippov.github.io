economic strategy

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkɑːnəmɪk ˈstrætədʒi / (AmE), / iːˈkɒnəmɪk ˈstrætədʒi / (BrE)
a plan or set of actions designed to manage and improve the economic performance of a country, organization, or business
plan for managing the economy

Examples

The government unveiled a new economic strategy to address unemployment and promote growth.
A successful economic strategy requires careful planning and long-term vision.
Companies often revise their economic strategies in response to changing market conditions.

Synset Examples

Many voters are concerned about NuLab's economic strategies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economic policy - a course of action adopted by a government or organization relating to economic matters
