exclusive title

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈskluːsɪv ˈtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈskluːsɪv ˈtaɪtəl / (BrE)
A game or product that is available only on a specific platform or system and not on others.
game/product only for one platform

Examples

Many gamers purchase a console specifically to play its exclusive titles.
The new action game will be an exclusive title for PlayStation 5.
Microsoft announced several exclusive titles at their annual gaming showcase.

Synset Examples

The Xbone offers a great library of exclusive titles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclusive - restricted to a particular person, group, or area
