fry cook

1. NOUN

/ fraɪ kʊk / (AmE), / fraɪ kʊk / (BrE)
a cook whose primary responsibility is to prepare fried foods, especially in a restaurant or diner
cook specializing in frying foods

Examples

The fry cook was busy preparing baskets of golden French fries during the lunch rush.
As a fry cook, he learned how to work quickly and safely with hot oil.
The restaurant hired an experienced fry cook to improve their fried chicken recipe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chef - a professional cook, often the head of a kitchen
  • Degree Lower: grill cook - a cook who specializes in grilling foods
