mock turtleneck collar

1. NOUN

/ mɑk ˈtɝt̬l̩ˌnɛk ˈkɑlɚ / (AmE), / mɒk ˈtɜːtl̩nɛk ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A collar resembling a turtleneck, but with both ends sewn to the neckline rather than folded over, standing up around the neck.
collar similar to a turtleneck; sewn to neckline

Examples

He wore a black mock turtleneck collar under his jacket for a stylish look.
Many athletic shirts use a mock turtleneck collar to provide added warmth without bulk.
The mock turtleneck collar stays in place better than a traditional turtleneck during physical activity.

Synset Examples

The mock polo neck clings to the neck smoothly, is easy to manufacture, and works well with a zip closure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtleneck - a high, close-fitting collar that folds over and covers the neck
