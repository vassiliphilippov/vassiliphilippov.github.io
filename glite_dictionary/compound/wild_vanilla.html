wild vanilla

1. NOUN

/ waɪld vəˈnɪlə / (AmE), / waɪld vəˈnɪlə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its leaves that have a fragrance resembling vanilla.
fragrant-leaved perennial plant

Examples

The leaves of the wild vanilla release a sweet aroma when crushed.
Botanists studied the unique properties of wild vanilla growing in wetland areas.
Many gardeners appreciate wild vanilla for its attractive foliage and pleasant scent.
