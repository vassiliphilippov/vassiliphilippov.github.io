poetic rhythm

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈɛtɪk ˈrɪðəm / (AmE), / pəʊˈɛtɪk ˈrɪðəm / (BrE)
the pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables in poetry, giving verse its musical quality and flow
pattern of stresses in poetry

Examples

The poetic rhythm of the sonnet enhanced its emotional impact.
She studied the poetic rhythm of different literary traditions for her thesis.
Children enjoy the poetic rhythm in nursery rhymes as they clap along.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meter - the structured pattern of rhythm in poetry, often organized into feet
