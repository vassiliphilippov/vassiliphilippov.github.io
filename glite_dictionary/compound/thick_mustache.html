thick mustache

1. NOUN

/ θɪk məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / θɪk məˈstɑːʃ / (BrE)
A mustache that is large and bushy, with dense hair growth.
large, bushy mustache

Examples

He wore a thick mustache that made him look older than he was.
Her uncle's thick mustache was the first thing people noticed about him.
The detective twirled his thick mustache thoughtfully while considering the evidence.

Synset Examples

She called him a walrus because of his thick mustache.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: mustache - Facial hair grown on the upper lip
  • Degree Higher: handlebar mustache - A mustache with the ends shaped upward
