twelve noon

1. NOUN

/ twɛlv nun / (AmE), / twɛlv nuːn / (BrE)
The middle of the day; 12:00 p.m.
12:00 p.m.; midday

Examples

Lunch will be served promptly at twelve noon in the dining hall.
The meeting is scheduled for twelve noon, so please arrive a few minutes early.
At exactly twelve noon, the bell in the town square began to chime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: midday - the middle of the day, around 12:00 p.m.
  • Synonym: noon - 12 o'clock in the middle of the day
  • Antonym: midnight - 12 o'clock at night, opposite of noon
