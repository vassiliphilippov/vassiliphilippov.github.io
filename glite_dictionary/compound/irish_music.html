Irish music

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.rɪʃ ˈmju.zɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.rɪʃ ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
Traditional music originating from Ireland, featuring characteristic instruments and styles.
traditional music from Ireland

Examples

He has collected rare recordings of Irish music from the 1920s.
At the festival, the band played lively Irish music that got everyone dancing.
Her favorite genre is Irish music because of its unique melodies and rhythms.

Synset Examples

He loves Irish music and dances every weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folk music - traditional music passed down through generations, often by oral tradition
