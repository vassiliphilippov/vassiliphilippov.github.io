coat button

1. NOUN

/ koʊt ˈbʌtn̩ / (AmE), / kəʊt ˈbʌtn̩ / (BrE)
a button used for fastening a coat
button on a coat

Examples

She sewed a new coat button on after the old one fell off.
He lost a coat button while rushing to catch the bus.
The child's coat button was shaped like a star.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: button - a small fastener used to join two parts of clothing
