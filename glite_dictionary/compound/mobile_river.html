Mobile River

1. PROPN

/ ˈmoʊˌbiːl ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌbiːl ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in southwestern Alabama, United States, that flows into Mobile Bay
river in Alabama, United States

Examples

The Mobile River delta is home to a rich variety of wildlife.
The city of Mobile lies on the western bank of the Mobile River.
Commercial ships travel up and down the Mobile River to reach the port.

Etymology

Named after the city of Mobile, which itself takes its name from the indigenous Maubila people.
