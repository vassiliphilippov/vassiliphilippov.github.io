foot file

1. NOUN

/ fʊt faɪl / (AmE), / fʊt faɪl / (BrE)
A tool used to remove dead skin from the soles of the feet, typically by scrubbing.
tool for scrubbing soles of feet

Examples

She used a foot file to smooth the rough skin on her heels.
After soaking her feet, he handed her a foot file to help exfoliate.
A foot file can make a big difference in keeping your feet soft.
