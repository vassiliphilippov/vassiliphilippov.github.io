hand to hand

1. ADV

/ hænd tuː hænd / (AmE), / hænd tuː hænd / (BrE)
involving close physical contact or combat, especially between opponents within arm's reach
at close quarters in combat

Examples

The two boxers fought hand to hand for the final round of the match.
Soldiers engaged in hand to hand combat during the battle.
When the ammunition ran out, the fighters were forced into hand to hand struggle.

Synset Examples

fought hand to hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: close combat - combat occurring at very short range
