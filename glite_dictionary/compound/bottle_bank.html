bottle bank

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdəl bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəl bæŋk / (BrE)
a large container in a public place where people can deposit empty glass bottles for recycling
container for recycling bottles

Examples

We took all our empty jars and bottles to the bottle bank at the supermarket.
Recycling glass at the bottle bank helps reduce waste in landfills.
The city recently installed a new bottle bank in the park to encourage recycling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recycling bin - a container for collecting items to be recycled
