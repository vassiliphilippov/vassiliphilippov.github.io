eastern fence lizard

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːstɚn fɛns ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / ˈiːstən fens ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, active lizard (Sceloporus undulatus) native to the eastern United States and parts of Canada, often found on fences, rocks, and trees
small lizard found in eastern North America

Examples

We spotted an eastern fence lizard sunning itself on a wooden rail.
The eastern fence lizard is commonly found in forests and rocky outcrops.
Children watched as the eastern fence lizard darted across the garden wall.
