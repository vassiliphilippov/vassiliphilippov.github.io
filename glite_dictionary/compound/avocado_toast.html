avocado toast

1. NOUN

/ ˌævəˌkɑdoʊ ˈtoʊst / (AmE), / ˌavəˌkɑːdəʊ ˈtəʊst / (BrE)
A slice of toasted bread topped with mashed or sliced avocado, often garnished with other ingredients.
toast with mashed avocado topping

Examples

She ordered avocado toast with a poached egg for breakfast.
Many cafés offer avocado toast with a variety of toppings such as feta or tomatoes.
The popularity of avocado toast has soared among young urban professionals.

Synset Examples

Bird and Baby has the best avocado toast in town.
