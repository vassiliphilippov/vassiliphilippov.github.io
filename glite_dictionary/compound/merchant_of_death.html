merchant of death

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜrtʃənt əv dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːtʃənt ɒv dɛθ / (BrE)
a person who profits by trading in weapons, especially illegally or in ways that promote violence and conflict
person who profits from arms trade or violence

Examples

The notorious arms smuggler became known as a merchant of death in the international press.
Many people view those who sell weapons to conflict zones as merchants of death.
After the scandal, politicians accused the company of being a merchant of death.

Synset Examples

His actions made him a notorious merchant of death.
They called him a merchant of death for selling weapons.
The arms dealer was labeled a merchant of death.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arms dealer - person who buys and sells weapons
