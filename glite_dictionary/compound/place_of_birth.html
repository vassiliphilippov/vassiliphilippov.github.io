place of birth

1. NOUN

/ pleɪs əv bɜrθ / (AmE), / pleɪs əv bɜːθ / (BrE)
the town, city, or country where a person was born
location where someone was born

Examples

Please write your place of birth on the application form.
The certificate lists his place of birth as Manchester, England.
Knowing someone's place of birth can help trace their family history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birthplace - the town or place where someone was born
