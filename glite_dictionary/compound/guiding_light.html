guiding light

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪŋ ˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪŋ ˌlaɪt / (BrE)
an influential person who leads, inspires, or serves as an example to others, especially within a group or movement
an inspiring leader or example

Examples

She has always been a guiding light to young artists in the community.
His work as a humanitarian made him a guiding light for countless people.
The founder remained the guiding light of the organization until her retirement.

Synset Examples

he was host to a large gathering of luminaries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role model - someone whose behavior is emulated by others
  • Synonym: inspiration - someone or something that gives you ideas for doing something

Etymology

Metaphorical use of 'light' meaning source of guidance or inspiration.
