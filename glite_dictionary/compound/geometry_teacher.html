geometry teacher

1. NOUN

/ dʒiˈɑmətri ˈtiːtʃər / (AmE), / dʒɪˈɒmɪtri ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a teacher who specializes in or teaches the subject of geometry
teacher of geometry

Examples

My geometry teacher assigned challenging problems for us to solve this week.
The new geometry teacher uses interactive activities to make the lessons more engaging.
When I struggled with shapes, my geometry teacher patiently explained each concept.

Related Terms
