pine hyacinth

1. NOUN

/ paɪn haɪˈæsɪnθ / (AmE), / paɪn haɪˈæsɪnθ / (BrE)
a climbing plant native to Florida with pink to purple flowers, belonging to the clematis family
a Florida clematis with pink to purple flowers

Examples

The pine hyacinth adds a splash of color to Florida's woodlands every spring.
Gardeners admire the pine hyacinth for its delicate, purple-hued blossoms.
During our hike, we spotted a rare pine hyacinth climbing along a tree trunk.
