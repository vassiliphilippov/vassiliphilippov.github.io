Argentinian

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑrdʒənˈtɪniən / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒənˈtɪniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Argentina
person from Argentina

Examples

The Argentinian greeted us warmly when we arrived in Buenos Aires.
She married an Argentinian she met while traveling through South America.
Many Argentinians are passionate about football.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Argentine - a native or inhabitant of Argentina (alternative form)

Etymology

From Argentina + -ian, denoting origin or association.
