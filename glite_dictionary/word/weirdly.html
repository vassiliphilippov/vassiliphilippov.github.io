weirdly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪrdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪədli / (BrE)
In a strange, unusual, or unexpected manner.
in a strange or unusual way

Examples

He looked at me weirdly when I suggested going for a walk at midnight.
The film ended weirdly, leaving the audience puzzled and silent.
