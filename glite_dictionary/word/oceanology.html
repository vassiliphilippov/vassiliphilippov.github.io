oceanology

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊʃiəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌəʊʃiəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the physical and biological aspects of the oceans
study of the oceans

Examples

She decided to study oceanology because of her fascination with marine life and sea currents.
Advances in oceanology have increased our understanding of climate change and its effects on the ocean.
The university offers a master's degree program in oceanology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oceanography - the scientific study of the physical, chemical, and biological features of the ocean

Etymology

From 'ocean' + Greek -logia (study of).
