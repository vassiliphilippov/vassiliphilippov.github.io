promiscuousness

1. NOUN

/ prəˈmɪskjuəsnəs / (AmE), / prəˈmɪskjuːəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or practice of engaging in casual and indiscriminate sexual relations with multiple partners
casual sexual behavior

Examples

The novel explores the consequences of promiscuousness in a conservative society.
Some critics blamed the decline of traditional values on increasing promiscuousness.
He was often judged harshly for his reputation for promiscuousness among his peers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promiscuity - engaging in casual sexual relations frequently

Etymology

From Latin 'prōmiscuus' (mixed, indiscriminate) + English '-ness'.
