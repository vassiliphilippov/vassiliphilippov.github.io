25th

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-fourth in position; ordinal number for twenty-five.
ordinal number for twenty-five

Examples

He celebrated his 25th birthday with a big party.
The races are scheduled for the 25th of every month.
She finished in 25th place at the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-fifth - the ordinal number corresponding to 25
  • Degree Lower: 24th - coming next before the twenty-fifth
  • Degree Higher: 26th - coming next after the twenty-fifth
