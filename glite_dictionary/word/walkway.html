walkway

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːk.weɪ / (BrE)
a passage or path, often paved or constructed, reserved for people to walk on
a path for walking

Examples

They built a walkway through the gardens so visitors could enjoy the scenery.
A wooden walkway connected the parking area to the beach.
Please use the designated walkway for your safety.

Synset Examples

after the blizzard he shoveled the front walk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: path - a way or track laid down for walking or made by continual treading
  • Synonym: sidewalk - a paved path for pedestrians at the side of a road (American English)
  • Antonym: roadway - the part of a road intended for vehicles
