Elanus leucurus

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈleɪnəs luˈkjʊrəs / (AmE), / ɪˈleɪnəs luˈkjʊərəs / (BrE)
A species of medium-sized, grey-and-white American kite found in warm and tropical regions, commonly known as the white-tailed kite.
a grey-and-white American kite species

Examples

The Elanus leucurus can often be seen hovering above open fields in California.
Scientists tracked the migration patterns of Elanus leucurus across Central America.
With its striking plumage, the Elanus leucurus is easily identified by birdwatchers.

Etymology

From Greek elanos (kite) and Latin leucurus (white-tailed).
