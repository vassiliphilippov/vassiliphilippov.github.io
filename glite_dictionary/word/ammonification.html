ammonification

1. NOUN

/ əˌmɑːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌmɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of impregnating or treating with ammonia or a compound of ammonia
impregnation with ammonia or its compounds

Examples

Industrial fertilizers are often created through ammonification of organic matter.
The soil showed increased nitrogen content after the ammonification treatment.
Biological ammonification is an important step in the nitrogen cycle.

Etymology

From ammonia + -ification, based on the pattern of scientific terms.
