to mud-wrestle

1. VERB

/ ˈmʌdˌrɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌrɛsəl / (BrE)
To wrestle with another person in mud, usually for entertainment or sport.
wrestle in mud for sport or entertainment

Examples

Several competitors will mud-wrestle during the town festival this weekend.
It's not unusual for friends to mud-wrestle after a heavy rain in the countryside.
She never imagined she'd one day mud-wrestle for charity in front of a crowd.

Synset Examples

some people enjoy watching people who mudwrestle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrestle - to struggle hand-to-hand; grapple

Etymology

Formed from 'mud' + 'wrestle'.
