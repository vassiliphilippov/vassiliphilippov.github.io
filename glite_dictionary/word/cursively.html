cursively

1. ADV

/ ˈkɜrsɪvli / (AmE), / ˈkɜːsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by cursive writing; using a flowing, joined style of handwriting or lettering.
in a cursive writing style

Examples

She signed her name cursively at the bottom of the letter.
The instructions were written cursively, making them difficult for some students to read.
He addressed the envelopes cursively, using elegant, flowing letters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cursive - written with the characters joined in a flowing manner

Etymology

From 'cursive' (from Medieval Latin 'cursivus', running) + '-ly' (adverb forming suffix).
