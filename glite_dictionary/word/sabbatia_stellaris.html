Sabbatia stellaris

1. NOUN

/ sæˈbeɪʃiə stəˈlærɪs / (AmE), / sæˈbeɪtiə stɛˈlarɪs / (BrE)
A pink-flowered marsh plant of the eastern United States, resembling a true centaury.
marsh plant with pink flowers (US)

Examples

We spotted a cluster of Sabbatia stellaris growing in the wetland near the river.
The delicate pink petals of Sabbatia stellaris make it a favorite among wildflower enthusiasts.
Botanists study Sabbatia stellaris to understand its adaptation to marshy environments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centaury - a plant of the genus Centaurium, with pink flowers

Etymology

Genus name from Latinized form of Italian botanist Liberato Sabbati's name; 'stellaris' is Latin for 'star-like', referring to the flower's shape.
