uninformatively

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈfɔr.mə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈfɔː.mə.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that does not provide helpful or useful information.
in an uninformative manner

Examples

He shrugged and replied uninformatively to the reporters' questions.
The spokesperson answered uninformatively, leaving everyone more confused than before.
She glanced at the menu uninformatively, offering no recommendations.

Synset Examples

‘I can't tell you when the manager will arrive,’ he said rather uninformatively

Related Terms

  • Antonym: informatively - in an informative manner
  • Synonym: uninformative - not providing useful or helpful information
