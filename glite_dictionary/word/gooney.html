gooney

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːni / (AmE), / ˈɡuːni / (BrE)
A type of large albatross with black feet, found mainly in the North Pacific.
a large black-footed albatross

Examples

The gooney circled above the waves, searching for fish.
Many sailors are superstitious about seeing a gooney during their voyages.
A flock of gooneys nested on the windswept island.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - a large seabird with long wings
