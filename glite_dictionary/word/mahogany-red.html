mahogany-red

1. ADJ

/ məˈhɑɡəni rɛd / (AmE), / məˈhɒɡəni rɛd / (BrE)
having a brown color tinged with red, resembling the shade of mahogany wood
brown with a red tinge, like mahogany

Examples

The antique chair was upholstered in a rich mahogany-red fabric that matched the wood frame.
Her hair glowed a deep mahogany-red in the autumn sunlight.
He chose a mahogany-red paint for the accent wall in the living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - a color intermediate between red and brown

Etymology

From mahogany (a type of reddish-brown wood) + red.
