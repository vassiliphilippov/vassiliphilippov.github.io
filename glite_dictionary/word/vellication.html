vellication

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛlɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛlɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A sudden, involuntary muscle spasm or twitch, often caused by a nervous condition.
sudden muscle spasm or twitch
medical

Examples

After a long day of stress, he felt a vellication in his left eyelid.
The patient complained of frequent vellications in her arms and legs.
A sudden vellication in his calf startled him during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twitch - a sudden, small, involuntary muscle movement

Etymology

From Latin 'vellicatio' (a twitch or nip), from 'vellicare' (to twitch, pluck).
