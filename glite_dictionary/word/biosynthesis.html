biosynthesis

1. NOUN

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈsɪn.θə.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˈsɪn.θɪ.sɪs / (BrE)
the production of complex chemical compounds by living organisms
production of compounds by living organisms
medical

Examples

Many antibiotics are obtained through microbial biosynthesis rather than chemical synthesis.
The biosynthesis of proteins is a crucial process in all living cells.
Scientists study the biosynthesis of natural products to develop new medicines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: synthesis - the production of a substance by combining simpler substances chemically

Etymology

From bio- (life) + synthesis (from Greek 'synthesis', meaning 'putting together').
