fivesome

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪv.səm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪv.səm / (BrE)
A group of five people considered as a unit.
group of five people

Examples

The band performed as a fivesome after recruiting a new guitarist.
We played golf in a fivesome to make it more interesting.
The project team started as a fivesome but soon expanded.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: trio - a group of three people
  • Degree Lower: quartet - a group of four people
  • Degree Higher: sixsome - a group of six people

Etymology

Formed from 'five' plus '-some', denoting a group of a specified number.
