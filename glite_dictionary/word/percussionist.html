percussionist

1. NOUN

/ pərˈkʌʃənɪst / (AmE), / pəˈkʌʃənɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays percussion instruments, such as drums, cymbals, or xylophone
a person who plays percussion instruments

Examples

The percussionist added depth to the music with a variety of rhythmic patterns.
During the concert, the percussionist played both the marimba and bongos.
As a skilled percussionist, she was invited to join several jazz ensembles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drummer - a person who plays the drums

Etymology

From 'percussion' (striking of one body against another to produce sound) + '-ist' (person who practices or is concerned with something).
