annular

1. ADJ

/ ˈæn.jələr / (AmE), / ˈæn.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
shaped like a ring or forming a ring
ring-shaped

Examples

The doctor noted an annular rash on the patient's arm.
During an annular eclipse, the moon covers the center of the sun, leaving a ring of light.
The coral formed an annular structure around the lagoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular - having the form of a circle
  • Synonym: ring-shaped - shaped like a ring

Etymology

From Latin 'annulus' (ring).
