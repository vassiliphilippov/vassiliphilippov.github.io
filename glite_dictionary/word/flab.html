flab

1. NOUN

/ flæb / (AmE), / flæb / (BrE)
Loose, soft body fat, especially as a result of lack of exercise; excess body fat.
loose or soft body fat
informal

Examples

After months of not exercising, he noticed more flab around his waist.
She joined a gym to get rid of the flab on her arms.
Trying on last summer's clothes, I realized I had more flab than before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fat - the soft substance in the bodies of animals and humans that stores energy
