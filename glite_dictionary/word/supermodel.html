supermodel

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚˌmɑː.dəl / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌmɒd.əl / (BrE)
a highly successful fashion model who has become widely recognized as a celebrity
celebrity fashion model

Examples

She became an international supermodel in the 1990s, appearing on magazine covers around the world.
The fashion show featured a famous supermodel as its star attraction.
Despite her supermodel status, she remains humble and approachable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person employed to display clothes or other merchandise

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (meaning 'above, superior') + 'model'. Popularized in the 1980s and 1990s.
