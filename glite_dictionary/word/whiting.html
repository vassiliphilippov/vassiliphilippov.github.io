whiting

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
A small silvery fish found off the Atlantic coast of North America, valued as food.
a small edible Atlantic fish

Examples

Fishermen caught several whiting off the coast of Nova Scotia.
I ordered grilled whiting with lemon at the seafood restaurant.
The market was selling fresh whiting alongside cod and mackerel.
