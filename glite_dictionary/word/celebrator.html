celebrator

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛləˌbreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪˌbreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is taking part in a celebration
person participating in a celebration

Examples

The city square was filled with celebrators dancing and singing late into the night.
Each celebrator wore a brightly colored costume for the festival.
As midnight approached, the celebrator raised his glass in a toast to the new year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrant - a person who is celebrating or performing a ceremony

Etymology

From Latin 'celebrator', from 'celebrare' (to celebrate).
