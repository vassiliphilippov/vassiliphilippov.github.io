undrinkable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdrɪŋkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɪŋkəbl / (BrE)
not suitable for drinking, especially because it is unpleasant or unsafe
not suitable for drinking

Examples

The tap water was so contaminated it was completely undrinkable.
After sitting out for days, the juice had become undrinkable.
They found the river water to be undrinkable due to pollution.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: drinkable - safe and pleasant to drink

Etymology

From un- (not) + drinkable.
