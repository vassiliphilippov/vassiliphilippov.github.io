Mary Douglas Leakey

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɛɹi ˈdʌɡləs ˈliːki / (AmE), / ˈmɛːɹi ˈdʌɡləs ˈliːki / (BrE)
(1913–1996) English paleontologist renowned for discovering the Zinjanthropus skull and major contributions to the study of early human evolution.
English paleontologist; discovered Zinjanthropus skull

Examples

Mary Douglas Leakey uncovered several important fossils at Olduvai Gorge in Tanzania.
The discovery by Mary Douglas Leakey changed our understanding of early hominids.
Researchers still build on the work initiated by Mary Douglas Leakey in the mid-20th century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Louis Leakey - Kenyan paleoanthropologist and husband of Mary Leakey
