HAZMAT

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæzˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈhæzˌmæt / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'hazardous material', used especially on warning signs to indicate the presence of dangerous substances
abbreviation for hazardous material
american

Examples

The truck was carrying HAZMAT and required special handling procedures.
Drivers must have a special license to transport HAZMAT across state lines.
Emergency crews responded quickly to contain the HAZMAT spill on the highway.

Synset Examples

No HAZMAT in tunnel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hazardous material - dangerous substances that pose a risk to health, property, or the environment

Etymology

Formed from the initial syllables of 'hazardous' and 'material'.
