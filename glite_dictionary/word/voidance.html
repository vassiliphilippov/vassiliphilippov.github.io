voidance

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪdəns / (BrE)
the act of emptying or removing the contents from something, especially a container or bodily organ
the act of emptying or clearing out contents

Examples

The voidance of the tank was necessary before maintenance work could begin.
Proper voidance of the bladder is important before the procedure.
A sudden voidance of contents from the container caused a spill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evacuation - the act of emptying something out, especially the bowels
  • Antonym: filling - the action of putting something into a container

Etymology

From Old French voidance (emptying, clearing), from 'vider' (to empty).
