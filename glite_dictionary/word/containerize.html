to containerize

1. VERB

/ kənˈteɪnəˌraɪz / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnəraɪz / (BrE)
to package (goods or cargo) in a container, especially for transport
package goods in a container

Examples

The company decided to containerize all exports to reduce shipping losses.
After the goods were containerized, transporting them overseas became much simpler.
Many shipping firms now containerize products as a standard practice.

Synset Examples

The cargo was containerized for safe and efficient shipping

Related Terms

  • Synonym: package - to wrap or box goods for transport or sale

Etymology

From 'container' + '-ize', as in American English word formation.
