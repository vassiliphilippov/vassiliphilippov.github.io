Lathyrus splendens

1. NOUN

/ ləˈθaɪrəs ˈsplɛndənz / (AmE), / læˈθaɪərəs ˈsplɛndənz / (BrE)
a shrubby perennial plant native to California, with large pink or violet flowers, often grown as an ornamental
a Californian shrubby flowering perennial

Examples

The garden's centerpiece is a stunning Lathyrus splendens covered in vivid violet blooms.
Many gardeners choose Lathyrus splendens for its showy flowers and easy care.
In California, Lathyrus splendens thrives in well-drained, sunny spots.

Etymology

From Latin; genus name *Lathyrus* and species epithet *splendens* meaning 'shining, splendid'.
