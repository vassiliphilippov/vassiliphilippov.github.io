Kedah

1. PROPN

/ kəˈdɑː / (AmE), / kɪˈdɑː / (BrE)
A state and former sultanate in northwestern Peninsular Malaysia, bordering Thailand and the Andaman Sea.
state in northwestern Malaysia

Examples

The state of Kedah is known for its scenic rice fields and historical sites.
During our trip to Malaysia, we visited beautiful beaches in Kedah.
Rice farming is an important part of the economy in Kedah.
