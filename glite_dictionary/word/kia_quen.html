kia quen

1. NOUN

/ kiːˈɑː kwɛn / (AmE), / kiːˈɑː kwɛn / (BrE)
a method of foot torture practiced in China, in which a person's foot was clamped between bamboo slats and crushed
Chinese method of foot torture

Examples

Historical records describe kia quen as a particularly cruel punishment in ancient China.
During his imprisonment, he suffered the painful ordeal of kia quen.
The museum displayed an exhibit explaining how kia quen was carried out on prisoners.
