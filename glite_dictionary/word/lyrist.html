lyrist

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪrɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes the words (lyrics) for songs; a lyricist
person who writes song lyrics

Examples

She is an accomplished lyrist whose words have touched millions around the world.
The band hired a new lyrist to give fresh meaning to their melodies.
As a lyrist, he believes every song should tell a unique story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lyricist - a person who writes the words to songs

Etymology

From Greek lyra (lyre), referring originally to a player of the lyre; extended to mean writer of lyrics.
