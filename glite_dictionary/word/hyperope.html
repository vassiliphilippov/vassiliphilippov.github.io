hyperope

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪpəˌroʊp / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪpərəʊp / (BrE)
a person who has hyperopia (farsightedness)
person with farsightedness
medical

Examples

The hyperope struggled to read the fine print without his glasses.
Unlike a myope, a hyperope sees distant objects more clearly.
Many elderly people become hyperopes as their eyes age.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: myope - a person with myopia (nearsightedness)

Etymology

From hyperopia (medical term for farsightedness) plus Greek -ops (eye).
