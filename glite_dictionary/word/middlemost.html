middlemost

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪdəlˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪdəlməʊst / (BrE)
Located exactly at the center or in the exact middle of something.
exactly in the middle

Examples

The garden path leads directly to the middlemost point of the courtyard.
She placed the vase in the middlemost position on the table for symmetry.
Of all the rooms in the house, his was the middlemost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: central - at or near the center; forming the center

Etymology

Formed from 'middle' + '-most', an adjective-forming suffix meaning 'furthest' or 'at the extreme'.
