sixty-seven

1. NUM

/ ˌsɪkstiˈsɛvən / (AmE), / ˌsɪkstiˈsɛvən / (BrE)
the number that is seven more than sixty; 67
the number 67

Examples

There are sixty-seven students enrolled in the course this semester.
The car was built in 1967, which means it celebrated its sixty-seventh birthday last year.
I counted exactly sixty-seven dollars in my wallet before going shopping.
