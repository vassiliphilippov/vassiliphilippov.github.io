sweet-faced

1. ADJ

/ swit feɪst / (AmE), / swiːt feɪst / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having a pleasant or gentle appearance that suggests a kind or amiable nature.
having a pleasantly gentle or amiable face

Examples

Everyone in the village adored the sweet-faced girl who always had a smile for everyone.
He adopted a sweet-faced puppy from the shelter last week.
Behind her sweet-faced innocence lay a determination few suspected.

Synset Examples

a sweet-faced child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angelic - resembling an angel in appearance or manner
  • Synonym: kind-faced - having a face showing kindness
