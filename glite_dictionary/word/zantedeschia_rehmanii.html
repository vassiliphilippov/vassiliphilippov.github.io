Zantedeschia rehmanii

1. NOUN

/ zæn.təˈdɛs.ki.ə reɪˈmɑː.ni.aɪ / (AmE), / zan.təˈdɛs.ki.ə rɛˈmɑː.ni.aɪ / (BrE)
A species of calla lily with a rose-colored spathe, cultivated as an ornamental plant.
a rose-colored species of calla lily

Examples

The Zantedeschia rehmanii brightened the garden with its unique rose-colored blooms.
Many florists use Zantedeschia rehmanii in bridal arrangements for its soft, elegant appearance.
Unlike the traditional white calla, the Zantedeschia rehmanii is prized for its vibrant pink color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calla lily - a common name for plants in the Zantedeschia genus

Etymology

Genus name from Italian botanist Giovanni Zantedeschi; species name honors botanist Anton Rehmann.
