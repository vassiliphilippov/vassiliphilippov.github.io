Coloradan

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌləˈræd(ə)n / (AmE), / ˌkɒləˈræd(ə)n / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the state of Colorado, USA
person from Colorado, USA

Examples

Many Coloradans enjoy skiing in the Rocky Mountains during the winter months.
As a proud Coloradan, she often shares stories of her home state with her friends.
The Coloradan delegation presented new environmental policies at the national summit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Coloradoan - another demonym for a person from Colorado

Etymology

From Colorado (US state) + -an (forming demonyms).
