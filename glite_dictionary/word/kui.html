Kui

1. NOUN

/ kuːˈi / (AmE), / kuːˈi / (BrE)
A member of the Dravidian people living mainly in southeastern India, particularly in Odisha and Andhra Pradesh.
a Dravidian ethnic group in India

Examples

The Kui have preserved unique customs distinct from neighboring communities.
Anthropologists have studied the traditional villages of the Kui in eastern India.
Many Kui people still speak their indigenous language at home.

2. NOUN

/ kuːˈiː / (AmE), / kuːˈiː / (BrE)
A Dravidian language spoken by the Kui people in southeastern India.
a Dravidian language of southeastern India

Examples

Many elders in the region speak Kui as their first language.
Linguists have documented several dialects of Kui.
Efforts are underway to preserve Kui through education and literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kuvi - a related Dravidian language spoken in India
