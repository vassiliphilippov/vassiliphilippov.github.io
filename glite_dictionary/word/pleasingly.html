pleasingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpliːzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpliːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that gives pleasure or satisfaction; in a pleasing manner.
in a pleasing or satisfying way

Examples

The restaurant was pleasingly decorated with fresh flowers and soft lighting.
She found his voice pleasingly calm during the stressful situation.
The report was pleasingly brief and to the point, making it easy to understand.

Synset Examples

the room was pleasingly large

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'pleasing' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
