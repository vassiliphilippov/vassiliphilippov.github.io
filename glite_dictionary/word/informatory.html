informatory

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfɔrməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːmətəri / (BrE)
providing or conveying information; serving to inform
providing information

Examples

The museum provided several informatory signs describing the history of the building.
He distributed informatory pamphlets to raise awareness about the issue.
The manual was not only detailed but also highly informatory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: informative - giving useful information

Etymology

From Late Latin informatorius (serving to inform), from informare (to inform).
