Toronto

1. PROPN

/ təˈrɑːn(toʊ) / (AmE), / təˈrɒntəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of the province of Ontario, and the largest city in Canada, located on the northwestern shore of Lake Ontario.
largest city in Canada; Ontario's capital

Examples

Toronto is known for its multicultural neighborhoods and bustling downtown core.
She moved from Vancouver to Toronto to start her new job.
The skyline of Toronto is dominated by the iconic CN Tower.

Etymology

Named after the Mohawk word 'tkaronto' meaning 'place where trees stand in the water.'
