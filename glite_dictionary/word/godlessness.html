godlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdləsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of lacking belief in God or being ungodly; irreligion or impiety
lack of belief in God; irreligion

Examples

The author was criticized for the perceived godlessness of his work.
Many feared that the decline in church attendance signaled a rise in godlessness across the nation.
Her open godlessness often sparked heated debates among her devout friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impiety - lack of piety or reverence, especially for a god
  • Synonym: atheism - disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of God or gods
  • Antonym: piety - the quality of being religious or reverent

Etymology

From 'godless' + '-ness', where 'godless' derives from 'God' + '-less'.
