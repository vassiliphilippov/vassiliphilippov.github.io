trade-last

1. NOUN

/ treɪd læst / (AmE), / treɪd lɑːst / (BrE)
a compliment about someone that is offered to exchange for a compliment they have heard about you
an exchanged compliment

Examples

Let me tell you a trade-last I heard; if you have one for me, I'd love to hear it.
We used to swap trade-lasts at school, making everyone feel good about themselves.
She smiled as she shared a trade-last with her friend, both enjoying the positive exchange.
