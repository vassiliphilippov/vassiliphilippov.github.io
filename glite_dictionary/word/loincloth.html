loincloth

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔɪnˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪnˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A simple piece of cloth worn around the hips to cover the loins, especially as typical dress in some traditional cultures.
cloth covering for the loins

Examples

The tribal chief wore a loincloth decorated with colorful beads.
In ancient times, a loincloth was often the only clothing people needed in warm climates.
He fashioned a makeshift loincloth from a torn shirt after his clothes were lost.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breechcloth - a similar garment worn around the hips and between the thighs

Etymology

From 'loin' + 'cloth', in use since at least the 16th century.
