affinal

1. ADJ

/ əˈfaɪnəl / (AmE), / əˈfaɪnəl / (BrE)
(Anthropology) related by or through marriage rather than by blood.
related by marriage (anthropology)
medical

Examples

In many societies, affinal relatives play a crucial role in family alliances.
The researcher distinguished between consanguineous and affinal relationships.
Inheritance rights sometimes extend to affinal kin in certain cultures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consanguineous - related by blood

Etymology

From Latin 'affinis' meaning 'related, connected'.
