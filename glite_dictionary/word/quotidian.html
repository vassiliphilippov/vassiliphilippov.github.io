quotidian

1. ADJ

/ kwoʊˈtɪdiən / (AmE), / kwəʊˈtɪdiən / (BrE)
ordinary or everyday, especially when mundane or common; found in the ordinary course of events
ordinary; everyday; commonplace

Examples

Her stories focus on the quotidian challenges of family life.
Even the most quotidian moments can be beautiful if you notice them.
He was bored by the quotidian routines of office work.

Synset Examples

a placid everyday scene
it was a routine day
there's nothing quite like a real … train conductor to add color to a quotidian commute

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyday - ordinary or normal; used or seen regularly
  • Synonym: routine - a regular or habitual course of procedure
  • Antonym: extraordinary - very unusual or remarkable; not ordinary

Etymology

From Latin 'quotidianus' (daily), from 'quotidie' (every day), from 'quot' (how many) and 'dies' (day).
