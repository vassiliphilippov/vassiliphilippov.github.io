Nevis

1. PROPN

/ ˈniːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈniːvɪs / (BrE)
an island in the West Indies that forms part of the nation of Saint Kitts and Nevis; its capital is Charlestown.
island in the West Indies, part of Saint Kitts and Nevis

Examples

The tropical climate of Nevis attracts many tourists each year.
I visited Nevis last summer and enjoyed its beautiful beaches.
Charlestown, the capital of Nevis, is known for its colonial-era architecture.
