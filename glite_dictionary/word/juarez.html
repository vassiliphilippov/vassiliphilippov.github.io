Juarez

1. PROPN

/ ˈhwɑːrɛz / (AmE), / ˈwɑːrɛz / (BrE)
A city in northern Mexico on the Rio Grande, opposite El Paso, Texas.
city in northern Mexico

Examples

Many people cross the border between El Paso and Juarez every day for work and shopping.
Juarez has grown rapidly over the last decade, attracting both industry and tourism.
The vibrant markets of Juarez are popular with visitors from the United States.

Etymology

Named after Benito Juárez, a former president of Mexico.
