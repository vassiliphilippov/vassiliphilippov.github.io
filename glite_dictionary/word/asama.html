Asama

1. PROPN

/ əˈsɑːmə / (AmE), / əˈsɑːmə / (BrE)
a volcano in central Honshu, Japan, located near Nagano and known as one of the country's largest volcanoes, rising to 8,340 feet (2,548 meters)
a large volcano in central Honshu, Japan

Examples

Mount Asama erupted in 2004, sending ash plumes high into the sky above Nagano Prefecture.
Tourists often hike up the slopes of Asama for spectacular views of the surrounding landscape.
Asama is considered one of Japan's most active volcanoes and is closely monitored by scientists.
