debater

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈbeɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a formal or informal debate
person who participates in debate

Examples

She is an experienced debater who has won many competitions.
The school selected its best debater to represent them at the national finals.
As a skilled debater, he was able to argue both sides of the issue effectively.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orator - a skilled public speaker

Etymology

From 'debate' (Middle English, from Old French 'debatre'), plus agent noun suffix '-er'.
