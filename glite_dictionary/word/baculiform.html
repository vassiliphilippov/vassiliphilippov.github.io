baculiform

1. ADJ

/ ˈbækjəlɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈbakjʊlɪfɔːm / (BrE)
shaped like a rod or bacillus; rod-shaped
rod-shaped; shaped like a bacillus
medical

Examples

The bacteria display a distinctive baculiform structure under the microscope.
Some crystals can develop a baculiform appearance after certain treatments.
The baculiform cells were aligned in rows throughout the tissue sample.

Etymology

From Latin 'baculum' (rod, staff) + '-form' (in the shape of).
