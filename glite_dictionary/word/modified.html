modified

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːdɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
changed in form, character, or function; altered from an original state, often to improve or adapt it
changed; altered; adapted

Examples

The modified car engine allows it to run more efficiently than before.
She wore a modified version of the traditional dress to suit the modern event.
After the rules were modified, more players could join the game.

Synset Examples

their modified stand made the issue more acceptable
the performance of the modified aircraft was much improved

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altered - changed in a way
  • Antonym: unmodified - not changed; original
