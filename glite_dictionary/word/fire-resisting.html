fire-resisting

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪɚ rɪˈzɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə rɪˈzɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a material or structure) not easily damaged or destroyed by fire; difficult to burn.
difficult to burn

Examples

The building was constructed with fire-resisting materials for increased safety.
We need to choose fire-resisting doors for the laboratory.
Modern regulations require fire-resisting wall panels in stairwells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fireproof - (of a material) not able to catch fire easily; highly resistant to fire
  • Antonym: flammable - easily set on fire; able to burn quickly
