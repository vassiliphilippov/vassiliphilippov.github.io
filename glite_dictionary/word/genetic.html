genetic

1. ADJ

/ dʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / dʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by heredity, especially the transmission of characteristics from parents to offspring through genes.
relating to heredity or genes
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the disease was genetic and could be passed on to future generations.
Some eye colors are determined by genetic factors inherited from both parents.
They studied the genetic similarities between twins separated at birth.

Synset Examples

an inherited disease
familial traits
genetically transmitted features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereditary - (of a characteristic or condition) passed from parent to child by genes

Etymology

From Greek *genetikos* (generative), from *genesis* (origin, birth).

Synset POS: s
