lithographer

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈθɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / lɪˈθɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who makes prints using the process of lithography
person making lithographic prints

Examples

The lithographer carefully prepared the stone for the next print.
As a skilled lithographer, she taught workshops on traditional printmaking techniques.
Many famous artists have worked with a lithographer to produce limited-edition prints.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printmaker - someone who makes prints as an art form or a profession

Etymology

From Greek 'lithos' (stone) + '-grapher' (one who writes).
