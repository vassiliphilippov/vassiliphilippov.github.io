regularly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛɡjələrli / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊləli / (BrE)
in a consistent or uniform manner, often occurring at fixed intervals or without variation
in a consistent or uniform way

Examples

It is important to exercise regularly to maintain good health.
She checks her email regularly throughout the day to stay updated.
The trains run regularly every fifteen minutes from this station.

Synset Examples

try to breathe regularly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irregularly - in an inconsistent or uneven manner

Etymology

From regular + -ly; regular from Latin 'regula' (rule).

2. ADV

/ ˈrɛɡjələrli / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊləli / (BrE)
In a regular manner; at uniform intervals or according to a constant pattern.
in a regular or consistent way

Examples

I try to go jogging regularly to maintain my health.
The teacher reminds us to check our homework regularly for mistakes.
New issues of the magazine are published regularly throughout the year.

Synset Examples

letters arrived regularly from his children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frequently - occurring often or at short intervals
  • Antonym: irregularly - not at regular intervals or patterns

Etymology

Formed from 'regular' + '-ly', where 'regular' is from Latin 'regularis' (rule).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00333223-r
Synset POS: r
