connector

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛktɚ / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
A device or part used to join two or more things together, especially in electronics or machinery.
device that joins things together

Examples

He searched the toolbox for a connector that would fit the two cables.
Make sure the connector is securely fastened to prevent any signal loss.
The technician replaced the broken connector on the back of the computer.

Synset Examples

he soldered the connection
he didn't have the right connector between the amplifier and the speakers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - a link or joining between things
  • Synonym: coupler - a device for joining two things, especially in engineering

2. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛktɚ / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
a device or component used to join two or more things together, especially in electronics or engineering
device or part that joins things together

Examples

Please make sure the connector is securely attached before powering on the device.
This model uses a new type of connector for its charging cable.
He couldn't find the right connector to link the two circuits.

Synset Examples

he soldered the connection
he didn't have the right connector between the amplifier and the speakers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coupler - device that connects two things, often in mechanics or electronics
  • Antonym: disconnect - to separate or detach things that are joined

Etymology

From 'connect' + '-or,' meaning one that connects.
