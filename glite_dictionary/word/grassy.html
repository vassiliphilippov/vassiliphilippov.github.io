grassy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræsi / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsi / (BrE)
(of an area or surface) covered with or having a lot of grass.
covered with grass

Examples

The picnic was held on a grassy hillside overlooking the lake.
After the rain, the grassy field smelled fresh and clean.
Children ran barefoot across the grassy lawn in the summer sunshine.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gravelly - covered with gravel
  • Antonym: bare - lacking vegetation or covering

Etymology

From 'grass' + '-y' (an adjective-forming suffix).
