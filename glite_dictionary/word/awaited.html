awaited

1. ADJ

/ əˈweɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈweɪtɪd / (BrE)
expected or looked forward to, especially with hope or excitement.
expected hopefully

Examples

The team's long awaited victory finally came after years of hard work.
Her awaited response brought relief to everyone worried about her.
The movie's awaited release attracted fans from all over the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anticipated - regarded as probable; expected or predicted
  • Antonym: unexpected - not expected or regarded as likely to happen

Etymology

From 'await' (Middle English awaiten), from Old French 'awaitier', from a- (to) + waitier (to watch).
