posh

1. ADJ

/ pɑʃ / (AmE), / pɒʃ / (BrE)
Elegant, fashionable, or luxurious; typical of or suitable for the upper class.
elegant and fashionable

Examples

They celebrated her birthday at a posh restaurant in the city center.
She wore a posh dress to the gala and turned many heads.
The hotel lobby looked incredibly posh, with crystal chandeliers and marble floors.

Synset Examples

classy clothes
a classy dame
a posh restaurant
a swish pastry shop on the Rue du Bac

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classy - stylish and sophisticated
  • Synonym: swanky - stylish and expensive
  • Antonym: downmarket - low in quality or price, not intended for the wealthy

Etymology

Possibly from British slang, origin uncertain; sometimes said to be an acronym for 'port out, starboard home,' but this is widely discredited.
