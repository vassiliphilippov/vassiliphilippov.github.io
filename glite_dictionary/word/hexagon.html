hexagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛk.sə.ɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈhɛk.sə.ɡən / (BrE)
A flat shape with six straight sides and six angles.
six-sided polygon

Examples

The soccer ball's pattern includes several hexagon shapes.
You can draw a regular hexagon using only a compass and straightedge.
The honeycomb cells are perfectly formed hexagons, maximizing storage space.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polygon - a flat shape with many straight sides
  • Degree Lower: pentagon - a five-sided polygon
  • Degree Higher: heptagon - a seven-sided polygon

Etymology

From Greek hexagōnos, from hex (six) + gōnia (angle).
