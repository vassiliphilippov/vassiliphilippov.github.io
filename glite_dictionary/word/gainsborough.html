Gainsborough

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡeɪnzbərə / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪnzbərə / (BrE)
(1727–1788) An English painter renowned for his portraits and landscapes, considered one of the most important British artists of the 18th century.
English portrait and landscape painter

Examples

Gainsborough is famous for his masterpiece 'The Blue Boy' and his stunning landscape paintings.
The art museum is hosting an exhibition dedicated to the works of Gainsborough.
Many consider Gainsborough's portraits to be defining examples of English portraiture.
