saw-toothed

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔˌtuðd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌtuːθt / (BrE)
having an edge or outline with sharp, regular notches or projections like those of a saw.
having a jagged edge like a saw

Examples

The leaves of the plant have a distinctive saw-toothed margin.
Along the trail, we found a saw-toothed rock formation jutting out from the hillside.
He admired the saw-toothed pattern along the edge of the old metal blade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serrated - having a jagged or saw-like edge
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even, unnotched surface or edge
