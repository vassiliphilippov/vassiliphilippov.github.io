liveborn

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪvˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvbɔːn / (BrE)
(of a baby or animal) born alive, showing signs of life after birth, as opposed to stillborn.
born alive; not stillborn
medical

Examples

The hospital keeps special records for every liveborn infant.
It is important to distinguish between liveborn and stillborn deliveries in medical reports.
All liveborn puppies appeared healthy and active immediately after birth.

Synset Examples

a liveborn baby

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stillborn - born dead; showing no signs of life at birth
