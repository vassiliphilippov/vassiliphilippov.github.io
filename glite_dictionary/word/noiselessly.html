noiselessly

1. ADV

/ ˈnɔɪz.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪz.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that produces no sound; silently.
without making any sound

Examples

The cat moved noiselessly through the darkened room, unnoticed by anyone.
He opened the window noiselessly to avoid waking his parents.
The thief crept noiselessly along the hallway so the dog would not bark.

Synset Examples

he stood up soundlessly and speechlessly and glided across the hallway and through a door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silently - without making any sound
