unmeasurable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɛʒ.ər.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛʒ.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to measure or quantify due to lack of defined limits or extreme magnitude
impossible to measure

Examples

The unmeasurable distances between galaxies fascinate astronomers.
Her grief was so deep it felt unmeasurable at times.
There are unmeasurable benefits to spending time with loved ones.

Synset Examples

unmeasurable reaches of outer space
immeasurable suffering

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immeasurable - too large, extensive, or extreme to be measured

Etymology

From un- (not) + measurable, based on Latin 'mensurabilis' (measurable).
