millionfold

1. ADV

/ ˈmɪljənˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈmɪljənfəʊld / (BrE)
by a factor of a million; multiplied by one million times.
by a factor of a million

Examples

Thanks to technological advances, processing power has increased millionfold in the last few decades.
The bacteria multiplied millionfold in just twenty-four hours.
His investment grew millionfold after the company went public.

Synset Examples

it increased a millionfold

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thousandfold - by a factor of a thousand
  • Degree Higher: billionfold - by a factor of a billion

Etymology

Formed from 'million' + 'fold', following the pattern of multiples such as 'twofold'.
