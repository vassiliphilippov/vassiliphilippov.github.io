polyhidrosis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑːlihaɪˈdroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪhaɪˈdrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a condition characterized by excessive and profuse sweating
excessive sweating condition
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with polyhidrosis after experiencing severe sweating even at rest.
Treatment options for polyhidrosis include antiperspirants and sometimes medication or surgery.
People with polyhidrosis often find it affects their daily activities and self-confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyperhidrosis - excessive sweating due to overactive sweat glands

Etymology

From Greek 'poly-' (many) + 'hidros' (sweat).
