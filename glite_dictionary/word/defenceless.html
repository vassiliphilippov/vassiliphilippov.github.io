defenceless

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈfɛnsləs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsləs / (BrE)
Without protection or support; unable to defend oneself from harm or attack.
without protection; unable to defend oneself

Examples

Many wild animals are defenceless against habitat destruction caused by humans.
She felt defenceless when confronted by the sudden storm without shelter.
The kitten looked so defenceless that everyone wanted to help it.

Synset Examples

a defenseless child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulnerable - susceptible to physical or emotional harm
  • Antonym: protected - shielded from harm or attack

Etymology

From defence + -less, meaning 'without defence'.

2. ADJ

/ dɪˈfɛns.ləs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns.ləs / (BrE)
Not able to protect oneself or lacking the means or weapons for self-defense.
unable to protect oneself

Examples

The cubs were defenceless against the larger predator.
Without the alarm system, the house was left completely defenceless.
Many protesters felt defenceless in the face of armed police.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vulnerable - able to be easily harmed or attacked
  • Antonym: protected - shielded from harm or attack

Etymology

From defence + -less, meaning 'without defence'.
