relentlessly

1. ADV

/ rəˈlɛntlɪsli / (AmE), / rɪˈlɛntlɪsli / (BrE)
In a way that does not stop or become less severe, determined, or intense.
in an unceasing, determined way

Examples

She pursued her goals relentlessly, never allowing setbacks to slow her progress.
The storm pounded the coastline relentlessly for hours, causing widespread damage.
Critics relentlessly questioned the company's decision until they provided an explanation.

Synset Examples

he worked relentlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relentless - never stopping or becoming less severe; persistent

Etymology

From 'relentless' + '-ly'. 'Relentless' is from 'relent' with the negative suffix '-less'.
