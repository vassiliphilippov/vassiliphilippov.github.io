virginity

1. NOUN

/ vɚˈdʒɪnəti / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪnɪti / (BrE)
the state of never having had sexual intercourse
state of never having had sex

Examples

She wore white at her wedding to symbolize her virginity.
The novel explores the social expectations surrounding a girl's virginity.
He felt no shame in discussing virginity openly with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chastity - the practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual intercourse
  • Antonym: experience - sexual experience; having had sexual intercourse

Etymology

From Latin 'virgo' (maiden, virgin), via Old French 'virginite'.
