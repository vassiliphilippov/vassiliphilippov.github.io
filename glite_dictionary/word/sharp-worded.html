sharp-worded

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp ˈwɝːdɪd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp ˈwɜːdɪd / (BrE)
(of speech or writing) harsh or critical in tone; using cutting or severe language.
harsh or cutting in tone

Examples

Her sharp-worded remarks left the committee feeling uncomfortable.
The editor wrote a sharp-worded response to the misleading article.
They had a sharp-worded exchange during the heated debate.

Synset Examples

sharp criticism
a sharp-worded exchange
a tart remark

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tart - (of speech or writing) sharp or cutting in tone
  • Synonym: biting - (of speech) sharply sarcastic or critical
  • Antonym: gentle - kind or mild in tone
