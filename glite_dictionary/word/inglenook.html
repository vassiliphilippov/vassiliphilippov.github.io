inglenook

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlˌnʊk / (AmE), / ˈɪŋlɪnʊk / (BrE)
a space or nook by a fireplace, often forming a cozy seating area
a cozy area beside a fireplace

Examples

We sat in the inglenook and warmed ourselves by the fire.
The cottage features an original stone inglenook dating back to the 18th century.
She placed a basket of logs in the inglenook to keep the fire going.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chimney corner - a recess or small area beside a fireplace

Etymology

From Scots 'ingle' (fire, fireplace) + 'nook' (corner, recess).
