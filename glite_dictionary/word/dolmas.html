dolmas

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊlməz / (AmE), / ˈdɒlməz / (BrE)
A dish consisting of grape leaves or other vegetables stuffed with a mixture of seasoned rice, sometimes including nuts, currants, or minced lamb, and cooked by simmering or braising.
stuffed grape leaves or vegetables (Mediterranean dish)

Examples

The restaurant's homemade dolmas were stuffed with herbed rice and pine nuts.
She served a platter of dolmas at the Mediterranean-themed dinner party.
I learned how to roll grape leaves to make traditional dolmas from my grandmother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffed grape leaves - grape leaves filled with rice and seasonings, a Mediterranean dish

Etymology

From Turkish *dolma* (something stuffed), from *dolmak* (to be filled).
