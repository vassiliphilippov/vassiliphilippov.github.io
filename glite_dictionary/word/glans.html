glans

1. NOUN

/ ɡlænz / (AmE), / ɡlanz / (BrE)
The rounded, sensitive part at the end of the penis or clitoris.
rounded, sensitive end part of penis or clitoris
medical

Examples

Doctors check the glans during routine physical examinations for any signs of abnormality.
The glans is highly sensitive due to its concentration of nerve endings.
In some mammals, the shape of the glans can vary significantly between species.

Etymology

From Latin 'glans', meaning 'acorn', referring to its shape.
