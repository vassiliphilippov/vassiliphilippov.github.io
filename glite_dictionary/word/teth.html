teth

1. NOUN

/ teɪt, teθ / (AmE), / teɪt, teθ / (BrE)
the ninth letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
ninth Hebrew letter

Examples

The Hebrew word for 'good' begins with the letter teth.
In his studies of the Torah, he learned the significance of teth.
When reciting the Hebrew alphabet, she paused after teth to check her pronunciation.

Etymology

From Hebrew טֵית (tēt).
