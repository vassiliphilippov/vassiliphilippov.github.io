interpersonal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈpɜrsənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈpɜːsənl̩ / (BrE)
Relating to or involving relationships or communication between people.
occurring between people

Examples

The training focuses on improving interpersonal skills in the workplace.
Effective leaders must have strong interpersonal communication abilities.
Conflicts often arise because of poor interpersonal understanding.

Synset Examples

interpersonal situations in which speech occurs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intrapersonal - existing or occurring within an individual's mind or self

Etymology

From inter- (between) + personal.
