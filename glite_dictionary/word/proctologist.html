proctologist

1. NOUN

/ prɑkˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / prɒkˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the rectum and anus
specialist doctor for rectum and anus
medical

Examples

After years of discomfort, she finally visited a proctologist for a proper diagnosis.
A proctologist can help treat conditions like hemorrhoids and anal fissures.
When his symptoms persisted, his family doctor referred him to a proctologist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastroenterologist - a doctor who specializes in diseases of the stomach and intestines
  • Synonym: proctology - the branch of medicine dealing with diseases of the rectum and anus

Etymology

From Greek proktos (anus) + -logist (one who studies).
