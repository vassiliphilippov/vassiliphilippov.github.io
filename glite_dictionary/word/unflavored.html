unflavored

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfleɪvərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
without any flavoring added; having no additional taste or seasoning.
without added flavoring

Examples

I bought a pack of unflavored gelatin for the recipe.
Some people prefer unflavored yogurt so they can add their own fruit.
The medication comes in both unflavored and fruit-flavored varieties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flavored - having flavoring added
