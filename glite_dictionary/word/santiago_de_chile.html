Santiago de Chile

1. PROPN

/ sɑnˌtjɑɡoʊ də ˈtʃiːleɪ / (AmE), / sanˌtjɑːɡəʊ də ˈtʃiːleɪ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Chile, located in central Chile
capital city of Chile

Examples

Many tourists visit Santiago de Chile for its vibrant culture and stunning mountain views.
The conference will be held in Santiago de Chile next month.
Her family moved from Buenos Aires to Santiago de Chile when she was a child.

Etymology

Spanish, meaning 'Saint James of Chile'.
