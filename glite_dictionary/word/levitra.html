Levitra

1. NOUN

/ ləˈvitɹə / (AmE), / lɛˈviːtɹə / (BrE)
A proprietary name for a drug used to treat erectile dysfunction in men.
brand-name drug for erectile dysfunction
medical

Examples

His doctor prescribed Levitra to help him manage his erectile dysfunction.
Many men have found Levitra effective when other medications did not work.
Before taking Levitra, you should consult with a healthcare professional.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vardenafil - the generic name for the drug marketed as Levitra
