groschen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒʃən / (BrE)
a former monetary unit of Austria, equal to one hundredth of a schilling
Austrian money unit, one hundredth of a schilling

Examples

I have an old Austrian coin collection that includes several groschen from the 1950s.
Before the euro, Austrians would often pay for small items with groschen.
He found a forgotten groschen at the bottom of the desk drawer.

Etymology

From German 'Groschen', originally from Middle High German, from Italian 'grosso' (large coin).
