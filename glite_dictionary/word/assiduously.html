assiduously

1. ADV

/ əˈsɪdʒuəsli / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjuːəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great care, attention, and persistence in carrying out a task.
with care and persistence

Examples

She completed her research assiduously, paying close attention to every detail.
The gardener assiduously tended to his plants, ensuring they flourished throughout the season.
He prepared assiduously for his exams, reviewing all his notes and practice tests.

Synset Examples

she worked assiduously on the senior thesis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligently - in a way that involves careful and persistent work or effort
  • Synonym: industriously - in a hardworking and energetic manner
  • Antonym: negligently - in a careless or neglectful manner

Etymology

From Latin 'assiduus' (persistent, continual) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
