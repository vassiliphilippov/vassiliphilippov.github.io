Ecuador

1. PROPN

/ ˌɛkwəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˌɛkwəˈdɔː / (BrE)
a country in northwestern South America, known for its diverse landscape including the Andes mountains and its independence from Spain in 1822.
country in northwestern South America

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Ecuador to see its famous Galápagos Islands.
The Andes mountain range runs through much of Ecuador, shaping its unique geography.
Elections in Ecuador draw attention from international observers every few years.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Ecuador' meaning 'equator', as the country is named after the equator which runs through it.
