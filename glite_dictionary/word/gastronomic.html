gastronomic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡæstrəˈnɑmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the art or practice of choosing, cooking, and eating good food
related to fine eating or cuisine

Examples

The city is a gastronomic paradise, offering a variety of world-class restaurants.
She decided to enroll in a gastronomic course to learn about gourmet cooking.
Our trip included several gastronomic experiences, such as wine tastings and chef-led dinners.

Synset Examples

gastronomic adventures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: culinary - relating to cooking or kitchens

Etymology

From French gastronomique, from gastronomie (gastronomy), from Greek gastrós (stomach) and nomos (law).
