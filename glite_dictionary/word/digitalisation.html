digitalisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪdʒɪtələˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒɪt(ə)laɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the administration of digitalis for the treatment of certain heart disorders
use of digitalis in medical treatment
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted for digitalisation to control his arrhythmia.
Careful monitoring is required during digitalisation due to the drug's narrow therapeutic window.
Digitalisation helped improve her heart function after persistent symptoms.

Etymology

From digitalis, the genus name of the foxglove plant, source of the drug; formed with -ation.
