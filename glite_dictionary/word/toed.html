toed

1. ADJ

/ toʊd / (AmE), / təʊd / (BrE)
(in combination) having a toe or toes of a specified kind or number.
having toes of a specified kind (in combination)

Examples

The gecko is a five-toed lizard commonly found in warm regions.
She wore a pair of open-toed sandals to the party.
Many aquatic birds have web-toed feet that help them swim.

Synset Examples

long-toed
five-toed
