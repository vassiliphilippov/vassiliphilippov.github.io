misogynism

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈsɑdʒəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒdʒəˌnɪzəm / (BrE)
Hatred or dislike of women.
hatred of women

Examples

The film was criticized for its apparent misogynism and negative portrayal of female characters.
Her activism was motivated by a desire to fight widespread misogynism in society.
Scholars have analyzed how misogynism manifests in different cultures and historical periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misogyny - hatred of women (synonym)
  • Antonym: misandry - hatred of men (antonym)

Etymology

From Greek misos (hatred) + gynē (woman).
