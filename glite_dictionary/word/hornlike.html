hornlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɔrnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling horn or made of horn; having the appearance or qualities of horn.
resembling or made of horn

Examples

The insect’s antennae were long and hornlike in appearance.
Some fungi produce tough, hornlike structures for protection.
He admired the sculpture’s hornlike surface, which seemed almost natural.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horny - having the texture or appearance of horn
