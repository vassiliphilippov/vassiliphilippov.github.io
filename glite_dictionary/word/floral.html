floral

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈflɔːrəl / (BrE)
resembling, made of, or suggestive of flowers; decorated with or related to flowers.
resembling or decorated with flowers

Examples

The curtains had a beautiful floral pattern that brightened the room.
She wore a floral dress to the garden party.
The invitation cards featured an elegant floral border.

Synset Examples

an unusual floral design

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flowery - decorated with or full of flowers

Etymology

From Latin 'floralis', from 'flos' meaning 'flower'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01793214-s
Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ ˈflɔr.əl / (AmE), / ˈflɔː.rəl / (BrE)
relating to the plant life or flora of a particular region.
relating to plant life of a region

Examples

The region is noted for its unique floral diversity.
Researchers published a comprehensive report on the floral composition of the highlands.
Changes in climate are affecting the floral communities of the area.

Synset Examples

characteristic alpine floral elements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botanical - relating to plants or plant science

Etymology

From Latin flōrālis, from flōs (flower).
