Deutschland

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɔɪtʃ.lɑnt / (AmE), / ˈdɔɪtʃ.lant / (BrE)
the German name for Germany, a country in central Europe
German name for Germany

Examples

Many Germans proudly refer to their country as Deutschland in their native language.
The national football team of Deutschland has won several World Cups.
On the map, you can see that Deutschland shares borders with nine other countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Germany - a country in central Europe

Etymology

German, from 'deutsch' (German) + 'Land' (country).
