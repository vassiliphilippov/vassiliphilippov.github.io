Anna Mary Robertson Moses

1. PROPN

/ ˈænə ˈmɛri ˈrɑbərtsən ˈmoʊzɪs / (AmE), / ˈanə ˈmɛːri ˈrɒbətsən ˈməʊzɪs / (BrE)
(1860–1961) United States painter known for her colorful and primitive rural scenes, popularly known as Grandma Moses.
U.S. painter known as Grandma Moses

Examples

Anna Mary Robertson Moses gained fame late in life as Grandma Moses, a beloved American folk artist.
Collectors prize paintings by Anna Mary Robertson Moses for their charming depictions of rural life.
The exhibition featured works by Anna Mary Robertson Moses alongside other influential American painters.
