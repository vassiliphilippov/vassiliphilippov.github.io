Northern Mariana Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔrðɚn ˌmæriˈænə ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˌmærɪˈænə ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a self-governing territory in the western Pacific Ocean, comprising all of the Mariana Islands except Guam
self-governing territory in the western Pacific Ocean

Examples

The Northern Mariana Islands attract tourists with their beautiful beaches and rich marine life.
She moved to the Northern Mariana Islands for work in the hospitality industry.
The Northern Mariana Islands are a commonwealth in political union with the United States.

Etymology

Named after Mariana of Austria, Queen Regent of Spain.
