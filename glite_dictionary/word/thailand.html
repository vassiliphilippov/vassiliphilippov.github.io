Thailand

1. PROPN

/ ˈtaɪˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
A country in Southeast Asia, extending southward along the Isthmus of Kra to the Malay Peninsula; formerly known as Siam.
country in Southeast Asia

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Thailand because of its beautiful beaches and rich culture.
The capital city of Thailand is Bangkok, which is known for its vibrant street life.
Last summer, she traveled through Thailand and learned how to make authentic Thai food.

Synset Examples

Thailand is the official name of the former Siam

Etymology

From Thai ไทย (Thai) + land; formerly known as Siam until 1939.
