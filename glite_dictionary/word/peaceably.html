peaceably

1. ADV

/ ˈpisəbli / (AmE), / ˈpiːsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is calm and does not involve argument, violence, or disturbance; in a peaceable manner
in a calm, non-violent manner

Examples

The protesters marched peaceably through the city, carrying signs and chanting slogans.
The two countries were able to resolve their dispute peaceably without resorting to war.
She hoped that her neighbors would settle their differences peaceably rather than going to court.

Synset Examples

the tenant paying the rent hereby reserved and performing the several covenants herein on his part contained shall peaceably hold and enjoy the demised premises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peaceful - not involving or characterized by violence or conflict
  • Antonym: aggressively - in a manner showing aggression; ready to attack or confront

Etymology

Formed from 'peaceable' (ultimately from Old French *paisible*) + '-ly'.
