xix

1. NUM

/ ɛks aɪ ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks aɪ ɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for nineteen (19)
Roman numeral for 19

Examples

The inscription read 'xix' to mark the nineteenth anniversary of the founding.
Chapters were numbered using Roman numerals, so chapter nineteen was 'xix'.
The official document listed the year as 'xix' in Roman numerals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nineteen - the number 19

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10) + IX (9), totaling 19.
