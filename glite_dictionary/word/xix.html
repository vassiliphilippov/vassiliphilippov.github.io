XIX

1. NUM

/ ɛks aɪ ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks aɪ ɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number nineteen (19)
Roman numeral for nineteen

Examples

The book was published in the year XIX as printed on the first page.
Chapter XIX focuses on the main character’s journey through adversity.
In Roman numerals, XIX represents the number nineteen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nineteen - the number 19
  • Degree Lower: XVIII - the Roman numeral for eighteen (18)
  • Degree Higher: XX - the Roman numeral for twenty (20)

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'X' (ten) + 'IX' (nine), together signifying 19.
