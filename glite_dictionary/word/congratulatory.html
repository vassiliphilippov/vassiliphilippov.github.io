congratulatory

1. ADJ

/ kənˈɡrætʃəˌlətɔri / (AmE), / kənˈɡratjʊlətəri / (BrE)
expressing praise, pleasure, or approval for someone's success or good fortune
expressing praise for success or achievement

Examples

She sent a congratulatory message after her friend's promotion.
A congratulatory card arrived in the mail to celebrate his new baby.
The team received congratulatory emails from their colleagues around the world.

Synset Examples

a congratulatory telegram
the usual congratulatory crowd was conspicuously absent
a gratulatory address

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complimentary - expressing praise or admiration

Etymology

From Latin 'congratulatio', from 'congratulor' (wish joy to).
