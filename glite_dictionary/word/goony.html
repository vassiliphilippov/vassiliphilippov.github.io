goony

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːni / (AmE), / ˈɡuːni / (BrE)
A type of albatross, especially one with black feet, found mainly in the Pacific region.
type of albatross with black feet

Examples

The goony soared effortlessly above the waves, searching for fish near the shore.
Scientists are studying how the goony adapts to changes in ocean currents.
Tourists visited the remote island, hoping to catch a glimpse of a rare goony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - large seabird found mainly in the Southern Ocean
