Salvadorean

1. ADJ

/ ˌsælvəˈdɔːriən / (AmE), / ˌsælvəˈdɔːriən / (BrE)
Relating to El Salvador or its people.
of or relating to El Salvador

Examples

Many families in Los Angeles have Salvadorean roots and preserve their traditions.
She enjoyed eating traditional Salvadorean pupusas at the local restaurant.
The exhibit featured vibrant Salvadorean artwork inspired by folk traditions.

Synset Examples

Salvadoran coffee
Salvadoran guerillas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Salvadoran - relating to El Salvador or its people; variant spelling

Etymology

From Spanish 'salvadoreño', from El Salvador (the country in Central America).

2. NOUN

/ ˌsæl.vəˈdɔː.ri.ən / (AmE), / ˌsal.vəˈdɔː.ri.ən / (BrE)
A person who is a native or inhabitant of El Salvador.
native or inhabitant of El Salvador

Examples

Several Salvadorean artists displayed their work at the international exhibition.
The Salvadorean community in the city has grown significantly over the past decade.
He spoke with pride about his Salvadorean heritage and traditions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Salvadoran - native or inhabitant of El Salvador; alternative spelling

Etymology

Formed from the country name 'El Salvador' with the English suffix '-ean' denoting origin.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09720219-n
Synset POS: n
