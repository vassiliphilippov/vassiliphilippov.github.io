tosser

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːsər / (AmE), / ˈtɒsə / (BrE)
(British slang) An insulting term for a person regarded as stupid, annoying, or contemptible; originally used to mean a masturbator.
(slang) an insulting term for a contemptible person
slang offensive british

Examples

Don't be such a tosser; it's only a game.
He called his boss a tosser after a heated argument.
They thought the loud guy at the bar was a real tosser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wanker - (British slang) an insulting term for a contemptible person, literally a masturbator

Etymology

British slang, from 'to toss off', meaning to masturbate.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɑːsər / (AmE), / ˈtɒs.ə / (BrE)
(British, slang) A contemptible or foolish person, often used as an insult.
contemptible or foolish person (slang)
slang british

Examples

I can't believe what a tosser he made of himself at the meeting yesterday.
Ignore him; he's just being a tosser again as usual.
They all agreed that the manager was a complete tosser after his rude comments.

Synset Examples

She thinks he's a tosser for how he treated her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - A stupid or foolish person
  • Synonym: jerk - An obnoxious or contemptible person (informal, American)

Etymology

From British slang 'to toss' (to masturbate), with '-er' suffix.
