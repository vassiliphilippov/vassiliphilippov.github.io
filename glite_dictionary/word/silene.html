silene

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈliːni / (AmE), / sɪˈliːni / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Silene, which includes various flowering species found chiefly in temperate regions.
plant genus with various flowering species

Examples

The garden was filled with silene blooming in shades of pink and white.
Many wildflower meadows feature silene among other native species.
Researchers studied the pollination habits of several silene plants.

Etymology

From Latin *Silene*, the name of a woodland deity, used as a plant genus name by Linnaeus.
