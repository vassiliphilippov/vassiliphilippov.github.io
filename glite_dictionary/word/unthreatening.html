unthreatening

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing fear or suggesting harm; not threatening in manner or appearance.
not threatening or intimidating

Examples

The kitten's unthreatening appearance made even the nervous child smile.
His voice was gentle and unthreatening, putting everyone at ease during the meeting.
Despite her size, the dog was completely unthreatening and friendly to strangers.

Synset Examples

her well-meaning words were received in silence
the exasperation of a … well-meaning cow worried by dogs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: threatening - having a hostile or menacing quality

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'threatening'.
