somniloquism

1. NOUN

/ sɑmˈnɪləkwɪzəm / (AmE), / sɒmˈnɪləkwɪzəm / (BrE)
the act or habit of talking or uttering speech while asleep
talking during sleep
medical

Examples

His doctor diagnosed him with somniloquism after several nights of restless sleep.
People with somniloquism may be unaware they talk during the night.
Chronic somniloquism can sometimes disturb a bed partner's rest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep-talking - the act of talking during sleep
  • Synonym: somniloquy - the act of talking in one's sleep

Etymology

From Latin somnus (sleep) + loqui (to speak) + -ism.
