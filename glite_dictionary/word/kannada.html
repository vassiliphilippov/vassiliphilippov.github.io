Kannada

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.nə.də / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ə.də / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the state of Karnataka, southern India
Dravidian language of southern India

Examples

Many people in Karnataka speak Kannada as their first language.
She is learning to write and read Kannada at school.
The movie was dubbed into English from the original Kannada version.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Telugu - a Dravidian language spoken in southeastern India
  • Synonym: Tamil - a Dravidian language spoken in southern India and Sri Lanka

Etymology

From Kannada ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannaḍa), the name of the language and its people.

2. NOUN

/ kəˈnɑːdə / (AmE), / kəˈnædə / (BrE)
a Dravidian language spoken mainly in the state of Karnataka in southern India
a Dravidian language of southern India

Examples

Many people in Karnataka speak Kannada as their first language.
He is learning to write and read Kannada fluently.
The book was translated from English into Kannada for local readers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Telugu - a Dravidian language spoken in southeastern India
  • Synonym: Tamil - a Dravidian language spoken in southern India and Sri Lanka
  • Synonym: Malayalam - a Dravidian language spoken in southwestern India

Etymology

From Kannada ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannada), the native name of the language.
