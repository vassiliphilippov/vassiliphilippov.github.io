slinger

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who uses a sling to throw stones or other projectiles
person who uses a sling

Examples

The ancient army included a skilled slinger who could hit targets from great distances.
In some legends, the hero defeats his foes by being an expert slinger.
During competitions, each slinger demonstrated different techniques for throwing stones.

Etymology

From Middle English slengere, from sling + -er.
