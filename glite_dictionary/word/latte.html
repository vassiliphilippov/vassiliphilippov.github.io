latte

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːteɪ / (AmE), / ˈlæteɪ / (BrE)
a type of coffee made with strong espresso and steamed, frothed milk
coffee with steamed milk

Examples

She ordered a vanilla latte at her favorite coffee shop.
Do you prefer your latte with whole milk or oat milk?
He likes to start his mornings with a hot latte and a croissant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cappuccino - espresso-based coffee with steamed milk and foamed milk topping

Etymology

From Italian 'latte' meaning 'milk'.
