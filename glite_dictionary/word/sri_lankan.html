Sri Lankan

1. ADJ

/ sri ˈlæŋkən / (AmE), / sri ˈlæŋkən / (BrE)
relating to Sri Lanka, its people, or its culture
of or relating to Sri Lanka

Examples

The Sri Lankan government declared a national holiday for the festival.
She learned to cook delicious Sri Lankan dishes from her grandmother.
The exhibition features contemporary Sri Lankan art and sculpture.

Synset Examples

Sri Lankan beaches
Sri Lankan forces fighting the Sinhalese rebels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sri Lanka - an island country in South Asia

2. NOUN

/ ʃri ˈlæŋkən / (AmE), / sri ˈlæŋkən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Sri Lanka
person from Sri Lanka

Examples

Many Sri Lankans celebrate their nation's independence day with traditional festivals.
The city is home to a large community of Sri Lankans.
She met several Sri Lankans while studying abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sri Lankan - relating to or characteristic of Sri Lanka or its people (adjective)

Etymology

From Sri Lanka, the name of the country, with the English suffix -an to form a demonym.
