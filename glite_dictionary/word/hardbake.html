hardbake

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrdb.eɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdb.eɪk / (BrE)
a traditional British sweet made by baking molasses and butter with almonds until hard and brittle
British sweet of baked molasses, butter, and almonds
british

Examples

My grandmother always made hardbake for special occasions during my childhood.
At the bakery, I was tempted by the crunchy almonds in the hardbake displayed in the window.
Sarah wrapped pieces of hardbake as festive gifts for her friends.
