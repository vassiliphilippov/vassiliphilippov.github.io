oversuspicious

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚsəˈspɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəsəˈspɪʃəs / (BrE)
excessively or unduly suspicious; having too much suspicion or distrust.
excessively suspicious

Examples

His oversuspicious nature kept him from trusting even his closest friends.
You shouldn't be oversuspicious of their motives without any clear evidence.
The manager was sometimes oversuspicious, constantly questioning her employees' loyalty.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: suspicious - having or showing suspicion

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' meaning 'excessive' and 'suspicious.'
