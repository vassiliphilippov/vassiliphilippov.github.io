sourball

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊrˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a round piece of hard candy with a tart or sour flavor
tart hard candy

Examples

She offered me a red sourball from her candy jar.
The children laughed as they puckered their lips after eating a sourball.
He kept a bag of sourballs in his desk as a midday treat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hard candy - a confectionery that is solid and brittle
