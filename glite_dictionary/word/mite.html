mite

1. NOUN

/ maɪt / (AmE), / maɪt / (BrE)
a very small arachnid, often found infesting animals, plants, or stored foods
very small arachnid

Examples

The veterinarian found a mite living in the dog’s ear canal.
House dust mites can cause allergic reactions in some people.
Aphids and spider mites often damage garden plants in the summer.

Etymology

From Old English mīte (a small parasite).
