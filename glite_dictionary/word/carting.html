carting

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or business of transporting and disposing of something by cart or truck
transporting and disposing of goods by cart or truck

Examples

The company specializes in carting construction debris away from building sites.
New regulations have increased fees for carting household waste in the city.
After the festival ended, workers spent hours carting garbage off the field.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haulage - the business of transporting goods by road or rail

Etymology

From the verb 'cart,' derived from Middle English 'carte', referring to a wheeled vehicle, plus the -ing suffix for action or process.
