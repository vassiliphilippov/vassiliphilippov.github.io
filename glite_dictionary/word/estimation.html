estimation

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛstəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
a judgement or opinion about the qualities, character, or value of something or someone
opinion about quality or value

Examples

In my estimation, she is the most talented candidate for the position.
His actions increased his estimation in the eyes of the community.
The teacher’s estimation of the project was more positive than I expected.

Synset Examples

many factors are involved in any estimate of human life
in my estimation the boy is innocent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opinion - a belief or judgement about something
  • Synonym: assessment - a judgement about the quality or character of someone or something

Etymology

From Latin aestimatio (a valuation), from aestimare (to estimate).
