anginal

1. ADJ

/ ænˈdʒaɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ænˈdʒaɪ.nəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the pain associated with angina pectoris.
relating to angina pectoris pain
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted to the hospital with severe anginal pain that radiated to his left arm.
He takes medication to manage his anginal symptoms during periods of physical exertion.
Doctors closely monitor patients with frequent anginal attacks to prevent further heart complications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angina - chest pain due to reduced blood flow to the heart

Etymology

From 'angina' (quinsy, throat infection or pain in the chest) + '-al' (adjective-forming suffix).
