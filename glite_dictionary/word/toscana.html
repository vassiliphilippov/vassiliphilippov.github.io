Toscana

1. PROPN

/ toʊˈskɑː.nə / (AmE), / tɒsˈkaː.nə / (BrE)
the Italian name for Tuscany, a region in central Italy with Florence as its capital, renowned for its art, history, and landscape
Italian name for the Tuscany region, Italy

Examples

She spent a summer exploring the small towns of Toscana and learning about their history.
Many wines from Toscana are highly regarded around the world.
The rolling hills of Toscana attract tourists and artists alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tuscany - an English name for the region 'Toscana', central Italy

Etymology

Italian, from Latin 'Tuscana' (land of the Etruscans), related to 'Tuscan'.
