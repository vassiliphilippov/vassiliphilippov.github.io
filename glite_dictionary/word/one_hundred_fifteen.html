one hundred fifteen

1. NUM

/ wʌn hʌndrəd fɪfˈtin / (AmE), / wʌn hʌndrɪd fɪfˈtiːn / (BrE)
the cardinal number equal to one hundred and fifteen; five more than one hundred ten.
the number 115

Examples

The class has exactly one hundred fifteen students enrolled this semester.
Last year, we planted one hundred fifteen new trees in the park.
Her high score in the game was one hundred fifteen points, setting a new record.
