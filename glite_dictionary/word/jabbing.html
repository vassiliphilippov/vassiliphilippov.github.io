jabbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒæbɪŋ / (BrE)
a quick, sharp hand gesture, often resembling a punching or poking motion
a sharp hand gesture

Examples

She silenced him with a jabbing of her finger in his direction.
His angry jabbing at the elevator button didn't make it arrive any faster.
The coach corrected the player's stance after noticing his unnecessary jabbing motions during practice.

Synset Examples

he warned me with a jab with his finger
he made a thrusting motion with his fist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jab - a quick, sharp blow or poke
