bigwig

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɡˌwɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌwɪɡ / (BrE)
an important or powerful person, especially in an organization or group
an important or influential person

Examples

All the bigwigs from the industry attended the gala event last night.
As a junior employee, she rarely interacts with the bigwigs at headquarters.
Several bigwigs in government gathered to discuss the new policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: VIP - a very important person
  • Antonym: nobody - a person of little importance or influence

Etymology

Probably from the 18th-century fashion for high-ranking people to wear large wigs as a status symbol.
