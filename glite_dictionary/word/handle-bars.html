handle-bars

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændl̩ˌbɑɹz / (AmE), / ˈhændlˌbɑːz / (BrE)
a large, bushy moustache with long ends that may curl or hang down the sides of the mouth, resembling the handlebars of a bicycle
large moustache with curved ends

Examples

He proudly sported a thick handle-bars at the party.
His handle-bars gave him a distinctive, vintage appearance.
The character in the film was memorable for his outrageous handle-bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moustache - hair growing above the upper lip

Etymology

From the resemblance to the handlebars of a bicycle, first noted in the late 19th century.
