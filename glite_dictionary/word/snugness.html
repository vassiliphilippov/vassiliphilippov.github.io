snugness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌɡ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being comfortably warm, safe, and cozy
state of being warm and cozy

Examples

The snugness of the blanket made it hard to get out of bed on cold mornings.
She loved the snugness of the small cottage nestled in the woods.
After a long walk in the snow, they enjoyed the snugness of the warm living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coziness - the quality of being cozy, warm, and comfortable

Etymology

From the adjective 'snug' (cosy, comfortable) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
