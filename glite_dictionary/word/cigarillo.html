cigarillo

1. NOUN

/ sɪgəˈrɪloʊ / (AmE), / sɪgəˈrɪləʊ / (BrE)
A small cigar, typically wrapped in tobacco leaf rather than paper, and usually thinner than a regular cigar.
a small cigar wrapped in tobacco

Examples

He lit a cigarillo while waiting on the balcony.
The aroma of a vanilla cigarillo filled the room.
She bought a pack of cigarillos at the corner store.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: cigar - a cylindrical roll of tobacco for smoking

Etymology

From Spanish 'cigarillo', diminutive of 'cigarro' (cigar).
