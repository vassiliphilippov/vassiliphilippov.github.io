twenty-one

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈwʌn / (BrE)
A gambling card game in which players try to hold cards with a total value higher than the dealer's without exceeding 21; also known as blackjack.
card game, also called blackjack

Examples

They spent the night playing twenty-one at the busy casino table.
Many people use different strategies to win at twenty-one.
He lost his winnings after one unlucky hand of twenty-one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blackjack - A popular gambling card game where the goal is to get a hand totaling 21 or as close as possible without going over.
