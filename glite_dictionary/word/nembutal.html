Nembutal

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛmbjutɑl / (AmE), / ˈnɛmbjuːtɑːl / (BrE)
a trademarked barbiturate drug used as a sedative, hypnotic, and antispasmodic, often prescribed to manage anxiety or induce sleep
a barbiturate sedative drug (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Nembutal to help control the patient's severe insomnia.
Because of its potency, Nembutal is administered only under strict medical supervision.
He mentioned in his book that Nembutal was once a widely used sleeping aid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pentobarbital - the generic name for the drug sold as Nembutal

Etymology

Trademark, coined from 'N'-ethyl and 'butyl' (chemical structure).
