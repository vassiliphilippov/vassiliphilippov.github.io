Sajama

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈhɑːmə / (AmE), / sɑːˈhɑːmə / (BrE)
the highest mountain in Bolivia, located in the Andes, reaching 21,391 feet (6,542 meters) above sea level
highest mountain in Bolivia

Examples

Climbers from around the world travel to Bolivia to ascend Sajama each year.
The snow-capped peak of Sajama is visible from miles away across the Bolivian plains.
Many villagers revere Sajama as a sacred mountain in local tradition.
