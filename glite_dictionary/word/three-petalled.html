three-petalled

1. ADJ

/ θriːˈpɛtəld / (AmE), / θriːˈpɛtəld / (BrE)
(of a flower) having three petals.
having three petals (of a flower)

Examples

The three-petalled flowers of this species are easily recognizable in the wild.
We admired the delicate, three-petalled blooms in her garden.
Botanists classify the plant by its distinctive three-petalled structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petalled - having a specified number of petals
