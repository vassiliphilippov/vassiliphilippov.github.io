nonproprietary

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.prəˈpraɪəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.prəˈprʌɪətəri / (BrE)
not protected by trademark, patent, or copyright; open for use or distribution by anyone
not protected by intellectual property rights

Examples

This company focuses on developing nonproprietary software that anyone can use and modify freely.
Many generic medicines are nonproprietary and not restricted by patents.
The standards are nonproprietary, ensuring compatibility across different manufacturers.

Synset Examples

nonproprietary products are in the public domain and anyone can produce or distribute them

Related Terms

  • Antonym: proprietary - protected by trademark, patent, or copyright; privately owned

Etymology

From prefix non- (not) + proprietary (having ownership, esp. by trademark or copyright).
