endodontic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛn.doʊˈdɑn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊˈdɒn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to the branch of dentistry concerned with diseases and treatment of the dental pulp and root tissues
relating to endodontics (dental pulp care)
medical

Examples

The patient was referred to an endodontic specialist for root canal treatment.
Advances in endodontic techniques have improved the success rate of complex tooth procedures.
She is taking an endodontic course to further her dental expertise.

Synset Examples

an endodontic specialist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endodontics - the branch of dentistry dealing with dental pulp and tissues around the roots of a tooth

Etymology

From endodontics, which is from Greek endon (within) + odont- (tooth) + -ics.
