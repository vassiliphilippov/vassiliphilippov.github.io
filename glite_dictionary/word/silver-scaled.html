silver-scaled

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvɚ skeɪld / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə skeɪld / (BrE)
having the body covered or partly covered with silver-colored scales
covered with silver-colored scales

Examples

A silver-scaled dragon appeared in the legend, its body sparkling like polished metal.
