unsilenced

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsaɪlənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪlənst / (BrE)
Not silenced; allowed or able to speak, express, or make noise.
not silenced; allowed to speak

Examples

His unsilenced voice inspired others to speak out against injustice.
After years of oppression, their unsilenced protests echoed through the city streets.
The whistleblower remained unsilenced despite threats and intimidation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: silenced - made silent; suppressed or muted
  • Synonym: mute - unable or prevented from speaking
