telecommerce

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛl.iˌkɑː.mɚs / (AmE), / ˈtɛl.iˌkɒ.mɜːs / (BrE)
the use of the telephone as a medium for interactive promotion and selling of goods or services
sales and promotion via telephone

Examples

The company increased its profits by investing heavily in telecommerce.
Many businesses now rely on telecommerce to reach customers who prefer shopping from home.
As regulations tightened, telecommerce firms adapted their sales strategies to comply with new rules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telemarketing - direct marketing of goods or services to potential customers by telephone

Etymology

Formed from 'tele-' (from Greek tēle, meaning 'far') and 'commerce'.
