Mandalay

1. PROPN

/ ˈmændəˌleɪ / (AmE), / ˌmændəˈleɪ / (BrE)
a city in central Myanmar, known for its cultural heritage and historic sites, located north of Yangon (Rangoon)
city in central Myanmar

Examples

Thousands of tourists visit Mandalay each year to see its famous temples and palaces.
She grew up in Mandalay before moving to the capital city.
The Irrawaddy River flows past the western edge of Mandalay in central Myanmar.

Etymology

From Burmese မန္တလေး (Mantale), the name of the city.
