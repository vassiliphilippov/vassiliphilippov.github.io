Italy

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈɪtəli / (BrE)
a country in southern Europe, occupying the Italian Peninsula and known for its rich history as the center of the Roman Republic and Empire
country in southern Europe

Examples

My dream is to visit Italy and explore its historical cities.
She studied Renaissance art during her semester in Italy.
The cuisine of Italy is famous around the world, especially for its pasta and pizza.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Italian - of or relating to Italy, its people, or language
  • Synonym: Rome - capital city of Italy

Etymology

From Latin 'Italia', the ancient name for the region.
