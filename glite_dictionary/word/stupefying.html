stupefying

1. ADJ

/ ˈstuːpəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
so surprising or impressive as to stun, shock, or overwhelm someone
amazingly impressive or shocking

Examples

The magician performed a stupefying trick that left the entire audience gasping in disbelief.
The sheer speed of technological change is stupefying to many older people.
The artist's stupefying talent was evident in every brushstroke.

Synset Examples

such an enormous response was astonishing
an astounding achievement
the amount of money required was staggering
suffered a staggering defeat
the figure inside the boucle dress was stupefying

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - so surprising or impressive as to amaze greatly
  • Synonym: astounding - very surprising, especially in a way that is difficult to understand or believe
  • Synonym: staggering - so surprising or impressive as to be difficult to believe

Etymology

From Latin 'stupefacere', meaning to make stupid or numb.

/ ˈstuːpəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely surprising or shocking, causing great astonishment or consternation.
extremely shocking or astonishing

Examples

The stupefying speed of the roller coaster left everyone breathless.
His stupefying announcement caught the entire audience off guard.
The team's stupefying comeback in the final minutes amazed their fans.

Synset Examples

the stupefying impact of the tragedy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - Extremely surprising
  • Synonym: shocking - Causing surprise or dismay

Etymology

From Latin 'stupefacio', meaning 'to amaze' or 'to stun'.

3. ADJ

/ ˈstuː.pəˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstjuː.pɪˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Making someone unable to think or feel properly; causing insensibility or dullness.
causing insensibility or dullness

Examples

The stupefying boredom of the lecture made it hard to stay awake.
He suffered a stupefying headache after the accident.
The stupefying effects of the medication left her unable to concentrate.

Synset Examples

a stupefying blow to the head
the stupefying effects of hemp

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stunning - causing astonishment or disbelief; impressive or shocking
  • Antonym: refreshing - making someone feel less tired or more energetic

Etymology

From Latin 'stupefacere' (to stun), via the verb 'stupefy'.
