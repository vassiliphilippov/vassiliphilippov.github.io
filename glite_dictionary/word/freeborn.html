freeborn

1. ADJ

/ ˈfriˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
born free, especially not born into slavery or servitude
born free, not enslaved

Examples

She took pride in being freeborn and valued her independence deeply.
The rights of freeborn citizens were fiercely protected by law.
He was the first freeborn member of his family after generations of bondage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: slave - a person who is the legal property of another and is forced to obey them

Etymology

From Old English 'frēoboren', from 'frēo' (free) + 'boren' (born).
