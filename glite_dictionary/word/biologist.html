biologist

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / baɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies living organisms and their relationships to the environment
scientist who studies living organisms

Examples

The biologist presented her research on marine ecosystems at the international conference.
As a biologist, he spends much of his time conducting fieldwork in the rainforest.
Many biologists are concerned about the effects of climate change on biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals
  • Synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants

Etymology

From biology + -ist, with 'biology' from Greek bios (life) and -logia (study of).
