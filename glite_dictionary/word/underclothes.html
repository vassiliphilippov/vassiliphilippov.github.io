underclothes

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
clothes worn next to the skin and beneath outer garments
garments worn under outer clothes

Examples

He packed a change of underclothes for the weekend trip.
After the hike, her underclothes were damp with sweat.
Please put your dirty underclothes in the laundry basket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underwear - clothing worn under outer garments, typically next to the skin

Etymology

Formed from 'under' + 'clothes' in English, attested since the 17th century.
