unseemly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsimli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːmli / (BrE)
not proper or appropriate according to accepted standards of behavior or society; inappropriate or indecent
improper; inappropriate behavior

Examples

Such unseemly conduct is not tolerated at this school.
The politician apologized for his unseemly remarks.
It would be unseemly to argue loudly at a funeral.

Synset Examples

was buried with indecent haste
indecorous behavior
language unbecoming to a lady
unseemly to use profanity
moved to curb their untoward ribaldry

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seemly - proper and appropriate in behavior or appearance
  • Synonym: indecorous - not in keeping with good taste and propriety; improper

Etymology

From Old English un- (not) + seemly (fitting, suitable), from seem.
