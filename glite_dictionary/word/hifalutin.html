hifalutin

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪfəˈluːtən / (AmE), / ˌhaɪfəˈluːtɪn / (BrE)
(informal) Pretentiously lofty or pompous; affectedly grand, genteel, or snobbish.
pretentiously grand or pompous
informal

Examples

He always talks in that hifalutin way, as if he's better than everyone else.
They threw a hifalutin party full of celebrity guests and gourmet food.
I prefer simple language to all that hifalutin jargon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pretentious - Attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or merit
  • Antonym: modest - Having a humble view of one's importance

Etymology

Probably an alteration of 'highfaluting', itself of uncertain origin, possibly from 'high' + 'flute' or 'falutin'.
