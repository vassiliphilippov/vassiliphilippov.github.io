thanks

1. NOUN

/ θæŋks / (AmE), / θæŋks / (BrE)
words or expressions of gratitude or appreciation
expressions of gratitude

Examples

Let me express my thanks for all your help and support.
She sent a card to give her thanks for the lovely gift.
He nodded his thanks to the waiter before leaving the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gratitude - the feeling of being thankful
  • Synonym: appreciation - recognition and enjoyment of the good qualities of someone or something
  • Antonym: complaint - an expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance

Etymology

From Old English 'þanc' (thought, gratitude, favor).
