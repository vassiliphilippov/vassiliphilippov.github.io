El Iskandriyah

1. PROPN

/ ɛl ɪsˌkændɪˈriːjə / (AmE), / ɛl ɪsˌkændɪˈriːjə / (BrE)
The Arabic name for Alexandria, the principal port city of Egypt, situated on the Mediterranean coast and founded by Alexander the Great.
Arabic name for Alexandria, Egypt

Examples

Many historical travelers wrote about El Iskandriyah during the medieval period.
The bustling markets of El Iskandriyah attract visitors from around the world.
She spent a summer studying at a university in El Iskandriyah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alexandria - Port city in Egypt founded by Alexander the Great

Etymology

From Arabic الإسكندرية (al-Iskandariyya), literally 'Alexandria'.
