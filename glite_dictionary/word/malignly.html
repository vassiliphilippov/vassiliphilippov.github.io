malignly

1. ADV

/ məˈlaɪnli / (AmE), / məˈlaɪnli / (BrE)
In a malign, malicious, or evil manner.
in an evil or malicious way

Examples

She looked at him malignly, her eyes cold and unforgiving.
Rumors were malignly spread to ruin his reputation in the village.
He malignly sabotaged her efforts at every opportunity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maliciously - in a manner showing a desire to harm someone

Etymology

From 'malign' (from Latin malignus, 'evil-natured') + '-ly' adverbial suffix.
