Himalayan

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɪməˈleɪən / (AmE), / ˌhɪməˈleɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Himalayas, the mountain range in Asia
of or relating to the Himalayas

Examples

Many climbers dream of reaching the summit of a Himalayan mountain.
The Himalayan region is known for its unique biodiversity and challenging terrain.
Researchers studied the effects of high altitude on health in several Himalayan villages.

Synset Examples

Himalayan peaks

Etymology

From the Himalaya, the mountain range in Asia, plus the adjective-forming suffix -an.
