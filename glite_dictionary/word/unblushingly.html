unblushingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈblʌʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no shame or embarrassment; without blushing.
without shame or embarrassment

Examples

She unblushingly asked for a raise, confident in her worth to the company.
The politician unblushingly denied any wrongdoing despite the evidence.
He unblushingly took credit for the team’s work during the meeting.

Synset Examples

his principal opponent unblushingly declared victory before the ballots had been counted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamelessly - in a way that shows no shame

Etymology

From unblushing + -ly.
