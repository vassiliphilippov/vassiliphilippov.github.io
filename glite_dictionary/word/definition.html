definition

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a concise explanation of the meaning of a word, phrase, or symbol
explanation of a word's or symbol's meaning

Examples

The dictionary gives the definition of each technical term.
She looked up the definition of 'democracy' in several reference books.
His definition of success is different from mine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meaning - what is meant by a word, text, concept, or action
  • Synonym: explanation - a statement that clarifies or explains something

Etymology

From Latin 'definitio', from 'definire' (to define).

2. NOUN

/ ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the degree of distinctness or clarity of an outline or detail, especially in an image or a physical form
clarity or distinctness of outline

Examples

The athlete's intense training improved the definition of his muscles.
High definition television offers clearer images than standard-definition programs.
You could see every definition of the leaf against the sunny backdrop.

Synset Examples

exercise had given his muscles superior definition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharpness - the quality of being clear and distinct
  • Antonym: vagueness - the quality of lacking clarity or being indistinct

Etymology

From Latin 'definitio' (a limiting or determining).
