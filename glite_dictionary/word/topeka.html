Topeka

1. PROPN

/ təˈpiːkə / (AmE), / təˈpiːkə / (BrE)
the capital city of Kansas, located in the eastern part of the state on the Kansas River
capital of Kansas, USA

Examples

The state legislature meets in Topeka each year to discuss new laws.
She grew up in Topeka before moving to Chicago for college.
A major storm caused significant flooding in Topeka last summer.

Etymology

From a Kansa-Osage word meaning 'a good place to dig potatoes'.
