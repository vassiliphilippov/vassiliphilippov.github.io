Pete Seeger

1. PROPN

/ pit ˈsiːɡər / (AmE), / piːt ˈsiːɡə / (BrE)
(1919–2014) An influential American folk singer and songwriter who played a major role in popularizing folk music in the 20th century.
American folk singer and songwriter

Examples

Pete Seeger was known for his powerful performances and commitment to social justice.
Many contemporary folk musicians cite Pete Seeger as a major influence on their work.
The legacy of Pete Seeger lives on through his music and activism.
