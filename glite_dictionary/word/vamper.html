vamper

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæmpər / (AmE), / ˈvæmpə / (BrE)
a seductive woman who uses her attractiveness to manipulate or exploit men
seductive woman who exploits men

Examples

Rumors spread about her being a vamper, but she insisted that her intentions were always genuine.
In the silent film era, actresses who played the vamper were both admired and criticized.
He realized he had been fooled by a skilled vamper and regretted his decisions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vamp - a woman who uses her charms to seduce and exploit men

Etymology

From 'vamp', short for 'vampire', referring to a woman who preys on men, + -er.
