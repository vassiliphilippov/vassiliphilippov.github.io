spender

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛndər / (AmE), / ˈspɛndə / (BrE)
a person who spends money, especially in a generous or extravagant way
a person who spends money

Examples

He's known as a big spender and loves to buy the latest gadgets.
While his sister is a saver, Tom is definitely a spender.
The company is looking for ways to attract more spenders during the holiday season.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thrifter - a person who saves money and spends carefully
  • Synonym: spender - someone who spends money freely or extravagantly

Etymology

From the verb 'spend' + -er suffix indicating agent.

2. NOUN

/ ˈspɛndər / (AmE), / ˈspɛndə / (BrE)
a person who spends money, especially frequently or in a particular manner
person who spends money

Examples

As a careful spender, she always shops around for the best deals.
He's known to be a generous spender when he goes on vacation.
The company is looking for a big spender to sponsor their event.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saver - a person who saves money rather than spending it

Etymology

From the verb 'spend' + agent suffix '-er'.
