Jomada I

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmɑːdɑ aɪ / (AmE), / dʒəˈmɑːdə aɪ / (BrE)
the fifth month of the Islamic calendar.
the fifth Islamic calendar month

Examples

Many important events in Islamic history occurred during Jomada I.
The fasting period does not usually fall in Jomada I, as it is not Ramadan.
He planned his trip to coincide with the start of Jomada I.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Jumada II - the sixth month of the Islamic calendar
  • Degree Lower: Rabi' II - the fourth month of the Islamic calendar

Etymology

From Arabic 'Jumādā al-Ūlā' (the first Jumada).
