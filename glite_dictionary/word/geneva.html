Geneva

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈnivə / (AmE), / dʒɪˈniːvə / (BrE)
a city in southwestern Switzerland at the western tip of Lake Geneva, known for being the headquarters of various international organizations
city in Switzerland; international organizations' headquarters

Examples

The United Nations has an office in Geneva where international conferences are frequently held.
She spent her summer studying French in Geneva and exploring the nearby Alps.
Geneva is renowned for its beautiful lakeside setting and cosmopolitan atmosphere.
