unshaped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪpt / (BrE)
not fully formed or given a definite shape; incompletely or imperfectly shaped.
not fully formed or shaped

Examples

The sculptor started with a block of unshaped marble.
He mixed the ingredients into an unshaped lump of dough.
Several unshaped pieces of wood lay scattered in the workshop.

Synset Examples

unshaped dough
unshaped timbers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shapeless - having no definite shape or form
  • Antonym: formed - having a defined or finished shape
