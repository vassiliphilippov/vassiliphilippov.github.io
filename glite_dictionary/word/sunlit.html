sunlit

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌn.lɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.lɪt / (BrE)
Illuminated by sunlight.
illuminated by sunlight

Examples

She stood in the sunlit garden, enjoying the warmth of the afternoon.
The sunlit room felt cheerful and inviting compared to the dark hallway.
Through the window, we could see the sunlit mountains glowing in the distance.

Synset Examples

the sunlit slopes of the canyon
violet valleys and the sunstruck ridges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunny - bright with sunlight
  • Antonym: shadowy - full of shadows; not illuminated

Etymology

From 'sun' + past participle of 'light'.
