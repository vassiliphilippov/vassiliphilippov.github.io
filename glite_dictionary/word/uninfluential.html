uninfluential

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl / (BrE)
Not able to influence people or situations; having little or no influence.
having little or no influence

Examples

The new policy was largely uninfluential in changing public opinion.
Despite his efforts, he remained uninfluential within the organization.
Some critics argue that the artist's early works were relatively uninfluential.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: influential - having the power to influence people or situations

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'influential.'
