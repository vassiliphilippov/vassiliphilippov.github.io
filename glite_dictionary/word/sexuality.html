sexuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the characteristics or qualities that distinguish the sexes, especially in terms of reproductive roles or biological differences
reproductive characteristics distinguishing sexes

Examples

Scientists study the sexuality of various species to understand their reproductive strategies.
The sexuality of the plant determines whether it produces male or female flowers.
Understanding the sexuality of an organism is crucial for breeding programs.

Synset Examples

she didn't want to know the sex of the foetus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex - the biological distinction between male and female

Etymology

From Medieval Latin sexualitas; from Latin sexualis (relating to sex) + -ity.
