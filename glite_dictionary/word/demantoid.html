demantoid

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmæntɔɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈmæntɔɪd / (BrE)
a green variety of andradite garnet valued as a gemstone, known for its brilliance and fire.
green gemstone variety of andradite garnet

Examples

The vivid green hue of the demantoid makes it a prized collector's gem.
Her engagement ring featured a rare Russian demantoid set in platinum.
Jewelers admire demantoid for its exceptional sparkle and brilliant fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garnet - a group of silicate minerals used as gemstones and abrasives
  • Synonym: andradite - a kind of garnet mineral, of which demantoid is a variety

Etymology

From German 'Demant' (diamond), referencing its diamond-like luster.
