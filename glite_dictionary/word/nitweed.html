nitweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈnɪtˌwiːd / (BrE)
an annual North American weed with thin wiry stems, tiny scalelike leaves, and small yellow flowers
a North American annual weed

Examples

The field was overrun with nitweed after a particularly wet spring.
Gardeners struggle to control nitweed because it grows quickly in poor soil.
The small yellow flowers of nitweed make it easy to identify among native grasses.
