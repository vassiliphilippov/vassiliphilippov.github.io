Grevillea

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈvɪliə / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈvɪliə / (BrE)
A genus of Australian shrubs and trees, often with conspicuous orange or red flowers.
genus of Australian flowering shrubs and trees

Examples

The garden featured several species of Grevillea, attracting native birds with their bright flowers.
She planted a Grevillea in her backyard to add color and attract wildlife.
Many Australian gardens include Grevilleas for their hardiness and vibrant blooms.

Etymology

Named after Charles Francis Greville, British botanist.
