strengthening

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛŋθənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming or making something stronger
the act of becoming stronger

Examples

The strengthening of the economy led to more job opportunities.
Regular exercise contributes to the strengthening of your muscles and bones.
There has been a noticeable strengthening of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weakening - the process of becoming weaker

Etymology

From 'strengthen' (verb), from 'strength' + '-en'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛŋθənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθ(ə)nɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something stronger or increasing its strength
act of making stronger

Examples

The new policy is aimed at the strengthening of economic ties between the two countries.
Physical exercises focus on the strengthening of the back and abdominal muscles.
The bridge required considerable strengthening after years of heavy use.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weakening - the act of making something weaker
