ba-dum ching

1. NOUN

/ bəˌdʌm ˈtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / bəˌdʌm ˈtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an imitative sound effect used to indicate the delivery of a punchline or joke in a comedic setting
sound effect for a joke's punchline
informal

Examples

After the comedian told his joke, someone in the audience made a ba-dum ching sound.
He ended his story with a clever punchline and a dramatic ba-dum ching.
The television show added a recorded ba-dum ching whenever a character made a bad pun.

Synset Examples

Everyone laughed, and the drummer hit a ba-dum ching.
He ended the joke with a confident ba-dum ching.
After his punchline, he added a playful ba-dum ching.

Etymology

Imitative of the sound made by a drum and cymbal after a joke, commonly used in comedy.
