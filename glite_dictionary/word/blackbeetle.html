blackbeetle

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈblækˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a dark brown or black species of cockroach, originally from the Orient but now found nearly worldwide
a species of cockroach

Examples

A blackbeetle scurried across the kitchen floor when I turned on the light.
The old house had a problem with blackbeetles in the cupboards.
We learned that the blackbeetle is actually a type of cockroach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cockroach - an insect of the family Blattodea, known for scuttling movements and resilience
