collection

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
a book, recording, or other publication containing a variety of works, such as stories, poems, or articles
book or publication of various works

Examples

The library acquired a new collection of essays by contemporary authors.
She edited a collection of short stories focusing on life in the city.
This collection features rare recordings from jazz legends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthology - a published collection of literary pieces, such as poems or stories

Etymology

From Latin collectio (a gathering together), from colligere (to gather).

2. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of gathering things together, especially for a particular purpose
act of gathering things together

Examples

The collection of data took several weeks to complete.
A collection was organized for the victims of the flood.
His duties included the collection of taxes from local businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gathering - the action of coming together or collecting

Etymology

From Latin 'collectio' (a gathering together), from 'colligere' (to gather together).

3. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈlɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a group of things that have been gathered together or are considered as a whole, often because they are related or of interest
group of gathered things

Examples

She keeps a large collection of rare coins in a locked cabinet.
The museum's latest collection features modern art from around the world.
His stamp collection has grown steadily since he was a child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assemblage - a collection or gathering of things or people
  • Synonym: hoard - a hidden or carefully guarded collection of valuables
  • Antonym: dispersal - the act of distributing things over a wide area

Etymology

From Latin collectio (a gathering together), from colligere (to gather together).

Source: WordNet

4. NOUN

/ kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
an act of requesting and gathering money, especially for a charitable purpose
request for charitable donations

Examples

There was a collection at the end of the concert to support the local shelter.
The school organized a collection for disaster relief victims.
He donated generously during the Sunday collection at church.

Synset Examples
