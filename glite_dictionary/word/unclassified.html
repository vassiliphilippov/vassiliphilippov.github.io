unclassified

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈklæsɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈklæsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not designated as secret or confidential by a security classification; open to general access or disclosure.
not subject to security classification

Examples

The report was marked as unclassified and available to the public.
Only unclassified information may be shared outside the organization.
He requested unclassified documents through a freedom of information act.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: classified - restricted according to security level

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'classified'.

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈklæs.ɪ.faɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈklɑː.sɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
not arranged into or assigned to a specific class or category; not classified.
not categorized; not classified

Examples

The files remained unclassified until the researchers analyzed their contents.
Many species in the sample were still unclassified due to lack of information.
Her documents were strictly unclassified and open to public access.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: classified - arranged into categories; officially designated as secret

Etymology

From un- (prefix meaning 'not') + classified (past participle of classify, from French classifier)

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00416521-a
Synset POS: a
