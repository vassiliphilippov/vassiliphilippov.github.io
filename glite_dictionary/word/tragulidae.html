Tragulidae

1. NOUN

/ træˈɡjuːlɪdiː / (AmE), / trəˈɡjuːlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of small hoofed mammals known as chevrotains, native to Asia and Africa.
family of small hoofed mammals (chevrotains)

Examples

Tragulidae are the smallest hoofed mammals, commonly known as mouse deer.
The members of the family Tragulidae are found in forests of Asia and Africa.
Scientists study Tragulidae to understand the evolution of ruminants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chevrotain - small, deer-like mammal also known as mouse deer

Etymology

From modern Latin Tragulidae, based on Tragus, a genus name, from Greek tragos (goat).
