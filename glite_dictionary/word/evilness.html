evilness

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈiːvəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being morally wrong or wicked.
moral wickedness or wrongdoing

Examples

The evilness of the villain's actions shocked the entire community.
Writers have long explored the evilness inherent in human nature.
She was disturbed by the evilness lurking beneath his charming appearance.
