shorts

1. NOUN

/ ʃɔrts / (AmE), / ʃɔːts / (BrE)
Men's underpants that cover the hips and buttocks, typically short in length.
men's underpants

Examples

He always buys cotton shorts for comfort.
After his shower, he put on a clean pair of shorts.
Those old shorts need to be replaced soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxer shorts - loose-fitting men's underpants
  • Synonym: briefs - tight-fitting style of men's underpants

2. NOUN

/ ʃɔrts / (AmE), / ʃɔːts / (BrE)
A piece of clothing worn on the lower part of the body, covering from the waist to at or above the knee, and with separate sections for each leg.
trousers ending at or above the knee

Examples

He wore shorts and a T-shirt to the park on the hot day.
Many schools do not allow students to wear shorts as part of their uniform.
She packed several pairs of shorts for her summer vacation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trousers - outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles, divided into two separate parts for each leg
  • Synonym: pants - outer garment covering each leg separately, typically extending from the waist to the ankles

Etymology

From 'short', referring to the length of the garment.
