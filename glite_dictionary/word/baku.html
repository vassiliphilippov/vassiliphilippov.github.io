Baku

1. PROPN

/ bɑːˈkuː / (AmE), / bəˈkuː / (BrE)
the capital city of Azerbaijan, located on the Caspian Sea, known as a major port and important center for oil production
capital city of Azerbaijan

Examples

The president of Azerbaijan delivered a speech in Baku last night.
Many international companies have offices based in Baku due to its oil industry.
Tourists were fascinated by the blend of old and modern architecture in Baku.
