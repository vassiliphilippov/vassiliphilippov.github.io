Lanchou

1. PROPN

/ lænˈdʒoʊ / (AmE), / lanˈdʒəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of Gansu province in northwestern China, located on the Yellow River; also spelled Lanzhou
capital city of Gansu, China

Examples

The train from Beijing to Lanchou travels through breathtaking landscapes.
Many historical trade routes passed through Lanchou on the ancient Silk Road.
The Yellow River flows directly through the city of Lanchou.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lanzhou - modern standard spelling of Lanchou, city in Gansu, China

Etymology

From Chinese 兰州 (Lánzhōu), sometimes romanized as Lanchou.
