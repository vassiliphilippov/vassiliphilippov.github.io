bocci

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑː.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈbɒtʃ.i / (BrE)
An Italian bowling game played on a long, narrow dirt court, in which players try to throw balls closest to a smaller target ball.
Italian ball game similar to bowling

Examples

My grandfather taught me how to play bocci in his backyard.
Every Sunday, the club members gather to compete in bocci tournaments.
The local park features a bocci court open to the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bocce - Alternate spelling of the Italian bowling game

Etymology

From Italian 'bocce' (plural of 'boccia', meaning 'ball').
