expertise

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛkspɚˈtiz / (AmE), / ˌɛkspɜːˈtiːz / (BrE)
special skill or knowledge in a particular field; expert skill or judgment
specialized skill or knowledge

Examples

Her expertise in data analysis helped the company solve complex problems.
We hired him for his expertise in environmental law.
The chef’s culinary expertise impressed every guest at the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: competence - the ability to do something successfully or efficiently
  • Antonym: incompetence - lack of skill or ability

Etymology

From French 'expertise' (assessment, expert appraisal), from 'expert' (expert).
