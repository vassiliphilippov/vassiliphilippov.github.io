Al Ladhiqiyah

1. PROPN

/ æl lædˌhiːˈkiːjə / (AmE), / al lædˌhiːˈkiːjə / (BrE)
A seaport city on the western coast of Syria, serving as an important port on the Mediterranean Sea.
seaport city in western Syria

Examples

Many goods are imported through the port of Al Ladhiqiyah every year.
Tourists often visit Al Ladhiqiyah for its Mediterranean beaches and vibrant culture.
After the storm, aid shipments arrived quickly at Al Ladhiqiyah.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latakia - alternative name for Al Ladhiqiyah, a seaport city in Syria

Etymology

From Arabic اللاذقية‎ (al-Lādhiqīyah), the Arabic name for Latakia.
