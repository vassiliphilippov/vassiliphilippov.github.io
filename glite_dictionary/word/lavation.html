lavation

1. NOUN

/ læˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / læˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of washing or cleansing, especially using soap and water
the act of washing or cleansing

Examples

The nurse performed a careful lavation of the patient's wound before dressing it.
Frequent lavation of the hands can help prevent the spread of illness.
In certain rituals, a full lavation of the body is required before entering the temple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washing - the act of cleaning something with water and usually soap
  • Synonym: cleansing - the act of making something clean

Etymology

From Latin 'lavatio', from 'lavare' (to wash).
