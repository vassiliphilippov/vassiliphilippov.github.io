bakshish

1. NOUN

/ bɑkˈʃiʃ / (AmE), / bakˈʃiːʃ / (BrE)
A small sum of money given as a tip or gratuity, especially in South or Central Asian countries.
tip or gratuity (esp. in Asia)

Examples

In some countries, it's customary to give a bakshish to hotel porters.
The guide waited patiently for his bakshish after the tour ended.
He handed the taxi driver a small bakshish for his help with the luggage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tip - A small amount of money given to someone for their service
  • Synonym: gratuity - A sum of money given to someone for performing a service

Etymology

From Persian *bakhshīsh* (gift, present).
