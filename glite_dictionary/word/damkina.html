Damkina

1. PROPN

/ dæmˈkiːnə / (AmE), / damˈkiːnə / (BrE)
In Babylonian mythology, the earth goddess, consort of Ea and mother of Marduk.
Babylonian earth goddess

Examples

Damkina was revered as the nurturing earth goddess in ancient Babylonian religion.
According to legend, Damkina was the mother of the powerful god Marduk.
Rituals honoring Damkina were held to ensure fertile lands and abundant harvests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ea - Babylonian god of water, wisdom, and creation; husband of Damkina
  • Synonym: Marduk - Babylonian chief god, son of Damkina
