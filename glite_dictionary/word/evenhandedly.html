evenhandedly

1. ADV

/ ˌivənˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌiːvənˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
In a fair and impartial manner, without favoring any side.
in a fair and impartial manner

Examples

The judge acted evenhandedly when listening to both sides of the argument.
All employees expect to be treated evenhandedly regardless of their position.
The new policy was applied evenhandedly to every department in the company.

Synset Examples

deal fairly with one another

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fairly - in a way that is fair or impartial
  • Synonym: impartially - in an impartial manner, without bias
  • Antonym: unfairly - in a way that is not fair or impartial

Etymology

Formed from 'evenhanded' + '-ly'; 'evenhanded' from 'even hand' (impartial hand).
