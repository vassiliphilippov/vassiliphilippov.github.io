Gymnadeniopsis

1. NOUN

/ dʒɪm.nəˌdiːniˈɑːp.sɪs / (AmE), / dʒɪm.nəˌdiːniˈɒp.sɪs / (BrE)
A genus of North American terrestrial orchids, usually classified under the genus Habenaria.
genus of North American orchids

Examples

The rare Gymnadeniopsis orchid can be found in certain wetlands in North America.
Many botanists classify Gymnadeniopsis species under the broader genus Habenaria.
Researchers studied the pollination patterns of Gymnadeniopsis to understand its evolutionary history.

Etymology

From Greek 'gymnos' (naked) + 'aden' (gland) + '-opsis' (appearance).
