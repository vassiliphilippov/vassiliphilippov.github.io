homogenous

1. ADJ

/ hoʊˈmoʊdʒənəs / (AmE), / hɒˈmɒdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
consisting of parts or people that are all of the same or similar type; uniform in composition or character.
all of the same or similar kind

Examples

The town had a homogenous population with little ethnic diversity.
A homogenous mixture looks the same throughout, with no visibly different parts.
The company's leadership team is surprisingly homogenous in terms of background and education.

Synset Examples

a close-knit homogeneous group

Related Terms

  • Synonym: homogeneous - of the same or similar kind or nature (standard spelling in scientific use)
  • Antonym: heterogeneous - consisting of different kinds or types

Etymology

From Greek 'homogenēs' meaning 'of the same kind'.
