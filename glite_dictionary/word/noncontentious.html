noncontentious

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnkənˈtɛnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtɛnʃəs / (BrE)
not inclined to argue, dispute, or cause controversy; peaceable or uncontroversial in attitude or manner
not inclined to argue or dispute

Examples

Despite the heated debate, his noncontentious nature helped keep the conversation calm.
She's known in the office for her noncontentious approach to team discussions.
We need a noncontentious mediator to resolve this family dispute.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contentious - tending to argue or cause controversy

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'contentious' (from Latin 'contentiosus').
