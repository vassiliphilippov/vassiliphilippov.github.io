ani

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːni / (AmE), / ˈɑːni / (BrE)
A black tropical American bird of the cuckoo family, found in open and semi-open habitats.
a black American cuckoo bird

Examples

We spotted an ani perched on the fence during our trip to Brazil.
The ani is known for living in small flocks and its glossy black feathers.
Farmers sometimes see the ani hunting for insects in open fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuckoo - a bird of the Cuculidae family, known for its distinctive call

Etymology

From Tupi *ani*, a local name for the bird.
