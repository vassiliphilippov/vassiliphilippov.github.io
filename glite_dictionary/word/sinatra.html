Sinatra

1. PROPN

/ sɪˈnɑːtrə / (AmE), / sɪˈnɑːtrə / (BrE)
Frank Sinatra (1915–1998), an American singer and film actor known for his smooth voice and influential contributions to popular music and entertainment.
American singer and film actor (1915–1998)

Examples

Sinatra was famous for his charismatic stage presence and classic recordings such as "My Way."
Many critics regard Sinatra as one of the greatest musical artists of the 20th century.
During his career, Sinatra starred in numerous films alongside his successful music career.
