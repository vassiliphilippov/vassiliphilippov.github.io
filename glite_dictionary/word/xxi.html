XXI

1. NUM

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈwʌn / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number twenty-one (21)
Roman numeral for 21

Examples

The Super Bowl XXI was played in Pasadena in 1987.
Chapters are sometimes labeled with Roman numerals, so after chapter XX comes chapter XXI.
The clock face displayed the year as XXI to commemorate the 21st anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 21 - the Arabic numeral for twenty-one
  • Degree Lower: XX - the Roman numeral for twenty (20)
  • Degree Higher: XXII - the Roman numeral for twenty-two (22)

Etymology

From Latin numerals, where X = 10 and I = 1; XXI means 10 + 10 + 1.

2. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number twenty-one
Roman numeral for 21

Examples

The clock tower was completed in XXI century style.
She found a coin marked with the year XXI.
Chapters in the book are numbered I to XXI.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-one - the cardinal number following twenty
  • Degree Lower: xx - Roman numeral for 20
  • Degree Higher: xxii - Roman numeral for 22

Etymology

From Latin numeral system, where 'X' is 10 and 'I' is 1.
