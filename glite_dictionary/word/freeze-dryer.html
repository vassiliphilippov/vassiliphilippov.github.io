freeze-dryer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriːz ˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈfriːz ˌdraɪə / (BrE)
a machine used to preserve perishable materials by freezing them and then removing the moisture through sublimation under reduced pressure
machine for preserving materials by freeze-drying

Examples

The laboratory recently installed a new freeze-dryer to process and preserve sensitive biological samples.
With a freeze-dryer, you can extend the shelf life of fruits and vegetables for years without losing nutritional value.
After the solution solidified, the technician placed the vials in the freeze-dryer overnight.

Synset Examples

When drying in vials, the freeze-dryer is supplied with a stoppering mechanism that allows a stopper to be pressed into place, sealing the vial before it is exposed to the atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lyophilizer - machine for freeze-drying; synonym of freeze-dryer
