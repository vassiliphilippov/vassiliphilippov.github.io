hatrack

1. NOUN

/ ˈhætˌræk / (AmE), / ˈhæt.ræk / (BrE)
A tall stand or rack with hooks for hanging coats and hats, typically found in entryways or hallways.
stand for hanging coats and hats

Examples

I hung my jacket on the hatrack by the door when I came in.
The old wooden hatrack in the hallway was covered with coats and hats.
They bought a new hatrack for their office reception area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat rack - A rack for hanging coats; closely related in meaning to 'hatrack'
