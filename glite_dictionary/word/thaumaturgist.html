thaumaturgist

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔməˌtɝdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈθɔːməˌtɜːdʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices magic or sorcery; a magician or miracle worker
person who practices magic

Examples

The ancient texts describe a thaumaturgist who could summon rain at will.
Many legends feature a thaumaturgist performing feats that defy explanation.
The villagers turned to the thaumaturgist for solutions to their problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magician - a person who performs magic, especially as entertainment
  • Synonym: sorcerer - a person who claims or is believed to have magical powers

Etymology

From Greek thaumatourgos (wonder-working), from thauma (wonder) + ergon (work).
