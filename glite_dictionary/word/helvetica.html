Helvetica

1. NOUN

/ hɛlˈvɛtɪkə / (AmE), / hɛlˈvɛtɪkə / (BrE)
a widely used sans-serif typeface known for its clean, modern appearance and uniform width of strokes
a popular sans-serif typeface

Examples

The company's new logo uses the Helvetica typeface for a clean, modern look.
Most designers appreciate the versatility of Helvetica when creating branding materials.
Many signs and public transit systems around the world feature Helvetica for easy readability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Arial - a sans-serif typeface similar to Helvetica

Etymology

From Latin *Helvetica* (meaning 'Swiss'), referencing the typeface's Swiss origin.
