filature

1. NOUN

/ fɪˈleɪtʃər / (AmE), / fɪˈleɪtjʊə / (BrE)
A bobbin or device used for spinning silk into thread.
bobbin for spinning silk

Examples

The worker carefully placed the silk onto the filature before starting the spinning process.
Each filature is designed to ensure the delicate threads are spun evenly and smoothly.
During the tour, we saw an antique filature still used for demonstrating traditional silk spinning.

Etymology

From French 'filature' (spinning), from 'filer' (to spin).
