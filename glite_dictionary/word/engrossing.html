engrossing

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈɡroʊsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡrəʊsɪŋ / (BrE)
so interesting or exciting that it completely holds your attention
captivating; holding attention

Examples

The documentary was so engrossing that I lost track of time.
She read an engrossing novel on the train ride home.
His engrossing performance captivated the entire audience.

Synset Examples

a fascinating story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fascinating - extremely interesting or charming
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; dull

Etymology

From the verb 'engross', itself from Old French 'engrossier' (to put into a large account).
