teensy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtin.si / (AmE), / ˈtiːn.si / (BrE)
Very small; tiny.
very small; tiny (informal)
informal

Examples

He handed her a teensy piece of chocolate, hardly enough for one bite.
There's just a teensy chance of rain this afternoon, so don't worry.
The kitten curled up in a teensy ball on the cushion.

Synset Examples

a wee tot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - very small in size
  • Antonym: gigantic - extremely large

Etymology

Formed as an informal diminutive from 'teeny', itself derived from 'tiny'.
