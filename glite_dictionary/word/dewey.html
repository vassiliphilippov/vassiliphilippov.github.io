Dewey

1. PROPN

/ ˈduːi / (AmE), / ˈdjuːi / (BrE)
(John Dewey) United States philosopher and educational reformer known for promoting progressive education and pragmatism.
U.S. philosopher, advocate of progressive education

Examples

Dewey emphasized the importance of experience in education and learning.
Many schools today still follow principles established by Dewey.
The influence of Dewey's philosophy can be seen in modern classrooms.
