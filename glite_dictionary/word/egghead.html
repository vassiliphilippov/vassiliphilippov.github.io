egghead

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌhɛd / (BrE)
an intellectual; a person who is highly academic or studious, often with the suggestion of being out of touch with practical matters
a highly intellectual or academic person
slang

Examples

They teased him for being an egghead because he spent every evening reading science journals.
It's not just eggheads who enjoy going to museums; many people find them fascinating.
Despite being called an egghead, she was also an outstanding athlete.

Synset Examples

in spite of her love of reading she denied being an egghead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intellectual - a person possessing a highly developed intellect
  • Synonym: nerd - a person who is extremely interested in academic or intellectual pursuits
  • Antonym: practical - concerned with actual use or practice rather than theory

Etymology

Originally American slang (1940s), possibly referring to the shape of a bald or academically absorbed person's head.
