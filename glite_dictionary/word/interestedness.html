interestedness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪntərəstɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɪntərɪstɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being interested or showing interest in something
state of being interested

Examples

Her interestedness in the topic was evident from the thoughtful questions she asked.
The teacher appreciated the students' interestedness during the presentation.
A lack of interestedness can make even the most exciting subject seem dull.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interest - the feeling of wanting to know or learn about something
  • Antonym: boredom - the state of being bored

Etymology

From 'interested' + '-ness', a common noun-forming suffix indicating state or quality.
