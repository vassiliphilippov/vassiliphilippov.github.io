plutocratical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpluːtəˈkrætɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpluːtəˈkrætɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a plutocrat or plutocracy.
relating to a plutocrat/plutocracy

Examples

The country is governed by a plutocratical elite who wield enormous financial influence.
Their plutocratical policies favor the interests of the very wealthy.
Many critics argue that plutocratical systems undermine democracy and equality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plutocratic - relating to or characteristic of a plutocrat

Etymology

From 'plutocrat' (wealthy ruling person) + '-ical' (adjectival suffix), ultimately from Greek 'ploutos' (wealth).
