compartmental

1. ADJ

/ kəmˌpɑːrtˈmentl̩ / (AmE), / kəmˌpɑːtˈmentl̩ / (BrE)
divided into separate sections or categories; relating to compartments.
divided into sections or categories

Examples

The organization used a compartmental approach to streamline communication between teams.
Much of the curriculum remained compartmental, lacking integration across disciplines.
Security measures are often compartmental, with information restricted to specific groups.

Synset Examples

most sciences have become woefully compartmentalized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compartmentalized - divided into isolated sections

Etymology

From 'compartment' + '-al', modelled after words like 'segmental'.
