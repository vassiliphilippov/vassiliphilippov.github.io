contracted

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtræktɪd / (AmE), / kənˈtræktɪd / (BrE)
reduced in size or drawn together; made smaller or tighter by contraction.
made smaller or drawn together

Examples

The surgeon examined the patient's contracted pupils in response to the medication.
The contracted fabric made the shirt feel tight after washing.
Her face showed contracted features as she winced in pain.

Synset Examples

the contracted pupils of her eyes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dilated - expanded or made wider

Etymology

Past participle of 'contract', from Latin contrahere (to draw together).
