foppishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːpɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɒpɪʃnəs / (BrE)
The quality or manner of behaving or dressing in an excessively refined, vain, or affected way, especially in regard to fashion.
excessively refined or vain manner/dress

Examples

His foppishness was evident in his meticulously chosen outfits and elaborate accessories.
The critic mocked the actor's foppishness on the red carpet, calling it excessive.
Despite his foppishness, he was respected for his sharp wit and intelligence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandyism - the style or conduct of a dandy

Etymology

From 'fop' (a man excessively concerned with appearance) + '-ishness'.
