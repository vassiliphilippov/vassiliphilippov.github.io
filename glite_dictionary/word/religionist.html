religionist

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒənɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒənɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices, follows, or is devoted to religion, often with marked zeal or enthusiasm
a highly religious or zealous person

Examples

The heated debate quickly escalated when a religionist in the audience began preaching fervently.
As a dedicated religionist, she spent much of her time volunteering at her local temple.
Some view the religionist's devotion as inspiring, while others see it as excessive zeal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zealot - a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of their religious ideals

Etymology

From religion + -ist, meaning one who practices or is concerned with something.
