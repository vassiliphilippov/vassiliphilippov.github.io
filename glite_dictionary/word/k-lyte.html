K-lyte

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪ.laɪt / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.laɪt / (BrE)
A trade name for a tablet form of potassium chloride used to treat potassium deficiency.
potassium chloride medication (trade name)
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed K-lyte to correct her low potassium levels.
After starting K-lyte, he noticed his muscle cramps improved within days.
If you have a potassium deficiency, your physician may recommend K-lyte as a supplement.
