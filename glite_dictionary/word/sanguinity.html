sanguinity

1. NOUN

/ sæŋˈɡwɪnəti / (AmE), / sæŋˈɡwɪnɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being optimistic, cheerful, and confident about the future
optimistic confidence or cheerfulness

Examples

Despite recent setbacks, her sanguinity remained unshaken.
The team's sanguinity inspired confidence among investors.
He faced the upcoming challenge with remarkable sanguinity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimism - hopefulness and confidence about the future
  • Antonym: pessimism - tendency to see the worst aspect of things

Etymology

From Latin sanguinitas (healthiness, cheerfulness), from sanguineus (of blood).
