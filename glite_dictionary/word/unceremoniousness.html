unceremoniousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.səˌrɛ.məˈnoʊs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.səˌrɛ.məˈnəʊs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unceremonious; lack of formality or ceremony
lack of formality or ceremony

Examples

He appreciated the unceremoniousness of her greeting, which felt warm and sincere.
The unceremoniousness of the announcement caught everyone by surprise.
Their unceremoniousness at the event contrasted with the formal atmosphere expected.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unceremonious - not formal or ceremonious in manner

Etymology

From unceremonious + -ness.
