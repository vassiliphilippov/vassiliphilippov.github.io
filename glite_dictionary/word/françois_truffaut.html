François Truffaut

1. PROPN

/ fɹɑnˈswɑ tɹuːˈfoʊ / (AmE), / fʀɑ̃swa tʀyˈfo / (BrE)
(1932–1984) a French film director, screenwriter, and critic, regarded as a leading figure of the French New Wave cinema.
French filmmaker and critic

Examples

François Truffaut's films have had a lasting influence on world cinema.
My favorite director from the French New Wave is François Truffaut.
The works of François Truffaut are often studied in film schools.
