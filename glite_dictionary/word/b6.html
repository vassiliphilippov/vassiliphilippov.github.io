B6

1. NOUN

/ bi ˈsɪks / (AmE), / bi ˈsɪks / (BrE)
A standard paper size measuring 125 x 176 millimeters, commonly used internationally.
standard paper size (125 x 176 mm)

Examples

Please cut the sheets to B6 size for the flyers.
All invitations were printed on B6 paper to fit the envelopes.
The notebook is compact, about the size of a B6 sheet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: A6 - A standard paper size measuring 105 x 148 mm
  • Degree Higher: B5 - A standard paper size measuring 176 x 250 mm
  • Degree Lower: B7 - A standard paper size measuring 88 x 125 mm

Etymology

From the ISO 216 international paper size standard, where 'B' denotes a specific series and '6' indicates its order in the series.
