to agenize

1. VERB

/ ˈeɪdʒəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to treat flour with Agene (nitrogen trichloride) to age or bleach it
treat flour with Agene

Examples

Many mills used to agenize flour to improve its baking qualities.
The company decided to agenize their wheat flour before the process was banned.
Some bakers noticed changes in taste when flour began to be agenized.

Etymology

From Agene, trade name for nitrogen trichloride, plus -ize.
