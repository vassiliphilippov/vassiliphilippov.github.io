expanded

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspændɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændɪd / (BrE)
Having increased in extent, size, bulk, or scope; made larger or more comprehensive.
made larger or broader in extent or scope

Examples

The company launched an expanded range of products this year to meet customer demand.
The expanded edition of the book includes additional chapters and illustrations.
Owing to the new funding, the research team worked in an expanded laboratory space.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contracted - made smaller or reduced in size or scope
  • Synonym: enlarged - increased in size; made larger

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'expand', from Latin 'expandere' meaning 'to spread out'.
