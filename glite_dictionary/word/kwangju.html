Kwangju

1. PROPN

/ kwɑŋˈdʒu / (AmE), / kwæŋˈdʒu / (BrE)
A city in southwestern South Korea, known for its historical significance and as an important military base during the Korean War.
city in southwestern South Korea

Examples

The city of Kwangju played a major role in South Korea's modern history.
Many soldiers were stationed in Kwangju during the Korean War.
Today, Kwangju is also known for its vibrant art scene and cultural festivals.
