aspidistra

1. NOUN

/ ˌæspɪˈdɪstrə / (AmE), / ˌaspɪˈdɪstrə / (BrE)
an evergreen perennial plant with large glossy leaves, often grown indoors as a foliage houseplant
evergreen houseplant with large leaves

Examples

The aspidistra in the corner of the living room has thrived for years with minimal care.
He gave his grandmother an aspidistra as a housewarming gift because it is easy to maintain.
Victorian homes were often decorated with a sturdy aspidistra in the hallway.

Etymology

From modern Latin Aspidistra, from Greek 'aspidion' (small round shield), referring to the shape of the leaves.
