Aspidistra

1. NOUN

/ ˌæspɪˈdɪstrə / (AmE), / ˌaspɪˈdɪstrə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen perennial plants native to eastern Asia, often grown as houseplants for their broad, tough leaves
genus of Asian perennial plants

Examples

The Aspidistra is valued as a hardy houseplant that thrives in low light.
Several species within the genus Aspidistra are commonly found in East Asian forests.
Many Victorian homes featured an Aspidistra in the parlor as a symbol of resilience.

Etymology

From Greek 'aspidion' (small shield), referring to the shape of the leaves.
