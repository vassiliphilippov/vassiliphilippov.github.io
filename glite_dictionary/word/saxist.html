saxist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæksɪst / (AmE), / ˈsæksɪst / (BrE)
a musician who plays the saxophone
musician who plays the saxophone

Examples

The talented saxist captivated the audience with his soulful solos during the concert.
She is not just a composer but also an accomplished saxist in the jazz band.
As a young saxist, he practiced every day to master the instrument.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saxophonist - a person who plays the saxophone

Etymology

Formed from 'sax' (short for 'saxophone') + '-ist' (a person who practices or is concerned with something).
