unrealised

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈriəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a person or their abilities) not having achieved or reached their full potential or capabilities.
not having reached potential; unachieved
british

Examples

Many talented athletes remain unrealised because they never get the chance to compete at a high level.
Her unrealised potential troubled her parents, who believed she could achieve great things.
The team's unrealised ambitions were a constant source of frustration for the coach.

Synset Examples

unfulfilled and uneasy men
unrealized dreams and ambitions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unfulfilled - not satisfied or having failed to realize potential
  • Antonym: realised - having achieved or brought to fruition
