exhausting

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɑstɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel extremely tired; causing exhaustion.
making you extremely tired

Examples

The hike up the mountain was absolutely exhausting for everyone involved.
She found working two jobs at once to be completely exhausting.
After an exhausting day, all he wanted to do was sleep.

Synset Examples

an exhausting job in the hot sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiring - making you feel tired
  • Antonym: refreshing - making you feel less tired or more energetic

2. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɔstɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing someone to become extremely tired; very tiring.
very tiring; causing exhaustion

Examples

The long hike through the mountains was absolutely exhausting.
After an exhausting day at work, she just wanted to relax at home.
Running a marathon can be both mentally and physically exhausting.

Synset Examples

an exhausting march
the visit was especially wearing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiring - making someone feel tired
  • Antonym: refreshing - making someone feel more energetic

Etymology

From the verb 'exhaust', from Latin 'exhaurire' (to drain out).
