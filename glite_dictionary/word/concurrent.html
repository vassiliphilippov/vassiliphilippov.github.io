concurrent

1. ADJ

/ kənˈkɝːənt / (AmE), / kənˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
existing, happening, or done at the same time as something else.
happening at the same time

Examples

Three concurrent meetings were scheduled for Monday morning.
The artist held concurrent exhibitions in Paris and New York.
The project experienced concurrent delays due to supply issues and staffing shortages.

Synset Examples

a series of coincident events

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneous - existing, occurring, or done at the same time

Etymology

From Latin 'concurrere' meaning 'to run together'.
