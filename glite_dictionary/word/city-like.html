city-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪt̬i laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪti laɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics resembling those of a city; urban in nature.
resembling a city; urban-like

Examples

Their apartment building offers a city-like atmosphere in the heart of the suburbs.
The new shopping complex has a distinctly city-like feel despite being in a rural area.
Efficient public transport gives the town a much more city-like character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urban - relating to a city or town
