manageress

1. NOUN

/ ˌmænɪdʒərˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌmanɪdʒəˈrɛs / (BrE)
a woman who manages a business, team, or organization, especially in settings where gender distinction is emphasized
female manager
archaic

Examples

The new manageress quickly improved the restaurant’s reputation for service.
Many hotels in the early 20th century advertised for a competent manageress.
She was appointed as the first female manageress of the club.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manager - a person who manages an organization or business

Etymology

Formed by adding the feminine suffix -ess to 'manager'.
