data

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə, ˈdætə / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə, ˈdɑːtə / (BrE)
Facts and statistics collected together for reference or analysis.
facts or statistics for analysis

Examples

The researchers collected data from hundreds of participants over several years.
Good decisions require accurate data to support them.
Large companies use customer data to improve their products and services.

Synset Examples

statistical data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: information - knowledge or facts learned, especially from investigation

Etymology

From Latin 'data', plural of 'datum' (something given).
