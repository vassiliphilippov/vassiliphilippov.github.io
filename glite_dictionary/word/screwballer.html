screwballer

1. NOUN

/ ˈskruːˌbɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈskruːˌbɔːlə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a pitcher who throws screwballs as a primary pitch.
pitcher who throws screwballs

Examples

The team's best screwballer baffled hitters with his unique pitching style.
As a screwballer, she often kept batters off balance throughout the game.
Although not many use the technique today, a skilled screwballer can still be very effective.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher - the player who throws the ball to the batter in baseball
