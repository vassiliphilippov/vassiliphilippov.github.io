violet-colored

1. ADJ

/ ˈvaɪələt ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪələt ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having the color of violets; purplish-blue or purple in hue.
of a violet color

Examples

The artist chose a violet-colored fabric for the curtain, adding a touch of elegance to the room.
A field of violet-colored flowers stretched as far as the eye could see.
Her dress was a stunning, violet-colored gown that caught everyone's attention at the gala.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violet - purplish-blue color
