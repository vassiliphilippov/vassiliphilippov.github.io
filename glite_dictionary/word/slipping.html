slipping

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
The action or process of moving smoothly or accidentally across a surface, typically losing balance or control due to slipperiness.
moving on a slippery surface

Examples

He tried to cross the frozen pond without slipping, but it was impossible.
Despite her best efforts, slipping on the wet kitchen floor left her with a bruised knee.
The children enjoyed the feeling of slipping down the icy hill on their sleds.

Synset Examples

his slipping and slithering progress over the ice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sliding - moving smoothly along a surface while maintaining continuous contact
  • Synonym: skidding - slipping sideways or forwards, especially of vehicles, on a slippery surface
