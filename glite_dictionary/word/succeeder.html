succeeder

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsiːdər / (AmE), / səkˈsiːdə / (BrE)
a person who achieves success or consistently accomplishes goals
a person who is successful

Examples

The company rewards every succeeder with generous bonuses at the end of the year.
It takes determination and resilience to become a consistent succeeder in this competitive field.
She admired her brother for being a true succeeder in both business and life.

Synset Examples

his son would never be the achiever that his father was
only winners need apply
if you want to be a success, you have to dress like a success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achiever - a person who achieves a lot or is successful
  • Synonym: winner - a person who wins; a successful person
  • Antonym: failure - a person who fails to succeed

Etymology

Formed from 'succeed' + '-er,' denoting a person who succeeds.
