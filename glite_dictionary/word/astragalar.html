astragalar

1. ADJ

/ əˈstræɡələr / (AmE), / əˈstræɡələ / (BrE)
relating to the astragalus (the anklebone) or to the ankle region.
relating to the anklebone (astragalus)
medical

Examples

A fracture involving the astragalar surface may impair ankle movement.
Early reptile species show unique astragalar characteristics in their skeletons.
The surgeon noted damage to the astragalar articulation during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astragalus - the anklebone; the largest bone in the ankle joint

Etymology

From astragalus, from Greek 'astragalos' (anklebone) + -ar (adjectival suffix).
