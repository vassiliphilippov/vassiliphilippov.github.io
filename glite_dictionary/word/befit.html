to befit

1. VERB

/ bɪˈfɪt / (AmE), / bɪˈfɪt / (BrE)
to be suitable or appropriate for someone or something; to befit means to be worthy of or in keeping with.
to be suitable for; to suit

Examples

Her elegant dress truly befits the occasion.
Such language does not befit a person of your position.
His calm demeanor befits someone in a leadership role.

Synset Examples

This kind of behavior does not suit a young woman!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suit - to be right or appropriate for someone or something
  • Synonym: to become - to be appropriate or suitable to; to befit
  • Antonym: ill-suit - to not be appropriate or fitting

Etymology

From Old English 'befyttan' (be-' + 'fitten', to fit).
