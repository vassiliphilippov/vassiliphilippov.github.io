I Samuel

1. PROPN

/ ˈfaɪrst ˈsæmjuːl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈsæmjuːəl / (BrE)
the first of two books in the Old Testament of the Bible that recount the stories of Saul and David
first Old Testament book about Saul and David

Examples

Many important events in Jewish history are found in I Samuel.
She taught a lesson centered on the story of David in I Samuel.
Next week, our Bible study group will begin reading I Samuel.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: II Samuel - the second book in the Old Testament about Saul and David

Etymology

From Latin 'Liber Samuelis', meaning 'Book of Samuel', after the Hebrew prophet Samuel.
