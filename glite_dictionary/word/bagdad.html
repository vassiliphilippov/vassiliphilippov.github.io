Bagdad

1. PROPN

/ bæɡˈdæd / (AmE), / bæɡˈdæd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Iraq, situated on the Tigris River
capital city of Iraq

Examples

After the war, reconstruction efforts in Bagdad continued for many years.
His family moved from Bagdad to Europe when he was a child.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Arabic بغداد (Baghdād), originally from Persian, with various suggested meanings.
