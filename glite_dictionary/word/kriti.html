Kriti

1. PROPN

/ ˈkriːti / (AmE), / ˈkriːti / (BrE)
the Greek name for Crete, the largest island of Greece in the Mediterranean, notable as the site of the ancient Minoan civilization.
Greek name for the island of Crete

Examples

The ruins of Knossos on Kriti attract thousands of tourists each year.
Many myths of ancient Greece are set on the island of Kriti.
She spent her holidays exploring the beaches and mountains of Kriti.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Crete - the English name for the island known as Kriti in Greek

Etymology

Greek Κρήτη (Kríti), the modern Greek name for the island known in English as Crete.
