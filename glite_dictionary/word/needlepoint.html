needlepoint

1. NOUN

/ ˈniː.dl̩.pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈniː.dl.pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a type of embroidery in which designs are stitched with yarn on a canvas, creating a textured pattern similar to tapestry
type of embroidery using canvas and yarn

Examples

She spent her evenings working on needlepoint projects for her friends and family.
The museum displayed an exquisite collection of antique needlepoint from the 19th century.
Learning needlepoint can be relaxing and allows you to create beautiful home décor items.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - the craft of decorating fabric or other materials with needle and thread or yarn

2. NOUN

/ ˈniː.dl̩ˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈniː.dlˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a type of lace or embroidery made by hand with a needle using a buttonhole stitch on a canvas or paper pattern
decorative hand-stitched embroidery or lace

Examples

Her grandmother taught her how to do needlepoint using traditional patterns.
The walls were decorated with beautiful needlepoint samplers framed in wood.
He spent hours working on a needlepoint cushion for his mother's birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embroidery - the art of decorating fabric with needle and thread or yarn
  • Synonym: lace - a delicate fabric made of yarn or thread in an open weblike pattern

Etymology

From 'needle' + 'point,' referring to the pointed needle used for hand stitching.
