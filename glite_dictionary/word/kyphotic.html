kyphotic

1. ADJ

/ kaɪˈfɑːtɪk / (AmE), / kaɪˈfɒtɪk / (BrE)
Having or relating to an excessive outward curvature of the spine, typically referring to kyphosis.
relating to excessive spinal curvature (kyphosis)
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited a kyphotic posture due to a deformity in the thoracic spine.
Prolonged poor posture can result in a kyphotic spinal curve.
The doctor noted that her back appeared kyphotic on examination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoliotic - relating to or affected by scoliosis, a lateral curvature of the spine

Etymology

From Greek kyphos (hump) + -otic (relating to a condition)
