Anagallis tenella

1. PROPN

/ ˌænəˈɡælɪs təˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌanəˈɡalɪs tɛˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A small creeping European herb with delicate pink flowers, commonly known as bog pimpernel.
a small European herb with pink flowers

Examples

The moist meadows were dotted with the delicate pink flowers of Anagallis tenella.
Botanists carefully surveyed the wetland to record populations of Anagallis tenella.
She identified the plant as Anagallis tenella by its creeping habit and pale blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bog pimpernel - common name for Anagallis tenella, a small herb with pink flowers

Etymology

From Latin 'Anagallis' (a plant genus) and 'tenella' (delicate).
