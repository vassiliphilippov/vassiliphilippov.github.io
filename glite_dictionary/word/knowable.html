knowable

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Able to be known or understood.
capable of being known

Examples

Whether the true motives behind the decision are knowable remains a matter of debate.
Scientists strive to make the workings of the universe more knowable through research and experimentation.
Some mysteries may never be knowable, no matter how much we investigate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unknowable - impossible to know or understand

Etymology

From late Latin 'knowabilis', from Latin 'noscere' (to know).
