Iguassu

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈɡwɑsu / (AmE), / ɪˈɡwɑːsuː / (BrE)
A large waterfall on the border between Argentina and Brazil, renowned for its impressive size and beauty.
a large waterfall between Argentina and Brazil

Examples

Many tourists visit Iguassu every year to see its breathtaking waterfalls.
The Iguassu Falls are shared by both Argentina and Brazil, forming a natural border.
She included a trip to Iguassu in her South American itinerary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Niagara Falls - a major waterfall on the border of Canada and the United States
  • Synonym: Victoria Falls - a major waterfall on the border of Zambia and Zimbabwe

Etymology

From Guarani 'ygûasu' (big water).
