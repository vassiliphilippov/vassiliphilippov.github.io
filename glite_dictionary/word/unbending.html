unbending

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
unwilling or unable to change, adapt, or compromise; firm and inflexible in attitude or character
firm, inflexible; unwilling to change

Examples

Despite criticism, she remained unbending in her views and refused to compromise.
His unbending attitude made negotiations almost impossible for the team.
The judge was known for her unbending commitment to fairness and the law.

Synset Examples

a rigid disciplinarian
an inflexible law
an unbending will to dominate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inflexible - unwilling to change or adapt; rigid
  • Synonym: rigid - not willing to change opinions or behavior; stiff in character
  • Antonym: flexible - willing to change or compromise; adaptable

Etymology

From un- (not) + bend (to curve, yield) + -ing.
