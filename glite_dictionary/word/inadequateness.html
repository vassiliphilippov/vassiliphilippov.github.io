inadequateness

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈædɪkwətnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈadɪkwətnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being inadequate; lack of sufficiency, suitability, or competence
the state of being inadequate

Examples

The inadequateness of the facilities became clear during the crowded event.
Many critics pointed out the inadequateness of the proposed solution.
She was frustrated by the inadequateness of the available resources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inadequacy - the quality or state of being inadequate

Etymology

From inadequate + -ness, formed after the pattern of English abstract nouns.
