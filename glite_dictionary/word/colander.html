colander

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑləndər / (AmE), / ˈkɒləndə / (BrE)
A bowl-shaped kitchen utensil with holes for draining water from foods such as pasta or washing vegetables.
bowl-shaped strainer for draining food

Examples

After boiling the pasta, she poured it into the colander to drain the water.
He rinsed the strawberries in a colander under cold running water.
A metal colander works best for washing leafy greens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strainer - a kitchen utensil for separating liquid from solid foods

Etymology

From Old French *colandre*, from Latin *colum* (sieve).
