protip

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.tɪp / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tɪp / (BrE)
an especially useful or clever piece of advice, often offered informally or online
a clever or useful piece of advice
slang

Examples

Here’s a protip: save your work regularly to avoid losing progress.
One protip for cooking steak is to let it rest before slicing.
As a protip, use keyboard shortcuts to work more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tip - a useful piece of advice or suggestion

Etymology

Blend of 'pro' (professional) and 'tip' (advice), originating in internet slang.
