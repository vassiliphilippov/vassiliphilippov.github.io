eleemosynary

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛlɪˈmɑːsəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪˈmɒsɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to or dependent on charity, especially in providing assistance to the poor; charitable.
relating to charity or charitable giving

Examples

The foundation has established several eleemosynary programs to assist disadvantaged communities.
The old hospital relied on eleemosynary donations for much of its funding.
He devoted his life to eleemosynary work, helping the poor and homeless.

Synset Examples

a benevolent contributor
eleemosynary relief
philanthropic contributions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: philanthropic - seeking to promote the welfare of others, especially through charitable acts
  • Synonym: charitable - generous in donating to those in need

Etymology

From Medieval Latin eleemosynarius (relating to alms), from Greek eleēmosynē (pity, charity).
