nonspatial

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈspeɪʃəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈspeɪʃəl / (BrE)
not relating to or having the characteristics of space; not spatial.
not relating to space

Examples

The mathematician proposed a nonspatial model to describe the phenomenon.
Consciousness may involve nonspatial properties beyond physical dimensions.
Music is often analyzed in terms of time, a primarily nonspatial dimension.

Synset Examples

a nonspatial continuum

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spatial - relating to or involving space

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'spatial,' from Latin 'spatium' (space).
