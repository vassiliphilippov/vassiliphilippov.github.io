elephantine

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛl.ɪˈfæn.tin / (AmE), / ˌɛl.ɪˈfæn.taɪn / (BrE)
very large, heavy, and clumsy, like an elephant; huge and bulky.
huge and bulky; like an elephant

Examples

The elephantine statue dominated the entrance to the museum, impressing every visitor.
She moved with elephantine grace, careful but far from delicate.
They struggled to move the elephantine sofa through the narrow doorway.

Synset Examples

a jumbo jet
jumbo shrimp

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gigantic - very large; enormous
  • Antonym: tiny - very small; minuscule

Etymology

From Latin elephantinus (of an elephant), from elephantus (elephant).
