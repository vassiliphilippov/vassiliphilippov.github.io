PR

1. PROPN

/ pi ɑr / (AmE), / piː ɑː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for Puerto Rico, a self-governing commonwealth associated with the United States in the Caribbean.
abbreviation for Puerto Rico
american

Examples

I'm flying to PR next week for vacation.
Many U.S. citizens live in PR.
Hurricane Maria caused severe damage throughout PR.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puerto Rico - a self-governing commonwealth of the US in the Caribbean

Etymology

Abbreviation of Puerto Rico, from Spanish.

2. NOUN

/ ˌpiːˈɑːr / (AmE), / ˌpiːˈɑː / (BrE)
the activity or process of promoting a person, organization, or product to create goodwill and manage public perception; public relations
promotion to create goodwill; public relations

Examples

The company hired a new PR firm to improve its public image.
After the scandal, they launched a PR campaign to regain customer trust.
Good PR can make a significant difference to a brand's reputation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public relations - the practice of managing the spread of information between an individual or organization and the public

Etymology

Abbreviation of public relations.
