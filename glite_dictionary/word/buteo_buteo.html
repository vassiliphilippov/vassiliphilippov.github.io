Buteo buteo

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːti.oʊ ˈbjuːti.oʊ / (AmE), / ˌbjuːtɪəʊ ˈbjuːtɪəʊ / (BrE)
The common buzzard, a medium-sized bird of prey native to Europe and parts of Asia, known for its broad wings and rounded tail.
the common buzzard, a European bird of prey

Examples

Many birdwatchers were excited to spot a Buteo buteo circling above the meadow.
The Buteo buteo is often seen gliding over open countryside in search of small mammals.
Conservation efforts have helped increase the population of Buteo buteo in several regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buzzard - a medium-sized hawk, especially Buteo buteo

Etymology

From Latin, where 'Buteo' designates a type of hawk or buzzard, used in scientific taxonomy.
