mensch

1. NOUN

/ mɛnʃ / (AmE), / mɛnʃ / (BrE)
A person of integrity and honor; someone considered decent, responsible, and admirable.
person of integrity and honor

Examples

Everyone at the company agrees that John is a real mensch who always looks out for others.
She proved herself a mensch by supporting her friend through a difficult time.
It's hard to find a mensch these days who truly puts others first.

Etymology

From Yiddish מענטש (mentsh), from German Mensch (human being, person).
