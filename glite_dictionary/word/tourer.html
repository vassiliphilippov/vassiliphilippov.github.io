tourer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrər / (AmE), / ˈtʊərə / (BrE)
a person who travels for pleasure, especially on holiday or vacation
person traveling for pleasure

Examples

As a keen tourer, she visited more than twenty countries last year.
The hotel was popular among tourers exploring the countryside by bicycle.
He considers himself an experienced tourer after spending years traveling around Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure, usually visiting different places

Etymology

Formed from 'tour' + agent suffix '-er'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrər / (AmE), / ˈtʊərə / (BrE)
A large open car seating four people, typically with a folding top; a touring car.
large open car with folding top
british

Examples

We drove the old tourer along the coastal road, enjoying the sea breeze.
His grandfather restored a classic tourer from the 1920s, complete with leather seats.
With the top folded down, the tourer was perfect for summer countryside drives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convertible - a car with a roof that can be removed or folded down

Etymology

From 'tour', referring to traveling for pleasure, + '-er'.
