Mn

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ˈɛn / (AmE), / ɛm ˈɛn / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for manganese, a hard, brittle, grey metallic element used in steelmaking and found in many minerals
chemical symbol for manganese

Examples

The chemical symbol Mn represents the element manganese on the periodic table.
A trace amount of Mn is essential in the human diet for proper metabolic function.
Steel alloys containing Mn are known for increased strength and durability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manganese - a hard, brittle, grey metallic element used to make steel

Etymology

From the first two letters of 'manganese.' Chemical symbols are standardized by the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC).
