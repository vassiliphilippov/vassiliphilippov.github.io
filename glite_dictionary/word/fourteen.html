fourteen

1. NUM

/ ˌfɔrˈtin / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈtiːn / (BrE)
the number 14; one more than thirteen
the number 14

Examples

There are fourteen players on the team roster this season.
She celebrated her fourteenth birthday with all her friends.
Only fourteen tickets remain for tonight's theater performance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirteen - the number 13; one less than fourteen
  • Degree Higher: fifteen - the number 15; one more than fourteen

Etymology

From Old English 'feowertīene', from 'feower' (four) + 'tīene' (ten).
