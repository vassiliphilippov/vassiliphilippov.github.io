extragalactic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstrəɡəˈlæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəɡəˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
located outside or beyond a galaxy, especially the Milky Way.
outside a galaxy

Examples

The Hubble Space Telescope has captured images of extragalactic star clusters.
Scientists are studying extragalactic radio sources to learn more about the universe.
A team of astronomers discovered a new extragalactic supernova last year.

Synset Examples

extragalactic nebula

Etymology

From extra- (outside, beyond) + galactic (of a galaxy)
