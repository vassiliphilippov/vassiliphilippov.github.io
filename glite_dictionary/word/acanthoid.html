acanthoid

1. ADJ

/ əˈkænˌθɔɪd / (AmE), / əˈkanθɔɪd / (BrE)
shaped like a spine or thorn
spine- or thorn-shaped

Examples

The plant's acanthoid leaves deterred most animals from eating it.
Marine organisms with acanthoid structures are adapted to protect themselves from predators.
The fossil showed an acanthoid projection resembling a small thorn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiny - covered with or having spines; thorny
  • Synonym: thorny - having many thorns or difficult to deal with

Etymology

From Greek akantha (thorn, spine) + -oid (resembling).
