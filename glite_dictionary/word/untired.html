untired

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
not tired; having full or unreduced energy or strength
not tired; full of energy

Examples

Despite the long journey, she felt untired and ready to explore the city.
The children remained untired even after hours of playing in the park.
His untired enthusiasm for his work inspired everyone around him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tired - feeling a need to rest or sleep
  • Synonym: rested - feeling refreshed after rest

Etymology

un- (not) + tired (wearied or fatigued)
