milkwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to deliver milk to people's homes
woman who delivers milk

Examples

In the early mornings, the milkwoman would leave fresh bottles on every doorstep.
The children greeted the milkwoman as she pushed her cart down the street.
Before supermarkets became common, a milkwoman was a familiar figure in many neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milkman - a man whose job is to deliver milk

Etymology

Formed from 'milk' + 'woman', modeled after 'milkman'.
