opportunity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpərˈtuːnəti / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
a favorable time or situation that makes it possible to do something
favorable chance or occasion

Examples

She saw the job opening as a great opportunity to advance her career.
We should not miss the opportunity to learn from our mistakes.
The new policy gives everyone equal opportunity to participate.

Synset Examples

the holiday gave us the opportunity to visit Washington
now is your chance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chance - a possibility of something happening
  • Synonym: possibility - a thing that may happen or be the case
  • Antonym: risk - a situation involving exposure to danger

Etymology

From Latin opportunitas, from opportunus (favorable, advantageous).
