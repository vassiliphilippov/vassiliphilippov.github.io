coccoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑː.kɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.ɔɪd / (BrE)
shaped like a coccus; spherical, especially in reference to microorganisms.
spherical in shape, like a coccus
medical

Examples

The bacteria isolated from the sample were primarily coccoid in appearance.
Several coccoid species are known to cause infections in humans.
Under the microscope, the structure revealed a cluster of coccoid cells.

Synset Examples

a coccoid microorganism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coccus - a spherical bacterium

Etymology

From Greek 'kokkos' (berry, seed) + '-oid' (like, resembling).
