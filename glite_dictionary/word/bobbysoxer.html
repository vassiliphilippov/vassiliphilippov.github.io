bobbysoxer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑbiˌsɑksər / (AmE), / ˈbɒbiˌsɒksə / (BrE)
A teenage girl, especially in the 1940s and 1950s, who wore bobby socks and often followed popular music and fashion trends.
teenage girl of the 1940s-50s wearing bobby socks
archaic

Examples

The radio station was popular among bobbysoxers in the early 1950s.
Many bobbysoxers idolized Frank Sinatra during his rise to fame.
Her grandmother once showed her photos of herself as a bobbysoxer at a school dance.

Etymology

Formed from 'bobby sock' + '-er', referring to the fad of wearing ankle-high socks among teenage girls.
