meshuggener

1. NOUN

/ məˈʃʊɡənər / (AmE), / məˈʃʊɡənə / (BrE)
A senseless or crazy person, often used humorously or affectionately.
crazy or senseless person
informal

Examples

He’s acting like a total meshuggener chasing that cat up the tree.
My grandmother always said my uncle was a bit of a meshuggener after his wild ideas.
Don’t listen to that meshuggener; he’s always telling outrageous stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - A person who is mentally ill or wildly foolish
  • Synonym: madman - A man who is insane or behaves in a wildly foolish way

Etymology

From Yiddish משוגענער (meshuge-ner), from Hebrew מְשֻׁגָּע (meshuggá, 'crazy').

2. NOUN

/ məˈʃʊɡənər / (AmE), / məˈʃʊɡənə / (BrE)
(chiefly slang) A crazy or foolish person.
crazy or foolish person (slang)
slang american

Examples

Don't mind Sam—he's just a meshuggener, always coming up with wild ideas.
Only a meshuggener would try to fix the roof during a thunderstorm.
She called her brother a meshuggener for buying a car without seeing it first.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madman - a person who behaves in a wildly foolish or dangerous manner
  • Synonym: lunatic - a mentally ill person (not in technical use) or a mentally unstable or crazed person
  • Antonym: sane person - a person who is mentally healthy or sensible

Etymology

From Yiddish מְשׁוּגֶענֵער (meshuggener), meaning ‘crazy person’.
