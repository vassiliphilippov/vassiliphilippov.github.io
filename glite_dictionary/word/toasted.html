toasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtoʊ.stɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.stɪd / (BrE)
(of food) browned, especially on the surface, by being exposed to heat.
browned by exposure to heat

Examples

She made a toasted cheese sandwich for lunch.
The toasted bread smelled delicious when it came out of the oven.
Marshmallows are often toasted over an open fire when camping.

Synset Examples

he liked toasted marshmallows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled - (of food) cooked by direct exposure to heat
  • Synonym: roasted - (of food) cooked by prolonged exposure to heat in an oven or over a fire
