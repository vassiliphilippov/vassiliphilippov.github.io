spangly

1. ADJ

/ ˈspæŋɡli / (AmE), / ˈspaŋɡli / (BrE)
Covered with shiny beads, sequins, or jewels that reflect light; glittering or sparkling in appearance.
covered with glittering sequins or beads

Examples

She wore a spangly dress to the party that caught everyone's eye.
The stage costumes were all bright and spangly under the lights.
His jacket was so spangly that it shimmered with every movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkly - shining with flashes of light; glittery

Etymology

From 'spangle' + '-y', with 'spangle' from Middle English referring to a small sparkling object.
