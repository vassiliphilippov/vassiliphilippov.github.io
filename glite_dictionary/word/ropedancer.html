ropedancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊpˌdænsər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊpˌdɑːnsə / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs balancing acts on a rope stretched above the ground, typically as an entertainer
acrobat performing on a rope

Examples

The ropedancer captivated the audience with her daring stunts high above the stage.
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a ropedancer in the circus.
Every ropedancer spends years practicing balance and poise before performing publicly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tightrope walker - a person who walks or balances on a tightrope

Etymology

From 'rope' + 'dancer', indicating a person who dances or performs on a rope.
