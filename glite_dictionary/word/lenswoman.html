lenswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛnzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɛnzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who takes photographs professionally.
female professional photographer

Examples

The renowned lenswoman captured striking portraits of local residents for the magazine.
As a pioneering lenswoman, she was often the only woman covering major sporting events.
This exhibition celebrates the works of talented lenswomen from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lensman - a man who takes photographs professionally

Etymology

Patterned after 'lensman,' from 'lens' (the optical device) and 'woman.'
