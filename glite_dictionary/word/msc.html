MSc

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm ˌɛs ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌɛm ˌɛs ˈsiː / (BrE)
an advanced university degree in a science or technical subject; Master of Science.
Master of Science degree

Examples

She completed her MSc in biology at a prestigious university.
After earning his MSc, he began working in pharmaceutical research.
The department offers both a MSc and a PhD program in computer science.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: PhD - doctorate degree (Doctor of Philosophy)
  • Degree Lower: BSc - bachelor's degree in science (Bachelor of Science)

Etymology

Abbreviation of Master of Science.
