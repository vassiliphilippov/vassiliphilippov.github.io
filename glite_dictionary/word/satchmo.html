Satchmo

1. PROPN

/ ˈsætʃˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsætʃməʊ / (BrE)
the nickname of Louis Armstrong, a pioneering American jazz trumpeter and bandleader (1900–1971)
nickname of jazz musician Louis Armstrong

Examples

Many fans consider Satchmo the greatest jazz trumpeter of all time.
The influence of Satchmo on modern music cannot be overstated.
A photograph of Satchmo graced the cover of the magazine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Louis Armstrong - American jazz trumpeter and singer (1901–1971) known as Satchmo

Etymology

Nickname for Louis Armstrong, possibly from 'Satchel Mouth' referring to his wide grin.
