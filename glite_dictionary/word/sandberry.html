sandberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsændˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen, mat-forming shrub native to North America and northern Eurasia, which bears small white flowers and red berries; its leaves turn red in autumn
evergreen shrub with red berries

Examples

The sandberry provides food for wildlife in many northern forests.
In autumn, the leaves of the sandberry turn a striking shade of red.
We found a patch of sandberry next to the hiking trail, covered in tiny red berries.
