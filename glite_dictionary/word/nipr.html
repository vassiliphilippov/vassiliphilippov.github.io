NIPR

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.aɪ.piːˈɑːr / (AmE), / ˈɛn.aɪ.piːˈɑː / (BrE)
A clandestine group of leftist extremists in Italy that opposed labor policies and foreign policy; responsible for a series of bombings in Rome from 2000 to 2002.
Italian leftist extremist group (2000–2002)

Examples

The Italian authorities intensified their investigation into the activities of the NIPR after a string of bombings.
Few public details were available about the elusive members of NIPR during their campaign in Rome.
Historians still debate the true motivation behind the NIPR's attacks in the early 2000s.
