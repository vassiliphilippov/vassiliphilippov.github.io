nauseating

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔziˌeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːziˌeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a feeling of nausea; making someone feel sick or disgusted.
causing nausea or disgust

Examples

The nauseating stench from the garbage made me cover my nose immediately.
His nauseating behavior at the dinner table ruined everyone’s appetite.
She found the sight of the spoiled food absolutely nauseating.

Synset Examples

a nauseating smell
nauseous offal
a sickening stench

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nauseous - causing nausea or feeling sick
  • Synonym: sickening - causing feelings of sickness or disgust

Etymology

From English 'nauseate' + '-ing'; 'nauseate' from Latin 'nauseare' (to feel seasick).
