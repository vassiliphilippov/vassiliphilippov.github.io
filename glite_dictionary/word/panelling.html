panelling

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpænəlɪŋ / (BrE)
a panel or a series of panels fitted together, especially as a lining for walls or doors
panelled lining for walls or doors

Examples

The room's wooden panelling gave it a warm, classic feel.
The old library has ornate panelling along all four walls.
Victorian houses often feature decorative panelling on their doors and hallways.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: panel - a flat, typically rectangular piece forming part of a surface

Etymology

From 'panel' + '-ing', signifying the process or result of applying panels.
