percent

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
one part in every hundred; a proportion or rate per hundred
one part in every hundred

Examples

Only five percent of the students passed the exam this year.
The interest rate was reduced by two percent last month.
A service charge of ten percent will be added to your bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percentage - a rate, number, or amount in each hundred; more general

Etymology

From Latin 'per centum' meaning 'by the hundred'.
