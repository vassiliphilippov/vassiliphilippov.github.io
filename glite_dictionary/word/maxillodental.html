maxillodental

1. ADJ

/ ˌmæksɪloʊˈdɛntl̩ / (AmE), / ˌmaksɪləʊˈdɛntl̩ / (BrE)
Relating to the upper jaw and its associated teeth.
of the upper jaw and its teeth
medical

Examples

The dentist examined the patient's maxillodental structures to assess the alignment of the upper teeth.
Certain speech sounds are produced using maxillodental contact.
An X-ray revealed an abnormality in the maxillodental region.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mandibulodental - relating to the lower jaw and its associated teeth

Etymology

From Latin 'maxilla' (jaw) and 'dentalis' (of the teeth).
