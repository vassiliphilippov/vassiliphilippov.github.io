potoroo

1. NOUN

/ ˌpoʊtəˈru / (AmE), / ˌpɒtəˈruː / (BrE)
A small marsupial native to Australia, resembling a rat kangaroo, belonging to the genus Potorous.
small Australian marsupial

Examples

The long-nosed potoroo is now considered a vulnerable species due to habitat loss.
We spotted a potoroo foraging quietly in the undergrowth at dusk.
Conservationists are working to protect the remaining wild potoroos in southern Australia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bettong - a small Australian marsupial similar to a potoroo

Etymology

From Dharug (an Australian Aboriginal language) 'badurru', the local name for the animal.
