craft

1. NOUN

/ kræft / (AmE), / krɑːft / (BrE)
a skilled activity or occupation that involves making things by hand, often requiring special training or ability
skilled manual occupation or trade

Examples

After years of training, she finally mastered her craft as a jeweler.
The exhibition celebrates the traditional craft of pottery making.
He takes great pride in the craft he learned from his father.

Synset Examples

he learned his trade as an apprentice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade - a skilled job, typically one requiring manual skill and special training
  • Synonym: art - skill acquired by experience, study, or observation; craftsmanship

Etymology

Old English *cræft* (strength, skill, art).
