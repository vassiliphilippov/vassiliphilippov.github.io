to rewatch

1. VERB

/ riˈwɑtʃ / (AmE), / riˈwɒtʃ / (BrE)
to watch (a film, television program, or video) again
watch again (a film, show, etc.)

Examples

Every year, I like to rewatch my favorite holiday movies.
She decided to rewatch the first season before starting the new episodes.
We used the streaming service to rewatch the documentary series from the beginning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - to look at or observe attentively

Etymology

From re- (again) + watch.
