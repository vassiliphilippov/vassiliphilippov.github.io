Trandate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrænˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtræn.deɪt / (BrE)
A trade name for labetalol, an antihypertensive drug that blocks alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors to lower blood pressure.
trade name for a blood pressure medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Trandate to manage her high blood pressure.
Patients taking Trandate should monitor their blood pressure regularly.
He experienced dizziness as a side effect of Trandate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: labetalol - an antihypertensive drug blocking alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors
