orangeade

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔr.ɪnˈdʒeɪd / (AmE), / ˌɒr.ɪnˈdʒeɪd / (BrE)
a sweetened drink made from diluted orange juice, often served chilled
sweetened orange-flavored drink

Examples

Would you like a glass of orangeade with your lunch?
The picnic hamper included sandwiches, fruit, and a pitcher of chilled orangeade.
She prepared homemade orangeade for the children's party, adding slices of fresh orange.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemonade - a sweetened drink made from lemon juice and water

Etymology

From 'orange' + '-ade' (as in lemonade), denoting a citrus-flavored beverage.
