dieting

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪ.ɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə.tɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of restricting the amount or type of food eaten, especially to lose weight or improve health.
restricting food intake for health or weight loss

Examples

She has been dieting for several months to prepare for the marathon.
Many people start dieting after the holidays to shed extra weight.
He finds dieting challenging, especially when dining out with friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fasting - abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink

Etymology

From the verb 'diet', meaning 'to regulate food intake'.
