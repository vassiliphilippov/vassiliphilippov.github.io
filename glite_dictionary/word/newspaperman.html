newspaperman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəmæn / (BrE)
a male journalist who writes or edits news stories for newspapers or other media
male newspaper journalist

Examples

My grandfather was a respected newspaperman who covered politics for decades.
The newspaperman rushed to report the breaking story before his deadline.
As a young newspaperman, he dreamed of uncovering major scandals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports news, especially for newspapers or broadcasts
  • Antonym: newspaperwoman - a female newspaper journalist

Etymology

Formed from 'newspaper' + 'man', in use since the late 19th century.
