prohibited

1. ADJ

/ prəˈhɪbɪtɪd / (AmE), / prəˈhɪbɪtɪd / (BrE)
forbidden by law or by an authority
forbidden by law or authority

Examples

Smoking is strictly prohibited in all public buildings.
The sale of alcohol is prohibited to anyone under the age of 18.
Some items are prohibited from being brought into the country.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permitted - allowed by law or by an authority
  • Synonym: illegal - against the law
  • Synonym: forbidden - not allowed by rule or law

Etymology

From Latin 'prohibitus', past participle of 'prohibere' (to forbid, hinder).
