sorriness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒr.i.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being insignificant or worthless.
state of being insignificant or worthless

Examples

They were disappointed by the sorriness of his excuses for not attending.
The sorriness of the abandoned lot was visible in its neglected state.
She regretted the sorriness of her actions, recognizing their lack of importance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthlessness - the quality of having no value or use
  • Antonym: value - relative worth or importance

Etymology

From 'sorry' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality.
