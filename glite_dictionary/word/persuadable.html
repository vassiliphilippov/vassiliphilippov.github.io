persuadable

1. ADJ

/ pərˈsweɪdəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈsweɪdəbəl / (BrE)
easily influenced or convinced by argument or appeal; open to persuasion.
easily convinced or influenced

Examples

The jury proved to be persuadable once they heard new evidence.
Children are often more persuadable than adults when it comes to advertising.
His persuadable nature made him easy to influence during group discussions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: convincible - capable of being convinced
  • Antonym: inflexible - unwilling to change or be persuaded

Etymology

From persuade + -able, patterned after Latin '-abilis' meaning 'capable of'.
