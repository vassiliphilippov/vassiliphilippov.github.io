telegraphist

1. NOUN

/ təˈlɛɡrəfɪst / (AmE), / tɪˈlɛɡrəfɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to send and receive messages by telegraph
person who operates a telegraph

Examples

During the 19th century, every railway station had a telegraphist who relayed vital information.
She worked as a telegraphist during World War II, sending coded messages to the front lines.
The telegraphist tapped out the urgent message in Morse code.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telegrapher - a person who operates a telegraph; alternative form

Etymology

From telegraph + -ist, denoting a person who operates a telegraph.
