Capella

1. PROPN

/ kəˈpɛl.ə / (AmE), / kəˈpɛl.ə / (BrE)
the brightest star in the constellation Auriga, one of the brightest stars in the night sky
the brightest star in Auriga

Examples

Every winter, Capella appears high overhead in the evening sky.
The constellation Auriga is easy to spot because of Capella's brightness.
Astronomers have studied Capella for centuries due to its unique properties.

Etymology

From Latin 'Capella' meaning 'little she-goat'; named for its mythological association.
