organizational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an organization or the way it is arranged
relating to an organization

Examples

The new manager proposed several organizational changes to improve efficiency.
We are facing some organizational challenges after the merger.
His role focuses on organizational development and team building.

Synset Examples

organizational structure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizationally - in a manner relating to organization

Etymology

From organization + -al, based on French 'organisationnel'.
