unshapen

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃeɪpən / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪpən / (BrE)
incompletely or imperfectly shaped; not having a definite or finished form.
imperfectly shaped; without definite form
archaic

Examples

The unshapen lump of clay waited on the potter's wheel.
Much of the wood arrived unshapen, requiring hours of work before it could be used.
In the story, the artist struggled to transform the unshapen marble into a masterpiece.

Synset Examples

unshaped dough
unshaped timbers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unshaped - not shaped; lacking definite form
  • Synonym: shapeless - without a definite or regular shape

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'shapen' (archaic past participle of 'shape').
