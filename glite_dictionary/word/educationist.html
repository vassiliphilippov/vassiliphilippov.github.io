educationist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who specializes in the theory and principles of education; an expert in educational methods or policy
expert in educational theory and practice

Examples

The conference brought together leading educationists from around the world.
As a dedicated educationist, she developed new teaching theories for primary schools.
The university hired an experienced educationist to reform its curriculum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educator - a person who provides instruction or education; a teacher

Etymology

From 'education' + '-ist', indicating a specialist or adherent.
