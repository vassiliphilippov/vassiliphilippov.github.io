zymurgy

1. NOUN

/ ˈzaɪmərdʒi / (AmE), / ˈzɪmjʊəˌdʒi / (BrE)
the branch of chemistry concerned with fermentation, especially as it relates to making wine, beer, or spirits
branch of chemistry dealing with fermentation

Examples

He developed an interest in zymurgy after taking a course in brewing science.
The laboratory specializes in zymurgy and the production of fine craft beers.
Advancements in zymurgy have improved the efficiency of biofuel production.

Etymology

From Greek *zyme* (leaven) + *-ourgia* (work).
