spiritualistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌspɪrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌspɪrɪtjuəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of spiritualism or its beliefs and practices
relating to spiritualism

Examples

The medium claimed to use spiritualistic methods to communicate with the dead.
She became interested in spiritualistic practices after attending a séance.
His spiritualistic beliefs influenced every aspect of his life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spiritualist - a person who believes in or practices spiritualism
  • Antonym: materialistic - relating to or concerned with material possessions or physical comfort

Etymology

From 'spiritualism' + '-istic', based on Latin 'spiritus' (spirit).
