Bangalore

1. PROPN

/ ˌbæŋɡəˈlɔr / (AmE), / ˌbaŋɡəˈlɔː / (BrE)
a large industrial city in southern India, noted for its technology industry and often regarded as the country's 'Silicon Valley'
major city in southern India

Examples

Bangalore has become renowned for its thriving information technology industry.
The weather in Bangalore is known to be pleasant throughout the year.
Many multinational companies have established offices in Bangalore due to its skilled workforce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bengaluru - the official name of Bangalore
