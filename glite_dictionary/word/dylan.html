Dylan

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɪlən / (AmE), / ˈdɪlən / (BrE)
A Celtic god of the sea, considered the son of Arianrhod in Welsh mythology.
Celtic sea god in Welsh mythology

Examples

In Welsh mythology, Dylan is revered as the god of the sea.
The story of Dylan and his mother Arianrhod is well known among Celtic legends.
Some poets draw inspiration from Dylan when depicting the power of the ocean.

Etymology

From Welsh mythology; from Welsh Dylan, meaning 'son of the wave'.
