enormousness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈnɔrməsnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːməsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely large in size, extent, or number.
the state of being extremely large

Examples

The enormousness of the new stadium left the visitors in awe.
Scientists were amazed by the enormousness of the prehistoric creature's skeleton.
We struggled to grasp the enormousness of the task ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immensity - the extremely large size or scale of something
  • Synonym: vastness - the great size or extent of something
  • Antonym: smallness - the quality of being small in size, extent, or number

Etymology

From 'enormous' + '-ness'; 'enormous' from Latin 'enormis' (irregular, immense).
