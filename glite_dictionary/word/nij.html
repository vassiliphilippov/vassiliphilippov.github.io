NIJ

1. NOUN

/ ɛn aɪ dʒeɪ / (AmE), / ɛn aɪ dʒeɪ / (BrE)
an agency of the U.S. Department of Justice responsible for research and development in law enforcement and criminal justice.
U.S. Department of Justice research agency

Examples

The NIJ funded a study to evaluate new forensic techniques.
She attended a conference sponsored by the NIJ last year.
Funding from the NIJ supports innovative policing research nationwide.

Etymology

Abbreviation for National Institute of Justice.
