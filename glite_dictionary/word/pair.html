pair

1. NOUN

/ pɛr / (AmE), / peə / (BrE)
two similar or matching things that are considered together as a single unit.
two items regarded as a unit

Examples

She bought a new pair of shoes for the wedding.
I can't find a matching pair of socks this morning.
This puzzle requires you to find each pair of identical cards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: couple - two people or things of the same kind considered together

Etymology

From Latin 'par' (equal, pair), via Old French 'paire'.

Synset POS: n
