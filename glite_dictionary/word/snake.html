Snake

1. PROPN

/ sneɪk / (AmE), / sneɪk / (BrE)
A faint constellation in the Southern Hemisphere near the equator, stretching between Virgo and Cancer.
constellation near Virgo and Cancer

Examples

The Snake is visible in the night sky during spring in the Northern Hemisphere.
Astronomers use the Snake constellation to help locate other celestial objects.
According to mythology, the Snake represents a serpent held by the healer Ophiuchus.
