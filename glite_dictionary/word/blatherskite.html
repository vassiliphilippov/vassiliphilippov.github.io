blatherskite

1. NOUN

/ ˈblæðərˌskaɪt / (AmE), / ˈblæðəˌskaɪt / (BrE)
foolish talk; nonsense; gibberish
foolish talk or nonsense

Examples

His stories were nothing but blatherskite and wild exaggerations.
Tired of all this blatherskite, she left the meeting early.
The politician's speech was dismissed as empty blatherskite by his opponents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

From Scots 'bletherskate', from 'blether' (talk nonsense) + 'skite' (contemptible person).
