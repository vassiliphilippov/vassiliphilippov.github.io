twinkly-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɪŋkli aɪd / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋkli aɪd / (BrE)
having bright, sparkling, or lively eyes that appear to twinkle with amusement, joy, or friendliness.
having sparkling, lively eyes

Examples

Her twinkly-eyed smile made everyone feel welcome at the party.
The twinkly-eyed old man told stories that enchanted the children.
He gave a twinkly-eyed glance before delivering the punchline of his joke.

Synset Examples

The twinkly-eyed puppy stole everyone's hearts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twinkling - shining with a flickering or sparkling light
