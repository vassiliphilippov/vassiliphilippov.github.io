reputability

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛpjətəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌrɛpjʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being respected and considered to have a good reputation
quality of being reputable

Examples

The reputability of the institution attracted many international students to apply.
Questions were raised about the reputability of the source cited in the article.
Her reputability as an expert in the field was never in doubt.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disreputability - the quality of being not reputable or lacking good reputation
  • Synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions that are generally held about someone or something

Etymology

From 'reputable' + '-ity', ultimately from Latin 'reputare' (to consider, reflect upon).
