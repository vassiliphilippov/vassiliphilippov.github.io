Mrs.

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪz / (BrE)
a title used before the surname or full name of a married woman
title for a married woman

Examples

Mrs. Johnson will be the guest speaker at tonight's event.
Please address the invitation to Mrs. Lisa Thompson.
It was Mrs. Carter who organized the charity fundraiser.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ms. - a title for a woman regardless of marital status
  • Antonym: Miss - a title for an unmarried woman

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Mistress', from Middle English 'maistresse' (woman in authority), from Old French.
