unannounced

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈnaʊnst / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈnaʊnst / (BrE)
happening or arriving without prior warning or public declaration
without prior warning or announcement

Examples

The inspector stopped by the restaurant unannounced to check on sanitation practices.
She walked into the meeting unannounced, surprising everyone in the room.
Packages arriving unannounced can sometimes catch people off guard.

Synset Examples

they arrived unannounced
a totally unheralded telegram that his daughter … died last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpected - not expected or foreseen
  • Antonym: announced - made known publicly or officially

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and 'announced' (from Old French annoncer, from Latin annuntiare 'to announce').
