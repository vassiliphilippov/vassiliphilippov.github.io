Musgoi

1. NOUN

/ musˈɡɔɪ / (AmE), / mʌsˈɡɔɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, especially in parts of northern Cameroon
Chadic language of northern Cameroon

Examples

Linguists have documented the grammar of Musgoi in several fieldwork studies.
She grew up speaking Musgoi at home with her family in northern Cameroon.
The Musgoi language faces challenges due to the influence of larger regional languages.
