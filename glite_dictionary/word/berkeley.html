Berkeley

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɝːkli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːkli / (BrE)
A city in California, located on the eastern shore of San Francisco Bay; known as the site of the University of California, Berkeley.
city in California, USA

Examples

She moved to Berkeley to attend graduate school at the university.
The vibrant cultural scene in Berkeley attracts people from all over the world.
After living in Berkeley for ten years, he became passionate about environmental causes.
