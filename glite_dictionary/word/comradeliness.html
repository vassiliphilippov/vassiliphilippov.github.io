comradeliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmˌrædli.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmˌrædli.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly and sociable, especially as shared among people with a common aim or bond
friendliness; sociability among comrades

Examples

There was a genuine comradeliness among the team members as they worked late into the night.
Her comradeliness helped new employees feel welcome and included in the group.
The hiking club is known for its spirit of comradeliness and cooperation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
  • Synonym: camaraderie - mutual trust and friendship among people

Etymology

From 'comrade' plus the noun-forming suffix '-liness'.
