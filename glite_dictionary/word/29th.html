29th

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛnti ˈnaɪnθ / (AmE), / ˈtwɛnti ˈnaɪnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-eighth in a sequence; being number twenty-nine in order.
ordinal: number twenty-nine in order

Examples

Her birthday falls on the 29th of February, which only comes every four years.
This is the 29th book in the series, and it's just as exciting as the previous ones.
He finished in 29th place in the marathon, improving his time from last year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-ninth - ordinal: number twenty-nine in order (spelled out)
  • Degree Lower: 28th - ordinal: number twenty-eight in order
  • Degree Higher: 30th - ordinal: number thirty in order
