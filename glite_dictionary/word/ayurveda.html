Ayurveda

1. NOUN

/ aɪ.ərˈveɪ.də / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.ʊəˈveɪ.də / (BrE)
the traditional Hindu system of medicine, based on ancient texts focusing on balance in bodily systems and the use of diet, herbal treatment, and yogic breathing
traditional Hindu system of medicine

Examples

Many people seek Ayurveda for natural remedies and holistic wellness.
She traveled to India to study Ayurveda and become a practitioner.
Ayurveda emphasizes the importance of diet, lifestyle, and natural therapies for health.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'āyus' (life) + 'veda' (knowledge), meaning 'science of life.'
