choirgirl

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪɚˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪəˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A girl who sings in a choir.
girl who sings in a choir

Examples

The choirgirl sang her solo beautifully during the Christmas concert.
As a choirgirl, she attends rehearsals twice a week at the cathedral.
Several talented choirgirls auditioned for the role of lead singer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: choirboy - a boy who sings in a choir
