half-term

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈtɜrm / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈtɜːm / (BrE)
A short holiday about halfway through a school term, especially in British schools.
short school holiday in mid-term (UK)
british

Examples

We're planning a family trip during half-term this year.
Many museums offer special activities for children over half-term.
He visited his grandparents at half-term.

Synset Examples

he came to visit at half-term

Etymology

From 'half' meaning midway and 'term' referring to a division of the academic year.
