horseshit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrʃˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːʃˌʃɪt / (BrE)
Vulgar slang for something considered nonsense, lies, or grossly unacceptable behavior.
vulgar term for nonsense or lies
vulgar slang

Examples

Don't give me that horseshit—I know you're lying.
He called the politician's promises nothing but horseshit.
I'm tired of all this horseshit at work lately.

Synset Examples

I put up with a lot of bullshit from that jerk
what he said was mostly bull

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - vulgar slang for nonsense or lies
  • Synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'bullshit', using 'horse' + 'shit' for emphatic effect.
