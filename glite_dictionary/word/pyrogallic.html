pyrogallic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpaɪroʊˈɡælɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaɪrəʊˈɡælɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from pyrogallol, especially in chemical context.
relating to pyrogallol

Examples

He conducted experiments using a pyrogallic solution as a photographic developer.
The pyrogallic process played a significant role in early photographic history.
A pyrogallic acid test was used to detect certain metals in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pyrogallol - a trihydroxy benzene compound used in photography and chemistry

Etymology

From 'pyrogallol' and the suffix '-ic', denoting relation or derivation.
