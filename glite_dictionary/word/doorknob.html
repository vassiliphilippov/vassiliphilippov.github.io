doorknob

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔr.nɑb / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.nɒb / (BrE)
A round handle that is turned to open or close a door.
round handle for opening a door

Examples

She twisted the doorknob and slowly pushed the door open.
The old brass doorknob had become loose and needed tightening.
He replaced every doorknob in the house with modern handles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorhandle - a handle used for opening or closing a door
