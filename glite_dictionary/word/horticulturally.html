horticulturally

1. ADV

/ ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃɚəli / (AmE), / ˌhɔːtɪˈkʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is concerned with horticulture.
in a horticultural manner

Examples

The garden was designed horticulturally to thrive in the local climate.
This region is horticulturally important due to its variety of fruit crops.
She manages her property horticulturally, focusing on sustainable plant care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticultural - Relating to horticulture

Etymology

From 'horticulture' + '-ally', formed on the model of other adverbs.
