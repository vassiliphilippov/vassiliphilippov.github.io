basidiomycetous

1. ADJ

/ bəˌsɪdioʊmaɪˈsiːtəs / (AmE), / bəˌsɪdiəʊmaɪˈsiːtəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of fungi of the class Basidiomycetes, which includes mushrooms, toadstools, and related species
of or relating to Basidiomycetes fungi
medical

Examples

The researcher identified the specimen as a basidiomycetous fungus because of its spore-producing structures.
Many edible mushrooms are basidiomycetous organisms commonly found in forests.
A group of basidiomycetous fungi was responsible for the tree decay observed in the park.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ascomycetous - relating to fungi of the class Ascomycetes

Etymology

From 'Basidiomycetes', a class of fungi, plus the suffix '-ous' forming adjectives.
