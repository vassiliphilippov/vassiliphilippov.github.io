unsanctioned

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsæŋkʃənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaŋkʃənd / (BrE)
not officially approved or authorized; not given explicit permission by an authority
not officially approved or authorized

Examples

The company faced legal issues over the unsanctioned construction of the warehouse.
Any unsanctioned gatherings in the building will be shut down immediately.
She was reprimanded for her unsanctioned absence from the mandatory meeting.

Synset Examples

unsanctioned use of company cars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unauthorized - not having official permission or approval

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + sanctioned (from Latin sanctio, sanctionis, meaning a decree or law).
