phytology

1. NOUN

/ faɪˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / faɪˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of biology that studies plants; botany.
study of plants; botany

Examples

She decided to pursue a career in phytology after discovering her passion for plant life.
The advancements in phytology have contributed greatly to agricultural science.
His lecture on medicinal plants showcased his expertise in phytology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botany - the scientific study of plants

Etymology

From Greek 'phyton' (plant) + '-logy' (study, science).
