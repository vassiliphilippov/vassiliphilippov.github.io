gratitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræt̬.əˌtud / (AmE), / ˈɡræt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
the feeling of being thankful and appreciative for something received or done
thankfulness; appreciation

Examples

She expressed her deep gratitude to all who supported her during the difficult time.
His heart was filled with gratitude after receiving such a thoughtful gift.
We would like to extend our gratitude to everyone who volunteered.

Synset Examples

he was overwhelmed with gratitude for their help

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thankfulness - the quality or state of being thankful
  • Antonym: ingratitude - lack of gratitude or thankfulness

Etymology

From Latin 'gratitudo', from 'gratus' (pleasing, thankful).
