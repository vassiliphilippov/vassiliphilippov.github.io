Farsi

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrsi / (AmE), / ˈfɑːˌsi / (BrE)
the Persian language, spoken chiefly in Iran and also in Afghanistan and Tajikistan
Persian language of Iran

Examples

Many Iranians speak Farsi as their first language.
She is taking a course to learn how to read and write in Farsi.
Important works of Persian poetry were originally composed in Farsi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Persian language, also known as Farsi

Etymology

From Persian 'Fārsi', the native name of the Persian language.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrsi / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsi / (BrE)
the Persian language, spoken primarily in Iran and widely used in literature, media, and education
the Persian language of Iran

Examples

Many Iranians speak Farsi as their native language.
She is taking a course to learn how to read and write Farsi.
Classical poetry by Hafez is often studied in its original Farsi form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Persian - the Persian language

Etymology

From Persian فارسی (fârsi), which is the endonym for the Persian language.
