pursual

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsuːəl / (AmE), / pəˈsjuːəl / (BrE)
the act of pursuing, especially in an effort to overtake, capture, or achieve something
the act of pursuing

Examples

The detective was relentless in his pursual of the truth.
Her pursual of higher education inspired everyone in her family.
The team's pursual of victory kept them motivated throughout the season.
