starry-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑriˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɑːriˌaɪd / (BrE)
having an unrealistically or naively optimistic outlook; idealistic to an impractical degree
unrealistically optimistic or idealistic

Examples

Her starry-eyed enthusiasm for the project faded after a few setbacks.
Many starry-eyed graduates think they'll change the world overnight.
The starry-eyed proposal ignored the practical limitations of the plan.

Synset Examples

a starry-eyed reformer
starry-eyed idealism

Related Terms

  • Antonym: realistic - having a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved
  • Synonym: idealistic - believing in or pursuing noble ideals, often unrealistically

Etymology

Formed from 'starry' (full of stars) and 'eyed' (having a specified kind of eyes), suggesting dreaminess or idealism.
