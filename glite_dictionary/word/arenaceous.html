arenaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛrəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of a substance or soil) resembling, containing, or abundant in sand; relating to or growing in sandy areas.
containing or resembling sand

Examples

The geologist noted the arenaceous texture of the rock sample.
Several species of arenaceous grasses thrive in coastal dunes.
The landscape was dominated by arenaceous soils, making it challenging for trees to grow.

Synset Examples

arenaceous limestone
arenaceous grasses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sandy - consisting of, containing, or resembling sand

Etymology

From Latin 'arenaceus' (sandy), from 'arena' (sand).
