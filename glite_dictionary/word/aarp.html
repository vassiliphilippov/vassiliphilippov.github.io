AARP

1. PROPN

/ ˌeɪ.eɪ.ɑːrˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.eɪ.ɑːˈpiː / (BrE)
An American organization that advocates for the interests and welfare of people aged 50 and older, especially senior citizens.
American senior citizens' advocacy organization
american

Examples

AARP provides resources and information on health care and retirement planning for older adults.
Many seniors join AARP to take advantage of its discounts on travel, insurance, and other services.
The AARP annual conference attracts thousands interested in aging and retirement issues.

Etymology

Initialism of ‘American Association of Retired Persons’.
