Nina

1. PROPN

/ ˈniː.nə / (AmE), / ˈniː.nə / (BrE)
A goddess in Babylonian mythology associated with the watery deep, daughter of Ea.
Babylonian goddess of the watery deep

Examples

In Babylonian mythology, Nina was revered as a deity of the primeval waters.
The temple dedicated to Nina stood at the center of the city's ceremonial district.
Scholars debate the role of Nina within the wider pantheon of Mesopotamian gods.
