VCR

1. NOUN

/ viː siː ɑːr / (AmE), / viː siː ɑː / (BrE)
a video cassette recorder; an electronic device for recording and playing television programs on magnetic tape cassettes
video cassette recorder device

Examples

We watched old family movies on the VCR last night.
She set the VCR to record her favorite show while she was out.
Their living room still has a VCR next to the DVD player.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: videotape recorder - a machine that records and plays video on magnetic tapes

Etymology

Acronym of 'Video Cassette Recorder'.
