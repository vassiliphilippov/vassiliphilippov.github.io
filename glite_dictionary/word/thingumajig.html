thingumajig

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɪŋəməˌdʒɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋəməˌdʒɪɡ / (BrE)
an object whose name is unknown, forgotten, or unspecified
an unspecified or unnamed object
informal

Examples

Can you pass me that thingumajig on the top shelf?
I fixed the machine, but there’s still one thingumajig left over.
She pointed at the thingumajig on the table, unsure what it was called.

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamabob - an object whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: whatchamacallit - used to refer to something whose name one cannot recall
  • Synonym: doohickey - an object or gadget, especially one whose name is unknown or forgotten

Etymology

Alteration of 'thingummy', possibly imitative, with the addition of the fanciful 'jig' ending.
