sauna

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑnə / (AmE), / ˈsaʊnə / (BrE)
A small room or building used for relaxing and sweating by means of steam, usually produced by pouring water on heated rocks; originally Finnish in origin.
Finnish steam bath; room for steam bathing

Examples

After a long run, I like to relax in the sauna to soothe my muscles.
The hotel’s sauna is open to guests every evening until ten o’clock.
She invited her friends to join her for an evening in the backyard sauna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam room - A room heated with steam for the purpose of relaxation and sweating, similar to a sauna but usually at a lower temperature and higher humidity.

Etymology

From Finnish 'sauna' (bath, steam bath).
