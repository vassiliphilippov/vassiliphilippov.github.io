weltanschauung

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛltˌɑnʃaʊʊŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛlt.anʃaʊ.ʊŋ / (BrE)
A particular philosophy or view of life; a comprehensive conception of the world and humanity's place in it.
comprehensive worldview or philosophy

Examples

Her weltanschauung was shaped by years of travel and exposure to different cultures.
The philosopher discussed the social implications of a scientific weltanschauung.
Every generation tends to develop its own distinctive weltanschauung.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldview - personal or cultural way of thinking about the world

Etymology

From German Weltanschauung, from Welt (world) + Anschauung (view, outlook).
