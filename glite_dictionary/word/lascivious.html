lascivious

1. ADJ

/ ləˈsɪviəs / (AmE), / ləˈsɪvɪəs / (BrE)
showing strong sexual desire, often in a way that is considered offensive or inappropriate
showing sexual desire

Examples

The actor was criticized for his lascivious behavior both on and off the screen.
She shot him a lascivious glance that made him immediately uncomfortable.
The novel was banned for its lascivious content and explicit scenes.

Synset Examples

libidinous orgies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lecherous - having or showing excessive sexual desire
  • Synonym: prurient - having or encouraging an excessive interest in sexual matters
  • Antonym: chaste - abstaining from sexual intercourse; not having sexual nature

Etymology

From Late Latin lasciviosus (wanton, playful), from Latin lascivia (playfulness, wantonness).
