toponymy

1. NOUN

/ təˈpɑː.nə.mi / (AmE), / təˈpɒn.ɪ.mi / (BrE)
the study of place names, including their origins, meanings, and use within a particular region or language.
study of place names

Examples

She specialized in toponymy and published a book about the origins of English place names.
Toponymy can reveal fascinating stories about the history and culture of a region.
In his talk, the professor explained how toponymy helps linguists trace ancient migrations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onomastics - the study of the origin, history, and use of proper names

Etymology

From Greek topos (place) + onoma (name), referring to the naming of places.
