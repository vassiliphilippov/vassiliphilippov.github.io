amative

1. ADJ

/ ˈæmətɪv / (AmE), / ˈamətɪv / (BrE)
showing or feeling love, especially sexual love; amorous.
showing or inclined to love

Examples

Her amative glances left no doubt about her intentions.
The poetry was notable for its amative themes and sensual imagery.
He wrote a novel full of amative adventures and passionate confessions.

Synset Examples

feeling amorous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amorous - showing or expressing love or desire

Etymology

From Latin 'amativus' (loving), from 'amare' (to love).
