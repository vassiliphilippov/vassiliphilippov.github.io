Sid Caesar

1. PROPN

/ sɪd ˈsiːzər / (AmE), / sɪd ˈsiːzə / (BrE)
(1922–2014) An American comedian and actor, best known as a pioneer of television comedy shows in the United States.
American comedian and TV pioneer

Examples

Sid Caesar transformed American television comedy with his innovative sketch shows in the 1950s.
Many comedians cite Sid Caesar as a major influence on their own comedic style.
Sid Caesar starred in 'Your Show of Shows,' a groundbreaking program of its era.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Milton Berle - American comedian and television host, early TV comedy pioneer
