Ulster

1. PROPN

/ ˈʌlstər / (AmE), / ˈʌlstə / (BrE)
a historic province in the north of Ireland, comprising nine counties, six of which are part of Northern Ireland and three in the Republic of Ireland
historic province in northern Ireland

Examples

The six counties of Ulster that form Northern Ireland have a complex political history.
She spent her studies researching the cultural traditions of Ulster.
Some of Ireland's most beautiful landscapes are found in Ulster.

Etymology

From Irish 'Ulaidh,' the name of an ancient Irish kingdom.
