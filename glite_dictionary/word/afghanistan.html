Afghanistan

1. PROPN

/ æfˈɡænɪˌstæn / (AmE), / æfˈɡænɪstæn / (BrE)
A mountainous, landlocked country in southern-central Asia, bordered by Iran, Pakistan, and several Central Asian nations.
a country in southern-central Asia

Examples

The war in Afghanistan lasted for more than two decades.
She spent a year teaching English in Afghanistan.
Trade between Afghanistan and its neighbors is vital for the region's economy.

Synset Examples

Soviet troops invaded Afghanistan in 1979

Etymology

From Persian 'Afghānistān', meaning 'land of the Afghans'.
