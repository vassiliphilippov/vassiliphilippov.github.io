tayra

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪrə / (AmE), / ˈtaɪərə / (BrE)
A long-tailed, arboreal mammal of the weasel family, native to Central and South America.
arboreal mammal of Central/South America

Examples

We caught a glimpse of a tayra darting through the rainforest canopy.
The tayra is known for its agility and keen hunting skills.
Tayras are omnivorous and adapt well to different forest habitats.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'taíra', via Tupi 'taira'.
