St. Johns

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
A major river in northeastern Florida that flows north to Jacksonville and then east to the Atlantic Ocean.
major river in northeastern Florida

Examples

The St. Johns River is one of the few rivers in the United States that flows northward.
Several small towns have developed along the banks of the St. Johns.
Fishing and boating are popular activities on the St. Johns throughout the year.
