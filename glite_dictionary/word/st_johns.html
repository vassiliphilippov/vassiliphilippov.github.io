St. John's

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Antigua and Barbuda, located on the island of Antigua

Examples

Many cruise ships dock at St. John's during the winter season.

St. John's is known for its colorful architecture and lively waterfront.

2. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
the capital city and major port of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada, located on the eastern coast of the island of Newfoundland.
capital of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada

Examples

Many tourists visit St. John's to experience its colorful row houses and vibrant culture.
The weather in St. John's can change rapidly due to its coastal location.
He moved to St. John's to study at Memorial University.

Etymology

Named after John the Baptist; 'St.' is an abbreviation of 'Saint', honoring the biblical figure.
