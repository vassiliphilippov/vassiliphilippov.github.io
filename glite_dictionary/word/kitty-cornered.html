kitty-cornered

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪtiˌkɔrnərd / (AmE), / ˈkɪtiˌkɔːnəd / (BrE)
Situated or moving diagonally across from something, especially across the corner of an intersection or area.
diagonally across from

Examples

The café is kitty-cornered from the library, so you have to cross two streets to get there.
Their house sits kitty-cornered from the post office, making it easy to watch the street.
The playground is kitty-cornered from the school, right across the intersection.

Synset Examples

set off in a catty-corner direction across the vacant lot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagonal - lying in a slanting direction across a space or surface

Etymology

Alteration of 'catercornered', from French 'quatre' (four) and English 'corner'.
