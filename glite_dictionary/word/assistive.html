assistive

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɪstɪv / (AmE), / əˈsɪstɪv / (BrE)
providing help or support, especially to people with disabilities or special needs
providing assistance or support

Examples

The school uses assistive technology to help students with learning difficulties succeed.
She relies on an assistive device to help her walk after her injury.
Many assistive products are designed to improve accessibility for people with disabilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supportive - giving support, encouragement, or help

Etymology

From 'assist' + '-ive', meaning having the nature of assisting.
