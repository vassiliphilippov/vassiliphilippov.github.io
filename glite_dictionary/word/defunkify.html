to defunkify

1. VERB

/ diˈfʌŋkəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / diˈfʌŋkɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to remove unpleasant odors, especially from carpets, fabrics, or rooms
remove unpleasant odors
informal

Examples

After the party, we had to defunkify the entire living room carpet.
This spray is designed to defunkify your gym bag after heavy use.
She called a cleaning company to defunkify the basement before her guests arrived.

Synset Examples

They hired a service to defunkify the carpets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to deodorize - remove or conceal an unpleasant smell

Etymology

Formed from 'de-' (remove) and 'funk' (strong, unpleasant smell) with '-ify' (to make or become).
