dishevelled

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈʃɛvəld / (AmE), / dɪˈʃɛvəld / (BrE)
(of a person or their appearance) untidy, with hair or clothes in disorder.
untidy or in disorder

Examples

She arrived at the party looking quite dishevelled after being caught in the rain.
After a long night's work, his hair appeared dishevelled and his shirt was rumpled.
The children came home dirty and dishevelled from playing outside all day.

Synset Examples

her clothing was disheveled
powder-smeared and frowzled
a rumpled unmade bed
a bed with tousled sheets
his brown hair was tousled, thick, and curly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untidy - not tidy or neat
  • Antonym: neat - orderly and clean in appearance

Etymology

From Old French 'deschevelé' (with disordered hair), from 'des-' (away) + 'chevel' (hair).
