pilous

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaɪləs / (AmE), / ˈpɪləs / (BrE)
(of a surface or part) covered with hairs, especially fine or soft ones.
covered with fine or soft hairs

Examples

The leaves of the plant are pilous, giving them a soft, velvety texture.
Some caterpillars have a pilous body that protects them from predators.
Under the microscope, the insect's legs appeared distinctly pilous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy - covered with hair

Etymology

From Latin pilosus, meaning 'hairy', from pilus 'hair'.
