paintball

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a small capsule filled with water-soluble dye, used as a projectile in the game of paintball
dye-filled capsule used in paintball

Examples

He loaded his paintball into the marker before the game started.
The field was covered in brightly colored paintballs after the match.
Always wear goggles when playing with paintballs to protect your eyes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paintball gun - air gun that shoots paintballs

Etymology

From 'paint' + 'ball', denoting a ball containing paint.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A game in which players eliminate opponents by shooting capsules filled with coloured paint, often simulating military combat.
a game with guns that shoot paint capsules

Examples

The group organized a paintball match for their weekend teambuilding event.
He wore protective gear to avoid getting hurt during the intense game of paintball.
Many people enjoy playing paintball as a fun and energetic outdoor activity.
