titty

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪti / (AmE), / ˈtɪti / (BrE)
A vulgar or informal term for either of the two soft, fleshy milk-secreting glands on a woman's chest; a breast.
informal/vulgar term for a woman's breast
informal vulgar

Examples

She complained that her dress was too tight around her titty.
The comedian joked about how babies always seem to reach for a titty.
He tried not to stare at her titties while they were at the beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast - either of the two soft, protruding organs on a woman's chest
  • Synonym: tit - informal term for a woman's breast

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'tit', itself of Germanic origin.
