Ceratostomella ulmi

1. NOUN

/ sɛrəˌtɒstəˈmɛlə ˈʌlmaɪ / (AmE), / sɛrəˌtɒstəˈmɛlə ˈʌlmiː / (BrE)
a species of fungus which causes Dutch elm disease in elm trees
fungus causing Dutch elm disease

Examples

The spread of Ceratostomella ulmi has devastated elm populations across Europe and North America.
Researchers are studying how Ceratostomella ulmi infects elm trees to develop better control methods.
Dutch elm disease caused by Ceratostomella ulmi changed the appearance of many city parks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ophiostoma ulmi - fungus species also known for causing Dutch elm disease

Etymology

From Neo-Latin genus name 'Ceratostomella' and species name 'ulmi' referring to elm.
