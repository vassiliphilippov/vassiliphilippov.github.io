Jane Austen

1. PROPN

/ ˌdʒeɪn ˈɔstɪn / (AmE), / ˌdʒeɪn ˈɒstɪn / (BrE)
(1775–1817) An English novelist renowned for her novels about middle-class families and society, noted for her wit and social commentary.
English novelist (1775–1817)

Examples

Many scholars consider Jane Austen one of the most influential authors in English literature.
I have read all of Jane Austen's novels, including Pride and Prejudice and Emma.
The witty writing style of Jane Austen continues to captivate readers two centuries after her death.
