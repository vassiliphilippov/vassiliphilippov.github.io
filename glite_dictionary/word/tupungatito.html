Tupungatito

1. PROPN

/ tuːˌpʊŋɡɑːˈtiːtoʊ / (AmE), / tuːˌpʊŋɡəˈtiːtəʊ / (BrE)
an inactive volcano in central Chile, last known to have erupted in 1959
inactive volcano in central Chile

Examples

Climbers attempted to reach the summit of Tupungatito during their expedition in the Andes.
Recent studies have monitored volcanic gases around Tupungatito to assess potential hazards.
Despite its last eruption in 1959, scientists still keep a close watch on Tupungatito.
