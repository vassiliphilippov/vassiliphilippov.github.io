modeler

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːdələr / (AmE), / ˈmɒdələ / (BrE)
a person who creates or constructs models, especially as a profession or hobby
a person who builds models

Examples

The modeler spent weeks perfecting the details of the miniature train station.
As a professional modeler, she specializes in architectural replicas for new building projects.
The club welcomes both novice and experienced modelers interested in constructing scale models.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a three-dimensional representation of a person or thing
