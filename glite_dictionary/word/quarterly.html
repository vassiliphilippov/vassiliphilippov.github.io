quarterly

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɔr.tɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɔː.tə.li / (BrE)
(in heraldry) arranged with charges or fields in diagonally opposed quarters of a shield or escutcheon.
heraldic arrangement in quarters

Examples

The shield was divided quarterly to display the different family arms.
In traditional heraldry, coats of arms may be placed quarterly to represent lineage.
The banner featured emblems quarterly, as prescribed by heraldic custom.

Synset Examples

two coats of arms borne quarterly

Etymology

From Middle English, formed from 'quarter' + '-ly', relating to division into four parts.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00438873-r
Synset POS: r

2. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔːr.tɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɔː.tə.li / (BrE)
a periodical or journal published four times a year, once every three months
journal published four times a year

Examples

She subscribes to a science quarterly that features the latest research articles.
Their company's financial results are published in an industry quarterly.
The university library just acquired the most recent art quarterly for its collection.

Etymology

From 'quarter' + '-ly', denoting something occurring every quarter (three months).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06606829-n
Synset POS: n

3. ADV

/ ˈkwɔrtɚli / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəli / (BrE)
every three months; at three-month intervals
every three months

Examples

The company reviews its financial statements quarterly to track progress and set new goals.
Dividends are paid quarterly to shareholders according to the schedule.
The maintenance checks are performed quarterly to ensure safety compliance.

Synset Examples

interest is compounded quarterly

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: monthly - occurring every month
  • Degree Higher: annually - occurring every year

Etymology

From quarter + -ly, meaning 'of or pertaining to a quarter (of a year)'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00438741-r
Synset POS: r

4. ADJ

/ ˈkwɔːrdɚli / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəli / (BrE)
Happening, done, or produced four times a year; relating to or consisting of a quarter (three months).
occurring every three months

Examples

The company releases a quarterly report to keep investors informed.
Many magazines are published on a quarterly basis rather than monthly.
Our team holds quarterly meetings to review progress and set new goals.

Synset Examples

quarterly report

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: annual - occurring once every year
  • Degree Higher: monthly - occurring every month

Etymology

From Latin 'quartarius' (a fourth part), via 'quarter' + '-ly'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03009554-a
Synset POS: a
