untethered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtɛðɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛðəd / (BrE)
Not confined, restricted, or attached by a tether; free or unrestrained.
not tied or restricted

Examples

The astronaut floated untethered outside the space station, relying on her suit's propulsion system.
After quitting his job, he felt untethered and ready to explore new possibilities.
The dog ran untethered across the open field, chasing butterflies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tethered - confined or attached with a tether
  • Synonym: free - not confined or restrained, able to act as one wishes

Etymology

From un- (not) + tethered, past participle of 'tether' (to tie with a rope or chain).
