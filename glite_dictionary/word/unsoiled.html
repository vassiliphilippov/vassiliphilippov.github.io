unsoiled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɔɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɔɪld / (BrE)
not dirty; free from dirt, stains, or spots.
not dirty; clean

Examples

Her white dress remained unsoiled throughout the outdoor ceremony.
He took pride in keeping the kitchen floor perfectly unsoiled.
The letter arrived unsoiled, despite its long journey through the mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities
  • Antonym: stained - marked or soiled with a stain
  • Antonym: soiled - made dirty; stained

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + soiled (made dirty), with 'soil' from Old French souillier.
