wampee

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæm.pi / (AmE), / ˈwɒm.pi / (BrE)
An American aquatic plant with spikes of blue flowers, typically found in shallow water of streams and ponds.
American aquatic plant with blue flowers

Examples

The pond was covered with blooming wampee during the spring season.
Scientists studied the habitat of the wampee to understand its ecological role.
Bright blue flowers made the patches of wampee stand out in the shallow stream.
