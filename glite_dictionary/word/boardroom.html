boardroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdˌruːm / (BrE)
a room where the board of directors or a committee meets, typically in a company or organization
meeting room for a board of directors

Examples

The executives gathered in the boardroom to discuss the company's new strategy.
After the heated debate in the boardroom, a decision was finally reached.
The boardroom was equipped with state-of-the-art video conferencing technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting room - a room designated for holding meetings

Etymology

Formed from 'board' (referring to the board of directors) and 'room'.
