tearoom

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌruːm / (BrE)
a restaurant or establishment where tea and light meals are served
restaurant serving tea and light meals

Examples

After shopping, we relaxed in a cosy tearoom and enjoyed some scones.
The historic tearoom offers traditional afternoon teas with cakes and sandwiches.
Many tourists seek out a local tearoom for an authentic English experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant serving coffee and light refreshments
  • Degree Higher: restaurant - a place where people pay to sit and eat meals cooked and served on the premises

Etymology

Blend of 'tea' and 'room', originating in early 19th-century Britain when such establishments became popular.
