time-honored

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪm ˌɑnɚd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm ˌɒnəd / (BrE)
respected or established because it has existed for a long time
respected due to long tradition

Examples

The festival features time-honored rituals that have been passed down through generations.
She follows the time-honored custom of sending handwritten thank-you notes.
Their restaurant still prepares dishes using time-honored recipes.

Synset Examples

time-honored customs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traditional - something based on tradition
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, not old or traditional
