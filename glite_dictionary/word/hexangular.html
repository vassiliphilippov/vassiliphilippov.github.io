hexangular

1. ADJ

/ hɛkˈsæŋɡjələr / (AmE), / hɛkˈsaŋɡjʊlə / (BrE)
having six sides or shaped like a hexagon; divided into six angles or parts
having six sides or angles

Examples

The architect designed a hexangular tower that resembled a honeycomb structure.
Under the microscope, the mineral revealed a hexangular pattern.
The artist created a hexangular tile for the new mosaic installation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hexagonal - having the shape of a hexagon; with six sides and six angles

Etymology

From Latin 'hexangulus' (hex-'six' + '-angulus' 'angle').
