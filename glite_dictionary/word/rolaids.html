Rolaids

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊˌleɪdz / (AmE), / ˈrəʊˌleɪdz / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of antacid tablets used to relieve heartburn, acid indigestion, and upset stomach.
brand of antacid tablets

Examples

After eating spicy food, he chewed a Rolaids to ease his heartburn.
She keeps a bottle of Rolaids in her desk for quick relief from acid indigestion.
Many people rely on Rolaids to soothe an upset stomach after a heavy meal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antacid - a substance that counteracts stomach acidity
