Urex

1. NOUN

/ ˈjuːˌrɛks / (AmE), / ˈjʊə.rɛks / (BrE)
A trademarked antibacterial drug, also known as methenamine, used to treat urinary tract infections.
trademarked drug for urinary infections
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Urex to help clear up the patient's recurring urinary infection.
My pharmacist explained that Urex is effective in preventing certain types of urinary tract infections.
After taking Urex for a week, she noticed significant improvement in her symptoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: methenamine - antibacterial drug for urinary infections, generic name
  • Synonym: Mandelamine - trade name for methenamine, antibacterial drug
