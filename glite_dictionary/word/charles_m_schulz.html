Charles M. Schulz

1. PROPN

/ ˈʃuːlz / (AmE), / ˈʃʊlz / (BrE)
(1922–2000) American cartoonist best known for creating the comic strip Peanuts, featuring the beagle Snoopy and other popular characters.
American cartoonist, creator of Peanuts

Examples

Charles M. Schulz created the beloved Peanuts comic strip, which introduced the world to Snoopy and Charlie Brown.
The influence of Charles M. Schulz can still be seen in comic art and popular culture today.
Many fans celebrate the birthday of Charles M. Schulz in honor of his contributions to comics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peanuts - comic strip by Charles M. Schulz
  • Synonym: cartoonist - person who draws cartoons, like Schulz
