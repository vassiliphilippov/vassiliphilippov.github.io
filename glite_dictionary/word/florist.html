florist

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪst / (BrE)
a shop that sells flowers and ornamental plants
shop selling flowers and plants

Examples

I stopped by the florist to pick up a bouquet for my mother.
There are several lovely florist shops along Main Street.
The florist had a wide selection of fresh roses and lilies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: florist - a person who sells or arranges flowers

Etymology

From French *floriste*, from Latin *flos* (flower).

2. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪst / (BrE)
a person who sells and arranges flowers and plants, often making bouquets and other floral decorations
person who sells and arranges flowers

Examples

She bought a beautiful bouquet from the local florist for her friend's birthday.
The florist created stunning arrangements for the wedding ceremony.
After years of training, she finally opened her own florist shop downtown.

Synset Examples

the florist made up an attractive bouquet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower shop - a shop where flowers are sold

Etymology

From French *floriste*, from Latin *flos* (flower).
