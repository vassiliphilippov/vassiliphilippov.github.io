esthetical

1. ADJ

/ ɛˈsθɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪsˈθɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the appreciation of beauty or good taste; concerned with aesthetics.
concerning beauty or good taste

Examples

The garden was designed with esthetical principles in mind, making it a soothing place to relax.
Her esthetical sense influenced every aspect of the house's decoration.
Many critics praised the esthetical qualities of the new museum.

Synset Examples

the aesthetic faculties
an aesthetic person
aesthetic feeling
the illustrations made the book an aesthetic success

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aesthetic - concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty

Etymology

Derived from 'aesthetic' + '-al', ultimately from Greek aisthētikos (perceptible by the senses).
