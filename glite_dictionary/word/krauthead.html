Krauthead

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraʊtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊtˌhɛd / (BrE)
An offensive term used for a person of German descent, considered highly derogatory.
offensive term for a German person
offensive

Examples

The soldier insulted his enemy by calling him a Krauthead.
Historical films sometimes depict Allied troops referring to Germans as Krautheads.
Using words like Krauthead is now considered deeply offensive in modern society.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Kraut - offensive slang for a German

Etymology

From 'kraut' (a slang term for German, originally from the German word for cabbage) + 'head'.
