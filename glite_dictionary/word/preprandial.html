preprandial

1. ADJ

/ priˈprændiəl / (AmE), / priˈprændiəl / (BrE)
Occurring or taken before a meal, especially dinner.
before a meal (especially dinner)

Examples

He enjoyed a preprandial cocktail in the lounge before dinner.
The doctor recommended checking blood sugar at preprandial times.
A short walk outdoors was part of their preprandial routine.

Synset Examples

a preprandial drink

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postprandial - occurring after a meal

Etymology

From Latin praeprandium, from prae- (before) + prandium (meal).
