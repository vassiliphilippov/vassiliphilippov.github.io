coquettishly

1. ADV

/ koʊˈkwɛtɪʃli / (AmE), / kəʊˈkwɛtɪʃli / (BrE)
in a flirtatious or teasing manner intended to attract attention or admiration
in a flirtatious manner

Examples

She glanced at him coquettishly, hoping to catch his attention.
He noticed her chatting coquettishly with the other guests at the party.
The actress smiled coquettishly during her interview, charming everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

she smiled coquettishly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flirtatiously - in a flirtatious manner

Etymology

From 'coquettish' + '-ly'; ultimately from French 'coquette' (a flirt)
