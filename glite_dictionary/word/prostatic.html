prostatic

1. ADJ

/ prɑˈstætɪk / (AmE), / prɒˈstatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the prostate gland.
relating to the prostate gland
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the patient had a prostatic enlargement.
He underwent surgery to address a prostatic disorder.
Certain medications are prescribed to treat prostatic symptoms in older men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostate - a gland surrounding the neck of the bladder in male mammals

Etymology

From prostate + -ic, the suffix forming adjectives of relation.
