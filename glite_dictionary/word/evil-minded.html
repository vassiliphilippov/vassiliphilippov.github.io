evil-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːvəlˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈiːvəlˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having wicked or malevolent thoughts or intentions; disposed to harm or wrongdoing
having wicked intentions

Examples

Many believed the villain to be truly evil-minded and unforgiving.
Her evil-minded actions shocked everyone in the small community.
He suspected that someone evil-minded was behind the mysterious disappearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malicious - having or showing a desire to cause harm
  • Antonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
