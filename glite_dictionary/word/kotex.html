Kotex

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɛks / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɛks / (BrE)
A disposable absorbent pad used to absorb menstrual flow, originally a trademarked brand name.
brand of menstrual pad

Examples

She asked her friend if she had a Kotex because she had forgotten to bring one.
The store carries several brands of pads, including Kotex and Always.
During her period, she prefers using Kotex for comfort and absorbency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanitary napkin - a disposable absorbent pad worn to absorb menstrual flow

Etymology

Trademark, coined from 'cotton' and 'texture'.
