unplayful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpleɪfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪfl / (BrE)
Not playful; completely lacking in playfulness or sense of fun.
not playful; serious

Examples

His unplayful manner made it difficult to relax around him.
The meeting was so unplayful that everyone felt tense afterward.
Children usually dislike unplayful adults who refuse to join their games.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: playful - full of fun and high spirits
