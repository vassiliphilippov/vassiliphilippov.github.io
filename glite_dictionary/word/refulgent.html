refulgent

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈfʌldʒənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfʌldʒənt / (BrE)
Shining very brightly; radiating light; brilliant or resplendent.
shining brightly; radiant

Examples

The golden crown gleamed refulgent in the sunlight, capturing every eye in the room.
A refulgent moon hung above the quiet village, flooding the fields with silver light.
Her smile was refulgent, brightening the mood of everyone around her.

Synset Examples

the beaming sun
the effulgent daffodils
a radiant sunrise
a refulgent sunset

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant - shining or glowing brightly
  • Synonym: effulgent - shining brightly; radiant
  • Antonym: dull - lacking brightness or sheen

Etymology

From Latin 'refulgere' (to shine back, to reflect light), from 're-' (back) + 'fulgere' (to shine).
