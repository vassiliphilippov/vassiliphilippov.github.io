spindle-shanked

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɪndl̩ ˌʃæŋkt / (AmE), / ˈspɪndl̩ ˌʃæŋkt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having long, slender legs.
having long, slender legs

Examples

The young deer looked spindle-shanked as it wobbled through the tall grass.
Her spindle-shanked silhouette was unmistakable on the dance floor.
He has always been spindle-shanked, making him an excellent runner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: long-legged - having long legs
  • Antonym: short-legged - having short legs

Etymology

Formed from 'spindle' (a slender rod) and 'shanked' (having legs of a specified kind).
