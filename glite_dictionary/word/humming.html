humming

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
A continuous, low, steady sound, like that made by bees, machinery, or traffic.
continuous, low, steady sound

Examples

The humming of the refrigerator filled the quiet kitchen.
He could hear a soft humming coming from the engine.
The gentle humming outside her window lulled her to sleep.

Synset Examples

the hum of distant traffic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hum - a low, steady, continuous sound

2. NOUN

/ ˈhʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of producing a tune with closed lips, typically without articulating words
singing with closed lips

Examples

She sat by the window, lost in gentle humming as she worked.
The quiet humming of the audience filled the theater before the show began.
He relaxed on the porch, his soft humming blending with the sounds of the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singing - the act of producing musical tones with the voice
