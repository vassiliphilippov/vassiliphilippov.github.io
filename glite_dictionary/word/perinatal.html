perinatal

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛrɪˈneɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈneɪtəl / (BrE)
Relating to the period shortly before, during, and after birth, typically defined as from five months before to one month after birth.
around the time of birth
medical

Examples

The hospital specializes in perinatal care for both mothers and newborns.
Research into perinatal health has improved outcomes for premature infants.
Certain infections can be transmitted during the perinatal period.

Synset Examples

perinatal mortality
perinatal care

Etymology

From Greek 'peri-' (around) + Latin 'natalis' (of birth).
