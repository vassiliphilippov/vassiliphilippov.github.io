together

1. ADV

/ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
in or into one group, place, or assembly; in company with others
assembled in one place or group

Examples

All the students were sitting together in the auditorium.
We gathered together in the park for the picnic.
The family spends every Sunday together at their grandmother's house.

Synset Examples

we were gathered together

Etymology

From Old English togædre, from to (to) + gædre (together).
