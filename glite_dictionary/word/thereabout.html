thereabout

1. ADV

/ ðɛr.əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ðɛər.əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
near that place; in or around that location
near that place

Examples

The old mansion is somewhere thereabout if I remember correctly.
They settled thereabout after leaving the city for good.
Most of the ancient ruins can be found thereabout the river valley.

Synset Examples

he stayed in London or thereabouts for several weeks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearby - close to a particular place; not far away

2. ADV

/ ðɛr.əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ðeər.əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
At or near that time, date, or point.
near that time or date

Examples

The meeting is scheduled for noon or thereabout.
We arrived at the station at ten o'clock or thereabout.
The bill will be ready tomorrow or thereabout.

Synset Examples

come at noon or thereabouts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approximately - around a certain time, number, or amount
  • Antonym: exactly - precisely at a certain time or amount

Etymology

From Middle English 'þereaboute', combining 'there' and 'about'.
