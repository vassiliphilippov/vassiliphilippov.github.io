likuta

1. NOUN

/ liˈkuːtə / (AmE), / lɪˈkuːtə / (BrE)
A former monetary unit of Zaire, equal to one one-hundredth of a zaire.
former Zairean coin, 1/100 zaire

Examples

During the 1980s, a loaf of bread cost just a few likuta in Zaire.
Collectors often seek rare likuta coins for their historical value.
My grandfather kept an old jar filled with likuta and other coins from Zaire.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: makuta - plural of likuta, small denomination of Zairean currency
  • Degree Higher: zaire - the basic former monetary unit of Zaire, superseded by the likuta as a subunit
