spirit

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
the prevailing mood, feeling, or character of a place or situation that influences people present

Examples

She tried to capture the spirit of the 1960s in her artwork.
The spirit of cooperation was evident throughout the company's annual meeting.

/ ˈspɪr.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪr.ɪt / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms
