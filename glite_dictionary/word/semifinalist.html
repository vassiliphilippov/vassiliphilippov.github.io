semifinalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛmiˈfaɪnəlɪst, ˌsɛmaɪˈfaɪnəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈfaɪnəlɪst, ˌsɛmaɪˈfaɪnəlɪst / (BrE)
a person or team that reaches the semifinal round in a competition or tournament
person or team in semifinals of competition

Examples

After a series of intense matches, she became a semifinalist in the national chess tournament.
Only four semifinalists were left to compete for a spot in the final.
The coach praised the efforts of all his players who made it to the semifinalist stage.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: finalist - a person or team that reaches the final in a competition
  • Degree Lower: quarterfinalist - a person or team that reaches the quarterfinal round in a competition

Etymology

From 'semifinal' + '-ist', indicating a person in a semifinal.
