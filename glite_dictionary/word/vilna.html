Vilna

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɪlnə / (AmE), / ˈvɪlnə / (BrE)
an alternative historical name for Vilnius, the capital and largest city of Lithuania, located in the southeast of the country.
historical name of Vilnius, Lithuania

Examples

Many Jewish families in the nineteenth century emigrated from Vilna to the United States.
The library’s archives housed several rare maps of Vilna from the early twentieth century.
His grandmother often spoke of life in Vilna before the war.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vilnius - the capital and largest city of Lithuania

Etymology

From Polish Wilno, itself from Lithuanian Vilnius.
