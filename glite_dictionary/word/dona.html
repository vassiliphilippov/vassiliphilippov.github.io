Dona

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nə / (BrE)
a Spanish courtesy title used before the first name of a woman, equivalent to 'Mrs.' or 'Lady'
Spanish courtesy title for women

Examples

The famous singer was known as Dona Isabel throughout Spain.
We were introduced to Dona Carmen at the reception.
History books often mention Dona Ana de Mendoza.

Synset Examples

Dona Marguerita

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Don - Spanish courtesy title for a man

Etymology

From Spanish 'Doña', derived from Latin 'domina' (lady, mistress).
