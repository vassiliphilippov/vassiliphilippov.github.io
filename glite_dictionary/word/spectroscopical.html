spectroscopical

1. ADJ

/ ˌspɛktrəˈskɑpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌspɛktrəˈskɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving spectroscopy, the scientific study of the interaction of light with matter
relating to spectroscopy
medical

Examples

The scientist relied on spectroscopical methods to identify the chemical composition of the sample.
During the experiment, spectroscopical data revealed new insights into the molecular structure.
Advanced spectroscopical equipment is essential for modern research in physics and chemistry.

Synset Examples

spectroscopic analysis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectroscopic - relating to or involving spectroscopy

Etymology

From spectroscopy, based on Greek spectron (appearance) and -scopy (viewing) with suffix -ical.
