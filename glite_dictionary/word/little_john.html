Little John

1. PROPN

/ ˌlɪt̬.əl ˈdʒɑn / (AmE), / ˌlɪt.əl ˈdʒɒn / (BrE)
a legendary member of Robin Hood's band of outlaws, renowned for his great size and strength.
Robin Hood's strong companion in legend

Examples

Little John is often depicted as Robin Hood's loyal companion.
The tale describes how Little John defeated Robin Hood in a quarterstaff duel.
Children listened in awe to stories about Little John and the Merry Men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Robin Hood - legendary English outlaw and leader of Merry Men
  • Synonym: Friar Tuck - another member of Robin Hood's band of outlaws
