Mounties

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaʊntiːz / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntiːz / (BrE)
Members of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police, the federal police force of Canada.
Canadian federal police officers

Examples

The Mounties are famous for their distinctive red uniforms and wide-brimmed hats.
Canadian Mounties patrol both urban centers and remote regions of the country.
Many tourists take photos with Mounties during official ceremonies in Ottawa.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Royal Canadian Mounted Police - the federal police force of Canada

Etymology

From 'Mountie', a shortened form of 'mounted police', referring to the Royal Canadian Mounted Police.
