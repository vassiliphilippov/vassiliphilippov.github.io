bliny

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːni / (AmE), / ˈblɪni / (BrE)
A Russian pancake made with buckwheat flour and yeast, usually served with caviar and sour cream.
Russian buckwheat pancake

Examples

Traditional Russian bliny are often topped with caviar, sour cream, or smoked salmon.
For the festival, she prepared a large batch of bliny for her family.
Many restaurants serve bliny as both a savory and sweet dish, depending on the toppings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blini - A Russian pancake; alternate spelling of 'bliny'

Etymology

From Russian блины (bliny), plural of блин (blin, pancake).
