cowpie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊˌpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌpaɪ / (BrE)
A flat, round mass of cow dung found on the ground, typically in pastures or fields.
flat mass of cow dung

Examples

Watch your step in the field or you might step in a cowpie.
The farmer complained about cowpies scattered all over the pasture.
Children often joke about the smell of a fresh cowpie on a hot day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowpat - a flat, round mass of cow dung

Etymology

Formed from 'cow' + 'pie', likening the shape of dung to a pie.
