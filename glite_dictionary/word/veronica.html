veronica

1. NOUN

/ vəˈrɑː.nɪ.kə / (AmE), / vəˈrɒn.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Veronica, typically with small, showy blue or white flowers; also known as speedwell.
plant of the genus Veronica

Examples

A bright patch of veronica bloomed at the edge of the path.
Gardeners often plant veronica for its long-lasting flowers.
Several species of veronica thrive in both sunny and partially shaded areas.

Etymology

From New Latin Veronica, traditionally linked to Saint Veronica.
