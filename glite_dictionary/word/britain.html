Britain

1. PROPN

/ ˈbrɪ.tən / (AmE), / ˈbrɪ.tən / (BrE)
the island of Great Britain, or more broadly, the United Kingdom; a country in northwestern Europe consisting of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland
the United Kingdom; Great Britain

Examples

Many tourists visit Britain each year to explore its rich history and vibrant cities.
The weather in Britain is often described as unpredictable.
She moved to Britain for university and ended up staying for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United Kingdom - a country in northwestern Europe; officially the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland
  • Synonym: Great Britain - the largest island of the British Isles, comprising England, Scotland, and Wales

Etymology

From Latin 'Britannia', the Roman name for the island of Great Britain.
