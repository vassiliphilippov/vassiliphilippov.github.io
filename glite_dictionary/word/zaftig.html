zaftig

1. ADJ

/ ˈzɑːf.tɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈzæf.tɪɡ / (BrE)
(of a woman) having a full, rounded figure; plump and attractive.
plump and curvaceous (of a woman)

Examples

The actress was known for her zaftig figure that defied conventional Hollywood standards.
At the party, she wore a dress that complimented her zaftig curves beautifully.
In the 1940s, many fashion models were celebrated for their zaftig appearances.

Synset Examples

a generation ago … buxom actresses were popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buxom - (of a woman) plump, with a full figure

Etymology

From Yiddish *zaftik* meaning 'juicy' or 'succulent', from German *saftig* (juicy).
