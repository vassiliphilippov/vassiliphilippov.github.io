unscheduled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈskɛdʒuːld / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛdjuːld / (BrE)
not planned or arranged in advance; not occurring at a regular or predetermined time
not planned or arranged in advance

Examples

The train had to make an unscheduled stop due to technical issues.
She called an unscheduled meeting to address the urgent problem.
An unscheduled power outage left the whole neighborhood in darkness.

Synset Examples

an unscheduled meeting
the plane made an unscheduled stop at Gander for refueling

Related Terms

  • Antonym: scheduled - planned or arranged to happen at a particular time

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'scheduled', from Medieval Latin 'schedula' (slip of paper, schedule).
