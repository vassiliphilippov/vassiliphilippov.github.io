Lathyrus odoratus

1. NOUN

/ ləˈθaɪrəs oʊˈdɔrətəs / (AmE), / ləˈθɪərəs əʊˈdɒrətəs / (BrE)
A climbing garden plant, commonly known as sweet pea, with fragrant pastel-colored flowers.
climbing plant with fragrant pastel flowers

Examples

Many gardeners love to grow Lathyrus odoratus for its wonderful fragrance and pastel blooms.
The trellis in our backyard is covered with flowering Lathyrus odoratus.
Florists often use Lathyrus odoratus in bouquets due to its beautiful scent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet pea - common name for Lathyrus odoratus, a fragrant garden flower

Etymology

From Latin: 'lathyrus' (a kind of pea) and 'odoratus' (fragrant).
