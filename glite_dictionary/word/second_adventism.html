Second Adventism

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈæd.vɛn.tɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈad.vɛn.tɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A branch of Christianity characterized by the belief that the second coming of Christ is imminent.
Christian belief in imminent second coming

Examples

Some denominations of Second Adventism have predicted specific dates for the return of Christ.
The roots of Second Adventism can be traced to 19th-century movements emphasizing Christ's imminent return.
Members of Second Adventism often engage in evangelism to spread their message about the coming apocalypse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Adventism - Christian beliefs centered on the second coming of Christ

Etymology

From 'Second Advent' (meaning the second coming of Christ) + '-ism' (denoting a doctrine or movement).
