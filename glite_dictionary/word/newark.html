Newark

1. PROPN

/ ˈnuː.ɑrk / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.ɑːk / (BrE)
a city in northeastern New Jersey, the largest city in the state, known for its major transportation hubs and proximity to New York City
the largest city in New Jersey, USA

Examples

Newark is a major hub for flights in and out of the New York metropolitan area.
Many commuters travel daily from Newark to Manhattan by train.
The Prudential Center in Newark hosts concerts and professional hockey games.
