tagged

1. ADJ

/ tæɡd / (AmE), / tæɡd / (BrE)
bearing or marked with a label or tag; having a tag attached.
bearing a label or tag

Examples

All the new equipment in the lab was tagged with inventory numbers.
The dogs were tagged before being released back to their owners.
Each attendee received a tagged badge at the conference.

Synset Examples

properly labeled luggage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: labeled - marked with a label or sticker

Etymology

From the verb 'tag' + -ed, forming the past participle/adjective.
