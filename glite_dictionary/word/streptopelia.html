Streptopelia

1. NOUN

/ ˌstrɛp.toʊˈpiːli.ə / (AmE), / ˌstrɛp.təʊˈpiːlɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the dove family, including many species of turtledoves.
genus of turtledoves

Examples

The genus Streptopelia includes several species of turtledoves found around the world.
Ornithologists have studied the mating calls of Streptopelia doves for decades.
The Eurasian collared dove belongs to the Streptopelia genus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turtledove - a small dove with a gentle, cooing call

Etymology

From Greek streptos (twisted) + peleia (dove).
