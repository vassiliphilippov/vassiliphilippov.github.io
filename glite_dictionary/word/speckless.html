speckless

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɛkləs / (AmE), / ˈspɛkləs / (BrE)
Perfectly clean; without any spots, marks, or dirt.
completely clean; spotless

Examples

She wore a speckless white shirt to the important meeting.
The kitchen was surprisingly speckless after the party ended.
His speckless record at work earned him the trust of his manager.

Synset Examples

the apartment was immaculate
in her immaculate white uniform
a spick-and-span kitchen
their spic red-visored caps

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotless - completely clean; without any spots
  • Synonym: immaculate - perfectly clean; free from flaws or mistakes
  • Antonym: dirty - covered in or marked by an unclean substance

Etymology

From 'speck' (a small spot or stain) + '-less' (without).
