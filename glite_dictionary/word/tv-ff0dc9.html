tv

1. NOUN

/ ˌtiː ˈviː / (AmE), / ˌtiː ˈviː / (BrE)
An electronic device that receives television signals and displays them on a screen.
device for watching television

Examples

We bought a new tv for the living room last weekend.
He likes to watch movies on a big tv screen at home.
She turned off the tv before going to bed.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a ‘telly’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - an electronic system or device transmitting and receiving visual images and sound
  • Synonym: telly - British informal term for television set

Etymology

Abbreviation of ‘television’.
