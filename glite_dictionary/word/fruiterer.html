fruiterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfruːtərəɹ / (AmE), / ˈfruːtərə / (BrE)
a person who sells fruit, especially from a shop or stall
person who sells fruit

Examples

My grandfather worked as a fruiterer in the busy city market.
The fruiterer displayed baskets of ripe peaches and plums outside his shop.
She asked the fruiterer for the freshest apples he had in stock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greengrocer - a person who sells fruit and vegetables

Etymology

From fruit + -erer, patterned after trader or retailer.
