pigpen

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡˌpɛn / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡpɛn / (BrE)
An enclosure or pen where pigs are kept.
enclosure for pigs

Examples

The children stopped to look at the pigpen on their neighbor's farm.
After a heavy rain, the pigpen became muddy and needed to be cleaned.
He built a sturdy fence around the pigpen to keep the pigs safe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sty - a pen or enclosure for pigs
