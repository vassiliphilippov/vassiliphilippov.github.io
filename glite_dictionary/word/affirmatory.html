affirmatory

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɜrməˌtɔri / (AmE), / əˈfɜːməˌtɔːri / (BrE)
affirming or expressing approval or agreement; giving assent
affirming; giving assent

Examples

The committee issued an affirmatory statement following their review of the policy.
She gave an affirmatory nod to show her approval of the plan.
An affirmatory response from all board members was required to proceed.

Synset Examples

an affirmative decision
affirmative votes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affirmative - expressing agreement or assent
  • Antonym: negative - expressing denial, disagreement, or refusal

Etymology

From Latin 'affirmare' (to affirm) + English '-tory' (forming adjectives).
