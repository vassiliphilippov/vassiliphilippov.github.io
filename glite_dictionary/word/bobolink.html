bobolink

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑbəˌlɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈbɒbəˌlɪŋk / (BrE)
a migratory American songbird, Dolichonyx oryzivorus, with a distinctive bubbling song, found in North America
a North American migratory songbird

Examples

Each spring, the bobolink returns to the northern fields to breed and sing its cheerful song.
A flock of bobolinks fluttered above the tall grass in the meadow.
The distinctive black-and-white markings make the male bobolink easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: songbird - a bird known for its singing ability

Etymology

Imitative of the bird's song, first recorded in the early 19th century in American English.
