despised

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspaɪzd / (AmE), / dɪˈspaɪzd / (BrE)
Regarded with strong dislike or contempt.
treated with contempt or dislike

Examples

He felt like a despised traitor after betraying his friends.
The despised policy was eventually repealed due to public pressure.
Once despised, the artist's work is now celebrated around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to despise - to regard with contempt or strong dislike (verb)
  • Antonym: admired - regarded with respect or approval

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'despise', from Old French 'despire', from Latin 'despicere' (to look down on).
