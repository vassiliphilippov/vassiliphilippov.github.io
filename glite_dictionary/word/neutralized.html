neutralized

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuːtrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəˌlaɪzd / (BrE)
Made neutral in some way, or deprived of distinctive or active qualities or characteristics.
made neutral; lacking distinct features

Examples

The neutralized solution prevented the chemical reaction from continuing.
After the policy changes, the department's role was essentially neutralized.
The artist's use of gray tones created a neutralized atmosphere in the painting.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: neutral - not supporting any side or without distinctive qualities
