expansile

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspæn.saɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspæn.sail / (BrE)
(of substances, especially gases) capable of expanding or tending to expand.
able to expand or capable of expansion

Examples

The expansile nature of gases causes them to fill any available space.
An expansile tumor can exert pressure on surrounding tissues as it grows.
Researchers studied the expansile properties of different materials under heat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expandable - able to be expanded; capable of expansion

Etymology

From Latin expansus (spread out, expanded) + English suffix -ile, indicating capability.
