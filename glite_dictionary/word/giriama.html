Giriama

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪˈriːəmə / (AmE), / ɡɪˈriːəmə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in the coastal regions of eastern Kenya
Bantu language of eastern Kenya

Examples

Linguists have documented the structure of Giriama to compare it with related Bantu languages.
She grew up speaking both Swahili and Giriama at home.
The Giriama language is still vibrant among many coastal communities in Kenya.
