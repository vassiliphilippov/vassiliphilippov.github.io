rebus

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːbəs / (AmE), / ˈriːbəs / (BrE)
a puzzle in which words or syllables are represented by pictures or symbols, and the solution is found by interpreting their meaning together
puzzle using pictures to represent words or parts of words

Examples

The magazine included a clever rebus that challenged readers to solve it for a prize.
Children enjoy solving a rebus with pictures and symbols during language lessons.
He designed a rebus that spelled out his name using images of a bee and a bus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzle - a game or problem designed to test ingenuity or knowledge

Etymology

From Latin rebus (by things), ablative plural of res (thing).
