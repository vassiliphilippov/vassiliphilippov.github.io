Sao Luís

1. PROPN

/ saʊ luˈis / (AmE), / saʊ luˈiːs / (BrE)
a port city located on an island off the northeast coast of Brazil, known as the capital of the state of Maranhão.
port city in northeastern Brazil

Examples

The historic center of Sao Luís is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
He traveled to Sao Luís to experience its vibrant culture and colonial architecture.
Many tourists visit Sao Luís each year for its festivals and beaches.

Etymology

From Portuguese, meaning 'Saint Louis,' named after King Louis IX of France.
