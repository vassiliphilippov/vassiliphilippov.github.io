Tanga

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡə / (BrE)
A port city in northeastern Tanzania on the Indian Ocean, known for its strategic location and trade.
port city in northeastern Tanzania

Examples

The cargo ship docked at Tanga before continuing its journey down the coast.
Tourists often visit Tanga to explore its beautiful beaches and historical sites.
Trade has flourished in Tanga due to its location on the Indian Ocean.
