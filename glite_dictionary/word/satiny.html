satiny

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæ.t̬ə.ni / (AmE), / ˈsæt.ɪ.ni / (BrE)
having a smooth, soft, and lustrous surface, similar to that of satin; sleek and glossy
smooth and glossy like satin

Examples

The rose had satiny petals that glistened in the morning sun.
She ran her hand over the satiny surface of the fabric, admiring its smoothness.
His hair looked especially satiny after he used the new conditioner.

Synset Examples

satiny gardenia petals
sleek black fur
silken eyelashes
silky skin
a silklike fabric
slick seals and otters
silky auburn hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silky - having a smooth and soft texture like silk
  • Synonym: sleek - smooth and glossy in appearance
  • Antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse texture

Etymology

From satin + -y, indicating resemblance to satin fabric.
