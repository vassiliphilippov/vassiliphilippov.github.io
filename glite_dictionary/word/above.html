above

1. ADJ

/ əˈbʌv / (AmE), / əˈbʌv / (BrE)
mentioned or appearing earlier in the same text or document; previously stated.
mentioned earlier in the text

Examples

According to the above statement, the company will restructure its management.
Please refer to the above table for the detailed figures.
The flaw in the above argument is immediately apparent to experts.

Synset Examples

flaws in the above interpretation

Etymology

From Old English 'abufan', from 'a-' (on) + 'bufan' (above), related to Middle English and Old Norse forms.

2. ADV

/ əˈbʌv / (AmE), / əˈbʌv / (BrE)
in or to a place that is higher than a particular point or object
in/to a higher place

Examples

The stars shine brightly above at night.
Raise your hand above your head if you have a question.
The helicopter hovered above, searching for the missing hikers.
