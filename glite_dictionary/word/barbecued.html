barbecued

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑrbɪˌkjud / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuːd / (BrE)
cooked over an outdoor grill, especially with a smoky flavor, typically by using a barbecue or grill.
cooked on an outdoor grill

Examples

We enjoyed a plate of barbecued chicken at the summer party.
The aroma of barbecued ribs filled the backyard.
She prefers barbecued vegetables over fried ones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled - cooked on a grill

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'barbecue', which comes from Spanish 'barbacoa', from Taino 'barabicu'.
