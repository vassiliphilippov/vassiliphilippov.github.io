uninterruptedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪn.təˈrʌptɪd.li / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪn.təˈrʌptɪd.li / (BrE)
In a way that is continuous and not interrupted; without any pause or break.
continuously, without interruption

Examples

She spoke uninterruptedly for nearly an hour during the lecture.
The factory has operated uninterruptedly since it opened in 1975.
Rain fell uninterruptedly throughout the night, soaking the entire garden.

Synset Examples

this pleasant state of affairs had continued peacefully and uninterruptedly for many years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuously - in a continuous manner, without stopping
  • Antonym: intermittently - with intervals or interruptions; not continuously

Etymology

From "uninterrupted" + "-ly".
