soteriological

1. ADJ

/ soʊˌtɪriəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / səʊˌtɪəriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of religious doctrines of salvation (soteriology)
relating to salvation in theology

Examples

The book offers a comprehensive analysis of the soteriological themes present in early Christian texts.
His soteriological arguments focus on the concept of redemption and grace.
The professor discussed various soteriological theories proposed throughout the history of theology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soteriology - the study of religious doctrines of salvation

Etymology

From Greek sōtēria (deliverance, salvation) + -logical (relating to the study of).
