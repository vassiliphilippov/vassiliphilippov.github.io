abbatial

1. ADJ

/ əˈbeɪʃəl / (AmE), / əˈbeɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to an abbey or to an abbot or abbess
relating to an abbey or abbot/abbess

Examples

The abbatial church stands at the center of the monastery grounds.
He devoted his life to abbatial duties, overseeing the well-being of the monks.
Architectural changes in the abbatial buildings reflected the order's growing influence.

Etymology

From Late Latin abbatialis, from abbas (abbot).
