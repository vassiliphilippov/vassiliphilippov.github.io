Lynx lynx

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪŋks ˈlɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋks ˈlɪŋks / (BrE)
the scientific name for the Eurasian lynx, a large wild cat native to northern Eurasia
scientific name for the Eurasian lynx

Examples

Wildlife biologists tracked the elusive Lynx lynx in the dense Siberian forests.
The conservation efforts have led to an increase in the Lynx lynx population.
In biology class, we learned that Lynx lynx is the scientific name for the Eurasian lynx.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eurasian lynx - A large species of lynx native to Eurasia

Etymology

From Latin 'lynx' for the animal, used in binomial nomenclature.
