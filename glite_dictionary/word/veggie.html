veggie

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒi / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒi / (BrE)
(informal) a vegetable; any edible part of a plant such as seeds, roots, stems, leaves, bulbs, tubers, or nonsweet fruits.
informal term for vegetable
informal american

Examples

I always try to include a green veggie with every meal.
The kids refused to eat their veggies last night.
She bought fresh veggies from the local farmers' market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable - an edible part of a plant, such as a root, stem, or leaf

Etymology

Shortened form of 'vegetable', first recorded in American English in the 20th century.
