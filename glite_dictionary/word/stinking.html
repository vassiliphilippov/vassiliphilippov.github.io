stinking

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
having a very strong, unpleasant, and offensive smell; malodorous.
having a foul, unpleasant smell

Examples

The garbage left out in the sun became absolutely stinking after a few hours.
We had to air out the stinking shoes in the balcony.
A stinking canal ran behind the old buildings.

Synset Examples

a foul odor
the kitchen smelled really funky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant and sweet smell

/ ˈstɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)

very bad; extremely unpleasant
informal

Examples
