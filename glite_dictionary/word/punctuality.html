punctuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌŋktʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌpʌŋktʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being on time or consistently arriving or completing something at the agreed or scheduled time
habit of being on time

Examples

Her punctuality at work earned her the respect of her colleagues.
The success of the railway depends on the punctuality of its trains.
Parents often try to instill the value of punctuality in their children.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lateness - the fact or quality of being late

Etymology

From Medieval Latin punctualitas, from punctualis (punctual).
