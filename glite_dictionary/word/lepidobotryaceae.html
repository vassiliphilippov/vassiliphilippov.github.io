Lepidobotryaceae

1. NOUN

a family of flowering plants created in 1950 for classifying a distinctive African tree, formerly placed in other families
family of African flowering plants

Examples

The rare African tree belongs to the family Lepidobotryaceae and has few close relatives.

2. NOUN

A small family of flowering plants created in 1950 to classify a distinctive African tree, formerly grouped with other families.
family of African flowering plants

Examples

The Lepidobotryaceae are known primarily from a single African genus, Lepidobotrys.
Recent studies have confirmed that Lepidobotryaceae belong to a unique lineage among flowering plants.
Botanists debated for decades before recognizing Lepidobotryaceae as a distinct family.

From genus name Lepidobotrys + standard botanical family suffix –aceae.
