Ragnarok

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːɡ.nəˌrɑk / (AmE), / ˈræɡ.nəˌrɒk / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the prophesied final battle that leads to the destruction of the gods and the end of the world.
Norse myth of the end of the gods/world

Examples

Many stories and comics reinterpret the legend of Ragnarok for modern audiences.
According to Norse mythology, Ragnarok marks the end of the current world order.
Some scholars debate the original meaning of Ragnarok in ancient texts.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Ragnarök', meaning 'fate of the gods'.
