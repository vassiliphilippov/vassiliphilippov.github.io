bigheartedness

1. NOUN

/ bɪɡˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / bɪɡˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being kind, generous, and compassionate towards others.
kind and generous nature

Examples

Her bigheartedness made her popular with everyone in the community.
Through his bigheartedness, he helped many families in need during the holidays.
The teacher's bigheartedness inspired her students to support one another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: generosity - the quality of being generous
  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly and considerate

Etymology

Formed from 'bighearted' + '-ness', where 'bighearted' means generous and kind.
