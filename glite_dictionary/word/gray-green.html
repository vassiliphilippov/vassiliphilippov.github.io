gray-green

1. ADJ

/ ɡreɪ ˈɡrin / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color tinged with gray; having a subdued shade of green mixed with gray.
green tinged with gray

Examples

The walls were painted a subtle gray-green that brought a calm feeling to the room.
She chose a gray-green skirt that matched the autumn scenery perfectly.
The gray-green leaves of the sage plant added texture to the garden.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: green - a color between blue and yellow on the spectrum; color of grass
  • Degree Lower: gray - a color between black and white
