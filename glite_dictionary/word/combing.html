combing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of drawing a comb through hair to arrange or untangle it
the act of using a comb on hair

Examples

After a quick combing, her hair looked neat and tidy.
Regular combing helps prevent tangles and breakage in long hair.
He spent several minutes combing his hair before the interview.

Synset Examples

his hair needed a comb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brushing - the act of using a brush to smooth or arrange hair

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'comb' + '-ing' suffix.
