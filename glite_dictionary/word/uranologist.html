uranologist

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌrænəˈlɑdʒɪst / (AmE), / jʊəˌranəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist or physicist who specializes in the study of astronomy
astronomy specialist

Examples

The uranologist presented her latest findings about distant galaxies at the conference.
Ever since childhood, he dreamed of becoming a uranologist and exploring the stars.
Many uranologists use advanced technology to observe phenomena far beyond our solar system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astronomer - a person who studies or is an expert in astronomy

Etymology

From Greek ‘ouranos’ (sky) + ‘-logist’ (one who studies).
