quatercentennial

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɑːtɚˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɔːtəˌsɛnˈtɛnɪəl / (BrE)
The 400th anniversary of an event or the celebration marking it.
400th anniversary or its celebration

Examples

The city organized a quatercentennial festival, drawing visitors from all over the world.
A special coin was minted to commemorate the nation's quatercentennial in 2020.
Several academic conferences were held during the university's quatercentennial celebrations.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: centennial - the 100th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Lower: bicentennial - the 200th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Lower: tercentennial - the 300th anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

From Latin 'quater' (four times) + 'centennial' (hundredth anniversary).
