self-respectful

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf rɪˈspɛktfəl / (AmE), / ˌself rɪˈspɛktfəl / (BrE)
Having or showing self-esteem and regard for one's own worth.
having self-esteem or self-regard

Examples

She remained self-respectful despite the criticism she faced from her peers.
A self-respectful attitude can help you stand up for your beliefs.
It is important for leaders to be self-respectful in order to earn the trust of others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-respecting - having self-esteem and pride in oneself
