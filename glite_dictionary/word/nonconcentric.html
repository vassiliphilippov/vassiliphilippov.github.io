nonconcentric

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnkənˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
not having a common center; not sharing the same center point
not sharing the same center

Examples

The engineer pointed out the nonconcentric circles in the diagram.
Some planetary orbits are slightly nonconcentric due to gravitational influences.
The design featured a series of nonconcentric rings, creating a unique pattern.

Synset Examples

eccentric circles

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concentric - having a common center

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'concentric' (having a common center), from Latin 'concentricus'.
