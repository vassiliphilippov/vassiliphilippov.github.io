Kuvi

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːvi / (AmE), / ˈkuːvi / (BrE)
A Dravidian language spoken in southeastern India, primarily by the Kuvi people.
a Dravidian language of southeast India

Examples

The Kuvi language is mainly spoken in the Indian state of Odisha.
Linguists have studied Kuvi to understand its Dravidian roots.
She is fluent in both Kuvi and Telugu.
