babu

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːbuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbuː / (BrE)
a respectful title or form of address for a man, especially in India; equivalent to 'Mr.'
Indian courtesy title for a man

Examples

The clerk, addressed as babu, processed our paperwork efficiently.
During the meeting, everyone respectfully referred to him as babu Sharma.
In many Indian offices, senior employees are often called babu as a mark of respect.

Etymology

From Hindi 'bābū', meaning 'sir' or 'father', itself from Persian 'bābā'.
