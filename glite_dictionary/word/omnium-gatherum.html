omnium-gatherum

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːmniəmˈɡæðərəm / (AmE), / ˌɒmniəmˈɡæðərəm / (BrE)
A miscellaneous or heterogeneous collection of things or people.
miscellaneous collection

Examples

The box under my desk is an omnium-gatherum of old photographs, receipts, and tools.
Her story was an omnium-gatherum of rumors, half-truths, and wild exaggerations.
The festival offered an omnium-gatherum of musical styles, from folk to electronic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hodgepodge - a confused mixture
  • Synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things

Etymology

From Latin 'omnium' (of all) + English 'gather' + '-um', likely coined humorously in the 18th century.
