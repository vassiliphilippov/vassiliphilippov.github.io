Bairiki

1. PROPN

/ baɪˈriːki / (AmE), / baɪˈriːki / (BrE)
the islet that serves as the administrative center and capital of Kiribati, located in the Tarawa Atoll in the central Pacific.
capital of Kiribati

Examples

Bairiki is home to many government offices of Kiribati, including the president's office.
During our trip to the Pacific, we spent two days exploring Bairiki.
The population of Bairiki has grown steadily over the past decade.
