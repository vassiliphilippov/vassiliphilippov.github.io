aggressively

1. ADV

/ əˈɡrɛsɪvli / (AmE), / əˈɡrɛsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner showing readiness to attack, confront, or assert oneself forcefully.
in an aggressive manner

Examples

The lawyer questioned the witness aggressively during the trial.
She aggressively pursued new business opportunities after her promotion.
The team aggressively defended their lead in the final minutes of the match.

Synset Examples

she was being sharply questioned

Related Terms

  • Antonym: passively - in a passive manner

Etymology

Formed from 'aggressive' + '-ly'. 'Aggressive' is from Latin 'aggredi' (to approach, attack).
