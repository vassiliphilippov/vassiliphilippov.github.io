sickbed

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.bɛd / (BrE)
the bed on which a sick person lies, especially during a serious illness
bed occupied by a sick person

Examples

She spent several weeks confined to her sickbed after the surgery.
From his sickbed, he dictated letters to his secretary.
Visiting her grandfather at his sickbed was an emotional experience for the whole family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deathbed - bed on which a person dies or is dying

Etymology

From 'sick' + 'bed', indicating a bed used by someone who is ill.
