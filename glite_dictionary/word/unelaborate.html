unelaborate

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪˈlæb.ɚ.ət / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪˈlæb.ər.ət / (BrE)
not elaborate; simple or lacking rich or complex detail
not elaborate; lacking detail

Examples

His explanation was refreshingly unelaborate and easy to follow.
The design of the room was intentionally unelaborate, favoring minimalism over decoration.
She appreciated the unelaborate nature of the ceremony, which felt sincere and unpretentious.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: elaborate - involving many careful details; highly detailed or complicated

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'elaborate.'
