antisemitic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ti.sɪˈmɪ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪ.sɪˈmɪ.tɪk / (BrE)
showing or characterized by hostility, prejudice, or discrimination against Jewish people
hostile or prejudiced against Jews

Examples

The party was accused of making several antisemitic statements during the campaign.
He condemned the antisemitic graffiti that had appeared on the synagogue.
Any form of antisemitic behavior will not be tolerated in this school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-Jewish - hostile to or discriminating against Jewish people
  • Antonym: philosemitic - showing respect or appreciation for Jewish people

Etymology

From anti- (against) + Semitic, referring to Jewish people.
