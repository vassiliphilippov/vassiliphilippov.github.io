Cotacachi

1. PROPN

/ ˌkoʊtəˈkɑːtʃi / (AmE), / ˌkəʊtəˈkɑːtʃi / (BrE)
an inactive volcano in northern Ecuador, part of the Andes, last known to have erupted in 1955
Andean volcano in northern Ecuador

Examples

The snow-capped summit of Cotacachi is a striking landmark in northern Ecuador.
Many hikers travel to see the stunning views from Cotacachi National Park.
Researchers studied the last eruption of Cotacachi to better understand the region's volcanic activity.
