unenlightening

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈlaɪtn̩ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈlaɪtənɪŋ / (BrE)
not providing useful information or insight; failing to inform or clarify.
not informative or clarifying

Examples

Her report was disappointingly unenlightening, leaving the team with more questions than answers.
The interview was largely unenlightening, offering no new perspectives on the issue.
I found his explanation rather unenlightening and still didn’t understand the process.

Synset Examples

an unenlightening comment

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enlightening - giving more information; making clearer or easier to understand

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈlaɪ.tn̩.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈlaɪ.tn̩.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Not giving new information, insight, or understanding; not enlightening.
not providing insight or new information

Examples

The lecture proved to be completely unenlightening and added nothing to our understanding of the topic.
His explanation was so vague and unenlightening that I remained confused.
The book offered an unenlightening perspective on a subject that deserved much deeper analysis.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enlightening - giving new information or insight
