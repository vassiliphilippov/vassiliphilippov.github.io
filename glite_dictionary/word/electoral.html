electoral

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛktərəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktərəl / (BrE)
relating to elections or the process of voting and electing people to office.
relating to elections

Examples

The country is undergoing significant electoral reforms this year.
Many people participated in the electoral process for the first time.
He was accused of trying to influence electoral outcomes through illegal means.

Synset Examples

electoral process

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elective - relating to or filled by election
  • Antonym: appointive - relating to positions filled by appointment, not election

Etymology

From Latin 'elector' (chooser, voter) with the suffix -al, meaning 'pertaining to'.
