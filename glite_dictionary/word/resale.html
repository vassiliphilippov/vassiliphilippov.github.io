resale

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːˌseɪl / (AmE), / ˈriːseɪl / (BrE)
the act of selling something that has previously been bought
selling something again after purchase

Examples

After renovation, the house's resale value increased significantly.
He bought limited-edition sneakers solely for resale at a profit.
Certain items are restricted from resale under the event's ticket policy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sale - the act of selling something

Etymology

From re- (again) + sale; first recorded in the early 15th century.
