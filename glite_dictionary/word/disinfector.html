disinfector

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktər / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktə / (BrE)
a device or apparatus used to apply disinfectants for destroying harmful microorganisms.
apparatus for applying disinfectants

Examples

The hospital uses a new disinfector to ensure all medical instruments are properly sanitized.
After each use, the laboratory glassware is placed in an automated disinfector for thorough cleaning.
A portable disinfector can be carried to different rooms to treat surfaces quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sterilizer - an apparatus for making something free from bacteria or other living microorganisms

Etymology

From disinfect + -or, meaning one who or that which disinfects.
