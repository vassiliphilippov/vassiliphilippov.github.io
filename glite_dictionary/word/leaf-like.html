leaf-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈlif.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈliːf.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a leaf
resembling a leaf

Examples

The plant's leaf-like structures help it blend in with the surrounding foliage.
Some insects have evolved leaf-like wings for camouflage.
The sculpture was decorated with leaf-like patterns along its edge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foliaceous - resembling or pertaining to leaves
