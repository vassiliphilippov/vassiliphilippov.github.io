dismissed

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈmɪst / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɪst / (BrE)
having been removed from a job; no longer employed, especially as a result of being fired or let go
having lost one's job

Examples

After the company restructured, several employees were dismissed last month.
He felt devastated when he was dismissed for breaching company policy.
The dismissed workers are now looking for new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fired - having lost one's job, especially for wrongdoing
  • Antonym: hired - given a job or position

Etymology

Past participle of 'dismiss', from Latin 'dimittere' meaning 'send away'.
