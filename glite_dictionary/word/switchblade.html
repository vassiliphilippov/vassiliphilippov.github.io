switchblade

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃˌbleɪd / (BrE)
a pocketknife with a blade that opens automatically when a button is pressed
spring-loaded pocketknife

Examples

He carried a switchblade in his jacket for protection.
The police confiscated a switchblade from the suspect during the search.
Some countries have strict laws against possessing a switchblade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pocketknife - a small folding knife that can be carried in the pocket

Etymology

From 'switch' + 'blade', referring to the blade's spring-activated movement.
