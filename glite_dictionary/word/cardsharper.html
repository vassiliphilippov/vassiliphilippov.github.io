cardsharper

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌʃɑrpər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌʃɑːpə / (BrE)
A person who cheats professionally at card games in order to win money.
professional card game cheater

Examples

The notorious cardsharper was finally banned from every casino in the region.
She realized too late that she had been playing against a skilled cardsharper all night.
The old western movie portrayed the villain as a ruthless cardsharper who preyed on unsuspecting gamblers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardsharp - a person who cheats at cards professionally

Etymology

From 'card' + 'sharp' or 'sharper', meaning swindler or cheat; 19th-century usage.
