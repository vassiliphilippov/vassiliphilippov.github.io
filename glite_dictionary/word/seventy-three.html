2. NUM

/ ˌsɛv.ən.ti ˈθri / (AmE), / ˌsɛv.ən.ti ˈθriː / (BrE)
The number 73; the cardinal number that is three more than seventy.
the number 73

Examples

Only seventy-three people attended the conference this year.
She received seventy-three votes in the election.
The train arrived at platform seventy-three minutes past the hour.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seventy-two - the number 72
  • Degree Higher: seventy-four - the number 74

Etymology

From 'seventy' and 'three', combining forms representing 70 and 3.
