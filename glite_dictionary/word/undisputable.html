undisputable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈspjuː.t̬ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈspjuː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to question or doubt; certain to be true.
impossible to question or doubt

Examples

The scientist presented undisputable facts that convinced everyone in the room.
It is an undisputable truth that honesty builds trust in relationships.
Her talent is undisputable, as shown by her numerous awards and achievements.

Synset Examples

indisputable evidence of a witness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indisputable - impossible to doubt; unquestionable

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'un-' (not) + disputable (able to be disputed).
