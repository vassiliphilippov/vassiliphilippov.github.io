fruitfully

1. ADV

/ ˈfruːtfəli / (AmE), / ˈfruːtfəli / (BrE)
In a way that produces good results; in a productive or beneficial manner.
in a productive or beneficial way

Examples

The research team collaborated fruitfully on several groundbreaking projects.
He spent his time fruitfully, learning new skills and making valuable connections.
The meeting ended fruitfully, with clear plans for future cooperation.

Synset Examples

they worked together productively for two years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: productively - in a way that produces good results
  • Antonym: unproductively - in a way that does not produce good results

Etymology

From 'fruitful' (productive, yielding results) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
