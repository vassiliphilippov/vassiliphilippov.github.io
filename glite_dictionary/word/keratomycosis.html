keratomycosis

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɛr.əˌtoʊ.maɪˈkoʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɛr.ətəʊ.maɪˈkəʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
a fungal infection of the cornea
fungal infection of the cornea
medical

Examples

Early diagnosis of keratomycosis is crucial for successful treatment and prevention of vision loss.
The patient developed keratomycosis after suffering a minor eye injury while gardening.
Ophthalmologists often prescribe antifungal eye drops to manage keratomycosis cases.

Etymology

From Greek 'kerato-' (horn, referring to the cornea) + 'mycosis' (fungal infection).
