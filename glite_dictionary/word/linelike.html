linelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a line, especially in shape or appearance.
resembling a line

Examples

The artist used a linelike brushstroke to create a sense of movement.
Examining the image, scientists noticed a linelike structure running through the sample.
Clouds formed linelike patterns across the twilight sky.
