Bayamo

1. NOUN

/ baɪˈɑːmoʊ / (AmE), / baɪˈɑːməʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Cuban dance, especially performed at festivals and celebrations
traditional Cuban dance

Examples

During the parade, local dancers performed the Bayamo in colorful costumes.
She learned to dance the Bayamo while visiting Cuba.
The festival concluded with a group Bayamo on the main stage.

Synset Examples

He danced the Bayamo at the festival.
They performed the Bayamo during the celebration.

Etymology

Named after the city of Bayamo in Cuba, where the dance originated.
