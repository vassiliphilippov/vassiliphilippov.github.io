flavoursomeness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfleɪvərsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəsəmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a pleasant and appetizing flavor
quality of being full of flavor
british

Examples

The flavoursomeness of the stew made it the highlight of the meal.
Chefs strive to enhance the flavoursomeness of their dishes with fresh herbs and spices.
The cheese was known for its rich flavoursomeness and creamy texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flavorfulness - quality of being full of flavor; US spelling and usage
  • Antonym: blandness - lack of strong flavor or taste
