hencoop

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛn.kuːp / (AmE), / ˈhen.kuːp / (BrE)
A small building on a farm for housing chickens or other poultry.
poultry house; chicken shelter

Examples

The farmer cleaned the hencoop every morning to keep the chickens healthy.
Their old wooden hencoop needs repair before the next batch of chicks arrive.
At dusk, the hens would return to their hencoop for the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - an enclosure or building for keeping chickens

Etymology

From 'hen' (a female chicken) + 'coop' (an enclosure for animals).
