Hieronymus Bosch

1. PROPN

/ haɪˈrɑːnɪməs bɑʃ / (AmE), / hɪəˈrɒnɪməs bɒʃ / (BrE)
(c. 1450–1516) A Dutch painter known for his fantastical imagery and detailed landscapes, famous for works such as 'The Garden of Earthly Delights.'
Dutch painter, master of fantastical art

Examples

A retrospective of Hieronymus Bosch's paintings drew large crowds at the museum.
Many art historians study the symbolism in Hieronymus Bosch's most famous works.
The surreal landscapes of Hieronymus Bosch continue to inspire contemporary artists.

Etymology

Hieronymus is a Latinized form of the Greek name Hieronymos; Bosch derives from his birthplace, 's-Hertogenbosch in the Netherlands.
