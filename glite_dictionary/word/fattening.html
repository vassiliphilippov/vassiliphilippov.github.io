fattening

1. ADJ

/ ˈfætənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfætənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of livestock or equipment) used for making animals fatter before slaughter.
used for fattening animals for slaughter

Examples

The farmer placed the pigs in a fattening pen before sending them to market.
Cattle kept in fattening sheds are given a special diet to gain weight quickly.
The ranch invested in new fattening equipment to improve their livestock's growth.

Synset Examples

a fattening hog
fattening pens

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finishing - livestock feeding practice for preparing animals for market

Etymology

From the verb 'fatten' + -ing suffix.
