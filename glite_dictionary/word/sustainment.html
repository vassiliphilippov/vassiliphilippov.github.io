sustainment

1. NOUN

/ səˈsteɪnmənt / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act of sustaining life or providing the means of subsistence, especially through nourishment or support
the act of sustaining life or subsistence

Examples

The humanitarian aid focused on the sustainment of local communities during the crisis.
Fishing was their primary source of sustainment throughout the harsh winter months.
The mission's success depended on the careful sustainment of food and water supplies.

Synset Examples

they were in want of sustenance
fishing was their main sustainment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sustenance - the maintaining of someone or something in life or existence, especially food or nourishment

Etymology

From sustain + -ment, based on Middle English and Old French roots.
