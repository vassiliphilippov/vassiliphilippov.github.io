to coronate

1. VERB

/ ˈkɔrəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ə.neɪt / (BrE)
to invest with royal power; to crown or enthrone as a monarch
to crown or enthrone as monarch

Examples

The new king will be coronated in a grand ceremony next month.
They coronated her as queen after the previous monarch abdicated the throne.
The tradition of coronating rulers dates back centuries in many cultures.

Synset Examples

The prince was crowned in Westminster Abbey

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crown - to ceremonially invest with regal power
  • Antonym: to dethrone - to remove from the position of monarch

Etymology

From Latin 'coronare', meaning 'to crown'.
