Roanoke

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊ.əˌnoʊk / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.əˌnəʊk / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Virginia, United States, known for its location in the Blue Ridge Mountains.
city in southwestern Virginia, USA

Examples

Roanoke is famous for its iconic star that overlooks the city from Mill Mountain.
After moving to Roanoke, she quickly fell in love with the vibrant arts scene there.
The drive from Washington, D.C., to Roanoke offers beautiful views of the Blue Ridge Parkway.

Etymology

From an Algonquian word meaning 'shell money', referring to wampum.
