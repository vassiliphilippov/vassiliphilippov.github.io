pessimal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛsɪməl / (AmE), / ˈpɛsɪməl / (BrE)
(of an environment or condition) least favorable or most disadvantageous for survival or functioning.
least favorable condition

Examples

Temperatures below freezing are considered pessimal for the growth of this species.
Plants exposed to pessimal light conditions showed a significant decline in health.
Rapid environmental change can push organisms into a pessimal state for survival.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: optimal - most favorable or advantageous condition

Etymology

From Latin 'pessimus' meaning 'worst', modeled after 'optimal'.
