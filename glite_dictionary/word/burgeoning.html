burgeoning

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɝːdʒənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdʒənɪŋ / (BrE)
beginning to grow or develop rapidly; expanding quickly
growing or developing rapidly

Examples

The city's burgeoning tech industry has attracted young professionals from across the country.
There is concern about the burgeoning population putting strain on local resources.
The museum showcases the burgeoning art scene emerging in the region.

Synset Examples

flourishing businesses

the burgeoning population

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thriving - growing or developing successfully and vigorously
  • Synonym: flourishing - developing rapidly and successfully; thriving
  • Antonym: declining - becoming smaller, fewer, or less; decreasing

Etymology

From Old French 'bourgeonner' (to bud, sprout), from 'bourgeon' (bud).
