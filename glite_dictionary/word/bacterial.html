bacterial

1. ADJ

/ bækˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / bækˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by bacteria.
relating to bacteria

Examples

The doctor diagnosed a bacterial infection in her throat.
Antibiotics are used to treat bacterial diseases but not viral ones.
Researchers are studying bacterial growth under different environmental conditions.

Synset Examples

bacterial infection

Related Terms

  • Antonym: viral - relating to or caused by a virus

Etymology

From 'bacteria', which derives from Latin, itself from Greek 'bakterion' (little staff, rod).
