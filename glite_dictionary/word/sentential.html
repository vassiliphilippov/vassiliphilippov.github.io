sentential

1. ADJ

/ sɛnˈtɛnʃəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛnʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or forming a sentence; pertaining to the structure or function of a sentence.
relating to a sentence

Examples

In linguistics, a sentential adverb modifies an entire sentence rather than a single word.
The judge emphasized the sentential structure in the disputed legal text.
We discussed the sentential subject and its grammatical role in the clause.

Synset Examples

the sentential subject

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentence - a set of words that expresses a complete thought

Etymology

From Medieval Latin sententialis, from Latin sententia (sentence, opinion).
