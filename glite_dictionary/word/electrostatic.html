electrostatic

1. ADJ

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by static electricity, or producing static electric charges
relating to static electricity

Examples

The electrostatic force can cause small particles to stick to surfaces.
An electrostatic precipitator is used to remove dust from industrial exhaust gases.
Please be careful with electrostatic sensitive devices when assembling the computer.

Synset Examples

an electrostatic generator produces high-voltage static electricity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrical - relating to electricity in general
  • Antonym: magnetic - relating to or caused by magnetism

Etymology

From electro- (relating to electricity) + static (relating to stationary charges).
