Cape Girardeau

1. PROPN

/ ˌkeɪp dʒɪˈrɑːrdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪp dʒɪˈrɑːrdəʊ / (BrE)
A city in southeast Missouri, United States, located on the Mississippi River; the sixteenth largest city in Missouri.
city in southeast Missouri, U.S.

Examples

She moved to Cape Girardeau for her new job at the university.
After the flood, volunteers from Cape Girardeau helped with relief efforts in nearby towns.
The historic downtown of Cape Girardeau attracts many tourists each year.

Etymology

From French, meaning 'Cape Girard', named for Jean Baptiste de Girardot, an early French settler.
