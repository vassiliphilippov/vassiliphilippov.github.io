cps

1. NOUN

/ si pi ɛs / (AmE), / siː piː ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for cycles per second, a former unit of frequency equivalent to one hertz
cycles per second; unit of frequency

Examples

The signal was measured at 60 cps, matching the local power frequency.
Older equipment often lists frequencies in cps rather than hertz.
A frequency of 1,000 cps is equivalent to 1 kilohertz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hertz - unit of frequency equal to one cycle per second

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'cycles per second'.
