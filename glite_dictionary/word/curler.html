curler

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɝːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːlə / (BrE)
a small cylindrical device around which hair is wound to create curls or waves
device for curling hair

Examples

She left the house wearing a single curler in her hair, much to her embarrassment.
After washing her hair, Jane put in her curlers and waited for it to dry.
The old beauty salon was filled with women gossiping under domed dryers and wearing curlers.

Synset Examples

a woman with her head full of curlers is not a pretty sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roller - a device used similarly to curl hair; often used interchangeably

Etymology

From 'curl' + '-er', meaning something that causes curling.
