Ayapana

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪ.əˈpɑː.nə / (AmE), / ˌaɪ.əˈpɑː.nə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American herbs, sometimes classified within the genus Eupatorium.
genus of tropical American herbs

Examples

The medicinal properties of Ayapana have been used in traditional remedies for centuries.
Several species within the Ayapana genus are known for their aromatic leaves.
Botanists debated whether Ayapana should be classified as a separate genus or included in Eupatorium.
