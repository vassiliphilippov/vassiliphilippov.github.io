dairymaid

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛrimeɪd / (AmE), / ˈdeəriˌmeɪd / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to work in a dairy, especially one who makes or looks after milk and milk products
woman working in a dairy

Examples

The dairymaid rose before dawn to milk the cows every morning.
In the old estate, the dairymaid was responsible for churning butter and making cheese.
Her grandmother once worked as a dairymaid on a large farm in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairywoman - a woman who works in a dairy
  • Antonym: dairyman - a man who works in a dairy

Etymology

From 'dairy' + 'maid', where 'maid' means a female servant or worker.
