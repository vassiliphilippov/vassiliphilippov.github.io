smooth-skinned

1. ADJ

/ smuð skɪnd / (AmE), / smuːð skɪnd / (BrE)
having skin that is smooth to the touch or in appearance, without roughness or blemishes.
having smooth skin

Examples

The peaches were smooth-skinned and soft, perfect for eating fresh.
She was often complimented for her smooth-skinned complexion.
Some frogs are smooth-skinned, while others have a bumpy texture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rough-skinned - having rough or coarse skin
