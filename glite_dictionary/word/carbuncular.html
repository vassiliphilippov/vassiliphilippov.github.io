carbuncular

1. ADJ

/ kɑrˈbʌŋ.kjələr / (AmE), / kɑːˈbʌŋ.kjʊlə / (BrE)
afflicted with or resembling a carbuncle; relating to or characterized by carbuncles
relating to or resembling a carbuncle
medical

Examples

The patient developed a carbuncular swelling on his neck that required immediate medical attention.
The medieval chronicler described the king's mysterious illness as marked by carbuncular lesions.
His skin had a carbuncular appearance after weeks of untreated infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carbuncle - a painful cluster of boils caused by bacterial infection

Etymology

From Latin 'carbunculus' (small coal, carbuncle) with suffix '-ar'.
