allotted

1. ADJ

/ əˈlɑːtɪd / (AmE), / əˈlɒtɪd / (BrE)
given or assigned as a duty, share, or task
assigned as a task or portion

Examples

Each student must complete their allotted reading before class.
She worked late to finish the allotted tasks on her list.
We met every deadline within the allotted time frame.

Synset Examples

her allotted chores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assigned - given as a task or responsibility

Etymology

From the verb 'allot', from Old French 'aloter', from a- (to) + 'lot' (portion, share).
