Jose Julian Marti

1. PROPN

/ hoʊˈseɪ huːˈliːən mɑːrˈtiː / (AmE), / həʊˈseɪ huːˈliːən mɑːˈtiː / (BrE)
(1853–1895) Cuban poet and revolutionary leader, known for his role in the fight for Cuban independence from Spain.
Cuban poet and independence leader

Examples

Many schools and monuments in Cuba are named after Jose Julian Marti.
As a national hero, Jose Julian Marti is celebrated every year on January 28th.
Historians praise Jose Julian Marti for his literary works and political activism.
