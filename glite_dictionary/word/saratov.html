Saratov

1. PROPN

/ səˈrɑːtɑv / (AmE), / səˈrɑːtɒf / (BrE)
an industrial city on the Volga River in the European part of Russia; the administrative center of Saratov Oblast.
industrial city in Russia, on the Volga River

Examples

The city of Saratov is known for its rich cultural heritage and thriving industries.
She traveled by train from Moscow to Saratov to visit her relatives.
Many scientists and artists were born in Saratov over the past century.

Etymology

From Russian Саратов (Saratov), the city's name.
