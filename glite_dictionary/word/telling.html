2. NOUN

/ ˈtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of informing, revealing, or giving information by words
the act of informing by words

Examples

The telling of the story captivated the audience all night.
His telling of the events was detailed and honest.
The telling of the secret changed their friendship forever.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: informing - the act of giving information
  • Antonym: concealing - the act of keeping something secret

Etymology

Derived from the verb 'tell', from Old English 'tellan' (to count, recount, relate).
