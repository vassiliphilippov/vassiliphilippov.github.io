xciv

1. NUM

/ ɛks siː aɪ viː / (AmE), / ɛks siː aɪ viː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing ninety-four (94)
Roman numeral for 94

Examples

Chapter xciv of the ancient manuscript detailed the legendary battle.
The inscription on the monument read 'Anno Domini XCIV'.
Page xciv begins with an illustration from the medieval period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 94 - the cardinal number ninety-four

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), C (100), IV (4); in Roman numerals, XC = 90, IV = 4.
