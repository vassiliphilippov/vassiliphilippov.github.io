moonflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːnˌflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A tropical or subtropical climbing plant that produces large, fragrant, white flowers which bloom at night.
climbing plant with nocturnal white flowers

Examples

The moonflower blooms at dusk, filling the garden with a sweet scent through the night.
She planted a moonflower vine to enjoy its beautiful white blossoms every evening.
Many pollinators are attracted to the moonflower because of its fragrance and nighttime blooming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morning glory - climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom during the day

Etymology

From 'moon' (referring to night) + 'flower', describing its nocturnal blooming habit.
