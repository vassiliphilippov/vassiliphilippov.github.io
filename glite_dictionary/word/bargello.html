bargello

1. NOUN

/ bɑrˈʤɛloʊ / (AmE), / bɑːˈʤɛləʊ / (BrE)
a needlepoint embroidery stitch that produces zigzag patterns, often used to create vibrant, geometric designs
needlepoint embroidery stitch with zigzag pattern

Examples

She decorated the cushion with a classic bargello design in shades of blue and green.
Learning the bargello stitch can add unique texture and movement to your embroidery projects.
The exhibit showcased several 18th-century tapestries featuring intricate bargello patterns.

Etymology

From Italian Bargello, possibly named after a Florentine palace where this embroidery style was used.
