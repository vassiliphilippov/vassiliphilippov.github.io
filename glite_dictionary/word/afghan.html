afghan

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfˌɡæn / (AmE), / ˈæf.ɡæn / (BrE)
a long, usually sheepskin coat with a soft wool lining, traditionally associated with Afghanistan
sheepskin coat

Examples

He wore an afghan over his clothes to keep warm in the winter.
Her vintage afghan was decorated with elaborate embroidery.
They bought a handmade afghan at the bazaar when traveling through Central Asia.

Etymology

Named after Afghanistan, where this style of coat originated.

2. NOUN

/ ˈæfɡæn / (AmE), / ˈæfɡæn / (BrE)
a blanket, usually knitted or crocheted in strips or squares, often used as a covering or sometimes as a shawl
knitted or crocheted blanket

Examples

My grandmother made a colorful afghan for the living room sofa.
She wrapped the handmade afghan around her shoulders when she felt chilly.
The old afghan added a cozy touch to the guest room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throw - a light blanket used for warmth or decoration

Etymology

Named after Afghanistan, where similar textiles were traditionally made.
