at

1. NOUN

/ æt / (AmE), / æt / (BrE)
A former monetary unit of Laos, equal to one hundredth of a kip.
minor Lao monetary unit (obsolete)

Examples

The item cost fifty at in the local market.
During the 20th century, 100 at made up one kip in Laos.
Old coins denominated in at can still be found among collectors.
