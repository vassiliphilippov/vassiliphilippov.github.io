to double-glaze

1. VERB

/ ˌdʌblˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˌdʌblˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
to fit a window or building with two layers of glass to improve insulation
fit with two layers of glass

Examples

We decided to double-glaze all the windows to reduce heating costs.
Many modern homes double-glaze their windows for better energy efficiency.
The old cottage was recently double-glazed to keep out drafts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glaze - to fit glass into a window or door
