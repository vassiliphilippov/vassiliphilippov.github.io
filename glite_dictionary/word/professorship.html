professorship

1. NOUN

/ prəˈfɛsərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / prəˈfɛsəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or rank of a professor at a university or college
position or rank of a professor

Examples

After years of dedicated research, she finally earned a professorship at a prestigious university.
The university established a new professorship in renewable energy technology.
He resigned from his professorship to focus on writing and consulting.

Synset Examples

he was awarded an endowed chair in economics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chair - a professorship, especially a distinguished one

Etymology

From 'professor' + suffix '-ship', denoting state or condition.
