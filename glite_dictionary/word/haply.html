haply

1. ADV

/ ˈhæpli / (AmE), / ˈhapli / (BrE)
by chance; accidentally; perhaps or possibly (used especially in literary or archaic contexts)
by chance; perhaps (archaic)
archaic

Examples

On a quiet evening, she found the old letter haply tucked inside a book.
He may haply return before the sun sets, though none can say for certain.
The news haply reached the king before his departure.

Synset Examples

betrayed by a word haply overheard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perhaps - possibly; maybe
  • Synonym: by chance - happening accidentally or without intention

Etymology

From Middle English hap (chance, luck) + -ly, meaning 'by chance.'
