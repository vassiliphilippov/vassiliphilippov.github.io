pappa

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæpə / (AmE), / ˈpapə / (BrE)
An informal term for a father, often used by young children.
informal term for father
informal

Examples

When I was a child, I used to run to my pappa every time I was scared.
She drew a picture for her pappa on Father's Day.
Little Timmy called out for his pappa as soon as he woke up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dad - informal term for father
  • Synonym: papa - another variant of the informal term for father
  • Synonym: father - male parent

Etymology

Imitative in origin, from infantile speech; similar forms found in many languages.
