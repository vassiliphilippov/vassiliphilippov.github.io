uneasily

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈiːzəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːzəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows worry, discomfort, or anxiety.
with worry or discomfort

Examples

She glanced uneasily around the crowded room, searching for a familiar face.
He shifted uneasily in his seat during the tense discussion.
The students waited uneasily for the results to be announced.

Synset Examples

we watched anxiously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiously - in a way that shows anxiety or worry
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or relaxed manner
