arcdegree

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrk.dɪˌɡriː / (AmE), / ˈɑːk.dɪˌɡriː / (BrE)
A unit of measurement for angles, equal to 1/360 of a full rotation.
unit of angle measurement

Examples

The central angle of the sector is 45 arcdegrees.
One full rotation is equal to 360 arcdegrees in geometry.
You can convert radians to arcdegrees using a simple formula.

Synset Examples

there are 360 degrees in a circle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: degree - A unit of measurement for angles or temperature

Etymology

From 'arc' (part of a curve) and 'degree' (unit of measurement), used in geometry.
