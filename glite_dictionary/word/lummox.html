lummox

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌməks / (AmE), / ˈlʌməks / (BrE)
A clumsy, awkward, or unintelligent person.
clumsy or awkward person

Examples

He can be such a lummox sometimes, always knocking things over.
Don't be a lummox—watch where you're going!
She called her brother a lummox after he tripped on the stairs again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things
  • Synonym: oaf - a stupid, uncultured, or clumsy person

Etymology

Origin unknown; possibly an alteration of dialectal 'lummock' (to move heavily).
