informative

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfɔrmətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
providing useful information or knowledge; giving facts or details that help to instruct or enlighten
providing useful information

Examples

The documentary was extremely informative and increased my understanding of climate change.
Her lecture on ancient history proved to be both engaging and informative.
Thank you for your informative feedback—it helped me improve my report.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - providing education or instruction
  • Antonym: entertaining - amusing or enjoyable rather than instructive

Etymology

From Latin informare (to form, shape, instruct) via Medieval Latin informativus.

2. ADJ

providing useful or interesting information; giving knowledge or insight.
providing information or knowledge

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - providing education or related to teaching and learning
  • Antonym: uninformative - not providing useful or interesting information

Etymology

3. ADJ

providing useful information

Examples

I always look for informative articles when researching a new topic.

  • Synonym: educational - providing education or serving to educate
  • Antonym: uninformative - not providing useful or interesting information

Etymology
