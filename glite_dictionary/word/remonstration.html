remonstration

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛmənsˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of expressing forceful opposition or protest, especially formally or earnestly
forceful protest or objection

Examples

The workers' remonstration against the new policy was ignored by management.
Despite her father's remonstration, she decided to travel alone.
His letter of remonstration outlined all of his concerns in great detail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protest - a statement or action expressing disapproval or objection
  • Synonym: objection - an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition

Etymology

From Latin 'remonstratio' (a showing, pointing out), from 'remonstrare' (to show, point out).
