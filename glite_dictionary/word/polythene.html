polythene

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːliˌθin / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪˌθiːn / (BrE)
a lightweight, flexible, and durable thermoplastic material, mainly used for packaging, insulation, and plastic bags
lightweight plastic material used for packaging

Examples

The groceries were packed in a polythene bag to prevent them from getting wet.
Many households recycle polythene wrappers to reduce plastic waste.
Old polythene sheets can be reused for covering and protecting furniture during painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polyethylene - a polymer of ethylene, also known as polythene

Etymology

Shortened from 'polyethylene', referring to the polymer of ethylene.
