to fornicate

1. VERB

/ ˈfɔrnɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to have sexual intercourse with someone who is not your spouse, especially outside marriage
have sex outside marriage

Examples

The law in some countries punishes those who fornicate outside marriage.
The preacher condemned those who fornicate as sinners.
They were accused of having fornicated during their travels abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adultery - voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and someone who is not their spouse
  • Antonym: to marry - to become legally joined as husband and wife; to wed

Etymology

From Latin fornicarī (to have illicit intercourse), from fornix (arch, vault, a place where prostitutes waited).
