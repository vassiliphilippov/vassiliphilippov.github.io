outerwear

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊ.tɚˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈaʊ.təˌwɛə / (BrE)
clothing worn outdoors over other clothes, such as coats, jackets, or raincoats
clothing worn outdoors over other clothes

Examples

She hung her outerwear in the closet as soon as she entered the house.
During the winter, thick outerwear is essential to stay warm outside.
The store offers a wide selection of outerwear for hiking and camping trips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat - a garment worn on the upper body outdoors, typically with sleeves
  • Synonym: jacket - a short coat worn outdoors
  • Antonym: underwear - clothing worn under other clothes

Etymology

From 'outer' (outside) + 'wear' (clothes for a particular use).
