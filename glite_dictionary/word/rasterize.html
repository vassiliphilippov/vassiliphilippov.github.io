to rasterize

1. VERB

/ ˈræstəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɑːstəraɪz / (BrE)
To convert (an image or vector graphic) into a raster image made up of pixels.
convert an image to pixels

Examples

The graphics editor can rasterize vector images for use on webpages.
After you rasterize the logo, you won't be able to edit the layers individually.
He accidentally rasterized the text, so he couldn't change the font anymore.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vectorize - to convert an image to a vector format

Etymology

From 'raster' (a grid of pixels) + '-ize' (verb-forming suffix).
