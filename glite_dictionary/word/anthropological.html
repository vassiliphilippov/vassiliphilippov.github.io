anthropological

1. ADJ

/ ˌænθrəpəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌænθrəpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the scientific study of human beings, their societies, customs, and cultures (anthropology)
relating to anthropology

Examples

She specializes in anthropological research on indigenous communities.
The museum houses many anthropological artifacts from ancient civilizations.
His anthropological study revealed new insights into family structures.

Synset Examples

anthropological studies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropology - the scientific study of humans, societies, and cultures

Etymology

From anthropology + -ical, ultimately from Greek anthrōpos (human) + -logia (study).
