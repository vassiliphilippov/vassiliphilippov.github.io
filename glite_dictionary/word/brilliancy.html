brilliancy

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪliənsi / (AmE), / ˈbrɪliənsi / (BrE)
The quality of being exceptionally bright, impressive, or outstanding.
exceptional brightness or excellence

Examples

The brilliancy of her performance captivated the entire audience.
The diamond was admired for its exceptional brilliancy under the spotlight.
The brilliancy of his idea gave the team a new direction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliance - exceptional brightness or excellence

Etymology

From French 'brillance', from 'briller' (to shine), ultimately from Latin 'beryllus' (beryl, a precious stone).
