dysfunction

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
Impaired or abnormal functioning of an organ, system, or social group.
impaired or abnormal functioning
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with erectile dysfunction.
Family dysfunction can affect children's emotional development.
Liver dysfunction was indicated by the abnormal blood tests.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malfunction - failure to function properly
  • Antonym: function - normal or proper action of something

Etymology

From Greek dys- (bad, difficult) + Latin functio (performance).
