Quadragesima

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɑːdrəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (AmE), / ˌkwɒdrəˈdʒɛsɪmə / (BrE)
the first Sunday in Lent, especially in the Christian liturgical calendar
first Sunday in Lent

Examples

Many traditional hymns are sung during Quadragesima to mark the beginning of Lent.
The church was especially decorated for the service on Quadragesima.
In medieval times, Quadragesima was observed with special fasting and prayer.

Etymology

From Latin 'quadragesima' (fortieth), referring to the fortieth day before Easter.
