tremendous

1. ADJ

/ trəˈmɛndəs / (AmE), / trɪˈmɛndəs / (BrE)
Extremely good, impressive, or great in amount, quality, or degree.
extremely great or impressive

Examples

She did a tremendous job organizing the conference.
The team made tremendous progress this year.
His performance was met with tremendous applause from the audience.

Synset Examples

a fantastic trip to the Orient
the film was fantastic!
a howling success
a marvelous collection of rare books
had a rattling conversation about politics
a tremendous achievement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marvelous - extremely good; wonderful
  • Synonym: fantastic - extraordinarily good or attractive
  • Antonym: terrible - very bad; causing fear or terror

Etymology

From Latin 'tremendus', meaning 'to be trembled at', from 'tremere' (to tremble).
