Crateva

1. PROPN

/ krəˈtiːvə / (AmE), / krəˈtiːvə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees or shrubs in the family Capparaceae, found in Asia, Africa, and the Americas.
genus of tropical trees or shrubs

Examples

Crateva species are known for their ornamental flowers and medicinal properties.
The leaves of Crateva are used in traditional remedies across many cultures.
Botanists discovered a new variety within the Crateva genus in tropical Africa.

Etymology

From Latin 'Crataeva', named after the Greek botanist Cratevas.
