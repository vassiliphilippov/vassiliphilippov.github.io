azygos

1. ADJ

/ ˈæzɪˌɡɑs / (AmE), / ˈæzɪˌɡɒs / (BrE)
(of an anatomical part) occurring singly; not one of a pair.
occurring singly; not paired (anatomy)
medical

Examples

The azygos vein is a major vessel draining blood from the chest wall.
An azygos lobe is a rare anatomical variation in the right lung.
The muscle is azygos, meaning it does not have a counterpart on the opposite side.

Synset Examples

the azygous muscle of the uvula

Etymology

From Greek 'azygos', meaning 'unpaired'; 'a-' (not) + 'zygon' (yoke, pair).
