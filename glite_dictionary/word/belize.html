Belize

1. PROPN

/ bəˈliz / (AmE), / bɛˈliːz / (BrE)
A country in Central America, bordering the Caribbean Sea, formerly a British colony.
country in Central America

Examples

My friends traveled to Belize to enjoy the beautiful beaches and explore the Mayan ruins.
The official language of Belize is English, making it unique in Central America.
Several endangered species can be found in the rainforests of Belize.
