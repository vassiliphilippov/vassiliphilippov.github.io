splitsville

1. NOUN

/ ˈsplɪtsˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈsplɪtsvɪl / (BrE)
(informal) The end of a romantic relationship, especially a breakup or divorce.
end of a relationship (informal)
informal slang

Examples

After their big argument, it looked like they were headed straight for splitsville.
Once trust is lost in a relationship, sometimes splitsville is inevitable.
She joked with her friends about her frequent trips to splitsville.

Synset Examples

after 15 years together they are headed for splitsville

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakup - the end of a romantic relationship; separation
  • Synonym: separation - ending a relationship or marriage

Etymology

Formed from 'split' meaning to separate and humorous use of the suffix '-ville' to mean a place or situation.
