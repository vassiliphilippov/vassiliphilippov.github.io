Sir Yehudi Menuhin

1. PROPN

/ jəˈhudi mɛnˈjuːɪn / (AmE), / jəˈhuːdi ˈmɛnjuːɪn / (BrE)
(1916–1999) A celebrated British violinist, born in the United States, renowned for his virtuosity and contributions to classical music.
British violinist and child prodigy

Examples

Sir Yehudi Menuhin captivated audiences around the world with his extraordinary talent and expressive performances.
The concert hall was named in honor of Sir Yehudi Menuhin, a legendary figure in classical music.
As a child prodigy, Sir Yehudi Menuhin began touring internationally when he was just a young boy.
