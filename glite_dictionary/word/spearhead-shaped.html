spearhead-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɪrˌhɛd ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspɪəˌhɛd ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
(of a leaf or object) having a shape like that of a spearhead, with a pointed tip and flaring pointed lobes at the base.
shaped like a spearhead

Examples

The plant is known for its distinctive spearhead-shaped leaves that stand out among the foliage.
The artist designed a spearhead-shaped pendant for the new jewelry collection.
We noticed several spearhead-shaped marks on the rock, possibly from ancient tools.
