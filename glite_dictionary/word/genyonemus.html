Genyonemus

1. NOUN

/ d͡ʒɛn.joʊˈniː.məs / (AmE), / d͡ʒɛn.jəˈniː.məs / (BrE)
A genus of marine fish in the family Sciaenidae, including the white croaker and related species.
genus of marine fish (white croaker, etc.)

Examples

The Genyonemus genus includes the commonly found white croaker along the Pacific coast.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of Genyonemus species in their natural habitat.
Fishermen often catch Genyonemus during the summer months near California beaches.

Etymology

From Greek *genys* (jaw) and *nema* (thread), referring to distinguishing features of the genus.
