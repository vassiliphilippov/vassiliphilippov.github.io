unsoldierly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsoʊldʒɚli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊldʒəli / (BrE)
Not conforming to the standards or behavior expected of a soldier; lacking military discipline or bearing.
not conforming to military standards

Examples

His unsoldierly conduct drew criticism from his commanding officer.
The recruits were reprimanded for their unsoldierly appearance during inspection.
She noticed his unsoldierly posture as soon as he entered the room.

Synset Examples

unsoldierly posture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soldierly - having qualities befitting a soldier

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'soldierly' (having the qualities of a soldier).
