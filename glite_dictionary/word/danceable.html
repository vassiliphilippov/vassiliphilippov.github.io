danceable

1. ADJ

/ ˈdænsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsəbəl / (BrE)
(of music) having a rhythm or quality suitable for dancing to.
suitable for dancing (music)

Examples

The new song has a very danceable beat that quickly gets people on the floor.
Her playlist includes only the most danceable tracks from the 1980s.
Critics praised the album for its catchy, danceable melodies.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undanceable - not suitable for dancing

Etymology

From the verb 'dance' + suffix '-able', meaning 'able to be'.
