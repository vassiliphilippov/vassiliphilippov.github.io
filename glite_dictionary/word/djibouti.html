Djibouti

1. PROPN

/ dʒiˈbuːti / (AmE), / dʒɪˈbuːti / (BrE)
A country in northeastern Africa, located on the Somali Peninsula, bordering the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aden.
country in northeastern Africa

Examples

She traveled to Djibouti for an international conference on regional security.
Djibouti is known for its strategic location along the Red Sea.
The capital city of Djibouti shares the same name as the country itself.

Etymology

From the French rendering of the Afar name Gabuuti.

2. PROPN

/ dʒɪˈbuːti / (AmE), / dʒɪˈbuːti / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Djibouti, located on the Gulf of Aden; also an important port city in East Africa
capital and largest city of Djibouti

Examples

She traveled to Djibouti to experience its unique culture and bustling markets.
The port of Djibouti is vital for shipping goods in East Africa.
Many international organizations have offices in Djibouti due to its strategic location.

Etymology

From Afar and Somali languages; also the name of the country.
