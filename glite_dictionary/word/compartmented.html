compartmented

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpɑːrt.men.tɪd / (AmE), / kəmˈpɑːt.mənt.ɪd / (BrE)
divided or separated into compartments or distinct sections
divided into compartments

Examples

The museum stored its artifacts in a compartmented cabinet for easy organization.
Modern offices often have compartmented workspaces separated by dividers.
Her life felt compartmented, with strict boundaries between work and personal time.

Synset Examples

a compartmented box
the protected and compartmented society of Beacon Hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sectioned - divided into sections
  • Synonym: partitioned - divided by partition or dividing walls

Etymology

From 'compartment' + '-ed', indicating having compartments.
