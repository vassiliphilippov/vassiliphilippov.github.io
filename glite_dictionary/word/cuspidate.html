cuspidate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌs.pɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkʌs.pɪ.deɪt / (BrE)
having a point or pointed ends; shaped like a cusp.
having points or cusps

Examples

The leaf is cuspidate, ending in a sharp tip.
Some teeth in carnivorous animals are distinctly cuspidate.
The cactus has cuspidate spines that protect it from herbivores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointed - sharply tapered; having a tip or point

Etymology

From Latin 'cuspidatus' (pointed), from 'cuspis' (point, tip).
