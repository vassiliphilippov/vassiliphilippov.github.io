Dasht-e Kavir

1. PROPN

/ dɑʃt eɪ kɑːˈvɪr / (AmE), / dæʃt eɪ kəˈvɪə / (BrE)
a large salt desert in north-central Iran, known for its arid climate and salt marshes
salt desert in north-central Iran

Examples

Scientists studied the unique ecosystem of the Dasht-e Kavir for climate research.
Crossing the Dasht-e Kavir requires preparation for extreme heat and arid conditions.
Satellite images show the vast, cracked salt flats of the Dasht-e Kavir in Iran.

Etymology

From Persian 'Dasht-e Kavir' meaning 'great salt desert'.
