Adelaide

1. PROPN

/ ˈædəˌleɪd / (AmE), / ˈædəleɪd / (BrE)
the capital city of South Australia, situated on the southern coast of the country
capital city of South Australia

Examples

I spent two weeks in Adelaide exploring its art galleries and beaches.
The climate in Adelaide is known for being Mediterranean, with hot summers and mild winters.
Adelaide is famous for its many festivals and vibrant food scene.

Etymology

Named after Queen Adelaide, consort of King William IV of the United Kingdom.
