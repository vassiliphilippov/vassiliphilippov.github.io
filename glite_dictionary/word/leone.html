leone

1. NOUN

/ liˈoʊneɪ / (AmE), / lɛˈəʊneɪ / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Sierra Leone, equal to 100 cents
currency unit of Sierra Leone

Examples

The price of rice increased to 20,000 leone per bag last month.
She exchanged her dollars for leone at the airport in Freetown.
The leone weakened against the US dollar following the election.
