self-worship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlf ˌwɜːrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɛlf ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
excessive admiration or adoration of oneself; the act of worshipping oneself
excessive admiration of oneself

Examples

His self-worship eventually drove away even his closest friends.
The novel explores the dangers of self-worship in a modern, image-obsessed society.
She mistook her self-worship for healthy self-confidence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcissism - excessive self-love or self-centeredness
