chef

1. NOUN

/ ʃɛf / (AmE), / ʃɛf / (BrE)
A professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant or hotel kitchen.
professional cook

Examples

The hotel chef designed a special menu for the wedding reception.
As a talented chef, she prides herself on using only fresh, local ingredients.
He worked as a pastry chef in a famous Parisian restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cook - a person who prepares food

Etymology

From French 'chef' (chief, head), shortened from 'chef de cuisine' (head of the kitchen).
