masjid

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæs.dʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈmas.dʒɪd / (BrE)
A Muslim place of worship; mosque.
Muslim place of worship

Examples

The community gathers at the masjid every Friday for prayers.
A new masjid was built to accommodate the growing Muslim population.
He visited the historic masjid during his travels in Turkey.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mosque - a building where Muslims worship

Etymology

From Arabic *masjid* (place of prostration), from the root s-j-d (to prostrate oneself).
