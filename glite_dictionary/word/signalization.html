signalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪɡ.nələˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡ.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a noticeable sign or indication that draws attention to something
a conspicuous indication or sign

Examples

The bright colors serve as a signalization of danger in the animal kingdom.
Effective signalization on the roads helps prevent accidents.
Her unusual behavior was a clear signalization that something was wrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign - a symbol or object that gives information, direction, or a warning

Etymology

From 'signal' + '-ization', formed after French 'signalisation'.
