bedded

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of rock or soil) deposited or arranged in distinct horizontal layers.
deposited in horizontal layers

Examples

The limestone in this region is bedded horizontally, making it easy to excavate.
Geologists studied the bedded sandstone to understand the area's history.
The construction crew discovered bedded clay deposits while digging the foundation.

Synset Examples

stratified rock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stratified - arranged in layers

Etymology

From the verb 'bed' + -ed, meaning to lay in layers or beds.

2. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a room or accommodation) having a specified number or type of beds.
having a specified number or type of beds

Examples

We booked a single-bedded room for our stay in Paris.
The hotel offers several double-bedded suites for families.
She requested a twin-bedded room for her and her colleague.
