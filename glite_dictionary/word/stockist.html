stockist

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑː.kɪst / (AmE), / ˈstɒk.ɪst / (BrE)
a retailer or distributor that keeps a supply of particular goods for sale or distribution
retailer/distributor who stocks goods

Examples

We are the exclusive stockist of this brand in the city.
The website can help you find your nearest stockist.
Many local stockists offer home delivery services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods to the public in small quantities
  • Synonym: distributor - a person or organization that supplies goods to stores and other businesses

Etymology

From 'stock' + '-ist', denoting one who deals in stock (goods).
