nerk

1. NOUN

/ nɜrk / (AmE), / nɜːk / (BrE)
an insulting or mocking term for a silly or foolish person; a fool
insulting term for a silly person
slang british

Examples

He got called a nerk after tripping over the same step twice.
Don’t be such a nerk, it was just a harmless joke!
She laughed and playfully called her brother a nerk for forgetting his keys again.

Synset Examples

She called him a nerk for his lack of enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nerd - a person seen as overly studious or socially awkward
  • Synonym: jerk - a contemptibly obnoxious person
