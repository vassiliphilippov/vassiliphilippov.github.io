belt

1. NOUN

/ bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
A strip of leather, cloth, or other material worn around the waist to support clothing or as a fashion accessory.
strip worn around the waist

Examples

He tightened his belt before leaving the house.
The dress looked even better with a black belt around the waist.
She bought a leather belt to match her new shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girdle - a belt or cord worn around the waist

Etymology

From Old English 'bælt' (belt, girdle), from Latin 'balteus' (belt, baldric).
