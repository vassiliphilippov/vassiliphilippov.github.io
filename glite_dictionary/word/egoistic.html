egoistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
thinking only of yourself and what is best for you, rather than considering other people; selfish or self-centered.
thinking only of yourself; selfish

Examples

Her egoistic attitude often made collaboration within the team difficult.
It was an egoistic decision that ignored the needs of everyone else involved.
People who are overly egoistic frequently find themselves isolated from friends and family.

Synset Examples

Selfish men were … trying to make capital for themselves out of the sacred cause of civil rights
The merely egoistic satisfactions of fame are easily nullified
However, because of their strong-minded nature, they are often perceived as difficult and egoistical individuals.
I will never forgive her for her egocentric and shameful conduct towards Gilliatt
What I can't believe is that people are so stupid and self-centered that they think they HAVE to comment on things they don't like.
We're asked to see Rachel as this spoilt, self-centred woman but the rest of them are just as bad, if not worse.
As the crying baby, we express purely the self-serving thoughts that arise in our minds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with personal profit or pleasure
  • Antonym: altruistic - showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish

Etymology

From French 'égoïste', from Latin 'ego' (I, self).
