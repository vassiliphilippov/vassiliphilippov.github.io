jobcentre

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑbˌsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒbˌsɛntə / (BrE)
In the UK, a government office where information about job vacancies is displayed and unemployment benefits are administered.
UK government office for jobs and benefits
british

Examples

After being made redundant, he visited the jobcentre to search for new employment opportunities.
The jobcentre provides support for people looking for work and those claiming unemployment benefits.
Many recent graduates check the jobcentre listings when starting their job search.
