unwatchful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɑtʃfəɫ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒtʃfʊl / (BrE)
not noticing or alert to potential danger or problems; lacking watchfulness.
not alert or vigilant

Examples

The hikers got lost because their unwatchful guide failed to notice the trail markers.
An unwatchful pet owner might overlook signs of illness in their animal.
She was unwatchful for just a moment, and her purse was stolen.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: watchful - paying close attention to possible danger or problems
  • Synonym: vigilant - keeping careful watch for possible danger

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'watchful'.
