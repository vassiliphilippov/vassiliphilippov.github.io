Md

1. NOUN

/ ɛm di / (AmE), / ɛm di / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for mendelevium, a synthetic radioactive metallic element with the atomic number 101
symbol for mendelevium, element 101

Examples

The radioactive isotope Md-256 was first produced in 1955.
Scientists use Md for research purposes due to its rarity and instability.
The element Md is named after Dmitri Mendeleev.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mendelevium - a synthetic radioactive element (symbol Md, atomic number 101)

Etymology

From the name Mendeleev (after Dmitri Mendeleev), with the symbol Md assigned by IUPAC.
