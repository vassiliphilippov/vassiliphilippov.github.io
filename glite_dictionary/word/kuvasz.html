kuvasz

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuː.vɑs / (AmE), / ˈkuː.væz / (BrE)
a large, sturdy, white sheepdog breed from Hungary, traditionally used for guarding livestock and sometimes for hunting
Hungarian breed of large white dog

Examples

The family's new kuvasz quickly became a loyal guardian of their sheep.
We saw a beautiful white kuvasz at the dog show yesterday.
Traditionally, a kuvasz would protect rural Hungarian farms from predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: komondor - Hungarian breed of large, white, corded-haired livestock guardian dog

Etymology

From Hungarian 'kuvasz', denoting the breed.
