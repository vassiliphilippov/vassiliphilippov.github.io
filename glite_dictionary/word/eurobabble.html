Eurobabble

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʊroʊˌbæbəl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌbabəl / (BrE)
complex and often obscure jargon used in European Union documents and regulations, especially regarded as excessively bureaucratic or difficult to understand
jargon of EU documents and regulations

Examples

The report was written in Eurobabble that few outside Brussels could understand.
Many critics argue that Eurobabble alienates ordinary citizens from the EU process.
She struggled to translate the Eurobabble in the lengthy regulatory document.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jargon - specialized language used by a particular group

Etymology

Blend of 'Euro-' (relating to Europe or the European Union) and 'babble' (meaning incoherent speech).
