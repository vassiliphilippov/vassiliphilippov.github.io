swine

1. NOUN

/ swaɪn / (AmE), / swaɪn / (BrE)
a domesticated pig, or more generally any animal of the pig family; a stout-bodied, short-legged omnivorous mammal, especially of the genus Sus.
domesticated pig or pig family animal

Examples

The farmer raised several swine on his property for meat and sale.
Yesterday, we learned about how swine are omnivorous and intelligent animals.
The epidemic was traced back to a herd of domestic swine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pig - a domesticated animal of the species Sus scrofa domestica
  • Synonym: boar - a wild male pig or the species as a whole in the wild

Etymology

Old English swīn, related to Dutch zwijn, German Schwein, from Proto-Germanic *swīnan.
