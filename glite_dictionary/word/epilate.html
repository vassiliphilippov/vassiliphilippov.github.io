to epilate

1. VERB

/ ˈɛpəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪleɪt / (BrE)
to remove hair from the body by pulling it out at the roots
remove hair from the body

Examples

Some people choose to epilate their underarms instead of shaving.
The aesthetician will epilate your legs using a special device.
She epilated her upper lip to achieve a smooth finish.

Synset Examples

epilate her legs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to depilate - to remove hair from the body, especially by chemical means

Etymology

From Latin epilare, from 'e-' (out) + 'pilus' (hair).
