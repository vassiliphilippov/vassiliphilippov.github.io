to reillume

1. VERB

/ ˌriːɪˈluːm / (AmE), / ˌriːɪˈluːm / (BrE)
to light up again; to illuminate anew, often in a figurative or poetic sense
to light up again

Examples

The exhibition was so vibrant it seemed to reillume the entire gallery.
Her cheerful personality can reillume even the darkest of rooms.
After years of neglect, the restoration project hopes to reillume the community theatre.

Synset Examples

The artist's work seemed to reillume the gallery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to illuminate - to light up something

Etymology

From re- (again) and illume (from Latin illuminare, to light up).
