artillerywoman

1. NOUN

/ ɑːrˈtɪlərɪˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪlɪrɪˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who serves as a member of the artillery in the armed forces.
female member of the artillery

Examples

The first artillerywoman to reach the rank of captain was honored last week.
As an artillerywoman, she operated and maintained complex weaponry systems.
The army now encourages women interested in becoming an artillerywoman to apply for specialized training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artilleryman - a male or generic member of the artillery

Etymology

From 'artillery' + 'woman', modeled after forms like 'policeman/policewoman'.
