unexpired

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a period, document, or right) still valid or not yet ended by the passage of time.
still valid; not yet ended

Examples

You must renew your passport before your unexpired time runs out.
She was allowed to drive because her license was still unexpired.
He completed the unexpired portion of his predecessor's term.

Synset Examples

elected to fill the senator's unexpired term
an unexpired driver's license

Related Terms

  • Antonym: expired - no longer valid; ended by passage of time

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and expired (from Latin expirare, to breathe out/end).
