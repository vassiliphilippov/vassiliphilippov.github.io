trinucleate

1. ADJ

/ traɪˈnuːkliːət / (AmE), / traɪˈnjuːklɪət / (BrE)
(of a cell or structure) having three nuclei.
having three nuclei

Examples

Certain protozoan species have trinucleate stages during their life cycle.
The trinucleate condition was confirmed through microscopic analysis of the tissue samples.
Some fungal spores are trinucleate before undergoing further division.

Etymology

From Latin 'tri-' (three) + 'nucleus' (kernel, nucleus).
