Warji

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑr.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈwɑː.dʒi / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, particularly in Bauchi State and parts of Jigawa State
Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

Many people in northern Nigeria speak Warji as their first language.
Researchers have documented the vocabulary and grammar of Warji.
The Warji language is endangered due to increasing use of Hausa.
