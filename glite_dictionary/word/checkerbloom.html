checkerbloom

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkɚˌbluːm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəˌbluːm / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the mallow family, native to western North America, with purple flowers, sometimes grown as an ornamental
purple-flowered wild mallow of western North America

Examples

The checkerbloom blooms in early spring across California's grasslands.
Gardeners appreciate the checkerbloom for its vibrant purple flowers and easy maintenance.
We spotted several checkerbloom growing wild along the hiking trail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallow - any of various plants of the genus Malva, with five-petaled flowers
