Kanara

1. PROPN

/ kəˈnɑːrə / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːrə / (BrE)
a historical region on the southwest coast of India, known for its distinct culture and geography
historical region in southwest India

Examples

The coastal areas of Kanara are famous for their scenic beauty and beaches.
Many communities in Kanara speak Kannada and Konkani as their main languages.
Historians have documented the ancient trade routes passing through Kanara in detail.
