Saratoga Springs

1. PROPN

/ ˌsærəˈtoʊɡə ˈsprɪŋz / (AmE), / ˌsærəˈtəʊɡə ˈsprɪŋz / (BrE)
A town in eastern New York State, renowned for its mineral springs and horse racing events.
town in New York famous for springs and horse racing

Examples

Every summer, thousands of tourists visit Saratoga Springs for its famous horse racing events.
She spent a relaxing weekend in Saratoga Springs enjoying the historic mineral baths.
The Saratoga Performing Arts Center in Saratoga Springs hosts concerts and cultural festivals year-round.

Etymology

Named after the nearby Saratoga Lake; 'Springs' refers to the area's mineral springs.
