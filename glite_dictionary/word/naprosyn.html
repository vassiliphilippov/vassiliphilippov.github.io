Naprosyn

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæp.rə.sɪn / (AmE), / ˈnap.rə.sɪn / (BrE)
a trademarked nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (naproxen), used for treating arthritis, musculoskeletal inflammation, and moderate pain
trademarked anti-inflammatory drug (naproxen)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Naprosyn to help reduce the swelling in my joints.
She experienced fewer side effects when taking Naprosyn compared to other painkillers.
If you are allergic to aspirin, consult your doctor before using Naprosyn.
