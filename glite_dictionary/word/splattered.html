splattered

1. ADJ

/ ˈsplætɚd / (AmE), / ˈsplætəd / (BrE)
covered with drops or patches of liquid or another substance scattered over a surface; marked by splatters.
covered with scattered drops or patches

Examples

The artist's overalls were splattered with bright paint from her latest project.
After the heavy rain, the path was splattered with muddy water.
He removed his splattered apron before sitting down to dinner.

Synset Examples

waves dabbled with moonlight
a blood-spattered room
gardens splashed with color
kitchen walls splattered with grease

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spattered - covered with small drops of liquid
  • Synonym: stained - marked or discolored with a patch or spots

Etymology

Past participle of 'splatter,' likely of imitative origin.
