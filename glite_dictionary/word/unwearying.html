unwearying

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɪriɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪəriɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing with relentless or tireless enthusiasm and energy, without losing vitality
never tiring or losing energy

Examples

Her unwearying support gave the team the motivation they needed to succeed.
He pursued his goals with unwearying dedication despite numerous obstacles.
The volunteers worked with unwearying enthusiasm throughout the long campaign.

Synset Examples

an indefatigable advocate of equal rights
a tireless worker
unflagging pursuit of excellence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tireless - not becoming tired; showing sustained effort
  • Synonym: indefatigable - never tiring or giving up; persistent
  • Antonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness

Etymology

From un- (not) + wearying (causing to become tired).
