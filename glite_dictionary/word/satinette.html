satinette

1. NOUN

/ ˌsætnˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌsætnˈɛt / (BrE)
A fabric with a surface finish similar to satin, but made partly or entirely from cotton or synthetic fibers.
fabric resembling satin, made from cotton or synthetics

Examples

The dress was made from a soft satinette that shimmered in the light.
Many affordable evening gowns use satinette to mimic the luxurious look of silk.
The tailor recommended satinette for its smooth texture and durability.
