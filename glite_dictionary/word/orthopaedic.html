orthopaedic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔrθəˈpidɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the branch of medicine that deals with the correction of deformities or functional impairments of the skeletal system and associated muscles, joints, and ligaments.
relating to orthopedics (skeletal/muscular medicine)
medical british

Examples

She was prescribed orthopaedic shoes after her foot surgery.
The hospital has a renowned orthopaedic department specializing in joint replacements.
After the accident, he required extensive orthopaedic treatment for his fractured spine.

Synset Examples

orthopedic shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthopedic - relating to orthopedics (alternative US spelling)

Etymology

From French *orthopédique*, derived from Greek *orthos* (straight) and *paideia* (rearing of children).
