baya

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a small brown-and-yellow bird of South Asia, known for weaving elaborate nests; the common Indian weaverbird (Ploceus philippinus)
Indian weaverbird of South Asia

Examples

A flock of baya birds built intricate nests among the tall reeds.
Farmers often spot the baya weaving its nest during the monsoon season.
The delicate hanging nests of the baya are a popular sight in Indian villages.

Etymology

From Hindi 'bāyā', referring to the bird.
