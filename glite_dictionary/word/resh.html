resh

1. NOUN

/ reɪʃ / (AmE), / reɪʃ / (BrE)
the twentieth letter of the Hebrew alphabet
20th Hebrew letter

Examples

In Hebrew script, resh is written as ר.
The student practiced writing resh along with the other Hebrew letters.
Resh is used as the equivalent of the 'r' sound in Hebrew.

Etymology

From Hebrew רֵישׁ (rēsh), meaning 'head' or 'beginning'.
