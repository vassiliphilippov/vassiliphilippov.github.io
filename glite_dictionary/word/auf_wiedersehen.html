auf wiedersehen

1. NOUN

/ aʊf ˈvidɚˌzeɪn / (AmE), / aʊf ˈviːdəzeɪn / (BrE)
A German expression used in English to say goodbye or farewell.
farewell or goodbye (from German)

Examples

He waved and said auf wiedersehen as he left for the airport.
At the end of the party, everyone gave hugs and exchanged auf wiedersehen.
With a smile, she whispered auf wiedersehen before stepping out into the night.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - Used to express good wishes when parting
  • Antonym: hello - A greeting used on meeting someone

Etymology

From German 'auf Wiedersehen', meaning 'until we meet again'.
