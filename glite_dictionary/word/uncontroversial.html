uncontroversial

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kɑn.trəˈvɜr.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kɒn.trəˈvɜː.ʃəl / (BrE)
not likely to cause disagreement or public debate; not controversial.
not likely to cause disagreement

Examples

The new policy proposal is considered uncontroversial by most members of the committee.
Her views on recycling are fairly uncontroversial and widely accepted.
He made an uncontroversial statement that everyone agreed with.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: controversial - likely to cause disagreement or public debate

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + controversial.
