Durres

1. PROPN

/ ˈdurɛs, ˈdʊrɛs, duˈrɛs / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɛs, djʊˈrɛs, dʊˈrɛs / (BrE)
a port city in western Albania on the Adriatic Sea, one of the country's oldest and most important cities
port city in western Albania

Examples

The ferry departed from Durres early in the morning, bound for Italy.
