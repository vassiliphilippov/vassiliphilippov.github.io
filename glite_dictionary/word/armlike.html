armlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrmlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɑːmlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the form or qualities of an arm
resembling an arm

Examples

The octopus extended its armlike tentacle to grab the shell.
He admired the sculpture's armlike branches reaching toward the sky.
Some seaweeds have armlike fronds that wave gently with the tide.
