suety

1. ADJ

/ ˈsuː.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˈsjuː.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
Resembling or containing suet; having the appearance or texture of suet.
like or containing suet

Examples

The roast had a suety crust that made it rich and flavorful.
She noticed a suety layer floating on top of the stew.
The suety smell from the kitchen hinted at the dessert being prepared.

Synset Examples

suety lamb chops

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatty - containing or resembling fat

Etymology

From 'suet' + '-y', indicating a quality or resemblance.
