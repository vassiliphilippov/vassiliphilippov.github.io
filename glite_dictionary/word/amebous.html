amebous

1. ADJ

/ əˈmiːbəs / (AmE), / əˈmiːbəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an amoeba; having characteristics of amoebae, especially in movement or shape
resembling an amoeba

Examples

Certain cells exhibit amebous movement as they change shape to engulf particles.
The organism spread across the slide in a distinctly amebous manner.
Scientists observed an amebous pattern in the spread of the infection.

Synset Examples

amoebic dysentery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amoeboid - resembling an amoeba, especially in movement
  • Synonym: amoebic - caused by or relating to amoebae

Etymology

From 'amoeba' + '-ous', modeled after scientific taxonomic use.
