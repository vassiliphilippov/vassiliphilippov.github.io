skinless

1. ADJ

/ ˈskɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈskɪnləs / (BrE)
(of food or other items) having had the skin removed.
with the skin removed

Examples

She prefers to cook with skinless chicken breasts to reduce fat.
The skinless sausages are easier for young children to eat.
I bought a bag of skinless almonds for the recipe.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: skin - outer covering of an animal, especially as removed from food
