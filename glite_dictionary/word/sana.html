Sana

1. PROPN

/ sɑːˈnɑː / (AmE), / sɑːˈnɑː / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Yemen, located on the central plateau of the country.
capital city of Yemen

Examples

Many embassies in Yemen are based in Sana due to its status as the capital.
The architecture in Sana is famous for its distinctive ancient tower houses.
After years abroad, she finally returned home to Sana.

Etymology

From Arabic Ṣanʿāʾ (صَنْعَاء).
