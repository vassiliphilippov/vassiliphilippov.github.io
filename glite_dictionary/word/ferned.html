ferned

1. ADJ

/ fɜrnd / (AmE), / fɜːnd / (BrE)
Covered with or abounding in ferns.
abounding in ferns

Examples

The path wound through a ferned valley, shady and green.
She paused to sketch the ferned banks lining the river's edge.
The ferned glade was cool, with sunlight dappling through the leaves above.
