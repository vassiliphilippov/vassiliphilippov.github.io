Taurus

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔrəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
the second astrological sign of the zodiac, covering the period from about April 20 to May 20, traditionally represented by a bull
second sign of the zodiac (the Bull)

Examples

People born under Taurus are said to be reliable and practical.
The sun enters Taurus around April 20 each year.
His horoscope stated that Taurus would experience good fortune this month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zodiac - an imaginary belt of the heavens divided into twelve signs
  • Degree Lower: Aries - first sign of the zodiac
  • Degree Higher: Gemini - third sign of the zodiac

Etymology

From Latin *Taurus* (bull), used as a zodiac sign in astrology.

2. PROPN

/ ˈtɔːrəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
A zodiacal constellation in the Northern Hemisphere, located between Aries and Gemini, near Orion.
a zodiacal constellation

Examples

The bright star Aldebaran marks the eye of Taurus in the night sky.
Taurus is easily identifiable during the winter months in the Northern Hemisphere.
The Pleiades star cluster can be found within the boundaries of Taurus.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Aries - a zodiacal constellation next to Taurus
  • Degree Higher: Gemini - a zodiacal constellation next to Taurus

Etymology

From Latin Taurus (bull), also the ancient name for the constellation.

3. NOUN

/ ˈtɔrəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
A person born when the sun is in the zodiac sign Taurus, typically between April 20 and May 20.
person born under the Taurus zodiac sign

Examples

As a Taurus, she values stability and comfort in her daily life.
Many people believe that Taurus individuals are patient and reliable.
If you're a Taurus, your birthday falls in late April or early May.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Taurean - a person born under the sign of Taurus
  • Degree Lower: Aries - person born under the Aries zodiac sign
  • Degree Higher: Gemini - person born under the Gemini zodiac sign

Etymology

From Latin 'Taurus', meaning 'bull'.
