Andres Segovia

1. PROPN

/ ˈɑːnˌdɹeɪz seˈɡoʊviə / (AmE), / ˈændreɪs sɪˈɡəʊviə / (BrE)
(1893–1987) A Spanish guitarist celebrated for elevating the classical guitar to the status of a concert instrument.
Spanish classical guitarist (1893–1987)

Examples

Andres Segovia is often credited with establishing the guitar as a serious concert instrument.
Many aspiring guitarists look to Andres Segovia as a source of inspiration and technique.
Recordings by Andres Segovia remain influential among classical music enthusiasts today.
