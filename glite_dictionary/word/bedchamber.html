bedchamber

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbər / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a bedroom, especially in a large house or in historical contexts
bedroom; room for sleeping

Examples

The king summoned his advisor to the bedchamber late at night.
She decorated her bedchamber with elegant curtains and antique furniture.
In historical novels, characters often retreat to their bedchamber for privacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedroom - a room for sleeping in

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'bed' and 'chamber' (from Old French 'chambre', from Latin 'camera', meaning room or chamber).
