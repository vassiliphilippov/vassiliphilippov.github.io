gustatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡʌstəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌɡʌstəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to the sense of taste
relating to taste

Examples

The chef's new menu offered a gustatorial experience unlike any other.
Scientists studied the gustatorial responses of various animal species to sweet flavors.
The wine's gustatorial complexity appealed to even the most discerning critics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gustatory - relating to the sense of taste

Etymology

From Latin 'gustatio' (act of tasting) + '-al' (adjectival suffix).
