provenience

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈviːniəns / (AmE), / prəˈviːnɪəns / (BrE)
the place of origin or earliest known history of something, especially an object or artifact
place of origin; earliest history

Examples

The museum carefully documented the provenience of each artifact in its collection.
Questions about the provenience of the painting led to an investigation into its ownership history.
Archaeologists recorded the provenience of every bone fragment found at the site.

Synset Examples

the birthplace of civilization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provenance - place of origin or history, especially of an object

Etymology

From Latin provenientia (coming forth), from provenire (to come forth, originate).
