Helenium autumnale

1. NOUN

/ həˈliniəm ɔːˈtʌmnəl / (AmE), / hɪˈliːniəm ɔːˈtʌmnəli / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, commonly known as sneezeweed.
North American perennial with yellow late summer flowers

Examples

The bright yellow flowers of Helenium autumnale are a cheerful addition to late summer gardens.
Gardeners often choose Helenium autumnale for its hardiness and long-lasting blooms.
In the wild, Helenium autumnale is frequently found growing along riverbanks and wet meadows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sneezeweed - Common name for Helenium autumnale

Etymology

From the genus name Helenium, named after Helen of Troy in Greek mythology, and Latin ‘autumnale’ meaning ‘of autumn’.
