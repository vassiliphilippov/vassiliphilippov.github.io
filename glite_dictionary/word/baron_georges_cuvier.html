Baron Georges Cuvier

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæɹ.ɒn ˌʒɔːʒ kjuːˈvi.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈbær.ən ˌʒɔːʒ kjuːˈvi.eɪ / (BrE)
A French naturalist (1769–1832) regarded as the father of comparative anatomy and paleontology.
French naturalist and anatomist (1769–1832)

Examples

Baron Georges Cuvier revolutionized the study of animal anatomy in the 19th century.
Many museums feature fossils classified by Baron Georges Cuvier.
The work of Baron Georges Cuvier laid the foundation for modern paleontology.
