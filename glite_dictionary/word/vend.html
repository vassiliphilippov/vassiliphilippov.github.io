to vend

1. VERB

/ vɛnd / (AmE), / vɛnd / (BrE)
to sell or offer goods for sale, especially in a public place or from place to place
sell or offer goods for sale

Examples

Street vendors vend snacks and drinks to tourists along the busy promenade.
The artist decided to vend her handmade crafts at local fairs and markets.
He used to vend newspapers from a cart in the early mornings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sell - to give something in exchange for money

Etymology

From Latin 'vendere' (to sell), from 'venum' (sale) + 'dare' (to give).
