unneighborliness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈneɪbərli.nəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪbəlɪnɪs / (BrE)
a lack of friendliness or helpfulness towards one's neighbors
lack of neighborliness

Examples

Their continued unneighborliness made it hard for new residents to feel welcome.
Acts of unneighborliness can negatively affect a community's sense of unity.
She was surprised by the unneighborliness in what seemed like such a friendly town.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neighborliness - friendly, helpful behavior toward neighbors
  • Synonym: unfriendliness - the quality of being unfriendly
