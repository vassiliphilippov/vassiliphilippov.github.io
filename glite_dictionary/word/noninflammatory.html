noninflammatory

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌɪn.fləˈmæt̬.ə.ri / (AmE), / nɒnˌɪn.fləˈmæt.ər.i / (BrE)
Not causing or characterized by inflammation; not inflammatory.
not causing inflammation
medical

Examples

The doctor confirmed that the rash was noninflammatory and not related to an allergic reaction.
Arthritis symptoms can be classified as noninflammatory or inflammatory based on their cause.
This medication is prescribed for noninflammatory joint pain.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inflammatory - causing inflammation

Etymology

From prefix non- (not) + inflammatory.
