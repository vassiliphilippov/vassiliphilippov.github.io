shrewmouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃruːˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈʃruːmaʊs / (BrE)
A small, mouselike mammal with a long snout, closely related to moles but resembling a mouse in appearance.
small mammal related to moles

Examples

The rainforest is home to a rare species of shrewmouse found nowhere else.
Researchers studied the diet of the shrewmouse to understand its impact on the local ecosystem.
A shrewmouse scurried across the forest floor, searching for insects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrew - a small insectivorous mammal with a pointed snout

Etymology

Formed from 'shrew' (a small insectivorous mammal) and 'mouse.'
