Yemeni

1. ADJ

/ jəˈmɛni / (AmE), / jɛˈmiːni / (BrE)
of or relating to Yemen or its people
of or relating to Yemen

Examples

Many traditional Yemeni dishes are rich in spices and flavor.
The Yemeni landscape is known for its dramatic mountains and coastal plains.
A famous Yemeni poet recited his work at the international festival.

Synset Examples

Yemeni mountains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yemenite - of or relating to Yemen or its people

Etymology

From Yemen + -i, denoting origin or relation.

2. NOUN

/ jəˈmɛni / (AmE), / jɛˈmiːni / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Yemen
a person from Yemen

Examples

The Yemeni greeted visitors with warmth and hospitality.
Many Yemenis have emigrated to other countries in search of better opportunities.
As a Yemeni, she is proud of her country's rich heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yemenite - a native or inhabitant of Yemen (less common form)

Etymology

From Yemen, the name of the country, with English adjectival suffix -i.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-09770101-n
Synset POS: n
