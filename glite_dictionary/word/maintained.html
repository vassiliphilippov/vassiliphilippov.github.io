maintained

1. ADJ

/ meɪnˈteɪnd / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪnd / (BrE)
kept in good condition by regular care and attention
kept in good condition

Examples

The garden is always maintained beautifully throughout the year.
They bought a well-maintained historic home in the city center.
A regularly maintained car will run more efficiently and last longer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neglected - not receiving proper care, in bad condition

2. ADJ

/ meɪnˈteɪnd / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪnd / (BrE)
kept in a particular condition, continued in use, possession, or memory over time.
kept or continued in use or memory

Examples

The garden was beautifully maintained throughout the year by the groundskeepers.
She kept a maintained list of all the books she had read.
Their traditions have been maintained by each new generation.

Synset Examples

in … the retained pattern of dancers and guests remembered

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preserved - kept safe from harm or loss; maintained over time
  • Antonym: neglected - not cared for or maintained

Etymology

From maintain + -ed; 'maintain' from Old French maintenir, from Latin manutenere (to hold in hand).
