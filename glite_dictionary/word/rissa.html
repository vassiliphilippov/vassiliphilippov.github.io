Rissa

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪsə / (AmE), / ˈrɪsə / (BrE)
A genus of gulls in the family Laridae, comprising the kittiwakes.
genus of gulls (kittiwakes)

Examples

The black-legged kittiwake belongs to the genus Rissa.
Scientists observed several species within the Rissa genus nesting on the cliffs.
Both major kittiwake species are classified under Rissa by ornithologists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kittiwake - a type of sea gull belonging to the genus Rissa

Etymology

From New Latin, named after the Icelandic name for the kittiwake, *rissa*.

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɪsə / (AmE), / ˈrɪsə / (BrE)
A genus of seabirds in the gull family (Laridae), commonly known as kittiwakes.
genus of seabirds (kittiwakes)

Examples

The black-legged Rissa breeds in large colonies on sea cliffs.
Scientists observed the feeding habits of Rissa in Arctic regions.
Within the gull family, Rissa is easily distinguished by its unique call and nesting behavior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kittiwake - a gull of the genus Rissa

Etymology

Modern Latin, from Norwegian 'Rissa', a local name for the kittiwake.
