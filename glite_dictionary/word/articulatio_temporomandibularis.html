articulatio temporomandibularis

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˌtɪkjəˌleɪʃioʊ tɛmˌpɔroʊˌmændɪˈbjulərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪkjʊˌleɪʃɪəʊ tɛmˌpɔːrəʊˌmændɪˈbjuːlərɪs / (BrE)
the joint connecting the lower jawbone (mandible) to the temporal bone of the skull
jaw joint between mandible and temporal bone
medical

Examples

A disorder of the articulatio temporomandibularis can cause persistent jaw pain.
Doctors examined the function of the articulatio temporomandibularis after the patient reported difficulty chewing.
Clicking noises in the articulatio temporomandibularis are sometimes a sign of joint dysfunction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temporomandibular joint - the joint between the temporal bone and the mandible; the English synonym

Etymology

From Latin: 'articulatio' (joint) + 'temporomandibularis' (relating to the temporal bone and mandible).
