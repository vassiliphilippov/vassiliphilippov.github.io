Harding

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɑrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdɪŋ / (BrE)
(1865–1923) the 29th President of the United States, known for his administration's involvement in the Teapot Dome scandal.
29th U.S. President (1865–1923)

Examples

The presidency of Harding was overshadowed by political scandals.
Many historians debate the legacy of Harding in American politics.
Harding served as the 29th president of the United States from 1921 to 1923.
