dozen

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌzən / (AmE), / ˈdʌzən / (BrE)
A group or set of twelve people or things.
group of twelve

Examples

We ordered a dozen eggs from the grocery store.
Only a dozen people showed up to the meeting.
She baked two dozen cookies for the school fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twelve - the cardinal number equal to 12

Etymology

From Old French 'dozaine', from 'doze' (twelve), from Latin 'duodecim' (twelve), influenced by the French form.
