small-scale

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔlˌskeɪl / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːl skeɪl / (BrE)
of limited size or scope; not large in extent or operation.
of limited size or scope

Examples

They started a small-scale farming operation on their land.
The research project was conducted on a small-scale due to budget constraints.
This factory produces small-scale models for architectural presentations.
