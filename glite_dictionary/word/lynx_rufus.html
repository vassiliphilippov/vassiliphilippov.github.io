Lynx rufus

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋks ˈruːfəs / (AmE), / lɪŋks ˈruːfəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the bobcat, a small wild cat native to North America.
North American bobcat (scientific name)

Examples

The Lynx rufus has adapted to a wide variety of habitats across North America.
Researchers observed several Lynx rufus during their wildlife survey in the forest.
Unlike its Eurasian cousin, the Lynx rufus is smaller and more widely distributed in the United States.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bobcat - common name for Lynx rufus, a wild cat native to North America
  • Degree Higher: lynx - wild cats of the genus Lynx

Etymology

From Latin, lynx ('lynx') + rufus ('red, reddish').
