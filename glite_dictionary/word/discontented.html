discontented

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing dissatisfaction with one's situation; not content.
feeling or expressing dissatisfaction

Examples

Many employees grew discontented after the company announced a freeze on salary increases.
She sounded discontented with the outcome of the negotiations.
His discontented expression made it clear that he was unhappy with the results.

Synset Examples

saw many discontent faces in the room
was discontented with his position

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contented - feeling or showing satisfaction with one's possessions, status, or situation

Etymology

From Latin dis- (expressing reversal) + contentus (satisfied).
