silvex

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɛks / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvɛks / (BrE)
a herbicide formerly used to control woody plants, now banned due to its toxicity to animals and potential environmental dangers
toxic herbicide for woody plants

Examples

The use of silvex has been discontinued due to its harmful effects on wildlife.
Regulations now prohibit spraying silvex near water sources.
Scientists studied the environmental impact of silvex residues in soil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: herbicide - a substance used to destroy unwanted plants
