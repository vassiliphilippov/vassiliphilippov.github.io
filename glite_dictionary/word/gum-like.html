gum-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling chewing gum in texture, consistency, or appearance.
resembling chewing gum

Examples

The dessert had a gum-like texture that made it chewy and fun to eat.
A sticky, gum-like residue covered the inside of the bottle.
Their new material is gum-like and can be stretched without tearing.
