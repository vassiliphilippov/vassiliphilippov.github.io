orology

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ɒˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of mountains, including their formation, structure, and distribution
study of mountains

Examples

She dedicated her career to orology and traveled to several mountain ranges for research.
Advances in orology have deepened our understanding of how mountain ranges are formed.
The conference featured leading experts in orology from universities around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountainology - the scientific study of mountains

Etymology

From Greek 'oros' (mountain) + '-logy' (study of).
