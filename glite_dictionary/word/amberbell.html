amberbell

1. NOUN

/ ˈæm.bɚ.bɛl / (AmE), / ˈæm.bə.bɛl / (BrE)
An eastern North American plant of the dogtooth family, with single yellow flowers marked with brown or purple and spotted interiors.
North American plant with yellow, spotted flowers

Examples

The woodland floor was covered in blooming amberbells during the spring hike.
Botanists carefully documented the markings on the amberbell for their field study.
She photographed the delicate amberbell growing beside the stream.
