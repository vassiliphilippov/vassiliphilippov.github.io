lobated

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊˌbeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having or resembling a lobe or lobes, often used to describe shapes in anatomy or biology.
having lobes or resembling lobes

Examples

The leaves of the plant are lobated, giving them a unique appearance.
Her research focused on animals with lobated organs to understand evolutionary differences.
The fungus was identified by its lobated spores under the microscope.

Synset Examples

a lobate tongue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobate - having lobes or lobed shape

Etymology

From Latin 'lobatus', from 'lobus' (lobe) + '-ate'.
