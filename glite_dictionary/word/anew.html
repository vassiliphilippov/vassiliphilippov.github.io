anew

1. ADV

/ əˈnu / (AmE), / əˈnjuː / (BrE)
again but in a new or different way; once more, in a different form or manner
again in a new or different way

Examples

After moving to a new city, she began her life anew.
He decided to approach the problem anew after his previous attempts failed.
The author rewrote the ending anew to give it a more hopeful tone.

Synset Examples

start afresh
wanted to write the story anew
starting life anew in a fresh place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afresh - again, but in a fresh or different way

Etymology

From Middle English 'anewen', from 'a-' (on) + 'new'.
