asphaltic

1. ADJ

/ æˈsfɑltɪk / (AmE), / æˈsfæltɪk / (BrE)
containing, consisting of, or relating to asphalt.
containing or relating to asphalt

Examples

The road was resurfaced with a new asphaltic mixture to improve durability.
Engineers examined the asphaltic content of the pavement sample for quality control.
The refinery produces several asphaltic materials used in construction and waterproofing.

Synset Examples

asphaltic residues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bituminous - containing or resembling bitumen

Etymology

From 'asphalt' + '-ic', the adjectival suffix.
