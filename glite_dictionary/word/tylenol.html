Tylenol

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪlɪˌnɔl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪlɪnɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of acetaminophen, used as a pain reliever and to reduce fever.
brand of acetaminophen pain reliever
american

Examples

She took some Tylenol to ease her headache before the meeting.
The doctor recommended Tylenol for his mild fever.
Many parents rely on Tylenol for their children's minor aches and pains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acetaminophen - a medication used as an analgesic and antipyretic
  • Synonym: paracetamol - another name for acetaminophen, commonly used in British English
