Ovral

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvrəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊvrəl / (BrE)
A brand name for an oral contraceptive containing estradiol and norgestrel, used to prevent pregnancy.
oral contraceptive brand (estradiol, norgestrel)
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Ovral as a reliable form of birth control.
She discussed switching from Ovral to another contraceptive with her gynecologist.
Some women experience side effects when using Ovral.
