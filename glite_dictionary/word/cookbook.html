cookbook

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊk.bʊk / (AmE), / ˈkʊk.bʊk / (BrE)
a book that contains recipes and instructions for preparing food
a book of recipes and cooking directions

Examples

She gave me a beautiful cookbook for my birthday.
The cookbook includes easy-to-follow recipes for beginners.
He is writing his own cookbook featuring traditional family dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recipe book - a book containing recipes; synonym for 'cookbook'

Etymology

Formed from 'cook' + 'book' in English, first recorded in the late 19th century.
