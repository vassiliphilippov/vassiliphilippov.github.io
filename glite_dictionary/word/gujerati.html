Gujerati

1. NOUN

/ guːdʒəˈrɑːti / (AmE), / gʊdʒəˈrɑːti / (BrE)
the Indic language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Gujarat and by Gujarati communities worldwide
language of Gujarat, India

Examples

Many people in western India speak Gujerati as their first language.
She is fluent in both Gujerati and English.
The novel was originally written in Gujerati before being translated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gujarati - alternative spelling for the same language

Etymology

From Hindi 'Gujarātī', relating to Gujarat.

2. NOUN

/ ɡuːdʒəˈrɑːti / (AmE), / ɡuːdʒəˈrɑːti / (BrE)
a member of the people of Gujarat, a state in western India
person from Gujarat, India

Examples

Many Gujerati people have settled in the United Kingdom over the past few decades.
The festival was celebrated with great enthusiasm among the local Gujerati community.
As a proud Gujerati, she enjoys cooking traditional dishes from her region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Gujarati - a member of the people of Gujarat, alternative spelling

Etymology

From Hindi Gujarati, relating to Gujarat, a state in western India.
