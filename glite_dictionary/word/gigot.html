gigot

1. NOUN

/ ˈʒiɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʒiɡəʊ / (BrE)
a leg of lamb, especially one prepared for roasting
leg of lamb for roasting

Examples

She ordered a roast gigot with rosemary and garlic for Sunday dinner.
The chef recommended the gigot as the best cut for a special occasion meal.
At the French restaurant, the menu featured gigot served with seasonal vegetables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leg of lamb - the leg of a sheep used as meat

Etymology

From French 'gigot' (leg of lamb).
