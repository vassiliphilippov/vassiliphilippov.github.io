Luxemburger

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌksəmˌbɜrɡər / (AmE), / ˈlʌksəmbɜːɡə / (BrE)
of or relating to Luxembourg or its people.
of Luxembourg or its people

Examples

The Luxemburger government implemented new economic policies last year.
She has a Luxemburger passport and lives in the capital city.
Traditional Luxemburger cuisine is known for its hearty dishes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Luxembourgish - of or relating to Luxembourg, especially its language or people

Etymology

From Luxembourg + -er, forming a demonym.

2. NOUN

/ ˈlʌksəmˌbɜːrɡər / (AmE), / ˈlʌksəmbɜːɡə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Luxembourg
person from Luxembourg

Examples

The new ambassador is a proud Luxemburger who has served his country for decades.
She met a Luxemburger while traveling in Europe, and they quickly became friends.
Many Luxemburgers speak several languages, reflecting the country's multicultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Luxembourgian - a native, inhabitant, or citizen of Luxembourg; also an adjective describing things from Luxembourg

Etymology

From Luxembourg + -er, a common suffix denoting people from a place.
