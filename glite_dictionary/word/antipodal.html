antipodal

1. ADJ

/ ænˈtɪp.ə.dəl / (AmE), / ænˈtɪp.ə.dəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated at opposite sides of the earth or a sphere; relating to the antipodes.
opposite sides of the earth or sphere

Examples

The two cities are almost antipodal, each lying on opposite sides of the planet.
Researchers studied the climate of antipodal regions to compare temperature differences.
The explorer imagined drilling a tunnel through the Earth to reach its antipodal point.

Synset Examples

antipodean latitudes
antipodal regions of the earth
antipodal points on a sphere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antipodean - relating to the antipodes; opposite side of the world

Etymology

From Greek antipous (having the feet opposite), from anti- (opposite) + pous (foot).
