interpreting

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈtɜrprətɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of explaining the meaning of something that is not immediately obvious
the act of explaining or clarifying meaning

Examples

The student's interpreting of the poem offered a new perspective.
Religious texts have sparked centuries of interpreting among scholars.
Her interpreting of the unclear policy led to changes in the office.

Synset Examples

the edict was subject to many interpretations
he annoyed us with his interpreting of parables
often imitations are extended to provide a more accurate rendition of the child's intended meaning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interpretation - the act of explaining the meaning of something
