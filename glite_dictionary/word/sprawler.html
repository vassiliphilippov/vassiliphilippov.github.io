sprawler

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsprɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who lies or sits with arms and legs spread out in an ungainly or relaxed way
person who sprawls

Examples

My brother is such a sprawler that he always takes up the entire sofa.
The class clown was a notorious sprawler, draping himself carelessly across the chairs.
She's a quiet sprawler in meetings, often sitting with her legs stretched out under the table.

Synset Examples

he is such a sprawler he needs a bed to himself

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sprawl - to sit, lie, or fall with arms and legs spread out in an ungainly way
