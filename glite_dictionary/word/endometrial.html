endometrial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛndoʊˈmiːtriəl / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈmiːtriəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the endometrium (the mucous membrane lining the uterus)
relating to the uterine lining
medical

Examples

The doctor found abnormalities in the endometrial tissue during the procedure.
She was diagnosed with endometrial cancer after several tests.
Certain hormones influence endometrial growth each menstrual cycle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endometrium - the mucous membrane lining the uterus

Etymology

From endometrium + -al, where 'endometrium' is from Greek 'endon' (within) + 'metra' (womb).
