floodlit

1. ADJ

/ ˈflʌdˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈflʌdˌlɪt / (BrE)
Illuminated by strong artificial lights, especially floodlights.
lit up by floodlights

Examples

The tennis court was floodlit for the evening match.
They admired the castle's floodlit walls during their nighttime tour.
The park remained open late, its paths floodlit for visitors.

Synset Examples

the floodlit courtyard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illuminated - made bright with light

Etymology

From 'floodlight' + '-lit', modeled after 'moonlit', first attested in the mid-20th century.
