banging

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a continuous and very loud noise, especially one that is repetitive or forceful
loud, repetitive noise

Examples

The banging from the construction site kept me awake all night.
Suddenly, a loud banging came from the back of the house, startling everyone.
He could hear the banging of the drums echoing through the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crash - a sudden loud noise caused by the impact of two objects
  • Synonym: thump - a dull or muffled banging sound

Etymology

From the verb 'bang', imitative of a sudden loud noise, with -ing forming the noun.
