toke

1. NOUN

/ toʊk / (AmE), / təʊk / (BrE)
a puff or inhalation of smoke from a marijuana or hashish cigarette
puff of marijuana cigarette
informal

Examples

He took a long toke and held the smoke before exhaling.
She offered me a toke from her joint at the party.
After a few tokes, they started laughing uncontrollably.

Synset Examples

the boys took a few tokes on a joint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drag - an act of inhaling smoke from a cigarette
