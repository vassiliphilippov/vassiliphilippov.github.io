tidings

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪdɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdɪŋz / (BrE)
news or information about recent events, especially when delivered formally or in writing
news or information

Examples

She brought joyful tidings of her safe arrival.
The messenger brought bad tidings from the battlefield.
We received tidings of their victory late last night.

Synset Examples

they awaited news of the outcome

Related Terms

  • Synonym: news - newly received or noteworthy information

Etymology

From Old English tīdung (announcement, report), from tīd (time, event).
