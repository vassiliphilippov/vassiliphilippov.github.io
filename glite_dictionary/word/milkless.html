milkless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪlkləs / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkləs / (BrE)
Containing no milk; lacking milk.
without milk

Examples

She prepared a milkless version of the cake for her lactose-intolerant friend.
The mother was unable to nurse her newborn due to milkless breasts.
Many vegan recipes offer delicious milkless alternatives to traditional dairy dishes.

Synset Examples

milkless breasts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: milky - containing or resembling milk

Etymology

Formed from 'milk' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
