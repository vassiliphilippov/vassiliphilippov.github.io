expressionless

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsprɛʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃn.ləs / (BrE)
showing no emotion; having a face or manner that does not reveal one's feelings or thoughts
showing no emotion

Examples

She maintained an expressionless face throughout the entire interview.
His expressionless gaze made it impossible to know what he was thinking.
The actor delivered his lines in an expressionless manner, adding to the character's mystery.

Synset Examples

deadpan humor
his face remained expressionless as the verdict was read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassive - not showing or feeling emotion

Etymology

From 'expression' + '-less', meaning 'without expression'.
