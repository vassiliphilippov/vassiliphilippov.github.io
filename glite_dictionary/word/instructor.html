instructor

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstrʌktər / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to teach, especially in a college or a specialized field
person who teaches, especially in a college or specialty

Examples

The driving instructor showed her how to parallel park safely.
He works as a fitness instructor at the local gym.
Many students praised their biology instructor for making complex topics understandable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
  • Antonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college

Etymology

From Latin 'instructor' (teacher, trainer), from 'instruere' (to instruct).
