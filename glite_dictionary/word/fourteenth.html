fourteenth

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming next after the thirteenth in order or position; being number fourteen in a sequence.
ordinal number: fourteen in sequence

Examples

She finished in fourteenth place in the race, just ahead of her teammate.
This is my fourteenth birthday, and I'm excited to celebrate with my friends.
The fourteenth chapter of the book introduces a surprising twist in the story.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirteenth - coming next after the twelfth in position
  • Degree Higher: fifteenth - coming next after the fourteenth in position

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
the person or thing that is number fourteen in a series; the ordinal number matching the number 14 in position.
person or thing in 14th position

Examples

This is the fourteenth of our monthly meetings.
Her birthday falls on the fourteenth of April.
He finished fourteenth out of thirty runners in the race.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirteenth - person or thing in 13th position in a series
  • Degree Higher: fifteenth - person or thing in 15th position in a series

Etymology

Formed from 'fourteen' + ordinal suffix '-th'.
