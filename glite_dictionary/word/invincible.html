invincible

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvɪnsəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪnsɪbəl / (BrE)
too powerful to be defeated or overcome; impossible to conquer or subdue.
too powerful to be defeated

Examples

The team felt invincible after winning their tenth game in a row.
Despite many setbacks, her invincible hope inspired everyone.
The castle was thought to be invincible against any enemy attack.

Synset Examples

an invincible army
her invincible spirit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbeatable - impossible to defeat or surpass
  • Antonym: vincible - capable of being defeated or overcome

Etymology

From Latin invincibilis, from in- (not) + vincibilis (can be conquered), from vincere (to conquer).
