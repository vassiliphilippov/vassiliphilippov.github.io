abaya

1. NOUN

/ əˈbaɪ.ə / (AmE), / əˈbɑː.jə / (BrE)
A long, loose black robe, traditionally worn by some Muslim women to cover the body in public.
long loose robe worn by Muslim women

Examples

She wore a beautiful abaya embroidered with gold thread for the festival.
The abaya is commonly seen in many countries across the Middle East.
Her new abaya was both comfortable and elegant.

Etymology

From Arabic ʿabāya (cloak, outer garment).
