Malva

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæl.və / (AmE), / ˈmæl.və / (BrE)
A genus of herbaceous plants commonly known as mallows, characterized by their five-petaled flowers and mucilaginous leaves.
a genus of mallow plants

Examples

The garden features several species of Malva with delicate pink blossoms.
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of Malva leaves and flowers.
In some regions, Malva is grown both for its beauty and its edible foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mallow - common name for plants in the genus Malva

Etymology

From Latin *malva* (mallow).
