Panipat

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɑː.ni.pɑt / (AmE), / ˈpæn.ɪ.pæt / (BrE)
A major battle fought in 1761 in northern India, in which the ruler of Afghanistan defeated the Marathas, marking a turning point in Indian history.
site of the 1761 Afghan-Maratha battle

Examples

The Third Battle of Panipat in 1761 changed the course of Indian history.
Many historians have studied the tactics used at Panipat.
The loss at Panipat weakened the Maratha Empire significantly.
