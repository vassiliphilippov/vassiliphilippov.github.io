fat-free

1. ADJ

/ ˌfætˈfri / (AmE), / ˌfætˈfriː / (BrE)
containing no fat or fat solids; without any fat content.
containing no fat

Examples

She prefers fat-free yogurt with her breakfast.
The recipe calls for fat-free milk instead of whole milk.
Many people choose fat-free salad dressing to reduce calories.

Synset Examples
