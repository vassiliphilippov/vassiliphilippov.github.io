level-headed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛvəlˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəlˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
calm and sensible; able to make good decisions, especially in difficult situations
calm and sensible; good at making decisions

Examples

She remained level-headed during the crisis and calmly helped everyone to safety.
A level-headed manager can defuse tense situations before they escalate.
His level-headed response impressed the interview panel.

Synset Examples

healthy scepticism
a healthy fear of rattlesnakes
the healthy attitude of French laws
healthy relations between labor and management
an intelligent solution
a sound approach to the problem
sound advice
no sound explanation for his decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensible - having or showing good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: rational - based on reason or logic
  • Antonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment

Etymology

Formed from 'level' (even, balanced) and 'headed' (having a particular type of head or mind).
