inguinal

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪŋɡwɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡwɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the region of the groin
relating to the groin
medical

Examples

The patient complained of inguinal pain after heavy lifting.
An inguinal hernia is a common type of abdominal wall hernia.
The doctor examined the inguinal region for signs of swelling.

Etymology

From Latin inguinalis, from inguen (groin).
