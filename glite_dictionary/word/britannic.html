Britannic

1. ADJ

/ brɪˈtænɪk / (AmE), / brɪˈtænɪk / (BrE)
relating to Britain or the British people, often used in formal or official titles.
relating to Britain or the British people

Examples

The ship was named the Britannic to honor the nation.
Her Britannic Majesty's Government issued the declaration.
The medal bore a Britannic emblem signifying loyalty to the Crown.

Synset Examples

Her Britannic Majesty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British - relating to Great Britain or its people

Etymology

From Latin Britannicus, from Britannia (Britain).
