plowwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈplaʊˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈplaʊˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who plows fields, especially as an occupation.
woman who plows fields

Examples

The plowwoman guided the horses steadily across the field at sunrise.
In the photograph, a determined plowwoman is working the land after a rainstorm.
Stories about the hardworking plowwomen of the village have been passed down for generations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plowman - a man who plows fields

Etymology

Formed from 'plow' + 'woman', by analogy with 'plowman'.
