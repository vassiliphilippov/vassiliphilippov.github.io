measureless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛʒərlɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒəlɪs / (BrE)
too great to be measured; limitless in extent, size, or quantity.
without limits; too great to measure

Examples

The measureless sky stretched above us, making us feel small and insignificant.
His generosity seemed measureless, touching the lives of everyone around him.
Artists often strive to capture the measureless beauty of nature in their work.

Synset Examples

limitless vastness of our solar system
The long unmeasured pulse of time moves everything. There is nothing hidden that it cannot bring to light, nothing once known that may not become unknown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limitless - without any limit; boundless
  • Synonym: boundless - having no boundaries; vast or unlimited
  • Antonym: finite - having limits or bounds

Etymology

From 'measure' + the suffix '-less', meaning 'without'.
