William Holman Hunt

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈhoʊlmən hʌnt / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈhɒlmən hʌnt / (BrE)
(1827–1910) English painter and founding member of the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood, known for his detailed and symbolic works.
English Pre-Raphaelite painter (1827–1910)

Examples

The painting was created by William Holman Hunt during the height of the Pre-Raphaelite movement.
Art historians often discuss the influence of William Holman Hunt on later Victorian artists.
William Holman Hunt's works are displayed in many major British galleries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood - group of English painters, poets, and critics founded in 1848
