musclewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman employed as a thug or bodyguard, often using physical strength or intimidation.
female thug or bodyguard

Examples

The nightclub hired a musclewoman to keep rowdy customers in check.
Rumors spread that the corrupt politician was protected by a notorious musclewoman.
As a musclewoman, she was respected and feared in equal measure on the streets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscleman - a man employed as a thug or bodyguard

Etymology

Modelled on 'muscleman', from 'muscle' + 'woman' by analogy with 'muscleman'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who develops large and well-defined muscles through strength training and bodybuilding exercises
woman with very muscular physique

Examples

As a dedicated musclewoman, she spends hours in the gym every day.
The competition attracted several renowned musclewomen from around the world.
Her achievements as a musclewoman have inspired many young women to pursue fitness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilder - someone who lifts weights to develop large muscles
  • Synonym: muscleman - a man with very well-developed muscles

Etymology

Formed from 'muscle' + 'woman', modeled after 'muscleman'.
