inexpressively

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no emotion or expression.
without showing emotion or expression

Examples

She listened inexpressively, giving away nothing about how she felt.
He stared inexpressively at the painting, unmoved by its colors or shapes.
The actor delivered his lines inexpressively, which confused the audience.

Synset Examples

she looked at him inexpressively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expressionlessly - without showing any emotion on one's face

Etymology

From 'inexpressive' + '-ly'; 'inexpressive' from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'express' + '-ive'.
