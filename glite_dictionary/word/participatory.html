participatory

1. ADJ

/ pɑrˈtɪsəˌtɔri / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪsɪpət(ə)ri / (BrE)
allowing or encouraging everyone to take part in something; involving participation by all members of a group or community.
involving participation by all

Examples

The organization adopted a participatory approach to decision-making, ensuring all voices were heard.
Many educators believe in participatory learning, where students are actively involved in the process.
A participatory democracy relies on the involvement of ordinary citizens in shaping policies.

Synset Examples

participatory democracy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: democratic - relating to or supporting democracy or its principles

Etymology

From participate + -ory, from Latin participare (to share in).
