24th

1. ADJ

/ twɛnti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / twɛnti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the twenty-third in a sequence; the ordinal number corresponding to twenty-four.
ordinal number for position 24

Examples

My birthday is on the 24th of March every year.
This is the 24th time she has visited Paris.
The artist's 24th novel was published last month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-fourth - alternative spelling of 24th
  • Degree Lower: 23rd - coming before 24th in sequence
  • Degree Higher: 25th - coming after 24th in sequence
