chart

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑrt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a listing, usually published weekly, of the most popular or best-selling recorded music
music ranking list

Examples

The singer's new single quickly climbed the charts after its release.
That album stayed at the top of the charts for five consecutive weeks.
Making it into the charts is a significant milestone for any debut artist.

Synset Examples

number 2 on the charts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit parade - a list of popular songs or records, especially ranked by sales or popularity
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06500907-n
Synset POS: n

2. VERB

/ tʃɑɹt / (AmE), / tʃɑːt / (BrE)
to plan or lay out a course or strategy in detail
plan or map out in detail

Examples

The committee will chart a new direction for the organization.
She carefully charted her career path before making any decisions.
We need to chart our progress over the next year to measure success.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
