exultantly

1. ADV

/ ɪgˈzʌltəntli / (AmE), / ɪgˈzʌltəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great happiness or triumphant joy.
with triumphant joy or happiness

Examples

She cheered exultantly when her team won the championship.
He raised his arms exultantly after solving the difficult puzzle.
They danced exultantly through the streets, celebrating their victory.

Synset Examples

it was exultingly easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumphantly - in a manner showing triumph or victory
  • Antonym: mournfully - in a manner showing sadness

Etymology

From Latin 'exultare' (to leap up), through 'exultant' + '-ly'.
