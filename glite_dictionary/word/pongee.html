pongee

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑnˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˌpɒnˈdʒi / (BrE)
a soft, thin fabric made from raw silk, or sometimes an imitation, often used for clothing and linings
a soft, thin silk fabric

Examples

He chose a light pongee jacket for the warm summer evening.
The dress was made of cream-colored pongee and trimmed with lace.
Many umbrellas are lined with pongee because of its softness and durability.

Etymology

From Mandarin Chinese *pánzhī* (woven on a handloom).
