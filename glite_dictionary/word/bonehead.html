bonehead

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌhɛd / (BrE)
An unintelligent or foolish person; someone regarded as lacking good sense.
a stupid or foolish person
informal

Examples

I can't believe you locked the keys inside the car, you bonehead!
Only a bonehead would try to fix the computer by hitting it.
Sometimes, even the smartest people can act like a bonehead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - A person of low intelligence
  • Antonym: genius - A person of exceptional intelligence or ability

Etymology

Formed from 'bone' + 'head', originally American English slang (early 20th century) likening a person's head to bone, i.e., hard or thick.
