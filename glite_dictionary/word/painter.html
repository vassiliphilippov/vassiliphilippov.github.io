painter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
a rope attached to the bow of a boat, used for tying it up or towing
rope for tying/towing a boat

Examples

Attach the painter securely before you leave the boat at the dock.
The canoe drifted away because its painter was not tied properly.
He grabbed the painter and pulled the dinghy back to shore.

Etymology

From Middle English *paynter*, from Old French *pantoire* (a tether, strap).

2. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪn.tər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪn.tə / (BrE)
an artist who creates pictures by applying paint to a surface, especially as a profession
artist who paints pictures

Examples

The famous painter completed his masterpiece in just six months.
She aspires to be a painter like her grandmother was.
Many painters throughout history have struggled for recognition during their lifetimes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially visual arts
  • Antonym: sculptor - an artist who creates sculptures

Etymology

From Old French *peintour*, from Latin *pictor* (painter).

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply paint to buildings, objects, or surfaces
a person who applies paint as a job

Examples

The painter spent all day covering the kitchen walls with a fresh coat of blue.
We hired a painter to repaint the exterior of our house.
The painter carefully applied a second layer to the wooden fence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorator - a person whose job is to decorate buildings, especially with paint or wallpaper

4. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
(US, regional) A large wild cat of North America, also known as a cougar, puma, or mountain lion.
large American wild cat; cougar

Examples

We spotted a painter crossing the trail just before dusk.
The region is known for its population of painters in the mountains.
Old hunters often warn newcomers about the stealth of the painter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cougar - large wild cat native to the Americas; puma, mountain lion
  • Synonym: puma - another name for a large American wild cat, Felis concolor
  • Synonym: mountain lion - synonym for cougar or puma, large American wild cat

Etymology

From an alteration of 'panther'.
