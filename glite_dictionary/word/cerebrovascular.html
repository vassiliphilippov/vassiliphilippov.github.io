cerebrovascular

1. ADJ

/ səˌriːbroʊˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / səˌriːbrəʊˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to the brain and the blood vessels that supply it.
of the brain and its blood vessels
medical

Examples

He suffered a cerebrovascular accident, commonly known as a stroke.
The team is researching cerebrovascular diseases and their risk factors.
Routine checkups can help detect potential cerebrovascular problems early.

Synset Examples

a cerebrovascular accident

Etymology

From Latin 'cerebrum' (brain) + 'vascular' (relating to vessels).
