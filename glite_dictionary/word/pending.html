pending

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
awaiting a decision, settlement, or completion; not yet decided or resolved
awaiting decision or completion

Examples

The outcome of the court case is still pending.
Several important decisions remain pending until the next meeting.
Her application for the job is currently pending review.

Synset Examples

business still pending

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unresolved - not yet resolved or settled
  • Antonym: settled - finally decided or resolved

Etymology

From Latin 'pendere' (to hang).
