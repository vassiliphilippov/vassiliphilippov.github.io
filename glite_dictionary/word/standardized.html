standardized

1. ADJ

/ ˈstændərdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
brought into conformity with a standard; made uniform or consistent according to a set standard.
made uniform by standards

Examples

All students must take the standardized test at the end of the year.
The new regulations required standardized labels on all products.
We followed a standardized procedure to ensure consistent results.

Synset Examples

standardized education

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - not varying or changing; constant; the same throughout
  • Antonym: customized - made or changed according to the needs of an individual person

Etymology

From the verb 'standardize' + -ed.
