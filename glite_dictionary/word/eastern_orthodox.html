Eastern Orthodox

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːstərn ˈɔrθədɑks / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈɔːθədɒks / (BrE)
relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church or its traditions, beliefs, or members
relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church

Examples

She was raised in an Eastern Orthodox tradition and attends church regularly.
Many famous icons are found in Eastern Orthodox cathedrals throughout Eastern Europe.
The Eastern Orthodox liturgical calendar includes numerous unique religious holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Orthodox - relating to the Orthodox Church (often used for Eastern Orthodox)
  • Antonym: Roman Catholic - relating to the Roman Catholic Church (a major branch distinct from Eastern Orthodox)
