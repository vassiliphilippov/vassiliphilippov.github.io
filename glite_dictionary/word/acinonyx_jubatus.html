Acinonyx jubatus

1. NOUN

/ ˌæsɪˈnoʊnɪks dʒuːˈbeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌæsɪˈnɒnɪks dʒuːˈbɑːtəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the cheetah, a large, slender, spotted cat native to Africa and southwestern Asia, known as the fastest land mammal.
scientific name of the cheetah

Examples

The Acinonyx jubatus population has declined due to habitat loss and poaching.
Researchers studied the hunting techniques of Acinonyx jubatus in the open savannah.
Conservationists are working to protect the natural habitats of Acinonyx jubatus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheetah - a large, slender, spotted wild cat, fastest land animal

Etymology

From Greek *akīnēs* (motionless, unmoved) and Latin *jubatus* (crested, having a mane).
