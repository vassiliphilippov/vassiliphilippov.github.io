pansinusitis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpænˌsaɪnəˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌpanˌsʌɪnəˈsʌɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of all of the paranasal sinuses at the same time.
inflammation of all paranasal sinuses
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with pansinusitis after complaining of severe facial pain and congestion.
A CT scan revealed that she had developed pansinusitis rather than isolated sinus infection.
Chronic pansinusitis can lead to long-term breathing difficulties if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sinusitis - inflammation of a sinus, especially the paranasal sinuses

Etymology

From Greek 'pan-' (all) + 'sinus' (cavity) + '-itis' (inflammation).
