sannup

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæn.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈsan.ʌp / (BrE)
A married male American Indian.
married male American Indian

Examples

The tribal elders respected the sannup for his wisdom and leadership.
Only the sannup was allowed to participate in the ritual according to tradition.
Each sannup was responsible for providing for his family through hunting and fishing.

Etymology

From Eastern Abenaki 'sāniyabe', meaning 'man, husband'.
