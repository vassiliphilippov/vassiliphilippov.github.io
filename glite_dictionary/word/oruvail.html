Oruvail

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːruˌveɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːruˌveɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) containing ketoprofen, used to treat pain and inflammation.
brand of NSAID medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Oruvail to help relieve her arthritis pain.
After taking Oruvail, he noticed his joint swelling decreased significantly.
Patients should take Oruvail with food to reduce the risk of stomach upset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Orudis - brand name for ketoprofen, similar NSAID
