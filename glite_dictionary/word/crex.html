Crex

1. NOUN

/ kreks / (AmE), / kreks / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the rail family, including the corncrake.
genus of birds (corncrake)

Examples

The genus Crex contains only one extant species, the corncrake.
Scientists studied the migratory patterns of Crex across Europe and Asia.
The distinctive call of Crex is often heard in grasslands during the breeding season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corncrake - a migratory bird of the rail family, scientific name Crex crex

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'krex', a kind of bird.
