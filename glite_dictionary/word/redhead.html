redhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdhɛd / (BrE)
a person who has red hair
person with red hair

Examples

Her daughter is a natural redhead with bright freckles.
Many famous actors are proud to be redheads.
In high school, he was the only redhead on the football team.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brunette - a person with brown hair
  • Antonym: blond - a person with fair or yellowish hair

Etymology

From 'red' + 'head', referring to the hair color.

2. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (BrE)
A North American diving duck (Aythya americana) with a grey-and-black body and a reddish-brown head, especially the male.
a North American duck with a reddish-brown head

Examples

During migration, thousands of redheads gather in large flocks on inland lakes.
The male redhead is easily recognized by its striking reddish head color.
Wildlife photographers often seek out redheads in marshes during the breeding season.

3. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌhɛd / (BrE)
A North American woodpecker (Melanerpes erythrocephalus) with black-and-white plumage and a red head and neck.
North American woodpecker with red head

Examples

We spotted a redhead perched on an old oak tree in the forest.
The distinctive coloring of the redhead makes it easy to identify among other woodpeckers.
Each spring, birdwatchers hope to catch a glimpse of the rare redhead in their local parks.
