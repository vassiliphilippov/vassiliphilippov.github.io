constraining

1. ADJ

/ kənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
restricting someone's freedom of action or limiting what is possible or allowed
limiting or restricting action or possibilities

Examples

The rules can have a constraining effect on creativity.
Many students find the curriculum too constraining for their interests.
Her schedule was so constraining that she barely had any free time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restrictive - imposing restrictions or limitations
  • Antonym: liberating - making someone feel free and able to behave as they like
