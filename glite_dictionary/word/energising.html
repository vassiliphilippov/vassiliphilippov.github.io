energising

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛnəˌdʒaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Giving energy or supplying a driving force or impetus; stimulating activity or movement.
supplying energy or driving force

Examples

The new CEO's energising vision brought fresh motivation to the whole company.
A brisk morning walk can have an energising effect on your day.
Her speech was energising and inspired everyone to take action.

Synset Examples

the complex civilization of which Rome was the kinetic center

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stimulating - making something more active or lively
  • Antonym: lethargic - without energy or motivation

Etymology

From the verb energise, from Greek 'energein' (to operate, work).
