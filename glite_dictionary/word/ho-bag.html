ho-bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊˌbaɡ / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A derogatory term for someone considered promiscuous or seeking inappropriate attention.
derogatory slang for promiscuous or attention-seeking person
slang offensive

Examples

He laughed when she teased him about being a ho-bag.
Some people find the word ho-bag deeply offensive and inappropriate in conversation.
She stormed off after being called a ho-bag at the party.

Synset Examples

He laughed when she teased him about being a ho-bag.
She called him a ho-bag for hogging all the attention.
Don't be such a ho-bag and share the snacks!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slut - Derogatory term for a sexually promiscuous woman
  • Synonym: attention-seeker - Someone who deliberately seeks attention

Etymology

Formed from 'ho' (slang for 'whore') + 'bag' (as derogatory suffix).
