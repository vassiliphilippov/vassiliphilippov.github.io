vol-au-vent

1. NOUN

/ vɔloʊˈvɑn / (AmE), / vɒləˈvɑ̃ / (BrE)
A light, hollow case of puff pastry, typically filled with a savory meat mixture in sauce.
hollow puff pastry filled with meat or sauce

Examples

We served a platter of delicious vol-au-vents at the cocktail party.
Chicken and mushroom vol-au-vent is a popular choice at bakery shops.
The chef demonstrated how to fill a vol-au-vent with creamy seafood sauce.

Etymology

From French 'vol-au-vent', literally 'flight in the wind', referring to its lightness.
