centas

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntɑs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntæs / (BrE)
a former monetary unit of Lithuania, equal to one hundredth of a litas
former Lithuanian currency unit

Examples

During his trip to Vilnius, he found an old centas coin in a market.
One centas was worth one hundredth of a litas before Lithuania adopted the euro.
Collectors value centas coins as symbols of Lithuania's monetary history.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: litas - the former basic monetary unit of Lithuania

Etymology

From Lithuanian 'centas', modeled on Latin 'centum' (hundred).
