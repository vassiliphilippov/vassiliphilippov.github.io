horseshow

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
An event where horses are exhibited and judged in competitions for their appearance, training, and skills.
competitive exhibition of horses

Examples

The county fair hosted a horseshow featuring riders from all over the state.
She spent months training her mare for the annual horseshow.
Winning first place at the horseshow was a dream come true for the young rider.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horse show - event for exhibiting and judging horses
