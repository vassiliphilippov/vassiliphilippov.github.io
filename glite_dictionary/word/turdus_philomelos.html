Turdus philomelos

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝːdəs ˌfɪloʊˈmiːləs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːdəs ˌfɪləʊˈmiːlɒs / (BrE)
the scientific name for the song thrush, a common Old World thrush known for its melodious song
scientific name of the song thrush

Examples

The Turdus philomelos is easily recognized by its brown back and spotted chest.
Birdwatchers often record the song of Turdus philomelos during spring migrations.
Studies have shown that Turdus philomelos adapts well to suburban gardens across Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: song thrush - a common thrush known for its song; the common name for Turdus philomelos

Etymology

From Latin: Turdus (thrush) + philomelos (Greek, ‘lover of song’).
