chafed

1. ADJ

/ tʃeɪft / (AmE), / tʃeɪft / (BrE)
(of skin) made sore or painful by rubbing or abrasion.
sore from rubbing or abrasion

Examples

After running the marathon, his thighs were chafed and red.
The ill-fitting shoes left her heels raw and chafed.
Apply some ointment to the chafed area to soothe the discomfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abraded - worn away or scraped by friction

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'chafe', from Old French 'chaufer' (to warm), from Latin 'calefacere' (to make warm).
