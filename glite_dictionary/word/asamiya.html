Asamiya

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɑːmiˌjɑː / (AmE), / əˈsɑːmiˌjɑː / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Assamese people of northeastern India, closely related to Bengali
language of Assamese people

Examples

Many people in Assam speak Asamiya as their first language.
She is fluent in both Asamiya and Bengali.
The poem was originally written in Asamiya before being translated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Assamese - the Indo-Aryan language of Assam; another term for Asamiya

Etymology

From Assamese অসমীয়া (Asamiya), meaning 'of Assam'.
