changed

1. ADJ

/ tʃeɪndʒd / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒd / (BrE)
Made or become different in some respect from a previous state.
made or become different

Examples

After years abroad, he returned a changed man.
The changed circumstances required us to adapt our plans.
Her attitude towards her job has completely changed.

Synset Examples

he's an altered (or changed) man since his election to Congress

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unchanged - remaining the same; not altered
  • Synonym: altered - made different in some way
