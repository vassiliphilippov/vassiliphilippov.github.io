conspirator

1. NOUN

/ kənˈspɪrətər / (AmE), / kənˈspɪrətə / (BrE)
a person who is involved in a secret plan to do something illegal or harmful
a person involved in a secret plot

Examples

The police arrested each conspirator after uncovering the plot.
Every conspirator played a different role in the scheme to overthrow the government.
As the meeting ended, the conspirator slipped away into the night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plotter - a person who makes secret plans, especially to do something illegal
  • Synonym: accomplice - a person who helps another in committing a crime
  • Antonym: law-abider - a person who obeys the law

Etymology

From Latin 'conspirator', from 'conspirare' meaning 'to plot together'.
