traveled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəld / (BrE)
having visited many places or countries; familiar with many parts of the world.
having visited many places or countries

Examples

She is a traveled scholar who has studied in Asia and Europe.
His stories showed that he was quite traveled for his age.
The traveled businessman had friends all over the world.

Synset Examples

a traveled, educated man
well-traveled people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-traveled - having traveled to many places; very experienced in travel
  • Synonym: widely traveled - having traveled in many different places
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00639231-s
Synset POS: s

2. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl̩d / (BrE)
having journeyed over or through many places; having gained experience by traveling, often used in combination (e.g., well-traveled)
having journeyed widely; experienced by travel

Examples

She is a traveled writer whose books are inspired by her journeys around the globe.
The traveled roads of the countryside were filled with travelers and merchants.
He is a widely traveled expert on Asian art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-traveled - having traveled to many places; experienced by travel
  • Synonym: traveled - simple past and past participle of travel (verb form)
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02463260-a
Synset POS: a
