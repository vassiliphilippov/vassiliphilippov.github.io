Cameroonian

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæməˈruːniən / (AmE), / ˌkæməˈruːniən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Cameroon
person from Cameroon

Examples

She is a proud Cameroonian who celebrates her cultural heritage.
Many Cameroonians live and work in cities around the world.
The Cameroonian greeted us warmly when we visited Yaoundé.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cameroonian - of or relating to Cameroon or its people (adjective sense)

Etymology

From Cameroon + -ian suffix, indicating origin.
