cymbidium

1. NOUN

/ sɪmˈbɪdiəm / (AmE), / sɪmˈbɪdiəm / (BrE)
A tropical Asian orchid of the genus Cymbidium, valued for its long-lasting, showy, boat-shaped flowers, and widely grown as a houseplant and for floral arrangements.
tropical orchid with showy flowers

Examples

The cymbidium is a favorite choice for elegant floral arrangements due to its striking appearance and lasting blooms.
She carefully watered her collection of cymbidiums, admiring their vibrant colors.
Many florists recommend cymbidium orchids for wedding bouquets because they stay fresh longer than other flowers.

Etymology

From Latin *Cymbidium*, from Greek *kymbídion* (little boat), referring to the shape of the flower's lip.
