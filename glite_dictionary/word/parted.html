parted

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a leaf or similar structure) having the edge cut or divided nearly to the base, forming distinct divisions or lobes.
(of leaves) divided almost to the base

Examples

The maple has parted leaves that are easily recognizable in the fall.
This species is known for its deeply parted petals, which give the flower a unique appearance.
The botanist noted that the fern had parted fronds with narrow segments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobed - having or formed into lobes; divided by curved or rounded projections
