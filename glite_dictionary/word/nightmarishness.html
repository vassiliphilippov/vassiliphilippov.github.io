nightmarishness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌmɛrɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈnʌɪtmɛːrɪʃnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being extremely unpleasant, distressing, or terrifying, resembling a nightmare.
quality of being like a nightmare

Examples

The nightmarishness of the oppressive regime haunted her long after she escaped.
He described the nightmarishness of trying to find his way out of the tangled, dark forest.
Critics noted the nightmarishness of the film's surreal imagery and sound design.

Synset Examples

The horror and nightmarishness of the scenario is undercut by how mundane it is.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightmare - a frightening or unpleasant dream or experience
  • Antonym: pleasantness - the quality of being pleasant or enjoyable

Etymology

Formed from 'nightmarish' + '-ness', where 'nightmarish' means 'resembling a nightmare'.
