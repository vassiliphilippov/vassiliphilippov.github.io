unreflected

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪd / (BrE)
(of sound or light) not turned back or bounced off a surface by reflection.
not reflected; not bounced back (sound or light)

Examples

The unreflected rays of the sun passed through the window and warmed the room.
Any unreflected sound is absorbed by the heavy curtains, making the studio very quiet.
Scientists measured both reflected and unreflected light to analyze the surface properties.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reflected - (of sound or light) turned back or bounced off a surface by reflection

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + reflected, past participle of reflect.
