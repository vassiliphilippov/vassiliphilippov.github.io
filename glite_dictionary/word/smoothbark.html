smoothbark

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuðˌbɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðˌbɑːk / (BrE)
any of several Australian eucalypt trees with bark smooth except at or near the base of the trunk
Australian eucalypt with mostly smooth bark

Examples

The forest was dominated by towering smoothbark eucalypts that glistened in the sunlight.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the smoothbark compared to other local trees.
A smoothbark can be easily identified by its sleek trunk and rough base.
