single-barreled

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɪŋɡəlˈbɛrəld / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡlˈbærəld / (BrE)
(of a gun) having only one barrel.
having one barrel (of a gun)

Examples

He purchased a single-barreled shotgun for hunting.
The single-barreled design makes the rifle lighter and easier to carry.
Compared to a double-barreled firearm, a single-barreled gun is often less expensive.

Synset Examples

most guns are single-barreled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: double-barreled - (of a gun) having two barrels
