chink

1. VERB

/ tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
to make cracks or narrow openings in something
make cracks or fissures

Examples

Prolonged exposure to sunlight can chink the wooden beams.
The painters were careful not to chink the fresh plaster.
Repeated freezing and thawing may chink the concrete over time.

Synset Examples

  • Synonym: crack - to break or fracture without complete separation

2. VERB

/ tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
to fill small cracks or narrow openings, especially in a wall or between logs, with a sealing material such as caulking or plaster.
to fill cracks or small gaps

Examples

The workers carefully chinked the gaps between the logs in the old cabin.
He spent the afternoon chinking the cracks in the stone wall with mortar.
They chink every small gap to keep out the cold wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caulk - to seal a gap or seam with a waterproof filler

3. NOUN

/ tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for a person of Chinese descent.
offensive slur for Chinese person
offensive slang

Examples

The word chink is considered deeply offensive and should never be used.
He was shocked when someone called him a chink at school.
The movie was criticized for including the ethnic slur chink in its dialogue.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from the English word 'Chinese' or a variant of 'Chinee', used offensively since the 19th century.

4. NOUN

/ tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
a narrow opening or crack, especially one between two objects or parts, such as planks in a wall
narrow opening or crack

Examples

Sunlight filtered through a chink in the curtains, lighting up the room.
She peered through a chink in the wooden fence to see the garden beyond.
The cold wind came in through every chink in the old cabin's walls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crack - a narrow break or slit, especially in a surface
  • Synonym: gap - an opening or space, especially in something solid or between two objects

Etymology

Probably imitative, related to Middle English 'chine' (fissure).

make a high-pitched ringing sound

Examples
