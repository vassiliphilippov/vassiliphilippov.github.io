redheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈredˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈredˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) having red hair, often with fair skin.
having red hair

Examples

She is proud to be redheaded, following the family tradition.
The redheaded boy stood out in the group photo with his fiery locks.
At the audition, a redheaded actress caught the director's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auburn-haired - having reddish-brown hair
  • Antonym: blonde - having fair or pale yellow hair
  • Antonym: brunette - having dark brown or black hair
