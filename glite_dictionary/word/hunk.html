hunk

1. NOUN

/ hʌŋk / (AmE), / hʌŋk / (BrE)
a large piece or lump of something, especially food, with no definite shape
large irregular piece or lump

Examples

She sliced a hunk of cheese and placed it on the plate.
There was a hunk of bread left from last night's dinner.
He threw a hunk of coal onto the fire to keep it burning.

Synset Examples

a hunk of bread
a lump of coal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chunk - a thick, solid piece cut or broken off something
  • Degree Lower: sliver - a small, thin, narrow piece of something cut or split off

2. NOUN

/ hʌŋk / (AmE), / hʌŋk / (BrE)
an attractive, well-built man, especially one regarded as sexually desirable
attractive, muscular man

Examples

All the girls at the party were swooning over the new hunk from the football team.
She couldn't believe her luck when the tall hunk smiled at her from across the gym.
That actor has become a Hollywood hunk overnight after his latest movie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stud - a man who is very sexually attractive or sexually active
  • Synonym: beefcake - a man who is muscular and attractive
  • Antonym: bombshell - a very attractive person, especially a woman
