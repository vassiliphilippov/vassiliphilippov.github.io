dayboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who attends a boarding school during the day but does not reside there overnight
boy attending boarding school as a day pupil
british

Examples

Most of the dayboys leave school grounds by five o'clock each day.
Unlike the boarders, dayboys return home after classes.
The school offers scholarships for both boarders and dayboys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day pupil - a student attending a boarding school but not boarding there overnight
  • Antonym: boarder - a student who lives at school during term time

Etymology

Formed from 'day' + 'boy', referring to a male day pupil at a boarding school.
