impact

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪm.pækt / (AmE), / ˈɪm.pækt / (BrE)
the action of one object coming forcibly into contact with another
the forceful striking of objects

Examples

The impact of the meteorite created a huge crater in the desert.
Both vehicles were damaged at the point of impact.
The glass shattered on impact with the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collision - an instance of one moving object or person striking violently against another

Etymology

From Latin 'impactus', past participle of 'impingere' (to push against).
