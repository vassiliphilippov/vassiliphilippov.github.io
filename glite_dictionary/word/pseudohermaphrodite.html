pseudohermaphrodite

1. ADJ

/ ˌsuːdoʊhɜːrˈmæfrəˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊhɜːˈmæfrədaɪt / (BrE)
a person or animal whose internal reproductive organs are of one sex but who has external sexual characteristics of the other sex
person with mismatched internal and external sex characteristics
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the patient was a pseudohermaphrodite because their internal reproductive organs did not match their outward appearance.
Medical literature now rarely uses the term pseudohermaphrodite due to its outdated and potentially confusing nature.
A pseudohermaphrodite may be born with ambiguous genitalia, making diagnosis and management challenging.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hermaphrodite - organism having both male and female reproductive organs

Etymology

From pseudo- (false) + hermaphrodite, from Greek Hermaphroditos (mythical figure).

2. NOUN

/ ˌsuːdoʊhɚˈmæfrəˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊhɜːˈmæfrədaɪt / (BrE)
a person whose external genitalia are of one sex but whose internal reproductive organs are of the other sex; not a true hermaphrodite, as there is no ambiguity of external sex at birth
person with mismatched internal and external sex organs
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that the patient was a pseudohermaphrodite, possessing male testes but female external genitalia.
In older medical texts, the term pseudohermaphrodite is often used to describe certain intersex conditions.
Researchers now prefer more precise terminology than pseudohermaphrodite when referring to disorders of sexual development.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hermaphrodite - an organism having both male and female reproductive organs

Etymology

From pseudo- (false) and hermaphrodite, after Greek 'hermaphroditos'.

Synset POS: n
