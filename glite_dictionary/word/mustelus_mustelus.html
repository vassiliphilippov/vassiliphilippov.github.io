Mustelus mustelus

1. NOUN

/ məsˈtiːləs məsˈtiːləs / (AmE), / mʌsˈtɛləs mʌsˈtɛləs / (BrE)
A species of smooth dogfish shark found in European coastal waters, recognized by its slender body and smooth skin.
smooth dogfish shark species

Examples

The Mustelus mustelus is commonly found in the shallow coastal waters of the Mediterranean Sea.
Marine biologists tagged several Mustelus mustelus to study their migration patterns.
Local fishermen sometimes catch Mustelus mustelus while trawling for other species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smooth-hound - another common name for Mustelus mustelus, a type of shark

Etymology

From Latin 'mustelus' (a kind of fish or weasel); used in scientific taxonomy.
