excitedly

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈsaɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows excitement or enthusiasm
in an excited manner

Examples

She waved excitedly as the parade passed by.
The children talked excitedly about their upcoming trip to the zoo.
He opened the letter excitedly, eager to read the news.

Synset Examples

she shook his hand excitedly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calmly - in a quiet or peaceful manner, without excitement
