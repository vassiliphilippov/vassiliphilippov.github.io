materialistic

1. ADJ

/ məˌtɪriəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / məˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Concerned with material possessions and physical comfort rather than with spiritual or intellectual values.
focused on material possessions

Examples

Many people criticized the materialistic culture that values wealth above kindness.
He became less materialistic after traveling and seeing how others live.
Children can be materialistic if they are taught to equate happiness with owning things.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or non-material values

Etymology

From 'material' + '-istic', patterned after 'materialism'.
