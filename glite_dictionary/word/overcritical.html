overcritical

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
too ready to criticize; judging too harshly or severely
too ready to criticize harshly

Examples

Her overcritical attitude toward her colleagues made the office environment tense.
Being overcritical of your own work can prevent you from appreciating your progress.
Some students found the professor's overcritical comments discouraging.

Synset Examples

hypercritical of colloquial speech
the overcritical teacher can discourage originality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypercritical - excessively and unreasonably critical
  • Degree Lower: critical - inclined to find fault or judge severely

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessively) + 'critical'.
