geophysical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiː.oʊˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əʊˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the physical properties and processes of the Earth, especially as studied in geophysics.
relating to Earth's physical properties

Examples

The geophysical survey revealed significant mineral deposits beneath the surface.
Several geophysical instruments were deployed to monitor seismic activity in the region.
Rapid changes in climate can be studied using geophysical data collected over decades.

Synset Examples

geophysical sciences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geophysics - the physics of the Earth and its environment in space

Etymology

From geophysics + -al, combining Greek 'geo' (earth) and 'physics.'
