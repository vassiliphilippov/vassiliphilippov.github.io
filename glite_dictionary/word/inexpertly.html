inexpertly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈɛkspɚtli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɛkspɜːtli / (BrE)
In a way that shows lack of skill or experience; in an unskilled manner.
in an unskilled or clumsy way

Examples

The furniture had been assembled inexpertly, leaving some pieces loose.
She played the piano inexpertly, missing several notes in the melody.
The essay was inexpertly written, full of grammatical errors and awkward phrasing.

Synset Examples

an inexpertly constructed lean-to

Related Terms

  • Antonym: skillfully - in a skilled or expert manner

Etymology

From 'inexpert' + '-ly', where 'inexpert' is from Latin 'inexpertus' (untried, inexperienced).
