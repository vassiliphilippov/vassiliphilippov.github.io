volatilisable

1. ADJ

/ voʊˈlæt̬əl.ɪˌzaɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / vəˈlæt.ɪ.laɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
Capable of being converted into a vapor or gas, especially by the application of heat.
able to be converted into vapor

Examples

The compound was highly volatilisable and evaporated quickly during the experiment.
Some pesticides are volatilisable, which can affect their environmental impact.
The volatilisable fraction was measured using specialized laboratory equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volatile - easily evaporating at normal temperatures; tending to vaporize

Etymology

From Latin 'volatilis' (fleeting, able to fly) + English '-able'.
