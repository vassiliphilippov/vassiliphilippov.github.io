cookout

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈkʊk.aʊt / (BrE)
an informal outdoor meal where food is cooked and eaten, typically on a grill or over an open fire
informal outdoor meal cooked and eaten

Examples

We're planning a family cookout at the park this weekend.
The annual neighborhood cookout is a summer tradition.
Everyone brought a dish to share at the cookout last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbecue - a meal or gathering at which meat, fish, or other food is cooked out of doors on a rack over an open fire or on a grill
