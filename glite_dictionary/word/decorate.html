to decorate

1. VERB

/ ˈdɛkəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəreɪt / (BrE)
to make something look more attractive by adding ornaments, color, or other decorative elements
to add decorations or make attractive

Examples

Every year, we decorate the office for the holidays.
She loves to decorate her cakes with fresh flowers and colorful sprinkles.
They spent the afternoon decorating their new apartment with paintings and plants.

Synset Examples

dress the windows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to adorn - to decorate or add beauty to something
  • Synonym: to embellish - to make something more beautiful by adding details or decorations
  • Antonym: strip - to remove decorations or ornamentation from something

Etymology

From Latin 'decorare' (to adorn, embellish), from 'decus' (ornament).
