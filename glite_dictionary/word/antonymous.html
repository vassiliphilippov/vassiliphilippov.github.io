antonymous

1. ADJ

/ ænˈtɑː.nə.məs / (AmE), / ænˈtɒn.ɪ.məs / (BrE)
(of words) having meanings that are opposite or sharply contrasted to each other.
having opposite meanings (of words)

Examples

"Hot" and "cold" are antonymous terms because they have opposite meanings.
The words "happy" and "sad" are antonymous in English.
In the test, students were asked to find the antonymous pairs among the list of adjectives.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: synonymous - (of words) having similar meanings

Etymology

Formed from 'antonym' (from Greek anti- 'against' + onoma 'name') with the suffix '-ous'.
