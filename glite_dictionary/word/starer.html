starer

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈsteərə / (BrE)
a person who looks at someone or something with a long, fixed gaze, often in a hostile or unfriendly way
person who stares intently

Examples

The man felt uncomfortable under the intense gaze of the starer across the room.
She tried to ignore the persistent starer on the train, but it made her uneasy.
Children sometimes become accidental starers when something captures their curiosity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observer - a person who watches or notices something
  • Synonym: glare - to stare in an angry or fierce way; also, the act of staring angrily
