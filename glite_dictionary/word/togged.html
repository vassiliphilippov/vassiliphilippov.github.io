togged

1. ADJ

/ tɑgd / (AmE), / tɒgd / (BrE)
dressed, especially in smart or fashionable clothes; often used with 'up' or 'out'.
dressed in stylish or formal clothes
informal

Examples

He arrived at the party togged up in an expensive suit and tie.
She was togged out in her finest dress for the ceremony.
The children got togged up before going to the wedding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed - wearing clothes; attired
  • Antonym: undressed - not wearing clothes

Etymology

From 'tog', a slang term for clothing, probably from Latin toga (cloak).
