Joan Didion

1. PROPN

/ dʒoʊn ˈdɪdiən / (AmE), / dʒəʊn ˈdɪdiən / (BrE)
(1934–2021) An American writer known for her essays, novels, and literary journalism, often exploring themes of culture and personal experience.
American writer and essayist

Examples

Many consider Joan Didion one of the most influential American essayists of the 20th century.
I recently read a collection of essays by Joan Didion and was captivated by her unique style.
Joan Didion's book 'The Year of Magical Thinking' received critical acclaim worldwide.
