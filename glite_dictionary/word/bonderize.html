to bonderize

1. VERB

/ ˈbɑːndəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɒndəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to coat (metal, especially steel) with a phosphate solution to prevent corrosion
phosphate-coat metal to prevent corrosion

Examples

The factory routinely bonderizes car parts to improve their durability against rust.
After being bonderized, the steel sheets were painted for additional protection.
It is essential to bonderize metal surfaces before they are used in outdoor environments.

Synset Examples

bonderize steel

Etymology

From Bonderite, a trademark for a metal phosphating process developed in the early 20th century, plus -ize.
