untidily

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈtaɪdɪli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪdɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is messy, disorganized, or not neat.
in a messy or disorganized way

Examples

She stacked the papers untidily on the desk before leaving for lunch.
Children often leave their toys scattered untidily around the room.
His hair was brushed untidily, giving him a disheveled appearance.

Synset Examples

Rossi spat very deliberately, and very messily, upon Durieux's party card

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tidily - in a neat or orderly way
  • Synonym: messily - in a messy or disorderly way
