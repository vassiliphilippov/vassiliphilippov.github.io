phasianid

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪziənɪd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪzɪənɪd / (BrE)
any bird of the family Phasianidae, which includes pheasants, partridges, and related species commonly kept as game birds
bird of the pheasant family Phasianidae

Examples

The golden pheasant is a striking example of a phasianid native to China.
Many phasianids have elaborate plumage and complex courtship displays.
The biologist studied several species of phasianid in the Himalayan region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pheasant - large long-tailed game bird, member of Phasianidae
  • Synonym: partridge - medium-sized game bird related to pheasants

Etymology

From New Latin Phasianidae, based on Latin Phasianus (pheasant).
