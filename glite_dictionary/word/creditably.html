creditably

1. ADV

/ ˈkrɛd.ɪ.tə.bli / (AmE), / ˈkrɛd.ɪ.tə.bli / (BrE)
in a manner that is worthy of praise or respect; to a praiseworthy extent
in a praiseworthy manner

Examples

She performed creditably in the national exams, earning praise from her teachers.
The team played creditably despite not winning the championship.
He managed to represent his company creditably at the international conference.

Synset Examples

he did respectably well for his age

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respectably - in a way that is socially acceptable or decent
  • Synonym: commendably - in a way deserving praise

Etymology

From "creditable" + "-ly", formed on the model of similar adverbs.
