roily

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɔɪli / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪli / (BrE)
(of a liquid) agitated or stirred up, making it muddy or turbulent; in a disturbed or unsettled state.
turbulent or stirred-up (liquid)

Examples

After the heavy rain, the river became roily and unsafe for swimming.
The fishermen found it hard to see through the roily water near the shore.
Waves crashed over the boat, churning the lake into a roily mess.

Synset Examples

the river's roiling current
turbulent rapids

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turbulent - (of a liquid) moving in an irregular or violent way
