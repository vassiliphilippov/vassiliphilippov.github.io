lip-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪp ˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈlɪp ˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
shaped like or resembling a lip in form.
shaped like a lip

Examples

The flower has a distinctive lip-shaped petal at its center.
She chose a lip-shaped pendant for her necklace.
The unusual fungus had a lip-shaped cap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: labiate - having lips or lip-like parts (especially in botany)
