Hydrocortone

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪdroʊˈkɔrtɒn / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊˈkɔːtɒn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for hydrocortisone, an adrenal-cortex hormone used medically to reduce inflammation and regulate metabolism.

The doctor prescribed Hydrocortone to manage the patient's adrenal insufficiency.
After starting Hydrocortone, her allergic reaction improved significantly.
