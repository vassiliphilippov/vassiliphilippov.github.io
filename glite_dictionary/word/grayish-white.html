grayish-white

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
having a white color that is tinged with grey
white tinged with grey

Examples

The kitten had soft, grayish-white fur that made her look very gentle.
A grayish-white fog rolled across the fields in the early morning light.
The old photograph had faded to a grayish-white tone over the years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-white - slightly white in color, similar shade
  • Degree Higher: white - the color of pure snow
  • Degree Lower: grey - color between black and white
