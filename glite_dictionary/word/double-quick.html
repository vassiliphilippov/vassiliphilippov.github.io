double-quick

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʌb.əlˈkwɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʌb.əlˈkwɪk / (BrE)
(of marching) very quick; performed at a pace that is twice as fast as the normal marching speed.
very quick marching pace

Examples

The soldiers marched at a double-quick pace to reach the front line in time.
Orders came to advance at double-quick through the mud and rain.
Their drill instructor shouted for the squad to move double-quick across the parade ground.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quick march - marching at a standard brisk pace
