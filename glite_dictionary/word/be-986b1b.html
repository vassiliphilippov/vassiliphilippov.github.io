to be

1. VERB

/ bi / (AmE), / biː / (BrE)
to exist or to have actual being; to have presence or reality
to exist; to have being

Examples

Will there be life on other planets?
Many wonder if ghosts truly be or are just stories.
Does justice always be, or is it an ideal?

Synset Examples

Is there a God?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to exist - to have actual being; to be real

Etymology

From Old English 'beon' (to exist, come to be, become).
