erotically

1. ADV

/ ɪˈrɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈrɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or expresses sexual desire or excitement.
in a sexually exciting way

Examples

The novel was written erotically, with vivid descriptions of passion.
She moved erotically across the dance floor, captivating everyone's attention.
The painting depicts the subject erotically, blending sensuality with art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexually - in a manner relating to sex

Etymology

From 'erotic' + '-ally', ultimately from Greek 'erōtikos' (amatory).
