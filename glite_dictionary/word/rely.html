to rely

1. VERB

/ rɪˈlaɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ / (BrE)
to have trust or confidence in someone or something; to depend on someone or something for support or help
depend or have confidence in

Examples

I always rely on my sister to give me honest advice.
Many people rely on public transportation to get to work.
You shouldn't rely solely on memory when studying for exams.

Synset Examples

you can count on me to help you any time
Look to your friends for support
You can bet on that!
Depend on your family in times of crisis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to depend - to need someone or something for support or help
  • Synonym: trust - firm belief in the reliability or truth of someone or something
  • Antonym: mistrust - lack of trust or confidence

Etymology

From Old French *relier* (to bind together), from Latin *religare* (to fasten down).
