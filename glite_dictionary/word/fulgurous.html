fulgurous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʊlɡjərəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌlɡjʊərəs / (BrE)
Dazzlingly brilliant or flashing, like lightning; strikingly impressive or vivid.
flashing or dazzling like lightning

Examples

The dancer's fulgurous movements captivated everyone in the audience.
He delivered a fulgurous speech that left a powerful impression on the listeners.
The artist's fulgurous use of color brought the painting to life.

Etymology

From Latin 'fulgur' (lightning).
