benefactress

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛnəˈfæktrɪs / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈfæktrəs / (BrE)
A woman who gives help, especially financial assistance, to a person, organization, or cause.
female benefactor

Examples

The school was built thanks to the generous donation of its benefactress.
She became known as a leading benefactress in the local arts community.
The children wrote heartfelt letters to thank their benefactress for her support.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or help

Etymology

From Latin 'benefactrix', feminine of 'benefactor'.
