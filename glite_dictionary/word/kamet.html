Kamet

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːmɛt / (AmE), / ˈkæmɛt / (BrE)
a mountain in the Himalayas of northern India, standing 25,450 feet (7,756 m) high
mountain in the Himalayas, northern India

Examples

Kamet is the second highest mountain in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand.
The expedition team set out to climb Kamet in early summer.
Spectacular views of glaciers can be seen from the summit of Kamet.
