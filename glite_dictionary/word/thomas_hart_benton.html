Thomas Hart Benton

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑməs hɑrt ˈbɛntən / (AmE), / ˈtɒməs hɑːt ˈbɛntən / (BrE)
(1889–1975) United States artist noted for his paintings depicting life in the Midwest and South, central to the Regionalist art movement.
American painter; Regionalist artist

Examples

The works of Thomas Hart Benton capture the spirit and struggle of rural America.
A major exhibition of Thomas Hart Benton's paintings was held at the local art museum.
Thomas Hart Benton influenced many younger artists with his unique depiction of the American landscape.
