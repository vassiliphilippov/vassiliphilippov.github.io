Tropaeolum peregrinum

1. NOUN

/ troʊˈpiː.oʊ.ləm ˌpɛrəˈɡɪnəm / (AmE), / trəʊˈpiː.ələm ˌpɛrəˈɡɪnəm / (BrE)
A species of climbing flowering plant, commonly known as canary creeper, with bright yellow flowers resembling the color of canaries.
species of flowering climbing plant

Examples

The Tropaeolum peregrinum brightened the garden with its vivid yellow blossoms.
Many gardeners plant Tropaeolum peregrinum along trellises for its beautiful climbing habit.
We admired the delicate, fringed petals of the Tropaeolum peregrinum during our botanical tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canary creeper - common name for Tropaeolum peregrinum, a flowering climber

Etymology

From Latin: 'Tropaeolum' being the genus name, and 'peregrinum' meaning 'foreign' or 'wandering'.
