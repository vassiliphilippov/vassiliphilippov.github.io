barman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑrˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːmæn / (BrE)
a man who works at a bar, serving and mixing alcoholic drinks
male bartender or bar employee

Examples

The barman recommended a special cocktail to each guest.
She waved to the barman to order another drink.
After work, he found a job as a barman in the city centre.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bartender - person who serves drinks at a bar
  • Synonym: barmaid - female employee who serves drinks at a bar

Etymology

from 'bar' (counter for serving drinks) and 'man' (male person)
