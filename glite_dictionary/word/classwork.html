classwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈklɑːswɜːk / (BrE)
the tasks or assignments completed by students during lessons in the classroom, rather than as homework
work done by students in the classroom

Examples

All of the classwork must be completed before the end of the lesson.
She is very diligent about turning in her classwork every day.
The teacher collected their classwork for grading after the activity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: homework - schoolwork assigned to be done outside class

Etymology

Formed from 'class' + 'work', first used in the late 19th century.
