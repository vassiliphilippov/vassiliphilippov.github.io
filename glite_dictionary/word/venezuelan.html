Venezuelan

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛnəˈzweɪlən / (AmE), / ˌvɛnɪˈzweɪlən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Venezuela
native or inhabitant of Venezuela

Examples

Many Venezuelans have immigrated to neighboring countries in recent years.
She met a proud Venezuelan living in New York.
The Venezuelan spoke passionately about his homeland.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Colombian - a person from Colombia
  • Degree Higher: Brazilian - a person from Brazil

Etymology

From Venezuela + -an, denoting origin or nationality.

2. ADJ

/ ˌvɛnəzˈweɪlən / (AmE), / ˌvɛnɪzˈweɪlən / (BrE)
of or relating to Venezuela or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Venezuela

Examples

The Venezuelan ambassador attended the international summit.
We enjoyed a delicious meal of Venezuelan cuisine last night.
The Venezuelan soccer team made it to the finals this year.

Synset Examples

Venezuelan oil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Venezuela - a country in northern South America

Etymology

From Venezuela, the name of the country, with the suffix -an.
