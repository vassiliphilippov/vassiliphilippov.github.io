unbeloved

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnbɪˈloʊvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈləʊvd / (BrE)
Not loved or cherished; suffering from unrequited love.
not loved; suffering unrequited love

Examples

After years of feeling unbeloved, she finally found someone who cared for her.
The poet wrote of his unbeloved days, filled with longing and solitude.
He tried to comfort his friend, who felt unbeloved after the breakup.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: beloved - greatly loved; dear to the heart

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'beloved'.
