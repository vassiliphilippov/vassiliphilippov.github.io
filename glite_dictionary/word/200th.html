200th

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / tuː ˈhʌndrɪdθ / (BrE)
coming after one hundred and ninety-nine others in a series; the ordinal number corresponding to two hundred.
ordinal number for two hundred

Examples

She finished in 200th place in the marathon, but she was proud to have completed the race.
This is the company’s 200th anniversary, and they are planning a huge celebration.
He was the 200th customer of the day and received a special gift.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 100th - the ordinal number for one hundred
  • Degree Higher: 201st - the ordinal number for two hundred and one
