Cincinnati

1. PROPN

/ ˌsɪnsɪˈnæti / (AmE), / ˌsɪnsɪˈnati / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Ohio, situated on the north bank of the Ohio River.
a city in Ohio, USA

Examples

I grew up in Cincinnati and still visit my hometown every year.
The baseball team from Cincinnati is known as the Reds.
Many historic buildings line the riverfront district in Cincinnati.

Etymology

Named after the Society of the Cincinnati, which honored Lucius Quinctius Cincinnatus, a Roman statesman.
