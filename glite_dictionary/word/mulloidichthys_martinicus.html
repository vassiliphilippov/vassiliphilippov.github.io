Mulloidichthys martinicus

1. NOUN

/ mʌˌlɔɪ.dɪkˌθɪs mɑrˈtɪnɪkəs / (AmE), / mʌˌlɔɪ.dɪkˌθɪs mɑːˈtɪnɪkəs / (BrE)
A species of goatfish found in the western Atlantic Ocean, greyish in color with a yellow stripe along its body, often seen schooling over sandy bottoms.
a species of Atlantic goatfish with a yellow stripe

Examples

We spotted a school of Mulloidichthys martinicus swimming just above the sandy sea floor.
The bright yellow stripe on the Mulloidichthys martinicus makes it easy to recognize among other reef fish.
Researchers studied the feeding habits of Mulloidichthys martinicus in Caribbean coastal waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow goatfish - common name for Mulloidichthys martinicus

Etymology

From Neo-Latin: genus Mulloidichthys + species epithet referencing Martinique.
