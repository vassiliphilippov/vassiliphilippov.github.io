Coccus hesperidum

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑk.əs hɛs.pəˈrɪ.dəm / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.əs hɛs.pəˈrɪ.dəm / (BrE)
A species of soft scale insect that infests citrus trees and can cause significant damage to crops.
scale insect pest of citrus trees

Examples

The Coccus hesperidum can cause yellowing and wilting of citrus tree leaves.
Many growers monitor their orchards regularly to detect any signs of Coccus hesperidum infestation.
Controlling Coccus hesperidum often requires integrated pest management strategies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brown soft scale - Common name for Coccus hesperidum
  • Degree Higher: scale insect - Insects of the superfamily Coccoidea, many of which are plant pests

Etymology

From Latin: 'coccus' meaning 'berry' (referring to the shape), and 'hesperidum' relating to citrus fruits (the Hesperides).
