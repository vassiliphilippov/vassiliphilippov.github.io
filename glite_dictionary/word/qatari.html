/ kəˈtɑːri / (AmE), / kəˈtɑːri / (BrE)

3. NOUN

/ kɑːˈtɑːri / (AmE), / kəˈtɑːri / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Qatar
person from Qatar

Examples

The majority of the population in Doha are Qatari citizens.

Thousands of Qatari fans attended the football match.
