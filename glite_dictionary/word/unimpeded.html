unimpeded

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpid.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpiː.dɪd / (BrE)
Not slowed down or blocked; able to proceed without obstruction.
not blocked or slowed down

Examples

The river flowed unimpeded through the open valley after the dam was removed.
Their progress toward the summit was unimpeded by any obstacles.
He spoke with unimpeded enthusiasm, inspiring everyone in the room.

Synset Examples

a time of unimpeded growth
an unimpeded sweep of meadows and hills afforded a peaceful setting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unobstructed - not blocked by anything
  • Antonym: blocked - unable to move or proceed because of something in the way

Etymology

From un- (not) + impeded; impeded from Latin impedire (to hinder).
