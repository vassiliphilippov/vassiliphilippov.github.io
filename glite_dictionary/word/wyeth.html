Wyeth

1. PROPN

/ ˈwaɪəθ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪɪθ / (BrE)
Andrew Wyeth (1917–2009), a prominent American realist painter known for detailed works such as 'Christina's World'.
American painter (1917–2009)

Examples

The paintings of Wyeth often depict rural scenes with striking realism.
Art lovers gathered at the museum to view the exhibition dedicated to Wyeth.
Many critics regard Wyeth as one of the most influential American artists of the twentieth century.
