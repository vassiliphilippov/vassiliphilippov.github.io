Wichita Falls

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪtʃɪtɔ fɔlz / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪtɑː fɔːlz / (BrE)
A city in north central Texas, near the Oklahoma border.
city in Texas, USA

Examples

She grew up in Wichita Falls before moving to Dallas for college.
A strong storm caused flooding in Wichita Falls last summer.
The annual hot air balloon festival in Wichita Falls attracts visitors from all over Texas.

Etymology

Named after the Wichita Native American tribe and a natural waterfall on the Wichita River.
