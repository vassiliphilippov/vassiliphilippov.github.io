Mahayanist

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɑː.həˈjɑː.nɪst / (AmE), / ˌmɑː.həˈjɑː.nɪst / (BrE)
A follower or adherent of Mahayana Buddhism.
follower of Mahayana Buddhism

Examples

As a Mahayanist, he follows the teachings of compassion and universal enlightenment.
Many Mahayanists celebrate the Bodhisattva ideal in their practice.
The temple attracts both Theravadins and Mahayanists from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Theravadin - an adherent of Theravada Buddhism
  • Synonym: Buddhist - a follower of Buddhism

Etymology

From Sanskrit *Mahāyāna* (great vehicle) + English suffix -ist.
