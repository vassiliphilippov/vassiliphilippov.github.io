cupboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌbərd / (AmE), / ˈkʌbəd / (BrE)
a cabinet or recessed space with shelves for storing items, especially household goods
storage cabinet or small room

Examples

She keeps all her baking supplies in the kitchen cupboard above the sink.
We discovered an old china set in the dining room cupboard.
The children hid in the cupboard during the game of hide-and-seek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabinet - a piece of furniture with doors and shelves for storage

Etymology

From Middle English 'cubbert', probably from Old French 'coubrete' (cupboard, sideboard).
