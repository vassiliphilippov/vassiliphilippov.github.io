data-based

1. ADJ

/ ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪst / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪst / (BrE)
relying on information obtained through observation, measurement, or evidence, rather than theory or speculation.
relying on data or evidence

Examples

The company made a data-based decision to launch the new product after analyzing market trends.
Effective teaching methods are increasingly data-based, using student performance metrics to guide instruction.
Our data-based approach led to better outcomes than relying on intuition alone.

Synset Examples

experimental results that supported the hypothesis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evidence-based - relying on evidence or proof rather than theory
  • Antonym: theoretical - based on theory or speculation, not observation
