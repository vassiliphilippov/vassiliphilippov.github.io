dalesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪlzmən / (BrE)
a person who lives in the dales, especially in northern England
inhabitant of the dales (northern England)

Examples

The story follows a proud dalesman whose family has farmed the land for generations.
As a dalesman, he knew every hill and stream by heart.
The local museum features photos of dalesmen working in the fields.

Etymology

From 'dale' (valley) + 'man', denoting a man from the valleys (dales) of northern England.
