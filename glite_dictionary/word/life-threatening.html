life-threatening

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪfˌθrɛtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌθrɛtənɪŋ / (BrE)
serious enough to potentially cause death
serious; potentially fatal

Examples

Doctors acted quickly because her condition was life-threatening.
A life-threatening allergic reaction requires immediate medical attention.
Carbon monoxide poisoning can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.

Synset Examples

An aneurysm in the aorta can be life-threatening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatal - causing death
  • Degree Lower: dangerous - able or likely to cause harm or injury
