.22-caliber

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈtuː ˈkælɪbər / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈtjuː ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
(of a gun or its ammunition) having a bore diameter of twenty-two hundredths of an inch (0.22 inch).
describing a 0.22-inch bore in guns/ammo

Examples

He bought a .22-caliber rifle for target practice.
Many beginners use .22-caliber ammunition because it produces less recoil.
The police found a .22-caliber handgun at the scene.

Synset Examples

a .22 caliber pistol
