thereon

1. ADV

/ ðɛrˈɑn / (AmE), / ðeərˈɒn / (BrE)
On that thing or subject just mentioned.
on that thing or subject
legal

Examples

The will stated a trust should be established, and the property vested thereon is to be maintained.
The board discussed the new policy and the issues arising thereon.
He wrote an essay about the treaty and offered his opinion thereon.

Synset Examples

text and commentary thereon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thereupon - immediately or shortly after that
  • Antonym: hereon - on this subject or matter

Etymology

From Middle English, combining 'there' + 'on'.
