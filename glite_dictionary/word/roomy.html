roomy

1. ADJ

/ ˈruːmi / (AmE), / ˈruːmi / (BrE)
(of a building, room, or vehicle) having plenty of space; large and comfortable.
having plenty of space

Examples

Their new house is much more roomy than the old apartment.
The minivan is surprisingly roomy and can fit all our luggage.
She prefers roomy offices with large windows.

Synset Examples

a roomy but sparsely furnished apartment
a spacious ballroom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spacious - having lots of space; similar meaning to 'roomy'

Etymology

From room + -y, formed in English to mean 'having much room'.
