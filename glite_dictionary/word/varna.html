Varna

1. PROPN

/ ˈvɑrnə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
A port city in northeastern Bulgaria, located on the Black Sea; Bulgaria's third largest city and a major tourist, business, and cultural center.
port city on the Black Sea in Bulgaria

Examples

Varna is a popular summer destination for tourists seeking beaches along the Black Sea coast.
The international conference will be held this year in Varna, Bulgaria's third largest city.
Ships from several countries dock at the busy port of Varna.
