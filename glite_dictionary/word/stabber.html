stabber

1. NOUN

/ ˈstæbər / (AmE), / ˈstæbə / (BrE)
a person who stabs someone, especially with a knife or other pointed object
a person who stabs someone

Examples

The police quickly arrested the stabber after the attack downtown.
Doctors described the injuries inflicted by the stabber as serious but not life-threatening.
The stabber fled the scene before anyone could identify him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attacker - person who attacks another
  • Antonym: victim - person attacked or harmed
