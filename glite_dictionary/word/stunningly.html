stunningly

1. ADV

/ ˈstʌnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely impressive or attractive; in a spectacular or striking manner.
in a spectacular or impressive manner

Examples

The bride looked stunningly beautiful in her wedding dress.
The view from the mountain top was stunningly breathtaking at sunrise.
He performed stunningly well in the championship match, impressing the entire audience.

Synset Examples

the area was spectacularly scenic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectacularly - in a manner that attracts attention by being very impressive

Etymology

From 'stunning' + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
