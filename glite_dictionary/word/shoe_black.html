shoe black

1. NOUN

/ ʃuː blæk / (AmE), / ʃuː blæk / (BrE)
a large, showy Asian shrub or small tree, usually with large single or double red to deep-red flowers, commonly known as hibiscus.
a showy Asian shrub; hibiscus

Examples

The garden was filled with blooming shoe black, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
In some cultures, shoe black flowers are used in traditional medicine and hair care remedies.
She planted a shoe black near the porch to attract butterflies and hummingbirds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hibiscus - a genus of flowering plants with large, colorful flowers
