coalescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌkoʊəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊəˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
growing or coming together into a single mass; fusing or uniting to form one.
growing together; fusing

Examples

The festival showcased a coalescent blend of musical styles from around the world.
Scientists studied the coalescent bones to understand the healing process in mammals.
The coalescent traditions of the region reflect centuries of diverse cultural influences.

Synset Examples

coalescent traditions
coalescent bones

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fusing - joining or melting together
  • Synonym: uniting - coming or bringing together as one
  • Antonym: separating - moving or being moved apart

Etymology

From Latin coalescent-, present participle of coalescere (to grow together).
