show biz

1. NOUN

/ ʃoʊ bɪz / (AmE), / ʃəʊ bɪz / (BrE)
the entertainment industry, especially as related to radio, television, film, and theater; show business
the entertainment industry
informal american

Examples

She hopes to make it big in show biz one day.
Many people dream of a career in show biz but few succeed.
He's well known in show biz circles for his innovative productions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show business - the entertainment industry, especially in theatre, television, and film

Etymology

Shortened form of 'show business', originally American English, attested from the late 19th century.
