marbleised

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑrbəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɑːb(ə)laɪzd / (BrE)
having a veined or streaked pattern resembling that of marble, especially in color or texture.
having a marble-like veined pattern

Examples

The artist chose a marbleised finish for the exterior of the sculpture.
These marbleised tiles add elegance to the bathroom design.
She wore a dress with a striking marbleised pattern reminiscent of natural stone.

Synset Examples

marbleized pink skin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marbled - having markings or patterns like marble

Etymology

From 'marble' + '-ise', patterned after the look of marble stone.
