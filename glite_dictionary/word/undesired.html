undesired

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈzaɪrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈzaɪəd / (BrE)
not wanted or wished for; regarded as unwelcome or inappropriate
not wanted; unwelcome

Examples

The policy led to several undesired consequences for the community.
Taking too much medication can result in undesired side effects.
She apologized for any undesired interference in their project.

Synset Examples

an undesired result

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undesirable - not wanted, causing harm or difficulty
  • Antonym: desired - wanted or wished for

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'desired' (wanted)
