krubi

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːbi / (AmE), / ˈkruːbi / (BrE)
A malodorous tropical plant with a large spathe resembling a morning glory, often reaching several feet in diameter.
malodorous tropical plant with large spathe

Examples

The krubi is known for its enormous, foul-smelling spathe that attracts pollinators.
Botanists studied the unusual structure of the krubi during their expedition to the rainforest.
A single krubi can produce a bloom several feet wide, making it a remarkable sight.
