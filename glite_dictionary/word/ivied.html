ivied

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪvid / (AmE), / ˈaɪviːd / (BrE)
covered or overgrown with ivy plants.
covered with ivy

Examples

The old ivied mansion stood at the end of the street.
Tourists admired the ivied walls of the centuries-old church.
Many universities are known for their ivied courtyards and historic buildings.
