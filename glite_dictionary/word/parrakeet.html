parrakeet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæɹəˌkit / (AmE), / ˈparɪkiːt / (BrE)
A small, slender parrot with a long tail, typically colorful, often kept as a pet; variant spelling of 'parakeet'.
small, long-tailed parrot (variant of parakeet)

Examples

The parrakeet chirped happily in its cage every morning.
As a child, I owned a beautiful green parrakeet that could mimic simple words.
Many people keep parrakeets because they are easy to care for and very social.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parakeet - small, slender, long-tailed parrot (standard spelling)

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'parakeet', from French 'paroquet' or Spanish 'periquito'.
