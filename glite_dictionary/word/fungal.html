fungal

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by fungi
relating to fungi

Examples

The doctor diagnosed a fungal infection on her skin.
Certain plants are vulnerable to fungal diseases during wet seasons.
They used a microscope to study the fungal spores in the sample.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bacterial - relating to bacteria
  • Synonym: fungus - a group of organisms including molds, mushrooms, and yeasts

Etymology

From Latin fungus (sponge, mushroom) + -al (adjectival suffix).
