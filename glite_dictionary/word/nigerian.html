Nigerian

1. ADJ

/ naɪˈdʒɪriən / (AmE), / naɪˈdʒɪəriən / (BrE)
Relating to Nigeria or its people, culture, or language.
of or relating to Nigeria

Examples

The Nigerian economy has seen significant growth in recent years.
She cooked a traditional Nigerian dish for the festival.
Many Nigerian films are popular across Africa and beyond.

Synset Examples

the Nigerian capital used to be Lagos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nigerian - a person from Nigeria

Etymology

From Nigeria + -an, denoting origin or relation.
