swelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈswɛld / (AmE), / ˈswɛld / (BrE)
(of a person) having an exaggerated sense of self-importance; conceited or proud.
conceited; overly proud

Examples

After winning the competition, Jason became quite swelled and boasted about his achievements to everyone.
She was so swelled with pride that she barely acknowledged her teammates' efforts.
Don’t get too swelled just because you passed the test on your first try.

Synset Examples

too big for his britches
had a swelled head
he was swelled with pride

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conceited - having an excessively high opinion of oneself
  • Antonym: modest - not overly proud or boastful; humble
