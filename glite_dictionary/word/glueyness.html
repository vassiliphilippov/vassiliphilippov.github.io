glueyness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡluː.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡluː.i.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being sticky and tending to adhere to surfaces
state of being sticky or cohesive

Examples

The glueyness of the syrup made it difficult to wash off my hands.
Researchers measured the glueyness of the new adhesive to compare it with existing brands.
After being heated, the mixture lost some of its original glueyness and became easier to stir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky or adhesive

Etymology

From 'glue' + '-y' + '-ness', forming a noun from 'gluey' (sticky).
