flag-waving

1. ADJ

/ flæɡ ˈweɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈweɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
showing extremely strong or fanatical support for one's country; excessively patriotic
extremely patriotic or nationalistic

Examples

The politician's flag-waving speeches drew both praise and criticism from the public.
She found his flag-waving attitude off-putting, especially during international events.
Critics accused the movie of relying on flag-waving sentiment rather than genuine storytelling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
  • Synonym: nationalistic - having strong identification with one's own nation and support for its interests
  • Antonym: unpatriotic - lacking love for or devotion to one's country

Etymology

From 'flag' (symbol of nation) and 'waving', referring to the act of waving a flag as a patriotic display.
