Malosma

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɒzmə / (AmE), / məˈlɒzmə / (BrE)
A genus of shrubs in the family Anacardiaceae, native to California and Baja California, sometimes classified within the genus Rhus.
genus of shrubs in the Anacardiaceae family

Examples

The chaparral ecosystem is dominated by native plants such as Malosma and ceanothus.
Botanists debate whether Malosma should be classified under the genus Rhus.
Malosma laurina, commonly known as laurel sumac, grows abundantly in Southern California.
