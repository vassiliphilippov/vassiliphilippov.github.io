nonunionised

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˈjunjəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈjuːniənʌɪzd / (BrE)
(of a worker or workplace) not belonging to or represented by a trade union.
not belonging to a trade union

Examples

The company preferred hiring nonunionised employees to avoid collective bargaining agreements.
Many nonunionised workers have fewer benefits compared to their union counterparts.
Management faced little resistance from the nonunionised staff during the transition.

Synset Examples

the workers in the plant were unorganized

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unionised - belonging to or represented by a trade union
  • Synonym: unorganized - not affiliated with or represented by an organization, especially a union

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'unionised', referring to union membership.
