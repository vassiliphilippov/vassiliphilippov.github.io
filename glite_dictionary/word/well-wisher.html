well-wisher

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌwɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌwɪʃə / (BrE)
a person who expresses support or goodwill towards someone, hoping for their success or happiness
person expressing goodwill or support

Examples

Many well-wishers sent cards and gifts after her surgery.
He thanked his well-wishers for their encouragement during the campaign.
At the airport, a crowd of well-wishers gathered to see the team off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supporter - person who supports or backs someone
  • Antonym: adversary - person who opposes or competes with someone

Etymology

From 'well' (meaning 'in a good way') + 'wisher' (one who wishes).
