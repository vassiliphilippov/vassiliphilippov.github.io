basketball

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæs.kɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.skɪtˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A round, inflatable ball used for playing the game of basketball.
inflated ball for basketball game

Examples

He bounced the basketball across the court before passing it to his teammate.
The basketball needs to be properly inflated before the game can start.
After practice, the coach collected every basketball from the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basketball - team sport played with a ball on a court

2. NOUN

/ ˈbæskɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl / (BrE)
A game played by two teams of five players on a court, in which points are scored by throwing a ball through an elevated hoop.
team ball game with hoops

Examples

She joined the school's basketball team and played in tournaments.
Millions of fans watched the national basketball finals on television.
He spends every weekend practicing basketball at the local gym.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: netball - a similar team ball game played especially by women, mainly in Commonwealth countries
  • Antonym: football - a team sport played by two teams attempting to score goals or points; commonly refers to soccer or American football

Etymology

From 'basket' and 'ball', referring to the original peach baskets used as goals.
