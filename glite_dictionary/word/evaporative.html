evaporative

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈvæpɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈvapərətɪv / (BrE)
related to or resulting from evaporation, especially of a liquid turning into vapor
relating to evaporation

Examples

The evaporative process is crucial in cooling systems.
They bought an evaporative cooler for their home because it works well in dry climates.
In some plants, evaporative loss of water occurs through tiny pores called stomata.

Synset Examples

an evaporative cooler

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evaporation - the process of turning from liquid into vapor

Etymology

From 'evaporate' + '-ive'; 'evaporate' from Latin 'evaporare' (to disperse as vapor).
