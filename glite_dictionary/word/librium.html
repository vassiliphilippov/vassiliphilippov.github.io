Librium

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪb.ri.əm / (AmE), / ˈlɪb.ri.əm / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the tranquilizer chlordiazepoxide, used particularly in the treatment of anxiety and alcohol withdrawal.
brand of tranquilizer for alcohol withdrawal
medical

Examples

After the doctor prescribed Librium, her anxiety symptoms improved significantly.
Patients undergoing alcohol detoxification were given Librium to help manage withdrawal.
He asked the nurse about the side effects of Librium before starting the medication.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chlordiazepoxide - a benzodiazepine tranquilizer; the generic name for Librium

Etymology

Trademark; coined name for the drug chlordiazepoxide.
