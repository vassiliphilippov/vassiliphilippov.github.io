Hohhot

1. PROPN

/ ˈhoʊˌhoʊt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊˌhɒt / (BrE)
the capital city of Inner Mongolia in northern China, northwest of Beijing, known as an industrial and cultural center
city in northern China, Inner Mongolia

Examples
