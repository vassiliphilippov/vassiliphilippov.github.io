disused

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈjuzd / (AmE), / dɪsˈjuːzd / (BrE)
no longer used; having been abandoned or obsolete
no longer used

Examples

The disused railway station was covered in weeds and graffiti.
Many disused warehouses in the area have been converted into apartments.
Some of the language in the old law is now disused and unfamiliar to modern readers.

Synset Examples

obsolete words

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Synonym: abandoned - left without needed protection or care

Etymology

From prefix 'dis-' (expressing reversal) + 'used'.
