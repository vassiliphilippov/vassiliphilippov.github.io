Quran

1. PROPN

/ kɔːˈrɑːn, kəˈrɑːn / (AmE), / kɔːˈrɑːn, kəˈrɑːn / (BrE)
The sacred book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the word of God as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad during his life in Mecca and Medina.
sacred book of Islam

Examples

Muslims regard the Quran as the final revelation from God.
She spends time each day reading the Quran in Arabic and English.
The history lecture covered the influence of the Quran on art and culture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Koran - Alternative spelling for the Quran, sacred book of Islam

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Qur'ān' meaning 'the recitation'.
