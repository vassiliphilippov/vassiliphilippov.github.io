Hermosillo

1. PROPN

/ eɾ.moˈsi.joʊ / (AmE), / ɛː.məˈsiː.jəʊ / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Mexico near the Gulf of California, and the capital of the state of Sonora.
city in northwestern Mexico

Examples

The hot climate of Hermosillo attracts visitors looking for sunny weather throughout most of the year.
Many international companies have chosen Hermosillo as a site for their manufacturing plants.
After a long drive through Sonora, we finally reached Hermosillo just before sunset.
