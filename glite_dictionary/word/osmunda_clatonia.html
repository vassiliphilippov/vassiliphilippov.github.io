Osmunda clatonia

1. NOUN

/ ɑzˈmʌndə klæˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / ɒzˈmʌndə klæˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
A North American fern with tall, erect pinnate fronds and a few sporogenous pinnae near the center of the fertile fronds.
species of North American fern

Examples

The Osmunda clatonia thrives in moist woodlands across eastern North America.
Botanists studied the unique frond structure of Osmunda clatonia during their field research.
Gardeners appreciate Osmunda clatonia for its decorative foliage and resilience.
