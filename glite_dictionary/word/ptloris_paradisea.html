Ptloris paradisea

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪt.loʊˈrɪs ˌpær.əˈdaɪ.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˌpɪt.ləʊˈrɪs ˌpær.əˈdɪː.zɪ.ə / (BrE)
A velvety black Australian bird of paradise with green and purple iridescence on its head and tail.
Australian bird of paradise with iridescent colors

Examples

The Ptloris paradisea is renowned for its striking iridescent plumage found in the forests of Australia.
Birdwatchers travel from around the world hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive Ptloris paradisea.
Photographers marvel at how the light accentuates the vibrant colors of the Ptloris paradisea's feathers.
