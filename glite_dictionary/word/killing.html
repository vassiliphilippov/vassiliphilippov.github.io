2. NOUN

/ ˈkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of causing the death of a living being, especially intentionally or as an act of violence.
the act of causing death

Examples

The documentary investigates the mysterious killing of the journalist.
She was charged with the killing of her business partner.
After the killing, the community mourned for weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - the unlawful and intentional killing of a person
  • Synonym: slaying - the act of killing, especially in a violent or deliberate way
  • Antonym: saving - preventing death or harm

Etymology

From Old English 'cyllan' (to strike, kill).
