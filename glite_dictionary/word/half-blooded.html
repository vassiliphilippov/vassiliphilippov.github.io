half-blooded

1. ADJ

/ ˌhæfˈblʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈblʌdɪd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having only one parent that is of pure breed; not of purebred on both sides.
having one purebred parent (of animals)

Examples

The breeder explained that the puppy was half-blooded and therefore not eligible for registration as purebred.
Some farms prefer half-blooded livestock for hardiness and adaptability.
He bought a half-blooded horse, which had only one purebred parent.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: purebred - an animal with parents of the same breed
