lexicographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛksɪkəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌlɛksɪkəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the writing, editing, or study of dictionaries; of or concerning lexicography.
relating to dictionary making

Examples

The team conducted a lexicographical analysis of the new vocabulary.
He is recognized for his lexicographical expertise and numerous published works.
Lexicographical order is used to sort words alphabetically in this index.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lexicographic - alternative form meaning 'relating to lexicography'

Etymology

From Greek lexikographikos, from lexikon (dictionary) + -graphia (writing).
