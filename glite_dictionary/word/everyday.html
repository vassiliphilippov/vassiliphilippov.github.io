everyday

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛvriˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛvriˌdeɪ / (BrE)
suitable for ordinary, routine, or casual occasions rather than special or formal ones.
suitable for routine or casual use

Examples

She prefers to wear everyday clothes when she's not at work.
These shoes are practical enough for everyday use.
He keeps his expensive watch for special events and wears a simpler one for everyday activities.

Synset Examples

casual clothes
everyday clothes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casual - relaxed in style or not formal
  • Antonym: formal - suitable for official or important occasions

Etymology

From Middle English 'everi day', meaning 'each day'.
