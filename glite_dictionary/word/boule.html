boule

1. NOUN

/ bul / (AmE), / buːl / (BrE)
a type of inlaid furniture decoration using tortoiseshell and metal to create elaborate scroll designs, especially in fine cabinetwork
inlaid furniture decoration with tortoiseshell and metal

Examples

The antique cabinet featured an intricate boule design with shimmering metal inlay.
Collectors admire the skilled craftsmanship involved in creating authentic boule marquetry.
Restoring the boule work required sourcing materials similar to those used in the 18th century.

Etymology

From French, after André-Charles Boulle (1642–1732), French cabinetmaker.
