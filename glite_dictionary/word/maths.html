maths

1. NOUN

/ mæθs / (AmE), / mæθs / (BrE)
the study of the logic of numbers, quantities, shapes, and arrangements; mathematics
the study of mathematics
british

Examples

She has always excelled at maths, especially algebra and geometry.
Many students find maths challenging but rewarding once they understand the concepts.
You need good maths skills to study engineering at university.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematics - the study of quantity, numbers, and space

Etymology

Short for mathematics, from Greek mathēmatikos (fond of learning).
