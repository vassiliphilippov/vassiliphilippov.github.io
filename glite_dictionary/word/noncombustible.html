noncombustible

1. ADJ

/ nɑn.kəmˈbʌs.tə.bəl / (AmE), / nɒn.kəmˈbʌs.tɪ.bəl / (BrE)
not capable of catching fire and burning; not combustible.
that does not catch fire or burn

Examples

The building materials used are strictly noncombustible to ensure fire safety.
Make sure the walls are lined with noncombustible panels.
Because the container is noncombustible, it can store chemicals safely.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: combustible - capable of catching fire and burning

Etymology

From 'non-' (not) + 'combustible', from Latin 'comburere' (to burn up).
