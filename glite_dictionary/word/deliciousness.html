deliciousness

1. NOUN

/ dəˈlɪʃ.əs.nəs / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪʃ.əs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being highly pleasing to the taste; extreme tastiness or flavor.
quality of being delicious

Examples

The deliciousness of the cake had everyone asking for seconds.
He couldn't forget the deliciousness of the meal he had in Italy.
The chef took pride in the deliciousness she brought to every dish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - pleasing or appealing to the taste
  • Antonym: blandness - lack of strong flavor or excitement in taste
