Sceloglaux albifacies

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlɵˈɡlɔːks ˌælbiˈfeɪsiːz / (AmE), / ˌsiːləʊˈɡlɔːks ˌælbaɪˈfeɪsiːz / (BrE)
A species of owl native to New Zealand, commonly known as the laughing owl, now considered extinct.
extinct New Zealand owl species

Examples

The Sceloglaux albifacies was once widespread throughout New Zealand before its extinction.
Researchers have studied bones of Sceloglaux albifacies to understand its diet and behavior.
The unique call of the Sceloglaux albifacies inspired its common name, the laughing owl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laughing owl - Common name for Sceloglaux albifacies, an extinct owl from New Zealand

Etymology

From Greek 'skelos' (leg) and 'glaux' (owl); species name from Latin 'albus' (white) and 'facies' (face).
