consular

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːnsjələr / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to a consul or to the official duties, office, or services of a consul.
relating to a consul or their duties

Examples

She visited the consular office to obtain her travel visa.
The embassy provides consular assistance to citizens in distress abroad.
He was appointed to a consular position in the foreign service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consul - an official appointed by a government to live in a foreign city and look after its citizens

Etymology

From Latin consularis (of a consul), from consul (consul).
