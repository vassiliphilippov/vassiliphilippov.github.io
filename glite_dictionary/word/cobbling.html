cobbling

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒblɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or occupation of repairing or making shoes
the trade of shoemaking or shoe repair

Examples

He learned the art of cobbling from his father, who was a skilled shoemaker.
Traditional cobbling is becoming rare as factories mass-produce shoes.
His shop is known for old-fashioned cobbling and quality repairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoemaking - the making of shoes as a profession or craft

Etymology

From 'cobble' (to mend shoes) + -ing (forming a noun of action).
