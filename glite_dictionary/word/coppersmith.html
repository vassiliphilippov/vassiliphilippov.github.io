coppersmith

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑː.pɚˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈkɒp.ə.smɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects made of copper
maker or repairer of copper items

Examples

The coppersmith carefully hammered the metal into a beautiful vase.
Many historical buildings feature roofs and ornaments crafted by a skilled coppersmith.
She apprenticed with a coppersmith to learn traditional metalworking techniques.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blacksmith - person who makes and repairs things in iron

Etymology

From 'copper' + 'smith', modeled after similar terms like 'blacksmith'.
