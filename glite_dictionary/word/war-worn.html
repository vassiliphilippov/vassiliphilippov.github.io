war-worn

1. ADJ

/ wɔr wɔrn / (AmE), / wɔː wɔːn / (BrE)
Damaged, affected, or made weary by war; having endured the devastation of conflict.
damaged or affected by war

Examples

The war-worn city struggled to rebuild after years of conflict.
He gazed around the war-worn landscape, its buildings scarred by battle.
Refugees returned to their war-worn homes, hoping to start anew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: war-torn - damaged or devastated by war
