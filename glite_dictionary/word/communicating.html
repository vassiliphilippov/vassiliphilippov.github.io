communicating

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of sharing or exchanging information, thoughts, or feelings
the activity of conveying information

Examples

Effective communicating is essential for a successful team project.
She has always enjoyed communicating with people from different cultures.
Modern technology has completely transformed the way we approach communicating.

Synset Examples

they could not act without official communication from Moscow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the act of conveying meanings from one entity to another

Etymology

From Latin 'communicare' (to share, to impart), via communicate + -ing.
