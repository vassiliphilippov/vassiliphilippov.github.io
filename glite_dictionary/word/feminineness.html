feminineness

1. NOUN

/ fəˈmɪnɪn.nəs / (AmE), / fəˈmɪnɪn.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or characteristics regarded as typical of women or the female sex.
qualities typical of women

Examples

Her gentle manner and grace gave her an air of feminineness that people admired.
Many cultures have different expectations for feminineness and masculinity in dress and behavior.
The actress embraced her feminineness while also portraying a strong character.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: femininity - the quality of being female or womanly
  • Antonym: masculinity - the qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of men

Etymology

From 'feminine' + '-ness', modeled on similar abstract nouns in English.
