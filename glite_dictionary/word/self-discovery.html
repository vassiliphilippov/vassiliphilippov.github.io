self-discovery

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf dɪˈskʌvəri / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf dɪˈskʌvəri / (BrE)
the process of gaining insight into one's own character, feelings, or motives, often through personal growth or exploration
the process of understanding oneself

Examples

Her journey of self-discovery began when she moved abroad for the first time.
Meditation can be a powerful tool for self-discovery and personal transformation.
Many people travel as a means of self-discovery and understanding their place in the world.

Synset Examples

Her book is all about personal growth and self-discovery.
After years of self-discovery, she identified as a wasbian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-knowledge - understanding of one's own character or abilities

Etymology

self- + discovery; formed by combining 'self' (referring to oneself) with 'discovery'.
