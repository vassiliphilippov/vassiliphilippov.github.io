humanities

1. NOUN

/ hjuˈmænɪtiz / (AmE), / hjuˈmænɪtiz / (BrE)
academic disciplines that study aspects of human society and culture, such as literature, philosophy, and history
academic study of human culture

Examples

She decided to pursue a degree in the humanities rather than engineering.
The humanities foster critical thinking and cultural understanding.
Funding for the humanities has decreased in recent years.

Synset Examples

the college of arts and sciences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arts - subjects of study concerned with human culture, especially literature, philosophy, and the fine arts
  • Antonym: sciences - branches of study dealing with the physical world and its phenomena

Etymology

From Latin 'humanitas' (human nature, refinement, culture).
