googly

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuɡli / (AmE), / ˈɡuːɡli / (BrE)
(in cricket) a ball bowled with a deceptive spin so that it appears to break in one direction but actually breaks in the opposite direction.
deceptive spin ball in cricket

Examples

The bowler surprised the batsman with a skilful googly that knocked over the stumps.
It's hard to predict the direction of a well-bowled googly in a cricket match.
He has mastered the art of delivering a deceptive googly to unsettle the opposition.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: leg break - a type of cricket delivery turning from leg to off for a right-handed batsman
  • Synonym: wrong'un - another term for a googly in cricket

Etymology

Perhaps from 'googy', a pet name or nonsense word, origin uncertain.
