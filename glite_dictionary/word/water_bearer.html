Water Bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ ˌbɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbeərə / (BrE)
(astrology) A person who is born while the sun is in Aquarius, the eleventh sign of the zodiac.
person born under Aquarius sign

Examples

As a Water Bearer, she is said to value independence and originality.
My brother is a Water Bearer because he was born in early February.
Many astrologers claim that Water Bearers are forward-thinking and humanitarian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aquarius - the eleventh sign of the zodiac, symbolized by the water bearer
