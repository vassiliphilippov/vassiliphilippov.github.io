Isfahan

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪsfəˈhɑn / (AmE), / ˌɪsfəˈhɑːn / (BrE)
A city in central Iran, known for its historical architecture and as a former capital of Persia.
city in central Iran, former Persian capital

Examples

Many tourists visit Isfahan to admire its stunning bridges and mosques.
The city of Isfahan once served as the capital of the Safavid Dynasty.
Handmade Persian carpets from Isfahan are famous worldwide.

Etymology

From Persian اِصفَهان (Esfahān).
