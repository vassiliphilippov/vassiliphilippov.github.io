TRNC

1. PROPN

/ ti ɑr ɛn si / (AmE), / tiː ɑːr en siː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, a self-declared state on the island of Cyprus recognized only by Turkey.
Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus (unrecognized state)

Examples

Few countries have diplomatic relations with the TRNC due to its disputed status.
The TRNC declared independence in 1983 but is recognized only by Turkey.
Many international organizations do not recognize the TRNC as a separate country.

Etymology

Acronym from Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.
