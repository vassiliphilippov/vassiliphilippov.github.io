unfunny

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfʌni / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌni / (BrE)
not amusing or failing to cause laughter, especially when intended to be funny
not amusing; not funny

Examples

The movie was so unfunny that the audience barely reacted.
He told an unfunny joke that just made everyone groan.
Critics called the comedian's performance surprisingly unfunny given his usual wit.

Synset Examples

a very unfunny joke

Related Terms

  • Antonym: funny - amusing; causing laughter
  • Antonym: humorous - having or showing a sense of humor; funny

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'funny', meaning not funny.
