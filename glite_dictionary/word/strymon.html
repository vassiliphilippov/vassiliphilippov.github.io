Strymon

1. PROPN

/ ˈstraɪmɒn / (AmE), / ˈstraɪmən / (BrE)
A large and widely distributed genus of hairstreak butterflies found mainly in the Americas.
genus of hairstreak butterflies

Examples

The genus Strymon is well-represented in tropical and subtropical regions.
One of the most common hairstreaks in this area belongs to the Strymon genus.
Researchers discovered a new Strymon species during their expedition in South America.
