timberman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪmbərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtɪmbəmæn / (BrE)
a man who owns or manages a business engaged in cutting, processing, or selling timber
male owner or manager in lumber industry

Examples

The timberman oversaw the operations at several logging camps in the region.
As a successful timberman, he expanded his business throughout the state.
The meeting was attended by every major timberman interested in sustainable forestry practices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lumberjack - a person whose job is cutting down trees for timber

Etymology

From 'timber' + 'man', referring to a man involved with timber.
