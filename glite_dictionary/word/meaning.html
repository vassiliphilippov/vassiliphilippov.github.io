meaning

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the thing or idea that a word, sentence, sign, or symbol represents or is intended to express
the message or idea conveyed by words or symbols

Examples

Can you explain the meaning of this phrase to me?
The poem's meaning was open to interpretation.
Red roses can have a special meaning in different cultures.

Synset Examples

what is the meaning of this sentence?
the significance of a red traffic light
the signification of Chinese characters
the import of his announcement was ambiguous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: significance - the importance or meaning of something
  • Synonym: sense - a particular meaning of a word or expression
  • Antonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or do not make sense

Etymology

From Old English mænan (to intend, signify), related to German meinen (to think, mean).

2. NOUN

/ ˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the idea that a word, phrase, or statement expresses or is intended to express; the sense or significance of something
the idea or sense expressed by something

Examples

The meaning of his message was clear to everyone in the room.
Can you explain the meaning of this symbol to me?
After reading the poem several times, she finally understood its deeper meaning.

Synset Examples

What is the meaning of this proverb?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sense - the meaning of a word or expression
  • Synonym: significance - the quality of being important or of giving meaning
  • Antonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or sense

Etymology

From Old English *mǣnan* (to intend, signify) via *mean* + -ing.

3. ADJ

/ ˈmiːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a look, gesture, or silence) full of significance or conveying a hidden or implied message.
rich in significance; suggestive

Examples

He gave her a meaning glance as they left the room together.
There was a meaning pause before he answered her question.
The actor's meaning smile suggested he knew more than he let on.

Synset Examples

a meaning look
a significant silence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: significant - having a special or hidden meaning; noteworthy
