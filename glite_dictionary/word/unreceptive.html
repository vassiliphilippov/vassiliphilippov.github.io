unreceptive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈsɛp.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈsɛp.tɪv / (BrE)
Not willing to listen to or accept new ideas, proposals, or suggestions.
not willing to accept ideas or suggestions

Examples

The manager was unreceptive to our suggestions for improving the workflow.
She seems unreceptive to any constructive criticism offered by her colleagues.
Their proposal was met with an unreceptive response from the board.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: receptive - willing to consider or accept new ideas

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'receptive' (open to receiving).
