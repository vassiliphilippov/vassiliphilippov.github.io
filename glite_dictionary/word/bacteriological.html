bacteriological

1. ADJ

/ ˌbæktɪriəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbæktɪəriəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to bacteriology or the study of bacteria
relating to bacteriology or bacteria

Examples

The laboratory conducted a bacteriological analysis of the water sample before declaring it safe.
Strict bacteriological procedures are necessary in hospital environments to prevent infections.
She specializes in bacteriological research, focusing on antibiotic resistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacteriology - scientific study of bacteria

Etymology

From bacteriology + -ical; bacteriology from French 'bactériologie', from 'bacterium' + '-logy'.
