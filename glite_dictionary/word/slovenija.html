Slovenija

1. PROPN

/ sloʊˈvɛniɑ / (AmE), / sləʊˈvɛniːə / (BrE)
The native (Slovene) name for Slovenia, a mountainous country in central Europe that was formerly part of Yugoslavia and became independent in 1991.
native name for Slovenia

Examples

The official language in Slovenija is Slovene.
My friend spent a summer hiking in the mountains of Slovenija.
After the break-up of Yugoslavia, Slovenija declared its independence in 1991.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Slovenia - The English name for the same country in central Europe

Etymology

From Slovene 'Slovenija', the country's official name in the Slovene language.
