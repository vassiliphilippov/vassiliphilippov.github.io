xxxi

1. NUM

/ ˌθɜːrtiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌθɜːtiˈwʌn / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for thirty-one (31)
Roman numeral for 31

Examples

The page number was marked as xxxi in the introduction of the book.
She referred to chapter xxxi during her lecture on classical literature.
The ancient inscription bore the date in Roman numerals as xxxi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 31 - the cardinal number thirty-one

Etymology

From Latin numerals: XXX (30) + I (1).
