coalfield

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊlˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlˌfiːld / (BrE)
an area of land where large amounts of coal are found underground and can be extracted
region with underground coal deposits

Examples

The vast coalfield in the region fueled the local economy for decades.
Geologists are surveying the coalfield to determine its remaining reserves.
Many mining communities developed around the South Wales coalfield.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coal mine - a place where coal is extracted from the ground
