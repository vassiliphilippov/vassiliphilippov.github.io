Kitakyushu

1. PROPN

/ kiˌtɑːkiˈjuːʃuː / (AmE), / kiˌtakiˈjuːʃuː / (BrE)
A city in northern Kyushu, Japan, formed by the merger of several cities, known as an industrial and transport hub.
city in northern Kyushu, Japan

Examples

The port of Kitakyushu plays an important role in Japan's international trade.
Several universities and research institutes are based in Kitakyushu.
We visited Kitakyushu during our trip across Kyushu last spring.

Etymology

From Japanese 北九州 (Kitakyūshū), meaning 'North Kyushu'.
