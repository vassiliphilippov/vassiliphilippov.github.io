fireplug

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪərˌplʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌplʌɡ / (BrE)
A short, upright pipe with a nozzle, usually in the street, for drawing water to use in extinguishing a fire; a fire hydrant.
an upright hydrant for fighting fires

Examples

The firefighters quickly connected the hose to the fireplug at the corner.
A yellow fireplug stood near the school playground, freshly painted and easy to spot.
During the parade, a child decorated the fireplug with colorful ribbons as part of the festivities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire hydrant - an upright pipe with a valve for drawing water, usually for firefighting
