U.S.

1. PROPN

/ ju ɛs / (AmE), / juː ɛs / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States, a country in North America comprising 50 states, including Alaska and Hawaii.
abbreviation for the United States of America
american

Examples

The president of the U.S. gave a speech at the summit yesterday.
Products made in the U.S. are exported globally.
Many tourists dream of visiting the U.S. to see its famous landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: United States - full name of the country abbreviated as U.S.
  • Synonym: USA - another common abbreviation for the United States of America

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'United States'.

/ juː ˈɛs / (AmE), / juː ˈɛs / (BrE)

federal government of the United States
american

Examples

The U.S. has implemented new trade policies this year.
Representatives from the U.S. attended the international summit.
The U.S. Supreme Court made a landmark decision.

Related Terms
