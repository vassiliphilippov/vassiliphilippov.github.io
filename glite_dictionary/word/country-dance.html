to country-dance

1. VERB

/ ˈkʌntri dæns / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri dɑːns / (BrE)
to perform a traditional British or American folk dance known as a country dance (contradance)
perform a country/contradance

Examples

They country-danced all night at the village festival.
We learned how to country-dance during our trip to England.
The group happily country-danced in a circle to the lively music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contradance - to perform a traditional folk dance involving lines or sets of couples

Etymology

From 'country dance,' a traditional style of folk dance, first recorded in England in the 16th century.
