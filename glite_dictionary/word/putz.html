putz

1. NOUN

/ pʌts / (AmE), / pʌts / (BrE)
an incompetent or foolish person; an idiot (informal, slang)
a fool or idiot (informal)
informal slang american

Examples

I can't believe you let him drive; he's such a putz sometimes.
Don't be a putz—think before you act next time.
She called her brother a putz for spilling juice on her laptop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person

Etymology

From Yiddish פּוץ (puts), meaning 'penis', later used metaphorically for a fool.

2. NOUN

/ pʌts / (AmE), / pʌts / (BrE)

slang vulgar

Examples
