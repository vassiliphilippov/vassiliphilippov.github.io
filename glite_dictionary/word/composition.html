composition

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the art and technique of setting text in type for printing, especially using movable type
setting type for printing

Examples

The printer took great pride in his meticulous composition of each page.
Manual composition with movable type is now a rare craft.
Advances in technology have transformed the process of composition for publishing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typesetting - the process of arranging type for printing or publication

Etymology

From Latin 'compositio' (a putting together); related to 'compose'.
