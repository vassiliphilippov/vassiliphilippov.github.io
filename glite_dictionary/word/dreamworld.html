dreamworld

1. NOUN

/ ˈdriːm.wɜrld / (AmE), / ˈdriːm.wɜːld / (BrE)
an imaginary or idealized place, often viewed as perfect or unattainable, existing only in dreams or the imagination
an imaginary or idealized place

Examples

Some people escape from their problems by retreating into a dreamworld of their own creation.
The novel describes a dreamworld where everything is peaceful and nothing ever goes wrong.
He needed to face reality instead of living in a dreamworld filled with impossible hopes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasyland - a place of fantasy or make-believe
  • Antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist, as opposed to an idealized or imagined state
