fungous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
of or relating to fungi; caused by or resembling a fungus
relating to fungi

Examples

The leaves showed fungous growth after the heavy rains.
A fungous infection can destroy entire crops if not treated quickly.
Under the microscope, the fungous structures were clearly visible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungal - of or relating to fungi

Etymology

From Latin 'fungosus' (full of fungi), from 'fungus' (fungus).
