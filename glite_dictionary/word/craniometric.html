craniometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌkreɪniəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkreɪnɪəʊˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the scientific measurement of the skull, especially in anthropology or anatomy
relating to measuring the skull
medical

Examples

The study included a detailed analysis of craniometric differences among populations.
Researchers used craniometric data to classify the ancient skulls.
A craniometric approach can help determine evolutionary relationships between species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craniometry - the scientific measurement of the skull

Etymology

From craniometry (from Greek kranion 'skull' + -metry 'process of measuring') + -ic.
