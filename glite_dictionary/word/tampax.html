Tampax

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæm.pæks / (AmE), / ˈtæm.pæks / (BrE)
A proprietary brand of tampon used to absorb menstrual flow.
brand of menstrual tampon

Examples

She always carries a Tampax in her bag just in case.
The store was out of Tampax, so she bought another brand of tampons.
Many women trust Tampax for comfort and reliability during their period.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tampon - a plug of soft material inserted into the vagina to absorb menstrual blood

Etymology

Trademark, formed from 'tampon' and 'pack'.
