Carduelis spinus

1. NOUN

/ kɑrˈduːəlɪs ˈspaɪnəs / (AmE), / kɑːˈdjuːəlɪs ˈspɪnəs / (BrE)
A small yellow-and-black Eurasian finch with a sharp beak, also known as the Eurasian siskin.
small yellow-and-black Eurasian finch

Examples

Birdwatchers were excited to spot a Carduelis spinus in the park during migration season.
The distinctive markings of the Carduelis spinus make it easy to identify among other finches.
During the winter, flocks of Carduelis spinus can often be seen feeding on birch seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: siskin - a small finch with yellow-and-black coloring

Etymology

From Latin: 'Carduelis' meaning 'goldfinch', and 'spinus' meaning 'siskin'.
