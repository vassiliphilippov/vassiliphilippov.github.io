look-alike

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊk əˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʊk əˌlaɪk / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles another, especially a famous person
person who closely resembles another

Examples

Several fans thought he was the real celebrity, but he was just a look-alike hired for the event.
She earns a living as a Marilyn Monroe look-alike at parties and events.
The movie needed a convincing look-alike for the action star's dangerous stunts.

Synset Examples

he could be Gingrich's double
she's the very image of her mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - a person who looks like another and may substitute for them
  • Synonym: doppelganger - a person who looks exactly like someone else, often used in folklore

Etymology

From the phrase 'look alike', meaning 'to appear similar'.
