insanitary

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsænəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ɪnˈsænɪtəri / (BrE)
not clean or healthful; likely to cause disease or adversely affect health
not sanitary; likely to harm health

Examples

The slum's insanitary conditions led to the rapid spread of disease.
Authorities closed the restaurant due to its insanitary kitchen and improper food handling.
The refugees were forced to live in an insanitary camp with little access to clean water.

Synset Examples

unsanitary open sewers
grim and unsanitary conditions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsanitary - not clean or healthful; likely to cause disease
  • Antonym: sanitary - clean and healthful; free from dirt and germs

Etymology

Formed from prefix in- (not) + sanitary.
