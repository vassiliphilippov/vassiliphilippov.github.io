newsworthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuːzˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌwɜːði / (BrE)
sufficiently interesting, important, or unusual to merit mention in news reports
suitable for reporting in the news

Examples

The editor decided that the story was not newsworthy enough to make the front page.
They are constantly seeking newsworthy events to attract more viewers to their broadcasts.
The discovery of the endangered species was considered highly newsworthy by the local press.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noteworthy - deserving attention; notable

Etymology

From 'news' + 'worthy' (worthy of news).
