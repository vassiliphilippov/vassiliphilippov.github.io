half-length

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæfˌlɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌlɛŋkθ / (BrE)
a portrait or representation showing a person from the waist up
portrait from the waist up

Examples

The artist painted a stunning half-length portrait of the duchess.
A half-length photograph is often used for professional headshots.
The exhibition featured several half-length oil paintings from the 19th century.
