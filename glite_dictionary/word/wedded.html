wedded

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having been taken in marriage; joined in matrimony.
joined in marriage

Examples

They have remained wedded for over fifty years, celebrating every anniversary together.
He spoke of his wedded bliss during the reception speech.
Many couples share rings on their wedded day to symbolize their union.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: married - united in marriage; having a husband or wife
  • Antonym: unmarried - not married; single

Etymology

Past participle of 'wed,' from Old English 'weddian' (to pledge oneself, marry).
