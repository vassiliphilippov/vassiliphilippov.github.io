spelaeology

1. NOUN

/ ˌspiːliˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌspiːliˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study and exploration of caves.
the study of caves

Examples

She developed an interest in spelaeology after visiting several famous caves.
Courses in spelaeology include both geology and biology components.
Advances in spelaeology have led to new discoveries about underground ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speleology - the study and exploration of caves (alternate spelling)

Etymology

From Greek spelaion (cave) + -logy (study of).
