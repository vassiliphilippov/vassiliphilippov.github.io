kike

1. NOUN

/ kaɪk / (AmE), / kaɪk / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term for a Jewish person; considered an ethnic slur.
offensive slur for a Jew
offensive

Examples

He was shocked and angered to hear someone use the word kike in conversation.
The book discusses the history and impacts of slurs such as kike in Western societies.
Educators warn students about the harm inflicted by words like kike and other hateful language.

Etymology

Of unknown origin; first recorded in early 20th-century American English as an ethnic slur.
