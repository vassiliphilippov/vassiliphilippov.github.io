cussedly

1. ADV

/ ˈkʌsɪdli / (AmE), / ˈkʌsɪdli / (BrE)
In a stubborn, obstinate, or uncooperative manner.
in a stubborn manner
informal

Examples

He cussedly refused to admit he was wrong, despite all the evidence against him.
The old engine cussedly refused to start on cold mornings.
She cussedly held onto her beliefs, no matter what anyone said.

Synset Examples

she remained stubbornly in the same position

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stubbornly - in a stubborn or obstinate manner
  • Synonym: obstinately - in a stubbornly refusing to change opinion or action

Etymology

From the adjective 'cussed' (stubborn), itself a dialectal alteration of 'accursed'.
