uncluttered

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈklʌtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈklʌtəd / (BrE)
(of a space, mind, style, etc.) clear of unnecessary items or distractions; tidy and simple.
clear of unnecessary items; tidy

Examples

The new office design is uncluttered and promotes productivity.
She likes to keep her schedule uncluttered so she can focus on important tasks.
His writing style is uncluttered and easy to understand.

Synset Examples

an uncluttered room
the unlittered shoulders of the road

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cluttered - filled with too many things, untidy or messy
