eldorado

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛl dəˈrɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɛl dəˈrɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
an imaginary place of great wealth and opportunity, often pursued as a goal of great fortune
imaginary place of great wealth

Examples

Some believed California would be an eldorado for those seeking gold.
For tech entrepreneurs, Silicon Valley is often seen as an eldorado of opportunity.
Many explorers lost their lives searching for the fabled eldorado in South America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: utopia - an imagined perfect or ideal place

Etymology

From Spanish 'El Dorado' (the gilded one), originally referring to a legendary city of gold in South America.
