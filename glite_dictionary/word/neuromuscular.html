neuromuscular

1. ADJ

/ ˌnʊroʊˈmʌskjələr / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈmʌskjələ / (BrE)
Relating to both the nerves and muscles, or to their interaction.
relating to nerves and muscles
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that neuromuscular disorders can affect both movement and coordination.
She participated in a neuromuscular training program to improve her balance after the injury.
New research focuses on how neuromuscular diseases impact children differently than adults.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: muscular - relating to muscles
  • Degree Lower: neurological - relating to nerves or the nervous system

Etymology

From Greek 'neuron' (nerve) and Latin 'musculus' (muscle).
