prickling

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
A tingling or stinging sensation on the skin, as if caused by many tiny sharp points.
tingling, stinging skin sensation

Examples

She felt a sudden prickling on the back of her neck as she walked in the dark.
The prickling in his fingers made him worry about poor circulation.
After sitting too long, a prickling sensation spread through her legs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tingling - a slightly stinging or tickling sensation

Etymology

From Middle English 'priklen', related to 'prick', meaning to pierce or sting.
