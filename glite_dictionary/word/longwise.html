longwise

1. ADV

/ ˈlɔŋˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in the direction of the length; along the longer side of something
in the direction of the length

Examples

Cut the cucumber longwise to make thin strips for the salad.
The lumber was split longwise to create sturdy beams.
She folded the fabric longwise before sewing the edges together.

Synset Examples

He cut the paper lengthwise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lengthwise - in the direction of the length
