witchlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪtʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a witch or witchcraft.
resembling a witch or witchcraft

Examples

The old woman's witchlike appearance frightened the children.
She moved with a witchlike grace that mesmerized everyone in the room.
A witchlike cackle echoed through the dark, misty forest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: witchy - having qualities or characteristics of a witch
