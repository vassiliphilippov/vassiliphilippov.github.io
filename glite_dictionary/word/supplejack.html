supplejack

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌp.əlˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈsʌp.əlˌdʒæk / (BrE)
A walking stick made from the wood of an American tropical vine, particularly of the genus Berchemia.
walking stick from a tropical American vine

Examples

He carved a supplejack to help him on his hikes through the forest.
The old supplejack was passed down to him by his grandfather.
Local craftsmen often make supplejacks from the abundant vines in the area.

Etymology

From 'supple' (flexible) + 'jack' (a term for a man or implement), referring to the flexibility of the vine.
