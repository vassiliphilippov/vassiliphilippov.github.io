Linnean

1. ADJ

/ lɪˈniː.ən / (AmE), / lɪˈniː.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Carl Linnaeus or the system of classification he developed for organizing plants and animals.
relating to Linnaeus or his classification system

Examples

The Linnean system is still widely used in modern biological classification.
Many scientists attended the annual Linnean Society meeting.
Species names in the Linnean format use a two-part Latin designation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Linnaean - Alternative spelling; of or relating to Linnaeus or his system

Etymology

From the name of Swedish botanist Carl Linnaeus (Latinized form) + English adjectival suffix -an.
