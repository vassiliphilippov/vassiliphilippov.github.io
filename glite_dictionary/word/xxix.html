XXIX

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛks aɪ ɛks / (AmE), / ɛks ɛks aɪ ɛks / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number 29
Roman numeral for 29

Examples

The pageant was held for the XXIX time in the city.
He read about the events of the XXIX Olympiad.
The inscription on the statue was dated XXIX A.D.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 29 - the number twenty-nine

Etymology

From Latin numerals: XX (20) + IX (9).
