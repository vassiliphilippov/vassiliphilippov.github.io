tooshie

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːʃi / (AmE), / ˈtuːʃi / (BrE)
(informal) the buttocks; the fleshy part of the body you sit on.
(informal) buttocks; rear end
informal slang

Grandma told him to sit his tooshie down and finish breakfast.
After hours of sitting, my tooshie was starting to go numb.
