Mishnaic

1. ADJ

/ mɪʃˈneɪɪk / (AmE), / mɪʃˈneɪɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Mishna, the first part of the Talmud in Jewish tradition
relating to the Mishna (first Talmud part)

Examples

The rabbi specialized in Mishnaic Hebrew and its unique grammar.
Many Mishnaic laws differ from those found in later Jewish legal texts.
Her research focused on Mishnaic literature from the second century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Talmudic - relating to the Talmud

Etymology

From Hebrew משנאי (*mishnai*), from Mishna (oral law text) plus English suffix '-ic'.
