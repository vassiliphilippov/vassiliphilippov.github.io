nonflavoured

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / nɒnˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
(of a product) having no flavoring added; plain or natural in taste.
without added flavoring

Examples

I prefer nonflavoured yogurt, as it has a simpler, more natural taste.
This nonflavoured water is perfect for people who dislike additives in their drinks.
The company offers both flavoured and nonflavoured options for their tea blends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unflavored - having no flavor added
  • Antonym: flavored - having flavor added
