eye-drop

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌdrɒp / (BrE)
a method of applying small amounts of liquid directly to the eye, typically for medical treatment or irrigation
liquid medication/application for the eye
medical

Examples

The doctor administered an eye-drop to reduce the patient's irritation.
Always wash your hands before using an eye-drop to avoid infection.
After her surgery, she used an eye-drop several times a day as prescribed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye lotion - a liquid for cleansing or soothing the eye

2. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ ˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌdrɒp / (BrE)
a single drop of liquid, often medication, intended to be applied to the eye
a drop of liquid for the eye

Examples

She put an eye-drop into her irritated eye before leaving for work.
Always wash your hands before using an eye-drop to avoid contamination.
The doctor recommended an eye-drop to relieve his allergy symptoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eye drops - medication or liquid in drop form for the eyes (plural form)
