cadastral

1. ADJ

/ kəˈdæstrəl / (AmE), / kəˈdæstrəl / (BrE)
relating to the records, maps, or surveys of land boundaries and ownership, especially as part of a cadastre.
relating to land records or surveys

Examples

The city commissioned a new cadastral survey to update property boundaries.
All land ownership changes must be reflected in the cadastral records.
A cadastral map provides precise information about land plots and their usage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cadastre - an official register of property showing extent, value, and ownership

Etymology

From French 'cadastre' (register of property), from Italian 'catastro', ultimately from Greek 'katastikhon' (notebook).
