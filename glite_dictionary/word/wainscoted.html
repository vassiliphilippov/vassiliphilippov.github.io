wainscoted

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪnˌskoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪnskətɪd / (BrE)
(of a wall or room) fitted or decorated with wooden panels, especially on the lower part of the walls.
fitted with wooden wall panels

Examples

The grand dining room was wainscoted in rich mahogany panels.
Our study features beautifully wainscoted walls that add warmth to the space.
They restored the old house, keeping the original wainscoted corridors intact.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paneled - covered or decorated with panels

Etymology

From 'wainscot' (wooden paneling) + '-ed', forming a past participle and adjective.
