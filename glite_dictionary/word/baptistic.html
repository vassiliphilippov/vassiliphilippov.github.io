Baptistic

1. ADJ

/ bæpˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / bæpˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Baptist church or its members
of or relating to the Baptist church

Examples

The church adopted a distinctly Baptistic approach to its worship services.
His views are more Baptistic than those of other Protestant leaders.
The debate centered around Baptistic interpretations of scripture.

Synset Examples

Baptistic baptismal practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Baptist - a member of a Protestant Christian denomination emphasizing baptism by immersion

Etymology

From 'Baptist' + -ic, forming an adjective meaning 'of the Baptists'.
