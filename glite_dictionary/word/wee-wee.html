to wee-wee

1. VERB

/ ˈwiː ˌwiː / (AmE), / ˈwiː ˌwiː / (BrE)
(informal) To urinate; to pass urine.
urinate (informal, childish)
informal slang

Examples

The little boy asked if he could wee-wee before bedtime.
She realized her dog needed to wee-wee and rushed outside.
During the long car ride, Tim kept saying he had to wee-wee.

Synset Examples

Again, the cat had made on the expensive rug

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to urinate - to discharge urine from the body

Etymology

Reduplication of 'wee' (childish term for urine), imitating infantile speech.
