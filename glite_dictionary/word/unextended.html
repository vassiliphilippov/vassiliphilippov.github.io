unextended

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈstɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈstɛndɪd / (BrE)
not extended or stretched out; in its normal, contracted, or unreached form.
not stretched out or extended

Examples

With his unextended arm, he kept the book close to his chest.
The table remains unextended until extra guests arrive for dinner.
Keep the hose unextended to avoid unnecessary tangling.

Synset Examples

an unextended arm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extended - stretched out or made longer

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'extended'.
