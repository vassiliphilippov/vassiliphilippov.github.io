pugilist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpjuːdʒəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈpjuːdʒɪlɪst / (BrE)
A person who fights with their fists, especially as a professional boxer.
person who fights with fists (boxer)

Examples

The young pugilist impressed the crowd with his speed and agility in the ring.
Many famous pugilists have inspired generations of boxing enthusiasts.
The retired pugilist now trains aspiring athletes at the local gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boxer - a person who takes part in boxing matches, especially as a sport or profession

Etymology

From Latin 'pugil' (boxer), from 'pugnus' (fist).
