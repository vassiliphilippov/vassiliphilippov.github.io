USSS

1. PROPN

/ ju ɛs ɛs ɛs / (AmE), / juː ɛs ɛs ɛs / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States Secret Service, the federal agency responsible for protecting U.S. leaders, distinguished foreign visitors, and investigating counterfeiting and certain financial crimes.
United States Secret Service (federal agency)

Examples

The USSS coordinated a multi-agency operation to prevent counterfeiting during the event.
Members of the USSS escorted the foreign dignitaries to their hotel.
After leaving office, the former president continued to receive protection from the USSS.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Secret Service - the United States Secret Service, a federal investigative and protective agency

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'United States Secret Service'.
