Koran

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɑːn / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːn / (BrE)
the sacred book of Islam, believed to be the word of God as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad.
the sacred book of Islam

Examples

The teachings of the Koran play a central role in Muslim life and belief.
She studied passages from the Koran in her world religions class.
Many people learn to recite the Koran in its original Arabic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Qur'an - alternative spelling, identical meaning: the Islamic sacred text

Etymology

From Arabic 'al-Qur'ān', meaning 'the recitation' or 'the reading'.
