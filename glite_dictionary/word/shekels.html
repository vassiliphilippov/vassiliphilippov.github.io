shekels

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛkəlz / (AmE), / ˈʃɛkəlz / (BrE)
(informal) Money; cash.
money (informal)
informal

Examples

I need to save up some shekels before I can go on vacation.
He worked all summer to earn a few extra shekels.
Sorry, I can't lend you any shekels right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: money - general term for means of payment, currency
  • Synonym: dough - slang term for money
  • Synonym: cash - money in the form of coins or notes

Etymology

From Hebrew *sheqel* (a unit of currency).
