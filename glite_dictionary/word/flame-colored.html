flame-colored

1. ADJ

/ fleɪm ˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / fleɪm ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having the bright orange-red color of flames
bright orange-red in color

Examples

The flame-colored tulips stood out strikingly in the garden.
She wore a flame-colored scarf that caught everyone's attention.
The sky turned flame-colored during the spectacular sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiery - resembling flames in appearance or color
  • Degree Lower: crimson - deep red color
  • Degree Lower: scarlet - bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
