Motrin

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtrɪn / (AmE), / ˈməʊtrɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (ibuprofen) used to reduce pain and inflammation, often for arthritis and as a fever reducer.
brand of ibuprofen pain reliever
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended taking Motrin to help manage her arthritis pain.
After surgery, he used Motrin to keep his fever down.
Make sure to take Motrin with food to avoid stomach irritation.

Synset Examples

daily use of ibuprofen can irritate the stomach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ibuprofen - a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used as a pain reliever and anti-inflammatory
