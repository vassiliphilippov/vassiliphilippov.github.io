earreach

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌritʃ / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌriːtʃ / (BrE)
The distance or range within which a sound, especially a voice, can be heard.
range sound can be heard
archaic

Examples

The guide spoke loudly so everyone within earreach could hear the instructions.
Stay within earreach while we're hiking in the woods.
Her warning was only meant for those in immediate earreach.

Synset Examples

the children were told to stay within earshot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earshot - the distance within which a sound, especially someone's voice, can be heard

Etymology

Formed from 'ear' + 'reach', meaning the extent to which the ear can detect sound.
