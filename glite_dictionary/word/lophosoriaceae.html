Lophosoriaceae

1. NOUN

/ loʊˌfoʊsɔːriˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ləʊˌfɒsɔːriˈeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
A very small family of tree ferns found mainly in the Southern Hemisphere.
family of tree ferns

Examples

The Lophosoriaceae are primarily distributed in the tropical regions of the Southern Hemisphere.
Botanists have revised the classification of Lophosoriaceae in recent years due to new genetic information.
Specimens from the Lophosoriaceae family are often found in moist forest environments.

Etymology

From the genus name Lophosoria + standard family suffix -aceae, used for plant families (from Latin).
