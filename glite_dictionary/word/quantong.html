quantong

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɑnˌtʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.tɒŋ / (BrE)
an Australian red fruit, typically used in desserts or for making jam
Australian red fruit used in jam or desserts

Examples

The quantong is prized for its sharp flavor in homemade jam.
We enjoyed a traditional Australian dessert made with fresh quantongs.
She picked a basketful of quantongs during their bushwalk.

Etymology

From Wiradjuri (an Australian Aboriginal language) word for the fruit, likely via early colonial English.
