Lumpenus

1. NOUN

/ lʌmˈpiːnəs / (AmE), / lʌmˈpiːnəs / (BrE)
A genus of slender, elongated marine fishes in the family Stichaeidae, commonly known as pricklebacks.
genus of marine fishes (pricklebacks)

Examples

The Lumpenus genus includes several species found in the cold coastal waters of the North Pacific.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Lumpenus genus near the Aleutian Islands.
My research focuses on the ecological role of Lumpenus in rocky intertidal habitats.
