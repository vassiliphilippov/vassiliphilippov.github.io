Ludian

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːdiən / (AmE), / ˈluːdiən / (BrE)
A Baltic-Finnic language spoken in parts of northwest Russia.
Baltic-Finnic language of northwest Russia

Examples

The Ludian language is spoken by a small community in Karelia.
Linguists study Ludian to understand the evolution of Finnic languages.
He is fluent in Finnish, Veps, and Ludian.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Finnic - relating to the Finnic group of the Uralic language family
