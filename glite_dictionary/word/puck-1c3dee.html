puck

1. NOUN

/ pʌk / (AmE), / pʌk / (BrE)
A vulcanized rubber disk used in ice hockey, measuring about 3 inches in diameter, which players hit instead of a ball.
rubber disk used in ice hockey

Examples

The player shot the puck across the ice and scored a goal.
Make sure the puck stays inside the rink during practice.
He collected a souvenir puck after the championship game.
