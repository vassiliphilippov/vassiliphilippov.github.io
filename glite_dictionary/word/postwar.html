postwar

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊst.wɑr / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst.wɑː / (BrE)
Relating to the period immediately following a war, especially a major one.
relating to the time after a war

Examples

Many postwar developments changed the face of the city.
The country experienced rapid economic growth in the postwar years.
Her novels often explore the challenges of postwar society.

Synset Examples

postwar resettlement
postwar inflation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prewar - relating to the period before a war

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'post-' (after) and 'war'.
