vaping

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of inhaling and exhaling vapor produced by an electronic cigarette or similar device, typically containing nicotine
inhaling vapor from an e-cigarette

Examples

Many young adults have started vaping as an alternative to traditional cigarettes.
The government is considering new regulations on vaping products.
She quit smoking and switched to vaping to reduce her nicotine intake.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smoking - the act of inhaling and exhaling the smoke of burning tobacco

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'vape' (to inhale vapor) + -ing; 'vape' is a back-formation from 'vaporizer'.
