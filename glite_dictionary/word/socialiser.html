socialiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who frequently takes part in social activities or enjoys interacting with others
person who enjoys social activities

Examples

Emily has always been a natural socialiser, making friends wherever she goes.
Even at a crowded party, the socialiser feels right at home.
Tom isn't much of a socialiser, preferring quiet evenings to large gatherings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extrovert - an outgoing, sociable person
  • Antonym: introvert - a person who tends to be shy and reserved
