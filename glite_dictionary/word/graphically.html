graphically

1. ADV

/ ˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to visual or graphic aspects, such as form, design, or illustration.
in relation to visual or graphic aspects

Examples

The charts were presented very graphically to engage the audience.
This painting is graphically striking, using bold lines and colors.
The magazine specializes in graphically innovative layouts.

Synset Examples

graphically interesting designs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visually - in a way that relates to sight or seeing

Etymology

From 'graphic' + '-ally', patterned after the formation of adverbs from adjectives.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00133607-r
Synset POS: r

2. ADV

/ ˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that vividly describes or depicts something, often with clear or explicit detail.
in a vivid or explicit way

Examples

The crime scene was described graphically in the novel, leaving nothing to the imagination.
She explained the medical procedure graphically, which made some listeners uncomfortable.
The movie depicts the accident graphically, so viewer discretion is advised.

Synset Examples

he described the event graphically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vividly - In a way that produces powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind
  • Synonym: explicitly - In a clear and detailed manner, leaving no room for confusion or doubt

Etymology

From 'graphic' + '-ally' (adverbial suffix).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00124922-r
Synset POS: r

3. ADV

/ ˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses diagrams or visual representations rather than words
using diagrams or visual representations

Examples

The instructor explained the process graphically on the whiteboard so everyone could follow.
This concept can be represented graphically to make it easier to understand.
The changes in population size are shown graphically in the accompanying chart.

Synset Examples

the landscape unit drawn diagrammatically illustrates the gentle rolling relief, with a peat-filled basin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagrammatically - in the form of a diagram

Etymology

From 'graphic' + '-ally'; ultimately from Greek 'graphikos' (pertaining to drawing or writing).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00312240-r
Synset POS: r
