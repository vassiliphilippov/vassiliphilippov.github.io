raped

1. ADJ

/ reɪpt / (AmE), / reɪpt / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) violently robbed, damaged, or destroyed, especially by forceful or exploitative action.
violently robbed or destroyed

Examples

Many activists were outraged by the raped forest that once supported diverse wildlife.
The raped village bore scars of the long conflict.
Tourists were saddened by the sight of the raped coastline, stripped bare for development.

Synset Examples

the raped countryside

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastated - severely damaged or destroyed
  • Synonym: ravaged - severely damaged by forceful action

Etymology

Past participle of 'rape,' from Latin *rapere* (to seize, carry off by force).
