verrucose

1. ADJ

/ vəˈruːkoʊs / (AmE), / vəˈruːkəʊs / (BrE)
(of skin or other surfaces) covered with warts or wart-like projections.
covered with warts or wart-like growths
medical

Examples

The patient's hands appeared verrucose, with multiple raised bumps on the surface.
The botanist noted the verrucose texture of the plant's stem under the microscope.
Some amphibians have verrucose skin which helps them blend into their environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wart - a small, hard, benign growth on the skin

Etymology

From Latin verrucosus (full of warts), from verruca (wart).
