summery

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌməri / (AmE), / ˈsʌməri / (BrE)
characteristic of or suitable for summer; having the qualities typical of summer
typical of or suitable for summer

Examples

She wore a summery dress with bright floral patterns.
The café's interior had a summery feel, thanks to its vibrant colors and airy windows.
We enjoyed the summery weather by having a picnic in the park.

Synset Examples

summery weather
summery dresses

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wintry - characteristic of or suitable for winter
  • Synonym: summer - the warmest season of the year

Etymology

From 'summer' + '-y', an adjective-forming suffix.
