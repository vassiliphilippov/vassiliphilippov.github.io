undepicted

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈpɪktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈpɪktɪd / (BrE)
not shown or represented in a picture, image, or other visual depiction
not shown or represented visually

Examples

Much of the landscape remained undepicted in the artist's final painting.
The novel focuses on the undepicted struggles hidden beneath the family's cheerful exterior.
Many voices in history have gone undepicted in the official records.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: depicted - shown or represented visually

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) and depicted (from Latin depictus).
