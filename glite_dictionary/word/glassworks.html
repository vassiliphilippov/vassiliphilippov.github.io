glassworks

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlæsˌwɜrks / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːswɜːks / (BrE)
a factory or workshop where glass is manufactured
factory where glass is made

Examples

The old town is famous for its historic glassworks that once supplied glass across Europe.
A tour of the glassworks showed how artisans shape molten glass into beautiful objects.
The factory converted from an iron foundry to a glassworks in the nineteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glass factory - a factory where glass is produced
  • Antonym: steelworks - a factory where steel is manufactured
