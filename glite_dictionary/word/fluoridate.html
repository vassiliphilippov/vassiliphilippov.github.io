to fluoridate

1. VERB

/ ˈflɔrɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈflʊərɪˌdeɪt / (BrE)
to add fluoride to something, especially water, in order to reduce tooth decay
add fluoride, especially to water

Examples

Many cities have decided to fluoridate their water supply to promote dental health.
The government plans to fluoridate all community drinking water.
Some people oppose efforts to fluoridate public water due to health concerns.

Synset Examples

fluoridized water
fluoridize the teeth of children

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fluoridation - the process of adding fluoride to something

Etymology

From fluoride + -ate (verb-forming suffix).
