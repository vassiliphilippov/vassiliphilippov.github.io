handgrip

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌɡrɪp / (AmE), / ˈhændˌɡrɪp / (BrE)
a part of an object designed to be held by the hand for using or moving it
part of an object to hold

Examples

The bicycle's handgrip was wrapped in leather for extra comfort.
He bought a suitcase with a sturdy handgrip for easier carrying.
The camera's handgrip made it much steadier to hold while filming.

Synset Examples

he grabbed the hammer by the handle
it was an old briefcase but it still had a good grip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - the part of something that is designed to be held or operated by hand
  • Synonym: grip - a firm hold; also a part for holding
