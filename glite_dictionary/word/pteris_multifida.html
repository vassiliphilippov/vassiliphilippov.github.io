Pteris multifida

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrɪs ˌmʌl.tɪˈfaɪ.də / (AmE), / ˈtɛrɪs ˌmʌl.tɪˈfaɪ.də / (BrE)
An Asiatic fern species, commonly grown as an ornamental plant and sometimes naturalized in North America.
species of Asiatic fern

Examples

The garden center had several pots of Pteris multifida on display for houseplant enthusiasts.
Many gardeners appreciate Pteris multifida for its delicate, finely divided fronds.
After being introduced to new environments, Pteris multifida has naturalized in some parts of America.

Etymology

From genus name *Pteris* (Greek *pteris*, fern) and Latin *multifida* (many-divided).
