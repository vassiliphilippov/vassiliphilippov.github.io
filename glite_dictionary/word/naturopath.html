naturopath

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃərəˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈnatjʊrəˌpaθ / (BrE)
a person trained in naturopathy, a system of alternative medicine that emphasizes natural remedies and the body's ability to heal itself
practitioner of naturopathy

Examples

After experiencing side effects from medication, she decided to consult a naturopath about her health.
The clinic has a team that includes a naturopath, a dietitian, and a physical therapist.
Some people visit a naturopath for alternative treatments to complement conventional medicine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturopathy - a system of alternative medicine using natural remedies

Etymology

From Latin 'natura' (nature) + Greek 'pathos' (suffering, disease).
