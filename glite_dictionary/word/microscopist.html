microscopist

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈkrɑː.skə.pɪst / (AmE), / maɪˈkrɒ.skə.pɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the use or study of microscopes
scientist specializing in microscopes

Examples

The microscopist carefully adjusted the lens to observe the details of the cell.
As a professional microscopist, she often published her findings in scientific journals.
Our team consulted a microscopist to help identify the unknown bacteria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microscopist - scientist who studies with microscopes
  • Synonym: biologist - scientist specializing in biology

Etymology

From 'microscope' + '-ist', formed in English.
