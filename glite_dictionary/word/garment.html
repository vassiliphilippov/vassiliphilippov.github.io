garment

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt / (BrE)
An item of clothing, especially one that covers the body.
an article of clothing

Examples

She wore a beautiful silk garment to the evening party.
All garments must be washed before being donated to the charity.
The shop specializes in traditional woolen garments from Scotland.

Synset Examples

garments of the finest silk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clothing - items worn to cover the body
  • Synonym: attire - clothing, especially of a particular kind
  • Antonym: undress - to remove clothing

Etymology

From Old French 'garment', from 'garner' (to equip), from Frankish or Germanic origin.
