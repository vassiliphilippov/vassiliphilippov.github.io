Oireachtas

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɑːktəs / (AmE), / əˈræktəs / (BrE)
the national parliament of the Republic of Ireland, consisting of the President, Dáil Éireann, and Seanad Éireann
parliament of the Republic of Ireland

Examples

Debates in the Oireachtas often shape the direction of Irish government policy.
He was elected to serve as a member of the Oireachtas in 2020.
The legislation passed swiftly through both houses of the Oireachtas.

Etymology

From Irish Gaelic 'Oireachtas' (deliberative assembly or national parliament).
