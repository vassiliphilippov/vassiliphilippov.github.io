meanie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːni / (AmE), / ˈmiːni / (BrE)
an unkind or unpleasant person, especially used by or about children
unkind or unpleasant person (informal)
informal

Examples

Don’t be such a meanie—let your sister have a turn.
The kids called their strict teacher a meanie.
Nobody likes a meanie who never shares their toys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
  • Antonym: kind person - a person who is friendly, generous, or considerate
