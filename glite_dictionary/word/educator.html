educator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛdʒəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɛdjuːkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who teaches, educates, or is involved in the profession of education, especially young people
a person who teaches or educates

Examples

She has spent her entire career as an educator in public schools.
The conference brought together leading educators from around the world.
As an educator, he believes every child deserves equal opportunities to learn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
  • Antonym: student - a person who is being taught

Etymology

From Latin 'educator' (one who rears or educates).
