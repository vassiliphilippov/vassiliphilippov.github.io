uncorrectable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kəˈrɛk.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˈrɛk.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) incapable of being corrected, controlled, or managed.
incapable of being corrected or controlled

Examples

His uncorrectable habit of interrupting others made meetings very difficult.
Some behaviors in young children can seem uncorrectable at first but improve with patience.
The damage to the hard drive proved uncorrectable, so the data could not be recovered.

Synset Examples

uncontrollable children
an uncorrectable habit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncontrollable - impossible to control or manage
  • Synonym: incorrigible - impossible to correct, reform, or improve

Etymology

From un- (not) + correctable (able to be corrected).
