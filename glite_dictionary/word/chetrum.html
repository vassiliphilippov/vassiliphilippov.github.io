chetrum

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪtrəm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛtrəm / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Bhutan, equal to one hundredth of a ngultrum.
monetary unit of Bhutan

Examples

I exchanged my dollars for Bhutanese chetrum at the airport.
A cup of tea in Thimphu might cost just a few chetrums.
The price was listed as 5 ngultrums and 50 chetrum.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ngultrum - the main currency unit of Bhutan
