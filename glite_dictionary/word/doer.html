doer

1. NOUN

/ ˈduːər / (AmE), / ˈduːə / (BrE)
a person who takes action and gets things done, rather than just talking about it
person who acts, gets things done

Examples

If you want the job finished quickly, hire a doer rather than a talker.
She's always been a doer, taking initiative in every project she joins.
His reputation as a doer helped him rise quickly in the company.

Synset Examples

he's a principal actor in this affair
when you want something done, get a doer
he's a miracle worker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person who takes action, especially in a particular situation; not a performer in a play or film

Etymology

From 'do' (verb) + '-er' (agent noun suffix indicating someone who does an action).
