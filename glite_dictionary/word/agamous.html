agamous

1. ADJ

/ ˈæɡəməs / (AmE), / ˈaɡəməs / (BrE)
(of reproduction) not involving the fusion of male and female gametes; asexual.
not involving gamete fusion

Examples

Some plants reproduce in an agamous manner, bypassing the need for fertilization.
The scientist studied the mechanisms of agamous reproduction in certain algae.
Unlike sexual reproduction, agamous processes do not involve gamete fusion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sexual - involving the fusion of male and female gametes in reproduction
  • Synonym: asexual - not involving sexual activity or reproduction

Etymology

From Greek 'a-' (not) + 'gamos' (marriage, union).
