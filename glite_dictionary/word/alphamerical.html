alphamerical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.fəˈmɛr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæl.fəˈmɛr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of both letters and numbers (alphanumeric characters).
relating to letters and numbers

Examples

Passwords should be alphamerical for better security.
The system generates a unique alphamerical code for each transaction.
Please enter your alphamerical username to proceed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphanumeric - containing both letters and numbers

Etymology

Formed from 'alpha' (from Greek 'alphabētos', alphabet) and 'numeric' (from Latin 'numerus', number).
