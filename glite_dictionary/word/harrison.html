Harrison

1. PROPN

/ ˈhærəsən / (AmE), / ˈhærɪsən / (BrE)
(Benjamin Harrison) the 23rd President of the United States, serving from 1889 to 1893.
23rd U.S. President (1889–1893)

Examples

Harrison was elected President of the United States in 1888.
Many historians study the administration of Harrison for its economic policies.
President Harrison was the grandson of former President William Henry Harrison.
