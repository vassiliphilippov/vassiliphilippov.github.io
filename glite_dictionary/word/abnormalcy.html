abnormalcy

1. NOUN

/ æbˈnɔrməlˌsi / (AmE), / æbˈnɔːməlsi / (BrE)
An abnormal physical condition resulting from defective genes or developmental deficiencies.
abnormal physical condition
medical

Genetic testing can often reveal hidden abnormalcies before symptoms appear.
