Frederick Delius

1. PROPN

/ ˈfrɛdərɪk diˈliːəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛdərɪk dɪˈliːəs / (BrE)
(1862–1934), English composer known for his evocative and atmospheric orchestral works.
English composer (1862–1934)

Examples

Many critics regard Frederick Delius as one of the most original English composers of the early twentieth century.
The lush harmonies in Frederick Delius's music evoke the beauty of nature.
A concert featuring works by Frederick Delius is scheduled for next month at the symphony hall.
