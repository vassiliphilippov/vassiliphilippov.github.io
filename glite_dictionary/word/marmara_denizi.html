Marmara Denizi

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːrməˌrɑː dəˈniːzi / (AmE), / ˌmɑːmərə dəˈniːzi / (BrE)
an inland sea in northwestern Turkey, connecting the Black Sea to the Aegean Sea through the Bosporus and the Dardanelles
inland sea in northwestern Turkey

Examples

The Marmara Denizi is a crucial waterway linking the Black Sea to the Aegean Sea.
Many ships pass through the Marmara Denizi on their way to Istanbul.
The city of Istanbul lies on both sides of the Marmara Denizi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sea of Marmara - inland sea in Turkey between the Black Sea and the Aegean Sea

Etymology

From Turkish 'Marmara Denizi' meaning 'Sea of Marmara', with 'Marmara' referring to the region and 'Denizi' meaning 'sea'.
