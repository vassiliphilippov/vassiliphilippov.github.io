mishegoss

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪʃəˈɡɔs / (AmE), / ˌmɪʃəˈɡɒs / (BrE)
foolish or senseless behavior; craziness (especially in a lighthearted or exasperated sense)
senseless or crazy behavior

Examples

I can't deal with all this mishegoss at the office today.
Her family has a lot of mishegoss, but she loves them anyway.
Why do you always bring so much mishegoss into your relationships?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madness - the state of being mentally ill or extremely foolish
  • Synonym: nonsense - something that does not make sense; foolishness

Etymology

From Yiddish *meshugas*, from Hebrew *meshuga‘* (‘crazy’).
