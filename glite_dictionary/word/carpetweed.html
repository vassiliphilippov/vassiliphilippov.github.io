carpetweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrpɪtˌwid / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪtˌwiːd / (BrE)
An annual prostrate weed with whorled leaves and small greenish-white flowers, commonly found throughout North America.
a North American mat-forming weed

Examples

Carpetweed is often found growing in gardens and along roadsides in the summer.
Gardeners may find carpetweed difficult to control because of its rapid spreading.
The small greenish-white flowers of carpetweed are a distinctive feature of this common weed.
