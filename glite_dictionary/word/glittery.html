glittery

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɪt̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪt.əri / (BrE)
shining with many small, bright flashes of light; covered in or resembling glitter
shining with small bright flashes

Examples

She wore a glittery dress that sparkled under the lights.
The children's glittery Christmas cards brightened up the classroom walls.
He bought a glittery phone case that caught everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkly - covered with or resembling sparkles; shining with bright flashes
  • Antonym: dull - lacking brightness, vividness, or sheen; not glittery

Etymology

Formed from "glitter" (to shine with a bright, shimmering reflected light) + -y (adjectival suffix).
