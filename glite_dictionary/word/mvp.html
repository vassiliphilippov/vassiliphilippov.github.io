MVP

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm vi ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌɛm viː ˈpiː / (BrE)
the player in a sports team or competition who is judged to be the most valuable or important to their team's success
most valuable player (sports)
american

Examples

After a stellar season, she was named the team's MVP.
The crowd erupted when the announcer revealed the MVP of the tournament.
Winning the MVP award was the highlight of his basketball career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: most valuable player - player judged most valuable in a sport or competition

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'most valuable player'.
