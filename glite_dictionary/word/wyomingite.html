Wyomingite

1. NOUN

/ waɪˈoʊ.mɪnˌaɪt / (AmE), / waɪˈəʊ.mɪnˌaɪt / (BrE)
a native or resident of the U.S. state of Wyoming
person from Wyoming

Examples

As a proud Wyomingite, she loves the state's open spaces and natural beauty.
Several Wyomingites attended the annual festival in Cheyenne.
After moving to Laramie, he quickly felt welcomed by the friendly Wyomingites.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Wyoming - a state in the western United States

Etymology

Formed from 'Wyoming' + '-ite', a common English suffix for demonyms.
