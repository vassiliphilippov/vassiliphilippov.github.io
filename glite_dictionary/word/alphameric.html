alphameric

1. ADJ

/ ˌæl.fəˈmɛr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌal.fəˈmɛr.ɪk / (BrE)
Consisting of or relating to both letters and numbers; alphanumeric.
letters and numbers together

Examples

Please enter an alphameric code for your new password.
The form only accepts alphameric characters, so symbols are not allowed.
Many license plates use a mix of alphameric characters to increase the number of combinations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alphanumeric - Consisting of both letters and numbers

Etymology

Blend of 'alphabet' and 'numeric', modeled on 'alphanumeric'.
