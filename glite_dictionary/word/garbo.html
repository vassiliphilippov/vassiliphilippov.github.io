Garbo

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡɑːrboʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbəʊ / (BrE)
(1905–1990) A famous Swedish-born American actress known for her reclusiveness and performances in classic films of the early 20th century.
famous Swedish-American film actress (1905–1990)

Examples

Many film historians consider Garbo to be one of the greatest actresses of all time.
There was an air of mystery about Garbo that drew audiences to her movies.
Despite her fame, Garbo retired from acting at the age of 36 and lived a quiet life.

Etymology

Stage name adopted by Greta Lovisa Gustafsson, originally from Sweden.
