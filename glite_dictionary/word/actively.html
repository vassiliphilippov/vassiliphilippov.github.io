actively

1. ADV

/ ˈæktɪvli / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that involves taking action or participating energetically rather than being passive.
in an active or energetic way

Examples

He has been actively involved in several community service projects.
The company is actively seeking new investment opportunities this year.
Students are encouraged to actively participate in classroom discussions.

Synset Examples

he participated actively in the war

Related Terms

  • Antonym: passively - in a passive or inactive manner

Etymology

From 'active' + '-ly', with 'active' from Latin 'activus' (doing, active).
