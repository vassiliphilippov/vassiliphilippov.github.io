to systemize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪstəməˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəməˌaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or organize according to a system; to make systematic
arrange systematically

Examples

The new manager plans to systemize the company's workflow to improve efficiency.
Researchers are working to systemize the vast amount of data collected from the experiment.
In order to solve the issue, we need to systemize our approach and set clear protocols.

Synset Examples

systematize our scientific knowledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to systematize - to arrange according to a system or plan

Etymology

Formed from 'system' + '-ize', modeled after Latinized forms like 'systematize'.
