shmo

1. NOUN

/ ʃmoʊ / (AmE), / ʃməʊ / (BrE)
An incompetent or foolish person; a jerk (informal).
a foolish or contemptible person
informal slang

Examples

Don't be such a shmo; listen to what she's telling you.
He always acts like a shmo at parties, embarrassing everyone.
Nobody wants to work with a shmo who never pulls his weight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: schmo - a naive or stupid person (variant spelling)

Etymology

From Yiddish 'shmok' (penis, fool), likely softened in English slang.
