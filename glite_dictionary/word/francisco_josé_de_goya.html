Francisco José de Goya

1. PROPN

/ frænˌsɪskoʊ hoʊˌseɪ də ˈɡɔɪə / (AmE), / frænˌsɪskəʊ həʊˌseɪ də ˈɡɔɪə / (BrE)
(1746–1828) A Spanish painter renowned for his portraits and satirical works, considered one of the most important artists of the late 18th and early 19th centuries.
Spanish painter and satirist (1746–1828)

Examples

The works of Francisco José de Goya continue to influence modern art.
Many critics consider Francisco José de Goya a master of both portraiture and social commentary.
At the museum, we viewed several paintings by Francisco José de Goya.

Etymology

Spanish origin; full name Francisco José de Goya y Lucientes.
