woodcarving

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌkɑːrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌkɑːvɪŋ / (BrE)
a work of art or ornament that has been created by carving wood
a carved object made from wood

Examples

The museum displayed a beautiful woodcarving of a forest scene.
He collects antique woodcarvings from different cultures around the world.
That woodcarving above the fireplace was made by my grandfather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carving - a work of art created by cutting material such as wood or stone
