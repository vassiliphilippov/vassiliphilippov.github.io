Floreal

1. NOUN

/ floʊˈʁeɪl / (AmE), / flɔːˈreɪəl / (BrE)
the eighth month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding approximately to April and May and known as the month of flowers
eighth month of the French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

The treaty was signed on the 17th of Floreal, during the height of spring.
Many citizens celebrated the arrival of Floreal with festivals dedicated to flowers and nature.
The calendar reform introduced Floreal as a way to honor the beauty of spring.

Etymology

From French 'Floréal', from Latin 'flōs, flōris' (flower), named for the abundance of flowers during this time.
