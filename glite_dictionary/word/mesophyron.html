mesophyron

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛsəˈfaɪˌrɑn / (AmE), / ˌmɛsəˈfaɪrən / (BrE)
a smooth prominence of the frontal bone situated between and above the eyebrows, forming the most forward projecting point of the forehead in the midline at the level of the supraorbital ridges
forehead prominence above and between eyebrows
medical

Examples

The surgeon made an incision just above the mesophyron to access the frontal sinus.
In profile, her mesophyron was especially prominent, enhancing her facial features.
Anthropologists measured the mesophyron to study variations in skull shape among populations.

Etymology

From Greek mesos (middle) + phuron (brow, forehead).
