Kuenlun

1. PROPN

/ kuˈɛn.lʊn / (AmE), / kuːˈɛn.lʊn / (BrE)
a mountain range in western China extending eastward from the Indian border for about 1,600 kilometers (1,000 miles)
mountain range in western China

Examples

The Kuenlun mountains form a natural barrier along the northern edge of the Tibetan Plateau.
Few explorers have crossed the remote passes of the Kuenlun range.
Snow covers the peaks of the Kuenlun for much of the year.

Etymology

From Chinese Kunlun (昆仑), referring to the mythical or real mountain range.
