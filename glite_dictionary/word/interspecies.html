interspecies

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈspiːʃiːz / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
Arising, occurring, or existing between different species.
between different species

Examples

Scientists studied the effects of interspecies communication between dolphins and whales.
Many zoonotic diseases result from interspecies transmission of viruses.
The interspecies competition in the forest affects ecosystem balance.

Synset Examples

an interspecific hybrid

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intraspecies - occurring within a single species

Etymology

From Latin 'inter-' (between) and English 'species' (group of related organisms).
