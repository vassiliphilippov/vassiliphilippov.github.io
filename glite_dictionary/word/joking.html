joking

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒoʊ.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
intended in a humorous or playful way; characterized by jokes and good humor
humorous or playful; using jokes

Examples

She made a joking comment that lightened the mood in the meeting.
Although his words sounded harsh, his joking tone made everyone laugh.
He always adopts a joking manner when discussing serious topics.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: serious - earnest, not joking or playful
  • Synonym: playful - fond of games and amusement; lighthearted
