Nelson Algren

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɛlsən ˈælɡrən / (AmE), / ˈnɛlsən ˈælɡrən / (BrE)
(1909–1981) An American writer known for his novels and short stories portraying the lives of the poor and marginalized in Chicago.
American writer (1909–1981)

Examples

Nelson Algren won the first National Book Award for his novel 'The Man with the Golden Arm.'
Many of Nelson Algren's works depict life in the underprivileged neighborhoods of Chicago.
The influence of Nelson Algren on American literature remains significant even decades after his death.
