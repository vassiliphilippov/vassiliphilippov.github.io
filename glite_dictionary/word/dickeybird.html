dickeybird

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪkiˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈdɪkiˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a small bird, especially as referred to when talking to children
small bird (child-directed term)
british

Examples

The children watched a little dickeybird chirping on the garden fence.
She pointed out a dickeybird in the tree to her grandson.
“Look at that dickeybird!” he said, teaching his daughter about animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bird - a feathered, winged, egg-laying vertebrate
