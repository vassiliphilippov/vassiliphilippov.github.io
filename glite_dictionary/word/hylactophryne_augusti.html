Hylactophryne augusti

1. PROPN

/ haɪˌlæktəˈfraɪni aʊˈgʌsti / (AmE), / hɪˌlæktəˈfriːni ɔːˈɡʌsti / (BrE)
A species of frog found in the southwest United States and Mexico, known for its call that resembles a dog's bark.
species of frog with a bark-like call

Examples

The distinctive call of the Hylactophryne augusti can often be heard after heavy rain in the desert.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the Hylactophryne augusti in southern New Mexico.
The Hylactophryne augusti is sometimes called the barking frog due to its unusual vocalizations.
