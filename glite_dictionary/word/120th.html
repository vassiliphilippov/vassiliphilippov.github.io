120th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti ɛθ, wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛntiəθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti ɪθ, wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛntiəθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number equivalent to one hundred twenty; coming after the 119th in a sequence
ordinal number for 120th place

Examples
