taster

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪstər / (AmE), / ˈteɪstə / (BrE)
a person who samples food or drink to judge its quality or flavor
person who tests food or drink

Examples

The professional taster evaluated dozens of cheeses at the competition.
As a wine taster, she traveled the world attending exclusive tastings.
The company hired a new taster to maintain the quality of their products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tester - someone who tests something to assess its quality

Etymology

From Middle English 'tasten' (to taste) + '-er'.
