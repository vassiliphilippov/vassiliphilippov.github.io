Wallis Warfield Simpson

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɑːlɪs ˈwɔːrfiːld ˈsɪmp.sən / (AmE), / ˈwɒlɪs ˈwɔːfiːld ˈsɪmp.sən / (BrE)
(1896–1986) American socialite whose marriage to Edward VIII led to his abdication from the British throne.
American socialite, wife of Edward VIII

Examples

Wallis Warfield Simpson became infamous for her relationship with King Edward VIII of England.
Many historians credit Wallis Warfield Simpson with changing the course of the British monarchy.
The abdication crisis of 1936 was largely due to Edward VIII's desire to marry Wallis Warfield Simpson.
