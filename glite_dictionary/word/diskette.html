diskette

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈskɛt / (AmE), / dɪˈskɛt / (BrE)
a small flexible magnetic disk used for storing computer data, encased in a protective plastic cover
flexible computer data storage disk

Examples

She found an old diskette in the back of her desk drawer.
The software was originally distributed on a single 3.5-inch diskette.
Before USB drives, people commonly used a diskette to transfer files between computers.

Synset Examples

floppy disks are noted for their relatively slow speed and small capacity and low price

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floppy disk - another term for a flexible magnetic disk used for data storage

Etymology

Diminutive of 'disk', modeled after French 'disquette'.
