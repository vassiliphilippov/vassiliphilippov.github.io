shrimpfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a slender tropical fish of the family Centriscidae found in shallow waters of the East Indies, covered with transparent, plate-like scales
slender East Indian tropical fish with transparent plates

Examples

The shrimpfish swims upright, mimicking the sway of seagrass in its shallow habitat.
Aquarium enthusiasts find the unique appearance of the shrimpfish particularly fascinating.
Researchers observed a school of shrimpfish gliding gracefully above the sandy seabed.
