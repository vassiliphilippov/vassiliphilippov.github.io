Malaclemys

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæləˈklɛmiz / (AmE), / ˌmæləkˈliːmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of American terrapins, notably including the diamondback terrapin.
genus of American terrapins

Examples

The diamondback terrapin is the only member of the genus Malaclemys.
Researchers studied the nesting behavior of Malaclemys in coastal marshes.
Conservation efforts have focused on protecting Malaclemys populations from habitat loss.

Etymology

From Greek 'malakos' (soft) and 'klemmys' (tortoise).
