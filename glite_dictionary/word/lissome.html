lissome

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪsəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪsəm / (BrE)
Graceful and thin, moving in a flexible and effortless way.
gracefully thin and flexible

Examples

The dancer's lissome movements captivated the entire audience.
She had a lissome figure, gliding across the stage with remarkable grace.
Despite his lissome build, he was surprisingly strong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lithe - gracefully thin and flexible
  • Synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily
  • Antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or posture

Etymology

Alteration of 'lithesome', from 'lithe' meaning flexible.
