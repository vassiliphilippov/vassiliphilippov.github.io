humanoid

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmənˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənɔɪd / (BrE)
an automaton or entity that has the appearance or characteristics of a human being
an entity resembling a human being

Examples

The scientists unveiled a new humanoid robot capable of mimicking human gestures.
She wrote a novel about a distant future in which humanoids coexist with people.
The movie depicted a humanoid automaton with artificial intelligence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance, often specifically male
  • Synonym: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex tasks automatically
  • Degree Lower: machine - an apparatus using mechanical power

Etymology

From Latin 'humanus' (human) + '-oid' (from Greek '-oeidēs', meaning 'resembling' or 'like').
