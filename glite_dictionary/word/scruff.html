scruff

1. NOUN

/ skrʌf / (AmE), / skrʌf / (BrE)
the back part of the neck, especially the loose skin at the back of the neck of an animal
back part of the neck

Examples

The mother cat carried her kitten by the scruff of its neck.
He grabbed the boy by the scruff and pulled him away from the fight.
She felt a hand on the scruff of her neck, guiding her gently forward.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nape - the back of a person's neck
