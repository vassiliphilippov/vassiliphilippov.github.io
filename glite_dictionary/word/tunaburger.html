tunaburger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuː.nəˌbɝː.ɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtjuː.nəˌbɜː.ɡə / (BrE)
A sandwich similar to a hamburger, made with a cooked tuna patty instead of beef, typically served in a bun with various toppings.
hamburger-style sandwich with tuna patty

Examples

At the café, I ordered a tunaburger with lettuce and tomato for lunch.
She prefers a tunaburger to a hamburger because it's lighter and has less red meat.
They serve an excellent tunaburger topped with spicy mayo and fresh greens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hamburger - a sandwich consisting of a cooked beef patty in a bun
  • Synonym: tuna melt - a sandwich with tuna and melted cheese, typically grilled

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'hamburger', using 'tuna' as the main ingredient.
