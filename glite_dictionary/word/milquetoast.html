milquetoast

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌtoʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌtəʊst / (BrE)
a person who is timid, meek, or unassertive, especially a man or boy
timid or unassertive person
informal

Examples

Everyone always overlooked the milquetoast in meetings because he spoke so softly.
Don't mistake her gentle manner for that of a milquetoast; she is quite determined when needed.
He played the role of a classic milquetoast in the old comedy series.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coward - someone who lacks courage
  • Antonym: bold - showing courage or confidence

Etymology

From the comic strip character Caspar Milquetoast, created by H.T. Webster in the 1920s; his surname is a deliberate misspelling of 'milk toast'.
