sensually

1. ADV

/ ˈsɛnʃuəli / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or expresses sensual pleasure, especially sexual attraction.
in a sensual or sexually alluring way

Examples

The lead actress danced sensually during the performance, captivating the entire audience.
He whispered sensually in her ear, adding to the romantic atmosphere of their evening.
The perfume is advertised as a fragrance that should be applied sensually to the skin.

Synset Examples

the belly dancer moved sensually among the tables

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexually - in a sexual manner
  • Antonym: chastely - in a pure or modest manner

Etymology

From 'sensual' + '-ly', with 'sensual' from Latin sensualis (relating to the senses).
