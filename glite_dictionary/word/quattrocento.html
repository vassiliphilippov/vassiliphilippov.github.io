quattrocento

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɑː.troʊˈtʃɛn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˌkwæ.trəʊˈtʃɛn.təʊ / (BrE)
The 15th century, especially as a period of remarkable achievements in Italian art and literature.
15th century Italian art and literature

Examples

Leonardo da Vinci was a leading figure of the quattrocento in Florence.
The quattrocento marked a turning point in the development of Renaissance painting.
Many art historians specialize in the study of quattrocento sculpture and architecture.

Etymology

From Italian 'quattrocento' (four hundred), meaning the years 1400–1499.
