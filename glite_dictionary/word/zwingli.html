Zwingli

1. PROPN

/ ˈtswɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈzwɪŋli / (BrE)
(1484–1531) A Swiss theologian and reformer whose sermons initiated the Reformation in Switzerland.
Swiss Reformation theologian (1484–1531)

Examples

Zwingli was a leading figure in the Swiss Reformation.
Many historians compare Martin Luther and Zwingli for their differing reformative approaches.
The teachings of Zwingli had a lasting impact on Protestant theology in Switzerland.

Etymology

From the German surname Zwingli, notably borne by Ulrich Zwingli.
