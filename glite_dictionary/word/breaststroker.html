breaststroker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɛstˌstroʊkər / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌstrəʊkə / (BrE)
a person who swims using the breaststroke style
person who swims the breaststroke

Examples

The youngest breaststroker in the competition set a new personal best time.
As a breaststroker, she excels at short and middle-distance events.
The team needed a strong breaststroker for the medley relay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimmer - person who swims, especially as a sport
