trumpet-like

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌmpɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a quality or sound that resembles that of a trumpet.
resembling a trumpet in sound or quality

Examples

The bird let out a trumpet-like call that echoed through the forest.
Her laughter was trumpet-like, loud and joyful, making everyone in the room smile.
The elephant's trumpet-like trumpet startled the nearby antelope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brassy - having a loud, resonant sound like a brass instrument
  • Degree Higher: trumpet - a brass wind instrument with a bright, clear tone
