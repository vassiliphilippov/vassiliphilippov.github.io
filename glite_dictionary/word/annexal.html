annexal

1. ADJ

/ əˈnɛksəl / (AmE), / əˈnɛksəl / (BrE)
relating to the adnexa, especially the appendages of an organ, particularly in medical contexts
relating to the adnexa (medical)
medical

Examples

The ultrasound revealed an annexal mass that required further investigation.
The doctor noted signs of inflammation in the annexal region during the examination.
Ovarian cysts are one example of annexal pathology in gynecology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adnexal - relating to the adnexa (especially in medicine)

Etymology

From Latin 'annexus', meaning 'tied to' or 'connected', after 'adnexa'.
