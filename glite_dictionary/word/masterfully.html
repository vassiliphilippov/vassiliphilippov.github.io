masterfully

1. ADV

/ ˈmæstɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great skill or expertise.
with great skill or expertise

Examples

She handled the negotiations masterfully, impressing both parties with her expertise.
The artist masterfully blended the colors to create a striking effect.
He masterfully orchestrated the project, ensuring everything ran smoothly.

Synset Examples

the young boy played the violin sonata masterfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a skillful manner; with skill

Etymology

From 'masterful' + '-ly', forming adverbs from adjectives.
