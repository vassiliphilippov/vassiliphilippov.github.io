attacking

1. ADJ

/ əˈtækɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈtakɪŋ / (BrE)
ready or inclined to launch an attack; aggressive or offensive in nature or behavior.
disposed to attack; aggressive

Examples

The team adopted an attacking formation to pressure their opponents early in the match.
Her attacking style of debate made her a formidable opponent.
Lions display attacking behavior when defending their territory.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: defensive - protecting against attack; opposite of attacking
