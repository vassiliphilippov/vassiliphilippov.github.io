croupy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkruːpi / (AmE), / ˈkruːpi / (BrE)
having symptoms resembling those of croup, especially a hoarse, barking cough
resembling croup (illness)
medical

Examples

The child's croupy cough kept the whole family awake last night.
She called the doctor when she noticed her son's croupy breathing getting worse.
The nurse recognized the croupy sound immediately and recommended treatment.

Etymology

From 'croup' (referring to the disease croup) + '-y'.
