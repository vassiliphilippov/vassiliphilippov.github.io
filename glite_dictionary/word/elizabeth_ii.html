Elizabeth II

1. PROPN

/ ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪzəbəθ ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
(1926–2022) Queen of the United Kingdom and Northern Ireland from 1952 until her death, daughter of George VI.
Queen of the United Kingdom (1952–2022)
