peachwood

1. NOUN

/ ˈpitʃˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtʃˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tropical tree with a prickly trunk, whose heavy red wood is used for cabinetry and produces a red dye
tropical tree with red wood used in cabinetry

Examples

Furniture made from peachwood is prized for its durability and rich color.
Artisans value peachwood for creating intricate cabinets and decorative pieces.
A red dye extracted from peachwood is used in traditional textile production.
