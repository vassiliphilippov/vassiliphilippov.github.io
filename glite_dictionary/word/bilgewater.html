bilgewater

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪldʒˌwɔtɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɪldʒˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
Pretentious, silly, or nonsensical talk or writing; rubbish.
foolish or pretentious talk; nonsense

Examples

His entire argument was just bilgewater from start to finish.
Don't believe a word of that bilgewater she's been spreading around the office.
The politician dismissed the rumors as complete bilgewater.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that have no meaning or make no sense
  • Synonym: rubbish - foolish or untrue words or ideas
  • Antonym: sense - speech or writing that is logical and reasonable

Etymology

From 'bilge water', the dirty water that collects in a ship's bilge; used figuratively for worthless or nonsensical talk.
