FHA

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛf.eɪtʃˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛf.eɪtʃˈeɪ / (BrE)
an agency of the US Department of Housing and Urban Development that insures residential mortgages to encourage home ownership
US federal mortgage insurance agency
american

Examples

The FHA offers mortgage insurance to help first-time homebuyers secure loans.
Many lenders require FHA approval before offering certain types of mortgages.
After losing her job, she refinanced her house with the help of the FHA.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Federal Housing Administration - the full name of the FHA, a US government agency that insures mortgages

Etymology

Acronym of Federal Housing Administration.
