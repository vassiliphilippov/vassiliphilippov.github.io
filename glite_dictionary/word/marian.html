Marian

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛriən / (AmE), / ˈmɛəriən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Virgin Mary, or showing veneration for her, especially in religious contexts.
relating to the Virgin Mary

Examples

The cathedral is famous for its Marian shrine devoted to the Virgin Mary.
He attended several Marian processions during the festival.
The Marian doctrine plays a prominent role in Catholic theology.

Etymology

From Latin Maria (Mary), referring to the Virgin Mary.
