unwaveringly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈweɪvɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪvərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows firm, resolute determination and does not weaken or change
with resolute determination

Examples

She unwaveringly supported her team through every challenge they faced.
Despite criticism, he unwaveringly pursued his goals until he achieved them.
They unwaveringly defended their beliefs, even when it was unpopular to do so.

Synset Examples

we firmly believed it
you must stand firm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmly - in a strong, steady, or determined way
  • Synonym: steadfastly - in a firm and unwavering manner
  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or uncertain manner

Etymology

From 'unwavering' (not wavering) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
