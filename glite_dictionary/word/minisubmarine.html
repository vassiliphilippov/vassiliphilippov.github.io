minisubmarine

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪni səˈmɑːriːn / (AmE), / ˌmɪni səˈmɑːriːn / (BrE)
a very small submarine, usually designed to carry one or two people for naval operations or underwater exploration
very small submarine for one or two people

Examples

The research team used a minisubmarine to explore the coral reef's deeper areas.
A minisubmarine can maneuver into tight spaces that larger subs cannot reach.
During the operation, two divers descended in a minisubmarine to survey the wreck.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submarine - a vessel that can operate underwater

Etymology

Formed from 'mini-' (small) and 'submarine'.
