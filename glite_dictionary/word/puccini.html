Puccini

1. PROPN

/ puˈtʃini / (AmE), / pʊˈtʃiːni / (BrE)
Giacomo Puccini (1858–1924), Italian composer renowned for his operas noted for their dramatic realism.
Italian operatic composer (1858–1924)

Examples

Many consider Puccini to be one of the greatest opera composers of all time.
We attended a performance of Puccini's 'La Bohème' last weekend.
Puccini's music is known for its emotional expression and melodic richness.

Etymology

From the Italian surname Puccini; Giacomo Puccini is the most famous bearer.
