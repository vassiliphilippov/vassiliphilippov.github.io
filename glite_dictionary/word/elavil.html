Elavil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛl.əˌvɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛl.əˌvɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a tricyclic antidepressant drug, used to treat depression and certain other mood disorders.
trade name for an antidepressant drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Elavil to help manage her symptoms of depression.
After taking Elavil for several weeks, he began to notice some side effects.
Patients should be aware that Elavil can interact with many other medications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amitriptyline - the generic name for Elavil, a tricyclic antidepressant medication
