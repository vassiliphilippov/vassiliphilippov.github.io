Isaac Asimov

1. PROPN

/ ˈaɪzək ˈæzɪˌmɑv / (AmE), / ˈaɪzək ˈazɪˌmɒv / (BrE)
(1920–1992) United States writer, born in Russia, renowned for his science fiction and popular science works.
American science fiction writer

Examples

Many readers regard Isaac Asimov as one of the greatest science fiction writers of all time.
The works of Isaac Asimov, such as the Foundation series, have inspired generations of authors.
She found a rare first edition of a novel by Isaac Asimov at the bookstore.
