Lanchow

1. PROPN

/ ˈlænˌtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlan.tʃəʊ / (BrE)
a former romanized name for Lanzhou, the capital city of the Chinese province of Gansu, located on the Yellow River
former romanized name for Lanzhou, China

Examples

In the early twentieth century, Lanchow was a key trade hub along the Silk Road.
Many historical texts refer to the city now called Lanzhou as Lanchow.
The traveler was fascinated by the bustling markets of Lanchow in old photographs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lanzhou - capital city of Gansu province, China

Etymology

Older English spelling (Wade-Giles) for modern pinyin Lanzhou; from Mandarin 兰州 (Lánzhōu).
