downlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdaʊnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdaʊn.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling down or being as soft as down; having the softness or appearance of down feathers.
resembling or as soft as down

Examples

The baby's blanket had a downlike softness that soothed her to sleep.
She wore a scarf made of downlike material, light and warm around her neck.
The petals felt almost downlike to the touch, delicate and soft.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downy - covered with or resembling soft fine feathers or hair
