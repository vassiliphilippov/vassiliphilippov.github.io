doorwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to guard or supervise the entrance to a building.
female entrance guard or attendant

Examples

The doorwoman checked everyone’s ID before letting them into the club.
After many years as a doorwoman, she was promoted to head of security.
Please speak to the doorwoman if you have any questions about entry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doorman - a man who guards or supervises an entrance
