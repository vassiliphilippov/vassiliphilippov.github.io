ormer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːrmər / (AmE), / ˈɔːmə / (BrE)
a type of edible abalone found near the Channel Islands, valued as a delicacy
Channel Islands abalone

Examples

Harvesting ormer is restricted in the Channel Islands to protect the population.
The restaurant served a delicious ormer dish as its specialty.
Local fishermen know the best places to find ormer along the rocky shores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abalone - a large edible sea mollusk with a flat shell

Etymology

From Norman French *orme* (abalone).
