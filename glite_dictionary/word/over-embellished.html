over-embellished

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvər ɪmˈbɛlɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ɪmˈbɛlɪʃt / (BrE)
Excessively elaborate or decorated, often to the point of being showy or unnatural.
excessively elaborate or ornate

Examples

The story was so over-embellished that it was hard to believe a word of it.
Critics felt the novel's language was over-embellished and distracted from the plot.
His over-embellished presentation failed to impress the straightforward panel of judges.

Synset Examples

a writer of empurpled literature
many purple passages
an over-embellished story of the fish that got away

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornate - Elaborately or highly decorated
  • Antonym: simple - Not elaborate or complicated; plain
