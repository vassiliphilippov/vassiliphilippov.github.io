halfhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌhæfˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Without much enthusiasm, energy, or interest.
lacking enthusiasm or interest

Examples

His halfhearted apology did little to comfort her.
They made a halfhearted attempt to clean the garage before giving up.
She clapped in a halfhearted way, clearly not impressed by the performance.

Synset Examples

a halfhearted effort
gave only lukewarm support to the candidate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lukewarm - showing little enthusiasm or conviction
  • Antonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest

Etymology

From 'half' + 'hearted', meaning with only partial heart or enthusiasm.
