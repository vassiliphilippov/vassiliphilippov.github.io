salubrity

1. NOUN

/ səˈluː.brə.ti / (AmE), / səˈljuː.brɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being healthful or promoting well-being.
healthful quality

Examples

The salubrity of the mountain air greatly improved her health.
Many people move to the countryside seeking the salubrity of a natural environment.
Urban planners considered the salubrity of the area before approving the housing project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salubrious - health-giving; healthy

Etymology

From Latin 'salubritas', from 'saluber' (healthful).
