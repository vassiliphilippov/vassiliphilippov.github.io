approving

1. ADJ

/ əˈpruvɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpruːvɪŋ / (BrE)
showing that you approve of someone or something; expressing praise or acceptance.
expressing approval or praise

Examples

She gave him an approving smile as he finished his speech.
The teacher made an approving comment on my essay.
He received an approving nod from the manager after presenting his idea.

Synset Examples

approbative criticism
an affirmative nod

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disapproving - showing that you do not approve of something; expressing disapproval
  • Synonym: approbative - expressing praise or approval

Etymology

From the verb 'approve' + -ing, originally forming the present participle, later used adjectivally.
