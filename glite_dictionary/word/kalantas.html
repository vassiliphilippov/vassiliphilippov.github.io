kalantas

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlɑːntəs / (AmE), / kəˈlɑːntəs / (BrE)
A Philippine timber tree (Toona calantas) known for its hard, red, fragrant wood.
Philippine timber tree with fragrant wood

Examples

The furniture was crafted from kalantas, prized for its durability and beauty.
Many traditional houses in the Philippines are built using kalantas wood.
The distinct scent of kalantas filled the carpenter's workshop.

Etymology

From Filipino 'kalantas', the local name for Toona calantas.
