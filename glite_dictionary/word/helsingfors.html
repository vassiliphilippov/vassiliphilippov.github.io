Helsingfors

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛlsɪŋˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlsɪŋˌfɔːs / (BrE)
the Swedish name for Helsinki, the capital and largest city of Finland
Swedish name for Helsinki, Finland

Examples

The Swedish-speaking population of Finland often refers to Helsinki as Helsingfors.
During my trip to Finland, I visited several museums in Helsingfors.
Official documents in Finland are sometimes written in both Finnish and Swedish, using Helsingfors as the city's name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Helsinki - the capital city of Finland

Etymology

Swedish, from 'helsing' (person from Hälsingland) + 'fors' (rapids).
