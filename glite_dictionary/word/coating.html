coating

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
a heavy fabric, typically woolen, used for making coats or outer garments
heavy fabric for making coats

Examples

The tailor recommended a thick coating for the winter overcoat.
Factories produce a variety of wool coating fabrics each season.
Pure camel hair is considered a luxury coating in high-end fashion.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a thin layer of a substance that covers the surface of something
thin covering layer

Examples

The chocolate biscuit is topped with a thin coating of caramel.
The metal pipe had a protective coating to prevent rust.
Apply another coating of paint for a smooth finish.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layer - a sheet or thickness of material, typically one of several, covering a surface or body
  • Synonym: covering - something that covers, wraps, or surrounds something else

Etymology

3. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection, decoration, or a particular finish
a layer applied to a surface

Examples

He put a thin coating of varnish on the wooden table to protect it.
The chocolate cake is topped with a thick coating of glossy icing.
A waterproof coating was applied to the roof to prevent leaks.

Synset Examples

the boat had a metallic finish
he applied a coat of a clear finish
when the finish is too thin it is difficult to apply evenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - the final surface of something, often shiny or smooth
  • Synonym: covering - something that covers a surface

Etymology

From Latin *cooperīre* (to cover), via Old French *coter* (to cover).

4. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying a layer of a substance to a surface
the act of applying a layer to a surface

Examples

After preparing the surface, she applied a thick coating of varnish to protect the wood.
A coating of dust had settled on the old books after years of neglect.
The cake was finished with a smooth coating of chocolate icing.

Synset Examples

the doctor prescribed a topical application of iodine
a complete bleach requires several applications
the surface was ready for a coating of paint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: application - the act of putting something on a surface

Etymology

From 'coat' + '-ing', forming a noun meaning the act of covering or the substance applied.
