millenniumism

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈlɛniəmˌɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛniəmɪzm / (BrE)
Belief in the Christian doctrine of the millennium, a prophesied thousand-year reign of Christ described in the Book of Revelation.
belief in the Christian millennium

Examples

Some religious groups base their teachings on millenniumism and the expectation of Christ's thousand-year reign.
The rise of millenniumism brought about new interpretations of biblical prophecy.
Debates about millenniumism often focus on how literally to understand the Book of Revelation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millenarianism - belief in a coming major transformation of society, typically involving a thousand-year reign

Etymology

From Latin millennium (thousand years) + -ism (belief or practice).
