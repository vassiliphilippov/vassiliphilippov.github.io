unessential

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈsɛnʃəl / (BrE)
Not absolutely necessary; not essential or fundamental.
not necessary or fundamental

Examples

The committee decided to cut all unessential expenses from the budget.
She removed any unessential details from her report to keep it concise.
Travel was limited to essential personnel, with unessential staff working from home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inessential - not absolutely necessary
  • Antonym: essential - absolutely necessary; extremely important

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'essential'.
