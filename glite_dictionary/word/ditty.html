ditty

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪti / (AmE), / ˈdɪti / (BrE)
a short, simple song or a poem intended to be sung
short, simple song or poem

Examples

She sang a cheerful ditty as she walked through the garden.
The children made up a silly ditty about their school trip.
He composed a catchy little ditty for the advertisement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: song - a musical composition intended to be sung

Etymology

From Middle English *dittee*, from Old French *ditee* (composition, song), from Latin *dictatum* (something composed or dictated).
