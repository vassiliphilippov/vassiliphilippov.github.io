Brownie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraʊni / (AmE), / ˈbraʊni / (BrE)
A member of the junior division of the Girl Scouts organization, typically for younger girls aged around 7 to 10.
junior member of Girl Scouts

Examples

My daughter has just become a Brownie and is excited for her first meeting.
The Brownies went on a camping trip over the weekend.
As a Brownie, Emily sold cookies to raise money for her troop.
