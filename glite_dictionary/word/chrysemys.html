Chrysemys

1. NOUN

/ kraɪˈsiːmɪs / (AmE), / kraɪˈsiːmɪs / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater turtles known as painted turtles, native to North America, characterized by their bright markings.
genus of painted turtles

Examples

The genus Chrysemys includes the familiar North American painted turtles.
Researchers collected data on the habitat preferences of Chrysemys in several lakes.
Chrysemys is easily recognized by its colorful patterns and streamlined shell.

Etymology

From Greek 'chrysos' (gold) and 'emys' (turtle).
