chiggerflower

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪɡərˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪɡəˌflaʊə / (BrE)
An erect perennial plant native to eastern and southern United States, having showy orange flowers.
US perennial plant with orange flowers

Examples

The bright orange chiggerflower stood out in the wildflower garden.
Many gardeners in the South value the chiggerflower for its hardiness and color.
We saw several chiggerflowers blooming along the woodland trail in early spring.
