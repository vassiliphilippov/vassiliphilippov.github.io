objectiveness

1. NOUN

/ əbˈdʒɛktɪvnəs / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of judging or dealing with things based on observable facts, without influence from emotions or personal biases
quality of being objective

Examples

Her objectiveness made her an excellent judge in the debate competition.
The review was praised for its thoroughness and objectiveness.
It is difficult to maintain objectiveness when discussing sensitive topics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: objectivity - the state or quality of being objective
  • Antonym: subjectiveness - the quality of being influenced by personal feelings

Etymology

From 'objective' + '-ness', formed on the pattern of English abstract nouns.
