otorhinolaryngology

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊtoʊˌraɪnoʊˌlærɪnˈɡɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəʊˌraɪnəʊˌlarɪŋˈɡɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
medical specialty for ear, nose, and throat
medical

Examples

She decided to specialize in otorhinolaryngology after discovering her interest in ear and throat disorders.
Advances in otorhinolaryngology have greatly improved the treatment of sinus and hearing problems.
The conference brought together experts in otorhinolaryngology from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ENT - ear, nose, and throat (as a medical specialty)
  • Synonym: otorhinolaryngologist - a physician specializing in otorhinolaryngology

Etymology

From Greek 'oto-' (ear), 'rhino-' (nose), 'laryngo-' (larynx), and '-logy' (study).
