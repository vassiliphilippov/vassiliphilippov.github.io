coffeecake

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfiˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A sweet cake or bread, often with a crumbly topping or filling, typically served with coffee.
sweet cake or bread served with coffee

Examples

She brought a homemade coffeecake to the office for everyone to share.
Our favorite bakery makes a delicious cinnamon coffeecake every morning.
Would you like a slice of coffeecake with your coffee?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry - baked food made of dough and shortening
  • Synonym: cake - a sweet baked dessert, typically soft and spongy

Etymology

From 'coffee' plus 'cake', reflecting its traditional role as a cake served with coffee.
