Philippopolis

1. PROPN

/ fɪˌlɪpəˈpɑləs / (AmE), / fɪˌlɪpəˈpɒlɪs / (BrE)
an ancient city in southern Bulgaria, historically a commercial center and now known as Plovdiv.
ancient city in southern Bulgaria

Examples

Archaeologists have uncovered impressive Roman ruins in Philippopolis.
The ancient city of Philippopolis was a significant trading center in the region.
Today, Philippopolis is better known as the modern city of Plovdiv.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Plovdiv - modern name for ancient Philippopolis, city in Bulgaria

Etymology

From Greek Philippopolis, meaning ‘city of Philip’, named after Philip II of Macedon.
