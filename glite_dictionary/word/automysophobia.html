automysophobia

1. NOUN

/ ˌɔːtoʊˌmaɪsəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˌmɪsəˈfəʊbɪə / (BrE)
an abnormal or morbid fear of being dirty or of dirtiness
morbid fear of being dirty
medical

Examples

Her automysophobia made it difficult for her to engage in outdoor activities.
People with automysophobia may wash their hands excessively to avoid feeling dirty.
The therapist developed a treatment plan to help him overcome his automysophobia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mysophobia - morbid fear of dirt or contamination

Etymology

From Greek 'auto-' (self), 'mysos' (dirt), and '-phobia' (fear).
