to cocainize

1. VERB

/ koʊˈkeɪnaɪz / (AmE), / kəʊˈkeɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to anesthetize a part of the body with cocaine, especially for medical purposes
anesthetize with cocaine
medical

Examples

The surgeon decided to cocainize the nasal cavity before the minor procedure.
In early 20th-century medicine, doctors would sometimes cocainize wounds to manage pain.
Medical texts once described how to cocainize tissue for local anesthesia.

Etymology

Formed from 'cocaine' + '-ize' (to make or treat with).
