kami

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmi / (BrE)
A spirit, deity, or sacred power in the Shinto religion, often associated with natural forces, ancestors, or revered persons.
Shinto spirit or deity

Examples

Many Japanese shrines are dedicated to a specific kami that protects the local area.
People often visit Shinto temples to pay their respects to the kami during festivals.
According to legend, the mountain is the dwelling place of a powerful kami.

Etymology

From Japanese 神 (kami), meaning god, spirit, or deity.
