bedrest

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌrɛst / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌrɛst / (BrE)
prolonged confinement to bed, often as prescribed by a doctor for medical reasons, especially during illness or recovery
prolonged confinement to bed for health reasons
medical

Examples

Strict bedrest was recommended by the doctor after her surgery.
During her pregnancy complications, she was put on bedrest for three weeks.
Extended bedrest can sometimes result in muscle weakness if not properly managed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedridden - confined to bed because of illness or infirmity
