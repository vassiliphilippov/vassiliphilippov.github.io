tribadism

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪbəˌdɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtrɪbədɪzəm / (BrE)
a sexual practice in which a woman stimulates her vulva with her partner's vulva or body, especially among lesbians
female-female sexual practice

Examples

Some couples practice tribadism as a way to increase intimacy and pleasure.
The documentary explored representations of tribadism in art and literature throughout history.
She explained that tribadism is a consensual sexual practice between women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scissoring - sexual act where two women rub their vulvas together

Etymology

From Greek 'tribas', meaning 'she who rubs', from 'tribein' (to rub).
