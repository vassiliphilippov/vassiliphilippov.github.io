Charlotte

1. PROPN

/ ʃɑrˈlɑt / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈlɒt / (BrE)
a city in south-central North Carolina, noted as the state's largest city and a major commercial hub
largest city in North Carolina, USA

Examples

Many major corporations have their headquarters in Charlotte, making it a key business center.
The NBA team, the Hornets, is based in Charlotte.
Traffic in Charlotte can be heavy during rush hour.

Etymology

Named after Queen Charlotte of Mecklenburg-Strelitz, consort of King George III of Britain.
