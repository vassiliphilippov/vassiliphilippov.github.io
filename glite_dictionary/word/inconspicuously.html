inconspicuously

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kənˈspɪk.ju.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not easily seen or noticed; so as to avoid attracting attention.
without attracting attention

Examples

She entered the meeting inconspicuously, trying not to disturb the ongoing discussion.
The security camera was inconspicuously placed in the corner of the room.
He tried to inconspicuously slip out of the party before anyone noticed.

Synset Examples

he had entered the room inconspicuously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unobtrusively - in a way that does not attract attention
  • Antonym: conspicuously - in a way that is easily seen or noticed

Etymology

From 'inconspicuous' + '-ly'.
