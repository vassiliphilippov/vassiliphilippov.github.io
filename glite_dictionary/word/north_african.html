North African

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɔrθ ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθ ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the region of northern Africa or its people, culture, or languages.
of or relating to northern Africa

Examples

The North African desert is known for its extreme temperatures.
She specializes in North African history and archaeology.
Many North African dishes feature couscous as a staple ingredient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: North Africa - the geographical region comprising the northern part of Africa
