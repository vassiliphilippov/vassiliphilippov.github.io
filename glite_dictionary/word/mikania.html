Mikania

1. PROPN

/ maɪˈkeɪniə / (AmE), / mɪˈkɑːnɪə / (BrE)
A large genus of evergreen climbing plants (lianas) native to tropical America, some species of which are invasive weeds.
genus of evergreen tropical lianas

Examples

The invasive Mikania is threatening native plant species in several tropical regions.
Botanists have identified more than 400 species within the Mikania genus.
The rapid growth of Mikania vines can quickly overwhelm other vegetation.

Etymology

Named after Johann Christian Mikan, Czech botanist (1769–1844).
