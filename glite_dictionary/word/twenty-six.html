twenty-six

1. NUM

/ ˌtwɛntiˈsɪks / (AmE), / ˌtwentiˈsɪks / (BrE)
The number equal to twenty plus six; 26.
the number 26

Examples

I turned twenty-six last month and celebrated with my friends.
There are twenty-six letters in the modern English alphabet.
He bought twenty-six tickets for the raffle.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-five - the number 25
  • Degree Higher: twenty-seven - the number 27
