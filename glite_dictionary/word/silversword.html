silversword

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚˌsɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəsɔːd / (BrE)
a low-growing plant native to Hawaii, found only in volcanic craters, with rosettes of narrow, pointed silver-green leaves and clusters of red-purple flowers on a tall stem
Hawaiian plant with silver-green leaves and tall flower stalk

Examples

The silversword is a unique plant found only on the slopes of Hawaii’s volcanic craters.
Tourists are often amazed by the dazzling appearance of the silversword in full bloom.
Conservationists work hard to protect the rare silversword from extinction.
