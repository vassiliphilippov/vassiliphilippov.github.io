to run (for exercise)

1. VERB

/ rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
to move at a speed faster than a walk, usually as a form of physical exercise or workout
jog or sprint as exercise

Examples

She likes to run every morning before work to stay healthy.
Many people run in the park for exercise after a long day at the office.
He started to run regularly and noticed an improvement in his fitness.

Synset Examples

He decided to run for the exercise instead of joining the gym.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jog - to run at a slow, steady pace for exercise
  • Antonym: walk - to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
