Esidrix

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛsɪˌdrɪks / (AmE), / ˈɛsɪˌdrɪks / (BrE)
A trade name for a diuretic drug (hydrochlorothiazide) used to treat high blood pressure and edema.
diuretic drug trade name
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Esidrix to help control his high blood pressure.
Many patients experience increased urination after starting Esidrix.
If you miss a dose of Esidrix, take it as soon as you remember.

Related Terms
