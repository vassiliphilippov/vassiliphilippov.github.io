answerableness

1. NOUN

/ ˈæn.sər.ə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being answerable or responsible to someone or for something.
state of being answerable or responsible

Examples

The answerableness of politicians is crucial in a functioning democracy.
His role came with a heavy burden of answerableness to the company's board.
Discussing the answerableness of public officials is central to debates on good governance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsibility - the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something
  • Synonym: accountability - the fact or condition of being accountable

Etymology

From 'answerable' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality.
