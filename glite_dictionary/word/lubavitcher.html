Lubavitcher

1. NOUN

/ luˈbɑːvɪtʃər / (AmE), / luˈbavɪtʃə / (BrE)
a member of the Lubavitch movement; a follower of Chabad Hasidism
member of Chabad Hasidic movement

Examples

As a proud Lubavitcher, he attends community events and classes regularly.
Many Lubavitchers dedicate their lives to spreading Jewish teachings around the world.
The Lubavitcher welcomed them warmly into the Chabad House for the holiday celebration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hasid - a member of any Hasidic Jewish sect

Etymology

From Russian Лубавич (Lubavich), a town in Russia where the movement originated, + English -er.
