Bremerhaven

1. PROPN

/ ˌbreɪmərˈhɑːvən / (AmE), / ˌbreːmərˈhaːvən / (BrE)
A port city in northwestern Germany at the mouth of the Weser River on the North Sea, known for its large harbor and significance as a shipping center.
port city in Germany

Examples

The shipping industry is a major employer in Bremerhaven.
She visited Bremerhaven during her trip through northern Germany.
Cargo ships regularly depart from Bremerhaven to ports around the world.

Etymology

From German, literally 'Bremen's harbor', combining 'Bremen' (the nearby major city) and 'Hafen' (harbor).
