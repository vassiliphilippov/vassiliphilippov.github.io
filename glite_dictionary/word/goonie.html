goonie

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡuːni / (AmE), / ˈɡuːni / (BrE)
A large albatross, especially one with black feet, found mainly in the North Pacific.
a large albatross, esp. with black feet

Examples

The goonie soared effortlessly above the waves, searching for fish.
Several goonies nested on the rocky cliffs during the breeding season.
We spotted a rare goonie circling the ship early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albatross - a large seabird with long narrow wings
