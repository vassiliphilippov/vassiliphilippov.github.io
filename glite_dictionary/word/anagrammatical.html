anagrammatical

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.ə.ɡræˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.ə.ɡræˈmæt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving anagrams; containing or making an anagram.
relating to anagrams

Examples

The poem's anagrammatical structure delighted puzzle enthusiasts.
She enjoyed writing anagrammatical riddles for her friends to solve.
His nickname was an anagrammatical play on his real name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anagram - a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another

Etymology

From 'anagram' + '-matical', patterned after 'grammatical'.
