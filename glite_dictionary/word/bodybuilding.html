bodybuilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdiˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of developing muscle size and strength through regular exercise, especially weightlifting
muscle-building physical exercise

Examples

He started bodybuilding to improve his physical health and appearance.
Many people join gyms to get serious about bodybuilding.
Her dedication to bodybuilding led her to compete in fitness competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifting - the activity of lifting heavy weights for exercise or competition

Etymology

Formed from 'body' and 'building', referring to the literal building or shaping of the body.
