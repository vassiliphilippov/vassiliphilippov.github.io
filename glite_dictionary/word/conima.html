conima

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈnaɪmə / (AmE), / kəʊˈnaɪmə / (BrE)
a gum resin obtained from the poison hemlock (Conium maculatum).
gum resin from poison hemlock

Examples

Researchers analyzed the chemical properties of conima extracted from the plant.
Traditional healers sometimes used conima in their remedies despite its toxicity.
The sample contained traces of conima, confirming its botanical origin.
