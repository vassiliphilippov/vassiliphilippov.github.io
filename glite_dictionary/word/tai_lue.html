Tai Lue

1. NOUN

/ taɪ ˈluː / (AmE), / taɪ ˈluː / (BrE)
a language of the Tai language family spoken mainly in Southern Yunnan Province, China, particularly in the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, and in nearby areas
a Tai language spoken mainly in southern Yunnan, China

Examples

Many members of the community still speak Tai Lue as their primary language.
Researchers have published a grammar book on the Tai Lue language.
The Tai Lue script is distinctive and used in local literature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dai - a related group of Tai languages spoken in China and Southeast Asia
