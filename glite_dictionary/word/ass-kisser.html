ass-kisser

1. NOUN

/ æs ˈkɪsɚ / (AmE), / æs ˈkɪsə / (BrE)
a person who flatteringly tries to please someone, especially someone in authority, in order to gain a personal advantage
a sycophantic flatterer for personal gain
slang vulgar

Examples

Everyone knows that Mark is just an ass-kisser who agrees with the boss to get promoted.
She can't stand ass-kissers who always flatter managers for special treatment.
He earned a reputation as an ass-kisser after constantly complimenting those in charge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously towards someone important
  • Synonym: bootlicker - someone who acts in a servile, ingratiating way
  • Synonym: flatterer - someone who gives excessive praise for personal gain
  • Synonym: brown-noser - a person who tries to please someone in order to gain a personal advantage

Etymology

From 'ass' (slang for buttocks) + 'kisser' (one who kisses); alluding to sycophantic behavior.
