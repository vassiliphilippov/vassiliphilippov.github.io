highchair

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌtʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌtʃɛə / (BrE)
A chair designed for feeding a young child, with long legs, a tray, and often a footrest.
chair for feeding babies or toddlers

Examples

We placed our daughter in the highchair and secured the tray before dinner.
The restaurant provided a clean highchair for their youngest guest.
After each meal, she wipes down the highchair to keep it spotless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: booster seat - a seat designed to raise a child to the proper height at a table
