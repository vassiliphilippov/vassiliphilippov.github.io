Mogadiscio

1. PROPN

/ moʊˈɡɑːdɪsioʊ / (AmE), / məˈɡædɪsiəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Somalia, located on the coast of the Indian Ocean
capital city of Somalia

Examples

After years of conflict, life in Mogadiscio is starting to return to normal.
The port of Mogadiscio has long been a hub for trade in East Africa.
He traveled from Nairobi to Mogadiscio for a business conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mogadishu - the capital and largest city of Somalia

Etymology

Italian adaptation of the Somali name 'Mogadishu'.
