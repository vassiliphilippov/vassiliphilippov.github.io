hooey

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuː.i / (AmE), / ˈhuː.i / (BrE)
Nonsense; senseless or foolish talk.
nonsense; foolish talk
informal slang

Examples

Don't believe his story—it's just a load of hooey.
She dismissed the superstition as pure hooey.
All that advertising hype is complete hooey.
