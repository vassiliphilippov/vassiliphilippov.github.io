Ballota nigra

1. NOUN

/ bəˈloʊtə ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / bəˈləʊtə ˈnɪɡrə / (BrE)
An ill-smelling European herb with wrinkled leaves and whorls of dark purple flowers, commonly known as black horehound.
ill-smelling European herb; black horehound

Examples

The garden had several Ballota nigra plants growing near the wall.
Herbalists sometimes use Ballota nigra for traditional remedies despite its unpleasant odor.
The dark purple flowers of Ballota nigra attract pollinators in early summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: black horehound - common name for Ballota nigra

Etymology

From Latin: genus name 'Ballota' and species 'nigra' meaning 'black'.
