lowboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
A low chest of drawers or dressing table with drawers, usually supported on four legs.
low chest of drawers or dressing table

Examples

The antique lowboy in the hallway was crafted from cherry wood.
She kept her jewelry and brushes in the drawers of her lowboy.
They restored a Victorian lowboy to display in their living room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: highboy - a tall chest of drawers, often in two sections

Etymology

From 'low' + 'boy', coined in 18th-century English to distinguish from 'highboy'.
