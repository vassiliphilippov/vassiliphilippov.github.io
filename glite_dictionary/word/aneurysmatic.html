aneurysmatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.jəˌrɪzˈmæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌanjʊərɪzˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by an aneurysm
relating to an aneurysm
medical

Examples

The patient exhibited aneurysmatic dilation of the artery detected during the imaging scan.
Due to his aneurysmatic aorta, he required careful surgical monitoring.
Aneurysmatic changes are often observed in advanced cases of vascular disease.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aneurysmal - relating to or affected by an aneurysm (synonymous medical adjective)

Etymology

From aneurysm + -atic, modeled on similar medical adjectival forms.
