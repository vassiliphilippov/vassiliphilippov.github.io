swanneck

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑnˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈswɒnˌnɛk / (BrE)
A type of orchid belonging to the genus Cycnoches, characterized by slender arching flower stems resembling a swan's neck.
an orchid of the genus Cycnoches

Examples

The greenhouse displayed a rare swanneck in full bloom, attracting orchid enthusiasts from afar.
Botanists admired the delicate arch of the swanneck, noting how its form inspired its name.
She specializes in cultivating orchids, particularly the swanneck species native to South America.

Etymology

From 'swan' and 'neck', referring to the characteristic shape of the flower stem.
