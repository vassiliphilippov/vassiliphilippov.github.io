to re-incorporate

1. VERB

/ ˌriː.ɪnˈkɔːr.pəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.ɪnˈkɔː.pəˌreɪt / (BrE)
to incorporate again or anew; to integrate or combine something once more into a larger whole
incorporate again or anew

Examples

After years apart, the two companies decided to re-incorporate their assets into a single entity.
The committee voted to re-incorporate the previously excluded data into their final report.
Efforts to re-incorporate traditional practices into modern teaching methods have been met with enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorporate - to combine or include as part of something

Etymology

Formed from the prefix re- (again) and incorporate (from Latin incorporare ‘to form into a body’).
