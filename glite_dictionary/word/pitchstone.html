pitchstone

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃstəʊn / (BrE)
a dark, glassy volcanic rock of acidic composition, related to obsidian
dark glassy volcanic rock

Examples

The geologist collected a sample of pitchstone from the old volcanic flow.
Jewelry made from polished pitchstone can have a striking appearance.
Unlike obsidian, pitchstone often contains small crystals and water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsidian - a hard, dark, glasslike volcanic rock
