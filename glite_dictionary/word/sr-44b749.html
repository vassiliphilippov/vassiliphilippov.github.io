sr

1. NOUN

/ ɛs ɑr / (AmE), / ɛs ɑː / (BrE)
The symbol for the steradian, the SI unit of solid angle, equal to the solid angle subtended at the centre of a sphere by an area of the surface equal to the square of the sphere's radius.
symbol for the SI unit of solid angle

Examples

The lens captures a solid angle of 2 sr from its position.
Illuminance is often measured in lumens per sr per square meter.
A full sphere encompasses a solid angle of 4π sr.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steradian - the SI base unit of solid angle

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'steradian', from Greek 'stereos' (solid) + 'radius'.
