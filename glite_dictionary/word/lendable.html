lendable

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɛndəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈlɛndəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of something) able to be lent; available for lending.
able to be lent

Examples

Only lendable assets are considered in this calculation.
The library has a special section for lendable devices such as tablets and laptops.
Their available capital consists almost entirely of lendable funds.

Synset Examples

lendable resources

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loanable - able to be lent; alternative form
  • Antonym: nonlendable - not able to be lent

Etymology

From 'lend' + '-able', following the pattern of adjectives formed with '-able'.
