coreligionist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊ.rɪˈlɪdʒ.ə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.rɪˈlɪdʒ.ə.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who shares the same religion as another
someone of the same religion

Examples

She found comfort among her coreligionists during the difficult times.
At the international conference, he met several coreligionists from different countries.
The charity was organized by a group of coreligionists who shared similar values.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: believer - person who has religious faith
  • Antonym: atheist - person who does not believe in any deity

Etymology

From co- (together) + religionist, modeled after French 'coreligionnaire'.
