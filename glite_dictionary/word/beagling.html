beagling

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiːɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport of hunting rabbits with beagles.
rabbit hunting with beagles

Examples

He developed a passion for beagling after joining the local club.
Beagling was a popular pastime in rural England during the 19th century.
Many enthusiasts consider beagling a traditional form of countryside recreation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hound trailing - a type of dog racing or hunting using hounds rather than beagles

Etymology

From 'beagle', the breed of dog, plus '-ing' denoting the activity.
