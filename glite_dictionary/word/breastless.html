breastless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɛstləs / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstləs / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having no breasts; lacking a breast or breasts.
having no breasts

Examples

The sculpture depicted a breastless figure, unusual for that period of art.
After surgery, she became breastless but continued to feel confident in her appearance.
Some species of birds are naturally breastless due to their evolutionary adaptations.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bosomy - having large breasts
  • Synonym: flat-chested - having a flat chest, lacking prominent breasts

Etymology

Formed from 'breast' + '-less' (Old English/Proto-Germanic suffix meaning 'without').
