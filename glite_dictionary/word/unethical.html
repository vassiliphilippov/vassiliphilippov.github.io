unethical

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɛθɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛθɪkəl / (BrE)
not morally acceptable or not conforming to approved standards of social or professional behavior
not conforming to accepted moral standards

Examples

The company faced criticism for its unethical business practices.
Many considered the doctor's actions to be highly unethical.
Using someone else's work without credit is clearly unethical.

Synset Examples

unethical business practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immoral - not conforming to accepted standards of morality
  • Antonym: ethical - conforming to accepted standards of moral or professional behavior

Etymology

From un- (not) + ethical, from Greek ēthikos (moral).
