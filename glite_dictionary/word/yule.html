Yule

1. NOUN

/ juːl / (AmE), / juːl / (BrE)
an old term for the Christmas season or the festive period from December 24 to January 6, especially in historical or traditional contexts.
Christmas or winter festive season

Examples

Many ancient customs are still observed during Yule in Scandinavian countries.
The family gathered around the fireplace to celebrate Yule together.
Some traditions honor both Christmas and the older Yule festival.

Etymology

From Old English *geōl*, related to Old Norse *jól* (the midwinter festival).
