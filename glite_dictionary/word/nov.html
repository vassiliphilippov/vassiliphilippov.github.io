Nov

1. NOUN

/ nɑv / (AmE), / nɒv / (BrE)
The standard abbreviation for the month of November, following October and preceding December.
abbreviation for November (month)

Examples

Many school terms start in Nov and end in December.
Her birthday is in Nov, just after Halloween.
The leaves had mostly fallen by Nov in our neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: November - the eleventh month of the year

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'November,' from Latin 'novem' (nine), as November was the ninth month in the Roman calendar.
