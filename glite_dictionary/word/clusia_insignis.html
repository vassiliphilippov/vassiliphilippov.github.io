Clusia insignis

1. NOUN

/ ˈkluːzi.ə ɪnˈsɪɡnɪs / (AmE), / ˈkluːzi.ə ɪnˈsɪɡnɪs / (BrE)
An epiphytic species of the Clusia genus native to British Guiana.
epiphytic plant species native to British Guiana

Examples

The Clusia insignis is well adapted to growing as an epiphyte in the humid forests of British Guiana.
Botanists studying tropical ecosystems often encounter Clusia insignis high in the tree canopies.
The unique leaf structure of Clusia insignis helps it conserve water in challenging environments.

Etymology

From the genus name Clusia; species name from Latin 'insignis' meaning distinguished or remarkable.
