to alphabetise

1. VERB

/ ˈæl.fə.bəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ælˈfəbɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange (words or items) in alphabetical order
arrange in alphabetical order
british

Examples

Please alphabetise the files before you leave for the day.
The names were alphabetised for easier reference in the directory.
I spent an hour alphabetising my collection of books.

Synset Examples

Alphabetize the list

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to alphabetize - arrange in alphabetical order (American spelling)

Etymology

From alphabet + -ise, influenced by French 'alphabétiser'.
