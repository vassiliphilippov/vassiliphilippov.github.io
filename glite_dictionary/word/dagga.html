dagga

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑːɡə / (AmE), / ˈdaɡə / (BrE)
A South African herb, often Cannabis sativa, smoked recreationally like tobacco.
South African herb smoked like tobacco

Examples

Dagga is commonly used in some rural communities in South Africa for recreational purposes.
Police seized several kilograms of dagga during the raid.
My uncle warned me about the effects of smoking dagga.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marijuana - Cannabis used as a psychoactive drug
  • Synonym: cannabis - Plant genus including species used for hemp and marijuana

Etymology

From Afrikaans 'dagga', possibly from Khoikhoi 'daXa-b' (tobacco).
