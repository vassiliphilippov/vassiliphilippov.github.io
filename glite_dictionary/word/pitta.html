pitta

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtə / (AmE), / ˈpɪtə / (BrE)
A brightly colored, chiefly terrestrial bird of the genus Pitta, with short wings and tail and a stout bill, found in Asia, Australia, and Africa.
brightly colored terrestrial bird genus

Examples

The forest floor came alive when a rare pitta hopped into view.
Birdwatchers travel far to catch a glimpse of the colorful pitta during migration season.
Some species of pitta can be identified by their distinctive blue and green plumage.

Etymology

From modern Latin Pitta, the genus name, probably from Telugu (South Indian language) 'pitta', meaning 'small bird'.
