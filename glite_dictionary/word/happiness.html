happiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈhæpinəs / (BrE)
The state of feeling or showing pleasure or contentment.
state of pleasure or contentment

Examples

She found happiness in the simple things in life, like a warm cup of tea.
Many people believe that true happiness comes from helping others.
After years of struggle, he finally achieved the happiness he had always dreamed of.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joy - a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: sadness - the state of being unhappy

Etymology

From Middle English 'happinesse', from 'happy' + '-ness'.
