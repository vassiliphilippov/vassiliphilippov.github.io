low-cut

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌkʌt / (BrE)
(of a garment) having a neckline that is cut low, revealing more of the chest or shoulders.
having a low neckline (of clothes)

Examples

She wore a low-cut dress to the gala, turning many heads.
That top is too low-cut for school, according to the dress code.
He noticed her low-cut blouse as soon as she entered the room.

Synset Examples

a low-cut neckline

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-necked - (of a garment) having a high neckline

2. ADJ

/ ˌloʊˈkʌt / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈkʌt / (BrE)
(of clothing) having a neckline, back, or other edge cut lower than usual, so that more skin is exposed.
having a low neckline or back (clothing)

Examples

She wore a low-cut evening gown to the awards ceremony.
The swimsuit had a low-cut back, making it both stylish and daring.
Many summer dresses feature a low-cut neckline to keep cool in hot weather.
