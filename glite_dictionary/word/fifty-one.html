fifty-one

1. NUM

/ ˌfɪftiˈwʌn / (AmE), / ˌfɪftiˈwʌn / (BrE)
the number 51; being one more than fifty
the number 51

Examples

She celebrated her fifty-one years with a big family party.
There were fifty-one students enrolled in the seminar.
The winning number drawn was fifty-one.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifty - the number 50
  • Degree Higher: fifty-two - the number 52
