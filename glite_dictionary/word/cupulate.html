cupulate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːpjʊˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːpjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(especially in botany) shaped like or supporting a cupule, a small cup-like structure.
shaped like a cupule (botany)

Examples

The acorn is attached to the branch by a cupulate base.
Many species in this genus have cupulate fruit that resemble tiny cups.
The seed pods are cupulate in shape, fitting snugly around the seeds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cupule - a small cup-shaped structure (botany)

Etymology

From Latin cupula (little cup) + -ate (having the nature of).
