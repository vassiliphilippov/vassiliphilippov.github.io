Moquelumnan

1. NOUN

/ moʊˈkɛləmnæn / (AmE), / məʊˈkɛləmnæn / (BrE)
a member of the Penutian language family formerly spoken by the Miwok people of California
Penutian language of the Miwok people

Examples

Linguists have studied the Moquelumnan languages to better understand their relationship to other Penutian tongues.
The last fluent speakers of Moquelumnan passed away in the twentieth century.
Several Moquelumnan words survive in local place names throughout California.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Miwok - Native American people of California and their languages

Etymology

From 'Moquelumne', the name of a Native American group and river in California, with the suffix '-an' indicating relation.
