somnambulation

1. NOUN

/ sɑmˌnæmbjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / sɒmˌnæmbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of walking while asleep; sleepwalking.
walking while asleep
medical

Examples

His episodes of somnambulation often left him waking up in the living room.
Research on somnambulation has revealed links to stress and irregular sleep patterns.
She sought medical advice after her somnambulation led her to leave the house at night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleepwalking - the act of walking while asleep

Etymology

From Latin somnus (sleep) + ambulatio (a walking).
