dual-purpose

1. ADJ

/ ˈduːəl ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / ˈdjuːəl ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
designed for or serving two different purposes at the same time
serving two purposes

Examples

This dual-purpose cleaner can be used on both wood and tile floors.
The farm invested in dual-purpose cattle, which provide both milk and meat.
Her dual-purpose jacket works as both a raincoat and a windbreaker.

Synset Examples

dual-purpose trash receptacles

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-purpose - serving only one specific aim or function
