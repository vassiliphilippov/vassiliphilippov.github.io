salaam

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɑm / (AmE), / səˈlɑːm / (BrE)
a deep bow of respect or a traditional Muslim greeting or gesture of salutation
deep bow; Muslim salutation

Examples

He greeted the elder with a respectful salaam before entering the room.
In some cultures, it is customary to offer a salaam as a sign of respect.
The visitors responded to the host’s welcome with a graceful salaam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bow - a bending of the body as a sign of respect
  • Synonym: greeting - an expression or gesture of welcome or salutation

Etymology

From Arabic *salām* meaning 'peace', used as a greeting.

2. VERB

/ səˈlɑm / (AmE), / səˈlɑːm / (BrE)
to greet someone with a salaam, a gesture of respect or greeting commonly used in Muslim cultures, typically involving a low bow with the right palm placed on the forehead.
greet with a salaam gesture

Examples

The ambassador salaamed upon entering the room to greet his hosts.
During the ceremony, he respectfully salaamed before the elders.
She salaamed to show her gratitude and respect after receiving the award.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bow - to bend the upper part of the body as a gesture of respect or greeting

Etymology

From Arabic 'salām' (peace, greeting).
