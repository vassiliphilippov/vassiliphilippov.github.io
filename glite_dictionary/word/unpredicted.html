unpredicted

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.prɪˈdɪk.tɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.prɪˈdɪk.tɪd / (BrE)
not expected or foreseen; occurring without being predicted or anticipated
not expected; unforeseen

Examples

The team was eliminated after an unpredicted loss in the first round.
Sudden, unpredicted rain showers caught the hikers off guard.
The discovery of the lost painting was an unpredicted surprise for the art world.

Synset Examples

they arrived unannounced
a totally unheralded telegram that his daughter … died last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unexpected - not anticipated or foreseen
  • Antonym: predicted - foreseen or expected in advance

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + predicted, from Latin praedicere (to foretell).
