railroader

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌroʊdər / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrəʊdə / (BrE)
a person who works for a railroad, especially as an employee involved in its operations or management
railway employee

Examples

My grandfather was a proud railroader for more than forty years.
The museum honors the life and work of the American railroader.
As a lifelong railroader, she traveled across the country on freight trains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railwayman - a man who works for a railway
  • Synonym: railway worker - a person employed on a railway
