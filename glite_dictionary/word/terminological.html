terminological

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɝː.mə.nəˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌtɜː.mɪ.nəˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the terms or system of terms used in a particular subject or field
relating to terminology or terms

Examples

There are ongoing terminological debates among linguists about the precise definitions of certain terms.
The committee met to resolve a terminological issue that was hindering the agreement.
Her research paper addresses terminological inconsistencies found in scientific literature.

Synset Examples

terminological disputes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terminology - the set of technical words or expressions used in a particular subject

Etymology

From 'terminology' + '-ical'; 'terminology' from Latin 'terminus' (end, boundary).
