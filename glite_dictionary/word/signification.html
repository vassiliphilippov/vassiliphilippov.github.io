signification

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɪɡnəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the meaning or sense that is conveyed or expressed by a word, sign, or symbol
meaning expressed by word or symbol

Examples

The signification of the symbol changed over time due to cultural shifts.
Some Chinese characters have more than one signification, depending on context.
He struggled to grasp the precise signification of the legal term.

Synset Examples

what is the meaning of this sentence?

the import of his announcement was ambiguous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meaning - the thing that is meant or intended by a word or symbol
  • Synonym: significance - the meaning implied or intended by something, often with importance attached

Etymology

From Old French 'signification', from Latin 'significatio', from 'significare' (to signify).
