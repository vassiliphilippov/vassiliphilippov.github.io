multifaceted

1. ADJ

/ ˌmʌltiˈfæsɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈfæsɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having many different aspects, features, or elements.
having many aspects or features

Examples

Her multifaceted personality makes her both intriguing and difficult to understand.
The investigation was multifaceted, involving legal, financial, and ethical aspects.
This multifaceted approach to education addresses the needs of diverse learners.

Synset Examples

a many-sided subject
a multifaceted undertaking
multifarious interests
the multifarious noise of a great city
a miscellaneous crowd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: many-sided - having many aspects or facets
  • Synonym: multifarious - having many varied parts or aspects

Etymology

From multi- (many) + facet (a particular aspect or feature), originally referring to gemstones with many surfaces.
