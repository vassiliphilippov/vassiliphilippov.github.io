fetching

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛtʃ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfetʃ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
attractive in a way that captures interest or attention; charming
attractive, charming

Examples

She wore a fetching red dress to the party and turned many heads.
That puppy has such a fetching expression that everyone wants to pet it.
His manners were so fetching that he made instant friends wherever he went.

Synset Examples

a fetching new hairstyle
something inexpressibly taking in his manner
a winning personality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing to the senses
  • Synonym: charming - pleasant or delightful in manner or appearance
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or attractive in appearance

Etymology

From the verb 'fetch', meaning 'to attract' or 'to draw', with the adjectival suffix '-ing'.
