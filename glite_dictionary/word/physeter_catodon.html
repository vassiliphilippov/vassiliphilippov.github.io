Physeter catodon

1. NOUN

/ faɪˈsiːtər kəˈtoʊdɑn / (AmE), / fɪˈsiːtə kəˈtəʊdɒn / (BrE)
The sperm whale, a large whale with a distinctive block-shaped head containing spermaceti and oil, also known as a source of ambergris.
the sperm whale (large toothed whale)

Examples

The Physeter catodon can dive more than 1,000 meters in search of squid.
Scientists have studied the oil found in the head of Physeter catodon for years.
Ambergris, highly valued in perfumery, comes from the digestive system of Physeter catodon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sperm whale - common name for Physeter catodon

Etymology

From Greek 'physētēr' (blower) and Latin 'catodon' (downward tooth).
