New Delhi

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈdɛli / (AmE), / njuː ˈdɛli / (BrE)
the capital city of India, located in the north-central part of the country and serving as the seat of the national government.
capital city of India

Examples

Many foreign embassies are located in New Delhi because it is the capital of India.
The Prime Minister addressed the nation from New Delhi last night.
Flights to New Delhi are often fully booked during the festival season.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Delhi - a major city and union territory surrounding New Delhi

2. PROPN

/ nuː ˈdɛli / (AmE), / njuː ˈdɛli / (BrE)
the capital city of India, located in the northern part of the country and serving as the seat of the Indian government
capital city of India

Examples

She moved to New Delhi last year to start a new job.
The embassy is located in New Delhi, close to several government buildings.
Many international conferences are hosted in New Delhi each year.

Synset Examples

He spent a week exploring New Delhi's landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Delhi - historical city in India, adjacent to New Delhi

Etymology

Named in 1911, when the British selected a site south of Old Delhi as the new capital of British India.
