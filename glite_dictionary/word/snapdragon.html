snapdragon

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (AmE), / ˈsnæpˌdræɡən / (BrE)
a garden plant of the genus Antirrhinum, known for its spikes of brightly colored flowers that resemble the face of a dragon
a plant with dragon-shaped flowers

Examples

The children loved seeing the snapdragon flowers open and close like tiny mouths.
She planted pink snapdragons along the walkway for a splash of color.
Snapdragons thrive in full sun and well-drained soil.

Etymology

From the resemblance of the flower to a dragon's mouth that snaps shut when pressed.
