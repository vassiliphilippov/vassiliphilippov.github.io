meritoriously

1. ADV

/ ˌmɛrɪˈtɔriəsli / (AmE), / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːrɪəsli / (BrE)
in a way that deserves praise or reward; in a meritorious manner
in a praiseworthy manner

Examples

She acted meritoriously during the emergency, saving several lives.
The award recognizes employees who have performed meritoriously in their duties.
He completed the project meritoriously, exceeding all expectations.

Synset Examples

he served his country meritoriously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meritorious - deserving praise or reward

Etymology

From meritorious + -ly, following Latin meritōrius.
