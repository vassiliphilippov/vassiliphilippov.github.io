Podiceps ruficollis

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɒdɪˈsɛps ˌruːfɪˈkɒlɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒdɪˈsɛps ˌruːfɪˈkɒlɪs / (BrE)
A small European grebe, known commonly as the little grebe or dabchick.
a small European grebe (little grebe)

Examples

During our walk by the lake, we spotted a Podiceps ruficollis diving for food.
Birdwatchers in Europe often hope to glimpse a Podiceps ruficollis among the reeds.
The distinctive call of the Podiceps ruficollis echoed across the water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: little grebe - the common name for Podiceps ruficollis

Etymology

From Latin: 'Podiceps' meaning 'rump-footed' and 'ruficollis' meaning 'red-necked'.
