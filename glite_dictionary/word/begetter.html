begetter

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡɛtər / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɛtə / (BrE)
a male parent, especially a father; one who fathers or produces offspring
male parent; biological father

Examples

He was known as the begetter of three talented children.
The artist saw himself as the begetter of a new creative movement.
Many poets have praised their begetter in moving verses.

Synset Examples

his father was born in Atlanta

Related Terms

  • Synonym: father - male parent
  • Antonym: mother - female parent

Etymology

From Old English 'begietan' (to get, obtain), related to 'beget'.
