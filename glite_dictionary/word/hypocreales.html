Hypocreales

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪ.poʊˈkriː.eɪlz / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪ.pə(ʊ)ˈkriː.eɪlz / (BrE)
an order of fungi in the class Sordariomycetes, including many species known for their brightly colored fruiting bodies
order of fungi in Sordariomycetes
medical

Examples

The Hypocreales include many fungi that form symbiotic relationships with plants.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the Hypocreales order.
Members of the Hypocreales are often studied for their potential in biological control.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Hypocrea (a genus of fungi) + -ales (order suffix in taxonomy).
