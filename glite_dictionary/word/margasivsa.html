Margasivsa

1. NOUN

/ mɑrˈɡɑːsɪvsə / (AmE), / mɑːˈɡɑːsɪvsə / (BrE)
the ninth month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling in November–December in the Gregorian calendar
ninth month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

Many important Hindu festivals are celebrated during Margasivsa.
The weather in India during Margasivsa is usually cool and pleasant.
He planned his pilgrimage to coincide with Margasivsa.

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'Mārgaśīrṣa', the name of the month in the Hindu calendar.
