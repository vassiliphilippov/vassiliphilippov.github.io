to ooh

1. VERB

/ uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
to express admiration, pleasure, or excitement by uttering the sound 'ooh'
express admiration or pleasure vocally

Examples

The children oohed when they saw the fireworks light up the sky.
Her friends ooh and aah over her new haircut every time she tries a new style.
Fans oohed as the magician revealed his final trick on stage.

Synset Examples

They oohed and aahed when they unwrapped the presents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to aah - to express amazement, admiration, or pleasure by uttering 'aah'
