warehouseman

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛrˌhaʊs.mæn / (AmE), / ˈweə.haʊs.mən / (BrE)
a man who manages or works in a warehouse, especially as a laborer or supervisor
man working in a warehouse

Examples

The warehouseman stacked boxes carefully to maximize the storage space.
After years on the job, the warehouseman was promoted to shift supervisor.
The company hired a new warehouseman to handle incoming shipments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warehouse worker - a person employed in a warehouse
  • Antonym: warehousewoman - a woman who manages or works in a warehouse

Etymology

From warehouse + man; warehouse from Middle English, originally 'ware' (goods) + 'house'.
