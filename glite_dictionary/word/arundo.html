Arundo

1. NOUN

/ əˈrʌndoʊ / (AmE), / əˈrʌndəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of tall, coarse perennial grasses found in warm regions, commonly known as reeds
a genus of tall perennial grasses (reeds)

Examples

The lake's edge was lined with dense stands of Arundo that provided habitat for many birds.
Invasive Arundo threatens native plant species along the riverbanks.
Researchers are studying Arundo for its potential use as a bioenergy crop.

Etymology

From Latin 'arundo', meaning 'reed'.
