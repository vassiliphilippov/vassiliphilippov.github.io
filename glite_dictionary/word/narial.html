narial

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛəriəl / (AmE), / ˈnɛːrɪəl / (BrE)
relating to or near the nares (nostrils)
relating to the nostrils
medical

Examples

The bird's narial openings are elongated and situated high on its beak.
Some reptiles possess specialized narial passages for breathing while submerged.
The paleontologist found evidence of an enlarged narial cavity in the fossil skull.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nasal - relating to the nose

Etymology

From Latin 'nares' (nostrils) + English suffix '-al'.
