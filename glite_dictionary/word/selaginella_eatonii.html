Selaginella eatonii

1. NOUN

/ sɛləˈdʒɪnələ iˈtoʊni.aɪ / (AmE), / sɛləˈdʒɪnɪlə iːˈtəʊniː.aɪ / (BrE)
A species of spikemoss native to Florida and other regions in the southeastern United States.
spikemoss species native to Florida

Examples

The delicate fronds of Selaginella eatonii thrive in the humid regions of Florida.
Botanists collected samples of Selaginella eatonii for further research on spikemoss varieties.
Many gardeners value Selaginella eatonii for its unique appearance and ground-covering ability.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spikemoss - Common name for members of the genus Selaginella

Etymology

Genus name from Latinized form of Selago (a plant name); species named after botanist Daniel Cady Eaton.
