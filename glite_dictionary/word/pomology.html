pomology

1. NOUN

/ poʊˈmɑlədʒi / (AmE), / pəʊˈmɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study and cultivation of fruit and fruit crops
study and cultivation of fruit
medical

Examples

He decided to specialize in pomology after discovering his passion for fruit-bearing trees.
The university offers advanced courses in pomology for students interested in fruit science.
Researchers in pomology are developing new, disease-resistant apple varieties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botany - the scientific study of plants

Etymology

From Latin 'pomum' (fruit) + Greek 'logia' (study).
