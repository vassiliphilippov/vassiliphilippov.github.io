fashioned

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæʃənd / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ(ə)nd / (BrE)
made or shaped in a particular way, especially with artistic skill or careful planning.
shaped or made in a specific, often artistic, way

Examples

The gallery showcased a selection of fashioned vases by local artists.
She wore a beautifully fashioned gown to the ceremony.
These fashioned sculptures reflect a high degree of craftsmanship.

Synset Examples

beautifully fashioned dresses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crafted - made skillfully with hands or artistry
  • Synonym: constructed - built or put together; assembled
  • Antonym: crude - made in a rough, unrefined way

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'fashion', from Old French 'facion', from Latin 'facio' (make, do).
