Hellene

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛliːn / (AmE), / ˈhɛliːn / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Greece, especially in ancient times
native or inhabitant of Greece

Examples

Many philosophers of the ancient world were proud to be known as Hellenes.
The culture and achievements of the Hellenes greatly influenced Western civilization.
He traced his ancestry back to the Hellenes who founded the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Greek - a native or inhabitant of modern Greece or a person of Greek descent

Etymology

From Greek 'Hellēn' (Greek person), from Hellen, the mythological progenitor of the Greeks.
