kitbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɪt.bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag, typically a sturdy canvas bag, used by soldiers for carrying their clothing and equipment
soldier's bag for personal gear

Examples

The recruit stuffed his clothes into his kitbag before heading to the barracks.
She found an old army kitbag in the attic filled with memorabilia.
Each soldier carried a heavy kitbag during the long march.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knapsack - a bag for carrying belongings on the back
  • Synonym: backpack - a large bag carried on the back
  • Synonym: duffel bag - a cylindrical canvas bag for carrying personal belongings

Etymology

Formed from 'kit' (equipment) + 'bag'.
