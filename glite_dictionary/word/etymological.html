etymological

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛtəˈmɑlədʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛtɪməˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the origin and historical development of words and their meanings
relating to word origins

Examples

She gave an etymological explanation for the word's unusual spelling.
The etymological roots of 'salary' trace back to the Latin word for salt.
His argument relied on an etymological analysis of several key terms.

Synset Examples

I merely drew an etymological distinction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etymology - the study of the origin of words and how their meanings have changed

Etymology

From Medieval Latin etymologicus, from Greek etymología (study of the true sense of words).
