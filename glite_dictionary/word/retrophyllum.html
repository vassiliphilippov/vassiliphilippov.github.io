Retrophyllum

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛtroʊˈfɪləm / (AmE), / ˌriːtrəʊˈfɪləm / (BrE)
A small genus of tropical evergreen dioecious shrubs or trees native to Oceania and tropical South America.
genus of tropical evergreen shrubs or trees

Examples

The Retrophyllum genus includes several species valued for their ornamental foliage.
Researchers discovered a new species of Retrophyllum in the rainforests of New Caledonia.
Botanists studied the reproductive biology of Retrophyllum to understand its unique dioecious nature.

Etymology

From Greek 'retro-' (backward) and 'phyllon' (leaf), referring to the backward-facing leaves.
