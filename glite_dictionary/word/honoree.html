honoree

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːnəˈriː / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈriː / (BrE)
a person who receives an honor or award in recognition of their achievements or contributions
recipient of an honor or award

Examples

She was the distinguished honoree at the annual charity gala.
This year’s honoree gave an inspiring speech about service and perseverance.
Each honoree received a plaque and a certificate of achievement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awardee - a person who has received an award
  • Synonym: recipient - a person who receives something, especially an award or honor

Etymology

From honor + -ee, French-derived suffix indicating a person who receives an action.
