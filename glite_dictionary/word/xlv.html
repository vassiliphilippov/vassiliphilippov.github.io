XLV

1. NUM

/ ɛks ɛl viː / (AmE), / ɛks ɛl viː / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for the number 45
Roman numeral for 45

Examples

The page number was marked as XLV in the preface of the old book.
Super Bowl XLV was held in Texas in 2011.
This volume contains Roman numerals up to XLV on its initial pages.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: XLIV - Roman numeral for 44
  • Degree Higher: XLVI - Roman numeral for 46

Etymology

From Latin numerals: X (10), L (50), V (5); XL = 40 (50−10), V = 5.
