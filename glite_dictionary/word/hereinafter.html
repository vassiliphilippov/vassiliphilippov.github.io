hereinafter

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪr.i.ɪnˈæf.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌhɪəriɪnˈɑːftə / (BrE)
in a later section of this document or statement; from this point forward in the text
from this point forward in a document
legal

Examples

The company and its subsidiaries, hereinafter referred to as "the Group," agree to the following terms.
All rules stated hereinafter shall apply to both employees and contractors.
The parties agree that any disputes arising hereinafter will be resolved through arbitration.

Synset Examples

the landlord demises unto the tenant the premises hereinafter called the demised premises
the terms specified hereunder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereunder - in the following part of this document; as stated below

Etymology

From 'herein' + 'after', used in legal English since the 16th century.
