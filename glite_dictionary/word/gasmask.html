gasmask

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæsˌmæsk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːsˌmɑːsk / (BrE)
A protective mask equipped with a filter, worn to protect the face and lungs from poisonous gases.
protective mask against poisonous gases

Examples

The soldiers were ordered to put on their gasmasks before entering the contaminated area.
During the disaster drill, everyone practiced wearing a gasmask.
He kept a gasmask in his emergency kit just in case of a chemical spill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respirator - mask-like device for filtering harmful substances from inhaled air

Etymology

From 'gas' + 'mask', indicating a mask used against gas.
