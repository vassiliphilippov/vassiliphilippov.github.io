to birdwatch

1. VERB

/ ˈbɝdˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
to observe and study birds in their natural environment as a hobby or scientific pursuit
observe birds in nature

Examples

Many people travel to national parks to birdwatch during migration season.
She decided to birdwatch in the forest at dawn, hoping to spot some rare species.
They often birdwatch together on weekends, bringing their cameras and notebooks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: birdwatching - the activity or hobby of watching birds
