appreciator

1. NOUN

/ əˈpriʃiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person who recognizes the value, quality, or significance of something and understands it fully
someone who recognizes and understands value

Examples

As an appreciator of fine art, she visited galleries regularly.
Every appreciator of classical music will recognize this famous symphony.
He is a true appreciator of good food and wine.

Synset Examples

he is not an appreciator of our dilemma

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admirer - someone who admires or appreciates something

Etymology

From appreciate + -or, indicating an agent noun; ultimately from Latin 'appretiare' (to value).
