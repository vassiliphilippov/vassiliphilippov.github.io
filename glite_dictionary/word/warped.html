warped

1. ADJ

/ wɔrpt / (AmE), / wɔːpt / (BrE)
(of timbers, boards, or similar materials) bent or twisted out of shape, usually as a result of exposure to moisture or heat.
bent or twisted out of shape (esp. wood)

Examples

The old barn was full of warped wooden planks that no longer fit together tightly.
Her desk became warped after a leak in the ceiling let water drip onto it for weeks.
He tried to fix the warped shelf, but it was too bent out of shape to repair.

Synset Examples

the floors were warped and cracked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twisted - bent or distorted in shape or form
  • Antonym: straight - not bent, curved, or deformed
