accompanyist

1. NOUN

/ əˈkʌmpəniɪst / (AmE), / əˈkʌmpəniɪst / (BrE)
a musician, usually a pianist, who provides musical accompaniment for a performer or group
musician providing accompaniment

Examples

The talented accompanyist played softly as the soloist sang her aria.
She hired an experienced accompanyist for her violin recital.
A skilled accompanyist can greatly enhance a musical performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accompanist - a person who plays musical accompaniment, especially on the piano

Etymology

From accompany + -ist, patterned after 'accompanist'.
