Abuja

1. PROPN

/ əˈbuːdʒə / (AmE), / əˈbuːdʒə / (BrE)
The capital city of Nigeria, located near the center of the country.
capital city of Nigeria

Examples

Many government offices and embassies are located in Abuja.
She moved to Abuja for a new job opportunity.
The conference will be held in Abuja next month.
