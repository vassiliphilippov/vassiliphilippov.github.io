toothpick

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈtuːθˌpɪk / (BrE)
A small, thin stick of wood or plastic used for removing pieces of food from between the teeth.
small stick for cleaning between teeth

Examples

He used a toothpick to remove a bit of food from his teeth after dinner.
The restaurant offers individually wrapped toothpicks at the cash register.
She always keeps a toothpick in her purse just in case.

Etymology

Formed from 'tooth' + 'pick', referring to a pick for the teeth.
