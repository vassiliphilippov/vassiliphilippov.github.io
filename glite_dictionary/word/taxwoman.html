taxwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtæksˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who collects taxes for the government
female tax collector

Examples

The taxwoman visited the shop to review their financial records last week.
Local businesses were relieved when the new taxwoman turned out to be very approachable.
After years as an accountant, she became a respected taxwoman in her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxman - a man who collects taxes for the government
