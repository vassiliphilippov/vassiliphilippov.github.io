candlemaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæn.dl̩ˌmeɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæn.dl̩ˌmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or sell candles
person who makes or sells candles

Examples

The village candlemaker crafted beautiful candles for the entire community.
She apprenticed with a skilled candlemaker to learn the traditional techniques.
Long ago, every town had its own candlemaker to provide light for households.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chandler - a person who makes or sells candles

Etymology

From candle + maker; Middle English 'candil' from Latin 'candēla', and 'maker' from Old English 'macian' (to make).
