lissom

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪsəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪsəm / (BrE)
(especially of a person or their body) thin, supple, and able to move with ease and grace.
gracefully thin and flexible

Examples

The dancer's lissom figure drew everyone's attention as she moved across the stage.
With a lissom stride, the gymnast performed a flawless routine on the balance beam.
She admired the lissom branches that swayed gently in the spring breeze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supple - bending and moving easily and gracefully
  • Synonym: limber - able to move or bend freely and easily
  • Antonym: awkward - lacking grace or ease of movement

Etymology

Alteration of 'lissome', from 'lithe' + '-some'.
