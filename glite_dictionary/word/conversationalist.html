conversationalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən.əlɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən.əlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in holding conversations, especially one who talks in an interesting and engaging way
a person skilled at conversation

Examples

As a conversationalist, she could put anyone at ease in social situations.
He is such a gifted conversationalist that parties are never dull when he’s around.
Despite being a brilliant scientist, he was not much of a conversationalist.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially in a trivial way
  • Degree Higher: orator - a person who gives formal speeches, often very skilled

Etymology

From 'conversation' + '-alist', modelled after 'specialist'.
