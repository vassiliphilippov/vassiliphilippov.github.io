qoph

1. NOUN

/ koʊf / (AmE), / kɒf / (BrE)
the nineteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet, corresponding to 'Q' in the Roman alphabet.
Hebrew alphabet letter

Examples

In the Torah scroll, each qoph is written with a distinctive flourish.
The student practiced writing the letter qoph in Hebrew calligraphy class.
The Hebrew word for 'monkey' begins with the letter qoph.

Etymology

From Hebrew קוֹף (qōph).
