Dante Gabriel Rossetti

1. PROPN

/ ˈdænti ˌɡeɪbriəl rɑːˈsɛti / (AmE), / ˈdænti ˌɡeɪbriəl rɒˈsɛti / (BrE)
(1828–1882) An English poet, painter, and founder of the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood, influential in literature and art.
English poet, painter, Pre-Raphaelite founder

Examples

Dante Gabriel Rossetti was a leading figure in the Pre-Raphaelite movement of the nineteenth century.
Many art historians study the works of Dante Gabriel Rossetti for their symbolic depth and vibrant colors.
Dante Gabriel Rossetti's poems and paintings often explore themes of love and beauty.
