a cappella

1. ADV

/ ˌɑː kəˈpɛlə / (AmE), / ˌɑː kəˈpelə / (BrE)
(of singing) without musical accompaniment; performed by voices alone.
without musical accompaniment

Examples

The choir performed a cappella during the ceremony.
Their latest song is recorded entirely a cappella and showcases their vocal talent.
She enjoys singing a cappella with her friends at gatherings.

Synset Examples

they performed a cappella

Etymology

From Italian 'a cappella' meaning 'in chapel style'.
