schmuck

1. NOUN

/ ʃmʌk / (AmE), / ʃmʌk / (BrE)
a contemptible or foolish person; a jerk
a foolish or obnoxious person
slang

Examples

Don't be such a schmuck—apologize for your mistake.
He acted like a complete schmuck at the dinner party last night.
Sometimes you just have to ignore a schmuck and move on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or who acts foolishly

Etymology

From Yiddish *shmok*, meaning 'penis' (vulgar), used figuratively for a fool or jerk.
