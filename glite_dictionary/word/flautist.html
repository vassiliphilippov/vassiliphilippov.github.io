flautist

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈflɔː.tɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the flute, especially as a professional or skilled musician
person who plays the flute

Examples

The flautist played a beautiful solo during the symphony's second movement.
She trained for years to become a professional flautist in a renowned orchestra.
Many flautists also study other woodwind instruments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flutist - another term for a person who plays the flute

Etymology

From Italian 'flautista', from 'flauto' (flute).
