determinedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈtɜːrmɪndli / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪndli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows firm resolve or determination.
with firm resolve; resolutely

Examples

She determinedly pursued her career despite numerous obstacles.
He determinedly refused to change his opinion, no matter what others said.
The athlete determinedly finished the race, even though he was injured.

Synset Examples

he clung to the past determinedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolutely - in a determined, unwavering manner

Etymology

Formed from 'determined' + '-ly', from Late Latin 'determinare' (to set limits to).

2. ADV

/ dɪˈtɜrmɪndli / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪndli / (BrE)
in a way that shows firmness or resolve; in a determined manner
with firmness or resolve

Examples

She worked determinedly to finish the project before the deadline.
He determinedly ignored all distractions during his study session.
The team determinedly pursued victory despite numerous setbacks.

Synset Examples

she pursued her goals ambitiously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolutely - in a purposeful and unwavering manner
  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant manner; not firm or resolved

Etymology

From 'determined' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
