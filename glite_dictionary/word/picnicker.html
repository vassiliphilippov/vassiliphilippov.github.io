picnicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.nɪ.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪ.kə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a picnic outdoors, usually eating a meal in a scenic place
person participating in a picnic

Examples

The park was crowded with picnickers enjoying the sunny afternoon.
A group of picnickers settled under the old oak tree for their lunch.
Despite a few clouds, the picnickers were cheerful and continued their games on the grass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picnic - an outdoor meal, usually in a scenic place

Etymology

From 'picnic' + '-er', denoting a person involved in an activity.
