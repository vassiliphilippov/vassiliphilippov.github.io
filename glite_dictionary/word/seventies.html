seventies

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvən(t)iz / (AmE), / ˈsɛv(ə)ntiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1970 to 1979
the decade 1970–1979

Examples

Fashion trends of the seventies included bell-bottom jeans and bright colors.
Many classic rock bands rose to fame during the seventies.
Television shows from the seventies are still popular with audiences today.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: eighties - the decade from 1980 to 1989
  • Degree Lower: sixties - the decade from 1960 to 1969

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvənˌtiz / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ən.tiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 70 and 80
ages 70–80 in a person's life

Examples

After celebrating her 70th birthday, Mary enjoyed her seventies by traveling the world.
Many people in their seventies remain very active and involved in their communities.
He finally retired from work when he was well into his seventies.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: sixties - the period of life between ages 60 and 70
  • Degree Higher: eighties - the period of life between ages 80 and 90
