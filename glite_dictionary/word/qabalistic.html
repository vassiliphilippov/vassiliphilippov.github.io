qabalistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkabəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
having a secret, hidden, or mystical meaning; relating to or characteristic of the mystical interpretation associated with the Kabbalah.
having secret or mystical meaning

Examples

Many ancient manuscripts are filled with qabalistic symbols and references.
The magician's teachings often had a qabalistic quality that intrigued his followers.
Her explanation was so qabalistic that hardly anyone could understand its true meaning.

Synset Examples

cabalistic symbols engraved in stone
cryptic writings
thoroughly sibylline in most of his pronouncements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cabalistic - having hidden or mystical meaning; relating to the Kabbalah
  • Synonym: cryptic - having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'cabalisticus' (relating to the Kabbalah), from Hebrew 'qabbālāh' (tradition).
