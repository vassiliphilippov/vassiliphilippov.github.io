Muztag

1. PROPN

/ ˈmʊz.tɑːɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌz.tæɡ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Kunlun Shan range of China near the Indian border, standing 23,891 feet (7,282 meters) high
mountain peak in the Kunlun Shan, China

Examples

Climbers from around the world attempt to summit Muztag each year despite its challenging conditions.
You can see the spectacular snow-covered slopes of Muztag from miles away.
The expedition team documented rare wildlife during their ascent of Muztag.

Etymology

From Turkic languages, where 'muz' means 'ice' and 'tag' means 'mountain'.
