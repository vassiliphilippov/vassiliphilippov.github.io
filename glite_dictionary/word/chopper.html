3. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpə / (BrE)
A large, heavy knife with a broad square blade, used by butchers for chopping meat.
large butcher's knife with square blade

Examples

The butcher sharpened his chopper before cutting through the thick bones.
She bought a new chopper to prepare meat for the stew.
With a heavy chopper, he effortlessly separated the ribs from the carcass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaver - a heavy knife used by butchers for chopping meat

Etymology

From 'chop' + '-er', referring to a tool for chopping.
