limnological

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɪmnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌlɪmnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
of or relating to limnology, the scientific study of inland aquatic systems such as lakes, rivers, and wetlands.
relating to the study of inland waters

Examples

The limnological survey revealed significant changes in the lake's water quality over time.
She published several limnological studies on wetland ecosystems.
The university offers a course focused on limnological methods and field research.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limnology - the scientific study of inland waters

Etymology

From limnology + -ical; limnology from Greek limnē (lake, marsh).
