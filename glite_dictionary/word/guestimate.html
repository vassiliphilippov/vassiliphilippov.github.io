guestimate

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛs.təˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɛs.tɪ.meɪt / (BrE)
An estimate made using both guesswork and reasoning rather than precise calculation.
an estimate made partly by guessing
informal

Examples

Could you give me a guestimate of how much the repairs will cost?
I don't know the exact number, but my guestimate is around fifty people attended.
His guestimate turned out to be surprisingly accurate, considering how little data he had.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: estimate - an approximate calculation or judgment
  • Degree Lower: guess - an estimate made without sufficient information

Etymology

Blend of 'guess' and 'estimate'.
