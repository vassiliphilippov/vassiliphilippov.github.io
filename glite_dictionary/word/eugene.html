Eugene

1. PROPN

/ juˈdʒin / (AmE), / juˈdʒiːn / (BrE)
A city in western Oregon, United States, situated on the Willamette River and home to the University of Oregon.
city in Oregon, USA

Examples

She attended college in Eugene and fell in love with the city's vibrant art scene.
The track and field events in Eugene attract athletes from all over the world.
After moving to Eugene, they quickly adjusted to the Pacific Northwest climate.

Etymology

Named after Eugene Skinner, its founder.
