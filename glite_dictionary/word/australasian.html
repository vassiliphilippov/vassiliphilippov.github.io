Australasian

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔstrəˈleɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌɒstrəˈleɪziən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Australasia or its people, flora, or fauna
of or relating to Australasia

Examples

The Australasian climate is well suited for a wide variety of unique plant species.
Several Australasian birds are not found anywhere else in the world.
The conference welcomed leading experts in Australasian studies from around the globe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Australian - relating to Australia or its people

Etymology

From Australasia (region of Australia, New Zealand, and neighboring islands) + -an (adjectival suffix).
