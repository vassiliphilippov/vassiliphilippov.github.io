Erythrocebus patas

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌrɪθroʊˌsiːbəs ˈpɑːtəs / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪθrəˌsiːbəs ˈpɑːtəs / (BrE)
a reddish, long-tailed monkey native to West Africa, noted for its speed and terrestrial habits
reddish West African monkey

Examples

The Erythrocebus patas is known for its remarkable running speed across the African savanna.
Researchers observed a group of Erythrocebus patas feeding in the grasslands near the river.
The Erythrocebus patas has a distinctive reddish coat and a long tail used for balance.

Etymology

From Greek erythros (red), kebos (monkey); patas is the species name.
