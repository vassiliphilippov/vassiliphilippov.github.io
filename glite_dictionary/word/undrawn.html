undrawn

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdrɔn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɔːn / (BrE)
Not represented or depicted in a drawing; not drawn.
not shown in a drawing

Examples

The architect's plans included several undrawn features that were described in the written notes.
Many details of the ancient city remain undrawn in existing sketches of its ruins.
The illustrator left some elements undrawn to allow for future revisions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: drawn - depicted or shown in a drawing
