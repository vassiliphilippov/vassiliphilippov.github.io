Iowan

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ.oʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əʊ.ən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the U.S. state of Iowa
native or resident of Iowa, US

Examples

As an Iowan, she cherished the rolling fields and small-town charm of her home state.
Many Iowans gather at the state fair each summer to celebrate their traditions.
The senator proudly identified himself as an Iowan during his speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iowa - a state in the Midwestern United States

Etymology

From Iowa + -an (suffix forming demonyms).
