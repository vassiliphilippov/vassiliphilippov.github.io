rededication

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːˌdɛdəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of dedicating something again, especially through a formal or ceremonial act
act of dedicating again

Examples

The rededication of the historic church was attended by hundreds of parishioners.
After months of renovation, the museum held a special rededication ceremony.
Community leaders praised the rededication of the park to public use.

Synset Examples

the rededication of the Temple of Jerusalem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dedication - the act of dedicating something for a particular purpose

Etymology

From re- (again) + dedication.
