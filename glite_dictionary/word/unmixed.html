unmixed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɪkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪkst / (BrE)
not mixed with anything else; pure or without addition of extraneous elements.
not mixed with anything else

Examples

She prefers her coffee unmixed, without any milk or sugar.
The result was an unmixed delight for all those involved in the project.
He ordered a glass of unmixed whiskey at the bar.

Synset Examples

plain water
sheer wine
not an unmixed blessing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pure - not mixed with any other substance or material
  • Antonym: diluted - made thinner or weaker by the addition of another substance

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'mixed' (from Latin 'miscere', to mix).

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmɪkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪkst / (BrE)
(of a substance or quality) not combined with anything else; pure.
not combined with anything else

Examples

For this experiment, we used unmixed chemicals to ensure accuracy.
His motives were unmixed with any personal interest.
She prefers her coffee unmixed with milk or sugar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pure - not mixed or adulterated with any other substance
  • Antonym: mixed - combined with one or more other substances or elements

Etymology

Prefix 'un-' (not) + 'mixed' (from Latin 'miscere', to mix).
