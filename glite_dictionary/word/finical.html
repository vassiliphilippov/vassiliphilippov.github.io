finical

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
Very concerned with small details or with being precise and particular; fussy.
fussy about details

Examples

Her finical nature made planning group outings a challenge for her friends.
The editor's finical attention to detail improved the quality of the manuscript.
He is so finical about his appearance that he spends hours getting ready each morning.

Synset Examples

a finicky eater
fussy about clothes
very particular about how her food was prepared

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finicky - fussy or difficult to please about details
  • Synonym: fussy - very concerned with details and difficult to please
  • Synonym: particular - especially attentive to details and standards

Etymology

From finical, variant of finicking, an alteration of finikin, from fine (delicate, precise).
