Genesis

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
the first book of the Old Testament in the Bible, describing the creation of the world and early history of humanity
first book of the Old Testament

Examples

Many scholars study Genesis to understand the origins of Judeo-Christian beliefs.
The story of creation can be found in Genesis in the Old Testament.
Each week, the pastor read a passage from Genesis during the service.

Etymology

From Greek *Genesis* (birth, origin) via Latin, from *gignesthai* (to be born).
