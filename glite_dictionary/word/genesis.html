genesis

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the origin or mode of formation of something; the process of coming into being
origin or beginning of something

Examples

The genesis of the idea can be traced back to the 19th century.
Many historians have examined the genesis of the conflict in great detail.
Understanding the genesis of the disease could help researchers discover a cure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: origin - the point at which something begins or arises
  • Antonym: conclusion - the end or finish of an event or process

Etymology

From Greek *génesis* (origin, creation), from *gignesthai* (to be born).
