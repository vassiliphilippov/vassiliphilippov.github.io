batrachomyomachia

1. NOUN

/ bəˌtrækoʊˌmaɪəˈmækiə / (AmE), / bəˌtrækəʊˌmʌɪəˈmakɪə / (BrE)
A silly or trivial quarrel, especially one that is blown out of proportion.
silly or trivial quarrel

Examples

The committee's endless debate over punctuation became a classic batrachomyomachia.
Their argument about who should make the coffee was nothing more than a batrachomyomachia.
Let's not turn this minor issue into a batrachomyomachia.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek βατραχομυομαχία (batrachomyomakhía), the title of a comic epic poem meaning 'The Battle of Frogs and Mice'.
