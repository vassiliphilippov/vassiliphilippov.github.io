SIRC

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛs ˈaɪ ˈɑr ˈsi / (AmE), / ˈɛs ˈaɪ ˈɑː ˈsiː / (BrE)
(in Canada) the Security Intelligence Review Committee, a former agency responsible for overseeing the activities of the Canadian Security Intelligence Service.
Canadian security oversight agency (defunct)

Examples

The SIRC played a crucial role in ensuring oversight of intelligence agencies in Canada.
According to the SIRC report, there were several privacy concerns regarding surveillance practices.
Many believe the dissolution of SIRC left a gap in intelligence oversight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: CSIS - Canadian Security Intelligence Service, primary intelligence agency in Canada

Etymology

Acronym from Security Intelligence Review Committee.
