untipped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪpt / (BrE)
not having a special tip or end, especially used to describe cigarettes without a filter or special tip
not having a special tip or end

Examples

He prefers to smoke untipped cigarettes despite the harshness.
The matchsticks in this box are all untipped for safety reasons.
Many older brands offered both untipped and filtered versions of their cigarettes.

Synset Examples

untipped cigarettes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tipped - having a tip or special end, especially a filter on a cigarette

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'tipped' (provided with a tip).
