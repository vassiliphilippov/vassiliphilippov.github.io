Hamlet

1. PROPN

/ ˈhæmlət / (AmE), / ˈhæmlɪt / (BrE)
the prince of Denmark and main character in Shakespeare's tragedy, noted for his indecision and desire to avenge his father's murder
main character in Shakespeare's tragedy

Examples

Many actors consider playing Hamlet to be one of the greatest challenges in theatre.
In Shakespeare's play, Hamlet wrestles with the morality of revenge and justice.
The character of Hamlet has inspired countless interpretations and adaptations over the centuries.

Etymology

From the title of William Shakespeare's play 'Hamlet', itself derived from the Old Norse name Amleth.
