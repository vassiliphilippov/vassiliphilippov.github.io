unembellished

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈbɛl.ɪʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈbɛl.ɪʃt / (BrE)
not decorated with extra details or ornaments; plain and simple in appearance or style.
not decorated or ornamented

Examples

The room was furnished with unembellished wooden chairs and a simple table.
She preferred unembellished writing that got straight to the point.
The monument's unembellished surface gave it a modern and stark appearance.

Synset Examples

a plain hair style
unembellished white walls
functional architecture featuring stark unornamented concrete

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - simple or without decoration
  • Antonym: ornate - elaborately or highly decorated

Etymology

Formed from prefix un- (not) + embellished (from Old French embellir, from Latin bellus 'beautiful').
