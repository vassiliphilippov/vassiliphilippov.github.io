limited

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
including only a part; restricted in extent, amount, or scope.
restricted in amount or scope

Examples

The company offers a limited selection of products this season.
His ability to help was limited by the resources available.
Access to the database is limited to authorized users only.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlimited - without any restriction or limit
  • Synonym: restricted - confined within bounds; limited

Etymology

From Latin 'līmitāre' (to bound, limit), via Old French 'limiter'.
