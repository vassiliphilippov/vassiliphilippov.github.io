round-shouldered

1. ADJ

/ ˈraʊnd ˌʃoʊldɚd / (AmE), / ˈraʊnd ˌʃəʊldəd / (BrE)
Having the shoulders and upper back rounded forward instead of standing erect.
having shoulders and back rounded forward

Examples

The elderly man became increasingly round-shouldered as he aged.
Years of poor posture left her slightly round-shouldered.
The coach advised the round-shouldered player to work on standing straighter.

Synset Examples

a little oldish misshapen stooping woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stooped - having the head and shoulders habitually bent forward
