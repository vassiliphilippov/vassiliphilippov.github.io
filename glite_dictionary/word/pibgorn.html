pibgorn

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪbɡɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɪbɡɔːn / (BrE)
an ancient, now obsolete, single-reed woodwind instrument, usually made from bone
ancient single-reed woodwind instrument
archaic

Examples

The musician skillfully played a tune on the pibgorn during the historical reenactment.
Researchers found remnants of a pibgorn in the archaeological site, shedding light on ancient Welsh music.
Few modern craftsmen can authentically recreate a pibgorn using traditional materials.

Etymology

From Welsh 'pibgorn', meaning 'pipe horn' ('pib' = pipe, 'corn' = horn).
