worshipped

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɜrʃɪpt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpt / (BrE)
Regarded with deep or rapturous love or admiration, often as if for a deity.
regarded with deep love or admiration

Examples

As a child, he worshipped his older brother and wanted to be just like him.
The actress was worshipped by millions of devoted fans worldwide.
He felt worshipped by his students, who hung on his every word.

Synset Examples

adored grandchildren
an idolized wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adored - regarded with deep love or admiration
  • Synonym: idolized - regarded with intense admiration or devotion
  • Antonym: disdained - regarded with contempt or scorn

Etymology

From Middle English 'worshipe', from Old English 'weorðscipe' (worthiness, honor).
