innovativeness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈveɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being original by introducing new ideas or methods
originality; introducing new ideas

Examples

The company's success is driven by its commitment to innovativeness and forward-thinking solutions.
Universities encourage innovativeness among students through research and creative projects.
Her innovativeness was recognized with several awards for groundbreaking work in technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: originality - the ability to think or create in a new and unique way
  • Antonym: conventionality - adherence to established practices or conventional ideas

Etymology

From innovate + -iveness, indicating the quality of being innovative.
