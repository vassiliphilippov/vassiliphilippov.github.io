cross-linguistic

1. ADJ

/ krɒs lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / krɒs lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
Involving or relating to more than one language; comparing or analyzing data from different languages.
involving more than one language

Examples

Research in phonology often relies on cross-linguistic data to draw general conclusions.
The study provides cross-linguistic comparisons of grammatical structures.
They conducted a cross-linguistic investigation across several language families.

Synset Examples

cross-linguistic evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multilingual - speaking or using several languages
  • Antonym: monolingual - relating to or written in only one language

Etymology

From 'cross-' (across, between) and 'linguistic' (relating to language).
