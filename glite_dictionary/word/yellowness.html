yellowness

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛl.oʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being yellow in color; the color resembling that of sunflowers or ripe lemons.
the quality of being yellow in color

Examples

The yellowness of the autumn leaves brightened the whole park.
He was surprised by the intense yellowness of the paint on the walls.
With age, the pages had acquired a distinct yellowness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum

Etymology

Formed from 'yellow' + '-ness', where '-ness' denotes state or quality.
