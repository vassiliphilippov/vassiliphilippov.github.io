Thomas Jefferson

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɑːməs ˈdʒɛfərsən / (AmE), / ˈtɒməs ˈdʒɛfəsən / (BrE)
(1743–1826) The third President of the United States, principal author of the Declaration of Independence, and key figure in the Louisiana Purchase.
3rd President of the United States (1743–1826)

Examples

Thomas Jefferson was instrumental in drafting the Declaration of Independence.
Many historians consider Thomas Jefferson one of the most influential Founding Fathers.
The University of Virginia was founded by Thomas Jefferson in 1819.
