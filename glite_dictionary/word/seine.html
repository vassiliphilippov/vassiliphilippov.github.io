Seine

1. PROPN

/ seɪn / (AmE), / seɪn / (BrE)
a major river in northern France, flowing through Paris and into the English Channel
river in northern France, through Paris

Examples

Many artists have painted scenes along the banks of the Seine.
During our trip to Paris, we took a boat tour on the Seine.
The Seine is famous for its beautiful bridges and historic landmarks.

Etymology

From French 'Seine', from Latin 'Sequana', the ancient name of the river.
