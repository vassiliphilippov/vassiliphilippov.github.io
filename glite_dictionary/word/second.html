second

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
coming after the first in position, order, or rank; next after the first

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first - coming before all others in time or order; 1st
  • Degree Higher: third - coming next after the second; 3rd in a sequence
