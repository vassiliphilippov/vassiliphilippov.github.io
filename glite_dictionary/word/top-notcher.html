top-notcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑp ˌnɑtʃər / (AmE), / ˈtɒp ˌnɒtʃə / (BrE)
a person who excels or ranks among the best in a particular field or activity; a high achiever
a person who ranks at the top or excels

Examples

To be a top-notcher in your class, you need perseverance and focus.
The company only recruits top-notchers from the country's best universities.
Her dedication quickly made her a top-notcher in the competitive chess world.

Synset Examples

Being a top-notcher requires dedication and hard work.
He's known as a top-notcher in the sales team.
She is a top-notcher in her class.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high achiever - a person who achieves more than others, especially academically or professionally
  • Synonym: overachiever - a person who performs better or achieves more success than expected

Etymology

Formed from "top-notch" (of the highest quality) + "-er" (agent noun suffix).
