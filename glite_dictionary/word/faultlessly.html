faultlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔlt.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlt.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is free from faults or errors; perfectly.
in a flawless or perfect manner

Examples

She performed the entire routine faultlessly, earning top marks from the judges.
The text was translated faultlessly into several different languages.
He organized the event faultlessly, and everything went according to plan.

Synset Examples

he solved all the problems faultlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfectly - in a perfect manner, without fault
  • Antonym: imperfectly - in an imperfect or flawed manner

Etymology

From 'faultless' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
