Mithra

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪθrə / (AmE), / ˈmɪθrə / (BrE)
an ancient Persian god associated with light, truth, and the sun
ancient Persian god of light and truth

Examples

Worship of Mithra became widespread in the Roman Empire as part of the mystery religions.
The image of Mithra slaying a bull was central to Mithraic temples.
Historians have studied the influence of Mithra on later religious traditions.

Etymology

From Old Persian 'Mitra', also found in Sanskrit as 'Mitra' (friend, contract).
