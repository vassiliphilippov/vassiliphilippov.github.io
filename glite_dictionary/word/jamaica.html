Jamaica

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈmeɪ.kə / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
an island country in the Caribbean Sea, to the south of Cuba and west of Haiti, known for its diverse culture and history
island country in the Caribbean

Examples

We spent two weeks exploring Jamaica and enjoying its stunning beaches.
Reggae music is one of the most famous cultural exports of Jamaica.
Christopher Columbus was the first European to arrive in Jamaica in 1494.

Etymology

From the indigenous Taíno word 'Xaymaca', meaning 'land of wood and water'.

2. PROPN

/ dʒəˈmeɪ.kə / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪ.kə / (BrE)
An island country in the Caribbean Sea, south of Cuba, known for its rich culture, history, and tourism industry. It became independent from the United Kingdom in 1962.
country in the Caribbean

Examples

Next summer, I'm planning to visit Jamaica for a relaxing beach holiday.
Several famous reggae musicians, including Bob Marley, were born in Jamaica.
The cuisine of Jamaica is known for its bold flavors and unique spices.

Etymology

From the indigenous Taíno word 'Xaymaca', meaning 'land of wood and water'.
