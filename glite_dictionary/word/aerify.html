to aerify

1. VERB

/ ˈɛrəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛərɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to turn (a substance) into a gas or to change into air or a gas
to turn into a gas

Examples

The company developed a machine to aerify liquid chemicals for industrial processes.
When the temperature rises, certain compounds can aerify and escape into the atmosphere.
Scientists are studying new ways to aerify wastewater as part of advanced purification techniques.

Synset Examples

The substance gasified

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to gasify - to convert into gas

Etymology

From Latin 'aer' (air) + '-ify' (to make).
