nonflowering

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) not producing flowers or seeds.
not producing flowers or seeds

Examples

Ferns are a common type of nonflowering plant found in forests.
Mosses and algae are classified as nonflowering plants in biology.
The garden has both flowering and nonflowering species for variety.

Synset Examples

a flowerless plant

Related Terms

  • Antonym: flowering - producing flowers or bloom
  • Synonym: flowerless - lacking flowers or blooms

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + the adjective 'flowering'.
