deodourant

1. NOUN

/ diˈoʊdɚrənt / (AmE), / diˈəʊdərənt / (BrE)
A toiletry product applied to the skin to mask or eliminate unpleasant body odors.
product to mask body odor

Examples

She applies a fresh layer of deodourant every morning before leaving for work.
Running late, he forgot to use his deodourant and was self-conscious all day.
Some people prefer natural deodourants with herbal ingredients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deodorant - A toiletry applied to mask or eliminate body odors

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'deodorant,' from Latin 'de-' (removal) + 'odor' (smell).
