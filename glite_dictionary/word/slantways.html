slantways

1. ADV

/ ˈslæntˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈslɑːntˌweɪz / (BrE)
(adverb) In a direction that is at an angle; not straight or perpendicular, but slanted or diagonal.
at an angle; diagonally

Examples

She placed the picture slantways on the shelf for a more interesting look.
The rain came down slantways, driven by a strong wind.
He glanced slantways at his friend, unsure of what to say.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagonally - in a diagonal direction; at an angle

Etymology

Formed from 'slant' + 'ways', with 'ways' as a directional suffix.
