Fonteyn

1. PROPN

/ fɒnˈteɪn / (AmE), / fɒnˈteɪn / (BrE)
Margot Fonteyn (1919–1991), renowned English ballerina who danced with Rudolf Nureyev.
English ballerina, danced with Nureyev

Margot Fonteyn was celebrated for her partnership with Rudolf Nureyev.
