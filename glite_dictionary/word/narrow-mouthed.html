narrow-mouthed

1. ADJ

/ ˈnæroʊ ˌmaʊθt / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ ˌmaʊθt / (BrE)
Having a mouth that is relatively narrow in width.
having a narrow mouth

Examples

The chemist carefully poured the liquid into the narrow-mouthed flask to avoid spills.
A narrow-mouthed toad can catch tiny insects with its small opening.
We bought a narrow-mouthed jar for storing homemade sauces.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wide-mouthed - having a mouth that is wide in width
