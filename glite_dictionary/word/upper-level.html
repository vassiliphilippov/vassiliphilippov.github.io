upper-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpɚ ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈʌpə ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
of or relating to a higher rank, status, or position within an organization or system.
higher rank or position

Examples

She works closely with upper-level executives to coordinate company strategy.
The upper-level courses are only available to students in their final year.
Due to his experience, he was quickly promoted to an upper-level management position.

Synset Examples

a high-level official
a high-level corporate briefing
upper-level management

Related Terms

  • Synonym: high-level - of great importance or rank
  • Antonym: lower-level - of lesser importance or rank
