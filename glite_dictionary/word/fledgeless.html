fledgeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɛdʒləs / (AmE), / ˈflɛdʒlɪs / (BrE)
(of an arrow) lacking feather flights or fletching.
without feathers; unfledged (arrow)

Examples

The archer realized too late that the fledgeless arrow would not fly straight.
Curious about the effects, he crafted a fledgeless shaft for testing.
A fledgeless arrow lacks the stability provided by traditional fletching.

Synset Examples

shot an unfledged arrow

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fledged - (of an arrow) equipped with feathers
  • Synonym: unfledged - (of an arrow or bird) not having feathers or not fully developed feathers

Etymology

From 'fledge', meaning 'to equip with feathers', plus suffix '-less'.
