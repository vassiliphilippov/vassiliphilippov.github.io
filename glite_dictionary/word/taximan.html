taximan

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksiˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtæksiˌmæn / (BrE)
A person who drives a taxi as a profession.
taxi driver

Examples

The taximan waited patiently outside the airport for his next passenger.
She asked the taximan to take the quickest route downtown.
Many taximen work long shifts to support their families.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxi driver - a person whose job is to drive a taxi

Etymology

Formed from 'taxi' and 'man', patterning after occupational compounds.
