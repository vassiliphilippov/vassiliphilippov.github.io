oration

1. NOUN

/ ɔˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɔːˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal speech, especially one given on a ceremonial occasion
formal ceremonial speech

Examples

The president's oration inspired hope and unity among the citizens.
During the graduation ceremony, she delivered a heartfelt oration.
His powerful oration at the memorial service moved everyone to tears.

Synset Examples

he delivered an oration on the decline of family values

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speech - spoken address delivered to an audience
  • Synonym: lecture - educational talk to an audience
  • Synonym: address - formal speech delivered to an audience

Etymology

From Latin oratio (speech, oration), from orare (to speak, pray).
