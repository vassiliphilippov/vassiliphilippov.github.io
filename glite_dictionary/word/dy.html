Dy

1. NOUN

/ diː waɪ / (AmE), / diː waɪ / (BrE)
the symbol for dysprosium, a trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group that forms highly magnetic compounds
symbol for the element dysprosium

Examples

The magnet contained an alloy of iron, cobalt, and Dy.
Dy is often used in the production of laser materials and nuclear reactors.
Researchers examined the magnetic properties of Dy at high temperatures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dysprosium - a trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group; symbol Dy

Etymology

Abbreviation of dysprosium, from Greek 'dysprositos' (hard to get at).
