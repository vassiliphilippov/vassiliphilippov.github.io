bettor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛtər / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə / (BrE)
a person who places or wagers bets, especially on the outcome of an event
person who places bets

Examples

The experienced bettor knew exactly which horse to back in the race.
Online platforms have made it easier for any bettor to place wagers from home.
After a string of losses, the bettor decided to take a break from gambling.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gambler - a person who risks money in games of chance or betting

Etymology

From bet + -or, modeled after agent noun formations.
