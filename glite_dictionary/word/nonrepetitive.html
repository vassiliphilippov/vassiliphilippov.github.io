nonrepetitive

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌɹɛˈpɛtɪtɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˌrɛˈpɛtɪtɪv / (BrE)
Not involving or characterized by repetition; not repeated or recurring.
not involving repetition

Examples

The artist prefers nonrepetitive patterns to make each piece unique.
Researchers studied the function of nonrepetitive DNA sequences in the genome.
Her choreography was praised for its nonrepetitive and innovative steps.

Synset Examples

nonrepetitive DNA sequence
nonrepetitive dance movements

Related Terms

  • Antonym: repetitive - involving or characterized by repetition

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'repetitive' (involving repetition).
