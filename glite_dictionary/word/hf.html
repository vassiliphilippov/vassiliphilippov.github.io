Hf

1. NOUN

/ eɪtʃ ɛf / (AmE), / eɪtʃ ɛf / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for hafnium, a grey metallic element used in filaments and alloys
chemical symbol for hafnium

Examples

The element Hf is used in control rods for nuclear reactors.
Scientists analyzed the zirconium mineral to extract Hf for further study.
A sample containing Hf was prepared for the electron emission experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hafnium - a grey tetravalent metallic element, chemical element with symbol Hf

Etymology

Chemical symbol formed from the Latin name for Copenhagen, Hafnia, where the element was discovered.
