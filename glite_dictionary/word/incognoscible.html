incognoscible

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.kɑːɡˈnɑː.sɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.kɒɡˈnɒ.sɪ.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to know or understand; incapable of being perceived or comprehended.
impossible to know or understand

Examples

The true nature of the universe may be ultimately incognoscible to the human mind.
Some philosophers argue that certain metaphysical questions are incognoscible.
For centuries, the origins of consciousness seemed completely incognoscible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unknowable - not able to be known or understood
  • Antonym: knowable - able to be known or understood

Etymology

From Latin 'incognoscibilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'cognoscibilis' (knowable).
