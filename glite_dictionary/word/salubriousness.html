salubriousness

1. NOUN

/ səˈluːbriəsnəs / (AmE), / səˈluːbrɪəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being health-giving; conducive to good health and vigor.
the quality of promoting health

Examples

The salubriousness of the mountain air is said to have aided his recovery.
Many admired the salubriousness of the well-maintained parks and their invigorating atmosphere.
The architect designed the building with salubriousness in mind, ensuring plenty of sunlight and fresh air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salubrity - the quality of being healthful or healthy
  • Antonym: unhealthiness - the quality of being unhealthy or harmful

Etymology

From Latin salubris (healthful), via salubrious + -ness.
