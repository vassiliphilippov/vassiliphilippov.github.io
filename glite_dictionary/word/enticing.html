enticing

1. ADJ

/ ɛnˈtaɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
attractive or tempting in a way that arouses interest or desire
attractive; tempting

Examples

The aroma of freshly baked bread was incredibly enticing as we walked past the bakery.
She received an enticing offer to work abroad for a year.
The advertisement made the vacation package look extremely enticing.

Synset Examples

an alluring prospect
her alluring smile
the voice was low and beguiling
difficult to say no to an enticing advertisement
a tempting invitation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tempting - attractive in a way that makes one want to have or do something
  • Synonym: alluring - powerfully and mysteriously attractive

Etymology

From entice + -ing; entice from Old French enticier (to incite, entice).
