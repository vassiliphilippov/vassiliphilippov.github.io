contact

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.tækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
the state of communicating or being in close interaction with someone or something
state of communication or interaction

Examples

Regular contact with clients is essential for building trust.
The researcher lost contact with the rest of the team during the expedition.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the act of exchanging information
  • Antonym: isolation - state of being detached or separated

Etymology

From Latin 'contactus', past participle of 'contingere', meaning 'to touch'.
