Portland

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɔrtlənd / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtlənd / (BrE)
a city in northwestern Oregon, United States, known for its natural scenery, cultural diversity, and location on the Willamette River
city in Oregon, United States
american british

Examples

Portland is famous for its vibrant arts scene and eco-friendly initiatives.
They moved to Portland to enjoy the city's proximity to nature and mountains.
The conference will be held in downtown Portland next month.

Etymology

Named after the Isle of Portland in Dorset, England.
