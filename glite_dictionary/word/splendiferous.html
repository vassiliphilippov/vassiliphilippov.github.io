splendiferous

1. ADJ

/ splɛnˈdɪfərəs / (AmE), / splɛnˈdɪfərəs / (BrE)
extremely impressive or beautiful; splendid
extremely impressive or splendid

Examples

The wedding reception was absolutely splendiferous, with lights and decorations everywhere.
He wore a splendiferous hat to the party, attracting everyone's attention.
We enjoyed a splendiferous view of the mountains from our balcony.

Synset Examples

a glorious spring morning
a glorious sunset
splendid costumes
a kind of splendiferous native simplicity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splendid - magnificent and very impressive
  • Synonym: glorious - having great beauty or splendor

Etymology

Blend of 'splendid' and 'wonderful', modeled humorously on words like 'multiferous'.
