pro rata

1. ADV

/ proʊ ˈreɪtə / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈrɑːtə / (BrE)
In proportion to a calculated rate; in a way that corresponds to each individual's share according to a specific rate or proportion.
in proportion to rate or share

Examples

The interest will be calculated pro rata based on the number of days you borrow the money.
Employees who work less than full time are entitled to holiday pay pro rata.
If you cancel halfway through the month, your bill will be reduced pro rata.

Etymology

From Latin 'pro rata' meaning 'according to the rate'.
