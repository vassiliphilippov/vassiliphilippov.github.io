ever-changing

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛvərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
continuously changing or in a state of constant transformation
constantly changing

Examples

The ever-changing weather in this region makes it difficult to plan outdoor events.
Fashion is an ever-changing industry where new trends appear every season.
The landscape was transformed by the ever-changing flow of the river.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unchanging - not changing; remaining the same
  • Synonym: mutable - liable or subject to change or alteration

Etymology

Formed from 'ever' (always) and 'changing', meaning always undergoing change.
