gripping

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
having the power to arouse and hold the attention; very exciting or absorbing.
exciting and absorbing; holds attention

Examples

The novel was so gripping that I couldn't put it down until I finished.
She gave a gripping account of her adventures in the mountains.
The documentary was gripping from start to finish, holding the audience's attention throughout.

Synset Examples

a fascinating story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absorbing - entertaining and able to keep one's attention fully occupied
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting or engaging; dull

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'grip'.
