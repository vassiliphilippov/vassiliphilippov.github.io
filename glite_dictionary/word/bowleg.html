bowleg

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊˌlɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌlɛɡ / (BrE)
having legs that curve outward at the knees, causing the knees to be far apart when standing with feet together
legs curving outward at the knees

Examples

The child's bowleg was noticeable when he stood with his feet together.
After years of riding horses, he developed a slight bowleg.
Doctors recommended braces to correct her bowleg during early childhood.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: knock-knee - having knees that curve inward and touch, while ankles remain apart

2. NOUN

/ ˈboʊˌlɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌlɛɡ / (BrE)
A leg that curves outward at or below the knee, causing the knees to stay apart when standing with feet together.
outwardly curved leg condition

Examples

The doctor explained that his bowleg was caused by a childhood vitamin deficiency.
Her left bowleg made her gait distinctive but did not slow her down.
Surgery was recommended to correct the severe bowleg that was affecting his mobility.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: knock-knee - a leg deformity in which the knees angle in and touch but the ankles do not
