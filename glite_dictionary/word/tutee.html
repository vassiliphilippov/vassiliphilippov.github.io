tutee

1. NOUN

/ tuˈti / (AmE), / tjuːˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is taught or instructed by a tutor
person taught by a tutor

Examples

Each tutee receives individual attention during the weekly study sessions.
The tutee improved her mathematics skills thanks to her dedicated tutor.
A good tutor adapts their teaching style to suit each tutee's needs.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tutor - a person who teaches or instructs another

Etymology

From 'tutor' + '-ee', a suffix forming nouns denoting the person affected by an action.
