catholic

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæθəlɪk / (AmE), / ˈkæθəlɪk / (BrE)
including a wide variety of things; broad-minded or not limited by narrow interests or prejudices
broad-minded; wide in interests

Examples

She has catholic tastes in music, ranging from opera to hip-hop.
His collection of books is remarkably catholic, reflecting a broad range of interests.
The committee valued her catholic approach to new ideas and perspectives.

Synset Examples

catholic in one's tastes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrow - limited in interests or outlook
  • Synonym: broad-minded - willing to accept many different types of people or ideas

Etymology

From Greek *katholikos* (universal), via Latin *catholicus*.
