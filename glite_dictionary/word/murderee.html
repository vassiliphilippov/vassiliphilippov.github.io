murderee

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɜːrdəˈriː / (AmE), / ˌmɜːdəˈriː / (BrE)
a person who is murdered; a victim of murder
person who is murdered

Examples

The murderee was identified by the detectives after a week-long investigation.
Some legal scholars discuss whether the term murderee should be used officially.
In fiction, the identity of the murderee is often revealed at the story's climax.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: murderer - a person who commits murder

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'murderer' + '-ee', modeled on recipient-forming suffix '-ee'.
