Fortaz

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːrˌtæz / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌtæz / (BrE)
a brand name for a cephalosporin antibiotic (ceftazidime) used to treat moderate bacterial infections, often administered by injection
brand of cephalosporin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Fortaz to treat the patient's severe lung infection.
After receiving Fortaz, her symptoms began to improve within two days.
Some bacteria are resistant to other antibiotics but respond well to Fortaz.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tazicef - another brand name for ceftazidime
  • Synonym: ceftazidime - the generic name for this antibiotic
