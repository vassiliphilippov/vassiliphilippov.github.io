Scartella cristata

1. NOUN

/ skɑrˈtɛl.ə krɪsˈtɑː.tə / (AmE), / skɑːˈtɛl.ə krɪsˈtɑː.tə / (BrE)
a small species of combtooth blenny found along the tropical Atlantic coasts
species of combtooth blenny

Examples

The Scartella cristata is a common sight among the rocky shores of the eastern Atlantic.
Marine biologists observed a school of Scartella cristata near the reef during their survey.
Fishermen often spot Scartella cristata darting between crevices in the intertidal zone.

Etymology

From Latin; 'Scartella' is the genus, and 'cristata' means crested.
