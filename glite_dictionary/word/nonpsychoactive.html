nonpsychoactive

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˌsaɪkoʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / nɒnˌsaɪkəʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) not affecting the mind or mental processes.
not affecting the mind
medical

Examples

CBD is a nonpsychoactive compound found in cannabis plants.
Doctors often prescribe nonpsychoactive medications to avoid unwanted mental side effects.
Unlike THC, these extracts are completely nonpsychoactive and safe for everyday use.

Synset Examples

a nonpsychoactive pain reliever

Related Terms

  • Antonym: psychoactive - (of a drug or substance) affecting the mind or emotions

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' and 'psychoactive' (from Greek psychē 'mind, soul' + Latin activus 'active').
