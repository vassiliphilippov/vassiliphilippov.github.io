Hemerocallidaceae

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛməroʊˌkæliˈdeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˌrəʊkælɪˈdeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants including the genus Hemerocallis (daylilies), sometimes treated as a subfamily within Liliaceae in some classification systems
a plant family including daylilies

Examples

Botanists sometimes classify daylilies under the family Hemerocallidaceae.
Few modern taxonomists accept Hemerocallidaceae as separate from Liliaceae.
The genus Hemerocallis belongs to the Hemerocallidaceae in some taxonomic systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Liliaceae - a large family of flowering plants known as lilies

Etymology

From New Latin, based on genus Hemerocallis + standard plant family suffix -aceae.
