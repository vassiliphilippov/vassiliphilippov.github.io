sandpapery

1. ADJ

/ ˈsændˌpeɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈsændˌpeɪpəri / (BrE)
having a rough, abrasive texture similar to that of sandpaper.
having a rough, abrasive texture

Examples

The skin on his hands was sandpapery from years of hard outdoor work.
Her sandpapery voice made her singing style distinctive and memorable.
The surface of the table felt sandpapery after being stripped of its finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rough - having an uneven or irregular surface

Etymology

Formed from "sandpaper" + "-y" (adjectival suffix).
