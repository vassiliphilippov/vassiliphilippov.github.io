woodworker

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs objects made of wood, particularly as a profession or hobby
person who makes things out of wood

Examples

The woodworker carefully sanded the edges of the cabinet before applying the finish.
As a skilled woodworker, she creates custom tables and chairs from reclaimed timber.
Many hobbyists dream of becoming a full-time woodworker after retiring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpenter - a person who makes and repairs wooden structures and furniture
