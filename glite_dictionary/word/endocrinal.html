endocrinal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛn.doʊˈkraɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈkraɪn(ə)l / (BrE)
Relating to or produced by endocrine glands or their secretions.
of endocrine glands or secretions
medical

Examples

Certain diseases can disrupt the endocrinal balance of the body.
The endocrinal response plays a crucial role in metabolism.
Scientists studied the endocrinal functions of the new hormone.

Synset Examples

endocrine system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endocrine - relating to glands that secrete hormones internally

Etymology

From 'endocrine' + '-al', with 'endocrine' from Greek 'endon' (within) + 'krinein' (to separate).
