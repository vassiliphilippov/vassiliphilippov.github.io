actinomycetal

1. ADJ

/ æk.tɪ.nəˈmaɪ.sɪ.təl / (AmE), / æk.tɪ.nəʊˈmɪ.sɪ.təl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of actinomycetes, a group of filamentous bacteria
relating to actinomycetes bacteria
medical

Examples

The actinomycetal structure of the colony was observed under a microscope.
Researchers isolated several actinomycetal strains from the soil sample.
The antibiotics produced were the result of actinomycetal metabolism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actinomycetous - relating to or characteristic of actinomycetes

Etymology

Formed from 'actinomycete' (from Greek aktis 'ray' + mykēs 'fungus') with the adjectival suffix -al.
