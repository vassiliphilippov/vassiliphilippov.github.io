Aberdeen Angus

1. NOUN

/ ˌæbərˌdin ˈæŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˌæbədˌiːn ˈæŋɡəs / (BrE)
a black hornless breed of beef cattle from Scotland, known for its high-quality meat
a Scottish breed of hornless black cattle

Examples

The farmer raises Aberdeen Angus for their prized beef.
A herd of Aberdeen Angus grazed peacefully on the hillside.
Restaurants often advertise steaks from Aberdeen Angus cattle due to their quality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Angus - a breed of cattle originating from Scotland, often black and hornless

Etymology

Named after Aberdeenshire and Angus, regions in Scotland where the breed originated.
