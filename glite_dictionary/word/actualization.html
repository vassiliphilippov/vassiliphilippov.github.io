actualization

1. NOUN

/ ˌæk.tʃu.ə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæktʃu.əlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something real or giving the appearance of reality
making something real; realization

Examples

The actualization of her dream took many years and much effort.
Virtual reality technology aims at the actualization of immersive digital experiences.
Personal growth theories often focus on the actualization of an individual's potential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: realization - the act of making real or coming into existence

Etymology

From actualize + -ation; 'actualize' from Late Latin actualis (active).
