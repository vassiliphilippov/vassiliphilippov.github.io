costumed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑː.stumd / (AmE), / ˈkɒs.tjuːmd / (BrE)
wearing clothing typical of a specific period, country, or class, or dressed in a costume for an event or performance
wearing characteristic or period clothing

Examples

The actors were costumed in elaborate Renaissance attire for the performance.
She arrived at the party costumed as a 1920s flapper, complete with a feathered headband.
A group of costumed children paraded down the street on Halloween night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dressed - wearing clothes of a particular type or in a certain way
  • Synonym: disguised - wearing clothes to conceal identity
  • Antonym: undressed - not wearing clothing

Etymology

Formed from 'costume' + '-ed' suffix.
