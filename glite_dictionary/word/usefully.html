usefully

1. ADV

/ ˈjusfəli / (AmE), / ˈjuːsfəli / (BrE)
In a useful or advantageous manner; in such a way as to achieve a desired purpose.
in a useful manner

Examples

The funds were usefully invested in community projects.
Her time was usefully spent learning new skills during the holidays.
Extra storage space can always be usefully utilized in a small apartment.

Synset Examples

extra money could be usefully spent on this project

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpfully - in a helpful manner
  • Antonym: uselessly - in a useless or ineffective manner

Etymology

From 'useful' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
