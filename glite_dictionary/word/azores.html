Azores

1. PROPN

/ əˈzɔɹz / (AmE), / əˈzɔːz / (BrE)
A group of volcanic islands in the North Atlantic Ocean, belonging to Portugal.
Portuguese islands in the North Atlantic Ocean

Examples

We planned our summer vacation in the Azores to enjoy whale watching and hiking.
The Azores are known for their stunning volcanic landscapes and mild climate.
Due to its location, the Azores serve as an important stopover for transatlantic flights.

Synset Examples

the Azores are strategically located on transatlantic air and shipping routes

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Açores', possibly from 'açor' meaning 'goshawk', as early explorers thought the birds they saw were goshawks.

islands in the North Atlantic Ocean (Portugal)

Examples

The Azores are known for their lush landscapes and volcanic scenery.
Direct flights to the Azores have made the islands more accessible for tourists.

Synset Examples

the Azores are strategically located on transatlantic air and shipping routes

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Açores', meaning 'goshawks', named by early explorers who believed the birds were common on the islands.
