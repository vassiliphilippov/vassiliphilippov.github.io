ib.

1. ADV

/ ɪb / (AmE), / ɪb / (BrE)
(in citations) in the same place; used to refer to the same source as previously cited.
in the same place (citation)

Examples

The same author is cited again as ib. in the next footnote.
Refer to Smith (1999), p. 42; ib., p. 45 for further discussion.
The article lists three sources, with the second and third referenced as ib. in the bibliography.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ibid. - used to refer to the same source as the previous citation (bibliographic term)

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'ibidem' meaning 'in the same place'.
