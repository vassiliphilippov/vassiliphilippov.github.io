freethinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌθɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌθɪŋkə / (BrE)
A person who forms opinions about religion or other subjects based on reason and evidence, rather than accepting traditional beliefs or authority.
person who questions religious orthodoxy

Examples

As a proud freethinker, she encouraged open discussion and independent thought.
The club was founded by freethinkers who disagreed with established religious doctrines.
Many freethinkers have contributed to debates about morality and science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agnostic - a person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God
  • Synonym: skeptic - a person inclined to question or doubt accepted opinions
  • Antonym: believer - a person who accepts religious faith or doctrine

Etymology

From free + thinker, first recorded in the late 17th century.
