puddingheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʊd.ɪŋˌhɛd.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈpʊd.ɪŋˌhɛd.ɪd / (BrE)
Stupid and confused; lacking clarity of thought.
stupid and confused
archaic

Examples

She dismissed his puddingheaded ideas with a roll of her eyes.
Only a puddingheaded fool would forget their passport before an international flight.
The novel features a puddingheaded character whose absentmindedness causes endless trouble.

Synset Examples

blathering like the addlepated nincompoop that you are
a confused puddingheaded, muddleheaded fellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muddleheaded - confused and unable to think clearly
  • Synonym: addlepated - stupid or confused, scatterbrained

Etymology

From 'pudding' + 'headed', likening a person's head or mind to pudding (something soft or lacking substance).
