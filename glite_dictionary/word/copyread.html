to copyread

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːp.iˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɒp.iˌriːd / (BrE)
to edit and correct written or printed material, especially as part of preparing it for publication
edit and correct written material

Examples

She was hired to copyread the articles before they went to print.
After you copyread this manuscript, please send it to the layout team.
He spent the afternoon copyreading the company newsletter for errors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to proofread - to read and mark corrections in printed proofs

Etymology

From 'copy' (manuscript to be printed) + 'read' (examine for errors); modelled after 'proofread'.
