earlyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜːrliɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɜːliɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat early; not exactly early, but earlier than average or expected.
somewhat early

Examples

Let's meet at an earlyish time tomorrow to avoid the crowds.
He arrived earlyish compared to the rest of the group.
We had dinner at an earlyish hour before going to the theater.

Synset Examples

at an earlyish hour

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: early - happening before the expected time
  • Antonym: late - happening after the expected time

Etymology

Formed by adding the suffix -ish (meaning 'somewhat') to the adjective 'early'.
