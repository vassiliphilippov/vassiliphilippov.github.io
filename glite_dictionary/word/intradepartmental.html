intradepartmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrədəˌpɑrtˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrədəˌpɑːtˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
existing or occurring within a single department of an organization, institution, or company
within a department

Examples

All intradepartmental emails must follow the new communication guidelines.
There will be an intradepartmental meeting tomorrow to discuss project updates.
The committee is responsible for reviewing intradepartmental policies.

Synset Examples

intradepartmental memos

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interdepartmental - involving two or more departments

Etymology

From prefix 'intra-' (within) + 'departmental' (relating to a department).
