gold-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ɡoʊld ˈkʌl ərd / (AmE), / ɡəʊld ˈkʌl əd / (BrE)
having the color of gold; resembling gold in color
having a gold-like color

Examples

The artist used a gold-coloured paint to highlight the frame.
She wore a beautiful gold-coloured dress to the party.
A gold-coloured ribbon was tied around the gift box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden - having a color or appearance of gold
