ill-gotten

1. ADJ

/ ɪl ˈɡɑtən / (AmE), / ɪl ˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
(of money, goods, etc.) obtained by illegal or dishonest means.
obtained illegally or dishonestly

Examples

The authorities seized his ill-gotten gains after a lengthy investigation.
She felt guilty about her ill-gotten wealth and tried to make amends.
Many laws are designed to prevent criminals from enjoying their ill-gotten assets.

Synset Examples

dirty money
ill-gotten gains

Related Terms

  • Antonym: legitimate - obtained by lawful or proper means
