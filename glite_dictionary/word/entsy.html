Entsy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntsi / (AmE), / ˈɛntsi / (BrE)
the Uralic language spoken by the Enets people of Siberia.
Uralic language of the Enets in Siberia

Examples

The Entsy language is considered endangered, with only a few fluent speakers remaining.
Linguists have been working to document the grammar and vocabulary of Entsy.
Historically, the Entsy language was spoken along the Yenisei River in Siberia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Enets - the indigenous people of Siberia who speak Entsy

Etymology

From Russian Энцы (Entsy), referring to the Enets people.
