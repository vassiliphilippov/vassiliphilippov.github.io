uncordial

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɔrdiəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːdiəl / (BrE)
not warm or friendly; lacking cordiality or goodwill
not warm or friendly

Examples

Her response to the invitation was uncordial, leaving everyone slightly offended.
The manager's uncordial manner discouraged open communication in the office.
Despite our efforts to be polite, his uncordial behavior made the meeting uncomfortable.

Synset Examples

looked uncordial as we approached

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cordial - warm and friendly
  • Synonym: unfriendly - not friendly; antagonistic

Etymology

Formed from un- (not) + cordial (from Latin *cordialis*, 'of the heart')
