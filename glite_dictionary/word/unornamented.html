unornamented

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈɔrnəˌmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɔːnəməntɪd / (BrE)
not decorated or embellished; plain or lacking ornamentation.
not decorated; plain

Examples

The living room was intentionally left unornamented to create a sense of minimalist calm.
She preferred unornamented prose that got straight to the point.
The building’s facade is strikingly unornamented, emphasizing clean lines and open space.

Synset Examples

a plain hair style
unembellished white walls
functional architecture featuring stark unornamented concrete

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - simple in appearance, without decoration
  • Antonym: decorated - adorned or embellished

Etymology

From un- (not) + ornamented (decorated), based on Latin 'ornamentum' (equipment, adornment).
