Geum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiːəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəm / (BrE)
A genus of herbaceous perennial plants in the rose family (Rosaceae), commonly known as avens, native to temperate regions.
genus of herbaceous plants (avens)

Examples

Several species of Geum are popular in ornamental gardening for their bright flowers.
Researchers studied the hybridization of native and non-native Geum in the field.
The Geum thrives in well-drained soil and partial sunlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avens - Common name for plants of the genus Geum

Etymology

From Latin 'Geum', from Greek 'geuon', a plant name found in Dioscorides.
