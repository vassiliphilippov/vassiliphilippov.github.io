pointer

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
an indicator or needle on a dial or measuring instrument that shows a reading or position
indicator needle on a dial or gauge

Examples

The red pointer on the speedometer shows how fast you are driving.
He watched the pointer slowly move to the 'full' mark on the gas gauge.
If the pointer falls below the line, the machine needs to be serviced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needle - the thin, pointed indicator on a dial or gauge
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03981590-n
Synset POS: n

2. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
a breed of strong, slender, short-haired hunting dog, originally from Spain, with a white coat and brown or black patches, known for scenting out and indicating game.
a breed of hunting dog

Examples

The pointer stood motionless, signaling the location of the hidden pheasant.
Many hunters choose a pointer for its keen sense of smell and athletic build.
Her family has bred pointers for generations, specializing in dogs with strong hunting instincts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retriever - a breed of dog that retrieves game for hunters
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02102649-n
Synset POS: n

3. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
a mark, arrow, or object used to indicate a direction or relation, especially on instruments or diagrams
mark or object indicating direction

Examples

The red pointer on the gauge shows the current temperature.
Follow the pointer on the map to find your destination.
The pointer moved slowly around the dial as the machine warmed up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator - a thing that indicates the state or level of something

Etymology

From Middle English 'pointen' (to prick, indicate) + '-er'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-06830979-n
Synset POS: n

4. NOUN

/ ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
(computing) A movable symbol, often an arrow or other icon, on a computer display screen that is controlled by a pointing device such as a mouse and is used to indicate or select items on the screen.
movable indicator on a computer screen

Examples

She moved the pointer across the screen to click on the icon.
When the pointer changes shape, it indicates a different mode or option.
To select the text, drag the pointer over the words you want to highlight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cursor - a movable indicator on a computer screen showing where the next action will occur
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-03155461-n
Synset POS: n
