admiringly

1. ADV

/ ədˈmaɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ədˈmaɪərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows admiration or respect
in an admiring manner

Examples

She spoke admiringly of her mentor's achievements.
The crowd watched admiringly as the gymnast performed a perfect routine.
He gazed admiringly at the painting hanging on the gallery wall.

Synset Examples

he looked at his wife admiringly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admiring - showing admiration
