splayfooted

1. ADJ

/ ˈspleɪˌfʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈspleɪˌfʊtɪd / (BrE)
Having feet that turn outward.
having feet that turn outward

Examples

The splayfooted boy struggled to run as quickly as his classmates.
Some dancers work to correct splayfooted posture during training.
A splayfooted stance can cause issues with walking and balance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pigeon-toed - having feet that turn inward

Etymology

From 'splay' (spread or turn outward) + 'footed'.
