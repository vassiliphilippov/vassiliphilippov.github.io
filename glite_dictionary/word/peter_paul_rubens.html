Peter Paul Rubens

1. PROPN

/ ˌpiːt̬ɚ ˌpɔːl ˈruːbənz / (AmE), / ˌpiːtə ˌpɔːl ˈruːbənz / (BrE)
(1577–1640) A prolific Flemish Baroque painter known for his vibrant, dynamic compositions and influential role in European art.
Flemish Baroque painter (1577–1640)

Examples

The dramatic style of Peter Paul Rubens influenced countless artists across Europe.
