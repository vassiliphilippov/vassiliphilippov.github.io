fryolator

1. NOUN

/ ˈfraɪ.əˌleɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfraɪ.əˌleɪ.tə / (BrE)
A kitchen appliance used for deep frying food, typically found in commercial kitchens.
commercial deep-frying appliance

Examples

The restaurant just installed a new fryolator to handle the lunch rush.
After cleaning the fryolator, John refilled it with fresh oil for the next batch of fries.
Many commercial kitchens rely on a fryolator to prepare crispy fried chicken quickly.

Synset Examples

Deep fryers are used for cooking many fast foods, and making them crisp.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep fryer - an appliance for deep frying food

Etymology

From Fryolator, a trademarked brand name for a commercial deep fryer.
