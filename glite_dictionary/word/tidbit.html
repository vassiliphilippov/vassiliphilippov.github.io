tidbit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪdˌbɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪd.bɪt / (BrE)
a small and particularly tasty piece of food
small, tasty piece of food

Examples

She offered me a delicious tidbit from the tray.
He savored every tidbit at the party buffet.
Guests enjoyed picking up a tidbit between conversations.

Etymology

From English 'titbit', later influenced by 'tid' (a small amount) and 'bit'.
