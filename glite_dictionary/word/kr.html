KR

1. NOUN

/ keɪ ɑːr / (AmE), / keɪ ɑː / (BrE)
abbreviation for Khmer Rouge, a communist organization that ruled Cambodia from 1975 to 1979 and was responsible for mass atrocities.
abbreviation for Khmer Rouge

Examples

The brutal policies of the KR led to the deaths of millions of Cambodians.
Many families fled the country to escape the KR regime in the 1970s.
The legacy of the KR still affects Cambodian society today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Khmer Rouge - the communist party and regime that ruled Cambodia in the late 1970s
