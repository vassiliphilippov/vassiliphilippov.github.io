hatbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈhætˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈhætˌbɒks / (BrE)
a stiff, round container used for storing or carrying hats, often with a carrying handle
round case for carrying hats

Examples

She stored her grandmother's delicate bonnet in a vintage hatbox.
The hatbox was lined with soft velvet to protect the wide-brimmed hat.
Travelers in the past often carried a hatbox along with their luggage.
