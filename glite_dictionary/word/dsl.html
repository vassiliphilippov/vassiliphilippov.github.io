DSL

1. NOUN

/ di ɛs ɛl / (AmE), / diː ɛs ɛl / (BrE)
a technology for providing high-speed internet access over standard telephone lines, used especially in homes and small businesses
high-speed internet via telephone lines

Examples

We upgraded to DSL and our internet speeds improved dramatically.
Many rural areas still do not have access to DSL connections.
The company offers DSL as one of its primary internet services.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadband - high-speed internet access that is always on and faster than traditional dial-up
  • Antonym: dial-up - internet access via analog telephone lines, slower than broadband or DSL

Etymology

Abbreviation of Digital Subscriber Line.
