nuptials

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌp.tʃəlz / (AmE), / ˈnʌp.tʃəlz / (BrE)
a wedding ceremony; the formal event where a marriage is celebrated
wedding ceremony

Examples

The couple invited two hundred guests to their nuptials at the cathedral.
After the beautiful nuptials, the newlyweds departed for their honeymoon.
Friends and family gathered from afar to witness the nuptials of the young couple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony, especially one including the associated celebrations
  • Antonym: divorce - the legal dissolution of a marriage

Etymology

From Latin 'nuptialis', from 'nuptiae' (wedding, marriage).
