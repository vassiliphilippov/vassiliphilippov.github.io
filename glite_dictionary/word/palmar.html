palmar

1. ADJ

/ ˈpælmɑr / (AmE), / ˈpælmɑː / (BrE)
relating to the palm of the hand or the sole of the foot, especially in anatomical contexts.
of the palm of the hand or sole of the foot
medical

Examples

The surgeon made an incision along the palmar surface of the wrist.
Certain muscles in the hand are referred to as palmar muscles.
Numbness in the palmar region may indicate nerve compression.

Synset Examples

the volar surface
the palmar muscle

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dorsal - relating to the back of the hand or top of the foot
  • Synonym: volar - relating to the palm or sole; same as 'palmar'

Etymology

From Latin 'palmaris', from 'palma' (palm of the hand).
