highflyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪˌflaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌflaɪə / (BrE)
a person who is very successful, especially someone with a lot of ability and ambition
very successful, ambitious person

Examples

As a highflyer in the finance world, she was promoted to director before the age of thirty.
The company is known for recruiting young highflyers from top universities.
Although many expected him to be a highflyer, he preferred a more balanced approach to his career.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achiever - a person who achieves great success
  • Antonym: underachiever - a person who fails to achieve their potential or expected level

Etymology

From 'high' + 'flyer', metaphorically referring to someone soaring to great heights.
