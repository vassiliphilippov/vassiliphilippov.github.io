strengthened

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛŋθənd / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθənd / (BrE)
made stronger or given extra support; reinforced.
made stronger or reinforced

Examples

The building's structure was strengthened to withstand earthquakes after the recent tremor.
He felt more strengthened by the support of his friends during difficult times.
The bridge was strengthened with steel beams to ensure its safety.

Synset Examples

reinforced concrete contains steel bars or metal netting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reinforced - given additional strength or support, especially by adding extra material
  • Antonym: weakened - made weaker or less strong
