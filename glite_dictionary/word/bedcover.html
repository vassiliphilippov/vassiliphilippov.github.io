bedcover

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdˌkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌkʌvə / (BrE)
A decorative cover placed over a bed, often for protection or ornament.
decorative covering for a bed

Examples

She chose a beautiful floral bedcover to brighten up her bedroom.
The white bedcover was freshly laundered and neatly spread.
He folded the bedcover at the foot of the bed every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedspread - a decorative spread for a bed, usually reaching to the floor
  • Degree Lower: blanket - a large piece of soft material used to keep warm in bed
  • Degree Higher: comforter - a thick, warm cover for a bed, often filled with down or synthetic fiber
