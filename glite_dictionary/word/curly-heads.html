curly-heads

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜrliˌhɛdz / (AmE), / ˈkɜːliˌhɛdz / (BrE)
A shrubby clematis of the eastern United States, noted for its curly foliage.
a clematis plant with curly leaves

Examples

The curly-heads in her garden bloomed late this year, their leaves twisting into elegant shapes.
He identified the plant as curly-heads by its characteristic curly foliage and shrubby appearance.
Botanists conducted a study on the distribution of curly-heads across the eastern United States.
