academia

1. NOUN

/ ˌækəˈdiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌækəˈdiːmiə / (BrE)
the world of academic study, research, and universities; the academic community
the academic world or community

Examples

After working in industry for several years, she decided to return to academia.
Many debates in academia focus on the value of interdisciplinary research.
He dedicated his entire career to academia and never left the university environment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academy - an institution of higher learning or specialized study

Etymology

From Latin 'academia', from Ancient Greek ἀκαδημία (akadēmía), the name of Plato's school.
