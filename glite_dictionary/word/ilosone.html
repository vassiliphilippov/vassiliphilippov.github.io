Ilosone

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪloʊˌsoʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪləsəʊn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for erythromycin, an antibiotic used to treat infections caused by bacteria.
brand name for erythromycin antibiotic
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Ilosone to treat her respiratory infection.
Some patients may experience stomach upset when taking Ilosone.
Ilosone is effective against many Gram-positive bacteria.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erythromycin - an antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections
