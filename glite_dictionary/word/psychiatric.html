psychiatric

1. ADJ

/ saɪˈkjaɪətrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkɪˈatrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study, diagnosis, or treatment of mental illness and disorders.
relating to psychiatry or mental illness
medical

Examples

After months of struggling, she finally sought psychiatric help for her depression.
He spent several weeks in a psychiatric hospital for treatment.
The patient was diagnosed with a rare psychiatric disorder.

Synset Examples

psychiatric disorder
psychiatric hospital

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychological - relating to the mind or mental processes
  • Degree Lower: medical - relating to medicine and health care

Etymology

From psychiatry + -ic, psychiatric first used in the mid-19th century.
