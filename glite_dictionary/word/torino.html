Torino

1. PROPN

/ toˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / tɒˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
The Italian name for Turin, the capital city of the Piemonte (Piedmont) region in northwestern Italy.
Italian name for Turin, Italy

Examples

The Winter Olympics were held in Torino in 2006.
She traveled to Torino to study Italian art and architecture.
Juventus, one of Italy's top football clubs, is based in Torino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turin - English name for the same city in Italy

Etymology

Italian form of Latin Augusta Taurinorum, referring to the ancient Taurini people.
