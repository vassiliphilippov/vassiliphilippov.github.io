stippled

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪpəld / (AmE), / ˈstɪpəld / (BrE)
having a pattern or texture consisting of small dots, especially as a result of a painting or drawing technique
having a pattern of dots

Examples

The artist created a stippled effect by tapping the brush lightly on the canvas.
Some reptiles have stippled skin, making them difficult to spot in their environment.
The stippled texture on the pottery added an interesting visual dimension to the piece.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having a pattern of spots

Etymology

From 'stipple', derived from Dutch 'stippelen' meaning 'to dot'.
