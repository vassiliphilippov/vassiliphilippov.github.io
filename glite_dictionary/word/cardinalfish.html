cardinalfish

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːrdɪnəlˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪn(ə)lˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A small, usually red tropical fish found in coral reefs and inshore waters.
small tropical reef fish

Examples

The brightly colored cardinalfish darted among the coral branches near the shore.
Many marine aquariums feature the vibrant cardinalfish for their striking appearance.
We spotted a school of cardinalfish while snorkeling in the shallow reef.
