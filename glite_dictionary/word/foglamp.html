foglamp

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑɡˌlæmp / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡˌlamp / (BrE)
a powerful light on a vehicle designed to improve visibility in foggy conditions
vehicle light for use in fog

Examples

The car's foglamp made it much easier to see through the thick fog.
He turned on the foglamp as visibility dropped on the highway.
After replacing the broken foglamp, the driver felt safer during the early morning mist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headlight - a light at the front of a vehicle

Etymology

Formed from 'fog' and 'lamp', referring to a lamp for use in foggy weather.
