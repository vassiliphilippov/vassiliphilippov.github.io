scrumptious

1. ADJ

/ ˈskrʌmpʃəs / (AmE), / ˈskrʌmpʃəs / (BrE)
(especially of food) extremely pleasing to the sense of taste; delicious.
extremely tasty or delicious (food)

Examples

The chef prepared a scrumptious meal that everyone enjoyed.
These scrumptious cookies were gone in minutes.
She couldn't resist taking another bite of the scrumptious chocolate cake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delicious - having a very pleasant taste or smell
  • Synonym: tasty - having a strong and pleasant flavor
  • Antonym: unpalatable - not pleasant to taste

Etymology

Perhaps alteration of 'sumptuous' or blend of 'scrumptious' (slang for 'nice') and 'sumptuous'.
