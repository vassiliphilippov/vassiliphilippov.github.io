Tammy Wynette

1. PROPN

/ ˈtæmi waɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˈtæmi waɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
(1942–1998) An American country singer known for hits like "Stand by Your Man."
famous American country singer

Examples

Tammy Wynette became known as the "First Lady of Country Music" for her influential songs.
