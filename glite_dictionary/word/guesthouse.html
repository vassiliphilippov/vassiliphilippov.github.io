guesthouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɛstˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡɛstˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small building or house separate from the main home, used to accommodate guests.
house for housing guests

Examples

They built a guesthouse in the backyard for visiting relatives.
The guesthouse is equipped with a small kitchen and bathroom.
Many travelers prefer staying in a local guesthouse for a more personal experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guest room - a room for guests in a house
