mom

1. NOUN

/ mɑm / (AmE), / mɒm / (BrE)
(informal) a person's mother.
informal term for mother
informal american

Examples

I called my mom to tell her the good news.
His mom makes the best chocolate chip cookies.
When I was sick, my mom took care of me all day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mum - informal term for mother (primarily British)
  • Synonym: mother - female parent
