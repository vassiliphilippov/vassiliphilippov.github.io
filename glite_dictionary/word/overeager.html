overeager

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈiɡər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈiːɡə / (BrE)
Excessively eager or enthusiastic, often to the point of being imprudent or impatient.
excessively eager or enthusiastic

Examples

He became overeager during the interview and interrupted the interviewer several times.
Her overeager attempts to help only made the situation more chaotic.
The overeager fans crowded the stage before the concert had even started.

Synset Examples

overeager in his pursuit of the girl

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eager - keen or enthusiastic
  • Synonym: impatient - unable to wait calmly or eagerly desiring something to happen

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' meaning 'excessively' + 'eager'.
