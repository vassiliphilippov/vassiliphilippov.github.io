bugology

1. NOUN

/ bʌˈɡɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / bʌˈɡɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the informal study or scientific study of insects; another term for entomology
the study of insects (informal)
informal

Examples

Her fascination with insects led her to study bugology in college.
Some people joke that anyone who collects beetles has a degree in bugology.
The children's book turned bugology into an exciting adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entomology - the scientific study of insects

Etymology

Formed from 'bug' (insect) + '-ology' (study of); modeled after similar scientific terms.
