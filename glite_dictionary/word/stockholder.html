stockholder

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑkˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who owns shares of stock in a corporation
owner of shares in a corporation

Examples

Every stockholder was invited to the annual general meeting of the corporation.
As a stockholder, she is entitled to receive quarterly dividends.
The stockholder voted in favor of the merger proposal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shareholder - a person who owns shares in a company, synonym for stockholder

Etymology

From 'stock' (ownership shares) + 'holder' (one who possesses or holds).
