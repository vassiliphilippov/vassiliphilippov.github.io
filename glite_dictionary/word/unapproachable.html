unapproachable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈproʊ.tʃə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈprəʊ.tʃə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to reach or access; located or situated so as not to be easily approached
impossible or very hard to reach

Examples

The cabin was so remote it became almost unapproachable in winter.
A waterfall cascaded into an unapproachable gorge below.
The summit remained unapproachable until better climbing equipment was invented.

Synset Examples

an unapproachable chalet high in the mountains
an unreachable canyon
the unreachable stars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inaccessible - impossible or very difficult to reach or enter
  • Antonym: reachable - able to be reached or accessed

Etymology

From un- (not) + approachable (able to be approached).

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈproʊ.tʃə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈprəʊ.tʃə.bəl / (BrE)
not friendly or easy to talk to; appearing distant or reserved and discouraging others from approaching
not friendly or difficult to approach

Examples

Despite his great achievements, the professor remained surprisingly unapproachable to his students.
The new manager seems a bit unapproachable and rarely interacts with the team.
Many found her unapproachable because of her serious expression and quiet demeanor.

Synset Examples

an unapproachable executive

Related Terms

  • Antonym: approachable - friendly and easy to talk to
  • Synonym: distant - not friendly, emotionally detached

Etymology

From un- (not) + approachable (from approach + -able).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00135472-a
Synset POS: a
