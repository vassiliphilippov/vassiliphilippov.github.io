Alicante Bouschet

1. NOUN

/ əˌliːˈkæn.teɪ buːˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌæl.ɪˈkæn.teɪ buːˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
A type of red wine made from Alicante Bouschet grapes, known for its deep color and robust flavor.
red wine from Alicante Bouschet grapes

Examples

The sommelier recommended an Alicante Bouschet to pair with our steak dinner.
This vineyard is famous for producing high-quality Alicante Bouschet wines.
We enjoyed a glass of rich Alicante Bouschet on the terrace overlooking the fields.

Etymology

From French, combining the place name 'Alicante' and the surname 'Bouschet', the grape's breeders.
