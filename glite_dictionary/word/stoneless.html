stoneless

1. ADJ

/ ˈstoʊn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈstəʊn.ləs / (BrE)
(of fruit) having had the stone or pit removed.
without a stone or pit (fruit)

Examples

She bought a bag of stoneless cherries for the pie.
For easier eating, choose stoneless olives in your salads.
The recipe calls for stoneless dates to make the process faster.

Synset Examples

stoneless dried dates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitted - (of fruit) having had the pit or stone removed
