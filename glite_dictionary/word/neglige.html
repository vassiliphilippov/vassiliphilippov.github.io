neglige

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɛl.ɪˈʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɛl.ɪʒeɪ / (BrE)
A woman's loose, often sheer dressing gown, usually worn at home.
woman's loose dressing gown

Examples

She wore a silk neglige while reading in the sunroom.
The new boutique featured a luxurious pink neglige in its window display.
After her bath, she slipped into a comfortable neglige before dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negligee - a woman's loose dressing gown, especially a light, sheer one

Etymology

From French 'négligé', meaning 'careless' or 'neglected', referring to the informal style of the garment.
