plum-yew

1. NOUN

/ plʌm ju / (AmE), / plʌm juː / (BrE)
Any of several evergreen shrubs or small trees of eastern Asia with large seeds surrounded by a fleshy covering, resembling yew and often cultivated as ornamentals.
evergreen Asian shrub or tree resembling a yew

Examples

The plum-yew is prized for its attractive foliage in shaded gardens.
Gardeners often choose the plum-yew because it adapts well to different soils.
The seeds of plum-yew are encased in a soft, fleshy covering.
