approved

1. ADJ

/ əˈpruvd / (AmE), / əˈpruːvd / (BrE)
officially accepted or authorized by a person or body with authority
officially accepted or authorized

Examples

The company will only use approved suppliers for its materials.
She followed the approved safety procedures while working in the lab.
Only approved vaccines are distributed to the healthcare centers.

Synset Examples

a list of approved candidates

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rejected - not accepted or refused approval

Etymology

From the verb 'approve', from Old French 'aprover', from Latin 'approbare' (to assent to, to confirm).
