Spassky

1. PROPN

/ ˈspɑːski / (AmE), / ˈspaski / (BrE)
Boris Spassky (born 1937), Russian chess master and world champion from 1969 to 1972, known for his match against Bobby Fischer.
Russian chess grandmaster; world champion (1969–1972)

Examples

The legendary match between Bobby Fischer and Spassky captivated the chess world in 1972.
As a child, she was inspired by Spassky's brilliant endgames.
Spassky remains a respected figure in international chess circles.
