riding

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of sitting on a horse and controlling its movements.
sport or activity of controlling a horse

Examples

She has been interested in riding since she was a child.
He takes riding lessons every Saturday at the local stable.
Many people find riding both challenging and relaxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrianism - the art or practice of horse riding
  • Synonym: horseback riding - the activity of riding a horse

2. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or action of traveling by horseback; equestrianism
travel by horseback

Examples

She has always loved riding and owns three horses.
We went riding in the countryside last weekend.
His main hobby is riding, especially competing in horse shows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrianism - the skill or sport of horse riding

Etymology

Derived from the verb 'ride,' originally from Old English 'rīdan.'
