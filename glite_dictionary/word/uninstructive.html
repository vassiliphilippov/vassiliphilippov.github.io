uninstructive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪnˈstrʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈstrʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not providing useful information or instruction; failing to teach or enlighten.
not informative or educational

Examples

The professor's lecture was uninstructive and left the students confused about the topic.
I found his comments completely uninstructive and unhelpful for improving my work.
Despite the length of the meeting, it remained uninstructive and a waste of time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uninformative - not providing useful or interesting information
  • Antonym: instructive - providing useful or educational information

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + instructive.
