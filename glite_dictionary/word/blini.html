blini

1. NOUN

/ ˈbliːni / (AmE), / ˈbliːni / (BrE)
A thin, round pancake of Russian origin, traditionally made with buckwheat flour and yeast, typically served with toppings such as caviar or sour cream.
thin Russian pancake

Examples

At the party, she served blini topped with smoked salmon and dill.
Traditional Russian blini are often enjoyed with sour cream or caviar.
We learned to make blini during our cooking class on Eastern European cuisine.

Etymology

From Russian 'блины' (bliny), plural of 'блин' (blin, pancake).
