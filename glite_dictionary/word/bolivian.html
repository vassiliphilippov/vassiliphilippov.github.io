Bolivian

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlɪviən / (AmE), / bəˈlɪviən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Bolivia
a person from Bolivia

Examples

The Bolivian greeted us with a warm smile as we entered the market.
Many Bolivians celebrate Independence Day with colorful parades and festivals.
She befriended a Bolivian student during her semester abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bolivia - a country in central South America

Etymology

From Bolivia, the South American country, with '-an' forming the demonym.

2. ADJ

/ boʊˈlɪviən / (AmE), / bəˈlɪvɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Bolivia or its people, language, or culture.
of or relating to Bolivia

Examples

The Bolivian music festival attracts visitors from around the world.
She enjoyed tasting traditional Bolivian cuisine during her travels.
A Bolivian artist won the international painting competition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Peruvian - of or relating to Peru or its people
  • Synonym: Chilean - of or relating to Chile or its people

Etymology

From Bolivia + -ian, denoting association or origin.
