swell

1. NOUN

/ swɛl / (AmE), / swɛl / (BrE)
a gradual increase and then decrease in loudness or intensity, especially in music; a crescendo followed by a decrescendo
a crescendo and decrescendo in music

Examples

The orchestra created a powerful swell that filled the concert hall.
There was a gentle swell in the volume before the music softened again.
Composers often use a swell to build emotion in their pieces.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: crescendo - a gradual increase in loudness or intensity
  • Degree Higher: decrescendo - a gradual decrease in loudness or intensity
