Liakoura

1. PROPN

/ liˈakʊrə / (AmE), / liˈakʊrə / (BrE)
The modern name for Mount Parnassus, a mountain in central Greece regarded in Greek mythology as the home of the Muses and the birthplace of music and poetry.
modern name for Mount Parnassus, mythic Greek mountain

Examples

Many poets journeyed to Liakoura in homage to its mythical significance.
The snow-capped peaks of Liakoura are visible from miles around.
According to legend, the Muses inspired artists on Liakoura.

Synset Examples

Liakoura is the modern name of Mount Parnassus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Parnassus - the mountain in central Greece, mythological home of the Muses

Etymology

Modern Greek Λιακούρα, the contemporary name for Mount Parnassus.
