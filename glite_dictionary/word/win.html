win

1. NOUN

/ wɪn / (AmE), / wɪn / (BrE)
an amount of money or prize that someone wins, especially through gambling or a competition
something won, especially a prize or money

Examples

Her biggest win at the casino was over $10,000.
The team's latest win came after a string of defeats.
He celebrated his lottery win with his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winnings - money won, especially from gambling
  • Antonym: loss - an amount of money lost, especially the opposite of a win
