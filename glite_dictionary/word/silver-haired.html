silver-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪlvər ˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˌheəd / (BrE)
having hair that is gray or white, like the color of silver, typically as a result of aging
having gray or white hair

Examples

The silver-haired gentleman greeted everyone with a warm smile.
Many silver-haired athletes continue to compete in senior events.
She admired the silver-haired woman’s elegant sense of style.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray-haired - having gray hair
  • Synonym: white-haired - having white hair
