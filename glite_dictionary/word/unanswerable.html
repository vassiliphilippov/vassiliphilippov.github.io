unanswerable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈænsərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːnsərəbl / (BrE)
impossible to be answered or refuted; not capable of being responded to with a satisfactory answer.
impossible to answer or refute

Examples

The lawyer presented an unanswerable case in court.
Her criticism was so unanswerable that no one bothered to reply.
Some philosophical questions are simply unanswerable by science alone.

Synset Examples

an unanswerable argument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrefutable - impossible to refute or disprove
  • Synonym: undeniable - impossible to deny or dispute
  • Antonym: refutable - able to be proved wrong or false

Etymology

From un- (not) + answerable. Modelled after similar negative adjectives.
