Pr

1. NOUN

/ piː ɑːr / (AmE), / piː ɑː / (BrE)
the symbol for praseodymium, a soft, yellowish-white metallic element of the rare earth group
chemical symbol for praseodymium

Examples

Scientists use Pr in creating strong alloys for aircraft engines.
The periodic table lists Pr as a rare earth element with atomic number 59.
You can find Pr in specialized glass and ceramics used in the electronics industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praseodymium - a soft yellowish-white trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group

Etymology

From New Latin 'praseodymium', named from Greek 'prasios' (leek-green) and 'didymos' (twin), referring to its green salts and discovery history.
