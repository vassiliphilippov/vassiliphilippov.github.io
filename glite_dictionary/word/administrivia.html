administrivia

1. NOUN

/ ədˌmɪnɪˈstrɪviə / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstrɪvɪə / (BrE)
Minor, routine administrative tasks or details, especially those considered tedious or time-consuming but necessary.
tedious but necessary administrative details/tasks

Examples

The team spent hours dealing with administrivia before they could focus on their main project.
She wished someone else could handle all the administrivia involved in organizing the conference.
Despite his senior role, he still found himself bogged down in administrivia every week.

Synset Examples

he sets policy and leaves all the administrivia to his assistant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administration - the process or activity of running a business, organization, etc.

Etymology

Blend of 'administration' and 'trivia', first used in the mid-20th century.
