warmheartedness

1. NOUN

/ wɔrmˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / wɔːmˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being kind, sympathetic, and caring towards others.
kind and sympathetic nature

Examples

Her warmheartedness made everyone feel welcome at the party.
The warmheartedness of the community was evident after the disaster.
Despite his fame, his warmheartedness never faded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • Synonym: warmhearted - kind and sympathetic in nature
  • Antonym: coldness - the quality of being unemotional or lacking compassion

2. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrmˌhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being kind, sympathetic, and friendly towards others
kindness and friendliness

Examples

Her warmheartedness was evident in the way she welcomed new neighbors.
People are drawn to him because of his genuine warmheartedness.
The teacher's warmheartedness created a comfortable atmosphere in the classroom.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology
