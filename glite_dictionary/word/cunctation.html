cunctation

1. NOUN

/ kʌŋkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / kʌŋkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of delaying or postponing an action; procrastination
act of delaying; procrastination

Examples

The general's cunctation in the face of the enemy led to missed opportunities.
His chronic cunctation frustrated his colleagues, who preferred decisive action.
Despite her talent, cunctation often kept her from submitting her work on time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: procrastination - the act of delaying or postponing something

Etymology

From Latin 'cunctatio' (a delaying, hesitation), from 'cunctari' (to delay).
