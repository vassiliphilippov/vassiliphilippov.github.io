nightlong

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪtˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtlɒŋ / (BrE)
lasting or continuing throughout the entire night
lasting the entire night

Examples

The refugees held a nightlong vigil outside the embassy.
After a nightlong storm, the streets were flooded by morning.
The team worked at a nightlong hackathon to finish their project.

Synset Examples

a nightlong vigil
an all-night drugstore
an overnight trip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all-night - lasting, open, or operating all night
  • Synonym: overnight - lasting, done, or staying during the night
