environmentalist

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is concerned with or advocates for the protection of the environment from destruction or pollution
person who protects the environment

Examples

As an environmentalist, she campaigns tirelessly for the preservation of wildlife habitats.
The new regulations were praised by environmentalists seeking to limit industrial pollution.
He became an environmentalist after witnessing the effects of deforestation firsthand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conservationist - person who advocates for the preservation and sustainable use of natural resources

Etymology

From environment + -alist, formed in English in the 20th century.
