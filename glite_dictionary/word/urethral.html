urethral

1. ADJ

/ jʊˈriːθrəl / (AmE), / jʊˈriːθrəl / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the urethra.
relating to the urethra
medical

Examples

A urethral catheter was inserted to help drain the patient's bladder.
The doctor noticed signs of urethral inflammation during the examination.
He experienced discomfort that suggested a possible urethral infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urethra - the duct by which urine is discharged from the bladder

Etymology

From urethra + -al, from Greek ourēthra (passage for urine).
