timepiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌpis / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmpiːs / (BrE)
A device or instrument for measuring and keeping time, such as a clock or watch.
device for measuring time

Examples

She inherited a beautiful antique timepiece from her grandfather.
The museum displayed a rare 18th-century timepiece in its collection.
He checked his timepiece to make sure he wasn't late for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clock - an instrument for measuring and indicating time
  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece worn typically on the wrist

Etymology

Formed from 'time' + 'piece' in English, with usage dating to the 16th century.
