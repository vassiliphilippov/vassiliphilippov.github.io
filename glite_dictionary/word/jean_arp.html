Jean Arp

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑn ɑrp / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ aʁp / (BrE)
(1887–1966) Alsatian artist and poet, cofounder of Dadaism in Zurich, known for his abstract organic sculptures.
Alsatian Dadaist artist and poet

Examples

Jean Arp's sculptures are celebrated for their fluid, organic forms.
The influence of Jean Arp can be seen in much of modern abstract art.
Many regard Jean Arp as a pioneer of Dadaism and Surrealist art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dadaism - art movement founded in Zurich characterized by avant-garde and anti-art ideas
  • Synonym: Hans Arp - alternative German name for Jean Arp, the same individual
