overfamiliar

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərfəˈmɪljər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəfəˈmɪliə / (BrE)
excessively friendly or informal, especially in a way that is inappropriate or presumptuous
inappropriately familiar or friendly

Examples

His overfamiliar attitude made her uncomfortable at the meeting.
The coach warned the players not to become overfamiliar with their new teammates too quickly.
Many staff found the manager's overfamiliar style to be inappropriate in the workplace.

Synset Examples

young women disliked the overfamiliar tone he took with them

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: familiar - friendly and informal, sometimes excessively so
  • Synonym: impertinent - not showing proper respect; rude

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (excessively) + 'familiar'.
