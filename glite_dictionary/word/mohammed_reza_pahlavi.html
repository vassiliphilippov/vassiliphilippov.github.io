Mohammed Reza Pahlavi

1. PROPN

/ moʊˌhæməd ˈrɛzə pɑːˈlɑːvi / (AmE), / məˌhæməd ˈreɪzə pɑːˈlɑːvi / (BrE)
the last Shah (king) of Iran, who ruled from 1941 until he was deposed in the 1979 Iranian Revolution
Shah of Iran deposed in 1979

Examples

Mohammed Reza Pahlavi ruled Iran for nearly four decades before being overthrown in 1979.
Many Iranians still debate the legacy of Mohammed Reza Pahlavi and his regime.
The exile of Mohammed Reza Pahlavi marked a turning point in Iranian history.
