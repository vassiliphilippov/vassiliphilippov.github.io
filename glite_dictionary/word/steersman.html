steersman

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪrz.mən / (AmE), / ˈstɪəz.mən / (BrE)
a person who steers a ship or boat, especially one responsible for its direction
person who steers a ship or boat

Examples

The steersman kept a steady course through the rough seas.
During the race, the steersman gave instructions to the rowing crew.
The old fishing boat relied on the skill of its steersman to navigate shallow waters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helmsman - person who steers a ship or boat

Etymology

From 'steer' (to guide a vessel) + 'man'.
