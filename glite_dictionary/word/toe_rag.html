toe rag

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ ræɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ ræɡ / (BrE)
an unpleasant, contemptible, or worthless person (informal, chiefly British slang)
contemptible or worthless person
slang british

Examples

Nobody wanted to work with that toe rag after what he did.
She called him a toe rag for betraying her secret.
You absolute toe rag—how could you be so mean?

Synset Examples

Don’t be a toe rag; it’s just a small favor.
He's such a toe rag for not helping his friend.
Stop acting like a toe rag and apologize.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lowlife - a person of low moral character
  • Synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person

Etymology

Originally referred to cloth wrapped around the feet as a makeshift sock; later used as British slang for a despicable person.
