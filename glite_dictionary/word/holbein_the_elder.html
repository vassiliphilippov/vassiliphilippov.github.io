Holbein the Elder

1. PROPN

/ ˈhoʊlbaɪn ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / ˈhɒlbaɪn ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(1465–1524) A German painter known for his religious artworks and as the father of Hans Holbein the Younger.
German painter of religious works, 1465–1524

Examples

Many altarpieces were painted by Holbein the Elder in the late 15th century.
The museum's collection features several works attributed to Holbein the Elder.
Few artists influenced the German Renaissance as greatly as Holbein the Elder.
