Nestor notabilis

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɛstɔr noʊˈteɪbɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌnɛstɔː nəʊˈtɑːbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A large brownish-green parrot native to New Zealand, known as the kea.
New Zealand parrot (kea)

Examples

The Nestor notabilis is renowned for its intelligence and playful behavior in the wild.
Researchers observed several Nestor notabilis using tools to access food.
The Nestor notabilis, or kea, is considered a protected species in New Zealand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kea - Common name for Nestor notabilis, a large New Zealand parrot

Etymology

From Latin: genus name Nestor after the wise king in Greek mythology, species epithet 'notabilis' meaning 'notable, remarkable'.
