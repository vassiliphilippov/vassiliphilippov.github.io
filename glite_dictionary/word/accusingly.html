accusingly

1. ADV

/ əˈkjuːzɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that suggests someone is blaming or considering someone at fault.
in a manner showing blame or accusation

Examples

She stared accusingly at her colleague when he denied responsibility.
He pointed accusingly at the broken vase on the floor.
The teacher glanced accusingly at the students when she discovered the missing homework.

Synset Examples

he looked at her accusingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accusing - showing that someone thinks another is to blame
