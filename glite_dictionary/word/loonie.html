loonie

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːni / (AmE), / ˈluːni / (BrE)
the Canadian one-dollar coin, which features the image of a loon
Canadian one-dollar coin
informal american

Examples

He paid for his coffee with a loonie and some change.
Tourists often keep a loonie as a souvenir after visiting Canada.
The vending machine only accepts loonies and toonies.

Synset Examples

the Canadian dollar has the image of loon on one side of the coin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Canadian dollar - the official currency of Canada

Etymology

From 'loon', the bird featured on the coin, plus the diminutive suffix '-ie'.
