reflectometer

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌflɛkˈtɑmɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˌflɛkˈtɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the reflectance of a surface
instrument for measuring reflectance

Examples

The researcher used a reflectometer to analyze the surface coating's optical properties.
Maintenance staff checked the runway markings with a portable reflectometer.
Accurate readings from the reflectometer helped ensure the glass panels met quality standards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photometer - instrument measuring light intensity
  • Synonym: spectrophotometer - instrument measuring light intensity at different wavelengths

Etymology

From Latin 'reflectere' (to bend back) and Greek 'metron' (measure).
