Sundanese

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌndəˈniz / (AmE), / ˌsʌndəˈniːz / (BrE)
the Austronesian language spoken by the Sundanese people of West Java, Indonesia
Austronesian language of West Java, Indonesia

Examples

Many people in Bandung speak Sundanese at home and Indonesian at work.
She decided to learn Sundanese as part of her research into West Java’s culture.
This dictionary provides translations between English and Sundanese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Javanese - the language of the Javanese people, also spoken in Indonesia

Etymology

From Sundanese 'Sunda', referring to the ethnic group or region in West Java.
