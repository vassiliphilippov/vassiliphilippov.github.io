whatchamacallit

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌtʃə məˌkɔːlɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃəməˌkɒlɪt / (BrE)
used to refer to an object or thing whose name you do not know, have forgotten, or cannot recall at the moment
an unnamed or forgotten thing
informal

Examples

Hand me that whatchamacallit on the table, will you?
I can't fix this without the whatchamacallit they gave us last week.
What's that whatchamacallit you use to open bottles?

Synset Examples

she eased the ball-shaped doodad back into its socket
there may be some great new gizmo around the corner that you will want to use

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thingamajig - an object or gadget whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • Synonym: doohickey - a small object or gadget, especially one whose name you do not know or have forgotten

Etymology

Alteration of 'what you may call it,' first recorded in US English in the 1920s.
