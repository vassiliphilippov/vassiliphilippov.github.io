perceptively

1. ADV

/ pərˈsɛptɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈsɛptɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that shows keen insight or understanding; in a perceptive manner.
in a perceptive manner

Examples

She perceptively noticed the subtle changes in his behavior before anyone else did.
The critic perceptively analyzed the film's deeper themes in his review.
Children can sometimes perceptively sense tension between adults even without words.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insightfully - in a way that demonstrates insight
  • Synonym: perceptive - having or showing keen insight or understanding
