tostada

1. NOUN

/ toʊˈstɑːdə / (AmE), / təʊˈstɑːdə / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a flat, crispy fried tortilla topped with various fillings such as beans, meat, cheese, and vegetables.
Mexican flat crispy tortilla with toppings

Examples

He ordered a chicken tostada with extra salsa for lunch.
Each tostada was topped with fresh lettuce, beans, and cheese.
At the party, we served homemade tostadas as appetizers.

Etymology

From Spanish 'tostada', meaning 'toasted', from 'tostar' (to toast).
