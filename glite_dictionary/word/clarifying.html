clarifying

1. ADJ

/ ˈklær.əˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɑː.rɪˌfaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
making something easier to understand; helping to make something clear or free from confusion.
making something clear

Examples

The professor gave a clarifying summary at the end of her lecture.
He asked a clarifying question to better understand the topic.
A good diagram often serves a clarifying purpose.

Synset Examples

a clarifying example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explanatory - serving to explain something
  • Antonym: obscuring - making unclear or difficult to understand

Etymology

Present participle of 'clarify', from Late Latin 'clarificare' (to make clear).
