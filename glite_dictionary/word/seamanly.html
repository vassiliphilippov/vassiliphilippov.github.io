seamanly

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiːˌmænli / (AmE), / ˈsiːmənli / (BrE)
having qualities or behavior characteristic of a competent seaman; suitable for a sailor
befitting or characteristic of a seaman

Examples

The captain's seamanly conduct inspired confidence among the crew.
He responded to the storm in a calm and seamanly manner.
Her seamanly skills made her a valued member of the ship's company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seaman - a sailor; a person who works on a ship

Etymology

From 'seaman' + '-ly', in analogy with adjectives like 'manly'.
