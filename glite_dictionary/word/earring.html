earring

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
A piece of jewelry worn on the ear, usually attached to the earlobe by piercing or clipping.
jewelry worn on the ear

Examples

She wore a sparkling diamond earring to the party.
He lost one of his favorite earrings at the beach yesterday.
The jeweler helped her choose a gold earring to match her necklace.

Etymology

From Old English 'ēare' (ear) + 'hring' (ring).
