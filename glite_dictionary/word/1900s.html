1900s

1. NOUN

/ naɪnˈtin ˈhʌndrədz / (AmE), / naɪnˈtiːn ˈhʌndrədz / (BrE)
the decade from 1900 to 1909
the years 1900 to 1909

Examples

At the beginning of the 1900s, technological progress was rapidly changing urban life.
Many famous artists emerged during the 1900s and left a lasting mark on culture.
In the 1900s, electric lighting became widespread in major cities.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: 1910s - the decade from 1910 to 1919
  • Degree Lower: 1890s - the decade from 1890 to 1899
