hypophysial

1. ADJ

/ ˌhaɪpəˈfɪziəl / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈfɪzɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the hypophysis (the pituitary gland)
of the pituitary gland
medical

Examples

The doctor explained that a hypophysial tumor could affect hormone production.
Certain disorders can result from abnormal hypophysial function.
Researchers are studying hypophysial control of growth in children.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pituitary - relating to the pituitary gland

Etymology

From Greek 'hypophysis' (outgrowth) + English '-al' (adjectival suffix).
