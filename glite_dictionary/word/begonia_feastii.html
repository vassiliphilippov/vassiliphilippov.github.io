Begonia feastii

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡoʊniə fiˈæstiːaɪ / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə fiˈæstiːaɪ / (BrE)
A species of begonia with round, fleshy leaves that are reddish on the underside, grown as an ornamental plant.
species of begonia with reddish underside of leaves

Examples

The Begonia feastii in the conservatory thrived in the humid environment.
Gardeners prize Begonia feastii for its uniquely colored foliage.
She bought a Begonia feastii to add to her collection of houseplants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: begonia - a genus of flowering plants valued as ornamentals
