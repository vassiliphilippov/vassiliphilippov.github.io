padded

1. ADJ

/ ˈpædəd / (AmE), / ˈpædɪd / (BrE)
containing or covered with soft material to make it more comfortable, full, or protective.
filled or covered with padding

Examples

She bought a padded jacket to keep warm during the winter months.
The padded seats in the theater made the long show much more comfortable.
He wore padded gloves to protect his hands while boxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cushioned - made softer or more comfortable with cushioning

Etymology

From 'pad' (soft material) + -ed (past participle/adjectival suffix).
