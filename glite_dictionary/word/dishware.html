dishware

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪʃˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈdɪʃˌweə / (BrE)
Plates, bowls, cups, and similar items used for serving and eating food collectively.
items used for serving and eating food

Examples

We bought a new set of dishware for the dinner party.
Please put the dishware in the dishwasher after breakfast.
Her collection of antique dishware is displayed in the glass cabinet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tableware - utensils and dishes used at the table for meals collectively
