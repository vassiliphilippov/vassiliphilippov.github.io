humanlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics similar to those of a human being.
resembling a human; human in appearance or traits

Examples

The robot moved in a remarkably humanlike manner, startling everyone in the laboratory.
Her illustrations gave the animals humanlike expressions that appealed to children and adults alike.
Scientists are designing AI with increasingly humanlike abilities to interact and respond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropomorphic - attributing human characteristics to non-human things

Etymology

Formed from 'human' + 'like', meaning 'like a human'.
