Delhi

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɛli / (AmE), / ˈdɛli / (BrE)
A large city and union territory in north central India, which includes New Delhi, the country's capital.
city and territory in India

Examples

Many tourists visit Delhi to see the Red Fort and other historical landmarks.
He moved to Delhi for a new job opportunity.
New Delhi is a part of the larger metropolitan area of Delhi.

Etymology

From Hindi दिल्ली (Dillī), ultimately from classical name Indraprastha.
