waist-length

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪst lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ˈweɪst lɛŋkθ / (BrE)
(of clothing) reaching or extending only to the waist.
reaching the waist (clothing)

Examples

She wore a waist-length jacket over her dress.
The designer released a new line of waist-length coats this season.
A waist-length cardigan is perfect for layering in cooler weather.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hip-length - (of clothing) extending down to the hips
  • Degree Higher: knee-length - (of clothing) extending down to the knee
