tricycle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪ.sɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌɪsɪk(ə)l / (BrE)
a vehicle with three wheels, operated by foot pedals, often used by children
three-wheeled pedal vehicle

Examples

The little boy rode his tricycle around the park all afternoon.
She bought a pink tricycle for her daughter's third birthday.
Adults sometimes use a special tricycle for exercise or transportation.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels propelled by pedals
  • Synonym: trike - informal term for a tricycle, especially for children

Etymology

From tri- (three) + cycle (from Greek kyklos, circle or wheel).
