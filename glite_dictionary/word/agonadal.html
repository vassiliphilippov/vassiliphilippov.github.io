agonadal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæɡ.oʊˈnæd.əl / (AmE), / ˌaɡ.əʊˈnæd.əl / (BrE)
lacking gonads; having no testes or ovaries.
lacking gonads
medical

Examples

The patient was found to be agonadal due to a rare genetic disorder.
Some animals are naturally agonadal at certain stages of their lifecycle.
In medical literature, an agonadal individual is described as lacking functional gonadal tissue.

Etymology

From a- (without) + gonadal, from Greek gonē (seed).
