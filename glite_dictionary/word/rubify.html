to rubify

1. VERB

/ ˈruːbɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈruːbɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make or become ruby red in color
make ruby red

Examples

The sunlight seemed to rubify the clouds at sunset, giving them a jewel-like glow.
The artist used a special glaze to rubify the surface of the ceramic vase.
As autumn progressed, the leaves began to rubify, standing out vividly against the sky.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to redden - to make or become red

Etymology

From Latin 'rubeus' (red) + English '-fy'.
