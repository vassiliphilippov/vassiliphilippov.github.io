gassy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡæsi / (AmE), / ˈɡasi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) suffering from or affected by excessive gas in the stomach or intestines.
having excess intestinal gas

Examples

After eating beans, she often feels gassy and uncomfortable.
The pediatrician suggested remedies for a gassy baby.
He avoided carbonated drinks because they made him gassy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatulent - suffering from or marked by an accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal

2. ADJ

/ ˈɡæsi / (AmE), / ˈɡasi / (BrE)
Having the characteristics or quality of gas; resembling or full of gas.
resembling or full of gas

Examples

The swamp released a gassy odor as we walked by.
This drink is too gassy for my taste and makes me burp.
The atmosphere on that planet is thick and gassy, mainly composed of hydrogen.

Etymology

From 'gas' + suffix '-y', indicating 'full of' or 'characterized by'.
