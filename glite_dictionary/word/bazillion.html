bazillion

1. NOUN

/ bəˈzɪljən / (AmE), / bəˈzɪljən / (BrE)
an extremely large, indefinite number (used for emphasis, not exact counting)
extremely large, indefinite number
informal

Examples

I told you a bazillion times to clean your room.
There are a bazillion reasons why I love this city.
She has a bazillion ideas running through her mind every day.

Synset Examples

there were millions of flies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: zillion - an extremely large, indefinite number (informal, hyperbolic)
  • Degree Lower: million - the number 1,000,000, one million
  • Degree Lower: billion - the number 1,000,000,000 (or 1,000,000,000,000 in some countries)

Etymology

Formed as a humorous variant of 'million' and 'billion' in imitation of large number words.
