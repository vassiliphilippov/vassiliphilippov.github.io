murderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜrdərər / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdərə / (BrE)
a person who unlawfully and deliberately kills another person
person who commits murder

Examples

The police arrested the murderer after finding new evidence at the crime scene.
She was terrified when she realized the murderer was still at large.
The jury found him guilty and declared him a murderer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer - a person who kills another, intentionally or unintentionally
  • Antonym: victim - a person harmed, injured, or killed by another

Etymology

From Middle English murthrer, from Old French meurtrier, from Late Latin murderator.
