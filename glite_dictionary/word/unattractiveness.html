unattractiveness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being unpleasant or unappealing in appearance.
quality of being unattractive

Examples

The unattractiveness of the building discouraged potential buyers from showing any interest.
Despite its unattractiveness, the dish turned out to be surprisingly delicious.
Some people worry too much about the unattractiveness of minor flaws in their appearance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractiveness - the quality of being attractive or appealing
  • Synonym: ugliness - the quality of being very unattractive

Etymology

From the prefix un- (not) + attractiveness, itself from attractive (from Latin attrahere 'to draw towards').
