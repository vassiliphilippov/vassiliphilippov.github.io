Bufo viridis

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ ˈvɪrɪdɪs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ ˈvɪrɪdɪs / (BrE)
A species of Eurasian toad known for its variable, chiefly green coloration; also called the European green toad.
a Eurasian green toad species

Examples

The distinctive markings of the Bufo viridis help it blend into grassy environments.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of Bufo viridis in the Balkan Peninsula.
Sightings of Bufo viridis have been increasing due to conservation efforts.

Etymology

From Latin: 'bufo' meaning 'toad' and 'viridis' meaning 'green'.
