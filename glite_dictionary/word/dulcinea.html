dulcinea

1. NOUN

/ dʌlˈsɪniə / (AmE), / dʌlˈsɪniə / (BrE)
A woman who is a man's beloved or sweetheart, often used in a literary or slightly grandiose way.
a man's beloved woman

Examples

He claimed to have crossed continents for his dulcinea, though few believed his tale.
Though she had never met him, he wrote letters to his mysterious dulcinea every week.
In his poetry, the author refers to his dulcinea with admiration and longing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweetheart - a person with whom someone is in love
  • Synonym: beloved - a much loved person

Etymology

From Spanish Dulcinea, the name of Don Quixote’s idealized lady-love in Cervantes' novel.
