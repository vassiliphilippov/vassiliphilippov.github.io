barf

1. NOUN

/ bɑrf / (AmE), / bɑːf / (BrE)
the matter that is ejected from the stomach through the mouth during vomiting; vomit
vomit; matter ejected in vomiting
informal slang

Examples

There was barf all over the bathroom floor after the party.
She had to clean up the dog's barf in the kitchen.
The smell of barf made everyone feel nauseous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - the matter ejected from the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: emesis - medical term for vomiting or material ejected

2. VERB

/ bɑrf / (AmE), / bɑːf / (BrE)
to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth; to vomit
informal verb meaning 'to vomit'
informal

Examples

I thought I would barf after that roller coaster ride.
The baby barfed all over the new carpet.
Please open the window in case someone starts barfing in the car.

Synset Examples

After drinking too much, the students vomited
He purged continuously
The patient regurgitated the food we gave him last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Degree Lower: retch - to make an effort to vomit
