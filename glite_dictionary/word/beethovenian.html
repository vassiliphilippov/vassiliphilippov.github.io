Beethovenian

1. ADJ

/ ˌbeɪtoʊˈviːniən / (AmE), / ˌbeɪtəʊˈviːniən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Ludwig van Beethoven or his music.
of or relating to Beethoven or his music

Examples

The symphony was praised for its Beethovenian grandeur and emotional depth.
Her playing had a distinctly Beethovenian intensity that captivated the audience.
The composer's latest work takes a Beethovenian approach, emphasizing powerful contrasts and bold themes.

Etymology

From the name Beethoven + -ian suffix, indicating relation or connection.
