overseas

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvərˈsiz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈsiːz / (BrE)
in or to a foreign country across the sea or ocean, especially from the perspective of one's own country
in or to a foreign country across the sea

Examples

After graduation, she plans to work overseas for a few years.
Many products are manufactured overseas before being imported to this country.
His family moved overseas when he was a child due to his father's job.

Etymology

From over + sea, indicating travel or location across the sea.
