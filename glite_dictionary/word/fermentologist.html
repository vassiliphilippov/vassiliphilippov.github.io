fermentologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɜrməntəˈlɑdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌfɜːməntəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
A specialist in the science and practice of fermentation, particularly in wine making.
expert in fermentation, especially wine

Examples

The vineyard hired a fermentologist to improve the quality of its wines.
As a fermentologist, Martha studied the effects of yeast strains on flavor development.
Our team consulted a fermentologist to troubleshoot issues in the secondary fermentation process.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oenologist - a specialist in the science of winemaking
  • Synonym: brewmaster - a person who supervises the brewing process

Etymology

From 'ferment' (Latin 'fermentum', leaven) + '-ologist' (one who studies).
