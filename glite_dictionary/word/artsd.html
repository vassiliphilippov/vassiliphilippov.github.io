ArtsD

1. NOUN

/ ɑrts diː / (AmE), / ɑːts diː / (BrE)
An honorary degree awarded in the arts, typically a Doctor of Arts.
honorary arts degree (Doctor of Arts)

Examples

She was thrilled to receive an ArtsD from her alma mater in recognition of her achievements.
Many universities confer the honorary ArtsD on distinguished artists and scholars.
His contributions to literature were acknowledged with an ArtsD during the graduation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PhD - doctorate degree, Doctor of Philosophy
  • Synonym: honorary degree - degree awarded as an honor, not after examination

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Doctor of Arts'.
