swearword

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛr.wɝd / (AmE), / ˈswɛə.wɜːd / (BrE)
a word or expression that is considered profane or obscene, commonly used to express strong emotion such as surprise or anger
profane or obscene word

Examples

He let out a loud swearword when he stubbed his toe.
Using a swearword in class may get you into trouble.
She tried not to say a swearword in front of the children.

Synset Examples

expletives were deleted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expletive - a word or phrase used to fill out a sentence or as a profanity
  • Synonym: curse word - a profane or obscene word used as an exclamation
  • Antonym: euphemism - a mild or indirect word substituted for one considered too harsh

Etymology

From 'swear' (to use profane language) + 'word'.
