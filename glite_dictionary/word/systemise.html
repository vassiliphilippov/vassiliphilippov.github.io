to systemise

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪs.tə.maɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪs.tɪ.maɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or organize something according to a system; to make systematic
arrange in a systematic way
british

Examples

It is important to systemise laboratory procedures to ensure consistent results.
The organization began to systemise its training programs for all employees.
Efforts to systemise data collection have improved the accuracy of the research.

Synset Examples

systematize our scientific knowledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to systematize - to arrange according to a system (American spelling)

Etymology

From system + -ise, modelled on the American spelling systematize.
