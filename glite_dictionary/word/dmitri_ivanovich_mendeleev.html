Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev

1. PROPN

/ dəˈmitri ˌivəˈnoʊvɪtʃ ˌmɛndəˈleɪəf / (AmE), / dmɪˈtri ˌiːvənəˈvɪtʃ ˌmɛndɪˈleɪɛf / (BrE)
(1834–1907) a Russian chemist best known for formulating the periodic table of chemical elements and predicting the discovery of several new elements.
Russian chemist, created periodic table

Examples

Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev is credited with creating the first widely recognized periodic table of elements.
Many scientists were inspired by the groundbreaking work of Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev.
The predictions made by Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev guided the discovery of several new chemical elements.
