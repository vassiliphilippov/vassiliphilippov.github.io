DDS

1. NOUN

/ di di ɛs / (AmE), / diː diː ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Doctor of Dental Surgery, a professional doctoral degree for dentists
abbreviation for Doctor of Dental Surgery

Examples

After completing her DDS, she opened her own dental clinic in the city.
You must earn a DDS to become a licensed dentist in the United States.
Her diploma proudly displayed the title DDS after her name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DMD - Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry, another dental degree

Etymology

Abbreviation of Doctor of Dental Surgery.
