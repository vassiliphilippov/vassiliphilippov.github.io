female

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈfiː.meɪl / (BrE)
Characteristic of or relating to women; associated with women rather than men.
relating to women

Examples

The organization is focused on promoting female leadership in business.
She offers a valuable female perspective on the issue.
The country only granted female suffrage in the 20th century.

Synset Examples
