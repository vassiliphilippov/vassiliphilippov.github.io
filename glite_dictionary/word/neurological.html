neurological

1. ADJ

/ nuːrəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / njuːrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the nervous system or to neurology, the scientific study of the nervous system and its disorders
relating to the nervous system or neurology
medical

Examples

The patient underwent a series of neurological tests to assess her reflexes and brain function.
He specializes in treating neurological disorders such as epilepsy and Parkinson's disease.
Recent studies have found a possible neurological basis for some psychological conditions.

Synset Examples

neurological evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurologic - alternative form meaning relating to neurology
  • Synonym: neurology - the medical science dealing with the nervous system

Etymology

From neuro- (relating to nerves) + -logical, patterned after 'neurology'.
