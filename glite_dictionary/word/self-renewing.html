self-renewing

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf rɪˈnuɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlf rɪˈnjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
Having the ability to renew or regenerate itself, especially used to describe cells or entities capable of continuous renewal.
capable of renewing itself

Examples

Stem cells are unique because they are self-renewing and can differentiate into various cell types.
The garden’s self-renewing ecosystem required little human intervention.
Innovation and adaptability make the company a self-renewing organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-renewal - the process of renewing or regenerating oneself
