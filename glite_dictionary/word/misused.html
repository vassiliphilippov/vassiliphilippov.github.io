misused

1. ADJ

/ mɪsˈjuzd / (AmE), / mɪsˈjuːzd / (BrE)
used incorrectly, carelessly, or for an improper purpose.
used incorrectly or improperly

Examples

Many scientific terms are often misused in popular media.
She felt her trust had been misused by her business partner.
The data was misused to support a false conclusion.

Synset Examples

misused words are often laughable but one weeps for misused talents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abused - used wrongly or treated badly
  • Antonym: well-used - used appropriately and frequently
