sangaree

1. NOUN

/ ˌsæŋɡəˈri / (AmE), / ˌsaŋɡəˈriː / (BrE)
A sweetened alcoholic drink made with red wine and orange or lemon juice, served with soda water.
sweetened wine and citrus mixed drink

Examples

He ordered a refreshing sangaree to enjoy on the sunny patio.
At the party, pitchers of sangaree were served alongside finger foods.
The bartender recommended a sangaree for those who enjoy fruity cocktails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sangria - a Spanish drink of red wine with fruit and spirits

Etymology

From Spanish 'sangría', meaning bleeding (also the origin of sangria).
