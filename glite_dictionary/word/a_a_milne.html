A. A. Milne

1. PROPN

/ ˌeɪ eɪ ˈmɪln / (AmE), / ˌeɪ eɪ ˈmɪln / (BrE)
(1882–1956) English writer celebrated for his stories and poems for children, notably the 'Winnie-the-Pooh' series.
English children's writer, creator of Winnie-the-Pooh

Examples

Many parents read A. A. Milne's stories to their children at bedtime.
The beloved character Winnie-the-Pooh was created by A. A. Milne.
A museum in England features exhibits about A. A. Milne's life and works.
