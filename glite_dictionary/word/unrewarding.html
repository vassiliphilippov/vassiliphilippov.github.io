unrewarding

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈwɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈwɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
Not providing satisfaction or a sense of achievement; not rewarding.
not giving satisfaction or reward

Examples

She found the work unrewarding and decided to look for a new job.
Despite the effort, volunteering at the shelter often felt unrewarding to him.
Many people consider repetitive administrative tasks unrewarding after a while.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rewarding - giving satisfaction or benefit; providing reward
