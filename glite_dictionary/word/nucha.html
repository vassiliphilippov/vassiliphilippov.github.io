nucha

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːkə / (AmE), / ˈnjuːkə / (BrE)
the back part of the neck, especially in anatomical or medical contexts.
back of the neck (anatomy)
medical

Examples

During the examination, the doctor gently touched the nucha to check for swelling.
The collar is designed to support the nucha after neck surgery.
Pain in the nucha can be a symptom of meningitis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nape - the back of the neck

Etymology

From Latin 'nucha' (the nape of the neck), likely of Arabic origin.
