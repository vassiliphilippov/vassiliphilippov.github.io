cyclic

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaɪklɪk, ˈsɪklɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪklɪk, ˈsaɪklɪk / (BrE)
happening in cycles; occurring in a repeated and predictable order.
happening in cycles

Examples

The company faces cyclic downturns every few years due to changing market conditions.
Many natural phenomena, such as the seasons, are cyclic in nature.
Her mood swings seemed almost cyclic, repeating every few weeks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodic - occurring at regular intervals
  • Antonym: noncyclic - not occurring in cycles

Etymology

From Greek kyklikos, from kyklos (circle).
