Nijmegen

1. PROPN

/ ˈnaɪmeɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈnaɪmeɪɡən / (BrE)
an industrial city in the eastern part of the Netherlands, notable for its history and industry
city in eastern Netherlands

Examples

The university in Nijmegen attracts students from all over Europe.
During our trip to the Netherlands, we spent two days exploring Nijmegen.
Nijmegen is one of the oldest cities in the country, with roots dating back to Roman times.

Etymology

From Dutch 'Nijmegen', derived from the Latin 'Noviomagus'.
