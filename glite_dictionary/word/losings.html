losings

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːzɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈluːzɪŋz / (BrE)
money or other amounts that have been lost, especially as a result of gambling or unsuccessful ventures.
money or amount lost, especially gambling

Examples

He tried to cover his losings from last night's poker game by borrowing more money.
Her losings at the casino left her feeling frustrated and disappointed.
The gambler calculated his losings after a long night at the tables.

Related Terms
