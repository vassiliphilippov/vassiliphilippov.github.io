checklist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.lɪst / (BrE)
a list of items, tasks, or names to be checked or referred to
list of items to check or refer to

Examples

Before leaving for the airport, she reviewed her checklist to make sure she packed everything.
The safety inspector went through a detailed checklist before approving the machinery for use.
Creating a checklist helped him organize his daily tasks more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - a series of items written or printed consecutively

Etymology

Formed from 'check' (to mark off) + 'list' (enumeration of items), first used in early 20th century.
