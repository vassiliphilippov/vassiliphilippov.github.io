upended

1. ADJ

/ ʌpˈɛndɪd / (AmE), / ʌpˈɛndɪd / (BrE)
turned so that the end or bottom is facing upwards; overturned or set on end.
turned so an end is upwards

Examples

The chair was upended and blocking the doorway.
After the accident, the vehicle lay upended in the ditch.
She found her suitcase upended with all the clothes spilled out.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upright - in a vertical position; not overturned
