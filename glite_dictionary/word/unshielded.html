unshielded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃiːldɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃiːldɪd / (BrE)
Not protected or covered by a shield, especially with reference to machinery or electrical equipment.
not protected by a shield

Examples

The laboratory was equipped with several unshielded wires, posing a safety hazard.
Exposure to unshielded radiation can be extremely dangerous to workers.
Please replace any unshielded cables with shielded ones to prevent interference.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shielded - protected by a shield

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'shielded'.
