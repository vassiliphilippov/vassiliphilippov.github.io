cytology

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / saɪˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of biology that studies the structure and function of cells
study of cells in biology
medical

Examples

She is specializing in cytology as part of her medical research degree.
A course in cytology covers topics such as cell division and cellular structure.
Recent advances in cytology have improved the diagnosis of many diseases.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cell biology - the branch of biology dealing with the study of cells

Etymology

From Greek kytos (hollow, cell) + -logy (study of).
