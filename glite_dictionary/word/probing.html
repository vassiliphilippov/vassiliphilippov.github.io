probing

1. ADJ

/ ˈproʊbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊbɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a question, inquiry, or investigation) searching and thorough, aiming to uncover the truth or details.
searching and thorough inquiry or investigation

Examples

The journalist asked a series of probing questions during the interview.
Her probing analysis revealed inconsistencies in the financial reports.
He appreciated the detective's probing approach to uncovering the truth.

Synset Examples

a probing inquiry
a searching investigation of their past dealings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: searching - intensely inquisitive or thorough in investigation

Etymology

From the verb 'probe', derived from Latin 'probare' (to test, examine).
