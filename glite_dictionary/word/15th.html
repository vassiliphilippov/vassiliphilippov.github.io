15th

1. ADJ

/ fɪfˈtinθ / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the fourteenth in position; being number fifteen in a sequence
ordinal number: fifteenth

Examples

She finished in 15th place in the marathon, just shy of her goal.
The 15th of July is my cousin's birthday.
This is the 15th anniversary of the company's founding.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 14th - coming after the thirteenth and before the fifteenth
  • Degree Higher: 16th - coming after the fifteenth and before the seventeenth

Etymology

Formed from the cardinal 'fifteen' with the ordinal suffix '-th'.
