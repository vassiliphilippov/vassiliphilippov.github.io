businesspeople

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪsˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪsˌpiːpəl / (BrE)
Men and women engaged in commercial, industrial, or professional activities, especially as business executives or entrepreneurs.
people involved in business activities

Examples

Many businesspeople attended the international trade conference to network and find new opportunities.
The city is a hub for ambitious businesspeople seeking to launch their startups.
Experienced businesspeople often mentor young entrepreneurs in their industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: businessman - a man engaged in business
  • Synonym: businesswoman - a woman engaged in business
