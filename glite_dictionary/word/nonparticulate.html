nonparticulate

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌpɑrˈtɪkjələt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌpɑːˈtɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
Not composed of distinct particles; lacking a particle-based structure.
not composed of particles

Examples

The air filter separates nonparticulate gases from particulate matter.
This medication is available in both nonparticulate and particulate forms.
Researchers compared the effects of nonparticulate water pollutants with those from solid debris.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: particulate - composed of minute separate particles

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'non-' (not) + 'particulate' (composed of particles).
