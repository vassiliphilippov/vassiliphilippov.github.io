largish

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɑrdʒɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly large; somewhat large, but not very large
somewhat large in size

Examples

They bought a largish apartment in the city center.
A largish dog was sleeping by the fireplace.
He handed me a largish envelope filled with documents.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: large - of considerable or relatively great size
  • Degree Lower: medium - of average size
