Robert Lowell

1. PROPN

/ ˈroʊbɚt ˈloʊ.əl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbət ˈləʊ.əl / (BrE)
(1917–1977) an American poet known for his influential and confessional style, and for works such as 'Life Studies'.
American poet (1917–1977)

Examples

Robert Lowell was a central figure in the Boston literary scene during the twentieth century.
Many critics have praised Robert Lowell for his innovative use of form and personal subject matter.
The poetry of Robert Lowell continues to influence writers and students alike today.
