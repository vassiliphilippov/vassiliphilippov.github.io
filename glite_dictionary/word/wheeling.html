Wheeling

1. PROPN

/ ˈwiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
A city in the northern panhandle of West Virginia, situated on the Ohio River.
city in West Virginia, USA

Examples

My grandparents grew up in Wheeling and often tell stories about the city's rich history.
The bridge in Wheeling is a well-known landmark among locals and visitors alike.
After moving to Wheeling, she quickly became involved in the community.

Etymology

Named for Wheeling Creek; the name may derive from a Native American word.
