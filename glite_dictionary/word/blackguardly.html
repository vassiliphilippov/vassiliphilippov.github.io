blackguardly

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæɡˌɑrdli / (AmE), / ˈblæɡˌɑːdli / (BrE)
Behaving in a dishonorable or unprincipled way; characteristic of a person who lacks morals or scruples.
dishonorable; lacking morals or scruples

Examples

The newspaper condemned the politician's blackguardly behavior during the scandal.
She refused to associate with anyone who acted in such a blackguardly manner.
The judge denounced the defendant's blackguardly conduct in his closing remarks.

Synset Examples

the rascally rabble
the tyranny of a scoundrelly aristocracy
the captain was set adrift by his roguish crew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dishonorable - lacking honor or integrity
  • Synonym: scoundrelly - having the character of a scoundrel
  • Antonym: upright - behaving morally and ethically

Etymology

From 'blackguard' (a person of low morals or dishonor) + '-ly'.
