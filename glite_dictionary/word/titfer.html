titfer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪtfər / (AmE), / ˈtɪtfə / (BrE)
(British slang) A hat.
Cockney slang for hat
slang british

Examples

He strolled into the room wearing a flashy titfer that caught everyone's eye.
You can't go to Ascot without a proper titfer on your head!
My granddad always wore his favourite old titfer to the pub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat - a covering for the head, typically with a brim

Etymology

Cockney rhyming slang, from 'tit for tat' (rhymes with 'hat'), shortened to 'titfer'.
