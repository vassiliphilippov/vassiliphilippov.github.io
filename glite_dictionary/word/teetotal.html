teetotal

1. ADJ

/ ˈtiˌtoʊtəl / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌtəʊtəl / (BrE)
choosing or practicing complete abstinence from alcoholic drinks
abstaining from alcohol

Examples

After his illness, John decided to become teetotal and avoid all alcoholic beverages.
At the party, she politely declined the wine, saying she was teetotal.
Many people choose a teetotal lifestyle for health or religious reasons.

Synset Examples

he's been dry for ten years
no thank you, I happen to be teetotal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstinent - refraining from indulgence, especially of alcohol
  • Antonym: drinking - consuming alcoholic beverages

Etymology

Probably from 'tee-total' (with tee as an emphatic syllable), first used in the early 19th century in England.
