Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec

1. PROPN

/ ɑ̃ˈri də tuːˈluːs loʊˈtrɛk / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈri də tuːˈluːs ləʊˈtrɛk / (BrE)
(1864–1901) French painter noted for his portrayals of Parisian nightlife, especially scenes from cafes and music halls in Montmartre.
French painter noted for Parisian nightlife scenes

Examples

Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec is famous for his vibrant paintings of Parisian cabarets.
The works of Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec capture the energy of late 19th-century Montmartre.
Many art students study Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec for his innovative use of color and composition.
