neighbouring

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈneɪbərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of places or things) located next to or very near another; sharing a boundary or edge.
adjacent; next to
british

Examples

The council is considering merging with the neighbouring town for improved services.
Several neighbouring countries signed a peace agreement last year.
We walked through the park to explore the neighbouring neighborhoods.

Synset Examples

Rhode Island has two bordering states: Massachusetts and Connecticut
the side of Germany conterminous with France
Utah and the contiguous state of Idaho
neighboring cities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adjacent - next to or adjoining something else
  • Antonym: remote - far away in distance or relation

Etymology

From 'neighbour' + '-ing'. 'Neighbour' from Old English 'neahgebur' (near-dweller).
