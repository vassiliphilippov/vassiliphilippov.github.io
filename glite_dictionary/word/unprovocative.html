unprovocative

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prəˈvɑː.kə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈvɒk.ətɪv / (BrE)
not likely to cause a strong reaction or provoke interest, excitement, or controversy; not provocative.
not provocative

Examples

Her outfit was deliberately unprovocative to avoid attracting unnecessary attention.
The speech was so unprovocative that hardly anyone reacted to it.
He prefers films with unprovocative themes that do not stir up controversy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: provocative - causing a strong reaction or excitement

Etymology

un- (not) + provocative, from Latin provocare (to call forth).
