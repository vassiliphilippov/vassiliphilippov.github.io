lithology

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈθɑlədʒi / (AmE), / lɪˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the general physical characteristics, composition, and classification of rocks
study of rocks and their characteristics

Examples

The lithology of the region revealed a predominance of volcanic rocks.
Students in geology class spent a week learning about lithology and rock classification.
Understanding the lithology of an area helps in mineral exploration and resource management.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petrology - the branch of science concerned with the origin, structure, and composition of rocks

Etymology

From Greek lithos (stone) + -logia (study of).
