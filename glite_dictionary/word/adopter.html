adopter

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɑptər / (AmE), / əˈdɒptə / (BrE)
a person who adopts a child as his or her own
person who adopts a child

Examples

As an adopter, she understood the challenges of building a new family.
Open communication between adopters and birth parents can benefit the child.
The agency provides support groups for adopter families.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adoptee - a person who is adopted

Etymology

From adopt + -er, indicating a person who adopts.
