langset

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋsɛt / (AmE), / ˈlæŋsɛt / (BrE)
an East Indian tree that produces an edible yellow berry
East Indian tree with edible yellow fruit

Examples

The langset grows abundantly in the tropical forests of India.
She tasted the sweet yellow fruit of the langset for the first time.
Farmers harvest langsets during the late summer months.
