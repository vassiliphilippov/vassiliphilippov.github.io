gymslip

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɪmˌslɪp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪm.slɪp / (BrE)
a sleeveless tunic, usually with a pleated skirt, worn by schoolgirls in England as part of a school uniform
schoolgirls' sleeveless tunic (England)
british

Examples

She wore a crisp white blouse under her gymslip for the first day of school.
In the old photograph, every girl is dressed in a dark gymslip and long socks.
The tradition of the gymslip as school attire has largely disappeared from modern uniforms.

Etymology

Formed from 'gym' (gymnasium) and 'slip' (a type of garment), originally referring to sports attire.
