ketorolac tromethamine

1. NOUN

/ kɛˌtoʊrəˌlæk troʊˈmɛθəmiːn / (AmE), / kɛˌtɒrəlæk trəʊˈmiːθəmiːn / (BrE)
a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used for short-term management of moderate to severe pain, often administered intramuscularly or as an eye drop (brand names include Acular and Toradol)
nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used for pain management
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed ketorolac tromethamine for her postoperative pain.
Many patients experience relief after receiving an injection of ketorolac tromethamine.
Acular is the brand name for ketorolac tromethamine used in eyedrops.
