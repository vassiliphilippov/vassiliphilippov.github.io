self-education

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˌɛdjuˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of educating oneself through one’s own efforts, without formal instruction
the act of educating oneself

Examples

He devoted many years to self-education after dropping out of school.
The internet has made self-education more accessible than ever before.
Her success is largely due to her commitment to self-education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autodidacticism - the practice of self-directed learning

Etymology

From 'self-' (one's own person) + 'education' (from Latin *educatio*).
