spatterdock

1. NOUN

/ ˈspætɚˌdɑk / (AmE), / ˈspætəˌdɒk / (BrE)
A common water lily found in eastern and central North America, with broad leaves and rounded yellow flowers, growing in slow-moving or slightly brackish water.
North American water lily with yellow flowers

Examples

The pond was covered with thick leaves of spatterdock swaying gently in the breeze.
We paddled quietly through the still water, admiring the bright yellow blossoms of spatterdock.
Many ducks feed on the seeds and rhizomes of spatterdock in wetland areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yellow water lily - another name for spatterdock, a similar plant
