Al Jolson

1. PROPN

/ æl ˈdʒoʊlsən / (AmE), / æl ˈdʒəʊlsən / (BrE)
(1886–1950) A Russian-born American singer and entertainer, known for starring in the first full-length talking film, 'The Jazz Singer.'
Russian-born American singer and film star

Examples

Al Jolson is often credited as the world's greatest entertainer of his era.
The performance of Al Jolson in 'The Jazz Singer' marked a turning point in cinema history.
Many singers of the twentieth century cited Al Jolson as a major influence on their careers.
