baboon

1. NOUN

/ bəˈbun / (AmE), / bəˈbuːn / (BrE)
A large, ground-dwelling monkey of Africa and Arabia, with a doglike muzzle and large cheek pouches.
large ground-dwelling monkey with doglike muzzle

Examples

The baboon sat on a rock, closely watching the visitors at the zoo.
Researchers studied the social behavior of a troop of wild baboons in Kenya.
A baboon's powerful jaws are well adapted for eating tough plant material.

Etymology

From French babouin, possibly from Middle Dutch babūn or from baboue (old French for 'fool, simpleton') due to the animal's expressions.
