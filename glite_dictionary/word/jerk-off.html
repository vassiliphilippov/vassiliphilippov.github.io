jerk-off

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝk ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːk ˌɒf / (BrE)
A contemptible or foolish person (used as an insult); also, a slang term for someone who masturbates.
insulting slang for a contemptible person
slang vulgar offensive american

Examples

I can't believe what a jerk-off he was during the meeting.
Stop acting like a total jerk-off and help us out for once.
She called him a jerk-off after he made another rude comment.

Related Terms
