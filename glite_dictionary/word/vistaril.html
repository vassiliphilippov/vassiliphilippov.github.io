Vistaril

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪstərɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɪstərɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used as a tranquilizer and to treat anxiety and motion sickness.
trade name for an anti-anxiety and anti-nausea drug
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Vistaril to help control my anxiety symptoms.
The pharmacist explained the possible side effects of taking Vistaril for motion sickness.
Some patients find Vistaril effective for relieving itching caused by allergies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Atarax - trade name for the same drug, hydroxyzine
