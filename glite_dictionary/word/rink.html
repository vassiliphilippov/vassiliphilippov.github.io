rink

1. NOUN

/ rɪŋk / (AmE), / rɪŋk / (BrE)
A building or specially prepared area with a smooth surface for ice skating or roller skating.
skating surface or building

Examples

We spent the afternoon skating at the new indoor rink downtown.
The hockey team practiced at the local ice rink every morning.
Children gathered at the roller rink for a birthday party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arena - large enclosed area for sports or entertainment
  • Synonym: stadium - large structure for sports with seating

Etymology

From Scottish, originally meaning 'course' or 'track'.
