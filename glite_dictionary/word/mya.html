Mya

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.ə / (BrE)
A genus of marine bivalve mollusks in the family Myacidae, commonly known as soft-shell clams.
genus of marine clams

Examples

The soft-shell clam belongs to the genus Mya and is found in coastal waters worldwide.
Researchers collected several specimens of Mya from the mudflats for further study.
The Mya species are important both economically and ecologically in marine systems.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Myacidae - family of marine bivalve mollusks; family containing genus Mya

Etymology

From New Latin *Mya*, probably from Greek *mýa* (sea mussel).
