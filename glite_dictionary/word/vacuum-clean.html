to vacuum-clean

1. VERB

/ ˈvæk.juːn ˌklin / (AmE), / ˈvæk.juːm ˌkliːn / (BrE)
to clean something using a vacuum cleaner
clean using a vacuum cleaner

Examples

I need to vacuum-clean the living room before the guests arrive.
She vacuum-cleaned the entire house on Saturday morning.
Could you vacuum-clean the carpet while I dust the shelves?

Synset Examples

vacuum the carpets

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hoover - to clean using a vacuum cleaner (British usage)

Etymology

Formed from 'vacuum' (referring to the device) and 'clean'.
