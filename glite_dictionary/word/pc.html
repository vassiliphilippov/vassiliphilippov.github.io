PC

1. NOUN

/ piː ˈsiː / (AmE), / piː ˈsiː / (BrE)
a personal computer designed for use by one person at a time, typically small, digital, and based on a microprocessor
personal computer

Examples

My new PC has a faster processor and more memory than my old laptop.
Many offices provide a PC for each employee to complete their work.
He installed antivirus software on his PC to protect it from malware.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal computer - a computer intended for individual use

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'personal computer'.
