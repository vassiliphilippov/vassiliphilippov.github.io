Ghanaian

1. ADJ

/ ɡəˈneɪ.ən / (AmE), / ɡəˈneɪ.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Ghana, its people, or their language and culture
of or from Ghana or its people

Examples

Many Ghanaian artists have gained international recognition for their work.
The capital city, Accra, is the center of Ghanaian political and economic life.
She prepared a delicious Ghanaian meal for her friends.

Synset Examples

Ghanaian cocoa production

Etymology

From Ghana + English adjective suffix -ian, indicating affiliation or origin.

2. ADJ

/ ɡəˈneɪ.ən / (AmE), / ɡəˈneɪ.i.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Ghana or its people, language, or culture.
relating to Ghana or its people

Examples

The Ghanaian economy has grown rapidly in recent years.
She prepared a traditional Ghanaian dish for her friends.
A Ghanaian artist won an international award for his work.

Synset Examples

Ghanaian cocoa production

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Ghana - a country in West Africa

Etymology

From Ghana, the name of the West African country, with suffix -ian.
