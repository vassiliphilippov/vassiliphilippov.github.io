vesper

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛspər / (AmE), / ˈvɛspə / (BrE)
a late afternoon or evening worship service, especially in some Christian churches
evening worship service

Examples

The choir gathered for vesper at the old stone church.
She found comfort in attending the vesper service each Sunday evening.
A beautiful sunset illuminated the stained glass windows during vesper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evensong - evening prayer service, especially in the Anglican tradition

Etymology

From Latin 'vesper' meaning 'evening'.
