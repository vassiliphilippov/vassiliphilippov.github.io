ophthalmic

1. ADJ

/ ɑfˈθælmɪk / (AmE), / ɒfˈθalmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the eye or to the branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders
relating to the eye or ophthalmology
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed an ophthalmic ointment to treat the infection.
He specializes in ophthalmic surgery and treats various eye conditions.
New advances in ophthalmic imaging have improved early detection of diseases.

Synset Examples

ophthalmic surgery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocular - relating to the eye

Etymology

From Greek 'ophthalmos' (eye).
