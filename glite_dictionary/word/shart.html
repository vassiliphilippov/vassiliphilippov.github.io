to shart

1. VERB

/ ʃɑrt / (AmE), / ʃɑːt / (BrE)
to unintentionally defecate slightly while attempting to pass gas
accidentally defecate while farting (slang)
slang vulgar american

Examples

He tried to fart discreetly during the meeting, but unfortunately he sharted.
I laughed so hard that I almost sharted in my pants.
She worries every time she feels the urge, afraid she might shart again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fart - to expel gas from the anus
  • Synonym: to defecate - to discharge feces from the body

Etymology

Blend of 'shit' and 'fart' in American slang.
