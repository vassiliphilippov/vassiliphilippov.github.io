FARC

1. PROPN

/ fɑrk / (AmE), / fɑːk / (BrE)
a powerful Marxist guerrilla organization in Colombia, officially called the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia, known for its involvement in armed conflict and drug trafficking.
Colombian guerrilla organization

Examples

The Colombian government signed a peace agreement with the FARC after decades of conflict.
Reports indicate that the FARC financed its operations through drug trafficking and kidnapping.
Many rural communities were affected by fighting between the military and the FARC.

Etymology

Acronym of Spanish 'Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarias de Colombia' (Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia).
