blockhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɑːkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈblɒkˌhɛd / (BrE)
A person who is stupid or slow to understand things; a fool.
a stupid or foolish person

Examples

Don't be such a blockhead—read the instructions first!
He called me a blockhead for forgetting my keys again.
Only a real blockhead would believe that rumor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment; a simpleton
  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a very intelligent or talented person
