nutritionally

1. ADV

/ nuˈtrɪʃənəli / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In terms of nutrition; with regard to the nutritional value or effects of something.
with regard to nutrition

Examples

The meal was carefully planned to be nutritionally balanced for the children.
Nutritionally, fast food often lacks essential vitamins and minerals.
Her choices are not nutritionally sound, leading to poor health over time.

Synset Examples

nutritionally, her new diet is suicide

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritional - relating to nutrition or nourishment

Etymology

Formed from 'nutritional' + '-ly'.
