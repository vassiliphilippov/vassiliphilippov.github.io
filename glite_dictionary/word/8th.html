8th

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtθ / (BrE)
coming next after the seventh and just before the ninth in position; ordinal form of eight
ordinal number after seventh

Examples

This is the 8th time I've called you today.
He finished in 8th place in the race.
The meeting is scheduled for the 8th of September.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighth - the ordinal number corresponding to eight
  • Degree Lower: 7th - coming next after the sixth in position
  • Degree Higher: 9th - coming next after the eighth in position
