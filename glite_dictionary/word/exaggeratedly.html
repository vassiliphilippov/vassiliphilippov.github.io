exaggeratedly

1. ADV

/ ɪɡˈzædʒəˌreɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzædʒərətɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that makes something seem larger, better, worse, or more important than it really is.
in an exaggerated manner

Examples

She spoke exaggeratedly about how difficult her journey was.
He exaggeratedly sighed at the mention of extra work, making everyone laugh.
The actor gestured exaggeratedly to add humor to the performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overly - to an excessive degree
  • Antonym: understatedly - in a restrained or subtly effective manner
