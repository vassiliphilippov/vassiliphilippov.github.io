bonce

1. NOUN

/ bɑns / (AmE), / bɒns / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly British) a person's head.
informal term for the head
informal british

Examples

He hit his bonce on the door frame when he stood up too quickly.
Wear a helmet to protect your bonce while cycling.
She laughed so hard, she nearly fell and bumped her bonce on the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head - the upper part of the human body or the front part of an animal, containing the brain, eyes, ears, nose, and mouth
