wonderment

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚmənt / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəmənt / (BrE)
a feeling of surprise and admiration caused by something beautiful, unexpected, or unfamiliar
a feeling of amazement or awe

Examples

The children stared in wonderment at the magician's amazing tricks.
Her eyes were wide with wonderment as she gazed at the night sky.
He listened in wonderment to the incredible story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awe - a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder
  • Synonym: amazement - a feeling of great surprise or wonder
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From Middle English 'wonderment', formed from 'wonder' + '-ment'.
