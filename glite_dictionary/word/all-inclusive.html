all-inclusive

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔl ɪnˈkluːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɔːl ɪnˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
including everything or everyone; broad in scope or content.
including everything or everyone

Examples

The resort offers an all-inclusive holiday package that covers meals, drinks, and activities.
Their report provided an all-inclusive overview of the challenges facing the healthcare system.
We need an all-inclusive approach to address the problem, not just a partial solution.

2. ADJ

/ ˌɔl ɪnˈklusɪv / (AmE), / ˌɔːl ɪnˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
Including everything or all services, facilities, or costs in the total price.
including all services or costs

Examples

We booked an all-inclusive vacation package, so all meals and drinks are provided.
The all-inclusive price covers activities, entertainment, and transportation to the airport.
At this all-inclusive resort, you don't have to worry about any extra fees.

Synset Examples
