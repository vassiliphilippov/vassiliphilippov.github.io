skywards

1. ADV

/ ˈskaɪ.wɚdz / (AmE), / ˈskaɪ.wədz / (BrE)
In the direction of the sky; upward toward the sky.
toward the sky; upward

Examples

She glanced skywards as the first stars began to appear.
The rocket shot skywards with a powerful roar.
Birds circled skywards, disappearing into the clouds.

Synset Examples

look skywards!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upwards - toward a higher place or position
  • Antonym: downwards - toward a lower place or position

Etymology

Formed with 'sky' + '-wards', an adverbial suffix indicating direction.
