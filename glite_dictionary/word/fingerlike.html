fingerlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a finger
resembling a finger in shape

Examples

The coral has a fingerlike structure that reaches up toward the surface.
She noticed several fingerlike projections extending from the plant's main stem.
Clouds formed in fingerlike shapes across the early morning sky.
