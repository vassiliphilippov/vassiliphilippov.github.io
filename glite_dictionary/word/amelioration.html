amelioration

1. NOUN

/ əˌmiliəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌmiːliəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something better or less severe; improvement.
the act of making something better

Examples

Government initiatives led to significant amelioration of living conditions in the region.
The amelioration of air quality has become a top priority for city officials.
Her tireless efforts resulted in the amelioration of the school's reputation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the process of making something better

Etymology

From Latin 'amelioratio', from 'ameliorare' (to make better).
