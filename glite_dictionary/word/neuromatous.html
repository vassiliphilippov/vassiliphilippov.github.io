neuromatous

1. ADJ

/ nuˈroʊmətəs / (AmE), / njʊəˈrəʊmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a neuroma (a tumor growing from nerve tissue)
relating to neuromas
medical

Examples

Spreading pain in her foot was determined to be of neuromatous origin.
The surgeon removed the neuromatous tissue from the patient's hand.
Research into neuromatous growths has advanced treatments for nerve injuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neuroma - a benign tumor of nerve tissue

Etymology

Formed from 'neuroma' (from Greek 'neuron' meaning nerve + '-oma' meaning tumor) + the suffix '-ous'.
