good-naturedly

1. ADV

/ ɡʊdˈneɪtʃərdli / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈneɪtʃədli / (BrE)
in a kind, friendly, and pleasant manner
in a kind and friendly way

Examples

She smiled good-naturedly at his mistake, never losing her patience.
The students good-naturedly teased their teacher during the class party.
He accepted the joke good-naturedly and laughed along with everyone else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindly - in a kind or gentle manner
  • Synonym: graciously - in a courteous and kind way
