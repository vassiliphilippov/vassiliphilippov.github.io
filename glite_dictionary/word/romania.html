Romania

1. PROPN

/ roʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / rəˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
A country in southeastern Europe, bordered by Hungary, Serbia, Bulgaria, Ukraine, Moldova, and the Black Sea.
country in southeastern Europe

Examples

Romania joined the European Union in 2007.
The Carpathian Mountains run through the center of Romania.
Bucharest is the capital city of Romania.

Etymology

From Romanian 'România', derived from 'roman', referring to Roman heritage.
