insightfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪnˈsaɪtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪnˈsaɪtfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing deep understanding and perceptiveness
quality of being insightful

Examples

Her insightfulness impressed everyone at the meeting, as she offered perspectives nobody else had considered.
The author's insightfulness into human nature makes this novel especially compelling.
We appreciated his insightfulness when discussing complex political issues.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perceptiveness - the quality of being able to perceive or understand things quickly
  • Antonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity or perceptiveness
