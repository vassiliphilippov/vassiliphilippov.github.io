anti-white

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈwaɪt / (BrE)
Showing hostility, prejudice, or opposition towards white people or people of European descent.
hostile or prejudiced against white people

Examples

Many people criticized the policy as being anti-white and discriminatory.
He accused the organization of promoting an anti-white agenda in their outreach.
The film faced backlash for its alleged anti-white sentiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racist - showing prejudice or discrimination based on race
  • Antonym: pro-white - supporting or favoring white people or white interests

Etymology

Formed from 'anti-' (against) and 'white' (people of European descent).
