Sansevieria zeylanica

1. NOUN

/ sæn.səˈvɪə.ri.ə zeɪˈlæn.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / san.sɪˈvɪə.rɪ.ə zeɪˈlan.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
A tropical plant species with thick, fibrous leaves banded in light and dark green; commonly known as Ceylon bowstring hemp.
a plant with banded, fibrous leaves

Examples

The gardener recommended planting Sansevieria zeylanica because it thrives in low light.
Many interior designers use Sansevieria zeylanica to add greenery to offices and homes.
Fibers from Sansevieria zeylanica leaves are sometimes used to make rope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bowstring hemp - plant with fibrous leaves used for making bowstrings; common name for Sansevieria species

Etymology

Sansevieria named after Italian nobleman Raimondo di Sangro, Prince of San Severo; zeylanica refers to Ceylon (Sri Lanka).
