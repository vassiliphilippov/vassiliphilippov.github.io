Montenegro

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɑːntəˈneɪɡroʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɒntɪˈneɪɡrəʊ / (BrE)
A region and former country in southeastern Europe on the Adriatic Sea, which was a constituent of the former Union of Serbia and Montenegro; now an independent nation.
region and former country in southeastern Europe

Examples

After visiting Montenegro, she was captivated by its stunning coastal scenery.
The political history of Montenegro is complex, especially during the twentieth century.
He researched the breakup of the former Yugoslavia, including Montenegro's status changes.

Etymology

From Venetian Italian *monte negro*, meaning 'black mountain'.
