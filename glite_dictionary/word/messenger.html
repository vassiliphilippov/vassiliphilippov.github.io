messenger

1. NOUN

/ ˈmesəndʒər / (AmE), / ˈmesɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person who carries and delivers messages, packages, or documents to others
person who carries messages

Examples

The company hired a messenger to deliver important documents across town.
In ancient times, a messenger would travel for days to bring news between cities.
She handed the envelope to the messenger and asked him to deliver it quickly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: courier - a person who transports documents or goods; often used for urgent delivery

Etymology

From Old French messagier, from message (message).
