Synercus

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈnɜːrkəs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɜːkəs / (BrE)
A genus of African buffalo, specifically referring to the Cape buffalo.
African genus of buffalos (Cape buffalo)

Examples

The Synercus is known for its unpredictable temperament and impressive horns.
Herds of Synercus roam the savannahs of sub-Saharan Africa.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of Synercus in the wild.
