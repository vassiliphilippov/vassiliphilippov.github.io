to deice

1. VERB

/ diˈaɪs / (AmE), / diːˈaɪs / (BrE)
to remove ice from something, especially a surface such as a vehicle or an aircraft
remove ice from a surface

Examples

Before takeoff, the crew had to deice the airplane's wings to ensure safety.
He used a special spray to deice the front steps after last night's snowstorm.
The city quickly dispatched trucks to deice the main roads during the cold snap.

Synset Examples

Defrost the car window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to defrost - to remove frost or ice, especially by warming

Etymology

Formed from "de-" (removal) + "ice".
