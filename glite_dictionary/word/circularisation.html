circularisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɜrkjələrɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkjələraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of distributing printed notices or circulars, especially as a form of advertising or communication to a group
distribution of advertising circulars
british

Examples

The company's circularisation campaign reached thousands of potential customers across the region.
Many non-profit organizations rely on circularisation to inform supporters about upcoming events.
In the past, circularisation was a common way for businesses to advertise new products.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circularization - the act of distributing printed notices; US spelling
  • Synonym: mailing - sending printed material to many people by post

Etymology

From 'circular' (notice or letter sent to many people) + '-isation' (forming nouns indicating action).
