pointless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɔɪnt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪnt.ləs / (BrE)
Having no purpose or use; serving no useful purpose.
having no purpose or use

Examples

Arguing with him often feels pointless because he never listens to reason.
We decided to cancel the meeting since it seemed pointless to continue.
Writing more emails is pointless if they never reply.

Synset Examples

otiose lines in a play
advice is wasted words
a pointless remark
a life essentially purposeless
senseless violence

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meaningful - having meaning or purpose
  • Synonym: purposeless - lacking purpose or aim

Etymology

Formed from 'point' + '-less', meaning 'without point or purpose'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈpɔɪntləs / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntləs / (BrE)
(of an object) lacking a point or sharp tip.
lacking a point or sharp tip

Examples

All the knives in this drawer are completely pointless and need sharpening.
The pointless arrow could not pierce the target as intended.
After sharpening one pencil, I realized the rest were still pointless.

Synset Examples

my pencils are all pointless

Etymology

From the noun 'point' + suffix '-less' meaning 'without'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-01816789-a
Synset POS: a
