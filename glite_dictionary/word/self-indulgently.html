self-indulgently

1. ADV

/ ˌsɛlf.ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.ɪnˈdʌl.dʒənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that allows oneself to have or do what one wants, especially when it is not good for oneself.
in a self-indulgent way

Examples

She spent the afternoon self-indulgently watching movies and eating chocolate.
He self-indulgently ignored his responsibilities to pursue his hobbies.
The novel is written self-indulgently, with lengthy descriptions of the writer's own thoughts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-indulgent - allowing oneself excessive pleasure or luxury
