Homeric

1. ADJ

/ hoʊˈmɛrɪk / (AmE), / həʊˈmɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the ancient Greek poet Homer, his era, or the works attributed to him, such as the Iliad or the Odyssey.
of or about Homer or his works

Examples

She specializes in Homeric literature and ancient Greek poetry.
The Homeric epics have influenced countless writers throughout history.
He recited the lines in Homeric Greek, impressing his classmates.

Synset Examples

Homeric Greek

Etymology

From Greek Hōmērikos, from Hómēros (Homer).
