dislocated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɪsloʊˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɪsləʊkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a joint) forced out of its normal position in the body, so that the bones no longer connect properly.
joint forced from normal position
medical

Examples

He was unable to walk because of his dislocated ankle.
A dislocated shoulder requires immediate medical attention.
She wore a sling after suffering a dislocated elbow during the match.

Synset Examples

a dislocated knee
a separated shoulder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to dislocate - to put a bone or joint out of its normal position

Etymology

From Latin 'dislocare' (to put out of place).
