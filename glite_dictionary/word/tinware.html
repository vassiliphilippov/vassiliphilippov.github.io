tinware

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪnˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈtɪnˌweə / (BrE)
household or commercial articles and utensils made of tin plate.
articles made of tin plate

Examples

My grandmother still uses antique tinware in her kitchen.
Collectors often seek out old tinware at flea markets and antique shops.
The vendor displayed his handmade tinware on a wooden table at the fair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silverware - dishes and utensils made of silver
  • Synonym: earthenware - pottery made of clay

Etymology

From 'tin' + 'ware', patterned after similar compounds like 'ironware'.
