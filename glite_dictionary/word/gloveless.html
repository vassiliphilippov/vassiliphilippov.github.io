gloveless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlʌvləs / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌvləs / (BrE)
Not wearing gloves; lacking gloves.
without gloves

Examples

He shook my hand with his gloveless fingers, despite the cold weather.
The surgeon could not continue the procedure as he was gloveless.
Her gloveless hands were red from hours spent outside in winter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gloved - wearing gloves

Etymology

Formed from 'glove' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
