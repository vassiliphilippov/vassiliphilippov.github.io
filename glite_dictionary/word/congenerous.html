congenerous

1. ADJ

/ kənˈdʒɛnərəs / (AmE), / kɒnˈdʒɛnərəs / (BrE)
Belonging to the same genus, especially of species in biological classification.
of the same genus (biology)

Examples

The two bird species are congenerous and often mistaken for one another in the wild.
Several congenerous plants share similar medicinal properties.
Entomologists studied congenerous beetles collected from the same habitat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congeneric - of the same genus (biology), variant form

Etymology

From Latin 'congener' (of the same race or kind) + '-ous'.
