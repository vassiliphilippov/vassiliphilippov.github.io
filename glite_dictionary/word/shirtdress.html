shirtdress

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝtˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌdrɛs / (BrE)
A woman's dress styled after a shirt, typically with a collar and buttons down the front.
woman's dress styled like a shirt

Examples

She wore a crisp white shirtdress to the summer party.
This blue shirtdress is perfect for both the office and casual outings.
Many designers featured the shirtdress in their spring collections this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirtwaist - a kind of women's blouse or dress with a tailored shirt-like style

Etymology

Formed from 'shirt' + 'dress', reflecting its shirt-like style.
