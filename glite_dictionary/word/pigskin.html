pigskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡ.skɪn / (BrE)
leather made from the skin of a pig
leather from a pig's skin

Examples

The boots were made of pigskin and proved to be very durable.
He admired the smooth texture of the pigskin wallet.
Old footballs were traditionally made from pigskin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leather - material made by tanning animal hides
