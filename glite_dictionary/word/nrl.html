NRL

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɑr ɛl / (AmE), / ɛn ɑːl ɛl / (BrE)
the United States Naval Research Laboratory, the primary scientific research facility for the U.S. Navy, conducting basic and applied research in a range of scientific and technical fields.
U.S. Navy scientific research laboratory
american

Examples

The NRL has been instrumental in developing new radar technologies for the Navy.
After his PhD, he began working at the NRL on advanced propulsion systems.
Many important discoveries in atmospheric science have come out of the NRL over the years.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Naval Research Laboratory.
