scunge

1. NOUN

/ skʌndʒ / (AmE), / skʌndʒ / (BrE)
an informal term for a stingy or miserly person
stingy or miserly person (informal)
informal

Examples

My brother can be a real scunge when it comes to splitting bills.
Don’t be such a scunge—it’s just a few dollars for coffee.
Everyone calls him a scunge because he never chips in for group gifts.

Synset Examples

Stop being such a scunge and share your snacks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little as possible
  • Antonym: spendthrift - a person who spends money recklessly
