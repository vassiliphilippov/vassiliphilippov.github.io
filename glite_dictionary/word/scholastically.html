scholastically

1. ADV

/ skəˈlæstɪkli / (AmE), / skəˈlæstɪkəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to education or academic activities
in relation to academic matters

Examples

He performed scholastically better after switching schools.
Although she excelled athletically, she struggled scholastically.
Awards are given to students who have achieved scholastically throughout the year.

Synset Examples

scholastically apt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academically - in a way that relates to education or scholarship

Etymology

From scholastic, from Latin 'scholasticus', meaning 'of schools'.
