milkcap

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌkap / (BrE)
An edible mushroom of the genus Lactarius, which exudes a milky liquid when cut.
type of edible mushroom

Examples

We found several milkcaps growing under the pine trees in the forest.
The chef added slices of milkcap to the creamy mushroom soup for extra flavor.
Some species of milkcap are highly prized by mushroom collectors.
