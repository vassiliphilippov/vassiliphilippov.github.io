unmatched

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmætʃt / (BrE)
so good or impressive that no one or nothing else is equal to it; without an equal or rival; unparalleled
without equal or rival; unparalleled

Examples

Her unmatched dedication to the project ensured its success.
The chef is renowned for an unmatched sense of flavor pairing.
That athlete's speed and skill are simply unmatched in the world of sports.

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmatʃt / (BrE)
(of an item) being the only remaining member of a pair; not having its corresponding counterpart.
a single item from a pair

Examples

When I finished doing laundry, I found one unmatched sock at the bottom of the basket.
The drawer is full of unmatched gloves and mittens from past winters.
She tossed the unmatched earring into a small box, hoping to find its pair one day.

Synset Examples

unpaired socks
an odd glove

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpaired - not forming one of a pair
  • Antonym: matched - having a corresponding item to form a pair
