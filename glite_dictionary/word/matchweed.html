matchweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætʃˌwid / (AmE), / ˈmatʃˌwiːd / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Gutierrezia, with small yellow flower heads that resemble matches
plant of the genus Gutierrezia

Examples

The fields were dotted with matchweed, their tiny yellow heads brightening the landscape.
Ranchers often consider matchweed to be a troublesome weed in grazing areas.
After the rain, patches of matchweed quickly sprang up along the roadside.
