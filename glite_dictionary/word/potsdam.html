Potsdam

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɑts.dæm / (AmE), / ˈpɒts.dam / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Germany, known as the site of the Potsdam Conference in 1945.
city in northeastern Germany

Examples

The Potsdam Conference, held in Potsdam, was a key event in shaping post-World War II Europe.
Many tourists visit Potsdam to see its palaces and beautiful parks.
She studied German history with a focus on the events that took place in Potsdam in 1945.
