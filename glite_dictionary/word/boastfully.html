boastfully

1. ADV

/ ˈboʊstfəlli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊstfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities.
in a boastful manner

Examples

He spoke boastfully about his recent promotion at work.
She described her accomplishments boastfully, impressing some and annoying others.
The athlete boastfully declared he was the best in the league.

Synset Examples

he talked big all evening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proudly - in a proud manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a modest manner, not boastfully

Etymology

From 'boastful' + '-ly' suffix.
