November

1. PROPN

/ noʊˈvɛmbɚ / (AmE), / nəʊˈvɛmbə / (BrE)
The eleventh month of the year in the Gregorian calendar, following October and preceding December.
eleventh month of the year

Examples

Her birthday is in November, so she always hopes for cool autumn weather.
The election was held in November, as is tradition in many countries.
By November, the days had grown much shorter and colder.

Etymology

From Latin 'November', from 'novem' meaning 'nine', as it was the ninth month in the Roman calendar.
