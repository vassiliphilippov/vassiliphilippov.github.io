sexting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkstɪŋ / (BrE)
the sending of sexually explicit messages, images, or videos via mobile phone, computer, or other electronic devices
sending sexually explicit electronic messages
informal

Examples

Parents should talk to teenagers about the risks associated with sexting.
Several students were suspended after allegations of sexting surfaced at the school.
The law treats sexting among minors as a serious matter in many countries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: texting - sending written messages electronically, especially by mobile phone

Etymology

Blend of 'sex' and 'texting', first recorded in the early 21st century.
