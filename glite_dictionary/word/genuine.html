genuine

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛnjuɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnjuːɪn / (BrE)
truly what something is said to be; authentic and not fake or counterfeit.
authentic; not fake or counterfeit

Examples

The art expert confirmed that the painting was a genuine Rembrandt.
Only genuine leather was used in the seats of the luxury car.
She was relieved to find that her diamond ring was genuine and not an imitation.

Synset Examples

a genuine Picasso
genuine leather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine
  • Antonym: fake - not real; made to look like something else

Etymology

From Latin genuinus (innate, native, natural).

2. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛnjuɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnjʊɪn / (BrE)
truly what something is said to be; authentic or real, not false or artificial.
real, authentic, not fake

Examples

She was relieved to know his apology was genuine and heartfelt.
This handbag is made from genuine leather, not an imitation.
We had a genuine problem to solve, not just a minor inconvenience.

Synset Examples

her actual motive
a literal solitude like a desert
a genuine dilemma

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine
  • Antonym: fake - not genuine; a counterfeit or imitation

Etymology

From Latin 'genuinus' (innate, natural), from 'gignere' (to beget).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02470192-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛnjuɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnjʊɪn / (BrE)
(of a feeling, emotion, or expression) truly felt or sincerely expressed; not pretended or insincere.
truly felt; not pretended

Examples

You could tell from her smile that her happiness was genuine.
The apology sounded genuine and heartfelt rather than forced.
He showed genuine surprise when he received the award.

Synset Examples

genuine emotion
her interest in people was unfeigned
true grief

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sincere - free from pretense or deceit; genuine
  • Antonym: fake - not genuine; artificial or imitation

Etymology

From Latin genuinus (innate, native, natural).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02187907-s
Synset POS: s
