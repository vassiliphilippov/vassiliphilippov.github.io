Libreville

1. PROPN

/ liːˈbrɛvɪl / (AmE), / liːˈbrɛvɪl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Gabon, located on the northwest coast of Central Africa
capital city of Gabon

Examples

Many tourists visit Libreville to explore its vibrant markets and coastal views.
The embassy is located in the heart of Libreville.
After moving to Libreville, she quickly adapted to city life.

Etymology

From French 'Libre' (free) and 'ville' (city), referring to its founding by freed slaves.
