grown

1. ADJ

/ ɡroʊn / (AmE), / ɡrəʊn / (BrE)
(of animals or people) having reached full development; mature.
fully developed; mature (animals or people)

Examples

My cat is now a grown animal and no longer likes to play with kittens.
Only grown tigers are kept in the main enclosure at the sanctuary.
She adopted a grown dog from the animal shelter instead of a puppy.

Synset Examples

an adult animal
a grown woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adult - a fully developed and mature animal
  • Antonym: juvenile - not fully developed; young animal

Etymology

Past participle of 'grow,' from Old English 'grōwan'.
