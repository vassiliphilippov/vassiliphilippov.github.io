laughingly

1. ADV

/ ˈlæfɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɑːfɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner characterized by laughter or as if laughing
in a laughing manner

Examples

She dismissed his concerns laughingly, finding them too trivial to worry about.
He laughingly agreed to join the impromptu dance in the park.
After forgetting the answer, she laughingly admitted her mistake to the class.

Synset Examples

he told the story laughingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner, without joking or laughter
  • Synonym: jokingly - in a humorous or teasing manner, often not meant to be taken seriously
