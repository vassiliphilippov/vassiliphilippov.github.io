thud

1. VERB

/ θʌd / (AmE), / θʌd / (BrE)
to strike or fall with a dull, heavy sound
strike or fall with a dull sound

Examples

The rock thudded to the ground, sending up a small cloud of dust.
Heavy boots thudded across the wooden floor above us.
Raindrops thudded against the window as the storm intensified.

Synset Examples

Bullets were thudding against the wall

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bang - to strike or hit something with a sudden loud noise
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: v

2. NOUN

/ θʌd / (AmE), / θʌd / (BrE)
a dull, heavy sound made by the impact of a heavy object
dull, heavy impact sound

Examples

The door closed with a loud thud behind him.
She fell to the ground with a thud as the crowd gasped.
A heavy book landed on the floor with a soft thud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thump - a heavy dull sound, often from something hitting a surface
  • Degree Higher: bang - a loud, sudden noise, often made by something hitting a surface
  • Degree Lower: tap - a light or gentle sound of a slight blow
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-07411545-n
Synset POS: n

3. VERB

/ θʌd / (AmE), / θʌd / (BrE)
(of an object or mechanism) to make a dull, heavy sound, especially one suggesting lack of lubrication or rough movement.
make a dull, heavy sound

Examples

The old car began to thud as it sputtered down the road.
The engine would thud loudly whenever the oil ran low.
As they forgot to lubricate the machine, it started to thud and grind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thump - to make a heavy, muffled sound by hitting something heavily
  • Antonym: clatter - to make a loud, rattling sound
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-02189243-v
Synset POS: v

4. VERB

/ θʌd / (AmE), / θʌd / (BrE)
to make or cause to make a dull, heavy sound, especially when falling or being struck
make a dull, heavy sound

Examples

The book thudded to the floor, startling everyone in the room.
Heavy raindrops thudded against the windows throughout the night.
Her heart thudded in her chest as she waited for the results.

Synset Examples

the knocker thudded against the front door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thump - make a dull, heavy sound by striking heavily
  • Degree Higher: bang - make a sudden loud, sharp noise
  • Degree Lower: tap - make a light, gentle sound when hitting or touching something

Etymology

Imitative of the sound itself, first recorded in the early 16th century.
