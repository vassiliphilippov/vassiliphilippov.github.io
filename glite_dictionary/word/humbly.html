humbly

1. ADV

/ ˈhʌmbli / (AmE), / ˈhʌmbli / (BrE)
In a humble manner; showing or expressing humility or modesty.
in a humble manner

Examples

She humbly accepted the award, thanking everyone who supported her.
He humbly admitted his mistake during the meeting.
The monk humbly requested food from the villagers.

Synset Examples

he humbly lowered his head

Related Terms

  • Antonym: proudly - in a proud manner; with pride
  • Synonym: modestly - in a modest manner; with humility

Etymology

Formed from 'humble' + '-ly'. 'Humble' from Latin 'humilis' (low, lowly, humble).
