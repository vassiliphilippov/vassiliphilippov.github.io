Bole

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊleɪ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊleɪ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in northern Nigeria, closely related to Hausa, with dialects such as Bara and Fika
a Chadic language of northern Nigeria

Examples

Many linguists have studied the Bole language and its relationship to Hausa.
The Bole community preserves their cultural heritage through storytelling and traditional songs.
Several villages in Yobe State use Bole as their primary language of communication.
