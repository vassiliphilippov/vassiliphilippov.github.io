horse-head

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌhɛd / (BrE)
A common name for several silvery marine fishes with very flat bodies.
a marine fish with a very flat body

Examples

The horse-head is often found in coastal waters throughout the tropics.
Fishermen value the horse-head for its mild flavor and delicate flesh.
We spotted a school of horse-heads gliding gracefully near the coral reef.
