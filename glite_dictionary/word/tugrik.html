tugrik

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːɡrɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʊɡrɪk / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Mongolia
currency of Mongolia

Examples

The price was listed as 10,000 tugrik per night at the hotel.
After exchanging dollars, she received several crisp tugriks at the bank.
The tugrik has fluctuated in value against other currencies this year.

Etymology

From Mongolian төгрөг (tögreg), meaning 'circle' or 'circular', referring to coins.
