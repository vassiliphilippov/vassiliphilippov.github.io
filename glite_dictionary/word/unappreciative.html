unappreciative

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃət̬ɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpriː.ʃət.ɪv / (BrE)
not showing or feeling gratitude or recognition for something; not appreciative.
not showing gratitude; not appreciative

Examples

She felt hurt when her efforts went unappreciative by her colleagues.
Many children are unappreciative of the sacrifices their parents make for them.
The manager considered his staff unappreciative of the resources provided.

Synset Examples

unappreciative of nature's bounty

Related Terms

  • Antonym: appreciative - showing gratitude or recognition

Etymology

Formed by addition of the prefix un- (not) to 'appreciative'.
