inamorata

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.ə.məˈrɑː.tə / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ə.məˈrɑː.tə / (BrE)
A woman with whom a person is in love or has an intimate relationship.
a woman lover or romantic interest

Examples

He wrote countless poems dedicated to his inamorata, pouring out his heart in every verse.
The artist's inamorata was a frequent subject in his paintings.
She confided to her friend about meeting her new inamorata over the summer.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inamorato - a man with whom a person is in love
  • Synonym: lover - a person with whom one has a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

From Italian 'innamorata', feminine of 'innamorato', from 'innamorare' (to inspire love).
