to spot-check

1. VERB

/ spɑt tʃɛk / (AmE), / spɒt tʃɛk / (BrE)
To select and examine random samples from a group to ensure quality or compliance.
select random samples for inspection

Examples

The safety officer will spot-check the equipment to ensure everything is working properly.
They spot-checked several batches of products to maintain quality standards.
Every month, auditors spot-check random files to verify compliance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audit - to examine accounts or records officially
  • Synonym: to inspect - to look at something closely to assess its condition
