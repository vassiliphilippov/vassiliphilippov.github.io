ptyalism

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtɪəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
Excessive or abnormal production of saliva.
excessive flow of saliva
medical

Examples

The patient developed ptyalism as a side effect of the medication.
Persistent ptyalism can be uncomfortable and socially embarrassing.
Some neurological conditions are associated with increased ptyalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salivation - the process of producing saliva

Etymology

From Greek ptuālisma (salivation), from ptuō (to spit).
