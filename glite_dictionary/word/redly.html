redly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛdli / (AmE), / ˈrɛdli / (BrE)
In a red manner; with a red color or glow.
in a red manner or color

Examples

The sunset glowed redly behind the mountains, painting the clouds with vivid hues.
Embers from the campfire pulsed redly in the darkness as we told stories.
His answer made her cheeks burn redly with embarrassment.

Synset Examples

the forge belched redly at the sky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddishly - in a reddish manner or with a tinge of red

Etymology

Formed from 'red' + adverbial suffix '-ly.'
