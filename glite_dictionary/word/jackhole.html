jackhole

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒækˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk.həʊl / (BrE)
An insulting slang term for a rude or unpleasant person.
rude or mean person (slang insult)
informal slang

Examples

Don't be such a jackhole when someone asks for help.
She called him a jackhole after he made that comment.
Some people act like a jackhole just to get attention.

Synset Examples

He's such a jackhole for not helping his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
  • Synonym: asshole - a very rude or unpleasant person (vulgar slang)
