unreproducible

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ri.prəˈduː.sə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ri.prəˈdjuː.sɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to reproduce or duplicate.
impossible to reproduce or duplicate

Examples

The scientist realized her experiment was unreproducible due to missing data records.
After several failed attempts, the unique results were deemed unreproducible.
The artist's technique created effects that are essentially unreproducible by machines.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reproducible - able to be reproduced or duplicated

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'reproducible' (capable of being reproduced).
