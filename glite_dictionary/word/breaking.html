breaking

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of causing something to separate into pieces or become damaged
the act of causing something to break

Examples

The breaking of the vase startled everyone in the room.
New safety procedures aim to prevent the breaking of equipment during transport.
After the breaking of the silence, the discussion began.

Synset Examples

the breakage was unavoidable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakage - the action or result of breaking; something that has been broken
