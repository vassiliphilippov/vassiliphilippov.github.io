inspired

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈspaɪɚd / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪəd / (BrE)
of such surpassing excellence or creativity as to seem influenced by a higher power or extreme inspiration
of surpassing excellence or creativity

Examples

The conductor gave an inspired interpretation of the symphony that left the audience spellbound.
Her inspired painting captured the beauty and emotion of the moment perfectly.
The chef's inspired menu turned an ordinary dinner into an unforgettable experience.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

From Latin inspirare (to breathe into); originally with the sense of divine influence or guidance.
