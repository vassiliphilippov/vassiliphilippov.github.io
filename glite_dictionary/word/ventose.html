Ventose

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛntoʊz / (AmE), / ˈvɛntəʊz / (BrE)
the sixth month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding to late February and most of March, known as the windy month
sixth month of the French Revolutionary calendar

Examples

Many important events of the Revolution occurred during Ventose.
The law was enacted on the fifteenth of Ventose, Year III of the Revolutionary calendar.
Farmers dreaded the unpredictable storms frequent in Ventose.

Etymology

French 'Ventôse', from 'vent' (wind), reflecting the windy weather typical of the period.
