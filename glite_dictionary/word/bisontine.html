bisontine

/ baɪˈsɑnˌtaɪn / (AmE), / bɪˈsɒnˌtiːn / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of bison
relating to bison

Examples

The museum displayed a bisontine skull alongside other prehistoric animals.
His bisontine strength was legendary among his peers.

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from 'bison' with the suffix '-ine' (from Latin -inus, meaning 'of or pertaining to').
