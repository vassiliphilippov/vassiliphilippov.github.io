endearment

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdɪrmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪəmənt / (BrE)
the act of showing affection or love toward someone
expression of affection or love

Examples

His words of endearment made her smile.
The couple exchanged endearments before saying goodbye.
A gentle touch can be an act of endearment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking

Etymology

From endear + -ment, influenced by the verb 'endear' (to inspire love or affection).
