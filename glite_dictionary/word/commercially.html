commercially

1. ADV

/ kəˈmɝː.ʃə.li / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəli / (BrE)
In a manner related to commerce or business, especially with regard to profit or commercial success.
in a business-related way

Examples

The movie was commercially successful despite poor critical reviews.
This technology is not yet commercially viable, but research is ongoing.
He produces only what can be commercially marketed to consumers.

Synset Examples

the product is commercially available

Related Terms

  • Antonym: noncommercially - in a way not intended for commercial gain

Etymology

From "commercial" + "-ly", ultimately from Latin "commercium" (trade, commerce).
