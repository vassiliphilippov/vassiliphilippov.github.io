harmonical

1. ADJ

/ hɑrˈmɑnɪkəl / (AmE), / hɑːˈmɒnɪkəl / (BrE)
involving or characterized by harmony, especially in music or composition
relating to harmony
archaic

Examples

The harmonical arrangement of the choir filled the cathedral with beautiful resonance.
Their collaboration resulted in a harmonical blending of visual art and music.
Early theorists wrote treatises on the harmonical properties of sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonic - relating to or characterized by harmony; alternative modern term

Etymology

Formed from 'harmony' + '-ical'; related to Medieval Latin 'harmonicus' (harmonious).
