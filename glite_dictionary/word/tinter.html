tinter

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪntɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪntə / (BrE)
a person, especially a hairdresser, who applies tint or color to hair
person who tints hair

Examples

Rachel works as a tinter at the local salon, expertly transforming clients' hair color.
The tinter recommended a subtle shade that would complement my natural color.
After years as a junior stylist, she became a skilled tinter known for her creativity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colorist - someone who specializes in applying color, especially to hair
