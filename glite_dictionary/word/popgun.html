popgun

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɒpˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈpɒpˌɡʌn / (BrE)
A toy gun that makes a popping sound when fired, often used by children.
toy gun that makes a pop sound

Examples

The child aimed his popgun at the imaginary villain and shouted with glee.
She found her old popgun in the attic, still able to make that familiar sound.
At the party, the guests were startled when a popgun went off unexpectedly.
