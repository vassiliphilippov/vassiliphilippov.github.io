tightening

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something tighter or more secure
the act of making tighter

Examples

The recent tightening of regulations has affected many industries.
There was a noticeable tightening of security at the airport after the incident.
The tightening of the bolts ensured the engine was properly secured.

Synset Examples

the tightening of economic controls

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loosening - the act of making something less tight
