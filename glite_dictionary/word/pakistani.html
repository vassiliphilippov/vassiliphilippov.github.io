Pakistani

1. NOUN

/ ˌpækɪˈstɑni / (AmE), / ˌpakɪˈstɑːni / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Pakistan
native or inhabitant of Pakistan

Examples

Several Pakistanis have won international awards for their achievements in science and technology.
The festival was organized by the local Pakistani community.
She is proud to be a Pakistani living abroad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pakistan - a country in South Asia

Etymology

From Pakistan + -i, forming demonyms for people from a place.

Examples

Many Pakistani dishes are famous for their rich spices and flavors.
