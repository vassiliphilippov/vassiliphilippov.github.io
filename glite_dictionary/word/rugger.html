rugger

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡə / (BrE)
(British, informal) Rugby football; a form of football played with an oval ball, especially as played in schools.
British informal term for rugby football
informal british

Examples

Tom played rugger throughout his years at boarding school.
Many consider rugger to be one of the toughest team sports.
After the rugger match, the players celebrated together at the pub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rugby - A form of football played with an oval ball and H-shaped goals

Etymology

British informal abbreviation of 'rugby (football)'.
