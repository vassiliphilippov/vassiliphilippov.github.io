armour-clad

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrmər klæd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə klæd / (BrE)
covered or protected with heavy steel or armor plating
covered with heavy steel
british

Examples

The massive armour-clad battleship loomed on the horizon.
Engineers designed an armour-clad vehicle to withstand heavy artillery fire.
The ancient fortress stood armour-clad against every invading army.

Synset Examples

armor-plated vehicles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armor-plated - covered with a layer of metal for protection

Etymology

Formed from 'armour' (Middle English from Old French 'armeure') and 'clad' (past participle of 'clothe').
