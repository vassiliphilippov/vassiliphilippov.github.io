bismark

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪzˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbɪzˌmɑːk / (BrE)
A raised doughnut filled with jelly or jam.
doughnut filled with jelly or jam

Examples

I bought a bismark with raspberry filling from the bakery this morning.
The cafe's signature pastry is a bismark dusted with powdered sugar.
She prefers a bismark over a plain doughnut because of the sweet jam inside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jelly doughnut - A doughnut filled with jelly, similar in preparation to a bismark

Etymology

Named after Otto von Bismarck, German statesman; applied in North America to this pastry.
