jimdandy

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (BrE)
A person or thing considered to be outstanding or excellent of their kind.
someone or something excellent
informal american

Examples

The new car is a real jimdandy when it comes to performance.
Everyone agreed that the pie she baked was a jimdandy.
He considered himself a jimdandy at fixing things around the house.

Synset Examples

he's a jimdandy of a soldier

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress; also outstanding thing or person
  • Synonym: paragon - a person or thing viewed as a model of excellence

Etymology

Probably from the given name Jim plus dandy (meaning an excellent thing or person).

2. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪmˈdændi / (BrE)
A person or thing that is excellent or outstanding of its kind.
something or someone excellent
informal slang

Examples

That new coffee maker is a real jimdandy for busy mornings.
Her idea turned out to be a jimdandy solution to the problem.
He bought a jimdandy of a vintage car at the auction.

Synset Examples

the bike was a jimdandy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - something or someone excellent or first-rate
  • Synonym: marvel - something that causes wonder or astonishment

Etymology

From the personal name Jim Dandy, popularized by a 19th-century American song ‘Jim Dandy to the Rescue’.
