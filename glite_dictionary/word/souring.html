souring

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaʊr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming sour, especially in food or other substances
process of becoming sour

Examples

During the souring of milk, bacteria convert lactose into lactic acid.
The souring of the wine was accelerated by poor storage conditions.
He monitored the souring carefully to ensure the yogurt would have the right flavor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fermentation - the process of chemical breakdown, often producing sourness
  • Antonym: sweetening - the process of becoming sweeter
