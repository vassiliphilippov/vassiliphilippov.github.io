contractual

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtræktʃuəl / (AmE), / kənˈtræktʃu.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or arising from a contract, especially in law; based on or governed by the terms of a contract.
relating to a contract or legal agreement
legal

Examples

All employees must follow their contractual obligations.
The two companies have a contractual arrangement to share resources.
She is entitled to severance pay according to her contractual rights.

Synset Examples

contractual obligations

Related Terms

  • Antonym: noncontractual - not based on or not included in a contract

Etymology

From contract + -ual, ultimately from Latin contractus (drawn together, agreement).
