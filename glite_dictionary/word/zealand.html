Zealand

1. PROPN

/ ˈziː.lənd / (AmE), / ˈziː.lənd / (BrE)
the largest island of Denmark, east of the mainland, and the site of Copenhagen, the capital city
largest island of Denmark; site of Copenhagen

Examples

Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark, is located on Zealand.
She traveled from Jutland to Zealand to visit the famous castles.
Many Danes live on Zealand, making it the most populous island in the country.
