appetite

1. NOUN

/ ˈæpɪˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈæpɪtʌɪt / (BrE)
a natural desire or craving, especially for food or other particular experiences or things
desire or craving, especially for food

Examples

She lost her appetite after hearing the bad news.
His appetite for adventure led him to travel the world.
A good walk before dinner can increase your appetite.

Synset Examples

an appetite for life
the object of life is to satisfy as many appetencies as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunger - the physical feeling of needing food
  • Antonym: aversion - a strong dislike for something, opposite of desire

Etymology

From Latin appetitus (desire for), from appetere (to seek after, desire).
