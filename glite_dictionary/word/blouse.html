blouse

1. NOUN

/ blaʊs / (AmE), / blaʊz / (BrE)
A woman's loose-fitting upper garment, typically with a collar, buttons, and sleeves.
a woman's loose-fitting upper garment

Examples

She wore a white blouse with a bright red skirt to the party.
The blouse has delicate embroidery on the sleeves and collar.
After work, she changed out of her business blouse and into something more casual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shirt - a garment worn on the upper body, usually with a collar, sleeves, and buttons

Etymology

From French 'blouse' (worker's smock).
