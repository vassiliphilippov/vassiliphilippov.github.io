featheriness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛðəri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɛðəri.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being light and soft, resembling a feather
light, soft quality like a feather

Examples

The featheriness of the scarf made it perfect for spring mornings.
She admired the featheriness of the cake, which melted in her mouth.
With its featheriness, the pillow provided a gentle rest for her head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: softness - the quality of being soft
  • Antonym: hardness - the quality of being hard

Etymology

From 'feather' + '-iness', forming a noun describing the quality of being like a feather.
