pediatric

1. ADJ

/ ˌpiːdiˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪˈatrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the medical care or treatment of children.
related to children's healthcare
medical american

Examples

She took her daughter to a pediatric clinic for a checkup.
Advances in pediatric medicine have greatly improved child survival rates.
The hospital hired more pediatric nurses to work in the children's ward.

Synset Examples

pediatric dentist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paediatric - relating to the medical care of children (British spelling)

Etymology

From Greek pais, paidos (child) + iatros (doctor), via French pédiatrique.
