Bufo speciosus

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ spɪˈsioʊsəs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ spɪˈsɪəsəs / (BrE)
A nocturnal burrowing toad found in mesquite woodlands and prairies of the southwestern United States.
species of burrowing toad in US southwest

Examples

The Bufo speciosus emerges at night to hunt insects after heavy rains.
Researchers tracked the movement patterns of Bufo speciosus in Texas prairies.
Bufo speciosus prefers sandy soils for burrowing to avoid daytime heat.

Etymology

From Latin; 'Bufo' meaning 'toad' and 'speciosus' meaning 'showy' or 'handsome'.
