one-billionth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn bɪl.jənθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈbɪl.jənθ / (BrE)
One part of a billion equal parts; the fraction 1/1,000,000,000.
one part in a billion; 1/1,000,000,000

Examples

The concentration of the substance was measured to one one-billionth of a gram per liter.
Even a one-billionth decrease in error can matter in sensitive scientific experiments.
A one-billionth share of the profits would amount to only a few cents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billionth - One part in a billion equal parts
