content

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnt / (BrE)
the sum or range of concepts, knowledge, or information contained within something such as a book, speech, or subject
the substance of what is included or conveyed

Examples

The teacher was impressed by the rich content of the student's essay.
We discussed the content of the book during our meeting.
Online courses can vary widely in both quality and content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substance - the essential meaning or most important part of something spoken or written
  • Synonym: subject matter - the topic dealt with in a text, discourse, or artwork

Etymology

From Latin 'contentum' (that which is contained), from 'continere' (to contain).
