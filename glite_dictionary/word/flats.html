flats

1. NOUN

/ flæts / (AmE), / flæts / (BrE)
Women's shoes with no heel or only a very low heel.
women's shoes with no heel

Examples

She wore her favorite flats to the office because they are comfortable.
These black flats go well with both jeans and dresses.
After hours in heels, I switched to flats for the rest of the evening.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heels - women's shoes with a raised heel
  • Synonym: ballet flats - light, flat shoes similar to ballet slippers
