uninfluenced

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈɪn.flu.ənst / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪn.flu.ənst / (BrE)
not affected or changed by something; not influenced.
not affected or influenced by something

Examples

Her decision remained uninfluenced by public opinion or outside pressure.
He gave an uninfluenced account of the events as he saw them.
The results must be uninfluenced by personal bias or expectations.

Synset Examples

stewed in its petty provincialism untouched by the brisk debates that stirred the old world
unswayed by personal considerations

Related Terms

  • Antonym: influenced - affected or changed by something

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + influenced, based on influence from Latin influere (to flow in).
