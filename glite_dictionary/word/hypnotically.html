hypnotically

1. ADV

/ hɪpˈnɑtɪkli / (AmE), / hɪpˈnɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner related to or caused by hypnotism; as if under hypnosis.
in a manner relating to hypnosis

Examples

The patient was placed in a trance and responded hypnotically to the therapist's suggestions.
The snake moved hypnotically, captivating its prey with each gentle sway.
His voice was so soothing that it seemed to affect the crowd hypnotically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypnotic - relating to or producing hypnosis

Etymology

From hypnotic + -ally, patterned after hypnotic (relating to hypnosis).
