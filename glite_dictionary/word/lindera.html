Lindera

1. NOUN

/ lɪnˈdɛrə / (AmE), / lɪnˈdɪərə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic evergreen or deciduous shrubs and trees native to eastern Asia and North America, belonging to the laurel family (Lauraceae).
genus of aromatic shrubs or trees

Examples

The Lindera plants in her garden fill the air with a spicy fragrance in the spring.
Many species of Lindera are prized for their ornamental foliage and medicinal properties.
The botanist identified the shrub as a member of the Lindera genus based on its aromatic leaves.

Etymology

Named after Johann Linder, an 18th-century Swedish botanist.
