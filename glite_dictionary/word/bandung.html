Bandung

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑnˌduŋ / (AmE), / ˈbændʊŋ / (BrE)
a city in Indonesia, located in western Java, known for its cool climate and as a resort destination
city in western Java, Indonesia

Examples

Many tourists visit Bandung for its scenic views and pleasant weather.
The conference was held in Bandung, attracting delegates from across Asia.
She moved from Jakarta to Bandung to enjoy a quieter lifestyle.
