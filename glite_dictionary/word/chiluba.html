Chiluba

1. NOUN

/ tʃɪˈluːbə / (AmE), / tʃɪˈluːbə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group living in southeastern Congo
a member of a Bantu people in Congo

Examples

Many Chiluba still maintain traditional customs and ceremonies in southeastern Congo.
The Chiluba have a rich history of artistic crafts and oral storytelling.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of the Chiluba throughout the region.
