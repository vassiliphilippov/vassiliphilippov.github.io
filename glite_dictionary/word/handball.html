handball

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhandˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A small, typically rubber ball used in the game of handball.
small ball used in handball

Examples

He bought a new handball for the tournament next week.
You need a special type of handball that bounces well for the game.
She accidentally lost her handball at the gym.

2. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhandˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a game played in a walled court or against a single wall where players strike a small rubber ball with their hands
game where players hit a ball with hands against a wall

Examples

He has played handball competitively since high school.
We watched a fast-paced handball match at the local gym.
In handball, quick reflexes and agility are essential to win.

Related Terms
