cardsharp

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌʃɑrp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌʃɑːp / (BrE)
a person who cheats at card games for money, especially as a profession
a professional card cheat

Examples

The notorious cardsharp was banned from every casino in the city.
She suspected that the man at the table was a cardsharp after he won every hand.
The old Western movie featured a clever cardsharp who always had an ace up his sleeve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardsharper - a person who cheats at cards for money
  • Synonym: cheater - a person who cheats
  • Antonym: honest player - a person who does not cheat at cards
