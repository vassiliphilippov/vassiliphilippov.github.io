toxic

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
containing or related to a poison or toxin; capable of causing harm or death
poisonous; related to toxins

Examples

Some cleaning products contain toxic chemicals that can be harmful if inhaled or swallowed.
The factory was fined for discharging toxic waste into the river.
Wearing gloves is important when handling toxic substances in the laboratory.

Synset Examples

suffering from exposure to toxic substances

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poisonous - containing poison and capable of causing harm

Etymology

From Ancient Greek toxikon (poison for arrows), from toxon (bow).
