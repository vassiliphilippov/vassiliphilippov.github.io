Hermann Maurice Saxe

1. PROPN

/ hɜːrˈmæn ˈmɔːrɪs sæks / (AmE), / hɛˈʁman mɔˈʁis saks / (BrE)
(1696–1750) A French marshal known for his distinguished service in the War of the Austrian Succession.
French marshal and military leader

Examples

Hermann Maurice Saxe played a crucial role in several major battles during the War of the Austrian Succession.
The military strategies of Hermann Maurice Saxe are still studied by historians and officers today.
As a marshal of France, Hermann Maurice Saxe earned recognition for his tactical brilliance.
