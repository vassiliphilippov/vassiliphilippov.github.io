tormented

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔːrˌmɛn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.mɛn.tɪd / (BrE)
Experiencing or showing severe mental or physical suffering.
experiencing severe suffering

Examples

She appeared tormented by memories of her past mistakes.
The tormented artist expressed his pain through his paintings.
He gave a tormented sigh as he struggled with his decision.

Synset Examples

an anguished conscience
a small tormented schoolboy
a tortured witness to another's humiliation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anguished - experiencing severe mental or emotional pain
  • Antonym: content - in a state of peaceful happiness

Etymology

From the verb 'torment', which derives from Latin 'tormentum' (instrument of torture).
