cinnamon-coloured

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪnəmən ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a reddish-brown color like that of cinnamon
of a reddish-brown color

Examples

The artist chose a cinnamon-coloured fabric to enhance the warmth of the room.
The fox’s cinnamon-coloured fur blended perfectly with the autumn leaves.
She wore a cinnamon-coloured dress that complemented her complexion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reddish-brown - somewhat red-brown in color
  • Synonym: ginger - of a yellowish or reddish-brown color, especially of hair
