seedsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsidzmən / (AmE), / ˈsiːdzmən / (BrE)
a person who sells or deals in seeds, especially for planting
person who sells seeds

Examples

The seedsman recommended several varieties of tomato for my garden.
She went to the local seedsman to buy wildflower seeds.
In the 19th century, every town had a seedsman who supplied farmers with planting stock.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seed merchant - a person or business that sells seeds

Etymology

From seed + man, meaning 'seed seller' or 'seed dealer'.
