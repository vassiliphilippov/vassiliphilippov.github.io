summarization

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌmərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌməraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of producing a summary; stating something briefly and clearly
the process of making a summary

Examples

The teacher explained that summarization is a valuable skill for understanding complex texts.
Her report included a clear summarization of the main findings from the experiment.
Automated summarization tools are becoming increasingly popular in the field of information technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Antonym: expansion - the process of making something more extensive or elaborate

Etymology

From 'summarize' + '-ation', modeled after Latin-derived nouns with '-ation' endings.
