Omani

1. NOUN

/ oʊˈmɑːni / (AmE), / əʊˈmɑːni / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Oman
native or resident of Oman

Examples

The Omani greeted us warmly and offered traditional coffee.
An Omani explained the history of Muscat to the tourists.
Many Omanis take pride in their country's rich maritime heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Oman - a country on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula

Etymology

From Oman, the country name, with English adjectival and nominal suffix -i.

/ oʊˈmɑːni / (AmE), / əʊˈmɑːni / (BrE)
relating to Oman or its people or culture
of or relating to Oman or its people

Examples

The Omani coastline is famous for its beautiful beaches.
She enjoyed learning about Omani traditions during her trip.
Many Omani students study abroad each year.
