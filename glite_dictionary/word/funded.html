funded

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌndɪd / (AmE), / ˈfʌndɪd / (BrE)
having been provided with money for a particular purpose, especially by an organization or government.
having received financial support

Examples

The funded project was able to continue its research despite economic challenges.
Many scholarships are funded by private donors and foundations.
Our organization fully supports funded initiatives for community development.

Synset Examples

well-funded research

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unfunded - not provided with funds
  • Degree Higher: well-funded - provided with ample financial resources
