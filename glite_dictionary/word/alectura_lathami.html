Alectura lathami

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɛktjʊrə ˈlæθəmi / (AmE), / əˈlɛktjʊərə ˈlɑːθəmi / (BrE)
A large black megapode bird native to wooded regions of Australia and New Guinea, also known as the Australian brush-turkey.
Australian brush-turkey species

The Alectura lathami is a common sight in the forests of eastern Australia.
