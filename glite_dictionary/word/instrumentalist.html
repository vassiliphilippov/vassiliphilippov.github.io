instrumentalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.strəˈmɛn.tə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪn.strəˈmen.tə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays a musical instrument, especially as a profession
person who plays a musical instrument

Examples

She is a talented instrumentalist who plays both the piano and the violin.
The orchestra hired a new instrumentalist for their upcoming performance.
As a professional instrumentalist, he spends hours every day rehearsing his music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented

Etymology

From 'instrument' + '-alist', formed after 'instrumental' (pertaining to instruments).
