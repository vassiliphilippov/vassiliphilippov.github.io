calculating

1. ADJ

/ ˈkælkjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkælkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
acting with careful planning to achieve a personal goal, often in a shrewd or selfish way
deliberately shrewd or scheming

Examples

She always takes a calculating approach in business negotiations.
His calculating manner made it difficult to trust his motives.
The villain in the film was a cold and calculating character.

Synset Examples

the most calculating and selfish men in the community

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scheming - planning secret or underhanded actions
  • Antonym: naive - showing a lack of experience or sophistication

Etymology

From the verb 'calculate', from Latin 'calculare' (to reckon, compute).
