Edward IV

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdwərd ðə ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈɛdwəd ðə ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
(1442–1483) King of England from 1461 to 1470 and from 1471 to 1483, restored to the throne after the Battle of Tewkesbury.
King of England (1461–1470, 1471–1483)

Examples

Edward IV was restored to the throne after his victory at the Battle of Tewkesbury.
The reign of Edward IV marked a turning point in the Wars of the Roses.
Many historians debate the political strategies employed by Edward IV during his time as king.
