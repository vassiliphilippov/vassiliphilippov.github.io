taxonomical

1. ADJ

/ tækˌsɑːˈnɑːmɪkəl / (AmE), / tækˌsɒˈnɒmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on the principles of taxonomy (the classification of organisms or things)
pertaining to taxonomy or classification

Examples

The biologist conducted a taxonomical analysis of the plant species found in the rainforest.
Their debate focused on taxonomical distinctions between bacteria and archaea.
Botanists often disagree on the taxonomical grouping of certain rare flowers.

Synset Examples

taxonomic relations
a taxonomic designation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxonomic - relating to taxonomy (equivalent adjective)

Etymology

From taxonomy + -ical, based on French taxonomique.
