foyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
a large entrance hall or lobby in a building, especially a hotel, theater, or private residence
large entrance hall or lobby

Examples

She greeted the guests as they entered the foyer of the hotel.
The concertgoers gathered in the foyer before the show began.
A large chandelier hangs in the center of the mansion's foyer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lobby - a hall or waiting area near the entrance of a building
  • Synonym: vestibule - a small entrance hall or passage

Etymology

From French 'foyer' meaning 'hearth' or 'lobby'.
