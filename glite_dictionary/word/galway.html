Galway

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡæl.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.weɪ / (BrE)
a city and port in western Ireland, situated on Galway Bay, known for its history, culture, and vibrant arts scene
city in western Ireland, port on Galway Bay

Examples

She studied at the university in Galway before moving to Dublin.
Galway is famous for its lively arts festivals and traditional music.
They visited the picturesque harbor of Galway during their trip to Ireland.

Etymology

From Irish Gaillimh, name of the river and city.
