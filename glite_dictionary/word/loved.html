loved

1. ADJ

/ lʌvd / (AmE), / lʌvd / (BrE)
cherished and held dear by someone; greatly valued or cared for.
cherished; held dear

Examples

He lost his loved dog last year and still misses her dearly.
The photo shows her with her loved family at a reunion.
Each loved item she kept reminded her of happy memories.

Synset Examples

his loved companion of many years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beloved - dearly loved; cherished
  • Antonym: hated - intensely disliked

Etymology

Past participle of 'love', from Old English 'lufian'.
