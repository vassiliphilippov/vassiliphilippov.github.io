nonpartizan

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈpɑrtɪzən / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɑːtɪzən / (BrE)
not supporting or controlled by any political party or group; free from bias or affiliation with parties.
free from party affiliation or bias

Examples

The committee remained strictly nonpartizan throughout the decision-making process.
Her nonpartizan analysis earned her respect from politicians on both sides.
A nonpartizan approach is essential for fair and balanced reporting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonpartisan - not supporting any political party or group; unbiased
  • Antonym: partisan - strong supporter of a party, cause, or person; biased

Etymology

From 'non-' (not) + 'partizan' (variant of 'partisan', supporter of a party), influenced by Italian *partigiano*.
