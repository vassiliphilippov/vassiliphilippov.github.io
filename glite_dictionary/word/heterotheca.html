Heterotheca

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈθiːkə / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈθiːkə / (BrE)
A genus of yellow-flowered North American herbs in the daisy family (Asteraceae).
genus of yellow-flowered North American herbs

Examples

Several species of Heterotheca can be found in dry, sandy soils across North America.
Heterotheca is often mistaken for goldenrod due to its bright yellow flowers.
Botanists have reclassified some plants under the genus Heterotheca.

Etymology

From Greek heteros (different) + theke (case, receptacle), referring to the plant's unequal fruit achenes.
