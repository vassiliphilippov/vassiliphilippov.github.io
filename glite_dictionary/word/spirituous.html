spirituous

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɪrɪtuəs / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjuːəs / (BrE)
(of a beverage) containing alcohol; of the nature of alcohol.
containing alcohol

Examples

The sale of spirituous liquors is restricted to those over 21 years old.
The region is known for its spirituous beverages, particularly distilled whiskey.
Customs officers seized several bottles of spirituous drinks at the border.

Synset Examples

spiritous beverages
spirituous liquors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alcoholic - containing or relating to alcohol

Etymology

From Latin 'spirituosus' (full of spirit, alcoholic), from 'spiritus' (spirit).
