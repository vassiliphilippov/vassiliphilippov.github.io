cruisy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkruːzi / (AmE), / ˈkruːzi / (BrE)
(informal) Easy-going, relaxed, and stress-free; requiring little effort or tension.
easy-going or stress-free
informal

Examples

I'm looking forward to a cruisy weekend by the beach without any plans.
Her new job is much more cruisy than the last one, with less pressure and deadlines.
We took a cruisy drive along the coast, enjoying the scenery at our own pace.

Synset Examples

He described his weekend as cruisy and relaxing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relaxing - making one feel relaxed; free from tension or anxiety
  • Antonym: stressful - causing stress or anxiety

Etymology

From cruise + -y, originally Australasian slang.
