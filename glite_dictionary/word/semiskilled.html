semiskilled

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈskɪld / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈskɪld / (BrE)
Having or requiring only limited skill or training.
having or needing limited skill or training

Examples

Many semiskilled workers are employed in manufacturing plants around the country.
He applied for a semiskilled position at the warehouse, which required some basic training.
The company offers training programs to help semiskilled employees advance to higher-level roles.

Synset Examples

semiskilled dockworkers
a semiskilled job

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: skilled - having or showing skill; requiring training or experience
  • Degree Lower: unskilled - not having or requiring any special training or skill

Etymology

Formed from 'semi-' (meaning 'half' or 'partly') and 'skilled'.
