coextension

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊ.ɪkˈstɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪkˈstɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
The state or quality of being equal in extent, range, or duration.
equality of extent or duration

Examples

The coextension of the two projects meant they ended at exactly the same time.
In geometry, coextension can describe two lines of equal length.
The coextension of their tenures was purely coincidental.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneity - the occurrence of events at the same time

Etymology

From Latin coextensio, from co- (together) + extensio (stretching out).
