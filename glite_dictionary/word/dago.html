dago

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a person of Italian descent.
offensive term for someone of Italian descent
offensive slang

Examples

That kind of language, including words like dago, is completely unacceptable today.
He was sadly subjected to the slur dago by ignorant classmates in the past.
Many books now annotate when terms like dago appear in historical dialogue.

Etymology

Likely from Spanish 'Diego' used as a generic name for a Spaniard or Italian.
