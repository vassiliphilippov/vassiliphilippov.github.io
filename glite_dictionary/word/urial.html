urial

1. NOUN

/ ˈjʊriˌɑl / (AmE), / ˈjʊəriˌɑːl / (BrE)
a wild sheep with a reddish coat and a distinct beard, native to southern Asia
a bearded wild sheep of Asia

Examples

The endangered urial roams the steep hillsides of the Himalayas in small herds.
Researchers tracked several urials as part of a conservation project in Pakistan.
The distinctive horns of the male urial curve outward and upward.

Etymology

From Hindi 'uriyāl', referring to the wild sheep species of southern Asia.
