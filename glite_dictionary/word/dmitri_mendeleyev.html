Dmitri Mendeleyev

1. PROPN

/ dəˈmitri ˌmɛndəˈleɪəf / (AmE), / dmɪˈtri mɛnˈdɛlɪˌjɛf / (BrE)
(1834–1907) A Russian chemist who formulated the periodic table of chemical elements and predicted the existence of undiscovered elements.
Russian chemist; creator of the periodic table

Examples

Dmitri Mendeleyev's periodic table revolutionized the study of chemistry.
The achievements of Dmitri Mendeleyev are still celebrated by scientists today.
Many elements were discovered after Dmitri Mendeleyev predicted their existence.
