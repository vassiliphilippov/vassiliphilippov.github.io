batty

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæti / (AmE), / ˈbæti / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy, eccentric, or mentally irregular.
crazy or eccentric (informal)
informal slang

Examples

Her neighbors always thought she was a little batty for talking to her plants.
After staying up for three nights in a row, he was feeling completely batty.
That batty idea of his just might work after all!

Synset Examples

it used to drive my husband balmy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or wildly eccentric
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not crazy or eccentric

Etymology

Probably from 'bats in the belfry,' meaning eccentric or mad.
