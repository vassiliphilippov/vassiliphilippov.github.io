Katherine Mansfield

1. PROPN

/ ˈkæθərɪn ˈmænsfiːld / (AmE), / ˈkæθərɪn ˈmɑːnzfiːld / (BrE)
(1888–1923) a New Zealand writer renowned for her modernist short stories, noted for their psychological insight and innovative style.
New Zealand modernist short story writer

Examples

Many critics consider Katherine Mansfield one of the most influential short story writers of the early 20th century.
I have read several of Katherine Mansfield's stories for my literature class.
Katherine Mansfield was born in New Zealand but lived much of her literary life in England.
