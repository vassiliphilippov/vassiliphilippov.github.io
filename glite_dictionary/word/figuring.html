figuring

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of calculating or working out solutions, especially involving numbers or quantities
calculating or problem solving with numbers

Examples

After some quick figuring, she realized the bill had been calculated incorrectly.
He spent the afternoon doing figuring to estimate the total costs of the project.
The accounting department's detailed figuring saved the company a significant amount of money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculation - the process of using mathematics; careful thinking and planning
  • Antonym: guesswork - the process of estimating without sufficient information

Etymology

From the verb 'figure', meaning to calculate, ultimately from Latin 'figura' (shape, form, figure).
