zona

1. NOUN

/ ˈzoʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˈzəʊ.nə / (BrE)
(anatomy) any encircling or beltlike structure in the body.
encircling structure in anatomy
medical

Examples

The zona pellucida is a thick membrane surrounding the mammalian egg cell.
Certain parasites invade through the zona of a host's tissue.
Scientists observed a distinct zona around the affected lymph node.

Etymology

From Latin zona (belt, girdle), from Ancient Greek ζώνη (zōnē).
