rare

1. ADJ

/ rɛr / (AmE), / reə / (BrE)
not found in large numbers or not widely distributed; uncommon.
not widely distributed or uncommon

Examples

The rare herbs she collected grew only on these remote cliffs.
In the vast desert, rare patches of green were a welcome sight.
This species of bird is particularly rare in this region.

Synset Examples

rare herbs
rare patches of green in the desert

Related Terms

  • Antonym: common - occurring or found frequently; not rare

Etymology

From Latin 'rarus' (thin, scattered, uncommon).
