atmospheric

1. ADJ

/ ˌætməsˈfɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌatməsˈfɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or located in the Earth's atmosphere.
relating to the atmosphere

Examples

The scientists conducted several atmospheric tests to study air pollution.
Satellite data provide important insights into atmospheric changes around the globe.
Many atmospheric phenomena, such as clouds and storms, affect weather patterns.

Synset Examples

atmospheric tests

Etymology

From atmosphere + -ic, formed to denote relation to the atmosphere.
