a-hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈeɪ həʊl / (BrE)
A vulgar slang term for a rude, obnoxious, or unpleasant person.
vulgar term for an unpleasant person
slang vulgar

Examples

My boss was being a total a-hole about the deadline today.
She dumped him because he turned out to be an a-hole.
If you keep acting like an a-hole, nobody will want to work with you.

Synset Examples

Don't be such an a-hole; apologize already.
He acted like an a-hole during the meeting.
I can't believe he called her an a-hole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asshole - A very rude or unpleasant person (stronger vulgarity)

Etymology

Shortened euphemistic form of 'asshole'.
