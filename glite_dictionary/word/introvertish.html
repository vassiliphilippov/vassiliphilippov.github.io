introvertish

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˈvɜrtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈvɜːtɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat introverted in manner or personality.
somewhat introverted
informal

Examples

He's introvertish, so he prefers spending weekends at home rather than at big parties.
Though she's not shy, her introvertish side comes out in large groups.
My introvertish tendencies make networking events a bit draining for me.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: introverted - having a reserved or inward-looking personality
  • Antonym: extrovertish - somewhat extroverted in manner or personality

Etymology

Formed from 'introvert' with the suffix '-ish', indicating 'somewhat'.
