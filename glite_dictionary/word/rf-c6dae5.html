Rf

1. NOUN

/ ɑr ɛf / (AmE), / ɑː ɛf / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for rutherfordium, a synthetic radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 104
chemical symbol for rutherfordium (element 104)

Examples

The element Rf was first synthesized in the 1960s.
Scientists have studied the chemical properties of Rf to better understand superheavy elements.
Because Rf is highly radioactive, it exists only in laboratories for brief periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rutherfordium - a synthetic radioactive element with atomic number 104

Etymology

Abbreviation of rutherfordium, named after physicist Ernest Rutherford.
