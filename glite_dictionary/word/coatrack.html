coatrack

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtˌræk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtˌræk / (BrE)
a stand or frame with hooks or pegs for hanging coats and hats, usually placed near an entrance
rack for hanging coats and hats

Examples

He hung his wet jacket on the coatrack as soon as he entered the house.
The office has a coatrack near the door for visitors' convenience.
Please leave your hat and umbrella on the coatrack in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hatstand - a stand for holding hats, often similar in design to a coatrack
