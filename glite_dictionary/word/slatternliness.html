slatternliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈslætərnlinəs / (AmE), / ˈslætənlinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being slovenly, untidy, or dirty (typically in reference to a woman)
quality of being slovenly or untidy

Examples

Her slatternliness became evident in the cluttered, unkempt state of her home.
He was appalled by the slatternliness of the kitchen after the party.
Critics remarked on the slatternliness of the character's appearance throughout the play.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slovenliness - the quality of being untidy or messy
  • Antonym: neatness - the quality of being neat and tidy

Etymology

From 'slatternly' (untidy woman) + '-ness' (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
