cozily

1. ADV

/ ˈkoʊzɪli / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzɪli / (BrE)
In a comfortable, warm, and snug manner; in a way that gives a feeling of comfort and relaxation.
in a comfortable, snug way

Examples

She sat cozily wrapped in her favorite blanket, reading a book by the window.
The two friends chatted cozily in the corner of the busy café.
On winter evenings, the family would gather cozily around the fireplace.

Synset Examples

nestled cozily by the fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comfortably - in a comfortable manner
  • Antonym: uncomfortably - in an uncomfortable manner

Etymology

From 'cozy' + '-ly', forming the regular adverbial form.
