micromillimeter

1. NOUN

/ ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈmɪləˌmitər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˈmɪlɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
A metric unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter (10⁻⁹ meters).
one billionth of a meter

Examples

The microscope is capable of measuring distances as small as a single micromillimeter.
Technicians checked the part tolerances down to the level of several micromillimeters.
At the nanoscale, even a gap of one micromillimeter can be significant for precision engineering applications.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nanometer - a metric unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter

Etymology

From micro- (one millionth) + millimeter; intended to mean one thousandth of a micrometer, or one billionth of a meter.
