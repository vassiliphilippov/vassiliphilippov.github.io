Tenoretic

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛnəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛnəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
A trademarked antihypertensive drug composed of a fixed combination of atenolol and a diuretic, used to treat high blood pressure.
antihypertensive combination drug
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Tenoretic to help manage her hypertension.
Patients taking Tenoretic should have their kidney function monitored regularly.
He asked about side effects before starting Tenoretic for his blood pressure.
