Creation

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / kriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
(in theology) The act of God bringing the universe and all living things into existence.
theological act of divine origination

Examples

Many believers hold that the Creation was a direct act of God.
Stories of the Creation can be found in various religious traditions.
The debate between evolution and Creation has been ongoing for decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creationism - the belief that the universe and life originated from specific acts of divine creation

Etymology

From Latin 'creatio', from 'creare' (to create).
