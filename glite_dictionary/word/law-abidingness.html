law-abidingness

1. NOUN

/ lɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ nɪs / (AmE), / lɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of consistently obeying the law.
quality of obeying the law

Examples

The police chief praised the community's law-abidingness during his annual report.
Despite economic hardship, her law-abidingness never wavered.
Schools can help foster a culture of law-abidingness among young people.

Synset Examples

And while we should generally want to create a culture of law-abidingness, a culture of pacifism is a recipe for national disaster.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lawfulness - the quality of being lawful or legal
  • Antonym: criminality - the state of being criminal or engaging in criminal acts
