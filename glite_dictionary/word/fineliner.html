fineliner

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪnˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
A felt-tip pen with a very fine, thin tip, used for drawing or writing precise lines.
felt-tip pen with a fine tip

Examples

She used a fineliner to sketch the delicate details of the flower.
I prefer writing with a fineliner because the lines are so crisp and clear.
The technical drawing required a precise fineliner for all the fine lines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: felt-tip pen - a pen with a fiber tip

Etymology

From 'fine' (thin, precise) + 'liner' (from pen used to create lines).
