confusedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈfjuzɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːzɪdli / (BrE)
In a confused or disordered manner; lacking clarity or order.
in a confused or disordered manner

Examples

She answered confusedly when asked about her plans for the weekend.
The students spoke confusedly during the chaotic discussion.
He looked around confusedly, unsure of where to go next.

Synset Examples

Queen Augusta wrote him an hysterical letter in which she confusedly sympathised with him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - lacking clarity or order; perplexed

Etymology

From 'confused' + '-ly', forming the adverb.
