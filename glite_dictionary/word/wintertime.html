wintertime

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈwɪntəˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the coldest season of the year, occurring between the winter solstice and the vernal equinox in the Northern Hemisphere
the coldest season of the year

Examples

We usually go skiing during wintertime when the mountains are covered in snow.
The days are much shorter in wintertime than in summer.
In wintertime, many animals hibernate to survive the cold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winter - the season between autumn and spring
  • Antonym: summertime - the warmest season of the year

Etymology

Formed from 'winter' + 'time'.
