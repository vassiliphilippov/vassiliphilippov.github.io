noncombining

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.kəmˈbaɪ.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kəmˈbaɪ.nɪŋ / (BrE)
Not able to or not tending to combine with something else.
not able to combine

Examples

This particular chemical is considered noncombining under normal laboratory conditions.
The noncombining gases remained separate even under high pressure.
She studied the noncombining nature of certain rare earth elements.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: combining - able to combine

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'non-' (not) to 'combining'.
