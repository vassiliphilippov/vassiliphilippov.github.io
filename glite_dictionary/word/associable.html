associable

1. ADJ

/ əˈsoʊʃiəbl̩ / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being associated or connected with something else
capable of being associated

Examples

These symptoms are often associable with exposure to certain chemicals.
Some words are associable with particular emotions or memories.
The pattern is not easily associable with any known disease.

Synset Examples

words associable with politics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: related - connected in some way; similar meaning to associable

Etymology

From Latin 'associabilis', from 'associare' (to associate).
