shall-flower

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃæl ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈʃæl ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A plant cultivated for its shiny oblong leaves and arching clusters of white flowers tinged with pink, crinkled yellow lips, and magenta-striped petals.
plant with decorative white and pink flowers

Examples

The shall-flower brightened the conservatory with its dazzling display of blossoms.
She admired the delicate magenta stripes on the petals of the blooming shall-flower.
Gardeners value the shall-flower for its ornate foliage and unique flower clusters.
