classifiable

1. ADJ

/ ˌklæs.ɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌklɑː.sɪˈfaɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be classified; capable of being arranged or organized into groups or categories.
able to be classified

Examples

Only information that is classifiable under the law may be withheld from the public.
These fossils are easily classifiable according to their distinct features.
The data were not immediately classifiable due to their ambiguous origin.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unclassifiable - not able to be classified
  • Synonym: to classify - to arrange or organize into groups or categories

Etymology

From classify + -able, from Late Latin classificare (to classify) + -able (able to be).
