devotedness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvoʊtɪdnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊtɪdnəs / (BrE)
deep and sincere devotion or affection towards someone or something, especially of an ardent or loving nature
deep devotion or ardent love

Examples

Her devotedness to her family was evident in everything she did.
The devotedness he showed during her illness touched everyone around them.
Their devotedness to each other was admired by their friends.

Synset Examples

their devotion to each other was beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devotion - deep love, loyalty, or enthusiasm for a person, activity, or cause
  • Synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From 'devoted' + '-ness', patterned after 'devotion'; related to Middle English 'devoten', from Latin 'devotus' (to vow).
