Dinesen

1. PROPN

/ ˈdiːnəsən / (AmE), / ˈdiːnəsən / (BrE)
(Karen) Dinesen (1885–1962), Danish writer also known as Karen Blixen, remembered for her accounts of life in Africa.
Danish writer known for works about Africa

Examples

Dinesen is best known in the English-speaking world under her pen name, Isak Dinesen.
Many critics regard Dinesen's memoir 'Out of Africa' as a classic of 20th-century literature.
After living in Kenya for nearly two decades, Dinesen returned to Denmark to continue her writing.
