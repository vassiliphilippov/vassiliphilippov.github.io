3. PROPN

/ kəˈlʌmbəs / (AmE), / kəˈlʌmbəs / (BrE)
(1451–1506), Italian explorer and navigator who completed four voyages across the Atlantic Ocean, opening the way for the widespread European exploration and colonization of the Americas.
Italian explorer; discovered the Americas

Examples

Many historians debate whether Columbus truly discovered America.
Columbus sailed across the Atlantic in search of a new route to Asia.
The voyages of Columbus marked a turning point in world history.
