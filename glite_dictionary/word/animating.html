animating

1. ADJ

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈanɪˌmeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
causing someone or something to become lively, energetic, or full of spirit
giving life or energy

Examples

Her animating presence lifted the spirits of everyone in the room.
The animating speech inspired hope among the weary audience.
A brisk walk in fresh air had an animating effect on his mood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspiring - stimulating or encouraging, especially toward creativity or enthusiasm

Etymology

From Latin animare (to bring to life), via animate + -ing.
