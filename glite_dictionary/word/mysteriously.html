mysteriously

1. ADV

/ məˈstɪriəsli / (AmE), / mɪˈstɪəriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is difficult to understand or explain; in a cryptic or enigmatic manner.
in a cryptic or enigmatic way

Examples

The lights mysteriously flickered and then went out during the storm.
She mysteriously disappeared without leaving a single clue behind.
He mysteriously refused to answer any questions about his whereabouts.

Synset Examples

‘we will meet again,’ he said cryptically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptically - in a way that is mysterious or difficult to understand
  • Antonym: openly - in a way that is clear and not secretive
