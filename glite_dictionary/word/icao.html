ICAO

1. NOUN

/ aɪ siː eɪ oʊ / (AmE), / aɪ siː eɪ əʊ / (BrE)
an agency of the United Nations responsible for international civil aviation standards and regulations
UN agency for civil aviation

Examples

The ICAO sets global standards for aviation safety and security.
Countries must follow ICAO regulations when designing airport procedures.
The ICAO council met to discuss new environmental policies.

Etymology

Acronym for International Civil Aviation Organization.
