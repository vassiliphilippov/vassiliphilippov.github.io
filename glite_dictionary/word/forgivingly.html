forgivingly

1. ADV

/ fərˈɡɪvɪŋli / (AmE), / fəˈɡɪvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a forgiving manner; showing forgiveness or readiness to forgive.
in a forgiving manner

Examples

He smiled forgivingly after hearing her sincere apology.
She nodded forgivingly, letting go of her anger.
The teacher looked at the students forgivingly despite their late homework.

Synset Examples

‘Never mind,’ she said forgivingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: harshly - in a harsh or unforgiving manner
