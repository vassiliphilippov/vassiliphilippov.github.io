collards

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlərdz / (AmE), / ˈkɒlədz / (BrE)
a type of kale with large, smooth leaves, often cooked as a vegetable
a leafy green vegetable (type of kale)
american

Examples

She served a plate of collards seasoned with ham hocks and spices.
Many Southern dishes include collards as a main ingredient.
Fresh collards can be found at the farmers' market in late autumn.

Related Terms
