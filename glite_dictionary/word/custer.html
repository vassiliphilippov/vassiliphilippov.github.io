Custer

1. PROPN

/ ˈkʌstər / (AmE), / ˈkʌstə / (BrE)
(1839–1876) United States cavalry officer, remembered for his fatal defeat by the Sioux at the Battle of Little Bighorn.
U.S. cavalry officer killed at Little Bighorn

Examples

General Custer is famous for his last stand at the Battle of Little Bighorn.
Many books have been written about Custer and his controversial role in American history.
The defeat of Custer and his men marked a turning point in the Indian Wars.
