prepacked

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriˈpækt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpækt / (BrE)
(of goods or food) prepared and packed or wrapped before being offered for sale.
packed and wrapped before sale

Examples

Many supermarkets sell prepacked salads for convenience.
I prefer to buy fresh meat instead of prepacked products.
The prepacked sandwiches are located near the checkout.

Synset Examples

prepackaged foods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prepackaged - prepared and packaged before being sold

Etymology

From 'pre-' (before) + 'packed' (past participle of pack).
