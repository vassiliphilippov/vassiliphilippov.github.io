GST

1. NOUN

/ dʒi ɛs ti / (AmE), / dʒiː ɛs tiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for goods and services tax; a tax levied on most goods and services, collected at each stage of production or distribution.
goods and services tax (type of value-added tax)

Examples

Many countries have implemented a GST to simplify the tax system.
Business owners must file their GST returns every quarter.
The GST rate was recently increased by the government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: VAT - value-added tax, a related consumption tax

Etymology

Acronym from goods and services tax.
