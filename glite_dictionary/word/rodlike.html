rodlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɑdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɒdlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or shaped like a rod; long, straight, and thin.
resembling a rod in shape

Examples

The bacterium has a rodlike structure under the microscope.
The artist bent the metal into a rodlike form for the sculpture.
They found fossils with rodlike features in the ancient rock layer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rod-shaped - having the shape of a rod; cylindrical and elongated
