magnetics

1. NOUN

/ mæɡˈnɛtɪks / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪks / (BrE)
the branch of science concerned with the study of magnetism and magnetic phenomena
science of magnetism

Examples

He specializes in magnetics and develops new magnetic materials for electronic devices.
The university offers a graduate program focused on magnetics research and applications.
Recent advances in magnetics have improved data storage technology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magnetism - the physical phenomenon associated with magnetic fields

Etymology

From magnetic, formed by analogy to other scientific '-ics' terms (such as mathematics, physics).
