Rangoon

1. PROPN

/ ræŋˈɡun / (AmE), / ræŋˈɡuːn / (BrE)
the former name of Yangon, the largest city in Myanmar, located in the south near the Irrawaddy River delta
former name of Yangon, Myanmar's largest city

Examples

After landing in Rangoon, we took a taxi to our hotel in the old colonial district.
During the British Empire, Rangoon served as the main administrative centre of Burma.
The bustling markets of Rangoon attracted traders from all over Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Yangon - the current official name of the former Rangoon, Myanmar's largest city

Etymology

From Burmese Yangon, anglicized as Rangoon during British colonial rule.
