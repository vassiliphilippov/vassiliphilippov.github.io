quincentenary

1. ADJ

/ kwɪnˌsɛntəˈnɛri / (AmE), / kwɪnˌsɛntəˈnɪəri / (BrE)
Relating to or marking the 500th anniversary of an event.
relating to a 500th anniversary

Examples

The quincentenary celebration attracted visitors from all over the world.
A special exhibition marked the quincentenary of Leonardo da Vinci's birth.
The museum was renovated in time for its quincentenary festivities.

Synset Examples

the quincentennial celebration of the founding of the city

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quincentennial - relating to a 500th anniversary

Etymology

From Latin 'quinque' (five) + 'centenarius' (of a hundred).

2. NOUN

/ kwɪn.sɛnˈtɛn.ə.ri / (AmE), / kwɪn.senˈtiː.nər.i / (BrE)
The 500th anniversary of a significant event, or the celebration of such an anniversary.
500th anniversary or celebration

Examples

The city hosted a quincentenary festival to mark 500 years since its founding.
In 1992, Spain celebrated the quincentenary of Columbus's voyage to the Americas.
A special medal was commissioned for the quincentenary of the university.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: centenary - the 100th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Lower: bicentenary - the 200th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Higher: millennium - the 1000th anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

From Latin 'quinque' (five) + 'centenarius' (of a hundred).
