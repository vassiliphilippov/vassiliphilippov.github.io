untraveled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a road, path, or area) not having been traveled over or through; not visited or explored.
not traveled over; not visited

Examples

We decided to hike along the untraveled trail through the forest.
Many explorers are drawn to the mysteries of untraveled areas.
The book describes adventures on untraveled roads far from civilization.

Synset Examples

untraveled roads

Related Terms

  • Synonym: untrodden - not having been walked on or trampled
Source: WordNet

Synset POS: a

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a person) not having traveled much, especially to foreign countries; lacking experience gained by travel.
not having traveled much

Examples

Before her trip to Europe, Maria was quite untraveled and had never left her hometown.
The untraveled students were excited to participate in the school's exchange program.
He was untraveled and unfamiliar with customs outside of his own country.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-traveled - having traveled a lot, especially to many foreign countries

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'traveled'.
