to diffusing

1. VERB

/ dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
spreading gradually by diffusion or dispersal
spreading by diffusion

Examples

The scent of lavender was diffusing throughout the entire room.
The scientist observed gas particles diffusing across the barrier.
Light was diffusing gently through the frosted glass window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spreading - extending over a larger area or affecting a greater number of people

Etymology

From Latin 'diffundere' meaning 'to pour out'.
