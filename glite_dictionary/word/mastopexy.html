mastopexy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæstəˌpɛksi / (AmE), / ˈmastəˌpɛksi / (BrE)
A surgical procedure to lift or reshape the breasts.
surgical breast lift
medical

Examples

After her pregnancy, she underwent a mastopexy to restore the shape of her breasts.
The plastic surgeon explained the mastopexy procedure in detail during the consultation.
Scarring is a common concern for patients considering mastopexy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast augmentation - surgical procedure to increase breast size

Etymology

From Greek 'mastos' (breast) and '-pexy' (fixation).
