Pholistoma auritum

1. NOUN

/ foʊˈlɪstəmə ɔːˈrɪtəm / (AmE), / fəˈlɪstəmə ɔːˈrɪtəm / (BrE)
A straggling annual herb native to California, with deep purple or violet flowers; sometimes classified under the genus Nemophila.
annual herb with purple or violet flowers

Examples

Pholistoma auritum is commonly found in shaded woodlands throughout California.
Botanists sometimes debate whether Pholistoma auritum should be placed in the genus Nemophila.
The deep violet flowers of Pholistoma auritum make it a favorite among native plant gardeners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nemophila - a genus of flowering plants sometimes including Pholistoma auritum
