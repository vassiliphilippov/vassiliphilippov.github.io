Mylanta

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈlæntə / (AmE), / maɪˈlæntə / (BrE)
A trademarked over-the-counter antacid used to relieve heartburn, acid indigestion, and upset stomach.
trademarked antacid medication
medical

Examples

He took a spoonful of Mylanta to help with his indigestion.
The doctor recommended Mylanta for occasional heartburn relief.
After eating spicy food, Rachel always keeps Mylanta handy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antacid - a substance that neutralizes stomach acidity
