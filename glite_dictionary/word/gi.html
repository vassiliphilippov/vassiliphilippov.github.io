GI

1. ADJ

/ dʒiː aɪ / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ / (BrE)
of or relating to the stomach and intestines; gastrointestinal.
pertaining to stomach and intestines
medical

Examples

The patient was admitted for a GI examination after reporting severe stomach pain.
Certain medications can cause GI side effects such as nausea or bloating.
He specializes in GI disorders, particularly those affecting digestion.

Synset Examples

a gastrointestinal disorder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gastrointestinal - of or relating to the stomach and intestines

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'gastrointestinal'.

to clean thoroughly, especially in preparation for inspection, often in a military context.
clean thoroughly, esp. for inspection (military)
american informal

Examples

Before the inspection, the recruits GIed their entire barracks until every surface was spotless.
We have to GI the kitchen before the sergeant comes by.
They GIed the dormitories yesterday, so everything is sparkling clean now.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

3. NOUN

/ dʒi aɪ / (AmE), / dʒiː aɪ / (BrE)
(law) An abbreviation for 'geographical indication', a name or sign used on certain products which indicates their specific geographical origin and attributes or reputation due to that origin.
abbreviation for 'geographical indication' (legal product origin marking)
legal

Examples

Champagne has a GI, protecting its name under international trade law.
Many Indian teas have obtained GI status, helping to preserve their unique identities.
Only cheese made in certain regions can use the GI of 'Parmesan'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trademark - a symbol, word, or words legally registered for use by a single company

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'geographical indication'.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: n
