Gi

1. NOUN

/ dʒi / (AmE), / dʒiː / (BrE)
A unit of magnetomotive force equal to 0.7958 ampere-turns, used in electromagnetism.
unit of magnetomotive force

Examples

The magnetomotive force was measured as 3 Gi in the experiment.
One Gi is equivalent to approximately 0.8 ampere-turns.
Engineers sometimes express results in Gi for historical reasons.
