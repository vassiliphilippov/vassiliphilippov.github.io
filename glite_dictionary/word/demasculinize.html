to demasculinize

1. VERB

/ diˈmæskjəˌlaɪnˌaɪz / (AmE), / diˈmæskjʊlənaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the testicles of a male animal; to castrate
remove the testicles of a male animal

Examples

The veterinarian had to demasculinize the aggressive stallion for health and safety reasons.
After they demasculinized the stray dogs, the population became much easier to manage.
Some farmers routinely demasculinize male livestock to prevent unwanted breeding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: castrate - to remove the testicles of a male animal or person
  • Synonym: emasculate - to deprive a male of his male role or identity, or to remove testicles

Etymology

From de- (removal) + masculinize (to make male), patterned after words like dehumanize.
