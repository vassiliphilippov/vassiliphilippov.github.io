saucer-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔsər ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shallow, rounded, and concave shape similar to a saucer
shaped like a saucer; shallow and concave

Examples

The UFO was described as saucer-shaped and hovered silently above the trees.
She chose a saucer-shaped bowl for the soup to enhance its delicate presentation.
The flower's saucer-shaped petals collected drops of morning dew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concave - curved inward like the inside of a bowl
  • Antonym: convex - curved or rounded outward
