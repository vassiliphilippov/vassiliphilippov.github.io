Santa Fe

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn.tə ˈfeɪ / (AmE), / ˌsæn.tə ˈfeɪ / (BrE)
The capital city of the US state of New Mexico, located in the north-central part of the state.
capital city of New Mexico, USA
american american

Examples

Santa Fe is known for its Pueblo-style architecture and vibrant arts scene.
The weather in Santa Fe is often dry and sunny, attracting tourists year-round.
She moved to Santa Fe after falling in love with the city's culture and scenery.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Santa Fe', meaning 'Holy Faith'.
