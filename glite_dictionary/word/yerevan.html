Yerevan

1. PROPN

/ ˌjɛrəˈvɑn / (AmE), / ˌjɛərəˈvɑːn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Armenia, located in the west of the country on the Hrazdan River
capital of Armenia

Examples

She arrived in Yerevan to attend an international conference on education.
The ancient history of Yerevan attracts tourists from around the globe.
Direct flights to Yerevan have become more common in recent years.

Etymology

From Armenian Երևան (Yerevan).
