distillery

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɪləri / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪləri / (BrE)
A place or factory where alcoholic drinks are produced by distillation.
place where alcoholic drinks are made by distillation

Examples

The tour of the distillery showed how whiskey is made from start to finish.
She works at a family-owned distillery in Scotland.
New environmental regulations were introduced for every distillery in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brewery - place where beer is made

Etymology

From Latin 'distillare' (to trickle down, to distil) via French 'distillerie'.
