PHS

1. NOUN

/ piː eɪtʃ ɛs / (AmE), / piː eɪtʃ ɛs / (BrE)
an initialism for Public Health Service, a United States government agency responsible for protecting and promoting public health, including the office of the Surgeon General and related agencies
US Public Health Service agency (initialism)
american

Examples

After the hurricane, the PHS coordinated emergency medical services for affected communities.
The PHS works to prevent the spread of infectious diseases in the United States.
She started her public health career with the PHS in Washington, D.C.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Public Health Service.
