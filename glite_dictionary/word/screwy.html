screwy

1. ADJ

/ ˈskru.i / (AmE), / ˈskruː.i / (BrE)
strange, odd, or not behaving in a normal way; eccentric or peculiar.
strange or not normal; odd
informal

Examples

Ever since he quit his job, his behavior has been really screwy.
That is the most screwy theory I've ever heard.
The lights keep flickering in a screwy way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or very strange
  • Antonym: normal - conforming to standard or expected behavior

Etymology

Derived from 'screw' plus the informal suffix '-y', referring to something being loose or not fastened properly.
