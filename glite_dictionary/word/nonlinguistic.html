nonlinguistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
not consisting of, or not related to, language or linguistic forms
not related to language

Examples

Many forms of nonlinguistic communication, such as gestures and facial expressions, convey powerful messages.
The test measured both nonlinguistic and linguistic abilities in children.
Her research focuses on nonlinguistic aspects of cognitive development.

Synset Examples

depended on his nonlinguistic skills

Related Terms

  • Antonym: linguistic - relating to language or linguistics

Etymology

From prefix non- (not) + linguistic (relating to language).
