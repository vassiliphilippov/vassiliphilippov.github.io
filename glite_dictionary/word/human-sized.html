human-sized

1. ADJ

/ ˈhjuːmən saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən saɪzd / (BrE)
of a size similar to that of an average human being.
of a size similar to a human

Examples

The museum features a human-sized robot that interacts with visitors.
She built a human-sized sculpture out of recycled materials.
A human-sized shadow appeared at the entrance of the cave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life-sized - of the same size as the living person or thing represented
