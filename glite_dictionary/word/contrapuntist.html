contrapuntist

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɒn.trəˈpʌn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.trəˈpʌn.tɪst / (BrE)
a composer who specializes in the art of counterpoint
composer specializing in counterpoint

Examples

Bach is often considered history's greatest contrapuntist for his mastery of interweaving musical lines.
The contrapuntist delighted the audience with a fugue full of complex melodies.
As a skilled contrapuntist, she composed works celebrated for their intricate voice leading.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: counterpoint - the art or technique of setting, writing, or playing a melody or melodies in conjunction with another, according to fixed rules

Etymology

From Italian 'contrappuntista', based on 'contrappunto' (counterpoint).
