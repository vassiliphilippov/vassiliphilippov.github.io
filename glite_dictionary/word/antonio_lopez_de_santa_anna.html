Antonio Lopez de Santa Anna

1. PROPN

/ ɑnˌtoʊnioʊ ˈloʊpɛz deɪ ˌsɑntə ˈænə / (AmE), / ænˌtəʊniəʊ ˈləʊpez deɪ ˌsæntə ˈænə / (BrE)
(1794–1876) Mexican general and political leader, known for his role in the Texas Revolution and the Mexican-American War.
Mexican general and president, 19th century

Examples

Antonio Lopez de Santa Anna played a central role in Mexico's political and military affairs during the 19th century.
The defeat of Antonio Lopez de Santa Anna at the Battle of San Jacinto marked a turning point in the Texas Revolution.
Many historians consider Antonio Lopez de Santa Anna one of the most influential figures in Mexican history.
