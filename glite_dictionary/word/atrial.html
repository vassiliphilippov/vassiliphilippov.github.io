atrial

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪ.tri.əl / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.tri.əl / (BrE)
relating to an atrium, especially one of the upper chambers of the heart.
relating to an atrium (esp. in the heart)
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with atrial fibrillation after experiencing an irregular heartbeat.
Recent studies focus on the electrical activity of the atrial chambers in heart disease.
An atrial septal defect is a hole in the wall between two upper heart chambers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ventricular - relating to a ventricle, especially of the heart or brain

Etymology

From Latin atrium (central court or chamber).
