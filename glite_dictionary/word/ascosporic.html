ascosporic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæs.kəˈspɔr.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌæs.kəˈspɒr.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of ascospores, the spores produced within an ascus in certain fungi
relating to ascospores in fungi
medical

Examples

The ascosporic development in this species occurs under specific environmental conditions.
Researchers examined the ascosporic stage to better understand fungal reproduction.
The ascosporic structures can be observed clearly under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ascospore - a spore produced inside an ascus by fungi of the phylum Ascomycota

Etymology

From ascospore + -ic, indicating relation to ascospores.
