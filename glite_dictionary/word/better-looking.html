better-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛtər ˈlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌbɛtə ˈlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
More attractive or pleasing in appearance, especially by conforming to ideal standards of form or proportion.
more attractive in appearance

Examples

He was always considered the better-looking twin by their classmates.
That new car model is much better-looking than last year’s version.
Many people think her older brother is even better-looking than she is.

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-looking - attractive in appearance
  • Synonym: handsome - having an attractive, pleasing appearance (esp. of persons)
  • Antonym: less attractive - not as visually appealing as something else
