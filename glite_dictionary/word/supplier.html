supplier

1. NOUN

/ səˈplaɪər / (AmE), / səˈplaɪə / (BrE)
a person or company that provides goods or services to other businesses or customers
person or company providing goods or services

Examples

The restaurant switched to a new supplier for fresher vegetables.
Our company is looking for a reliable supplier of office equipment.
The supplier delivered the materials on time as promised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vendor - person or company selling goods or services
  • Antonym: customer - person or company buying goods or services

Etymology

From Middle English, derived from Old French 'soupleer', from Latin 'supplēre' (to fill up, complete).
