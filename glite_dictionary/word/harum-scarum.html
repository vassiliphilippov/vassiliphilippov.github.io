harum-scarum

1. ADV

/ ˌhɛɹəmˈskɛɹəm / (AmE), / ˌhɑːrəmˈskɑːrəm / (BrE)
in a wild, careless, or reckless manner
in a wild or reckless manner
informal

Examples

She drove harum-scarum down the winding country roads, barely slowing for the sharp turns.
The children dashed harum-scarum around the playground, shrieking with laughter.
He quit his job harum-scarum one morning, without any plans for what to do next.

Synset Examples

dashing harum-scarum all over the place
running pell-mell up the stairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recklessly - without regard for danger or consequences
  • Antonym: cautiously - in a careful or prudent manner

Etymology

Reduplicative form, possibly imitative of a jumbling or confused situation; first recorded in English in the 18th century.

2. ADJ

/ ˈhɛrəmˌskɛrəm / (AmE), / ˈharəmˌskarəm / (BrE)
Recklessly or cheerfully irresponsible; behaving in a wild, careless, or impetuous way.
recklessly irresponsible or wild

Examples

His harum-scarum spending habits soon landed him in trouble.
They lived a harum-scarum lifestyle in their youth, never thinking about tomorrow.
With her harum-scarum attitude, she was always up for an adventure.

Synset Examples

carefree with his money
freewheeling urban youths
had a harum-scarum youth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefree - free from worry or responsibility
  • Synonym: reckless - showing a lack of care about danger or consequences
  • Antonym: cautious - careful to avoid danger or mistakes

Etymology

Probably imitative, representing a sense of frantic or rushing confusion.

3. NOUN

/ ˌhɛrəmˈskɛrəm / (AmE), / ˌhɑːrəmˈskɛərəm / (BrE)
A person who acts recklessly, impulsively, or irresponsibly; a scatterbrained or wild individual.
reckless or impulsive person
informal

Examples

Everyone said that Jake was a harum-scarum who would never settle down.
The project failed because it was handed to a harum-scarum incapable of following instructions.
Her harum-scarum ways got her into trouble at school more than once.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is forgetful, disorganized, or lacking in concentration
  • Synonym: daredevil - a person who takes risks or behaves recklessly

Etymology

Reduplicated formation, possibly from 'harum' and 'scarum', both of uncertain origin, emphasizing disorder or confusion.
