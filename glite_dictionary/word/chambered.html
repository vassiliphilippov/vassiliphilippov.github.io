chambered

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃeɪmbərd / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbəd / (BrE)
divided into or having compartmental chambers.
having separate compartments or chambers

Examples

The chambered nautilus is famous for its beautifully spiraled shell.
Scientists discovered a chambered structure inside the ancient ruin, suggesting it was used for storage.
Many reptiles lay their eggs in chambered nests underground.

Synset Examples

a spiral chambered seashell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compartmentalized - divided into separate sections or compartments

Etymology

From the noun 'chamber' + suffix '-ed'.
