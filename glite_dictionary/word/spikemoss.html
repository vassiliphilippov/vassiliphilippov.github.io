spikemoss

1. NOUN

/ ˈspaɪkˌmɔs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪkˌmɒs / (BrE)
Any of numerous small, moss-like plants of the genus Selaginella, resembling true mosses but belonging to a different group of fern allies.
small fern ally of genus Selaginella

Examples

Many gardeners cultivate spikemoss for its delicate, moss-like foliage.
In damp forests, spikemoss often carpets the ground beneath larger trees.
Some species of spikemoss are used as decorative plants in terrariums.
