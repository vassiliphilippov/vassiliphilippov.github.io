Galleria

1. NOUN

/ ɡəˈlɪriə / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɪəriə / (BrE)
a genus of moths in the family Pyralidae, which includes the greater wax moth or honeycomb moth
genus of moths including the greater wax moth

Examples

Galleria larvae are known to infest beehives and feed on wax and honey.
The greater wax moth, belonging to the genus Galleria, is considered a pest by beekeepers.
Researchers studied the life cycle of various species in the Galleria genus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greater wax moth - species of moth in the genus Galleria, known for infesting beehives

Etymology

From Italian 'galleria' (gallery), adopted as a genus name in scientific taxonomy.
