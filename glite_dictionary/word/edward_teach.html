Edward Teach

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛdwərd tiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛdwəd tiːtʃ / (BrE)
(circa 1680–1718) An English pirate, also known as Blackbeard, who operated in the Caribbean and along the Atlantic coast of North America.
English pirate, known as Blackbeard

Examples

Historians believe that Edward Teach was one of the most feared pirates of his era.
The notorious pirate Edward Teach was better known as Blackbeard.
Stories of Edward Teach often mention his infamous black beard and ruthless tactics.
