autostrada

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːtoʊˈstrɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌaʊtəʊˈstrɑːdə / (BrE)
A high-speed expressway, especially one in Italy or an Italian-speaking country, often with tolls and limited access.
Italian expressway or motorway

Examples

To reach Florence, take the autostrada south from Bologna.
The Italian autostrada is known for its fast-moving traffic and toll booths.
While driving in Italy, we stopped at a service area along the autostrada for lunch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motorway - A wide road built for high-speed traffic, especially in the UK
  • Synonym: freeway - A high-speed, divided highway without intersections, chiefly in the US

Etymology

Italian, from 'auto-' (car) + 'strada' (road).
