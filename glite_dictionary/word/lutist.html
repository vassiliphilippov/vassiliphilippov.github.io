lutist

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːtɪst / (AmE), / ˈluːtɪst / (BrE)
A musician who plays the lute.
musician who plays the lute

Examples

The renowned lutist performed a beautiful solo during the Renaissance music festival.
As a lutist, she specializes in Baroque and early music performances.
It takes years of practice to become a skilled lutist and master the instrument's delicate sound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lutenist - a musician who plays the lute

Etymology

Formed from 'lute' + '-ist' (one who plays an instrument or is concerned with a subject).
