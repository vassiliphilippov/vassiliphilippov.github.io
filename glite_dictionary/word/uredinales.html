Uredinales

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌrɛdəˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / jʊˌriːdɪˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of parasitic fungi, commonly known as rust fungi, that cause rust diseases in plants
order of rust-causing parasitic fungi
medical

Examples

The crop was destroyed by an infection of Uredinales.
Researchers are studying Uredinales to develop resistant plant strains.
Many species within the Uredinales are economically significant due to their impact on agriculture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rust fungus - a fungus that infects plants and causes rust disease

Etymology

From New Latin 'Uredinales', based on 'Uredo' (a genus of rust fungi) + Latin '-ales' (suffix for orders).
