Viet Nam

1. PROPN

/ viːˈɛt ˌnɑːm / (AmE), / viːˈet ˌnæm / (BrE)
A country in Southeast Asia on the eastern edge of the Indochinese Peninsula, officially a communist state, and known for achieving independence from France in 1945.
country in Southeast Asia

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Viet Nam for its rich culture and beautiful landscapes.
After the war, Viet Nam underwent major economic reforms known as Đổi Mới.
Her parents immigrated from Viet Nam to the United States in the 1980s.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vietnamese - the language and people of Viet Nam

Etymology

From Vietnamese 'Việt Nam', meaning 'South Viet' or 'Southern Viet people'.
