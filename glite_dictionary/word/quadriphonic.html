quadriphonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkwɑːdrəˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkwɒdrɪˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the use of four separate channels to transmit and reproduce sound, especially for a surround-sound audio system.
relating to four-channel audio systems

Examples

The band recorded their latest album using a quadriphonic sound system.
He installed a vintage quadriphonic setup in his living room for a retro audio experience.
The concert featured an immersive quadriphonic audio arrangement that surrounded the audience with music.

Synset Examples

his quadraphonic sound system

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: stereo - Relating to sound that uses two channels to create a sense of width or directionality
  • Degree Higher: surround sound - A system using multiple channels for immersive audio beyond four channels

Etymology

From quadri- (meaning 'four') + phonic (relating to sound).
