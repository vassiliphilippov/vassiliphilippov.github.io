dunderhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌndɚˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʌndəhɛd / (BrE)
An unintelligent or foolish person.
foolish or stupid person
informal

Examples

Don't be such a dunderhead, just read the instructions.
The boss called him a dunderhead for making the same mistake twice.
Only a dunderhead would forget to lock the door every night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

Origin unknown; possibly from 'dunder' (fool) of uncertain origin and 'head'.
