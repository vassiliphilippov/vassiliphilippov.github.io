sponsorship

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɑːnsərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈspɒn.sə.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the act of supporting a person, organization, or activity by providing money, encouragement, or endorsement, especially officially or financially
the act of sponsoring or supporting

Examples

The event could not have happened without the generous sponsorship of several local businesses.
She is seeking sponsorship to help fund her trip across the country.
Corporate sponsorship plays a crucial role in the success of major sporting events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patronage - the support given by a patron, especially financial

Etymology

From sponsor + -ship, modelled after similar agent nouns in English.
