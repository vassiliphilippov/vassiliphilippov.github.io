kissing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of touching someone with the lips as a sign of affection, greeting, or love, often as playful or romantic foreplay.
touching with the lips in affection

Examples

The couple spent the afternoon kissing in the park, enjoying each other's company.
After a long day apart, their greeting included a lot of laughter and affectionate kissing.
Several guests noticed the newlyweds kissing quietly by the window during the reception.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smooching - the act of kissing amorously
  • Synonym: embracing - the act of holding someone closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection
  • Synonym: hugging - holding someone tightly in one's arms
