ranee

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːni / (AmE), / ˈrɑːni / (BrE)
A Hindu queen or princess, particularly the wife of a raja.
Hindu queen or princess

Examples

She was received at court as the ranee of a prosperous kingdom.
The ranee wore elaborate jewelry and traditional dress during the festival.
Historians praised the ranee for her wisdom and leadership.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: raja - a Hindu king or prince
  • Synonym: queen - a female monarch or wife of a king

Etymology

From Hindi rānī, feminine form of rājā (king).
