Walt Disney

1. PROPN

/ wɔlt ˈdɪzni / (AmE), / wɒlt ˈdɪzni / (BrE)
(1901–1966) American film producer, director, screenwriter, and animator who pioneered animated films and created iconic characters such as Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck.
American film producer and animation pioneer

Examples

Walt Disney transformed the entertainment industry with his innovative animated films.
Many children dream of visiting Disneyland, which was founded by Walt Disney.
Walt Disney's characters have become cultural icons recognized around the world.
