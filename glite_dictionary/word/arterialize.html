to arterialize

1. VERB

/ ɑːrˈtɪriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriəlaɪz / (BrE)
to change venous blood into arterial blood, typically by oxygenation
convert venous to arterial blood
medical

Examples

During the experiment, the device was used to arterialize the venous blood sample for better analysis.
Oxygen therapy can help arterialize the blood in patients with severe respiratory failure.
The process of arterializing blood is crucial in cardiopulmonary bypass procedures.

Etymology

From artery + -ize, meaning 'to make like an artery' or 'to give arterial characteristics'.
