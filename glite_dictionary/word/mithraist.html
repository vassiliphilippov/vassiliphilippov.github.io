Mithraist

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪθ.reɪ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɪθ.rə.ɪst / (BrE)
A follower or adherent of Mithraism, an ancient mystery religion centered on the god Mithras.
adherent of Mithraism

Examples

The ancient Roman soldier was a devoted Mithraist who regularly attended secret rituals.
Historians debate how widespread Mithraist beliefs were within the Roman Empire.
Few artifacts remain that clearly mark someone as a Mithraist rather than a member of another cult.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mithraism - an ancient mystery religion centered on Mithras

Etymology

From Mithra, the name of the Persian god, combined with the suffix -ist.
