perplexedly

1. ADV

/ pərˈplɛksɪdli / (AmE), / pəˈplɛksɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows confusion or puzzlement; in a perplexed manner.
in a confused or puzzled way

Examples

She glanced perplexedly at the complicated instructions on the package.
He answered the question perplexedly, unsure of what the interviewer meant.
The children looked perplexedly at the magician as the trick unfolded.

Synset Examples

he looked at his professor perplexedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusedly - in a confused manner

Etymology

From 'perplexed' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
