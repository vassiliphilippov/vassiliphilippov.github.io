bejeebus

1. NOUN

/ bəˈdʒiːbəs / (AmE), / bəˈdʒiːbəs / (BrE)
an exclamation or feeling of strong fear or shock; used informally to indicate being startled or surprised
strong fear or shock (informal)
informal

Examples

The horror movie nearly scared the bejeebus out of me.
When the car backfired, it startled the bejeebus out of everyone.
That enormous spider in the bathtub gave me the bejeebus!

Synset Examples

The thunderstorm last night gave me the bejeebus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bejesus - an exclamation of surprise or emphasis

Etymology

Variant of "bejesus" (an altered exclamation from "by Jesus" in Irish-English).
