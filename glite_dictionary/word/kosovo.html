Kosovo

1. PROPN

/ ˈkoʊ.səˌvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.səˌvəʊ / (BrE)
a partially recognized country in southeastern Europe, which declared independence from Serbia in 2008
partially recognized country in southeastern Europe

Examples

Many countries have recognized Kosovo as an independent state, but some have not.
She traveled to Kosovo to study its recent history and politics.
The status of Kosovo remains a topic of international debate.

Etymology

From Serbian and Albanian 'Kosovo', derived from Serbian 'Kosovo polje' meaning 'field of blackbirds'.
