increased

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkrist / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːst / (BrE)
made greater in size, amount, or degree; become larger or more numerous
made greater in size or amount

Examples

The company experienced increased demand for its products during the holiday season.
Increased rainfall this year has helped the crops grow well.
There is an increased risk of flooding after heavy storms.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decreased - made smaller in size, amount, or degree
