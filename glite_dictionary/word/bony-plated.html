bony-plated

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊni ˌpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊni ˌpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having the body covered with hard, bony plates, often for protection.
covered with bony plates

Examples

The bony-plated fish glided silently along the riverbed, its protective armor glinting in the sunlight.
Scientists discovered a bony-plated dinosaur fossil with overlapping plates down its back.
The bony-plated armadillo relies on its tough exterior to ward off predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armored - protected by armor or protective covering
