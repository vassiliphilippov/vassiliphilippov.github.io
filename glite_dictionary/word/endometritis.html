endometritis

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛndəʊməˈtraɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊmɪˈtraɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the endometrium, the lining of the uterus
inflammation of the uterine lining
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with endometritis following her surgery.
Antibiotics are typically prescribed to treat endometritis.
Fever and abdominal pain can be symptoms of endometritis in postpartum women.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metritis - inflammation of the uterus

Etymology

From endometrium (lining of the uterus) + Greek -itis (inflammation).
