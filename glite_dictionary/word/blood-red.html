blood-red

1. ADJ

/ blʌd ˈrɛd / (AmE), / blʌd ˈrɛd / (BrE)
Having a deep red color like that of blood, cherries, tomatoes, or rubies.
deep red color like blood

Examples

The sky turned a blood-red hue as the sun set behind the mountains.
She wore a blood-red dress that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The artist mixed various paints to achieve the perfect blood-red shade for his painting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crimson - deep red color inclining to purple
  • Synonym: scarlet - bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
