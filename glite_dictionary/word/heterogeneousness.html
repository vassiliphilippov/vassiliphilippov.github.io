heterogeneousness

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛt̬.ə.rəˈdʒiː.ni.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.ər.əˈdʒiː.ni.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being diverse in character or content
the state of being diverse or varied

Examples

The heterogeneousness of the city's population brings a rich variety of cultures and traditions.
The scientist emphasized the heterogeneousness of the experimental samples, which may affect the results.
In literature, the heterogeneousness of themes and styles often enhances the appeal of an anthology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heterogeneity - the state of being heterogeneous
  • Antonym: homogeneousness - the quality of being uniform or similar in kind

Etymology

From heterogeneous + -ness; ultimately from Greek hetero- (different) and genos (kind).
