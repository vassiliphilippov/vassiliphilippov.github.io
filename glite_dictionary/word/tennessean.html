Tennessean

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛnəˈsiən / (AmE), / ˌtɛnɪˈsiːən / (BrE)
a native or resident of the state of Tennessee, USA.
person from Tennessee

Examples

She is a proud Tennessean who grew up in Nashville.
Every Tennessean has their own favorite barbecue recipe.
The Tennessean delegation arrived early for the conference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alabamian - a native or resident of Alabama
  • Synonym: Tennessean - a native or resident of Tennessee

Etymology

From Tennessee (name of the state) + -an (forming demonyms).
