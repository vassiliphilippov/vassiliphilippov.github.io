Gianbattista Bodoni

1. PROPN

/ dʒiˌɑːnbɑːˌtiːstə boʊˈdoʊni / (AmE), / dʒiˌanbatˈtiːsta bəˈdəʊni / (BrE)
(1740–1813) Italian printer and typographer known for designing the Bodoni typeface.
Italian printer and typeface designer

Examples

The famous printer Gianbattista Bodoni revolutionized type design in the late eighteenth century.
Many books printed in the nineteenth century used fonts created by Gianbattista Bodoni.
Modern designers still admire the work of Gianbattista Bodoni for its elegance and clarity.
