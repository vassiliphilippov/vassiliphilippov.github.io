MapQuest

1. PROPN

/ ˈmæpˌkwɛst / (AmE), / ˈmæpˌkwɛst / (BrE)
A free, widely used online service that provides maps and driving directions.
online mapping and directions service

Examples

I used MapQuest to find the quickest route to the airport.
My parents still prefer MapQuest for planning long road trips.
Before smartphone apps, many people printed directions from MapQuest.

Etymology

Formed from 'map' + 'quest', referencing searching for maps.
