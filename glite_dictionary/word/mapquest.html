to mapquest

1. VERB

/ ˈmæpˌkwɛst / (AmE), / ˈmæpˌkwɛst / (BrE)
to look up directions or a location using the MapQuest website or application
look up directions on MapQuest
informal american

Examples

I'll mapquest the nearest gas station before we leave.
She mapquested the hotel address to make sure she wouldn't get lost.
Before smartphones, people often mapquested directions for their road trips.

Synset Examples

you can just mapquest the restaurant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to google - look up information online using Google; to search the internet

Etymology

From MapQuest, a popular web-based mapping service launched in 1996.
