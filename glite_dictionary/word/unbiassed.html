unbiassed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbaɪəst / (AmE), / ʌnˈbʌɪəst / (BrE)
not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; impartial and fair
impartial; not influenced by bias
british

Examples

The judge was praised for remaining unbiassed throughout the lengthy trial.
It is important to seek unbiassed advice when making financial decisions.
Reporting must be unbiassed to maintain the trust of the public.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbiased - not influenced by personal opinion; impartial
  • Antonym: partial - favoring one side over another; biased

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'unbiased', modeled after 'biased' (from bias + -ed) with prefix un-.
