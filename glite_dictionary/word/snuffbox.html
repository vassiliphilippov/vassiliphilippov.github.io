snuffbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌfˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈsnʌfˌbɒks / (BrE)
a small decorative container used for holding and carrying snuff, often kept in a pocket
small decorative box for snuff

Examples

He showed me his grandfather's antique snuffbox made of silver.
The museum displays a collection of ornate snuffboxes from the 18th century.
She found a small snuffbox hidden in the drawer while cleaning.

Etymology

From 'snuff' (powdered tobacco) + 'box', indicating its use to hold snuff.
