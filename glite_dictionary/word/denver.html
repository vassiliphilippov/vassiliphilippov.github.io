Denver

1. PROPN

/ ˈdɛnvər / (AmE), / ˈdɛnvə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Colorado, located in central Colorado on the South Platte River.
capital city of Colorado, US

Examples

She moved to Denver for a new job opportunity.
The weather in Denver can change rapidly throughout the day.
Denver is known for its proximity to the Rocky Mountains.

Etymology

Named after James W. Denver, a governor of the Kansas Territory.
