Neapolitan

1. NOUN

/ niˈɑːpəlɪtən / (AmE), / niˈapəlɪtən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Naples, a city in southern Italy.
native or inhabitant of Naples, Italy

Examples

Her grandfather was a proud Neapolitan who often talked about the beauty of his hometown.
The Neapolitans are famous for their vibrant culture and delicious cuisine.
Many Neapolitans have settled in cities around the world, bringing their traditions with them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Naples - a city in southern Italy

Etymology

From Italian 'napoletano', from Napoli (Naples).
