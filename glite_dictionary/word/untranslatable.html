untranslatable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌntrænzˈleɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌntrɑːnsˈleɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a word, phrase, or work) not able to be rendered into another language or form without loss of meaning or effect.
not able to be translated

Examples

The poem is filled with untranslatable expressions that lose their beauty in English.
Many cultural concepts are so unique that they seem untranslatable into other languages.
Her artwork communicates emotions that feel almost untranslatable.

Synset Examples

an untranslatable idiom
untranslatable art

Related Terms

  • Antonym: translatable - able to be translated into another language or form

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'translatable' (able to be translated).
