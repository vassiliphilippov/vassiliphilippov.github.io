crotaphion

1. NOUN

/ kroʊˈteɪfiˌɑn / (AmE), / krəˈteɪfiən / (BrE)
The point corresponding to the tip of the greater wing of the sphenoid bone in the human skull.
anatomical point on sphenoid bone
medical

Examples

In the anatomy lab, we identified the crotaphion as a key landmark on the skull.
Damage near the crotaphion could affect the surrounding cranial structures.
On the X-ray, the doctor pointed to the crotaphion for precise orientation.

Etymology

From Greek 'krotaphion', from 'krotaphos' meaning 'temple (of the head)'.
