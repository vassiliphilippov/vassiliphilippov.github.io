Adam

1. PROPN

/ ˈædəm / (AmE), / ˈadəm / (BrE)
In the Bible and Judeo-Christian tradition, the first man, husband of Eve, and progenitor of the human race.
the first man in the Bible

Examples

According to the Book of Genesis, Adam was created from the dust of the ground.
Many stories in Western culture refer to Adam and Eve as the ancestors of humanity.
The painting depicted Adam reaching out to touch the hand of God.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Eve - The first woman and wife of Adam in the Bible

Etymology

From Hebrew אָדָם (’Adam), meaning 'man' or 'human'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈædəm / (AmE), / ˈædəm / (BrE)
A slang term for the drug methylenedioxymethamphetamine (MDMA), commonly known as ecstasy.
slang term for MDMA (ecstasy)
slang

Examples

He was caught trying to sell Adam at the club last night.
Some people mistakenly believe that Adam is safer than other party drugs.
The new law increased penalties for possession of Adam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstasy - a common street name for MDMA
  • Synonym: MDMA - the chemical name for the drug called ecstasy
