shipowner

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌəʊnə / (BrE)
a person or company that owns a ship or a share in a ship
person or company owning a ship

Examples

The shipowner invested heavily in modernizing his fleet to comply with new regulations.
As a shipowner, she was responsible for both the profits and the risks associated with maritime trade.
Several shipowners attended the conference to discuss environmental challenges in the shipping industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mariner - a person who works on a ship; a sailor
  • Antonym: charterer - a person or company that hires a ship from a shipowner

Etymology

From 'ship' + 'owner', indicating a person or entity that owns a ship.
