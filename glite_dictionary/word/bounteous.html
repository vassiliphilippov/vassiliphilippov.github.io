bounteous

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaʊntiəs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊntiəs / (BrE)
Giving generously; showing or providing an abundance of generosity or resources.
generous; giving freely

Examples

The bounteous support of the community helped the charity reach its goal.
They were grateful for the bounteous harvest that provided food for the entire winter.
She attributed her success to the bounteous opportunities offered by her mentors.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bountiful - giving generously or in abundance
  • Synonym: munificent - very generous; more generous than usual
  • Antonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; not generous

Etymology

From Middle English, from Old French *bontieus*, from *bonté* (goodness), from Latin *bonitas* (goodness).
