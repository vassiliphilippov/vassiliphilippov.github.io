one hundred twenty-five

1. NUM

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti faɪv / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈtwɛnti faɪv / (BrE)
the number equivalent to the sum of one hundred and twenty-five units; written as 125.
the number 125

Examples

There are one hundred twenty-five employees working at the new office.
The machine can process one hundred twenty-five units per hour.
