timeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmləs / (BrE)
not affected by the passage of time; remaining beautiful, fashionable, or relevant despite changes over time.
not affected by time; always relevant or beautiful

Examples

Her timeless style makes her stand out at any event, regardless of changing trends.
Shakespeare's plays are considered timeless masterpieces that still resonate today.
The timeless beauty of the mountains attracted artists for centuries.

Synset Examples

few characters are so dateless as Hamlet
Helen's timeless beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternal - lasting forever; without end or beginning
  • Antonym: temporary - lasting for a limited time only

Etymology

From time + -less, meaning 'without time or not subject to time'.
