nonreticulate

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.rɪˈtɪk.jə.lət / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈtɪk.jʊ.lət / (BrE)
not forming or resembling a network; lacking a net-like pattern or structure
not forming a network

Examples

The leaves of this plant are nonreticulate, displaying parallel veins instead of a network.
Fossils were classified based on whether their structures appeared reticulate or nonreticulate.
The nonreticulate pattern of the wing distinguishes this species from its relatives.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reticulate - forming or resembling a network

Etymology

From prefix non- (not) + Latin reticulatus (net-like).
