assuasive

1. ADJ

/ əˈsweɪsɪv / (AmE), / əˈsweɪsɪv / (BrE)
Having the ability to calm fears or relieve anxiety; soothing or reassuring.
calming or soothing; relieves anxiety

Examples

The teacher's assuasive words reassured the nervous students before their exam.
She found his assuasive manner comforting during the stressful meeting.
An assuasive presence in the room helped ease everyone's anxiety.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soothing - having a calming effect; reducing discomfort or pain
  • Synonym: calming - making less agitated or anxious
  • Antonym: alarming - causing fear or anxiety; disturbing

Etymology

From Latin 'assuavis' (sweetened).
