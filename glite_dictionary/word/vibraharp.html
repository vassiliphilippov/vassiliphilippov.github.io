vibraharp

1. NOUN

/ ˈvaɪ.brəˌhɑrp / (AmE), / ˈvɪb.rəˌhɑːp / (BrE)
A percussion instrument similar to a xylophone but with metal bars and rotating disks in the resonators that create a vibrato effect.
metal-bar percussion instrument like a xylophone

Examples

The jazz musician played a mesmerizing solo on the vibraharp during the concert.
She decided to learn the vibraharp because of its unique shimmering sound.
Many orchestras now include the vibraharp in their percussion section for modern compositions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vibraphone - a percussion instrument with metal bars and resonators, producing a vibrato sound

Etymology

Blend of 'vibrate' and 'harp.'
