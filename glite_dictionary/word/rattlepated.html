rattlepated

1. ADJ

/ ˈrætlˌpeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrætlˌpeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking good sense or discretion; scatterbrained or foolish.
scatterbrained; lacking sense
archaic

Examples

Her rattlepated ideas often got her into unnecessary trouble.
He laughed off his mistake, blaming his rattlepated nature for the confusion.
In the story, the rattlepated character forgets even the simplest instructions.

Synset Examples

his rattlebrained crackpot ideas
how rattlepated I am! I've forgotten what I came for

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scatterbrained - lacking concentration or organization

Etymology

Formed from 'rattle' and 'pated' (headed), meaning 'having a rattling head.'
