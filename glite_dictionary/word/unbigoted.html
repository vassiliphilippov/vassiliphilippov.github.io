unbigoted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɪɡətɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪɡətɪd / (BrE)
not having or showing bigotry; open-minded and tolerant.
open-minded, not bigoted

Examples

She is known for her unbigoted attitude toward people of all backgrounds.
An unbigoted leader promotes understanding and respect within the community.
His unbigoted views made him well-liked by friends across different cultures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bigoted - having or showing an obstinate belief in the superiority of one's own opinions and a prejudiced intolerance of the opinions of others
  • Synonym: tolerant - showing willingness to allow the existence of opinions or behavior that one does not necessarily agree with

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'bigoted' (prejudiced).
