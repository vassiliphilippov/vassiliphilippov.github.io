shell-less

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɛl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɛl.ləs / (BrE)
(of animals or fruits) having no shell.
without a shell (of animals or fruits)

Examples

The shell-less variety of snail is known as a slug.
Some nuts are sold shell-less for easy consumption.
The biologist studied several shell-less marine animals found along the shore.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shelled - having a shell
