Daypro

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪˌproʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌprəʊ / (BrE)
A trademarked nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to relieve pain and inflammation, especially in conditions such as arthritis.
trademarked anti-inflammatory drug
medical

Examples

My doctor prescribed Daypro to help manage my arthritis symptoms.
After starting Daypro, I noticed a significant reduction in joint pain.
Some patients experience stomach upset when taking Daypro for extended periods.
