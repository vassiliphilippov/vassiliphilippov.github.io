nudist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːdɪst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdɪst / (BrE)
a person who chooses to go without clothing, especially in designated social settings, often for health, religious, or philosophical reasons
person who practices social nudity

Examples

The beach was reserved for nudists seeking a relaxed, clothing-free environment.
As a lifelong nudist, she enjoys the sense of freedom and community.
The resort caters to both families and nudists alike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturist - a person who practices social nudity, often emphasizing harmony with nature

Etymology

From 'nude' (bare, unclothed) + '-ist' (person who practices or believes)
