eastmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈistˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈiːstməʊst / (BrE)
Farthest to the east in position or location.
farthest to the east

Examples

The eastmost point of the island offers stunning sunrise views every morning.
They settled in the eastmost village of the valley, close to the border.
The eastmost window of the house receives the first light of day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easternmost - Farthest to the east; most eastern
  • Antonym: westmost - Farthest to the west; most western

Etymology

Formed from 'east' + 'most', similar to 'utmost', modelled on Old English word formation patterns.
