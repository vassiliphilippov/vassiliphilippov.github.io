unattired

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.əˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / ʌn.əˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
not wearing any clothing; having removed one's attire.
not wearing clothes

Examples

The children splashed in the river, completely unattired and carefree.
A group of artists sketched a figure model who posed unattired for the class.
He stood unattired before the open window, lost in thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undressed - not wearing clothes; naked
  • Antonym: clothed - wearing clothes

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'attired' (dressed); 'attire' from Old French *atirer* (to equip, dress).
