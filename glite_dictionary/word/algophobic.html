algophobic

1. ADJ

/ ˌælɡəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌalɡəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Having an abnormal or excessive fear of pain.
fear of pain (medical)
medical

Examples

Some patients with chronic illness may become algophobic after years of discomfort.
The psychologist specialized in treating children who were algophobic and avoided medical checkups.
Her algophobic tendencies made even routine dental visits extremely stressful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: algophobia - abnormal fear of pain

Etymology

From Greek 'algos' (pain) and '-phobic' from 'phobos' (fear).
