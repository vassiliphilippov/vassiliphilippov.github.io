keypad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkiːˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌpad / (BrE)
a small keyboard or a set of keys arranged for the input of data, numbers, or commands, typically on a computer or electronic device
small keyboard for input

Examples

Please enter your PIN using the keypad on the ATM.
The calculator has a numeric keypad for easy data entry.
Some laptops have a separate keypad for numbers on the right side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard - a panel of keys for typing on a computer or typewriter

Etymology

Formed from 'key' (button on a keyboard) + 'pad' (a panel or surface).
