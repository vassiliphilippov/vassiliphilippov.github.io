orthopedic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔrθəˈpidɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the branch of medicine concerned with the correction of deformities or functional impairments of the skeletal system, especially the spine and joints.
relating to orthopedics (medicine)
medical

Examples

The doctor recommended orthopedic surgery to correct her spinal curvature.
He wears orthopedic shoes to support his arches.
The hospital is renowned for its orthopedic department specializing in joint replacements.

Synset Examples

orthopedic shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orthopaedic - British spelling of 'orthopedic'

Etymology

From French *orthopédique*, ultimately from Greek *orthos* (straight) and *paideia* (child-rearing).
