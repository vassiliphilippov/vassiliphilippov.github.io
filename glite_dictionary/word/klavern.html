klavern

1. NOUN

/ ˈklævərn / (AmE), / ˈklɑːvən / (BrE)
a local branch or meeting group of the Ku Klux Klan
local unit of the Ku Klux Klan

Examples

Members of the local klavern held secret meetings in the woods.
Each klavern was led by a person known as the Exalted Cyclops.
The authorities were monitoring activities of the klavern in that rural county.

Etymology

Formed by blending 'Klan' with 'cavern,' imitating the pseudo-Greek style of Ku Klux Klan terminology.
