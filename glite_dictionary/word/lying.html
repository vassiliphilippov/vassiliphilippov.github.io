lying

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of deliberately making a false statement; the act of not telling the truth
deliberate act of telling a lie

Examples

He was accused of lying during his testimony in court.
Lying can damage your reputation and relationships.
Children are often taught that lying is wrong.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: truth-telling - the act of telling the truth
  • Synonym: deception - the act of deceiving or misleading
