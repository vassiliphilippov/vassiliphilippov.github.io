1 Samuel

1. PROPN

/ ˈfɜrst ˈsæmjuəl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈsæmjʊəl / (BrE)
the first of two books in the Old Testament recounting the stories of Saul and David, including the rise of Israel's monarchy
book of the Old Testament about Saul and David

Examples

Many biblical scholars study 1 Samuel to understand the origins of the Israelite monarchy.
The story of David and Goliath is found in 1 Samuel.
Our church group is reading through 1 Samuel this month.
