black-tie

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæk taɪ / (AmE), / ˈblæk taɪ / (BrE)
requiring or characterized by moderately formal evening dress, especially a dinner jacket (tuxedo) and bow tie for men
formal dress code; dinner jacket required

Examples

The charity gala is a black-tie event, so be sure to dress accordingly.
He arrived at the black-tie dinner wearing a sharp tuxedo and polished shoes.
Invitations specified black-tie, and all the guests wore elegant attire.

Synset Examples

he wore semiformal attire
a black-tie dinner

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: white-tie - the most formal evening dress code, typically tailcoat and white bow tie
  • Degree Lower: casual - relaxed or informal dress code
