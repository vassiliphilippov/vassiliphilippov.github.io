deservingness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈzɜrvɪŋnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being deserving of something, especially aid, reward, or attention
the quality of being deserving

Examples

The committee debated the deservingness of each applicant before announcing their decision.
Policies are often designed to assess the deservingness of those seeking public benefits.
Her deservingness was evident in the dedication and hard work she displayed.

Synset Examples

there were many children whose deservingness he recognized and rewarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merit - quality of being particularly good or worthy

Etymology

From 'deserving' + '-ness', formed in English.
