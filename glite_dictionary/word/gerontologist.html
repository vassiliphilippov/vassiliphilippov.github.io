gerontologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʒɛrənˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛrənˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who specializes in the scientific study of aging and elderly people
specialist in the study of aging
medical

Examples

The hospital employed a gerontologist to support its aging patient population.
After earning her PhD, she became a respected gerontologist in the community.
A gerontologist researches the psychological and medical issues faced by older adults.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gerontology - the scientific study of old age and aging

Etymology

From gerontology + -ist; gerontology from Greek 'gēras' (old age) and 'logia' (study).
