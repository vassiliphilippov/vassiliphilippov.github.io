Superior

1. PROPN

/ suˈpɪriər / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
the largest of the Great Lakes in North America and the largest freshwater lake by surface area in the world
the largest Great Lake in North America

Examples

Many ships have sunk to the bottom of Lake Superior during powerful storms.
The waters of Superior are famously cold, even in the summer months.
Tourists flock to Superior for its stunning shoreline and scenic views.

Etymology

From French 'lac Supérieur' (upper lake), referring to its position above Lake Huron.
