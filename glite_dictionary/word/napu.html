napu

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑːpuː / (AmE), / ˈnɑːpuː / (BrE)
A species of chevrotain, larger than the kanchil, found in India and Malaysia; also known as the greater Malay chevrotain.
a larger chevrotain species in India and Malaysia

Examples

The napu is noticeably larger than the closely related kanchil.
Wildlife photographers often seek the elusive napu in Malaysian rainforests.
Researchers studied the habitat preferences of the napu for conservation efforts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chevrotain - a small, deer-like hoofed mammal found in Asia and Africa
  • Degree Lower: kanchil - the lesser Malay chevrotain, a smaller related species

Etymology

From Malay napuh, referring to the greater Malay chevrotain.
