Emily Brontë

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛmɪli ˈbrɒnti / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪli ˈbrɒnteɪ / (BrE)
(1818–1848) English novelist and poet, best known for her novel 'Wuthering Heights'.
English novelist (1818–1848), author of 'Wuthering Heights'

Examples

Emily Brontë wrote only one novel, but 'Wuthering Heights' is considered a classic of English literature.
