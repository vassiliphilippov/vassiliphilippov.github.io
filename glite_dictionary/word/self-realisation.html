self-realisation

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlf ˌriəˈleɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ˌrɪəˌlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The fulfillment of one's own potential and abilities.
fulfillment of one's potential

Examples

Many people spend their lives striving for self-realisation through their careers and relationships.
Achieving self-realisation often involves deep personal reflection and growth.
The philosopher argued that self-realisation is the highest goal a person can achieve.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-actualisation - the realization or fulfillment of one's talents and potential

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'realisation', ultimately from Latin 'realis' (actual) + '-ation'.
