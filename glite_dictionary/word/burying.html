burying

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛriɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of concealing or putting something under the ground
the act of burying something

Examples

The burying of treasure was a common practice among pirates.
He watched the burying of the time capsule in the school yard.
The burying of toxic waste has raised environmental concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burial - the act or ceremony of placing a dead body in the ground
  • Antonym: unearthing - the act of digging up from the ground

Etymology

From the verb 'bury', Old English *byrgan* (to bury, hide, inter).
