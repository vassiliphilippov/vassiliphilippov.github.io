Belgium

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɛldʒəm / (AmE), / ˈbɛldʒəm / (BrE)
a country in northwestern Europe known for being a constitutional monarchy; the headquarters of the European Union and NATO are located there
country in northwestern Europe

Examples

The capital of Belgium is Brussels, where many international institutions are based.
She traveled through Belgium to experience its famous chocolate and medieval towns.
NATO has its main headquarters in Belgium.

Etymology

From French 'Belgique', from Latin 'Belgica', after the Belgae, a group of tribes living in the region in Roman times.
