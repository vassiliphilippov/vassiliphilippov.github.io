irresponsibleness

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌrɪˈspɑnsəblnəs / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪˈspɒnsəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lacking a sense of responsibility or not being accountable for one's actions.
lack of responsibility

Examples

His irresponsibleness caused problems at work and upset his colleagues.
The company's financial troubles resulted from years of management irresponsibleness.
Parents worry about the irresponsibleness displayed by some young drivers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresponsibility - the state of not being responsible; carelessness
  • Antonym: responsibleness - the quality of being responsible

Etymology

From 'irresponsible' + '-ness', denoting the state or quality.
