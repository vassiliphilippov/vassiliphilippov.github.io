device

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
an emblematic design, especially one used in heraldry to identify a person, family, or organization
heraldic emblem or design

Examples

Their family device features a golden eagle on a blue field.
The knight wore a shield adorned with his personal device.
Each competitor displayed a unique device during the tournament.

Synset Examples

he was recognized by the device on his shield

Etymology

From Old French 'devis', from 'deviser' (to divide, devise), ultimately from Latin 'dividere' (to divide).

/ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
