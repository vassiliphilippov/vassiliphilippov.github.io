virile

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪrɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɪərʌɪl / (BrE)
(of a male) capable of sexual intercourse; having the physical capability to copulate.
sexually capable (male)

Examples

The doctor confirmed that he was still quite virile for his age.
Many ancient sculptures celebrated the virile qualities of the male form.
Despite his graying hair, he remained a virile and energetic man.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: impotent - unable to copulate or achieve erection

Etymology

From Latin virilis ('of a man, manly'), from vir ('man').

/ ˈvɪr.əl / (AmE), / ˈvɪə.raɪl / (BrE)

showing strength and energy

Examples

/ ˈvɪr.əl / (AmE), / ˈvɪə.raɪl / (BrE)
having qualities traditionally associated with adult men, such as strength, energy, or a strong sex drive
having traditionally masculine qualities

Examples

He spoke in a virile voice that commanded attention.
The athlete's virile physique inspired admiration among his peers.
In old movies, heroes were often portrayed as strong and virile men.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

From Latin virilis, from vir (man, male).
