roasting

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cooking food, especially meat, by dry heat in an oven, often with added fat
cooking meat by dry heat in an oven

Examples

The slow roasting of the turkey filled the kitchen with a delicious aroma.
For best results, allow the meat to rest after roasting.
She learned the technique of roasting vegetables until they were golden brown.

Synset Examples

the slow roasting took several hours

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baking - cooking food by dry heat in an oven, usually without fat

Etymology

From roast (verb), ultimately from Old French 'rostir' and Old High German 'rosten' (to roast).
