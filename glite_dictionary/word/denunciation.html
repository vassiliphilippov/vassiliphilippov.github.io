denunciation

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of publicly condemning or criticizing someone or something
public act of condemning

Examples

The activist's denunciation of corruption stirred public debate across the city.
His sudden denunciation of his former allies shocked everyone in the room.
The organization's denunciation of child labor was widely reported in the press.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: condemnation - the expression of very strong disapproval; censure
  • Antonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration

Etymology

From Latin 'denuntiatio' (announcement, warning); from 'denuntiare' (to announce, denounce).
