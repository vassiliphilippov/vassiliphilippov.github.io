William Caxton

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪljəm ˈkækstən / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈkækstən / (BrE)
(c. 1422–1491) The first English printer, known for producing the first book printed in English in 1474.
first English printer (c. 1422–1491)

Examples

William Caxton is credited with bringing the printing press to England.
Many of Chaucer's works were first published by William Caxton.
William Caxton's contribution was crucial to the development of English literature.
