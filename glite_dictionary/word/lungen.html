lungen

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌn.dʒən / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.dʒən / (BrE)
A tree of southeastern Asia to Australia, grown mainly for its sweet, edible fruit resembling litchi nuts; sometimes classified under the genera Euphorbia or Nephelium.
tropical tree with sweet edible fruit

Examples

The market stall was selling fresh lungen alongside other tropical fruits.
She tasted a lungen for the first time on her trip to Malaysia.
Lungen trees are often cultivated for their delicious, lychee-like fruit.
