parachuter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpærəˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute
person using a parachute

Examples

The experienced parachuter demonstrated flawless technique during her jump.
A group of parachuters landed gracefully in the open field.
Every parachuter must check their equipment before boarding the plane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skydiver - person who jumps from an aircraft and performs acrobatics before opening the parachute

Etymology

From 'parachute' (from French parachute) plus agent suffix '-er'.
