uncertified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɝː.tɪ.faɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
not having the official documents, credentials, or endorsement required for a particular purpose or role.
lacking official certification

Examples

The company warned that using uncertified software could pose security risks.
Applicants with uncertified qualifications may not be considered for the position.
After checking the records, the inspector found several uncertified products on the shelf.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: certified - having official recognition or documentation

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'certified'.
