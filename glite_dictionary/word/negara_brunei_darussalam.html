Negara Brunei Darussalam

1. PROPN

/ nəˈɡɑːrə bruˈnaɪ dəˌruːsɑːˈlɑːm / (AmE), / nəˈɡɑːrə bruˈnaɪ dəˌrʊːsɑːˈlɑːm / (BrE)
the official name for Brunei, a sultanate country located on the northwestern coast of Borneo, which gained independence from Great Britain in 1984
official name for Brunei (country)

Examples

The official name of Brunei is Negara Brunei Darussalam, which means 'The Abode of Peace.'
In 1984, Negara Brunei Darussalam became fully independent from British rule.
Tourists are often attracted to Negara Brunei Darussalam for its unique blend of culture and modernity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Brunei - short form; sultanate on Borneo

Etymology

Malay, meaning 'Nation of Brunei, the Abode of Peace.'
