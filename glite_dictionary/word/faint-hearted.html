faint-hearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌfeɪntˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌfeɪntˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
lacking courage, conviction, or boldness; timid or cowardly
lacking courage or boldness

Examples

Only the faint-hearted would turn back at the first sign of difficulty.
His faint-hearted response disappointed those who expected him to lead bravely.
This is not a project for the faint-hearted, as it requires both commitment and grit.

Synset Examples

faint heart ne'er won fair lady

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
  • Antonym: courageous - having or characterized by courage

Etymology

Formed from 'faint' (lacking in strength or courage) and 'hearted' (having a specified kind of heart or courage).
