learnedly

1. ADV

/ ˈlɜrnɪdli / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnɪdli / (BrE)
In a scholarly or erudite manner; showing knowledge or learning.
in a scholarly or erudite way

Examples

She spoke learnedly about ancient Greek philosophy during the seminar.
The judge wrote learnedly on the subject of constitutional law.
He answered learnedly when discussing the origins of the language.

Synset Examples

he talked eruditely about Indian mythology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eruditely - in an erudite manner; with great knowledge

Etymology

From 'learned' (showing knowledge) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
