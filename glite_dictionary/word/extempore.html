extempore

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈstɛmpəˌri / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛmpəri / (BrE)
Done or spoken without preparation; impromptu.
without preparation; impromptu

Examples

She delivered an extempore speech that impressed the entire audience.
The actor's extempore performance drew praise for its spontaneity and creativity.
During the meeting, he responded to a question extempore, without consulting his notes.
