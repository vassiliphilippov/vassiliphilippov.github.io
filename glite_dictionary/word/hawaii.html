Hawai'i

1. PROPN

/ həˈwaɪ.i / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i / (BrE)
a state of the United States located in the central Pacific Ocean, comprising a group of volcanic islands known as the Hawaiian Islands
U.S. state in the central Pacific

Examples

We spent two weeks in Hawai'i exploring its beautiful beaches and volcanoes.
Many traditional hula dances originate from Hawai'i.
The culture and language of Hawai'i are deeply rooted in its Polynesian heritage.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Hawaiʻi, the native name for the largest island in the group.
