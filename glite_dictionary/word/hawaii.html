Hawaii

1. PROPN

/ həˈwaɪ.i / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i / (BrE)
a state of the United States located in the central Pacific, comprising the Hawaiian Islands
U.S. state in the central Pacific

Examples

I have always wanted to visit Hawaii for its beautiful beaches and volcanic landscapes.
Hawaii became the 50th state of the United States in 1959.
They celebrated their honeymoon in Hawaii, enjoying the tropical climate.

Etymology

From Hawaiian Hawai‘i, the native name for the largest island and later the whole group.

2. PROPN

/ həˈwaɪ.i / (AmE), / həˈwaɪ.i / (BrE)
The largest and southernmost island of the Hawaiian archipelago, known for its volcanic peaks.
the largest Hawaiian island ('Big Island')

Examples

We spent two weeks exploring Hawaii and its magnificent volcanic landscapes.
Mauna Kea, the highest peak in Hawaii, is a popular spot for stargazing.
Tourists flock to Hawaii for its black sand beaches and lush rainforests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Big Island - another name for the island of Hawaii
  • Degree Lower: Maui - an island in the Hawaiian archipelago, smaller than Hawaii

Etymology

From Hawaiian Hawai‘i, the native name for the island.
