to hypophysectomise

1. VERB

/ haɪˌpɑfəˈsɛktəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / haɪˌpɒfɪˈsɛktəmaɪz / (BrE)
to surgically remove the pituitary gland
remove the pituitary gland surgically
medical british

Examples

The surgeon decided to hypophysectomise the patient to halt hormone production.
Researchers hypophysectomised the rats to study the effects of hormone loss.
Before we hypophysectomise the animal, we must prepare all surgical instruments carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to hypophysectomize - to surgically remove the pituitary gland (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'hypophysis' (pituitary gland) + Greek 'ektomē' (excision) + '-ise' (forming verbs).
