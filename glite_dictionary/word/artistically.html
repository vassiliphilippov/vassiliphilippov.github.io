artistically

1. ADV

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows skill or creativity in art; in an artistic manner.
in an artistic manner

Examples

The room was artistically decorated with vibrant, hand-painted murals.
She arranged the flowers artistically in a crystal vase.
The filmmaker used lighting artistically to create a dramatic effect.

Synset Examples

it was artistically decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creatively - in a creative or imaginative manner

Etymology

From 'artistic' + '-ally', by analogy with other adverbs from adjectives ending in '-ic'.
