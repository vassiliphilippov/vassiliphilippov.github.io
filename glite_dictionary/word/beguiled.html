beguiled

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈɡaɪld / (AmE), / bɪˈɡaɪld / (BrE)
filled with wonder and delight; captivated or charmed
filled with wonder and delight

Examples

The children listened with beguiled faces as the storyteller continued.
She gazed at the painting, completely beguiled by its beauty and detail.
His words left the audience beguiled and longing for more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enchanted - filled with great delight or magic
  • Synonym: captivated - held the interest and attention of
  • Antonym: bored - feeling weary due to lack of interest

Etymology

From Middle English begilen, from be- + guile (trickery, charm).
