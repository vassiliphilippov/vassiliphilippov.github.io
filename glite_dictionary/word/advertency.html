advertency

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɝː.tən.si / (AmE), / ədˈvɜː.tən.si / (BrE)
the quality or state of being attentive or heedful; attention
attention; heedfulness

Examples

Her lack of advertency to the instructions resulted in several mistakes.
He listened with close advertency to every word the speaker uttered.
Proper advertency to detail is crucial in scientific research.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inattention - lack of attention or heedfulness
  • Synonym: heedfulness - the quality of paying careful attention; attentiveness

Etymology

From Latin 'advertere' (to turn toward).
