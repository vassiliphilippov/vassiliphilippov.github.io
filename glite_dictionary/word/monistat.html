Monistat

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːnɪˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪˌstæt / (BrE)
a trademarked brand of antifungal medication (miconazole nitrate) used to treat yeast infections, especially vaginal candidiasis
brand of antifungal medication
medical

Examples

My doctor recommended Monistat to treat my yeast infection.
You can buy Monistat over the counter at most pharmacies.
Many women find relief from symptoms after one dose of Monistat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miconazole - an antifungal medication; the active ingredient in Monistat
