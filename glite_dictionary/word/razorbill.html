razorbill

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪzərˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəbɪl / (BrE)
a black-and-white seabird of the auk family, found in the northern Atlantic, with a compressed, sharp-edged bill
black-and-white Atlantic auk

Examples

The steep cliffs along the coastline are nesting sites for the razorbill.
We spotted a razorbill gliding gracefully over the waves.
Several razorbills dove into the water in search of fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auk - a bird of the family Alcidae, to which the razorbill belongs
