Giuseppe Verdi

1. PROPN

/ dʒuˈzɛpeɪ ˈvɛrdi / (AmE), / dʒuːˈzɛpeɪ ˈvɛːdi / (BrE)
(1813–1901) Italian composer renowned for his operas, including La Traviata, Rigoletto, and Aida.
Italian opera composer (1813–1901)

Examples

Giuseppe Verdi is celebrated for composing some of the greatest operas in history.
The music of Giuseppe Verdi continues to be performed in opera houses worldwide.
Many consider Giuseppe Verdi a master of dramatic and emotional musical expression.
