to mispronounce

1. VERB

/ ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns / (AmE), / ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to pronounce a word or sound incorrectly
pronounce incorrectly

Examples

It is easy to mispronounce words that you have only read in books.
He was embarrassed when he mispronounced the host's name during the introduction.
Many people mispronounce 'quinoa' when they see it on a menu.

Synset Examples

She mispronounces many Latinate words

Related Terms

  • Antonym: to pronounce - to say a word or sound in the correct way

Etymology

From prefix 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'pronounce', from Latin 'pronuntiare' (to proclaim).
