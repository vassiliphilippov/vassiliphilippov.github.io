Nyamuragira

1. PROPN

/ njɑːˌmuːrəˈɡiːrə / (AmE), / njæˌmʊərəˈɡɪərə / (BrE)
an active volcano located in eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, known as Africa's most active volcano
active volcano in eastern Congo

Examples

The eruption of Nyamuragira in 2010 attracted volcanologists from around the world.
Lava flows from Nyamuragira have periodically threatened local villages.
Tourists sometimes visit the Democratic Republic of the Congo to see Nyamuragira and its nearby wildlife.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mount Nyiragongo - another major active volcano near Nyamuragira in Congo
