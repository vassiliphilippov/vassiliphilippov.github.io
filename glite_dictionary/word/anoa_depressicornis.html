Anoa depressicornis

1. NOUN

/ əˈnoʊ.ə dɪˌprɛs.ɪˈkɔːr.nɪs / (AmE), / əˈnəʊ.ə dɪˌprɛs.ɪˈkɔː.nɪs / (BrE)
A small species of wild buffalo native to Sulawesi (the Celebes), Indonesia, having small, straight horns.
small wild buffalo of Sulawesi

Examples

The Anoa depressicornis is critically endangered due to hunting and habitat loss.
Researchers studied the habitat requirements of the Anoa depressicornis in northern Sulawesi forests.
Unlike larger water buffaloes, the Anoa depressicornis has small, straight horns and a more compact body.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anoa - a wild buffalo of Sulawesi, Indonesia

Etymology

From Latin: 'Anoa' (genus name) and 'depressicornis' (from 'depressus', pressed down, and 'cornu', horn).
