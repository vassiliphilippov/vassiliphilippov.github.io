VA

1. NOUN

/ viː eɪ / (AmE), / viː eɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for Virginia, a state in the eastern United States; formerly one of the original 13 colonies and one of the Confederate States during the American Civil War.
abbreviation: state of Virginia (USA)
american

Examples

The package will be shipped to Richmond, VA by tomorrow afternoon.
She grew up in Fairfax, VA before moving to New York.
The address listed is 123 Main Street, Arlington, VA 22201.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Virginia - a state in the eastern United States; the full name VA abbreviates

Etymology

Abbreviation of Virginia.

2. NOUN

/ ˌviː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌviː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for the United States Department of Veterans Affairs, the federal department responsible for the interests of military veterans.
U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (abbreviation)
american

Examples

My grandfather receives medical benefits through the VA.
The VA hospital in our city provides excellent care for veterans.
After his service, he applied for assistance from the VA.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Veterans Affairs - the United States federal department for military veterans

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'Veterans Affairs'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-08161130-n
Synset POS: n
