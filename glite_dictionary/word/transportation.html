transportation

1. NOUN

/ ˌtrænspərˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the business or industry involved in moving goods and materials from one place to another
business of moving goods and materials

Examples

The company specializes in transportation of steel across the country.
International transportation is essential for global trade and commerce.
New regulations are affecting the transportation industry this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipping - the process or business of transporting goods

Etymology

From Latin 'transportare' (to carry across), via Old French and Middle English.

Efficient transportation of goods is crucial for international trade.
