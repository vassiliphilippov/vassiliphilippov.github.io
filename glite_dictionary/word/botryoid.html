botryoid

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑː.tri.ˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.rɪ.ɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling a cluster of grapes in form or appearance; grape-like.
grape-like in form; cluster-shaped

Examples

The mineral formed botryoid clusters on the cave walls.
Some tumors exhibit a botryoid appearance under microscopic examination.
The artist sculpted the beads into a botryoid pattern for the necklace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grape-like - having the appearance or shape of grapes

Etymology

From Greek botryoeidēs, from botrys (bunch of grapes) + -oid (like).
