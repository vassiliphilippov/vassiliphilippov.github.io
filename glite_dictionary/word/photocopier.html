photocopier

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊtəˌkɑːpiər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌkɒpiə / (BrE)
A machine for making paper copies of documents or images by photographic or digital means.
machine for copying documents

Examples

The office recently bought a new photocopier to handle the increased workload.
Please use the photocopier in the hallway if you need extra copies.
She spent the morning fixing the jammed photocopier after it stopped working.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copy machine - another term for photocopier
  • Degree Lower: scanner - device for scanning documents as digital images

Etymology

From photo- (relating to light) + copier (from copy).
