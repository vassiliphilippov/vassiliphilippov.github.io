Jacques Lipchitz

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑk lɪpˈʃɪts / (AmE), / ʒak lɪpˈʃɪts / (BrE)
(1891–1973) A Lithuanian-born American sculptor known for pioneering cubist sculpture.
pioneering cubist sculptor

Examples

Jacques Lipchitz was instrumental in advancing the cubist movement in sculpture.
Works by Jacques Lipchitz are displayed in some of the world's most famous museums.
The influence of Jacques Lipchitz can be seen in the work of many modern sculptors.
