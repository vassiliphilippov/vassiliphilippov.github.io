sociology

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊ.siˈɑː.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌsəʊ.siˈɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The study of the development, structure, and functioning of human society.
study of human society

Examples

She decided to major in sociology because she was fascinated by how societies work.
The professor published a series of influential articles on the sociology of education.
Understanding urban sociology can help city planners create more inclusive communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anthropology - the study of human societies and cultures and their development
  • Degree Lower: psychology - the scientific study of the mind and behavior

Etymology

From French sociologie, from Latin socius (companion) plus Greek -logia (study).
