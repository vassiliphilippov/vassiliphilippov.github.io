henpecked

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛnˌpɛkt / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌpɛkt / (BrE)
(of a man) continually harassed, criticized, or controlled by his wife or female partner.
dominated or nagged by wife/partner

Examples

Everyone assumed he was henpecked because his wife made all the major decisions at home.
He grew tired of being henpecked and finally spoke up for himself.
The henpecked husband followed his wife's instructions without complaint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dominated - controlled or ruled by someone else
  • Degree Lower: submissive - readily yielding to the will of another
  • Degree Higher: oppressed - subjected to harsh treatment or domination

Etymology

Formed from 'hen' (female bird) + 'peck', originally referring to a hen pecking, used metaphorically since the 17th century.
