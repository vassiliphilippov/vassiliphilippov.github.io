suck-ass

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌk ˌæs / (AmE), / ˈsʌk ˌɑːs / (BrE)
(informal, slang) Of very poor quality or worthless; also, a person who flatters or is excessively obsequious to others.
very poor quality or sycophantic person (slang)
informal slang vulgar

Examples

The team's suck-ass performance disappointed all their fans.
I can't stand working with such a suck-ass colleague who agrees with everything the boss says.
That was a suck-ass movie; I wish I hadn't wasted my money.

Synset Examples

His suck-ass excuse didn't convince anyone.
Don't be a suck-ass; just admit your mistake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sycophant - a person who acts obsequiously towards someone important
  • Synonym: flatterer - someone who praises others insincerely, typically to gain advantage
  • Synonym: bootlicker - a person who acts in an excessively obedient or servile way
