surmountable

1. ADJ

/ sɚˈmaʊntəbəl / (AmE), / səˈmaʊntəbl / (BrE)
Able to be overcome, dealt with, or ascended.
able to be overcome or climbed

Examples

Although the mountain looked steep, the guide reassured us that it was surmountable.
With enough determination, any obstacle can become surmountable.
The company faced many challenges, but most were ultimately surmountable with teamwork.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insurmountable - impossible to overcome or conquer
  • Synonym: conquerable - able to be conquered or defeated

Etymology

From surmount + -able, based on Old French 'surmonter' (to surpass).

2. ADJ

/ sɚˈmaʊn.t̬ə.bəl / (AmE), / səˈmaʊn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
Possible to overcome or deal with successfully.
able to be overcome

Examples

Although the task seemed daunting at first, it was ultimately surmountable with teamwork.
The new regulations present challenges, but they are surmountable with proper planning.
Her injuries were serious but surmountable thanks to quick medical attention.

Synset Examples

situations of measurable and surmountable danger

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insurmountable - impossible to overcome or deal with

Etymology

From surmount + -able; surmount from Old French surmonter, from Latin supermontare (to climb over).
