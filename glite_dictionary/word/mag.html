mag

1. NOUN

/ mæɡ / (AmE), / mæɡ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a magazine; a periodic publication containing pictures, stories, and articles of interest
informal abbreviation for magazine (publication)
informal

Examples

Have you read the music mag everyone is talking about?
He subscribes to a science mag to keep up with new discoveries.
