packthread

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækˌθrɛd / (AmE), / ˈpækˌθrɛd / (BrE)
A strong three-ply twine used for sewing or tying packages.
strong twine for tying packages

Examples

He used packthread to secure the boxes before shipping them.
The old parcel was tied neatly with a length of packthread.
She repaired the torn sack using some packthread and a needle.

Etymology

From 'pack' (a bundle or parcel) and 'thread', indicating thread used to secure packages.
