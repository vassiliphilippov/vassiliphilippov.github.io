sad-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæd ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈsæd ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
having a face showing a sad expression
having a sad-looking face

Examples

The sad-faced clown tried to make the children laugh despite his own troubles.
I noticed a sad-faced man sitting alone on the park bench.
She gave me a sad-faced look when I said I couldn't come to her birthday party.

Synset Examples

the sad-faced clown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downcast - having a sad or depressed expression
  • Antonym: cheerful - having or showing a happy and positive expression
