transmitting

1. NOUN

/ trænzˈmɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sending or conveying a message, signal, or information from one place or person to another
act of sending a message or signal

Examples

Transmitting important medical data securely is crucial in modern healthcare.
The transmitting of information via satellite has revolutionized global communications.
During the experiment, the scientist monitored the transmitting of signals from the device.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmission - the action or process of transmitting something
  • Antonym: receiving - the act of getting or accepting something sent
