to labialize

1. VERB

/ ˈleɪ.bi.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlabiəlaɪz / (BrE)
to pronounce (a sound) with the lips rounded
pronounce with rounded lips

Examples

In some dialects, speakers may labialize the initial consonant of certain words.
Linguists study how people labialize vowels in different languages.
You can hear how she labialized the sound when she pronounced the word.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to labialize - to pronounce with the lips, especially rounded; alternative spelling and synonym of 'labialize'
  • Antonym: delabialize - to pronounce without rounding the lips; antonym of 'labialize'

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *labialis* (of the lips) + English suffix *-ize*.
