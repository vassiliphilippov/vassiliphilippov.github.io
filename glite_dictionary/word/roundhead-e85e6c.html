roundhead

1. NOUN

/ ˈraʊndˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person with a head that is short and broad, having a rounded shape (brachycephalic)
person with a broad, rounded head

Examples

The anthropologist classified several individuals as roundheads due to the broadness of their skulls.
Studies have shown that roundheads are more common in certain geographic regions.
The term roundhead is used to describe people with a characteristic rounded cranial shape.
