unimpassioned

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpæʃ.ənd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpæʃ.ənd / (BrE)
not influenced by strong emotions; calm and reasonable in attitude or expression
not affected by strong emotions

Examples

She gave an unimpassioned explanation of the facts, sticking strictly to the evidence.
The judge made an unimpassioned decision after carefully considering both sides.
Despite the criticism, his response remained calm and unimpassioned.

Synset Examples

answered with an unimpassioned defense
the unimpassioned intellect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispassionate - not influenced by strong emotion; able to be rational and impartial

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- (not) + impassioned (filled with passion).
