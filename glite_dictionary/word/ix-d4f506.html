ix

1. NUM

/ aɪ ɛks / (AmE), / aɪ ɛks / (BrE)
The Roman numeral for the number nine.
Roman numeral for nine

Examples

The clock had IX in place of the number nine on its face.
She received ix out of ten marks on the test.
According to Roman numerals, ix represents the number nine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nine - the number 9
  • Degree Higher: x - Roman numeral for ten
  • Degree Lower: viii - Roman numeral for eight

Etymology

From Latin 'IX', a combination of 'I' (one) before 'X' (ten), meaning one less than ten.
