scaley

1. ADJ

/ ˈskeɪli / (AmE), / ˈskeɪli / (BrE)
Having the body covered or partially covered with thin, overlapping horny plates, as in certain fish and reptiles.
covered with thin horny plates

Examples

Many reptiles are scaley, which helps protect their bodies from injury.
The scaley texture of the fish made it easy to identify its species.
After the lizard shed its skin, it appeared even more scaley than before.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scaly - Having scales or covered with scales
