transportable

1. ADJ

/ trænˈspɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / trænˈspɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be moved or carried from one place to another; portable.
capable of being moved

Examples

The shelter is transportable and can be assembled in less than an hour.
Many medical devices are designed to be transportable for emergency response teams.
They rented a transportable cabin for their mountain holiday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
  • Antonym: immovable - not able to be moved or transported

Etymology

From Latin 'transportare' (to carry across) + '-able'.
