unblinkingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈblɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no emotion, surprise, or reaction; without blinking or flinching
without showing emotion or reaction

Examples

She stared unblinkingly at the judge as he delivered the verdict.
The actor listened unblinkingly to the harsh criticism from the director.
Despite the shocking news, he sat unblinkingly, refusing to reveal any emotion.

Synset Examples

the convicted killer listened unblinkingly to the reading of his sentence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unflinchingly - in a way that does not show fear or hesitation
  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm or relaxed manner
