unpleasant

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈplɛzənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛzənt / (BrE)
Not enjoyable or agreeable; causing discomfort, offense, or unhappiness.
causing discomfort or dislike

Examples

There was an unpleasant smell coming from the garbage bin.
She had an unpleasant experience at the dentist's office.
It's unpleasant to work in such a noisy environment.

Synset Examples

an unpleasant personality
unpleasant repercussions
unpleasant odors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'pleasant' (giving pleasure).
