extraverted

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛk.strəˈvɝː.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛk.strəˈvɜː.tɪd / (BrE)
outgoing and interested in engaging with other people and the world around them; concerned primarily with external things rather than introspection
outgoing; socially oriented

Examples

She is highly extraverted and enjoys meeting new people at every opportunity.
His extraverted personality makes him a natural leader in group settings.
People who are extraverted often thrive in dynamic and social workplaces.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: introverted - tending to be inward-turning or focused on internal thoughts and feelings
  • Synonym: outgoing - friendly and socially confident

Etymology

Formed from 'extravert' + '-ed'; 'extravert' from Latin 'extra' (outside) + 'vertere' (to turn).
