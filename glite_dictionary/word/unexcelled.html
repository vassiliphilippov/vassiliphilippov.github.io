unexcelled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈsɛld / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsɛld / (BrE)
better than any other; not capable of being surpassed or improved upon.
unsurpassed; not capable of being bettered

Examples

Her dedication to her craft was unexcelled among her peers.
The restaurant is renowned for its unexcelled service and cuisine.
Their team's performance in the tournament was simply unexcelled this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no equal; unmatched
  • Synonym: unsurpassed - not exceeded by anything else; the best

Etymology

From un- (not) + excelled (past participle of excel).
