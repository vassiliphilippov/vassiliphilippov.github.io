Legionnaire

1. NOUN

/ ˌliːdʒəˈner / (AmE), / ˌliːdʒəˈneə / (BrE)
a member of the American Legion, an organization of U.S. war veterans.
member of the American Legion

Examples

My grandfather was a proud Legionnaire who attended every Memorial Day parade.
The local Legionnaire chapter organizes several charity events each year.
After returning from service, she became an active Legionnaire in her community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Legionnaire - a member of the French Foreign Legion

Etymology

From French 'légionnaire', originally meaning a member of a legion.
