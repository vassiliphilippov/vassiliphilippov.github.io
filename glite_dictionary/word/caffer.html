caffer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæfər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːfə / (BrE)
An offensive and insulting term used for a Black African, considered extremely derogatory and unacceptable in modern use.
offensive term for a Black African
offensive archaic

Examples

The word caffer is considered extremely offensive and should never be used.
Historical texts sometimes used the term caffer to refer to Black Africans, reflecting past prejudices.
Scholars study the etymology and usage of caffer to understand linguistic history and racism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kaffir - an offensive and insulting term for a Black African in South Africa

Etymology

From Arabic *kāfir* (infidel, non-believer), adopted via Afrikaans.
