sea-green

1. ADJ

/ ˈsiˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌɡriːn / (BrE)
bluish-green in color, like the sea
bluish-green color, like the sea

Examples

Her dress shimmered in a beautiful sea-green shade under the lights.
The painter mixed blue and yellow to achieve the perfect sea-green for the seascape.
We chose sea-green tiles to give the bathroom a fresh, coastal look.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teal - a dark greenish-blue color
  • Synonym: aquamarine - a pale blue-green color
  • Degree Higher: emerald - a bright green color, greener than sea-green
