chartless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑrtləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtləs / (BrE)
(of a place or area) not shown on a map or chart; unexplored or unknown.
not shown on a chart; unexplored or unknown area

Examples

The explorers set sail into the chartless waters beyond the known coastline.
Much of the continent remained chartless until the late nineteenth century.
He described the territory as a chartless wilderness, full of mystery and danger.

Synset Examples

uncharted seas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uncharted - not shown on any map or plan; unexplored

Etymology

From 'chart' + '-less', meaning 'without a chart'.
