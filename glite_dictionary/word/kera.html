Kera

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛrə / (AmE), / ˈkɛərə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Mayo-Kebbi Est Region of Chad, mainly in the Mont d'Illi department near Lake Tikem.
a Chadic language of Chad

Examples

The linguist conducted fieldwork to document the Kera language.
Many communities in southwestern Chad speak Kera as their first language.
Efforts are underway to preserve the unique traditions carried in the Kera language.
