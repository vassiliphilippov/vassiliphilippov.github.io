barytic

1. ADJ

/ bəˈrɪtɪk / (AmE), / bəˈrɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing baryta (barium oxide) or its compounds.
relating to baryta or barium compounds

Examples

The mineral sample exhibited a distinctly barytic composition under analysis.
Certain glass products use barytic materials to enhance their optical properties.
Barytes are the main ore of barium and are often associated with barytic minerals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baryta - barium oxide, a chemical compound

Etymology

From baryta (barium oxide) + -ic, forming adjectives relating to chemical substances.
