refection

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈfɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a light meal or refreshment, especially a snack or light repast
light meal or refreshment

Examples

The travelers paused at midday for a simple refection by the roadside.
After the lengthy meeting, we were served a modest refection in the lounge.
He preferred a quiet refection alone to a grand dinner with company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
  • Degree Higher: feast - a large celebratory meal

Etymology

From Latin refectio (restoration, refreshment), from reficere (to restore, refresh).
