twat

1. NOUN

/ twæt / (AmE), / twæt / (BrE)
(vulgar slang) The vulva or vagina; also used as an insulting term for a person, especially a woman.
vulgar slang for female genitals or a foolish person
vulgar offensive slang

Examples

She was furious when she overheard him use the word twat in the pub.
He got in trouble for calling his boss a twat during the meeting.
The comedian's routine was filled with crude words like twat that shocked the audience.

2. NOUN

/ twæt / (AmE), / twæt / (BrE)
A man who is regarded as stupid or incompetent; an idiot (slang, often considered vulgar).
stupid or incompetent man (slang, vulgar)
slang vulgar british

Examples

Don't be such a twat—you forgot your own keys again!
He's a complete twat when it comes to organizing anything.
Stop acting like a twat and help me with this, will you?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or foolish behaviour
  • Synonym: fool - a person who acts unwisely or imprudently
  • Antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intelligence or talent

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from dialect forms with varied vulgar senses.
