Chennai

1. PROPN

/ tʃəˈnaɪ / (AmE), / tʃəˈnaɪ / (BrE)
a large city in southeastern India, the capital of Tamil Nadu, formerly known as Madras
city in India; capital of Tamil Nadu

Examples

Chennai is known for its vibrant culture and historical landmarks.
She moved to Chennai to pursue a new job opportunity.
The weather in Chennai can be very hot and humid during the summer months.

Etymology

Renamed from 'Madras' to 'Chennai' in 1996 based on a local Tamil name.
