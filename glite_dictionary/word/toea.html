toea

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Papua New Guinea, equal to one hundredth of a kina
monetary unit of Papua New Guinea

Examples

I found some old toea coins in a drawer from my trip to Papua New Guinea.
The market vendor refused to accept payment in anything less than a full toea.
You can exchange your dollars for toea and kina at the airport currency counter.
