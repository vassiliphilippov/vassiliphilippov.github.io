sluggish

1. ADJ

/ ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (BrE)
moving, reacting, or working more slowly than normal and with less energy or activity than usual; lacking alertness or vigor.
slow or lacking energy

Examples

After staying up late, I felt sluggish all morning at work.
The river moved at a sluggish pace during the dry season.
His sluggish response worried the emergency medical team.

Synset Examples

she was fat and inert
a sluggish worker
a mind grown torpid in old age

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lively - full of energy and enthusiasm

Etymology

From Middle English sluggy (slothful) + -ish; related to 'slug' (slow-moving animal).

2. ADJ

/ ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (BrE)
moving, reacting, or working more slowly than normal and with less energy or activity; slow or lazy.
moving or working slowly; lacking energy

Examples

After eating too much, I often feel sluggish and unmotivated to do anything.
The project's progress has been sluggish due to a lack of resources.
Traffic was sluggish this morning because of the heavy rain.

Synset Examples

a sluggish stream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lethargic - feeling drowsy and lacking energy
  • Antonym: energetic - full of energy; active

Etymology

From Middle English 'sluggy' (sluggish, lazy), from 'slug' (a slow creature) + '-ish'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00984894-s
Synset POS: s

3. ADJ

/ ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪʃ / (BrE)
(of business, market, etc.) not active, lively, or growing; showing little movement or activity.
not active or brisk (business, markets)

Examples

The sluggish economy has caused many companies to delay expansion plans.
Sales remained sluggish throughout the first quarter of the year.
A sluggish market left investors hesitant to buy new stocks.

Synset Examples

business is dull (or slow)
a sluggish market

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slow - not quick; lacking speed or briskness
  • Antonym: brisk - active, fast, and energetic

Etymology

From Middle English sloggish, from slog (to move slowly).

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00037111-s
Synset POS: s
