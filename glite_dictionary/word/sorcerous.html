sorcerous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɔːrsərəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.sər.əs / (BrE)
possessing or relating to supernatural or magical powers; characteristic of a sorcerer.
relating to magic or supernatural powers

Examples

Legends told of a sorcerous amulet that protected its wearer from harm.
The sorcerous atmosphere in the old castle sent chills down their spines.
Her sorcerous abilities were feared throughout the kingdom.

Synset Examples

charming incantations
magic signs that protect against adverse influence
a magical spell
'tis now the very witching time of night
wizard wands
wizardly powers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magical - possessing or using magic; supernatural
  • Antonym: mundane - lacking any supernatural or magical qualities; ordinary

Etymology

From Middle French *sorcier* (sorcerer), ultimately from Latin *sortiarius* (one who casts lots).
