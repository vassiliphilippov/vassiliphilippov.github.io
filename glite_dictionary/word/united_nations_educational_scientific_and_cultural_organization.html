United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization

1. PROPN

/ juˌnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz ɛˌdjuːˈkeɪʃənəl saɪˈɛntɪfɪk ənd ˈkʌltʃərəl ˌɔːɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / juˌnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənl saɪˈɛntɪfɪk ənd ˈkʌltʃ(ə)rəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A specialized agency of the United Nations that aims to promote international collaboration in education, science, culture, and communication.
UN agency for education, science, and culture

Examples

The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization declared the site a World Heritage landmark.
Education ministers gathered at the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization headquarters in Paris for discussions.
Many countries collaborate under the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization to preserve their cultural heritage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: UNESCO - abbreviation for United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization

Etymology

Formed in 1945. The name is an expansion of the acronym UNESCO.
