Emily Dickinson

1. PROPN

/ ˈɛməli ˈdɪkɪnsən / (AmE), / ˈɛməli ˈdɪkɪnsən / (BrE)
(1830–1886) an American poet renowned for her innovative and enigmatic poetry, much of it unpublished in her lifetime.
American poet (1830–1886)

Examples

Many scholars admire Emily Dickinson for her distinctive poetic voice and unconventional style.
The letters of Emily Dickinson provide insight into her personal life and creative process.
A new exhibition at the museum explores the legacy of Emily Dickinson and her poetry.
