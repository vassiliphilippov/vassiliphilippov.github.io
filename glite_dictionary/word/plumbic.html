plumbic

1. ADJ

/ ˈplʌmbɪk / (AmE), / ˈplʌmbɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or containing lead, especially with lead in the +4 oxidation state.
relating to lead (Pb), chemistry

Examples

The laboratory analyzed a plumbic compound for its toxicity.
Exposure to plumbic chemicals can be hazardous to human health.
Scientists studied the reaction of plumbic oxide with hydrochloric acid.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: plumbous - relating to or containing lead, especially with lead in the +2 oxidation state

Etymology

From Latin plumbum (lead) + -ic, indicating a higher valency.
