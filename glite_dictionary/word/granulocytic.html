granulocytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡræn.jə.loʊˈsɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡran.ju.ləˈsɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving granulocytes (a type of white blood cell containing granules in their cytoplasm)
relating to granulocytes (white blood cells)
medical

Examples

The doctor ordered tests to check for granulocytic abnormalities in the patient's blood sample.
The presence of granulocytic infiltration was noted in the tissue biopsy.
Some rare diseases are characterized by a granulocytic reaction to infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: granulocyte - a type of white blood cell with granules in its cytoplasm

Etymology

From 'granulocyte' (granule-containing cell) + '-ic' (relating to).
