Sir Charles Spencer Chaplin

1. PROPN

/ sɚ ˈtʃɑrlz ˈspɛnsɚ ˈtʃæplɪn / (AmE), / sə ˈtʃɑːlz ˈspɛnsə ˈtʃæplɪn / (BrE)
(1889–1977) English comedian, actor, and film maker, best known for portraying a downtrodden little man with baggy pants and a bowler hat.
English comedian and film maker (Charlie Chaplin) (1889–1977)
