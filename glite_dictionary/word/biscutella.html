Biscutella

1. NOUN

/ bɪs.kjuˈtɛl.ə / (AmE), / bɪs.kjuˈtɛl.ə / (BrE)
A genus of Eurasian herbs and small shrubs in the mustard family, commonly known as buckler mustard.
genus of Eurasian herbs; buckler mustard

Examples

Biscutella is commonly known as buckler mustard and is native to parts of Eurasia.
Botanists often study Biscutella because of its unique seed pod shape.
Plants in the Biscutella genus are sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Etymology

From Latin 'biscutella', referring to 'little biscuit', alluding to the shape of its seed pods.
