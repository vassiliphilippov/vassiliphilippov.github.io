liger

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪɡə / (BrE)
The hybrid offspring of a male lion and a female tiger.
offspring of a male lion and female tiger

Examples

The zoo welcomed a rare liger cub this summer.
A full-grown liger can weigh more than either parent species.
Many people have never seen a liger outside of documentaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiglon - offspring of a male tiger and a female lion

Etymology

Blend of 'lion' and 'tiger'.
