colitis

1. NOUN

/ kəˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / kəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the colon, often causing abdominal pain, diarrhea, and discomfort.
inflammation of the colon
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with colitis after several days of severe abdominal pain.
Chronic colitis can significantly affect a person's quality of life.
Symptoms of colitis include diarrhea, cramping, and bloating.

Etymology

From Latin 'colon' (colon) + '-itis' (inflammation).
