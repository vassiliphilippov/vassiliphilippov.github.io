speechmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːtʃˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who delivers a speech or formal address to an audience
person who delivers a speech

Examples

The speechmaker captivated the audience with her passionate words.
As a skilled speechmaker, he was often invited to address important gatherings.
During the ceremony, the main speechmaker spoke about unity and progress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orator - a person who delivers formal speeches, especially with skill

Etymology

From 'speech' + 'maker', meaning one who makes or delivers speeches.
