A/C

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˈsi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈsiː / (BrE)
An appliance designed to remove humidity from the air and cool it; air conditioner or air conditioning system.
air conditioner or air conditioning system

Examples

Could you please turn on the A/C? It's getting really hot in here.
The A/C in my car stopped working during the heatwave.
Many offices keep the A/C running all day during the summer months.

Synset Examples

we rarely turn on the air-conditioner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air-conditioner - a machine that cools and dehumidifies air

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'air conditioning' or 'air conditioner'.
