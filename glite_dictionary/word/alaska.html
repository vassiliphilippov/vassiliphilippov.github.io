Alaska

1. PROPN

/ əˈlæskə / (AmE), / əˈlɑːskə / (BrE)
the largest and northernmost state of the United States, located in northwestern North America bordering Canada and the Arctic and Pacific Oceans
a state in northwestern United States

Examples

We spent our summer vacation exploring Alaska's national parks and glaciers.
Alaska became the 49th state of the United States in 1959.
The cruise to Alaska offered breathtaking views of icebergs and wildlife.

Synset Examples

Alaska is the largest state in the United States

Etymology

From Aleut 'alaxsxa', meaning 'mainland' or 'object to which the action of the sea is directed'.
