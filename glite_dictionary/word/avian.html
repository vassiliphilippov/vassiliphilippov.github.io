avian

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪ.vi.ən / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.vi.ən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of birds.
relating to birds

Examples

The scientist studied avian migration patterns to understand changes in climate.
Many viruses, such as the avian flu, are named after their animal hosts.
Her interest in avian biology led her to a career in ornithology.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bird - a warm-blooded egg-laying vertebrate distinguished by feathers and wings

Etymology

From Latin avis (bird) + -an (adjectival suffix).
