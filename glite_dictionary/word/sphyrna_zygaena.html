Sphyrna zygaena

1. NOUN

/ ˈsfɪrnə zaɪˈɡiːnə / (AmE), / ˈsfɪənə zɪˈɡiːnə / (BrE)
the smooth hammerhead shark, a species of hammerhead shark found in warm temperate and tropical seas, fished for its hides and vitamin-rich liver
smooth hammerhead shark species

Examples

The Sphyrna zygaena is commonly found in coastal waters worldwide.
Unlike other sharks, the Sphyrna zygaena has a more rounded head.
Commercial fishing for Sphyrna zygaena is driven by demand for its liver oil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammerhead shark - a group of sharks with distinctive hammer-shaped heads

Etymology

From Greek sphyrna (hammer), referring to the hammer-shaped head.
