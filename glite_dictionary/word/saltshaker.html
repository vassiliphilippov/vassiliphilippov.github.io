saltshaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌʃeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈsɒltˌʃeɪkə / (BrE)
A container with a perforated top used for sprinkling salt onto food.
container for sprinkling salt

Examples

Please pass the saltshaker so I can season my eggs.
The saltshaker on the table was completely empty.
She bought a matching saltshaker and pepper shaker set for the new kitchen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pepper shaker - a container with a perforated top for sprinkling pepper
