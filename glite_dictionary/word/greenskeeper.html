greenskeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrinzˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnzˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person responsible for the care and maintenance of the greens, fairways, and other areas of a golf course
person who maintains a golf course

Examples

The greenskeeper mows the grass every morning before the golfers arrive.
After the storm, the greenskeeper worked overtime to repair the damaged bunkers.
She aspired to become a greenskeeper at the prestigious country club.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groundskeeper - person responsible for maintaining outdoor grounds

Etymology

Formed from 'green' (in the sense of areas of short grass on a golf course) + 'keeper' (one who cares for something).
