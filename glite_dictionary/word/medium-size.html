medium-size

1. ADJ

/ ˈmiːdiəm saɪz / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəm saɪz / (BrE)
of intermediate size; neither large nor small.
of intermediate size

Examples

We ordered a medium-size pizza for dinner.
She adopted a medium-size dog from the animal shelter.
The hotel offers medium-size conference rooms suitable for small groups.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of size less than average
  • Degree Higher: large - of size greater than average
  • Synonym: medium-sized - of intermediate size (alternate form)
