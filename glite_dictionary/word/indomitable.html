indomitable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɑmətəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(of a person or spirit) impossible to subdue or defeat; showing great determination and courage that cannot be overcome.
impossible to subdue or defeat

Examples

Despite the team's losses, her indomitable spirit kept everyone motivated.
His indomitable will helped him overcome every obstacle in his path.
The indomitable courage shown by the firefighters inspired the entire community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unconquerable - not able to be defeated or overcome

Etymology

From Latin indomitabilis, from in- (not) + domitare (to tame).
