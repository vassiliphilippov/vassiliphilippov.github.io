Alexis de Tocqueville

1. PROPN

/ əˈlɛksɪs də tɒkˈvɪl / (AmE), / əˈlɛksɪs də tɒkˈviːl / (BrE)
(1805–1859) French political writer and historian, best known for his analysis of American society and democracy.
French political writer and historian

Examples

Many scholars reference Alexis de Tocqueville when discussing the origins of American democracy.
The ideas of Alexis de Tocqueville continue to influence political thought today.
