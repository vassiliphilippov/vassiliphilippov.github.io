poultrywoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊltriˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈpəʊltriˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who sells poultry and poultry products.
female seller of poultry

Examples

The local poultrywoman offered fresh eggs at the farmers' market every Saturday.
As a successful poultrywoman, she managed both sales and animal care on her farm.
The poultrywoman greeted her regular customers with a warm smile.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: poultryman - male dealer in poultry and poultry products

Etymology

Formed from 'poultry' and 'woman,' following the same pattern as 'policeman' or 'saleswoman.'
