Swansea

1. PROPN

/ ˈswɑnˌzi / (AmE), / ˈswɒnzi / (BrE)
A port city in southern Wales, situated on an inlet of the Bristol Channel.
port city in southern Wales

Examples

She grew up in Swansea before moving to London for university.
The football team from Swansea won the match last night.
Many tourists visit Swansea for its beautiful coastline and lively cultural scene.

Etymology

From Welsh 'Abertawe' (mouth of the Tawe), but adapted from Old Norse 'Sveinsey' (Swein's island).
