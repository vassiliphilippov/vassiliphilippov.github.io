pattern

1. NOUN

/ ˈpætɚn / (AmE), / ˈpætn̩ / (BrE)
a graphical representation, often in polar or Cartesian coordinates, showing the spatial distribution of radiation from an antenna as a function of angle
graph showing antenna radiation distribution

Examples

The engineer analyzed the antenna's pattern to optimize signal coverage.
A directional antenna has a distinct pattern with strong radiation in one direction.
Comparing the patterns helped them select the best antenna for the application.
