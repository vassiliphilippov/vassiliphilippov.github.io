scrubbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈskrʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning a surface by rubbing it, usually with a brush, soap, and water
vigorous cleaning by rubbing

Examples

She spent hours scrubbing the kitchen floor until it shone.
The stubborn stains resisted even the most vigorous scrubbing.
A good scrubbing will remove most of the dirt from your shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrub - to clean a surface by rubbing it hard, typically with a brush
