convivially

1. ADV

/ kənˈvɪviəli / (AmE), / kənˈvɪvɪəli / (BrE)
in a friendly, lively, and enjoyable manner; sociably.
in a sociable and cheerful way

Examples

He greeted each guest convivially as they arrived at the party.
The colleagues laughed convivially over lunch after closing the deal.
She spoke convivially, putting everyone at ease during the dinner.

Synset Examples

‘Let's go and have a drink,’ she said convivially

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociably - in a friendly or social manner
  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic way

Etymology

From Latin 'convivium' (banquet, feast) via 'convivial' + -ly.
