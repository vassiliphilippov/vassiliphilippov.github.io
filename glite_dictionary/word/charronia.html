Charronia

1. NOUN

/ kəˈroʊniə / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊniə / (BrE)
A genus of mammals in the weasel family Mustelidae, containing the yellow-throated marten.
genus of weasel-family mammals

Examples

The yellow-throated marten is the only species classified under the genus Charronia.
Researchers studied the distribution of Charronia in different forest habitats.
Charronia is recognized for its distinctive throat coloration among mustelids.
