Dinka

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪŋkə / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋkə / (BrE)
the Nilotic language spoken primarily by the Dinka people of South Sudan
Nilotic language of South Sudan

Examples

Many people in South Sudan speak Dinka as their first language.
He is studying the Dinka language at university.
Some words in Dinka have no direct translation in English.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nuer - Nilotic language of South Sudan, closely related to Dinka
