Grenoble

1. PROPN

/ grəˈnoʊbəl / (AmE), / grəˈnəʊbəl / (BrE)
a city in southeastern France, situated on the Isère River, known for its history, universities, and as a center for alpine sports
city in southeastern France

Examples

She spent a semester studying at the university in Grenoble.
After visiting Paris, they took a train down to Grenoble for some skiing.
Grenoble is famous for hosting the 1968 Winter Olympics.
