attachment

1. NOUN

/ əˈtætʃmənt / (AmE), / əˈtætʃmənt / (BrE)
the act of attaching or affixing something to another object or surface
act of attaching something

Examples

The attachment of the label to the package must be secure.
Frequent attachment of decorations to the wall can leave marks.
Proper attachment of the cables ensures the device will function correctly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affixation - the process of affixing or attaching

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman and Old French 'atacher' (to attach) with suffix '-ment'.

Examples
