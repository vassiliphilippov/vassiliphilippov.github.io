dovekie

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌvki / (AmE), / ˈdʌvki / (BrE)
a small, short-billed auk (Alle alle) abundant in Arctic regions, also known as the little auk
small Arctic auk (bird)

Examples

Flocks of dovekie filled the Arctic sky as the ship approached the ice edge.
The dovekie is well adapted to survive in the harsh conditions of the northern Atlantic.
Birdwatchers spotted a rare dovekie near the coast outside its usual range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auk - a bird of the family Alcidae, related to the dovekie
