holidaymaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑːlɪˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈhɒlɪˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is travelling or staying away from home for pleasure during a holiday
person travelling for pleasure
british

Examples

The seaside town was full of holidaymakers enjoying the sunshine.
Every summer, thousands of holidaymakers flock to the Greek islands.
On their way to the airport, the family felt the excitement of being holidaymakers again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacationer - a person who is on vacation, especially in American English
