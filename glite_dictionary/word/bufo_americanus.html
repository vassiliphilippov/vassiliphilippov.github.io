Bufo americanus

1. NOUN

/ ˌbjuːfoʊ əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌbjuːfəʊ əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnəs / (BrE)
A species of toad native to North America, commonly known as the American toad.
species of American toad

Examples

During our hike, we spotted a Bufo americanus resting near the pond's edge.
The Bufo americanus is known for its distinctive call in the spring evenings.
Herpetologists often study the Bufo americanus because it is widespread in eastern North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toad - a tailless amphibian with dry, bumpy skin

Etymology

From Latin; 'Bufo' meaning 'toad', 'americanus' meaning 'of America'.
