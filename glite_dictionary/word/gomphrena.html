Gomphrena

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑmˈfriːnə / (AmE), / ɡɒmˈfriːnə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical herbs or subshrubs with flowers in dense heads, native to tropical America and Australia.
genus of tropical flowering plants

Examples

The vibrant purple Gomphrena added color to the tropical garden.
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of several Gomphrena species.
Many gardeners choose Gomphrena for its drought tolerance and extended blooming period.

Etymology

From Greek gomphos (club) + -rena, referring to the club-shaped flower heads.
