Defense

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
the federal executive department of the United States government responsible for safeguarding national security; oversees the armed forces and related agencies
U.S. federal department for national security
american

Examples

The President met with officials from the Defense Department to discuss military strategy.
Spending on Defense has increased significantly in recent years.
The Secretary of Defense announced a new cybersecurity initiative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Department of Defense - the formal name for the U.S. government department responsible for national security
  • Synonym: Pentagon - the headquarters of the U.S. Department of Defense, often used as a metonym
