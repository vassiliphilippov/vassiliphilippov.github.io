stepladder

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpˌlædɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌlædə / (BrE)
a portable ladder that can stand on its own, with steps on one side and hinges at the top so it can be folded
folding portable ladder with steps

Examples

He reached the top shelf by climbing the stepladder in the kitchen.
The painter unfolded his stepladder to reach the ceiling for touch-ups.
We stored the stepladder in the garage after finishing the repairs.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ladder - a structure consisting of two side pieces joined by rungs for climbing up or down

Etymology

Formed from 'step' + 'ladder', indicating a ladder with steps.
