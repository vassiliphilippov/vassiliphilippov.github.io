playmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who plays with another person
companion in play, especially for children

Examples

When I was a child, my brother was my favorite playmate.
The park was full of children searching for a new playmate.
She invited her playmate over for an afternoon of games and laughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friend - a person with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
  • Antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile to someone

Etymology

From 'play' + 'mate', meaning companion in play.
