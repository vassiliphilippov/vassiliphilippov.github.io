boffo

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑː.foʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒf.əʊ / (BrE)
Resoundingly successful or popular, especially in the entertainment industry.
very successful or popular
informal

Examples

The comedian's new show was a boffo hit with both critics and audiences.
After a boffo opening weekend, the movie set a new box office record.
Her latest novel received boffo reviews and quickly climbed the bestseller charts.
