Ashkhabad

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːʃkəˈbɑːd / (AmE), / ˌæʃkəˈbæd / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Turkmenistan, located near the border with Iran
capital city of Turkmenistan

Examples

The government of Ashkhabad announced new measures to improve public transport across the city.
After traveling through the Karakum Desert, we finally arrived in Ashkhabad.
Many international conferences are held in Ashkhabad due to its growing significance in Central Asia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aşgabat - alternative name/spelling for Ashkhabad, the capital of Turkmenistan

Etymology

Russian form of Turkmen 'Aşgabat', itself from Persian 'Ešqābād' (city of love).
