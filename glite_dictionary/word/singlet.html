singlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɡlət / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡlɪt / (BrE)
A sleeveless, collarless men's undergarment or athletic garment worn on the upper body.
sleeveless men's undergarment

Examples

He wore a white singlet under his shirt for extra warmth.
Wrestlers often compete in a tight-fitting singlet.
On hot days, he liked to relax at home in just shorts and a singlet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vest - a sleeveless garment worn on the upper part of the body

Etymology

From single (one) with the diminutive suffix -et, originally referring to a single piece of clothing.
