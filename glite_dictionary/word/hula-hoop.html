hula-hoop

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːlə huːp / (AmE), / ˈhjuːlə huːp / (BrE)
a large plastic ring that is swung around the waist, limbs, or neck as a form of play or exercise
plastic hoop toy for spinning around the body

Examples

She can easily spin a hula-hoop around her waist for minutes at a time.
Children played with a hula-hoop in the park, laughing as they competed to see who could keep it spinning longest.
After discovering a hula-hoop in the garage, he started practicing new tricks every afternoon.

Etymology

Named after the Hawaiian hula dance, due to the swaying hip movement, and 'hoop' for the ring shape.
