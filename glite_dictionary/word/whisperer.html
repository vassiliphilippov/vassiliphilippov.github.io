whisperer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪspərər / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərə / (BrE)
a person who speaks in a whisper
one who whispers when speaking

Examples

The whisperer moved closer so that only I could hear her message.
During the exam, a whisperer in the back row tried to pass answers to a friend.
Stories spread about the mysterious whisperer who delivered secrets in hushed tones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisper - to speak very quietly so that only someone close can hear you
