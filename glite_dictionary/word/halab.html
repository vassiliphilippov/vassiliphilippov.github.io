Halab

1. PROPN

/ hɑːˈlæb / (AmE), / hɑːˈlæb / (BrE)
The ancient and alternative name for Aleppo, a major city in northwestern Syria.
ancient/alternative name for Aleppo, Syria

Examples

Ancient traders traveled through Halab due to its strategic location.
The historic architecture of Halab attracts scholars and tourists alike.
Halab has played a significant role in Middle Eastern history for centuries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Aleppo - the largest city in northern Syria

Etymology

From Arabic Ḥalab, the historical name for the city now known as Aleppo.
