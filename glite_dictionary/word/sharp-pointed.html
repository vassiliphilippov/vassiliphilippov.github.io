sharp-pointed

1. ADJ

/ ʃɑrp ˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp ˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
having a pointed tip or end that is sharp; ending in a sharp point.
having a pointed sharp end

Examples

Be careful, the sharp-pointed needle can easily prick your finger.
She wore shoes with sharp-pointed heels that clicked on the floor.
The bird has a sharp-pointed beak perfect for catching insects.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: blunt - having a dull or rounded end, not sharp or pointed
  • Synonym: pointed - having a point or tip that is sharp or tapered
