tenderly

1. ADV

/ ˈtɛndɚli / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəli / (BrE)
In a gentle, loving, or careful way.
in a gentle or loving manner

Examples

She spoke tenderly to her newborn baby, calming him with her soft voice.
He held the old photograph tenderly, remembering happier times.
The nurse tenderly dressed the patient's wounds, making sure not to hurt him.

Synset Examples

tenderly she placed her arms round him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a gentle manner
  • Antonym: roughly - in a rough or harsh manner
