Levi's

1. PROPN

/ ˈliːvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈliːvaɪz / (BrE)
a popular American brand of denim jeans and related clothing, known for its durable, stylish designs
American jeans and clothing brand

Examples

He always wears Levi's because he likes their classic fit.
I bought a pair of Levi's on sale last weekend.
Levi's have been popular for generations around the world.

Etymology

Named after Levi Strauss, the founder of the company.
