Martynia fragrans

1. NOUN

/ mɑrˈtɪniə ˈfreɪɡrənz / (AmE), / mɑːˈtɪnɪə ˈfreɪɡrənz / (BrE)
A species of herbaceous plant in the genus Proboscidea, noted for its fragrant flowers.
species of herbaceous plant

Examples

Martynia fragrans is cultivated in some regions for its beautiful and aromatic flowers.
The garden featured several rare species, including Martynia fragrans.
Botanists studied the pollination of Martynia fragrans to understand its ecological relationships.

Etymology

Genus name from Latin 'martynia'; 'fragrans' from Latin meaning 'fragrant'.
