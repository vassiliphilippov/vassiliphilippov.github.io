ambassadorship

1. NOUN

/ əmˈbæs.ə.dɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / əmˈbæs.ə.dəˌʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, rank, or duties of an ambassador; the office of being an ambassador
the office of an ambassador

Examples

She was honored to accept the ambassadorship to France.
His ambassadorship marked a new chapter in diplomatic relations.
Rumors about the next ambassadorship appointment spread quickly through the ministry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambassador - a diplomat who represents their country abroad

Etymology

Formed from 'ambassador' + '-ship', denoting office or condition.
