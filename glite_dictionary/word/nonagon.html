nonagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑn.ə.ɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈnɒn.ə.ɡən / (BrE)
A polygon with nine straight sides and nine angles.
nine-sided polygon

Examples

The student drew a perfect nonagon for her geometry assignment.
A regular nonagon has equal sides and angles.
The design on the floor was based on the shape of a nonagon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polygon - a flat geometric figure with many sides
  • Degree Lower: octagon - an eight-sided polygon
  • Degree Higher: decagon - a ten-sided polygon

Etymology

From Latin 'nonus' (nine) + Greek 'gōnia' (angle).
