kentan

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛn.tæn / (AmE), / ˈkɛn.tæn / (BrE)
An East Asian perennial plant with large, reddish-orange, black-spotted flowers and strongly reflexed petals, commonly known as the tiger lily.
East Asian perennial plant; tiger lily

Examples

The garden was filled with kentan, their vibrant petals brightening the summer air.
She planted a row of kentans along the fence for a splash of color.
Few flowers attract more butterflies than the kentan in full bloom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiger lily - Common name for Lilium lancifolium, a perennial with orange-black spotted flowers
