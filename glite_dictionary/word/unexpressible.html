unexpressible

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Too strong, intense, or unusual to be described in words; defying expression.
too great to be expressed in words

Examples

The beauty of the landscape was simply unexpressible.
She felt an unexpressible sense of loss after the news.
Some emotions are so deep they remain unexpressible in ordinary language.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexpressible - too strong to be expressed in words

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'express' + '-ible' (able to be); modelled after 'inexpressible'.
