Ruandan

1. ADJ

/ ruˈɑːndən, ruˈændən / (AmE), / ruˈɑːndən, ruˈændən / (BrE)
relating to Rwanda or its people, culture, or language
of or relating to Rwanda

Examples

The museum displayed a collection of Ruandan artifacts.
She studied the history of Ruandan music for her thesis.
Tourists admired the beauty of the Ruandan landscape.

Synset Examples

Rwandan mountains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Rwandan - of or relating to Rwanda; alternative, more common form

Etymology

Formed from 'Rwanda' (the country) with the suffix '-an', standard for demonyms.
