Veronica anagallis-aquatica

1. NOUN

/ vəˈɹɑː.nɪ.kə ˌæn.əˌɡæl.ɪs əˌkwæt.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / vəˈrɒ.nɪ.kə ˌan.əˈɡæl.ɪs əˌkwæ.tɪ.kə / (BrE)
A species of flowering plant of wet places in Eurasia and North America, belonging to the plantain family; commonly known as water speedwell.
species of aquatic flowering plant

Examples

The banks of the river were lined with Veronica anagallis-aquatica in full bloom.
Researchers studied the habitat of Veronica anagallis-aquatica to understand aquatic plant diversity.
You can often find Veronica anagallis-aquatica growing in wetlands and shallow streams.

Etymology

From Latin; Veronica is a genus name, anagallis-aquatica means 'water pimpernel'.
