articulative

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊlətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving articulation, especially in speech or linguistics.
relating to articulation

Examples

The articulative properties of the tongue are crucial in producing clear speech sounds.
Her research focuses on articulative mechanisms in second language learners.
Phonetics students often analyze articulative features to understand how consonants are formed.

Synset Examples

articulatory features
articulatory phonetics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: articulatory - relating to articulation (especially in speech); essentially synonymous in linguistic contexts

Etymology

From Latin articulatus (divided into joints) + -ive (forming adjectives).
