chattily

1. ADV

/ ˈtʃætɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃætɪli / (BrE)
in a lively, informal, and friendly way, like someone who enjoys chatting
in a chatty manner

Examples

She spoke chattily about her recent travels, making everyone feel welcome.
The interviewer asked her questions, and she responded chattily and openly.
They laughed chattily over coffee at the small café.

Synset Examples

‘when I was a girl,’ she said chattily, ‘I used to ride a bicycle’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatty - talking a lot in a friendly, informal way
  • Antonym: reservedly - in a restrained or reserved manner

Etymology

From 'chatty' + '-ly' adverb suffix.
