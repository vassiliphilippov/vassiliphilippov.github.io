serviceman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪsmæn / (BrE)
a man who serves in the armed forces; a male member of a country's military
male member of the armed forces

Examples

After years as a serviceman, he returned home to start a new career.
The ceremony honored every serviceman who served in the conflict.
As a young serviceman, he was stationed overseas for two years.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: servicewoman - female member of the armed forces
  • Synonym: soldier - a person who serves in the army
  • Synonym: military personnel - persons serving in the armed forces

Etymology

Formed from 'service' + 'man', meaning man in military service.
