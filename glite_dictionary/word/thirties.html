thirties

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜrtiz / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)
the decade from 1930 to 1939.
the 1930s decade

Examples

The Great Depression had a profound impact during the thirties.
Several famous jazz musicians emerged in the thirties.
Art deco reached its peak popularity in the thirties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 1930s - the decade from 1930 to 1939

2. NOUN

/ ˈθɝːtiz / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)
the period in a person's life between the ages of 30 and 40
ages 30 to 39; period in life

Examples

Many people feel more confident in their thirties than they did in their twenties.
She travelled extensively during her thirties before settling down.
His thirties were a turning point in his personal and professional life.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: forties - the period of life between 40 and 50
  • Degree Lower: twenties - the period of life between 20 and 30
