Pierre Auguste Renoir

1. PROPN

/ piˌɛr oʊˈɡyst rɛnˈwɑr / (AmE), / pjɛər oʊˈɡyːst rɛnˈwɑː / (BrE)
(1841–1919) a French impressionist painter known for his vibrant use of color and depiction of everyday life.
French impressionist painter (1841–1919)
