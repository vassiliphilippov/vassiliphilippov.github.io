swiftly

1. ADV

/ ˈswɪftli / (AmE), / ˈswɪftli / (BrE)
quickly and smoothly; at high speed.
quickly; at high speed

Examples

The deer darted swiftly across the open field before anyone could react.
She swiftly apologized and corrected her mistake.
After the alarm rang, everyone exited the building swiftly and without panic.

Synset Examples

she moved swiftly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly

Etymology

From 'swift' (Old English swifte) + '-ly'.
