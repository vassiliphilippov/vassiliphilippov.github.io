Netherlander

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛðərlændər / (AmE), / ˈnɛðəlændə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Netherlands
person from the Netherlands

Examples

The famous artist Vincent van Gogh was a Netherlander celebrated worldwide for his work.
Many Netherlanders speak excellent English in addition to their native language.
She met a Netherlander while studying abroad in Amsterdam.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Dutchman - a man from the Netherlands
  • Synonym: Dutchwoman - a woman from the Netherlands

Etymology

From 'Netherlands', with the -er suffix denoting a person from a place.
