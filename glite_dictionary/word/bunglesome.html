bunglesome

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌŋɡəlsəm / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋɡəls(ə)m / (BrE)
difficult to handle or manage, especially because of awkward shape or clumsiness.
awkward and hard to manage

Examples

The oversized, bunglesome package was nearly impossible to fit through the door.
She struggled with the bunglesome bundle of old equipment up the stairs.
The new printer is too bunglesome to move without help.

Synset Examples

an awkward bundle to carry
a load of bunglesome paraphernalia
clumsy wooden shoes
the cello, a rather ungainly instrument for a girl

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awkward - difficult to use or deal with; causing inconvenience
  • Synonym: ungainly - moving in a clumsy or awkward way
  • Antonym: manageable - easy or possible to deal with

Etymology

From English "bungle" (to carry out a task clumsily) + "-some" (forming adjectives).
