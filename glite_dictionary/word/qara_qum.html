Qara Qum

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːrə ˈkuːm / (AmE), / ˌkɑːrə ˈkuːm / (BrE)
a desert in Turkmenistan, located south of the Aral Sea, known for its arid landscape
desert in Turkmenistan

Examples

The Qara Qum is one of the largest sand deserts in Central Asia.
Many irrigation projects have altered the landscape of the Qara Qum.
Camels are well adapted to the harsh conditions of the Qara Qum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Karakum Desert - alternative spelling for Qara Qum, desert in Turkmenistan

Etymology

From Turkmen 'gara kum', meaning 'black sand'.
