adulteress

1. NOUN

/ əˈdʌltɚəs / (AmE), / əˈdʌltərɪs / (BrE)
A woman who commits adultery; a female adulterer.
a woman who commits adultery

Examples

The scandal revealed that he had been seeing an adulteress for many years.
In the novel, the adulteress is shunned by her community.
Historical punishments for an adulteress were often much harsher than for an adulterer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulterer - a person who commits adultery; especially a man

Etymology

From Latin 'adulterissa', feminine of 'adulter' (adulterer).
