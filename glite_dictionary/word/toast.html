toast

1. NOUN

/ toʊst / (AmE), / təʊst / (BrE)
An act of drinking in honor of or to wish good health to a person or in celebration of an event.
honorary drink; celebratory act

Examples

He raised his glass to propose a toast to the newlyweds.
Let us drink a toast to the success of our project.
At the party, she gave a heartfelt toast in honor of her friend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salute - a gesture or action showing respect or celebration, often in drinking

Etymology

From Old French *tostée* (roasted bread) because spiced toasted bread was often placed in wine for flavor in ceremonies.
