bowelless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaʊ.əl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊ.əl.ləs / (BrE)
lacking compassion or pity; ruthless, especially in competitive situations
without compassion or ruthlessness
archaic

Examples

His bowelless business tactics left many competitors bankrupt.
The novel depicts a bowelless villain who stops at nothing to win.
In the glare of bowelless ambition, friendship often fades away.

Synset Examples

cutthroat competition
bowelless readiness to take advantage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruthless - having no pity; merciless or cruel
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

From 'bowel' (in archaic sense: seat of pity or compassion) + '-less'.
