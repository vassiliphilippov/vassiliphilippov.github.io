skillfully

1. ADV

/ ˈskɪlfəli / (AmE), / ˈskɪlfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows skill or expertise; with dexterity or competence
in a skillful or expert manner

Examples

The pastry chef decorated the cake skillfully, impressing all the guests with her steady hands.
He skillfully avoided the obstacles while driving through the narrow streets.
The artist skillfully blended colors to create a stunning sunset scene.

Synset Examples

fragments of a nearly complete jug, skillfully restored at the institute of archaeology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expertly - in an expert or proficient manner
  • Antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or unskillful manner

Etymology

Formed from 'skillful' + adverbial suffix '-ly'. 'Skillful' from 'skill' (Old Norse 'skil', distinction, discernment).
