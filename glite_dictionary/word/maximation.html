maximation

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæksəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæksɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of raising something to its highest possible point, condition, or position
raising to the highest level

Examples

The company's focus was the maximation of shareholder value.
Efficient resource allocation can lead to the maximation of productivity across departments.
Her strategy revolves around the maximation of opportunities and minimization of risk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maximization - the act of making something as large or as great as possible; most common form

Etymology

From 'maximize' + '-ation', after the pattern of 'maximization.'
