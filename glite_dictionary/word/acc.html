ACC

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪˌsiːˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌsiːˈsiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Air Combat Command, the primary provider of air combat weapon systems to the United States Air Force, operating fighter, bomber, reconnaissance, battle-management, and rescue aircraft
Air Combat Command of the US Air Force
american

Examples

The ACC plays a vital role in maintaining U.S. air superiority.
After the reorganization, several wings were transferred to the ACC.
Pilots assigned to the ACC receive advanced combat training.

Etymology

Abbreviation of Air Combat Command.
