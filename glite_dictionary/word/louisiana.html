Louisiana

1. PROPN

/ luˌiːziˈænə / (AmE), / luːˌiːziˈænə / (BrE)
a state in the southern United States, bordering the Gulf of Mexico, known for its diverse culture and history.
a state in the southern US

Examples

The city of New Orleans is located in Louisiana and is famous for its music and cuisine.
After moving to Louisiana, she quickly adapted to the humid climate and vibrant culture.
Many people visit Louisiana each year to experience Mardi Gras in person.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mississippi - a neighboring state to the east of Louisiana
  • Synonym: Texas - a neighboring state to the west of Louisiana

Etymology

Named after King Louis XIV of France; from French 'Louisiane'.
