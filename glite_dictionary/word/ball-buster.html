ball-buster

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌbʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbʌstə / (BrE)
A job or situation that is extremely demanding, arduous, or punishing.
very demanding or punishing task or situation
slang

Examples

Training for the marathon was a real ball-buster this year.
Finishing that project on time was a ball-buster for everyone involved.
The new manager's audit process is a total ball-buster.

Synset Examples

Vietnam was a ball-breaker

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ordeal - a difficult or painful experience
  • Antonym: cakewalk - something very easy to do

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌbʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbʌstə / (BrE)
(slang) A woman who is regarded as very demanding or domineering, especially one who is seen as undermining men's confidence or control.
demanding or domineering woman (slang)
slang offensive

Examples

Some coworkers said their manager was a real ball-buster who never showed any mercy.
In the film, she plays a classic ball-buster who won't let any man get in her way.
He's afraid of dating a ball-buster after his last difficult relationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harpy - a shrewish, unpleasant woman
  • Synonym: nag - a person, especially a woman, who constantly criticizes or complains

Etymology

Derived from the slang phrase 'bust someone's balls,' meaning to harass or dominate, with 'ball' as a euphemism for testicles.
