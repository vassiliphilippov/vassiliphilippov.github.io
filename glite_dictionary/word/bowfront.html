bowfront

1. ADJ

/ ˈboʊˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
having a front that curves outward; especially used to describe furniture or architectural features.
having an outwardly curving front

Examples

The antique cabinet had a beautiful bowfront that made it stand out from other pieces.
They admired the bowfront windows in the historic townhouse.
A bowfront dresser can add elegance to a bedroom's design.

Synset Examples

a bowfront dresser
a bowfront house

Etymology

Formed from 'bow' (curved shape) + 'front'.
