possessor

1. NOUN

/ pəˈzɛsər / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsə / (BrE)
a person who owns, holds, or has something; an owner
a person who owns or holds something

Examples

The new possessor of the painting displayed it proudly in his living room.
Under the law, the possessor of stolen goods can be prosecuted.
She is the rightful possessor of the estate after her uncle's passing.

Synset Examples

they are searching for the owner of the car
who is the owner of that friendly smile?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: owner - a person who possesses something
  • Synonym: holder - a person with legal possession of something

Etymology

From Latin 'possessor', from 'possidēre' (to possess, to own).
