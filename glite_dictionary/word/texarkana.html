Texarkana

1. PROPN

/ ˌtɛksɑːrˈkænə / (AmE), / ˌtɛksɑːˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
A city in southwest Arkansas on the Texas border, adjacent to Texarkana, Texas.
city on the Arkansas-Texas border

Examples

The train made a brief stop in Texarkana before continuing west.
Many travelers visit both Texarkana and its twin city across the state line.
She grew up in Texarkana, enjoying the unique culture of a border town.

Etymology

A blend of Texas, Arkansas, and Louisiana, the names of the bordering states.

2. PROPN

/ ˌtɛksɑːrˈkænə / (AmE), / ˌtɛksɑːˈkænə / (BrE)
a city in northeast Texas, located on the border with Arkansas and adjacent to a city of the same name in Arkansas.
city on the Texas-Arkansas border

Examples

Texarkana is known for being split by the state line between Texas and Arkansas.
She drove through Texarkana on her way to Dallas.
The unique layout of Texarkana attracts tourists interested in standing in two states at once.

Etymology

Formed from the names Texas, Arkansas, and Louisiana.
