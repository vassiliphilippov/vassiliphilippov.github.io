wordless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːd.ləs / (BrE)
expressed or occurring without the use of spoken or written words; silent or unspoken.
without words; silent or unspoken

Examples

She gave him a wordless look that spoke volumes about her disappointment.
Through a wordless gesture, he offered his comfort to the grieving family.
The wordless song carried deep emotion, moving everyone in the audience.

Synset Examples

a mute appeal
a silent curse
best grief is tongueless
the words stopped at her lips unsounded
unspoken grief
choking exasperation and wordless shame

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent - making no sound; not speaking
  • Synonym: unspoken - not expressed in words
  • Antonym: expressive - effectively conveying thought or feeling

Etymology

From 'word' + '-less', an Old English suffix meaning 'without'.
