Montpelier

1. PROPN

/ mɑntˈpiːliɚ / (AmE), / mɒntˈpiːliə / (BrE)
the capital city of the state of Vermont in the United States, located in north central Vermont
capital city of Vermont, USA

Examples

We visited Montpelier last summer and toured the statehouse.
Montpelier is the least populous state capital in the United States.
The conference will be held in Montpelier this October.

Etymology

Named after Montpellier, a city in southern France.
