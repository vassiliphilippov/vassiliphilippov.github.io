enrollee

1. NOUN

/ ɛnˈroʊˌli / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊˌliː / (BrE)
a person who is registered or enrolled in a class, course, or program
person registered in a class or program

Examples

Each enrollee must complete the online orientation before classes begin.
The total number of enrollees in the course exceeded the department's expectations.
International enrollees are required to submit additional documentation for admission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • Synonym: registrant - a person who registers for something, especially officially

Etymology

From French 'enrôler' (to enroll), with English suffix '-ee' indicating the person receiving the action.
