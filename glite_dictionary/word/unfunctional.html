unfunctional

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfʌŋkʃənl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌŋkʃənl / (BrE)
not suitable for practical or everyday use; not serving a functional purpose
not suitable for practical use

Examples

The designer chair looks impressive but is ultimately unfunctional in a real living room.
Many modern buildings are criticized for their unfunctional features that do not serve their occupants' needs.
Her proposal was rejected for being too theoretical and unfunctional for day-to-day operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonfunctional - not working or not having a function

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'functional'.
