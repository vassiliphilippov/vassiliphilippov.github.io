Costa Rica

1. PROPN

/ ˌkoʊstə ˈriːkə / (AmE), / ˌkɒstə ˈriːkə / (BrE)
a country in Central America, known for its political stability and biodiversity, with its capital at San José.
country in Central America

Examples

We spent two weeks exploring the rainforests and beaches of Costa Rica.
Costa Rica is renowned for its impressive commitment to environmental conservation.
She moved to Costa Rica to study wildlife biology and improve her Spanish.

Etymology

Spanish for 'rich coast', named by early Spanish explorers.
