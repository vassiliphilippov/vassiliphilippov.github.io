lilangeni

1. NOUN

/ li.lɑːnˈɡeɪ.ni / (AmE), / li.lænˈɡeː.ni / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Eswatini, equal to 100 cents
currency of Eswatini

Examples

The price of the souvenir was fifty lilangeni at the market.
She exchanged her dollars for lilangeni before entering Eswatini.
One lilangeni is divided into one hundred cents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rand - currency of South Africa, often traded at par with lilangeni

Etymology

From siSwati *lilangeni*, plural *emalangeni*, the currency name adopted in 1974.
