unhesitating

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌhɛzɪˈteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌhɛzɪˈteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
done or acting quickly and with confidence, without doubt or hesitation.
quick and confident, without hesitation

Examples

Her unhesitating decision impressed everyone in the meeting.
With unhesitating courage, he rushed to help the injured cyclist.
He gave an unhesitating answer to the difficult question.

Synset Examples

his reply was unhesitating

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hesitant - slow to act, lacking confidence or certainty

Etymology

from un- (not) + hesitating, based on Latin haesitare (to stick, hesitate)
