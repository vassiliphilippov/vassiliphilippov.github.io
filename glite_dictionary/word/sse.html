SSE

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛs ɛs ˈi / (AmE), / ˌɛs ɛs ˈiː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for the compass point midway between south and southeast, at 157.5° on a compass.
compass point between south and southeast

Examples

The ship changed course to SSE to avoid the storm.
Winds are expected to come from the SSE at 15 knots today.
Mountains lie to the SSE of the village, providing a dramatic backdrop.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: south - the compass point at 180°; opposite north
  • Degree Higher: southeast - the compass point at 135°, between south and east

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'south-southeast', formed from the initial letters.
