distrustfully

1. ADV

/ dɪsˈtrʌstfəli / (AmE), / dɪsˈtrʌstfəli / (BrE)
In a manner showing or expressing lack of trust or suspicion.
in a way that shows lack of trust

Examples

She glanced distrustfully at the stranger before answering his question.
The guard watched distrustfully as the visitor approached the entrance.
He distrustfully accepted the deal, still unsure of the other party's motives.

Synset Examples

she looked at him distrustfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspiciously - in a way showing suspicion or doubt
  • Antonym: trustfully - in a way showing trust
