consultancy

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌl.tən.si / (AmE), / kənˈsʌl.tən.si / (BrE)
the practice of giving expert advice professionally in a particular field
professional expert advice business

Examples

Her company runs a successful consultancy specializing in environmental policy.
They decided to hire a management consultancy to improve efficiency.
After retiring, he started his own IT consultancy to share his expertise.

Synset Examples

a business management consultancy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consulting - the business of giving expert advice to organizations

Etymology

From consult + -ancy, modeled on words like accountancy.
