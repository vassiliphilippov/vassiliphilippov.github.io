another

1. DET

/ əˈnʌðɚ / (AmE), / əˈnʌðə / (BrE)
one more person or thing in addition to a number already mentioned; a different person or thing from the one already mentioned or known about
one more; a different one

Examples

Can I have another cup of coffee, please?
She decided to stay for another week after her conference ended.
He looked at the problem from another perspective and found a solution.

Synset Examples

put it off to another (or some other) day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: other - used to refer to a person or thing that is different or distinct from one already mentioned
  • Antonym: same - identical; not different

Etymology

From Middle English, from an other (an + other).
