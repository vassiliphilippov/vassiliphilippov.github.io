pressman

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛsmən / (AmE), / ˈprɛsmən / (BrE)
a man whose occupation is to operate a printing press or is engaged in printing
person who operates a printing press

Examples

The pressman carefully watched the paper as it rolled off the press.
As a skilled pressman, he could diagnose and fix any problem with the machinery.
The printing company hired an experienced pressman to oversee the production of the new newspaper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: printer - person whose job is printing

Etymology

From 'press' (printing press) + 'man'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈprɛsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈprɛsˌman / (BrE)
a male journalist, especially one employed to provide news stories for newspapers or broadcast media
male journalist or reporter

Examples

The veteran pressman covered wars and political scandals throughout his long career.
Every pressman in the newsroom was eager to land the front-page story.
She spoke to a pressman after the award ceremony to discuss her upcoming project.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports news, especially for newspapers or broadcast media
  • Synonym: journalist - a person who practices journalism, reporting news for media

Etymology

From 'press' (printing or news media) + 'man'.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-87306750-n
Synset POS: n
