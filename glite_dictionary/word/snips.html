snips

1. NOUN

/ snɪps / (AmE), / snɪps / (BrE)
a type of hand shears used for cutting sheet metal or other thin materials
hand shears for cutting metal

Examples

Be sure to use snips to cut the metal flashing to the correct size.
The technician grabbed her snips to trim the sheet aluminum cleanly.
With a good pair of snips, you can easily make precise cuts in thin steel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shears - large scissors for cutting various materials
