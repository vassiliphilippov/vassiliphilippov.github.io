vulgarly

1. ADV

/ ˈvʌlɡɚli / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡəli / (BrE)
in a crude, coarse, or indecent manner, especially with reference to topics considered indecent or obscene
in a crude or indecent manner

Examples

The comedian spoke vulgarly about topics that shocked much of the audience.
Her remark was vulgarly suggestive, making everyone at the table uncomfortable.
He whistled vulgarly as the actress passed by, drawing disapproving looks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crudely - in a coarse or unrefined way
  • Synonym: obscenely - in an obscene or offensive manner
  • Antonym: refinedly - in a refined or elegant manner
