hormonal

1. ADJ

/ hɔrˈmoʊnəl / (AmE), / hɔːˈməʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to or affected by hormones, or caused by the presence or action of hormones
relating to hormones

Examples

Teenagers often experience hormonal changes during puberty.
Certain medications can disrupt the body's natural hormonal balance.
Her mood swings were later found to be hormonal in origin.

Synset Examples

hormonal changes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hormone - a regulatory substance produced in organisms to stimulate cells or tissues

Etymology

From hormone + -al, the suffix forming adjectives meaning 'relating to'.
