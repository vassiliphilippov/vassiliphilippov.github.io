advisee

1. NOUN

/ ædˈvaɪzi / (AmE), / ədˈvʌɪziː / (BrE)
a person who receives advice, especially in an academic context from an assigned advisor
person who receives advice, often a student

Examples

Each advisee should schedule a meeting with their academic advisor before enrolling in classes.
The professor tries to meet with every advisee at least once a semester.
As an advisee, you are encouraged to ask questions about your degree plan.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: advisor - a person who gives advice, especially in academic settings
  • Synonym: advisee - a person who receives advice (synonym: advisee)

Etymology

Formed from 'advise' + suffix '-ee' (one who receives an action), patterned after other agent nouns.
