mien

1. NOUN

/ miːn / (AmE), / miːn / (BrE)
a person's manner, appearance, or bearing, especially as it reveals their character or mood
a person's manner or appearance

Examples

Despite the chaos around her, her calm mien reassured everyone in the room.
His serious mien suggested he was deep in thought.
The teacher's stern mien kept the students focused and disciplined.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demeanor - outward behavior or bearing

Etymology

From French 'mine' (appearance), from Italian 'mina', from Latin 'minare' (to drive animals), related to 'minari' (to threaten).
