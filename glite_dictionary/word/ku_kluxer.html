Ku Kluxer

1. NOUN

/ ku ˈklʌksər / (AmE), / kuː ˈklʌksə / (BrE)
a member of the Ku Klux Klan, a secretive and racist organization in the United States
member of the Ku Klux Klan
offensive

Examples

The police arrested a known Ku Kluxer after evidence linked him to the burning cross.
She wrote a book detailing her experiences encountering a Ku Kluxer in her hometown.
Membership as a Ku Kluxer was shrouded in secrecy and fear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ku Klux Klan - a secretive, racist organization in the US

Etymology

From 'Ku Klux Klan', itself based on the Greek 'kyklos' (circle) and 'clan'.
