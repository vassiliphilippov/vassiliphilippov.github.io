hemopoietic

1. ADJ

/ ˌhiːməˌpoʊ.iˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊ.pɔɪˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involved in the formation of blood or blood cells.
pertaining to blood formation
medical

Examples

Researchers studied the hemopoietic activity of different bone marrow cells.
Certain diseases can impair hemopoietic function and reduce blood cell production.
The doctor explained how chemotherapy affects hemopoietic stem cells in patients.

Synset Examples

hemopoietic stem cells in bone marrow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematopoietic - relating to or involved in the formation of blood

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) + poiētikos (productive, making).
