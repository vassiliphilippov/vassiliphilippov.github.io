gratifyingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡræt̬.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈɡræt.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that gives pleasure or satisfaction.
in a pleasing or satisfying manner

Examples

The project was completed gratifyingly ahead of schedule.
Her efforts were gratifyingly recognized by the entire team.
The results were gratifyingly positive after months of hard work.

Synset Examples

the performance was at a gratifyingly high level

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disappointingly - in a disappointing manner

Etymology

From 'gratifying' + '-ly'. 'Gratifying' from Latin 'gratificari' (to do a favor, oblige).
