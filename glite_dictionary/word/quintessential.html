quintessential

1. ADJ

/ ˌkwɪn.tɪˈsɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌkwɪn.tɪˈsen.ʃəl / (BrE)
Representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class.
most perfect or typical example

Examples

The little black dress is the quintessential outfit for any formal occasion.
His performance was the quintessential example of classical piano playing.
The book is considered the quintessential guide to Mediterranean cooking.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: typical - having the distinctive qualities of a group or kind
  • Synonym: exemplary - serving as a desirable model; representing the best of its kind

Etymology

From Latin 'quintus' (fifth) and 'essentia' (essence); originally referring to the 'fifth essence' or purest form.
