entering

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛn.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of going or coming into a place
the act of going in

Examples

The entering of the guests was announced with music and applause.
Security checks are conducted before entering the building each morning.
Her nervousness was obvious when entering the crowded room.

Synset Examples

she made a grand entrance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entrance - the act of entering; an entry

Etymology

From the verb 'enter' + -ing, denoting the action or process.
