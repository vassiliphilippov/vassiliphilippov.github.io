impressively

1. ADV

/ ɪmˈprɛsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that evokes admiration or respect; in an impressive manner.
in an impressive manner

Examples

She delivered her speech impressively, captivating everyone in the room.
The team performed impressively despite facing many challenges.
He finished the project impressively ahead of the deadline.

Synset Examples

the students progressed impressively fast

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unimpressively - in an unimpressive manner
  • Synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention; notably

Etymology

From 'impressive' + '-ly', the adverb-forming suffix.
