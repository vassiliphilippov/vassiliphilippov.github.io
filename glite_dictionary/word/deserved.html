deserved

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈzɝvd / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
rightfully earned or justified; having a good or bad outcome that is appropriate for someone's actions
rightfully earned or justified

Examples

Her promotion was a deserved reward for years of hard work.
The team celebrated their deserved victory after a tough season.
He felt he had finally received the deserved recognition for his efforts.

Synset Examples

a merited success

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undeserved - not rightfully earned or justified
  • Synonym: earned - gained as deserved or merited

Etymology

From Old French 'deservir', from Latin 'deservire' (to serve zealously).
