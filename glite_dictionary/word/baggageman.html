baggageman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæɡɪdʒˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbaɡɪdʒˌman / (BrE)
A male attendant responsible for handling and taking care of passengers' baggage, especially on a train.
male attendant who cares for baggage

Examples

The baggageman quickly loaded the suitcases onto the train before departure.
As a baggageman, he ensured all luggage was transferred safely between stations.
The passengers thanked the baggageman for handling their bags with care.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: porter - a person whose job is to carry baggage for passengers

Etymology

From 'baggage' + 'man', indicating a man responsible for baggage.
