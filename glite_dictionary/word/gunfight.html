gunfight

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnfaɪt / (BrE)
a fight between people using guns, typically over a short duration
a fight involving guns

Examples

The film ended with a dramatic gunfight between the sheriff and the bandits.
There was a gunfight outside the bank that left several people injured.
After the gunfight, the police recovered several weapons from the scene.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shootout - a confrontation involving exchanged gunfire

Etymology

From 'gun' + 'fight', originally American English (19th century).
