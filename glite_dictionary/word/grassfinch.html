grassfinch

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræsˌfɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsˌfɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small, usually brightly colored Australian weaverbird, often kept as a cage bird
small Australian weaverbird

Examples

The pet store had several colorful grassfinches chirping in their cages.
Many bird enthusiasts admire the vibrant plumage of the grassfinch species from Australia.
She bought a pair of grassfinches for her aviary, attracted by their lively songs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finch - small to medium-sized seed-eating songbird with a conical bill
