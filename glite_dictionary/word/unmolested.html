unmolested

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.məˈlɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈlɛstɪd / (BrE)
Not interfered with, disturbed, or harmed; left in peace.
not disturbed or harmed

Examples

The bird built its nest unmolested in the quiet corner of the garden.
They were allowed to leave the meeting unmolested by the security guards.
The hikers passed through the forest unmolested by any wild animals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undisturbed - not interrupted or troubled; left alone
  • Antonym: molested - disturbed, attacked, or harmed

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'molested.' 'Molest' is from Latin 'molestus' (troublesome).
