cryptanalyst

1. NOUN

/ krɪpˈtænəlɪst / (AmE), / krɪpˈtænəlɪst / (BrE)
A person skilled in analyzing and decoding codes and ciphers.
person skilled in decoding codes

Examples

The military hired a brilliant cryptanalyst to break the enemy's coded messages.
As a skilled cryptanalyst, she could decipher even the most complex ciphers.
During World War II, Alan Turing worked as a cryptanalyst at Bletchley Park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cryptographer - person who creates or writes codes and ciphers
  • Synonym: cryptanalyst - person skilled in analyzing and decoding codes (synonym entry)

Etymology

From cryptanalysis, formed from Greek 'kryptos' (hidden) and 'analysis'.
