inane

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈneɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈneɪn / (BrE)
Extremely silly, pointless, or lacking sense; stupid.
extremely silly or pointless

Examples

The movie was filled with inane jokes that made the audience groan.
She grew tired of her colleague's inane comments during the meeting.
It's hard to concentrate when people keep making inane remarks nearby.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurd - wildly unreasonable, illogical, or inappropriate
  • Antonym: sensible - chosen in accordance with wisdom or prudence; likely to be of benefit

Etymology

From Latin inanis (empty, vain, foolish).
