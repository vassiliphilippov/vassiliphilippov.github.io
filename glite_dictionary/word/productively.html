productively

1. ADV

/ prəˈdʌktɪvli / (AmE), / prəˈdʌktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that produces good results or achieves a lot; efficiently and effectively.
in an efficient and effective way

Examples

The team worked productively throughout the entire afternoon to finish the project.
If you organize your tasks in advance, you can use your time more productively.
They discussed their differences productively and reached a compromise.

Synset Examples

they worked together productively for two years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficiently - in an efficient manner
  • Antonym: unproductively - in a manner that does not produce good results

Etymology

From 'productive' + '-ly'. 'Productive' from Latin 'productivus', from 'producere' (to bring forth).
