nation

1. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the people who live in a particular country, considered as a group
the people of a country

Examples

The whole nation watched the final moments of the match on television.
Her speech inspired hope throughout the nation.
The nation mourned the loss of their beloved leader.

Synset Examples

a statement that sums up the nation's mood
the news was announced to the nation
the whole country worshipped him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: country - the people of a country, the nation as a population

Etymology

From Latin 'natio' (birth, people, tribe), from 'nasci' (to be born).

2. NOUN

/ ˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A large community of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, often inhabiting a particular territory; especially, a federation of tribes, particularly referring to certain Native American peoples.
a large community or federation of tribes, especially Native American

Examples

The Shawnee nation played a significant role in North American history.
Treaties were signed between the government and the Creek nation.
Members of the Cherokee nation gathered for the annual festival.

Synset Examples

the Shawnee nation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tribe - a social group consisting of families or communities linked by social, economic, religious, or blood ties

Etymology

From Old French 'nacion', from Latin 'natio' (birth, tribe, people).

3. NOUN

/ ˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
A large group of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory and governed by a single government.
politically organized people under one government

Examples

France is a nation known for its rich history and culture.
The United Nations brings together delegates from almost every nation in the world.
Every nation has laws that reflect its values and traditions.

Synset Examples

the state has elected a new president
African nations
students who had come to the nation's capitol
the country's largest manufacturer
an industrialized land

Related Terms

  • Synonym: country - an area of land with its own government
  • Synonym: state - an organized political community under one government

Etymology

From Latin 'natio', meaning 'birth, people, nation'.
