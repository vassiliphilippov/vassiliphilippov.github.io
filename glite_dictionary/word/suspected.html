suspected

1. ADJ

/ səˈspɛktɪd / (AmE), / səˈspɛktɪd / (BrE)
regarded as likely to have committed a crime, done something wrong, or be present, but not yet proven
believed likely but not proven

Examples

The police arrested a suspected burglar after a nearby theft was reported.
Doctors isolated patients with a suspected viral infection to prevent an outbreak.
The authorities evacuated the building due to a suspected gas leak.

Synset Examples

a suspected thief
a suspected infection

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alleged - said without proof to have taken place or to have a specified illegal or undesirable quality
  • Antonym: confirmed - proven to be true or certain
