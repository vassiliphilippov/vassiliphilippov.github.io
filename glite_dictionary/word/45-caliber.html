.45-caliber

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɔrtiˈfaɪv ˈkælɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtiˈfaɪv ˈkælɪbə / (BrE)
relating to the bore of a firearm or its ammunition measuring forty-five hundredths of an inch in diameter
of a gun or bullet, .45 inch in diameter

Examples

He owns a .45-caliber pistol that he keeps locked away for safety.
The police recovered several .45-caliber bullets at the scene of the crime.
The hunter chose a rifle with a .45-caliber barrel for extra stopping power.
