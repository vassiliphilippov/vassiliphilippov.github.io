chickenshit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪkənˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt / (BrE)
A contemptible or petty statement, action, or person, often showing timidity, fear, or a lack of courage; can also refer to something considered insignificant or trivial.
petty, cowardly statement or person
informal vulgar american

Examples

He dismissed their concerns as pure chickenshit and told them to stand up for themselves.
She called out his excuse as chickenshit and refused to let him off the hook.
I can't stand working with chickenshit people who never take responsibility.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coward - person who lacks courage
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage

Etymology

Formed in American slang from 'chicken' (coward) + 'shit' (worthless thing/person), mid-20th century.
