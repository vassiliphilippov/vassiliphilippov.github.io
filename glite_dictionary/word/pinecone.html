pinecone

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪnˌkoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnkəʊn / (BrE)
the woody cone that produces and contains the seeds of a pine tree
seed-producing cone of a pine tree

Examples

A squirrel was nibbling on a pinecone in the shade of the large tree.
Children gathered pinecones to use in their holiday crafts.
The ground was scattered with dry pinecones after the windstorm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cone - a cone-shaped structure, often containing seeds, on certain plants

Etymology

Formed from 'pine' (tree) + 'cone' (shape of the seed vessel).
