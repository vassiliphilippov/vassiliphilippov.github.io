alongside

1. ADV

/ əˌlɔŋˈsaɪd / (AmE), / əˌlɒŋˈsaɪd / (BrE)
next to or close beside something or someone; side by side
next to; side by side

Examples

The two boats were moored alongside each other at the dock.
Workers stood alongside the road repairing the pavement.
She walked alongside her friend through the park.

Synset Examples

anchored close aboard another ship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beside - at the side of; next to
  • Antonym: away from - at a distance from; not near

Etymology

From 'along' + 'side', first used in the late 17th century.
