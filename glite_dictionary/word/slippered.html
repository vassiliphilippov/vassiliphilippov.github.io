slippered

1. ADJ

/ ˈslɪpɚd / (AmE), / ˈslɪpəd / (BrE)
wearing slippers or covered with slippers.
wearing slippers

Examples

She appeared at the door in a slippered robe, looking sleepy but content.
The slippered children padded quietly across the wooden floor.
He shuffled in his slippered feet to the window to watch the rain.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: barefoot - without any covering on the feet

Etymology

From 'slipper' + '-ed', the suffix forming adjectives meaning 'provided with' or 'covered by'.
