41st

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
the ordinal number for forty-one; coming after fortieth in order
ordinal number for forty-one

Examples

The 41st anniversary of the festival will be celebrated next year.
