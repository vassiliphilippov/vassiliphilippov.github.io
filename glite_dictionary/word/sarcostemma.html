Sarcostemma

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑr.kəˈstɛmə / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.kəˈstɛmə / (BrE)
A genus of succulent subshrubs or climbing vines native to tropical and subtropical regions of India, Africa, and Malaysia.
genus of tropical succulent plants

Examples

The Sarcostemma plant can thrive in arid environments due to its succulent stems.
Researchers discovered a new species within the Sarcostemma genus in southern India.
Many traditional medicines in Africa use extracts from Sarcostemma vines.

Etymology

From Greek 'sarx' (flesh) and 'stemma' (garland or wreath), referring to the fleshy stems and growth form.
