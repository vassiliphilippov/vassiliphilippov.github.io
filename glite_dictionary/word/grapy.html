grapy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪpi / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpi / (BrE)
having a taste or aroma similar to that of grapes, especially used to describe wine.
tasting or smelling like grapes

Examples

This grapy wine pairs perfectly with cheese and crackers.
The juice had a pleasantly grapy flavor that reminded me of fresh fruit.
She described the aroma as both sweet and grapy after swirling the glass.

Synset Examples

a grapey wine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fruity - tasting or smelling strongly of fruit
