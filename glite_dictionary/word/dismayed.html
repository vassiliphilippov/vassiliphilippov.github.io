dismayed

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈmeɪd / (AmE), / dɪsˈmeɪd / (BrE)
feeling shock, worry, or disappointment, often because something unexpected or unpleasant has happened
feeling shock, worry, or disappointment

Examples

She looked dismayed when she heard the unexpected news.
The teacher was dismayed by the students' lack of interest.
He felt dismayed at the prospect of starting over again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distressed - feeling extreme worry or pain
  • Antonym: encouraged - given hope or confidence
