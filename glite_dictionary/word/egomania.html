egomania

1. NOUN

/ ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an obsessive preoccupation with oneself and excessive concern for one's own needs or interests
obsessive self-centeredness

Examples

His egomania made it impossible for him to listen to anyone else's ideas.
The politician was criticized for his apparent egomania during the campaign.
Years of fame and attention had fueled her egomania beyond reason.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcissism - excessive self-love or self-admiration

Etymology

From ego (Latin, 'I') + mania (Greek, 'madness').
