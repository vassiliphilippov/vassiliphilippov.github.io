Genus Geum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːəm / (BrE)
A genus of perennial herbaceous plants in the rose family (Rosaceae), commonly known as avens, with small, often brightly colored flowers.
genus of plants known as avens

Examples

The Genus Geum includes several species with striking yellow or red flowers.
Gardeners often plant members of the Genus Geum for their ease of care and vibrant color.
Botanists recently discovered a new hybrid within the Genus Geum in the wild.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avens - Common name for plants of the genus Geum

Etymology

From Latin 'Geum', the classical name for the avens plant.
