walkingstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkɪŋˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋˌstɪk / (BrE)
a tropical insect with an elongated, twiglike body that resembles a stick, enabling it to camouflage among branches
long, twiglike insect

Examples

The walkingstick is a master of disguise, blending seamlessly with twigs and branches.
Biologists studied the feeding habits of the rare walkingstick in the rainforest.
Children marveled at the walkingstick crawling slowly along the branch at the insect exhibit.
