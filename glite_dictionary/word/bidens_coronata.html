Bidens coronata

1. NOUN

/ baɪˌdɛnz kəˈroʊnətə / (AmE), / bɪˌdɛnz kəˈrəʊnətə / (BrE)
A North American species of bur marigold known for its large yellow flowers.
North American bur marigold species

Examples

The bright yellow flowers of Bidens coronata bloom in late summer across wetlands and meadows.
Gardeners value Bidens coronata for its ability to attract pollinators.
Researchers studied the distribution of Bidens coronata along the riverbanks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bur marigold - a common name for several species in the genus Bidens, with yellow flowers

Etymology

From Latin; 'Bidens' meaning 'two-toothed', referring to the shape of the seeds, and 'coronata' meaning 'crowned'.
