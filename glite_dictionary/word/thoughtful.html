thoughtful

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.f(ə)l / (BrE)
serious and showing careful consideration or sincerity in manner or attitude
serious and sincere in manner

Examples

She spoke in a thoughtful tone, carefully considering each word before she answered.
His thoughtful expression showed he was deeply engaged in the discussion.
During the meeting, her thoughtful manner impressed everyone with her sincerity.

Synset Examples

a sensible young man
The serious-minded doctor carefully considered what to do next.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serious - showing deep thought or sincerity

Etymology

From 'thought' + '-ful', meaning full of thought.

/ ˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəl / (BrE)

Examples

A thoughtful driver always checks the road carefully before proceeding.
She remained thoughtful of her surroundings during the hike.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: careless - not paying attention or not careful

3. ADJ

/ ˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəl / (BrE)
Showing consideration for the needs, feelings, or well-being of others.
considerate of others

Examples

She gave me a thoughtful gift for my birthday.
His thoughtful gesture meant a lot to everyone involved.
It's thoughtful of you to call and check in on your neighbor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: considerate - careful not to cause inconvenience or hurt to others
  • Antonym: inconsiderate - not mindful of others' feelings or needs; thoughtless

Etymology

From English 'thought' + '-ful', meaning 'full of thought.'
