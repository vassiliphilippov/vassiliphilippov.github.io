overturned

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈtɜrnd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtɜːnd / (BrE)
having been turned upside down or so that the top and bottom are reversed
turned upside down

Examples

The truck was lying overturned beside the road after the accident.
The fisherman rescued the crew from their overturned boat in the storm.
She picked up the overturned chair and put it back in its place.

Synset Examples

an overturned car
the upset pitcher of milk
sat on an upturned bucket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: upturned - turned so that the top is facing upward or is higher than expected
  • Antonym: upright - in a vertical position; not overturned
