Danube

1. PROPN

/ ˈdæn.jub / (AmE), / ˈdæn.juːb / (BrE)
the second longest river in Europe, flowing from southwestern Germany to the Black Sea
major European river

Examples

The city of Budapest is divided by the Danube River.
A cruise along the Danube offers stunning views of several European capitals.
The Danube empties into the Black Sea after passing through ten countries.

Synset Examples

Vienna, Budapest, and Belgrade are on the banks of the Danube

Etymology

From Latin 'Danubius', likely from a Celtic origin.
