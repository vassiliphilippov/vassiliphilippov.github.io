dobra

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊbrə / (AmE), / ˈdəʊbrə / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in São Tomé and Príncipe
currency unit of São Tomé and Príncipe

Examples

The price of lunch was 50 dobra at the local café.
Travelers must exchange their euros for dobras when visiting São Tomé and Príncipe.
He collected coins and was pleased to find a rare dobra in his collection.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'dobra', meaning 'doubling', originally referring to a gold coin.
