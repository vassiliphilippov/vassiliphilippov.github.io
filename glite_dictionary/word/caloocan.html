Caloocan

1. PROPN

/ kəˈluːəkən / (AmE), / kəˈluːəkən / (BrE)
A city and suburb of Manila in southwestern Luzon, Philippines.
city near Manila, Philippines

Examples

Many commuters travel from Caloocan to central Manila each day for work.
Caloocan is known for its vibrant local markets and historic sites.
The mayor of Caloocan announced new development projects for the city.
