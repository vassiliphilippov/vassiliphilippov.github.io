tip

1. NOUN

/ tɪp / (AmE), / tɪp / (BrE)

Examples

9. NOUN

/ tɪp / (AmE), / tɪp / (BrE)
a small amount of money given to someone for a service they have performed, in addition to the basic payment

Examples

It is customary to give a tip to the housekeeping staff at a hotel.
She was delighted to receive a large tip from the satisfied customers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gratuity - a sum of money given for services, especially in hospitality; synonym for 'tip'
  • Antonym: fee - a fixed charge for a service, as opposed to a voluntary tip; antonym in context of compulsory payment

Etymology

Possibly from thieves’ slang for 'give' or 'hand over', late 18th century.
