East African

1. ADJ

/ ist ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (AmE), / iːst ˈæf.rɪ.kən / (BrE)
relating to East Africa or its people, languages, or culture.
of or relating to East Africa

Examples

The East African landscape is renowned for its breathtaking savannas and wildlife.
He specializes in East African history and culture at the university.
Many East African countries have experienced rapid economic growth in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: East Africa - the eastern region of the African continent
