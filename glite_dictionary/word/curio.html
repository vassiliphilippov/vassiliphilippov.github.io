curio

1. NOUN

/ ˈkjʊr.i.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈkjʊə.ri.əʊ / (BrE)
an unusual or rare object, often collected for its interest or curiosity value
unusual collectible object

Examples

The antique shop was filled with every kind of curio imaginable.
She displayed her favorite curio from her travels on a special shelf.
Collectors are always searching for a rare curio to add to their collections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collectible - object considered worth collecting

Etymology

Shortened from 'curiosity', which derives from Latin curiositas (desire of knowledge).
