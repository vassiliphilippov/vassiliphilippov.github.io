untwisted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtwɪstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtwɪstɪd / (BrE)
not twisted; straight or uncoiled after being twisted before
not twisted; straightened

Examples

After hours of careful work, the jeweler finally had the chain untwisted and shining again.
When the garden hose was untwisted, water flowed freely to the plants.
She gently untwisted the strands of rope that had become knotted over time.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: twisted - contorted or wound together in a spiral or coil
