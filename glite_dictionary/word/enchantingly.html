enchantingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtʃɑːntɪŋli / (BrE)
In a captivating or charming manner; in a way that attracts and delights greatly.
in a captivating or charming manner

Examples

The garden was enchantingly lit with twinkling fairy lights.
She played the song enchantingly, holding the audience spellbound.
The movie was enchantingly beautiful, with breathtaking scenery and music.

Synset Examples

she was bewitchingly beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: captivatingly - in a manner that attracts and holds interest
  • Antonym: dully - in a manner that is not interesting or lively

Etymology

From 'enchanting' + '-ly', with 'enchanting' from Old French *enchanter* (to cast a spell), from Latin *incantare* (to chant a spell).
