pinnule

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪn.juːl / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.juːl / (BrE)
a secondary or ultimate division of a pinnately compound leaf
division of a pinnately compound leaf

Examples

Each pinnule is finely divided, giving the fern a delicate appearance.
The pinnule structure distinguishes this species from other compound-leaved plants.
Botanists examined the arrangement of pinnules on the leaf axis.

Etymology

From Latin 'pinnula', diminutive of 'pinna' (feather, leaf).
