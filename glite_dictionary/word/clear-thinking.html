clear-thinking

1. ADJ

/ ˈklɪr θɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɪə ˌθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to think in a logical and rational way, without confusion or distraction.
able to think clearly and rationally

Examples

The company needs a clear-thinking manager to handle the crisis.
Despite the chaos, her clear-thinking approach helped calm everyone down.
He is known for being clear-thinking even under intense pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rational - able to think clearly and logically
  • Antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable
