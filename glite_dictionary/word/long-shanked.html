long-shanked

1. ADJ

/ lɔŋ ˈʃæŋkt / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈʃæŋkt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having long legs.
having long legs

Examples

The heron is a long-shanked bird often seen wading in shallow water.
The long-shanked youth easily outpaced the others on the track.
She admired the long-shanked elegance of the thoroughbred horse.

Etymology

Formed from 'long' + 'shank' (leg), referring to having long lower limbs.
