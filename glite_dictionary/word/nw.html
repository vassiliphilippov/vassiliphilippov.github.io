NW

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ˈdʌbəlju / (AmE), / ɛn ˈdʌbəlju / (BrE)
the compass point halfway between north and west, corresponding to an azimuth of 315 degrees
compass point between north and west

Examples

The city lies to the NW of the capital.
Winds are expected from the NW this evening.
The mountain range stretches from the NW to the southeast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northwest - the compass point between north and west (315° azimuth)

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'northwest'.
