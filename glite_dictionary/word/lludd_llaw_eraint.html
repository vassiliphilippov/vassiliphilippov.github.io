Lludd Llaw Eraint

1. PROPN

/ luːð lau ɛˈraɪnt / (AmE), / lɪð lau ɛˈraɪnt / (BrE)
A Celtic warrior god associated with Welsh mythology, known as Lludd of the Silver Hand.
Celtic warrior god in Welsh mythology

Examples

In ancient tales, Lludd Llaw Eraint was revered as a protector of Britain.
The myths recount the battles fought by Lludd Llaw Eraint against invading foes.
A festival in honor of Lludd Llaw Eraint was held during ancient times in Wales.

Etymology

Welsh mythology; name means 'Lludd of the Silver Hand' in Welsh.
