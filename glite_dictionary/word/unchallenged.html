unchallenged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
Not disputed or questioned; generally accepted as true or correct.
not disputed; accepted as true

Examples

His authority in the company remained unchallenged for over a decade.
It is an unchallenged fact that water boils at 100 degrees Celsius at sea level.
The results of the election were left unchallenged despite some controversy.

Synset Examples

the undisputed fact

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undisputed - not questioned or doubted; accepted by everyone
  • Antonym: contested - disputed or argued about

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'challenged' (from Old French 'chalengier', to accuse or dispute).
