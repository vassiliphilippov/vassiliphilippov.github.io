green-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrin ˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˌaɪd / (BrE)
Having or showing jealousy, especially in relation to rivalry or the fear of being displaced.
jealous; envious (esp. of rivals)

Examples

She grew green-eyed when her best friend got the promotion she wanted.
The green-eyed rival watched closely from across the room, unable to hide his suspicion.
Literature often describes love as green-eyed when jealousy intrudes.

Synset Examples

a jealous lover

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jealous - feeling or showing envy of someone or their achievements and advantages
  • Synonym: envious - feeling or showing envy

Etymology

Alluding to the idea of envy or jealousy as 'green,' popularized by Shakespeare's use of 'green-eyed monster' in Othello.
