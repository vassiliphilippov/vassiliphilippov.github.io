bastich

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæs.tɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbæst.ɪtʃ / (BrE)
(slang) An insult meaning a contemptible or annoying person, often used playfully or less harshly than 'bastard.'
playful or mild insult; contemptible person
slang

Examples

He's such a bastich when he wins at board games.
Don't be a bastich—let someone else have a turn.
The group laughed when she called him a bastich for teasing her.

Synset Examples

He called him a bastich for his rude comments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an unpleasant or despicable person; traditional pejorative

Etymology

Blend of 'bastard' and the suffix '-ich', popularized in comic books and subcultures.
