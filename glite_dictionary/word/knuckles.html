knuckles

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌkəlz / (AmE), / ˈnʌkl̩z / (BrE)
A small metal weapon designed to be worn over the knuckles on the back of the hand, used to enhance the force of a punch.
metal weapon worn over knuckles

Examples

The police found a pair of knuckles hidden in his jacket pocket.
Carrying knuckles is illegal in many countries due to their dangerous nature.
He showed off his old knuckles from his days as a street fighter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass knuckles - a weapon consisting of a band of metal worn over the fingers
