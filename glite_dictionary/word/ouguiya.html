ouguiya

1. NOUN

/ uːˈɡiːjə / (AmE), / uːˈɡiːjə / (BrE)
the basic monetary unit of Mauritania, equal to 5 khoums.
basic monetary unit of Mauritania

Examples

The shopkeeper only accepted payment in ouguiya when I visited Mauritania.
The bank offered to exchange U.S. dollars for ouguiya at the airport.
She counted out the exact amount in ouguiya to pay for her groceries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: khoum - a subunit of the Mauritanian ouguiya (1 ouguiya = 5 khoums)

Etymology

From Hassaniya Arabic أوقية (awkīya), a measure of weight.
