boronic

1. ADJ

/ bɔːˈrɑːnɪk / (AmE), / bɒˈrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from boron, especially in reference to compounds containing the element boron.
relating to boron or its compounds

Examples

The boronic compounds played a crucial role in the chemical reaction.
Researchers are studying the properties of boronic acids in pharmaceutical applications.
A boronic ester was synthesized to test its reactivity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boron - a chemical element with the symbol B

Etymology

From 'boron' (the element) + '-ic' (forming adjectives).
