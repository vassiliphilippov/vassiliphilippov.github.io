Parathelypteris

1. NOUN

/ ˌpær.ə.θɪˈlɪp.tə.rɪs / (AmE), / ˌpar.ə.θɪˈlɪp.tə.rɪs / (BrE)
A genus of terrestrial ferns native to warm and tropical regions of Asia and North America.
genus of terrestrial ferns

Examples

The shady woodland was filled with Parathelypteris growing along the moist stream banks.
Several species in the Parathelypteris genus are valued for their resilience in tropical gardens.
Botanists discovered a rare Parathelypteris during their expedition in Southeast Asia.

Etymology

From Greek 'para' (beside) + 'Thelypteris' (another fern genus name).
