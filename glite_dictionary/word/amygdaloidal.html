amygdaloidal

1. ADJ

/ əˌmɪɡ.dəˈlɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / əˌmɪɡ.dəˈlɔɪ.dəl / (BrE)
having the shape of an almond; resembling an almond in form.
shaped like an almond

Examples

Under the microscope, the mineral crystals appeared amygdaloidal in shape.
Some volcanic rocks contain amygdaloidal cavities filled with mineral deposits.
The artist chose to represent the eyes in an amygdaloidal style for added emphasis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: almond-shaped - having the form or shape of an almond

Etymology

From Latin amygdala (almond) + -oidal (resembling), based on Greek amygdaloeidēs.
