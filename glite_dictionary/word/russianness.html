Russianness

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌʃiənnəs / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃɪənnəs / (BrE)
The quality, characteristics, or nature distinctive of Russian people or culture.
the quality of being Russian

Examples

His deep sense of Russianness influenced the themes of his novels.
The exhibit explores the unique Russianness present in 19th-century art.
She feels torn between her Canadian identity and her Russianness.

Synset Examples

They were proud of their Russianness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Britishness - the quality or state of being British
  • Synonym: Frenchness - the quality or state of being French

Etymology

Formed from 'Russian' + '-ness', denoting state or quality.
