flutist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfluː.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈfluː.tɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the flute
person who plays the flute

Examples

The flutist played a beautiful solo during the concert last night.
As a talented flutist, she practices for several hours every day.
The orchestra's principal flutist received a standing ovation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flautist - a person who plays the flute (chiefly British term)

Etymology

From flute + -ist, patterned on other musical professions.
