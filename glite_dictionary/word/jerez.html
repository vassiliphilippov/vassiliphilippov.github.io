Jerez

1. PROPN

/ xəˈɾɛθ, həˈɾɛθ / (AmE), / xəˈreθ, həˈreθ / (BrE)
a city in southwestern Spain, renowned for its production of sherry wine
city in Spain famous for sherry

Examples

We visited Jerez to tour the historic sherry bodegas last summer.
The region around Jerez is also known for its beautiful Andalusian architecture.
Many of the world's finest sherries come from Jerez in Spain.

Etymology

From Spanish Jerez, short for Jerez de la Frontera.
