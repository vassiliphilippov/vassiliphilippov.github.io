guardedly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɑrdɪdli / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdɪdli / (BrE)
In a cautious or careful manner, so as to avoid risk or revelation.
in a cautious or careful way

Examples

She spoke guardedly about the details of her private life.
The company reacted guardedly to the news of the merger, waiting for more information before commenting.
He responded guardedly to the interviewer’s pressing questions.

Synset Examples

we estimated the number of demonstrators conservatively at 200,000.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cautiously - in a careful or wary manner
  • Antonym: recklessly - in a careless or rash manner
