fifty-two

1. NUM

/ ˌfɪftiˈtu / (AmE), / ˌfɪftiˈtjuː / (BrE)
the number 52; being two more than fifty
the number 52

Examples

She received fifty-two votes in the election.
The book contains fifty-two short stories, one for each week of the year.
