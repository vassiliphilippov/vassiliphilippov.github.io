subsidiser

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌb.sɪˌdaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.sɪˌdaɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person or organization that assists or supports something by providing a subsidy
one who provides a subsidy

Examples

The government became the main subsidiser of the national health program after budget cuts.
As a major subsidiser, the company supports numerous community art initiatives.
A hidden subsidiser can influence the market without the public even realizing it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to subsidize - to support financially by giving a subsidy
  • Synonym: benefactor - a person who gives financial or other help
  • Antonym: recipient - a person or organization that receives a subsidy

Etymology

From the verb 'subsidise', itself from Latin 'subsidium' (assistance, support).
