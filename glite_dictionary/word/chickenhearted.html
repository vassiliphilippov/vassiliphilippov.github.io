chickenhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪk.ɪnˌhɑːrt.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪnˌhɑː.tɪd / (BrE)
Easily frightened; lacking courage or bravery.
easily frightened; timid

Examples

My chickenhearted brother refused to watch the horror movie with us.
Don't be so chickenhearted; it's just a little spider.
The chickenhearted child clung to his mother during the thunderstorm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
  • Antonym: brave - ready to face and endure danger or pain; showing courage

Etymology

From "chicken" (metaphor for cowardice) + "hearted" (having a specified kind of heart).
