nonspherical

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈsfɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsfɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
Not having the shape of a sphere; not spherical.
not shaped like a sphere

Examples

The scientist explained that the asteroid was nonspherical due to its uneven mass distribution.
Many bacteria have nonspherical shapes, such as rods or spirals.
The object appeared nonspherical when viewed from different angles.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'non-' (not) with 'spherical', from Greek 'sphaira' (sphere).
