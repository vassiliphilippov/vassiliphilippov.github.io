circuitry

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːrkɪtri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkɪtri / (BrE)
the system or arrangement of electrical circuits, especially as a part of electronic equipment
system of electrical circuits

Examples

The complex circuitry inside modern smartphones allows for powerful computing in a small device.
After the lightning strike, the laboratory's circuitry was severely damaged and needed to be replaced.
She specialized in designing circuitry for medical diagnostic equipment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circuit - a closed path through which an electric current flows

Etymology

From 'circuit' + '-ry', denoting a collection or system.
