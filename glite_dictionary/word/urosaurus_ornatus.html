Urosaurus ornatus

1. NOUN

/ jʊˌroʊˈsɔrəs ɔrˈneɪtəs / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈsɔːrəs ɔːˈneɪtəs / (BrE)
A species of climbing lizard found in the western United States and northern Mexico.
species of climbing lizard

Examples

The Urosaurus ornatus is commonly found basking on tree branches in arid regions.
Biologists are studying the habitat preferences of Urosaurus ornatus in Arizona.
Because of its agility, the Urosaurus ornatus is often called the tree lizard.

Etymology

From Greek 'oura' (tail) and 'sauros' (lizard), Latin 'ornatus' (adorned), referring to its ornate appearance.
