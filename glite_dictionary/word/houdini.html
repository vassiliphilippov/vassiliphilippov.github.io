Houdini

1. PROPN

/ huˈdini / (AmE), / huːˈdiːni / (BrE)
(1874–1926) A famous American magician and escape artist, best known for his spectacular feats of escaping from handcuffs, chains, and locked containers.
famous magician and escape artist

Examples

Many people consider Houdini the greatest magician of all time.
The magician attempted a daring escape in the style of Houdini.
That escape was so impressive—it would have made Houdini proud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escape artist - a performer skilled in escaping from restraints or locked containers

Etymology

Stage name of Erik Weisz (later Ehrich Weiss), of Hungarian origin.
