Zambia

1. PROPN

/ ˈzæmbiə / (AmE), / ˈzæmbiə / (BrE)
A landlocked country in central Africa, formerly known as Northern Rhodesia, which gained independence from Britain in 1964.
country in central Africa

Examples

She spent a year volunteering in Zambia after finishing college.
Zambia is known for its stunning Victoria Falls and diverse wildlife.
Many travelers visit Zambia to experience its national parks and natural beauty.
