lentissimo

1. ADJ

/ lɛnˈtɪsɪmoʊ / (AmE), / lɛnˈtɪsɪməʊ / (BrE)
(in music) to be performed at a very slow tempo.
very slow tempo in music

Examples

The orchestra played the movement lentissimo, creating a deeply meditative atmosphere.
The conductor indicated that the next section should be taken lentissimo for dramatic effect.
Her interpretation of the nocturne was so lentissimo that every note seemed to linger in the air.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: largo - slow tempo in music

Etymology

From Italian 'lentissimo', superlative of 'lento' (slow).
