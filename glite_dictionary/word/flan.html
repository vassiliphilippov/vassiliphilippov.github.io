flan

1. NOUN

/ flæn / (AmE), / flæn / (BrE)
An open pastry or tart, typically filled with fruit or a sweet custard.
open pastry with fruit or custard filling

Examples

For dessert, she served a delicious flan filled with strawberries and cream.
The bakery down the street makes the best custard flan in town.
He requested a fruit flan instead of a cake for his birthday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tart - an open pastry case containing a sweet or savoury filling
  • Synonym: pie - baked dish with a pastry crust and filling

Etymology

From Old French flan (flat cake), from Medieval Latin fladō, fladōnem.
