chiliad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪliæd / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪad / (BrE)
A group or set of one thousand; the number 1,000.
a group or set of one thousand

Examples

The sculpture consists of exactly one chiliad of tiny metallic spheres.
Historians often refer to events that occurred over more than a chiliad.
A chiliad of years is known as a millennium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thousand - the number 1,000

Etymology

From Greek 'chiliás', from 'chílioi' (thousand).
