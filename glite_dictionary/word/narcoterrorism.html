narcoterrorism

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑːrkoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌnɑːkəʊˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)
The use of drug trafficking to fund or carry out terrorist activities.
terrorism funded by drug trafficking

Examples

The government launched a special task force to combat narcoterrorism along the border.
Experts warn that narcoterrorism poses a significant threat to national security.
Recent reports link narcoterrorism to several violent incidents in the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrorism - the use of violence and intimidation, especially for political purposes
  • Synonym: drug trafficking - the illegal trade of drugs

Etymology

Formed from 'narco-' (relating to narcotics or drugs) and 'terrorism'.
