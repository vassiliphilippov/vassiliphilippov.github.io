acoustical

1. ADJ

/ əˈkuːstɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of sound or acoustics
relating to sound or acoustics

Examples

The architect worked with engineers to improve the acoustical design of the concert hall.
They measured the acoustical properties of the new materials before installation.
Acoustical panels are often used to reduce noise in open-plan offices.

Synset Examples

acoustic properties of a hall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the sense of hearing

Etymology

From acoustics (the science of sound) + -al (adjectival suffix).
