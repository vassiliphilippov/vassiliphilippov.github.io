logo

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊɡəʊ / (BrE)
a design or symbol used by a company or organization as its official emblem
company or organization emblem

Examples

The company unveiled a new logo as part of its rebranding campaign.
You can easily recognize the brand by its distinctive logo.
We hired a designer to create an original logo for our startup.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emblem - a design or symbol representing an organization or concept

Etymology

From Greek 'logos' (word) via modern commercial usage.
