Pocatello

1. PROPN

/ ˌpoʊkəˈtɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˌpɒkəˈtɛləʊ / (BrE)
A university town in southeastern Idaho, United States.
city in Idaho, USA

Examples

I spent my college years living in Pocatello, where the winters were especially cold.
The train to Salt Lake City makes a stop in Pocatello every morning.
Idaho State University is located in Pocatello, attracting students from all over the region.

Etymology

Named after Chief Pocatello, a 19th-century leader of the Shoshone people.
