bristle-pointed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɪsəl ˌpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəl ˌpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
having a tip or end that is sharp or tapered like a bristle
having a bristle-like pointed tip

Examples

The caterpillar has several bristle-pointed hairs along its back.
She bought a paintbrush with a bristle-pointed tip for detailed work.
The arrow was bristle-pointed, making it suitable for piercing tough hides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapered end
