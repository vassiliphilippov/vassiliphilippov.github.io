trampling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræmplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræmplɪŋ / (BrE)
the sound of heavy treading or stomping
sound of heavy treading

Examples

The trampling of boots echoed down the hallway as the soldiers marched in.
She woke to the trampling of feet outside her window after the parade.
The quiet meadow was disturbed by the trampling of cows moving through the grass.

Synset Examples

he heard the trample of many feet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trample - to tread heavily or crush underfoot
