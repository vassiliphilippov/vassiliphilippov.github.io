Dilaudid

1. NOUN

/ daɪˈlɔdɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈlɔːdɪd / (BrE)
A trademarked medication containing hydromorphone, used to relieve moderate to severe pain.
brand of hydromorphone pain medication
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed Dilaudid for her severe back pain after surgery.
Patients receiving Dilaudid should be closely monitored for side effects such as drowsiness or respiratory depression.
Because of its potency, Dilaudid is only given in controlled medical settings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hydromorphone - an opioid pain medication, the active ingredient in Dilaudid
  • Degree Lower: morphine - a common opioid pain medication related to hydromorphone
