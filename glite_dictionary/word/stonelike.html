stonelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈstoʊnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling stone in appearance, texture, or hardness; very hard or stone-like
resembling stone; very hard

Examples

The fossil had a stonelike appearance due to years of mineral deposits.
Her expression was cold and stonelike, betraying no emotion.
The surgeon noted that the tissue had become unusually stonelike in texture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone - a hard, solid nonmetallic mineral matter
  • Synonym: rocklike - resembling rock in appearance or texture
