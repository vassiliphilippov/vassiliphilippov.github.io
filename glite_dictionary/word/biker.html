biker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪkə / (BrE)
a person who rides a bicycle, especially as a hobby or for sport
person who rides a bicycle

Examples

He considers himself an avid biker and goes on long rides every weekend.
A group of bikers gathered for a charity cycling event in the city.
Every summer, the biker packs his gear and explores new scenic routes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cyclist - person who rides a bicycle, typically in sport or as a hobby

Etymology

From 'bike' (short for 'bicycle') + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
