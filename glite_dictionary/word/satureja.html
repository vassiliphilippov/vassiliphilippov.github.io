Satureja

1. NOUN

/ sæ.tʃəˈriː.ə / (AmE), / sæ.tjʊˈriː.ə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic herbs, commonly known as savory, used especially in cooking for their pungent flavor.
genus of herbs called savory

Examples

The herb Satureja is prized for its aromatic leaves in Mediterranean cuisine.
Gardeners often plant Satureja to attract beneficial insects and to flavor dishes.
Cooks use both summer and winter Satureja to add a peppery note to their meals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savory - common name for the herbs of the genus Satureja

Etymology

From Latin Satureia, the classical name for savory plants.
