CSIS

1. NOUN

/ siː es aɪ es / (AmE), / siː es aɪ es / (BrE)
abbreviation for Canadian Security Intelligence Service, the main foreign intelligence agency of Canada responsible for gathering and analyzing security intelligence.
Canadian Security Intelligence Service (Canada's intelligence agency)

Examples

The CSIS released a statement regarding the recent cyberattack on government networks.
She applied for a position at the CSIS after finishing her degree in political science.
Canada's CSIS works closely with international intelligence agencies on security matters.

Etymology

Acronym formed from Canadian Security Intelligence Service.
