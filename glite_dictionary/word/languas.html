Languas

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæŋ.ɡwəs / (AmE), / ˈlaŋ.ɡwəs / (BrE)
a genus of perennial rhizomatous herbs native to Asia, Australia, and Polynesia, known for ginger-scented rhizomes
genus of rhizomatous herbs with ginger-scented rhizomes

Examples

Several species in the Languas genus are used in traditional medicine across Asia.
Botanists recently discovered a new member of the Languas genus in northern Australia.
The rhizomes of Languas plants impart a distinctive ginger aroma to many dishes.
