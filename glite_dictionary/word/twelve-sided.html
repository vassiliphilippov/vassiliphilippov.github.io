twelve-sided

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛlvˌsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈtwɛlvˌsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
having twelve sides, especially referring to a shape or object with twelve edges or surfaces.
having twelve sides
