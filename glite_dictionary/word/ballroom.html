ballroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌrum / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌruːm / (BrE)
a large room designed or used for formal dancing
large room used for dancing

Examples

The wedding reception was held in a beautifully decorated ballroom.
Hundreds of couples gathered in the grand ballroom for the annual gala dance.
The hotel offers an elegant ballroom available for private events and parties.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance hall - a public hall for dancing

Etymology

From 'ball' (a formal dance) + 'room'.
