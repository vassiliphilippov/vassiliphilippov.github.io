unassumingly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.əˈsu.mɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈsjuː.mɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a modest or humble manner; without drawing attention to oneself.
in a modest or humble way

Examples

She unassumingly accepted the award, giving credit to her team instead of herself.
The artist unassumingly chatted with fans, never mentioning his famous works.
He unassumingly offered his help, not expecting any praise or recognition.

Synset Examples

she is unassumingly skeptical of her own work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modestly - in a modest manner
  • Antonym: proudly - in a proud manner
