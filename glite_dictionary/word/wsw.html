WSW

1. NOUN

/ ˌdʌbəl.juː ɛs ˈdʌbəl.juː / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl.juː ɛs ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
the direction or compass point halfway between west and southwest, corresponding to a bearing of 247.5°
direction between west and southwest

Examples

The storm is expected to move in a WSW direction overnight.
He adjusted the sail, steering the boat toward WSW.
The wind was coming from the WSW, making the waves choppier than usual.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: west-southwest - the direction halfway between west and southwest
  • Degree Lower: west - the compass direction opposite to east
  • Degree Higher: southwest - the compass direction halfway between south and west

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'west-southwest'.
