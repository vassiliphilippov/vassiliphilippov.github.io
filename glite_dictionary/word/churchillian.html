Churchillian

1. ADJ

/ tʃɜːrˈtʃɪliən / (AmE), / tʃɜːˈtʃɪlɪən / (BrE)
Characteristic of, relating to, or suggestive of Winston Churchill, especially his style, leadership, or rhetoric.
of or resembling Winston Churchill

Examples

The prime minister delivered a Churchillian speech that inspired the nation.
Her Churchillian determination helped her overcome every obstacle.
The strategy had a distinctly Churchillian sense of resolve and optimism.

Etymology

Formation from the name Winston Churchill + -ian (denoting belonging or relation).
