dumbass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌmˌæs / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌas / (BrE)
A stupid or foolish person (slang and often offensive).
slang for a stupid person
slang offensive

Examples

Don't be such a dumbass and read the instructions first.
He called me a dumbass for forgetting my keys again.
Sometimes I feel like a total dumbass when I can't solve simple problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: moron - a person who is foolish or not very bright
  • Antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent person
