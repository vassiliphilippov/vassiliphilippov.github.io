bonsai

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔn.saɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.saɪ / (BrE)
a small, ornamental tree or shrub that has been artificially dwarfed and grown in a shallow container, as a traditional Japanese art form
miniature tree grown in a pot

Examples

She spent years carefully pruning her bonsai to achieve its delicate shape.
The exhibition featured dozens of beautiful bonsai from master gardeners.
He gave his father a maple bonsai for his birthday.

Etymology

From Japanese bonsai, from bon (tray) + sai (planting).
