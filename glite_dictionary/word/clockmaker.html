clockmaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɑːkˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈklɒkˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair clocks and watches
person who makes or repairs clocks and watches

Examples

The old clockmaker skillfully repaired the grandfather clock in my hallway.
She apprenticed with a renowned clockmaker to learn the intricate art of horology.
Antique shops sometimes consult a clockmaker to restore unique timepieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watchmaker - a person who makes or repairs watches

Etymology

From 'clock' + 'maker'.
