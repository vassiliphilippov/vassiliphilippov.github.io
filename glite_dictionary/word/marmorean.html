marmorean

1. ADJ

/ mɑːrˈmɔriən / (AmE), / mɑːˈmɔːrɪən / (BrE)
resembling, relating to, or characteristic of marble, especially in appearance or texture
resembling or relating to marble

Examples

The statue had a marmorean sheen that made it almost lifelike.
Her expression was cold and marmorean, betraying no emotion.
The architect selected tiles with a marmorean finish for the grand foyer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marble - a hard crystalline metamorphic form of limestone

Etymology

From Latin 'marmoreus' (of marble), from 'marmor' (marble).
