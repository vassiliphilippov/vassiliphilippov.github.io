Hylophylax

1. NOUN

/ haɪˈlɒfɪlæks / (AmE), / hɪˈlɒfɪlæks / (BrE)

genus of small antbirds

The species within the genus Hylophylax are known for their distinctive plumage and habitats in tropical forests.

Among the antbirds, Hylophylax stands out for its unique feeding behavior.

The Hylophylax genus includes several species of antbirds found in the Amazon rainforest.
