hangwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈhæŋˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to execute people by hanging
female executioner by hanging

Examples

The notorious hangwoman was feared throughout the region for her grim duties.
For the first time in history, a hangwoman was appointed to carry out the execution.
Literature rarely mentions a hangwoman, as the role was almost always held by men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hangman - a man whose job is to execute people by hanging

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'hangman', using 'woman' in place of 'man'.
