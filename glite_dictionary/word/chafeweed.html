chafeweed

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃeɪfˌwid / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfˌwiːd / (BrE)
a weedy perennial plant of northern temperate regions, with woolly leaves and dull white flowers arranged in a leafy spike
a perennial weedy plant with woolly foliage

Examples

The fields were overgrown with chafeweed by late summer.
Botanists identified the unusual plant as chafeweed during their survey.
She brushed aside the chafeweed to make a clear path.
