Aranea

1. NOUN

/ əˈreɪniə / (AmE), / əˈreɪniə / (BrE)
a genus of orb-weaving spiders that includes common garden spiders and barn spiders
genus of orb-weaving spiders

Examples

The genus Aranea includes several familiar species commonly found in gardens.
Scientists have studied the web-building behavior of Aranea for decades.
A barn spider belonging to the Aranea genus was spotted on the window sill.

Etymology

From Latin aranea (spider).
