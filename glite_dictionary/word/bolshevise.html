to bolshevise

1. VERB

/ ˈboʊlʃɪˌvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbɒlʃɪˌvaɪz / (BrE)
to make Communist or bring into accord with Communist principles
make Communist; bring into Communist accord

Examples

The regime attempted to bolshevise the trade unions after the revolution.
Some feared the new leaders would try to bolshevise every aspect of society.
Reforms began to bolshevise the educational system, aligning it with Communist doctrine.

Synset Examples

communize the government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to communize - to make (something) communistic in principles or organization

Etymology

From Russian Bolshevik (member of a majority faction), with the English verb-forming suffix -ise/-ize.
