Antananarivo

1. PROPN

/ ˌɑːn.təˌnɑː.nəˈriː.voʊ / (AmE), / ˌæn.təˌnæ.nəˈriː.vəʊ / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Madagascar, situated in the central part of the island.
capital city of Madagascar

Examples

She traveled to Antananarivo to learn more about Malagasy culture and history.
The population of Antananarivo has grown rapidly in recent decades.
Many international flights to Madagascar arrive in Antananarivo.

Etymology

From Malagasy 'Antananarivo', meaning 'City of the Thousand'.
