fallboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlbɔːd / (BrE)
the hinged cover on a piano that protects the keyboard when it is not being played
hinged cover for a piano's keyboard

Examples

Please make sure to close the fallboard when you finish practicing the piano.
The old piano's fallboard was engraved with the manufacturer's name.
A gentle push lowered the fallboard into place, hiding the piano keys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keyboard cover - a cover used to protect a keyboard, especially of a musical instrument
