venushair

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːnəsˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈviːnəsˌheə / (BrE)
A delicate species of maidenhair fern with slender, shiny black leaf stalks, found in many parts of the world.
delicate maidenhair fern species

Examples

The venushair thrives in moist, shaded areas near streams and waterfalls.
Botanists admire the delicate beauty of the venushair in its natural habitat.
In gardens, venushair is prized for its fine, feathery foliage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maidenhair fern - a type of fern with delicate, hair-like stalks

Etymology

From Latin 'Venus' (Roman goddess of love) and 'hair', referring to the plant's delicate appearance.
