unmalicious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.məˈlɪ.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈlɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
Not having or showing a desire to cause harm or pain to others; not malicious or spiteful.
not malicious or spiteful

Examples

Her comments were sharp, but completely unmalicious in intent.
He made an unmalicious joke at his friend's expense, which everyone understood was harmless.
The review was critical but unmalicious, focusing on constructive feedback.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: malicious - having or showing a desire to harm others
  • Degree Higher: benevolent - well meaning and kindly

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix un- (not) to 'malicious'.
