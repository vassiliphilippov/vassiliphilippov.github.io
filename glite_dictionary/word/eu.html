Eu

1. NOUN

/ ju / (AmE), / ju / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for europium, a bivalent and trivalent metallic element of the rare earth group
chemical symbol for europium (element)

Examples

The compound contained Eu and other rare earth metals in its structure.
Researchers measured the luminescence of Eu under various temperatures.
Adding a small amount of Eu can enhance the color properties of phosphors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: europium - a soft silvery-white rare earth element, symbol Eu

Etymology

From the element name 'europium', itself from Europe.
