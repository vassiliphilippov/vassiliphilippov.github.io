undersexed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈsɛkst / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈsɛkst / (BrE)
having less sexual desire or activity than is considered normal or typical
having low sexual desire or activity

Examples

He felt increasingly undersexed in comparison to his partner.
Some people worry that being undersexed affects their relationships.
The character is portrayed as shy, awkward, and undersexed throughout the film.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: oversexed - having an abnormally high sexual desire

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'under-' (meaning 'below, less than') and 'sexed'; pattern follows other 'under-' compounds.
