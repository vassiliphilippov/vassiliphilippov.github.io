Cabassous

1. NOUN

/ kəˈbæs.uːs / (AmE), / kəˈbæs.uːs / (BrE)
A genus of armadillos known as the naked-tailed armadillos, found in Central and South America.
a genus of naked-tailed armadillos

Examples

The Cabassous genus comprises several species of naked-tailed armadillos native to South America.
Researchers studied the burrowing habits of Cabassous in different environments.
Unlike other armadillos, members of Cabassous lack the prominent tail armor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armadillo - a burrowing mammal with a leathery armored shell
