vender

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛndər / (AmE), / ˈvɛndə / (BrE)
a person or company that sells goods or services
person who sells goods or services

Examples

The vender at the market sold fresh fruit and vegetables every morning.
We bought our souvenirs from a street vender near the museum.
Each vender had a unique selection of handmade crafts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vendor - person or company that sells goods or services

Etymology

Variant of 'vendor', from Latin venditor (seller), from vendere (to sell).
