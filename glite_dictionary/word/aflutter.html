aflutter

1. ADJ

/ əˈflʌtɚ / (AmE), / əˈflʌtə / (BrE)
feeling or showing excitement or nervous anticipation; in a state of tremulous excitement
excited in anticipation

Examples

The crowd was aflutter with excitement before the concert began.
Her heart was aflutter as she waited for him to arrive.
Social media was aflutter with rumors about the surprise announcement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excited - feeling very enthusiastic or eager
  • Antonym: calm - not excited or anxious; peaceful

Etymology

From the prefix 'a-' (on, in, into) + 'flutter'.
