bluish-gray

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ ˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with blue.
grey with a hint of blue

Examples

The sky was a bluish-gray just before dawn.
She chose a bluish-gray dress for the evening party.
A bluish-gray haze covered the distant mountains after the rainfall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gray - color between black and white
