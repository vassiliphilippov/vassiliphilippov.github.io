affectionateness

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɛkʃənətnəs / (AmE), / əˈfɛkʃənətnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being affectionate; the tendency to show love or fondness toward others
the quality of being affectionate

Examples

Her affectionateness made everyone in the family feel valued and loved.
The puppy's affectionateness was obvious as it eagerly snuggled up to its owner.
He was admired not only for his intelligence but also for the affectionateness he showed to his friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
  • Antonym: coldness - lack of affection or warmth

Etymology

From 'affectionate' (from Latin affectio, meaning 'disposition, affection') with the noun suffix '-ness'.

quality of showing affection

Examples

His affectionateness was apparent in the way he greeted his friends with warm hugs.
