pocket-size

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑkɪtˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtˌsaɪz / (BrE)
small enough to be carried in a pocket; easily portable
small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

She always carries a pocket-size notebook for jotting down ideas.
The dictionary comes in a convenient pocket-size edition.
I bought a pocket-size camera to take on my travels.

Synset Examples

pocket-size paperbacks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
  • Antonym: bulky - too large or heavy to be easily carried or stored

2. ADJ

/ ˈpɑːkɪtˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪtˌsaɪz / (BrE)
(of an object) small enough to fit in a pocket; relatively small in size or scope.
small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

He carried a pocket-size notebook everywhere he went.
The guidebook comes in a convenient pocket-size edition for travelers.
They released a pocket-size version of the popular game.

Synset Examples

a small business
a newspaper with a modest circulation
small-scale plans
a pocket-size country

Related Terms

  • Synonym: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
  • Synonym: miniature - very small; much smaller than usual

Etymology

From 'pocket' + 'size', meaning sized to fit a pocket.
