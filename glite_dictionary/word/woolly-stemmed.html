woolly-stemmed

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʊli stɛmd / (AmE), / ˈwʊli stɛmd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a stem covered with soft, wool-like hairs.
having a stem covered with woolly hairs

Examples

The woolly-stemmed sagebrush thrives in dry, arid climates.
Many gardeners admire the woolly-stemmed varieties for their unique texture.
This species is easily identified by its thick, woolly-stemmed appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairy-stemmed - having a stem covered in coarse or fine hairs
