diestock

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪstɑk / (AmE), / ˈdaɪstɒk / (BrE)
A tool that holds the dies used in cutting external threads on metal rods or pipes.
tool for holding threading dies

Examples

The machinist used a diestock to cut new threads onto the bolt.
Before threading the rod, make sure the diestock is adjusted properly.
Several types of diestocks are available for different threading tasks in the workshop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - a tool for cutting or shaping material, especially metal
