Mwanza

1. PROPN

/ mwɑːnˈzɑː / (AmE), / mwænˈzɑː / (BrE)
a city in northern Tanzania, situated on the southern shore of Lake Victoria
city in northern Tanzania, on Lake Victoria

Examples

I traveled to Mwanza to explore the shores of Lake Victoria.
The climate in Mwanza is pleasant throughout most of the year.
Many fishing communities depend on Mwanza for access to the lake.
