forget-me-not

1. NOUN

/ fərˈgɛt mi nɑt / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛt mi nɒt / (BrE)
a small perennial herb with bright blue or sometimes white flowers, belonging to the genus Myosotis
small plant with blue or white flowers

Examples

A patch of forget-me-nots bloomed beautifully in her garden every spring.
He gave her a small bouquet that included a few delicate forget-me-nots.
The blue petals of the forget-me-not are easy to recognize in the wild.

Etymology

From the phrase 'forget me not', used as the plant's name in English since the late Middle Ages.
