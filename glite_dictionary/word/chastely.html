chastely

1. ADV

/ ˈtʃeɪstli / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪstli / (BrE)
in a chaste and virtuous manner; without sexual activity or impropriety
in a chaste, virtuous way

Examples

She vowed to live chastely until marriage, despite social pressure.
The characters in the novel interact chastely, reflecting the values of their time.
He admired her ability to conduct herself chastely in every situation.

Synset Examples

she lived chastely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaste - not having engaged in sexual activity; pure or virtuous

Etymology

From 'chaste' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective.
