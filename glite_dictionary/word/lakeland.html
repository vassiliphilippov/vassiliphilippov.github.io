Lakeland

1. PROPN

/ ˈleɪklənd / (AmE), / ˈleɪklənd / (BrE)
a region in northwestern England known for its lakes, mountains, and scenic beauty, popular with tourists; sometimes refers specifically to the Lake District.
tourist region in northwestern England

Examples

Many hikers dream of exploring the trails in Lakeland during the summer holidays.
Lakeland attracts tourists with its picturesque lakes and rolling hills.
The weather in Lakeland can change rapidly, so visitors are advised to come prepared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Lake District - mountainous region in northwest England, also known as Lakeland
