hindmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪndˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndməʊst / (BrE)
furthest to the back or rear in position
furthest at the back; rearmost

Examples

The hindmost car in the convoy was responsible for bringing up the rear.
During the stampede, the hindmost animals struggled to keep up with the herd.
He grabbed the hindmost suitcase from the storage compartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rearmost - located at the very back
  • Antonym: foremost - located at the very front

Etymology

From Old English hindema (hindmost) + -most (most).
