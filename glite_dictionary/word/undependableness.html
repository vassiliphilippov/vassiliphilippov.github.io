undependableness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈpɛndə.bəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈpɛndə.bəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being not dependable or reliable.
lack of reliability or dependability

Examples

The undependableness of the old car made every trip a risky adventure.
Her constant changes of plan revealed a frustrating undependableness in her character.
Because of his undependableness, no one trusted him with important tasks.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dependability - the quality of being trustworthy and reliable
  • Synonym: unreliability - the quality of not being reliable

Etymology

From un- (not) + dependableness (quality of being dependable).
