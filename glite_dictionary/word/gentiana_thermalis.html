Gentiana thermalis

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛnˈʃaɪ.ə θɜrˈmeɪlɪs / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈʃɪə θɜːˈmɑːlɪs / (BrE)
A small blue-flowered gentian native to the western United States, especially around hot springs in Yellowstone National Park.
gentian plant of Yellowstone, blue-flowered

Examples

The rare Gentiana thermalis adds a splash of blue to the hot spring meadows each summer.
Botanists were thrilled to discover a thriving colony of Gentiana thermalis near the geysers.
Gentiana thermalis is highly valued by park naturalists for its striking fringed petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentian - any plant of the genus Gentiana

Etymology

Latin genus name 'Gentiana' and specific epithet 'thermalis' meaning 'of hot springs'.
