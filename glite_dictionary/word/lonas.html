Lonas

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, with one species, Lonas annua, known for its yellow flower heads.
genus of yellow-flowered plants

Examples

The Lonas in her garden bloomed bright yellow throughout the summer.
Botanists discussed the distinct characteristics that separate Lonas from similar genera.
An annual plant, Lonas thrives in well-drained, sunny locations.

Etymology

From scientific Latin Lonas, the genus name assigned in taxonomy.
