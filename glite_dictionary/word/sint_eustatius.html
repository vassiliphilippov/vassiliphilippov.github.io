Sint Eustatius

1. PROPN

/ sɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪnt juːˈsteɪʃəs / (BrE)
an island in the Caribbean that is an overseas municipality of the Netherlands, located in the northern part of the Lesser Antilles
island in the Caribbean, part of the Netherlands

Examples

The cruise ship made a brief stop at Sint Eustatius during its Caribbean tour.
Researchers visited Sint Eustatius to study its unique flora and fauna.
After moving to Sint Eustatius, she quickly adapted to the island's relaxed lifestyle.

Etymology

Named after Saint Eustace, a Christian martyr; Sint is Dutch for Saint.
