lawyerbush

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔɪərˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪəˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a stout-stemmed trailing shrub of New Zealand that scrambles over other plants
a scrambling New Zealand shrub

Examples

The dense stems of the lawyerbush make it difficult to walk through the forest.
In New Zealand, hikers often encounter lawyerbush clinging to trees along the trail.
The lawyerbush is known for its ability to scramble over other plants in search of light.
