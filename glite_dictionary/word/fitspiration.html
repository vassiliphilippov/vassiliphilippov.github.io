fitspiration

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪt.spəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɪt.spɪˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
Images, messages, or content intended to inspire people to exercise or live a healthier lifestyle.
exercise inspiration content
informal

Examples

She follows several fitspiration accounts on Instagram to motivate her morning workouts.
That blog is full of fitspiration if you need a push to get off the couch.
He made a collage of his favorite fitspiration quotes and hung it above his treadmill.

Etymology

Blend of 'fitness' and 'inspiration', modeled after 'thinspiration'.
