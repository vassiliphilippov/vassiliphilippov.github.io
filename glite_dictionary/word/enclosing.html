enclosing

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkloʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of enclosing something inside something else
the act of enclosing

Examples

The enclosing of valuable documents in a sealed envelope ensured their safety.
Proper enclosing of hazardous materials is required by law.
The enclosing of the field with a fence protected the crops from animals.
