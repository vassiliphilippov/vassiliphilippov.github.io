Lyon

1. PROPN

/ liˈoʊn / (AmE), / liˈɒ̃ / (BrE)
a large city in east-central France, located on the Rhône River, known historically for its silk industry and cultural heritage.
city in east-central France

Examples

Lyon is famous for its historical architecture and vibrant food scene.
During our vacation, we spent two days exploring the streets of Lyon.
Many international events are hosted in Lyon each year.

Etymology

From Latin Lugdūnum, an ancient Roman city name.
