mistrustfully

1. ADV

/ mɪsˈtrʌstfəli / (AmE), / mɪsˈtrʌstfʊli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of trust or suspicion.
with suspicion or lack of trust

Examples

She glanced mistrustfully at the unopened letter.
The children watched the stranger mistrustfully from behind the fence.
He answered mistrustfully, unsure of their true intentions.

Synset Examples

she looked at him distrustfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suspiciously - in a manner showing suspicion
