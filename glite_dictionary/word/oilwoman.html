oilwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔɪlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɔɪlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works in the extraction, production, or sale of petroleum
female worker in petroleum industry

Examples

She became the first oilwoman to lead a major drilling operation in Texas.
As an experienced oilwoman, she knows the industry inside and out.
The conference highlighted successful oilwomen from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oilman - a man who works in the extraction, production, or sale of petroleum

Etymology

Formed from 'oil' + 'woman', modeled after 'oilman'.
