Postum

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstəm / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstəm / (BrE)
A trademarked coffee substitute made from roasted grains and chicory, invented by C. W. Post.
trademarked coffee substitute with chicory and grains

Examples

Many people switched to Postum during the coffee shortages of World War II.
My grandmother always drank Postum instead of regular coffee for breakfast.
Some health food stores still carry Postum as a caffeine-free alternative.

Etymology

Named after its inventor, C. W. Post.
