rostrate

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɑː.streɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɒs.treɪt / (BrE)
Having a beak, snout, or beaklike projection.
having a beaklike snout or projection

Examples

The toucan is a rostrate bird known for its large, colorful beak.
Several fish have developed a rostrate snout to help them forage in the sand.
The orchid species has a rostrate feature which aids in pollination.

Etymology

From Latin 'rostratus', past participle of 'rostrare' (to provide with a beak), from 'rostrum' (beak, snout).
