speedometer

1. NOUN

/ spiˈdɑmədər / (AmE), / spiˈdɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument on a vehicle's dashboard for measuring and displaying its current speed.
instrument indicating vehicle speed

Examples

The car's speedometer showed they were traveling at 70 miles per hour.
He glanced down at the speedometer to make sure he wasn't speeding.
The mechanic replaced the broken speedometer in her old sedan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odometer - instrument for measuring distance traveled by a vehicle

Etymology

Formed from 'speed' + '-meter', after Greek 'metron' (measure).
