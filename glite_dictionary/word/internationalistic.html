internationalistic

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.təɹˌnæʃ.ə.nəˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ɪn.təˌnæʃ.ə.nəˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Advocating or influenced by internationalism; promoting cooperation or understanding between nations.
promoting internationalism

Examples

The organization's internationalistic approach helped foster peace between the neighboring countries.
Her views became increasingly internationalistic after studying abroad for several years.
Critics argued that the government was too internationalistic and neglected national interests.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: internationalist - a person who supports internationalism

Etymology

Formed from 'internationalism' + '-istic', following standard English word formation patterns.
