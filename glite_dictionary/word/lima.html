Lima

1. PROPN

/ ˈliːmə / (AmE), / ˈliːmə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Peru, located on the country's central western coast, and formerly the capital of the Spanish empire in the New World.
capital and largest city of Peru

Examples

She traveled to Lima to visit the historic city center and its museums.
The climate in Lima is mild and often overcast due to the nearby Pacific Ocean.
Many international conferences are hosted in Lima each year.

Etymology

Named by the Spanish after the indigenous people and river Rímac; spelling influenced by Spanish pronunciation.
