pip-squeak

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪpˌskwik / (AmE), / ˈpɪpˌskwiːk / (BrE)
an insignificant or unimportant person, especially one who is small or young
small or insignificant person
informal

Examples

He may act tough, but he's really just a pip-squeak in the company.
No one took the pip-squeak seriously when he complained in the meeting.
At school, she was a pip-squeak, always getting picked last for teams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nobody - a person of no importance

Etymology

Imitative; from 'pip' (a small seed) and 'squeak' (a short, high sound), suggesting something small and weak.
