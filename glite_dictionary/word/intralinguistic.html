intralinguistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring within a single language, especially in contrast to phenomena involving more than one language
within a particular language

Examples

The study examined intralinguistic variation among different regions of the country.
Researchers focused on intralinguistic changes that occur over time within the same speech community.
The project analyzed both intralinguistic and interlinguistic factors affecting language development.

Synset Examples

intralinguistic variation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interlinguistic - relating to or occurring between two or more languages

Etymology

Formed from Latin "intra-" (within) and "linguistic" (relating to language).
