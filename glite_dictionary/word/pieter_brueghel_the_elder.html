Pieter Brueghel the Elder

1. PROPN

/ ˈpiːtər ˈbrɔɪɡəl ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / ˈpiːtə ˈbrɔɪɡəl ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(c. 1525–1569) A renowned Flemish painter known for his detailed landscapes and scenes of peasant life.
Flemish painter (1525–1569)

Examples

Many art historians regard Pieter Brueghel the Elder as one of the greatest painters of the Northern Renaissance.
The landscapes of Pieter Brueghel the Elder often depict everyday peasant life in vivid detail.
A major exhibition featured works of Pieter Brueghel the Elder alongside his son's paintings.
