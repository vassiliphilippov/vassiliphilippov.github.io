obsessionally

1. ADV

/ əbˈsɛʃənəli / (AmE), / əbˈsɛʃənəli / (BrE)
In a compulsive or obsessive manner.
in a compulsive manner

Examples

He checked his work obsessionally, never satisfied with the results.
She cleaned the house obsessionally every weekend, unable to relax until everything was spotless.
The artist obsessionally adjusted every detail of the painting, striving for perfection.

Synset Examples

he cleaned his shoes compulsively after every walk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compulsively - in a way involving compulsion or an irresistible urge

Etymology

From 'obsessional' + '-ly'. 'Obsessional' from 'obsession', which is from Latin 'obsessio' (a siege, blockade).
