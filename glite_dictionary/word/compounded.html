compounded

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈpaʊndɪd / (AmE), / kəmˈpaʊndɪd / (BrE)
combined together, especially to form a chemical compound or mixture.
combined to form a compound

Examples

Many prescription drugs are compounded in specialized pharmacies to suit individual patient needs.
The scientist analyzed the compounded substance to determine its chemical composition.
These fertilizers are compounded with trace minerals to enhance plant growth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combined - joined together to form a single entity
  • Antonym: pure - not mixed with any other substance; unadulterated

Etymology

From the verb 'compound,' which comes from Latin 'componere' (to put together).
