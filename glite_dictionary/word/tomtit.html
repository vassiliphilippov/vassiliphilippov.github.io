tomtit

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑm.tɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒm.tɪt / (BrE)
a small European songbird of the tit family, with bright cobalt blue wings, tail, and head crown
small European songbird (tit family)

Examples

A flash of blue revealed a tomtit perched on the garden fence.
The tomtit builds its nest in tree hollows in the spring.
Many birdwatchers hope to spot a tomtit during their woodland walks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: titmouse - small songbird of the Paridae family

Etymology

Imitative, from the bird's call; influenced by 'tit' (small songbird).
