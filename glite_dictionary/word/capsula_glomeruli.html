capsula glomeruli

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæp.sjələ ɡləˈmɛr.jʊ.laɪ / (AmE), / ˌkap.sjʊ.lə ɡləˈmɛr.jʊ.li / (BrE)
the thin double-layered membrane that surrounds the glomerulus in the nephron of the kidney
membrane around nephron glomerulus
medical

Examples

The capsula glomeruli plays a crucial role in the kidney's filtration process.
Damage to the capsula glomeruli can impair kidney function significantly.
Pathologists examined the capsula glomeruli for signs of inflammation in the biopsy sample.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bowman's capsule - the double-layered capsule surrounding the glomerulus in a nephron
  • Degree Lower: glomerulus - a cluster of capillaries in the nephron of the kidney

Etymology

From Latin: 'capsula' (little box, capsule) and 'glomeruli' (plural of glomerulus, meaning 'small ball').
