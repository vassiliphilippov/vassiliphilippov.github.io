Victoriana

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɪktɔriˈænə / (AmE), / ˌvɪktɔːriˈɑːnə / (BrE)
objects, materials, or items typical of or relating to the Victorian era, especially as collected for their historical or decorative value.
items from or characteristic of the Victorian era

Examples

The museum's exhibit of Victoriana attracted visitors fascinated by 19th-century design.
Her living room was filled with Victoriana, from ornate lamps to delicate porcelain figurines.
Collectors travel far to find rare pieces of Victoriana at antique fairs.

Etymology

Blend of 'Victoria' (after Queen Victoria of Britain) and the common suffix '-ana', denoting a collection of items related to a person or period.
