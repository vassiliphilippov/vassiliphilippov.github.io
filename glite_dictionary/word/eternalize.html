to eternalize

1. VERB

/ ɪˈtɜrnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something last forever or ensure it is remembered always; to immortalize
make everlasting or immortal

Examples

The artist hoped his painting would eternalize the beauty of the landscape.
Their names were eternalized in the city's hall of fame.
The moving speech managed to eternalize her grandfather's legacy.

Synset Examples

This melody immortalized its composer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to immortalize - to make someone or something remembered forever

Etymology

From Latin aeternus (eternal) + -ize (verb-forming suffix).
