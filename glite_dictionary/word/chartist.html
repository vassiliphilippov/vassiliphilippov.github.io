chartist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who analyzes stock market trends and prices by studying charts and graphs of past performance to predict future movements
analyst who predicts markets using price charts

Examples

The chartist advised his clients to buy shares based on recent trends in the market graphs.
Many investors follow the recommendations of a chartist when looking for short-term trades.
She became a successful chartist by carefully analyzing securities' price patterns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: technical analyst - an analyst who studies past market data to predict future price movements
  • Antonym: fundamental analyst - a stock market analyst who focuses on company fundamentals rather than charts

Etymology

From 'chart' + '-ist', referring to the use of price charts in financial analysis.
