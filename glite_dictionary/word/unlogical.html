unlogical

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
lacking logic or not being in accordance with logical principles
not logical; lacking logic

Examples

Her explanation sounded strangely unlogical, leaving everyone confused.
It is unlogical to assume the results without evidence.
The theory, while creative, is ultimately unlogical and unsupported by facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illogical - not logical; lacking sense or clear, sound reasoning

Etymology

From un- (prefix meaning 'not') + logical; modeled after Latin influences in word formation.
