outfitted

1. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪd / (BrE)
provided with the necessary equipment, supplies, or clothing for a particular activity or purpose
supplied with necessary equipment

Examples

The hikers were outfitted with warm jackets and sturdy boots.
The laboratory is fully outfitted for advanced scientific research.
Each camper was outfitted with a tent, sleeping bag, and backpack.

Synset Examples

a well outfitted expedition to the South Pole

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipped - supplied with the necessary items for a purpose

Etymology

From 'outfit' (to equip), ultimately from English 'out' + 'fit'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌfɪtɪd / (BrE)
provided with the necessary clothing or equipment for a particular purpose or activity.
equipped with needed clothing or gear

Examples

The hikers were outfitted with rain jackets and sturdy boots for the mountain trek.
All team members must be outfitted appropriately for the competition.
The laboratory was outfitted with state-of-the-art safety equipment.

Synset Examples

children properly outfitted for school

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equipped - provided with the necessary items for a particular purpose

Etymology

From 'outfit' + '-ed'; 'outfit' from English out + fit.
