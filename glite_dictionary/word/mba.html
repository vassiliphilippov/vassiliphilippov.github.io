MBA

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛm.biːˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛm.biːˈeɪ / (BrE)
a master's degree in business administration, typically awarded after postgraduate study in business management
master's degree in business administration

Examples

She decided to pursue an MBA to enhance her career prospects.
Many companies seek candidates who have an MBA from a reputable university.
After completing his MBA, he was promoted to a managerial position.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: master's degree - an advanced college or university degree after a bachelor's degree

Etymology

Acronym from Master of Business Administration.
