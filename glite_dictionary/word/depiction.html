depiction

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈpɪkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of representing something in a picture, painting, or other visual image
representation in picture or portrait

Examples

The artist's depiction of the battle was both dramatic and detailed.
This novel offers a vivid depiction of life in nineteenth-century Paris.
The museum featured a stunning depiction of the city skyline at sunset.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: representation - the action of depicting or describing something, especially visually

Etymology

From Latin 'depictio' (a painting), from 'depictus', past participle of 'depinger' (to paint).
