shitweasel

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌwiːzəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtˌwiːzl̩ / (BrE)
A deceitful or untrustworthy person, especially one regarded with strong contempt (vulgar, slang).
deceitful or untrustworthy person (vulgar, slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

Nobody wanted to work with the shitweasel after he lied to the whole team.
He’s always plotting something behind your back, that little shitweasel.
I knew from the start she was a shitweasel who couldn’t be trusted.

Synset Examples

Don’t trust him; he’s a known shitweasel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat - a person regarded as treacherous or disloyal
  • Synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
  • Antonym: trustworthy - able to be relied on as honest or truthful
