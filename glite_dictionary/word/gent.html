Gent

1. PROPN

/ ʒɑnt / (AmE), / ʒɛnt / (BrE)
A port city in northwestern Belgium, known for its history and as an industrial center, especially famous for its cloth industry.
port city in Belgium

Examples

The medieval architecture in Gent attracts thousands of tourists each year.
He moved to Gent to work in the thriving textile industry.
We took a train from Brussels to Gent for a weekend getaway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ghent - Alternative English name for Gent, city in Belgium

Etymology

From Dutch 'Gent' or French 'Gand'; historically known as Ganda in Latin.
