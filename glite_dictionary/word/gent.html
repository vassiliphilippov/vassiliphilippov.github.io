gent

1. NOUN

/ dʒɛnt / (AmE), / dʒɛnt / (BrE)
an informal term for a man, especially one who behaves politely; abbreviation of 'gentleman'
informal term for a man; gentleman
informal

Examples

Only one other gent was waiting in the lobby when I arrived.
He’s a real gent—always holding doors open for others.
The bathroom marked ‘gents’ is down the corridor to the right.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentleman - a polite or honorable man; man of good breeding

Etymology

Shortened from 'gentleman', originating from Middle English 'gentilman'.
