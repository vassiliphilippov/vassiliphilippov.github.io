rotational

1. ADJ

/ roʊˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by rotation; involving the act of turning around an axis
relating to rotation

Examples

The Earth's rotational speed determines the length of a day.
Engineers calculated the rotational forces acting on the bridge.
The rotational motion of the fan kept the room cool.

Synset Examples

rotational inertia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotation - the act of turning around a central point or axis

Etymology

From 'rotation' + '-al', Latin root 'rotatio' (a turning around).
