Mitra Samaja

1. PROPN

/ ˈmiːtrə səˈmɑːdʒə / (AmE), / ˈmiːtrə səˈmɑːdʒə / (BrE)
A Hindu god associated with friendship and alliances, often invoked with Varuna as a supporter of heaven and earth.
Hindu god of friendship and alliances

Examples

The ancient Vedic hymns frequently mention Mitra Samaja as a deity of friendship and agreements.
Together, Mitra Samaja and Varuna were believed to uphold cosmic order.
Devotees often pray to Mitra Samaja when seeking harmonious relationships.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Varuna - Another Hindu deity, often paired with Mitra Samaja as a supporter of the universe

Etymology

From Sanskrit Mitra (friend) and Samaja (association or society).
