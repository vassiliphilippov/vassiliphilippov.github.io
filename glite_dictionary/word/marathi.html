Marathi

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɑːti / (AmE), / məˈrɑːti / (BrE)
an Indic language spoken primarily in the Indian state of Maharashtra and written in the Devanagari script
an Indic language of Maharashtra, India

Examples

Many people in Mumbai speak Marathi as their first language.
She is learning to read and write Marathi with the help of a tutor.
The festival was celebrated with songs and plays performed in Marathi.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hindi - an Indo-Aryan language spoken widely in northern India
  • Synonym: Gujarati - an Indic language spoken in the state of Gujarat, India

Etymology

From Marathi मराठी (Marāṭhī), the native name for the language.
