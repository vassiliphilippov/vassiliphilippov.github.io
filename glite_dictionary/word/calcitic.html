calcitic

1. ADJ

/ kælˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / kælˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, containing, or consisting of calcite.
containing or relating to calcite

Examples

The limestone in this region is predominantly calcitic in composition.
Geologists studied the calcitic structure of the fossilized shells.
Some marble types are prized for their pure calcitic content.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calcite - a common crystalline form of natural calcium carbonate

Etymology

From 'calcite', with the suffix '-ic' meaning 'of or relating to'.
