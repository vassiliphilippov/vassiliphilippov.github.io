pocket

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a small pouch sewn into or onto a garment, used for carrying small items
small pouch in a garment

Examples

She slipped her keys into her pocket before leaving the house.
His coat has a hidden pocket on the inside for valuables.
After washing his pants, he found money in the front pocket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pouch - a small bag or sack for carrying things

Etymology

From Old French poquette, diminutive of poque (bag), from Germanic origin.
