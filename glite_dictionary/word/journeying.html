journeying

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɝːniɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːniɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of traveling from one place to another, especially over a long distance
the act of traveling from one place to another

Examples

Her journeying across the country opened her mind to new cultures and perspectives.
Centuries ago, journeying by sea was fraught with danger and uncertainty.
After years of journeying, he finally settled down in a quiet village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveling - the act of moving from place to place; general term for going on journeys

Etymology

From the verb 'journey' + '-ing'; 'journey' from Old French 'jornee', meaning a day's travel.
