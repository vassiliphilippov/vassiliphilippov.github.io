Lusitanian

1. ADJ

/ luːˈsɪteniən / (AmE), / luːˈsɪteɪniən / (BrE)
Relating to Portugal, its people, or their language.
of Portugal or its people/language

Examples

The Lusitanian language influenced many words in Portuguese.
She studied the history of Lusitanian art and society.
A collection of Lusitanian folklore was published in English translation.

Synset Examples

Portuguese wines

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Portuguese - relating to Portugal, its people, or language

Etymology

From Latin 'Lusitania', the Roman name for the region roughly corresponding to modern Portugal.

2. ADJ

/ luːˌsɪˈteɪniən / (AmE), / luːˌsɪˈteɪniən / (BrE)
of or relating to Lusitania, its people, or their language
relating to Lusitania or its people

Examples

The Lusitanian language is believed to have been spoken in the western Iberian Peninsula.
He developed a keen interest in Lusitanian history and culture.
Some traditional Lusitanian customs are still practiced in parts of Portugal.

Etymology

From Latin 'Lusitania', the Roman name for a region roughly corresponding to modern Portugal.
