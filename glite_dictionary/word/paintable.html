paintable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntəbl̩ / (BrE)
suitable or able to be painted; easily accepting paint.
suitable to be painted

Examples

The table was made from paintable wood that was easy to customize.
Choose a paintable surface for best results when redecorating the room.
The artist searched for a paintable scene to capture on her canvas.

Synset Examples

a highly paintable landscape
made of sturdy eminently paintable wood

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpaintable - not able to be painted

Etymology

From 'paint' + '-able', a suffix meaning 'able to be'.
