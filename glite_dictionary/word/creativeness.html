creativeness

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the ability to create new ideas or things; inventiveness
ability to create; inventiveness

Examples

The artist's creativeness was apparent in every piece she made.
Many employers value creativeness highly when hiring new staff.
His creativeness helped the team come up with innovative solutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas to create something
  • Antonym: unimaginativeness - lack of creativity or inventiveness

Etymology

From 'create' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning the state or quality of being creative.
