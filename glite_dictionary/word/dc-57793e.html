D.C.

1. PROPN

/ di ˈsi / (AmE), / diː ˈsiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for the District of Columbia, the federal district containing Washington, the capital city of the United States.
abbr. for District of Columbia; U.S. capital area
american

Examples

The president lives and works in D.C..
Congress is headquartered in D.C., where important decisions are made.
Many tourists visit D.C. to see the national monuments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Washington, D.C. - the capital city of the United States, located in the District of Columbia

Etymology

Abbreviation of District of Columbia, named in honor of Christopher Columbus.
