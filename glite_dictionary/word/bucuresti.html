Bucuresti

1. PROPN

/ buːkuːˈrɛst / (AmE), / ˌbjuːkuːˈrɛʃt / (BrE)
the national capital and largest city of Romania, located in the southeast of the country
capital of Romania

Examples

She traveled to Bucuresti to attend an international conference on urban development.
Many artists from Bucuresti have contributed to Romania's vibrant cultural scene.
After moving to Bucuresti, he quickly adapted to the fast-paced life of the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bucharest - the English name for Bucuresti, capital of Romania

Etymology

Romanian form, ultimately from Turkish 'Bükreş' or via Slavic origins, known in English as 'Bucharest'.
