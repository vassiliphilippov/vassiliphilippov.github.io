forty

1. NUM

/ ˈfɔr.ti / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.ti / (BrE)
the number equal to four times ten; 40.
the number 40

Examples

She plans to retire at forty and travel the world.
There are forty students in the class this semester.
The building has stood for more than forty years.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirty - the number 30
  • Degree Higher: fifty - the number 50

Etymology

Old English 'fēowertig' (group of forty), from 'fēower' (four) + 'tig' (group of ten).
