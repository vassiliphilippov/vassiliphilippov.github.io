costless

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔst.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒst.ləs / (BrE)
Not costing anything; free of charge.
without cost; free

Examples

The museum offered costless entry to all visitors on Sundays.
She enjoys finding costless resources online for her students.
The service is not only convenient, but completely costless for users.

Synset Examples

complimentary tickets
free admission

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - provided without charging a price; costing nothing
  • Synonym: complimentary - given free of charge
  • Antonym: expensive - costing a lot of money

Etymology

From 'cost' + '-less', meaning 'without cost'.
