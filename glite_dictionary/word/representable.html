representable

1. ADJ

/ ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntəbl̩ / (BrE)
Capable of being expressed, depicted, or described in a particular symbolic or specified form.
capable of being expressed in a form

Examples

The mathematical function is representable by a simple equation.
All data must be representable in a digital format for processing.
Not every idea is easily representable through words or images.

Synset Examples

uniquely representable in the form …

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expressible - capable of being expressed
  • Antonym: inexpressible - not capable of being expressed

Etymology

From 'represent' + '-able', modelled on Latin roots.
