Slezsko

1. PROPN

/ ˈslɛz.skoʊ / (AmE), / ˈslɛz.skəʊ / (BrE)
the Czech name for Silesia, a historical region in central Europe, now mostly in Poland, known for its coal and iron deposits
Czech name for Silesia, central European region

Examples

The mining industry has played a key role in the history of Slezsko.
Many families in Slezsko have roots stretching back generations.
After World War II, most of Slezsko became part of Poland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Silesia - a region of central Europe, mostly in Poland, known for coal and iron; English name

Etymology

Czech name for the region; see Silesia for further etymology.
