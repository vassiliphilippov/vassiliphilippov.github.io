New Jersey

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈdʒɝːzi / (AmE), / njuː ˈdʒɜːzi / (BrE)
A state in the northeastern United States, located on the Atlantic coast; one of the original thirteen colonies.
a northeastern state of the USA

Examples

She moved to New Jersey for her new job at the university.
The beaches in New Jersey attract thousands of tourists every summer.
Many important battles of the Revolutionary War were fought in New Jersey.

Etymology

Named after the island of Jersey in the English Channel.
