ardently

1. ADV

/ ˈɑrdəntli / (AmE), / ˈɑːdntli / (BrE)
In a passionate or enthusiastic manner; with intense emotion or devotion.
with great passion or enthusiasm

Examples

She ardently supported the campaign for environmental reform.
He spoke ardently about his hopes for the future.
The students ardently debated the merits of each side.

Synset Examples

the spirit of God knew very well that there was a deeper question to be settled before there could be the intervention in power that was so ardently desired

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionately - in a passionate manner
  • Synonym: eagerly - with keen or enthusiastic desire

Etymology

From ardent + -ly; 'ardent' from Latin ardēns (burning).
