one-trillionth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn trɪl.jənθ / (AmE), / wʌn trɪl.jənθ / (BrE)
One part in a trillion equal parts.
one part of a trillion

Examples

A one-trillionth of a meter is called a picometer in scientific measurement.
The contamination level was less than one-trillionth of the acceptable limit.
Even a one-trillionth change in DNA sequence can have significant effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trillionth - one of a trillion equal parts
