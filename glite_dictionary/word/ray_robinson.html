Ray Robinson

1. PROPN

/ reɪ ˈrɑbɪnsən / (AmE), / reɪ ˈrɒbɪnsən / (BrE)
(1921–1989), American professional boxer who won the world middleweight championship five times and the world welterweight championship once.
American champion boxer (1921–1989)

Examples

Many consider Ray Robinson to be the greatest pound-for-pound boxer in history.
Ray Robinson won his first welterweight title in the 1940s.
The legendary career of Ray Robinson inspired generations of fighters.
