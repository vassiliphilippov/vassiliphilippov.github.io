Provera

1. NOUN

/ proʊˈvɛrə / (AmE), / prəˈvɪərə / (BrE)
A trademarked medication containing medroxyprogesterone acetate, used to treat menstrual disorders and other hormone-related conditions.
hormonal medication (brand name)
medical american

Examples

The doctor prescribed Provera to help regulate her menstrual cycle.
Some women take Provera to treat symptoms of endometriosis.
Before starting Provera, she discussed possible side effects with her physician.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medroxyprogesterone acetate - the generic name of the compound in Provera
