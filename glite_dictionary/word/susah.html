Susah

1. PROPN

/ suːˈsɑː / (AmE), / suːˈsɑː / (BrE)
A port city in eastern Tunisia on the Mediterranean Sea, known for its historical significance and ancient architecture.
port city in eastern Tunisia

Examples

The ancient ruins in Susah attract tourists from around the world.
She visited Susah during her tour of North Africa.
Trade has flourished in Susah since Roman times.
