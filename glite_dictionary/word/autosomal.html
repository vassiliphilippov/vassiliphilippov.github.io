autosomal

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔːtoʊˈsoʊməl / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈsəʊməl / (BrE)
relating to or denoting any chromosome that is not a sex chromosome; pertaining to an autosome.
relating to non-sex chromosomes
medical

Examples

Many inherited diseases are caused by autosomal mutations rather than defects in the sex chromosomes.
Cystic fibrosis is an autosomal recessive disorder.
The research focused on autosomal genes to map hereditary patterns.

Synset Examples

autosomal gene

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sex-linked - relating to genes or traits located on the sex chromosomes

Etymology

From 'autosome', from Greek 'autos' (self) + 'soma' (body) + '-al' (adjective suffix).
