Medellín

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɛdəˈjiːn / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɛˈjiːn / (BrE)
A large city in western Colombia, known as an important center for the coffee industry.
major city in Colombia, coffee center

Examples

I traveled to Medellín last summer to experience its vibrant culture and beautiful scenery.
Medellín has become known for its innovative public transport and urban development.
The coffee exported from Medellín is renowned for its high quality.

Etymology

From Spanish Medellín, named after a town in Spain.
