cufflink

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌf.lɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkʌf.lɪŋk / (BrE)
a small decorative fastener used instead of a button for securing the cuffs of a shirt, usually worn in pairs
decorative fastener for shirt cuffs

Examples

He wore silver cufflinks that matched his watch for the wedding.
I found a vintage cufflink at the antique market yesterday.
It's easy to lose a cufflink if you're not careful while changing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: button - small fastener sewn onto clothing

Etymology

From 'cuff' (the end of a sleeve) + 'link' (something that connects parts).
