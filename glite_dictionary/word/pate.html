pate

1. NOUN

/ peɪt / (AmE), / peɪt / (BrE)
the top or crown of the head, especially when referring to a bald head
top of the head, often bald

Examples

The old man was completely bald on top, his pate shining in the sunlight.
He wore a hat to protect his delicate pate from the sun.
She gently patted the baby's soft pate as she sang him to sleep.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scalp - the skin covering the top of the head

Etymology

From Middle English *pate* (head, skull), origin unknown.
