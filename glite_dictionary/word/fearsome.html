fearsome

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪr.səm / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.səm / (BrE)
causing fear, dread, or terror; frightening or alarming.
causing fear; frightening

Examples

The dragon was a truly fearsome creature, terrorizing the villagers for years.
She has a fearsome reputation as a prosecutor who never loses a case.
A fearsome storm swept across the coast, leaving destruction in its wake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frightening - causing fear or alarm
  • Synonym: terrifying - extremely frightening
  • Antonym: harmless - not causing harm or fear

Etymology

Formed from 'fear' + '-some' (suffix meaning 'characterized by').
