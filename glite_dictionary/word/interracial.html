interracial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntəˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
Involving or composed of people of different racial backgrounds.
involving different races

Examples

The city is known for its history of interracial cooperation and understanding.
They attended an interracial wedding last summer.
The new policy aims to encourage interracial friendships among students.

Synset Examples

interracial schools
a mixed neighborhood

Etymology

Formed from 'inter-' (between) and 'racial' (relating to race), first recorded in the early 20th century.

2. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈreɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪʃəl / (BrE)
involving or existing between people of different races
involving people of different races

Examples

The city has a long history of interracial cooperation and activism.
They faced criticism for their interracial marriage in the 1960s.
The documentary explores the challenges of raising children in an interracial family.

Synset Examples

interracial conflict

Related Terms

  • Synonym: multiracial - involving or composed of people from several races

Etymology

From inter- (between) + racial (relating to race), first used in the early 20th century.
