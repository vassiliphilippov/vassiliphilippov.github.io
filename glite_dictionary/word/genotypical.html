genotypical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiː.nəˈtɪp.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.nəˈtɪp.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a genotype; concerned with the genetic constitution of an organism rather than its observable traits.
relating to a genotype

Examples

The genotypical analysis revealed important differences among the plant varieties.
A genotypical pattern can persist even if environmental conditions change.
Researchers focused on the genotypical characteristics inherited by each generation.

Synset Examples

genotypical pattern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity

Etymology

From 'genotype' + '-ical'; 'genotype' from German 'Genotypus,' from Greek 'genos' (race, kind) and 'typos' (type).
