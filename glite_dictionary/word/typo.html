typo

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpəʊ / (BrE)
An error in printed or typed text caused by a mistake in typing or typesetting.
a mistake in printed or typed text

Examples

Please let me know if you spot any typo in the document.
He laughed when he noticed the embarrassing typo on the front page of the newspaper.
The email contained a typo that changed the entire meaning of the sentence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typographical error - an error in typed or printed material

Etymology

Shortened form of 'typographical error', from English 'typography'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪ.poʊ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.pəʊ / (BrE)
A small mistake in typed or printed text, such as a misspelled word or incorrect letter.
mistake in typed text

Examples

She corrected the typo in the document before submitting it.
There's an embarrassing typo in the headline of the newspaper.
If you find a typo in the report, please let me know.

Synset Examples

He always laughs about his typo in the email.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misspelling - the act of spelling a word incorrectly

Etymology

Shortened form of 'typographical error'.
