Tehran

1. PROPN

/ tɛˈhɒːrən / (AmE), / tɛˈhɑːrɑːn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Iran, situated in the northern part of the country
capital city of Iran

Examples

Many tourists visit Tehran to explore its rich history and culture each year.
The conference will be held in Tehran next spring.
After moving to Tehran, she quickly adapted to the city's fast-paced lifestyle.

Etymology

From Persian تهران (Tehrân), the name of the city.
