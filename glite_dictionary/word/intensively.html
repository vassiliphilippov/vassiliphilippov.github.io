intensively

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈtɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that involves a lot of effort, attention, or concentration.
with great effort or focus

Examples

He prepared for the exam intensively to ensure he understood every detail.
The crops were grown intensively to maximize the use of available land.
She trained intensively for the marathon, running every day for months.

Synset Examples

he studied the snake intensively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoroughly - in a thorough manner; completely and carefully

Etymology

From intensive + -ly, based on Latin intensus (stretched, intense) and English adverbial suffix -ly.
