greatly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡreɪtli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtli / (BrE)
To a very large or significant extent or degree; extremely or considerably.
to a very large extent

Examples

She appreciated your help greatly and thanked you in her speech.
The company's performance improved greatly after the new manager arrived.
Advances in medical research have greatly increased life expectancy.

Synset Examples

he improved greatly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: considerably - to a large or significant degree
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small extent or degree
  • Degree Lower: somewhat - to a moderate extent or degree

Etymology

From 'great' + '-ly', Old English 'grēat' meaning 'large'.
