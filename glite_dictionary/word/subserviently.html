subserviently

1. ADV

/ səbˈsɝviəntli / (AmE), / səbˈsɜːviəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows willingness to obey others unquestioningly or to be excessively submissive
in an excessively obedient or submissive manner

Examples

He followed his manager's instructions subserviently, never questioning her decisions.
The servant bowed subserviently as the guests entered the room.
She smiled subserviently at her employer, hoping to gain his approval.

Synset Examples

she acts obsequiously toward her boss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsequiously - in an excessively eager to please or obey manner
  • Synonym: servilely - in a manner showing an excessive willingness to serve or please others
  • Antonym: defiantly - in a manner that shows open resistance or bold disobedience

Etymology

From 'subservient' (from Latin subserviens, 'subject to, serving'), with adverbial -ly.
