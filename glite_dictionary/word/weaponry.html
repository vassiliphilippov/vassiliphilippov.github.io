weaponry

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛpənri / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənri / (BrE)
all the weapons of a particular type, country, or group, considered together
all weapons collectively

Examples

The country displayed its advanced weaponry during the military parade.
Modern weaponry has changed the nature of warfare significantly.
The museum holds a collection of medieval weaponry from across Europe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armament - military weapons and equipment

Etymology

From weapon + -ry (suffix denoting a collection of items).
