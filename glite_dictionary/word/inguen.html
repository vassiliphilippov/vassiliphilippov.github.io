inguen

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡwən / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡwɛn / (BrE)
the crease at the junction between the inner thigh and the trunk, often including the adjacent region and external genitals; groin
groin region; crease of thigh/trunk
medical

Examples

A hernia may appear in the inguen and sometimes causes visible swelling.
Pain in the inguen can indicate injury to the muscles or ligaments of the groin.
During the examination, the doctor checked the patient's inguen for any abnormalities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: groin - the area between the abdomen and the thigh on either side of the body

Etymology

From Latin 'inguen' meaning 'groin'.
