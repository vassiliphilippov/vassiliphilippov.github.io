Examples

Heavy precipitation caused flooding in several areas throughout the state last night.
Farmers closely monitor precipitation levels to predict crop yields each year.
Meteorologists forecast a week of scattered precipitation across the region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rain - water that falls from clouds in droplets
