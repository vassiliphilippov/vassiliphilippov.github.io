to slenderize

1. VERB

/ ˈslɛndɚˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈslɛndəˌaɪz / (BrE)
to make or become slimmer or thinner, especially by losing weight or by shaping
make or become slimmer

Examples

She decided to slenderize her figure by joining a local fitness club.
This dress is designed to slenderize your silhouette and create a more elegant appearance.
Many people try to slenderize through diet and regular exercise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim - make or become slim
  • Antonym: to fatten - make or become fatter

Etymology

From 'slender' + '-ize', forming a verb meaning 'to make slender'.

2. VERB

/ ˈslɛndərˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈslɛndəˌaɪz / (BrE)
to make or appear to be slender or thinner
make or appear slender

Examples

The designer created special panels to slenderize the dress and enhance the model's silhouette.
These exercises are meant to slenderize your waistline over time.
The new style of trousers is designed to slenderize your legs.

Synset Examples

slenderizing skirts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim - make or become slim
  • Antonym: to thicken - make or become thicker

Etymology

Formed from 'slender' + suffix '-ize'.
