celebratory

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛləˌbreɪtəri / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛbrətəri / (BrE)
done or used to celebrate a special event or occasion; expressing celebration
expressing celebration or festivity

Examples

The mood at dinner was delightfully celebratory after the team won the championship.
They opened a celebratory bottle of champagne on New Year's Eve.
Her speech had a celebratory tone, marking the company’s twentieth anniversary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festive - having or relating to a celebration or festival

Etymology

From Latin celebratus (celebrated, much-frequented) + English -ory suffix.
