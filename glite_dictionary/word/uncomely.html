uncomely

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkʌmli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌmli / (BrE)
(of behavior or appearance) not proper or appropriate in polite society; not in accordance with accepted standards.
not proper or appropriate

Examples

His uncomely remarks at the dinner table made everyone uncomfortable.
It was considered uncomely for women to speak so boldly at that time.
The neighbors gossiped about the uncomely state of the yard.

Synset Examples

was buried with indecent haste
indecorous behavior
language unbecoming to a lady
unseemly to use profanity
moved to curb their untoward ribaldry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improper - not in accordance with accepted rules or standards
  • Synonym: indecorous - not in keeping with good taste and propriety
  • Antonym: comely - pleasant to look at; appropriate

Etymology

Un- (not) + comely (pleasant to look at), from Old English 'cymlic' (beautiful, agreeable).
