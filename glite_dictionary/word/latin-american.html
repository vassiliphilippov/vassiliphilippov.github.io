Latin-American

1. ADJ

/ ˌlætɪn əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌlatɪn əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
Relating to Latin America or its people and cultures.
of or relating to Latin America

Examples

Many Latin-American countries share a rich tradition of music and dance.
The festival features Latin-American cuisine from Brazil, Mexico, and Argentina.
She is researching Latin-American literature for her thesis.

Synset Examples

Latin-American countries
Latin-American music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Latino - A person of Latin American origin or descent (US usage)
  • Synonym: Hispanic - Relating to Spanish-speaking countries, especially those of Latin America

Etymology

Formed from 'Latin America' (term for the Spanish- and Portuguese-speaking countries of the Americas) + '-an'.
