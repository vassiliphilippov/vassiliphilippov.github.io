bitey

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaɪti / (AmE), / ˈbaɪti / (BrE)
(of an animal, or sometimes a person) tending to bite, especially aggressively.
tending to bite; aggressive biter
informal

Examples

You have to be careful with that puppy; he can be quite bitey when he gets excited.
The toddler went through a bitey phase at nursery school last year.
Some snakes are more bitey than others and should only be handled by experienced keepers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressive - behaving in a hostile or forceful manner
  • Antonym: gentle - mild and kindly; not aggressive or violent
