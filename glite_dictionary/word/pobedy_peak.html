Pobedy Peak

1. PROPN

/ poʊˈbɛdi pik / (AmE), / pəʊˈbɛdi piːk / (BrE)
A mountain peak in the Tien Shan mountain range, known for its height and prominence as one of the highest peaks in the region.
a major peak in the Tien Shan mountains

Examples

Climbers from around the world have attempted to reach the summit of Pobedy Peak.
The harsh weather conditions on Pobedy Peak make it a challenging destination for mountaineers.
Photographs of Pobedy Peak capture its dramatic slopes rising above the Tien Shan range.

Etymology

From Russian 'Pik Pobedy', meaning 'Victory Peak'.
