haemopoietic

1. ADJ

/ hiːməˌpɔɪˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / hiːməʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the formation of blood or blood cells.
relating to blood cell formation
medical british

Examples

Haemopoietic disorders can lead to severe anemia and immune deficiencies.
The doctor explained that bone marrow is the primary haemopoietic tissue in adults.
Scientists are studying haemopoietic stem cells to treat various blood-related diseases.

Synset Examples

hemopoietic stem cells in bone marrow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hematopoietic - relating to the formation of blood or blood cells (American spelling)

Etymology

From Greek haima (blood) + poiein (to make), via medical Latin.
