typology

1. NOUN

/ taɪˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / taɪˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or systematic classification of things according to their general types or categories
systematic classification by type

Examples

The typology of ancient pottery helps archaeologists identify different cultures.
She developed a typology of leadership styles to categorize managers in the company.
This book presents a new typology for urban communities based on their social structure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classification - process of organizing things into groups based on shared qualities

Etymology

From Greek 'typos' (type) + 'logia' (study of).
