fearful

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪrfl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfɪəfəl / (BrE)
feeling or showing fear; afraid or anxious about something.
feeling or showing fear

Examples

The child gave a fearful look when she heard the loud noise.
She was fearful of making a mistake during her presentation.
He spoke in a fearful whisper, afraid someone might overhear.

Synset Examples

fearful of criticism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afraid - feeling fear or anxiety; frightened
  • Antonym: fearless - without fear; brave

Etymology

From Old English 'fyrþfull', from 'fyrhto' (fear) + '-ful'.
