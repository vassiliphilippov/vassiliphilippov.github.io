dracaena

1. NOUN

/ drəˈsiːnə / (AmE), / drəˈsiːnə / (BrE)
A tropical plant with stiff, sword-shaped leaves, often grown indoors for its ornamental foliage.
tropical ornamental plant with sword-shaped leaves

Examples

The dracaena is a popular choice for office plants due to its low maintenance needs.
She decorated her living room with a large dracaena in the corner.
Many gardeners appreciate the striking foliage of the dracaena.

Etymology

From Latin 'dracaena', from Greek 'drakaina' (female dragon), referring to the red resin resembling dragon's blood from some species.
