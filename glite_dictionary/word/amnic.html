amnic

1. ADJ

/ ˈæmnɪk / (AmE), / ˈamnɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to the amnion, or characterized by developing an amnion
relating to the amnion
medical

Examples

The amnic sac plays a crucial role in the protection of the developing embryo.
Researchers observed changes in amnic structures during different stages of gestation.
Certain disorders may cause complications in the amnic membrane.

Synset Examples

amniotic membrane

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amniotic - relating to or derived from the amnion

Etymology

From amnion (a membrane enclosing the embryo) + -ic (adjective suffix).
