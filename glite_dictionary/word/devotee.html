devotee

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛvəˈti / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəˈtiː / (BrE)
a person who is very enthusiastic about someone or something, often showing great devotion or admiration
enthusiastic follower or admirer

Examples

She is a lifelong devotee of classical music, attending concerts at every opportunity.
The restaurant's signature dish has gained many devotees over the years.
As a yoga devotee, he practices every morning before work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for someone or something
  • Synonym: admirer - one who admires someone or something deeply
  • Antonym: detractor - a person who disparages or criticizes someone or something

Etymology

From French dévot (devout), from Latin devotus (devoted, pious).
