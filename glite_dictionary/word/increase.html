increase

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌkris / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːs / (BrE)
a process of becoming larger, greater, or more numerous
process of growing larger or more numerous

Examples

There was a noticeable increase in sales during the holiday season.
The doctor warned about the risks of a sudden increase in blood pressure.
An increase in temperature could have serious environmental consequences.

Synset Examples

the increase in unemployment
the growth of population

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decrease - a process of becoming smaller or fewer
  • Synonym: growth - an increase in size, amount, or degree

Etymology

From Old French *encreistre*, from Latin *increscere* (to grow in).
