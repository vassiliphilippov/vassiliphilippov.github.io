appreciable

1. ADJ

/ əˈpriʃəbəl / (AmE), / əˈpriːʃəbl / (BrE)
large or important enough to be noticed or measured; considerable.
large or significant enough to be noticed

Examples

There was an appreciable increase in temperature over the weekend.
The change in her attitude was barely appreciable to those who knew her well.
Only appreciable amounts of the substance are considered dangerous to health.

Synset Examples

appreciable amounts of noxious wastes are dumped into the harbor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: measurable - able to be measured
  • Antonym: negligible - so small as to be unimportant or not worth considering

Etymology

From Late Latin 'appretiabilis' (estimable), from Latin 'appretiare' (to value).
