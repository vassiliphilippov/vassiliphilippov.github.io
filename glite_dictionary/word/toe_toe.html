toe toe

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊ toʊ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ təʊ / (BrE)
A tall grass native to New Zealand, grown for its large, plumelike flower heads.
tall New Zealand grass with plumelike heads

Examples

The wind rustled through the toe toe along the riverbank.
In spring, the toe toe produces beautiful, feathery plumes.
They used dried toe toe leaves to weave traditional baskets.

Etymology

From Māori 'toetoe', referring to these native grasses.
