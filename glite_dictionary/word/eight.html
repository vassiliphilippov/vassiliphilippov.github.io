eight

1. NUM

/ eɪt / (AmE), / eɪt / (BrE)
the cardinal number that is the sum of seven and one, represented as 8
the number 8

Examples

There are eight students in the classroom today.
The spider has eight legs, which makes it unique among insects.
She bought eight apples from the market.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: seven - the cardinal number before eight
  • Degree Higher: nine - the cardinal number after eight

Etymology

From Old English 'eahta', from Proto-Germanic '*ahtōu', from Proto-Indo-European '*oḱtō(u)'.
