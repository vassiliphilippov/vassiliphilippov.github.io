pro-inflammatory

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊɪnˈflæməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌprəʊɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri / (BrE)
Tending to cause or promote inflammation in the body.
causing inflammation
medical

Examples

A diet high in sugar is considered to have pro-inflammatory effects on the body.
Certain autoimmune diseases are fueled by pro-inflammatory cytokines.
Doctors study how pro-inflammatory factors influence chronic illnesses like arthritis.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anti-inflammatory - reducing inflammation

Etymology

Formed from 'pro-' meaning 'promoting' and 'inflammatory' from Latin 'inflammare' (to set on fire).
