sleekly

1. ADV

/ ˈsliːkli / (AmE), / ˈsliːkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is smooth, glossy, or elegantly streamlined.
in a smooth or glossy manner

Examples

The car's surface reflected the sunlight sleekly after it was polished.
She styled her hair sleekly for the evening gala.
The panther moved sleekly through the underbrush, barely making a sound.

Synset Examples

the wet road was shining sleekly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoothly - in a smooth manner; without difficulty or interruption
