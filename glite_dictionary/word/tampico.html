Tampico

1. PROPN

/ tæmˈpikoʊ / (AmE), / tamˈpiːkəʊ / (BrE)
A port city in eastern Mexico, located on the Gulf of Mexico, known for its oil industry and shipping.
port city in eastern Mexico

Examples

Many international ships dock at Tampico to load oil and other goods.
During my travels in Mexico, I spent two days exploring Tampico.
The hurricane caused significant flooding in Tampico and surrounding areas.
