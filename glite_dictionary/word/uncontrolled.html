uncontrolled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈtroʊld / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈtrəʊld / (BrE)
Not regulated or restrained; not kept within limits or under control.
not regulated or under control

Examples

The fire spread rapidly because it was uncontrolled for several hours.
He showed uncontrolled anger during the meeting, surprising everyone in the room.
There was uncontrolled laughter when the comedian told his best joke.

Synset Examples

the greatest uncontrolled health problem is AIDS
uncontrolled growth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: controlled - regulated or restrained, under control
  • Synonym: chaotic - completely confused and unpredictable, in a state of chaos

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'controlled', from Latin 'contrarotulus' (register), through Old French 'contrerole'; meaning not controlled.
