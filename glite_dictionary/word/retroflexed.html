retroflexed

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛtroʊˌflɛkst / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌflɛkst / (BrE)
bent or curved backward, especially referring to a bodily part or structure.
bent or curved backward

Examples

The doctor noted that the patient's uterus was retroflexed during the examination.
Some reptiles have a retroflexed tongue, allowing them to capture prey more effectively.
In rare cases, the tip of the nose may appear retroflexed after certain surgical procedures.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: flexed - bent, especially referring to a limb or joint

Etymology

From Latin 'retroflexus' (bent backward), from 'retro-' (back) + 'flectere' (to bend).
