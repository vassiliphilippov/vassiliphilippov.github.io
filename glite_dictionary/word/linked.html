linked

1. ADJ

/ lɪŋkt / (AmE), / lɪŋkt / (BrE)
connected by a link or links; joined together.
connected; joined together

Examples

The linked train cars moved smoothly along the track.
These two systems are closely linked in operation.
She wore a bracelet made of linked silver loops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connected - joined or linked together
  • Antonym: unconnected - not joined or linked together
