play-actor

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪ ˌæktər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ ˌæktə / (BrE)
an actor, especially one who travels to different places to present plays
travelling stage actor

Examples

The old village hall often welcomed a troupe led by a famous play-actor.
As a play-actor, she journeyed from town to town performing Shakespeare.
Many play-actors in the 19th century relied on their skills and adaptability to survive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actor - a person whose profession is acting on the stage, in films, or on television
