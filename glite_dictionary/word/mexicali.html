Mexicali

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɛksɪˈkæli / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪˈkɑːli / (BrE)
A city in northwestern Mexico, located on the border with California, United States.
city in northwestern Mexico

Examples

The border between the United States and Mexico runs just north of Mexicali.
Many people visit Mexicali to experience its vibrant culinary scene.
After crossing into California, we stopped in Mexicali for lunch.

Etymology

Blend of Mexico and California, representing its location on the Mexico–California border.
