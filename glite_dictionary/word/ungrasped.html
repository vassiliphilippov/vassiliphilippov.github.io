ungrasped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡræspd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrɑːspt / (BrE)
not fully understood or comprehended; not apprehended mentally
not fully understood or comprehended

Examples

The underlying principle of the theory remains ungrasped by many students.
She found herself drawn to the mystery of the ungrasped aspects of the universe.
Much of his explanation was lost, leaving the main idea still ungrasped.

Synset Examples

the ungrasped infinite ground of all being

Related Terms

  • Antonym: understood - fully apprehended or grasped mentally
  • Synonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand or grasp

Etymology

From un- (not) + grasped, past participle of grasp.
