opencut

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊpənˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənˌkʌt / (BrE)
(of a mine or mining) worked from the exposed surface rather than underground.
worked from the exposed surface (mining)

Examples

The company invested heavily in a new opencut coal mine to increase production.
Safety measures in opencut mining are different from those used in underground operations.
The landscape was dramatically changed by decades of opencut excavation.

Synset Examples

opencast mining
an opencut iron mine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: opencast - worked from the exposed surface (mining)
