polemoniaceous

1. ADJ

/ ˌpoʊlɪˌmoʊniˈeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌpəʊlɪˌməʊnɪˈeɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants of the family Polemoniaceae, which includes phloxes and their relatives
of the Polemoniaceae plant family

Examples

Many polemoniaceous species are popular in gardens for their colorful blooms.
The scientist studied the distinctive reproductive structures of several polemoniaceous plants.
Phlox, a common garden flower, is a well-known polemoniaceous genus.

Etymology

From modern Latin Polemoniaceae (the botanic family name), from the type genus Polemonium.
