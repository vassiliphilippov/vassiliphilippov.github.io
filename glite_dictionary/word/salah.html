salah

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɑː / (AmE), / səˈlɑː / (BrE)
the ritual prayer of Muslims performed five times a day as a religious duty, facing towards Mecca
Muslim ritual prayer performed five times daily

Examples

Muslims are required to perform salah five times each day.
He set his alarm early for morning salah before sunrise.
The Imam led the congregation in salah at the mosque.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prayer - an act of communicating with a deity, especially as a religious ritual

Etymology

From Arabic ṣalāh (prayer, devotion).
