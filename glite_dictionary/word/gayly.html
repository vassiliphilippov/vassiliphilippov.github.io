gayly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡeɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪli / (BrE)
In a joyous, cheerful, or lively manner.
joyfully; cheerfully

Examples

The children danced gayly around the maypole at the festival.
She skipped gayly down the street, humming a tune to herself.
The room was decorated gayly for the holiday celebration, with ribbons and balloons everywhere.

Synset Examples

they shouted happily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaily - in a joyous, cheerful, or lively manner

Etymology

From 'gay' (meaning cheerful, lively) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
