Albatrellus

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.bəˈtrɛl.əs / (AmE), / ˌal.bəˈtrɛl.əs / (BrE)
a genus of fungi in the family Polyporaceae, comprising several species of mushroom-producing fungi
genus of mushroom-producing fungi

Examples

The species Albatrellus ovinus is a type of edible mushroom found in coniferous forests.
Researchers collected several specimens of Albatrellus for laboratory analysis.
Some members of the genus Albatrellus are known for their distinctive texture and color.

Etymology

From New Latin, based on Greek 'albe', possibly meaning 'white', and 'trellus', diminutive form; used in taxonomic naming.
