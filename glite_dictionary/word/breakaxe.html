breakaxe

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkˌæks / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌaks / (BrE)
A West Indian timber tree known for its very hard wood.
West Indian tree with hard wood

Examples

The wood from the breakaxe is prized for making durable tools and furniture.
Local carpenters prefer breakaxe because of its resistance to splitting.
We saw a towering breakaxe tree in the rainforest while hiking.
