citric

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪtrɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from citric acid
relating to citric acid

Examples

The sour flavor in the drink comes from its citric content.
Some fruits, like lemons, are naturally high in citric compounds.
The citric acid cycle is an essential process in cellular respiration.

Etymology

From French 'citrique', from 'citrate', relating to 'citron' (lemon).
