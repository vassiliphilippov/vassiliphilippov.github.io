shtikl

1. NOUN

/ ʃˈtɪkəl / (AmE), / ʃˈtɪkəl / (BrE)
A small piece or bit of something, often food.
a small piece or bit

Examples

He offered me a shtikl cake after dinner.
Just a shtikl of cheese remained on the plate.
She asked for a shtikl of bread with her soup.

Synset Examples

have a shtikl cake

Etymology

From Yiddish שטיקל (shtikl), diminutive of שטיק (shtik, 'piece').
