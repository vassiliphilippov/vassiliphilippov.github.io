roleplaying

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊlˌpleɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlˌpleɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of acting out a specific role, often as a method in psychotherapy, training, or education
acting out a specific role (therapy, training)

Examples

During the session, patients engaged in roleplaying to better understand each other's perspectives.
The workshop included several roleplaying exercises to practice conflict resolution skills.
In therapy, roleplaying can help clients prepare for challenging real-life situations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: role play - to act out or perform the part of a character or situation
