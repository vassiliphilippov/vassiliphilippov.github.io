transmundane

1. ADJ

/ trænzˈmʌn.deɪn / (AmE), / tranzˈmʌn.deɪn / (BrE)
existing or extending beyond the physical or material world; supernatural or transcending normal experience
beyond the physical world

Examples

The philosopher explored transmundane realms that defy ordinary understanding.
Some poets seek inspiration in transmundane sources beyond the physical senses.
He claimed to have had a transmundane vision during meditation.

Synset Examples

whatever of transmundane … insight … we may carry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supernatural - existing beyond the laws of nature; not explainable by natural laws
  • Antonym: mundane - relating to the physical or material world; ordinary

Etymology

From Latin trans- (across, beyond) + mundanus (of the world).
