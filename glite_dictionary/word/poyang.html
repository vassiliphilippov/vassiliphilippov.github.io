Poyang

1. PROPN

/ poʊˈjɑŋ / (AmE), / pɔɪˈjæŋ / (BrE)
a large lake in central China that is connected to the Yangtze River by a canal
lake in central China

Examples

The water level of Poyang Lake changes dramatically throughout the year.
Many bird species migrate to Poyang during the winter months.
Environmentalists are concerned about pollution in Poyang due to industrial runoff.
