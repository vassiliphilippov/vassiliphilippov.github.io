round-backed

1. ADJ

/ ˈraʊndˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈraʊndˌbækt / (BrE)
having the back and shoulders rounded, not straight or erect
having a rounded back

Examples

The elderly man walked slowly, his round-backed figure stooped with age.
A round-backed chair offers extra support for the spine and shoulders.
The statue, depicting a round-backed monk, stood quietly in the garden.

Synset Examples

a little oldish misshapen stooping woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stooped - having the head and shoulders habitually bent forward
  • Synonym: hunched - having the back and shoulders curved forward
  • Antonym: erect - upright in posture; not slouched
