benzoic

1. ADJ

/ bɛnˈzoʊ.ɪk / (AmE), / bɛnˈzəʊ.ɪk / (BrE)
containing or derived from benzoic acid or benzoin
relating to benzoic acid
medical

Examples

The researchers studied the effects of benzoic compounds on microbial growth.
Benzoic derivatives are widely used as preservatives in the food industry.
The color reaction indicated the presence of benzoic substances in the solution.

Etymology

From benzoic acid, itself from benzoin (a resin) + -ic (forming adjectives).
