starfighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrˌfaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
A spacecraft designed for combat in outer space, especially in science fiction works.
spacecraft for combat in outer space

Examples

The rebel pilot quickly boarded his starfighter and prepared for the upcoming battle.
Several starfighters engaged the enemy fleet, dodging lasers and asteroids alike.
In the popular film, the hero's starfighter is heavily modified for speed and maneuverability.

Synset Examples

A starfighter hurtled through the scattered metal, the pilot desperate to find a way to stop himself from joining the ever growing population of this interstellar graveyard.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: spaceship - a general term for a spacecraft, not necessarily for combat

Etymology

Formed from 'star' and 'fighter', popularized by science fiction works such as Star Wars (1970s onward).
