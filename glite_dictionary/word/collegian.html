collegian

1. NOUN

/ kəˈliːdʒiən / (AmE), / kəˈliːdʒiən / (BrE)
a student or former student at a college or university
college or university student

Examples

During her years as a collegian, she joined several academic clubs and organizations.
The reunion was attended by collegians and alumni from all around the world.
As a collegian, he developed a strong interest in political science.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undergraduate - a university student who has not yet received a first degree
  • Synonym: alumnus - a former student of a particular school, college, or university

Etymology

From Latin 'collegium' (association, society) via French 'collégien'.
