lymphocytic

1. ADJ

/ lɪm.fəˈsɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / lɪm.fəˈsɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of lymphocytes, a type of white blood cell involved in immune response
relating to lymphocytes
medical

Examples

The doctor diagnosed her with lymphocytic leukemia after a series of blood tests.
A lymphocytic infiltration was observed in the tissue sample.
Increased lymphocytic activity can indicate an ongoing immune response.

Synset Examples

lymphocytic leukemia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lymphocyte - a type of white blood cell involved in the immune response

Etymology

From 'lymphocyte', from 'lymph' + Greek kytos (cell), with '-ic' suffix meaning 'of or relating to'.
