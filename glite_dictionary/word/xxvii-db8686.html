xxvii

1. NUM

/ ˌɛks ˌɛks ˈvʌɪ ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛks ˌɛks ˈvɪ ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the Roman numeral representing the number 27
the Roman numeral for 27

Examples

The page was marked as xxvii in the introduction of the book.
Chapters were numbered in Roman numerals, and I started reading from xxvii.
His reference was on page xxvii of the manuscript.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 27 - the cardinal number twenty-seven

Etymology

From Latin numerals: XX (20) + VII (7).
