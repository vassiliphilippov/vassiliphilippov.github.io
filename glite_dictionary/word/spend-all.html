spend-all

1. NOUN

/ spɛnd ɔl / (AmE), / spɛnd ɔːl / (BrE)
a person who spends money wastefully or extravagantly; a prodigal spender
person who spends money extravagantly

Examples

Everyone knows a spend-all who can never keep any money in their wallet.
His reputation as a spend-all made lenders wary of giving him credit.
Despite his modest income, Tom lived like a spend-all and was always broke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spendthrift - someone who spends money recklessly
  • Antonym: miser - a person who is reluctant to spend money
