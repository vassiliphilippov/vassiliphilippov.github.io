Curacao

1. PROPN

/ ˌkʊr əˈsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkjʊərəˈsaʊ / (BrE)
an island in the southern Caribbean Sea, part of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, known for its popular resorts and the capital city of Willemstad.
island in the southern Caribbean Sea

Examples

She spent her vacation in Curacao, enjoying the beaches and coral reefs.
Many cruise ships make a stop at Curacao to allow passengers to explore Willemstad.
The colorful colonial architecture is one of the main attractions of Curacao.

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Curaçao', possibly from Spanish 'corazón' (heart).
