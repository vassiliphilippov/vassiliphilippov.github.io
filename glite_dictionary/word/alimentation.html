alimentation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.ə.mənˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌal.ɪ.mənˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of supplying food and nourishment to a person, animal, or organ
process of providing nourishment

Examples

Proper alimentation is essential for the recovery of patients after surgery.
The veterinarian discussed the alimentation of newborn kittens to ensure their health.
Modern diets often focus on the quality of alimentation rather than simply calorie intake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition - the process of obtaining and using food for health and growth

Etymology

From Latin 'alīmentum' (nourishment), from 'alere' (to nourish).

2. NOUN

/ ˌæl.ɪ.mənˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæ.lɪ.menˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the provision or supply of nourishment or nutrients to the body
the supply of nourishment

Examples

Proper alimentation is vital for the healthy growth of children.
The doctor discussed ways to improve the patient's alimentation during recovery.
Many diseases are linked to poor alimentation and inadequate diets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutrition - the process of taking in and using food for health and growth

Etymology

From Latin alimentatio (nourishment), from alimentum (food, nourishment).
