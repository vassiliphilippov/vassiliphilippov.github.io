barky

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈbɑːki / (BrE)
having a rough texture or appearance like the bark of a tree.
resembling tree bark in texture

Examples

The old fence was covered in barky paint that was peeling off in strips.
His rough, barky hands showed years of hard outdoor work.
The artist chose a barky material for her sculpture to evoke a natural look.

Synset Examples

the rattlesnake's barky scales

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bark-like - resembling or having the qualities of tree bark
