hebdomadary

1. ADJ

/ hɛbˈdɑːməˌdɛri / (AmE), / hɛbˈdɒmədəri / (BrE)
Happening or recurring every seven days; weekly.
every seven days; weekly
archaic

Examples

The meetings are held on a hebdomadary schedule.
She sends a hebdomadary report to her supervisor.
This ritual became a hebdomadary tradition within the community.

Synset Examples

a weekly visit
weekly paper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weekly - occurring every week

Etymology

From Late Latin hebdomadarius, from Greek hebdomas (seven, week).
