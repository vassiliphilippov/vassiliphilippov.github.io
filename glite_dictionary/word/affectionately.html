affectionately

1. ADV

/ əˈfɛkʃənətli / (AmE), / əˈfɛkʃənətli / (BrE)
In a way that shows affection or fondness.
in a loving or fond manner

Examples

She hugged her children affectionately before leaving for work.
He always speaks affectionately about his hometown.
The puppy licked its owner's face affectionately after being reunited.

Synset Examples

she loved him dearly
he treats her affectionately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondly - in a loving or affectionate manner
  • Antonym: coldly - in an unemotional or detached manner

Etymology

From 'affectionate' + '-ly'.
