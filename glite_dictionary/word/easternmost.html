easternmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːstərnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈiːstənˌməʊst / (BrE)
Farthest towards the east or most easterly in position or location.
farthest to the east

Examples

The easternmost point of the island is marked by a lighthouse.
They traveled to the easternmost province in search of rare wildlife.
The city is situated at the easternmost edge of the country.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: westernmost - farthest to the west
  • Synonym: northernmost - farthest to the north
  • Synonym: southernmost - farthest to the south

Etymology

Formed from 'eastern' + 'most', following the pattern of superlative direction names.
