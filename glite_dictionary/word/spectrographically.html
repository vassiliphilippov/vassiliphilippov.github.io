spectrographically

1. ADV

/ ˌspɛktroʊˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌspɛktrəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
By means of a spectrograph; in a manner relating to or using spectrography.
by means of a spectrograph

Examples

The chemical composition of the sample was determined spectrographically in the laboratory.
Researchers analyzed the starlight spectrographically to detect the presence of exoplanets.
The vibrational modes in the crystal were identified spectrographically using advanced equipment.

Synset Examples

the speech spectrum was displayed spectrographically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spectrography - the process or technique of making and analyzing spectra

Etymology

From 'spectrographic' (relating to spectrography) + -ally (adverbial suffix).
