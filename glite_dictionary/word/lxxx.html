LXXX

1. NUM

/ ˈɛl ˈeɪt̬i / (AmE), / ˈɛl ˈeɪti / (BrE)
the Roman numeral for eighty (80)
Roman numeral for 80

Examples

The inscription on the statue reads 'LXXX' to signify its eighty-year anniversary.
Chapter LXXX of the book contains an important revelation.
The Roman soldier reported that his unit consisted of LXXX men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighty - the number 80

Etymology

From Latin numerals: 'L' for 50 and 'X' for 10, repeated three times for 30, making 80.
