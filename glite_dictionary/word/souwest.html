sou'west

1. ADV

/ saʊˈwɛst / (AmE), / saʊˈwɛst / (BrE)
In or toward the southwest direction.
in or toward the southwest

Examples

The ship sailed sou'west to avoid the storm.
They traveled sou'west across the open fields.
The wind shifted and began blowing sou'west early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: southwest - in or toward the southwest

Etymology

Shortened form of 'southwest', influenced by nautical and dialectal speech.
