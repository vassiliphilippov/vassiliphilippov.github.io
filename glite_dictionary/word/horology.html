horology

1. NOUN

/ hɔˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / hɒˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study and art of measuring time and making clocks and watches
study and making of clocks and watches

Examples

She studied horology at university to pursue a career as a watchmaker.
Museums dedicated to horology often display antique clocks and timekeeping devices.
His passion for horology led him to collect rare and unusual watches from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chronometry - the science of measuring time accurately

Etymology

From French horologie and Latin horologium (clock, timepiece), from Greek hōrologion (from hōra 'hour' + -logion 'a device').
