Handelian

1. ADJ

/ hænˈdɛliən / (AmE), / hanˈdiːlɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of George Frederick Handel or his music
of or relating to Handel or his style

Examples

The orchestra performed a Handelian oratorio to commemorate the composer's birthday.
Her singing style in the aria was distinctly Handelian, evocative of the Baroque period.
The new concert hall's acoustics are said to suit Handelian works especially well.

Etymology

Formed from the name of George Frederick Handel (1685–1759), German-British composer, plus the suffix -ian.
