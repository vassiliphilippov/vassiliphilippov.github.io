guarded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdɪd / (BrE)
careful and restrained in expressing opinions, feelings, or expectations; showing caution or prudence
cautious or reserved in expression

Examples

His response was guarded, as he did not want to reveal too much.
Analysts are expressing guarded optimism about the company's future performance.
She offered only a guarded welcome, uncertain of his intentions.

Synset Examples

guarded optimism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cautious - showing care about avoiding risks or danger
  • Antonym: open - expressing feelings or opinions freely and honestly
