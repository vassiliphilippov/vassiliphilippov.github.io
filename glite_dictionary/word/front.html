front

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt / (AmE), / frʌnt / (BrE)
the side of something that is forward or most prominent
forward-facing side; most prominent side

Examples

Please write your name on the front of the envelope.
The front of the house faces the street and gets lots of sunlight.
He stood at the front of the line, ready to enter first.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: back - the side that is opposite to the front; the rear side
  • Synonym: face - the front part of the head; also the surface of something that is seen most

Etymology

From Old French *front*, from Latin *frons* (forehead, front).
