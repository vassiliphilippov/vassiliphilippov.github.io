Namur

1. PROPN

/ nɑˈmʊr / (AmE), / naˈmyːr / (BrE)
a city in south central Belgium, located at the confluence of the Meuse and Sambre Rivers, known for its strategic importance and historical battles during World War I and II.
city in south central Belgium

Examples

The fortified city of Namur played an important role during both world wars.
She visited Namur to see its historic citadel and impressive riverside views.
Many travelers pass through Namur on their way to explore the Ardennes region.
