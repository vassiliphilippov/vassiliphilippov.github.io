benedictory

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛnəˈdɪktəri / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktəri / (BrE)
expressing or containing a blessing; relating to or giving a benediction.
expressing a blessing

Examples

The priest gave a benedictory speech at the end of the ceremony.
Her benedictory words left everyone feeling hopeful and uplifted.
A benedictory gesture concluded the official proceedings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessing - a prayer asking for divine favor and protection
  • Antonym: maledictory - expressing a curse; opposite of benedictory

Etymology

From Latin 'benedictus' (blessed), via Late Latin 'benedictorius'.
