inadvertently

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.ədˈvɝː.tənt.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ədˈvɜː.tənt.li / (BrE)
Without intending to; as a result of neglect or oversight; unintentionally.
unintentionally, by accident

Examples

She inadvertently left her keys at home, causing her to be locked out.
If you inadvertently offend someone, it's best to apologize quickly.
He inadvertently sent the email to the wrong recipient.

Synset Examples

he unwittingly deleted the references

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accidentally - by accident; unintentionally
  • Antonym: deliberately - in a way that is done on purpose; intentionally

Etymology

From 'inadvertent' (from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'advertere' (to turn toward)), with adverbial suffix '-ly'.
