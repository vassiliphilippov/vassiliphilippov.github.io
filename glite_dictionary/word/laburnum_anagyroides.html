Laburnum anagyroides

1. NOUN

/ ləˈbɜrnəm ænəˈdʒɪrɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ləˈbɜːnəm anəˈdʒɪərɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
a species of laburnum tree or shrub with hanging clusters of yellow flowers, widely grown as an ornamental plant
ornamental tree with yellow flowers

Examples

The Laburnum anagyroides blooms in late spring, covering the garden with golden yellow cascades.
Gardeners often use Laburnum anagyroides to add bright color and structure to their landscaping.
Every Easter, the churchyard is decorated with flowering branches from the Laburnum anagyroides.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laburnum - any tree or shrub of the genus Laburnum, with hanging yellow flowers

Etymology

From Latin genus name Laburnum and species epithet anagyroides, meaning 'resembling Anagyris' (a related plant genus).
