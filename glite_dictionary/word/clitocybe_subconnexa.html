Clitocybe subconnexa

1. NOUN

/ klaɪˈtɑːsɪ bi ˌsʌb kəˈnɛksə / (AmE), / klaɪˈtɒsɪ bi ˌsʌb kəˈnɛksə / (BrE)
An edible white mushroom that grows in dense clusters, with narrow, crowded gills and a fleshy, unpolished stalk.
an edible white mushroom species

Examples

The forest floor was dotted with clusters of Clitocybe subconnexa after the first autumn rain.
He carefully identified the Clitocybe subconnexa to ensure it was safe to eat.
Researchers documented the growth patterns of Clitocybe subconnexa in mixed woodland habitats.

Etymology

From modern Latin: 'Clitocybe' (genus) + 'subconnexa' (meaning 'somewhat connected').
