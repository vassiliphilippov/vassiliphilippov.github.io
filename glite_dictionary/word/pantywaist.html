pantywaist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.tiˌweɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tiˌweɪst / (BrE)
a man or boy who is regarded as weak, timid, or lacking in courage; a person considered unassertive or childish
timid or weak man or boy
informal

Examples

He called his brother a pantywaist for being too scared to climb the tree.
The coach warned that only a pantywaist would back down from a challenge.
Some critics dismissed the candidate as a political pantywaist lacking leadership.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wimp - a person who is weak or ineffectual
  • Synonym: sissy - a boy or man regarded as effeminate or cowardly
  • Antonym: brave - someone showing courage

Etymology

Originally from 'panty-waist', referring to a child's undergarment combining panties and a waist covering.
