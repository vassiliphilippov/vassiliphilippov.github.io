boric

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing boron, or derived from boron, especially in chemical compounds.
containing or relating to boron

Examples

Boric acid is commonly used as an antiseptic and insecticide.
The laboratory tested the sample for boric compounds.
Manufacturers often add boric substances to glass to improve its resistance to heat.

Synset Examples

boric acid

Etymology

From boron + -ic, indicating a relationship to boron.
