nodulose

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊdʒəˌloʊs / (AmE), / ˈnɒdjʊləʊs / (BrE)
having small nodules or lumps, especially on a surface; marked by the presence of minute nodules.
having small nodules

Examples

The surface of the leaf was nodulose, dotted with tiny lumps.
Some minerals display a nodulose texture under the microscope.
The botanist described the root as nodulose, featuring many small growths.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nodular - having nodules; characterized by nodules

Etymology

From Latin 'nodulus' (a small knot or nodule), diminutive of 'nodus' (knot).
