Henry III

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛnri ðə θɜrd / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri ðə ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
(1207–1272) King of England from 1216 to 1272, son of King John, whose reign was marked by civil unrest and conflict with the barons.
King of England, 1216–1272

Examples

Henry III ascended the throne of England when he was only nine years old.
The long reign of Henry III was troubled by frequent disputes with his nobility.
Many historic cathedrals were expanded during the time of Henry III.
