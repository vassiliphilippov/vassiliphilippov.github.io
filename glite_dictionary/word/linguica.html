linguica

1. NOUN

/ lɪŋˈɡwisə / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwiːsə / (BrE)
a highly seasoned Portuguese pork sausage flavored with garlic and onions
Portuguese pork sausage

Examples

The chef added slices of linguica to the traditional stew for extra flavor.
We bought some linguica at the farmers' market to grill for dinner.
Portuguese rolls with linguica and peppers are a favorite at local festivals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chorizo - a spicy Spanish pork sausage

Etymology

From Portuguese 'linguiça' (sausage), ultimately from Latin 'lingua' (tongue).
