northwester

1. NOUN

/ nɔrθˈwɛstər / (AmE), / nɔːθˈwɛstə / (BrE)
A wind blowing from the northwest.
wind from the northwest

Examples

A strong northwester swept across the open plains, bringing a sudden chill.
The sailors prepared for rough seas as a fierce northwester approached.
After days of heat, a cool northwester finally brought relief to the city.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: northwest wind - a wind coming from the northwest direction
