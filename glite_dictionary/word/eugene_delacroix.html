Eugene Delacroix

1. PROPN

/ juˈʒin ˌdɛləˈkrwɑ / (AmE), / juːˈʒeɪn ˌdɛləˈkrwɑː / (BrE)
(1798–1863) A French Romantic painter noted for his expressive brushstrokes and vivid use of color, regarded as a leading figure in 19th-century European art.
French Romantic painter (1798–1863)

Examples

Eugene Delacroix is renowned for his masterpiece "Liberty Leading the People."
Many art historians believe that Eugene Delacroix greatly influenced the Impressionist movement.
The vibrant colors in Eugene Delacroix's paintings set him apart from his contemporaries.
