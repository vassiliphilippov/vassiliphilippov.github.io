ways

1. NOUN

/ weɪz / (AmE), / weɪz / (BrE)
a sloping structure down to the water used for launching or repairing ships in a shipyard
sloping structure for launching ships

Examples

The ship slid smoothly down the ways into the water during the launching ceremony.
Workers repaired the ways before starting on the next vessel's construction.
After months in dry dock, the boat was finally returned to the water via the ways.
