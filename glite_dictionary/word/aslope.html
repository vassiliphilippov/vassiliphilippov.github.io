aslope

1. ADJ

/ əˈsloʊp / (AmE), / əˈsləʊp / (BrE)
sloping or inclined at an angle; not level or straight
sloping or inclined; not level

Examples

The roof was built aslope to help rainwater run off easily.
He rested his painting aslope against the wall to let it dry.
Snow lay aslope on the hillside, sparkling in the morning sun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sloping - inclined from a horizontal or vertical line
  • Antonym: level - having no incline; completely flat or horizontal

Etymology

From prefix 'a-' (on, in, at) + 'slope', modeled after analogous forms in Middle English.
