Pleven

1. PROPN

/ ˈplɛvən / (AmE), / ˈpliːvɛn / (BrE)
A city in northern Bulgaria, known for its historical siege during the Russo-Turkish War of 1877–78.
city in northern Bulgaria

Examples

The city of Pleven played a crucial role in the Russo-Turkish War.
Many tourists visit Pleven to learn about its historic siege.
He was born and raised in Pleven, Bulgaria.
