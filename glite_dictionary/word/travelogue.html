travelogue

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrævəˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɒɡ / (BrE)
a film, lecture, or written work describing someone's experiences while traveling, often featuring illustrations or footage from the journey
film or lecture about travel experiences

Examples

The filmmaker presented his travelogue about trekking through the Himalayas to a captivated audience.
Last night's travelogue showcased breathtaking footage from the Amazon rainforest.
She decided to create a travelogue documenting her European adventure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel journal - a written record of a person's travel experiences
  • Synonym: travel diary - a diary kept during journeys

Etymology

Blend of 'travel' and '-logue', from Greek 'logos' (word, speech).
