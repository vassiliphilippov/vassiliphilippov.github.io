Vanua Levu

1. PROPN

/ vəˌnuː.ə ˈleɪ.vuː / (AmE), / vəˌnjuː.ə ˈleɪ.vuː / (BrE)
the second-largest island of Fiji, located in the South Pacific and known for its volcanic origin
second-largest island of Fiji

Examples

Many tourists visit Vanua Levu for its untouched rainforests and beautiful beaches.
Cyclones occasionally affect Vanua Levu, causing significant damage to the island's infrastructure.
Researchers studied the unique animal species found only on Vanua Levu.

Etymology

From Fijian, meaning 'big land'.
