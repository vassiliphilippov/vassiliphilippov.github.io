preternaturally

1. ADV

/ ˌpriːtərˈnætʃɚli / (AmE), / ˌpriːtənˈætʃrəli / (BrE)
In a way that is beyond what is normal or natural; extraordinarily or unusually.
in an extraordinary or unnatural way

Examples

Her strength was preternaturally great for someone her size.
He moved preternaturally fast, almost as if he was floating.
The room fell preternaturally silent as the announcement was made.

Synset Examples

she was preternaturally beautiful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supernaturally - in a supernatural or unexplainable way

Etymology

From Latin 'praeternaturalis' (beyond nature), from 'praeter' (beyond) + 'naturalis' (natural).
