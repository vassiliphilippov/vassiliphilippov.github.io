note

/ noʊt / (AmE), / nəʊt / (BrE)
a short informal written message or letter, especially one sent to a friend or acquaintance

Examples

He sent a quick note to his friend before leaving for the airport.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: letter - a written or printed message addressed to a person or organization
