adamantly

1. ADV

/ ˈæd.ə.mənt.li / (AmE), / ˈæd.ə.mənt.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is unyielding or inflexible; with firm determination.
with unwavering firmness

Examples

She adamantly refused to change her decision despite pressure from her colleagues.
The mayor adamantly opposed the proposal, citing concerns about public safety.
He adamantly denies any involvement in the incident.

Synset Examples

adamantly opposed to the marriage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmly - in a strong or definite way
  • Antonym: flexibly - in a way that is willing to change or adapt

Etymology

From 'adamant', ultimately from Latin 'adamans' (unconquerable, hard steel or diamond).
