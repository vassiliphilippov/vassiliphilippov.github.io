unthinkably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkəbli / (BrE)
To an extent that is impossible to imagine or comprehend.
to an unimaginable extent

Examples

The cost of the repairs was unthinkably high, far beyond what anyone expected.
The mountain peak rose unthinkably above the clouds, looking almost unreal.
She was unthinkably brave in the face of overwhelming danger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unimaginably - to an extent that cannot be imagined
