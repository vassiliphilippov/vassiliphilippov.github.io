aftercare

1. NOUN

/ ˈæftɚˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌkɛə / (BrE)
care and treatment given to a patient after an illness, operation, or medical procedure to aid recovery
care after illness or treatment
medical

Examples

Good aftercare is essential for patients recovering from surgery.
The hospital provides extensive aftercare to support patients through their rehabilitation.
Parents received advice on the aftercare needed following their child's dental procedure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rehabilitation - the process of restoring health or normal life through therapy after illness or injury
