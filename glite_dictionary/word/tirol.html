Tirol

1. PROPN

/ tiˈroʊl / (AmE), / tɪˈrɒl / (BrE)
a picturesque mountainous province in western Austria and northern Italy, known for its Alpine scenery and resorts
province in Austria and northern Italy

Examples

Many tourists visit Tirol to enjoy its famous ski resorts.
Her family owns a chalet in the heart of Tirol.
The traditions of Tirol are celebrated with music and dance throughout the year.

Etymology

From German Tirol, named after Castle Tirol in northern Italy.
