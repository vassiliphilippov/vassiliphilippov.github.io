Thessalian

1. NOUN

/ θɛˈseɪliən / (AmE), / θəˈseɪliən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Thessaly, a region in central Greece
native or inhabitant of Thessaly

Examples

The Thessalian cavalry was renowned for its speed and effectiveness in battle.
He traced his ancestry back to a proud Thessalian family.
Several ancient poets wrote about the customs of the Thessalians.

Etymology

From Latin 'Thessalius', from Greek 'Thessalos' (pertaining to Thessaly).
