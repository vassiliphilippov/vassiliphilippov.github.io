goldworker

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
an artisan who crafts jewelry and other objects from gold
person who makes objects from gold

Examples

The goldworker spent countless hours designing the intricate necklace.
She apprenticed with a renowned goldworker to learn the secrets of the craft.
Exquisite ornaments made by the local goldworker are displayed in the museum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goldsmith - a person who makes or repairs objects in gold
