stone-grey

1. ADJ

/ stoʊn ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
of a grey color resembling that of stone, especially slate or granite.
grey color like stone or granite

Examples

Her coat was a lovely shade of stone-grey that matched the wintry sky.
The artist chose stone-grey paint for the walls to create a calm atmosphere.
He wore a stone-grey suit that gave him a sophisticated appearance.

Synset Examples

the slaty sky of dawn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slate-grey - of a grey color resembling that of slate
