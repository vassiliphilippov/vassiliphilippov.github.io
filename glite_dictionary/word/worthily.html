worthily

1. ADV

/ ˈwɝːðɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːðɪli / (BrE)
in a way that deserves respect, recognition, or praise; with worthiness or merit
in a deserving or worthy manner

Examples

She worthily carried out her duties as chairperson of the committee.
After years of dedication, he was worthily rewarded for his service.
The award was worthily bestowed upon someone who truly made a difference.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unworthily - in a manner not deserving respect or honor

Etymology

From Middle English 'worthili', from 'worthy' + '-ly'.
