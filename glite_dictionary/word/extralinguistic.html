extralinguistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstrəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
Not relating to or contained within language; occurring outside the scope of language itself.
occurring outside language

Examples

Facial expressions are considered extralinguistic cues in communication.
Cultural context is an important extralinguistic factor in understanding meaning.
The researcher analyzed both linguistic and extralinguistic elements in the conversation.

Etymology

From Latin extra- (outside) + linguisticus (of language).
