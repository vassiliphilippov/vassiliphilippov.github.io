creedal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkriːdəl / (AmE), / ˈkriːdəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a creed; based on a system of beliefs or a formal statement of faith.
relating to a creed or system of belief

Examples

Many churches maintain a strict creedal statement for their members.
The debate centered on creedal differences between the groups.
His approach to religion was more spiritual than creedal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credal - alternative spelling meaning 'of or relating to a creed'

Etymology

From 'creed' + -al, modeled on Latin-derived adjectives.
