sissyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪsiˌɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɪsiɪʃ / (BrE)
having qualities that are considered overly or inappropriately feminine, often used in a disapproving way
having inappropriately feminine qualities
informal offensive

Examples

His friends teased him for his sissyish love of romantic movies.
The boy's sissyish behavior attracted unwanted attention at school.
She disliked the sissyish decorations chosen for the party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly

Etymology

From 'sissy' (a disparaging term for an effeminate boy) + '-ish' (forming adjectives).
