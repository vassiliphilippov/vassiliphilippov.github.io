Meclomen

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛk.loʊ.mɛn / (AmE), / ˈmiː.kləʊ.mɛn / (BrE)
A brand name for meclofenamate sodium, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat arthritis and relieve pain and inflammation.
