unsympathising

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɪmpəˌθaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪmpəˌθaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Not showing or expressing sympathy; lacking compassion or understanding for others' feelings or situations.
not showing sympathy

Examples

Her unsympathising remarks made the situation even more difficult for everyone involved.
The boss was seen as harsh and unsympathising toward employees' personal problems.
He accused his friend of being unsympathising during a time when he needed support.

Synset Examples

an uncharitable and unsympathizing attitude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsympathetic - not feeling, showing, or expressing sympathy

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'sympathising', indicating the absence of sympathy.
