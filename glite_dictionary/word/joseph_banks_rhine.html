Joseph Banks Rhine

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒoʊzəf bæŋks raɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊzɪf bæŋks raɪn / (BrE)
(1895–1980) United States parapsychologist known for pioneering experimental research into extrasensory perception (ESP) and psychokinesis.
U.S. parapsychologist, ESP pioneer

Examples

Joseph Banks Rhine conducted experiments that shaped the field of parapsychology.
Books by Joseph Banks Rhine are frequently cited in studies on extrasensory perception.
Many still debate the methods used by Joseph Banks Rhine in his ESP research.
