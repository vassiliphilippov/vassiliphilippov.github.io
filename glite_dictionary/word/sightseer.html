sightseer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪtˌsiɚ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtsiːə / (BrE)
a person who visits places of interest, especially while traveling as a tourist
person visiting sights as a tourist

Examples

Many sightseers flock to Paris each year to visit the Eiffel Tower.
We joined a group of sightseers on a guided tour of the old city.
The bus was filled with sightseers eager to explore the famous landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure

Etymology

Formed from 'sight' + 'see' + '-er' indicating one who sees sights.
