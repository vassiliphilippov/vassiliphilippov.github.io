Bizet

1. PROPN

/ biˈzeɪ / (AmE), / biˈzeɪ / (BrE)
Georges Bizet (1838–1875), a French composer best known for his operas, especially 'Carmen'.
French composer (1838–1875), known for operas

Examples

Bizet composed some of the most memorable melodies in the history of opera.
Many music critics regard Bizet's 'Carmen' as a masterpiece of the genre.
The works of Bizet remain popular with both audiences and performers today.
