trafficator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtræf.ɪ.keɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈtræf.ɪ.keɪ.tə / (BrE)
a blinking light on a motor vehicle that signals the direction in which the vehicle is about to turn; an indicator
blinking turn signal on a vehicle

Examples

The old car's trafficator no longer worked, so the driver had to use hand signals.
Vintage vehicles often have a mechanical trafficator that pops out from the side instead of using modern lights.
He restored the classic car, including installing a period-correct trafficator on each side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator - a device that signals the intended turning direction of a vehicle

Etymology

Blend of 'traffic' and 'indicator'.
