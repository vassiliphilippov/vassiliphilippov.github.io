smut

1. NOUN

/ smʌt / (AmE), / smʌt / (BrE)
an offensive or indecent word or phrase, especially one referring to sex
offensive or indecent word or phrase

Examples

Some parents were upset about the amount of smut on late-night television.
The magazine was banned for being full of smut.
He dislikes movies filled with pointless smut and crude jokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obscenity - offensive or indecent word or expression

Originally slang for 'dirt, stain'; figurative use for indecency from 17th century English.
