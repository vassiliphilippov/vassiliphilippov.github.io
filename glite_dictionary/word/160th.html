160th

1. ADJ

/ wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈsɪkstɪəθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈhʌndrəd ˈsɪkstiəθ / (BrE)
being the ordinal number of one hundred sixty in a sequence; coming after the 159th and before the 161st.
ordinal number: one hundred sixtieth

Examples

She finished in 160th place out of more than a thousand contestants.
This is the company’s 160th anniversary celebration.
The runner crossed the line to claim the 160th position in the marathon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one hundred sixtieth - the ordinal number of 160th written in words
  • Degree Lower: 159th - ordinal number just before 160th
  • Degree Higher: 161st - ordinal number just after 160th
