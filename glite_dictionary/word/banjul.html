Banjul

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæn.dʒuːl / (AmE), / ˈbæn.dʒuːl / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of The Gambia, located on an island at the mouth of the Gambia River
capital city of The Gambia

Examples

The president delivered a speech in Banjul to mark Independence Day.
After landing in Banjul, we explored its vibrant markets and colonial architecture.
The river ferry provides regular transport between the outskirts and the center of Banjul.
