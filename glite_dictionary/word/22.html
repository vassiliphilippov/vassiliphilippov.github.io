.22

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɔɪnt ˈtwɛnti tuː / (AmE), / ˌpɔɪnt ˈtwɛnti tjuː / (BrE)
A firearm, such as a pistol or rifle, that uses .22 caliber (5.6 mm) ammunition.
firearm of .22 caliber

Examples

He brought his .22 to the shooting range for target practice.
The .22 is popular among beginners due to its low recoil.
After cleaning his .22, he stored it safely in the case.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: .38 - a firearm of .38 caliber
  • Degree Higher: .45 - a firearm of .45 caliber

Etymology

Refers to the diameter of the bullet (0.22 inches).
