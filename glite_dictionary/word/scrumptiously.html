scrumptiously

1. ADV

/ ˈskrʌmpʃəsli / (AmE), / ˈskrʌmpʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely tasty or delicious; so as to give great pleasure to the taste.
in a very tasty or delicious way

Examples

The chef prepared the meal scrumptiously, adding just the right blend of herbs and spices.
Everyone enjoyed the scrumptiously baked chocolate cookies at the party.
The freshly picked strawberries tasted scrumptiously sweet and juicy.

Synset Examples

I bought some more of these deliciously sweet peaches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliciously - in a way that gives great pleasure to the senses, especially taste

Etymology

From 'scrumptious' (delicious), with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
