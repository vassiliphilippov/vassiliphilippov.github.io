Zomba

1. PROPN

/ ˈzɑːmbə / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbə / (BrE)
A city in southern Malawi, formerly the capital until 1971.
city in southern Malawi

Examples

She visited Zomba during her trip to Malawi.
Zomba is known for its scenic plateau and colonial architecture.
After 1971, the capital of Malawi moved from Zomba to Lilongwe.
