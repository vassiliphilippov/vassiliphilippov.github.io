maltwoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɔltˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who makes or sells malt, especially for brewing or distilling
female malt maker or seller
archaic

Examples

The maltwoman supplied the local brewery with her best batch of malted barley.
In the old village, every maltwoman was known for her unique recipe.
The role of a maltwoman was essential in traditional beer production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maltster - a person who makes or deals in malt

Etymology

From 'malt' (grain soaked and dried for brewing) + 'woman'.
