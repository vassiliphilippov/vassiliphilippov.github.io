Louis Henri Sullivan

1. PROPN

/ luːˈiː ˈhɛnri səˈlɪvən / (AmE), / luːˈiː ˈhɛnri sʌˈlɪvən / (BrE)
(1856–1924) United States architect known for his pioneering steel-framed skyscrapers and for coining the phrase 'form follows function.'
U.S. architect, coined 'form follows function'

Examples

The designs of Louis Henri Sullivan greatly influenced modern American architecture.
Louis Henri Sullivan is often called the 'father of skyscrapers' due to his innovative building techniques.
The phrase 'form follows function' is famously attributed to Louis Henri Sullivan.
