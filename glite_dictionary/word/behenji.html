behenji

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.hen.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.hen.dʒi / (BrE)
(informal, Indian English) A woman regarded as traditional, conservative, or socially old-fashioned.
traditional or conservative woman (informal, Indian English)
informal

Examples

Even though she is young, the behenji in her prefers modest clothing and traditional values.
It's hard to ignore the behenji side of me when I visit my family.
Despite her modern job, people still see her as a behenji because of her conservative outlook.

Synset Examples

The behenji in her was evident as she helped others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auntie - A woman regarded as old-fashioned or conservative, especially in manner and dress (British/Indian English informal)
  • Antonym: modern woman - A woman embracing contemporary or progressive ideas and styles

Etymology

From Hindi 'behnjī', meaning 'elder sister' (behn 'sister' + -ji honorific).
