to outrival

1. VERB

/ aʊtˈraɪ.vəl / (AmE), / aʊtˈraɪ.vəl / (BrE)
to surpass or exceed another in rivalry or competition; to be more successful than a rival
to surpass a rival

Examples

The younger athlete eventually outrivaled his experienced competitors to win the championship.
Despite fierce competition, her beauty outrivaled that of all her peers at the festival.
The new technology quickly outrivaled existing products on the market.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to outdo - to do better than someone else
  • Synonym: to surpass - to be greater or better than someone or something

Etymology

From 'out-' (beyond) + 'rival' (Latin rivalis, 'competitor').
