plumate

1. ADJ

/ ˈpluː.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈpluː.meɪt / (BrE)
having an ornamental plume or feathery tuft; resembling or decorated with feathers.
having a feathery plume or tuft

Examples

The ancient helmet was decorated with a plumate crest that caught the sunlight.
Several birds displayed their plumate tails during the mating ritual.
The artist painted a plumate headdress inspired by tropical birds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plumed - having a feather or feathers

Etymology

From Latin 'plumatus', from 'pluma' meaning feather.
