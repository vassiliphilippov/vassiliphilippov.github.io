Lashkar-e-Tayyiba

1. PROPN

/ ˌlæʃkər eɪ taɪˈjiːbə / (AmE), / ˌlæʃkɑː eɪ taɪˈjiːbə / (BrE)
an Islamist militant organization based in Pakistan, known for carrying out terrorist attacks, chiefly in the Indian region of Kashmir
militant Islamist organization (South Asia)

Examples

The Indian government has accused Lashkar-e-Tayyiba of being responsible for several deadly attacks in Kashmir.
International observers have condemned Lashkar-e-Tayyiba for targeting civilians in its operations.
The suspected links between Lashkar-e-Tayyiba and other militant groups are under investigation.

Synset Examples

Lashkar-e-Toiba has committed mass murders of civilian Hindus

Etymology

From Urdu لشکر طیبہ (lashkar-e-tayyiba), meaning 'army of the good' or 'army of the pure'.
