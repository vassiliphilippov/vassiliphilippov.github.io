lisper

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪspər / (AmE), / ˈlɪspə / (BrE)
a person who speaks with a lisp
person who speaks with a lisp

Examples

He has become a confident lisper after years of speech therapy.
The child was a shy lisper, but her friends never teased her.
A professional lisper might seek guidance from a speech therapist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lisp - a speech defect involving pronouncing s and z as th
