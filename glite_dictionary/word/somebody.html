somebody

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈsʌmˌbɒdi / (BrE)
a person whose identity is unknown or unspecified
an unspecified or unknown person

Examples

Can somebody please help me with this box?
I heard somebody knocking at the door late last night.
There is somebody waiting for you in the lobby.

Synset Examples

there was too much for one person to do

Related Terms

  • Synonym: someone - an unspecified or unknown person
  • Synonym: anybody - any person, used in questions or negatives
  • Antonym: nobody - no person; not anyone
