determining

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈtɝːmɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
having the power or quality of deciding something; decisive
decisive; having deciding power

Examples

Her speech was the determining factor in our final decision.
The weather may be a determining influence on the outcome of the match.
He cast the determining vote that settled the debate.

Synset Examples

cast the deciding vote
the determinative (or determinant) battle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decisive - having the ability to decide; determining
  • Antonym: indecisive - not having the ability to decide

Etymology

Present participle of 'determine,' from Latin 'determinare' (to limit, to decide).
