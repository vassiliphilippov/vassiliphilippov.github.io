Eurafrican

1. NOUN

/ jʊrˈæfrɪkən / (AmE), / jʊəˈræfrɪkən / (BrE)
a person of mixed European and African descent
person of mixed European and African heritage

Examples

Many Eurafricans played significant roles in the cultural life of the region.
He identifies as a Eurafrican due to his parents' diverse backgrounds.
The documentary focused on the experiences of Eurafricans living in the diaspora.

Etymology

Formed from 'Europe' + 'African'.

2. ADJ

/ jʊrˈæfrɪkən / (AmE), / jʊəˈræfrɪkən / (BrE)
Relating to or coming from both Europe and Africa.
from or relating to Europe and Africa

Examples

The Eurafrican trade route played a crucial role in the exchange of goods and cultures.
She studied Eurafrican history at university, focusing on the colonial period.
A Eurafrican summit was held to discuss environmental challenges across both continents.

Etymology

Formed from 'Europe' + 'African', indicating a connection to both continents.
