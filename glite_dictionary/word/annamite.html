Annamite

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈanəmaɪt / (BrE)
an old term for the Vietnamese language, or a Mon-Khmer language formerly spoken in parts of Vietnam
old term for the Vietnamese language

Examples

The ancient text was written in Annamite, making it difficult for modern readers to understand.
During the colonial period, French scholars studied the Annamite language extensively.
He is fluent in both Annamite and modern Vietnamese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vietnamese - the official language of Vietnam

Etymology

From French 'Annamite', from Annam (a region in Vietnam).
