northbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔrθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of vehicles, travelers, etc.) moving or heading toward the north.
moving or heading toward the north

Examples

The northbound train departs at 8 a.m. each morning.
Heavy traffic was reported in the northbound lanes after the football game.
We stopped at a rest area while traveling northbound on the highway.

Synset Examples

the northbound lane
the northward flow of traffic

Related Terms

  • Antonym: southbound - moving or heading toward the south
