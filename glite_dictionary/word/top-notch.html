top-notch

1. ADJ

/ tɑp nɑtʃ / (AmE), / tɒp nɒtʃ / (BrE)
Of the highest quality; excellent.
of the highest quality

Examples

Her presentation was top-notch and impressed everyone at the conference.
The food at this restaurant is always top-notch, no matter what you order.
They hired a top-notch designer to renovate their apartment.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the highest quality
  • Synonym: superb - excellent; of very high quality
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

Originally American English (late 19th century); 'notch' refers to a point or level on a scale.
