refiner

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈfaɪnər / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnə / (BrE)
a person or company whose job is to purify or improve a substance such as sugar, oil, or metals by removing unwanted elements
person who purifies substances

Examples

The refiner inspected the raw oil before starting the purification process.
As a refiner of precious metals, she takes great pride in her work.
The city grew rapidly after a sugar refiner set up his business there.

Synset Examples

he was a sugar refiner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purifier - a person or device that removes impurities

Etymology

From refine + -er, meaning one who refines.
