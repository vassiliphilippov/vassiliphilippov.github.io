23rd

1. ADJ

/ ˈtwɛn.ti ˈθɜrd / (AmE), / ˈtwɛn.ti ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
coming after the twenty-second in a sequence; ordinal form of twenty-three
twenty-third in order

Examples

His birthday is on the 23rd of July every year.
She finished in 23rd place in the marathon.
This is the 23rd time the event has been held.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twenty-third - another way of spelling 23rd; third after the twentieth
  • Degree Higher: 24th - ordinal number after 23rd
  • Degree Lower: 22nd - ordinal number before 23rd
