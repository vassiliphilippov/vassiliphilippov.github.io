Weisshorn

1. PROPN

/ ˈvaɪsˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈvaɪs.hɔːn / (BrE)
a mountain in the Alps of Switzerland, 4,506 meters (14,804 feet) high, known for its pyramidal shape
mountain in the Swiss Alps

Examples

The Weisshorn is often considered one of the most beautiful peaks in the Swiss Alps.
Climbers require considerable skill to ascend the challenging ridges of the Weisshorn.
Many photographers are drawn to the Weisshorn for its striking triangular silhouette.

Etymology

From German 'Weiss' (white) + 'Horn' (peak, horn), referring to its snow-covered appearance.
