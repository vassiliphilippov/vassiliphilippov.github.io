bitch

1. NOUN

/ bɪtʃ / (AmE), / bɪtʃ / (BrE)
A female dog, or the female of any member of the dog family.
female dog or dog family member

Examples

The bitch cared for her puppies attentively after they were born.
At the dog show, the judge awarded first prize to the bitch in the working group.
A bitch in heat may attract several males during the mating season.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog - domesticated canine, usually male or generic

Etymology

From Old English *bicce* (female dog).
