square-tailed

1. ADJ

/ skwɛr teɪld / (AmE), / skwɛə teɪld / (BrE)
Having a tail that is square or squared-off at the end.
having a square-shaped tail

Examples

The square-tailed kite is easily identifiable by its unique tail shape.
Many butterflies in this region are square-tailed, distinguishing them from other species.
Researchers observed a square-tailed specimen during the morning survey.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fork-tailed - having a tail that is forked or split at the end
