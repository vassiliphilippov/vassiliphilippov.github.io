multistage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌltiˌsteɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌsteɪdʒ / (BrE)
Consisting of or occurring in more than one stage or phase.
having multiple stages or phases

Examples

The engineer chose a multistage compressor for greater efficiency.
This water filtration system is a multistage process to ensure purity.
They developed a multistage rocket to reach higher altitudes.

Etymology

From multi- (many) + stage.
