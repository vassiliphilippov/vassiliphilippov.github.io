attitudinal

1. ADJ

/ ˌæt̬ɪˈtuːdɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌætɪˈtjuːdɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on attitudes, especially personal views or feelings.
relating to attitudes or opinions

Examples

The survey was designed to measure attitudinal differences between the two groups.
Changes in company culture often require an attitudinal shift among employees.
The research focused on the attitudinal factors influencing consumer choices.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: behavioral - relating to behavior

Etymology

From attitude + -inal, modeled after the pattern of 'conditional', 'occasional', etc.
