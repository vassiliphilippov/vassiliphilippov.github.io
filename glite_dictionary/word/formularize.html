to formularize

1. VERB

/ ˈfɔrmjələrˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmjələraɪz / (BrE)
to express or represent something in the form of a formula
express as a formula

Examples

Scientists often formularize complex processes to make them more understandable.
Can you formularize the relationship between these two variables?
She formularized her observations into a single mathematical equation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to formulate - to express in a precise form; to devise methodically

Etymology

From 'formula' + '-ize'.
