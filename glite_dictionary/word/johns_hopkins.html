Johns Hopkins

1. PROPN

/ ˌdʒɑnz ˈhɑpkɪnz / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒnz ˈhɒpkɪnz / (BrE)
a private research university located in Baltimore, Maryland, known for its programs in medicine, science, and engineering
US university in Baltimore, Maryland

Examples

She completed her undergraduate studies at Johns Hopkins before moving on to graduate school.
The medical school at Johns Hopkins is highly regarded worldwide.
Many groundbreaking discoveries have been made at Johns Hopkins over the years.

Etymology

Named after its benefactor Johns Hopkins (1795–1873), an American entrepreneur and philanthropist.
