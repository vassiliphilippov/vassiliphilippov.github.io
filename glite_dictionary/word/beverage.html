beverage

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɛv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a drink, especially one other than water
a drink (usually not water)

Examples

We offer a wide selection of beverages including coffee, tea, and juice.
Please choose a beverage to go with your meal.
Alcoholic beverages are not served to anyone under the age of 21.

Synset Examples

may I take your beverage order?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drink - any liquid that can be swallowed as refreshment

Etymology

From Old French *bevrage*, from *beivre* (to drink), from Latin *bibere* (to drink).
