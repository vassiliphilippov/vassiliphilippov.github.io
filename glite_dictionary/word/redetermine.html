to redetermine

1. VERB

/ ˌriː.dɪˈtɝː.mɪn / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈtɜː.mɪn / (BrE)
to fix, find, or establish (something) again, often after a reassessment or review
determine or establish again

Examples

After new evidence emerged, the committee decided to redetermine the winner of the science fair.
The court may redetermine the amount of damages in light of the appeal.
Scientists had to redetermine the measurements after noticing a calibration error.

Synset Examples

the physicists redetermined Planck's constant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to determine - to establish or decide something after consideration

Etymology

Formed from re- (again) + determine, from Latin determinare (to set bounds, limit).
