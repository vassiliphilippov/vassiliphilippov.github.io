Br

1. NOUN

/ biː ɑːr / (AmE), / biː ɑː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for bromine, a nonmetallic, volatile, dark brown liquid element of the halogen group, commonly found in seawater
chemical symbol for bromine element

Examples

The chemical element Br is rarely found in its free form in nature.
When conducting experiments, always handle Br with care due to its corrosive properties.
Seawater contains trace amounts of Br, which can be extracted for industrial use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bromine - a nonmetallic halogen element, chemical symbol Br

Etymology

Abbreviation of bromine, from Greek 'brōmos' (stench).
