westernmost

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛstɚnˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənˌməʊst / (BrE)
Farthest toward the west; located at the greatest distance west compared to other points in a group.
farthest to the west

Examples

Iceland is the westernmost country in Europe.
The westernmost tip of the continent offers spectacular sunsets over the ocean.
This town is the westernmost settlement accessible by road in the region.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easternmost - farthest to the east
  • Degree Lower: northernmost - farthest to the north
  • Degree Lower: southernmost - farthest to the south

Etymology

Formed from 'western' + 'most', meaning 'most to the west'.
