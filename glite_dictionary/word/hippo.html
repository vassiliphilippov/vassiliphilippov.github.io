hippo

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɪpəʊ / (BrE)
a large, heavy plant-eating mammal with thick skin, living mainly in or near rivers in Africa; short for hippopotamus.
large African river mammal

Examples

We saw a hippo basking on the riverbank during our safari.
The hippo spends most of its day submerged in water to stay cool.
Children loved learning about the hippo at the zoo's African exhibit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hippopotamus - large African plant-eating mammal with thick skin

Etymology

Shortened form of 'hippopotamus', from Greek 'hippos' (horse) + 'potamos' (river).
