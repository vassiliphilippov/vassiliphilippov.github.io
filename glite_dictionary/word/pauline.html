Pauline

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɔːˌliːn / (AmE), / ˈpɔːliːn / (BrE)
Relating to Paul the Apostle or his doctrines or writings.
relating to Paul the Apostle or his teachings

Examples

Early Christian writings often distinguish between Pauline and non-Pauline texts.
The Pauline epistles are an important part of the New Testament.
Her research focuses on Pauline theology and its influence on Western thought.

Etymology

From Latin Paulus, the name of Paul the Apostle.
