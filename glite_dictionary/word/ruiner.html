ruiner

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːɪnər / (AmE), / ˈruːɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that ruins, destroys, or brings to ruin
person or thing that ruins or destroys

Examples

She was considered the ruiner of the family's reputation after the scandal.
Over time, neglect became the silent ruiner of the historic building.
His reckless actions made him the ruiner of the team's chances for victory.

Synset Examples

a destroyer of the environment
jealousy was his undoer
uprooters of gravestones

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destroyer - a person or thing that destroys
  • Synonym: undoer - a person who undoes or brings to ruin
