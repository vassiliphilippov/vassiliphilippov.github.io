unwrapped

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈræpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈræpt / (BrE)
not wrapped or having had its wrapping removed.
not wrapped or with wrapping removed

Examples

The children hurried to play with their unwrapped toys on Christmas morning.
Please leave the unwrapped gifts on the table for inspection.
He was surprised to find the pastry already unwrapped and ready to eat.

Synset Examples

she faced a mountainous pile of presents still unwrapped
the floor around the tree was littered with gifts already unwrapped

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wrapped - covered or enclosed in wrapping material

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (meaning 'not' or 'opposite of') to 'wrapped'.
