advertence

1. NOUN

/ ædˈvɝːtəns / (AmE), / ədˈvɜːtəns / (BrE)
the act of paying attention; heedfulness or the process of being attentive to something
the act of paying attention

Examples

Her advertence during the lecture impressed the professor.
Driving safely requires constant advertence to the road and surroundings.
The law distinguishes between actions done with advertence and those done inadvertently.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inattention - lack of attention or heedfulness
  • Synonym: heed - careful attention

Etymology

From Latin 'advertere' (to turn toward).
