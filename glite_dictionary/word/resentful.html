resentful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈzɛntfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɛntfəl / (BrE)
Feeling or showing bitterness or indignation at having been treated unfairly.
feeling bitter due to unfairness

Examples

She still feels resentful about being passed over for the promotion.
His resentful tone made it clear he was not happy with the decision.
Despite his apology, she remained resentful for weeks.

Synset Examples

resentful at the way he was treated
a sullen resentful attitude

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grateful - feeling or showing appreciation for something received

Etymology

From resent + -ful, based on Latin *resentīre* (to feel again or deeply).
