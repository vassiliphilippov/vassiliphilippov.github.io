ammobium

1. NOUN

/ əˈmoʊbiəm / (AmE), / əˈməʊbɪəm / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Ammobium, typically having yellow flowers and silvery foliage, native to Australia.
plant genus with yellow flowers and silvery leaves

Examples

The ammobium in the garden bloomed with clusters of bright yellow flowers.
Botanists often study ammobium for its drought resistance and distinctive foliage.
She added an ammobium plant to her collection of Australian natives.

Etymology

From Greek 'ammos' (sand) + 'bios' (life), referring to its habitat.
