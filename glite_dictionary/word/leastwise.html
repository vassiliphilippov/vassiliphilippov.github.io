leastwise

1. ADV

/ ˈlistˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈliːstwaɪz / (BrE)
used to indicate the least that can be said or done; at least; anyway.
at least; anyway (informal)
informal

Examples

She didn't win the race, but leastwise she finished it.
I can't come tomorrow, or leastwise, I don't think I can.
We should leave now, leastwise if we want to get there before dark.

Synset Examples

at least he survived
they felt — at any rate Jim felt — relieved though still wary
the influence of economists — or at any rate of economics — is far-reaching

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at least - used to indicate the minimum, or to introduce a correction or concession
  • Synonym: anyway - in any case; regardless
  • Synonym: leastways - colloquial variant of 'leastwise', especially in dialectal English
