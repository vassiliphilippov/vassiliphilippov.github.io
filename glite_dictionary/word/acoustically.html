acoustically

1. ADV

/ əˈkuːstɪkli / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to sound or the science of acoustics
relating to sound or acoustics

Examples

The concert hall was designed acoustically to enhance every note of the orchestra.
This foam panel improves the room’s acoustically responsive qualities.
Certain materials behave very differently acoustically when compared to others.

Synset Examples

acoustically ill-equipped studios

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the sense of hearing

Etymology

From acoustic + -ly; 'acoustic' from Greek akoustikos (relating to hearing).
