Trinidadian

1. ADJ

/ ˌtrɪnɪˈdædiən / (AmE), / ˌtrɪnɪˈdædɪən / (BrE)
relating to Trinidad or its people, culture, or products
from or relating to Trinidad

Examples

The company specializes in importing Trinidadian cocoa beans.
We enjoyed a delicious meal of Trinidadian cuisine at the festival.
She wore a dress inspired by traditional Trinidadian patterns.

Synset Examples

Trinidadian rum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Trinidadian - a native or inhabitant of Trinidad

Etymology

From Trinidad, the name of the island, with the English adjectival suffix -ian.

2. NOUN

/ ˌtrɪnɪˈdædiən / (AmE), / ˌtrɪnɪˈdædɪən / (BrE)
a person who is an inhabitant or native of Trinidad
inhabitant or native of Trinidad

Examples

Many Trinidadians celebrate Carnival with vibrant costumes and music.
The famous cricketer Brian Lara is a well-known Trinidadian.
As a Trinidadian, she often cooks spicy dishes from her homeland.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tobagonian - inhabitant or native of Tobago

Etymology

From Trinidad, the island in the Caribbean, plus English suffix -ian
