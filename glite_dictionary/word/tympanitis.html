tympanitis

1. NOUN

/ tɪm.pəˈnaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / tɪm.pəˈnaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the tympanic membrane or middle ear.
inflammation of the middle ear
medical

Examples

The patient was diagnosed with tympanitis after complaining of severe ear pain and hearing loss.
Antibiotics are often prescribed for children suffering from tympanitis.
If untreated, tympanitis can lead to complications such as persistent hearing problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: otitis media - inflammation of the middle ear

Etymology

From Greek tympanon (drum) + -itis (inflammation).
