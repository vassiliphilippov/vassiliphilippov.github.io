bewhiskered

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈwɪskərd / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪskəd / (BrE)
having whiskers or a beard; with hair on the cheeks and chin
having whiskers or facial hair

Examples

The bewhiskered old man smiled warmly as he told his story.
A bewhiskered cat sat purring on the windowsill.
He portrayed a bewhiskered character in the play, wearing a fake beard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard; facial hair on the jaw and chin

Etymology

From be- (forming adjectives meaning ‘provided with’) + whiskered (having whiskers).
