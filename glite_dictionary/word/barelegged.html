barelegged

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛrˌlɛɡd / (AmE), / ˈbeəˌlɛɡd / (BrE)
(of a person) having the legs uncovered by clothing; not wearing anything on the legs.
with legs uncovered by clothing

Examples

On hot summer days, many children run around barelegged in the garden.
She attended the outdoor wedding barelegged, despite the cool breeze.
The hikers crossed the stream barelegged to avoid getting their pants wet.

Synset Examples

barelegged children on the beach

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barefoot - having the feet uncovered by shoes or socks

Etymology

From 'bare' (uncovered) + 'legged' (having legs).
