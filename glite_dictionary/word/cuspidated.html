cuspidated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkʌspɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌspɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having cusps or pointed projections; ending in or characterized by sharp points.
having points or cusps

Examples

The leaf margins are cuspidated, giving them a jagged appearance.
Some animal teeth are highly cuspidated for tearing flesh.
The sculpture featured cuspidated edges extending from its crown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapering end
  • Antonym: blunt - not having a sharp edge or point

Etymology

From Latin 'cuspidatus', past participle of 'cuspidare' (to make pointed), from 'cuspis' (point).
