New Hampshirite

1. NOUN

/ ˌnuː ˈhæmpʃərˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˌnjuː ˈhæmpʃəˌaɪt / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the U.S. state of New Hampshire
person from New Hampshire

Examples

As a proud New Hampshirite, she enjoys hiking in the White Mountains every summer.
Many New Hampshirites cherish the tradition of town meetings.
He moved to Vermont but never stopped calling himself a New Hampshirite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Yorker - a native or resident of New York
