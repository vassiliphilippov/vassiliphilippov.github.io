Syria

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪriə / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriə / (BrE)
A country in southwestern Asia, located in the Middle East at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea.
country in the Middle East

Examples

Syria has a rich and complex history dating back thousands of years.
Many refugees have fled Syria due to ongoing conflict in the region.
Syria borders countries such as Turkey, Iraq, Jordan, and Lebanon.

Etymology

From Latin Syriā, from Ancient Greek Συρία (Suría).
