dreamland

1. NOUN

/ ˈdriːmlænd / (AmE), / ˈdriːmlænd / (BrE)
an imaginary and delightful place that exists only in dreams or in the imagination
imaginary pleasing place in dreams

Examples

As a child, I often escaped to dreamland to forget my worries.
The book transports readers to a magical dreamland filled with wonder.
Sometimes, sleep takes you to a dreamland far from everyday concerns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy - an imagined place or situation that is not real
  • Antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist

Etymology

From 'dream' + 'land', first recorded in English in the 19th century.
