spiral-shelled

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪrəl ˌʃɛld / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl ˌʃɛld / (BrE)
having a shell that forms a spiral shape.
having a spiral-shaped shell

Examples

The spiral-shelled snail moved slowly across the wet leaf.
Many ancient fossils are spiral-shelled and resemble modern-day nautiluses.
Children collected spiral-shelled seashells along the shoreline.
