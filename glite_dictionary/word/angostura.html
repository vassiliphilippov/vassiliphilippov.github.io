angostura

1. NOUN

/ ˌæŋɡəˈstʊrə / (AmE), / ˌaŋɡəˈstjʊərə / (BrE)
the bitter bark of a South American tree, used as a flavoring and in medicines and liqueurs
bitter bark used for flavoring and medicine

Examples

A dash of angostura gives cocktails a complex, aromatic flavor.
Some traditional medicines include angostura as a key ingredient for its bitter properties.
He learned that angostura bark was once used to treat fevers and stomach ailments.

Etymology

From the name of Angostura, a town in Venezuela, near where the bark was first found.
