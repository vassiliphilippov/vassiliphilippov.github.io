conelike

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊnˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or characteristics of a cone; resembling a cone.
resembling a cone in shape

Examples

The mountain had a distinct conelike shape visible from miles away.
Several pine trees produce conelike fruit that contain their seeds.
The architect designed a conelike roof to allow snow to slide off easily.

Synset Examples

conical mountains
conelike fruit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conical - having the shape of a cone
