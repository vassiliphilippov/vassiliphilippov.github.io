Faisal of Saudi Arabia

1. PROPN

/ faɪˈsɑl əv ˌsaʊdi əˈreɪbiə / (AmE), / faɪˈsɑːl əv ˌsaʊdi əˈreɪbiə / (BrE)
(1906–1975) King of Saudi Arabia from 1964 to 1975, known for modernizing the country and overseeing significant economic and social reforms.
King of Saudi Arabia, 1964–1975
american british

Examples

Faisal of Saudi Arabia implemented major reforms that transformed the country's economy during his reign.
International relations improved between the West and the Middle East under Faisal of Saudi Arabia.
Many Saudis remember Faisal of Saudi Arabia for his commitment to modernization and religious leadership.
