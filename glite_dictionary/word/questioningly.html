questioningly

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a manner that shows doubt, curiosity, or the desire to ask questions
in a curious or questioning way

Examples

She looked at him questioningly, waiting for an explanation.
The dog tilted its head questioningly at the unfamiliar sound.
He raised his eyebrows questioningly when he saw the mistake.

Synset Examples

they turned their heads questioningly, as if awaiting further instructions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: questioning - showing curiosity or a desire to ask questions
Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-00359488-r
Synset POS: r

2. ADV

/ ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner showing doubt, curiosity, or a desire for more information; in a questioning way
in a questioning or curious manner

Examples

She glanced questioningly at her friend, unsure of what to do next.
He raised an eyebrow questioningly, waiting for an explanation.
The child looked questioningly at his parents when they mentioned a surprise.

Synset Examples

they looked quizzically at the doctor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quizzically - in a manner showing puzzlement or curiosity
