saclike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsækˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsakˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or qualities of a sac; shaped like a pouch or bag
shaped like a pouch or bag

Examples

The fungus grows in saclike structures attached to tree bark.
Some animals have a saclike stomach, allowing them to digest cellulose.
Her backpack was adorned with a saclike pocket on the side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pouchlike - resembling or shaped like a pouch
