enceinte

1. ADJ

/ ɛnˈseɪnt / (AmE), / ɒ̃ˈsɛ̃t / (BrE)
(of a woman) pregnant, especially in an advanced stage.
pregnant (advanced stage)

Examples

She was visibly enceinte, her due date only weeks away.
The portrait depicts the queen enceinte with her first child.
In the village, news spread quickly that Maria was enceinte.

Synset Examples

was big with child
was great with child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pregnant - having a child developing in the body

Etymology

From French, from Latin *incincta*, feminine past participle of *incingere* (to gird or surround).
