anoestrum

1. NOUN

/ ˌænoʊˈstrʌm / (AmE), / ˌanɪˈɒstrəm / (BrE)
a period of sexual inactivity between two periods of estrus in nonhuman mammals
sexual inactivity period in mammals
medical

Examples

Many wild animals enter anoestrum during the non-breeding season.
The vet explained that the cow was in anoestrum and not ready to breed.
Hormone levels drop and reproduction pauses throughout anoestrum in these mammals.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: estrus - period of sexual receptivity and fertility in female mammals

Etymology

From Greek an- (not) + oestrus (gadfly, frenzy, estrus).
