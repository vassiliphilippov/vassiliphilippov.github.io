unpolitical

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.pəˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ʌn.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Not interested in or connected with politics; politically neutral.
politically neutral; not interested in politics

Examples

She remained unpolitical throughout her career, refusing to support any party.
Their organization is strictly unpolitical, focusing only on community service.
I prefer to keep my social media unpolitical to avoid controversy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apolitical - Not interested in or connected with politics

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'political'.
