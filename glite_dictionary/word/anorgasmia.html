anorgasmia

1. NOUN

/ ˌænɔrˈɡæzmiə / (AmE), / ˌanɔːˈɡazmiə / (BrE)
the inability to achieve orgasm during sexual activity
inability to achieve orgasm
medical

Examples

Some medications have been linked to anorgasmia as a side effect in both men and women.
Therapists may address anorgasmia through counseling and behavioral interventions.
Chronic anorgasmia can significantly impact a person's relationships and quality of life.

Etymology

From Greek prefix an- (without) + orgasm + -ia (condition).
