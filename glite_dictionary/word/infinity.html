infinity

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfɪnədi / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪnɪti / (BrE)
an endless or unlimited period of time; time without limit or end
time without end; endless duration

Examples

The concept of infinity fascinates philosophers and mathematicians alike.
Many believe that the universe will exist for infinity.
In poetry, love is sometimes described as lasting for infinity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternity - endless or infinite time; timelessness

Etymology

From Latin infinitas (boundlessness), from infinitus (unlimited).
