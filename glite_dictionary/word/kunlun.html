Kunlun

1. PROPN

/ kuːnˈluːn / (AmE), / kʊnˈlʊn / (BrE)
a major mountain range in western China, extending eastward from the Indian border for about 1,000 miles (1,600 kilometers)
mountain range in western China

Examples

The Kunlun Mountains are known for their striking beauty and remote location.
Many rivers in China originate from the glaciers of the Kunlun range.
The expedition spent weeks exploring the rugged terrain of the Kunlun Mountains.

Etymology

From Chinese Kunlun (崑崙/昆仑), a name with ancient mythological associations in Chinese culture.
