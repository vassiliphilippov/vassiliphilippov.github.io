unsaponified

1. ADJ

/ ən.səˈpɑː.nəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌn.səˈpɒ.nɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially a fat or oil) not converted into soap by the process of saponification.
not converted into soap

Examples

The analysis showed a significant amount of unsaponified fat in the sample.
Products with unsaponified oils can retain more of their natural properties.
Chemists tested the unsaponified residue for further impurities.

Synset Examples

unsaponified fat

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saponified - having been converted into soap by saponification

Etymology

From un- (not) + saponified (from Latin 'sapo' meaning 'soap').
