oviform

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊ.vɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of an egg; egg-shaped.
egg-shaped; resembling an egg

Examples

The architect designed an oviform dome that stood out against the city skyline.
Many seeds have an oviform appearance, making them easily distinguishable.
The sculpture was oviform, gently rounded at the top and narrower at the base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oval - having a rounded and slightly elongated shape, like an egg
  • Antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

From Latin 'ovum' (egg) + '-form' (having the form of).
