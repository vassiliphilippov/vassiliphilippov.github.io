wolfhound

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊlfˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
a very large breed of dog, originally bred for hunting wolves
a large dog breed for hunting wolves

Examples

The wolfhound stood head and shoulders above the other dogs in the park.
Traditionally, a wolfhound was valued for its strength and hunting prowess.
She adopted an Irish wolfhound that quickly became the family favorite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Irish wolfhound - a specific breed of wolfhound originally from Ireland
  • Synonym: hound - a dog bred for hunting by sight or scent

Etymology

From 'wolf' + 'hound', referring to a dog that hunts wolves.
