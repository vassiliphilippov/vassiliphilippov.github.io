knee-length

1. ADJ

/ ˈniˌlɛŋkθ / (AmE), / ˈniː.lɛŋθ / (BrE)
(of a garment or object) extending to or reaching the knee in length.
extending to the knee

Examples

She wore a knee-length skirt to the office.
The designer introduced a collection of knee-length coats for the fall season.
A knee-length dress is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: ankle-length - extending to the ankle
  • Degree Lower: mini - (in fashion) very short in length, especially above the knee
