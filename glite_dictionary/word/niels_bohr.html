Niels Bohr

1. PROPN

/ ˈniːlz ˈboʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈniːlz ˈbɔː / (BrE)
(1885–1962) A Danish physicist known for his foundational work on atomic structure and quantum theory, notably developing the Bohr model of the atom.
Danish physicist, atomic structure pioneer

Examples

Niels Bohr received the Nobel Prize in Physics for his work on the structure of atoms.
Many students study the Niels Bohr model when learning about atomic structure.
The theories of Niels Bohr revolutionized our understanding of quantum mechanics.
