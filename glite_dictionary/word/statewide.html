statewide

1. ADJ

/ steɪtˈwaɪd / (AmE), / steɪtˈwaɪd / (BrE)
happening or existing throughout all parts of a state; involving an entire state
covering an entire state

Examples

The new law brought changes to education statewide.
Their campaign is gaining support statewide as the election approaches.
The governor announced a statewide emergency due to the hurricane.

Synset Examples

the statewide recycling program

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: nationwide - happening or existing throughout an entire nation

Etymology

Formed from 'state' + 'wide', following the pattern of other adjectives like 'nationwide'.
