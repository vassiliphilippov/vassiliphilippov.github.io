pct

1. NOUN

/ piː siː tiː / (AmE), / piː siː tiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for percent; used to denote a proportion out of one hundred
abbreviation for percent (%)

Examples

The company's revenue grew by 12 pct last year.
Interest rates dropped 0.5 pct compared to the previous month.
Only 6 pct of applicants were accepted into the program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - one part in every hundred; %
