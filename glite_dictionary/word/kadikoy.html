Kadikoy

1. PROPN

/ ˌkɑːdiˈkɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌkædɪˈkɔɪ / (BrE)
A district in Istanbul, Turkey, formerly a separate town on the Bosporus and historically known as the site of the Council of Chalcedon.
district in Istanbul, Turkey

Examples

Many visitors enjoy the vibrant markets and restaurants in Kadikoy when they travel to Istanbul.
The Council of Chalcedon was held in what is today known as Kadikoy.
She rented an apartment in Kadikoy because of its lively atmosphere and rich history.
