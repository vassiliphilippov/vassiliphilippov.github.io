restfully

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛst.fəli / (AmE), / ˈrɛst.fəli / (BrE)
In a manner that is peaceful, calm, and conducive to rest.
in a peaceful and restful way

Examples

After a long hike, she slept restfully through the night.
The cat lay restfully in a patch of sunlight on the sofa.
He gazed restfully at the quiet scenery from his window.

Synset Examples

the streets are restfully sunny and still for the town is at mass

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peacefully - in a calm and peaceful manner
  • Antonym: restlessly - in a manner lacking rest or peace

Etymology

From 'restful' + '-ly', forming an adverb from the adjective.
