Woodrow Charles Herman

1. PROPN

/ ˈwʊd.roʊ tʃɑrlz ˈhɜr.mən / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.rəʊ tʃɑːlz ˈhɜː.mən / (BrE)
(1913–1987) United States jazz musician and bandleader, known for leading one of the most influential big bands in jazz history.
American jazz musician and bandleader

Examples

Woodrow Charles Herman was celebrated for his innovative approach to jazz and his leadership of The Herd big band.
The influence of Woodrow Charles Herman can still be recognized in modern jazz orchestras.
Many musicians started their careers under the direction of Woodrow Charles Herman.
