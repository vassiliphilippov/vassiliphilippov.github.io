vandyke

1. NOUN

/ vænˈdaɪk / (AmE), / vanˈdaɪk / (BrE)
a short, pointed beard, especially one with a separate mustache, named after the artist Anthony van Dyck
pointed beard style

Examples

He decided to grow a vandyke to give himself a more artistic look.
The actor's distinctive vandyke became his trademark in several films.
Portraits from the 17th century often depict men sporting a vandyke.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goatee - a small, pointed beard on the chin

Etymology

Named after the Flemish painter Anthony van Dyck (1599–1641), famous for painting men with this beard style.
