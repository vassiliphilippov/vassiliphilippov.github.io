assiduousness

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɪdʒuəsnəs / (AmE), / əˈsɪdjʊəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing great care, attention, and persistent effort in completing tasks
persistent diligence and attention

Examples

Her assiduousness in documenting every detail impressed her supervisor.
Due to his assiduousness, the project was completed ahead of schedule.
Academic success often requires patience, persistence, and assiduousness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort
  • Synonym: industriousness - habitual hard work and effort
  • Antonym: laziness - unwillingness to work or use energy

Etymology

From Latin assiduus (persistent) + -ness.
