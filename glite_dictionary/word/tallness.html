tallness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔlnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being tall, especially above average in height
the quality of being tall

Examples

Her tallness made her easy to spot in a crowd.
He was often self-conscious about his tallness during high school.
The tallness of the building impressed every visitor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: height - the measurement of how tall someone or something is
  • Antonym: shortness - the quality of being short in stature

Etymology

From tall + -ness, a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɔlnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being tall; the extent of vertical height from base to top.
quality of being tall

Examples

Her remarkable tallness made her stand out in a crowd.
The tallness of the skyscraper impressed all the visitors.
He was self-conscious about his tallness in school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: height - the measurement of how tall someone or something is
  • Antonym: shortness - the quality or state of being short; lack of height

Etymology

Formed from 'tall' + '-ness', a suffix denoting a state or condition.
