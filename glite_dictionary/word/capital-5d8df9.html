capital

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæp.ɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
of greatest importance; principal; chief

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primary - of chief importance; principal

2. NOUN

/ ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)

Examples

Banks provide capital to entrepreneurs to help them launch new businesses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asset - anything valuable owned by a person or organization
  • Antonym: liability - financial obligation or debt

Etymology

From Latin 'capitalis' (of the head), from 'caput' (head), referring to principal sum of money.
