celioma

1. NOUN

/ siːliˈoʊmə / (AmE), / siːliˈəʊmə / (BrE)
A tumor located in the abdominal cavity.
abdominal tumor
medical

Examples

The doctor confirmed the presence of a celioma in the patient's lower abdomen.
Histopathological analysis revealed that the celioma was malignant.
Surgical removal of the celioma was deemed necessary for the patient's recovery.

Etymology

From Greek 'koilia' (belly, abdomen) + '-oma' (tumor, swelling).
