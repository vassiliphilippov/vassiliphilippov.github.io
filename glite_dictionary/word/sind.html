Sind

1. PROPN

/ sɪnd / (AmE), / sɪnd / (BrE)
a region in southeastern Pakistan, along the lower Indus River, known for its historical and cultural significance
region in southeastern Pakistan

Examples

The fertile plains of Sind have supported agriculture for thousands of years.
Hyderabad is the second largest city in the province of Sind.
Many ancient civilizations thrived in the Sind region along the Indus River.

Etymology

From Persian سند (Sind), the name of the region and Indus River.
