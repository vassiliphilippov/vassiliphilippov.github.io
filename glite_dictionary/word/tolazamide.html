tolazamide

1. NOUN

/ toʊˈlæzəˌmaɪd / (AmE), / təʊˈlæzəˌmaɪd / (BrE)
A drug used to help control blood sugar levels in adults with stable adult-onset diabetes mellitus (type 2 diabetes).
a drug for type 2 diabetes
medical

Examples

The doctor prescribed tolazamide to help manage his blood sugar levels.
Patients taking tolazamide are advised to monitor their glucose regularly.
Her diabetes improved significantly after she started on tolazamide.
