Redonda

1. PROPN

/ rɛˈdɑndə / (AmE), / rɛˈdɒndə / (BrE)
an uninhabited island in the Caribbean, part of Antigua and Barbuda
uninhabited island in Antigua and Barbuda

Examples

The government of Antigua and Barbuda maintains jurisdiction over Redonda.
Seabirds are among the only permanent residents of Redonda.
Scientists are studying the unique ecosystem found on Redonda.

Etymology

From Spanish 'redonda', meaning 'round', referencing the island's shape.
