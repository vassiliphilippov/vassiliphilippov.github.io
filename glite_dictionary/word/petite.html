petite

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtit / (AmE), / pəˈtiːt / (BrE)
A clothing size designed for women who are short or slender in build.
clothing size for short/slender women

Examples

She always shops in the petite section to find clothes that fit her properly.
This dress is available in both petite and regular sizes.
As a petite, she sometimes struggles to find jeans that are the right length.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: regular - standard clothing size
  • Antonym: plus size - clothing size for larger women

Etymology

From French 'petite' (feminine of 'petit', meaning 'small').

2. ADJ

/ pəˈtit / (AmE), / pəˈtiːt / (BrE)
(of a woman or her figure) attractively small and dainty in build.
small and dainty in build (woman)

Examples

She wore a dress that accentuated her petite frame.
In the modelling world, being petite is considered unique and appealing.
Despite her petite stature, she has a commanding presence on stage.
