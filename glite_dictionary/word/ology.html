ology

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
An informal term for a branch of knowledge or academic study, especially one whose name ends in '-ology'.
informal term for a branch of knowledge
informal

Examples

She majored in some obscure ology I'd never heard of.
Whenever he talks, he fills his sentences with impressive-sounding ologies.
The university offers dozens of different ologies to study.

Etymology

Formed by abstraction from the suffix -ology, as found in terms like biology, geology.
