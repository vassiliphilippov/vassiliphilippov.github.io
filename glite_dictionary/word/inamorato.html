inamorato

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪn.ə.məˈrɑː.toʊ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ə.məˈrɑː.təʊ / (BrE)
A man with whom someone is in love or has an intimate relationship; a male lover.
male lover; man someone is in love with

Examples

Her inamorato sent her a dozen roses every week.
In the novel, she leaves her husband for her young inamorato.
His friends joked about meeting his mysterious inamorato at last.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inamorata - a woman with whom you are in love; female lover
  • Synonym: lover - a person having a romantic or sexual relationship with another

Etymology

From Italian 'innamorato', past participle of 'innamorare' (to inspire love), from Latin 'in-' + 'amor' (love).
