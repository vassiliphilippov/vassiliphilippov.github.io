unwed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛd / (BrE)
not married; having never entered into marriage.
not married

Examples

She decided to raise the baby as an unwed mother.
Many unwed fathers are active participants in their children's lives.
The documentary focused on the challenges faced by unwed women in the 1960s.

Synset Examples

unwed mother

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married - having a spouse; joined in marriage
  • Synonym: single - not married or in a relationship

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'wed' (to marry), from Old English 'weddian'.
