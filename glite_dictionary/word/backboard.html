backboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board placed behind or beneath someone or something to provide support, especially for the back
supportive board for the back

Examples

Paramedics carefully lifted the patient onto the backboard before moving her to the ambulance.
The carpenter designed a sturdy backboard for the old bench to improve its support.
Doctors often use a backboard to prevent spinal injury during emergency situations.

2. NOUN

/ ˈbækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a raised, vertical board with a basket attached, used as part of a basketball hoop to bounce the ball off when shooting
vertical board behind basketball hoop

Examples

He banked the ball off the backboard to score.
The glass backboard shattered after a powerful dunk.
Players practice layups by aiming at the corner of the backboard.

Synset Examples

he banked the shot off the backboard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basketball hoop - circular rim and net used in basketball
