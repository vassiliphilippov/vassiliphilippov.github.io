NTIS

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ti aɪ ɛs / (AmE), / ɛn tiː aɪ ɛs / (BrE)
a U.S. government agency that provides access to scientific, technical, engineering, and business information funded by the government
U.S. agency for technical and scientific information

Examples

The NTIS is a valuable source for government-funded scientific research reports.
Researchers often obtain technical documents through the NTIS.
Many scientists rely on the NTIS to access up-to-date engineering data.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: National Technical Information Service - the full name of NTIS, a U.S. government agency for scientific and technical information

Etymology

Acronym for National Technical Information Service.
