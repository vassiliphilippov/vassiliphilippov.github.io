Datril

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtrɪl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for acetaminophen, used as an analgesic for mild pain and as an antipyretic.
brand name of acetaminophen drug

Examples

The doctor recommended Datril to help reduce my fever.
Some people prefer Datril over other brands for mild headaches.
She always keeps a bottle of Datril in her medicine cabinet.
