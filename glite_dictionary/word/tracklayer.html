tracklayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrækˌleɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈtrækˌleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to lay and repair railroad tracks
worker who lays or repairs railroad tracks

Examples

The tracklayer was responsible for repairing the broken section of rail during the night shift.
After years as a tracklayer, he knew every mile of the railway by heart.
A team of tracklayers worked together to install new tracks before the train's arrival.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ganger - foreman in charge of a gang of workers, especially on the railways
  • Synonym: railwayman - a person who works on a railway
